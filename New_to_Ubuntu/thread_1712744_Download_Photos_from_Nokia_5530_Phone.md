### Post by darolu on 2011-03-23
Hey, I have the same phone; I use Bluetooth to transfer files since I lost the USB cable :( but I initially used wired transfers, it was with Ubuntu 10.04 though, but I don't think it has changed at all. I may be wrong though.

I simply used Nautilus to transfer files (I still do the same with Bluetooth), it mounts your phone's memory card as any other storage device. So why don't you try not-running Shotwell and browse your phone's memory in Nautilus and transfer files like you do with any other directories/folders.

If you still have mounting problems even when you don't launch Shotwell just post again and we'll try to help you.
