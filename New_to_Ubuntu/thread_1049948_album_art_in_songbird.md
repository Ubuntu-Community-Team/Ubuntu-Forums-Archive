### Post by clive littlewood on 2009-01-25
Hi

I do it by selecting a song then right click and choose metadata.

You will see an album art square on the right of the box.

If no art is there press add and a file browser will appear.

I have all my album art in a seperate file so it can be backed up for safety !!

Just click on the appropriate artwork and open.

Your artwork is then asigned to that album.     :D

Hope this helps

Clive
