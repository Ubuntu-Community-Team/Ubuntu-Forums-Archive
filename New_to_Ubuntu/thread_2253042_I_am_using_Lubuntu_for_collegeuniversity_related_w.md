To change the menu backgrounds in Lubuntu (both main menu and drop down menus) here is the easy way:

Install **gtk-theme-config**

It provides a graphical tool with "custom menu colors" as one of the categories. It will appear under **Preferences > Theme Configuration**. Click the color boxes to make your color selections, and set the switch for that category to 'ON'. Log out and back in may be needed.
