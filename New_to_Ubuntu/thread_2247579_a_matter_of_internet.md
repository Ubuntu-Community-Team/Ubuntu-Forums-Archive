### Post by mcduck on 2014-10-09
Depends on thw wireless router and how it deals with load balancing. In most cases none of the would be able to sue all avalable bandwidth, as the router would balance it between all of them, giving each one equal bandwidth.

Some routers might detect the video streaming as more important than others and try to give it higher priority, while some others might not be even smart enough to balance the load evenly (for example if the router tries to give each *connection* same bandwidth, the one opening most connections would end with larger portion of the available total bandwidth.)

So, the simple answer is that there's no simple answer, but usually none of the users would get full bandwidth.
