### Post by mastablasta on 2011-07-25
you can either get a PCMCIA card for older laptop or a USB wi-fi key. both are relatively cheap. just dont' expect any super speeds on this wi.fi. also check if connection is encrypted. often public connections are not which might be a security risk (someone could be "listening").
