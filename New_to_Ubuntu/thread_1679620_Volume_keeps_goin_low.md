### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-02-01
Check the mute buttons in System > Preferences > Sound .  What mother board and does it have an on-board  sound card or an independent sound card ?  There may be a  driver requirement for the new board.

I you have some sound check your connections because if you removed the speaker wires to install the mobo it may be a wire or connection.
