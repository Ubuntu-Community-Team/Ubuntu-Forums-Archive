### Post by OffAxis on 2008-06-11
I think you're talking about the 'System Menu' (which shows the different Places - home, network, etc).
Right click on the taskbar-->Configure Panel-->Menus--> (Optional Menus) and put a check in the box for 'System Menu'.
That'll put it back on the K Panel.
