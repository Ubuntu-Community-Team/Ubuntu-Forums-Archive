### Post by nhasian on 2010-06-02
yeah hellanzb is hella old.  development has stalled on it.  thats why lottanzb is switching their backend to SABnzbd+

you dont need lottanzb at all though, you can simply use SABnzbd+ yourself.  

[http://wiki.sabnzbd.org/install-ubuntu-repo](http://wiki.sabnzbd.org/install-ubuntu-repo)

after you've got sabnzbd+ running you should also install [nzbdStatus]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/7617/") plugin for firefox. let me know if you have any other questions.  good luck!
