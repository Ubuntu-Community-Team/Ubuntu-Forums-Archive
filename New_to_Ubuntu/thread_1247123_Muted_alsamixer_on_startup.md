### Post by Addiction2Code on 2009-08-22
Are you properly shutting down the computer? A lot of times the computer will remember the volume setting from the last shutdown, if you are it could be something with xfce. Also try opening alsamixer in the terminal and setting the values, type: alsamixer and hit ESCAPE to exit, then restart properly and see if the preferences are saved.
