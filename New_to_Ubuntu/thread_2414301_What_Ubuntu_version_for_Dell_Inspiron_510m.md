### Post by ajgreeny on 2019-03-08
I think Lubuntu is the only *ubuntu OS that is worth trying on such a lowly speced machine, and even that may run rather slowly.

Libreoffice may also be a bit too much for it and I suggest you see if Abiword and Gnumeric are worthy replacements for LO Writer and Calc.
It will probably also be worth investigating a RAM increase as I believe that could well be a small investment for big performance improvement.

There are other distros, some at least based on Ubuntu, that may also be worth trying to see if they fit the bill better such as Bodhi or Puppy, and if you are able to deal with the command line when installing you could use the [Minimal CD]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD") install and then add just the packages you want; that way it's possible to get a very lean OS on a low spec machine.
