### Post by tom.swartz07 on 2009-12-29
in response to #1, go to users and groups. click the keys to unlock the applet.

Click on your name profile and hit properties.
Go to the tab that says 'user privileges' and click off the ones that you dont want to have unlocked.

Please note that its not quite advisable to give yourself *every* privilege on the list, as it might compromise your account security.
