### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-05-10
Launch Applications --> Displays.

Have to unclick the Mirror displays box.

Then, drag the right screen to the left on the Displays window.

Click on the right screen, under Launcher placement, select the right screen.

Click the Apply button.
