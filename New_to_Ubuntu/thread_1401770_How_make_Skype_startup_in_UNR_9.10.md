### Post by skvn on 2010-02-08
It says in [http://blog.dipinkrishna.info/2009/10/how-to-install-skype-on-ubuntu-910.html]("http://blog.dipinkrishna.info/2009/10/how-to-install-skype-on-ubuntu-910.html") that you should do the following:
Go to "System -> Preferences -> Startup Application", Click "Add" and enter the following details:
    * Name: Skype
    * Command: skype

I would try filling those two and not touch the other fields.
