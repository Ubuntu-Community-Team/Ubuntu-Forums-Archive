### Post by atomkarinca on 2008-05-17
When you restart your computer hit the Delete key a couple of times, it will take you to your boot screen, you will see a tab called like "Boot options" or something, hit enter and as your first boot device select cd-rom. Now you will be able to boot into your cd.
