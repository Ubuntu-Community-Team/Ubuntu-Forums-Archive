### Post by frankos44 on 2008-06-07
Compiz is good but I only like a few of the compiz features such as the "Ring switcher". It depends on what you use your pc for.

If you want an apple look for your launch icons you could try Kiba-dock. Its not straight forward to install but again I got fed up with it in the end and went back to the gnome panel organized how I like it.

Personally I just want to get the job done (writing software that is) and simplicity is a virtue. One thing I do insist on is installing mscorefonts for a nice focused crisp look and a simple uncluttered organized desktop and simple wallpaper.
