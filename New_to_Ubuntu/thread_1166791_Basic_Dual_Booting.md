### Post by lkraemer on 2009-05-22
sarum,
Here is the site you were talking about.
[http://imhdd.ms11.net/SWITCH/sw01.html](http://imhdd.ms11.net/SWITCH/sw01.html)

And here is the Ubuntu thread.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=340706&highlight=Dual+booting+two+hard+drives+as+master](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=340706&highlight=Dual+booting+two+hard+drives+as+master)

Another idea would be to have both hard drives on the IDE cable, and just use a switch (DPDT)
to switch one in as MASTER, or the other one as MASTER, since SLAVE is with the jumper REMOVED
on most drives.  I'd think that would be much easier than switching the +5VDC & +12VDC power.
But, both drives would be powered up all the time the computer was powered up.

Good Luck!
