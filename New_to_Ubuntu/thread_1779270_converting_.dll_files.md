### Post by nomko on 2011-06-10
A Windows specific DLL file won't work on Linux. There's a great difference in architecture of both operating systems. However, there's a version of Wireshark available for Linux. You migth check synaptic if there's a version available: go to System > Administration > Synaptic packagemanager. Click on Find and enter in the field Wireshark.
 
Or:
 
[http://www.wireshark.org/download.html](http://www.wireshark.org/download.html)
