### Post by coffeecat on 2010-10-31
Since the Open Printing site [says your printer works perfectly in Linux]("http://www.openprinting.org/printer/HP/HP-DesignJet_430"), that should mean that the driver will be included in a default install.

Have you tried connecting the printer, switching it on and going to System > Administration > Printing? If it's a usb printer you'll probably find that it's autoconfigured by the system (once you switch it on) quicker than you can get to the Print administration utility. If it isn't autoconfigured, just click on 'Add' and follow the prompts.
