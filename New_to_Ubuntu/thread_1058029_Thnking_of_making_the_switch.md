### Post by Therion on 2009-02-02
If you want to run serious PC games (and from your list, you do) I'd say dual-boot.  Yes, you could try Wine, but if you want my my opinion there it is: If you want to game, you dual boot.  

If you decide you want to go that route, I would agree your 80GB drive is a bit small; but then too hard drives are cheap these days and cloning them is easy enough.  Besides, with the new drive installed you can use the existing 80 gigger as an internal backup drive.  And dual-drives are the ONLY way to fly, in my opinion.  Do it... You'll be glad you did.
