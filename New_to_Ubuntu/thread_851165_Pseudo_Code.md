### Post by jpkotta on 2008-07-06
Pseudocode is one of those fuzzy things, you can't really apply a test and say it's "correct".  The purpose is to try to understand the logic of your program and not necessarily the syntax of the language used to implement it.  If the logic is clear, and sufficiently (another fuzzy term) detailed, then your pseudocode is good.

I tend to make pseudocode a bit closer to a real language, but yours looks OK.
