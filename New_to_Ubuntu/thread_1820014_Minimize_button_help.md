### Post by peter d on 2011-08-08
When an application is minimized it has small triangle to the left of the icon on the launcher. When a window is active it also has a small triangle (pointer) on the right of the icon. If you have two windows of an application open there will be two triangles on the left of the icon.

To activate a window click the icon. If there are two windows they will both be displayed abd you need to click on the one you want.
