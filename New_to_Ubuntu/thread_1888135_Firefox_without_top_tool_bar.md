When FireFox is updated, I lose the top tool bar - no bookmark choices. It frustrates me. Right click doesn't do a thing. Thanks.

UPDATE: Problem solved. Apparently, when FF was updated, display size was changed and hid the top orange toolbar. When display was minimized, the orange toolbar reappeared. Thanks.
