### Post by Darkade on 2008-07-18
[GRUB]("http://www.gnu.org/software/grub/") is the boot manager, that is to say the menu that first appears when you boot, it displays the OS that you have installed like windows, if you have it, and some versions of your linux kernel. Here are some links to know more about GRUB

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GNU_GRUB](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GNU_GRUB)
[http://www.gnu.org/software/grub/](http://www.gnu.org/software/grub/)

And you laptop may not support suspend. Suspend and hibernate are two things that the ubuntu team is working because they just kinda don't work.

Look for you laptop in [this]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam") list, from the ubuntu laptop testing team. and see if they have setup suspend in your lappy
