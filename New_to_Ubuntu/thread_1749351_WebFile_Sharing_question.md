### Post by tkelito on 2011-05-07
Yes your client would need to be on since I am guessing you are the only one "serving" the file.  Since you didn't use a torrent tracker URL when you made the .torrent file you will be relying on DHT (Distributed Hash Table) to make connection between the two of you.  May not work out if both your clients don't support DHT. 

Truthfully just grab a Dropbox account (you get 2GB storage and if you find some of the easter eggs around the internet you can get more) and stick it in your public folder.  Email him the link.  Dropbox is my best friend.
