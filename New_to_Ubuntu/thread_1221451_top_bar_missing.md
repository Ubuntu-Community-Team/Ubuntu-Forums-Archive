### Post by jrothwell97 on 2009-07-23
If you can still actually *see* the panel, then right click on it, select "Add to Panel", and use that to restore your panel applications.

If you can't see the panel, then I'm not sure if this will work, but it's worth a shot: hit Alt+F2 and enter "gnome-panel" in the run dialog. Press Enter or click Run, and the panel *should* appear. *Should.*
