### Post by Exeleration-G on 2011-04-07
I'm sorry for the necro, but this problem can be 'solved' by going to Settings --> Subtitles. You can change the subtitles' size there. Change it from 'Normal' to 'Small'. That will give you better looking subtitles. Alternatively, you can change them to 'Large'.

Hope that this has solved your question if it hasn't been answered yet.
