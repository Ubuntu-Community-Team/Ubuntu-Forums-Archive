### Post by MrWES on 2010-06-06
> **DLBuggs said:**
> What is "Daemon" and what does it do particularly ?  :confused:

Similar to a "Service" in Windows; a task, job, or program that normallly runs at boot up and and the background. For example, cupsd - the printing Daemon is running or some call it sleeping in the background, and listening to a specific port, waiting for a print job to execute.
