### Post by nhasian on 2009-07-11
yeah the specific key to enter the bios or even temporarily change the bootup sequence does differ from one motherboard/bios to the next.  the only surefire way to know is to read the text at the bottom of the screen for instructions on what to press.  but since it goes by so quickly, then trick is to press the PAUSE button on your keyboard before the POST screen appears.
