Hi all,
[B]
WARNING:[/B] TEST YOUR SPEAKER and Headphone right after UPDATE Manager updates. Of course, you probably rebooted your system already by the time you discovered that there is no sound on your speakers. Good news is you can rollback if you have this issue.

After the update by update manager, the speakers on my ACER ASPIRE 6530 (AMD-64, ubuntu 10.04) became unavailable. However, sound was still available via head phone. This issue was caused by the SOUND-INDICATOR Version 0.2.5. I have to rollback to version 0.2.2-0ubuntu1 to have sound on the speaker or headphone. 

For more information on how to rollback follow this thread (REPLY #7 by [untitledtv]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=862667")): [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=890672](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=890672)
