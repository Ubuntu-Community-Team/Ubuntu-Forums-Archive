### Post by jizou on 2010-10-14
Hi.
I am Japanese.
I hope follows method  will solve your problem.
 
Press button on Menu-bar System~Performance~"IBus Performance"

And select "Input Method" tub.
Choose Japanese and select IME such as Anthy or others on Select-form named "Select Input Method". 

Addition, you had better add japanese-key-board layout.
You can edit your layout System~Performance~Keyboard.
More additional japanese support,you can get System~Administration~"Helper
 for Japanese Environment"
Some fonts,software exist.
Thank you for your reading.
