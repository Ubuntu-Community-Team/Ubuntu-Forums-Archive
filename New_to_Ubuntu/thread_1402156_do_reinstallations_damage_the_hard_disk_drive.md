Hard to see how a few install attempts can damage the hdd. Worst case you used a few of the fairly large number of hours before it is due to fail :)

### Post by ajaysutton on 2010-02-08
No, re-installations won't damage hard drives.  You can whack partition tables and screw them up (form a software) perspective but I've never seen a drive damaged by installation and re-installation.  I'm curious why abhishek.malhorta35 says this could have been an issue with older hard drives.
