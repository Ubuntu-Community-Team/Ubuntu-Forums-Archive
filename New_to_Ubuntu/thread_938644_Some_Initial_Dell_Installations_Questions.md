### Post by PierreDeKat on 2008-10-05
First, welcome!

And I'm afraid I don't have a lot of specific answers for your networking situation, etc., but I'm sure some others will come along shortly who can be of greater assistance.

However, I do have a couple observations. First, I started using Ubuntu on an old Dell C600 laptop, mainly to get away from having to pay for yet another year's worth of antivirus subscription for it.

Now a few months later, I have Ubuntu installed on five different computers. And I have all but abandoned Windows. I still have it if I need it, but I have found suitable, and oftentimes *better*, replacements for all of my Windows applications.

As far as Frontpage is concerned, I'm using the Seamonkey suite's WYSIWYG html editor to maintain my website. But I think some other folks are using Dreamweaver on Ubuntu, IIRC.

Anywho, go ahead and take the plunge. Set up a dual boot system on your oldest, most raggedy computer, and start using it. You're going to have to figure out a few things along the way, but that's okay, because there's no such thing as useless knowledge.

And I will just almost bet that you'll do what I did and install it on all of your other computers, once you get in and realize the water's just fine.
