### Post by oldsoundguy on 2009-08-20
In most instances, unless you are installing restricted drivers for your NVidia or ATI display card, Ubuntu has already checked your hardware (sic) and installed the required drivers.
Unlike Windows, you don't have to go searching all over the web for the newest updated drivers.
There are a FEW exceptions, so you need to ask for help on getting those specific drivers from the repositories (again .. you don't have to search the web), and get them installed.
