### Post by michelebachmann on 2012-08-09
My laptop's battery can only run one minute, so I always have it plugged in. Sometimes to turn it off, I just unplug it and then it shuts off a minute later. Is this bad? Should I always click turn off?

---

### Post by anewguy on 2012-08-09
Depending on the OS involved, such things as cached (either in the system or in the drive memory) may not get written to disk.  In addition, if any form of write is happening to your hard disk at the time (watch the activity light), you can corrupt the data and file structure.

Best advice - power down using the OS, not the power button.
