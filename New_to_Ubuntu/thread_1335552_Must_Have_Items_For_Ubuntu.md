### Post by Paqman on 2009-11-25
Everybody will be different, and it really depends on what you use your machine for. Pretty much everybody will recommend installing ubuntu-restricted-extras straight away though.

Some of my favourites:

**vlc** - Media player that just plays everything.
**nautilus-gksu** - Gives you an "open as administator" on right-click in Nautilus.
**nautilus-image-converter** - right click to resize or rotate images.
**avant-window-navigator** - a Mac-style dock, best of the bunch IMO.
**preload** - adaptive caching tool that should speed up your machine.
**pysdm** - Storage device manager utility.
**compizconfig-settings-manager** - The last word in Compiz tweaking.
**wine** - Handy if you ever need to run Windows apps.
