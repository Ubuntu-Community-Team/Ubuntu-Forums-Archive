### Post by Tatty on 2008-12-21
It sounds likely that you have a bad burn.

Burn a new CD, but check the integrity at each stage.  Check the md5 hash of the .iso and then check the md5 sum after you have burned the CD.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)
