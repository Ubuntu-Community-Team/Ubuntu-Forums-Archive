### Post by mali2297 on 2008-07-18
There is [mp32ogg]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/mp32ogg"). 

Players for mp3 (mpg123) and ogg (ogg123) come as dependencies.

If you want more featureful players, there are cmus, moc and of course mpd/mpc/ncmpc.
