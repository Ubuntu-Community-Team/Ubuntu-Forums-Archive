### Post by caljohnsmith on 2008-08-01
It could be a problem with the way your volumes are set if the distortion you are hearing is due to "clipping." Try setting the Ubuntu PCM sound volume to 74% or less, and when you use VLC, set the main volume to no more then 50%. If that doesn't solve your distortion issue, then it is not due to clipping and you'll have to do more troubleshooting.
