### Post by t0p on 2008-08-29
Your modem is what's known as a "winmodem" - part of its functionality is provided by Windows.  As you want to use it in Linux, it won't work.

Winmodem support under Linux isn't great.  There is a project, trying to provide drivers so winmodems can work in Linux - it's at [linmodems.org]("http://linmodems.org").  If you want to get your modem to work, you should go to the linmodems site and see if there are drivers available.
