### Post by Paqman on 2012-01-07
The most important thing is going to be which network provides the best coverage. You'll want to talk to other people in your area about what they use and what kind of speeds they get. The [Portsmouth LUG]("http://www.portsmouth.lug.org.uk/") might be a good place to ask.

As far as compatiblity of dongles goes, I've used a Vodafone one in the past and it was plug and play. Support for these things has improved vastly in the last couple of years, so make sure you're on a recent version of Ubuntu.
