### Post by mcduck on 2014-10-11
I'm pretty sure there is no such thing as "single-click sensitivity", at least outside of what your mouse itself recognises as a click. From operating system point-of-view it's just a question of either the mouse telling it you just clicked a button, or nothing at all. It's not an analog value that could be adjusted.

If your mouse is registering click even when you are not clicking the button, then it's time to replace the mouse with a new one.

(also what you describe you are doing sounds more like double-click, or drag selection, uploading anything based on single click on a file, without even separate button to confirm, sounds pretty strange UI design)
