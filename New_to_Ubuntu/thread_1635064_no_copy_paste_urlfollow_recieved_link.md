### Post by houseworkshy on 2010-12-01
I haven't seen this one however there are a couple of things I'd try.  See if this is the case with all methods of pasting; right click context menu, middle wheel/button click when text is highlighted and from "edit in the menu bar.
Take a look at any firefox addons/plugins and see if one of those may be blocking it.  You could disable them one at a time and see if that solves it.  If it does then look at the settings of which ever one is blocking paste.
Then take a look at the firefox helps, this one may be usefull

   [http://kb.mozillazine.org/About:config]("http://kb.mozillazine.org/About%3Cb%3E%3C/b%3E:config")

If nothing else works you could try System>Administration>Synaptic package manager.  Then completely reinstall the firefox package.
You could also try another browser, seamonkey, chrome .... there are many and see if one of those does better.

Not much advice there, I know, but you've not had a reply for a while so if you've tried all that already just call it a verbose bump.  Good luck

Strangely when I tested the link which I'd copy pasted from the title bar it didn't work, maybe I have a problem too.  Anyway just search for +"about:config" +firefox and you'll find it.
