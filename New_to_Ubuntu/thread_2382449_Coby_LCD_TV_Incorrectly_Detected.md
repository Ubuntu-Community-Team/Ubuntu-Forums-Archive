### Post by Autodave on 2018-01-13
2 thoughts. First off, make sure that the monitor is on before you start the PC. I have seen that work before.

Secondly, and this works for one really weird one that I ran into, when you go into settings and it shows both monitors: chose the Coby and click "Use this display." Now, un-click "Use this display" and then re-click it.
