I'm not sure where this has anything to do with Ubuntu.  To me, it sounds in general like a Microsoft problem.  In specific, it sounds like their systems messed up on the backend.  

It happens; most e-mail providers are good about keeping backups, but you may want to consider finding an e-mail provider that will let you use IMAP.  With it you can keep local copies on your machine of what you have so far (for when you can't connect) as well as keep copies on the server (for when you need to access it by webmail).  Then it doesn't affect you as much when things mess up on the backend.
