### Post by abhishek.malhotra35 on 2010-02-11
I am assuming that you are using pop3 in evolution. Check your settings for pop3. When you enable pop3 for your email, check for remove mails from server. Else every time you try to sync your email with server, old messages will keep on duplicating in client.
