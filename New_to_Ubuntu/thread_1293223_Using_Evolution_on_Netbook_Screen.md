### Post by neu2buntu on 2009-10-19
i have figured out a workaround for this,* open evolution and unmaximize{alt f5} ,* now stretch out the 2 sides to fill your screen ,* goto your workspace switcher on bottom panel (or add it) right click and choose preferences ,*change workspaces to 1 column and at least 2 rows (i use 4),* now pull evolution about half way down the screen , this puts the other half into workspace 2, *now click workspace 2 on your panel and you can work on the bottom half of evolution...
