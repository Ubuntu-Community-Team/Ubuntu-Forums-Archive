### Post by jimmy the saint on 2008-12-21
[http://www.openprinting.org/show_pri...Dell-photo_924](http://www.openprinting.org/show_pri...Dell-photo_924)
according to this the driver you need is the same as the driver for the hp deskjet 200.
Go to
system>administration>printing
click on the NEW button
make sure that the make dropdown bos says HP and if there is any and hit next. don't worry about the rest of the boxes and lists. Click NEXT
under model select HP Deskjet 200 and hit forward
name the printer if it asks you to and then try to print something
