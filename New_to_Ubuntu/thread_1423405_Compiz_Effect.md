### Post by howefield on 2010-03-06
Have you installed compizconfig-settings-manager ?

Load it up and click on the  Scale plugin icon from the Window Management section, .

Press the button to the right of "Initiate Window Picker" this will give you a window which you can use to select the area of the screen you want to initiate the plugin from.

Press OK, then Back, then Close.
