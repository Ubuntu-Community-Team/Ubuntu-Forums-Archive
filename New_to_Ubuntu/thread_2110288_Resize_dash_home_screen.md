### Post by kanikilu on 2013-02-01
If the dash is fullscreen, simply click the "maximize/restore" (square icon) button in the top left of the window:

[http://askubuntu.com/questions/68788/how-can-i-resize-the-unity-dash](http://askubuntu.com/questions/68788/how-can-i-resize-the-unity-dash)

It looks like you cannot change the size of the window beyond that, so if it's still taking up too much space on your screen to be able to see the desktop, just close the Dash when you go to drag-and-drop:

1) Go to the Dash and find the launcher you want for your desktop.
2) Click and hold that launcher.
3) While still holding, press the keyboard shortcut for the Dash, which is usually the "Windows key".
4) That will close the Dash, now just drop the launcher on your desktop.
