### Post by snowpine on 2010-05-01
> **beantownmp said:**
> So there really wouldn't be any benefit for speed, performance, and battery life using UNE over the full edition?

UNE's main benefit is its "launcher" interface, which is visually optimized for small screens. It uses the same kernel and applications as "regular" Ubuntu, and therefore offers no speed, performance, or battery life advantages that I am aware of.
