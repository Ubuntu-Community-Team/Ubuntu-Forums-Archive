### Post by brianmc on 2009-10-11
Go through your keyboard settings very carefully, and assign a key for "Compose".

For example, I have set the left key with the Windows logo on it as compose.

Others above have listed some compose codes, but you can guess a lot of them.

The pound sign? (£) It's an "L" with a "-" on top of it. So, press compose, press L, then press -

Cent? (¢) A "c" with a "|" on top of it.

Registered? (®) It's an "O" with an "R" inside it

Trade Mark symbol? (&#8482;) It's a "T" and an "M" in one character space.
