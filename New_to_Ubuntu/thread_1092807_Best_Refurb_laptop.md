### Post by ozneil on 2009-04-08
I am running Ubuntu 8.04 on a Dell D630. Installation was easy and I have no problems.

Apart from Linux compatibility, you might also like to consider warranty status when buying a refurbished laptop. Dell and Compaq/HP (for example) have transferrable warranties. Many of the corporate Dell machines (Latitudes etc) are sold with three year warranties and if you know the service tag/serial number you can look up the warrantly status on the manufacturers web site.

If you know you still have a year's warranty remaining (for example), then this can take much of the worry out of buying a second hand laptop.

Neil.
