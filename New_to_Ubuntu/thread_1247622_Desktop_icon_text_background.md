### Post by Kobalt on 2009-08-23
Maybe take a look in System > Pref. > Appearance. Your current theme is highlighted, click on "custom" and the second tab of the opening window is "colours". Maybe what you're looking for is here.

### Post by kaitwospirit on 2009-08-23
Go to System -> Preferences -> Appearances. Click the "background" tab. The first option listed should be a solid color, and hovering over it will give the words "no desktop background." Click on that one!
