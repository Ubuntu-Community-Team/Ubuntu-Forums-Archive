### Post by howefield on 2011-03-02
You can run and use them side by side, they carry out different functions.

Compiz gives you desktop effects (wobbly windows, desktop cube and the like) and Ubuntu Tweak is an application to configure and tweak some of your Ubuntu settings and options.

[http://ubuntu-tweak.com](http://ubuntu-tweak.com)
[http://www.compiz.org/](http://www.compiz.org/)
