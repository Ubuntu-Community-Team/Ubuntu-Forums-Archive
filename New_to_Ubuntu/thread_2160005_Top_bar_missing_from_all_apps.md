### Post by BBQdave on 2013-07-05
> **Rulius said:**
> Its not the menu bar I was missing. It was the top part of the window where the "close", "minimize" and "full screen" buttons are and where you can grab the window and move it around.

If you click on the *square* in the top right menu of an application window - it maximizes or minimizes that window. If you are in maximize mode, global menu is in affect and you cannot resize your application window. Clicking on the *square* will get you out of maximize and to the sub-menu for resizing and moving your application window. :)
