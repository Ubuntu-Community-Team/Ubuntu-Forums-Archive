### Post by mcduck on 2009-05-23
Sure, you just haven't added the Window List-applet to your panel. Do that and you'll see your minimized windows again.. :)

(right-click on empty spot in the panel, select "Add to Panel", find the Window List-applet and drag&drop it into your panel. You can then move it around by dragging with middl mouse button or by right-clicking it and selecting "Move")

### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-05-23
Hi,

Just Right-click on the panel and choose "Add To Panel", scroll down to "Window List" (or "Window Selector" if you prefer menu-style) and click the "Add" button.

You'll see a little light-gray bar on the panel, right-click that and position as you like, then "Lock To Panel".
