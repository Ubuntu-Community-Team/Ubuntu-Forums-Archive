### Post by Lord Stig on 2010-01-24
You haven't said what the error messages are, but it should run on that sort of hardware. I have a Pentium 4 2.6GHz with 1.3GB RAM and it runs really fast. You (or your friend) could try installing Xubuntu, which is the same as Ubuntu but with a lighter (but still feature-packed) desktop - it might help as your RAM could be a small bottleneck there (you said you wanted to do a bit more than internet now). 
Maybe installing that fresh will help? 

Do you have plenty of hard drive space free? I got lots of errors coming up when I had no space left on my hard drive.

If you post back with the error messages you're getting others more technically-gifted than me can help!
