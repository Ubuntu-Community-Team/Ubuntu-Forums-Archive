### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-12-17
Shockwave Flash is the same as Flash Player. However, Adobe produces another player named [Shockwave Player]([http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adobe_Shockwave_Player), that is not available on Linux.

What you see in Firefox [noparse]about:plugins[/noparse] is the Flash Player.
