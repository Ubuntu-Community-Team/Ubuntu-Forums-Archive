### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-11-28
Have you actually downloaded it or is it streaming, as your post title suggests.  If it's streaming it is probably caused by an intermittent stream. The gaps will be when the audio buffer runs out and before a bit more has streamed down the line.  If you really have downloaded the file, then the file must be incomplete or corrupt in some other way.
