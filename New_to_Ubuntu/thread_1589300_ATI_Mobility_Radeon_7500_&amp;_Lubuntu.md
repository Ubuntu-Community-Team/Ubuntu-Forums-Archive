### Post by rockerphil on 2010-10-06
well typically in the past Ubuntu doesn't seem to play nice with a lot of the newer video cards (although i don't know how old your video card is) and i've heard of a LOT of video card problems arising after a driver update. in my humble opinion i'd leave it as is. since you've said you're not going to be playing any games you shouldn't need any 3D rendering which is what most of the major games call for these days. although if you're wanting to install Compiz (all the really pretty eye candy for Linux) you'll need the 3D driver, but if not, as i said, i'd leave it as is if it already does everything you want it to do. hope this helps,
