### Post by quelx on 2008-05-15
I think what you are asking for is a drawer...

Right click the panel and **Add to Panel**

Click **Drawer**

You can put all kinds of stuff in there, for Firefox bookmarks just drag the tab from the browser into the drawer (or right onto the panel)
