### Post by chopinhauer on 2010-08-12
> **the.dark.lord said:**
> 
Can anyone elaborate on what this means? And, is this problem serious?


One sector on your hard drive may be defective, if the error persists it will be reallocated to one of quite a few spare sectors the hard drive has.

I would say that there is nothing to be worried about, the “Reallocated sectors count” is what you should be watching.
