### Post by Temposs on 2010-02-21
You could grab one of the images listed in the google images search: [http://images.google.com/images?q=ubuntu%209.10%20splash%20screen%20image&oe=utf-8&rls=com.ubuntu:en-US:official&client=firefox-a&um=1&ie=UTF-8&sa=N&hl=en&tab=wi](http://images.google.com/images?q=ubuntu%209.10%20splash%20screen%20image&oe=utf-8&rls=com.ubuntu:en-US:official&client=firefox-a&um=1&ie=UTF-8&sa=N&hl=en&tab=wi)

Download it and put it in your Pictures folder as the only picture. Then set the screen saver to Pictures Folder. 

That's the most quick and dirty way to do what you want.
