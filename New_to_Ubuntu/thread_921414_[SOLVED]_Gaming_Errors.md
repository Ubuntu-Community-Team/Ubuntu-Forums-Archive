### Post by LinuxFox on 2008-09-16
Are you playing a 3D game?  If so you might want to change the effects settings in the Appearance menu to none.  I remember when I had effects set to extra, or Advanced Desktop Settings turned on (example: rotating desktop cube), it would not let me play 3D games.

I hope this helps a bit.  If you like these features, you can always turn them back on after playing. ;)
