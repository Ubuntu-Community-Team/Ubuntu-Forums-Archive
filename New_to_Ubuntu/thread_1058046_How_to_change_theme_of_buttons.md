### Post by mcduck on 2009-02-02
Program buttons, and actually the complete way your running programs look like, depends on the GTK2 theme you are using.

Icon theme are, well, icons. :)

Window borders and titlebars depend on the window manager/window decorator theme you are using. (Metacity or Emerald themes)

GDM themes are for the login screen.

None of these themes has any effect on others, with the exception that some Metacity themes use colors from your current GTK2 theme.

edit: and the best place to find themes for all these purposes, and some others, is [http://gnome-look.org]("http://gnome-look.org")
