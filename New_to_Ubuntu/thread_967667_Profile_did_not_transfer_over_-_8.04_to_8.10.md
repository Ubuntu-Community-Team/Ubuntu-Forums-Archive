### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-11-02
Unfortunately, if you use the whole disk, it formats the HD before installing - hence all your profile etc are wiped - and it will not be able to restore it.

Bit silly that the installtion recognises the profile and offers to copy across when you are formatting that profile.

For future re-installs, consider a separate /home partition.
