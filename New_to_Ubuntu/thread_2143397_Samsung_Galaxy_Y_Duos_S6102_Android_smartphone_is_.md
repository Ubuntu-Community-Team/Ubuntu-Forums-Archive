### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-05-09
Aditya, your phone does not need to appear on the desktop in order to be used as an internet connection. Plug it in, turn on USB tethering on the phone and Ubuntu will tell you that is has connected to a new "Ethernet" connection. That is all you need to do.
