### Post by RequinB4 on 2008-09-08
meh... Easytag can do this:
```

sudo apt-get install easytag
```

Just go to your mounted ipod directory (/media/IPOD?) and edit tags to your hearts content.

One drawback - Ipods usually have incomprehensible filenames, so you'll have to click on a mp3 in the list to see what it is... You'll see what i mean when you try it :)
