### Post by Paul820 on 2010-10-18
> So is ubuntu for geeks or just a great OS.

For geeks? No. The thing about Ubuntu is if you want to get to know every part of your OS it will let you. Nothing is hidden, everything can be tweaked and modified to how **YOU** want it. Windows doesn't let you do this. Microsoft locks things down so they can't be modified by you, which means Microsoft has control of your OS, not you. Ubuntu gives you control, it lets you modify things, move thing around, tweak etc. That's how it should be, after all, it's your computer!

If you don't want to tweak then that is your choice as well. You can use Ubuntu perfectly fine without tweaking or modifying. But believe me, eventually you will want to tweak because it's fun getting it to how you want it to function/look. Ask anyone on here!:P

Great OS? Yes. Give it time, learn how to do things the Linux way and you will see. :P
