### Post by halitech on 2010-02-09
when you are looking at the main window, on the left you will see the title section. simply add a new title and a new file. Use the Properties button under Titles to change the title. Menu options will allow you to add backgrounds and music to the menu if you want. Also, click on where it says Adjust Disk Usage after you have added the episodes you want to add.

I can usually add 4 1 hour tv shows to a single dvd this way.
