### Post by philinux on 2008-06-07
I just successfully remastered my system. 

I had a few snags.

1. Keeping the image file to less that 4.7 gig. Had to exclude folders. I'm backing these up separately.

Using a DVD-RW.
2. K3b failed to burn - result one coaster, yes an rw coaster.
3. CD/DVD creator failed, ended up with a blank disk even though it said burning.

4. Finally gnomebaker burnt it and it boots.

5. It kept my login theme and my desktop wallpaper.

6. Only change was login background colour reverted to beige.
