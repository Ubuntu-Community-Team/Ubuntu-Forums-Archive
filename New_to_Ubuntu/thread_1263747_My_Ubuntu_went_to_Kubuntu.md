### Post by nynoah on 2009-09-11
Just wondering if the original poster knows that he can chose which desktop environment when he boots up.  There is a section in the log in screen that allows you to chose which one you want to use.  If you have Ubuntu and install Kubuntu you do not lose Ubuntu.  You just added another option.

Also the "splash screen" that says Kubuntu now really does not mean much.  Its just you installed that graphic last over top the original Ubunutu one.  It means nothing really.  Its just a picture. You can change it back but it does nothing to the original Gnome (ubuntu) desktop environment.
