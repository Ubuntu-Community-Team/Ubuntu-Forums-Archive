So I right-click and deleted it but now, when I minimize other icons (of any program or directory), they don't appear on the taskbar. ### Post by H.Callahan on 2008-10-02
Right-click an empty spot on the taskbar (where you want the icons to appear), select Add to Panel.  In the resulting dialog box, scroll down to "Windows List" and highlight it.  Click on the Add button.  Your running programs should now appear on the taskbar.
