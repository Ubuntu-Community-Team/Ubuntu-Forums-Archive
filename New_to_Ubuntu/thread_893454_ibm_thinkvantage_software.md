### Post by schwascore on 2008-08-18
Laptop batteries are lithium-ion based and don't really need to be "refreshed."  It seems as though that software is just a gimmick, AFAIK.

It used to be that with NiCad batteries, it was best to fully discharge/fully charge rather than keep them plugged in because they would develop a "memory" which could reduce the total charge capacity over time. That's why modern laptops use lithium-ion

I really don't think that software does anything other than satisfy users who don't know better.
