### Post by slooksterpsv on 2012-04-13
What you're referring to is Unity just as an informational measure. I'm tired of it too, so I went with Xubuntu for XFCE4.8 - it's fast, lightweight, and works with the fglrx drivers I install. There is LXDE (Lubuntu uses it) which is even more lightweight. Kubuntu is a bit heavier on resources, and I've had varied results on fglrx working with it.

Personally I'd say XFCE or LXDE or even Linux Mint which has MATE (Gnome 2.x fork).
