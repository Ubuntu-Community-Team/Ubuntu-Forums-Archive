### Post by dwb814 on 2012-11-03
Do you have Ubuntu-tweak installed? If not here's the link:

[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu-tweak/0.8.x/0.8.1/+download/ubuntu-tweak_0.8.1-1%7Eprecise1_all.deb](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu-tweak/0.8.x/0.8.1/+download/ubuntu-tweak_0.8.1-1%7Eprecise1_all.deb)

Once installed, go to system tools > system settings > click on the Ubuntu tweak icon. Click on the "tweaks tab" "file manager" and click the "Automatically mount media" button. 

Hope that helps
