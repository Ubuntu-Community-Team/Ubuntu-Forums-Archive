### Post by Temposs on 2009-10-29
If you're just upgrading, your apps won't get uninstalled.

But if you do a fresh install of 9.10, then they will get wiped, of course. I think in general you should just manually reinstall your apps. But, it shouldn't really take that long if you installed most of your apps from the repos because you can just go through and check everything once and do a big download/install.

You don't want to save the apps you installed before because when you install Ubuntu 9.10, it will install new versions of those apps that are compatible with Ubuntu 9.10. The older versions you have on 9.04 won't necessarily be compatible with Ubuntu 9.10, so you shouldn't really save them.
