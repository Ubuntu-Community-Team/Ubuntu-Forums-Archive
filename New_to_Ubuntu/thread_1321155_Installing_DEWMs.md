### Post by Buuntu on 2009-11-09
I would install xubuntu-desktop.
Some gnome-specific apps might not work on there though, but there are alternatives in Xubuntu.  Most of your day-to-day apps like firefox, pidgin, evolution, etc... work just as well on either DE so you should be fine.

If you are really low on space, I would recommend installing xfce4 instead, as it doesn't come with some of the extras that xubuntu-desktop comes with.

Sizes came up as 295 mbs for xubuntu-desktop, and 95.8 mbs for xfce4 - so that's how much space you will need for each of them.  (might vary, but this is what it would take up on my computer)
