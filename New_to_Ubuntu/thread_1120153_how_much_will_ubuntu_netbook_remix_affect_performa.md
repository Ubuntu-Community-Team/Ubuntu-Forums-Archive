### Post by ddrichardson on 2009-04-08
Can't speak for the machine you have but on my Aspire One it's significantly faster, especially in booting and resuming. There are a few tweaks I did for installing on SSD - moving logging in to tmpfs and removing swap file. Also used ext2 to restrict writes.

Mine is running Office in WINE and can push/pull bazaar branches - so I guess it depends on your needs.

It is still pre-release though and I feel the concentration is Asus/Acer so your mileage may vary,
