### Post by Javelin Dan on 2014-02-24
Open Synaptic and click on "Reload" in upper left corner. This will check for all available updates - may take a while depending on your internet connection and length of time since last update. Once it's done and the Synaptic page is re-highlighted, click on "Mark all upgrades" just to the right of "Reload". A window will open  and if expanded, will show you all the upgrades allowing you to cherry pick if desired. Click on "Mark" in lower right corner of window, then click on "Apply" just to rightr of "Mark". Another window will open - click on "Apply" in lower right corner of that window. Your updates will now be downloaded and installed.

Edit: I casually mentioned you could cherry pick your updates. I definitely do NOT recommend this unless you know exactly what you are doing - way too many dependencies involved and you could easily break your system.
