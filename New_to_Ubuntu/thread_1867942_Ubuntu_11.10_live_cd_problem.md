I do believe the majority of the posters here are having troubled with the Ubuntu Live CD, not Xubuntu. I strongly recommend perusing any Live CD posts with the Xubuntu tag.

Regarding everyone else:

As Teo said, the accessibility and alternative options logo screen is part of the boot sequence for the Live CD; if you don't ever see it, then chances are your download is faulty.

If you do see it and it still wont log in, try running a disk check from the alt options menu -- chances are your disk is still faulty.

Repeating what LinuxFan999 said (because it's quite relevant), did you all download the .iso from a legitimate Canonical source or from a mirror or alternative site?
