### Post by coffeecat on 2012-11-20
Have a look at one of the first two links in my sig, depending on whether you are using 12.04 or 12.10. Those are good guides to all the features of the Unity desktop.

Tap the Super (Windows) key to open the dash and start typing the name of an app you want, or even a generic word such as "image" for the various image editing apps, and a list of matches will appear in the dash. Click on whichever you want. That's just to get you started. There's a lot more!
