### Post by Blivia on 2010-11-18
If you say you get to that Who are you screen, but it wont let you go forward, you could've filled something in wrong. Your Name can be anything you want, uppercase, lowercase, whatever works for you. (Not sure about spaces.)

The Computer name can not have spaces, that's the only thing I know for sure.

Your username Must be all lowercase, and you must have a password filled in. (The password can be uppercase, lowercase, numbers and letters, but you need one.)

Only once you've correctly filled out these fields will the button light up, I hope this helped. ^_^, I had the same problem before I realized I was typing in my username with an uppercase letter, then I facepalmed. Best of luck getting Ubuntu up, and running. =D
