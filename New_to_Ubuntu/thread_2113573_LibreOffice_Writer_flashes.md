### Post by blackbird34 on 2013-02-07
What, you mean when all the toolbar icons go grey for a split second and a quick progress bar goes across the bottom of the screen? Yeah, just LibreOffice's way of saving. By default it auto-saves an emergency backup copy of the files you're working on every 15 minutes, so the "animation" is similar to when you save. Your computer may be fast, which makes it look like a graphical glitch.

Not especially elegant, but your LibreOffice sounds fine. And your computer too.
