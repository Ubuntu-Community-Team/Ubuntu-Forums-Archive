### Post by -kg- on 2009-05-15
Both of these engines are in the repositories.

In my installation, the Murrine GTK+ 2 theme engine is already installed.  I found several entries dealing with Pixmap, but didn't find anything concerning a GTK+ 2 based engine for it.  Just launch Synaptic, do a search for Pixmap, and see what you can find out.

BTW, I just Googled Pixmap ("Pixmap engine," actually) and got all kinds of hits.  [Here's a tutorial]("http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials/GtkEngines/PixmapEngine") on Pixmap off the first page.
