### Post by Inxsible on 2008-06-05
When you build up from the server, you get some apps like apache server etc etc.

which you may or may not want. Minimals are best because they install nothing but the core...so you can decide what you want on your system.

Having said that, I am sure you will probably be building from ground up at least a couple of times. So for now, you should be ok.

Also, you can get a different minimal CD. you dont even have to get 650 MB of alternate if you are going to install minimal. Minimal CD's for Ubuntu are available [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD"). They are all less than 10MB for i386 and 64 bit pcs.  Saves a lot of bandwidth :)
