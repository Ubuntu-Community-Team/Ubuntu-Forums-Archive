### Post by Martin Houlton on 2011-10-21
Hi

I recently upgraded to Ubuntu 11.10 and in the process got the Unity desktop. I like it generally but I can't seem to control the order that programs appear in the launcher. I would like Firefox and Thunderbird at the top, then the LibreOffice icons, and Banshee, GIMP and UFRaw at the bottom. I seemd to be able to move icons around at first, but now I can't. Thunderbird has got stuck at the bottom and won't move. Even if I remove it from the launcher and reinstall it from the Dash (?) it goes back to the same place. Help please!

---

### Post by kolinab on 2011-10-21
What works for me is a click and HOLD on the icon you want to move, drag it OFF the launcher, then back on in the position you want it in. I know that seems sort of obvious but dragging it off the launcher space and back on seemed to do the trick for me.
