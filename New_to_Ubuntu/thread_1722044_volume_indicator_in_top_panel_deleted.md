### Post by dcsoldschool53 on 2011-04-05
> **rez182 said:**
> i clicked quit on an app in the top panel but it also removed evolution mail icon and volume icon in the top panel. how can i bring them back?

thanks

Click Systems > Preferences > Startup Applications, and click on it. Scroll down to evolution and volume control. Make sure to check mark both to add them back to startup.

If you do not see it there, it can be re-added to line up.
