### Post by anewguy on 2010-07-26
To expand on the above request:

- do you want to be able to access the internet from any of the computers?  If so, what type of internet connection do you have?

- what are the specs on the computers you want to connect?

- what brand/model of wireless adapter are in each of the computers?

- what wireless equipment do you currently have?

- And a more general question - what did you want to do?  It makes a difference if you have 1 machine running Ubuntu and another running Windows as the instructions will be different.

- Do you want the computer just to be able to talk to each other wirelessly but not connect to the internet?
