### Post by audiomick on 2011-12-16
I'm not sure that you can. I noticed on mine that it started doing that a while back, I think with Ubuntu 9.10, but I am not sure. It was caused by the sound system (Pulse? I can't remember... ) starting up. I saw a number of posts about it at the time. I haven't paid any attention to it recently as I now have speakers that only get turned on when I want to hear something, i.e. after the computer has been fired up.

I thought it was pretty poor, actually. I am a sound engineer, and know that this sort of behaviour can damage speakers. I wouldn't worry too much about computer speakers for $20, but if anything better quality is connected, I'd be leaving it off until the computer is on, and turning it off before the computer is shut down.
