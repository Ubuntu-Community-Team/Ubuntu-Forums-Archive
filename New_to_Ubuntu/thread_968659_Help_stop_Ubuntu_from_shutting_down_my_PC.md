### Post by rouge568 on 2008-11-02
Your problem is that your power management settings are turning off your computer when you're not using it, and fortunately, it's an easy fix. 
[LIST]
[*]On the top bar, right-click the battery icon, and select 'Preferences'. 
[*]If you have a desktop PC, push the 'Put computer to sleep...' slider all the way to the right, so it says 'Never'. 
[*]If you are using a laptop, you may want to do this is the 'On Battery Power' tab as well.
[/LIST]
As to your rebooting problem, I don't have much knowledge in that area. Someone else might be able to help you, however.
Hope this helps!
