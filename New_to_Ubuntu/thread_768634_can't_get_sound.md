### Post by neoMAX on 2008-04-26
This is what worked for me, hopefully it will help you out...

Go to System > Preferences > Sound

Try changing Sound Events and Music And Movies to different mixers and hitting the Test button until they play a tone.

Under Default Mixer tracks, try changing that as well. Then, once you get it all working, simply click Close and restart your system, should work fine.

I hope this helps! I'm fairly new to Ubuntu myself, so if it doesn't work, I do apologize :(
