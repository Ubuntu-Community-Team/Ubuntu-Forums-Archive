### Post by wjaydavis on 2009-03-22
I recently installed Ubuntu 8.10, which comes with Firefox 3.0.7.  Now my Close (X), Unmaximize (_) and minimize (-) button disappear.  I can press F11 and they will appear temporarily, but if I go to a new site they are gone again!

---

### Post by chuuk on 2009-03-22
Press F11 one more time? You're going into full-screen mode. And your unmaximised windows is going above and behind the bar at the top of the screen. Press Alt+click in the middle of the window and drag the window down a bit, then maximise it. Things should be fine.

---

### Post by wjaydavis on 2009-03-22
Thanks Chuuk.  Dragging the window down seems to have solved my problem.
