### Post by Tatty on 2008-04-26
How far have you got in your linux journey so far?  Are you having specific issues or are you simply asking what the first things are you need to do?

In short:

1. Dowload a CD image of a distro you want (eg [ubuntu]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download"))
2. Burn the image to a CD
3. Reboot - the computer should boot to the CD (if not, you will need to change the boot order in your BIOS)
4. Install

I reccomend Psychocats as a brilliant selection of guides for beginners [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/) - read through at least all of the Just Beginning guides before you start.

You can do it on a flash drive I believe, but I would not reccomend this to a beginner, as it is not yet a common method of installation so it requires a little more work to do.

This link will tell you how to do it: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick)

Also, you do not have to remove windows from your machine, as you can dual boot both OS's - infact i would reccomend you keep on windows until you are happy and confident with ubuntu.
