### Post by Dennis N on 2013-12-22
You can possibly override a web page's colors in Firefox. See Preferences > Content, and there you find the section "Fonts and Colors" Click the "Colors" button, and uncheck "Allow pages to choose their own colors...". It should then use the colors you specify in that same dialog box for text and background.
