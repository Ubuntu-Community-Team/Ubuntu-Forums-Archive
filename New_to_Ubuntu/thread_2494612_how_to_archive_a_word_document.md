### Post by Dennis N on 2024-01-22
You will find the steps for creating an archve by selecting 'help' in the menu. The only thing that might not be clear is that after you check the boxes for files you want to add in step 5, you need to press the Add button in upper right. The archive is then automatically saved in the location you specified.
