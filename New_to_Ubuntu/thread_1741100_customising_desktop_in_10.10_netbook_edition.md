### Post by StarZtorm on 2011-04-27
Tootler, as for adding applications in the bar on the left, when you have an application open, it will appear there. Then u can right click its icon and click on the option "keep in starter" or whatever it says (im using norwegian language on my setup, so it might be named something else) When you do this, you can close the program, and access it later from the bar since it will be saved there.. (You can also scroll the bar up and down with the mouse wheel if theres alot of open programs on your system)

As for changing backgrounds, you need to look at your system tools. click the top left button (the one with the ubuntu icon on it) and then you can either navigate around or just type appearance in the search bar.. This should bring up the appearance program, (icon with shirts and ties) open that, and then choose the second tab in the program which should read "background". From there you should be able to add and change backgrounds. Good luck!
