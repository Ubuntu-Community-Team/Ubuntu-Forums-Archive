I have the answer, even though its been months.
Step 1: Download sound theme from gnome-look.org and extract anywhere. (I suggest Borealis, it's what I have.)
Step 2. Open up synaptic package manager and search for ubuntustudio-sounds
3. Mark for installation and wait.
--There will be a new folder in usr>share>sounds called "Ubuntu Studio," it holds your sounds.
4. Goto: System>Preferences>Sound
5. On the box next to "Sound themes" click and change from "Ubuntu" to "Ubuntu Studio."

Remember, This hasn't been tested on Karmic, and it hasn't been tested with another sound theme (Borealis)
I really hope this helps.
