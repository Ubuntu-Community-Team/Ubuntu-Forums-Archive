### Post by CatKiller on 2009-07-05
There should be instructions where you got the themes from.

Otherwise, you can put them in, as an example, ~/.screenlets/WorldClock/themes if you have the screenlet installed in your Home folder, or /usr/share/screenlets/WorldClock/themes if you have the screenlet installed system-wide. I believe the themes for WorldClock, Clock and CairoClock are interchangeable. Probably others, too.
