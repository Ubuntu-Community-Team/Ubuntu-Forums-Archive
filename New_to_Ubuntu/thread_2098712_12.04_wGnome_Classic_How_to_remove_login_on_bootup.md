### Post by aeolibus on 2012-12-27
Try the following : 
go to 

   -  User Accounts
unlock it (right- top)  by entering your pw and then activate automatic login
This should deactivate the pw requirement after booting.

The lock for the screensaver can be deactivated in 
- system settings
  - Brightness and lock
Click the lock to "off" position.
