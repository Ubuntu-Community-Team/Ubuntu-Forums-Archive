### Post by Steelmourne on 2009-06-04
The dust theme should be built in. Right-click on a blank part of your desktop, choose change desktop background, then switch to the themes tab at the top. Dust should be installed. Just click it to change the theme you are using now.

### Post by QIII on 2009-06-04
Not at my Ubuntu machine right now, so this may not be entirely correct.

Right click on panels.

Go to Properties.

Fiddle through the tabs until you find one that has a color selector and a slider for transparent -> opaque.

Select a dark color (say black) and adjust the transparency to your liking.

If you want, you can download .png images for your panels (gnome-look.org, I think) and use the same properties to set the image...

Or, as the other poster said, check the readme.
