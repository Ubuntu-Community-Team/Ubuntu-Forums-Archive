### Post by mattharris4 on 2015-02-26
I have been using either Ubuntu or Edubuntu since May 2014 and I still usually receive an update at least every other day.  This is more frequent than Windows which usually updates 1-2 times a month.  If you are using a satellite internet provider set your computer to inform only and update during your restriction-free hours (either 12-5am or 3-9am depending on your provider/package last I checked).  There may be a way to automatically update at a certain hour of the night but I am not finding that option easily, unfortunately.
