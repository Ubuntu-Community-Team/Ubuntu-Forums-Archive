### Post by uRock on 2010-09-10
You aren't going to find much WEP support. It is too insecure. Why not just change your router to use WAP2? WPA2 is much more secure and there isn't any compatibility issues.

Ubuntu should run decently on those specs, but you could use Lubuntu(lightest of the *buntus) or Xubuntu, which is a little lighter than Ubuntu.
