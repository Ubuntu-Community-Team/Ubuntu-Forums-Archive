Hello! I went through exactly the same thing two weeks ago. The MB on my pooter died and I had to get a new system immediately.

I bought a Dell similar to what you describe but with Dual Core E2160 (1.80Ghz), 2GB memory and the 250 MB HD. Also added a Firewire card. No idea what the MB is, but I assumed Dell would select something that will work reliably.

I also bought a 22 ultra sharp flat screen monitor.

I am pleased with the pooter and enjoying learning Ubuntu (which I had never used before). I partitioned the HD so that I have about 10GB for / and 2 GB for swap. The remainder is my /home.

I would guess that you should be happy with an even hotter set-up than the one I got. Dell got the whole thing to me in six days.

I don't see that you can beat Dell on the price.
