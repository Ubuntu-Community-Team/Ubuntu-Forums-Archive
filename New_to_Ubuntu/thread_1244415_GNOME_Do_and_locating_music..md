### Post by Simian Man on 2009-08-19
> **tmurph said:**
> Question: Is there a way for GNOME Do to look for songs off of a connected iPod? I can play songs off my iPod normally thru Rhythmbox under devices, but I'd like to be able to use gnome do to search as well!
I don't believe so no.  Gnome-do searches your Rhythmbox music library which is separate (ie you can search w/o RB even running).

> **tmurph said:**
> Also, for a bonus, can you copy songs from an iPod directly onto a computer with ubuntu?

Yes!  Just drag the music from the iPod to your My Library.  It will copy it to your library and create folders and file names correctly as if you had ripped the music from a CD.
