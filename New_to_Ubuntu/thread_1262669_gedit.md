### Post by dzon65 on 2009-09-10
How can I make gedit to open twice.For instance,when I want to compare two css files,I can't put them next to one another.One of them will close.

---

### Post by ad_267 on 2009-09-10
When you open separate files in tabs in gedit, you can click on a tab and drag it out of the gedit window and it will open a separate window.

---

### Post by SteveNorman on 2009-09-10
other way is to use terminals, either one for each or use the & symbol after each to bring back a cursor.

```
ad_267$ gedit file1.css &
ad_267$ gedit file2.css &
```
