### Post by jmszr on 2010-06-27
First, do you have the proper resolution for your monitor?

You can change the font size under System > Preferences > Appearance > Fonts.

You state "everyone so small", I take it you mean images also. In that case try the Firefox Addon NoSquint: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2592/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2592/) .
I have found it to work quite well with both text and images.
