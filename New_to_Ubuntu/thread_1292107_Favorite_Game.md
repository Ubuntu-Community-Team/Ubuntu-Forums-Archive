### Post by marcelluspye on 2009-10-15
Over the summer I learned about and torrented a 1998 bungie RTS called Myth II. The game has amazing graphics for its time, and with the user-created patch 1..7 (allowing online play through a site called MariusNet) I found it very fun. The game is available for Linux, though the torrent I got I did for Windows and use Wine. It is a rather gory but immensely fun game with a well-plotted single-player campaign and an extremely-well designed multiplayer (MariusNet, the only working server for the game, only has about 3 running games and 40 players in the server lobbys at any time.) go to thetain.totalcodex.net for the 1..7 patch, and mariusnet.com to get a multiplayer account. As for the game, you'll have to buy it (it may be 11 years old, but is still 50 bucks for Myth: The Total Codex (the first two games plus bonus features)) or torrent it, like I did. See you server-side!
PS my avatar is an artist's rendition of the dwarf in-game, who literally runs around laying satchel charges and throwing molotov cocktails!
