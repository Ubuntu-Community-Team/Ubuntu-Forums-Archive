### Post by Lateralis on 2011-05-01
Programs that you've installed will remain, including all of your customisations and personalisations.

Before you do the upgrade, please, please please ensure that you have created a full backup of all of your important data and files (photos, music, documents...) to an external medium, e.g. CD, DVD, USB pen drives or external hard drive.  This is of course good practice anyway, but in some cases the upgrade can go wrong and some people end up doing a fresh install.  In such a situation you'll be glad you backed up!  

Also, when you do the upgrade make sure you have a good, stable internet connection and do *nothing* whilst it is doing the upgrade.  If you upgrade using the CD, the same still applies - let it do its thing without interruption.
