### Post by vmsda on 2012-12-26
Having decided to change my hardware, I opted for the abovementioned portable (it is really portable, not just transportable). Since the 12.04 LTS was also available around the same time frame, I decided to go for it; not without some misgivings, given the wealth of snags reported in various docs I read beforehand.

Well, I am glad to inform whoever it may concern that eveything has run absolutely smoothly so far, with not a single hw vs sw glitch. To top it all, version 12.04 is decidedly a smooth step forward in the usability chapter. All in all, I have never had a more satisfying hw-sw pair since I became a regular Linux user a decade and a half ago.

I still have to keep a Windows partition because of two or three applications, but to me Windows is more and more old hat.

Thanks to all who put up so much effort to make all this possible. I have never spent better money than the one forked out to download the 12.04.
