### Post by d4m1r on 2012-03-26
FYI, your motherboard has to support UEFI for you to be able to use it....You can't switch between BIOS and UEFI, even if your motherboard supports both as far as I know.

Currently, VERY few motherboards actually support UEFI, and of the ones that do, they are expensive. If your PC was under $1000 USD, you don't have UEFI.
