### Post by csutherland on 2013-04-21
I have seen several methods for installing Ubuntu 12.10, but none of them worked until I found the following:[HR][/HR]First, go to "Downloading Ubuntu," at [COLOR=#dd4814][http://www.ubuntu.com/download/](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/)[/COLOR][desktop/windows-installer]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/") where I found a button (Get the Installer) that downloads the installation tarball Ubuntu-12.10-wubi-amd64.tar.xz.  (The button is a little misleading because you get not only the installer but the complete Ubuntu 12.10 installation.  And, although the tarball is labeled -amd64, Ubuntu 12.10 installed fine on my 32-bit machine.) [HR][/HR] Second, get a copy of the manual "Getting Started with Ubuntu 12.10" (pdf).  117 pages of great stuff, well-written.  [HR][/HR]:D
