I also have a "server" (running xubuntu-desktop) that serves pictures (photos) to a "digital photo frame" that I've made from an old Compaq laptop. It sits on the wall as a frame, but pulls images from the server and keeps displaying them (like a slideshow).

It also plays music to a (home-brew) FM transmitter, so that others in the house can tune in (to a specific band) to music. 

And I have another box running MythBuntu for PVR functions; unfortunately that's not fully setup yet; once it's ready, I plan to have it stream video for remote devices to access (I imagine a day where I will watch tv serials while I'm in the shower).
