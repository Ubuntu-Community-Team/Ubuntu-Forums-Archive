### Post by sgosnell on 2009-10-27
No, they don't have to be stored anywhere.  You can "Move to trash" or "Delete", and they are not the same.  When you delete a file, use Shift+Del, not just Del.  That completely deletes the file without moving it to trash.  The external drives **should** be mounted as user-owned, and you should have no problems deleting the files and folders, but glitches can happen.
