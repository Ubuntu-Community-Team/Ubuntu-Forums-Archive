### Post by Matt__ on 2011-06-02
Log out and select ubuntu classic from the drop down menu at the bottom of the login page.

---

### Post by el_koraco on 2011-06-02
Logout, choose your username, look down, there's a drop down menu for choosing a session, select Ubuntu Classic.

### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2011-06-02
The task bar is gone under Unity.  What is on the left is called the launcher I believe.  You can return to the old Gnome classical view before you log in.  Just select classic view.
