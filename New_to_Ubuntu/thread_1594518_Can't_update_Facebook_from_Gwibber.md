### Post by Ranger_Joe on 2010-11-03
Hey guys, I found a great work-around. I shouldn't say "great" because it is in no way ideal. However, it does allow you to update your Facebook from Gwibber.

I'm sure MANY have already thought of this but... here it is:

Go to Facebook and search for "Twitter." Click on the app and sync it to your Facebook account. Make sure to click the box that allows updates to sync.

Then, if you haven't already, add your Twitter account to Gwibber. Then, when you update, make sure the Twitter button is lit up and boom! You can update your Facebook from Gwibber, with Twitter as the go-between.

This forces you to have and use a Twitter account but it's better than nothing. Plus, I think Twitter is cool because it allows you to do some things that Facebook doesn't... at least not quickly. 

Anyway, there it is. Cheers.
