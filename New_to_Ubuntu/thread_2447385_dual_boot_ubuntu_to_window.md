### Post by Mark Phelps on 2020-07-19
Depends on what you mean by "booting from Ubuntu".

Dual boot is the process of establishing two (or more) bootable OSs on the same PC, and in the process you can create a menu, that displays when you boot, allowing you to choose Ubuntu or Windows.

But if you're asking can you boot into Windows from the Ubuntu desktop, the answer is no.
