### Post by archolman on 2012-05-08
I have it in the top-panel all the time, as my connection is sometimes glitchy, so it's a useful tell-tale for the state of the connection.

Right Click on the panel, select 'Add to Panel', & see if it's in the list. Select & press 'Add'.

### Post by Azdour on 2012-05-08
Hi,

It may be that the notification area has been removed from your top panel. Just right click on the top panel, select 'Add to Panel' and add the 'Notification Area' back.

The network manager always appears (unless the notification area has gone) and to the best of my knowledge it has nothing to do with updates...
