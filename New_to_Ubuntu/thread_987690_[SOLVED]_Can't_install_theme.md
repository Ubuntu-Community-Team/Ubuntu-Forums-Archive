### Post by fooman on 2008-11-19
what kind of theme is it? ...gtk theme?...icon theme?

if its a gtk theme,  open your home folder and in the toolbar click on "view"...put a check next to "show hidden files"

find the .themes folder and open it.  you should find the theme in there and be able to delete it.  if its an icon or mouse theme...look for it in the .icons folder.

once its deleted from the folder,  you should be able to reinstall it.
