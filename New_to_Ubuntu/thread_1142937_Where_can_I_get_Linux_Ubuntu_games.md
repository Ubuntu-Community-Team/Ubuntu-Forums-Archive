### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-04-29
C'Mon Lester, you at least should make an effort to find it yourself first.

1. On the Applications menu, choose Add/Remove. Then scroll down to Games. There are quite a few there.

2. Open Synaptic (System/Administration) and scroll down to the Games and Entertainment section. On my PC there are three sections, the regular one, Universe and Multiverse.

3. There are games out on the web too, but you have to decide which ones to trust. You could check your ISP - many of them have sections for their members devoted to stuff like this.
