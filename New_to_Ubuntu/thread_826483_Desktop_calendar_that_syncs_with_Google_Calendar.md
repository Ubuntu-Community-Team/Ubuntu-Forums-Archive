### Post by onamattuphere on 2010-08-23
I am also in the search for the perfect calendar app. Actually I'm also looking for the perfect mail client as well. I need the calendar to be viewable on the desktop at all times, as well as tasks and todos. The latter being the most crucial. Unfortunately Evolution does neither.

So far my favorite calendar app is Rainlendar. The only problem is that it's not open source and some say it's too bloated. While I avoid heavily bloated software, I do prefer it over the fat free option, if it has the functionality I need. I do recommend that you try Rainlendar. 
You can also try to set up conky as it has calendar support. I might give it a try again, now that I have a bit more linux knowhow. Both these options should have google calendar support.Good luck
