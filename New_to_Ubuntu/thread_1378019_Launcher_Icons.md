### Post by howefield on 2010-01-10
> **lizandeen said:**
> I recently installed Spotify (with Wine) and was wondering how to change the icon  on the desktop launcher 

Go to System > Preferences > Main Menu, and in the left pane, scroll down to Wine > Programs and highlight the application you want to change, select properties and click on the icon in the top left corner. You might be restricted to the icons in your wine installation, so may need to copy your desired icon there first. 

> also how to add the launcher (with relevant icon) to the Applications panel?

Right click the panel and select Add to Panel, click on Application Launcher and press the forward button, navigate to the application you want, then highlight it and press the add button. Then right click the icon on the panel to move to the position you want.
