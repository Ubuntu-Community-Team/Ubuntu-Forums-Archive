### Post by ibjsb4 on 2014-09-10
The gnome-panel ..

Your panel is not disappearing; your panel is black on black.

I have found this to be a common failing in both of my builds of 12.04 and 14.04.  This can be cause by two things.  First ..

At the initial install of flashback the panel is simply black on black.  This can be fixed by *right-click* on the panel and go to properties>background and use solid color.

#2
After the initial install the panel can revert back to black on black.  This can be fixed by ..
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2242008&p=13117502&viewfull=1#post13117502](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2242008&p=13117502&viewfull=1#post13117502)
This seems to happen when either updating or installing new packages.  By the way, this is with the default theme.

For me, #1 also holds true for gnome-terminal.  It is black on black after the initial install.

*right-click*
Is usually 'Windows (Super) key' plus 'Alt' plus 'r-click', but some find that 'Alt' plus 'r-click' will work.
