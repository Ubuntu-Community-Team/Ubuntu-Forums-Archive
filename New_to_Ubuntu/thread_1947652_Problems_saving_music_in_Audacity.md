### Post by Carnivorum on 2012-03-27
I'm having problems with saving/exporting a file to another folder with Audicity. 

When I go to the exporting process, and tell him which folder to save to,and click on save, I get a pop up, about "saving meta data".  When in that I click on save, I get a new pop up:  "save meta data as".  When I click in  that on save, I get back to the first pop up, "saving meta data", and when I click on that one on save, I'm back in pop up #2, and I never get it saved.

I installed LAME, and it happens when I want to save it as waav or mp3

---

### Post by Carnivorum on 2012-03-27
I managed to bypass the problem, by going in Audacity to edit, - preferences,  - import/export, and unmarking the option "Show metadata editor prior to export step"
