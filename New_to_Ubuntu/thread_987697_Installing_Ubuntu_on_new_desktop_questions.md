### Post by jimmy the saint on 2008-11-19
It is the processor that must be 64 bit capable, and yours is.  The drive is just a drive, no matter what system its in!  Ubuntu is actually really easy to install.  Just put in the CD, turn on the computer, choose install and follow the directions.  Just pick Guided entire drive when it asks you how you want to partition the drive.  Once that is all done, you will have a new Ubuntu system and probably lots of questions about using it!!  thats what the forums are for.

This is a guide, should you want to read through it first though!
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation)
