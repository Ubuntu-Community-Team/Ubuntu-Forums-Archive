### Post by carusoswi on 2008-11-28
Finally, with the current upgrade to version 2 TurboPrint finally appears to fully support my Canon i960 printer.  The interface seems to be great, and includes some features that even the original OEM driver's interface doesn't include, like a little item that shows you how much ink of each color you use for any given print project - not that it matters, but it's still a cool feature.  Most important, I can actually print with what appears to be the same quality that the printer can achieve from the OEM drivers in XP.  At last . . . at last!
