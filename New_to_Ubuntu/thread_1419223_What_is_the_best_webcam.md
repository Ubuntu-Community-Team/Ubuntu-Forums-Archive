### Post by Dermot Doyle on 2010-03-09
Hi, first let me say that I am not very computerate, but I just bought a Logitech C200 webcam with microphone for 12.05 sterling, threw away the Windows software and plugged it in. Opened Skype, went to 'options' and 'video devices', selected 'test' and it worked straight off. The sound out didn't work so I went to 'sound devices' and selected 'default device' and everything works perfectly. Very impressed with it. Good luck.
