### Post by howefield on 2010-04-17
> **harry003 said:**
> Looking at Update Manager, I see that there are over 300 updates waiting to be performed, at nearly 300M total, which is large but not huge.

Should I just do them all and get it over with, or is that a waste of time? 

All updates are intended to improve and fix what you have. Lucid is the current development release, so you should be aware that you may encounter breakage of your system.

Updating isn't a waste of time. It is part of the development process and part of the reason you are running it, unless you just want the latest before it is ready. :)

> when the final release comes along, will it just slip into place or should I plan on doing everything over?

By using Update Manager to keep your install up to date, you will end up with the release version.
