### Post by Marlonsm on 2009-11-28
After clicking the arrow there shold be an "Install" button.
Just click that, put in your password and Ubuntu will do the rest.
After it's done the Software Centre will go back to where it was, but this time, instead of the "Install" button, you'll see a "Remove" one.
Now you can close the Software Centre and the application should be in the "Games" section of the main menu.
