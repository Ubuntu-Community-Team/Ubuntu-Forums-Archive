### Post by pme 72 on 2009-04-01
My ATI Radeon Xpress 200 card worked fine with Open Source drivers in Intrepid Ibex. The only functionality missing was Suspend/Hibernate and my desktop machine boots so quickly I just turn it off rather than Suspend. Enabling some of the advanced Compiz funtions caused some minor issues but nothing that prevented me from enjoying the feature. I never bothered to enable the proprietary drivers in Intrepid. 

Have you tried Hardy Heron, 8.04? Suspend/Hibernate worked for me when I enabled the proprietary drivers. Compiz worked too. Unfortunately Compiz is enabled by default in Hardy and it is tricky to get the Live CD to run. If you load the OS you can boot into FailSafe Gnome and either disable compiz or enable the proprietary drivers.
