### Post by fooman on 2009-10-26
any reason why it wouldn't work for a dvd?

after installing, open kover....go to kover in the toolbar, select "image embedding".  navigate to image and choose where to place it on dvd cover.  insert any text you need,  choose print...make sure landscape is selected in printer properties and choose long side.
