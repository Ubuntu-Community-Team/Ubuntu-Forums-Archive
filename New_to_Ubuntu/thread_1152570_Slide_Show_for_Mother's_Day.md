### Post by egalvan on 2009-05-07
This is a Mother's Day Project....
feel free to copy it, use it, appropriate it, etc.
Help spread the love...


I took a 19" LCD monitor, and a laptop,
stripped the un-essentials,
and had a custom wood frame built to house them both.

The laptop currently has 1G RAM, network connection, and  video.
(broken display, so it was cheap)

I want to find a SLIDESHOW type software,
 to display pictures...

the software needs to access a given directory, and pick up all graphics, then drop into any sub-directory, etc, etc. 
and repeat.
(If this is a problem, I could try to cram all pictures into one directory.
There are over 2,000 pictures, and counting.)

It should  "dissolve" from one picture to another, nothing fancy needed, just don't want a  "flip"-type segue..

I may later on add video, to show home-movies...

This can be command-line.

It can run on Debian, Ubuntu (Gnome,KDE,xfce, or cli), Puppy, etc.



Our Mother no longer recognizes any of her grown children..
yet she does know who those little children in the pictures are...

so we are busy collecting and scanning them.


Remember your parents this Mother's Day/Father's Day.

If they are still around, give them a hug and your love.
