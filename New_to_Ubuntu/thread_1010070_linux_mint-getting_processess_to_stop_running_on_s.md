### Post by RealPSL on 2008-12-13
System > Preferences > Sessions for the processes related specifically to your login.

System > Administration > Services for the processes for all users.

Note that the later should be done very carefully. Some processes are very important to the functioning of your computer. This also assumes that Mint did not customize these menus significantly.
