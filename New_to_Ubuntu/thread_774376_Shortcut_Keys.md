### Post by kestrel1 on 2008-04-29
If you go to System/Preferences/Keyboard Shortcuts you can decide what key combo does what. You can't directly set a key combo for hibernate, shutdown etc, but you can use ctrl/alt/delete or another key combo to bring up the logout/ shutdown etc menu & use:
alt/l = Logout
alt/s = Shutdown
alt/h = Hibernate
etc.
There may be another way, but I have not found it yet.
