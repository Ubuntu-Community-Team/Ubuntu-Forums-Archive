Try "The Linux Command Line", by William E. Shotts, Jr. It is available for download free, although if you are happy with it, you can make some donation to his ongoing efforts or buy the paper version or e-reader version outright. I have no affiliation with Mr Shotts, but have found his book both friendly and useful for someone trying to learn the command line.

link is: [http://linuxcommand.org](http://linuxcommand.org)
