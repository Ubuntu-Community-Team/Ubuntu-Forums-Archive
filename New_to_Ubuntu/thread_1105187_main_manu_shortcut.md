Right click on the bottom panel>add to panel. choose Main Menu. You can then right click unlock and move it to the bottom left if you wish.

### Post by Simian Man on 2009-03-24
Go to System->Preferences->Personal->Keyboard Shortcuts.

Under Desktop, there is an entry "Show Main Menu".  Click it and then click your Windows Key.  It should show up as "Super L" or something.

Hope that helps.
