### Post by tea for one on 2021-08-02
According to your specification link, your HP 2133 notebook only has 512MB RAM.

You will need to find a suitable lightweight distro (possibly 32-bit) for this device such as [https://antixlinux.com/](https://antixlinux.com/)

Ubuntu 20.04 will not be suitable for such minimal hardware.
