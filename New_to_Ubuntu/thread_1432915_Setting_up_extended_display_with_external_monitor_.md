### Post by nagarajan_s on 2010-03-18
I could not get my external monitor to work in an extended desktop/display mode with my netbook (Asus 1005 HA) for quite a long time.

I went distribution after distribution (Ubuntu, Mint, Fedora, Mandriva) - but could not get it working in any of them. After a lot of reading on this subject I figured it was something to do with Compiz and the virtual resolution.

Finally all I had to do to get it working was to have the extended display in "top/down configuration" instead of "side-by-side" configuration. A very simple thing actually - the idea behind is simple as well. 

Written a post about it : [http://www.geekytalk.snaga.net/2010/03/compiz-and-virtual-resolution-handling.html](http://www.geekytalk.snaga.net/2010/03/compiz-and-virtual-resolution-handling.html)

Hope this helps anybody, who wants to have extended display with their netbooks.
