### Post by Allavona on 2011-05-21
Reboot, then when the grub menu appears, use your arrow keys to move down to the recovery option.

If you miss this you can enter it from the GDM menu at login.

From the 10.10 menu click on your name, but do not enter your password. On the bottom of your screen, click on the menu that gives you your desktop options, recovery console should be one of them.

Why do you need to recover, if I may ask?
