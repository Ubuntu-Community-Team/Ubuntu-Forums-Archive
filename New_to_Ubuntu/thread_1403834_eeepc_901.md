### Post by sgosnell on 2010-02-10
The 901 and the 900HD are not the same thing, FWIW.

9.10 was very problematic on my 900, and I removed it.  9.04 works perfectly.  Your parents might like the netbook remix, but I don't.  It's way, way too cluttered on a small screen.  I prefer the standard Gnome desktop, but either should work.  In any case, I advise Jaunty, 9.04, **not** 9.10.  There will be issues with 9.10 on a 900.
