### Post by -kg- on 2011-08-13
I'm not sure what you're trying to block, or for whom, but Blocksite isn't the only site blocking add-on out there (that, and I couldn't bring their site up, anyway...that and they aren't listed in the add-on search I did)

You might try [LeechBlock]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/leechblock/?src=api") (password protected) or, if you're looking to block porn, [CensureBlock]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/censureblock/?src=api") (though it has a purported complete lack of control...no word of a password on the page).

I did finally find BlockSite, and you're right...there is a complaint that the add-on can easily be disabled or uninstalled without a password.  That sure defeats the purpose!

You probably already know of the add-ons I mentioned.  AFAIK, there's no way to control that, other than it being built in to the add-on.  You might try asking in the Firefox forums.  You'd be more likely to find an answer to your question, if there is one.
