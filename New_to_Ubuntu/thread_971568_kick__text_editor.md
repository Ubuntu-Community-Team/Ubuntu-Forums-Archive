### Post by Jeff Seager on 2008-11-05
Have a look at eMacs, Bluefish, Quanta Plus and/or Vex. One of those should satisfy your needs for a code editor, though you may need to tweak settings or change options to restore sessions, etc.

The learning curve for eMacs is a bit steep, IMHO, but its capabilities are amazing and the documentation is extensive and excellent. The large community of eMacs users is helpful, too.

Good luck!
Jeff
