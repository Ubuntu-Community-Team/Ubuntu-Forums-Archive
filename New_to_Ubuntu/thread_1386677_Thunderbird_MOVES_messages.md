### Post by iMisspell on 2010-01-21
1)Its an option (see (3))
2)Not that i know of, but that don't mean anything ;)
3)While in Thunderbird, right click on the email account in question and select 'Properties...'
Now click on 'Server Settings'
Add a check mark to 'Leave Message On Server' along with desired sub-options.
