### Post by corncob on 2010-07-06
Often, when you press the [ESC] key after you turn the computer on, you get a menu where you can select the boot device.  You'll want the CD drive in this case.  If that doesn't work you may be able to go into SETUP.  While the computer is booting, it might tell you what to press to enter SETUP (often [DEL] or [F10])  While in SETUP go to the boot menu and change the first boot device to the CD drive.
