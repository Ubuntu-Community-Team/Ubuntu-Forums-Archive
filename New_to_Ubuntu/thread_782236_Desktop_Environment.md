### Post by tyroeternal on 2008-05-04
There is a difference in that there are a lot more packages than just the gnome desktop that are installed with the official ubuntu-desktop.  You can install, as the two earlier posts indicated, the full ubuntu-desktop package, but essentially you are dealing with a very close version to what you're going to get by doing that.
