### Post by Jon Monreal on 2010-04-29
It's not available from the repos yet. You can add the svn by going to System>Administration>Software Sources, going to the "Other Software" tab, clicking "Add", typing in
```
ppa:team-xbmc-svn/ppa
```but be aware that svn versions may not be stable.

The other option would be to wait for Lucid packages to be added to the repos. It probably won't take too long.
