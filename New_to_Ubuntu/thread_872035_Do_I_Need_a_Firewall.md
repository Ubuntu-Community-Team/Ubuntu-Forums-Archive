### Post by halitech on 2008-07-27
anti-virus - yes and no. No for your own protection but yes if you share files or email attachments to windows users if you want to make sure you don't inadvertantly send something out.

firewall - yes and no. 1 is installed by default however if you are behind a router, typically not needed
