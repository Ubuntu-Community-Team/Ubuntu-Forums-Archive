### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2008-12-18
You should probably go into your control center and check your volume control to see how things are set.

You can access the control center by going to 

System-> Control Center (if it's not available right click on your menus and click edit menu then under system make sure the box is checked, also while your there you may want to make sure that the volume is checked under preferences)

Once you have the volume control open, you'll want to open preferences and make sure everything is checked. Then you can go through the settings and try to set things properly as well as checking to see if it's muted.
