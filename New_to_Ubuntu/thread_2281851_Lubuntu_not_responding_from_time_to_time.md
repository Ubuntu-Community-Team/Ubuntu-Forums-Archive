### Post by kerry_s on 2015-06-10
it's the flash drive. there read speeds are just not that fast.

flash drive life is tricky to calculate, depends on a lot of things. quality, age, etc...

i ran a linux on a flashdrive for like 2 years without issues. of course eventually 1 day it will just quit.
i've had good luck with sd card, even used a couple in a sata adapter as my hard drive. they were 32gb 90mb/s cards running as raid, felt just like a cheap ssd drive.
