I don't have any idea if it ships with Disk Usage Analyzer, sorry.Chris, right-click on either the top or bottom panel (the bar that holds icons) and select "Add New Item" from the resulting menu. You'll get a list of things that can be added to the panel. Free Space Checker is one, and Mount Devices is another, that will let you check your free disk space by clicking on them. It's not taking up any resources (except for the disk space it occupies, and at this point you have plenty of that) unless you run it, and you may find you need it for some other game or Windows program in the future.
