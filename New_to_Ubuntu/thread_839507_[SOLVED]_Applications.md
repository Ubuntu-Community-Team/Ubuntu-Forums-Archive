### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-06-24
Right-Click on your Desktop and select "**Change Desktop Background**".
Click **Add** and go into the location
```
**/usr/share/backgrounds**
```
in the file browser.

That be the folder where they are kept.

### Post by mcduck on 2008-06-24
as many ways as you might want :D

From the Appearances-windows background tab you can use the Add-button and browse to your images.

Or you can just drag&drop them into the list of available backgrounds.

Or if you open the picture, the default image viewing program has the option to use the picture as wallpaper (it's somewhere in the menu)..

edit: If you have just installed them you could also try logging out & back again, they might appear in the background dialog automatically..
