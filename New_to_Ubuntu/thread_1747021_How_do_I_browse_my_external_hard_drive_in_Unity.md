### Post by lifelike27 on 2011-05-02
The 'Home Folder' is nautilus. The options you're referring to (i.e file, edit etc) are located in the menu bar at the top. However, they only appear when nautilus is the active application (the program you've currently selected) and when you move your cursor to the menu bar at the top.

It's apart of the unity experience. It's actually an OS X feature used in Ubuntu. 

There's a nice guide to the new features of Unity [here]("http://omgubuntu.co.uk/natty/").
