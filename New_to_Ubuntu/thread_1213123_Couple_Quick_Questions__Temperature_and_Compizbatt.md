### Post by Fatal Toenail Infection on 2009-07-14
There are several options for monitoring temperature, but a good one is xsensors.  As far as compiz goes, it depends really a lot on your graphics card/drivers and how they scale the GPU when you are running on battery power.  But in my experience, running compiz has not made a drastic difference in battery life.  Maybe 10-20 minutes less than normal battery life.  But my battery life has never been great on Ubuntu anyways.
