### Post by silkstone on 2008-08-21
I doubt if you can, unless the printer is supported by Turboprint which is a commercial third-party application.

Unfortunately, Canon's support for Linux is very patchy. I have an MP610 all-in-one for which Canon do produce both printer and scanner drivers, but my larger photo printer - an i9950 - will not work under Linux.

You can't really blame Linux or Ubuntu, since they can't force manufacturers to produce drivers. In general, HP offers the best support, but none of this will be any consolation to you.
