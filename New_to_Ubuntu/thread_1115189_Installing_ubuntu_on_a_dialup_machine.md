### Post by ranch hand on 2009-04-07
I have been on dial up always.  I bought my CD from linuxCD.org.  They are very fast and give great service.

When it comes to updates it is best to pick just the security updates and then uncheck some of them to make each bunch of downloads a managable size.

If you are using a winmodem I highly reccommend that you get a real modem.  An external is appearently easier to configure.  I put in an internal USR5610c and it is from 20 to 35% faster than the winmodem that came on this box.  Those speeds are from when there was Vista on here.  I think it is actually a little faster under Ubuntu than it was under Vista.

Even so, about the best download speed I can get 54 miles from town is 4.4Kb/sec.  A kernal update takes hours, I just fire it up and let it run while I sleep.
