### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-05-15
Do you get a Grub boot menu? If so, then at the Grub menu select Advanced options for Ubuntu (it is a sub-menu) and select Recovery Mode. At the Recovery mode screen select Resume. That may get you to a working Ubuntu Desktop. From there go to System Settings>Software & Updates>Additional Drivers tab and change the video driver. You may need to experiment to find a driver that works for your hardware.
