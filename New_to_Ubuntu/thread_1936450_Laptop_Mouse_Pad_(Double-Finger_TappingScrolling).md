### Post by S2UIRR3L on 2012-03-15
Got it!
I've messed around with the mouse for so long that I found where the "spots" are on the pad. I've tried downloading a bunch of programs and nothing had any settings to tweak. So I un-installed them and found out that all I needed was already here. I just had to mess with it long enough to find everything by accident. These are the commands that I found (the ones that I was looking for). As for anything else, I don't know and really don't care to know because below is all I need/use. However, if I find anything else, I'll add it to this thread. This one is SOLVED!

**LEFT-CLICK** = one finger tap, anywhere on the pad.

**RIGHT-CLICK** = one finger tap, on the bottom-right.

**VERTICAL-SCROLL** = one finger sliding up and down on the right side, unless tweaked for two fingers sliding up and down anywhere on the pad.

**HORIZONTAL-SCROLL** = one finger sliding left and right on the bottom side, unless tweaked for two fingers sliding left and right anywhere on pad.

**CENTER WHEEL CLICK** = one finger tap on the very top right corner of the pad where the top of the vertical scroll is, opens links in new tap (with Firefox).
