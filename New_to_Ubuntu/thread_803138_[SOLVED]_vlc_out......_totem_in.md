### Post by AndyCooll on 2008-05-23
> **steve allen said:**
> I think I read on the forum that you shouldn't keep on changing your default player will it cause a problem if I change default and will totem stop working (shared file probs ) if I remove vlc.
That's a myth. You can change your media player as often as you want. Indeed some people seem to change their player almost daily ...that's the beauty of Linux, choice.

What they may have been referring to in terms of breakages is the method you use for installing and uninstalling apps. It's always best to install using the official tools (e.g. Synaptic) and through the repos. This way all files, dependencies and links are registered with the database properly and hence shared files won't be accidentally uninstalled if it is still required by another app.
