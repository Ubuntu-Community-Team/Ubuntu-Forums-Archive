### Post by mansonfan78 on 2015-01-27
I've had an 8400 gs, which made a HUGE improvement after updating (especially with hd video) and a 6800 gt which made no difference at all (although it worked perfectly from the beginning, so it really didn't need it).  Basically, if it ain't broke, don't fix it.  Video drivers in linux are finicky.  But if you are going to try it, I highly recommend backing up your hard drive (with Clonezilla or something similar) before you even consider updating the driver.
