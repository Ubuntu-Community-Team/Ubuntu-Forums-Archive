### Post by Terry of Astoria on 2009-12-21
The point is so that if your computer or drive is stolen, no-one can steal your identity/files without your passphrase.

### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2009-12-21
This is also useful for situations where multiple local users will share the same system. Brothers and sisters can have their own files that are inaccessible by other users, same for roommates and the like as long as each user has their own login.
