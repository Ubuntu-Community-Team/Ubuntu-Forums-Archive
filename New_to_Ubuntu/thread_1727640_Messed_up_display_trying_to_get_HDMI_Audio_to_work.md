### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-13
I think you need to begin by regenerating your xorg.conf file.

```
sudo nvidia-xconfig
```

You can read about what and how it does:

```
man nvidia-xconfig
```

We can get your audio going, too.  I have mine working (nVidia card, HDMI, Samsung LED LCD as monitor)

Once you have a working /etc/X11/xorg.conf file, make a copy of it before you play with the audio settings again.
