They haven't updated the lists to the newest version of Ubuntu, but the list of the DVD contents for 8.04 Hardy can be [found here]("http://mirror.mcs.anl.gov/pub/ubuntu-iso/DVDs/ubuntu/hardy/release/ubuntu-8.04.1-dvd-i386.list").
