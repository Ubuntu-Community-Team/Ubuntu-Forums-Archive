### Post by waynefoutz on 2011-07-03
I have yet to find a better solution than OpenDNS. [http://www.opendns.com/](http://www.opendns.com/)

This works on any platform. You basically set up a free account, put their DNS numbers into your kid's computer's network settings, or your router if they are smart enough to remove them. All the filtering is done at their end. And you can use this on any platform, Linux, Windows, or OSX. It works really well, and you can customize the filters to your heart's content on the Opendns website.

I tried dansgaurdian, it's been a while since it's been updated, and it's nowhere near as simple to set up.
