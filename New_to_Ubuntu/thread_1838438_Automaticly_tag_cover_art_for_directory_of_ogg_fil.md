### Post by AlphaLexman on 2011-09-03
Most, but NOT all music players on a linux distro will recognize a file named '**cover.jpg**' in the artist/album directory and assume it is the album cover image.

This may be easier than trying to embed the exact same image inside every mp3/ogg file of a whole album.

Good Luck!
