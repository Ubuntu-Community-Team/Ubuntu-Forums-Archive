### Post by wolfen69 on 2011-04-13
[Here]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/maverick/b43-fwcutter") is a link to the files you need. Download the libc6 and the i386 file. Install the libc6 file first. These will install simply by clicking on them. Of course you will need to get to a net enabled computer first.

### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-04-13
You could also take a look at the STA drivers from Broadcom, I've generally had better luck with them for supported devices. The STA drivers are available for download [here]("http://www.broadcom.com/support/802.11/linux_sta.php"), maybe download them on your phone, then plug your phone into your computer and copy the file over to install?
