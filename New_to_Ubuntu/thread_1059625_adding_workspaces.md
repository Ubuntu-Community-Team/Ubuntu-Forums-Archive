### Post by EdThaSlayer on 2009-02-04
When it comes to adding workspaces it's pretty simple.
Just right-click on the 2 workspaces you should have by default and then click on "Configure Desktops" and you can choose the number of workspaces you want from there on.

Fallout 1 and 2 could most probably work as long as they are at least 3 years old or so.
Should ask in the [Gaming&Leisure]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=93") section if you want more information concerning that issue.
