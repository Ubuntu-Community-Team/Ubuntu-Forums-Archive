### Post by Dude-PWB- on 2010-11-02
When you open update manager you need to click the settings button on the bottom left corner and click the dropdown box and change it from Long Term Support releases to New releases.

### Post by germanix on 2010-11-03
You are in the right place. Where it says "release upgrade" . "long term releases only", click on the arrow at the right and a drop down menu will open. Here you select "new releases"
Then go back to the up-date manager and and you will then see the option to upgrade.
