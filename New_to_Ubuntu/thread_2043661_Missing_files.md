### Post by Wim Sturkenboom on 2012-08-17
The trash?

Next step is to restore the backup that you obviously have.

If you did not make a backup, you need tools like photorec that can scan the disk for photos and recover them. To increase your chances on recovery, absolutely limit the amount of writes to the HD (preferably don't use the laptop) and don't let the recovery tool write to the same HD but to an other one.

Some distributions have it on the live CD; check [http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk_Livecd](http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk_Livecd). Use another computer to create it!

And again, let the photos be restored to another disk, not the HD of the laptop! Buy one if you have to!!
