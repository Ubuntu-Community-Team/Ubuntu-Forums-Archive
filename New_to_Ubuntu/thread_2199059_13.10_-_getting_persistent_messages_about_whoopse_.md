### Post by ian-weisser on 2014-01-12
Looks to me like whoopsie is itself crashing and respawning. And that's strange - respawing is supposed to be limited by /etc/init/whoopsie.conf

Also looks to me like a bug: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/whoopsie/+bug/1217407](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/whoopsie/+bug/1217407)

If you're not having a lot of crashes to report, I suppose a workaround is to disable whoopsie.
