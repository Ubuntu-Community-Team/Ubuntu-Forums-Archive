### Post by jmszr on 2009-03-03
Edward B.,

          Click Applications > Add/Remove > Show: All Available Applications > Search: Battle Tanks > Check box next to Battle Tanks > Apply Changes. That should do it.

### Post by hansdown on 2009-03-03
Hi Edward B.

Click system> administration> synaptic package manager.

Enter your password.

Search for btanks in synaptic.

Check the box for btanks.

Click mark for installation.

Click mark all upgrades.
 
Click install.

Click install.

After this click applications> games. Click on btanks and have fun!
