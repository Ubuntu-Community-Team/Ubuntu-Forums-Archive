### Post by nhasian on 2009-07-05
go to System->Preferences->Keyboard, then click on the *Layout* tab.  Select your layout and then click on *Layout Options*.

Now open the triangle labeled *Key(s) to change layout* and choose whatever key press you would like to toggle your keyboard layouts.  

Also I recommend using the option labeled *Use keyboard LED to show alternative layout*  so for example your CapsLock LED light on your keyboard could be a visual cue to which layout your in.
