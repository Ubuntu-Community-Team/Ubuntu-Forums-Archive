### Post by sydbat on 2009-03-29
Is it the whole screen (desktop) or just the window you have open? 

If just the window, it is because the application has encountered something (perhaps insufficient RAM to run at full speed) or an error of some type. It should resolve itself after a short time. If not, there is an app that can force it to quit. I have this in one of my panels so I can click on it, then click on the misbehaving app to close it. Right click a panel > Add to Panel > Force Quit.

If it is the entire desktop, I am not sure what could be causing it. Someone with more experience would be able to answer that.
