### Post by fatality_uk on 2010-09-15
OK. Right click the icons and remove from panel.
Now, righ tclick the panel and select **Add to Panel...**
At the top, you should see, "Application Launcher..."
Select that and click **forward**
All of your menus are now there
Select the item you want and click add.

That will restore an icon to the top panel.

When that's done, right click on the icon and select **Lock to panel** to ensure it stays there :)
