### Post by Paqman on 2008-09-01
> **paker said:**
> Should I buy a sound card first?

Depends. You sound quality is only going to be as good as your weakest link. If you're playing a low-bitrate mp3 then onboard sound is fine. But if you're playing flac on good speakers, then a soundcard will help out massively.

> 
A related question. Do most DVD players (for home stereo) accept mp3 or flac data dvd's? Thanks.

Generally mp3, yes. I don't think you'd have any luck with flac though. A lot of DVDs are also very picky about CD-R/RWs in general.
