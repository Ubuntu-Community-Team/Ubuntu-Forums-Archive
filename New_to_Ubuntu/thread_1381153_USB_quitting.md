### Post by corncob on 2010-01-14

I was having trouble with a wireless USB adapter going to sleep.  (I solved that problem by buying a PCI one).  At the time I was told that the ports in the back of the computer have more power than the ones in the front -- maybe this is true in some cases.  Try using a powered USB hub.
