### Post by laidback on 2008-12-06
Welcome to Ubuntu
My suggestion is this...run Synaptic, goto Settings>Repositories then Ubuntu Software tab and check that a) you have the ones you want listed and that b) they are ticked as active. Also check the Third Party Software tab. If any are missing in the Third Party tab you can add them by pressing the add button at the bottom of the screen.
You can also alter the repository that you download from, Ubuntu Sofware tab again then select from.. Download from.. dropdown in the lower part of the screen.

Hope this helps.

I always use Synaptic and have been very successful. Others use the Command Line, that's getting straight to the heart of the matter, we all have our preferences.
