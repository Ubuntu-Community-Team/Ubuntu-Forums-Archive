### Post by lazyart on 2008-06-05
Check by your clock-- you should have an icon from the update manager.  Click on it and you'll get the updates window.  At the top of that window should be a notice that a new release is available.  Tell it to upgrade and it should put you at 7.10.

If there is no icon, go to your menu under system->administration and find update manager.

### Post by Paqman on 2008-06-06
> **fmpfmpf said:**
> is it possible for me to use the live cd to upgrade?

Nope.

You've got several options:

1) Open up Update Manager and hit "Check", it should announce that a new version is available at the top. This will download all the updates to bring you up to 7.10
2) Get hold of the 7.10 Alternate CD and put it in the drive. A message will show up saying that the CD contains packages you can use to do an upgrade.
3) Get the 7.10 LiveCD and do a reinstall over the top of your 7.04.

Whatever you do, back up your data first.
