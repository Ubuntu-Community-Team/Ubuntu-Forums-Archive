### Post by -kg- on 2009-01-22
Your #2 problem:

You need to go into Compiz Settings Manager (if you don't have it installed, it can be installed from Synaptics).  Click System/Preferences/Advanced Desktop Effects Settings. Or you can launch it from the terminal by typing in:

```
ccsm
```

Click on Utilities/Workarounds, and unclick "Legacy Full Screen Support."  That will solve the problem of Firefox taking the whole screen permanently.
