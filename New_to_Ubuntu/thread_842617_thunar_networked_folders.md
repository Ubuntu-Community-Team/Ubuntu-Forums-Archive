### Post by Hospadar on 2008-06-27
try network:///

The thing is, your network files are probably managed by gnome, so if you had originally mounted something through nautilus (or through the "places" menu) It may not show up in thunar.  You may want to look into tools like smbfs or sshfs that allow you to mount network locations in a generic, system-wide way
