### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-05-14
Well:

video editing: this is a field that linux has not covered much in, but you can try kdenlive, it works simular to windows movie maker.
also try kino too.

screen capture: you mean like screen shots or recording videos of your desktop?

webcam drivers: this is a little harder, webcams are give or take in linux.

audio recording/audio editing: audacity can probably solve this for you.

graphic design: well other then gimp, there is inkscape, and there are certain versions of photoshop that work in WINE

animation for flash: tricky, but I do know that its possible to get some versions of flash studio working under wine
