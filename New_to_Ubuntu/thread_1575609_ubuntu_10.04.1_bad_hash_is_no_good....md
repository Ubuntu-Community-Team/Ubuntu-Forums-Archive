A few thoughts. If the md5sum fails to match on a torrented download, then the md5sum hash you are using is the wrong one. As others have said, a torrented download is good. Where did you get the md5sum? Post a link. Are you sure you're not using the 10.04 md5 hash? The 10.04.1 ISO will be different.

Also - when you say md5 hashes not matching, I presume you mean you've done 'md5sum -c' from the command line. However, if you mean that the CD is failing its integrity check then perhaps your CDs are bad. Your machine is 9 years old. The optical drive may be wearing out.
