### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-03-28
If you just copy a URL from the URL bar it will not download the video in the webpage. To download a video you must find the URL of the video which most likely will end in .flv or .swf

One way to find the URL of a video might be by right clicking on it, although this will not work on all videos.

Hope it helps.
