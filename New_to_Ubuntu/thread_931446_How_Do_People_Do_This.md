### Post by Genius314 on 2008-09-27
You don't have to install anything, actually. (You can install CCSM if you want, but that's just to change specific settings.)

Go to System>Preferences>Appearance, click on the "Visual Effects" tab, and select either normal or extra.
Now you can use the cube by pressing Ctrl-Alt-Left or -Right.
Or you can zoom out and manually rotate the cube with Ctrl-Alt-Left Click.
