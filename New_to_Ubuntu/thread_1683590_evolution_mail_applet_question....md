### Post by Luinar on 2011-02-09
In Evolution preferences, under Mail Preferences, there is a dropdown setting "When new mail arrives in...". Change the setting from "any inbox" to "any folder" and make sure the checkboxes for "display notification" and "indicate new messages in the panel" are ticked.

See if that works?
