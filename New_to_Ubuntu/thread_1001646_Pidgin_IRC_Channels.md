### Post by billgoldberg on 2008-12-04
[http://linuxowns.wordpress.com/2008/04/23/ubuntu-irc-channel/](http://linuxowns.wordpress.com/2008/04/23/ubuntu-irc-channel/)

That's the easiest guide you'll find.

Basically, when setting up your irc account, add as server irc.freenode.net

Then open it, in the window that will pop up, just press

/join #room

For the Ubuntu irc room that will be

/join #ubuntu
