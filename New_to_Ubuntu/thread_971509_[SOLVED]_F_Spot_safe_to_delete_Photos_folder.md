### Post by Sealbhach on 2008-11-05
Hi, I just had a look at the F-Spot user guide here:

[http://f-spot.org/User_Guide/Organize#Import](http://f-spot.org/User_Guide/Organize#Import)

> By default, F-Spot will make a copy of photos imported from your hard drive. Uncheck the "Copy" option on the import dialog or hold "Shift" when dragging photos into F-Spot if you do not wish to copy them from your hard drive. 

This seems to suggest that it makes a copy, rather than move the pictures.

Just to be on the safe side I suggest you back up anything you don't want to lose onto a CD or a USB drive.
