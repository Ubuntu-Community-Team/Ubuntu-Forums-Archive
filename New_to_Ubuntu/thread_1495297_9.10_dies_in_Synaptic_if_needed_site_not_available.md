### Post by cariboo on 2010-05-28
I've been using synaptic for about 10 years, and I've never seen that happen, perhaps you should try a different server.

> What if I decide I do not want to complete the download or I suspect it may not be legitimate?
How do I kill it?

If the cancel button doesn't work, press Alt-F2 and type:

```
xkill
```

The move the **x** over the window you want to kill and click.
