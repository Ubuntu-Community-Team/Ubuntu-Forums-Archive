### Post by DoodleTech on 2012-11-25
Hello Dave. May I ask why are you using an old version of Evolution? I would upgrade to 3.6.


On that note, open up evolution and click on Edit and then click on Preference. Once you click on preference, click on mail accounts. Once you are in mail accounts click on your mail account that is in the selection area and then click the edit button. If there are no accounts, click on the add button and add an account.
