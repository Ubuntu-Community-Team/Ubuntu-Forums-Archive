### Post by lechien73 on 2012-02-05
The files are located in: /usr/share/sounds/ubuntu/stereo

The "jungle" login sound is desktop-login.ogg, and the drums sound is "system-ready.ogg".

The file needs to be in ogg format, and it's the same for 32-bit or 64-bit systems :)
