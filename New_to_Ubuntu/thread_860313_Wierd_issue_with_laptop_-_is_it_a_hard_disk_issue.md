### Post by OffAxis on 2008-07-15
memtest runs the RAM through it's paces, but not the hard drive. 
you could test the hard disk with a hard drive utility (downloadable from the drive manufacturer's website).
Odds are they've got a bootable CD that'll let you check the drive as well as copy files off of it (without booting to a full blown operating system).
