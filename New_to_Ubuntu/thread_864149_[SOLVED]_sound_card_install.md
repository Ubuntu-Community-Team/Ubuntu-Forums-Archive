### Post by Malta paul on 2008-07-19
I would just install your sound card, Then when you boot-up go into your BIOS and disable any mother-board sound settings to ensure your new card doesn't have any conflict.
Then boot into Ubuntu and your card should be detected.
You will then have to choose a media player to play your CD or music.

Enjoy:)
