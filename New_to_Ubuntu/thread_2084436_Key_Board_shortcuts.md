### Post by deadflowr on 2012-11-15
The default layout to open your trash is super+T.(the windows button)

If you hold down the super key the default keyboard shortcuts will appear on the screen.

Review your keyboards layout at system settings> keyboard layout. Click Name(English or other name) and then click the ungrayed keyboard icon below that.
