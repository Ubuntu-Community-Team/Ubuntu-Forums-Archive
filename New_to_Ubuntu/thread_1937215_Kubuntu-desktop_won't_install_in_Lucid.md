### Post by Cheesemill on 2012-03-07
It's the log on screen where you click on your name and then enter your password.

There is a menu that you use to select your session, it's labelled either session or with an image of a cog (I can't remember which and I'm not on my Ubuntu machine at the moment).

Of course if your machine is set up to automatically log you on you won't see this screen, you'll have to disable auto log on first.
