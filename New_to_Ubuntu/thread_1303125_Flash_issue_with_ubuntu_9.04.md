### Post by garvinrick4 on 2009-10-27
Uninstall flash    ( Most do not  say you have to but I like to, only takes a second)

sudo app-get clean.         (In Terminal.)


reinstall from synaptic.


The clean just cleans out your cache of Apps. 
So you can fetch new one's just incase the one you have in your cache is defective.
