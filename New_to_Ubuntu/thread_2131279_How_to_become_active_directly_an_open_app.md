### Post by Impavidus on 2013-04-01
If I don't need to view them at the same time I put windows in different workspaces and use ctrl+alt+arrow or the mouse (if I already have my hand on it) to move around. I've 15 workspaces in a 3x5 grid. That's at most 3 key presses and I don't have to remember the IDs of the workspaces, and I've got only 12 F keys.

To move to a different window on the same workspace I hover with my mouse, click on the buttons on my panel or use alt+tab, whichever is more convenient at the moment.
