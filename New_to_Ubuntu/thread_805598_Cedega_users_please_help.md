> 13) How do I purchase a Cedega subscription?
Cedega is available to end-users through TransGaming's subscription service, [http://www.cedega.com/subscription/subscribe.html](http://www.cedega.com/subscription/subscribe.html). For a monthly fee of only (with a discount if a 12 month subscription is purchased in advance) subscribers receive ongoing access to Cedega updates, releases and patches, have access to TransGaming's discussions forums, and can influence TransGaming's development activities through a unique voting ability. Subscribers who maintain an uninterrupted subscription for a period of 18 months become "Emerald TransGamers" where they receive increased voting power, access to some releases before anyone else, and many other benefits. Cedega can also be licensed for commercial use. Please contact us at [email]sales@transgaming.com[/email] to discuss your specific commercial needs.
