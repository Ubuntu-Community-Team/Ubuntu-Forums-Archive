### Post by bnjmnwroberts on 2010-11-22
I just installed 10.10 desktop on my m-250 and it's quick and flawless. Only trouble was the initial installation had to be done while online so I could update my wireless driver. The system does not have a b43 compatabile driver, so what you have to do is install the sta driver instead, and then restart it. Then you can go online wirelessly and go to ubuntu software( in your applications)and click installed software, and in the top right corner, search b43. Then click to uninstall all of b43 drivers and software. ( do this only after you have installed the sta driver)Now your system should be quick and easy to use, and with no failed application signs.
