### Post by 73ckn797 on 2009-03-08
Try this entry in Applications/Accessories/Terminal

```
 ./compiz-check
```You may be able to disable compiz and return to the Metacity, default, manager. There are several questions to answer there. I do not have compiz in Ubuntu 8.10 Intrepid 64 bit where I am currently running from but it may help.

You also may be able to disable desktop effects via System/Preferences/Appearance/Visual Effects.
