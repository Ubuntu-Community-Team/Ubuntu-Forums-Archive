### Post by Ms. Daisy on 2011-12-14
If the sound is part of the video, there's a little speaker icon on the "tracks" at the bottom. You can click it to turn the sound on or off for each track.  It's a nice feature so you can layer images and not have layered sounds if you don't want.  

If sound is separate, make sure you imported the sound file into the audio tab.

I found this [manual ]("http://www.openshotusers.com/help/1.3/en/")to be very helpful.  A lot of open shot is intuitive, but some of it is a bit more complicated than that.
