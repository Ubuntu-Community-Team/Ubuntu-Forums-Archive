### Post by Feathers McGraw on 2013-04-11
**+1 for LaTeX** if you ever have to submit large reports.

It's easy to learn using the [LaTeX Wikibook]("http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/LaTeX") and makes your documents look a lot more professional.

Using a WYSIWYG office application will slow you right down and use loads of power (I used to hate the way my laptop would hang when I scrolled over any special fields like a table of contents or bibliography), but LaTeX documents are edited using a text editor, nothing more. Nice and lightweight!

You'll probably save yourself some time in any situation where you might have to produce a TOC, references list, or a list of figures & tables too.

Feathers
