### Post by eolson on 2009-05-13
Have not experienced that particular problem,  but to give you something to play with until somebody who has comes along.   Try right clicking on the speaker icon and mess with the settings,  change one, if it doesn't work, change it back then try another.   If all that fails try double left,  clicking the icon and do the same with the settings there.   Just one at a time, and if it doesn't work, change it back before trying another one.
