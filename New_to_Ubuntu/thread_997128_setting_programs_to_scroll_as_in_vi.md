### Post by Eddie.bear on 2008-11-29
Try [vimperator/muttator]("http://vimperator.org") to make Firefox/Thunderbird work with vi(m)-like keybindings. I suspect you could change the keymap to have h,j,k,l mapped to left,down.up,right but it does leave the issue of how you would then type 'h','j','k' or 'l'.
