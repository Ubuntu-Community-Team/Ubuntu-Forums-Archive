### Post by mutesounds on 2012-06-05
I am running Kubuntu 12.04 64-bit, with KDE 4.8.3, with all updates applied.

I have my screensaver set to blank screen at 45 minutes, and everything unchecked in "Energy Saving Settings".

When I leave everything alone, mouse, keyboard, etc., the screen will turn blank, then the monitor turns off, all in about 30-45 seconds.  What can I do to stop this from happening?  This does not happen in any other distro I have tried.  Thanks!
