### Post by philinux on 2009-12-17
I just have /home on it's own partition. This way I can reinstall in half an hour, I just leave home as not formatted during the regular install. e.g. when new release comes out or if I've been tweaking a tad to much. ;)

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome)

It's much easier to do a clean install and create a separate home partition but the method above works well too.
