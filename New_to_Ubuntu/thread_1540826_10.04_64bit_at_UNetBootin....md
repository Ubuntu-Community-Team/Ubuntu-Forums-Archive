### Post by snowpine on 2010-07-28
32-bit will work on any computer (32- or 64-bit).

64-bit only works on 64-bit comptuers.

So they are trying to prevent noobs from accidentally downloading 64-bit.

If you are smart enough to know you have a 64-bit computer and that 64-bit Ubuntu is right for your needs, you may safely ignore the warning. ;)
