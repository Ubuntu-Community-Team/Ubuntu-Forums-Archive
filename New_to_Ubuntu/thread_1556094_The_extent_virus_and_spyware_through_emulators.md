### Post by Windows Nerd on 2010-08-19
Unless you ran WINE with root privileges (using sudo) the Linux security model would just not let the virus do damage outside of WINE. Even if you did launch it with root privileges, I doubt the theoretical virus would be able to do much (WINE has a hard enough time running regular apps very well :O)
