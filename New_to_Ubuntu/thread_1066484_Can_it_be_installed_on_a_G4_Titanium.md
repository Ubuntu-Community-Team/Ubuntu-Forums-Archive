> **SunnyRabbiera said:**
> Yeh PPC has been unsupported for a while.
You may wish to invest in a PPC friendly distro like debian
Sunny, with respect; true, there is no official support for Ubuntu ppc versions past 6.10 (IIRC), and the downloads are not available on the official mirrors. However, I'm using 8.10 on G4 Cubes, the iso obtained from the site mentioned in the sticky referenced. There is good community support given at the forum I referenced. Thus, there is support, just not official support from Canonical.

To the OP: give it a go. If you wish to consider other distro, Debian is "officially" supported; openSUSE is "officially" supported; there is also Yellow Dog Linux, specifically for ppc machines, which are other options available. For me, Ubuntu works on my Cubes, and is worth a try.
