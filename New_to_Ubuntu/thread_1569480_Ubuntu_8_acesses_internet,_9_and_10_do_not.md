### Post by garvinrick4 on 2010-09-07
If /etc/resolv.conf is empty then you are not getting anything with your internet connection. First right click on icon in upper right that is your connection applet.
Make sure the box's are checked to enable. Now left click on the applet and choose
your wireless router if on wifi. Type in password for router and that is normal procedure.
When you right click on applet you can also choose connection information. Should have
your info in there if connected. The package is called network manager that you are using.
I will give you a pdf file link of a great little book all about Ubuntu and its file system.
Download it and go through it as time permits. Previous link was all the installations for various versions of Linux.
[http://www.ubuntupocketguide.com/index_main.html](http://www.ubuntupocketguide.com/index_main.html)
