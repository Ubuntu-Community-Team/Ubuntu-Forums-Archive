### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-08-11
Gyachi is supposed to support Yahoo Messenger with webcam, but it's unmaintained and it didn't work for me... although I think it might have been because I was trying to use a 32-bit binary on my 64-bit machine, rather than compiling it from source.

If your webcam works with Flash Player on Linux, then your best bet is to use the Meebo instant messenger ([www.meebo.com](www.meebo.com)). When you initiate video chat with another Meebo user they will automatically have a video window pop up - when you initiate video chat with a non-Meebo user they will be sent a link to follow in their web browser to join the chat.
