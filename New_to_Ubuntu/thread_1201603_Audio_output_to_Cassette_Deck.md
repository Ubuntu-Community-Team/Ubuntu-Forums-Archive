### Post by jmfal on 2009-07-01
re:mistypotato
There are no line outs on onboard sound cards, the speaker jack is a line out.
You need a patch cord with the same plug as your pc speakers on one end and 2 rca plugs on the other end that your cassette deck has. you are probably only getting sound out of one jack on your pc because it is set up for 2channels>stereo< thats ok . I don`t know where you live but if you are in U.S radioshack,walmart,or your localcomputer store should have one. when you start recording your volume control on your pc will be your line level adjustment you will have to play around with that if your cassette plays back at alow volume or is real loud and distorted.I`m not sure about the quality of the sound doing it this way, but it is easyist and cheapest.
BTW the jack that goes into your pc is called a mini-stereo,3.5mm
hope this helps out
