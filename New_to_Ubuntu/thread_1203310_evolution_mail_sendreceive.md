*Gone?*  Do you mean greyed-out or completely vanished?  Cos I know the button is disabled if evolution is set offline.  But vanished?  Hem.

**EDIT:**  Oh yeah.  Under **View > Layout** ensure that **Show Toolbar** is ticked.  If it's unticked, the toolbar (including Send/Receive) will not be visible.
