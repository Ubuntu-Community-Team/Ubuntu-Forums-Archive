### Post by NightwishFan on 2010-09-16
Try waiting a moment for the launcher to spawn. Click and hold until an icon appears below your cursor. Dragging and dropping works here. Perhaps it is a netbook issue. Are you using the standard menu?

### Post by rayliam on 2010-09-16
I waited for almost a minute for the launcher to spawn on the dock but it never happened. I used cairo-dock in Ubuntu 9.04 and never had a problem dragging and dropping from the applications menu to the dock. Strange that I can't get it to do what I need it to do.

However, after digging around on the forums a little bit more, I figured out that I can drag and drop the applications from usr/share/applications instead of gnome panel - applications. It doesn't exactly solve the problem but it's better than nothing. I'm able to customize my dock the way I want now. Thanks for the reply.
