### Post by newb85 on 2012-09-09
Your screenshot is missing, but your description gives me a good enough idea of what's going on.

Instead of simply clicking on the link for the .torrent file, right-click and select "Save Link As..." and save the .torrent file to your Downloads folder.

Open your downloads folder and find the .torrent file.  Right-click it and select "Properties".  On the "Open With" tab, select Transmission.
