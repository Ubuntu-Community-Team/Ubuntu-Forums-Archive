### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-06-24
The current installation disk of Ubuntu 8.04 will not be updated.
All sequential updates are to be done through the online repositories that Ubuntu (and other non-Offical outlets) offer.

But, for your information. Ubuntu 8.04.1 is in the works.
I suspect an updated ISO download will arrive sometime around this fall. Possibly before the release of Intrepid, but I wouldn't hold my breath or get excited about it.

If you're system is up-to-date, there will be nothing new in it (In 8.04.1 that is).
Intrepid, on the other hand promises some very exciting bleeding edge technologies. The 2.6.26-2 kernel being one of them...
