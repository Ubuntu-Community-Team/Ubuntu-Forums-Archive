### Post by _0R10N on 2011-06-28
From my point of view there's two choices.

a- unplugging prematurely your camera corrupted the file system in its memory.
b- something in your netbook is in short and killed the flash memory.

Possible solutions:

for a: try some tool for managing partitions, and that kind of stuff.

for b: there's nothing left to do to bring the flash memory back to life. Give it a proper burial.

Something to consider: turn on your camera and try to take a picture or explore pics previously taken. If you success, then there's hope!

Question: did you try plugging your camera in another computer?

Kind regards!
