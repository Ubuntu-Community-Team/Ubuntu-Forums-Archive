### Post by nhasian on 2010-05-30
to drag windows to other workspaces launch compizconfig settings manager and go to Desktop-> Desktop Wall Plugin-> Edge Flipping and tick the box for "Edge Flip Move"

---

### Post by wojox on 2010-05-30
Open ccsm and go to Desktop-> Desktop Wall Plugin-> Edge Flipping and tick the box for "Edge Flip Move"
