### Post by jbrown96 on 2008-06-11
The network is probably protected. How do you login to the Windows machine? Is it through a domain or locally? If it is a domain, then it is probably like my office and no computers can get on the network without being authenticated by the domain. There are some guides for setting up Active Directory on Ubuntu; you might want to check them out.
