### Post by Amarsingh0793 on 2008-10-28
That's probably because the installation failed half - way and so it has overwritten your previous installation (not completely though). I recommend you order a free CD via Shipit, or burn another disc and try again. If you are in a desperate hurry, you should buy a CD/DVD from the ubuntu store (and it would reach you within 3 days approx). 

By the way, you didn't need to reinstall if you wanted to have Kubuntu - you could have installed "kubuntu-desktop" from synaptic and that's all!

Good Luck :)
