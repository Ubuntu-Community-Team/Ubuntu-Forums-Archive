### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-02-10
Unity is "cameleonic" - it changes its colours based on the colour of your desktop background. You shouldn't get clashes, though, because the semi-transparent background of the Dash darkens the image behind it.

Is this not happening?

If the background of the Dash is darker than what's underneath it, but it's still too bright to read the white text in the Dash, then changing the desktop background colour is probably the best option. I don't think you can change the text colour, although I may be mistaken.
