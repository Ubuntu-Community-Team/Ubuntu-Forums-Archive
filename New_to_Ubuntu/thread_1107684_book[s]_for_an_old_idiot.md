If you are beginning with Ubuntu Linux, perhaps moving from Windows and not familiar with the Linux/Unix environment, buy the book Beginning Ubuntu Linux (Keir Thomas and Jaime Sicam). It is worth its weight in gold and will save you an enormous amount of time, not only in adapting to the new environment but also in telling why something in Linux is not working for you.

For example, I just spent ages thrashing around Websites and forums trying to find out why Flash content does not run in my Ubuntu Firefox browser. I typed in recommended scripts ad infinitum, installed and de-installed the various packages that were suggested, all to no avail.Finally I remembered the Thomas/Sicam book and found the solution there in 30 seconds.
