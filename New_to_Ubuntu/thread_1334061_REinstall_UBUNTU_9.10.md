### Post by BenAshton24 on 2009-11-22
1.  Backup your data!
2.  Download the ISO image from:[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)
3.  Burn it to a CD by double clicking it... should open with brazero
4.  Put it in your computer
5.  Reboot and press F8 a few times when you first start your computer.
5.  Select your CD drive and it should boot Ubuntu from the disk.
6.  Select "install ubuntu" when it gets to the first menu and then the wizard should guide you through the rest of the process.

Good luck,
Ben.
