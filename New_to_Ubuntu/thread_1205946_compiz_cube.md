### Post by Tholley on 2009-07-06
If you want it to rotate, make sure you have "rotate cube" checked in compiz, then hold Ctrl & Alt key (I believe that is the right combo), then while holding your left clicker down, move the mouse around and it should pull away and you can rotate it around.
 
Also make sure you have 4 virtual desktops set up, not the default 2.

Open the "Desktop Cube" plugin.
At the bottom, adjust the "Zoom" slider to, say, 0.3000 or thereabouts. 
 The *higher* the number the *smaller* the cube appears during rotation.

### Post by philinux on 2009-07-07
To make the cube smaller on rotate do this.

Open compiz settings manager. Click the Desktop option then the rotate cube plugin. At the bottom is zoom. The default setting is 0. Increasing this will make the cube progressively smaller.
