### Post by blazemore on 2010-01-23
Ubuntu-tweak is just a collection of settings for new users, and specifically former Windows power-users. Otherwise these users might not find the settings, as Ubuntu doesn't have a centralised control panel.
Obviously use common sense, don't change all your icons to a black square or anything.
