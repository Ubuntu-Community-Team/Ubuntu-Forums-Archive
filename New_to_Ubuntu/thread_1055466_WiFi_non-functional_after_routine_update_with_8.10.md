### Post by yeats on 2009-01-30
Fortunately, the logs record most of what's going on behind the scenes.  You can see which specific programs that were installed by date in /var/log/dpkg.log (and dpkg.log.1, etc. -the files with numbers are older).  You can do Alt-F2 and

```
gedit /var/log/dpkg.log
```

to see what was installed in the suspect update that might have changed the configuration somehow.  Feel free to post what you find here and maybe someone can help troubleshoot.
