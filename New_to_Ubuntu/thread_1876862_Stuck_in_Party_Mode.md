### Post by John Peschken on 2011-11-07
I decided to try Rhythmbox's Party Mode to see what that was all about.  Now I am stuck there.  The Top Line/Integrated menus do not appear like normal, and all the advice I can find online tells me to turn it off in the menus.  I can stop and switch away from RB, but when I open it again I am still in party mode.  Is there a magic key combo or something?  I am trapped!

---

### Post by John Peschken on 2011-11-07
I figured it out.  I rebooted.  Selecting Shutdown in the usual way just put me back into RB.  I had to hit the power button to get a shutdown menu that worked.  When I started RB again, it was out of Party Mode, I was able to get at the menus and see that F11 was the key for toggle key for Party mode.

### Post by Curtis6767 on 2012-04-27
I found this to be a problem when just listening to the radio on RB.

So click anywhere on RB to make it the active window. Look for its menu's in the upper left hand corner of the screen.  Under 'Music' select 'Quit' and you're done.

For me, even though I closed RB, the music played on.
