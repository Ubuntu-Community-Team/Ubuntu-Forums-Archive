### Post by someonestolemyname on 2008-07-18
First of all: it's not worth it. Dialup sucks. Anything faster is worth the hassle. PErsonal experience. 

All of the 'accelerated' internet actually just reduces the quality of the images with an Internet Explorer ActiveX so the images look like crap but load a bit faster. I'm sure there is no Linux equivalent, nor is it worth it. If you must get dialup, get the cheapest 56k around. It'll be slow either way.

If you only have an RJ-45 port, you can't use dialup since you don't have a modem. You'll need one to get it. They are really cheap. Or ask a friendly geek, he probably has a few he'll give away.

I'm an hour south of Chicago (kankakee), and my cable is okay. You have Comcast?

Also, never get dial-up w/ a Windows application that goes with it (I.E. Netzero, AOL). You can't connect w/ Linux.

Good luck, and reconsider.

Daniel
