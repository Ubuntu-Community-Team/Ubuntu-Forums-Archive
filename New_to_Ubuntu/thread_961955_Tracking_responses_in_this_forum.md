### Post by blackened on 2008-10-29
In the upper left, click on User CP. On the left hand navigation bar, click on "Edit Options" (see screenshot)

[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=90050&stc=1&d=1225254178[/IMG]

On the right, under "Default Thread Subscription Mode" select "No email notification" (or however else you would like to be notified).

[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=90051&stc=1&d=1225254238[/IMG]


You can view your subscribed threads under the User CP in the top left of all forum menus.
