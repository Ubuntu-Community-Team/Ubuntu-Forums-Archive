### Post by The Cog on 2008-11-04
I recommend the Devolo d-link (blue) ones. I bought a pair a couple of weeks ago, and they work a treat. One is on an extension lead downstairs, and the other is upstairs (different ring) the other end of the house. They report about 75 Meg throughput (they reported 85M when both on the same ring). I can't confirm the rates reported are real because I haven't done throughput tests, but they "feel" right when I am going through them.

I specifically bought this make because they are the only manufacturer (as far as I know) that provide a configuration program for Linux - all the others need Windows to set the encryption keys.
