### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-10-13
Before you use Wine, it would be worth your trouble to go to the CodeWeavers Application Compatibility site (like below) to check out the apps you want to run.  While some stuff works great in Wine, other stuff does not, and some stuff doesn't work at all.

Link:  [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true")
