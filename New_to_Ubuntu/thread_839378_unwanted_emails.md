### Post by bigfootnmd on 2008-06-24
In the modern world of today most INTERNET providers
offer free and automatic email filtering.  Most providers also allow you to access your email through a web page.  Check with your provider.  If they offer spam filtering and it is not on you can turn it on. My provider automatically filters mail and puts it in a folder called junk which I check regularly through a web page (in case a legit email is mistakenly flagged as spam).  I also use Yahoo which has excellent filters.  If a spam email does not get flagged I check it and report it as spam.
