### Post by macaholic on 2008-05-09
Not sure if it is exactly what you are looking for, but [baobab]("http://www.marzocca.net/linux/baobab.html") (Disk Usage Analyzer) gives similar information. It is part of the gnome-utils package, which can be installed from synaptic or apt-get, and is launched from Applications > Accesories > Disk Usage Analyzer.
