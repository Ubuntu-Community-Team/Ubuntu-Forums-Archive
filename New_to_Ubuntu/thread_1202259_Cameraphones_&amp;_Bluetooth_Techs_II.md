### Post by scorp123 on 2009-07-02
> **faron45 said:**
>  my phone then asks me for a "passcode". Standard procedure. You have to type a code, usually a 4-digit number, and then confirm the number again on the PC (there should be a pop-up asking for the same code again). So you type one code into your phone, e.g. your spouse's birthday, and then repeat it again on the pop-up on your PC's screen. Voila. Done.

If that mechanism wasn't there then anyone could link with your phone and download your pictures, contacts, etc. off the phone. You don't want that I guess? Hence the need for the passcode between Bluetooth devices. Both parties (e.g. phone + PC) need to confirm a passcode before the connection is allowed.
