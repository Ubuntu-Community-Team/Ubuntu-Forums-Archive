### Post by robsoles on 2010-10-23
That's your monitor, I hate these modern monitors that display a perfect picture and then complain because the frequency isn't exactly what they are best at or the size is not optimal for them.

'System'->'Preferences'->'Monitors' may allow you to set optimal settings for your monitor whilst booted from the LiveCD.
