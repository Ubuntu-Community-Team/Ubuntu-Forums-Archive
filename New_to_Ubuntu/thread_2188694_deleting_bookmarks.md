### Post by tea for one on 2013-11-18
Yes, good question.

On the left hand side of PCManFM, there is a pane with a choice of Places and Directory Tree

Select Places

Left click on the bookmark you wish to delete and you can choose Rename or Remove.

However, it looks like some bookmarks are fixed such as User and Trash Can so I would also be interested in further advice?

### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-11-19
Yes, right click>remove, but this will ONLY work for bookmarks under the line in the left column. The ones above the line are not bookmarks exactly, but partitions, trash and a symlink to the apps directory.
