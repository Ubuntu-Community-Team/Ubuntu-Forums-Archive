### Post by BillyBoa on 2011-10-29
LibreOffice is good but has compatibility problems with MS Office. Interchanging files between them is not always recommendable. As you said macros are not always working in both programs.
About the programs you mention I feel that is very difficult to work in Linux and if they work through an emulator, they never will have the full workability they have in Windows.
You have to try a dual boot installation with both Windows and Ubuntu, or to use something like Virualbox.
