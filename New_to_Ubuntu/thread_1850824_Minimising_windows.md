You minimize windows by pushing the middle button of the window controls at the top of the window.  It usually has a horizontal line on it.  You can also minimize windows by pushing alt+F9.

If you are referring to minimizing to the notification area, that tends to be a per-app setting.  The option will usually be somewhere under Edit->Preferences.

Speaking of Edit->Preferences, you can open that in firefox, open the "Content" dialogue, and there is a place to change font settings if you would like larger fonts.
