### Post by cipherboy_loc on 2011-03-21
If you go with Fluxbox, the two most important things you need to add are:

1) Conky -- It gives you stats about your system (real time, updates every second, but can be changed)
2) A custom menu. You have to right click on the desktop to get a menu (unless you use a taskbar menu program (docks like conky don't work in Fluxbox) ), so a self-organized menu is key.


BTW, I use Fluxbox, but I often switch back to a customized GNOME with compiz effects.
