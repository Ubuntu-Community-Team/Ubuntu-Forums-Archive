### Post by aeiah on 2008-10-13
i agree with the wireless card. the graphics card should be something to consider also. intel should be a safe bet for graphics (and indeed wireless but dont quote me).

something you should consider when buying a used laptop regardless of what you're installing on it is the battery. if you're planning on using it when its not plugged in, make sure spare batteries are easily and cheaply available for that kind of laptop, because its the first thing to go. your used laptop will probably have a battery that doesnt last too long.
