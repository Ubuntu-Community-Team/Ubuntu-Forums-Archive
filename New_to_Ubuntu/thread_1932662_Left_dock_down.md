### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-02-27
It's called Software Center, and you can find it by pressing Alt-F2 and typing "software center"; it will appear as one of three results.

Once opened, you can right-click its icon in the left panel and choose "Keep in Launcher".

However, Software Center won't help you move the Launcher to the bottom. Moving the Launcher is not officially supported, it can only be done through a hack at present.

The instructions at: [http://www.unixmen.com/move-unity-launcher-to-the-bottom-with-unity-bottom-launcher-ppa-ubuntu/](http://www.unixmen.com/move-unity-launcher-to-the-bottom-with-unity-bottom-launcher-ppa-ubuntu/)

will help.

Not everything about this works, so it might be better to leave it on the left side where it is for the moment. The reason it's on the left is so it doesn't take up precious vertical space on a widescreen monitor.
