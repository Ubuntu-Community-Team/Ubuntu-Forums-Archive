### Post by underskor on 2009-02-12
I have been messing around with flash and You Tube and Firefox3 all night, trying to figure out what the problem was.  I have read a few posts by other beginners like myself asking why it is such a problem....while others simply reply that it should not be a problem.

The solution was very simple for me but it just took me forever to think of trying it.  Mostly because all options are new right now and everything is pretty different from the "other" operating system.

The solution is (for me anyways):

"System"
"Preferences"
"Appearance"
"Visual Effects Tab"
"None Option"

This has given me a huge boost of speed.  My system is only running a 900mhz processor and about 700 megs of ddr 100 ram with a 128 meg radeon 9200 se series agp card.

Under the "Fonts" Tab I also adjusted the Font Rendering to monochrome.....perhaps unnecessary but I also noticed an other increase in speed.

Youtube is now working great...even in full screen and Firefox3 is now as fast as snot!  Everything seems to be running as fast as my other operating system.   I hope that this helps someone else...cause it's been driving me crazy until just now.
