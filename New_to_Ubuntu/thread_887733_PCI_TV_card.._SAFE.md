### Post by LowSky on 2008-08-12
Seriously?
If you are getting a jolt of electric by touching the card then perhaps you should stay clear of it.
My TV card doesn't Zap me at all, and if its the Coax cabling doing the zapping then it means that your Cable line isn't grounded correctly outside of your home, and you should call your cable providers to fix that. I agree, cable is low voltage.  There should be no zapping, even if you touch it with your bare hand.  If it's coming from the cable/coax ([coax]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coaxial_cable") is the type of physical wire by the way) then you need to talk to you cable company and have them check it out.  You can also buy surge protectors that protect against surges in your coax line too.
