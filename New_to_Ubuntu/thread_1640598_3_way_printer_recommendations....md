### Post by walt.smith1960 on 2010-12-08
I have 2 Brother 4in1 MFDs.  One was out-of-box for printing (MFC7820N-since superceded), one was not (MFC6490CW).  You can tell which models are supported by googling brother+linux+drivers or similar. Brother provides .deb & .rpm files for supported devices and they're pretty simple to install.  I didn't mess with installing fax functions, just print & scan.
