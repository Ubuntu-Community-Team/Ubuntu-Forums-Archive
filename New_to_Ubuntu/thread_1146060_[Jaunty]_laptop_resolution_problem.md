### Post by Bodsda on 2009-05-02
Hi guys, I just upgraded my laptop to jaunty and now i have some strange display problems. The desktop displays perfectly, everything runs smoothly but Ubuntu does not fill the screen, it has two strips top to bottom on the outer left and right of the screen that are not being used, it reminds me of the annoying black lines on the top and bottom of your tv when watching a film on tv. 

Hope this makes sense, if you need more information please ask.

Any help would be greatly appreciated, thanks,

Bodsda

### Post by Bodsda on 2009-05-02
Fixed it,

all credit goes to 'dotblank1' from #ubuntu on irc.freenode.net

simply changing the resolution to 1280 x 800

```
xrandr -s 1280x800
```

changes the resolution and fixes the problem

Thanks dotblank1 :),

Bodsda
