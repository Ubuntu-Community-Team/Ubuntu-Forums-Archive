### Post by oldrocker99 on 2019-05-22
It appears, from the picture, that you already your have text files pinned. If you want program icons to be in the taskbar, I don't really know GNOME 3, but with MATE, a fork of GNOME 2.3, you right-click the icon in the menu, and select "Pin icon to taskbar."

Ubuntu MATE is very configurable, lightweight (less than 500 MB at idle), and can look like Windows, Mac, Unity, and itself, with top-down menus and small icons, which is one of the many reasons I use MATE.
