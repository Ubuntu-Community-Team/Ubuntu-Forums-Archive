### Post by sayakb on 2008-05-27
No need of reinstalling. Believe me, it's really a minor problem. You can add as many panels as you like by Right clicking on a panel and selecting "New panel". Then you might add as many applets to it as you like. Just right click on any panel and click "Add to panel". Just select what you want to add, press Add button. If you don't like it, right click on it and select "Remove from panel"
Just play around with everything for sometime and this will all seem very comfortable and customizable. :)
