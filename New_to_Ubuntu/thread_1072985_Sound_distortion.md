### Post by JustANewb on 2009-02-17
When you are turning the music up is it in the app or the volume of the speakers themselves?  If you turn the music up by the app, then you might get a distorted sound.  To remedy, just turn the volume down on the app and then turn the speakers up.  Also, you might want to check if the app is using a gain on the sound or something.  Keeping it at 0dB is best unless you have high powered speakers to play that loud...
