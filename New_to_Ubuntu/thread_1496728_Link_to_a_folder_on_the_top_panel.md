### Post by da burger on 2010-05-29

### Post by drs305 on 2010-05-29
For a folder, you should be able to open nautilus and just drag a folder to the top panel and release. For apps, you should be able to do the same thing from the "Applications" menu.

For other applications, one method is to right click the panel, Add to Panel, then either find the app already listed in the lower pane or clicking "Application Launcher".  If you can't find it, choose "Custom Application Launcher" and build it.
