### Post by arochester on 2010-06-15
You need Medibuntu Repository and: libdvdcss2

Look at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)
Scroll down to "Playing Encrypted DVDs"
