### Post by mc4man on 2011-01-06
> What it's doing is 90% of the time when I RIGHT click on something to see the menu of things I can do, that RIGHT click will execute...
There are several threads on this, so the short of it

With many themes, when r. clicking on the desktop or in nautilus the cursor is in the context menu.
Two things could be happening - 
An inadvertent 2nd r. click will execute the highlighted option.

More likely - there is an extremely short time frame between the up on a right click doing nothing and the up executing the highlighted option.
(never timed but probably between 1/2 to 1 sec.

So you could, amomg several things - 
Use a theme where the cursor is outside the menu
Do a tap right click instead of a push and release, the menu will stay exposed, nothing executed till you re-click, left or right.
Use a right click and hold, nothing will happen until you release the button. (or nothing at all if you move outside the menu,
