### Post by SlappyPappy on 2008-04-30
Well I have a way for you lads. K3b was driving me nuts because it would burn about three disks and then go wonky. Had no idea how to force quit the thing and found this.

Right click on the panel at the top and go to Add to Panel. Then in Desktop and Windows you'll see a force quit button you can add to the panel. Add that sucker and away you go. 

When K3b goes nuts click on the force quit button in the panel, click on the window of K3b and you're done!  :)

Sweet eh?
