### Post by Lightning Dragon on 2013-01-21
Hello,

First, when you highlight something, can you not right click it, or can you and the paste/copy part is just grayed out? 

Can you copy and paste things *outside* of Firefox? Try opening up LibreOffice or a text editor, make some text, copy and paste it. If it doesn't work, then it isn't Firefox that is the problem.

Do you have any other add-ons enabled? If so, what and is one of them Stumble Upon? Also make sure you have Java installed and enabled.
