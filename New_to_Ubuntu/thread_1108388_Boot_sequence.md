### Post by presence1960 on 2009-03-27
Every BIOS is a little different, look for something similar to "boot priority". This will give you the option to choose. Some use + or - key to move the drives up or down in the priority list.

---

### Post by sgosnell on 2009-03-27
Sometimes it's the page up/page down keys, sometimes +/-, sometimes something else.  BIOS programmers aren't at all consistent.  There are generally two different boot options, one for the available drives, and another for the priority, and the way they're presented can be confusing.
