For your "Save session" option, you could go to System, preferences, sessions, Session options tab, and check the "automatically remember running applications" box. This will have Ubuntu automatically restore programs you have running on shutdown.

For a lightening fast boot up; I'd invest the time in getting "Suspend to Ram" working...it's sometimes a hassle (depending on your hardware) but once it works, it's sweet!
