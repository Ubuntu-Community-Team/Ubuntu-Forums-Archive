### Post by bobnutfield on 2008-10-07
Not to throw too many wrenches in the gears, but I have a Toshiba laptop with the ATI X1250 graphics suggested by the OP.  I had it working great with Hardy and the FGLRX driver: full compiz and video editing with a breeze.  ATI will work fine, but you have to use a stable system, such as Hardy.  I have since upgraded to Intrepid, which is also working great on this laptop, but as yet the FGLRX driver is not available for the new Xorg.

So, if you stay with Hardy (not Intrepid yet, but soon after its release), ATI will work fine for you.
