Hi Folks:

After more poking arround here is what worked:

open firefox.

Bookmarks > set as speed dial > speed dial options > actions > restore backups > then choose the most recent date.

Close firefox.

Open firefox.

RIght click on the "+" sign... your speed dial tabs will reappear.
