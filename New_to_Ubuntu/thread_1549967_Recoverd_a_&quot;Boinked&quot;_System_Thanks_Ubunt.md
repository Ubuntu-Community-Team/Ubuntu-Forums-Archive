### Post by Ron Carson on 2010-08-10
Some how I "boinked" my sytem this morning.  Rather than trying to figure how to fix it, I opted to reinstall from a CD.  I previously had placed my home directory on a separate partition so there was no needed changes to that.

After 20 minutes of installing from the CD, I successfully rebooted.  I then copied a backup copy of my home directory, installed Thunderbird, Firefox, Virtual Box and couple other programs and I was back to where I was before.

I am AMAZED at home quick I can recover from a "boinked" system without having an image of my drive.
