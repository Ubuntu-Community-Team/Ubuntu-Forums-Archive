### Post by sleepingdragon on 2008-07-03
you probably won't encounter any real problems, the 4x/8x AGP speed denotes how fast data is sent to the graphics card. Your graphics card should handle the 4x speed perfectly well. If your card is capable of 8x, then 4x is a stroll in the park (metaphorically speaking).

I'm surprised at the 256Mb RAM option. If the graphics card says 128Mb on the outside of the box, then select that. 

You might encounter problems with some high-end graphics software, such as 3D games or even using Compiz, but this will only result in slow performance in your graphics. Video quality might be degraded, but there should be nothing there to "break" your system. 

Not sure about the game, but if it seem to be working after a reinstall, then that's great.

Hope that helps.
