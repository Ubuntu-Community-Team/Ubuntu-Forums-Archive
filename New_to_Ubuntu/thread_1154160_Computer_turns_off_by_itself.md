### Post by cariboo on 2009-05-10
I would check the hardware, make sure your cpu ins't overheating, and either have the power supply checked, or purchase a power supply tester and check it yourself. Installing software usually doesn't cause a computer to shut down. THe log snippet you provided looks perfectly normal.

### Post by presence1960 on 2009-05-10
I had the same thing a while back, my machine would randomly shut down on me. I open my box every 6-8 weeks on average to blow it out so the dust does not accumulate on hardware. I checked all my power cables from the PSU and the mobo power supply was not fully seated. One side was not completely down. Fixed that and the problem was solved. I would check all hardware connections anyway.
