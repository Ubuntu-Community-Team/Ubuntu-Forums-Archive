### Post by rybaxs on 2008-09-29
Just run the Ubuntu live CD then follow the wizard in installation. It will not overwrite the previous OS. At the partition wizard part just click and drag the amount of space shared on your disk capacity for your new OS. Remember that the left side is your previous OS amount of space in the disk and the right side is the new one. It will take an hour if the disk is to large.
