### Post by guiverc on 2019-11-16
You haven't said what release of Lubuntu you are talking about  (I'll assume the latest 19.10 or 2019-October release). 

I'll provide the manual link - [https://manual.lubuntu.me/stable/](https://manual.lubuntu.me/stable/)   (*mostly as I think it find it useful*)

If I have problems I usually re-check my install-media; so I'd suggest that (it's the "*Check disk for defects*" option in the Lubuntu manual (or for more info [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/CDIntegrityCheck](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/CDIntegrityCheck)).   I'm also assuming you validating your ISO post-download ([https://tutorials.ubuntu.com/tutorial/tutorial-how-to-verify-ubuntu#0](https://tutorials.ubuntu.com/tutorial/tutorial-how-to-verify-ubuntu#0) which applies equally to flavors) though I usually skip that myself, as the `zsync` program I do my downloads with validates post-download for me.  Those are what I'd check first.
