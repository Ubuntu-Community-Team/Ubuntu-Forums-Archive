### Post by HermanAB on 2009-04-13
Here is my flaky computer repair recipe:
[LIST]
[*]Open box,vacuum out all the dirt.
[*]Check to see whether the CPU fan works - not not, replace fan.
[*]Check for distended or leaky capacitors - if so, replace mother board or discard whole machine.
[*]If everything seems fine, replace the PSU.
[/LIST]
That takes care of 99.999% of flaky PC problems.
