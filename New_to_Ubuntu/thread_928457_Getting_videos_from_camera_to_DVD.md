### Post by NewJack on 2008-09-24
For transferring the video from the camera, you may want to check out Kino.  As far as putting it onto DVD afterwards, there are a few DVD editing / authoring programs out there.  Avidemux (Editor), DeVeDe and DVDStyler (Authoring), then use K3b or Brasero (Burn).

I believe most of these are in the repos.  Hope this helps.
