Greetings, wise ones.

I've got an issue with Totem, as you may have deduced by the thread title. I am unable to watch videos on my TV, which is connected by s-video. The TV shows exactly the same thing as the computer screen, as far as windows, applications, etc, but when a video is playing there's just black in Totem on the TV, though it plays fine on my laptop. 

I know that Ubuntu/Totem **can** do this, as there was once that I restarted my comp & the opposite happened; the TV showed the video while the regular display was black. 

I don't need the TV to be recognized as another monitor; Ubuntu's virtual workspaces are far more useful, but I would really like to be able to view video on the TV screen. Totem has 'TV out' radio buttons in Preferences, but they're greyed out. Anyone have an idea? I'm running Ubuntu on a Dell Inspiron 1525 laptop. Thanks in advance!
