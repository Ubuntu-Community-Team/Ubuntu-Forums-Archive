### Post by Orange_and_Green on 2008-10-07
Hello :)

I Googled and found [this article]("http://www.linux.com/feature/113920") that mentions a list of all USB adapters that are compatible with the latest linux kernel. I'll just copy-paste the link:

[http://www.linux-wlan.org/docs/wlan_adapters.html.gz](http://www.linux-wlan.org/docs/wlan_adapters.html.gz)

(download the file, then extract it and open it. Search for "USB" and make a note of all the adapters that have entries in either of the two columns called "Linux support", then take your pick)

Hope this helps, good luck :KS
