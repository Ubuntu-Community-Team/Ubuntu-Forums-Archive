### Post by jgervais89 on 2015-08-20
Hi there,  

 Just want to add a little bit more information about my experience. 

 I got the new laptop (the same one) some days ago, and everything worked fine, until this morning; I had the exact same problem. Not only that, it was the same keys that were not working. Again, the USB keyboard was working fine. Moreover, I tried to boot with an Install/Live DVD of Ubuntu 15.04 (which is not the version that I am currently using; I use the 14.04), and the keyboard was not working.  

 Following that, I decided to change my assumptions. That is, rather than searching for Linux/Ubuntu and keyboard, I searched for Dell laptop keyboard, and found that it seems to be a common problem.  Here [http://en.community.dell.com/support-forums/laptop/f/3518/t/19345921,           ]("http://en.community.dell.com/support-forums/laptop/f/3518/t/19345921")you                       can find an user who offers a way to deal with the problem. Following is part of his/her answer:

>                          

"You need to shut down your Studio 15 laptop. Unplug power cord and take out the battery. Then hold down the Power on button for approx 30 secs. Once done replace the battery and power cord and start up as normal."
  
                  
Although I do not have the same laptop, it worked well for me. I still don't know if it will be a permanent solution, and  why it happens in the first place, however. 

I thought sharing this information, just in case another user experiences the same kind of problems, with a Dell computer.
