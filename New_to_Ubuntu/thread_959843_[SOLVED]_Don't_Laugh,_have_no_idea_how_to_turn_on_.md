### Post by CatKiller on 2008-10-27
> **Kellemora said:**
> BUT, HOW do you get the small cube in the center of the screen?????

In CompizConfig Settings Manager (or Advanced Desktop Effects Settings, if you prefer) select the Rotate Cube plugin. At the bottom of the General tab is the slider that controls the Zoom level. A larger number means a smaller cube. I find that 0.15 is quite nice, but you might prefer something different. The default binding to rotate the cube is a middle-click on the Desktop, but you can change it to something else on the Bindings tab, if you prefer.
