### Post by t0p on 2008-09-11
You don't need backports.  They are newer versions of software in the supported repos, but the backports are *not* supported.

---

### Post by LowSky on 2008-09-11
backports are software that is schedles to be updated for the newer version of ubuntu, but most can be ported to work on older version, hence the term backports.
Now be careful with these as some can break your system if your not too careful as they are not tested the same or may need another newer application that has not been backported yet.
