### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2010-12-01
Can you give us a little more information on what is happening that is not allowing you to change your location?

You can add many different locations to your panel, but the weather that is displayed on the panel bar will be the weather from the location that is set as "Home".  If you click on the time on your panel, you'll get a little drop down window and you should be able to see all of your locations.  When you mouse over them, you should be able to set whichever one you want to "home".

### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2010-12-01
I am not using Maverick, but it has to similar so here goes.  When you click on the weather indicator in the panel you should be able to edit your location or the location near you that you are using.  That is where you tick off that you want to see the weather, the seconds on the clock, the wind speed in MPH or knots, etc.  Anyway click edit, and then location, and add.  That should be where you can add your location.  If your exact location is not in the data base you can always use one that is close to you.  That is what I do.  Maverick might be a little different.
