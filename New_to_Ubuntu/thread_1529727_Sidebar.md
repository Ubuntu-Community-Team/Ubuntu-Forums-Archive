### Post by jimmy the saint on 2010-07-12
screenlets can run google widgets and it is in the repositories.

In addition, with compiz enabled, you can assign a widget layer so that your widgets are hidden until you press a hotkey, at which time they all appear, then disappear when you hit it again.  Very hand to reduce screen cluter!

[http://www.screenlets.org/index.php/Information](http://www.screenlets.org/index.php/Information)
