Linux doesn't use and can't run.exe files. I would suggest going to System-->Administration-->Hardware Drivers and  enable the suggested driver. Remember Linux is not Windows and most of what you know about computers is of no use.

In linux there are at least three ways to do everything and none of them are wrong. To install programs you can use Applications-->Add/Remove Programs or System-->Administration-->Synaptic Package Manager. There is no need going out and searching for drivers and programs.
