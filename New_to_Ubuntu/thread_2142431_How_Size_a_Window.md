### Post by Rafacky on 2013-05-05
You can also use a combination of Ctlr+Alt+Num, if you have a keyboard with numeric keypad.

E.g. Ctrl+Alt+7 places chosen window in upper left quarter of the screen, Ctrl+Alt+8 in upper right quarter, etc. Just experiment with it a bit, you'll see.
