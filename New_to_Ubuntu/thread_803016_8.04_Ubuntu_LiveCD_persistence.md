### Post by Nepherte on 2008-05-23
When you run the LiveCD, the parts it needs will be loaded into the ram or a swap partition if available. That's th reason it's not that fast. The purpose of a LiveCD is just for a quick test of ubuntu to see what it is or to recovery something when the main os is broke or something alike. If you plan on using Ubuntu frequently, you better install it from the LiveCD on your disk.
