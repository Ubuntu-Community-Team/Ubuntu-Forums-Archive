### Post by knattlhuber on 2008-10-02
I don't think your songs are gone, they are probably just 'invisible' to Amarok. One thing you could do to confirm this is look at the disk space used on your iPod using Nautilus.
To recover the songs, I would probably try to connect it to a Windows PC or a Mac and see what happens.
However, make sure that that iTunes doesn't have automatic sync enabled. That would potentially (if your iPod is registered on that machine and iTunes version) wipe out your songs.
