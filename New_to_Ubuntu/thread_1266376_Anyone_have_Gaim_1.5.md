### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-09-15
You will need to compile this from source. Gaim 1.5 is so old that any Debian packages that exist will be attempting to pull in dependencies that don't exist anymore in today's Ubuntu.

It's also unlikely that Gaim 1.5 will be able to connect to MSN or Yahoo networks; I believe MSN and Yahoo have changed their protocols since Gaim 1.5 was released.

I personally use Meebo as my instant messaging client. It's web-based, can connect to every IM network I've ever heard of, and it is immediately updated for new protocols without you needing to download an update.
