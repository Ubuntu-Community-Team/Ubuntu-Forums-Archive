### Post by Sinkingships7 on 2008-10-05
They're the configuration files for all of the various programs installed on your computer that you use.

For example, if you deleted the .mozilla folder, you'd lose every bookmark, saved password, and setting related to Firefox. If you deleted .purple, you'd lose all of your settings and configurations for Pidgin.

Make sense?
