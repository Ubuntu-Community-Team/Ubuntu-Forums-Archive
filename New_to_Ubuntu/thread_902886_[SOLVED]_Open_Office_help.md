### Post by ilrudie on 2008-08-28
The table will probably work fine but I think there is a more "correct" way.  What you want to do is create the new document.  Then go to the insert menu and select section.  Insert a new section.  You can call it title or whatever you want.  The title will go in this first section.  Then go to the insert menu and select section again.  This time when the menu pops up call this section story or text or something so you can tell them apart. Before you click insert click on the columns tab and choose the desired number of columns.  The default for columns is to spread the text evenly across the columns which is not what I expected and is probably not what you want so you may want to uncheck that option on the columns tab also.  Play around and see which behavior you like (you can change it by going to the format drop down menu and selecting sections, then select the section you want to change and click the options button).  It will make the section small at first but as you fill it it will resize to fill up as many pages as you type.

Hope that helps and if something is unclear just ask and I can get back to you.  Good luck with your story.

*note if you format the columns directly from the format drop down menu (not from the sections popup windows) it formats the whole page and puts the sections (which may contain columsn) into columns so don't do that unless its what you want.  You can make some very intricate pages this way.
