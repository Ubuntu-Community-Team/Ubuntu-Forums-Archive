### Post by blazemore on 2009-12-11
Next to the Go Home applet there's the Window Picker. On Ubuntu Netbook Remix all the panel items are crammed in together with no actual blank panel space. You can fix this by right-clicking to the left of the Window Picker, and unticking "Lock to panel"
Then right-click it again and choose move. Move it a little away from the go home applet.

Now you can right-click on the blank panel space and choose "add to panel", then "application launcher"
