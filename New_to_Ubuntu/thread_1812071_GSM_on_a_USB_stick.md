Hi, I'm not sure how to speed it up, but there is a way to monitor send/receive data.  Go to System>Administration>System Monitor.  (Resources tab)

I also recommend more advanced programs, like:
[http://sourceforge.net/projects/nload/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/nload/)

Hope this helps.

### Post by ubudog on 2011-07-26
Yes, sorry, I didn't realize you were on Unity until now.  

But yes, just click on the Ubuntu logo at the top left of the screen, then go to Installed, and search for System Monitor.  Open that, and click on the Resources tab.  

Also, if you want to use the classic desktop (GNOME), log out, and at the bottom of the screen there should be a drop down menu that contains "Ubuntu Classic".  Select this, and log in as normal.
