### Post by humphreybc on 2009-03-13
Actually to get skype working properly I remember what I had to do. 

I am on a laptop so I right clicked on the volume icon, went to "open volume control" and then clicked the recording tab. I turned the Capture volume up all the way, clicked on the mic icon so it removed the X on it and then went to the options tab and chose "front mic" as my input instead of "mic." 

This told ubuntu to use the microphone inbuilt above my screen instead of the mic jack at the front of the computer.

Then I went into Skype and changed the input device and made test calls until it worked.
