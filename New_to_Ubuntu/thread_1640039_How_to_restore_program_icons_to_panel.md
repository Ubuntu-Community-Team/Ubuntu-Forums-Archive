### Post by daneforce on 2010-12-07
Right click the top panel and select "Add to Panel", then add "Notification Area" applet. Hope it works out for you.

---

### Post by mcduck on 2010-12-07
Right-click in some empty spot in the panel, select "Add to Panel", find the "*Notification Area*"-applet and drag&drop it into the panel.
