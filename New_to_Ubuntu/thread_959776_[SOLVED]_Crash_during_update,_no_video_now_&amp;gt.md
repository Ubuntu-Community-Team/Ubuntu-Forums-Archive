### Post by DoubleMcLovin on 2008-10-26
So I was running a system update, and then all of a sudden the system locks up, and of course the update manager is installing updates for the hardware-drivers-extras or something like that that I just knew would break my display as soon as I hit the hard reset.... I was right....

So here I am, I start up the computer and get the login screen, login then nothing. I have a mouse and a black screen. Everything else is gone. I am able to get to the tty1-6 screens no problem, and the system isn't freezing. Any suggestions?

EDIT: I love linux =D

Wow... I went into the recovery mode, and there was an option for exactly this, fix broken packages. I am completely amazed. After restart my video driver had been un installed but the display loaded fine. I soon reloaded the driver and restarted and everything went fine!

After this incredibly easy fix for a possibly catastrophic problem that I was almost certain would lead to a full re-installation I am proud to say I am now a linux fan for life.
