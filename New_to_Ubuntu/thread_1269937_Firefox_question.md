### Post by nhasian on 2009-09-18
what is a mouse wheel section of a numberpad?  i dont think i've ever seen such a thing.  are you referring to the hotzone of touchpad that works like a mouse wheel?  in that case you can go to System->Preferences->Mouse Preferences then go to the Touchpad tab.  you can untick the box for enable scrolling.  

Also in Ubuntu Karmic Koala 9.10 there is an additional option in this section that allows you to temporarily disable the touchpad entirely while typing.  I find it a must have if your on a laptop.  i hate it when my palm brushes the touchpad and moves the cursor around when i'm typing.
