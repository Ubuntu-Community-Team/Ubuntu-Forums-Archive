### Post by janmartin on 2008-04-26
You can enable additional Repositories in
Menu: System, Administration, Synaptic Package Manager
Settings, Repositories
on Tab "Third Party Software".
Also you can change the Servers to download from on first Tab "Ubuntu Software" at "Download From".

Give it a try with Synaptic Package Manager first, then try Google. Searching for "max data eco 4000A Ubuntu" or "WUSB54GS V2 Ubuntu" I immediately found lots of good info on the driver situation.

Good Luck!
