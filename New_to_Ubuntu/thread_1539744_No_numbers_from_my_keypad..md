### Post by myolbug on 2010-07-27
From the Linux Mint forums:


    
Post by Pilosopong Tasyo on Tue Jul 27, 2010 4:49 am
Try this:

Go to Mint Menu > Control Center > Hardware group > Keyboard > Mouse Keys tab. If there's a checkmark beside the item Pointer can be controlled using the keypad, uncheck it and click the Close button.


Works like a charm.
