I recently lost the LibreOffice Writer icon on the stack of icons located on the left side of the desktop.  What must I do to get it back?

---

### Post by deadflowr on 2014-05-12
If you click on the top icon there(in the launch bar), the one that looks like the ubuntu logo, and then in the search box, type writer, does it show up in the list of apps?

If so, then try dragging the icon over to the launcher.
Or click on the app and launch it, then it should put a temp icon on the launcher, which you can then right-click, lock to launcher, which should re-stick it there, permanently.
