### Post by newb85 on 2014-07-01
Welcome!  Sorry you're having a bad initial experience.  F.Y.I, it's Ubuntu, not Ubunto.

I suspect the slow install is due to your hardware limitations.  (Last week I did a fresh install of Ubuntu 14.04 on my 4-year-old laptop, and it took less than an hour.  Add another half hour to get all the apps I wanted installed and everything configured the way I wanted.)  Keep in mind, Ubuntu 14.04 is less than 3 months old.  Many of the XP-era machines are too week to run regular Ubuntu.  That's why Lubuntu exists.

More directly to the point of your request, you might consider using Evolution instead of Thunderbird.  Version 3.10.4 (the one in the 14.04 repos) comes with a .pst import plugin installed.
