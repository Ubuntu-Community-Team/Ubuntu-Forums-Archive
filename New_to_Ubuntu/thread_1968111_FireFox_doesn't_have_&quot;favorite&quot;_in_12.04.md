### Post by Zukaro on 2012-04-28
It should still be there.  It's called "bookmark this page".  You can also click the star in the URL Bar or press Ctrl-D (it's a hotkey to bookmark).

If you want to group things together open your bookmarks menu, go to show all bookmarks (or press Shift-Ctrl-O), go to bookmarks menu, and then right click and make a new folder.  Then just drag your bookmarks into the folders you want them to be in.

### Post by ubuntu27 on 2012-05-18
To bookmark multiple sites in a group, first open the desired websites in different tabs (close any tabs that you don't want it to be bookmarked), and then right click on a open tab and choose "Bookmark all tabs..."
