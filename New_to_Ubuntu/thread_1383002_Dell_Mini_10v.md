### Post by MarkC502 on 2010-01-16
I would try googling and reading for your specific graphics card and wifi. I have installed Ubuntu on many different PC's and it is quite frequently bad when first installed if the graphics chipset has a better driver solution etc. I would find a guide to dell mini 10v Ubuntu setup and read it as that would be your best bet to figure out what is best for your hardware.

I googled around and looked at your situation and I saw someone who reported that their wifi was working with 9.04 on 10v then broke with 9.10 update, so you may want to think about trying 9.04 (there's nothing wrong with it - I have 9.04 on 3 PC's and 9.10 on 2 PC's and I have had more issues with 9.10).
