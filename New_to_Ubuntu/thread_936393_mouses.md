### Post by bwang on 2008-10-02
The double clicking thing could be hardware or software. 

Software: Go to System>Preferences>Mouse and change "Double Click Timeout". (My mouse is set at 400 ms)

If its hardware, see if the mouse button is stuck. My mouse will also occasionally double click for me. I think its hardware, because if it were software, then the double-clicking would be on all the time.
