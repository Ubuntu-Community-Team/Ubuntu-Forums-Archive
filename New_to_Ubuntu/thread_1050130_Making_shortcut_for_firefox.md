### Post by llamakc on 2009-01-25
Right click on the Desktop and choose "Create Launcher".

In the command field, put:

```

firefox "http://path.to.URL"

```

replacing path.to.URL with the address you want. Remember to place the URL in quotes.

Voila!

### Post by Norm24 on 2009-01-25
Go to Applications>Internet>Firefox Web Browser.

Right click on FF and choose "Add this launcher to desktop".This also works on any app listed.
