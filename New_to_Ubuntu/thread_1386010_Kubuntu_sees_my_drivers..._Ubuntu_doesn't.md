### Post by MoebusNet on 2010-01-21
Linux in general (including Ubuntu) has issues with some hardware because the manufacturers are often slow, reluctant or unwilling to release enough information to design and build perfect drivers for their products.

That said, you can try to resolve your issues by reading:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=205449](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=205449)

There is a lot of accumulated information about Ubuntu on this forum, I encourage you to dive in and read up on the topics that interest you.

There does not exist a perfect Operating System, but with a little effort and a willingness to learn I believe you will find that Ubuntu will work for you. I know it works well for me.

Welcome to the Ubuntu community!
