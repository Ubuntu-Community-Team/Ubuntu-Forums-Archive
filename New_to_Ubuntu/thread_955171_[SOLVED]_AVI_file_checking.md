### Post by kostkon on 2008-10-22
You can check for errors with [*DivFix++*]("http://divfixpp.sourceforge.net/home.htm").

You can't find it in the repos or *getdeb.net*, unfortunately.

If you have a 64bit system, then you can download a binary from its site, otherwise you'll have to compile it yourself.
