### Post by SPazzo on 2009-11-19
I take it you are recording from something such as an Internet Radio station or a Youtube video.  In Audacity there is a little pull down menu next to the microphone input slider:

[IMG]http://i49.tinypic.com/x6bno2.jpg[/IMG]

It says Microphone right now.

Click on it and change it to *Stereo Mix*:

[IMG]http://i47.tinypic.com/2qa3adx.jpg[/IMG]

Then, make sur the microphone slider (next to the menu) is pretty quiet.  Hit record and play on whatever you're trying to record and it should work.

(NOTE:  The menu layout on yours might be a little different...  I got those screenshots on a Windows computer.)
