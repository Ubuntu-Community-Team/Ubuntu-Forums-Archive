### Post by stinger30au on 2008-05-11
it may not be a bad idea to check the hardware of your pc with a free boot disk to test.

i suggest this

[http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/](http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/)

its free and it will test ram and hdd and other stuff too.

believe it or not your pc has a few fans in it.
they suck dust in from the outside world and the fans glog up.
clean them out with a clean paintbrush and some compressed air.

make sure you stop the fans from spinning before you shove the compressed air on to them.
the reason i say this is some fan blades fan fly appart if the fan is spun too fast.

i bet if everyone checked the simple stuff first before making a post it would certainly help a lot!
