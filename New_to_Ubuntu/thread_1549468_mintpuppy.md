I would reccomend puppy.
Firstly, before I start telling you why, Mint = ubuntu.
Ubuntu is generally heavier than other distros, and requires 1GB of ram to function at full speed. Puppy requires very, very, very little memory.

Puppy has firefox, and all of that stuff, but it may be slightly harder to use since you are used to ubuntu.
