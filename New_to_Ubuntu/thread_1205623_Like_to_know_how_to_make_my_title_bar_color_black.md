### Post by drs305 on 2009-07-06
enseyn has a good memory. Once you have the Themes tab open, select the theme you want to modify, then "Customize" . Select the Window Border tab if you want a different stye, then the  Colors tab and choose a new color in the "Selected Items" (not Windows, thanks psycosmith) section. The change will take effect when you hit OK, and you will be using the "Custom" theme.
