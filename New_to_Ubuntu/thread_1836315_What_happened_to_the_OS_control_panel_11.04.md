I was having trouble adjusting to the new setup and found a tip here somewhere on returning to the "Ubuntu Classic" screen.

Shut down, restart but, before you sign in, go to the bottom bar and select "Classic" then sign in again. This will open your computer with the same "start" point as you had in 10.04.

Made it much easier for me to find what I was looking for. May switch over at a later date, but no real enthusiastic about it.
