### Post by earthpigg on 2009-07-17
it really sounds like either the download was corrupt, or the burn to cd was corrupt, or the optical drive in the target machine is busted.

torrents are the most reliable way to download anything (shhh, dont tell the corporations this little secret!), and slower burn speeds always yield more reliable and consistent burns (for an OS install CD, your best bet is probably the slowest speed possible... maybe as fast as 4x, but avoid 16x or faster.).


[for an audio cd, yeah, go ahead and burn at 48x. you'd have to have pretty sharp ears to notice the difference.]

### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-07-17
> Also, my Google search bar in Firefox doesn't work, I have to go to the Google website every time, not sure if this is an Ubuntu or Firefox isue though.
Open a new window and in the address bar, type "about:config" without the quote marks.  At the warning window, click "I'll be careful".  Type "search" in the filter and see what is set for default.  Even if it says Google, double click it and type Google in the box that pops up.  You may have to reboot, but it should work now.  :P
