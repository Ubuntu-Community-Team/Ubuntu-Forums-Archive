### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2015-12-15
When you "turned the computer off" . . . did you do a hard shutdown (pressing the power button until system shuts down)?     IF so, that is always risky and can result in ruining the installed OS.    There are ways to shut down without doing that, and by doing a controlled reboot via the CLI (command line/terminal) or by use of the magic key sequence.
