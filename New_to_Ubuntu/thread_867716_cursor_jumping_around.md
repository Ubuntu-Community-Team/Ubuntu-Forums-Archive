### Post by Tony Flury on 2008-07-23
I know this sounds like a stupid question, but i assume that your laptop has an in built touch pad. 

I had excatly the same problem, and it turned out that as I was typing i was brushing the touch pad, and the linux was reading that as a mouse move followed by a click (i.e. a touch pad "tap"). It seemed to me that the linux driver was very sensitive (compared to windows), and i had the "tap" functionality turned off on windows, but could find no way of turning iot off on Hardy.

my solution was to start using a mouse (and turn off the touchpad) which is also a lot better for my hands.
