### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-12
First thing I would do is swap the Sys fan with the NB fan and see if the Sys fan now starts, and if the NB fan now starts.

IF the Sys fan starts but the NB fan does not, that means the NB fan header is bad.  If the Sys fan does not start, but the NB fan does, that means the Sys fan has failed.
