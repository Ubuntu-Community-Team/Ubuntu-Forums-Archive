I'm afraid the low speed is really a consequence of your graphics hardware - that sort of drop is an Inevitable  consequence of running compiz, but on most graphics cards the original performance is high enough that it doesn't generally matter.

Unless you're running on a laptop, I'd seriously suggest replacing the graphics card since you can get a much faster AGP card very cheaply.  Something like a 7600GS AGP should set you back about $50 and dramatically improve compiz/graphics performance.
