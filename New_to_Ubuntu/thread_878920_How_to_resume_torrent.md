### Post by Xiong Chiamiov on 2008-08-03
If Deluge isn't showing any of your torrents in it, you'll have to open the torrent files in it again.  When you tell it to download them to the same place you have partial files, it will first check the files and then continue from where they left off.

### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-08-03
Backup the partial downloaded files (just incase), and retry the torrents.

From memory, if it finds the partials in the designated place, then it checksums it and continues where you left off.
So no harm done.
