### Post by migs73 on 2010-06-11
Martijn

Welcome to Ubuntu! I am not entirely sure what you require, but to create a shortcut on your desktop, go to the file you want in the menu, eg. Applications> Internet> Firefox, and leftclick and hold on the file/program and drag it onto the desktop. Voila a shortcut. This should in no way interfere with the cube etc. 
You will have this shortcut on all your desktops (all sides of the cube).
