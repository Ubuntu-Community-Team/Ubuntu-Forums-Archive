### Post by phidia on 2008-07-18
Motherboards have a simple sound code so techs can more easily diagnose problems.
I'm not sure what 3 beeps means but it's not good, The normal system ok startup is one beep.

Added: Take a look at [this guide]("http://www.pchell.com/hardware/beepcodes.shtml") on the bios beep codes.
Perhaps you are getting 1 long beep and 3 short beeps-that would indicate a graphics card problem. Is your computer still under warranty?
