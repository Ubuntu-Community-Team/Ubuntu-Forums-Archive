### Post by jpoRS on 2009-02-09
> **jmedoro said:**
> "proprietary to Linux."

That is a bit of a non-sequitur.  Linux is Open Source, so nothing is "proprietary".  I will assume that you just made a word selection error.  If you didn't though, the first thing you should do is read up on what (and why) Linux is.

Anyway, for the complete beginner I have found that the best course of action is to just poke around here and the rest of the Internet, look for someone doing something with their computer, and try and do it.  Hands on experience is the best teacher.

If you aren't an absolute beginner and are looking for something more intense, think of something you want your computer to do.  Now make your computer do it.  Hardware limitations aside, you can get your computer to do whatever you can think of, assuming you have the know how.

Good luck, and welcome to the community.
