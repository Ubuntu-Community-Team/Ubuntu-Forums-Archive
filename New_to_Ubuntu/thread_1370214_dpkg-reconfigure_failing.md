### Post by AI52487963 on 2010-01-02
So the problem was partially solved. I copied the xorg.conf file provided by another Toshiba R100 user here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=301082](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=301082)

That got my resolution into 1024x768. However, the bug still exists when you take it off the battery charger and the resolution gets all messed up. A quick fix is to ctrl+alt+F1 then ctrl+alt+F7 to restart the graphics back up into 'normal' mode. Doesn't seem like a permanent solution, though.
