### Post by Cypher on 2008-08-08
Do you have an additional sound card in addition to the on-board and have the on-board enabled? Having two sound cards enabled and available at the same time will thoroughly confuse the sound drivers..

Display the on-board sound card in the BIOS and see if things behave better..
