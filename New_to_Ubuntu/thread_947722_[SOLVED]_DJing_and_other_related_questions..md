### Post by Nielsoerbaek on 2008-10-14
A very nice program is Mixxx ([http://www.mixxx.org/](http://www.mixxx.org/)), which has 2 built-in players, allows synching and pitchshiftning and pitch-stabile tempo-changing. It also works withs tons of controllers and al kinds of stuff.
You should really check it out.

### Post by slugboat on 2008-10-14
You may also want to familiarize yourself with JACK - which is more or less a digital patchbay for your audio cards. For example, JACK could send my mains out to a PCIMCIA addon card and my monitor player to the headphone jack on the laptop. Plenty of info here: [http://jackaudio.org/]("http://jackaudio.org")

DJplay offers the same funcionality of Mixx, but looks a lot better on the default ubuntu desktop. I havn't done anything more than play with DJplay or Mixx however, but they are both in the repos.
