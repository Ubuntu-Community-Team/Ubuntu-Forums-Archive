### Post by krytorii on 2011-08-31
I first learnt some bassic C. Just to get the fundamentals down. It's all nice and easy and you don't have to worry about Objects and stuff to start with.

I am currently learning java, which is real fun. You get to use a piece of software called eclipse as well, which is far better than what I was using before.

[http://www.thenewboston.com/](http://www.thenewboston.com/)

For the java tutorials I'm using. He also hasa  load of others, including C.

Also, don't rush things. Take your time and practice. If you get the first language down nice and good, learning others is relatively easy. It's real tempting to go charging in and watcha  video, do the stuff in said video and move on. The best thing you can do is set small tasks for yourself, and try deviating from the tutorial so you're not just copying everything on screen.


EDIT: Reading what people here say about C, don't get put off. It's not so bad.
