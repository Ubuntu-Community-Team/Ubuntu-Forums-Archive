### Post by boswell on 2011-09-29
I just installed xubuntu and for the most part it is fine. The one thing I am having a problem with is clicking on a desktop icon. If I do the usual left mouse click, nothing happens. I have to do a right-click and if it is a launcher, choose 'execute', and if it is a file or folder, choose 'open'.

It is probably something obvious I am overlooking, but I have gone through the various settings menus, and changed what I dared; but nothing helps.

The os is fully updated - there were about 140 updates ready after I installed and they all went in without a problem.

Thank you for any replies.

### Post by boswell on 2011-09-29
I guess that was one of the dopier problems I have come up with. It turned out that the icons require a double-click, even though I had set Thunar's behavior to require a single-click - I thought that setting extended to all icons, but I guess not.

The other thing was that with the default mouse setting with this os you had to do the two clicks rather close together (at least for my reflexes). By changing the mouse settings, I got them spaced out enough to be comfortable for me.
