> hello 
... when i insert a line of text and am at the end of the line and press escape to enter command mode the cursor moves in one character 
This is normal.

> **DanYoSon said:**
> and i cannot move to the end of the line anymore.Press "a" (lower-case) to **a**ppend after the current cursor position.  Press "A" (upper-case) to append to the end of the line.  Obviously: If the cursor is over the last character on the line, "a" and "A" will have the same effect.

While I'm at it: Press "i" to **i**nsert at the current cursor position (or in front of what's currently under the cursor is another way to think of it).  Press "I" to insert at the beginning of the line.
