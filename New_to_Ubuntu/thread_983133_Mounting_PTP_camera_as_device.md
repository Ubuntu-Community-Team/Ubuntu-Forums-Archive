### Post by isuseu on 2008-12-28
The best way to get access to the card in your camera is to switch your camera to MSC mode or use a separate card reader (which will be recognized as MSC).  

If you can't find a way to switch your camera's mode from PTP to MSC then you might wanna take a look at this : [http://www.dankulp.com/ptpfs/](http://www.dankulp.com/ptpfs/) or this [http://ptp.sf.net](http://ptp.sf.net)
