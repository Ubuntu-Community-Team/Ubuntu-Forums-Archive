AWN requires compositing but not necessarily compiz. If compiz won't work, you could try in-built window manager compositing. Metacity and XFCE window manager (xfwm) both support compositing. You could also try xcompmgr. Google to find out more.

You could also setup a mac-like dock with gnome-do docky. But I'm not sure if it requires compositing. Again, google is your friend.
