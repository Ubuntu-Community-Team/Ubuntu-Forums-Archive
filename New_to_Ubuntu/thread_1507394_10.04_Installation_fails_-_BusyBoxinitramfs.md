### Post by Hospadar on 2010-06-11
You could also try making a live usb that you can install from, if there's something strange with your cd drive that might be better than making a bunch of coasters.

The easiest way is to use unetbootin: [http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)  It'll either download an image for you, or you can choose one on your hard drive (i.e. the one you already downloaded) and it will write everything you need to a usb drive.  I think it might also wipe the usb drive, so be advised if your usb drive has important data.

Then get your bios to boot from usb and see what happens.

This method is my first choice nowadays since it means no wasted cds
