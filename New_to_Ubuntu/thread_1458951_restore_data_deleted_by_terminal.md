### Post by aysiu on 2010-04-20
> **xumuk37 said:**
> It was a bit ritoric question xD) thanks anyway, but I'd like to know where it stores after deleting (something like /trash or /tmp)
It doesn't store it anywhere.

*rm* removes the file.

As I stated in the link above, though, a deleted file is not really gone. Its space is just designated as available. That doesn't mean it's been moved to /tmp or some other directory. It means it's not referenced at all.
