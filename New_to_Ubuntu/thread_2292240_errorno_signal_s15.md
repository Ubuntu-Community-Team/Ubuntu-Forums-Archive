### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-08-26
If the monitor is showing no signal, then it is not getting any video signal from the computer. And there can be several reasons for that. Starting with Monitor switched On - Computer switched Off. I get a no signal message in that situation.

The reference s15 is most likely a monitor reference. So, do a web search for that error message in relation to the make of the monitor. Otherwise, start checking the video cables for breakage. Swap for another cable known to be good. Swap for another monitor known to be working. Swap for another graphic adapter known to be working. Most of us will not have a spare monitor or video adapter but swapping suspect components for known working components is often the best method for identifying failed components .
