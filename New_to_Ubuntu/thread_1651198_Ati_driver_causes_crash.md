### Post by khelben1979 on 2010-12-23
If the ATi driver is the cause of the crash, then you can try and install an older version of the ATi driver or submit a bug report to the ATi developers and inform them!

ATi Radeon 3200 works good with Ubuntu. I tried it myself, but at the time I used Ubuntu Hardy. That version of Ubuntu was released in may of 2008. You can try with a driver from that year instead if you want.

If it's not a driver issue, like if your computer got problems with too much heat inside, then changing graphics driver will not help you!
