### Post by Vaphell on 2011-02-22
behavior of the space between lines growing with the size of the font is normal, because it's proportional to the font size. It's measured in multiples of line height by default. When you select a text and right click on it you can choose that the separator is 1x line, 1.5x line, 2x line. When you dig deeper into the properties of paragraph style (also in the right click menu), there are tabs with much more options that should allow to better fine tune paragraph's behavior, including space size.
