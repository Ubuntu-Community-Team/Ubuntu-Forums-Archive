### Post by robstel on 2009-03-18
IcePodder [http://www.icepodder.com/?page_id=2]("http://www.icepodder.com/?page_id=2") -- basically a Linux port of iPodderLemon/Juice. Development seems to have stalled but I've found that it "does what it says on the tin" and it copes with invalid RSS feeds (all too common) better than most of the alternatives.
