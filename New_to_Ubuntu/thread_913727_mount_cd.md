### Post by john.nicholls on 2008-09-08
You don't mount audio CDs; they don't contain a valid file system and hence can't be mounted.

If you can play DVDs but not CDs, I have seen some reports that faulty drives can sometimes play DVDs and not CDs, or CDs and not DVDs.
