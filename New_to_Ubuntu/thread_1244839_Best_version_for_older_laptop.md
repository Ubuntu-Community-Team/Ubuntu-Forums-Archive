### Post by alexlafreniere on 2009-08-20
I second the nomination for CrunchBang, have it running on a system with just 128MB of RAM and it flies compared to Ubuntu or Xubuntu, but still has the apt package system, which I love. In my experience installing different versions of Ubuntu over the years (since Breezy Badger) I've come up with a general rule of thumb: If the system has 256MB or less of RAM, it gets CrunchBang. Between 256 and 1GB it gets Xubuntu. and 1GB+ gets Ubuntu. Hope this helped.
