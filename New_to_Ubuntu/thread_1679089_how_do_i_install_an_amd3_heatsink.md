### Post by Vaphell on 2011-01-31
I dropped quite a few F-bombs myself when i assembled my quad amd box :)
Yes, it requires some considerable force to hook down both ends of the spring, just make sure that the lock lever is in a correct (loose) position where the available length of the spring is the biggest. Pressing hard to the point of mechanical damage is not advised of course, you need a proper combination of adequate force and technique :)
Do you know someone who is experienced enough with pc building to help you?
