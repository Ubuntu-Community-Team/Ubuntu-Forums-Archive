### Post by Therion on 2009-04-20
You may need to open your Software Sources "Updates" tab and, in the dropdown menu, change the option about what sort of distribution upgrades you will be notified about.  The menu may be set up to only display distribution options for other LTS releases.  You can choose "Normal Releases" however and since Jaunty is a 'normal release" you should get the notification about upgrading.

See this [Illustrative Picture](http://www.refolder.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/01/top5-ubuntu-02.png)!

### Post by mister_pink on 2009-04-20
