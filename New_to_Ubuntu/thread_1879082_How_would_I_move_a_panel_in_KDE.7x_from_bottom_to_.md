### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-11-11
I don't know if the "cashew" is still present at the right-hand end of the panel in 4.7.  If so, click that, or right-click an empty portion of the panel and chooose Panel Options.  Click on the Screen Edge control and drag the panel to where you want it to be.
