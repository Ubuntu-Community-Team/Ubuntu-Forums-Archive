### Post by craig10x on 2014-09-15
Why go to an ubuntu derivative (assuming you otherwise like ubuntu)...when there is a 30 second solution for you:

Go to "System Settings" then click on "Security and Privacy" hit the "search" tab and you will see the following:
When Searching in Dash include Online Results (button is On)  hit OFF...
DONE ;)

PS: I think Stallman is a kook...but that's my opinion...The amazon searches aren't any more spyware then when you get target ads when you use, say, Yahoo search or browse the Linux Mint forums, for example...
But if you turn off the Online Search in ubuntu, then that will easily take care of it for you...

By the way, as of Ubuntu 14.10, i believe the plan is to have that setting OFF by DEFAULT, so one would have to actual turn it on if they want it in future versions of ubuntu...
