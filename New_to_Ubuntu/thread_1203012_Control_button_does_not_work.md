### Post by Prince Valiant on 2009-07-02
Hey-

I've installed the Compiz Config Settings Manager, and after changing several key combinations, I can no longer get basic key combos involving "Control" to work; e.g.  control-z, control-c, etc.  They don't work in Openoffice, and they don't work in Gmail under Firefox.  The control button works in compiz "eyecandy" functions.  Any ideas?  Thanks in advance!

I'm running Ubuntu 8.10.

-Val.

### Post by ubudog on 2009-07-02
Go to System>Prefs>Appearance and go onto the "Visual Effects" tab.  Select one of them - it doesn't matter - and try your control key. 

PS: This will make your Compiz Settings Manager not work anymore until you change a setting.

---

### Post by Prince Valiant on 2009-07-02
Ubudog,

I did what you said, and the control button worked.  I switched a setting in compiz (to activate it) and the button failed.  Then I eliminated the control button from all of the Shift Switcher shortcuts (I was playing around with this when the button failed in the first place) and it worked.  Problem solved!  Thanks a whole lot.  :-)
