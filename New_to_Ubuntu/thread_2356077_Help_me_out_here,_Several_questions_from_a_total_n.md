### Post by Bucky Ball on 2017-03-20
Welcome. 1.4Gb (simply go to [the download page]("https://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop") and hit download; you will be offered a screen asking whether you want to save or open and the size of the ISO is clearly shown there).

Creating an installation CD does not involve the black arts. [See here]("https://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop/install-ubuntu-desktop"), again linked from the downloads page which you'll find[ a link to this ]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootFromCD")which explains CD installs.

If you hit a brickwall, let us know.
