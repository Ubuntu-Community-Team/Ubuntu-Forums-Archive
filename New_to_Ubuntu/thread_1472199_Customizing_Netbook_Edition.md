### Post by ibuclaw on 2010-05-04
You can't show the desktop unfortunately, the key that controls it is mandatory (cannot alter) and set to false.

You can use the go-home applet to add it to your favourites though.

Go to "Files and Folders" and open up your home directory, then click and drag the 'Desktop' icon to the top left corner - so you will see a '+' plus sign over the cursor. Let go and a notification should popup:
"Desktop added to Favourites".
