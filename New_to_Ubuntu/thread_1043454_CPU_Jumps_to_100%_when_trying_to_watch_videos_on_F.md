### Post by kansasnoob on 2009-01-18
Also install Flashblock! Not the one from the repos, it's older and does not work as well.

I know it sounds counter-intuitive, and I can't explain why it works, but in Firefox go to the upper toolbar > Tools > Add-ons > Get Add-ons and search for and install Flashblock 1.5.7.1. (You may have to remove the repo version if already installed)

For me it improved overall Flash performance - including a decrease in CPU hunger.

Of course I'm assuming you already have Firefox 3.05
