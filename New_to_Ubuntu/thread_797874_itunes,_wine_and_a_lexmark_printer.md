### Post by EXCiD3 on 2008-05-18
First of all, dont bother with IRC in pidgin..its missing a LOT of basic functionality and is horrid. Download and install XChat through Add/Remove Programs in the Applications menu instead.

You can use a different media player to play aac formatted music in Ubuntu. Note that files in linux do not matter what extension they have as the types are based off the data inside the files instead. Here is how to enable your aac music to play in Ubuntu along with many other formats/media: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)

You wont be able to use the lexmark software at all with ubuntu. Its written for windows and will only run in windows. You should be able to add your printer via Printing in the System menu. It will take care of installation of drivers for you. Here is a link to get you started with printing and scanning: [https://help.ubuntu.com/8.04/printing/C/index.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/8.04/printing/C/index.html)

Good luck! Just start reading information online and you will get comfortable with linux in no time. Dont be afraid to try new things and mess up your system, just back up your data! ;)
