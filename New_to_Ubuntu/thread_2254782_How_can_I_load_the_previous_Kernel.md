### Post by oldfred on 2014-11-29
Do you not get grub menu?
Or do you only have Ubuntu installed, and then it does not show menu by default.

If BIOS install, you can hold shift key from BIOS until menu appears.
If UEFI you press escape key during or just after UEFI and menu should appear.
