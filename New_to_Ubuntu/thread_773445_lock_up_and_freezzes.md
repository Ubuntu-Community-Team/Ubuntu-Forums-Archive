### Post by shakabra on 2008-04-28
Please don't just go for the power button.  You have several different ooptions.  You can press CTRL+ALT+BACKSPACE, this will log you out.  If that doesn't work try to open a virtual terminal with CTRL+ALT+F1.  Virtual terminals can be open with F1-F7.  Inside the virtual terminal (don't be scared) type "shutdown now -r" without the quotes.  That will reboot your computer.  If none of these options work (and they always have for me) hold 
ALT+SysReq(print screen) and slowly type REISUB.  That will definitely reboot your computer.
