### Post by S2UIRR3L on 2012-01-16
Try the most simple of trouble shooting first. And if that doesn't help, then go into more advanced stuff.

Tweak your screen saver options (I shut mine off completely)

Tweak your standby options (I have my standby option disabled)

Tweak your battery settings (if this is a laptop) and see if there's something you can adjust

I have my computer tweaked to stay on at all times and any type of power settings have been set not to conserve energy, but more for performance instead. I have it set to where I have to manually put my desktop to sleep or shut it down. If not, it will stay on for ever and the screen will not go into screen saver mode or turn off to conserve energy.

Hope this works!
