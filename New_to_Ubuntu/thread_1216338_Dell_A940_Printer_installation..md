### Post by candtalan on 2009-07-18
Welcome to Ubuntu!

I am sorry to be the bearer of probably bad news. Dell use printers from other manufacturers and unless that manufacturer is committed to sharing information with the Linux development community, it can be difficult or impossible to get or create suitable drivers.

The open printing database is very useful, although is not necessarily th every last word:

[http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Dell-all_in_one_A940_printer](http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Dell-all_in_one_A940_printer)

If the information is correct then you find yourself currently 'locked in' to using Windows, unless you can find a suitable printer. For example, most HP printers are fine, and many other makes too. I have had most problems with Lexmark. I think Dell use these, not sure.
