### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-07-22
Done that sort of thing myself, in the past!

Relax! Very easily fixed.

1. Right-click on a vacant section of your taskbar.

2. When the menu opens, Left-click on [COLOR="Blue"]Add to Panel[/COLOR]

3. When the window opens, scroll down to the [COLOR="Blue"]Notification Area item[/COLOR] and Left-click on it.

4. Click on [COLOR="Blue"]Add[/COLOR] and then [COLOR="Blue"]Close.[/COLOR]

5. The Notification Area should now be on your taskbar.

To place it where you want, just Right-click on it and then Left-click on Move.

You can now move it to where you want on the Task Bar.
