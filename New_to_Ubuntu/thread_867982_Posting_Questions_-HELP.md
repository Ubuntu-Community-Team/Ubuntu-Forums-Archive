### Post by H3retic on 2008-07-23
> **munyaradzi said:**
> How do you keep track of your posts? It becomes hard :-(:(

In your user control panel (UserCp, upper left of this page), there is a panel to subscribe to threads on the right column.  By subscribing, you can have emails sent to you when someone responds to your post.

Alternately, you can bookmark it in your browser (ctrl-D), and check back later, which is much easier. :)
