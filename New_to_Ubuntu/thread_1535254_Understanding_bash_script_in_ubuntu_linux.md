### Post by MattBD on 2010-07-20
The best way to understand how Linux works in general is to use it over a long period of time - there isn't really any great secret to it. That said, you do have to constantly challenge yourself to learn something new.

One tip I will pass on is that virtualization is your best friend - try installing various Linux distros in Virtualbox and messing around with them, and you'll soon pick up a fair bit of knowledge, as a virtual machine makes an excellent safe area to tinker with. Once you have a bit more experience, you could try building a Linux from Scratch system.
