### Post by Jahid65 on 2011-01-27
1 Search for driver in System > Administration > Hardware Drivers (it may be broadcam STA or something like that)
2 If you found any, download, install & restart the pc.
3 After restarting, click network icon on the panel. You can see "Create New Wireless Network"
[IMG]http://img522.imageshack.us/img522/6581/screenshot1xd.jpg[/IMG] 

4. Network Name: Whatever you like
Wireless Security: None or WPA & WPA2 Personal
Then click create.
