### Post by drmrgd on 2011-12-23
There should be settings for your shortcuts in the System Settings menu under keyboard.  It might take a little searching around for the Alt + RMB shortcut in there.  Once you find it, though, you can disable it or reassign it in the way you want so that it no longer brings up a menu.
