### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-04-17
I have never even heard of APA format papers, but I have googled, so now do know what it is.

I don't know if this will work for you, but it does do the job as I have just tried it out successfully.  Try using **autocorrect** and add an entry to change a "period plus one space" to "period plus two spaces".  This means that if you use a period as decimal point, for example, an extra space will not be added as it will not already have a space after it.
