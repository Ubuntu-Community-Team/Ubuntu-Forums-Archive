### Post by sandyd on 2011-06-30
Bibble5 works great on a ATI 3800 mobility (1GB).
Also works fine on ATI 3650 mobility (512mb).

Used with a Core2Duo processor (ATI 3650), and a Core2Quad (ATI 3950).

Therefore, any modern video card that you buy should work nicely, as most video cards will be higher than those specs.

The processor will be fine as well.

Note that the Core2Duo + ATI 3650 mobility lags horribly when processing EXTREMELY high quality pictures. Extremely high, as in 40MP.
