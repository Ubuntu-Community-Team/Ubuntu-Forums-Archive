### Post by bumanie on 2009-09-21
As far as I am aware, I would have to answer no to your questions. I don't believe dell sell ubuntu computers outside of North America. Never heard of anyone, getting a refund for windows if they disagree with the licence agreement. I tried to buy a laptop with no OS on it, but no-one would sell me one. There are no laptops of the size you are after with linux pre-installed available within Australia as far as I know. May be you should check out Consumer Affairs in NSW and see what their view would be on supporting you in obtaining a refund in the event of not agreeing to Microsoft's terms and conditions re their licence agreement - in theory, one should not have to pay for a pre-installed OS that they don't want, in practice, I am not sure what practical support you would get from Comsumer Affairs. No harm in asking.
