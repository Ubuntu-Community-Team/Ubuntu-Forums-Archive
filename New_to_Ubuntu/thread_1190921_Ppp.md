### Post by jimmy the saint on 2009-06-18
Look at the bar at the top of your desktop and go to
Applications>Add/Remove programs

type ppp in the search bar and Gnome PPP should be among the top results

Check the box and then click on "apply changes"

Its just that easy!  Getting a modem to work on the other hand can be tricky. If it doesn't just work you may need to get a hardware modem.  Many built in modems are actually winmodems and expect windows to be there to do most of the work.  Obviously if you are running linux, windows isn't present.  You may get luck though so give it a shot.
