### Post by AncientPC on 2008-11-15
The volume keys on my keyboard modify the master sound volume level.  However modifying the master volume does not alter my sound, only the PCM channel does.

So either:
1) How do I link the master volume to adjust sound?
or
2) How do I bind my volume keys to modify PCM sound?

---

### Post by madverb on 2008-11-15
Change the track to control should do it.
Right click the sound manager icon and select Preferences and select PCM.

---

### Post by AncientPC on 2008-11-15
Thanks, I did that and now I can control the PCM channel through the system tray icon but the media keys are still bound to the master channel.

---

### Post by madverb on 2008-11-15
System>Preferences>Sound
You'll see down the bottom is:
> Select the device and tracks to control with the keyboard.
Make sure PCM is highlighted. This worked for me.
Peace.
