### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-08-30
Do you mean you want the whole Pictures folder to open in gthumb automatically when you double click on it in nautilus, or just you want the files in it to open in gthumb instead of image-viewer?

It's easy to do the second of those, but I'm not sure about the first, as it will automatically open all folders with gthumb if you change the Pictures folder.  I assume you are aware of the preferences in gthumb to make the default for it to open at your Pictures folder instead of the root of your home folder.

You can also try right clicking on the Pictures folder in nautilus and choosing "Open with Other Application" and see if that makes the change you want.
