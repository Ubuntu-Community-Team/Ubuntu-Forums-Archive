### Post by Lars Noodén on 2012-04-15
Go with the 10.04 LTS or get a jump start on the 12.04 LTS here:

[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/daily/current/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/daily/current/)

It will be officially released on the 26th but it is near enough complete that you can begin now if you are prepared to to a little extra updating between now and then.  

You'll want to set the stations up as either Kiosk or thin clients, to minimize the maintenance.  Thin client material can be found if you search for LTSP.  A good example of a kiosk can be found here:
[http://www.alandmoore.com/blog/2011/11/05/creating-a-kiosk-with-linux-and-x11-2011-edition/](http://www.alandmoore.com/blog/2011/11/05/creating-a-kiosk-with-linux-and-x11-2011-edition/)

If you want to cut back on hardware, then you could also look at setting up Multiseat systems.  That can be combined with kiosks or LTSP.
