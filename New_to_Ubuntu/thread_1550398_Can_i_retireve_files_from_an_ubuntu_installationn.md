### Post by Thelinuxgeek on 2010-08-11
First of all, the word you're looking for is RETRIEVE not "retireve".
Second of all, you certainly can recover your files!!!
simply put your live disc in the tray, boot ubuntu, when the desktop loads open the file browser. your ubuntu partition will be named ///media, where /// equals the amount of space allocated to your ubuntu partition. for example, my ubuntu partition is 500 GB and my windows is 1.5 TB. so, my ubuntu partition is called 500 GB media. (hopefully you remember how big your ubuntu partition is.) next, just navigate to the folder where your music and movies are and put em on a flash drive or whatever you're gonna do with them. HOPE THIS HELPS YOU!!!!!!!!
