### Post by pofigster on 2008-07-09
Download the icon you want to use from the internet.

Right click on the FF3 launcher and select "Properties"

In the upper left hand corner of the screen will be the icon currenly used by Gnome.  Click on it and it ought to let you select a new icon by browsing the filesystem.
