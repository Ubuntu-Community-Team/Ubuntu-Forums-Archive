Does anyone else notice the behaviour?

### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-12-14
I've certainly never noticed that in my setup, but wonder if you have "smooth scrolling" enabled in preferences. Have a look and if it is, disable it to try that possibility.

Also as a quick way to get to the top of a page just hit your "Home" keyboard button; it will take you there immediately.
