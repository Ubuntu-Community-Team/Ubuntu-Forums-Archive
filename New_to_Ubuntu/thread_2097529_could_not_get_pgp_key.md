could not get pgp key
so i pinged {ping keyserver.ubuntu.com} in terminal
then opened another terminal ,while 1st terminal was pinging {ping keyserver.ubuntu.com}
then i added {sudo add-apt-repository ppa:stebbins/handbrake-releases}
and then it connected
and gave me the key {ppa}


it also worked for sudo /usr/share/doc/libdvdread4/install-css.sh
i pinged {ping packages.medibuntu.org} in the first terminal  and opened second terminal and it downloaded

do not do this to your installation of ubuntu unless you know the risks
