### Post by blazemore on 2009-10-30
Go to [www.ubuntu.com](www.ubuntu.com)

Click Download Ubuntu

Click "Alternative Download Options"

Click "BitTorrent"

Click the version you want (Usually ubuntu-9.10-desktop-i386.iso.torrent)

Open the downloaded .torrent file in your torrent client of choice (Mine's Deluge)
