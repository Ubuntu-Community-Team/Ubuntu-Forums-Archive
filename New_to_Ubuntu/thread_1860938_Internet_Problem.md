### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-10-18
> **kc1di said:**
> What manufacturer?
is it a full modem or win modem?

It's a WiMax modem. It communicates with a WiMax tower located somewhere in the city. It's like mobile broadband. The term "winmodem" only describes dialup modems, not any kind of broadband.

Blackreaper: When you say "it's not detected", exactly what is happening? If it appears on the desktop as a CD, just ignore that behaviour. Click on the Network Manager icon on your top panel and choose Edit Connections...

Then go to the Mobile Broadband tab and click on Add. Follow the directions and hopefully it should work.

I really don't know anything about WiMax support in Ubuntu, so my apologies if my information is wrong.

You might want to check on Google for the model number of the device and the word "ubuntu" to see if there's a particular driver you need to install.
