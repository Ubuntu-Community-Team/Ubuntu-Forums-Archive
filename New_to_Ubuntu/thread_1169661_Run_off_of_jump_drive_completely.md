### Post by rogueleader12345 on 2009-06-15
Format the flash drive, then go into an installed version of Ubuntu or a Live Cd. Go to System->Administration->USB Startup Disk Creator. DO NOT USE THE ISO OFF OF THE CD! Use the iso from where you saved it. Select your flash drive and then click the radio button next to Stored in Reserved Extra Space. Slide the bar to however much space you want to leave open for anything you want to install. Enjoy!
