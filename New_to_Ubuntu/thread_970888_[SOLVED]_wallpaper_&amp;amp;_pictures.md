### Post by FAMUgolfer on 2008-11-04
If you need to move that particular picture to a different folder, then yes it will no longer be in System>Preference>Appearance>Background.

If you are worried that you may lose your desktop settings, icons, etc, then no you will not lose these, just your wallpaper.

Just go back to appearance (or you can right-click on the desktop-->change background) and just add the picture back to the Background location where it will be stored unless you remove it manually (Remove) or again change the location of the picture as you already did above.

Good-luck
