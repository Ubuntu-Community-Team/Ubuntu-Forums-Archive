Just had the same pb
1st you have to restore the Notification Area in the Panel : right-click on the panel then select Notification area .... 
After you have to re-configure each module ie: for the Battery icon, go in System - preferences - energy preferencies (Sorry for the translation  My distro is in spanish). In the genaral tab active the tick box Show always ....
You have to restart Gnome !
