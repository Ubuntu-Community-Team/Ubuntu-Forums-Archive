### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2011-02-10
First, please create new threads in the future for different problems rather than reusing an old one.

Per your original question - I use gFTP, though mounting from Nautilus (e.g. on the desktop from System->Connect to Server) also works.

For your printer problem, if they are sharing the printers through a windows print server, you may need to configure samba to connect using your university credentials.  Check out [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Samba](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Samba)
