### Post by 73ckn797 on 2009-01-29
When you have Xsane open to the scanner panel you should see the place to designate where to save scanned files (see attached screenshot). In mine it is the second row from the top. You may have it set to default directory. There is where you will find the images. You can change the location. It is the section with the floppy disk icon on the left side.

Also make sure to select the form of how it is saved. That is at the top right side. There you can specify whether to save as fax, copy, email and other options.
