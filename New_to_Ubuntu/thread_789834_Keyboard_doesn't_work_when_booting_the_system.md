### Post by warbread on 2008-05-10
This has nothing to do with your OS or drivers.  The problem is before the OS even loads.  In fact, the OS is what's working now.

Try the PS/2 adapter again and see if that gets you into the BIOS.  From there, look for a setting called something like "USB control" and set it to 'BIOS'.  I would also try other keyboards at this point as it may very well just be a bad keyboard.
