### Post by mapes12 on 2009-01-06
Firstly, try to connect with whatever device is installed on your machine. If you need a native Linux supported device then try these guys: [http://www.linuxemporium.co.uk/products/wireless/](http://www.linuxemporium.co.uk/products/wireless/)

I have a Thinkpad T23 and bought (£10) one of their PCMCIA cards to get wifi working which it does faultlessly. Just inserted it and it works! No problems in over a year now. And have run 7.10 and 8.04 and 8.10 with it.
