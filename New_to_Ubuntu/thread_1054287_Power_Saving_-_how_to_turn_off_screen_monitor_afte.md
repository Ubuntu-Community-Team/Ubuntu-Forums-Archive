### Post by mc4man on 2009-01-30
you can adjust this all from the gui. There's a relationship between 'regard the computer as idle' in preferences -> screensaver and the preferences -> power management times.

The min time in power management is 'time to idle' + 1 min (may actually be 30 sec
By adjusting both you can get the display sleep time you want.
