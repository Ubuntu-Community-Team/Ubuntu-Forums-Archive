### Post by Docaltmed on 2011-12-09
What type of files are you trying to play? Are you talking about streaming audio from the internet (thus the firefox connection)?

If you are using Banshee or Rhythmbox to play your audio files, the status of Firefox should make no difference whatsoever. 

If you are talking about playing flash stuff, first thing I would do is get the Flash-Aid addon for Firefox and put it to work.

And if you are playing files through Clementine, just make sure you have the "All Glory to the Hypnotoad" box unchecked. Seriously. That one had me buffaloed for two days once.
