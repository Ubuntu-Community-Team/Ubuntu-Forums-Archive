### Post by pipyui on 2008-12-25
**Story:**
I'm brand new to ubuntu (Im using eeebuntu on my new eee i got for christmas). I've spent all day trying to figure out why my wireless internet died after updating on my supported Atheros card. After reading many tips and fixes that all sounded to complicated for my very low level of expertise, so I tried figuring out myself.

**What I did to fix it:**
Turns out after the system update, when I checked my user privileges, I wasn't granted access to wireless internet anymore! After changing that and rebooting, internet works fine. Clean and simple fix.
