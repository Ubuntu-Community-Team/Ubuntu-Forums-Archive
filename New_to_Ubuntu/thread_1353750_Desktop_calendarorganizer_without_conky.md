### Post by onamattuphere on 2009-12-13
I have to say, I have tremendous respect for all the people who are diligently scripting and making conky so amazing. But getting involved with conky is a daunting task when you have very little experience with scripting/programming, or when it is enough of a struggle to just set up Ubuntu the way you want it. I.e: Me=Ubuntu-n00b

If you're looking for a calendar with an organizer, which also stays visible on your desktop; look no further ..

[http://www.rainlendar.net/cms/index.php?option=com_rny_download&Itemid=32](http://www.rainlendar.net/cms/index.php?option=com_rny_download&Itemid=32)

It is easily my favorite calendar since I can always tell what's up and coming, and features alarm functions as well.
You can also change skins to personalize it.

Have a look at my desktop. Not really amazing, but functional and personalized.
