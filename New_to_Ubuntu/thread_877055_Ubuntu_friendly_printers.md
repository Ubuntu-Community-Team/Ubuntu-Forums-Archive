### Post by timcredible on 2008-08-01
between myself, friends, and relatives, i've connected about 11 HP printers to linux machines, and I've never had a problem with any of them - most of them are printer/scanner/copiers, and a couple were printer/scanner/copier/fax machines.  the last few ubuntu releases automatically setup the printer with no interaction from the user.  scanning works on all of them, and my current printer, the c5280, even the cd/dvd printing worked with no effort (although i did have to work on the margins a little to get it just right).  when you get low on ink with a hp printer, ubuntu has a pop up box that alerts you to that fact.  hp toolbox works too.  so, as you can tell, i like hp printers.  they also last many years too - i haven't had one die yet.  and i only paid $89 for the c5280.
