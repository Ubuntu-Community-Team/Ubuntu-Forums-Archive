### Post by keith11 on 2008-04-27
I will be answering my own question about whether the KDE 4 version is worth it or not. According to what I saw after installation of KDE 4, it is missing some basic features right away; there is no "Refresh" option in the context menu when you click on the desktop, the taskbar panel has a limited number of applets and portions of the taskbar could not be moved to accommodate the applets.

There were minor annoyances like these which one would think would be gotten rid of in a superior desktop environment. I also ended up losing the taskbar once the system crashed and could never get it back althought I tried a lot through research. SO, I finally installed the KDE 3.9 version of Kubuntu 8.04 and it is so much better. The features or leack thereof in KDE 4 right away, that I mentioned could be installed with some effort, but I don't know. Anyways, I just wanted to put this out for someone like me who would be wondering if it was worth installing KDE 4.

Keith.
