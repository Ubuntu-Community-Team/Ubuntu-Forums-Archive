### Post by Paqman on 2011-01-19
Just a guess, but i'd imagine they're referring to a pending kernel for Maverick. If so, you'll receive it in due course as part of your normal updates. 

You might get it sooner if you enable "proposed", but bear in mind that doing a full update with that enabled will pull down a lot of packages that might be a little green. I'd try enabling "proposed", checking for a kernel update in Synaptic and just installing that, then disabling "proposed".
