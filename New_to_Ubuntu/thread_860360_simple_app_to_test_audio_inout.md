### Post by silkstone on 2008-07-15
You may find that the Recording Level Monitor is already installed, although it may not show in the Sound & Video menu.

Right-click on Applications, select Edit Menus, click on Sound & Video in the left pane, and check the Recording Level Monitor box. It should then appear in the menu.
