### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-12-19
> **Bucky Ball said:**
> You could try Pitivi Video editor. Change 'Project' settings and export. I would use Brasero for burning DVD once you have your converted file.

Better idea: Take your AVI file and feed it straight into Brasero. It can convert it to DVD format on-the-fly and burn it straight away.

There's no such thing as a "pal" or "ntsc" extension as these are types of actual video signal. Brasero should give you the option of whether to burn a disc as PAL or NTSC anyway, because video files aren't guaranteed to be either of those.
