### Post by blueridgedog on 2009-05-17
To really get a handle on what you need, you should estimate the load your application will have, based on the overhead as determined by the estimated traffic and the processing power needed by your application on a per transaction basis.  The short answer is most any hardware will work with little demand, but high demand may push you into big iron realms.
