### Post by PierreDeKat on 2008-10-20
What Miljet said.

Also, the way that your preferences may have been set on a particular computer is stored in a Yahoo cookie on that computer. And if that cookie gets manually deleted or damaged, Yahoo may go to a default setup.

One other thing, that webpage you have attached looks damaged to me, like perhaps it was supposed to have a cascading style sheet linked to it, but the CSS was linked incorrectly, or something along those lines.

Such a problem may clear up, once the folks at Yahoo figure it out (or you tell them).
