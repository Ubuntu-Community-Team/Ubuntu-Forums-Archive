### Post by colobix on 2010-03-14
Try kdenlive. it's in synaptic. 
it works like a charm and reminds me of Sony Vegas.
Or for more professional editing you can try Cinelerra.
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cinelerra](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cinelerra)

To install, add the following to your software sources.
9.10 Karmic Koala:
Code:

deb [http://akirad.cinelerra.org](http://akirad.cinelerra.org) akirad-karmic main

9.04 Jaunty Jackalope:
Code:

deb [http://akirad.cinelerra.org](http://akirad.cinelerra.org) akirad-jaunty main

And you should also wait 3 days for the release of Lucid.

Or you can try OpenShot: [http://www.openshotvideo.com/](http://www.openshotvideo.com/)
