### Post by Galgotha on 2012-09-24
Nomad,
 
I am not sure what games you are looking to play, you will have to list those you are looking to play see if there is Linux compatible appliciation available you will not be able to directly install your windows games into Linux OS. 
 
As for Win 7 you can get OEM Win 7 32 or 64bit off Amazon for 90 bucks. The OEM version is locked to one Motherboard but most times that is all a user needs. I would actually recommend Win 7 64 over XP Home. There are vast improvements in gaming experience much more stable OS enviroment, and gaming compaines are finally moving to 64bit clients ever so slowly for example if you play WoW you can use thier 64bit client gaming experience was greatly improved for me on my system.
