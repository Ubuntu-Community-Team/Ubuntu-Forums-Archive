### Post by iaculallad on 2008-08-27
> **Mr-Axel said:**
> When iopen my Drives

it's appear on my desktop

it's normally or ahave problem?

It's just normal for a drive to appear in the Desktop when it gets mounted. If you want, you can change the default action for mounted drives to show on your Desktop, to Hide it: Press Alt+F2, and input "gconf-editor" (w/o quotes) and press Enter. Navigate to Apps->Nautilus->Desktop, and uncheck "Volumes_Visible".
