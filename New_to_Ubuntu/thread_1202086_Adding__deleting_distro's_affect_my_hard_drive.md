### Post by DGortze380 on 2009-07-01
It simply increases the number of read/writes you've made to the drive...

Theoretically, it will decrease the life of the drive... every read/write causes a certain amount of wear to moving parts...

... that being said, I wouldn't worry about it. The difference in the life of the drive will be somewhere between non-existent and negligible.

If you're still concerned, pick up a used 20GB drive on ebay and pop it in to do your 'tests', then install the distro you choose on your regular drive.
