### Post by sloggerkhan on 2008-09-27
vlc, mplayer (smplayer frontend is best), and xine are really the 3 choices on linux.
Of them, xine is best for DVD playback (best menu support), and mplayer is best for formatted subtitles. VLC and mplayer (mencoder) are both good for encoding/reencoding video and audio, vlc tends to have a few more features for streaming than the others, and you can to the encoding stuff w/gui rather than command line.

Otherwise, it's your choice.
