### Post by bergfly on 2010-08-31
Just a heads up, during the install, it will ask you if you want to gdm or kdm as the display manager. Although there is very little difference between the two and you can use either one with either gnome or KDE, if you use KDM you will keep the KDE login page, if you choose gdm you will get the ubuntu login screen.
