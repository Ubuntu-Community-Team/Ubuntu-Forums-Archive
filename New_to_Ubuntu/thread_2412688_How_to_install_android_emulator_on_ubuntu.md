### Post by bitsnpcs on 2019-02-23
Hello Zulfi,

**Android Studio** is available to download in Ubuntu Software.
Its website shows the emulator, and IDE, [URL="https://developer.android.com/studio/"]https://developer.android.com/studio/

[/URL]To install it you x1 left click to highlight your VirtualBox machine of Ubuntu, and on the top menu click **Start**, to boot it up. 
When you are on your Virtual Ubuntu Desktop click the Orange Dash Icon to open the Ubuntu Software.
In the search bar type **Android Studio**, click it in the results, and then click install, once completed it is now ready to use.

I think you will find a lot more tutorials for Android Studio.
To help you get started here is a YouTube beginners series of 4 videos. 
[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dFlPARW5IX8&list=PLp9HFLVct_ZvMa7IVdQyUUyh8t2re9apm](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dFlPARW5IX8&list=PLp9HFLVct_ZvMa7IVdQyUUyh8t2re9apm)
