### Post by dancresswell on 2008-04-25
You can try switching your respository to a better one in Synatpic package manager.  I had the problem with the defaults until I did this and now its flying! Once inside the package manager, click "Settings" -> Repositories.  On the "download from" box, click "other".  A box will come up with all the repositories available.  Click the button "Select best server".  It will take a minute or two to contact them all and will automatically select the fastest one from the test.  Click "choose server" and close the window.  Hit reload in the main window and continue as you please.

If this doesn't resolve your problem, you can always reset the repository back to the default.

Cheers.
