To answer your questions...1.  Yes, that card should do just fine..I am currently running a nVidia 8800 GTX OC, I know it is a step up from that, but you will use the same drivers.  2.  As far as if you should use Gnome or KDE---that is a personal preference....personally I LOVE Gnome, and really don't like KDE all that much, but you will find people who are the opposite.  and 3.  YES, in my opinion you should (when possible) put XP on a separate HDD from Ubuntu (Gnome plug...:))  I have found from personal experience that it saves you from many headaches later on if you want to further partition your drive...in addition, Windows likes to be the "main" OS....and I have run into issues after windows updates and booting into Ubuntu, so I would recommend putting the two on different HDD.  

I hope this helps, and Good luck!
