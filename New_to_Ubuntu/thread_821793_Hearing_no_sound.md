### Post by Nepherte on 2008-06-07
The X-Fi line of Creative will not work by default. You can either try to install the driver as delivered on the Creative site or you try OSSv4 which now includes beta drivers for the X-Fi cards: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=780961](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=780961) I got my X-Fi extreme music working with the latter option.
