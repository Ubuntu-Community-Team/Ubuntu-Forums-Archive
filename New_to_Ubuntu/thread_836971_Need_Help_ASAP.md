### Post by AndThenWhat on 2008-06-22
Since you already have most of your hard drive full and it would be a bad idea to resize it now, I would recommend you just get Wubi (The Windows-Ubuntu-Installer): [http://wubi-installer.org/](http://wubi-installer.org/)

It will take a long time to download the ISO, but this program allows you to install Ubuntu under windows as though it is a Windows program.  That is a much more simple approach and you take almost zero risk of ruining your windows partition.
