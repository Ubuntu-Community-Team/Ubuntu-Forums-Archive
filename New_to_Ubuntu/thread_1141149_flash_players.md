### Post by gandaran on 2009-04-28
both are exactly the same adobe flash plugin, I wouldn't recommend installing flash from adobe.com for one simple reason, you will not get updates for this package, installing flash from the repository you will get the update when adobe releases a new flash version.
if you are using ubuntu 8.04 hardy then it's better to install flash 10 from adobe.com because for some reason (due to some bugs in 8.04) adobe flash 9 will not be updated to flash 10, flash 10 will not work if you have flash 9 installed.
