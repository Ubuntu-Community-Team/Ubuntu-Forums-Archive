### Post by Ebere on 2010-04-11
If you have tried posting here, and when you hit the submit button, or the preview button...

You are brought back to the 'post' window, (It is now empty. Your entire post is gone), and given an error message that says something like:

"Your message is not long enough. It has to contain at least one character"

If you have installed "No-Script" in your firefox and have marked "yahooapis" as untrusted...

Give yahooapis permission to run. 

You'll be able to post again.
