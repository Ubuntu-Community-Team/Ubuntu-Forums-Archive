sudo aptitude install ubuntu-desktop xubuntu-desktop kubuntu-desktop 

That will give you all 3 (obviously you can exclude whichever one you already have). When you log in, choose which one you want from Sessions. The only drawback is that your application menus will be a little more crowded.
