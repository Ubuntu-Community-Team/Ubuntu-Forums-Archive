### Post by growingneeds on 2012-04-26
Hi,

Please do not upgrade at this point in time. It is reflected in the release notes that > It is generally recommended that users of Ubuntu 10.04 LTS wait until the first point release, due in July, before upgrading. I am assuming that they will be addressing 10.04.4 to 12.04.1 migration bugs. If you wish to use 12.04, please perform a backup and do a clean installation.

Also, once you upgrade, you would lose the GNOME 2 interface. I recommend that you try the Unity interface off the Live CD first. It'll give you a heads-up on what to expect before upgrading.
