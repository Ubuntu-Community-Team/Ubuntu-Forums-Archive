### Post by deadflowr on 2013-09-01
> **kevin_arksey said:**
> so what bit torrent can i use and how do you get them to play

Ubuntu comes with transmission, which is a bit-torrent client.

You can play the downloaded files just like any other file by double clicking it in the folder it was downloaded into.
The default folder torrents download into is the Download folder.

There are other torrent clients available both in the ubuntu software center and through out the web, if transmission doesn't fit your needs.
