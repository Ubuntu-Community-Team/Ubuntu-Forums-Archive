### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-05-03
If tweaking RecordMyDesktop doesn't work, then you may be able to try out glc, it's an openGL capture application that works quite well, but is a little hard to install, capture, and convert into a handy format.
[http://nullkey.ath.cx/projects/glc/](http://nullkey.ath.cx/projects/glc/)
- I've used glc to capture sauerbraten (openGL game), it works very well, particularly on a resolution of 1280x1024 or less.
