### Post by gdonwallace on 2011-02-04
Since reformatting will erase everything on your laptop, the easy way is to boot from the Live CD, and when it comes up choose install Ubuntu and when it gets to the partition part of the install, choose entire disk.  This will reformat and reinstall Ubuntu for you
