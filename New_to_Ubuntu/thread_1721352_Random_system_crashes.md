### Post by Kirboosy on 2011-04-04
It might be the hard drive. I had a computer one time that would run perfectly fine until you bumped the system. That little disturbance in the hard drive spin would cause it to crash and do the exact same things. (and this was with Windows). I popped a new hard drive in it and reinstalled everything. Everything was fine after that.

Fortunately hard drives are relatively cheap. If its under warranty its even cheaper. :)
