### Post by Smiletastic on 2012-11-16
Hallo Michael und danke für deine Antwort. Das Problem ist schon gelöst! The problem is fixed. To solve the problem I did two things: 
1) In 'Sound Settings' I muted 'Sound effects' - that was the main reason of the noise.
2) I also tried another microphone. Some guys in a computer shop told me that there are very sensitive mics which always produce noise unless u suppress it. The fact is that even after switching off 'Sound effects' I had some noise though it was much weaker.

So, after doing these two things I finally:P managed to record sound.
Thank u once again.
