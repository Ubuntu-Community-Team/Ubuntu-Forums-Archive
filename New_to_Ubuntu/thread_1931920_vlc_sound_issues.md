### Post by papibe on 2012-02-26
A couple of ideas:

- If the audio is 5.1, you may be listening just a couple of channels. I don't know about VLC, but SMplayer can downmix pretty well, and XBMC even better

- Regardless of that, you may benefit of 'Volume Normalization'. Look for that option on Tools -> Preferences -> Audio.

Hope it helps.
