### Post by capnthommo on 2009-04-27
hi myggsyti
a couple of thoughts occur to  me.
have the top and/or bottom bars on the screen disappeared?  if so do you have a small (probably grey) square at any of the corners of the screen?  if so then left click on it - that should restore the bars.
can you see the trash bin icon at the bottom right hand corner of the screen and the (probably) 2 rectangles on the right of this?  if so then click on the rectangles one after the other.  this is a way of selecting between the 2 desktop screens - its a way of having more than one desktop at one time - kind of like having two actual desks.
maybe these arent the answers but i thought it worth checking.
