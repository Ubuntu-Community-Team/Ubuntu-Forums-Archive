### Post by kostkon on 2009-02-09

Thanks guys!
You need to download the .deb from its site, since the version that exists in the repositories is very old.

Thus, [go to the download page on the site]("http://deluge-torrent.org/downloads.php"), and download the deb for 8.10. Then double click on it to install it.

Or you can add [its *PPA*]("https://launchpad.net/~deluge-team/+archive") (i.e. repository) and get automatic updates every time there is a new version.

If you want to add the *PPA* and you don't know how, don't hesitate to ask here for help.

Hope that helps.
