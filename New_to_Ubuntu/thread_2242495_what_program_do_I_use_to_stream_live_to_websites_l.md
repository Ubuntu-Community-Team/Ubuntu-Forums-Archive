### Post by Urbanmasque on 2014-09-02
I would use OBS [[https://obsproject.com/]](https://obsproject.com/]) or Simple Screen Recorder.  I'm not too familiar with VaughnLive but if they give you an RTMP URL and Stream key in order to stream to your player you can input those into either OBS (which has a screen capture functionality) or Simple Screen Recorder (available for download in the Software Center).

Hope this helps.
