### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-05-17
Hi,

It sounds like you don't have a Window List. Right-click the panel, choose **Add to Panel** and scroll down until you see the **Window List**. Add that to the panel, then Right-click the little dotted-line to the left, choose **Move** and position it as you prefer.

That will give you a row of buttons for the apps you have open.


Regards,

Didius
