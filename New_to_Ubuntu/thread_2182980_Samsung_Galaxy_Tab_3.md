### Post by ricoris on 2013-10-23
Mm I think your Ubuntu is set to automatically reproduce the songs in your USB device, that's why Rythmbox, which is the default software to do this task opens. You should try pausing Rythmbox, opening your Home folder and looking on your left, where there should be a list of the main folders, including the folder of your USB device with all its subfolders. Depending on the kind of USB you are using, it may be the case that it is not totally compatible with Ubuntu, although this is not frequent. Could you give more information? What model of USB is it? What ubuntu version are you using?
