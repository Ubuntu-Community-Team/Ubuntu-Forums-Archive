A good idea would be to talk to the guys in #ubuntu-motu on Freenode. They will till you everything you need to know and someone will probably be willing to guide you through making your first patch. Another place to look is at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU/GettingStarted](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU/GettingStarted).

@Jay Li: MOTU means "Master Of The Universe", which means "an Ubuntu developer that can upload directly to the Universe component of the repositories". (Universe is 99% of everything you can install from the repositories, that isnt on an official ubuntu cd). Basically its an Ubuntu developer.
