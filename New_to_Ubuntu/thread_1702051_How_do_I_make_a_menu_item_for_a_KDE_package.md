### Post by Catbells on 2011-03-07
I've installed KMines and enjoyed playing it.  It's part of the KDE games module.  It doesn't appear on the Applications menu and was wondering if it would be possible to add it to a menu or a panel.  

I have a drawer.  I tried a right-click, selected 'Add to Drawer,' and added a custom launcher but it couldn't find KMines that I'd used as the command.

---

### Post by oldos2er on 2011-03-07
Use **whereis kmines** to find it; most likely it's in /usr/bin
