### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-01-21
As I understand it...

If you are making a copy for yourself, you can do what you want.
If you are making an unmodified copy for general distribution, you just need to make sure it includes where others can find source.
If you are distributing modified copies, you need to include your source, or an offer to give people source on request.

For much more accurate info, see:
[http://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-faq.html](http://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-faq.html)
