### Post by DuckHook on 2013-10-03
What do you mean by "sleep mode"? Ubuntu has only two modes in which computer activity is actually turned off: *suspend* and *hibernate*. Of the two, *hibernate* is disabled and you must take special steps to reactivate it. Therefore, most computers can only *suspend* and must actually be suspended before activity ceases.

Many people have their power settings set to turn their monitor off after X minutes of inactivity. The monitor is in "sleep mode", but it's only the monitor. The computer is still as active as ever.
