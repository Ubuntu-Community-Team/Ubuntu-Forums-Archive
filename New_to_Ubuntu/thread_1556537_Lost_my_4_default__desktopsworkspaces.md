### Post by bergfly on 2010-08-23
Shouldn't need a bash command. The desktops are in the "system settings"  menu, in the top group (look & feel) under "desktop". 

Once in desktop, select "multiple desktops" from the left hand menu and you can set the number there.

Hope it helps and you enjoy KDE
