### Post by SNWOLF on 2008-08-16
Cool! I've finally found a solution, after doing another google search, and that solution can be found at this page: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/7429](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/7429)

The add-on is called Hide Unvisited 3, and it works! It removes Bookmarks that you haven't visited since clearing out the History. Perfect.
