### Post by Bölvaður on 2008-09-01
Make sure you burn the cd at low speed to avoid data corruption.

Also when you are on the live-cd find a program called Partition Editor (gparted if you type in terminal to bring it up or use alt+f2). There you can make partitions on your harddisk.

This is a recomended setup for most people:

"Swap" (debatable size, 50% to 150% of your ram is mostly used)
"/" (root aka filesystem) have it on the smaller harddisk, this is where your system and your programs will be installed.
"/home" is where all your personal data (and user preferences for your programs and stuff) so you might want the big harddisk for that one.

Btw keep coming to the forums, even after you've stopped being angry at MS :)
