### Post by harfa on 2009-11-24
Not in a reliable or easy way, no
you can backup your home folder before reinstalling 8.10 and put it back into your home afterwards, but you might lose settings, if they weren't in your dot files in your home.

I would advise you to just back up the files you want to save, and reinstall, unless you like pain.

trying to figure out why something is broken after doing something unsupported (downgrading) is a series of pointless struggle.

sorry

next time back up your system in a way you can completely rollback.
lesson learned?
