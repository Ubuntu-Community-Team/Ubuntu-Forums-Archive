### Post by anewguy on 2011-09-17
Just to test this, I installed camorama and tested it with an old Creative Live! USB web camera.  When I looked through camorama, there is no option to record video, only to take snapshots.

As mentioned by trozamon, cheese supports video capture.  The only thing I have no clue about is the motion-sensing part of this, unless your camera does that automatically.  But I don't think that will make any difference, as cheese still expects a manual start and stop recording from you.  So, motion detection then recording, and stopping when motion stops, probably is not an option with this either.
