### Post by undecim on 2010-01-12
To read any sensor from the command line, have a look at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SensorInstallHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SensorInstallHowto)

However, it's not possible to read your power supply temperature unless you buy a heat sensor and place it on your power supply. (unless you have a fancy power supply that has a sensor built in) Though please DO NOT put a new sensor IN your power supply.
