### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-09-14
To answer your questions:

1. Unity will most likely be the default in 12.04 LTS, but the development for 11.10 hasn't even finished yet; so it's not 100% certain and confirmed.

2. Your graphics card should be supported with Unity. My graphics card is an older generation than yours and it's fine. I was having a problem with Unity on 11.04, which appears to be fixed on 11.10; so maybe give 11.10 a try. Give it a week.

3. There is also a 2D version of Unity in 11.10 (and 11.04's repositories) that will work no matter what graphics card you have.

4. You won't be able to make it look and behave exactly like classic Gnome, but the gnome-fallback-session in 11.10's repositories (and presumably 12.04's) gets reasonably close
