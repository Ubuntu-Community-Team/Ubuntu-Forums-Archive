Do you want a global shortcut or an app shortcut?

To set a shortcut in amarok:

[list=a]
[*]Open amarok
[*]Click settings
[*]Click configure shortcuts
[*]Scroll/Search to/for Play/Pause (better than just play IMO)
[*]
[*]For a **global** shortcut:
[list=1][*]click on Shortcut
[*]Select global
[*]Press the button next to it
[*]Put in your key combo
[/list]
[*]Press OK to exit
[*]It should now work, if it does not restart amarok
[/list]

edit the second: you can specify just pause if you want but then you'd have to have another shortcut for play or go into amarok
