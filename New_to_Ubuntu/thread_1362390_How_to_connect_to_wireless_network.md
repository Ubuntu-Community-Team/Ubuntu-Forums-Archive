### Post by plusnplus on 2009-12-23
Hi jonglah,
1. it is more easy if you try in wireless network zone area.
2. make sure your wireless card/device is on.
3. click wireless network icon (on the right hand side of upper status bar)
4. you should see one or some wireless network available.
5. if not automaticly connect to your pc, choose one and you will be connected.
