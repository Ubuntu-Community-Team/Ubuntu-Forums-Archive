### Post by mbcrui on 2010-01-07
Well, it's taken me most of a week and many people giving suggestions, but I fixed it.  <buffing nails on chest> I used to be a damned good admin before I had the lobotomy and became a manager and no one would let me play anymore. </bnoc>  :D.

Here's how it goes: 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=8302](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=8302)
This works up to the 5th step.  But then, you have to also add a Modeline.

Modeline can be generated by *cvt  *


[INDENT]>cvt 1280 1024
[/INDENT][INDENT]# 1280x1024 59.89 Hz (CVT 1.31M4) hsync: 63.67 kHz; pclk: 109.00 MHz Modeline "1280x1024_60.00"  109.00  1280 1368 1496 1712  1024 1027 1034 1063 -hsync +vsync

[/INDENT]Copy the Modeline information (both lines) and paste it under the Horisync and Vertrefresh lines.

The continue with the HOWTO directions.
