### Post by snakeman21 on 2009-07-05
There IS a solution.  If you go to [THIS LINK]("http://www.macromedia.com/support/documentation/en/flashplayer/help/settings_manager06.html"), you will be at your settings manager for adobe.  Kind of like your driver's seat for your flash player.  In the "visited websites" box, find the site you are trying to change the settings for.  Customize your settings, then close the tab and refresh the page you were having trouble with.  Your new settings should automatically be applied.
