### Post by Old_Man_Unix on 2011-01-25
You don't say which version or derivative you are using, so I'm assuming you don't have the codecs preinstalled.

You can get most of the codecs you need by simply installing the "restricted-extras" package  for your derivative.  Additional packages will be needed to decode and play DVDs.

It's all covered here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)
