### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-06-26
You will need to highlight the email message text and copy/paste that to open office.  Then go back to the email and save the attachment to disk.  Now depending on what the attachment file type is you can also copy that to the open office file, or if it's a picture you will need to insert it into the open office file.  Now save the open office file as a .doc, and there you are.

### Post by Golem XIV on 2008-06-26
