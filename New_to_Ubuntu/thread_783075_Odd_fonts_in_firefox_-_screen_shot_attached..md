### Post by otrojake on 2008-05-05
The way I have it set is to not allow sites to choose their own fonts.  Go to edit>preferences in Firefox.  Move to the Content tab and where it says "Fonts & Colors" click on the Advanced button.  In the window that comes up uncheck "Allow pages to choose their own fonts, instead of my selections above."  It's an ugly workaround and some sites will be distorted depending on what font you go with.  I'm using bitstream vera sans at 15 because I like the look of the font the most out of the ones in Ubuntu, but I do run into some issues with it from time to time.
