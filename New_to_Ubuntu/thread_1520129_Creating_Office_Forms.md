### Post by rmockler on 2010-06-29
I had an office forms need very similar to what you describe, and here is how I resolved it.  Open the OpenOffice Word Processor, click on Help, OpenOffice.org Help, and then in the search argument field key in forms.  Down near the bottom you will see XForms.  Double click on XForms and the Help text will open up. There is a learning curve for XForms, but with a little time and effort, you can do some amazing stuff with it.  You can create a form, then fill in the values interactively, or just print the form out and write them manually.
Good Luck...
