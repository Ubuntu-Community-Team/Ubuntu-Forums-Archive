### Post by dwhite on 2011-04-04
right click on the bottom panel (you have to click in an unoccupied region of the panel or you will simply get a menu for the particular device in the panel) once you do that select "add to panel" and you will get a window from which you can add either "window list" or "window selector" I like window selector because it uses a menu.  i've attached a screenshot of the "add to panel" window
