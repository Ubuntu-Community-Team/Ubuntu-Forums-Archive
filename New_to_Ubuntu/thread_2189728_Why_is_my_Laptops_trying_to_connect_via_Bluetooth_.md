### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-11-23
> **kuv02 said:**
> I once had my phone and my laptop connected via Bluetooth. From this day on, on every start/wake up my laptop trys to connect to the phone and will fail to connect error messages and notification boxes.
This is so annoying. How can I stop my laptop from trying to connect? Why is it trying to connect at all!?

If the phone is shown as paired in the Bluetooth preferences panel in Settings, you could unpair (delete) it.

if you don't need bluetooth at all on the laptop, disable it in the same Panel.

Once a system is paired with another device, it will seek to connect with it each time the system restarts.
