### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2015-11-02
Folder view is dependant on zoom percent chosen (in edit>preferences) AND in monitor resolution.   It's a lot different on a 1920 X 1280 than it is in 1024 x 768 for example.   Also, Nautilus is not the only file tool in Linux.   There are many, many others such as "Midnight Commander" . . . which is one of the most flexible and displays tons of info per screen.   This is really kind of a non-issue if you check out all the alternatives.
