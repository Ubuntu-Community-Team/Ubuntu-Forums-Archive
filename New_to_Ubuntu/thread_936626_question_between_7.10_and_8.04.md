### Post by Paqman on 2008-10-03
> **jdcnosse said:**
> 
How do you all feel about 7.10 vs 8.04?

For a server, I wouldn't think you'd notice much difference. Most of the changes introduced in Hardy were more desktop oriented. One notable improvement for servers would be ufw, which is a simplified system for managing the firewall.

Check out the [Hardy release notes](http://www.ubuntu.com/testing/hardy/beta) for more info about the differences.

Of course, Hardy is also Long Term Support release, whereas Gutsy support ends in a few months.
