### Post by RedRat on 2009-02-12
Now this is coming from someone who has not yet upgraded to 8.10 yet. I run 8.04LTS. Depends on what you are upgrading from. Here and in the newsgroups, I have heard that there are problems with the upgrade, e.g., lost sound, video drivers, wireless, etc. Now that was some months ago and I presume 8.10 has now matured so that many of these early problems have been overcome. But if you really do not need to upgrade, I would advise against it until you get a little more Ubuntu under your belt. If you have a stable installation, e.g., 8.04, you may just get more troubles than benefits.
