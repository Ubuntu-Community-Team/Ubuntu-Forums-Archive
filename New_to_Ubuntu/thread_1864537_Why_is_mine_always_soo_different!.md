### Post by feedmebits on 2011-10-19
If you have compiz installed to rotate the cube you press ctrl+alt and then left click and move your mouse around and the cube wil rotate. You can add your own wallpapers if you go to configcompiz settings manager under settings, and search for wallpaper. You can also change more settings there to your like. And you can Als go to settings Simple CompizConfig Manager to set a default configuration. I set mine to Ultimate and then to change specific settings you can change those under settings--> CompizConfig Manager. Hope that helps?
