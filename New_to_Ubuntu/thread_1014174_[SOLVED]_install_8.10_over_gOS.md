### Post by adult swim on 2008-12-17
that shouldnt be a problem.  whenever you are installing and you get to the partitioner, dont choose any of the guided options.  choose the manual option and then click next.  on the screen that comes up, double click your gOS partition and mark it to be formatted and choose / as the mountpoint.
