### Post by Mike_IronFist on 2009-09-16
> **zebraneo said:**
> I am trying to install my printer, and I get a message the the printer is not connected. the Printer is Brother MFC-210c. What do I do

Where are you getting the message? On the printer? On your computer?

If your computer is telling you there's no printer connected, go to "Administration -> Printing" and in the Printer config window, click "Server -> New -> Printer" and then the rest should be easy.

### Post by plucky on 2009-09-16
> **zebraneo said:**
> I am trying to install my printer, and I get a message the the printer is not connected. the Printer is Brother MFC-210c. What do I do

Open Synaptic Package Manager and search for MFC-210C.Install both 
the LPR and Cupswrapper drivers.

Connect and power on printer,then go to **System > Administration > Printing** and select "New Printer".Search and install the correct driver.

You should then be able to print.


Good Luck
