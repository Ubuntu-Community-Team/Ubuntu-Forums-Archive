### Post by lordadi on 2008-07-25
Hi,

For evolution you can do (inside evolution) File > Backup settings and go through with the wizard. This will, AFAIK, backup mail, acct settings and calendar.

Once updated, just go to file > restore settings and find that *.tar.gz that was created.
