When you say FF do you mean your browser (firefox) when accessing webmail or Thunderbird as in Mozilla ? because FF has nothing to do with that, auto responses are done generally on a email account basis, so if you have a gmail account you need to go into your gmail settings to set up a auto-response or out of office reply, but it depends on the email server.

Gmail for example offers a canned response for junk mail which you could utitlise for a per person basis, you need to look at the filter options for your specific email account
