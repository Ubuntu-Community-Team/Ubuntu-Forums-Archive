### Post by freeman2000 on 2009-05-28
The HP printer will work on 8.04 up to 9.10 alpha.  Also go to System --> Admin --> Synaptic and download/install the following packages:  "hplip", "hplip-data", and "hplip-gui".  This will give you a graphical interface to use with HP printers.  Good luck.
