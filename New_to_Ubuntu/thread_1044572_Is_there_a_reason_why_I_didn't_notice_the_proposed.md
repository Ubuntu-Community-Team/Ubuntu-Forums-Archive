### Post by OrangeCrate on 2009-01-19
A lot of proposed updates are posted there for testing. Be wary, there are stories here on the forums about some of those updates borking an install. Use your best judgment, but unless you have the skills to walk your way out of a potential showstopper, it may be best to leave the proposed repositories unchecked.
