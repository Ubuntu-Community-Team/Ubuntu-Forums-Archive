### Post by maniheer on 2008-09-04
Right click the zip file and click "Extract Here"

There should be a file called xxxxxxx.tar.gz

Now right click the Desktop>Change Desktop Background and go to the tab Theme. Now drag the xxxxxx.tar.gz into the window and apply.

Hope this helps

It doesn't have to be a tar.gz. You can just extract the folders and drag them onto the Appearances pane, and they will be installed. Click customize, and choose graphite from Controls and Icons, and the office-metacity-theme under Window Borders.
