### Post by yeats on 2010-01-23
Hi,

Quick forum tip:  allow a little more time for a response - also - if you add this many bumps, it looks like your thread has that many responses and people are *less* likely to respond (or even open it).

> 1- What's the difference between activities and virtual desktops? the desktops are similar to the one in gnome, right? and I saw that I can have many activities for one desktop, but I can't seem to delete or remove an activity. And what do people use more, activities or desktops?

I'm not sure I'm familiar with the terminology you're using ("activities" and "virtual desktops") in this context.  In KDE 4.X the desktop is a widget that lives on your workspace.  If by "activities" you mean "widgets", then they are editable/removeable by clicking on the multicolored button in the upper right of the screen.

> 
2- How to stop certain applications from running at startup? like the bluetooth app?

In the start menu, there should be a "System Settings" (or similarly named) item that has tools as the icon.  All of these sorts of settings are set there.

> 3- I changed the theme of the KDM, but now when I start my laptop the default theme flashes for half a second, then the new installed theme is shown. its nothing serious, but its quite bothering

I don't know why this would be happening, but there is a KDM log in the /var/log directory - perhaps there would be clues there.

One more thing:  I think most people on this forum, especially Absolute Beginner Talk, are using regular Ubuntu and aren't as familiar with KDE/Kubuntu.  There is a Kubuntu forum you might consider joining, though I have always preferred this one, even though my preferred environment has often been KDE.
