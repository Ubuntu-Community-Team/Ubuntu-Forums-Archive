Not quite what you are looking for, but you might like my solution...

I use the Compiz Scale plugin, with a shortcut that activates it when I move my mouse pointer to the bottom left corner.  This is a very quick action (especially on a touchpad).

With Scale, you can click on any of the windows to bring it to the front, or (as in your case), if you click on the desktop, it minimizes all the windows to leave the desktop visible.
