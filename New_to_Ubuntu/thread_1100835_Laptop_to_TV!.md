### Post by Coreo on 2009-03-19
S-Video is video only, so you'll need to connect your sound output from your laptop to your TV, or your sound system. Depends how you have your home system set up.

An HDMI cable has both audio and video, and will provide higher quality video than S-Video.

Mine laptop only has S-Video, I have never used HDMI output on a laptop before.

### Post by Coreo on 2009-03-19
Do you happen to know what kind of cable came with your laptop?
Did it come with any stickers on the cable, or in a bag or anything?

Sometimes they'll send S-Video cables with laptops. They could be sending out HDMI cables, but I haven't heard of it.

S-Video *should* work with your TV.... maybe it just needs some setting up.

HDMI is the way to go though, if you have it. It's higher image quality, and saves from having to use another wire, unless you want to use a different sound system than the speakers in your TV.
