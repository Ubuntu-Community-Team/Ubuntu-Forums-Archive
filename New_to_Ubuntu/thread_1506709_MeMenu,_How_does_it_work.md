### Post by tom.swartz07 on 2010-06-10
> **nuwave said:**
> That's what the wiki said but there is also a notification applet & that where I click and bring up my gwibber app. Is this applet also part of the "memenu"?

Technically, yes and no.

It is officially called the Notification applet, and although it is related to the memenu, it is a separate entity. That menu will allow you to access your IM, Gwibber, and email accounts. Other applications are available that allow you to add more functionality to it also; such as a gmail notifier that specifically checks your gmail account and notifies you of new messages.


In Maverick Meerkat, the memenu and the notification applet will be better integrated, but for now, they are 2 different animals. (no pun intended!)
