### Post by richardjh on 2012-02-06
You may be able to do it but I think it will be complicated.

As an alternative, you could mirror the remastersys repo to a DVD using debmirror on another PC and then add the DVD as a Repo to your machine that is offline. Then install remastersys using apt/aptitude/synaptic.

A guide is at [http://www.howtoforge.com/dvd_images_of_ubuntu_repositories](http://www.howtoforge.com/dvd_images_of_ubuntu_repositories)

Another idea is to email Tony Brijeski, the developer/maintainer. His email address is on the remastersys website. He may be able to help you out.
