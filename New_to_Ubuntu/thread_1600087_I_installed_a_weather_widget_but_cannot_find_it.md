### Post by Paqman on 2011-11-06
> **billyquizboy7 said:**
> Hi, I installed a weather widget:  **plasma**-widget-yawp   but I am unable to find it on my computer.  Where is it hiding?  I want to use it.  **I am running Ubuntu 10.04.** 


Plasmoids are for KDE, and don't work on the standard Unity/Gnome desktop that comes with Ubuntu. However, what you may find is that installing the widget has dragged in some or all of what you need to log in to a KDE desktop. Try logging out and see if you can select KDE as your desktop when you log in (the option to switch is at the top right of the box where you log in).
