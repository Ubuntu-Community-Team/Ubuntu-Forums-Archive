### Post by arochester on 2008-06-15
1) You can only upgrade from one edition to the next e.g. from Breezy to Dapper - and not across several e.g. from Breezy to Hardy.

2) Since you have just installed and have nothing to lose you would be better to get the lastest edition and install that.

3) Even the latest edition will probably NOT automatically identify Broadcom. There might be several steps to install it.
