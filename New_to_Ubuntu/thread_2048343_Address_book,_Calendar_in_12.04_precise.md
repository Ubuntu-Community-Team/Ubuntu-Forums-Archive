### Post by newb85 on 2012-08-26
For the record, Thunderbird has add-ons for both of those.

However, if you have a strong affinity to Evolution, I see no reason you shouldn't use that, instead.

Thunderbird takes some work to make it what I would consider a full-featured client, which Evolution is out-of-the-box.  I know a lot of people aren't looking for something so full-featured, but most of them would be perfectly happy just using a webmail interface.  I think Ubuntu's switch to Thunderbird was a mistake.  But then, that's just my two-cents-worth.
