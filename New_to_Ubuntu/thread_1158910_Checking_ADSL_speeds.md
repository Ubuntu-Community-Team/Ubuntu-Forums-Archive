### Post by Cheesemill on 2009-05-14
Are you getting your units mixed up?

ISP's (and speedtest.net) measure your speed in bits/sec whereas most apps report your speed in bytes/sec.

If speedtest.net is reporting your speed as 1.48 Mbit/s then your download speed is 1480/8 = 185 kB/sec which is what you are seeing.

Hope this helps,

Cheesemill


Edit - Of course 1.48 Mbit is nowhere near the advertised 16 Mbit advertised by F2S, but if BT say that you can only get up to 1 Mbit then you're actually doing quite well.
