### Post by squenson on 2012-08-19
Welcome to this forum boyiri!

You don't need any specific plugin to view an html document. By default, you should have one or two browsers installed. If not, go to the Ubuntu Software Center and search for Firefox or Chromium.

Before an upgrade, ALWAYS do a backup of your important data. The upgrade process itself will not delete any of your files but you never know what can happen, Furthermore, it is ALWAYS good to perform a regular backup.
