### Post by Impavidus on 2017-06-14
Computers run on logic, not on magic, so with plain deductive reasoning you can solve your problems.

Read error messages, read man pages. Error messages always mean exactly what they say, but it isn't always the root cause of your problem. Find out what happens behind the scenes. The difficulty is that not all projects are equally well documented. Just reading the forums and man pages teaches how things work behind the scenes. It taught me how to solve problems I never encountered before.
