### Post by richiewrt on 2008-07-17
I just came back to say that, both PoP and Imap need to be enabled through gmail whichever you decide to use. On the top right on the gmail page go to settings, and then fowarding and the enable pop or imap access.

Difference between PoP and Imap if you didn't know is POP downloads everything from the server and doesn't leave anything on the server, except for the backups. Imap just initially downloads the headers and then when you click on the email it downloads the rest of the message. It also syncs with the server and will leave your stuff where it is at on the server. Good if you check your email from more than one computer.
