### Post by inameiname on 2010-05-28
It has all that the original nautilus has, and then some. Soon, it'll include an undo/redo button too, but they haven't updated it yet. Expect that in a day or two.

As far as your worries, they do. Just remember the full address bar (not just breadcrumbs) was removed from the original nautilus itself too.

Oh, and if you do decide to get it, definitely get that breadcrumbs hack where it'll tweak the breakcrumbs to look really cool for all themes.
