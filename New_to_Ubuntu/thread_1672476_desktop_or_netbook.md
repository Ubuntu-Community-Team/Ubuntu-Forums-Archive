### Post by Kalimol on 2011-01-21
I tried UNR back when it was UNR when I first got my Asus Eee S101, and I switched quite happily back to the desktop edition. The desktop environment isn't really the thing - I mean, you can use the Gnome desktop from the netbook edition or the new Unity interface from the desktop edition. I had other problems with UNR.

You'll probably be able to enable some Compiz plugins without any problem. Some of the prettier ones, like the ring switcher, are actually surprisingly light on the processor. I didn't really have any major problems before I upgraded my RAM unless my processor was on low-power mode. (That said, S101 also ships with an SSD, so I had that advantage and your mileage may vary.)
