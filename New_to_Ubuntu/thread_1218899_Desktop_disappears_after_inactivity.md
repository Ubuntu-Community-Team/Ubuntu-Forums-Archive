### Post by elianthony on 2009-07-21
I can't help with the disappearance of your panel, etc. But, for the meantime, instead of doing a hard reboot, you could do a CTRL+ALT+BACKSPACE to get out of your current session. That will drop you at a login prompt. I'd advise this instead of doing a manual reboot.
