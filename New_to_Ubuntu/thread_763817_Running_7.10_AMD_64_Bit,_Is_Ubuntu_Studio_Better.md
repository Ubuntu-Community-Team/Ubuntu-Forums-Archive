### Post by phidia on 2008-04-23
Any video editing software that's in Studio is also available in Ubuntu.
If you like the default gui or arrangement better then maybe it's worth it.
To go from what you have to Studio would require changing the repos in your /etc/apt/sources.list. I think the general concenus on that is that it's better to do a clean install. You could also check the forums at [Ubuntu Studio]("http://ubuntustudio.org/").
