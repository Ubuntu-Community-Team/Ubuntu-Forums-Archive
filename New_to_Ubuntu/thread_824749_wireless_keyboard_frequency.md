### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-06-11
From what I've read, it is possible to change the channels that wireless keyboards and mice use.

To do this (read your instruction manual), press the connect button on the reciever, then press the connect button on your keyboard and mouse.

This simple act should change the channel that it's using and should enable you to use 2 mice and keyboards simultaneously.

My knowledge about this could be a bit out dated (4 years ago this works anyway), so it may be better for you to read the instruction manual / refer to Logitech's website.
