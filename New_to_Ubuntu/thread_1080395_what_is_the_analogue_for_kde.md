### Post by OffAxis on 2009-02-25
System Settings-->"Look and Feel" has a lot of options for modifying your currently installed theme, or downloading some new ones (from kde-look.org if I recall).

in 4.2 System Settings-->Advanced Tab-->Desktop Theme Details will let you browse, install, and manipulate the look of things in general.
