### Post by aysiu on 2010-04-12
I have a MyTouch.

This is what I do:

1. Plug the USB cord into both the phone and the Ubuntu computer.

2. On the phone, swipe down the notification bar and select to mount the SD card.

3. When asked to confirm the mount, confirm.

4. In Ubuntu, it should show up on the desktop as a removable drive.

5. When done using the removable drive, right-click it and select to unmount or safely remove it.

6. Go back to the phone, swipe down notification and turn off USB storage.

7. Confirm unmount.

8. Unplug the USB cord from both computer and phone.
