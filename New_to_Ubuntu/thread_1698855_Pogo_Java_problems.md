### Post by flyfishingphil on 2011-03-07
If you have been having the same problems there is an update to FF that is listed as 3.6.15 that corrects this situation. If you have not received the update here are the esy steps to getting it.

Click on System > Administration > Update Manager and click on "check updates". I had not received the update but this brought up the FF 3.6.15. Click on "Install updates". I then closed FF, reopened, and tested the Pogo site. Seems to work just fine.

Hope this helps.
