### Post by levlaz on 2012-07-29
Not sure about IceWM, but in Gnome and XFCE there should be an option to "edit menu" if you go in there there will be a list of most (if not all) of your applications. If you click on the boxes next to the app you should be able to see in on the menu. 

Check out [this article]("http://www.ghacks.net/2009/08/17/edit-your-gnome-menus/") for more info. 

Best,
