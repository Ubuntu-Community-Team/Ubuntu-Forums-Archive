### Post by mapes12 on 2009-08-14
> **289531.EXE said:**
> does anyone know how to fix this slow setting?
Have a look at the user manual that came with your new modem and see if you can bring up its configuration admin page in a browser window. There should be a reference in the manual to type "http://x.x.x.x" where the "x" are numbers. Then check the various settings, particularly what speed it is set for.

Do the same on your PC. Right click the Network Manager icon then Connection Information. It should show your speed. Mine is 54Mb/s which is correct for this wifi connection to my router. If your connected via an ethernet lead you should expect at least 100Mb/s.

Once you know whether it's the modem or your PC that's slowing things up post back what you have found out and someone should be able to pick things up from here.
