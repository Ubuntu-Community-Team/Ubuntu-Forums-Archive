### Post by john_mccollum on 2008-05-18
Better solution - (only checked in FF3)

type 'about:config' in the address bar. You will see loads of settings. Look for or search for the one marked 'browser.urlbar.clickSelectsAll' and change this to 'TRUE'.

Done!
