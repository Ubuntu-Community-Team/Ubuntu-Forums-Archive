### Post by lightwarrior on 2011-09-26
I have my menu in Spanish, so it might differ a bit from your English menu.
Go to System -> Preferences -> Energy Preferences (battery and plug icon).
Go to third tab (General) and select at Notification Area any of the five options (never show icon, only when battery is low, only when charging or discharging, only when battery is present, or always).
Close... you should have your battery icon back.  Always will always show it.
