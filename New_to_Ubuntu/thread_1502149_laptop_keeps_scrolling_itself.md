### Post by sdennie on 2010-06-05
It sounds like your trackpad is registering constant mouse scroll events.  You could try to clean the edges of it and see if it helps.  Or, you could plugin a mouse to the machine and disable the trackpad and see if you still have the problem.  It's probably a hardware problem though.  If the machine is under warranty, get them to come out and look at it.  If not, and your machine is constructed in a sane way, find a replacement hand rest/trackpad on ebay.   It's fairly easy to replace the top part of your laptop.  I did it 2 weeks ago on my XPS M1330 and it was disappointingly easy.
