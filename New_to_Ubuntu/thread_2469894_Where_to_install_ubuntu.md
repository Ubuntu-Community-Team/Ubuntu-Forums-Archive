### Post by Impavidus on 2021-12-13
We have a sticky thread for that: [https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1543006](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1543006)

Having said that, most hardware just works. Wifi and nvidia graphics may take some effort to get working. I read about problems with touchscreens too. Bluetooth keyboards and mice and fingerprint readers can be a problem, but from a security point of view those are a bad idea anyway.

Apparently, Lenovo and Dell are very reliable, but that doesn't mean other brands are unlikely to work.
