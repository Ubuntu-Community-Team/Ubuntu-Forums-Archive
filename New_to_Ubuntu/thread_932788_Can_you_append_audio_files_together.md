### Post by blingo on 2008-09-28
With ease, 

Backup first.

Go to Applications,
Add/Remove,
And Install Audacity.
Open the file from Audacity.

The lame way to do it, is to select the part with the mouse, copy the part with CTRL+C, than go to the start of the part and
press CTRL+V several times, or something like that,
But I also think there's a special option to do it.
