### Post by timcredible on 2008-06-25
i've worked with 2 different ipods, gtkpod and amarok worked fine on both (i think amarok is easier to use), it doesn't matter if you visited itunes or not, it's just that the music files from itunes are encrypted, so you can't play them on linux.  but you can add and delete music just fine.  be aware that linux lags a little behind the newest ipod hardware because apple doesn't release any info about how the ipods work, and each ipod has different databases, etc. in an effort to lock you into itunes, so if you have the latest and greatest ipod, you may or may not be able to use it with linux.
