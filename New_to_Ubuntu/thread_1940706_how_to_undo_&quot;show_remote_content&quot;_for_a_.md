### Post by Azdour on 2012-03-16
Hi,

In my Thunderbird 3.1.7 when I click on show remote content is adds that email address to my address book.

If you go to your address book, find the email address in question, right click on the email address and select properties.

For me on the contact page there is an Allow remote content checkbox which you can untick.

It may not be exactly the same in your Thunderbird but hopefully it will point you in the right direction of where to look.
