### Post by kellemes on 2010-06-07
> **Ron Carson said:**
> Other than the obvious visual interface, what are the differences between using the Software Center and the Synaptic Package Manager?

Nothing important, Software Center is made with Windows users in mind I guess.. it's all very simple. You may like it, but a lot of people don't.
Synaptic has a little more options, you can find any package available to you including all the info about it, included files, dependencies etc..
Choose whatever you like, it's all up to you, in the end it does not matter, both do the same, manage your packages.
