### Post by Jack M. on 2008-09-05
Many of the Places menu entries are taken from your bookmarks in Nautilus (the file browser). Open Nautilus and right-click > 'Remove' any folders that you'd like to get rid of from the sidebar on the left.

### Post by Jack M. on 2008-09-05
You won't delete the folders from your system if you remove them from the bookmarks, you'll just remove the links to them from the Places menu and the Nautilus sidebar. I've attached a screenshot showing where this is. (Though, the Home Folder and Desktop menu entries, as well as all of the entries under the first divider, can't be easily removed. There should be some way to get rid of them if you really want to, however.)
