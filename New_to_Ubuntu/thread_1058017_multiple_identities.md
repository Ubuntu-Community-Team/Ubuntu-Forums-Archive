### Post by schwascore on 2009-02-02
That depends - if you just want separate e-mail addresses, yes, just add another e-mail account.  If you want separate user logins each time you open the e-mail client, then no, not that I'm aware of.  The good news, though, is that if you are trying to provide separated e-mail profiles for different local users you can just give them their own usernames.
