### Post by chronographer on 2008-12-14
what are your hardware specs? Do you use a ATI graphics card? If it stops at a white screen you can switch to the command line with "ctrl alt F1" and back with "alt F7" but once at the command line you will want to fix the problem. I would try to reinstall first (use the latest cd from Ubuntu, either 8.04.1 or 8.10) and then get onto IRC at freenode and #Ubuntu to see if someone can walk you through the steps to diagnose then fix the problem.
