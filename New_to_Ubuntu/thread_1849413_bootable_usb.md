### Post by varunendra on 2011-09-25
As far as I have seen, unetbootin has been most successful with 11.04.

Download both [unetbootin]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/") and [11.04 iso]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/alternative-download") (I recommend downloading the iso via torrent), then use that iso image in unetbootin to make your flash drive bootable Live USb.
