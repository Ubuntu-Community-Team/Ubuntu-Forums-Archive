### Post by tom56 on 2008-05-31
The heart and rose tell you whether the package came from main, universe restricted or multiverse. At the moment I can't remember which is which. Though I know the Ubuntu logo tells you the package is from main.

If you don't know what any of this means then it doesn't matter; it's just for those who are a bit picky about the licensing/development of the programs they use.
