### Post by cavalier911 on 2011-04-25
Drag/drop doesn't work anywhere on **L**ubuntu.
Lxpanel/Rt click Add/Remove Panel Items/Panel Applets/Application Launch Bar(highlight)/Edit button/Add button/firefox.desktop or any other shortcut *program name*.desktop , move shortcuts within the launcher by highlighting and clicking the up/down arrows.
