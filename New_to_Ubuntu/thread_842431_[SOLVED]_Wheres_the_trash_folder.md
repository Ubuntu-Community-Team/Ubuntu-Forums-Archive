### Post by llama320 on 2008-06-27
i may be misunderstanding your question, but trash:/// is where your trash should be kept

If you can't see the folder you want to delete from trash, it may be hidden. You can view hidden by going to (you guessed it) View > Show Hidden Folders or just press Ctrl+H

Hope that helps
