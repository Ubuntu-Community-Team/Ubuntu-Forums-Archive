### Post by wangsuda on 2010-10-18
> **thebarisaxkid said:**
> I have installed 10.10 and I can't seem to find the "Openoffice.org Formula" program at all.  It is not found in the applications menu or anywhere else.  Anyone know where it can be found?
Go to System>Preferences>Main Menu. When the menu window opens, click on "graphics" you should see formula on the right. Click the checkbox to make it visible. You can also move it where you want.
