### Post by Krytarik on 2012-06-29
> **enetic said:**
> When I type in something in the google searchbar, google shows up in portuguese. When I write [www.google.com]("http://www.google.com") , I also end up having portuguese Google.
It's not a Firefox setting, it's a Google setting - you can change the language/s for both Google's UI and the search results here:

[http://www.google.com/preferences?hl=en](http://www.google.com/preferences?hl=en)

Notice that these preferences show the Google UI language as being set to the language they are called with (via the "hl" parameter in the URL), and in turn also the main language for the search results. But they are not *actually* set, until you hit the Save button.

Regards.
