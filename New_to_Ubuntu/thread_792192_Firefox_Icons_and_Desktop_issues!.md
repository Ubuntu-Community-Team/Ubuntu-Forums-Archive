### Post by tjwoosta on 2008-05-12
to make the trash icon show on the desktop, do this

go to terminal and type 
```
gconf-editor
```

go to apps then nautilus then desktop (you will see a little checkbox on the right that says trash_icon_visible)


and the way i change icons on awn is just right click the icon and click change icon
