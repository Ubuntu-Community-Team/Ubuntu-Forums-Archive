### Post by Dermot Doyle on 2008-11-01
Hi,
This may not be what you want to hear, but as a non-computerate newbie to Linux I bought a Dell pre-loaded with 8.04 and couldn't get the speedtouch 330 to work. The best advice I got was to chuck it and buy a better one. I was recomended the Linksys AM200 which works perfectly for around 30 quid. I baulked at the idea of having to buy something else to get running with Linux, but I have to say I think it was worth it. Good luck if you decide to plow on with the speedtouch.
