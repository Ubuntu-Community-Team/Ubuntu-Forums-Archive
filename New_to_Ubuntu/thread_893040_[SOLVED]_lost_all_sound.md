### Post by tonymoloney on 2008-08-17
I started this thread some time ago and just about gave up solving the problem, but I persisted and am now going to mark this new one as solved, with my solution as it is rather interesting.
I am running Gutsy on a Lenovo laptop and had been doing so for at least twelve months, when I lost all sound.
Following all the advice I could find on this forum, I eventually had an inspirational thought and went back to my BIOS looking for anything to do with sound. There was nothing there but then I remembered seeing something about the sound card and the internal modem being vaguely the same.
I had disabled my internal modem, thinking that I would never need it, so, just out of curiosity, I re-enabled it, and you guessed it, all sound came back.
That's not something I really expected, so users of laptops, check it out if you have the same problem.
