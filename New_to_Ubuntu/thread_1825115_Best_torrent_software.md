### Post by dniMretsaM on 2011-08-14
qBittorrent is my favorite. Deluge is also good (I had graphical issues with it though). Transmission is good for occasional torrenters, it has less configuration options. But it's great for people new to BitTorrent or people who only use it every once in a while. uTorrent is not a good option since (as far as I know) it is CLI/WUI only. I've also heard that it is slow. Both qBittorrent and Deluge are available through the Software Center. Transmission is the default in Ubuntu.
