### Post by foxkate on 2009-09-16
ABBYY released beta version of FineReader Online &#8211; [www.finereaderonline.com]("http://ubuntuforums.org/www.finereaderonline.com")

The service converts scanned documents and digital images online &#8211; so it is suitable for any OS. The OCR quality is really very good. I&#8217;m not sure if it recognizes PDFs, but you can scan to any other format (for ex. TIFF, which is supported).

For now FineReader Online supports 6 recognition languages and also can process multilingual documents.

Hope it will be helpful for you ;)
