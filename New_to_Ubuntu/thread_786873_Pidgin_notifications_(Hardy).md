### Post by madhusudancs on 2008-05-08
Hey right click on Pidgin icon in System tray and goto Plugins. There select Message Notification Plugin and click on Configure Plugin. In the main dialog that appears you can check/uncheck when you want the notification(i.e blinking) to go away. I suggest you to check all the 4 or whichever way you want and close it. It should work as you wanted. Please reply if its not working.
