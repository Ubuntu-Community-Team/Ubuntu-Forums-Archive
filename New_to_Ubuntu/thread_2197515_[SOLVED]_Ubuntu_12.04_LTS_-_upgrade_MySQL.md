### Post by ptn107 on 2014-01-04
For MySQL grab it from the website: [http://dev.mysql.com/downloads/mysql/](http://dev.mysql.com/downloads/mysql/)

Scroll down and change your platform to 'Debian Linux' and you can download 32 or 64 bit.

For workbench you can go here: [http://dev.mysql.com/downloads/tools/workbench/](http://dev.mysql.com/downloads/tools/workbench/)

Scroll down and change your platform to 'Ubuntu Linux' and pick your deb.
