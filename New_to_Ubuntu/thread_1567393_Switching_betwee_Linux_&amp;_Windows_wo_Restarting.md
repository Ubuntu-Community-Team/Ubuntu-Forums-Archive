### Post by jhsu802701 on 2010-09-03
Use VirtualBox to run Windows within Linux.  Not only does this allow you to switch back and forth without restarting the computer, the inevitable deterioration of Windows won't bring down your whole computer - just the virtual hard drive.  Best of all, installing or reinstalling Windows won't monopolize your computer.
