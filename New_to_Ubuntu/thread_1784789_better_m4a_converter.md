### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-06-18
AAC audio (m4a) and MP3 are both lossy codecs - they lose quality whenever you encode to them, and the deficiencies in the original file become more pronounced when you convert it to another lossy format.

Either encode from the original CD/WAV file to MP3, or choose a higher bitrate as the target for the MP3 - probably 192kbps or above would be better.
