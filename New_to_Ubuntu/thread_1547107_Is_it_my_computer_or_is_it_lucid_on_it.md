### Post by TBABill on 2010-08-06
Have you confirmed it's not temperature related? My machines will cut off if the temps reach ~100C. I have never hit that point on my current machine, but in Karmic I kept having the temp sensors shut the laptop down - always at random times because sometimes I did very little and the temps skyrocketed quickly. Ended up just needing CPU frequency scaling and the proprietary video driver to bring them way down.
 
Your problem could be way more complex than just heat, especially if it's a desktop since they often run cooler than laptops. just throwing it out there in case it's a possibility. You can install temperature apps/widgets just to verify.
