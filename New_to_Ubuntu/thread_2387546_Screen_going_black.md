### Post by TheFu on 2018-03-20
I'd check the screen-saver settings. [https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/session-screenlocks.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/session-screenlocks.html)  If they are too short for your needs, change them to longer. My Ubuntu uses xscreensaver and it has different controls for blanking, password locking, and turning off the power to the screen.  Each of these can be disabled too.
