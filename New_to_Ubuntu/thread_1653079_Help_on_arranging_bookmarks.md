### Post by germanix on 2010-12-26
In Firefox go to "bookmarks", then to "organize bookmarks", then click on the organize drop down menu, from here choose new folder. After the folder is creatd you can drag-drop your bookmarks from the right side of the window to the new folder.

### Post by cipherboy_loc on 2010-12-26
You need to double click on a subset of your bookmarks. If you want to organize the bookmarks that are on your toolbar, then you need to click on Bookmarks Toolbar, else if you want to organize those that are in the menu, click on Bookmarks Menu. Both can be found on the right under All Bookmarks -> Bookmarks (Toolbar/Menu).


Cipherboy
