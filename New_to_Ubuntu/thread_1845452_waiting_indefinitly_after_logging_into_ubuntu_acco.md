### Post by coffeecat on 2011-09-17
Your Mobility ATI Radeon 4250 should be able to support the 3d acceleration needed for the Unity desktop with the default open source driver. I say should - I can get Unity with my Mobility 4200, but I've been helping out on another thread where the OP can't get to a desktop with their 4200. I think other hardware factors may be a problem.

Anyway, first thing: boot up and when you get to the log in screen, enter your name, but before you enter your password, have a look at the bottom of the screen. Choose "classic" and then enter your password. Does that take you to a usable classic gnome desktop with panels top and bottom? If so, we can see if the proprietary video driver may help.
