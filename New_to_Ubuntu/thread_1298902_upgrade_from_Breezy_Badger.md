### Post by undecim on 2009-10-23
If you can back up the data, it is advised to back up at least your most important files before any major system changes (upgrade, messing with partitions, etc) just in case something goes wrong.

You shouldn't need to do one upgrade a time. An upgrade basically just changes the name of the release and installs updated packages to match.

Also, download new and updated packages after your upgrade, look at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AptGet/Offline/](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AptGet/Offline/)
