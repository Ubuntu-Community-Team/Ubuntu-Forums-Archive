### Post by bumanie on 2008-07-19
Just install over the top of the previous installation, ensuring you format the partitions or else you can format with something like gparted first. I would suggest manual partition at the partitioning stage and make a / swap and a separate /home partition. / -8-10gb; swap 1-2gb; /home as large as you like. Thus, in the future if things go wrong, you can just reinstall the / filesystem and all data will be safe.
