### Post by ptn107 on 2010-02-06
Though the content of getdeb.net may not be malicious, almost all of the packages there do not follow the Debian\Ubuntu package guidelines.  There are many packages that install files in the wrong locations or install unnecessary files altogether.  They are inconsistent and sloppy.
