### Post by garyjmellor on 2011-02-17
Exactly so. If you go:

Applications > Ubuntu Software Center

The Ubuntu Software Center should now be open in front of you. In the left-hand pane, if you click the 'Installed Software' item, the right-hand pane will show you what is currently on your machine.

Highlight an entry and it will expand slightly and take on an orange background. You should also now see two buttons: one labelled 'More Info' and one named 'Remove'.

The 'Get Software' item in the left-hand pane is now the equivalent of 'Add'. You can search by drilling down into the categories displayed in the right-hand pane or just by typing in search strings in the search box in the top right-hand corner (small text entry field with a magnifying glass icon).

Hope this helps.
