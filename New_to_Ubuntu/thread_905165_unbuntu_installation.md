### Post by gmxgeek on 2008-08-30
It is possible, but only if your BIOS have the option of booting from flash drive

---

### Post by Dracus124 on 2008-08-30
I can tell if my BIOS can boot from flash drives?

---

### Post by gmxgeek on 2008-08-30
reboot and open your bios. there should be a bit of text that says press <x> to enter setup. look under boot or something similar and see if USB is available. if so, then set it to the first device. if not, then your system doesn't support usb boot.
