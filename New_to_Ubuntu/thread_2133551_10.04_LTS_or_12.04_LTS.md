### Post by papibe on 2013-04-08
Hi Gannas.

Contrary to the evolution of the desktop editions, which have changed "a lot", both server versions, 10.04 and 12.04, do not differ drastically. In other words, what you have read and studied will be help you either way.

**12.04** is newer and also a Long Term Support (LTS) version. I would go with that if I were you.
