### Post by Kevin McCready on 2013-10-06
In my smartphone with android, I go into the phone app as if I want to make a call. Then I press contacts, then I press the menu key, then I press manage contacts. Then I have a choice of four things. I usually press backup to SD card, then I back that up separately. Thanks for reminding me.
