### Post by adam_kimber on 2008-10-26
You are guaranteed that the packages will be up-to-date but they might not be tested so much and therefore have more bugs (however they will probably have fixes for previous bugs). If you require support from Ubuntu they then do not support those repos. 

You can get into trouble with using external repos, as they can add packages that conflict with the main Ubuntu ones and also overwrite files they shouldn't etc. It can get messy and I speak from experience. :P
