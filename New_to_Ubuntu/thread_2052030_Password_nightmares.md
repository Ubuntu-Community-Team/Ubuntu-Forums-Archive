### Post by deadflowr on 2012-09-02
I'm guessing you disabled your admin and other accounts.
In the user account screen, you get an option to autologin, which is what you wanted. And you get an Account option which will give you the option to either set a password or disable that user.
It would seem you disabled all your users, hence no passwords will work.
When you follow the guide in post #2, remember to set a password in at least one admin account and do not disable it. You can still set it to autologin.
