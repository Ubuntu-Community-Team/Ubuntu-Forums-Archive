### Post by d4m1r on 2012-08-28
1) To open a new instance of the same applicable just middle click on its icon (usually on the unity bar).

2) This will bring it into focus meaning it will be on top of any previously running windows.

3) To bring a different windows back into focus (on top) either just click anywhere on the window (if it is not maximized) or left click on its unity bar launcher bar to show the existing instance of it (if it is minimized).
