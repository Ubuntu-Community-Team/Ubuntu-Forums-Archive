### Post by squeabs on 2009-02-01
I'm not 100% sure on this, but you should be able to right-click, unmount.

By the way, you should never just "pull out the cable" on any external device, especially one as sensitive as an external hard drive. It can cause damage to the device.
Always unmount it, whichever way your OS specifies, then pull the cable.

Best of luck with Ubuntu.
