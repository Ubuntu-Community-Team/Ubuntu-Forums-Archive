You installed the phone management on Windows, but on Ubuntu as well??

In general phone (or other device, eg Windows mobile PDAs) support is not ideal in linux.  If you are trying to sync a calendar or contacts list I think you'll be disappointed.

My advice - wait for an Android phone (open source, free push GMail, Gcal and contacts).  Sync support will probably be added to linux apps fairly quickly (no reverse engineering required!!)
