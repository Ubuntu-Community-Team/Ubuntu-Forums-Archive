If you have enabled "Broadcast Name" on your wireless router, but cannot see it listed, then I'd say your HP wireless adapter is not configured (or maybe not supported) under Linux.

If you haven't enabled "Broadcast Name" then start there, and also disable WEP or any other security temporarily.
