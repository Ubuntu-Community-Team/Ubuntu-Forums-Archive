### Post by -kg- on 2010-10-08
Sorry, my friend, but Pro Evolution Soccer 2011 is a Windows based game, and cannot be installed directly under Linux.  The only chance you have of it is to configure it to run under Wine, and the outlook on that is kind of bleak:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=21569]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=21569")

One guy got it running, though without sound functions.  The rest of the testers couldn't even get it to run.

The only recourse you're going to have is either to dual-boot with Windows or run Windows under a VM and run your game there.  That or get really good with Wine.  Perhaps someone that is good with Wine will come along, but I'm not that person.
