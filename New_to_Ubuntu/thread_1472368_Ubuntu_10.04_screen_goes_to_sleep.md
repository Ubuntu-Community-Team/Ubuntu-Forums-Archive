### Post by tica vun on 2010-05-04
To clarify: Is this ubuntu putting the display to sleep, or your monitor's built-in powersave function?

If it's ubuntu, you can set it not to do that under System->Preferences->Power management. If not, the solution is specific to your monitor, check the manual.
