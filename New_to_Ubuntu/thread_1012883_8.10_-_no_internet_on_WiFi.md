### Post by Michael.Godawski on 2008-12-28
Well your drivers seem to be installed and active.

What soft of encryption are you using? WEP? WPA2?

Try to deactivate the encryption and connect without it. If you manage to connect without the encryption we know your wlan net is working, and there is only a configuration issue.

What makes me little nervous is this line:
```
wlan0     Interface doesn't support scanning : Resource temporarily unavailable
```

It seems as if your wlan area was not powerful enough to reach your wlan card...or some other communication issue with the router and the wlan card.
