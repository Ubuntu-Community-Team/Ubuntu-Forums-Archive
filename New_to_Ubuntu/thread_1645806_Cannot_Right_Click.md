### Post by coffeecat on 2010-12-15
I notice you're running what appears to be the netbook version of the Unity desktop. Are you running it on a netbook or on a laptop? If a laptop you might be better off using the classic gnome desktop at the moment. Post details of your hardware and we can see. Also, which version of Ubuntu? It looks to be 10.10, but let's confirm that.

Where or when are you advised to right-click on a menu? Is that on a desktop popup or in a howto or guide you've found somewhere? The gtweakui is a 3rd party app (not official Ubuntu, although available through the package manager), a front-end to gconf. I am wondering if you've found instructions for the classic desktop and are trying to apply them to Unity.

Last point. Ignore any advice to log in as root - not that you can on a default install anyway. It is highly inadvisable and contrary to forum policy.
