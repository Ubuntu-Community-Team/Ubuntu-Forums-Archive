You can use either KDE or Gnome in any Ubuntu release. You can see here [https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+question/8951](https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+question/8951). As for your specs, your laptop should run any Linux distribution well. It just comes down to whichever you prefer based on the packaged themes and looks.
