### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-06-16
You can add any launcher as a starter for a program (or for a google link, as you suggest) most easily in UNR by first adding a new launcher to one of the groups such as Internet, in the menus using the menu editor, (System-Preferences-Main Menu).  This new launcher will appear in the grouped launchers of the UNR desktop in whatever group you added it, and can then be added to the favorites by clicking on the + sign that appears when you hover over the new icon in that group.

The icon you use can be changed in the usual way by right clicking on the icon in the menu editor and picking Properties, then clicking on the icon that shows in the properties window dialog, and choosing an icon of your choice.
