Not necessarily true, but that doesn't matter.

I'm not exactly sure where it is on 11.10, but there is a thing called "hardware drivers" that goes off and looks for proprietary hardware drivers for your machine. If you type "hardware drivers" in the search window on the dash (click on the ubuntu symbol in the launcher panel) you should be able to find that. You'll need an internet connection for it to work properly, so plug a cable in. Let it search, and let it install the drivers it finds.

Just to rule out the really simple things: make sure that the wireless isn't just switched off with a hardware switch on the computer somewhere. ;)
