### Post by Temposs on 2009-07-13
You don't need to update. Just ignore the update program once you have a clean install.

The worst case is that if you don't update at all, there are multiple security flaws you will be vulnerable to. From then, you may be vulnerable to various malware, hacking exploits, and various system instabilities that may cause crashes and the like.

If you want to reduce your updating, I recommend for you to only enable the security updates(disable recommended updates and any others). These are the most important for the security and stability of your system.
