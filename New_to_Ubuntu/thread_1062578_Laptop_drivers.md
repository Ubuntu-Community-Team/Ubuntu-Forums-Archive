### Post by MockY on 2009-02-07
If you are thinking of buying the laptop in a retail store, burn the latest Ubuntu release on a CD, and test various computers by rebooting them with the disc. If everything seems to be working out of the box, go with that one. 
I used this method when I recently purchased a Sony Vaio for my wife, just to be sure it would work.
