### Post by CatKiller on 2011-07-15
Sounds like it's being automatically underclocked to keep the fans quiet. Adding the CPU Frequency Scaling Monitor to the Panel will let you check this and adjust how much you want it to do this.

---

### Post by PureNRG on 2011-07-15
Worked perfectly. Thanks for the help!
