### Post by stinger30au on 2008-05-31
the standard canon driver that comes with ubuntu will not tell you your ink level (theres a few other things it wont do either)

but to show how much you you have click on 

applications

add remove software

and search on inklevel

you will find two programs appear, one called qink the other called inkblot
i have found that qink works after it is installed and so long as it is run with "sudo" infront of it
