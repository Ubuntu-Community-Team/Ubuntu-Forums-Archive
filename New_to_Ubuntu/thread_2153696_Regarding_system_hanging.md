### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-06-12
Control Alt Delete does nothing outside Windows.

To restart the X server, press Alt-Printscreen-K.

However it sounds like your computer has bad RAM. Try taking one stick out, then see if you have the problem again. If you do, put it back in and take another stick out, test, until you find the bad RAM.
