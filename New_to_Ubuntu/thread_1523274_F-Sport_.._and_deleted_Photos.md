### Post by lkjoel on 2010-07-03
Are the pictures deleted permanently?
If so, there is not much you can do to recover them.
[http://www.cyberciti.biz/tips/linuxunix-recover-deleted-files.html](http://www.cyberciti.biz/tips/linuxunix-recover-deleted-files.html)
if they are in the trash, you can recover them by using nautilus:
```
nautilus --no-desktop --browser
```
