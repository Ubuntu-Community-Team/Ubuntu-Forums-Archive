### Post by LastDino on 2017-04-29
Backspace works, but you'll have to remember the exact space where you did mistake.

Lets say you did mistake at 3rd place and you realize that after typing 5th, you will need to hit the backspace to erase till 3rd word by pressing it 3 times. If I do mistake, I generally just hit it for more than my entire password length and type again.

Your sudo password is the one at your login if you didn't change it.
