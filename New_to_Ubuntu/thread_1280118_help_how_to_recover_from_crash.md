### Post by Yvan300 on 2009-10-01
The real problem when doing a hard reset is the chance of the hard disk itself being damaged. If the disk is reading or writing to the disk and the power is cut off the head may scrape the surface of the disk rendering it unreadable unless you go to one of those data recovery places. But that said, i have done countless hard resets with no problems.
