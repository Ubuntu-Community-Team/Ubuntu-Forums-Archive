### Post by wbar2 on 2010-03-25
Good question. Here's how to restore it:

1. Right-click the top panel.
2. Select "Add to panel..."
3. Scroll til you see "Indicator Applet". Click it.
4. Click "Add" button at the bottom. Then click "Close".

The evolution mail button should then be restored. If you want to move it, right click it and click "Move." Once you get it where you want it I would suggest right-clicking it again and selecting "Lock to Panel." You may have to unlock the system notification applet (right click, uncheck "Lock to Panel") in order to move the mail applet past it near the date in the original position.

If this solves your problem please update your post to say "[SOLVED]". You can do this by editing your post and using the "Thread Tools" dropdown menu.
