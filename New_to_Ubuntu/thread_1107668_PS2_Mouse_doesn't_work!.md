### Post by egalvan on 2009-03-27
Three things come quickly to mind...

1) PS/2 mouse port option is shut off in BIOS... boot to the BIOS and check; enable if "off" (or  "disabled" or similar words).

2) mouse is defective... check it on another machine, or try a KNOWN GOOD mouse.

3) mother-board is defective... if above two options pan out, this might be the case...sorry, no helpful hints on this.
