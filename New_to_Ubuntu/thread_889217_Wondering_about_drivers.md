### Post by Victormd on 2008-08-13
Using envy-ng should solve your graphics card problems, if you run into any. As for your wireless, I must say that I was pleasantly surprised with 8.04 (ubuntu though) and had no problem whatsoever with my wireless (after all the updates) and have to say that chances are, you shouldn't run into any problems either. If you do run into problems, you can resort to ndiswraper to solve them.

Keep in mind that during your install, it's a good idea to have your computer connected to the internet through your ethernet that way you can get the updates and reduce chances of running into any problems.
