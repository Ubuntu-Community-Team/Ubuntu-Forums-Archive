### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-11-02
Go to the envelope icon towards the right side of your top panel.

Come down to Broadcast.

This opens the Gwibber program. In Gwibber's Edit menu go to Preferences, and then set the "Show notifications for:" thing to "Mentions and replies only" or "Do not display notifications".
