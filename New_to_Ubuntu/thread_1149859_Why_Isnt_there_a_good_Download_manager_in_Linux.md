They're not meant to integrate with browsers.  You grab the URL of whatever it is you want to download and give it to the DL manager.

There are *tons* of DL managers for Linux, as the above post shows.  I personally use DownThemAll for grabbing a bunch of mp3, images, etc from a listing on a webpage, and gwget for downloading single large files when I can't get a torrent or a NZB of the file in question.
