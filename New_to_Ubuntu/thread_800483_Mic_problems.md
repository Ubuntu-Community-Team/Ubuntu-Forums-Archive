### Post by HotShotDJ on 2008-05-19
> **roc320 said:**
> I've tried fooling around with the sound levels, but have had no luck. Any help would be great, thanks.Have you looked at the Volume Control in your panel?  If its not there, you need to add it by right clicking on an empty spot in the panel and then clicking "Add to Panel"

After you do that, right click on the volume icon and open volume control.  If you don't have a "Recording" tab, add it through the Edit --> Preferences menu and just check off all the options available.  Now try turning the microphone channel all the way up... it might be "Capture" or it might be "Digital"  But you can experiment and see which one helps.
