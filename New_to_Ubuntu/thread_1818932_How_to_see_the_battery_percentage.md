### Post by peter d on 2011-08-05
To find out the percentage remaining click on the battery icon. Then click on battery time left. Choose the Detail tab. Percentage is in the list.

I don't know how to make the widget show the percentage rather than the time remaining, but I guess you need to edit a configuration file.
