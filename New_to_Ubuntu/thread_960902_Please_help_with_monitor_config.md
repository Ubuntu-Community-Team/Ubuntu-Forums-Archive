### Post by epitaph on 2008-10-27
Is the resolution limitation only present on this specific monitor or have you tried using other monitors and used higher resolutions with your current video card and drivers?

The native resolution of that monitor should be 1440x900, is this not a selectable option in the Screen Resolution panel under System > Preferences?

If not, you may have some issues with video drivers. A reinstall of the correct drivers for your video card may help.

Make sure the refresh rate is not out of range of what the monitor can display also.
