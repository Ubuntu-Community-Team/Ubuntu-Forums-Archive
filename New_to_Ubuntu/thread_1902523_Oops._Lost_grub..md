### Post by Potters Son on 2011-12-31
Believe it or not, there are entire articles written about reinstalling GRUB; it's such a common issue. Granted, most of the time people are installing Windows from scratch after installing Ubuntu rather than running system repair, but it all comes down to the same thing:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling_GRUB2]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling_GRUB2")

I recommend taking your Ubuntu CD (if it's still handy), and downloading & running [Boot Repair]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair") in the LiveCD environment. You should be up and running in no time.
