### Post by westie457 on 2011-07-29
If your laptop cd drive has a tray, the lens should be visible when the tray is fully opened. The lens will look like a little dot (or the pupil of an eye) very easy to clean. If your cd drive is a slot then it going to be much harder to clean the lens.
