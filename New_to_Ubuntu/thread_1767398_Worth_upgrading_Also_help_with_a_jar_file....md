### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-05-25
If everything works well for you in 10.04, I'd say no, it's not worth upgrading. The latest version of Ubuntu is 11.04, and while it features a lot of new stuff, you'll have to go 10.04->10.10, 10.10->11.04 to upgrade to it. I'd suggest waiting another year and upgrading directly to the next LTS, 12.04.
