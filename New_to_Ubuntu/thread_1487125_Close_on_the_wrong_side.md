### Post by r-senior on 2010-05-18
> **eltonw said:**
> This is the default behaviour of Nautilus in LUCID
More accurately, it's the position of the buttons in the Ambiance, Radiance and Dust themes shipped with Lucid.

Note also the warning from [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1469475]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1469475")

"Using gconf-editor is not the right approach as this could bork future themes. This change makes it easier for themes to do interesting things with window borders. Unfortunately, if the wrong approach spreads, they won't be able to do that".
