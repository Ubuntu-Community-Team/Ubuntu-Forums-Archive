### Post by gigabyte411 on 2008-10-22

If your on the inside of your cube instead of looking at it from the outside like we all want to be then you have to go to-- CompizConfig setting manager -- Desktop Cube -- behavior -- deselect Inside Cube.  

That's it.  all fixed.
