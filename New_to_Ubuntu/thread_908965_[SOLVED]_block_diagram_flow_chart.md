yEd is much better than open office draw. It snaps automatically to center of objects. When you draw an arrow between two rectangles, the arrow comes in the centers of the rectangles and and they form a group automatically.  Even when you move the rectangles the arrow becomes longer to touch the rectangle where it was joined, all automatically. When you export your drawing to a jpg, png or pdf the page size is automatically adjusted to your drawing.  Even online Google draw program is better than openoffice draw.

I hope yEd will replace open office draw as the standard drawing application that comes with the installation of new releases.  Gimp is too complicated to put it in standard applications but open office draw is not smart enough as yEd to understand what you are doing.
