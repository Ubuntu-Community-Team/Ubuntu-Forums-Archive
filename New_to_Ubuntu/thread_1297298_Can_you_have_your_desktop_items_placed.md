### Post by sgosnell on 2009-10-22
On my desktop, they pop up in the same place I put them the last time.  If you drag everything to where you want it, they should stay there.  I'm not certain this lasts through a reboot, because I don't have much on my desktop (one icon) and don't tend to plug in many drives at a time, usually 2 at most, so I don't see much clutter.  But if I unplug a drive and plug it in again, it shows up wherever I left it, so you may be able to arrange things once and keep them there.  You may also want to right-click on the desktop and in the popup, check Keep Aligned.
