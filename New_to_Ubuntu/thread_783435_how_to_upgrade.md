### Post by Tatty on 2008-05-05
You will need to alternate CD to do this (check the alternate cd option on the download page)

However, you cannot upgrade straight from 7.04 to 8.04, you will have to upgrade to 7.10 first.

Personally I would recommend a clean install of 8.04.  It might be worth dual booting 8.04 first so you still have your working 7.04 to fall back to should things go wrong.  Always back up before installing an OS though :)
