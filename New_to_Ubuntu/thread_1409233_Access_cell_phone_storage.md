### Post by 311005901 on 2010-02-17
Usually, if you want to access the actual phone memory, you need to root/hack/mod your phone. :p Good luck with that...
Manufacturers place this restriction on phones to prevent people from taking their applications and other files on the phone. It either hard or impossible to successfully root a phone and access the files (unless your phone runs a widely used OS like Android or an iPhone).
