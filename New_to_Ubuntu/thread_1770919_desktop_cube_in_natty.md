### Post by athenroy on 2011-05-29
I use the classic desktop with 11.04 with Gnome 2 and have it working, but you have to install the CompizConfig (ccsm) packages including Extras.  If you don't like the Unity desktop, when you click on your name to log in, look in the center of the bottom bar and you can select whether you want Unity or the Ubuntu Classic Desktop.  Than put in your password and login as usual.  Once set, it should stay there.
