### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-05-01
A router is a box that you plug a line from your modem into, then you can plug multiple computers into.  They are used for networking computers.  A wireless router is just a router that includes wireless capability so that some computers can connect wirelessly to the network.

Usually, you can set up your router with your user name and password so that the router does the work of connecting to your PPoE account for you, and your computer just has a connection that's already on.

But if you don't have a router, I guess you can't really do it that way.
