### Post by coldraven on 2012-01-05
Also something important to consider is if you can get a continuous ink supply system (CISS) for whatever model you buy.
I've had an Epson Stylus Photo R300 for several years and I buy ink at £12 for 600 ml.

If you buy cartridges for a Canon it costs about £1000 per litre!
