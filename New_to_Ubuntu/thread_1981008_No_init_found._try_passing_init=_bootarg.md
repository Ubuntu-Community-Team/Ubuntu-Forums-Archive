### Post by Lisiano on 2012-05-16
The best course of action (Both painless, fast and not so nerve wrecking) is to reinstall Ubuntu.
Head over to [Ubuntu.com](Ubuntu.com) and download the latest CD then burn the disk (Or USB if you wish) and put/plug it in your laptop. After that, turn it on and look closely for something refering to Boot-Menu. Press the button it refers, if you can't find it, try pressing Escape, F12 or F10, the most common buttons for it (In my opinion). If you still can't find it, tell us the model and we could try finding it. Also you can try googling using "*laptop model* vaio bootmenu key"

After that, select either CD or USB, depending on what you burned and wait for a little while. It'll startup and offer you to install or try Ubuntu.
