### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-12-09
If you mean "talk", as in a phone call or video conferencing, then Skype can do it. It's free to download and is available for Linux and Windows, among other platforms.

If you mean "instant messages", then Facebook works for that, assuming you're both Facebookers. If you're not, then you can use the Yahoo or MSN protocols which are both fairly popular on Windows. There's no official Yahoo or MSN program for Linux, but the preinstalled Empathy program on Ubuntu can talk to Yahoo and MSN without issue.

Skype also works for instant messaging.
