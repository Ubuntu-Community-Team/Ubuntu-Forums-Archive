> **F411en said:**
> So ... now i have 3 different icons . 1 in "a-la start menu" 1 in quick launch panel , and 1 in taskbar of course .
[img]http://img2.pict.com/50/44/87/1576239/0/800/image.png[/img]
[http://img2.pict.com/50/44/87/1576239/0/800/image.png](http://img2.pict.com/50/44/87/1576239/0/800/image.png)

Yes, you would have 3 icons.  Each location you mentioned, the "start menu" if you will, the quick launch panel, and the taskbar are 3 separate "shortcuts" to launch Firefox so if you change 1, the other 2 will remain what they were.  If you want them all to be the same, you have to change all three.

Edit...

I was a bit mistaken...The taskbar icon you cannot change, as far as I know.  That is the icon associated with the program itself, so when you open FireFox and it puts the entry on the taskbar, that icon is from within Firefox itself and, again as far as I know, cannot be changed.
