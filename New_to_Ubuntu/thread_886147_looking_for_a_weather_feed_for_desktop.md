### Post by speeddad on 2008-08-10
For KDE 3

Click on the big K &#8594; choose System Settings &#8594; under Personal click on Regional & Language &#8594; County/Region & Language should already be highlighted, so click on the Other tab to the right &#8594; in the Measure system drop down select Imperial

For KDE 4

Click on the &#8592; arrow &#8594; under computer choose System Settings &#8594; under Personal click on Regional & Language &#8594; County/Region & Language should already be highlighted, so click on the Other tab to the right &#8594; in the Measure system drop down select Imperial

You will need to start Kweather in order for the change to take effect.
