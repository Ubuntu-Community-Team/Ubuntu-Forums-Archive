### Post by eldragon on 2008-11-13
to setup your audio through the usb headphones, you need to go to:

the blue S at the bottom of the skype window, click there, then click on options.

there you need to go to audio

and set incoming and outgoing, through your usb headphones (there should be a name identifying them, if they are configured correctly)
