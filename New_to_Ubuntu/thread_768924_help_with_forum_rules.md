### Post by kellemes on 2008-04-26
> **1scorpio said:**
> I keep seeing the word bump (ie bumped your thread) but I am not sure what that means. is there a way to repost the same thread more than once?

You can bump your thread you self if you had a long wait without any answer, by 'bumping' you're thread will fly to the top of the list again, increasing the chance people will find your thread.
This way you don't need to create a double post.

Not sure what the forums rules say.. but for me it's reasonable to bump not sooner then a 24 hour wait.
