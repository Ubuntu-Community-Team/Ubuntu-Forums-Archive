I want to temporally but completely drive away the gnome panel to work with the newly installed dock. When I gave the command "killall gnome-panel" all two panels disappear but after a second they reappear. How can I "Kill" that process to stop it re-appearing until I restart my pc?

### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-12-07
Go to: **Main Menu > System > Preferences > Options**, and click on **Save Currently Running Application**.
