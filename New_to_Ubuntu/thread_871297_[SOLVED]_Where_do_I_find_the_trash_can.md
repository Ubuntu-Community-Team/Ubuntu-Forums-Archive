### Post by caljohnsmith on 2008-07-26
The trash is located in ~/.local/share/Trash. The ~ means your home directory, so you can navigate to the trash in Nautilus (the Gnome file browser) by going to /home/<username>/.local/share/Trash. Or maybe there is a Trash icon you can click on in Nautilus, I don't remember and I'm not there now. :)
