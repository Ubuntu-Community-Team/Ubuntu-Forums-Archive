### Post by ginbuntu on 2008-11-13
I don't think that will work with the iphone unless you jailbreak the iphone. Because the filesystem is encrypted. 
One way to transfer music to your iPhone without jailbreaking is to Vmware with "Shared Folder" enabled.  then install iTunes inside vmware.
To connect the iphone to the VM, just go to VM->remoavble devices in the menubar of vmware then chose connect iphone. be sure to unmount your iphone first, because Ubuntu asks you to mount it as a camera or not when you plug your iphone in. 
I am using this method on Ubuntu Intrepid amd64 and is working great.
