### Post by Darkwing-Duck on 2009-09-30
Assuming you already have Compizconfig installed...

System&#8594;Preferences&#8594;Advanced Desktop Effects Setting

Click on the Cube Caps plugin and there is a place for the top and bottom images.

---

### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-09-30
Pick "Cube refection..."  from the Compiz menu and then pick "cube cap".

The setting for the top and bottom of the cube is there.
