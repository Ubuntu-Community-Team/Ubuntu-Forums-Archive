### Post by andreazzz on 2013-10-14
Can you make sure you do not have WiFi turned of with a switch on the laptop? Most laptops have such a switch; with an illuminated WiFi logo/text or with a function key (fn-key near alt and ctrl) and a numeric key (with a WiFi logo).
