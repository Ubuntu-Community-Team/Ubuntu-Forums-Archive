### Post by dcsoldschool53 on 2011-04-12
**Right click on what you want to burn. Click open with or open with other application. Scroll down until you see Brasero. Click Brasero. When Brasero opens, you will be ready to burn your iso.**

### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-04-12
Just as the above posts say - simply open Brasero and select to burn an image to a disk, then burn at the slowest speed available.

If you are or need to burn on a Windows computer I like [ImgBurn]("http://www.imgburn.com/"). Follow the same process, open ImgBurn, select burn image to disk, then write at a slow speed.
