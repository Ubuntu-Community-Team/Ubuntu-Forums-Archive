I just a week ago used aptoncd to install 10.10 on another machine without all the downloading.  

I put the dvd i burned into the drive and after it saw the disc it asked if i wanted to run synaptic , i said yes then after synaptic started i just clicked the apply button.  All the packages on the dvd that could be installed were all ready selected.

I installed what was on the dvd.  You must first make a iso from the aptoncd command and then burn it to a cd or dvd which ever you are using.

I have never had a problem with aptoncd.
