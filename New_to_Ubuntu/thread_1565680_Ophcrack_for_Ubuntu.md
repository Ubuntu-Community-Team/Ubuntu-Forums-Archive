### Post by ayenack on 2010-09-01
Hello newport_j.

You can download a Live CD of Ophcrack and install it to a USB Stick or CD. 

[http://ophcrack.sourceforge.net/](http://ophcrack.sourceforge.net/)

It will most likely not be what you are looking for as it automatically recovers passwords with very little input from the users. If you're interested in cryptography I'd suggest searching synaptic for such apps and buying a few books also.

If I remember correctly Ophcrack will not recover passwords longer than 15 characters long either, as these are stored in a different place in windows boxes and encrypted also.

I have had cause to use Ophcrack in the past when recovering passwords for Windows systems and it's very efficient at what it does.
