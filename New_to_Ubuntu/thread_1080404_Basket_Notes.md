### Post by Aearenda on 2009-09-18
I just came across this - Basket seems to be a KDE3 app, and takes its defaults from the KDE settings. It ignores qt3-config, and the answer ought to be 'kcontrol' but since KDE4 came out that's gone and there is 'systemsettings' but this doesn't seem to be backward-compatible. In the end I used a script called uniformUI to achieve this, from [this page]("http://andrealazzarotto.com/2009/06/14/uniformui-is-the-definitive-way-to-integrate-qt-and-kde-apps-in-the-gnome-desktop/"). Kudos to Andrea Lazzarotto!
