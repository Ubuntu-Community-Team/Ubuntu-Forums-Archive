### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-04-30
Before you jump into using Wine, it's worth checking the CodeWeavers compatibility database to see if your app is listed as having been tested and to see the rating.

Below is one of their pages:

[=ASC;curPos=50"]http://www.codeweavers.com/compatibility/browse/name/?letter=l;showall=1;sort[app_name]=ASC;curPos=50]("http://www.codeweavers.com/compatibility/browse/name/?letter=l;showall=1;sort[app_name)

As you can see Lightspeed is not even listed.  So, unless you're really lucky, it's not likely to work at all.
