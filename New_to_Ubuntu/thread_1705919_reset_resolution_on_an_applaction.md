### Post by Perfect Storm on 2011-03-13
Try look for its configuration file/folder in your home directory (hidden). If you delete that file/folder it will reset to default (beware that user changes, saved stuff etc. in dreamchess will be deleted as well).

### Post by wolfen69 on 2011-03-31
> **neanderslob said:**
> For the record guys, could you fill me in on what exactly that file was?  Thanks! :)

If the app in question is called dreamchess, then you would go to your home folder and do Ctrl-H to see your hidden files. Then you would look for a folder called .dreamchess or something similar. If you do not see one, look in the .config folder for it. This applies to all apps with config files.
