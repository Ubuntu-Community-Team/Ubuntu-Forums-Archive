### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-09-24
You may need to switch from the internal to external mic. To do that, open Volume Controls from the speaker icon in the panel, click Preferences and check the boxes related to input of microphone. You'll then have more tabs in the Volume Controls window, and one of them should have the switch.
