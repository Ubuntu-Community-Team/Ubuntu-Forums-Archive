### Post by fraser_m on 2009-08-22
Right click on your Desktop, and uncheck "Keep Aligned".

### Post by kelvin.illa on 2009-08-22
if i get your question:

if you've the icon (the Trash, in particular) you want to move already on the desktop, then just right click on the desktop and uncheck "Keep Aligned." That will enable your icons to be free from the imaginary grid.
