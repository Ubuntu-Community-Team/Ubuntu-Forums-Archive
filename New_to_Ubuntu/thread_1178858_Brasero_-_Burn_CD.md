### Post by woedend on 2009-06-05
That depends on your speed.  The speed is 150KB/S x you write speed.  As an easy way to remember...I always think of a 1x of taken 1 hour to burn a full CD(although not perfectly accurate) and then 2x taking a half hour...etc etc.  Most burners run much faster.  But to be fairly accurate, multiply .15 by your burn speed, then divide 700 by that number.  Thats about how many seconds it will take.
