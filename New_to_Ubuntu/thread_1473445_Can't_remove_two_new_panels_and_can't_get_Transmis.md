### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-05-05
Hi,
For the panel question, log out, and then log back in, then those two side panels will appear. Then you can keep them or delete them.

Do you have Indicator Applet in the panel? Indicator applet has the application icons for certain applications that are compatible with it. All other application icons go into the notification area. If you don't have it, simply right click on the panel, select "Add to Panel", then choose Indicator Applet.
