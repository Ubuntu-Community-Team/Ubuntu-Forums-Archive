### Post by Anzan on 2008-08-13
Yes, just backup your home dir. 

You are probably using Simple Home Backup.

One thing: don't forget to have it delete previous full backups a day or so  after your cycle of 7 days.

And never forget to have that drive running when the backups are set or else it will likely back up your home directory to your home directory thus doubling the contents. (I did this last year once,)
