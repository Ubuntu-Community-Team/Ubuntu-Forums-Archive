### Post by jtarin on 2010-09-26
You need to be careful what you update. If something is not broken why fix it? Normally the driver/module for your chip that is included in Ubuntu is the best one available for working with your system. If you download and install one from Intel that is not always a guarantee for better performance. The chip your using is not the best you can possibly have for gaming. It doesn't work that well in Windows. If your a serious gamer I would recommend upgrading to a video card, but check the Linux hardware compatibility list before you buy. Nvidia is a good choice but know your hardware and installation methods before proceeding with any video updates. If you lose your screen....what will you need to do to recover?
