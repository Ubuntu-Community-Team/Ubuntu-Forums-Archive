Isn't the full message 'iTouch disabled. Connect to iTunes.'?

If so, connect it to iTunes and click on the iTouch and click 'Synchronize'. It should ask you for a passcode and unlock it.

If the passcode has been forgotten, click through the tabs until you see the button 'Restore'. This will reimage your iTouch - so you will lose all data on it.
