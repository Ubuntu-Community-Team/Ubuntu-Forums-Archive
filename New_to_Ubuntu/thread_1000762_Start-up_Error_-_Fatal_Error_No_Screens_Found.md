### Post by bumanie on 2008-12-03
Probably your best bet would be to try the alternate installation cd. It doesn't come in an 'ultimate' version as this is (ASFAIK) a third party version, not supported by Canonical. The alternate cd is a text based installer that usually overcomes installation issues such as you are having. Anything on the 'ultimate dvd' can be added later - most of the programs will be in the repositories.
