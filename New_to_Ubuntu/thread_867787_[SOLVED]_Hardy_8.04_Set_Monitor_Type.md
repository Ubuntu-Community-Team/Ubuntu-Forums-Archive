Quick way: from the terminal ```
 gksudo displayconfig-gtk
```Otherwise, right click Applications, select Edit Menu, scroll down to Other under Applications, to the right will be some options, one of which is Screens and Graphics. Click on it, and that will cause that application to appear under the Applications Menu, Other (it shows up on the right).
