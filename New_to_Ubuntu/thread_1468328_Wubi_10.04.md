### Post by bubblehead74 on 2010-05-01
Windows is unaffected by Wubi installation, but I suggest to study Wubi FAQs first:
[http://wubi-installer.org/faq.php](http://wubi-installer.org/faq.php)

"The [Ubuntu] performance is identical to a standard installation, except for hard-disk access which is slightly slower than an installation to a dedicated partition. If your hard disk is very fragmented the performance will degenerate."
