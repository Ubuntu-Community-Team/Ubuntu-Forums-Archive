### Post by wredxiii on 2013-01-04
I know this thread is almost a month old, but I wanted to point out that a low latency kernel will not improve gaming performance. I say you should stick with the vanilla kernel, and if you want to experiment there are tons of different kernel patches on the net which do certain things (or more likely nothing).

The bottom line though; there are no kernels which will improve your FPS (a noticeable amount). Only a better GPU will do that.

But there's no reason why you switch from Studio!  XFCE is awesome.
