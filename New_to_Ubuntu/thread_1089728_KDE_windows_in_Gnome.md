### Post by jpeddicord on 2009-03-07
Because that Oxygen theme was made for KWin, you can't do it easily. It's possible to replace metacity with KWin in your GNOME session, but you might lose some integration features.

GNOME-Look might have what you are looking for:
[http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=101](http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=101)
There might be an Oxygenish theme in there made for Metacity.
