### Post by vikramaditya on 2008-08-01
I love Compiz.
And AWN/Cairo/Kiba
And Screenlets.
I just really do.
I crave all the spinning, bouncing, leaping, swinging, twisting, fading, pulsing, kaleidoscopic 3D eye-treacle a human brain can endure.
Damn the seizures, full OpenGL ahead.
The only problem is my crusty Radeon 7500 PCI vid card.  Oh, it *kinda* works *kinda* okay with Compiz enabled...but not like...well, you know.  And just trying to scroll in my fave browser?  Fugeddaboudit.
So, I do some research.  
Lots of it.
And the *buntu forum sages nod wisely as one and say "Well, dude!  There's your problem!  Dontcha know ATI is ****?  Yeah, man, ya gotta go nVidia to breed with the big penguins.  And it's gotta have a fan on it, man, cuz it's, like, hot, man.
Well, it turns out newegg has a card with nVidia's FX 5500 chipset.  A tad "legacy", but hey, it's 256mb 128-bit DDR AGP 4X/8X.  Yeah, that's the ticket.  But, what's this?  No fan?  "Dont fret", the newegg reviewers all say, "It's got a great heat sink".  Hmmm...$29.99 + $6.99 S&H.  Holy ****, that's almost half-a-tank of gas.  This little slab of Greater East Asian Co-Prosperity Sphere wizardry better whistle Dixie in 12 ancient languages.
So, I bought it.  In fact, it'll be here next Tuesday.
Now, I've berserked around this forum long enough to gather that I may be asking for trouble.  Not a day goes by without legions posting reams of Terminal jargon in a desperate plea for answers to...yeah...nVidia problems.  Sometimes, the threads get marked "SOLVED".  Sometimes, they live forever.  I just want the pretty little 3D thingies to joyfully jump and play until I'm totally bored with them.  So, please remember me and open your hearts if I grovel humbly on bended knee in my (possibly pending) hour of darkness. :)

... and this:

[http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Check](http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Check)

In the right hand column, Forlong has a downloadable patch called 'Compiz-Switch'. Great for easily switching off and on if you are starting to get a bit dizzy!!! Good luck and have fun (try and stop him!) lol :)
