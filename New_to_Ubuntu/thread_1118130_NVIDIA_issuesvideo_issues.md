When I have it activated, my FPS is at around 6000 to 7000 (which makes gaming very smooth), but videos are really weird. ### Post by Sealbhach on 2009-04-06
I had those weird colours using Totem movie player. Try this with Totem:

Edit/Preferences/ click the Display tab.

Turn the Hue all the way down to zero.

It's a workaround.

See here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=769209](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=769209)
