### Post by mlentink on 2009-03-01
Is this a USB-WLAN adapter you are talking about? If so, you cannot use it to connect to a Wireless Internet. The latter usually works on GPRS/UMTS bandwiths, different from those used by WLAN. You would need a USB-dongle or PC-card which usually is provided by your Wireless Internet provider. If you have a standard ISP, you can connnect to it wirelessly through a WLAN router.
