### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-01-08
Personally, I see no pressing reason to do so.

Let me explain:

10.04 is an LTS release, meaning it has updates for the Desktop version until 2013

10.10 is new and there are still issues with it

10.04 is already 'mature,' meaning that most of the initial problems have been ironed out and it is stable

10.10 is not that different from 10.04; a few cosmetic changes, a few programs replaced or updated

Just my opinion.
