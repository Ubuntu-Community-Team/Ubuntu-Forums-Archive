### Post by valke on 2008-05-09
so you are installing a XP Pro on a virtual partition image and it just hangs...

First, check the disc for errors. If it hangs that would be my first guess. 

Second, when it comes to Ubuntu hanging, kill the VirtualBox process by going to System>Administration>System Monitor and look for Vbox in the process list, and kill it.
