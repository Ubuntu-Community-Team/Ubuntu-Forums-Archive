### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2008-12-19
Are you wanting to completly remove the current 8.04 install? If so, when you get to the partioner in the 8.10 installation, format you current partion to whichever filsystem you use (ext3 is the default) and you will end up with a clean install. Just be sure to back up all of your important data, because you will loose it with this method. You can backup your entire home directory to preserve most of your settings.
