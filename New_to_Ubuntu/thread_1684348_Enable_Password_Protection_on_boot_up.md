### Post by matt_symes on 2011-02-09
Hi

Not sure what version of Ubuntu have installed but i will go out on a limb and guess.

Go to 'System->Administration->Logon Screen' from the main menu. Press the unlock button and enter your password.

Uncheck 'Log in as ... automatically' and close the dialog.

If you cannot find the 'Logon screen' dialog from the menu open a terminal and enter

```
gnome-control-center
```

and you should find it there.

Kind regards
