### Post by Mortesins93 on 2010-07-18
try Alt-F2 and then type "xfce4-panel"
If this works just for one session and doesn't work when you reboot then try adding it to the Applications autostart in Settings>Session and Startup
You should find your network manager applet in your top panel on the right, if not Alt-F2 and then type "nm-applet"
