### Post by robsoles on 2010-12-25
Best way to learn: Try stuff, ask questions, try your question in Google before posting it.

How long till you are accustomed: Longer if you focus on how long it's taking.

Best Language: Depends on your purpose - C is a good base language, it is itself powerful and it's derivatives are very powerful. Other languages that suit your target platform(s) should be considered as well though.

How long to learn <specific-language>: If you 'gel' with the language then it is roughly the time it takes to read about all the commands and structures the language uses/allows plus the time to try enough to be sure it does what you think it will - there are too many variables to decide how long an individual will take to learn anything but it will be longer if they don't feel comfortable (gel) with it.


These are, of course, just my opinions on the questions posed and needn't be taken as law or anything silly like that ;)
