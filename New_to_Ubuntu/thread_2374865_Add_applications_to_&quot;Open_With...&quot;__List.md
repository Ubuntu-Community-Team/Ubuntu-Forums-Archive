### Post by sterator on 2017-10-19
When I right click on images in folders, and select "Open With.." I get about 8 candidates, but none of them GIMP, which I also have on this machine.

How can I add Gimp or any other apps to this list?

Thank you!

---

### Post by drs305 on 2017-10-19
When  you right click the file in nautilus and look at the Open with Other Application, there should be a panel at the bottom to Find New Applications. Clicking on that should open Ubuntu Software Center where you can select Gimp.

Added: Also, while looking at the available apps after selecting "Open with Other Apps", you can right click an app in the list to remove it from future selection options.
