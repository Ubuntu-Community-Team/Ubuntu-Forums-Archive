### Post by Kevin McCready on 2012-03-26
I'm a Chinese translator and use ibus-pinyin, which needed a bit of tweaking to make it work. 

I also had to download the language files. In Lubuntu this was under Preferences > Language Support > Install/Remove Languages (you can also check your input method in the same dialogue box). Then a box comes up which says "installed languages". 

In fact the title is misleading because the languages aren't installed until you double click on them (a flaw in Lubuntu/Ubuntu?). 

Then I reboot, just to make sure it all sticks. 

The help screens for ibus are awful and it might take time to figure out the inbuilt but badly documented functionality.

Good luck.
