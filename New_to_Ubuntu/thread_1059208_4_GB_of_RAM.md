### Post by jeffathehutt on 2009-02-03
In general, RAM is backwards compatible, meaning if you buy the faster ram, it would slow itself down to whatever your computer can handle.  BUT, not all systems can do that.  So, I would just buy the RAM you know will work because you won't notice much difference in speed.
