### Post by Kevin McCready on 2013-02-19
In order to install remastersys I'm trying to confirm:
1.  my version of Lubuntu (12.04, 11, 10 or whatever) and 
2. the name of it, eg precise, oneiric, lucid etc

I followed the instructions on 
[http://www.remastersys.com/ubuntu.html](http://www.remastersys.com/ubuntu.html)

When I got to the point 3 I might have made a mistake.
3.  Still in synaptic, go to "Other Software" tab and click "Add", then enter the apt line and replace oneiric with either lucid, maverick, or natty to match your Ubuntu version:

    deb [http://www.remastersys.com/ubuntu](http://www.remastersys.com/ubuntu) precise main

    or if you are using quantal

    deb [http://www.remastersys.com/ubuntu](http://www.remastersys.com/ubuntu) quantal main
