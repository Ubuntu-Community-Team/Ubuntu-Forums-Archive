### Post by oldrocker99 on 2019-10-04
In the "Shut Down" wizard, there's a "Suspend" option. Hit that. It's the same thing the system does when you close a laptop.

Ubuntu 14.04 reached its End of Life last April 30, 5 years after release, and there's no more support. 

By **ALL** means, install 18.04 as soon as possible. 20.04 is coming next April.

A new system install might speed up boot time, also. Things have changed in the last 5 years.

You do have a separate / partition and a separate /home partition, I assume. Back up the entire /home folder, anyway.
