### Post by teward on 2009-11-23
I know keyloggers exist on Windows, but I'm not sure they exist on Ubuntu.  (ATTN: Experienced Techs.  Feel free to correct me)

As far as I know, most keyloggers embed themselves in the input/output stream areas of the computer, where the computer translates the electronic codes into the codes the OS understands, then makes the keyboard work.  Also, keyloggers only log keyboard input.  Most security companies recommend the use of an on-screen keyboard for secure typing because keyloggers don't track mouse-clicks.  However, these on-screen keyboards are tedious, and not very useful for everyday computing.
