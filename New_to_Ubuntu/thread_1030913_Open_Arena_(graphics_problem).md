### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2009-01-05
This might be caused by your screen-saver trying to come on, or your power-saver features trying to turn off your monitor.  For some reason, Ubuntu doesn't always notice any user activity when you are playing full-screen games.

Try turning off your screen-saver and playing the game again and see if that helps.
