### Post by dondiego2 on 2010-02-02
> **philipballew said:**
> well the device is on the desktop with its icon and it gives me the option to unmount when i right click so i assume its mounted,


1) Eject your iPod
2) alt F2 and type nautilus -q and check run in terminal and hit the run button
3) plug your iPod in again
4) go to Places and find your Ipod and click it.
5) Open up Banshee and it should be there.

According to some stuff I found this is known bug that is being fixed.
