### Post by jimreynold2nd on 2008-11-19
Well, AFAIK, there is no forked Ubuntu version for the Palm Treo :( Sorry. Actually, if you think about it, Windows (on computers) and Windows Mobile are two completely different OSes, they uses different kernels and modules.
To put it simple: you cannot run your Windows programs on your Win Mobile and vice versa.
Conclusion: The fact that you can replace Windows(NT kernel) with Ubuntu(linux kernel) doesn't mean you can replace Windoze Mobile(CE kernel) with Ubuntu.
