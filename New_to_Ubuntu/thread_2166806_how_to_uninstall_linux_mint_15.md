### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-08-11
Confused about what you said about Win7.  Once activated, a legal copy does NOT expire.  If you don't activate it, it will run for 30 days after install, after which it will complain and drop into reduced usability mode.  There are also ways to extend that 30 days up to 120 days -- but after that, you do have to activate it.

We need to see the layout of your drive, so boot into Mint, open a terminal and enter "sudo fdisk -lu" (lowercase L, not a one). Post the output back here.
