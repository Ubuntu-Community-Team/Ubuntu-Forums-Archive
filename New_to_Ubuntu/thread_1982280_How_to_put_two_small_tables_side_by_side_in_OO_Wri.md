### Post by EB0V on 2012-05-18
What you can do to have perfectly symetric tables is to create a single table of 9 columns x 4 rows and make the central column's outlines invisible.

### Post by swarup on 2012-05-18
Thank you! That is an excellent idea. What I just did was to make two frames and put a table inside each- and that actually worked very nicely. But your idea gives a perfect symmetry as you note, which is very helpful indeed.:p
