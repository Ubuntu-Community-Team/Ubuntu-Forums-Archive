### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2014-07-18
Language / Regional Formats settings and keyboard layout settings are independent. I'm using a notebook with a US International keyboard layout in Spain but with language and regional settings to Portuguese / Portugal.

I've heard something about a recent bug resulting in the keyboard layout reverting to US regardless of the system wide settings. In standard Ubuntu there's now an applet to toggle keyboard layouts and the workaround is to change to US and back to the other.
