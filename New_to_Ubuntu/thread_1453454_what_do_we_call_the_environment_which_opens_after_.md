I usually refer to it as a "Virtual Console"

tty3 is the device name that the computer uses when running, for example, the login program. If you were to press Ctrl+Alt+F2, you would open tty2.

And the gnome-terminal window I refer to as a terminal, though console and terminal are often interchangeable.
