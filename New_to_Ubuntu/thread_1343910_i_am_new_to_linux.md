### Post by nothingspecial on 2009-12-03
Good morning from Manchester England.

Using your windows computer.......

Once you have downloaded Ubuntu 9.04 go [[COLOR="Magenta"]here[/COLOR]]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/") and download the windows version.

You will need a usb stick, a 1 gigabyte one will be fine. When unetbootin has downloaded open it up and check the disk image box half way down the window.

To the right of that there is a square to click that will allow you to navigate to where you saved the ubuntu iso file.

Select your usb stick in the at the bottom. I think you will have to safely remove it first, but leave it plugged in.

Click ok, it will take about 4 or 5 minutes to complete.

Once it`s done, plug the usb into your aspire one and restart. As soon as the screen lights up your going to have to press F12 or F8 or one of the F buttons to get into your netbooks bios. When your in, you need to find the boot order section and select boot from usb drive.

When ubuntu comes up click the install button and follow the instructions.

If you have nothing you want to save on your netbook, select use the entire disk - you`ll see what I mean when you get there.
