### Post by jemp on 2010-07-25
Hey guys - I have moved a room in halls of residence and could't get ubunto to connect to the internet here. I have an AMD 64 compaq laptop running both window vista (I know I am sorry :)) and ubunto lucid.

I have not changed the network connection settings at all, it still uses DHCP. When I attempt to connect to the internet through Auto eth0 it says something like: No wired connection - you are now offline. 

If anyone knows what might be going wrong here your help will be much appreciated!. Please note that internet connection works fine on vista.

---

### Post by uRock on 2010-07-25
Right click on the network manager status and select edit connections. Click on Wired Connection 1 and delete it. Then create another one and use the same open settings. I have had to do this a couple of times, so hopefully it will work for you, too.
