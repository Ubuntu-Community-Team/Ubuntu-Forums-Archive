### Post by oldos2er on 2009-03-20
The NNTP server that gives you Usenet access. It used to be that ISPs provided Usenet access through their own newsservers, nowadays many ISPs no longer do this. You need to determine whether your ISP offers Usenet or not. 

 There are quite a few free newsservers available, but most of them are read-only.
