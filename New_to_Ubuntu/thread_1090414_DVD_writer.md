### Post by mrbiggbrain on 2009-03-08

Yes however you need UDF 2.5 (Ubuntu 8.10 Intrepid Ibex) as well as not haveing any support for BD+ or other encryption methods for playback. also becuse of these limitations blu-ray movies need a full dump via HDdump, and to be decrypted well being dumped. That takes about 50GB on your HDD to do. there are no software players for raw blu-ray disks (that i know), but it does get detected, and cd/dvd disks can be play automaticly, and the data can be read if UDF 2.5 is loaded
