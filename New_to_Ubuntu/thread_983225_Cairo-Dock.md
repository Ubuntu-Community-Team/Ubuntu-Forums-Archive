### Post by whitethorn on 2008-11-15
There are a couple ways (I can think of 3).
1)
> **Squid Tamer said:**
> I think you can use the "run" program, or you could add a button to the panel. My cario-dock added a button to Applications>System tools, so you might be able to click that.

2) You press Alt + F2 at the same time and type cairo-dock in.

3) If you what to cairo-dock to start whenever you log in, you have to add it to your sessions.  To do that click on System -> Preferences -> Sessions.

Then under the Startup Programs tab click on add.  Then type cairo-dock in each.
