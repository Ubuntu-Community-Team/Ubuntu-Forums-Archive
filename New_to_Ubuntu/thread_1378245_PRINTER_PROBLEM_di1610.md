### Post by cariboo on 2010-01-11
Your printer isn't listed in the [Openprinting database]("http://www.openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=KONICA+MINOLTA"). I would check the cups web interface to see if the printer is paused there. To access the web interface, open your favorite browser and in the url bar type:

[http://localhost:631]("http://localhost:631")
