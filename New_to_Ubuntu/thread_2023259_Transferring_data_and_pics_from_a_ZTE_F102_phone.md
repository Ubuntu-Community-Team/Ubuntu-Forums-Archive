### Post by Kelvari on 2012-07-23
Though I'm not entirely sure what Scandisk would be (maybe Sandisk?), I'd be willing to bet that that is either an SD or a MiniSD card reader. Your phone may have come with a memory card adapter, if the memory card came with the phone, or the card itself may have come with the adapter. 

There will be no question about Linux compatibility with the adapter and Linux, as the adapter itself is barely more than a hunk of plastic with traces going from the MicroSD card to the appropriate contacts within the reader.
