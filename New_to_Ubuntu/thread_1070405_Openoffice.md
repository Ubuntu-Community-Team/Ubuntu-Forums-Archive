### Post by Paqman on 2009-02-15
Is it a Writer document, or a spreadsheet in Calc?

If it's Calc, then just click to highlight the column you want, then hit the little A-->Z button.

If it's Writer, then select your text then Tools > Sort and the defaults should be what you want.

Same as MS Office, really.
