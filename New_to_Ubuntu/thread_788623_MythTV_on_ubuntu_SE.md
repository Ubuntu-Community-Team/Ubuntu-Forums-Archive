### Post by Monicker on 2008-05-25
Oops.  Sorry for the delay in getting back to you. Basically mythtv is a linux PVR (personal video recorder) system.  I use it mainly to record tv shows for me and my family, and watch them at a later time.  Its nice to be able to skip the commercials.  My wife has a favorite soap that I burn to DVD+RW for her.  It has other plugins and features, such as playing music files, but I rarely use those.  The web interface comes in handy for scheduling shows and changing the priority of what needs to be recorded.  I use a pci capture card which gets fed by our cable.  Now I just with I had a cared with more than one 1 tuner, so that I can record more than one show at the same time.  :)
