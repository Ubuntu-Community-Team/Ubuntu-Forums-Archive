### Post by dominiquec on 2010-04-08
If you're talking about a wired connection, just plug an Ethernet cable into the port of your computer.  Ubuntu is set up for DHCP by default.

If you're talking about wifi, look through the icons on the top right edge of your icons.  Clicking on one of those will show you what available access points there are.

### Post by adam22 on 2010-04-08
At the top right, right click the internet connection icon and ENABLE Wireless. You should then be able to see any networks that are available, and connect to them if unsecured. If secured, right-click>manage connections, and add yours. Scan for local networks and enter the security key, and you are done.
