I got no reply from here, however, what worked for me was this:

[http://monespaceperso.org/blog-en/2010/05/02/upgrade-alsa-1-0-23-on-ubuntu-lucid-lynx-10-04/]("http://monespaceperso.org/blog-en/2010/05/02/upgrade-alsa-1-0-23-on-ubuntu-lucid-lynx-10-04/")

Then just after finishing make sure that the Analog Output Audio (not the HDMI) is detected.

Then from the sound setting under System > Preferences, make sure you choose Analog Output from the OUTPUT tab.
