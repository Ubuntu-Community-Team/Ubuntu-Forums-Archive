### Post by sdennie on 2008-06-17
For some laptops there will be an option in System->Preferences->Power Management called something like "Dim when idle" (I don't have the option so, I'm not sure of the exact name).  See if you have that option in Power Management settings and uncheck it in both the "On AC" and "On Battery" tabs.
