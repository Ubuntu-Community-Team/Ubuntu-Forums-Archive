### Post by kingfisher.ca on 2010-06-06
I have 10.04 and just plugged my 595u in 10 minutes ago for the first time. I opened network manager applet, and clicked on the available mobile broadband and followed the instructions (click on country and click on provider -  in my case Canada and Telus). 10 seconds later I was connected. I tried this on my desktop to see how it would work before deciding to move my laptop form XP to Ubuntu as well (I really need the 595 to work on my laptop).
