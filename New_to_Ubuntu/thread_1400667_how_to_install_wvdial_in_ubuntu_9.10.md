### Post by thulefoth on 2010-02-07
Hi rishabh1711,  

You don't have to use the source code method for wvdial, or worry about its dependencies. 

Just go up to the top of your screen in the 'panel' area, and click System > Administrator > Synaptic Package Manager.  After you have typed in your password for SPM, type 'wvdial' in the little Quick Search box at the top.  It will then show the program you want.  Check it's box to select it, then go up to the Menu area and click Apply. 

When you select wvdial, it will show a list of dependencies it needs.  Click Mark to select them, too.  When you click Apply, there is another info-box shown.  Look them both over, glance through the other tabs a bit, just to familiarize yourself ... but there isn't much else to do.

Hope it goes smoothly for you ... let us know?
