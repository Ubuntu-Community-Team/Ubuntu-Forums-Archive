### Post by quadproc on 2011-02-10

> Can someone provide me with some documentation and linkage - or perhaps explanations, if anyone feels frisky - of the following things?

My response doesn't exactly answer your questions but since you seem to have an intestest in crypto then you will probably be interested in [http://www.philzimmermann.com/EN/essays/WhyIWrotePGP.html](http://www.philzimmermann.com/EN/essays/WhyIWrotePGP.html)

Please note that PGP is readily available to anyone who wants it, but that part of it is not free software.  Accordingly, the folks interested in free software modified it slightly to avoid the need for proprietary code and came up with GPG.  GPG is free software.  You may wish to look at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=680292](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=680292)

If your interest includes hard copy then Kahn's _The Code Breakers _is a good choice.
