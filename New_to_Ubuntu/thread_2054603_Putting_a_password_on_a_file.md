### Post by papibe on 2012-09-07
Hi DaveMcC.

Yes. There are several solution depending on your situation.

For example, if the 'other' user has her own account, you can easily solve the privacy problem just setting the proper permissions for your files and folders.

If you both share/use the same user, the safest solution would be some sort of encryption. For instance, you could just zip/rar your files using a password. That would solve the problem, but it would make your own access to your files not that convenient.

A further step would be to encrypt a folder using enfs. Take a look at this [tutorial]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FolderEncryption").

Lastly, you may want to encrypt a whole hard drive or partition. In that case you may take a look ate [TrueCrypt]("http://www.truecrypt.org/").

Hope that helps, and let us know how it goes.
