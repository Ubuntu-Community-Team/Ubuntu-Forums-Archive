### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-07-04
You click the icon that looks like a home on the upper left and then click the entry for your Win7 partition.

But -- be advised -- that if you mount your Win7 OS partition read-write and make changes to it from inside Ubuntu, you risk corrupting the file system and rendering it unbootable.
