### Post by appier on 2008-08-13
Using the Applications, Add/Remove Programs, add a program called "Startup-Manager".  Once installed, you may find it under System, Administration, Startup-Manager.  The second tab over says, "Appearance".  At the bottom of that tab is a place to select or manage your Usplash themes.

Hope this helps!
