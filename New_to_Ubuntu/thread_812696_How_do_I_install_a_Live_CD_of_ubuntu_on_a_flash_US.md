### Post by k@e on 2008-05-30
I have installed various distributions on my USB stick several times so I think I can help you with that. Of course, you should check first, if your computer is capable of booting from USB devices.

First, you need to get straight what kind of constellation you want to have in the end. The easiest is to have two partitions, one FAT partition for copying data from one system to another and an ext2 partition where the Live CD contents need to be installed. I can guide you through that if you desire.

If you however want a persistent installation, that is quite a bit more complicated. In this case I suggest you follow the link of the above poster. It may differ from your desired constellation though.
