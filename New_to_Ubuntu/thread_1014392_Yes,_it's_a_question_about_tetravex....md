### Post by Hospadar on 2008-12-17
Do you know how to program? That would of course be step one.

While it is possible to download the source for just the package, I would go to the developers homepage (which I believe is here: [http://live.gnome.org/GnomeGames](http://live.gnome.org/GnomeGames) ) and get the source from them.  I would also try and get in contact with the developers of the project (email list, irc channel are good ways) as you'll probably need their help to get acquanted with the code.  Also, if you do manage to change the code such that you can disable the timer (or whatever other changes) you might be able to submit your patch to them and get it integrated into the package, so everbody can make use of your work.

More practically, you might be able to make a suggestion or feature request at their website, or to one of the developers directly.

happy programming!
