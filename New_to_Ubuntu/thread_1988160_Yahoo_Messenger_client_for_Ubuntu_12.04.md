You don't need to look very far. Empathy comes preinstalled on Ubuntu, and can handle the Yahoo Messenger protocol.

Otherwise you could look at Pidgin, or one of the web-based messengers such as Meebo (which is multi-protocol, just like Pidgin and Empathy).
