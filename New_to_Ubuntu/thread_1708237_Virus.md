### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-03-16
> **Grenage said:**
> Forget about viruses, you don't have one; you'll be wasting your time.

Try alternative DNS servers, clean your local cache, et cetera.


Aye, in Firefox go to Tools -> Clear Recent History, then you can clear Firefox's stored data.

If you are getting a poor response (very slow) from your ISP, an easy test is to switch to Google's open DNS servers at

```
8.8.8.8
8.8.4.4
```

You can do this by doing System -> Preferences -> Network Connections -> Edit your connection, then in the IPv4 tab, add you DNS servers
