### Post by viniciuscarvalho on 2010-05-17
Hi there! I've just moved from AWN to Cairo Dock.

One thing that I can't find how to configure is to keep the active window icon on the dock.

For instance, when I maximize the Chrome it vanishes from the dock, moving to another maximized window (Eclipse for instance) still does not bring the icon to the foreground. Only when either minimizing or clicking on the "Show Desktop" applet, the icons are displayed again.

How can I change (if I can) this behavior?

Regards

---

### Post by Calash on 2010-05-17
Go into the Configure area and press the button on the bottom that says "Advanced Mode"

At the top is a button that says "Taskbar"

Unchecking the second option should get you the behavior you are looking for.
