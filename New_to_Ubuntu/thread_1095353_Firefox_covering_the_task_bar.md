### Post by Therion on 2009-03-13
Are you running Compiz?  You are, arent't you?

Go to System/Advanced Desktop Settings (Compiz Config Settings Manager wherever it's located, I forget).
Scroll down to the "Utilities" section.
Open the "Workarounds" plugin.
Clear the checkbox for **Legacy Fullscreen Support**.


Joy?
