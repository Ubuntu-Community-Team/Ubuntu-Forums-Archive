### Post by RiceMonster on 2008-05-14
You're going to have to make the bar at the top again, but don't worry, it's easy. 

1) First, right click on the panel at the bottom and select "New Panel"
2) If it's not at the top, you should be able to change that with Right Click >> Properties, and change "orientation" to top
3) to get the menu back, right click on the panel at the top and select Main Menu
4) to get the date and time back, repeat step 3, but add clock
5) and finally, to get the "tray" back, add a notification area.

Lastly, if you're having try removing launchers/icons from the panel, just right click on it and select remove from panel.
