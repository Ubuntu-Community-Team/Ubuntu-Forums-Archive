### Post by karthick87 on 2010-11-25
Goto System>>Preferences>>Keyboard

It will open keyboard preferences,select Layouts tab

[[IMG]http://hosting11.imagecross.com/image-hosting-56/3280Screenshot-Keyboard-Preferences.png[/IMG]]("http://www.imagecross.com/")

Click Add.It will open a window,goto Language tab and select portuguese and then greek.
