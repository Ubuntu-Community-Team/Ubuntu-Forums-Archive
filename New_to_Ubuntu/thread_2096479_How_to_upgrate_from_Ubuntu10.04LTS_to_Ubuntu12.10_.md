### Post by zombifier25 on 2012-12-20
(you can't upgrade from 10.04 to 12.10 directly via the upgrade method. You must upgrade to 12.04 first)

You can download the 12.10 (or 12.04) .iso file, burn it to a CD or write it to a flash drive, bring it to your offline computer and have it boot from there. Then choose to install Ubuntu, and when asked how, choose to upgrade.
