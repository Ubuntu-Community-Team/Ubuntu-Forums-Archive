### Post by corncob on 2010-01-10
Hey Dude, open terminal in the Accessories menu and type
```
alacarte
```
Click on New Menu and give it the name you want.  You'll have to have something in it for it to show up on your desktop so highlight the folder that just appeared on the left hand side of the alacarte screen and click on 'New Item' and add what you want.
