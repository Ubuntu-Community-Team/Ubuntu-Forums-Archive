### Post by rax_m on 2008-08-27
I've used both evolution and thunderbird for my works desktop for the last 8 months. I started off with thunderbird and am now using evolution. 
I do like that with evolution I can see the days with appointments (bold) as well as a list of appointments displayed. If I recall you may be able to click on the appointment to open it. 
I think that even if you do not have evolution open, you can still get reminders popping up. I think if you try an edit a reminder/event it will open evolution. 

However, evolution does seem to be slightly slower for me than thunderbird. But not unusably so.
