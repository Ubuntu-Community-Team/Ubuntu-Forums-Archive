### Post by llamabr on 2009-02-14
This is unlikely to be a driver problem.

A quick google search tells me that the original firmware is somehow "locked", keeping you from mounting it as a usb device.  But I don't know anything about this device.

Next, you should make sure it's set to msc, not usb, mode.  This may be an easy switch in your settings.

Here's some instructions for unlocking it, which I can't vouch for:

[http://people.uleth.ca/~daniel.odonnell/Blog/unlocking-the-samsung-yp-k3j-for-use-with-as-a-usb-mass-storage-device-with-ogg-vorbis-support](http://people.uleth.ca/~daniel.odonnell/Blog/unlocking-the-samsung-yp-k3j-for-use-with-as-a-usb-mass-storage-device-with-ogg-vorbis-support)

Have you thought about rockbox?

Finally, plug it in, give it a few seconds, and then post the last few lines of dmesg
