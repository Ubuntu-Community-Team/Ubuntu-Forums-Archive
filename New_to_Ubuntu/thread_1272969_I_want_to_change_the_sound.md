### Post by mac9416 on 2009-09-22
Go to System -> Preferences -> Sound. Then under the "Sounds" tab look for Desktop -> Login. Click where is says "Default" and chose "Custom..." then browse to find your sound file. Then reboot and enjoy your new startup sound.

Good luck
