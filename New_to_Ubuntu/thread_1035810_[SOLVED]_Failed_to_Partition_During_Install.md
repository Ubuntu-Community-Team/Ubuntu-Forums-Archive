### Post by wizard10000 on 2009-01-10
> **Intercamino said:**
> Have I killed my computer?

Nah.  With only two exceptions I know of it's not possible to damage hardware with software  :)

The only way to reinitialize all the hardware in a PC is to unplug the thing from the wall - warm boots and even cold boots won't do it.

So shut the thing down, unplug it from the wall for a few seconds and then plug it back in - if that doesn't work check your BIOS settings to make sure that booting from CD is still enabled - I've seen computers crash hard enough to revert BIOS settings back to the default.

Good luck -
