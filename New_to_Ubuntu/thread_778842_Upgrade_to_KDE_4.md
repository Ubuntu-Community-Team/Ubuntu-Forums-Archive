> **lucky.developer said:**
> HI all,

i just downloaded KDE packages and installed it in my system.. and finally found that it was not KDE4 but KDE3 .
I now have KDE3

I want to upgrade it to KDE4. How should i do that:confused:Go to and click on the kmenu icon. Choose "system,adept manager, and when it loads put in your password & search for kde4-core. Right click on the line for kde4-core and choose request install. Then click on "apply at the top. The program will then install. You will now have the choice to load kde3 or kde4 in the menu when you first log into kde (click on the icon in the lower right that looks like a page before you enter your password). Good luck. P.S. I have had kde3 since it came out. I tried kde4 and didn't like it because it still has bugs.
