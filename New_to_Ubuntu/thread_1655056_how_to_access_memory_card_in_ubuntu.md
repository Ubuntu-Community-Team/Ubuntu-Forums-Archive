### Post by audiomick on 2010-12-29
Go to this icons down the left, and look for one that is called "Files and Folders" or something similar to that. My system is in German, and it is called "Datien und Ordner".

On mine, it is the second from the top, under Favorites.

Click on the "Files and folders" (or whatever it is called) entry, and you should see something similar to what it in the attached screenshot.

In the screenshot, my SD card is visible in the bottom section on the right. It is called "4.1 GB Dateisystem".


To unmount your card before you remove it, click on the little icon that looks a bit like the eject button on a lot of CD players and DVD players. A little triangle above a horizontal bar.
