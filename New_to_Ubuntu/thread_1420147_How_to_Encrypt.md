### Post by crlang13 on 2010-03-02
I'm not sure about encryption, but to make a folded/file hidden you just need to put a period in front of the name.

e.g.

.hidden_folder

But if you want to see it, all you have to do is show view > hidden folders

edit: 
By the way, the period thing is discussed in the beginner's guide: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1052065](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1052065) it's VERY helpful in converting you from being an absolute newbie to only a regular newbie!

### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-03-02
If you own the computer and don't have any qualms about installing a later version of Ubuntu, you could install 9.10. During the installation, it gives you the option to encrypt your entire home directory ("Require password to login and decrypt home directory" is the label on the button).

Obviously, if your home directory is encrypted, then those financial documents are also encrypted.

And it will be easier than setting up one of those aforementioned encryption packages.
