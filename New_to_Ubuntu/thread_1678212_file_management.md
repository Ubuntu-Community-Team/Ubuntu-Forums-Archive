### Post by robsoles on 2011-01-30
No. As soon as you 'cut', or 'copy' a second item the second item will replace the first on the 'clipboard' - if you 'cut' twice in a row, without an intervening 'paste' then the first file you 'cut' will be effectively restored. Nothing actually happens when you 'cut' (*not that I've seen in Nautilus) a file without a 'paste' action before your next cut or copy.
