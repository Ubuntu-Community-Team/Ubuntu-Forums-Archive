### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-11-19
Be aware that Wine is not enough for gaming -- all it does is allow you to install and run the programs (SOME of them).  You also need DirectX support in your video drivers and in your video card.

If you're running open-source drivers, I'm pretty sure you won't be getting the DirectX support you need for any of the current Windows games.
