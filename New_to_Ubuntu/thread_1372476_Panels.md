### Post by lotharmat on 2010-01-04
> **Greyhame said:**
> I just accidentally removed the volume control and networking tool from the panel at the top right of the screen.  How do I get it back?


If you are using ubuntu 9.10 then the volume control and network are all in one item called 'Notification Area'

Right click on the top panel and click 'add to panel' then select (or just begin typing) 'notification area'

Have fun!
