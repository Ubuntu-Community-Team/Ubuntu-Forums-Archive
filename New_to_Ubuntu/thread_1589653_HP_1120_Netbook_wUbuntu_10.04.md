### Post by mcmueller2711 on 2010-10-06
Hey everybody, just wanted to say that I have lurked and listened for awhile, recently dumped HP MIE OS on my 1120 netbook and gave Netbook Remix a try; didn't like the graphics and couldn't get the wireless working, so I tried 10.04 and voila! In minutes everything worked! There was a slight workaround on the wireless that was easily resolved via your forum on this subject; The results:

Boot time w/HP MIE OS: 35 seconds. Wireless connection establishes 1 minute 30 seconds after logon.
Boot time with Ubuntu 10.04: 18 seconds, wireless connects immediately at logon. 

Thanks for a job well done, guys!
