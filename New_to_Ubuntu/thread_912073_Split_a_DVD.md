### Post by vanadium on 2008-09-10
Anyway, you see that there is no easy solution in Linux to split such a DVD. An easy solution is DVDShrink for Windows, which in fact runs very well under Wine as well. Linux solutions would involve to demux audio and video, split and reauthor. If your original dvd material has menus etc, there is no easy way to preserve these.
