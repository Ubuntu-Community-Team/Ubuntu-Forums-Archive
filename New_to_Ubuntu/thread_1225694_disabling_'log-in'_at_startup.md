### Post by philcamlin on 2009-07-28
[INDENT]System > Administration > Login Window
[/INDENT] You will have to provide your password for authentication and then you’ll be presented with the Login Window Preferences tool.  To disable Automatic Login you’ll want to navigate to the Security tab.
