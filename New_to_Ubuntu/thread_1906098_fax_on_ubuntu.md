### Post by jjex22 on 2012-01-08
Hi there! I looked into a similar situation a bit back, couldn't find any free services as it were - as someone has to pay the landline costs somewhere.

Seemed efax were the most trusted and not too costly - 30 day free trial then £7.50 ($11.57 USD) per month then 6p per page (10 cents). It also lets you choose how to set up your fax line - do you want a local or national number, and do you want to set up a toll free number so that you pay incomming fax costs (presumably for customers).

The system works by emailing the fax as an attachment to the service, so the app would be whichever email client you happen to prefer?

Hope it helps!
