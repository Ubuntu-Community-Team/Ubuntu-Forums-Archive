### Post by Rasa1111 on 2010-09-19
chances are , it is in the theme you have running. 
different themes always have different logos there.
I Used Ubuntu tweak to change mine,
not 100% sure at the moment how to do it without Tweak. 
[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=170015&d=1284939757[/IMG]
But if you changed your theme back to "ambiance" or another theme that came in your 10.04,
the logo should go back to the original, white, or black logo.

But whenever I get a new theme, with a lame logo, like the gnome foot or something,
i go into the file folder and replace it with a nice ubuntu one,
