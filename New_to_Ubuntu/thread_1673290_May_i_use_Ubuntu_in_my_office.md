### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-01-22
The only two restrictions to Ubuntu (and other GPL software) are:

If you make changes to the software and you spread them (you sell them or give them to others), you should
[LIST]
[*]give credits to the original authors
[*]make the changes public (release the modified code)
[/LIST]

As long as you don't make changes that you sell, you may do EVERYTHING with GPL software.
