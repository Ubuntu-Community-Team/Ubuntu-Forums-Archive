### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-06-11
Hi. Which applications are giving you trouble? Try going to 'System->Preferences->Sound' and on the 'Devices' tab change the first four dropdown menus to 'PulseAudio Sound Server', then restart Ubuntu; see if that helps.
