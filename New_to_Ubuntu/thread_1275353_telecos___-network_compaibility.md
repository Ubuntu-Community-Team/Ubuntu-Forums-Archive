### Post by egalvan on 2009-09-25
Here in Deepest South Texas, the main choices are

Time-Warner Cable (DOCSIS)
AT-T (DSL)

Linux was(is) totally un-supported, even for an initial install.

What I did to get ATT DSL

tossed together an old computer, installed XP,
and told Tech Support that my CD drive was dead,
could they help?

The Tech rattled off the needed DNS, email,  and other info,
I plugged into XP, verified it worked,
said "thank  you" :P

used the info to connect Linux, and things have worked since then.

and it's a technique I still use...
you can also use the temporary XP machine to do the initial install/connect,
and get the info off it.
