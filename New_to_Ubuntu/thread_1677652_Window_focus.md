### Post by jermza on 2011-01-29
Let's say I have two or three Nautilus windows open (Videos, Pictures, and whatever else).

They're obviously ordered in terms of visibility.  That is, one of them sits on top of the other, which sits behind, and so on.

Let's say I want to drag a file from one window (which is behind) to another (which is in front).

The moment I click on the file in the unfocused window, the window becomes focused.

Is there a way / trick to keep the unfocused window behind the focused window when clicking on the file to drag across to the focused window?

(Apologies for the complicated and confusing sentences...)

---

### Post by Nytram on 2011-01-29
Not sure if this is exactly what you want, but it should have a similar effect. In System->Preferences->Windows there's an option to have "Select window on mouse over."

---

### Post by treesurf on 2011-01-29
Another option, which doesn't quite answer your question, is to pin one window to always be on top.  Right click the window title bar for the "Always on top" option.
