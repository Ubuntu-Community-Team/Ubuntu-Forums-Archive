### Post by viperdvman on 2011-10-10
CCSM can be a little finicky, as some of the features use the same key bindings and/or edge bindings as other features. CCSM will usually tell you what "This feature conflicts with that feature" when you go to activate it.

But as far as keyboard bindings go for your window switcher, check all of your window management options in CCSM... one of them has the <alt> tab keybinding somewhere... or had it removed. Like the previous poster said, you can always revert to default. From there, you can look around and see what all is activated by default, and where your <alt> tab keybinding is.
