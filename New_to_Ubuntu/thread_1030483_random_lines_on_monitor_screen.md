### Post by wootah on 2009-01-04
I know this is not what you want to hear, nor perhaps fully useful, but purchasing a cheap dedicated graphics card will help a lot.

SiS graphics' drivers have come a long way, but only through the dedication of the Open Source community. The support for Intel, nVidia and ATI's cards are much better.

EDIT: The last computer I worked on with SiS graphics (embedded), I was unable to get 3D acceleration. A cheap AGP card (or even PCI-E) definitely helped here.
