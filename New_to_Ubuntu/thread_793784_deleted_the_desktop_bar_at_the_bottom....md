### Post by KingWilliam on 2008-05-14
first remove the awn bar:
```
sudo apt-get remove avant-window-navigator
```

Then right click the top panel--> new panel
Then right click on the new panel and choose "add to panel..."
Choose "Window List"

Done ;)
