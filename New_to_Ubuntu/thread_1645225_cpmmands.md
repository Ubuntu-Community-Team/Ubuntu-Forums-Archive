### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2010-12-14
They are all the same in every linux distro - apart from commands specific to the distro, ie. its package manager: apt for Debian/Ubuntu, yum for Opensuse, emerge for Gentoo, pacman for Arch etc.
[This]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/linuxcommand/files/TLCL/09.12/TLCL-09.12.pdf/download") book helped me a lot.
