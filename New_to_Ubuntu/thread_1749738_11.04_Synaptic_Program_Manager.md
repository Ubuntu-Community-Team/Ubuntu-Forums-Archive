### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-04
When you click Apply, it has two (or more) dropdown sections:

To be installed (/updated)
Unchanged

Unchanged is just packages that can be upgraded, but are not going to be in this particular operation because you haven't requested it.

xdx should appear in the Applications lense when it's installed. To add it to the launcher permanently, you need to open Xdx and then right-click its icon in the launcher, and click on "Keep in launcher".
