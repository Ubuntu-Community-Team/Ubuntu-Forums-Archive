### Post by mcduck on 2013-08-13
Don't worry, there are no such things as "malicious installs" through the Update Manager.
It will only download updates from Ubuntu's software repositories (or any third-party repositories you have added yourself), and only for packages you already have on your system.

What comes to bamfdaemon: [http://askubuntu.com/questions/260734/what-does-bamfdaemon-do]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/260734/what-does-bamfdaemon-do")
