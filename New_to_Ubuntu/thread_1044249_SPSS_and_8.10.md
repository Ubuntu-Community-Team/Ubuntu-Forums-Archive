### Post by stefangr1 on 2009-01-19
No, spss doesn't work in wine and there's no linux version. One of the best packages available for linux is stata (which is also more often used generally, so investing time in learning stata could be more productive than spss).

There are also open source alternatives like R and Gretl, but they're not always very userfriendly or complete. Besides, they tend not to be default packages on the work floor.
