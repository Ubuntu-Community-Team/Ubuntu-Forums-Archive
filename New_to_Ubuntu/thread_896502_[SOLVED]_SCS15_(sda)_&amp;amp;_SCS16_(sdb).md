### Post by pro003 on 2008-08-21
yes, sda indicates your first hdd and sdb indicates your second hdd

for example my first sata disk is 250gb 'sda' 
and my second sata disk is 160gb 'sdb'

installation of ubuntu is pretty easy, you can also see what partitions are under ntfs, etc...
