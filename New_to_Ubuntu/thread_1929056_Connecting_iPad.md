### Post by Ms. Daisy on 2012-02-21
itunes doesn't support Linux. You may find a workaround to mount the ipad here:
[http://ubuntu-install.blogspot.com/2011/04/connect-ipad-and-ipod-with-ubuntu.html](http://ubuntu-install.blogspot.com/2011/04/connect-ipad-and-ipod-with-ubuntu.html)

Here's a link for a bunch of possible solutions for itunes on Linux.
[http://www.ubuntuka.com/itunes-ubuntu-linux/](http://www.ubuntuka.com/itunes-ubuntu-linux/)

My personal solution (not the best solution, but it works for me) was to dual boot win 7 & Ubuntu.  I have itunes on the Win 7 side. I mount the windows hdd in Ubuntu and I imported the music into banshee.  Now I can listen to my music no matter which side I'm booted into.  I add new music in the Win 7 partition.
