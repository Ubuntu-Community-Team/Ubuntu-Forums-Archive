### Post by Dennis N on 2018-11-14
Insert blank CD into CD/DVD slot or tray. Open the burner program, and select "Data CD project" (NOT "Audio CD project"). You add your music files (mp3, ogg, wav) into the project window of the burner, and then click "Burn" button. 

The CD will play on a device which supports playing those file formats you burned - audacious or rhythmbox, for example.
