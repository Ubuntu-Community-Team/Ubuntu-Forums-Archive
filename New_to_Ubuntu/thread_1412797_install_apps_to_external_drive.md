### Post by mikeyphi on 2010-02-22
I suspect that the installation process would always put at least a small part of the program on the OS partition.
I suggest you consider having the '/' partition on your HD and the 'home' partition on the SD card... and buy a bigger SD card!!

That is how my netbook is configured; the basic OS is on the 4GB internal drive and the 'home' part is on a 16GB SD card
