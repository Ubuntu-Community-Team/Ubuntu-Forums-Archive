### Post by audiomick on 2011-05-27
Next helpful tip:

When you come across a command and are not sure what it does, you can always look at the man page in the terminal. Just type "man command" in the terminal. For instance

```
man shutdown
```

will explain the ins and outs of the "shutdown" command.
