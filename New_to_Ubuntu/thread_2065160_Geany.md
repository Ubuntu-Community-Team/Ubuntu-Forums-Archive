### Post by 2F4U on 2012-10-01
Geany is very usable without the plug-ins. The plug-ins basically enhance the usablity of Geany and serve a special purpose. If you are unsure whether you need/want any of them, you can install any plug-in at a later point in time. 

If you want to read more about those plug-ins, they have their own web presence:

[http://plugins.geany.org/](http://plugins.geany.org/)
