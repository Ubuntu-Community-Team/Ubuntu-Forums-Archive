### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-09-06
You'll need to be able to boot into something to see if your files are still there in Win7.  I'm presuming your netbook does NOT support booting from optical drive, right?  That being the case, you will need the following:
1) Access to another PC that has internet access
2) Unformatted or formattable USB stick
3) Google for Unetbooting -- this will tell you how to make a bootable Ubuntu setup on your USB stick
4) Boot the netbook from the USB stick (after loading Ubuntu to it)
5) Look in your Windows partition to confirm your files are still there.

IF your files are gone, you might as well do a full restore of Win7 -- but if you have trashed the Recovery partition installing Ubuntu, you will have to contact Samsung to see if they will send you a set of Restore disks.
