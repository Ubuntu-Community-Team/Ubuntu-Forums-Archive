### Post by drarem on 2008-05-27
Sometimes it just wears out. 

I was nervous at first when I had to replace my really loud cpu fan, but after a little research and visiting the #hardware irc, I bought a heatsink, the fan, the grease, and went to work with canned air and really cleaned it up in there. Now I have another 2-3 years of somewhat-quiet functionality :)  I can still hear the thing but it's not as loud as before.


I also cleaned up the fan on my video card and anything else that gathered dust. The dust was caked in my old heatsink, and I could tell when I compared the old/new fan that the old one was on it's last legs. The new grease also helped my video card temperature.
