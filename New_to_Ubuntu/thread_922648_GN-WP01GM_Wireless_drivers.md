### Post by Titan8990 on 2008-09-17
From my experience wireless devices will either a) use Windows drivers + ndiswrapper or b) use madwifi drivers.

Check the madwifi compatibility list for your card.

Other than that if you can't find any first hand testimonials you will just have to try your luck with ndiswrapper.
