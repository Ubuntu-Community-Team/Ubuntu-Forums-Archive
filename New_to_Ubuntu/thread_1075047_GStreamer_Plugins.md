### Post by frank002 on 2009-02-19
I have the following and have no trouble with dvd's:
  gstreamer0.10 ffmpeg
  gstreamer0.10 fluendomp3
  gstreamer0.10-plugins base
  gstreamer0.10-plugins good
  gstreamer0.10-plugins bad
  gstreamer0.10-plugins ugly

  libdvdread3
  libdvdnav4

  you can get all these in Synaptic. Hope this helps.
