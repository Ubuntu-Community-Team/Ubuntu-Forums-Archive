### Post by jnorthr on 2011-11-04
this may not be an issue with ubuntu. It is related to your firefox browser. Do you know where the start menu is ? This is a menu normally located at the bottom of your screen. If you place your mouse cursor over the start menu it will show you several choices of different things you can do. ONe of these choices is the 'Internet' choice, which you can click to see more choices of things that are internet related. You might see the name of other browsers listed there such as Google Chrome or Chromium. If you click one of those you will startup a different browser than firefox. You can then use the same address as you typed into your firefox address line and type that into a different browser. this might work/not work differenly from firefox. In this way you can determine if your issues are specific to firefox or more generic in that you webpage has an issue than baffles most browsers. If so, you will need to fix the web page or get a new/fresh copy. :o
