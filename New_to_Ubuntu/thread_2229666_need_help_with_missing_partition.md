### Post by TheFu on 2014-06-14
The best answer I have is to seek out your local LUG (Linux User Group), contact them, ask for help.  For a beginner, trying to talk someone through an install is VERY DIFFICULT, especially over a forum. My metro area has about 5 of these groups - one of them meets every Sunday and it especially good at helping new users get Linux installed on their laptops.  The face-to-face aspect is really important, plus having 3 other people to help and explain things makes it more likely that data loss can be avoided.
