### Post by Bucky Ball on 2009-04-12
I would say pin down exactly what you want from your OS and work it out from there. Do you love slickness, glitz and a disco finish? Go for Kubuntu (KDE). Do you want lightweight lightning fast? Go for Xubuntu (xfce). There is more lightweight again. Damn small and puppy linux for instance. Somewhere in between? Ubuntu (Gnome).

They are called desktop environments and you can mix and match. I have all three of my machines loaded with Xubuntu. Two of them then have Ubuntu desktop loaded with that.

What hardware do you have? Low specs, Xubuntu. KDE and Gnome can be more resource hungry.

Bottom line: They all run on the same kernel; the desktop environment is the face that goes on top. :)
