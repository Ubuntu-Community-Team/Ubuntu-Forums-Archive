### Post by Sorivenul on 2008-08-18
To answer your questions:
Yes, most of the material should still be applicable to Hardy, and due to magic of Linux (Ubuntu in this case), you're only a dist-upgrade away if you should so choose.
As far as whether Gutsy is better than Hardy, opinions differ.  Hardy comes with a newer kernel (the core of the system), and thus supports more hardware "out-of-the-box".  Some people still have various problems with hardware (ATI/Nvidia, getting PulseAudio to work), but that what these forums are for - helping people make things work they way they want.

Welcome to the community and good luck!
