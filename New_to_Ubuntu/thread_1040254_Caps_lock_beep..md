### Post by savanna on 2012-03-27
Here's a solution - I get the screen to flash when I press capslock, but you could also beep. Ubuntu 10.04 LTS:

System -> Preferences -> Assistive Technologies. Accessibility tab, Audio Feedback. Check "Beep when a toggle key is pressed" + "Show visual feedback for the alert sound", "Flash entire screen".

My motivation is that I'm learning Emacs, and I've swapped my Control and Capslock key. I want to be reminded when I press Capslock instead of Control, until I learn new finger habits...
