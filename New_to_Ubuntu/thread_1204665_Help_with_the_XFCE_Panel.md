### Post by Ji Ruo on 2009-07-05
I recently installed Xubuntu 9.04 on an old Acer Travelmate someone gave me. I have a couple of queries about the XFCE panel -

1. Can someone give me the instructions for how to put a terminal icon on the panel (I'm using the xfce terminal).

2. How would you get rid of a program launcher on the panel? Because it's a text input, right clicking it doesn't give you the remove option. I managed to get rid of it by deleting the panel and then adding it again, but I would like to know a way without having to delete all the other icons.

### Post by RobbMeex on 2009-07-05
1. Right Click, Add New Item, Drag Launcher to bar, Right click Properties, I put in under command "gnome-terminal" no quotes, or you can use xterm. Then change icon and give it a name.:popcorn:
