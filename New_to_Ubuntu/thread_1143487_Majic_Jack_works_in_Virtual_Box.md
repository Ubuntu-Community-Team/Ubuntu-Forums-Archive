Hi all,

I got Magic Jack to work in Virtual Box for those of you who are interested. I'll admit, the sound quality was not acceptable - but I'll post here and maybe it'll be a start for some of you.

What I did:

1. Unmount the Magic Jack from Ubuntu. In COMPUTER, right-click and unmount.

2. Bring up Virtual Box and go to SETTINGS. In the USB section, click on add a USB device and select Magic Jack.

3. Start your Windows OS and it sees it and loads it up.

That was it.

Good luck,

mdpalow
