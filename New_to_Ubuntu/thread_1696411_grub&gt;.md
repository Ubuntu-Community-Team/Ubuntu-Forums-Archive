### Post by sikander3786 on 2011-02-27
Welcome to the forums :-)

The Grub prompt means that Grub was not installed successfully and there is a problem with your boot setup. What we need to do you is to boot and Ubuntu Live CD/USB and post the output of bootinfoscript as per instructions here.

[http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net](http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net)

While posting, click the # icon in post menu and paste your text in between the generated [code] tags for readability purposes as this will be a long output.
