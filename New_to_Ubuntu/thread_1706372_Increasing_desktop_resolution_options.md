### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-03-18
If you don't have the options you'd like in System -> Preferences -> Monitors, and you know that your monitor and video card can handle larger resolutions, ensure that you have the drivers downloaded for your video card.

You can either get the drivers directly from ATI/Nvidia or by going to System -> Administration -> Hardware Drivers and installing any available from there.
