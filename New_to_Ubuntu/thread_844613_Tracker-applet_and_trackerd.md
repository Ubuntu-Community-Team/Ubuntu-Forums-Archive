### Post by neurostu on 2008-06-29
Tracker is a program that indexes all the files on your disk. Its kinda like a linux version of google desktop. If you go to System-->Prefs-->Search and Indexing you can change the settings associated with Tracker

Tracker-applet is the applet to control tracker
Trackerd is the daemon of the tracker application.
