### Post by kanikilu on 2009-02-13
I can't speak to Kubuntu, but I have [Ubuntu Unleased 2008]("http://search.barnesandnoble.com/Ubuntu-Unleashed/Andrew-Hudson/e/9780672329937"), and the [Pro Ubuntu Server Administration]("http://search.barnesandnoble.com/Pro-Ubuntu-Server-Administration/Sander-van-Vugt/e/9781430216223") books, both of which have been very useful, but at the end of the day just about everything you need to know can be found online for free. That doesn't mean you *shouldn't* get a book, though. You probably can't go wrong with the [Official Ubuntu Book]("http://search.barnesandnoble.com/The-Official-Ubuntu-Book/Benjamin-Mako-Hill/e/9780137151028") :)
