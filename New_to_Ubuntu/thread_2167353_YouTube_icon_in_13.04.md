### Post by ikt on 2013-08-13
You can lock the icon to the launcher so that any time you want to go to youtube you just click on it instead of opening firefox and then typing youtube.

Too remove search for 'unity-webapps-youtube' in the ubuntu software centre and remove.

Source: [http://askubuntu.com/questions/166655/how-do-i-remove-a-website-from-ubuntus-web-applications](http://askubuntu.com/questions/166655/how-do-i-remove-a-website-from-ubuntus-web-applications)
