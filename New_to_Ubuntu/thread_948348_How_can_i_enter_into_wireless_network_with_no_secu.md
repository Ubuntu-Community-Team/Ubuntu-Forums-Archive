### Post by Aero-Z on 2008-10-15

Are you using NetworkManager? I've had no problems connecting to unsecured netowrk with it. You have to choose None for Security option when connecting.
You could also try the Connect to Other Wireless Network.. option. Enter the ESSID of your wireless router and None for security. If it still asks for password then you might have one for your router.

Click on properties and make sure roaming mode is ticked,
