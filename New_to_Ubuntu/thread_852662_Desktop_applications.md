### Post by kk0sse54 on 2008-07-07
For desktop icons in order to change them just right click on them and you can either restore them to their origianl size or set them to something smaller. For icons in nautilus all you have to do is to right click in nautilus and either click on zoom in or zoom out to make them larger or smaller.

### Post by aktiwers on 2008-07-07
You could also open your Home folder and in the Window pick "edit" then "preferences".

Under the "View" tab there is a place called "Icon View Default". Under that you can change the % of "Default Zoom Level" and that will change the size of all Icons on your desktop and system.

Edit: You use nautilus to open folders and such, and Nautilus also controls the Desktop. Therefore thats where you want to change the icons.
