### Post by audiomick on 2010-01-05
As far as I can tell from the posts here, the most likely issue would be graphics, followed by wireless networking. If the screen is ok, and you get a connection on the live CD, they can be made to work.
Apart from that, try out anything your friend has that is important to him (mp3 player, camera, usb drive, firewire bull riding simulator;) ) and see if it goes.

The AMD processor is very unlikely to be an issue.
