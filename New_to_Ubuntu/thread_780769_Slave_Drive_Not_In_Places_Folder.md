Hi sithpigeon

[LIST=1]
[*]Open Thunar and go to media folder where you see the drive.
[*]Now simply drag it to the blank spot on the left below "Floppy Drive"
[*]Close Thunar and check "Places" - it will be there.
[/LIST]

**In Thunar:** You can do the same with any folder, reorder them (drag them up or down), right click on them gives other options as well.

Hope this helps.
Bruce
