### Post by drs305 on 2008-06-12
Before you start tinkering with your compiz settings, there is an option to save your settings: System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects Settings > Preferences > Export. If compiz isn't the problem, you won't have to remember every setting you tinkered with trying to restore it. ;-)
