### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-05-14
I use clive, it works great.  It's a command line program, though, so it doesn't show up in menus.

Basically you just open a terminal and type "clive url" where url is the full URL of the youtube page with the video you want, and it takes care of the rest.  Downloads, gives it a sensible name, and converts to mp4.
