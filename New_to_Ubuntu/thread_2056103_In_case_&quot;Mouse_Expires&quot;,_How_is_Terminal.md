### Post by PaulW2U on 2012-09-10
> **GregA said:**
> Recently, had mouse failure ...... software related.  What is the hot key combo in Kubuntu 12.04 to call the terminal window?

I don't think there is one but as almost everything in KDE is configurable why not create your own shortcut?

Go to "Shortcuts & Gestures" in System Settings then "Custom Shortcuts". Under "Examples" is "Run Konsole". Put a check mark next to both items and define your shortcut on the "Trigger" tab. Click on Apply, OK etc and you're done!
