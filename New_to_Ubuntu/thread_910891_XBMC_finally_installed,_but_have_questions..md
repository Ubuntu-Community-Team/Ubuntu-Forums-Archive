### Post by adam_kimber on 2008-09-05
Nvidia drivers are currently better than ATIs for Linux. However things are changing in the ATI world. 64MB graphics cards are not-so-hot these days. I expect you would have a sluggish interface in Vista with that card. 

An 8000 series nvidia card or better would easily do the trick. Unless you want to drive a massive 1680x1050 resolution (or higher) go for a slower card (like the 8500), I have a 8800GT and its great! Not sure it your budget can stretch that far.

The flickering is probably the card struggling to render the frames per second required to display both Google Earth and compiz. Your processors are nice and fast so you should have no problems if you got a better graphics card.
