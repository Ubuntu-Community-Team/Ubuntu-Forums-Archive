### Post by Tholley on 2010-11-04
Boot from your Ubuntu Live CD, pick try Ubuntu w/o installing, once it loads, then double click on the install Ubuntu icon on your desktop. Then you will have the choice to install Ubuntu over everything which will format the entire hard drive as well.
