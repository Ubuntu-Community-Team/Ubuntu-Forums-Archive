### Post by ohzopants on 2008-12-22
.avi files do not support metada but you may be able to find a video library management program (sort of like rhythmbox for video) that will save extra info for you.

I think amarok has video support and I know banshee does (I've never tried either for video though).

Additionally, if you do find a good video library management program you might be able to show the resolution of the videos in it (sort of like you can show the length or bitrate of an mp3 in rhythmbox).
