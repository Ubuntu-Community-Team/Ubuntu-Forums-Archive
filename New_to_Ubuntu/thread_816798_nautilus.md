### Post by pedro_orange on 2008-06-03
First of all. Google is your friend. Your questions can be easily answered with a google or two.

Nautilus is the GNOME File manager.

You can make CDs/DVDs with the built-in Brasero.

I use the following commands when making .iso:

```
dd if=/dev/dvd of=dvd.iso
```

Read up on the manual for more info.

"Distromaking" as you call it, I presume, is the act of building your own Distro of Linux. 
(Examples of distros include: Ubuntu, Fedora, SuSE, Slackware etc.etc.)
[http://www.linux.com/articles/41279](http://www.linux.com/articles/41279)
