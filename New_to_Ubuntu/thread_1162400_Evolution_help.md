### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2009-05-17
Inside gmail in settings -> Labs there is a feature called "Advanced IMAP Controls" enable it then go to Labels and uncheck "Show in IMAP" for the folders you wish to hide in evolution. You might need to restart evolution to see the changes.
