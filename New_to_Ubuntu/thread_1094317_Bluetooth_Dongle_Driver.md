### Post by Kevbert on 2009-03-12
Do you have Bluetooth under System-Preferences ?  It may just be that you need to activate the icon. Select the 'General' tab and under 'Notification area' select 'Only display when adapter present'. As soon as you plug in the USB adapter you should get a bluetooth icon in the top right-hand corner of your screen.
