The only way that could possibly occur is if the CD wasn't written to and you just cleared what it was "cached" to write to the CD.

The files should still be in their original location.

### Post by pricetech on 2010-05-10
It sounds like you had a multisession disk.  When you chose to import the previous session it should have brought the files from the previous session to the new one.  Apparently this process failed.

I'm not aware off hand of anything that will read files from an "old" session on a CDR disk.  Try searching in your preferred engine for something like "multi session reader" or "read old session" or something along those lines.

Good luck finding your lost stuff.
