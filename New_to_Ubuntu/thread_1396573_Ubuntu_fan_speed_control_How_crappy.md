### Post by mkvnmtr on 2010-02-02
As I understand it your operating system does not control your fans. Your machine bios is what controls them. The best I have found is some programs to view the temp. I have had no problems on desktop systems but on the some of the laptops I have installed on it was indeed a problem. Folks say relax and let your bios do it but that does not seem to always work. I have also had the problem running mac distros. I would sure like to see a solution to this but I guess it is a cross platform problem. They do not want to run the fans more than the minimum for battery life.
