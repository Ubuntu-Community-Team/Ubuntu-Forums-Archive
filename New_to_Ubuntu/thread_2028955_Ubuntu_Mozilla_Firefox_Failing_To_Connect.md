### Post by dcsoldschool53 on 2012-07-29
[SIZE=2]In Firefox click help > restart with addons disabled to go into safe mode. Then, click edit > preferences > advanced > network > settings, and check to see how you connect to Internet. If you connect to the Internet through a proxy, put your information in there. If you connect directly to the Internet without a proxy, click no proxy. Then, click OK.

If this does not work, try clicking help > troubleshooting information > reset firefox. Hope this help!
[/SIZE]
