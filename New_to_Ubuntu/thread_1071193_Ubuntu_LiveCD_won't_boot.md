### Post by jommoner on 2009-02-16
Just to add my experiences - when you burn a LiveCD, use the slowest possible setting to increase the chances of a good burn; for some reason, while multimedia or other backup CDs can be burnt quickly, LiveCDs are less tolerant to errors during the burn process!
