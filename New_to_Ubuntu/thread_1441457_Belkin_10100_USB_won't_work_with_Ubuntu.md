### Post by anewguy on 2010-03-28
With the device plugged in, do:

lsusb

and post the output back here.


If you don't know how to do the lsusb, do this:

go to applications, down to accessories, over to terminal and click.  This will bring up a terminal window.

At the prompt:

type:

lsusb <press enter>

copy and paste the output back here.
