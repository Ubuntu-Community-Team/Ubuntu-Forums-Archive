### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-02-02
Make sure your username doesn't have spaces or capital letters, and don't use technical words like "root", "user", admin, etc as your username. These words confuse the Ubuntu 11.04 installer. It's a known bug fixed in 11.10's installer.
