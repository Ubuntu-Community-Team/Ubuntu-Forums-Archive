### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-07-17
Hi. If you want to backup your important files, they necessarily must go somewhere; you say you have 80 GB of data to backup, but don't say how large your harddrive is. Since you don't want to burn discs or buy another harddrive, try making another partition to the HD, and copy your files there. you would then end up with a vista partition, a shared data partition, and a Linux partition.

Vista has a backup utility in the control panel, so you can use that, or you can copy the files manually.

All this assuming you have enough space in the HD to do all that.
