### Post by lmarmisa on 2011-04-11
You could use [Clonezilla]("http://clonezilla.org/") Live for cloning your internal hard drive into an external USB hard drive. This solution would be optimal if both hard drives have identical size.

Clonezilla is able to make a full backup of your system on the external hard drive too. Maybe that second alternative would be more appropriate for taking advantage of your external hard drive.
