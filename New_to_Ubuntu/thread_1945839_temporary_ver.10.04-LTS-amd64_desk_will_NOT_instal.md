### Post by helmcken on 2012-03-24
I solved this myself. I downloaded, md5-hash-checked, burned to disc and verified burn on the* non-64bit-version*! **When I ran the Ubuntu 10.04.4-desk-i386.iso version, it actually installed as it is supposed to! **Now, I do not know why the 64-bit version of Ubuntu will not install on a 64-bit architecture computer currently running Win 7-64bit. All I can say_ is if you are experiencing these same troubles, try this fix! _
