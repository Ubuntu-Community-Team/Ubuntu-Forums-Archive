### Post by TheFu on 2012-07-28
gscan2pdf - scans, cleans up images, can OCR, then save to different formats, including PDF with text and images combined.  Two OCR engines are available in the tool - GOCR and Tesseract.  Sometimes they work really well, then there are the rest of the times. ;)

Dependencies will be handled by the repo.
