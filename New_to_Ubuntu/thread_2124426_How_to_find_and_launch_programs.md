### Post by JEBB on 2013-03-10
After being away a while from Ubuntu I installed 12.10 on a laptop.  Now, how can I see what programs are on my machine and launch them?  I'm interested in finding the Ubuntu equivalent of the Start>All programs in Windows or the Applications window or Dock in Mac OSX.  Otherwise I don't know what tools are available and then how to launch them.  Thanks.

---

### Post by ManamiVixen on 2013-03-10
Click the start button, then click the icon at the bottom of the menu that looks like a crayon, ruller, ect..., then hover over installed and click it. That will show you all installed software.
