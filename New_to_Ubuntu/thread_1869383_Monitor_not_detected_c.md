### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-10-25
If the entire PC crashes when the graphics "crashes" -- it's more likely a heat-related issue, especially if the PC has been on for a while when it crashes.

That's unlikely to have anything to do with your video card -- since you're not running some super high-end, multi-card SLI rig (translation -- LOTS of heat introduced into the case due to the graphics cards).
