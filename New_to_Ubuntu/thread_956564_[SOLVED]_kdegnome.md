### Post by perlluver on 2008-10-23
Kde has one big bar at the bottom, it is called Kubuntu. Ubuntu, or Gnome, has two bars, if you have a System drop down menu, and you can click about Gnome, then you have Gnome.

### Post by neurostu on 2008-10-23
If you see a bar at the top, click System. If you see "About Gnome" then you are running gnome...

Also if you look at your applications you will see a lot of applications spelled with K instead of C in kde (like Konquerer)
