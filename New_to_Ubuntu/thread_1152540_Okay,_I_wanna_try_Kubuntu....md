### Post by Zoowey on 2009-05-07
Yes, the only thing that won't be the same is your personal customizations such as wallpapers, themes and such but they won't be erased, you just need to apply different themes and such for different DE's since their separate.

Fer example, you got a nascar wallpaper in Gnome, you won't have that in KDE until you apply it. However, if you log back into Gnome your wallpaper won't be changed or anything.
