### Post by Paddy Landau on 2009-01-11
How large is your .MOV file?

You cannot send an email larger than 20MB.

[http://mail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?hl=en-uk&answer=6584](http://mail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?hl=en-uk&answer=6584)
[http://mail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?hl=en-uk&answer=6589](http://mail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?hl=en-uk&answer=6589)

Remember that a file grows in size (temporarily) while being sent over the Internet, so a 15Mb video file may well exceed your 20Mb limit.
