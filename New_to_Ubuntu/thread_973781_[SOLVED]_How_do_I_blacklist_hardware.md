### Post by Rovdjur on 2008-11-07
Well, I have a laptop with a bluetooth module and I just don't use bluetooth, so I would like to blacklist it so it does not turn on when I start up my computer so I can help squeeze a few more minutes of life out of my battery :)

How do I do this? Please let me know, thanks!

---

### Post by iponeverything on 2008-11-07
Go to:
System->Prefences->Session->Startup-Programs

---

### Post by bscbrit on 2008-11-07
Try System->Administration->Services, and deselect Bluetooth.
