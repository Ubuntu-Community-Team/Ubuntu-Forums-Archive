### Post by Temposs on 2010-10-13
The Google Talk program does not have an installer for Ubuntu, so you cannot use it.

You can use **Google Voice and Video Chat** as arochester suggests.

I would suggest that you use **Empathy**, which is installed by default on Ubuntu, and which will bring you all the functionality of Google Talk and Google Voice and Video Chat.
