### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2011-08-03
Welcome to the forums.  Am I correct to say that you installed 11.04 on your old computer?  Is it the only OS on the computer?  If you do not have WIFI yet because of a driver, connect the computer to your router with an ethernet cable and connect to the Internet that way.  You should then be able to download your driver.
If 11.04 doesn't work out for you and since you said that this is an old computer, why not try Lubuntu 11.04.  It is made for older slower computers with less RAM.
