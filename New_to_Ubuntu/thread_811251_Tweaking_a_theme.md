### Post by Can+~ on 2008-05-29
gtk Theme files are just compressed files with icons and images inside. You can uncompress it on a folder on your desktop, and change the colors:

Aero/gtk-2.0/gtkrc
Aero/gtk/gtkrc

Pack it again, and apply.
