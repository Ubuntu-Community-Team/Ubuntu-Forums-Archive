Using 8.10, Rythmbox.  I've extracted (ripped) tracks from several CD's.  They're all in one big list when I open Rythmbox.

What do Linux users use for organizing music (e.g. ripped CD tracks)?  And if they have to be all in one file, is there a way to get Rythmbox to show the tracks from just one album at a time?

### Post by kanikilu on 2009-02-12
Click the Browse icon on the toolbar, or go to View and select Browse, or press Ctrl+B. This will give you a 3-pane view similar to the old Winamp with a list of artists on one side and a list of albums on the other.
