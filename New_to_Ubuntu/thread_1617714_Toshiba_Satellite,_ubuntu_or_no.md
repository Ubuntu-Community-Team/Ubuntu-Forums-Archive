### Post by 3Miro on 2010-11-09
Canonical recommends 32-bit, many people say that unless you have 4GB there is no point of going with 64-bit. On the other hand, unless you have some specific driver (like some printer) or you have low RAM (like 1GB), there is no reason not to use 64-bit.
