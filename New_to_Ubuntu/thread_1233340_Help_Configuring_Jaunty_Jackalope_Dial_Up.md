### Post by lkraemer on 2009-08-06
It isn't likely, but you can try.  Use Synaptic Package Manager
to install Gnome-PPP.  Then set it up for the modem and dialout
number.  But, chances are you won't get it working without some
of the other things setup.

It will depend on the version of Ubuntu you are running, and what 
kind of modem you have.  If you are trying a WinModem, I'd bet
that it is 100% you won't get it working, without going through
the guide step by step, and for a WinModem you are looking at 
3-4 days of emails, and reading, and testing.

Good Luck.
