### Post by tuskenraider on 2009-08-06
if you have xp pro then you can use remote desktop....  right click my computer go to properties. Then to remote tab then check the box in the allow users to connect remotly to this computer.  then go to select users then add then another box will pop up click advanced then find now... select your username from the list and confirm(click apply and yes till you get back) out to desktop.

i use gnome-rdp for my 4 remote desktop connections.... i love it... great little program that sits in your taskbar...  i suggest it..


also.. tightvnc is great.. tho i have a hard time getting any vnc to work for me... i think im allergic to it.. lol hope that helps...
tusken

p.s  tight vnc is your best bet....  xp home has no rdp ability.
