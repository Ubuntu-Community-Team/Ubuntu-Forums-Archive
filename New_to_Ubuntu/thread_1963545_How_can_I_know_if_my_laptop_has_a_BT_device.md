### Post by audiomick on 2012-04-22
> **cr15t1 said:**

Do you know the bluetooth symbol? 

This:
[IMG]http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/da/Bluetooth.svg/170px-Bluetooth.svg.png[/IMG]

Look for that symbol in  your task bar. It is there on my desktop, which does not have a bluetooth device. Click on that and see if you might have to turn the bluetooth device on, and the look at "preferences" and see if you might have to make the computer visible.
