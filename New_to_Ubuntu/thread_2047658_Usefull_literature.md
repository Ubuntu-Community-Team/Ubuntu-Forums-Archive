### Post by pme 72 on 2012-08-25
If you are looking to purchase a book take a look at Ubuntu Made Easy by Rickford Grant and Phil Bull. The authors walk you through setting up all the basics. Log on to Amazon.com, search for the title and look through the Table of Contents to get a feel for the topics covered.

The Official Ubuntu Book will take you through a guided tour of the world of Ubuntu. The 8.04 edition was not so much a how to book but a great source for getting a feel for the enormity and spirit of the project. 

[http://ubuntu-manual.org/](http://ubuntu-manual.org/) will take you to a site where you can download a free beginners guide. The 10.04 version was well worth the time and effort.
