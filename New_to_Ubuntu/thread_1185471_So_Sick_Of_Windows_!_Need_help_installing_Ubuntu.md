### Post by aysiu on 2009-06-12
I would highly advise against just erasing Windows.

I know you say you're sick of Windows and want to get rid of it. You shouldn't just jump straight into Ubuntu without even knowing if you like Ubuntu or if your hardware works with it.

The Ubuntu Desktop CD is both a live and installer CD. The live session allowsy you to try out Ubuntu without affecting your hard drive (it runs off the CD and your computer's RAM).

If you like that, [set up a dual-boot with Wubi](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/wubi). This will allow you to get used to Ubuntu for a while without having to use Windows *but still having Windows available as a safety net*.

If you find yourself *never* booting into Windows, then just pop the Desktop CD in again and just click the *Install* button on the desktop, and it'll erase everything on your drive.
