### Post by 2F4U on 2012-10-03
If you are looking at ATI cards, it may be best to go directly to the ATI/AMD site and see if the card is supported:

[http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/Pages/index.aspx](http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/Pages/index.aspx)

From the Archlinux Wiki ([https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/ATI_Catalyst](https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/ATI_Catalyst)):

> **Catalyst 12.6 stable news**: 
- **supports Radeon HD >= 5xxx**. 


So, the latest drivers from ATI dropped support for older graphics cards, and it is likely that this will also happen in the future.
