### Post by Churchwarden on 2011-06-07
I am setting up 11.04 and on my own log in I have the network icon at the top left of the screen.  On my wife's log in this icon is missing so I can't set her up with an internet connection.

### Post by el_koraco on 2011-06-07
Go to System Settings (on the shutdown icon, the last one), Users and Groups (or just hit the Super key and start typing), check your wife's account, and click on the "Advanced tab". Make sure the account has permission to "connect to wireless and ethernet networks". Also, on your account, make sure the box "Available to all users" is ticked in your network properties. Finally, on your wife's account make sure that "Network manager" is one of the startup applications - log in on her account, open System settings - Startup Applications (Super, start typing), and have a looksee.
