Nvidia are basically foolproof under linux. You either want a card with dvi, hdmi or s-video out. With dvi you you will need a dvi to hdmi adaptor but your tv will need hdmi inputs. With hdmi out you could obviously plug straight into your hi-def tv. But if you dont have a tv with hdmi input you could go for a card with s-video out and get an s-video to scart/coaxial converter (cost about £5).
