### Post by Kellemora on 2008-11-21
Hi Kramer

You can get any color of the rainbow you want and even make your own custom colors for the drop down color pallet too.

Just go to the Tools drop down at the top, select Options from the bottom of the list, click the + sign in front of OpenOffice.org if that doesn't open automatically, then select COLORS.

You can add to, change, adjust, almost anything you want to do.  And I think it's much easier than the WinDOZE method of hunt n peck on a color wheel.  AND you can NAME your color anything you want too!  Now how cool is that eh!
