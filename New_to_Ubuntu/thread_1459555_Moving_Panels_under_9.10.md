### Post by pj_kare on 2010-04-22
I can't test this myself because I don't have extra screen but maybe you can try?

Right-click the panel you wish to move and select &#8220;Properties&#8221;.

Uncheck the &#8220;Expand&#8221; option under the &#8220;General&#8221; tab.

Grab one of the edges of the panel by clicking on the left or right end (top or bottom end for vertical panels).

Drag the bar to the desired screen and position.

Check the &#8220;Expand&#8221; option in the &#8220;Panel Properties&#8221; window and click &#8220;Close&#8221;.
