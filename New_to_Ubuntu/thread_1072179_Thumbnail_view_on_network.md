### Post by longtom on 2009-02-17
Hi,

Since, with your big help, I got my Ubuntu running more and more the way I want it, I just realised, that I have no thumbnail view on Nautilus once I browse any shared folder on the network (windows workgroup).

Is there any way I can persuade Nautilus to let me see thumbnails over my network or is there any other way to view these jpgs in thumbnail view.

Any ideas will be appreciated

longtom

---

### Post by longtom on 2009-02-17
Figured it out - Nautilus > Edit > Preferences > Preview - change everything to "always".

Solved

longtom
