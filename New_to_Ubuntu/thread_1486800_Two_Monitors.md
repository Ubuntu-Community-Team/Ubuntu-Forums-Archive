### Post by grobar87 on 2010-05-18
Go to system->preferences->monitors...and check "same image in all monitors".

### Post by agm. on 2010-05-18
In case you wanted to use both monitors instead of just having it mirror the primary monitor, you can follow steps in this  [post](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1417900&highlight=expand+panel+monitor+dual).

It seems that the best thing to do is create a new panel and use the ALT+drag panel to the screen of choice.  That way it will be on both. I was struggling with a similar problem recently as well. 

Hope this helps!

For more references, search for "expand panel" and "dual monitor"
