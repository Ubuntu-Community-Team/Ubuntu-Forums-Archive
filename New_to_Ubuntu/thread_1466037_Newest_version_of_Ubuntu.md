### Post by Sealbhach on 2010-04-29
The newest version is fresh out of the oven today. You can get it here:

[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)

Make sure you find out a little more about what you're doing before you go ahead and install it. The good thing though, is you can burn it to CD, then boot up your computer from the CD (rather than your hard drive) so that you can have a test drive of Ubuntu without making any changes to your computer. It's called a Live CD session.
