### Post by coffeecat on 2011-11-15
Quick answer: no. Only the LTS releases (10.04, and 12.04 next year) have periodic updated ISOs.

Your problem may indeed be hardware related, or it may be a bad ISO download or bad burn. Did you do all the usual things such as check the md5 hash of the ISO and do a disc integrity check on the CD?
