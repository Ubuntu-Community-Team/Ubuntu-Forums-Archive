### Post by Patriot1776 on 2008-06-21
I was looking at a favorite website when I accidentally hit the right mouse button and the images on the site disappeared.  Investigating, I think I inadvertantly told Firefox 3 to block images from the site.  How do I undo it?

---

### Post by drs305 on 2008-06-21
You might be able to right click on the page image and see if the Block Images tick box is checked.
 
Or:
Try Edit, Preferences, Content. See if Load Images Automatically is checked. Next open the Exceptions and see if the site has been listed to be blocked and edit the entry as desired. There is also an 'allow' option.
