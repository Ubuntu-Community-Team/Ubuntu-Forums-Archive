### Post by pnutzh4x0r on 2009-04-22
Try looking under the Viewport Switcher Settings, and then The Desktop-based Viewport Switching.  You can change Move Next and Move Prev to whatever mouse buttons you wish.

You can also try looking at the settings under Desktop Wall and then Bindings.

Hope that helps.  Good luck.
