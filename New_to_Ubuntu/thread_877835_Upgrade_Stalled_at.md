### Post by bumanie on 2008-08-02
There can be problems with upgrades - there is a warning from canonical stating that upgrading may 'break the system.' If the upgrade won't continue, you will have to do a fresh reinstall; if you stop it part way, you will have to do a fresh reinstall. Don't think there is much option other than to do a fresh reinstall.
