### Post by Rooker on 2008-09-24
This is Firefox blocking the page from doing an automatic refresh. This is usually done - usually by news sites - to artificially increase pageviews and can be very annoying. It also happens on message boards after you log in or reply to a post to redirect you back to the topic you were reading.

It is an optional setting with a checkbox in Firefox options > Advanced > General
