### Post by diablo75 on 2009-12-27
To change your background:  Right-Click on it and then click "Change Background".  You'll have to click the Add button to add custom photos to your wallpaper inventory.  You can also reach this Appearence Settings control panel via System>Preferences>Appearence.

To delete a file, you can right-click on it and select "Move to trash".  You can also just press the Delete key on your keyboard when the file(s) you want to delete are selected, as long as you have permission to delete the file.
