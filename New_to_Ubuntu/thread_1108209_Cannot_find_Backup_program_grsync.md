### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2009-03-27
Hmm, well you could add the application manually to the menus's, some apps dont have menu entries.
right click your menu and select "edit menu's"
This will open up the menu editor, double check your categories and see if its there.
If not its simple to create a entry for it.
Go to the "other" category and on the side select "create launcher"
This shouold bring up a new window where you can edit stuff for your launcher.
hit the "browse" button and go to usr/bin and search for your app in there.
I am not guaranteeing it will be there though so cross your fingers.
after finding the app, in the name box just name your app :D
