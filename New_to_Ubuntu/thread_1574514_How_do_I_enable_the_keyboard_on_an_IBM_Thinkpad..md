### Post by asmoore82 on 2010-09-14
I've seen this happen twice where system updates are interrupted and the laptop keyboard/touchpad stops working.
Once was on a Thinkpad and the next time was a Dell(a real Ubuntu model!).

You'll have to use a spare usb keyboard/mouse to fix it - maybe as simple as finishing the updates.
I was in a hurry and wanted to keep a pristine system and already had the data backed up,
so I just did a fresh re-install - being careful not to be tweaking while updating the next time.
