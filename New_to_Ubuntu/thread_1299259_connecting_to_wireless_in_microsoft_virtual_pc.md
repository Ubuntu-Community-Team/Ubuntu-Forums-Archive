### Post by earthpigg on 2009-10-23
hi,

i very much suspect that you will have problems finding someone on these forums with the knowledge required to help you get Microsoft Virtual PC working.

most of us around here use [VirtualBox]("http://www.virtualbox.org/"), so that is what we would be best qualified to help you with.

I may be wrong, there may be a Virtual PC guru around here that pops in. Time will tell.

What i can tell you about VirtualBox, though, is that whenever my host operating system is connected to the internet by any method, the guest machines always pick up that connection immediately without any further hassle on my part. They pick it up as a wired broadband connection requiring no configuration, even if the host OS is connected via encrypted wifi. 

One thing to consider: Do you think Microsoft ***wants*** it to be easy to use linux in a VM without you having purchased Microsoft Windows Vista Super Ultra Ultimate Professional Edition for $900?

I suggest giving VirtualBox a shot. :D
