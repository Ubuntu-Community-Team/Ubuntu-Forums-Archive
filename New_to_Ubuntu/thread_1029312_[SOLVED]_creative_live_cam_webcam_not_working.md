### Post by freddybob on 2009-01-10
I have a camera that identifies itself as "Creative Technology, Ltd Live! Cam Vista IM" (when I type lsusb in a terminal window).

If you need drivers try running EasyCam 2 ([https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EasyCam](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EasyCam)).

It is working for me in Ubuntu 8.10 using Cheese and aMSN.  The only problem I've found is that the Adobe Flash 10 plug-in does not work correctly with the webcam.  Pictures are streamed but they are at the wrong resolution and are broken up and stripy.
