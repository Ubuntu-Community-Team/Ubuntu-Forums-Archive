I do not know if Synaptic comes built in but check in the dash.  That is the top icon that shows up when you bring the mouse curser all the way to the left side of the screen. The dash is the top icon.  Click on it and in the search bar start typing synaptic. By the time I typed sy it showed up down below.  If your looking for other programs do the same start typing its name it shows up down below.  If you are looking for the folder and can not find it. Make sure you have hidden folders turned off.  If it does not come built in then move the mouse to left to bring out the side icons like before and hit the icon that looks like a suitcase.  Then click where it says provided by Ubuntu and in the search type synaptic and download it.
