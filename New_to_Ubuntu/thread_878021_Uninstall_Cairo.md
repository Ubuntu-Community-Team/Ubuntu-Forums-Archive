### Post by Hamchan on 2008-08-02
System->administration->synaptic package manager

Just click search button and search for Cairo. Then select the green boxes next to Cairo-Dock (and Cairo-Dock-Plug-ins if you have them), in the menu choose "Mark for complete removal"

That should get rid of Cairo dock for you.
