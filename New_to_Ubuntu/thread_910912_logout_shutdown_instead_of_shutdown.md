### Post by tmshih on 2008-09-27
Okay, it turned out to be an easy fix for me. I went to "Applications", "Settings" and clicked "Settings Manager" to bring up the Xfce settings manager. I then clicked "Sessions and Startup" and made sure that the "Prompt on logout" button was filled in under "Logout Settings". That brought back the logout prompt and bypassed the login screen that was popping up before.
