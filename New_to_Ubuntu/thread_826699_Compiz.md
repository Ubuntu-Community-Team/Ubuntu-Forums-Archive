### Post by kesman on 2008-06-12
What version of ubuntu are you running? In 8.04 compiz is already installed, and for cube you need ccsm(compiz config settings manager). Then go to system -> preferences -> appearance -> visual effects -> custom and enable "desktop cube" and "rotate cube"
