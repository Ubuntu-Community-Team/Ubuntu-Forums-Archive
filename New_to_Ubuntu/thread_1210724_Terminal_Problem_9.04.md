### Post by earthpigg on 2009-07-11
aha!

i just played around and now see what you are doing.

when you are hitting the right mouse button, your hand is moving the mouse down every so slightly... just enough to highlight 'open terminal'. then, when you release the mouse button, it is doing just that.

try this:

1) click quicker, or ensure you have a steady enough trigger finger that only your finger is moving and not your whole hand. :P (yes, that is actually what they mean in movies when they talk about 'steady trigger fingers'... moving just your finger, while keeping the rest of your hand motionless... if your whole hand moves, the gun will move, and you wont hit where you are aiming. harder than one may think!)

2) if that is proving difficult for you, then click **_and hold_** the mouse button without releasing it... move down to 'paste' and _**then**_ release it. instead of accidentally choosing 'open terminal', it will chose 'paste', which is what you want.
