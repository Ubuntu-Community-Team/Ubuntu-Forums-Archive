### Post by tahina on 2008-08-16
There's a menu that you can add to the panel that is a bit more like the start-menu, in that it has "Applications", "Places" and "System" under one menu instead of three menus...

Right-click on a panel and choose "Add to panel..." then find "Main menu" from the list and double-click that.

You can remove the menu you have now (with the three menus) by right-clilcking it and choosing to remove it from panel.
