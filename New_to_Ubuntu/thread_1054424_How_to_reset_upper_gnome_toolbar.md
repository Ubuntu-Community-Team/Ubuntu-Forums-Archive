### Post by greyfox1 on 2009-01-29
Hi Scitrek,

Welcome to the forums!  To add the object you accidentally removed, just right-click the panel and choose "Add to Panel".  From the dialog that comes up, choose "Notification Area".  

If you need to reposition the Notification Area once it's added, just right click it (take care to click the Notification Area and not one of the icons in it) and choose "move".  Then, just drag it where it needs to go!
