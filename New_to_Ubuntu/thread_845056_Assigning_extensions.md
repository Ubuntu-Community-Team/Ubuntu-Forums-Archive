### Post by Canis familiaris on 2008-06-30
Do you have 9000 different extensions?
Right clicking the file and selecting its extension will apply to all the files of that extension.
So if you have 9000 .ogg music files, you could set them to say Amarok by right clicking one music file, clicking on Properties->Open with. And it will be applicable to all the ogg music files.
Hope this helps.
