### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-09-20
The program you should use depends on what you want to do with your webcam.

If you just want to take pictures of yourself, then use Cheese.

If you want to make video calls, then use Skype.

If you want to video chat to people on IM networks, then try [www.meebo.com](www.meebo.com)
