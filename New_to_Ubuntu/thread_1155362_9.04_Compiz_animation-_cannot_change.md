### Post by Redache on 2009-05-10
Are you sure that you have the correct Graphics Driver installed? this could be causing problems with Compiz, or the level of Graphical Power allocated to Compiz is set lower.

Try using the Restricted Drivers Manager (System->Administrator->Hardware Drivers) and check that the Drivers installed are correct and up to date.
