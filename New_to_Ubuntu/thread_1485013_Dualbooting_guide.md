### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-05-16
Unfortunately psychocats pages on dual booting now only talk about wubi or virtualbox, neither of which are a standard dual boot.  If you want to do things "properly" have a look at another of his pages [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installseparatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installseparatehome) which although it is about a separate /home partition, also gives a lot of useful info about setting up ubuntu in general terms.

If you have time to study this page in full, I do recommend that you try a separate /home, as it will make life agreat deal easier in future.  It may look frightening at first glance, but it really isn't.
