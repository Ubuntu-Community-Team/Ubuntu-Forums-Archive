### Post by kpkeerthi on 2008-04-25
The upgrades should go real smooth if you do not have 3rd party repositories (other tham main, universe, multiverse, backports, medibuntu, parner) added to it. Otherwise, you may want to remove those and packages you installed from them first before you do an upgrade.

I upgraded from dapper -> feisty -> edgy without any problems (no gusty as I was using arch).

And yes, all your customizations should remain as they are.
