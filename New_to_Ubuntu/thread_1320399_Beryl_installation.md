### Post by ukripper on 2009-11-10
To play around with advance effects. Go to Applications-->Ubuntu Software Center and there in search box put Advanced Desktop Effects and then just install it by clicking on the arrow. Once installed then go to System-->preferences-->CompizConfig Settings manager and play around with different settings there. You will get all your youtube effects there..

Have fun!
