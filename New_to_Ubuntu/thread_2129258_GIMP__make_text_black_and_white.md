### Post by Impavidus on 2013-03-26
Keep in mind that gimp will convert your text pdf (probably vector graphics) to a raster image. If it's just for printing that doesn't really matter, as long as you make sure the resolution is high enough – that is, equal to the resolution of the printer.

Else you may try inkscape to modify your pdf. Change text colour to black, remove background. I don't have any real experience with inkscape, but I heard it's pretty versatile.
