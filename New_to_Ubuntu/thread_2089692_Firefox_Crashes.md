### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2012-11-30
Make sure your Firefox is [the latest version]("http://www.mozilla.org/firefox"), your [plugins are updated]("https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/plugincheck/") and your add-ons are running correctly. 

You might want to narrow down the cause of the crashes by disabling add-ons one by one. Or starting it on [Safe Mode]("https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/troubleshoot-firefox-issues-using-safe-mode"). 

Or you can also try, this very good feature [Reset Firefox]("https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/reset-firefox-easily-fix-most-problems").
