### Post by BeaverDono on 2011-07-11
All depends on your preferences really, each [repository]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu") has its own slew of stuff. I know a few people who host their own repositories that love to share some of their music editing software that they use. 

And some programs (Like [Ubuntu Tweak]("http://ubuntu-tweak.com/")) will install their own repositories and will let you download even more software. 

If anything, I'd keep my repository lists small so it won't have to load so much stuff when searching through everything..
