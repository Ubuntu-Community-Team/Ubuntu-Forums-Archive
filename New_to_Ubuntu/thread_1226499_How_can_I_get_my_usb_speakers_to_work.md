### Post by cosmicappuccino on 2009-07-29
This may not be the same issue as mine, but just in case it helps: When my earphones didn't work with Ubuntu, I found that it was because I had turned on the computer with them plugged in. The solution was to keep the earphones out until logged into Ubuntu, then to plug them in, and they worked fine. 

Good luck (:
