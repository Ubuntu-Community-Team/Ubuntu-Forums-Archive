### Post by presence1960 on 2009-07-29
I had problems with firefox fonts, no matter what settings I put in for fonts only FF would look ugly. I solved it by going in FF Edit > Preferences > Content Tab. Click advanced under fonts & colors (where the pointer is in screenshot) When the fonts window opens make sure the box "allow pages to choose their own fonts instead of my selections above" is left unchecked. You may want to try installing the MS fonts package  and liberation fonts package from Synaptic also.
