### Post by bumanie on 2008-05-06
Go into vista's disk manager and extend the partition over ubuntu. Then you will have to go into vista's recovery console via your rescue cd and fix vista's mbr. My experience with vista is limited, but I did it once on someone else's computer and the procedure worked fine.
