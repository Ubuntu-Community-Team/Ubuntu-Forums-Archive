### Post by wrgb2 on 2009-11-23
Use Kpackage Kit (Applications > System > Software Management) to add and remove software.  Just type in the search bar what you are looking for.  The KDE games package is called kdegames -- search for that, mark it for insallation by clicking on the down arrow on the right, and press the apply button.  Not sure why your panel icons didn't stay where they were put, they should.
