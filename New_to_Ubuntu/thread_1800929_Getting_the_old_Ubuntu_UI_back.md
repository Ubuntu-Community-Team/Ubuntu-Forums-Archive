### Post by coffeecat on 2011-07-09
At log in screen: Enter your account name. Now choose "Classic" from the bottom of the screen. Now enter you password. You will be taken to the classic gnome desktop.

Classic gnome similar to Windows in design? :o Not on my machine. :-k I think it's reminiscent more of the old (pre OSX) Mac desktop.

Anyway... before you write off the new Unity desktop, have a look at the first link in my sig and try it out from time to time. Some did not like Unity at first (myself included) but have come to prefer it.
