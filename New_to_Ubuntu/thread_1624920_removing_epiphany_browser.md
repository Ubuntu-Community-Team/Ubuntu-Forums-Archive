### Post by beew on 2011-07-21
> **darthpenguin said:**
> I know this is an old thread. But I've got a similar problem. I removed Epiphany but it still shows in the Unity menu under Applications (not unity launcher). How can I hide it from Unity?

Go to the power button on the upper right corner of the the top panel, righ click and choose System settings and then choose main menu. Open it and on  the left panel choose "internet", on the right you should find the entry for Epiphany. You can either uncheck it or delete it and it should be gone. You may need to logout or reboot though (In earlier ubuntu you don't need to but with Unity you may)
