### Post by chrisod on 2008-10-10
You can stream any FM station that offers streaming from their website. Any of the usual audio applications for Ubuntu should be able to handle it with the proper codecs installed.

I think you'd need a hardware FM Tuner to actually use the PC as an FM receiver though. I can't think of anyway to replicate an FM antenna with just software.

### Post by H.Callahan on 2008-10-10
Actually, a cheaper alternative (that you may already have lying around) is just to get a cheap AM/FM radio and plug the headset jack into your sound card.  Tune to whatever station you are interested in and you can listen or record, if you want.
