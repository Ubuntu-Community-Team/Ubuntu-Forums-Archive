### Post by dearingj on 2010-05-05
I'd suggest you try to get comfortable using the terminal. It's actually very handy once you get over your initial fear of it, and often faster and more powerful than the graphical interface. Specifically, I think you should learn about the 'man' (short for manual), 'cd' (change directory), and 'apt-get' commands.

As for contributing to Ubuntu, I'd recommend you:
[LIST]
[*]subscribe to the [Ubuntu Weekly Newsletter]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-news"), as it often often includes links to things you can help with
[*]help others on these forums :)
[*]vote on [Brainstorm]("http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/") ideas, and contribute some yourself
[/LIST]
