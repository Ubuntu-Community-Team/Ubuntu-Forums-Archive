### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-04-12
Yep it'll work fine. The powerline adapters just pretend to be a long Ethernet cable, so it'll work exactly like connecting a long Ethernet cable between routers/switches. Except a little slower - I'm sure you're already aware, but the throughput of those adapters is not quite as good as real Ethernet, and if you overload the capacity of the adapters you'll get total particle reversal. :-P
