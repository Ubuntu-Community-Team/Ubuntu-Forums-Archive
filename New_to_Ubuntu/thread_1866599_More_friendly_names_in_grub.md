### Post by Big Lizard on 2011-10-21
Another good GUI tool is Boot-Repair. You can get more details here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair#Using%20Boot-Repair](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair#Using%20Boot-Repair)
