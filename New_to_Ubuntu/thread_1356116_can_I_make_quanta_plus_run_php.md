### Post by northern lights on 2009-12-15
Your familiar with being able to preview html and want to do the same with your php files. The issue isn't with quanta. Your browser and quanta can interpret html they can't handle php on their own.

php is server-side-scripting and hence needs a phpserver to be viewed. Install lamp on your local machine and you don't have to upload php to your hoster everytime you want to preview.

Read [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ApacheMySQLPHP]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ApacheMySQLPHP")
