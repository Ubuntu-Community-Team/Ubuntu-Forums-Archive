### Post by DuckHook on 2019-03-06
It is notoriously difficult to install iTunes on Ubuntu or Linux natively, and I have not heard any stories with good results:

[LIST=1]
[*]iTunes cannot be installed natively, as there is no native Linux app or support from Apple.
[*]Some have tried installing under WINE, but with generally dismal results.
[*]The only reliable way appears to be installing into a VM running Windows. This is the method I would recommend.
[/LIST]
Full disclosure: I do not use iTunes, so my knowledge is second hand. You may wish to wait for further responses from others.
