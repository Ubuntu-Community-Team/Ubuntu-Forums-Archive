### Post by harrisonk on 2010-06-27
Quick answer is Yes, You will lose the *option* to boot Ubuntu not the files, second you will need to reinstall grub, and third you can do it from a live USB stick. 

For info on reinstalling grub go to [wiki.ubuntu.com/Grub2]("wiki.ubuntu.com/Grub2") It is near the bottom and anywhere it says "LiveCD" replace that in your mind with Live USB.

Harrison
