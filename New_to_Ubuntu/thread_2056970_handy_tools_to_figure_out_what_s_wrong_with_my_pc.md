### Post by deadflowr on 2012-09-12
How quickly does it crash?
I asked, because, in my own experience, systems that boot normal but seem to crash relatively quickly, soon after I've logged in and am up and running, tend to crash because of loose heatsinks, and other heating related factors.
Other reasons for a crash, can be, but not limited to, bad ram, failing hard drive, or simply burned out components.
What kind of system is it? Laptop? Tower desktop?
