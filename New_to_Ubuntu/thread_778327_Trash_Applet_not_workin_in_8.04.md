### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-05-02
I encountered this too, dont worry you can still get a trashcan on your desktop.
right click on your menu and select "edit menu's"
after that go down to system tools and click on the box next to "configuration editor"
this will make the configuration editor easily available to you
after that open up the configuration editor.
Then after that click on the little triangle next to "apps"
and scroll down to "nautilus"
then click on the triangle next to that and click on "desktop"
this is where you can add a shortcut to your trashcan and other junk to your desktop.
