### Post by deyanyosifov on 2010-10-17
You could try RSS-aware: [http://freelanzer.com/rss-aware/index.html](http://freelanzer.com/rss-aware/index.html) It displays the titles of the news, but lacks much of the functionality of Liferea. That's why I use the two programs simultaneously: RSS-aware to display the OSD notifications and Liferea to read the feeds.
