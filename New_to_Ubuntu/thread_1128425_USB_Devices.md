### Post by LunaticHiatus on 2009-04-17
if a device is drag and drop supported. It will usually have an icon appear on your desktop as soon as you plug it in. you will also probably see it on the left in your file manager. If you don't see it in either places, it might not support drag and drop (my friends ipod had this problem) in which case, you might want to look in add/remove programs for something that will put your music/movies etc. on it (that was the sollution for my friends ipod)
