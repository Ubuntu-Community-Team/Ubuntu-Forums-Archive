### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-04-27
There's also newer software across the board, including bugfixes that will never make it into 9.10 (usually because the change is too significant). New features will never go into a distribution after "Feature Freeze" (which is before the release date) due to software stability.

If you're happy with everything the way it is, then stick with Karmic. You may be able to find PPAs containing the Me Menu and Gwibber for Karmic.
