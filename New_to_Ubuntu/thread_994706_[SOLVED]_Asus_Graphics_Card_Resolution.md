### Post by Dedoimedo on 2008-11-27
Hi,

You need to install drivers for it. If you go under System > Hardware drivers, does the driver manager recognize your card?

In other words, you should have your card listed, but the driver not installed. Select to use and install, reboot and you should be fine.

Ask again, if you need more help, clues.
