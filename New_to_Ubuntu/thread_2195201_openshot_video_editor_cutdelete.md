### Post by varunendra on 2013-12-23
You can either use "Razor" tool to cut the part and delete it, or use "Resize" tool (between "Snapping" and "Razor" tools) to resize the loaded clip to resize it (from the end) upto the starting point of the part to be removed, then append the same clip again, this time resized (from the beginning) upto the end of the part to be removed.
