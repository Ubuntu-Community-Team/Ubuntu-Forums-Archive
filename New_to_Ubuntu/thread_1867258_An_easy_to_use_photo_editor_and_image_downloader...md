### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-10-22
Why not get a cheap card reader;  they are usually much faster than importing by connecting the camera, and it is now the way I always import photos into my own folder and sub-folder hierarchy.

I don't like the idea of just using tags on photos, which are all in the same photo or pictures folder, to manage my collection, having been caught out doing that in the past when tags from one application were not read and used by another application.  That caused me a huge amount of work.

As for photo editing, it depends what work needs doing.  Gimp, which works very well for me is extremely powerful, and probably over-the-top for a simple contrast or brightness change, so for simple edits I tend to use gthumb, which incidentally is getting better and better as time goes by.
