### Post by e4uforums on 2010-04-16
I use PlayOnLinux too and I manage about 10 different versions of WINE.

Click on the menus at the top of the screen, one of them will have an entry "Manage WINE Versions", click on this.

The next screen will have two tabs - one will allow you to download and install other WINE versions, and the other tab will let you assign WINE versions to programs you have installed.

Give it a shot, I've used this to resolve a few different WINE version problems.
