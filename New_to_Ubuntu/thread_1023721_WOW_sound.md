### Post by weezerisrock on 2008-12-29
Short answer I have found?  honestly, don't minimize.  If you run WoW in windowed mode and check the maximized button it gives the appearance of being in full screen and allowing you to ctrl+alt+left or right arrow to get to another desktop.  If you want a better answer than that, there is some useful information on [wow wiki's wine page]("http://www.wowwiki.com/Wine_(software)").
