Depends on what settings you mean. The fallback session of Gnome 3 is not the same as the old Gnome 2 desktop you had in previous Ubuntu versions, it only looks and behaves quite similar.

Your program settings will of course stick, but most of the desktop settings won't and neither will the old GTK2 themes you might have used in 11.04.
