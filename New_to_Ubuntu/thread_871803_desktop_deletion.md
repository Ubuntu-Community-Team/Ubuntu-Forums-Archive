### Post by northern lights on 2008-07-27
You have a LiveCD to install from right?

Boot from the CD and before choosing to reinstall Ubuntu, back up /home. Apart from your personal data it carries a good amount of your settings for the software you use in hidden directories (press Ctrl+H to see).

At best you'd move it to another drive or partition. If you have a burner you can burn it to a CD/DVD also. USB flashrive would do the job, too.
