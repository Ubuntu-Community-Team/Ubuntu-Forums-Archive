### Post by ibuclaw on 2009-04-15
Scroll down below this post I have made, you'll see the words [COLOR="Red"]Quick Reply[/COLOR]. Now, look at the bar immediately above it named **Tags** and follow it to the right hand side of the firefox browser. You should now see a hotlink named **Edit Tags**. Click it, and add the tag "**[SOLVED]**" or just a plain "**solved**" and it will show up attached to this thread in the main forum area. :)
