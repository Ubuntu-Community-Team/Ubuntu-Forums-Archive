### Post by koffeinöverdos on 2008-08-02
I'm pretty new to Ubuntu as well, and I had that same problem the first couple of days I had it. Turns out "Show Actions Menu" under 'Menu Bar' in the login theme selector was unchecked. Checked it and that fixed it. That could be your problem too, I don't know.
