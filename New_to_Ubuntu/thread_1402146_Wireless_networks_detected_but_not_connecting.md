### Post by mwcrowley on 2010-02-10
Some laptops have a button, usually somewhere above the keyboard that enables the built in wireless card.
There are different types of wireless security, wep, wpa, wpa2.  Ensure that you have selected the right one for your network, otherwise the passcode will not work.
