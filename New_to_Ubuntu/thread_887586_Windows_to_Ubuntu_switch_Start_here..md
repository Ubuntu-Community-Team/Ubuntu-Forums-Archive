So, you've got an ubuntu operating system...and you were really excited about it, because you've heard about how great all the open source was...but now you can't get anything to work! Well here's some help. It's a sort of a Q/A guide.

Q. How to install things? 
A. [http://monkeyblog.org/ubuntu/installing/](http://monkeyblog.org/ubuntu/installing/)

Q. What are the open source equivalents of windows programs?
A. [http://www.linuxrsp.ru/win-lin-soft/table-eng.html](http://www.linuxrsp.ru/win-lin-soft/table-eng.html)

Q. How to get my windows audio/video files to work?
A. [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats) 

Q. How do I compile from source? (tarballs)
A. [http://www.linuxjournal.com/article/216](http://www.linuxjournal.com/article/216)

Q. how do I find out more about ubuntu? how do I get answers to my questions?
A. Use Google!! Almost 100% of my problems are solved just using google. Most of the time I end up on this forum. So there it is- google, and linux/ubuntu forums.
