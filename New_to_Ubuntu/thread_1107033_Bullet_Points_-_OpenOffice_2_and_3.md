Generally, when taking notes on a subject I will set out my document as follows:

```

[COLOR="Red"]

[/COLOR]

```

Simple enough right? Well not in OO 3.0, each time I hit enter to start a new line (with a new "dash" bullet point), its formatted in slightly different ways:

