### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2009-03-10
Your post got me thinking as I play guild wars in window mode, and alt is used to show the names of NPC's while you look around the game. I discovered that setting is a compiz setting. I was able to change it by installing the Advanced Desktop Effects Settings (ccsm), then looking under the configuration for Move Window. The Default Setting is 

<alt>Button 1 

I just changed it to 

<ctrl><alt>Button 1 

and now I'm able to look around guild wars while holding my alt button down without a problem.

Hope this helps ;)
