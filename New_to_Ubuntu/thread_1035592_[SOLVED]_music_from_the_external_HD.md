### Post by djbushido on 2009-01-09
You might be able to convert the files from wma. use pacpl, available [here]("http://pacpl.sourceforge.net/"). 
As to the Zune: you're screwed. Microsoft did a good job making sure only the zune software can touch it. Hard-disk is encrypted, hidden, and loads of other stuff, meaning it can't really be touched. Sorry.
So, in summary, you can convert the files, i recommend pacpl but there are numerous audio converters, but the zune will never work on linux. sorry.
