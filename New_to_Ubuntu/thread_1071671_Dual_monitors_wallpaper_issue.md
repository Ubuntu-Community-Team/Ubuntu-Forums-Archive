### Post by Bölvaður on 2009-02-16
Wallpaper:

I recommend merging 2 wallpapers, as the settings you have is in my opinion the best. Even though it makes your desktop supersized it had an advantage. It is reliable, stable and works exactly like if you would have a single display.
But I guess you can check out KDE4 for having different background on each display. KDE4 has it out of the box.


Panel:

Just drag it over. Or right click it and create a new panel and drag it there. Then you can add what ever you like onto that panel with "Add to panel"
