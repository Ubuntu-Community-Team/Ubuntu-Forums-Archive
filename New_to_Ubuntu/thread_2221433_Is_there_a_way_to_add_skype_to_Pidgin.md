### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-05-02
As far as I'm aware there is nothing other than skype which will talk to skype; it will not work with other applications.

If you want alternatives to skype that can work with one another have a look at all the SIP applications; most are cross platform so work in Linux, Windows, Mac, so I'm sure you should find something there.

Have a look in synaptic or software-centre [synaptic is better for searching in my opinion] and you'll see a few, but I think google+ also works with SIP applications.  I've not tried anything but skype personally, as that is what all my distant family uses, so proceed with some caution.
