### Post by tristan16 on 2015-09-09
That's not an Ubuntu password, it's a BIOS password. BIOS is the Basic In/Out System, and makes sure your computer is working before booting the OS (Windows, Mac, or Linux).

To reset your BIOS password, try the tips from this article and hope for the best. Good luck! [http://www.computerhope.com/issues/ch000235.htm](http://www.computerhope.com/issues/ch000235.htm)
