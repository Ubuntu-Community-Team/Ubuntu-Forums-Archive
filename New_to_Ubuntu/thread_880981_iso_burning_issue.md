### Post by Oldsoldier2003 on 2008-08-05
ah what was I thinking? :) for some reason I thought you had a working Ubuntu install.

Some things to think about when burning isos.

To make sure you have a good download- always make sure that the checksum of your downloaded iso matches the published checksum.

Also we recommend that you burn the ISO at the lowest supported speed of your cd writer on a good quality CD or CD/RW. 

Also make sure you aren't using a 640 MB cd that you found under the couch or in a corner of the attic :)
