Somehow my torrents started opening with gedit, I have to right-click and choose transmission. How do I change it back?

---

### Post by jgoguen on 2009-01-16
Find a torrent file, right-click it, and choose **Properties**.  From there, choose the **Open With** tab.  If Transmission is in that list, click the circle beside it.  If not, click the **Add** button, find Transmission in the list that appears, and click **Add**.  Once Transmission is selected, click **OK** and torrents should start opening with Transmission again.
