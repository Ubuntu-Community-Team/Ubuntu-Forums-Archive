### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2011-10-21
They are for your 11.04 system.
Only after you upgrade will your repositories change to the ones of 11.10.

If it bothers you, you can open the update manager, click on settings and uncheck the "Prompt me for new releases" (or something) checkbox.

Now unless you upgraded to 11.10 you will only receive update for your current version, so if your running 11.04 and it says you have update then they are for 11.04.
