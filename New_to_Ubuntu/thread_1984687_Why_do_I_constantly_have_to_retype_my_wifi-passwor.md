### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-05-22
Try right clicking the network icon in the panel, and select "Edit connections".

Choose the wireless connection tab, click on Edit again, entering the password when asked, and make sure that the two tick boxes for "Automatically connect" and "Available for all users" are selected.

If that is not successful come back again and we can think about other options.
