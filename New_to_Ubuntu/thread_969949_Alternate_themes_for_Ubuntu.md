### Post by Bölvaður on 2008-11-03
[gnome art]("http://art.gnome.org/")
[gnome look]("http://www.gnome-look.org/")

1. Download a theme.
2. Right click the background and  then "change desktop background"
3. Change tab to Theme
4. Drag the downloaded files to there
