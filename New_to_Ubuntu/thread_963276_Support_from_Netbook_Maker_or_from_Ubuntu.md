Microsoft doesn't support those OEM installs... the OEM's themselves do.  If you buy a Dell, Microsoft will tell you to call Dell.  Same is true here.  Call the OEM first (be it dell, acer, asus, etc.), and they can direct you to further support if they cannot help you.
