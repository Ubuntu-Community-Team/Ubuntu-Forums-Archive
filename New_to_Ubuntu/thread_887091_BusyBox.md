### Post by spiderbatdad on 2008-08-11
Suddenly getting busybox the way you describe, suggests that there was an improper shutdown or hardware removal on the xp system. The easiest thing I can think of is to clear the system cmos. Unplug the power and remove the battery for about 15 minutes. If this is a tower, you'll need to remove the internal battery or move the cmos jumper in and out.
