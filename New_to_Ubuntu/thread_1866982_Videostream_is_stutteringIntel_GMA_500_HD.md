### Post by thatguruguy on 2011-10-22
There aren't any proprietary drivers for intel chipsets.

As far as the stutter in streaming videos, it can be caused by several different things.  For instance, it can be caused by problems in the stream itself and bandwidth issues.  If you don't have issues on youtube but do have issues on other sites, it may be a bandwidth issue with those sites.
