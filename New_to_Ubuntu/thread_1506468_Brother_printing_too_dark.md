As a starting step, does everything look normal in your printer's options?  Check via System > Administration > Printing and right click on your HL-4030 to select Properties.  Pay special attention to the settings under Printout Mode and Ink Type.

### Post by steveneddy on 2010-06-10
Check all settings to ensure they are correct.

Check the printer itself for any options for lightening the printing.

Make sure you have the correct printer drivers installed for your printer. There are a few Brother printer drivers in Synaptic you might look at.

Look here for further info:

[http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/index.html](http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/index.html)
