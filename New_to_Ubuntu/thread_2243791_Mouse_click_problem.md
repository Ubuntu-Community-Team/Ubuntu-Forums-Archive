### Post by JKyleOKC on 2014-09-11
I had a similar problem a few months ago, in that my (wireless) mouse would frequently fail to click -- but banging it down on the desk would restore operation for anywhere between seconds to days. I finally decided that perhaps the contacts beneath its buttons were wearing out, and replaced it with another one. The problem hasn't recurred since.

Mice are inexpensive these days. Replacing it is worth a try. But why deal with a wire, if a wireless one works for you?
