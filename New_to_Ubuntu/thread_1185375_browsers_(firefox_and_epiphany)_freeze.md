I'm a bit surprised that no one has responded to your concern in the past 12 hours.

I think that the basic problems with Jaunty are driver related, in particular, the graphics drivers. If you have an Intel or ATI graphics card, I would strongly suggest getting rid of the jaunty drivers for these cards and installing the open source (metacity) drivers. I fought with my jaunty installation until I was worn out. Like your machine, mine would freeze when opening a multitude of tabs in Firefox. 

I just think that you can do better than the restricted graphics drivers. Hopefully, someone else will come along within a few minutes with a better suggestion than mine. 

Good luck Phil.
