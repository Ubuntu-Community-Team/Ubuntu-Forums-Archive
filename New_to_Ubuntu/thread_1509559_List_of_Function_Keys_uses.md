### Post by Ozymandias_117 on 2010-06-14
f1 through f12 are application specific. Meaning they do different things depending on the application you're using. You'd have to look at the applications settings and help files to find out what they do.

Edit: F1 is commonly Help and F5 is usually Refresh if the application has a refresh.
