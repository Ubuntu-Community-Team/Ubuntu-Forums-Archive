### Post by mechro on 2010-03-17
I was referring to setting Brightness, Contrast and Gamma in nvidia-settings. The default settings (for my nvidia card) in nvidia-settings are Brightness 0.00, Contrast 0.00, Gamma 1.00.

I don't know what the buttons on your flat screen should be set at. There's probably a factory default setting but essentially it's a personal choice.

I've done a quick search for a simple explanation/tutorial on these settings but I'm afraid I haven't found anything suitable. There is plenty of information out there but it quickly gets very technical. Probably the best advice is for you to make a note of your current settings and then experiment.
