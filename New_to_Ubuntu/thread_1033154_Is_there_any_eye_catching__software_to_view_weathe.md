### Post by resander on 2009-01-07
CNN and BBC weather forecasts may not be what you are looking for.

This is probably more beautiful:

[http://wwwghcc.msfc.nasa.gov/GOES/](http://wwwghcc.msfc.nasa.gov/GOES/)

Interactive Global Geostationary Weather Satellite Images
Select a weather satellite image map to view data from that sensor.
(a website)

OR

if you are looking for awesome weather/clouds/storms/wawes, try webilder at [www.webilder.org](www.webilder.org), which offers webshots and more.
