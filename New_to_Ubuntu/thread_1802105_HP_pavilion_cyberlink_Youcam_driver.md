### Post by mapes12 on 2011-07-12
In the bottom left hand corner of your keyboard there should be a key with "Fn" in blue writing. Then at the top of your keyboard on one of the keys labelled F1 to F12 there should be a key with a camera symbol on it. Press and hold down the FN key then press the key with the camera icon on it. Like you would for holding down Shift for a capital letter. 

This should turn the camera on and off with each subsequent press of the keys.
