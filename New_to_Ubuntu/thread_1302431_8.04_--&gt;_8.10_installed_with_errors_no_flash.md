So I had some problems with Flash and that is when I found out there are several ways to install it. What may have happened is previously you may have installed it for your Firefox only in the home directory, so you can reinstall by downloading the deb from [http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer](http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer) . Other wise you can retry the global installation agian, or you can try to fix the install with a reinstall -f...
