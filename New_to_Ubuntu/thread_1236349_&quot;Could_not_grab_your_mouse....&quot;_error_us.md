### Post by coffee412 on 2012-04-24
This thread is kinda old but I wanted to post a word about this type of error. The poster said its solved but there are other things that can cause this problem. 

After working on a Toshiba laptop replacing the motherboard I ran into this problem and spent alot of time googling for an answer. All the replies where centered around a security thing. Well, In my case it was actually a bad cable to the touchpad/mouse. It was making an intermitten connection. Therefore, anyone having this issue might investigate that.

Just my .02c worth here.
