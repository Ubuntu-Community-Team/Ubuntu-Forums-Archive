### Post by MikeTheC on 2009-02-07
I have a little experience messing around with TV tuner cards.

In my experience, as long as the device is basically supported by Linux, what you find is that the various TV tuner-related apps tend to just glom onto it and work with little to no problems.

I can only imagine that a USB device would function in about the same manner.

What I would suggest is that you simply buy one of these devices, try it, and if it doesn't work (with insufficient likelihood of being supported in the near term) you return the device.

In the final analysis, nobody online can ever be more of a "guarantee" than actually experiencing the working (or not) of a device itself. Give it a shot.
