### Post by n8marti on 2011-08-14
I have used Ubuntu since 9.04, and I have noticed the same thing: the volume on the movie player just doesn't go as loud as I would expect or want while watching a movie.  You can click on the speaker icon at the top-right of your screen, then go to *sound preferences* and there adjust the volume above 100% for a little help.
