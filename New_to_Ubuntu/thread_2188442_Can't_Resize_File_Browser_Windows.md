### Post by Frogs Hair on 2013-11-17
Finding the re-size threshold in the bottom right corner  can be difficult on some themes so consider a different theme . Right clicking the title bar on top of the window allows vertical re-size but that won't help with width. _If the windows are maximized _with no visible title bar the un-maximize button will appear in the top left corner of the top panel and will appear during mouse hover.
