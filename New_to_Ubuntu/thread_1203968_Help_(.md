### Post by austinpsycho on 2009-07-04
It's definitely faulty hardware; but its going to be difficult to diagnose over the interenet... if the live cd loads AND works properly; then i would say your hard drive got some damage; or is just toast.  if the live cd doesn't load or acts just like your windows and current operating system.. dont bother reinstalling; it wont do anything.  Instead, slowly remove bits of your computer till one of them loads properly; then skipping the last thing you removed; start replacing hardware (one at a time.. boot shutdown next boot shutdown next etc) hopefully then you will find the culprit; *usually ram, but could be anything
