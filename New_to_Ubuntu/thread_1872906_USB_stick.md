From you directory on your hard drive or a CD drive you simply open one file browser at your file(s) and one browser at your USB stick. Then drag and drop!

From picasa you use your internet browser (Firefox) to download the file(s). If you want to you can download them directly to your USB stick. Otherwise download to your hard drive and then drag and drop to your stick!

### Post by WasMeHere on 2011-11-02
Hi again,

Here is a step by step instruction to save a picture with Firefox to a USB stick.

Look at a picture for example
[[COLOR="RoyalBlue"]_https://picasaweb.google.com/110003558527670900853/VissendeFuut?feat=featured#5587779403956531474_[/COLOR]]("https://picasaweb.google.com/110003558527670900853/VissendeFuut?feat=featured#5587779403956531474")

and browse in the window to your USB drive according to the attached picture
