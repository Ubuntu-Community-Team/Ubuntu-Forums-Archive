### Post by emnaki on 2008-11-09
I am using Intrepid from a Samsung Q45. 
When I am running on battery acpi automatically dims my screen when I am idle. Is there a way to prevent Auto dimming when I am on battery? I like to set my monitor to be fairly dim to begin with and when this dimming effect takes place, I can hardly see my monitor.

### Post by bryncoles on 2008-11-10
click (or right click, i forget which) on the battery notification in the top-task bar (assuming you've not moved it). this will bring up power management options. 

its also in system, control panel, power management

there *should* be a tab for 'on battery power' where you can set the dim behaviour to 'dont do it', though i only have this option when im actually running on battery power fro some reason. hope that fixes ya!
