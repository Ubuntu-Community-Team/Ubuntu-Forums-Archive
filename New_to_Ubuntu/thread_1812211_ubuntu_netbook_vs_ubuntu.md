> **Ubuntu Desktop Edition**
1 GHz x86 processor (Pentium 4 or better)
[B]512 MiB of system memory (RAM)
[/B]5 GB of hard-drive space
Graphics card and monitor capable of 800x600
Either a CD/DVD drive or a USB port (or both)
Internet access is helpful

> [B]Ubuntu Netbook Edition
[/B]Intel Atom processor @ 1.6 GHz
[B]386 MiB of system memory (RAM)
[/B]4 GB of disk space
Screen of 1024x600 resolution
Graphics chipset with support for visual effects


What is the specification of your machine?
The minimum system requirements not necessarily to be 100% accurate.

Minimum System Requirements for Linux, in general, are vary from 32MB to more than 1GB of RAM.
