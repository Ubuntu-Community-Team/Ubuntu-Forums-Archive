### Post by QIII on 2013-05-05
Hello and welcome to the forums!

10.10 is long past its end of life and receives no further security updates.  You would have a hard time installing anything on it unless someone told you how to find the deactivated repositories -- which seems a bit silly at this point.

If you don't like Unity, you can look into Xubuntu with Xfce, Kubuntu with KDE or Lubuntu with LXDE as a start.  I switch between Xubuntu and Kubuntu from time to time.  I've never used Lubuntu, but it comes well recommended.  There are plenty of other desktop environments (DEs) you can install as well.

Best wishes!
