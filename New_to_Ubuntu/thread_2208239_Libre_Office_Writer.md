### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-02-27
Which versions of Ubuntu and Libreoffice are you using and what grammar checker do you have added?

Grammar checking has always been a waste of time as far as I can remember in the past when I used it in MS Word (can't remember which version) but to check it out I have just downloaded **LanguageTool** from [http://extensions.libreoffice.org/extension-center/languagetool](http://extensions.libreoffice.org/extension-center/languagetool) in order to try it out again.  A quick check on one or two long .odt documents seems to suggest that it works well enough for my needs, having found a few minor errors, so I shall keep it for now and see how it manages in future.

 I find the spellchecker works fine on my system, though it does, of course, depend on which languages you have set as default for Writer in LO Options, and which languages you have in myspell and/or hunspell on your system for the correct dictionaries, as well as the correct dictionary extension for LO, many are available at [http://extensions.libreoffice.org/extension-center?getCategories=Dictionary&getCompatibility=any](http://extensions.libreoffice.org/extension-center?getCategories=Dictionary&getCompatibility=any)
