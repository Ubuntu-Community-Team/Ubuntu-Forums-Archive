### Post by ibuclaw on 2009-06-28
Try running compiz-check first, that should tell you what is going wrong, or why you can't run compiz on your computer (yet).
[http://forlong.blogage.de/entries/pages/Compiz-Check](http://forlong.blogage.de/entries/pages/Compiz-Check)

Although, 9 times out of 10 it is because you have Metacity simple compositing effects turned on, and this is blocking compiz from starting.

Open **gconf-editor** and browse to the following key:
```
/apps/metacity/compositing_manager
```
and ensure that the box is **unchecked**.
