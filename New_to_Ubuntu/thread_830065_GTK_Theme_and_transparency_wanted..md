### Post by Nepherte on 2008-06-15
To get the panel transparency, right click on the panel and choose properties. Afterwards you click on the background tab and you move the transparency slider.

You can accomplish the same for your terminal. Open up the terminal and choose edit > current profile. Under the effects tab, you find a similar slider.

Not sure what the theme is, but it's most likely on [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org)
