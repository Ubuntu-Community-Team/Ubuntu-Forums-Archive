### Post by WRDN on 2008-08-04
When you say you ran the uninstaller, did you open the uninstaller file, or use the "Uninstall Wine Software" tool (Applications > Wine > Uninstall Wine Software)?

You could just delete the files and remove the entry from the programs list. To do this, click Applications > Wine > Browse C:\ Drive, then navigate to the applicable directory, and delete it.
To remove the entry from the Applications menu, right click on the menu, click "Edit Menus", right click on the entry for IE, and click Delete.
