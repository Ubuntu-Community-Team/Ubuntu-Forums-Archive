The program you're looking for is known as "GTK Wiimote Whiteboard" in Ubuntu. You can download and install it via "Add/Remove Applications". Just search for "GTK Wiimote Whiteboard" (without the quotes obviously) and you'll be on your way.

Make note that you need an LED light or some kind of infrared light source to use it.
