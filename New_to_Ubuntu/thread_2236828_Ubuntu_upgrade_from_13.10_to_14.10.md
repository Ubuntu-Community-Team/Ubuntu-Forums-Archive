### Post by howefield on 2014-07-29
Yes, the direct way is a clean install.

Upgrades are sequential, eg 13.10 to 14.04 to 14.10, except for LTS (long term support) releases which can be upgraded directly to the next LTS, eg 12.04 to 14.04. 

It usually takes almost as much data to upgrade as downloading the live cd.
