### Post by Paqman on 2009-12-30
Sounds like something is a bit messed up in your Firefox profile. You can scrap the profile and start with a fresh one by deleting the .mozilla folder in your home directory. Hit ctrl-H to view all the hidden .folders, then close Firefox and delete .mozilla. When you restart Firefox you should have a nice fresh profile.

You could also use a service like Xmarks (browser plugin) or Google Bookmarks (online) to back up your bookmarks so they don't get lost.
