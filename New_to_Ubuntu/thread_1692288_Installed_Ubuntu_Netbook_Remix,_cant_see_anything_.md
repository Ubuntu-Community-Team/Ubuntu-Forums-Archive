### Post by fooman on 2011-02-21
just ubuntu or a dual-boot with windows?

when it boots up,  do you first get to a log-in screen? ...or does it boot directly to the desktop?

if you first go to a log-in screen,  try this:

at the login window,  click once on your user name,  then at the bottom, middle of the panel you will see ubuntu-netbook listed....click the arrow at the end of that space and you should see some other choices.  choose "ubuntu-desktop version",  type in your password,  press enter and see if that loads up properly.

hope that helps
