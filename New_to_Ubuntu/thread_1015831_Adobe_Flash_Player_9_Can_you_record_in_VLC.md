### Post by PierreDeKat on 2008-12-19
An easier way would be to look in your /tmp folder *after* the animation/video is fully loaded and *while* you still have the webpage open, but *before* the animation/video finishes playing. 

The animation/video gets cached there, and you can simply copy it and paste it someplace else, like in your /home/username directory.

Once you close the webpage or once the animation/video finishes playing, the cached animation/video disappears from /tmp.
