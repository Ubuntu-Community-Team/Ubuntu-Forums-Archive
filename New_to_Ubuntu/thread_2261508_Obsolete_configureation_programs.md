### Post by mooreted on 2015-01-19
They are all for different things. CCSM is to tweak Compiz which is the compositing used by the Unity desktop environment. The Unity Tweak Tool is for changing specific settings for Unity itself. Tweak Tool is probably a Gnome thing. None of these are for configuring your mouse.

What sort of configuration do you want for your mouse? What sort of mouse is it? Most gaming mice have Windows-only configuration tools.
