### Post by Nascentes on 2010-04-06
If you don't do any gaming, then there really isn't much point in upgrading your card.
You have a good enough card to run everyday programs and whatnot.

Although, it's always nice to have a good video card and if you have the extra cash to spend, I'd say go for it :)

I would personally go with the nVidia GeForce 9600GS. I have always used this line of video cards in the past and have never had any unfixable issues with them. I would highly recommend the line for anybody :)
