### Post by mister_pink on 2008-05-14
You can have your google calendar in thunderbird: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/addon/4631](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/addon/4631)
Check out [http://wiki.mozilla.org/Calendar:GDATA_Provider](http://wiki.mozilla.org/Calendar:GDATA_Provider) for more instructions.

If you only want to do this one off then export your calendar to an ics file from thunderbird, I dont think its stored in that format usually. (Right click the name of the calendar for export)
