I don't know how it happened, but I am currently missing the notification area in the Kubuntu 9.04 panel.  I assumed that it is called the notification area, where I can see the volume icon, icons for other programs, and update notifications.  I would like to know how to restore it to the panel.

---

### Post by starcraft.man on 2009-08-06
Right click blank space on panel > Panel options > Add widgets > System Tray (use search at top). If you want to move it around (or other widgets) right click panel > Panel Options > Panel Settings. Incidentally, you can also add widgets from panel Settings pull out screen.
