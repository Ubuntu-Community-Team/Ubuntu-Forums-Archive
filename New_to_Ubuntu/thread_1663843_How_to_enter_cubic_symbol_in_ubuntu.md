### Post by AVanover5 on 2011-01-10
Do you mean the 'superscript 3' symbol or one of those 'box-drawing characters'? You can either enter this via the [CharacterMap]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CharacterMap") application or [ComposeKey]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ComposeKey"). ComposeKey I find to be much more convenient. Since you're looking to enter it in unicode: you can use [this method]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ComposeKey#Unicode composition").

Unicode values for:
[Box-Drawing Characters]("http://www.unicode.org/charts/PDF/U2500.pdf")
[Block Elements]("http://www.unicode.org/charts/PDF/U2580.pdf")
[Superscripts and subscripts]("http://www.unicode.org/charts/PDF/U2070.pdf")
