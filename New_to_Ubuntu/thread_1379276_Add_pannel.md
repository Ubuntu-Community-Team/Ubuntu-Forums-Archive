### Post by anaconda on 2010-01-12
right-click on the remaining panel, and choose "New panel" And then you can drag it to the place you want it to be..

After that you can right-click to the new panel and select "Add to panel" and try to add all the items that should be on the panel....  eg. Menu bar and notification area.. are usually on the top panel...

PS. You need to right-click on an empty place on the panel..
