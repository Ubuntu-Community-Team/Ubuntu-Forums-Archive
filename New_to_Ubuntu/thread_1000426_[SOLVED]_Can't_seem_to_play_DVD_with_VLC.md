### Post by nkri on 2008-12-02
By default, Ubuntu doesn't have support for encrypted DVDs because of legal reasons.  You can enable it by following the instructions on [_this_]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs#Ubuntu%208.10%20(i386)") page.

Good luck!
-nkri
