Is that the proprietary product, TurboPrint? If no one knows the answer on this forum, you'd be best to ask the suppliers, or try [the Turboprint forum.]("http://www.turboprint.info/support/")

> **Karlof said:**
> Ubuntu keep on insisting on upgrading from 10.10.to narwhal 

It is not insisting; it is merely suggesting. You do not have to upgrade if you do not want to. If you don't want to be notified of new releases, open Update Manager, click on the Settings button, make sure the updates tab is focussed, and change the setting under "Show new distribution releases" from "Normal Releases" to "Never" or "Long Term Support Releases only".
