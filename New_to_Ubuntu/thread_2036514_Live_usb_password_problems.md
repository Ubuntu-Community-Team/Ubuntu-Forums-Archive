### Post by Cheesemill on 2012-08-03
Every time that I've had a Live CD or USB ask for a password it's been because of a bad burn/copy.

Check that your downloaded ISO file is good by checking the [MD5SUM]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM/"), when you are sure that your download is good then try recreating your bootable USB.
