### Post by ubuntu27 on 2012-04-09
Oh, by the way, you can take a screnshot of an active (focused) window by hitting

**ALT + Print Screen**   (a.k.a Prt Sc)


So, let's say that you want to take a screenshot of just Firefox, open Firefox, and then hit ALT+ PRT SC :guitar:
