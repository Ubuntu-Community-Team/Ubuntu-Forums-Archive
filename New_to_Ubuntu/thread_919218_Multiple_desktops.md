### Post by gali98 on 2008-09-13
On the bottom right of the screen there should be a trash icon. Right next to it are some boxes.. these represent desktops. each box is a desktop. Just click on the boxes to go to those desktops.

Its actually very nice to have if you have lots of things going on at once.
You can actually increase this number if you want.

### Post by tarahmarie on 2008-09-13
> **crazypenguin2008 said:**
> how do you increase the number of desktops??

System Settings--Desktop---Multiple Desktops  

Increase the number of virtual desktops.

If your widget isnt' showing all the desktops (i.e. you have four desktops enabled, and your pager widget only shows 2, right-click the widget and change its settings.
