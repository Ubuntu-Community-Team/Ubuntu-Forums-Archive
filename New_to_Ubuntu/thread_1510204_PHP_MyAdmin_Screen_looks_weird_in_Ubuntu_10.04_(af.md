Hello everyone!

After I upgraded to Ubuntu 10.04, I noticed an unusual symbol on my phpmyadmin screen. It works fine but this 'extra character' keeps me thinking what it could be.

On the screen shot, you'll see the phpmyadmin screen, and there's the list of databases in the drop-down box. To the right of the text box below it, I noticed the 'X'. I haven't clicked it to find out but placing the mouse over it shows the word 'reset'.

Any idea what this is? I never saw this on my old Ubuntu 8.04, this wasn't there.

Thanks for your ideas!

---

### Post by Ryan Dwyer on 2010-06-15
It's a new feature in the latest versions of phpMyAdmin. Type in the box to filter your tables, and click the X to clear the filter.
