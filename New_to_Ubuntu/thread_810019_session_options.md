what do the options (Automatically remember running applications when logging out, and Remember currently running applications) under System/Preferences/Sessions/Session Options tab do in ubuntu 8.04?  thanks.

---

### Post by quelx on 2008-05-27
It remembers applications you had open and to a degree the state they were in when you quit Gnome.  When you start your session again it attempts to load those applications (ie. recreate your session)
