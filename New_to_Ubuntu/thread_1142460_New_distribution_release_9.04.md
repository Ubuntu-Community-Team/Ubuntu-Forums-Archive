### Post by kpkeerthi on 2009-04-29
If you want to "upgrade", I suggest you upgrade using the [Alternate CD ISO]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors#alternate") by following [this]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/upgrading#Upgrading%20Using%20the%20Alternate%20CD/DVD") method. Online upgrade takes a lot of time.

Be sure to backup your stuff, just in case.
