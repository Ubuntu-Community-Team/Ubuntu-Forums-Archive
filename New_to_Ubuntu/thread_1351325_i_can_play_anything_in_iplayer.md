Okay, I've got to say this -it really does need saying.  The OP of this thread is not the only person guilty of this, I've seen it in other threads.  When you say "I can play anything in iPlayer", you are actually saying that you *are able* to play anything; that you don't have any problem playing anything.  If you want to say that you are *unable* to play anything, you should say "I *can't* play anything".
