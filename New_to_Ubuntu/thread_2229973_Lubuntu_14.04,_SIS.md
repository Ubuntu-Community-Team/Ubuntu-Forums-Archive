### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-06-16
Welcome. No, Lubuntu 12.04 won't solve your problems as it is no longer supported. They don't have an LTS (long-term support) release.

Lubuntu 14.04 may solve your problem. Once installed let us know if you're still having the same issues or if any new ones have come up. 

Yes, you might be able watch Youtube on it, but don't expect much. The SIS graphics *is* horrible and old and occasionally nightmarish on Linux.

### Post by claracc on 2014-06-17
About watching youtube videos, you cannot use adobe flash because of the freezing (too little ram memory and an old graphics sis card with I assume little memory too).

I recommend to watch them trough firefox browser with greasymonkey add on installed and then install viewtube script in it to play videos with mplayer in other than flash format (resources hog). Here you have a link: [http://isebaro.com/viewtube/?ln=en](http://isebaro.com/viewtube/?ln=en) to know howto.

This way I think you will obtain reasonable performance for playing youtube videos in this old computer.
