### Post by Ylon on 2009-12-04
Probably the quickest way is to look in the settings of your TV. They are usually delivered with all formats (pal/ntsc/secam).
usually: remote control > menu > image/video/colors



Alternative:
[alt] + [F2]> nvidia-settings > X Server Settigs > Layout


click on the box that represents the TV-out and change the resolution and everything from there.
