Depends on what you want from a "light weight browser".

midori works well, but it is a little rough around the edges.

Firefox without extensions works well, IMO.

IMO chromium and opera are a bit better then midori (midori is #4 on my list).

You can try filing a bug report, but you may not have much luck.

You could try one of the open source alternates to flash, gnash or lightspark:

[http://sourceforge.net/apps/trac/lightspark](http://sourceforge.net/apps/trac/lightspark)

At least that way, if you have a problem, you may have better luck with bug reports.
