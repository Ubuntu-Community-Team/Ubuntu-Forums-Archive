### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-10-29
How large is the hard-drive? Remember that there will be space considerations depending on the usage and needs of each person.

One alternative to consider is to have one admininstrative account and then 3 separate accounts for each user, each with their own home folder.

Each account would have its own password for login but the mother can be admn and decide what software is installed etc.
