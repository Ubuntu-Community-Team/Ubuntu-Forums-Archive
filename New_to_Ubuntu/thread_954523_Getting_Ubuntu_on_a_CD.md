### Post by semitone36 on 2008-10-21
Wow. hold up.  This is a lot of confusing advice all at once.  Allow me to answer your question wownerd.  

Whatever program you use to burn your music with (I usually use Nero) should have a burning option to burn "images" or .iso, you said you have that button.  Then just burn the file you downloaded to the disk.

Something to keep in mind: iso files can be touchy so its a good idea to set the burn speed of the program to AT MOST 4x.  This helps to keep the iso from getting corrupted during burning.
