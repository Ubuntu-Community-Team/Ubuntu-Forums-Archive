### Post by winh8r on 2012-04-04
There is NO substitute for doing "real" backups. Cloud is fine for 

convenience, but it is always a good idea to continue to back up your 

valuable data locally and on another drive or media to the one where your 

installation is running.

I tend to look at the cloud as a smaller safety net below the big one (traditional backups done locally).

It might seem like a hassle to do, but believe me it is well worth it when disaster day comes!!
