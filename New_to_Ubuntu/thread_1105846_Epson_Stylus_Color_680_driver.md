### Post by yeats on 2009-03-25
The simple answer is that the type of printer control programs designed for Windows are not available on Linux.  If you're able to print, the driver is not the problem and I would be VERY wary of changing from the driver you know works.  It might be worth investigating the repositories (by doing a search in the Synaptic Package Manager) or Google for options.  Someone may chime in with a favorite program...
