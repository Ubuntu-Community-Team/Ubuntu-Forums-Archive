### Post by blueridgedog on 2009-01-21
Openbravoerp is a "big iron" ERP product and the installation of it is pretty complex for a beginner's forum on Ubuntu.  Take a look at:

[http://sourceforge.net/projects/openbravo](http://sourceforge.net/projects/openbravo)

You can access forums there that will be of help.

You can launch the remote desktop viewer by going to applications -> Internet then selecting remote desktop viewer.

To configure your IP, select system -> administration -> network tools, then pick your Ethernet controller from the drop down list (probably eth0) and configure it with your ip/gateway/etc
