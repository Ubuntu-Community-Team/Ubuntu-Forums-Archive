### Post by laidback on 2011-07-09
I have copied mine across to a large USB HDD with Gparted. It's probably not the most efficient method but it has worked for me. Gparted needs to be run from a Live CD as you cannot copy from your booted system. And as the copy is on your external HDD you can access the files at any time.

I started doing this when messing about with coning my system. I wanted to switch to a different HDD, it worked.
