### Post by Pletched on 2013-01-15
The same addon is available for fx. Mozilla designed fx to make it simple to get addons. Doodle over to Tools/Add-ons, first. In the "get addons" page search for "ABP" or "adblock". 

If you think you have found the right one after searching around, click install.

Addblock+ needs a list. It prompts choose one after install occurs. Don't forget...
