What version of Ubuntu you are using? If its precise, the process is as follows:-
1. Click on the top right corner of the window, where you will see your name on the top. A drop down menu will appear. 
2. Then click on the User Accounts... option. A window will appear.
3. The click on the top right corner of the window, where you will see a lock icon. It will then ask you for the administrator's password. type in the administrator's password and then press enter.
4. The click on the your name, it will automatically will come in edit mode. Delete your name and update it.
