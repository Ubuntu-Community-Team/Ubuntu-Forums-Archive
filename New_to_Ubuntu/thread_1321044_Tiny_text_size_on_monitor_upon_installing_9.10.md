### Post by AlexOslo on 2009-11-09
I just installed 9.10, but the text on the GUI is just tiny.
I went to System -> Display, and it detected my Hansol 900P monitor with the right settings (1600*1200, 75 Hz).
What can I do to change it? 

Monitor specs:
[http://www.computerdisplays.co.uk/19%20inch%20monitors/hansol_900p.htm](http://www.computerdisplays.co.uk/19%20inch%20monitors/hansol_900p.htm)

### Post by Paqman on 2009-11-09
Go to System > Prefs > Appearance and see what the DPI is set at. Default is 96 I think? Setting it higher should give you bigger fonts.

---

### Post by AlexOslo on 2009-11-10
> **Paqman said:**
> Go to System > Prefs > Appearance and see what the DPI is set at. Default is 96 I think? Setting it higher should give you bigger fonts.

Excellent, worked perfectly, setting DPI under Fonts > Details... to 168 (from 96).
