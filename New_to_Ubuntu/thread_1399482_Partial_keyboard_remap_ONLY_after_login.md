### Post by audiomick on 2010-02-05
The first guess is that your user profile has somehow gotten a foreign keyboard assigned to it. I assume you are using a machine with a qwerty keyboard. The German one, for instance, is qwert[COLOR=Red]z[/COLOR], with the y where the z is on a qwerty.

Relating to your description, right of p is ü, right of l is ö ä, right of m is , . - , and right of 0 (zero) is ß. Shift and a number is all different too.

Does that ring any bells?
