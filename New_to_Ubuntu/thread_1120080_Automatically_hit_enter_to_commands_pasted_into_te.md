I've noticed that when I paste some commands into terminal they are automatically entered, while other times I have to manually hit enter after pasting. ### Post by The Cog on 2009-04-08
The number-keypad Enter should work if you have one.

Pressing Ctrl-M should also work.

When you copy, dragging the cursor down to the start of the next line (instead of to the end of the current line) will normally copy the newline into the copy/paste buffer so that you don't need to type enter.
