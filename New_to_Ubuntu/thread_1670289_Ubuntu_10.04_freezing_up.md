### Post by frank0936 on 2011-01-18
Found it. I had two batteries in and the laptop won't run at all on battery power. I swapped the batteries from one bay to the other. I took the older battery out, left the newer one in and it started working right again. It's a Dell Inspiron 8200. Apparently Ubuntu has a thing about batteries. There's been too many threads about battery problems for it to be otherwise..
