Hi Blake

I sometimes have over 15 instances of Firefox open and running doing different things.

Every once in awhile, one of them may act up and decide it's going to hog 100% of the CPU's resources.

I've checked a dozen times and found no reason for it.  It's just does sometimes.

My CPU normally runs along at 15 to 30% with programs running.  Opening another instance seems to only bring it up maybe another 2 to 5%.

Everything can run just fine sometimes for days, then suddenly Firefox will decide to eat it all again.

Since all instances are running the same program at the same web site, it's not logical that it's something being sent in differently than to all the others.

However, I have noticed this happens more often on some computers than others, but it seems to happen on all of them on occasion.
