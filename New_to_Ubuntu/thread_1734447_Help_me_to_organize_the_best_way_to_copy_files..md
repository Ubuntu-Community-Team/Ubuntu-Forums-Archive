### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-04-20
A nice easy way to transfer files over the Internet (I think that's what you want to do) is with Skype. It's cross-platform, so you don't have to worry about what system you're using.

Install Skype on both computers, and you can safely share files with each other (the connection is encrypted) very easily.
