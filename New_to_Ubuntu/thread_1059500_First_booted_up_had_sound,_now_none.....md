> **mrbreese said:**

I had the same problem. Double click the speaker icon in the upper right, select the sound board/dev you have your speakers plugged into, then go to the switches tab and if the digital output box is checked, clear it.

It worked for me, but not all of my sound devices have that option. Just the one I happened to be using.
