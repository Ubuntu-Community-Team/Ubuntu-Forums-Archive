Welcome to Ubnutu and, since you asked, you can look into your computer.

Click on Applications > Accessories > Terminal

Type sudo lshw (or copy/paste this command) and enter.

You will be asked for your password but, when you do so, you won't see it.

Click 'enter'

You will now get a list of all the hardware on your machine.

Hope this will be interesting for you.

yes you can spend hours tweaking compiz-fusion desktop effects.  it has tons of eye candy like the rotating cube, or different animations for closing/minimizing windows.  I set mine up so that when i minimize any window it folds the window into a paper airplane and zooms down to my taskbar.  If i press X to close a window instead, the window bursts into flames and disappears.  my friends are totally envious :lolflag:

If you've got a fresh install of Ubuntu Hardy Heron, then i recommend adding VLC to watch videos instead of the builtin totem.  Same goes for the builtin bit torrent client transmission.  ditch it and switch to Deluge.  I know Evolution comes standard with ubuntu but i'm familiar with Thunderbird and its cross platform so I downloaded that too.  

Its so easy to install or remove software with Add/Remove button in Applications.
