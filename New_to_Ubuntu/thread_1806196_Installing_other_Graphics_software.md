### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-07-17
You should NOT be installing anything using Wine until you have first visited the WineHQ website and looked through the ratings for the app and version you want to use.

The link below is the WineHQ page for PhotoPlus:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=6033]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=6033")

You need to click on the right-hand side of the linked page to see the individual test results.
