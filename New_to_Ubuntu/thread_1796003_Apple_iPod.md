### Post by robbiemacg on 2011-07-03
If you're using 11.04, you should be able to sync using Banshee, the default audio player/manager.
Are you getting an error message when you plug in your iPod? What sort of an iPod do you have?

Take a look at the this page: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone)
Follow the steps to install the PPA described at the bottom of the page and install **libimobiledevice2** and **libimobiledevice-utils**.

In many cases that'll be enough to get things up and running, though you may additionally need to do what I describe in this thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1770199](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1770199)
