In my fresh install of Hardy, Kde won't remember maximized window of firefox after closing the window.

How do i fix that?

---

### Post by Christmas on 2008-05-06
You can try this:

Go to Window Menu (press ALT+F3), then go to 'Configure Window Behavior...', and the 'Window-Specific Settings' tab. Hit the New button on the right, detect the Firefox window by hitting Detect and pointing to it, hit OK in the window that appears (no changes here), then go to the Geometry tab and either select 'Size - Apply Initially' or both 'Maximized vertically' and 'Maximized horizontally'. Hit OK and it should be done.
