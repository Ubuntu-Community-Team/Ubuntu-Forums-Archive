### Post by warfacegod on 2010-01-18
If you are reasonably good at updating your system on your own then you can disable the update notification entirely. Go to: System> Admin.> Software Sources> Updates tab and uncheck the Check For Updates: box. Just remember to check for updates manually every week or so.
