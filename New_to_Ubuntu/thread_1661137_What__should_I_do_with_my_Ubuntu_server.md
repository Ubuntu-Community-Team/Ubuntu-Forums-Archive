### Post by carandraug on 2011-01-06
You can set up a [unison server]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unison_%28file_synchronizer%29") to keep your computers synchronized.

There's also [tracks]("http://getontracks.org/") to organize your todos and access them from everywhere (I wrote a tutorial here on [how to do it]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1580690"). 

Or a teeworlds server to play with friends every once in a while if you're into it.
