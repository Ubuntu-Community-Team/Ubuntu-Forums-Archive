### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2010-07-16
In linux , there are programs and applications that do not appear in the menu, they run in a terminal or on the command line.

This is usually the case with services, such as Apache or in your case anon proxy.

You start anon proxy on the command line and configure your browser to use it.

For an example using a different proxy, privoxy, see

[http://blog.bodhizazen.net/linux/how-to-transparent-proxy/](http://blog.bodhizazen.net/linux/how-to-transparent-proxy/)

I am not familiar enough with anon proxy to give you more specific advice.
