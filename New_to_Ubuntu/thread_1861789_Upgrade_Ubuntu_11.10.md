DesktopNova hasn't been updated for Gnome 3 as of yet, unfortunately.  Wallch is an alternative available in the Software Centre, though it lacks one or two features, or if you have ccsm, gnome tweak, and the extra compiz plugins installed, you can use the Wallpaper plugin to cycle through your wallpapers (and have one for every workspace), though there is a slight dip in compiz performance with that.
