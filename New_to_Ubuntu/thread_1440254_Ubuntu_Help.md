### Post by Tallman on 2010-03-27
Hi frillybob, and welcome to Ubuntu!

From what I read in your first post it seems like you have a badly burned disk. I suggest you do the following:

1. Check you downloaded iso file for errors. This will make sure your downloaded file is not corrupt. See this link:[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

2. After you made sure your iso file is ok, burn a new cd at the lowest possible speed. The higher the speed, the bigger the chance that something goes wrong.
