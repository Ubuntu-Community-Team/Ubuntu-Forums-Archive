### Post by col48 on 2016-10-23
What I did was go into my newsagent and buy a magazine with an enclosed DVD.  This had both 32-bit and 64-bit versions.  Simpler (although more expensive) than downloading and burning my own DVD.

As a Live DVD, you can try before installing and if things go pear-shaped and you lose confidence in the integrity of the installed (or part-installed) OS you still have the DVD to start again from.
