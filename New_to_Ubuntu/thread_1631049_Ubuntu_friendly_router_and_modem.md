### Post by Paqman on 2010-11-26
> **manojchandra said:**
> Will buying a new router modem help? 

Unlikely, but it's hard to be sure without knowing a bit more about the nature of the trouble you're having. Do you have trouble getting a connection, or do you have performance problems once connected? Have you hooked either machine up via ethernet cable?

If the problem you're having with wifi is due to the specific wifi chipset in the NC20 you're better off swapping the wifi card for a better supported one. It's easy to do once you've the case open. Intel cards tend to work without problems, and you could pick one up very cheaply off eBay.
