### Post by jingaling on 2012-10-05
Hi Yezinki,

It's very difficult to answer your question *"Does one need to install any thing else besides Libre Office Writer, Office Calc & Office Impress?"* unless we know what you want to use the OS for.

If all you need are documents, spreadsheets and presentations then yes, that's all you need. If you want to ability to create and manilulate databases or create graphics like posters then you will need Libre Base and Draw respectively.

Like with any OS, the software you have installed will be dependant on your requirement(s).
