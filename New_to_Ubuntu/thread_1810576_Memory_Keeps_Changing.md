### Post by Swagman on 2011-07-23
Hi Donna.


Thing the first... Two different kinds of memory,  Ram & hard drive (Temp & Permanent)

I'll hazard a guess that when you installed Ubuntu you didn't resize your Vista partition so Ubuntu tried to squeeze into the tiny amount it found available.

I've done that too !!

It might be easier to re-install only this time resize (shrink) your Vista partition

Move stuff you have saved in your ubuntu partition first.

Little tip.. boot into Windows and defrag before starting partitioning.
