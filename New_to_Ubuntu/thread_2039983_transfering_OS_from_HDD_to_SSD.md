First things first: Before you do anything, ***_make sure you have viable backup_***.

You can do this with a Clonezilla Live CD.
 
Download "CloneToASmallerDrive_0.2.odt" instructions [here]("http://clonezilla.org/downloads/doc/clonezilla-live-stable-doc.php") and follow them. 

I've skimmed them and they look fairly user friendly. You can do it!
