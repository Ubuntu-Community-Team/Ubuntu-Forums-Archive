### Post by jaddi27 on 2012-02-18
After you have installed Google Earth, you should be able to launch it using the Dash in Ubuntu 11.10. Click on the Ubuntu logo on the Launcher, or press the Windows key on the keyboard to open the Dash. After this, you should be able to type in Google Earth, and it should appear in the list of programs available to open. Click on the icon to launch Google Earth.

I am not sure why installing Google Earth should stop videos from working. What browser were you using for this? I suggest making sure all updates are installed, and if this does not work, try using another browser (Chromium or Firefox, depending on which one you tried before). You might also need to install the flash player and make sure it is updated for the videos to work.
