### Post by Paqman on 2009-12-22
Sure, just plug it in with the USB cable, the phone will notify that a connection has been established. Just hit the "mount" button on the phone to have your SD card mount on your Ubuntu machine. When you're done hit "unmount" on the phone and unplug the cable.
