### Post by prshah on 2008-11-01
> **fignig said:**
> i want all of my windows to be opened maximized, is there anyway of doing this? like when i open firefox i always have to drag it down, then maximize it. and i just would like it to open maximized

> **fignig said:**
> also i need help w/ my keyboard shortcuts they dont seem to be working, like when i hit the right super button, it won't lock my screen

To open all windows maximised; click System->Preferences->CompizConfig Settings Manager->Window Management->Place Windows->Placement Mode="Maximize". To only maximize firefox windows, add ```
title=firefox
``` to the "Force Placement Windows" option box.

To remap your keyboard shortcuts, click on System-Preferences-Keyboard Shortcuts-Desktop-Lock Screen, then press the right super (windows) key. Now the shortcut should be defined as "Super R"
