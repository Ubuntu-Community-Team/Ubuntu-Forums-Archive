### Post by nhasian on 2010-04-30
to prevent ubuntu from requiring a password after the screen blacks, go to system-> preferences-> screensaver

then untick the box labeled "Lock screen when screensaver is active"
while your at it you can disable the screen saver too.  i just use power management to turn the display off after 30 mins.
