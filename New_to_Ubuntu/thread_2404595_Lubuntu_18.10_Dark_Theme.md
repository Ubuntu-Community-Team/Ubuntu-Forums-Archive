### Post by john-donahue on 2018-11-01
Try:
[FONT=courier new]Applications menu> Preferences>  KDE System Settings>
Appearance> Workspace Theme> Look And Feel> Breeze Dark
[/FONT][FONT=arial]Press [FONT=courier new]**Apply **[FONT=arial]button at bottom.[/FONT][/FONT][/FONT]

Also under  [FONT=courier new]KDE System Settings>[/FONT]
  [FONT=courier new]Appearance> Colors> Application Color Scheme: Breeze High Contrast [/FONT](or other dark choices in list)
Press **[FONT=courier new]Apply[/FONT]**[FONT=courier new][FONT=arial]&#8203;.[/FONT][/FONT]

Log out from Lubuntu and Login.

### Post by Dennis N on 2018-12-05
> **schamane2 said:**
> How comes I don't have "KDE System Settings" in a freshly installed Lubuntu 18.10?
KDE System Settings would be in an OS using the KDE Plasma Desktop. You're using LXQt desktop used by Lubuntu 18.10. KDE applications and KDE Plasma Desktop would be found in Kubuntu, another Ubuntu flavor.
