### Post by TheFu on 2014-05-15
Could just be DNS issues - slow DNS from the ISP.  Hard to tell from your description.  Sometimes the internet just gets slow in parts of the world due to misconfiguration of routing in far-off places.  Once [Pakistan took over the IPs for youtube]("http://www.wired.com/2008/02/pakistans-accid/") by accident. They intended to block internal Pakistani viewers, not the entire world.

Doubleclick is a google-owned tracking company. If you don't want to be tracked, install ghostery and an ad-blocker addon into your browser. If you also disable javascript and flash, the internet will get much faster.

Also, check the system log files for hints on any issues. Sometimes hardware fails slowly and this shows up as getting parts of the system getting slower.
