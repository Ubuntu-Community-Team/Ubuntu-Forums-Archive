### Post by webofunni on 2008-07-08
Try this :

1. Right click on any empty space in the GNOME panel and click "Add to panel"
2. Add "WorkSpace switcher"
3. Right click on the new "Work space switcher" and select preferences, where you can specify the rows and columns.
