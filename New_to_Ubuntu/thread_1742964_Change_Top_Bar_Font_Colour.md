### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-04-29
Appearance Preferences > Customize > Controls > Colors. Some themes allow color change and text is included . If you change the color in one place it may make it unreadable in another place . If it does not work simply select restore to defaults . Have fun , I just customized a theme yesterday . Welcome to the forums !
