### Post by benfindlay on 2008-07-28
Right click the volume icon by the clock and choose "Open volume control" Then click edit, choose preferences and make sure Headphone and Headphone Jack Sense are checked. Then you should be able to turn up the volume on the headphone connection.

Hope this helps!
