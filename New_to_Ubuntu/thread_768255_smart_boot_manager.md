### Post by Big Luke on 2009-07-28
Try [Super Grub Disk]("http://www.supergrubdisk.org/").  It has worked for me many a time.  You can burn it to floppy, disk or i think you can even use UNETBOOTIN to make a bootable USB drive with SGD on it...but not 100% sure on that so dont alert the media.  I will admit it is a pain to work with but after a lil bit you will get the hang of it and then its all gravy.  Good luck.
