### Post by coool on 2008-04-27
I installed this program called wallpaper-tray and i want it to start every time i load ubuntu...how do i do that?

---

### Post by hackle577 on 2008-04-27
Go to System > Preferences > Sessions. Click Add.

For name, just put Wallpaper-Tray, and for Command put "wallpaper-tray" without the quotes of course.

Click OK then close.

---

### Post by scragar on 2008-04-27
System>Preferences>Sessions

click the startup programs tab, then add.
