### Post by logos34 on 2008-11-24
the recommended way is through the online distribution upgrade.  You can also do it directly from the CD (*text-mode alternate installation CD only).  

TBH, the official procedure doesn't always work as advertised--too many times there's a glitch and you're left hanging with an inoperable system or at the very least some irritating minor issues.  That's why I personally prefer to use the alternate CD.  

I'd recommend making an image of your / and then go ahead with the upgrade.  If something goes wrong simply restore the backup image and then ponder your next move.
