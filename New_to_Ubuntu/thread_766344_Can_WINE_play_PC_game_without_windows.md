### Post by apienk01 on 2008-04-25
In fact a **lot** of games work, even if they're not listed on [Wine's application database]("http://appdb.winehq.org"). Just try to install and run, and you'll have quite a big chance of success. I have installed various unlisted software, and a majority worked fine.

BTW, don't forget to use the newest Wine. Just add [Wine repository]("http://www.winehq.org/site/download-deb") to your software sources.
