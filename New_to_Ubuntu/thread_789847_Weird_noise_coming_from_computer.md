### Post by warbread on 2008-05-10
Chances are it's you're hearing some new vibration caused by the added force of your new hard drive spinning.  When you take a metal disk that only weighs a few grams and spin it 100+ times a second, the amount of force is non trivial.  Multiply that by the number of disks in a hard drive and there's no reason to think it's not the drive.  

If you want, tighten all the screws in your case.  It could be any of them, or even a loose panel on the case itself.  Maybe even a screw that you dropped and is sitting on the bottom of your case.

### Post by warbread on 2008-05-11
It could be a large particle of some sort or a wire hitting a fan.  

Just to be sure, listen close to your hard drive and make sure it's not the read head.  Any unusual sound coming from there is bad news.  I would put my money on something to do with the fans or something loose in the case, though.
