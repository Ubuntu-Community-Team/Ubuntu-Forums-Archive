### Post by coldcritter64 on 2014-06-23
> I would be interested in why the other users would prefer a fresh install over an upgrade?                 If old ppa's or other non-standard modifications are present during an upgrade the upgrade can fail leaving a broken install. If you don't use ppa's or heavily modify an install and you follow the proper upgrade paths for the releases involved, upgrading is alright.

Basically there is less chance of hassles with a fresh install in comparison to upgrading; especially if there are ppas/mods involved in an upgrade situation.
