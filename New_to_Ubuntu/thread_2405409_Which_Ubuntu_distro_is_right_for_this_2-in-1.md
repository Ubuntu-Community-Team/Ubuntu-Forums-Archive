### Post by Mark Phelps on 2018-11-07
Specs about processor, RAM size and SSD size say nothing about availability of drivers for stuff like the touch screen and support for screen rotation.

Unless you get responses from someone who has used that exact model PC, the only real way to know what will work and what will not is to run the Linux distro on it in Live mode.

That will tell you what drivers are available and how well those drivers work.
