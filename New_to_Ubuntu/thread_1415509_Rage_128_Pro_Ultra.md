### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-02-24
Anything in the ATI Rage line is too old for Compiz (visual effects), sorry.

Also, as you might have gathered, don't try to install the ATI proprietary driver for Linux, it won't drive your card and you'll be left with a blank screen.
