### Post by cjhabs on 2011-02-03

Unfortunately I don't have a magic answer, but maybe it has something to do with the graphic itself that Open Office doesn't like.
Have you tried taking a totally different type of graphic, such as some clipart from the web, and tried putting a border around that? That may narrow down where the problem lies.

I have seen some incorrect graphics handling by OO - where Word handles it correctly.
