### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-11-18
Its easy to get things back, right click on your top panel and select "add to panel"
this is how you add new things to your panel.
Add the following panel applets:
Main Menu (there are actually two, one is the menu bar and the other is a simple menu button, choose the one you like)
clock
quit
deskbar applet
notification area

arrange them any way you like :D
