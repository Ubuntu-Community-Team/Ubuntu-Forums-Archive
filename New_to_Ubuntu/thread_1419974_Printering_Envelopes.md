### Post by lyall on 2010-03-02
what is your printer?

does your printer have a manual tray?
printers that have a manual tray, is a place to put your envelopes in.
the manual tray can also be used for other paper sizes so that you do have to change the paper the your standard paper tray

when you have to print envelopes in Open Office you have to tell your print to use the manual tray for envelopes (or any other size you want)
 
in Open Office select File > Print
on the Print window click on Properties
on the Properties for your printer
change the Paper size to Envelopes
then on the Paper tray select Manual
and click OK
then click OK 
it should print your envelopes for you

you will have to set the properties back so that you can print your letter size paper again

good luck and have fun learning
