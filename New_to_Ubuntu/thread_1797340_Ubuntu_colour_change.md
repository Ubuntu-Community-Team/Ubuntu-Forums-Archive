### Post by johnb39 on 2011-07-04
I am running Ubuntu 10.10 and have a wee problem. Occasionally, the screen of my laptop slowly loses colour to a drab grey-nothing works for half a minute or so, then colour returns and all is well (till the next time). Anyone know what could be going on? It is not isolated to any particular programme running.....hmmmm

---

### Post by nzjethro on 2011-07-04
This may be because of your power setting. Fading to grey is typical of Ubuntu's power saver setting. What are your wait time in System > Settings > Power Management?

---

### Post by johnb39 on 2011-07-05
Thank you..the setting for AC power was 15 seconds!
