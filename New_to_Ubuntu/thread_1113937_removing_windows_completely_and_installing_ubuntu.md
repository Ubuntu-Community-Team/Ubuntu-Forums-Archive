### Post by kiridude on 2009-04-02
Although you will love Ubuntu and never look back to Windows once you learn your way around the new OS, I suggest you partition your disk and keep Windows as backup in case you get stuck somewhere in Ubuntu and need to get something done fast.

When you feel comfortable in Ubuntu, then you can drop Windows entirely. I say this cuz when I made the switch it helped to have something familiar around in the beginning.

Do not fear the disk partitioning procedure. Ubuntu can do this for you during the installation process.

Have fun!
