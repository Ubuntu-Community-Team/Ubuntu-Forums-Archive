### Post by Daveth on 2011-03-12
I assume that Ubuntu, as Windows and Macs, dislike a change of such fundamental hardware as a motherboard swap. Even if the motherboard is the "same", different motherboard versions often have different chipsets etc, so are not the "same".
I suggest a re-install is needed to get it working properly.
