### Post by rbniel on 2011-04-07
I find personally that uduntu works well on the X61 Tablet. I'm running Ubuntu 10.10 from it myself. However, there is one problem. You cannot boot from USB on the X61t. Either you need an external optical drive, or else you need to look into some of the alternative installation methods.
