### Post by bubba_169 on 2008-06-25
AFAIK Emerald just does window decorations so what you're experiencing is about right. I think its the gtk theme that deals with the menus and icons. You can find these on gnome-look.org under GTK 2.x in the menu.

Sometimes if you look on the same page where you downloaded an emerald theme, the artist will give you links to anything else they used to make the screenshots...

EDIT: Desktop background can be changed by right clicking on the desktop and selecting 'change desktop background'. Most images found on the web or even personal images can be used as your wallpaper!
