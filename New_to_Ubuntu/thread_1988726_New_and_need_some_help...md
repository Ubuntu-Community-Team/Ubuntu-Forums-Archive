### Post by fantab on 2012-05-28
It is possible that something went wrong during Installation, so reinstall. 

When you boot your Ubuntu LiveCD and you are presented a screen with an option to choose between "Try Ubuntu" and "Install Ubuntu", choose "Try Ubuntu". Play around a bit... see all the default software there is and make sure things are good to go (LiveCD will be slower compared to Hard-disk boot). Connect your internet to check everything is ok. 

A fully functional desktop will confirm that all is well with your hardware and you may proceed.

Then from Desktop or Launcher Choose to install Ubuntu. Be patient.
