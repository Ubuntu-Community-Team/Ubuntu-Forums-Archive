### Post by compmodder26 on 2011-05-24
It could just be an issue with the driver for you card.  I know back in days before 8.04, my Thinkpad T60 wireless light would stay constantly lit even though no extra traffic was coming in/out.

Open System->Administration->System Monitor.  Click on the resources tab and you should be able to see your network activity at the bottom.  That should give you an indication of just how much net activity you are having.
