### Post by ellgor on 2010-12-10
Hi,

Go to the "Avasys" site and get their Imagescan package, go through the form filling in bit (for your model, or closest to it) and when you get to the download page scroll right down until you see "Iscan or Imagescan deb" which is a completely seperate app  from the full printing package, once downloaded use gdebi to automatically install it, needs a reboot to work properly.  

Regards, Ellgor.
