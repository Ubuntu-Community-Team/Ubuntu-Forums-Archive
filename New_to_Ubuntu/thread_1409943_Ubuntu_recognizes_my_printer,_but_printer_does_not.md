### Post by bullet311 on 2010-02-18
Do you mean that the computer makes a sound thats wierd? Or the printer?
If a wireless printer, 
You need to find your printers ip address. It should be in the settings of your printer if it has a little screen. If not, using another computer you should be able to find it in (windows Xp) control panel-printers and faxes- choose your printer-properties. The ip should be in there. Then in Ubuntu go to system-admin-printers. Hit new printer and add the ip address. Choose your printer drvier from the large list and then it should be connected. 
If wired, (not completely sure on this)
Go to printers -same as above. And if your printer isnt already there add a new one and click "search for printers". If it finds it it should ask to download drivers from a huge list. After choosing and installing drivers it should connect and work.
 
Good luck man. 
I just got my wireless printer working yesterday so I am fresh with info.
Hope this helps.
