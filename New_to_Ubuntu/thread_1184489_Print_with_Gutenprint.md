### Post by ellgor on 2009-06-11
Hi,

Go to >System click on Administration and then click on Printing, a screen will be presented with details of your current printer, in the box that says Make and Model click on change, a new window will open with more details of your printer, click on the relevant model etc., and here you can change the driver option, hope this helps.

Regards, Ellgor.
