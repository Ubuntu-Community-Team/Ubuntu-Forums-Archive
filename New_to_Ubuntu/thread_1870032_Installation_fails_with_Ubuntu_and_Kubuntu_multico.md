### Post by jnorthr on 2011-10-26
you may be able to achieve a successful boot if you can choose the safe video mode at system boot time. I believe there is a choice for this just as the boot loader hands over to the o/s loader. If you can see that choice, try to take it as it uses generic drivers that may work better with your video card and present fewer problems. It is not a long term solution, but may get you past square one.
