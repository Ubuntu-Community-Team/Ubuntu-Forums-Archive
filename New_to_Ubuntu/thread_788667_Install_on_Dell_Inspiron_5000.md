Hi,

Obviously I am new to this forum and a new and extremely enthusiastic Linux convert.  After installing 8.04 on a AMD 64 processor e-Machine (one that I had been using as a file server with Windows), I have successfully installed 7.10 on a Dell Inspiron 5000 with 512k of ram and a Pentium 3 using a Linksys notebook adapter WPC54Gv2. I now have a dual boot laptop running Windows 2000  and Linux...what fun! 

After reading these posts on the Beginner Talk forum, I just wanted to let people know that this software can work almost out of the box on a wide variety of computers...obviously something that I think most of you already know.  

I am going to explore the capabilities of my two refreshed computers and want to thank all the posters on this forum who posted information on how to configure this OS.
