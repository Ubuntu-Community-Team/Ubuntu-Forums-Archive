### Post by danastasio on 2010-01-03
I dont know about gnome-do, but under the interface tab of the preferences, you can choose to always show a notification icon. this will put an icon in the notification area of your computer, and will change and blink when you get a new message. Also, if you have sound, a sound should play when getting a new message, and you should get a system notification popup in one of your screen corners.

Unless your talking about empathy.

Hope i could help.
