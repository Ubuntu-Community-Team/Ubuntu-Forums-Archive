### Post by oldsoundguy on 2009-11-15
in 9.10, put the file on your desktop (a drag and drop will do)  Right click,  select properties, in there is permissions tab .. click that and check the grant permissions and exit.  You should then be able to double click on the folder and go through the installation procedure.

Can't do that on most older versions.  There you have to get to the folder via your terminal and then set up the chmod 777 your alluded to.
