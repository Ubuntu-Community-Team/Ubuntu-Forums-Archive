### Post by Chesamo on 2010-04-02
> **nauziem said:**
> **1. Will I have to re-partition the hard drive using my Win7 Starter before installing Ubuntu?**
No. The Windows partitioner can't handle non-Windows filesystems (ie. ext3/4). The Ubuntu partitioner works just fine for this task.
> **nauziem said:**
> **2. Should I preserve Win7 Starter on a hard drive partition, or am I likely to never need it again? **(I do have other windows systems).
**EDIT:** Whoops. Reply fail. Anyway, there's really no need to keep Windows, if you're using a netbook for standard netbook things.
