### Post by Quackers on 2011-01-17
One of your /boot/grub files is missing (core.image) from the Ubuntu partition. I don't know how that has happened.
I suggest that you go to the guide below, by drs305, and follow the procedure to purge/re-install grub to your installation using the full chroot method.
The partition you need to mount is sda5 and grub should be installed to sda (NOT sda5).
Any problems come back.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1581099](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1581099)
