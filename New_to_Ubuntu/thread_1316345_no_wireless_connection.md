### Post by anewguy on 2009-11-05
First, open a terminal window:

applications/accessories/terminal

Next, type the following:

lspci <press enter>

copy/paste the information from that back here in a reply.

type:

exit <press enter>

Will close the window.


Second, check for a restricted driver:

system/administration/hardware drivers

Is there a driver listed there?  Is it enabled?
