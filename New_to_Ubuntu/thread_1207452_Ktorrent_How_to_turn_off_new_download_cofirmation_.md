### Post by oldos2er on 2009-07-15
In Ktorrent, click Settings, Configure Notifications.

---

### Post by stoeptegel on 2009-07-15
Search for something like "open torrents silently", this will disable you to choose which files to choose and so will go around the confirmation dialog.

---

### Post by powel212 on 2009-07-15
Thanks. Found it.

In settings under "scan folder" plugin put a check in the "open silently" box.

Did the trick. Nothing like automation to make ones life better.
