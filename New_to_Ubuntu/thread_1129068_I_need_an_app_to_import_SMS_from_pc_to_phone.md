### Post by hesjnet on 2009-04-18
Wammu is a phone manager for Linux. It is said to only support some functions of your phone
> Reading messages works, sending not.
[http://cihar.com/gammu/phonedb/samsung/1972/]("http://cihar.com/gammu/phonedb/samsung/1972/") The info may or may not be correct.


to install:

Open terminal and write:

```
sudo apt-get install wammu
```

Or open Synaptic and search for it there.
