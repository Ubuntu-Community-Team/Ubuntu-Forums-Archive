### Post by Novega on 2008-05-08
I don't really know if the way I do it is 100% right (cuz I'm a newbie), but it works.

I just download the icon pack to my desktop, 
right click on an empty space on the desktop,
Click on "Change Desktop Background",
Click "theme" tab,
Click on the "install" button
Direct it to the compressed file I downloaded and select ok
It will ask you if you want to apply the theme now? select yes or no

I usually select "no" and just choose to customize my current theme, the new icon set will show up under the "Icons" tab


I hope the explaination is clear enough for you, and everything works :)
