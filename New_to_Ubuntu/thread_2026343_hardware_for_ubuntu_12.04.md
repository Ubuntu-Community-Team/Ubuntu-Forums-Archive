### Post by Shadius on 2012-07-14
Ubuntu's designed to work with just about any hardware, new or old. Some general system requirements for Ubuntu 12.04 LTS is to have at least 1 GB RAM and 10 GB Hard Disk Drive space or better. Those are the minimum system requirements. There are other "flavors" of Ubuntu that work well on systems with less specifications, such as lower memory, lower Hard Disk Drive space, etc.
