### Post by LeSaint on 2009-05-30
I would recommend going along to [http://gnome-look.org](http://gnome-look.org)

They have tons of themes and icon sets there.

You might want to go into synaptic package manager as well, and look for gtk2 engines.  Some themes use different engines and wont display quite as the authors intended.  If a specific engine is not listed you can always download it and build from source.


Hope this helps
