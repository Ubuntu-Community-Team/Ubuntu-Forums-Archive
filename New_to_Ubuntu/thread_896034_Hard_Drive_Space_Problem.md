### Post by kk0sse54 on 2008-08-20
Yes it's very easy to expand a partition. Just pop in the LiveCD of Ubuntu and open up Gparted. From there shrink the partition wou would like to make smaller and then just expand the ubuntu partition to include the new space. Plus it won't delete your data and current installation, but remember always back up important data before doing partition work :)
