### Post by Eiji Takanaka on 2012-03-20
The biggest thing would be to knock wi-fi off if you can live without it. 

It increases battery life significantly, obviously it varies but it can be as much as a 50% increase in battery life. 

Reduce screen brightness, run the computer at a less intensive processor speed. (again if you can live with the performance drop).

Doing these 3 things will increase your battery life significantly dude.
