### Post by audine on 2009-02-19
I've solved my own problem, by uninstalling Ubuntu with instructions from this forum, but I can't find them now.  It involved downloading Bootit and burning it to a CD.  You can then run Bootit from the CD and remove the partitions.  Once I uninstalled Ubuntu, I re-installed in the safe graphics mode, and now I can see the desktop.  Now if I could just get the wireless working...
