### Post by steveneddy on 2009-10-22
You will need to install the universe, restricted and multiverse repos to get extra software.

System --> Administration --> Software Sources

Just tick the boxes for universe, restricted and multiverse and it will ask you to reload the info, just click yes.

THEN go to Synaptic and find your new software.

Cheers.
