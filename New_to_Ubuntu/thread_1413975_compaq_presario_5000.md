Two things come to mind.  First try verifying the MD5SUM or SHA256 of the
ISO file.  Then boot the LiveCD and use the menu selection to verify it again.

My Compaq V5201US doesn't like some brands of CDR's.  It might take a
couple of tries to find one it likes.  I just booted 9.10 and 10.04 Alpha2
and they both work, so yours should also.
