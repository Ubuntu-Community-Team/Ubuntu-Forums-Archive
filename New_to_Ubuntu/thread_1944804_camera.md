### Post by bcschmerker on 2012-03-21
After locating the appropriate [ArchWiki page](https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Webcam_Setup) via Google® Search, I found that your integrated camera is most likely a Ricoh® R5U870, which Hewlett-Packard® used on recent laptops.  Canonical® has [information on the appropriate video driver(s)](https://launchpad.net/ricoh-r5u87x) at Launchpad™.
