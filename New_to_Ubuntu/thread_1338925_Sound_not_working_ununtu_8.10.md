### Post by daniel_g_z on 2009-11-27
I had the same exact problem, some times stereo sound, some other times just one channel, and most of the time no sound.  I tried checking all possible options with QAMIX and still didn't work, my last attempt was to reinstall the whole Intrepid Ibex and got no response.  I got to the conclusion it was a complete hardware incompatibility.  I was using an ECS P4900T motherboard with on board sound. I switched to GIGABYTE motherboards and haven't had that problem again. Hope you have more luck than me.
