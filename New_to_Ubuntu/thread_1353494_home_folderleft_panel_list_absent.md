### Post by lidex on 2009-12-12
Hover your mouse cursor over left edge of nautilus window until you see the resize/dual-arrow cursor. Just click on that or right-click and drag. Its' very easy to inadvertently click it in the first place, which causes the pane to collapse. Should be a thin vertical bar just inside the border with a series of dots in two vertical columns near the center.
