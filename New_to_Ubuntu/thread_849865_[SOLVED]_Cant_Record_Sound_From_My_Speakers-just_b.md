### Post by PinkFloyd102489 on 2008-07-04
Open the Volume Control. Hit Edit and select Preferences. Check the boxes beside Capture and Mix. A new tab will pop up called Switches. Select Mix and then go to the Recording tab. Make sure there's a little picture of a microphone at the bottom. Adjust the capture level as needed.

Mix is the sound that is leaving the sound card. What you just did will pipe it into the recording program you decide to use.

### Post by tonychar on 2008-08-10
Go back into the << Recording Level >> settings, and adjust the volume to about a quarter.

The buzzing is because they are set too high, the sound is there, but it's recorded at such a high level, it totaly distorts out.
