### Post by diablo75 on 2008-11-02
The chances of it removing applications only comes up in those applications are deemed to be obsolete, and odds are that you don't use any of these programs (knowingly).  Your themes also remain; in fact you'll get a couple new ones added.

However, some have had trouble with upgrading the last few days.  It's probably best to hold off on the upgrade for at least a few days.  And when you do upgrade, use the 8.10 Alternate CD to do it.  Simply burn an alternate CD and insert it in Ubuntu.
