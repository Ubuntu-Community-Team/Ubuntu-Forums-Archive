### Post by Dynaflow on 2009-03-16
Personally, I would use a LiveCD distro that's purpose-made for retrieving information from uncooperative computers, rather than just using the Ubuntu LiveCD as a Swiss Army knife.  I'm fond of [BackTrack Linux]("http://www.remote-exploit.org/backtrack.html"); others would recommend [Knoppix]("http://www.knoppix.net/").  The instructions in the blog post above are pretty good, though.  Give them a shot, and if they fail, bring out more specialized tools.
