I have the MyBook World II, which connects via Ethernet. It works fine, but keep in mind it needs to be accessed as part of a Workgroup in a Windows network, and that could call for additional steps in Ubuntu (check out Samba). You are looking for it it via Places -> Network -> Windows Network? The workgroup does not show up? Strange - perhaps Samba can help? (other experts may tune in)


I have no experience with the WD drives that connect via USB, I use a Seagate drive that initially had some issues (timeouts and such, but looking through the forums there are scripts to solve the issues).

On the other hand, if you are looking into buying a drive, why not buy a drive that offers better Linux support than Western Digital - there are a few recommendations out there.
