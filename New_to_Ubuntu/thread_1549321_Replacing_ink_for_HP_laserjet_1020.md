### Post by Jerry N on 2010-08-09
Get the instructions at [http://h10032.www1.hp.com/ctg/Manual/c00264334.pdf](http://h10032.www1.hp.com/ctg/Manual/c00264334.pdf).  This manual tells how to replace the toner cartridge (not ink).  It also tells how to redistribute the toner in the cartridge and you might try that first to see if it helps.

Jerry

By the way, the toner cartridge is HP 12A, $77.99 directly from HP (in the US).  You can generally find these at Officemax and Office Depot, probably no cheaper than ordering direct from HP.  In my experience, buying a non-HP refill at a much lower cost  is usually a bad deal.  Pertinent instructions are on pages 49-51 in the manual.
