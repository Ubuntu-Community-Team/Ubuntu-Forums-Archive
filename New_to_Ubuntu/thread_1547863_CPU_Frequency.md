### Post by cj.surrusco on 2010-08-07
The easiest way is to add a Frequency Scaling Applet to your panel. Just right-click empty space in a panel and choose "Add to Panel", then find "CPU Frequency Monitor" and then select "Add". 

You should be able to choose the frequency from there, or use one of the modes that automatically adjusts it for you.

By the way, there is no attached image.
