### Post by coffeecat on 2011-07-14
Which version of Ubuntu are you using, and which desktop?

If you are using Unity in Ubuntu 11.04, then apps must be unmaximised to less than 75% for them to re-open unmaximised. If they are closed when sized to >75%, they will re-open maximised.
