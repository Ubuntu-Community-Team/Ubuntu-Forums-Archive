### Post by Dennis N on 2020-07-15
For terminal, right-click in the terminal window, and check the box "Show Menubar"
I can't say about Rhythmbox, since I uninstall it.

In Ubuntu, many applications don't have traditional menus available as terminal does. You will have to rely on the buttons on the top bar of the window. Those will vary too as to what is provided. However, usually there is an 'Options' menu. See screenshot.
