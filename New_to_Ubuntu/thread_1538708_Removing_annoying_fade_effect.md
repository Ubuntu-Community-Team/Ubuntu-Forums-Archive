### Post by Anuovis on 2010-07-25
*EDIT: I was a few minutes late with my reply. But I will leave it here, in case somebody else comes looking for similar answers. Glad you solved that.*

If by "visual settings" you mean the level of eye-candy in the Appearance settings (assuming you use Gnome) and various customizations of those then you need Compiz. 

Maybe there are some alternatives to it, I am not so sure. But Compiz comes by default and have tons of settings and effects. Reinstall it and see if that works. You can also add *Advanced Desktop Effects Settings*, it is a nice graphical tool to customize everything. Can be found in the *Ubuntu Software Center*.
