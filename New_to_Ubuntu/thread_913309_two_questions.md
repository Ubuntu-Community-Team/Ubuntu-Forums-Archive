### Post by drs305 on 2008-09-07
Do you have the 'workspace switcher' box down in the lower right? If you have 'window list' on your bottom (or top I suppose) you can drag the app across and drop it in the desired window switcher box.

**[COLOR="Red"]Added:[/COLOR]** I saw you said you were new so: On the bottom pane, you can right click, Add to Panel, and add "workspace switcher". If you have two or more workspaces, you can see a series of boxes, each representing one of the workspaces/desktops.
