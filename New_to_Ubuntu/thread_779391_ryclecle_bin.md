### Post by Tom--d on 2008-05-02
Go to your home folder (or any folder) and on the left hand side there will be a recycle bin. 

Also you can add one to the panel. 

Right click a panel, then Add to Panel. then find 'Deleted Items'.

### Post by monkeymoo on 2008-05-02
The above reply is perfect, but in case you deleted it from the Desktop by accident, you can get it back by right-clicking on the desktop and choosing "Create Launcher". Choose "Location" in the "Type" selection, type "Recycle Bin" (if that's what you want to call it) in the name box and "trash:" in the command box. You can choose any icon you want for it.
