### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-01-18
The other option is to create a template. 

Open Libreoffice>Set the font/size and anything else you want in the untitled document that opens (margin size, whatever)>File>Save as template>My templates>Save>Add template name.

Double click on 'My templates'>select your new template>Set as default.

That's it. ;)

claracc's approach is equally feasible, but I've never tried that way.
