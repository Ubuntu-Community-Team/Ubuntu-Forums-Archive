I will show you how to enable desktop effects in Ubuntu 10.04.:mad: This also may work with earlier versions of ubuntu.
**_[COLOR="Red"]Remember to close all programs that is currently running![/COLOR]_**
[LIST=1]
[*]Go to System, Administration, Hardware Drivers
[*]Wait for the drivers to be done searching
[*]Click on the Graphics driver that says [Recommended] and click "Activate"
[*]Wait for the driver to download and install
[*]Close all programs and restart your computer into ubuntu again.
[*]Go back to System, Preferences, Appearance
[*]Go to Visual Effects
[*]You can choose between Normal, or Extras.
[/LIST]
