### Post by Linuxisfast on 2012-10-26
Your drivers are already installed via the kernel, if you wish to keep them latest, install the xswat ppa at [https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-x-swat/+archive/x-updates](https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-x-swat/+archive/x-updates)

For your system info to show the graphic card properly,install mesa-utils package from USC.
