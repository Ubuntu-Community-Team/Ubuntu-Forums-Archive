### Post by kaldor on 2011-05-02
Just installed 9.10? It might be a bummer, but that's a pretty outdated release and support is being dropped very soon.

You have three options for an updated setup:

- 10.04; the LTS (Longterm Support) version which offers updates until mid-2013

- 10.10; the previous release, supported for about another year

- 11.04; the release that came out a few days ago.


If you don't want to bother with updates and want a solid system for a few years, use 10.04.

Edit: Which graphics card are you using? I remember some newer releases fixing issues with AMD/ATI for me.

Edit 2: Just checked, 9.10 has gone EOL (End of Life) in April and will no longer be supported with updates. 

Sorry if it seems like I'm pushing to upgrade, but it really might make a difference considering the age of the release.. a single year can make a lot of difference in the world of Open Source ;)
