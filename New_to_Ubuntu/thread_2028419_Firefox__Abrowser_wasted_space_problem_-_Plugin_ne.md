### Post by yeehi on 2012-07-18
Thanks to ajgreeny I learned that it was called the title bar. I was able to search for Title Bar extensions and found a good one to remove the title bar, [Hide Caption Title Bar Plus]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/hide-caption-titlebar-plus-sma/").

The default setting is to shrink the title bar. After configuring the extension preferences the title bar went completely.
