HP Deskjet F2280 - No Linux drivers available from HPs site.

I find ink jet printers cheap to buy but expensive to keep.  The cartridges dry out and they cost around $30 for new ones and they last roughly 500 pages.

Color laser printers on the other hand cost a little more but are much cheaper to use.  I have an HP color laserjet 2605dn and it works perfectly with Ubuntu.

Stick with HP as they are the best supported by Linux.

If you must, the HP ink jet works perfectly in Linux.
