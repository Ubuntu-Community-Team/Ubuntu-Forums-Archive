### Post by andrewski on 2008-06-11
That sounds like you have a lot going on. I can't speak to it helping with dyslexia, but [Remember the Milk]("http://www.rememberthemilk.com") is pretty awesome. You can integrate it with your desktop via [Tasque]("http://live.gnome.org/Tasque").

If you're interested in using it for organization and sub-tasks, there's an interesting article here: [http://blog.rememberthemilk.com/2008/05/guest-post-advanced-gtd-with-remember.html](http://blog.rememberthemilk.com/2008/05/guest-post-advanced-gtd-with-remember.html).
