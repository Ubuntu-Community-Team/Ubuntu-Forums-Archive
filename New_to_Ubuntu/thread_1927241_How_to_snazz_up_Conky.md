### Post by drmrgd on 2012-02-17
Welcome to the world of conky.  In order to get a nicer looking conky, you'll have to create a .conkyrc file with the stuff you want.  It's a matter of playing around with the variables and file settings to make what you want.  Although a little daunting at first, here's a list of conky [configuration settings]("http://conky.sourceforge.net/config_settings.html"), and a list of [conky variables]("http://conky.sourceforge.net/variables.html").

You might also find this thread helpful for ideas, other conky scripts that you can take and make your own, and great advice from the conky pros:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=281865](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=281865)
