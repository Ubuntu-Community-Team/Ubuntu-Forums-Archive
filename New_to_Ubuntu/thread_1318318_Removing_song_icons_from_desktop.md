OK, I haven't asked a stupid question for at least a month, so here it is:

I bought two songs from Amazon...downloaded them and imported into Rhythmbox, Amarok, and aTunes.

Now, is there a way to remove the icons for them from the desktop and still have the songs in Rbox, etc.? When I remove the desktop icons, they're gone from the music players until I restore them from Trash.

---

### Post by herbertportillo on 2009-11-07
Make a copy of the files into the folder that you want them to be in, and then delete them from the desktop. Places -> Music is a pretty easy and convenient place to put them, that's where all my music is.
