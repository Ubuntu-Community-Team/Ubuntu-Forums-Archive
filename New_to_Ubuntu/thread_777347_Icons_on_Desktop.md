### Post by Nepherte on 2008-05-01
Open up gconf-editor: alt+F2 and type gconf-editor. Navigate to apps > nautilus > desktop and check/uncheck what you want.

### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-05-01
Yeh the gnome development team (Gnome is your user interface) think that icons on desktop= bad.
there is another way to get into gconf if you wish, right click on the menu and select "edit menus"
then move your way down to system tools and click on the box next to "configuration editor"

if you want application shortcuts, no prob as you can go into your menu and right click on a application you use a lot and select "add to desktop"
