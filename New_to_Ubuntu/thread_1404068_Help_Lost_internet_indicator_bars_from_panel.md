### Post by herbertportillo on 2010-02-11
I'm guessing you removed the notification area. Right click on the panel, and click on "Add to Panel". Scroll down until you see "Notification Area" and add it to the panel. Cheers.

---

### Post by jfloydb on 2010-02-11
