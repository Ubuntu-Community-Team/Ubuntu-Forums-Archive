### Post by Archie69 on 2008-10-09
Found a resolution on another site.
Turns out all you have to do (in my case at least) was to go to Totem preferences and adjust he HUE to 0

Edit -> Preferences -> Display ->  Then in the colour balance section move the HUE to zero (all the way to the left).

Seemed to work for me.
Thanks, and hope I helped someone else
