### Post by Impavidus on 2015-02-09
Is it a special kind of mouse? Cheap mice often work best, expensive onces may be a bit problematic.

Mice (the optical ones, but I don't think any others are still around) may be a bit jumpy when sitting on a shiny surface. Try something dull. I don't use any other OS than Xubuntu, but maybe some other system you may be used to has some sort of algorithm to filter these jumps out.
