### Post by madmax75 on 2010-03-17
Right-click the upper panel with your mouse, and you should get a list of things you can add to the panel.

Find "Notification Area" and add that. Your Network Manager should now be back at the panel.

I know that particular thing is confusing - if you don't know it or accidentally stumble upon it, how can you know that your Network Manager icon comes back to the panel by adding... a "Notification Area"?

If this is not in the 100 Paper Cuts Ubuntu-annoyances-removing project already, then it is a prime candidate IMHO.
