### Post by westie457 on 2012-05-28
At the Log in screen, whichever option you choose out of the options on your computer will stay as the default.

Unity 2d works for me as well.

Also if your hardware is not capable of running Unity in 3d it sometimes drops back to Unity 2d for you. My laptop struggles with 3d so a while ago at the log in screen I selected Unity 2d and it has stayed that way.

FYI if you are not sure which you are running look at the bar across the top of the screen. If you can see what looks like a shadow underneath then you are running in 3d mode.
