### Post by karthick87 on 2010-11-01
I am not using an multimedia keyboard,using an ordinary keyboard..Is it possible to make a key to increase and decrease volume level like we have in multimedia keyboard..?

---

### Post by ubunterooster on 2010-11-01
Go to >system>preferences>keyboard shortcuts>sound >volume down> change> (ctrl)+(shift)+(-)

That is a simple way to do it

---

### Post by karthick87 on 2010-11-01
Will it increase / decrease the master volume..?

---

### Post by ubunterooster on 2010-11-01
That will decrease it

---

### Post by ubunterooster on 2010-11-01
To increase it:Go to >system>preferences>keyboard shortcuts>sound >volume up> change> (ctrl)+(shift)+(+)

Now Ctrl Shift - will decrease the volume
and Ctrl Shift + will increase it
