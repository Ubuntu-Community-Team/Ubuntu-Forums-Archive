### Post by Xiong Chiamiov on 2008-05-17
Pidgin allows communication with people across many different networks, but you have to be registered on that network to talk on it.

It's not the devs' fault; it's just the way that IM networks work, for good reason.  If I have the same name on Y! as someone else on GTalk, how is Google supposed to know who I am?

However, jabber is an open protocol, and that's what GTalk runs off of.
