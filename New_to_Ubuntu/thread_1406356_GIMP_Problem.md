### Post by SoFl W on 2010-02-13
One of the things I dislike about GIMP, you can't select the layer you want to work on in the layers menu, you have to click and drag on a visible part of the layer you want moved.

If you had a layer with only one pixel on it, and you wanted to move that one pixel, you would have to click and drag on the pixel.  So if you want to move the line, click and drag on the line.


Whoops, you said on a single layer project.  Sorry.  My suggestion is place the line on a separate layer.
