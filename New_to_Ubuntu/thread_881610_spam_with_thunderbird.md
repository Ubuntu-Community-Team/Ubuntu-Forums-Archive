### Post by philinux on 2008-08-06
Also turn off the preview pane.

View>Layout and untick message pane. This is the preview pane. Spammers will know your account is legit by using this to send back data as a graphic is downloaded.

If you are unsure about an email dont open it. Instead click View>Message Source.
