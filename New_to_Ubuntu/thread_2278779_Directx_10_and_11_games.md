### Post by QIII on 2015-05-19
DirectX is a Microsoft technology for Windows.  It is not native to Linux.

The only way to take advantage of DirectX would be to install Steam via Wine, which might not provide satisfactory performance for your particular game.

You may have a look at WineHQ's [software database]("https://appdb.winehq.org/") to see if your game has been tested and rated.

You might check the [Steam Store]("http://store.steampowered.com/") to see if there is a version of the game that has been ported to Linux/SteamOS.
