### Post by murderslastcrow on 2010-08-15
[https://launchpad.net/gdm2setup](https://launchpad.net/gdm2setup) This is a much simpler solution. Just add this PPA and install the gdm2setup package. It has a nice pretty GUI for changing this, and a few more options.

If you wanna' get your hands dirty with a TTY (Teletype Prompt), you can even change the theme used on the login screen. Hopefully this becomes as simple to modify as the old GDM in the near future- I didn't really find any issues with the prior method, while I hope this method becomes more customizable and pretty. We had some pretty flashy login screens not too long ago.
