### Post by HotShotDJ on 2009-07-31
I think what you are looking for is some form of OCR (optical character recognition) software.  There are several options available, and I believe you can get it to work with xsane.  As far as I can see, they all provide the results in simple text files, which you can then open and edit with OpenOffice or whatever other word processor you choose.  Most of them are available through Synaptic.  Google "ocr linux" or "ocr ubuntu" and see what can find that might suite you.
