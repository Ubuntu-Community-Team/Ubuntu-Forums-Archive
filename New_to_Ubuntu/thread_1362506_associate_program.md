### Post by mgranet on 2009-12-23
It's a fairly simple procedure, once you know where to look. First, right click on the file type you wish to change the association for. In this case, you would right click on a .torrent. From there, go to Properties. Then, click on the tool icon. From there, you can see the list of apps which are associated with the program. You can move whichever program you want to the top of the list to make it the default, or you can add a different program. You can also do this in System Settings-->Advanced tab-->File Associations.
