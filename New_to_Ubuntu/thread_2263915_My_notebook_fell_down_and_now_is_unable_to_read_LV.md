### Post by andrew196 on 2015-02-06
I'm new to Linux as an OS, but I do have extensive experience with computer hardware. Unfortionately it sounds like your hard drive was damaged, that's the down side to disk drives. If the drive was damaged physically by harsh fall that shook everything up you're probabaly SOL when it comes to data recovery. This happened to me with one of my data drives after I dropped my backpack once. Sucks :( 
My advice would be to invest in a solid state hard drive, they're much faster and it's impossible for this to happen with a SSD. (They have no moving parts)
