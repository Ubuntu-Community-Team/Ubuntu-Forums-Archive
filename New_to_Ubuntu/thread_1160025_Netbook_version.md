### Post by bennachie on 2009-05-15
Standard Ubuntu 9.04 runs quickly and efficiently on my eeePC 4G, and actually takes up less space than netbook remix. If your needs are really basic, you could save a little space by dropping OpenOffice and installing Abiword and Gnumeric instead. Desktop effects are a no-no, though; apart from anything else, they prevent you from using Click-Alt-Drag to navigate windows (mostly preference panes) that don't respect the boundaries of the small screen.
