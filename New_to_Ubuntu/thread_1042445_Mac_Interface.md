### Post by tsali on 2009-01-17
Go to Applications > Add/Remove Programs

Then, search for "avant window navigator", then select and apply. If you like your dock at the bottom as you say, this works beautifully.

### Post by ChildOfMana on 2009-01-17
You can also look [here]("http://linuxowns.wordpress.com/2008/05/08/the-best-and-worst-docks-for-ubuntu/") for a comparison of all the different Docks available for Linux.

Both AWN (as mentioned by tsali) and Cairo Dock are heavily recommended.

Personally I use Cairo Dock as I find it easier to configure but I've used AWN in the past too. Both suited my needs.

Hope this helps :)
