### Post by jflaker on 2010-01-19
Click on PLACES>>HOME FOLDER
Press CTL-H to expose the hidden folders and files and rename .gconf folder to like .gconfbak....

logoff and log back in and you should have the default desktop back to "out of the box" settings.
