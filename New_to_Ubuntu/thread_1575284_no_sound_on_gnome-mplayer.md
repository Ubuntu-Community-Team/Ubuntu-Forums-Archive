### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-09-15
Let's start with the obvious!  Have you checked the volume control icon in gnome-mplayer itself, or in **pulseaudio-volume-control** "Output" tab whilst gnome-mplayer is playing something, rather than just the volume control icon in the panel?

They are not the same thing, and can be set differently, one being muted without affecting the other, which may be full volume, and this can be a bit confusing if you just look at the panel volume icon.
