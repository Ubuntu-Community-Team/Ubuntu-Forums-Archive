### Post by waspbr on 2009-05-07
just as a quick temporary patch, you may want to enable your compositor in metacity. 


press alt+f2, then type
 ```
gconf-editor
```

go to Apps>metacity>General and tick the box that says compositing manager, you should at least be able to use cairo dock again.
