### Post by dFlyer on 2011-06-21
If you have the arrows on the side of the icon you click, that is both left and right arrow, it means the program is open and running. If you have just the left arrow, it means the program is running but closed to the unity bar, which would have been the lower panel in gnome. Just click it to open it again.
