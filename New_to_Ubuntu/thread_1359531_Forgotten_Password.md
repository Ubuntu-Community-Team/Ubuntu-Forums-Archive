### Post by tom.swartz07 on 2009-12-19
> **Timoteo32 said:**
> whoa.  I understood almost none of that.  What the hell is the grub prompt?  What's a passwordless shell? And I still have no clue in all that where to find my password.

Grub is the black and white prompt that flashes on your screen when you turn on your computer- just after your BIOS (the screen that flashes who made your computer)
it goes: 
Power Button
Bios loader
Grub bootloader
Ubuntu loads

the passwordless shell is the command-line-looking input.

unfortunately, you need to use some techie-nerd-like commands to recover your password, but its very do-able. If this was a windows box, you would probably be better off re-installing the whole OS.

Try using the Psychocats post; it has pictures to help you along.
