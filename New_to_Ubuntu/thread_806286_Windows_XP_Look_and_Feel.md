### Post by HotShotDJ on 2008-05-24
> **dalezjc said:**
> Is there an Ubuntu distro that emulates the look and feel of Windows XP?  I'm trying to get my Mom and Dad onto Ubuntu, but they're so used to XP, it's almost impossible.There are ways to theme Ubuntu to look like XP.  Here is the first one that popped up on a google search for "XP Look Gnome": [http://nithinkamath.info/archives/2006/12/make-windows-xp-desktop-look-like-gnome/](http://nithinkamath.info/archives/2006/12/make-windows-xp-desktop-look-like-gnome/)

Having answered your question, please allow me to suggest that you NOT do that. :)  If you make it look like XP for Mom and Dad, then Mom and Dad will inevitable expect it to be like XP in other ways besides just the look and feel.  This could lead to some unexpected consequences.... "Why can't I install ***.exe on this computer?"  and such things.

For my mother, I made sure things looked and acted VERY different from what she was used to.  I even set it for single-click to launch, rather than double-click.  This way, she understood that she was NOT working with Windows and she did not expect it to behave like Windows.  While she complained bitterly for the first few days, she got used to it and now works with Ubuntu easily.
