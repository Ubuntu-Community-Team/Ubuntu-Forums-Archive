### Post by Sarah L on 2008-06-21
Check your CD/DVD drives to see if you left a disc in there.
You can click on the Audio CD icon to see if there are any files inside.

The icon generally means that there is a CD or an .iso mounted. If you find it annoying, you should be able to unmount it by right-clicking and selecting eject or unmount.
