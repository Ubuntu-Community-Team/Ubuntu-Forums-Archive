### Post by ukripper on 2009-11-26
> **Alex82 said:**
> The LED was on but it had turned off enabled? I enabled it, restarted and now it is running at 1280x800 with a 50hz refresh rate and an unknown output?

When you upgraded i guess your nvidia drivers were not loaded properly therefore were messing up your xorg.conf. 

As you want to learn I'd explain how things were fixed - when you followed steps of reconfiguring xorg, it generated new xorg for you and then you followed steps of enabling nvidia drivers which sorted out resolution issues for you. Now only thing is left which is your refresh rate as you are getting 50hz not 60hz. So to fix that you need to add Horizontalsync and verticalrefresh follwo steps below:

As i already asked you to post output of your cvt which gave us your horizontal and vertical refresh rate.

Now do below

```
sudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf
```
Now find Section "Monitor" and add below lines in bold under identifier:

> Section "Monitor"
Identifier "Configured Monitor"
[B]HorizSync 47.0 - 50.0
VertRefresh 59.0 - 60.0[/B]
save and exit.

reboot and see if it makes any difference and check if refresh rate is set to 60hz now.
