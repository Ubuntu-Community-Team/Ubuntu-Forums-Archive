### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-07-17
Please notice that the linked driver thread contains the statement > This method PROBABLY WILL NOT WORK for HD 5xxx or earlier cards. 

You have a mix of HD 5x and HD 6x video chips -- so it's likely you're not going to be able to get Linux drivers that work other than the default Radeon open-source drivers.
