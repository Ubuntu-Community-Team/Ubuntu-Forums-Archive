### Post by billgoldberg on 2008-06-07
PulseAudio has problems playing sounds from two different sources.

So if you are wachting a youtube video and your audioplayer is still open, it won't play the sound and visa versa.

You can [switch to ALSA]("http://linuxowns.wordpress.com/2008/05/07/hardy-sound-problems-2/") to avoid this problem.

You could also install "libflashsupport" instead but that **could** make flash and firefox less stable.
