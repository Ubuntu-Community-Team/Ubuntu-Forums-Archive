### Post by duvalx7 on 2009-09-21
No, you will have to use Banshee, it is one of the best media players for Linux now, there are others that can display album art but I found banshee to be the best, has good support for Ogg Vorbis too. you just click on the now playing icon in the side pane and it will display album art in a nice way for you, i actually prefer this interface to Windows Media Player, Quod Libet will display album art also but seem to have a problem displaying artwork from Ogg Vorbis.
