### Post by GreenWhite on 2012-03-29
Yes, the new flash 11.2 update for Firefox 11 in 10.04 killed it.  You can revert to an older release via Synaptics. Search for flash, then  from the menubar select Package/Force Version and choose from there.
  
       I know its not ideal but at least it will work while waiting for a fix.
