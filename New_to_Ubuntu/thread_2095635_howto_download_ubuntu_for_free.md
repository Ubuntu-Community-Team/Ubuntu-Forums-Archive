### Post by lisati on 2012-12-18
*cough*
/me spells out step by step for getting a free copy of recent desktop versions, while safely ignoring request for money:
[LIST=1]
[*]Go to [http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop)
[*]Choose the version you want, 32 bit or 64 bit, 12.04 or 12.10
[*]Click on the "Get Ubuntu xx.xx"
[*]Go down the page, and click on "Not now, take me to the download"
[*]Choose "Save to disk"
[*]Wait patiently for the download to complete, visiting other web pages if you want.
[/LIST]
