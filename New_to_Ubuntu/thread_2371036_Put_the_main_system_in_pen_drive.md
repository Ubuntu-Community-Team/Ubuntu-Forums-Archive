### Post by ian-weisser on 2017-09-10
> **Edgar_Oliveira said:**
> The velocity will be enough to has a good and fluid system?

This has been fully discussed in this forum, and tried by several users.
You need a fast USB connection, preferably USB3. For many flavors of Ubuntu, USB2 is too slow.
A standard install of Ubuntu will wear out the USB stick, the rate of wear varies. So you will still need backups.
