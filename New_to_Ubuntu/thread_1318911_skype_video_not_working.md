### Post by laidback on 2009-11-08
Try this:-

Botton left of your Skype contacts window is the Skype blue "S" press it and choose "Options". From options choose Video devices and see whether you have the top 2 boxes clicked (Enable my Skype video and Start my video...). Try the test screen. Also check that the select Webcam drop down has the correct device selected.

I'm using 9.04 with a Logitec 3000 webcam, no problems.
