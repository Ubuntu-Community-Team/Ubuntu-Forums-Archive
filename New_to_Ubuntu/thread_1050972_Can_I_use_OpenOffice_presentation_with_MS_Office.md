### Post by nlz on 2009-01-26
if you finished your presentation, go to file > save as

Then save as powerpoint presentation. Then it will work.

95/100 cases everything is fine, some templates, pictures, lay-out things can turn out a bit different. So go through your slides before you do your presentation.

Good luck!
