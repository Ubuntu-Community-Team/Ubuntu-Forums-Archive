### Post by twisted_steel on 2008-10-12
I'd take a look at SynCE.  Before you start playing around with the sync settings, I'd back it up in Windows first.  When I was playing around with the SVN development version, I managed to clear out all the contacts on my phone (BlackJack II with WM 6).  They were all Outlook contacts rather than the standard type that stays on the SIM card.  I did back them up in Evolution previously, so I did manage to get everyone back on there.  I haven't tried it recently with WM 6.1, but I think the program is also supposed to work with that.  I do recall reading that the [Conduit Project]("http://www.conduit-project.org/") was working on adding in support for a graphical sync using SynCE as a backend.

Wiki page for SynCE on Ubuntu: [http://www.synce.org/moin/SynceWithUbuntu](http://www.synce.org/moin/SynceWithUbuntu)
Overview of Conduit integration (still in development): [http://blogs.gnome.org/johncarr/2008/07/25/syncing-my-phone-its-actually-easy/](http://blogs.gnome.org/johncarr/2008/07/25/syncing-my-phone-its-actually-easy/)
