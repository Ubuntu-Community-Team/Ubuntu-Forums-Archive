### Post by Orion8 on 2009-02-16
As far as Firefox goes, I'd export and then re-import her bookmarks.  I am not sure where the cookies hide, but if you download from Firefox the "Password Exporter" extension you should be able to export (and the upon installing the extension in Ubuntu) re-import most of the passwords.  This worked really well when I moved from my old Mac to an Ubuntu system.

I have less experience with Thunderbird but I guess I'd try to export any folders and re-import them in the new OS.  Maybe there is some way to transfer a hidden folder but this seems safer to me.

Alternately, if you have an IMAP account available you could just move her saved mail folders up to the mail server and then sync to the new machine.
