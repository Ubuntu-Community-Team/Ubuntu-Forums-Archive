### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-05-05
Hi,
Have you already tried doing this?

Click on the indicator applet, then click on your name (or where the picture was next to it).

Your "About <You>" window should now open (it's your contact info). 

Next, click on the empty picture box in the upper left corner (right next to your name). A window to browse for images will open. This is how we select another picture. 

If it doesn't open up to the correct folder location, the default location of those little images is at: "File System" /usr/share/pixmaps/faces. Click on any of those images, then click on the "open" button.

Hope this helps.
