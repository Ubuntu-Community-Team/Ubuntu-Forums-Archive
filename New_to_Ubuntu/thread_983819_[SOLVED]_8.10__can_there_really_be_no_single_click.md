### Post by ciscosurfer on 2008-11-16
If I'm understading you correctly, you *can* change how items are opened (Single click vs Double click).

Go to **Places** > **Home Folder** (or any nautilus window) > Click **Edit** > Choose **Preferences** > Select the **Behavior** Tab > Under ***Behavior***, choose **Single click to open items**.

### Post by Elfy on 2008-11-16
Impossible to say whether you're looking in the wrong place as you don't say. 

Open Nautilus - Preferences - Behaviour, you can change mouse behaviour there to open with single click.
