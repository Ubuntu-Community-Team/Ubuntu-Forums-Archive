### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-08-08
You're sending out mixed signals: You say you want a program to make Flash movies, but the name of your message is "Need Flash Player". So don't be surprised if people give you a link to Flash Player.

I don't believe there's anything out there for Linux to create Flash movies of any complexity. Openoffice.org 3 can export presentations to Flash but this is rather basic. Your best bet would be to buy a Wine-supported version of Adobe Flash and run it in Ubuntu.
