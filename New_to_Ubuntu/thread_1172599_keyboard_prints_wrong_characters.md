### Post by Dngrsone on 2009-05-29
You are running a laptop and Vista thinks you are in numbers mode.  There is going to be a special function key (mine is the second one in on the bottom left row for my HP, it's labeled 'fn' and the text is circled.) and there will be a corresponding 'num lk', 'num lock', or somesuch key that shoyuld take you out of this mode.

It may be you accidentally enabled this once and Vista is remembering it, or it could just be a hiccup.
