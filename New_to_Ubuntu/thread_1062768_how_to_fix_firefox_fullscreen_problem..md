### Post by Joshua Wells on 2009-02-07
ok, so you may have had that firefox issue where you start firefox, and it is full screen, but it thinks it isn't.
i know i have.
to temporarily fix that problem, you would normally double tap f11.
this only solves the problem temporarily.
in fact, it is more of a walkaround.
to fix the problem,
1. Start Firefox.
2. double tap f11.
3. press the unmaximise/restore button.
4. move the window, and resize it to about just under your screen size.
5. restart firefox
6. it should be fixed!
et voila! my second real post, and i hope that this works for you!
