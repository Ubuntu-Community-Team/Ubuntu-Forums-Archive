---

### Post by I&#9829;HTML5 on 2011-02-06
Open Evolution (Applications | Internet | Evolution Mail), go into preferences (Edit | Preferences), select the account, click the "Edit" button, click the "Receiving Email" tab, and change the server. If it was SMTP server you misspelled, follow the above directions, but click the "Sending Email" tab.
