### Post by nhasian on 2008-11-28
on your dell mini running windows, you need to share whatever folder you like with either read or read/write previliages.

on your Dell Lattitude running Linux you'll need to install samba to be able to share folders to the other pcs.

same thing for your Mac running OSX - you'll need samba installed so you can see the other shares and share folders of its own.

not sure how your wireless hard disk works but i imagine you need to plug it into a computer via an ethernet cable and configure it for your wireless network before you can access it.
