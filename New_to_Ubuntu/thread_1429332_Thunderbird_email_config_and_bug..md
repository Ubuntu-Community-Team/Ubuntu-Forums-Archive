### Post by iMisspell on 2010-03-13
To make Google Chrome your default, open the browser, click the 'wrench' to the far upper-right of the window (just under the X to close the program) then go to 'Options' and under the 'Basic' tab, you can click the "Make Chrome Default" button at the bottom.

Cant help with the double emails.
