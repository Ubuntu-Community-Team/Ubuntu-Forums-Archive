This may be as good a place as any to mention something again:

You hear a lot about "hackers" and the havoc they create with breaking into computers, stealing information, creating viruses and trojans, etc..  But it is really the incorrect use of the term "hacker".  I come from the very old (read earliest days) of any type of home computer, and a hacker has always been someone who can take the hardware or software and make it better or do something it wasn't created to do.  NOT something bad - something good.  The site you pointed to is more of a hacking thread, and it looks great to me.

The people who do all of the bad things are not hackers, but a lot of them can correctly be called criminals.

I don't see any malicious commands mentioned in the site you pointed to.
