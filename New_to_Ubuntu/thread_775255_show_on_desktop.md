**Desktop Icons 101**

Right click on the desktop and choose: Create Launcher:

Name: MousePad
Comment: Your choice here.
Command: click on the Icon on the right... start typing: mouse ... it will take you to mousepad.  Select that.

Should see:
Command: /usr/bin/mousepad

Next where it says [No Icon] <--- click on that and select an Icon.

Options: [x] Use startup notification

Putting one on the panel is very similar.
Hope this helps
Bruce
