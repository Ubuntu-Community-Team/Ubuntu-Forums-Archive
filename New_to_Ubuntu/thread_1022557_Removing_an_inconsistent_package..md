You did not state whether you are trying to install the Brother printer package from System/Administration/Synaptics or not. I find several packages for Brother printers. If you are trying to load something from another source then that could be the issue you are dealing with. I find no match for "hl1440lpr" when searching in Synaptic Package Manager.

The thanks to the left are there when someone provides a solution that helps the person who is asking a question. If the solution solves the problem, as a courtesy, you thank them. There is an icon to the far lower right that you can click on that will register your thanks to whoever has helped you solve your problem. You can also, under thread tools, mark the thread as solved, so that someone else can benefit from your problem, when they may be experiencing a similar issue.
