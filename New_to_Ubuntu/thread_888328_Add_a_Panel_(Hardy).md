Right-click on your top panel and select "New Panel". New panel will appear and you have to drag the panel to the bottom of the screen. Right-click on your new bottom panel and select "Add to Panel", select "Show Desktop" and click on Add. You're done.

### Post by jmacdallas on 2008-08-13
If you still have the panel at the top, you can just right click that and say *new panel*.

Then drag the new panel back down to the bottom. 

To add icons and menu bars to the panel, right click the panel and say *add to panel*
