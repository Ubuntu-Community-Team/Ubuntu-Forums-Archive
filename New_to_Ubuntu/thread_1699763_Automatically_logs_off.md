### Post by robsoles on 2011-03-04
Laptop or Desktop?

If it's a Desktop then pop off a cover and have a good look at the larger electrolytic capacitors on the motherboard and any peripheral cards - if any of the caps (more specifically on the motherboard) are bulging in any way whatsoever then it is the probable cause of your problem.

### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-03-04
Have you noticed any pattern?

I have noticed this before when there is a problem with the graphics driver or graphics card. Try turning off special effects (System > Preferences > Appearance > Visual Effects > None) and see whether that makes a difference.
