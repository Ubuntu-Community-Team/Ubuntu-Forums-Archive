### Post by vasa1 on 2012-10-01
You could also consider choosing another font for your terminal that would help distinguish between "1" and "l".
For example:
[FONT="Courier New"]1 l[/FONT] >>> Courier New
[FONT="Arial"]1 l[/FONT] >>> Arial (and other proportional fonts are not really recommended for terminals)

If you look at Edit, Preferences in your Terminal, you should see an option to set the font of your liking.
