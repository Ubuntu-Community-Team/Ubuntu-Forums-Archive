### Post by Edward_Fish on 2016-03-16
Of course, it depends on what you use your computer for. If you just watch YouTube and play online games, you might get away with an 8GB drive.
If you store some music/pictures, or fancy applications, you'll need 16GB.
If you store lots of music/thousands of pictures/videos then 32GB.
If you download operating system ISOs like me, you might even need 64GB.
If it were me, I'd use a 32GB drive, or put 2 16GB drives in RAID 0 for extra speed.
