### Post by SeijiSensei on 2013-08-13
The method you describe uses "[Bayesian]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bayesian_spam_filtering")" filtering.  Not all mail services offer that, and as a mail administrator, I can report that it sometimes causes more problems than it solves.  Once a couple of spams are tagged as non-spam in a Bayesian filtering setup, future messages that look like the mistagged ones will start to be passed through as non-spam.
