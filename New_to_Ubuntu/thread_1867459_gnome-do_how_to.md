### Post by maskiepop on 2011-10-23
I have installed xubuntu/ubuntu 11.10 recently. It has an app called gnome-do which seems quite useful. There is a small triangular icon at the top right hand corner of the window that brings up a menu:
[LIST]
[*]About Do
[*]Preferences
[*]Donate
[*]Quit
[/LIST]

None of the options works. I mean I select an item, and nothing happens. What am I doing wrong?

### Post by hormo on 2011-10-24
I had the same problem. The menu is somehow in the background and it can't be clicked on the available options.

BUT: I was able to access the menu by using the arrow keys (down). I first click the triangle and then the arrow key (down), choose my option and hit Enter.
