So. . .

If Natty uses GNOME 2, but also can support GNOME 3 

Does that mean that Natty has both the GTK 2 toolkit AND GTK 3 toolkit (since GNOME 2 uses GTK 2, right?) ?

---

### Post by mcduck on 2012-03-28
No, it uses GTK2 and Gnome 2. Installing Gnome 3 will remove Gnome 2 so you can only have one or the other, not both.

(GTK2 and GTK3, on the other hand, can live together on the same system.)
