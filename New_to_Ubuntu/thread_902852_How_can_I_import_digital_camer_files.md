### Post by silkstone on 2008-08-27
I know this doesn't really address the problem, but I would strongly recommend using a USB card reader rather than connecting the camera.

Several reasons - transfer is usually quicker, you don't need to find USB cables and hook things up, it doesn't drain your camera battery, and you don't have to worry about whether your camera is recognised. :)

A Hama 35-in-1 reader from Amazon cost less than £4.00, and saves a lot of trouble - just slot the card into it and you're away.
