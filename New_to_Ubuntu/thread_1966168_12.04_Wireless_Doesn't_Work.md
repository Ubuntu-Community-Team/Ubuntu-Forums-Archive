### Post by kipjones on 2012-04-26
Before having wireless trouble, I had to turn acpi=off to regain control of my laptop's brightness controls.

So, it turns out that booting the LiveCD with acpi=off somehow locks the wireless hardware into airplane mode.  Editing the boot parameters with the oft-cited workaround

acpi_osi=Linux acpi_backlight=vendor

allows me to boot normally into 12.04.  Looking forward to trying this out!
