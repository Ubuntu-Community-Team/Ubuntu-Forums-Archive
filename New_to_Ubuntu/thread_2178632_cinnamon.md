### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-10-04
If you have more than one interface installed, you switch between them when you log in. 

The system remembers the interface in use when you reboot. So, if you selected "automatic login" during the install, it will boot into the interface previously in use.

To switch to Cinnamon, then, select "log out" rather than restart. At the log-in screen, you will notice a small round icon at the upper right of the password field.  Clicking that will display icons for every interface installed on the system.  Select the Cinnamon icon, enter your password, and enjoy.
