### Post by bug67 on 2015-02-24
Google Image search an image that is the size of both monitors.  For example, both my monitors are 1920 X 1200.  Together, they would be *3840* X 1200.  So, if I google image search "3840 X 1200 wallpaper, Bam!  All kinds of stuff.

Now, I'm not using vanilla Ubuntu but, it should be a similar setting scenario.  In your desktop settings, choose your newly double wide image and look for a setting or a check box that says "span."  Let us know how you get on.  :D
