### Post by ibuclaw on 2009-03-31
In GIMP, you go to **Image->Scale Image**.

Or if you prefer a more batch way of doing it. Then go to **System->Administration->Synaptic Package Manager** and do a quick search for 
**nautilus-image-converter**. You may need to logout/login for the changes to take effect, but what that does for you is give you a new option to resize/rotate images when you select+right click on them.
