> Hi having problems with Shiretoko not accepting cookies. 
I have looked under preferences/ privacy/and cannot see anyway 
of enabling them.

Anybody got any suggestions?

Click on the Tools-menu in Shiretoko
Click on the Options... item in the menu - a new window opens
Click on the Privacy selection in the left part of the window. (See image below)
Expand the Cookies section.
Check the Enable cookies and Accept cookies normally checkboxes.
Save changes by clicking Ok and go to one of the configuration pages again
