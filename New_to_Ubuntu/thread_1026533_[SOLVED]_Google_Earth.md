### Post by jboy012000 on 2009-01-01
FOUND THE FIX - thanks to TV Truck Chick, its all to do with the Appearence settings, goto SYSTEM > PREFERENCES > APPEARENCE > CLICK THE VISUAL EFFECTS TAB > THEN SELECT "NONE"

The above has worked for me, however you could try either NONE or NORMAL and see which one works for you.

Restart Google Earth and it should work with no problems.
