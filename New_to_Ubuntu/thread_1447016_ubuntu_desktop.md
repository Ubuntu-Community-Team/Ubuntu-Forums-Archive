### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2010-04-04
The hot keys to change desktops I believe are installed disabled by default.  You must enable them System->Preferences->Keyboard Shortcuts.  You realize that you can add more desktops by right clicking the desktop image in the bottom panel and then go to Preferences.
