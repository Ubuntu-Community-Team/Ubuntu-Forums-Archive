### Post by Redmage913 on 2009-11-29
If your ultimate intention is to install Ubuntu on a netbook, I'd suggest starting over.

Download the ISO image of Ubuntu, whatever flavor you wish.  If you're doing this on a Windows computer, Download Unetbootin from:

[http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)

Run unetbootin, choose the ISO install, choose your ISO CD image, and select your thumbdrive from the 'install to' option.  You can also choose whether or not you want persistent mode.

This should turn your thumbdrive into a Live CD!  Make sure that your netbook starts up from the USB device (go into boot options or the BIOS on your netbook to accomplish this).

Good luck, and reply back if anything I said was unclear or entirely wrong (i'm not on my windows computer so I'm doing this from memory....)
