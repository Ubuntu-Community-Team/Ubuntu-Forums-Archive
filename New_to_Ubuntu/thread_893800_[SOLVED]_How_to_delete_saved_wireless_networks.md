### Post by seamustry on 2008-08-18
I have a home wi-fi network with one name and I recently had a problem with it so I connected to a different network in my area. 

The problem is that now Ubuntu always tries to connect to the other network and I have to manually select my own home network.

How do I make Ubuntu forget about the other network and automatically connect to my home network?

---

### Post by Joeb454 on 2008-08-18
Right click the wireless icon in the top right of your screen, and choose "Edit wireless networks"

You can delete them from there :)
