### Post by Paddy Landau on 2009-08-04
Near the top of the page, you'll see a long bar with "User CP", "Forum Help", etc.

Click on "Search", and select either Find All Your Threads or Find All Your Posts.

You can find extra tools in your [User CP]("http://ubuntuforums.org/usercp.php").

### Post by halitech on 2009-08-04
in the upper left you should see a button that says User CP, click on that and any threads with new replies will show up. To check old threads, click on show all threads (lower right I think)

### Post by snakeman21 on 2009-08-04
Visit the thread you want to keep track of, and near the top is a link called "thread tools", click it and click "subscribe to this thread."  On the page it brings you to, select "instant notification by email" and confirm.  When someone replies to the thread, you will receive and email.

### Post by drs305 on 2009-08-04
Once subscribed, you can quickly get to your subscribed posts by clicking the "Quick Links" button at the top right and then "Subscribed Threads". Your posts should then be among those displayed.
