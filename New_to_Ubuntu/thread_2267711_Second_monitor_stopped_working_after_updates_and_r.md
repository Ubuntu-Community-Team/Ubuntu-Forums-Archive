### Post by Mark Phelps on 2015-03-03
What is the video chip you're using? Asking because older AMD video chips (HD 2x/3x/4x-series) were only supported with the fglrx driver up through Ubuntu 12.04.1.  When Ubuntu updates beyond that, the driver quits working.
