### Post by sirgogo on 2009-05-31
Are you sure you have all your plug-in packs installed?

Add New Programs --> Search for mp3, or maybe "mplayer", or wav

The plug in packs should be "the good", "the bad", and "the ugly".

If thats not the issue, maybe your mixer didn't install properly. Go to the Synaptic package manager module in Tools and look for "ALSA mixer" and such. Also, you may want to try ensuring your sound card is supported by Ubuntu. It may have worked on your previous installation, but it may have defaulted to a generic version of the sound driver.
