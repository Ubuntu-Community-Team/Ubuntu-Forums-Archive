### Post by Impavidus on 2013-06-19
Either your browser window is too narrow or the website is too wide. Unfortunately, you cannot make your browser wider than the screen minus the width of the launcher (when not hidden) and websites can and often do request a minimum width (in pixels), which may be too wide for your browser. It's basically bad website design, because it doesn't take your 1024×768 screen into account.

On the other hand, 1024×768 is an uncommon resolution for laptops. Usually they are widescreens. If your's is too, the screen isn't set to the correct resolution. Try to change that. If you can't, there may be a problem with your video driver.
