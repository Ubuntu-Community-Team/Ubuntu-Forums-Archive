### Post by asmoore82 on 2009-03-01
Above all else, I would suggest a decent nVidia card.
[P.S. - looks like you've already got that covered]

I would assume that Age of Emp. 3 is going to be the only spot of extreme difficulty -
due to reliance on Direct-X rendering.

The really cool thing is that if you can get everything set up to meet your needs,
the ".wine" folder is complete portable between systems.
I myself have a backup archive of a ".wine" with Counter Strike installed that has been with me across 5 desktops and 2 laptops.
And when the game has any stability/crashing issues whatsoever(3rd party bot mods),
I can just re-unpack the archive and its back to a pristine state.
