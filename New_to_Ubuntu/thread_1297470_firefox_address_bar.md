### Post by fooman on 2009-10-21
you can easily make it behave as it does in windows (single click in address bar to highlight entire address...)

open firefox,  type "about:config" (no quotes) in address bar

click on "i'll be careful, i promise"

copy/paste the following into the filter bar:
     
```
browser.urlbar.clickSelectsAll 
```change false to true....restart firefox.

hope that helps.
