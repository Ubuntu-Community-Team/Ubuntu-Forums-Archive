### Post by houseworkshy on 2010-01-04
Blender is superb but really aimed at 3d rendered work.  Gimp would work and with it's animation plugin, GAP, could preview for you too.  It takes a while to find one's way around.  For simple stuff I think pencil may be your best bet. Its' paint program is very basic but as it can import that doesn't matter.  It is designed for 2D animation and very easy to use, has all you need, onion skin, player, layers, various movement tools.  It was in the repositiories but doesn't seem to be there now.  Should be on getdeb.net anyway.  Ktoon and stopmotion are supposed to be good too but as I haven't really used them I couldn't say.
