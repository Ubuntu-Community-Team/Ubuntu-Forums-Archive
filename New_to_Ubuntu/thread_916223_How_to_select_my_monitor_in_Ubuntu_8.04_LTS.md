### Post by roger_1960 on 2008-09-10
Hi

Open a terminal window by going "applications - accessories - terminal"

then enter

sudo displayconfig-gtk

then enter your password (as requested - note that it wont appear)

You then have a gui window.  Select your own monitor and then resolution and it should work.  If its not there, select "generic" and then the resolution of your monitor.
