### Post by Claus7 on 2012-11-23
Hello,

and... welcome!

Concerning your problem I do not think that you need a lot of help. Many errors might be the result of a wrong installation procedure. Are you sure that you have wiped out everything that had to do with windows8?

I would advice you:
1) to burn iso to a dvd to the slowest possible speed (to ensure that dvd is working ok)
2) to download the version of ubuntu corresponding to your hardware architecture (32 bit or 64 bit)
3) boot from dvd drive and since you do not need anything, wipe out everything.

which guide did you follow to install ubuntu? I guess that you have confused the installations side by side and independent one. I would advice you to see this:
[http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/install-desktop-latest](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/install-desktop-latest)

and this in case you have some more troubles:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation)

Also, do not forget to post exactly which errors you got and which procedure you followed. That way, someone with same problems before, will be able to help you.

Regards!
