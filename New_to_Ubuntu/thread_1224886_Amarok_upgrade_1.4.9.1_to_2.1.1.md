### Post by gunksta on 2009-07-27
The easiest way to use a newer version of Amarok is to upgrade to 9.04 or wait for 9.10 to get a little stronger and update to it. Alpha 3 for 9.10 was released recently. Give it a month and you could consider jumping straight up to that. Amarok 2.x depends on KDE4 and although I believe 8.10 does have some KDE4 stuff in it, you don't want to use it. Trust me. The 9.04 release has much nicer KDE4 stuff.
