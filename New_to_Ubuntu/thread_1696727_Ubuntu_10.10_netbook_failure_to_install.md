### Post by snowpine on 2011-02-27
It sounds like the GRUB2 bootloader is not properly installed.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling) GRUB 2

Also check your BIOS settings to make sure the hard drive to which you installed is the first boot option.
