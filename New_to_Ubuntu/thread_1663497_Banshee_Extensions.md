### Post by Kasami on 2011-01-09
What do you mean by auto import, Banshee looks at your "Music" folder and should update it's library on startup everytime there is a change.

To stop video acting in the same way Right click on video in the left pane and go to preferences then choose a folder that won't have videos in it for your source folder.
