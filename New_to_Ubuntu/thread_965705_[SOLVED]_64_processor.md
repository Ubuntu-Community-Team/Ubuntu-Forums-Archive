### Post by aeiah on 2008-10-31
the performance increase isnt that great. if you're reading with heavy duty video or audio encoding/decoding you may find the 64bit version a bit quicker, because certain programs have been optimised to take advantage of 64bit numbercrunching stuff, but the difference wont be massive. it used to be the case that the 64bit version was less stable than the 32bit, with some problems arising from flash etc but i think this has been pretty much solved. in short, perhaps try out the 64bit version next time, but there's no point wasting time reinstalling because the difference is minimal.
