### Post by deadflowr on 2013-01-03
How do you plan on running it?
Cd or USB?
If Cd then burn it with brasero, for usb read this maybe:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LiveUsbPendrivePersistent]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LiveUsbPendrivePersistent")

or

[http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/burn-a-dvd-on-ubuntu]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/burn-a-dvd-on-ubuntu")

or

[http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/create-a-usb-stick-on-ubuntu]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/help/create-a-usb-stick-on-ubuntu")

When you've got your medium set, stick in and reboot, make sure that your system is set to boot from that medium ahead of your normal hard drive.


Edit: I remembered that 12.10 only burns to a DVD, as it is too big for a standard CD.
