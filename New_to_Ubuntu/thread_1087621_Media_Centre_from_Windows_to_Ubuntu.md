### Post by cwsnyder on 2009-03-05
You might check out [this]("http://www.hydrogenaudio.org/forums/lofiversion/index.php/t68457.html") discussion of portable FLAC players from the hydrogenaudio.org forums.

ffmpeg is the (non-graphical) basis for most of the converter programs and players, including VLC.  The only WMA formats which I know of which are not supported are the DRM protected forms.  The best way I know of to convert these is to burn to either virtual-CD or actual audio CD and re-rip to lossless WMA/FLAC.
