### Post by whywouldyoudothat on 2010-07-22
Seems that music is being previewed when I mouse over it in the file browser. How do I stop this? Not sure if this is an ubuntu "feature" or something from one of the media players.

---

### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-07-22
In Nautilus (the file browser), go Edit->Preferences->Preview-Tab, change the setting for 'Sound Files' to 'never'.

---

### Post by varunendra on 2010-07-22
Open nautilus (computer browser) goto Edit>Preferences then click on 'Preview' tab. There, against 'Sound Files' select 'Never' from the drop-down list.
