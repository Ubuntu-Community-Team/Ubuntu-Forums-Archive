### Post by nkri on 2008-09-20
To save settings, right-click on the dock>Cairo-Dock>Manage Themes.

In the Save tab, check "current behavior" and "current launchers"

Click OK.  If you want to go back to the settings you just saved, go back into Manage Themes, and in the Themes tab, find your personal theme from the drop-down list and click OK.

If you force the version, I'm not 100% sure if it will overwrite the theme you just saved, but it shouldn't since it is saved in your home folder.

Good luck!
-nkri
