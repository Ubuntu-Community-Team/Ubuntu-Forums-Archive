### Post by unutbu on 2009-04-23
Go to System>Pref>Keyboard.
Click the Layout tab.
Click the Add button (if you haven't already) to add the layouts you desire (e.g. English and Russian)
Click the "Other Options" button.
Click the "Layout Switching" triangle.
Enable the box next to "Alt+Shift" enables switching.
