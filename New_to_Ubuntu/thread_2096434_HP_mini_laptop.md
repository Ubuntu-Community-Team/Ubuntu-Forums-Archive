### Post by mlentink on 2012-12-20
The HP Mini has a little knob on the front (right afaik) that turns wifi on/off. Try that. And then you can connect with a cable, and go to Software Center. Click on Edit > Software Sources, and subsequently on the Tab 'Additional Drivers'. Check if the proprietary driver is available and select it.
