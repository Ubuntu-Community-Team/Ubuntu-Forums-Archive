### Post by scouser73 on 2010-04-17
[COLOR="Red"]**In Thunderbird do the following**[/COLOR]

1 - Click **Edit**, select **Account Settings**

2 - Scroll to **Account Actions** and select **Add Mail Account**

**You will now see a dialogue box appear.**

3 - Enter your **Name**, **Email Address** & **Password**

4 - Press **Continue**

Your email account will now be set up.
