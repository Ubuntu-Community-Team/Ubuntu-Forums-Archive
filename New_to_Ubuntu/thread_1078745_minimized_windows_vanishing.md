### Post by listerdl on 2009-02-23
You need to right click on the top horizontal panel and 'add to panel'

Then you need to choose either windows selector or Windows List and that will stop windows vanishing. That ought to work

However, worked out what the problem was - the task manager widget had been removed somehow; once I added that to the bottom panel the usual behaviour of windows minimizing to the bottom bar returned.
