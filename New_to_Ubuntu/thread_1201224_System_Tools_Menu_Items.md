### Post by jwulff on 2009-06-30
How do the menu items get displayed in the Systems Tools menu that appears under the Ubuntu logo? How can I remove those items. I had uninstalled a package in Synaptic but the links still appear in System Tools. Thanks.

---

### Post by fooman on 2009-06-30
right-click on the ubuntu logo and choose edit menus from the list.  when the main menu pops up,  click on "system tools" in the left hand column,  then *un-*check the item in the middle column which you wish to remove from the menu.  you can also choose to delete the item entirely by highlighting it,  then clicking the delete button on the right.
