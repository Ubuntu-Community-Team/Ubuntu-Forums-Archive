To do a bit-torrent download of Ubuntu itself:

[http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/alternative-download]("http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/alternative-download")

Once installed, you can use the very handy 'Ubuntu Software Center' to install many useful applications and fun games.

There is free music on Amazon - lots of 'samplers' of new bands that are quite good.  They are all Mp3 files.  You can also buy music on the Ubuntu music store, or play the music files you areadly have, too.

Downloaded movies play fine too, as do DVDs.

Choose an Ubuntu install that matches your hardware, then use the ISO to burn a CD and take a test drive to make sure Ubuntu runs on your PC.

**Software for linux**
Downloading software from torrent sites is a pretty risky move no matter which os its for, even linux apps are susceptible to tampering. Stick to trusted sources like supported repositories, sourceforge, or even the softwares website.
