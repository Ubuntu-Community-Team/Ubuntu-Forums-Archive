### Post by hugodaniel2 on 2015-02-02
Hi, I'm having some problems with Dropbox after my upgrade to Ubuntu 14.04.

Starting Ubuntu at work, where I'm behind a proxy, I keep getting the following error message after trying to finish the Dropbox installation:

***"Trouble connecting to Dropbox servers. Maybe your internet connection is down, or you need to set your http_proxy environment variable."***

At home, even after starting Dropbox and finishing the download, I can't end the installation, because I get the same error message.

What could I do?

Thanks

---

### Post by hugodaniel2 on 2015-02-26
Hi,

Yesterday, I've uninstalled DropBox, and removed all it's hidden folders at the /home dir.
Afterwards, I've reinstalled the new DropBox installer **"dropbox_2015.02.12_amd64"**, which I've downloaded directly from their website, and it finnally worked again.

[https://www.dropbox.com/install](https://www.dropbox.com/install)

So, now it's a solved problem.
