>  until I changed the path of /home without backing up first.Why were you doing this?  You can not simply change the path to home by moving things to somewhere else as it does not work that way.  The linux and ubuntu filesystem demands that /home is either a specified folder in / (root) or is a separate partition that is chosen at install, and you can not just move it without a complete re-arangement of the filesystem.

What exactly did you do?  It may be possible to put things right.
