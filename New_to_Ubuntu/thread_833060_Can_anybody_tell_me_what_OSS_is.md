### Post by Cadmus on 2008-06-18
OSS in this context means Open Sound System, it's another way of getting audio from your programs to your hardware. By default Ubuntu uses ALSA, I didn't have much luck getting Ubuntu system sounds through OSS, but applications were fine.

The OSS site is [here]("http://www.opensound.com/")
