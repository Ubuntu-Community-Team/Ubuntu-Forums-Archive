### Post by peace.gangsta on 2010-10-12
Well lets try this :
Go to System->Preferences->Keyboard->Accessibility 
Now under 'Accessibility' you have **Slow Keys** option with a a dialog 'only accept long keypresses'.
Just uncheck this. 
I expect all is well now.
Do let us know.
