### Post by teward on 2011-03-07
Not to be annoying, but there have been issues with MSI boards and systems, and Ubuntu / Linux.  I've had a few come past my workbench, with various kernel panics (the Windows equivalent of a BSOD), and it occurred over 3 different Linux distributions, including Ubuntu, Debian, and Arch (of all evil things in the world), and the kernel panics still occurred.  100% valid images, and it still imploded.

The issue might not actually be your web cam, but maybe how the MSI board and Linux are interacting?  I mean, i know your issue isnt kernel panics, but i have seen minor hardware issues with Linux and MSI boards/hardware... just thought i'd mention it, in case the rest of the Ubuntu Forums community cant find a resolution to your issue (in which case we might be able to attribute the issue to MSI hardware and Linux)
