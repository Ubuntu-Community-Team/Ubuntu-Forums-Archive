### Post by Audacitor on 2009-01-18
Click on the System menu in the upper left-hand corner of the screen, go into Administration, then hit Users and Groups all the way at the bottom. Click the Unlock button in the window that comes up and enter your administrator password. Now click on the Add User button to add an user. Repeat as desired.
