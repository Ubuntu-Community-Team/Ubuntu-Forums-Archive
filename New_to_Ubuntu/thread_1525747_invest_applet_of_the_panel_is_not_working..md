### Post by hotwax on 2010-12-02
There are a few bugs with the applet that still need to addressed, but  I found a work around, not sure what the cause of it is but you can follow this process to get your stock quotes. 

Delete all your stock symbols and then remove the applet from the panel.

Add the applet again to the panel. This time leave the default google stock symbol as it is. Put in a dummy stock, for example: 
GE with 100 shares and purchase price of $1.00. After that you can add what ever symbols you want.

My problem involves multiple stock quotes of the same company that have been purchased at different times at different prices. Some how when i display the percentage change and the value, they dont add up because the quotes of my 3 stocks are lumped up into 1 quote.
