### Post by entropy1 on 2013-12-25
In the Firefox menu bar under Bookmarks, click on "Show All Bookmarks".  This will bring up a dialog box.  From the "Import and Backup" menu, select "Restore" and from the pop-up menu, at the bottom, select "Choose File...".  Then navigate to the bookmark file you want to restore from.
