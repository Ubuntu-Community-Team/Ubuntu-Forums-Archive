### Post by lisati on 2010-05-05
A lot of things can mess with your download speed. These include how busy your home network is, how busy the server is, and how busy the path from your home network to the server is. Some ISPs even slow down connections for reasons such as going past your monthly allowance and, if you're on a "managed" plan, how busy their system is. Even the quality of the wiring between your computer and your ISP can sometimes make a difference.
