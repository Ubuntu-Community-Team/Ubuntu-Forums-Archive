### Post by Technoviking on 2009-08-17
You may need new drivers for a NVidia 9800 card.

You may want to add this ppa, which has 185.18 drivers:
[https://edge.launchpad.net/~ubuntu-x-swat/+archive/x-updates](https://edge.launchpad.net/~ubuntu-x-swat/+archive/x-updates)

Please note, the drivers in this ppa are not officially supported.


Also, read [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/Nvidia](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/Nvidia)

T-V
