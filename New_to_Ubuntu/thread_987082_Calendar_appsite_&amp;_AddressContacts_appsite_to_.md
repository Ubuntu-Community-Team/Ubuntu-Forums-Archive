### Post by cozmicharlie on 2008-11-19
I just use Google calender with Thunderbird.  Thunderbird has the lightning plugin that syncs your Google calender right in Thunderbird and of course it has an address book.  You can add appointments etc either through Thunderbird or Google calender and they show up on both.  Once I add my info in either it stays on even off line.  Their is a program called basket that works well for To Do's and notes.  Remember the milk is also very good.  What OS are you running on your eeepc (Xandros, eeebuntu, XP or ??).  The stock Xandros has a PIM that looks OK. You might give it a try see if you like it.  

Another option is to check out the forums at eeeuser.com.  They have a very active and friendly community of eeepc users and the forums and wiki are a great source of info.

Enjoy
