### Post by mcduck on 2009-06-04
If you are downloading mouse cursors they are not programs..

Just open System/Preferences/Appearance and drag&drop the cursor theme package to that window. If the cursor theme isn't applied immediately then go to Theme-tab, click Customize and select your cursor theme on the Pointer-tab. Quite often you need to restart your applications or log out & back again before the new cursor theme is working correctly for all your applications.

Alternatively, you can just extract the theme package into ~/.icons
