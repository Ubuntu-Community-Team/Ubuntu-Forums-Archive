### Post by XCan on 2009-06-22
After wine has been installed according to Jimmyfj's instruction, you can run (some) Windows programs by simply right clicking on the .exe files and select Open with Wine. However, I do have to stress that if(when) you encounter some issues you should always checkout [http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/) and update your wine to the newest version according to the instructions in [http://www.winehq.org/download/deb](http://www.winehq.org/download/deb) .
