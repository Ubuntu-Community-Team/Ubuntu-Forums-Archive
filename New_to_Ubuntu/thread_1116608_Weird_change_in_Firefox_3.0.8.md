I just tried it on Windows.  There is a menu bar at the top of the screen that appears when your mouse gets close to the top of the page.

I've had the menu bar disappear on me in Ubuntu before, without being in full screen mode.  Here's what I did to fix it:
[LIST=1]
[*]Right click on Firefox in the menu bar
[*]Select move window
[*]Move the window up, until you can see the bottom of it.
[*]Click
[*]Drag the bottom of the window up to make it smaller
[*]Repeat steps 1 and 2
[*]Move the window down to make the top menu bar visable
[*]Click
[/LIST]
