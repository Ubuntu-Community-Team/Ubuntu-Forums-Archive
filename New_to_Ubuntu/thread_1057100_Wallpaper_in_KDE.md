### Post by AlanR8 on 2009-02-01
No real need for a how to!

Right click the desktop and then Appearance Settings. You'll find a setting for Picture and to the right an icon of a folder. 

If you have existing pictures on your system, click the folder and navigate to where the pictures are. Select and enjoy!
