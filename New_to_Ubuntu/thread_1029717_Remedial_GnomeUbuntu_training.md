### Post by Michael.Godawski on 2009-01-03
hey Goliath!,

If I were you, I would just explain it to her... :P
Stand behind her and tell her over her should what to do.
But be patient and helpful.

Let her perhaps install a new game via the Add/Remove application.

Or let her watch a video with the Movie Player, or hear some music using Rhythmbox.

Learning by doing, and without the fear to damage something is the best way to acquire new knowledge.
