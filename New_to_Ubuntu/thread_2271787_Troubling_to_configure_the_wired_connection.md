### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2015-04-01
If you have a standard ubuntu desktop (Unity), to get that info, just click on the network icon in upper right corner of screen (looks like a fan with bars), then click on "connection information" in the drop-down menu.   Procedure is similar for other flavors of buntu.   Plus there are ways to get this data from command line also by just entering "ifconfig" if I recall correctly.
