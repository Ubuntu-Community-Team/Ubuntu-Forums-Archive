### Post by Big Luke on 2009-02-20
It may be a hardware issue.  What are your system specs...more specifically, how much RAM do you have?  How fast is your Internet connec?  The quality/speed of either of these may affect how fast/slow videos load and buffer on a video site...I assume that is your problem.

To find out how much RAM you have, go to System->Administration->System Monitor and once it is open go to the "System" tab and under Hardware it should say Memory: ___ GB.  That is the amount of RAM you have.

To find out how fast your internet connection is you can go [***here***]("http://www.speakeasy.net/speedtest/") for a free speed test.  Mine is:

Download Speed: 2133 kbps (266.6 KB/sec transfer rate)
Upload Speed: 412 kbps (51.5 KB/sec transfer rate)

With my connec speed I have no problem watching videos, it practically starts as soon as the page loads.
