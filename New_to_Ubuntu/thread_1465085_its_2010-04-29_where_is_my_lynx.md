### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-04-29
> **leonhook said:**
> not sure yeah it does suck tho they should just release it a midnight everywhere.

They have to fix last minute bugs from the release candidate and then make sure the mirrors are fully up-to-date. There are three supported architectures, six different distros and two different install CDs, plus Ubuntu Server, which makes 3x6x2=36 different ISOs to mirror, making (36x700mb)=25.2GiB of data to send to the mirrors. Oh, and there's DVD images too.

Be patient! You've waited 6 months, another day won't hurt. If another day WILL hurt, then just download the release candidate.
