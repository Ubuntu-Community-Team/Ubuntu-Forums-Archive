### Post by mapes12 on 2008-05-12
I tried:

AcidRip
Dvd95
dvd::rip
and K9copy

Out of them all K9copy works for me the best. It's user GUI is easy to understand with all the options you need. It made a backup copy of one of my movie DVD's no problem. It creates an ISO image of the disc ready for burning. It also has the option to shrink big movies (>4.5GB) to a size for burning on a standard recordable DVD. And you can convert to mpeg4.

System>Administration>Package Manager will install it and any dependacies.

Tip: I tried it first on my old PIII but the PC didn't have enough ooomph and copying failed. So I tried it on another of my boxes with a better CPU and memory and it worked fine
