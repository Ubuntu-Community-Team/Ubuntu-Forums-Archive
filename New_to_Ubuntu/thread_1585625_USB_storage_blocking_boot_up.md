### Post by msakms on 2010-09-30
My HP desktop workstation does the same issue and I had to remove my external hard on reboot. So, I got over this by removing USB-HD and USB-pen drive from the boot-up list of the BIOS. Basically, so that it only looks at my internal HD. Give it a shot and see if that works for you
