### Post by sandyd on 2009-12-04
you will have to download a FTP client.

a nice good one is Filezilla ([http://filezilla-project.org/](http://filezilla-project.org/))
enter in the computers ip address as the host, enter in your username and password

IE / Firefox can also access FTP servers by putting [ftp://computer.ip.address.here](ftp://computer.ip.address.here)
however, ie6 doesn't work well with non-windows ftp servers.
