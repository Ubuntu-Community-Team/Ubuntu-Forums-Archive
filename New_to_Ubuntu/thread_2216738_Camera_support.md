Does your camera have a removeable SD Card?

Do you have a USB activated SD Card reader?

If you do, remove the SD card from the camera, insert the SD card into the card reader and you should be able to transfer the photos to your PC.

### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-04-13
Go into the Camera settings and check for PTP versus MTP settings.  Don't forget to power on the camera in "Play" mode after it is plugged into USB.  I have an A70 and A85 and they worked out of the box, so I don't know why you would be having problems.
