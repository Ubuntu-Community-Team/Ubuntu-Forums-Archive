### Post by amanchesterman on 2013-03-14
I find that for LibreOffice issues you *can* get responses in these forums but it's also worth looking at the LibreOffice forums. If you do you will find, unfortunately, that your experience isn't uncommon. Working in .docx format with Writer is known to be risky and several other users have reported losing pages from long documents as you have done. (See [http://en.libreofficeforum.org/node/3831](http://en.libreofficeforum.org/node/3831) for example.) The advice is to (a) always keep backup copies, (b) convert a document to .doc format and work with that whenever you can and (c) heed the warning in LibreOffice about saving in anything other than .odf (Open Document) format.

I see that LibreOffice 4 has now been released and I intend to try it out when I get the chance, to see if it is more reliable in this respect. Fingers crossed! :-)
