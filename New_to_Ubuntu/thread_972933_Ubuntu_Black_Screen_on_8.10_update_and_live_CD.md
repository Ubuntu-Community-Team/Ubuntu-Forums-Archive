### Post by bscbrit on 2008-11-06
Others are having a similar problem.  As it is likely to be caused by a bug in 8.10 you might be better off reverting to 8.04 for a few weeks until the kinks in 8.10 can be removed.

I have been involved in fixing one computer with this fault in the last 24 hours on this forum, but that is no guarantee that yours is exactly the same fault and the fix itself requires quite a bit of time particularly for someone who is not well versed in linux.

Its your choice.  If you want to try to fix it we can, but it might not be a pleasant introduction to linux or Ubuntu, or you can install 8.04 which will probably work straight out of the box.  As an aside, from the user's point of view there is nothing in 8.10 that would make it an vital upgrade.  Many are still quite content using earlier releases and several people have announced that they are switching back to 8.04 from 8.10 until the bugs are sorted out.
