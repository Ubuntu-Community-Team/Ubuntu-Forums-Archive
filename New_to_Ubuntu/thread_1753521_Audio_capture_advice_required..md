### Post by wep940 on 2011-05-09
1 device you may want to look at, and read the threads on the forum for getting it to work, is the EasyCap - you can find it on Ebay.  It has video inputs as well as RCA-type stereo audio jacks for sound capture as well.  I've never tried just capturing sound, as so far all I've done with mine is plug it into my VCR so I could copy my old VCR tapes to DVD.
