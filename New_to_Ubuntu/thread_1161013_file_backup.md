### Post by Paddy Landau on 2009-05-16
Do you want to back up your entire partition or just the files in your /home directory?

[LIST]
[*]Your entire partition: Try [Partimage]("http://www.partimage.org/"). Download and burn a CD from [SysRescCD]("http://www.sysresccd.org/"). Partimage is included. You'll probably need an external hard drive, depending on how much space you need.
[*]Just your /home directory: In nautilus or terminal, copy your entire /home directory to a CD, DVD or external hard drive depending on how much you've stored.
[*]Or, if you only want your data files and not your configuration files, copy your data from your /home directory to a CD, DVD or external hard drive.
[/LIST]
Let us know if you meant something else.
