### Post by Jeroenix on 2008-12-09
You could use CTRL-ALT-left/right to rotate the cube.

To get the workspace switcher back, right-click on the bottom task bar, choose 'Add to panel..'. The workspace switcher should be there somewhere.

### Post by festlig-faetter on 2008-12-09
You left click on the bottom panel, and select "Add to panel"
Then at the bottom, you should find a 'Workspace switcher'
