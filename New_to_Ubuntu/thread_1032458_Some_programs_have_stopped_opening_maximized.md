### Post by m3lt on 2009-02-12
While firefox is the active window...
Alt-F3 -> Advanced -> Special Window Settings -> Geometry Tab 

Check the boxes by Maximize Horizontally and Vertically, then in the drop down menus select Apply Initially

May be an easier way but it worked for me. I hated maximizing the window every time I started it and finally found out how to fix it.


BTW this was done in KDE...maybe somebody could verify steps in gnome.
