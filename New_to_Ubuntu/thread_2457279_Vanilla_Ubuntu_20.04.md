### Post by crip720 on 2021-01-29
Vanilla(default) Ubuntu just means Ubuntu with the gnome desktop.  Depending on your computer specs, it can be a bit on the heavy side(computer might be slow) using it.  Some people like it, some will use one of the other desktops(flavours).  Ubuntu also lets you try before installing, so if hardware does not work or you just don't like it, can try another flavour.
