### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-05-06
I can't help with Sound Juicer, but there is a 3rd party plugin for RhythmBox that will connect to FreeDB and download the information for that CD.

If you prefer to do it yourself, just highlight the track, Right-click and go to **Properties**.

Here is a link to the third party plugins page:

[http://live.gnome.org/RhythmboxPlugins/ThirdParty](http://live.gnome.org/RhythmboxPlugins/ThirdParty)

I hope this helps...
