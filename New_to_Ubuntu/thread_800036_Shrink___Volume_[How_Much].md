### Post by Monicker on 2008-05-19
It depends on how much you intend to seriously use Ubuntu, and what  kind of files you will be using in linux.  I would give it at least 5GB minimum.  Later on can always expand the linux partition to give it more space.

Since you have plenty of space, you might as well give it more from the start.  I would go with at least 20gb myself.
