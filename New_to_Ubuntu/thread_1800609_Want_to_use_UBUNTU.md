### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-07-10
Since you're new to Ubuntu, I can't overstress how important it is to boot from the Ubuntu CD in TRY IT mode first.  It's critical that you see how well Ubuntu detects your hardware, installs the right drivers and generally, works for you.

Anything that is not detected and installed during the LiveCD mode is going to range from simple to extremely hard (if not impossible) later to fix.

So, before you make the jump, presuming everything will be better, you need to check it out first.
