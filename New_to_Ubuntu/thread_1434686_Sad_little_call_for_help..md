### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-03-20
Choose the Manually Choose Partions (Manual Partitioning) option then click Forward for it to load the manual partitioner and you will be able to select it.

Don't let the Manual partitioning scare you it just gives you more control.

No changes will be made until after step 7 so you can always change your mind up to that point.
