### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-05-02
It has an AMD APU. 
The optional-for-most-users AMD proprietary drivers are in your case, Samsung 535U3C, kinda mandatory. It's mostly for the graphics but also for almost everything else.
As you noticed, you can boot in recovery. Install the drivers offered in System Settings > Software & Updates (the rightmost tab, Additional Drivers) and you should be good to go...
