### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-09-15
Make sure that there is a tick mark against Enable Networking and while you are at it Enable Wireless. Also you must give us more information. What error messages is Ubuntu giving you after you log in?

If you see the message

Network Disconnected. You are now off line

Then you need to enable networking by clicking the line Enable Networking.

If you get the message

Wired network disconnected. You are now offline

then make sure that the ethernet cable is plugged into both the computer and the router, or that the router is switched on, or that Enable Networking is ticked.

What do you mean, when you say that you cannot connect to the internet? Is the router making a connection to your Internet Service Provider?
