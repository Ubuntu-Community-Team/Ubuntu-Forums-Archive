### Post by dmillerct on 2010-01-19
> **bamim2 said:**
> I have GNOME & in the upper left corner of the panel there was a menu that said "Applications" (I think). I accidentally removed that menu. Is there any way to get it back?

Right click on the panel (where it was before) and click "add to panel" from the list select "Menu Bar" (or Main Menu whichever one you were using. It was probably Menu Bar as is the default).
