### Post by PaulW2U on 2018-07-30
> **MSGone said:**
> Please tell me that I didn't lose the 5 year plan of updates. I didn't even know when it went to 18.04.1.
The point releases basically mark the release of an updated ISO which minimises the amount of updates downloaded when installing well after 18.04's initial release. As you continue to update you'll find that you're using 18.04.2 through to 18.04.5. Your current installation is supported until sometime in April **2023**. :D
> **Autodave said:**
> You will *not *need to worry about updates for at least 5 years.
Well technically a little less than five years now as the clock started ticking last April when 18.04 was released. :)
