### Post by Linux_junkie on 2012-01-06
The problem there is in converting a OOo document to MS Word document.  OOo is not 100% compatible with MS Office and therefore formatting will not be right.

Anyone using OpenOffice / LibreOffice or any other office based applications that are not MS Office should really be exporting their documents in PDF format that way everyone can read the document regardless of what wordprocessor they use.
