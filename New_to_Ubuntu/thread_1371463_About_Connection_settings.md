### Post by Portable_Jim on 2010-01-04
When you try to browse to a website (try [http://www.google.com](http://www.google.com)), what are the first two lines of the error that comes up in firefox?

Go to "Applications" => "Accessories" => "Terminal" and type in
```
ping -c 2 google.com
```then hit enter.

Copy and paste the output of the terminal window into your reply.
