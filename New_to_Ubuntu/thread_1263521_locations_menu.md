### Post by ankspo71 on 2009-09-11
Hi,

Open your favorite folder. 
(you can do that by opening home folder first if you like).

Next, look up at the menu on top, and click on "bookmarks".

Next, click on "+add bookmark"

Your favorite folder should now appear where you want it to, in the places menu.
(The places menu is also your bookmarked folder menu)

Hope this helps.
