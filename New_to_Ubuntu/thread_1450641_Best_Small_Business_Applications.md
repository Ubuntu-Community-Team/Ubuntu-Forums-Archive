### Post by yeehi on 2010-04-09
Any online business, for example a web store selling ping pong balls.

With regard to aps, communications are super important and with [Google Aps]("http://www.google.com/apps/intl/en/business/gmail.html") you can set up a gmail account which will handle your email without having @gmail.com at the end of it. It would be like [email]info@smallbusiness.com[/email].

The google Aps would also be able to supply you with calenders and maps and so on, all for free! You can even have a google checkout, to take payments. It is a great resource.

There are other applications too, for example [sugarcrm]("http://www.sugarcrm.com/crm/community/sugarcrm-community.html") might be one people would recommend for customer relation management, though I am not sure what people think about this one, there may be others that are better.

What about business accounting applications? What about web store applications?

Maybe there is a one-ap-does all application, like [NetSuite]("http://www.netsuite.com/portal/home.shtml") - that would be fantastic! 

I hope that people who have tried online business will have a few ideas they would like to share :)
