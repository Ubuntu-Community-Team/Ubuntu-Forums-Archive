### Post by mcduck on 2009-08-20
Actually quite many music players have their own EQs. You could try Audacious, for example. It has EQ plugin and can also act as host for LADSPA plugins which makes it able to run quite a large selection of sound effect & filter plugins.

And the trick with using equalizers is to not use it to boost anything. It will only ruin your sound quality. Instead drop the unwanted frequencies. So, if you want more bass you'd drop everything else down a bit, and then increase the master volume.
