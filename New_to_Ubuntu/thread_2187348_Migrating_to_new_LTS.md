### Post by ant2 on 2013-11-11
An upgrade from 12.04 to 14.04 will be possible, your data **should** be OK. However, a full back-up of your data should be made before making the upgrade, just in case of problems. A separate /home partition can also make the data stored in your user folder safer, bu,t back-ups are the best way to secure your important data, no matter what you are doing.

### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-11-12
Backup any data you wouldn't want to lose before upgrading. Rule of thumb, bottom line. Good luck.

One of the advantages (and reasons) for having your personal data on a separate /home partition (or drive altogether) is that when you upgrade/reinstall that data is not in the same / partition as your OS.
