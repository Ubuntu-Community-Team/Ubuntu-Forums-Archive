### Post by Frogs Hair on 2014-01-16
Emerald is an old program and it used to possible to use Compiz, and Emerald Themes with it. Compiz was not official Ubuntu project at one time and Emerald was dumped at some point before or when Ubuntu took over. Canonical  hired one of the main Compiz developers and he or she has now moved on . Emerald is now maintained as a PPA as you may know . It is my guess that due to the changes in Compiz that many themes may no longer work . Compiz is not native to XFCE which has its own compositor and that may also be part of the problem.
