### Post by janmartin on 2008-04-25
Menu: System, Preferences, Keyboard:
2nd tab: Layouts
Add
In "Layout" pick your country
In "Variants" pick Dvorak.

In "Layout Options..." choose the keys you want.
Done.

3rd from the bottom of that list is 
"Left Ctrl key changes layout"

Voila!
