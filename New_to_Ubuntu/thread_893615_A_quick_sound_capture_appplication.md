### Post by HittingSmoke on 2008-08-18
AFAIK that wasnt a feature in MS Sound Recroder, but in your sound driver's mixer. In Soundblaster card's Windows mixer there is a recording device called "What U Hear" and basically patches any external audio directly to a recording device so you could record streaming audio and such at its native quality.

When I was using Windows I would use this feature with Audacity to record streaming radio shows to listen to later, so the program is not your problem, its the drivers.

I had this feature on my Audigy in Windows but I'm using AC97 right now and havent tried it.

One way around it is to get a splitter for your speaker out jack and a double ended cable to loop back in to your mic port.
