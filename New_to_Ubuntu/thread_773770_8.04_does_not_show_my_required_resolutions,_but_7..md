### Post by skip mcshwang on 2008-04-29
Go into System > Preferences > Main Menu > Applications > Other and turn on Screen and Graphics. 

Then go to Applications > Other > Screen and Graphics. Select your monitor, (don't chose plug and play.) and the resolution you want. Then I think you'll need to restart.
