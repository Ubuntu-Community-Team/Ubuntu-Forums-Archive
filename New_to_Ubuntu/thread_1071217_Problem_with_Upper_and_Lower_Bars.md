### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2009-02-16
Right click on the panel of your choice (or bar in your case) and select "add to panel"
Add "notification area" to get your network applet back
and "window list" to get your taskbar back.
But you will have to log out to make the net applet re appear.
There is another applet for network monitoring too, its listed as "monitor network" in the list.
Everything is drag and drop so its easy to figure out.
