### Post by DonaldJ on 2009-03-31
Update on this little irritating troublesome glitch...

I find that should I get right into using Ubuntu immediately after a fresh install, and start saving files and pix and such to desktop, from removable media, without first getting all the OS's updates, seems to be when and why these file-browser lock-up problems occur.. But after deleting those files, and updating properly, these problems never happen...

I suspect that my immediately installing SeaMonkey after a new install, before doing the updates, might have a little to do with it too..?

Conclusion, and suggestion for the novice:  After a new install immediately do the couple-hundred critical updates, then customize and personalize and add desired peripherals...
