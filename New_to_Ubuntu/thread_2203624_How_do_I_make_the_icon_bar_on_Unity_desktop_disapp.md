### Post by PotatoHead007 on 2014-02-04
Hey, not sure if its the same as on Ubuntu 12.04, but it should be. Here's what you do:
Click **System settings** in your icon bar. Go to **Appearance**, and select the **Behaviour** tab. Then click on the on/off bar that says "Auto-hide the aluncher". All done :)
