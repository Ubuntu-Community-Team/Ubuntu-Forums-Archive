### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-02-09
Not specifically for that machine but I suspect Lubuntu will be the best choice for any netbook of that type.  It is the *ubuntu family OS that requires the least resources of all of them.

Try Lubuntu 14.04 for a start and see what you think.  I have it running superbly on a re-badged MSI netbook with atom cpu and 1gb ram; not the fastest of machines but adequate for my needs.
