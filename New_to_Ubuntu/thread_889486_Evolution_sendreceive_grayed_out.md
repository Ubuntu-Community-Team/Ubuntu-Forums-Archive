Problem Solved!
I have Ubuntu 8 installed (dual boot).  Evolution has worked perfectly up to now (3 months). But somehow between working yesterday and now (10.50 am) the send/receive button in Mail and in Tasks has become grayed out. The other buttons in the Evolution toolbar are OK (live).  Sending email from the new message window puts the message in the Outbox but there it stays.  I have tried a computer restart but the problem remains. Is this a bug, a virus, or something I have done?  I have checked that my mail accounts are still enabled.  They are.  I have checked the bug lists but cannot find a report on this problem. Not being able to send and receive email is a critical problem for me.

Oh dear! Silly me! Problem solved!  I have just noticed that Evolution was running offline.  All is well now that I have found and hit the online button.
