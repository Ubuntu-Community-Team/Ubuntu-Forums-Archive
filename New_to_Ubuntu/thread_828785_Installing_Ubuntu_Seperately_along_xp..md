### Post by hyper_ch on 2008-06-15
well, do you already have the 20 gb partition separated?

If so, just delete that partition and leave the 20 gb UNPARTITIONED.

Then, during the install, select "USE LARGEST CONTINUOUS FREE SPACE".

It will then install it in that unpartitioned space and create the necessary partitions itself.
