### Post by nicedude on 2008-05-15
This shouldn't be a problem or destroy any data, I think they are so marked by windows. If they are text you could go to their permissions under properties for each and remove executable check mark so they just open with an editor directly. Executables from windows ie. EXE files won't work in ubuntu except for in wine or another windows emulator so you can't hurt anything with them either. In no way will any of this activity destroy your data.

Hope this calms your fears and enjoy Ubuntu :-)
