### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2011-11-16
That is the cache where all of the programs that you have installed reside.  In theory I believe that you could back them up to a flash drive, but unless they are taking up a great deal of space and you are running short of space on your hard drive, you don't really have to do anything with them.  You can always just delete them, but in your case since you want to keep them just in case see:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AptProxy](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AptProxy)
