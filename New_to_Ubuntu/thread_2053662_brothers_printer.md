### Post by kurt18947 on 2012-09-06
Brother has a BASH installer script which makes installing their printers pretty much a no-brainer.  Not taking anything away from HP,  there are a few good choices for printing under Linux.  Epson has a pretty good rep as well though I don't know that laser printers are Epson's strong suit.  Personally, I would avoid Lexmark and Dell which is often a Lexmark with a different name plate.  Canon seems good or bad depending on model.  Quite a few people in N. America with Canon machines have better success  on Canon's Asian or European support sites.

I would suggest going to the manufacturer's web site support section.  If there's no mention of Linux,  that should tell you something.
