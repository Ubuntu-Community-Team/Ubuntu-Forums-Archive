### Post by NightwishFan on 2011-01-27
There are two types of upgrades in ubuntu, simple security and bug fix updates which sometimes happen every day. You can upgrade these at your leisure (weekly or biweekly is good).

There is also upgrades (think xp->vista) every 6 months. You would notice a few major changes if you did this upgrade, such as newer artwork, etc. These are also optional. If you are using a long term release such as 10.04 (lucid), you will not see the 6 months update by default and it will only upgrade to a new release every 2 years. You can do this manually of course for the 6 month ones.
