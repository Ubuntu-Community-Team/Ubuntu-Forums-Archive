### Post by QIII on 2009-09-27
Skype latency is a function of traffic on whatever medium the signal is transmitted over.  It is often out of your control, that of your ISP or that of any number of people in between.

If your connection is through a satellite at any point and higher priority traffic is queued for the satellite, you get bumped.

Try talking to kids in Iraq or Afghanistan using Skype.  We just deal with it.
