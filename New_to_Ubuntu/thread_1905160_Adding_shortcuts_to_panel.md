### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-01-06
On Lubuntu you will have to add an **Application Launch Bar** applet and then add the specific application shortcuts to that.

---

### Post by nothingspecial on 2012-01-06
Right click where you see the chromium and file browser icons, just next to the menu. Then choose application launchbar settings. From there you can add anything in your menus.
