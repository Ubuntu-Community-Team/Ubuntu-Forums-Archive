### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-01-11
Hi,
Does this concern having multiple people use your computer?

As far as I know, the password you are entering is the password to your email account so you can download new messages. If you have multiple users on your computer, *and* they each have their own Ubuntu user account, they will not be able to see your messages in their user account, because your messages are located in your own personal home folder, and theirs will be located in their own home folder.

Don't forget that allowing someone to use your own ubuntu user account allows anybody to have access to your own personal files, including browser bookmarks, browser history, email, and or other things you might not want them to see.

You can easily create new ubuntu user accounts by going to "System" menu > "Administration" > "Users and Groups"
Hope this helps.
