### Post by howefield on 2010-09-02
At the bottom of the page you got the ppa instructions from, you will see a list of packages in the repository.

[https://launchpad.net/~banshee-team/+archive/ppa?field.series_filter=](https://launchpad.net/~banshee-team/+archive/ppa?field.series_filter=)

Doesn't look like the latest version is available from there. You have the most up to date version from that ppa.

The ppa that you need appears to be available here...

[https://launchpad.net/~banshee-team/+archive/banshee-unstable](https://launchpad.net/~banshee-team/+archive/banshee-unstable)
