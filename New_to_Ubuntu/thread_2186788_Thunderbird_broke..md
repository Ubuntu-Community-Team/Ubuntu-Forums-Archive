### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-11-09
I would start by exporting all your addressbook to a file for backup, to restore later and renaming the hidden .thunderbird folder in your /home as .thunderbirdOLD.  Don't delete it as it will have all your emails and settings which can be copied back later.

I suspect a corruption somewhere in your profile which hopefully this will overcome by making a new profile.  If that works you can get all your mail and contacts back again quickly
