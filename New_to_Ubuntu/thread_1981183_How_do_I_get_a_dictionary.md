### Post by km3952 on 2012-05-16
Menu > System Tools > Synaptic Package Manager > Search (top right)
 Enter 'dictionary' 
It will take you to a selection of installable packages.

('wbritish' is probably the one I, as an English speaker, would choose)

Hope that helps.
