### Post by lechien73 on 2012-02-09
Are you trying to access through a proxy server? That's often why the address won't resolve.

Otherwise, you could have some corruption in your apt lists.

To fix that you could try the following:

```
sudo rm /var/lib/apt/lists/* -vf
sudo apt-get update

```
