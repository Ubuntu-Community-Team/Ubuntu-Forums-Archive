### Post by renzokuken on 2008-09-17
sodipodi is actually a vectors grafix creation program but from what i remember is now discontinued. Fortunately it was forked into the excellent **Inkscape**.

have a look at the inkcape site, [http://www.inkscape.org/](http://www.inkscape.org/) , there might be a similar collection of users clipart there.

another option might be to scour deviantart.com

EDIT: had a quick look on the inkscape site and found the following link [http://openclipart.org/](http://openclipart.org/) in the galleries section
