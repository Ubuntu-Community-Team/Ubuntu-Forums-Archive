### Post by Dutch70 on 2011-03-17
XFCE is a desktop environment. It's the one used by Xubuntu. It's lighter than Gnome & KDE desktop environments used by Ubuntu & Kubuntu, but not as light as LXDE used by Lubuntu. 

Now you know where the K, X, and L come from in Kubuntu, Xubuntu & Lubuntu. 

The lighter the desktop environment is, the better it will run on older or slower computers.
