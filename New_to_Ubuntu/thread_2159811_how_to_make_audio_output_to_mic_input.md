### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-07-04
Is this so you can record the sounds from your soundcard that you are normally hearing through your speakers?  If so have a look at **audio-recorder** which you can get from the ppa at [https://launchpad.net/~osmoma/+archive/audio-recorder](https://launchpad.net/~osmoma/+archive/audio-recorder).

It works brilliantly simply and takes almost no setting up.

Just ignore me if I am completely wrong about why you want to do this, or add it anyway; you'll probably find it very useful.
