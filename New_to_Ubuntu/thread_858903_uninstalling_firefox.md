### Post by aysiu on 2008-07-14
In your /home/*username* directory, press Control-H to view hidden directories.

The .mozilla folder should appear as one of many hidden folders. Delete .mozilla, as it has all your user settings (bookmarks, passwords, etc.) for Firefox in it.
