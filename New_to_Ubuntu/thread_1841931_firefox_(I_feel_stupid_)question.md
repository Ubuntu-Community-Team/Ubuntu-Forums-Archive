### Post by Learning Linux 2011 on 2011-09-10
You can also press the 'alt' key on your keyboard when in Firefox.

I don't think it is a Unity thing.  The same thing happens in Windows.  At some point Firefox/Mozilla decided to hide the menu bar, I guess to make it look "cool" or something.  

Once you make the menu bar visible, you can go into the 'View --> Toolbars' menu and make it permanently visible.
