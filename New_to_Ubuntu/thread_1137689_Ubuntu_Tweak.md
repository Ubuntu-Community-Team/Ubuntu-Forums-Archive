Sorry - I should have been more specific.

Just my opinion, but -

Jaunty's got a perfectly good disk cleanup program called Computer Janitor and apt will let you know if you've got packages you don't need lying around.

I don't think it's a good idea for inexperienced users to get packages from other than standard repos until they know what they're doing, and when they *do* know what they're doing they don't need ubuntu-tweak to find and install software  ;)
