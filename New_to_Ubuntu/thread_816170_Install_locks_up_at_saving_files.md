### Post by Paqman on 2008-06-03
You might want to check the disk's integrity. You can do that from the boot menu of the LiveCD, and you can also [check the MD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

It's also important to burn your install disks at the slowest possible speed to minimise errors.
