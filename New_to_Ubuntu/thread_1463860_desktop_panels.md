### Post by Merk42 on 2010-04-27
Figure out what item you want to add to what panel by right clicking it and going to About.

Currently the default desktop has the following items in the top bar:
Menu Bar   (bunch of space) Notification Area; Indicator Applet; Clock; Indicator Applet Session

The bottom bar has
Show Desktop; Window List; Workspace Switcher; Trash


Simply delete one of the panels, and all any or all of those items back in

### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-04-27
Instead of deleting them in one panel and adding them to the other you can right click on them and clear the Lock to Panel box.
Right click again and select move, then drag them to the other panel.
Then right click again and lock to panel to keep them in place.
