### Post by Ozymandias_117 on 2010-05-20
I think you're asking about not needing two monitors, mice and keyboards... If that is what you mean, you might want to look into a KVM switch. You should be able to find one for about $20 and it will allow you to switch your keyboard, video and mouse (KVM) between multiple machines.
