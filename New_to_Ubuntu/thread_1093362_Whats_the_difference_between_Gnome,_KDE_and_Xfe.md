### Post by LowSky on 2009-03-11
Gnome --- not the mystical creature its actually pronounced like the word Genome
Is the GUI from Ubuntu,  is similar to OS/X and Windows. Meant to be a simple  and not too hard to learn but with many additional packages to make things easy, can seem a heavy package comapered to other GUIs. Has been called "the GUI that thinks its users are stupid" by none other than Linus Torvalds (linux creator)

KDE --- looks alot like Windows XP, but from there it is very different, many application have the letter K, A very cutomizable GUI and considered much more eye pleasing than Gnome, was the first GUI to introduce desktop widgets as a native aspect. 

XFCE --- basically a lighter full fledge GUI, very simular to Gnome, but was designed to be able to use the same libraries as KDE and Gnome, so applicatinos could be used on the system that could use Gnome or KDE because of their age. Today XFCE is not considered that light, those honors go to GUIs like Fluxbox and IceWM
