### Post by robsoles on 2011-03-05
If it's a flash based problem then you can very likely fix it by opening firefox and getting the 'Tools'->'Add-ons' out, go to the 'Get Add-ons' page and search for flash-aid.

flash-aid removes current installations of flash and uses your system details to identify and download the correct one for you.

How's that?
