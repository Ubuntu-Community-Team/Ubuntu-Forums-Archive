### Post by pablolie on 2011-05-15
This is what worked for me (just installed 11.04 and didn't like the new look)...

Simple as
System->Administration->Login Screen->Select Ubuntu Classic as default session (The Login Screen control is under system in 11.04 as well if you look for it)

The good ole familiar format will be there again...

PS: I think there's a good idea there somewhere in the new Desktop, however I wish it allowed me an easier way to control what *I* want there and how to prioritize it, as well as create a better hierarchy and layout that don't take up the entire screen and never quite reflect what I need... I like 11.04, it works well with the latest hardware (10.04LTS does not support 1155 integrated graphics or networking well at all anymore). I think 12.04 might be the best release yet when it comes out.
