### Post by anjilslaire on 2008-09-09
Many newer titles have more then just CSS protection on them, and will fail to rip. Unfortunately, many Windows rippers are much better in this regard. 

I recommend using the free [DVDFab HD Decrypter]("http://www.dvdfab.com/free.htm") under wine (works great) to get the vob files to your hard drive, then do what you want with them, either shrink them to single layer, encode to xvid, etc.

I just posted a howto on my blog (see sig), actually...
