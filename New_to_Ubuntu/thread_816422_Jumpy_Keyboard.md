What is happening is that your hands, wrists, are touching the touchpad, and that is the source of the problem. So, you need to "type" as we all learned to do "back in the day" with your wrists elevated so no contact is being made with the touchpad; configure the touchpad to not be so touchy; or disable the touchpad while you type (there are a few threads on this in the forums). I recognize the problem, as I had/have it myself. I've disabled horizontal and vertical scrolling with the touchpad while typing, but that is a pain. I find it easier to hold my hands in the "correct" position so there is no contact made with the touchpad while typing. Others may have better suggestions.
