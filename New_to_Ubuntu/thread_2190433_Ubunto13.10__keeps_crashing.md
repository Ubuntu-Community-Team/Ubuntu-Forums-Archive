### Post by germanix on 2013-11-27
I said goodbye to windows in 2008 and installed ubuntu 8.04. Over the years I have used ubuntu, kubuntu, Xubuntu, Mint and openSuse on various hardware like Sony Viao and lenovo Thinpad. Currently I am running Ubuntu 13.10. I have had a few minor problems, mostly WiFi in the early years but those were quickly sorted by very helpfull persons on this forum. I have never experienced a crash.
Having said that I do understand your disappointment and frustration. Obviously something either went wrong with the installation or there is some hardware conflict. These problems can all be sorted I am sure. To get the help you need you would have to be somewhat more specific like:
What hardware are you using, are you dual booting wirh Windows, did you check the Md5 sum of the installation disk to ensure that the iso you burned was complete (no broken packages) etc., your bios settings- do you use "secure boot" and many more.
Do not loose faith, this is not an unfriedly forum, sometimes how quick you get an answer depends on the time of the day. People are all over the world and in different time zones and may be sleeping or working right now.
Once you get the hang of Linux you will see it was worth it.
