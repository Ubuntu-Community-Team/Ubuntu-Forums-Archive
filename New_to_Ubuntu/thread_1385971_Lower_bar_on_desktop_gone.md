### Post by fromthehill on 2010-01-20
right-click on the top bar
click on "new panel"

i will probably appear at the bottom of your screen

right click on the bottom bar
click "add to panel"

you need the "window list"
click add or drag it to the bar

this way you can also add the other things that are on the bottom-bar (trash, workspace-switcher, show desktop). or you can place them on the top bar (or wherever you want them)
