### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-10-21
If you look on the hp website you will see that the printer is supported by hplip v0.9.5, but only as a parallel port printer; I don't know even if it is available as usb connected.  You will certainly need to install hplip from the repos or direct from hp (I don't know if the version from hp is different than the ubuntu repos version) and it will also be wirth installing the hplip-gui, which gives you the hp-toolbox.
