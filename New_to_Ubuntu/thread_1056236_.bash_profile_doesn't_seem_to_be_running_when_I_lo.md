### Post by lswb on 2009-01-31
Are you talking about logging in to the gnome desktop? When gdm starts gnome, it does source your .profile (or .bash_profile) but your X server may not have started at that time, so evolution or other gui apps may not run from a .profile or .bash_profile script.

If your goal is to have evolution (or any gui or X app) start when you log in to the desktop, a better way is to use Main Menu/System/Preferences/Startup Programs, click on Add, then enter the command in the dialog that appears.
