### Post by tomBorgia on 2009-08-12
Sounds like you need to check you have cookies enabled - Go to your firefox preferences (Edit > Preferences) > Privacy.

Select "Accept cookies from sites". Its good practice to set "keep until I close Firefox" option for the sake of privacy but it's not critical. It'll also stop your Son seeing where you've been browsing.
