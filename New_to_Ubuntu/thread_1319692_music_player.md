### Post by devehf on 2009-11-09
Amarok is solid for updating music tags and artwork. I just upgraded to Karmic from Hardy, Amarok 1.4 to 2.2, and all my playlists are there and well and good. Amarok is pretty smart at letting you move your files around in your directory structure and maintaining pointers from the data in your collection to the new file location.

I also have an iPod classic 80GB. iPod syncing was not intuitive. I accidentally clicked on "toggle unified view mode" and this did not allow me to see that the iPod was connected.

Also if you want to copy or move files to the iPod, you need to right click on an album in the collection (or file or folder in file/folder view mode) and select copy or move to "ipod". I'm doing this now for the first time and it appears to be slower than I remember it being in 1.4.

It took me a while to figure this out. Had to get on freenode.net and chat with a programmer on #amarok.
