### Post by connorh123 on 2009-05-12
Type
```
sudo apt-get aumix
```
When it's done, open it up, and there will be all these weird ruler looking things. The first one that you'll be on will say V and look like a ruler. That's your volume. Press the right arrow-key to turn it all the way up, and their's your sound.
