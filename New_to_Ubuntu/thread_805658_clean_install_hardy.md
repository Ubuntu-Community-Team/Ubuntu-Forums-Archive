### Post by ajmorris on 2008-05-24
hi there,
Because of the way Hard disks are manufactured, and the way operating systems specify space, you will not get the full 250gb.

Hard disk manufacturers manufacture their hard disks with 1 gb being 1000 x 1000 x 1000 bytes, however, an operating system does it at 1024 x 1024 x 1024 bytes, meaning you dont get the full 250 gb.
