### Post by Hospadar on 2009-04-07
Try to isolate the cause of the problem?

Does this happen every restart? how often do you restart?  If you just turn your machine on and off how many cycles does it take to reproduce this.

There might be some wierd permission issues, or your session sometimes crashes when it is trying to shut down (And as such doesn't shut down cleanly saving all your settings).

It may be hard to really track down the problem, a re-install or upgrade might be your best bet.
