### Post by Bölvaður on 2009-09-01
if you have the window open that says "Buddy List" on top and has this status bar you describe but does not have anything between those two, then you have yet to log in (click Accounts and enable one of your accounts or add more)

If you dont get that window at all, try double click the pidgin icon in the task tray. Or right click it and select "show buddy list"
