### Post by nlz on 2008-07-29
battery charge monitor is way better than power manager. Especially if you are also using a scaling program such as powernowd or something like powertop.

The latter two programs you can use to let your laptop life longer when running on batteries. the first (powernowd) runs as a daemon and scales your CPU to usage. The second one disables power consuming stuff you don't use anyhow.
