### Post by Temposs on 2009-03-12
Are you quite sure that this bug is present in Ubuntu 8.10? I've not heard of this bug, and it looks like no one else has either...People run their hard drives perfectly well on Ubuntu for a long time. Hard drives naturally fail eventually because they have moving parts. Get a high quality SSD if you don't want your storage to fail.

You would know a click coming from a hard drive if it was happening. It sounds like flicking a thin piece of metal against another hard surface.
