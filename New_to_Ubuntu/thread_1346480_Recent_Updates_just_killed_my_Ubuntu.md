### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-12-05
If you install your graphics card drivers manually (by downloading an installer from [www.nvidia.com](www.nvidia.com) or [www.ati.com](www.ati.com)) then you must reinstall the driver after every kernel or Xorg update.

That's a very good reason to use the Hardware Drivers program, wherever possible, because it keeps your driver in sync with your kernel.
