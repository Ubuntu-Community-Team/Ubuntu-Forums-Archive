### Post by RLCGRN on 2010-10-16
Ever feel like all your efforts are akin to beating your head on the wall, only to discover that the solution was so easy you just want to beat your head on the wall?  That's me.  

> Does sound work in programs other than flash, like rhythmbox or when you watch videos from your computer?

I tried out rythmbox and an mp3 file separately with no sound.  Then it occurred to me - maybe there's a "software" volume control as opposed to the hardware knob on the speakers proper.  I looked around and there it was, a speaker icon on the top of the window.  It had three dashes following it (to my untrained eye representing sound should be coming out) but I clicked on it anyway.  Apparently those dashes mean MUTE! D'OH!!
I've got sound now.

Thanks!
