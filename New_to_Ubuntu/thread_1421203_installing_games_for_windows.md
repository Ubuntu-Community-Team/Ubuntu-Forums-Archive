### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-04
Before you got to all the trouble to install and configure Wine, you would do better to go to the WineHQ Application Compatibility site (link below) and do searches based on the game name and version you want to run.

A rating of Garbage means the game won't run -- no matter what you do. Ratings of Bronze or Silver mean you will have some work to do to get the game to run.  No rating means you're on your own.

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true")
