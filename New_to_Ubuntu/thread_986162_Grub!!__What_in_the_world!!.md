### Post by phidia on 2008-11-18
You need to boot from a live or install cd and re-set up grub.
See the guide [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GrubHowto")- the "Backup, repairing and re-installing Grub" section. Sometimes this happens because a change was made to bios or the drive configuration.
