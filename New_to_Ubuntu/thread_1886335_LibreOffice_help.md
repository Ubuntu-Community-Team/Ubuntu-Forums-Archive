### Post by FatAngus on 2011-11-24
When saving a file in Libre Office Writer to be opened in Micorsoft Word you need to select the drop down arrow to the right of filter and select Microsoft Word 97/2000/XP/2003(.doc).

---

### Post by DapperMe17 on 2011-11-24
You can also go to Libre Office settings, and under Tools-Options-General-Load/Save....you'll see a couple of boxes that you can navigate around in. 

You can set your Libre Office suite to "auto" save....for Windows capability, in Writer, you'll save (as indicated in a prior post) as a Windows (.doc), or you can also save at Real Text (.rtf).
