### Post by chadk5utc on 2012-12-24
not likely the OS damaged the speakers it is however possible that the sound card can or could have over driven or over powered them. In my experience crackling noise can be a damaged cone ie blown speaker or loose, dirty or damaged connectors, even bad wires. Also using sound system at 100% can wear speakers out over time and do the same thing.
