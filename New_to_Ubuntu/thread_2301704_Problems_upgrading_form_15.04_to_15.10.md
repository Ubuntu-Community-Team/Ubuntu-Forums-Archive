### Post by tea for one on 2015-11-07
It is possibly that the monitor and 15.10 are not working together correctly. Do you have a separate graphics card?

As you mentioned in your earlier posts, Ubuntu 15.04 functions OK then I would stick to using 15.04 for the time being.

You also wrote that you upgraded from 15.04 to 15.10, there are many reports of upgrade difficulties on these fora.

Then, I would download a fresh ISO of 15.10 and install it onto a USB stick as if you were installing to a hard drive (i.e. with a login and user account) By doing this, you, hopefully, may be able to reproduce the problem without the difficulties of a corrupt Ubuntu installation on your large SSDs.

Possible a fresh installation will solve your login difficulty as well as garbled text.

The beauty of Ubuntu is that it is often easier and less stressful to re-install rather than suffer the frustrations of trouble-shooting problems which are impossible for other users to reproduce.

Kind regards
