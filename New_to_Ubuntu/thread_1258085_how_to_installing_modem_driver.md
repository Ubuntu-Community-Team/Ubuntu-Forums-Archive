### Post by lkraemer on 2009-09-07
The first place to start is:
[http://www.linmodems.org/](http://www.linmodems.org/)
To download their script and see if you can find what chipset is 
installed on the modem.  Then subscribe to their forum (and send
an email IN PLAIN TEXT for support help).  You are looking at 3-4
days with lots of emails and questions.....is it worth the trouble?

The easiest thing to do is search Ebay for a US Robotics
Serial, External Hardware Modem, and be up and running in 30 minutes
or less.
