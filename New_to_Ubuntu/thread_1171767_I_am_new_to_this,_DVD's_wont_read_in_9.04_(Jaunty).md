### Post by -kg- on 2009-05-27
What enabled me to watch DVDs (perfectly, BTW) is to go to the [Medibuntu page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu"), install the repositories and key as instructed, then go further down the page, installing the libdvdcss2 library package, and then a little further down the page install the non-native codecs.

At first I was unable to get DVDs to play, but once I installed these packages they played without a hitch.
