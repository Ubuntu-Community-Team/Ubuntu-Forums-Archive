### Post by MrJoeyUK on 2008-07-24
If the download appears in the firefox browser in the 'Downloads' window you can right click the specific download and click Open Containing folder. To open the download window go tools > Downloads(within Firefox) or CTRL + Y.

As far as programs go, if it isn't in the menu, you can go Applications > Add/Remove, and browse through until you find it, this shows every program you have installed,it should be there, providing it is installed properly. 

Hope this helps.
