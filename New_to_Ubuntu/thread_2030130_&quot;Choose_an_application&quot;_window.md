### Post by Krytarik on 2012-07-20
> **sophie2cu2 said:**
> I installed qBitorrent which works really well when I do a search from there. But when I go to a specific site and click on a magnet to download a torrent I get a choice between Transmission and ... nothing else. When I click on "choose an application" I don't know where to look.
According to [packages.ubuntu.com]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/precise/i386/qbittorrent/filelist"), qBittorrent's executable is: "/usr/bin/qbittorrent". So just browse down to it in the file chooser dialog that pops up, and choose it.
