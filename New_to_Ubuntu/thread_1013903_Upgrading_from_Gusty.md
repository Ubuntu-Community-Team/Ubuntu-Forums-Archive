### Post by Tatty on 2008-12-22
On my laptop i keep a separate /home partition and two partitions of 10Gb.  One of the 10Gb partitions i have the root partition installed on.

When each ubuntu version is released I simply install it to the spare partition, sharing /home, so I have the option of booting back into the old release until i know that the new version works for me.

Then i simply keep the old release as a spare for disasters, until the next version comes out.
