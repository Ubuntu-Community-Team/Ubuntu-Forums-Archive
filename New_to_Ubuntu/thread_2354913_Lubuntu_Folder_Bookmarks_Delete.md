### Post by vasa1 on 2017-03-08
Just to expand on rburkartjo's point. I purposely created two bookmarks for the same folder called "New". You can see the bookmarks in *two* places:
[LIST]
[*]The dropdown as shown in the image you posted
[*]The Side Pane (with the "Places" heading) in the image named 1.png below.
[/LIST]
I've marked them in red boxes.

Now, if you want to delete a bookmark, the way I do it is to right-click on the bookmark in the side-pane, *not* in the Bookmarks dropdown. When you do as I suggest, you'll get a context menu with an option to delete the bookmark as in image 2.png.

All this assumes that you've enabled the Side Pane in View > Side Pane (or by appropriately toggling with *F9*)!

BTW, instead of hosting your image on a third-party site, you can click on the paper-clip icon just above the area where you're composing your post.
