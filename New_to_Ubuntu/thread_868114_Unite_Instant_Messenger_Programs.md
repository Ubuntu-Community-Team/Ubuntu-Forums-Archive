### Post by Xerp on 2008-07-23
Pidgin comes preinstalled and handles many instant messenger protocols.

    *  AIM
    * Bonjour
    * Gadu-Gadu
    * Google Talk
    * Groupwise
    * ICQ
    * IRC
    * MSN
    * MySpaceIM
    * QQ
    * SILC
    * SIMPLE
    * Sametime
    * XMPP
    * Yahoo!
    * Zephyr

So its just missing Skype...
