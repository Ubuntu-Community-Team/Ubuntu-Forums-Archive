The feature you are looking for is called persistence. If you've created your live USB using the USB creator tool in Ubuntu you can select how much MB's of your USB stick you want to dedicate to persistence. That is one way of doing it that works, but in my experience fails at some point in the long run.

Another option is to create an extra partition on your USB stick. One for your Ubuntu Live environment and the other for your data.
