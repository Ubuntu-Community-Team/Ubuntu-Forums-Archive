### Post by The Real Dave on 2010-05-04
The simplest thing to do is to take an image of the drive with [Clonezilla]("http://clonezilla.org/"). Then, if you don't like 10.04, you can restore 8.04 as if nothing ever happened. Clonezilla is a brillaint tool. Best way to do it is to download the latest ISO and burn a copy :)
