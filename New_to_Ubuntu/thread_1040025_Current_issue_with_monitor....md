### Post by HavocXphere on 2009-01-15
Sounds like a hardware issue to me. Specifically the power brick/battery setup. Most laptops reduce the brightness when the lose mains power. If something in the battery/brick/charging setup is flakey then it could cause that I suppose.

The other thing I've noticed is that the hinge between the screen and laptop is sometimes flakey. Check if it makes a difference if you change the angle.
