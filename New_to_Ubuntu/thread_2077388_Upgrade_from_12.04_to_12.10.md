### Post by DanR01 on 2012-10-28
If you are using 12.04 LTS you will not automatically be offered the option to upgrade. Open the software center and click settings in the bottom left. Change 'Notify me of a new LTS release' to 'Notify me of a new release'.

As to whether it is worth upgrading that is a personal choice. Personally, I'm going to stick with the LTS for now.

### Post by VE6EFR on 2012-10-28
Instead of doing an upgrade I would suggest backing up your data and doing a fresh install. There seems to be far less problems by doing a full install.

However, if you really want to attempt an upgrade go into software updater and click on settings. Where it says "notify me of a new Ubuntu version" select for any new version in the dropdown. That should let you know that there is an upgrade and allow you to install.
