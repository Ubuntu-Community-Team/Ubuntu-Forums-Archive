You could do this by replacing the trash can images *.svg with images of your own. 

Usually the icon themes are store in /usr/share/icons, so you will need sudo powers to change them. 

They need to be the same size, and renamed exactly the same as the images you are replacing. It's a messy job, because there are a lot of them and they are all different sizes. See here for a picture of what I mean... [screenshot]("http://imgur.com/IQQtO").
