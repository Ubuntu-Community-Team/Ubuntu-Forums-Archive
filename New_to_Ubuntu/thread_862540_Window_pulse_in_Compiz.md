### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-07-17
Open a terminal and press backspace...

Also, go into **Wobbly Windows** and enable "shiver" too.

That appears to get a satisfying pulsing of the windows...

As for other effects, I'm not too sure. I tend to only go light on my laptop (conserve power and all). But you may want to look it up in synaptic.

[EDIT]
A search for "compiz plugins" brings up the following

[B]compiz-plugins
compiz-fusion-plugins-main
compiz-fusion-plugins-extras
compiz-fusion-plugins-unsupported[/B]

Perhaps installing them all will give you the effects you need..
