### Post by mkvnmtr on 2009-03-22
I had a problem like that. After the time passed for the screensaver to come on  the screensaver would begin using a lot of cpu. As time went on more and more. It would bring on a freeze. I switched to just a blach screen and it used less resources but after some time it would freeze on the black screen. I set it to never go to sleep and never use a screen saver and this quit happening. It was on a couple of laptops and they began to get rather warm also. Never had it happen on my desktops. Never found another thread on the problem or any advise except a few people saying that screensavers use a lot of resources.
