### Post by BrockStrongo on 2010-08-12
Go to System>Administrator>Software Sources and make sure that the first four boxes in the first tab are marked. This should enable the main, universe, restricted, and multiverse repo's. 
Hope this solves your problem.

---

### Post by hankaaron88 on 2010-08-12
They were checked but I unchecked them and then rechecked them and went to Other Software and checked everything there too, and now it works. Thanks
