### Post by kanikilu on 2012-11-01
Is the issue that you only have a CD (not DVD) drive in that laptop? If so, just download the 12.04 LTS ISO, it should fit on a CD. Then if you want to upgrade to 12.10, you can.

Alternatively, look into using the "Minimal CD":

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/MinimalCD)
