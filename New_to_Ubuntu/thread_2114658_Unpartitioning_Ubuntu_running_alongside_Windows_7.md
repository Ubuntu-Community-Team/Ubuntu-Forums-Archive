### Post by El Tito on 2013-02-10
Once in ubuntu, if you open the file browser, you should be able to see a column (left side) with your home folder, documents, pictures ... and the devices that ubuntu detects. In my case it is the device called OS, click on it and normally go to c:\users\your_user_name\whatever_folder
