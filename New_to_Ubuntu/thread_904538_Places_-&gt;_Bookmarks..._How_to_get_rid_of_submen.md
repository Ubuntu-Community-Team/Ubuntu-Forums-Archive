### Post by scorp123 on 2008-08-29
I can't help you with the submenu ... But: You could open the "Nautilus" filemanager (Places > Home Folder) and check if all your location bookmarks are shown in the left menu pane (they should be). So instead of messing around with the "Places" submenu you could just open "Home Folder" right away and then easily access all your bookmarks from the left menu pane.
