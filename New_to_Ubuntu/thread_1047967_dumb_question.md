### Post by mcduck on 2009-01-23
I'd say that your bank just tries to tell you that you need to be able to read PDF documents (poor guys only seem to know about Adobe's reader). Whichever reader you use, they won't be able to know what it is. Or if you have any PDF reader at all. ;)

Ubuuntu includes a great PDF reader, Evince, by default. You'd be just as fine with it as you are using Adobe's slower program.

The warnings on Medibuntu web site are because their repositories also contains some multimedia codecs which have patent/licensing issues in USA. Almost everything in the repositories is still legal to use even if you live in USA. (And no, once again, nobody will be able to tell if you choose to install those codecs yourself. Even if they did, it's not likely that anybody would care. The warnings are just legal protection for the admins of Medibuntu website)
