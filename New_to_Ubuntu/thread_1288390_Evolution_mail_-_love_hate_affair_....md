### Post by donato roque on 2009-10-12
I've been using Evolution since I started using Ubuntu Linux and it is a love/hate affair for me too.  I did not want to give up on the relationship because it's important to me.  I got this computer for communication.  So whatever it is that's not working with Evolution I have to learn.  That's how I got into the underbelly of Ubuntu Linux. Forced me to understand why it's doing what.

It's now satisfying to use Evolution after all these years.  I believe Evolution is why I remain a Gnome DE guy.  Just the other day I tried sending a GPG encrypted email to a friend and it works.  I have multiple accounts (POP and IMAP), and free web mail that I thought I can't open in an email client, any email client.  It turns out there's freepops.  I also downloaded rss-evolution so I can read feeds in Evolution.  I don't open the browser often now and I believe it has made my computer secure. (no java, no flash, spam filters on and clamav installed).
