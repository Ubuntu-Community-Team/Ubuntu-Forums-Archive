### Post by Mazza558 on 2008-04-25
Yes, you can burn the CD from a Mac and then use it on your laptop. 

In terms of installing, usually you can just restart your PC with the CD in the disc drive and it'll load it from disc. You should choose the "Install Ubuntu" option that appears on the menu when you restart.

If 'there' means your Toshiba notebook -- then yes.  You can download the distribution to any machine, burn an ISO image disk, otherwise known as a -- Live CD -- and boot the Toshiba with that disk you've created.  Where you create the disc is irrelevant in the big picture.  ;)
