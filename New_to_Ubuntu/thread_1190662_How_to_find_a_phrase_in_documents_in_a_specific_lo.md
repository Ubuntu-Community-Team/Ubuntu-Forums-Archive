I assume that you are looking for a command-line tool. Also, "documents" is a rather general term, and, for lack of additional information, I will assume a text file.

There are a number of command-line tools that will do the job. Grep is probably the easiest to use:

[http://linuxcommand.org/man_pages/grep1.html](http://linuxcommand.org/man_pages/grep1.html)

Other options that you may wish to consider are discussed on this page:

[http://tldp.org/LDP/abs/html/textproc.html](http://tldp.org/LDP/abs/html/textproc.html)
