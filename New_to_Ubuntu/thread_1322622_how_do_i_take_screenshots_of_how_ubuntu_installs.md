### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-11-11
> **nitstorm said:**
> oh, wondering if i could do it without the virtual machine, coz i wanna get rid of windows completely..

Whenever you want to take a screenshot, just hit the Print Screen key on your keyboard (it might be marked with "SysRq"). This does depend on Gnome being loaded, so you can't take pictures until your computer has loaded up the desktop (you'd need to use a virtual machine to take screenshots before then, but of course you can install Ubuntu into a VM on Ubuntu).
