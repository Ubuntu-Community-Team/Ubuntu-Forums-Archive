### Post by nothingspecial on 2010-03-09
Hi Ian

Go to the page you want to link to.

Highlight the address, the thing in the top bar

```
http://ubuntuforums.org/blah/blag/1344=?/blah
``` then right click and choose copy

Then in your reply to the new thread, right click and choose paste.

### Post by Robster2 on 2010-03-09
In the search menu find "All your threads."

Find the thread and copy the contents of the navigation bar of your browser.
