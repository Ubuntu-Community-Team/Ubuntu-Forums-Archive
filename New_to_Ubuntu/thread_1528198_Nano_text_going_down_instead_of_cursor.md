### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-07-10
Hi,
I won't know the answer to the problem but I think I have a little advice that you could use. 

Does using the right arrow key allow you to move the cursor from left to right, then go down a line, and so on, without moving any text? OR, can you use the arrow keys on your num-pad part of the keyboard? (the 2,4,6 and 8 keys on the right side and assuming you have a num-pad on your keyboard)

"vi" (and "vim") are other command line only text editors that can be used as an alternative to "nano". Here is a cheatsheet that shows the commands for "vim" (which should also work with vi)  [http://www.tuxfiles.org/linuxhelp/vimcheat.html](http://www.tuxfiles.org/linuxhelp/vimcheat.html)
