### Post by chrisamiller on 2009-04-26
When you upgrade, your data should remain intact. Certain packages may get replaced by their newer versions, but in almost all cases, your user data is preserved.  For example, even if rhythmbox is updated to a new version, your music library will still be there.

Songbird isn't in the repos, so it definitely won't be touched.  As for your torrents, I'd be willing to bet that they'll be fine.

### Post by Michael.Godawski on 2009-04-26
That is true. 
Many users report successful upgrades, but a fresh install is just the safer way to "upgrade". If you have a separate /home partition, all your personal preferences will remain untouched. Just do not mark the /home partition for partitioning during the installation.

If you don't have a separate home partition, make a back-up, and go for it.
