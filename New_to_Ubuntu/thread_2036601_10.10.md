### Post by tartalo on 2012-08-02
1) Using your working Windows machine download something that can boot from CD and mout ext partitions, like for example: [http://releases.ubuntu.com/lucid/ubuntu-10.04.4-desktop-i386.iso](http://releases.ubuntu.com/lucid/ubuntu-10.04.4-desktop-i386.iso)

2) Burn the ISO in your Windows machine, using for example Imgburn: [http://www.imgburn.com/](http://www.imgburn.com/)

3) Boot your faulty PC with it ("Try without install" option), save the super-urgent documents to a Pendrive.
