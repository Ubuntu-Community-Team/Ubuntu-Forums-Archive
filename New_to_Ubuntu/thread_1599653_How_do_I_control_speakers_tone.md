### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-10-18
Hi,
Have you tried the Pulseaudio Graphical Equalizer? 
[http://www.unixmen.com/linux-tutorials/954-add-equalizer-for-pulse-audio-in-ubuntu-lucid-lynx](http://www.unixmen.com/linux-tutorials/954-add-equalizer-for-pulse-audio-in-ubuntu-lucid-lynx) 

I have bass and treble controls on my desktop speakers, and even though I have a subwoofer, the sound was still too dull/flat. Installing that EQ makes a big difference on my computer. I haven't tried it in Ubuntu or Kubuntu 10.10 yet, but it worked good in 10.04. PS. I remember the EQ being very touchy on my system, so if you notice that too, turn down the Preamp in the EQ so it won't be as touchy.

If you have any problems running it, WebUpd8 has a fixed version:
[http://www.webupd8.org/2010/09/download-pulseaudio-system-wide.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2010/09/download-pulseaudio-system-wide.html)

Hope this helps.
