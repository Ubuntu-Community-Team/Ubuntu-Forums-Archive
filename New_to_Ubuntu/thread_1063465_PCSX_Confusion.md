### Post by N4zgu1 on 2009-02-19
Ok, i will post my instructions once more (this time without warez)


Yes it is posible with pSX

1 First you need to join your parts, usually they are splitted as rar archives

2 You have to download the pSX emulator for linux, here is the link.

[http://psxemulator.gazaxian.com/](http://psxemulator.gazaxian.com/)

3 Install the dependencies if needed, (and shutdown compiz-fusion effects, at least in my case, i cannot run psx while fusion its running, use the compiz-switch program)

4 paste the Bios in the bios folder

Search for the bios in the internet it is called "SCPH1001.BIN"

5 Just open your game, idont remember which one you had to open, the bin or the cue, just try with both
