### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-03-29
What version of Ubuntu do you have? I shall assume it is 13.10. So, if the instructions are different it is not my fault. Put your pictures into the Pictures folder. Then open System Settings>Appearance. In the Look pane and on the left side you will see the default selection of wallpapers. Above that will be a panel that Says Wallpapers. It is actually a drop down menu. Select Pictures Folder as the location of the wallpapers and that is it, done.

That is the simple way.
