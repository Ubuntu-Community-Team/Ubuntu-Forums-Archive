### Post by YuiDaoren on 2010-05-07
I'm afraid that's just the new behavior of Rhythmbox's integration with the "Indicator Applet". You can turn this off by turning off the "Status Plugin" plugin. (edit -> plugins) You lose pop-up notifications, though.

If you're just looking for a more convenient access to stop/play buttons, try out "Music Applet", which will put controls and other info on one of your panels.

---

### Post by rf9rider on 2010-05-07
