Most broadband modems I know of require you to log into their web interface with a _separate_ user-name and password to that of your broadband account. I seriously doubt the user-name and password for your ISP will be accessible directly from Linux itself. Basically, the answer is **no**. It would be a very poor security practice on the part of the modem manufacturer if you could.

If you can log into your ISP's site and check your e-mail, that will be the same user-name and password as is stored in your broadband modem.
