### Post by dzponce11 on 2012-04-18
If you have a USB device, you can go to pendrivelinux.com and download their Live USB installer tool, then, provided you have wine or are running windows, choose "Old syslinux" on the dropdown menu on the application. After you have done that, if you have the .iso file downloaded, choose the .iso and just follow it's instructions.

**NOTE**
Your computer MUST have Legacy USB support to be able to do this.
