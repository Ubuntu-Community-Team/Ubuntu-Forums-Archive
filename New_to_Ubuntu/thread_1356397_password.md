### Post by Temposs on 2009-12-16
it should take only a second to set a password. To change your password in Ubuntu, you do System->Administration->Users and Groups. In there you click on your user, then click Properties, then click Change Password.

If you're setting your password for the first time, it should have taken care of that during the installation/setup process when you first created your user.
