### Post by kimda on 2009-11-18
Backup all your valueble data from Vista. I would copy all that stuff to an external drive (like email, bookmarks etc..). Also if you use certain email programs and you want to keep your emails you might want to read up on that.
Ubuntu has no problems with accessing ntfs data. Its just another filesystem only different from ext3, ext4. 

Another idea is buying a new disk and build it into your PC and install Ubuntu on this new disk. Then you can always access Vista if you want to. Ubuntu will recognise it and you will be able to dual boot.
