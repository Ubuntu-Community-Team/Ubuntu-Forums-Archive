### Post by pi.boy.travis on 2009-04-04
Double check your settings in System > Preferences > Sound  and check Multimedia systems selector.  If it isn't there, you can enable it by right clicking the menu, and selecting Edit Menus.  MAky sure you are using ALSA or PulseAudio, and not OSS.
