### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-03-30
When you get to the first screen of option 'Try Ubuntu' 'Install Ubuntu' etc, along the bottom there are a row of selections. Hit F6 and you should find the option:

nomodset

Choose it and continue with the install. If this still fails, repeat process but this time choose the option:

acpi=no
