### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-21
To answer your original question -- NO.

Some optical drives and some CD burning software support something know as overburn -- which WILL allow you, in some cases, to burn more than 700MB on a CD.

But -- that is a risky operation, and attempting anything over 720MB is doomed to failure.
