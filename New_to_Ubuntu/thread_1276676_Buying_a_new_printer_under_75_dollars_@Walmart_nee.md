### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-09-27
Many Epson work well also, but if you print very little there is a good chance that the print heads/jets will dry out and become blocked.  You can spend a long time and a lot of ink just getting them going again.  The problem is that Epson printer heads are not an integral part of the ink cartridge, and are not, therefore, renewed when you change ink cartridge, like most other makes.  However the ink refills are very cheap in comparison, so perhaps that is part of the decision equation you have to make.  Personally, I would go with an HP.  Their software is extremely good, and the package hplip-gui is the best I have seen for a printer maker on linux.
