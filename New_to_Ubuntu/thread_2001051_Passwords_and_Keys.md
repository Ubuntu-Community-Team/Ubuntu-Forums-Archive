### Post by orange2k on 2012-06-10
Have no fear, you didn't upload anything...

Passwords and keys is an application for managing keys and passwords on your computer. There you can create and edit for example your personal PGP key so you can encrypt e-mails you send to other people. Your password is safe...:)

### Post by orange2k on 2012-06-11
> **KID1410 said:**
> Thank You !
The application have a sync function. What is it used for ?

It is used to upload your public keys to the servers, but it is for PGP public keys only - your login pasword or any other password won't be uploaded. 

If you are interested in cryptography, you should read more on wikipedia: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Public-key_cryptography](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Public-key_cryptography)
