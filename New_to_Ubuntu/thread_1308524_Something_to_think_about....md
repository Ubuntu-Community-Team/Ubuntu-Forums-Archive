### Post by mesmith on 2009-10-31
After looking through the forum over the past 3 releases there are recurrent themes.  The most prominent seems to be "this didn't work for me, how do I go back to n.nn."  How about screen 1 of an install gives a warning, followed by some suggestions as to backing up a system?  Perhaps links to partimage and clonezilla with an explanation of the difference.

I know that there are plenty of suggestions sprayed around the forum, plus the release notes, but maybe a warning on screen 1 of the install would make people stop and think.  Especially if it gave some suggestions as to readily available software that could solve so many problems.
