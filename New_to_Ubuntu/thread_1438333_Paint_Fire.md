### Post by themusicalduck on 2010-03-24
You could disable compiz while you change the setting back. Go to System > Preferences > Appearance. On the Visual Effects tab, select None. Then set it back when you want it back to normal.

(by the way, you will lose a custom configuration of compiz unless you have simple-ccsm installed first.)
