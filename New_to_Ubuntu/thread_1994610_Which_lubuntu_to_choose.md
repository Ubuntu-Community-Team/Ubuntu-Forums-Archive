### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2012-06-03
You will have to burn the image to a CD or a flash drive before you can install it to your hard drive.  After you download you will need to check the Md5Sum to ensure that you have a good download.  See these sites.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

Then you can burn the ISO to the medium of your choice.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)

That should help you along.  Try Lubuntu out before you install to ensure that everything works before you make the commitment.
