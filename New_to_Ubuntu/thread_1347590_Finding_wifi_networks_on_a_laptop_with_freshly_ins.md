### Post by Defiant Rat on 2009-12-06
I might be stating the obvious, but is the wifi switched on? Most laptops have a switch and/or a function button to switch the wireless on and off (e.g mine has a switch on the front and FN + F8 also turns the wireless on and off although for me that doesn't work in Ubuntu)

Also, there should be a network icon on the panel in the top right. Try right clicking on it and checking the "Enable _W_ireless" box.

### Post by cartisdm on 2009-12-06
After a fresh install, you should see in the upper right hand corner the wireless network.  Simply clicking on it should display the available networks.  If you do you not have this you might either:

1. Have a card that isn't supported by ubuntu out-of-the-box (which likely can be solved with some simple adjustments

or

2. It could be as simple as switching on your wireless connection via shortcut keys on your keyboard
