### Post by forger on 2008-06-23
> **btermeli said:**
>  When I zoom in quality of all jpgs and gifs  are lost.

firefox 3 has image zoom enabled by default, to disable it:
- in the address box (where you type your [www.example.com](www.example.com) to go to a site), type: **about:config**
- search for: **browser.zoom.full**
- double click to change its value to "false"
