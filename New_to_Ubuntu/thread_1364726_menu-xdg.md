### Post by barnaclebill18 on 2009-12-26
This is a great application that I just learned about for the first time after starting with Ubuntu about a year ago.

I would install a little application, say xmag, a screen magnifier and then I could never find it or have to use the terminal to open it.

Sometimes I couldn't remember the name or forgot it was installed, not real important stuff, mostly gadgets to make things easier or something I did not use very often.

I installed menu-xdg and extra-menu-xdg then edited my Application Menu and put the Debian launcher on it.

Now I can easily open all those forgotten apps and a whole bunch of others I did not even know were installed.

I really like Ubuntu and all it stands for and this forum is truly the best forum I have ever read,

I want to thank everybody that has helped a computer dummy like me have a great Ubuntu experience and to wish everyone a Happy Holidays.
