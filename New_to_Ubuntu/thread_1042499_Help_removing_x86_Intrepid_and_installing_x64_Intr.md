### Post by logos34 on 2009-01-17
You'll have to do a fresh install of x64.  Just overwrite 32-bit intrepid.  If you have lots of files and want to preserve settings, prefs, etc., consider moving /home folder to a separate partition beforehand (see link my signature), then following [this howto]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/UpgradeFromOldVersion") (->'Clean install').
