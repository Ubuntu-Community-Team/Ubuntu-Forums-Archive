### Post by DuckHook on 2013-02-14
And make sure that you burned the ISO properly to the DVD. Just came off a 50-post thread where the OP could not install and no one could figure out why until it occurred to someone that the OP may have just copied the ISO image to his DVD without actually burning it. To burn, you must translate the ISO image into actual directories and files on your DVD. Just dragging and dropping the ISO onto a DVD won't work. You must use the DVD burning software of your current OS and tell it to convert the ISO image into a proper data DVD.
