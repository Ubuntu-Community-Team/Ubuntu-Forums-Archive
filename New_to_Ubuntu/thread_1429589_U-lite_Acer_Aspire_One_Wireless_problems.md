### Post by Son of William on 2010-03-14
Sorry, no solutions but here are some troubleshooting ideas:

1) Can you see any wifi connections?  Try taking it to a public library or a similiar spot with known wifi coverage.  That will rule out if it is a problem only with your current wifi network.

2) I assume your AspireOne had some form of Windows on it when you bought it.  Did it connect then? If so you can assume it is not a hardware error.

3) Try removing and reinstalling the drivers.

Good luck.
