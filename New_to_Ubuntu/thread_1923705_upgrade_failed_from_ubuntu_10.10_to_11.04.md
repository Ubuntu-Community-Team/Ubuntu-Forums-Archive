### Post by Scott Baker on 2012-02-11
A couple of things here. First, like Microsoft, as Ubuntu versions advance, they require more "machine" to do the job ( it's like trying to install Win7 on a 20 year old Acer. It may happen, but you WON'T be happy with the outcome ). Provide us with some machine specs and that will help. Next, I'm running 11.10 on a machine that's 7 years old ( a Lenovo/IBM ) and while it works, it's a bit pokey at times, but it DOES work. Fresh installs seem to work the best. Just make sure that you are plugged into the internet ( ethernet ) when you do your updates/upgrades, as opposed to trying to do updates/upgrades wirelessly. ( That way, you don't have an issue with possible data loss, and package damage ). Give us some specs, and let us know how all is going.  ](*,)
