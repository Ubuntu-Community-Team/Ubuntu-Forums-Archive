### Post by Cypher on 2009-01-23
If you want to wipe out your HD and do a fresh install without keeping any of your configuration on your current Gutsy installation, then grab the Intrepid Ibex (8.10, newer than Hardy) LiveCD, burn it and boot your computer with it. Once in the GUI, you will see the Install button on the desktop that will install to the HD.
