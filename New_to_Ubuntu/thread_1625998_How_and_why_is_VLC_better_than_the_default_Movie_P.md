### Post by JustinR on 2010-11-19
Its special because:

1. It works on Windows/MacOS/Linux
2. It supports pretty much every video and audio format available (MOV files play fine with me)
3. You can access web streams (video/audio)
4. You can access hardware like your webcam/tv-tuner with it.

There's a lot you can do with the software, it also has things like an equalizer and playlist support. Its also lightweight, it doesn't use many system resources, and plays full HD great.
