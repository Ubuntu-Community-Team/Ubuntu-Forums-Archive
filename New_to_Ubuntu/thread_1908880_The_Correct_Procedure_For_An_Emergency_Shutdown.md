### Post by jockyburns on 2012-01-14
Ctrl +Alt + Del should bring up a window with options for shutting down, restarting logging off, hibernating your computer, or cancelling the action. Once this window is open, if you select nothing, your computer will shutdown in 60 seconds. 
If it were an emergency situation, I'd be tempted to hold the start button on your computer for about 10 seconds.
