### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-05-27
Hi,
Alot of people simply refer to them as "tty's", so if you ever the phrase "drop to tty" you'll know what they are talking about.:)

Teletype (TTY) display: 
[http://linux.about.com/cs/linux101/g/teletypelpartty.htm](http://linux.about.com/cs/linux101/g/teletypelpartty.htm)

Each teletype has a run level assigned to it too, which is what the F1-F7 keys are for.
[http://www.debianadmin.com/debian-and-ubuntu-linux-run-levels.html](http://www.debianadmin.com/debian-and-ubuntu-linux-run-levels.html)
Hope that helps.
