### Post by ad_267 on 2009-06-20
Is there an opera in the menu? If there is you can drag this onto the panel or the desktop. Otherwise you can right click on the desktop and select "Create launcher" then enter Opera for the name and opera for the command.

---

### Post by snakeman21 on 2009-06-20
I'll just go a little more in depth on what ad_267 said.  Go to Applications > Internet and click and drag Opera to the Desktop or panel.  Alternatively, right-click on your desktop and choose "Create Launcher".  Make the title "Opera" and the command "opera".  Then choose your image for the icon.  You may want to download a decent Opera icon.
