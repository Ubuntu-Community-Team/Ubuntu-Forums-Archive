### Post by skrållarn on 2008-09-02
soundcards are often badly shielded from interference. try to move cables ,inside your computer, away from soundcard or onboard soundchip. both power cables and SATA/PATA cables generate interference. try also to raise the system volume in your OS and lower the volume on the external amplifier.
