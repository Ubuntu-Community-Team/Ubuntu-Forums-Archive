### Post by Qrikko on 2008-06-20
in firefox:

Edit | Preferences
Advanced and under browsing you find: "Use autoscrolling".

autoscrolling is what you are looking for :)

### Post by kk0sse54 on 2008-06-20
Auto scrolling is definitely what you are looking for and if you want to take it one step further search Mouse Gestures under Add-ons and you will be able to control the entire browser by just dragging your mouse in different directions.
