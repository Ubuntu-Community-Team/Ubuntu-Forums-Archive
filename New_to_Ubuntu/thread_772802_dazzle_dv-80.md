One problem you are definitely going to have is getting it running in Wine.  Wine seems like the logical choice to most people for a lot of Windows things that they haven't been able to get working in native Linux.  Trouble is, most of those are USB related, such as your Dazzle DVD80.  Wine doesn't support USB devices.
