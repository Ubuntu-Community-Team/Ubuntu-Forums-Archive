### Post by anewguy on 2012-06-10
Here's the best advice myself and many others can give you and others in similar circumstances:

using google, yahoo, what ever search engine you prefer, do a search with:

ubuntu <version> <your PC>

so in your case:

ubuntu 12.04 Fujitsu LH531

In your case, MANY posts are returned.  You'll need to do what we do in order to answer questions:  read those posts and look for things that will be problems, and look for their solutions.

If that returns nothing, then change the word ubuntu to linux:

linux 12.04 Fujitsu LH531
