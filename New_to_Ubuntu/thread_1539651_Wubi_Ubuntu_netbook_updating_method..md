### Post by cjack007 on 2010-08-07
well its been months now and i am on a wubi install of ubuntu 10.04 and it updates flawlessly. i think that some how your update was interrupted 
which broke your installed packages. i recommend that you check your power options and temporarily disable any sleep and hibernate and also lock screen on screensaver and icrease your idle timeout from 5min(default) to 2 hrs (max) this way you will have nothing interrupting your update.
there is no separate mechanism for updating an wubi install and by the way ubuntu does not turn off your comp. after an update so something must have have gone wrong with the update
but i still recommend that you sit and watch the entire update process and report any error that occurs during the update.
