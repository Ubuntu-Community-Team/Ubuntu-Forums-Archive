### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-08-04
I doubt there is anything wrong with the graphics card or the driver in your particular case. There is a more general problem of Linux traditionally not being a gaming platform and suffering from the lack of attention  as far as graphical performance goes. Just look at benchmarks comparing FPS on Linux and Windows, Linux would usually be slower, especially with old Intel GPUs.
