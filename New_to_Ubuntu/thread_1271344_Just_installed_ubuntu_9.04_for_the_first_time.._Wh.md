### Post by slakkie on 2009-09-20
If you want to be a realy Linux/Unix master, learn to master the command line. Read man-pages, play around, break stuff and don't be afraid to ask questions on forums and on irc. 

You might wonder why I say break stuff? If you encounter issues, you will have to look for an answer and you will read documentation, forcing you to understand what you did wrong and how you can fix it. It is like a little kid that breaks its toy in order to see how it is made and too see if he can fix it again. 

But the real first thing to do:

Download [clonezilla](http://www.clonezilla.org) backup your current OS and have fun.
And read the wiki, [https://help.ubuntu.com](https://help.ubuntu.com).

Best of luck.
