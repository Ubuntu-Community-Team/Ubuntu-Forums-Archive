### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-02-28
Also realize that with commercial movie DVDs you have two other complications ...

First, the standard commercial DVD is aroung 9GB, not 4GB.  So, to make a full copy of it, not only will you need 9GB disks (which are generally known as dual-layer (DL)), you will also need a DL burner.

Second, it's not unusual for commercial DVDs to be protected by DRM -- which will interfere with your ability to copy it.

Don't know how to bypass the second, but I believe there's something you can install in Ubuntu that will do that.
