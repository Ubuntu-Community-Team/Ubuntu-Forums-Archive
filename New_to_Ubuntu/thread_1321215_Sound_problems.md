### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2009-11-14
I haven't tried this in 9.10 because my sound has been working fine.  But it has helped in past versions of Ubuntu.

Open synaptic and search for alsa.  Install alsa-firmware, alsa-firmware-loaders, and alsa-mixer-gui.

Go to System > Preferences > Sound and set everything to Alsa.

You may have to restart, I can't remember for sure.
