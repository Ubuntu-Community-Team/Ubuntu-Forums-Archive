### Post by adobrakic on 2008-12-07
How do I make the page actually landscape? I go to print options and set it to landscape, but all it does is print how the text is written on portrait, but sideways (landscape).

---

### Post by jimmy the saint on 2008-12-07
Format>page>[page tab]
Under the size options is an option for landscap or portrait.  Chage the orientation through there, not through the printing window.  That way, the program knows to reflow the document.
