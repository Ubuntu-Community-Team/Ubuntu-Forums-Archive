### Post by HermanAB on 2012-09-29
Well, if you really need to format your heart drive, then I suggest that you go and see a doctor post haste...

Formatting a disk drive is a Windowsish term.  In Linuxish, it is called Make File System, or mkfs.  You should not need to do it more than once in the lifetime of a disk patition, but if you insist, you can do it as many times as you like.  Your patience will wear out, long before the disk wears out.
