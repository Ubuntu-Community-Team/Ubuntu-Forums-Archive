### Post by hansolo4949 on 2011-02-27
This sounds EXACTLY like you are overheating your pc. The next time you boot it, listen. Do you hear the fans coming on at all between boot and the lockup? if you don't then Ubuntu isn't activating your fans, due to a software incompatibility or bug. I would suggest using another pc (if you have one) to look for a solution to make the fans at least come one, before you start trying to fine-tune it.
