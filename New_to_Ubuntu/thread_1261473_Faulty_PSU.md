### Post by earthpigg on 2009-09-08
coudn't hurt to run memtest86 and maybe badblocks as well.

have you moved your computer around lately? something inside could have just come lose. 

make sure you unplug your computer and protect your machine from static electricity if you open it to check.... touch the PSU or case before you touch anything else, and thereafter every 10 seconds or so... or just rest a hand on the case/PSU.
