### Post by Waappu on 2009-09-18
Hi,

Yes, it is good idea have /home in different partition. It make life easier if you need re-install
But 320GB is quite mutch for / I think.

I would partion e.g. 40GB ~60Gb for / and rest of that disk to /home.
Then mount another disk to e.g. /media/storage to hold extra data you have
