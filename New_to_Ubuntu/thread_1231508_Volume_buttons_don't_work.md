### Post by CatKiller on 2009-08-04
System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Sound. The bit at the bottom, that says "Default Mixer Tracks," controls which channel gets controlled by the keyboard buttons.

You also want to make sure that you've got the buttons listed appropriately in Keyboard Shortcuts for Volume up and Volume down (just click in the box and press your button), but you imply that you've already checked that.
