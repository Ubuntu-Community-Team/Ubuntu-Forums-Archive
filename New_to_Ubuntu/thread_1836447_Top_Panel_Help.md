### Post by mr_luksom on 2011-08-31
Looks like the theme has changed. 

To change it to something else, open the menu by clicking on the Ubuntu icon, then click 'More Apps'. Change 'All Applications' to 'Themes and Tweaks', and select Appearance. 

Alternatively, type Appearance into the search bar.

You can then select a new theme to suit the desired aesthetics.
