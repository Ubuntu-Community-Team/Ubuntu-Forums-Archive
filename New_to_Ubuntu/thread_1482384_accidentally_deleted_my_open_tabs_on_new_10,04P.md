### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-05-14
Hi,
In Kubuntu you need to add a widget to the panel called "Task Manager". 

Right click on your panel and select Panel Options > Add Widgets. You will see a bar full of widgets open up. There is a search bar on the left, use it to search for "Task Manager". When you find it, double click on it to add it to the panel. 

To move the Task Manger to the correct location, right click on the panel again and select Panel Options > Panel Settings. When you see that open up, drag the Task Manager to where you like it.

In Kubuntu, it is kind of easy to accidentally remove something from the panel, so it's best to keep them locked, until we need to add or move something on the panel. So Right click on the panel again, then select Panel Options > Lock Widgets.

Hope this is what you were looking for. :)
