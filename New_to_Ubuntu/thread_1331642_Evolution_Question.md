### Post by mprince on 2009-11-23
Not sure if you are aware of this, but you can press 'Ctrl+I' or check *View-> Load Images* in the evolution menu. That should download and show images for the current message, but you have to do it for each message. I would think that evolution would do it automatically based on what you have selected as a preference.
