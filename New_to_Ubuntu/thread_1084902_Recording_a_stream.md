### Post by perspectoff on 2009-03-03
Use the Video downloader plugin for Firefox. You can save video from many websites, including Flash video (which most of the content on YouTube is).

The VLC mediaplayer allows you to save streams from anywhere. It is a kick-axx media player!

FFMPEG allows you to convert video formats. Learn about FFMPEG. 

You can convert flash video, for example, to MPEG-4, MPEG-2, AVI, WMV, or whatever, using FFMPEG. 

You can also just save the MP3 or AAC part of a Flash Video using FFMPEG.

Some tips are available at 

Ubuntu Guide ([http://ubuntuguide.org](http://ubuntuguide.org))
 or
Kubuntu Guide ([http://kubuntuguide.org](http://kubuntuguide.org))
