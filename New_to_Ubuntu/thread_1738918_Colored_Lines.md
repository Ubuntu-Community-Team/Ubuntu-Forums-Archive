### Post by Ghost|BTFH on 2011-04-27
#1: You're running an ATi video card in that laptop - it could very well be the driver, depending on what version you have installed, etc.

#2: Compiz + Games = unpredictable results.

Sometimes games will run smooth as silk with Compiz active, other times it can cause:

* Artifacts
* Funky colors/graphics
* Game crashes
* System crashes

And pretty much everything in between.  The first thing I would recommend (since it's the easiest) is to go into System -> Preferences -> Appearance -- Visual Effects tab and turn off visual effects completely (select None).

Then try running Tux Racer.  If it works, problem solved.  If it doesn't, look at video drivers next and perhaps look up what the best driver version is for your particular card.

Cheers,
Ghost|BTFH
