### Post by Bradtek on 2009-05-25
It's possible you deleted your Window List from the panel.

Right click on the panel
Add to panel
Select Window List
Click Add

See if that fixes it

---

### Post by djyoung4 on 2009-05-25
thats exactly what it was thanks for the help

### Post by bcoffield on 2009-05-25
Hi,

There's a couple possibilities I can think of... One is that they are minimizing to your tray (windows term i guess).  Do you see a wireless/wired networking icon down there?  If so then this part of your tray is there.  Unless I'm wrong, that is "Notification Area" in your add to panel list.

The other is if you accidentally removed your window list from your panel....  It is "Window List" in the add to panel options.  

Also, when you hit alt-tab are your applications still there and running?
