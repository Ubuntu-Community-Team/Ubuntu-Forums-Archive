### Post by madmax75 on 2011-03-31
OMG Ubuntu:

[http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/)

Web Upd8:

[http://www.webupd8.org/](http://www.webupd8.org/)

Make Tech Easier has Linux articles quite frequently:

[http://maketecheasier.com/](http://maketecheasier.com/)

These are the first ones that comes to mind. Just google for "Ubuntu Linux blog news review" or something like that and you should find plenty more :)

Oh, and this:

[http://www.thecodingstudio.com/opensource/linux/screenshots/](http://www.thecodingstudio.com/opensource/linux/screenshots/)

This site has nice screenshots of various Linux distros, so you can see what different and upcoming distros look like by default etc.

Don't forget Distrowatch - a great place to keep updated about what's happening in distro world, also plenty of links to reviews etc.

[http://distrowatch.com/](http://distrowatch.com/)
