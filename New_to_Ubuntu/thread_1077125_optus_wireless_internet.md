### Post by dastasha on 2009-02-22
Which modem are you using? The huawei e220 or e169?

In Ubuntu 8.04 I use a package called Vodafone Mobile Connect (VMC). You could google for VMC... get it from a site called vodafone betavine. I reckon you would just change some settings (APN etc) for optus.

Kubuntu 8.10, Knetwork automatically found my e220.  All I had to add was the APN detail for my network provider and I was away.

Either that or you will be mucking with the WVdial.conf file...

There are a few threads on this.
Just google details appropriate to yourself.
Follow links back to this forum... The people here are really helpful...and generally much more knowledgeable than myself;)
