### Post by bioterror on 2010-10-31
Install Windows and then ubuntu on the new one.
If possible, keep all 3 at the same time attached to your computer and move data from both drives to your new.

After that format your two previous drives.
Use ext4 or XFS or what ever you want and what ever is your need.
