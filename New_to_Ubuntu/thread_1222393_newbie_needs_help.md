### Post by mapes12 on 2009-07-25
Unfortunately, there is no system restore in Ubu presently. If everything worked OK before you intalled the driver it may be easier to go back and do a fresh install. If you only need UBU on your machine then it doesn't matter much if you break things a few times. I certainly have! But if you have a dual boot machine (e.g. windoze) then be careful you don't lose your other OS in the process............easier said than done :oops:
