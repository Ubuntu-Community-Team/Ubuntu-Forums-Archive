### Post by Mike_Walsh on 2014-08-29
Hello, david.

When you say 'the startup bar', are you referring to the launcher on the left side of the Unity desktop?

Drag-and-drop doesn't work with Unity in quite the same way as it does with Windows. A better way to place items on the launcher is to locate your application in The Dash (the symbol at the very top of the launcher), and once they have opened, locate the icon in the launcher, right-click it, and select 'pin to launcher'. It will then remain on there.

Hope that helps.

Regards,

Mike.
