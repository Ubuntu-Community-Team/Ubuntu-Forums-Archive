### Post by timcredible on 2008-10-29
not all dvd players play burnable media (dvd-r and dvd+r).  do a search and you'll find that dvd-r discs play in approximately 94% of dvd players, and dvd+r discs play in about 89% of dvd players.  remember, burning dvds are only faking the dvd creation process - the real process involves putting pits in a layer of plastic and then covering that layer with another layer of plastic.  all burnable discs do is discolor ink in between 2 layers of plastic, it doesn't create a 'real' dvd.  so, devede has nothing to do with it, it's the physical media.  also, as others pointed out, it could be that the dvd disc is of poor quality, causing issues.
