### Post by Celauran on 2009-07-02
I'd start with the basics. Check the lights on your modem. Check that your computer has an IP assigned (ifconfig). If all the correct lights are on and you have an IP, ping your router. If you're getting ping replies, try pinging an outside site (Google, for example). If you get a ping back from Google, then you are connected and the problem is with your browser. If you do not get a ping back from Google, call your ISP again.
