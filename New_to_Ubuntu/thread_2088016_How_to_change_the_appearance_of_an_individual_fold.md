### Post by coffeecat on 2012-11-25
Right-click on the folder you want a custom icon for and choose "Properties". Top-left of the small properties window (in the Basic tab) is the orange folder icon - just to the left of Name:. Click on the folder icon and a "Select Custom Icon" window opens. Browse to where your custom icon is, select it and click on "Open". Voilà!
