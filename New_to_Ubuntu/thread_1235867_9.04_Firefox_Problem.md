### Post by Wyphy on 2009-08-10
In the address bar type:
```
about:config
```
Agree to the warning (after reading it, of course).
In the filter bar type:
```
browser.urlbar.autoFill
```
It should say 'False', so double click the entry and it will change to 'True'.
Your autocomplete is enabled again.
