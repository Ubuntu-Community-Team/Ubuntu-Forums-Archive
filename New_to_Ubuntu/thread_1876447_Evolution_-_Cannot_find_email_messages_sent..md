### Post by dazvansgillio on 2011-11-06
Have you checked the Save folder in Account settings. Go to Edit>Preferences and select your e-mail account. Then go to Edit account and check the Default settings from the Tab at the top. It should tell you where the default folder is for Draft and Sent messages.

Dazvansgillio.
