### Post by IWantFroyo on 2011-08-18
If you have your own domain you want to install it on, your hosting provider should have documentation. Mine (iPage) even installs it for me if I click a button.

If there's no documentation available from your provider, [here]("http://codex.wordpress.org/Installing_WordPress")'s Wordpress's official guide.

Alternatively, you could go to [www.wordpress.com](www.wordpress.com) and sign up. It's free, provided you let there be a .wordpress.com in your domain name.
