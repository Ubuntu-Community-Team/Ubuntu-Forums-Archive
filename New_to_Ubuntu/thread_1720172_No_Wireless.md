### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-04-02
Welcome to the forums!

The first thing I'd do is plug the computer into a wired internet connection, then check for hardware drivers, System -> Administration -> Hardware Drivers. If you have a Broadcom device, choose the newer SDA drivers over the older B43 drivers.

Once you install the drivers, test your wireless capabilities and see if you are still having problems.
