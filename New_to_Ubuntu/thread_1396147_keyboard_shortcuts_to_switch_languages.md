### Post by BlueGene1402 on 2010-02-28
[FONT=Comic Sans MS][SIZE=2]Hello from another new user who has only had Xubuntu for 3 days lol

This worked for my Karmic Koala & I can now quickly & comfortably switch between English & Arabic in all applications through my chosen keyboard shortcut. Hope it helps :)

 Right click on the top panel.
 Choose "Add new items..."
 Choose "Keyboard Layouts" (Keyboard layouts setup & switch plugin) & Add it.
 When it appears right click & go to Properties. It's very easy from here on..
You can add languages from the "Keyboard Layouts" button then choose your preferred keyboard shortcut from Change layout option.

Good luck!
