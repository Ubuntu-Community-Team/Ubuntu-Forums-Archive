### Post by 3rdalbum on 2014-06-14
In Transmission, go to the Edit menu and come down to Preferences.

Go to the Desktop tab. Click on "Show Transmission icon in the notification area".
This does what you want.

It is not enabled by default because it is inconsistent behaviour - 95% of other programs close when you close the last window. To have some programs stay open in the notification area after their last window closes is quite confusing to users, that's why it's not enabled by default in any preinstalled Ubuntu programs.
