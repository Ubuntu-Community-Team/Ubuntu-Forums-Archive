### Post by larjan on 2012-02-02
Though there are certainly many things that can cause problems on an install, I can't help but wonder how many of them are just caused buy a bad download or cd burn. I have had two installs that had various issues, and in both cases, the md5 checksum was wrong. I didn't think to check it until after the fact. In one case I had fixed most of the problems, but was curios as to why I had them in the first place. So, if you check your download ( before you burn it or load it to a flash drive) and check it again, after you burn it, you could save yourself alot of aggravation
