### Post by davidr700 on 2011-03-22
After a recent new install of Ubuntu 10.10 desktop I had a couple issues that actually turned out to be rather minor. I would like to share my fix for the following issues: 

The first issue  I was unable to hear sound from speakers. It turned out my sound was turned off by default. 
Tip: If you don't have sound, don't panic, just make sure your sound is turn on. Just 'double click' the sound icon on the panel. :D

My second issue was that I had sound but could not view videos on Youtube. Also, page scrolling  was  very slow and bunched up the text. After some searching I went to a Youtube site that gives you a work around fo this issue that worked for me. 
Tip: If you have sound but can't see the video and experience poor page scrolling, go to [www.youtube.com/swf_test.html]("http://www.youtube.com/swf_test.html"), right click  on the video and find settings tab and uncheck  the 'hardware acceleration box', close, and refresh the page and you should be good to go. This fix worked great for me.  :D 

Hope this helps!
