### Post by Mornedhel on 2009-06-20
> **powel212 said:**
> I don't know what the Dell-Ubuntu is all about but I am sure that Jaunty will run perfectly on your box.

I own a Dell M1530 as well, and I can confirm a Jaunty Jackalope (my own installation) will run almost without troubles. The hardware is almost entirely supported out of the box.

The nVidia card (mine is an 8400M GS) is supported by the restricted drivers ; they segfault sometimes but X restores itself immediately. This happens about once a week on a laptop almost constantly running (CS student...).

The wireless was extremely dodgy at first, after a few updates it now works well.

The fingerprint reader I didn't install the drivers, but I had it working in Hardy previously and I hear the drivers are now neatly packaged in the Jaunty repositories.

The rest works well, except that heavy CPU/GPU usage (3D renders, 3D games) will quite quickly result in overheating and auto shutdown. This happens in Vista as well, so I guess it's the result of bad air flow in the laptop. (Actually, it happens faster in Vista for me.)
