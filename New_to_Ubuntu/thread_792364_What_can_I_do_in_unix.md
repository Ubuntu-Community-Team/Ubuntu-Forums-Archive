### Post by tamoneya on 2008-05-13
you can do almost anything you want in unix.  You may have trouble getting infected by a virus but other than that there is very little that you cant do in windows that you can in unix.  Most of the internet (routers, webservers, DNS servers) runs on linux.  Also mobile phones and super computers run on unix/linux.  The new Large Hadron Collider at CERN runs unix.  

You can definately accomplish all of the things you listed above with linux.  I have seen all of them done personally with the exception of the FPGA but it shouldnt be a problem.
