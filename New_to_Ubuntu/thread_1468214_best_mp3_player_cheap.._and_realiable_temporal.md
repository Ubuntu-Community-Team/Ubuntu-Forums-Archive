### Post by dracayr on 2010-05-07
> **durand said:**
> How did you do the wikipedia thing? It sounds cool!
It is really cool :) Every time I look sth. up with it, people ask me "Do you have internet on your mp3-player??" or "is there wlan here?".. "no, I just downloaded wikipedia" :)

You have to download the source, apply a patch, and compile it yourself. There's some guides for that on the rockbox homepage. The patch you need is this one: [http://www.rockbox.org/tracker/task/4755?getfile=15014](http://www.rockbox.org/tracker/task/4755?getfile=15014). download the latest patch file (it's included in one of the last comments on the page). At some point there, I wrote fuze support into the patch :).
After you've compiled and installed, you can follow this guide on how to download wikipedia and convert the xml file to a file the player can read: [http://www.rockbox.org/tracker/task/4755?getfile=15014](http://www.rockbox.org/tracker/task/4755?getfile=15014). There is one difference to the guide though. In the later versions of the patch, the converter is located in the source tree of rockbox.. in the tools/converter directory.

So for someone who doesn't know linux or the CLI very well it's probably not easy.. just post your questions here ;)

dracayr
