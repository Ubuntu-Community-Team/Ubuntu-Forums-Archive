### Post by Frogs Hair on 2014-01-16
If you are referring to window rules there are some settings in the dconf editor . The path is org > gnome > desktop > wm > preferences . The compiz configuration settings manager available in the software center has a window rules plug-in with further settings , but I have never used the plug-in .
