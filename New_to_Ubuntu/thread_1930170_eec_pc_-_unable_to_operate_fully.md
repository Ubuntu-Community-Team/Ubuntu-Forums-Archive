### Post by HermanAB on 2012-02-23
Well, EeePCs are rather old now, so you need to repair the battery pack.  

Don't buy a 'new' battery pack, since it will be the same age as the present one and therefore also bad even if it has never been used, or maybe especially if it has never been used.

All major cities have one or two places that can slice open a battery pack and put new cells in it - doesn't cost much.
