### Post by Paqman on 2011-05-10
You should back up your entire home folder and [create a list of your installed apps]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=261366"), then reinstall Ubuntu. Copy your home folder files back in and reinstall all your apps.

All your software will be restored with the same settings and all your data in your home folder will be safe.

### Post by Paqman on 2011-05-10
You can also back up all your apps by simply taking a copy of the folder /var/cache/apt/archives. Unless you cleaned it out at some point it'll contain all the .debs you need to install your apps.
