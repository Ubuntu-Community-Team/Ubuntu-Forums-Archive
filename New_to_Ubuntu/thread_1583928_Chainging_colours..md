### Post by s0rc3r3r on 2010-09-29
I think I have found the solution

Right click on the desktop and select 'Change Desktop Background'
Assuming that you are on the 'BACKGROUND' tab.
Now choose each images you have Listed there..
When you are choosing it..Do pay attention to the SMALL BOX that shows 'SOLID COLOR'.

You can see that..with every image you select..the 'SOLID COLOR' changes depending up on the 'MAXIMUM color gradient' of that particular image you selected.
I seriously dont know what logic is defined by the coder to choose the 'SOLID COLOR'.

Now..when you are not defining any background....The 'Solid Color' will be displayed.

I hope its Lime green because you must have chosen a wall paper with color gradient similar to Lime green color So it displays lime green.

I hope this lil experiment solves your cofusion
