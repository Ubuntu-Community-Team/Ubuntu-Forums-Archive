### Post by Greg Mueller on 2011-05-30
This has been going on for just a couple months and I can't find any reference to it.

The first thing in the morning, when I "Reply" to an email in Thunderbird, the reply comes up minimized. If i maximize it and then quit and then reply again, it comes up maximized and is fine for the rest of the day.

I have looked through my Thunderbird preference/settings a number of times and can't find anything that has to do with this.

### Post by Greg Mueller on 2011-06-03
Solved

I stretched the minimized reply out as far as I could get it (but did not maximize it). Then grabbed that reply and moved it around the screen as much as I could. Then quit the reply. Then quit Thunderbird and rebooted.

Replies come up maximized now, as they used to.
