The issue with printed material is that it's often patronizing (This is your music player , to open you click on what's called a "Link") and with an ever evolving OS it's out of date by the next LTS (Long Term Support) release.

It's best to use the User generated docs or learn *Unix and get a strong grasp of what this thing is built on , becuase the GUI (Graphical User Interface) is ever changing and for Ubuntu will forever be , Because Ubuntu has a very strong propelling focus on progression.
