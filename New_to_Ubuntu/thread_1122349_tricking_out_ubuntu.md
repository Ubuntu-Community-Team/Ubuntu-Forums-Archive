### Post by King_Critter on 2009-04-11
Well, for getting a transparent terminal: open one up, hit the button, click Profile Preferences, then click the Background tab and set the slider to what you want it. You'll need to be running Compiz (AKA desktop effects) or similar for true transparency, otherwise it'll just show your wallpaper. 

The clock is undoubtedly Cairo-Clock.
