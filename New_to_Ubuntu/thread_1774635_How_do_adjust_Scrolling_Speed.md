### Post by ClientAlive on 2011-06-03
> **CleveRuse said:**
> its cool thnx 4 tryin... hey u think u could help me with how to veiw and track all of my Threads and Posts on this Forum -i know its off-topic, i hate to start a thread 4 such newbish question


There are some links across the page in every thread that are colored red (at least they are in my browser with it's default settings). Going from left to right it starts with - "view First Unread," then, "Thread Tools," then, "Search This Thread," then, "Display Tools." If you click the link "Thread Tools" you will see a drop down list come down and the last item in the list is - "Subscribe to this Thread." By clicking that link it will add a subscription to that thread and keep it in a list for you.

Now, when you want to see the list of subscribed threads, the easiest way is to click the link - "Quick Links" (which is almost directly above "Thread Tools") and look for the third link from the bottom called - "Subscribed Threads."

Another way to get subscriptions to the threads you want is sometimes considered a little more convenient to some - it is what I have done. So the way they've worded/ organized it isn't very intuitive but if you want to check it out you would do thus:

Click the link "User CP" you will then see a link in the column on the left called "Edit Options" - click it. In that page, under the second section heading (called "Messaging and Notification,") in the second box in that section called - "Default Thread Subscription Mode" there is a single drop down menu in the corner of the box. If you select anything other than, "Do Not Subscribe" (which is the default) you will automatically be subscribed to threads you start and threads you post in. Then, with that part happening automatically, all you need to worry about is looking at the list when you want to.

You can also find the list of your subscribed threads by first clicking "User CP" then looking for the link to it on that page. (It's an alternative way for that part of things).

Hope it helps. Sorry I can't help with your original question. I just don't know the answer to that one.

Have a great weekend.
