### Post by finer recliner on 2009-05-05
every command uses its own flags, so just look at man pages if you want to know what options are available to the command(s) you are interested in. sometimes they overlap (like -R is commonly used for recursive, and -v for verbose output), but they don't have to be, and sometimes are completely different.
