### Post by chastrell70 on 2008-12-02
My brother recently gave me a HP Pavillion ze5700 without windows disk. this computer is several years old. I was about to post a thread about slow internet connection. during conversation about computers he told me that he had disassembled the computer and cleaned the fans. This sparked a question about that maybe being the problem. After disassembling the computer i found that he simply placed the heat sink with rubber pad back on the processor. This was the whole problem. I cleaned the rubber pad off and used heat sink compound. moral of the story is that overheating can cause a lot of problems that may seem like OS or internet speed. there maybe a need for a beginners thread that would address these problems and how to solve them. thought that may help. I work on heavy equipment and most of my repairs are the simplest things that should have been taken care of through proper maintenance. and people whom are trying to bring an old computer back to life. may be having the same problem. thank you for the free OS. I have some old hardware that i am putting together to donate. i cannot afford to send cash. but i believe these items will help. Thanx again for Ubuntu.
