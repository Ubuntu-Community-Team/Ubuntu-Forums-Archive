### Post by astrognome on 2011-12-18
What I would do would be the following:

Right click on the panel and select add to panel.  Then choose custom application launcher.  At this point, you can change the logo, give it a name and a comment (that appears when you mouse over the launcher).  The important part is the command.

If you use libreoffice, the command would be:

libreoffice /path/to/the/file.odt

For openoffice.org:

oofice /path/to/the/file.odt

That should launch the editor of your choice and open the file that you wish to edit.  Let us know if you have any further questions. =)
