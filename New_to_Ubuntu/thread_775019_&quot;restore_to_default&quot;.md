### Post by DezSP on 2008-04-29
Well, the simple method is to just chuck the installation CD back in and overwrite the original installation... You probably want to back some stuff up beforehand of course, any local config files (e.g. your Firefox profile) are stored in your home directory, just press Ctrl+H to see them in the file manager. If you go into Synaptic, there's a way of saving a list of all the packages you have installed to be restored at a later date, if you don't feel like re-installing everything you have on the system.
