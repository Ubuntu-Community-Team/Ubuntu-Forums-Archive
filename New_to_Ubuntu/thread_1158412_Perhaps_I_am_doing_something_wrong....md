Did you have the screen locked, and got that message after unlocking the computer? In that case it could just be that somebody left you that message while you were away from the computer. The unlock dialog is provided by gnome-screensaver, and the dialog has an option which allows people to leave messages. When the user then logs in the message and time it was left will be displayed to the user.
