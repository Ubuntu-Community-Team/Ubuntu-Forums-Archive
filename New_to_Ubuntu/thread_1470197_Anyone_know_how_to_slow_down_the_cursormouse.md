### Post by quadproc on 2010-05-02
> **listerdl said:**
> Hi my mouse or rather cursor or super fast, anyone know where the settings are hidden to s-l-0-w it down?

From a command line you can enter mouse parameters by typing ```
xset m <parameters>
```
See man xset for the details on parameters.  I tend to like xset m 3/2 0.
