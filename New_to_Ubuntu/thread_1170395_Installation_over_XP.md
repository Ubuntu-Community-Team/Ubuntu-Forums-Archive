### Post by Xero Xenith on 2009-05-26
All you have to do is get the Ubuntu 9.04 LiveCD and an external hard drive or pen drive. Insert them both to the computer, and start it up. Follow the onscreen instructions until you arrive at a desktop.

Here, at the top, there should be a menu titled "Places". Your Windows hard drive and your pen/USB hard drive should both show up here. Treat that menu like My Computer on Windows. From there, you can copy and paste files exactly like Windows Explorer in Windows.
