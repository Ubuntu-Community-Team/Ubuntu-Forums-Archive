### Post by -kg- on 2010-06-13
If you have rebooted a time or two since the "problem shutdown" and have had no issues, I wouldn't worry too much about it.  If it happens again, and especially if it ***keeps*** happening, that's when you should start to worry and find out what's wrong.  Over the years, I've found that occasionally, the computer will "burp."  It scares the be-Jesus out of you, but never occurs again.
