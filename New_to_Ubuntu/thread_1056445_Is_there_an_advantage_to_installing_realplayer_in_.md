### Post by HotShotDJ on 2009-01-31
I can think of no reason whatsoever to install realplayer in Ubuntu.  The "Download Helper" plug-in for Firefox will help you download FLV's from the web, and everything else can be accomplished with VLC (with ubuntu-restricted-extra or kubuntu-restricted-extras).  You might find the Medibuntu repositories useful as well.

But realplayer?  It is superfluous at best in Ubuntu.
