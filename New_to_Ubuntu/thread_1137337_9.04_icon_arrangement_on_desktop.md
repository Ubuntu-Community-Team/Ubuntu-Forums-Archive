Do you not have that option to align them?

Or do you mean how it lines the icons up from left-to-right? Yes, that would be a cultural thing.

I think the lack of end-user control over desktop icons was a conscious choice. My take on it is that the designers are trying to encourage the use of the command line, panels and menus, rather than having a desktop full of icons.
