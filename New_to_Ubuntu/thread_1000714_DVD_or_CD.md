### Post by PocketDog on 2008-12-03
The actual installs from either disc are *exactly* the same. All that's different is the software available on the disc post-install. The DVDs are useful for a machine with dial-up or no internet access; it has lots of software and most, if not all, of the multimedia codecs necessary to play MP3s and videos
