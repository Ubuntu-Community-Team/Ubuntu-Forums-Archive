### Post by cmay on 2008-12-20
try use knoppix or the 64studio livecd for this. i had some problems with audacity before but not like this where buttons and toolbar is missing.  about the mic and line in a mic is a very sensitive input and so is line in. you can destroy the mic and line input by pluggin a microfone into a line in and vice versa. i have tried once to use a older fourtrack recorder where i  plugged my studio microfone into the line in by accident and it broke it. the most sensitive input channel is the one for old vinyl records but the needs a pre amp and i think you can still get those to use for recording old vinyl into newer things such as a laptop. its a little switch box that should cost less money now than it did some years back and it was little expense .
