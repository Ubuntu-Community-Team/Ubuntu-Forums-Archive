### Post by mastablasta on 2015-09-16
username is username - you chose it when you installed the OS. best thing to do is to know the user name (easy to find out - check the home folder name on disk from live CD -- /home/user) and then reset the password for that user. it should work. try a simple password first (12345), you can change it later.

I am not sure what other is, but I do know Ubuntu has guest account - say you want to hand over your PC temporary to a friend - the would use the guest account and when they log out, all their stuff get's removed.
