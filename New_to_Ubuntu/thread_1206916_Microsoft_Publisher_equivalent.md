### Post by aysiu on 2009-07-07
Scribus is a desktop publishing program, so it is an alternative to Publisher (I think it's better than Publisher, actually, but not as good as InDesign).

It will *not* open or save Publisher files, though. Apparently, there's some kind of complication with desktop publishing that opening non-native formats isn't worth it for developers (Publisher can't open Scribus or InDesign files; InDesign can't open Publisher or Scribus files).

So if you already have Publisher files, you'll have to recreate them in Scribus.
