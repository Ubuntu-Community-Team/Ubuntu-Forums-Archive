### Post by hryanjones on 2011-05-12
The color (and usually **bolded**) text is to signify that the user is calling something to your attention by using your name.  This is eye catching to people doing other things and just hanging out in the channel.  Also, it's common to do something like type the first couple letters of a username and push <TAB> (to autocomplete) if you want to highlight something to a specific user.
