### Post by fr.theo on 2008-11-15
I have also been having the same problem, you might want to load your volume control in your >>applications> sound and Video > volume control


select preferences and tick mic boost and any other selection concerning the mic. Once you have done that you should then go back to your volume control and under the tab switches you will notice mic boost tick it and give that a go.

P.s check all you volume controls as well in recording tab and play back tab, I still can hear my self clearly in the head set, but i require the mic close to my lips to get a clear recording. I have a Sound blaster Audgy 2 card.


Good Luck.
