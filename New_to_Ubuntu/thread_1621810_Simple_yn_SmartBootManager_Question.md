### Post by cariboo on 2010-11-14
Smart Boot Manager was written in 2000, and really hasn't been updated since 2001 (if [this]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/btmgr/files/") is the one you're asking about) usb thumb drives hadn't even come into existence then. 

I'd suggest having a look at [Syslinux]("http://syslinux.zytor.com/wiki/index.php/The_Syslinux_Project") if you want to create a bootable USB thumb drive.
