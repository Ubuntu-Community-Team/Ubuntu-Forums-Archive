### Post by Desert Sailor on 2011-04-30
I know I should have waited a few more weeks, maybe even a month, but a new release is like money burning a hole in my pocket. :D  I couldn't resist the temptation and after a back-up of my "/home" directory, (just in case) I let update manager update my system.  I'm on a DSL connection, and the entire process took about 2 hours.  Went flawlessly without any hangups, and the system seems to be working properly for me now on 11.4.  

My box is a home-made AMD Athalon 3200, with only one (1) gig of memory. 
340 Gig hard drive with Windows XP partition (40 gig) and Ubuntu 11.4 on the remainder. I am still playing around with the "Unity" desktop system, but I think I'll like it once I get used to where things are.  

Just wanted to leave a positive report for those who are on the edge of upgrading or not.
