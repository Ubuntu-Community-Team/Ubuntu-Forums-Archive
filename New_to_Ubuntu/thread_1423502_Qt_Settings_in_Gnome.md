### Post by HalfEmptyHero on 2010-03-06
I'm trying to get qt apps to look like gtk apps. I installed qt4-qtconfig and changed/saved the settings in Qt 4 Settings, however when I open up any qt app (kate for example) it looks the same as before.

Edit: I figured it out. For anyone else who is curious, just install systemsettings and  kdeartwork, and then run systemsettings and under appearance choose widget style gtk+, and then change the icons to your theme.
