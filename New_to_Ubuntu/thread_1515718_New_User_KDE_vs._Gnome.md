### Post by slooksterpsv on 2010-06-23
Personally, I like Gnome better than KDE, for one simple reason:
Screen Size

Both Gnome and KDE recognize 1366x768, and even sets that resolution for my computer. Perfect, that's the max I can have.

KDE - everything is still huge, I've tried adjusting the settings, but it seems I can't have more than 1 window open without having to minimize everything. I use the multi-desktops but that still doesn't help when I need two apps on one screen.

Gnome - everything is laid out perfect, everything seems more uniform and condensed to where I'm not trying to shift everything around for more Window space.

Preference definitely - that's mine.
