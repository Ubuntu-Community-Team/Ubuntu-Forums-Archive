### Post by Zralou on 2008-04-27
Since the release of Hardy, i've been playing around with different distro's, comparing the different versions.  So far, i've tried Kubuntu 8.04(KDE 3), Ubuntu 8.04, Kubuntu 8.04(KDE 4), and Mandriva One.
When I first installed Linux back in march, I used the Kubuntu 8.04 beta(7.10 wouldn't even boot into graphical UI), and after trying all the other flavours, I am soooo glad I started with this one!!.

I actually installed Mandriva One on the HDD as a triple boot (XP/Kubuntu/Mandriva) what a pain it was, had to redo grub 3 times before I could get it to boot properly.  Half the programs in the start menu don't work, and trying to setup hardware just takes you round in circles.

So, now i'm back with my old faithful, Kubuntu 8.04(KDE 3) beta(updated).  I've had my problems in the past with this distro, but most were minor graphic card settings, overall, it's been a very stable, easy for me, distro that I wouldn't change for any of the other one's now.

Sara Lou
