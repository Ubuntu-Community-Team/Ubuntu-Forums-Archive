### Post by daslinkard on 2012-09-23
You should be able to tap the windows sign on your keyboard to display the Dash home so you can type in terminal.

I use 12.04 and I hold down>  **CTRL+ALT+T**  to automatically call up the terminal.

Also I would suggest this link [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2015424") for the information Ubuntu Forums have on the Terminal.
