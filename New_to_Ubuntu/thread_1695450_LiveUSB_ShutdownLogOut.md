You can do a couple of things, right click on the panel and add to panel and add the other shutdown button. There is also a shutdown in the menu.

There is a softshut down and reboot option.
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reisub](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reisub)

You can open a terminal and run reboot, or
shutdown -h now (for shutdown and power off)
shutdown -r now (for shutdown and restart then)

It may be that the iso or the thumb load is corrupted as well.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)
