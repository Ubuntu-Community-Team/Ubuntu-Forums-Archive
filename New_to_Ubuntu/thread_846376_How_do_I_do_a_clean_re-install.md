### Post by bumanie on 2008-07-01
Have you installed the graphics card driver yet? If not, System --> Administration --> Hardware Drivers and enable the card. As you wireless is not working, you may have to plug straight into the ethernet port from your cable/dsl connection. Unfortunately, wireless in linux at present is a bit hit and miss - some work fine straight away, whilst others are a real pain to setup.
