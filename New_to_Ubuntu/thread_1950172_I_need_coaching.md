There are a lot of good guides, this one is  free and nicely written;

 [http://ubuntupocketguide.com/index_main.html](http://ubuntupocketguide.com/index_main.html)  Is good for an overview.

This is good too [http://ubuntu-manual.org/downloads](http://ubuntu-manual.org/downloads)  Choose the relevant version.
