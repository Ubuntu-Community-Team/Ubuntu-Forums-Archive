### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-08-29
Hello and Welcome

I have seen this once with a different model and it was because the paper was not making contact with the sensor and the message originated from the printer in my case. Simply moving the paper around solved the problem. 

There are a number of trouble shooting resources for your printer on line if this is not the problem. One thread I read stated that if paper is added while printing false messages may appear and the solution was to turn the printer off check the paper feed/path and turn the printer back on.
