### Post by bobblehat on 2008-05-20
You can certainly run Linux from flash but whether you can boot from a flash drive depends to a certain extent on your Bios. Check that out first. 

Either way, I'm not sure if simply copying the ISO to the flash drive would do what you want - I think you'd have to actually do an installation onto the drive (but someone feel free to jump in and correct me if that's wrong).

I've had Ubuntu running well off a compact flash card via an internal CF to SATA adapter but treated it as a normal drive and installed onto it.
