### Post by tea for one on 2022-01-25
I do not think that this is a bug - it's been like this in Ubuntu for a few years.
Stop the playback and close the application, two mouse clicks instead of one.
Not really a problem in terms of using your PC for entertainment.

More info in posts 4, 5 and 6 here [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/rhythmbox/+bug/1754125](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/rhythmbox/+bug/1754125)

Quite probably, Rhythmbox in Raspberry Pi OS has a different default configuration.
