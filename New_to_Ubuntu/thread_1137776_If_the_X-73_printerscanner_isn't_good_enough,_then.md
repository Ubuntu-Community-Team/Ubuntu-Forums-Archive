### Post by Sef on 2009-04-25
Since your printer/scanner is a lexmark, it would be best to get another one.   However, the [printing]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-X73") and [scanning]("http://www.sane-project.org/cgi-bin/driver.pl?manu=lexmark&model=X73&bus=any&v=&p=") can work partially.   

HP is what I recommend.   I have a 1610 PCS, and it works out of the box.  Check the [HPLIP website]("http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html") to find ones that should work out of the box.  Also Epson is pretty Linux friendly, and Brother does have some debs.
