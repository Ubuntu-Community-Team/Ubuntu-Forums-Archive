### Post by rahul4557 on 2014-05-31
Best way for a beginner is a easy to use GUI tool available in Ubuntu Software Center called "Disks" (available with Ubuntu by default)

Choose the Disk and then select the partition you want to automount on startup and goto its mount options to edit its mounting options.
