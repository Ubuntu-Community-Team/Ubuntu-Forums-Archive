### Post by Bennett2005 on 2008-05-08
I have a couple suggestions that are not books :(

Podcasts.  I know of two very good ones to help get you started.  The first one is Linux Reality, a very well known podcast by Chess Griffin.  You can download all the episodes from [www.linuxreality.org]("www.linuxreality.org"), or find them on Podcast Alley or iTunes.

Fresh Ubuntu is another good linux-learner podcast.  The larger portion of the show is dedicated to current events in Linux and general tech, but you can pick up some very good tips from their tutorial sections of the show.  You can find their show at [freshubuntu.org]("www.freshubuntu.org"), or Podcast Alley or iTunes.

A lot of other linux-related podcasts are out there too.  It's a great way to learn more about the system because you can listen to them at any time and replay them as often as you need to.
