### Post by audiomick on 2011-10-26
I have my /home on a separate drive to the system, and have also had, at times, a test system with its' own /home on the same drive on this machine. I don't do anything labour intensive, I should say, but I didn't notice any real difference. I have also not seen any referance anywhere to anything along those lines. I think the main advantage of a separate /home partition is to be able to re-mount it when you re-install, and it doesn't have to be on a different drive for that.
