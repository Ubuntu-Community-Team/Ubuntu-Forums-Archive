### Post by joshrobinson on 2009-04-26
Go to System > Preferences > Power Management, General tab.
Then change When the power button is pressed to Shut Down.

I know its not exactly what you asked for, but you can then just click the power button on your computer and it will shut down instead of asking what action you want to take.
