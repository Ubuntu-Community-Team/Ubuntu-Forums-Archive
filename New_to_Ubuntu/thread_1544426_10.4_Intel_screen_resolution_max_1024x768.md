### Post by RtVD on 2010-08-07
Just had this this issue with a LG tv being used as the monitor.

Solved it by going into the graphics area and turning off mirroring. (for some mad reason it thought I had a second monitor plugged in with a max res of 1024x768 on it).

in the monitors section, (system/preferences/monitors), the 'same image on all monitors' was ticked.


Hope this helps.
