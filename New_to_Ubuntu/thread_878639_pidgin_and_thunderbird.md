### Post by pbpersson on 2008-08-03
Um.....how to learn how to configure Pidgin and Thunderbird.

You would read the instructions - the manual as it were.

Pidgin will ask you for your accounts at various systems such as:

[email]JohnDoe@Yahoo.com[/email]
[email]HowdyDoody@hotmail.com[/email]

.....and you will need to know the passwords for the accounts.

In Thunderbird it will want to know your email settings.  Let us say you get your internet connection through your ABC cable company and you want to use your email account at your cable company.

You would go to your cable company web site and get your email settings from there and plug it into Thunderbird.  You are most likely using POP and SMTP settings.

You will need to do some reading - those thoughts might get you started if you are using the systems I think you are using.  There can be many different answers depending upon what you want to accomplish.
