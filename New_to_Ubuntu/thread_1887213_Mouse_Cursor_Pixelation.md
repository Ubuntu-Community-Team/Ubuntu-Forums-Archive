### Post by jb61264 on 2011-11-26
OK...I downloaded and installed an NVidia graphics device driver and this has resolved my issue...now everything looks perfect
 
Very strange how this issue appeared as it was just the mouse and any "windows" that might be open with "working" graphics associated with it (ie...bar advancing on a download or the mouse cursor spinning)...those things were pixelated and in the case of my mouse, there was a duplicate (like a shadow) of the pixelated mouse appearing.

Downloading and installing the Nvidia device driver has resolved the issue.
