### Post by aeiah on 2009-06-26
ones with intel or atheros chipsets are usually a safe bet (but its worth checking first). aircrack-ng maintain a list of devices that allow packet injection used for cracking wireless access points. packet injection is only possible on very well supported cards so anything that does packet injection should work very well for normal use too, although some may not work out-of-the-box (some ralink / belkin cards).
