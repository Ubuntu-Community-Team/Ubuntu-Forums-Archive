### Post by wednesday allfather on 2008-06-05
There sure is.

Search for cWiid in google and you will find a tons of links to how-to for this.  I did it awhile ago, and it is pretty cool.  I have it controlling my media server.  You should also check into an on-screen keyboard, so you can just use the wiimote to control everything.  

Fair warning, I initially got the right-click working as "B" on the 'mote, but something I have done has caused it to become BTN_MIDDLE on my mouse.  

You will also need some candles or lights or that NYCO wireless sensor bar if you want to "point and click."  Otherwise you will have to roll the 'mote left and right and tilt up and down to move the mouse.
