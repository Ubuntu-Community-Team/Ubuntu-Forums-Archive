### Post by Sef on 2010-09-01
> Thanks for the help. I will try the alternate one. I went to the site, very confusing. What do I choose? How do I use it? Do I burn it to a cd or do I download it into my laptop and open it?

1) How to download the alternate cd:

Click [here]("http://mirror.pnl.gov/releases/lucid/") for the alternate download > Scroll down to Alternate CD section > Choose either 32-bit or 64-bit to download.  (If you are not sure, download the 32-bit.)

2) Burn the iso to a CD?

After download, if by mirror, md5sum the download, then burn it to a CD, or if by torrent, just burn it to a cd.  Make sure you burn it as an iso.

3) How to install using the alternate cd:

Just click on install Ubuntu.  The install is just common sense. If you are using the whole disk, then just click on the default.  If you have a home or other partitions that you do not want reformatted, ask how to manually partition.
