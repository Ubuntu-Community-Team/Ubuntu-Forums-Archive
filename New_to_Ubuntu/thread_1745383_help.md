### Post by Quackers on 2011-05-01
Welcome to UF.
Which version have you downloaded? 10.04 is the latest long term supported version.
You would normally over-write your current system by installing the new version over the top of the old one. You do this by booting from the cd and installing to the same partition that the old version is in.
You can upgrade from 8.04 to 10.04, I believe, online.
