### Post by SoftwareExplorer on 2009-06-10
If you are using screenlets, and you right click on the screenlet, click properties, go to the Options tab and check Treat as Widget, the screenlet will disappear. Then when you press the key for the widget layer, it will reappear with the effect that you were seeing. If you want to see it on all of the desktops, check the Stick to Desktop box on the options tab of the screenlet.

Hopefully that makes things clearer...

### Post by GameManK on 2009-06-12
