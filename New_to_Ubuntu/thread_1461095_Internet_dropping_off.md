### Post by oldsoundguy on 2010-04-23
I live in a giant RF soak.  Concrete block building with  steel re-bar and concrete .. GROUNDED.  Radio signals get lost in this place .. cordless phone will not work on the patio .. some cell phones will not work inside .. and so on.
I put in an access point in the middle of the place and ran a cat5 to the router.  It helped
THEN I went to eBay and bought a handful of 4db gain external antennae. Slapped them on everything that was partially nailed down.  
Now I have strong signal everywhere in the place and can take a laptop or pda out to the parking lot (live in a 4 plex) and work out of my car or hang out under the cedar trees at the picnic table!

(also make sure that you are trying to access the strongest signal .. use your network manager.)
