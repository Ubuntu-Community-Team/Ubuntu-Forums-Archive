### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-01-20
I suspect the drive itself.  I had an older Sony Optiarc drive and it would not read DVD-Rs, no matter what I did.

If you have a bootable SW DVD, stick that in the drive and see if your PC boots from it.  That will rule out Linux or driver problems.

If it boots, it's likely a driver problem; if it doesn't boot, it's likely a hardware problem with the drive.

You could check the firmware version of the drive and see if Sony has a newer version available for download.
