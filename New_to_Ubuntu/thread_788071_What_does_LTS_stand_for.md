### Post by dorkdork777 on 2008-05-09
Most Ubuntu versions recieve support of 18 months, but LTS releases get 3 years on the desktop and 5 on the server. Only 6.06 and 8.04 have been LTSs so far.

Also worth mentioning that 'recieves support' does NOT mean recieves free tech support. Support means recieving bug fixes and stability updates.
