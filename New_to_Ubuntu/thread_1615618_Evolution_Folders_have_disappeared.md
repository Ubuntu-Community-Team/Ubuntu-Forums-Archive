### Post by pauwl on 2010-11-07
Dear Jon69,

it looks like you gave people 30 minutes to answer..... a little short maybe for a rainy Sunday (at least here in France it's raining).

First let's try to understand a little better what you do/don't see.

So please make sure when you go to View...Layout that "Show Sidebar" has a little tick mark next to it. If not, please select it so the sidebar appears.

On the sidebar you should see "On This Computer", and possibly an email account that you have setup.

Click around to see if in the "On this computer" section you have items in the Inbox or Sent items.

If so, that's where your mail is, and you can make new folders under the Inbox folder.

Let us know what happens.

regards,

Pauwl
