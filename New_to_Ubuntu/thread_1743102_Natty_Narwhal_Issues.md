### Post by rosencrantz on 2011-04-29
Well, Natty comes with Libreoffice instead of Openoffice and Unity instead of Gnome as default interface. Well you did the upgrade to 11.04 and everything is as expected: you get the new default desktop and the new default applications (you have LibreOffice which is the new default AND OpenOffice from your old install) So what is the problem??
