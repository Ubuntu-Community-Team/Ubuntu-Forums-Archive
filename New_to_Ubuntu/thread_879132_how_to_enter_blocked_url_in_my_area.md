There are some very easy-to-use proxy set-ups at:

[www.youhide.com/]("http://www.youhide.com/")

[www.zend2.com/]("http://www.zend2.com/")

[www.the-cloak.com/anonymous-surfing-home.html]("http://www.the-cloak.com/anonymous-surfing-home.html")

Or, if you want to configure your browser to use different proxies, try the list and instructions at:  

[www.samair.ru/proxy/]("http://www.samair.ru/proxy/")

You say your trying to access a site that is blocked by your government.  Well, the 2nd site on my list ([http://www.zend2.com/]("http://www.zend2.com/")  says that it has set up its proxy so it can defeat the blocls put up by the Chinese government.  Maybe that's the one for you?
