### Post by finer recliner on 2008-05-20
rename or delete your ~/.gconf/ directory (holds all your personal settings for gnome). then restart the computer. a new ~/.gconf directory will be created for you with all the ubuntu defaults.

please note you will lose all other settings relating to gnome as well.
