### Post by garthcrocker on 2009-05-04
I've eventually found the answer to the problem. You have to use a sledgehammer to do it though. First use Control A to highlight the whole document, then Go to FORMAT - CHARACTER and reselect the font, etc and at the bottom of the box select the LANGUAGE as English-UK. This forces the document into the right language as required by the OO 3 spellchecker. Save it, clear the highlighting and the spell checker suddenly becomes aware that the sun does pass over other countries after it sets over California before reappearing over Rhode Island. Yeah!
