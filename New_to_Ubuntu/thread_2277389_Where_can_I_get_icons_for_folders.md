### Post by leunam12 on 2015-05-09
Download the desired icons from the internet; google "folder icon" or something like that. Save in dedicated folder, something like: /home/username/Pictures/Custom_Icons or wherever you want as long as you can find them later. Now open Nautilus and navigate to the directory that contains the folder whose icon you want to change and right-click the folder and select "Properties" from the pop-up menu; click the little icon on the upper left corner of the "Basic" tab and change it to one of the images you downloaded.
