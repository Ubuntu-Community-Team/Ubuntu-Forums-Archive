### Post by Sirron on 2009-03-02
Themes installed using your user account will be installed to a folder named .themes, located in your home directory. Files beginning with "." are hidden, to display them in Nautilus, press Ctrl+H.

---

### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2009-03-02
New themes should install to ~/.themes. There are also themes in /usr/share/themes
