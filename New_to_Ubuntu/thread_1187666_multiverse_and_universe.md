### Post by freeman2000 on 2009-06-14
"Universe" is the majority of the Free Software that is not covered in the "Main" or "Restricted" repositories.  Much of this software is borrowed from the Debian repository and is not supported by Ubuntu (Canonical), which means there's no guarantee of updates.  "Multiverse" is software that is released under a software license that is incompatible with the Free Software spirit.
