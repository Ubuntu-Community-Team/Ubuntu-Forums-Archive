### Post by NikTh on 2013-01-06
Hi , 
only help I can provide for Ubuntu 11.04 is the suggestion to upgrade to a supported version. 
Ubuntu 11.04 is EOL and not supported anymore. You can have a brief info here -> [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases)

Newer version means , newer kernel , packages , better hardware support (maybe keyboard and mouse work out of the box) ..
