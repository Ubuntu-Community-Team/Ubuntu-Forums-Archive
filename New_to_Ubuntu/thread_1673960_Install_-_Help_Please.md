:)I have a feeling that the reason the computer stopped working in the first place was due to worn out / bad hardware. If the LiveCD won't load anymore, it is most likely the motherboard, processor, or an attached component (obviously not the video card). 

When booting your father-in-laws box, what are the POST beeps? Are there a series or just one? One is normal booting. A series tells you that it is an error code. Looking up the motherboard and beep codes will give you more info.
