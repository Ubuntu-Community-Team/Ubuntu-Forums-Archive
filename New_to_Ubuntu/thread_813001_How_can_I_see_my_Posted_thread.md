### Post by george9233 on 2008-05-30
In the panel on the top, press "search", then press "Find all your threads" or "Find all your posts".

Alternatively, you can subscribe to it once you finished writing it. It's under the text box. Or you can subscribe to it later if you forgot to do it in the first place by using "Thread Tools" above the first post.
