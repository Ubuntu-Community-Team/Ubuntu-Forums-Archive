I think what you are seeing is this:

There is just enough on the hard drive that the PC you built is starting to boot from the hard drive, the hangs.

Please set you boot order in your bios to boot from the CD before the hard disk.  Then reboot.

Ummm...  You want to do the 'use the entire disk' install, so the hard drive is wiped clean and it gets a fresh start.
