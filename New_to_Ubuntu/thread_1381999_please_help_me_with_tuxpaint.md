i have a little sister who adores tuxpaint, she makes tons of pictures on it all the time. and i am going to put windows on the computer that she uses... so i need to know: how do i get the tuxpaint files backed up, for example; in an email to myself or on a cd. please help asap!

### Post by rbc on 2010-01-15
If the saved pictures were created using tuxpaint on a linux computer, open your sister's home directory, then hit Ctrl + h, to reveal the hidden directories and files. There will be a directory called .tuxpaint, then within that is a directory called saved. Open that, then save the .png files to CD or flash drive
