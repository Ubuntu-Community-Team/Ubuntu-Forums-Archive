### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-06-25
On one computer with Lucid 10.04 64-bit, I have installed Google Gears without any problem, and it works.

But, on another computer with Lucid 10.04 32-bit, Google Gears does not install: "Incompatible extension".

Curiously, the [download page]("http://gears.google.com/") says that 32-bit is supported and 64-bit is not supported, whereas I have it working on the 64-bit computer and not on the 32-bit one. :confused:

In both cases, I'm using the default Firefox 3.6.3 that comes with Ubuntu.
I've cleared the cache, signed out of Google, cleared the cookies, and signed in again, but I still get the problem when I try to download and install.

Any ideas how to get Google Gears working?

I've just found a solution.

The Google forum has a [thread on this issue]("http://groups.google.com/group/gears-users/browse_thread/thread/82f4ca1eac385772/f124f683078e69ef").

It suggests installing the apparent [64-bit extension hack]("http://gears64.org/build/05340/3417") even for 32-bit (don't ask me how that works!).
