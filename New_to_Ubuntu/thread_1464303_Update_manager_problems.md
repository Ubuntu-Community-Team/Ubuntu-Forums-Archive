### Post by levelhead21 on 2010-04-28
I was searching for a solution to my Update Manager problem (not being able to download packages... kept saying the URL could not be found) when I stumbled upon this thread and read it from start to finish. Being a noob, I learned a lot and made some notes for future reference. THANKS to all you great people who care enough to waste your time helping others!         I solved my own problem by choosing 'SETTINGS' in the Update Manager and in the tab labeled, Ubuntu Software, changing the 'download from' CANADA (where I live) to MAIN SERVER. I have no idea why I was able to use the previous setting before and then during the last update some files could not be fetched and since then, no files could be fetched until I made the change. Anyway; it all works once again and I hope my little contribution helps someone else.
