### Post by igknighted on 2008-12-30
Ok, I have no idea why the glx command won't work.  That's really strange.

But I think I have found out why compiz won't work.  Your video card cannot handle compiz at the resolution that your dual monitors creates (2880 wide).  Try running the command 'compiz --replace' on the terminal, and I bet it will fail at "Comparing resolution (2560x1024) to maximum 3D texture size (2048): Failed."

You can only run compiz by changing your resolution to 1024x768 for each screen, or running only one monitor.

EDIT: See [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/146298](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/146298)
