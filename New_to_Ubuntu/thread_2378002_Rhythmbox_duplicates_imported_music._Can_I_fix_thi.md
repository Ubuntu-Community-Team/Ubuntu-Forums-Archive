Found the answer on this forum via googling...


> It's happened to me a few times...

The easy way to solve this issue is:

    Open "Home"
    Press Ctrl+h (this will show your hidden files and folders)
    Navigate to .local/share/rhythmbox
    Delete rhythmdb.xml
    Restart Rhythmbox
