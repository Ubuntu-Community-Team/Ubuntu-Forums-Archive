### Post by gettinoriginal on 2008-12-19
When you are in sounds, highlight the sound you want to change, left click and hold on the word default and you should get a drop down that says "default, diabled, custom", drag your cursor down to custom, and it will bring up your file browser, find the sound you want and choose it.  To get to sounds, File System > User > Share > Sounds
