### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-04-30
Ubuntu will automatically detect and install a driver for your video card. If your card is supported for 3D, Hardware Drivers should pop up a notication and let you know.

You can also check it manually by going to  **System/Administration/Hardware Drivers** in your menu.
