### Post by izziere on 2009-09-12
What signal strength do you have?  From a terminal tap in:

```
iwconfig
``` and take a look at the link quality.  Alternatively add the network monitor applet to your panel and get a visual idea of signal strength.

May just be a link problem or may be a problem with driver or with the card/device itself.
