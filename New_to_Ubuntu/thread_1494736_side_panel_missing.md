### Post by Exershio on 2010-05-27
I'm not sure if this is your problem, but it's worth a shot. There is an option in nautilus under preferences titled something like "Always open in browser window". I'm not using GNOME atm so I can't tell for sure, but you may have unchecked that box. 

That'll change it from this:
[http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/0e/Nautilus-spatial.png](http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/0e/Nautilus-spatial.png)
to this:

edit: that is if this is a nautilus problem. without more details on the issue, I could be mistaken

by the way, you'll have to reopen nautilus for this change to take effect
