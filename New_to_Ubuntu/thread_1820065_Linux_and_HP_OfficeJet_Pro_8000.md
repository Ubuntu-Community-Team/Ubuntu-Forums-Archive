### Post by vmsda on 2011-08-07
This is a positive discrimination post; for a change.

After many years of hesitation, I finally gave in and bought a printer; I chose the one in the title, and only after I arrived home did I think about the possible problems I might have, if Linux did not take a liking to it, or vice-versa.

Much to my dismay, when I connected it (via USB), I was asked whether I would like to install the driver, answered yes, and could not believe my luck: everything I tried with the printer worked like a charm.

For once, the devil was absent, even in the details. Now, who should be credited: Linux or HP?

PS: by the way, I never had any connection with HP, professional or personal.
