### Post by Georgia boy on 2010-05-29
I have both Evolution and Thunderbird activated on my PC. Personally I prefer Thunderbird. You have all the add-on if you wish to use them. Also with Thunderbird I like the idea of creating my own templates for email backgrounds and signatures. If I need a calendar I'll add Lightning. Otherwise I'm quite satisfied with Thunderbird. Whatever you feel most comfortable with is the one that you want to use. I know that one of my sisters and a niece uses Incredimail also. I suggest that you try Evolution, Thunderbird and whatever else that is in the repros and then decide which one you like better.

### Post by lisati on 2010-05-29
I currently use Evolution as my main client. I've also used Thunderbird - no major hassles. 

I've used the original Eudora and its successor, Penelope (based on Thunderbird made to look like Eudora). In some ways I prefer the original, but the free version has been discontinued.

Opinion: Incredimail = bloatware. Some of its features are nice, but most are eye candy. And, as with Mailwasher, be careful of the "Bounce" feature: with the number of junk emails with forged "from" email addresses, it's far too easy to fire off a "bounce" message to the wrong person. 

BTW, I used Incredimail for a while on one Windows machine. When I finally uninstalled it, I had to remove some folders manually.
