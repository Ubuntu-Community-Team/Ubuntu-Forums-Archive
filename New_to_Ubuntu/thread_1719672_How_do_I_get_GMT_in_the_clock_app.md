### Post by rewyllys on 2011-04-02
Right-click on the Clock app.  Choose Preferences. In Preferences, choose the Locations tab.  Click on Add.  In the resulting window, go to the Timezone slot, and click on the Scroll button.  Greenwich Mean Time is at the top of the list.  Choose it.  You're done.
