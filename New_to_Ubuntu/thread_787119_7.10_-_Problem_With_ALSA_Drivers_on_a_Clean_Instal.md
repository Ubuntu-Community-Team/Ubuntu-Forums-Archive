Did u try changing the sound output sound system in menu - sounds or something?

also in the CLI 

first:

sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.bak

(to restore just change xorg.conf to xorg.bak and xorg.bak to xorg.conf)

sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg -phig
