### Post by Guilden_NL on 2011-02-16
More info please.
Are you using POP for your inbound email, or IMAP?

Loads of email apps, but Evolution is pretty straightforward compared with Thunderbird, etc.  So best to give Evolution a shot with a little troubleshooting.

This error in Evolution tends to be related to a corrupt email in your (especially POP) in-box.  Can you get to your email via web interface and move it to a new folder?  Then send yourself a new email via your web interface as a test and see if it still errors out using Evolution.  No guarantees, but this is a simple troubleshooting step to check the most obvious problem.
