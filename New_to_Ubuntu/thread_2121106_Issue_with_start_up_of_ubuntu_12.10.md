### Post by Mark_in_Hollywood on 2013-03-05
OK, so it's not likely to be the "old" CRT monitor. The next problem is maybe a GRUB issue. At your next re-boot or power up, select the "recovery" option. This happens BEFORE you would enter your password.

From the Recover select

Update Grub

resume or re-boot when prompted.

If that doesn't fix it, you are going to have to get someone much more expert with GRUB than I am. Try Mr. Charles or Buckyball. Those guys rock. PM them and give them this URL.
