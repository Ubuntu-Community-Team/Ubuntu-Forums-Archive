### Post by haqking on 2012-06-16
I dont know if it is related.  However I use Tbird to access 4 hotmail accounts amongst many others.  The only problems i have ever had was with hotmail accounts.  Using Kopete to talk to some msn buddies was also an issue, in the end i discovered that by making the password less than 12 characters that kopete would then connect, as a result the issues with tbird dissappeared also.  I changed my passwords on those accounts back to the 30 + chr and again there was issues, i changed back to sub 12 chr and all issues dissapeared.

So for my Hotmail/MSN accounts which are not important anyways i use less than 12 chrs and i now get no issues.

YMMV however and may be a separate issue.
