### Post by gjoellee on 2008-08-04
I am using Intrepid Ibex! [COLOR=Red]**Do not do an upgrade, do a clean install!**[/COLOR] Doing an upgrade may just use your time...
Download the live CD here: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/intrepid/alpha-3/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/intrepid/alpha-3/)

A few facts about Intrepid Alpha 3 (what I have experienced):
-More stable then Hardy (lol)
-Booting and starting applications take slightly longer time, but running applications goes very smooth
-_IT IS IMPORTANT TO KEEP INTREPID UP TO DATE (it may eliminate critical bugs and other dangerous things) you *should* enable all 3rd party software as well._
-New theme, but it is not longer default..you should see that in later alpha releases!
-There are a few annoying bugs and errors, but they does no harm (report them to launchpad)
