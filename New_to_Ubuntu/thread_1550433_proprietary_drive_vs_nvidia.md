### Post by cascade9 on 2010-08-11
Unless you have a card that isnt suppotred by the ubuntu nvidia drivers, its easier, and less likely to give you trouble to just use the ubuntu provided nvidia drivers

The only nVidia cards that arent supported by nvidia-current (195.36.15) are GT3XX and GTX4XX cards.
