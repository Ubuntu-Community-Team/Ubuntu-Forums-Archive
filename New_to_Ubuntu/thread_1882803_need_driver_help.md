### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-11-18
I'm afraid you're probably out of luck for both.  

If your XBox controller is wired, and you had it plugged in when you installed Ubuntu, it would have already found drivers for it and installed them.

Headphones don't use drivers, they just plug into audio jacks.  However, if you't not getting sound, that is an audio chipset driver problem, not a headphones problem.
