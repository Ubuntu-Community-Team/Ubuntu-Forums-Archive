### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2010-01-30
At the end of the toolbar is a little arrow.  If you click on that arrow you will see another menu.  If you click on the visible buttons, you will see a third menu.  Just scroll down until you see background and fonts.  Place a check by each and they will be activated.
