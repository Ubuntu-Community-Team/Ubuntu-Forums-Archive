Are you looking for xmms or xmms2?  xmms is a stand alone player with a GUI (sounds like that is what you want).  xmms2 is for network streaming.  Unfortunately, xmms is no longer in Synaptic but their are deb packages available.

This thread has links and info on installing xmms.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=795242](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=795242)

FYI - you will get a number of posts from people that direct you to Audacious.  I find xmms to be much better.  Plays a greater variety of music files, light weight and more stable.

Post back if you need help installing.

Enjoy
