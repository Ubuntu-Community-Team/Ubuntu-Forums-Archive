### Post by h8uthemost on 2010-05-23
I've just recently upgraded to 10.04. And everytime I leave my computer for a few minutes and the screen goes dark, I come back to it and Ubuntu wants my password to unlock the screen. I'm not logging in, it just wants the pass to unlock the screen.

Is there anyway to turn this off? I've been all through the settings but I can't find anything.

Hopefully someone knows how to get rid of this. Thank you.

---

### Post by wojox on 2010-05-23
You need to go into Screensaver Preferences.

---

### Post by coolbrook on 2010-05-23
From your panel

System > Preferences > Screensaver

Ensure 'Lock screen when screensaver is active' is unchecked.

---

### Post by h8uthemost on 2010-05-23
Damn...I can't believe I never thought to check the screensaver prefrences.

That definitely did it. Thank you so much for the help wojox and coolbrook.
