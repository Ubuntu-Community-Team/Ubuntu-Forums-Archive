> How do you edit your bookmarks in Nautilus?

In Nautilus, click Bookmarks>>Edit bookmarks.

> How do you change a link's icon?

If its on your desktop, right click on it. Then click "Properties" and click on the icon to change it.

Telling what you did to make that function work might be a bit more helpful.

To get this function working you will have to open Firefox, then click **Edit**, then **Preferences**, then click **Advanced**, then in the **General** tab under **Browsing** you will see a check box for **Use autoscrolling**, which once checked will allow you to do the scrolling when clicking the middle button and moving the mouse around.
