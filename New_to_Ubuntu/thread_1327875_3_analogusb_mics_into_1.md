### Post by SPazzo on 2009-11-15
Hi!

What you need is a USB mixer.  Basically it's a little box that all 3 mics plug into, and out from the box comes a USB cord that plugs into the computer.  Audacity should record from it.

[This]("http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001T9O5VG") is the kind of thing you're looking for.

Hope that helps.
