### Post by PriceChild on 2009-04-07
I think that these days a good proportion of wireless dongles work fine with Ubuntu... however even if the packaging is the same, the chipset used inside may vary.

Your best bet is to go to where you are buying it from with your laptop to see if it will work, or take a livecd and boot it up on a computer there with the dongle in to test.
