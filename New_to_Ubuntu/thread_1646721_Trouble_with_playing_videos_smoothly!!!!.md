### Post by RutherfordTheBrave on 2010-12-16
I've had the same experience with Ubuntu, both Karmic and now Lucid. You could try updating your graphic card drivers with System > Admin > Hardware Drivers, but make a manual backup of xorg.conf just in case stuff goes screwy.
The graphics card that comes with my machine (Dell Latitude) does not play nice with the public drivers recommended by Ubuntu, so I'm unable to do anything about this problem but accept that I will not be able to watch Netflix or play Minecraft on my work laptop (which is probably for the best anyways).
