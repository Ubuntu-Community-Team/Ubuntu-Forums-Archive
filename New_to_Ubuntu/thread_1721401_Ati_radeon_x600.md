### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-04-04
That's a very old card/chipset -- for which there are no current restricted drivers that will work with any recent versions of Ubuntu (newer than 8.04).

When Ubuntu is installed, it configures the default open-source drivers -- which are all that is available anymore.  There are no drivers you can download that are any more recent.

Wish folks would do their research before telling you to go after drivers that simply do not exist.
