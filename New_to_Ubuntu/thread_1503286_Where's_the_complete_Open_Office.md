### Post by jmszr on 2010-06-06
Legendary_Bibo,

I'm not familiar with how MS Office has it, but the other possibility that I'm aware of in Writer is:

On the right side of the Writer toolbar click on the little downward pointing arrowhead, click on Customize Toolbar > on the box that comes up click on Toolbars (should be default), under Openoffice.org Writer Toolbars put Formatting (or More Controls)and then under Toolbar Content
click on Add, then under Category click on Insert and under Commands find the two Formula, click on one and then Add and then the other. Perhaps that is what you want?
