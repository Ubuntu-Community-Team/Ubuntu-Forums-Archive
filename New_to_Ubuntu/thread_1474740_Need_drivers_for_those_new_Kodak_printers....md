### Post by dalee on 2010-05-06
Hi,

Kodak has little to no support for Linux systems. Nor do they want to support them. So don't hold your breath. And the advice to look at other printers is sound.

That said, I have my eeePC 1101HA able to connect and print wirelessly to a 5250. Thanks to a user here by the name of paulnewall. He has written a driver for the 52xx. You can find it by searching sourceforge for Kodak. Make sure you have libcupsimage2-dev installed. I have yet to try it on my desktop. Since that one is connected to a Hp.
