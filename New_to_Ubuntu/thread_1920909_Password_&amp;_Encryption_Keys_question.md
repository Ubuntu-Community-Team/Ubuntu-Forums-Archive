Well, if you don't intend to use encryption then it doesn't matter. But encrypting your home volume or perhaps your entire hard drive protects your data in case your computer is stolen. I would argue that everyone who has a laptop should encrypt their home partition in case of theft. Desktops, well, I guess that depends on how good you think your home safety is. :D
