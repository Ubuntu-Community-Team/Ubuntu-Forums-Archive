### Post by papibe on 2012-06-18
Hi RTIInstaller.

The best solution for local media (local HD or LAN shares) is not MythTV but [XBMC]("http://xbmc.org/").

Depending how much time did you invest on your Mythbuntu installation, you may either:
[LIST]
[*]Install XBMC inside Mythbuntu.
[*]Install regular Ubuntu and install XBMC.
[*]Use or install XMBCbuntu.
[/LIST]
I hope that points your in the right direction. Tell us how it goes.
Regards.
