### Post by ubudog on 2014-07-04
Yes, gedit supports text indentation, as do emacs and vim and most other text editors.  Personally I'm partial to vim, but emacs is also a great text editor.  Both vim and emacs come with a tutorial for newcomers, and in about 15 minutes you can master the basics.
