### Post by Jagot on 2011-02-02
Are you sure it didn't start up?  Or do you just mean you weren't automatically connected to your wireless network?

By default, Ubuntu seems not to save the setting to connect to the wireless network automatically - you have to set this.

Right click on your networking icon in the top right hand corner and click 'Edit Connections'.  A new window will open in which you need to go to the 'Wireless' tab.  If you see your wireless network listed there, highlight it and then click 'Edit'.  A new window will open, and near the top there'll be a tick box labelled 'Connect automatically'.  Tick that and you should be fine.
