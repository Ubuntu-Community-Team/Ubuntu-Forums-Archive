### Post by Kellemora on 2009-02-28
Hi NNJ

I've had trouble with getting Midi's to play for 3 months and finally figured out what the problem was only a couple of weeks ago.

Edited to ADD:  Every web site I went to with MIDI's they would only load to 0.46% plus or minus and say STOPPED after reaching that point.

The first thing you want to do is REMOVE the Gnome Media Player, called MPlayer.

Then Install Timidity.

Install Mozplugger and the Totem Mozilla PlugIn.

It will show MPlayer as back again, but at least it will work now!
Or at least it did on my computers, all of them!

For just playing on-board Midi's, Timidity works GREAT!
If not blocked by the Gnome Media Player that is!
