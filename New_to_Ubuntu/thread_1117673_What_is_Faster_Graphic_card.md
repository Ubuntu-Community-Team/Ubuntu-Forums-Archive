### Post by JohnLM_the_Ghost on 2009-04-06
Graphics card (aka Video Card) is what drives most that comes up on your screen and (usually) comes with it's own set of processor (GPU unit) and memory (colloquially called VRAM)

Faster Graphics card usually refers to a graphics card with more VRAM and faster clock rates for GPU and VRAM.

NVIDIA GeForce series or ATI Radeon series cards are two examples.

So what is your current graphics card?

EDIT: Considering your specs I assume you might have one of those integrated Intel cards.
They are very basic and don't have really processing power for fancy graphics.
