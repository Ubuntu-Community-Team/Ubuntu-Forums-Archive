### Post by HittingSmoke on 2008-11-03
> **stonecoldjha said:**
> as u can see in the screenshot,the background image of Mac OS themed cairo-dock is not as good as that of the one shown above it.how and where from can i have a beautiful background image like that?

As far as I can tell the only difference there is the rounded corners.

Right click on your dock and click Configure.
Click Advanced Mode int he bottom left corner
Go to backgrounds and under Extern Frame and make sure "Are the bottom left and right corners rounded?" box is checked
Then adjust the Corner radius to your liking, a lower number will make a longer slope on the edge of the dock, a higher number (up to 30) will make a steeper slope

The color is semi transparent and reflective so it will respond differently to different backgrounds you use. You can also adjust the background color in the same settings area, including opacity.
