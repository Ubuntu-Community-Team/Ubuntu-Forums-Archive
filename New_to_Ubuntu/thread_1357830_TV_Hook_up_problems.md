### Post by tom.swartz07 on 2009-12-17
Hey-o

I just hooked up my Karmic box to a big screen the other day. Look on your TV menu to see if there is a 'picture-refresh' option, or something of the sort.

When I was having problems with mine, the resolution was fine, but the picture was skewed off screen. Once I went into the menus on the TV itself and refreshed the video, it worked like a charm!
