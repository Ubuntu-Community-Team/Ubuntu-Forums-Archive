### Post by shadow-of-sin on 2008-07-15
The logon screens are the "GDM Themes" (here:[http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150](http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150) ). To install a theme, go to System-->Administration-->Login Window, click on the local tab, and then click on the Add button and select the theme you downloaded.
