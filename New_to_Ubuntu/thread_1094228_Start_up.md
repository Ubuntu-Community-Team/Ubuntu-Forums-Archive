### Post by overdrank on 2009-03-12
Hi and welcome, what is the model of the graphics card?
You may try and boot into recovery mode and use the xfix option to correct the graphics. Recovery mode is usually the second choice when booting at the grub. After completing the xfix option you should be given the choice to boot normally.
