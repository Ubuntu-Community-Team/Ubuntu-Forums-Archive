### Post by jrothwell97 on 2009-08-26
Assuming you're running vanilla Ubuntu, right-click the volume icon in the top panel and select "Open Volume Control".

If the channel 'PCM' is set low, move the slider up so that it's near the top (but not *at* the top - generally, this is *too* loud and causes the sound to be clipped.)

To switch on the headphones, in the volume control window, select the "Switches" tab and toggle the switch that says "Headphones" or something to that effect.

Good luck!
