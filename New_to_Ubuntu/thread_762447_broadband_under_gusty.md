### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-04-22
What sort of broadband? Cable, ADSL, wireless?

Is the modem connected through Ethernet or through USB? If it has an Ethernet port, that's what you should use. Usually, the username and password is something that you put into the modem's web-based interface, so the modem will connect without you having to explicitly connect. You should look in the manual that came with your modem, for instructions on whether there is a web-based interface, and if so how to get to it.
