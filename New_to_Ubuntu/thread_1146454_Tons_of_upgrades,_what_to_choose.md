### Post by oldsoundguy on 2009-05-02
download them all.  It will upgrade anything you are running and it will go into the synaptic library and update anything you MAY want to use in the future.
Unlike Windows, Linux does not leave a huge pile of garbage behind when it does an update.

BUT

If you do want to "clean" run:

sudo apt-get autoclean  

in terminal should get most of it.  (there are some other steps, but unless you are a constant installer/uninstaller and have a TINY hard drive, really not needed all that much.)
