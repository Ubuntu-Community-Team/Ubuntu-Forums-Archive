### Post by jtarin on 2010-07-09
If you have and use BitDefender now and your happy with the performance, you need to ask yourself, "Why do I need this upgrade?" If you see it listed inSynaptice with an upgraded version that is differnt than what is installed it is your choice to upgrade to that version. You should have no problems upgrading unless their are some drastic changes in the software. More than likely a database upgrade.
