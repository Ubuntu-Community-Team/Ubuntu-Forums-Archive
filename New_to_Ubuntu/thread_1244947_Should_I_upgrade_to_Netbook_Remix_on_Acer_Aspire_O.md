### Post by swoody on 2009-08-20
Well it seems more of a personal choice than anything else. If you already have the standard Ubuntu install, and everything's working fine for you, you can stick with it if you'd like. The netbook remix is geared towards the usability and interface of a netbook, so it is a little more custom tailored to your usage, but again, there's nothing saying you have to use it. I would recommend giving both a shot, and seeing which you prefer. As mentioned above, it's always good to make backups of anything you don't want to risk losing :)
