### Post by LewRockwell on 2009-08-14
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=7737863](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=7737863)

forbid all cookies

disable java
(had to enable javascript for flashblock)

block all pop-ups

use:

ghostery(notifies you of trackers and gives the ability to block them)

ad-block plus(make sure you subscribe to at least one filter)

flashblock(blocks flash cookies(local shared objects)

firebug(easily see the exact source of each and every webpage as desired)

noscript(self-explanatory)


you'd be surprised how much your browsing will be hampered by the above

it should also make you more cautious and selective in the sites you visit

****
also see:

Practical Internet Privacy by Paul Green:
[http://www.lewrockwell.com/orig9/green-p2.1.1.html](http://www.lewrockwell.com/orig9/green-p2.1.1.html)

DNS Leak Fix:
[https://forum.perfect-privacy.com/showthread.php?t=702](https://forum.perfect-privacy.com/showthread.php?t=702)

DNS Spoofing checker:
[https://www.grc.com/dns/dns.htm](https://www.grc.com/dns/dns.htm)

****


you could also check into proxies, PGP, and encryption methods and procedures if you were so inclined...
