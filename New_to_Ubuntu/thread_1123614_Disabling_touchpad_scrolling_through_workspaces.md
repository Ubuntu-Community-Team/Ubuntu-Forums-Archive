### Post by volatilesquirrel on 2009-04-12
I am using Ubuntu 8.10 on a Dell Inspiron 6000 Laptop.  The default setting will scroll though my workspaces when the mouse is positioned over the desktop and the touchpad's scroll bar is vertically scrolled.  I wish to continue to use my scroll bar but not for the purposes of workspace switches.  Anyone know how to change this setting?  I was unable to find anything in preferences or on previous forum postings.

---

### Post by mprince on 2009-04-12
If you are using Compiz, I think this thread will help:

[http://forum.compiz-fusion.org/showthread.php?p=33069#post33069](http://forum.compiz-fusion.org/showthread.php?p=33069#post33069)


ccsm->Viewport Switcher->Desktop-based Viewport Switching->Move Next (default: scroll up)
ccsm->Viewport Switcher->Desktop-based Viewport Switching->Move Prev (default: scroll down)

(ccsm = CompizConfig Settings Manager)
