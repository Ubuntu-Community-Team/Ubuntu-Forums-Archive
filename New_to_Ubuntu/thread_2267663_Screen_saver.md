### Post by hmiersch on 2015-03-03
One thing that drives me nuts about Ubuntu is when I watch a long video on YouTube, the screen saver kicks in without warning, and then I have to enter my password. Is there anything I can do about that? In OSX the screensaver is inactive when a video is being played, which would be the ideal solution...

---

### Post by buzzingrobot on 2015-03-03
In Systems Settings, make sure the "Require my password when waking from suspend" option is unchecked and set "Turn screen off when inactive" to "Never". (Look in the "Brightness and Lock" panel.
