### Post by john stiles on 2009-08-14
Check in the OpenOffice menu, Tools, Options, Languages that Russian appears and is enabled. The text in the document is also probably default English US. Ensure that the doc defaults mark your language Russian. You can quickly check this by blocking some text and clicking on bottom language info box (3rd from left on mine).
