### Post by chrisod on 2009-01-01
Copy the contents of your profile folders in FF and TB. Then in the new install, open FF and TB so that it creates a profile for you. Then close the programs. Then paste the contents of the old profile folders into the new profile folders. Everything will be there when you open the apps again.
