### Post by warfacegod on 2009-12-28
Right click your Panel where it says Applications Places System and select edit menus. Select the appropriate heading under the applications list (I'm guessing Sounds and Video judging from the apps name) and in the pane on the right put a check in the box next to your app. Close that window and go there in the actual menu.
