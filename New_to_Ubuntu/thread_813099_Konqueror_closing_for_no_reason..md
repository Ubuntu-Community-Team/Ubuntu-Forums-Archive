### Post by Tony Sivori on 2008-05-30
This is not a fix, but instead a suggested workaround that you may wish to consider. 

Install Firefox. Use Firefox for all sites that you wish to use and that require JavaScript, Java, Flash and junk like that. 

Then turn all that stuff off (and animations too) in Konqueror, and use Konqueror for everything else. Your general browsing will be much more enjoyable; never a pop up, no garish distracting fast motion ads. In short the www is more like a library rather than a TV channel with constant loud commercials. 

I've done it this way for years, and it has worked great for me.
