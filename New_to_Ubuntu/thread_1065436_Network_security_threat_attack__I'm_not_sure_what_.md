### Post by adult swim on 2009-02-09
open up firefox, then in the address bar where you type in website addresses, type in 192.168.1.1 and hit enter.  a box may appear asking for your username and password, leave the username box blank and for the password put in admin
hit ok and then youll be in your router.  there should be a wireless link, click on that and then look for wireless security and click on it.  now select what type of security you want, and put something in for the passphrase.  once you have done all of that click save settings.  now when you log on to your internet, youll need to know the passprase to get on.
