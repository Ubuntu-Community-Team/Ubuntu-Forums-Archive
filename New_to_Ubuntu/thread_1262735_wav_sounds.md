### Post by mcduck on 2009-09-10
Do you mean the sound that plays when the login screen loads, or the sound that plays when you have logged in?

The first one is set in System/Administration/Login Window, in the Accessibility-tab.

And the later can be changed in System/Preferences/Sound, Sounds-tab, under Alerts and Sound Effects change the Desktop->Login.
