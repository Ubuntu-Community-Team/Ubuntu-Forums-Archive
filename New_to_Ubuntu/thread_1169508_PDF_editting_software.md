I have been looking at various pdf editing options in Ubuntu.
Me and a colleague found pdfedit very difficult to use, I tried all kind of other options (OpenOffice pdf extension, AbiWord, Scribus, Gimp, and more)

My conclusion is that Inkscape works the best so far for editing pdfs, but it can only work on 1 page from a pdf at the time.

There's also commercial pdf editing software for Linux :
[http://www.qoppa.com/psindex.html](http://www.qoppa.com/psindex.html)

I've also tried various MS-Windows based pdf editors in Wine, but
I'm not completely with testing the variety of pdf editor options.
