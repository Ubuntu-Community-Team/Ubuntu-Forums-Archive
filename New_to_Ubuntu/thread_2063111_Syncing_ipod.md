### Post by spaceshipguy on 2012-09-29
Any recent update of iOS will break sync because Apple frequently changes the codes on their database. (Checksum codes I believe). Brave and dedicated people then have to reverse engineer the new changes to work out how to talk to iPods, Iphones etc. all over again.

 I have an iPod touch with iOS 5.0.1 and it will not sync. 

Workarounds include running iTunes in a Windows virtual machine, works for me - and downloading apps that provide a folder to drag and drop files without need to sync (I use OPlayer Lite for music and CloudReaders for comic books).

Blame Apple - they make their devices hard to interact with on purpose.
