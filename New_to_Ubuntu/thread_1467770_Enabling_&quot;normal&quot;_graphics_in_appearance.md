### Post by anewguy on 2010-05-01
Unless something has changed greatly, which I doubt because of the age and cheapness of the S3 UniChrome, you won't be able to run desktop effects (compiz).  I went through a lot of different ideas when I had a laptop a few years ago with that graphics adapter.  The only drivers to try are the openchrome drivers, and the 3d version was iffy at best.  I also started a terminal session and did compiz --replace, at which time some messages appeared on the terminal that showed the Via S3 UniChrome Pro did not support something that compiz needs.

Sorry for the bad news, but it is really just eye candy anyway.  I've never heard of anyone saying that the lack of using compiz made anything unusable to them.
