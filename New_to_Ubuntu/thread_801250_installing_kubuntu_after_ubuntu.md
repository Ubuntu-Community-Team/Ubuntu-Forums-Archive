### Post by Inxsible on 2008-05-20
Yeah just install it in a separate partition and that would keep them isolated from each other. Transferring files between them would be easy, by simply mounting the home drive of ubuntu in kubuntu and vice versa. You can even do it thru a shared partition which is separate than the home drives. That way even your home drives would be isolated.
