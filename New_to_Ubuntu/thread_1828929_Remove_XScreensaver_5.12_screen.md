### Post by JC Cheloven on 2011-08-19
I'd say you don't need to unistall it, since the behaviour is very configurable. 

From the screensaver preferences window you can uncheck "activate screensaver when the pc is inactive" or "block screen when screensaver is active". If I correctly understand, this last one is what you want. 

The timing for the pc to be considered "inactive" are set in power preferences (you have a link to that in the same screensaver window).
