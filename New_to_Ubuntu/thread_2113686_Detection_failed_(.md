### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-02-08
Ubuntu 10.10 is not supported anymore. It cannot recognise any modems made after August 2010, which is probably why your modem is not detected.

Upgrade to 12.04 or 12.10 to get the latest features, hardware support, and more importantly, security updates.

Your modem will probably work in 12.10. I can't guarantee it, but I'm fairly sure.
