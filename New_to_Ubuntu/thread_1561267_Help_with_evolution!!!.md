### Post by TNT1 on 2010-08-26
In evolution, go Edit/Preferences All your accounts will be listed, double click on the one you want, or select and and click edit, will open up settings for that account.

Also, if you don't have the setup assistant plugin enabled, you wont get the wizard thingy even in a new install.
