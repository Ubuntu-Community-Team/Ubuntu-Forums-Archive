### Post by clive littlewood on 2009-01-01
Hi Mantru

Digicam is installed by going to Applications > add/remove

Then in the box that opens you will see a search box

Enter digicam into the box which will the appear on the list.

Tick the box and click apply changes.

The program will be installed for you and appear in applications > Graphics > digicam

Hope this helps and have a happy New Year      :D


Edit:  The software that came with your camera will be for windoze
