### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2019-04-23
The 'PrtSC'-key doesn't put the screenshot in the clipboard, it saves the image in a file named 'Screenshot'+date+time.png in your ~/Pictures directory. To put the image in the clipboard instead you've got to hold the ctrl-key while hitting 'PrtSc'. For more detaile s see [this page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/lts/ubuntu-help/screen-shot-record.html.en") in the Ubuntu Desktop Guide.
