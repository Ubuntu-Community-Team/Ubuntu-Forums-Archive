The infamous MS Windows BSODs could be related to almost anything!

Although folks here DO provide SOME support on MS Windows related problems, BSODs aren't really our specialties.  If you want support on those, suggest you check out MS Windows forums.

You didn't say if your OS was Vista, XP, or Seven. A google search on forums for these should yield some useful results.

But be prepared to do a LOT of troubleshooting yourself.  In most cases, running down the exact source of a BSOD and repairing it involves a lot of work.  Don't expect a one-click kind of solution.
