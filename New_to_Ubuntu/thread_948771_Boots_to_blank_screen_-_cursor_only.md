### Post by Pro-reason on 2008-10-15
From that screen, you can probably get to a text console by hitting Ctrl-Alt-F2.  You can then log on and start typing commands.

It's hard to know what the problem is at this point.  Things you could try include reading the message log (with the *dmesg* command), or installing a proprietary video driver (perhaps with *envyng*).  It would be good if someone knowledgeable could sit down at the computer with you.
