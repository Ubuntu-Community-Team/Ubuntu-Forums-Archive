### Post by Paqman on 2008-04-25
You settings are generally all stored in your /home. Apart from that you might want to take a copy of your menu.lst, sources.lst and maybe fstab if you've got shares mounted. Back all those up and just copy them over.

Then to sort all your packages, you'll want to do this:
[How to reinstall all your packages in one command](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=261366)
