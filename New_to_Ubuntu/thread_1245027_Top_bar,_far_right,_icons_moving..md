### Post by mcduck on 2009-08-20
> **PGScooter said:**
> That works for me on the icons that I add to the panel, but on the system and program added icons, that method does not work for me.

I bet you are now talking about notification icons..

They are not individual applets or panel launchers, just icons that running programs place inside the Notification Area-applet. So you can't move them separately, but you can move the whole Notification Area-applet itself. Immediately to the left of these icons you should see a small handle that allows you to move the Notification Area-applet and access it's settings.
