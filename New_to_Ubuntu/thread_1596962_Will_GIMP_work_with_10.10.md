### Post by Ctrl-Alt-F1 on 2010-10-14
As someone earlier stated it's in the software center.  Or "sudo apt-get install gimp"

The reason it's not on the cd by default is because a lot of people don't need an image editing tool as complex as Gimp, so they took it out to save room on the cd.
