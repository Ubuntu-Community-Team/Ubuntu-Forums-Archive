### Post by ubunterooster on 2010-10-19
You need to customize your theme. Right-click on the desktop and choose "change desktop background" Go to the tab that says "theme" and choose the "Customize..." button.

Go to the "colors" tab and change the color from orange to blue

### Post by ivarn on 2010-10-19
You can also change the colours, buttons and icons on the theme if you go to your home folder /.themes. there you'll find all your installed themes.
You can find more themes on gnome-look.org if you want to.
To install a new theme, go to change my background, theme tab, and just drag & drop the archive you downloaded.

### Post by ivarn on 2010-10-19
I mean don't extract the archive. Just drag the archive file from the desktop to the theme window in Change my background > theme.
You can also click the install button there and browse to the archive.
The drag n' drop method works for most things you wanna install (icons, coursers, backgrounds, screensavers, themes etc ).
