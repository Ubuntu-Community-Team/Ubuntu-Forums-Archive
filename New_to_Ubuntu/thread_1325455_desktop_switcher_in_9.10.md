### Post by cg49me on 2009-12-14
The easiest way I've found is to go to System > Preferences > Startup Applications, disable Netbook Launcher, then restart - you should be back to your normal desktop.

*edit*

You may also want to disable Maximus - it automatically maximizes opened windows, and removes their title bars when maximized.
