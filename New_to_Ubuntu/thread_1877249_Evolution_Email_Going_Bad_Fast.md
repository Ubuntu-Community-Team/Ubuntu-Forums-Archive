### Post by LinuxCharms on 2011-11-07
Don't feel bad, this a typical problem. 
Good news: You won't need to scrap your version of Gnome/Ubuntu.
Just go into Applications>Ubuntu Software Center and search (in the upper-right hand corner) for Evolution. Uninstall it then re-install it by searching for it the same way. But wait for it be taken off the system first.

Once that's done open it up the same way as always and check if it's working. If not, something is screwy with your build of Ubuntu itself. You still won't have to scrap the whole thing, but you will ***_NEED_*** to get Gmail. For one, it's simple to use, it adds contacts automatically if you email all of them just some simple message which can be copy and pasted. You will be a lot happier, I tried Evolution. Sucks, to be honest.

Good luck! <3
