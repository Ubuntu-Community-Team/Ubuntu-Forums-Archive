### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2008-04-23
ffmpeg and mencoder are the major video encoders.  They are command line:
ffmpeg -i inputfile outputfile

There are some GUIs for them: [**Fuoco Tools**]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=652843") and [**WinFF**]("http://www.winff.org/").
