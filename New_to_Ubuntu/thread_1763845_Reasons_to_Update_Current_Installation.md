### Post by Zill on 2011-05-22
Redblade20XX:  Three questions:  1)  Is this your only PC?  2)  Does it run a single OS (eg Ubuntu)?  3) Do you *need* to use this PC (eg for work)?

If the answer to these three questions is "yes" then I suggest you should stick with your working system until the support period expires.

Otherwise, if the system is non-critical then, of course, upgrade whenever you like.  Just be prepared for some breakage of things that worked previously!  Newer releases *may* have eliminated some old bugs but, unfortunately, may have introduced some new ones. ;-)
