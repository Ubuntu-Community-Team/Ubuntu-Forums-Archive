### Post by PinkFloyd102489 on 2008-06-11
I dont know how well it'll work, but you can use ffmpeg on the command line. Fairly simple to do and it's what I use.

```

ffmpeg -i orignal.avi new.wmv

```

Or if you want graphical, try out Avidemux.
