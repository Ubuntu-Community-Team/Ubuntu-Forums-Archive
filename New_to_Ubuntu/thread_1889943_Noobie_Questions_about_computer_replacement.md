### Post by snowpine on 2011-12-02
I assume you're talking about Firefox bookmarks? They are stored in your ~/.mozilla hidden folder. It is easy to sync your Firefox information across two (or more) computers following this how-to: [http://support.mozilla.com/en-US/kb/How%20to%20sync%20Firefox%20settings%20between%20computers](http://support.mozilla.com/en-US/kb/How%20to%20sync%20Firefox%20settings%20between%20computers)

Easy instructions for installing Ubuntu on your new computer are here: [http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download)

Last but not least, this list will help you shop for a new computer: [http://www.ubuntu.com/certification/](http://www.ubuntu.com/certification/)

Installing Ubuntu is a 4-step process as described here: [http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download)

This will WIPE YOUR DATA so be sure you have a backup!

Or, you can upgrade-in-place, keeping all your data and installed applications: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes)

**In all cases I recommend a full backup of your data/documents, don't skip this step!**
