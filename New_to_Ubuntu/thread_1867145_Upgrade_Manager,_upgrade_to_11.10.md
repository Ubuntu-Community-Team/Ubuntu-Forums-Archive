### Post by ubupirate on 2011-10-22
> **Sunfist said:**
> Not a problem really but I am curious why my update manager still shows my available update as 11.04 when the (supposedly) stable version of 11.10 has been released and has been out for over a week. How long does it take before it starts saying 11.10 or is there something I have to do before it switches over.

Because you would have to upgrade to 11.04 and then upgrade to 11.10. The only option that lets you skip releases is going from LTS->LTS such as 10.04 to 12.04 in April 2012.

And I would advise against upgrading, unless you absolutely have all your data backed up and after testing 11.10 to see if it works for you. And even then I'd do a clean install over upgrade, too many nightmare stories of borked systems.
