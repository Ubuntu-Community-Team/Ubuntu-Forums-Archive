### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2010-08-29
The [Ubuntu Manual]("http://ubuntu-manual.org/") is pretty much what you need to start of learning Ubuntu/Linux. The document is easy to understand, it has lots of screenshots, and more. It is actually titled "Getting Started with Ubuntu 10.04". It's free to download. Just follow the link above.

You can also interact with many Ubuntu users over IRC -- a dedicated channel [Ubuntu Beginners Team]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BeginnersTeam/") #ubuntu-beginners

And of course, don't forget to post your queries here in the [Ubuntu Forums]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org").

As for your bootsplash issue, perhaps you can use [StartUp-Manager]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/StartUpManager"). Open Ubuntu Software Center and install it from there. You'll see it in System > Administration afterwards.
