### Post by Pogeymanz on 2010-08-18
Firefox does not depend on Gnome or KDE or GTK or QT. Firefox uses XUL, which, by default tries to imitate your current GTK settings.

Only Firefox and Chrome have their own graphics toolkits as far as I know. Everything else depends on GTK or QT at least. And Epiphany depends (kind of) on Gnome and Konqueror depends on KDE.
