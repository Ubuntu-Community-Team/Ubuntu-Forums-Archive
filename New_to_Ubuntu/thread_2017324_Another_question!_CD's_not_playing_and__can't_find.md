### Post by deadflowr on 2012-07-05
Is a CD icon showing up in the launcher dock(at the bottom area of the launcher)?

If not, a quick way to see the disk is to open up the program disk utility, look at the left side pane and find the CD/DVD device you put the disk into and click on it. In the main area, if any disk is installed some sort of reading should show up, however the most likely scenario would be the disk will say unknown as it is probably unreadable by your hardware.
