### Post by Ubuntscrewed on 2010-04-03
This is a weird one.  

I'm trying to retrieve the clear text of my wife's saved yahoo mail password. It's saved in Firefox 3.5.8 and she can login using the saved password; however, she can't remember it and also doesn't remember the answer to the security question.  Therefore she can only access her emails via our desktop computer and if we somehow lose the cookie (or whatever it is that stores it) then she'll be locked out of her email account forever.  

We're using Karmic.

Thanks in advance.

---

### Post by empty_spaces on 2010-04-03
Edit - Preferences - Security Iab - Saved Passwords - Show Passwords.

---

### Post by Ubuntscrewed on 2010-04-04
Awesome. Thank you.  That worked perfectly.

### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-04-04
You can also export your passwords using [Password Exporter](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search/?q=Password+Exporter) extension.

If you want to make your passwords more accessible by your system, you can use [KDE Wallet Integration](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search/?q=KDE+Wallet+Integration) extension (I use it and it is great) or [Gnome Keyring Integration](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search/?q=Gnome+Keyring+Integration), depending on which desktop environment you use.
