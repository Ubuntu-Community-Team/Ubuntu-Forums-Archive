### Post by Earsplit on 2011-05-30
Try installing the extension Flash-Aid.  It solved most of my flash problems in firefox.  

To install, search for "Flash-Aid" in Tools > Add-Ons

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/)
