### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-06-14
> **vasa1 said:**
> I agree with Kurt. If you're in a situation where you have to use docx for work, just use the real thing, MS Office. I'm not sure why people require docx but that's another issue.

Folks actually don't "require" docx.  

The reason that shows up so often is that, starting with Word in Office 2007, MS changed the default file extension for saving Word documents from ".doc" to "docx".  They continued this with Word in Office 2010.

You have to go out of your way to force Word to save files in the OLD ".doc" format -- something most folks either don't know how to do, or aren't bothered to learn.
