### Post by Geoff918 on 2009-09-03
You can Google "[app-type] Ubuntu", but in many cases the things you're asking for are already included in Ubuntu by default. Ubuntu and most other Linux distros are intended to have most broad spectrum applications already installed. Sometimes it's just knowing what is at the CLI and not in the menu system (e.g. ftp)
