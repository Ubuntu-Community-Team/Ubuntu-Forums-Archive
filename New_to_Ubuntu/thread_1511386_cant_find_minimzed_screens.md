### Post by dnairb on 2010-06-16
I assume that you have a panel at the bottom of the screen.

Right-click this panel and select **Add to Panel...**

A list of items will appear. Towards the bottom of this list is an item called **Window List**. Select this, click **Add** and then **close**.
