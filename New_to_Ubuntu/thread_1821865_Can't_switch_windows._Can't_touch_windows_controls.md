### Post by Miljet on 2011-08-09
I don't know if this applies in your situation, but you can add an app to one of your panels called "force quit".  As long as your mouse still works, it works a treat to close frozen applications.

Right click on empty spot on panel, select "add to panel", and select "force quit".
