### Post by donnagarnet on 2011-07-21
Last week I reinstalled Ubuntu in a (successful) attempt to clear up several problems.

One really annoying one was that Rhythmbox was the only media player I could get to work, & that didn't work very well.

I tried Banshee because I've read that a) it works well with Creative Labs products, or b) it was made to work with Creative products.

I loaded it with music (some from a memory stick, some from albums). I selected an album to listen to. It was one I haven't listened to in several years, & by the middle of the first song, I was reminded why I hadn't listened to the album in so long.

Banshee won't play any other album, not even now that I've deleted it from Banshee's library.  I've eliminated all non-utility extensions, & that didn't help at all.

On the other hand, as soon as I plugged in my Zen, Banshee grabbed it, showed that it was mounted & asked me what I wanted to do with it (nothing until I figure out why Banshee won't play music).

What happened was when I clicked on a different album, Banshee showed it was being played, but it was actually playing only the first album.  Now that album's been deleted, when i pick another one, Banshee shows that it's being played (by showing me the album & song) but nothing happens.  The "on" button stays 'off'.

Any ideas at all?  Thanks so much, kids.

### Post by Muskegman on 2011-07-21
If you have added all your songs to your home/music folder Banshee should automatically detect those songs and add them all to your Banshee list.

You may also have to create a song list which will play all the songs continuosly. If you try and play them directly from the file folder they will only play 1 song at a time.

I have a large song database 23k + and I have had to try several different music players until I found 1 that worked well for me.

Clementine seems ok and I have had some success with Amarok, you could also give Guayadeque a try I am currently using that in ubuntu 11.10 alpha 2.

Try different players until you find one you like , 

Good Luck:D
