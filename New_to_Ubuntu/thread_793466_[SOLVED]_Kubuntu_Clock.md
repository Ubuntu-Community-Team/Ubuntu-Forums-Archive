### Post by helives95 on 2008-05-13
The clock that came with Kubuntu is set to 24 hour. Is there any way to set it to am/pm format?

---

### Post by NightwishFan on 2008-05-13
Yes right click the clock choose time/date format, and change to am/pm accordingly, hit apply, then log out and back in.

---

### Post by Mhurst1 on 2008-05-13
Yes right click on it and select date & Time format. Then select Time and dates. Then change it under Time format.
