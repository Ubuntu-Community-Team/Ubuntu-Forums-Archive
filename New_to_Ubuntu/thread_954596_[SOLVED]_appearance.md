### Post by taqkavar on 2008-10-21
I am assuming the by appearance, you mean desktop background or as some call it, wallpaper.
Right-click on the desktop, click on "Change desktop background". Then click on "add..." and browse to the background you want. select, click open and there you have it , do this with all 4 backgrounds and each will be added to the thumbnail view so you can choose between them with ease.

### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-10-21
What I would do is create a folder in my home directory, and save the pictures in there. Then open Appearance click Add, and navigate to the new folder and double click on the desired picture. That's it.
Good luck.
