Probably. This took me a second to find:

[http://www.google.com/cse?cx=partner-pub-9300639326172081%3Ac6lzq8-dhwz&ie=UTF-8&sa=Search&q=upgrade+10.04+10.10&hl=enSorry]("http://www.google.com/cse?cx=partner-pub-9300639326172081%3Ac6lzq8-dhwz&ie=UTF-8&sa=Search&q=upgrade+10.04+10.10&hl=enSorry"). I know nothing about Cryptkeeper. 

If the encrypeted files are stored on the same partition as your OS (no separate /home partition) then you would lose them. If they are on a /home partition, no. Just install over the existing 10.04 but DON'T format the /home partition. :)
