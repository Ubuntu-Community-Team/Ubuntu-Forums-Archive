### Post by Norm24 on 2008-06-22
Trying to reduce the size of some concert photos to upload as attachments to a website.The photos are to big and I need to reduce their size below 2MB before the site will allow me to upload them.

Without sacrificing any of the actual photo how do I do this?

---

### Post by lswest on 2008-06-22
open GIMP and open the image.  Then go to image-->scale image then change one value (it will adjust the other one so it stays proportional).

Then save the image as a .jpg at max quality.  Check the file size.  If it's still >2MB, then change the quality when saving it as a .jpg (or use a .png if you want - since it's intended for web use it'll offer better compression)

### Post by silkstone on 2008-06-22
When you've scaled/resized it, you may need to do a little sharpening...

Filters > Enhance > Unsharp Mask, and then try it with the radius between 1 and 2, Amount between 0.5 and 0.8, and Threshold at zero.
