### Post by deadflowr on 2013-04-27
Open the dash and type gnome-screenshot or just screenshot.

Open it and  set delay however many seconds, and the screenshot will capture after whatever time you set.

You'll be able to capture the expo workspace view, or dropdown menus(which aren't capture-able normally)

Then wait and eventually, you should get a save screen.
