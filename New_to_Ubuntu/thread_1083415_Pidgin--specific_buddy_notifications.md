### Post by tarahmarie on 2009-03-29
Hey, all:

You can set something called a "Buddy Pounce" in Pidgin when you see a contact online; it will let you execute actions like a popup notification or opening a chat window.  Apparently, I have to wait until the people I want to be notified about are online, then right-click on them for a context menu, but at least it's a solution.

There you go!
