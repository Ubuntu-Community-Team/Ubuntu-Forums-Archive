### Post by silverglade00 on 2011-05-11
Just a different approach to picking package managers. Ubuntu Software Center is made to be more like browsing through an app store. Synaptic just lists the packages so you can pick them. Synaptic can be easier if you know what you are looking for and USC can be easier for finding different programs that do the same thing. USC also hides all of the dependency files so you just choose the programs you want. Synaptic shows you every package.
