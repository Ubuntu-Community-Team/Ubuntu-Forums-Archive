### Post by stanz on 2009-03-11
Have you checked out 'Preferred Applications'?
Main Panel, System->Preferences->Preferred Applications->Internet {Tab}...
Is your browser chosen?
And "Open link with/in... checked--with your preference?

Other then that, I find those options in the particular programs "options/preferences"
menu....somewhere.
