### Post by lidex on 2009-08-17
Could be an errant entry in your bookmarks menu. Try opening the library via the "Bookmarks\Organize Bookmarks" menu option or key combo "Ctrl+Shift+o". In the left pane click to drop-down under"All Bookmarks". Click on "Bookmarks Toolbar" to highlight. Its contents should display in the right pane. Right-click and delete any unwanted entries.

If that doesn't work it may be a "Smart Bookmark". I found that feature to be slightly annoying so I disabled it in "about:config" by changing value for "browser.places.createdSmartBookmarks" from true to false.
