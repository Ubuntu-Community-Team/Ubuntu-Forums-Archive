### Post by Kdawkins on 2008-04-24
You don't need the CD, if you have 7.10 installed, you can go to the update manager and hit "check for updates." Then in the upper half it should say "distribution upgrade" and just follow the easy instructions.

--Kevin

### Post by ace007 on 2008-04-24
Are you using the alternative CD?  It is possible to upgrade 7.10 with the 8.04 alternative CD.  The simplest thing to do would be upgrade without the CD.  To do this launch the update manager and select the upgrade button.
