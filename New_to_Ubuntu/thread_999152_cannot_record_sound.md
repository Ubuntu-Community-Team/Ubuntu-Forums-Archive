### Post by markbuntu on 2008-12-01
In System/Preferences/Sound/Audio Conferencing/Sound capture what is the setting?
Try changing it to ALSA or PulseAudio.

There is a little guide here for getting sound set up properly. Once it is set up, you should be able to record no problem. It is for Intrepid but should also work for Hardy:


[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=997506](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=997506)
