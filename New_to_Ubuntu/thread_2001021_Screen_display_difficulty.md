### Post by Reason NL on 2012-06-10
I haven't seen any options within any OS to fix this particular issue with digital outputs.
The nVidia driver does support this for analog video ouput.

Haven't seen a TV that did not have this option on a digital input.
Be sure you use HDMI(DVI) and select 1920x1080 as resolution and search for the option within the menu of your TV to disable overscan or something like full size or pixel mapping.
