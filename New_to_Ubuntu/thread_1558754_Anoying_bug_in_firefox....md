### Post by Vaphell on 2010-08-22
iirc it's a compiz bug. After going out of screensaver it erroneously draws firefox suggestions behind the firefox window, thus making it invisible for the user. Workarounds fooman mentioned work or you can disable compiz.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/firefox-3.5/+bug/438868](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/firefox-3.5/+bug/438868)
