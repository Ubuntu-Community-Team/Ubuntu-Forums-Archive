### Post by Favux on 2009-04-30
Hi linuxneeewb,

If you go here you will find step by step instructions:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1038949](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1038949)  It may look a little overwhelming at first but if you read through it you'll see it has been broken down fairly well.  Ask if you have questions.  Since you have Jaunty after you look over the first HOW TO go back up to the top and read Jaunty Users.  Just be sure to read through it and get a handle on things before starting.  You shouldn't have to compile the drivers, you should use the ones that come with Jaunty.  You do have to compile 0.8.3-2 just to get the kernel driver wacom.ko (used by usb tablets like yours).

I think you would probably want Xournal and CellWriter at a minimum.
