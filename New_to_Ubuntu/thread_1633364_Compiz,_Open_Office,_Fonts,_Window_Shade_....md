### Post by Yarui on 2010-11-29
Are you sure it's something that is being caused by compiz?  Have you tried disabling compiz to see if the problem continues?  In case you don't know how to do that, just right click your desktop and select "Change Desktop Background" then go to the "Visual Effects" tab and selet None.  Your screen will flash and compiz will be disabled.  If the problem continues while compiz is off, I doubt there is anything compiz could do to fix it.
