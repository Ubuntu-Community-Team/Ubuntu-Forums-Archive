### Post by orlox on 2009-05-17
I have a dv6000. The wireless device is a broadcom card wich needs to download restricted firmware to work correctly. For this, youll need to plug your computer to a wired network, open system->administration->hardware controllers
and install one of the ones that says broadcom (as far as I remember both work, but you can try to switch if it doesnt work for you)
