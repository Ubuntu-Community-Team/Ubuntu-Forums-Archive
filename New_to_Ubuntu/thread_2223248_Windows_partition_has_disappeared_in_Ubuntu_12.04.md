### Post by oldfred on 2014-05-10
It may need chkdsk from Windows?

Ubuntu will not mount hibernated nor damaged partitions needing chkdsk to prevent further damage that chkdsk or hibernation may cause.
And sometimes Windows will mount a partition that need chkdsk.
