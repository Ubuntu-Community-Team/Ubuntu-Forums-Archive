### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-07-05
This is a shot in the dark, but you haven't provided much information.

At the bottom left corner of the main Evolution window should be a cable plug icon  - is that disconnected or does it appeared to be plugged together? If it's unplugged left click on it and it should reconnect (work offline to work online). You can also find this by selecting "File/Work Online" is it's offline.

If it is connected (I'll assume your network is working - internet browser) in evolution open "edit/preferences" and under "Mail Accounts" is at least one of them enabled? If not enable your mail account by checking the box next to your account.

Perhaps a place to start.
