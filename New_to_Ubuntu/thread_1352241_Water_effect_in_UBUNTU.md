### Post by kellemes on 2009-12-11
Open your Compiz Settings Manager (ccsm from commandline), enable the Water Effect, set the initiate-key to something like <Control><Super> (default setting in my case), and now pressing Ctrl-Super (windows-key) makes the screen ripple like water.

Fun for about 2 minutes at best.. ;-)
