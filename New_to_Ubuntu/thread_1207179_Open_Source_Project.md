First, PLEASE look through this page on Debian GNU/Linux's site. There are links to many things that already exist that need a maintainer. See if there is something there that you'd be interested in working on. 

[http://www.debian.org/devel/wnpp/]("http://www.debian.org/devel/wnpp/")

[http://wiki.debian.org/HelpDebian]("http://wiki.debian.org/HelpDebian")

Ubuntu is derived from on of the newer, more unstable branches of Debian -- I forget which one -- so your help will flow through into not just Ubuntu, but the tens and hundreds of other Debian derivatives as well.

(And while you're at it, might I beg you to take at least a passing glance at TED? :-( It's a wordprocessor that makes Abiword look like OpenOffice in size, doesn't drag in half of GNOME when you don't want it to, and was originally designed as a MS Wordpad replacement. It filled it's role very well, when it was maintained. I'd work on it myself, but I don't have the coding knowledge yet.)
