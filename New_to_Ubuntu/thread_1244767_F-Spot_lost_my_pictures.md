### Post by Guilden_NL on 2009-08-19
Art,
Right click on any of the thumbnails of the 97 photos that you can't find.  At the top of the pop up menu should be "Copy Photo Location"  left click on that.  Then go to the Ubuntu menu Accessories and open Text Editor, then paste the photo location into the text editor and that's where you will find the photos.

To set where you want FSpot to import the photos to, while in FSpot go to Edit, Preferences and then Import Settings Folders.  As a suggestion, I created a folder called FSpot_Import under Photos.  That way if I don't finish moving them to the folders I want, I can come back and know that those photos need to be properly filed.

I hope this helps you out!
