### Post by Shazaam on 2008-07-06
A few ways..
System>Preferences>Main Menu
Right-click the panel near Applications-Places-System, choose "Edit menus"
Depending on your Ubuntu version open a terminal- type in...
```
alacarte
```
or...
```
gmenu-simple-editor
```

Have fun.
