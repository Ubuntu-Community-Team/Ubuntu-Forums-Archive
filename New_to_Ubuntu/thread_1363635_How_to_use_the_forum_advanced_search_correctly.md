### Post by cforput on 2009-12-24
I seem to have a very difficult time when searching these forums. The trouble appears when I want to search for multiple words (which is just about every time!). I can't find a way to make the search tool look for ALL words in a post. For example, if I put in Thunderbird Full Screen, my results will come back with any post that has Thunderbird, Full or Screen in them which doesn't help me at all. How do I get the search tool to only show posts with ALL words entered?

### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-12-24
Either try a search using plain old google with **search phrase site:ubuntuforums.org** as your search term, or try using **[www.uboontu.com](www.uboontu.com)**, a specialist search engine.  This latter looks in other sites as well, but can be very helpful.

Both are much, much better than the forum search.
