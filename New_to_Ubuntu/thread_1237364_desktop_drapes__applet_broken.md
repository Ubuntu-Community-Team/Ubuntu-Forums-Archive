### Post by mystmaiden on 2009-08-11
I'm going to answer my own post here just in case someone runs into the same difficulty. I'm not sure why others who had posted bug reports ran into this problem, but I now know why I did. 

I had removed the 'universal access' icon from the panel, and when I did it somehow interfered with drapes. When I added it back to the panel - voila! there was drapes again.
