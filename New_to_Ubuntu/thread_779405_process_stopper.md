### Post by monkeymoo on 2008-05-02
You can add the "Force quit" button to a panel. 
Right Click on the panel -> Add to panel, choose "Force Quit" and click Add. 
Then when you want to kill an app you click on the panel icon and then click on the program window you want to kill. 

I did this ages ago but rarely use it as most things in Ubuntu work!
