### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-07-27
Try this:

Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]System>Preferences>Main Menu[/COLOR]

When the window opens, use the scroll bar on the right to scroll right to the bottom of the list.

Make sure that there is a tick in the box for the [COLOR="Blue"]Add/Remove[/COLOR] item. If not, click in the box to add it.

Close Main Menu.

When you click on [COLOR="Blue"]Applications[/COLOR], the Add/Remove function should now be at the very bottom of the drop-down menu.
