### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-07-07
Did you burn the "**iso image**" or the "iso file" onto the DVD disk?

Are there multiple folders/files on the DVD when you open its contents? or just one file.

Because the former in both cases would imply that you've done it right.

The latter means that you have not burned the image to disk. And you should use an image burner to extract and burn the image. Dragging and dropping the file won't solve your issues.
