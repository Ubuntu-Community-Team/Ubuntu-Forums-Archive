### Post by Carborundum on 2012-06-03
The Unity Launcher does not auto hide in the most recent version of Ubuntu (12.04). This behavior can also be configured through System Settings > Appearance.

IIRC, in older versions you can modify the launcher's behavior through compizconfig-settings-manager (which is not installed by default, and should be used with care as it is can mess up your system).

To answer your question, you can make all kinds of suggestions - technical and non-technical both - at [brainstorm.ubuntu.com]("http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/").
