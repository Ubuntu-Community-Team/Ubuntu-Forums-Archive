### Post by corncob on 2011-08-24
Yea, It opens up in file browser as soon as I insert an SD card.  An icon also appears on the desktop as well as in the launchers.  As a workaround till we find out what's wrong -- does the camera connect to the computer with a USB cable?  If so, you could leave the card in the camera and retrieve your pictures that way.  There is also such a thing as a USB adapter for an SD card.  They used to come with some SDHC cards in case you had an older card reader that couldn't handle the HC format.
