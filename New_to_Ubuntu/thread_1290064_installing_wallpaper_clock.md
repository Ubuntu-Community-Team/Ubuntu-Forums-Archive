### Post by swoody on 2009-10-13
To install the other wallpaper clocks, did you right click the wallpaper clock screenlet on your desktop and choose &#8220;Install Wallpaper Clock&#8221;?

Then to change the clock to a different one, right click the screenlet again and choose &#8220;Change Wallpaper Clock&#8221;. It takes a few seconds to load so just give it a little bit for it to change.

Let us know if this takes care of your question, or if you were having errors with one of these steps :)
