### Post by davdo2004 on 2011-09-13
Open up synaptic and click on 'installed' and see if there is anything there that you can remove that you never use to free up disk space. You could even uninstall software centre itself. Also, have a look how much junk is in your browser cache, that can often reclaim 100's of megs of space. Installing things outside of your main system is inviting big problems.
