### Post by bcom on 2008-06-16
I don't know if your headphones are USB or not.  If they are here is what I have done to get my USB headphones to work:

Reboot system with USB headphones plugged in.

Go to System Preferences - Sound and under Devices I choose USB audio in all of the drop done boxes.  

I'm using Ubuntu 8.04.

### Post by bobnutfield on 2008-06-16
If your laptop is a Toshiba, this is a common problem.  I simply open the volume control and mute the speakers I do not want to hear.  If I want the internal laptop speakers to be hear, I unmute them.  Not the way it is supposed to work, I know, but many other Toshiba users have reported this.  There is probably a fix, but this is an easy solution for me.
