### Post by AndyCooll on 2008-04-25
If you're feeling confident you could always try another server to download your apps from.

If you click System > Administration > Software Sources then choose the Ubuntu Software tab. Where it says "Download from" click on that and choose "Other". Then click on "Select Best Server". That might get you a faster download, since certain servers are going to be hammered more at this time than others.
