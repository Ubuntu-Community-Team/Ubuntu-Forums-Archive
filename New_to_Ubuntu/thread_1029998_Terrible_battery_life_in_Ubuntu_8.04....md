### Post by semi_fiction on 2009-01-04
You could wear the battery down to see if it discharges slower after a while, battery power detection isn't always correct. You can also configure your laptop to shut down when your battery gets critically low, then see how long it takes before it shuts itself down. That way you won't have to completely drain the battery.
I also noticed a difference between Ubuntu and Vista, I get about 3 hours battery life in Ubuntu and 4 hours in Vista.
