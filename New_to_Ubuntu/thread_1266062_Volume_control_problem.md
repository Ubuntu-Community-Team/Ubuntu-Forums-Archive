### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-09-14
Your volume control should be in the top panel; it's a little speaker icon. Right-click it and choose Open Volume Control.

This brings up a detailed mixer. Make sure that Master, PCM and Front are all turned up. PCM should **not** be at the very top, maybe around 3/4. If you don't have Front, then don't worry.

### Post by ankspo71 on 2009-09-14
Hi
It is also hidden in the start menu, that is if you removed the volume control from your top panel and prefer it like that. You can right click on your start menu (in the corner of the panel) the click on 'edit menus'. Navigate to 'sound and video' and enable volume control.
