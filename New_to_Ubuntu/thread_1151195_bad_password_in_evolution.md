### Post by mprince on 2009-05-06
Have you tried unchecking the box that says 'Remember Password' under your mail account.

Edit>Preferences> then click on your account

You should uncheck 'Remember Password' in both the 'Receiving Email tab' and the 'Sending Email tab'.

You can recheck these later, but you want Evolution to forget your password for now.
