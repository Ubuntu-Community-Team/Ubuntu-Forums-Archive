### Post by st33med on 2008-12-24
For your iTunes needs, you will probably want to use Banshee, as it has a familiar interface and can sync with your iPod.
```
sudo apt-get install banshee
```

However, you cannot sync videos bought on iTunes since they have DRM on them, and you cannot get around it either.  But, for the music, you might want to burn a CD or DVD of all your purchased iTunes Store Music that is not DRM-Free and then re-rip it to your hard drive.
