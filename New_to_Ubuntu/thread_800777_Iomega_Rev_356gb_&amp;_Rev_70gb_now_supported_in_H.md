### Post by Donalb on 2008-05-20
Hey all, 
Since I did a search on this myself recently and got no answer, it's possible others may do the same...so just to say my Iomega Rev Drives, 35GB & 70GB,  are now natively recognised in Hardy Heron (8.04) with full UDF write support. Great! (This wasn't the case in 7.10)

This will mean the just released Rev 120GB will also be supported.

For those interested they are recognised as the correct Rev drives in the file browser, not as CD drive, once the media is inserted. When ejected they are seen as CD drives, unsurprisingly.. and quite correctly!

I haven't yet tried any of the free Open Source backup tools ( as LoneTAR & Microlite have always worked but I just want a home backup solution.) 

Regards
Donal
