### Post by avtolle on 2008-07-03
One other thing I thought of after posting initially; for whatever reason, 8.04 seems to have brought more users in, and the added volume is likely also slowing things down a bit. 

redfox1160, to follow up a bit on monkey56657's thought a bit, in software sources, you may select other, and the app will try to find the "best" server for you. Be aware that if on a mirror other than main or U.S. main that sometimes there is a delay in updates, that is, some updates will be available later on the "best" mirror than otherwise on the main mirror. Something I noticed.
