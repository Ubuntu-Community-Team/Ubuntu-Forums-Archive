> **audiomick said:**
> You will never read the words "consult your system administrator" ever in any Ubuntu or any other Linux help or on the forums. Isn't that nice?:P

This is not strictly accurate.  Some man pages (generally for older GNU utils) contain that phrase, as do some of the docs at [the Linux Document Project]("http://tldp.org").  You just need to remember that *you* are your own system administrator; so when a guide says "consult your system administrator", it means that you need to do something as admin.

That phrase turns up pretty infrequently nowadays, but it *is* still out there.  It might be a good idea for authors of manpages and guides to go and edit it out of existence.
