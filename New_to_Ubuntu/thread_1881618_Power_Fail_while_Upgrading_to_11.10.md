### Post by hansdown on 2011-11-15
Welcome to the forum, ile3000gt.

If you have a "live" boot disk, start it, and, open a terminal...

ctrl+alt+t.

Enter,

```
gksudo nautilus
```

You should be able, to drag and drop your files, to a usb,etc.
