### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-11-10
When you first install, your login password and the keyring password are the same.  If you later change only your login password, you will start getting these messages for entering the keyring password because they don't match.  Once you reset your keyring password to match your new login password, you should not be getting the messages anymore.
