### Post by achase79 on 2009-07-03
download [scanModem]("http://132.68.73.235/linmodems/packages/scanModem.gz") and run the executable.  This will tell you what modem you have.  You can check out [www.linmodems.org]("http://www.linmodems.org") for more info for your specific modem type.
