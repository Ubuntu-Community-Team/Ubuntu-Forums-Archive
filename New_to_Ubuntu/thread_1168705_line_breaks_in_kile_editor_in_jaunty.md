### Post by utillich on 2009-05-24
Hi everyone,
I'm new here (and to linux in general), so forgive me if this is a dumb question:

I upgraded to Ubuntu 9.04 and installed Kile from the repository. I like the look of the new Kile, however for some reason the lines don't break at the end of the editor window, meaning I have to do a lot of horizontal scrolling wich i find really anoying. 
I searched in the settings and even downgraded to 2.0.1 (which i think was the standard with ubuntu 8.10), yet the lines still dont break as they used to.

Any help would be much apreciated.

---

### Post by utillich on 2009-05-28
Ok I found it (and feel kinda dumb now)

its: View -> Dynamic word wrap
      or simply pressing f10
