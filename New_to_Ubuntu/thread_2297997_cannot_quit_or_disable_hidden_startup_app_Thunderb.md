### Post by howefield on 2015-10-08
Try Thunderbird  > Addons > Extensions and disable the "Messaging Menu and Unity Launcher integration" addon.

You will probably need to restart Thunderbird, then logout and back in to Ubuntu, maybe even a full shutdown and powerup.
