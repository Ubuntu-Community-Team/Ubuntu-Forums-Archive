### Post by TheFu on 2013-08-25
My laptop has a setting to toggle through 3 choices for how external monitors are used.
* mirror
* extension
* ignore
The video card controlling software manages that, so if you can tell us about your specific video card in the laptop, we might be able to help.

Or you could just drag the browser over to the TV screen "window" and change to full-screen viewing. I'd be surprised if that didn't work.
