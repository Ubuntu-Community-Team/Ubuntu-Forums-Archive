### Post by 3Miro on 2012-06-12
You do know that all of those program do not improve the security of your Ubuntu box, there is no reason for you to feel "naked". AV programs for Linux scan only for Windows viruses and the only time it makes sense to use them is when you are dual-booting or transferring files between Ubuntu and another Windows machine. And even in those cases, if you have good AV on your Windows machine, you should be fine without AV for Linux (at least until AV software update their version to work with 12.04).

Generally speaking proprietary companies take some time to update their software to the latest kernel, but since 12.04 is such a major release, it shouldn't be long.
