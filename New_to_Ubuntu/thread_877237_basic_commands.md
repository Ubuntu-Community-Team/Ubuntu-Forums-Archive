### Post by prshah on 2008-08-01
> **bravoall1552 said:**
> Can someone give me a whole list of basic commands (like make, ln, ls, sudo) and their uses and options?

Open a terminal (Applications-Accessories-Terminal), then give the command```
info coreutils
``` this will open up a screen with a list of the commands on the right side, and a summary of what they are useful for on the left. Scroll to any/each command and press enter for more help on that command.
