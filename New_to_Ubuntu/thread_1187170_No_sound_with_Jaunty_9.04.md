### Post by Tholley on 2009-06-14
I think I figured it out after I found this link: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1130384&highlight=hardware+list](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1130384&highlight=hardware+list)

The very first section, I right clicked the speaker on the upper panel, and opened Volume Control, then I saw on the far right, PC Speaker was muted. 

Am listening to music now as I type!

Now that was an easy fix (just like I was thinking)!
