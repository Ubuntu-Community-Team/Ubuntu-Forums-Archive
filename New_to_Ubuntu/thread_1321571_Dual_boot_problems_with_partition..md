This will take a lot of research for me but there is one thing I would like you to try.

When you get to the menu and highlight the XP entry, hit "e".

This will take you to grub2 edit.  Go down the entry and remove the line;
```

drivemap -s (hd0) ${root}

```
Then hit Ctrl + x to boot.


This will change what grub is doing for that boot only.  It will change nothing on your actual menu (it is safe to experiment this way)
