### Post by snowpine on 2010-06-29
The Ubuntu hardware requirements are well-documented: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements)

Based on your limited specs, the only members of the Buntu familiy that seem possible are Lubuntu and Xubuntu. Additionally, you might want to test drive some "lightweight" distros such as Puppy and SliTaz. I never recommend running an old, outdated version of any distro (not when there are so many good active projects targeting old hardware). Good luck! :)
