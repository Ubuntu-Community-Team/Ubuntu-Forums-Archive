### Post by Tom--d on 2008-09-29
There are 2 ways of going around this:

Move the Window to Desktop 1 :p

or disable the session manager in compiz (saves window states, where they are etc)

Go to Compiz Config Manger? (forgot what its called) and go down to utility and disable Session Manager. But all your other windows you have moved will go back to default. And will always go back to default.
