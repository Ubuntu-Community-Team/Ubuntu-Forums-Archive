### Post by sandyd on 2010-06-23
just do a dual boot (install windows first) and make sure you follow these steps...

1. Go to Control Panel -> Administrative Tools -> Disk Management
2. Choose a partition and shrink it (leave it as free space)
3. When you get to the partitoning stage of the ubuntu setup, choose "use largest block of free space"

otherwise windows will complain and tell you to insert your recovery disc..
