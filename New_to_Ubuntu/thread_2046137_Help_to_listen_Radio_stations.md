### Post by Lisiano on 2012-08-22
AFAIK, you can't make something do it can't do, so if your tuner can only do 87.5 to 108 then you probably can't do anything.
I suggest you just listen to online streams since they are always better quality (Less noise) and you can listen to them regardless of your location, even if you are exactly on the other side of the globe.
Also those stations that don't have any official streams (Are there still any that don't?) then there is probably who is retransmitting it.
You can find a ton of stations on [ShoutCast]("http://www.shoutcast.com/")
