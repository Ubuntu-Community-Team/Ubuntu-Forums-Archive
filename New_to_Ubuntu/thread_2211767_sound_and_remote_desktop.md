### Post by TheFu on 2014-03-18
**pulse audio** can do the remote audio.  Video doesn't work well with any remote desktop that I've seen that isn't commercial. Out of the free choices, only SPICE seems to work even a little and it is limited to KVM virtual machines. This is on a GigE wired network. Anything less, is worse, IME.
