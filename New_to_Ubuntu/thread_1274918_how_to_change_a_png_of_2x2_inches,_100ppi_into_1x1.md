### Post by mikeyphi on 2009-09-25
Have you tried doing that with GIMP?

Applications > Graphics > GIMP

OPEN the image

from the menu select 'image' > 'scale image'

then change the 'pixels/inch' and the 'width/height'
make sure both settings are in the 'chained' mode to retain proportion.
