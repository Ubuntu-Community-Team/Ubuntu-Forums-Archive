### Post by DuckHook on 2016-01-13
Hello adarsh5, and welcome to the forums.

Your description and requirements are too general for anyone to really help. Please provide actual name of software and the actual dependencies. QT is now on version 5, and version 3 is obsolete. Why are you trying to install such old libraries? They are full of bugs and security holes that are no longer being fixed. Moreover, they will conflict with and probably break any new app that requires QT5.

Also, are you trying to install an app from outside of the repositories? Unless this app is mission-critical and cannot be substituted with a newer app from the repositories, you are just opening another huge security hole. It is not a good idea to install apps from outside the repositories. The world of Linux is very different than the world of Windows.

Please provide complete info along with your ultimate end goal and let's see if there is not a better solution than a possibly ancient app that is dependent on obsolete libraries.
