### Post by premsted69 on 2009-11-10
My wife and I share an e mail address. She looks at hers through yahoomail in Windows Vista. 
If I want to look at my emails  in Evolution, the inbox in yahoo is emptied out into Evolution but then my wife loses all her e mails.
Is there a way of looking at the emails in both inboxes without actually moving any of them. 
It's possible to do this if we look at them through btinternet.com but I want to start using evolution all the time. I'd rather not have to change my email address although this would fix it at a stroke.
I'd be grateful for any advice that anyone has to offer. Thank you

### Post by laidback on 2009-11-10
I think what you are looking for is:-

in evolution edit>preferences>mail accounts> (choose the one you want) >edit>receiving options>leave messages on server..should be selected.

Now when you view your emails they'll still be on the server to be viewed by another email system.

### Post by underquark on 2009-11-10
Edit...Preferences
Mail Accounts  - choose account and click on Edit (button at right of screen)
Choose Receiving Options tab
Ensure that "Leave Messages on Server" is ticked

[Edit]
I type slowly and someone beat me to it.
You can also opt to have messages deleted after a certain number of days (but you'll have spotted that if you have followed the above)
