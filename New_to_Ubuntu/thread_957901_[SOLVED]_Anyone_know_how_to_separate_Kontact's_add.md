### Post by ellgor on 2008-10-25
Hi,

Kontact does have these funtions, try this:

When you open Kontact, click on the contacts icon which highlights it, then click on "file" (on the toolbar) from the drop down list click on "new contact", when this new window opens there's a whole bunch of options, but near the bottom left is "Select Categories" click on that and another little window opens with categories you can choose or make your own, hope this helps.

Regards, Ellgor.

Regarding choosing who to see etc.:
