### Post by Leshrac on 2011-09-03
For your external sound card you should try this: [http://www.ffado.org/](http://www.ffado.org/)
You can probably find some detailed how-tos by googling or searching the forums, like for instance this thread [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1488678](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1488678) I don't know if it applies to your specific card though.

About C++ development, there are lots of IDEs and text editors around, some of the most popular ones are codeblocks, eclipse and netbeans, you should be able to find all of these in the repositories.

Discussions about whether a certain IDE or text editor is better than a certain other one sometimes get pretty heated up and are akin to asking what religion is best.
