### Post by BC59 on 2012-01-28
I didn't use recently the Remastersys, but I remember it's producing an image (.iso) on home directory. Means you have to go to nautilus left panel and check File System. Then click Home and it's there. If you burn it to a DVD, you have a normal installation disk, with the only difference, the system installed it's the exact system you copied from your computer.
