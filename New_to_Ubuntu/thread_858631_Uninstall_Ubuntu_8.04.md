### Post by halitech on 2008-07-13
a word of caution on removing Ubuntu, make sure you have your windows CD to restore your MBR if you installed grub to boot Ubuntu otherwise you may not be able to boot into windows (if you are dual booting). If it's the only OS on your computer, just install another and format your drive when you install.
