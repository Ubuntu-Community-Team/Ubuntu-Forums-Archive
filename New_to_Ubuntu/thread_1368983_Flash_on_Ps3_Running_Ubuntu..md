### Post by foldingstock on 2009-12-31
The PS3 uses a PPC processor, not an x86 class. Adobe has not released a Flash player for the PPC line, which is why you can't get it to work (the x86 version will not run on a PPC). 

You may find this link helpful: 

[http://boardsus.playstation.com/playstation/board/message?board.id=ps3media&thread.id=67144](http://boardsus.playstation.com/playstation/board/message?board.id=ps3media&thread.id=67144)
