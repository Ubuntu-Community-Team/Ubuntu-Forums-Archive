### Post by itisbasi on 2010-11-02
Navigate to the following in openoffice
tools>options>openoffice.org writer>formatting aids and check the box which says "direct curzor"
Restart openoffice and you should be able to direct your curzor to the desired place on a blank page. 
I hope that is what you are looking for .
