### Post by mbzn on 2009-11-13
Go to System>preferences>keybord. Click the second tab(Layouts) then click on layout options. Look for "compose key" then make it (right alt).

You can now the this- AltGR(hold)+(Shift+6)for^ and then press e, Now release alt. and this gives you 'ê' you can try `~^-'". Play with it, you'll get the feel.
