### Post by NewJack on 2008-10-14
You can do the same thing in Ubuntu:

- Go to your Desktop.

- Right click on the background.

- Click on "Create Folder" and name it whatever you want

- Open up TextEdit or OpenOffice to write your document (poem in this case)

- Go to File>Save>"Look for the folder location where you created your new folder".  In this case on the Desktop.

- Click SAVE, and you are done.

Ubuntu isn't that much different in the way you did things in Windows when it comes to this sort of thing.  Actually, you would have done it the EXACT same way in Windows.

Don't overthink Ubuntu/Linux.  It isn't the complicated monster that Windows fanboys would like to make you think.  Most things are done differently, but not with more difficulty.
