### Post by Dennis N on 2018-06-03
Menu > View > Uncheck 'Side Panel'
The menu in question on my system is the button with three dots on the application window's top bar. You may have global menu in use? But look around and you should find the 'View' option in a menu.

### Post by sisco311 on 2018-06-03
