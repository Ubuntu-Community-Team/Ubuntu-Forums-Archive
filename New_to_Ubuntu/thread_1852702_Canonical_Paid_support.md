### Post by ejt on 2011-09-30
I have been using Ubuntu/Kubuntu for a few years now and decided to pay canonical for support to show my support and also to solve problems. Out of 15 support tickets submitted in the past year only 2 were resolved by the paid support. The rest were resolved by the participants in these forums. So my advise to new users is to save your money and use the forums. Donate to Canonical but don't expect problems to be solved by their paid support.
