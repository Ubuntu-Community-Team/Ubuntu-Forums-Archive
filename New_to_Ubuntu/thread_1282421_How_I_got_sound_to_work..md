### Post by donsy on 2009-10-04
After much tinkering with the settings, I finally got Sound Recorder to work on my Dell Inspiron 1200. Here are my settings: 

System > Preferences > Sound
- Audio Conferencing
----Sound Playback: PulseAudio Sound Server
----Sound Capture: PulseAudio Sound Server
(Everything else is Autodetect.)
- Default Mixer Tracks
----Device: Capture: Intel ICH6 – Intel ICH6 (PulseAudio Mixer)
    Master

Speaker Icon on top panel -- Volume Control
--Playback: PulseAudio Sound Server
--Capture: PulseAudio Sound Server
preferences: Master

Hope this helps someone else.
