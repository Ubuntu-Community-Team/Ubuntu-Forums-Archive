### Post by lisati on 2009-03-22
If you're new to ubuntu, have a look at the installed help.
One tweak you can do straight away is to apply available updates. This can be done by clicking on System->Udate Manager and then clicking on Check. Chances are there will be a list of available updates, which can then be installed.
