### Post by 5149.5 on 2011-04-09
The previous poster is correct. Unity has the launcher on the left and is the default for Natty. If you want the upper and lower panels, you can select Ubuntu classic at login. You may have to install the drawers on the top panel by right clicking the panel. Thats how I did it but that was on a 10.10 UNR install.
