### Post by kanikilu on 2009-10-23
On my keyboard, my "F" keys also have other functions when the F lock is turned on, and when it's on, things like CTRL+ALT+F1 don't work as intended.

Could it be that your keyboard also has a function lock that is turned on? Look for a key labeled "F Lock" or something similar and if it exists, toggle it and try again.
