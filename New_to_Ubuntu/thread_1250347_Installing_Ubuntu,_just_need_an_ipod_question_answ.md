### Post by motorcity909 on 2009-08-26
While I've successfully transferred music to my iPod (80gb Classic) using Rhythmbox, Banshee and Amarok, I won't be using any of them. Reason being is my obsession with album covers.

Rhythmbox can't transfer album covers to iPod.  Banshee and Amarok not only can't transfer album covers but they both wiped every album cover from every song already on my iPod.

Ended up having to format my iPod and re-add all my songs with iTunes on my wife's XP laptop.

For playing music on Ubuntu, I've started using Songbird from Mozilla - it looks just like iTunes.  No iPod support yet but I think it's in their longer-term plans.

So,  if you're not as anal as me about album covers on your iPod then any of Rhythmbox, Banshee or Amarok will do the job.
