### Post by kaibob on 2009-01-19
I had a similar problem. After a search of this forum for advice, I opened synaptic and uninstalled all flash plugins (flashplugin-nonfree in my case) and installed adobe-flashplugin. 

Before doing the above, when I entered "about: plugins" in Firefox, it showed that I had Flash 9. Now it shows Flash 10, and all web sites work fine. I'm running Hardy like you.
