Welcome to Ubuntu!

How do you connect your computer to your broadband modem? If you connect it through USB, you might want to consider connecting it via an Ethernet cable. (A USB plug looks [like this](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:USB_TypeA_Plug.JPG), and an Ethernet connector (also called an RJ45) looks [like this]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Ethernet_RJ45_connector_p1160054.jpg").)
