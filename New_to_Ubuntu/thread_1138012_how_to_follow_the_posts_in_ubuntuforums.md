### Post by null.byte on 2009-04-26
User CP -> Edit Options -> At the "Default Thread Subscription Mode" section, choose: Instant email notification from the list.

### Post by nandemonai on 2009-04-26
That will automatically subscribe you to any thread you post in. If you want to keep it less spammy then you can just use the thread tools link to subscribe to a particular thread.
