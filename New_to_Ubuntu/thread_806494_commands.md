### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-05-26
For screen capping, Istanbul and gtk-recordMyDesktop are great. The latter has more features, but doesn't work too well with sound. Both output into Ogg Theora, but you can easily convert them into other formats using editing software such as Kdenlive or PiTiVi.
