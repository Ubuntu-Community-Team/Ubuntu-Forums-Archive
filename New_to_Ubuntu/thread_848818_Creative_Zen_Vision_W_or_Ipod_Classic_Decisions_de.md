I have a Creative Zen Vision W 60GB, and I like it a lot.  Of course, it work best in Windows, but with Ubuntu it's still ok (functional with limitations).

I use Rhythmbox to transfer my MP3; no problem here.  But when transfer, it's copying, not syncing.  So, if you are looking for incremental changes, it's not so friendly.

For transfer of video, I use gnomad2.  The GUI is clunky, but it works.

What I like about Zen Vision W is almost flawless xvid playback, and the bright wide screen.

I have not used an iPod, so I can't comment there.
