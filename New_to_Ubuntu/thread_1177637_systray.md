### Post by dnairb on 2009-06-03
The area that minimised programs go to is called the "Window List".

The "systray" where icons relating to network, laptop battery state, etc, sit, is called the "Notification Area"

Either can be added to any panel in any position. The bottom panel is not necessarily a "systray".
