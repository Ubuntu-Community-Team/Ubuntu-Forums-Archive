### Post by ian-weisser on 2016-05-02
Packages in Main are supported by Canonical.
Packages in Universe are supported by the community.
PPAs are supported only by the PPA Author/Team, not by Canonical nor the Community.

"Community Supported" means here and other forums for some issues, upstream for security patches and bugfixes.
