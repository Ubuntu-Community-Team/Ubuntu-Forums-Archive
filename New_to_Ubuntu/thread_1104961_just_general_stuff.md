### Post by rolandrock on 2009-03-24
Response to two of your questions:

To change your home page in Firefox, click Edit->Preferences type in your new home page in the "Home Page" field then click "Close".

To find out about SIP phones, try [http://www.sipphone.com/basics/](http://www.sipphone.com/basics/) .

Bear in mind that applications (like Firefox, Voipcheap) are not the same as your operating system (Windows, Ubuntu).  e.g. Firefox runs on Windows/Mac/Linux and the method for changing homepage is the same in each.

Please persevere with your adventure.  There is great satisfaction to be had from overcoming these teething troubles and you will acquire general skills that can be applied to other areas of your life.

Also, you will become a better more rounded individual and your love life will improve ;o)

### Post by blazemore on 2009-03-24
Actually, the method for changing preferences is slightly different under Linux, as it is in Edit, rather than Tools.

Also, Firefox IS a part of Ubuntu, as it is part of the distribution, along with all the other apps, Gnome, X and the kernel.

About your banking program, if it is an application you are used to under Windows, it may be possible under Linux. If this is the case, say so, and I will give you the command to install Wine and keep it up to date.

I would also recommend using Intrepid Ibex, as it is very stable but more up to date than Hardy Heron. I doubt you need the uber-stability of a Long-Term Support release, as this is generally reserved for more conservative Desktop users, as well as enterprise and servers.
