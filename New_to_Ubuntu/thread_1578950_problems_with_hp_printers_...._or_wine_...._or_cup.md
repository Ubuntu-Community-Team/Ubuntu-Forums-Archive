### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-09-21
If your company relies on "enterprise" MS Windows stuff, you're going to have a very hard time using Linux with or without Wine, to do what you need.  MS Access, for example, simply will not work with Wine or anything like it.  I've read that Outlook is the same situation.  As to stuff like MS Project, it could be similar.

Not saying you can't use Linux in the enterprise world, quite a few companies do, but you can't integrate it with enterprise versions of MS Office apps and do well.  IF your company relies on the MS Office apps, you're better off staying with MS Windows.
