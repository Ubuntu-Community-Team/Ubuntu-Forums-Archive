### Post by swoody on 2009-10-13
The .program folders are typically settings for those specific programs. They will keep your settings in-tact, but not the programs themselves. You will need to re-install all of those apps, but once you do, your settings for those programs should be restored. When you re-install the programs, they will create their own menu entries as they did before.

**Please Note: Before doing any kind of upgrading or fresh re-install, make sure to backup all of your important files and data that you don't want to risk losing**
