### Post by dnRoyston on 2009-01-23
Bumping a thread is done by replying to it with no real intention but to move it to the top of the page. This should only be done once every 24 hours.

An example of 33,600 bumps to a thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=520091](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=520091)
