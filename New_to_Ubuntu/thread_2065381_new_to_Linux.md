### Post by newb85 on 2012-10-01
Welcome to Ubuntu!

Sound like you're off to a good start.  For reference, what commands tutorial are you working on?

Where you go from here really depends on what you want to do with Linux.  Ubuntu as already pretty well-suited for casual computer use (browsing the web, checking email, documents, spreadsheets, presentations, etc.), but even for that, you can customize the experience with alternative applications.  The Software Center is a great place to start.

If you want to focus on something more specific, like programming, audio/video production, or whatnot, again look for helpful applications in the Software Center.

I hope you find it as useful as Windows, if not more.  One of the most important things that make Linux different than Windows is the openness of its community.
