### Post by ghostborg on 2009-10-16
K9copy.

Handbrake - once you read your source you can select each title from the title drop down box and then add it to the queue-adjust the destination file every time you change the title. Then click on show queue and all your titles should be listed, just click on encode. That should allow you to walk away and come back later.
