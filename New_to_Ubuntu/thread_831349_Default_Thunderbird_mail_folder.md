### Post by cariboo on 2008-06-16
Open up Places-->Home Folder and press Ctrl-h this wil show all the hidden files and directories in your home directory. Your Thunderbird files are located in .mozilla-thunderbird/xxxxxxx.default where
xxxxxxx is the random string of letters before .default
