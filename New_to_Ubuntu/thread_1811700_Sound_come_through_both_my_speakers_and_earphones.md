### Post by relay_man on 2011-07-25
You might try:
 
System > Preferences > Sound

Choose the output Tab.
In the dialog box that opens, look towards the bottom near the "connector"
You should have a drop down list that will let you choose
Speakers or Headphones.

Hope this helps
