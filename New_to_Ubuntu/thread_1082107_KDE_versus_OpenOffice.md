### Post by sydbat on 2009-02-27
AFAIK, yes to all. Have fun.

More specifically, you would have to log out, change your desktop session, log back in to go from Gnome to KDE (and vice versa).

You can use either Koffice or open office in Gnome or KDE.

Kubuntu does not have the Gnome desktop installed, just KDE. But you can always install whatever DE you want (Gnome, KDE, Xfce, etc) through the repos and switch back and forth at your leisure.
