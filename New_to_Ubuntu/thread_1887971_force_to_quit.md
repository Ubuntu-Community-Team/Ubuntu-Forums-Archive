### Post by Paqman on 2011-11-28
Do you mean the old "force quit" applet that you could put in your panel? If so the switch from Gnome 2 to Gnome 3 has meant that a lot of these old applets are no more, and I'm not sure if anyone has got around to making a Gnome 3 one. In the meantime you can hit Alt-F2 and type:
```
xkill
```
and click on the misbehaving app. It does exactly what the old applet did.
