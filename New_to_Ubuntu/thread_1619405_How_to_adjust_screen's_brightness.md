This is weird but it works for me. Left click on brightness app to bring up the slider, **right** click it then  click somewhere nearby to make the context menu go away. Now the slider works.  If you're using a mouse with a scroll wheel, try putting the cursor over the brightness applet, don't click anything and move the wheel.  My brightness works well with the scroll wheel.
