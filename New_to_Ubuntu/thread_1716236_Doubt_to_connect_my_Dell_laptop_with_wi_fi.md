### Post by Enigmapond on 2011-03-28
Connect with a wired connection and then navigate to Administration/Additional Drivers (or Hardware Drivers) and let it install the broadcom driver. After that just open the network manager from the panel icon and enter the wireless network info in "Edit Connections". Then just make sure both network and wireless are enabled by right-clicking the same icon.
