### Post by mkvnmtr on 2009-02-11
If upgrades are giving you a problem you might want to look at Linux Mint. It is based on Ubuntu but they give you the option to hold back upgrades that they think might cause problems. It is kind of a neat distro. I have it dual booted on a box with Ubuntu 9.04. I think I am going to like it but have not had much time on it. Works just like Ubuntu so the learning curve was about 10 minutes.
