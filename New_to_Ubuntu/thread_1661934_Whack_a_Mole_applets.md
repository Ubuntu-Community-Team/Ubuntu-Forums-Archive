### Post by 3602 on 2011-01-07
Yes this has been a bug for some time now. I've been experiencing the same issues: Applets are Locked, but move around upon boot/reboot.
A "solution" is to try and keep it short and sweet. For example, I used 24-hour format to get rid of the PM and put it on the bottom panel; Deleted the Trash applet and don't use the Indicator applet if possible.
My applets are now stable (don't move around) even after I added Weather Control applet on the top panel. 
Also, this may or may not be related: Auto-hide. I chose auto-hide on both panels right after shortening everything and haven't a problem since.

### Post by heyandy889 on 2011-01-07
When I had this bug, I found that rebooting changed the positions of my panel icons. The solution I landed on was leaving a little bit of space between panel icons and then right clicking so I could "Lock to Panel."
