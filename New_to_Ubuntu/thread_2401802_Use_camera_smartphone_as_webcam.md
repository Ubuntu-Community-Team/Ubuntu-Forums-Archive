### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2018-09-22
There's an app for Android called IP-Webcam. It makes your phone serve an MJPEG-stream by http on port 8080. You'll find a script and instructions on how to use this as a webcam at this [page on github]("https://github.com/bluezio/ipwebcam-gst/").
