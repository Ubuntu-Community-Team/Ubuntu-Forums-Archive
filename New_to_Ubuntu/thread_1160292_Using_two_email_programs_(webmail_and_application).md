### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2009-05-15
Gmail can sync your emails to one place. Inside gmail if you look under settings -> Accounts and Import they have an option to check other email accounts using pop3. Once you have all your accounts setup then you can use Evolution, thunderbird, or your mail client of choice to check your mail via IMAP. If your wanting to keep the calandar synced I suggested google calander and [Thunderbird with lightning]("http://www.mozilla.org/projects/calendar/lightning/"). There are also plenty of thunderbird extensions which allow for google syncing, see [HERE]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/search?q=google&status=4")
