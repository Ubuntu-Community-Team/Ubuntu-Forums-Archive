### Post by iSKUNK! on 2009-11-12
The M35X shipped with a buggy ACPI implementation in the BIOS. Try upgrading the BIOS to the latest version. My dad's M35X-S149 had a similar problem, and after the upgrade, suspend/hibernate worked perfectly.
