### Post by penguinbreath on 2008-05-12
Are you sure your laptop is not overheating? I recently had a problem with my secondary desktop PC constantly shutting down for seemingly no reason at all. I found out it was doing this from an overheating CPU. I bought a new heatsink/fan and my problem went away.

You can check your laptops temperature in the BIOS, or by a program. I am not aware of a program for Ubuntu that can monitor the temperature of your CPU, however I imagine there is one.
