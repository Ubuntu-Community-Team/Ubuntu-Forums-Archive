### Post by emtom on 2013-04-17
On the menu bar, click file > print, and that displays the print dialog window. 

With LibreOffice Writer document open, to make the menu bar visible, hover the cursor over the bar at the top of the screen (the bar that says "LibreOffice" Writer on the extreme left and has the clock in the extreme right).
