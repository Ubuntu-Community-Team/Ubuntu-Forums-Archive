### Post by corncob on 2011-09-16
First off, click on the speaker icon and select 'Sound Preferences' (or go to System > Preferences > Sound).  Make sure its not muted or the volume turned all the way down.  Also, there might be a multimedia key on your keyboard that turns the sound off.

### Post by technosysind on 2011-09-16
you know sometimes the output selection goes to digital HDMi where it should remain Analog

Go To System>>Preferences>>Sound>>Hardware
                                                            Select Analog Stereo Duplex
and then click on    Output and select Internal Audio Analog Stereo


I hope this helps. Lemme know if u still have a problem
