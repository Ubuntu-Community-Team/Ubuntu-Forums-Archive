### Post by swoody on 2009-10-13
Well, I'm glad it worked out for you :)

As far as your menu, that does seem a bit odd. You are running Ubuntu, with the Gnome interface, correct? Try right-clicking on your menus, and selecting 'Edit Menus'. Now in the window that appears, in the left-hand column, under the 'System' tree, there should be 'Preferences' and 'Administration'. Do you see both of these? Now click on 'Preferences' in that left-hand side column, and on the right side, you should see a bunch of menu options. Are any of these selected? If none of them are, you can simply select all the ones you wish to have shown in your menu. After selecting the ones you want, go ahead and click 'Close' to close that window. Now go back to your menu, and see if System>Preferences is there. If this doesn't help you out, you should start a new thread about this issue, as it is seperate from your original question here.
