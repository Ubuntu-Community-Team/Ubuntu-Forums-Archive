### Post by silverglade00 on 2008-11-21
I would go into Windows and plug it back in. Then let Windows "look" at it for a few minutes. After that, do the safe remove thing with the green arrow by the clock. Go to your Ubuntu and plug it in. After a few moments, it should pop up an icon on the desktop and a folder should open with the contents of the drive.
