### Post by shuttleworthwannabe on 2012-04-08
If you have antivirus on while writing to USB, it may prevent writing of the instructions to boot off USB; I use Avira and as a result the USB is unbootable. I have to temp disable the antivirus while making the bootable USB, then it works fine. Also there are many other tools you could try besides pendrive, like [unetbootin]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/")--works like a charm or even [LiLi]("http://www.linuxliveusb.com/").[LiLi link seems to be down at time of writing this].
