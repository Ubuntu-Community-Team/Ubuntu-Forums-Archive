### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2020-06-09
Two things:
[LIST=1]
[*]14.04 has been out of support for about a year. You should update to something current.
[*]Current Versions of LibreOffice (since about 2017) have a menu-item (File->Digital Signatures->Sign existing PDF) to do what you want. Haven't tried it myself since I don't need it and don't have the kind of certificate needed but AFAIK it's the easiest way to sign a PDF on Ubuntu.
[/LIST]
