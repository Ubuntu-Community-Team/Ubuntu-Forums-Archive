### Post by Impavidus on 2013-06-07
You can unpack the package, place the executable wherever you like and start it. However, I remember other people with similar problems and it appears that firefox 21 needs libaries not present on old Ubuntus. If it doesn't work, you may want to try an older version of firefox, somewhere around 15 may still work on your Ubuntu. Or even better, get a supported version of Ubuntu. You will automatically get the latest firefox, security updates and no more worries over missing dependencies.
