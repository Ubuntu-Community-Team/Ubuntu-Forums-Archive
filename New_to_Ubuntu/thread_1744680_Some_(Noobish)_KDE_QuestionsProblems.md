### Post by Chronon on 2011-04-30
You can drag and drop launchers from the menu into the panel.  I just created a new empty panel (I usually just use one) and dropped a firefox launcher in it from the menu.

To change the window decorations go to [FONT="Courier New"]System Settings > Workspace Appearance > Window Decoration[/FONT].  Click on "Configure Buttons" and check the box for "Use custom titlebar button positions".  Then move the controls to whatever layout you want.
