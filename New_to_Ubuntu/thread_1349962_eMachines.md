### Post by Locke_99GS on 2009-12-08
"Data" partitions manufacturers use as a place where you can put all your random stuff. Really, you can put anything you want onto that drive. I usually wipe them and use their space for other partitions.

If you put your documents and such into there, when Windows borks itself and needs reinstalled, all your important data will still be in tact on that other drive.
