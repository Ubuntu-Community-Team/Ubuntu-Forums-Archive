### Post by Tatty on 2008-12-09
one of your F-keys (often either F2 or F12) will get you into your bios, it should flash up a message on the screen telling you what it is.

If you cant get into your bios, try unplugging everything from your motherboard (hard disks, RAM, CDROM, card readers, PCI cards, GFX card) then switching it on to see if it makes any better progress.  If it loads your BIOS then start plugging in the devices again one by one to see what is causing the problem.
