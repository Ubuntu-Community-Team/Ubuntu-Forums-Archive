### Post by dFlyer on 2011-06-30
I would suggest you explore the usage of synaptic. It's very easy to use. Under filter just enter what your looking for and it pops up. To add a program just select it and mark for install. To remove a program just select it and select for removal or complete removal. Than just click apply. I myself find synaptic more user friendly than the Ubuntu Software Center. Synaptic will show you what is being install including it's libs. USC just install a program, and doesn't show you the libs.
