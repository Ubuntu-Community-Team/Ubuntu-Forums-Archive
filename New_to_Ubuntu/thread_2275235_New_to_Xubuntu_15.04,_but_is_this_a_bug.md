### Post by doninmedford on 2015-04-24
I installed Xubuntu 15.04 on a PC today. So far I like it a lot. I did notice one thing though and I thought I would ask here in case I have overlooked something. When I right click and choose "Arrange Desktop Icons" the Icons disappear off the screen and I can't get to them to pull them back visible. I even tried to click the parts of the icon labels that were still showing to drag them back on screen, with no luck. If I restart the computer then the Icons stay in the proper place until the "Arrange Desktop Icons" is chosen. Obviously its a pretty minor problem, but I thought I would ask. The computer uses an Asrock A75 Pro4 motherboard and an integrated HD65XX graphics card according to the factory install book. I tried setting each of one the two monitors to the primary at different times and the problem is there on both. The monitors are Asus, purchased within the last few years.

Yep, happens to me too.

Right click on desktop and select Desktop Settings > Icons and click on "Show Hidden Files" and they come back to the desktop.
