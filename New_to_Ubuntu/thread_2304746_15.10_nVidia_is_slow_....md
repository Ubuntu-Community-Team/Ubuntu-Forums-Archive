### Post by blm-ubunet on 2015-12-03
You can add to your stripe/byline "collects antique video cards"...

The 7300 was not very good new.

You could try disable any compositing effects manager.
Mate uses marco or compiz  (IIRC) & there is a setup/config tool.

Failing that, look into "/var/log/Xorg.0.log" Xserver logfile to see if nvidia driver is loaded right.
Check openGL is not failing back to CPU rendering.

There is a brute force disable composite option for nvidia driver via the /etc/X11/xorg.conf file.
