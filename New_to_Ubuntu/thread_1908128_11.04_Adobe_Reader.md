### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-01-12
Often , software versions in the Ubuntu repository are not updated until the next release unless needed . The reasons for updating may vary.

Adobe Reader is proprietary and is updated often due to security issues and most often these vulnerabilities are specific to Windows operating systems . As with Flash , people exploit weaknesses and Adobe fixes them and the cycle goes on and on .

I don't see Adobe Reader in my 11.10 software center , I guessing a repository would have to be enabled to install it . I don't use Adobe Reader in Windows or Ubuntu because there are much lighter alternatives for both that suit my needs.
