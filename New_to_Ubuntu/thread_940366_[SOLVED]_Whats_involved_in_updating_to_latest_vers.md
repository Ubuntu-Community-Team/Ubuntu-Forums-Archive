### Post by lazyart on 2008-10-07
You're probably making it hard on yourself by upgrading to the beta and what issues that will come along with it... the final release in of itself will have issues.

If you don't have the time simply don't do the upgrade right away.  It gives the extra bonus of giving time for more updates to come down the line.  Case in point-- I just updated my system to Hardy this past weekend.

Expect to do some work with the video drivers.  Not always, but be prepared.  Otherwise your settings should pretty much stay intact.  I'm guessing you've never done a distro upgrade before.

Just do it on a Friday evening so you have time to get over any hurdles.  Installing a text mode browser is also a good idea in case you get stuck at the console.```
sudo apt-get install links2
``` is a good one that will even display graphics if you launch it with the -g switch.
