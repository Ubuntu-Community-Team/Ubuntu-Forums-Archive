### Post by e4kuber on 2010-12-15
I got it working finally:D.  I went to this website and went to the touchscreen part and followed the directions to instll the nwfermi 4.2 update.  I guess I had an old one.  I know it says its for HP Touchsmart 600 with 10.04 but it worked for me.  This is Great!!  Everything is working.:P

[https://www.ebower.com/docs/ubuntu-touchsmart/index.html#AEN83](https://www.ebower.com/docs/ubuntu-touchsmart/index.html#AEN83)
