### Post by VoodooLoveDog on 2011-10-27
My wife had the same problem with our computer. Some of the coupon sites require a piece of software installed on your computer that is normally done via ActiveX (IE). 

I had a VM setup so I just installed it on there. However, I'm not a fan of the software. It tracks habits and is what I would consider spyware. The VM I use I snapshotted and is powered off most of the time (it was originally setup for iTunes).

If you're hell bent on saving a few bucks at the expense of your surfing habits then you may want to VM it or go back to Windows.
