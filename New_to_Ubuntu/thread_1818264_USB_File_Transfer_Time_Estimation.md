### Post by Redblade20XX on 2011-08-04
Hey everyone,

 I've just got a question about file transfers via usb and the estimated time of completion given by my Kubuntu distro. Whenever I try and transfer a huge amount of data (ie. Music) to my mp3 player, Kubuntu gives a changing amount of time for the task completion which really doesn't help when you actually need to know when this will be done. Is this normal? Also for very small transfers, i.e one or two songs, the kubuntu notification system doesn't even register the transfer, as in notify you that the transfer has started or end, but it completes the task. Again, is this normal?    

Thanks for any responses!     :)

-Red

---

### Post by gordintoronto on 2011-08-04
Yes, those are normal behaviors. Many USB devices accept data faster than they can write it to permanent memory. Once the buffer is full, they tell the computer to slow down.
