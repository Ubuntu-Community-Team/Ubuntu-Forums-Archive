### Post by Elfy on 2008-09-06
In nautilus - drag the folder form the right hand pane in to the places and it will make a shortcut for you.

### Post by nothingspecial on 2008-09-06

When I started this I created a note called "useful commands", it has spiraled from there. Try it.

### Post by Zimmer on 2008-09-06
