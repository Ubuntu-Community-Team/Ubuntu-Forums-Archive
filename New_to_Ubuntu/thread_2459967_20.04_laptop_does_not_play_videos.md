### Post by CelticWarrior on 2021-03-30
A Dell Latitude E7470 has a very standard Intel based CPU and graphics for which drivers are installed by default.
What isn't installed by default - it must be opted-in during Ubuntu installation - is codecs (and firmware, etc.). Codecs can be installed later with 'ubuntu-restricted-extras'. Have you did at least one of this options?
