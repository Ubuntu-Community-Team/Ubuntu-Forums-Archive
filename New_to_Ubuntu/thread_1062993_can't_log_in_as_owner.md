### Post by igknighted on 2009-02-07
You can't delete all the gnome panels, there must always be one left.

Also, anything configuration-wise belongs to the user, so you don't need to log in as an admin.  Actually, admin login is disabled by default, and while a google search will turn up how to enable it, it is against forum policy to post how to do so.
