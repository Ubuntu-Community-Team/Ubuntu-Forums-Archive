### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-07-04
Try turning off all visual effects.

Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]System>Preferences>Appearance[/COLOR]

When the window opens, click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Visual Effects Tab[/COLOR]

Select [COLOR="Blue"]None[/COLOR].

Quite often Compiz can cause all sorts of problems with some video cards.

Now try running Evolution and see if the problem persists.
