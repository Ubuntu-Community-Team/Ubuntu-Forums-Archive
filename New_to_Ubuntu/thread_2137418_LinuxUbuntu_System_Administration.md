### Post by Derxst on 2013-04-20
I got my first SysAdmin job at age 16 (sheesh 27 years ago!) There some topics that are universal regardless of the operating system you are administering.
Some that come to mind:
- How and when do I perform backups?
- How and how often do I perform restores? (Restoring a backup is the ONLY way to fully know if you are getting good backups)
- How are new users provisioned? What is the password policy?
- How will shares and permissions be handled?
