### Post by snowpine on 2010-08-05
Hi Babloo, you will automatically get the new version of Transmission (and every other application) when you upgrade to 10.10 in October.

If you can't wait that long, you can download and install the new version straight from the Transmission website. Please note however that doing so is not supported and so you become personally responsible for updates and the stability of your system.
