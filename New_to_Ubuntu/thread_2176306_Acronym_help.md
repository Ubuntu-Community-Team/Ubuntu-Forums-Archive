### Post by The Cog on 2013-09-23
I'm guessing that most of the "acronyms" are actually commands - sudo, apt-get, cp, rsync etc. I think the only way to get a handle on all of these is to read up on them when you see them mentioned. Most of the commands will have a manual page. For instance, to find the manual page for cp, type the command **[FONT=Courier New]man cp[/FONT]** and use 'Q' to quit. The ones that catch your interest and strike you as useful, you will remember. Some you will forget unless you keep seeing them. It's a slow and never ending process - there are too many to be able to remember them all. Learn the tools that are useful to you, and remember that others exist even if you forget the details.
