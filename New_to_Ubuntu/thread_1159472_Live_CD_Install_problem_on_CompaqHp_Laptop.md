### Post by Jazzy_Jeff on 2009-05-14
Do you know what video card you have in it? Does it share some of your 256 of ram? Ubuntu needs the full 256 meg of ram for the live cd. You may need to use the text based alternate install cd. You can find it [here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors#mirrors"). Let me know if this helps. Just click on one of the links for your country and then click on 9.04 and choose the alternate cd. 

You may want to try a lighter version of Ubuntu called Xubuntu for older computers.
