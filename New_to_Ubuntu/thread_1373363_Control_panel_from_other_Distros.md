### Post by howefield on 2010-01-05
Not sure if you are asking for a control centre for Ubuntu, if so, it has one.

To enable it, go to System > Preferences > Main Menu and in the left hand pane, click on System and in the right hand pane check the box to the left of Control Centre.

You will now have a new menu item System > Control Centre.
