### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-04-15
> **dwhite said:**
> Hi,

Recently my startups have looked funny, somehow the settings in the bios that have the wifi, 3g and webcam be on at startup got changed, and it is also troubling that on startup there used to be a phoenix logo that would be on briefly that is no longer present on startup, (only some text that passes too quickly for me to read now)
The default BIOS setting is usually to show some splash screen instead of the progress text.  Somehow, your BIOS settings got changed and they now show the progress text.  You should be able to go back into the BIOS, and somewhere in the BOOT settings, there should be an option to show the splash screen.

> is this the beginning of a long slide into system failure or something others have seen?  i would really feel better if things worked like they did before.
Could simply mean that your BIOS battery is dying and unable to retain your custom settings.  I would replace that at first opportunity.
