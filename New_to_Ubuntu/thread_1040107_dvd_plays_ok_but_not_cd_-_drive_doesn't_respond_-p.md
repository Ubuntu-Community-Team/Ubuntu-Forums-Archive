### Post by bloodniece on 2009-01-15
Sounds to me, if your DVD media plays but the CD media doesn't, you have dirty or scratched media or a dirty lens on the CD/DVD drive.

I assume you've tried various other CD media all to the same effect?  Is it a manufactured/pressed CD or a CDR or CDRW?  

Most laptop CD trays pop out.  Maybe you could check the laser lens for debris.  Don't touch it with your finger if it looks dirty.  Just a quick swab with a qtip will suffice.   

That's all I can offer.
