### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-12-12
Do you have the windows CD that possibly came with the laptop?  If so you may be able to extract the driver that works best with ndiswrapper from that CD.

In some cases the drivers from the manufacturer of the card, which you would expect to work best, may not do so.  Often in my experience, ndiswrapper works best with windows 2000 drivers, or failing that, XP drivers.  Later ones for vista and win7 sometimes are not any use.
