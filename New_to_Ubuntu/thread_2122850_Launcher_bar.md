### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-03-06
The launcher only contains a few of the applications installed on your system by default, you can hit SUPER+A to open the dash at the applications lense to see all of your installed apps.

For an intro on using Unity check out the official documentation...
[https://help.ubuntu.com/12.10/ubuntu-help/index.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/12.10/ubuntu-help/index.html)
