### Post by Jon Monreal on 2009-11-16
What you could do is mount the partition you want to access by clicking on it in "Places", navigating to the folder you want, right-clicking on it, selecting "Make Link", and then clicking and dragging the link to the desktop. You can then rename it to whatever you want.
