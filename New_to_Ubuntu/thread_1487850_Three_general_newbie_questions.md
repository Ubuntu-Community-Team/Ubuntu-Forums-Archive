### Post by jrothwell97 on 2010-05-19
Alternate means that instead of having a Live CD installer, it uses the "alternate" text-mode installer. This is usually what you use if you want to upgrade releases from the CD, or bypass the live CD environment (which can be sluggish at times).

Oh, and AMD64 is just convention (like i386). It'll still work on your machine :D
