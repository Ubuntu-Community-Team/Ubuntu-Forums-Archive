### Post by thtrgremlin on 2009-03-10
sorry for such the delay, been away. It honestly sounds like a loose wire or bad grounding, especially if audio makes a difference. Unplug each end of your VGA cable and check for any bent pins. If pins are crossed, or if a bend pin was touching the case, you would get the kind of problem you are describing.

And for the sake of argument, are your cards "seated" correctly? Any loose wires hanging in the case that might be touching the card?

Are you using the proprietary / latest driver?

If it isn't any of those things and you are still having problems, it sounds much like a  defective card from what you are describing. Your warranty should cover that. If it is an integrated card, which it sounds like, they will replace your motherboard if need be.

Best luck, and sorry again for leaving you hanging.
