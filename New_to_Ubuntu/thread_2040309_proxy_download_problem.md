### Post by cariboo on 2012-08-10
The message you are getting just means that some of the packages you are trying to download, don't have a digital signature, this usually means you are downloading packages from the extras or partner repositories. it's up to you whether you want to install them or not. So far no one has managed to insert a package with malicious content into any of the Ubuntu repositories. 

By the way, I usually just tell the system to install the package, and ignore the notification.
