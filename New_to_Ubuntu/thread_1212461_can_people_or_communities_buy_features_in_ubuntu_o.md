### Post by hellocatfood on 2009-07-13
Take a look at Linux Fund [http://www.linuxfund.org/](http://www.linuxfund.org/) It funds projects within the open source community, though not specifically Ubuntu.

As others have mentioned, if you want something in Ubuntu tell people about it (like here on the forum) submit a bug (launchpad.net/ubuntu), or an idea ([http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/))

The more people there are behind it the more likely it is to get implemented. The time it'll take to be implemented is another question entirely.
