### Post by steveneddy on 2010-01-21
Here's the manual you can download in .pdf:

[http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/BSC/public/files/dlf/doc002117/Mini3_MfcLcdW_UsaEngUsr_D.pdf](http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/BSC/public/files/dlf/doc002117/Mini3_MfcLcdW_UsaEngUsr_D.pdf)

Under section three - copy - in page 93 you can change the setting to Fast to use less ink.

These are settings on the printer itself.

Good luck.

Post back if you have any issues.

Also on the Brother website there is a driver section and a help section here:

[http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/index.html](http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/index.html)
