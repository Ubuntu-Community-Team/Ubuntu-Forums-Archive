### Post by bcooperb on 2011-05-10
@ubume2 The process that should would should be as follows(or at least worked when I tested it):

In FF4 MenuBar Bookmarks > Show All Bookmarks 
[this should bring up the "Library"]

Find your Folder listed either under Mozilla Firefox, Unsorted Bookmarks or where ever else you put it on the left hand side. Once you have selected it, you should be able  to right click on it and go to Delete.

Hope this helped.
