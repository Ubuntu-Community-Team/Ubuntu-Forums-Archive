### Post by bennachie on 2011-05-21
Well, 8.04 was the former long-term-support version, and support for it has only recently been discontinued. The current LTS version is 10.04.2, and the current normal version is 11.04. The status and quality of the Kubuntu distribution has improved very rapidly in recent years, and you might be better to try again with a later version.

Unfortunately, Canonical, and more or less all the other major distributions, have now dropped full PPC support, although community-based versions are available. At the moment, AFAIK, the most recent official versions of Kubuntu available for ppc would be 10.04.2 and 10.10. The latter iso is available from:

[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/kubuntu/ports/releases/10.10/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/kubuntu/ports/releases/10.10/release/)

Replacing 10.10 with 10.04 will get you the LTS version.
