### Post by phidia on 2008-05-08
If you are at the CLI (commandline interface) you can type > startx that should restart x. 
There are other ways to do that too depending how you got to CLI in the 1st place. 
Alt+Ctrl+backspace keys pressed together should restart x too, and if you had opened another tty 
then Alt+Ctrl+F7 keys should resume your previous session.

Hope that helps.
