### Post by dethorpe on 2011-08-10
If your using sudo and its prompting for password then the password will not appear on screen when you type it, thats normal, not even replacement characters (e.g. *). 
 
Just type your password and hit return and should be OK.

### Post by moody_mark on 2011-08-10
Hi, you mean to are typing the password in the terminal but not seeing any echoed characters like "****"? Im assuming this is what you mean, and to put your mind to rest, this is normal. The default terminal setting is not to echo any characters for passwords, just enter your password and hit enter, does that work?
