Hi,

Rather pointless to hide your IP address. Nothing (the packets) would be able to find you. IOW it's like a street address and not divulging that means no mail :)
IOW2: not possible.

MAC address can be changed. Since this info is readily available via google, i'll leave it at that. Also rather pointless - it's not hard to figure out where a connected wireless device is. Doesn't require fancy hi-dollar tech gear either, just a laptop and kismet.
