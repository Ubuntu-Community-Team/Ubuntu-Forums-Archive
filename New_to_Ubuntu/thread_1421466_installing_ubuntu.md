### Post by viper250 on 2010-03-04
I find its a lot easier to wipe the drives before installing a new os even with upgrades.
because sometimes problems can occur with upgrades. grub errors are common and can be a headache from time to time.
as long as your data is backed up then there is no issues to deal with between old os and new os

the best practice for any os whether it is linux , mac, or microsith is to back up your data regular basis and keep the backups properly organized.
depending on the work you are doing you can find that the data you back up can be worth more than your computer
