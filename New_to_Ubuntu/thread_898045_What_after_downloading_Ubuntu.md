### Post by tuxerman on 2008-08-22
Download the ISO image from the site, and save it to your hard disk. THEN open Nero and choose "Burn a CD image", and select your newly downloaded ISO to burn onto a CD. Reboot the computer with the CD in the drive. (Make sure the boot-order has the CDdrive before the hard disk in your BIOS). 
Once you boot from the CD, you can either enjoy the liveCD and install later ("Try ubuntu without any change to your computer") or install it right away.

If it's still confusing, dont hesitate to ask again :)
