### Post by Paqman on 2010-04-15
First of all, you don't actually need Samba to share from Linux to Linux. Samba is designed for sharing with Windows machines. You should just be able to share folders across your network from a right click.

As for syncing folders, the new version of Ubuntu One in Lucid can do that for you so just upgrade in a couple of weeks when it's released and you'll be sorted.
