### Post by JKyleOKC on 2011-02-23
To get larger type when browsing, use the CTRL-Plus hotkey combination while viewing a message. This will enlarge your browser display without you having to use a larger point size.

I don't know about other browsers, but in FireFox 3.6 the effect is tied to a particular site, so you can have various amounts of magnification for different sites. And if you go overboard with it, you can use CTRL-Minus to reduce the size...
