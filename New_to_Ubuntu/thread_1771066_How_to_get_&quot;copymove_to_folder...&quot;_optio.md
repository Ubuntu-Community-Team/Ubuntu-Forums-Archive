### Post by Paqman on 2011-05-30
> **rimibchatterjee said:**
> I find the <copy to..>. option in folder view only allows me the sub-options <home folder> <desktop> or <other pane>. The last is however greyed out and unclickable.

To get another pane to copy to, hit F3. You can also open folders in another tab (slightly different to a pane), although you can't right click to copy to another tab, you can drag and drop.

I agree with pagman, in order to get other pane high lighted or working. Open nautilus and click the F3 key. When the other panel opens, navigated in the other panel to where you want to move or copy that item too. Right click on the item to be copied or moved. You will now see other pane highlighted in both copy to and move to menus.  Click other pane and it will move or paste it self there.
