### Post by pablo180 on 2009-09-25
Gnome-Do

The amount of times I have been hitting super-spacebar on my Windows PC repeatedly before I realise my mistake! Then of course I have to trawl through the slow loading menu's to find the program I was looking for. I couldn't cope with out it and it would be the first program installed on a new installation. 

Add to that:

Evolution : Outlook's great but Evolution is a pretty close second.
KDE BasKet: Not perfect by any means, but a good program. 
Ubuntu Tweak: Great program that brings all those hard to find settings into one place, and makes them simple to use. 
Screenlets: OK, most are pointless, but they look good!
