### Post by cariboo on 2011-11-25
Can you show us a screenshot? To take a screenshot click on the BFB at the top of the launcher and type:

```
scr
```

Then click the screenshot icon, set the delay for a couple of seconds, so the screenshot window doesn't get in the way, then click the Take screenshot button.

Once you have taken and saved the screenshot, open the advanced editor on the forum page, and scroll down to the manage attachments section. From there everything is pretty self evident.
