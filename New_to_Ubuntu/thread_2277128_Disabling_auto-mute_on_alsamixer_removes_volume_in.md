### Post by yoann4 on 2015-05-06
I'm new to Linux, just installed. My problem was laggy sound(music), especially when the volume is set up to 100%. I found out that you can get rid of that by disabling auto-mute in alsamixer. I did that but now the volume indicator in the system tray above is gone. When I go to the system preferences, the box to show the volume in the menu bar is checked. If I uncheck it and then check it back, the volume indicator appears but it displays as muted, even though the sound is working fine. I can't drag the line to adjust my volume. FIxing the volume indicator goes back to the first problem: laggy music.
