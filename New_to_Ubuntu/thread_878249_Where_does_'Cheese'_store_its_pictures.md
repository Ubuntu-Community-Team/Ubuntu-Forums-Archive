### Post by kostkon on 2008-08-02
> **linkmaster03 said:**
> Where does the webcam app Cheese store the pictures you take with it?
In *Cheese* you can right click on an image and save it. But just to know, it saves them in
```
~/.gnome2/cheese/media
```

Just open your home folder, select *View -> Show Hidden Files* and you'll managet to see the *.gnome2* folder.
