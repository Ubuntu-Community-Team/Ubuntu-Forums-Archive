### Post by mcduck on 2013-10-25
You could probably find one you like from [http://gnome-look.org]("http://gnome-look.org"). Just download a GTK3 theme you like and extract into ~/.themes or /usr/share/themes.

...just keep in mind that unless you have an OLED display, using a dark theme will actually use more power, not less.
