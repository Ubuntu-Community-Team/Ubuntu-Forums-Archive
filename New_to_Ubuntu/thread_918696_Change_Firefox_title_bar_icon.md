### Post by adam_kimber on 2008-09-14
If you mean the icon that shows up in the Window List then I you might be able to edit those that reside in /usr/lib/firefox-3.0.2pre/icons or /usr/lib/firefox/icons depending on version. 

If you mean the shortcut that is on the desktop or on the panel then right click go to properties and click on the icon on the left. You can now change it.
