### Post by Oldsoldier2003 on 2008-08-31
The first thing to do is check the iso against the md5sum to make sure that you got a good download.

If your download is good it is suggested to burn the CD at the lowest possible speed your burner supports and use a good quality CD. Believe it or not this advice really makes a difference.
