I use two monitors. I'd like to move the upper panel (Applications, Places etc. ) to the secondary monitor and I'd like no panel on the main monitor.

### Post by linuxuser21 on 2009-04-24
Yes. Just right-click on where there is no icon on the panel and go to properties.  Then deselect "expand". Drag it over to the other screen and than you can expand it again if you want and that's it.
