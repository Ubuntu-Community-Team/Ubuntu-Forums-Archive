### Post by warbread on 2008-05-11
Yeah, you can kill it.  Beagle basically searches your hard drive really well.  I never use it because I'm so well organize, I never need to search for anything other than the occasional "whereis".  

Also, go into "System -> Preferences -> Indexing Preferences" and disable everything.  That should free up some resources that are needlessly taken for searching.
