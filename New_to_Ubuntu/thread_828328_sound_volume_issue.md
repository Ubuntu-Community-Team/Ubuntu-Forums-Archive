### Post by kansasnoob on 2008-06-13
Try installing gnome-alsamixer from synaptic. Then Gnome Alsa Mixer will show up in Sound and Video. Then try adjusting volume levels again, especially the three furthest to the left and the four furthest to the right.

I know it makes no sense but I've found that sometimes Gnome Alsa Mixer plays better with some hardware than alsamixer with or without alsamixergui.

It's simple and easily reversible if it doesn't help:)
