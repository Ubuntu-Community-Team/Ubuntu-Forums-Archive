### Post by howefield on 2017-02-28
> **spockswhitepants said:**
> I will go LTS if the alternative is that I have to do a fresh install every few months.

Installing 16.10 effectively means that you will be upgrading every 6 to 9 months as the support life cycle for non LTS releases is 9 months.

So that pretty much means you'll probably prefer the LTS, currently 16.04.2. If you are close to a stock install ie, no installed ppas and no installed proprietary drivers and upgrade from 14.04 should be fairly painless.
