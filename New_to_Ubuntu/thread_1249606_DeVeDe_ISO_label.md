### Post by fooman on 2009-08-25
not sure if this is what you mean, but....

go to applications > sound & video > devede

in the first window that opens, choose "video dvd"

then when the main devede window opens, look in the "titles" window and highlight (click once on) "title 1",  then under that box choose "properties"

when that window opens, type the title of your choice in the title's name box,  then click ok

that should make an iso named "whatever you chose.iso"

hope that helps.
