### Post by kaivalagi on 2009-01-03
Just a thought...

I call Fiji from the UK regularly and have found the cheapest way is through a landline service where you call a number up front and then the number you want to call

An example is [http://www.cheapestcalls.co.uk/index2.php](http://www.cheapestcalls.co.uk/index2.php) - 1.5p / min for India from the UK
