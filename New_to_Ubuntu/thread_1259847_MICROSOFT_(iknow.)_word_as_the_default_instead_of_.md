### Post by ad_267 on 2009-09-06
Open up your file browser and find a word document, right click on it and go to properties, then Open With. Word probably won't be listed there so you'll have to click add, then use a custom command. To find out what command to run have a look at the properties of the launcher for MS Word in your menu.
