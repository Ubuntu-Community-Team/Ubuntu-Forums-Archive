Well hex usually goes letter,number,letter,number,number,number,letter,number,letter,number - so if yours follows that pattern then try that.

But if you do connect every now and again its not your password thats causing the problem. 

When you do connect are you sure your connecting into your own wireless network or have you accidenaly connected into a random network?

Is your wireless card inserted correctly - has your computer taken a knock because that can jolt a card enough to stop it working. If you go into - system, administration, hardware drivers - is your wireless card displayed?
