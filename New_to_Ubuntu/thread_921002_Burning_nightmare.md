### Post by Therion on 2008-09-15
Two suggestions.

1.  Have you checked the MD5Sum on the download so that you know you have a good download of the source .iso?

2.  When you burn the CD, burn it sloooooow.  We're going for accuracy here, not speed.  Burn your .iso to CD damn near as slow as your application will allow.  Something like 8x.  4x would be even better, IMO.
