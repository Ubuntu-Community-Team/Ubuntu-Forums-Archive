### Post by LowSky on 2011-03-29
If you are using any ATI product before HD series on Ubuntu 8.10 or higher you are out of luck.

AMD stopped support after Ubuntu 8.04. The problem was the change to important system files and that AMD didn't have resources to fund two driver teams. Because of this AMD decided to develop for the newer HD line. Does it put many users in odd place, of course. But simply put there is no support from AMD for legacy ATI cards. 


sorry, but if you need full graphic support your only option is Windows or very out of date Linux editions.
