### Post by Paqman on 2010-03-27
Intel chipsets will normally work really well, as Intel are supporters of Linux. Nvidia graphics also work really well if you use the proprietary drivers that the system will prompt you to install.

The best way to find out is to boot up into the LiveCD, but you should be fine.

You can get the LiveCD [here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/GetUbuntu/download"), make sure you hit "alternative download options" and tick 64-bit.
