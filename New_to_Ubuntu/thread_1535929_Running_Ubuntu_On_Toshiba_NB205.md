### Post by snowpine on 2010-07-21
Ubuntu and Ubuntu Netbook Edition share the same "core" system (they are just different user interfaces or "skins") and therefore switching will not solve your microphone, sound, and webcam issues.

I would suggest starting a new, separate thread for each of your hardware questions. (Sorry that I do not have specific solutions, but I am not familiar with your specific hardware.)

ps If you decide to switch to the Ubuntu Netbook interface, you do not need to reinstall. You can install it from the Ubuntu Software Center.
