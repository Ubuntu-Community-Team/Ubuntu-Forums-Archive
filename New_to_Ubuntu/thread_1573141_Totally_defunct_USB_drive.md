### Post by coffeecat on 2010-09-12
> **jre6 said:**
> Could this be the result of repeated use of the USB pen drive in Ubuntu, which may have messed up with the file system?

Repeated use - yes. Repeated use in Ubuntu - no more likely than with another OS. Except it's not the filesystem that's messed up - the whole drive has probably failed. USB pen drives typically fail suddenly and much sooner than hard drives. See wikipedia on longevity:

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USB_flash_drive#Longevity](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USB_flash_drive#Longevity)

That's not much at all. I've heard of pendrives failing after only one month of (admittedly heavy) use.

Sorry that I can't be more helpful. If it has failed then I don't know a way of accessing the data.
