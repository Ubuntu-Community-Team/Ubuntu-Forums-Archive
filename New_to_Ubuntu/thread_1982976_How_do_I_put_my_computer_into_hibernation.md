### Post by billyseth on 2012-05-19
Yeah, I read somewhere they disabled it to avoid "confusion" between hibernate and suspend. Open a terminal and run

```
sudo pm-hibernate
```

[http://www.howtogeek.com/113923/how-to-re-enable-hibernate-in-ubuntu-12.04/](http://www.howtogeek.com/113923/how-to-re-enable-hibernate-in-ubuntu-12.04/)
