### Post by ubunterooster on 2010-05-20
System Monitor shows the correct name of running processes.

I also right-click the menu and choose "edit menu" this starts the program```
alacarte
```. 
Go to the item you want and double-click it. This brings up name, command, and icon of a said program. 

Most programs have an "about" button in their menus and this (as far as I have seen) shows the commandline name.

Three ways to do it, I hope one helps. :D
