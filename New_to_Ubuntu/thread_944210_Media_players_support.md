### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-10-11
Well there are all kinds of media players for Ubuntu out there, but the question I must ask is what are you looking for in a media player?
Are you looking for something itunes like?
Rhythmbox, Exaile and Banshee would suit you there.
Want something like winamp?
I suggest audacious
Want a full featured video player?
Totem, Xine and VLC are good choices, as is Mplayer or SMPlayer.
Please tell us what you need and we can try to provide.
