### Post by ace007 on 2008-05-19
I know that the gtk2.x themes from gnome-look are hit or miss.  I have found some excellent ones that have skins for each part of gnome.  Others have just a skin for the window.

I am not sure exactly what you are asking for though?  You want more built-in controls for changing the theme?  What else are you looking to change.  The gnome panel colors? Icons? Font? 

If you install one of the gtk2.x themes from gnome-look (my favorite is [[COLOR="Blue"]_Moomex_[/COLOR]]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Moomex-Theme?content=57063")) and then navigate to /home/username/.themes/Moomex/gtk-2.0 you can take a look at the gtkrc file, which is similar to a config file for the theme.

EDIT: OR ignore what I said and check this out [http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials](http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials)
