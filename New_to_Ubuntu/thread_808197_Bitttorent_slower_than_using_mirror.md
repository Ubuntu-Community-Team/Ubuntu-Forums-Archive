### Post by Ansible on 2008-05-26
Often a bittorrent will start slow and then speed up as more connections are made with peers.  That's normal.  

bittorrent is certainly not guaranteed to be faster than a regular download, you just have the satisfaction of knowing you're saving bandwidth for the mirror servers.  In fact, it may be considerably slower, especially if the mirrors are not under heavy use.
