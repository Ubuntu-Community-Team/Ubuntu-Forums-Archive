### Post by skyjacker on 2010-09-16
Try this.... power down, plug in the headphones, and then re-start.  When you are powered back up open the sound again, and choose the "Hardware" tab. Are the headphones listed?
P.S. If you really want to turn off the PC speakers, go to sound preferences, click on the hardware tab, and in the "profiles" box select "off" for the internal audio..
