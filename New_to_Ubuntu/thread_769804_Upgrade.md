### Post by Tatty on 2008-04-26
There is no way to say for certain that the upgrade will work.  The chances are that it will, but upgrading an OS is inherently a risky thing to do.

If you are happy with your desktop then just stick to your current system.  The changes are not likely to be noticed in your day to day computing needs.

Dont be afraid of re-installing from scratch should an upgrade fail.  Whilst it would be nice to not have to go through all the setting up again, you have proven to yourself that you can do it.  And this time it will probably go much smoother and quicker as you know what you are doing now.
