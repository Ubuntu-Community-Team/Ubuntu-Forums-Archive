### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-02-03
Note of caution is in order here.  AMD (who bought ATI) discontinued Linux driver support for your card years ago.  When you install Ubuntu, it will automatically install the default open-source video drivers for your card.

There are NO OTHER DRIVERS for that card that will work with current versions of Ubuntu.

So, don't let anyone pressure you into downloading and installing video drivers -- that will only make the matters worse.
