### Post by coldraven on 2013-05-29
Bear with me, this story has some relevance, I hope.
A friend of mine moved to Germany with his German girlfriend, he could not speak German.
Some time later I rang him to see what he was doing, he said "I'm teaching English".
This surprised me because he was not a highly educated person. When I asked how he taught he said "I give them an English newspaper and tell them that I can speak for an hour or they can speak for an hour". So while the student read the newspaper he would just correct their mistakes.
He made good money doing this.

So here is my idea:
Advertise for teaching computer skills at a low cost. Teach one student at a time.
Let the student ask the questions and solve them together using the resources that you find online.
That way you both learn and if the cost to the student is low they will not mind if you do not know everything.
Gradually you will become an expert in Gnu/Linux.
I wish you well, good luck.
