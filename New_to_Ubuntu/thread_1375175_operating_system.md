### Post by mlentink on 2010-01-08
I am also having trouble understanding just what it is you want to achieve.

You cannot make a linux operating system. It already exists. I am using it to type this message. What you could do is develop a new Linux distribution, which is a combination of the Linux kernel with a selection of drivers, utiities and application software.

Obviously, if you feel up to it, you could develop your very own operating system from scratch. That would be some task. It would take a few years as well. See e.g. here to get an idea
[http://scholar.google.nl/scholar?q=operating+systems+development&hl=nl&lr=&um=1&ie=UTF-8&oi=scholart](http://scholar.google.nl/scholar?q=operating+systems+development&hl=nl&lr=&um=1&ie=UTF-8&oi=scholart)

Does this help to rephrase your question in such a way that we could point you in the right direction?
