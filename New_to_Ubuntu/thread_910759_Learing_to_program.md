### Post by Cheesehead on 2008-09-04
If you want a job doing it: Get books and study. Get graded. Get a degree in it. Drink a lot.

If you simply want to learn it, then *write something*. Build a script that downloads your porn faster, or scans Craisglist for you, or sends 'thank you' notes to all those nice guys in Nigeria who want you to have their money. Then use Google and forums and references and tutorials to figure out how to do it.

I wrote a very annoying 'Reminder Bot' that sent random e-mail to my friends 'You have an appointment at the STD clinic on Thursday. Bring $25 and your last three partners'. Then I fixed it so it reminds me to pay the rent.
