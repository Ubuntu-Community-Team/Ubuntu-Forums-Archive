### Post by tacantara on 2009-12-31
Window borders are set up under the Theme tab in Appearance Preferences.  To get that open, just right-click on the desktop and select "Change desktop background."

If you want a wider variety of window border/icon themes, click on the "Get more themes" link in the appearance preferences.

I hope this info will help you.
