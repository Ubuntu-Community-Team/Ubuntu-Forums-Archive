### Post by pparks1 on 2008-04-26
Wireless is quite often a problem, regardless of the Linux distribution in use.   Most problems come down to the actual wireless card/chipset that you have in your computer.

For example, my Dell has a Broadcomm card and these are often quite a pain to get working.   It usually takes me an hour or two to get it working on any random distribution that I try.

However, if I simply connect or install an Intel card it works pretty much right out of the box.   Sometimes it's best to just scrap the onboard card and use a PCMCIA card.

i wish you the best of luck.
