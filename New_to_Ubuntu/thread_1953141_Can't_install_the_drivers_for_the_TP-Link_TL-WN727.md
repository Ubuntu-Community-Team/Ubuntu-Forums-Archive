### Post by kurt18947 on 2012-04-05
According to WikiDevi the TL-WN727Nv1 uses a Ralink RT3070 chip and rt2800usb or rt2870sta are supposed to drive it.  Note this is version 1.  I don't know if there are others but one of the maddening things to me about wifi adapter is that model xyz v1 may use one chip, model xyz v2 may use a different chip set.  Assuming it is indeed an RT3070, you could search in the networking & wireless forum for  that chip set.  Look especially for the 'solved' threads.  Here is one that is not marked 'solved' but the person was successful.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1781517&highlight=rt3070](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1781517&highlight=rt3070)

There are a few people on this forum that are wizards with wireless problems.  I am not one.  Perhaps they will check in here.   O.T. I've been using Linux for a couple years now.  One lesson I've learned is to do a little homework when shopping for hardware, especially wireless devices and printers.  Some are dreams, some are nightmares.  Some time spent on this forum makes it easy to tell which is which.  I try to leave the nightmares on the shelf at the store.
