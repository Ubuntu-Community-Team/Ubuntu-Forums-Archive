### Post by QIII on 2012-05-10
If it is a Silverlight application, Moonlight may not work.  It's hit or miss.

Although Moonlight is sanctioned by Microsoft as an open source implementation of Silverlight, not all sites will work with it.

Try going to another site that serves Silverlight content and see if you have any luck there.  I know it won't work for viewing Silverlight content on Xfinity for me.

Let us know.
