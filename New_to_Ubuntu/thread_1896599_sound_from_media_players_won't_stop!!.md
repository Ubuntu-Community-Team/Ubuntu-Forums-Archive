### Post by scorchgeek on 2011-12-17
Recent versions of Ubuntu have made media players (at least Banshee, and Rhythmbox before Ubuntu switched) minimize to the tray instead of closing. If you take a close look at Banshee's file menu, you'll see that there's actually no Quit button, just a Close button.

The only way I know of to truly quit the player is to kill the process, but you can get around it by just making sure you pause the player before you close it. And if you close it and still have the music running, you can stop it by clicking the speaker icon next to the clock and clicking the pause button.
