### Post by DarinB on 2009-09-17
I couldn't get "CD Quality, MP3" to appear in the drop down list under Edit -> Preferences -> Music -> Preferred Format. I needed to have Ubuntu packages BOTH liblame0 and gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly-multiverse installed.    After some more research,  installing gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly-multiverse. This package includes liblame0, 

Then MP3 appeared in the "Preferred Format" dropdown for Rhythmbox and it will transcode to MP3.
