2)im thinking in the future to replace my 500GB hdd with an ssd.But i i'm not sure if my current hdd is connected via sata 2 or 3. How can i check this? 
This is motherboard dependent, check your bios to references of SATA III or check the motherboard manufacturers website. (Note: Don't replace, add. large ssd's are [rediculously expensive]("http://www.ebuyer.com/search?page=1&sort=popularity&limit=10&store=33&cat=385&filtersubcat=3440&filterea19=480+%26amp%3B+Above"))

> 
3)does it matter if i have a ssd with sata 3 port and connect him via sata 2 to the laptop?SATAIII drive connecting to a SATAII port will probably not work (Comparing this to say, connecting a wiimote to a gamecube)
SATAII drive connecting to a SATAIII port will probably work (Comparing this to say, connecting a gamecube controller to a wii)

Hope this helps,
Bodsda
