### Post by grahammechanical on 2020-09-07
The first repository is an official Ubuntu repository (archive.canonical.com/ubuntu focal InRelease). The other repositories are mirrors. They may not be authenticated by Ubuntu developers.

[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+mirror/mirror.exid.us-release](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+mirror/mirror.exid.us-release)

[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+mirror/mirror.exid.us-archive](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+mirror/mirror.exid.us-archive)

The organisation hosting these mirrors might be having some problems. You could try changing servers. We do that from Software and Updates>Ubuntu software>Download from. Click the down arrow and then click Other and make a choice.
