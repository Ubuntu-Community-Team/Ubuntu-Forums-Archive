### Post by Peter09 on 2010-08-18
NoScript will prevent sites from accessing other parts (illegal?) of the disk. Presumably you are opening a site that is attempting to do this. Application Boundaries Enforcer is the type of denial which is active.
 
You can change this by editing the preferences in Noscript and putting the site in question on the Whitelist.
 
[http://noscript.net/abe/](http://noscript.net/abe/)
