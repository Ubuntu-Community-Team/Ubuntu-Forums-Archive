### Post by kerry_s on 2010-05-17
try-> **alt+space**

you know you can control the mouse with the keyboard numberpad.
i think it's-> **Alt+Shift+NumLock**

1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 7, 8 and 9 keys are used to move the mouse cursor
5 is used as mouse click button select from:
/ left click
* middle click
- right click
