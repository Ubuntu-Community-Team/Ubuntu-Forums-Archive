### Post by Shazaam on 2009-01-22
Per Logitech, manual configuration of "Secure Connect" is not required...
```
With Logitech’s new SecureConnect technology, the cordless keyboard, mouse and receiver are all pre-synchronized and pre-connected during manufacturing.
They’re ready to go right out of the box, with a secure link and without the hassle of a manual connection process.
Simply insert the batteries and go.
And to assure security, encryption of keyboard activity is enabled by default.
```
From here (pdf)...
[http://www.logitech.com/pub/pdf/bluetooth/secure_connect_whitepaper.pdf](http://www.logitech.com/pub/pdf/bluetooth/secure_connect_whitepaper.pdf)
