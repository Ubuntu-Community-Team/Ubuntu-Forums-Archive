### Post by migs73 on 2011-02-23
Assuming bt is bluetooth, it is because the Linux kernel has many built in drivers for thousands of different types of hardware. Obviously your bluetooth hardware is supported out of the box, but your broadcom wireless card requires the downloading of a proprietary (therefore not included in the kernel) driver.
