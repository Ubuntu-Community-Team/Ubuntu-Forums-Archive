### Post by howefield on 2011-10-15
Use the Dash (top  button on the launcher) to search for terminal or press Ctrl - Alt - T.

Once opened, you can right click its icon on the Launcher and select Keep in Launcher if you want an easy way to start in future. Same goes for pretty much any application.
