### Post by sudo_chop on 2008-10-01
Brasero (Applications->Sound & Video->Brasero Disc Burning) has a tool to "blank" a disk. I've never used it but I'm sure its what you are looking for. Its under tools->erase when you open Brasero. Hope that helps!
 
Edit: and then for burning to it again, brasero can be used to make data cds. Its one of the options when you first open brasero.
