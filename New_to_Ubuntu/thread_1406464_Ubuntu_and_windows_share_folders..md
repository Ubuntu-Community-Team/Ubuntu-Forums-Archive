How exactly did you install ubuntu ?
If you installed ubuntu inside windows(using *wubi.exe*) then you can see the drive in which you installed by opening _~/host folder_

You can also view them in  places.


If on the other hand you have installed Ubuntu on a separate drive then you don't have any problems .. just see your windows partitions in **Places** drop-down in the top left.Or may also view them in **Computer**
