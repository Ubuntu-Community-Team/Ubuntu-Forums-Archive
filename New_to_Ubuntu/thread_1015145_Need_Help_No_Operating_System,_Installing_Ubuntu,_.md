### Post by jimmy the saint on 2008-12-18
Don't worry about updating your bios.  The most you should have to worry about is setting the bios to boot from a cd.  Wiping your HDD will not affect the bios.  You don't need to backup the bios.  The bios is stored on the MOBO and, while I am sure there is a complex way to update it with a live cd, floppy and lots of time, you will be just fine without doing it before you install Ubuntu.
