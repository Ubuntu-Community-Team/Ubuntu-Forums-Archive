### Post by zontiboy on 2011-12-29
It's not like symbols or special characters, those are special ASCII characters.
What I meant is that, for example you live in US, there the keyboard differ from the Spanish or french ones.
That mean that one key with an unique ID must be interpreted as the letter that shows in the keyboard, depending from the location that it come from it will be interpreted in different ways.
That is what input mode switcher do, changes the interpretation of the keyboard.
