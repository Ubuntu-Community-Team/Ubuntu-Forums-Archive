### Post by lechien73 on 2012-01-10
It doesn't look like you can change the format of the notification too much. The settings for this are contained in **Edit -> Preferences -> Advanced -> Config Editor**

Search for **alert**

It's a bit more usable if you set **mail.biff.alert.show_preview** to false. At least you can then see the subject line and "from *Name of Sender*".

The notification is generated by your email client. Please look for relevant settings there. For example, in Thunderbird, look at Edit-Preferences-General-Show an alert-Customize... 

You will have options to display "Message preview text", "Subject" and "Sender".
