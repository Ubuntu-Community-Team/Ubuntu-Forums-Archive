### Post by korleon on 2010-04-24
You may have removed or not started the "Windows List" on you panel.  Right click on the panel and then choose "Add to Panel" its near the end of the list.  Hope that helps.

### Post by Hamchan on 2010-04-24
We need more information on your setup.

Are you using Gnome and if so, did you do anything to change the panels?

Assuming that you are using Gnome, you should be able to add a 'window list' to the panels. Just right click on a panel and select 'add to panel' and find the entry called 'window list' on the list that will be brought up.
