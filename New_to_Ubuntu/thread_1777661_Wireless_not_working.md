### Post by wolfen69 on 2011-06-08
Ubuntu is not by default a router. Just because ubuntu is wired to the net doesn't mean the ipod can connect. You would need to turn your ubuntu install into a transmitter.

If you were connected for a bit on the ipod, it was probably connected to an open network for a little while and then disconnected. You can't just leech off your ubuntu connection without a bridge of some sort.
