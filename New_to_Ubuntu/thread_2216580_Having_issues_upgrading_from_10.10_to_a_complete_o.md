### Post by meistersreign on 2014-04-12
Well a couple of questions:
1.) Are you trying to install 13.10 from the usb drive onto the netbook, or just trying to use it as a live cd type of deal without installing?
2.) If you're trying to install, maybe the usb wasn't installed correctly. There's a good guide for it here - [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick)

Another guess is that maybe your netbook isn't up to par with what 13.10 asks for as far as a minimum requirements, and perhaps another edition such as LUbuntu, or XBuntu would work better for you. Those two are much "lighter" on computer resources. Also, 14.04 which is a LTS (Long Term Support) version of Ubuntu is set to be released in less than a week (the 17th). If you are wanting to install, perhaps waiting for that release would be better.
