### Post by InfectedWithDrew on 2009-06-07
Replace Winamp with Songbird or Amarok in my opinion, but since it's a Mini 9... you're probably going to get stuck with VLC or Banshee.

Replace Dreamweaver with [KompoZer](http://kompozer.net/).

Other things: KMyMoney to replace Quickbooks, GIMP for Photoshop, Pidgin for IM of any kind, AbiWord for MS Word (OO Writer is too laggy on your small laptop).
