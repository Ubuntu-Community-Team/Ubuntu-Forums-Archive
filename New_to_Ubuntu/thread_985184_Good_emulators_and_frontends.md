### Post by sirjoebob on 2008-11-17
SNES- ZSNES. Good frontend and runs great. Install from Synaptic.

Nintendo- GFCEU. Another easy-to-use frontend and installs from Synaptic.

Sega Genesis- GENS. See [this post]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=290008") on installing, etc.

Not aware of any one-emulator-to-rule-them-all but it could be done...
