### Post by blueridgedog on 2009-01-21
Well, logic tells me that either you need to get your audio system on your home net with an input device that can connect to the net and has an output that your amp can take (RCA for example) or you need to simply connect your laptop to the amp via a micro stereo to RCA cable using the headphone jack.

If you are interested in the first approach, the network media tank line of equipment has caught my eye.

See [http://www.mpcclub.com/modules/Reviews/reviews/NMT-CLASH.pdf](http://www.mpcclub.com/modules/Reviews/reviews/NMT-CLASH.pdf) for some good info on the range.
