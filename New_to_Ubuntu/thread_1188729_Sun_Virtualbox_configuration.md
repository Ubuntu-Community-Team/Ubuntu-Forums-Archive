### Post by PureLoneWolf on 2009-06-16
I believe you are referring to Seamless mode, but I don't think you can get it to launch in seamless mode from the GUI.

According to the VirtualBox forums, if you shutdown the machine in seamless mode, it should remember it the next time you launch the virtual machine.

Unless you have changed it, pressing RightCTRL and L will get you in and out of seamless mode
