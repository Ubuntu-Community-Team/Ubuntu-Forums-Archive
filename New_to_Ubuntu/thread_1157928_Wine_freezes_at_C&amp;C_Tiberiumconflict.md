### Post by Aaardwark on 2009-05-21
The first thing you should do before trying out a game in wine, is to have a look at the [wine appdb]("http://appdb.winehq.org"). There you will find out if the game works or not, you can also find helpful workarounds or improvements. Looking at [Tiberium Wars]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=7440"), there are some workarounds (don't know if any of the applies to your problem).

Another thing to do if you want the best out of wine, is generally to have the [latest and greatest]("http://www.winehq.org/download/deb"). Just follow the instructions to ad it to the repository. Sometimes wine gets regressions, but generally it's moving forward...FAST. If you look around among the games and apps in the appdb, you will quickly realize why you should have a newer release.
