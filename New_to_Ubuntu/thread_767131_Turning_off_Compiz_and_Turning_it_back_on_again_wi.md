### Post by chrispche on 2008-04-25
How do I disable Compiz, then re-enable Compiz in Hardy Heron without losing my settings?

I need to disable it to play some games (Half Life 2 The Orange Box) then when I have finished playing re-enable it back to how I set it up.

If I go to appearance and go to none for Desktop effects. Then go to extra. I lose all my Compiz settings. Then have to go through the tick boxes again.

This is frustrating in Gutsy. You could just re-enable by going to custom in the appearance menu.

---

### Post by sayakb on 2008-04-25
Create a launcher for the command: metacity --replace
And another for: compiz --replace

Use each to disable/enable compiz.
