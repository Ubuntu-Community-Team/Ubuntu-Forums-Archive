> **mac143_01 said:**
> Do the games you mentioned are for FREE? whera can I get those games?

Open up Add/Remove (Applications > Add/Remove) and select "Games" tab, you will see a list of games, select whichever you want and hit "Apply". There are great games in the repositories. To name a few: Flight Gear, Nexuiz, Scorched 3D, Vega Strike.
