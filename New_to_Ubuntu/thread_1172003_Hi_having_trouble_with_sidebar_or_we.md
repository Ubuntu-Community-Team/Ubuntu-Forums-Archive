### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2009-05-28
Right now there is not much like the Vista sidebar for Ubuntu, though there are a few promising tools.
One is google gadgets, then there is Karamba, and the ever developing plasmoids for KDE4.

I would say KDE4 comes closest to a vista like experience in terms of having a sidebar, there are many widgets for it called plasmoids that give you clocks, calendars, etc.
But KDE4 is still very new and therefore I would not say install it if you are not a experienced user.

Gdesklets is another one but I think it sucks, there are many themes for it yes but its rough around the edges.
