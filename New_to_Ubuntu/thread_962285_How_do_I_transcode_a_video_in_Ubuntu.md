### Post by acsworrell on 2008-10-29
Actually, you have several options. You could use DVD::Rip, Acidrip, or Handbrake GTK. I've found that Handbrake GTK is about the best. There are presets you can use on the right hand side of the gui if you know what you want but not how to set it up. Here is the link. This guy did and awesome job explaining the install of Handbrake.
