### Post by Maheriano on 2009-08-04
The problem with a lot of beginners is they know how to do something in Windows and when they apply that in Ubuntu, it doesn't work. You have to learn from the beginning again, but I promise, once you learn it you will love it!

What you do is simply plug in the webcam and try it. If it doesn't work, try going to Add/Remove under applications and installing a program called Cheese. Once that installs, your webcam should for sure work. If it doesn't then it's likely really old and won't work. I bet it will though.
