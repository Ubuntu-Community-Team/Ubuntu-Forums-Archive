### Post by FuturePilot on 2008-06-11
Open your home folder and press Crtl+H to show hidden folders. Find the one called .purple. Inside that one there will be one called smileys. Now extract the package of smilies you have and you should get a folder out of the archive. Place that folder in the "smileys" folder.
