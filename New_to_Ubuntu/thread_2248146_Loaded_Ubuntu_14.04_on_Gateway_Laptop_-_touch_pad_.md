### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2014-10-12
You should do the basic troubleshooting before anything else:
- Check at BIOS as some allow several internal devices including touchpads to be enabled/disabled.
- Check if there's a key or key combo to enabled/disable the touchpad.

---

### Post by cwmoser on 2014-10-13
Thanks.  You got me on the right track.
Pressing F6 by itself (not Fn + F6) enables/disables the touch pad
