### Post by stderr on 2009-09-13

A 3GHz CPU = not bad at all. 128MB RAM = very, very, very minimal. Even with small distros such as Puppy, or graphically simple DEs such as LXDE, you're likely to run into problems trying to run modern applications such as Firefox or Openoffice. As arochester says, could you not possibly slot a larger module in? They're very cheap these days.
