### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-11-29
No drivers are being offered because, most likely, that is one of the many older Radeon cards that got moved to "legacy" status years ago by ATI.  That means, there are no current restricted drivers available for that chipset -- and nothing you download from the Web is going to work, either.

The open-source drivers are installed by default, and those are the only ones available now.
