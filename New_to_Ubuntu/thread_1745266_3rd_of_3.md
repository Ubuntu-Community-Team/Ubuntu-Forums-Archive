### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-30
Hey there weezyrider!

To turn off the update 'nag', menu to System >> Preferences >> Startup Applications:

[IMG]http://img405.imageshack.us/img405/1935/startupapplications.png[/IMG]

Then uncheck the 'Update Notifier'.  It will stop bugging you then.


Natty/11.04 does offer the UI you are used to: [**_Natty Info: Your UI options_**]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1741293"), but don't be in any hurry to upgrade. (and I don't think you are) :D


Going to any other distro is always an option, but right now every disto is having to deal with the death of Gnome 2.x, so most are scrambling about, too.

For you, I would suggest waiting out the crazyness by staying in 10.10, getting stellarium to run the way you want :D , and decide on a possible distro change after the dust settles.
