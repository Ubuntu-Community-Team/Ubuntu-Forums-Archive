### Post by Dennis N on 2019-03-15
Click on the shutdown icon on the panel. At the bottom of the menu that appears are three icons. The one on the left is for 'settings'. For the dock, you can 'autohide' or always show the dock. Those are the only two choices here. autohide 'on' setting still shows the dock as long as it doesn't overlap any open windows.
