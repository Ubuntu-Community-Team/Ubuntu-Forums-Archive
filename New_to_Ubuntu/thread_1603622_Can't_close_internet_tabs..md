Hello

This got rid of the unwanted tabs.  

Firefox:
edit--> preferences
"Privacy"--> "Settings", check Cookies.

When the computer rebooted it said it was having a hard time connecting to tabs.  The options was to reestablish connection or fresh start.  or some thing like that.  When i picked fresh start all was clear
