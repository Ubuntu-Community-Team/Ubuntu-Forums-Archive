### Post by sydbat on 2010-08-03
> **tc101 said:**
> How do I make everything one the screen larger?  The type is to small to see.  I went to System/Preferences/Appearance, but don't see how to do it.It's under the "Fonts" tab. Once there, you can choose to make each font larger, and change them to suit your tastes. 

Under the "Rendering" section at the bottom is a "Details" button. You will find a "Resolution" drop box at the top of the ensuing screen. It is defaulted at 96, but you can increase the size to whatever you like.
