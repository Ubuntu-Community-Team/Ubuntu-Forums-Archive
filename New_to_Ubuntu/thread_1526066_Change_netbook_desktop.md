### Post by fooman on 2010-07-07
if you want "kubuntu netbook remix"....then search in synaptic for "kubuntu netbook" and install.

after the install completes,  log-out and when you get to the log-in screen...click once on your user name,  then at the bottom of the screen,  click the drop-down menu and choose "kubuntu netbook" (or kde netbook? ...not sure on the wording).

switch back the same way.
