### Post by cozmicharlie on 2008-12-23
I would recommend that you do a little reading in the wiki to familiarize your self with how things work in Ubuntu.  To point you in the right direction for most of your questions:

> is there any way to change the "applications places system" font or make it bolder?

All fonts etc are controlled by going to System>Preferences>Appearance

> with cairo dock:
Not sure what you are asking?

> i can't see all the available configs (making it 3d and such)
System>Preferences>Appearance>Visual effects
You can use compiz manager to set 3D effects etc

> i don't know how to make starting programs on ubuntu
Not sure what you mean.  Starting programs is just like Windows.  Go to the Applications menu and find the program you want and click on the menu entry to start it.

> is there any way that i can make a folder appear on it?
Do you mean you want to place a folder on the desktop?  Just drag it to the desktop.

> whenever i try to open elisa, i get this black screen with random colors all over the place.
You need to install multimedia codecs in Ubuntu.  See this tutorial:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683)

> o also, the sound is a lot lot lower than it is in vista...
Make sure all the channels are turned on in the mixer. Double click on the applet that looks like a speaker.

> also... is there a way i can change the battery preferences?
You can do some modifying at System>Preferences>Power Management
