### Post by DuckHook on 2016-07-01
Updating is not just about improving performance. In fact, it is most often about closing security holes, fixing bugs or keeping the app/driver/service current  with newer system libraries. You really should keep drivers updated for security reasons. If you don't trust the driver update, you should reconsider having the driver in the first place.
