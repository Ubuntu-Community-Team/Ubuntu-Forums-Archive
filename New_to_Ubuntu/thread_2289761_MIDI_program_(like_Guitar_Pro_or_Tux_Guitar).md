### Post by Dennis N on 2015-08-06
Players:
I know that you can play MIDI with Audacious, Timidity (from command line), and also VLC from what I have read. I have Audacious set up to play them. 
For Audacious, you use its AMidi Plugin with a sound font - best are probably the fluid-soundfonts from the Ubuntu repository.

I know timidity will also output MIDI to another format - mp3 or ogg for example, using the command line. There may be better tools for this, however. Someone may give you some suggestions.

I can't answer the other question - someone else may be able to help.
