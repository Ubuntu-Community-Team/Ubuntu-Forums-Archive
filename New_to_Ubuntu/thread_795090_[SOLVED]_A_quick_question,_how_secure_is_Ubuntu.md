### Post by Cypher on 2008-05-15
You don't have to worry about viruses on Linux..the same goes for spyware that wants to install itself on your machine and then do bad things.

So you are secure as far as that goes, but you are still vulnerable to phishing attacks or anything that can cause harm remotely.

You don't need to worry about anti-virus or spyware software on Linux. Additionally, since Firefox is the default browser in Ubuntu you don't have to worry about all of IE's exploits..
