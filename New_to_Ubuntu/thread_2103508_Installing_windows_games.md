### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-01-11
> **d4rk0wl said:**
> ... I haven't had any personal experience running games through it, though I use it to run MS Office and it works great...

Which is an ENTIRELY different situation ... because you do NOT need hardware acceleration to use MS Office, and you don't need the latest DirectX implementation, either.  Also, MS Office is unlikely to overheat your GPU.

Gaming, in contrast, DOES need 3D hardware acceleration to get decent frame rates, needs the latest DirectX implementation, and due to the demands on the GPU(s) nearly always needs proprietary drivers to allow proper management of heat issues.

Gaming, especially Window gaming, is a whole different animal.
