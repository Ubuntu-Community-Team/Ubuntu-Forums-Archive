### Post by newb85 on 2013-10-26
Those two programs are set to run in the background when you close their windows.  They can still be accessed through the indicators in the top-right corner of your screen.  For Rhythmbox, click the sound menu (the volume control icon) and then click Rhythmbox.  If you want to stop it from running in the background, go to Music>Quit.  Skype is similar, but to make it stop running click the Skype icon in the lower-left corner of the window and then click Quit.
