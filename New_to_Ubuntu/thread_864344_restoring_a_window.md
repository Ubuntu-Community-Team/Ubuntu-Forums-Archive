### Post by unutbu on 2008-07-19
The name of the window should appear on the gnome panel at the bottom of your screen. Click it, and the window should pop back up.

---

### Post by Paddy Landau on 2008-07-19
You should see the minimised window as a small bar on the bottom of your screen.

Just click it once.

Alternatively, right-click it and choose "Unminimize".
