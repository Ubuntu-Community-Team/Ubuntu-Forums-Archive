### Post by Riffer on 2008-10-26
I think it might be your graphics card.  On most comps (I think that some laptops may not have it) there is something called a post beep and these beeps can tell you if everything is ok or not.  If you have no beep for most comps that means your graphics card is no good.

You could try gently taking out the graphics card and trying the onboard graphics.  Bear in mind that with some mother boards you also have to change a jumper setting.  I believe you can get that sort of info on the net.

Good luck
