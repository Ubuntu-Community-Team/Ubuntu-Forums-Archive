### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-11-24
Sorry, not familiar with Intel-specific drivers, but with ATI drivers, the answer is a resounding NO.  The drivers were developed for specific kernels, and since the kernel in use in 9.10 is very different than the kernel that was in use for 7.10, the drivers won't work.

I suspect that the same limitation applies with Intel drivers.
