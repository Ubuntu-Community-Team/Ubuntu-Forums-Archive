### Post by aeiah on 2008-10-09
if it isnt in your menu under games, perhaps you could see if its been put in a menu section that isnt being displayed. right click the menu and click 'edit menus'. if you find it, put it where you want (ie, in the games subcategory). if you cant see it (most likely), or you're lazy, just add it as a new entry. the command will be dopewars. you can confirm this first by typing dopewars into the terminal and seeing if it launches.
