### Post by startherepodcast on 2008-12-23
If wireless doesn't even show up in Network Manager, then you probably have to install a driver for the card. 
Go to ystem -> Administration -> Hardware Drivers and see if there is anything available for your card. 
Hope that helps you get your card working. If your wireless card does 100% not work with ubuntu you can still buy a USB wireless stick, they are small, cheap and really handy when you want to connect to a wireless network on a desktop or a laptop.
