### Post by fooman on 2008-10-26
did you try the alsamixer?  ...double click the speaker icon in your top panel.  click the "preferences" button and enable the master, surround, center, lfe and any other controls you would like to be available from the mixer.

adjust the levels to your liking.

hope that helps.
