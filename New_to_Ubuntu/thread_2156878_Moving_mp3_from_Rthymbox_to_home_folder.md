### Post by deadflowr on 2013-06-23
Rhythmbox doesn't hold any songs.
To locate where the song's file is, right-click on the song and go to properties, then details.
It'll show you where the song is saved in the location line.
Usually it's saved in the Music directory.
Once you find where the file is, open the home folder and go to where it said.
Then copy or move it to the desired location.
