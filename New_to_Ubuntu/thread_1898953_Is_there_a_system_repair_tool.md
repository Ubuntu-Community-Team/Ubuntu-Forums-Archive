### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2011-12-22
When the flash drive was attached to ubuntu, did you empty your trash before unmounting it?
When you delete files/folders from a removable drive in ubuntu, they are not gone forever, they go to the .Trash folder in the removable media, so no new space is gained unless you empty the trash folder with the drive attached.
