### Post by dnairb on 2009-05-19
What you need is the **Notification Area**:

Right click panel --> select "Add to Panel" --> Click "Notification Area"

<rant>a few times people have asked about this, and have been told to add "Network Monitor" to their panel. This is wrong. The network monitor applet appears in the notification area.</rant - thanks for listening :)>
