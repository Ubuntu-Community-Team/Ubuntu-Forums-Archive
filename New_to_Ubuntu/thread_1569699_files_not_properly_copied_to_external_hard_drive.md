### Post by bredman on 2010-09-07
You may be wondering why you are not seeing lots of replies. The reason is that all we can do is offer condolences. It is very unlikely that you will be able to recover your files.

Backing up important files is not an easy job, and is more difficult when you ask one Operating System (Ubuntu) to back up files from another Operating System (Windows). If you are new to Ubuntu, this was a difficult job to try as your first major task.

It's not much consolation, but there is a chance that your Windows installation had corrupted the disk before you tried to copy it.

I hope this bad experience does not tarnish your opinion of Ubuntu.

Does anybody have any suggestions?
