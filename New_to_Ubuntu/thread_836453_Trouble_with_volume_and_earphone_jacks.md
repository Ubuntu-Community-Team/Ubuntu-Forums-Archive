### Post by danbodoh on 2008-06-21
On my inspiron 1525, I got better volume control by using the PCM control.

Try this:

Double click on the speaker icon in the upper right.  Try controlling both the Master and the PCM volumes.  If the PCM gives better control, then you can set all volume controls to use the PCM instead of Master.

To do so, first turn the Master to 100%.  Then go to "Preferences...Sound" then choose "PCM" in default mixer tracks.

Hope that works for you.  It worked great for me.
