### Post by DetroitLibertyPenguin on 2008-10-16
Have you tried simply doing a soft reset on your phone? (Similar to restarting your computer, turn the phone off remove the battery and the SIM for a few moments, put it back in and turn it back on, this actually fixes most connection issues, hurray for low RAM in most modern cell phones!) 

If that doesn't work we'll need additional information to decipher the issue. It all depends on a few other variables: Number 1 what phone are you using (manufacturer, model, and service provider)? What Bluetooth hardware are you using? (Dongle, built in, designed originally for Windoze/mac, etc)? 

What kind of files are you trying to send? 

What Bluetooth packs have you installed on your Ubunutu.
