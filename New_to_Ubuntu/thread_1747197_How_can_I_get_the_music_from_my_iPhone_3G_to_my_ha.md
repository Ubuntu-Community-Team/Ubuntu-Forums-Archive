### Post by wigout on 2011-05-02
I don't use banshee and I don't have an iphone.

Google lead me to look at the terms:
banshee iphone import

and I found this page ([http://www.sythe.org/showthread.php?t=419355](http://www.sythe.org/showthread.php?t=419355)) which has a 16 step guide to installing and using banshee on ubuntu.

The last few steps are:
> **[COLOR=DarkOrange][SIZE=3]13.[/SIZE][/COLOR]** Plug in  your iPhone/iPod if it isn&#8217;t already, and it should appear in the left  column. High-light files you want to copy to your iPhone/iPod, and drag  them to the iPhone/iPod entry;

**[COLOR=DarkOrange][SIZE=3]14.[/SIZE][/COLOR]** Select  your iPhone/iPod from the left column and confirm that the tracks you  added are listed. Click the Synchronize iPhone/iPod button on the  top-right corner of Banshee;

**[COLOR=DarkOrange][SIZE=3]15.[/SIZE][/COLOR]** Review the info in the Synchronize iPhone/iPod window and then click Save Manual Changes;

**[COLOR=DarkOrange][SIZE=3]16.[/SIZE][/COLOR]** The syncing will start, here you must wait untill it finishes;

There you have it, you have succesfully added music from your PC to your iPhone/iPod [IMG]http://img.sythe.org/smilies/smile.gif[/IMG] 


Hope that helps.
