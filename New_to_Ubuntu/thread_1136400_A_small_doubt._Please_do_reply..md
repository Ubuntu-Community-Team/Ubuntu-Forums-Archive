### Post by pbpersson on 2009-04-25
Without a default gateway you can communicate with other machines on your subnet but you cannot get outside your subnet.  Therefore, I vote no, you cannot surf the net without a default gateway.

However, if this is a trick question then yes - I think you could.  If you had a computer with a public and private NIC you could remote into the machine through the private NIC and then get out to the net through the public one - but now I am rambling.
