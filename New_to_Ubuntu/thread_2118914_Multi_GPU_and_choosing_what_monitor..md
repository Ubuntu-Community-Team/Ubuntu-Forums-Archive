### Post by DuckHook on 2013-02-22
I run four monitors, but through only one high-end video card. Multicard support in Ubuntu is very lacking and I doubt you will be able to get it working with all the eye-candy that you may expect. Many reports of workable success with a 2-D desktop like LXDE and Xinerama. Have never tried it. The big hurdle is stitching together multicards with full 3-D like Unity's spinning desktop cubes and translucency.

If you have 7970s, what on earth are you doing still bothering with a 5670? Just hitch all four monitors up to one 7970 and problem solved.
