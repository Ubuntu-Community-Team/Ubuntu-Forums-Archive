### Post by 4Orbs on 2009-05-28
You might only have moved it over to your second Workspace (Desk 2), in which case you can just switch to that second workspace using the Workspace Switcher applet on the panel (the two little side-by-side boxes on the task bar), then drag the application to the right or left as far as possible and it will jump back over to the first workspace.
