### Post by logos34 on 2009-04-27
why not try a regular installation?  Shrink the xp partition down and put ubuntu in the new space. 

With 1 GB of ram you technically don't need a swap, so maybe specify none (for now at least).  You just won't be able to hibernate ubuntu.  If it installs ok, you can try adding a swap afterward.
