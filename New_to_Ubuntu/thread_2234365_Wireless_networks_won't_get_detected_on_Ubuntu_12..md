### Post by coffeecat on 2014-07-14
Welcome to the forum.

Ubuntu 12.10 is now past end of life and is unsupported. You won't be able to install upgrades or the package you need to get that wireless card working. Since this appears to be a new installation, I suggest you simply re-install with the current supported LTS (long term support) version, 14.04. If you need help with that, post back. Please don't think of trying to do an upgrade. Just make a fresh installation.

In the meantime, have a look at the stickies in the wireless and networking sub-forum:
