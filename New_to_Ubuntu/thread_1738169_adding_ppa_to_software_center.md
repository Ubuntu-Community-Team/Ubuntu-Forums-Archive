### Post by ivanovnegro on 2011-04-24
It could if you later want to make the upgrade to Natty. But when you upgrade you could disable the PPAs and later enable after the upgrade or you can also remove before the upgrade the Firefox 4.0 PPA becasue you will have anyway the newer one on the new release.
And if you make a clean install, no worries about the PPAs.
