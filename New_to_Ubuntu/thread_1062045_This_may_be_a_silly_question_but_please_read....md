### Post by oldsoundguy on 2009-02-06
In Ubuntu using Gnome you can utilize both the top and bottom panels for icons/panel items .. and I utilize my top one for quick launch items (done by drag and drop)

Right click on a blank space (in your case you want the trash dump at the top so click in the top)  select "add to panel"  scroll down and slect the items you want and install them.  IF you have a duplicate in the lower bar, a right click and remove will suffice.

You will find you can actually make the panels very functional when you use spacers and lock items to the panel(s)

### Post by leonardo_neo on 2009-02-06
You can have your trash icon on the desktop itself. If you are previous windows user, then this will suit you even better. 

To get the trash icon on desktop

Press alt+f2 --->type gconf-editor --->select apps---> nautilus---> desktop

On the right side check "trash_icon_visible".

You are all set now. 

Hope this helps. :)
