### Post by egalvan on 2009-09-25
Right-click an empty space on the top panel...

choose the "+Add to Panel..." option

scroll down the list until you find "Weather Report"
(or start typing "weather" into the "find an item to add to the panel: option..


highlight it and click the "add" button on the bottom of the window.

close the window...

a 'weather' icon will appear... right-click and choose "preferences"

either find or enter the location you want to monitor.

Note, the "calendar" icon at the top-right corner also has a "weather" option, it's just not as much info...
but it will let you add more locations...
I have it set to show the different cities my family lives in...

Further note... there are un-countable "conky" weather scripts out there...
