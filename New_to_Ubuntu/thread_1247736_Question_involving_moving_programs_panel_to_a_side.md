### Post by mcduck on 2009-08-23
..actually the panels are just fine, it's just the Window List-applet that isn't able to rotate it's contents.. (rotated text is not a problem for the panel, or GTK2, for example the Menu Bar-applet rotates just fine :))

You could replace the Window List-applet with DocBar (or DocBarX) applet, they don't show any text, icons only, which makes them behave better with vertical panels.
