### Post by drs305 on 2010-11-16
> **morgan365 said:**
> Hello just switched to ubuntu hopefully permanently but i use my computer to watch tv online but after 5 minutes or so the screen fades to black the audio is still there when i move my mouse i have to give my password to get it to come back how do i stop this thanks

Sounds like the screensaver, whose default slowly dims the screen. 
System, Preferences, Screensaver. Check the slider "Regard the system as idle after: ..."

Also untick the "Lock screen when screensaver is active" to avoid the password request.
