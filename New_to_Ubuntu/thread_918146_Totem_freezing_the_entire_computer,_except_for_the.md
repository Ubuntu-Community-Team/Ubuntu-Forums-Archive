### Post by Comhra on 2008-09-17
One of the first things that I do when I install Ubuntu is to get rid of Totem.  MPlayer seems like a superior Video player to me.  If you want to try it, go to Add/Remove, select All Available applications, select:Sound & Video in the left-hand Panal.  Search for: MPlayer Movie Player & MPlayer Plugin for Mozilla.  Enjoy the difference & and don't forget to uninstall Totem in Synaptics.
