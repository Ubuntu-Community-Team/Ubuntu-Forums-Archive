### Post by sallam on 2010-12-13
At last I've found out the solution:
use Alt & middle mouse button to resize windows.
With that method, we can resize from anywhere within the left third or right third of any window to resize its width, and we can resize from anywhere within the top third or bottom third of any window to resize its height.

I wonder why is this little insanity-resquer piece of info is being kept in secrecy!
