### Post by skymera on 2008-12-03
Removing Ubuntu-Desktop will only remove that package and no others.

It is a meta-package that when installed, installs all the packages related to it. Such as Gnome games and gnome panel etc.

You can remove Gnome or other packages seperately if you do not wish to have them installed.
