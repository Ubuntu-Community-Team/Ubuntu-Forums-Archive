### Post by jamesrl on 2008-12-09
Assuming you use ubuntu (as opposed to KDE based kubuntu), just go to a panel (e.g. the bar across the edge of the screen) and right click, select add-to-panel, and select workplace-switcher.

Then right click on the workplace switcher that it just added to the panel, go to preferences, and configure as many workspaces as you need.
