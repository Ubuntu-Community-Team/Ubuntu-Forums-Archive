### Post by wilf182 on 2011-06-03
Do you have a home router?

If yes, use an Ethernet cable and connect your computer to it with a wired connection. Then log in, click the Ubuntu sign at the top left. Then type in Additional Drivers. Click on icon, should come up with a Broardcom wireless driver or something similar.

Click on it and then click on the button just above the close button. (don't if it says remove).

should install wireless driver.
