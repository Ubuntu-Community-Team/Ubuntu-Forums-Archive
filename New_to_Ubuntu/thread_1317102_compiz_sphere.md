### Post by gbold on 2009-11-06
I have my desktop set up so that there are 4 workspaces (which gives you a cube, 3 will still give a sphere) and you have to have Cube Reflection and Deformation selected under Effects in CompizConfig Settings Manager.  Select Cube Reflection and Deformation, go to the Deformation Tab and Select Sphere (or Cylinder or NONE).
Greg
