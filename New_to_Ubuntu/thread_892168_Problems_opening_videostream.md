### Post by jualin on 2008-08-17
I don't know italian but I tried playing a Video of the Olympic Games in rai.it and one of the plugins needed is Microsoft Silverlight. If the video you want to play needs that plugin then you cannot play it using Ubuntu, since Silverlight is not compatible with Ubuntu just yet. They are working on it, something called Moonlight.
