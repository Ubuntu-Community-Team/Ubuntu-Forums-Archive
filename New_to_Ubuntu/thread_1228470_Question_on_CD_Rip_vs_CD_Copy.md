### Post by Bucky Ball on 2009-07-31
What you want is sound-juicer. Find it in the repositories. I have it set to boot when I insert a CD and it pops up ready to rip (in a wide variety of formats, including my favourite, FLAC).

Sound-juicer will appear as 'Audio CD Creator' in your menu. :)

Once ripped, you can use Brasero to 'Make Audio CD'. Odd that Brasero won't just copy one. My guess is your Brasero 'temp' file (where it stores the data it has ripped from the CD before it plonks it on the new CD) is maybe too small or full. Have a look.
