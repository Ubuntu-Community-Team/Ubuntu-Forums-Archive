How does one escape out of a man page and get back to the prompt? In other words, I open terminal and type man ifconfig, for instance, and scroll through the information, and it gets to the end. I cannot figure out how to get back to this:
bjj@bclaptop:~$
without completely closing terminal and reopening. And I welcome any ridicule for asking something that probably has a very simple answer

### Post by JoshuaRL on 2009-04-16
Another way to do this is Ctrl+Z.  That will exit most terminal apps.  Useful if something hangs or you need to end it quickly.
