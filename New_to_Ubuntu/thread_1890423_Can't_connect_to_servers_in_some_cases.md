### Post by lechien73 on 2011-12-03
These kind of things can happen when your connection is glitchy. The stylesheets don't seem to be loading properly (or at all).

You can try:

[LIST]
[*]Reloading the page, but hold down the Shift key while clicking reload - this bypasses the cache in Firefox.
[*]In Firefox, go to **View** -> **Page style** and change "No Style" to whatever else is there.
[*]Clear the cache and cookies (**Tools** -> **Clear Recent History**)
[/LIST]

Otherwise, you just have my sympathies. I had to use a satellite internet provider when I was living in the wilds of County Tipperary, and had similar problems.
