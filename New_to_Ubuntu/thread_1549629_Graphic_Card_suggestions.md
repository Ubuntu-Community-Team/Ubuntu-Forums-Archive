Not a problem...

The 100-240V refers to the INPUT voltage for the Power supply unit (PSU) of the computer..

That 100-240V AC is then rectified and regulated to 12volt and/or 5v OUTPUT from the PSU... 

There is NO need to worry, the output voltages are STANDARD across all computer PSUs... The only thing that changes is the power output as measured in watts. Your card needs a PSU capable of supplying 300W. A 300W unit is actually pretty small for a desktop, and I guess you will not have an issue.


If you look at the specs of my machines below you will notice the first machine runs a similar Motherboard with the same VIA chipset, and it's running an apg graphics card which is similar also..really don't worry insert the card into the brown slot, screw it up tight and then alter the bios settings to tell it your now using APG slot, and enjoy.
