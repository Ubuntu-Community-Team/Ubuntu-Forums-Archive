There are no silly questions. Computers are silly because they don't understand us. And its not too much to show CET.

 -Go to System > Administrator > Time and Date
 -Click on Unlock and type you administrator password (this is your normal password if you are an administrator on your computer).
 -Now select a suitable location in Europe (remember, us Brits are not really in Europe).

If you have selected somewhere suitable (e.g Europe/Zurich) the file time in Nautilus should now be CET.
