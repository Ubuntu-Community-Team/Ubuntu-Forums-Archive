### Post by Metaleks on 2008-06-04
If you've never programmed before, I'd start with something simple.  To get you used to the syntax, and the program's flow.

With that said, and since you're using Ubuntu, I'd recommend learning Python before leaping into another language.  But don't just jump into the I-Want-To-Learn-A-Language-For-The-Sake-Of-Learning-It kind of mentality.  Have a goal in mind! ^_^  I also began a programming tutorial series for complete beginners [here](http://metaleks.net/tutorials/python/).  Maybe it'll give you an idea of how to program...without teaching you any heavy concepts just yet.  Keep in mind that I just began it, it's far from being finished.
