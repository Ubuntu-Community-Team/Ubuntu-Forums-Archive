Sure thing!

Open your home folder from Places on the panel.
enable hidden files (either through the file menus or just hit CTRL H)

Look and see if you have a .fonts folder, thats "period+fonts". if you dont- then just make one and put your new fonts there. Then, just log out, and log back in. Youre all set!
