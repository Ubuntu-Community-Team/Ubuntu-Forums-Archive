### Post by prshah on 2008-12-15
> **BastardNamban said:**
> 
If not, is there a way I can make my own emblems?

Yes; use your favorite picture editor, then save the result as a .gif / .png / .svg / .jpg.

Now, open your home folder, and display the sidebar if it is not already present.

Along the top edge of the sidebar, will be a dropdown (currently showing "Places"). Click on that, change it to "Emblems".

Drag and drop your custom emblem into that sidebar; you will be prompted for a title; give a short title.

From now on, whenever you right click an folder and choose "Emblems", your custom emblem will appear in the list.
