### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-06-25
Hi. Regarding the main desktop environments for Ubuntu, the main difference between Gnome (Ubuntu) and KDE (Kubuntu), besides their appearance, is that Gnome favors simplicity and ease of use, while KDE offers more control of the options and settings. XFCE (Xubuntu) is lighter on the resources and is thus better suited for lower end machines, or for users that want more speed out of their systems, at the possible cost of being a little less feature filled.
