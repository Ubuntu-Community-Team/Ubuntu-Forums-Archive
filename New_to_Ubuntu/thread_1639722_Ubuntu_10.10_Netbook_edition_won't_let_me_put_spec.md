### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-12-07
Desktop effects cannot be enabled in netbook-edition (it uses mutter and unity). If you want those effects, you need to logout, click your username, select ubuntu-desktop from sessions in the bottom of screen and login ;-)

That will give you a regular Gnome session though.
