### Post by robafo on 2010-03-01
So I finally got around to installing Karmic on a 6 year old Dell Inspiron 6000 last night. My last experience with Ubuntu was about 5 or 6 years ago on an old Compaq desktop (not sure which version that was, but I think warty warthog rings a bell). I got the old desktop up and running, with quite a bit of work (I vaguely remember having to use ndiswrapper for a wireless card, and never quite getting multimedia to work right). I gave up for a few years, and I was a bit apprehensive making the move to doing this on a laptop, as I figured there are a whole new set of potential problems (internal wireless, dashboard, touchpad, etc.) I just want to say that I am IMPRESSED with the changes made. Installed clean (Ubuntu only), wireless card was detected off the bat (its an Intel pro/wireless 2200), and even the dashboard works!!!! I just ran updates last night (about 225 of them), so we'll see what happens today, I haven't had much chance to play with it. Just want to give a thumbs up to everyone involved!!!! My old laptop runs twice as fast as before, and looks good too!
