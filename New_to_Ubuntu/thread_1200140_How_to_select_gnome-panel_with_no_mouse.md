### Post by beastrace91 on 2009-06-29
alt+f1 is the default to drop down the applications bar, from there use the arrow keys to navigate over to system.

~Jeff

### Post by Sealbhach on 2009-06-29
You could do Alt+F2 then type gnome-mouse-properties

but then the Touchpad settings are on a different tab, you can just arrow right to get to them.

.
