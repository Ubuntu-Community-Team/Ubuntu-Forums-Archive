### Post by appi2012 on 2008-08-15
Google doesn't make google talk for linux. However, you can set up pidgin to use google talk. it is fairly straightforward just open Applications -> Internet -> Pidgin Internet Messenger. It probably will pop up a window telling you to click the "add account" button. Click on it, and choose Google Talk from the drop down list of service providers. Now just enter your username and password. That's all.

Pidgin doesn't show offline buddies by default. If you want it to show them,  go to Buddies -> Show -> Offline buddies.
