### Post by Mahkoe on 2011-12-24
There are two solutions to your problem:
The first one is to look around LibreOffice and seeing if the shortcut is different (different shortcuts in familiar software is a common issue with Ubuntu immigration).
The second one, although I do not recommend it, is to change the keyboard shortcut for workspace switching, by clicking System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts.

Good luck
