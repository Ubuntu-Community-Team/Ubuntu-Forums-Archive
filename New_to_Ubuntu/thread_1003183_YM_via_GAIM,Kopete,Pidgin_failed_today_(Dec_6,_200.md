### Post by free10 on 2008-12-05
At roughly 3PM Pacific today, Yahoo Messenger went down for some accounts. A number of Twitter users have noted the outage -- including the Yahoo Messenger Twitter account which noted just before 3 o'clock that maintenance was being performed:

    Msgr users: we're doing some maintenance starting @ 3pm PT some users may notice some hiccups for several hours. Don't worry it's temporary

It seems odd that the company would perform maintenance at this time of day, unless it was something very urgent. I've reached out to my contacts at Yahoo to find out what's going on and will update when I learn something.

Update 5:08PM Pacific: A Yahoo spokesperson gave me this statement:

    Due to an unplanned power equipment issue at one of Yahoo's third-party co-location facilities, a small portion of Yahoo! services and features may be temporarily unavailable. Our provider is working expeditiously to complete the maintenance, which we expect to last a few hours.

Yahoo told me that most services from the co-lo have been shunted to the company's other facilities. However, the company also noted that most of the users running into issues are running older versions of the Yahoo Messenger client, and that upgrading to Yahoo! Messenger 9.0 would solve the connectivity problems.

Earlier this week, users had problems connecting to Yahoo Mail and Yahoo Messenger during a "service disruption".
