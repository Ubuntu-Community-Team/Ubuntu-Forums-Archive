### Post by st33med on 2009-09-01
Set the screen section of your xorg to look like this.

```
Section "Screen"
        Identifier      "Default Screen"
        Device          "S3 Inc. 86c775/86c785 [Trio 64V2/DX or /GX]"
        Monitor         "HannStar U17"
        DefaultDepth    24
        SubSection "Display"
                Depth           24
                Modes           "1024x768"
        EndSubSection
EndSection
```

That will make the resolution 1024x768.  Hopefully, the monitor can handle that. :)
