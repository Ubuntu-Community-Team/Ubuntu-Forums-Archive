### Post by Merk42 on 2009-08-17
Do you see the windows (usually at the bottom) when they aren't minimized?
You may have just accidentally moved/deleted your window list.  Try moving around the panels, or looking for the separator that defines the beginning of the panels.  You can also just re-add the Window List to any place of any panel by right clicking saying "Add to Panel" then scrolling down to "Window List".
