### Post by EvilRobotDrew on 2008-11-24
If you are anything like me, you will love linux for several months, until you break it, and have to reinstall. I have probably put Ubuntu on my computer 5 times now, but it was by screwing up my installs that i learned the most. Be sure to keep your backups up-to-date, and if you have no experience with *nix, i would keep a minimal XP install dualbootable, just in case you break xorg or do something monumentally stupid (like when i changed all the permissions on "/" to me).

linux is fun and rewarding, remember that google and these fourms are your best friends. the best thing about linux, IMHO is that no matter what problem you have, or what you want to do, some geek on the interwebs usually already thought of it, and put the answer in a blog somewhere.
