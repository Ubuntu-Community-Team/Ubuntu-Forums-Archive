### Post by phidia on 2008-06-04
The bad news is that your printer is unsupported see the openprinting page about [your printer]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Epson-Stylus_Photo_R360"). You can always use the [openprinting database]("http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi") to check the status of any printer you're considering.
You could check out a program called [turboprint]("http://www.turboprint.de/english.html"). 
I don't use turboprint so I can't offer any advice on it.
