### Post by mbuchoff on 2009-06-03
When I plug my mp3 player in, it used to execute "VirtualBox --startvm WindowsXP".  I want to change the command to "VirtualBox --startvm Zune".  Where do I go to change what automatically runs when I plug it in?

---

### Post by durand on 2009-06-04
Open the filemanager. Go to Edit > Preferences and go to the Media tab. Click on Music Player > Custom Command.
