### Post by cornelis spronk on 2010-03-24
I feel stupid now, but I struggled for 2 weeks to get a BBC podcast download into my mp3 player.

When you are asked to click on the green line and then requested to right click and choose "Save target as" I got confused.  There is no "target" to choose from.  Instead, you are given the phrase "Save*link As...".  At this point I gave up.

It turns out that if you choose "Save link as.." a box will appear that shows the file name with an mp3 extension (Ex. xxxxxxx.xxxx.mp3) in a small box.  Below is the line "Save in Folder".

Then scroll through the folders, and make a choice to save this mp3 file into your plugged in mp3 player  (or any other folder in your computer).  Once it is in you mp3 player, you can walk away with it and listen to it anytime.

It works in much the same way as it does in Windows.

I hope this my help someone else.
