### Post by sdennie on 2008-05-29
It will depend on a lot of variables.  The easiest thing to try would be to go to System->Preferences->Appearances->Customize...->Colors.  If that doesn't work for your theme, you'd have to edit it directly.  To change the appearance of the panel, you should just be able to right click on it and choose Properties.
