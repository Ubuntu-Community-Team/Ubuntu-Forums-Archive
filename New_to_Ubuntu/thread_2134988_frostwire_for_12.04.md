### Post by Ender Shadow on 2013-04-12
I would recommend that you don't use Frostwire. That program, like Limewire, is very susceptible to viruses. While it is very difficult to infect Ubuntu directly, a virus can always hijack Frostwire and use it to cause mayhem on your computer. Same thing happens with web browsers.
