### Post by duanedesign on 2010-04-22
If you preserve your /home your themes and icons will be safe. Additionally your Firefox bookmarks and add-ons will also be ok. As for the verbose boot-up it is hard to know what is causing that without knowing more. However a new install will likely not inherit this from your /home.
