### Post by ingrid_ozikem on 2008-08-22
VLC.

 I saw several posts on this forum and I had the problem myself -- CNN Live streams are impossible to watch without recompiling some mplayer plugins and whatnot.. nothing really worked for me. (mplayer -playlist [http://www.cnn.com/video/live/cnnlive_1.asx](http://www.cnn.com/video/live/cnnlive_1.asx) got close to working for me but just one frame would show up and freeze)

But the simple answer turned out to be VLC! God bless VLC!

 Just open Network Stream in VLC under File.. and choose HTTP and enter [http://www.cnn.com/video/live/cnnlive_1.asx](http://www.cnn.com/video/live/cnnlive_1.asx) .. change to live_2,live_3 etc for various live streams they have.

That's it! Works beautifully with audio and everything..
