### Post by fooman on 2009-06-20
try *right-*clicking on the icon,  then choose "properties" from the list.  when the properties box pops up...click on the "basic" tab.

now look on the left side in the properties box and you will see the icon...*left*-click on it and then navigate to the icon you would like to use.
