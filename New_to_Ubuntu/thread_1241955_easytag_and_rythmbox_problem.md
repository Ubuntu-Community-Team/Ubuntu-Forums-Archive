After you edit the tags with EasyTag, goto Rhythmbox and highlight the tracks you edited, click the right mouse button and then remove. Close Rhythmbox, startup again and when it rescans it'll add the tracks with the edited tags.

Easier to always Easytag new tracks before starting Rhythmbox.
