How can I see the IP address of a web site I am visiting? I am using  firefox 3.6.10 on Lucid Lynx

---

### Post by spillin_dylan on 2010-10-04
What I use is the 'Flagfox' extension.  It puts a flag of the country where the page is located, and hovering over the flag will give you the server's IP.  

I'm sure there are other ways of doing this, too, but this is simple and unintrusive.
