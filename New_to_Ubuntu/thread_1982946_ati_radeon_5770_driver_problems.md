### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-05-19
Unless you really need hardware 3D accleration for games, or reduced GPU usage due to overheating, there is really no value to installing the restricted AMD drivers.

The open-source Radeon drivers are much better than they used to be -- I know because I used the restricted drivers to get multiple monitor support and full resolution.  But now, those come with the open-source drivers.

Also, I doubt changing desktops is going to fix the problem.
