No, copying music onto another format is not officially illegal in the UK. Our copyright etc laws are derived from EU law, and that gives us the right to make back-ups of our music etc, and to copy it into other formats so we can, for instance, play our albums on a CD player, on an mp3 player, etc.  Oh, and you wouldn't need to prove it was for personal use - when you're accused of a crime in the UK (and many other countries) you don't have to prove your innocence - the prosecution has to prove your guilt.
