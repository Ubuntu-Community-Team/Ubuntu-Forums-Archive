### Post by zombifier25 on 2012-08-28
The Launcher is the bar to your left which contains running applications and applications pinned to it for quick launching (if you Windows 7, then its taskar is almost identical to it). If you want to pin running apps to the launcher (like desktop shortcuts), open it, right click the icon and choose "Pin to Launcher".

To change its size, right click the desktop, choose "Change Desktop Background", and adjust the slider below.
