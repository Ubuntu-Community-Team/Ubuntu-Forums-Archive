### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-01-18
A concern I have about your "upgrade" is that Hardy Heron included restricted video driver support for lots of ATI cards -- which are no longer supported.  So, if that PC does have one of the (now) unsupported cards, and you do an upgrade, you might very well be faced with a black screen and blinking cursor when you're done.

I strong support Herman's suggestion -- become familiar with what it there now BEFORE you attempt any upgrade.

Once you gain more of a working knowledge of how Ubuntu works and what is installed on the PC, it will be much easier to attempt an upgrade later.
