### Post by White Barry on 2008-11-07
As for aircrack, it can be found in System->Administration->Synaptic Package Manager. Your repositories should be active already but if not go to Settings->Repositories and activate the universe and multiverse ones then on the main screen for Synaptic, click Reload button.

And for your drivers, try google. Thats where I found mine for an atheros card I needed.
