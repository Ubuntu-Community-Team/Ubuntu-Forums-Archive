### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-03-27
Audacity for audio editing, quick and easy (search for/install from Software Centre or Synaptics).

I use Easy Youtube Video Downloader for the other; you can grab the whole clip or just the audio. It is a Firefox plugin and you can find it if you do a search in Firefox>Tools>Add-ons>Get Add-ons.

When you've installed, close and reboot Firefox, go to Youtube and you should see the plug-in at the bottom right of the Youtube clip. Click and you'll get a drop down menu which gives you the download options. ;)
