### Post by gwaar on 2010-06-07
Hmm, searching around, this seems to be a more difficult problem than it should be. As some sort of fix, you can turn off the notification icon in banshee, which also prevents the notifications from showing up. Which is silly, but seems to be the most concrete way of dealing with it. It looks like it's been submitted as a bug for not having separate plugins to deal with notifications and the icon. 

On another note though, those notifications have begun to grow on me. I use rhythmbox and they used to annoy me there as well, but after a while, they begin to melt into the background pretty easily, and still be useful if you want to know a track. Not saying give up on fixing banshee though :) .
