### Post by centaurusa on 2011-10-18
[QUOTE=But, I'm curious. Is there a way to open a terminal using the menu?[/QUOTE]

Click on the top button (Dash Home)  in the left side bar.  In the "Search" box, type t (or term).  The first item will be the icon for Terminal.

Without typing in the program's name, it's a little more difficult (in my view) to navigate through the menu.  You can click on "More Apps", then navigate through "Installed" down to "Terminal".
