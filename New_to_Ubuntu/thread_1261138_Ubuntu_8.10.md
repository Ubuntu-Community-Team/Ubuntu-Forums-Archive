### Post by arochester on 2009-09-08
1) First I would wonder how much RAM memory you have. The Desktop (LiveCD) takes 300+Mb to install. The Alternative disk (straight install) takes 64Mb. If you do not have enough RAM memory you are going to have trouble just running Ubuntu.

2) Could be a bad burn. Look at "Top 20 Kubuntu FAQs & Answers " Question #1 at [http://kubuntuforums.net/forums/index.php?topic=3099811.0](http://kubuntuforums.net/forums/index.php?topic=3099811.0) . Also look at "BurningIsoHowto" on [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)
