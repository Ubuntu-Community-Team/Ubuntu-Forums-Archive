### Post by a.v.l on 2008-05-02
I've had no video in the Opera browser since installing Hardy although mine is not a 64 bit version machine. This is how I resolved my video problem, perhaps it may help.

In Opera I enabled plugins by going to Tools > Preferences > Advanced > Content > "Enable plugins" Then I install Adobe Flash Player from the Synaptic packet manager. Once done Youtube and video elsewhere worked perfectly again.
