### Post by briguy47 on 2009-06-14
Well you can start with CUPS (Common Unix Printing System), foomatic, HPIJS, and openprinting.

You're best bet is to open Synaptic and do a search for "printing".  Then just unmark all the packages you don't think you will need.  Just make sure you read the descriptions and watch the dependencies.  :)
