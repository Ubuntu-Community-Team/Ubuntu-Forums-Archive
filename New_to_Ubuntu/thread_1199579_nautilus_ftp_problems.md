### Post by diablo75 on 2009-06-29
Nautilus works good for most things for me, but I always copy stuff to my local PC before attempting to edit it, and the upload back after I'm done.  And I'm not sure how seriously I could take the "nautilus is not an FTP client" considering when you attach to an FTP resource, it treats it like a mounted drive just like it were another hard drive or flash drive (you get an icon that requires "unmounting" if you want to end your connection to the server).

However, it isn't the best.  Once I had to upload over 6 gigs of AVI files that I shot myself to my server for others to download and use in an editing project, and nautilus would timeout the upload early.  Retry after retry failed.  So I downloaded gftp via Add/Remove and used that instead.  Worked flawlessly.
