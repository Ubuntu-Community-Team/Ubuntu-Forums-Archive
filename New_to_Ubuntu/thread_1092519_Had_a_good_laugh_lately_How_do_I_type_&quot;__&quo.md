### Post by kpatz on 2009-03-10
Looks like you're already typing it... unless you're pasting it. ;)

It's the key above the Enter key, with the \ and the | above it (hold Shift to get the | character).

On some keyboards (laptops especially), the key may be in a different location, but it'll have the same characters on it.
