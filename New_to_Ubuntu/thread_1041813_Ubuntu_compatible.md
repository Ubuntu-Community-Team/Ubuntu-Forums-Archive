### Post by Reliable on 2009-01-17
Yes, but the part I found the most confusing when I first installed Ubuntu was the partitioning section.  The rest of the install almost runs on autopilot.  As **markusf21** suggests above, when you get to the partitioner, you can simply accept the defaults to use Guided Partitioning and your Entire Disk if Ubuntu will be the only system on your hard drive, unless you have a reason to do otherwise.
