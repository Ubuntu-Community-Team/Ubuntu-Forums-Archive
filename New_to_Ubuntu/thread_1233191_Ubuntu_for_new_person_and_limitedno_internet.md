### Post by Paddy Landau on 2009-08-06
Ubuntu will be perfectly fine.

Before giving it to him, ensure that you put in Medibuntu and all the codecs for him. Follow the [Multimedia & Video How-to instructions]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683").

Ensure that his OpenOffice, wireless, email and Firefox are all working.

Ensure that he has a backup procedure in place.

And, of course, do a full update just before he goes.
