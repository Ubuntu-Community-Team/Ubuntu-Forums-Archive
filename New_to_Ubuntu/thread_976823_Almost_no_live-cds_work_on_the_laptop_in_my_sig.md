### Post by Zill on 2008-11-09
> **mike941 said:**
> ...i messed up my grub installing a different distro on another partition. So is this a hardware problem or is it a problem with my dvd drive or what...
If you have managed to install a different distro on another partition then I doubt if it is a hardware problem.  I suggest you try another live CD, making sure the md5 sum is correct and that the disk check runs OK.  If the 64-bit version still has a problem then you could always try the 32-bit version.

If you want a really quick test of your hardware then just download [DSL]("http://www.damnsmalllinux.org/") (50MB) and burn it to a CD.  It may not have *all* the bells and whistles of Ubuntu but it will give you a quick confidence test that your hardware is basically good.
