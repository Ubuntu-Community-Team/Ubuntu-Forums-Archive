I see it.

If you don't mind, you could click on _View_ from the menu and then choose _Web Layout_ instead of _Print Layout_. This is just a workaround and not a solution and you may not like it.

The "solution", again with a cost, is to turn off Tips under Tools, Options: here, expand the LibreOffice tag at the top left of the panel and choose General (below User Data). You get the option to untick **Tips**. That will make the stuff disappear. The downside is that you won't see tips any more :(

BTW, I'm on Ubuntu 11.10 Unity 3D, fully updated.

Edit: you could also edit the tooltip text and background color to make it stand out less.
