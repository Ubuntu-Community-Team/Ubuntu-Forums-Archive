### Post by komputes on 2011-03-01
You should be able to set your shortcuts in System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts.

The shortcuts you won't be able to use are the ones that are (on your other computer) related to action in compiz (compositing window manager - used when effects are enabled).

Can you give some examples of shortcuts that don't work?
