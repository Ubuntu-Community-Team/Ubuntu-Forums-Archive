### Post by kcdiaz on 2013-06-21
AHCI is a simple radio button in the Hard drive settings in the BIOS. If your computer's BIOS doesn't show this option then its ok, don't worry about it. AHCI is supposed to help SSDs perform better. Most computer BIOS can be accessed by pressing F12 or F2 as soon as your computer boots up.

I didn't do any partitioning at all. I just took the default install settings in Ubuntu.
