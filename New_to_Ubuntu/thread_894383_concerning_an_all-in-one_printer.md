### Post by OldTimeTech on 2008-08-19
I went to the link....and according to it, there are no linux drivers for this, this is a windows or Mac capable machine.  Not saying that some of the linux drivers won't work, but you may not get all your capabilities to work.

Secondly...if you look at the back of the machine there is an RJ-11 connection and with most fax machines they need to have a land line....now having said that it doesn't clearly say that in any of the synopsis's that I read....but I don't think the high speed internet access is going to help you fax.
