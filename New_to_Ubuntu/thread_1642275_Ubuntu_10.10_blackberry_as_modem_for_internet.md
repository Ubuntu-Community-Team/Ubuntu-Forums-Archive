### Post by cariboo on 2010-12-10
I don't have a blackberry, but my Samsung instinct works either via usb cable or bluetooth. For bluetooth, all I have to do is pair the phone, and once it's paired Ubuntu asks if I want to use it as a modem. clicking the checkbox, brings up a window to setup your Mobile Broadband Connection, just fill in the blanks and you should be able to connect. 

I don't know where you are located, but I would suggest you check what data transfer prices are, as the last time I used my phone for a modem my carrier charged $1.14 per megabyte.

**Note:** I don't have a data plan with my carrier (Telus) so prices may be different if you have one.
