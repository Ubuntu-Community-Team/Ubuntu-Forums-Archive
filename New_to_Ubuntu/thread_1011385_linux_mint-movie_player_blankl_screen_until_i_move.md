### Post by dwasifar on 2008-12-14
Yeah, your screensaver's activating.  Movie players are supposed to suppress that, but apparently yours isn't.

Try unchecking "Allow screensaver to activate" in Edit>Preferences>Display.  That's supposed to be for audio only, but maybe it'll help you.
