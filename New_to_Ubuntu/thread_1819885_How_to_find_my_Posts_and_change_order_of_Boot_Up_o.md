### Post by jramshu on 2011-08-06
To find you posts, in between 'New Posts' and 'Quick Links' hit search, toward the bottom of the list it has a selection to find your threads and posts.

When grub boots just use the arrow key and scroll down to Windows, or you will have to change your grub configs to put Windows back on top.
