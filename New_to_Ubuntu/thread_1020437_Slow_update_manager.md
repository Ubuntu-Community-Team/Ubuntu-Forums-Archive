### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-12-24
In Synaptic, go to 'Settings->Repositories' and on the first tab (Ubuntu Software) you'll see a drop-down menu ("Download from:"); you can let Synaptic search for a more appropriate one by selecting "Other" in the drop-down menu and then pressing "Select Best Server". Then Synaptic will search for the server closest to your location.

However, since you're already updating the machine, you'd have to abort the current update before changing servers, and it's not guaranteed you'll see a speed increase; the problem might be on your ISP's end.
