### Post by Baldrick_NZ on 2011-05-09
My advise would be to go with 10.04 for now. 

I think that 11.04 is too buggy still, it'll take a good couple of months to sort out the issues, and there is a lot of devices that won't work (or at least 'out of the box') like nVidia drivers and wifi for example.

The best thing about an LTS desktop version is that it's supported for 3 years, unlike the standard versions which are only supported for 18 months, meaning you'll have to update to a new version sooner.

Also, 10.04 is hugely customisable, enabling you to have the ui the way you want it. My ui currently looks exactly like an iMac - it fools many people ;-). 
11.04 (at this time) does not afford you the luxury for fully customising it. 

For me, I'm going to wait until 12.04 before upgrading, they should have sorted all the unity issues by then, and have a primo crack at being back in the game.
