### Post by HighCommander540 on 2008-09-25
For anyone that found this topic below:

[Cannon i9900 printer drivers?]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=368013")

It is true that CUPS doesn't support the i9900 specifically, but cups does offer a driver that works with the i9900 and will print just fine. The only glitch with the driver is that it only prints up to 600x600 DPI.

When you think about it though unless you are going to be using it for ridiculously hardcore quality image printing 600x600 DPI is just fine. If you are going to use it then, you should just invest it what the other topic says to TurboPrint. Will do the trick. It does a completely awesome job of it too.

The driver that you need to use is the: **_BJC-8500_**.

As of right now I am going to try and develop and improve upon the CUPS driver to get a fully functional Cannon i9900 driver working.
