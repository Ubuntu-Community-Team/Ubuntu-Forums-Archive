### Post by CJay554 on 2010-01-31
you can also go to [http://ubuntu-tweak.com/](http://ubuntu-tweak.com/)

installing that gives you great options as how to customize through a simple gui. 

When installed, open it from applications > system tools > ubuntu tweak, and under the "Desktop" section, go to "Desktop Icon Section" then to the very bottom uncheck the "Use Home Folder As Desktop" and log out and back in and that should do the trick!
