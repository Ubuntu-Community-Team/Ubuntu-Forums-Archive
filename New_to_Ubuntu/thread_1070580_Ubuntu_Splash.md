### Post by freesitebuilder on 2009-02-15
System > Admin > Login Window lets you change your login screen, and you can download from places like [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) or [www.art.gnome.org](www.art.gnome.org)

If you want to change your boot screen, here's the howto:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GrubHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GrubHowto)
