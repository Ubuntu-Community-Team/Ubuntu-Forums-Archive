### Post by CRCarl on 2009-03-25
It depends on where you run the games from. If the games are in your home directory he won't be able to access them. If the games were installed to /usr/bin/ or /usr/games/ or anywhere other than your home directory there won't be a problem, he should be able to start them without a problem.
