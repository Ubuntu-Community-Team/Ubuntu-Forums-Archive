### Post by rg4w on 2011-07-14
> **donnagarnet said:**
> I've looked at Linux magazines, but they all seem to be written in "technical" that's often way beyond my knowledge, & I spend twice as much time Googling terms than I do reading articles.
When I started out I had a similar reaction, but the more time I spend here in this forum and reading those mags the more fun they become.

My personal favorite is Linux Journal.  As a long-time Mac user, Kyle Rankin's column on shell scripting was cryptic for me at first.  But intriguing.  So I jumped in started working through the examples he provides, and now I know a lot more than when I started and have had a great time learning along the way.

Ubuntu User is another one I enjoy, more geared for newcomers with less technical info and lots of richly illustrated how-tos on using the GUI.
