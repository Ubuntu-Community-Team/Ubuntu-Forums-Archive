### Post by fancypiper on 2009-11-20
Play around with your configuration and you will fix those two problems.

Configure the mouse speed and sensitivity with the mouse utility and check out the configs in your browser.  There is also System>Preferences>Appearance to check out in Gnome.

Compiz affects some things as well, the desktop switching middle wheel is disabled.  A quick forum search should smooth those things out for you.

BTW, you aren't limited to Gnome as a desktop environment.  I use just a window manager running a gnome panel in it (Fluxbox).  It is much snappier without all the eye candy that desktop environments have.
