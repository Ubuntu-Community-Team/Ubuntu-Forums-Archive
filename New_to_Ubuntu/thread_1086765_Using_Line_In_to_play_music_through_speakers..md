### Post by cj1404 on 2009-03-04
If you cant play music from your 'Line-in' try the following:

Right click the little volume control icon (shaped like a speaker) and select "Open Volume Control", and then unmute the option called 'Line-in' (also look at the preferences to familiarize yourself with it.)

If you do not have the Icon on your menu bar, right click on the menu bar, then click "+Add to Panel" and scroll down to Volume Control click on it then click add button. 

[COLOR="Red"]**Note:**[/COLOR] Audacity's play through gives poor sound quality and should not be used for general playing of music through speakers and is not needed.
