### Post by rcayea on 2011-04-06
I have a lot of ideas, but the first thing that comes to mind for me is to pick a window manager or desktop environment you like. If you aren't sure what these are please let us know. I don't want to insult your intelligence and go off on you about window managers or anything.

You could try various right-click type managers or left-click (like with enlightenment). Try to imagine how you have always wanted your desktop organized, what type of shortcuts, program locations, panel options you have always wanted and go for it. 

Good luck!
