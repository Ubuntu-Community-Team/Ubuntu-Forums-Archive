### Post by lisati on 2009-05-19
The first thing you will need to enable pop access from within Yahoo's webmail. You can log in to Yahoo's web mail, and click on "options". There should be an option for that there which will also provide you with the proper settings to put in at the appropriate places within Evolution.

Note: whether or not Yahoo will let you do this *can* depend on what kind of Yahoo account you have. I have several yahoo.co.nz email addresses, which can be used. Apparently many American-based yahoo.com email addresses can't be used.
