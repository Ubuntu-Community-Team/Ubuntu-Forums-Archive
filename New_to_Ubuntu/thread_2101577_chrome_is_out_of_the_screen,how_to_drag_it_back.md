### Post by DuckHook on 2013-01-05
1. <Alt>+<space> will always invoke the window menu.
2. Then <m> will invoke *move*.
3. *Without* clicking any mouse button, move the mouse. Your window will follow.
4. Clicking the mouse button releases the window where you want it.

<Alt>+click (both held down during the drag) will also always move the window.
