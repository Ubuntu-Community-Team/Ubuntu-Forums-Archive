Actually it is surprisingly easy to put a launcher wherever you want. Just go to System >>> Preferences >>> Main Menu and add a launcher to the Applications >>> Accessories folder (or wherever else you want it for that matter). 

If you aren't sure what to put in the launcher just look at the properties of the launcher on your panel by right clicking over the icon and selecting properties... then just copy/paste to the new one you create.

You might have to click on the icon in the launcher from the panel to find out where to pull the icon for the new menu item. 

Good luck and enjoy.
