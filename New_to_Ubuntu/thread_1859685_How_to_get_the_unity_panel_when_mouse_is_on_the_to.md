### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-10-15
I ran into this issue the other day.

You have the Unity launcher set to show when you move the mouse to the top-left, correct?

This behaviour doesn't work anymore. It's a sadly-removed feature from Unity. You'll need to switch it back to showing the launcher when you move the mouse to the left edge.

Yeah, I'm not happy either, but apparently It Is So. Your other option is to just hold the Super key when you want to look at the launcher.
