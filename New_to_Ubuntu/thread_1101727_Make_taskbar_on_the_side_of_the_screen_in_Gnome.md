### Post by kanikilu on 2009-03-20
For people who don't use it, it might be considered more of a "feature request". For people with widescreens and moving from Windows or OS X where this works as expected, I think it poses a usability problem and is therefore a bug...

Also, considering there's an open bug report in launchpad and has been an open bug in upstream Gnome's Bugzilla since 2002, I'm pretty sure of two things: 1) It's a "bug" by more than one person's definition, and 2) It doesn't seem likely that it's going to be fixed anytime soon :(
