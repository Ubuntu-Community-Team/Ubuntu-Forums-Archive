### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2009-06-11
According to this [thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=465925"), that can be modified by changing the settings of the **Automatic Service Discovery** box in **System** > **Administration** > **Network**.

However, **Network** could not be found in 9.04 menu. [Another thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=591792") says it's in **System** > **Administration** > **Services** then disable the **Muliticast DNS Service Discovery**.
