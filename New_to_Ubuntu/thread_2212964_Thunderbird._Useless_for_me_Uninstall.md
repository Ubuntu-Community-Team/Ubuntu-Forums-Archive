### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-03-24
> **Barkester said:**
> I haven't found much documentation for Thunderbird and what little I've found seems to say that all its good for is making my e-mail a little prettier..

It's an email *client* from the Mozilla Foundation. (What people used before webmail showed up :); and as many still do for lots of reasons.)

Thunderbird has been around for years and a good bit of documentation is available.  Here's one place: [https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/products/thunderbird](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/products/thunderbird).

Like any package, if you don't want it, you can uninstall it. Be sure to inspect the list of other packages that will also be removed.
