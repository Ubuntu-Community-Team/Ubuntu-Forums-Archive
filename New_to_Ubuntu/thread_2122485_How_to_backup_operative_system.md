### Post by HermanAB on 2013-03-05
Howdy,

Making a total system backup is generally a total waste of time - multiple reasons for that.  You should only backup your data.

You could make a backup of your whole home directory including the hidden files that hold the desktop settings, but that also tends to cause more problems than it is worth.
