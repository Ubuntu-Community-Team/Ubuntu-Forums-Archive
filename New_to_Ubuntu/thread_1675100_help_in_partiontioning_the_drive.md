### Post by LewRockwellFAN on 2011-01-25
Yes. But you will have to do it by booting from the live disk unless you have another bootable partition on an installed HD. You select the disk from a little drop down selector near the upper right and then highlight the partition (only one in your case) and then on the partition menu select resize. After that I think you'll see what to do. You want to shrink that partition to whatever size you want and then you can partition the space made available.

P.S., It will be under System, Administration, Gparted.
