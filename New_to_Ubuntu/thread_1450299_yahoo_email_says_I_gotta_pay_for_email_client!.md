### Post by tgalati4 on 2010-04-09
I've used sugarcrm to send email campaigns through gmail to ~500 contacts (sending ~100 per hour), so it works well as a pass-through mail agent as well.

With 8 GB of space for free, you can't beat it.  It handles bounced emails well and a great feature is threaded email.  You can delete entire threads which helps with mail management.  Labels and filters for auto-labeling is a neat feature.  And of course, the best feature is google search, which is superfast through your 8-GB pile of electronic clutter.

One possible downside, google reads all of your email, then pastes small ads in the window frames.

I would love to work at google and read other people's email all day long.
