### Post by M Star on 2013-04-26
There's an easy solution. I made a customer query to QUBEE about this. They called me right then. They sent a technician to my house. He flashed my modem with new drivers. Now I don't need to install any software for QUBEE modem. I just plug it in on any OS and soon the connection is established. The modem is actually made host-less. There's a script in its firmware that connects itself automatically. 

The new drivers may face problem in WindowsXP SP2 or lower. Otherwise it's great!!
