### Post by ibjsb4 on 2012-12-27
Looks like your missing the bottom panel.  

Alt + right click on a blank spot in the top panel And add a new panel.  Then (with Properties) move the panel to the bottom.  Then move your window's list to the bottom.

Or move the entire to the bottom with Ctrl + right click>properties.
