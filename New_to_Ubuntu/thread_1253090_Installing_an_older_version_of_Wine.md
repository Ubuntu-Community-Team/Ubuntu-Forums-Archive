### Post by XCan on 2009-08-29
Do you mean a newer or older version? There's little reason for you to download the .tar.bz2 files, as Wine supply .deb files. Check [http://wine.budgetdedicated.com/archive/index.html](http://wine.budgetdedicated.com/archive/index.html) . Or just follow the instructions at [http://www.winehq.org/download/deb](http://www.winehq.org/download/deb) if you want the newest versino of Wine.
