### Post by jtarin on 2010-07-17
Your python libs are mismatched probably. You should look for a newer version of EasyCam or downgrade your Ubuntu install to match.
Cheese, Camorama, xawtv, VLC, aMSN, and Kopete are in the Ubuntu repositories. They all can be used to test and use your webcam.
