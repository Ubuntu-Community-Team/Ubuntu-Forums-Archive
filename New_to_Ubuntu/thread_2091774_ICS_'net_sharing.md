Best option:Buy a cheap little USB wifi device that works with Linux, for the second computer to connect to your daughter's router.

If the range is too great for this to work, do the following:

1. On the computer that can connect to the internet, go to the Network Manager menu on the top panel. Go to Edit Connection...

2. On the Wired tab, click Add. In the new window that pops up, click the IPv4 tab and in one of the pop-up menus there select Shared To Other Computers.

3. Save your changes and close all those windows. Plug your two computers together with your Ethernet cable and it should start working.
