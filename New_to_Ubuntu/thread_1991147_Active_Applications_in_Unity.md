### Post by deadflowr on 2012-05-30
If you're keyboard centric atl+tab and super+w can be effective enough. 
If your mouse centric, just leftclick on the launcher icon in the launcher dock(left side), it will focus on whatever open app you pick (don't worry it will not open up a new window, only what you already have opened. If you want to open multiple windows for a program, click middle mouse button, to switch between those click the icon and a 3d? window will show all open windows for that program, click on the one you want to use.)
The one thing that bugs be about using the mouse way is even though I can focus on a program by clicking the icon, I can't minimize the program by clicking on the same launcher, like the ole taskmanager of yonder.
Notice open apps have arrows on the left of the launcher, and focused apps have an arrow on the right of the launcher.apps with multiple open windows will have multiple arrows on the left both only a focused window will have one on the right.
