### Post by undecim on 2009-11-13
Either AWN, or gnome-do with the "docky" are my favorites. In my experience, cairo has more features, but it's also less stable.

There's also wbar, which I'm currently using, but that's strictly launchers only.

Really, the best way to decide is to try them all out and see what works for you.
