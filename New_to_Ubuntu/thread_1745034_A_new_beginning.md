### Post by mr_luksom on 2011-05-01
When you do reinstall, if you are up for something a bit for advanced, make a separate partition for /home.

That way, if you ever want to wipe it again, you can still keep your documents and application settings while resetting everything else (applications, system-wide settings, etc).
