### Post by ono2010 on 2010-04-04
hellolife,
I found a workaround by booting up with the VGA cable already connected and the external monitor on. I was able to use 640X480 and 1024X768 resolution.

When I tried to bump the resolution to a higher level than that, the blackout that you describe happened. I suspect the video card in the eeepc is not powerful enough to drive a high resolution monitor.

The 1024 resolution will work for PowerPoint but TV would probably look pretty poor.
