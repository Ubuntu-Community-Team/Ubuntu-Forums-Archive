### Post by Vaphell on 2012-02-21
in preferences of your movie player set sub encoding to windows cyrillic one (windows 1251). Subs are targeted for windows mostly, so windows based encodings are the norm. Most likely your player tries ubuntu's default utf8 and the results are not pretty
