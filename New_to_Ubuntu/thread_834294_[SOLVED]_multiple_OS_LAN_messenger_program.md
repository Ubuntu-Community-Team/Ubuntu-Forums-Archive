### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-06-20
Jabber. Pidgin can connect to a Jabber server, and there's a Finch-based IM program for Mac OS X that can connect to Jabber servers too. And, of course, you can run a Jabber server on a computer on your network and tell the clients to connect to that.

You won't get videoconferencing or voice though - it's just IM, file transfers, and maybe blackboards (not sure about that).
