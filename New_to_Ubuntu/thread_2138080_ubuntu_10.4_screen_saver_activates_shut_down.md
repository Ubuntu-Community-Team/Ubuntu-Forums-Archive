Need far more info than you've provided to make heads or tails of it.

1. What do you mean by "freak Out"? Need specifics.
2. What are your computer specs? Need them in detail.
3. Is it a laptop or desktop?
4. Why are you running 10.4 which will die off in a matter of days?

Screen savers will send a warning if your battery has lost its charging capacity. This is due to the fact that screen saver is tied into power management, and the last thing you want is to count on your suspend feature when your battery can no longer generate the juice to hold your session in RAM.
