### Post by t0p on 2009-12-01
There is a program called [**get_iplayer**]("http://linuxcentre.net/getiplayer") which enables you to download (and keep) BBC programmes that are available through BBC iPlayer - that's TV and radio content.  There is a version of get_iplayer for Ubuntu (.deb format).  More info [here]("http://ihatehate.wordpress.com/2009/07/15/get_iplayer-the-c00l-way-to-download-bbc-shows/").

Incidentally, just because you've paid your licence fee, does *not* make you entitled to download and keep whatever you want.  You're only entitled to download BBC programmes for the purpose of [time-shifting]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_shifting").  Fortunately, get_iplayer allows time-shifting on much more reasonable terms than the BBC's own software.
