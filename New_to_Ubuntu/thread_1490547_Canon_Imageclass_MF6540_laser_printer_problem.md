### Post by PackManT on 2010-07-26
I have had success using the Canon UFR II/UFR II LT Printer Driver found at the Canon Australia site found at the following address:

[http://support-au.canon.com.au/contents/AU/EN/0100093001.html](http://support-au.canon.com.au/contents/AU/EN/0100093001.html)

I also found similar drivers on this Canon Europe site:

[http://software.canon-europe.com/software/]("http://software.canon-europe.com/software/0037431.asp")
complete the online search information by searching for MF6540 in the desired language.
The key to finding the drivers was to search on the "UFR II/UFR II LT" part of the driver name.
