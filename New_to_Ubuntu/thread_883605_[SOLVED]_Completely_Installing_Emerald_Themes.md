### Post by jocko on 2008-08-08
> **kajillin said:**
> emerald is the KDE window decorator, in gnome it will only change your window boarders, for all others u need to use the gnome decorator . As far as i know anyways could be and probly am wrong.

Partially wrong. Emerald is a compiz window decorator, independent from kde or gnome.
Emerald ONLY affects the window decorations, nothing else. For everything else (panels, menus, buttons *etc*.) you need to get gnome (gtk 2) themes. In the description for many emerald themes you may see a suggestion for which gtk 2 theme to use.
