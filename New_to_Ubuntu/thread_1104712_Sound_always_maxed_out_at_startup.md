### Post by FlatBlackIan on 2009-03-24
Im having a similar issue with my Inspiron 9100.  On startup the volume is maxed out.  Temporarily I have all the master volume controls muted, and it seems to hold this.

I also have a related issue, my volume control buttons on my keyboard can adjust volume, sort of, but they do not control the master.  I think I will need to reset their functions some how, I just have not figured it out yet.

**Edit

I did a little searching, and came upon this: [Sound solutions](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=997506)

It walks you through installing the correct audio drivers, and configuring the sound settings.  Once I had everything installed, I locked the Master and PCM volume sliders to my keyboard controls.

The volume is now normalized, I can control it properly with the keyboard, and it holds its settings when the machine is rebooted.
