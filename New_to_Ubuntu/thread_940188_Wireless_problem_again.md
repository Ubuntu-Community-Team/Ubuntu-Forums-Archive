You can try to "Connect to Other Wireless" and type in the ESSID of the wireless network you are trying to connect to (the name that usually shows up), and see if it finds it.

What I find is the most common little mistake some people make is on some laptops there is a switch that shuts off the wireless and some people switch it off without realizing it.
