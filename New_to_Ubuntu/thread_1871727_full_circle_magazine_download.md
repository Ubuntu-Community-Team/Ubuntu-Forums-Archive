### Post by vasa1 on 2011-10-29
You need to check your browser settings. I assume you are visiting the full circle site using a browser.

In Firefox, you can specify the download location by going to Edit, Preferences, General.

When you initiate a download, you'll get a download window showing the progress of the download. That *normally* appears at the top left of the screen. You can set it to close when the download completes.

In Chrome, you can go to Wrench, Preferences, Under the hood, and set your download preferences there.
If you are using Chrome, **not Chromium**, there's a chance the PDF file will open with the built-in PDF file reader.
