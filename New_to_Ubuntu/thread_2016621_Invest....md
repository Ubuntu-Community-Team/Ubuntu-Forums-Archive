Other than the one paid option there is the Unity stock lens. see the link . [http://iloveubuntu.net/easy-guide-ubuntu-1204s-unity-lenses](http://iloveubuntu.net/easy-guide-ubuntu-1204s-unity-lenses)
If you choose to install it open Dash and select the indicator in the screen shot. Enter the stock prefix in dash and a graph will appear. double click the graph and the browser will open to Yahoo Finance. KDE has a plasma widget and Screenlets may offer a widget for Gnome/ Unity
