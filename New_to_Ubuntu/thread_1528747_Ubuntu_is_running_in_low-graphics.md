### Post by cavh on 2010-07-11
Sounds like a hardware issue. If you can open the computer, do so; and try taking the graphics card out and then putting it back in again. Do the same for the memory stick(s), noting carefully to place these items back in exactly the manner they were found - there are tabs on the bottom of the graphics card and the memory sticks which ensure you can insert them in only one manner. Do not force them if they appear to be hard to get back in, just check again and try once more.
