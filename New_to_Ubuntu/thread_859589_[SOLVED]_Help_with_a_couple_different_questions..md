### Post by Hospadar on 2008-07-14
1) 
Yup, go to Edit->Preferences->Views and you can change that

2)
I use devede to convert movies, it just makes a real simple dvd with your video file.  You can put multiple video files in as seperate chapters, but it doesn't do any menues or anything.  It's very easy to use and almost always works just like it should.

If you want something more advanced, probably the best choice would be QdvdAuthor, it will do menues and whatnot, transcoding, as well as burning.

If you want qdvdauthor to do transcoding, you'll also need to install a transcoding backend like transcode or mencoder

All this software is available from the repositories, check synaptic
