### Post by bobbob1016 on 2008-07-13
Not to be rude or insulting, but try searching the forums first.  I've forgotten to search before too, so no big deal, just saying.  I searched for "dvd playback" and got this [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683&highlight=dvd+playback](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683&highlight=dvd+playback)

I read under Part 4, open a terminal (Applications -> Accessories -> Terminal) and type "sudo apt-get install libdvdcss2 libdvdread3 libdvdnav4 vlc" without the quotes then enter your password when asked.

Edit:  You might want to try installing Quickstart [http://quickstart.phpbb.net/viewtopic.php?f=8&t=11](http://quickstart.phpbb.net/viewtopic.php?f=8&t=11)  It does a lot of this stuff for you, as in installing codecs and things, it gives you a prompt, you click "Install DVD and Video Codecs" or something similar, and they are installed.
