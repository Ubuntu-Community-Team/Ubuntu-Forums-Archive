### Post by 5mattyb5 on 2009-11-17
If your goal is to look more like windows than maybe you want to use the "gnomenu" available from

[http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/GnoMenu+-+consolidated+menu+for+gnome?content=93057]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/GnoMenu+-+consolidated+menu+for+gnome?content=93057")

It is quite customizable and can actually look just like windows xp vista or 7 amongst many other great themes.  Actually gnome-look.org is my first stop for anything eyecandy.

After installing it just enable by right clicking on the panel then "add to panel" you'll find it in there.
