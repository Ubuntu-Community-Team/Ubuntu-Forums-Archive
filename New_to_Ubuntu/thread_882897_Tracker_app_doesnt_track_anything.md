### Post by caprilo on 2009-05-05
Tracker does not search files in the moment you tell him to. Tracker continuously makes an index of your filesystem, and searches from the index. While it is creating the index for the first time, no results may be shown. You may have to enable the indexing, and wait until the index is complete to have search results.
