### Post by coffeecat on 2011-08-16
Thumbnails should be set to show by default, so it's odd that you are not seeing them. Anyway, open a Nautilus window. From the Edit menu, choose preferences. Then Preview tab, and have a look at what is set under show thumbnails. "Always" will show thumbnails when you browse shares on another machine - which may slow things down a bit. "Local Files only" is the default.
