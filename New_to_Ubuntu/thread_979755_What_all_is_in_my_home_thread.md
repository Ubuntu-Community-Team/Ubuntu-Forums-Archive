### Post by PierreDeKat on 2008-11-12
From the sound of it, I almost think you'd be better off starting from scratch, including the /home/ directory. 

All your settings will be carried over, and if those settings are messed up now, they'll be messed up in the new installation.

About the only thing you'd want to save would be pictures, documents, stuff like that. And you should pretty much know what all that stuff is.

The rest, just pitch it and start over, hopefully getting things the way you want them the next time.
