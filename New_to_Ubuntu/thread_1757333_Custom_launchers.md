### Post by JDShu on 2011-05-13
1. Right click on the desktop.
2. In the menu that pops up click on "Create Launcher..."
3. In the command field type the command that you want the Launcher to execute.
4. Optionally choose a name, change the icon, or add a comment.
5. Drag the launcher onto the dock on the left.

Theres some fancier stuff you can do, but the above should suffice.
