### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-07-24
If your custom distribution:
[LIST]
[*]A) Uses Apt/Yum/Yast/Portage/any other package manager.
[*]B) Has the correct source list files in place for those managers to work.
[*]C) The kernel is include in at least one of those repositories.
[/LIST]

Then you'll get automatic kernel upgrades.

...Whether or not they will work, is a completely different matter.
