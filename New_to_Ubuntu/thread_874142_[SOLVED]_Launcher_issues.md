### Post by mike2357 on 2008-07-29
System -> Preferences -> Main Menu

On the left-hand panel, click on Games.  If the games you loaded are in the right-hand panel but are unchecked, simply check them and then after you Close, your games should show up under Applications -> Games.

If they don't show up in the right-hand panel, you'll need to click on New Item, and then enter the Name and Command.  If you want, you can enter a comment.  You can customize the icon by clicking on the little icon thingy in the upper left-hand corner of the window in which you enter the command.
