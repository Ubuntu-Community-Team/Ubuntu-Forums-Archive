### Post by coffeeaddict22 on 2009-05-13
Probably not a driver problem.  the default sound setup changed in jaunty, so there's been some problems with the way the system is set up when you upgrade.  
Easy fix which might work: right click on your sound applet up in the top right, open the control, and go through it flicking all the controls up; it was the major issue on one of my machines for video playback in particular.
There's a few sound troubleshooters out there: try [this one]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=205449") (which sits at the top of the multimedia forum when it's at home)
