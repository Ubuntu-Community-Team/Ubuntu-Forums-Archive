### Post by Hospadar on 2009-08-18
I would say step 1)
get familiar with the command line.  aircrack is rarely easy to use, as it is designed by hackers for their own use.  Often it requires kernel patching and recompiling, especially for some of the more advanced attacks (read: faster, more likely to work).  Furthermore, not all wifi cards support all elements of aircrack.

I think you'll have a pretty hard time getting anything to work unless you are very lucky and happen to have a wifi card that is totally supported out of the box, without having some terminal and compiling skills.

Look to google, there is every software building guide and terminal tutorial you could imagine.
