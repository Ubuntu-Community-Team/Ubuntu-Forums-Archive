### Post by Lusse on 2008-12-09
Try using the D F J K keys, on most keyboards you can press all those at the same time, and I actually thinks it's easyer to play stepmania with those, more space for the fingers you know... The reason why you can't use the arrows(or whatever they are called) is that they probaly lies in the same buffer on the keyboard.

In stepmania, go to "options" -> "test input" and see if you can hold down all the keys, that you have choosen, at the same time.
