Hi 28021986,

First off, why did you install Ubuntu 10.04 instead of (the latest version) 10.10? That might fix your problem right away. :)

If you look in the upper-right part of the screen, you can left-click on the little speaker icon. This brings up a screen called "Sound Preferences".
In the tab that says "Output" there is a slider bar for the balance of your sound device. If I slide mine to the right, I will only hear audio from the right side. In the middle everything is normal.

Does this solve your problem?
