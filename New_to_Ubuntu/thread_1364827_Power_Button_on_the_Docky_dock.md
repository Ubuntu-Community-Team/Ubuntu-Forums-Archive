### Post by cszikszoy on 2009-12-27
Sorry, but Docky (as included with GNOME Do) has pretty much reached end of life.  We will be removing it within the next release or so.  I and several others are, however, working on Docky 2.  We have not made a formal release yet, and still consider it "alpha" quality, but it is quite stable.
