### Post by cariboo on 2009-04-26
Open nautilus, Places-->Home Folder and click Edit-->Preferences-->Behaviour, that is where the click preferences are located.

### Post by joshedmonds on 2009-04-26
Go to the **Places** menu in your top panel, select **Home Folder** 

The file manager (Nautilus) will open, and you can change the preferences as per Cariboo907 above.
