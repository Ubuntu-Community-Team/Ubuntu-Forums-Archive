### Post by lswb on 2011-10-30
See if you have "Indicator Applet" installed on the panel. (Right click on panel, select "Add to Panel" select "Indicator Applet," click on Add button) The developers moved the Volume Control to the indicator applet, because of course it makes perfect sense (to them) that you would want to group your volume control together with Network Manager and your email/IM notifications. :)
