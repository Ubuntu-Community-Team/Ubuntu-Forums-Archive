### Post by Hospadar on 2008-06-21
yeah, you can delete all your hidden folders generally.  What I would do though, is just back up the things you want to keep (photos documents music etc) and do a fresh install, and then copy those things back over.

Really though, I wouldn't even worry about it, first off, all those hidden folders are all text files for the most part, and take up almost no space, and won't really interfere with anything.  If you notice a specific program that has some old holdover settings you don't like, just hunt down that folders config files and delete them specifically.  While I think you would be safe deleting everything in your home folder, it could cause some annoyances, and might end up being more trouble than it's worth.
