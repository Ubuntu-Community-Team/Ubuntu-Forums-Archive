### Post by winh8r on 2012-03-08
If you go into Firefox Edit> Preferences > Privacy you can set firefox to "never remember history" or set it to your own custom settings of what to remember and what to delete.

The "clear current history" option will also provide you with an instant way to remove various instances of saved data too.

You can also add and remove search engines from the search box in the top right hand corner of the browser.

Try using a search engine like

[https://startpage.com/]("https://startpage.com/")

to get the benefit of google results without directly accessing google.

hope this helps you.
