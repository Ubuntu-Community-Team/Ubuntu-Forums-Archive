### Post by vasa1 on 2012-07-29
> **uRock said:**

I'm not saying that there's fundamentally anything wrong with installing a KDE app on a computer that has Unity/GNOME or that performance will suffer or that Update Manager cannot handle the KDE dependencies.

But personal situations differ. A person should know or be informed of the consequences in case they're unaware.
For a person with RAM to spare, it's not an issue.
For a person without disk space constraints, it's not an issue.
For a person without bandwidth constraints, it's not an issue.

A nice, but not conclusive, read is here: [http://ingwa2.blogspot.in/2012/05/overhead-of-kde-software.html](http://ingwa2.blogspot.in/2012/05/overhead-of-kde-software.html)
