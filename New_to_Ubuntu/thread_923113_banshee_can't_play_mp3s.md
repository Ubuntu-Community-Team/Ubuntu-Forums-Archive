### Post by adult swim on 2008-09-18
try this, go to applications>accessories>terminal, and type in ```
sudo apt-get install ubuntu-restricted-extras
```it will ask you for your password, type that in and hit enter.  (you may need to restart) now open banshee and try to play an mp3.
