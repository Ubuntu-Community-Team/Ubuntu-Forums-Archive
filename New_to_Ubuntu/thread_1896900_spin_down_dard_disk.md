### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-19
From your settings, it looks like you disabled suspend and sleep -- which is where spinning down hard drives actually helps.

As long as you are using the laptop actively, the hard drive will not spin down.

So basically, you are preventing it from working.

As to 2.5" vs 3.5", laptops use 2.5" drives.
