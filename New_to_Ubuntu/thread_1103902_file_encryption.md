### Post by kevdog on 2009-03-23
There is no "best" way -- just what meets your needs best.  Both gnupg and truecrypt are options.  GnuPG offers both asymmetric and symmetric encryption.  I believe Truecrypt offers only symmetric encryption.  There are other methods also, however without telling us specifically what you want to do with your files, its difficult to recommend one particular method.
