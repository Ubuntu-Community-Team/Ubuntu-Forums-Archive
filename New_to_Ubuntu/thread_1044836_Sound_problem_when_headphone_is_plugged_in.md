I have a strange problem with the sound in Ubuntu 8.10. When I plug in my headphones the sound comes out of both the headphones AND the laptop speakers. The laptop speakers work fine when the headphones are not plugged.

My sound card is SoundMAX Integrated Digital Audio. That should be the standard card that comes with my laptop.

Any suggestions? I've attached a screen shot of the current Sound Preferences.

---

### Post by h2o4444 on 2009-03-26
I just found the solution to this problem:

Double click the Volume Control on the top right of the Gnome desktop (if this is for Ubuntu). Then click on Switches, check the box for "Headphone Jack Sense".

This solved my problem. I think Ubuntu needs to do this by default so that no one thinks this is a glitch.
