### Post by bark50 on 2009-09-05
Problem solved thanks to Soulcage pointing out the notification area applet.  In my haste to try out his suggestion, I at first added several notification area applets to both my top and bottom panels, and this apparently got things “confused” as to where the icons for the running applications should go.  I could not see any of the notification area applets because of my dark color theme.  I temporarily changed to a lighter theme so all those notification area icons were visible, deleted the superfluous ones, rebooted, and I'm back in business.

Thanks, Soulcage!
