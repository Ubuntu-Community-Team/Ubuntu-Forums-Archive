### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-03-19
+1 for HP.

I recently picked up a new DeskJet 2510 all-in-one for £25, plugged it in and everything worked out of the box. All of the required drivers were already built in to Ubuntu.

For a run down on which specific printers are supported, check out the HP Linux website...
[http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html)

For other manufacturers either check their websites, or there is a database at openprinting.org that lists the printers supported by cups.
[http://www.openprinting.org/printers](http://www.openprinting.org/printers)
