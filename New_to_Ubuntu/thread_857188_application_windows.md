### Post by mcduck on 2008-07-12
Do you have the desktop effects enabled? Install Compizconfig Settings Manager (which allows you to configure deskktop effects settings) and inder general settings there's option for "Focus Stealing Prevention". Change it's level to "low" or turn it off completely.
