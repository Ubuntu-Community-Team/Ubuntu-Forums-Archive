### Post by pricetech on 2010-12-02
Prolly need more information, but I can tell you the steps I follow.

I have the adapter plugged in and bluetooth installed via package manager.

I set my phone to be discoverable, then click on the bluetooth icon and select Add New Device (I thin that's right, but I'm in front of that computer)  It finds my phone.  Then I either browse the device if I want pictures that are on the phone, or I can push them from the phone.  If I'm putting stuff from the computer onto the phone I click the bluetooth icon and choose Send to Device (again I'm not in front of that computer) choose the file, then the device.

I may have missed some details, but yes it is that simple.
