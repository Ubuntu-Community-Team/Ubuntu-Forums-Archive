### Post by whitethorn on 2008-07-23
I just had a quick look, if you're using firefox 3, go to Preferences -> Applications -> Web Feed 

Click on it once then on the right side you can choose a program.  Hopefully this works, I use Liferea as my RSS Reader, and it was a bit of a pain telling Firefox to use it instead of the built in Reader.

Well that is easy ... 

i Will assume that you have set up an Rss Section in thunderbird.

So click on the small Rss Button it may take you to 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/external.php?type=RSS2](http://ubuntuforums.org/external.php?type=RSS2)
that page.
If it wants to take you to google reader then register it there, and go to google reader.
CLick on Manage Subscriptions and find the Rss that was send .IT could be the same like above or something similar.

Copy that one , open Thunderbird, click on your Rss section -> Subscribe and then paste the link inside there//
That is it ... :) :KS
