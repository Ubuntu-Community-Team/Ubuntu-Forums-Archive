### Post by Impavidus on 2021-08-18
This is the right place for people who attempt their first Ubuntu install.

Direct downloads are sometimes corrupt, although that rarely happens if you've a reliable connection. Torrent downloads are checked during download, so they are never corrupt (unless you hard drive is broken, which can corrupt anything). Have you downloaded the .iso from the official site, ubuntu.com? Then it should be right.

Ubuntu 21.04 will only be supported until January 2022, so you'll have to upgrade to 21.10 before then. We usually recommend new users to stick to the LTS releases, which get 5 years of support and are released in April in even years. The current LTS release is 20.04.

Maybe these links are helpful:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick)
[https://ubuntu.com/tutorials/create-a-usb-stick-on-windows#1-overview](https://ubuntu.com/tutorials/create-a-usb-stick-on-windows#1-overview)
