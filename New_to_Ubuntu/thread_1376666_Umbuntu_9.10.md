### Post by sonicx059 on 2010-01-09
Well the link Hellokitty provided works. 
The steps follows as:

1) hold shift
2) go to recovery console
3) type passwd {username} - you will be prompted to enter password
4) enter new password

There are many other ways but this seems the simplest I seen thus far.
