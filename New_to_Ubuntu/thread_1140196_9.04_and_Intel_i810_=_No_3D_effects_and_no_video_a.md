### Post by atulai on 2009-04-29
Okay so I got mine enabled although I don't know if that was a good idea.

Apparently there is a bug w/mesa that was found so this driver was blacklisted.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/359392](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/359392)

I removed the driver from the blacklist and I could enable compiz.

/usr/bin/compiz

Found solution here:
[http://www.reddit.com/r/Ubuntu/comments/8eptw/blacklist_drivers_on_ubuntu_904/](http://www.reddit.com/r/Ubuntu/comments/8eptw/blacklist_drivers_on_ubuntu_904/)

Seems to be working okay for me at the moment.
