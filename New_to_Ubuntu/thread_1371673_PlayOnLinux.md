### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-01-03
The program is not "rubbish" but it suffers from the limitations of what will not work well with Wine.

Before you criticize the program, you should go to the WineHQ application compatibility site and look up the Windows apps/games you're trying to install.  If they're not listed, or if their rating is Garbage or Bronze, you're most probably wasting your time.

Link below:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true")
