### Post by coolbrook on 2009-03-20
[See "Installation instructions for tar.gz"](http://www.adobe.com/products/flashplayer/productinfo/instructions/#section-3)

In terminal
```

cd Desktop
tar xfvz install_flash_player_10_linux.tar.gz
./flashplayer-installer

```

You can also (more easily) download and just double click the .deb from Adobe.
