### Post by Mongo5000 on 2008-09-11
Quantra is more of a code based editor. It will give you a gui output as you code, but can't edit in the gui from what I remember.

Your best choices are Kompozer or Dreamweaver in Wine.  CS3 will run, but it will take some tweaking.  Kompozer is good, but im too used to dreamweaver and I just couldn't get into it.

What I do is use VMware and install XP.  Then use that to run Dreamweaver CS3 and Photoshop.  Works well for me.  Dreamweaver in wine didn't work out well.
