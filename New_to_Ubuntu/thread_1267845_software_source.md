### Post by drs305 on 2009-09-16
This can be done from the Synaptic Settings, Repositories, Third Party or Other Sources (Karmic) tab. Click on the Add Source tab. 

Take a look at Section 4 of the Ubuntu wiki Repository page:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu")

Look at the format for an NFS entry. I believe this will work.
