### Post by Curtis6767 on 2012-05-08
Have you tried clicking on the transparent icons at the bottom of Dash.

On my system, when I open Dash, it defaults to the programs I've used last. But If I click on the icon on the bottom that is 2nd from the left, I get another lens, as they're called, and in that one I get a list of apps.

In the top right hand corner look for Filter Results. Click on that and you'll get a list of available subject searches. If you click on Games, your games as well as other games should pop up.

Hope this answers your question.
