### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-06-04
I presume you have already a TV card installed and working right? If you have the TV card, but need a program to setup and use it, then I recommend TVTime.

If TVTime works for you then you could try my [media center extension for Firefox](http://fmc.isgreat.org/). This extension is relatively simple and allow you to record TV among other things.

MythTV is a much more powerful media center, best suited for those who wants to setup a server for recording TV and distribute videos and live TV to other computers in your network or TV extension hardware.

There are also simpler options, like [creating scripts for TV recording](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=921233).
