### Post by SeijiSensei on 2013-01-26
1)  Hold down the shift key when you press delete.

2)  In Dolphin, click on the little wrench icon and pick Configure Dolphin, then navigate to General > Context Menu and check the box to add the Delete option to that menu.  When you right-click an entry or set of entries, you'll have the option to choose either Trash or Delete.
