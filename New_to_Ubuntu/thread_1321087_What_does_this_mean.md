Well I can speak from personal experience with nVidia cards it's like this:
Display (System > Preferences > Display) uses one way of changing resolution and color settings, where as propriety nVidia drivers use a difference incompatible way (System > Administration > Nvidia X Server Settings)

So basically two different ways to do the same thing.
