### Post by rosencrantz on 2011-04-27
If you are using a software synthesiser like most of us do, it depends on the sound fonts you have installed  and not on the machine. If the e.g. font for bass guitar is not installed, you will not hear the bass line.
This guide should help you:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Midi/SoftwareSynthesisHowTo](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Midi/SoftwareSynthesisHowTo)
