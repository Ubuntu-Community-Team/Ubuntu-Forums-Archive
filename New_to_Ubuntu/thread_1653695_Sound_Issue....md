### Post by VonSpyder on 2010-12-28
> **Linyx said:**
> [SIZE=2]You means to say that i Get another Card....?[/SIZE]

Buy a cheap pci soundcard (like a soundblaster, so you get good support) slap it into a pci slot, then disable the realtek in bios and buy some PC speakers. This way you can plug your headphones into the speakers or unplug them and have real speaker sound at your disacreation. New soundblaster card on the cheap end should cost you more than 20$ (or 35 Euro whichever you use) and new PC Speakers will run you 5-10$ or 10-20 Euros. Make sure you have a free pci slot before you do that.
