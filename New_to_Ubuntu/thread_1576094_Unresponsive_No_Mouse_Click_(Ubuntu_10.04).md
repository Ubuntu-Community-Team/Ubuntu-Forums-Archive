### Post by charlie_b on 2010-12-19
I occasionally have a similar problem to in 10.04. The mouse will suddenly freeze up, but the keyboard still works okay. As near as I can tell, it is most likely to happen using processor intensive programs such as video editing software, and possibly happens when the mouse flicks quickly across two or more controls such as buttons or sliders on the application gui.
It's not a major problem - one has to of course back up one's work regularly. It is a minor annoyance.

### Post by garolou on 2011-03-22
I may have found the culprit. I use a wireless mouse with an emitter/receiver plugged in a usb port. By removing and plugging the e/r in another usb port -- but not the same one -- I was able to get control again when things started to go haywire.
I'll confirm if this is really the case when things go crazy again.
