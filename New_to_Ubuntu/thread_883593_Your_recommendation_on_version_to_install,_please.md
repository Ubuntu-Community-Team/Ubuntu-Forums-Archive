### Post by Riffer on 2008-08-08
Edubuntu is good and should fairly well on your computer.  Having said that, you need to be aware that Edubuntu is designed for Teachers to use in the classroom.  It may not be what you want/need for the home setting.

If you decide on a regular desktop, Xubuntu will work really well on your computer.  Since it uses Gnome as the desktop environment, there are some  HOWTO's and programs that you can use to restrict your son's abilities to make changes on the computer.

For internet monitoring theres Dansguardian found in the repos.
