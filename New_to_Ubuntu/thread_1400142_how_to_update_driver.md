### Post by Temposs on 2010-02-06
> **Erik M said:**
> Dang. And how was Linux going to beat the other two (three) did you say?

Neither Linux nor Ubuntu has anything to do with how nVidia drivers downloaded from nVidia are installed . nVidia has chosen to keep their drivers and specs closed source, and only nVidia has the power to release drivers for their product. They could release a nicer installer for their newest drivers if they wanted, but they don't.

If you notice, Ubuntu has a super nice driver installer in System->Administration->Hardware Drivers, which gives you the Ubuntu-approved driver. 

But, if you want the bleeding edge drivers direct from nVidia, you have to get their crappy terminal-based installer.

Please don't place blame where it doesn't belong.
