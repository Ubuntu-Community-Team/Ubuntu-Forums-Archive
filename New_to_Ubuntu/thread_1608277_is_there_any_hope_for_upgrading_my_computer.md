### Post by PhilGil on 2010-10-28
> **sandyd said:**
> Things you can do
- add new graphics card (pci/agp)
- add RAM
Agreed, lots of P4's still doing useful work out there, my daily driver at the office is a P4 with 1GB of RAM. Be sure you get the right RAM and video card, none of the new stuff will be compatible. As sandyd said, you'll need a PCI or AGP video card. RAM could be either SDRAM or *DDR* SDRAM, you'll have to check your system or motherboard documentation to find out which.

If you don't feel like spending any money on the machine, use it as a test bed for one or more really lightweight distros. Puppy or Lubuntu come to mind, and there's many more.
