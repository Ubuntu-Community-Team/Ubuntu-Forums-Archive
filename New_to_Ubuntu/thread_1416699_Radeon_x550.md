If you're running an Ubuntu newer than 8.04, this slowness is likely due to no ATI drivers being available for your card.  So no, there is really nothing you can do to improve this.

> So my question is this...
Would I be better off getting a new more advanced card and power supply or would it be feasible to get two "cheaper" cards and SLI them

Thanks

As far as I know, SLI is only really useful in 3D gaming -- and then, you face the problems with running MS Windows games using Wine or something similar.

If all you want is higher resolution or faster video performance, a single, relatively new video card will suffice.
