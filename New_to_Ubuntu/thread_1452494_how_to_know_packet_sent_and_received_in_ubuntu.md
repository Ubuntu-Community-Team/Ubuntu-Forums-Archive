### Post by Drenriza on 2010-04-12
Go system - administration - systemmonitor

click on resources. And in the bottom you can see
networkhistory

With send and received package information.

NOTE: i have my ubuntu in danish and the words on your machine can differ abit. But its in the same place.

Hope it helps, kind regards.:KS
