### Post by Volt9000 on 2009-04-26
Just go to a site that requires Flash and you'll get the notification at the top saying you need a plugin. Just click there and select the non-free Adobe plugin, then restart Firefox. That should work.

On a side note, is this Flash, or Shockwave? They are two different things, and apparently there is currently no Linux support for the latter.
