### Post by 4Orbs on 2008-08-20
This is not a solution, but I have experienced some rips that were out of sync when played on Totem or VLC, but not out of sync when played with gXine or mPlayer. Nowadays when encoding I always encode the audio to CBR rather than VBR. I believe variable bitrate audio is the culprit when things are out of sync. I could be very wrong in my assumption.
