### Post by chrisod on 2009-06-06
1. Try previewing the scan first and making sure the entire document is selected before scanning. I've found the logic that automatically minimizes white space in Xsane can be a little flakey at times.

2. Make sure you are scanning at at a high enough resolution. 150 dpi for line art and 300 ddi for graphics is usually good enough without making the file size too large.
