### Post by WanderingAlbatross on 2011-10-23
I have a real nice setup for this.  I got tired of making disks, so I began to use an external drive and a neat little program.  It is located [here]("http://liveusb.info/dotclear/").  I once tried setting up a single drive with 7 OS on it, but they wanted to compete with each other, and there was always one needing updated.

This program allows you to put multiple iso on one drive and boot from them.  Most of the common distros work on it.  I use it to introduce people to linux by booting it on their computer and letting them see which distro might suit them best.
