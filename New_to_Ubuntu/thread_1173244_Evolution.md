### Post by Johan-Steyn on 2009-05-29
What email client do you use Evolution or Thunderbird?

If it is not Evolution, you can set Thunderbird or another mail server as default client by accessing:

System>Preference>Preferred Applications and select your installed (preferred) mail reader from the drop-down menu.

When you access email from the shell (i.e. send to:) it will list your selected mail client and not Evolution (default).
