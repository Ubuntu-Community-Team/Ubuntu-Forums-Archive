### Post by ZomboidHell on 2013-06-20
Assuming you have already downloaded and burned your installation disk
    1st set your Bios to boot from the cd first. you should see a press <escape> or <f2> to bring you into the bios setup depending on what computer /bios you have. when you boot.
     then boot
     it's pretty much self explanatory after that, just follow the prompts.
if it does not boot into the installation you may have burned the cd/dvd wrong and not made the disk bootable.
