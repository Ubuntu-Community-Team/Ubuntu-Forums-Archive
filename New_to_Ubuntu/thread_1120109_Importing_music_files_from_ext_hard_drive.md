### Post by durand on 2009-04-08
Go to Places and click on the hard drive. Copy the songs over to your computer. You can just copy them to your home folder. Then go to Rhythmbox > Music > Import Folder and find the folder and that should work.

### Post by aysiu on 2009-04-08
Rhythmbox (and most Linux music players) manage songs by monitoring a directory and looking for newly added songs.

So in your Rhythmbox preferences, select a folder in your home folder to monitor, and then copy the songs from your external hard drive to that folder.
