### Post by mc4man on 2009-08-29
Maybe not what you have in mind but....

Using only** one launcher in dock**

Open firefox from launcher, then for more instances 
on laptop -  put cursor on launcher and press between left and right buttons (ie. press them both at same time

on desktop with scroll wheel mouse - with cursor on launcher press scroll wheel (if no scroll wheel then middle mouse button, or if no middle then l&r at once

The reason " Maybe not..." each newly opened instance will create a new firefox icon, (no way around that), but they only exist while that instance is open

You can middle click on any of the icons (instances) to open new instances
(( may crash firefox the first time doing, should be fine after that
