### Post by vegetarianshrimp on 2008-12-25
Try this:

1. go back to one panel.
2. right click
3. Add to Panel
4. Seperator -- Add
5. Close
6. Right click on the new seprator
7. Move 
8. Move to the middle of the panel

This is still one panel, but you could pretend its two.
