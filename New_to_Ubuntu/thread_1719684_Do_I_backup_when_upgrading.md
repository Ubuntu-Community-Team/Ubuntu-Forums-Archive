### Post by HermanAB on 2011-04-02
Well, if you read these forums for a while, you will notice that a large number of problems are caused by updates and upgrades. 

If all your data resides in /home AND /home is a separate partition, then you can take your chances and re-install WITHOUT formatting /home, but bear in mind that there are only 10 kinds of computer users: Those who have lost data and those who will.

---

### Post by HermanAB on 2011-04-02
This why you must always install a system with a separate /home partition.  You can then re-install without formatting /home.
