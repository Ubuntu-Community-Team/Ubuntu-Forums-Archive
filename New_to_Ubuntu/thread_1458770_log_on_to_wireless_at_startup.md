### Post by chrisxuk on 2010-04-20
Yeah you need to make sure a box is ticked :P

**_To find this box:_**
[LIST=1]
[*]Right click on your signal indicator icon on the top toolbar and a drop down window should appear.
[*]Click "Edit connections..." and a window should appear.
[*]Click the wireless tab, and the screen should change and so your wireless connection.
[*]Click once on your connection, and press the "Edit" button. A window should appear asking if you can "Allow application access to keyring", click Allow once.
[*]A new window should appear. It'll display your Connection name just at the top and there should be box under that called "Connect automatically", make sure that this box is ticked and press apply.
[*]A window might appear asking for you to "Allow application access to keyring" again. Like before, just click "Allow once" and you should be left with the original window.
[*]If your connection is going to connect automatically now, you should notice that the word "Auto" has been added before your connection name. Click close, and you're done!
[/LIST]
