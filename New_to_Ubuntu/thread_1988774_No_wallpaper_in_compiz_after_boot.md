Problem: 
After a reboot there is no desktop wallpaper anymore.

How comes:
I wanted to have a diffrent wallpapers for each viewport.
I installed Compiz Config Manager and all connected stuff. 
Works perfectly when I enter all information in compiz config managers plugin "wallpaper", but after a reboot its all gone (but the settings didn't change in the wallpaper plugin)

So I have to go after every reboot to the  compiz config managers plugin "wallpaper" change something back and forth and then it works again. 
The plugin is active and remembers the settings from last time but after a reboot there is no wallpaper until you change one of the attributes of any wallpaper (i.e. centered to tiled and back) and then all wallpapers suddenly pop up and work correctly.
