### Post by swoody on 2009-05-04
Hi doctorphoto, and Welcome to Ubuntu and the Forums ):P

It would be of great use to anyone trying to help you out here if you could post some more info about your system. Is it a wireless connection, ethernet, other? Do you know what type of adapter you have? Manufacturer, model name/number, etc? Also, is there any encryption on the network you are trying to connect to - WEP, WPA, WPA2?

If you're connecting wirelessly, the first thing to check would be to see if there's a driver available for your hardware. Simply click on 'System'>'Administration'>'Hardware Drivers' and see if there's a driver for your wireless card available in there. If there is, you just have to select it to activate it, and then it'll say you need to restart your computer for the driver to take effect. So simply restart your computer, and the driver will work.

Please fill us in a little bit more about your system. Any info you can provide us will be of great help for us to be able to get you rolling :D
