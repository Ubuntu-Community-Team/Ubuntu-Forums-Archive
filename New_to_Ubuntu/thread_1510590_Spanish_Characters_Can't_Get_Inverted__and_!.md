### Post by gsocker on 2010-06-15
Assuming the right alt key is set up as the "compose" key, according to Google "!!" will give you the inverted exclamation point. "??" will do the inverted question mark.
Hold down Compose + shift + press the key twice. 
Just checked and it works fine on my system. 
I don't have an international keyboard, but I do have a key set as the compose key. It should be whichever key you are pressing to add the accents.

Link with listing of all the key sequences:
[http://www.hermit.org/Linux/ComposeKeys.html]("http://www.hermit.org/Linux/ComposeKeys.html")
