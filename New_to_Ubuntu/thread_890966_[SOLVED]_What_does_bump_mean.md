### Post by finer recliner on 2008-08-15
old threads get "buried" by new threads when nobody responds to them. when threads are replied to, they show up at the top of the list of threads in that sub-forum and are more likely to be seen.

when someone thinks their thread is "buried" and won't be seen (but still needs help), they reply to their own thread to make it jump to the top of the list. the word "bump" is meaningless outside of letting others know that they just want their thread to be seen again.
