### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2008-05-02
With just 512ram I think you need a swap partition. 
Swap is something like a pseudo-ram that the system uses whenever things get tight in terms of resources.
So, yeah, 1.4gb sound quite reasonable for your case.
