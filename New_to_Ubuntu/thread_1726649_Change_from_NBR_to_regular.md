### Post by 5149.5 on 2011-04-11
It sounds like it's really the Unity DE that you don't like and you would like the desktop without the panel on the left and the top and bottom panels instead. 

To do that you can logoff and then when you click your user to log back in, look at he bottom panel. There is a place to select your desktop session. Click that and choose Ubuntu classic instead of Unity. Put in your password to finish login. 

After the desktop loads, Right click the top panel and add the menus, I can't remember what they're called but you'll figure it out.
