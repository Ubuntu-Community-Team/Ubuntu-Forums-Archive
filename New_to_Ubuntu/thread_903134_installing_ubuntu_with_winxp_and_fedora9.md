### Post by Blue_Lander on 2008-08-28
In most cases Ubuntu's graphical installer will see the other partitions and allow you to simply install alongside them. If you need to resize one of your other partitions, its best to resize fedora. If you resize XP you have to make sure your hard drive has NO fragments, otherwise it may overwrite files.
