### Post by kestrel1 on 2009-02-06
If your PSU had a problem, it is possible that the correct voltage was not getting to your CPU via the motherboard & making your machine respond slowly when attempting to run Gimp. Not seen this sort of behaviour with a CPU being affected, but the PSU can cause many different problems if it is faulty.
As you have replaced your PSU & Gimp now runs, this seems like a possibility.
