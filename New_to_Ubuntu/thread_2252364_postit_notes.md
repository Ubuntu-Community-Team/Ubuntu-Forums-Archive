### Post by amanchesterman on 2014-11-11
I use Xubuntu and there is a plugin called 'Notes' which sits on the top panel bar and does most of what you want: the notes you create are sticky, they re-appear on the desktop when you re-start the computer, and they can be hidden. I'm not sure whether you can make them transparent but you can certainly change the background colour. Try running Xubuntu from a live disk or USB stick (i.e. don't actually install it on your machine) and see if it meets your needs.
