### Post by mcduck on 2010-08-01
I can't really say how closing a process would permanently remove the Window List-applet from your panel, but anyway getting it back is really easy:

Just right-click on the panel, select "Add to Panel", find the "Window List"-applet and drag&drop it into the panel.
