### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-11-07

One thing to keep in mind is available memory. If your low (system and/or video) on memory or use the HDD for significant caching, I think you'll find little improvement when opening multiple tabs.

### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-11-08
chazn85

Three more extensions that might help speed up performance are Fasterfox, Adblock Plus and NoScript. Adblock Plus and NoScript have significant impact of both page ads and unnecessary script. Fasterfox in turbo mode will help pipelining without going into the about:config.

Of course you may wish to look at better privacy if your getting a lot flash cookies. Since I've installed it's blocked or deleted over 5000 flash cookies. Most are probably from the same sites that continue trying to load them.

Anyway, good luck and hope your happy with the improvement.
