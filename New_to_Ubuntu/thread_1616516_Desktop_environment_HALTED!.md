I actually was not able to diagnose any problems when I ran the script. What worked was after I reinstalled the OS from the USB, it goes to a prompt that prompts you to restart. I only noticed this after several attempts, but a series of settings or something looks like they are being saved. Upon my last installation, I waited until the computer powered off to to restart to remove the USB so that it would boot from my computer's hard drive; I now can log into desktop environment normally.
