### Post by swarup on 2008-06-24
I agree wholeheartedly with linuxlizard.

I myself have an older laptop, and have spent a treat deal of time researching the matter of a smaller, faster distro. I installed several and tested them extensively. 

This included Fluxbuntu, which I found to be a mess on several counts. Perhaps the main problem behind all of it is that they have never been able to come out with a final version of it. It just remains as a release version, and works like something incomplete too. A work in progress. I'd let them finish it before messing with it any more.

I also tested Puppy linux, Tinyflux (based on pcfluxboxos), and antiX. I found all three to be EXCELLENT. Very fast, very dependable.

I ended up settling on Puppy linux myself, because it has been around much longer than the others, has by far the largest user base, and the most active support forum. The support forum is as active as Ubuntu's in my experience. Any problem you have has been faced by someone else already who is ready to tell you the solution. The OS is also smaller than the other two I tested, and really really fast. I think it is the fasted of the ones I tested. And the final clincher is that it has much better international and multilingual support than the others. 

I did also like both Tinyflux and AntiX. Both have very dedicated staff (on their support forums) who are ready to help you with anything you need. Of the two, Tinyflux in particular I found to be extremely smooth and polished. It has a control center which makes all your initial setup such as wireless etc into a piece of cake.

I didn't try DSL (DamnSmallLinux) because I read that it is so small that it can't provide the breadth of tools, programs, conveniences, of the others. 

I'd recommend Puppy Linux first for the reasons mentioned above, although Tinyflux is a very close second. Second only because it is newer and so doesn't have its multilingual (i.e. South Asian and Far East Asian) support established yet. Third is AntiX, also very good.
