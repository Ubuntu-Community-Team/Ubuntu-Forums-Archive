### Post by anaconda on 2010-08-25
just type (in terminal)
ktorrent

or when you download a .torrent file from internet you can choose open with ktorrent
(or save .torrent file and then open it with ktorrent)

(the executable is in /usr/bin/ktorrent)

ktorrent can also be started from menu
Applications>internet<ktorrent

### Post by nerdy_kid on 2010-08-25
