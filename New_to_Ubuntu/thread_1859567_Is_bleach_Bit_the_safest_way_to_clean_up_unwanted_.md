### Post by Andrew.Z on 2011-10-14
BleachBit is pretty safe.  As a new user, you may want to avoid cleaning free disk space, localizations, and APT.  Also pay attention to any warnings that popup as you check a box to clean.

BleachBit does not explicitly look for duplicate files (such as two identical copies of the same photograph).
