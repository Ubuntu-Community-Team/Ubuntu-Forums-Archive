I've done multiple searches on here trying to figure out how to set my mouse to "single" click instead of "double" click, but the only few things I found did not make any sense to me. Rob

### Post by RedSingularity on 2009-08-24
Go to System>Preferences>Mouse.

Accessibility tab>Simulated secondary click.

---

### Post by tubasoldier on 2009-08-24
Open up a window like your home folder, go to Edit --> Preferences --> Behavior --> then fill in "single click".
