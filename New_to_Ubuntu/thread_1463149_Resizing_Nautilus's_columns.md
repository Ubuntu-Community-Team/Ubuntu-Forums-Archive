### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-04-26
The default is for the other three columns to be as wide as needed to show all the info in them and the first column is the remaining width.

If you narrow the nautilus window, that first column will reduce to a minimum of the widest content width, and eventually a horizontal scroll bar will appear, once it can not be narrowed further without hiding some of the content.

Seems a good way to do it to me!
