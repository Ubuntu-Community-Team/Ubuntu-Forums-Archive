### Post by anewguy on 2011-10-18
This is just my view - if a device is working with the driver installed, and it is working up to specs (wireless speed, encryption, etc.) then I see no need to change it.  If it's an open driver versus a proprietary driver you get the advantage of people changing it as needed for Linux.  So - personally I would just leave it alone if you are satisfied with performance and options.
