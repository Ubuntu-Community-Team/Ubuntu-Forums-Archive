### Post by newb85 on 2012-10-19
Broader info on your situation:

When Ubuntu replaced Gnome with Unity as the default Desktop Environment (starting with Natty), menu-style application access was replaced with the Dash.  Access the Dash by hitting the super (Windows) key on your keyboard or clicking the Ubuntu icon in the top-left corner of the screen.  Search functionality is at the center of the Dash's design, so start typing what you want and see what shows up.
