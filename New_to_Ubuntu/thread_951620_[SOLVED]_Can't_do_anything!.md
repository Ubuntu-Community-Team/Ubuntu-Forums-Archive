### Post by mj0lnir on 2008-10-18
Ok, I went for a walk, thought about it, came back and solved the problem. In case anyone else runs into it, I had the wrong Ubuntu logo up in the upper left corner. The one that was there was for the home screen. 

So, I put the mouse all the way into the upper-left corner and right-clicked, and picked "Add to Panel..." and then selected "Main Menu". This added the Ubunto logo again, but this time it has a little white triangle pointing down underneath it. That's the one I needed.
