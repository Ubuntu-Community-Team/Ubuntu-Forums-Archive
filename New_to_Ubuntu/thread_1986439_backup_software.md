### Post by dwhite on 2012-05-26
Deja Dup backup to Ubuntu One has worked very well for me.  I just backup my files, if i end up having to do a clean install that means i'd have to reload my apps but i'm willing to do that. 

Since I only back up files I only use about 1.5G on Ubuntu One, leaving plenty of space for other things (5G free storage on U1).  Now i just have a few pictures (~200-300) and no movies or other space hungry files and i realize your situation may be different but for me it works great, peace of mind :)
