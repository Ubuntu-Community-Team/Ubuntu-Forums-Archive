### Post by halitech on 2008-07-10
Its not so much a case of Ubuntu not liking ATI cards as ATI has yet to release any open source drivers. IN most cases they will work with the drivers that Ubuntu has by default but may not allow you to enable things like Compiz. As to killing hard drives, I've had Ubuntu for almost 2 years now and no problems with any of my machines or hard drives. I don't think its so much Ubuntu doing it as one of the indexing programs using the drive alot while searching and indexing files but it can be turned off.
