### Post by Cheesemill on 2012-12-25
Do you mean 10.04? There is no such thing as 10.01.

There is no way to roll back an upgrade, you will need to back up your data and do a fresh installation.

While you are at it it may be worth trying a different version of 12.04, both Lubuntu and Xubuntu work much faster on older hardware and have the traditional menus and panels look and feel. Another option would be to stick with Ubuntu 12.04 but switch your session to 'Classic Gnome' as this uses less resources. Even if you do stick with 12.04 it would definitely be worth tying a clean installation instead of an upgrade as sometimes upgrades don't work properly and leave your system in a sub-optimal state.

Whatever you choose I would definitely recommend 12.04 as it has 5 years support, whereas support for 10.04 runs out in a few months time meaning it will no longer receive any bug fixes or security updates.
