### Post by Jose Catre-Vandis on 2008-12-22
I think it is simply a case of "push" or "pull". If you have a media server streaming media out, you control what is pushed out. For example, using VLC I can broadcast live tv or a movie to all lan addresses in the house, users can then open up the broadcast link and watch what is being served (push). Alternately if users want to watch a movie they simply connect to the file store and click (pull). Think, in music terms, of listening to the radio or playing your own cd. If I have been too simplistic, clever people might have more to add.
