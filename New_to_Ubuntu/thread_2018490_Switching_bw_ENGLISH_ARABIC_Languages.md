### Post by Musicmaker384 on 2012-07-06
To do this, follow these steps:

Assuming you're running Ubuntu 12.04 LTS, click 'Dash Home'.
Search for 'System Settings'.
Select 'Keyboard Layout'.
Click the plus sign in the bottom left corner of the Keyboard Layout window.
Select 'Arabic', and click 'Add'.

You'll then have added the Arabic layout to your keyboard.
When two keyboard layouts are activated, Ubuntu works much like Windows in the respect that you can switch between the two by selecting the icon in the taskbar.

Hope this helps.
