### Post by diablo75 on 2009-01-25
The difference is mostly visual.  You should hit up google and click on "Videos" at the top to do a video search.  Then look for Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Xubuntu one after another to see each one in action and get a clearer idea of what each one looks like and functions.

And Ubuntu (not just Kubuntu) is also eye candy (if you have compiz running and throw on a fancy theme... the default theme is arguably so 1990s).
