### Post by mprince on 2009-05-31
Did you follow this procedure?

> While the unit is turned off, press and hold the POWER button for 7 seconds or more to standby for pairing.

After about 3 seconds, power is turned on and the blue and red indicator flash together twice. Keep the button pressed without releasing until both indicators start flashing together, then release the power button. 

Then the headset is in pairing mode. You have about 5 minutes to pair the device. It should show up as DR-BT21G
