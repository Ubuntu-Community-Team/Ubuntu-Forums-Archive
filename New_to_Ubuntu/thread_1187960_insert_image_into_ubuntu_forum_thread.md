### Post by Sir Jasper on 2009-06-15
Hi,

Take and save the image say, to your Home directory.

Type [www.imagehost.org](www.imagehost.org) directly into your browser and then click.

Seach for your image then upload it.

Choose the first option and copy the line of text.

Paste it into your post. Do not worry that it still looks like text.

My regards

This is not the only way.
