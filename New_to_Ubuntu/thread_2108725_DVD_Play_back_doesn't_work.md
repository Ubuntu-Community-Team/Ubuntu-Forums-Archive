### Post by d4m1r on 2013-01-25
I would say the format/app you used to burn the video files to a DVD are wrong, not the drive itself...

Install K3B, burn a data DVD, select the video files, and hit burn! If you have a relatively new DVD player with good codec support, the DVD should play just fine. I have no problem burning media using K3B under Ubuntu that works fine in Windows and standalone DVD players.
