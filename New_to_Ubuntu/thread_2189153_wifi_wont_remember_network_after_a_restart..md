### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-11-21
What can I say? Except to ask the obvious questions which need to be asked as a way of ruling out the obvious.

Is your preferred network/access point set to connect automatically? Network icon>Edit Connections>WiFi connection name>General tab>Automatically connect to this network when it is available.

Is the wireless access point (router/hub) experiencing power loss - disconnecting and reconnecting to the power supply?

Is your connection to the ISP breaking down - disconnecting and reconnecting?

I have had occasions ( just a few) when my connection to the ISP broke and during the time for the router to re-establish the connection to the ISP Network Manager suggested that there were wireless networks available and offered to connect to them.
