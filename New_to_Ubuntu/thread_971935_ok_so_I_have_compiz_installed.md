### Post by philinux on 2008-11-05
System>Prefs>compiz config.
Click General options the desktop size. Set horizontal to 4.

Then click back and scroll down and tick desktop cube and rotate cube.

Then ctrl alt hold left mouse key to rotate. Or on blank area of dekstop hold down scroll wheel and have fun.
