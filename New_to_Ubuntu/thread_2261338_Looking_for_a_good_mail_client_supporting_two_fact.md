### Post by buzzingrobot on 2015-01-18
Google turned this up rather quickly:  [https://support.google.com/mail/answer/1173270?hl=en](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/1173270?hl=en).

Your mail is "backed up" on Gmail in the sense that it isn't deleted unless you specifically direct it to delete mail. If you look around, you can find a how-tos about backing up Gmail locally, as well.

Frankly, I have much less confidence in the reliability of my own local backup hardware than I do in Google's huge server farms. But, I did manage to delete a GMail account with 30,000+ items by accident once.  Poof! All gone!  Local backups would have been nice at that point.
