### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2010-12-30
Though this might post some security, nevertheless, here's how:

1. click the Tool button on the far upper right side of the browser
2. select Preferences
3. under Personal Stuff, click the Autofill options
4. Make sure the "Enable Autofill to fill out web forms in a single click" is checked
