### Post by VeeDubb on 2010-09-08
Yup. Bit-torrent itself is 100% legal, in every civilized nation on earth.  (Probably illegal in North Korea)

Downloading and distributing copyrighted material is illegal in most countries, and your ISP also has the right to disconnect your service and/or report you to authorities for illegal activity in most countries.

Most ISP's will either do things to slow/stop the function of bittorrent, or simply ignore it all together.  Very few will actually report you.

In Canada, it's still illegal, but their privacy laws are much more strict than the US, and they do not have the right to report you.
