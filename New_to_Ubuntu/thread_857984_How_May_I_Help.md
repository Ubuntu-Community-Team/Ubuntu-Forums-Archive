### Post by rraj.be on 2008-07-13
I am too at the same stage dudes.

you can contribute to open source through launchpad if you are good programmers.

This cannot be attained as a single leap.It should of a long journey started with small step.

First create a launchpad acount and get introduced to what is launchpad.

[www.launchpad.net](www.launchpad.net).

get source code and try to learn how they are working and how you can add extra feature's

get involved in different teams.

Get into malling list and share your ideas with other porgrammers who are currently active and get a good trust and impresion on you.

Then you can submit your own projects there.

Beyond programming you can help open source community in many ways.

you can send all bug reports that are occurring in your system.

help new one in forums and LP.

Create documents for things that lag in documentation.

Get a project and try to maintain them.


Get new users to open source and encourage others to use open sourec.

Contribute Howto's and wiki's.


and there are many more.

you can feel free to contact me in private message's for further information.
