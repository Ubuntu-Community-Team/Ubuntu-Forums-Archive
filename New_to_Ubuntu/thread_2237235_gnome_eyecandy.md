### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-07-31
Forget about antivirus and scans, as there are no linux viruses in the wild; just make sure you stick to reputable download sources, which means mainly the ubuntu repositories.

However, having said that for eye-candy for gnome or gtk OSs go to [GNOME-Look.org]("http://gnome-look.org/") and have a good search for themes, icon sets, wallpapers, etc etc.
