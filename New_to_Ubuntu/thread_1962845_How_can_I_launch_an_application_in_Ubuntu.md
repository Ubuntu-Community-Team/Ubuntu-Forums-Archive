### Post by jerome1232 on 2012-04-21
Click on the Ubuntu logo in the top left of your screen, start typing the first few letters of the application you want to launch and it will show up in your dash.

If you want it on your launcher drag it there.

### Post by tmaranets on 2012-04-21
click the "dash home" button in the launcher on the side. Then search for whatever app you want to use, and then click the app. If you want to move the app to your launcher, drag it from the "dash home" to your launcher.

### Post by d4m1r on 2012-04-21
Short of having to search apps you run often, you can drag icons of applications from the dash window to the unity launcher on the left side of your screen and it will pin it there. Then you just click on it to launch it, like a Windows desktop shortcut ;)
