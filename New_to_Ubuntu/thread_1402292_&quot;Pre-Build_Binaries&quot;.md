### Post by 311005901 on 2010-02-11
Hey benwayj!
I downloaded the tarball and opened it with Archive Manager. I clicked and dragged it to my desktop. Then I right clicked on one of the files and went to the "Permissions" tab and checked the box to 'Allow executing file as program'.

Then, I opened Applications>Accessories>Terminal and clicked and dragged the file into the terminal from my desktop and pressed enter.
