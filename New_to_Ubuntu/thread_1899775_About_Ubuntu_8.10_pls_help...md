### Post by waynefoutz on 2011-12-24
> **asvh_17 said:**
> awts.. tnx 4 d reply..
Upgrading my OS 2 a newer version will not delete my files ryt?

You should back them up. The newer builds use ext4 file system, Intrepid was still using ext3. You could install a newer Ubuntu oon top of Intrepid, and have it overwrite the partition, you'd keep your files, but you'd still have to reinstall all your applications. You best bet is to get some blank dvds or a backup device and back up your critical data and start fresh. You'll get the best results and performance that way in the end.
