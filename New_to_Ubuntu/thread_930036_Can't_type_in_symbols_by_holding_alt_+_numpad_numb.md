### Post by eggdeng on 2008-09-25
If by squared, you mean squared as in 4² = 16, you can type it by hitting 4, circumflex, 2. For most of the other symbols you might need, just right click on the panel, Add to Panel and choose character palette. This will give you a series of handly little sets of characters which you can insert with Ctrl+V.
