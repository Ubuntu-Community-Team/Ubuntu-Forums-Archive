### Post by HotShotDJ on 2009-01-15
Based on your description of the problem, the standard Desktop LiveCD may be incompatible with your video card (not a frequent problem, but neither is it a rare problem) -- What video card is installed in the target system?

You will probably have success using the Alternate installer.  You can download it from one of the servers listed at the bottom of the following page: [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors)

Once installed, you should be able to easily get your video card up and running.
