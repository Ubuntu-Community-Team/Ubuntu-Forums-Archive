### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2012-03-18
Not really anything you can or should have to do, as far as I can see.

Your best bet might be to report the bug (problem) on  [_Launchpad_]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/rhythmbox/+filebug") (you need an account, very quick and simple), then the developers should be able to fix it, if not for 12.04, for 12.10. You might be told it's a duplicate, or already fixed in beta, but that's good news. You also might be asked for some extra information, but they should be extremely helpful, if a little brusque. You'll also get up-to-date information on how your bug is going on the way to be fixed.

This is an excellent way of "giving back", and I heartily recommend it.
