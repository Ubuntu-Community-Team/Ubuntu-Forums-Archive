### Post by mgmiller on 2009-12-25
In the "Add To Panel" dialog, look for "Notification Area"  That will give you network and bluetooth and update stuff.  

To get battery status, go to System > Preferences > Power Management
and then on the General tab, in the Notification area, click off Always  Display an Icon and then click Close.
