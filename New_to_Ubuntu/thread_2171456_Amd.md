### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-08-30
When driving multiple monitors, some graphics cards have a limitation where the resolution needs to be the same or some multiple.  It is related to the horizontal synchronization frequency and possibly the vertical sync.  Try setting lower resolutions on your 17" monitor, or find a different monitor and see if you get any farther.  You can also set a low resolution on your AOC and see if the 17" comes to life.
