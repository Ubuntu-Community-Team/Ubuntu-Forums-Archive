### Post by dasan on 2011-07-17
Have you confirmed your version of grub before doing it..

type [COLOR="Blue"]grub-install -v[/COLOR] in terminal and if the out put is 0.97 or older it is old grub and if it is more than that it is GRUB 2 follow the tutorials accordingly.
