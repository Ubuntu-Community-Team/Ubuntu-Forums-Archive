### Post by SPazzo on 2010-01-14
So open up Totem, and press F9.  (Or go to View > Side Bar)  A side bar should open up.  There should be a drop down menu from the top.  Click it and go to Playlist (if it isn't there already).  In that playlist option you can add songs, and save the playlist to open up later.  (Just double click the file).

But if you really want some sort of music organizer, I would suggest Amorak or Songbird.  Much better for making playlists and organizing.

Hope that helps!

### Post by duanedesign on 2010-01-14
For music playing Totem is not quite as feature rich as say Rhythmbox or Banshee. You might be better served using one of those for your music playing.

That said there is a 'playlist'
View > Sidebar Use the selector in the sidebar to select playlist
Then you can drag and drop files from your music directories into the playlist.
