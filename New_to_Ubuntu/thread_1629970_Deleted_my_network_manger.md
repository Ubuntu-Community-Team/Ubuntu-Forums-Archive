### Post by uRock on 2010-11-24
Right click on the panel where you want to put the network manager/network monitor, then select "Add to panel," then scroll down to Network Monitor, click it once, then click the "Add" button.

Sudo apt-get install network-manager will reinstall it, but you may have to insert the LiveCD if your network connection is inoperable.
