### Post by starcannon on 2009-12-27
[LIST=1]
[*]With your cursor over the word "System" right click your mouse.
[*]Click on "Edit Menus" from the drop down list.
[*]In the window that appears, scroll down to "System"
[*]Click on "Preferences"
[*]Put a check mark in "Remote Desktop" if it doesn't have one already, if it does have a check mark in it, then uncheck it, and recheck it.
[/LIST]
