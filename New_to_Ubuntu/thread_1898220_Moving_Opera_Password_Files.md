### Post by BNZBKK on 2011-12-20
I am about to change computers and want to transfer by USB my Opera browser info, how do I do this and retain my 'wand passwords'.

Reading Opera's help page gives up 'Password Manager data wand.dat' but I cannot search/find the relevant files 


Or is there an easy method ?

---

### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-12-20
Open your home folder, hit Ctrl+h to show hidden files, and look for the '.opera' folder. The . in the beginning is not a typo, the name starts with a ., '.opera'. You can copy the folder itself, if you want the whole profile, or have a look inside for the 'wand.dat'.

### Post by BNZBKK on 2011-12-21
Excellent mikewhatever it was there and now copied.

But why wouldn't 'Search' give it up ?

---

### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-12-21
Probably because it's a hidden configuration folder, and search is intended for user files like documents, pictures, etc.
