### Post by cascade9 on 2010-04-24
With 4GB, you could probably do without a swap (but I wouldnt). How much swap you 'need' will depend on your usage- if you want to use suspend/hibernate you need to have more swap than RAM. 6-8GB would be more than enough for hibernate to work. 

Anything more than 8GB is just a waste, unless you are doing some very nasty 3D modeling, or something that else uses huge amounts of RAM.
