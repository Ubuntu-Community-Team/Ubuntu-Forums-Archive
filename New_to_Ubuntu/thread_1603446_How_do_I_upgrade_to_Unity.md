### Post by ArgusVision on 2010-10-22
Longer answer...
 Depending on your hardware you may experience some issues. Particularly Atheros and broadcom wireless drivers. These should get worked out soon, but right now I would wait. If you want to test it out and have the hard drive space (or an extra HDD) you might make a small partition 8-10 GB and do a test install. Then if you like it / don't have any major issues, then go for an upgrade.
