### Post by dearingj on 2010-04-21
I see a line too - not several lines like in the OP's screenshot. I found that i can remove the line by selecting cell B9 (the one that's supposed to say "Year-End Summary") and choosing Format->Default Formatting.

Edit: Looks like it's the text orientation setting (Format->Cells->Alignment tab). The orientation is set to one degree; setting it to zero fixes the problem.
