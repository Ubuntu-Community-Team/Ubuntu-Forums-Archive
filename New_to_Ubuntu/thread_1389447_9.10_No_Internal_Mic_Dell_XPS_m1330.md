### Post by Methuselah on 2010-01-24
I can't tell you exactly what to do but sometimes changing the capture settings in alsamixer will help.

Type alsamixer in a terminal and press F4 to go into capture mode.
L/R Arrow keys select channel and U/D changes volume.
One if them should say 'L R Capture', space bar can toggle than on if not.

My mic works with the first capture set and not muted and digital also given an appropriate volume.
I'm not sure what will work for you but maybe you'll make some progress experimenting.

### Post by gigi1234 on 2010-01-24
I tried again to see what I change using the Pulseaudio volume control.

Under Configuration->Profile I changed the setting to "analog stereo duplex" and this fixed the problem.

Thanks everyone for your suggestions! You helped a lot!
