nope. when you select something(text) using left-click/drag, its copied to the clipbord and when you middle click, its pasted(in a browser, its loaded on to the url bar). if the url deasn't exist, your dns provider would search and bring up a similar address. and if it's nothing like a url/search pattern or if the clipboard is empty, then the 'illegal string/.....' would popup

in firefox, to get back the 'scrolling icon', type *about:config* in the url bar, then search and set **general.autoScroll** to true. dunno much about opera though...
