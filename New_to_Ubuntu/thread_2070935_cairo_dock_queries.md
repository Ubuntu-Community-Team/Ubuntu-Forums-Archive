1. You can't. Cairo Dock does have its own session that does not have the Unity Launcher in the left side (you can choose to keep the Uity Panel, the panel in the top). Log out and chose from the Cairo Dock sessions.

2. You need to add it to the startup applications, or if you're using the Cairo Dock session, you don't need to.

3. No.

4. Which border?

5. Right click on the dock. The options should be obvious.

6. They're just themes. I don't see any stability issues being involved.
