### Post by compiledkernel on 2009-02-06
You can boot from an external device, sure, as long as your BIOS supports USB (Im assuming thats the medium your going to use) booting. 

Fairly easy to do as well -- [http://www.pendrivelinux.com/installing-ubuntu-to-a-usb-hard-drive/](http://www.pendrivelinux.com/installing-ubuntu-to-a-usb-hard-drive/)

That Howto is old, but the general idea remains the same.

### Post by jimv on 2009-02-06
The install pretty much  goes like usual, you just need to make sure that on the final screen of the installation (before you actually click Install) that you click Advanced and set Grub to be installed onto the portable drive.
