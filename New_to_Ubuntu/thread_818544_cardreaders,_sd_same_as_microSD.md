### Post by Cypher on 2008-06-04
An SD slot will accept a MicroSD card as long as you use the proper MicroSD->SD adapater. This is also true for MiniSD. However, know that most laptop SD readers are SD-Memory only. So they will only recognize storage devices, they are not SDIO interfaces, so SDIO WiFi chips will not be recognized or done anything with..
