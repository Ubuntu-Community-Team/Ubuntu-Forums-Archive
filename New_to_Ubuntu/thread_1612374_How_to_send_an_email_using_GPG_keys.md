### Post by JustinR on 2010-11-03
If your using Ubuntu's default mail client, Evolution, open Evolution up:

Go to > Edit > Preferences > Mail Accounts > Click you email account > Click the 'Edit' button, Click 'Security' put in your GPG key and check any checkboxes below that you want (optional).
