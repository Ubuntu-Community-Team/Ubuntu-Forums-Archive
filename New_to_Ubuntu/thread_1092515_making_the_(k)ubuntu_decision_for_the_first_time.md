### Post by lobes on 2009-03-10
i've just started using linux so i may not be the gy to help but heres my opinions.  I have Ubuntu with the Kubuntu packages installed. Your can choose at logn screen to boot into GNU(ubuntu) or KDE(kubuntu).  Ubuntu "just works" features have pulled through pretty well for me except I have no audio over hdmi.  The KDE (Kubuntu) interface has a few problems that I have yet to work out.  On my 1280x720 plasma display everything in Kubuntu is just too small. I cant read anything.  

You may want to look into Ubuntu Studio dist.  [http://ubuntustudio.org](http://ubuntustudio.org) for multimedia editing and production.  I havent used it but It looks promising and may provide a little more fuctionality for your uses.

One thing is for sure,  if you run into problems, like my no audio over hdmi, they are not an easy fix in most cases.  Unlike with OSX and Windows there is no way to just install 3rd party drivers.  It requires a lot of know how.  And I just dont know how. lol
