confirmed.

amd64 aka x86_64 is 64 bit.
i386, i586, or i686 is 32.

that applies to all distros.

ubuntu specific:
i386 is 32
amd64 or x86_64 is 64

(you may see amd64 if you are trying to repackage a 32-bit deb to run on your 64 bit system.)
