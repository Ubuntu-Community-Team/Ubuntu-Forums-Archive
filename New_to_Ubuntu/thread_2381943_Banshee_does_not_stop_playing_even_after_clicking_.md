### Post by deadflowr on 2018-01-07
It's by design.
So that user's can keep music going without the app needing to be open all the time.
Same design that rhythmbox does.
To stop you simply need to stop the song (not sure about banshee but pause in rhythmbox is the stop because well heh), then once the song is stopped, closing the app will close the app.

You are only closing the window. To bring it up again, click on your speaker icon, it should be listed there as running.
