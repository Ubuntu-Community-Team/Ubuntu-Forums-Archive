### Post by Presto123 on 2009-06-09
My first suggestion is to go to System/Administration and click on Software Sources. Make sure all of your boxes are checked in the first tab: Unverse, Multiverse, Restricted and Main. Open up your package manager again and it should give you an option to reload and try your download again.
