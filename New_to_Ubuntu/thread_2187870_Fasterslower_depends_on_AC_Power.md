### Post by stefbeukers on 2013-11-14
Why is my laptop slower when its on battery power? When its on ACPower it is a fast as it should be...

---

### Post by Impavidus on 2013-11-14
When it's on battery power it may start conserving power by trottling the CPU, spinning down the disks etc. You can switch it off, but your battery will last shorter.

---

### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-11-14
You *may* be able to adjust the throttling back in the BIOS.
