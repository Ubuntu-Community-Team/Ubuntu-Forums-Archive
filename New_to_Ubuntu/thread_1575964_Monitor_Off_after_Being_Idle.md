### Post by EnigmaticCoder on 2010-09-16
On my parents xubuntu computer, the monitor won't automatically turn off after being idle; the screensaver persists indefinitely. I'd rather save some power by having the monitor turn off after a while. How can I do this?

---

### Post by JKyleOKC on 2010-09-16
Open the Applications menu, select "Settings" and then "Screensaver" to open the screensaver configuration dialog. Select the "Advanced" tab and you'll see three times in the upper right quarter of the window. You can set them to any values you like.

This will tell your system to send a shutdown signal to the monitor at the desired time, but not all monitors obey such a signal. Most modern ones, however, do...
