### Post by BugBuster on 2010-12-06
Assuming you have two ethernet cards on the ubuntu computer,

1) Right click network-manager
2) Select Edit connections
3} Select your ethernet card that you will use to share internet
4} Select Edit
5) Under IPv4 tab; select "Shared to other computers"
6) Plug your windows computer to the one with ubuntu(using a crossover cable) and you   should be off!!
