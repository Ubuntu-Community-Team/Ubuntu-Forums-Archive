### Post by NikTh on 2012-10-22
I always suggest [Lubuntu]("http://lubuntu.net/") for netbooks , because of the very light environment.

Although I believe that [Ubuntu 12.04 LTS]("http://releases.ubuntu.com/12.04/") with ubuntu-2d environment it will fit good as well in the netbook. Also Unity plugin (Ubuntu 12.04 included) is a very usable environment for netbooks.

The only thing you have to do , is to choose the ubuntu-2d (lightweight) environment from the login screen.
