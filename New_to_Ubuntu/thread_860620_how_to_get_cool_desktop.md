### Post by seagullplayer77 on 2008-07-15
If you have Compiz installed, you can go into System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects Settings and enable "Desktop Cube" and "Rotate Cube" and work from there. I believe the default key combo to rotate the cube is Ctrl+Alt+Left/Right, or Ctrl+Alt+click and drag. Another quick note: You might have to go into System > Preferences > Appearance > Visual Effects and select the "Extra" option in order to get Compiz to work.
