### Post by oldfred on 2013-11-09
Moved to your own thread. Do not hijack an older thread on a different topic.

You do not show a vendor recovery partition. Did you overwrite it when you installed?
Did you make backups of the recovery partition before you installed?
Or do you mean the Windows recovery or boot partition?

If vendor recovery, often better just to have a full backup of Windows anyway. The vendor recovery restores to as purchased and will not have all the updates or changes you have made.

       The vendor recovery DVDs are just an image of your drive as purchased. If you have housecleaned a lot of cruft normally included, run many updates with many reboots, and added software you may want a full back up.
