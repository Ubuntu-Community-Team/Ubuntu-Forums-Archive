### Post by audiomick on 2013-01-30
> **Tarka Dal said:**
> It does not work on either Ubuntu or Windows.

This is a dual boot on one machine, or two different machines?

If it is one machine, I strongly suggest trying your mic on a different machine, and/or a different mic on your machine. If it is two different machines, I suspect the mic has a problem.

You say that the mic does not work in Ubuntu or in Windows. This is an indication that the mic or the connector on the computer might indeed have a problem. That is why I would try the mic in another machine, and another mic on you machine. You need to be absolutely sure that the mic is not broken before you start looking for software problems.
