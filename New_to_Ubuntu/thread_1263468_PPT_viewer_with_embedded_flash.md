### Post by thegreatsnafu on 2009-09-11
Hi,

Power Point animations don't use flash. The animations are designed to work inside of Power Point only. (It's just another small way that Micro$oft makes money.) When you open PPT files inside of Open Office, It does it's best to match up the Micro$oft animations, with it's own. The animations are generally the same, but just a little different. So you may have to use the Open Office animations instead...
