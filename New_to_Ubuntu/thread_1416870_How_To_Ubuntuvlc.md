### Post by kd.zombrainy on 2010-02-26
Artificial Intelligence beat me to it, however here's another way

Right click any selected wav file, click properties, click the "open with" tab, if vlc isn't one of the programs there click add. Go through the list of programs and vlc will be there, it will then become the default program for opening wav files and in the default list as well
