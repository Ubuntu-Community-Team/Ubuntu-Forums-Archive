### Post by Liolikas on 2009-08-27
You can try things out with LiveCD mode. 
Ubuntu CD do not just install it boots into working desktop (Firefox, office etc.) and there is icon "install to hdd' on it.

If drive is bootable and writable you can install Ubuntu on it. Also if you lack free space for dual boot it is not hard to grab screwdriver and add second internal hdd into your PC just google for tutorial with images.
