### Post by halitech on 2009-08-09
Depends on the options you select. It can save everything on the hard drive, it can create a live cd or it can backup just the home folder (from what I can remember) Chances are, unless you have nothing installed for apps and only a few files to save, you can do a complete backup to a DL dvd. If you have alot of apps or alot of data, you may want to look at simply saving your data from the home folder and starting over.
