### Post by erginemr on 2008-06-24
I'd say backup your system first in accordance with:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=35087&highlight=restore+howto](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=35087&highlight=restore+howto)

before upgrading to Hardy, as it is known to create quite a lot of problems for many users who were happy on Gutsy:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=768200](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=768200)
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=764847](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=764847)

This way, you can roll back your system if you are not happy with what you get at the end of the upgrade.
