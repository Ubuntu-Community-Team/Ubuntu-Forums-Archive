### Post by tnseditor on 2008-10-29
When you do the installer it may want to overwrite the whole thing (it will warn you) or you can resize the partition(s).  It should show your Windows partition and your new Ubuntu partition in a slider thing.  You will be able to move the slider to select the size.  Remember to back up your Windows partition just in case and to defragment also.
