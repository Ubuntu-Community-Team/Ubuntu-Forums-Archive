### Post by blade4 on 2011-09-10
Here's what you need to do : 

1) Type "about:config" in Firefox’s address bar without the  
   quotes.
2) In the Filter box, type click.
3) Look for "browser.urlbar.clickSelectsAll" , right  
   click on it and click Toggle.
4) Re-start Firefox and see the magic. Enjoy!

Cheers
