### Post by thomas144 on 2010-03-07
I know that I've read some bad things about wubi. If you have a Live CD, I recommend that you install from that. Open up your disc drive in Vista and turn your computer off. Put in the Ubuntu disc, start the computer, and close the drive. When you get to the bootup screen, select, "Try Ubuntu without any changes to your computer." Once everything loads, click on the desktop icon that says "Install Ubuntu."
