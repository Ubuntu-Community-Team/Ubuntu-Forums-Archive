### Post by diablo75 on 2009-01-18
There is probably an internal menu interface built into the monitor itself.  You typically have to press a button on the monitor to make it appear.  You'll have to troll through the different menus using other buttons on the monitor (like up/down arrows, and "Enter" key) to find the option you are looking for.  This type of screen adjustment goes right along the lines of other common adjustments such as horizontal/vertical positioning, width, height, brightness, contrast, and a whole list of other possible settings.  All of this is done within the monitor itself, and the OS (whether it be Ubuntu or Windows) plays no part.
