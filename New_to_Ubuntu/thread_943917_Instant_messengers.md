You set up Yahoo! in a similar way. In the main Pidgin "Buddy List" screen go to Accounts > Manage Accounts > Add (or alternatively if you right click on the green Pidgin icon in system tray you just need to select Accounts > Add). Then next to the option where it says "Protocol" click the button with arrows to the right of it. That displays a whole range of protocols,simply selecte Yahoo. In the other boxes add your Yahoo! account details and password, click save, and away you go.
