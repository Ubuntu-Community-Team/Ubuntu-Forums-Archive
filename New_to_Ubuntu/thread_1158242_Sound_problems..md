### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2009-05-13
Ubuntu sound can be frustrating, as some programs seem to hog the sound card and not let other programs use them.

There are very complicated solutions to this.  What I have found that is easy to do and solves *most* of the sound conflicts is this:

1) Install [alsa-firmware]("apt:alsa-firmware")

2) Go to your sound preferences and set everything to use alsa.
