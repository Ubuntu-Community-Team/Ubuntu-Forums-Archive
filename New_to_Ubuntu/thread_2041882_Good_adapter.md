### Post by d4m1r on 2012-08-13
I'd recommend spending $20-40 for a quality one that won't give you any issues, regardless of OS. Specifically, it should be 300MBPS and Wireless N. D-Link would be my personal recommendation (and they are popular with linux users) but others might say Cisco or Asus..
