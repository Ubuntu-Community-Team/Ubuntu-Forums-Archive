### Post by rogueleader12345 on 2009-06-16
If you don't want to hassle with removing Ubuntu and installing Kubuntu, you can just add KDE to Ubuntu. Go to synaptic and search for "kde-desktop". Mark it for install, let it load. Then restart. At your login screen click "Sessions" and choose KDE. Login and enjoy!
