### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-12-16
> **Legeril said:**
> My trash wont delete, when I open it - it says that there is nothing in there. I know I have deleted stuff, and from the icon on my task bar it the mouse-over says two items. I am using 10.04, anyone have any advice?

I have had the exact situation here (with 26 files). Ultimately found they were deleted originally from a mounted folder, and the problem existed when the folder was no longer mounted (encrypted folder - cryptkeeper in use). My solution was to remount the encrypted folder then empty the trash bin.

Are you using encrypted folders/cryptkeeper by any chance? :)

### Post by Legeril on 2010-12-16
HA that worked! I had deleted files from a USB earlier, I just stuck the USB back in my computer and they deleted fine.
