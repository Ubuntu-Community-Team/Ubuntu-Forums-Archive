### Post by wilee-nilee on 2010-04-25
Probably a graphics problem look if there are any drivers available in systems>administration>hardware drivers.

Also there is a protocol to do a soft-shutdown or reboot that is what you want to use.
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reisub](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Reisub)
You will almost never have to hit that power button if you learn this key sequence.
