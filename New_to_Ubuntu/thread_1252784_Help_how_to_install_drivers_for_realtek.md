### Post by NoaHall on 2009-08-29
Right click and extract here.

Or, from the command line tar yxf /home/$YOURUSERNAME/Downloads/linuxPkg_5.12.tar.bz2

Then to install, it could either be chmod +x , then cd /home/$USER/Downloads, then ./linuxPkg.bin

Installing really depends on what you get out after decompressing
