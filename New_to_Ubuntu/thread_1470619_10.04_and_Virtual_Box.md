### Post by lkraemer on 2010-05-03
You state, "just loath to upgrade and lose all my stuff in XP running on virtual box".
Why do you lose your VDI Images?  Just as you would in M$ backup your
Bookmarks, Documents, Data files, and anything else you need to restore
your system.  I keep a document of what needs to be saved and
continually add/remove items, making it rather painless.

Your VirtualBox VDI images are located at /home/user/.Virtualbox/HardDisks
and you can copy or move them to the new install of 10.04 with Virtualbox.
