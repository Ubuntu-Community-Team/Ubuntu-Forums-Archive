### Post by germanix on 2008-10-11
Seeing as you are planning to get rid of your xp anyway, and would like only one partition for your xubuntu, and you have very little or no experience using Gparted, your best bet would be to newly inslall Xubuntu, and using the option when installing "use the whole disk" That way Xubuntu will re-format and use the whole hard drive, and you will have what you wanted. Make sure however before you re-install that you back-up any personal files on a ext. drive or usb stick so you can restore those files after the installation.
