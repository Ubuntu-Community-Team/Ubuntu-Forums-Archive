### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-04-19
When you look at a mail (e.g. number 3), just press [FONT=Courier New][SIZE=3][COLOR=Navy]d[/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT] to delete it.

Then, be careful when you exit.

If you exit with [FONT=Courier New][SIZE=3][COLOR=Navy]exit[/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT], no changes will be saved.

To save your changes, exit with [FONT=Courier New][SIZE=3][COLOR=Navy]quit[/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT].
