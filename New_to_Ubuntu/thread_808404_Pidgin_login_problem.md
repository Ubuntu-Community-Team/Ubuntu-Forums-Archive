### Post by AndrewTheArt on 2008-05-26
Whenever you create a new account in pidgin, go to the "Advanced" tab. Under "Proxy Options" and "Proxy type", type in your proxy info.

[http://mytechxp.blogspot.com/2007/10/pidgin-through-proxy.html](http://mytechxp.blogspot.com/2007/10/pidgin-through-proxy.html)
