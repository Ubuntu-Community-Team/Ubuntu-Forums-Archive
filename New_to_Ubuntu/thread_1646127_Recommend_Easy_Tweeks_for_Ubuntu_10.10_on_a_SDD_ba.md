### Post by e4uforums on 2010-12-15
What kind of HD videos are giving you trouble?

I would suspect a software problem or CPU bottleneck if you're having problems.  Try firing up one of the videos that you're having problems with, then go to System>Administration>System Monitor.  This will show you CPU and RAM usage - you can use this to identify whether or not you have a hardware bottleneck.

For software, try using another player.  VLC is good and is available in the repos.
