### Post by kurt18947 on 2011-11-17
One thing you might try.  Look in the lower right-hand corner of the screen, just to the left of the zoom slider. There are 3 page-looking icons called view layout. You have the center one selected. You might select the leftmost icon(single page)then scroll and zoom to suit.  I've had the same thing happen and that seemed to help me.

If you don't have it, you might take a look at this site.  I find it pretty useful for LibreOffice guidance:
[http://www.libreoffice.org/get-help/documentation/](http://www.libreoffice.org/get-help/documentation/)
