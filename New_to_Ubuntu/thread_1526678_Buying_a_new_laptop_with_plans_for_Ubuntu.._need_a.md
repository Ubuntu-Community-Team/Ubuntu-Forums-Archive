### Post by Paqman on 2010-07-08
> **YeeP said:**
> I have moved one registered windows OS from one machine to another

You own the licence to use Windows, not the actual copy you install. So you can transfer your licence from one machine to a new one as long as you don't leave a copy on the old machine. The licence remains in your possession, which is why it's allowed.

What you can't (technically) do with an OEM licence is transfer it to someone else. You could have a go at refusing the EULA and trying to get a refund from the OEM, but you'll have to work hard and I wouldn't rate your chances.
