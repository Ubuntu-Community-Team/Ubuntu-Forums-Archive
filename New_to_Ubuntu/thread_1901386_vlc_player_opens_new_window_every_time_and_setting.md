### Post by GWBouge on 2011-12-28
Goto Tools -> Preferences, click the 'All' dot on the bottom left to display all settings.

For opening a new window each time, goto the Advanced section, and check 'Allow only one running instance'.

For the equalizer, goto Audio -> Filters -> Equalizer.  From there, you select a preset from the drop-down or adjust the bands gain to your liking (the numbers are displayed at the bottom of the bands in the equalizer window).
