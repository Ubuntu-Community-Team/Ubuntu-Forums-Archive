### Post by sudodus on 2011-11-23
It should be something like this (the wording may differ, but the meaning should be)
the menu Edit -- Settings
the tab Plugin Modules
untick Word Completion

---

### Post by Michael Dooley on 2011-11-23
In the Gedit menu bar, click on Tools. At the drop down menu, uncheck Autocheck Spelling.
