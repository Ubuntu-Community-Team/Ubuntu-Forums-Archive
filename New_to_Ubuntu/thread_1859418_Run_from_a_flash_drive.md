Hi,
If you go to [the download page](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download), and scroll to step 2, then you can select USB Stick then Mac or Ubuntu, and "Show me how" for instructions of how to do this.

If you have Ubuntu installed on another machine, then it will be easier to use that than the Mac.

Alternatively, you could look at [UNetbootin](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)

Hope this helps,
Ezra.
