### Post by Bartender on 2010-01-25
I went into Firefox add-ons and typed 'pdf download' in the Search box.  Installed PDF Download.  Once it installs, a link to the utility is placed in the Firefox "File" dropdown.
It's slow on dial-up.  Apparently once you click on "save to PDF" the webpage is sent to nitro, where the conversion is done, then it's sent back to you.  Something like that.  OK, the page was just sent back to me as I was writing this and it worked.

If you're not really concerned about exact formatting, you could just highlight the entire page, save it to OpenOffice, then "Export" it as a .pdf.  That would be faster than PDF Download, but you'll lose some of the HTML formatting.

[Here's]("http://www.firefoxfacts.com/2007/05/21/convert-to-pdf-from-firefox/") another Firefox app, looks interesting but I haven't tried it
