### Post by myromance123 on 2010-05-14
I know this is a relatively old thread but since you didn't get a reply, I'll reply for you.

 To turn off desktop effects, right click on your Desktop.
Select and click 'Change Desktop Background'.
Click on the 'Visual Effects' tab.
Click the first radio button with the title 'None'.
That should disable your Desktop Effects.
