### Post by coffeecat on 2008-07-14
Have you got Brasero installed? I'm fairly sure it's available for Gutsy, but I'm using Hardy so I can't be sure. There's a facility there for blanking discs. You get the choice of quick or complete (can't remember the actual words). The latter takes a long time so I imagine it's overwriting the whole disc, rather than just resetting the filesystem.

**Edit:** just checked. Open Brasero and choose Erase from the Tools Menu. Untick fast blanking and go and make yourself a cup of coffee - it takes that long. :wink:
