Not completely sure what it is you are after - more explanation might help.
But assuming that you are after auto mounting other partitions then you need to look at fstab - [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Fstab](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Fstab)

You can also install 2 apps which will help to automate the process - pysdm and ntfs-config(if it is an ntfs partition)
