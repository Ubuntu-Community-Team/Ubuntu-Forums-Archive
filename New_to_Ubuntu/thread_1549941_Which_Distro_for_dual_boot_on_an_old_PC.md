### Post by Macskeeball on 2010-08-10
[Xubuntu](http://www.xubuntu.com) is an official Ubuntu variant from Canonical that is designed for older hardware, because it uses a more lightweight desktop environment called XFCE. Another option is the reportedly even faster (haven't tried it myself) [Lubuntu](http://www.lubuntu.net), but that's not an official variant. I'd suggest giving those a try, and then to make her computer faster still you could upgrade the RAM if possible.
