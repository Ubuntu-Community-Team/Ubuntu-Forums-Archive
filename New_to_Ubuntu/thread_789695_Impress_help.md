### Post by bclintbe on 2008-05-18
Choose the very first slide. On the right hand side "Task" pane, click on "Slide Transition" (near the bottom). You'll see something that says "Modify Transition" and you can use a drop down to select a sound... choose "Other" from the drop down, and then browse to find the song you want to put in. Leave the "Loop until next sound" box empty. Should play through the whole presentation now.
