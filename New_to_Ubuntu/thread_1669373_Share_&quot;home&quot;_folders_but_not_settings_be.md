### Post by Wtower on 2011-01-17
There are many possibilities, but for me the right thing to do is to mount the drive as home, make her a second account and then another folder that will be shared for both of you.

Besides, it is a good security practice each user to have his/her account. In this way, for Linux systems at least, anything wrong he will do will not affect other people's life or the system security (unless they authorise as root).
