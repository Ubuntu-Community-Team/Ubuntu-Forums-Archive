### Post by finer recliner on 2008-08-27
what version of ubuntu are you using? Hardy Heron should have no problem with the newest "classic" ipods. In previous versions of ubuntu you need to do a couple extra steps to sync with it.

If you are running hardy heron (8.04), open amarok, and go to the devices tab. you may have to click the button at the top that says "connect device" (or something similar)
