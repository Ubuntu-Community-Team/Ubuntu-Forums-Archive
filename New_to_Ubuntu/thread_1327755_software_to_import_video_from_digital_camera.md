### Post by laidback on 2009-11-15
I don't use any software at all, I have a simple Casio Exilim Z850 which takes photos in JPG and videos in AVI format. The camera came with a docking/recharge unit which also has a USB cable. I simple plug the USB into my PC and Ubuntu mounts the SD card as if it were a memory stick. I then open the folder and move across any pictures/videos that I want into my own file structure. To activate the mounting process I simply press a button on the recharge unit that says USB, perhaps your camera has a similar option.
