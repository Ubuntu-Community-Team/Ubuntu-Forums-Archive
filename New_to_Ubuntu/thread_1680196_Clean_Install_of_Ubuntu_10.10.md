### Post by jonbonjovi on 2011-02-02
You havo to open a burning dvd software (such as nero burning, power dvd, etc) and select "burn image", not anything else, not "data cd/dvd", just "burn image". At the end, you shouldn't see 1 file .iso on your burned cd, but many files, which are the content of the .iso file you should have burned.
