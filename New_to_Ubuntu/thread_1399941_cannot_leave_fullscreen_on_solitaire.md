### Post by DariaJun on 2010-08-09
I went to all the settings, did some other things suggested from other threads and nothing worked.

I did, however, just fix it. Basically it's set to start at the last screen size it was when it was closed. So you have to close it when it's not full screen. If you time it exactly right, press F11 to attempt to leave full screen, which it will do for a split second. During that split second it is not full screen, quickly do the ctrl+w to close the program. If you managed to close it while it was out of full screen, it will reopen resized.
