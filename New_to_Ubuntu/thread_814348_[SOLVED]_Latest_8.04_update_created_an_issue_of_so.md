### Post by norfair on 2008-05-31
My update icon appeared a few days ago, and after clicking it and updating my system Evolution now asks for my password (key ring logger or something like that). It only asks for it once when I boot up and open the program, not each time I access it. But it keeps asking after each shut down and start up. I have "remember passord" checked, but other than that I don't know what to do. Has anyone else experienced this issue or know what I can do to resolve it? Thanks.

---

### Post by sayakb on 2008-05-31
Goto Applications->Accessories->Passwords and Encryption
Click on Passwords tab
Right click on the Evolution entry (can be identified by your email address)
Select Delete Key
Now open Evolution and save the password again.
