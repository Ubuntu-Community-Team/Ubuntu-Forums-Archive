### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-05-12
Why not get a new internal one?

Most laptops have a mini-PCI slot.  Almost any Intel, and most Atheros cards will work fine.

You can get them on ebay or amazon.

If you want an external, an RT2500 PCMCIA is probably your best bet.
