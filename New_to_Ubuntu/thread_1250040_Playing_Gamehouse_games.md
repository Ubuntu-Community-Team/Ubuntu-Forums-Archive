### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-08-26
Gamehouse has LOTS of games.  To see if the ones you want to run will work well with Wine, go to the site below and enter the name of the game:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true")

If you get nothing found, or if the rating is less than Gold, you're wasting your time trying to get the game to run in Wine
