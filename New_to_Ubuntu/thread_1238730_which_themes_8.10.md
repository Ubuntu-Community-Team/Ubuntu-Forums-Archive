### Post by wfp on 2009-08-12
I get most of my themes here > [http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/) just pick a theme you like under GTK 2.x  Then download the theme tar.gz to your desktop
Open change desktop background, and choose the theme tab. Drag and drop the new theme you just downloaded into the theme window.
