### Post by jon.reeve on 2009-06-18
Anyone know where nautilus bookmarks are stored? (the quick links to Documents, Music, etc) I just lost all mine for some reason and I'm trying to see if there's a backup somewhere.

---

### Post by geirha on 2009-06-18
In the file .gtk-bookmarks in your home-folder. You can open it in a regular editor and see its contents. 

Alt+F2 -> gedit ~/.gtk-bookmarks
