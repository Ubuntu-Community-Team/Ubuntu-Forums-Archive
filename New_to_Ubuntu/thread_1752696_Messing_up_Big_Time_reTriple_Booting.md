First question: Why did you open a second thread, instead of continuing in your [first thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1751818&goto=newpost")?  

Second question: What is the status of your installs now?  Have you retained everything?  Have you installed Ubuntu to a separate partition or are you still using Wubi? 

On your internet troubles, the internet indicator on the top toolbar will have a fairly typical WiFi signal if it is trying to connect via a wireless network, or two antiparallel arrows if it is trying to connect via a wired connection.  Rather than just hovering over the indicator, try clicking on it and selecting one or t'other, e.g. wired connection or a wireless network.  You can even turn off the wireless networking, for the purposes of using the wired connection if it is available.  

I have read recently on these boards about people having trouble with their wireless/wired connections in 11.04, in a similar way to that you are describing.  So on that front, have a search of the forums for recent threads and posts.
