### Post by spectrevk on 2008-05-22
You can disable the wireless in the laptop usually with a button (mine lights up), or by just shutting it down/restarting. I had this problem last night, actually. Restarting fixed it; logging out does not. If you continue to have Wireless problems, I suggest looking into WICD, which is much better than Network Manager. I used WICD when I was running Gutsy, and it was great. NM has been behaving itself (mostly) in Hardy, so I haven't bothered.
