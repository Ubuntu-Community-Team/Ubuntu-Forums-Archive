### Post by aka-John99 on 2012-06-16
Whilst I do not know what is going on, I will make the comment that although the Ubuntu and indeed the Firefox software is easily & freely available the personal information, including bookmarks is not. 

It does make sense to backup such information. I do not know much about Ubuntu, but IIRC some earlier versions may have been limited to the one firefox version. Firefox is not always too good at keeping software extensions compatible.

Another point is that Firefox does not consider sync to be a backup tool. True the Mozilla Firefox server may retain information, but it is considered any backup is the fact of syncing between your own devices.  

Sorry if my answer is not too helpful, but at least it has served to bump your post.
