### Post by audiomick on 2012-03-10
> **banksiaman said:**
> 
after BIOS the disc runs and says "Crc error press any key to try again" I Try again computer  screen puts up a promt and stays there and with no keyboard access.
This sounds a bit like the CD is no good. If you want to install again, I would suggest making a new one.

Download the .iso again and check the MD5 Sum
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

If you are making a CD, use a slow burning speed.

You might want to look into making a USB installer. 
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick)

I like to have at least one CD or USB around the place in case I need to boot into the live environment for something. That works a lot faster from USB. I believe the installer also runs faster from a USB, although I'm not sure of this. On top of that, once you are finished with it, you have a USB stick that you can re-use instead of a CD that is hard to dispose of rubbish.
