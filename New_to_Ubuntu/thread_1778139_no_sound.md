### Post by Toz on 2011-06-08
Here's how I fixed my issue.
Install pavucontrol (Pulseaudio Volume Control). Start playing the movie, then fireup pavucontrol, go to the playback tab and change output to your HDMI device. From then on in, this setting will be remembered.
