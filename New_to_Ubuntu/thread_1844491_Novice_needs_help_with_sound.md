### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-09-15
After you install Ubuntu, the volume is usually muted. Unmute it from the little speaker icon in the top panel. While there you can check in the Sound Preferences and make sure the "Hardware" and "Output" tabs all look okay.

### Post by dfarrell07 on 2011-09-15
If all of that other great advice does not work, and you are testing your sound by playing videos, like from YouTube, the trouble might be Flash. To fix Flash, first open Ubuntu Software Center, which you can find by searching using the windows-key search feature, or it is on the left Unity bar by default. Search for Adobe Flash Plugin, and install it. You may need to restart your browser and/or computer. Flash almost always works after that.
