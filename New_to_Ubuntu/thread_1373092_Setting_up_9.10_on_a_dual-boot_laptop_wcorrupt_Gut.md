### Post by audiomick on 2010-01-05
Yes to both, at least theoretically. You should also be able to shift any data out of the corrupt Gutsy that you might want to keep. As far as I know, you should be able to access the windows partition from there, and just copy stuff across, or onto an external drive or USB stick.
