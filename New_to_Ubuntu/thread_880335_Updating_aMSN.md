### Post by Paqman on 2008-08-04
Whenever the version of a package in the repos is updated, you'll get the update automatically through the update manager.

Looking at the repos it seems 0.97 is the current version for Ubuntu. The aMSN site has an installer for 0.97.2, but since it isn't a .deb I wouldn't recommend using. It would mean you'd miss out on any future updates, and would mess up the dependency system.

Getdeb has a .deb for 0.97.1, though.
