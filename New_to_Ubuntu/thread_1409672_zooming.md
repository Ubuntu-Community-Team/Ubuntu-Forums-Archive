### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-02-17
Touchpads usually have an area where, if you rub your finger across it, it's like a scroll wheel. Usually the right-hand edge.

Also, you don't just install CompizConfig Settings Manager - you need to actually turn Compiz on. System > Preferences > Appearance and go to "Extra" in the Visual Effects tab. Now turn on Enhanced Desktop Zoom in CompizConfig Settings Manager.
