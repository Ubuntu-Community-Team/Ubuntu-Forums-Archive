### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-01-17
Check your imports on the left pane to see if there is anything for that date . _As far as I know  _, import doesn't equal save to file . Just as if  a document was saved on a removable device it can't be viewed  again unless  device is plugged in or the document is saved to a folder .
