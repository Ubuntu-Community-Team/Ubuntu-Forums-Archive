### Post by mistypotato on 2010-10-17
One of the REALLY great things about Ubuntu is that no matter what your issue, you can usually Google and find a solution.

So I did..........and found this..........

**sudo mv ~/.config/menus/applications.menu ~/.config/menus/applications.menu.bak**

as soon as I ran that in a Terminal...VOILA!  My menus were back !!!


Hope it works for you!
