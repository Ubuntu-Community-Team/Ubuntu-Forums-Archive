### Post by diablo75 on 2009-03-29
1.  Click System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager.
2.  Search for kubuntu-desktop, mark it for installation, and then click the apply button at the top.

Now you just need to change your session type during login to KDE instead of GNOME.  From the login window, click on the options button in the lower-left.  Then click Change Session and select KDE from the menu.  Then login.
