### Post by papibe on 2014-07-24
Hi Quaixemen.

Open 'System Settings', go to Brightness and Lock', and then select 'Never' for the field called 'Turn off screen when inactive'.

You can also, set the field 'Lock' to 'Off'.

Hope it helps. Let us know if that worked.
Regards.
