### Post by bumanie on 2008-06-23
You may find gimpshop easier if you are proficient with photoshop. Gimpshop has had a GUI made to look and work very much like photoshop. As for awn go to System --> Preferences --> Sessions. Click on sessions and click add. In the boxes 
Name: Avant Window Navigator
Command: avant-window-navigator
Comment: Avant Window Navigator
Now it should autostart as soon as the OS is loaded.
