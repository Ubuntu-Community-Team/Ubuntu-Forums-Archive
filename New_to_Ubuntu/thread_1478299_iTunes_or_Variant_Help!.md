### Post by beatlesfan94 on 2010-05-09
Ubuntu comes with Rhythmbox which allows for iPod syncing right out of the box. And as of the latest release of Ubuntu, Lucid Lynx, iPod touch and iPhone syncing is also supported out of the box.

It would rather unnecessary to run iTunes in Wine, and I can't guarantee it would work. Besides, there's a music store built into Rhythmbox called Ubuntu One Music Store that allows you to buy music just as you from the iTunes Store.
