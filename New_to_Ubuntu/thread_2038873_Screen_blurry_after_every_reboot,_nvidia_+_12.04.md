### Post by Wim Sturkenboom on 2012-08-17
I suspect a refresh rate issue.

Did you check your monitor (when you have the garbled screen); it should somewhere tell you which resolution is used as well as vertical and horizontal refresh.

Do the same once your screen displays correctly; difference? If yes, use those settings in Xorg.conf. The flatpanels that I know only use 60Hz vertical refresh.

An alternative might be to empty the monitor section except for the identifier.

No garantee ;)
