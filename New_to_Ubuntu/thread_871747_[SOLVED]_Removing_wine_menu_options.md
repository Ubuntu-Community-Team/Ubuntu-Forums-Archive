### Post by dazzlindonna on 2008-07-27
Finally I can answer something...

**How To Remove Wine Menu Items**
Go to Home folder
View / show all hidden files.
Open .local folder
Open share/applications/wine/Programs
Delete items of choice
Go back to .local folder
Open share/icons
Delete icons of choice
All done.
