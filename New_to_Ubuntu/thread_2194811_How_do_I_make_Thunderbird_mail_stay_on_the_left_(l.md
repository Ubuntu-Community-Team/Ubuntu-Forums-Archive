### Post by devine.steve on 2013-12-21
I see that it is working as you wish now. But in case it disappears on you again:
Start Thunderbird manually ... while it is running right click on the icon in the menu bar - Select "Keep in Dock"
Now when you shut down Tbird it's start up icon will stay in the dock. 
Alternatively you can remove items from the dock by the same method. "Remove from Dock"
