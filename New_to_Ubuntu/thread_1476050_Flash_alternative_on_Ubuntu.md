### Post by Jon Monreal on 2010-05-07
I would say that Flash IDEs for Linux are very primitive compared to Adobe's tools.

There's [UIRA]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/uira/"), which seems to be one of the most advanced, but that's no longer under active development.

There's also (the even more primitive IMO) [Salasaga]("http://osflash.org./salasaga"), and an online [Ajax Animator]("http://antimatter15.com/ajaxanimator/wave/") that can export to SWF.
