### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-04-29
Ubuntu probably already has the driver for your mobile broadband device, but you haven't told it what network to connect to.

Toward the top-right of the screen there's an icon that looks a bit like an ice-cream cone. Click that and then go to "Edit Connections". Click the "Mobile Broadband" tab and click the Add button. Follow the directions and it should recognise your mobile broadband; then all you'll need to do is go back to the ice-cream cone icon and choose your connection.
