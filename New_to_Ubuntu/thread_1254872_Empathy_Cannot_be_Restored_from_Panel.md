### Post by Cheezespread on 2009-08-31
Hi Jopesh , 

You can add the icon to the panel with the below steps :

1.Right click the panel
2.Click on " Add to Panel"
3.Select Application launcher from the selection criteria ( Copy a launcher from Application menu)
4.Double click it and add Empathy from Applications >Internet > Empathy.

If this is what you meant.
