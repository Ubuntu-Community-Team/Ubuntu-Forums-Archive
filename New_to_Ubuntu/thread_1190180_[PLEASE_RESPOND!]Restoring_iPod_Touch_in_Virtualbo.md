### Post by jbrown96 on 2009-06-17
Sorry to break it to you, but there's no way to update an iphone/itouch in virtualbox. when the device reboots, virtualbox can't identify it to know to pass it through to windows. And each time it reboots, it identifies itself differently. You will need to use a computer running itunes natively to fix it.

It's a huge PITA; I tried it on my iphone, and that was fun.
