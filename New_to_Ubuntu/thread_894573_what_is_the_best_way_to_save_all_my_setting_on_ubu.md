### Post by ptn107 on 2008-08-19
All your personal settings are in your home folder in 'hidden' files.  From a terminal you can see them by typing *ls -la* in your home folder.  There is no universal backup solution, though there are great applications that will suit your needs*.

* [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BackupYourSystem](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BackupYourSystem)
