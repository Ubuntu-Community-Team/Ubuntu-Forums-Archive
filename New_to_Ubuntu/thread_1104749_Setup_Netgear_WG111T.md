### Post by joewski on 2009-03-24
I have one of those similar adapters.

Yes you need to configure your password and default connection.

There is a black monitor icon in Ubuntu on the top right hand corner.

If you click on that you will see a number of wireless hotspots to connect to if your lucky. 

1. Pick your router by clicking on button. 
2. You will be asked for a password. This password is set in your router.

3. If you do not filter MAC address on your router then you should be connected. If you do you need to add your devices mac address if you haven't previously.

Should work now.
