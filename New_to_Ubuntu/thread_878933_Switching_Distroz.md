### Post by Average Joe on 2008-08-03
I have no experience with either Alcohol 120% or Rawdump running through Wine. But you might want to check out some Linux alternatives for those. I'd suggest you could use 'rsync' (or [grsync]("http://www.opbyte.it/grsync/") for a GUI) to make any kind of backup. Mounting (iso) images is straightforward with 'mount -o loop' or you could use [Isomaster]("http://www.littlesvr.ca/isomaster/") for that. These are all in the Ubuntu repositories.

When switching distro's it is sometimes good to switch applications as well.
