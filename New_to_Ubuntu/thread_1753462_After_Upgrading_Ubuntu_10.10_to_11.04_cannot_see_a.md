### Post by Paqman on 2011-05-09
The default interface in 11.04 is called Unity and does not use standard Gnome panels. Instead it has the launcher down the left hand side. If you want to get back to the old Gnome desktop with panels top and bottom then log out and choose "Gnome classic" from the sessions options menu at the bottom of the login screen.
