### Post by irv on 2011-02-02
I find most new users to this forum community wait awhile before setting up their profile. He is a quick howto to at lease help you get the basics done.
1) Select the User CP at the top of the forum page.
2) To the Left select Edit Your Details
3) Scroll down the page and fill in your “Ubuntu Distro, by selecting from the drop-down list.
4) Next if you want to add or edit your Avatar select the Edit Avatar on the left.
5) Select Edit Signature next and try to keep it informational and short.
6) Under the Edit Options you can choose to Show or Hide your post count.
There are other things you can do but these are just the basic things I find helpful to other trying to answer your threads and posts. It is nice to know the version of Ubuntu someone is using when trying to help them.
One thing new users might find helpful is to checkout the Forum Help> Forum FAQ at the top of the forum page.
