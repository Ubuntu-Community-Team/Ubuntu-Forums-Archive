It will look best in native resolution but will support others (non-widescreen ones will look odd though).

Will it work with a standard VGA cable? Yes and No. Depends on what ports it uses on the back of the monitor. It'll have DVI / VGA or both. Regardless They will include the cables you need to hook it up. If it's DVI only, it'll have a DVI to VGA adaptor included. ;)

### Post by mgranet on 2009-09-10
Make sure to turn on subpixel smoothing under System Settings-->Appearance-->Fonts. Select Use AA from the dropdown, then go into configure. This will make your fonts nice and smooth on the LCD.
