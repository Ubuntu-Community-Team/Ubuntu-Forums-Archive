### Post by dazvansgillio on 2011-05-18
Go to Edit>Preferences>Mail Preferences and select HTML Messages Tab at the top. Then select always load images from Internet.
If you just want to load images for the currently selected e-mail go to View > Load Images.

### Post by donato roque on 2011-05-18
You may add the contacts who send you images and pictures in your addressbook. Then configure Evolution to automatically show images from these contacts. Or you may just type Cntrl+I.This command applies on just the focus message. 

Images in emails are a favorite vector for virus. You might want to protect yourself and think about the settings in your email client.
