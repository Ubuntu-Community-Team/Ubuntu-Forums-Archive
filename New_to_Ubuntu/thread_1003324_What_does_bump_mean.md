### Post by cdtech on 2008-12-06
Bringing your post to the top dosen't really help, so bumping is frowned upon. By having a false reply to your post (using a bump) makes others think the post has been replied to.

Having a zero response will be answered as quickly as possible and shows others including the "unanswered post team" that the question is still open.
