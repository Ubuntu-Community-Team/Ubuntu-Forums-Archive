### Post by Kareeser on 2009-03-16
Updates are handled by what we call the "package manager". Ubuntu uses a package manager called "Synaptic".

Synaptic will handle the order in which to install the updates. All you have to do is click the little icon in the top right, enter your password, and the computer will do the rest!

So sit back, relax, and have a coffee while your computer is brought up to date :)
