### Post by dwhite on 2011-04-21
Hi,

Go to the Main Menu, choose System-->Preferences-->Keyboard

once the keyboard window opens click the "layouts" tab click "add" to add a new keyboard

pick Japan and Japan Kana variant and add.


Now when you open a document and want to type with Japanese characters choose the keyboard (should be showing up in your notification area of the panel) and type away.

hope this helps.
