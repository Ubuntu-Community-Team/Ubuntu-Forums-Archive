### Post by uberdonkey5 on 2008-08-27
I'm no expert, but I had a brightness problem in my dell when I upgraded to hardy (would only change it in large steps. What I did is right click on the toolbar/panel in a space (the bit where applications, places, system and your icons is listed) and click 'add to panel'. There is a brightness icon in there somewhere (looks like a white sun - called 'brightness applet'). Stick this on your panel and this will allow you to change the brightness.

If you can't find this icon, then I presume there must be some software missing.
