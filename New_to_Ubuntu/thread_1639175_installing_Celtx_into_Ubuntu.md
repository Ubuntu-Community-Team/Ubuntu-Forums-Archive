### Post by cariboo on 2010-12-06
All you have to do is extract the file you downloaded, by double-clicking it, the file archiver will open, and you can select where to extract it in your home directory.

Once you have extracted the files, navigate to the directory and double-click celtx.

To create a launcher on your desktop, right-click anywhere on the desktop and select **Create Launcher**, fill in the details and your done.
