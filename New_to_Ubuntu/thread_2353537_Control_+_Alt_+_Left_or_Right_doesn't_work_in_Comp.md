### Post by #&amp;thj^% on 2017-02-22
(Just for clarity) You can use only one of those at a time IE: Desktop Wall or Desktop Cube...but not both.
To enable these you need to open "compizconfig-settings-manager" and be sure the Desktop Cube is ticked or the Desktop Wall which ever you prefer.
But you need to be sure the "Rotate Cube" is also Ticked for the Option "Desktop Cube" to function.
