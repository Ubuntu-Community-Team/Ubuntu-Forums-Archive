### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-07-12
> **~sHyLoCk~ said:**
> What you are looking for are the gtk2 and metacity themes.

GTK 2 themes are for buttons, scrollbars, text fields, menus and menubars, things like that. Some GTK themes also control the colour of your panel.

Metacity themes control the look of your window borders.
