### Post by impert on 2009-11-12
Since 9.10 there's a black notification box which comes on at every change of track in both Exaile and Rhythmbox. It's not part of either, as far as I know, so I presume it's Pulse or Gnome.
Does anyone know how to get rid of the damn thing?

---

### Post by impert on 2009-11-14
OK, found it. In Exaile, Edit>Preferences>Plugins> and disable "Notify".
This does not disable the (usually coloured) and configurable native Exaile notification, only the black box.

In Rhythmbox you can do it by enabling  the tray icon right-clicking on it, and un-ticking the box marked show notifications. Letting the mouse hover over the tray icon will produce a notification box whenever you want it.

### Post by impert on 2011-04-30
This seems to want correction/updating:
In Rhythmbox you can do it by:
1 If the tray icon is enabled, by right-clicking on it, and un-ticking the box marked show notifications. Letting the mouse hover over the tray icon will produce a notification box whenever you want it.
2 If the tray icon is not enabled,
Edit>Plugins>Status Icon>Configure Status Icon prerferences>Notification>Never Shown
You can then also enable/disable the status icon.
