### Post by mk1w86 on 2010-02-18
Atom CPU's are designed for power efficiency, not performance.  This means many older machines will still outperform an Atom machine and in this case the SSD will not make much difference because of the CPU bottleneck.

You can find many articles on the internet about Atom performance and benchmarks if you're interested. ;)
