### Post by Zill on 2010-05-28
1)  Open "Help" menu in OOo Calc
2)  Select OpenOffice.org Help
3)  Select "Index" tab
4)  Type "removing" in the Search Term box
5)  Double click on "manual column breaks" and/or "manual row breaks"
