### Post by nhasian on 2009-08-17
it sounds like your missing the taskbar at the bottom of the screen.  

At the top bar right click and select "new panel"  now it should appear on the bottom.  Now right click on the new panel and choose "add to panel" and select *Window List*

alternatively you can always switch between open programs with alt-tab or superkey-tab
