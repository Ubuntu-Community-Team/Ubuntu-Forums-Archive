### Post by taurus on 2009-02-19
If you have a separate /home, then you can mount that partition to /home again but do not format it.  All your personal settings will be saved.  But if you only have one partition for /, then all your settings will be gone UNLESS you decide not to format the partition when you reinstall which I don't think it's a good idea.  Always format it so you would have a fresh copy unless you have a reason not to format the partition, /, when you reinstall.
