### Post by synapse13 on 2009-01-17
I have one of those old Sonys also.  I found it helpful to go into the BIOS setup screen before booting the computer to allow the CD-ROM time to power up. (press DEL when "Sony" comes up on the screen to go to the BIOS)   No changes made to BIOS, just go in there for a short bit and then exit to restart the boot process.
