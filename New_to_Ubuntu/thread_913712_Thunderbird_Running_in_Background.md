### Post by jorje_villafan on 2008-10-02
Using Firetray you can fix it to where Thunderbird stays docked after you close the window.... While in Thunderbird go into Tools>Add-ons, find Firetray and click on it. The Preference option should appear. Click on it and there will be three options, check the one the says "Close button minimizes to tray" and it should be fixed. 

This add-on makes using Thunderbird much more inviting.


Good luck.
