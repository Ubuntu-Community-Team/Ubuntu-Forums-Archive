### Post by bogan on 2012-02-03
H1, **KaliMojo**,

You Posted: > thanks. alt + right click works. now how do i remove them ?     Assumimg "them" means the Apps' Launchers you added, then:

To Move or Remove a Launcher from a Panel - not from the LaunchPad, on the left if you have one -  with the cursor on the Icon of the Launcher you wish to alter,  do the same Alt+Right-Mouse Button Press: then select the action you want, in the Drop-down Menu.

To save you asking, If you want to Edit a Launcher, Right-Click on it - without the 'Alt' - and select Properties. 
 
Chao!, **bogan**.
