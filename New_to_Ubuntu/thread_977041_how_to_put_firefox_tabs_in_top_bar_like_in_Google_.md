There's an experimental Add-On for Firefox that does this:
[Tabs on top]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/13091")

There's also [Stylish]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2108") and a [Userstyle]("http://userstyles.org/styles/10750") that will do the tabs-on-top for you.
