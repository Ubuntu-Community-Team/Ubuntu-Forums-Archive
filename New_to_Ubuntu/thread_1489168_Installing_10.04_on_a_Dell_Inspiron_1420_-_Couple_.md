### Post by ScottinSoCal on 2010-06-04
Some Dells use the Intel chipset for wireless, some use the Broadcom. The Intel is flawless, the Broadcom can have some issues. I've got it running on both my personal and my work Dell (Latitude) and it runs great, but both mine are the Intel. I started with 9.10 on my personal laptop, and still run that on my work laptop. My home laptop was upgraded to 10.04.

With 10.04, I've twice had my laptop lock up on resume from suspend. I have to do a hard reset (hold the power button until the laptop shuts off), both times it's come up with all networking disabled. That's been the only real glitch so far - well up until this update thing, and I'm not sure what's going on with that. I'm holding off upgrading my work laptop until 10.04 has been out a little longer. Can't have that down.
