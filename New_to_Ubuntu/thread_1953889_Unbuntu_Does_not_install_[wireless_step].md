Did you give the process some time, before removing your USB key or resetting your computer? Sometimes there can be quite significant delays when the process is working in the background (but not necessarily indicating activity by visual means).

Indication of disk activity is often a sign that *something* is going on, and system has not truly frozen. In such cases I give it a long time (half to one hour) before concluding that it is some kind of deadlock and reset.

So, we need more information from your end as what exactly happened. Was there disk activity? Were any error messages shown? Was your mouse pointer responsive? Did you try restarting installation and trying again?
