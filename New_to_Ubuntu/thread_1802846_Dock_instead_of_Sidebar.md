### Post by wep940 on 2011-07-12
I'm going to assume that when you say Sidebar, you mean that you have installed 11.04 and using the default Unity.  When you click on your username at login, but PRIOR to putting in your password, there is a bar at the bottom of the screen that lets you select the session type.  Just select Ubuntu Classic - this gives you the standard Gnome desktop manager, and you can install things like Cairo dock in Gnome.
