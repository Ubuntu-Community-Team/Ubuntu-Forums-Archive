### Post by ruffEdgz on 2008-06-04
I've only used foobar2k a few times but when I uploaded my files from iTunes (windows) to foobar2k, not all of those tags were perfect as well (some albums, artists, etc.. were missing).

If you are concerned about the metadata tags for your mp3, check out a tag editor like **Ex Falso** or **EasyTAG** to actually see how the tags are displayed (in the standard form). 

I moved my music to Banshee 0.94 beta and only a few of my songs need track numbers.

Hope that helps
