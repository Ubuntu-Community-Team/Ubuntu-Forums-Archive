### Post by Sef on 2009-09-09
That's what it is supposed to be doing.  However, there is an easier way to install flash.

Applications > Add/Remove > Show: All Available Applications > Search: Adobe Flash > tic the box next to Adobe (Macromedia) Flash > click apply changes > click apply > close.
