### Post by morphyrichards on 2013-01-18
Hello again,

I managed to solve my problem, here's how:

1. Uninstall current Nvidia drivers
2. Use Synaptic PM to download Nvidia-experimental-310 and all other necessary packages

important: do not download/install new drivers yourself, I have tried that and it only caused troubles

Case closed.
