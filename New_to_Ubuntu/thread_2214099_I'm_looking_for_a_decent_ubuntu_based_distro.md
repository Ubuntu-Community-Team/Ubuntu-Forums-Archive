### Post by Rob Sayer on 2014-04-01
Xubuntu shouldn't be that slow on such a machine.  I suspect graphic driver issues too.

If you find xubuntu slow installing the Mate desktop is just plain ridicuous.  I've tried it, it's not bad per se, but it's actually quite slow considering it's relatively light.

Anyone posting in the beginners section should absolutely, positively not install an alpha or beta ubuntu release.  It's ridiculous.  I know guys who have been maintaining unix and linux systems for a living for 20 years.  They wouldn't touch an alpha/beta release unless they had to.  And they have the skills to recover from the crash you'll almost certainly get at some point.

There's a lot of very good information about linux on the web.  There's also a lot of *bad* info.  The trouble for newbies is that it takes a while to tell the difference.  Hint: the number of forum posts is actually pretty irrelevent.  I've seen posts here by people with 10 posts who are clearly expert.  And vice versa.
