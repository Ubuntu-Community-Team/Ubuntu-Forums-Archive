### Post by Fuzzv on 2012-06-10
.ashx is an ASP.NET format. Unless you upload it to a Windows-based server and open it in your browser, if it is actually .ashx you probably can't open it.

However, [this article]("http://pcsupport.about.com/od/fileextensions/f/ashxfile.htm") says they are often other formats such as PDF that were saved as ASHX when you downloaded it. Try changing the extension to PDF (or whatever you may have been trying to download) and see if it opens then. Also, if all else fails, just try opening it in as a text file in gedit.
