### Post by drowner on 2008-10-21
Of course you can do it!

I have no experience installing ubuntu next to vista, but i've done it numerous times with XP and Ubuntu.

What you will need to do is

1. Back up everything you can't afford to lose. This is always important.

2. Use vista's partitioning tool to resize the vista partition

3. Boot from the ubuntu CD, and install it!

[http://apcmag.com/how_to_dualboot_vista_with_linux_vista_installed_first.htm](http://apcmag.com/how_to_dualboot_vista_with_linux_vista_installed_first.htm)

Have a look through that tutorial, and post back with any problems or questions. and welcome back!
