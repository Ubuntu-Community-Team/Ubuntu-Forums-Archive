### Post by Meskarune on 2008-04-26
I guess the coolest thing about screenlets is you can add google widgets to your desktop. But I really don't use any desklets, as its just clutter, and I can get the same information online. But each person is different, and you might find a facebook widget or del.icio.us widget on your desktop really useful. 

If you want a launch bar/task manager you could look into kxdocker, which is pretty nice and functional, but with low overhead. 

If you want AWN to start everytime you boot you can add it to your auto-started applications list.
