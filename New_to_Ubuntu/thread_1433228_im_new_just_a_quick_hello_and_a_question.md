### Post by Helix7 on 2010-03-18
Hi I have the 1gb western digital book world as well.

I just connect to mine with the Places > Connect to Server.

Service type is Windows Share, Because the book runs samba.

Fill out your details and click connect. 


There is a good wiki that describes how to do some really cool stuff with your book.
A few examples of the things I have done with my book are.
Installed Transmission Bit-torrent client with the web interface thereby centralizing all torrent downloads in one place.
Installed IRC Ratbox for a local LAN instant messaging.
Got SSH Access to the drive.
Disabled resource hog Mionet.

There are lots of things you can do with that drive I personally love it. 
Here is the link to the wiki. [http://mybookworld.wikidot.com/](http://mybookworld.wikidot.com/)

Enjoy and make sure to backup any data you have on the drive before playing with it.
