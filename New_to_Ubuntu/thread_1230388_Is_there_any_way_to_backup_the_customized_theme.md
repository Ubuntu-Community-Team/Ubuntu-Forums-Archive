### Post by Regenweald on 2009-08-03
Themes are stored in /Home/.themes
You can back up the files in there, but remember on a fresh install you must have the relevant theming engines and controls etc installed for it to work. No worries though, if you put your old theme in and something is missing, it will let you know what it is.
