### Post by howefield on 2010-12-12
If you are trying to reclaim the hard disk space that you used for your virtual machine, it is likely stored in your home folder.

Go to Places > Home, and press Ctrl + H keys together to show your hidden folders.

Navigate to your Virtualbox folder and delete, if you do not need it any longer.
