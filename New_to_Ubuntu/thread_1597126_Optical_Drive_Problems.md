### Post by Ctrl-Alt-F1 on 2010-10-15
On my laptop Ubuntu used to have a hard time using my combo drive (cd-rw, dvd-rw).  The problem stemmed from a defective firmware that Asus had provided with the laptop.  I upgraded the firmware for my drive and I've never ever had a problem again (in Windows or Ubuntu).
