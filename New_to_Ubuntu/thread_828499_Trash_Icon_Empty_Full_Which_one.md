### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-06-13
The trash icon on your desktop is not a regular icon.
You can't just choose one from an icon list and expect it to work, because it won't.

The best way to do it is to choose an icon theme and install that theme. It will then set-up the trash icon on your desktop to that particular theme.

However, if you just want to change the trash icon and leave the theme untouched, this link may be of help to you:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=99030](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=99030)
