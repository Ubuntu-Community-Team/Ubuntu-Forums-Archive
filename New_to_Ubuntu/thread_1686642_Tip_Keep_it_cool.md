### Post by dalekirkwood on 2011-02-12
Hi All,

Just thought I would let you all know I recently purchased a laptop cooling pad - []("http://shop.ebay.co.uk/?_from=R40&_trksid=p5197.m570.l1313&_nkw=laptop+cooler&_sacat=See-All-Categories") - I got mine from poundland and they are only £3/4 on Ebay with shipping but basically I ran some tests using HardInfo 0.5.1 (Which is a great tool) on the temp of my AMD Sempron M100 2GHz CPU. The temp started at 50 Degrees and after about 1 minute it was down to 47 Degrees, a slice of pizza later (about 5 minutes) it had dropped to 40 Degrees. I then decided to run the CPU at a full 2 GHz and it managed to keep the temp down with only a 1 - 2 degree rise. I run a HP which I find are really good with heat but I know Acer's and some Toshiba's can seriously struggle with overheating. A definite recommendation for anyone who wants a cooler laptop.
