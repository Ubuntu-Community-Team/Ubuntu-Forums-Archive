### Post by verymadpip on 2011-09-18
Hello PhibreOptix.

The dialogue box you want is brought up by right clicking the panel & selecting panel settings.

You will need to adjust the appearance & geometry.

Set appearance to solid colour (with opacity) & that will remove the horizontal lines, but you may have to fiddle with the colour until you get something you like, as the shading will disappear.

In geometry just change the width of the panel to your liking.

There are usually helpful people on the #lubuntu irc channel on freenode.

Hope this helps & good luck.

P.S.  It may be easier to create a new panel & add all the items manually.  Be sure to check where the menu icon image comes from as I think you get a different one by default if you create a new panel.  You can find that by opening the "menu settings" dialogue.
