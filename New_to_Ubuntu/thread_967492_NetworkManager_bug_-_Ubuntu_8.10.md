### Post by Paqman on 2008-11-02
Bugs in Ubuntu packages are handled through a website called Launchpad.

For example: [Launchpad bugs in network-manager](https://bugs.launchpad.net/network-manager/+bugs?field.searchtext=&search=Search+Bug+Reports&field.scope=project&field.scope.target=network-manager) or in [network-manager-applet](https://bugs.launchpad.net/network-manager-applet/+bugs?field.searchtext=&search=Search+Bug+Reports&field.scope=project&field.scope.target=network-manager-applet).

If you spot a bug that hasn't been reported yet, feel free to do so yourself. You can also subscribe to get updates on any outstanding bugs.

This is one of the nice things about open source. The development process is really transparent, so you can see what's being done.
