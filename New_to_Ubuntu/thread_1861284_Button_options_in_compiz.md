---

### Post by Lisiano on 2011-10-15
1 - Left click
2 - Middle click
3 - Right click
4 - Scroll UP
5 - Scroll Down
6 - Scroll sideways
7 - Scroll sideways
8 - 1st side button
9 - 2nd side button
n - n-7 side button

Basically every button after 7 is a side button, not every mouse has those, 8 and 9 usually correspond to "Back" and "Forward" if you don't put anything on them.
