### Post by mapes12 on 2010-06-05
> My question is this: How often does an Ubuntu LTS get new hardware support added on?
Hardware support is included in the Linux kernel, irrespective of which version of Ubu you use provided that it is still a maintained version.

First, I would check how you have configured your HDD. If it's not partitioned properly or you have formatted with a a version of ext that your drive doesn't like (ext 4 in my case) then that may be causing the problem. Check this out:- [http://members.iinet.net.au/~herman546/index.html](http://members.iinet.net.au/~herman546/index.html)
