### Post by northern lights on 2008-10-24
> **niyonk said:**
> I do not think you can delete all panels, 
You certainly can.

Hit Alt+F2 and execute ```
gnome-panel
```
to get it/them back for the session.

Thereupon, naviagte to "System > Preferences > Sessions" and add *gnome-panel* to the StartUp Programs.
