### Post by hakermania on 2012-06-25
Hello
Click on upper right on the icon and choose 
"start up applications'
Than Click On 'Add'
In the name field place: Hamster
In the command field place hamster-indicator
and in the comment whatever you want.
Click OK and you're done :)
