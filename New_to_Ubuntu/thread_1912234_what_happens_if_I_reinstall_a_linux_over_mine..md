### Post by Lars Noodén on 2012-01-20
If you have a separate /home partition, then you can choose to manually partition the disk and tell it not to format that partition.  

If you do not have a separate /home partition, then you will have to back up to external media (a good idea regardless) in order to save your settings.

Make a separate /home partition if you haven't one already.
