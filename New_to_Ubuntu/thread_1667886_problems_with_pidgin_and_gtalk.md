### Post by wog on 2011-01-19
Apparently, in gtalk and pidgin, the answer is:
1. Make sure the people you want to chat with are in your contacts list
2. make sure gmail chat is activated
3. download a copy of gtalk for windows and run it in wine
4. make sure the person you want to chat with is listed in gtalk under wine
5. shut down gtalk under wine
6. go back to pidgin, where you will see your contact in the list
