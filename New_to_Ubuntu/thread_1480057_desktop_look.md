### Post by Calash on 2010-05-11
Not familair with "Candy Bar", but if you mean the dock you have several options.

[Cairo-Dock (glx-dock)](http://www.glx-dock.org/)
[Avant Window Navigator (AWN)](http://code.google.com/p/avant-window-navigator/)
[Docky (Gnome-Do)](http://do.davebsd.com/wiki/Docky)

There are others as well, but these three I have had experience with.  Personally I love Cairo-Dock but I really have nothing bad to say about any of them.
