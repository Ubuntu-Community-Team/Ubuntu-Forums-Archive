I assume you are talking about autoscrolling in your web browser (as far as I know that's not a system feature even on Windows, but can be donw either by applicatiosn themselves, or in some cases through a setting provided by 3rd-party mouse drivers).

Anyway, if you wan autoscrolling in Firefox, just go to preferences->Advanced->General and mark the "Use autoscrolling" checkbox.
