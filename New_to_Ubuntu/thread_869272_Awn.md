### Post by purplepaint on 2008-07-24
If you have AWN running, right-click on it and click on "Dock Preferences".

A window should open, where you can click on "launchers" on the left-hand side to add application launchers, and in the "General" section, check the box next to "Auto hide bar when not in use".

### Post by Bodsda on 2008-07-24
The easiest way to add launchers (icons) to AWN is to drag and drop. Open a menu, find an application you want in AWN then click and hold on that icon then drag it over the dock and drop, it should add the launcher to the dock.

Hope this helps
