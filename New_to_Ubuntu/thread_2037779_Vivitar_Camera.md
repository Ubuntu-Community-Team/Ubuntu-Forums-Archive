### Post by Hadaka on 2012-08-05
Hi, plug your camera into the USB port, then bring up a terminal   ctrl/alt/t
enter:
```
 gstreamer-properties 
```

when the box comes up..select VIDEO tab,....upper left
then click TEST ..lower right.....if the camera shows an image
then you  will need to read the instructions from skype on your
type camera set up....or google your camera,your computer,skype
or someone on here may be able to help you. I dont use skype but
at least the command i gave you will let you test your camera.
good luck
