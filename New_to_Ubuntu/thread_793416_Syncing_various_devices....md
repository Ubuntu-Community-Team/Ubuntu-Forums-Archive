### Post by asrai on 2008-05-13
how are you trying to sync your phone? via usb cable, bluetooth?  Can you tell me a little more about what is happening when you try and access data stored on the phone's memory from your computer?

I synchronise two SE phones (one is mine, one is my husband's) with ubuntu using multisync over bluetooth.  This program allows me to sync contacts, calendar and tasks.  

To copy data to/from your phone, if you have bluetooth you can right click on the bluetooth icon on your computer and browse nearby devices.  You just need to hop into preferences from the right-click menu and make sure all the options are checked.

If you're connecting via usb you should have the option to view the internal phone memory also but if you can tell me what is coming up I can try and help further.
