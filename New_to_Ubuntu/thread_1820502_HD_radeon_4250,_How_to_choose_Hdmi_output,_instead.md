### Post by LowSky on 2011-08-08
Hello welcome to the forums.

As for your video issue: Pick one or the other, you do not need both VGA and HDMI. Disconnection of VGA should make the system automatically pick HDMI.

There isn't going to be an option for HDMI or VGA. It is going to have an option for which screen to use, usually saying one is HDMI or digital, the other will say VGA or analog. Just turn one off if necessary.
