This is actually normal and expected behavior, it's registering the keystrokes but doesn't echo them back to the screen.

This way someone looking over your shoulder can't see how many characters your password is. It's also nice when posting code that asked for your password, as it will just show a blank line.

### Post by joshmuffin on 2008-12-23
It doesn't show that your typing a password but don't worry because you are.
Just type it and press enter. I hope that helped. If you are getting error's about a wrong password then try changing it? System > Administration > Users and Groups. Unlock it (which will need your password. Hopefully when you enter it everything will work) then highlight your user and click properties. Make sure "Set password by hand is selected. Then enter the password you want, confirm it and click Ok.

Hope this was helpful, Joshmuffin:)
