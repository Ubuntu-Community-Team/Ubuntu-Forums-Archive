You can also download the packages you're interested in to your USB drive,then double click the .deb files and you'll install the SW you want.
However there could be a dependency problem,in that case you can't install a newer version of the SW you want on your current version of Ubuntu,if this is the case and you still want the nwer versions of th SW you'll have to upgrade your version of Ubuntu :)
