### Post by mr.thraz on 2013-01-23
while mixxx allows  one to DJ digitally, there is nothing out there like mixmeister.

mixxx is like traktor, seranto, dekadance, or virtual DJ

mixmeister allows one to create traditional mixes in a daw like environment.

the closest thing would be app's like ableton live  where live mixes are the point,

but  mixmeister keeps the traditional DJ paradigm while giving you a d.a.w. environment.

there's nothing else like it, not in the UNIX, Windows or Macintosh world.
