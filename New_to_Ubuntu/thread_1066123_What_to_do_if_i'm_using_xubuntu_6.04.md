### Post by jpoRS on 2009-02-10
Upgrade to a newer version.  If that isn't the cause of your current problem, it will be the cause of future problems.  Ubuntu has a new release every six months, and for the most part old releases stop being supported three months after they are replaced*.

There are various ways to get the freshest Ubuntu, but the easiest (depending on your resources) is to download it from [here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download").  Then burn it to a disc (as an image not a file, let us know if you need help with that)

* There are Long Term Support (LTS) releases that have a much longer support cycle.  The current LTS release is 8.04 and is found at the above link as well.

Good luck and welcome to the community!
Peace!
