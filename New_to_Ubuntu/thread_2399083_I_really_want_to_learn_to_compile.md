### Post by oldrocker99 on 2018-08-22
I have compiled a number of programs over the years. The last one I compiled was Aqualung, the gapless music player which is my go-to music layer. Many of the libraries were available from the repos, but I had to compile GTK2, ffmpeg, and libxml. Just saying that you will likely learn the meaning of "dependency hell."

And remember that the libraries you need to compile all have a -dev suffix.
