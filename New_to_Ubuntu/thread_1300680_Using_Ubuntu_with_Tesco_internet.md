### Post by clive littlewood on 2009-10-25
Hi

When you have downloaded 9.04 boot from the CD and you will be able to "Try Ubuntu without altering your current system" as a Live CD.

When your happy click the install icon on your desktop and Ubuntu will install it'self.

When you arrive at the partitioning screen you will be offered the dual boot option alongside XP.

Then when you boot you will either have XP or Ubuntu.

Hope this helps and good luck !!!

Clive
