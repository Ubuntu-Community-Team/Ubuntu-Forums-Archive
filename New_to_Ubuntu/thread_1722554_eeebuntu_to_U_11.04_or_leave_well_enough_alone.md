### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-04-06
Given that, Ubuntu has no way to roll-back to a previous version, I would avoid upgrading to a pre-release version.

If you're willing to go to the trouble to do an image backup and then have a way to restore from that, it's worth tryiung it out to see what it gives you.

But, if you don't have a restorable backup and the Beta "breaks" your current install, you will have to reinstall from scratch to get back a working system.
