### Post by Chemical Imbalance on 2009-07-20
If you are using firefox, install the  Adblock Plus add-on: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1865](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1865)

You could also be using an ISP that uses phorm or phorm-like technology: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phorm](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phorm)
If you find out your ISP is using phorm, then dump them.

If you are using Windows, you may have spyware or other malware.

### Post by CatKiller on 2009-07-20
Adblock Plus is in the repositories. Another +1, though.

Another useful things to do is to find a custom hosts file and change your /etc/hosts accordingly. These work by redirecting a list of know ad servers to your own computer. Since your computer isn't serving ads you won't get them displayed, and you don't have to spare the bandwidth downloading ads that you don't want to see in the first place.

EDIT: Another +1 for rejecting third-party cookies. We obviously think alike, Chemical Imbalance, although you do it quicker :)
