### Post by geirha on 2008-06-23
It sounds like your keyboard isn't work during the boot. There's usually an option for this in the BIOS, regarding USB keyboards. Could this be the case for you?

### Post by ukripper on 2008-06-23
This definitely keyboard issue. unplug and plug your keyboard back and try again.

If it is wireless keyboard check batteries and then try to reconnect your wireless keyboard to its access point.
