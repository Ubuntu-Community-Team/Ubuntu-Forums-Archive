### Post by ohzopants on 2009-08-17
Assuming you have either the proper data cable or bluetooth set up, you should check out a program called wammu (which also has a gtk frontend called gammu (?)).

Both are in the repos and it works beautifully with my phone which is also a nokia.

Got to admit though that I use it for calendar/contacts management, I've never tried managing media with it.  I still think this is your best bet for starting off.

Another phone management program is called bitpim (also in the repos), but I believe that's designed mostly from CDMA phones (not sure why they're different).
