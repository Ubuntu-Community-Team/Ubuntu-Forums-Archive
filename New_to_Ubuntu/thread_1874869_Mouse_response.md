### Post by doctorbighands on 2011-11-04
From within that Update Manager window you have open, try doing the following:

1) Click the Settings button (bottom left), then enter your password
2) Click on the Other Software tab
3) Untick the boxes next to the ppa sources for f.lux (the ones mentioned in the error message you get when trying to update)
4) Click Close, then Reload

With any luck, you should be good to go!
