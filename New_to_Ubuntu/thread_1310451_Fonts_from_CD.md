### Post by kostkon on 2009-11-01
Then, you could do it like this:
copy the fonts you want from the CD to a folder on your desktop and then either double-click on each one to install it or just copy and then paste them altogether in your hidden *.fonts* folder.

To access this hidden folder (the *.fonts* one) just go to your home, then select *View &#8594; Show Hidden Files*.

If the folder does not exist, then just create it yourself.
