### Post by mwcrowley on 2010-02-20
more info would be helpful.  But I'm guessing you installed the program and have not subscribed to a usenet access service like giganews.com   If you have and you have entered your name and password in the pan configuration, then I would check that the correct ports are forwarded.  If you have a subscription to a provider like giganews, doublechecked your password and login name, forwarded the ports on your router and/or firewall, and you are still not getting a connection, then check the faq or support page on your service provider.
cheers.
