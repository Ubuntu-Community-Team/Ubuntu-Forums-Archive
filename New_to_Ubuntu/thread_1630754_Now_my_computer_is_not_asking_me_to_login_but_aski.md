### Post by 12apennachi on 2010-11-25
I am pretty sure that if you go to System-> Preferences-> Network Connections and choose the network selection you use, hit edit and in the bottom left corner "Available to all users" Then the keyring shouldn't pop-up... I think. 
Also, go to System-> Admin-> Login screen and options will be available there for login.

Edit: If your keyring is the same as your login it automatically activates the keyring for use after you login with your password. Probably.

Edit2: After rereading it says you chose "don't ask for password on login," but it sounds like you do want it to ask you for your password.
