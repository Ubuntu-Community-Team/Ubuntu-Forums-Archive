### Post by ozzy6900 on 2012-06-25
I upgraded from 10.04 to 12.04 two weeks ago. I ran into a couple if insignificant error messages as the upgrade was progressing but nothing disastrous. When my laptop booted (it took a little time to finish up) I was running 12.04 with no real problems. The WiFi was operational along with all of the settings. I did have to upgrade Virtual Box to run my few Windows programs. I also lost my backgrounds from 10.04 - no big deal. Other than a learning curve, I have had no problems at all.
