### Post by OM NOM NOM on 2011-06-01
I'd say that you might want to steer clear of it. PowerPC platforms are like the ibook are a secondary and unsupported architecture. While there are PowerPC ports of Ubuntu it is largely community driven. You also will be unable to use flash and certain other multimedia codecs with it. 

If you were to obtain an iBook G3 the best thing to run on it would be an earlier version of OSX, 10.3 or so.
