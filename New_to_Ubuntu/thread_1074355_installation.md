### Post by Peter09 on 2009-02-19
Just to follow up on what Doug11 has said.

The LiveCD runs from your CD drive and makes no changes to your system at all. Only when you are ready to install Ubuntu would you have to make a change to Hard drive. Its a good way to get a feel for Ubuntu, although it is a bit slower due to the speed of the CD drive.
