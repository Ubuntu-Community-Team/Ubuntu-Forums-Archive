### Post by TheFu on 2018-09-25
[https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/keyboard-layouts.html.en](https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/keyboard-layouts.html.en) just in case you didn't find it already.  
The <super> + space will change keyboard layouts.  Just keep holding down the <super> key after you hit the spacebar a few times. An overly keyboard layout chooser should show up.

Next, it seems that only 1 Japanese input method works via ibus. [http://www.localizingjapan.com/blog/2014/05/25/japanese-input-on-ubuntu-linux-14-04-lts-trusty-tahr/](http://www.localizingjapan.com/blog/2014/05/25/japanese-input-on-ubuntu-linux-14-04-lts-trusty-tahr/)

Could be important to know which flavor and release of Ubuntu you are using. IDK, but it seems important in that 2nd link.
