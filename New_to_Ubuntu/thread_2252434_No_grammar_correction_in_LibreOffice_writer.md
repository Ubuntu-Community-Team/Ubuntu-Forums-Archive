### Post by Dan_L. on 2014-11-11
I have no idea why there is no grammar correction in Libreoffice writer. For example, if I deliberately write "he do not play video games", when I check for grammar error by pressing F7, it shows no error since all the spellings are correct. But obviously there is an error in grammar (corrected one: he does not play video games"). Also, Writter neither correct misused word. For example if I say, "A ant is eating my cake.", it does not point out that I should use "An" instead of "A". And if I say "Their do not play video games", it does not point out that I should use "They" instead of their. Do I need to enable something in the setting to make the grammar and spelling checker work or is LibreOffice simply not smart enough regarding checking that kind of mistake?

### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-11-11
Open a document (not a blank one) and select Tools> Spelling and Grammar  (F7) and in the dialog open Options and make sure that "check grammar as you type" is ticked. If it is ticked, then I do not know what to say.
