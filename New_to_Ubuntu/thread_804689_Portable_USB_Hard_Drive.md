### Post by bumanie on 2008-05-23
Can't give you a precise answer, however I have a samsung 2.5" laptop hdd in an external case, bought separately - it works fine. I have read varying things about proprietary ready-made usb hdd's. Seagate freeagent does not seem to work well with ubuntu according to some things I have read. I recently read about someone not getting much joy from from a pre-made western digital external hdd. I have no first hand experience with any of these, so can only suggest you use caution before buying anything and check with the retailer or manufacturer whether the drive you are buying is meant to work with linux or not.
