### Post by xPr0star on 2009-08-21
I also use the WRT160n. They work fine, especially for home/office things. I don't know why you would need the cd i threw it out. All the cd does is spoon feed you pictures of how to plug in wires. That part is pretty self explanatory. The feature its supposed to have with the button on the front is useless though. It's supposed to allow new devices to be able to "sync" better with the router. Useless. The sleek compact design is nice to. It also has good range i can go to the end of my driveway with my iTouch and pick up wireless(a good 150 yards+). Security is ok on it, you cant expect much security on a commercial access point. The wireless n network is also nice much better speed. Best of luck with it, its easy to set up just plug in your internet, plug in anything if it's going wired and plug it into the wall. Also for the router setting type 192.168.1.1 in your browsers address bar. Default username and pw i think are admin(un and pw) it could also be blank i cant remember because i changed it immediately.
