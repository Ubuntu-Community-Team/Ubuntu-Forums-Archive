Well, I'm pretty sure that it'll work just fine, first go ahead and check the drivers for compatibility with 9.04 on google before doing anything, and also, go ahead and burn a LiveCD of 9.04 too just to see how that will work out... Personally, I think doing a fresh install'd be best but nonetheless it'll probably work fine. Also remember that you're going to have to go through 8.10 then to 9.04, unless you do the fresh install..
If you don't wanna fresh install tell him to go ahead and run "Update Manager" From "System->Administration->Update Manager" and check all options, should keep all his drivers and install 8.10 then 9.04 with hopefully no problems...

### Post by Slim Odds on 2009-08-13

But for most everything else, definitely try the LiveCD first.
