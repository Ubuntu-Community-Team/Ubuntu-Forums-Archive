### Post by SunnyDan on 2010-10-23
ok, first, "eyecandy" is just a term for visual effects, it's not a program. second, to get to the cube usually you either press down on the scroll wheel, or hold both left and right click at once.
lastly, I'm no expert at installing things in ubuntu either, but i'm pretty sure you have to extract your said themes to your .themes folder under your home folder, then i think it will appear under your chooseable themes. hope this helps.
