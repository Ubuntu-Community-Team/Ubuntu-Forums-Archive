### Post by Cheesemill on 2009-07-05
If you really want to customize the colouring of your BASH prompt then take a look into modifying your ~/.bashrc file. Details can be found here:
[http://tldp.org/HOWTO/Bash-Prompt-HOWTO/](http://tldp.org/HOWTO/Bash-Prompt-HOWTO/)

For example I have different coloured prompts depending on the user (green for normal and red for when I've done a sudo -i).
