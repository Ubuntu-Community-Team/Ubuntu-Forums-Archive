### Post by txcrackers on 2009-01-20
Locate "Plasma Workspace" in the *KDE Component* drop-down. Click on the **>** next to *Application Launcher* and (try to) set your custom key. If the key isn't recognized, you may want to try changing your keyboard layout to something that is either your exact keyboard or something that's like it (Regional & Language -> Keyboard Layout). That's how I got my Logitech Wave multi-media keys working, anyway...
