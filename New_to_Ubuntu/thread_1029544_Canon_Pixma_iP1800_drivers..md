### Post by NewJack on 2009-01-20
From [www.openprinting.org:](www.openprinting.org:)

[http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-PIXMA_iP1800](http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-PIXMA_iP1800)

States that the printer works perfectly with this driver:

[FONT=ms sans serif, helvetica] [B]IJ Printer Driver Ver. 2.70 for Linux

[/B]Here is the drivers page for the Canon.com.au site:

[http://support-au.canon.com.au/EN/search?canonsearch=1&lang=EN&category=Printers&series=Colour+Bubble+Jet+Printers&model=PIXMA+iP1800&menu=Download](http://support-au.canon.com.au/EN/search?canonsearch=1&lang=EN&category=Printers&series=Colour+Bubble+Jet+Printers&model=PIXMA+iP1800&menu=Download)

The iP1800 drivers are available as .rpm files (no .debs listed).  You convert those to .debs though using ALIEN which is available in the Repos.  

Here is how to convert a .rpm with Alien:

[http://ubuntu.wordpress.com/2005/09/23/installing-using-an-rpm-file/](http://ubuntu.wordpress.com/2005/09/23/installing-using-an-rpm-file/)

Good luck!!!
[/FONT]
