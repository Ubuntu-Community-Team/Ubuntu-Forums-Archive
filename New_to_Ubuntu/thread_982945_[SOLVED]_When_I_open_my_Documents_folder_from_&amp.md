### Post by backflip on 2008-11-15
I had a similar problem after upgrading to 8.10, except Realplayer wanted to open ALL the folders in 'Places'...documents/Home Folder/Music etc.etc.
I had to go to 'places' then computer/file system/home and so on down to the affected folders where I right clicked, chose 'properties' and changed the 'open with' from Realplayer to 'open folder.'
