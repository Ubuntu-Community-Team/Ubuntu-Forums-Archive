### Post by u.b.u.n.t.u on 2009-11-26
**ANNOYANCE**

Google search "Query Suggestions" enabled by default and setting Google preferences to "Do not provide query suggestions in the search box." not working.



**SOLUTION**

Set home page to [http://www.google.com/webhp?complete=0&hl=en](http://www.google.com/webhp?complete=0&hl=en)



**TURN OFF FIREFOX SUGGESTIONS**

1. click Edit
2. click Preferences
3. click Privacy
4. set "Firefox will: Use settings for history"
5. untick "Remember search and form history"



**CONCLUSION**

With a bit of luck you can now use google to search the internet without Google and Firefox annoying you with their intrusive "suggestions".
