when using your mobile phone to connect to the internet for your computer, you are using the phone as a modem. As long as the phone contains the facility then you may just need to know the settings in ppp or whatever, to connect. 
However, often this facility also needs to be enabled or turned on, back at your phone service provider, where unfortunately it is so rare and unusual that they might not know what you are talking about.
