### Post by scorp123 on 2008-07-21
> **cosine352 said:**
> Graphics: Intel® Graphics Media Accelerator X3100
I was just wondering if compiz will work on this thing. Yes, like all Intel cards from the i8xx series onwards. BUT: Don't expect to run any fast-paced 3D games on such hardware. While the hardware is good enough for eye-candy (I myself use Compiz-Fusion on Intel GMA-945 and 950 cards ... these are the precursors to your X3xxx cards!) it definitely isn't for 3D games.
