Just wondering if anyone else has had this problem. I'm using a Samsung SyncMaster 2220wm, and during video play back I'm getting horrible screen tear (not sure if this is the right term for it... little lines when there seems to be anything intensive going on). I'm not sure if it's the monitor, the playback software or the hardware... While video really shouldn't matter (video card wise), I'm running a GTX260, and using VLC. I'm pretty stumped on this one. ---

### Post by BloGTK on 2008-08-14
It's caused by Compiz (the 3D desktop effects manager) - you can disable 3D effects in the Appearances control panel and the tearing goes away.

### Post by erickghint on 2008-08-14

### Post by erickghint on 2008-08-15
I did a little research and found out a solution that seems to be working ok for me. In the compiz menu I enabled sync to v blank and it looks like it got rid of the tearing for now. Thanks so much for the quick answer =), otherwise I'd still be pondering video drivers.
