### Post by diablo75 on 2009-01-20
I've found that it's often easier to setup a static IP by having your router reserve an IP for your PCs network adapters' unique MAC address, although not all routers have this feature (mine is a netgear and it does).

Port forwarding is also configured within your router.  You can find more information about how to setup port forwarding by visiting [http://portforward.com/routers.htm](http://portforward.com/routers.htm)
