### Post by Billisnice on 2010-10-08
I found the answer. IF you have a flip video with h.264 go to ubuntu software center and install Kdenlive. Take some time to look at the online help. Move windows around to see online help on one side of the screen with Kdenlive on the other side to make it easier to see the program with the instructions. The instruction are a little different that the new version but enough to get you started.

When finished render to Google WebP format. It works great in youtube.
