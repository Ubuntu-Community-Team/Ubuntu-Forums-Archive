### Post by jellybaby on 2010-07-25
Hi,

this worked for me with my Nvidia card (different chip - FX 5200 - but it's worth a try):
">System>Administration>Hardware Drivers".
The system should list drivers for your card, with one recommendation. Just click on that, and all should be well. Afterwards, you can do
">System>Administration>NVIDIA X server settings" and arrange things for your card and monitor.

Good luck
