### Post by DezSP on 2008-04-28
Hi Jason,

To get the Deleted Items icon on your desktop, first open up a terminal. Type in gconf-editor, then browse to apps -> nautilus -> desktop. Tick the entry marked "trash_icon_visible". The effect should be immediate. Good luck!

Edit: Oh, and to get rid of it from the lower panel, just right click and choose "Remove from panel". :)
