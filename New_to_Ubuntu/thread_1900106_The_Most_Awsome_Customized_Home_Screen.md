### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-12-25
I suggest that you make a note of what you do, what commands you run, etc., and then when you break something you know what you have to undo to get back to where you started from.

Sometimes people post here asking for help and they say something like: I can't remember what I did but now all I get is a black screen." The simple answer to that situation is to re-install but they do not want to hear that.

So, make sure you have a backup of any important data. There are some very clever people on this forum but they cannot work miracles even over Christmas.
