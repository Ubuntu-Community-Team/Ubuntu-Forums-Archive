### Post by cryptotheslow on 2012-08-14
I recommend you set a password back on your acount using the recovery mode.

There is a walkthrough on how to do that here:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/resetpassword](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/resetpassword)

You can then set your account to automatically login without entering a password here:
[http://www.liberiangeek.net/2012/05/windows-7-vs-ubuntu-12-04-how-to-login-automatically/](http://www.liberiangeek.net/2012/05/windows-7-vs-ubuntu-12-04-how-to-login-automatically/)

However if you encrypted your home directory when installing auto-login will not be very helpful as your passphrase is required to de-crypt your files and folders.
