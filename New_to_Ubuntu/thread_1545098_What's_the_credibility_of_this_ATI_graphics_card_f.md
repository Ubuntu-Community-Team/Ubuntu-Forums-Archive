### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-08-03
It's not actually a "card fix", it's a patch to the ATI proprietary drivers -- and one that appears to deal only with a lag problem, and even then, only with 58xx cards, and maybe only 5850 cards.

Perhaps someone else here has tried this already and can tell you about their experiences.

But if not, you can always check in with the Phoronix forums.  They do a LOT of detailed work with ATI cards and their drivers.
