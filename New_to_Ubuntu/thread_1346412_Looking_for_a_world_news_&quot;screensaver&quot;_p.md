A while back when I was using Windows, I used to have this "World News Screensaver" that I bought for about 10 dollars.  What it did was render a 3D globe of the earth, and then transposed news articles one after another on top in relation to where the news was occuring.  It would pull the news articles from several RSS feeds from sources like the AP, Reuters, BBC, and others.  It's kind of similar to the way the Nintendo Wii now presents news to the user.  You are shown on a map where the news is actually happening.

I was wondering if there is anything like this in Linux.  It doesn't have to be a screen-saver, per say, but if it can auto-scroll through several different news stories from multiple sources and project them over a map of some kind, that would be awesome!

Here's where I got the screensaver I mentioned above:  [http://www.3dwonders.com/product.php?directory=worldnewsscreensaver](http://www.3dwonders.com/product.php?directory=worldnewsscreensaver)
