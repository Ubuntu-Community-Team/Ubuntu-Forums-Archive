### Post by swoody on 2009-03-03
I have installed Ubuntu on 2 HP laptops, and a Compaq that were all using the Broadcom wireless adapters, and just recently, my fiancée's Toshiba Satellite. They have all done superbly out of the box, and the Toshiba - surprisingly - had EVERYTHING working on the install disc. 

That being said, if you dig deep enough, you're going to find cases where 'everything just works', and horror stories about the same manufacturers. I'd recommend to narrow down your search a bit, and once you find something you like, I'd recommend posting the hardware specs on that lappy here, and let people give you feedback about your particular model. You can also check out these links for more info on supported hardware. Try searching these for the hardware your prospective laptop has:
[http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/](http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/)
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=361236](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=361236)
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=361237](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=361237)
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport)

Best of luck on your hunt :)
