### Post by anewguy on 2012-09-08
Sometimes these little dudes require a sync before they are recognized.  If you do as fantab suggests and just unplug/plug the adapter back in it will go through syncing with the devices again.  Sometimes you may have to turn the mouse off/on and then click the left button to wake it up also.  Not sure if there really is anything that can be done.  I'll do some searching and see if anything looks promising.

EDIT:  Forgot......there should also be a little button on the bottom of the mouse and the bottom of the keyboard you can push to sync each back with the transceiver.
