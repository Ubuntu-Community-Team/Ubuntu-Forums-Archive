### Post by Ripfox on 2008-05-31
You can customize your login screen by going to [http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/)

then download a "gdm" theme.

Go to System/Administration/Login Window and click on the tab for the actual theme for the login window and you can actually drag and drop the theme file onto that.
