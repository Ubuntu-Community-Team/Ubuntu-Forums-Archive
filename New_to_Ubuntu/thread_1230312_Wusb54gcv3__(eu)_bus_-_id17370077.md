### Post by Hospadar on 2009-08-03
You may want to take a look here:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)
It's a pretty complete listing, hopefully you can either find some tips on getting things working, or find something else that does.

Be careful of version numbers, often different versions of the same card can have totally different chipsets.

In my experience, most wifi just works nowadays, but clearly there are exceptions.  Just make sure if you get something new you can try before you buy or return it if it's a no-go.
