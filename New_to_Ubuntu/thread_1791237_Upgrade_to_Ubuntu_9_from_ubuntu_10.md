### Post by Paqman on 2011-06-26
> **gemma.laming said:**
> That 9 series Ubuntu is no longer supported is of no interest to me: it worked and that is the bottom line.

You could continue to use 9.04 or 9.10, but not if the machine is connected to the internet. Using an OS that is no longer getting security updates means you be vulnerable to any exploits that are found in your software. You're just asking for your machine to get compromised and end up part of some script kiddy's botnet.

I would strongly advise you to either run a supported version (10.04, 10.10, or 11.04) or disconnect your computer from the internet.

You described three problems: Firefox has been crashing, Kompozer "needs prodding", and Open Office is slow to open. What would be helpful for all of those would be to open a terminal and launch the apps from there. For example, to launch Firefox, open a terminal and type:
```
firefox
```
then if it crashes, post the output that it produces in the terminal when it crashes.

What exactly do you mean when you say Kompozer "needs prodding" btw?
