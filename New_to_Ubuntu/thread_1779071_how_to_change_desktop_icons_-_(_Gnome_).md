### Post by CryptSphinx on 2011-06-09
hello , the arrow I think you are referring to is to designate the icon as a shortcut.

Some icon themes may not utilise this arrow as an indicator but I imagine most will.

If my assumption is correct then , system wide ,wherever there is a shortcut that indicator will be present (much like the lock indicator on certain files).

After a quick poke around gconf-editor I can't see anyway to easily disable this.

However if you do wish to remove it there appears to be a step-by-step guide located here.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=554820&highlight=remove+shortcut+arrows]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=554820&highlight=remove+shortcut+arrows")
