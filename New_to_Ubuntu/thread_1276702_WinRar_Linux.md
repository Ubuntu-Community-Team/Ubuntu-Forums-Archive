### Post by Bölvaður on 2009-09-27
> **RealG187 said:**
> 
Also I have the tar.gz, do I extract it, CD into it and type:

[QUOTE]make
make install/QUOTE]

too install it?

No you don't!

To install stuff you should never download anything your self without knowing it is the only available option.
[LIST=1]
[*]Look at the top panel and click
[*]*System &#8594; Administration &#8594; Synaptic Package Manager*
[*]Insert your password
[*]type into the filter: *rar*
[*]Click the box next to *unrar* (note that you might want some different package, this is just a suggestion, the nonfree one might be what you want) and click *Mark for Installation*
[*]Click Apply
[*]Insert your password
[*]Watch it being downloaded and set up
[*]Use Archive manager with .rar files
[/LIST]
