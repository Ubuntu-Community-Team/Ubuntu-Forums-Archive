### Post by drs305 on 2008-06-27
One solution I've used is to make a small graphic with text identifying the item. You store them somewhere and then select the appropriate customized icon in the Properties section of the launcher. I used a terminal icon and then just placed letters within.

Maybe not what you are looking for but it's an option.

BTW, the terminal icon I modified was /usr/share/pixmaps/gnome-terminal.png  I just filled in the center with white and then added a letter or number.
