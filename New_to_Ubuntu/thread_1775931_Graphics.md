### Post by Drate on 2011-06-05
Because Ubuntu is just that awesome?

I imagine it will come down to simply how the OS uses the drivers available.  Ubuntu is intended to work on a wide range of hardware, thus forcing design decisions that require greater efficiency.  Windows , having such a heavy market share and typically being shipped WITH the computer, is not bound by the same necessity for efficiency.

Of course, there are occasions when Ubuntu won't like your hardware at all, and that has happened to me before as well.  But yeah, when it works (which is most of the time in my experience), it tends to work very very well.
