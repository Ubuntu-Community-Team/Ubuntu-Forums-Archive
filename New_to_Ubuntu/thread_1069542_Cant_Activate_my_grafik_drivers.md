### Post by HavocXphere on 2009-02-14
Had the same issue. The activating thing is a bit flakey.

At first I also concluded that its not activating.

Tried again 2 weeks later and it worked. You need to:
[LIST]
[*]Update the whole system as far as possible using the standard repos.
[*]Make sure you've got it connected to the internet
[*]Restart before trying to activate
[*]Patience...lots. Just go and make a tea or something. It takes forever to come alive and looks like its dead for a solid 5 mins or so.
[/LIST]

In my case it even crashed towards the end...but the driver got activated nonetheless.;)
