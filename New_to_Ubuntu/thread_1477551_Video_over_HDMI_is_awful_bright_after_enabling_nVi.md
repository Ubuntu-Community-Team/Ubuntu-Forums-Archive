### Post by diablo75 on 2010-05-08
I have a PC with an nVidia GeForce 240 PCI-Express card installed.  For testing purposes I attached it to my TV with an HDMI cable and it worked great... until I enabled the proprietary drivers for the card.  The video on the screen looks bright and discolored... everything looks kinda purple instead of what it's supposed to look like.  Any ideas about what might cause this to happen?

---

### Post by diablo75 on 2010-05-08
FIXED IT.

Turns out I just had to shut the TV off and turn it back on.  Must have been something about the way the video stream was encoded over the cable by the non-proprietary drivers.
