### Post by srs5694 on 2011-04-01
Your /dev/sdd uses 4096-byte physical *and logical* sectors. Some BIOSes and boot loaders can't cope with such disks, so there's a chance that this is the source of your problem. Unfortunately, the installer will hide a lot of details from you, so you'll need to drop down to a command line and try installing the boot loader manually to see what errors it's reporting in detail. This can be a bit tricky and I don't have exact procedures to offer you offhand, but perhaps a Web search will turn up instructions, or somebody else can offer you more specific suggestions.
