### Post by sudodus on 2014-02-11
I think it only means that the built in linux KVM system will not work (because it is disabled by BIOS). There may or may not be a setting in the BIOS menu system to enable or disable virtualization. Since Vmware works anyway, there is nothing to worry about (unless you want to use KVM).
