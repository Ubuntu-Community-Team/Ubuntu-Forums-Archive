### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-04-05
First things I'd check with any laptop heating issue:

[LIST]
[*]Are all the fans running? Are any burned out?
[*]Is there dust build up in any of the heat sinks? Can you blow them out with compressed air?
[*]Are you on a bad surface? (i.e. fabric absorbs heat like crazy, laminated desks disperse heat a little better)
[/LIST]
