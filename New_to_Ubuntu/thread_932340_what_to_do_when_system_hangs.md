### Post by shifty_powers on 2008-09-28
you can do a couple of things. if only a app has hung, go to administration>system monitor and terminate the app.

you can add a kill app icon to your gnome panel which will allow you to kill any misbehaving app.

last resort is always control-shift-back spacem which will terminate your whole sessions and take you back to the log in screen..
