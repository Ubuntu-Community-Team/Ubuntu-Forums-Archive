If you hold down the special - "windows" key (between left Ctrl and left Alt) and left click on any window, a hand shows and you can move windows around.

that should give you enough space to access the window, that is hidden/overlapped.

### Post by sgosnell on 2011-01-23
Hold down the Alt key while dragging the window, from anywhere in the window, you don't have to grab the top border.  You only have to move the window a little way.
