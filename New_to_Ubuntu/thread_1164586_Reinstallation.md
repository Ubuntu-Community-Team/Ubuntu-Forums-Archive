### Post by MuH4hA on 2009-05-20
Hey!

The screenshot is from a 8.10, but doing it with a 9.04 - Setup should
look similar - just select manual partitioning when asked.

In the next step, you'll get an overview of Harddisks and Partitions
present in your system. Just select your old Linux (probably ext3)
Partition for being the root Partition (/).

To make sure you won't harm your Windows-Partition, make sure the
'Format?' box is NOT checked for your Windows Partition (NTFS).
