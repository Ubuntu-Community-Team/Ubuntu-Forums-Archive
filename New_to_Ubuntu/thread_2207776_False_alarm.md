### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-02-25
Whilst not suggesting you continue your style of browsing to such "interesting" web sites, I wonder if your whole system really was infected with ransomware, as you believe, or if it was just locked completely for that session.

If it happens again (for the same, or any other reason) hold down the right hand **Alt-Gr key + Print Screen/Sys Rq** and slowly type **r e i s u b **about about a second between keypresses.  That may reboot your machine and allow you to get back up and running.

If you're already aware of this trick, I apologise, but I note this is only your third post on the forum so you may be very new to Linux, and unaware of these tricks available when things go wrong.
