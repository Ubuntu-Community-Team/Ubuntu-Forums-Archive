### Post by kaldor on 2011-08-26
Virtualbox is actually quite easy and straightforward. Download a distro you want to use, and then make a new entry in Virtualbox. Doubleclick the new entry when done, and select the .iso you downloaded as the source. All should be fine then.

Advice: Never give the virtual machine more than 50% of your RAM.
