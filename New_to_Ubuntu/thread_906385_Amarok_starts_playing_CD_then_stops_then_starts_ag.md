### Post by Victormd on 2008-08-31
This is probably a dumb question, but does Amarok do this for all your CDs? Does the same CD play nice with Rhythmbox and not with Amarok? Is your CDDB enabled?
This can cause amarok to wait a bit before playing the CD. If it's a compiled CD, then it might take even longer. You might want to disable this and see if you get the same behavior. As for your audio settings, leave them at Automatic (BTW, alsa is ok but pulse... not so much).
