### Post by cariboo on 2009-05-20
This is really a support question and should be in Absolute Beginners Talk, but I'll answer some of your questions here. To add program icons to docky, just drag them from the menu. To configure how it behaves, right-click on the icon on the far left and select the options that you want. To resize Docky gab the seperator to the left of the clock and drag it to the size you want.

To remove docky, go to System-->Administration-->Synaptic Package Manager and type gnome-do in the search box, then right-click and mark the package for complete removal, then click apply.
