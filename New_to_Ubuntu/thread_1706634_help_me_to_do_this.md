### Post by owiknowi on 2011-03-14
eh, since i'm not that brilliant don't know if it is proper to try to answer your question.
oh, well, what the hell, i'll give it a try:

right click on the network icon in the left side of the task bar of your active wireless connection.
choose from the menu 'edit connections...' (network manager).

choose the tab 'wireless', click on your network and next the button 'edit' now you will be asked for your or the root password.

in the next screen check/mark the options 'connect automatically' and 'available to all users'.
if asked give the password for your wireless.

hope this works and if not just ask an you'll probably get a true brilliant answer ;)
