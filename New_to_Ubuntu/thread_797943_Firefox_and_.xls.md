### Post by y-lee on 2008-05-20
I am sorry no one has answered this after 2 days.

You did not say which version of firefox and I am using version 2.x so hopefully this will apply to whichever version you are using.

In Firefox go to **Edit -> Preferences** and then in the **Firefox Preferences** windows click on **Content** and then the **Manage** Button under *File Types*. In the **Download Actions** window which should pop up scroll thru the list of file types until you find XLS and click it to highlight it and then click **Change Action.**

Now in the **Download Actions** window which pops up click on **Open them with this application**, another window will pop up a **Select Helper Application** window and here you have to find Open office on your computer (not sure here whether to chose ooffice or oocalc) and once you find it click open. And then click Ok and then close all the open popup windows.

Note on my system Open Office is found in usr/bin.
