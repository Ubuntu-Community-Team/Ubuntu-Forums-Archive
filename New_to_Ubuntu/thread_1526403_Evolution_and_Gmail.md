### Post by Noo 2 Ubuntoo on 2010-09-28
> **metallicamike said:**
> I have it set to check every 2 minutes. It receives the mail, I just don't get a notification about it, and I also have play sound, show new message in panel, and display notification checked. I mainly just want to figure out how to get the new messages in the "On this computer" inbox instead of the "gmail" one.

Maybe create a rule? Click any message in your gmail inbox, then click -Message-Create Rule-Filter on Recipients. 

This should bring up a dialogue box where you give instructions as to what to do with mail sent to you at your gmail address. In your case you will use the drop down boxes to tell it to move any messages to you in the gmail inbox to you in the "On this computer inbox". Alternatively, install mail notification from the software centre and set it up for your gmail account. That way you won't need evolution running all the time but you will have to open evolution to read your mail, unless your happy going in via your browser direct from mail notification.
