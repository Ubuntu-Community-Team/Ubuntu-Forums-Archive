### Post by yahs on 2009-03-11
The reason why the Quickcam E1000 DOES work out of the box on Ubuntu 8.04 is that Ubuntu 8.04 uses v4l1 driver which was changed to v4l2 driver for Intrepid. this is a known bug with lots of info and advice - some good , some not so good on launchpad.

### Post by BrainwreckedTech on 2009-04-17
I just went out to the local Wal-Mart and picked up this thing for $17.  Just to clarify -- the QuickCam E1000 ***does*** work out of the box in Ubuntu 8.10 for applications that are using ***V4L2***, such as Cheese 2.24.2 and Ekiga 3.0.0 SVN.  If you have an app like Skype or Camorama that can only use ***V4L1***, Check out [https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+question/49739](https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+question/49739) for something that *might* help.
