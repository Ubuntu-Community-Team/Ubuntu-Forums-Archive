Thanks!If you're referring to use of the mouse wheel, there's a setting that controls this. Go to the settings manager, select the Window Manager applet, there click the Advanced tab, and you'll find two options for workspace switching. The default is to switch using the mouse wheel on the desktop; the other one turns it off and requires you to click on the workspace icons of the panel to make the switch.

Hope this helps!
