### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-05-28
You didn't mention what Ubuntu version you're running, so I'm going to presume it's 9.04.  

The latest restricted driver from AMD/ATI what works with your card (and mine) is Catalyst 9.3 -- but that won't work with the Xorg in 9.04  That requires Catalyst 9.4 or later -- which doesn't support our cards.

This means that like me (and lots of other folks), you're now stuck using the non-hardware-accelerated open source driver.
