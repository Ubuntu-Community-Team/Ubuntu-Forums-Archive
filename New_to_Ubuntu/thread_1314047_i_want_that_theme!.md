### Post by bcoffield on 2009-11-04
I too use the bricopacks from that site in my xp installation.  They are very impressive and give a nice thorough overhaul.

From my experience the best way to emulate that style is to use compiz/emerald.  I did that for a time but eventually switched back to using GTK for performance reasons.  And I don't think that there is a great solution for you using gtk themes, though some may come close.

Someone above has suggested gnome-look and that is where I also found most of my cool emerald themes etc.  Also above someone suggested new icon sets, which I agree with as well.  A combo of emerald borders and snazzy new icons will probably satisfy your eye candy desires :)
