### Post by howefield on 2011-09-04
Sharing a printer between computers running Ubuntu is usually fairly straight forward. Go to Printing settings on the printer connected computer and check off the option to Publish shared printers connected to this system.

On the others, check off the Show printers shared by other systems and then go through the add printer wizard on each.
