The middle-click copy/paste is a standard feature of Linux systems, I doubt there really even is a way to disable it (not that I'd even bothered looking, it's by far the best way to copy/paste ;))

Anyway, since you just want to use the mouse wheel to scroll around in Firefox, the solution is easy. You can configure that in Firefox itself.

Just go to Firefox preferences, and under Advanced/General turn on the "Use autoscrolling"-option.
