### Post by gpilkay on 2009-04-10
That I know of, no.  There are many backup programs available, though.  My own external HD works fine, and it's an NFTS system.  You should have no problems.  True plug and play software has never really worked well for me in Linux, though I know that's not the case for everyone.  I'd search for backup software and just designate your external as the storage medium.
