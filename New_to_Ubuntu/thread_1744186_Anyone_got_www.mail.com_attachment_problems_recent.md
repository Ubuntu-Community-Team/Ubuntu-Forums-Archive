### Post by Anuovis on 2011-07-20
The best way is to ask at mail.com support since it might not be related to Ubuntu.

I experienced a similar problem on another mail provider, [www.gmx.com]("http://www.gmx.com") a while ago. According to their forums it was some sort of Flash bug and it might be possible some Windows users suffered from it too.

Flash is generally buggy and annoying on Ubuntu but sometimes it works smoother with other browsers. Try Epiphany or Opera, see if that helps.

Another solution is to use a mail-fetching program at home, like Thunderbird or the default Evolution. You download/access the mail from your PC and don't have to deal with buggy interfaces.
