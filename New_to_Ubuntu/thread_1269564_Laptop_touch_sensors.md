**The Lights:**
The sad truth is that Linux doesn't usually have very good support for obscure things like turbo buttons because most companies write drivers for Windows, but not Linux. Perhaps you could use some kind of wrapper or something with the manufacturer-supplied drivers? I don't know anything about wrappers so perhaps someone else who knows more will come along.
