### Post by deltacomp on 2011-05-03
Ok, I am little confused as to what happened after you got back to Ubuntu classic. But I will try to walk you through what I know. Hit ALT+F2 and type GDM. Them unlock it and select classic ubuntu. Then restart your comp. The majority of your old settings will be reset in Compiz. Open Applications and select System tools to access the Compiz CSSM (settings manager) and reactivate what you need to make the desktop the way it used to be. Hope this helps.
