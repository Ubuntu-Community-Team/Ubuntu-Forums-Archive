You may not have the applet that "catches" the minimized apps.

Right click on the panel.

Click Add.

Scroll down.

I'm  not at my Ubuntu machine right now, but there should be something called "Window List" or something like that. Add it if it is not there.

### Post by viperdvman on 2011-10-05
Always make sure you have someplace for all your minimized windows to go, whether it's a task manager in one of your GNOME panel bars, or a task manager in a dock. If for any reason you accidentally delete the task manager applet from your dock or GNOME panel, there's always ALT+TAB to switch between open apps until you restore said task manager
