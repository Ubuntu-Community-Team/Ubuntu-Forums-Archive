Hi there!

welcome to to forums.

i'm afraid I must be the barer of bad news. When you ran fdisk -l the system shows all detected disks.

now one disk has been reformatted for linux with ext4, logical and swap partitions. The other disk is formatted for windows ntfs.

i'm very sorry to say, but if you have your old c:\ drive available on the machine, it is the d:\ drive you selected for erase in the install process.

you should have no trouble recovering your backup onto the old c:\ partition?

sorry to be the barer of bad news - we've all been there :-(
