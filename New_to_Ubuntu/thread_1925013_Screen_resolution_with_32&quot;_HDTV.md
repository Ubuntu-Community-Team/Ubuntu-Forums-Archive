### Post by Baldrick_NZ on 2012-02-13
It maybe that your video card requires proprietry drivers, to make the resolutions more compatible with your TV.

The first thing I'd do would be to open dash and type 'add' in the search box. One option to come up should be 'Additional Drivers'. Ensuring you are connected to the Internet, click on the icon. It'll tell you whether you require drivers, and what options are available. If it comes up with some options, choose the recommended drivers, activate them and when it tells you, reboot your PC. That should then fix the prob. 

If not, report back any info that's given.
