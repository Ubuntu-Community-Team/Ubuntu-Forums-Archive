It probably is not your Hotmail account that is deleting them, it might be responding correctly to the commands it receives. When you setup evolution did you choose the option to leave a copy of the mail on server? That might be what you want to do (sometimes people do that on POP3 accounts so they can download their mail to several computers)

Does Windows Live Hotmail have IMAP, if it does you might want to consider that if you check mail from several computers.
