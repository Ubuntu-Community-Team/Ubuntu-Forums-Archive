### Post by TheFu on 2015-10-07
For what inputs?

ffmpeg, avconv, simplescreenrecorder, obs, cheese are options. There are also hardware devices which make recording hidef video really easy, but it loses any multi-channel audio. The HW solution is best to capture gaming, since the computer processing is not impacted at all.

What do you want to record and from where does it come?  Screen recording is different than webcam recording and both of those are different from recording TV or recording inputs using some capture device. 

You didn't mention audio recording - is that needed too?
