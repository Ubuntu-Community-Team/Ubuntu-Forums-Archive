### Post by WeyOh on 2008-08-09
All your personal files are stored in /home. If you put this folder on a different partition, you can just reinstall Ubuntu and you won't lose your files. Otherwise you should copy those files to a safe place before you reinstall.

### Post by pparks1 on 2008-08-09
My typical solution is to just keep a larger USB flash drive on hand (2-4GB) and just copy to that, reload the OS and then move the files back.    It's quick, it's easy and I have a backup copy.
