### Post by starcraft.man on 2009-06-20
Have you installed your computers recommended restricted drivers for your graphics card? It can be found easily in System > Administration > Hardware drivers. It usually pops up in the tray and suggests you do. Sounds to me like that's the trouble, especially since you can play same videos in Windows.
