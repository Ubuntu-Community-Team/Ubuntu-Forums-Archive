### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2009-08-13
Ubuntu doesn't offer a Live CD for UNR, obviously for some reasons like, most netbooks don't have optical drives, etc. but you can have a copy of the .IMG (image/installation file) by downloading from here: [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download-netbook](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download-netbook).

The instructions... are also there in the page. Check this out: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromImgFiles](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromImgFiles)
