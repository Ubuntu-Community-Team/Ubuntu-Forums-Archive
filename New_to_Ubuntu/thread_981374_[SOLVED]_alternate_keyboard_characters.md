### Post by creperum on 2008-11-14
My favourite way of getting interesting characters is the "compose key."  You can set the Compose key by going to  

System > Preferences > Keyboard > Layout Options... > Compose key position

Then to get accented characters, you type Compose followed by two keys.  For example,  Compose ` a  gives à,  Compose = e  gives €, and so on.  (That's pressing the keys in succession, not together.)  

Many of the combinations are intuitive, or there's a chart [here]("http://docs.sun.com/app/docs/doc/806-4743/6jdq6q2n7?a=view")  (I make no guarantees about its correctness or completeness).
