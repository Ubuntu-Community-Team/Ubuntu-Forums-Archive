My personal experience has been with my HP 2605dn Color Laserjet Printer.  When I first installed Ubuntu 9.10, Karmic Koala, I navigated to "Applications, Ubuntu Software Center".  Once there I typed "HP" into the search dialog box and it came up with the "HPLIP Toolbox".  

I read the description, installed the application, followed the simple instructions, and viola I was printing.  I had more difficulty getting the printer recognized by Windows Vista then I did Ubuntu.

I have read on the HP forums that HP considers the 2605 a throw away printer but mine is 3 years old and works like a champ.

Just my $1.98 worth!
