### Post by Seks on 2009-01-04
8.04 = 8.04 LTS, the LTS is implied. You can upgrade to 8.10 with the update manager

EDIT: since 8.04 is long term support, upgrades to normal releases might not be enabled by default. Go to system>administration>software sources>updates and choose "normal releases" to remedy that
