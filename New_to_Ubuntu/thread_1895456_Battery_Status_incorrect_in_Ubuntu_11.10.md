### Post by usmanasim on 2011-12-15
Ubuntu Detects battery timings pretty accurately...

It can be either the two of the following

1. It's time to get your battery replaced, because your battery capacity has been reduced to 45% of the original capacity. (Usually happens after 1-2 years of laptop usage.)

2. If you are absolutely sure that your battery is in as good condition as when you've purchased, Try updating the system. (Very unlikely to solve if the problem is with battery.)
