### Post by the-edmeister on 2010-12-11
Firefox uses a Site Identity button that gives more pertinent information than the "lock" did. As mentioned above, the lock is in the Status bar on Firefox 3.6 and earlier versions. On the Firefox 4.0 beta's the lock is gone, as is the Status bar.
[https://support.mozilla.com/en-US/kb/Site+Iddentity+Button](https://support.mozilla.com/en-US/kb/Site+Iddentity+Button)
