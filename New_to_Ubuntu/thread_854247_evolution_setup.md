Evolution is a mail client, like Microsoft's Outlook. You dont really need to use it, but it is helpful for people who want to manage their multiple email accounts or want access to older emails while not connected to the internet. If you like using a web based Email system then continue to use that.

### Post by AOZ on 2008-07-09
Evolution is an e-mail manager/calendar organizer thing. basically linux version of outlook. I use evolution for its agenda and gettin e-mail from my university account, gmail and hotmail. Its easier having them all come into one inbox than having to go to each website and log in bla bla bla. As for that whole server thing you need to tell evolution how its going to send and receive your mail. Gmail uses POP and SMTP and the servers are pop.gmail.com and smtp.gmail.com. There are many how-tos across the web to set-up whatever mail service you use.
