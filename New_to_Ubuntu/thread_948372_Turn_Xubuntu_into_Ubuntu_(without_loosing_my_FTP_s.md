### Post by low-res on 2008-10-15
> **ronnielsen1 said:**
> sudo apt-get install ubuntu-desktop

You will still have all your files (including xfce)

After that you "log out".
At the loginscreen in the bottom left corner you can choose "options" then "select session" and you will be able to choose a different X.

You could even "apt" more different windows managers if you like. they will appear in you session screen.
