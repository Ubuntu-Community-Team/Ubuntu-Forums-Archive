### Post by Fuith on 2009-07-26
Hi there,
       If you plan to use the vga out on your laptop to go to an external monitor, I can assure you it works. I have a bottom of the line Acer (I think it might even have the same Intel graphics card), which I outputted to a 26" Samsung. Worked like a charm for the gimp and video editing. Just plug in the cable, go to System -> Preferences -> Display and hit Detect (I think, can't check the laptop right now). You will also have the option to either mirror the laptop screen or extend the desktop to the second screen. I use the latter for graphics work, with the brushes, tools etc windows on the laptop screen and the image I'm working on in the TV screen.
       Good luck! :D
