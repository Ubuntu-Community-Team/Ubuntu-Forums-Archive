### Post by Zorael on 2011-01-01
I'm not sure of what pages you're referring to. Are you running the netbook interface? Does it look like the attached screenshot?

If so, it's a new workspace metaphore; an alternative to the normal desktop. You set a page up with the widgets you like (rss feeds, weather reports, comics etc) and use it as your newspaper, more or less. You can add stuff by clicking the cashew in the bottom left corner, unlocking the page and then clicking Add Widgets. The cashew can also be broken out into a small tab and moved around.
