### Post by dargaud on 2013-05-27
Standard Linux filesystems such as ext2/3/4 do not require drive maintenance (you are probably thinking about defragmentation). If things slow down it can be due to a number of reasons: drive damage (check the SMART status), some program/service that you installed and which hogs resources, etc...
