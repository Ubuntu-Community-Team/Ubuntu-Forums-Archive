### Post by DapperMe17 on 2011-12-06
Aprilmoon,

I understand your printing issue....for example, when wanting to print out coupons from stores like Target, Walmart, etc. 

Usually, those sites are set up for Windows & Mac, and they don't provide support for Linux. 

No biggie!

What you can do...when you are at the coupon page (assuming you're using Firefox)....

1. Go to File
2. Click on Print
3. Click on "Print to File" when the print dialogue box appears
4. Save it to a location of your choice (it will automatically save as a .pdf file)
5. Go to that file, and you'll be able to print off the coupon page

That easy!
