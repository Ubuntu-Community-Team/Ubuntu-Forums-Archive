### Post by chrisamiller on 2009-08-18
It's storing your password correctly. For security reasons, though, it won't show you the password after the first time, just a hash of it.  It's very sensible, because you don't want someone who borrows your laptop to be able to check a box and get your password.

I agree, though that the UI needs to be improved to make it clear that what you're seeing does not indicate that your pass wasn't correct.  Your connection woes are not due to this problem.
