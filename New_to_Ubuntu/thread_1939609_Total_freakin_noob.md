### Post by Paqman on 2012-03-12
Could be a few things then:
[LIST]
[*]Check that the 32-bit install image you've got is ok by [checking the MD5SUM]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM"), you should get a value of c396dd0f97bd122691bdb92d7e68fde5 for the 11.10 32-bit desktop image.
[*]Try a fresh disk, and burn it at the slowest speed possible. You can't burn software at the kind of speeds you'd burn MP3s or video.
[*]Alternatively, try using a USB stick, as it'll avoid wasting CDs.
[/LIST]

Apart from that, there's not a lot we can help with unless you tell us exactly what went wrong. We'd just be guessing really.
