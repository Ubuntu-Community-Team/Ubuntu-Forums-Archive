### Post by lintoon on 2008-10-08
There will be no sound coming out of your speakers when your headphones are plugged in so check your volume levels.  Look in the top right hand corner of your screen for an icon of a speaker and double click on it.  This will open your volume control.  Here you can slide the volume for your headphones up.

### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-10-08
It depends entirely on the internal jack for the headphones.  Some of those jacks are just wired parallel in the audio circuit, however some are in series and will "intercept" the signal as they are first in the chain.  This is for "private listening" as is provided on many laptops.  Shuts the speaker system off so the unit can be used in a public place, for instance, without being obnoxious to the person sitting next to you on that bus/train/plane.

If I plug a headphone set into my PDA, the internal speaker shuts off, for instance.
