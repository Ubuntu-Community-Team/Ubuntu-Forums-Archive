### Post by Mr. Shannon on 2011-05-19
Have you set up email in Evolution?  I don't think the send via email option is there until you setup Evolution (thats the default email client in Ubuntu).

If you don't want to use Evolution then you should be able to upload attachments to an email from your web browser with your email service.

This link has some links to setting Evolution with several major email services.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Evolution]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Evolution")
