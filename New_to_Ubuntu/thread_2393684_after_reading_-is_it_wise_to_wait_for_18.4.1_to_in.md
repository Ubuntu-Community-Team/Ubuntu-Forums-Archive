### Post by sudodus on 2018-06-06
04.1 *is* the first point release.

If you can wait and want a smooth ride: Wait for the first point release and installing/upgrading will be smoother than now. I would say that it is the optimal time to switch to a new LTS release.

A fresh installation is much smoother than an upgrade from a previous release. *So if you have to install something now, I would recommend a fresh installation of 18.04 LTS* (better than installing 16.04.4 and then upgrading to 16.04.5 and then upgrading to 18.04.1).

If you have a 16.04.x version, that works well, you can wait longer until you upgrade (actually until getting close to the end of life of the version you are running. "If it ain't broke, don't fix it!"
