### Post by yragrelluf on 2008-07-30
In my experience, to burn a mpeg or avi or whatever to a dvd readable in a home dvd player or playstation or whatever, you should use devede and select the box that says create iso ready to burn to disc, then use brasero or cd/dvd creator to burn it. If you need to backup an existing dvd, I use k9copy to rip it to computer and convert to iso, then brasero or cd/dvd creator. The only problem I have is the finished dvds skip in my playstation. I dont know if its because of my cheap blank dvds, my low quality burner, or if its my dvd player. Hope that answers your questions.
