### Post by papibe on 2012-07-16
> **hanzj said:**
> Are PDFs programs?

Not exactly, but they can have code (JavaScript).

There are risks in opening a PDF on your web browser. Although, most of the exploits are implemented for Windows.

According to [this]("http://net-security.org/malware_news.php?id=1863"), 48% of the Window's virus infections come from Adobe products: Adobe Reader/Acrobat and Flash.

The safest procedure is save the PDF and read it with Ubuntu's 'Document Reader' (or other open source reader). You won't get a virus from those tools.

Regards.
