### Post by nhasian on 2009-07-07
first things first.  connect your cable/DSL modem to the Internet or WAN port on your router.  Now lets check to make sure your router is communicating properly with your modem.

plug your computer via an ethernet cable (not wireless) into one of the LAN ports in your router.  when you turn your computer on, you should have access to internet automatically.  if not, you need to change some settings in your router.  If you ARE able to access the internet through your router's ethernet port then you can attempt to setup your wifi adaptor.
