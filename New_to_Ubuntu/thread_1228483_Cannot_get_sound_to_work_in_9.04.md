Here is something  that you may do to see if you can your sound system up and going . 

* you must to aware of the sound icon in the upper panel of your desktop . Click it , hit "Volume control '  in the following dialog . In the window that appears after this , see whether all the sliders are turned up and nothing is muted . Now try playing audio files to see if if your sound system works . 

*Go to System --> Preferences --> Sound  , and fiddle with the options over there to see whether something happens . Here you have a " Test " button beside every option .
