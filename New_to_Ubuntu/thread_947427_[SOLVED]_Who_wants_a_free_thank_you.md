### Post by Kevbert on 2008-10-14
Enter a word you want to show, highlight it click on the Insert link icon (just hover the mouse over the icons when you make a post) and enter the hyperlink.  I normally change the colour to make it more obvious that it's a hyperlink, so with the word still highlighted click on the colors icon (A underlined) and change it to red.  Funnily enough I asked this question a few months back when I was a noobie.
