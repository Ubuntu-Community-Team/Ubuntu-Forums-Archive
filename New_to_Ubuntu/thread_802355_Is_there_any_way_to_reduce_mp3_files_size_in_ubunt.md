### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2008-05-21
You would have to recompress the mp3 files.  Since you will be going from a lossy format to the same lossy format, the sound quality will be lower.  It would be like saving a jpg image file and then resaving it again.  Going from mp3 to wav is an extra step that won't be any different than going from mp3 to mp3.  Depending on your hearing, audio hardware and software, compression amount, and location you may or may not notice a difference.
