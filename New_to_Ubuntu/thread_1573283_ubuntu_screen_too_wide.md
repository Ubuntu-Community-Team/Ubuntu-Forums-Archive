### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-09-12
At what resolution is the system running?

Resizing of the screen is an option present on the monitor. If it displays the resolution correctly, resize from the monitor.

To move any panel icon, right click and deselect "Lock to panel", right click again, select move and shift it where ever you want it to be. Again lock it to panel.
