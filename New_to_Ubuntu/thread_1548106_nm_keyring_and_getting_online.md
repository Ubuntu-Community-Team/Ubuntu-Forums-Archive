Hi,
 
The GNOME Keyring is a password manager for things such as Network Keys and Login passwords. When you enter in the network key for your wireless connection - it encrypts it into the GNOME Keyring. The GNOME keyring password, by default, is your login password. So yes, it's perfectly normal.
