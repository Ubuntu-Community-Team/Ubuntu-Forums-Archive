### Post by anotherdisciple on 2008-08-13
You don't need an anti-virus for linux. As far as passing one on in an email... it wouldn't have any control over your linux, so it couldn't pass itself on through your email.

The only way you could pass a virus on to a windows friend is by attaching a file that has a virus attached to it. So I'd just be careful about what you pass along.
