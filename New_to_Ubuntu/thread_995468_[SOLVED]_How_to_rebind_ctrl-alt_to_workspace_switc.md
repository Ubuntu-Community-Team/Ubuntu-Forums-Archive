### Post by epitaph on 2008-11-27
I'm not sure if this is what you're referring to. I assume you are talking about the ctrl+alt+right and ctrl+alt+left shortcuts to go to other workspaces.

Go to System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts and scroll down to the Window Management category and set your binds for the workspace switch commands if they are not present.
