### Post by Zzl1xndd on 2008-10-06
You can upgrade to the newest version just by using the update update manager, so you wont have to redo anything.

The closest thing to photoshop is The Gimp and its already installed just look under applications > Graphics.

### Post by mikewhatever on 2008-10-06

To backup your photos, use an external HDD or a second internal drive.
