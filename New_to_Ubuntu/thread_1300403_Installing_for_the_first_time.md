### Post by jmszr on 2009-10-24
BigIrv2210,

Here is a link to the Free Ubuntu Pocket Guide by Keir Thomas: [http://www.ubuntupocketguide.com/](http://www.ubuntupocketguide.com/) . It is a free book in .pdf form. Near the beginning of the book is a discussion/howto on the different types of installation.
