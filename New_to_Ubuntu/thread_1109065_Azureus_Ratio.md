### Post by ronocdh on 2009-03-28
Having a client-side ratio won't adversely affect your rapport on filesharing sites. Basically, most private trackers watch ratios to ensure that no one's downloading too much without also uploading. Many public trackers, though, do not watch ratios.

If you see one already, never having used BitTorrent before, I would presume Azureus is using your machine to distribute updates or perhaps the Azureus installer itself. That would explain if your client has uploaded or download anything before you've manually selected a torrent to add yourself.
