### Post by John Wilson on 2010-12-29
Domenick2x,
I hope you don't have the problem I had with my desktop which had an old ATI video card.
In 10.04 it started responding slowly and in 10.10 it would not run unless I booted up in recovery mode and choosing the low definition graphics option.
Otherwise I would have the boot up stalled at the background of the desktop or the black screen of death.
The problem is no-one is making a proprietary driver for these video cards and the work arounds in Ubuntu forums are very difficult. In the end I bought a new Nvidia card which is not an option for you but booting up in recovery mode is
Good luck
