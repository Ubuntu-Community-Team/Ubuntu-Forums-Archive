A sold copy of Ubuntu is not necessarily a "con". There are many businesses in many countries providing a paid service to their customers, selling CDs or DVDs of many Linux distros, not just Ubuntu. This is neither against the law nor a contravention of open source licenses. In these cases what is being charged for is a burnt disk, not the operating system, which is particularly useful to people with limited bandwidth who are not able to download the ISO for themselves. As such, these businesses are providing an invaluable service.
