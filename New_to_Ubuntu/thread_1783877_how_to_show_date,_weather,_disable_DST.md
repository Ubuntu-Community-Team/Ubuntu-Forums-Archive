### Post by cariboo on 2011-06-16
DId you try clicking on the time and selecting Time and Date Settings? There are separate indicators for weather, sound, networking and date/time, so there isn't one place to change the settings. You can also use dconf-editor and gconf editor to change some of the indicator settings.
