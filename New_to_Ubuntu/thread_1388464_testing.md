Happens every once in a while when we fiddle with stuff.  Just delete one of the entries and you'll be fine.

Right click on your Applications menu button (or whatever we call it!?), click on Edit Menus.  Delete the extra entry.  Presto.

There really is generally only one place stuff installs to (some exceptions, but unlikely in this case).
