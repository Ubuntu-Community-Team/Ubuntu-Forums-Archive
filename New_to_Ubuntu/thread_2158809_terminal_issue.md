### Post by jeffro97 on 2013-07-01
The terminal does not actively display what you type in for a password. It will keep anything typed in hidden. There are no asteriks, no nothing. Just hit enter when your done, and (pending on if you typed it in right) you should be given permission. If not, type it again and hit enter once more.
