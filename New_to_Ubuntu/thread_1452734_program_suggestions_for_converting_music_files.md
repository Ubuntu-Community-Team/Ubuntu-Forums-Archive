### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-04-12
All you need to do once you have installed the ubuntu-restricted-extras is open brasero, choose Audio project, add files of any sort, mp3. ogg, flac, and they will be converted to the needed files (wav) for an audio CD to be burned.

There is absolutely no need to convert anything manually before you burn the CD, so forget about that.  This assumes it is audio CDs you are trying to burn to play in a CD player in your car.

Some newer cars have CD players that are able to play mp3s direct, in which case you just burn the mp3s to a CD as data, and they should then play for you.
