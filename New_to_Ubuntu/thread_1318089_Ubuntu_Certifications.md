### Post by yeats on 2009-11-07
> Ubuntu is under free software foundations .Why they are charging money for Ubuntu Certifications ?

Well, to be completely clear, Ubuntu is NOT affiliated with the Free Software Foundation ([www.fsf.org](www.fsf.org)), if that's what you meant.  There is nothing about free/open source software that prevents a company from charging for expertise or support (including training & certification) - in fact, that is a way that many open source companies DO make money.  Being free/open source is only in regards to the way the software itself is licensed.  The Open Source Definition ([www.opensource.org](www.opensource.org)) may be a good reference for you (as would the FSF link I've also provided).
