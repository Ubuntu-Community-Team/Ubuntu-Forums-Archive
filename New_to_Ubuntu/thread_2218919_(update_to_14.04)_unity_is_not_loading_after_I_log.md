### Post by donrudi on 2014-04-22
A reply not from a Lord of Knowledge but rather from a humble Emissary of Ignorance. I had the same experience as you did when I upgraded my 12.04 dual-boot desktop (XP/Ubuntu) to 14.04. Admin login appears as usual, but afterwards a desolate pink screen devoid of icons appears with the lone words "Ubuntu 14.04 LTS" in the lower left hand corner. I tried for days implementing fixes I found in these and other forums, all to no avail. However, I did conclude that the problem likely resides in some sort of incompatibility between NVIDIA chipset-driven video cards and Ubuntu's Unity shell interface for the GNOME desktop environment. Many people are experiencing this problem, and I have yet to see a viable solution. If it exists, it will most certainly involve some arcane command line hocus-pocus.
