### Post by bumanie on 2008-06-26
You should upgrade to the stable/final release via update manager. Be aware that there will be 500+ updates, but the bandwidth used will be less than downloading a new LTS .iso. Beta versions, as I am sure you aware, are versions still under development, thus, a final release has a number of advantages, as it is 'properly finished' and fully tested etc.
