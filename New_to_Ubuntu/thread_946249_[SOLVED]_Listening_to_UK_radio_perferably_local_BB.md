### Post by AndyCooll on 2008-10-13
As has already been mentioned in this thread, you shouldn't need to install any additional media player (or indeed any plugins) to gain access to online radio stations (and in your case specifically BBC stations). 

You'll probably need to install relevent codecs, and in the case of BBC iPlayer you'll need to install Flash too. The easiest way to do this is by installing ubuntu-restricted-extras. After that they should work straight from the web.

You could alternatively play many radio stations through your favourite audio player (e.g. Rhythmbox, Totem, Amarok etc) and for that you'll need the codecs as mentioned above and relevent url for the radio station you want to play.
