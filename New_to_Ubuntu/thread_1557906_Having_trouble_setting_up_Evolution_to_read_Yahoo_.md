### Post by Vrroom on 2010-08-21
you can install thunderbird from software center. Thunderbird will set your mail accounts with just a username and password.

Also if you are not into desktop clients, then you can download desktop- webmail. You can select your mail service provider and it will take directly to your mailbox in a web browser. 

If you choose any of these methods, don't forget to change your preferred mail account from preferences.

### Post by theepdinker on 2010-08-21
I use Evolution with gmail.
Here are my server settings:
receiving: pop.gmail.com
sending: smtp.gmail.com:587
