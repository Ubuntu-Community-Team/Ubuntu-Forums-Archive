### Post by deadflowr on 2012-08-08
There's an up to date guide on your system. Tap the windows key on your keyboard and type help. Click enter and it will open the ubuntu desktop guide. It can also be found here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/12.04/ubuntu-help/index.html]("https://help.ubuntu.com/12.04/ubuntu-help/index.html")

Also found in the same area is the community wiki here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community")

And of course, the Ubuntu Manual here:

[http://ubuntu-manual.org/]("http://ubuntu-manual.org/")

Another source of good reading can be found here, on the forums.
In fact I'd suggest heading over to the Sub-forum in the other community discussions called Ubuntu Weekly Newsletter. They post the news letter every week, and if you go back, say, to April and parse through them, you'll find several helpful how-tos about the latest version(Precise Pangolin).
I guess my point is there is plenty of cost-free reading material available. And if something doesn't make sense, start a thread.
