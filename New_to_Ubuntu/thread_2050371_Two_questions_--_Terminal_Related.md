### Post by papibe on 2012-08-30
'sudo apt-get update' would be the equivalent of pressing 'Check' on the Update Manager'. That would update the sources, but not the actual packages.

'sudo apt-get upgrade' is the equivalent of 'Install Updates'. That would actually download and update your software.
