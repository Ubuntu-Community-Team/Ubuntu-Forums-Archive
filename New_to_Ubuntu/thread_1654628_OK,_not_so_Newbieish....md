### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-12-28
my tips would we

1. stay clear of toshiba's - stick with HP if you can...
2. ensure the laptop has either nvidia or intel graphics
3. has a min of 1GB Ram

Stuff like webcams you'll have to suck and see unless you can physically get hold of the laptop on a try-before-buy basis to see if ubuntu recognises it.  10.04 & 10.10 recognises most webcams now - but there are some issues with older/cheaper equipment.
