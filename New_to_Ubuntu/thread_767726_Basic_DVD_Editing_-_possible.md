### Post by Irihapeti on 2008-04-26
Yes, it can be done, but you may find that you need more than one program to do it. Be prepared to do some experimenting with different programs to see what works best for you.

I've used Kino, Kdenlive, Avidemux and DVDstyler for different parts of the process. Other programs have their enthusiastic followers. The following (somewhat old) article has details about using Kino and DVDStyler, which might help you get started.

[http://www.linuxjournal.com/article/8413](http://www.linuxjournal.com/article/8413)

I've had problems with Kdenlive getting the audio and video out of sync. Nowadays I prefer OpenMovieEditor. Unfortunately the version in the 7.10 repositories is rather old. It's actually not too hard to compile if you feel up to doing that sort of thing, or there is a precompiled deb available somewhere on the internet.
