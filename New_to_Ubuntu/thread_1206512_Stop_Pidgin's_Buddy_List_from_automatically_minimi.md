### Post by EssexEagle on 2009-07-07
If I set my Pidgin status to "Away", "Busy" or, indeed, anything other than "Available", the Buddy List window is automatically minimized to the system tray.  Is there a way of stopping this behaviour so that the window is always visible?

---

### Post by darthmob on 2009-07-07
Have a look at Tools -> Plugins or CTRL+u. There is one called 'Iconify on Away' which most likely does what you are looking for. ;)
