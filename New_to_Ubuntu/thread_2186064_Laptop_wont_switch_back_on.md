### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-11-05
Take a voltmeter and measure the voltage coming out of the connector--it should match to voltage printed on the power brick--typically 12VDC to 19VDC.

When a battery is drained and you are also running the computer, that puts the maximum current drain on the power supply.  It has to simultaneously charge the battery and keep power to the computer so it can keep rendering cat videos.  Even though the computer is 2 months old, your laptop may be suffering from an early death--[bathtub failure]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bathtub_curve").
