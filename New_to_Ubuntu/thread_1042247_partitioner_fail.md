You might try checking the install disk at the splash screen when the computer boots off the CD. I've never seen the partitioner do something like that before...a bad disk is the only thing I can think of. Did you check the MD5 of the ISO after you downloaded it?

Even if the disk and the checksum are OK, it seems that sometimes, if you burn a disk at high speed (32x or even 16x), it might not work properly. For whatever reason, burning the alternate CD at 8x or 4x seems to be more reliable.
