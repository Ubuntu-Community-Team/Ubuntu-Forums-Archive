### Post by Krytarik on 2012-04-05
These were the available color options in Gnome 2:

[LIST]
[*]fg_color
[*]bg_color
[*]text_color
[*]base_color
[*]selected_fg_color
[*]selected_bg_color
[*]tooltip_fg_color
[*]tooltip_bg_color
[*]link_color
[/LIST]
Hope this helps.
