### Post by Niniel on 2009-05-08
There's no much to it, really.
Did you already physically install the disk in your computer?
For partitioning and formatting, there's  a Partition Editor in System/Administration. You will need at least one (primary) partition.
Do you have a Linux-only system or do you plan on sharing the disk with other operating systems, e.g. Windows? In that case, formatting the disk in NTFS is probably best, otherwise use ext3 or ext4.
