### Post by Frogs Hair on 2013-05-17
One thing comes to mind , the profile folder in home/hidden .opera is corrupt.You can delete the profile folder and reinstall but you will have to restore your extensions bookmarks. If you use Opera Link you can restore your bookmarks by logging in or export them for later use. I have used Opera on Linux and Windows and have not seen this problem.
