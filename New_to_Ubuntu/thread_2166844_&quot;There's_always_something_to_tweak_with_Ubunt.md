### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-08-11
He can't be that informed if he didn't tell you what to do !!!

Move your mouse to the top of the screen to see the Firefox menu and then click on Tools > Clear Recent History (or just hit CTRL + SHIFT + DEL).

Select what you want to delete and click Clear Now.
