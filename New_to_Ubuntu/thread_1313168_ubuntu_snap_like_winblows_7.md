### Post by Tyke91 on 2009-11-03
```
sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager
```that will give you more control over your compiz window manager.

Then just look through the settings manager(System>Preferences>CompizConfig Settings Manager) for snapping windows (at the bottom, usually) and play around with the settings until you have them they way you want ^_^
