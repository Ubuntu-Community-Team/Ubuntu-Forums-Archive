### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-09-26
Do not forget that even with an update authentication is required. We may get upgrade notification automatically (if set) but the upgrade will not happen unless we give authentication. This is the secure way that Ubuntu does things.

I did not upgrade to 11.04 until about two weeks after it was released. And I will not upgrade to 11.10 until the same amount of time has passed. I am testing 11.10 on a separate partition.

My advice is to authenticate updates but do not click the button that offers an upgrade until you want to.
