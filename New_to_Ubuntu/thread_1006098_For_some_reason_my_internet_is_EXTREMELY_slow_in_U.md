### Post by Shazaam on 2008-12-09
What driver are you using for your dialup modem? If it is from linuxant the free version is crippled to 14.4k. The pay version is full 56k compatible.
That said, if you are using gnome-ppp to connect open it, go to Setup>Modem>Speed and make sure it is set to 57600 (or higher). Another problem may be the phone number you are dialing into to connect. Try different numbers.
