### Post by vasa1 on 2016-07-04
You should also be aware that once you've assigned a purpose to a particular combination, that combination may not be available elsewhere. Example: Alt+T to open a new terminal. But Firefox uses Alt+T to open the Tools dropdown, as does LibreOffice!
