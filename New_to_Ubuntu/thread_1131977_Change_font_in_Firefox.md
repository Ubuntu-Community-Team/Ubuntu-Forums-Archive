### Post by coffeecat on 2009-04-22
It does actually, but cmnorton missed a step.

In firefox:

Edit -> Preferences -> Content Tab > Fonts and Colours. Click on the Advanced tab if you want to change the sans and serif fonts and change the default from seriffed to sans serif.

---

### Post by Therion on 2009-04-22
You also have to *clear* the checkbox, under the Advanced tab that says something to the effect of allowing web pages to choose their own fonts.  If you want web pages to display using the fonts you choose instead you need to turn that option off.
