There's no "invisible notification there", but the space is reserved for certain types of notifications, like the ones you get if you adjust volume, or display brightness.

The locations of notifications have been designed this way to avoid the situations where a notification would move on your screen as a result of multiple notifications appearing at the same time, and to make sure that the most important notifications always appear in the same place.
