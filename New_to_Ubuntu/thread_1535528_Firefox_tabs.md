### Post by Messyhair42 on 2010-07-21
I just upgraded to Lucid and started using Firefox 3.6 and the tabs are annoying me. if i'm in one window and open a new tab by [right click, open in new tab] it opens right next to the tab i'm using, whereas in previous versions they opened as the last tab. and i can't figure out how to change it to the previous version. i'll downgrade firefox if i have to but is there a way to manages this in 3.6?

---

### Post by Dustin2128 on 2010-07-21
you can change this by going to about:config, and changing the value of browser.tabs.insertRelatedAfterCurrent to false.
