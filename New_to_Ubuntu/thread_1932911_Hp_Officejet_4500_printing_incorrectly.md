### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-02-29
Try plugging the Officejet into another PC and seeing if the problems continues.

What you are describing can often be the result of clogged printheads.

You may need to run an inkjet cleaning routine on the Officejet.  Your manual will have the information on what keys to press on the printer to run this cleaning routine.
