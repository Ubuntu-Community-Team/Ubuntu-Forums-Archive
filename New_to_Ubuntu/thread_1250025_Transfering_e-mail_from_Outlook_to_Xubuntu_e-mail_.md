### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-08-26
Y'know, I tried all kindsa stuff with mine too, and ended up just saving my e-mail stuff as text files; and storing them (along with pictures and music and stuff) on a normal backup; then manually entering all the settings and stuff in Thunderbird. It really only takes a few minutes anyway. 

But Thunderbird can import all your e-mail stuff from Outlook, supposedly. If you have Thunderbird in both Windows *and* Xubuntu, [mozbackup]("http://mozbackup.jasnapaka.com/") is a good utility you can try. Back up in Windows, then Restore in Xubuntu.
