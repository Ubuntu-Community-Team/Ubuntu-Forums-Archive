### Post by Growbag on 2008-09-08
I went out of my way, and out of my price bracket to buy an IBM thinkpad to use as my server (for various reasons, one being a built in UPS :).

It's an old T23 which I picked up on ebay for €200, and it came with docking station, travel power adapter (runs off car fag lighter and has aircraft adapter too!), and an original IBM carry bag (if you've ever seen one, you would know why they are so good!).

It was definitely worth the extra cash, they are built well and work flawlessly.

Everything but the built in winmodem works out of the box, and I simply installed "martianmodem" to get the modem working.

For a rock solid everyday working laptop I would definitely recommend IBM over the others.

Now for gaming and 3d effects, that is another story :).
