### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-01-21
For your second issue, you can Alt-drag windows upwards to get at the contents below the bottom of the screen. I'm not 100% certain this works in the window manager that Ubuntu Netbook Edition uses, but I'd be surprised if it doesn't work.

Ubuntu Netbook Remix was the earlier name for Ubuntu Netbook Edition.
