### Post by spillin_dylan on 2009-04-24
For the record, I have the new iPod classic 120Gib, and I have been able to get it to work with Rhythmbox somewhat.  I can transfer songs to and from the iPod, as well as delete songs off the iPod from Rhythmbox.  The biggest issue I have found is that I can't add a "pre-made" playlist onto the iPod, and cover art doesn't load initially.  

I have found some workarounds for these issues, though.  If I play the contents of the iPod *through* Rhythmbox while it's connected, Rhythmbox will download and update cover art onto the iPod, one song at a time. 

Also, to create a playlist on the iPod, it must again be connected to Rhythmbox, and you must manually create a playlist from the files that are on your iPod.  

I know it's not necessarily as friendly as iTunes is, but it does work.  I'm constantly on the lookout for something that might work better, but so far this is the best I've found.
