I've been toying with Linux/Ubuntu for a while now, but never managed to get the wireless internet etc working. It never seemed to recognize my wireless card in my HP 6735s laptop.

It installed the updates no problem and I managed to download a few apps, and on the whole l like the system so far, especially 'Rhythmbox' media player.

Will recommend this to friends now!
