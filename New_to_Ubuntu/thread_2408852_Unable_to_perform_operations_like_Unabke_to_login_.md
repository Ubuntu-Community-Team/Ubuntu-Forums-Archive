### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2018-12-24
There is a [procedure for upgrading an EOL system]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades"). But you'd have to go through that at least twice to get to a supported release (13.04 to 13.10, 13.10 to 14.04) and release upgrades have been know to create problems and leave behind quite a bit of stuff that isn't needed or used any more. Backing up your data and doing a fresh install would probably be quicker and create less problems.
