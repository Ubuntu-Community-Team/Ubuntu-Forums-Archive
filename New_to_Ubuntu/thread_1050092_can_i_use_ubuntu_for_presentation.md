### Post by diablo75 on 2009-01-25
You should be able to open powerpower presentations with Open Office, or create your own with it as well.  Don't think you'll encounter any problems, but I have occasionally seen formating anomalies crop up when opening up a powerpoint presentation that wasn't made with open office.  Best advice of course would be to actually practice your presentation before giving it.
