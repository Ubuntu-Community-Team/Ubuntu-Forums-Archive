### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2008-06-02
Of course you can upgrade from 7.1 to 8.04 through update manager, but there is nothing like a fresh install...

I'm sorry to say that, but for me 7.1 was a bit buggy - don't know what other users will say.
In 8.04 everything, and I mean everything, just works and it works faster.

A new version of Ubuntu is released every 6 months. 7.10 means the version that was released on 2007 (7) on the 10th (10) month.
8.04 is the version that was released on April 2008 and so on. The next version (codename Intrepid Ibex) will be released on October (8.10). There will be a major update of 8.04 in the summer, when the stable version of Firefox will be released I think.
