### Post by HermanAB on 2010-02-14
Howdy,

Let me share a dirty little secret with you: I almost never update my systems.  I re-install once every year or three depending on what the machine does.

Look at it this way: If your machine is working properly, then an update cannot make it better.  It can leave it either the same, or worse, but not better.  So, updates must be applied with care.  

Any automatic update can potentially be an automatic screw-up, so it is the first feature that I disable on a new system.
