### Post by mcduck on 2011-09-26
Every Android phone in existence does what you are asking for, perhaps apart from the price which I can't comment on as it depends on where you live and what you consider expensive.

If you want wireless tether (as opposed to having to connect the phone with USB cable) look for any phone with Android 2.3. Although I've understood that the cell phone operators in USA would have some artificial restriction on tethering, so you should probably check that first.
