### Post by James_Lochhead on 2009-04-16
Well, to get visual effects working you need a driver for your graphics card. Drivers for NVidia are in the repos and there are open source drivers for ATI somewhere.

The web cam might be a tricky one. Rather than scrap the computer I would just buy an external one (that you are sure is compatible with Linux).

I am not sure about the card reader.

You could go back to Vista, there are pros to doing so and it is a matter for you to decide really. If you ask in this forum lots of people will just bash "Windoze". 

I wouldn't scrap a perfectly good computer under any circumstances. :-D
