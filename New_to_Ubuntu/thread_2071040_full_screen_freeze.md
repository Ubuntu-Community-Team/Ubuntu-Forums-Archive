### Post by deadflowr on 2012-10-14
I've had similar problems with flash.
However, I run chrome, and my flash problems come and go per each new release.
A solution, if viable, is to try to toggle super+S. This is the key binding for the workspace switcher. I find pressing this and then pressing again, the esc function works as normal.
Another important factor is the specs of your system. My one hatred of flash is that it(And Steve Jobs is right) is a resource hog.

Added: You might want to try to toggle off the hard acceleration in the flash settings.
