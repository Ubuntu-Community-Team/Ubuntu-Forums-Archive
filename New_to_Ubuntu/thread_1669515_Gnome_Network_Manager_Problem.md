### Post by fabricator4 on 2011-01-17
On the top panel you will see an indicator applet for the network devices.  
Right click no it and edit connections.  Select the tab for 3G networks, select your service, and click edit.  At the top you will see a tick box "connect automatically". Tick this on and apply/close.

You are now "good to go".
