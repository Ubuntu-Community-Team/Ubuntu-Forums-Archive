### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-05-30
Dell mini 10 has a partially supported graphical chipset, also known as gma500 (poulsbo). Jaunty and Karmic can be made work relatively well, with 3d, correct resolution and decent refresh rate, but Lucid is still a work in progress. Currently, it's not quite clear weather Lucid will have full gma500 support.
For more info see the links
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsPoulsbo](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsPoulsbo)
