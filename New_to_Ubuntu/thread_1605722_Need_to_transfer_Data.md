### Post by endotherm on 2010-10-25
well, take your dying hdd out, and put it in the enclosure. 
install your new hdd and install the OS of your choice.
plug in the external drive, and try your best to copy the files off. if they won;t copy they are probably already corrupted. a filesystem scan may help, or it may kill your drive, so leave that as a last resort. 

only copy user files, do not copy any system files from the old drive. just stuff within documents and settings or in any custom folders you may have created in the root of c or wherever. 

good luck
