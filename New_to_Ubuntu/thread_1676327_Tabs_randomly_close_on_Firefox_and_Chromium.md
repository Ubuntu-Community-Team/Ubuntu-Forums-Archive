### Post by Townley89 on 2011-01-27
If you hit both the left and right mouse buttons on a laptop, it can register that as a middle click, which is the hotkey for closing a tab. Experiment with this: click on a tab clearly left, then clearly right, then do both together. If you think its possible that you're hitting both, that could be why you're getting the random closures.
