### Post by QIII on 2017-02-08
> **dusty1980 said:**
> I'm losing my mind....I can not find the linux download link on the Juno website.

Are you on a Linux machine right now?  I see Linspire [here]("http://www.juno.com/signup/requirements.html#lindows").  Although Linspire has been discontinued, it was a Debian/Ubuntu-based OS, so it may very well be that using it will be little trouble.

You won't be able to find another service, free or otherwise, where she will have the same email address.  If others she corresponds with attempt to use her Juno address, she won't get the emails.

While it is a bit disheartening that you may have to pay again, this is what happens when we move hastily and do not research whether our desired software has a Linux port.  Consider it tuition.

If you simply can't get things to work, I would suggest getting your mother's machine back to Windows, whatever that costs.
