### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2011-07-03
I believe the way to do that is to change the boot order to boot from the pen/flash drive first instead of the hard drive.  Then choose to install over 9.10.  Be careful when you choose so you do not over-write the wrong partition and above all back up any data that you cannot afford to loose.  I'm not saying that you will, but Murphy's Law indicates that if you do not back it up you may loose it.  Enjoy.
