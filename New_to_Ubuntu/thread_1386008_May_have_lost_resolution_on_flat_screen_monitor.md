### Post by NCLI on 2010-01-20
The main problem(and, I presume, the reason why people have been hesitant to answer your post, is that all of this functionality was recently moved out of a human.readable text file called xorg.conf, and made completely automated. What I would do is use you CRT monitor, set the resolution to 800x640, and then plug your LCD back in. 

All monitors should be able to handle that resolution, so if it doesn't work, it may be something more serious.
