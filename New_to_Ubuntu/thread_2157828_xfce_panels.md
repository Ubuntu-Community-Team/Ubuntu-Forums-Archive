### Post by Toz on 2013-06-26
You have to unlock it first. Right-click the panel and select "Panel->Panel Preferences" and uncheck the "Lock panel" checkbox. Handle boxes will appear at the beginning and end of the panel. Drag them to move the panel. Don't forget to lock the panel after you have re-positioned it.
