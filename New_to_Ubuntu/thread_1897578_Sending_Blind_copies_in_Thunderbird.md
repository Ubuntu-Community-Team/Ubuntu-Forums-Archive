### Post by zerubbabel on 2011-12-19
> **daniell59 said:**
> How can I send an email to a few people without the recipients seeing the other people that I sent it to?

Thanks

Click on the "To:" field to the left of the first address field when you compose the message and you will see a menu appear. Choose "Bcc:" and then enter your recipient's address(es). That's all there is to it!
