If playing dvd jumps, with a simple test you can check if the problem is with dvd our the dvd player (VLC). Open your DVD from Places menu. In opened nautilus file manager window, try copy all content of your dvd to a place with enough space on your Hard Drive.
After copy finished, play copied files from the Hard Drive, if the problem still exists, it may be with the player software (VLC). But if you can not copy the dvd on to your HDD, then you can be sure that it's with your DVD!;)
