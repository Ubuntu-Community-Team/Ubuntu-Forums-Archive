### Post by OutOfReach on 2008-12-28
They are pretty much the same except the ones that you just "extract and run" are pre-compiled. And yes, the "extract and run" kind also have dependencies, it just doesn't inform you if you have them or not. 

Installing is really just putting the files of a program into different places in the filesystem.

Hope that made sense.
