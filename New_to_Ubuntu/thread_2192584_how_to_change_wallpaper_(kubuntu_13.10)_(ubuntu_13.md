### Post by codenine75a on 2013-12-08
[Kubuntu] Right click on the desktop -> Default Desktop Settings -> (look at the near bottom of the window) Open... -> Choose your picture -> Click Ok.
[Ubuntu]  The easiest way to set your wallpaper is to open it with firefox and right click on the view and Set as wallpaper.
