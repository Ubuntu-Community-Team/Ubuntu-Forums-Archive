### Post by andrewthomas on 2011-03-31
Go to [http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer10_square.html](http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer10_square.html)

click on [Download plug-in for 64-bit Linux]("http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplayer10/flashplayer10_2_p3_64bit_linux_111710.tar.gz") (TAR.GZ, 4.1 MB)

[http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplayer10/flashplayer10_2_p3_64bit_linux_111710.tar.gz](http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplayer10/flashplayer10_2_p3_64bit_linux_111710.tar.gz)

extract the file in your home directory, then copy the libflashplayer.so file to /usr/lib64/mozilla/plugins

Then restart your browser.
