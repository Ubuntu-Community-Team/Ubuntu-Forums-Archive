### Post by brazilla ray on 2008-05-02
I had a similar problem, try installing the libstdc++5 package.  You may need to re-install the Lightning extension after doing this.  From what I remember reading, Lightning needs libstdc++5 to compile correctly, but only libstdc++6 is installed on Hardy by default.  Having both installed is no problem, apparently.  Hope this helps.
