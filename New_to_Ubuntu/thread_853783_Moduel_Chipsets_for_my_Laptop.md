### Post by st33med on 2008-07-08
I hate to tell you this but...
Short Answer: You can't remove the Intel Integrated Card
Long Answer: You could, however, you would have to do some serious micro soldering to get it to work. You see, Intel Integrated cards are not meant to be removed because they are integrated into the motherboard. So, if you want a brand spanking new GPU, you will have to get another motherboard that supports removable GPU cards.
