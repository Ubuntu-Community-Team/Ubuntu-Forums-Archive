### Post by underquark on 2010-02-24
> **Nebelhom said:**
> Must be the earings of the fan then.Oh, the visual imagery that this conjures up.

You could try lubricating the fan.  Ensure any dirt has been removed, remove the seal over the fan shaft and use just a tiny drop of light oil (sewing-machine or hair-clipper oil, for instance).  Will fail eventually, but will keep it quiet while you either search out a replacement fan (measure the fan diameter and look up any details you can find on the internet regarding voltage for your graphics card) or can save up for a new graphics card.
