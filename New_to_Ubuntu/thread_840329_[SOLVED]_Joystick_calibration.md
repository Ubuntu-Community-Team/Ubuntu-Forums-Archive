### Post by erginemr on 2008-07-10
There are two more generic joystick calibrators that you can try:
1. jscal: [http://linux.die.net/man/1/jscal](http://linux.die.net/man/1/jscal)
2. jscalibrator: [http://linux.about.com/cs/linux101/g/jscalibrator.htm](http://linux.about.com/cs/linux101/g/jscalibrator.htm)

The second one is in the repositories.
