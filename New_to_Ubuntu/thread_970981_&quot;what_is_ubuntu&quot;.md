### Post by ecs_pf5 on 2008-11-04
It's that thing you revisit at regular and decreasing intervals everytime you run into attempts to prevent you doing what you want with your hardware unless you $$$cough up the wonga$$$.. Every time something happens to remind you that there's a conspiracy out there amongst many bloated hardware and software organisations, to screw as much money out of you as possible in return for as little effort as possible. A fair day's pay for a fair day's work is one thing but using various contrived lock-ins to milk the public really gets my goat

This time for me, the spark for another round of Ubuntu is having run into a Fujitsu Siemens laptop in which the BIOS is locked by Trusted Computing - based methodologies. Fine, no prob, somebody forgot their password.. I don't mind paying for a service to reset it, but today they want £100 for what amounts to a 3 minute phonecall and a bit of keyboard tapping. Get them cash cows into the parlour!

Okay, that's a silly example because a locked BIOS isn't really an OS thing and Trusted Computing is set to affect all OS's but, it kinda illustrates how I feel about computing today in general. Our freedoms are being methodically stripped away by a conspiracy amongst the Largest OS supplier, hardware vendors and government, under increasingly flimsy veneers of 'security' and fake 'trust'. I love the fact that Linux is as free as you're going to get. That used to mean to me, 90% that it cost no money, and 10% that anyone could have a hand in the code. Over time that's changed, I'm now much more interested in the open source ethos than the fact that there's no monetary fee. 

Every time I return to Linux after an absence, it's that little bit better and I find it that little bit easier. I don't know how much of that's because I'm ever so slowly building knowledge upon knowledge about the OS, and how much is because of actual improvements in the code of Linux.. 

Last time I left was about 6 months ago and I kinda drifted away back to XP because try as I might, I couldn't find a solution to a very weird keyboard issue I was having. Maybe it's come good now. Hope so..
