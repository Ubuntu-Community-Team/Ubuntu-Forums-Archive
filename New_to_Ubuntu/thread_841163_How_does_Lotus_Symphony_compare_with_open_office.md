### Post by tact on 2008-06-26
I installed OpenOffice v3.0 beta and also Lotus Symphony beta 4 and Lotus Notes 8.5 beta 1 .  

Of course I also have OpenOffice 2.4 that comes with Hardy.

Of the 4 applications I like OpenOffice 3.0 beta the best.   It is faster than the others, smoother, and can handle Microsoft Office 2007 new formats for documents, presentations etc.

Symphony and Notes are slow, look dated, clunky, and crashed on me more often than the OpenOffice 3 beta
