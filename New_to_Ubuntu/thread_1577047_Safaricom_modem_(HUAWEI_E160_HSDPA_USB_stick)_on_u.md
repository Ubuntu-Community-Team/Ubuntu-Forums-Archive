### Post by abs_kkk on 2010-09-18
ubuntu is easy for new users too. :)

ok first I assume u have the network settings of your service provider.
secondly to create new network connections go to "System" (located in the upper panel) then highlight the "Preferences" sub menu and choose "network connections"
After the window opens choose the type of device u r using (mobile broadband) then click "Add"
then it is a simple step by step instructions ,just follow them.

Good luck ^^
