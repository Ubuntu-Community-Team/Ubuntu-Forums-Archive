### Post by kudos1uk on 2009-12-31
I fixed it the clue was here:

Section "Extensions"
        Option      "Composite" "disable"
EndSection


Changed disabled to enable and all is perfect, even got compiz and my cube back even though my understanding was that it could not work without the ati restricted driver.

Happy again....
