### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2012-01-12
Press the top button, the one with the ubuntu logo on it. You can then search for apps that you've installed.
To add them to the bar, just drag and drop them into the bar. To remove them, either right-click and untick 'keep in launcher', or drag them to the rubbish bin. :)
