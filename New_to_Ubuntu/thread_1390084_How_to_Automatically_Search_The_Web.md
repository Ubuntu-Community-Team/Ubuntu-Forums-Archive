### Post by t0p on 2010-01-25
There's a (very short) discussion about automating Google searches [here]("http://www.unitedforums.co.uk/vb/website-development-scripting/9241-automated-google-search.html").  Very short and not much use.  But one of the participants suggests [Google Search SOAP API]("http://code.google.com/apis/soapsearch/")... no longer available, but apparently the [AJAX Search API]("http://code.google.com/apis/ajaxsearch/") will do the same.  This will be of limited use if you're not a coder.  But if you can code, get scratching that itch!  Also, in the aforementioned discussion, someone mentions screen scraping.  This will require knowledge of HTML.
