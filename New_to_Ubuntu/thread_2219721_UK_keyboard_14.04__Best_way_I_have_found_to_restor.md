### Post by chamberhorse on 2014-04-25
I notice that many others have trouble getting the keyboard layout of their country in U 14.04. I do not undertsand some of the solutions given as I am an absolute novice. Whilst "setxkbmap gb" gives a UK board temporarily it is not a permanent solution. I have found that by installing the latest U 14.04 BETA and then letting it update to the final release that the updates leave the ability to determine the keyboard mapping intact.

Hope this helps somebody. It did me.

### Post by coffeecat on 2014-04-25
I find it simpler to use the GUI tools provided. System Settings -> Keyboard -> Text entry link.

Maybe you have come across threads where people are describing unusual situations, or have unknowingly caused a problem in their system settings. I doubt whether keyboard layout problems are really that common.
