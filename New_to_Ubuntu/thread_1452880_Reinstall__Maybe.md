### Post by e4uforums on 2010-04-12
During your boot process you should have the option to press a button and enter your BIOS.  Usually this is the delete key or F10 or something.  Press this then you should come across an option to select your boot order.  Place your CD drive first, save, and reboot.

### Post by dFlyer on 2010-04-12
On my computer, a dell pressing f12 at boot brings up alt boot methods. I don't think all computers us f12 as my daughter requires escape pressed to bring up alt boot methods. When you get into alt boot just select cd/dvd from the list and you should be able to reinstall. Just make sure you back up everything in windows should you change your mind and want to revert back.
