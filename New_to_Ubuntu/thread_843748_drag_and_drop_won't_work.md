### Post by markbuntu on 2008-06-28
You know, I had that problem once and I just can't remember how I fixed it so it had to be pretty quick and easy.

OK, try this, log out and log back in in anything but the default previous session. Then log out and log back in as normal. This will basically reset a lot of things that can get screwed up back to the defaults because when you log in the previous session is loaded and if that is screwed up your next session will be too.

There is a little icon on the bottom left of the login screen that you can use to do this.
