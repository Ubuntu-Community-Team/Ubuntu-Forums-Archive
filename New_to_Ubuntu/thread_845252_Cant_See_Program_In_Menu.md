### Post by ayenack on 2008-06-30
Right click on **Applications>Edit Menus** then look for KOrganizer in Office of the Applications menus if it's there make sure it's ticked and it'll appear in the Applications menu Office where you ticked it.

The reason Other un-checks its self is because there's nothing in it.

### Post by ayenack on 2008-06-30
Do this. It'll work!

1, Right click on Applications
2, Select Edit Menus
3. Click on Office
4, Tick KOrganizer
5, Close the Edit Menus window
6, Left click on Applications
7, Scroll down to Office
8, There you will see KOrganizer
