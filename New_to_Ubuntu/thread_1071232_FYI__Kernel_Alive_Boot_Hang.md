### Post by jeepfreak2002 on 2009-02-15
I am running a Core 2 Quad, 4 Gig Crucial Memory, Dual Boot on Hardy.

I began experiencing boot hangs out of the blue, saying" Kernel Alive, mapping tables up to 130000000 @ 8000-e000"

Turns out that my i-pod shuffle and Seagate external USB hard drives were screwing things up.  They both work fine once booted, but if they are plugged in it hangs.  Just an FYI.  Personally, I think that it is just the i-pod, but I unplugged the Seagate 1st.  Once I unplugged the i-pod shuffle, the compute proceeded to boot just fine!.
