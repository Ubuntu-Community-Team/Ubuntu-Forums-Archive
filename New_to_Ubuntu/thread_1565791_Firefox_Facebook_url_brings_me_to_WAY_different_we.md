### Post by Rubi1200 on 2010-09-01
Try clearing the cache and cookies in Firefox:
Tools > Clear Recent History > choose to delete everything.

Restart Firefox and see if the problem still exists.

If it does, consider switching to another DNS server such as Google DNS:
[http://code.google.com/speed/public-dns/docs/using.html](http://code.google.com/speed/public-dns/docs/using.html)

Scroll down to find the instructions for use on Linux.

Other DNS services include Open DNS and Comodo DNS.

Also, take a look here for possible solutions:
[http://firefox-tutorials.blogspot.com/](http://firefox-tutorials.blogspot.com/)

(courtesy of forum member lovinglinux)

Hope this helps.
