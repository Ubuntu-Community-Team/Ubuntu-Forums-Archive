### Post by laffinet on 2009-02-01
In short: yes. 

Once you have Linux installed you then need to configure it so it "sees" all the other drives and knows where the videos are.

What system are you planning to install ? Mythbuntu ? What hardware do you have ? Have you checked compatibility ?

What other media do you have that you'd like to keep ?

I would maybe suggest to do a dual boot installation, that way you get to keep your windows system in case things don't work out or you don't like it or whatever and can easily revert back to windows (which you hopefully won't have to).
