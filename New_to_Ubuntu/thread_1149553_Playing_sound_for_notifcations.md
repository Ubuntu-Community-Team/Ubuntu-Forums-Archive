[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NotifyOSD#Sound](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NotifyOSD#Sound)

Here is a link to a specs page. From my understanding of it, this version of notify-osd doesn't play any sounds itself. It's design-intent is to provide a non-intrusive, non-interactive notification.

The program calling it could have a sound associated with it, though that would be up to the program itself, and any settings the end-user chose.

I'm one of those people that doesn't want my PC squawking at me for every little thing, so one of the first things I do is turn off any sound events. I don't like anything between me and my music.... :guitar:
