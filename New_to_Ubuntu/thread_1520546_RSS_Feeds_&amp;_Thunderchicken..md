### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-07-09
Hi,
Open Thunderbird, then go to the menus;
Edit > Account Settings > Account Actions (button at the bottom), then select "add other account", choose "blogs and newsfeeds", give the account a name, click next, then click finish. 

Now on the left hand side of the Account Settings window, click on the account you just created. Then click the button on the right side called "manage subscriptions"... then the rest should be smooth sailing. 

I would recommend using an RSS reader though such as "liferea", unless you only have a few RSS subscriptions to manage. Thunderbird doesn't want to import my bunch of subscriptions through a single xml or opml file.
