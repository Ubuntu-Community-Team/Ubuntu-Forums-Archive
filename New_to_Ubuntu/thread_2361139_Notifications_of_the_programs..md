### Post by vasa1 on 2017-05-13
> **howefield said:**
> Is the notification before you get to the Desktop or after the Desktop has loaded ?

If it is before I am wondering if it is the fsck information that you are seeing.

And that would look something like```
/dev/sda1: clean, 3811/49152 files, 105570/171776 blocks
```at the top left of your screen. And it would disappear in a couple of seconds. See [https://unix.stackexchange.com/questions/185265/what-does-clean-x-y-mean-in-fsck-output](https://unix.stackexchange.com/questions/185265/what-does-clean-x-y-mean-in-fsck-output) for more.
