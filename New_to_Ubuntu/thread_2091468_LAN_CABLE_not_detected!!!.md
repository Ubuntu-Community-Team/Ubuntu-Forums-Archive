### Post by Ms. Daisy on 2012-12-08
I would suspect problems with the cord itself before I suspected a problem with Ubuntu.

Check that the cable is good (does it work on other computers?)
Check that the cable is completely plugged in on both ends.
Check that the ethernet port is fully functioning on your laptop.

If you have determined that it's not hardware, come back and we'll look into problems with Ubuntu.
