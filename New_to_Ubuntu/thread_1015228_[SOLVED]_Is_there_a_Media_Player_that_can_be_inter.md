### Post by fooman on 2008-12-18
there is a package in the repos called "music-applet" that will allow you to control your music players like that.

```
sudo apt-get install music-applet
```

after it installs,  right click on a panel and choose "add to panel".  then find music applet in the list.

when its in the panel, right click and choose plugins to change the player.
