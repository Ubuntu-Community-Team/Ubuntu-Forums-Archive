### Post by Silent Warrior on 2010-05-02
What chip is that, precisely? ALC888? First order of business is to make sure your speakers aren't muted - put your mouse-cursor over the volume-applet and scroll up and down, before settling on a suitable level. Then open the very same applet - if we're still using PulseAudio, you should see a Hardware-tab. If there is something there, this'll be dealt with in a flash, most likely.
