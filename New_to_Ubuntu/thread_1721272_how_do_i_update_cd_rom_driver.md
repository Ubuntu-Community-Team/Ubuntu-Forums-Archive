### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-04-04
The fact that is not detected by both operating systems seems to indicate a possible connection problem , the drive is bad , or there is no available driver (out dated) .
 
From the Windows side ,  select contol panel > device manager and see if it appears on the list . If it is on the list , select it to locate driver information and update if possible.
 
I would also power down the computer , remove the panel and inspect the mother board to drive cable.
