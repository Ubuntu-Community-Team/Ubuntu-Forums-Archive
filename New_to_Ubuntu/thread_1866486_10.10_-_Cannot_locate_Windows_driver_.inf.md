### Post by cozumel on 2011-10-21
As TeoBigusGeekus says, either one of the first two drivers would have worked.  Just not the one for 2.6.34 as that is for older kernel.

The first set of drivers covers the whole card and all of its functions (incl 8188CE I think) whilst the second driver is only for the 8188CE component and nothing else.

So in effect, when you installed 8188CE 2.6.35 and later version you were replacing part of what was already installed in the first download lol.  But is all okay.  Should not cause any problems.  Ubuntu appears to ignore the driver for incorrect (older) kernel.
