If Transmission is always seeding, then chances are that Transmission will run incredibly slow, as it will always be looking for parts of files that take time to pull off of the external drive.

However, I am unsure whether or not Transmission looking for files would make the OS or other programs run slowly.

If you plan on seeding a lot of torrents, I would really try to either put the HDD inside the box, or connect the external with a connection faster than USB 2.0. Seeding torrents will always slow your regular HDD traffic on the external.

For example, let's say that you want to watch a movie that's on the external, you'd be trying to cram all the data for the torrents and the movie through the slow USB connection, which would cause the torrents to upload slowly, and would probably cause the movie to be really choppy.

-Kurt
