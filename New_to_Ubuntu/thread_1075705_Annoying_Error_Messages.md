### Post by Therion on 2009-02-20
Maximus is a package installed by default.  It has something to do with windows being maximized when you open certain applications (like Firefox) I believe.

You might be able to solve this by going to Preferences -> Sessions and unchecking "Maximus" but too be honest with you, we're on the ragged edge of my understanding of Ubuntu right now.  This is the most delicate (meaning easily reversible) solution I can think of.
