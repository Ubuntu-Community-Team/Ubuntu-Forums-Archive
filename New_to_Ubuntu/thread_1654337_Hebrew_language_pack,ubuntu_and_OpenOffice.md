### Post by RBarak on 2010-12-28
Dear all,
My problem was: I added Hebrew to my Ubuntu. I could type in Hebrew in OpenOffice, but the signs and numbers all came wrong. In other words, I couldn't Indent Right.

I solved this by installing a full language support:

System --> Administration --> Language Support
then in the next window:
--> Install/Remove Languages...
then in the next window:
--> Hebrew (and here I added everything I could, Translations, Spellchecking and writing aids, Extra fonts)

Now I can type and indent and everything is just as I like it.

Hope I helped.

Reut
