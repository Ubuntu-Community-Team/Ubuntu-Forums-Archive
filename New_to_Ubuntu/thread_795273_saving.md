### Post by Paqman on 2008-05-15
You can subscribe to a thread using the "thread tools" button. Alternatively just hit CTRL-D to save it in your browser.

---

### Post by HotShotDJ on 2008-05-15
Or, also in Thread Tools, choose "Show Printable Version" and then use File --> Save Page As to save the resultant page to your harddrive.
