### Post by 2F4U on 2012-04-07
I would suggest that you start by giving the specs of your machine. Long boot times are sometimes due to a wireless adapter searching for a suitable connection. So if you are using wireless, one thing you can try is using a wired connection and see if boot times decrease.
That your graphics card is listed as unknown is probably because the proprietary drivers are not installed. This may or may not be related to the black screen issue.
I would also suggest that you look into the system lock and see if something unusual, e. g. an error message, happens during the boot phase.
