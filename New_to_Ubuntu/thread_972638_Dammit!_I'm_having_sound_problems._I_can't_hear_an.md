### Post by NightGoddess21 on 2008-11-06
My husband had the same problem, and I have finally figured it out. Follow these simple steps and you should have sound going in no time at all. 

1. Double click the speaker icon at the top right of your launcher pannel.

2. Click "edit". 

3. Click "preferences".

4. In the volume control preferences window click "External amplifier".

5. now there will be a new tab labeled switches next to playback in the volume control window. click that tab labeled "switches".

6. now uncheck the "external amplifier" and close all windows out.

7. everything should be working fine now it did for us.

Let me know how it went! Good Luck!!!
