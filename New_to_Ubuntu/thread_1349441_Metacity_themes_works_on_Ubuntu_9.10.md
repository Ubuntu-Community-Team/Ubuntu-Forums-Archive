### Post by VCoolio on 2009-12-08
They will work; you'll have to press customize in the appearance window and go to the borders tab to find them. Some gtk2 themes also include a metacity theme.
The only alternative is emerald (on gnome-look you can check the beryl themes), but that is no longer maintained. Metacity is default in gnome.
