### Post by CatKiller on 2009-05-31
> **steeleyuk said:**

That's what I do. I did have a dual-screen setup for a while, but I found that most of the time I just had one turned off. So I got a bigger monitor and learned to enjoy the convenience of just being able to spin the cube round to see what I had open on another workspace. And you can often see the quick bit of information that you might want to look at with the Scale plugin. Using Compiz has given me desk space for bigger speakers :)

The only thing I miss having two screens for is editing large images in the GIMP. Then it's handy to have the image fullscreen on one monitor and all the tools and dialogues on the other. But I don't do that very much.
