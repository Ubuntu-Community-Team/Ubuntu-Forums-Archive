### Post by Penguin Guy on 2010-03-04
I just received the following error message:

[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=148977&stc=1&d=1267723846[/IMG]
> 
**Could not grab your mouse.**

A malicious client may be eavesdropping on your session or you may have just clicked a menu or some application just decided to get focus.

Try again.

After a bit of research I found out that it's a security feature used to detect key-loggers, unfortunately it is also activated when you have the main menu open. A short-term fix for this is to simply click off the menu before you open any password dialogue. If this message appeared when you did not have the main menu open you should check that you don't have a key-logger or [submit a bug report]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/update-manager/+filebug") (possibly related to [this bug]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/update-manager/+bug/508218")).
