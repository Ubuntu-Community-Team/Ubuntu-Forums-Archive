### Post by Tatty on 2008-09-02
In addition to the excellent psychocats tutorial linked to above by sef, you may want to read the page from the ubuntu community help pages [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WindowsDualBoot](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WindowsDualBoot)

This is trivially easy to do with current ubuntu installations, however make sure you read carefully what the installer is telling you - its never good reading a forum post from a user who rushed through without reading and has installed over their windows partition :)

And as sef said, make sure you back up all your valuable data first! Just in case.

Good luck.
