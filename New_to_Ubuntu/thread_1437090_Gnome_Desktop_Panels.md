### Post by mechro on 2010-03-23
You have to add a **Window List** applet to a panel... Right-click panel > Add to Panel > Window List. Having said that it will only repeat all your working windows on each panel, so there's little point in adding extra panels for this situation.

You can configure **Window List** to group your windows and/or show only windows on current workspace... Right-click the very faint button on the left of all your listed windows and you can edit Preferences.

Did you take note of johngreth's suggestion of using more Workspaces? From your screenshot your **Workspace** switcher is next to the Trash icon on your bottom panel. You can have more than two Workspaces.

So combine grouping of your Window List with multiple Workspaces and you can make things more efficient.

### Post by johngreth on 2010-03-23
The main purpose of adding panels is to fit more application launchers. It's only useful if you want to always be able to see a launcher for every application you have on your computer.
