### Post by Duskao on 2009-04-30
This might seem dumb, and sorry if you already checked, but did you make sure that all the volume controls are turned up? Click on the speaker icon near the top of the screen, then volume control and go through them to make sure. I had the same problem when I upgraded and found that a few of them defaulted to low/mute, PCM and Front were both down for me. That fixed my problem, hope this helps.
