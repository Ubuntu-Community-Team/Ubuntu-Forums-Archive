### Post by Dynaflow on 2009-05-12
Try to avoid keeping the Prt Scr (Print Screen) key pressed for prolonged periods of time.

If it's not that hotkey being triggered somehow, and it's not a take-screenshot panel applet that's misbehaving, then there's something weird going on.  If you're willing, you could kill two birds with one stone by doing a fresh install of the latest version of Ubuntu, 9.04.
