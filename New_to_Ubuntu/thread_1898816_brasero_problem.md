### Post by crazy bird on 2011-12-22
You're not the only person suffering from Brasero issues. Do a search on this forum with the keyword brasero and you see what i mean.
 
Brasero is well known of producing coasters. It is unstable, unreliable and not really user friendly due to this. Try Xfburn, or K3B (if you're familiar with Nero Burningtools under Windows you'll see the strength and capability of K3B) or the other tools mentioned above.
