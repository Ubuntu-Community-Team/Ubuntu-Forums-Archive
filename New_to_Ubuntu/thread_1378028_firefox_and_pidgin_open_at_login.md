### Post by LuisGMarine on 2010-01-11
Go to System > Preferences > Preferred Applications.  With the "Startup Programs" tab selected scrub through and remove firefox and pidgin if you see it there.  Also if this doesn't fix the problem, open up pidgins preferences and look for a "start at login" option and disable it.
