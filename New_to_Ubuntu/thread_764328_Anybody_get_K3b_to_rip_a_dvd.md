### Post by Ubuntiac on 2008-04-23
I tried for a long time to get K3B to rip a DVD without success. Then I tried AcidRip and haven't looked back. Just pop in the disc, click "Load Disc" on the right, select the longest chapter and click the Encode button. Easy.

I also like to add "Crop" (to take off black bars), add some gain (usually 20db) and change the file name. The other cool thing is that you can look at the beginning of the ripped file after only a few seconds to check that the settings are good.

The only downside is that it doesn't seem to use multi-cores. Other than that smooooooth. :)
