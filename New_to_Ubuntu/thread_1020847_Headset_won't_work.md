### Post by lordadi on 2008-12-24
Hi,

Right click on the speaker on the top right panel and select "Open Volume Control". In there, make sure that the Headphone out is not muted. Also, if that does not work, you may have to change the device to get it all working. In this case, select File>Change device in the window opened earlier and choose a different device. Generally, in this situation (unless you have a mic), you are looking for any device that lets sound out of your headset so the device should NOT say capture (unless you have a mic). So you are looking around for a device that is either playback or does not specify.

You will have to experiment and see which device works for you.

Cheers,



Lordadi.
