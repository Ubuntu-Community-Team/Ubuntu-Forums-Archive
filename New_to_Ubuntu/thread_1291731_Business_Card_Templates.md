### Post by wnderinguy34 on 2009-10-15
I use the business card templates from [http://www.avery.com/avery/en_us/Templates-%26-Software/Templates/Cards/Business-Cards/_/Ns=]("http://www.avery.com/avery/en_us/Templates-%26-Software/Templates/Cards/Business-Cards/_/Ns=") for MSWord in Open office and then print them on Avery  8371 business card stock . I get 10 cards per page and the perforations don't leave any little pieces behind,resulting in a very clean edge.
