### Post by NightwishFan on 2011-09-05
You can set your music library in preferences, then you drag stuff from the left to the popup in the middle. Simple. :)

Also you can re-arrange the interface, there is a way to unlock it in one of the menus.

Overall I am sorry to say I dislike Amarok2. It is not bad, but Amarok1.4 was like the Terry Kath of media players. Clementine has replaced it and I suggest you give that a try.
[http://www.clementine-player.org/](http://www.clementine-player.org/)


Edit: good point above, the vast majority of software runs perfectly on another environment like KDE/Gnome.
