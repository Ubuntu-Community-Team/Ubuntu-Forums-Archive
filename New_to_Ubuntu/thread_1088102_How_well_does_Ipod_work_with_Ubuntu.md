### Post by noobuntu7 on 2009-03-05
Ubuntu works very well with iPods (besides the iPod touch and iPhone). Ubuntu doesn't have iTunes support, but using Banshee, Amarok, or gkpod you can sync your music and video to the iPod.

You can find more documentation here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPod](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPod). 

Also, I would highly recommend [Linux Multimedia Hacks]("http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0596100760?ie=UTF8&tag=supersonics-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=0596100760") by Kyle Rankin. Hope that helped!
