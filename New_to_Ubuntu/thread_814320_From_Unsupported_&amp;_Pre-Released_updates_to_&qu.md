### Post by sayakb on 2008-05-31
If you don't intend to get involved with beta software testing, keep the proposed and unsupported updates disabled from System->Administration->Software Sources.
Different versions of software may reside together in the recommended and unsupported sections that might cause conflicts or even a broken cache.
