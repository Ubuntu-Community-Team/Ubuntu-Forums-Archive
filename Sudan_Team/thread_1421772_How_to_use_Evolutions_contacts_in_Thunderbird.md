### Post by zero-n on 2010-03-04
1- In Evolution open your contact list and select [B][COLOR=Red]File[/COLOR] >> [COLOR=Red]Save Address Book As VCard
[/COLOR] [/B]
2- Open this website to change contacts type from** VCard** to **CSV** or **LDIF** which are supported in Thunderbird:
[URL="http://labs.brotherli.ch/vcfconvert/"][COLOR=Red][B]
http://labs.brotherli.ch/vcfconvert/ [/B][/COLOR][/URL] 

3- In Thunderbird Open your address book from **[COLOR=Red]Tools[/COLOR] >> [COLOR=Red]Address Book[/COLOR] or use CTRL+SHIFT+B**

4- Import your LDIF of CSV contact list from **[COLOR=Red]Tools[/COLOR] >> [COLOR=Red]Import[/COLOR]**
