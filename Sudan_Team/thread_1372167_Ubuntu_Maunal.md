### Post by zero-n on 2010-01-04
A complete Beginners Manual for Ubuntu, featuring comprehensive guides, howTos and information on anything you need to know after first installing Ubuntu.
 
Designed to be as user-friendly and easy to follow as possible, it should provide the first point of reference to any Ubuntu newcomer with lots of information in one easy to access PDF file. Plus, every six months there will be a new revision released to coincide with each new release of Ubuntu.
 
 
 
**_Goals for the guide:_**
 
- Well written, easy to follow
- Professional looking
- Detailed, but not overwhelming
- Revisions every 6 months
 
Wiki : [**[COLOR=red]http://wiki.ubuntu.com/ubuntu-manual[/COLOR]**]("http://wiki.ubuntu.com/ubuntu-manual")
 
Luanchpad link : [**[COLOR=red]https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-manual[/COLOR]**]("https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-manual")
