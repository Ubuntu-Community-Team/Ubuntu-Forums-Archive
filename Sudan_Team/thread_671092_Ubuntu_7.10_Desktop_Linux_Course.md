### Post by Y2J on 2008-01-18
Official Ubuntu project is providing free training manuals in PDF format. There are 10 lessons in total with the last lesson covering Partitioning and Booting optional. The course is modular - 2 days if all lessons are covered however, topics and lessons can be selected as required.

There are 2 versions of the course:
=> An Instructor Guide which has instructor notes and answers to the exercise questions together. 
[Download link]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Training?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=instructor.pdf") [72M]:

=> A student guide which does not have instructor notes; answers to the exercises are provided separately.
[Download link]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Training?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=student.pdf") [73M]:


More information available at [Ubuntu Training Wiki]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Training").
