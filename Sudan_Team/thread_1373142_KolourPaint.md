[SIZE=2]**What is KolourPaint ?**[/SIZE]

KolourPaint is a free, easy-to-use paint program for    [KDE]("http://www.kde.org/") (a desktop environment for UNIX). 
  It aims to be conceptually simple to understand; providing a level of functionality targeted towards the average user.  KolourPaint is designed for daily tasks like:
  
[LIST]
[*]Painting - drawing diagrams and "finger painting.
[*]Image Manipulation - editing screenshots and photos; applying effects
[*]Icon Editing - drawing clipart and logos with transparency
[/LIST]
[SIZE=2]**Website:**[/SIZE]
[http://kolourpaint.sourceforge.net/](http://kolourpaint.sourceforge.net/)

[SIZE=2]**Screenshots:**[/SIZE]
[COLOR=Red][http://kolourpaint.sourceforge.net/screenshots.html](http://kolourpaint.sourceforge.net/screenshots.html)

[/COLOR] [SIZE=2][B]Download:
[/B][/SIZE]```

```
