### Post by zero-n on 2011-04-09
I have install Firefox from it official PPA but i had these problems:
- History is not working.
- Bookmarks disappeared.
- Back & Froward buttons won't works.

after some googling i found a solution for it in [Mozillazine.org Knowledge Base]("http://kb.mozillazine.org/Bookmarks_history_and_toolbar_buttons_not_working_-_Firefox").

**Steps to fix the problem:**
1- Open Firefox profile folder:
[COLOR=Red]**Help**[/COLOR] >> **[COLOR=Red]Troubleshooting[/COLOR] [COLOR=Red]Information[/COLOR]**.
& then in Application Basics click on [B][COLOR=Red]Open[/COLOR] [COLOR=Red]Containing[/COLOR] [COLOR=Red]Folder
[/COLOR][/B][COLOR=Red][COLOR=Black]this will open a window of profile folder location.
2- Close Firefox.
3- Delete every file beginning with places.sqlite
4- Restart your Firefox & it will automatically restore your bookmarks
[/COLOR][/COLOR]
