### Post by Terrycymru on 2009-10-25
To answer the original question here is my experience. Things have gone disastrously wrong for me upgrading a WUBI install of 9.04 to 9.10. Details at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1299796](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1299796) .

If you are contemplating an upgrade make sure you copy \ubuntu to say \ubuntucopy before you start then if things go wrong you can go back to your current working version simply by renaming \ubuntucopy to \ubuntu.

I intend waiting a few weeks before I try again!
