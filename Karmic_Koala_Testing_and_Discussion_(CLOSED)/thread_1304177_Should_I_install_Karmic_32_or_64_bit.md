### Post by bribera on 2009-10-29
The [Adobe Penguin.swf team finally released]("http://blogs.adobe.com/penguin.swf/2008/11/now_supporting_16_exabytes.html") a native 64-bit plugin. Unfortunately, it's slower than its 32-bit friend (if you can believe that Flash on Linux could get any slower).

If flash is important to you, you could go without 64-bit support a little longer. I find it the slower 64-bit version tolerable, and I need 64-bit support for some development work. But for most use cases, it really won't make a big difference.
