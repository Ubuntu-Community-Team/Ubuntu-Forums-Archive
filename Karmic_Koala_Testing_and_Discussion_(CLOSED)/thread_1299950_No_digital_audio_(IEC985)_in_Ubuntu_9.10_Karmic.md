### Post by Skerit on 2009-10-24
Just a little heads up, because this can be an annoying problem for people who have no idea how to fix this.

Even though I had set up everything as it was supposed to be in the new sound-preferences dialog (choosing the "Digital Stereo (IEC985) output"), it wasn't enough.

When I opened up alsamixer in the terminal it appeared that the IEC985 tab was completely muted. 
Turning up the volume there fixed the problem, of course.

I can not understand how this dead simple feature didn't make it into the sound-preferences dialog.

I see a lot of people struggling with this.
