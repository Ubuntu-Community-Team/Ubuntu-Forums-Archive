Yes, I can confirm that setting radeon.modeset=1 in the kernel line of the boot stanza in menu.lst causes kernel 2.6.31-2 to show the same vertical line pattern as we got with 2.6.31-1.  Grub flags the option as "unknown" and says it will ignore it; presumably it is passed intact to the kernel, which switches on KMS.

So we know that the 2.6.31-2 kernel has KMS for Radeon included, but switched off.

And that it's not a good idea to switch it back on!

Note that the . between radeon and modeset is required.....

### Post by BwackNinja on 2009-07-08
If you're using radeon r300-r500 and want to have your userland up to date (read: dri2), update from:
[https://launchpad.net/~xorg-edgers/+archive/radeon-kms](https://launchpad.net/~xorg-edgers/+archive/radeon-kms)

and update your xorg-server here to 1.6.2 (1.6.1.901 has a transparency bug fixed in 1.6.2, I can't wait until it hits the repos)
[https://edge.launchpad.net/~xorg-edgers/+archive/ppa?field.name_filter=&field.status_filter=published&field.series_filter=karmic](https://edge.launchpad.net/~xorg-edgers/+archive/ppa?field.name_filter=&field.status_filter=published&field.series_filter=karmic)

You'll also want to disable splash screen in your grub/menu.lst

Its been working perfectly for me so far, no crashes and still fast on my ol' radeon X300.
