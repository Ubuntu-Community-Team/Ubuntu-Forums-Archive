### Post by freeman2000 on 2009-05-28
My fonts in Shiretoko 3.5 & Minefield 3.6 look exactly as they do in Firefox 3.0.10.  If you want to change the fonts in either 3.5 or 3.6, go to the Menu Bar and click on  Edit --> Preferences, then click on the "Content" tab, go to the middle of the window and select the font type, size, and color that you want.  Good luck.
