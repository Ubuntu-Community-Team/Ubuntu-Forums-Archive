### Post by Flimm on 2009-10-03
Hello everyone, I've made an xsplash theme for Ubuntu Karmic. Just download [this archive](http://dl.getdropbox.com/u/1198858/sunrise_xsplash.tar.gz), extract it and run install.run in a terminal. I've also attached a screenshot of the theme in action.

The theme is a proof-of-concept, I just pinched a freely licensed wallpaper and turned it into a theme.

The way the theme works is to replace /usr/share/images/xsplash/bg_*.jpg with files of my own. I could theoretically also replace the throbbers and the logos, but I haven't. I also changed the icon and metacity themes gdm uses to go back to less dark colours.

I've only tested this in a VirtualBox, tell me if it doesn't work. One thing I've noticed is that the throbbers don't appear anymore, does anyone have any idea why?
