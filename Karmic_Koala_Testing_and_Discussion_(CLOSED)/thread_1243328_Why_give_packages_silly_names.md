### Post by mejy on 2009-08-18
The package information on launchpad [seems to explain this]("https://launchpad.net/byobu") - rather than being somebody's nickname it's a name that is appropriate if obscure, and gives the package some sort of identity and makes it less generic.  You're welcome to disagree with this policy, but it's common throughout the free software world (eg. Ubuntu, Gnome, Gimp, Firefox all give very little indication of the application's function).
