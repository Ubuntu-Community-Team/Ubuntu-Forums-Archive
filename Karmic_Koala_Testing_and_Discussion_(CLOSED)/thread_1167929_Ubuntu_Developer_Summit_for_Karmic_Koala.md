### Post by 23meg on 2009-05-23
The Ubuntu Developer Summit (UDS) is the periodic gathering where Ubuntu developers / contributors and a broad range of key participants in free software come together to shape the next release of Ubuntu. The summit for Karmic Koala is kicking off on the 25th of May at Palau de Congressos de Catalunya in Barcelona, Spain.

You can find detailed information about various aspects of the event, including the schedule and remote participation information (you'll be able to listen to and join the talks via VoIP and IRC, and get live updates via microblogging streams) at the wiki page:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UDSKarmic](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UDSKarmic)

I'll be flying to Barcelona shortly to attend the event, and will report on the proceedings in the forum and elsewhere.
