### Post by slumbergod on 2009-10-25
I've noticed a lot of people asking questions about whether installing Karmic Beta or RC will still allow upgrading to final later in the week. In response, I have noticed a few sarcastic people spouting the usual stupid response: use the search.

I have two things to say to those people:
1. The search facility in Ubuntu Forums is perhaps the most useless search implementation I have seen. It is easier to use Google search externally to recover threads from this site. Expecting new users to come here and find what they are looking for on such a huge site and with such a pathetic search function is really unfair.

2. The moderators for Ubuntu Forums should take some initiative and actually make an informative sticky that shows across all sections. Better still, how about a special Karmic section? Sure, the info is scattered across the net but THIS is where people come for information. But it is dying under its own weight. 

This is a fantastic resource and it is certainly the first place I come when I look for help. At the same time it is getting very big and finding information here is getting hard...that is one reason why the same questions get asked again and again. So before people start getting snarky at questions being asked again and again, try being silent instead - just offer suggestions if you actually have something helpful to say. And moderators, take some initiative - you know Karmic is coming out so surely you can prepare for it with some easier to find Q&As.
