### Post by Arand on 2009-09-01
It all depends on what you want to test. Obviously just running livecds (even if it's the up-to date dailies) will not enable testing of all features: apps are not persistent, hibernation/restarts won't be tested; I'm not sure if you can install video drivers properly.. etc.

If you have one, consider installing onto a USB stick/drive (>4GB) to be able to test it without touching your harddrive (depending on USB it will often be slower than HD install, but usually completely acceptable).
- Arand
