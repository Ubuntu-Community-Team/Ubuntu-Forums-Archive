### Post by Rich_Roast on 2009-10-11
This is a compiz fusion effect; non-responsive windows are darkened and go black and white. In CompizConfig this behaviour may be stopped by going to Fading Windows, and unchecking "Dim Unresponsive Windows". The cause of the unresponsiveness is another issue, the OP could try using top to see what process is eating up all the system resources. If it is happening while typing in forum posts it could be a Firefox issue, or whichever browser the OP is using.
