That is good.

FWIW it reinforces something I have been saying for about 15 years.  While Icons may be language independent, they are useless and confusing if they are not intuitive and clear.

 To this day the first thing I do is make the "text only" setting anywhere I can eliminate an icon!

Somewhere, deep in the process, the concept got lost, the icons got smaller and more "gee-whiz" and unless you take the time to memorize each and every one, or wait for the [sometimes non-existent rollover pop-up], the "user experience" of modern software becomes annoying and frustrating.  Additionally, as screens get more resolution and as our eyes (and minds) age, what once may have been cute, logical and appropriate "picture" now becomes a fuzzy blob of a few apparent pixels!

End of rant, but developers should keep this in mind.
