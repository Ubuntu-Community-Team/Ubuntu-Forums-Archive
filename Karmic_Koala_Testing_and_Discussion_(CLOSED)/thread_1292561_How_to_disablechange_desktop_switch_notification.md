### Post by ianprinz on 2009-10-17
It needs the "CompizConfig Settings Manager" installed to access these features, so you need to install it if you haven't done it already.

In your Compiz settings go the section "Desktop" and you will see the "Desktop Wall" plugin, unchek it and choose something else.

EDIT
Just realised that you can also leave it checked and in its settings uncheck the "Viewport Switch Preview".
