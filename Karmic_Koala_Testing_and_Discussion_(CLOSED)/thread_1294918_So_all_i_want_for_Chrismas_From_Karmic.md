### Post by Sophont on 2009-10-19
OPs question assumes that those problems are/were universal - but they are not.

On my hardware (see signature) I had only negligable problems with PA and Hibernation has worked for years - though it's not faster than shutdown/boot. So far I had 0 problems with PA with Karmic on XPS 16.

Standby/sleep has been hit and miss. It's not recovering well with Karmic on the XPS 16 but it did on the older Inspiron. In both cases BT doesn't re-activate after wakeup - but it's not properly waking up  with Vista on the same machine either.

Vista OTOH sometimes looses WLAN connectivity on the XPS 16 - which hasn't happened with Karmic.

Those having problems tend to write more about it, while those with well running systems have less reason to post. That can lead to the impression that - for example - PA has more problems than it actually does.
