### Post by 23meg on 2009-08-28
That's obviously a loaded question, and will lead to a bashing fest, so I'm closing this thread.

The people who work at Canonical on visual design and user experience (not "art" or "artwork") are the [Canonical Design and User Experience Team]("https://launchpad.net/~canonical-ux"). 

The volunteer community team that works on alternative themes to the default look, and various other visuals, is the [Ubuntu Artwork Team]("https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-art"). Rather than being a team in the usual sense, it's a loose gathering of people with a common aim; it has only recently been coming together in small theme groups working on multiple alternate themes.
