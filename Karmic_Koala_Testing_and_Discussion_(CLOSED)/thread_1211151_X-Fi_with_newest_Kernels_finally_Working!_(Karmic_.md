### Post by EagleDM on 2009-07-12
I already posted this, but I wanted to make sure everyone reads it.

The newest kernel in Karmic Koala daily build (as of July 12 - 2009) includes supports in ALSA for X-Fi that's right!


Not only that, X-Fi now is working in FULL MULTICHANNEL MODE (front, surround, center, subwoofer and even Side speakers are working now)

I already tested and checked that both X-Fi PCI and X-Fi Titanium cards are working,  the sound quality is simply AMAZING!


GIVE THIS A TRY GUYS!

Finally, we have full support in the kernel with no need to do anything to get it working.

The new Kmixer in KDE 4.3 in Karmic really rocks, now I can enjoy and have peace again.



Eagle
