### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-09-08
> **Galban said:**
> ... I hope the Release version of Karmic will have the drivers that will support 3D acceleration to graphics cards like mine, because until the Alpha 5, this issue persist.

If you're asking about ATI-provided drivers, the answer is NO -- they won't be providing any for their legacy cards anymore.  They said that very clearly in their Release Notes when they discontinued support in Catalyst v9.4.

As to the open source drivers, I've found them to be a lot better in 9.04 than they were in previous releases, but don't know how much 3D acceleration they're planning to support in future releases.
