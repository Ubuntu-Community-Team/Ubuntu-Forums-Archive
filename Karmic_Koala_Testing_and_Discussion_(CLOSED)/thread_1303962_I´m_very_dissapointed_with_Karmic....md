### Post by jbslaw on 2009-10-28
There are always bugs.  This laptop dual-booting Vista and Karmic has experienced bugs in both.  There are annoyances like the difficulty Vista has printing to a remote printer on a Windows network, and there are show-stoppers like the inability of Linux to reliably maintain a wireless connection on the RTL-8187B wireless card or to suspend and resume.
