OK since I didn't try this interface take the following with a grain of salt.

I don't think this would work on a larger display. I mean, imagine a 20" display that has on the most part of it a huge app launcher. Not very efficient use of space. 

A launcher is a one-time deal. You open it and launch the app. Having it constantly on a huge (netbook-wise) display is just a waste of space that should be filled with other more relevant information for me. I would rather have widgets on desktop then a single launcher.

This works on small displays since your entire screen is filled with the currently running application and you don't see the launcher. On desktop windows usually aren't maximized since there is more room.
