### Post by steeleyuk on 2009-08-27
Its not a rewrite per se, at least not now it isn't.

2.20 was the GDM version included in Jaunty, Intrepid, Hardy and Gutsy (I think). Its been around for a while anyway.

Later versions have been written (as seen in Fedora for about the last three versions), but Ubuntu continued to stick with 2.20 because of a lack of maturity in the newer version. Having no tools to configure GDM was a big part of that as far as I know.

I'm not 100% sure of all the new features but I think a big part of the newer GDM is to allow for smoother transitions from login to desktop.
