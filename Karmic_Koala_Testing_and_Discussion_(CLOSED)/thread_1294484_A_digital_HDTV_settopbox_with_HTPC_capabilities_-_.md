### Post by steefjeqv on 2009-10-18
Hi all,

gda and hotzenplotz5 have combined their launchpad repos into one super ppa which basically gives you a digital TV settopbox with HTPC capabilities, and then some more... .

It is based on Klaus Schmidinger's development VDR 1.7.9, derived from the debian packages maintained by etobi, Tomg and Hanno and also containing Zulu's extensions version.

I haven't tried this myself (still using gda's 1.6.0 version), but feel free to give it a spin. I'm going to try it out next week.

What do you need :

- Karmic (maybe Jaunty works too)
- a VDPAU able Nvidia graphics chipset : 8-series or more recent.  
- a digital TV tuner card

Look here :

[https://launchpad.net/~the-vdr-team/+archive/vdr-ubuntu-karmic/+packages?start=0&batch=75]("https://launchpad.net/~the-vdr-team/+archive/vdr-ubuntu-karmic/+packages?start=0&batch=75")

If you like to combine this with XBMC, then there's the ppa from Hepi :

[https://launchpad.net/~henningpingel/+archive/xbmc]("https://launchpad.net/~henningpingel/+archive/xbmc")

Any feedback or questions are welcome.

A big thank you to all who made this possible.
