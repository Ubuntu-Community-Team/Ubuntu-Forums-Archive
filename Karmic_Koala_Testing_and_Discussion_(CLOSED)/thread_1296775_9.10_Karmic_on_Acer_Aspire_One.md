### Post by Aearenda on 2009-10-24
Studon, my fan is quiet, most of the time. I suspect you need to set the temperature threshold higher. My fan settings in /etc/modprobe.d/acerhdf.conf are like this:
```
options acerhdf verbose=0 fanon=62 fanoff=52 interval=10
```
The fan comes on at 62 degrees, goes off again at 52, and it is checked every 10 seconds. If you set verbose=1 you can see what happened in /var/log/syslog.
