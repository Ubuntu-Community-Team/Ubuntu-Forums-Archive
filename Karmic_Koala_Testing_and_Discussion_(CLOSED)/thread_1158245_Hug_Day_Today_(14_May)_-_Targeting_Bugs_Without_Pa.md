### Post by 23meg on 2009-05-13
Hug Day is a [bug triage]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Bugs/HowToTriage") event where triagers of varying experience levels come together with a shared goal of triaging a specific package or set of packages. It especially presents a good opportunity for people who want to get started in [helping with bugs]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HelpingWithBugs").

This week's Hug Day is targeting bug reports that aren't assigned to any package. We'll be working on a list of bugs reported in Ubuntu but with no particular package selected, and assigning each of them to the correct package, as well as starting to triage them where possible.

The upcoming Hug Day is scheduled for May 14th in all timezones, and is to take place in the #ubuntu-bugs [IRC]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetRelayChat") channel on irc.freenode.net. Further information, including the list of targeted bug reports (people have already started working on them!), is available on the wiki page:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBugDay/20090514](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBugDay/20090514)

Feel free to ask any questions you may have regarding the event in this thread.
