### Post by socool274 on 2009-10-25
Today I got Ubuntu 9.10 Karmic. Installation took a couple of hours, but I find this update worth the wait. While booting, the first thing I noticed is a different booting screen. They did change it slightly. This loading screen is slightly more interesting than the previous one. The login screen is also slightly different. The login screen now has users in a graphical list, so you can select them with your mouse. Again, and improvement from the previous version. I looked around on the desktop a bit. I found that there are a few more backgrounds. It also has more themes. The new version of ubuntu has updated software, so you can get newer versions of the software you have. Karmic did fix some of the driver problems I have been having, one of the most notable being wireless. I am now able to use wireless. The most disappointing of all of this is the lack of working sound drivers. The icons did change, too. So, the new version of Ubuntu: better than the previous version.
