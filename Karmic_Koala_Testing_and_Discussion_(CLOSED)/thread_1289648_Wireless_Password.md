I have Karmic installed, and fully updated (32 bit). I also have automatic login enabled, but when it boots, it asks me for my password to unlock and use the wireless phrase-key to connect to my WiFi.

### Post by Peter09 on 2009-10-13
Go into the network manager (by right clicking on the icon (top right) and selecting edit) then edit the wifi connection. Tick the box for share the connection with other users.
