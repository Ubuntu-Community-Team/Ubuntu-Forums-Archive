### Post by forumache on 2009-08-17
I had this problem with the new 2.6.31-6 and my it87 hardware monitor.
Google found the answer: acpi_enforce_resources was set up by default to "strict" in the recent kernels. You can find the reason why by googling.

As a solution, add acpi_enforce_resources=lax on the kernel boot line, or add it to grub.conf for a permanent fix.
