### Post by mocoloco on 2009-10-18
Yes they will both get you to the same place, so go with the daily.  When Karmic final is release you will be able to update everything and it *should* or *might* be just as good as a fresh install, however with beta releases sometimes there are issues that are not completely resolved by updates or oddities, and I think it's best to at least be prepared for having to do a fresh install when final is released.
