### Post by wOOge on 2009-10-26
**Problem:** After installing Virtual Box Additions, you reboot to get the benefits, but the system doesn't come up —*or reboots twice then goes to a black screen.

**Solution:** In VirtualBox, under the guest settings, turn OFF 3D acceleration, then reboot the guest OS.

This might have been an isolated issue with my setup, but maybe it will help someone else. 


Host OS: Mac OS X Now Leopard
Guest OS: Ubuntu Server 9.10 (gui xUbuntu installed after the fact)
VBox: V 3.0.8 r53138
