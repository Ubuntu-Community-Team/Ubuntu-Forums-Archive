### Post by etnlIcarus on 2009-09-23
First thing to do is to go into totem's preferences and on the Display tab, reset all colour settings to their default.

I encountered a bug like this ages ago: changing totem-gstreamer's xvideo colour settings managed to affect VLC's xvideo colour settings.

Edit: worth checking this in VLC, too. Often VLC is a cow when it comes to settings randomly changing themselves or inconsistently being remembered.
