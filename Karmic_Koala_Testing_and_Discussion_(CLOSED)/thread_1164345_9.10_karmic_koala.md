### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-05-19
I'm running the Alpha of Karmic. Personally, my sound is very sporadic - I've had to set it up again numerous times. No love on the ATI fglrx driver so far, a few kernel oops, yelp crashes on load, sometimes I have to try 2x before it will let me sudo.

All in all, considering it's less than a week into Alpha, it's very stable for me. The only showstopper has been the ATI driver, and that's easily fixed from the command line. Even when the kernel crashes, I've been able to keep running the same session.

Of course, who knows what will happen next - that's why it's fun. ;)
