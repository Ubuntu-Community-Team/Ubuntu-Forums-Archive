### Post by freeman2000 on 2009-05-21
My Weather Applet also has everything "greyed-out" as you describe.  Fear not.  Just do the following:

1) right-click on the applet
2) click on "Properties"
3) go to "Locations" tab
4) go to "Find" and type your location in the box
5) click "Close"
6) right-click the applet again and click on "Update"

This should hopefully get the info for you.  Good luck.
