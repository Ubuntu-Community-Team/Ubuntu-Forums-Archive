### Post by GeorgeVita on 2009-10-13
Hi **Michael**, delete existing applet and try again to setup layouts:

Keyboard switching can be configured from:
[B]System > Preferences > Keyboard > Layouts
then **Add** new layout and change shortcuts from **Layouts options**

Right click on top panel, **Add to Panel** the **Keyboard Indicator**

I have my 9.10 fully updated and well functioning on this subject.
