### Post by psyke83 on 2009-07-16
> **Eclipse. said:**
> This is not a kernel problem its the intel driver.I get the same crappy performance using jaunty (with the jaunty kernel) with the xorg-edgers ppa.

No, if you read his post you'll see it **is** a kernel problem. I also experience regressions from 2.6.30-10-generic to 2.6.31-*-generic.

Most likely there's a regression in the drm/i915 kernel modules for our chipsets.

In the case of Jaunty, you probably had kernel **and** driver issues (the Jaunty intel driver has early/buggy UXA support, and the 2.6.28 kernel has many DRM regressions). My guide explains the situation in more detail.
