Is this a troll post?  The wording would seem to imply that.

I have used Jaunty for the past year with almost no problems (I have not posted a single formal bug report).  Have you posted any official bug reports to substantiate your statement about Jaunty being "buggy"?

Each release does seem to have a few things that some users would like to work better, different, faster, etc.  These personal desires may, or may not, reflect actual bugs.  Some so-called bugs may be incorrectly configured hardware, or incorrectly configured options in the applications themselves.  And...some may be actual bugs.  Those who can verify that an actual bug exists should go to the effort of filing a bug report. Please give the developers a chance to do their jobs.  Inflection and innuendo only lower credibility.
