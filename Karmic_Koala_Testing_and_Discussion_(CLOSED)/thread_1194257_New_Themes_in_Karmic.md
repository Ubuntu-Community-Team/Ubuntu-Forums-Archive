### Post by andrewsomething on 2009-06-24
> **Starks said:**
> I hope the new Shiki-Dust is included and that all of the Shiki themes are of the Murrine engine version.

Yes and yes. The dust themes will be in Karmic as well, and they are the murrine versions. 


> **plun said:**
> Well, its about time.....:D

Bring the community-themes which nearly noone knows about.



Part of that is that the two most popular themes from community-themes got promoted to main, New Wave and Dust. Right now it just has Impression and Kin (which I personally find a little stale). This package is developed by the community Artwork Team. We'll be updating the package for Karmic, almost certainly adding some new stuff. Anyone working on a thme is encouraged to start sharing stuff on the list and wiki. The point of this specific package is to highlight stuff done by the Artwork Team community not just random themes that we think are cool. So if you've got something you'd like to see in it, jump on the mailing list.

> **keenish27 said:**
> ok...I might be dumb.  but what is the package name to download these themes?

They're not in the repo just yet. They're sitting in the Debian NEW queue, waiting to be approved. They'll be synced into Karmic as soon as that happens. We want to give back to Debian too.

But as I'm not a Debian Developer, I have to go through the sponsorship process there. But even if we have to jump ahead of Debian a little bit, I'll make sure that Karmic will release with the most recent versions at the time.

The meta packages will be called gnome-colors, shiki-colors, and arc-colors. The individual color packages will be: gnome-$COLOR-icon-theme, shiki-$COLOR-theme, and arc-$COLOR
