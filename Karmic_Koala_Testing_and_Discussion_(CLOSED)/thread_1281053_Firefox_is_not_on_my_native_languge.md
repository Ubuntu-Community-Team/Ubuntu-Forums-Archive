### Post by Bert Mariën on 2009-10-03
I do not know which language you use (you do not mention it).

But normally your native language must be installed to get anything in your natiove language.

So go and see:

system >> administration >>language support

and install the language you need.
Especially see that the 1st language is in your native language.

If that is the case, every program (that is translated in your language) MUST appear in your native language.

So I assume that you better have a look at:

system >> administration >>language support
