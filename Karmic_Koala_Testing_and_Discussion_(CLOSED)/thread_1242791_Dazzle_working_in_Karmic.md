### Post by Skipnaz on 2009-08-17
This morning I thought I'd try my Dazzle DVC100, streaming video from my Sony DCR-DVD301 video camera to Skype and it worked! My arrangement never worked in versions 8.04 or 9.04 so for me this a first. I think this is a big deal. I like using my video cam with Skype because it has optical zoom that I can zoom in/out showing my desktop to my friends discussing things.
I set it on a tripod beside me. I only use the yellow video RCA plug from the camera to the red tear-drop shaped Dazzle RCA/USB adaptor. There seems to be a green bar at the bottom of the screen from left to right side about ~20% the height of the screen but I'm not complaining, I been waiting for this for too long. Its the last thing I don't have to do in Windows other than some high dollar games.
Thank you development team!
