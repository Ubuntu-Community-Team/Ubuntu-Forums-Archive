### Post by number nine on 2009-05-19
(disclosure: I work for [DisplayLink]("http://displaylink.com"))

There are USB monitors available with DisplayLink built in from a bunch of vendors (Samsung has the most variety) and there are lots of USB to DVI adapters too (EVGA or HP tend to be the cheapest in US retail). You'll also find DisplayLink in some USB docking stations and USB projectors. A fairly comprehensive list is here: [http://www.displaylink.com/shop.html](http://www.displaylink.com/shop.html).

I'd be really happy to get DisplayLink support into Karmic (obviously)! If anybody at Canonical wants to work with us on it send an email to linux{at}displaylink.com or post to the libdlo mailing list at [http://www.displaylink.org](http://www.displaylink.org).
