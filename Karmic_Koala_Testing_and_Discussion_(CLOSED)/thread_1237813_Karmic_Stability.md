### Post by Sophont on 2009-08-11
> **sox fan Matt said:**
> Honestly at the moment, how stable is it and when would a good time be to upgrade before the official release?

I have upgraded early a couple of times with past alphas and I have KK in a Virtualbox VM - on the whole Ubuntu alphas tend to be solid enough in general. Particular apps will temporarily break sometimes.
Expect some regressions in some apps for a while.

Good time to upgrade? Is your current system stable? If yes - why upgrade to a test version? You have to be prepared for plenty changes and at least some breakage until a week or 2 before release.

Upgrading now is for bleeding edgers or those who have known problems with their current release that the newer release fixes.
Either way - it's still in development and called an alpha version for a reason.

If in doubt - don't upgrade.
If you do anyway ;-) - make backups first and follow this forum.
