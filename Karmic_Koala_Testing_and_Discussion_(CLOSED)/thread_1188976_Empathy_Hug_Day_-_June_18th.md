### Post by 23meg on 2009-06-16
Hug Day is a [bug triage]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Bugs/HowToTriage") event where triagers of varying experience levels come together with a shared goal of triaging a specific package or set of packages. It especially presents a good opportunity for people who want to get started in [helping with bugs]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HelpingWithBugs").

This week's [Hug Day]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBugDay") is targeting a relatively short list of Empathy bug reports:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBugDay/20090618](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBugDay/20090618)

If you want to help triage bugs in Empathy in preparation for its default inclusion in Karmic, this is the place to be. The Hug Day takes place in all timezones this Thursday in the #ubuntu-bugs [IRC]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetRelayChat") channel at Freenode (irc.freenode.net).
