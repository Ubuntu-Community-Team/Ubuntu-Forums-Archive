### Post by tlacuache on 2009-10-20
I had this problem, but when I went in to "CompizConfig Settings Manager", selected "Viewport Switcher", went to the "Desktop-based Viewport Switching" tab, and set "Move Next" to Button5 and "Move Prev" to Button4 it worked fine for me just like before.
