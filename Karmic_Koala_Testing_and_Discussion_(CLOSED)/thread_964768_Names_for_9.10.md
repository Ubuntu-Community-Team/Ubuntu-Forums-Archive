### Post by Giant Speck on 2008-11-01
This is how I see it.  In order to come up with a good name for an Ubuntu release, you need to analyze the past few releases.

Dapper Drake
Edgy Eft
Gutsy Gibbon
Hardy Heron
Intrepid Ibex
Jaunty Jackelope

Dapper, edgy, gutsy, hardy, intrepid, and jaunty are all adjectives that have to do with a bold, bright, or active attitude.  Therefore, the next few releases should reflect that trend:  kinetic, lambent, mighty, nimble, etc.

Drake, eft, gibbon, heron, ibex, and jackelope are certainly not common animal names.  They are unique.  Therefore, the next few releases should feature more uncommon and unique animals: kiwi, lemur, mink, narwhal.
