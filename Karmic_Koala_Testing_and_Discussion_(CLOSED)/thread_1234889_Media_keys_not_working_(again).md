### Post by phenest on 2009-08-08
This was reported to Gnome, and I thought they fixed it. If you open Mouse Preferences and enable 'Show position of pointer when control key pressed', all media buttons such as play/pause, next, previous, and stop, stop working. Only volume buttons work. Now uncheck that option, and all media keys work fine.
