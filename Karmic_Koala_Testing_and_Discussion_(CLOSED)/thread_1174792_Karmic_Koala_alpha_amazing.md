### Post by johnhenry on 2009-05-31
I am amazed at how good the alpha installation disk of Ubuntu 9.10 (Karmic Koala) already is. I have installed it on my Acer Aspire, and have yet to encounter any bug apart from one minor one during the instllation process. Everything I have tried, sound, graphics, nvidia card, updates, codecs etc. are all working perfectly. This is fit to be at least a beta version, and is already better than most distro releases.

The little bug during installation was that it refused to delete the system on the partition chosen for installation, although this was already ext4. However it was easy to get round that by deleting the partition itself, and then recreating it. Everything then was a doddle.

Congratulations to all involved. I am VERY impressed. It is by far the best alpha release I have ever encountered.
