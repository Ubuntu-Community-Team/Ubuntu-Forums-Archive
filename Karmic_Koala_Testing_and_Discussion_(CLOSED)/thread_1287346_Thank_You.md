### Post by Copernicus1234 on 2009-10-10
I just want to say a big thank you to the developers of Karmic Koala and everyone who has contributed in some way to the upcoming release. Its the best one Ive ever seen.

Finally the distribution has made some progress in the looks department and this will increase interest in Ubuntu across the world. For every future screenshot in magazines, it will no longer look like Windows 3.1 but instead a much more modern and mature product. This will greatly increase the interest in the operating system. Technical superiority alone cannot sell a product. For example, there are much better music players than the IPod but which one sells the most? The IPod. The reason is obvious. Its design.

So hats off to Canonical and every volunteer for focusing on the right things like design and startup time in Karmic Koala. Im certain it will be a great success with a large increase in new users who appreciate the concepts of a fast, secure, easy to use operating system that can be personalized in a endless amount of ways for free. It brings the enjoyment and creativity back into this business.
