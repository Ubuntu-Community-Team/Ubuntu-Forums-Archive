### Post by NRDNick on 2009-09-25
If you right click on a panel and select "add to pannel..." then look for an app called Hardware sensors monitor. If you add that to your panel you can then right click on the applet and choose your preferences. IE what you want the applet to display. If you just want a stand alone application that you can start to check this stuff and then close, you can try "X sensors" from the software store. Oh and you can also open a terminal and type "sensors". Hope that helps.
