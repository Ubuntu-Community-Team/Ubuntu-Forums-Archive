### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-10-17
When Karmic is ready, you'll be prompted to upgrade in the update manager window. It might or might not break things, but should retain most of the settings and applications. It's also possible to reinstall while keeping you home directory in a backup and restore the settings from there later, but, requires a bit of confidence and know-how.
On the other hand, if Jaunty works great for you, there might not be enough compelling reasons to upgrade at all.
