### Post by sol3observer on 2009-10-24
I've had this problem too.  Easy quick fix...

1. Open the File System Icon
2. Double-click on usr
3. Double-click on bin
4. Hunt down xcfe4-panel
5. Right click & Execute
6. Your Top Bar should now be displayed
7. Click the top right-hand top bar icon to restart
8. Tick the Save session for future use
9. Restart

Problem fixed :)
