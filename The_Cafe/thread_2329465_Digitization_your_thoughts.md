### Post by yoshii on 2016-07-02
archiving good old music is worth it.  
24-bit ADC's have lower self-noise than 16-ADC's so keep that in mind.  
Audacity doesn't alter the sound unless you tell it to.  

Software can implement superior RIAA turntable curves than the old fashioned kind.
