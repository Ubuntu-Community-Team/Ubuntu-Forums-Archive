### Post by p_quarles on 2007-09-13
[KEXP]("http://kexp.org/home.asp?") in Seattle.

My college radio station. It used to be KCMU, but then Paul Allen gave them a truckload of cash in return for a name that would associate the station with his Experience Museum. Fortunately, they didn't change their programming at all, it's still public radio, and the cash allowed them to buy the bandwidth for a pretty big internet presence. 

They've got a great range of pop-40 free programming. Give it a listen.
