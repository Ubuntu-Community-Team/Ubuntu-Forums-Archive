### Post by t0p on 2008-09-05
If google's data mining concerns you, there's a service called [scroogle]("http://scroogle.org") that might be of use.

Scroogle calls itself a "google scraper" - it submits your request to google, then gives you the results.  So if you use scroogle, you're still using google... except they can't gather any info on you.

Sweet... so long as you trust scroogle.  And, considering the fact that you have no idea who scroogle actually are: why on earth *would* you trust them?
