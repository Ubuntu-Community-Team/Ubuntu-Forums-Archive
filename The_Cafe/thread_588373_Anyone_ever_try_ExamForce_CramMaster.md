### Post by jkeyes0 on 2007-10-23
The main problem I can see from that is their guarantee: 
We are so confident that you will pass any corresponding exam by using ExamForce's CramMaster, that if you fail the test we will refund your purchase!

To qualify for a refund you must:

   1. Be the registered purchaser
   2. Fail the corresponding exam twice (no time limit )
   3. Contact our Customer Service Department at (800) 845-8569 or via email at [email]customerservice@examforce.com[/email] to claim this guarantee and provide your proof of purchase along with copies of failing exam notices for both failed attempts
   4. Return the corresponding software

What I see here is that they will only refund the price of the training/test prep materials if you pay to take the test twice and fail it both times. No real guarantee there, just the possibility of losing hundreds of dollars if their product isn't as good as they say.
