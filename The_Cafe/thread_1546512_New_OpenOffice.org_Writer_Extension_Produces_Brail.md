### Post by newbie2 on 2010-08-05
> Leuven, Belgium, 4 August 2010 
– The Katholieke Universiteit Leuven (K.U.Leuven) today released an extension for OpenOffice.org Writer that enables users to save documents as Braille or to send them directly to a Braille embosser. “odt2braille” ([http://odt2braille.sourceforge.net/](http://odt2braille.sourceforge.net/)) is a freeware extension for OpenOffice.org, a office suite that is freely available for Microsoft Windows, Mac OS X, Linux/Unix and Solaris.
[http://www.accessforall.eu/tag/odt2braille/](http://www.accessforall.eu/tag/odt2braille/)
