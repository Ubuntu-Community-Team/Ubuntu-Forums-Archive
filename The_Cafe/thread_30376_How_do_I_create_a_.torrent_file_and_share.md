### Post by bored2k on 2005-04-28
You can go to demonoid.com for it. They have excellent tcp as well as udp trackers. You need to be registered for this though.

[http://tracker.prq.to/announce](http://tracker.prq.to/announce) is a good no registration needed tracker. It's the one mininova.org users like. 

You could also use torrentbox's trackers, but theyre not that good. I suggest you go with demonoid's [selecting the option to allow non members to leech] or prq.to.

[http://torrentreactor.net/add.php](http://torrentreactor.net/add.php) also ^_^
