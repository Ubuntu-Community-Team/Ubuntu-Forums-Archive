Hi everyone

Since Ubuntu 9.04, one of the many things Canonical’s Design team has been working on is making panel elements more consistent.

As part of that work, I am researching how people use Bluetooth, and in particular, the Bluetooth menu in the Ubuntu panel.

If you use Bluetooth with your computer, it would be very helpful if you could reply to this thread with your answers to these questions:

[LIST=1]
[*]What kinds of Bluetooth device do you use with Ubuntu? How often?
[*]Do you use the Bluetooth menu in the panel? If so, what do you use it for, and how often?

[/LIST]
Thanks in advance for your help!
