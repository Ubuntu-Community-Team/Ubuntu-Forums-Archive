### Post by Arktis on 2005-09-13
> Seems pointless to me. What's the need for two different bookmarks menus?

1.) When the browser isn't open and I want to go to a website in my bookmarks, I eliminate several steps by opening the site directly from the pannel menu. Instead of going to Applications > Internet > Firefox, waiting for firefox to load, then going to the bookmarks menu in firefox and selecting the site, I just open the site directly from the pannel.

2.) It's easier for me to use the bookmarks menu this way. I just throw my mouse to the top of the screen to get to the menu; I don't need to be that precise.

3.)  It's keen. :razz:
