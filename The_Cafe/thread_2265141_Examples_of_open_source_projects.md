### Post by PondPuppy on 2015-02-14
Here are some questions for you to research:

Is the Apache web-server open-source? How widely is it used? What difference has it made in the Internet? How about the SQLite database (which is probably in your phone and tablet and web browser)? What about OpenSSL?

What software is used by dissidents around the world when a secure OS is needed (think TAILS, Liberte, etc), and for encrypting files and communication? Which of these applications are open-source software? How have these enabled changes in the geopolitical world in the 21st century?
