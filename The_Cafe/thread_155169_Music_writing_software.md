### Post by mscman on 2006-04-04
Brunellus,
Your question caught my eye when I visited the forums today, since I commonly hear of people looking for music recording software, but never hear of anyone using notation software on linux.  After a quick search, I found a great website that anyone doing [music editing on linux may find useful]("http://www.hitsquad.com/smm/linux/"). There  are numerous notation programs as well as recording software.  I checked out a few of the notation programs, and found one that's really easy to use called [Denemo]("http://www.hitsquad.com/smm/programs/Denemo/").  It's easy to install and use.  Hope this helps!
