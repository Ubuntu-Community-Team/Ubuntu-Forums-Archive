### Post by Dr Small on 2008-09-08
Was looking through the books in Goodwill in my small little town, and my eyes was scanning through the books sideways. Woah! Wait, what was that? I pulled the book out.... "Postfix". Aha! Is this indeed a great find.

I scanned around some more. Another word caught my attention! Perl. "Object Oriented Perl". I went and told my mom, and she's like "Ok." with a confused look. :D

So I went back and searched more thoroughly threw the titles. Then I saw "Linux" YES! A linux book :) "Linux in a Nutshell 2nd Edition by O'Reilly" This indeed was a great find.

Here are the books on the web:
 [http://www.ercb.com/feature/feature.0061.html](http://www.ercb.com/feature/feature.0061.html) (Postfix)
[http://oreilly.com/catalog/9780596009304/index.html](http://oreilly.com/catalog/9780596009304/index.html) (Linux in a Nutshell .. well, I couldn't find the 2nd edition on their site, so..)
[http://www.manning.com/conway/](http://www.manning.com/conway/) (Object Oriented Perl)

And guess what I paid for them in total? 6$ :)
According to the backs of the books, if I would have bought them from the publishing company, I would have had to spend 118$!
