### Post by scouser73 on 2010-10-20
iPod Touch works well under Ubuntu using either Rhythmbox or Banshee music players.  You can both add and remove songs for the device, I believe that if you want to add videos then the user has to SSH into the device (although I am uncertain as to how).

When you buy the device you first need to initialise it using Windows & iTunes, then remove the iPod and attach it to your Ubuntu PC, upon connection it will be recognised.
