### Post by Shibblet on 2020-01-27
> **Frogs Hair said:**
> The store has been closed , but I do see items including hoodies available from other sources online. You would need permission. 

[https://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2019/05/canonical-shop-ubuntu-closed](https://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2019/05/canonical-shop-ubuntu-closed)

You do not need permission to be a "fan-boi."  If you want to print out the Ubuntu Logo and put it on your own personal hoodie, you are okay to do so.

You CAN NOT, however, sell said hoodie as Ubuntu Merchandise.
Think NFL.  If you're a Dallas Cowboy's Fan, you can put the silver and blue star on your own things, but the NFL and the Dallas Cowboys have the rights to sell these items as merchandise.
