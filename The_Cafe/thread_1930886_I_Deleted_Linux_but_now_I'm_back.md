### Post by Merisi on 2012-02-25
I'm going to try and reply to all the post directed towards me in one post to avoid spamming this thread with multiple posts.

Firstly it's really nice to be welcomed back to Ubuntu by everyone.  It also seems I'm not the only one who is left with an OS dilemma and vacillates from Ubuntu to Windows.  At the moment I'm really resisting the urge of buying a Macbook or an iPad.  I think I'm a dual boot type of a person but sometimes the other OS on your computer can seem such a distance away.

To Eiji Takanaka and anyone else who is interested, the reason I left Linux is complicated. After a few months I realised I did need Windows for things like Spotify, Steam and Audible.  I know I could use WINE but I feel uncomfortable with it. I've missed Ubuntu a lot and it's hard for others to understand (apart from here of course) how much I enjoy Linux.

I'd just like to thank everyone again for posting back to me.  Great to be back,
