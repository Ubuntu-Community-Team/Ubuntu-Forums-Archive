### Post by mobilediesel on 2011-02-26
ChaCha will definitely get you **some** income. there are two different pay methods: "Pay Me Now" and "Pay Me Monthly". The Pay Me Now option charges a $2 fee to transfer your earnings to your bank account. The monthly option has no fee but your balance has to be $100 by the 15th of the month. Most new guides can set up a bank account with First Internet Bank of Indiana. When you do the Pay Me Now, the money is usually in the account within a few minutes.
