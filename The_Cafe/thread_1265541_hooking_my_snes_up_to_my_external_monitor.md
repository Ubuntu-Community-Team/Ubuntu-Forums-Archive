### Post by BoyOfDestiny on 2009-09-13
That adapter you linked looks like it's for the computer out to a TV.

Google snes vga adaptor.

What you want is something that connects from the SNES video out to VGA in.

As for the monitor, which you want connected the to snes directly...
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vga](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vga)

Just make sure it has the port to plug it into.

If you have something different, i.e. dvi, you can get a dvi to vga adaptor, and plug in any vga cable just fine.

Also, does your dell monitor have any video in?
I know the newer SNES came with only an RF-out (I replaced my old one when it broke, but luckily I had composite out)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Composite_video](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Composite_video)

If your monitor has those (usually three, one yellow for video, one red and white for stereo, although you could just hook the audio to speakers etc...)
Then just get a composite out cable for your snes. Not sure if it applies, but just mentioning it as another option.

If you post the model number of your screen, I could check for you.
