### Post by kpholmes on 2009-11-15
[B]please dont think that im trying to promote Microsoft in anyway, im just sharing my experience 
[/B]
today i went down to the shops at mission viejo, a fairly large shopping mall and made a nice visit to the Microsoft store. it was larger then any other apple store ive ever been in, but had the same exact look and feel. the employees wore plain shirts in 4 different colors of blue, red, yellow, and green. with khaki or jean pants. upon entering i sat at the closest laptop and was going go hot on the forums to announce that i was there until i was approached by a sales rep who was very knowledgeable about the laptops for sale, windows 7, and the zune hd. 

i got an eerie feeling when the sales rep went over all the products and advantages of windows 7 because they were definitely mimicking/targeting apple. telling me how the zune is perfectly integrated with windows 7, and the zune hd's touch screen that had multi-touch support. the GUI was different but all the finger movements were the same.

all the computers were open to mess with and browse around much like the apple store they had a "guru" bar in the back where i was informaed they provide free classes about windows 7. the store had a very clean, sleek look to it. Microsoft definitely put some work into this as the store had a large amount of visitors. 

there were 2 things that i found pretty nifty.

1) was the Microsoft surface, you know, that coffee table that runs windows and what not. it was cool to see it able be able to mess with but when i found out what that it was a large brick that really couldn't do to much i was kinda disappointed, it does has some room for improvement though and i was told that all the features were not enabled due to it was a floor model or something. pretty much it has "bing" maps lol, and 5 games like chess, and stuff. 

2) around the whole inside wall was a huge line of tv's kinda hard to explain but imagine one tv stacked on-top of another, then tv's spanning left and right till it made a horse shoe around the store. i was told that it had 5 times the defanition of a imax theater, it did look pretty good. it was displaying a scenic video of mountains and forest. (kinda hard to explain, just look at the pics below)

all in all, they put on a good show and it was an interesting visit. you could tell they were going after apple, but they didnt once even mention apple. it was like apple didnt exist. i took some pictures of the outside but i didnt get any of the inside due to the fact that they wouldnt leave me alone, and i have an iphone, hehe.

the store is 2 weeks old, and an apple store is under construction and due to open on the 27th i believe just 5 stores down.
