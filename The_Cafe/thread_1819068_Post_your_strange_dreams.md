### Post by SoylentSteak on 2011-08-05
For many years I've had very odd and quite vivid dreams. I been told again and again by therapists to write them down, but I've just now started. I'd like to share the one I had last night.

It started as a company drilling for oil deep underground. But they extracted more like miners get minerals--by people physically going underground.

Somehow, this idea morphed into the drilling/mining site becoming a POW/concentration camp. The conflict that caused its existence lasts for a very long time, decades or perhaps centuries.

To decrease the likelihood or the prisoners overthrowing the camp's leadership, an impostor Amy Winehouse is sent among the prisoners to to appease them. The leaders of the would-be  revolt catch wind of this, and send out their own impostor Winehouse. This is  meant to further their cause. In the dream, this made perfect sense, The prisoner's Winehouse attempts to escape in a camp setting that now looks like an above-ground supermarket, but is apprehended at the door.

It returns to a more prison-like setting. The food they receive is slimy and disgusting. A female prisoner is offered a job working preparing the food. She discovers that below where the prisoners are kept, corpses are being butchered for their meals. They float around in waist-deep, watery filth. There are genetically engineered monsters with whips who act as slave-drivers. There are other prisoners working down there, in nurse uniforms. They eventually wear out and die off. Then, they are butchered.

A reporter who is me, yet not is doing a story on this. He is outside, above ground amongst ruins, where some prisoners are allow to have free time on in-line skates. He/me works for Starfleet. The me/not me reporter is teachng advanced skating techniques to the prisoners, in the hopes that this will help the gain the upper hand. His/my daughter is there. I have no daughter in real life. She is not interested in his teaching. He's glad, because he doesn't want to put her in danger. 

At the end of the dream, everything is changed from reality to it all being a movie, with me as the director. I put myself in a cameo at the happy ending, where I'm walking up hill to a school-like place with a large crowd of people. It's a nice summer day.
