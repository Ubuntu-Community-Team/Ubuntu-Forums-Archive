### Post by Techsnap on 2010-12-22
Bit of an advertisement but I'm sure that some members here will be interested. 
 
For those who don't know OSTalk was a continuation of the Other OS Talk boards which were here at UF. Due to a couple of database issues the forums were restarted several times and eventually closed. 
 
You can now find OSTalk at [http://www.ostalk.co.uk](http://www.ostalk.co.uk) . 
 
OSTalk is once again focused on any Operating System and general technology and should be considered a continuation once again of Ubuntu Forum's own Other OS Talk boards, although there is an Ubuntu boards included because obviously the forum is now separate from UF. 
 
There is also an IRC Channel on Freenode, more information on the forums.
