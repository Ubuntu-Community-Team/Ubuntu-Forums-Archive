### Post by ugmoe2000 on 2011-07-26
Gphoto2 is the command-line client for the cross-platform digital camera library that is libgphoto2. Gphoto2 can do some pretty amazing things and supports a great many cameras presently on the market including the majority of Canon and Nikon DSLRs.
I found-out about Gphoto2 when trying to find out how many pictures (shutter actuations) my newly purchased refurbished Canon T2i had taken prior to my owning it. In addition to Gphoto2&#8242;s many other features which I won’t cover, (look at the man page “man gphoto2&#8243; for all the available features) it has the capability of listing the number of shutter actuation a given camera has on record. Depending on your camera model there may be no way to view this number anywhere else.
