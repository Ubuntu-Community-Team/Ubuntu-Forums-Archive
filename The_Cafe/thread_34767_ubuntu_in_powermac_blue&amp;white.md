### Post by HungSquirrel on 2005-05-17
[QUOTE=ssam]yes you can use any cd writing software. just make sure you use the option to burn the iso to the disk, not as a file on the disk. (can someone phase that better?).[/QUOTE]
Burn the ISO as a disc image.  Do not simply drag the ISO to the disc and burn it.  Dragging the ISO to the disc and burning it as a file on the disc just makes a CD with the ISO on it, rather than a cd *out of the ISO*.
