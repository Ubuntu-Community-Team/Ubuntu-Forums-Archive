### Post by UbuWu on 2005-05-09
That was a long long time ago  :wink: But here is what I remember from it:

1. Install YPOPs! from [http://yahoopops.sourceforge.net/](http://yahoopops.sourceforge.net/) 
2. Load your mail with thunderbird
3. Use GML to upload your mail to gmail [http://www.marklyon.org/gmail/default.htm](http://www.marklyon.org/gmail/default.htm) ([instructions](http://www.marklyon.org/gmail/instruction.htm))
4. Import your contacts in gmail ([instructions](http://gmail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?answer=8301&topic=42) )

Probably easier to do on Windows, because you don't have to compile ypops. 
Doing it this way I ended up with all the emails in the reverse order than they were reveived, so you may want to find a way to upload them in the right order.
