### Post by CryingFreeman on 2007-11-06
In large parts of Europe and other countries (I don't have statistics, but it seems like most countries in the world) computers, and especially laptops, come preinstalled with some sort of Microsoft Windows on it.

Some countries have a few stores where OS-free computers are sold, like my country Sweden, other countries have a few more, but the predominant picture is that you have to buy Windows in some form to get a computer.

In an effort to counter this anti-free-market system, I and some other people has started a FaceBook-cause to show that there are people that does not accept this. The goal is to use this cause to leverage our demands for a fair market place and customers rights to choose what they would like for themselves.

In the end we hope for at least the possibility to say thanks but no thanks to the preinstalled operating system, usually Windows Vista, to have a OS-clean computer at a reduced price.

If you are on Facebook, join the cause here:

[http://apps.facebook.com/causes/view_cause/31942](http://apps.facebook.com/causes/view_cause/31942)

It will cost you nothing, and take just a few seconds. :) Please spread the word if you are on any LoCo-forums as well. :)
