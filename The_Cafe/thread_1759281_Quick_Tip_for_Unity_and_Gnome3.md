### Post by plurworldinc on 2011-05-15
One of the small problems I was having when i began using Unity or Gnome 3 was not being able to get to the software I want as easy as I could before the two came along. Now I know you can just type in the name of the program you want, but what if you don't know the name.

   My tip is install Cairo Dock. It doesn't take anything away from Unity or Gnome3, just adds a little something extra to make your life a little easier. Personally I hide my dock at all time for I can have the natural look of the UI with an little surprise on the bottom.

   So try it out, it does give the UI a little more of a Mac look, but if it works does it really matter, we all want a smooth Ubuntu...
