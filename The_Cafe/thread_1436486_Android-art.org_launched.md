### Post by bwhite82 on 2010-03-22
If you're a fan of the *-look.org or *-art.org theming sites (as I am) you'll be pleased to know that this is now available for your perusal. Please note, because it is just launched, content is lacking. Get there and get uploading!

I suggested the idea to the OpenDesktop community after seeing a weather widget for sale on the marketplace. I was disheartened by this, as I believe something as simple as a widget should be readily available for free en masse. Not that I have anything wrong with wanting to make a buck or two for your work, but by providing content like this for free spurs innovation and competition. After all, the Android OS is FOSS and this site continues in that spirit.

Frank over at OpenDesktop has graciously offered me an administrator position for the site so wish me luck!
