### Post by aysiu on 2008-10-05
I think it depends greatly on what profession you want to go into. I know someone who went to Harvard and found that it wasn't too helpful for breaking into the film industry. I also know some pretty high-powered execs who went to less prestigious schools.

For jobs, experience and networking matter more than what college you went to. Of course, what college you went to also affects your network (fellow alumni).
