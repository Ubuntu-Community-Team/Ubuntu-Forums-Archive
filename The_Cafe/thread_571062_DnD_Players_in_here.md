### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-10-09
> **Spr0k3t said:**
> I have the paperback rulebook that came in a ziplock bag with the original price point on it somewhere.

THAC0 is your friend.  Wow, I feel old.

Wow.. it's been almost a decade since I last saw THAC0 written anywhere...

My friends and I started with Basic Set rules and "[Keep On The Borderlands]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Keep_on_the_Borderlands")". As it turns out, I got to be the first DM, which of course meant that I didn't get to play Borderlands. It was sort of awkward at first, since none of us had played a role game before--or knew what roleplaying was, for that matter, so for the first ten sessions or so we ended up talking to each other like, "My character says this this and that to so and so", until we threw self-consciousness out the window. Eventually we got a pretty good hold of the rules, but over time we became bored with those and started making our own as well as writing our own campaigns. I'm still kind of fond of my own rules set, which still saw play even after I left the game.

Of course, a few months after we began playing, others wanted to be DM, so I finally got a chance to be in the players' seat and experience the game from that perspective.

To Hit Armor Class 0 indeed...
