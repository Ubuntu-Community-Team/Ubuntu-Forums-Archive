### Post by kinematic on 2010-12-18
Recently I bought an OCZ Vertex2 SSD only find out afterwards they didn't provide a Linux firmware updater but a couple of days ago OCZ announced the first beta release on their forum. It's doesn't contain any bugs that can ruin a drive and it has worked flawlessly for me. It detected the drive, fetched the latest firmware from the OCZ server and applied it to my drive. It's good to see a company like OCZ providing support for Linux users.

[http://www.ocztechnologyforum.com/forum/showthread.php?82289-1st-Public-beta-test-of-OCZ-Sandforce-Linux-based-firmware-upddate-tool](http://www.ocztechnologyforum.com/forum/showthread.php?82289-1st-Public-beta-test-of-OCZ-Sandforce-Linux-based-firmware-upddate-tool)
