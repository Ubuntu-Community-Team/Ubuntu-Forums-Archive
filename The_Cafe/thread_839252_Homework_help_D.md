### Post by samjh on 2008-06-24
> **marenkar said:**
> Which is the easiest and which is the hardest for you among these sciences in High School?

Please answer if you have taken up Biology,Chemistry, and Physics in high school.
Which is the easiest and which is the hardest for you and why?

I can't tell yet as of now which is the easiest for me since I have just started Physics, but so far, Chemistry was the hardest for me because of all those things I had to memorize.

I took all three: physics, chemistry, and biology.

For me, none were really harder than another.  They just required different styles of learning and problem-solving.

I was most interested in physics, so it was most enjoyable and resulted in the best marks.  If you know a few basic principles, you can derive a lot of the more complex principles from them.  It requires a lot more application and thinking than rote memorisation.

Chemistry wasn't too bad.  I wasn't really interested in chemistry, so didn't put a lot of effort into it, nor did I pay much attention in class.  Decent scores, but nothing brilliant.

Biology was interesting, but too much rote-learning for me.  I can learn facts, but the subject didn't stimulate enough interest to devote the needed time to memorise all the complicated terminology and processes.  Still got good marks though.
