### Post by adamlau on 2009-02-09
Bash scripting. There is a ton of scripting to do if you want to set up servers for ease of administration. Review man pages, absorb everything /etc has to offer in the way of documentation. If you must stray from Linux, consider learning a BSD OS. That way you will be able to compare and contrast the diffs. Try your hand at Gentoo, or Arch and see first hand how much faster they really are compared to even a minimal Ubuntu install. Tons of stuff to do.
