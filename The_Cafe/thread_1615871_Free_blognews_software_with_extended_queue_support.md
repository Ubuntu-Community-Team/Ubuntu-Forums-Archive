### Post by siddartha on 2010-11-07
I keep searching high and low for a CMS specialised in simple sorting. Blog, news, whatever - it's only one poster and only one blog or, if you preffer, news stream. The comments are useless as disqus is doing a fine job so far, although I have nothing against a good comment management system (also hard to find). It would be nice to have some webpage optimisation instead of just dumping everything from the theme.

Anyway, the most important part to me is the message/post/news queue. It has to be quite well done and not just a silly scheduler/cron job. It has to be able to post at a certain time of the day the next post in queue and it should do the sorting well. At the time I was fine with the tumblr queue, only that at any change the whole queue was published. And it happened so many times I'd rather use a scheduler like the one in blogger than do anything with tumblr.
