There are already several threads about that distro and believe a lot of people is missing an important point - trademark and copyright. When someone uses the Ubuntu logo or the Tux image for an unrelated marketing campaign, like the corn flakes box with the penguin, a lot of people complain about it. I don't believe a simple theme would generate any legal issues, but an OS using another company trademark as name is a serious thing. IMO, that's not good for the FOSS image.
