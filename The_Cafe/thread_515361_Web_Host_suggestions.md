### Post by kadath on 2007-08-02
> **bmeyer said:**
> How's the backend/control panel from them?  Also, do they allow external MySQL connections?  How much are you typically paying a month?

Their control panel is their own creation. It's very simple and straightforward. They don't use CPanel or Fantastico or any of that stuff. You're expected to know what you're doing, generally, but they have a lengthy user FAQ and their support is super fast. They also have a member forum which is moderately active.

They do allow external MySQL connections. They don't limit users very much at all.

I barely pay anything now, since I mostly use it to host random files for another site. I used to have a blog hosted there, but decided to switch to Tumblr for my blogging stuff. So it's VERY cheap for me now.

Cost really depends on how big your site is and how much traffic you get. NFS's rates are 1 cent per MB a month, and $1 per GB of traffic. So if your site is relatively small and only gets a few gigs of traffic a month, you could only spending a few dollars a month. Many people pay a lot less.
