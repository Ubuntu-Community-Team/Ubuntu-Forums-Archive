### Post by swegner on 2008-09-10
There are a couple of alternative OS'es for iPods and other mp3 players out there.  iPodLinux runs a full Linux Kernel, although I think it is a little rough for actual media player usage.  RockBox is another open-source alternative, and runs on a variety of different hardware.  I've used it before, and I think it does a pretty good job.
