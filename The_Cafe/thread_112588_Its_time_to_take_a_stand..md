### Post by bonzodog on 2006-01-04
There is a difference between the team members, forum staff, and admins.
Team members are just that: we take part in different forum teams that concentrate on different areas of ubuntu in the forums. 

Team Bahamut, for instance looks after [http://doc.gwos.org](http://doc.gwos.org). our job is to take how-to's form the forums and move them to the doc facility.

Then there are forum staff: they moderate forum posts, and generally keep an eye on things. 

Then, theres the Administrators, of which there are only 3. the admins have top level access to the forums and it's software and can make changes to the way the forums are run.
