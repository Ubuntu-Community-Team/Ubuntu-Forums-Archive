### Post by bhursey on 2008-10-15
Well I don't know if its dead but it has slowed down. While I was in college I was a developer on it. 

I was working on the weather app to have live weather 3d background in it. Like if its raining you would like see rain out your virtual window and so on.  I think that lg3d was a good concept. However there were not enough developers to keep it going. We needed more people to step in and help. The 3d api was a little involved in learning but it was a good concept. I think that xgl and compiz when it was released bypassed lg3d. However it still is cool to play with. I no longer am a developer in my life. I do unix/linux support at EMC corp and I am married so I no longer have time to develop. Im sure if people get intrested agian they could revive the project. They inventor of project looking glass actual left sun in 2007 although the project is still open if anyone wants to continue work on it. 

Last fall I sent a email to sun saying I no longer can contribute and im sorry. I then removed my self from the developers wiki. I think I still have access to my code I am not sure.

You can access the project at [https://lg3d.dev.java.net/](https://lg3d.dev.java.net/) there are some life cd's and it works in ubuntu. I dont know if it works in 8.04 now though.
