### Post by doas777 on 2009-04-30
um. ok then. 

if your not getting views, then look at your subject line. does it concisely sumarize the issue or type of issue you are experiencing?

then look at the body. are you providing detail sufficient to the problem, so the community can help you with it? is your tone polite and are your requirements reasonable? is there any troubleshooting information you've noticed anyone else using for similar types of problems while researching your problem online? if so, attach it.

if you don't get hits within the first day, bump. if you are getting hits but no replys, try reorganizing your post, and wait.

thats the best advice I can give you. I've had many a thread with no good answers (you can check). you can't expect everything to work perfectly for everyone.
