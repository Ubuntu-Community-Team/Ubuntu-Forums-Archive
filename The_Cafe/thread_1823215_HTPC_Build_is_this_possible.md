### Post by coffee412 on 2011-08-11
In all actuality, YOu can run mythtv with maybe a suitable capture card like the hdhomerun (google it). You CAN keep the same quality as the origional capture. Just make sure your bitrate on mythtv is set correctly. Your not going to notice the difference and should be close enough to satisfy you.

I have been running mythtv for years with 2 tuner cards and capturing from DirecTV. 

Mythtv can be a bit of a bear to setup along with controlling set top boxes (STBs) as I do it with a serial to usb converter. 

Of course, If you are married you will have to put up with the wife using all the storage for her junk. Lifetime, Soaps, Stupid shows. :)

If your looking for a format for your iphone or whatever you can setup the transcoding of the capture to meets its specs and just transfer it over.

What I like most about Mythtv is that it WILL edit out your commercials. It will transcode to a different codec and it also has a HTML interface (mythweb) that you can use to setup recordings, Download shows, Watch via a stream.

So yes, You pretty much can do it. Just take your time and it will pay off well.
