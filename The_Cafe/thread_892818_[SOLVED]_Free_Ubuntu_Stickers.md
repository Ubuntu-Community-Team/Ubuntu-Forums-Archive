### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-09-22
All sticker requests received in our office up to today have now been fulfilled. So, if you do not receive yours in the next several days, it is probably because:

1. We did not get your envelope
2. You did not put a return SASE in your envelope
3. You put System76's address on your return SASE, and we sent it out and it came back to us

If you think you may have fallen into one of the above categories, please send me an email at support(at)system76(dot)com.

NOTE: A few of you very kindly put money in your envelopes for us. Thank you. We will put it towards the next batch of stickers so that we can keep spreading awareness of Ubuntu.
