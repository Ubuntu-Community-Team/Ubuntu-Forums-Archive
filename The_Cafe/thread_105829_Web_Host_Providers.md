### Post by Zotova on 2005-12-19
I've been with 1&1.co.uk for about a week and so far they seem very solid and fast, a bit pricey but I don't mind paying more for a good service as I have had a string of bad isp's in my past.

For very cheap domains which have web forwarding and email forwarding I'd recommend 123-reg.co.uk (who are now owned by pipex (big uk isp)). I was with them for a few years when I only wanted a domain for email and I never had any problems with their service, nor have I with pipex my broadband isp.

**AVOID** these at all costs:

ukhost4u.com - Constant cannot connect to this site errors. If they would have sorted that out they were otherwise a cheap and feature packed host.

streamlinenet.co.uk - mysql servers are slow. My phpbb forum generally took about 1 minute to load (it is only a small forum with about 8000 posts). At peak times my forum was unusable. Also only this week I had an email from these complaining that they could not draw money from my debit card for a renewal fee for a domain name... a domain name I had never even owned and clearly was owned by some other company. Thankfully the money was not in my account or I would never have seen it again.

dream-hosting.co.uk - my site went offline for days on end - I still have yet to have a reply from my support tickets, owner ignored users posts for help on their own forum, then suddenly one day the link to their support forum disappeared so potential new users wouldn't see how crap the service was.
