### Post by PsyberOneZero on 2005-10-11
As part of the plan to help spread Ubuntu, we've created the Ubuntu MarketingTeam [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarketingTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarketingTeam)
We are looking for creative people to help design, create documents, presentations, and other media for promoting Ubuntu.  Anyone is welcome, there are several projects in need of leaders.

We are also in need of a few liaisons, their role would be, to be a single point of contact between the Marketing Team and other Team. Right now we are looking for:

Art Team Liaison
Installation Team Liaison
Local Team Liaison

Stop by and take a look, anyone can help whether you want to lead a project, just help on one, or submit ideas for others to play with [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarketingTeamBrainstorm](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarketingTeamBrainstorm)
