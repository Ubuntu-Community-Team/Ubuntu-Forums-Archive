### Post by Dragonbite on 2007-05-16
Do you mean import the actual feeds or just your subscriptions?

You can import your Google Reader's feeds by saving it as an OPML and import it into another program.  They have a little information in their [[COLOR="Blue"]FAQ[/COLOR]]("http://www.google.com/help/reader/faq.html#export"). The page also has a list of readers you can export Google Reader stuff to.

I used Thunderbird until I found Google Reader, then using the OPML I moved all of my feeds I collected in Thunderbird and moved it to Google Reader.

I know there's one in KDE (aggreKator .... something or other,  I can't remember it's name for the life of me right now ).

Not sure if that helps.
