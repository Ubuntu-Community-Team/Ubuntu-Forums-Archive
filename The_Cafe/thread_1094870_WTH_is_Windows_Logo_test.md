### Post by rickyjones on 2009-03-13
> **amitabhishek said:**
> ^^ Since its a laptop I have the admin rights for this machine.

It did give "Proceed anyway" option & after 5 warnings I was able to install virtual box. However I was wondering what this warning was...? Does M$ hate freeware(s) from Sun so much...:D?

More likely than not the warnings you saw were in regards to unsigned device drivers that Microsoft couldn't certify. Sun didn't provide a driver that Microsoft could verify compatibility with.

That warning is actually for your protection so you know what drivers are certified to work with Windows. As it stands, you should be fine.

And no, Microsoft doesn't hate freeware that much to put up a warning about it... otherwise 95% of the software on my laptop would throw up a funky warning during installation.
