The security + Exam is fairly straight forward, short-ish questions with multiple choice answers.

Study your material, like anything else and you should be fine.

It covers multiple domains, you can check the areas of study at the CompTIA + site.

There is no one area of it to study, you need Telecoms to Network Inf to Cryptography (know your symettric/asymetric, DES,RCA, SHA ad nauseum ad infinitum all the way to physical security and fire suppression technologies ;-)

As for Linux +, there is 2 exams, and when you take them if you tick the box you will also be awarded LPI level 1 and Novell certified Linux Administrator.

Not many people are aware of this, so you get 3 certs for the price of 1 as it were.

Good luck
