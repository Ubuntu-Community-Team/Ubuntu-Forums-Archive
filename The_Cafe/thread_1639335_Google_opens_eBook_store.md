### Post by Dr. C on 2010-12-06
They are working on bringing the store to more countries. Selling books internationally is very tricky because of exclusive distribution rights in many countries. In some cases the exclusive distribution rights are part of copyright law. 

As for DRM: If there is DRM there will be problems for legitimate users sooner or later. I have yet to see a DRM scheme that does not cause  serious problems to legitimate users that pay for content, while having a minimal impact if at all on pirates.
