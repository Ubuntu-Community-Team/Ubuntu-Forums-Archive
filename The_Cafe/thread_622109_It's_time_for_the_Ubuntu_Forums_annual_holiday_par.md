### Post by matthew on 2007-11-24
Welcome one and all to the annual Official Ubuntu Forums [COLOR=DarkRed]winter[/COLOR] [COLOR=DarkGreen]holiday[/COLOR] [COLOR=Navy]party.[/COLOR] Some of us celebrate Christmas, others Hanukkah, still others will be enjoying the Aid Al Kebir or something else this winter. 

It is not our goal to promote any specific religion, but rather to let everyone know they are welcome to wish one another a joyous season. 

In that vein, we invite everyone to join in the fun and add something appropriately seasonal to your avatar. Some good choices would include things like Santa hats, snowmen, snowflakes, and yes, even a nativity scene or menorah, etc. Please don't get obnoxious about it, though, okay? :)

Let's all get carried away being welcoming and friendly to one another ***and*** stay far away from being easily offended, grumpy, or feeling the need to proselytize...this is all in fun, let's keep it lighthearted and enjoy one another's holidays (more to celebrate that way) instead of getting involved in some people's exclusive "my beliefs are better than yours" weirdness that sometimes accompanies this time of year.

Thanks, and again...Welcome!!!
