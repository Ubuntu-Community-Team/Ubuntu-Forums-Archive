### Post by Tyche on 2007-03-25
For those who do not know, the Kodak tower is in the middle of the city of Rochester, New York, on one of the busiest streets in the city.  Kodak has been doing this for a number of years.  It started as an effort to get falcons to move into the city, in the hopes that it would reduce the pigeon population.  At least the falcons are healthy :lolflag:
