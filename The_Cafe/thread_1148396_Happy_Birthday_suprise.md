### Post by sirjoebob on 2009-05-04

> notify-send ["Happy birthday"] "Hope you aren't too old to read this"

run in terminal or alt+f2 to test it out and you could add it to startup applications.

### Post by NCLI on 2009-05-04
You could also do a:
> 
notify-send ["Happy birthday"] "Hope you aren't too old to read this" && espeak "Happy Birthday bro"
This would make Ubuntu say "Happy Birthday bro!" as well as display the text notification. You can simply add the line to Startup Applications after testing it in the commandline(or with alt+F2).
