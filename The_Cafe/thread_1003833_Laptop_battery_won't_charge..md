### Post by grazed on 2008-12-06
lithium ion batteries have something like 800-1000 max charge cycles before they cannot be charged anymore.

so every time the battery dips down, and you plug it back in, even if it's just for a minute or so, it counts towards that number.

although, if the notebook is less than a year old it's hard to get there that quickly, in that case i would contact the manufacturer for warranty service.
