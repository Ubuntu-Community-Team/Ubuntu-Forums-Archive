### Post by mamamia88 on 2011-11-01
Before I was using my cable box and usb hub but it was very disorganized and the cables where everywhere.   Inspired by an article from lifehacker where someone built a charging hub from parts at ikea I decided I wanted to do something like that but coooler and geekier.   So I looked around the house for stuff I had on hand.   I found an old bricked wrt54g router I had on hand.   So I popped it open pryed the main board off and fed the cables out of the back where ethernet ports where.    I then plugged the power supply into a wall charger that came with my phone.    Works great.  What do you guys think?
