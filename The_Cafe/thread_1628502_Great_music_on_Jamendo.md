### Post by joepie91 on 2010-11-22
I'm not exactly sure whether it fits in here (seeing as the Community Cafe is still full of tech-related topics) but just wanted to tell you guys about some excellent music I found a while ago (and it's Creative Commons-licensed of course :)):

[http://www.jamendo.com/en/artist/zero-project](http://www.jamendo.com/en/artist/zero-project)

The music is a tad... weird if you compare it to the regular music you buy on a CD, but if you like soundtrack-ish and instrumental music, it would be a good thing to check out. I found it's especially great to listen to when reading a book or writing something. My own favorites are the Ambient Symphony and Fairytale (1 + 2) albums.

While on the subject, for those who have listened to music on Jamendo before, what's your favorite music on there?

### Post by chessnerd on 2010-11-23
[Josh Woodward]("http://www.jamendo.com/en/artist/joshwoodward") - By far my favorite CC artist; most of his music falls under the genre of pop rock. My favorite album of his so far is ["The Simple Life"]("http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/23755") but they are all good. I highly recommend him.

[Jason Silver]("http://www.jamendo.com/en/artist/Jason_Silver_(2)") - A Christian artist. He has a nice [Christmas album]("http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/56539") and I like his album ["Seek"]("http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/55245")

[Fresh Body Shop]("http://www.jamendo.com/en/artist/freshbodyshop") - They're pretty good. They have a rather unique style that you need to listen to to get a feel for. I like their album ["Tasteless"]("http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/9604") and I'm not alone...

### Post by Dry Lips on 2011-08-07
Here are a few of my favourites:


**My, Bubba & Mi**: This lovely girl trio makes these übercool 
folk/country/blues/jazz kind of love songs. 
[http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/63029](http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/63029)

Check especially out the songs *Steamengeene* and *Really Really*.

---
[B]
Bella Ruse[/B]:
Beautiful and catchy, melodic and sweet... Who could ask for more?
[http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/56070](http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/56070)

*Hold Me Close* is one of the best songs...

---

**icare:**
The album "*Empire of Lights, part II*" is happysad melancholic pop. Not suitable
for nice summer days, but is an excellent choice for cold, dreary autumn
nights.
[http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/85479](http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/85479)
