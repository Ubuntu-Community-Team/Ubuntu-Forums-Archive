### Post by Palmyra on 2008-02-10
As others have, I would recommend you clean up the education part.  A Levels means nothing to someone who lives in the United States.  The number of years may be more useful.  You might also ask how far one is from working a full time job or something.  

College and University are, in some places, considered the same.  In the United States, college = university, and university = college.

Some of the other questions require clarification such as "How often do you use a computer?"  I wanted to ask "per day, per week, or per month?"
