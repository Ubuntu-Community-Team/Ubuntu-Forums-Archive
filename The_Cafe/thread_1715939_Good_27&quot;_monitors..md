### Post by Cracklepop on 2011-03-27
> **Newuser901 said:**
> Is the baclick better than the fLuro?

All LCD screen are LCDs at the front, and some form of backlighting behind.  Until recently the backlighting has been fluro tubes, but LED backlighting is pretty common now (and to add to the confusion they call LED backlit LCDs 'LED' monitors, when they are in fact the same old LCD we're used to...)

LED backlighting is thinner and more economical (and possibly slightly more expensive), which makes it better IMHO.
