### Post by samalex on 2010-07-13
If you're connected to the IRC server send a message to anyone with @ in front of their nick, that's an admin.  Or outside of that you may be able to contact someone through one of the email addresses on Leo's PR page - [http://leoville.com/pr](http://leoville.com/pr) or worse case [email]leo@twit.tv[/email].  If you're looking for general support you might not get a response, but if you have a major problem or bug to report, they might be able to help if it doesn't go to Leo himself.
