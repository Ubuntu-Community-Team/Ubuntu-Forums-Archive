### Post by futz on 2005-12-20
Just buy a molex (4 pin) splitter. They're common as dirt at any computer store and only about two bucks. You're not likely to find sata to molex adapters. If they're made, they're not common. Molex to sata, on the other hand, are available, tho still not nearly so easy to find as plain old molex splitters.
