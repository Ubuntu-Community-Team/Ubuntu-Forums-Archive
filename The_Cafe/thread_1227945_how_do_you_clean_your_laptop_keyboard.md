### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-07-31
Spittle and an old sock...  well, it keeps people from borrowing my laptop!


Seriously though, I've found that a small paintbrush --like the kind that comes in a child's water color paint set -- is pretty good for getting hair and lint out from between the keys.  Just run it along the rows.  If you do that before vacuuming you'll get a lot more out in a lot less time.
