Hotmail suggested I use copenhagen57, copenhagen58, choose a different one, or use my real name.

I chose copenhagen57, and it was shortened to Cope57 for forums and other online nick name usage. So, there you have it, even though I no longer use the Copenhagen product I kept Cope57 as my nick.
