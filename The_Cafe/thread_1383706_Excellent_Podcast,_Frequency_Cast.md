### Post by the.scarecrow on 2010-01-17
I would like to recommend the podcast from Frequency Cast as I am sure that many of you will enjoy listening to it.

[http://www.frequencycast.co.uk/](http://www.frequencycast.co.uk/)

The subject matter is all things techi with a bias towards video and audio product reviews. It is made in the UK with a unique style and lots of humor making it enjoyable for a wide range of listeners.

They work to a very reliable schedule, each episode is a gem. But they have fallen to a low rating in iTunes and for some reason this bothers them. I can't vote on iTunes for them as I don't do iTunes so hence this genuine recommendation to you all here.

I hope you will give them a try of a few episodes and I am sure you will enjoy them and become one of their loyal listeners.

Even if the subject matter doesn't "float your boat", you are sure to enjoy the unique presentation style.
