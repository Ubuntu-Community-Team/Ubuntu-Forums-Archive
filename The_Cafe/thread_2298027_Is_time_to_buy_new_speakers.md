### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-10-08
Take a voltmeter and measure the resistance across the leads of the speaker.  It should be 8 Ohms, or possibly 16 Ohms.  You may have to open up the enclosure to get to the speaker.  If you really like the speaker, you can find replacement drivers at [http://www.allelectronics.com/make-a-store/category/580/speakers/1.html](http://www.allelectronics.com/make-a-store/category/580/speakers/1.html).

If you are not up to fixing it, then check craigslist or a local donation store.  There are usually lots of PC speakers available.
