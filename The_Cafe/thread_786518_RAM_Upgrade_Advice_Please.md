### Post by mips on 2008-05-08
> **Chessmaster said:**
> Hi thanks for the advice.



Does that mean that you cannot mix RAM types? I have two RAM slots so does that mean that I cannot have some DDR400 RAM with a DDR333, but you can have two of the same. 

Cheers

From personal experience you can have both. The ram will however be clocked at the lower 333mhz speed and not 400mhz. *If* your laptop allows you to change your ram clock speed and you are getting two sticks then go for 400mhz. Mixing ram speeds in this scenario will be fine as the 400mhz ram can be clocked at the lower 333mhz of your existing ram.

I will have the exact same scenario. My laptop has 2x256mb 333mhz sticks in it already, I'm replacing the one with a 1GB 400mhz stick which works fine. Done it before. Sometimes it's also easier or cheaper to find 400mhz ram.
