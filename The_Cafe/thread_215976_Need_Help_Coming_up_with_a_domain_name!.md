### Post by Biltong (Dee) on 2006-07-14
Keep it to the point. If you are creating a commercial website the URL is CRITICAL.
For example - take zebraafrica.com.  They sell Zebra skins. 
People Google under subject and location. "ZEBRA - AFRICA"
They are based in Africa. 
Ergo, they are No 1.
Xebra is not a word that people would use much, but most sites selling Zebra products would have it in their meta-keywords anyway, thus ensuring that if someone did enter this as a search their website would be No1 anyway.
Because there are so many Zebra websites all taking advantage of misspelled words they would effectively push your website way back on Google, probably to page 5.
Choose wisely and carefully.
