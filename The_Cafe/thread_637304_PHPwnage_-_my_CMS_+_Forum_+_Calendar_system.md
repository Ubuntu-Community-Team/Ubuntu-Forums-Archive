### Post by klange on 2007-12-10
Phew, been working on this new release of my forum software for the past few weeks and I think it's finally ready to release.
Anyway, PHPwnage is a PHP + MySQL-based news, forum, and calendar system that I designed for my team website ([Oasis-Games.com](http://home.oasis-games.com)). It started out as a simple news system, and the forum was later added due to a bet (which I won, having created a full forum system)

This new release has a ton of new features over my previous development versions, most notably calendar integration and custom ranks. It's also far more secure than previous versions, so much so that this is the only release I consider to be truly stable.

You can check out the CMS and download it at [Oasis-Games.com](http://home.oasis-games.com) and see the newest development build in action at [OGUnderground.com](http://home.ogunderground.com). PHPwnage is licensed under the GPL.
