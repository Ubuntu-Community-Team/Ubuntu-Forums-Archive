### Post by RizSilverthorn on 2006-11-13
I went for the newer 275MHz GP2X (didn't realise that was 200MHz standard, 275MHz overclocked). The website I got mine from ([www.gp2x.co.uk]("http://www.gp2x.co.uk")) fails to make this clear, but I'm acually happy with the unit. Battery life does suck abit, but if you avoid using it to transfer the files onto a SD card and use a card reader instead, the battery life is extended.

Stick the Qtopia port on it and then the software base expands dramatically :)
