### Post by mattl on 2012-12-24
hey, I'm the founder of Libre.fm -- I'm happy to help.

We can help you get your scrobbling data out of Last.fm and into Libre.fm.

If you use a player we support (which is most of them) then scrobbling to us is really easy. Some players, like Quod Libet and Rhythmbox, even support Libre.fm natively on Ubuntu.

What browser are you using?

We're very much alive.

If you can, join #libre.fm on irc.freenode.net for live support, or you can email me [http://matt.lee.name/contact/](http://matt.lee.name/contact/)
