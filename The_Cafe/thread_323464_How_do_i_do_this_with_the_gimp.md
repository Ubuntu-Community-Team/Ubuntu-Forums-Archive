### Post by rowanparker on 2006-12-22
I managed to do it using [this](http://www.taimila.com/images/maarit.jpg) picture. (See attatched).

All I did was open the pic in Gimp, highlight all (Ctrl+A). Then from the main Gimp window I selected the "Perspective" tool (last one on second row). Then I dragged down from the top right corner then up from the bottom right corner. Then when you're happy, click transform.

Hope that helps, Rowan.
