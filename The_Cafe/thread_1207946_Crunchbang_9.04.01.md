> Can I update with software update for must I backup and fresh ISO install?

You can update.

You can either update the sources.list files to say "jaunty" instead of Intrepid, or you can do:

sudo apt-get install update-manager
sudo update-manager -d

(I believe that is the correct command) There are other ways also, but if you have any questions, the #! Forums are maybe the better place to ask, where I, and the people who know better than I, reside. ;)
