### Post by LowSky on 2011-07-18
Newegg prices have gone up especially in shipping. It used to be free 3-5 day shipping for most things, around 5+ years ago. Then they started charging and keeping free shipping only on rare sales. Still one of the better places to shop no doubt. Its usually the first and last place I will shop for PC gear.  

Another decent site is Tigerdirect.com but their selection is much smaller. Amazon prices can be great but there selection can be odd and sometimes items can be listed multiple times and sold through other companies, so be careful there.
