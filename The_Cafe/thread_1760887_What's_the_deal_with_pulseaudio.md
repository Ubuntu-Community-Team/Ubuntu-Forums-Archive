### Post by Bandit on 2011-05-17
Although Pulse Audio is over all better sound sever, its not as mature as ESS or ALSA that are much more stable and offer better compatibility. 
For example with Pulse Audio you can adjust the sound for each application individually and mix the sound each on a separate channel. Were as with ALSA for example you can not, adjusting the Wave sound on one application effects the Wave control for all applications and they can only be pipped through one mixer.

That being said, and I may be wrong, but I really wished the PA team would focus more on stability and compatibility for a few months.
