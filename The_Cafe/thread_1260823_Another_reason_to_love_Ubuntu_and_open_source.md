### Post by bvanaerde on 2009-09-08
In my "Windows days" I used EAC:
[http://www.exactaudiocopy.de/en/index.php/resources/download/](http://www.exactaudiocopy.de/en/index.php/resources/download/)
As an encoder, I used Lame. You'll just need the executable:
[http://lame.sourceforge.net/links.php#Binaries](http://lame.sourceforge.net/links.php#Binaries)

Normally, the install guide of EAC will configure everything for you. There are [EAC + Lame guides]("http://www.music-reviewed.com/guides.php?page=eac_config_01"), but they can be a bit dated.

I think it's worth the complicated configuration, as this is the only setup that could make high quality rips without errors, even from scratched CDs.
