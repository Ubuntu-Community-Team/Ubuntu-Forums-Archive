### Post by abhiroopb on 2008-06-11
Its admirable that you want to create your on distro. But is there any specific reason you want do so? If it is because you haven't found a distro that suits your purposes, then I suggest looking harder. There are SO many available distros! Perhaps something you want to see changed in Ubuntu.

If you're still really keen on it, I suggest you look into some of the younger distro's that are just starting up and try and help there.

### Post by leg on 2008-06-11
[Linux from scratch]("http://www.linuxfromscratch.org/") is where you need to look. You get yourself an old laptop with a x86 processor, download their live cd and build your own operating system. I doubt very much that you will end up creating a new distro and I agree with others that there are more than enough out there to suit any needs at the moment. You will learn the ins and outs of Linux though which is probably why you are thinking on these lines.
