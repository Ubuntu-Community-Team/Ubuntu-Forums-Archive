### Post by Rhubarb on 2007-01-18
You could make frisbees or ninja stars out of 'em.

They make cheap reflective diffraction gratings - so you can study (in a poor way) the spectrum of a particular light source.

You could chuck them in the oven (to soften them up) - so you can make a cool little sculpture out of 'em.
