### Post by Onyros on 2007-10-19
All-nighters have kinda become my lifestyle. I pull a couple, sometimes more, per week.

I usually sleep 3 to 4 hours per night, so when I pull an all-nighter I usually compensate by sleeping an extra 2 or 3 hours the next day, or when I can.

I used to be a full-scale insomniac, going for several days without sleeping, but things were complicated then. I used to start seeing things with delay, feeling somewhat out-of-body and all.

As strange as it may seem, a part of me misses that part of my life. Life was much more surreal back then :P
