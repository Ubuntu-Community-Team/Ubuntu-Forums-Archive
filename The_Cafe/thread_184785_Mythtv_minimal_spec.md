### Post by joker on 2006-05-30
Check out knoppmyth (also Debian based) for a pain free mythtv install if that is all the machine will be doing.  The 450 will probibly be ok for your backend server (recording and streaming to your frontends) as long as you don't want to use it for playback or high def.  It is a little slow for simultanious playback and record.  I believe the PVR-500 is supported out of the box with knoppmyth too, I know for fact that my PVR-150 and PVR-250 are.  The Knoppmyth forums will be able to give you some good reference points regarding your hardware.
