### Post by NightwishFan on 2008-03-31
I remember I managed to get it working by installing compiz and emerald. There was a guide somewhere. I prefer not to use Compiz and just use the Xfce compositor though. I am not saying this will work but its worth a try. Install compiz and emerald and then I think you just have to do:
```
compiz --replace
```
Then to get the window borders you need:
```
emerald --replace
```
