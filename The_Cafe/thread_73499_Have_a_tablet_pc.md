### Post by 23meg on 2005-10-09
almost all tablet pcs from the last two years have keyboards. what's commonly referred to as a tablet pc today is a computer that can be used in both tablet and laptop modes.

> Is there any reason to buy a tablet pc instead of a laptop? 

i can speak for myself; for me the reasons were the intuition of the pen interface in apps like gimp and photoshop (nothing beats the ability to draw directly on the screen), the specs vs. price of my particular tablet pc, and my graduation project in part of which i'll be experimenting with alternative input devices.
