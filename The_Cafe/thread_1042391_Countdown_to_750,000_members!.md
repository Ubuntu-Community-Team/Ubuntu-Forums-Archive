As a few of you may have noticed, the forums are getting very close to reaching 750,000 members, the third milestone in Ubuntu's forum community. Does anyone remember the IRC chat/party going on when we reached 500,000 members?

As of this post, we stand at approximately 748,712 members, meaning we have just under 1,300 members to go before hitting the target. :)
