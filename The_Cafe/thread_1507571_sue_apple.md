### Post by samjh on 2010-06-11
> **chuckbenger said:**
> Just seen a ipad commcercial and it said all the worlds websites in your hands. But.... the ipad doesn't support flash websites so thats false advertising.Marbles.

False advertising generally doesn't include mere puffery.  No reasonable person would think "all the world's websites in your hands" is intended to be taken seriously, or induce someone in the iPad's market demographic to purchase the product.

As a comparison, a university or college can claim that it is "the most innovative in the country" or has "world-class researchers".  Both are mere puffery.  Likewise, a car manufacturer might say one of its car models has "comfortable leather seats", but the mere fact some people find them uncomfortable does not mean the statement is false advertising.  Would any reasonable person truly believe that a brand of laundry detergent will make their clothes "whiter than white"?  Most likely not.
