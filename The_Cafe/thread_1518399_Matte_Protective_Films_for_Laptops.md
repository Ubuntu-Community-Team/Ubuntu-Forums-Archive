### Post by Hated On Mostly on 2010-06-26
Generally privacy filters for LCD screens also reduce glare and have a matte finish. Certainly the 3M privacy filters do this and I have seen others that claim the same features.

[http://solutions.3m.com/wps/portal/3M/en_US/SDP/Privacy_Filters/Products/Catalog/Product-Catalog/?PC_7_RJH9U5230GEMB0IGF3MOBJ3CN7_nid=VSSZ8073WWbeD13D156TKGgl](http://solutions.3m.com/wps/portal/3M/en_US/SDP/Privacy_Filters/Products/Catalog/Product-Catalog/?PC_7_RJH9U5230GEMB0IGF3MOBJ3CN7_nid=VSSZ8073WWbeD13D156TKGgl)

> Reduce glare

> Light matte finish reduces screen glare

The other major brands of privacy filters are Fellowes and Targus and both also claim anti-glare for their products as well.
