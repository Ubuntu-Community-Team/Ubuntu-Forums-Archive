### Post by Moustacha on 2008-06-02
LGA775 has been the "same" for a while, but the chipsets don't support the newer cpus and newer chipsets don't support older cpus. how's that for you? Also, when Intel's new cpu comes out, nehalem i think, it has the memory controller onboard and uses a Hyper Transport like bus, so I'm gonna say it's going to be a new socket. Definitely new boards.

AM3 is supposed to come next year I think, when DDR3 becomes the mainstream, until then it's AM2/+
