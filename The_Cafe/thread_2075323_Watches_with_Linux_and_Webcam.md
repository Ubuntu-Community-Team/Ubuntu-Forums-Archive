### Post by mr john on 2012-10-23
As a runner I'd be interested in an android wristwatch with GPS built in. Why? because it can track my runs. It would tell me how fast I'm going, how far I've gone and could sync with my training plan so I would know if I was close to reaching my goals.  I normally carry a small, cheap Android phone during my runs to do all of this. I was able to slash my 10k times by about 10 minutes using the Micoach application. I signed up for a training program on their website and it sync'ed to my phone. During the run my phone showed me my speed, time and distance  and sent all the data to the website so I could see a more detailed analysis later. When I ran a marathon it was a bit of a hassle carrying the phone for several hours.
