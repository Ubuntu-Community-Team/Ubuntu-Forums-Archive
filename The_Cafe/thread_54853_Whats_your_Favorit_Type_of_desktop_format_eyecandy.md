### Post by poofyhairguy on 2005-08-06
[QUOTE=Omnios]Just wondering what is everyones favorit desktop layout pertaining to eye candy. [/QUOTE]

Hmm....I like Enlightenment 17's "lightening strikes the taskbar of the focused window" trick. I like Kompose for KDE. I like a few gdesklets. 

By far though, my favorite Eye Candy trick is xcompmgr's fade trick. If you have not seen that trick working on an Nvidia card with official drivers and the composite extension, you have not seen the best Eye Candy in Linuxland. This single trick lead me to buy 2 Nvidia cards.

Problem is its pretty unstable. And it hates Metacity. I use it as much as I can....but sometimes its too much. Enlightened Gnome stops the Xserver crashes that Metacity produces, but it still messes with video and sometimes the screen has draw problems. My biggest hope is that this stabilized more....but it seems many have given up on it because of its instability so its development rate is slow.

In all the videos of Vista I have seen, that future OS has a trick much like xcompmgr's trick. Hopefully it become more stable by then, and the playing field can be even.
