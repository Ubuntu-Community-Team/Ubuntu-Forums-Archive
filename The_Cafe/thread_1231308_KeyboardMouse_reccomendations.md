Keyboard: BTC model 6300CL, a backlit keyboard - very flat, nice scissor mechanism keys and has survived two helpings of Coke Zero. Cost me US$12.00 on Geeks.com Sadly, I don't think it's manufactured anymore.

Cost me US$20.00 on [NewEgg]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16826104019&nm_mc=OTC-Froogle&cm_mmc=OTC-Froogle-_-Mouse-_-Logitech+Inc.-_-26104019"), but has gone down a bit in price.
