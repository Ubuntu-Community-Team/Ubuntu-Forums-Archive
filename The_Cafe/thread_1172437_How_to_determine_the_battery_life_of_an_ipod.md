### Post by suitedaces on 2009-05-28
Two ways, one was charge it fully, put one song on repeat, wipe the play count then let it play til it's flat and then check the count. Another was to connect it up to a mic input on a computer, and record it with a sound capture program, and go back to see when the recording stopped.
