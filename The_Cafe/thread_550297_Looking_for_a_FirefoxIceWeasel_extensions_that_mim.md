I have found a solution that even works a bit better the Operas default function!

         **[Scroll Search Engines]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3696")**

          by [Einar Egilsson]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/user/61607")     

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3696](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3696)

This is actually a very cool extension, it works two ways.

1. mouse over the search engine selector in the search bar and scroll between the search engine needed, no need to click and select.

2. this is the most important feature, highlight your desired text>right click>then scroll through your search engines here and select your desired search engine. No need for a long list of search engines like in Opera.


Very, very awesome extension!
