I seem to be getting about 50 emails a day from people wanting to unsubscirbe from a Lauchpad bug mailing list. 

Step 1. go to [https://bugs.launchpad.net/](https://bugs.launchpad.net/)
step 2. Search for the bug you want to unsubscribe to
Step 3. Once you are on the bug page look  to the right and you will see unsubscribe.
