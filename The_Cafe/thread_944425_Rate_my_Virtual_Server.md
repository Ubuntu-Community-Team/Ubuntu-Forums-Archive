### Post by Canis familiaris on 2008-10-11
You brought a Virtual Server? 
> Virtual Dedicated Server: dividing a server into virtual servers, where each user feels like they're on their own dedicated server, but they're actually sharing a server with many other users. The users may have root access to their own virtual space. This is also known as a virtual private server or VPS.

Now I hope you use it for more than just hosting your blog since they are pricey. If you are using it for just hosting your blog, it's far more than what you need and thus waste of money.
If you had not already taken hosting I would have recommended a Shared Web Hosting.

I am not sure it why it offers slowdown at times, maybe there is maintenace or something. If it is slow enough so frequently that it'll bother your visitors than contact your service provider.
