### Post by TheFu on 2022-03-09
Yes. My order of importance: 
Please 
* Use the Thread tools button to mark it as SOLVED
* Say, in a clear way, what the solution was if the thread answers aren't perfectly clear. This is the pay-it-forward aspect.
* If you can, say which part of the posts helped for you to find the solution. You may see 5 different people providing an answer, but only 1 of them 'clicked' for you. This can help others get better responses, faster, for the next person.
* If you like, thank the people who helped, but just being polite is excellent.  People trying to help really do want the feed back about the actual solution that worked.

Thanks for asking. the last 2 in the list above are nice, but not necessary, at least to me.
