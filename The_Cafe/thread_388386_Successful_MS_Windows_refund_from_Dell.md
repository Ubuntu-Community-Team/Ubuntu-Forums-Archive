### Post by der_vegi on 2007-03-19
Hi!

Wow, i'm happy. Today I got back 94€ from Dell!

I just bought a Dell Inspiron 1501 Notebook with Windows XP and Works 8.5 preinstalled. When I first started the notebook, it said i had to accept the Dell Eula by pressing any key. If i didn't accpet, i would have to call Dell. So did I, keeping my fingers off the keyboard. The technical support couldn't help me, so I wrote an email to the customer support, telling them, that I don't want to accept the Eula. First they where a little bit surprised, telling me clicking somewhere in Windows, which I of course couldn't. After my second Email they told me to delete the software, destroy the CDs and give them my bank account for a refund, which I did. Only some days later I received the money.

Yeah! No more MS tax! :)
