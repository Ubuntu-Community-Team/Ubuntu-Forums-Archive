While looking through the blogs at planet.ubuntu.com I came across the [What The Hack website]("http://wiki.whatthehack.org/index.php/Main_Page"), where it seems you can download videos of every single lecture given at this event. For those not in the know, What The Hack was a huge open air hacker/geek meeting largely focused on open source software that took place in Netherlands this summer. Just thought I'd share it with those interested. My favorite so far is Kevin Warwick's cyborg lecture.
