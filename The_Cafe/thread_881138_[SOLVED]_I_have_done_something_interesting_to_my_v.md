### Post by Ebuntor on 2008-08-05
This topic should probably be moved to a support forum.

To answer your question: Go to System => Preferences => Sound

In the Sound Preference window at the bottom under the "Default Mixer Track" there should be a list with the options "Master" "PCM" etc. Select "Master".

Hope that helps. :)
