### Post by Harpalus on 2007-11-30
I recommend an old Thinkpad. Some of the Thinkpad T23 line have internal wireless. Dirt cheap laptops, mine was $300, P4, decent specs - with internal wireless. One of the best laptops I've had.

You can replace the existing internal wireless with a more modern card, too, if you like. If it has internal wireless, then it has an antenna built into the lid, and you can replace the card. Just don't assume that T23's *without* internal wireless can get it easily enough. If they didn't come with a wireless card, they didn't come with an internal antenna either. (Friend made this mistake, assumed that he could just install an internal wireless card)
