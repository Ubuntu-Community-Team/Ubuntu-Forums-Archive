I've mentioned this device in two older threads I thought I'd bump, but the iPhone / iPod Touch keeps getting bumped after my posts. Here's a video of a working prototype:  [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=76NviXg_1g4](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=76NviXg_1g4) ... it has the new Intel Atom processor & should be out before the end of the summer!! MIDs or Netbooks - Welcome to the future of portable computing!!
