### Post by dgd on 2006-01-19
Hello, my name is Dennis.

I'm a teacher and have, in the past, built an LTSP network for my English class. I've since started teaching computing to students on an XP network. Yes, it chafes me but I've kept going with Linux on the side and I'm now doing screencasts of applications and posting them to video.google.

[http://video.google.com/videosearch?q=dennis+daniels&so=0](http://video.google.com/videosearch?q=dennis+daniels&so=0)
At last count I had about 160 screencasts demonstrating applications. 

I'm now working to get more screencasts of Linux and specifically Edubuntu. My end goal is to screencast the full start up process from zero to a full LTSP network on video. I would like to make a plea to all Edubuntu users to try and make at least one screencast and post it. Linux doesn't have a happy face for most computer users, let's give it one by showing the power, flexibility and usability of Linux.

best to all,
Dennis
