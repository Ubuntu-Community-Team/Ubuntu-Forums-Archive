### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-09-23
I'll give a +1 for the Sansa e280.  Very nice system for price.  Massive battery life (I've gone about 17hrs of play time without a charge so far), good support for Linux, native RockBox port, better features than iPod Nano.  Also, the battery is user replaceable.  I liked the e280 so much, that I picked up a couple e250s for my kids.  You can get a e250 (2GB storage with expansion up to 32GB through microSDHD).  On the e250s I put approximately 15 complete albums with lyrics, graphics, and a few music videos to go with each.  Most of the music was ripped at high quality OGG but a few 80% VBR from [http://ExactAudioCopy.de](http://ExactAudioCopy.de) .
