### Post by imhdd on 2006-11-22
I have a simple, personal Web site that contains items of interest to my family and friends. I began constructing the site three years ago and it has grown to about 300 pages.

Some of my subjects bring in Email enquiries from various locations. I always wondered how many visitors I had and where they were located but I didn't want the banner ads and pop-ups that most free counters would place on my home page.

Then I found the ClustrMap. It's free and unintrusive. It shows me the 'how many and where from' of all my visitors. If you want one for your blog or Web site, click on my ClustrMap to zoom in, then choose from the ClustrMap navigation bar.

My home page is located at: [http://imhdd.ms11.net/]("http://imhdd.ms11.net/")
 
My ClustrMap is located at the bottom left side of my home page.
