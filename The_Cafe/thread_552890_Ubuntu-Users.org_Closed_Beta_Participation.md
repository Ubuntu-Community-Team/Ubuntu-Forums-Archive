### Post by kevinf311 on 2007-09-17
Greetings Everyone,

I return to the forums today to let everyone know of the recent developments in the [Ubuntu-Users.org]("http://www.ubuntu-users.org/") world. The latest development is in the form of starting a closed beta to track server statistics.

As you may already know, Ubuntu-Users.org is the project started to provide the community with a site more geared towards social networking to use in conjunction with the other Ubuntu resources we all know and love. 

If you recall, a few weeks ago a thread was started to describe to the community what the focus of the project would be and to keep everyone updated on the progress. That thread can be found [here.]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=535741")

Unfortunately that thread was closed, for further info on what will be happening with the Closed Beta of Ubuntu-Users.org please visit the thread on the Ubuntu-Users.org site located [here.]("http://ubuntu-users.org/forum/viewtopic.php?f=4&t=6")

An unfortunate side effect of the recent events on Ubuntu Forums is that Beamerboy/Paladine/Alexander lost the ability to access the PMs that he received from anyone that was interested in helping out with the site. 

Because of this he requests that anyone who sent him a PM here on Ubuntu Forums regarding the happenings over at Ubuntu-Users.org contact him directly on Ubuntu-Users.org as per the instructions in that thread.

Alexander also apologies for the confusion and any inconvenience that this may have caused you. The project is still going strong and we appreciate all of the support that has been offered.
