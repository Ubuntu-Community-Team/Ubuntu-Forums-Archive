I know this is the worlds most stupid question, but does a pci-e card fit into a pci slot? If not, are there any adaptors?

---

### Post by CharlesA on 2012-07-12
A PCIe card won't fit in a PCI slot and there are no adapters.

You can however use a PCIe x1 card in a PCIe x16 slot.
