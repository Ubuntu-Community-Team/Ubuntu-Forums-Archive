### Post by I-75 on 2008-12-05
Top Favorites

1.Linux Cranks 
2.The Linux Link tech Show
3.Gusty Geeks
4.Linux Outlaws
5.Lotta Linux Links with Dave Yates

Linux Cranks ..... What is really great you have other hosts from other podcasts all getting together weekly for one big podcast. It is informative, straight forward, very funny and highly informative and entertaining all at the same time. It is almost like having a audio Linux Fest every week. It is not family friendly and not for the kiddies.

[http://linuxcranks.info/mp3/LinuxCranks3x21.mp3](http://linuxcranks.info/mp3/LinuxCranks3x21.mp3)

The Linux Link Tech show is been around longer than any other with a amazing 276 shows. The most recent program had Keith Curtis formerly of Microsoft and author of "After the software wars" it was probably one of their best shows. Sometimes not family friendly.

[http://feeds.feedburner.com/~r/TheLinuxLinkTechShowMp3Feed/~3/474315862/tllts_276-12-03-08.mp3](http://feeds.feedburner.com/~r/TheLinuxLinkTechShowMp3Feed/~3/474315862/tllts_276-12-03-08.mp3)


Gusty Geeks is a commercial radio program based out of Phoenix Arizona at KXAM radio. Their greatest program was a non stop 40 minute interview with Richard Stallman.

[http://www.gutsygeeks.com/audio/listen.php?show_id=36&code=4939274b&format=mp3](http://www.gutsygeeks.com/audio/listen.php?show_id=36&code=4939274b&format=mp3)
