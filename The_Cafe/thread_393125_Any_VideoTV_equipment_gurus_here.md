### Post by mips on 2007-03-25
You do get short range video transmitters but the quality is not that great. This would require a sender unit at your sattelite receiver & a receiving unit at your laptop that would connect to a video in rca jack, assuming you have such a jack on your laptop which is rare. this would allow you to capture the video to your screen.

The above however would interfere with those watching upstairs. Unless you have what we call here a dual-view sat. receiver. This allows for two remotes and outputs to two different tv's/monitors. The sat. receiver basically has two internal tuners.

Not the easiest thing you want to do. might be easier to simply install another tv and a dual-view sat receiver.
