> **Nokia Unveils Netbook Dubbed "Booklet 3G"**

Posted by Eric Savitz

Nokia (NOK) this morning officially announced its entry into the netbook market, unveiling a Windows-based unit called the Booklet 3G. The Booklet will have an Intel Atom processor, a 10-inch display and up to 12 hours of battery life. The device weighs 1.25 kg, and measures slightly more than 2 centimeters thick, which for the metrically impaired is 2.75 pounds and 0.8 inches. The Booklet will have 3G, WiFi and GPS built in.
