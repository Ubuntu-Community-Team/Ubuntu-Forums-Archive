### Post by llanitedave on 2013-02-16
A U.K. company is trying to trademark the name "Python" for the exclusive use of its own software and services. 

[The Python Software Foundation]("http://pyfound.blogspot.com/2013/02/python-trademark-at-risk-in-europe-we.html") is asking for help.

Groklaw is discussing it, and anything the community can do will be helpful to all of us.
