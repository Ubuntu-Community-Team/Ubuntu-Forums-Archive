### Post by Occasionally Correct on 2009-10-30
[NoScript]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/722") and [OptimizeGoogle]("http://www.optimizegoogle.com/") satisfy the paranoid little man in the back of my head. :p

[Places in Tab]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/14575") and [Source Viewer Tab]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5908") satisfy the part of me that wants things to be consistent (for a while I also had [Downloads in Tab]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4526") too, but I got rid of it because I was already using Download Statusbar).

[Smart Stop/Reload]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/7401") is a nice, contextual way to combine the stop and reload buttons.

[Stylish]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2108") and [Firebug]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1843") satisfy my inner webdesigner.

[FirefoxNotify]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/9622") satisfies my need for things to look and act natively.

[CyberSearch]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/7931") was just one of those things I always wanted. :)
