Strange indeed! Do you use your optical drives a lot, more than others ?
My current Sony DVD writer is over 2.5 yrs old and still going fine.

As for which models to get, go for the latest/fastest and look at some reviews/benchmarks. The Plextor PX-800A & PX-810SA are their current top of the line dvd burners and cater for PATA & SATA so you have a choice as to the interface. They also have a Blu-Ray burner but I don't know about that.

For LiteOn the latest burners are the LH-20A1H/L/P models. Dunno the differences between them.

Now you just need to figure out if you want to pay about $30 more for the Plextor over the LiteOn.
