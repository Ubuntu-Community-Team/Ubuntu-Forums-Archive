### Post by Denis on 2006-08-04
Hi

The game *Frets On Fire* was on [http://happypenguin.org](http://happypenguin.org) two days ago. I thought maybe you'd like it too. The game lets your play the guitar with your keyboard :) You use F1-F4 and enter to play. I've included a screenshot. It's not very hard, you'll get away with it rather quickly. 

There are only three songs because the game is still new, but you can make your own songs, so I expect more songs to come soon. Make sure you also play the tutorial, it's funny...

This is the homepage of the game: [http://louhi.kempele.fi/~skyostil/uv/fretsonfire/](http://louhi.kempele.fi/~skyostil/uv/fretsonfire/)
You can download the Linux binaries [here]("http://louhi.kempele.fi/~skyostil/uv/fretsonfire/dist/FretsOnFire-1.0.184-linux.tar.gz").
It's a tar.gz archive that you just have to extract to any place. Run ./FretsOnFire to play. 
You may need some more libraries, I had to install libsdl-mixer1.2 and libsdl-ttf2.0-0 (with apt-get). 

The game is licenced under GPL. 

So take your keyboard and have fun :D
