### Post by bonzodog on 2007-08-17
Congratulations, man.
I am a father of 3 children, and it was the best thing that ever happened to me. It's well worth it.
Yes, children are *expensive* creatures, as you will no doubt discover in the next few months -- just try buying a whole bedroom suite for a babies room cheaply!

When baby is born, be prepared for sleepless nights, and make sure to let your boss know-- you are almost certainly entitled to  parental leave of some sort.
 I would personally try to get a fortnight off when the baby is born, as the first few weeks are the most sleepless as baby wakes to feed at 2 in the morning, and she gives you the bottle and asks you to feed baby, and change him/her.
Be prepared for smelly nappies, and endless bouts of buying baby clothes, as babies grow fast -- you might be as well to find friends or relatives with babygros they aren't using and use them for the few weeks that they will fit the baby. Yes, they will only fit for about 6 weeks, before baby grows out of them.

Otherwise, good luck, and don't be scared to come in here and ask for advice on children, as there are a fair few parents in here, with plenty of experience.
