### Post by skoal on 2008-09-06
> **vinboy said:**
> What is the difference between drum unit and the toner? 
The drum rotates the paper, and the toner is an ink cartridge. I had a laser for 10 years; replaced toner twice, and the drum once when it developed a scar. The drum cost me $40 and the toners about $30 or so. Pretty economical in comparison to inkjets at the time, which had a higher maintenance cost than laser.

Brother, I'd go with the Brother. I've seen them mature over the years from a retrofitted toaster with a hand crank to a viable Xerox replacement.
