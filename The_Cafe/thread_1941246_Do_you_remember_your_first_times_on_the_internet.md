### Post by TBABill on 2012-03-15
This question sure brought back some memories. ### Post by Brian0312 on 2012-03-23
Wow this has sure brought back some memories.
 
I think my first modem adventure was in the late 80's or early 90's. I can tell you I was on a 286 with 1Mb of extended ram and a 9600 baud modem.  My parents had bought a used computer so it was old even by the standards of the day. I was not yet a teenager and I'd spent weeks and weeks teaching myself DOS 4.2 from a DOS for Dummies book. Then I spent more time figuring out how to dial into the local Radio Stations BBS based on some books I got from the library.
 
The day all my learning came together, it was way past my bed time and I was not in any way prepared for the fact that the damn modem was going to play a full volume connection tune when I was successful. Later I learned the commands to make it connect silently, but not before the lecture.
 
I also remember how many braging rights my 33.6 external modem and NetCom subscription gave me back in the day. But that was about 6 years later.
