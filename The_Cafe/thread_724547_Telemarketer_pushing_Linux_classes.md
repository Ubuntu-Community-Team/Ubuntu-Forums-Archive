### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-03-14
You may want to talk to the cops about it, actually. The way it comes across, it sounds like a scam, and that is still a crime. While learning about people online is not illegal, using it to claim that they owe you money is illegal. Although picking on Linux users to try to scam is a bit like poking a beehive. It's not like we are all that dumb usually, and even if one of use did, our sense of community would propel us to share it at a faster rate than is normal for scam announcements.

Talk to the cops after you actually talk to one of the calls. I have turned in two scams that have been running in the Classified around here. They used a money laundering scheme basically.

It's up to you, since it's you they're calling.
