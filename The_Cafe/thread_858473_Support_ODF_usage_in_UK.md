### Post by jomiolto on 2008-07-13
I wonder what the planned timeline for this is, since the OOXML specs are not even finished yet, and there will not be a single program that outputs the ISO standard OOXML for probably quite some time. I haven't followed the news about this, but didn't Microsoft announce that even they would be adding ODF support in MS Office before updating it to use the ISO version of OOXML?

And, by the way, that petition isn't exactly correct when it says the OOXML is not supported outside of MS Office -- support for the format is being slowly added to many of the Open Source office applications. It is going to take time, though, and most likely there will never be a 100% support, due to the size of the specs.
