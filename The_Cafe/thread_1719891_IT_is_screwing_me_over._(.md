### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-04-02
Thing is -

How much trouble would you get in if you cracked it? I mean, erased the admin password, create a backdoor account etc. Because if you can boot backtrack (CLI mode will do), you can crack the password using chntpw. Works every time, I've used it on 2000, XP, Vista and 7. Even on my dad's super locked down work laptop.

Or, stick the HDD in a SATA to USB enclosure and pull your data off it. I do all my important work in my dropbox sync folder, just in case I have a hard drive crash or my laptop is stolen (Who would steal a laptop I found in a dumpster I don't know, but it happens). That way, I just boot another PC and it syncs, and everything is there.
