### Post by pete on 2005-11-28
Are you looking for something that will let the other 10 users see what's happening on your desktop, or something that will let you see what's going on the other users' desktops?

If it's the second, then Remote Desktop is the way to go (yes, it is VNC-based).  However, I *think* it will only allow one connection at a time.  In other words, if you wanted to demo something on your desktop and have more than one remote user at a time see what you're doing, RD won't work for you.

FreeNX won't fit the bill either, as it sets up a remote X session.

Unless, of course, I've totally misunderstood what you're asking...  :D
