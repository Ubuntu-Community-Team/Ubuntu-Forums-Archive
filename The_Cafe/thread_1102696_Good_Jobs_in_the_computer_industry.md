### Post by Icehuck on 2009-03-21
Your co-workers will complain all day regardless of what field you work in. People will always have problems with the place they work or with work in general. Office politics is usually the main cause of most of the grief. Keep yourself out of it and you usually end up less stressed.

I will say that IT work in general has a high burn out rate when you work for larger companies. The bigger the company the more overtime you will put in. So I will say if you are already weary about a career in this field it might not be the place for you.
