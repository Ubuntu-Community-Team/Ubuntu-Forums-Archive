From man page:
[quote="man"]
NAME
       Ekiga - SIP and H.323 Voice over IP and Videoconferencing for UN*X

SYNOPSIS
       ekiga [-d level] [-c URL]

DESCRIPTION
       Ekiga  is  a  SIP  and  H.323 VoIP, IP Telephony and Video Conferencing
       application which complies to the SIP and H.323 protocols. It can  con&#8208;
       nect to a variety of other SIP and H323 applications including specific
       hardware. Ekiga can work with or without a webcam, and is able to  cre&#8208;
       ate  pure  audio  communications or traditionnal audio+video communica&#8208;
       tions. Ekiga was fomerly known as GnomeMeeting. Ekiga has been designed
       for  the  GNOME  desktop and therefore uses gconfd-2(1) for storing its
       userdata. It offers to configure almost every option  from  within  the
       GUI. Command-line options include -d to turn on debugging during calls.
       -c to call another URL.
[/quote]
