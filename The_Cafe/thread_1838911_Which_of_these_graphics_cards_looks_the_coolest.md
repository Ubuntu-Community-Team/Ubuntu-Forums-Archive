### Post by IWantFroyo on 2011-09-04
How you find the best one:
1) Make sure all of them will fit in your computer.
2) Do some research on the specifications. Make sure they'll work on Linux.
3) Plug the best cards in and try them for yourself (make sure the computer is unplugged while putting the card in).
4) You'll likely have one or two cards left. Then, it'll be easy to decide.

Going by looks alone with graphics cards is a very bad idea.
