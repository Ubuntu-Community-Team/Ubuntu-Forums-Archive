### Post by finferflu on 2006-12-26
Hi everybody!

It's a couple of months now that I've been collaborating (translating) with the [Jamendo]("http://www.jamendo.com") project, and it has been improving on and on, and it looks everytime more awesome.

I just want to encourage the Ubuntu community to give a look and spread free culture around the globe. I think the Free culture brings a very positive impact to our societies, it's very healthy, so I always feel like supporting such projects. 

Jamendo is a project for Free music, released under the Creative Commons, which can be freely and legally downloaded and distributed. For me it's like putting the capital A in Art, to make it priceless again. The scope of the project is not entirely based on that, but that is what is relevant for me. I also feel it's a very important step towards freedom, in the era of DRM. 
It is all just amazing, translation are provided by the community and various pieces of software are released under the GPL. Moreover the website source code will be soon released under the GPL as well.
I just think this is a step forward, and felt like sharing it with you, and encourage you to contribute, listening to the Jamendo music is already a contribution!

Thanks for your time :)
