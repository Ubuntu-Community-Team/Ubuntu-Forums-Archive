### Post by nemik on 2006-03-26
Hey guys,

It was my spring break from university this past week and I made a little site to keep my busy: [http://www.letmeparty.com](http://www.letmeparty.com).

When you sign up there, you can add your Livejournal, Blogger, Xanga, or Wordpress blogs and then each SMS message you send to that phone number at the top will be posted to your blog. :)
It recognizes you from your mobile phone number. :)

Best of all, it was all done with Ubuntu linux; the development and even the server powering the phone number!

Try it out and let me know what you think!

Thanks,
Nemik
