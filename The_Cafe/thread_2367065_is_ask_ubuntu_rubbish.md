### Post by yoshii on 2017-07-29
I find most "Ask..." types of websites are OK for quick websearches for answers related to a problem, yet they typically suffer from problems:  

1) locked down (updated answers are just not available and never will be, because they were locked down too early)
2) too many derivative conversations and spin-off answers that don't really solve the problem/question
3) they dominate the results of internet search engines often

On the plus side, however, they can sometimes give somebody an idea of which "keywords" to use, (which technical terms are most appropriate), and then they can come here to UbuntuForums and get a more solid answer.  I find that true forums are much more powerful than the "Ask..." kiosks, which are kind of a recent gimmick/trend.
