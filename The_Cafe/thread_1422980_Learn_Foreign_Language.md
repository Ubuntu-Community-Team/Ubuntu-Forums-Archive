### Post by lisati on 2010-03-06
If you can, spend time around native speakers of the language you wish to learn. It can help with pronunciation and getting a feel for the way they think. It also provides opportunities for practice that you can't get from a book or multimedia presentation.

(Spoken from experience: I spend time with speakers of Maori and Samoan and have picked up some of what I know from them)
