### Post by thewump on 2007-11-19
I'm a big XFCE fan for the lightness, but switched to Gnome after upgrading to 7.10.   Getting tired of Gnome-slow though I switched back and missed two things, big time:

1) gnome-panel drag and drop to awn
2) gnome keyboard mapping really great ( for Microsoft Ergo 4000 keyboard )

I was very surprised to find that all I have to do is login to xfce then run a script:

killall xfce-mcs-manager
gnome-settings-daemon &
gnome-panel &

and I have XFCE speed and gnome panel and settings goodness.

LIKE IT.. Highly recommended. 

Keith
