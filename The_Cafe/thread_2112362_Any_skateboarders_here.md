### Post by BrokenKingpin on 2013-02-05
I skateboarded for a good 10 years when I was younger. I eventually stopped in early collage after wrecking my knee trying to kickflip a 12 stair. No major lasting damage, but after a long history of knee and ankle injuries (and a number of broken bones), I decided to stop. I also wanted to focus on school (also had a girlfriend, which did not help lol).

I miss it a great deal, and still go out in my drive way and mess around a bit. I can still do a lot of the flip tricks I did back in the day, which I am surprised as I have not really skated in 6 years.

I actually came across a VHS tape of me an a buddy skating (I was probably 15). It is actually quite well done for the time. I am going to try and transfer it to my PC and upload it to youtube... so I might post a link back here in a few days.

EDIT: My online name "Brokenkingpin" actually came from my skateboarding days. I had a habit of breaking the kingpin on the trucks of my skateboards, so it sort of stuck.
