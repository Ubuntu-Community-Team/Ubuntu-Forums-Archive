### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-06-01
I don't have any spam problems with Linkedin and it is a good networking tool. I had to join as part of a job networking program at collage.

The group I belong to shares information about job/career opportunities often before they appear anywhere else which is of great value. 

Having contacts outside of your area of interest is good thing also because they may know people that are willing to help in your own career quest . I don't use it as a social medium though.
