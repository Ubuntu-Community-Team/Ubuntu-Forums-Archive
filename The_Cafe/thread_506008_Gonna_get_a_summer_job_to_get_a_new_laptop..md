### Post by prizrak on 2007-07-22
Don't bother with a Blu-Ray burner, it's useless. If you just want a backup solution you can get an external HDD for much less money, Wait for one of the formats to win first then you can grab a burner for them. Also don't go for a 10,000 RPM HDD, it won't help your speed much and you couldn't even breathe at the comp for fear of a head crash (I've known people with those). Grab two identical 3.0Gbps HDDs and RAID them, that will give you great speed boost. DO NOT put a Pentium D into a gaming comp, Core 2 Duo or QUAD will work much better. You might also get two GeForce cards to SLI them or you can get an expensive and powerful GeForce and then just add another one on some time down the line when you want to increase speed.
