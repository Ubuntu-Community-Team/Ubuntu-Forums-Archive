### Post by superyounan1 on 2007-09-02
its from gtk or metacity themes. Many themes come with panel images. Also you could create your own glossy or 3d looking image and apply it yourself by right clicking on the panel > properties > background > background image.

for themes, go to [http://gnome-look.org/]("http://gnome-look.org/")

download a theme, then apply it, system > preferences > Theme

or in a console (or alt+f2) type
```
gnome-theme-manager
```
