### Post by yetiman64 on 2012-02-17
It looks a bit like a desktop recorder that has a defined area to record just for the 2 windows mainly. Some shots of what appear to be a browser seem cropped at the sides in a few scenes. 

Something like gtk-recordmydesktop can be set up to do this sort of capture including the sound. 

Don't know how they set up the conferencing but it appeared the interviewee was in a totem window and the 2 interviewers looked like they were in a vlc or similar style window. Gnome2 being in use was mentioned towards the end of the show so it may well be 10.04 or similar.
