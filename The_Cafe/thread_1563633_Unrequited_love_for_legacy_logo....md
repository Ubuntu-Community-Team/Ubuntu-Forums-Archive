### Post by candtalan on 2010-08-29
I can't help it. I have tried not to. I have embraced change continuously. I am the most energetic of supporters and advocates for Ubuntu and its aims.

However, I still really like the old logo, the three coloured Circle of Friends. No, more than that, I love it. 

When I first saw it a long time ago, I thought it was an inspired design. It has joy, colour, and even in 2D, it has a 'depth'.

I have some wearables and some other merchandise with the old logo and I know I will treasure these.

I realised I had missed out on Ubuntu mugs. 

The old logo mugs presumably were withdrawn from the Shop promptly. But I managed to find some elsewhere, and have bought a couple.

I have confidence that there are excellent benefits for the Ubuntu rebranding, however, I really hope that the legacy logo can survive in some way.
