### Post by bonzodog on 2006-08-11
The archival team take how-to's from the forums and post them to the Ubuntu Document Storage Facility (UDSF).

The Green team offer help and support in the Absolute beginners section

The Kubuntu team are there to lend support for Kubuntu.

The Desktop team help in the Desktop sub forum

The Hardware team (currently one member!) help with hardware issues. 

Most forum team members are nothing more than voluntary tech support, people who might know that little bit more than others.

The other teams, part of the offical wider community:

Doc Team - maintain [http://help.ubuntu.com](http://help.ubuntu.com)

MOTU - Build Packages for the Universe Repositories

Development - Paid full time positions to actually develop Ubuntu.

Loco Teams work locally in your country to provide a local community structure for you.

Marketing come up with ideas to 'sell' ubuntu to the wider world.
