### Post by darkapec on 2012-08-18
The second adapter will work great, but you will need 3 of them to split the 2 channels of audio and the video.  

You can find the second adapter on ebay etc for $.99 or less (and free shipping), there very common and very cheap. The link below is for 3 of them including shipping $3.88 and its shipped from california.

[http://www.ebay.com/itm/3-Pack-2-RCA-Female-1-RCA-Female-AV-Audio-Video-Splitter-Adapter-AR2-2R2-3PK-/270955505377?pt=US_Audio_Cables_Adapters&hash=item3f1634e6e1](http://www.ebay.com/itm/3-Pack-2-RCA-Female-1-RCA-Female-AV-Audio-Video-Splitter-Adapter-AR2-2R2-3PK-/270955505377?pt=US_Audio_Cables_Adapters&hash=item3f1634e6e1)

On second thought if you don't really care about the looks you can manually split the RCA cable by soldering a little wire from the middle pin of one rca to the middle pins of the other 2 rca and then do the same with the outer shields. You can actually just get away with wrapping some wire with the ends stripped around rca.

I do not know if you are a DIY type of person or if u need this done ASAP then the picture attached will provide you a quick way to do so. I have had to do similar things in the past. 

[IMG]http://darkapec.net/images/rca1.JPG[/IMG]

It is definitely not pretty but it will work.
