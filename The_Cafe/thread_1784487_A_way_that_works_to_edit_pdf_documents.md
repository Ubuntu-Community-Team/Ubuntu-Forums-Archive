### Post by ClientAlive on 2011-06-17
I went through hell and back trying to find something that could do just two things I needed to a pdf document.

(1) Insert and image from a file (ie: your signature)
(2) Add text

What I found what that the pdf import extension allowed me to do this. It makes it so you can open a pdf in open office draw and edit it.

[http://wiki.services.openoffice.org/wiki/Pdf_Import_Extension](http://wiki.services.openoffice.org/wiki/Pdf_Import_Extension)  -"This page was last modified on 3 July 2009, at 20:52"
