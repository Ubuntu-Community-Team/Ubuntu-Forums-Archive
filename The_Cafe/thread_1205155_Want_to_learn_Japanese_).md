### Post by reyfer on 2009-07-05
Spoken japanese is actually relatively easy to learn, the difficulty comes with the written language, which is composed by 3 alphabets, Kanji (complex characters derived directly from chinese), Hiragana (syllabic characters used for japanese words) and Katakana (syllabic characters used for words that come from other languages). Try this sites  [http://www.sf.airnet.ne.jp/~ts/japanese/cover.html](http://www.sf.airnet.ne.jp/~ts/japanese/cover.html)

[http://www.guidetojapanese.org/index.html](http://www.guidetojapanese.org/index.html)
