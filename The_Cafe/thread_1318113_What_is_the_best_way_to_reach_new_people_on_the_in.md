### Post by DeadSuperHero on 2009-11-07
If you're not against doing music for free and letting people re-interpret and re-use your music, you could always try Jamendo (Jamendo.com)

But a good way to reach people is to find some venues. Add some local bands on Myspace, find out where they're playing near you next. Get to know the owners of said venue, help out a bit.

With bands, you usually want to start small.

A tip of advice though: it's better to build up grassroots-style support and true fans than to do some gimmicky music video on YouTube and become a one-hit wonder. At best, you get remembered for that one song and everybody tries to cover it. At worst, you get labeled "Poser"

Best of luck to you guys.
