 If you are anything like me, you started off the day not knowing what or how to deal with a music folder that has had little or no thought with regards directory structure, maybe its a single folder housing hundreds of different songs and now is the time to reorganise things. 

Today, I discovered something new in... amarok. and now it would please me to introduce you to one features that I had overlooked for far too long, Media organisation.

**Before you begin**
 
  Please be forewarned that this requires that the music files you wish to organise be correctly identified with id tags and [SIZE="3"]should at least display the artist and album name[/SIZE] meta information.

**Step 1. Load an Album as a playlist** 

eg: "Back to Bedlam" by James Blunt

[[IMG]http://img85.imageshack.us/img85/7966/amarokplaylistfilterde1.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img85.imageshack.us/my.php?image=amarokplaylistfilterde1.jpg)

**Step 2. Select the playlist entries (Ctrl+A)**

[[IMG]http://img86.imageshack.us/img86/3688/amarokplaylistselectallxt5.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img86.imageshack.us/my.php?image=amarokplaylistselectallxt5.jpg)

**Step 3. Right-click on the selection, goto "Manage Files" - and click "Organise N Files...**

[[IMG]http://img204.imageshack.us/img204/2514/amarokplaylistcontextmelz8.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img204.imageshack.us/my.php?image=amarokplaylistcontextmelz8.jpg)

**Step 4. Setup how files should be organised**

[[IMG]http://img465.imageshack.us/img465/6/amarokplaylistorganisefcw4.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img465.imageshack.us/my.php?image=amarokplaylistorganisefcw4.jpg)

 It's really a wonder why I hadn't seen this dialog earlier.

**Step 5. Review the 'Destination Preview'**

 Its probably a good idea to have a quick glance at the destination preview. 

[[IMG]http://img141.imageshack.us/img141/7209/amarokplaylistorganisefbo1.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img141.imageshack.us/my.php?image=amarokplaylistorganisefbo1.jpg)

I enabled the details button [this is not required], simply tick the boxes shown that apply. Press Ok when you are certain you want to make the changes.

**Step 6. Troubleshooting Errors**

amarok will now restructure the selected media according to the rules you set above. should anything go wrong, a small notification popup will appear in the lower left of amaroks mainwindow.

 It should now be easy enough to re-organise your music files into a manageable and consistent directory structure.  I found it best to process one single entire album at a time, although if the tags are setup correctly there is no reason why you couldn't reorganise your entire library in one hit.

 And to think I only discovered this today.
