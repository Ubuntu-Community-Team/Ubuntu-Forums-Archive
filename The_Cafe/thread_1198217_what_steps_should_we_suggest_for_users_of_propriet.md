### Post by brallan on 2009-06-27
I think most people are willing to see the wisdom in an argument for free software, but the practical aspects may be preventing them from joining us, particularly as it seems like a great leap for many. If we get most people to start making small compromises that help in this movement, we all stand to gain.

How can we make this road clearer, by illuminating a series of smaller steps, such as using firefox instead of IE or Safari? Or taking part in supporting hardware manufacturers which release their drivers under the GPL? or using only nonproprietary formats in their publications, or at least when it's not work-related?

ideas?
