Actually, you dream every night, unless you've had a brain injury of some sort it's possible you wouldn't. But more than likely you have dreams every evening, and it's simply that you don't remember them. The most likely way to remember a dream is by waking up during your REM cycle - which is the period of sleeping when your dreams are most active/strange/vivid.
