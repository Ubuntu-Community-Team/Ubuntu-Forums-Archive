### Post by Dr. C on 2012-07-21
The process is no different from eveyone else. They go to a domain registrar who then obtains the domains from the respective registries. What is different is that certain domain registrars have specialized on this clientele by offering domain registration as part of a very comprehensive package of brand protection involving monitoring, registering domains accross multiple registries, ensuring registrations are up to date, etc. A popular provider in this market is [https://www.markmonitor.com/]("https://www.markmonitor.com/").

To find out who is the registrar for a particular domain big or small say: example.com. Start a terminal and enter at the prompt:

```
whois example.com
```
