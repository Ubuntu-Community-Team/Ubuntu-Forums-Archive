### Post by Shibblet on 2011-09-20
Everybody has their own favorites when it comes to music, but have you ever heard lyrics that just spoke to you?  Even if it's not from your type of music?

I was at my dad's house (he's an old hippie), and he was playing Janis Joplin's Pearl album.  I've heard the songs before, of course, but never really "listened" to the lyrics.

There are some lines such as "Freedom is just another word for 'nothing left to lose.'" and "I'd trade all of my tomorrows for one single yesterday."  These are pure poetry.

So, I want to start a thread where people can post their favorite lines from any song.  Make sure to post the song, and group (or songwriter).
