When I was a wee lad Kirk's Star Trek was a popular show. I imagined that I would grow up and man would have found his way to the stars; not the moon, but beyond.
 
Here I sit today listening to Floyd's Learning to Fly from A Momentary Lapse of Reason and the reflections of the past shine a light on just how far man has yet to go. I now know, painfully, that man will not reach the stars before I die. I am left wondering how many of my fellows have looked back to see forward and found the progress lacking.

If humanity were able to tap into the vast amount of raw material and energy available even within our own solar system, the scarcity of commodity would vanish.

[LIST]
Curb the concept of Intellectual Property, and scale back the terms of copyright and patent in order to revitalize the advancement of science and the useful arts.
[*]Discontinue use of fossil fuel.
[*]Convert the current power grid over to solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal forms of energy.
[*]Encourage and subsidize personal energy generation through any practical combination of the above, by region and availability.
[*]Decentralize the distribution of energy as outlined the above and discard the current forms of distribution of energy.
