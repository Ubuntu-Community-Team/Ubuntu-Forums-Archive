### Post by mr.propre on 2008-02-14
I only know one router who can do it (for a reasonable price).
A router with [dd-wrt firmware](http://www.dd-wrt.com/wiki/index.php?title=NoCatSplash). @ home i use a Linksys WRT54GL router with dd-wrt on. Total cost &#8364;50,00.

But I don't use the hotspot function. You can also look into [fon](http://www.fon.com/en/).

Another way (i think) is placing a pc between the router and the modem. (Router - PC - modem). The PC need to run all the time, so you might thinking about buying a pico-ATX PC, what will cost about &#8364;200 - &#8364;300.
