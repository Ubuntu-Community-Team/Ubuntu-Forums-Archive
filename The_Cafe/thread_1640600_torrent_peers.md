### Post by 3Miro on 2010-12-08
The number of peers shown by the client is not the total number of people downloading. I am more concerned with speed than number of peers. If you get 8 fast peers on the one machine, then the client has no need to look for more peers than that. 8 fast may be the in some sense equal to 14 slow.
