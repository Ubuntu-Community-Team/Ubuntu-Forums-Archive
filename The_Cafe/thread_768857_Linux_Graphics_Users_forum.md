### Post by Dadster on 2008-04-26
Hi all, I am Dadster co-founder of Linux Graphics Users forum. I'm here to extend an invitation to all Ubuntu users. Please stop in and check us out.

Our forum favors no one Linux operating system. We felt there was a need for a central forum that supports and encourages use of free Linux open source graphics art software. This forum benefits all Linux users from artists sharing knowledge and helping to guide newcomers that want to learn some of the tricks and shortcuts in graphics art to our members volunteering to be part of other artwork teams.

We are a very young forum as far as age but not in talent. Some of Linux best artists are there ready to greet and help the forum succeed. Some of our artists are helping develop themes for Linux distros besides lending a hand at LGU.

Please feel free to visit LGU. If you like what you see, become a member. You don't need to be an artist to join. Maybe just have a desire to learn but don't know where to start.


When you stop in, say "Hi"

Dadster
