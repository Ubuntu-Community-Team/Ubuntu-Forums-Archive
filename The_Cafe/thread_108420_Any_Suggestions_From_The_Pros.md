### Post by xmastree on 2005-12-26
I presume you mean advice about linux for the inexperienced?
Here are a couple of commands you can't live without.

1) **Apropos**
Want to know the command for something, copy for example.
Type **apropos copy** in a terminal and it will list what's appropriate.

2) **man**
Manual pages. A little difficult to read sometimes, but they tell you about a command and all its options.
Type **man cp** in a terminal and you'll see how to copy files.

As for advice about these forums, search first before asking about a problem. Whatever solution you seek may already be here.

And above all, have fun. :-)
