Martín Varsavsky announced on his blog that his company is now migrating to Ubuntu, and they will no longer buy any Windows licenses. One of the main reasons is that administrating an Ubuntu machine is less of a hassle than doing the same with a Windows box: no AV, defragging, spyware, etc.
