### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-09-17
> **sudoer541 said:**
> does meebo have mic and cam support? 
how?

Try it and you'll see :-)

It uses Tokbox, which is a Flash-based a/v chat service.

If you're doing the chatting from within Meebo, then the Tokbox screen appears inside your Meebo window. If you're not on Meebo, then you get sent a URL to click on, which takes you to Tokbox and puts you into the a/v chat.

You just need Flash Player and a supported webcam.
