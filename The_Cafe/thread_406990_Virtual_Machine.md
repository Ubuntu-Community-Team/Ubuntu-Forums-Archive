### Post by heimo on 2007-04-11
I'm pretty sure you can do that with Xen. Yesterday I installed Xen and Edgy on it, it's now on a file, but it could as well be on a partition. This may not be quite as easy to setup as vmware, and even though graphical interfaces do exists (I think xenman is one), I haven't used them. Xen - itself is extremelly nice. You can even live-migrate virtual machine from one host to another.
