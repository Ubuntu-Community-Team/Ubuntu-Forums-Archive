### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-04-30
I've ran across some interesting Linux pages that aren't ubuntu.

What you should do is: goto your Stumbleupon tool bar and select your drop down menu, then select the ALL drop down menu, and then select the Computers section and then click on the Linux/Unix selection. Then when you Stumble, the only choices are Linux/Unix and you will get a more diverse selection of pages.

There are also groups to join that have pages linked and discussions.
