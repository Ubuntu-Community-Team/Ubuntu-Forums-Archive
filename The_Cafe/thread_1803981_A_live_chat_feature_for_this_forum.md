### Post by forrestcupp on 2011-07-14
As others have said, that's what IRC and personal messaging are for. I know it's not the same as being able to chat with people you are already having a discussion with.

I think a big problem with having live chat on here is that people would be tempted to try to get their help quicker that way, which would negate one of the primary purposes of this forum. When people post questions and get solutions on here, it creates a vast searchable knowledge base that people can go to to find answers. If everyone started resorting to live chat, we would lose a lot of that.
