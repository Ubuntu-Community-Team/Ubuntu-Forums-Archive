The largest reason for people dual-booting is probably because of games. Basically, it almost sounds like you want to switch to Linux to play your games better, that's a pretty crazy idea. Steam will run on Ubuntu through Wine, but it won't work quite as well as Windows (Steam works quite well, but it's pretty sluggish compared to running it on Windows). If all you're interested in doing is playing games with your PC, I wouldn't suggest switching. If there's quite a bit of other stuff you use your PC for, you might as well give it a try, but keep Windows installed for the games.
