### Post by Groucho Marxist on 2010-08-06
> **balaknair said:**
> I second that. Devede is probably the best app on Ubuntu for what you described.

A quick note for the OP; make sure that under "File Properties" prior to encoding that you click "Dual Pass Encoding." I made a DVD-RW test copy of a Stephen Fry special on Gutenberg's press and the video would occasionally skip due to how quickly the video was encoded. However, upon enabling "Dual Pass Encoding," the video was smoother and never skipped :)
