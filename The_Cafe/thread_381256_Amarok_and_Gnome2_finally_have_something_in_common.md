First of all, I didn't know where to post this, so I took the most appropriate place, in my opinion.

Secondly, I am proud to announce the first release of the Amarok Gnome2 Applet (AGA). The applet sits on the Gnome2 panel and it controls Amarok (amarok.kde.com) through a nice "toolbar" or as it called in Gnome2 - Applet. The first release of the applet contains 4 buttons which give a basic control on Amarok.

In future releases, AGA will contain a more advanced control on Amarok plus a graphic installer.

So, if you use Amarok and you want a more convenient control on Amarok, give AGA a try.

The link: [http://amarokapplet.sourceforge.net/]("http://amarokapplet.sourceforge.net/")
