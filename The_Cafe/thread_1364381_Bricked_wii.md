### Post by RATM_Owns on 2009-12-26
> **KiwiNZ said:**
> The 4.2 update you applied has corrupted the boot device. There appears to be no solution to a Korean unit converted to US.
Nah, it's not the boot2 update. It's the new system menu.

It happens when a Korean region-changed Wii (changed FROM Korean, not TO Korean) does the 4.2 update. It gives the 003 error.

See the WiiBrew page: [http://wiibrew.org/wiki/Error_003](http://wiibrew.org/wiki/Error_003)

If you have an Infectus, you can fix it. BootMii won't work because it was overwritten in the 4.2 update. ([http://wiibrew.org/wiki/System_Menu_4.2#Changes](http://wiibrew.org/wiki/System_Menu_4.2#Changes))
