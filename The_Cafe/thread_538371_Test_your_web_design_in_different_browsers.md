### Post by ThinkBuntu on 2007-08-29
I use BrowserShotsw for those times when I simply don't have IE6 or 7 on hand to test. Generally, you'll need to renew your request once to get it through for the IEs. The other browsers are fairly consisten across platforms, so no big deal.

Be warned -- don't pay too much attention to the good or bad results you see in IE6 if it's tested using Ubuntu (which some of the browser shot servers do). I have tested IEs4Linux against a real copy of IE6, and the two do not match up right! Having IEs4Linux is better than nothing, but as a web designer, it can't be trusted for cross-browser compatibiltity.

Besides that, it's a nice site for those cases when you need to emergency-test a page.
