### Post by prshah on 2008-03-26
> **kpkeerthi said:**
> If you are importing something that is free (should I say 'has no money value') do you have to pay import duty?

Not a question of import duty; the official claims that he does not have a LICENSE to import. In India, businesses should:

1) Register for sales tax
2) Register for income tax
3) Open a suitable multi-currency bank account if reqd
4) Register as an importer/exporter with the above information
5) Deal with various statutory forms for every transaction

Then they get an import license. On the face of it, it seems reasonable; every country would have similar procedures.

In reality, people have to pay money every step of the way or are pushed from pillar to post trying to meet "requirements". A typical application for an import license should take less than a week but will take between 30-90 days.

For a PERSONAL import, you don't need a license provided you are not importing anything prohibited; and you can't import anything prohibited in any case, license or not; at least officially.
