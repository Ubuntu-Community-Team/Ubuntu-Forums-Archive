I come across things while wandering around in Ubuntu that make me wonder why life can't be that simple.  Has anyone else experienced this?

For example,

If like were more like Linux:

Getting a new job would be a piece of cake.  Interviewer looks over your resume and says "You know... you've got a strong background in sales, but you need some more managerial experience for this position."

You reply "Hang on a sec" and you execute sudo apt-get install -f
You instantly resolve your dependencies and get the job.
