### Post by matthew on 2005-09-22
The French organization "Reporters sans frontieres" or "Reporters Without Borders" just published a pdf online entitled "Handbook for bloggers and cyber-dissidents" outlining how to blog anonymously and it is very interesting. About a 1.6 Mb download and available in English, French, Arabic, and Farsi. Much of what is discussed in the book I have seen elsewhere, but if you want to get/give a brief introduction to privacy/security as well as the art of blogging it's a great little read.

[http://www.rsf.org/rubrique.php3?id_rubrique=542](http://www.rsf.org/rubrique.php3?id_rubrique=542)
