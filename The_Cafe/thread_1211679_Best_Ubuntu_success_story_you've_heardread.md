### Post by Gizenshya on 2009-07-13
FOSS?

There are some good stories somewhere on the Ubuntu Disc.  I forgot where... but they are copied to disc during install I belive.  I'll search for them.

ok, here they are: /home/username/Examples

look around.  the case studies most directly address your question, though.

One of my best friends is now an Ubuntu user.  He was not tech savvy by really any stretch of the word.

I knew he hated Windows as much as anyone else, but I didn't bother mentioning Ubuntu to him because I thought he would be turned off by having to learn it.  One day he was over and I was booted into Ubuntu.  I moved a window and he was like... wait... do that again, with the window!  It took me a second to realize that he was talking about wobbly windows, and then I gave him the a tour.  He stopped me just part of the way through showing him some of the programs, because he had already made his decision: he wanted ubuntu!  So, I went over and installed it through Wubi (after a backup, of course).  There was an issue with the monitor (it was an HP, and the monitor size and refresh rate wasn't auto-detected), but a quick trip to the forums here fixed that.

He took to Ubuntu better than I did!  I talked with him today and to this day he has never booted back into windows.  :)  He was never into gaming much on XP, but he really liked Warzone 2100.  Ubuntu just does everything for him that XP did, but better.

It was some time in February that he switched over.
