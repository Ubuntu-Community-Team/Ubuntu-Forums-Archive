### Post by Maheriano on 2010-01-13
> **DillByrne said:**
> i only like his first album infinite , rest lack flow and are just trying to offend rather than display skill! and knowledge. but to each his own

Infinite is okay, would you believe it sold less than 100 copies when it came out? I prefer his Slim Shady LP and I disagree with you about lacking flow. Even though I find his albums lose rap skill as they progress, it seems he's focusing more on the production and trying new things with each project. I don't particularly like what he's doing, it's okay, but I much prefer his first (second) album. Some of the things he comes up with on Slim Shady LP are just ridiculous, "...then I remembered this magic trick. Da da da da da....Go Go Gadget dick!" Hilarious.

I'm actually very impressed to see people enjoying the actual skilled artists instead of naming whatever comes on the radio. Here are some of mine, most of which have been mentioned:

**Immortal Technique** - Crazy lyricist, always gets my attention with his metaphors and great beats too.
**Atmospher**e - Saw this guy live last year and it was one of the best concerts I've ever been to. Small venue, live crowd, it was crazy. Very talented rapper and surprisingly a white guy from Cincinnati.
**Jay-Z** - Even though he creates radio songs you can't ignore his talent. He never writes anything down, he simply makes stuff up and performs it on stage the next day, it's nuts. And the only other musical artist to have more number 1 albums are The Beatles. He just passed Elvis and The Rolling Stones, that says a lot right there.
**2Pac** - He's been played out simply because he obviously doesn't have anything new coming out like all the other rappers but it doesn't take away from the skill the guy had when he was rapping fresh. He was insane to listen to on the microphone and after 14 years his music/beats are still amazing.

Some that aren't the best in any sense but definitely don't get enough credit:
**Da Brat** - Love listening to her rhyme, great voice, great lyrics, great beats.
**Vai** - I know zero French but this guy has wicked beats, especially the Street Life remix and flows very well. Whatever he's saying. It's awesome.
**Skee-Lo** - Yes, the guy who made I Wish. I have his album and I personally think every song is very good. It'll surprise you, have a listen.
**TQ** - Kind of an R&B singer but you could call him a rapper. He's kind of like Craig David but with better skill and more of a westside feel since (I believe) he's from Compton, California.
