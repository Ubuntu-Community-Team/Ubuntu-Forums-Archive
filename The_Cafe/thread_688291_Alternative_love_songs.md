Hi guys! I have decided to revive the honorable tradition of the mixtape for this year's valentine's day. So I bummed around the net, looking for a solution that would be playable on what we have in our apartment, yet still with the element of writing the tracklist and decorating it and whatnot.. And there it was: the modern mixtape

Now to fill it with delicious love songs! The thing is that I need some help kickstarting my brain and remember all those great songs, so I'd like to ask if you guys could help me think up some good alternative love songs - or maybe just some artists to get me in the right frame of mind. ### Post by Tristam Green on 2008-02-05
I love mixtapes.  I would make mix-cd's for friends at work years ago, while I was building up my music collection through *cough* less-than-legal means.

Here are my suggestions:

The Cure - nearly anything off "Disintegration".  Particularly, "Plain song" and "Love Song" (Love Song of course being a dead giveaway).  As well, "Just Like Heaven" is almost a necessity.
Tal Bachman - She's So High - great cute song made popular in the mid to late 1990s.
The Heights - How do you Talk to an Angel? - Theme song for Fox's short-lived "The Heights"
Collective Soul - Tremble for my Beloved, Needs  (both off "Dosage")
Lenny Kravitz - Let Love Rule
INXS - Never Tear Us Apart


I'll have more later as I peruse my music library.



And, if I may say, kudos for the addition of Placebo.  Damn fine band, damn great song.  I love their covers of The Smiths' "Bigmouth Strikes Again" and T.Rex's "20th Century Boy".

*edit* - see, I knew it.

Keane - Somewhere Only We Know
Maroon 5 - Sweetest Goodbye (easily the prettiest song they've got on the first album)
Muse - Falling Away With You


and if you're feeling like edgier love songs; Stabbing Westward - You Complete Me
