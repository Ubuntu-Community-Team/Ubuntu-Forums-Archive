### Post by igknighted on 2009-02-03

+1

The only real challenge about running (or cycling, or any other activity) outside in the cold is mental.  I regularly run and commute to work on my bike (by choice... I could drive) in blizzards and near 0 (Fahrenheit) weather, and once you get outside it really isn't any worse than if it is 60 (Fahrenheit) out.  All you need to do is dress properly.  No cotton, layering, and wind/water resistance if needed.  The hard part is getting over the mental block.

Plus, it's just exhilarating after you are done if you get outside when the weather is really bad.
