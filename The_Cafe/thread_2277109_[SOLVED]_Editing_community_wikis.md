### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-05-06
Here is how I would do it. And I have just tested the method.

Go to the wiki page and click Login to Edit. I was re-directed to the Community wiki home page. Open another tab and go again to the wiki page. Login and there at the top is the Edit button.

Another method is to open the wiki page and click Login to Edit and when re-directed to the Community wiki home page search for the wiki page and when it opens click Login to Edit and there are the top should be the Edit button.

A third method is to start at the Community wiki home page and click Login to Edit and then search for the desired wiki page. When it opens Click Login to Edit.

### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-05-07
This may or may not have something to do with it:

> [h=2]If you have a Ubuntu One account, but no Launchpad account[/h] 
[LIST=1]
[*]Go to [https://launchpad.net/](https://launchpad.net/) 


[*]Click "Log in / Register". 
[*]Insert your Ubuntu One credentials, and click "Log In". 
[*]If prompted, confirm the details you want to share and click "Yes, log me in". 
[*]This  should create a Launchpad account linked to your Ubuntu One account.  You should be returned to the Launchpad home page, and you should see  that you have now been assigned a launchpad username. 
[*]Return to the wiki and login by clicking the "Login to edit" link. 
[*]When you are successfully logged in, you will see an "Edit" button below the wiki page title.
[/LIST]


[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WikiGuide/Registration](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WikiGuide/Registration)
