Free FON wireless g routers.  
Order one, but dont want to use their firmware (ya ya, it is the right thing to do, share your wireless)

Wire it to a serial port and you're done. I actually think I have an old serial to JTAG pin type cable from an old old Pentium Genuine. Might be fun.
