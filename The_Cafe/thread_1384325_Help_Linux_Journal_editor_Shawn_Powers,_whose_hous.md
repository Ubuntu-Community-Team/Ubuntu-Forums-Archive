### Post by cdean on 2010-01-18
Hey folks,

[Linux Journal](http://www.linuxjournal.com) editor [Shawn Powers](http://www.brainofshawn.com) was Sunday a victim of fire at his house in Indian River, Michigan. 

His family is alright; they were out of the house for church in the morning. However, their pets and those pets in their care are gone. The house is still standing, but there's not word yet if it will be a total loss. It seems that most of their possessions are unrecoverable.

You may know Shawn from his LJ tech tips, a few of which have had some discussion on this forum.

If you can provide anything -- toys for his daughters, computer/gadgets for him, anything for his wife, or money for the family -- please help out.

There's a ChipIn going on: [http://helpshawnpowersfamily.chipin.com/help-shawn-powers-family](http://helpshawnpowersfamily.chipin.com/help-shawn-powers-family).

Linux Journal is forwarding stuff to him, as well: [http://www.linuxjournal.com/content/urgent-help-shawn-powers-family](http://www.linuxjournal.com/content/urgent-help-shawn-powers-family)

Let's show Shawn and his family that the Ubuntu community cares and appreciates his contributions to the furtherance of Linux and open source software. Ubuntu does mean "humanity to man," so let's come together in support of this great person.
