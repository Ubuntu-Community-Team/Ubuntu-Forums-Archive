### Post by eBopBob on 2005-07-11
I wouldn't touch IXWebHosting with a barge pole. Have you seen their prices? Don't be fooled by their cheap prices and large amounts of space and bandwidth... or be fooled if you like, but don't then come back crying.

Firstly I'm sceptical as to whether you would be able to use all of your bandwidth, secondly I wouldn't be surprised if they oversold and also overloaded their servers, and for that price I'm wondering what sort of technical support you'll get.

Web hosting, despite prices being at an all-time low, is still not at the level to be able to offer such prices, and still offer a respectable level of customer service. There are multiple companies offering such things, although if all of their clients started using all of their web space and bandwidth and started submitting at least two tickets per night, those companies would have major problems.

Although I've never used GoDaddy for web hosting, or domains, there are many people who do use them - so they cannot be all that bad; although having said that, I have heard of people who have had problems, but of course all companies have their problems.
