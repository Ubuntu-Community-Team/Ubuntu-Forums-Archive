### Post by DownTown22 on 2009-08-08
I've noticed a few websites feature a logo at the bottom of their web page stating they were made on a mac. ([[COLOR="Blue"]_www.auslander.net/Auslander/Welcome.html_[/COLOR]]("http://www.auslander.net/Auslander/Welcome.html"))

Now, I say we start adapting this and any webpage that you've made, using Ubuntu as your OS, should have a note at the bottom or somewhere saying it was made on Ubuntu.

Because really, it's little things like this that get Ubuntu out there.  People stumble upon your website and see a little logo at the bottom saying it was made on Ubuntu, and then say, "What's Ubuntu?".  Then they click on the link and it takes them to the world of Ubuntu.

Just a thought.
