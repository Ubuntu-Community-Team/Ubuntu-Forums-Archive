Videocanvas is a (featurewise) basic painting program, or or maybe as others would put it; toy.
Anyway, what makes it stand out is that the program records your actions and saves them in a chronologic list, so you at any time can "replay" the painting.

Other features:
Different brush radii (radius plural??)
Alpha channel
Save and load to own format.
Save bmp's
Unlimited undo
Brush radius randomization
Brush position randomization
