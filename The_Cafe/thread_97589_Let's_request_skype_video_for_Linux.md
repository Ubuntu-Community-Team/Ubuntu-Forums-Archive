### Post by arunsub on 2005-12-01
I read in the [news]("http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/10280416") today that Skype is offering video conference for Windows platform. I went to the online Skype support and sent an email to them requesting them to include the video conference facility in Linux version also. I would request all Ubuntu and Linux community members to do the same. You can go to [http://support.skype.com/?_a=tickets&_m=submit](http://support.skype.com/?_a=tickets&_m=submit) and submit your request. Select suggestions from the department. If we all do that, they might try to do what we suggested.
