### Post by Paqman on 2011-09-20
Amazon is good if you have a complicated site (eg: web app) and want it to be able to scale quickly and easily if your business grows. There's absolutely no need to use them if you're just hosting a forum. Any old VPS or your own machine at home will do fine for that.
