### Post by EngDrewman on 2008-10-29
I'm doing a project for my engineering product design class and would like your opinion on it. We all know how much computer geeks & coffee go together, so for my project my team is designing a "Smart Mug"- a USB coffee travel mug that includes 5GB of flash memory and has a thermoelectric heater/chiller to keep hot beverages hot and cold drinks cold. The USB cord and thermoelectric heatsink would be covered by a removable, watertight rubber base so that the mug can be washed in a dishwasher. One of my teammates suggested making the body out of carbon fiber to add geek appeal, but I think that would jack the price up too much.

So, the survey is, how much would you be willing to pay for such a mug? Any suggestions?
