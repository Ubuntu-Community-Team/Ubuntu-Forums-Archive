Police in Finland believe they have found the body of a gunman responsible for killing five people in a shooting rampage in the southern city of Espoo.

Police said 43-year-old Ibrahim Shkupolli shot dead three men and a woman with a 9mm pistol at a grocery shop inside the Sello shopping centre.

One of the shop's employees, reportedly Mr Shkupolli's ex-girlfriend, was later found dead at a flat in the city.

The incident is Finland's third major shooting in the past two years.
