### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2008-06-15
Angloscot,

I don't know if you are hosting the website on your own computer or using a hosting service. 

If you are using a hosting service, they may only support some of the languages used for BB's like ASP, CGI/Perl, Java/JSP, or PHP. They may not support some of the databases like MySQL, PostgreSQL, DMB, MS Access, MSSQL, etc. 

What a website hosting service supports will limit your options. You may want to ask them what they suggest you use.
