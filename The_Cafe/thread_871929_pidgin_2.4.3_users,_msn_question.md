### Post by boom2k1 on 2008-07-30
My pidgin didn't get cut off for the last 30 minutes after I tried using the msn-pecan implementation of the msn protocol.

It is pre-mature to say it works, but you may give it a try. I will also report that it doesn't if I still get disconnected periodically.

Perhaps you may want to try that as well.

Please refer to
[http://code.google.com/p/msn-pecan/wiki/HowToInstall](http://code.google.com/p/msn-pecan/wiki/HowToInstall)
for instruction.

At this moment this msn-pecan plugin actually does more than what Pidgin offers with its old protocol, but the sore point is that offline messaging is not yet supported. (I read from the author's log that he is really working on it and is almost done with it.)

You mention about the next major release of Pidgin might fix it (when they merge the msn changes to Pidgin 2.5.0), but who knows how long it will take.
