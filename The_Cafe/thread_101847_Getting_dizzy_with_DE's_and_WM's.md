### Post by xequence on 2005-12-10
What do you want in a DE? If you want minimal and fast, go with openbox. A little less minimal, go fluxbox or blackbox. Sort of minimal, but gnomeish, go with XFCE. Traditionalist, go with Windowmaker or something similar. Full featered super desktop environments are Gnome and KDE. They have everything.

There are much more though.
