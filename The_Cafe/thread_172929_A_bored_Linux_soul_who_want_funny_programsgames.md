### Post by Monster_user on 2006-05-09
Torcs is an Okay game. Linux really neads some good racing games. Something that compares to Nascar 2003, NFS:III Hot Pursuit, or NFS:Underground.

When I have some free time, **Frozen Bubble**, **LBreakout2**, **Neverball**,  and **PlanetPenguin Racer** (formerly "TuxRacer: Open Source Edition"), occupy my time.

They are a lot of fun. Various Quake based games are what I play otherwise. Nexuiz, Jedi Outcast, Elite Force, Half-Life/DoD 3/Counter-Strike 1.6.

**Frozen Bubble** = is a high quality game, where you shoot marbles at an upside down stack of marbles. Match the colors to make them fall. If the stack touches the bottom, you loose a life.

**LBreakout2** = Is a breakout style game. It is also similiar to Pong. The goal is the clear out the blocks in each level. The ball bounces off of every surface it touches, with a steady velocity. You move a bar across the bottom, and you have to prevent the ball from traveling off the bottom of the screen.

**Neverball** = Is why I am writing these strange explanations. **Neverball is a real nice looking 3D game.** The goal is to collect all the coins, by making the ball roll into them. You don't control the ball, but the platform that the coins, and the ball are on. You tilt the platform to roll the ball. This makes it challenging, and quite fun. Some levels make use of some nice reflections, which dazzle. Okay, so its not Doom3, it is still pretty though.

**PPRacer/TuxRacer** = is a Penguin sledding game. You are Tux the Linux peguin. Your goal is to race down the hill or mountain as fast as possible, AND get as many Herring/fish as you can. This game is one of the simplest games out, and it is a blast to play. The original author is on to something here, and should release a version for the Gaming consoles. Every kid that has played it on my PC, has loved it. A lot more than NFS:U, etc...

**Torcs** = Is a 3D car racing game. One of the best Open Source racing games, if not the best. To get the best out of Torcs, you really have to install a few "mods", or car add-on packages. The default cars are not that great.

**Wesnoth** = Is a 2D turn-based strategy/adventure game. It is one of the most popular games in Linux right now. It has a VERY high quality, and polished feel to it, and it only keeps getting better.

**Glest** = Is a 3D real time strategy game. It reminds me of the later Warcraft games, for some reason. It is also very popular.

**Nexuiz** = Is a 3D Quake based shooter. It was based on the original Quake engine, after it was Open Sourced. It actually looks like a Quake 3 engine game, with all of its Curved surfaces, and lighting effects. With a proper video card, this rivals many games that are only 2 years old.
