### Post by H264 on 2007-08-11
Don't forget what size these things are, if you convert the measurements to decimal, then they are about 1.1" x 0.75" in size, draw a box that size and you will find out that the black one (design 1) will look better ;)

these things will be very stiff by my estimation, don't know if DoctorMO explained any of this, but anodizing aluminum is kinda like heat treating steel to make it harder... only it does it on the outside of the aluminum.
In this case, the aluminum is slightly thinner than .003", so the whole thing becomes very stiff, then I'm not sure if it is dipped or wrapped, but another thin layer of aluminum gets on it to give it a finish look.

anodizing aluminum is fairly easy, just simply zap it with a fair amount electricity, and you are done. 

Don't think this kind of label can be done at home, because it can't.
