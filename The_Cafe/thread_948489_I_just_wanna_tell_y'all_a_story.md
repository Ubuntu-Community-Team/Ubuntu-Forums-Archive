It even got to the point where I had to take away admin rights on his Windows box. A box that he paid for. Yet and still, he managed to put the **** right back on there. If you're in IT, this is the **** (can I say ****? Guess I can't lol) you get tired of. Eventually I got fed up and decided he was gonna use Ubuntu.

Now I'm a beginner with Linux and setting up his box took a little time, but I got the hang of it quick. After building him an Ubuntu box with small customizations here and there I was worried that he'd want Windows back. So now it's time to test it out. 

After getting him to log into the machine, my uncle was enamored. After showing him the customization he was sold. He loved it. All the time spent on his machine paid off. And if you're in IT this is the **** you enjoy.

I hope this doesn't sound like an Ubuntu commercial but I just wanted to share.
