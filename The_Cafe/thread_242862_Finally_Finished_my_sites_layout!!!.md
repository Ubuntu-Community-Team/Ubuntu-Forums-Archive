### Post by Tomosaur on 2006-08-24
1: I'm on 1280x1024 - when I view your website, there are two scroll bars - one on the far right, like a normal page would have, and one in the middle content box. This is very irritating to use. I need to scroll down with both scroll bars to be able to read everything. I suggest you widen your content box and trim its height so that only the content scroll bar is visible.

2: Your banner is difficult to read in that font.

3: Some of your external links load into the content box - meaning they display horrible. Check your Flash page in your Portfolio for example. You should make it so that the page loads into a new window, or takes over the main page of your website instead.

Aside from that, it looks fairly nice. In your portfolio, the actual buttons to get to the different sections (ie Programs, Art etc) are way too big. There's no reason for them to be so large. In most cases, you should strive to fit all of a page's content onto the page so that the user doesn't have to scroll down.

### Post by Tomosaur on 2006-08-26
use the 'target' attribute. For example:
<a href="http://www.blah.com" target=_blank>HELLO</a> will make a link of the text 'hello' and load the page into a blank page.
