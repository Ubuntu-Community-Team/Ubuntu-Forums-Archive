### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-08-29
I recommend [TerminalRun ](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/9738), which integrates nicely with Ubuntu forums.

I'm the developer of two extensions for Linux: 

[Umarks](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/13153) - Create, delete, backup and restore lists of installed/deinstalled Ubuntu packages and search for installed packages on allmyapps.com, appnr.com, getdeb.net, packages.ubuntu.com, launchpad.net, linuxappfinder.com and Google.

[FoxMediaCenter](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/10991) -  is a media center extension for Linux, primarily developed for TV and video enthusiasts. It is basically an EPG with an integrated TV recorder, media and playlists manager, programme info, stats and much more.
