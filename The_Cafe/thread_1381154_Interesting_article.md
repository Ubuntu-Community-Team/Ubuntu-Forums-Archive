> 
Yes, as lxers we know that it&#8217;s remarkably close to &#8220;just working.&#8221;  We know that if something doesn&#8217;t work there are a hundred different sites we can go to for help.  We know that driver issues are rarely Ubuntu&#8217;s fault &#8211; they&#8217;re the fault of lazy hardware vendors.

Not the Computer Science major or Linux geek that lives on your block.
