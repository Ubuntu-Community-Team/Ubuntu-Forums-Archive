### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2015-05-23
I don't put stickers on my car except those required by law. If I see a car with stickers on it, I know the owner hasn't lived here very long. The summers here are very hot and the sun light is intense. The sticker glue melts, dries, and cracks, or the plastic sticker melts, or the paper sticker oxidizes, or the colors fade. Even the sticker required by law placed on the inside of the windshield of the vehicle starts to pealing off after only one summer; but, they have to be replaced yearly.

I actually don't think people seeing a LINUX distro logo on a vehicle will cause them to be interested in LINUX. To many, it would be some odd meaningless symbol sticker on the vehicle, if they notice it at all.
