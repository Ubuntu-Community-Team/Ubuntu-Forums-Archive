### Post by eenu on 2010-09-10
Hi Guys,

Just thought I would say hello! I have been in IT for many years, I remember the days when Linux used to be a real pain in the *** to get working and it was for geeks not your average joe. For many years now I have been a mac user, liking the depth I had with Unix if i needed it, coupled with a nice GUI for just getting normal stuff 'done'.

Recently I have become a little miffed with that platform though...... more bugs than I have ever seen before, closed ecosystem that I have spent thousands of ££ on. So, I have recently decided to carry out an experiment to see if I can convert to open source completely. You can find a couple of the initial posts over at my[ blog ]("http://www.davidjmoore.com")which I hope you will comment on, offer advice on as well as helping me out here when I no doubt ask for help and advice. 

Hopefully I will become a useful member of the community and be able to help out more as I get more experience.... thanks in advance for any help. For any Ubuntu staff type peeps, well done on 10.10, its beautiful and has come on leaps and bounds over the last few years!

Dave ):P
