### Post by Sandsound on 2008-04-09
> **aeiah said:**
> so they're free as in... free to listen to? is that it? why not free to download, free to modify etc?

There are pros and cons to make music under the GPL license.

Thou music and programming are both a form of art, it's hard to compare the two when it comes to the possibility of make a living.

Thats why I have chosen to split my work into two separate projects, a standard proprietary project and a GPL-kind'a project.

There are LOTS of success-stories when it comes to GPL software, but I haven't heard of anyone making a living with GPL music ?
