### Post by mcduck on 2006-01-30
Using Jack to route sound from mic to some LADSPA plugin comes to mind.. But setting that up just for playing with speech might be too much of trouble :D

So, I did a search with google and found you these: 

[Zerius Vocoder]("http://www.epiphyte.ca/code/vocoder.html") - Vocoders create cool speech effects, you might be able to compile this one.
[http://www.tml.tkk.fi/~tilmonen/mustajuuri/index.html]("http://www.tml.tkk.fi/~tilmonen/mustajuuri/index.html") - This one looks a bit comlicated for your needs, but it supports realtime audio processing and LADSPA plugins..
[http://www.linux.org/apps/AppId_7032.html]("http://www.linux.org/apps/AppId_7032.html") - this is actually a guitar effects program, but it looks fairly simple, and sure will help you to distort your voice :D
