### Post by DrMega on 2008-09-28
The best hangover cure is to follow this procedure:

1. Have a glass of water just before you leave the pub.
2. Have a pint of water just before you go to bed, and take another glass of water with you to sip through night when you wake up parched but can't be bothered to move.

Those two steps help ward of dehydration which is the biggest part of a hangover.

3. In the morning, get up and have a greasy breakfast made up of as many as possible of the following: bacon, fried eggs, sausages, fried mushrooms, beans. Wash it down with a mug of sweet tea.
4. Go back to bed (taking another glass of water), and sleep for a max of one hour extra while your body is breaking down the breakfast.
5. Get up, cold shower, get dressed, get out in the fresh air.

Job done.

### Post by ZarathustraDK on 2008-10-03
Anything containing salts will do. What happens when consuming alcohol is that it upsets the homeostasis of the body, forcing liquids (and salts) out of the body. Everytime you take a piss while drunk (which is a lot if you keep count) your body looses salts = you loose the ability to keep water inside due to salts osmotic properties.

That's why the best bet is some kind of isotonic drink like gatorade et al, or soda for that matter. Salty food like chips will also do. Drinking buckets of water may make you feel better short-term, but it'll just make you get up in the night to take another piss. Of course you can just keep on drinking water, but in the end you'll have to replenish the salt you've lost.
