      Objectives 'English'

The exchange of addresses of ubuntero who are prepared to share their houses or apartments to make them available as a place to stay and sleep, for example during release parties, fairs, and any contact-exchanges related to Ubuntu. This gives participants a cheap alternative for impersonal hotels and strengthens the social bond with each other. In addition, individuals may be able to share and exchange their houses as a cheap address for the holiday period. It must be made clear, who are willing to take part, so that contact can be made with each other.
