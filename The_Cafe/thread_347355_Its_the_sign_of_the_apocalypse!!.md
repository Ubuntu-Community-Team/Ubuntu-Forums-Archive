### Post by Redlance on 2007-01-27
DukeNukem was 3drealms take on 3rd person shooter after doom. Met with a good following about 10-12 years ago. They promised a sequel to it with great expectations. Code got scrapped and rewritten for various reasons. Repeat that reason about 10 times. about once a year. It has made the top ten 'vaporware' lists on many websites. And is often used as an inside jab of a game that will never be. Each year they (3Drealms) releases a tidbit of its progress. or takes down the website or changes the links.
And I believe ZDNET(I think) gave it the outstanding achievement award for achieving the #1 spot of its vaporware list for 4 years out of 10.
