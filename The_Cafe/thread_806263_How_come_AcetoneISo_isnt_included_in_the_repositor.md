### Post by bulletxt on 2008-05-25
gmountiso only mounts iso files and requires root password.AcetoneISO2 mounts all images iso mdf img nrg bin and does NOT require root password, converts all known images to iso and does plenty of other things. comparing gmountiso to AcetoneISO2 is quite silly.

here is a complete feature list:

[http://acetoneiso2.svn.sourceforge.net/viewvc/*checkout*/acetoneiso2/FEATURES](http://acetoneiso2.svn.sourceforge.net/viewvc/*checkout*/acetoneiso2/FEATURES)
