### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-06-20
To be honest, on a netbook I would not suggest Ubuntu at all, but rather Xubuntu or Lubuntu, though I accept that Ubutnu may be preferred as it is the GUI already known by the eventual user.  Is the school using 12.04, which is very different to most previous versions with the gnome desktop, as it is using unity.

I suggest you find out which GUI/DE the daughter's school uses, and if it is classic gnome you could perhaps still use Ubuntu 12.04, but follow the info at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1966370](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1966370) to turn it into the standard desktop that she is used to.
