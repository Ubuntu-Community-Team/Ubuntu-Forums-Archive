### Post by tehwa on 2005-10-27
Once you get the system installed, check out the official starter guide on the main menu at:

System > Help

Then through the "Ubuntu 5.10 Starter Guide" link.

Also available online at [http://help.ubuntu.com/starterguide/C/faqguide-all.html](http://help.ubuntu.com/starterguide/C/faqguide-all.html)

This document will save you a lot of time when it comes to tweaking aspects of your system.
