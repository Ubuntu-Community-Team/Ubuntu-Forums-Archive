### Post by MetalMusicAddict on 2006-08-15
I have a widescreen monitor and make alot of walls myself to fit my display. I recently became a member at [Widescreen Gaming Forum]("www.widescreengamingforum.com") and stumbled across a thread with GREAT links to HiRes and Widescreen Art/Wallpaper. I wanted to share 'em.

[LIST]
[*][Mandolux]("http://mandolux.mine.nu/archive/index.html") -You MUST go here! I love the stuff so much here Im donating. This site has ALOT of stuff for us 24" and 30" screen guys.
[*][Widescreen Babes]("http://www.widescreenbabes.com/") -For the guys (and gals nowadays :)) who like girly pics.
[*][Serious Wheels]("http://www.seriouswheels.com/") -This site is lots of things for the car person but has alot of hires pics. Just dig around.
[*][Interfacelift]("http://interfacelift.com/wallpaper/index.php?sort=ratings") -Just a cool site.
[*][RTT-Walls]("http://gir.bit-tech.net/~rtt/walls/") -Some dir link someone posted. [THIS]("http://gir.bit-tech.net/~rtt/walls/00199_africanlandscape_1920x1200.jpg") one would be a good Ubuntu one. I think this person uses linux because there is a tar.gz at the bottom with all the pics in it.
[*][Stock.XCHNG]("http://www.sxc.hu./") -Lots of free stock photos
[/LIST]

Happy downloading. :)
