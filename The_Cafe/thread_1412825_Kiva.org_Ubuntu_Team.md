### Post by -Phi- on 2010-02-21
Come join the [Ubuntu team]("http://www.kiva.org/team/ubuntu") on [Kiva]("http://www.kiva.org/")! I haven't found another thread about Kiva, so I figure maybe people don't know about its awesomeness.

Kiva is a website that connects people through lending to alleviate poverty. Basically, Kiva works with microfinance institutions all over the world to make small loans to entrepreneurs. You [pick a loan]("http://www.kiva.org/lend") and put in $25, along with a bunch of other people, and Kiva passes the money on to the microfinance institution, who manages the loan with the entrepreneur. There's no interest, but you get your $25 back when the entrepreneur repays. Then you can withdraw it, or loan it to someone else. You can look at the [About Kiva]("http://www.kiva.org/about") section to learn more about how Kiva and the loans work.

If you're interested Kiva AND Ubuntu, you might be interested in the [Ubuntu Team]("http://www.kiva.org/team/ubuntu").

It's fun! It's microfinance! You connect with people all over the world! It's in the spirit of ubuntu! I am what I am because of who we all are!
