### Post by OZFive on 2008-06-18
Doctor Who (ALL OF THEM)  The classic series was just awesome sitting in the living room with my dad and me watching Tom Baker and the Tardis fly through space and time.  Great memories.
[IMG]http://i30.tinypic.com/23it0gn.jpg[/IMG]

Battlestar Galactica.  I was a fan of the original series when I was a kid.  I just thought it would be so cool to be a Colonial Warrior and destroying Cylons in my Viper.  The new series is so awesome.  It is a grown up Battlestar that allows me to indulge in that universe again without laughing at the dated classic series
[IMG]http://i30.tinypic.com/30w6vy0.jpg[/IMG]

FarScape.  What a concept.  A human is the alien in a long lost universe.  The story lines and arcs were wonderful.  The imagery was complete and detailed.  The plots were not typical old sci-fi regurgitated, but original and new.  I was FURIOUS at the series ending (if you saw it you kmnow what I mean)  But mollified with the Peacekeeper Wars Movie.
[IMG]http://i29.tinypic.com/2jb62h1.jpg[/IMG]

The big bad, kick all of Sci-Fi series there was and is.  Atlantis does not hold a candle to it no matter what they try to do.  The chemistry between the actors and the stories and action, made this series one of the longest running series in Sci-Fi and well worth every minute of my time.
[IMG]http://i29.tinypic.com/28wo0n6.jpg[/IMG]

Oh Firefly, what can I say but that I lost you before I hardly knew ya.  I total f'Up at Fox screwed what was due to become a classic long running and rich series.  I still can watch the shows over and over and never be bored.  The mixture of good characters out on the fringe of things doing questionable acts to survive in a tough verse was a change form Star Trek's future of humankind where everything was just about handed to you and we lived (by todays standards) a utopia life.  Fun fun fun and sometimes compelling a wonderful little show.  Serenity was great!  But I am upset at a few characters leaving he show prematurely (damn you Joss!) and disappointed at having River channel Buffy to save the day.
[IMG]http://i27.tinypic.com/24d1s44.jpg[/IMG]
