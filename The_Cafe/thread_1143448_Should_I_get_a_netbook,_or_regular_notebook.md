### Post by Skripka on 2009-04-29
ANY kind of photo editing beyond rotating the image 90 degrees left or right, NEEDS a screen larger than 10"....and needs a better monitor than what netbooks and most laptops offer.

A netbook combined with a dektop tower is a winning combination--do the resource intensive tasks, and the tasks requiring LARGE amounts of screen space on the tower--do the general typing/playing on the internet on the netbook.

Netbooks are not intended to be the only computer you own...they are best used as a ultra-mobile supplement to a larger screened machine.
