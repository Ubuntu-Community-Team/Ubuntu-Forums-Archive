### Post by Paqman on 2012-06-08
The fan may have an arrow marked on its casing showing the direction of flow. Otherwise, if in doubt connect it to the power and (briefly) switch the power on. You can use a sheet of paper to detect the flow if you aren't sure.

### Post by Paqman on 2012-06-08
> **bananaboatcoat said:**
> Should air be flowing towards the processor or be flowing away?
As long as it's moving as much air as possible across the heat sink it doesn't matter, although in practice away will generally be best for ATX style layouts. You want to ensure a smooth flow of cool air into the box, across the hot components, and then the hot air smoothly  exhausted. In the standard ATX box that will mean fresh air coming in through the front of the box and out the back and/or side.
