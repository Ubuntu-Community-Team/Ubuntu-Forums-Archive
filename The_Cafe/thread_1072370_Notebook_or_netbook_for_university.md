### Post by Zenze on 2009-02-17
I personally think that while netbooks are pretty cool and a good idea, they would not make for a good choice if that was your only computer.

I am currently enrolled in a University and taking a few programming classes. From my experience having a netbook would really not suit. My laptop has a 13.3 inch screen and I already feel its a bit small. Being able to look at a couple terminals at a time and also maybe a webpage one the same screen is nice. Its not terrible switching focus around all the windows but going back and forth between two windows can get really annoying.

I don't know how your school will be but when I am in big classroomes not all of them have an outlet at every seat, so battery life is my #2 concern, and a netbook might be fine here. For obvious reasons if you can't make it through those classes before you get to one that has an outlet you can use you might have a problem. Plus, if your like me you will want to take you laptop everywhere with you, so having the convieniance of long battery life is great.

As far as processor speed faster is always better, but you could probably get by fine with a netbook.
