### Post by Sinkingships7 on 2008-09-17
The recent [thread on touch-typing]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=922552") gave me the idea to start this thread.

Using a standard QWERTY keyboard, how many words per minute can you type? Note: WORDS per minute, not strokes. You can figure out a fairly accurate estimate of your WPM by using [this site]("http://www.typingtest.com/"). If you'd be so kind, please play fair and don't attempt to cheat in any way. I would say that 2 times is fair: one for a quick warm-up, and another as your final. Any more times, and I feel that it wouldn't give the fairest results, as you would know what you were going to type ahead of time, and would have had too much practice with it.

The poll included has separate options for those who touch-type, and those who do not. I'll start. I type an average of 85 words per minute, touch-typing.

Have fun!
