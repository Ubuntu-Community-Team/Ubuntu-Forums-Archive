I like the idea of the Jamendo, but I haven't found a lot of good music on it (a lot of ambience trance stuff).

On [the Julandrew website](http://www.julandrew.com/), it says right on the front page: > **Linux Users Outnumbered Microsoft Users On This Site**
This month 50% of you are visiting our site using Linux. 39% are Windows users.  Help get that Linux number even higher and try Ubuntu.  And also never have to worry about the anti-virus racket or giving up your existing windows installation. Pretty cool.
