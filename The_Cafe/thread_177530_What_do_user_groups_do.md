### Post by Lord Illidan on 2006-05-16
They usually have mailing lists where they can share news and information..
Most have a forum of some kind.

Then they might meet every month, to discuss issues, the handling of the user group, the committee and so on.

Basically, it is just so you can interact physically with other linux users near you instead of just through the internet.
