### Post by JuanC on 2006-02-28
I try the last version of [ffmpeg2theora](http://www.v2v.cc/~j/ffmpeg2theora) v0.16 and i get good results.

ffmpeg2theora has input support for all that [ffmpeg](http://ffmpeg.sourceforge.net) can handle.

And also there is an example of encoding to Theora from DVD :
[http://trac.v2v.cc/wiki/FFmpeg2TheoraUsage](http://trac.v2v.cc/wiki/FFmpeg2TheoraUsage)
