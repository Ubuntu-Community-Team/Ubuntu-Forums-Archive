### Post by TheFu on 2017-04-20
LVM. Use lvm.   [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lvm](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lvm)  This is what the professionals use and there are very good reasons why.

Make certain you have excellent backups, however.  Old disks are always about to fail. Don't be surprised with they actually do fail and data is lost.  Actually, all storage is **about to fail.**  That is what storage is.
