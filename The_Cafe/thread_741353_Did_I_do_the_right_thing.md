* Is the data overwritten? If it is, it is way to expensive to get back
* Is the data continious? It wouldn't help in this case to get pieces of a file
* Did anything happen between the overwrite and now? Even booting causes the chances of recovery to disappear
* Is the data worth it? 

I have seen the option to resize missing on some installations, possibly if the existing partition cannot be resized or there are too many partitions.

With data recovery, it is best to bring hopes to a realistic point of view, then hope for the best.
