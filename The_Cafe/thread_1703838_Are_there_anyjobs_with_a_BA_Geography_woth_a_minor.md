### Post by treesurf on 2011-03-10
I've got a BA in geography and I'm working as an arborist.  Somewhat unrelated.

That being said you should look at environmental consulting firms, which should be looking for people with your skillset.  

BLM and Forest Service are other places to look, although they aren't hiring much these days.  BLM does do a fair bit of seasonal hires for plant survey, etc., but don't expect much in the way of pay for that kind of work.  State level forestry, natural resource, and agriculture departments are good places to check as well.
