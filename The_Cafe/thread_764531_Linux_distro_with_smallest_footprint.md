### Post by BrendanM on 2008-04-24
It's not a Linux distro, exactly, but you can run Minix ([http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minix](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minix)) on a 286 with 20 MB of HDD space and 2 MB of RAM.

You might also try DeLi Linux ([http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deli_Linux](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deli_Linux)) which has a 486 w/16 MB of RAM as it's **recommended** specs, it will run with less. DeLi even has a GUI!
