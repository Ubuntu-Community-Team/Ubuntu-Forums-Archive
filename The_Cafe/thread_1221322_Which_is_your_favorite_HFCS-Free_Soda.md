[B]HFCS-Free Soda = Soda that is made without High Fructose Corn Syrup.

[/B]> **High-fructose corn syrup** (**HFCS**) – called **isoglucose**[[1]]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/High-fructose_corn_syrup#cite_note-0") in Europe and **glucose-fructose** in Canada – comprises any of a group of [corn syrups]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corn_syrup") that has undergone [enzymatic]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enzymatic") processing to convert its glucose into [fructose]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fructose") and has then been mixed with pure corn syrup (100% [glucose]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glucose")) to produce a desired [sweetness]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sweetness").
