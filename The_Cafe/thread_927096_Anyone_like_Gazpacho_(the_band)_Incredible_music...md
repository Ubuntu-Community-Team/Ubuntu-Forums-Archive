### Post by Mazza558 on 2008-09-22
[http://www.gazpachoworld.com/music/streaming/]("http://www.gazpachoworld.com/music/streaming/")

I very recently discovered these guys from Norway, and their music could be classed as "progressive ambient rock". I can't get enough of their songs. 

I seriously, seriously recommend listening to "Massive Illusion" (at the bottom of the page), and listening to it the whole way through. The song has elements of loads of different genres merged into one, even with a violin solo at the end. If there's a bit you don't like, keep listening as it gets better and better. 

In fact, the whole album is incredible - some of the most original music I've heard in ages.
