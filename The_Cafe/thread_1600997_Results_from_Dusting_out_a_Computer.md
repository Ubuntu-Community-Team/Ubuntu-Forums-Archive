### Post by The Real Dave on 2010-10-19
How old is the computer, Intel or AMD, laptop or desktop? 5year old, Intel Pentium 4 (3GHz), desktop
How long did it go between being dusted? 4 years.
What was idling the CPU temp before being dusted out? ~50C
What was the CPU temp during intense use before being dusted out? >70C
What was the CPU temp after being dusted out, idle and intense use? ~40C



That was quite  a while ago. The system in question had been running slow, which later turned out to be the CPU throttling itself to prevent burning up. It was choked with dust. 

The temps above were improved by adding more fans, improving cabling, and changing pwm configs. When clean, it now idles around 29C and at full tilt, will happily sit at 35C. 

Which ain't bad for a Pentium 4 ;)
