When you use a 'text editor' such as gEdit, I would expect it to do exactly that: keeping all keystrokes as faithfully as possible.

In that case, any spaces only act as delimiters for the words which make up the text. A delimiter not followed by anything is not a useful thing to display or print and can be ignored.
