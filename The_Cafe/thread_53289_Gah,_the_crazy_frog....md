I love that hypnofrog!!!

But the crazy frog I could do without.

It all started when a kid thought it would be funny to record himself making noises like a moped speeding up. This got circulated around the net pretty fast, then got turned into the Insanity Test (I failed by the way, I started laughing immediately). Fast forward about 2 years. Funkyjunk.com approaches this kid, asking for the rights to his recording. He sells them to Funkyjunk (don't remember how much) and then the crazy frog is born. Since then, there has been the Crazy Frog Song, which has sold millions of copies (electronically) in Great Britain.

I can never think of the insanity test the same again.
