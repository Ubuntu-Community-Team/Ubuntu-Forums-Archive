### Post by hizaguchi on 2006-04-21
[QUOTE=Copter]ok, maybe i am an idiot.... but how to show how much memory is being used by apps? my kde info center says this:[/QUOTE]
Open a terminal and type "free -m" and it'll round your usage off to MB.  The 2nd two lines are the interesting ones.  The "+/- buffers/cache" is how much physical memory your applications are using and "swap" is how much swap space they're using.  The total of the 2 is your total memory usage.
