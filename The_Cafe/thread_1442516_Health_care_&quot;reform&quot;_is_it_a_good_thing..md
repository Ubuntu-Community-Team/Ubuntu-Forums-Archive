**[B]"Health care IT**[/B]. This sector is likely to have winners as President Obama makes a push for national, electronic health records. In other words, you won't have to explain your health needs every time you visit a doctor, specialist or pharmacy. Everything including medical records and appointments would be coordinated. "

So suppose Doctor Ducky tells me I have colon cancer, and that information goes on my electronic records, but I have my doubts, and want it independently confirmed (maybe by several different, independent clinics)
