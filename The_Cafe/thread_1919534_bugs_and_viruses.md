### Post by Copper Bezel on 2012-02-03
True, but don't confuse matters with a simple question. = )

*[Bugs]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_bug")* refer to glitches in software due to accidents or unexpected behaviors. *Viruses* are a particular type of *malware*. *[Malware]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malware")* in general is any code or program written for malicious reasons, such as to steal your data or damage your system.

For questions like this, start with a little Google searching, or take a look at the Wikipedia entries.
