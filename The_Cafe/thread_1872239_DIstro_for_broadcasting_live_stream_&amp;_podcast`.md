While most Linux distros probably use less RAM and disk space, that's hardly relevant for flash streaming or broadcasting sites. Flash on Linux has become OK in the past year or tow, so that any main stream distro like Ubuntu, OpenSuse, Fedora, Sabayon, etc should equally suite you.
