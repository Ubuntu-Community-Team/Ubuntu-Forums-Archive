### Post by irish_flu on 2007-01-16
Mine's a little weird.  I'm quite left-handed, but I'm right-eyed; it's called cross-dominance.

The most odd (to me) thing about it is that my eyes don't do the thing where you  close one eye and the picture moves a little; when I close and open my left eye, the picture never moves (because my brain isn't really using that image).

It's a bummer in that I have no real depth perception, but the brain has pretty much learned to compensate (although I still get hit in the face with a ball).

On the other hand, since 3d games have pretty much the same depth as real life life to me, I'm decent at those.  For some reason, I'm also a pretty good shot, when I shoot targets and "clay pigeons" in real life.  Of course, neither of those things is really very useful....  ;)
