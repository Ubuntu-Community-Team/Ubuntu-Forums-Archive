### Post by GrouchoMarx on 2013-02-26
Hey everyone,

Just played an incredible game released native to Linux call 'Kentucky Route Zero'. Has anyone else had a chance to play this? It's not for everyone, I admit, as the pacing is a little slow. But the absolutely gorgeous world and great story are so immersive that I almost wish it was slower. Real work of art. Easily one of the best games I've played in years (though I much prefer weirder games, not your typical gamer). Glad to see such great original games being released native on Linux!

[http://kentuckyroutezero.com/](http://kentuckyroutezero.com/)

You can get it from the Humble store or Steam.

From the website:

"Kentucky Route Zero is a magic realist adventure game about a secret highway in the caves beneath Kentucky, and the mysterious folks who travel it."

### Post by GrouchoMarx on 2013-02-26
