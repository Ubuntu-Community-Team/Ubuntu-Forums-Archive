[http://www.hamiltonzoo.co.nz/page/pageid/2145849719](http://www.hamiltonzoo.co.nz/page/pageid/2145849719)

Having a  Rhinoceros called `Ubuntu' would be way cool. He'd make a neat mascot. People might get away with dissing the penguin, but nobody messes with a rhinoceros.
