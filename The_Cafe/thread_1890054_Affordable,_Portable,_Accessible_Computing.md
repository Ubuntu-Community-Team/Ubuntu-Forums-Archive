Six years ago I bought an accessible PDA for US$5K + tax.
That PDA has died on me, without my realizing it. The replacement costs US$7.5K + tax. 

The iPad has an MSRP of US$799. The Xoom has an MSRP of US$499.
System76 offers the Lemur Ultra with a base price of US$615.

(This is the inevitable result of having party # 1 pay for the item, party # 2 receiving the item, party # 3 organizing things, and party # 4 supplying the item, with party # 4 not responsible to any of those parties in terms of quality.)

Does anybody have any thoughts on even where to begin, to get the cost of Braille Display Monitors down.
