### Post by Nicole@litmos on 2008-09-06
Hi FRUP, couldn't help but notice the discussion involving Litmos. If you have any questions I'd be happy to answer them! 
In brief we are an on-demand platform to deliver training online to a wider audience. Trainers simply build courses by uploading Powerpoint, video (100MB), PDF and any other existing training materials in to the Litmos system. A training framework is then wrapped around these files that converts them all in to a format that is viewable online through the web browser. 
 
Alternatively, trainers can build new slide-based courses and upload additional reference documents like .doc .pdf .xls and image files to accompany courses. These courses are then assigned to trainees and progress is tracked via the reporting suite. Litmos also includes an internal messaging system for increased communication between trainers & trainees, and an alerts system to keep trainers with their 'finger on the pulse' of trainee and course progress.

I hope that gives you some idea of what we do. As far as I know we are the only eLearning/online training platform that offers these kind of capabilities. But I'd be happy to hear otherwise!
