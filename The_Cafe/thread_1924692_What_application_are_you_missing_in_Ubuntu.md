### Post by Copper Bezel on 2012-02-13
I have a dream of a decent word processing application, but it doesn't exist for any platform. MS Word is slightly better than LibreOffice, but it's a beauty contest for naked mole rats.

I really love PDF X-Change Viewer, an app for making comments on .pdf files that work whether onscreen or printed. Compared to using, say, Xournal's notes or MS Office's comments for those purposes, it's an extraordinary little app, as fluid as writing comments on a paper draft but with the advantage that they're legible and editable (in neat little callout bubbles with clear arrows.) It's Windows only, although it does work swimmingly in Wine, so I'd *like* it to have an Ubuntu version, since it's the only reason I have to have Wine installed, but it's obviously not a real problem.
