### Post by @trophy on 2007-08-25
The first seven levels are easy.  They all have to do with viewing the source.  8 is the first one to give me any trouble at all.  Here's spoilers (in white text) for all of them that I've done so far:


Level 1 
[COLOR="White"]URL: [http://www.hackerskills.com/](http://www.hackerskills.com/)
Method: View Source to get password.
Solution: "null"[/COLOR]

Level 2 
[COLOR="White"]URL: [http://www.hackerskills.com/null.htm](http://www.hackerskills.com/null.htm)
Method: View Source again.
Solution: "l3l"[/COLOR]

Level 3 
[COLOR="White"]URL: [http://www.hackerskills.com/l3l.htm](http://www.hackerskills.com/l3l.htm)
Method: View source again... the password is equal to the color of "alink".
Solution:  "#000000"[/COLOR]

Level 4 
[COLOR="White"]URL:  [http://www.hackerskills.com/abrae.htm](http://www.hackerskills.com/abrae.htm)
Method: Umm... am I missing something?
Solution: Click the link.[/COLOR]

Level 5 
[COLOR="White"]URL: [http://www.hackerskills.com/sdrawkcab.htm](http://www.hackerskills.com/sdrawkcab.htm)
Method: View source... You know... I'm sensing a theme here...
Solution: "SAvE-as hELpS a lOt"[/COLOR]

Level 6 
[COLOR="White"]URL: [http://www.hackerskills.com/save_as.htm](http://www.hackerskills.com/save_as.htm)
Method: View javascript file: "pwd.js".  Finally something other than "View Source!"
Solution: "hackerskills123"[/COLOR]

Level 7 
[COLOR="White"]URL: [http://www.hackerskills.com/included.htm](http://www.hackerskills.com/included.htm)
Method: View /images/included.gif
Solution:  u:"phat" / p: "jerkybar3"
[/COLOR]

Level 8 
URL: [http://www.hackerskills.com/pwd2.php](http://www.hackerskills.com/pwd2.php)
[COLOR="White"]Method:  View phat.php to get the background image, /images/phat.gif.  View that and it tells you to look for a .PSD.  Open phat.psd (use the GIMP if you don't have Photoshop).  Remove watermarks.  This is utterly retarded.
Solution:  u: "zadmin" / p: "stebbins"[/COLOR]

Level 9
[COLOR="White"]URL: [http://www.hackerskills.com/phat.htm](http://www.hackerskills.com/phat.htm)
Method: View source to get a weird looking string.  It's Base 64 encoded, so use a PHP page to run in through base64_decode().
Solution: http:// www . hackerskills . com / gazebruh . htm[/COLOR]




Hope that helps... I'll be adding more once I pass them.
