It depends if you want to be official or not.

England has a few songs that they would consider as anthems. Jerusalem, Swing Low and God Save the Queen [http://uk.youtube.com/watch?v=NuEbGwWUb1M](http://uk.youtube.com/watch?v=NuEbGwWUb1M).

Wales has a Welsh national anthem and a few other song s like 'Bread of Heaven'
