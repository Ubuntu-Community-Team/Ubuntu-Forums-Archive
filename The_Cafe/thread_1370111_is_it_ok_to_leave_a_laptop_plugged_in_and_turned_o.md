### Post by Icehuck on 2010-01-01
You can do it but I would just remove the battery. As long as your battery is connected to the laptop, it will always be charging and discharging. This will kill the life of your battery in the long run.

It won't hurt your hardware unless the machine can not properly cool itself. Make sure the vents aren't blocked and that air can flow underneath it.
