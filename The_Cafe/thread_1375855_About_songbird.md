### Post by geoken on 2010-01-10
> **LeifAndersen said:**
> I find it interesting that it has a web browser, but not even a descent Podcatcher (or did they change that).

I used the most recent build and it's still bad. 80% of the music I listen to comes from podcasts so they're a big deal for me.

The current state of the podcatcher is essentially a mass downloader that accepts it's download lists in XML/RSS/Podcast format. Give it your feed URL and it will promptly begin downloading every single episode of that podcast while giving absolutely zero control. There are no settings for auto expiry or date/play count based functions.

If you're used to podcast subscriptions that will auto erase when you have x amount downloaded or after you've fully listened to them then Songbird will fall short. If you'e fine with just downloading every episode and manually deleting them then it's ok.
