### Post by gslug79 on 2009-11-28
As previously posted, Android is Linux based.

Palm WebOS (used on the Palm Pre) is also Linux based, although I believe most of the userland is closed-source.

Both of the above are fairly locked down out of the box.

Maemo was originally found only on Noika Internet Tablets, but is now on the N900 phone. This is probably the most desktop-like mobile Linux - it is based on Debian and uses GTK, though is moving to QT for the next version.
