### Post by Rhapsody on 2007-10-26
I've seen plenty of images on Wikipedia and Wikimedia Commons that were licensed under the GFDL, so it would seem that you can. My preference (as shown on [pointlessness](http://pointlessness.freehostia.com/) is to dual-license my content under both the [GFDL](http://www.gnu.org/copyleft/fdl.html) and [cc-by-sa](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/) as they're very similar to each other but everyone seems to have a different opinion on which they prefer.
