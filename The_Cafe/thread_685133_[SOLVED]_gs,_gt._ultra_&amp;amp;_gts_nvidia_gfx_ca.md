### Post by conehead77 on 2008-02-01
Its something like this:

GS - very weak version
GT - standard version
GTS - more powerful version
GTX - very powerful version (only high-end)
Ultra - most powerful version (only high-end)

I think they change some minor features on the graphics chip to make the differences.

edit: from german wikipedia:
x4xx/x5xx: Low-End
x6xx: Mainstream
x8xx: Performance and High-End
