### Post by hyper_ch on 2008-12-21
Hi there

some may remember source-o-matic by Seveas. Unfortunately it has gone offline a while ago. As I liked the tool quite a lot I thought it was nice to have it back on.

The reason why it was taken offline was, that it's not needed anymore because in synaptic you can re-create your sources list if you messed it up. However I think on a server you just can't run synaptic (although if you operate a server you'd normally know how to fix such problems). Also Seveas added a few extra repos.

Because of that I created now my own little tool that does that. A couple of third party repos that I use are already added.

Have a look at [http://repogen.simplylinux.ch](http://repogen.simplylinux.ch)
