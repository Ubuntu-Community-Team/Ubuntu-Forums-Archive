### Post by t0p on 2009-09-25
It varies.  On a UK keyboard it is to the left of the Return key, below the "]" (you have to press it with <Shift>). On a US keyboard, it's on the key to the left of the "1" - again, press that key with <Shift> to get the "~".  Other keyboards may vary.
