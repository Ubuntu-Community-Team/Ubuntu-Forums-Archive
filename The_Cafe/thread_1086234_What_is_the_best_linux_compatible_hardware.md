CPUs ad HDDs are pretty much non-issues.  Even video cards for the most part will not give you problems (I would choose nVidia though).  Choose anything you like!

The real problem areas, for me at least are, things like webcams, music players, remotes, joysticks, printers and other peripherals.  Most will work with Linux but sometimes with varying degrees of research and tinkering.  What kind of peripherals will you be using?
