### Post by TriBlox6432 on 2011-02-22
Find out all the hardware and see if there's any driver problems.

The 1GB HDD or SSD *will* be a problem.  Not large enough.  You need at least 5GB, 10 is better.

The 256mb RAM is pushing it.  You *can* run Ubuntu with that much, but you really should have 512 or more.
