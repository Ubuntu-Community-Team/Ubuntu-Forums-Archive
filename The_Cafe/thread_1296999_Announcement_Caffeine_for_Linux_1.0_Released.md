### Post by nobodysbusiness on 2009-10-21
[Caffeine for Linux]("http://launchpad.net/caffeine") is a coffee-cup applet that allows you to quickly and easily disable your screensaver and powersaving modes. This can be very useful if you are giving a presentation or watching a long flash video.

Now, with the new 1.0 release, it is possible to *automatically* disable your screensaver when certain conditions are met. My blog post on the release (including installation instructions) is [available here]("http://pragmattica.wordpress.com/2009/10/21/caffeine-1-0-released/"). Isaiah's blog post (with useful information for fans of [hulu.com]("http://www.hulu.com")) is [available here]("http://blog.mahboy.com/archives/223").

As always, we're happy to hear any comments or suggestions.
