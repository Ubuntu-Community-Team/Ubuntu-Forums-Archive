### Post by cholericfun on 2010-10-22
get used to the command line, simple stuff like changing directories,
viewing textfiles, creating/ renaming/ deleting/ copying

stuff like this
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Beginners/BashScripting](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Beginners/BashScripting)
and the basics of any commandline text editor (vi is my personal favourite)

when you need it / have  a specific task in mind.
