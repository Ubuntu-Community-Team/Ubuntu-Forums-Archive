### Post by Copper Bezel on 2012-03-12
Ubuntu's Netflix is Hulu Plus running in Google Chrome.

Live streams are a fun activity, but they're not a solution to the problem you're describing. You'd have to depend on the altruism of the users who actually own the movies to connect whenever they watched anything, and then everyone else gets to jump in late. It makes much more sense just to organize live stream movie nights the usual way.
