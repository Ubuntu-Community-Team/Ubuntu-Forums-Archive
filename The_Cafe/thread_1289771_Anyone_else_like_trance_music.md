Armin van Buuren, Tiesto, Oakenfold, Paul van Dyk, OceanLab, Sandra Collins, and the complete Cafe del Mar collection (among many, many others) are all on frequent rotation in my world.  I think some of those have been classified by some as trance, so I'll go with that.

I'll listen to pretty much anything except country and rap (though there are exceptions to that too).
