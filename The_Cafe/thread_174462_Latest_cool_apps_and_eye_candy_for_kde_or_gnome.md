### Post by hizaguchi on 2006-05-11
For KDE there is:
Kompose, which is like Expose from OS X.
Karamba, for desklets that do just about anything.
Yakuake, which is a terminal that rolls down from the top of the screen.
Kooldock, in case Kicker isn't fancy enough.

And of course, the greatest eye candy of all, xgl and compiz.  But that requires more patience than just installing a couple programs. :)
