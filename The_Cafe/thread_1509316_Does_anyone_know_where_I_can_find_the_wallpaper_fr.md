### Post by xc1024 on 2010-06-14
Just quick advice. Next time, try [tineye]("http://tineye.com"). This site gives you the ability to do a reverse image search. That means that you upload a picture or give a link to it and it matches other pictures in the database. With enough luck you may find the desired wallpaper in a few seconds. With not enought luck, you may find just a 200x150 picture or none at all.
