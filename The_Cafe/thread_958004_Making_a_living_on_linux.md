Nah don't listen to the FUD. :guitar:

I make a living from linux. It's good. But it's not a get rich quick scheme. If you commit to using and learning linux, get to know it intimately, practice setting up every service you can think of (http,https with apache,php,java, mail, databases, antispam, dns, dhcp, firewall, samba, nfs/nis, time, network monitoring, etc) you will obtain some very usable skills.

You will also be head and and shoulders above those who use ms windows for everything, but somehow succeeded in getting through a linux install in vmware and think that gives them sort of expert status.
