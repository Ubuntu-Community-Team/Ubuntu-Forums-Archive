### Post by aleska on 2006-08-21
While most of you viewing this don't need any help installing Ubuntu, you may know some people who are "thinking" about switching and might need a little hand holding and encouragement.  
Now before evangalizing you should probably send them to aysiu's post, [Is Ubuntu For You?]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=63315").  
Are they still interested?  Good!  Could they use a little hand-holding or encouragement?  If so, I think the following could be very helpful.  
Linux.com's Roblimo has posted several how to training videos.  They don't presuppose any technical knowledge and know how, but walk you through baby steps from downloading Ubuntu to burning the LiveCD to installing.  I won't say that I "needed" them, but I do admit that I viewed them before I converted.  Frankly, I think his voice alone makes them fun for viewing.  
Unfortunately, they are a little hard to find, so I put organized links to them in [one place]("http://www.skarulis.com/?p=23") for ease of access and viewing pleasure.
Hopefully someone in your life might find this helpful and it might encourage them to take the plunge!
