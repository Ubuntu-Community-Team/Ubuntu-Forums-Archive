### Post by blueshiftoverwatch on 2009-03-01
You could use encryption programs like [Gnu Privacy Guard]("http://www.gnupg.org/") for email and [Off-the-Record Messaging]("http://www.cypherpunks.ca/otr/") for instant messaging. Whatever third party entity that you don't want intercepting your messages can't spy on what they can't read. But it only works if you can convince your friends to use use encryption as well. Other wise you won't have anyone to talk to.
