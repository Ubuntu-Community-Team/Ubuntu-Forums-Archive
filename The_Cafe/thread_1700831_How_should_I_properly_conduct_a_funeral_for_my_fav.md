### Post by kansasnoob on 2011-03-10
I have to be the luckiest man alive :D

None of my other mice felt right to me, just downright clumsy, so yesterday I walked to the little brick-n-mortar puter-shop downtown hoping to find something comfortable, and expecting to spend way too much.

Lo-n-behold the owner/tech took one glance at my old mouse, didn't say a word, walked back into his work area, came back a couple minutes later and plopped down a pristine looking identical mouse.

He then explained that he'd taken it as part of a computer "trade-in" on a new sale, but the buyer couldn't find the USB receiver, so he'd just tucked it away like most of us do with our spare parts. When I asked "how much" he just said, "take it home and make sure it works".

After simultaneously pressing the connect "buttons" on both the mouse and receiver it works like my old mouse did when it was brand new. My middle button (pressing the wheel) hadn't worked for opening new tabs in ages but this works great!!!!!!!!!!!!!

Better yet, when I called to tell him it works great, and shuddering at the thought of what he'd want for it, he told me, "don't worry about it, it wasn't doing me any good". I'll grant you I've sent a little business his way, but I never expected that.

So my arthritic old hand is happy and, as I said earlier, I'm the luckiest guy in the world right now \\:D/

............... and now, rather than being the recipient of a token funeral, my old favorite mouse is now in the spare parts pile.

I wonder what my shrink will say about this :tongue:
