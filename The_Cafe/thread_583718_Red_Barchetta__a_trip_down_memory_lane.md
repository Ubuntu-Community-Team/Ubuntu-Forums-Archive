### Post by RAV TUX on 2007-10-20
Red Barchetta is a song by [RUSH]("http://www.rush.com/v4.html") first released on "[Moving Pictures]("http://danielekrauz.multiply.com/music/item/63/Rush_-_Moving_Pictures_1981")" in January 1981.

The song is about the future and the "Motor laws" have been enforced making all fossil fuel vehicles illegal. The singer takes you on ride on his uncles illegal Red Barchetta in a secret country hide-away.

A nice trip down memory lane, for those who remember RUSH's Red Barchetta song, for those who don't this is a nice introduction into a not-so-popular song by RUSH.
