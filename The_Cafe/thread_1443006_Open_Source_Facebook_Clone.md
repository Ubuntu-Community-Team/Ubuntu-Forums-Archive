### Post by DeadSuperHero on 2010-03-30
Lot of discussions have happened about this on UF. I even contributed my fair share of ideas.

One idea is to support the [GNU Social]("http://groups.fsf.org/wiki/Group:GNU_Social") project. They're looking at building a social network system called daisycha.in, which is currently in the planning stage. Lots of novel ideas are [being tossed around]("http://groups.fsf.org/wiki/Group:GNU_Social/Ideas").

I actually [did a blog post]("http://writerofthings.blogspot.com/") on my own thoughts on this yesterday, so good timing! :D

For reference, here's some of the old old old social networking threads I recall starting at one time or another. Might at least be some useful nuggets of truth in there somewhere.
