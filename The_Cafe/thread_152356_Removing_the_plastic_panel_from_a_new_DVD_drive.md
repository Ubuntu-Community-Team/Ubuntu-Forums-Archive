### Post by teet on 2006-03-31
Just rip it off of there!

1. Pop the drive open with a paperclip like others have suggested.
2. You'll have to remove the cover to the cd tray itself.  Grasp it with both hands and use your thumbs to push kind of up and out from below the piece (cd tray cover).  It should pop right off.
3. There's probably tabs on the side, one on the top, and on the bottom of the faceplate itself.  Use a paperlip to push in the tabs on the side, bottom, and other side.  Then grap the tip piece and basically rip it off (the top tab catches sometimes).  When I say "rip" I mean with gentle force :)
4.  Get your new faceplate and snap it on.
5.  Put on the cover of the cd tray (either the new or old one).

It's really easy.  I've done it like 200 times (we got in a shipment of grey cdroms that needed to be black...ugh), although I admit I was very slow the first couple of times I did it.
