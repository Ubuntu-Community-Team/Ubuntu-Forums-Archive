### Post by Garyu on 2007-06-15
I use [www.one.com](www.one.com) because they are cheap with relatively high quality. The best hosted solution I have come across.

If you want your own server, all you need is an ISP that gives you a fixed IP-adress and allows that kind of activity. Then just register a domain and direct it at that IP.

At [www.one.com](www.one.com) you can use their hosting for your domain, and then direct a virtual domain at your own IP giving you full control over part of the site. For example: register [www.mydomain.com](www.mydomain.com) and host it at their site, and then direct [http://home.mydomain.com/](http://home.mydomain.com/) to your fixed (or through dyndns.org) IP-adress at home. Then you will have a capable and secure site while being able to provide unlimited storage with full control and what not as well. :)
