First, I am not the seller nor am I associated with them in any way.

I spent a whopping 99 cents on a [bluetooth dongle]("http://cgi.ebay.com/ws/eBayISAPI.dll?ViewItem&item=190480438208") just to see if it would work. It did! 

It took 12 days to get from China to Florida, came in a little plastic bag with no directions or paperwork of any kind. 

I plugged it in and the Bluetooth icon appeared in the Panel. Pairing my headset (Motorola S305) was a snap, static code '0000' and it was found. Preferences, Sound, output, moved it to my headset and POOF! Stereo wireless tunes.

Haven't done any distance testing yet. Not that I need it to work across the room.
