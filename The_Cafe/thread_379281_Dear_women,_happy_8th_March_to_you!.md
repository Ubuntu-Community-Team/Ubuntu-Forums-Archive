### Post by cendant on 2007-03-08
8 March is called **Women's Day** in Russia. It is very popular. Women (girls, ladies, your girl friends, anyone female) get all kinds of presents, flowers, affection, love. 

They are spoilt for a day. Even husbands who cannot cook try to do their best and prepare a treat!

It is one of the most awaited public holidays as on this day you can give flowers and presents to total female strangers (not only your family!) without rejection. It is a day for romance and love

Actually, we call it International Women's Day. I am not certain why as it did not blend into into many cultures as deep as into Russian. 

So let's correct this and wish all (Ubuntu forums) women lots of love, happiness and beauty!

These flowers are for you, lovely ladies
