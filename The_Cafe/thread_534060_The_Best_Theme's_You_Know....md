### Post by Lux Perpetua on 2007-08-24
I love dark themes, but good ones are hard to find. Right now I'm using Khali Blue from gnome-look. (I had to hack it a little to increase the contrast for some widgets like scrollbars and tooltips, but otherwise, it's great.) If the slight blueness  clashes with your green snake, there's a more neutral (and also very good) version called Khali. 

Another excellent dark theme is Onux, also on gnome-look. It suffers from similar contrast issues, though.
