### Post by andrewabc on 2010-10-19
> **CharlesA said:**
> Mine's an N270 with a GMA950. The newer ones are probably more.. refined.. :P

Yep the new ones that have gma3150 chipsets are MUCH better with electricity.

[Here](http://www.anandtech.com/show/2889/11) is comparison of both chipsets.

Basically new chipset uses 1/2 the power. Compare D510 to N230 in graphs. So if your computer is idle (and assuming screen is off etc just processor/chipset on), your battery will run out twice as fast using older chipset.

39.1 watts idle for old chipset vs 21.2 watts for new chipset.

Heck even the nvidia ion chipset (dual core processor +vid card) used less power than gma950 chipset idle and under load.

Although according to that chart I am confused by the eeebox using less power. I have one of those, and they are supposed to use 20 watts idle. So not sure why nettop atom gma950 used less than d945 chipset+atom. Since I figured they were similar.
