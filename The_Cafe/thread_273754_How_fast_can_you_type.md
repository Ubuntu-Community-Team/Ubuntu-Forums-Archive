### Post by Wallakoala on 2006-10-08
So I thought that maybe we could have a fun little competition here...

If it is not already installed, fire up the terminal and install gtypist:
```
sudo apt-get install gtypist
```
Now run gtypist, and scroll down to where it says "Speed drills". Press enter and then choose "Lesson S1" (the first one) Follow the directions until you get to the "warm-up". The sentence is "The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dogs."

Type as fast as you can, but try not to make any mistakes because they lower your actual wpm.

When you are done, take note of the "Adjusted speed" and post it. You can repeat the exercise many times.

After about 70 tries, I got 141.00 wpm.

Post your fastest.
