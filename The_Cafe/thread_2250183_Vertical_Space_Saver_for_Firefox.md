### Post by cAzJJBx on 2014-10-27
I was wondering why FF for Linux doesn't put its tabs in the title bar to save vertical space as it does in Mac and Windows.  In my search, I discovered this add-on: [Hide Caption Titlebar Plus]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/hide-caption-titlebar-plus-sma/")


[IMG]http://i.imgur.com/9LonBNj.png[/IMG]


Hovering over the "Firefox" button, which can be shrunk, will show the menu bar.  It works very well so far and is quite customizable.
