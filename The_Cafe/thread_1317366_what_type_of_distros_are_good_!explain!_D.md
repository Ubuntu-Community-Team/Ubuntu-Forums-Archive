### Post by squilookle on 2009-11-06
Both OpenSUSE and Fedora are excellent distributions: Suse was the first distro I used about 6 years ago and I stuck with it for a long time. I try the newer ones every so often.

I agree that it is rock solid, and YaST is a fantastic tool. The only problem I ever had with Suse was that I used to find it a little bloated. 

Fedora on the otherhand seems to be incredibly light weight, even running Gnome or KDE. The downside I have experienced is that some things tend to not work properly without some tweaking: Pulseaudio for me in Fedora 11.
