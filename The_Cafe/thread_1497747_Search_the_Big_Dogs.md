**Search the Big Dogs**
[img]http://yeoworks.hostzi.com/searchthebigdogs/stbd2.png[/img]

**Search the Big Dogs** allows you to search Google, Bing and Yahoo! at the same time. Other search engines coming soon! You can also slide the frames to see more of a frame's view (easily click and move the middle frame to adjust or use the sides of the frame) and navigate each frame to different URLs. You can also watch videos while searching. If you love using split browsers and using them to search lots of information, then you will love Search the Big Dogs. Instead of downloading software, you can just use this webapp online. This is the preview version. Some features are still being developed and will be finished and uploaded soon.
