### Post by Miguel on 2009-02-26
During my 2 month stay at the US, I contracted a service with Diamondcard, the default SIP provider for Ekiga. I don't know if this is the best or not, but it certainly is cheap. As an additional advantage, a small amount of money goes to the Ekiga project if you get the Diamondcard account from the links in Ekiga. 

Note that while phoning to fixed spain numbers is cheap, other countries and cell phones may be expensive. One should note though that calling spanish cell phones is expensive. As a referene, US to Spain is I think $0.02 a minute, paid in fractions of half a minute.
