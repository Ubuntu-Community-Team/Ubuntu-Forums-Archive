If I could ask something of you it's to stop watching crappy reality tv shows, try and find truth/science. My heros are people like Carl Sagan, Albert Einstein, Neil deGrasse Tyson, Chris Hadfield etc and some really old philosophers who's names I cannot pronounce :D Don't judge people, the garbage man can teach you some good lessons, after all his life was probably way harder than yours.

Never blame others for your shortcomings or failures, never ever! You will find the majority of people are there to lift you up and carry you! Be kind to your aggressors, you might find it insulting/agressive etc but some people have never known a nurturing environment, all they know is pain & hate, it's your duty to show them the other side of life. Avoid those that are negative and try and drag you down, you deserve better in life and so do they if you can get it across to them.

Be yourself, take pride in what you do but most importantly BE HAPPY!
