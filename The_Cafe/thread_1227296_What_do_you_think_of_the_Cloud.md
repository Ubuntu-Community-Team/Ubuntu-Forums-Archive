### Post by Dullstar on 2009-07-30
Post your opinions about the idea of the cloud here.

-------
Dumb idea:  see reasons.

**1.**  All your private data is on Internet.  Hackers are also on Internet.
**2.**  Sure, you might have a secure password, but is that enough?
**3.**  The service you have might have a way of accessing your password.
**4.**  What if the server goes down?
**5.**  Accessing your files could become a subscription service.  When I buy storage, I prefer to pay for the hard drive once and then be done monetarily with it.
**6.**  What if you are at a time when you can't connect to the Internet?
**7.**  What if your service is driven out of business without warning and doesn't warn you or move your files?
**8.**  How do you have an OS on your computer?  Hard drives may be driven out of existence.
**9.**  Some of us don't want to feel pressured to use the cloud.
**10.** Two words:  Connection speed!
