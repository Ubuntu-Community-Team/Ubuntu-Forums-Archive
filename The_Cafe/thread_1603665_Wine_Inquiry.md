Good day fellow connoisseurs, 

I would be remiss if I failed to mention that I have yet to thoroughly peruse this forum's winery content. I set forth on a great search to find the most popular winery forums and color me ecstatic to find the sheer number of patrons on this site.

As an aside, let me just be frank on noting I am beyond excited to getting to know and pick the brains of your champion Oenologists.
