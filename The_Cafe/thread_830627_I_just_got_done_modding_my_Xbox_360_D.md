### Post by Lord Xeb on 2008-06-16
I have done the following:

-Removed grill from exhaust to increase airflow
-Used external power source to run origional fans (this increased their aiflow by nearly 150%)
-Used relay to use origional fan power source to turn off and on the modified fans
-Added a dial to allow adjustment of the fan speed (and noise level because at max speed, the fans sound like a cross between a blow dryer and a jet engine)
-Modified heat sinks so that air only flows through the front of them ([See]("http://www.hardware.info/images/articles/xbox360-open.jpg") the white shroud, that directs through the heat sinks, covering the top of the heat sinks increase the airflow)
-Split air channel to increase airflow between both heat sinks and ensure equal flow


>_> This all made it so that my 360 is now cool to the touch and it runs a lot cooler too :D
