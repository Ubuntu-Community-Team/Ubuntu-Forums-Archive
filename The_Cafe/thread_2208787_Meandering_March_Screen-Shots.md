### Post by TeamRocket1233c on 2014-03-18
Completed my Archbang -to- Arch conversion today. The steps I took are listed below:
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Step 1) Install LXDM, disable SLiM and enable LXDM.
Step 2) Experiment with other environments besides Openbox, played around with GNOME 3 for a while, but went for MATE instead, speaking of which....
Step 3) Remove SLiM, Openbox, Tint2, Conky, Thunar, LXTerminal, LXAppearance, LXRandr, OBTheme, and GNOME, replacing it all with MATE.
Step 4) Replace Archbey with Screenfetch, making the terminal readout as Arch instead of Archbang.
------------------------------------------------------------------------
The theme is listed in the terminal readout, while I'm using 'The Pokemon Zodiac' as my wallpaper. Also, enabled unredirect fullscreen rendering on Compton, and reversed the button positioning on the titlebar, moving the close, minimize, and maximize buttons to the left and the menu button to the right.

Using the stock panel config, as I kinda prefer it, actually.
