[LEFT]                         [FONT=Arial]Things It Takes Most Of Us 50 years to learn: [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]1. The badness of a movie is directly proportional to the number of helicopters in it. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]2. You will never find anybody who can give you a clear and compelling reason why we observe daylight-saving time. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]3. You should never say anything to a woman that even remotely suggests you think she's pregnant unless you can see an actual baby emerging from her at that moment. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]4. The one thing that unites all human beings, regardless of age, gender, religion, economic status or ethnic background, is that, deep down inside, we ALL believe that we are above-average drivers. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]5. There comes a time when you should stop expecting other people to make a big deal about your birthday. That time is: age 11. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]6. There is a very fine line between "hobby" and "mental illness." [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]7. People who want to share their religious views with you almost never want you to share yours with them. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]8. If you had to identify, in one word, the reason why the human race has not achieved, and never will achieve, its full potential, that word would be "meetings." [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]9. The main accomplishment of almost all organized protests is to annoy people who are not in them. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]10. If there really is a God who created the entire universe with all of its glories, and he decides to deliver a message to humanity, he will NOT use as his messenger a person on cable TV with a bad hairstyle or in some cases, really bad make-up too. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]11. You should not confuse your career with your life. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]12. A person who is nice to you, but rude to the waiter/janitor, is not a nice person. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]13. No matter what happens, somebody will find a way to take it too seriously. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]14. When trouble arises and things look bad, there is always one individual who perceives a solution and is willing to take command. Very often, that individual is crazy. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]15. Your true friends love you, anyway. [/FONT]
  
  [FONT=Arial]16. Nobody cares if you can't dance well. Just get up and dance. [/FONT]
  
            [/LEFT]
