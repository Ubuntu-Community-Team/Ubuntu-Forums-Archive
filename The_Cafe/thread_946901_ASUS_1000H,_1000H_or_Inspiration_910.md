### Post by titus on 2008-10-14
I've been looking at the same issue myself and have decided on the Asus 1000h. In my opinion the SSD of the 1000 is not a great advantage, as the speed and power usage does not offer much advantage over the hard drive (the SSD drives they use are not great)*. The keyboard on any of the 90x series is also much too small for me. I actually had a black 900 which I returned as the keys were too small. 

A valid alternative for me was the lenovo s10, which I saw today. This is a much better looking machine than the 1000h (again my opinion, I prefer the flat design), but suffers from low battery life (3 cell vs 6 cell on the asus), a slightly smaller keyboard (I saw that it's 85% on a review site vs 92% of full size for the Asus), and apparently it gets hotter than the 1000h. They are planning a 6cell battery for the s10, but as I write this, it's not available here (China).
