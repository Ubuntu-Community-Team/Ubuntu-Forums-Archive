### Post by irrdev on 2009-12-23
I personally have heard some questionable stories about Acer laptops; two of my friends had their new Acer laptops experience hardware problems within a year of purchase. Thankfully, they were both covered by a warranty. For an affordable and very functional laptop, I prefer Toshiba laptops. I bought myself a new Toshiba Satellite laptop from Future Shop in June for $599, with a Centrino processor and 4GB of RAM. It's the "Future Shop Exclusive" Toshiba laptop, and it comes it slightly varying hardware specs. The hardware specs of the Staples laptop are quite good, but I would just question the quality of the Acer brand.

EDIT: It appears that Future Shop has raised the price of their Toshiba laptops since I bought mine. You might also want to try buying from Dell online - Dell has a fairly solid reputation and offers competitive pricing.
