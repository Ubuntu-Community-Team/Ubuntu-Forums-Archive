I got mine on June 23rd, the day it came out :D.

Though they seem to be moving farther and farther from their old style, it's still a great album.  This will probably sound like a cliche Dream Theater fan, but I really want another Images and Words.  I listened through it a couple times on my drive to Minnesota to tour the university and every time it blew my mind a little more.  Their newer, heavier stuff (Train of Thought and on) is still awesome but I'd like to see some more of their older stuff.

Or, Liquid Tension Experiment style instruments with vocals.  Paradigm Shift + James LaBrie = :guitar:.


Edit:  I forgot to mention, I'll be attending Progressive Nation in Milwaukee, WI on August 23rd.  For those who don't know who's playing, it's going to be Dream Theater, Beardfish, Zappa plays Zappa and Pain of Salvation.  I originally wanted to go to see Dream Theater but when I found out Pain of Salvation was going to be there, I was sold.  They haven't done a US tour for 5 years so I wasn't about to miss my chance :).  If any of you Dream Theater fans haven't heard any Pain of Salvation, check them out.  Instrumentally, they aren't as technical as Dream Theater but they are an incredible band.  One of my top 5 of all time.
