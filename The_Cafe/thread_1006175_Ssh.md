### Post by Oliver.BS on 2008-12-09
Is it possible to use SSH to access a Dell running Linux using a Mac Laptop.

---

### Post by Joeb454 on 2008-12-09
Of course it is :D

Mac's come with SSH installed anyway, so that's not an issue :) For the Dell however, you'll need to install [openssh-server]("apt://openssh-server") and then configure your router to forward the SSH port to your Dell :)

I believe there's plenty of guides for this kind of thing if you have a look around :D
