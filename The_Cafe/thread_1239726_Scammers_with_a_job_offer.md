### Post by samjh on 2009-08-13
Hmmm...

Just got an email from a place called Alliance Asset Management Systems, telling me they have "received your resume"!  WTF?  I've never heard of these people.  Furthermore, they're using a well-known online job advertisement site (mycareer.com.au) to make themselves look legit.

Some Google-Fu reveals there are a lot of complaints about them, regarding bogus debt-chasing, fraud, etc.  Seek.com.au has confirmed they are frauds: [http://www.seek.com.au/general_id_items/content/safejobsearching.ascx](http://www.seek.com.au/general_id_items/content/safejobsearching.ascx)

Anyway, if you receive a letter like the one attached, ignore it and don't reply.

Anyone else receive bogus job offers and scam emails like this?  Perhaps we should make a compendium of scam emails and job ads.
