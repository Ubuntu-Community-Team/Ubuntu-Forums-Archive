A completely integrated computer house-domotica-ish system... Doesn't it sound delicious!?

The way my home-utopia would look like:

I'd remove all the phones,
the reaaaally-old security system (with quirky infrared sensors),
the radios,
the broken DVD-players,
our POS-system AND
the doorbell (with really backwards 20th century black&white camera and which doesn't even make a "doorbell" noise all of the time)

... and replace them with laptops (not necessarily new ones).

Imagine ... so now you have so many machines for 1 task;
you can't really answer the phone with the doorbell-camera-phone thing,  but they could all be integrated.

So, if anyone would be at the door, every computer in the house could get a ring, never miss anyone again! (Unless you want to :))

You got yourself an in-house intercom! With video support, forget about having to run to the other side of the house for a silly question. 

And this one idea is really cool, even though you might not want it too happen:
