If you have a limited budget, you are better off using an off-the-shelf program and work around your specific needs.

A developer *MAY* tailor the package to suit your needs but it will take time (both the developer's and yours by testing) to get it to the point of acceptance.

### Post by Dr. C on 2012-06-22
If there is a Free Libre Open Source software product that does most but not all of what you need then I would hire a developer to customize that product to your needs. 

There is no need to code the whole thing from scratch and if you hire a developer to do this most of the work and cost will be to re invent the wheel and only a small part will be for your specific customizations.
