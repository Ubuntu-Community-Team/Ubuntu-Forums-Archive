### Post by fatality_uk on 2008-08-03
I'd suggest creating a random word including those 3 letters. It's likely that [www.mmbhousing.co.uk/com](www.mmbhousing.co.uk/com) etc is already taken or someone is sitting on that domain. Something like **m**ol**m**er**b**y property associates gives a sense that it's a well esstablished company. In the logo design, you could highlight the MMB.

Just checked, **[www.molmerby.com](www.molmerby.com)** and **[www.molmerby.co.uk](www.molmerby.co.uk)** are both available.

On a wider note. I hope the properties will be seen as a long term, i.e. 15 years+ investment. Anything less and you will unlikely to reap any serious rewards. I have properties in the UK, Poland and Slovakia and the UK properties are currently less profitable than the others. my LTV for a remort on one is weak. I am holding off till 1st Q 2009 to re-finance.

Unless you already have property, I would suggest that you hold off starting to buy until the new year. The trends are looking for another 2 quarters of small contraction in the domestic hosuing sector. If it's commercial property, you should be VERY careful. It's basicallt bombing at the moment and a lot of commercial stuf is coming onto the market with pre-planning for conversion to domestic use. That could seriously effect the domestic market long term.
