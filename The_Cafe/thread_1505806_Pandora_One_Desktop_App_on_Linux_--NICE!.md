Hey Guys --

I tried Pandora a LONG time ago and honestly didn't care for it, but with all the hype it's been getting I decided to give it another swing.

Luckily my account was still there, so I logged in and found they now have a desktop app that is compatible with Linux (through Adobe AIR).  So I downloaded Adobe AIR and then the Pandora One client and within a few minutes it was going.

It has great desktop integration with Gnome and works wonderfully!  I wish they had a Rhythmbox plugin for Pandora, but this is the next best thing.  At any rate I'm impressed with the song selections, and the stations I setup are really setting a nice mood while at work :)

Anyone tried it ??
