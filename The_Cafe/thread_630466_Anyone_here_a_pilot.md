### Post by gn2 on 2007-12-03
My PPL expired some years ago, I have over 1000 hours in single engines and gliders, did two summer seasons as a gliding club towplane pilot flying Supercubs and Pawnees.

I held an AEI instructor rating for gliders and used to really enjoy taking first timers up in a glider.

The experience of soaring far surpasses powered flight, anyone can fly a plane, it's really easy, but having a good flight in a glider is dependent on finding and exploiting good conditions.

It's almost spiritual and I recommend you get down to a gliding club and have a go at  a purer form of flight.

It's cheaper and more sociable too.
