### Post by LaRoza on 2007-10-15
There are many posts expressing hate/dislike/disdain for particular distributions. I would like to take this moment to tell everyone that we are almost always using the SAME operating system and such wars should not be so heated. To remedy this situation, I suggest we all take the time to say something we like about a distro we would normally  criticise.

In this thread, do not say anything negative about the distro(s). The mere fact you are saying something nice about it tells use you dislike it. Also, don't say anything that is an insult to the users, such as: "Its so easy a monkey could do it", and don't say anything trivial as in: "I like the font on their website", because that implies everything else is bad.

If you wish to provide distros you recommend, include only the distro names in parentheses at the end of your post.
