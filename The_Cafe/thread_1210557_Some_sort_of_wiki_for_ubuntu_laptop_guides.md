### Post by ddrichardson on 2009-07-11
People tend to look for the model and make rather than the term laptop - which frequently become confused with "laptop mode".  However, as you found the testing team I suspect you were on the team wiki and not the community wiki, I did a quick search there and there were lots of results: [http://is.gd/1v3qN](http://is.gd/1v3qN)

In fact, there is a problem with the help wiki, that people often create pages that aren't located under others - in this case under laptops.
