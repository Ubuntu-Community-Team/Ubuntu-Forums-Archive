### Post by haqking on 2012-04-25
login to your live id

Go to the Account Summary
click Settings.
Under Common tasks, click Close your account

It is effective immediately

[http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows-live/account-close-account](http://windows.microsoft.com/en-US/windows-live/account-close-account)
