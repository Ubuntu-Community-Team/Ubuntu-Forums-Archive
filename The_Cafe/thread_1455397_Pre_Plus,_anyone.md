### Post by HeresJohnny on 2010-05-14
That's a great deal for the price, and you'll be able to use your phones as a hotspot wherever you go. Think of a long highway drive- your phone will be the wireless connection, and other people can surf the web on their laptops or iPod Touch.  If you have kids, that's an instant no-brainer. Of course, you could also use the phone as a GPS to get you where you're going. For apps, do yourself a favor and install Preware to give yourself access to system tweaks and apps outside the Palm catalog. I use Twee Free for Twitter, but have heard good things about Tweed. Here's what I use everyday: Music (Remix), WootOn!, drPodder, Comics, Mileage Monitor, Express Stocks, Flixster, WikiHow, Evernote, and Sporting News Pro Baseball.  Good luck to you!
