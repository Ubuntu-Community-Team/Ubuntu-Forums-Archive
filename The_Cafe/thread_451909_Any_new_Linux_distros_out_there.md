### Post by Tundro Walker on 2007-05-22
[Rockbox]("http://www.rockbox.org/") ... screw the firmware!  Make your mp3 player do what YOU want it to do.  Like...play Doom on your iPod.

[ReactOS]("http://www.reactos.org/en/index.html") ... Open-Source Windows XP (if you're gonna be a hypocrite and dual-boot with XP, why not make it an Open-Source XP?  LOL!)

[Hactar]("http://anywherebb.com/hactar.html") ... fits on a 3 1/2 floppy disk.  It's not a full OS, though.  No drivers, etc.  Pretty much you can boot from it, and then check out its literature, then check out the source code.  Alex is an interesting developer with lots of side projects, and a genius that borders almost on insanity.  Really nice guy, too.
