### Post by icett on 2007-09-23
Whenever anyone asks for PC games compatibility for Linux the typical reply is " If you want to play games you should use consoles for that. PCs are not for games". I think that is a lame excuse. Just look below the quantity of games for PCs and other top gaming consoles according to Gamespot below:



PC Games: 8712

PS 3 Games: 372

PS 2 Games: 2956

XBox 360 Games: 573

Wii Games: 594



Now you can see for yourself that PC has the largest quantity of games than any other console. And you must be knowing for sure that most of them would be for Microsoft Windows. This also clears the fact that PCs can be used for games too. This is one of the major plus points for Windows and one of the major shortcomings for Linux. If we wish to see Linux atleast as popular as Windows, PC games must be easily playable in Linux too. I request to the Linux developers to give their attention to this matter.
