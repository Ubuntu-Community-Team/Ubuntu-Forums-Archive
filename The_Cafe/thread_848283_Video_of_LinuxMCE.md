Speaking as one of the developers:

The system is incredibly themable, but not in the way that you might think. 

Orbiter is a highly retargetable system, that currently has targets for everything from PDAs, to cell phones, to on screen TV displays, tablet PCs, and even Cisco 7970 touch screens.

As such, a special inherited design system had to be created by Pluto to deal with designing user interfaces for many targets..

If you log onto our forums and do a search for HADesigner, you will see my screencasts, showing how to use the UI designer for your own work.
