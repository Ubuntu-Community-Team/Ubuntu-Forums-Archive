### Post by LowSky on 2011-08-13
If you dont have the bags that will fit your motherboard please dont use the smaller ones and try to knit or tape them together.

The "best" homemade remedy is to wrap the motherboard in aluminum foil then wrap it in plastic wrap.

### Post by Kromgol on 2011-08-13
I'd just put it in the anti-static bag, surround it with some layers of bubble wrap, put some foam peanuts in your package, put it there and make sure it doesn't throw around when you shake the package. Should sit quite tightly.
