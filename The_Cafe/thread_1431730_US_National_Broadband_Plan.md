Was surprised to not see this mentioned already. For the Americans on the board, the FCC is making huge plans for a massive overhaul of our national grid. They plan to make 100 Mbps an affordable low end while pushing for a wireless grid capable of up to 1 Gbps. No doubt they plan to use the wireless devices jointly developed by key members in the computer and electronics industry to make use of analog spectrum freed up after the switch to digital.
