### Post by blueturtl on 2012-01-16
> **cbowman57 said:**
> I can't even participate in the poll.

Rolling release, oldest component 12 years old, newest about 4 months.

Which is why I still prefer a Desktop PC over a notebook for a primary machine.

To all you upgraders:

I suggest answering the poll based on the age of the motherboard, the heart of the PC. When you swap a mobo pretty much all the other major internals go too (CPU, RAM, etc.). If the mobo is the same, then you're just doing minor upgrades. Doesn't make sense to count the box or the peripherals like keyboard or mice.

By this logic, my computers are from 2002, 1998 and 1996 - even though some of their parts are newer upgrades.
