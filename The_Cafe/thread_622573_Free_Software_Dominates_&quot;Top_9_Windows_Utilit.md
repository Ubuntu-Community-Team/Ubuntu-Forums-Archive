### Post by 23meg on 2007-11-24
Mozilla developer Robert Accettura [notes]("http://robert.accettura.com/archives/2007/11/24/top-windows-programs-open-source/") in his last blog post that 7 out of 9 titles in [a recent roundup of top 9 Windows utilities by download.com editors]("http://www.download.com/8301-2007_4-9820407-12.html?tag=ss_rltd") are open source. Accettura prefers the term "open source", but all the licenses in question are [Free software licenses]("http://www.gnu.org/philosophy/license-list.html").

That's quite something, isn't it?
