### Post by bonzodog on 2006-03-08
Does anyone have this working yet? I have seen screenshots of it, and it looks very cool, and I know it needs the directfb libs to work, but it would be cool if someone got this login manager to work in ubuntu. For those of you who don't know what I am talking about, it is a login manager that runs in the framebuffer/console and enables you to theme it. It will start your favourite X session, then when you logout, you go back to console effectively.

[http://qingy.sourceforge.net/](http://qingy.sourceforge.net/)
