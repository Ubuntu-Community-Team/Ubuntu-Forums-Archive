### Post by Vadi on 2008-12-30
Just thought I'd point out that GScrot 0.64 is out today. It sports a much improved drawing area, faster startup time, and a bunch of other updates. Full changelog is [available here]("https://launchpad.net/gscrot/+announcement/1704").

[**Download it here**]("http://gscrot.ubuntu-projekte.de/wp-content/uploads/releases/ubuntu_debs/gscrot_0.64~ppa9_all.deb") (Ubuntu 7.10 - 8.10 are supported), or [add the PPA]("http://gscrot.ubuntu-projekte.de/?page_id=243") if you'd like updates delivered via the update manager ;)

About GScrot:
> GScrot is a feature-rich screenshot program. You can take a screenshot of a specific area, a window, fullscreen, or even of a website - apply different effects to it, draw on it to highlight points, and then upload to a image hosting site, all within one window.

Homepage: [http://gscrot.ubuntu-projekte.de/](http://gscrot.ubuntu-projekte.de/)
