### Post by ravingmad on 2010-03-26
Hi all

Just thought I'd share my experiences with Ubuntu. I'm a traditional Windows User since Dos 1.0 days and in the past struggled with getting my head around Linux OS's. Last year I bit the bullet and installed an Ubuntu server and went through a lot of teething but gained a lot of help and insight from these forums. After a year I feel pretty confident that I'm quite up to scratch with things, still a lot I don't know but I know enough to make me feel comfortable in using it for mission critical stuff and I'm learning new little tricks all the time.

After 16 years of hosting with Windows Servers, all new sites I create these days go straight onto the Ubuntu boxes and even a couple of older ones have been moved. In the past running PHP scripts like Joomla, Wordpress etc on a Windows machine came with many headaches, compromises and potential security risks but running these now on Ubuntu servers they all just work like a charm and as they were intended to work.

I am thoroughly impressed at the stability and functionality of Ubuntu and the amount of help out there should one run into a glitch. I run Webmin which is such an awesome management package and helped me to make sense of things a lot quicker and with each upgrade the app just gets better. 

I might just become a total Ubuntu convert in the years to come :)

Thanks to those who offer help so freely on these forums and are willing to share their expertise with everyone. Viva Ubuntu !!!
