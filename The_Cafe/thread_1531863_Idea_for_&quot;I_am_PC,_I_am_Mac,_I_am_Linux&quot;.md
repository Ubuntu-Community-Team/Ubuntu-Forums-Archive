Wynnie (I know, female name.. but it's a creepy young man, so I think its fine if I keep him a little "ambiguous") - look young as a kid, I know that Microsoft it's long stage from long time.. but their Windows is tipical to "completely change face" on every version (98/Me/XP/Vista...) usually for let to the past the disastrous image of the previous windows. ### Post by chessnerd on 2010-07-15
This reminds me of a comic idea I had a while ago. I was going to have it be about the various web browsers.

The IE character would look kinda like the PC guy from the commercials. He would also be pretty full of himself despite the fact that nobody likes him.

The Firefox character would be the "popular guy" and would give these grand speeches about freedom and openness. He would also have a lot of gadgets and gizmos with him (extentions) all the time.

The Chrome character would be a younger guy who is "new". He would be very thin (light-weight) and well-liked. Firefox wouldn't like him very much and would feel threatened by him.

The Safari character would be a female character. She would be professional and strong-minded, not caring what anyone thinks of her.

The Opera character would be the guy that nobody notices. He would always be trying to get attention by doing some amazing feat, but none of the other characters would pay him any mind.
