This is a common misunderstanding of the situation. It is in fact NOT illegal in the US. There has simply (or not so simply) been legislation passed that makes the banking involved difficult on the poker rooms.. and by proxy the players. 

Full Tilt and PokerStars both have taken measures to make the process as simple as possible for American players and have done a good job in my opinion.

It is a bit easier for non-US players like myself to get in the game.. as many other poker rooms don't allow US players for this reason, but it's a non-issue on Full Tilt and PokerStars.

I've heard that American players sometimes have delays on their cashouts compared to non-US players... but that is the only problem I am aware of for American players.

Glad you brought that up though. Thanks.
