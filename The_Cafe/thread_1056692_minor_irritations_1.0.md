What are some human behaviours/affectations/gaffs, etc. that make you squirm, purse your lips, clench your fists, etc.?

Here's my kickoff . . . the terms "dealbreaker" & "showstopper" are _not_ interchangeable, despite widespread notions to the contrary.

A "dealbreaker" is simply an optional component of a flexible process.  The "deal" can always be modified to arrive at a mutually agreeable resolution.

A "showstopper" is an immutable component of an inflexible process.  The "show" cannot proceed under any circumstance.

So there! :)
