I watch it all the time. My family has kept every episode of the third season saved on our DVR and I regularly go back and re-watch episodes. We also have the first and second season on DVD, and I watch those. I've probably seen every episode at least 3 times.

It's the best sitcom currently on TV.

He mentions it in two episodes:

The Psychic Vortex - Raj is looking for something to do and Sheldon says "Well, you're in luck. I just found out I don't have enough room on my hard drive for a Linux partition. So, you and I are going to perform a full backup, reinitialize, and then reinstall all my operating systems." Raj, as you might expect, isn't happy about that prospect. So he bribes Sheldon with his limited edition Green Lantern lantern to go to a university mixer with him.

The Staircase Implementation - We found out what Linux distro Sheldon installed when he says "Oh, Ubuntu. You are my *favorite* Linux-based operating system."
