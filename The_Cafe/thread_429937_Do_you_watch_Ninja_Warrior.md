For those who done know Makoto Nagano is a 35-year old fisherman[1] who is regularly seen on the television show SASUKE, otherwise known in the United States as Ninja Warrior. Nagano spends 300 days a year on his fishing boat, the 28th Konpira Maru, and he uses his boat to train for the Ninja Warrior competitions. He is a champion of the Japanese TV show Ninja Warrior, he is the only contestant to successfully complete all the various obstacle courses since 1999. [2] Nagano supposedly is a disciple of "Mr. SASUKE," Katsumi Yamada.

The show SASUKE (Romanized in Japanese) is a competition held in Japan produced by Monster9 [1] and broadcast on the Tokyo Broadcasting System (TBS) television network twice a year between Japanese television drama seasons. It is one of the spin-offs of Muscle Ranking Series (Japanese: &#31563;&#32905;&#30058;&#20184;&#12471;&#12522;&#12540;&#12474;, Kinniku Banzuke Shiriizu?), a former TBS physical tournament program. SASUKE consists of four increasingly difficult obstacle courses. Each competition is taped prior to the air date and is shown as a three-hour show with edits made for time consideration. Its like an actually challenging version of American Gladitor sans the gladiators.
