### Post by linuxforartists on 2010-12-02
From OMG! Ubuntu! [Dell's new Vostro V130 Ultra-Thin Ubuntu Laptop]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/11/dells-new-vostro-v130-ultra-thin-ubuntu-laptop/")

Looked promising, until I saw that it had an Intel Celeron processor.  I went on the Dell website to look: [Vostro V130 laptop details]("http://www.dell.com/us/business/p/vostro-v130/fs").  Only the "Essential" Vostro build, a base model, comes with Ubuntu 10.10.  If I selected any of the higher-spec ones, Windows was the only choice.

At $430, it's a nice price.  The cheapest Linux laptop I've seen.  But maybe you get what you pay for?
