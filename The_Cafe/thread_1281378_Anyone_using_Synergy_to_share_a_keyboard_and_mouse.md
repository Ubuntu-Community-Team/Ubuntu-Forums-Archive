### Post by markbuntu on 2009-10-03
I have a monitor that I used with two machines. It has dvi and vga connectors. I had one machine on each conector and switched from digital to analog on the monitor controls. It was a few months ago that I was doing this but I don't remember having any problems. I made sure the monitor was on the proper input when I booted up just as a precautionary measure but I don't think that was strictly necessary.

If you can do this, give it a whirl.
