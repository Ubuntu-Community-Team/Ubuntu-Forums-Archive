### Post by Nerd King on 2009-10-23
Dependencies..

sudo apt-get install apache2 php5 dict dictd dictd-gcide dict-moby-thesaurus

Then just extract this somewhere in your /var/www (you may need to use sudo to do copying) and go.

It's completely ugly, unsupported, and done in 5 minutes, so quick, dirty and probably full of holes. However, it's really only built for me but I figured I'd share it with other people if they want it. Feel free to modify it and make cooler versions. One day I may get round to using proper CSS, etc but for now, ugly is good.
