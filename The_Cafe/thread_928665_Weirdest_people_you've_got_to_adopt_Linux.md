### Post by patricknsmith on 2008-09-24
I set a computer up for my 80 year old grandma to use, I use Ubuntu and I figured since she had problems using windows, when she tried to learn how to use a computer, I'd install Ubuntu on it. She knows how to do everything she wants now, after only a week (email, browsing, word processing, the usual) bearing in mind she failed to really do anything using Windows.

Even better is that my Uncle was so impressed by Ubuntu he wants it installing on his computer too.

Why do people choose to pay upwards of £90 for Windows, just to do simple tasks like the ones mentioned earlier? And who are the weirdest people you've got to adopt Linux?
