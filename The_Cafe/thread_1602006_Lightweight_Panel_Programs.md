### Post by Version Dependency on 2010-10-20
I love bmpanel2 on openbox (a recent [screenshot]("http://versiondependency.deviantart.com/#/d2zujzb")). It's very customizable as far as designing goes...more so than any other panel I've tried.  And it comes with a pager, launchpad, taskbar, clock, systray...everything you need in a panel.

Installing was a bit tricky for me as well because there are some dependencies you will need to install first that are likely not already on your system (I assume you are using Ubuntu).  I know I needed git, cmake and make.  And I think I needed a pango something another.  Just pay attention in the terminal, because usually it will tell you exactly what you are missing.  If you want to try installing bmpanel2 again, I may be able to talk you through any steps that are confusing.
