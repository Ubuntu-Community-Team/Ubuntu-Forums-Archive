### Post by Bandit on 2011-05-17
> **Johnny420 said:**
> I am real close to getting my boss to switch all his client machines to Ubuntu. My problem is I need a *great* soft-phone to get him to see things my way. I have done some research and have a couple of ideas (Ekiga && Gizmo) but I was wondering if anyone here had a better Idea.

First off you need to do a cost comparison and calculate a Return on Investment. 
- Does he have existing data or software that requires windows?
- Does he have existing hardware that may have to be purchased for compatibility reasons?
- How much revenue/man hours will be lost during the conversion?
- How will the conversion to Linux profit the company?
- Will converting computer hardware to Linux void any essential manufacture warranties? 
- How bad will the conversion to Linux slow employee production down?

The list can go on and on. 
Best bet is to setup Linux only when the company is starting up fresh or during a major technological overhaul. But you sure dont want to cause a unneeded overhaul when its not needed as this will cost the companies large sums of money.
