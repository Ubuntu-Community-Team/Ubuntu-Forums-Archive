### Post by Perfect Storm on 2010-04-08
FSpot uses GTK2 engine
Digikam uses QT4

As Gnomes stuff are GTK2 based then FSpot is more "home" there, and KDE uses QT4 so Digikam feels more home in KDE. But you can use apps cross DEs but the look and feel might not be as espected and fireing up another engine will taakes some more memory and the engine takes a little extra space on the HDD.
