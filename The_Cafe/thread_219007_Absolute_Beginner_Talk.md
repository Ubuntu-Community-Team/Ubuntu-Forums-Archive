### Post by Derek Djons on 2006-07-19
Being inspired by side projects of others I decided to start also one. I've made a blog especially for the Ubuntu beginners. On this blog I will be posting Ubuntu news, info, tips and what not in an understandable way. I want to provide info which I've discovered myself or came across the internet which makes using Ubuntu Linux easier, more functional and fun. With my posts I hope beginners will understand the way Ubuntu works easier and faster.
Of course a blog wouldn't be nothing without my 2 cents... so I'll be also posting a lot of thinking and reviewing.

At the moment I've quickly wrote a basic article. Take a look and tell me what you are thinking about it. [http://absolutebeginner.wordpress.com/](http://absolutebeginner.wordpress.com/)
