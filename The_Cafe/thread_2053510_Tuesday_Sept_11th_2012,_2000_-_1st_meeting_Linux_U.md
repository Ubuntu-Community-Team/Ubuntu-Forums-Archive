### Post by pe7er on 2012-09-05
On Tuesday, September 11th, 20:00, the first meeting of Linux User Group Nijmegen will be organized.
Date: Tuesday, September 11th, 2012 (= 2nd Tuesday of the month)
Location: De Klinker, Van Broeckhuysenstraat 46, 6511 PK Nijmegen, The Netherlands

This Linux usergroup is not targeted at a specific distribution (eg Ubuntu) but we want to discuss about general linux stuff (commands, applications) and different distributions.
(though a small poll showed that about 80% of the current Nijmegen LUG members use an Ubuntu / Debian-like Linux distribution)

Schedule for this first meeting:
* A short demonstration of "CrunchBang" (Debian distribution + OpenBox window manager)
* A short demonstration of the Raspberry Pi
* Finally, there is room for unstructured knowledge sharing about Linux

More information, see Dutch website: [http://www.linuxnijmegen.nl/bijeenkomsten/6-lugn1-dinsdag-11-september-2012](http://www.linuxnijmegen.nl/bijeenkomsten/6-lugn1-dinsdag-11-september-2012)
LinkedIn Group: [http://www.linkedin.com/groups/Linux-User-Group-Nijmegen-4475292/about](http://www.linkedin.com/groups/Linux-User-Group-Nijmegen-4475292/about) (currently 22 members)
