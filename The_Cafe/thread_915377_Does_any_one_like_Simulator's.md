### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2008-09-09
The only flight simulator I like is Orbiter - a very deep space flight simulator (which also does just fine with terrestrial flight).

Alas, it only runs in Windows and has no collisions but is superbly awesome nontheless. It is particularly satisfying to dock a space shuttle with ISS - far moreso than landing an airplane on a particular runway since this is incrediblly difficult and rarely done! Findimg ISS, let alone sticking to it (without disturbing the orbit!), is like finding a needle in a haystack.

There is even a community build module simulating the Apollo missions with accuracy down to the control knobs. You need the mission documentation to even launch the thing :)
