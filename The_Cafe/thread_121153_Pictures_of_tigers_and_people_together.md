### Post by fuscia on 2006-01-24
check out this site - [http://www.cptigers.org/animals/species.asp?speciesID=9](http://www.cptigers.org/animals/species.asp?speciesID=9) (it's a link to the tiger page). it's the carnivore preservation trust in pittsboro, nc. i went there about ten years ago, when i was working for the nc wildlife federation. at that time, they had about 18 tigers there. the biggest one was named ian, weighing in at 950lbs. he was as big as a cow. (i'm sure he was a pig, like my cat, as they don't get that big in the wild, usually.) they had one tiger, named michael, who used to wait until one of the volunteers brought food to the food slot and then pounce in the puddles near the wall, splashing the poor, unsuspecting victim.
