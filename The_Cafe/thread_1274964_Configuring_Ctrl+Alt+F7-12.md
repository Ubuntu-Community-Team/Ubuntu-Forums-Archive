### Post by Xbehave on 2009-09-25
switch users -> new session 
They are not really ttys, so unless xorg is running in them you get a blank display.
p.s some systems don't have the 1st xorg set to ctrl+alt+f7 any more, but ctrl+alt+f8 will still be the shortcut to your 2nd login.
