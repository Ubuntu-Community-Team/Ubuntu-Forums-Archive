### Post by monsterstack on 2009-07-05
Your applications will not be installed after the restore. But what will happen is that after you've installed your applications again, the settings for all of your applications will return to what they were before you nuked the hard disk. Themes, icons, recent documents, preferences. It's pretty handy.
