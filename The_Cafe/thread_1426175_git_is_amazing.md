### Post by handy on 2010-03-10
Only 2 or 3 days ago I started using a pre compiled -git repo in Arch, that covers the kernel & all of the other packages required for ATi GPU suport.

Everyday (or however often I choose) I can upgrade my entire system, & now the upgrade includes the aforementioned -git packages.

I get to play on the absolute cutting edge & if for some reason there is/are serious bug(s), then I can reboot & choose the non -git kernel. :)

As a non-programmer (these days), I find -git to be great, as it allows some of us users & some of us testers an arena in which we can be of help to the dev's & the community, & in a variety of ways due to the existence of forums. :)
