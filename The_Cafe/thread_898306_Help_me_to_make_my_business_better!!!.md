### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-08-23
Do your job better and faster than others .. then you can justify your rates.
Make no excuses and don't search for blame when something goes wrong (which, if you have employees, WILL) and admit it is your fault, since you are in control.
Take that extra step to make sure that the customer is satisfied as word of mouth is your best advertisement.
When you don't know what to do about an issue, admit THAT and hire an expert to assist.
AS and IF you expand, surround yourself with people that know MORE about the specific areas of your business than YOU do.  
And, running a business is not just about what you do, but about the business of business itself. which means you need to know about BUSINESS, too!
