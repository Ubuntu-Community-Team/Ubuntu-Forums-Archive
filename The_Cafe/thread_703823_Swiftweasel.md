### Post by Linux_Man on 2008-02-21
Has anyone besides me checked out Swiftweasel? Basically its optimized Firefox for certain CPUs. It seems to be much faster then Firefox (albeit a bit more CPU/RAM hungry) even with some about:config tricks used. The site is [http://swiftweasel.tuxfamily.org/](http://swiftweasel.tuxfamily.org/) and they have a Ubuntu/Debian repository on there. To me Swiftweasel is a lifesaver because it saves me the trouble to recompiling Firefox -O3 (Yah, I am a geek, my browser MUST be the fastest it can be) every time.
