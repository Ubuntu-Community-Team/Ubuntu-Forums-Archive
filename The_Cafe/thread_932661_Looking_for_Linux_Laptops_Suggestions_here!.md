Hello to you all, Ubuntu forum frequenters such as myself..

I just want to try out a little experiment...Stumbled upon a new Google mechanism today..thought I'd throw one this community's way...Something rather interesting may emerge....or perhaps it won't...I urge you to visit the link below and offer your opinion...it won't take a moment and might turn out to be a valuable resource.

Let me tell you a little about where you're headed..

Basically the Google Moderator series --> "Linux Laptops" has been created for laptop users of the Linux-using persuasion to offer their suggestions regarding Laptops which satisfy the following criteria:

Laptops which the sight of doesn't make design conscious owners weep.
Laptops which don't make Linux users bang their heads in frustration. 

I've purposefully left it open ended...basically I'd like for you to submit a "question" {Basically a reply to the "Linux Laptop" topic} ; giving what you consider to be: your favorite/the best/an absolutely groovy laptop for a user running Linux. Feel free to offer your own personal experiences with the laptop if you happen to already be the proud owner of this great Linux Laptop of which you speak :)


The idea is to offer the Laptop seeking individual a constantly up to date list of excellently compatible Laptops they can happily run Linux on without having to endure any undue sacrifices of functionality.

I myself am a Linux user and have been looking for a laptop to call my own.I was quite surprised to find very few communities to call upon for advice. If you're interested, help this attempt at creating one by visiting the link below and submitting your suggestions :) 

Regards,
