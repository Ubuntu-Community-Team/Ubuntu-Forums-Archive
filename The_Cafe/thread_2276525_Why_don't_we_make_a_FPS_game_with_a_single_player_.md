### Post by Stefan_Vukanovic on 2015-05-06
Lets see....
None of them have enemy models, they do have models of bots, but meeting same 5 enemies through the hole game... really? Next, textures. It takes less textures to make a MP game than an SP one. 50 times more. Same for models. Next, maps. I do not think i need to explain this one. Now, if we get only 5% of the game already done and for that our game becomes a mod, really. Look, don't get me wrong, mods are not bad, but "This was the first linux open-source free game ever made" vs "This was first open-source single player mod that works under linux" is really different.
After all, we will be using ID Tech 3, game engine made by ID (actually IoQuake3, its successor based on Quake 3 Arena's open source release) and used in OpenArena and most others. It was made for multiplayer, but as it was proven in RtCW(Return to Castle Wolfenstein) and most of the Star Wars games, it can be used for singleplayer.
