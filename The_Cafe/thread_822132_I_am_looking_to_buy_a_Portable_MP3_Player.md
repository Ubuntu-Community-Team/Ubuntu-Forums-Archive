### Post by DarKnyht on 2008-07-16
I just switched from an iPod Nano to a Sansa Fuze 4GB.  It is supported in Linux by using MSC mode, but the video player capability does not work (it requires a windows only program).  Make sure you check/fix your MP3 tags with EasyTag or Audio Tag Tool before moving them over.  Otherwise you might find all your songs lumped together because of incompatible tags.

The player also mounts strangely after the first plugin.  When you plug it in a second time it only shows up under the "Places" menu until you open it there.  Once you open it there, it mounts and functions normally.

Overall, I like my purchase and the fact it is not tied to one OS or another.  I do wish they would open the video player to be usable on all the Operating Systems.
