### Post by suoko on 2008-12-04
i noticed that the battery with compiz available lasts half the time if compared with compiz disabled.
i then created a "metacity --replace" app on the desktop.
i did that since i use compiz (on eeePC) with every effects disabled. i find it useful only for windows speed.
In case i disable compiz from the screen settings and then reenable it with normal effects, i loose my custom minimal configuration.
by just running "metacity --replace" i keep my settings at reboot (or by running "compiz --replace")
