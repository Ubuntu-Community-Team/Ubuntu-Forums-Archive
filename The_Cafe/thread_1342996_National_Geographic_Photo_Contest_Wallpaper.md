### Post by mobilediesel on 2009-12-01
National Geographic has posted a ton of wallpapers from their international photo contest. Go [here]("http://ngm.nationalgeographic.com/photo-contest/2009-wallpaper") for the 2009 wallpapers, [here]("http://ngm.nationalgeographic.com/photo-contest/wallpaper") for 2008.

You can only download one at a time from their flash photo viewer or you can go [here]("http://www.webupd8.org/2009/11/automatically-download-all-wallpapers.html") to download a couple bash scripts that will download all of them. One script for 2008, another for 2009.

There are a lot of excellent pictures. The 2008 pictures are available in 800 x 600, 1024 x 768 and 1280 x 960 resolution. The 2009 are only available in 1600 x 1200.
