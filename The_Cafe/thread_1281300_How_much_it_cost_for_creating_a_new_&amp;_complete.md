### Post by DeadSuperHero on 2009-10-05
I think it depends on two factors:

1.) How much you're willing to spend.

2.) How much the artist is willing to be paid for.

If both of these strike a good balance with each other and the artist does well on his commission, I think it's entirely possible to pump out all sorts of high-quality themes due to the time the artist has now set aside to make the theme.

Personally, if I were the artist I'd demand maybe 100-200 USD for a commission, and as the person paying I think I'd be willing to pay about the same amount.

That said, some people just like doing it for free. Look up Macchiato, Giant Speck made it in his spare time and it's an exceptional theme.
