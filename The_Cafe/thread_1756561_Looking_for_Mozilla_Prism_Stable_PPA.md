### Post by beew on 2011-05-12
> Prism is no longer developed by Mozilla Labs, although this  documentation will still be useful to those using WebRunner (see next  paragraph). There are now two alternatives:
 The Prism code was picked up by Matthew Gertner who started a company to continue its open source development under the name [WebRunner]("http://www.salsitasoft.com/webrunner/"). See his blog post at [http://www.salsitasoft.com/blog/2011/04/18/packaging-webrunner-applications/](http://www.salsitasoft.com/blog/2011/04/18/packaging-webrunner-applications/)
 Mozilla Labs itself started a new project called [Chromeless]("https://developer.mozilla.org/en/Chromeless"). See their blog post at [http://mozillalabs.com/blog/2011/02/prism-is-now-chromeless/](http://mozillalabs.com/blog/2011/02/prism-is-now-chromeless/)
