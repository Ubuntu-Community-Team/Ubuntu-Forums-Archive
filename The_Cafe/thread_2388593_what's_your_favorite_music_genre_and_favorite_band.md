### Post by deanr2 on 2018-04-11
I'm into anything 'psych' regardless of how light or heavy it is or from which era it comes from. 

Fave band probably has to be the Grateful Dead although I've not really listened to them since I stopped drinking and smoking :P.

I tend to go through phases so at the moment I'm listening almost non-stop to Terry Reid's 'Seed of Memory' from 1976. Do yourself a very big favour and check it out on YouTube. If you like Led Zeppelin you might like this. It's not as heavy but he actually turned down the role of lead singer before Robert Plant took it on as he wanted to stay solo. And you can hear how his voice would've worked just as well as Plant's when he's at full pelt :guitar:.
