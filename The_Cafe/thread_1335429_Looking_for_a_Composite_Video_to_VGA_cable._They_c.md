### Post by doorknob60 on 2009-11-23
Closest I could find: [http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16882339047&cm_re=vga_to_rca-_-82-339-047-_-Product](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16882339047&cm_re=vga_to_rca-_-82-339-047-_-Product)

WARNING:
> This adapter works with graphics cards that have TV-Out function capability through the VGA connector. 

Check your Video Card manual or manufacturer to make sure that your VGA card has TV-Out function capability


> Your video card must support S-Video out via VGA - MOST DO NOT. You need to have a particular card that can output the S-Video/Composite signal type over VGA. Some Matrox cards do this. NVidia and ATI cards won't. Your laptop won't, etc.
The cable works fine, it is your card that isn't providing the signal needed.

> Most video cards do NOT provide the functionality to use this cable. If your video card says it supports "TV Out", it most likely is NOT talking about "S-Video over VGA", as most cards do NOT work that way.
