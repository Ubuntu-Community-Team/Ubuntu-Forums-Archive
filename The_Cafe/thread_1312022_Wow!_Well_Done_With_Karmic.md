I just did a clean install of Karmic today - and wow - what a difference.

What makes Karmic such a pleasure was the culmination of all the little things.

1.) Installation seemed faster and simpler - the slide show was a nice bit of polish.

2.) Network manager - wow! It detected all my networks - including wireless.  I entered my passphrase and BOOM! Connected.  I don't even need the little script I ran in the terminal to get a decent data rate. It appears that problem has been fixed.

3.) Speed - everything is faster and snappier.

4.) Connecting to my wife's home Windows network - three steps - BOOM! - connected to all share files.

5.) Nice improvement on icons and look and feel.  I am buying a bigger wide screen tomorrow. 

6.) The one thing I was concerned about - Software Store - like it more now.

I can honestly say this has been the most polished experience with a new Ubuntu I can remember.

Well Done!
