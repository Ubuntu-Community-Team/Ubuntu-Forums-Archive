### Post by Burgundavia on 2005-04-20
There are several linux certification programs. 

The best, IMO, and community driven is Linux Professional Institute. Real people submit the questions, and is mostly volunteer driven. There are 2 current levels, and one in development. Each level is 2 tests, at $100 USD a piece.

See at [www.lpi.org](www.lpi.org)

There is also CompTIA's Linux+, which is not recommended.

Also, Novell and RedHat offer vendor specific stuff. Again, not recommended.
