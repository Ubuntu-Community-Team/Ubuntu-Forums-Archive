### Post by ewenss on 2013-08-14
[Docear]("http://docear.org"), the free and open source academic literature suite, and the Ubuntu-based distro, [UberStudent]("http://uberstudent.com"), are joining to **call for a developer to code Docear4Libre/OpenOffice**.


**You will be paid.** Please read [the required specifications]("http://www.docear.org/2013/08/14/who-wants-to-develop-docear4libreoffice-or-docear4openoffice/") and how to submit your proposal.


**Docear works well under Linux**, because it is written in java, but since there is only a Docear4Word plugin for it, Linux adoption of Docear hasn't really happened, for obvious reasons. Let's change that! 


**Please ask your friends if they can develop such an add-on -- write about this opportunity on Facebook, Twitter, etc., to help us to reach as many potential developers are possible!**
