### Post by Ender Black on 2008-05-24
Any PMP made by Cowon is a good choice.  They have the best sound engines I have tried so far.  They also come out of the box ready for linux, including advertising that fact on their documentation.  The D2, which I have and I believe is probably their most popular device in the USA is now available with 16GB internal memory and an SDHC card slot for additional storage.  The 16GB version goes for $233 at jetmall.net if I recall.

D2 plays .flac and .ogg and has a battery life of 52 hours continuous playback (10 continuous video playback).
