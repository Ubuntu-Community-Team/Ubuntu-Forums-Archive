### Post by BigCityCat on 2010-01-19
I realize a lot of you probably know this but this is for people like me.

I was looking around for more cowbell and I noticed that the videos were disappearing from the net. I wanted to save a copy so I could have more cowbell, but I really didn't know how to save it. When right clicking didn't work I did a search and was amazed at how easy it is. I decided I would share and for those that are interested there is a cowbell link below.

To save flash goto your flash website and open your file manager. Navigate to root/temp and rename your flash, copy and save to your videos. Now you can have more cowbell.


[http://www.dirtybutton.com/videos/978-i-need-more-cow-bell/](http://www.dirtybutton.com/videos/978-i-need-more-cow-bell/)
