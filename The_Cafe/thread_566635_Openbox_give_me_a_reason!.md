### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2007-10-03
I almost always stick to fluxbox myself, but I've seen many conversations about this in the past (usually on the Gentoo forums).  It seems to come down to two main reasons.  First off, Openbox is smaller and lighter on the resources than is Fluxbox.  Personally, I think that you would have to be using fairly old/low end hardware in order to notice the difference, but there is one.  Second, Openbox is fully compliant with the ICCCM and EWMH specifications while Fluxbox is not (currently, but they are getting closer with each SVN commit).
