### Post by Onyros on 2007-05-02
Nine Inch Nails - The Warning (from Year Zero)

It's a totally dissonant song, with no chorus, per se, with a whole lotta noise & distortion.

I love the way the lyrics follow the "melody", especially in one part where Trent sings:

```
I was standing right there
When it came down from the sky
They were expecting us
We felt it from ***inside***
``` and he suddenly adds depth to the entire thing, kinda like submerging the bass line (as if one were feeling it from inside, indeed).

Great song, tends to go unnoticed by most people listening to the album, though.

Kinda like my favourite song from The Fragile ("*I'm Looking Forward to Joining You, Finally*")
