### Post by arnieboy on 2006-10-08
> **Lord Illidan said:**
> Hey, seems good. Looks good too. I only wish it was fully GUId..without that xterm in the background, but hey..if it works..don't fix it! Downloading things as we speak!

But about Frostwire, it does work with Edgy...er...but not very well...it seems. Has some bugs...just opened it now :S Is this why it is incompatible?

we are not using xterm here (except for proprietary licenses).. AX2 used a python based terminal and we believe that every automated process should give out as much info as possible instead of trying to conceal the same. That is very much in line with the the philosophy of open source and Automatix. The more we hide, the more it induces paranoia and false impressions.
In fact AX2 logs everything that the terminal spits out in ~/.automatix/activity.log for future reference.
-Arnie
