### Post by Macskeeball on 2011-06-22
Seagate's STAE102 is the accessory you need to give your drive a FireWire 800 port.  You'll find it on here: [http://www.seagate.com/www/en-us/products/external/external-hard-drive/portable-hard-drive#tTabContentAccessories](http://www.seagate.com/www/en-us/products/external/external-hard-drive/portable-hard-drive#tTabContentAccessories)

I prefer to buy drives that have FireWire 800, eSATA, and USB all built-in, and they come with cables for all of those. Your drive is weird.
