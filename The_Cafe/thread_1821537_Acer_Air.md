They're not, however that has nothing to do with the Air.

You see, the Macbook Air doesn't have a real i5 or i7 CPU. It uses a CULV version.

Desktop i5 > Mobile i5 > CULV i5.

Intel takes a decent desktop CPU, hobbles it by making it use less power and put out less heat, and then does it again to come up with the CULV chip.

Combined with a CULV motherboard and other CULV components, you end off with one sluggish computer.
