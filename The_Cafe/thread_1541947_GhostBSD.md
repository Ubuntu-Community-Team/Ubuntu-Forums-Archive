> What is GhostBSD.

GhostBSD it is a Gnome based FreeBSD distribution  in a form of a live CD. In future GhostBSD is being worked on to be installable on a disk.

Why GhostBSD.

There are no new user friendly distributions of FreeBSD based on gnome.The GhostBSD project&#8217;s goal is to improve Gnome desktop environment  on a  FreeBSD system.[http://goo.gl/aWbw](http://goo.gl/aWbw)
