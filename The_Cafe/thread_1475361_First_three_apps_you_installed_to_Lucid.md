### Post by standingwave on 2010-05-06
Assuming a new install, of course. 

[Gnome Commander]("http://www.nongnu.org/gcmd/") - I just can't give up my Norton styled [OFM]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orthodox_file_manager#Orthodox_file_managers"). Been using one in one form or another for almost twenty-five years. Hard to believe that the same company that produced such an elegant file manager now makes Norton Antivirus.

[Grpn]("http://lashwhip.com/grpn.html") - I use Google calculator mostly these days but I think better in  RPN. And yeah, I programmed in [Forth]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Forth_%28programming_language%29") in the old days.

[FooBilliard]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FooBillard") - My favorite OpenGL game. Just one glitch. If possible, the AI will always foul  the eight ball behind the head string, giving the human play the win by default.

Others:

[Googlizer]("http://www.linuxplanet.com/linuxplanet/opinions/3198/1/") - So handing to just highlight text from any app and launch a google search  with a single click.

[Stellarium]("http://www.stellarium.org/") - looking up...

[Google Earth]("http://www.jonathanmoeller.com/screed/?p=1613") - looking down...

[Filelight]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Filelight") - for figuring out what's hogging my drive space.

[Sauerbraten]("http://sauerbraten.org/") - I'm not a big gamer but when I want a fifteen minute break I like to work through one of the levels without getting dinged.

[GIMP]("http://www.gimp.org/") - I'm just used to it I guess. 

[Google Calendar Prism App]("http://micke.hallendal.net/blog/google-calendar-and-prism/") - I like having a separate app, especially since the odds of the calendar successfully loading within my GMail page is less than 50/50 for some reason.

[Chromium]("http://micke.hallendal.net/blog/google-calendar-and-prism/") - because it's fast and I like the minimal interface.
