Here's the thing about 11.04, if you want to use Unity, 11.04 isn't a good way to be introduced to it. I have 11.04 on my work computer, but use the old Gnome2 option. Unity just seemed horribly frustrating on 11.04, and I didn't have the patience or time (a few months ago) to fuss with it.

But now I also have 11.10 installed, and am using it more and more as I learn my way around in Unity. It has helped to have a safe place to go back to (which, in my view, is the only real value of Natty right now) when I muck things up in 11.10.

Unity is still not my favorite thing in the world, but it's actually fun to watch it mature (somewhat). Right now Gnome 3 is more fun and looks very pretty. I think it's important to learn both of them. 

When the dust settles, I'm thinking we'll have some great choices available.
