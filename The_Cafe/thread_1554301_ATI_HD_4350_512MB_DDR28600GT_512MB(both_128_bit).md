### Post by Zorgoth on 2010-08-17
8600 GT is a faster card with better drivers.

However, I would recommend looking for a better card.

I don't know how it is over there, but here you can pick up a lot of cards that are better for under 75 USD - GT 220, GT 240, 9600 GT, Radeon HD 4670.  If you play any 3D games at all, it will be worth the difference easily - most important thing is make sure the memory is fast - don't buy a card with DDR2, and prefer GDDR3-GDDR5 if you can get it.
