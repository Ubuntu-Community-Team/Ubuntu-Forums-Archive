### Post by CraigPaleo on 2009-02-04
If you're planning to put Ubuntu on it. Here's a list of supported hardware: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/)

Where to get it? Shop around and do Google searches for the individual components I suppose, and build your own machine. 

I don't know of any forums or a how-to but if you stick around, I'm sure others will chime in. 

My brother built his own in a day after he got the parts, though it was an XP machine. He didn't have any experience. You could build much more than a few over the next two years, whether they be Linux or Windows machines.
