### Post by cmnorton on 2007-08-05
I have passed LPI 101, and am taking exam 102 soon.

i purchased long distance learning from LinuxCertified.com. It is expensive; geared to RedHat; and is installation/configuration based. The production of the audio visual is very good.

I also purchased three books certification books. In addition, I already had a copy of Linux in a Nutshell O'Reilly:

LPI Linux Certification O'Reilly
LPIC-1 Linux Professional Institute Certification Cybex
Exam Cram 2/LPIC I

The latter two books come with exam engines, which I have found very helpful. O'Reilly has very good chapeter and final exam questions. Basically, I have been at this since February earlier this year, and I am throwing myself into studying for and taking the second exam.
