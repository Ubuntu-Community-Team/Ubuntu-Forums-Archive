I have two Thinkpads, an R61i and X61.  They're both pretty much "Intel inside". Both were plug -n- play with Ubuntu.  Wireless works with no fussing,  screen brightness keys work,  just a good experience.  I haven't tried to get the fingerprint reader to work;  that was iffy in Windows.

I would be sure I could return anything purchased new for your budget.  'Oddball'(cheap) hardware can be a hassle with Ubuntu.
