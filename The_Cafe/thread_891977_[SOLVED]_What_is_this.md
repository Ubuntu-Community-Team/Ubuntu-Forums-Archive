### Post by tom66 on 2008-08-16
Possibly a Winmodem, because of the lack of modem hardware and the phone jack. The modem signal is decoded by software. Winmodems are difficult to install because of this.

Found it - it's a Conexant KB5815G
Device type: Modem (ISDN, 56k)

Look on the PCB.
