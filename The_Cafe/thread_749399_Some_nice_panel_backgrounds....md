### Post by Chokkan on 2008-04-09
> **myusername said:**
> I didn't know that you could change the xfce panel... How do you do it?

I edited my .gtkrc-2.0 and set the entry 
```

style "panel"
{
bg_pixmap[NORMAL] = ".panelbackground.png"
}
```I then put an image in my home directory. I just made a 1px wide image of the same height as my panel with a gradient or sth. Still trying to find something that looks good.
