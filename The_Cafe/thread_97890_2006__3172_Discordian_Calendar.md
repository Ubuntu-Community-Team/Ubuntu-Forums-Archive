### Post by Rev. Nathan on 2005-12-01
As you may or may not know, typing 'ddate' in your terminal will give you the current Discordian date. Following the same calendar outline as the gregorian calendar (the common, 365 day, 12 month, 7 days in a week calendar most of us use today), it's been remodeled slightly around events in Discordianism. Anyways, I've grown a liking to it, but you cannot buy a Discordian calendar in a store. So I've made a printer-friendly version of the calendar, complete with alignments to the gregorian dates, United States-observed holidays, and the Discordian holidays. It will print out on traditional US letter paper and A4 paper.

Season 1 (Chaos), Page 1: [http://www.bigrevmedia.com/coronets/chaos1.pdf](http://www.bigrevmedia.com/coronets/chaos1.pdf)
Season 1 (Chaos), Page 2: [http://www.bigrevmedia.com/coronets/chaos2.pdf](http://www.bigrevmedia.com/coronets/chaos2.pdf)

I learned about the Discordian calendar here, so I thought this would be the place with the largest following to it. I only did one month, as to not over work myself if no one is intrested in these. Give me some feedback if you like 'em. :)
