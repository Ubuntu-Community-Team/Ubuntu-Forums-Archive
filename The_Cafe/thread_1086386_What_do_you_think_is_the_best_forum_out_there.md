***[Edit:]** (which is a strange thing for a vegetarian to say isn't it? lol) /edit*

They started off by introducing me to Linux, after that they introduced me to other people's thoughts, feelings & predispositions via the now defunct Backyard sub-forum. 

After that they helped educate me with regard to other operating systems/distro's/BSD's, in the now defunct Other OS Talk sub-forum.

We feel that the wondrous UF's have lost some of the heart that we valued so much, though I'm sure that this is just a selfish feeling that has been motivated by an emotional response due to the losses of community that the few of us have been experiencing due to these changes.

Fortunately we have a new Other OS Talk forum now, which has certainly helped our tiny sub-community to survive & hopefully grow into the future.
