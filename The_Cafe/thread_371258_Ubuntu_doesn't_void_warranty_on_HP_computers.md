### Post by purplearcanist on 2007-02-26
Interested in buying a new computer, but disturbed by reports of Ubuntu voiding the warranty, I contacted HP customer service to see if installing Ubuntu would void it.  Here is the reply I got:

> That [Ubuntu] will not void the warranty but any part stops working due to the operating System [Ubuntu] will not be covered.

I understand why they wouldn't cover a part due to the operating system, since the part can break because of a corrupt driver.

Just wanted to show you guys.:)
