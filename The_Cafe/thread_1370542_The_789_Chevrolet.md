### Post by Uncle Spellbinder on 2010-01-02
1957, 1958 & 1959 All Rolled Into One!
 
This car was built by [**N2A Motors**](http://www.n2amotors.com/) (No Two Alike).  

The company is planning a production run of about 100 vehicles. It sits on a Corvette C6 chassis, front styled like a 57 Chevy, side like a 58, rear like a 59.
Hence the designation "789."
