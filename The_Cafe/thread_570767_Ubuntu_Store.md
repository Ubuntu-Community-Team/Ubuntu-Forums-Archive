### Post by Tomosaur on 2007-10-08
On the 'contact us' page they say this:

> 
[B]Q. Are the items on the Canonical Shop Organic/Fair Trade? 
[/B]A. Currently the products are not Organic or Fair Trade, however all our items are ethically sourced and produced and we will never use any source that uses child labour.

I imagine 'ethically sourced' means that no sweatshops are used. It may be worth asking them via this page: [https://shop.canonical.com/contact_us.php](https://shop.canonical.com/contact_us.php) to make sure :)
