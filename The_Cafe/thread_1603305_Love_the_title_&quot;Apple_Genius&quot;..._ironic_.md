### Post by youbuntu on 2010-10-22
I was just thinking about the Apple "Genius" guys & girls @ their stores, and how very inaccurate and ironic a title that is. Firstly, Stephen Hawking & Einstein are (and was) geniuses, not some dude in a blue tee shirt, who can backup your hdd, and advise you how to repair permissions (probably using Disk Utility - n000b!).

I have nothing at *all* against the nice people who work for Apple, NOR am I "anti Apple" for any reason; I am sitting here on a 2009 MacBook (Maverick :)) but I find it crazy that you'd call what amounts to a technical advisor, a "genius". I once asked one of them about something trivial in terminal, and he replied "Oh, that's a little beyond our level of knowledge"...

:D LMAO!

A lot of my friends call *me* a genius, just because I can do a little toe-dipping in basic code, and can control most of my computer with bash. I think the term is relative, to my friends, as they obviously imagine me to be some kind of guy with computer super powers... LOL! Far from it, and I often question how much I know. 

Genius I ain't.
