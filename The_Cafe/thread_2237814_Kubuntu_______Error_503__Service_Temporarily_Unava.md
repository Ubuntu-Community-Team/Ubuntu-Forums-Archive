### Post by marco-parillo on 2014-08-05
On [KFN]("https://www.kubuntuforums.net/showthread.php?66052-Error-503-Service-Temporarily-Unavailable&#8207;&p=355590&viewfull=1#post355590"), this was recently posted:

[COLOR=#000000][FONT=sans-serif]If you trigger too many 403 errors, zbblock will lock you out for a time period (default being 24 hours, IIRC)...during that time you'll only get 503.[/FONT][/COLOR]

[COLOR=#000000][FONT=sans-serif]Notice that every connection attempt during the lock out will reset the time period (extending the lock out another 24 hours)[/FONT][/COLOR]

[COLOR=#000000][FONT=sans-serif]If you make no connection attempts in 24+ hours, you'll be allowed back in (an admin can also clear the block for you)[/FONT][/COLOR]
