Hello people,

As 13.10 is getting closer, it's time to announce the Lubuntu community wallpaper contest.

The contest will decide five additional wallpapers that will be included by default with Lubuntu 13.10.

Submissions are done through this Flickr group: [http://www.flickr.com/groups/lubuntu1310contest/](http://www.flickr.com/groups/lubuntu1310contest/)

And you'll find all the necessary information about the contest there as well.

Polling will start ASAP after the submission deadline and will be announced to the public.

Please help us spread the word and good luck to all contestants!
