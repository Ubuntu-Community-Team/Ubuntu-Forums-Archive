### Post by -grubby on 2010-08-24
Dial-up is 56 kilo**bits** per second, and it downloads at  about 7 kilo**bytes** per second. Your download speed, 48 kilo**bytes** per second, is nowhere near that slow, and its equivalent to 390 kilo**bits** per second (you multiply the bytes by 8, or divide the bits by 8 to get the other.)
