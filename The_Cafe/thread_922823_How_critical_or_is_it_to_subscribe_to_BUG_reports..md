### Post by KIAaze on 2008-09-17
From my personal experience, the only bugs that were worth subscribing to were the bugs I found by searching manually on the net (or which I submitted myself of course).

Because those are usually the bugs that really bother you and to which you want a solution.

The other bugs, the ones where you suddenly get a crash report while you have no idea what crashed, are not worth subscribing too IMO.
That being said, I still subscribe to them anyway since they get labelled automatically for me in gmail, so getting a lot of them is not a big issue for me. (they don't get a lot of comments anyway)

Also, since you can always unsubscribe from a bug if you think you get to many mails because of it, I don't see where the problem is. ;)
