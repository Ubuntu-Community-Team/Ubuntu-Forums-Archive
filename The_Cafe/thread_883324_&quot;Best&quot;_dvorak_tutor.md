### Post by NovaAesa on 2008-08-16
What I really found the best was just typing on forums and whatnot. If you spend lots of time on a computer, your typing should start to become "not painful" in less than 4 or 5 days (at least that's what it was like for me). My best piece of advice is to make sure you are using the correct fingers for the right keys. This makes it easier to remeber where the keys are initially and will make your typing faster overall once you are done learning.
