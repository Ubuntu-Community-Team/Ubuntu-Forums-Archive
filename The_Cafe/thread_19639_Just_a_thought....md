### Post by mark on 2005-03-12
I'm one of the Hoary/nVidia users that got caught by the kernel update "oops!" yesterday.  I must say, one of the things that impresses me most about the free and open software community (Ubuntu in particular!) is that, instead of panicking, I said "give it a day or so...".  I booted back into Warty to do what I needed to do.

Today, I come home from work, fire up Hoary in recovery mode, run *apt-get update && apt-get dist-upgrade*, reboot - and problem solved.

I just don't see commercial (ahem) OSes responding that quickly.

To the Ubuntu devs, once again, "thanks, people!".

Mark
