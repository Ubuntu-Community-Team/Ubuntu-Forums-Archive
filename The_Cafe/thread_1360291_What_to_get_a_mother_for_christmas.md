### Post by LowSky on 2009-12-20
Give Cash if you Dont know, gift cards are today's version of Fruit cake. Gift card means you had no idea, but you think that *_INSERT STORE NAME HERE_* is a good choice for buying whatever amount you think the gift should have been. At least cash says " Here is*_INSERT NUMBER HERE_* dollar[s], buy whatever you like."

Personally I find getting something for them that they would never do but still in ther comfort zone is the best gifts you can give. Weekend stay at a Local bed and breakfast, maybe tickets to some event they might like, maybe paint the house or a just room like the kitchen, or maybe just ask them what they would really want or like from you.
