### Post by Legendary_Bibo on 2011-11-02
Using a blindfold won't actually simulate being blind. For one thing you've already seen the world around you so your mind has already built that world in your head. Sub consciously if your mind can't receive visual information, it'll fall back to your memory (think about how you can get around the house when it's pitch black). If you have no memory then it panics (imagine being in a dark cave) which is what happens when you can see your whole life. If you're blind for life your mind has no visual memory of the world, and it cannot receive visual information, therefore it relies more on your other senses. Also, because they're relying more on those other senses, their minds train them to become better (hence, the whole heightened senses thing (i.e. better hearing)).
