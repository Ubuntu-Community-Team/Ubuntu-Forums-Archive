### Post by 1comment on 2008-03-28
I put ubuntu on my 512MB usb =D

It wasn't easy for me... I had to mix together things from pendrivelinux.com and some article on livecd customisation (might have been [http://www.debuntu.org/book/export/html/216](http://www.debuntu.org/book/export/html/216) , but I can't remember now)

By taking the windows apps, non-English locale files, help files and open-office out, i managed to squeeze it down to 510MB total (and i even installed the compizconfig-settings-manager *and* the nvidia restricted driver!)

It boots super-fast, thanks to almost nonexistent flash seek times, and because it boots read-only, it doesn't chew through the 10,000-100,000 flash memory write cycles.
