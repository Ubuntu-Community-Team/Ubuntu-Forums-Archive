### Post by Paqman on 2010-05-10
Looks like our Windows using friends could be finding a super fast-booting stripped down copy of [Ubuntu Light]("http://www.canonical.com/products/unity") pre-installed on their netbooks some time in the future.

Interesting snippet from Mark Shuttleworth:
> We wanted to be surfing the web in under 10 seconds, and give people a fantastic web experience. *We also wanted it to be possible to upgrade from that limited usage model to a full desktop.*

(emphasis mine)

Generic images won't be available for download, as Canonical will be customising it for each OEM's particular hardware.
