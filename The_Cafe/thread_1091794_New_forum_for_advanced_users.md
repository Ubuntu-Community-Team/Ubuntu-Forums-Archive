### Post by parnote on 2009-03-09
There's a new forum that just started (within the last week) dedicated to finding solutions for Linux. It's called "HackMy..." and is located here: [http://hackmy.pclosbe.org/index.php]("http://hackmy.pclosbe.org/index.php"). It's dedicated to ALL Linux users. Please, come join in on the discussions and help find solutions to problems plaguing all Linux users. Just remember, the moderators won't tolerate any "fanboi" comments, nor will they tolerate any distro bashing. There is a separate section for Ubuntu, Debian, openSUSE, PCLinuxOS, and others. The moderators formulated this forum to help bring the Linux community together to find and share common solutions to problems and issues that plague the Linux community as a whole, and help one another.
