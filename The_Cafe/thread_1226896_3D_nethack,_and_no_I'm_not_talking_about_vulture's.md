### Post by Dustin2128 on 2009-07-30
fans of nethack that hate it's primitive interface and feel that vulture's/falcon's hasn't gone quite far enough (dang that was a mouthfull) rejoice! I found this project on sourceforge that replaces the 2d sprites of vulture's/falcon's into actual 3d models. I just tested it on wine and it looks like a nice start. plenty of nice animations, very smooth. Sound effects too. However, every character looks like a samuri :confused: and it needs some major improvement on a lot of the models and the interface. but aside from that, wow. here's the site: [http://sourceforge.net/projects/nethack3d/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/nethack3d/)
