### Post by imbrandon on 2007-10-05
UbuntuWire is a search engine, powered by Google, which searches Ubuntu and Kubuntu related sites. This is useful if you are starting to use Ubuntu and experience difficulty but find immense difficulty trawling through pages of unrelated Google results!

UbuntuWire, meanwhile, searches a small selection of Ubuntu/Kubuntu-related domains like ubuntuforums.org ; this gives you a smaller amount of results but they are likely to be more related to what you're searching for.

In all fairness to Google, they try to accommodate Linux, OS X, Windows and BSD users by providing specialised search engines for them all. Sadly, this doesn't quite cut it for Linux or BSD users as they have a number of different "distributions" which each differ a great deal.

Comments and Sugestions very very welcome.
