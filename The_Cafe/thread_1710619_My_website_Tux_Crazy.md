### Post by anchorschmidt on 2011-03-20
I think that this is a good place to introduce my website. I created a Linux website in November which I feel is now good enough to be shown to people. The URL is [tux-crazy.com]("http://www.tux-crazy.com")

Tux Crazy is a website for Linux users by a Linux user and has a clean layout, ads are kept out of your way as my main motivation is qiving people a good experience. I try to keep my articles at a high quality. I normally release articles every 4-5 days or so but due to recent exams, the interval has lengthened to 6-12 days with no compromise made to quality. I don't do a half hearted job at anything and I test everything that I write about. There are some Windows articles but my main audience is Linux users.

It's for everyone, from skilled users to less skilled users. I hope that you find something that you may not have known before. If you are interested, please go to the All Articles section and see if you find anything interesting. Please let me know what you think.

Thank you
