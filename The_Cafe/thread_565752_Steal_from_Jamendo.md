### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-10-02
Well, it's not stealing if Jamendo is using bittorrent to distribute the music.  Perhaps there are not enough seeders for bittorrent to be effective for this site.

This is not rocket science, ninja, or super ninja:

Take the Listen Link and paste it in the command line:

This is the contents for jamendo-playlist.m3u  (m3u files are playlist files):

#EXTM3U
#EXTINF:209,75 Clan : Money - Money
[http://stream5-6.jamendo.com/7371/ogg2/01%20-%2075%20Clan%20-%20Money.ogg](http://stream5-6.jamendo.com/7371/ogg2/01%20-%2075%20Clan%20-%20Money.ogg)

Perhaps:

>wget [http://stream5-6.jamendo.com/7371/ogg2/01%20-%2075%20Clan%20-%20Money.ogg](http://stream5-6.jamendo.com/7371/ogg2/01%20-%2075%20Clan%20-%20Money.ogg)

### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-10-10
To get a playlist file, click on the small speaker icon and it will open a jamendo music player with a list of tracks.  At the bottom of the player is a link: export to:  Podcast or m3u.  Choose m3u and you will get a small download *jamendo-playlist.m3u*

Open the m3u file and it looks like:

#EXTM3U
#EXTINF:312,DJ Mark Hollinger : The Evolution is a Lie (album) - HeavAnthem (extended club version)
[http://stream3-4.jamendo.com/3797/mp31/03%20-%20HeavAnthem%20extended%20club%20version.mp3](http://stream3-4.jamendo.com/3797/mp31/03%20-%20HeavAnthem%20extended%20club%20version.mp3)

>wget (highlite and paste the link with the middle mouse button)

Now, there are probably easier ways to do this, but I'll leave others to figure it out.
