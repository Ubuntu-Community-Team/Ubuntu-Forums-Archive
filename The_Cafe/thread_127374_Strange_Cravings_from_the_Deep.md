### Post by Maupertus on 2006-02-08
I was browsing a distro list and although I know I'm making things hard on myself by running an AMD64, I just wanted to ask:

Am I the only one that wants to try them all?

I mean, it's very nice to have a Distro chooser and although one of the reasons I want to use Linux is the Opensource aspect and hence a couple of distro's will never be used out of principle, I can't help wanting to try them all out.

I've used SuSe way back, and I just read somethings about the new boxed book+cd set and started to get "the urge" and I got the same vibe when I visited the Arch and Gentoo sites.

There are two reasons for this 'craving'.
One, it's to easy to install a new distro, at least in most cases, it's no problem at all, so that's no hurdle to take.
Two, there's to much to choose! Ubuntu is now the only Distro I use on this system, and I'm content with it, but I keep wondering how some of the others would be.

In a way I feel like some straying husband, although I'm happily married, I'm looking at everything that's coming my way ;)

So I was wondering if I was the only one with this ailment.
