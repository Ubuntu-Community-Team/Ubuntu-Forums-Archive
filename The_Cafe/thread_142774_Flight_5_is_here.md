### Post by welsh_spud on 2006-03-11
> Is dapper basicly just new packages? Or what 

Dapper is the next version of Ubuntu. When it reaches stability (sometime just after April) you will be able to upgrade to it as if it were a package by dist-upgrading.

When you upgrade, your computer will download a shedfull of files and other hoo-haa. It will reboot once or twice and then dump you back at your desktop. All your programs you installed in Breezy will still be there in Dapper.

Going from Breezy > Dapper is like going from Windows 2000 > Windows XP, except without the install CD (you can use a CD if you wish though).

Can't wait for Dapper \\:D/
