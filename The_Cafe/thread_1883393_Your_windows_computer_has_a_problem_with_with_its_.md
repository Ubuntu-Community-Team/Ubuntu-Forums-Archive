Had that call here, but it was on my SIL's phone.

Them: *(Indian accent)* Good morning, I am calling from Microsoft about your computer.
Me: No you're not!
Them: Not what, sir?
Me: You're not from Microsoft.
Them: Yes I am.
Me: OK then, I'll play your game.
Them: What game?
Me: This scam game you're playing.  What do you want me to do?

Silence ........ Hang up!

They rang again a few weeks later,  but on our other phone.

Them: *(Indian accent)* Good morning, I am calling from Microsoft about your computer.
Me: Oh good, let me go and sit in front of it.

*In the meantime, I pick up my mobile phone*
Me: *(Press a few buttons on the mobile whilst holding the mobile close to the phone.)* OK, how can you help?
Them: What was all the beeps?
Me: Oh, that's nothing to worry about.  I have just connected this call so it can be traced by the authorities.
Them: *Click*

Haven't had another call since.
