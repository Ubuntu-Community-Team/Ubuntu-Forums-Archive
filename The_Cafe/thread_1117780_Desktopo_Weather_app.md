### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2009-04-06
Right click on your clock and go to Preferences. Make sure it is set to show weather in the General tab, then add some locations! (It expects the first one you add to be your home). Doesn't do forecasts, but it does weather with style :)

### Post by mgmiller on 2009-04-06
If you want one with forecasts, and you like it in the top panel, just right click the panel and "add to panel" and choose "Weather Report"  I love it, is has the next 5 days or so of forecasts and even a radar map.

If you want a nice one on your desktop, try installing screenlets from synaptic and turn on the "Smooth Weather"  or "Weather" applet.
