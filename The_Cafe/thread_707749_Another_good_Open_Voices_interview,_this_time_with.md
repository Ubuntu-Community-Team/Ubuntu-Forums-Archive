Jim Zemlin's Open Voices podcast has [another episode](http://linux-foundation.org/weblogs/openvoices/2008/02/24/open-voices-podcast-with-mark-shuttleworth/), this time an interview with Mark Shuttleworth. ### Post by k2t0f12d on 2008-02-25
Actually I think the interview with Shuttleworth was much more insightful.  Chatting with Linus provided a great insight into the ideal free software development paradigm, but Shuttleworth has taken that paradigm and described it in its larger context on the world stage.  His points were brilliantly concise, both in the fairly self-evident properties of free software and the world market, but also in some of the very subtle relationships that do not present to the casual user.
