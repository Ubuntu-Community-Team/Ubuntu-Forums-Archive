I'd second that, Arch will fit your needs perfectly. It would definitely be lighter than Ubuntu. Just remember to follow the beginner guide and you shouldn't go wrong: [http://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Beginners_Guide](http://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Beginners_Guide)
