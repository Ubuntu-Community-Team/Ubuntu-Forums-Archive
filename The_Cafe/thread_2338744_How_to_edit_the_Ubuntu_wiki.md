### Post by PaulW2U on 2016-10-01
> **saverio.miroddi said:**
> How can I edit it? Should I get permissions from somebody? Who? Or should I send a request? Is there a bug tracker?
Due to several prolonged and severe spam attacks on the wiki editing of wiki pages has been restricted in the main to members of the [Ubuntu Wiki Editors]("https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-wiki-editors") Launchpad group. By all means apply for membership but if you have no history on Launchpad or no-one can vouch for you then you might find that your membership is refused.

By the way, [Ubuntu Members]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership") and Canonical employees also have edit access.

If you are not granted membership then you could send an email to the [ubuntu-doc]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-doc") mailing list to request the change that you wish to make. There's no point creating a bug report for a wiki page as it's not official Ubuntu documentation.
