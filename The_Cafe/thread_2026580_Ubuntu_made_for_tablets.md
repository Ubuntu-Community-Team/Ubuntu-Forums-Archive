### Post by mr john on 2012-07-15
To have a successful tablet you have to have the apps as well as a good user interface. Blackberry had a great user interface and good hardware specs, but fell over when it came to the availability of apps. Even if the main interface of Ubuntu was touch screen friendly, most of the existing apps for Ubuntu were designed for non-touch  systems. For Ubuntu to be successful on the tablet platform there would  need to be thousands of touch friendly apps.  Google Play already has hundreds of thousands of touch friendly apps available, so Ubuntu would be wayyyyyyyyy behind the major players .
