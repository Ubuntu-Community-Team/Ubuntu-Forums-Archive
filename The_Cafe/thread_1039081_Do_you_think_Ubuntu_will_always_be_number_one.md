### Post by gnomeuser on 2009-01-14
No, I have seen distros come and go, at one time the very same question was posed about Gentoo, Mandrake (now Mandriva) and Red Hat. 

I think that to stick around and have a defining presence Canonical will need to contribute some more. Right now ever technical decision being made is largely done without Ubuntu since there is nearly no code coming from here.
