### Post by user1397 on 2010-01-31
I deleted my facebook account successfully one time.

First I individually removed all of my friends, then deleted all of my apps, then any and all information in the about me part, then deleted all my messages one by one, then changed my name to a random name.

Basically it was stripped bare, except for wall posts (there were too many to delete individually, so I said screw it)

Then I went here: [http://www.facebook.com/help/contact.php?show_form=delete_account](http://www.facebook.com/help/contact.php?show_form=delete_account)

to permanently delete it.

If you don't log in for 2 weeks after deleting it, it IS permanently deleted.
I don't know if the servers still keep some info, but I don't care either way too much.

Also for good measure you can delete your email associated with your facebook account and just get a new one (I did that for several reasons, one being I got scammed that week :()
