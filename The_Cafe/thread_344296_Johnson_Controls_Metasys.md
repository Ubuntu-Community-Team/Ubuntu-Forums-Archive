### Post by scoot714 on 2008-02-13
Hi,
I applaud your effort in taking ownership of your Metasys system, but be aware of a few things. If you have no knowledge of HVAC systems, you are taking on a monumental task. Even if you do have some HVAC background, maintaining a Metasys system without any formal training will be very difficult. That's not to say it can't be done, but you must understand that Johnson Controls fully expects their customers to attend formal Johnson Controls provided training classes to teach them how to operate their systems. If you are serious about taking over responsibility for this system, I seriously recommend JC training. In the meantime, here is where you will find ALL JC literature: [http://cgproducts.johnsoncontrols.com](http://cgproducts.johnsoncontrols.com)
 
Good Luck!
