### Post by darweth on 2007-05-03
The internet is a scary scary world these days.  I miss the simpler times of old before the blogosphere exploded and "web 2.0" was in full effect.  Surfing the net is so difficult these days.  I have RSS feeds flying at me from every corner.  Then I finally read something and am bombarded with hyperlinks to other blogs and other sites.  All kinds of videos and mashups.  Then there are 4,000 comments to browse through and digest.  There are buttons to digg here and reddit there and newvibe on the left.  I get motion sickness just browsing the web these days.  Everything is so overwhelming and dense.  And the sad part is 99% of it is superficial and useless, but you still feel that urge or need to waste your life perusing it anyhow.  ARGH!!!  Does anyone else ever feel this way?  I know I can just stop or fight back this urge to attempt to digest everything but sometimes I feel like I should just blow up my PC and get back to a real life.  I seriously wonder how anything ever gets done anymore.  I mean... high-tech professionals are blogging all day, scoping all this out, etc.  WTF?
