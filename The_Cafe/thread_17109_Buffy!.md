Alright, as will become obvious, I'm a big fan.  Really big.  Unhealthy, really.

And as for relationships:  Buffy has horrible relationships every time.  They're not bad guys, she's just bad at being with anyone, friends included.  She is, by far, my least favorite character. I admit it, I cried when she died...), and Kaylee.  And every time, that girl is my favorite character.  Ok, so I'm a sucker for socially impaired women who are actually really, really good at what they do.  I guess maybe I identify a bit.  Well, with the socially impaired part, at least...

Buffy, Angel, and Firefly are all brilliant.  Hooray, Joss!
