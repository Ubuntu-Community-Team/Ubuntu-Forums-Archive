### Post by G Morgan on 2007-03-09
You have to expect an English winner given the advantage in numbers in the quarter finals. United have found that which made them a great team in the past. I don't know how Ferguson got them out of playing also ran Football and back into this sort of ruthlessness but they are serious opposition who are quickly becoming favourites in games the stats suggest they should lose 9-0. They are back to where they were when you just knew that they would win in the last minute in spite of the opposition dominating them.

Can't see Roma troubling them too much but Bayern or Milan would provide a test.

I think the same goes for Liverpool and Chelsea, they are both favourites in their ties. Obviously the interpretations of the rules can again derail the English teams as the game suddenly becomes a tame non-contact affair. Who the referees are is vital.

Anyway I expect a Man Utd v Chelsea final though either Milan or Bayern on one hand and maybe Valencia or Liverpool on a good day could upset that. To knock out either team will require absolute ruthlessness though, it's not enough to dominate against them since they will convert almost everything that is offered to them. A few teams need to realise that possession is meaningless if the opposition is capable of breaking away and scoring or putting away a set piece.

Still think the game has gotten too soft in the last few years. It wasn't exactly prison rules 6/7 years ago but the 99 season was much tougher than anything I've seen since some in Europe have castrated the game.
