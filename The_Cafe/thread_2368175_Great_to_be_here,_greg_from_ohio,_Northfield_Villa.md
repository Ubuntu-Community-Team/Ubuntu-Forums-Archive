Hello!

Ubuntu.com is a commercial site for Canonical's OS, Ubuntu.

Ubuntuforums.org is a community user support forum.  It was originally started as a personal project by a user called ubuntu-geek, who personally paid for the hosting.  In time, the UF caught the eye of Canonical.  By the end of 2004, Canonical liked it so much it was made the official forums.  In 2006, Canonical graciously assumed hosting.  In 2007, the forums' domain name, license, and assets were all transferred to Canonical, which now maintains sole ownership.  But the Ubuntu Forums are not "run" by Canonical.  We are an all-volunteer user-to-user community -- including the Staff and Admins.  

You may edit your posts, but you may not alter your profile until you have reached the minimum required posts.  Please do not post chaff to get there.  We do not cotton to that.
