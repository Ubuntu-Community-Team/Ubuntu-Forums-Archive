### Post by chriskin on 2010-01-29
is there any way to make all bold letters on a big text file into italic? (other than change them one phrase at a time of course)

edit : never mind,  i found it

click on find and replace 
click on advanced
choose the "find" option and then on "format" select what it is like
choose the "replace" option and then on "format" select what you want to turn into

click on "replace" or "replace all"
