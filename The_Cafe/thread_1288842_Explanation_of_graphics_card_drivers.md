Like the Pro's and Con's of:

NVIDIA Open Source

NVIDIA Proprietary

ATI Open Source

ATI Proprietary

Do you want something that (always) WORKS on Nvidia?  Use the proprietary driver

Do you want to make a FOSS statement on Nvidia?  i.e. A driver that doesn't always work (i.e. 3d), but it at least is open source?  Use the OS drivers that sort of work in many situations.

Are you stuck with an ATi card?  Use whatever works.  The ATi proprietary linux drivers are developed by a room full of monkeys.  The OS ATi drivers barely work some of the time also.
