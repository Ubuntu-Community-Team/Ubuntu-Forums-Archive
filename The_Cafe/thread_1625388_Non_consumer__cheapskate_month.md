### Post by weasel fierce on 2010-11-19
So its close to 30 days to christmas, the holy grail of the consumer god.

As such, I am going to do an experiment this year.

Until December 25th, the following things will be in effect for me:

All music must be from bands that give it away for free online. 

All computer games must be freeware or open source.

All tabletop games I play (RPG and wargame) must be freeware games.

All movies or shows watched must be things that are available online for free.



The plan is to experience as much content as possible, hear new bands, try new things.. while spending as little as possible.
