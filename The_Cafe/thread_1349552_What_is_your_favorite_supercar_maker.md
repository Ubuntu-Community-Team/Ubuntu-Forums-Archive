The VN (1988 ), saw the first of the V6's in the Commodore's. Initially an Oz based GM Holden, modified version of the Buick engine, (which was apparently used in the States in front wheel drive configuration).

The VR (1993), saw the latest version of the Buick engine used in the Commodore's.

Then the VS (1995), saw the reworked Ecotec, Buick engine used.  This engine had many changes to the point where you could almost call it a new engine.

VZ (2004), saw the new 3.6 litre Alloytec V6, released. It has alloy heads, twin overhead cam's, & 4 valves per cylinder, more power/torque, & emits less pollution than the Ecotec, it also boasts similar fuel consumption.

I currently drive a VS, & new that it was the first with the Ecotec V6.

If nothing else I have now got the history of the V6's used by GM Holden, straight in my mind.
