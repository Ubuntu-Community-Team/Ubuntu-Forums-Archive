### Post by uberdonkey5 on 2009-01-12
Just a big 'whoooooooooaaaaaaah!' for ubuntu.

Most people do not convert from windows to linux because it is 'different' to windows (well, yeh. Why would you change to something that is the same??).

My girlfriend works in dance/theatre and rarely uses a computer. For documents she gets a friend to type them out in Word.

However, recently she wanted to use my computer, so I said fine, and she was working away on the internet and with open-office, and downloading files. Next thing I hear her talking to her friend on the mobile saying 'this is so much better than your computer, it just does everything I want straight away and it doesn't crash.'

She then had to explain to her friend that my computer doesn't have 'word' but can load and save word documents. Also, we both use modems for internet.

Well, her friend uses windows XP and has similar specs to my computer. I'm guessing her friend needs to defrag (or reinstall). But it is an interesting control group that shows that people DO like ubuntu if they are just not accustomed to windows. 

Doubt if its a real market opportunity (she'll just use my computer and get me to do her work now!), but I think definately if we want to increase the community, targetting children with ubuntu is a definate.
