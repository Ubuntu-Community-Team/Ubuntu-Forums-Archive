### Post by Bil-E-daKid on 2007-03-14
Oh - I've got one!

Years back, a Japanese company I was working for had a staff conference away in another city here in NZ.

One morning, I woke up early and went for a swim in the conference center pool - was early so I was the only one there.

I wear glasses so I had taken them off and left them with my towel over at the edge of the room.  After swimming some lengths, I stopped to take a breather and, while sitting at one end of the pool, I heard some people come in behind me.

Wondering who it was but not having my glasses, I peeked over the edge of the pool and stretched the edge of eyes using my index fingers - just to bring things a bit more into focus.

That's when I could see a group of about 8 Asian guys looking at me - looking like I was taking the piss out of them with my eye-stretch.
