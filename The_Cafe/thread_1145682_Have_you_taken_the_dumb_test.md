So the quantity idea is surely the "right" one. The correct answers are mostly ones that involve simple logic and a very careful reading of the question, rather than specific knowledge, like the value of old coins.

The "collector's value" is the *decoy* interpretation -- the one you're *supposed* to make, *wrongly*. In this case, there's also a decoy answer -- the one about melting them down. But the "real" answer is much simpler, requiring only the knowledge that 2007 is the largest number among the options.
