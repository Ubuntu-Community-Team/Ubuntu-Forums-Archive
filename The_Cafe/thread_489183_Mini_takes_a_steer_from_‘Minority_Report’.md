### Post by Ebuntor on 2007-07-01
I'm a little surprised large companies like these are actually still using RFID. Lots of experts and studies have show that RFID is a seriously flawed technology. A good example is the Guardian Project by the Vrije Universiteit.
They have shown that from 50 meters away it's very easy read data from a RFID chip, you can also write data to it. Securing it properly is very hard/almost impossible.

Having RFID in your mini is just one step, next we'll have all our personal data on RFID chips and I'm very worried about that. It's pretty surprising such  a completely flawed technology could get so popular.
