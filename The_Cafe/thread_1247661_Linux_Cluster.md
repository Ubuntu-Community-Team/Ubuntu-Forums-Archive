### Post by markbuntu on 2009-08-23
I met a guy at MIT who is using a linux cluster to run his solar powered super-collider project. He has a couple hundred 386 boards.

Hollywood uses linux clusters in their renderfarms to make all those CGI graphics. The small ones have over 2,000 quad core machines running 24/7 to render the frames for movies like Transformers and Lord of the Rings. They do like 30 frames a day for the easy stuff.
