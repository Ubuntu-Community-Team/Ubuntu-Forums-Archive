### Post by Mazza558 on 2007-06-01
In the misc options tab:

Offset Scale = 8.1221
Water Viscosity = 0.1900
Initial Vertices = 2587

In the "title wave" tab:

Amplitude = 1.00


These settings seem to yield the most realistic water you can get on Beryl. Try it out! :D
