### Post by Crunchy the Headcrab on 2010-03-31
No.  Most of the songs in the Ubuntu One Music Store are .mp3 with 256kpbs or greater quality.  There is no DRM on those files.  A few of them are still .wma and you will want to avoid those files because they are not even playable.
