### Post by NormanFLinux on 2011-03-21
At Haiku, they're working slowly towards an R2 release. They have decided to speed things up by porting much of the hardware support from BSD and Linux than re-invent the wheel. Its much harder forking over support for modern software from Unix and Unix-like based operating systems.

Its not there yet but it has the potential to be a greater OS system than BeOS was back in the day.
