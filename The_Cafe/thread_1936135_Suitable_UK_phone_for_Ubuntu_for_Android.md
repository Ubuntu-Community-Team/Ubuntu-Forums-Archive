### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-05
Watch this video demonstration

[http://www.v3.co.uk/v3-uk/news/2156344/-mwc-ubuntu-android-video-demo]("http://www.v3.co.uk/v3-uk/news/2156344/-mwc-ubuntu-android-video-demo")

Ignore the MS advert. It will get you talking to yourself. That is a sign of madness.

Note the questions asked during the demonstration. Listen to the replies.

This set up is not Android and Ubuntu on a smartphone. The Android OS and the Ubuntu OS both run on the same Linux kernel. That is a level of integration that cannot be done DIY. 

This is something that is embedded at the factory and is integrated with the hardware. It requires the phone to have 2GB of storage space to put Ubuntu in.

I am repeating what is being said during the demonstration.

I doubt very much that you could take a standard Ubuntu do this yourself.

For a start, do you have the right kind of dock?
