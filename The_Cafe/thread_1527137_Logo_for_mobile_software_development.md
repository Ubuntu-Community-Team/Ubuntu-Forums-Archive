### Post by theraje on 2010-07-08
Fair enough. :)

A popular image in gaming is that of Ye Olde Joystick... like back from the Atari 2600 days. You know - a box with a stick coming out of it... the stick usually being capped with a ball.

An interesting thought might be to make a light bulb be the stick. There are a number of logos one could make with that idea.

Apart from that, other entertainment icons could be used. Suit icons from a deck of playing cards or cube dice can make some really interesting designs. You could also try incorporating any of the above ideas into your company name (substituting a spade for a letter 'A', for example).

These are just off the top of my head. Go draft some rough sketches, and you may be surprised what you can come up with. :)
