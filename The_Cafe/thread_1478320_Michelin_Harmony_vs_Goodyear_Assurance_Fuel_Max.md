### Post by cariboo on 2010-05-09
All season tires are just a solution looking for a problem. They aren't that great in the summer or winter. They don't work very well in the mud or snow.

A better bet would be to buy a good set of summer and winter tires. In my part of Canada they haven't made it law that you have to have snow tires in the winter, but if you end up in the ditch running all season tires and the police attend, you get a fine.
