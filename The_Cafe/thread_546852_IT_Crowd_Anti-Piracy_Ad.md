### Post by trim17 on 2007-09-09
Notice how they did say "stop *buying* pirated movies." I think they're targetting off the street DVD sales very common in places like New York and San Francisco, and a lot of other cities around the world. The whole "they're copying electronics and medicine too!" comes from the hundreds of iPod knock-offs and DVD players and crappy trinkets. And counterfeit prescription medicine made by actual criminals.

Of course, you can help fight that piracy by downloading movies off the Internet, instead of buying them on the street and funding criminals. At least I think that's the message here. :)
