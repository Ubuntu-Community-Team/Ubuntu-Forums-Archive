### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-09-17
I have not used scribeon, but I have used Google Voice.  You leave yourself a voice message and you get back an email with a text transcription.  Works pretty well.  It depends on what your use case is.  Otherwise you could load Dragon Naturally Speaking in a virtual machine or run it with Wine.

There are some google summer-of-code projects that use the Google Voice transcription API's to provide a similar service, but you will have to search for them.  I don't know what level of maturity or usability for those programs.  The google voice transcription accuracy is pretty good without learning, for anyone that has used it on an Android phone.
