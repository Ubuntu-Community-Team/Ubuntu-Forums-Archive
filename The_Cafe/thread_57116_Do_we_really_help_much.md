I have been planning the mass-motivation of forum users to contribute to the wiki ([https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UserDocumentation](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UserDocumentation) and [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/forum](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/forum)) but forum administration has been in a standby mode for a month now...
