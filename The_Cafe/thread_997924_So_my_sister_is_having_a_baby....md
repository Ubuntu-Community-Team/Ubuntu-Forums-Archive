### Post by ColeJayStooks on 2008-11-30
Yep, it's happening this morning (Mountain Time), or maybe in the afternoon. Time seems to go slower now, waiting here, watching her hooked up to machines. I forgot to sleep last night; and the few nights before. It never really struck me before that my sister is having a baby, and now these are the final hours - the End of a long, physically brutal pregnancy and the Beginning of a new human life, one that awaits the fun roller coaster of emotion, growing up, innocents, and many other things.

It seems like only yesterday, we were just little tykes in the back yard on our trampoline, or a Christmas morning surprise, or playing a spook alley game in our grandmothers basement. Time goes by too fast; I can't wait to see a new childhood, a new progress of growing up. I can only imagine how things were years ago, the care free, wonderful childhood life we all shared.

Here's to you, sister; may your child bring you happiness, and your child have a happy, healthy life.
