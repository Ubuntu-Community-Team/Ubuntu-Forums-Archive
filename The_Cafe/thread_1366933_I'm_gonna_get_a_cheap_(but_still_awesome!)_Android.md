### Post by herbertportillo on 2009-12-29
I have a Samsung Moment. I got it about two months ago, and it has Android 1.5 but Sprint is going to upgrade it (and the HTC Hero) to Flan sometime in the first half of 2010.

I love the Samsung Moment. It's great. I chose it over the Hero because I didn't want a custom UI on Android made by a private company. I just wanted the standard Android UI and that's what the Moment uses, and it's fast and snappy. I also chose it over the Hero because it has a physical keyboard, and the physical keyboard makes texting and typing URLs waaaay easier. The only thing that sucks about the Moment is the battery life. I can drain it in about three hours, and that's just browsing the internet, listening to music, not particularly demanding tasks. But I hope that battery life will improve with the 2.1 upgrade.

Anyways, it's a pretty sweet phone. Take a look at it and see if you like it. Cheers.
