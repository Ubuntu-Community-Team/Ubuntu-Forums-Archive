### Post by pe7er on 2012-07-13
I hope that this is the right place to post this message...

In the Joomla (open source CMS) community I experienced how fun and educational user group meetings are. Unfortunately there is no Linux User Group in my hometown Nijmegen (Netherlands) so I decided to start one myself.

My goal is to meet with other Linux users to share Linux knowledge and Linux experience. It is not just for Ubuntu users but also to for other Linux distributions.

Personally I have experience with Ubuntu, Debian and Arch Linux.
And with plug computers (the SheevaPlug which runs on an ARM processor) and the Raspberry Pi.

If you live in or around Nijmegen, please join!
Website: [http://www.linuxnijmegen.nl/](http://www.linuxnijmegen.nl/)
Linkedin Group: [http://www.linkedin.com/groups/Linux-User-Group-Nijmegen-4475292/about](http://www.linkedin.com/groups/Linux-User-Group-Nijmegen-4475292/about)
