### Post by Dustin2128 on 2010-09-19
IMO you're ranting more about your laptop not being forward compatible. Do you expect to play playstation 3 games in a playstation 2? I don't expect to be able to stick a PCI express Geforce 295 in my desktop from 2003- AGP was the standard back then. The point is, though you could probably do it on, say, a 4 year old laptop, standards change a lot in 7 years. Should we all be using SVGA instead of DVI for compatibility reasons? Standards are updated for a reason. That said, I'm sorry about your laptop's failure, though I think I would replace the HDD in your situation.
