### Post by bruce89 on 2007-10-19
> **D-EJ915 said:**
> That looks nice, where could I get the GTK theme from?

It's just Clearlooks with modified colours.

~/.themes/Night Vision:
```
[Desktop Entry]
Name=Night vision
Type=X-GNOME-Metatheme
Comment=Night vision theme, saves the eye in the dark.
Encoding=UTF-8

[X-GNOME-Metatheme]
GtkTheme=Glossy
GtkColorScheme=fg_color:#000000000000,bg_color:#8b8b00000000,text_color:#eeee00000000,base_color:#000000000000,selected_fg_color:#000000000000,selected_bg_color:#eeee00000000
MetacityTheme=Glossy
IconTheme=Tangerine
```
