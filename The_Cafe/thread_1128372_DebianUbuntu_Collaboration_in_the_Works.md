### Post by ghindo on 2009-04-17
Mark Shuttleworth's latest [blog post]("http://www.markshuttleworth.com/archives/288") mentions an exciting prospect:  Ubuntu and Debian collaborating for their next big releases (the next Ubuntu LTS and Squeeze).

This sort of cross-distro collaboration is pretty exciting for me.  Hopefully this will mean the next LTS will have Debian stability, and the next Debian release will have a bit more up-to-date software.
