### Post by beew on 2011-08-05
10.10 definitely, faster and smoother, less buggy, better hardware compatibility out of the box. Unless you are running legacy hardware I wouldn't recommend 10.04. 10.10 is at a very sweet spot right now, it is stable since it has been out for a while but it is still up to date with tons of ppa supports.

P.S. If you have no problem with 11.04 other than Unity you should simply switch to the classic desktop as I think functionally 11.04 is the best of all three, it is the most up to date and is quite stable by now.
