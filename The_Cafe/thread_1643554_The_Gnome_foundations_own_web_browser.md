### Post by CarpKing on 2010-12-12
Epiphany is pretty good.  It does lack some features (even more than the developers intend, as IIRC some of the extensions have yet to be ported to the webkit version) and there are some random sites that don't work as well as in Firefox, but for most uses it works well and feels a little snappier than Firefox.  

Last time I used Midori it looked nice but had a ton of unimplemented features.  That was probably over two years ago, though, so that has undoubtedly improved and may be fixed entirely.  

Galeon is pretty much a clunky frontend for Firefox, and hasn't been developed much since it was forked to create Epiphany.
