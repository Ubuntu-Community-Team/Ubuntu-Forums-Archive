### Post by aysiu on 2011-11-07
Support threads for technical issues should absolutely not be megathreads. If you spot a support thread that is 1000s of posts, report it to the mods, and we'll take a look at it.

Discussion threads are actually better as long threads. God knows we don't need twenty separate threads about Revolution OS or about patent trolls. But, yes, if you are looking for a specific keyword in a discussion thread, it's far easier to do so within one megathread than through the whole forum among many threads.
