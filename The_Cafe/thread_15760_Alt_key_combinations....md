### Post by adbak on 2005-02-26
True, there aren't any Alt + number combinations, but there's a charmap.  Applications, Accessories, Character Map.

If that still inconveniences you, you could always right click on any panel, left click Add To Panel, left click Character Pallette, and customize a list of characters you normally use.  Or you could right click on the panel, left click Add To Panel, left click Keyboard Indicator and use a different keyboard layout (like one from Spain, France, Italy, etc).
