### Post by mips on 2008-11-15
I accept no responsibility for this but the double ended usb cables are not always needed, it all depends on how much power the drive and interface electronics use. The dual ended connectors are usually used more in the case of laptops than desktops, apparently the usb ports on a laptop don't supply as much power as on a desktop.
