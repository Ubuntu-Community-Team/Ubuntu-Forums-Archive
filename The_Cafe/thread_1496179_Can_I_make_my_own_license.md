### Post by chessnerd on 2010-05-29
You are the copyright holder. Your license can even say this:

```
 The Get-Rich-Quick License

 Copyright (c) <year> <copyright holders>
 
 Any person who uses this software may do so,
 without restriction, as long as they agree to pay
 the copyright holders $1,000,000 USD each at some point
 in the next three years.

```

The only thing you need to worry about is whether or not your license will hold up in court. I doubt that the "Get-Rich-Quick" license will, and I don't think yours would either.

If you want an ultra-simple, easy-to-understand license that does basically what you describe, try the MIT license. Here it is:

```

 anger from third parties.
