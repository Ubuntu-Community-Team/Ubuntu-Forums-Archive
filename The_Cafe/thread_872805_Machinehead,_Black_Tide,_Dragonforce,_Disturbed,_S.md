### Post by Luke has no name on 2008-07-28
Mayhem Fest '08!

Holy effing HELL, that was the most intense night of rocking I've ever had in my life. I thought I was going to die in the mosh of Slipknot, I don't know how people can stay in there for 3 hours straight. Disturbed put on a hell of a show, their music is so awesome. 10 hours of rocking in the Houston sun, damn! 

The circle pits are insane... God, what an awesome day. This is not the last time I see Disturbed. I only got to see one Dragonforce song, due to losing the person I was watching over and returning a lost phone :(. There will be more concerts though!

Now, if I can see Metallica, RATM, SOAD (maybe?), Godsmack, Journey.. <_<
