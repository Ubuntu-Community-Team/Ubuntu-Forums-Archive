If you are behind a restrictive proxy there is a good chance Firefox won't display everything properly either.  I had a short stint at a company that used a fairly restrictive proxy.  They hadn't upgraded to IE7 yet and, while they allowed the use of Firefox, it still had a problem with some sites including the Google and occasionally the Ubuntu forums.

If you do want to use a thumb drive and portable Firefox you will likely have to configure your browser to use their proxy settings or it won't work at all.  Just a heads up.  Check in the "Internet Options" menu under "Connections" and use that proxy info or ask a co-worker for help.
