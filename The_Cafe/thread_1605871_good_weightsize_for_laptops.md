### Post by Windows Nerd on 2010-10-25
4.4 pounds is about the same weight as the average textbook, depending on the textbook's size. If you are used to carrying around 2 textbooks (like most highschoolers do) it shouldn't be a problem. It really depends on a lot of factors, such as how much/far you will be walking carrying this laptop around, as well as what you carry around other than the laptop.
