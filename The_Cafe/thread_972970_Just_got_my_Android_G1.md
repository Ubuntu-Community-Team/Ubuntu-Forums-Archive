### Post by teknotuck on 2008-12-17
Also got my G1 when they first came out. Love it! Can easily connect to Amarok cause its just a removeable drive as far as linux (K)ubuntu is concerned so that is very simple just configure it to show up as a generic audio player device in Amarok and then when you plug it into your linux box, on the G1 in the notification window you will see a notification that it detected a USB connection, select that and on the resulting screen click on Mount. Back on your desktop just accept the mount window that pops up after you accept it on the phone and if you have amarok configured correctly it will get automounted at the same time and you can transfer music. Longer to write it up than it is to do it. 

In terms of Syncing with Kontact you can with not to much effort sync your calendar and its a fully bi-directional sync, if you enter something via korganizer, the G1 or via the Gmail website they all get synced. Do a search for GCALDaemon and that should get you syncing pretty quickly. (does eat a lot of Ram when it syncs, requires a working java setup too) What i have not been able to get syncing easily or at all actually is my kaddressbook, which for me is critical. Btw, the GCALDaemon also works for syncing Palm devices with gmail.

One of the other questions i saw asked here is how is some of this stored back on the G1 and from what i have figured out so far is its in a sql-lite db which if anyone EVER builds a solid sql syncing script for kontact it should be fairly trivial to have fully bi-directional syncing between all devices and the cloud. Hopefully that helps a few people get things going,
