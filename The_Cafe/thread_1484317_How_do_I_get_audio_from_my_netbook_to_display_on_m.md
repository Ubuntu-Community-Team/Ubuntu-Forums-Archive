### Post by GarmaZed on 2010-05-15
Yes, you'll need a separate cable, VGA/RGB only transmits video.  Unlike HDMI which transmits both audio and video.  
 
Your computer probably has a 3.5mm jack for audio like headphones and such, if your TV has a compatible audio-in jack of that format you could just get a simple 3.5mm audio cable, it's pretty common to find in a retail store, and some can be as low as $10.  If that doesn't work, or you don't have one, what other ports do you have available to use?
