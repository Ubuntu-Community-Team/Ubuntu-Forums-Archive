### Post by Paqman on 2009-12-06
> **steveneddy said:**
> 
Don't get the guns too close to the flame because the ammo will start going off by itself.


You'd be surprised how un-dangerous this is. Small arms ammo is class 1.4S, "S" for "safety". That's defined as  not presenting a serious explosive danger, and normally contained within it's own packaging. Basically they just go "pop" and rupture through the weak case wall. The worst you'll get is flecks of hot brass and unburnt powder on you.
