### Post by Frak on 2008-10-17
While this isn't actually predictions, they can very much serve as one. These were recommendations from Wired to Apple on how they could improve their business. If you read over it, you'll find they are very close to what happened, well, except for "Adopt the NT kernel, for then you could run PPC and WinTel..."

Apple migrated to Intel machines
Apple made a laptop that weighed under 2lb
Apple put Steve Jobs back in charge
Apple did not sell licenses to vendors
Apple did not migrate to the NT kernel
Apple did put more research into voice recognition

I have to hand it to them, they were very close.
