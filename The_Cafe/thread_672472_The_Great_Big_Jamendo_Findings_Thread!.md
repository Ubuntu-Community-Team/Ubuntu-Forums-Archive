### Post by Mazza558 on 2008-01-19
This thread is for finding the real gems of music on Jamendo. If you dig through some of the stuff, you'll strike gold!

Here's my favourite artist so far on Jamendo:

[JT Bruce: The Dreamer's Paradox]("http://www.jamendo.com/en/album/3317") - A fantastic instrumental Progressive Metal album, especially for such an indie artist. Obviously not quite the height of true prog metal, but very close. Thinking about it, the music's a cross between Muse and Dream Theater. Highlights include tracks 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 9. It ranges from synth to heavy guitar playing to piano to psychedelia and back again in a single track.
