### Post by wersdaluv on 2008-02-09
Before, I have been a qt junkie. I did everything just to go all-qt. Now, I'm all gtk+.

I can say that I still believe that Kontact is better than Evolution but generally, I prefer gtk+ apps now. I prefer Firefox, Pidgin, vlc, and Rhythmbox for some reason. 

Amarok is nice but it's too much and too heavy for me. Konqueror is nice but the web is slowly being designed for Firefox. There are qt apps that I still prefer like Kontact (with all its components), K3B, and Kaffeine but those aren't apps that I use most of the time now. 

Also, apps that aren't designed specifically for GNOME are usually gtk+ like Songbird, Transmission, VLC, etc. I think, more and more great gtk+ apps emerge as time passes by.
