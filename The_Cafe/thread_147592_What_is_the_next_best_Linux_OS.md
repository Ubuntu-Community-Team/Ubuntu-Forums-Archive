### Post by Kevin Carmony on 2006-03-20
That's sort of like saying "A Lexus LS400 is the best car.  What's the next best?"

Well, best at what?

For a farmer, it may be a pick up truck.
For a family a mini van or station wagon.
For a teenager a cute sports car.
For ecology minded folks a hybrid.

And so on...

Linux distro's are all different for a reason.  They cater to different types of users with different needs.  

I'd vote like this...

For die-hard "geeks" who want to really get their hands dirty - Gentoo.
For businesses that use RH on their servers, perhaps - Fedora.
For hobbiest who want to "sort of" get their hands dirty, but not too much - Ubuntu
For consumers, and the non-techie - Linspire
For graphics designers - OS X

Kevin
