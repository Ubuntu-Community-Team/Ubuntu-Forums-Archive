### Post by Ioky on 2008-05-07
mh Sound interesting, How about make a personal Site? you know just a site about yourself, and what you do. I personally think you shouldn't put too many idea, and feature in one site. Focus one thing than make any other site for other thing. Go found some Musician, Artist, or even girl who like take photos. and told them, you can make a free site for them. 

Really, making a site is like write a sentence in English, First you need a subject (what is the site about) a Verb (What feature you are going to to put it there for the visitor) and an Object (Who will be your Visit.) 

There is a lot you can do. I personally love web design. As you say, sometime you just don't know what you can work on, and when you get something to work on, you don't have too much time in your hand. And it makes it hard to get into the industrial. That is why you want to make something simple first, just so you at least have something to show people. focus on the quality instead quantity.

As a Web Design Lover myself, I wish you the best Lucky
