### Post by Paqman on 2011-10-28
Depends if the DNS available from your ISP sucks or not. 

Google DNS doesn't offer a lot of the extras you get from other alternatives like DynDNS and OpenDNS, so if your ISP is giving you the same kind of speed you get from Google then there's probably not much incentive to switch.
