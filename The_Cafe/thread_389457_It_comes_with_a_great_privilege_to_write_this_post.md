### Post by RAV TUX on 2007-03-20
I have been thinking all day about the recent controversy concerning my volunteering, my time as a Staff member:

I have volunteered countless hours helping out here by eliminated SPAM, moderate threads, moving threads, merging threads, etc. primarily I have moderated the "Other OS forum" and the "Cafe"....thus my activity has been mostly seen there.

On a larger scale I have been mostly concerned with what is best for ubuntuforums.org, I wanted to assure that whatever decision the FC made I would support 100% and stand behind that decision 100%.

Regrettably there is only one final solution; I must step down as a volunteer moderator...I am stepping down on my own accord for the greater good of the community and the integrity of the staff. I will mostly miss moderating where I am most active in: the "Other OS Forum" and "The Cafe"...I wish I could stay on and micro-mod these two forums, but I know this opportunity does not exist.[FONT=monospace]

[/FONT]I value all the friendships I have made here with my fellow staff and hope these friendships will continue.

I have only every had the best intent for ubuntuforums.org in mind and always will.

I have volunteered countless hours here working to help the forums overall, and my wife misses me and I miss her...time to make up for precious lost time with the one I love the most.
[COLOR=Silver](she is overjoyed to see me make a commitment to spend more time with her by stepping down).
[/COLOR] 
I do hope that I can stay on as a valuable and trusted member of ubuntuforums.org and look forward to contributing to the greater good of the community as I always have.

I must personally Thank; Ryan, Mike, and John for their wonderful opportunity to volunteer my time and serve the ubuntuforums.org community. Thank you to all the other staff for a wonderful team spirit I have yet to find elsewhere. It has been an honor to serve as a fellow staff member.

my deepest heartfelt regards, 
Jozef
