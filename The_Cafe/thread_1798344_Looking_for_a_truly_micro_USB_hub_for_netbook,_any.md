### Post by imachine on 2011-10-20
Maybe you could solder together some microUSB ports (the same ones that new mobile phones have), firstly ripping out the electronics from some tiny usb hub - like the [http://www.thinkgeek.com/computing/usb-gadgets/e935/](http://www.thinkgeek.com/computing/usb-gadgets/e935/) or [http://gizmodo.com/190891/lindys-micro-usb-hub](http://gizmodo.com/190891/lindys-micro-usb-hub) - to later reuse that electronics with the physical microUSB ports.

that way, you'd get a proper micro USB hub.
