### Post by PryGuy on 2008-09-18
Three days passed and I feel I have to share my thoughts with somebody. It appears that Rick is my personal friend. Not a rock/pop idol many people would die to get autograph or something but just a good friend. I think many people here can actually say the same. Pink Floyd is not about the fan thing in general IMHO. One of the reasons of their popularity is that they were always honest with their audience let alone their extreme professionalism and talent.

I have survived many deaths of my good friends and I can say that it takes you years to accept some of them. Rick actually taught me many things, he made me kinder and more tolerant. Just remember his 'Us And Them". There's something you gain just looking on some people I think, Richard was one of them for me. A type of persons that give your eyes rest and comfort if you understand me.

Too many words probably, but *I had to* share it, you see... So, shine on, Rick!
