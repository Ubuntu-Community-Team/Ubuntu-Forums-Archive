### Post by Sealbhach on 2008-10-21
Back in the 1990's the term "soccer mom" became widely used to refer to the large demographic group which voted for Bill Clinton. 

So the term "hockey mom" has a certain ironic sense to it. The implication being that soccer is a sissy sport for liberals and hockey is a tough sport for conservatives.
