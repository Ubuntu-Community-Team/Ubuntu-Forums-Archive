In KDE
Plasma Theme is an obsoleted term as the the current 4.4 series.
What was called Plasma theme is now known as "Workspace Theme"

Titlebar themes are known as Aurorae. The obsoleteness of the "Plasma theme came because of this adition in 4.X to ease title bar theme creation / separation with widget and taskbar styles.

So if you want the taskbar look search for "Air" and Oxygen" clones for GNOME. Air is default "White, cloudy" and Oxygen is "black"

The default titlebar theme is called Ozone so look for clones of that.

### Post by Half-Left on 2010-05-11
The plasma-desktop is very light actually. Many people have commented on how fast it is when using other window managers and such. 

What makes KDE4 seem so "heavy" are some Qt problems, Kwin issues and Xorg issues, not that KDE4 is bloated, as people seem to think. KDE4 is the first desktop to use SVG and ARGB extensively on the desktop, since plasma rely on both to make look good and be flexible.
