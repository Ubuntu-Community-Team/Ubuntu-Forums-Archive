### Post by icett on 2008-02-11
Linux means freedom! Isn't it? But uptill now I have found out on many Linux forums that many of my fellow Linux users do not like my thought of making Linux look like Windows and the usual response is that if you are so fond of using a Linux OS very similar to Windows why not go and use Windows. I would like to clarify that although I know that Windows is crap I like the Windows look and feel, eye candy look I mean, ease of use, knowing very well that it is unstable and insecure. I wish to make Linux look like Windows XP in its appearance and functions like theme, icons and other stuff as much as possible as it would be a good experiment for me and would improve my skills as well as it would give me some sense of accomplishment. There would be something I would feel proud to show to my family and friends. Does that mean that I dont like Linux and am pro Windows? I have found Linux very stable and secure and whats wrong with me if I wish to make it Windows like in look and feel, ease of use, as much as possible? I love Linux based on UNIX and not Windows based on NT so please do not tell me to use Windows. As Linux gives everyone the freedom as well as the capability to make their desktops look as they want, many Linux users give their Linux desktops the feel and look as they wish. So if I want to make it like Windows I don't think that there is something wrong with that and I wish and hope that the community would support and guide me. So I would request the Linux community to provide me guidance in this matter. Thanks in advnace and I hope you will help me.:)
