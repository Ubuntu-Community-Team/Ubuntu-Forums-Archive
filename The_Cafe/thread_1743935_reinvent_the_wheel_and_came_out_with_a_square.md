### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-04-30

is there away to get rid of the left hand menu???

Yes, and it's been mentioned in roughly 90% of the forum posts in the last couple of days. Surprised you missed it.

Install compizconfig-settings-manager and run it. Go to the Ubuntu Unity plugin inside that. Change the "Hide Launcher" setting to "Dodge Windows". Close. It now hides whenever a window is overlapping it, and you can get it back by *throwing* your mouse pointer against the left side of the screen.
