### Post by hyperyoda on 2009-02-22

Well some people are paranoid. It's true there is a risk using Google services (such as Gmail or Blogger etc.) but *any* service on the net has risk. Any unsecured data path between your computer (or a remote service) is liable to be attacked. The question is which should you worry about. Google is a huge company, if they are caught snooping on people or selling their private data it could have huge negative repercussions for them. I think it's far more likely some bored 15 year old may attack your machine and try to compromise it than a Google employee. So read the Ubuntu Security guides (search on bodhi_zazen) and get a firewall setup, etc.
