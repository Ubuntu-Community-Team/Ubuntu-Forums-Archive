Well, normally they're insecure before they even meet you. When you like something else that no one else likes, it really singles you out and makes you easy prey. Were they picking on you because you didn't like a certain game or were they merely trashing the game in your presence?  For example did they say "You like that game?  You must be a tool."  Or did they simply say "That game sucks."
