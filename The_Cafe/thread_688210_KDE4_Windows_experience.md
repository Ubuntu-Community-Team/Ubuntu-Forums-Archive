### Post by SupaSonic on 2008-02-05
It's one of those days that I really didn't want to work, so I decided instead of coding I'd try out KDE4 on Windows.

I used [this link]("http://techbase.kde.org/index.php?title=Projects/KDE_on_Windows/Installation") as a guide. Some stuff works, some stuff doesn't. There's no amarok or kopete. There's Konqueror though!

Anyway, I thought I'd share a screenshot. It was really cool to use konqueror to browse Windows folders, it just felt so... weird but at the same time nice. And yes, I use a ported ClearLooks theme for windows.

EDIT: Dolphin works too, though it's progress bar is constantly flickering. But Dolphin's really fast, definitely faster than Explorer )
