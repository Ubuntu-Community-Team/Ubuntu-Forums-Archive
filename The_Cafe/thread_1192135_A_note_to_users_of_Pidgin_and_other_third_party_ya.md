### Post by reprobus on 2009-06-19
It seems that the guys at Yahoo are updating their servers and they are using a new authentication system. As a workaround until Pidgin is updated you can add cs101.msg.mud.yahoo.com in the Pager Server field. To get to this field, in Pidgin, click on Accounts, then on your Yahoo account, next on the Modify button and last on the Advanced tab of the window that just opened.
