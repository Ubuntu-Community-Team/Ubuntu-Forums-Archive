### Post by jacksaff on 2009-03-07
First hour and a half was great, but it really started to drag towards the end. I loved the little touches - the old film clips, the tunes, the (squillions) of pop-cultural references - but the whole last hour was just dross. Should have cut all the mars and south pole scenes out. It was the anti-superheroes interaction with everybody else that was making the film fun and they killed it with the James-Bond-hide-out-in-the-middle-of-nowhere ending.
Never read the book so I'm not a fan, but this could have done with more vigorous 'adapting' IMO. Sticking to the whole story ruined what was looking like a great film.
