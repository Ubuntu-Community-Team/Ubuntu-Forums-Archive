### Post by ice60 on 2008-03-29
i just tried out pdfmenot and it's really good, it's just like using a PDF reader within a browser. there's even a bookmarklet - drag the bookmarklet to your toolbar, then when you see a link to a PDF drag the bookmarklet to the link, then click the link and it opens in pdfmenot.

try it out, either put this link (below) to a PDF in the box at pdfmenot
[http://www.novell.com/documentation/apparmor/pdfdoc/apparmor201_quick_start/apparmor201_quick_start.pdf](http://www.novell.com/documentation/apparmor/pdfdoc/apparmor201_quick_start/apparmor201_quick_start.pdf)
[http://pdfmenot.com/](http://pdfmenot.com/)

or, go to [this page](http://www.pdfmenot.com/tools/) and drag the bookmarklet (where it says [color=blue]_PdfMeNot Bookmarklet_[/color]) straight to your toolbar, then when you have the bookmarklet on your toolbar do this search at google 'apparmor pdf', the 2nd and 3rd result links should be PDFs, drag the bookmarklet to one of the links, then click on the link.
