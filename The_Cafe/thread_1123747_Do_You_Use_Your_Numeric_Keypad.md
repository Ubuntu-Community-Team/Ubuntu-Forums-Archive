### Post by RandomJoe on 2009-04-13
I don't like the number pads, but not because there's something intrinsically wrong with them.  If I have one available, I might use it - I just so seldom do that I don't think about it.

My problem with them is where they are located.  I like my keyboard "centered" in front of the screen.  As in, my hands are right in front of me.  If I adjust a keyboard-with-number-pad so that happens, the mouse/trackball winds up waaaaay over to the right.  If I center the keyboard as a whole, my hands are shifted to the left some.

Yeah, maybe that's a bit OCD... ;)

So all my keyboards do NOT have number pads.  I have a couple "short" IBM "clicky" keyboards, that still have the insert/delete/etc keys.  That's tolerable.  But my favorite keyboards now are the Happy Hacker Lite 2s.  I have several of them!  Not only do I not have the cruft hanging off the side, I have almost no frame at all anywhere.  Perfect!
