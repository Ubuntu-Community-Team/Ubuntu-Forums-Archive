### Post by Docaltmed on 2010-01-29
I just updated to the new Banshee, ver 1.6 beta 4, and I have to say ***hats off to the developers***. In the past couple of years I've been bouncing around among all of the music management apps -- Amarok, Songbird, Rhythmbox, Exaile and previous versions of Banshee -- all of them are good, all have their strong points, but none of them quite filled the bill for me.

Until now. This release does it all. It recognizes and manages my iPod, no sweat. Podcasts? check. Audiobooks? check. Links to online music stores? check. 

I'll stop, but there's more. If you are, like I was, still looking for that iTunes killer, check out the new Banshee. For me, this one does it all.

Congratulations again to the devs.
