### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2010-04-02
Dr. Roberts was one of those people that opened the door for us to have home computers.

The 8080 microprocessor was intended to be used in peripherals; such as, "smart terminals", connected to mainframes. It wasn't intended to be a computer in itself.

Dr. Roberts proposed using it **as a computer**, and made the Altair 8800.

I was an engineer at the time. Many people thought the idea was ridiculous, it would never work, and who would use it anyway.

RIP, Dr. Roberts
