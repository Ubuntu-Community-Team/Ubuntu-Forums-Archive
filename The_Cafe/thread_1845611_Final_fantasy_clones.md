### Post by ninjaaron on 2011-09-17
There are a few turn-based RPG's in the software center, both free and pay.  I've messed with a couple of them, never held my interest for long enough to keep going.  They mostly have sprite-based graphics, but that's A-OK with me.

Wesnoth, on the other hand, is fantastic, which, if you haven't already played it, is a turn-based strategy game with heavy RPG elements, a bit like FF Tactics and Ogre Battle.  I love it. You can sort of tell that the stories were written by programmers, but it sort of adds to the charm, in a way.  It definitely won't bring you to tears, like FF can at times.
