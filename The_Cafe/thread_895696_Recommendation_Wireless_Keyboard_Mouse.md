### Post by Warpnow on 2008-08-20
I have a logitech I like.

I also have a Microsoft one.

The microsoft one *feels* better. Typing on it is just comfortable.

The logitech one *works* better. Works better more often. Doesn't drop connection randomly, and doesn't lag, which are problems I have with my Microsoft one.

My logitech is an EX110 and it works 100% with ubuntu, even the special keys. Not pre-programmed but work fine once they are setup.
