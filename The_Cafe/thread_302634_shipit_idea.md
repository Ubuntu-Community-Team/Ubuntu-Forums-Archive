### Post by towsonu2003 on 2006-11-18
> **jonathan21 said:**

sure, why not. they would have two problems:
1. Will they be conflicting with their own "freedom of software" philosophy? Here's a metaphor from Washington: as your senator gets more money from an interest group, s/he'll be more attached to that interest group's influence...
2. Who will they get the ads from? Will they be able to "choose"? Example: If they get ads from Novell, they'll be in serious trouble... Where is the line drawn? 

Other then these, I don't see any problems with having ads in shipit CDs. It's a good idea to self-sponsor.
