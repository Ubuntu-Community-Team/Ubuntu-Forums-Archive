### Post by JohnFH on 2008-12-17
Don't see the point of Cloud OS at all.  Yes it has the advantage of being accessible anywhere, as long as you have an internet connection.  It has that one advantage, but it has many disadvantages and much less freedom:

- Need to trust third party. if their site goes down for a while, you're stuffed and you'll be in deeper doo if they go out of business.  That would never happen though, would it?  Internet companies as safe as banks ... oh hang on ...

- Needs good internet connection otherwise you're going to have a sloooowww experience no matter what speed of computer you have or what servers they have.

- I keep backups of my data and also of my OS.  If my hard drive went pop, that's ok by me (except I'd have to buy another one at some ridiculously low price).

Clouds ... insecure, risky, slow, restricting and pointless.
