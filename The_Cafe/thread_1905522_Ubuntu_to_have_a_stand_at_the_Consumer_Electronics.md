### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-01-07
According to the BBC, [Canonical will have a stand at the Consumer Electronics Show]("http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/technology-16415136") (CES) in Las Vegas this year.

"[T]his is the first time [Canonical] has its own display booth. It has made the leap to promote an early prototype of a Ubuntu-based  connected television as well as to show off the netbooks and laptops  normally associated with its code."

"We are launching some new products with a greater emphasis on consumer devices and CES is frankly the place to do that," Jane Silber says.

The results should be interesting.
