Hi, I've 10 images which have text on them, the text is small but visable, I would like to somehow smuge the text on the images so it's not as noticable, can anyone recommend a way please, many thanks

---

### Post by underquark on 2010-03-24
Open in GIMP.
Use the rectangular selection tool to enclose the area with the text.
Choose Filters...Blur...Pixelise and set it at about 20 or so (experiment).
