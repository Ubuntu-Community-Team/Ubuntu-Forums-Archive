### Post by ThatBum on 2014-10-09
I would have thought that "wireless" and "backlight" would be mutually exclusive, seeing as the backlight would eat through battery life. Hence why I haven't really heard of any. But, if it's what you need for your application, you have little choice.

You should get the mechanical one, as I have a mechanical keyboard and it's a pleasure to type on. It's not that loud once you train your hands not to hammer they keys, unless you have MX Blue switches which have intentional noise-making ratchet things in them.
