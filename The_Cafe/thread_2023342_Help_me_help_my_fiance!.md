### Post by oldsoundguy on 2012-07-12
You could develop a website.

Print out some business cards including the web link.

Create a flyer/hand out with pictures of her work and information.

Get and set up a booth at a local crafts fair. Hand out the business cards and flyers. 

OR

Get and set up a booth at your local Saturday Market. Hand out the business cards and flyers.

OR BOTH.

PLUS, your local gem shop (where she gets her supplies) should also have information on where and how.  After all, she sells the stuff, she is going to have to buy more supplies in order to make more!!
