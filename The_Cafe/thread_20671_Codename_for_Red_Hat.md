### Post by jfdill_2 on 2005-03-17
Red Hat was lately [going after the CentOS website](http://www.centos.org/modules/news/article.php?storyid=66) and that worries and annoys me.  I don't care for Fedora Core nor RHEL anyway, but it's the principal of the thing.  I can't help but wonder if they are going to go after all of the RHEL clones.  So I had an idea to parody the situation, and to avoid promoting the name of Red Hat by not having to mention it.

In Harry Potter, Voldemort is "He who shall not be named" or "You Know Who."  Prince was "The Artist Formerly Known as Prince" for awhile.  Could we come up with a codename for Red Hat that people "in the know" would understand, but that would not promote the name of Red Hat?  [Green Shoe](http://cgi-now.de/linux/greenshoe.html) is already taken for a German linux distro.  It might be convenient to have the same first initials, like "Rat Head" so that you could refer to "Rat head Package Manager" for example, but Red Hat might argue that was too obvious.

This episode also reminds me of my days hanging out on [SITO](http://www.sito.org) which was originally Operative Term Is Stimulate (OTIS) until the [Otis College of Art and Design](http://www.otis.edu/fileadmin/homepage/index.html) bullied them with the threat of a lawsuit.
