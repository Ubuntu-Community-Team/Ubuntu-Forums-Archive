### Post by marchwarden on 2009-09-14
If you are indeed referring to a supplied router and not the cable modem, then any Cable/DSL router will do.  I have used a netgear WGR614 for about two years now without any hiccups.  On the other hand, if you are talking about the cable modem (don't know what yours is likely to be, but mine is a Motorola Sufboard) then, as far as I know, you can not replace it.
