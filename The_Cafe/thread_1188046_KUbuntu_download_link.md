### Post by Closed_Port on 2009-06-15
[http://www.kubuntu.org/getkubuntu/download](http://www.kubuntu.org/getkubuntu/download)

Choose "64bit AMD and Intel computers" under type of computer, then use a download location near to you and that should be it.

For example, choosing Tokyo university I get this link:
[http://ftp.ecc.u-tokyo.ac.jp/UBUNTU-CDS/kubuntu/jaunty/kubuntu-9.04-desktop-amd64.iso](http://ftp.ecc.u-tokyo.ac.jp/UBUNTU-CDS/kubuntu/jaunty/kubuntu-9.04-desktop-amd64.iso)

Hope this helps.
