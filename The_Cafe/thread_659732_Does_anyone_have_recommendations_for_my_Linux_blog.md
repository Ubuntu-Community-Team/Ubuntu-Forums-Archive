### Post by BrianElliott0218 on 2008-01-06
Number one suggestion is to get off Blogspot and run your own CMS with WordPress or Joomla.  You can then offer folks downloads from your site and syndicate your blog out as well as let people become members of your site.  You can even make some money from the adsense ads you can run from a plugin on both of those CMS.

Number two (big one here):  Find the solution to the slow internet connectivity issue in just about every version of Linux including Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Knoppix, and others I have tried.

This would bring you a lot of loyal readers!  I am regrettably considering dumping Linux if the solution is not presented soon.  I can't evangalise linux to my clients if they get crappy downloads on the internet.  Youtube videos and Pandora music is choppy.

Linux is supposed to kick Windows' butt on downloads, and I like that idea a lot!  Find that solution, and you find the Linux Holy Grail.

Good luck with your blogging!  :-)
