### Post by Elfy on 2012-01-21
I'd take it back to wood, a _good_ sand and then a wash with soapy water and start again with the varnish after a dry with nature's harirdryer. 

Edit - get a couple of good brushes - put one coat - really push it on - let it dry for a day or two then follow up a very very very light sand and dry with the same hairdryer then  with a second coat with the other bush - _much much ligther_ or even lighter - if you can use the first brush still then you didn't push hard enough ... 

Works for 20 years on dinghies so a desk should waltz it ;)
