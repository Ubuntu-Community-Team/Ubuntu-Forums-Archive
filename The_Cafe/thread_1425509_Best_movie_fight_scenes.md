### Post by samjh on 2010-03-09
Random topic of the day: post your favourite movie fight scene.
:popcorn:

My pick is the fight scene between Nick Condon (played by James Cagney) and Captain Oshima (John Halloran) from Blood on the Sun, a 1945 anti-Japanese propaganda film:
[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MqjBMhV4uSM](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MqjBMhV4uSM)

Ironically for an anti-Japanese film, both actors in the scene used Judo: mostly throws, but also several ground techniques and some rarely-practiced striking techniques.  Halloran was a former LAPD officer and Judo instructor who - along with another Judo exponent, Ken Kuniyuki - taught the art to Cagney.  The scene harks back to the days when movie fights actually looked like proper fights, instead of the completely unrealistic foom-foom-foom of more modern times.

And a runner-up, for pure bad-assery, is Chen Zhen (Jet Li) vs General Fujita (Billy Chow):
[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TjYj_F6KmMI](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TjYj_F6KmMI)

For those who don't know, Billy Chow is a former world kickboxing champion.  The style shows.  Jet Li requires no introduction. :)
