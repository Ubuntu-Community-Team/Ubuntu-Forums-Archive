### Post by fatality_uk on 2009-05-27
For those living in the UK, who are in the market for a widescreen VGA monitor, I suggest a visit to either a local Tesco or get online. [http://direct.tesco.com/q/R.200-3517.aspx#](http://direct.tesco.com/q/R.200-3517.aspx#)

I have just bought one of the above monitors @ £129 and it is very good value. The contrast is spot on, great picture quality and it's fast.

Sat here looking at a 1920x1080 screen was odd for aboput 5 mins, now it's great. And playing AlienArena @1280x1024 on this is wild.

I have seen these @ £140+ so a good price.

### Post by omar8 on 2009-05-27
Not a particularly good deal (not a bad deal either - maybe £10 cheaper than other places) for a VGA monitor even in the UK. However, for about £160 (only £30) you can get a better brand and better connectivity. Also, this model does "buzz" so if you like to work in a silent environment this isn't the model for you.
Acer also have a poor dead pixel policy, I recently bought an Acer monitor which had about 10 dark black dead pixels but because of the spacing they decided that it was a completely perfect monitor, eventually I managed to to get a refund but that was through the store i bought it from rather than Acer who were no help at all.
