### Post by TheFu on 2021-03-14
There are lots of charter jet companies around the world who will provide a ride for a price.  But that will be a fairly local question. The cargo will matter greatly too.  You can setup a trip using an AN-225 or AN-124 if you like or a B757 or something smaller like a Folker or Cessna.  The smaller the plane, the more localized the rental/crew will be, since getting a Cessna from Europe to South Africa doesn't make sense.

I googled "rent a jet" and found lots of charter companies.

Sometimes, if you have 7+ people flying, getting a charter flight is the same price as flying 1st class commercial. More people just makes more sense, because the flight doesn't leave until you say in either direction. If you are going multi-stop in the same day, it can be even more efficient, since you'll fly into smaller airports that are in-town.

At one company where I worked, we had 2 planes that did multiple daily trips between 2 specific locations for employees and contractors. It was determined this was cheaper than paying for the wasted time and mileage of vehicles. They flew out of an airport that was closer for almost everyone than the huge international, commercial, airport here.  It was nice to show up 5 minutes before a flight, park for free inside the gate, show the company badge and get on the plane - saving at least 90 minutes each way vs road travel.  It made commercial travel a hardship.  Nobody patted me down for weapons or took my tiny keychain knife and water bottle away, like happens here on commercial flights.  The negative, was no FFQ mileage, no hotel perks, but not being away from home overnight was very nice and their wasn't any reimbursement paperwork.
