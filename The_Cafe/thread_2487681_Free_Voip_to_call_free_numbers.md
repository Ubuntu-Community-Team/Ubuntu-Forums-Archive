### Post by TheFu on 2023-06-12
I have a paid VoIP service with my home phones connected.  $5/month, but it isn't unlimited calling, just unlimited inbound.  Outbound is expensive, but with all the savings the rest of the year compared to all other telecom options, it is a bargain.  If you are dialing SIP connections directly, not POTS phone numbers, you can do that from Ubuntu using almost any SIP client for free.  I don't do that.  My SIP clients connect to my paid SIP service.

BTW, I don't have a cell phone plan, except when I'm traveling.  So that POTS line over SIP is my only connection. that isn't email.
