### Post by AdamZ on 2006-02-20
Many laptops will run Ubuntu just fine. I've got a Dell Inspiron 600m from last July, and it works almost perfectly (no 3d acceleration, but that may be my own fault). IBM (Lenovo now I guess) apparently also has really good linux support. There are also a couple of places online which sell laptops certified for linux.

Your best bet is to look for a model you like, and check out its page at [http://www.linux-laptop.net/](http://www.linux-laptop.net/) . The information might be a bit old, especially on how to get certain devices working, but for general hardware support information, it's great. Hope this helps.

- Adam
