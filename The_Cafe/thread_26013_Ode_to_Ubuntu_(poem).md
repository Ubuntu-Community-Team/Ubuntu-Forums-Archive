### Post by Psquared on 2005-04-11
**Ode to Ubuntu**

I love your brown human theme,
and your smoothgnome scheme.
Your menu apps thrill me most
on the forum I must post.

Windows can never match you,
In Redmond they just sue you.
It takes them years to code,
and that they probably stole.

Then viruses they attack,
and hackers bring their hack.
A Trojan horse will soon get in,
and break your box again.

Workgroups started it all,
then 95 came one fall.
98 was supposed to be the best,
but it was buggy like all the rest.

So out came the second edition,
to improve your pc's condition.
But ME was right behind,
then XP came down the line.

Now longhorn is riding into town,
but it looks more like a clown.
While Gates looks like lead,
Ubuntu Linux moves ahead.
