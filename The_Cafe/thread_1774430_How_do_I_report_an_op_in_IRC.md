### Post by samalex on 2011-06-03
The OP can do pretty much what they want, it's not a democracy, but if you really feel like you were crapped on visit #defocus and #freenode (assuming this is in Freenode) and talk to those guys.  You can also /msg another OP in the channel, tell them what happened, and maybe they'll unban you.  Maybe the person who did the banning has a wrap for doing such things and another OP will let you back in.

And if all else fails depending on how the ban was implemented either change your Nick or release your DHCP lease to get a new IP which will have you appearing as a completely new user.  Or find one of the free shell accounts and go through that way.  Granted a Channel isn't Invite Only there are lots of ways to get back in.
