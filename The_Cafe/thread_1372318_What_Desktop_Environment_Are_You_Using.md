### Post by Mark76 on 2010-01-07

Is that really ROX? It looks like KDE, or even GNOME.

BTW, is ROX lightweight? I mean as light as Openbox?

I would miss openbox's menus, though.:(

Yes that's really ROX. And I guess it could be (almost) as lightweight as OB if you don't run the sheer number of panel plug ins I do (14 in all + Wallpaper applet).

You could use ROX as a desktop manager with Openbox, with or without a panel (rox --pinboard=MyPinboard for just the background or rox --session/-S for background and a panel) which would also give you desktop launchers (if you wanted them) and iconised windows (again... if you wanted them).

As for the Openbox menu there's a pass all background clicks to window manager option in Options/Compatibility in the right click menu (if you need to use the ROX menu after setting that option just right click on a filer icon).
