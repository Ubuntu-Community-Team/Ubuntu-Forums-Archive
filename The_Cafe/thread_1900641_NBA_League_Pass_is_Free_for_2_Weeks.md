Hi Everyone,

As some of you have already known, **NBA** was in **lockout** and the new season has just started yesterday. **NBA League Pass Broadband** which broadcasts NBA games online is free until **9th of January**. It supports almost all operating systems which have Flash Player.

At the moment, I am watching the games with my Ubuntu 10.10 Maverick Meerkat on Firefox 9.0.1 and the quality of the broadcast is remarkable.

It is worth to try... :guitar:
