### Post by handy on 2010-05-28
See of if you can find a site that has evaluated the tent?

What you need to know is how windproof it is & if it will meet your requirements in that regard.

I had (gave to someone after the trip) an admittedly cheap 2 person dome tent, that I used on a trip in central Oz, & more than once the fibreglass poles were pushed into the tent, curving the opposite way to what they are supposed to by wind. 

I learned a lesson I won't forget from that experience on that trip. :)
