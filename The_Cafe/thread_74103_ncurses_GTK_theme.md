### Post by SilentCacophony on 2005-10-10
Bah, just do the real thing. Do a 'server' install run **aptitude** for package manager, get **gpm** for mouse support, and **screen** for multiplexing the console, use **mc** for filemanager/reader/editor, use **emacs** or **vim** for serious editing. You have centericq for messaging, mutt for mail, links for www, and there's even a music play that I can't remember... ;)

More on-topic, but not quite, there is **twin** - [http://linuz.sns.it/~max/twin/](http://linuz.sns.it/~max/twin/)
