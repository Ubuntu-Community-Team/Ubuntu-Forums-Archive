### Post by crun on 2005-02-22
Here are three applets I like which aren't included in the default "Add to Panel" window:

[Browser Bookmark Menu](http://browserbookapp.sourceforge.net/) - dropdown list with your prefered browser's bookmarks
[Quick Lounge](http://quick-lounge.sourceforge.net/) - dropdown menu that you can fill (and order) with programs, a sort of little Applications Menu.

[IMG]http://www.imageafter.com/temp/lex/images/forums/applet_quick_lounge.png[/IMG] 

[Web Sharing](http://veldstra.co.uk/websharing/) - for easily running a little server ([Mathopd](http://www.mathopd.org/)) where you can host downloads or html files. Just start it up and point your browser to your computer's IP, port 8000 (e.g. xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx:8000). Drop any files you whish to share in the "Website" folder placed in your home dir.

[IMG]http://www.imageafter.com/temp/lex/images/forums/applet_web_sharing.png[/IMG]
