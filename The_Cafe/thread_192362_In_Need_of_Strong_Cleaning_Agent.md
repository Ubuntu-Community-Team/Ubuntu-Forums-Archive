### Post by mcduck on 2006-06-08
If you need anything stronger than plain water for cleaning, I recommend using isopropanol (isopropyl alcohol). 

It's the best stuff for cleaning plastic, electronics and other sensitive stuff. It doesn't damage plastic and it evaporates quickly leaving no marks. And it's cheap. You can probably get some from your local pharmacist, at least that's where I buy it here in Finland.

I use it for everything, cleaning camera lenses, CD's and DVD's, cleaning computer parts and even for windows ;)
