Well, I love KDE. I find it to be a much more well thought out Desktop Environment. I would use it except for issues with Compiz integration, and just a general lack of Ubuntu support. Plus, it's rather difficult to make KDE3.5 look good. I love the KDEMod theme, but I can't find a version of it that works with Ubuntu.

Anyway, I'm babbling. KDE4 looks beautiful, and leaps and bounds better than KDE3 (imo). I've tried it a couple of times, but it doesn't have a lot of the features I would need to make it my permanent desktop environment. The composting manager is way too slow, most of the widgets/plasmoids are a bit useless, and I can't (well) resize the dock/panel thing. 4.1 also looks great and seems to resolve a lot of those issues. My questions (for now) are these:

What do you do for compositing? From what I've seen KDE4 compositing just isn't ready yet. How well does KDE4 integrate with Compiz Fusion?

Do you find yourself having to use Gnome apps often?

Do you find yourself having to use KDE3 apps often?

What are your favorite KDE4 features?

What bugs you the most?
