### Post by samjh on 2009-10-09
Gnome-Shell is not the entirety, but it is the centre-piece of Gnome 3's user interface.  Gnome 3 is also about consolidating dependencies and improvements to the internal workings of the software.

Basically, if you run Gnome-Shell in the current Gnome release, you're seeing what Gnome 3 will look like on the surface.
