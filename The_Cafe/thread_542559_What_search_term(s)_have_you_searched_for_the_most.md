### Post by MethodOne on 2007-09-08
I usually search for the exact files I need on Google without having to going through queues (like on FilePlanet) or download limits without a subscription (like on RapidShare) by using this string:

```
intitle:"index of"+"name last modified size description"+"*file*.*ext*"
```Unfortunately, you will get links to torrents (if you don't like or can't use P2P), ultra-cheap (not free) MP3 downloads, covers of popular songs, lyrics (you might not want them now), people's playlists, search engine spam, and other annoyances.  

Just tweak the string a little bit to get different results.
