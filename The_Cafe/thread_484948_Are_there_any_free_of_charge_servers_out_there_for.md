### Post by michaelzap on 2007-06-26
If you want the general public to be able to download these files, then "Nope" is indeed the correct answer. But if you just want some trusted friends to download them then you could share this gmail account's login info with them and they could access the files the same way that you do. Besides the Firefox add-on, there's also software that lets you mount your gmail account as if it were a hard drive on your system.
