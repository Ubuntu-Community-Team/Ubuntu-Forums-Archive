### Post by haqking on 2011-06-28
> **cptrohn said:**
> Thank you, I will have to remember that... but the way I am understanding it is that you have to pass two tests now correct?  And both are 60 questions and you need a 500 to pass them..  Didn't know about the little extra bit of info you provided though!

yeah 2 exams and 90 minutes each and 60 questions, you need a score of 500 on a scale of 200-800 as the questions are sometimes for research.
but make sure you choose to take the compTIA (all the same exams) and say yes to forward info to LPI.

you then get 2 certs, once you get your LPI you can go here [http://www.lpi.org/eng/certification/lpi_and_novell_partnership](http://www.lpi.org/eng/certification/lpi_and_novell_partnership) and get your Novell cert with no extra charge or exam.

So you get 3 certs from one exam (well 2 exams but you know what i mean) ;-)

good luck
