### Post by asimon on 2006-01-20
[QUOTE=bulldogzerofive]Hey all

I noticed my avatar has been changing as I post more...[/quote]
Actually, what's changing is not your avatar. What you mean is a graphical representation of your post count. Currently you have no avatar. You can set an avatar, if you choose "Edit Options" from "Quick Links" on the brown link bar above. Then on the left menu choose "Edit Avatar". An avatar is a picture of your choosing and will be displayed below this graphical post count.

[QUOTE=bulldogzerofive]
Does anyone know what the benchmarks are for the different avatars? 

(I mean, with 15 you will get this, with 30 you will get this... etc)
[/QUOTE]
I don't think there is a list of the titles and respective post count when they change. It's part of the fun to see what titles there are and when they change. But don't take them serious. From time to time the forum administrator change these titles and their graphical representation.
