You can easily tunnel google search through DDG with the following technique: [https://duckduckgo.com/bang_lite.html](https://duckduckgo.com/bang_lite.html)

This anonymizes you by using DDG to strip the search request of all originating ID data. Best of all, it's easy. It can also be leveraged for many other sites.

However, if you then enter more searches through the google search box, you've just bypassed DDG and undone all of your good work. To remain anonymous, ***all*** searches must be done using the above technique.

Of course, you now have to trust DDG, but I've satisfied myself that they are a far better bet than anything Google.

Another worthwhile technique is to use a meta&#8209;search engine. These are basically search agglomerators that return combined results from Google, DDG, Bing and others, again, anonymized by stripping the search of all originating ID. Of course, you now have to trust these meta&#8209;search operators, so do your research. When researching, be wary of the FUD put out by Google and Microsoft trying to scare you against using these meta&#8209;search engines. Although a few have been taken over by shady operators, there are lots that are legit. I use Disroot because I've satisfied myself that they are honest and don't keep logs.

FWIW, I've found that DDG returns good results. It's usually all that I need. Moreover, it often returns better results than google since google returns results weighted in favour of their advertisers. Even though they deny this, I don't trust them.
