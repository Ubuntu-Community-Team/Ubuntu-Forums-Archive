### Post by Nevon on 2010-02-17
I just had my request to have my work with [Quickshot]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ubuntu-manual/quickshot") for the [Ubuntu Manual Project]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ubuntu-manual/"), approved as a university course. In other words, I'll be able to dedicate part of my time to work on an open source project, and have that count toward my degree! This is absolutely great, and I urge anyone who is in a similar position as me to give it a shot and see whether or not the same opportunity is available at your university.

Just wanted to share my happiness and see if anyone else has done something similar! :D
