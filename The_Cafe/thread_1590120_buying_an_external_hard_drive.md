### Post by Sporkman on 2010-10-07
Not exactly what you're looking for, but I just purchased one of these "external disk enclosures":

[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16817182155](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16817182155)

Which is a hard drive case with a usb interface & a power supply. You then (separately) get an internal SATA hard drive (like you'd install in a desktop computer) and install it inside.

Really neat concept IMO, then you can swap drives in & out of it without having to re-purchase the usb & power supply portion, like you're doing every time you buy a one-piece external drive.
