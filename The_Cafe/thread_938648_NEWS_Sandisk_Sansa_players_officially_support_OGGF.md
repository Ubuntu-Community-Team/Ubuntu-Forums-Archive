You'll have to update the firmware, but that isn't so bad. I have an 8GB player, and it is nice. Small, light, great battery life, FM radio and voice recording are excellent. With this update to allow OGG and FLAC audio, it seems open source advocates finally have a major music player company to look to for their needs.

Seriously, this player is NICE. Thin, shiny, easy to use, cross platform. the 8GB version was $150 total, including tax and a 2 year warranty at Best Buy.
