### Post by loudmouthman on 2007-07-23
Currently I am using Thunderbird and GoogleCalendar

I am supplementing this as follows.

1) I install a use PLAXO for synchronising my contacts across any Thunderbird installs I need
2) I use Scheduleworld.co.uk to synchronise Google Calendar between Thunderbird and my Mobile phone via SYNCML
3) I use Del.icio.us to synchronise my bookmarks by way of a RSS feeds for my profile into thunderbird and firefox. 

I hope this gives you some advice forwards
