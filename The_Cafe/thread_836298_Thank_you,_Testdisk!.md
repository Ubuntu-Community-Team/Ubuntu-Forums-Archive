### Post by init1 on 2008-06-21
Recently, I damaged my hard drive's partition table and it looked as if I had lost a lot of data. Thankfully I had Testdisk on a pendrive. It restored my partition table and now I've got my data back! This is the second time that Testdisk has recovered data on my hard drive. I strongly recommend that you make bootable disks in case of emergency. 
[http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk](http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk)
