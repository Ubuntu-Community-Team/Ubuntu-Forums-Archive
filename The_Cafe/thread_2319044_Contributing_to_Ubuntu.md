### Post by sudodus on 2016-03-31
You can also participate in ***iso-testing*** of the deveoping version of standard Ubuntu or one of the Ubuntu family flavours (Kubuntu, Lubuntu ... Xubuntu). See this link to the iso tracker: [http://iso.qa.ubuntu.com/](http://iso.qa.ubuntu.com/)

If you find bugs, please report them at Launchpad: [https://launchpad.net/](https://launchpad.net/)
You need an account at Launchpad to perform these tasks: [https://help.launchpad.net/](https://help.launchpad.net/)

After a while you will probably find a program that 1: needs improving, and 2: is within your scope to fix.
