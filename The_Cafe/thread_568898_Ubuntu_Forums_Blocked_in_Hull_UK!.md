### Post by eph1973 on 2007-10-06
To view the forums, even though they are blocked:

Go to [http://www.google.com/translate_t](http://www.google.com/translate_t)

Under the translate a web page, enter
```
http://ubuntuforums.org
```Select English to Spanish on the click down menu.

Then click the Translate button

You will then see the forums in Spanish.  Up along the top on the URL block in your browser, you should see something like:
```
http://www.google.com/translate?u=http%3A%2F%2Fubuntuforums.org&langpair=en%7Ces&hl=en&ie=UTF8
```Then where it says ```
langpair=en%7Ces
```swith the en and the es so that that section of the URL reads ```
langpair=es%7Cen
```After switching, and hitting the enter key with the new URL, you should be able to browse through the website normally now.
