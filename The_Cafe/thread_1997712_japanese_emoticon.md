I am a bit confused.

The Chinese character above &#22721; means "wall" and is read "*kabe*". I do not think that it has anything to do with an emoticon.

The last three characters &#65409;&#65431;&#65391; are katanata syllables which read "*chira'* " and mean "very briefly", "hardly" or "just a little". 

In between, you have the cyrillic letter &#1076; for "d".
