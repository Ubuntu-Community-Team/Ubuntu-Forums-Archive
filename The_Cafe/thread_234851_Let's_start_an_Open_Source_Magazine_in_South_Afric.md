### Post by schurtek on 2006-08-12
This has been something I have wanted to do for as long as I have been using open source (couple of years now).

I have started putting ideas together with a friend of mine (who converted me to the church of open source) and have now put together a website to facilitate this project (courtesy of my company). 

So any one interested in taking part... please come and join me at [www.osm.za.org]("http://www.osm.za.org/") (yes that is the right address). 

My aim is to make this magazine a community project, and hopefully create some jobs for some south africans who are passionate about open source, while at the same time, spreading the good news...
