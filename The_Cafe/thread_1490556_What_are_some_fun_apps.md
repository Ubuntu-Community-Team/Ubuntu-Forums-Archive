### Post by DeadSuperHero on 2010-05-22
[Makehuman]("http://www.makehuman.org/"). It's geared more towards people that aren't really sure about making their own 3D character models, but it's a great starting point for customizing all of the features of a character.

The new Alpha supposedly supports things like hair, special rendering modes, an improved interface, better texture support, better lighting, etc. 

It exports to OBJ format, and I think a few others. Works great with Blender.
