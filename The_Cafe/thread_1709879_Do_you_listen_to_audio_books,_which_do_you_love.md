I have accumulated a collection over the years. Not all for free.

Does anyone else listen to audio books when they are driving or otherwise travelling? Or because they like to hear the voices & sound effects of a great BBC production (for example), or because their eyesight is not good or they suffer from insomnia?

As an example, the Hobbit & The Lord of the Rings are absolutely brilliant audio books produced not only by the BBC.

There exist wonderful audio books on history & historic personages, the complete works of Charles Dickens; books written by Tom Robbins, & on it goes.
