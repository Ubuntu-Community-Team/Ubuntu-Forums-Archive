### Post by kebes on 2007-02-02
Well since you have at least some background in the language, the only way to really learn more is to practice by reading things written in french and speaking (or typing!) to others in french. If you can find a local "french club" that would do wonders, although who has the time?

So I would find a subject you're currently interested in, and go read the french (instead of english) instructions or forums.


Et, bien sur, il y a beaucoup de personnes sur l'Internet qui veulent aussi jaser en Francais. Il suffit de les trouver!
