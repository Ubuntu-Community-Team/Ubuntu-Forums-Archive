### Post by beast-usa on 2008-04-14
Hello new here & new to Linux.

I just setup a QX9650, Abit IX38 dual ATI 3870x2's, 4 gigs GeIL Esoteria.

It smokes!
I'm new to this forum & linux, but I will tell the trouble I had in-case it helps.

Ubuntu 804 server x64 read's all the drives IDE (SCSI) & sata.
Ubuntu 804 desktop x64 only finds IDE (SCSI) and it will only find
and install to that drive if you fdisk/format before you install.
I used WD disk utilities 0'ed out the drive and left it blank and it didn't
see the drive. Then I used paragon drive back-up 8.5, made a ntfs partition, and it found it NP. I let ubuntu use the whole and set it up.

2nd boot drive Vista & XP (still changing boot drive in the bios I will install boot loader later)

Running great!

On your PSU if you haven't bought it already?
Make sure to get single 12v rail & check what people are getting on
their 12v rail. Lots of those PSU say 80amp 12v divided by 4 =20amp
=crash LOL at 100% load all 4 cores.

I have a few 1000w PSU's PC Power & Cooling being the best for no drop
in the 12v rail 12.36v never changes.
UltraX 3 1000w has a small drop when all 4 cores are 100% 12.28v drops to 12.18v (ati card's) still good but the drop will cause problems.
(12.22v with nvidia card)
