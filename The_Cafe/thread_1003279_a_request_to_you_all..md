+1

Also, some odds and ends:

Just blanking out the image on an LCD will not save any power worth speaking of. It's the ***backlight*** which uses the most power in that kind of display.

The ***voltage*** of your power brick has no influence on your electricity bill. It's the wattage (i.e. Volts times Amperes) that has. 

Your typical laptop uses anywhere between 20 and 90 Watts. Therefore, current draw is measured in Amperes and not Milliamperes @ 19.8 Volts.
