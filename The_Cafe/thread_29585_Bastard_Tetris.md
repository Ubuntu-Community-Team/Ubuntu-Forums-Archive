### Post by jdong on 2005-04-25
Tetris is an integral part of our robotics team, so we all rushed to play Bastet. Comments:


* Great that there's a Debian package, and that the latest version is in Hoary Universe

* It IS EVIL -- it doesn't give you the pieces you need, until you don't need them anymore!

* Slide delay -- to be a Tetris(R) clone, you have to have that 1-second (relative) delay after dropping a piece when you can still slide it a bit -- otherwise it's just not Tetris(R).


Overall, great work. I'm working on getting it to look like Netris, and prank a few Netris-loving friends.
