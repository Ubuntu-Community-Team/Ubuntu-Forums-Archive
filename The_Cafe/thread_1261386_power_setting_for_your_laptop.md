### Post by Whiffle on 2009-09-08
The ondemand processor governor is pretty good.  

And if its accessable (and you never use it), drain your battery to 40% or so and pull it out.  Having it constantly charging to 100% plus the heat of the laptop will kill it.  Some laptops (Thinkpads, maybe the business dells), can be set to stop charging at a certain charge in order to keep the battery living longer.
