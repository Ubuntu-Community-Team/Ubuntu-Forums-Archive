### Post by Mazza558 on 2008-02-24
> **DoctorMO said:**
> What is the point of freedom in your slavers garden?

I like to think of this as a half way point between the "no-risk liveCD" (which offers no customization) and a full-blown install. With this, users can install Ubuntu just like another Windows program (like they're used to) and keep using Ubuntu until they're comfortable enough to try dual booting (at which point they can back up their files and go true dual-boot). I already know several people who would love this - people who are on the fence about Ubuntu, and I know they'll be much more likely to try it out with this method.
