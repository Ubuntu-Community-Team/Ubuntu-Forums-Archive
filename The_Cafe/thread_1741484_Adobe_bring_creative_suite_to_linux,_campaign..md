I just read (i don't know if this is new news) that Adobe has since last year investigated if the interest was big enough on the linux platform for Adobe products. To make the Adobe suite native available.
Adobe has now made it available that linux users can request Adobe to make the product available native for linux by going to their site and select the product you want. You can select all but only one at a time.
