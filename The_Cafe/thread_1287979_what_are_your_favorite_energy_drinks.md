Perceived energy and actual energy are different.  Caffeine boosts alertness, thus giving the perception of energy without the empty calories of sugar.  The military has done lots of research on energy and alertness aids, and caffeine is overall the best, giving much of the benefit of amphetamines without any of the drawbacks.
