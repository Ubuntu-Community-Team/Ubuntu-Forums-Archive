### Post by Bachstelze on 2010-03-31
From [font=monospace]/usr/share/doc/humanity-icon-theme/copyright[/font]:

> The Humanity Icon Theme is licensed under the GPL v2.

Basically, you can use them on your website if:

-> You excplicitly say where they come from and give credit to the authors, and
-> You provide a copy of the icons to anyone who asks for it.

Also download needn't be free.
