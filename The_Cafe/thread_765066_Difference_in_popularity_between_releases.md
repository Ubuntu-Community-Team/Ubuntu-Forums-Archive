### Post by jaytek13 on 2008-04-24
KDE4 is generating a lot of buzz, but whether it will become the "norm" or not is remaining to be seen. We probably wouldn't know that for a year or 2, well after 4.1 is released. Even then, Gnome will still always have the dedicated gnome users who hate kde. KDE will always have dedicated users who hate gnome. The rest of us will just sit in between the fence and switch back and forth on a whim. 

There will for the foreseeable future always be a place where Gnome and KDE can coexist, so instead of using the desktop environment that is or has created the most buzz, just stick with what you like. You can always switch back at a later time, then again and again.
