Just wanted to share a painting i just finished. I do graphics as a hobby and from time to time i paint pictures which are inspired by my dreams.

This painting is based on a dream i had a week or so ago. I was outside, it was dark and very random. I looked up in the dream and wondered if there was anything watching over us. As soon i as looked up i saw these Eyes. The first one was closest to us, dark but fitting in the night sky. As i kept looking up i saw the second eye, A reflection to where i was and the Eye was hellish looking, with a dark red aura. 

I think this dream has some kind of meaning lol, but i'm really not sure. Might be something DEEP haha :confused:

Well, i hope you like it at least, Comments and Critiques appreciated. Thank You :)
