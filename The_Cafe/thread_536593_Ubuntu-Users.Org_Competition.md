### Post by Beamerboy on 2007-08-27
OK I was going to leave this until Wednesday but I may as well start the ball rolling now.

The first stage of the Ubuntu Users Social Network project is coming up with a nice design for the user interface.  As promised this is an Ubuntu Users project developed and maintained by Ubuntu Users, so we are opening up the design process to you guys.

Anyone who wants to have a go at designing the User Interface (as in the graphical layout) here is your chance to get involved.  Please post links to your designs in this thread, I will set a preliminary closing date for submissions of September 14th which may be extended if required.

Once the closing date arrives a poll will be setup where users can vote for their favourite design.  We will do this as a two part process, the Logo and the general layout graphics.  You are welcome to attempt both if you wish.

The winner(s) will have the honour of their designs being used and they will appear in the site credits with a link directly to either their personal website or their ubuntu-users.org profile/blogspace.  They will also be guaranteed entry into the beta program and invited to join the development committee.

If you don't know what the Ubuntu Users Social Network project is, please see the following link:

[http://blog.paladine.org.uk/?p=38](http://blog.paladine.org.uk/?p=38)

Good Luck everyone!

Paladine
