### Post by LordDelta on 2013-05-26
> **mitoni said:**
> in modern os such as Mac Os or WIndows programmers always try to migrate to GUI which is handy

No, they always try to get more people on board to use their software.

These people don't always know everything about the program, so they always try to give their users as many crutches as possible. Mistaking a UI for something other than a crutch is a grave mistake, IMHO.

Think about a human, for instance. We suck at adding numbers. Why? Because we like to do things graphically.

Machines have no such inhibition, symbolic manipulation is rather proven to be much superior here. Graphical representations are crutches we can use to interact with programs we have never seen before, but when we actually want to do something, its much better to just do it directly.
