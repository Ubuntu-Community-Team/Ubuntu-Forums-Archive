### Post by OperaKicksA*s on 2009-11-03
> **NinjaNumberNine said:**
> If you had pick between Google Wave and Opera Unite, which would you choose? 

It's strange, I have seen these sort of posts all over the Internet Wave vs. Unite threads.


I think it odd because they are not really related in any way.  In most ways they are the opposite of each other.

It's like asking if you are going to drive to work today or are you going to drink coffee on your way to work today?


They have nothing to do with each other really and their not mutually exclusive.


If anyone has used Google Wave, they know it is a sort of combination of a new type of Email, but much quicker like IRC.  Sure you can embed photos or files or Youtube videos, but it's not the best way to share a lot of files or photos or whatever.  Also, if someone adds a person to a Wave (like forwarding an Email) that person has access to everything in the wave including whatever files and photos you put in it.  Wave can end up with tons and tons of people that you have no idea who they are.


Opera Unite on the other hand really has nothing to do with Email.  There are Unite apps that let you share you entire Music collection.  Apps that let you upload files to your computer.  Apps to share all the Photos you want.  Webserver App, etc...  


The concepts behind them are also different.


Google Wave for example is more of a Cloud model.  Your messages, photos or files you post are out on the Google machine.  Out in Cyberspace.

Opera Unite is the exact opposite.  Your files are on your computer.  Not out on some other computer's hard drive somewhere. 


Which would I choose.  Both of course.  


Google Wave so far seems like a better way to Email.  You can collaborate faster and more easily especially with groups of people.  

Opera Unite is great for hosting my files, music, etc to access from wherever I go and by whom I chose to give access.


I just don't see how you can compare/relate the two.
