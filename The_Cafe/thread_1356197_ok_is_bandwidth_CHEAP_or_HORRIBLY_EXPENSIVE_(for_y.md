no no.. not the sale of Items over the internet. Tax free only applies to 'internet services'. For example a web cafe would not have to pay taxes on money made from selling internet usage on their computers. You still have to turn it in to the IRS as income. But you dont pay taxes on that. Now if the cafe sells coffee to the users that are using the PCs, then they will have to pay sales tax on the coffee.

So if you buy something off Newegg for example, depending on the state that could be taxable. As its a physical item per se.

But the 39.99, 79.99 or 99.99 you pay your ISP a month for internet usage. They do not have to pay sales or federal taxes on that.
