### Post by megamania on 2007-01-08
> **tito2502 said:**
> I think I have ordered more than once.
That isn't the point of shipit though, people should order one and burn it.
No. As I said in another thread, Canonical is a for-profit company, and if they give away Ubuntu cd's there's a reason.

They WANT you to spread the word by giving away good-looking cd's rather than home-made copies. That increases their market penetration with a extremely limited financial effort. That's why you can order as much as 50 cd's, if I remember correctly.
In my opinion, the "one shipment only" policy lies (or lied) in the fact that postal charges have a higher impact than the cost of the cd's themselves, so they ask you to plan ahead and make a single shipment.
