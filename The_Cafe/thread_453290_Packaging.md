### Post by PriceChild on 2007-05-24
Hey there. [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU/Documentation](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU/Documentation) is the best place to start, especially [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PackagingTips](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PackagingTips) , [http://www.debian.org/doc/manuals/maint-guide/index.en.html](http://www.debian.org/doc/manuals/maint-guide/index.en.html) and [http://www.debian.org/doc/debian-policy/](http://www.debian.org/doc/debian-policy/)

I'm currently packaging a bitch of a source which you can see at [http://revu.tauware.de/details.py?upid=5249](http://revu.tauware.de/details.py?upid=5249)

But the biggest tip I have for you is to hang out in #ubuntu-motu on irc.freenode.net !


That may seem like a lot of documentation I have dumped on you but I'm afraid you need to read it and understand it. That means the whole thing say 5 times before you get it right. Its the only way unless you want someone else to do it for you.
