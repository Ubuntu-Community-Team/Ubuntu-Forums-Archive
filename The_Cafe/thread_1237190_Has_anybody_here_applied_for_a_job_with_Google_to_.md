### Post by kernelhaxor on 2009-08-13
I applied for an internship .. After a few days they called me and scheduled a phone interview.  Had the phone interview (during the interview, was collaborating with the interviewer on Google docs to write code!), but didn't get a call back after that.

The guy actually told me the reason why he picked up my resume .. other than regular old things people look for in a resume, he said he was looking for something that stands out, like very strong skills in some particular area or innovative projects that you might have done ..

I've heard from my friends at school who were interviewed by Google that they were asked to solve simple problems "differently" .. For example, take a simple question like write a program to sort numbers, my friend gave a solution then my friend was asked how he would modify that if he was given say 100 computers and how he would make it run much faster ..

I think Google would be a great company to work for .. Good luck!
