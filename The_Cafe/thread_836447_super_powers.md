Someone can be invincible in the pure form by means of the dictionary definition: "incapable of being overcome or subdued" or "Incapable of being conquered, overcome, or subdued".

Where as someone would be immortal in the pure form much like that dictionary definition: "lasting forever; having unlimited, or      eternal, existance" or "destined to live in all ages of this world; abiding".

While one might indirectly imply at a notion of similarity to the other, neither of them have an exact, or at least synonymous, relationship (in the purest form).

"Invincible" = incapable of harm and/or destruction (but a normal duration of life).
