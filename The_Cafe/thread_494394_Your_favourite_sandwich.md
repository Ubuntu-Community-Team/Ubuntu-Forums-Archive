### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-07-07
**Nutter**
Natural peanut butter, blackberry jam (seeds included), and a layer of sliced banana on homemade cracked wheat.  Excellent with light/baked potato chips.

**Prime Roast Kaiser**
Hot roast beef cut in chunks not slices, carmalized carrots, and onions with horsradish and au jus for dipping on a toasted kaiser.  This goes quite well with a good caeser salad and chardonnay au rosé. (j'aime les vin rouge)

**Black Tie**
Angus rib eye sliced and sautéed with shiitake and portabello mushrooms topped with altamonte suisse topped open face on toasted potato bun, garnish with sliced raw bell peppers of various color.  Served best with steamed veggie medley.

**Deep Sea**
Crab meat mixed with diced celery, minced green onion, in a cream cheese/mayonnaise sauce.  Top with slices of cucumber and avacado.  Garnish with sprouts or grape tomato slices.  Serve on italian loaf.  Best served with chilled veggies.

These are my favorites.  Any questions on "building" these mugs, let me know.
