### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-03-22
Gnome-Look.org has previews. Deviantart.com has tiled previews, more (I think) like what you're asking for, but many, many more people contribute themes on Gnome-Look.

Like a lot of folks, I'm using [Orta]("http://www.webupd8.org/2010/12/orta-theme-gets-ubuntu-ppa.html") right now. It's a uniquely well-integrated theme with a lot of thought put into usability and visual coherence.
