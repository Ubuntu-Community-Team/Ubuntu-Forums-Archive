[QUOTE=codypumper]I've never used IRC, and jabber doesn't seem to be working. How can I join the iRC chat?[/QUOTE]
1. Download [http://jircii.hick.org/cgi-bin/jirc.cgi?ACTION=VIEW&PAGE=Download](http://jircii.hick.org/cgi-bin/jirc.cgi?ACTION=VIEW&PAGE=Download)
2. Install and open it up
3. Click on the lighting bolt symbol in the upper left hand corner
4. On menu hold your mouse over Connect
5. Click on "Server"
6. Look for freenode on the list of servers
7. Connect to freenode and click the 4th icon on the bar of icons at the top. It looks like a globe siting on a newspaper
8. When all the channels come up do a search for #Ubuntu and double click on it

jIRCii is good and it'll find the more popular of IRC networks on it but it doens't automatically connect to IRC if you click on a link in your web browser and they haven't updated it in forever so it makes me wonder if its an abondoned project. Most people on the forums will tell you to use XChat which is probably better but all I've used is jIRCii so I can give you exact instructions on how to use it, not with XChat. Also, the j in the name stands for Java so you've gotta have that installed.
