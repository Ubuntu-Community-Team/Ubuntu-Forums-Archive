### Post by Footissimo on 2007-05-19
Well there's lots of ways..but one really easy thing to do is to put your hardware in the [Hardware Compatibility List (HCL)](http://www.linuxquestions.org/hcl/) at LinuxQuestions.org and then to review it...if it's there already, then add another review.  It's a really useful resource and has been around for a while..and only a tiny proportion of the ubuntuforums users reviewing their hardware would make a difference.

Apologies if this is rather adverty..but I have no connection with the site and it would be really good to expand on one of the best HCLs out there.
