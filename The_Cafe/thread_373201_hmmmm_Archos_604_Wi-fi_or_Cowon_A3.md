Ive also heard that audio qaulity is not all that great on them but I find that hard to believe since my other 2 players (Gmini 120 and Gmini 402 CC) have excellent audio quality. The Archos also has a touchscreen (which I would sooooooo love to have) and the Wi-fi capabilities are freakin awsome. When I speak of the Wi-fi im not just talking about the web browser (which is awsome too) but the ability to add/delete files on over the wi-fi and to be able to stream vidoe, music, pictures from other computers in the network you are using. the Archos is also somewhat smaller and has a larger screen but Ive heard picture quality isn't as good as the Cowon (but that makes sense because of the touch screen)

Basically if the money were not a factor I would totally go for the Archos (I can afford the Archos but I'll be coughing up a fair amount more money for it over the Cowon) (DVR station = $100 US, extra codec packs = $20 US, case = $25 US)
