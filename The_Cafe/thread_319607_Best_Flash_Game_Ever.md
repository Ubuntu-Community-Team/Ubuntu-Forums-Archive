### Post by ~LoKe on 2006-12-15
[Winter Bells](http://www.ferryhalim.com/orisinal/g3/bells.htm)
Hitting each bell adds +10 points to the next bell.  Example, the first bell is worth 10 points, second is worth 20, third is worth 30, etc.  Hitting the birds will double your score.  It's impossible to get an insanely high score if you miss any bird other than the first or second.

Post your scores, screenshots required for proof.
