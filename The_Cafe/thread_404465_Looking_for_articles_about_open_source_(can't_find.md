### Post by josephus on 2007-04-08
Sorry can't really do your homework for you. But it's not too hard to find what you are looking for on Google.  Here's a hint: if you want sources from a particular agency such as the bbc then limit your search with the site option:

For example, this search term in google returns a number of good hits

```
open source software site:bbc.co.uk
```

But if you want primary sources rather than secondary sources like news agencies, then try limiting your results by specific government sites (e.g. site:parliament.uk).
