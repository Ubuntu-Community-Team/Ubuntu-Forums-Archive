### Post by Thee on 2014-08-21
From a designers point of view, I would remove all the logos, reorganize the text and change the fonts and background too. Here is why:

- All of the logos are in different color, making the cover too colorful, which doesn't look good.
- 4 different fonts on one cover doesn't really work. 2 would be enough. And no "comic sans" type fonts... it looks unprofessional.
- I know the background is suppose to resemble the Ubuntu theme, but that can be done in a nicer way. This just doesn't fit good.
- Text needs to be properly organized, watching out for common mistakes, like positioning, style, etc.
