### Post by andyfied on 2013-12-11
I have used the Sansa Clip+ for many years with no problems. I recently got a Clip Zip which also connects to Ubuntu perfectly, but seems a bit more sensitive to not being ejected from my Win8 machine (I always seem to remember to eject it from my Lubuntu box!). AFAIK the Fuse+ should be fine as well. They all mount as drives with the internal memory and SD card showing as separate drives. The Fuze+ is also compatible with [http://www.rockbox.org/](http://www.rockbox.org/) if you get bored of the stock interface or need more features. RockBox will play near enough any audio file and scrobbles and does all kind of other stuff.

Compatibility aside, they are all great little mp3 players with good audio and decent battery life. Bear in mind that I have never used a Fuse+ and there are a few complaints about the button sensitivity on the internet.
