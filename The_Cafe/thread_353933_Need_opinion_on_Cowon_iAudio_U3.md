### Post by Crashmaxx on 2007-02-05
I have their U2 and it is excellent. The quality is above that of most mp3 players and it works fine with Ubuntu.

The only thing I recommend watching out for, is that it will be mounted as removable media, and if you have your settings wrong, it won't delete files. Which can cause some major problems. The first time I used it with Ubuntu, I deleted all the music off it and then was wondering why I had no space left on it. It turns out that everything got moved to the trash folder, but not deleted. So I stupidly formated it and reloaded the firmware, but all I needed to do was plug it back in and empty my trash.

So either have your settings so you delete files immediatly for removable drives, or make sure you empty your trash when you delete files off it. That is the only problem I had.
