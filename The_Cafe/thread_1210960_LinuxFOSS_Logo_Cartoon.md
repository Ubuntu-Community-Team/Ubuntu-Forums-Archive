### Post by Sean4000 on 2009-07-12
Hello everyone,

With the release of Blender 2.5, I wanted to begin my biggest project to date; a 5-10 minute cartoon featuring animated Linux logos solving IT problems in a fictional toon world. It might sound lame but I am aiming at the slightly younger crowd in an effort to raise awareness of Linux in newer generations. This is a completely non-for profit endeavor. 

My concerns are mainly copyright and trademarks.

The characters are:

Tux the penguin
Suzanne the blender ape
Konqi the KDE dragon
Geeko the SuSE Lizard
Pidgin's purple bird
Firefox' orange fox
GIMP the dog
Cinepaint's cartoon drip
Adium's green duck
X the xubuntu mouse

Which of these will get me thrown in jail?

I would love to proceed with this endeavor but I do not want to step on anyone's toes, so to speak.

Take care,
Sean
