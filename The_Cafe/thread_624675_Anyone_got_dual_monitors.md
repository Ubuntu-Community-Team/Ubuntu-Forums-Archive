### Post by mustang357 on 2007-11-27
Yes I do.  I have two Samsung Syncmasters at 20" each.  I've had them since August of this year and I swear by them.  I recently went home from school with just my laptop, and dragged out an old 17" monitor just to be able to work in dual screens.

As for desktop configuration I have a single desktop that spans both screens, but provide the Xinerama info through Twinview so that a maximized window will span just one screen.  It's convenient for watching a video while working, or working with multiple windows where it would be a major inconvenience to keep obscuring them.

Recently there have been many sales on LCD panels.  If you have the money (and desk space) to do so, I highly recommend two identical panels.
