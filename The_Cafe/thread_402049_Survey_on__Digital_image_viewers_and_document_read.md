### Post by sharad on 2007-04-05
I'm working on a open source project for document readers, for which
I'd like to invite you to complete this survey,

[url]http://www.uni-koblenz.de/~soma/surveys/index.php?sid=2

In this survey, we are trying to get a first insight into the way
people use document and image viewers.Our goal is to adjust the Open
Source document reader Okular to people's needs.The survey will take
about 10 -15 minutes of your time. We will ask a few questions about
yourself and your computer experience to gather statistical data on
how you interact with images and documents about your usage of
document and image viewers and lastly, we'll ask you about the
importance of several features for your use.
