### Post by MrD_scifi on 2010-04-29
The term is "borrowing" when they use words from another language in their own, especially between themselves.  Usually because of cultural aspects, the words are cool, and a lot of it is because of internet growth around the world bringing language and media into all countries.  Don't mistake it for a pidgin or creole.  Those are used to communicate between users speaking different languages requiring a tool where a lingua franca is not available.
