### Post by Rasa1111 on 2010-10-09
Well,

 My experience~

 I had no desire to even try 10.10, I was perfectly happy with Lucid, 
even though I still had a few minor issues with it. [graphics]. 
But I loved it, and didnt care to switch at all. 

 Then I got thinking.. 
"hmm,  I wonder if the few bugs I still have in 10.04 will be fixed in 10.10?"

So,  my curiosity got the best of me, and I downloaded 10.10 Beta. 

To my surprise~ everything worked perfectly! 
except for the few bugs a beta still has, 
There were no major issues at all.

 So I went ahead after a few days running from the CD,
and installed it on its own partition.  

 Now I am running 10.10 RC, on full install, 
and it is just amazing!

I completely removed 10.04 and running only 10.10 now. 

 Ive installed 10.10 RC on about 5 different PC's now, 
laptops and desktops..
and have not seen one issue yet! (knocks on wood)

in 10.04,  GIMP had a few small issues, stellarium would not work at all,
AWN had a couple small issues, and a few other things I cant recall right now. 

 But, I have seen nothing of the sort in 10.10. 

 Everything absolutely, "Just works!" 

 It is beautiful! 

 This should be the LTS. lol  <3
