### Post by zenwhen on 2006-06-25
It is a non-issue. I should preface this post by stating that I am the channel owner.

Mark Shuttleworth and I have discussed the channel via email, and he was very positive about it. He doesn't care if the channel is "official" or not so long as the CoC is being respected. We are "official" in that we are recognized by the people who run these forums as the official channel of these forums. We may not be "official" so far as official Canonical pages are concerned, but I am not so much concerned with that, as providing a channel to mirror these forums. 

I suppose I could contact someone about getting the channel listed on that page, but truthfully it won't much affect the channel's operations or purpose.

The channel exists to provide our users a place to talk and enjoy themselves. If it continues to exist, it will be for that reason.
