### Post by DirtDawg on 2008-04-08
There are all kinds of great games for cli, especially roguelikes. Check out [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=603292&highlight=empire") and [this one]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=603292&highlight=empire") for some ideas. I think "Dungeon Crawl" is now called [Stone Soup]("http://crawl-ref.sourceforge.net") (great game), and you should check out [Doom Roguelike]("http://doom.chaosforge.org/").

I'm also a fan of [Super Star Trek]("http://almy.us/sst.html") and the fun, but complex, Empire(check the repositories). 

There's lots more too. You just have to do a little scrounging around on the net. I could never figure the bsd games out, I think the controls are confusing, but for a good tetris game, check out [vitetris]("http://victornils.net/tetris/").
