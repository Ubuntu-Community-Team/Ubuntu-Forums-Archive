### Post by lemming465 on 2011-04-18
Be sure to play with either Fedora or Redhat as well; a lot of the commercial Linux use is Redhat, and while the both the underlying principles and components are pretty similar between different Linux distributions, the details of system administration (How is networking configure?  What does package management look like? Does it use SeLinux?) vary more than you might expect.
