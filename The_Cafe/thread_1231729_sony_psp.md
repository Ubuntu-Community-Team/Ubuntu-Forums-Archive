### Post by Tipped OuT on 2009-08-04
The 2000 model is the best one.

1. Slimer
2. Lighter
3. More features in the firmware available (like Skype)
4. More RAM (smoother game play)
5. It's hackable (can use custom firmware)

The 3000 model is just an overpriced 2000 model with ugly scan lines on the screen, and it's not hackable.
