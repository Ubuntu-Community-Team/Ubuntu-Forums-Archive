UbuntuHCL.org is a hardware compatibility database for Ubuntu, Debian, and LInux Mint. We are looking at adding more distros in the near future, as well as a complete site redesign.

The site is growing exponentially:

151,994 hits in in Sept-Dec 2007
Over 19,000 hits already in 2008!

The site is very user friendly, and in my opinion it beats the official HCL wiki by far. Features include average manufacturere ratings, average product ratings, distribution/version specific reviews, and even allows users to review existing products, or add their own to the database!

Visit today and contribute!
[www.ubuntuhcl.org](www.ubuntuhcl.org)
