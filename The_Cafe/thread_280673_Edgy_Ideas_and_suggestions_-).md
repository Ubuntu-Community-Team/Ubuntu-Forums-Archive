### Post by unlokia on 2006-10-19
Hi people!. Just downloaded and installed the daily Edgy build i386 ISO for 18th October - I cannot WAIT for this to be released as final edition!. Some (possibly minor) points I have noticed, I thought that I would just give my opinions, because every opinion counts:

(1) The default wallpaper - what gives?! - the KNOT3 default wallpaper was a VAST improvement - why have we gone back to the *okay*, but not QUITE so aethetically appealing, "old style" default background??. I think maybe a vote could be taken on this.

(2) The startup screen (where the progress bar scrolls across) looks VERY amateurish - apologies to the creator, but it looks just thrown together - not nice - the colour scheme is halfway there, but it looks tacky ihmo. Some subtle styles and colours would be a lot nicer to look at, for what is (in some cases) quite a substantial time.

(3) I like the way that the login/logout sounds have been edited, so they retain the audible style, but are trimmed down to suit the login/logout period. Very good!. The old Ubuntu versions were fine, but getting a little stale (But never as stale as w1nd0z3.... Motorola mobile phones have a habit of recycling stale outdated ringtones also :???: )

(4) As the owner of a laptop using the intel 915M chipset, I think it would be a great option to be able to select "915resolution" in Synaptic, by default, when the distro is a fresh install, instead of having to edit sources.list or update Synaptic. The 1280x800 WXGA widescreen notebook panel is a very widely used format nowadays, and I just think this should be catered for by some easy-to-install means, instead of having to be "Linux-Guru" material. I mean it is okay for us, but what about the people who don't know bash or command line stuff??.

I hope this post isn't mis-interpreted as nitpicking - I *LOVE* Ubuntu now, I have a desire to use it everyday, whereas before ( < 6.10) it was a matter of "yeah it's okay but over-hyped".

I absolutely LOVE Ubuntu :D

Please just take these views into account - (please :-D )

Best Regards to you all - GREAT WORK... AMAZING OS!!!
