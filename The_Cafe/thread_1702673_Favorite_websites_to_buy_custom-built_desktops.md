### Post by Lucradia on 2011-03-08
I go in person to a local computer shop after finding each part separate on a site like newegg.com. They order the parts, build it, boom; computer. It's still going to end up more expensive if you really desire raw video editing, etc. I suggest tweaking your budget higher.

If you plan to do webcam + screencast, it may cost even more. If you need HDMI Input, you need to order a BlackMagic PCI device.
