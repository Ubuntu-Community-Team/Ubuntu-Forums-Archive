Computer Chronicles and Net Cafe were two of my fave computer shows going back to the early 80's, and I attribute much of where I'm at today to the shows since they were the only televised computer-related shows during the 80's and early 90's.

It's funny this comes up now because Retrobits Podcast did an [interview with Stewart Cheifet]("http://retrobits.libsyn.com/index.php?post_id=620244") that's really good.  Part 1 came out I think a few weeks ago and I'm still waiting to hear Part 2 since it hasn't been released yet.  And yeah most of the episodes are now available on Archive.org.  For anyone interested in tech history these are timeless to watch.
