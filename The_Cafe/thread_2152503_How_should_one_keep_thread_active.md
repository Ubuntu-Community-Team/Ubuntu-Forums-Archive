### Post by t0p on 2013-06-09
As far as I know, a thread doesn't automatically close.  It stays open, but as more posts on other threads are made, they push your thread down the list, until it's not on the 1st page, and no one's likely to post on it unless they actively search for it.

So bumping doesn't "keep the thread open" - it just ensures that that the thread is bumped back up to the top of the list.  If it disappears off the front page, it means no one has posted on it.  So you could bump it again... but in my experience 1 bump is generally enough, if it still attracts no posts that probably means no one can help, and you'll have to find help in some other way. [ Google Is Your Friend]("https://ihatehate.wordpress.com/2010/03/29/how-to-search-the-internet-1-the-history-of-search/").  ;)
