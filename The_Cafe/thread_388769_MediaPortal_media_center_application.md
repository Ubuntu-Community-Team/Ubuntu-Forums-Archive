### Post by teet on 2007-03-19
MediaPortal better than MythTV?  

I've tried to use mediaportal a few times but it was always slow and crashed a lot (on two different machines, with two different tuner cards).  MythTV was much faster and is a snap to setup if you have the right hardware (e.g. with a Hauppauge PVR-150 all you have to do is copy and paste a few commands from the community wiki to get it running like a champ).

I would highly suggest sending the card back.  I've really been pleased with my Hauppauge PVR-150...plus it was less than $60 (with shipping!).
