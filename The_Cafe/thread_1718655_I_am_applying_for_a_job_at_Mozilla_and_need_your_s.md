### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-03-31
Some time ago, someone suggested here in the forums, that I should apply for a job at Mozilla, because of my passion about Firefox Add-ons and community support.

Today I received a rss feed from Mozilla about job openings and one of the positions is "Add-ons Community Manager", which I believe is perfectly tuned with what I have been doing here in the forums and through extension development.

I really would like to be part of Mozilla team and I couldn't think about a better way of showing my involvement with Firefox users support, than asking the Ubuntu community opinion.

So here I am. If you think the job below has my face on it, please leave some words of encouragement.

> **Add-ons Community Manager **

Marketing | Mountain View (Remote Possible), CA, United States 

Mozilla is looking for an experienced Community Manager to join the Firefox Add-ons team. With more than 2.2 billion downloads and hundreds of millions in use every day, add-ons are critical to Firefox's success as the most customizable browser. We're committed to enabling new and innovative web experiences for ~450M users and tens of thousands of developers worldwide. As a public face of the add-ons team, you will engage with our community of users and developers to solicit feedback, provide support, organize events, and share findings with the team. 

**Responsibilities:**

[LIST]
[*]Proactively engage and assist add-on users through official channels (addons.mozilla.org, forums, blog, Twitter, Facebook, etc.) as well as unofficial channels (external blogs, forums, etc.) 
[*]Identify, analyze, and report on top issues, patterns, and trends surfaced through user feedback and outreach 
[*]Establish and monitor key community participation and satisfaction metrics 
[*]Evangelize product features and provide support by writing blog posts, maintaining FAQs, creating videos/screencasts, and other tools 
[*]Collaborate with Product Marketing Managers to develop and execute campaigns to increase add-on awareness, usage, and participation in features like user reviews and collections 
[*]Work with the Add-on Developer Relations Lead to ensure communication channels are open between Mozilla, users, and developers 
[*]Plan and organize events and contests for add-on users and developers
[/LIST]

**Requirements:**

[LIST]
[*]Excellent written and verbal communication skills 
[*]Familiarity with web technologies and Firefox add-ons 
[*]Experience building and nurturing a community 
[*]Passionate about helping people and solving problems 
[*]Enjoys learning and teaching others 
[*]Works effectively in a fast-paced, start-up environment 
[*]BA/BS, or equivalent experience
[/LIST]
