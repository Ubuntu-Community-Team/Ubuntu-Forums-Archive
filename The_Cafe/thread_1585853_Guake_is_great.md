### Post by papangul on 2010-10-01
Actually it's amazing! For the clueless, let me mention here that Guake is a terminal emulator like Gnome terminal. It has replaced the functions of both gnome terminal and the Alt+F2 window for me. 

It has so many features but out of those the following need special mention:
1. It's transparent.
2. When it starts-up, it's window is not visible, only it's icon is visible in the tray area. **Press F12 and voila!** The window appears in a "top-down" way.
3.Press F12 and it becomes full screen.
4.Double-click on the bottom-bar and another tab opens.

To enjoy it fully, it has to be added to System->Preferences->Startup Applications.
