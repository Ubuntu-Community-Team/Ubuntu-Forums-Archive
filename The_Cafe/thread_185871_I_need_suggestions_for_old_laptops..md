### Post by arsenic23 on 2006-06-01
Here's something I would do, until it got on my nerves.

I'd take about 6 fairly useless laptops and removed the screens.  Then run layout on a large board, setting all the screens up so that their cables run behind them and then through the board.  Mount the main units on the back and install something old and pretty on them.  Windows98 and winamp2 or 3 maybe, with Geiss or something similar installed, it doesn't really matter.  You can then hook them all up to on power strip you also mount on the back.

You could them have a very large wall hanging that could be changed to suit your need.  You could even have them all cycling through images of your family or of places you've been.  They could run movie trailers or anything really.  

You might want to put another very thin board over top and cover it with cloth or something, leaving holes just enough to see the screen area of the LCD panels and not the trim.
