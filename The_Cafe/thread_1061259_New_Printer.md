### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-02-06
I have a couple of HP Officejet 5610 all-in-one printers that work great on the HPLIP downloaded driver. Even the scanner is fully functional (XSane). I purchase the rebuilt NuKote cartilages locally for about $12.00 US. We don't do much with photos and the fax option (it does work - just don't fax that often).

One of the few vendors that appears to truly support Linux. Sad to think we can't convince other vendors to meet the challenge. Printers seem to be one hardware group that either supports Linux or doesn't - very little in between. HP is one that has shown a perpensity to not only writes drivers for Linux, but also keep them updated.

I actually sent a e-mail to HP thanking them for this service and notifying them of my loality. They responded with an extremely nice return and thanked me for reccommending HP to others. IMHO it never hurts to inform vendors of our appreciation and remind them that we commend their efforts.
