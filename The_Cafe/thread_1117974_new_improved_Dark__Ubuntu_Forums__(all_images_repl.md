### Post by tjwoosta on 2009-04-06
Ok, So Ive been spending alot of time this week working on making a darker easier on the eyes, Ubuntu forums

I have replaced 90% of the icons as well as fixed all of the colors for the reminder boxes, announcements, etc.

Now some of you may remember seeing something about this last week on the forums, but unfortunatly not many of you were happy with what I achieved, and to be honest neither was I

But after some constructive criticism, and a few hours of tweaking I feel that I have finally achieved what I was after


on to the point !

The way this works is through the use of a firefox extension called stylish

what is stylish?

> Stylish allows easy management of user styles. User styles empower your browsing experience by letting you fix ugly sites, customize the look of your browser or mail client, or just have fun. With an online repository at userstyles.org, you don't even need to know how to write styles yourself; just a couple clicks and the chosen style is applied. Stylish is to CSS what Greasemonkey is to JavaScript, and unlike other methods of using user styles, most styles take effect immediately.


To use the new Dark Ubuntu Forums follow theese simple steps

**1.** Install the stylish extension in firefox  
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2108]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2108")

**2.** Download my style  
It comes in 5 patrs, with two different versions of part 1 (one is for borderless and the other is with the border)

(you can find links to the other 4 parts from part 1)

Its simple just start with part 1 (either borderless or original, make a choice) and click the link that says "load into stylish" then go the the next part, and so on untill you have all 5 parts

part 1 (original) [http://userstyles.org/styles/16624]("http://userstyles.org/styles/16624")

part 1 (borderless)  [http://userstyles.org/styles/16677]("http://userstyles.org/styles/16677")


**3.** ENJOY!


**4.** Comment! Please, I would love to see some feedback from the community

Constructive Critisism Welcome!

if there are any problems with any of the links or pages that you can see please let me know and i will try to fix it 


also if anybody notices any icons that are missing, or need touching up, please just attach or paste a copy of the page source and describe what you are talking about and i will see what i can do

EDIT: 

also sry for the inconvience, but any of you who are using the version of the forums that i released last week will need to remove the old style and install all 5 parts of the new one in order to get everything working
