### Post by MikeTheC on 2009-05-09
I remember one time, it was the summer of '83, and I was hiking through the dense tropical wilderness of Southwest Florida when I spotted one of these legendary creatures. It was amazing and thrilling to see them traveling along the western edge of the forest, which eventually terminates in a beach, and they would sprint in packs for miles and miles at a time. Oftentimes they would turn east and head for the more mountainous terrain, keeping their distance and ranging through their territory.

Majestic animals, all of them. You should see the markings on the females, especially about their fore-shoulders and flanks. They would make equines the world over weep with envy.
