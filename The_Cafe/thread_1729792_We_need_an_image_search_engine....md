### Post by forrestcupp on 2011-04-15
Tineye for the win.

It's not perfect, but it's the best we have. Sometimes you can figure out what something is based on the search results.

If you have Android, there is a pretty awesome app called Google Goggles. You can use your camera to take a picture of something, it scans the picture to figure out what it is, and brings up search results based on what it figures out. Pretty cool.
