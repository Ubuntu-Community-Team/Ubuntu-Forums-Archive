### Post by Cope57 on 2011-04-27
Religion and politics will always be subjects of debate, and this forum is "G" rated.  Having discussions about religion or politics is most likely to end in a flame war or such.

There are plenty of religious and political forums out there, and those discussions should be handled in a manner which those forums are accustomed to.

This forum has a wide range of religion, and politics which all members have, but this is not the place to do so.  If you are so inclined to convey your opinions on the matter, try these forums.


[LIST]
[*]At [Religious Forums.com]("http://www.religiousforums.com/forum/") you can discuss, compare, and debate world religion, religion and spirituality, true religion, religious education and many other religious issues in our Religious Forum.

[*][The Politics Forum]("http://politicsforum.org/") is intended for use by adults to discuss politics. If you wish to partake in discussion you must abide by the forum rules. If you are a first time visitor you may find A Quick Introduction to the Politics Forum helpful.
[/LIST]

### Post by tjwoosta on 2011-04-27

There are many other forums where such topics are not only accepted but thrived upon. (check my signature ;) ) I recommed posting at those places instead as they tend to pull a more open audience thats willing to hear out and debate such things in a calm and collected manner.
