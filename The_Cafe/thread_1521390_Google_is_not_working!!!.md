Here's the "problem." Google has moved the new SSL Beta to [encrypted.google.com]("https://encrypted.google.com"). The reason for this is because many school networks began blocking [https://*.google.com](https://*.google.com) to prevent students from browsing anonymously. In doing so, they were also accidentally blocking Google Apps for Education. As a result, Google changed the address so that schools can now block encrypted.google.com and still use Google Apps.
