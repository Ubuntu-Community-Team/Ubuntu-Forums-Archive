### Post by ExpatPaul on 2008-02-05
> **ewanchic said:**
> 2. MS wins be scoping up linux programmers and abandoning good linux technology on the shelf. Boo Hoo opensource users.


This is why I think Microsoft will struggle if this deal goes ahead. 

Most (if not all) of the companies that have built successful web businesses have done so on the basis of predominantly open standards. Their sites work regardless of the particular combination of browser and OS that you happen to have. And this will create a dilemma for Microsoft.

Either, they scrap all the open technologies that Yahoo! is using and spend a fortune building yet another proprietary web presence that no-one visits. Or, they stick with the open technologies which will make it increasingly difficult to sell their proprietary software to anyone else.

Microsoft managed to miss out on the web and are getting increasingly desperate to catch up.
