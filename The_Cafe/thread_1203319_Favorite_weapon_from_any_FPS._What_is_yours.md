### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-07-05
I liked the Fusion Pistol from Marathon. The primary trigger fired a slow-moving shot that was a little more powerful than the pistol, and more effective than the pistol against robots. The secondary trigger, when held down, would charge up a slow-moving bold that could be as powerful as a combat shotgun. The rate of fire in primary fire mode was pretty good too.

3rd-party mods usually set the second-fire shot speed for the Fusion Pistol at zero, effectively creating a "fusion mine".

I also liked the shock rifle from the original Unreal Tournament. The secondary trigger fired a large, slow-moving projectile. If you hit it with the instantaneous primary shot, it would explode and do much more damage to nearby enemies. I could never make it an effective weapon, but it was pretty cool.
