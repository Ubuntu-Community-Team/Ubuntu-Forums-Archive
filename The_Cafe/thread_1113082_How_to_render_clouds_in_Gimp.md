### Post by Jordanwb on 2009-04-01
I'm trying to make clouds in Gimp. I set the foreground colour to a shade of blue but when I use the Reder clouds filter I get black and white clouds.

---

### Post by saulgoode on 2009-04-01
After you have rendered your black & white clouds, perform a "Colors->Map->Gradient Map" (with your FG and BG colors blue and white).
