### Post by migla on 2007-07-25
Get together with a few local Linux-users (unless you're rich enough to go about this on your own) and donate a subscription for some linux magazine (or several of them) to your local library. This way you can save money by sharing the cost of a subscription and maybe recruit unsuspecting librarygoers to the wonderful world of a free operating system.

I haven't queried whether my library would accept a donation like this, but I have seen some sticker on some book stating that it was donated by some organization.
