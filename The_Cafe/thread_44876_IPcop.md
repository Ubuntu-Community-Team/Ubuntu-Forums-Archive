### Post by bdamon on 2005-06-27
IPCop has served me well for a couple of years as a home/small office firewall. I have not had a high number if users behind it, but for what I have used it, it has performed admirably There are also some excellent addons available and a pretty decent mailing list.

Also have a look at Endian Firewall which is based on IPCop and has some great features - antivirus support, virus and spamfiltering for email.

[http://www.efw.it/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://www.efw.it/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)

I have not tried Endian yet but I do want to give it a look.
