### Post by t0p on 2009-11-18
There's a Firefox add-on called **Gspace**.  It enables you to use a Gmail inbox as an online drive.  The [Gmail front page]("http://mail.google.com") currently claims that it provides over 7000 megabytes inbox space.  Go make yourself a few Gmail accounts, and you'll get several gigabytes of free online storage.
