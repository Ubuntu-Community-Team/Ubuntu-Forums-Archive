### Post by sdowney717 on 2010-10-02
[http://www.localphone.com/](http://www.localphone.com/)
If you use localphone, then you need to turn on voip in the user control to get the ATA to register.

[http://www.iptel.org/](http://www.iptel.org/)

I found either one of these works. I was using sipgate, but then it quit working with GV. Sipgate will give you a free DID incoming number in your area code.
Once registered you link the voip user to IPKALL phone number
[http://www.ipkall.com/](http://www.ipkall.com/)

Then you link your GV to IPKALL

When making outgoing calls, I noticed I need to let my phone ring on ring or GV wont call the other party.
And I noticed I need to wait a minute after logging into GV to get GV to work making calls.

MY ATA device is a Linksys SPA2102
