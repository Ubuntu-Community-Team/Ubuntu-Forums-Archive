### Post by Supergoo on 2007-03-18
I made a earlier thread about debating on dropping Windows Vista, Well it finally happened and my PC is now 100% free of any Microsoft Products. :KS I had planned to use Winxp but it seems when you activate Vista you XP Key is junked. SO I sat down and thought I do not game anymore besides World of Warcraft and I have my iMac for that which works just fine. So other then that I did not need to have Windows I have everything I needed to fit my needs. My Wife just wants to surf the web and Check her Webbased Yahoo Mail and play Majaongg. being able to customize it to her needs is nice since she has some health issues and being able to configure the Desktop so its easy for her has been a blessing. You know Vista was a pain to install and then less then 1 month from the install it had already started to have issues , computer slow downs and asorted other items that are to new to even name. I would recommend anybody who is considering switch to use these steps and I beleive that ubuntu will win you over, it did me . 

1. Play with the Live CD
2. Partition and instal ubuntu along side you Windows OS,play with ubuntu try installing programs try the other desktop Managers, install the drivers for you Video card ect ect, live with it kick the tires and enjoy. If after this you do not like ubuntu its ok try another Linux or just delete Linux and head back to Windows ( We still Like you :) )
3. Format the harddrive and install ubuntu and do not look back :popcorn: 



Good Luck and thanks to all the people who helped make this conversion fun ,exciting and almost painless :lolflag:
