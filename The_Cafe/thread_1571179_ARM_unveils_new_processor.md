> **ARM unveils processor for new markets; shares up**

Thu Sep 9, 2010 4:50am EDT
By Paul Sandle

LONDON, Sept 9 (Reuters) - ARM Holdings (ARM.L) unveiled a new low-power processor for next-generation smartphones, mobile computing, servers and wireless networking on Thursday, sending shares in the British chip designer to an eight-year-high. The A15 processor, codenamed Eagle and part of ARM's Cortex A family, delivers five-times better performance than today's advanced smartphone processors, ARM said in a statement.

It will support functions such as 3D navigation, augmented reality -- where data is superimposed on real images -- and high-speed broadband...
