### Post by stephanie-s on 2020-04-06
Well I'm posting this from my brand new Galago Pro 4 from System76.  It arrived by UPS today.

I had it pre-configured with Ubuntu 18.4 rather than System76's own Pop!_OS. Unboxing was simple enough, and they even include stickers.  The basic specs  are:

Intel® Core™ i5-10210U CPU @ 1.60GHz × 8 
Intel® UHD Graphics (Comet Lake 3x8 GT2)
Samsung SSD 970 EVO Plus 250GB
8 Gb Ram
Gnome 3.28.2

With the conversion from US$ to Cdn and import duty added it was a little more than I wanted to pay, but after working with it for a day I'm very, very happy, and glad that I bought it.  I can now put away my old 2012 MacBook Pro.  Happy Days.
