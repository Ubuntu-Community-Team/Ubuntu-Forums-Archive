### Post by handy on 2008-12-12
There is quite a range of MiniITX motherboards, cases & the like.

There are more than one that have 2 NIC's built in, though with the 1 PCI slot, you can easily add another NIC, but it can mean that you would have to go to a bigger case than if the 2 were built in.

MiniITX make great server's, & workstations providing your needs don't go beyond their limitations.
