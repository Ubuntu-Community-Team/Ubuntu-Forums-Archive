### Post by jespdj on 2008-12-09
> 5 reasons you should quit your job!

I quit my job in October and I'm starting my own one person IT consulting (software development) business. It's very popular to do this, I have a whole list of ex-colleagues who have done the same in the past two years, and all of them say they have absolutely no regrets - they earn much more money and have much more freedom then when they were working as an employee.

Ofcourse you have less security - you'll have to hunt for work yourself, and if you don't work you don't get any money.

On the other hand, if I look at how much money my employer now earns on me and how little my employer really does for me, then I'd be crazy to let them have all that money. (About three quarters of the money that they get from the client goes into my employers' pocket!).
