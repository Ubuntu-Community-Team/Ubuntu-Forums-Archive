### Post by beercz on 2009-03-05
I have recently sat the[ LPI 101 and 102]("http://lpi.org/eng/certification/the_lpic_program/lpic_1") exams and am currently waiting the result, which is due any time now!

I wanted non-specific distro certification and assuming I pass I will start working on my level 2.

I think certification adds to one's credibility - and as I am setting myself up as an independent consultant, I think the certification will improve my credentials.  Again, assuming I will pass I will add the accreditation to my website (see my sig).

Besides that I learnt an awful lot during the training prior to sitting the exams and it was good to have a couple of weeks intensive linux discussions with like minded people - something I don't do very often.

I must say I did find the exams a little tricky.

Well, we'll see how it goes - wish me luck guys!!!
