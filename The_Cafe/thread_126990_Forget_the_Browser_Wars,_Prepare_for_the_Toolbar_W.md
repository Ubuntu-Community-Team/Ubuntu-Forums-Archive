### Post by cutOff on 2006-02-07
Fighting for icon space on the desktop is so 2001. The new frontier on a virgin PC is the browser, and Internet companies like Google are jostling for space on the browsers of new PCs. Dell and Google are [evaluating a partnership]("http://news.com.com/Forget+the+browser+wars%2C+prepare+for+the+toolbar+wars/2100-1012_3-6036263.html?tag=nefd.top") in which the Google Toolbar, Google Desktop Search and a [Google-designed Dell home page are included on new Dell]("http://www.google.com/ig/dell") PCs, a Dell representative confirmed.
