### Post by NetDoc on 2011-06-11
Toss your cookies! IOW, delete your internet history from your browser and see if that makes a difference. Google tries to anticipate WHAT you are looking for. Past history affects that anticipation considerably. In fact, many people who check to see if their site is #1 for a particular search criteria get fooled by these artifacts.
