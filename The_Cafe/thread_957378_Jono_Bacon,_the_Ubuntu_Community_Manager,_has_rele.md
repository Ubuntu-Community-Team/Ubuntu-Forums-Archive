### Post by PmDematagoda on 2008-10-24
Jono Bacon has been working on a project of his own called "Severed Fifth" whose purpose (as quoted verbatim):-
> Severed Fifth is a music project designed to explore how far a musician can take his music with the new economics of the music industry.

The website is [here]("http://www.severedfifth.com/"). And you can download the album in both [OGG]("http://audio.lugradio.org/severedfifth/deniedbyreign/ogg/severedfifth-deniedbyreign-full-ogg.zip") and [MP3]("http://audio.lugradio.org/severedfifth/deniedbyreign/mp3/severedfifth-deniedbyreign-full-mp3.zip") format, I believe that FLAC is [coming soon]("http://www.severedfifth.com/forums/comments.php?DiscussionID=53&Focus=249#Comment_249").

Personally, I am no great rock fan, but Jono Bacon's music was(In my opinion) pretty good. I especially love:-
Kickback
Edge of Design
War
although my only criticism is about the drums which could have used a little bit more work.

What do you think?
