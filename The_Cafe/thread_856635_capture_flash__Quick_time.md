### Post by whollycow on 2008-07-11
vlc media player is capable of playing flash video files.  you can find it in the repositories through synaptic and you can read up on it here: [videolan.org]("http://www.videolan.org").

EDIT: you could also try the [mediaplayerconnectivity extension]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446") in firefox.  together with vlc, you should be able to get the files you need.
