Awesome Chrome Extension!

[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=150648&d=1269028852[/IMG]

> An icon docks to your toolbar with a colored square indicating when your next appointment is scheduled.  Clicking the icon allows you to view your Google calendar schedule and add events.

Features: 
 - A next event tooltip with countdown and reminder badges
 - Miniature calendar and schedule listing
 - Pulls weather feed from main calendar, if enabled
 - Pulls many other calendar feeds - interesting and shared
 - Link to Calendar Home and a Quick Add input box
 - Internationalized Options Page.  See Support & FAQs link below[https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/emambmpgicpidmncfacjkeicobamadod?hl=en](https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/emambmpgicpidmncfacjkeicobamadod?hl=en)
