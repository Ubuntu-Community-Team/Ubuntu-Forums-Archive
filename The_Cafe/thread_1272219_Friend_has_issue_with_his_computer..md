### Post by Megrimn on 2009-09-21
well, he may have the ram installed incorrectly, or too much ram.  it depends on the motherboard, and bios won't POST (power on self-test) if that's the case.  

As for the 'melted plastic' smell, I don't know if ram could have caused that, as I've never had that problem... you sure he didn't perhaps bump or dislodge something and it somehow got into a position where it would get really hot?
