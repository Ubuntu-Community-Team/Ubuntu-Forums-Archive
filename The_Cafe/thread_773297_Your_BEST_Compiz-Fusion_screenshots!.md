I just took what I think is a rather cool picture with the help from Compiz. So I though: Why in a coffee cup ain't there a thread like this around already? 

Please include details on how you made the "effect".

Mine:

4 horizontal desktops
Expo with black reflection colors (far away set to 0 if you want nice reflection)
Enable cube, Opacity when not rotating: 0,0000.
Start expo and drag all windows to workspace 3.


The most important rule: Be creative!
