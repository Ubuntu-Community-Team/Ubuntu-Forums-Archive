### Post by ve4cib on 2010-04-05
A few from my collection of so-bad-it's-good movies:

- Santa Claus Conquers the Martians
- Godzilla (the original English translation, with Raymond Burr hacked into it to give American audiences a familiar face)
- War of the Gargantuas
- Plan 9 from Outer Space
- Glen or Glenda (this one is actually nigh-unwatchable)
- Manos: The Hands of Fate (this one actually is unwatchable -- the only version I could find was the MST3k one)
- The Dunwich Horror (the old one starring Dean Stockwell)
- Zarzoz (with Sean Connery wearing the worst costume in cinema history, complete with red loincloth, ammunition belts across his chest, knee-high leather boots, and a 70s porn star moustache)
- Snakes on a Plane (the intentional B-movie)
- Dungeons and Dragons 2: Wrath of the Dragon God (it's actually much better than the original D&D movie, despite being a direct-to-DVD sequel)
- Starship Troopers 1, 2, and 3 (all bad, but all fun in their own ways)
- Messiah of Evil
- Voyage to the Prehistoric Planet (this one looks like it was originally in Russian, but the Americans imported the footage and re-dubbed in in English with an unrelated plot during the Cold War)
- The Horrors of Spider Island
- Robot Monster (this is on Wikipedia's list of Worst Movies Ever)
- Eegah
- Flesh Gordon and Flesh Gordon Meets the Intergalactic Cheer Leaders (porn meets sci-fi parody -- the first one is better than the sequel)
- The Day of the Triffids (the original black-and-white one with the annoying ice cream truck)
- They Came from Beyond Space
- Thor and the Amazon Women
- Cannibal Women in the Avocado Jungle of Death (A comedy featuring Bill Mahr as the studly, heroic lead character.  The plot revolves around the Strategic North American Avocado Reserve.  Enough said.)
- Black Sheep (a zombie movie with genetically-engineered sheep instead of zombies; what could go wrong?)
- Cube and Cube 2: Hypercube (some people hate these movies, but I think they're both really good.  In a lot of ways I think Cube is the better of the two)

*whew* That was a long list.  I have many, many more bad movies in my collection.  And you can find many of them online at [Public Domain Torrents](http://www.publicdomaintorrents.com/).  Download and seed these movies; they're copyright-free and therefore legal to distribute!
