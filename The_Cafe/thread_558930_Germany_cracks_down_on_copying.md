### Post by t0p on 2007-09-24
This is *sooo* out of order!!  And, I suspect/hope that it will have to be overtuned because it contravenes European Union laws on copyright.  The German government spokesman claims that the law "brings Germany into line" with EU rules, but that's a load of crap.

In the EU, as in most "developed" countries, copyright law allows for fair use copying.  For example, it's perfectly legal for you to make a back-up copy of an audio CD you've bought; to rip an mp3 from it to listen to with your mp3 player when you're out and about... fair use is a legitimate legal expression, and no country in the EU can just ignore it.  Plus there's the Berne Convention, which laid down international copyright laws.

German governments have a habit of coming up with absurd legislation.  But, as noted by a previous poster, this law's gonna be kinda hard to enforce.  Will there be "copyright SWAT teams", bursting into your bedroom to inspect your sounds collection?
