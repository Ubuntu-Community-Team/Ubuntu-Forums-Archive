### Post by Jucato on 2007-01-05
[http://help.ubuntu.com](http://help.ubuntu.com) - contains the official Ubuntu documentation

[http://help.ubuntu.com/community](http://help.ubuntu.com/community) - being targeted as the new community/user-submitted documentation site, formerly [http://wiki.ubuntu.com](http://wiki.ubuntu.com)

[http://wiki.ubuntu.com](http://wiki.ubuntu.com) - this was formerly used to to hold the user-submitted guides and documentation. They are in the process of moving those guides to [http://help.ubuntu.com/community](http://help.ubuntu.com/community). [http://wiki.ubuntu.com](http://wiki.ubuntu.com) will become a developers' wiki, for placing development information, specs, etc. It might soon  be renamed to dev.ubuntu.com for that purpose.
