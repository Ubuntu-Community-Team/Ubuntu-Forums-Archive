### Post by Bigbluecat on 2007-05-04
This is pretty interesting.

Announced at the recent Intel Developers Forum in Beijing by Anand Chandrasekher was a new mobile device. Anand heads the group responsible for ultra mobile PC.

Amongst other things he talks about mobile internet and a new 45nm processor called Silverlight.

About two thirds of the way through the 30 minute video he pulls a prototype out of his pocket. From a distance it looks like a Nokia N800 device.


Then he announces this is running Ubuntu and demonstrates it. It is codenamed Menlow.

Ubuntu are names as an official OS partner.

The downside is that the only way I could get this video to play was to switch my Firefox rendering engine to IE. But the video is probably on YouTube.

The Intel link is here:

[http://www.intel.com/idf/webcasts.htm](http://www.intel.com/idf/webcasts.htm)

Go to Anands video at the bottom.

Well done Ubuntu guys.
