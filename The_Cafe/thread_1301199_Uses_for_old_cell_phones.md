### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-10-25
Take the batteries and glue them to the back of cheap mp3 players.  Solder wires to the internal battery.  Massive battery life for listening to 2-hour linux podcasts.

A water-logged iTouch that I found at a park gave up it's battery to my nokia n800.  6-8 hours of use vs 4 hours.
