There's no "right way" to spell it in English. There are several methods for converting arabic text to western text, so Qaddafi and Khadafi are both "correct", just like "arigatou", "arigatõ", and even "aligatou" are all correct ways to write the Japanese word for "thank you", though the last one looks silly.
