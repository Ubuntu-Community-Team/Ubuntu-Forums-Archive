### Post by lswb on 2008-08-15
Check out ebay for inexpensive notebook hard drives. An older model say 20GB will not cost much, and sometimes can be found still in the factory package.

There are also cheap external enclosures that convert a laptop drive to an external USB drive, again available on ebay. Last time I bought some they were under $10 IIRC.
