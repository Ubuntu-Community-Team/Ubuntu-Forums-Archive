### Post by d-cosner on 2015-04-05
They make SATA power adapters that fit on to the regular molex connectors. I took some off a bad power supply and put them on an older working power supply for my father-in-law. eBay might be a good place to look for these cheap.

Craigslist and eBay are good places to find cheap used computers and parts. I upgraded my processor for $10.00! Sometimes computer shops will also have used parts cheap. I have been to a couple that had bins full of used parts really cheap.

### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2015-04-05
This is hit-or-miss, and takes time for results. Some of the towns close to where I live have electronic recycling days. They are advertised on the towns website. People bring a bag of tools and wait outside the recycling center. By the time a computer gets dropped off and makes it to the recycling center they are nothing but the carcasses left over from a meal.
