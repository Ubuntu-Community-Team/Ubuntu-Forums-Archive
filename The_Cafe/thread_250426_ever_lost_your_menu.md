### Post by schurtek on 2006-09-04
The menu at the top of gnome... that reads, applications / places / system... well I lost mine over the weekend... had a small stroke... but after sitting for half an hour with a mate on the phone we figured it out... if you right click on the menu you can remove it... (which must have happened when I dumped some books on my mouse while my monitor was turned off...) but if you right click on the panel bar at the top where the menu appears... you will get an option add menu... and it will open a window with a list of panels that can be added (some really cool ones i have now discovered) and the main menu is at the bottom. Also you can get to the menu (i have now learnt) by pressint Alt+F1... I love ubuntu... with windows this would have meant a re-install...
