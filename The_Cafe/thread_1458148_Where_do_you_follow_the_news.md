### Post by samalex on 2010-04-19
I have a number of RSS feeds on my Google.com/ig homepage:
- SlashDot - [http://rss.slashdot.org/Slashdot/slashdot](http://rss.slashdot.org/Slashdot/slashdot)
- Digg - [http://digg.com/rss/index.xml](http://digg.com/rss/index.xml)
- Wired - [http://www.wired.com/news/feeds/rss2/0,2610,,00.xml](http://www.wired.com/news/feeds/rss2/0,2610,,00.xml)
- TechCrunch - [http://feeds.feedburner.com/Techcrunch](http://feeds.feedburner.com/Techcrunch)
- CNet - [http://news.com.com/2547-1_3-0-5.xml](http://news.com.com/2547-1_3-0-5.xml)
- TechDirt - [http://www.techdirt.com/techdirt_rss.xml](http://www.techdirt.com/techdirt_rss.xml)
- LinuxToday - [http://linuxtoday.com/backend/biglt.rss](http://linuxtoday.com/backend/biglt.rss)

Between all these it covers most news I care to see.  I also have about 20 podcasts I listen to weekly, though much of the news there is outdated by the time I hear it, unless I can listen to TWIT on Monday morning.

Outside of these I try to catch MSNBC.com plus the websites for a few local newspapers for local news.
