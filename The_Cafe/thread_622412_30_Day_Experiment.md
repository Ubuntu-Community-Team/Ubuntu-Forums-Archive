### Post by nickburns on 2007-11-24
In an attempt to make my life a little better, I am going to try a simple experiment. 

My 30 day experiment:

1.  No surfing the web.
2.  No RSS feeds.
3.  No Podcasts or MP3 player.
4.  No Laptop(s) at home.
5.  No Email at home.

_Because I need to stay employed:_

6.  Work emails (Only during work hours of course).
7.  Remote desktop into work, if night operator needs help.
8.  Work on computer required to keep my job. (email, programming and reports only)

_The other exception:_

9.   Post comments to my blog, concerning only the experiment. (During lunch break at work.)

Basically, I am going to make a sincere attempt to not use a computer, unless required at work.  I am able to preform all essential tasks at my work without the internet.   No more forum, no more surfing, no more wasting time.  (At least for 30 days.) 

I will start at midnight on November 24th, and go till Christmas morning.  Hopefully I will have my [XO laptop]("http://www.flippinsweetdude.com/xo-laptop/") by then.

Visit my blog [here]("http://www.flippinsweetdude.com/blog/").

Wish me luck, and see you all on the other side....
