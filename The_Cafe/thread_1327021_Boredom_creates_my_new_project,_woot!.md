### Post by nw2001 on 2009-11-15
I suppose this could be seen as some form of bump(BUUUUUUUUUUUUMP!), Anyways going to post some of my concepts for the RPG system.

First off a short concept for the combat system

Combat
There will be turns but not like most games where during your turn your opponent has limited actions.

For example, lets say theres a fight going on between two fighters and fighter1 runs low on action points(Which allow him to attack or move for instance) so he ends his turn, and the enemy starts, at the beginning of the fighter2's turn fighter2 regens all of his action points, and attacks, Fighter1 has several choices, he can dodge or parry the attack using his spare action points or perhaps just attack back.

This introduces a few new concepts, The idea of trying to keep it your turn as long as possible to keep the enemy from regenerating action points, the moment you run out of things to do on your turn it ends, It also introduces the idea that not just the player whos turn it is can do stuff, Just because its player 1's turn doesnt mean player 2 cant attack. This will introduce things like playing defensively by keeping action points for the enemies turn so you can defend, or as previously mentioned trying to keep the enemy from regaining Action points so you can take a lead, and should also make things MUCH more fast paced.


My apologies for the horrible structure but I don't have time to rewrite it, Anyways it tries to solve one major problem most of my friends tend to have with PNP RPGS, how slow paced they can be, It also makes it some what tactical imo. Hope you enjoyed the read.
