Love Notify OSD (ie Jaunty notifications), depressed that you don't get enough notifications? Announcing the 0.4 release of Rss Notify, a program that notifies you of new rss news items. 0.4 now has opml file support which means you can import feeds from other rss related programs and export all your feeds from rss notify.

[IMG]http://www.ubuntu-pics.de/bild/19165/screenshot_003_ZEfT92.png[/IMG]

Download the [deb]("https://edge.launchpad.net/rssn/+download") or add the [PPA]("https://edge.launchpad.net/~freshapplepy/+archive/rssn-ppa").
