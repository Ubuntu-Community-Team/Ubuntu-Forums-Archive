### Post by ChrisBookwood on 2008-12-05
That's right guys - Many big Linux distros (Ubuntu, Fedora, Mandriva, and others I believe) builds there openoffice *edition* upon Go-oo, since there's many features in the official OpenOffice.org suite, that could have been, but because if copyright this, and you can't do that thingies, Sun doesn't let those features get through to development, so if you wan't those features, you have to use Go-oo.

Not to say, that OpenOffice.org suite is a waste of time, cause without it, Go-oo wouldn't be anything, since the people behind Go-oo is all about adding features, while the folks at OpenOffice.org take care of the rest of the application.
One could say that it's a very nice companionship ... not officially, though.
