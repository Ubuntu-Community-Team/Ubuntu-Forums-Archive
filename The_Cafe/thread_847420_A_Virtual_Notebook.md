### Post by joninkrakow on 2008-07-02
Yeah, the Apple Newton Messagepad. It was a wonderful toy. Besides its rather controversial handwriting recognition (which always worked excellently for me), it could also do free-hand drawing, assisted drawing of shapes (draw a cruddy circle, and a perfect one popped out on the screen), but it also did so much more. Unfortunately, this was back in the days before blogs, but I seem to remember reading that there was some software for sending text to blogger... It was so far ahead of its time that there has yet to be anything that comes close to what it did--and people thought it was overpriced! ;-) No, I don't recommend anybody buying one nowadays--technology has moved on--unless you are willing to be very dedicated to it, and devoting hours upon hours to getting it to work (and some money as well)

-Jon
