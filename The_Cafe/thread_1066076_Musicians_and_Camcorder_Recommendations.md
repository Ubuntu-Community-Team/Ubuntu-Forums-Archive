### Post by Tomosaur on 2009-02-10
I have a Panasonic SDR-S7 which I use for this exact purpose. It's a great little camera, and the sound quality is good too. I have recorded my band playing live with it (very loudly!) and the sound quality is far better than I expected it to be. Obviously it's not really intended for this use, but it does a better job than I thought it would do, and recording myself playing and singing with an acoustic guitar - the quality is fine.

As a bonus - it connects fine via USB to Ubuntu and Totem played the .MOD files (the video format it creates) first time I tried - although for uploading to Youtube or whatever you'll have to convert them to some other format (which is easy).
