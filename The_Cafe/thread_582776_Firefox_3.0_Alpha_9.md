### Post by kerry_s on 2007-10-20
grab the tar and unpack it to your home, then make the launcher>
~/firefox/firefox
link your plugins> rm -rf ~/firefox/plugins <> ln -s /usr/lib/mozilla/plugins ~/firefox/plugins

back up your .mozilla folder!

you can separate it using the profile-manager if you want, then make your launcher> ~/firefox/firefox -p (the 1 you created)
