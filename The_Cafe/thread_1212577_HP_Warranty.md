### Post by Kingsley on 2009-07-13
The easiest thing to do is go to the HP website and have an "active chat" with customer support. Tell them your battery is dead, as you checked it HP Battery Check. If they try to give you BS, say that you tested a friend's HP battery and it worked, yet your battery won't charge or work with other HP laptops. They'll then send you a replacement ASAP and require that you send the defective battery back to them within 2 or 3 weeks. HP never has to know that you have Ubuntu installed on your laptop.

I did this last summer to get a free replacement immediately before my warranty ran out.
