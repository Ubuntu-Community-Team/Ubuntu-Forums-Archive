Yet another win for Linux....
-------------------------------------------------------------
Microsoft tactics push India toward Linux

[http://www.linux-watch.com/news/NS6504473525.html](http://www.linux-watch.com/news/NS6504473525.html)



One of India's 28 states plans to distribute 100,000 Linux laptops to students there. It sounds like Tamil Nadu's volume purchasing agent decided to use Linux exclusively after being put off by Microsoft's bundling tactics for academic users.

 ELCOT says it will purchase more than 100,000 laptops this year, selling them to Indian students for about $800, a considerable mark-down compared to retail value, it says.
