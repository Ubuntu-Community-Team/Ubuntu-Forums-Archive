### Post by 23meg on 2009-09-27
You're best off asking or searching for this at the Interaction Design Association mailing list at [ixda.org]("http://ixda.org"). Here's a start:

[http://www.ixda.org/search.php?tag=education](http://www.ixda.org/search.php?tag=education)

My impression, based on my experience with the current generation of UX / IxD people, a lot of whom haven't gone through dedicated programs (because they didn't exist at the time) is that formal education seems less important than work experience, especially given the huge volume of freely available online material and growing number of books and seminars on the field, and the cost of education. This is not to say that you won't benefit from a formal education, of course, but it may be hard to justify.
