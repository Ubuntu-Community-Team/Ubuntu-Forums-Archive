### Post by 4Orbs on 2010-06-07
I know this isn't worthy of a thread on the forums.... but I just had to tell someone. My Logitech wireless keyboard uses two AAA batteries, and I replaced them today for the first time after an entire year of use. Now, I'm not a heavy keyboard user, but I do post on the forums occsionally and do some internet room chatting, editing text files, login several times a day, and gaming (like Halo using the keyboard). I really expected the batteries to die after a few months at best. But a whole year is marvelous. On the other hand, I need to replace the AA mouse batteries every two or three weeks without fail. Thanks for allowing me to voice my delight.
