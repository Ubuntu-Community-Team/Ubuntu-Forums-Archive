Hi folks,

For the last few years, I have maintained my website:
[http://linuxsoftwareblog.com/](http://linuxsoftwareblog.com/)

It has grown in popularity, and now receives approximately 300 hits a day. I've been thinking about the best way forward for the website, in recent times.

I'm doing a PhD in Computer Science at the moment, and I'm always pushed for time. I would *love* to unlock the power of sharing and collaboration, synonymous with Open Source software, and I think it would be great to open up authorship of the site, to other people who have a passion for Linux and open source software.

So, do you want to join me?
I'm looking for posts including, but not limited to:
- Open Source Software Reviews
- Linux tutorials
- Linux tools/scripts
- Educated and provocative posts
- open source game reviews
- Plenty more!!

If you think you might like to join me, pleas go to the site [http://linuxsoftwareblog.com/](http://linuxsoftwareblog.com/) and click "Contact Me" at the top. Tell me about what you might like to post, now and in the future. The site also allows you to post an avatar, to be associated with every Post you publish.

You would have your own user ID and password to contributed, independently of my control. We all know that decentralized systems are always more scalable ;-)

Cheers,
