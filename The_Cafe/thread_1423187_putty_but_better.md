### Post by Mike'sHardLinux on 2010-03-06
As koenn said, you CAN copy and paste in PuTTY.

To copy, you simply highlight the text with your mouse...iow, click and drag. This is quicker than having to highlight and then hit a key combination. To paste, you simply right-click with your mouse.

I am not sure what all features you need, but check out PuTTY Connection Manager. It adds a lot more functionality to PuTTY and is still free, unlike SecureCRT.
