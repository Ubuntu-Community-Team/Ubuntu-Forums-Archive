### Post by samalex on 2011-03-01
For Internet stations I listen to pretty much everything on [Digitally Imported]("http://www.di.fm/") and [SomaFM]("http://somafm.com/").  Plus [Twit Live]("http://live.twit.tv/") is great, though I generally listen to most of the Twit shows via podcasts.

As for radio stations, I generally listen to these:
- [WBAI 99.5 NYC]("http://stream.wbai.org/")
- [WUSB 90.1 Stony Brook, NY]("http://www.wusb.fm/")
- [LBC 97.3 London, Eng]("http://www.lbc.co.uk/listen-to-lbc-3576")
- [KQED 88.5 (NPR) San Francisco]("http://www.kqed.org/radio/listen/")
- [KCSM 91.1 San Mateo, Ca]("http://kcsm.org/")

Also [Al Jazeera English]("http://english.aljazeera.net/") has a good video and audio feed I try to catch from time to time.

I have an account for Pandora, but honestly I don't care to listen to it much since the variety seems shallow.  It's a great service, but I don't like the way they limit the playback of some songs or disallow moving up and down a play list.
