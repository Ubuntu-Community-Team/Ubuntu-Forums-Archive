### Post by mainalisuyog on 2007-06-05
right click on the volume control applet, and choose "open volume control"
Go to edit, choose preferences
Mark on "Mic boost (20db) and close this window
Now you should have a tab called "switches" on the volume control window. go to this tab.
Activate Mic boost (+20db)

Hope this helps
