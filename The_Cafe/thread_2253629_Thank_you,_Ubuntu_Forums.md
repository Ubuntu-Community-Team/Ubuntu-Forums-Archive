### Post by amjjawad on 2014-11-21
Hi,

Ubuntu Forums was the very first place that I have joined in October 31st, 2009 that is related to Ubuntu, GNU/Linux and FLOSS in general. And, it is the very first place that I have learned a lot from. Of course, it is the very first place where I met great and unique people and still working with some of them until this very moment on several projects. It has all started from this place. 

Here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1812877](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1812877)

When I started to realize that I need to move to the next level and the next level was joining Lubuntu Team:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/amjjawad/Lubuntu-Team](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/amjjawad/Lubuntu-Team)

After 2 years with tons of achievements with Lubuntu, I decided yet again to take it to the next level:

StartUbuntu was born:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/StartUbuntu](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/StartUbuntu)

And Joined Ubuntu GNOME:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/amjjawad/UbuntuGNOME-Team](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/amjjawad/UbuntuGNOME-Team)

When I felt that wasn't enough, the next step was:
[https://www.gnome.org/foundation/membership/](https://www.gnome.org/foundation/membership/)

Yet again, I realized I need more and here came:
[http://torios.org/](http://torios.org/)

And, few days ago:
[https://launchpad.net/~ubuntumembers](https://launchpad.net/~ubuntumembers)

Yes, it is all started from here.

This is a message to all new people to this forums, those who are still at the beginning of their journey and to those who don't really know the great importance of this place ... I can assure you that without this place, I would never get such Wiki:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/amjjawad](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/amjjawad)

Because of:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UCADay](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UCADay)

I'd like to say:
THANK YOU SO MUCH, Ubuntu Forums.
Thanks a million to all those great, kind, nice, supportive and amazing people who have helped, guided and all ... the list is really long ... thank you!

I had some bitter experience here but the sweat moments are much more so will never let a bad yesterday ruin a great today ... 

This is an open invite to everyone over here to work together and believe in Ubuntu Philosophy because trust me, the power of **we** will never fail.

** All of us are smarter than anyone of us!**

Thank you for reading this :)
