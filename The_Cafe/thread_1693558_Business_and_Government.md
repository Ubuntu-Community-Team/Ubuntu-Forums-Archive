### Post by HermanAB on 2011-02-23
Well, in the developed world, the military and governments are heavy users of FOSS (USA, Canada, UK, France...), while in the poorer world areas, whole countries have or are switching to FOSS (Brazil, South Africa).  So the influence of FOSS is pervasive.  Just have a look at the Microsoft share price to see the effect.
