### Post by crimesaucer on 2008-12-06
This is to all of the Adblock users:


As someone who used Adblock plus and the filterset G since Firefox 1.5..... I recently un-installed all of the Adblock Add-ons.


I did this because the deviantART site upgraded it's format and the new search mechanism would not load the thumbnail pages if any Adblock extension was installed. 


You couldn't add deviantART to any preferences or settings to make it work with Adblock and the only solution offered from the deviantART sys-admins was to un-install all Adblock(s).


After removing Adblock Plus (and the filter set G), the deviantART site worked perfectly. What I really noticed was how many other sites began working properly. I couldn't believe it. I had thought that these sites were incompatible with Linux, or the Linux media players, or just broke for some reason..... but now sites like MSNBC's Keith Olbermann: [http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/3036677/](http://www.msnbc.msn.com/id/3036677/) and videos from Surfing Magazine: [http://www.surfingthemag.com/surfing-photo-video/surfing-videos/flash/](http://www.surfingthemag.com/surfing-photo-video/surfing-videos/flash/) all worked perfectly once Adblock was removed.


Yeah, adds still suck on some pages, but I think it limits a lot of sites from showing like they're suppose too.
