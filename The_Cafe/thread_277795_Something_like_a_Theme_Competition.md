Many people are saying that ubuntu has such an ugly UI theme.

So I thought, maybe someone can organize a theme competition, so that ubuntu can get a face-lift. The top 3 or 5 finalists will get into  the next ubuntu release as a preinstalled theme and the top 1 would be the next main ubuntu theme.

Theme themes may range from anime to space, even without any relation to ubuntu, but any relation to the African word might be a plus. Wallpapers, fonts, cursors and etc. are optional. I dont know whether to support compiz themes or gnome-only, it's debatable.

To organize something like this though, we need:
* a reward ( something to motivate the themers to go on their edge )
* a site to host the themes (maybe it can happen here )
* a moderator ( someone who has power over the themes that could be installed in the next version of ubuntu )

I got the idea from the GUI Champs Competition.
[http://www.guichamps.com/](http://www.guichamps.com/)
