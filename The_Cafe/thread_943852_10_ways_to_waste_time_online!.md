### Post by crimesaucer on 2008-10-12
With Linux, you can:

- Create a gtk theme of your own, take a screenshot of it, and maybe share the gtk by uploading it to somewhere like deviantART or Gnome-Looks.org.

- Create a metacity/xfwm4/emerald theme of your own, also upload and share it.

- Create an icon pack, or redo someone else's with your own colors.

- Make a new conky script, take a picture, and share the conkyrc with someone in the conky thread.

- Search for the perfect wallpaper, or make your own.

- Optimize your entire system to be as fast as it can be.

- Test new software, maybe report bugs. (I've only done this once with a Firefox nightly build) 

- Waist your time in this forum answering meaningless questions to pass the time.

- Help people in this forum or other forums with answers to their questions about Linux and problems that they might be having with an install or a certain app.
