Setup a gmail account for yourself and set it up to collect your mail from the hotmail servers.

After this every mail you reply to or send from gmail will have the return address/path as your gamil account. Send out a change of email address to all your contacts informing them of you new gmail address. Update all sites profiles where you have registered with your hotmail address to the new gmail address.

After about 3 months or so you should not really be receiving any mail (unless it's spam) in the hotmail account and then you can bin it.
