### Post by fatality_uk on 2008-08-26
Sorry if you were expecting a link, just an idea which sprang from a conversation. :)

Just wondering if the idea of an "OpenSource" car is a silly one?

I mean what if the blueprints for a well design sports car or even small city car were available to download?

Would anyone try and build one?

And before any petrol heads get to excited, no I don't have a web site to promote or plans ready to roll out :D purely theory!

If a "OpenSource" car were ever to be released into the wild, imagine the panic at GM, Ford and Toyota?

Imagine if a design student were to release a design for a car that looked like the *DROP DEAD GORGEOUS* **Lotus Evora**! Engineering students chipped in with radical chassis design and software engineers worked on engine management systems programming.

Because the designs were free for anyone to use, anyone could take them to a reputable and/or approved list of engineering companies in order to fabricate some of the parts! Engines, say a stock straight 6, V8 V10, tuned and that info passed back.

If the handling is poor, imagine having 1,000's of users and builder giving feedback!

Anyways, just a thought I had one night while drinking nice Polish beer :D
