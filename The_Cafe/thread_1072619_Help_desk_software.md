### Post by ww711 on 2009-02-17
Trac - [http://trac.edgewall.org/](http://trac.edgewall.org/)
Mantis bugtracker - [http://www.mantisbt.org/](http://www.mantisbt.org/)
rt (Request Tracker) - [http://bestpractical.com/rt/](http://bestpractical.com/rt/)

Those are the ones that I know of and have used before, all quite straightforward to install and to get up and running.
