### Post by Patrick-Ruff on 2008-05-17
hey all, I've been playing this open source RTS game for a while, it's based on the TA spring engine, the way the TA spring engine works is it gives a basis for mods to work.  complete annihilation is by far the best mod for this game. if you want to play it (it works on linux and windows) go to [http://trac.caspring.org/](http://trac.caspring.org/) and follow instructions on the page, you'll want the CA downloader and then you need to download spring and all that(it's pretty straightforward.)

gameplay is a but harsh on newbies but it only takes a couple hours to get it down. 

so for serious RTS gamers, I highly suggest you check it out, it's based on the game Total Annihilation from the 80's or 90's . . . forgot.

so for people who have already played it, have anything to add?
