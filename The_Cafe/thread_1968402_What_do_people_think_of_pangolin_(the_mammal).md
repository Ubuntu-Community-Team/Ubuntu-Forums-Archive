### Post by Copper Bezel on 2012-04-29
It's one of my favorites for its weirdness - anything I didn't know about as a kid that seems like something you'd tell kids about to get them interested in ecology is a permanent fave. I like that it independently developed armor on a like totally distal branch of the (placental mammal) tree from the armadillo, and it looks funny and bouncy and rounded in all the same ways its name is. It wouldn't be cute if not for the eyes and snout, but damn, those bulgy little eyes would be cute on *any*damnthing.

Really, anything that looks like it was designed as a puppet for an '80s sci-fi pet, it's hard not to appreciate.
