### Post by Malcolm on 2008-11-06
The news really saddened me... I loved his novels, I think he was a great storyteller, and the scientific twist made them unique. "Congo" is a masterpiece.

He was criticized for "State of Fear", but while the subject of climate warming is debatable and whether you agree with him or not depends on your own personal opinion, some of the points he makes there about the way our society is controlled by the media with a constant fear of impending doom are very interesting. He was inviting the readers to inform themselves and form their own opinions rather than following the mass-thought like sheep. It was an important message, in my opinion.
