### Post by samjh on 2007-05-25
FLOSS is not illegal on Vista.

But "Trusted Computing"  could restrict the use of FLOSS in the future.

However, I'd say given the enthusiast market's influence on Microsoft (evidenced by loosened license restrictions on VIsta when the enthusiast community raised up uproar over the license key issue in Vista's original EULA), it's unlikely that Microsoft will prevent FLOSS software running from its operating systems.  What is more concerning is that future services from service provides (eg. banks) could be restricted from being accessed by FLOSS if they adopt "Trusted Computing", or some hardware and software may not function properly with FLOSS.
