### Post by mdmarmer on 2009-10-23
It's not a true rolling release. 

If you try to upgrade from one version to another without doing an install, the probability of success for most users is probably 50% or less.  I think the chance of a good upgrade from one version to another without a new install is much less than in Debian or Ubuntu.
