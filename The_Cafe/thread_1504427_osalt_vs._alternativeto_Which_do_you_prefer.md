### Post by chessnerd on 2010-06-08
Both are great websites for finding alternatives to programs. 

osalt specializes in OSS alternatives while alternativeTo offers alternatives for all license types.

So, which do you prefer? 

[osalt.com](osalt.com) or [alternativeto.net](alternativeto.net)

I prefer alternativeTo because of it's search feature. You type in Microsoft Word and up comes the MS Word page with all the alternatives listed. And it is set up to offer alternatives for a specific OS and even cloud services.
