Since you haven't mentioned whether you are looking for laptops or desktops(macs come in minis too :) )..I am going to go on a whim and assume laptops.

Lenovo Thinkpads - the ones I have heat up too much....but other than that they are pretty durable.
You could also try out the newer Ideapads ..but they are on the heavier side.

HP - biggest bang for the buck. Would recommend taking additional warranty on it. One of my HP 17" started having motherboard issues 15 days after the warranty expired - although they did change my motherboard(or so they say) for free once i complained.

Dell - They sell Ubuntu machines as well.

Sony - I have never been a fan of Sony machine :(
