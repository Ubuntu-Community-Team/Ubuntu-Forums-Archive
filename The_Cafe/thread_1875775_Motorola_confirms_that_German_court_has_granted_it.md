> And now a very important point: even if one wanted to argue that Apple GmbH can sell whatever products it has, how can Apple GmbH receive any new shipments in the future under that injunction?

The injunction doesn't allow Apple Inc. to "deliver" any goods to Germany. That would include shipments to Apple GmbH.

Again, they could try to work around this somehow, too. But it would raise issues.

### Post by Dr. C on 2011-11-06
> **KiwiNZ said:**

One word deterrence, or to borrow a concept from the Cold War Mutual Assured Destruction or MAD. The idea is to acquire a large enough patent portfolio that if a competitor sues you for patent infringement you can retaliate against their products so that deters the litigation in the first place. The strategy only works against legitimate competitors and does not work against pure patent trolls since in the latter case there is nothing to retaliate against.
