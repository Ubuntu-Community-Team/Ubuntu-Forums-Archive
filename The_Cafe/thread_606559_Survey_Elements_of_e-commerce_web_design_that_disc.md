### Post by bmeyer on 2007-11-08
Hello!  I represent a group of students at Valparaiso University, in the Computer Science department.  We're developing a robust model to simulate the success of a given e-commerce web site, based on the presence of negative elements users find discouraging.  We need lots of data regarding what discouraging elements users are the most intolerant of, so we developed a quick survey.

If you wouldn't mind taking it (takes less than a minute), we'd sincerely appreciate it:
[http://student.valpo.edu/bmeyer2/survey.php](http://student.valpo.edu/bmeyer2/survey.php)

Feel free to ask questions, make comments, or discuss on this thread.  I'd be more than happy to discuss the results, as well as provide more details about the model (if anyone would find it beneficial).

Thanks in advance!
