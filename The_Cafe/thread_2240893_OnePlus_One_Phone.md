### Post by cristian19 on 2014-11-10
I have the 64 version since September, I am a heavy business user, with around 5,5GB 4G LTE traffic monthly, while multitasking like crazy and i can say this:
1. Any benchmark ive run puts it above the HTC M8 by 6% to 12% specially in multitasking. 
2. The 3Gb of RAM make it possible to really take full advantage of the 801 CPU ; real life test, surpasses by far the Samsung Alpha with its 805 CPU.
3. Battery, at least 2 to 3 days heavy usage ( mail, virtual computing, internet, radio always on background, Google Navigation and at least 5 hours of video);  Normal usage and with no google navigation, i took it out on a Saturday morning and i still had 12% battery left on Wednesday night.
4. The only thing that i found annoying was the keyboard and gestures but that can be sorted out.
