### Post by pfeiffep on 2014-11-21
I'm ready to move on to the next phase of my life with my wife of 40 years.):P Yesterday we decided on a duplex cottage in a nearby retirement community. 
We'll be selling our lovely home and divesting the 'accumulated but not necessary' possessions. This is both exciting and daunting as we're moving from 3600 to 1700 sq feet.
The community we selected is a full service, certified and [nationally accredited Lifecare facility]("http://carf.org/home/") .
The cottage will be completely renovated prior to our move in (at facility expense).
There will be opportunities galore to help folks with technical problems and I'll introduce Ubuntu when appropriate!
