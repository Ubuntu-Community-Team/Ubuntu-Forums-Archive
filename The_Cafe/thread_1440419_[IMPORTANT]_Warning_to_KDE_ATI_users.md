### Post by sandyd on 2010-03-27
Please do not install ATI Catalyst 10.2 & 10.3.

These versions of ATI catalyst contain new drivers that support Direct 2D. However, these drivers break Kwin and render them unusable.

Instead, stick with ATI Catalyst 10.1, which is avalible here: [http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/10-1/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx](http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/10-1/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx)

The complete details are avalible here: [http://www.phoronix.com/forums/showthread.php?t=22057&page=3](http://www.phoronix.com/forums/showthread.php?t=22057&page=3)
