### Post by mr john on 2013-04-24
Depends how much you're wanting to spend. Samsung Galaxy Tab 2 tablets and Asus Transformers are probably the best, but they are going to cost a bit. At the lower end Arnova/Archos might be worth looking at. If you're in the UK try lookin gon the Carphone Warehouse site. They usually have good deals on tablets. The most important thing, make sure the screen is capacitive. Resistive screens are not good at all on tablets.
