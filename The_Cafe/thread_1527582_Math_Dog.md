### Post by McRat on 2010-07-09
Our dog does math.  

In the morning before work, part of my routine is to give my dog treats.  This has gone on for years.  If you want somebody to love you, do something nice for them every morning.  It brightens their whole day.

Well, we did "the test" again this morning.  Because treats are expensive, I ration them.  I give her 5 treats every morning.

Lately, being the sadist I am, I will throw out 2 or 3 treats.  She will look down, then look back up with a puzzled expression.  Then I will throw down another.  She will count them, and look up again.  She won't start on the treats until she gets her correct allotment.

This morning it was 4.  Could she tell 4 from 5?  Absolutely.  She looked at them then stopped.  She looks back up waiting for the last treat to be thrown down before starting on her feast.
