### Post by kansasnoob on 2012-05-21
For whatever reason some of us may need or desire to change the forum theme. My intention is only to create a place where optional forum themes or tweaks can be shared with other forum users.

This thread is only for sharing our Ubuntu Forum theme variations and tweaks, not for approval or criticism of any specific official or unofficial forum theme.

Constructive suggestions regarding the official forum theme should be posted here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1977943](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1977943)

Actual Forum Theme bugs should be reported as described here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=11936681&postcount=238](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=11936681&postcount=238)

If the bug truly effects accessibility you should add the "a11y" tag to the bug report.

General complaints and whining belong here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1984188](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1984188)

In my particular case I found that using Stylish with Firefox provided a decent solution. Once I'd installed Stylish I simply navigated to Ubuntu Forums and clicked on the icon in the add-on bar and used "find styles":

[ATTACH]218418[/ATTACH]

Then I selected what I wanted:

[ATTACH]218419[/ATTACH]

That works for me :)

Please feel free to share your tweaks and tricks, but let's keep it civil.
