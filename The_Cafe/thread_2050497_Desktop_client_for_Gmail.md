### Post by yeehi on 2012-08-31
Since you are talking about gmail, I recommend taking a look at [smartr]("https://www.xobni.com/download/gmail").

It is not an email client, but a contacts app. It goes through all your gmail history automatically creating contacts for you and intelligently linking them to each other. 

You might not want to do this if you don't like the idea of another organization getting access to all your personal contacts information and hosting it on their cloud.



It runs on iphone, blackberry, android and windows.
