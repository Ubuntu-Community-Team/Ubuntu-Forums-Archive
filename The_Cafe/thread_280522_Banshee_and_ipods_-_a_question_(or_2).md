### Post by moore.bryan on 2006-10-19
[I]you actually have a lot of choices... keep at banshee, try amarok, use gtkpod or yamipod, or install rockbox onto the nano and use a filemanager to transfer files.  

each one has ads/disads... as a personal note, i tried all of the above (banshee, amarok, gtkpod, yamipod, pos-pod... okay, i made the last one up, just get the point i tried them all) and got HORRIFICALLY bad speeds.  for two other guys i was posting with, reinstalling ubuntu while the nano was connected fixed their speed issues, but i wasn't willing to do that... so...

[rockbox]("http://www.rockbox.org") was the answer for me.  i also find it appealing because of it's skinning abilities and the fact it plays any type of music files, not just mp3's or aac's.
