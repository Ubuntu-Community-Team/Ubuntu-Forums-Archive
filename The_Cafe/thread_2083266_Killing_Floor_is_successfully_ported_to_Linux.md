### Post by GrubThemeArtist on 2012-11-12
So yeah, I recommend this game. It has an awesome play style and there's so many levels from the community for this game it's incredible. My favorite being The Hive which is a simulation of the Hive from the first Resident Evil. You start at the mansion and work your way down to the Red Queen and then you pretty much reenact the movie.

If you own it, and want to play the Linux native port just snag the Steam for Linux Beta deb, and even if you're not in the Beta you can now download the game through the steam client (used to be that you wouldn't get the the executable bin until today after an update to steam).

You'll have to run steam from the command line with

steam steam://open/games

so you don't have to deal with the pesky warning about you not being a beta tester.
