### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2012-10-29
Things can just take a while to be polished up and uploaded to YouTube. For the sessions that happen at the right time for you, there is a live video stream for the plenaries at [http://video.ubuntu.com/live/](http://video.ubuntu.com/live/) and there is an audio stream for each room at [http://icecast.ubuntu.com:8000/status.xsl](http://icecast.ubuntu.com:8000/status.xsl). There is usually a good summary in the Etherpad document for each session at [http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-r/](http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-r/). In particular, things that have been *decided on* end up being listed as work items.
