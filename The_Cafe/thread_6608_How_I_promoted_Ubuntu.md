Hi,

Just wanted to say how I promoted Ubuntu in my way. I'm a 17 years old student in College (Quebec, Canada) in a Computer Science program.

Actually, I was very lucky because I received my 15 CDs the day before my oral  :wink:

So, that's not very big, but that's still something, and students where happy to receive something after my presentation.
