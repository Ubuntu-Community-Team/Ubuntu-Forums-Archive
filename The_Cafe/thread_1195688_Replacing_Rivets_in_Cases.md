### Post by .Maleficus. on 2009-06-24
It won't change the integrity at all (or not a noticable amount) if you do it right.  After you drill out the rivets, you'll need to replace them with a larger rivet to compensate for the material that you lost when drilling.  There isn't a whole lot to it other than that.  And if you like modding, check out the website [www.thebestcasescenario.com](www.thebestcasescenario.com).  It's a great forum that I used to frequent a lot but don't have them time for anymore.  There are a lot of good guides and tutorials for every aspect of modding.

Drill out the rivets, and then use some machine screws and nuts of the appropriate size, you may have to drill the rivet holes out a bit to fit the screws, but it should not be a big deal.
