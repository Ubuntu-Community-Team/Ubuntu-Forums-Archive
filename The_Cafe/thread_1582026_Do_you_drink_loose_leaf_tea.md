### Post by snowpine on 2010-09-28
The "tea" in teabags is the dust & crumbs that's left over from handling/processing higher-quality loose-leaf tea. On top of that I do not like the taste of paper. :(

Loose-leaf is far superior in taste (at least the green teas that I drink) plus you can get several steepings out of the same leaves, so it is surprisingly economical. 

The leaves sink to the bottom when they're ready, so there is very little chance you'll accidentally choke on one... but if you're concerned, you can strain/filter/infuse. :)
