### Post by Chilli Bob on 2008-09-12
I did an install last week using the live CD.  Basically it took ages and the result wasn't noticeably faster than Ubuntu on the same PC.  This was the dumb-guy version of the install, accepting defaults in most cases.  If you want the real benefit of Gentoo you really need to read and understand the MASSIVE install guide. (I read some of it and understood less).   Unless your Mum is a Linux guru I would give her Ubuntu or Mint.  If there is a power/memory issue with the laptop, I'd give her Zenwalk.

(I'm not bagging Gentoo, I just don't have the time or energy at the moment to invest in the neccessary learning.)
