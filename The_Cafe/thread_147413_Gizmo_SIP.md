Sorry - I should have read your request more closely:

DialOut - seems to work pretty well - I have never had any failure but I find the sound quality is not always very good gatewayed onto a POT system.
Internal calls - within LANs, across the WAN and Internet do not seem to suffer significant lag but I have found significant volume loss on WAN and Internet connexions (I use standard ADSL).
Once only have I had to abandon a call due to lag but several times because of low volume.
