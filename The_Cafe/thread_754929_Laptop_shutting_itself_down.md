### Post by pedro_orange on 2008-04-14
Is the fan underneath the laptop? 
Try resting the back of the laptop on a book (I use my old LPI  certification book) so that the fan is not obstructed by the desk, and has a nice air flow.

You could also check the fans are clean (If it's old and you've not cleaned it before, do it - you'll be surprised how much "dust" is in there)

### Post by Pathfinder_ on 2008-04-14
Sounds like it's overheating. As mentioned above make sure the fans are clean and there's nothing that is blocking the airflow near the vents.
