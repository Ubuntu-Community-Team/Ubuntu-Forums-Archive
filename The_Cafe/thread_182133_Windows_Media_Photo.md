### Post by sha_man on 2006-05-27
[QUOTE=3rdalbum]
Also, I noticed that Microsoft only compared WMP (photo) to JPEG, not PNG.[/QUOTE]

That's because jpeg and png are used on *different* aspects for pictures. Jpeg is for photos, pictures with a lot of colors/gradients, and it is lossy, whereas png is for drawings, text, etc... where there are a lot of unicolored fills, and it's lossless. PNG is the concurrent of GIF, not JPEG. PNG is not very good for photos, for example, it will have low compression and bad color rendering, wheres jpeg will produce artifacts etc... on drawings, etc...
