### Post by schtufbox on 2012-03-17
This was my first. [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mattel_Aquarius]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mattel_Aquarius") 

Parents got it in 1983 when I was 8.  It was rubbish and had no real games, but I learned some programming on it and it fuelled my interest in all things computer related.
It also left me with an interest in odd underdog computers and I have owned many strange machines over the years due to this, such as the Jupiter Ace, Dragon 32, SAM Coupe  etc.

The first computer I bought for myself, was a ZX Spectrum 48, the old dead flesh keyboard version (the rubber keyboard felt like what we imagined dead flesh would feel like)  I think I bought it in 1985 or 1986 with some money my Gran gave to me.
