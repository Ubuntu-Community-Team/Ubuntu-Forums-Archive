### Post by Brunellus on 2005-11-30
count me as another vote for the iRiver H340.  Mounts as a USB mass storage device with no problems--drag your media onto it in Nautilus.

It also supports OGG:  better sound and smaller filesizes than mp3...and it's a free, open-source codec.  If you're going to run free software, I encourage you to buy from hardware vendors who support free software (including codecs) as well.
