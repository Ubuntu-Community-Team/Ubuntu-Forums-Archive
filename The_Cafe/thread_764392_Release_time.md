### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2008-04-23
The day after tomorrow you can be certain that the web site will tout 8.04 as a stable and finished release.

What is this, Star Wars?! :P


In other words, no, there is not a set time. On the 24th, the final builds will be sent out to download mirrors. Indeed, some mirrors will have it up ahead of time but the hope is that people do not rush to use them in interest of speeding up the process. The actual call of "it's completely out!" happens pretty late in the day, once it is confirmed that download links actually will work for people.
Think of it this way: There is no black box "release date". This is in development up until the last second.
