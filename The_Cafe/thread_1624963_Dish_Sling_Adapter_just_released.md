### Post by samalex on 2010-11-18
Hey Guys --

Dish just released Dish Sling Adapter for the VIP 722 and 722K receivers, so if you're a Dish subscriber you can now stream anything from your DVR or live programming from anywhere.

[http://www.dishnetwork.com/tveverywhere/](http://www.dishnetwork.com/tveverywhere/)

The cool thing here is they just released an Android client, so even though we can't get Hulu or Netflix this is one way to get live and recorded TV on your Android device.

The device is only $99 and there's no monthly fee, which is odd for Dish since they love to nickle and dime you, but I'll probably be checking this out very soon.

Anyway FYI for any Dish customers or anyone who really wants to get video content to their phones.
