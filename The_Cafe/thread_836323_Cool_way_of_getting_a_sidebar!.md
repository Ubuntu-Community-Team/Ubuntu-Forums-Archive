### Post by KenBW2 on 2008-06-21
One of the (few) things I miss from Windows is a sidebar (of the Google variety - I was on XP). I spent ages looking round for sidebars for linux/ubuntu, eventually deciding that Desklets was the best of a bad bunch. Yesterday the obviousness struck me in the face though...
- Right-click Gnome Panel > New Panel
- Right-Click > Properties. Orientation: Right; Size:100px. Add a funky background image and add applets at will. There we go: instant sidebar!:guitar:

Whatcha think?
