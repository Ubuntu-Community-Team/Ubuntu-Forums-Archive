### Post by Dragonbite on 2009-04-16
Short Answer: Yes.

Bad News : I don't remember where it was and I haven't seen the person who told me about it lately.

Actually it was a paid site where you give them your artwork and they would scan the internet to see if anybody is using your image without permission. It scanned by image not by filename which is easily modified.

If you are looking for something on your local system you can use the application imgseek which you can use a picture, text or draw on a canvas to search your library for the closest equivalent.  Kinda fun, but so-so successful for me.


So I guess the short answer should be : Sort-of
