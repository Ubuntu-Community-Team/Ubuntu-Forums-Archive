### Post by gonzomalan on 2009-07-16
> **swoll1980 said:**
> I don't understand what all this cloud stuff is about. It's like having a freaking dumb terminal. Who the hell wants a dumb terminal?

cloud computing isn't as minimal as being at a console/terminal/cli, it's just a very bare OS/environment where it's not expected that you're going to being using a lot of software on your computer, but rather using a lot of internet programs to get your needs met.
example: using google docs instead of OOo or MS office. the advantages are that you risk losing less if your computer crashes, because your work is in the "cloud", and you leave a smaller footprint on your computer because you're downloading/updating less programs. hence, this is why netbooks are prime for cloud computing.
