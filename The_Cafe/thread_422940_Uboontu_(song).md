### Post by Balaam's Miracle on 2007-05-02
YES!!!! I've finally found it!
The song is by **Lucky People Center** and is called **Ubuuntu**, note the 2 "u"'s.

But i can do even better, i've scoured Google and found the following links:
[http://www.tranceproject.com/audio_release/43429](http://www.tranceproject.com/audio_release/43429) (apparrently, you'll need to sign up if you want to listen to anything)

The album it was on is called *Welcome To Lucky People Center* and was released in 1993.
