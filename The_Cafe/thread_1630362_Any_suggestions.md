### Post by Sean Moran on 2010-11-25
> **ki4jgt said:**
> I thought about running a little star light through it. Kind of behind the text
Must agree with that.  Also if you might be familiar with some of the extras in GIMP, you could even add a reflection at the bottom, as if the whole image was floating on water.  It's a bit of an effort the first time, but only a couple of minutes once you get it worked out.  

A little starlight in the top left or right corner would be a great addition, and GIMP does all that fairly neatly without too much hard work.



---o0o---

If you did want a reflection, you could add the starlight first, then cut&paste the lot into a new image, then invert it 180 deg.  Then add the reflection with the ripples/waves, and then scale down the image vertically to around 20% of the original, and then add 20% to the canvas size on the original, and cut&paste the reflection on the water back underneath the original image, and save, save save!
