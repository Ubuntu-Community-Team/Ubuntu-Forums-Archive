### Post by CarpKing on 2007-05-19
Hmm let me see what I can think of off the top of my head...

-New and better Save/Open File dialogs for GTK (w/ thumbnail view)
-GUI for editing GRUB (may already exist)
-Email client along the lines of Thunderbird or Outlook Express, but running on Evolution Data Server (might take a look at [Tinymail]("http://www.tinymail.org"))

I know a lot of these are bigger projects than you want, but maybe something will inspire you.  I assume from your post that you are already looking through people's suggestions for Gutsy; those should be a good source of inspiration as well.
