### Post by shurup on 2009-07-05
This spring, Novell launched Linux video contest named "**What do you do with Linux?**".

A few days ago, contest judges picked 14 finalists of this contest. And now, users can choose the winners.

We have participated in this contest and made the "**Living with Linux**" video. It tells about one day of the geek's life in 1 minute (that's the limitation of contest rules). Yet we're not professionals in this field, we had a lot of fun doing it. And, as we're appeared among finalists and final voting goes on, it seems that we're not alone who thinks this way ;-)

Our video on YouTube is [_here_]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JwEi7-F-KL4&feature=channel_page").

You can watch other videos and vote for them at the [_contest site_]("http://howdoyoulinux.com/#watchvideos").

Have fun!
