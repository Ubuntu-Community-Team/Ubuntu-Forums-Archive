### Post by chaplanger on 2007-02-24
I'd suggest you check out the United Micro page ([http://www.unitedmicro.com/](http://www.unitedmicro.com/)) -- They have a configurator for AMD / Intel based systems.  You can mix and match all types of components.  Best of all when you complete -- the configurator points out incompatibilities.  So you could either order from them or order parts (via newegg) and DIY.

You might also find that their systems (with burn it testing) might be the way you want to go. . .
