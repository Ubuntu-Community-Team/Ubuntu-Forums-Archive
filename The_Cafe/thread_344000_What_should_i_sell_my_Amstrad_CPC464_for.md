### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-01-22
I don't know if I would sell it, if it were me. I sell vintage computer stuff (mostly through ebay) sometimes, and the market is kind of at a low now.

About four years ago, you could get $50 or so for a nice Atari 600XL (just as an example), if it was in good shape and had the original box. Now you can't really unload them except in giant lots.

It comes and it goes. When emulation became a fad in the mid-90s there was a surge in hardware sales too. Maybe it'll pick up again in a year or so.

I'd watch ebay for a month or so and see if there's any rationale for selling it.
