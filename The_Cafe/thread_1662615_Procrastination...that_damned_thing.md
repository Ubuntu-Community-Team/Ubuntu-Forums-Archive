### Post by Irihapeti on 2011-01-08
Here is what I've found helpful for dealing with procrastination.

Start doing something, however small, on your project. If you see your project as a single entity, something that has to be done all at once, it can seem overwhelming and that causes you to put it off.

E.g. if you have a load of papers to file, don't saying "I must do the filing." Instead, undertake to put away three items. You'll feel better for having started, and chances are that you'll end up doing more, anyway.

If you have to write something, just start writing a few lines or paragraphs - even just the heading. It doesn't have to be perfect - you can come back and edit it, anyway. Nothing is more intimidating than a blank page or "untitled" screen. Once that barrier is broken, you might be surprised at how much else gets written...
