Sorry to crush your dreams, but IMO there is no such thing as software (OS or App) that is "completely stable" Murphy's law forbids such a thing (not to mention, a user will inevitably, in all likelihood make a mistake, I have). That said, I suppose dapper doesn't have the problems we have been having with Feisty (the tty problem for instance with new kernel), but I don't think its really that much more stable once installed completely (I have had my computer on uptime for 4 days for instance). So no, in the strict sense, I don't think anything is "completely stable". The updates are usually to patch security flaws and other bugs uncovered in the software, it doesn't strictly have to be something tied to dapper though.
