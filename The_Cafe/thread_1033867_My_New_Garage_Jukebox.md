### Post by Bungo Pony on 2009-01-07
Figured I'd show off my new Linux-powered Garage Jukebox. I built an entirely new one out of a computer I found in the trash, and actually put some money into it.

Specs:

Pentium 199MHz
64M RAM
2 GB Compact Flash Card for Hard Drive
1 DVD-ROM Drive
USB 2.0 Card 
Hacked USB hub internally mounted 
Three 8G USB Flash Drives for music 
OS: Damn Small Linux v3.4.11

It boots in -29 degrees celcius, which makes me very happy! The old one had four DVD-ROM drives, most of which would fail at this temperature.

If you want to see more pics (internal and external) they're on my blog:
