### Post by Dry Lips on 2012-06-19
Not Ubuntu, but the Vivaldi tablet with KDE is worth having a second look at:
[http://makeplaylive.com/](http://makeplaylive.com/)

It runs on *Mer Core* (a continuation of the MeeGo project), and uses KDE's Plasma Active interface. 

If you want to check out what the Plasma Active interface is like, then I suggest that you download the "*Kubuntu Active Technology Preview [..] a preview of Kubuntu using the Plasma Active tablet experience*". Then install it in Virtualbox...

[http://www.kubuntu.org/getkubuntu/download](http://www.kubuntu.org/getkubuntu/download)
