You had to configure single software to manage it. For gimp look after:

Edit > Preference > Input devices > [configure Input devices] > device: [look for your device] > mode: [from "disabled" to "screen"]
Then test it with the inking tool of gimp ("k" key)


I suggest you to practice it... the image in my avatar is made with that lovely "inking pressure" giving a really cool effect: if you save it as PNG you can even have background transparency with fade (GIFs don't have)
