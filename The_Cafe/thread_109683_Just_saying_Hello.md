### Post by Titus A Duxass on 2005-12-29
Hello everyone,
I have been lurking around this forum for a while, thought I'd say hello.
The latest Kubuntu (along with Automatix) is responsible for my dumping of SuSE.  I've been using SuSE since 7.3 and despite all the updates, upto and including 10.0, there are always problems with SuSE.

Kubuntu, however, recognized and configured my HP PSC 1610, gives me no USB hardware problems, burns DVDs, accepts that I change bits of my hardware regularly and even accepts (with ndiswrapper) my wireless card.

So you can say that I'm well chuffed with the distro.

The only bit I do not like is the lack of root to carry out system maintenance.

So there you go I am a happy bunny.

Seasons Greetings
Titus
