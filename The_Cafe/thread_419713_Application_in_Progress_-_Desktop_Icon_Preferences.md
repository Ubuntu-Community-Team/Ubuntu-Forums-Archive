Hi All,

I was hearing many queries regarding enabling/disabling desktop icons such as computer,documents,trash,volumes visible and many would point them to GConf Editor and sure newbies are going to feel tough about it.

So, I am developing a simple appliction which will enable and disable all these icons in your desktop and you can do it without any pain.
