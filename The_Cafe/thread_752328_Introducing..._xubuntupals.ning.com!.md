Hello everyone,

I'd like to introduce the [[COLOR="Blue"]Xubuntupals Community[/COLOR]]("http://xubuntupals.ning.com")

I'm[EMAIL="itsanudae@gmail.com"][COLOR="Blue"] itsanudae[/COLOR][/EMAIL] (aka Leo) and I've constructed a[[COLOR="Blue"] Ning.com[/COLOR]]("http://www.ning.com") community for[ [COLOR="Blue"]Xubuntu[/COLOR]]("http://www.Xubuntu.org") users in general to share information and views, tips and ticks, and talk about their favorite or least favorite stuff about Xubuntu,[ [COLOR="Blue"]Xfce[/COLOR]]("http://www.Xfce.org"), and []GNU/Linux in genera[/COLOR]l]("http://www.whylinuxisbetter.net/"[COLOR="Blue")

I've been putting up some initial content, and it's ready for everyone to come find it.

There's an initial questionnaire about your experience/interest in Xubuntu/Xfce/GNU-Linux and after that you get a homepage in the group where you can express your views and upload your own content...

I'm happy to start it off, as a peripheral to our Ubuntu community focused on the Xubuntu distribution.

It is intended to be a sort of "watercooler" zone or "break room" where everyone who is interested in Xubuntu/Xfce can have a voice.

It is NOT intended to replace Ubuntuforums or the Xubuntu Wiki. Those are official resources and could use EVERYONE:s help. I myself am attempting to contribute to the wiki before Hardy's official Release Date.

Please drop by and check it out at [http://xubuntupals.ning.com](http://xubuntupals.ning.com). Check out the main page and Forums.

My most recent features are the Phatch program which is shaping up nicely for an 0.1.x series application, and a How To on MPD/Xfmpc|Sonata, as well as some theming of Sonata for prettier Icons... 

Best, and hope to hear from you! It's free to opt in to the[[COLOR="Blue"] Xubuntupals Communit[/COLOR]]("http://xubuntupals.ning.com")y
