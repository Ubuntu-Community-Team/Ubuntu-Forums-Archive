### Post by Keith1212 on 2010-03-19
I have a dell latitude d520 that i broke the lcd in. I had been using it anyway to hook to a 22" lcd screen. Well i went ahead and order a new lcd screen to install in the laptop. The problem is i ordered a 15.1" instead of the 14.1". Theres plenty of room in the top part of the laptop that holds the screen for a larger size. I decided since this larger screen was cheaper to go ahead and keep it and try to make it fit.

All the connectors fit and the screen works fine when i turn it on. I'm gonna start by removing the metal beackets that hold the lcd in and then using  a dremel to cut back the plastic tabs that they connect to.

I will post some pics here soon. My main reason for posting this is i couldn't find anything on anyone doing this before. Is there anything i'm not seeing that will cause me problems?
