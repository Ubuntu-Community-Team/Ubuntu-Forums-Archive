### Post by rv_happy on 2014-07-29
I use GMX, but the other way around - I collect emails from GMX into Gmail!

I too have searched the web interface but can't see any obvious way to import contacts full stop. You can create a contact from an email sent to you by clicking on the plus sign next to the sender's email address. So perhaps you could setup pop3/IMAP forwarding for your Gmail to GMX and add the senders to your contacts from your imported emails.
