Your thread title makes it sound like this is a new kid in town. LAC has been a preinstalled Linux vendor for over 7 years, literally since they opened their shop. :) Only part that's new is adding gNewSense to their list of distro options. They have a series of workstations and servers that I believe are built in-house, and they also resell Lenovo's line that once was the IBM PC Division (ThinkPad, ThinkCentre). Distros available include all the major ones (Ubuntu, Debian, Fedora, CentOS, Slackware, Red Hat), plus now they have gNewSense.
