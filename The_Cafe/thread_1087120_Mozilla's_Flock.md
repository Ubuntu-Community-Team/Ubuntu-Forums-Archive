### Post by jeyaganesh on 2009-03-20
> **yabbadabbadont said:**
> It isn't listed at the Mozilla site

It is listed under 'Mozilla-Based Applications' [http://www.mozilla.org/projects/mozilla-based.html](http://www.mozilla.org/projects/mozilla-based.html)

I am using Flock since last year. It is similar to Firefox in everything.

Features that present in flock are;

1. It notify you whenever you get any message or update from social websites like Facebook, Myspace, Twitter, Flickr, Youtube.

Sidebar is dedicated for this purpose. It is called 'people sidebar'. There is another sidebar called 'RSS sidebar'. It gives RSS feed.

2. 'Media Bar' - Loads of photos from Flickr and other photo sharing websites and videos from Youtube will show as like 'stream' beneath the favorite bar of the broswer.

You dont need turn page by page to see 100s of photos. You can just see the stream of photos and click your interesting one.

3. Similarly you can save lot of your email accounts in the favorite bar. You will be notified whenever you get new email.

Except these other things are just like in Firefox. All firefox add-ons, extensions and themes work with Flock.

I am using Flock on Ubuntu, Mac OSX, XP and Vista.
