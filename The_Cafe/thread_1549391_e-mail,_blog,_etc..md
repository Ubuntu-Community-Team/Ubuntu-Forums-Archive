### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-08-09
Although I have already used Joomla and Wordpress on a host, I'm recently using Google's Blogger and I'm very happy with it. It's free, easy to edit and provides unlimited bandwidth.

It has a limit of 10 static pages per blog (not posts, which are unlimited), but you can create an unlimited number of blogs in your account.

More info on Blogger limits at [http://www.google.com/support/blogger/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=42348](http://www.google.com/support/blogger/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=42348)

Blogger also has integration with Picasa Web Albums. You can easily upload or sync your local image folders with Picasa Web using Picasa photo manager, which makes organizing your blog images pretty easy. Picasa has a limit of 1 Gb storage tho.

An Ubuntu mirror is a different story. You will nee lot's of storage space and bandwidth for that.
