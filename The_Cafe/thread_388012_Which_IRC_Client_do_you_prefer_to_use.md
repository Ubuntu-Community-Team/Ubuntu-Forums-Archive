### Post by bonzodog on 2007-03-19
I am a terminal/Console freak, so I use weechat, on the freenode server, where I hang out at the #ubuntuforums channel there, and some other channels as well.

I have been using IRC since 1996 (the same year I also discovered Linux and the net), and AFAIK, it is one of the oldest protocols still in Active use, and almost impossible to police or pin down - take one server down and there are always others on the network still active, often in countries where diplomacy is difficult. For this reason, it is often used for things like warez, and various other nefarious pirating and hacking activities. 
A lot of Botnet attacks originate over the IRC network, which generally speaking uses port 6667 to communicate.
