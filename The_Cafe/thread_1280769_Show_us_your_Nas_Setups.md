### Post by toupeiro on 2009-10-02
The softRAID of freeNAS is fine.  What I like about ZFS is the ability to have snapshot reserves, which is a form of online backup.  VERY nice feature.  last time I used freeNAS, it couldn't do this.  Maybe it can now.
