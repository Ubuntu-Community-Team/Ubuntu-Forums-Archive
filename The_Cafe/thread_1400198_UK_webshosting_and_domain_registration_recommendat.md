### Post by Untitled_No4 on 2010-02-06
For domain registration I like daily.co.uk. At the time when I checked they were one of the cheapest (if not the cheapest) but they have a nice control panel that allows you to change all the settings yourself, so if you want to change your IPS tags you don't need to ask anyone for favours but rather do it yourself. 

For hosting I usually recommend your-name-here.co.uk since they are small and have very good service if you ever need anything, but they are not really cheap and don't offer unlimited bandwidth/space, so they don't fill your requirements. 

I actually use hostgator.com who are in America, but for ~$7 a month I get unlimited bandwidth, space, databases and guest domains, so I can host all my customers' beta websites on under one package and I also use the space for remote backup. Despite not being in the UK the speed of their servers is very good. I also like their control panel which is very extensive and allows you to manage all aspects of your account.
