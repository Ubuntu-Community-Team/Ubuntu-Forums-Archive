### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-01-10
Perhaps this has been mentioned elsewhere, but if you keep a blog about Ubuntu, there is a planet specific to users opening at [Planet Ubuntu Users]("http://www.ubuntuweblogs.org/"). 

There's a submission form for your blog [here]("http://www.ubuntuweblogs.org/submit.html"). Note that Ubuntu members are not included on that planet; members have their blogs collated at [Planet Ubuntu]("http://planet.ubuntu.com"). This one is specific to *users*.

Thanks to [bapoumba]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=171805") for mentioning it ... on her [blog]("http://bapoumba.free.fr/"), of course. ;)
