### Post by maniacmusician on 2007-04-13
I was reading Planet KDE, and came across [Vladimir Kuznetsov's ]("http://ksvladimir.blogspot.com") post about his application for Google Summer of Code being accepted. He is the developer of [Step]("http://edu.kde.org/step/"), which is a physics simulator that he is developing for KDE4. It would allow you to simulate experiments and whatnot. I thought this was really amazing, and could be another great addition to kde-edu. KDE already has a lot of great educational apps, and it looks like it will keep pioneering in that area. 

I thought I'd share, since I know a lot of members here are still kids in high school that'll be taking physics. Maybe even college-level students would be able to use this application. It's exciting stuff, I think. I'm looking forward to it.
