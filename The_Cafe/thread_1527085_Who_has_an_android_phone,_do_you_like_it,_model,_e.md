When I installed it, all you had to do was put the clockwork (update.zip) and the Darky zip files in the root of your internal sd card. The darky rom forums should have articles and videos showing how to do the install. It really is very simple. 

I used tethering with darky's rom very succsfully using the standard Android 2.2 feature for this. With Linux and NetworkManager this just works. Your market place keeps working including paid applications.
