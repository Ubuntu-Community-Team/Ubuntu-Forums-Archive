### Post by timcredible on 2008-05-09
i have sirius in my wife's minivan, xm in my gto, xm at work, sirius at home via dish.  i've traveled from windsor, canada, to middle of il, to florida with both services, and they work great.  the only time i had issues is going through the mountains of west va, xm flaked out some due to the fact the satellites are at an angle for the eastern side of the country.  i have a few issues now and again with sirius in heavy tree cover on both sides.  i find the services to be great because i like alternative rock and there are very few stations that play that.  also, not having to listen to 40 minutes of commercials every hour is great.  you can listen to both online with linux also.
