[SIZE="2"]**_Important Note_**[/SIZE]
If the release mirror is slow, you can pick another mirror from [https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+cdmirrors](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+cdmirrors)

Just click on the [http] button on the mirror you choose, remove 'http://releases.ubuntu.com' from the Ubuntu/Server link posted above, and tack it onto the address in the tab your browser just opened.

For example:
[http://releases.ubuntu.com/12.10/ubuntu-12.10-desktop-amd64.iso.torrent](http://releases.ubuntu.com/12.10/ubuntu-12.10-desktop-amd64.iso.torrent)

can become

[http://linuxfreedom.com/ubuntu-releases/12.10/ubuntu-12.10-desktop-amd64.iso.torrent](http://linuxfreedom.com/ubuntu-releases/12.10/ubuntu-12.10-desktop-amd64.iso.torrent)

You can also use your country code, i.e. [http://ca.releases.ubuntu.com/](http://ca.releases.ubuntu.com/) , [http://us.releases.ubuntu.com/](http://us.releases.ubuntu.com/) to automatically select servers in your country.

**[COLOR="Orange"]This only works for ubuntu/server, and some mirrors may take time to update their servers to host the iso. If you are getting a 404 error, try another mirror[/COLOR]**
[COLOR="Red"]
Please download through torrents if you can to avoid overloading the servers.[/COLOR]
