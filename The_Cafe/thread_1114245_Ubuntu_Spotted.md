### Post by Mehall on 2009-04-02
My mum works for a local college, and today I was there, gonna be helping out in some places, work experience kinda thing.

I was talking to Sarah, who is gonna be my boss if I don;t get a paying job before then ;) and we were chatting about stuff, and they have Ubuntu servers.

Their workstations are all Windows XP. (The only people who would wanna use Linux can't because they do flash dev and stuff.)

They also have a few macs, so they have test beds for all the stuff they make to ensure it works for most everyone. (Any BSD users are hung out to dry ;)

But it was nice to see, even at a small local college level, FOSS was there, and would be used more if it was easy to do for the things they need to do.
