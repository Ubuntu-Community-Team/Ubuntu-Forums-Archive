### Post by damis648 on 2008-08-23
Agreed. Either the PSU or the Mobo. Check for leaking or blown capacitors on the mobo. If you find one, it is bad. A bad capacity will either have stuff leaking out of the top or bottom, or a bulge on the "X" on the top... but this sounds more like a PSU issue.

I suggest mobo because I had two computers with a similar problem: You could turn it on, but nothing special would happen. The fans would spin, you could open the CD drives, but that was it. No output, no input. One of them had a bad mobo, one a bad PSU.
