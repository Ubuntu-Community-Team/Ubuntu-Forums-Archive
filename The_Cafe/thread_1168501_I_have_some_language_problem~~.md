### Post by bryonak on 2009-05-24
I'm not sure if I mean what you mean, but when you click on the little arrow right of the 'Search' entry in the menu bar, you may select 'Show Threads' or 'Show Posts'.

A little trying shows you that 'Show Threads' returns all threads that have postings in them which match your search query (this is the common search).
'Show Posts' shows only the posts that match directly, not the whole threads.
