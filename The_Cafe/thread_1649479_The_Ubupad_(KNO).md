### Post by azangru on 2011-06-10
Remember the ads of a students' pad based on Ubuntu that was about to hit the market? Well, I almost forgot too, but happened to stumble on an old ad and decided to check whether the pad has actually appeared.

It turns out the [Kno site]("http://www.kno.com") doesn't even mention Ubuntu tablets. Apparently, they never materialized at all. And now Kno seems all focused on the iPads.

Not that I liked the idea of those Kno Ubuntu tablets that much in the first place, but still, it's a lesson. Never, ever, believe any linux-related ads unless you see the actual product. Because too frequently the companies go back on their promises.
