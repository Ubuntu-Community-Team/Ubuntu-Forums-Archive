### Post by irabinovitch on 2023-02-19
Hi Ubuntu Friends,

[SCALE]("https://www.socallinuxexpo.org") is back in Southern California on March 9-12, 2023 for our 20th year running the event. We are a community and volunteer run Linux, FOSS and DevOps event held each year in the Los Angeles area. This year we have keynotes by Ken Thompson (creator of Unix and Go), Arun Gupta (Intel and CNCF), and Kitty Yeung (Physicist and Artist).

As usual Ubuntu and Canonical will have a booth and a number of sessions at the show. Come by and meet your fellow Ubuntu community members.

We over a hundreds sessions on everything from open data to free culture, systems engineering, DevOps, databases and more. Whether you're using open source at work or at home on the desktop we have something for you.

Along side SCALE, you can also pop into [DevOpsDay LA]("https://devopsdays.org/events/2023-los-angeles/welcome/") or join the Kubernetes community who are running run [KCD LA]("https://community.cncf.io/events/details/cncf-kcd-los-angeles-presents-kcd-los-angeles-at-scale-20x/").

Ubuntu Forum members can get 50% off registration with the promo code UBNT.

We hope to see you in SoCal.

Cheers,
Ilan
