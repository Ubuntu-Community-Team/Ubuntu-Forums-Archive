Things to consider:
-Size
-Noise
-Heat

So you prob dont want nothing larger then a mATX board. Preferably something smaller as it will be less cumbersome in the long run.
Mini ITX would be prefered but really hard to get a good one.
I however have had really good luck with Shuttle bare bone systems in the past.
[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16856101115](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16856101115)
It comes with PSU, Mobo, Case and onboard Radeon 3000 video which can be turned off and use a PCIX card of your choosing. Also less then 200 USD with Tax and Shipping. Also Comes with a heat pipe to pipe the warm goodness off the CPU. Get a regular dual core 65watt Athlon x2 for about 45-60 USD and then a 1 TB Western Digital Caviar Green drive for about 100 USD and your set. You will have no heat issues or sound issues to worry about.
I think total cost is like 330 USD, Throw in a DVD Burner for an extra 20 Bucks and your set..

EDIT:
Forgot RAM LOL.. Grab 2GB stick for about 25 bucks and your good to go..
