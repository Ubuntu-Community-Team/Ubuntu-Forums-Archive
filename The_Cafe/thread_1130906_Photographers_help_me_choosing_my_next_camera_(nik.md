Nikon kit lenses are ok. Canon ones are generally awful.

You should always spend the majority of your DSLR budget on the lens, not the camera body. DSLR bodies are obsolete in 18 months, lens arent. And they don't depreciate much either, especially good lenses. In 3 years time, a D90 will be next to worthless.
