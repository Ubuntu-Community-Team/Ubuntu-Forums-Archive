### Post by mishranurag on 2006-01-27
I started using UBUNTU about two months back. My printer (DELL 720) didn't work :(, and I gave up and used the printer with other computer. BUT! I got some instructions somewhere in this forum and I followed those instructions ( I don know an iota of it), and woila! it worked perfectly. UBUNTU recognized the printer without restarting. 

I got too excited and tried my camera (FUJI Finepix 3800), and UBUNTU recognized the camera within 30 seconds, mounted it and showed the photos to me. So that was lot more than what I was expecting. However, the camera didn't work in webcam mode. 

Also while copying photos, UBUNTU opened a special folder where I could edit, rotate photos, and preview them as slide show. I don know where I can find that special folder when I am watching photos stored in computer, but right now I am very happy about whatever I got and just wanted to share :)

Anurag
