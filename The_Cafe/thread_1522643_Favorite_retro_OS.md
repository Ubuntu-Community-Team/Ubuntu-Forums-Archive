The first edition of Dec AXP was only available on Alpha processor systems but later ported to the x86 structure.  Later, most of the development team working on the OS was bought out by Microsoft. Not close. AXP was just DEC's codename for the Alpha processor. Windows 2000 was written by MS, it was actually a fairly minor revision to NT4.
