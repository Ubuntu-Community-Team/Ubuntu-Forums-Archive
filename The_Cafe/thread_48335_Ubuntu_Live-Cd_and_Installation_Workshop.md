### Post by Henrik on 2005-07-16
This is very cool!

I guess for install-fests it is common to ask people to bring along their own computers to install on. That is of course a big hassle for them and quite scary (data loss, what if I want Windows back, etc.)

Here it seems you simply ask them to install it on a standard system to get the experience of doing it. And since the systems are all the same, you can do it together as a group and the result is predictable. They leave with more confidence in the whole system. Brilliant!

(has anyone else been doing it this way?)
