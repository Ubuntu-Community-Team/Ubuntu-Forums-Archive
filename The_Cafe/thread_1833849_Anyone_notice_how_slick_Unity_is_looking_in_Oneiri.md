I honestly feel more comfortable on Windows 7 than I do on Oneiric. In no other desktop do I constantly need to fight with things to make sure I don't launch or close something I didn't want to.

I understand why some people can love Unity, but the point is that there are extremely annoying usability issues in which I cannot understand why more people don't find it frustrating. As always, I am going to keep trying it out throughout its iterations, but I really hope Ayatana eventually comes to their senses about their nonsensical decisions. 

I know that I  probably sound like "just another Unity hater" when posting lately, but I think my ideas regarding the UI are actually valid unlike people who say "Unity sucks, I want my panel".
