### Post by RAV TUX on 2007-11-23
**AbiWord vs Kword vs OO.o vs LyX  vs Ted vs Groff**

Having all these options installed I wanted to find out which was the fastest, fastest meaning fastest to load when I select it, also which is the easiest to use.

Unanswered for me is which one is the most compatible with other programs.

First a quick look at which is the fastest and the easiest to start.

1. Abiword is super fast and beside the splash screen open instantly to the page I can begin typing on. Editing the preferences I can quickly turn the splash screen off and it opens instantly. Nice 1 step function.

2. Kword also opens instantly but goes to a selection screen of templates, upon selection it opens the text document instantly. Fast 2 step function.

3. OO.o takes the longest of any word document to load, then when it does finally open it takes you to a selection menu.
This so far is the least liked option and reminds me why  OO.o is the first program I remove upon any new distro install.

4. Lyx, faster to load but then the selection process is again needed, and it may just be me but it looks a bit alien.

5. Ted...for me only available by typing Ted in the terminal, wonderful artwork opens up where you select a new document, very minimalistic.

6. Groff...installed but no quick launch and doesn't open via the terminal, perhaps I am missing something.

2nd step will be which is the easiest to use, I don't have time for this now but will try later.

I would say that upon first opening I like them the best in the following order:

1. Abiword
2. Kword
3. Ted
4. LyX
5. OO.o & Groff tied for the least favorite position.

a more in depth analysis will follow
