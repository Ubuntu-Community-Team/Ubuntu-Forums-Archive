### Post by earthpigg on 2011-10-10
The program seems to be forcing me to consent to a "confidentiality and data retention" contract.

I can only click on the window containing that agreement, with the other windows unresponsive.

If I have "I do not agree" checked, then the "close" button is shaded and unresponsive.

If I have the "I agree to participate in this study" box checked, the "close" button becomes usable and presumably will allow the rest of the software to work. 

In short, there doesn't seem to be an opt-out even if I don't want to contribute in the wiki.

> Freedom 0: The freedom to run the program for any purpose.
[...]
Freedom 3: The freedom to improve the program, and release your improvements (and modified versions in general) to the public, so that the whole community benefits.


Is my being *compelled* to share my improvements if I use the software the same as the *freedom* to do so...? Why does my use of this software *require* that part of my purpose be contributing to the data-set and the study, instead of me being allowed to run it for any purpose?

> AdaptableGIMP Logging Consent Form

[...]

Study Description
You must be 18 years or older to participate in this research study, or you must obtain the consent of your parent or legal guardian. Participation is completely voluntary and can be stopped at any time by removing this software or discontinuing its use.
We may also contact you through the software or the wiki to request further information about you and/or your use of the software. Providing this information would be purely voluntary, as well.

[...]

Adaptable Gimp Client
On the client-side (that is, on your computer), AdaptableGIMP automatically logs usage information and sends this data to a server owned and administered by the University of Waterloo Human Computer Interaction lab. We call this usage data. The usage data collected is described in more detail below.
***Any customizations of the software that you make using our new customization tools are also recorded. We call this customization data. Any customization you make is automatically sent to the wiki and made publicly available to the entire user community.***
We intend to use the logged usage data, user-created customizations, and content created on the wiki to build new innovations and improvements of the software, and to forward our research into new paradigms of user interaction.

[...]

Confidentiality and Data Retention
All logged data will be kept indefinitely by the researchers. These data may be shared with other researchers, under the condition that they follow the same terms and conditions that we have set out above. We may publicly release summary statistics or data that has been anonymized.
You should be aware that we make no guarantees regarding confidentiality, even though we do not intentionally collect personal information, and will make attempts to sufficiently anonymize any publicly-released data.


EDIT:

> Usage Data Collected
[...]
-Statistical summaries of the file names you use when saving a document
[...]
-If you create your own custom scripts, your script names will be recorded (because ALL command names are recorded). Thus, if you name your script "john_smiths_acme_script", that script name will be recorded in the log files whenever the script is used

I'm going to go ahead and hit "I agree" and play around with this for a while, but I don't think I will be replacing GIMP with AdaptableGIMP until some concerns are addressed.
