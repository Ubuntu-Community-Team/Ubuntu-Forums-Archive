### Post by slowth5 on 2008-11-29
No, there will be no loss since FLAC is a lossless codec.  However, if you decide to compress that same file once again with a lossy codec (mp3, ogg vorbis, aac) then you will lose even more information than the compressed file originally contained.  That will give you a twice lossy compressed file, and most likely will not sound pleasant.
