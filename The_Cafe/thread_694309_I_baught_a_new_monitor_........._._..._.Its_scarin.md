I have my Eizo 24" set to 15% brightness (otherwise it would have fused my pupils shut by now).

I went from 800 x 600 to 1920 x 1200 in one step, and it was quite a shock! 

I didn't like it at first, because it felt like too much of the screen was out of my direct field of vision (and moving the pointer to the edges of the screen seemed to require huge long mouse movements). I also found it difficult to get used to the look of the taskbar on a flat screen (because my previous screen was a CRT with a curved front). On the TFT, the taskbar looked like it was curved upwards in the centre.

However, I soon got used to the space on new monitor (and surprisingly quickly too). It only took about 2 or 3 days to acclimatise. Now I'd never go back to a smaller screen. In fact I've just placed an order for the new 30" Eizo (2560 x 1600).
