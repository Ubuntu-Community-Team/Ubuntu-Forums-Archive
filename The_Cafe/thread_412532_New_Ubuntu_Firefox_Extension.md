### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-04-18
Hello there Ubuntu Folk. Wanted to point out to people here a very useful firefox extension for those who frequent the forums. It basically adds a menu next to tools called Ubuntu Forums and lets you instantly navigate from one section to the next these forums without having to load pages in between. I'm still not sure how to get the context menu to work, but menu bar is good enough. It also links instantly to unanswered posts, subscirbed posts and searches. Enjoy :)

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2302]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2302")
