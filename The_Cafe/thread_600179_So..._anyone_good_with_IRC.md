### Post by v8YKxgHe on 2007-11-02
Quite simple, type:

/msg nickserv ghost NormalNick Password

(or just /ghost NormalNick Password)

There is also the channel #freenode which is very helpful, the freenode staff will be more than welcome to help you with anything in there.

### Post by PriceChild on 2007-11-02
You were disconnected somehow however freenode wasn't notified... so your "ghost" remains. It will timeout in a few minutes, but you won't get your ops back. For this the channel needs to be recreated, and so you must be the only one in there.

If you registered your nickname use the above command to get it back.

I'd advise that you register channels though, and use the chanserv access list to help negate these problems next time. I'm normally around under the nick PriceChild or Pricey as well as #freenode if you ever need some help.
