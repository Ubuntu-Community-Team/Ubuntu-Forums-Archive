### Post by DarkOx on 2008-03-24
Take a look at [kmess]("http://www.kmess.org/"), which is kind of like kopete except it's MSN-only. It can handle MSN's offline messages.

An older version of it is in the repo's, though I'd consider installing the autopackage version found on their site, as the latest version is quite improved. (Alternately, just wait for Hardy, which'll have the 1.5 version available via apt).
