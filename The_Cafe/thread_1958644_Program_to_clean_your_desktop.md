### Post by thejambi on 2012-04-14
Just wanted to share a program I'm working on. It is a panel indicator that can help keep your desktop clear by creating a folder named with the day's date and moving everything on your desktop into it. I hope that someone finds it useful! Check it out and download it at [http://zburnham.co.cc/dayfolder/](http://zburnham.co.cc/dayfolder/). 

I'm only just working on it, and it hasn't messed up any of my files or sent them where I haven't wanted them, but if that happens to you, don't blame me!

Hi - I've been working on this in the past month and you can now choose a directory instead of having it always look at the Desktop.
