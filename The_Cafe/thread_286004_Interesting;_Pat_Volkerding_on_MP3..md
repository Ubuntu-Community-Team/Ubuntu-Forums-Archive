### Post by bonzodog on 2006-10-27
Pat volkerding has been interviewed on the llts show, and was asked why he includes the mp3 codecs in Slackware.

He says that people have badly misinterpreted the licensing terms of mp3. It **IS NOT** illegal to *decode* mp3's. But, **it is** illegal to *encode* mp3's, or so he claims, so he can legally include the codecs in Slackware.
The ogg feed is here:
[http://feeds.feedburner.com/TheLinuxLinkTechShowOgg-vorbisFeed](http://feeds.feedburner.com/TheLinuxLinkTechShowOgg-vorbisFeed)

Listen to the section about 20 minutes through. (it's a long interview).
