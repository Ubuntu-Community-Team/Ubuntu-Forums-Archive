### Post by ibjsb4 on 2014-09-15
We also camp.

Got a monster big tent.  One bedroom and a living area.  We have had this tent for years.  I initially lay it out and stake it to the ground, then we both raise it.  This is the fun part, other campers look at us two old folk and think never going to happen.  People have even offered to help.  We have raised this tent so many times that it has become second nature, there is no discussion or talking between us, we got this down to an art.  It takes us 10 minutes to raise it while others have stood there and gazed in amazement.

Of course it takes a while longer to get the inside setup.  The king size air bed, the his and her recliner, battery for the lights and so on :)
