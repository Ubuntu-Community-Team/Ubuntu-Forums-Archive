### Post by FrankQuist on 2007-10-07
No idea if these things have an unified interface. You should try a cable (didn't your phone come with a USB cable? :-/) and otherwise you could try going to a dealer and see if they could help you out with some tools. Maybe they could hook it up to a screen temporarily or at least have a cable to send all your contacts to some file.

I have instructions here for how to do it on my phone, but I don't have any clue if it works on yours. For all you know, steps that result in copying the files on my phone could end up being the steps to delete all contacts on yours (a manual could maybe help you out figure out if the steps are somewhat the same... you have one, do you?)

On my Samsung U600:

Menu button
Up (navigates to phonebook), Enter
7 (navigates to management)
2 (navigates to Copy all to)
2 (navigates to "SIM")
Now a "Copy all to SIM?" prompt comes up. Press whatever is usually the yes button on your Samsung phone (top left button for me, above the phone button and left to the navigation buttons)

Bear in mind that I don't think all contacts are completely copied. It seems it cuts off part of long names on the SIM and only store one number per contact and nothing else. At least on my phone.
