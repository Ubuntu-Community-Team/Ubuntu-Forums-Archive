### Post by starcannon on 2009-09-17
Netbook <= 10.1 inch screen (by most standards). Notebook as far as I have seen is generally equivalent to Laptop, but in some circles is means a small laptop, say between 12 and 14 inch screens. Then there is the Desktop Replacement, large screens, large batteries, weight is not a deciding factor as long as it is just light enough that the average human can lug it around.
