### Post by popch on 2008-02-28
Nice try.

Pro: You won't poke yourself in the eye any more.

Cons: You won't be able to drink all of the coffee in the cup because some will be left in the 'gutter'. If you attempt it, you'll have the ball in your face. You might earn strange looks if you offer your guests 'drinking vessels with balls'. You won't find the stirring balls if you are living with someone who likes to play with them. You guests are bound to pour the coffee on their laps or on your rug while trying to see the cup-with-ball from every side. 

Neutral: you'll still have to wash cup and stirring tool separately, and you'll still have to 'assemble' them when serving the beverage. 

And I don't think I could resist buying it, either, if I saw it in a store.
