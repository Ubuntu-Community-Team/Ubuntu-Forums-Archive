Hi nick34.

There are core concepts for which getting a book might be a good alternative. For instance, how TCP/IP works, the concepts behind DNS, etc.

Having said that, IMHO, everything moves very fast these days, and books can't keep up with the changes. Most up-to-date documentation, tutorials, etc. is found online: in forums, wikis, and alike. I'd also recommend looking for podcasts (webcasts) related to system administration. Here's an example: [TechSNAP]("http://www.jupiterbroadcasting.com/show/techsnap/").

Just my thoughts,
