### Post by richs-lxh on 2010-07-18
Hi everybody,

[Conky  Hardcore!]("http://conky.linux-hardcore.com") is still number 5 on Google when you search for Conky, even though there has been little activity lately, and it would be a shame to see a great site cease to exist.

CH! Was started by Bruce (many of you will know him from LxH, Ubuntu Forums, CB Forums etc), he was awesome with Conky and rapidly made friends with fellow Conky hackers and devs, and provided support on at least 6 forums.

Bruce decided to have a break from Conky and Linux for personal reasons, but I kept the site up on my server just in case he came back.

It's been a while now without news, so I am offering the site up to anybody who wishes to add to it/maintain it.

It has great scripts, screenshots by a number of Conky hackers from many distros and forums, and from the hits I see each week, is a very popular site.

So if anybody is interested, PM me here or at LxH.

rich
