### Post by xeth_delta on 2008-03-14
Gentoo is indeed a great distro worth of considering, if you are interested in compiling a lot. I used for quite some time and enjoyed and learnt a lot from it. It was fast and light, but the disadvantage is that compiling can take an awfully long time for big packages. For instance it took me 8-10 hours to compile KDE and several related packages on a Pentium 4 @2.8GHz. It can be left to work over night, as long as the computer is in a different room, as the harddisk and fan noise can really disturb the sleep.
