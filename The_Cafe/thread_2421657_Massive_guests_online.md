### Post by kpatz on 2019-06-25
You can view who's online here: [https://ubuntuforums.org/online.php](https://ubuntuforums.org/online.php)

Guests are often bots, such as search engine bots.  Sometimes they're spam bots scraping pages looking for ways in to spam the site, or other malicious entities.  They are also people who view the site when not logged in.  Guests can't post, they would have to register an account first, or log in first.

It could also be members whose SSO login expired and they are viewing logged out.
