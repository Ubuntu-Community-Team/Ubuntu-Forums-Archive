### Post by DeadSuperHero on 2010-10-16
Since I can't just pick a specific one, I guess I'd just have to say the Space Quest and Quest for Glory series by Sierra were amazing. Really, they're the games that made me love adventure games so very much.

Edit: Also, Sierra kind of pioneered the whole "tons of cool crap in the game box" concept. In the Quest for Glory boxes alone, they came with fake adventure trade school correspondence guides, a big fat game manual, and occasionally a hint book. It makes me sad since including novelty items as part of a game's packaging is really kind of unheard-of anymore.
