### Post by odiseo77 on 2008-03-26
I think the closest you can get to do yahoo voice chat on linux is [gyachi]("http://gyachi.sourceforge.net/") (Gutsy package is available [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=2582647&postcount=1")). However, due to the fact that yahoo uses a proprietary audio codec for audio conferencing, the voice chat capabilities of gyachi are not good and don't work for everyone. If you can instruct your parents on how to use skype, that would be a better option for voice chat, in my opinion (and maybe for webcam too, if your webcam is supported).

EDIT: I forgot to say, I believe gyachi only supports voice chat on chatrooms, not on private audioconference rooms, but you could try it anyway.
