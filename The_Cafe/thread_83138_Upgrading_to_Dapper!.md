### Post by 23meg on 2005-10-28
It's quite possible, and Steve is having a good time, probably. Since not much is uploaded to Dapper at the moment, it means the difference from Breezy will be minimal. The real unstability would start be observed a few weeks ahead from now, when goals will have been set and big  changes will start to occur.

And the analogy to Leopard just doesn't work; proprietary operating systems are developed in a closed manner and in release time they're quite complete, whereas when an open source OS development branch is opened, new work just starts pouring in from everyone and it's far from being complete, but since it's based on the stable foundation of the former version, it's more or less stable. 

In other words, the real chaos probably hasn't yet begun.
