Survivorman
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Survivorman](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Survivorman)
[img]http://away.com/images/outside/200510/live-through-this.jpg[/img]
My favorite realistic survival show, this show has nothing to do with another show called Survivor. This show is about a guy, who puts himself into realistic situations in the wild and tries to survive. What's most notable is that he has NO film crew, he films everything himself.
