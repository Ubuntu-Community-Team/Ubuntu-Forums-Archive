### Post by m3topaz on 2009-11-04
I've no opinion on the N810 but I have pre-ordered an N900. Why? As it is a phone, Nokia's 5Mp camera is about the best in the business and that counts for a lot. I've got an N95 and 5800 - the former is a good phone, the latter a really good value phone. I think the N900 might be a hybrid of the two!
The online video material of the N900 is pretty enticing, and I think it's a better deal than the N97 (which is overpriced). The only worry I have with the 900 is that it has the same battery as the 5800 and that might not be big enough. I would suggest that if you like music, Firefox, linux and a decent handset the 900 is worth a play - but unlike me try before buy to see if you can get on with it :-)

### Post by ssam on 2009-11-04
i had an nokia 770 and a n800. great devices. good for light weight web browsing, portable music player, offline wikipeida (aarddict), reading a pdf on a bus (laptop feels to bulky).
