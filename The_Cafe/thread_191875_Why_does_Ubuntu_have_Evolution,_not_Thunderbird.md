### Post by asimon on 2006-06-08
Evolution is the choosen default PIM app of the Gnome desktop. It's the decision of the upstream Gnome developers. Ubuntu just follows this decision.

The Gnome desktop wants to have a complete personal information management solution. Thunderbird may be a good email client but it not a full PIM app, for example it's lacking calendar functionality and exchange support.

Another point is that Thunderbird is probably not as good integrated into the Gnome environment than Evolution (I don't use either).
