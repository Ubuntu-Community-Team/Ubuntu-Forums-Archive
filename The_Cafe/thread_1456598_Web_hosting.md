### Post by The Real Dave on 2010-04-18
If you just want a wordpress blog, Wordpress.com is utterly perfect. I've had my blog with them for quite a while, and love it. The hosting is free, with 3Gb of space on the free plan for photos etc. You can upgrade your account as needed. The uptime is great, and you don't need to work about upgrading you Wordpress install.

For other hosting, I've found to [000webhost]("http://www.000webhost.com/") to be the best free solution, that I could find at least. 1.5Gb of storage, 100Gb of bandwidth, and quite a few other features. The member's area is very well put together, and support can be found through the forum. If you want more, you can get premium hosting for $5 a month. Uptime is great, it's never been down for me, and speed is great, never any lag, seeing as the free accounts are hosted on paid-for servers, so you don't get 3000 websites getting served from one machine ;)

I suggest you check them out, if you want to have complete control.

EDIT: They also have a very handy backup facility, both manual and automatic :)
