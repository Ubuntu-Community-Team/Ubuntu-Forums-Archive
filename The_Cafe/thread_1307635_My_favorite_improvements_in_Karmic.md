### Post by ankspo71 on 2009-10-31
Hi,

Since there seems to be alot of people having problems with Karmic, I thought it would be nice to read what people say about their favorite parts of Karmic. In my case (so far), it is some things that I have seen improved since Jaunty.

1 'sudo apt-get autoremove' has improved. It hasn't broken my system yet and finds more packages to remove than before. This might be my most favorite improvement. (I still recommend using this with caution though.)

2 The appearance is much better overall.

3. A bigger choice of wallpapers.

4. Trash applet works better. The icon showed trash in my trash every time so far.

5. I can use ALt + Left Click to drag a window up while compiz is running.

6. Shadows don't overlap windows anymore.

7. Alt + Ctrl + Backspace can easily be enabled.

8. The live cd seems to be more responsive than Jaunty's.

So what do you all especially like about Karmic (so far)?
James.
