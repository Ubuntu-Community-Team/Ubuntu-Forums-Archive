### Post by Xian on 2005-05-12
Click on 'User CP' on the forum toolbar.
Goto 'Edit Options'.

Scroll to the 'Number of Posts to Show Per Page' section.

Adjust as desired from the menu box.

---

### Post by elsewhere on 2005-05-12
User CP (top left corner of the forum menu bar) -> Edit Options

If you scroll down, there's a section for thread viewing options, one of them is the number of posts to show per page.
