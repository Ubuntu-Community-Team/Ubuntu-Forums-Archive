### Post by _simon_ on 2006-10-09
The nvidia drivers that come with Ubuntu do not have 3D acceleration which is needed by games and Compiz (Beryl).

To enable 3D acceleration you have to install the official Nvidia drivers.

It's actually very easy to install the nvidia drivers, I'm not sure why people have a problem with it.
