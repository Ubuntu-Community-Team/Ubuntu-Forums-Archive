### Post by sgosnell on 2009-06-18
The poll is flawed.  It's neither, somewhere in the middle in most cases.  Using a holster or something similar makes sense in some cases, if you have to wear clothes without pockets or work in an environment where the phone can be crushed in a pocket.  I used to use one, when I wore a uniform that had few pockets and most of my time was sitting in a cockpit where the phone would be mashed in my pocket, while causing me pain.  Now I wear a flightsuit with 10 or 12 pockets, so I put them (I have to carry a work phone in addition to my personal phone) in one of those.  Off work, I usually use a pocket, because I haven't bothered to buy another holster that fits the phone I have now.  There are lots of situations that most of you simply haven't encountered.  Don't judge others by your (perhaps limited) experience.  There are places for holsters, even if they don't have one in your world.
