### Post by ryanVickers on 2007-05-25
Um, that's a really old feature that's been in various distros of linux for quite a while now.  But yes, you can also just point at them, you don't have to hold.  I think the idea was that "if images and movies have a thumbnail, why shouldn't a sound file!"  but there are lots of plugins to get to be able to play all files, including mp3.  I've got all of them, so here's a list:

> 
Gstreamer series:
-Gstreamer extra plugins
-Gstreamer ffmpeg video plugin
-Gstreamer plugins for mms, wavpack, quicktim, musepack
-Gstreamer plugins for aac, xvid, mpeg2, faad

Xine series:
-Xine extra plugins


Get all those and you can play anything!
