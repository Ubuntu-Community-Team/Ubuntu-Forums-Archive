### Post by kid1000002000 on 2010-05-15
Interesting idea to be applied in the sciences.  Its a good thought, and you can definately boot from the USB drive via ubuntu's native tools (System>Administration>Startup disk creator).  However, you might want to think twice about this though; since its going to contain scientific research/data, booting from a flash drive is probably not the safest thing for your data- it wears out the flash really quickly.

If you boot the distro, though, why not simplify the desktop and add a nice big search bar in the middle or something somehow?
