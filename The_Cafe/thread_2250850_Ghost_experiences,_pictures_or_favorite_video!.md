I decided I would go outside and walk down my parents backyard to the gate that opens up on the park.
At this time you see I was living with my parents.
My orange Tabby cat tigger always came with me for walks in the park.
So we marched down the back, out the gate and down the path into a large feild wehere we stood and listened to nothing but the quite sound of nature.
After some very peacfull time tigger and I decided to head back up the path through the gate and when we got to the top of my parents backyard I stopped!
There I saw a glowing ball spher of light, it was a light that I can't to this day describe it was bright yet warm. and seemed to move float silently of it's own accord.
