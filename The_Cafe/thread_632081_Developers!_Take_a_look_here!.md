### Post by Darkhack on 2007-12-05
I would recommend you find an project you're interested in and find a real, well known, developer from their mailing list, rather than Mr. Nobody on a forum.  It's always better to get somebody with a proven track record.  Don't expect to get someone like Linus Torvalds (he is extremely busy), but you could find a developer who is happy to help from smaller projects.  Are you a fan of the Epiphany browser?  I'm sure that Christian Persch or Xan Lopez would be able to help.  Both are great programmers and extremely nice guys.
