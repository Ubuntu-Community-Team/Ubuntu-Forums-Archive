### Post by Diluted on 2010-06-04
If the seller does not have a recovery DVD, then have a look and see if there are any recovery partitions on your hard disk.

Otherwise you will have to get it from other sources, such as ISOs or a friend's disk. I believe the product key will work on any Vista DVDs.
