### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2008-04-19
N810 is almost PDA sized, and any extra volume is put entirely to good use :)
Granted, it doesn't have a great setup for PIM stuff yet. GPE works, and Pimlico works (albeit with a few interface problems due to the window manager), but synchronization is anything but sreamlined. You have Multisync + OpenSync for GPE, which is currently rather ugly seeming but shaping up to something good. Conduit may be hitting the Maemo platform soon, so that should remedy the Evolution situation to an extent, although it would still be nice to have a seamless / magical sync tool that works over Bluetooth and activates whenever the paired devices are freshly noticed...

To its benefit, Nokia's tablets behave more like "full-sized" computers. For example, Video Center and the Canola media player take the initiative to *gasp* download episodes from podcasts *themselves*!
