Their style is just simpler to understand -- I was teaching a computer-illiterate family friend how to use Windows XP to test out of an intro computing class, and had my GNOME/Ubuntu laptop out, and he found it significantly easier to get stuff done in GNOME than in Windows XP's "easy to use" interface.
