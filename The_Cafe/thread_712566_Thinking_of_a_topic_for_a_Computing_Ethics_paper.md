### Post by Forrest Gumpp on 2008-03-05
@zachtib

A number of ethical issues have either arisen, or would seem to be latent in, discussion of the topic 'An Ubuntu Forums Thanks Bank', see  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=711065](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=711065)

Essentially the ethical issues I can see relate to the promotion of voluntary financial contribution by members:  such things as the legitimacy of express or implied 'guilt tripping' of the target audience, what may be seen as deliberate non-malicious rip-offs, perceived similarities with on-line gambling, and so forth.

It might provide not only a topic for you, but you may even see yourself contributing through your specialty to this possible Forum activity.

Your input, if it interests you, I am sure would be at least welcome if not positively useful to the shaping of such a proposal.
