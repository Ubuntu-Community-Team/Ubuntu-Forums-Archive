### Post by I-75 on 2008-10-22
> **jimi_hendrix said:**
> so my plan is almost up and its time for a new cell phone...only thing is i dont know what i should get any suggestions?

in the past ive only used verizon but im happy with any major US provider

 I use Verizon, before that I had Sprint. Sprint turned out to be fairly good, the coverage in the sticks was amazingly good (sprint roams on other networks with no issues) and the the free roaming plan was good. We went to Verizon, because their roaming coverage a bit better and works great in the sticks and had some better features than Sprint. Both Verizon and Sprint had no billing issues. Verizon and Sprint still offer tri mode phones when I last looked. 

 We had Cingular (now at&t), and for months on end they screwed up the bill, got charged for roaming in what should have been a extended included area. Their service barely worked in our house despite the cell tower being only 2 miles away. We dumped them as soon as our contract was up. 

I believe this service now uses GSM only for the newer phones and in my past experience GSM wasn't all that reliable as Verizon's and Sprint's CDMA. 

I know GSM is popular everywhere else like in Europe. I still think analog is superior than digital as it can reach out farther...but the building of new cell towers out even in the boonies has made digital cell phone service about on par now.

Ask Verizon what kind of deal they can offer to you, it might be worth staying with them another two years.
