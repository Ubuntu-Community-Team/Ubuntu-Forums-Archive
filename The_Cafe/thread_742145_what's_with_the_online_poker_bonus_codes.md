I have been playing T.H. for about 7 years, poker for about 20 and seriously online for about 3. I would suggest if you are new to poker, use the virtual money accounts at first. Most of the sites have this feature. Once you happy and understand the game, might take a year or two, then place a limit on the amount of **REAL CASH** you want to risk in a year. I say a year as it's easy for people to put down £50 a month and think nothing of it, and then top up. After a year, that's a summer holiday spent on Poker. I play a lot online and in real tournaments and I see people lose a LOT of cash. 

Ok specifics:
freeroll. Most tournaments require you to pay to enter. A freeroll usually means that you can enter a tournament without paying for it. Its funded by the host and usually small money $2500 first prize etc. Most sites have a daily freeroll or at least once a week. Many sites have a qualifier to get into a freeroll on ever take the number of virtual points you have to determine if you can enter.

As a rule find a guide for tight aggressive play. This will benefit you in the long run.

The site I suggest you start with is PokerRoom. There's plenty of "fish" and a few sharks :D but on the whole the level ofplay is usually quite good.

As for PokerTH. I usually play on that a couple of times a week. If you want to PM me we can arrange a table and maybe go through some basics!

Most of all enjoy!!! It's a great past time!!!

The Devilfish :D
