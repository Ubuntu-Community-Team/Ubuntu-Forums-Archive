### Post by Mazza558 on 2008-11-18
Hi all,

I happen to have an extra "Gift" copy of ***[Half-life 2]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Half_life_2")*** lying dormant and unused in my Steam account (friends already have the game), so I thought I'd put together a competition for the community to participate in. Whoever wins will get this copy of Half-life 2 **absolutely free**, through email (I'll send it as a gift to the winner's email address).

**Why am I doing this?**

Because the Ubuntu Forums are fantastic, with users constantly showing selflessness by participating and helping around the Beginners talk and all the other support sections of the community. I thought this would be a pretty nice prize to give away.

**Why reward people with a Windows game?**

EDIT: Since Steam appears to be coming out on Linux, this question is irrelevant. 

Because I'm confident that there are plenty of people who still have a dedicated gaming PC, or still dual-boot for various reasons. Of course, Half-life 2 will run through Wine, but slowly. So, the situation is this: I have an extra copy of Half-life 2, and organizing a fun competition in recognition of the quality of the forums is a lot more fun than giving it to a friend.

Even if you don't have Windows/don't want the game yourself, you could win this for a friend who does, and pass it on to them :)

**The Competition**

**Already been won!**
