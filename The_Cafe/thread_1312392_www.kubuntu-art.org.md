### Post by praveesh on 2009-11-03
There's a site named [www.kubuntu-art.org](www.kubuntu-art.org)    . I just discovered it recently . It's hosted by opendesktop.org. You can upload your artworks and download artworks from there . There's a section for brainstorms . There you can share your ideas to impove kubuntu, but I couldn't find not even a single entry though . You can easily become the first one to contribute an idea  .  If you wish to contribute artworks to kubuntu , you have to first upload them to [www.kde-look.org](www.kde-look.org) (yeah , not to the [www.kubuntu-art.org)](www.kubuntu-art.org))   and  then inform the kubuntu developers
