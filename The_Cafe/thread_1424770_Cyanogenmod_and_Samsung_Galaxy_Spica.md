### Post by aysiu on 2010-03-10
My guess is probably not.

From what I have seen in Xda-Developers.com and Cyanogenmod.com, each rooted rom has to be tailored to a very specific model of phone. In fact, they're even careful to differentiate between the HTC Magic 32A and HTC Magic 32B.

Cyanogen himself has separate roms for the MyTouch/G1 and for the Nexus One. So my guess is he'd have to create a separate rom for the Samsung Galaxy Spica, too.

That said, Cyanogen is not the only rom modder around. So someone else may create a rooted rom for the Spica.
