### Post by Vert on 2006-11-07
The Cell Processor uses a PPC architecture.  I'm curious as to whether the Ubuntu PPC version will currently run on a Playstation 3 (albeit not at it's full potential).  If not, I'd like to know if the Ubuntu Dev Team or an independent programmer has any plans to develop an Ubuntu specifically for this platform.  
  I believe that Ubuntu on the Playstation 3 will have multiple advantages.  First and foremost it would provide a free Linux OS for that platform and raise Linux awareness.  Second, this would greatly benefit the Ubuntu Dev Team, because the Cell Processor (presumably) will become the major CPU choice within the next 2-3 years and the programming experience gained from previous work on the Playstation 3 would prove to be invaluable during this era.  The Playstation 3 will be the most powerful computer on the low end market and when combined with the power and ease of Ubuntu could be the most widely used PC Platform ever conceived.
