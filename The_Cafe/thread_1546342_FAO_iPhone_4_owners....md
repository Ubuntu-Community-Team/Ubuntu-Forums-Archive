### Post by KdotJ on 2010-08-05
Hey people, I recently got myself an iPhone 4 (on Monday infact). I have to say, it is by far rye best piece of hand held technology I've even owned. However, since yesterday I have noticed a small annoyance with it.
When I shake the phone left to right (e.g to "Shake to reload" apps) I can hear a small rattle noise coming from the top end of the device. It's not the power/sleep button rattling, nor the volume buttons. I have googled and researched this on forums and I have found out that many people have experienced the same issue. Apparently Apple say this is completely normal and may be the camera's auto focus device / the sensor for shaking / the giro for the compass. The phone works perfectly fine and there is no difference in performance and the camera still works fine. I'm not too bothered by this rattle, but I just wondered if anyone else here has the same issue?

Thanks
