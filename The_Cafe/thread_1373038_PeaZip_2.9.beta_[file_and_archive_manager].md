PeaZip 2.9.beta [file and archive manager]

PeaZip 2.9.beta was released today, the new version improves the file manager and updates the backend binaries to FreeArc 0.60 and UPX 3.04.
[http://peazip.sourceforge.net/index-next.html](http://peazip.sourceforge.net/index-next.html)

Alongside for testing, the beta version is meant to give time to people willing to contribute a translation (that I thanks in advance!) to provide language files to be included in 2.9 packages.

The guide for translation is available in this package: [https://sourceforge.net/projects/peazip/files/PeaZip%20translations/1/peazip-2.9.beta.about_translations.zip/download](https://sourceforge.net/projects/peazip/files/PeaZip%20translations/1/peazip-2.9.beta.about_translations.zip/download)
Newer translations are made available, as soon as they are provided, at: [https://sourceforge.net/projects/peazip/files/](https://sourceforge.net/projects/peazip/files/) in "PeaZip translations" group.
