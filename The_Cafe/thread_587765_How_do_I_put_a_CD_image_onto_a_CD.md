### Post by popch on 2007-10-23
(1) Buy a CD printer. This is a device which prints on CDs instead of paper

-or-

(2) Buy a lightscribe CD drive and matching media. I don't know if there a lightscribe drivers for linux yet.

-or-

(3) Use a OHP pen (water resistant) and manually write on the CD.

-or-

(4) put your CD into a paper sleeve or a jewel case (etc) on which you can write anything.

DO NOT stick anything on the CD such as a self-adhesive label. It might damage the layer which holds your data. It might also make for uneven distrubution of the wight which can kill your drive if it turns the CD at high speeds.
