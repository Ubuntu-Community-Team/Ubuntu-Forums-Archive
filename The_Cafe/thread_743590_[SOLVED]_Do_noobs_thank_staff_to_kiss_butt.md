### Post by justin whitaker on 2008-04-03
Tactical Gamer used to use this forum software for their forums, and they had the thanks plugin enabled as well...when I saw that UF went in this direction, I thought oh-oh. 

Cults of personality form quickly. If you are allowing people to thank others or provide some sort of acknowledgment for their help, then the system can be gamed. What ended up happening at Tactical Gamers was  that the most prolific posters ended up getting thanked so much that it skewed any reporting...and they ended up dropping the thanks system.

It needs to be policed rigorously...something which I doubt admins really want to do.
