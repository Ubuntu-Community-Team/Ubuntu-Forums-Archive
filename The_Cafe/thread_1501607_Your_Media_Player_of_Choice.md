### Post by Spice Weasel on 2011-01-01
> **frenchn00b said:**
> I clicked other: cuz simply mine is my favourite:)
[http://box-look.org/content/show.php/?content=127893](http://box-look.org/content/show.php/?content=127893)
 
It is powered in background by mplayer and hard coding.

```

*FvwmWorkbar: (5x2, Colorset 18, Frame 0, Icon /usr/share/icons/Bluecurve/24x24/actions/player_play.png, Action(Mouse 1) `Exec "audacious" exec audacious -p`)
*FvwmWorkbar: (5x2, Colorset 18, Frame 0, Icon /usr/share/icons/Bluecurve/24x24/actions/player_rew.png, Action(Mouse 1) `Exec "audacious" exec audacious -r`)
*FvwmWorkbar: (5x2, Colorset 18, Frame 0, Icon /usr/share/icons/Bluecurve/24x24/actions/player-fwd.png, Action(Mouse 1) `Exec "audacious" exec audacious -f`)
*FvwmWorkbar: (5x2, Colorset 18, Frame 0, Icon /usr/share/icons/Bluecurve/24x24/actions/player_pause.png, Action(Mouse 1) `Exec "audacious" exec audacious -u`)

```

It's not hard coding, it's 1 minute scripting. I knocked that up in about 30 seconds, just had to read the Audacious manpages beforehand.
