### Post by moffatt666 on 2007-05-11
OK, here's what you need to do.  Type the following into the (centre)console:

su
<password>
cd /usr/bin/jag
./configurecar
make
make xjs
modifytyres -- avon
modifyride -- stiffer
modifyrideheight -- 100mm
modifyengine -- 3.2L
regno  --  <existing car's reg no.>
notifyla
buildvehicle

There you have it, a brand new(ish) Jag.
