### Post by stmiller on 2007-11-09
Make a OS X Time Machine-style backup of your documents. There are a couple of similar apps for Linux like this one: [http://code.google.com/p/flyback/](http://code.google.com/p/flyback/)

And check out firefly:
[http://www.fireflymediaserver.org/](http://www.fireflymediaserver.org/)

To share music and videos on a network. The shared music shows up as a shared computer in iTunes for iTunes users, also.
