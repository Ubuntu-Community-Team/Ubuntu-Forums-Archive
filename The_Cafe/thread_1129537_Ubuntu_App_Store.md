### Post by Josef_A on 2009-04-19
What might work well as opposed to aping Apple is an application-independent music store.

A nice prominent and simple application which accesses a music store and where purchased music is automatically registered in whatever media players the box has installed, e.g. amarok or rhythmbox or what have you.

The thought of a music store isn't innovative, but decoupling it from the playback application while preserving the ease and transparency of playing the just-purchased music does showcase what is different about free software at a level more people are likely to be able to understand.
