### Post by gn2 on 2008-05-06
1: Buy *any* laptop.

2: Remove the hard drive and store securely.

3: Fit replacement blank hard drive.

Bingo, brand new laptop without OS.

Alternatively:

1: Buy *any* laptop

2: Use [**Boot & Nuke**]("http://dban.sourceforge.net/") to wipe the hard drive clean.

Bingo, brand new laptop without OS.
