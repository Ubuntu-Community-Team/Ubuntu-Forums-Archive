### Post by p_quarles on 2009-02-03
Wbar looks nice, but it's just a launcher. I think the term "dock" usually refers to something like OS X Dock or Avant Window Navigator, which are task managers as well as launchers. 

The only launcher/task manager I'm aware of that doesn't require compositing is kooldock. At this point it's kind of an abandoned project, but there is a version with a couple of improvements over the one in the repos at kde-apps.org.
