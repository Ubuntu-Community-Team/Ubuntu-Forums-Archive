### Post by aysiu on 2007-05-31
> **miggols99 said:**
> Ok. Anyone know some UK music stores? I'm not liking using MSN music (:() and iTunes. My sisters are saying, "Why won't this music copy to my mp3 player?" and "Why won't this play?". I tell them it's DRM, but we don't any *decent* UK alternatives :(
Unfortunately, most major labels (with the recent exception of EMI) distribute online music with DRM. There's no legal way around that.

If you're willing to go independent (instead of using major labels), you can download legally from Magnatune, eMusic, and Jamendo.

If you must buy from the major labels, you're best off ordering CDs (or buying them in person from your local record shop).
