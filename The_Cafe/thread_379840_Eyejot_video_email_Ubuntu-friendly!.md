Hope it's OK to post this sort of thing in the Cafe ... I'm not affiliated with Eyejot.com, but I wanted to put in a good word for them here, because they were very responsive when I had a problem with their interface. It was really nice to find a company that didn't brush me off with, "Sorry, we don't support Linux." They were very interested in helping and getting this working for Linux users -- and it does now!

You need proprietary stuff like the Flash 9 plugin to use Eyejot, but other than that, no download is necessary. They have a nice, clean, simple interface that I like a lot. Try them out if you want to send/receive video email!
