### Post by nicolasdentremont on 2024-11-03
Hello and welcome. GIMP is a pretty good image editor and painting program. Inkscape is another that does vector graphics. I use both of them and they work well for me.

Some other resources that I've used to learn Linux that I like to share are:

[https://explainshell.com/#](https://explainshell.com/#)
Where you can get information on commands and what they do. It's a good reference for when you're following online instructions and tutorials.

and

[http://www.linuxcommand.org/tlcl.php](http://www.linuxcommand.org/tlcl.php)
There are a couple of free PDF books there that really helped me with working with the terminal and command line tools.
