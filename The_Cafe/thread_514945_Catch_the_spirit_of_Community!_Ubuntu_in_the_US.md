### Post by Vorian on 2007-08-01
[SIZE=3]*update [DIGG this and keep the momentum going]("http://digg.com/linux_unix/Catch_the_spirit_of_Community_Ubuntu_in_the_US")![/SIZE]


 The Ubuntu Community showed, once again, it is the most dynamic group of individuals in the world. Yesterday was the greatest day the US Teams project has experienced since the group was founded. This is a result of the perfect storm: [Jono’s blog post]("http://www.jonobacon.org/?p=1007"), [Digg]("http://digg.com/linux_unix/Ubuntu_In_The_United_States"), and most importantly, a bunch of hardworking Ubuntu advocates. Nine new teams were formed yesterday, and I want to wish the best of luck to the brave soles who took up the role of leader.


 I am amazed mostly at the Spirit of Community that I felt yesterday. It is gives me a great feeling to see all of the teams in the US coming together to help support and build each other up. That is what this project set out to do back in January, and it is wonderful to see the mission of the US Teams project being realized.


 We still need 10 teams to get to the intermediate goal of having a LoCo team in every state. Once this is accomplished, we can get to the hard work of US wide approval! Take a look at the [Team List on the Ubuntu Wiki]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/USTeams/TeamList"), there are some team needs listed on the bottom.
