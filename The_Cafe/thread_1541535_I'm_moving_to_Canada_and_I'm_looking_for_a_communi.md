### Post by gordintoronto on 2010-08-01
There is more Ubuntu activity in Waterloo than in Toronto.

I doubt if you will find a Plug for sale in a store in Canada.

Most Canadian ISPs will not let you run a web site from your home. Some of them let you run a low-volume web site on their computers. I have a low-cost ISP, and a web site on godaddy.
