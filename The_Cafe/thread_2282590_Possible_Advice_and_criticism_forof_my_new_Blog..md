Simple really, you're displaying too many items on the front page (and in archives) I usually limit the display to only 5 or 7 items per "page", and require a user to click "page 2" to see the next 5 or 7 items. Having too much content overwhelms people. I believe it's best to limit the amount of content displayed, and improve navigation by adding tags and hyperlinks. People won't navigate by manually browsing through your entire archive! That defeats the purpose of electronic data.
