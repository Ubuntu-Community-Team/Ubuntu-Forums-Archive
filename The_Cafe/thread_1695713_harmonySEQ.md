### Post by rafalcieslak on 2011-02-26
Hello guys!

I am developing a **loop-based** MIDI software **sequencer** named harmonySEQ (v 0.14 recently released).
You can find more information about it at [http://harmonyseq.wordpress.com]("http://harmonyseq.wordpress.com") (or a video demonstration at [http://harmonyseq.wordpress.com/video/]("http://harmonyseq.wordpress.com/video"))

It differs from other popular sequencers. But _I am not going to list all the features_, you can find them on the website.
In fact, I just think some of you may be interested in it. It's intended to help music composers and performers, and it offers unique features when it comes to creating loop-based music (and in fact, other too).

If you are interested in harmonySEQ, I encourage you to visit the website, download it, and give it a go. If you know someone who may be interested, tell them. harmonySEQ is relatively new software, so it's not famous at all, but, frankly speaking, I think it deserves even a bit of popularity, because it is really useful for some musicians. (Well, and as I'm posting this just to spread hamonySEQ, I would be extremely thankful if you helped me spreading it! **Could you please share your tips on how to promote software, and how to let more people know about it?**)

And of course Ubuntu packages are available to download (as well as a PPA):)

Moreover, I'd love to get feedback from you. Any comments, ideas, opinions are very welcome:) If you downloaded it, or just had a look at the website, please share your opinion! :)

I hope some of you will find it useful!:) And any tips are welcome!
