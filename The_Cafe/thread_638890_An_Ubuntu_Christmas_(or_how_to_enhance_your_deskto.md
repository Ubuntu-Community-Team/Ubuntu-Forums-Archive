Bump.

I went about this myself and found [a guide for installing the Snow plug-in]("http://forum.compiz-fusion.org/showthread.php?t=5303") for desktop-effects.

After I completed the installation I downloaded some [free snowflake images]("http://graphicssoft.about.com/od/freedownloads/l/blfreepng05.htm").

Note for all who try, I had to login again for the plug-in to start working (I would get white squares falling down instead of the snowflakes I had selected).

Complete the effect with a wintery background image. There are plenty [here]("http://www.vuni.net/misanthropia_1.html").

Hope someone finds this useful! Happy [insert your preferred celebration here] everybody!
