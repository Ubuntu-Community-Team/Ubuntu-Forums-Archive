> 
Also, usually how long does Ebay usually take to deliver the item(s) ?  It can take upto 9 days ? This wait period is normal in online shopping ?


it's not e-bay that is delivering but the seller. so depends on the sellers terms and condition on shipping. haven0t done it on ebay but on some other sites like dealextreeme where you have free-shipping available and it could take a long time. in my case it was over a month. but express shipping (to be payed for) would take much less. a couple of days at the most.

9 days is nothing. depending on destination but for example from my experience (I sell & ship spare parts all over the world) to get it to the other side of the world with normal airfreight it would take us 6-8 days to airport, then customs and delivery to address usually all in all it takes about 14 days. unless it's some really exotic location (e.g. a very remote island...).

DHL (as well as other courier shipments) mostly does it in a couple of days at a much larger price.

So it all depends where the seller is and where the buyer is as well as how well the seller is stocked (own stock or they get stock from outside)
