mikogo is what we use. [https://www.mikogo.com](https://www.mikogo.com)

BBB (Big Blue Button) [http://bigbluebutton.org/](http://bigbluebutton.org/) is a 100% F/LOSS solution - but it lacked a pretty front-end last time I looked at it.
