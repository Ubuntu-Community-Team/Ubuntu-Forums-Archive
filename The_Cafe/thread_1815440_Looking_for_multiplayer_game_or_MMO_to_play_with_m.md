### Post by BeRoot ReBoot on 2011-07-31
[EVE online]("www.eveonline.com") has a native mac client and pretty much works perfectly in Wine, only has one server/shard (timezone independant) and strongly relies on player cooperation to get anything done - for example, the NPC market is more or less nonexistant, almost every item you use in game is made and sold by other players. The game client itself is free/gratis and so are all the expansions, but there is a monthly subscription for server access.
