### Post by handy on 2010-08-18
I haven't bothered reading the thread, so what I'm about to say has probably been said at least once:

If you use GQView, as your image viewer, you can set any image that you view via it, as your wallpaper.

Pretty easy solution for those of you that want wallpapers.

If for some reason the wallpapers won't stick through reboots then one solution is to use feh (which is integrated into the GQView menu) to maintain your wallpaper choice.

I expect that if you are using Gnome (at least) that you can get around having to use feh to maintain your wallpaper anyway.
