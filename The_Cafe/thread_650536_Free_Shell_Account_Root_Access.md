### Post by dasunst3r on 2007-12-26
I agree with what everybody else is saying.  Sadly, there are way too many evil people out there for any sane host to give root access.  However, there are hosts that give shell access, like DreamHost (that's what I've been using for about two years now).  Indeed, SSH/SFTP is a very legitimate alternate to FTP, and you will probably see more hosts offering SSH/SFTP access as they deprecate the latter.
