### Post by knight2000 on 2012-03-21
I use my own, because I don't want disqus being able to track which pages my users are visiting . I am concerned about the number of tracking scripts being quietly embedded into websites. For example, yahoo normally have around 8 third party tracking scripts in the code of each news article. Other mainstream news sites I've visited have up to 15 tracking scripts per page. I'm sure disqus does the comments job they claim to do, but possibly their script will also collect data about your users. For more information about tracking techniques Google Ghostery. That's what I use to track the trackers.

If you don't mind that the disqus is pretty easy to set up and works well for most people.
