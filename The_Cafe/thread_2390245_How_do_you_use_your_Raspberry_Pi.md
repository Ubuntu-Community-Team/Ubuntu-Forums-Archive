### Post by QIII on 2018-04-27
I have one running a large format, wall mounted electronic picture frame using mxplayer to display images of nature or art in slideshows with panning and fading between images.  I ssh to it for maintenance or to load new slide shows.  I use Ansible to do software updates on it along with all the other machines on my network.

It's mounted in a glossy, partly gilded, dark wooden frame I built in my woodworking shop.  The power cord is hidden in the wall to avoid a messy appearance.  Is very nice.
