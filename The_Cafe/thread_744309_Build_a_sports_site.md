### Post by tamoneya on 2008-04-05
I agree that there are not many sports fans here and i dont exactly nba.com very often but we would be glad to help you out. 

One thing that you should make sure to consider first is RSS feeds.  These seem to be what you are looking for.  They are just the content and articles of the site and what you can do is you can take multiple RSS feeds from different sites and put them into an RSS feed reader and you will be able to get all your news through just one site.  Take a look at google reader if you are looking to get started.  Then add this feed (NBA.com) [http://www.nba.com/rss/nba_rss.xml](http://www.nba.com/rss/nba_rss.xml).
