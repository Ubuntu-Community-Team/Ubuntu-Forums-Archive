Hi I have been studying screen printing and the T-shirt idustry for about a year now and there is are huge differences in the quality fit and feel of shirt. Higher end shirts tend to be softer to the touch and fit better and pricewise there is not a huge difference compared to the increase in quality.
 
 This is a discussion about t-shirts relevent to tux and linux as there are many offerings of linux t-shirts and huge differences in the quality and pricing of different t-shirt supliers for screen printing and DTG. direct to garment printing.
