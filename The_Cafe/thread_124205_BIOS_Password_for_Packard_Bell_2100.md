### Post by TechSonic on 2006-01-31
Remove the clock battery and leave it sit over night.  Put it back in when you wake up and all the CMOS will be cleared 'Bios Reset'.  Either that you punch random stuff up before the password diolog comes up and eventually you might hit the bypass combo.  At least the random thing works on Gateway comps.
