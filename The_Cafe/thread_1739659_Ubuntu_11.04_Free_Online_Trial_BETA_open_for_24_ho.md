Hi all 

Dear Forum members,

Click, share, enjoy the online beta: 
[http://try-ubuntu-beta.ec42.net/](http://try-ubuntu-beta.ec42.net/)

We are expecting to launch an Ubuntu free trial for any users from next Tuesday, 3rd of May. We would really appreciate your help with testing it. We are making it available for 24 hours from midday UTC on the 26th of April. We need  as many people from as many parts of the world to trial it. We want 800 concurrent users at any time. So feel free to share with friends, especially Mac and Windows users.

Feedback, and more feedback required in the following categories
  - speed to load
  - speed to perform
  - the USSO login sequence - how was it for you?
  - your location
  - was the language of the image you accessed correct for your browser?
  - was the time allowed enough for you to get a feel for Ubuntu
  - would you use this as an introductory tool for Windows users
  - do you love it? do you hate it? Why?
  - how can we improve it?

There is a feedback tab in the website itself. Please use that but we will also follow the forum thread if you prefer to discuss it there.

Again, click, share and enjoy;
