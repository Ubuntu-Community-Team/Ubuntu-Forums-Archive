### Post by Frak on 2010-03-24
Celerons are perfectly OK for many tasks. What you want to avoid are constant/intensive apps. As the celeron has such a tiny amount of L1/L2 cache, many clocks go to waste as it takes time for more information to be sent from the RAM and stored in the processor cache.

Other than that, Celerons are just fine.
