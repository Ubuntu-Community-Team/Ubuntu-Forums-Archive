### Post by solitaire on 2008-02-04
the 2 main reasons Schools still use M$ are:

1) The state / region / school district have signed an agreement with M$ to get it cheap (usually bundled with a load of "free" comps or Tech equipment for schools)

2) They think the Support for anything other than M$ Operating Systems and software will cost them far more than the offer from M$.

Basic facts are that School's are stretched for funding and they usually take the "Safe" way out when it comes to spending money. Safe these day's is usually Microsoft as everyone can use it quickly and easily.
