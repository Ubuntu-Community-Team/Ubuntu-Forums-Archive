### Post by MaxIBoy on 2009-06-24
Well, there are USB turntables you could buy (I have one.)

However, it's still a turntable, you need to manually put the record on, flip it when side A is done, and then move onto the next record.

Maybe a more expensive model might have an automatic record changer? If so, you could load the records five at a time. You could also get a bank of three or four record players side-by-side. The sound will NOT all get mixed together, so you don't have to worry about that (each device creates its own channel.)
