### Post by basketcase on 2006-04-28
Nailed it!! 

Everything that didn't work in 5.10, works w/o issue(s) in Dapper.  I know I'm a little premature having only been on this for about 10 minutes or so...Here's where I'm at:  

Inspiron 6000
1.6ghz Pentium M
1GB RAM
60GB 7200 RPM HDD
IPW2200 B/G Wifi (using)
Broadcom 440 Ethernet

What didn't work in 5.10:
Front Audio Control Panel -- 
Wifi -- Maybe I messed this up in 5.10, but could never get it to work -- Works out of the box with Dapper
Touch Pad -- On the Dell Notebook(s) they have scroll bars on the right and bottom.  SUSE 10.0 was the only distro that it worked of out of the box...not any more.  Works out of the box with Dapper

What Doesn't work: 
The LED for the Wifi When it is on.  
Stop/Pause, Next and Previous Track and Stop on the Front Audio Control Panel

I have to say the above was what made me go back to Windows, mind you I still need to dual boot and will probably do a clean install/dual boot when the final version comes up, but all my issues (well the LED) have been taken care of.  

Back in the day I liked to 'geek out' and dig into a config file, now I just want it to work. AND IT DOES!! 

I'll continue to play with it, report any bugs and see what I can do to help the cause.  

Thanks to everyone who made this what I believe to be the BEST Distro yet!!
