### Post by kutjara on 2008-04-05
You have to understand the intent of such messages. While they appear to be designed to inform you about some decision you're about to make, in the interests of making your choices easier, they're really about covering Microsoft's *** from a legal standpoint. You can tell the messages that are really meant to help from the ones that were written by the legal department, because the former tend to mean something while the latter don't.

Just read those choices as the software equivalent of "objects in the rear view mirror..." or "Wearing Superman costume doesn't enable flight." The companies don't really care if you smash your car into an 18-wheeler or throw yourself off of a rooftop while yelling "up, up and awaaaaaay!" as long as they don't get blamed for it.
