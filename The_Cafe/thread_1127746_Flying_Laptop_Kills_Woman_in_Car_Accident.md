### Post by Sef on 2009-04-16
The [Globe and Mail]("http://www.theglobeandmail.com/servlet/story/RTGAM.20090416.wlaptopdeath0416/BNStory/National/home") (Canada) has reported on a woman being killed, in an otherwise survivable accident,  by her flying laptop.  It was unsecured on the back seat and hit the back of her head.  Death was listed as blunt force trauma.  The police advised people to secure one's belongings in the car (besides the people who also can be projectiles in an accident.)
