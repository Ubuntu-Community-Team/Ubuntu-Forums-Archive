### Post by HungSquirrel on 2005-03-17
> (except for sound in gaming--issue has yet to be resolved)
When I can't hear sound in a game, esd is usually to blame.  *killall esd* in console will end any esd processes out there that are fighting with SDL for control of the sound device.  This might not be your problem, but I thought I'd throw it out there.
