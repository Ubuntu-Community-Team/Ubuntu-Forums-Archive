### Post by egorgry on 2007-05-27
I can only comment the first part of your question based on my dell 600m which came installed with XP. I ran XP for two days to make sure everything was working as expected after I was confident with the hardware I installed hoary the hedgehog... I think? Regardless. The laptop is now almost 3 and it's been nothing but outstanding for me and I'm a terrible laptop owner; I beat the crap out of the poor thing. My screen is great, very bright, clear, 1400x1050 resolution on a 14.1 in screen. I've never had any issues with any component not working with any flavor of ubuntu. wifi, sound, video, dvd/cd writing, all have been great. No hardware issues at all. Battery isn't quite two hours but it's been consistently about 1hr 40min at **best** since I got it and it's aged well. Overall no complaints with the 600m. I'd get another Dell laptop if mine died. 

The system is fast and stable my specs were top notch at the time and I still no complaints about how it runs.
Dell 600m
2.0 ghz pentium M
2 GB ddr 400 ram
80 GB 5400 rpm HDD
64mb ati card
sxga+ screen @1400x1050
soundblaster audio (speakers suck) **but I haven't heard good laptop speakers ever. sound is great with senheiser cans.*
