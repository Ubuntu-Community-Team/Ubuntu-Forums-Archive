Ubuntu's indicator stuff is actually an implementation of KDE's Status Notifier specification (while working with the KDE developers to make said specification work well for us). Here's part of KDE4's implementation:
[http://api.kde.org/4.x-api/kdelibs-apidocs/kdeui/html/classKStatusNotifierItem.html](http://api.kde.org/4.x-api/kdelibs-apidocs/kdeui/html/classKStatusNotifierItem.html)

And given that the stable version of Dropbox on Linux now uses libindicate, I think we're on the right track for everyone to have their &#8220;aha!&#8221; moment and switch over ;)
