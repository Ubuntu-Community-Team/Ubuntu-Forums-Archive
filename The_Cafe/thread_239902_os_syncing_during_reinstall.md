### Post by Sam on 2006-08-20
Between two Linux installations ? Yes, keep youe home folder in a separate partition from the system, then during the installation, in the partitionner, use your home partition as /home and set the partitionner to NOT format it. Then when the install finishes, you'll have the exact settings as before.
