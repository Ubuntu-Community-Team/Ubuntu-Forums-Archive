Has anybody experience with this tool ? :

> The application also offers a few simple yet genuinely useful editing tools. The clever part is that Shotwell provides a non-destructive way to tweak your photos. Instead of modifying the original photos, Shotwell stores all edits in a database and applies them on-the-fly as necessary. This means that you can easily undo all edits using the Photos -> Revert to Original command. In addition to the common editing features such as crop, rotate, and remove red-eye, Shotwell offers the Enhance tool which lets you improve your photos with a single click. Despite its simplicity, this feature works surprisingly well.
