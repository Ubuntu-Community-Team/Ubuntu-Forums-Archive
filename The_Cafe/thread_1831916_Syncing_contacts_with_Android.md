Greetings.

I was in a similar situation a year ago, and I ended up using the Google account option; you can pry the calendar/mail/contacts sync from my cold dead hands.

All of your Google 'contacts' should sync across.  For example, you have:

My Contacts
Most Contacted
Other Contacts

*My Contacts* are the contacts that I have manually added; my Wife, friends, neighbour's cat, etc cetera.  *Other Contacts* are the contacts gleaned from your mail, and *Most Contacted* are, well, that much is obvious.

Only the *My Contacts* contacts should sync - that's how I have mine working.  Thunderbird (I used to use Evolution) also syncs with my account at work and home, so I can make changes from any location.
