Ok, I found the song after some googling.

It's called **The End** or **Creasy Dies** for the movie. Vocals were by [Lisa Gerrard]("http://www.lisagerrard.com/") and the score was by [Harry Gregson-Williams]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harry_Gregson-Williams")
