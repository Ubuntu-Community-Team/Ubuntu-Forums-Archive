### Post by niftynev48 on 2008-10-11
If any members have been having trouble changing desktop icons,as I have,there is one quite suitable way,I eventually found..If you have too many generic icons,and just want very attractive alternatives,there is a way to change all icons at once,to very suitable ones,even though you do not get to pick them yourself..It selects appropriate ones,ie Google (globe of world)Telstra(phone) etc..If anyone is happy with this method,please let me know (niftynev48@aapt.net.au)

Method..Right click desktop..Click Properties..Click Appearance tab..Click 'Advanced'..In the drop down menu click 'Icons'..Choose size from menu (eg,40)..Click OK and OK.
