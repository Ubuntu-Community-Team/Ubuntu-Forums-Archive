### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-07-19
Feisty is the latest stable version released for Ubuntu.

Dapper is the long term support version released 2 versions ago designed for those running businesses or who simply don't want to have to upgrade every release cycle (Ubuntu has a bi-yearly release cycle). 

Gutsy is the next version of Ubuntu, it isn't really ready for prime time.

Your using the right version, stick to Feisty until Gutsy comes out.
