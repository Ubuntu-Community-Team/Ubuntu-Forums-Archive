### Post by Phases on 2008-12-01
Made the full switch on two home computers and my work computer (virtualized XP for what little I still need it for) to Ubuntu 8.04 a few months back. 

Dual 19" screens at work and 22"s on my main at home - with synergy to my server box..

It's been a few months and I'm still excited about it and still love getting on my computers. I have fun tinkering with the stuff. 

I put web and mail services on my server at home for the site in my sig, have it doing tar backups nightly with cron jobs and a places file.. can vpn to work with one three letter command (alias' rock mmkay?), got a linux script for my UPS's (so they email and text me when power goes out and shutdown the comps when batterys get low), got IRC bots goin'...etc etc. 

It's **too** fun. 

And, a big thanks to THIS community for all the answers I've found here (and tutorial/walk throughs!) and help I've received from members. There's only been one or two issues that I (and looks like many others like me) haven't been able to solve. This place is awesome.
