### Post by doorknob60 on 2013-05-21
It depends on the game. If it's an FPS like TF2 or even Portal, keyboard and mouse work fine. It really just depends on the game, but it's always good to have both available and use what fits best.

### Post by 3Miro on 2013-05-22
Different devices, different interfaces.

You can never play Civilization or Starcraft without mouse/keyboard.

Dragons Age Origins came with different interfaces for consoles and PC and each was optimized for the corresponding controller.

Skyrim comes with identical interface on both platforms, however, I prefer to just install SkyUI mod and then get a PC interface for my mouse/keyboard.

It all depends on the game and the interface.
