### Post by Datum47 on 2011-07-04
I don't know how many UF members are familiar with netlabels, but they are increasingly becoming my primary source of music. The fact that the music is free (as in beer, and often as in freedom!) appeals to me because this means what is released and promoted will generally not be motivated by commercial interests - as in the mainstream music world.

If you are like me in that you enjoy netlabel music,

1. What are your favorite netlabels?
2. What are your favorite netlabel releases?
3. Do you run your own netlabel, or leave it to others?

I will be interested to hear your responses.

Incidentally, if you are wanting to get into netlabel music, [http://www.archive.org/details/netlabels](http://www.archive.org/details/netlabels) would be an excellent place to start!
