### Post by Dale61 on 2008-12-08
You'll need to learn the language.  Although Kiwis speak English, there are definite characteristics you need to know.

For example, 'fish and chips' in pronounced 'fush und chups', 'apples' is 'epples', and the country itself is said as 'Noo Zillan'.  In a lot of cases, it's just a subtle re-arranging of vowels.

Also, you'll have to learn to finish a spoken sentence with 'bro'.  'Choice' is another word that is prevalent on the other side of the ditch.

Just jokes people, just jokes.

As you may have gathered, Aussies and Kiwi's have the same friendly banter as does the US and Canada.
