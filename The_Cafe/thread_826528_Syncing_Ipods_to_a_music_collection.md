### Post by zmjjmz on 2008-06-12
The obstacle: Apple doesn't release the code necessary to sync it.
Anyways, it really matters what iPod she has.
Most music players for Linux currently support iPod Syncing OTB.
Exaile, Banshee, and Rhythmbox are good examples.
gtkpod doesn't play the music, but it will sync it.
