### Post by wildman4god on 2009-01-29
> **lukaszr said:**
> i can't say i miss OSx but one thing i'd like to know, what are those side panel things, with like the cpu ram weather and all. Can i get that on ubuntu?

That is called conky, you can find it in synaptic or sudo apt-get install conky

becarefull though it is configured via editing a text file called .conkyrc, although if you look in the ubuntu community cafe an other forums you can usually find people .conkyrc file which you just download and throw into your home folder and restart conky. also you may find .conkyrc files at gnome-look.org, just tool around there a little andd you'll find them or just search conky at gnome-look or .conkyrc on google. best of luck.
