### Post by misconfiguration on 2007-04-30
I used to mod xboxs, I actually still have one, I don't play console games very often, but I have a 200gb hdd in it I have games on it (that I own), but the main purpose for my xbox is of course, my front-end media server. I have the xbox hooked up to the Living room tele, the xbox is connected to my smb share, so I just stream all of the media I want to my tele, since I already have a huge fileserver full of my DVD's etc. I don't have to go through switching my dvd's etc. I just use my xbox controller, I love it for this fact and it's cheaper than a Mythbox + I don't have digi cable :(.

### Post by AusIV4 on 2007-04-30
I bought an XBox recently for the sake of putting Linux on it and using it as a MythTV frontend. The guy I bought it from warned me that the DVD drive was flakey, but I figured it wasn't going to matter if I could just get it softmodded and booting Linux. Soon after I got it softmodded, the DVD drive died completely, and I discovered that XBoxes won't boot if they can't find the DVD drive. So then I bought a mod-chip that should make it ignore the dead DVD drive and boot anyway. I haven't had a chance to put the mod chip in yet, but I may try and do that this afternoon.
