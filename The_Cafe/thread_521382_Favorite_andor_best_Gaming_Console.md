### Post by RChickenMan on 2007-08-09
I chose the Super Nintendo Entertainment System.  The reason for this is a combination of enjoyability of game play, durability of hardware, and graphics and sound.  The SNES has the same style of game play as the NES.  This is undoubtedly superior to the 3D style employed by such exciting contemporary video games as "Shoot the Kids at School 3D" and "Angry Angry Puppy Killer." Also, like I said, improved graphics and sound can't hurt when they don't interfere with the style of game play.
