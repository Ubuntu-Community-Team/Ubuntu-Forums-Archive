### Post by BWF89 on 2006-12-29
It's a graphic novel about a pride (pack) of four lions that escape from the Baghdad Zoo after bombing from the 2003 invasion destroys part of the perimeter. They walk through Baghdad and meet other animals along the way, including a turtle old enough to have lived through World War I.

I saw it on Attack of the Show. They have a section of the show called "Fresh Ink" where they talk about comic books and said it was the best that came out this year. I don't usually read comic books but it was especially good.
