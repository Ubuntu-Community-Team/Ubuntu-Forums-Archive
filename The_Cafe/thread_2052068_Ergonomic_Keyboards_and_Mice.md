### Post by kurt18947 on 2012-09-03
> **matthekc said:**
> Does anyone here use a trackball the figertip or thumb kind or any other type of pointing device for RSI purposes?

I use and prefer a trackball.  I have no RSI and would like to keep it that way.  I have a Kensington orbit trackball with scroll ring.  Love the scroll ring.  I wasn't fond of the plastic add-on piece they include,  I felt it was a waste of plastic.  i cut a piece of wood so that it provided a flat surface for my wrist and the heel of my hand then tapered it so the front of the trackball was about 3/8" or about 1 cm. lower than the back.  I find that very comfortable, my wrist and hand are nearly flat when using the trackball.
