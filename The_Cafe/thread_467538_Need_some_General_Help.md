### Post by Foxmike on 2007-06-07
Hi and welcome!  You might want to make a look to: [https://help.ubuntu.com/](https://help.ubuntu.com/) , [http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/FrontPage](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/FrontPage) , and [http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Main_Page](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Main_Page) .  Those are great sites for documentation!  Therefore for Desktop effects, I would suggest you to install it first and install proper drivers for your video card.  A nice way to do this is with the "Proprietary Drivers Managers" that can be found under the "System" -> "Administration" menu.

Good luck!:)
