### Post by tamoneya on 2008-07-08
Definately do not use the wireless.  The throughput may be high enough depending on the game you are playing but the ping times are miserable for gaming.  Instead just grab a switch and plug it into your router.  You may want to just do it right the first time and get a decent 16 port switch so that if you have more people over next time you dont have to keep buying up (provided you have the money for it).  Either way switches are rather cheap especially if you get your friends to chip in so you guys can split it.  

[http://www.newegg.com/Store/SubCategory.aspx?SubCategory=30&name=Switches](http://www.newegg.com/Store/SubCategory.aspx?SubCategory=30&name=Switches)
