### Post by darkmatter on 2005-10-31

First of all, glad to see you. Just couldn't stay away, huh?;) 

With the refresh rate problem you have, if you know the vertical/horizontal ranges of the monitor - you can then edit  xorg.conf to reflect those, or you can run:

```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```

and pick 'advanced' when asked for the frequency ranges...
