### Post by jespdj on 2008-10-25
Did you read the FAQ on [www.linuxmark.org?](www.linuxmark.org?) It says:
> I am making T-shirts, mugs, etc. for sale, and I want to include the "Linux" on them. Do I need a sublicense?

No, this is generally considered fair use. However, your goods still should attribute ownership of the Linux mark to Linus Torvalds. For more information on attribution, follow the &#8220;Trademark Attribution&#8221; link at the right.
So, just for the T-shirt you don't need it.
