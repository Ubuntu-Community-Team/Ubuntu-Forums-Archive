### Post by Corfy on 2008-11-02
Our trick-or-treat was Halloween night. My wife and I don't have kids, so we just gave out candy, although we both dressed up (I was a Ghostbuster, complete with my own custom proton pack... my wife dressed up in a Victorian-era outfit).

Our first trick-or-treat at our current house, we had 46. The next year, we had 67. Last year, we had 80. I don't know if it was the good weather, the fact that it was a Friday night, or a combination of the two, but this year we had 168 trick-or-treaters come to our door.

We didn't have anyone come to our door Thursday night, and we were at an Ubuntu release party Saturday, so I don't know if anyone came, or wanted to come, to our house then.
