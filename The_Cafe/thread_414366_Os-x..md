FreeBSD is NOT Linux. FreeBSD is UNIX allthough you are not allowed to call it that. See the history, [http://www.levenez.com/unix/](http://www.levenez.com/unix/)

The base of OSX is Darwin. Darwin is based on the Mach3 micro-kernel and FreeBSD5 services.
