### Post by gn2 on 2008-11-12
First thing to do is check on [SamKnows]("http://www.samknows.com/broadband/search.php") to see what's available on the exchange, also find out if you can get Virgin Cable.

If you can't get any of the unbundled options (e.g. Be) or Virgin Cable, then probably the best ADSL Max ISP would be an EntaNet re-seller, like [UKFSN]("http://www.ukfsn.org/home/internet/adsl/maxallowance.html").
