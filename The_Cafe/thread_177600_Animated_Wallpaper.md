### Post by mcduck on 2006-05-17
If you have XGL running, it's very easy to get a screensaver or a movie as your wallpaper with xwinwrap. And if you choose a screensaver that doesn't take too much CPU it's not going to slow your machine down or anything.. Most OpenGL-based ones work pretty nice with right settings.

I often have GLSlideshow screensaver running as wallpaper, slowly browsing through all my wallpapers and photos.. It's not distracting at all (at least compared to toasters flying above your desktop, which is also possible with xwinwrap..).

There is more info about this in the dapper forum.
