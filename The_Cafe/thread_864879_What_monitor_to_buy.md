### Post by silkstone on 2008-07-20
I have two 22" widescreen monitors - an LG L226WTQ and a Samsung SM2232BW. The LG is far better - the Samsung has a noticeable blue cast at default settings, and needed profiling to give good results.

The big problem with Samsung is that they put at least three different panels into the same model, and you can't easily tell which you're getting until you switch it on and look at a hidden service menu. The panels actually made by Samsung are very good, and these are the ones sent to reviewers - no surprise there. The other types are not as good - more backlight bleed and less accurate colours.

There's plenty of info about this on the web, if you google 'Samsung panel lottery' or something like that.
