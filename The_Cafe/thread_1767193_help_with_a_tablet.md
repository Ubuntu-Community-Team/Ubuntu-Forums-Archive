### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-05-25
If you want to draw, most tablets are out. Very few to none of them have pressure sensitivity, and most are capacitive, meaning you can only use a finger or other object with similar conductivity, so you have to buy a specific stylus to use with it.

If you want to draw, you're looking at a dedicated drawing tablet, any of Wacom's range should work with the latest version of Linux.
