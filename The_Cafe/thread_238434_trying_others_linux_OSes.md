### Post by jISh on 2006-08-17
VMWare is your best bet! Grab the VMware server from [www.vmware.com](www.vmware.com). It's totally free, and lets you install operating systems to a file that is treated as if it were a hard disk.

No mess to clean up, just delete the folder.

Of course, some of the distros you come across may also have LiveCDs.
