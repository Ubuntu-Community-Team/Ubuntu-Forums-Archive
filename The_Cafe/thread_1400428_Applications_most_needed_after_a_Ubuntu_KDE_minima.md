### Post by Nerd King on 2010-02-07
plasma-widget-networkmanagement I think is what you'll need (I'm not a great KDE expert so feel free to tell me I'm wrong).
For package management Synaptic is still king but comes with Gnome dependencies. For me that's no biggie but you may disagree. Failing that there's always apt-get and the terminal which I use most of the time anyway :)

For file extraction grab rar and unrar otherwise you'll not be able to do those, it'll automatically integrate into whatever you use. I use file-roller in gnome, but not sure what you use in kde. Sorry to be not much help.
