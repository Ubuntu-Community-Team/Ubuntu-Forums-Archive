### Post by mips on 2007-07-08
Impatient are'nt we ?

Anyway, the cpu fan usually plugs into the Motherboard and not the PSU. Whatever you feed into the mb is picked up by the cpu fan from a small connector located close to the cpu socket.

Edit:

If you have a look at the manual, [http://global.msi.com.tw/index.php?func=downloaddetail&type=manual&maincat_no=1&prod_no=1140](http://global.msi.com.tw/index.php?func=downloaddetail&type=manual&maincat_no=1&prod_no=1140)  on page 1-4 it shows you where the cpu fan plugs in. Page 2-10 deals with the power connectors (24&8pin) and page 2-16 with the cpu fan.
