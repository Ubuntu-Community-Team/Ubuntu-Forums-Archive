What happens to me a lot:
 - I see a post I can help with
 - I start typing a response, only forget one or two parts of the answer
 - I open a new tab to double-check my answer
 - I get distracted reading someone's blog that I found googling for information on the answer
 - Phone rings/ coffee cup is empty / kids start fighting / meteor strikes the backyard and I get pulled away.
