### Post by alphaniner on 2009-12-21
To anyone who has a couple hours to spare, I highly recommend the movie [Ink]("http://www.hulu.com/ink") (Hulu link).  This was one of the most amazing movies I've ever seen.  The premise of the movie is that the forces of good and evil interact with people through dreams (or nightmares in the latter case) in order to influence their behaviour in waking life.  It sounds trite, I know, but the execution is pretty incredible.  Some folks see the movie as a knock on capitalism, and there's clear nature vs. technology dualism at work in the imagery of the good and evil groups, respectively.  But I think most people will be able to find something to appreciate.  Meh, I'm no critic, just watch it.
