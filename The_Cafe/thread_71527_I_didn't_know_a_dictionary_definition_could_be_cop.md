### Post by skoal on 2005-10-03
I think Lexico is copyrighting *their* use of said content, not the actual content.  This is merely a standard EULA for any interested parties wishing to *refer* to this site as their source, modify it midstream (or whatever), and (potentially) claim it's derivation was from dictionary.com to begin with...

In other words, they have toolbars and such you can download and maybe even an indirect link you can use to feed into your own dictionary database application.  I dunno.  Probably overkill on dictionary.com's part...
