### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-02-17
You need a mobile broadband dongle, not just a SIM card (the SIM card goes into a mobile broadband dongle or mobile phone).

Some dongles work. Check what models are being offered by your local ISPs/telcos and do a Google search for the model number plus the word "ubuntu": for instance "E160G Ubuntu".
