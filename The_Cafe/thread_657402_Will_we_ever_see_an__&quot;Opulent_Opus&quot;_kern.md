### Post by coljohnhannibalsmith on 2008-01-03
Well, it occurred to me that since as Linux users our mascot is a penguin and our kernel releases are named after animals, then why not have a kernel release named after another penguin; specifically "Opus," a cartoon character by "Berke Breathed," whose comic strip "Bloom County" has been running in newspapers around the country for decades; and why not an LTS version while we're at it?

One of the more humorous instantiations of this character is one in which he dresses in Drag (put's on a dress and pearls); so I thought of the name "Opulent Opus" to refer to this instance of the character.  It makes sense to me, since the kernel nicknames are always two words in which the first letters of each word are the same; such as in Feisty Fawn; Gusty Gibbon, etc.

**I know I'm going to get flamed for this; so go ahead, give me you're best shot!
