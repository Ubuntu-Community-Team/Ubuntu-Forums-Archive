### Post by dh04000 on 2011-03-28
I converted a RC plane that was broken into a RC boat. I used the propeller as a prop in the back, and used the two servo's in unison to control the fin that directed the air from the prop in order to turn the boat left and right. That was a fun project. I think its still in my parents basement somewhere.
