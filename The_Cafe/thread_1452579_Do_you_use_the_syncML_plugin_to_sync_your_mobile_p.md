### Post by Irihapeti on 2010-04-12
It is proposed to remove the syncML plugin from the Lucid repos.

If you use this to sync your syncML-capable mobile phone with Ubuntu, this means that you'll need to find some other way to get the plugin and supporting libraries.

You might be interested to look at [bug 524938]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/libopensync-plugin-syncml/+bug/524938") in Launchpad and maybe add a comment. There are a few there already.

I'll go on record as saying that I'm not very impressed with this idea.
