### Post by SomeGuyDude on 2009-09-26
> **rob86 said:**
> I seem to have figured out Sonata + MPD. Does CMUS not use MPD? That had me confused, I was thinking it did, but then I realized I didn't need to use mpd. I seem to have them both running now, I found the man page for CMUS and MPD + Sonata is relatively easy to set up without instructions.

Also: if you use hotkeys, remember that Sonata has a bunch of commands. I use the following:

Super+Left: sonata --prev
Super+Right: sonata --next
Super+Up: sonata --pp
Super+Down: sonata --toggle

Now Sonata hides in my tray and I can scroll through the playlist, stop and start, and then if I want to manually mess around I can instantly bring 'er up and do it, then hide again.
