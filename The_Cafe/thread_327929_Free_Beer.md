### Post by RChickenMan on 2006-12-30
I'm kind of surprised no one has posted about this yet...  I heard a story on Marketplace today about a guy who is fed up with corporate ownership of intellectual property and decided to start brewing a beer based on the same philosophy as free/open source software.   The way the story was presented makes this link very clear.  The beer is not free as in no cost, because of course it costs money to brew beer.  However, the recipe for the beer (or "source code" if you will) can be freely distributed for others to brew and profit off of as well.

[Link to marketplace story]("http://marketplace.publicradio.org/shows/2006/12/29/PM200612295.html")
