### Post by el_koraco on 2011-05-02
Ubuntu's 6 months release schedule is geared towards having the users test the OS in preparation for the LTS releases, which Canonical markets to enterprises and sells their services for. They sure as hell wouldn't present their customers with a release that had a host of undocumented bugs. That's where us freeloaders come in. If you're into stability, you're good with Lucid. With the PPA system, backporting is so easy, the only reason to use the regular release schedule is to have fun when thing break down :D
