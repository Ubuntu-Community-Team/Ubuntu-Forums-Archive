### Post by Corfy on 2010-07-17
My all-time favorite movie is [1776](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0068156/), based on the Broadway play of the same name. It is an historial musical comedy about the events leading up to the signing of the Declaration of Independence. And other than the fact that the Second Continental Congress didn't spontaneously break out into song, the movie is surprisingly historically accurate.

And some of the issues they faced are things that still face us today:

John Adams: A second flood, a simple famine, plagues of locusts everywhere, or a cataclysmic earthquake, I'd accept with some despair. But no, You sent us Congress! Good God, Sir, was that fair? 

John Adams: I have come to the conclusion that one useless man is called a disgrace; that two are called a law firm, and that three or more become a Congress!
