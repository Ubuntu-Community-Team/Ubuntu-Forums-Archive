### Post by Aetherius on 2008-02-06
Its a pure gravity simulation (without collisions), the conditions **can** exist for nested orbits, but its unlikely in such a small scale demo, however i assure you that each and every body exerts an appropriate force on each every other body (trust me on this one, testing this with hundreds of bodies made my head hurt and arrays explode). I have, using two large bodies and several small bodies, managed to achieve a few different *sustained* orbits :)
