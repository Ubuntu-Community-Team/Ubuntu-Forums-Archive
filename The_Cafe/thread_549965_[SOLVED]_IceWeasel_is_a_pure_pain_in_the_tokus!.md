### Post by Ebuntor on 2007-09-13
> **ukripper said:**
> Interesting, I only use knoppix for recovery purposes only..is it any better than swiftfox?

The [Iceweasel wiki page]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iceweasel") explains the differences between Firefox and Iceweasel.

Swiftfox is an optimized version of Firefox while Iceweasel is (almost) a perfect copy of Firefox. There is however also a optimized version of Iceweasel called [Swiftweasel]("http://swiftweasel.sourceforge.net/").

EDIT: The Swiftfox developers don't fully comply[ with the     MPL, GPL, or LGPL]("http://www.getswiftfox.org/"). So if you have a problem with that you're better of with Swiftweasel.
