### Post by zeljkojagust on 2012-05-02
Ok, this is simple. A short story of your encounter with a celebrity. Here's mine:

01. Mikael Stanne ([Dark Tranquility]("http://http://www.darktranquillity.com/") voice/frontman) - 2008 on a concert in Ljubljana/SLO. My wife wanted to meet the band so much, so I went backstage after the show and after pushing around with security and almost getting slapped Mikael gets out from backstage to hall and notices my shirt. I had a black custom made one with big Quark portrait in front (Ferengi from Star Trek DS9). He said heeey nice shirt man, you want to swap for mine. And I said sure no problem, only of you and the rest of the band put a signature on it... so I got Decibel T-Shirt for my Ferengi one with signatures from the complete band and my wife got to meet the guys... she was ecstatic. 

02. Dennis Casey ([Flogging Molly]("http://http://www.floggingmolly.com") guitarist) - ran on to him after the 2010 show in Zagreb/CRO, we went for a drink, which led to a whole night drinking. Such a great guy, it was a pleasure to meet him.

03. [Evergray]("http://http://evergrey.net/") tour bus party after the show in Budapest/HUN in 2006... I think I don't have to tell you how that went, you can imagine... :)
