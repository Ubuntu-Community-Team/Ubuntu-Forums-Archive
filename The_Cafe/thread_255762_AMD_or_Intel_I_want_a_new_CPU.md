### Post by drucer on 2006-09-13
Get Intel core2duo! Two reasons:

[http://www.tomshardware.com/2006/07/14/core2_duo_knocks_out_athlon_64/](http://www.tomshardware.com/2006/07/14/core2_duo_knocks_out_athlon_64/)
