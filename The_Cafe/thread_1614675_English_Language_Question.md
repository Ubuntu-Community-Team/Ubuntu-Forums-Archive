Hi,

Many from English-speaking countries can glance at a person and guess that at least part of his or her family is from somewhere in Eurasia that is East of India (Including China) and north of the Pacific Islands & Australia.

Fill in the blank. Do we have a word, in English, that expresses what Oriental once did without a high probability of being offensive?

Without resorting to: "He's tall and looks like he's got some European and east-of-india-including-china-and-north-of-the-pacific-islands blood in him."
