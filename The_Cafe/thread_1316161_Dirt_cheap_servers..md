### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-11-06
Get some sort of Mini-ITX motherboard with an Atom or a Pentium M, some sort of power supply and a little fan, cut a few holes into a Tupperware container for some airflow and you've got yourself a beautiful little server.

If you're not a do-it-yourself kind of person, you could buy a "nettop".

If you go dumpster diving or buying old computers, you're almost certain to find a computer that will chew through the watts, and probably won't be as powerful as an Atom anyway.
