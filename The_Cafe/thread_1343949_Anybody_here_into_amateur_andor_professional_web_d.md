### Post by Squonk07 on 2009-12-02
A cursory forum search revealed a few mentions, but I was wondering who in here is into this kind of stuff.

I'm still very much a beginner, and I started a little over a year ago. So far I've got HTML and CSS pretty much down, and I can do some basic JavaScript and PHP.

One of the reasons I got into it was because it's a great, cheap hobby. All you really need to do almost anything is a decent text editor, an image editor, some web space, and an FTP. Microsoft and Adobe would like for people to purchase expensive programs with GUIs, but I'm always amused that I've done far and away better with just Bluefish, FileZilla, and GIMP than I ever did with MS FrontPage.

Not to lionize, but here's some of the stuff I've done:

[My homepage]("http://ryderduncan.com/index.html")
[Another site page]("http://ryderduncan.com/wisdom.html")
[The Stained Glass Mosaic]("http://ryderduncan.com/stained_glass")
[Old Gallery page from family business site]("http://icthyosenterprises.net/Gallery.htm") - Done with FrontPage years ago and pretty bad
[New Gallery page]("http://icthyosenterprises.net/newstuff/gallery.html") - Made with Bluefish and still a proof-of-concept

One of the other main reasons I got into this is because a lot of non-professional, user-generated pages are difficult to look at, to be kind. Also, they don't follow web standards. I see web design as a form of art, and while I'm not nearly on the level of the professionals, I still enjoy being creative.

With this thread I just want to see how many of you out there have ever done some web design. Also, if you've got some examples you'd like to share, that would be awesome.
