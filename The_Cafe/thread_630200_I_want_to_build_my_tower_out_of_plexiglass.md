### Post by svtfmook on 2007-12-03
i've done it before.  but with what you spend on good plexi and hardware, it's cheaper to just buy one of the premade kits.  i used mine for about 2 weeks before going back to a regular case.  it looks like poo with all the wires exposed.  with a regular case, you can manage cables to be better concealed.  imo, just get a decent clean case with a window.  like the cooler master centurion 5 or something along the lines of "decent mfg'er and a case with a name that doesn't contain the words "fighter", "gaming" or something that looks like a cyborg abortion.

but if you want a complete acrylic case, but one of the premades.
