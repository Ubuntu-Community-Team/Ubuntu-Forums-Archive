### Post by RandomJoe on 2008-10-12
I'm sick to death of blues, especially the paler shades.  Not sure whether I got OD'ed on Windows or if it's just a general dislike of blue - used to be my favorite color, but now I don't even like blue in real life.

The default Ubuntu colors are fine with me, although not terribly exciting.  I'm generally happier with darker colors for the desktop and general system UI.  I also don't like paper-white backgrounds on anything - which means web pages frequently annoy me!  As a result I tend to do a lot of work in terminals with the background set to black, default foreground to something around amber to red.

I've been using the theme "Orange-LiNstaBlackPlastic" for a while - dark, with some nice dimension to things so it doesn't look flat.

Going off-topic, I dislike blues so much I even got rid of the cool-white fluorescent tubes in my office at work.  Unfortunately, it seems I can only get down to about 3000K "warm white" in those fixtures (at least, easily) whereas I would prefer something farther down toward 2800K.  I also bought warm white tubes for my fluorescent camping lanterns!  (I already use warm white CFLs at home, those are around 2800-2900K.)
