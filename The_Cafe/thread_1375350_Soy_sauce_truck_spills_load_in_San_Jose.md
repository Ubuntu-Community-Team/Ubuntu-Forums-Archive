> **Soy sauce truck spills load in San Jose**

By Lisa Fernandez
[email]lfernandez@mercurynews.com[/email]

It was just before lunchtime today, and there was plenty of a popular Asian condiment to go around.

Unfortunately, the soy sauce wasn't in a restaurant. It was all over 13th Street near Highway 101 in San Jose.

Around 11:30 a.m., a truck spilled a load of soy sauce, according to police Sgt. Ronnie Lopez.

The accident caused one minor injury and a traffic head ache for motorists as crews cleaned up the salty black mess.
