### Post by gletob on 2010-01-17
A direct cable is not possible.  You'll need to find something the will scale the resoloutiong to one appropriate for the TV, and VGA doesn't carry any type of sound so you would need a seprate 3.5 Mm to RCA adapter.

3.5 to Stereo RCA: $5

VGA to Composite adapter: ~$50-75
