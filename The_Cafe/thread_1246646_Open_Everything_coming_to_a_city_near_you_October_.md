### Post by DouglasAWh on 2009-08-21
Open Everything is a global conversation about the art, science and spirit of 'open'. It gathers people using openness to create and improve software, education, media, philanthropy, architecture, neighbourhoods, workplaces and the society we live in: everything. It's about thinking, doing and being open.

The list of cities for October 31st is:

North America
# San Francisco, CA, USA
# Washington, DC, USA
# Madison, WI, USA
# New York, NY, USA
# Ann Arbor, MI, USA
# Toronto, Canada
# Montreal, Canada

Europe
# Berlin, Germany
# London, UK
# Madrid, Spain
# Innsbruck, Austria

Asia
# Delhi, India

We'd love to add more!  My subject can only be true if you help us make it true!  If you are in a country that celebrates Halloween, please feel free to 

A) Hold the event either on the 30th or Nov 1st (or another week, if that is better for you)
B) Wear costumes!  You could even have a theme...like best FOSS mascot!

Please send information about this event to your local hippies and your local libertarians.  This is *not* a technology event, though we certainly welcome technologists.  This is not a political event, though we do welcome discussion on open government/politics.  This event is really about talking about openness in *all* walks of life.  For us in Madison, it will be our 3rd event.  For many places this will be the 1st or 2nd event.  Please become a part of something special!

Please feel free to contact me directly if you have questions about the event.

For more info, see [http://openeverything.wik.is/](http://openeverything.wik.is/)

Also, join our discussion group at [https://groups.google.com/group/open-everything-discuss](https://groups.google.com/group/open-everything-discuss)

We can be found on freenode at #openeverything
