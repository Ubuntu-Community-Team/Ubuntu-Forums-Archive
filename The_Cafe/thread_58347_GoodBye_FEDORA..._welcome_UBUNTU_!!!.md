### Post by nama4ever on 2005-08-19
Hi everybody !!

I've just left this message on fedoraforum.org....

_____________________________________________________________

Hi everybody...

I've been with Rh/Fedora since Rh9, and I had never tried another distro. I was satisfied of those system-config-tools and of the great number of continuosly release updates... but there were always something missing: this runs but breaks that, and so on... even avoiding "bleeding edge" versions I always had [minor, but tiring] problems... in the end, everything seemed ... unstable.Eg: Repos messed up continuosly [livna+rpmforge].

Last week I gave Kubuntu a try: All the repos are configured by default, plenty of packages, synaptic is much more responsive than yum... after 3 days of downloading and configuration I've realized my "dream machine" and nothing is broken... Eg: all Kde packages are packaged separately, so that I don't have to install gigs of software to find an app.

And .... AMAROK IS DEFAULT IN KUBUNTU !!!

So I'm leaving: thanks to those who helped me [BTW: ubuntuforum.org support is IMPRESSIVE] and greetings from Italy.
