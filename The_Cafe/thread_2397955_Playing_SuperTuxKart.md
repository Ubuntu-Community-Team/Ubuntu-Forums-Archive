### Post by deadflowr on 2018-08-06
I would think story mode is self explanatory, but basically your tasked with saving the world or the universe or something. (Truth be told, I never care about whatever the motive is in any game I ever play)
To play you start in a wide open area and you need to find bubbles with floating numbers hanging around. 
It's progress based, so the only numbers you can play are ones you have tokens for. (bubbles that you don't have access to cannot be breached (entered, accessed, what have you), if that makes sense)
When you enter the bubbles, a challenge notification will show telling you to press your fire key to start it.
As you win you gain more tokens, which then unlock more races and more bubbles.

There should be a tutorial bubble that explains all this in the very beginning of the game (the story mode game that is)
The tutorial bubble does not have a number, but it is a bubble.
