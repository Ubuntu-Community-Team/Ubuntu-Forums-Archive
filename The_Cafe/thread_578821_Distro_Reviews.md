### Post by cogadh on 2007-10-17
I think you will find that many of the negative reviews are done by fan-boys/girls of a distro that is losing ground to Ubuntu in the popularity game. For some reason, rather than approach Ubuntu with an open mind and actually try to see why it is so popular, they instead try to find the little things that are wrong with it, sometimes going as far as to make up BS and half-truths to justify their dislike of Ubuntu. 

Its kind of sad really. I've used several different distros over the years and really liked many of them, but I never let my feelings over a distro dictate my opinions of other distros or let it prevent me from trying yet another one out. If I had, I never would have ended up with Ubuntu in the first place, and even though I currently think Ubuntu is the best distro out there, if something else catches my eye, I'm not against giving it a shot.
