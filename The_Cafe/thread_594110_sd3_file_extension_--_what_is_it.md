### Post by pieisgood4589 on 2007-10-27
By analyzing the difference between files
sdelta3 outputs tiny highly compressible patches.
Patches translate source tarballs into newer
or older versions or similar sources such as:
mozilla into firefox or firefox into mozilla.

sdelta is honed to the characteristics of source tarballs.
For deltas of binary files try: bdelta and edelta.
