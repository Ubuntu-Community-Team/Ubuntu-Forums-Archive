### Post by Kvark on 2005-11-09
sudo apt-get intstall wesnoth wesnoth-data wesnoth-music wesnoth-httt wesnoth-trow wesnoth-sotbe wesnoth-ei

...and then you got the game plus 4 campaigns from the universe repos.

Add wesnoth-server if you plan on hosting multiplayer games and wesnoth-editor if you plan on making scenarios.
