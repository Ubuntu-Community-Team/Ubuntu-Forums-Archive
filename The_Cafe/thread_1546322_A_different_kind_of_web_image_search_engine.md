### Post by undecim on 2010-08-05
For finding different sizes/versions of images (for example, you have a captioned image that you want the text removed [http://www.tineye.com](http://www.tineye.com) has always worked best for me. Most other places will give you similar images, but TinEye gives you images that are likely from the same source image.
