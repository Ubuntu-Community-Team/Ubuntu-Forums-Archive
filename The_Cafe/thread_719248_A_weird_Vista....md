### Post by gashcr on 2008-03-08
I just wanted to share a very funny thing that happened today. In the store I work for we ( well, OK, I ) put PCLOS to a box we have for display with a very nice 22 inch display, essentially for ethic reasons... you know... I configured compiz on it and all the bells and whistles I could so people who like to mess around with it could appreciate the nice effects. 

But today something almost scary happened...

A group of guys came into the store and one of them began to play with the PC. He was amazed with the effects, but confused at the same time because there was obviously something different about it. So he asked the other guys what they thought could it be that makes it look different. Then one of them step aside and said: "Oh, It obviously a weird Vista".

I was hearing from the other side of the counter, and I almost got knocked to the ground... half laughs... half disappointment... I didn't even bother waking up and going there to explain them what they had in front... I was bind by perplexity... 

So, today I discovered there's a new version for Windows... Vista Ultimate Weird.
