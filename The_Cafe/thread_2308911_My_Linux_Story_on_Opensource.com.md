Awesome story man.:) Mine started back in 1995 when Linus Torvalds was looking for beta testers to test his floppy boot disk. (this was pre-ubuntu days). Every PC that I  had  that had MS XP on it became dog-slow and loaded with malware. I was going nuts! :)  Then I loaded hardy Heron. I goes .. "ah haa" ... went back to XP misery for a while and finally converted when Lucid Lynx was released. Maveric Meekat sealed my migration from Windows to Ubuntu.

Moral of the story .. don't throw out those old PCs ...lol
