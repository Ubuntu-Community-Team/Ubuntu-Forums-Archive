### Post by altonbr on 2009-03-10
I recently purchased a Blackberry Curve and was deeply disappointed to hear that Linux wasn't supported and in fact, wasn't even an afterthought to RIM.

Someone posted a [petition for getting RIM to support Blackberrys under Linux]("http://www.petitiononline.com/bb1d1234/petition.html") on the [community help page for Blackberry]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/Blackberry").

I just wanted to point this petition out to the community in case anyone else is disappointed with this move. I'm especially disappointed because the [co-CEO Jim Balsillie]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jim_Balsillie") is from my hometown.

Luckily (and hopefully), we'll be able to use a program called 'barry' in Ubuntu 9.04, but as of right now, it's not working for me.

Please please PLEASE sign the petition!
