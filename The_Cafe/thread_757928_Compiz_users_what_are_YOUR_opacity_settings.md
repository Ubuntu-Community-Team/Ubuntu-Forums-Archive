Since I exist largely to counterbalance the "compiz sucks" crowd, I'll open this one up, too!

I love toying with opacity in Compiz, so I'm wondering what you use. Here's my short list:

1) ALL menus are 90%. Tooltip | Menu | PopupMenu | DropdownMenu set to 90.

2) Pidgin IM windows (not the buddy list) are set to 95%. class=Pidgin & !title=Buddy List set to 95.

3) Also under "Move Window" I have opacity set to 80%. The nice thing here is if I need to translate directions from one window to another, I can just click on the titlebar of the window I'm working on and see what's under it.

What do you use?
