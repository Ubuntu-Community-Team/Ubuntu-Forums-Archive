### Post by John T. Monkey on 2008-05-09
I have a Sony Ericson K800i, and it is by far the best phone I have ever had. Also, there are most likely newer ones out now, but this one seems to meet all the requirements you set out. 

Basics are all there 

It does play mp3's. I have 2 1gb memory cards for it, so it doesn't hold as much as my 40gb Creative Zen, but its enough for most things

It could not possibly play any nicer with Linux: you plug the usb cable in to the computer, the phone does things and then a menu flashes up giving the option to transfer files, printer use mobile internet. Once you've chosen the transfer files option on the phone, the computer mounts 2 usb storage devices, 1 is the phone and the other is the memory card. Then a case of copying your files and unmounting. No additional software, drivers or tinkering needed. 

It also came with the usb cable and hands free... my previous phones were nokias and they didn't have the usb cable, so I was impressed with this.   

Also the caera s the same quality as my digital camera, it plays nicer with Linu than my camera, and it has more memory: my digital camera has been gathering dust since I got this phone. Perfect replacement.
