Well. first of all, watch the original Macross series, not the mutilated version the US put out.

After that, Gundam is another classic, and so is Escaflowne, though it is a very different cup of tea. When it comes to realistic mecha series, my all-time favourite is probably Gunbuster. It's a short series, 4 episodes, but it is packed with awesomeness.

Moving forward, my all-time favourite mecha series is Neon Genesis Evangelion, though YMMV. Some people hate it, some people love it. You should watch it regardless, find out what you think. Personally, I think it is the greatest piece of art in the history of the human race, but I am an evageek :D

If you're in for a series with completely over-the-top action, you should go get Tengen Toppa Gurren Laggan. Amazing series.
