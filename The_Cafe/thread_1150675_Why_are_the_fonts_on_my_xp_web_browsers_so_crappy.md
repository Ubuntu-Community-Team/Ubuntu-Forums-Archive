### Post by Kareeser on 2009-05-06
Ubuntu 9.04 autodetects graphics card capabilities and monitor settings and turns on subpixel smoothing when it is supported, which is most of the time.

Windows has it off by default.

You can turn on "use smooth screen fonts" somewhere in Windows. It's in the Appearances tab of the Display Properties, I think.
