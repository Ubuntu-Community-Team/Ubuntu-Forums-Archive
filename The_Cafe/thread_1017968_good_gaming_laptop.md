It's true that a lot of apps aren't multi-threaded apps and they won't take advantage of more than one core.  But nevertheless, multiple core processors *are* still useful for multitasking.  If you're running more than one app at a time, they're useful.  If an app running on one core becomes processor intensive, your app running on another core won't take a hit for it.
