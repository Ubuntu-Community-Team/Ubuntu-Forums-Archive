Has anyone here seen this film? It is a "cult classic" (so I'm told) from the '80s that used to be shown at sci-fi conventions. Well it has been (re-)released on DVD and can be found at Amazon.com:

[http://www.amazon.com/Hick-Warpd-Sci-Fi-Double-Feature/dp/B002C4JUXA/ref=sr_1_2?ie=UTF8&s=dvd&qid=1245447827&sr=1-2]("http://www.amazon.com/Hick-Warpd-Sci-Fi-Double-Feature/dp/B002C4JUXA/ref=sr_1_2?ie=UTF8&s=dvd&qid=1245447827&sr=1-2")

Before I go any farther, let me state that I have NO financial stake/interest of any kind with this Moovie. I am simply a fan (a big fan, I guess) who wants to share. It is, of course, a Star Trek spoof: "A space satire of trailer park proportions". It was shot on Super 8 film by (then) amateur film makers. But it is truly classic, if not a work of true art.

I put a trailer on YouTube, if you want to take a peek: 

[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=06B7-LSNA_E]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=06B7-LSNA_E")
