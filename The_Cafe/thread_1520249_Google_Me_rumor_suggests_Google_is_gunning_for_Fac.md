### Post by samalex on 2010-06-29
I think many of Google's social networking and blogging tools are great in theory, but most are poorly executed.  I use Buzz, Blogger, Picasa, and a few other social networking sites they built/bought, but other than Picasa most aren't nearly as robust as their competitors.  Buzz is worse than Facebook when it comes to setting-up privacy settings (is it supposed to compete with Twitter?), Blogger has nothing on WordPress, and I'd say Picasa is on bar with Flickr, though I like Flickr better.  Also I still haven't quite found a use for Google Wave, which looks like a competitor to IM or IRC.  

So I'll wait and see what Google Me has to offer, but I agree with ubunterooster in that not many will move away from Facebook for yet another Google toy.  Also Digg 4 is coming out soon (already out in Beta to some), so I bet it'll pull more people from Facebook than Google Me will.
