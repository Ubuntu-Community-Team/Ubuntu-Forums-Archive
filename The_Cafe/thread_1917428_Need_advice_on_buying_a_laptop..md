### Post by LowSky on 2012-01-31
> **ask_ said:**
> One thing I wish to know is whether 2 GB RAM is sufficient for my purposes. I have a few old computers with 1 GB RAM and they run fine. What sort of purposes does a 4 GB RAM satisfy  ?

If those old PCs run fine why upgrade?
Go big or go home. 4GB is the minimum you should get with a new PC. My new one I upgraded to 8GB once I got it home. No lie. As for what processor to get. Get the i3 or better. The Pentium isn't bad but the i3 runs smoother. My mother just got the newest Pentium and my sis got the newer i3. The i3 just feels smoother in day to day operation. Comparing my own new laptop with an AMD A8-3500M to the i3, the i3 stinks at graphics, 1080i/p is not as smooth as Intel puts on. Just fair warning.
