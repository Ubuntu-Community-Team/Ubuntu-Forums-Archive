### Post by HermanAB on 2009-08-02
You can get openfitness here:
[http://www.workoutware.com/](http://www.workoutware.com/)

" Instructions: After you download the file, make it executable (chmod +x openfitness_unix_2_0_0.sh) and then run it."

To install it in your home directory, try something like this:
Right click browser, save as (ends up on Desktop)
$ cd
$ mkdir ww
$ cd ww
$ mv ~/Desktop/openfitness[tab] .
$ chmod  +x open[tab]
$ ./open[tab]

and see what happens.
