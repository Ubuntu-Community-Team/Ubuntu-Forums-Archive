> "France's top trade official was meeting Friday with the financial director of Apple France to discuss several recent claims by iPhone users that their phone screens cracked or exploded....French media have reported more than 10 cases of iPhone screens cracking or phones exploding. The first case that caught attention was in early July, when an iPhone explosion reportedly sent glass into the eye of an 18-year-old from Aix-en-Provence....The European Commission, the European Union's executive arm, said last week it spoke to Apple about reports in different EU countries of problems with iPhones and iPods, and that the company is investigating."[http://online.wsj.com/article/SB125146150549366525.html](http://online.wsj.com/article/SB125146150549366525.html)
