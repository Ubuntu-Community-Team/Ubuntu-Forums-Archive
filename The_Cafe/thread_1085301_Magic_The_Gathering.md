I used to play.  when I realized that teh rich kids always won, me and my friends started playing over the internet.  It didn't matter how much money you had, you could build the deck exactly the way you wanted it.

A couple of applications you might enjoy:

For Windows:
Apprentice: [http://www.magic-league.com/download/apprentice.php](http://www.magic-league.com/download/apprentice.php)

Mindless: [http://mindless.sourceforge.net/](http://mindless.sourceforge.net/)


These two applications are compatible, give your Windows friends Apprentice and you can play them using Mindless on Ubuntu ;)
