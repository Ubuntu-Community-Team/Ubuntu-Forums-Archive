### Post by juancarlospaco on 2010-10-07
Graduated from University means nothing if only you got that,
i work at an university, i see on people that finish the career quickly,
they are GREAT on math, _seriously great_, but they dont have bare-metal-IT Skills,
they didn't learn how to get an LXC up'n'running via SSH, 
dont learn how to configure Port-Channels on Cisco routers,
they didn't learn how to re-ensamble a crashed RAID 6.

I dont say that they dont want/try to, 
but things like that seems too hard to learn for these people,
even if they finish the career faster, its like a different set of skills,
not so noticeable on Development area, but on SysAdmin or Network Admin.
No offense.
