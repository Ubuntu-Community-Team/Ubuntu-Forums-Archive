### Post by neoargon on 2010-07-18
I recently tried the [Menuet os ]("http://www.menuetos.net/")and it's fork [kolibri]("http://www.kolibrios.org/") os . It is wonderful that these operating systems with a good enough GUI can less than 2MB . The menuetos's window decorator supports transparency !!!!. Not just that , a lot of applications are included in this 2 MB .
The system boots in 1or 2 seconds. That's the coolest thing

For those who don't know , [Menuet os ]("http://www.menuetos.net/")and [kolibri]("http://www.kolibrios.org/") os are operating systems created in assembly language other than"c" . So the size can be made small ,though I didn't think it can be this much smaller
