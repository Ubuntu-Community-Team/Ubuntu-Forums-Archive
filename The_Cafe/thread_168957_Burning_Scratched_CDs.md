### Post by gingermark on 2006-05-01
In grip, experiment with turning off paranoia and extra-paranoia - this will speed up the rip, as there is less error-correction, and sometimes this can help get a rip of a scratched CD. Of course, it might turn out rubbish, but you can but see.
