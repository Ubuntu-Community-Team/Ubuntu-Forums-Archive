### Post by Kvark on 2005-10-07
Gnome is default in Ubuntu, KDE is default in Kubuntu, you must have picked Kubuntu instead of Ubuntu by mistake. Install the package ubuntu-desktop and then you'll get to choose between KDE and Gnome when you log in.
