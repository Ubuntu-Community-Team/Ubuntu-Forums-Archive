### Post by igknighted on 2007-09-19
I find Zimbra to be nice, but not quite as nice as Scalix.  They offer a very similar product, but with Zimbra you are rather tied to their PIM software or the browser-based client.  With Scalix you can use Evolution, Kontact or even Outlook with the proper plugins.

Note that Scalix is now owned by Xandros as of a couple months ago, and Zimbra was recently purchased by Yahoo.  So pick your poison there.
