### Post by matthew on 2008-12-01
Greetings, everyone!

In the spirit of fun, for the holiday season, I invite you to join me in dressing up your avatar. Let's keep this fun and frivolous, please. 

I'm wearing a Santa hat. You can use holly, snowmen, Christmas trees, a Menorah, a Nativity, whatever, but please don't go overboard into offensive (or easily offended) mode. 

If this makes you feel grumpy, please [read this before posting]("http://matthewhelmke.net/wordpress/2007/12/23/holiday-greetings-and-hyper-sensitivity/").

Technically, political and religious stuff is not generally welcome on the forums, but if you can keep your avatar directly related to the season and celebratory in nature, that will fulfill the spirit of what we are trying to prevent, which is in-your-face arguments or people feeling forced to follow other people's belief systems while feeling unwelcome themselves.

I'm also okay with avatars related to Yule, Winter Solstice, Kwanzaa, Festivus, and so on. Anyway, you all get the idea. Let's have a fun, inviting, and friendly time of celebration.

Thanks!
