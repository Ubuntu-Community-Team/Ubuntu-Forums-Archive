### Post by racerraul on 2009-07-09
Man you just hit me were it hurts... I have a weakness for retro gaming.

I have a ton of consoles but my favorite is the Atari 2600/7800 and the 800xl.  As a matter of fact I just bought some homebrew titles for the 7800.

If you like PacMan you owe it to yourself to get the [PacMan Arcade]("http://www.atariage.com/store/index.php?main_page=product_info&cPath=21_85_39&products_id=104") hack of Mrs.PacMan by Rob Kudla [(screenshots)]("http://www.atariage.com/hack_page.html?SystemID=2600&SoftwareHackID=5")... it really is what PacMan should have been back when Atari released that horrible rushed out junk they called PacMan... till this day I hate them for that huge disappointment I felt when I brought that crap home.

Now days the homebrew crowd is putting out some quality games that makes even the best games by Imagic & Ativision pale in comparison.

One in particular... [LadyBug]("http://www.atariage.com/store/index.php?main_page=product_info&cPath=21_85_24&products_id=829").  Let's just say that game made my jaw drop.

In case you are not in tune... make your way to [AtariAge]("http://www.atariage.com/index.html") and join the fun with the latest and greatest from that community... I been a part of it for a few year now, and have bought a ton of their new games including a flash cart that allows me to load ROM's to test demos and help with reporting bugs of games in development...

Of late... the 7800 has been blessed with titles never released before of aracade classics...
Burger Time 
Q-Bert
Super PacMan
Jr PacMan
PacMan Collection (includes all this in 1 cart)
    * Pac-Man (The Original!)
    * Puck Man (Same game, more difficult maze)
    * Hangly Man (Empty Mazes? Vertical Tunnels? Invisible Mazes?)
    * Ultra Pac-Man (6 New Mazes for a new variety)
    * Random Mazes* (You just never know which maze will appear next!)
    * Ms. Pac-Man (The second installment of the Pac-Family)
    * Ms. Pac-Attack (Same game, more challenging mazes)
    * Ms. Random Mazes* (Like Random Mazes above, but with the Ms. flavor)
Asteroids Deluxe
Space Duel
Space Invaders

All nearly perfect ports... yeah I'm loving it :lolflag:
