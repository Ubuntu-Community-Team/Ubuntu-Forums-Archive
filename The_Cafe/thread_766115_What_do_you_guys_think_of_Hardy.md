### Post by nina_aoki on 2008-04-25
I downloaded 8.04 to two machines earlier tonight -- the download and update took forever, but it was well worth it.

This version is incredibly slick and all of the wireless issues I was having with my HP Notebook were also fixed with this release too.  Also - new versions of Open Office and lots of other apps - all extremely cool.

I'm personally pleased to see how much Ubuntu has matured as an OS - especially with Red Hat saying that they were staying out of the desktop market last week.  A few more tweaks and I think Ubuntu will be poised to challenge the establishment as a reasonable alternative for those who don't consider themselves 'geeky enough for linux' --  but it just works!

I think everyone should take a good look at this release and update.  Both of my machines are running perfectly.
