### Post by Chilli Bob on 2009-02-20
[www.jamendo.com]("http://www.jamendo.com")

It has a huge range of music, some of it REALLY good.  Go in through Rhythmbox, (there is a link under "stores") and listen to random songs, or go to the website and try music and download whole albums.  I've found a heap of stuff in my taste by searching for "fusion" or "progressive", but all genre are represented.  It's awesome.
