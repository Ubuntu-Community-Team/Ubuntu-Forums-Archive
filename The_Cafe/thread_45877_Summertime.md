### Post by weekend warrior on 2005-07-02
Well it's the first Saturday in July and for me that means summer break time! It also means that just like years past, the computer gets put away for a couple months. *gasp!* Yes that's right, other than an occasional email check, (mostly at netcafes) I stay off the computer for the remainder of summer. Think you could do the same? 

I've found that since I started this habit, my summers have been more relaxing, enjoyable, memorable, and feel longer. So I guess this goes out as a small piece of advice - give up the computer for a bit this summer. You *will* feel the difference. If it's really tough, well then honestly that should tell you something about how much power it has over you and your priorities in life. 

So with that, I'll be dropping off the screen for now, getting ready to enjoy summer - appreciate the company of friends and family, do some traveling and hiking, hit the beach and the waves, laze away under a tree with a good book, take long walks at sunset, whatever strikes my fancy, in short - enjoy life.  


Be good, be kind, be safe, be well. 

I be gone.......
