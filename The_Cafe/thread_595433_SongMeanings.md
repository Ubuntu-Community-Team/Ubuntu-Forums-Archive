### Post by GStubbs43 on 2007-10-28
>  SongMeanings was created when we saw a void in most of the lyric sites around -- they didn't have any user interaction.
You goto the site, get your lyrics and that's about it. We're different.

We allow users to post their comments about their favorite (and not so favorite) songs and even submit their own lyrics.
Our goal is to create the world's largest and most functional lyrics site, period. We also have a community aspect with user profiles, message boards and more.

[http://www.songmeanings.net/](http://www.songmeanings.net/)

Basically, it's a site for lyrics and comments on the lyrics for people to talk about the songs and their meanings.

>  The site queries a MySQL database with PHP scripts written by our own developers. The server runs Apache on Linux.

We are currently non-profit and are here 'for the fun of it.' Any money we acquire through advertisements is put back into the site completely.
