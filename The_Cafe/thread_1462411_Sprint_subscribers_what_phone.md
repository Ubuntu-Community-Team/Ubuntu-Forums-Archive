### Post by kavon89 on 2010-04-25
Don't waste your time with Palm anything, they're going out of business for a reason.

I recommend looking into Android phones. HTC Hero or wait until the HTC EVO is out. You could also look into the Nexus One's Sprint compatible version, but I've researched the N1 and read some complaints on their official forum about signal issues etc, and I was dumping by blackberry and T-Mobile for that very reason... So I went with the Droid.

It all depends on what you plan to use it for. I send texts & emails often so I like the flexibility of the physical or virtual keyboard on the Droid. I haven't spent much time looking around for apps but I've found a couple handy ones and there are thousands added every month. Can't comment on the game selection because I'm not a gamer.

Also, I've found the virtual keyboard on Android to be overall fantastic, so don't worry about not having a physical one.

Another Also: The Hero seems to have similar tech specs as the Droid and from personal experience, it's fast enough. I've never had noticeable lag and the web browser renders pages and scrolls through them just fine.
