Yesterday and it's amazing!

I do miss my scroll wheel but just like the keyboard you get used to it after a while. Surfing the web, checking email, chatting with meebo.. It's what an ultra portable should be. I'd never suggest buying an iPhone for hardcore foss geeks but for folks who want a nice computer/phone that can fit in your pocket this is the best....
