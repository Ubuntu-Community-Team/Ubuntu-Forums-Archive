"Ally," is another Pandorabot. Alice has referred me to a few others before as well. I think she must have an AIML file of specialty bots. {AIML- Artificial Intelligence Markup Language is a derivative of XML (Extensible Markup Language.) Please see: [http://www.alicebot.org/aiml.html](http://www.alicebot.org/aiml.html) } 

Unlike the older AIML model, the new one seems to be able to follow the conversation much better and group the session into a category (see- [http://www.alicebot.org/aiml/aaa/](http://www.alicebot.org/aiml/aaa/) ) to better follow along. 

I have also been very impressed, where multiple choices are presented in the srai, the bot seems to, more often than not, choose the correct response. 

If anyone does make their own pandorabot, i definitely suggest using the newer "Annotated" AIML sets. You have access to all conversations with your bot, and there is a training interface that allows you to revise answers if they are not to your liking. 

Good luck/Gan Batte!
