### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2008-09-16
I can't vote properly on this pole.

I'm the person that gives away their old computers to family and friends. I wipe the HDD to DoD 5220 standard then install Ubuntu.

I already have plans for my next discarded computer. I am planning to give my grandchildren an old HP Pavilion with an AMD Athlon 2.8GHz dual-core processor, 1.5GB RAM, 250GB hard drive, and a CD/DVD burner with LightScribe. It currently has Fedora running on it dual booting with Windows XP Multimedia Edition.

I will be discarding it within the year when my grandchildren are between the ages of 7 and 10 years old.

I have given away several computers over the years. I bought my first home computer in 1980. No one that has received one of these computers has ever complained about what Operating System was running on them.

You may ask why I don't sell them. Simple, I don't need the money, and I am helping family and friends to have something they could not otherwise afford.
