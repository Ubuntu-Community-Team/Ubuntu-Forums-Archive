### Post by muguwmp67 on 2007-03-01
Will it boot without the CD drive attached?  Are you sure it is a cd error?  I have a thinkpad (much older than yours) that gave me an error code, and after googling it, it was a dead CMOS battery.

I have also found that parts for old laptops are much less expensive than I expected.  You might want to check ebay for a replacement CD drive or ribbon cables.

If you get into the BIOS and can't get it to boot from usb, you might want to try a PXE (i.e. network) boot if it has an integrated ethernet port (and you have a working ubuntu machine on your network)
