### Post by kostkon on 2008-10-29
Yes (assuming it is *TTF* or *OTF* font), you can put your font in the hidden folder *.fonts* that exists in your home folder.

If this folder does not exist, just create it yourself.

If you need help accessing hidden folders, don't hesitate to ask for help here.
