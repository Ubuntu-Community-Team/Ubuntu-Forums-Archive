### Post by rai_shu2 on 2013-09-04
I'm pretty sure no one has made this, so feel free to take this if you like. Okay, here it is:

You play the role of a fed up parent whose child has gotten into one form or another of trouble at school. What to do? That's right. You go on a blame rampage.

I see the first level as your character going through video game stores and smashing the evil video games with a hammer before they can infect you with evilness. The next level, you take out a music store, and so on. If you get far enough, you can warp back in time and stop the evil jazz from corrupting youth from a hundred years ago.
