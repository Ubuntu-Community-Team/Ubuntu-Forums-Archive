### Post by jwb on 2005-05-05
I highly suggest dotProject, an open sourced, PHP/MySQL based PM that is fabulous. 

We've used it here in my company for over a year now and it has been great. It has a ticket system, forums, project management (extensive project management), file sharing, contacts, calendars and more. 

The install was easy, we were able to have it up and running and USEFUL in 1 day. 

We've used it for project management between 2 groups involved in online development, but we are looking at it to roll out on a much larger basis. (I work for a 400+ person company, and it looks like it will work nicely on that scale or even larger.)

I can't say enough good about it. And since it's open source- you can even dig away at it's source to add your own improvements. But it's full featured right out of the box (or .tar as the case may be). ;-)

[http://www.dotproject.net/](http://www.dotproject.net/)
