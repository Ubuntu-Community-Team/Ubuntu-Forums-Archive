### Post by Legeril on 2011-08-24
Big horror fan here, I'll list a few I think people have missed

1. Paranormal Activity 1 and 2 - not bad for the 'home video' genre, if you into this sort of thing it's quite creepy
2. The Tunnel - fairly new one from Aus, starts great but I think they went completely the wrong way with the 'monster' about 1/2 way into the movie
3. Salo or the 120 Days of Sodom - I guess not strictly a horror movie but defiantly disturbing if a little on the pointless side
4. The Last Exorcism, I thought this movie was quite good defiantly a new take on the handi-cam horror

6. The Poughkeepsie Tapes - one of my favourites, not really a horror its more of a mock-umentary but one that you'll kick yourself for missing
