They all seem confident that one could just run a cable from a dvi-i out to a component in. They do it with salesmanship, I think. So, Has anyone ever converted digital video to an analog monitor? Was it easy? Is anyone's computer using an hdtv as a monitor?

No, hdmi can contain both analogue and digital signals, hence you can get a composite signal out of a hdmi connector.

It's simple, I have a s-video cable running from my pc to my tv. No converter required as my gfx card provides the output.

If you only have vga (which is analogue btw) you will need a converter box. The box basically lowers the frequency of the signals and uses the RGB signals to generate a composite output.
