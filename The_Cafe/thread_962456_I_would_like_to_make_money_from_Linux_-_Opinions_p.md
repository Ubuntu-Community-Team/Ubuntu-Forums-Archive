### Post by aeiah on 2008-10-29
linux wouldn't be half what it is now if people weren't involved to make money. look at what canonical, novell and redhat have contributed back, as well as projects like drbd, linuxha, kvm, apache etc. a lot of this stuff wouldnt get off the ground without companies employing people full time to develop them. even things like compiz were funded by business to kick start it (novell in this case wasnt it?)
