### Post by 1/0 on 2007-08-06
I don't have a link or anything but I remember reading a very interesting article about why things go wrong when switching to Linux. 

The key seemed to be not rushing into the whole thing. Instead step one is switching application by application to open source types. That usually is the worst part. When all that is done, its much easier for everyone to switch to Linux. 

The worst scenario is to switch all things at once, having to deal with users not understanding the new OS as well as not understanding one or many of the new applications. 

I hope that helps. Google is your friend btw.
