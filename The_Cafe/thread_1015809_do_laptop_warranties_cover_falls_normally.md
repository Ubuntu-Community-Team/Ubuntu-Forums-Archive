### Post by grazed on 2008-12-19
warranties typically never cover physical damage, other than wear and tear. cracked or broken lcd's... on cellphones, pda's, notebooks, etc.. are not covered by the warrenty. extended warrenties also usually don't cover this.

like the previous poster said, unless you paid for insurance on it, you're kind of out of luck. =/

best bet would be to find a new lcd and fitting for the notebook on ebay. ($200-300 USD)
