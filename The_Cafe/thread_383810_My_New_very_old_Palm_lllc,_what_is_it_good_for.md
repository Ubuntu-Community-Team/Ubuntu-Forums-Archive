### Post by Stew2 on 2007-03-13
I have an old Palm IIIE that has a black and white screen. I am not sure about linux support as I use it on my old 600X laptop with Windows 2K, but I have found it quite useful for an outdated gadget. I downloaded the palm connect software from the Palm site and then picked up a sync cradle off of ebay so I could sync it and install programs and such. [This site]("http://www.djajic.com/") has some handy freeware programs to check out. I mostly use it for phone numbers and email addy's, which it handles just fine, I also found a freeware app that allows me to play old infocon text adventures on it (like Zork). I like using it a lot more than our old address book with all the scribbles in it every time someone moves or gets a new phone :) . Plus it was cheap enough that I don't have to worry about breaking it :D
