### Post by p_quarles on 2008-02-27
Kopete. I've used Gaim/Pidgin in the past, and don't really think there's much difference between the two, but I use mainly Qt apps and like to keep an integrated theme on my desktop. 

They're not exactly the same, though. Pidgin has a very nice CLI client built-in (Finch), whereas Kopete ships with all of the plugins that give people trouble in Pidgin. I don't use any of these things, though, so for me they're pretty much the same.
