My current phone: **Sony Ericsson K800i**

Oh, I also installed MobiTwitter, which would allow me to tweet.  But I don't use Twitter.  Maybe one day I will...

As for my dream phone... probably one of those Linux smart phones.  When they get better and cheaper (at the moment they are ridiculously underpowered and overpriced, but that will change with time).  But for now, my crappy little dumbphone is plenty good enough!

### Post by Statik on 2012-01-11
After my experience with Sony Ericson, I'd recommend against them. They seem to have a habit of dropping support for their products as soon as they are released. I had the Xperia X10a, which was a nice phone. After 9 months, the USB port stopped working as a charging port. Wasn't able to get it repaired under warranty.

My replacement is the Samsung Galaxt S2 X (SCH-T989D) which, as far as I can tell, is the most perfect phone I have ever seen. The only two troubles I have had with it are: By default it comes locked to Vlingo as a voice dialer, which is nice unless, like me, you don't have a data plan. I had to root the phone to remove Vlingo. Its replacement is as good, but doesn't need data. The other problem is the camera's auto focus while filming which seems to have trouble with point lights, like Christmas lights. The still shots are great and most video is great but in some videos the focus tends to hunt, even after finding focus.

It comes with Kies Air, which allows me to share files, photos, etc. with my ubuntu laptop painlessly.

I love this phone!

Statik
