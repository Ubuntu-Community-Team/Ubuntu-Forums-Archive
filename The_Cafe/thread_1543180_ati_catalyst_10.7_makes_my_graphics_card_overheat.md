### Post by Legendary_Bibo on 2010-07-31
So I installed the new ati driver to fix my flickering cairo dock, which it did, but at the cost of causing artifacting, and other things like compiz effects not working correctly. The artifacting occurred after a while, and when looking at my laptop's temperature it was at 53C a few minutes after bootup which was strange because normally it's at around 40C and maintains that until I do something CPU intensive. I then realized the new driver was making my graphics card overheat for no apparent reason. So yeah, it's buggy. Good thing it was an easy fix, all I had to do was click on the fglrx driver in Hardware Drivers and it overwrote it. I'll live with a flickering cairo, but not a fried graphics card.
