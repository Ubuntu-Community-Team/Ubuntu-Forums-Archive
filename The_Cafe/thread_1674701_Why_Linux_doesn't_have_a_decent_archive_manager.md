I'm not a programing genius who can cook up some thing as I wish so I'm pleading the community to take the initiative to create a kick *** software for archive handling.

For a example there are two very good BT clients in the linux community one Deluge the uTorrent of the nix world and the qBitorrent a path finder with QT both are very good and competitive but when it comes to archiving we're way behind.
