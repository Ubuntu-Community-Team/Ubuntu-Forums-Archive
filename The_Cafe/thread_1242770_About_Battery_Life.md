### Post by Stex on 2009-08-18
I was told by a call center technician once that keeping the battery in after it is fully charged whilst on mains will shorten it's lifespan. However, call center people are often misinformed.

I would suggest that if you are leaving your laptop plugged in overnight you take the battery out anyway. If not for the idea of constant-recharging then because of the heat/explosion possibilities already mentioned.

Charging batteries can turn into dangerous things, so if you don't need them don't use them. Ask a firefighter, they'll tell you never to sleep in the same room as a charging battery.
