### Post by Excedio on 2009-04-15
Ok...background information. I work for a VRS (Video Relay Service) called CSDVRS, LLC. We employ a couple hundered Sign Language Interpreters that process phone calls for the deaf and hard of hearing. When a call center closes for the day, if an Interpreter is still in a call, s/he will send out a broadcast asking for someone to transfer the d/hoh customer to. Today I saw my favorite broadcast to date....
 
> [FONT=Calibri]Hi, have a customer I need to transfer because our center is closing **poor guy has been transferred by microsoft 100 times already and  still doesn't have an answer**, if you are the wonderful interpreter who takes this call I will fill you in  on a few more details, thanks[/FONT]
 
Anyway...I saw that and could not contain my laughter in the office. Seems the deaf and hard of hearing community are not immune to the ways of Microsoft.
 
Thought I would share. :)
