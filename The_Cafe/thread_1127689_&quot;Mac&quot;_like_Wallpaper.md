### Post by hyperdude111 on 2009-04-16
After using nature wallpapers for a while on my desktop my tastes took a slight change. Instead of a mountain picture I wanted one of those abstract alien like wallpapers similar to what you often see on a mac. After finding nothing which was either branded with the apple logo or was just stolen from leopard I decided to make my own.

After multiple efforts (and failures) I finally had one decent enough to use however I want to see what other people think of it. So I will upload it here.

Note: I made these primarily for myself so the most common resolution is 1440x1024
