### Post by irabinovitch on 2006-08-15
[SCALE 5x](http://www.socallinuxexpo.org), the Southern California Linux Expo has [issued a call for papers](http://www.socallinuxexpo.org/scale5x/cfp/scale5x.pdf).  SCALE 5x will be held on Feb 10-11, 2007 at the Los Angeles Airport Westin.  Past speakers have included Chris Dibona, Jon "maddog" Hall, and Andrew Morton.  If you are working on an interesting open-source / Linux related project, or just want to address the community SCALE welcomes you to submit a presentation proposal.
