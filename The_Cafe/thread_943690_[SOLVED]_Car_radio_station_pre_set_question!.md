### Post by hellion0 on 2008-10-10
Does anything else reset, like the clock? (Assuming it has one independant of the radio, that is. Most recent ones do.)

Is it an aftermarket radio? If it's not wired properly, it won't receive enough power when shut off to retain presets. Check the manual, as well as your car's own wiring. Alternately, you *could* wire it straight to the battery instead of running through the fusebox. (I don't advise it, though...)

And if it's neither of those, check your battery terminals. There might be a bad connection.
