### Post by mips on 2007-02-28
Looks like you need to look out for FlexATX form factor motherboards. However, the case you mentions specifies it is a microATX case so there seem to be some confusion here.

[http://www.formfactors.org/formfactor.asp](http://www.formfactors.org/formfactor.asp)

Tyan, Gigabyte Asus & IBM have FlexATX boards. Tyan makes very nice boards btw.

Another option for you would be mini-ITX/ITX and the recently announced micro-DTX. You will have to check compatibility though. 

FlexATX, mini-ITX/ITX & micro-DTX are the only sizes small enough to fit into the space you have. If i'm not mistaken I think the Shuttle range of PCs might use FlexATX motherboards.
