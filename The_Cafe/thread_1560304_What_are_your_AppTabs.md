### Post by The Real Dave on 2010-08-24
Those of you using some of the latest versions of Firefox, Firefox 4 Beta2 and up, will know about [AppTabs]("http://blog.mozilla.com/faaborg/2010/07/28/app-tabs-in-firefox-4-beta-2/").

Essentially, an AppTab is a tab that's always kept open, ready for use, showing just the favicon. AppTabs help keep your browser uncluttered, and help you get to you favorite sites quickly.

[IMG]http://cache.gawkerassets.com/assets/images/17/2010/07/500x_firefox4_b2.jpg[/IMG]

So the question is, what do you keep in your AppTabs? Mine are;

Gmail,
Facebook,
Wordpress Dashboard,
UbuntuForums ;)

You can download the latest version, Firefox 4 Beta4 [here]("http://www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/4.0b4/releasenotes/") by the way.
