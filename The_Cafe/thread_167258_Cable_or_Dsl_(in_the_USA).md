### Post by wmcbrine on 2006-04-28
In my experience, and by reputation, DSL is highly reliable. I haven't had cable Internet, but my impression from what others say is that it's less reliable. (For what it's worth, you could compare the reliability of cable TV and regular telephone service. My experience there is that cable is the first to go out in a storm, and stays out the longest; then power; then phone, which almost never goes down.) However, I have still had to powercycle my DSL modems on occasion.

Cable is potentially faster, but the speed is less stable, and you're faced with invisible caps -- warning letters, etc., with high usage of your supposedly unlimited service. DSL doesn't have this problem, TTBOMK.

If you can get it, Verizon FiOS is a better service than either.
