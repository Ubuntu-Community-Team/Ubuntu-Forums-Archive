### Post by jeyaganesh on 2008-07-03
OEM softwares should be come with hardware. Like, Windows OS CD coming with PC or laptop. If it is not coming with any hardware, except some softwares, it is illegal to sell or buy. For instance you might see selling Adobe products mostly selling as OEMs. That is illegal. If you buy one, you cant upgrade or get any of the concern company's support. So dont buy OEM softwares.
