### Post by grahammechanical on 2022-10-02
Video cameras and microphones in modern laptops are very small. They fit inside the plastic rim surrounding the screen and at the top. So, very small cameras and microphones are indeed available. What is not so small is the electronics that is needed to receive the video and sound, store it and transmit it by radio to whoever has installed the spy device. That being said, I would not be surprised at how small these devices can be. In the UK police offers routinely wear video cameras attached to their helmets or their shoulders.

I do not worry too much about this because of the country I live in. If I was living in some other countries that I can think of I would not even be discussing this subject over the internet or the telephone. 

Also I do not have a smartphone, tablet PC, smart TV connected to the internet or some other smart device connected to the internet. I do not need them. I also switch off the router/hub when I have no need to use the internet. I switch off the laptop when I have finished using it. If I had a smartphone or I carried a tablet around then I would disable WiFi until I need it.

Oh, I nearly forgot. I never visit social networking sites or use messaging apps other than email. It is not because I am privacy conscious but because I do not want to be drawn into that always online lift style.
