### Post by ssam on 2014-04-07
The donate page is shown when you try to download an ubuntu release. Go to [http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop) then click any of the download links. (I don't know why they make it hard to find).

You might also want to look at the [Ongoing crowdfunding campaigns]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1946197") thread (go to the last page to see campaigns that are currently active). Or go to the web pages of programs you use and donate directly to them.
