### Post by forrestcupp on 2010-04-01
> **whiskeylover said:**
> \0 makes it an Oct code. So, Octal 101 = Dec 65 = 'A'.

This is right.  All echo does is print out in your console exactly the same thing you tell it to print.  The -e tells echo to translate anything that comes after the '/'.  So it's just the octal values for the ASCII codes that print out April Fools.

Echo is harmless. it's just like the Basic Print command.
