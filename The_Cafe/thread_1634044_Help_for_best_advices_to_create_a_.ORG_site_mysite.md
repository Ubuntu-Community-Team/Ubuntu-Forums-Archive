### Post by samalex on 2010-11-30
.Org top level domain names generally have the same price and availability as .Net or .Com unless ICANN has changed things.  I've had a .Org for our LUG for about 5 years now and it's around $15/year each time it's up for renewal.  I don't think there's anything special you need to do to register a .Org domain.

As for hosting, I like HostGator or 1and1, but there's TONS of them out there.  If you can afford or want a VPS then go with Linode.
