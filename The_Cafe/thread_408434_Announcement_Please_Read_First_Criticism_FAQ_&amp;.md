### Post by John.Michael.Kane on 2007-04-13
For users having trouble with linux. Or feel it's not up to their standards eg: "not ready for the desktop"

Please read this faq:
[**Criticism FAQ Page**]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CriticismFAQ")

After reading should you still feel the need to express your feelings on linux not being ready please feel free to do so,however. It should be noted that these linux is not ready issues have been debated back & forth since December 2004. I have included a link to the thread below. 

[**The Linux Desktop Readiness Thread**]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=113874")

Now you are free to add to this thread or even start a new thread provided the information you wish to post in your new thread is not covered in the one above.  

Should you go the new thread route, and the information or post there after is the same as already posted. you will receive a PM letting you know your thread/post has been merged with the thread above.


For users who feel their experiences with installing/using/setting up ubuntu is different then the issues posted in the desktop readiness thread. You may want post your thoughts in the thread below.

[**Ubuntu Testimonials and Experiences**]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=103") **note: should any thread posted here be found to fit in the desktop readiness thread theme your post or thread in full will be merged into that thread.**
