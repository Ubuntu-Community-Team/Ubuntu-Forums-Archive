### Post by drascus on 2008-07-18
Anyone into Graphics Development or just like those cute pixar movies? Well Big Buck Bunny is out and it is a movie developed with Free and Open source software. It is released under a creative commons license. If you order the DVD they send you the files with the DVD necessary to do total remixes of the movie. They even give you tips (in the form of special features) on how to do this!! WOW very cool. whats more is if you look at the credits for the Software they use right there is good 'ole Ubuntu. so check it out. 

[http://www.bigbuckbunny.org/](http://www.bigbuckbunny.org/)

thanks blender community.
