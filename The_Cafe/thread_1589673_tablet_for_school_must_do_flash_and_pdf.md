### Post by era86 on 2010-10-06
I have an iPad and I am currently in the market for an ultraportable laptop (Thinkpad X-Series hopefully!).  There are things slates just aren't meant to do, and productivity is one of them.  It's great for browsing the web and playing games.  It isn't meant for productivity: word processing, note taking, reading PDFs, etc.  I don't think it was ever meant for that.  

There is no file-browser, so you need to get a dedicated app for PDF reading and to move files over you have to sync it through iTunes.

Trust me, you don't want to use a slate as a productivity tool.  I've tried...
