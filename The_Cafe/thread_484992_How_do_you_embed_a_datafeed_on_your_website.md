### Post by bobbocanfly on 2007-06-26
The embedded RSS Feed on these forums

```
<link rel="alternate" type="application/rss+xml" title="Ubuntu Forums RSS Feed" href="external.php?type=RSS2" />
```

### Post by bobbocanfly on 2007-06-26

