### Post by amitabhishek on 2010-08-04
Buy a system which supports Dolby TrueHD and DTS-HD Master Audio. They don't cost a bomb. Onkyo and Yamaha systems with these config. start at $777 (incl. speakers) which is less than what Apple charges for puny iPhones. This translates into an AV system with 7.1 channel speaker system & HDMI input for sound. 

5.1 systems were meant for DVDs and it doesn't make sense to buy them now.
