### Post by RichardLinx on 2016-01-15
[http://linsux.org/index.php?/announcement/12-linsux-eol///](http://linsux.org/index.php?/announcement/12-linsux-eol///)

A few members that have remained here probably remember when it was a pretty active and somewhat well known site in Linux circles, even having a representative at LinuxCon around ~2009 or so. I'm not going to go into the history too much, but they used to criticize this board a lot, and we would criticize them back. It was a great little "forum war" thinking back on it. But obviously the site stagnated over the last few years and became something like a cheap version of 4chan. Kind of a shame, but it had a good run.

Just thought I'd mention it, since UF has a long history with that site.

To victory, brothers.

Press F to pay respects.
