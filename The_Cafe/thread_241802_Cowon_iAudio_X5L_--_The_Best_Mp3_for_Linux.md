### Post by Luggy on 2006-08-22
I have an iAudio X5 and I absolutely love it.
If you use Linux you really should be looking for a media device that plays ogg or flac.

The only drawback to it is the software that Cowon has to convert the videos for them is only for Windows. There is an application called [iRiverter]("http://iriverter.sourceforge.net/") which can do it in Linux but it's kind of a bitch to install. The good news is that you could just use mencoder to convert it as Cowon gives the specs for how it is converted.
