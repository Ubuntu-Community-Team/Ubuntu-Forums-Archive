### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-12-08
I also find it hard to follow the line of execution to other files when I'm looking at someone else's source code.

When I'm writing something new, it can sometimes take me a long time to learn enough about what I'm writing. One thing we sometimes do is "pseudocode" - writing in a sort of English-like language what the program is doing at each step. This can sometimes help you think about what you need to learn how to do, without it holding the rest of your program to ransom.
