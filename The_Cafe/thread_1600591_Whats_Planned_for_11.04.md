### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2010-10-19
We don't really know yet, but this will all be sorted out at [UDS]("http://uds.ubuntu.com/") next week :)

Blueprints proposed for discussion (via 50-minute sessions spread across five days) are up at [https://launchpad.net/sprints/uds-n](https://launchpad.net/sprints/uds-n)

There are some doubts about Gnome Shell (the big replacement for the panels, notification system and desktop), but Gnome 3 should definitely be there with all its other wonderful changes like a redesigned control center / system preferences interface, Yelp 3 (which actually helps people instead of making problems, and does it *well*), further refined Folks in Empathy (metacontacts), and Gtk3 / GApplication and all the neat things that stuff brings.
