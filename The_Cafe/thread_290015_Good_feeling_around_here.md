### Post by hkgonra on 2006-10-31
I am not sure if this goes in forum feedback, testimonials or here so I picked here. Sorry if I messed up. 

I have been around here a few weeks. 
I am still VERY new to linux overall, although I have used some specialized distros (Ipcop, smoothwall,smeserver , some more than others) for a while. 
It is just strange , but in a good way, the openess around here when it comes to other distros than ubuntu. 
I rememeber the first time I saw someone pointing somebody else to another distro other than ubuntu because it might fit their needs better/easier. I thought " Either a mod will delete this or this guy is about to get blasted." 
Since then I have seen it numerous times. 
I just wanted to acknowledge that I had seen it because I think it is a great attitude. 
It is the kinda thing that really makes people consider this distro to come back to or use for other purposes the next time around when they may have a choice between a few other good ones that would fit their needs as good as ubuntu. 
Keep up the good work. :)
