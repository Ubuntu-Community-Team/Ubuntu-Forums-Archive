### Post by Icehuck on 2009-04-27
It's thin because you don't have enough workspaces to have a cube. You need 4 workspaces to get the cube. Bottom right corner next to the trash can are the workspaces. Just right click on one of them and select preferences. Change the columns from 2 to 4 and you are good.
