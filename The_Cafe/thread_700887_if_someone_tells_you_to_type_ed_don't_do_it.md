### Post by peabody on 2008-02-19
For all the people bashing ed, there was a blind Linux programmer who said it was one of the most useful pieces of software that he had ever worked with because it told him information in a form that was ideal for a blind user.  It is completely line oriented which is essentially the world blind users are stuck in.

To use it

```
ed myfile.txt
i
This is a line of text
and another line of text
I can keep typing until a line with a single period
.
1
i
This line is above line 1.
.
w
q

```

Now less myfile.txt and see how it looks.
