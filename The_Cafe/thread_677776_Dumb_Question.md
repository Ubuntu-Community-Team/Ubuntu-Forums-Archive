### Post by Circus-Killer on 2008-01-25
first off, the video card isn't integrated with the cpu. it's integrated with the motherboard, and uses some of your RAM as its memory.

and for the most part, no it wont use both at the same time. it will use either one or the other. usually, the computer will pick up both, and use the pci over the integrated one. but sometimes (especially on older pc's), you have to specify which card to use in the bios.
