### Post by Frumious Boojum on 2008-01-24
Decent start, so I'll give some suggestion...

Since the target audience is probably the newest user, you may want to start using more screenshots to illustrate the tutorials, even if it's somewhat not needed.   To an absolute beginner, the more graphical a tutorial is, the more likely they'll be to understand that they are doing everything right.

As for the post from the guy on ZDNet Forums, when you do posts like that -- try to throw in your personal commentary, not just his point of view.   Absolute beginners can get confused very easy, so it's possible that people at that stage may mistake the clueless opinion as truth.

You may consider getting somebody to join the blog as a co-blogger so that you can cover a little more territory than if you were to blog alone.   Team blogging can be fun.  :)
