### Post by Mr_Congeniality on 2006-09-19
I was sitting here tonight overgoing osdir.com and came across Knot 3 screenshots for Edgy Eft.  I was impressed with the improvements of the graphical resolution while booting (finally.)

I have a few ideas, however, on what the Ubuntu desktop should really look like.  Even though Ubuntu should be a milestone in processing and development, the graphics of Ubuntu could really use a big boost.  Here's what I think could bring a breathe of fresh air to Ubuntu.  The images will have to be opened in a new window to avoid clutter in the topic.

Before:
[[IMG]http://img227.imageshack.us/img227/6969/theoldqo3.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img227.imageshack.us/my.php?image=theoldqo3.jpg)
After:
[[IMG]http://img215.imageshack.us/img215/6785/thenewij5.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img215.imageshack.us/my.php?image=thenewij5.jpg)

A breath of fresh air.  First off, I'm not sure if the Kernel can isplay Transparentcy like this, but on the "Before", you're notice transparent edges.  If alpha blending cannot be done, I can change it to this instead:

[[IMG]http://img180.imageshack.us/img180/5909/thenew2yb8.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img180.imageshack.us/my.php?image=thenew2yb8.jpg)

It looses a bit of the glass look, but still looks great nonetheless.  I hope something like this can be done.  Add in Compiz, and Ubuntu could easily draw more users in.  Compiz did that for me, even if going through painstaking steps (which weren't THAT hard), it was worth it and the result is beautiful.
 
The background was originally blue, but Ubuntu uses an orange color.  The background was made by me with Swift3D.  If anyone wants the background, let me know.  I'll post the blue and orange versions.

And lastly, the startup screen.  I like how developers finally made a 1024x768 resolution boot up screen.  It's really breathing fresh life into Ubuntu.  But the look is a bit boxy, but looks great nonetheless.  Here's what I think it should look like...

Before:
[[IMG]http://img206.imageshack.us/img206/3379/theoldbootnv8.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img206.imageshack.us/my.php?image=theoldbootnv8.jpg)
After:
[[IMG]http://img205.imageshack.us/img205/6867/thenewbootew4.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img205.imageshack.us/my.php?image=thenewbootew4.jpg)

The new boot image will have to have a color reduction, just a wild guess.  However, the "Glass" Human Circle logo in this one shouldn't have to be used, but stick to a dark background and circle of friends with the ubuntu name logo.

Just some ideas I thought I would throw everyone's way.  What do you think?

Oh yeah, the glass effect for the Human Circle logo took me a crazy amount of time.  I felt going to a 3D modeling program was a dumb idea.
