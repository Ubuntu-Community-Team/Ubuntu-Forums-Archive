### Post by JDShu on 2012-08-24
Not sure if this was posted before on the forums, but I came across this today and I feel like it's something that the community here would be interested it.

Ken Starks is the founder of [reglue.org]("http://reglue.org/about.html") which aims to provide Linux computers to children who would not normally have access to the digital world. He has been diagnosed with a curable form of cancer, but is unable to afford it for various reasons. His friend has launched an initiative to try to raise the funds get him treatment.

I think anything I say would take away from what I consider a very worthy cause, so I urge you to follow the link and perhaps consider donating (I did).
