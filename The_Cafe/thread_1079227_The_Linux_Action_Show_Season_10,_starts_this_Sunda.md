### Post by Nevon on 2009-02-24
For those of you who don't know, the Linux Action Show is a one hour Linux oriented podcast that used to be released on a weekly schedule (if I remember correctly). While it started out as an audio only show, it has now moved on toward becoming more of a video show.

[IMG]http://www.jupiterbroadcasting.com/wp-content/themes/wp-multiflex-5/badges/las227.jpg[/IMG]

After the last season, Bryan and Chris (the show hosts) decided that they were going to stop doing the longer show, and instead break it up into ~10 minute videos, in order to make it easier for people to find content which they were interested in. However, a lot of the old fans have been complaining about this, so now they've agreed to try making a longer show again, and see how the feedback is. 

If you're interested in watching one of the highest quality Linux shows there is, I strongly suggest you head over to [Jupiter Broadcasting]("http://www.jupiterbroadcasting.com/"), where you can find not only the Linux Action Show, but also all the other shows from the guys at Jupiter Broadcasting.
