### Post by jespdj on 2008-03-17
I have a white Logitech V470 with my laptop and it's very nice. To get Ubuntu to find the mouse for the first time, I had to do
```
sudo hidd --search
```
That was only necessary the very first time I used it, after that it finds the mouse automatically.
