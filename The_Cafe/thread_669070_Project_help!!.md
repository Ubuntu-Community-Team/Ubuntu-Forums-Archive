> **Hello Kitty Will Kill You said:**

1. What is your name?
Tommy Brunn

2. How old are you?
17

3. What are your hobbies?
Why do you think I'm on a Linux forum...? 

4. What music do you like?
I'm not all that interested in music, really. I do have a relatively small collection of mostly indie rock, but that's it.

5. What do you think of when you hear the word street art?
Well, here in Sweden we have a lot of pissy "street art". You know, pen tags and stuff like that. However, I tend to separate those things from real street art. To me real street art can be really really cool, and adds to the feeling of a city.

6. What do you think of street art? An art form or vandalism?
Real street art is a real art form. As long as they don't do it on other people's property, I'm all for it. That goes for both private property as well as public buildings.

7. Do you ever stop at street art, and take a good look at it?
Sure. There are a few very cool murals here in the city where I live, and I often stop to look at them whenever I'm passing by.

8. Do you think that street artist have a message behind their art?
Some do, some don't. Simple as that.

9. If so, does this message come across?
I think most people don't bother to even look for the message, and that's a shame. I suppose the massive amounts of crap that is being painted everywhere has made people blind to the really good ones.

10. Do you know any street artists?
I know a few, although I would be reluctant to call them real street artists. I think some of them might get there some day, but for now they're stuck in the "I-want-to-write-my-name-here"-phase.

11. If so, do they have a message behind their art? Or do they just do it for fun?
Some of them have political messages behind their "art", but most of them do it just for fun.

12. Have you ever made any form of street art, or would ou like to?
Nope. I respect the fact that I shouldn't vandalize other people's property, and around here there aren't a lot of spots that you could practice on.

13. If so, did/would you want to say something with that?



14. Is there anything else you would like to say about street art?
