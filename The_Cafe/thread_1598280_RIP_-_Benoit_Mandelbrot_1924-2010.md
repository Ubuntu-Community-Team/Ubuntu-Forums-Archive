### Post by ibuclaw on 2010-10-16
Nothing in the news media yet, but many folks on Twitter and colleague [Nassim Taleb]("http://www.fooledbyrandomness.com/") are reporting that Benoit Mandelbrot has died at age 85.

I think we should take this time to share a few thoughts and condolences in respect of the [father of fractal geometry]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benoit_Mandelbrot"). We're not there yet, but someday Mandelbrot's name will be mentioned in the same breath as Einstein's as a genius who fundamentally shifted our perception of how the world works.
