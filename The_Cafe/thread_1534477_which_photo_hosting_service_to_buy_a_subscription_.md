### Post by samalex on 2010-07-19
I pay for Flickr which has always been good and easy to use, or Google Picasa is free.  If you're looking for something with more professional services Smugmug is good.  I haven't used them as a photographer, but several local photographers we've used around town use Smugmug and it's great from a customer standpoint.

You can also get your own web host and run something like [Gallery]("http://gallery.menalto.com/") which gives you much more flexibility to do what you want, but that may be overkill.
