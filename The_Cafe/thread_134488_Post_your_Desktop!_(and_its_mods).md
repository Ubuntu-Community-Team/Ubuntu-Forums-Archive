Hello Ubuntu community! Do you want to show off your nicely modded Ubuntu Distro? Or are you tired of seeing other peoples pimped out desktops and want to know how they got it that way? Well this is the thread for that.

Rules are:
1) Feel free to post your post a screenshot of your desktop (optionally with a few windows open) that showcases your customizations
2) Post the modifications that are actually visible in your picture and optionally, where to get these modifications from.
3) Optionally post a description of what the mods do, and what your experience is with them.
4) Try to keep the filesize of any images you post directly on the forums to a reasonable level (ie: under 120k if possible)
