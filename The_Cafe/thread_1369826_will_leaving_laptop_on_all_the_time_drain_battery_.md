### Post by aysiu on 2010-01-01
It wouldn't drain your battery any, but keeping the battery at 100% all the time does it no favors. Lithium ion operates best when it is regularly charged and discharged (but not completely).

If you intend to leave your netbook on 24/7, first drain your battery to about 70% and then remove it and place it in a cool and **dry** place. You can then leave your netbook plugged in without the battery.
