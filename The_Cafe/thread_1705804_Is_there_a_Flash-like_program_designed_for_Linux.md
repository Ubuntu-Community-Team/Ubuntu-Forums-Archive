### Post by 3Miro on 2011-03-12
Closed source, not very transparent standard. Adobe doesn't seem interested in their product working well under Linux. We are all hoping for HTML5 that is open standard and should be able to replace all the functionality of Flash (and then some). Unfortunately, HTML5 will take some time to spread to all useful web-pages.

Try the flashvideoreplacer plugin for Firefox 
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flashvideoreplacer/]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flashvideoreplacer/")
