### Post by Phrea on 2011-05-09
> **TeoBigusGeekus said:**
> Root partition. 
The difference in speed with a sata one is overrated...

There is a noticeable difference.
But the real problem here is it's size. Small hdd's are *a lot* slower, due to the fact that the bits are spread out more, and of course older technology.

One fun thing to do with it is to open up the top, connect it to your computer, and read/write to the disk. Fun to see !  
It won't last very long, a few minutes to maybe half an hour or so, but it's mesmerizing to look at.  
After doing that, remove the magnets, they're rare earth, so very strong, and remove the platters, they make nifty little mirrors. You can also make a platter into a clock or so,
