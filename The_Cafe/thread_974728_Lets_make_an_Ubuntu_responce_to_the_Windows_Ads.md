### Post by Mardoct909 on 2008-11-12
I really think advertising Ubuntu isn't a good idea. Perhaps, just perhaps, it would be a good idea for the entire GNU project to have a blanket ad for Open-source software and such.

An ad specifically for Ubuntu would not be very fair considering that most of the OS wasn't generated by the Ubuntu project, and also that Ubuntu is hardly the only distribution that's given back to the Linux kernel and other Open-source initiatives.

Really, though, ad campaigns are costly and time-consuming, and us mere mortals lack the resources to orchestrate a good one. Mark Shuttleworth/Canonical are really the ones with the ability to pull it off; and despite being influenced by the community, they still need to look out for their assets. Not waste a great sum of cash on minimally effective ads.
