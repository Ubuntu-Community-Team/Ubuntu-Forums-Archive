### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-07-06
Launchpad will be your friend:

[https://launchpad.net/](https://launchpad.net/)

There is a ton of information out there about what you can do to get involved with Ubuntu, Linux and open-source generally so just start digging. Perhaps a search on:

> ubuntu contribute

... or:

> ubuntu testing

Get involved with the next release, 13.10, which is a baby and not released until October. Install a stable LTS and leave two or three partitions spare for testing. The avenues are up to you ... good luck. ;)
