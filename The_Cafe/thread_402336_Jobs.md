### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-04-06
Just think... 80hrs a week or more for that meager quarterly $150 bonus and free lunch with the boss.  It's a cut throat business.  Find your enemies and know their weakenesses.  Build your time with your customers and set your goal for follow ups regarding sales satisfaction.  Use your enemies as tools to get things done.  Don't be the one who rides on the curtails, instead make the difference and climb on the heads of others.  Also, know your key hours for flux time.
