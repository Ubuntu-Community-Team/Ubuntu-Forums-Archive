### Post by speedwell68 on 2009-09-29
I work at our local park and ride service, Park for Truro.

[http://www.parkfortruro.org.uk/](http://www.parkfortruro.org.uk/)

We use realtime information screens to inform passengers of the arrival time of the next bus.  The realtime information system uses a bespoke distro.  The system uses SD cards to boot each screen.  The distro used to create the bootable SD cards is Ubuntu.  I only discovered all of this today as we had an engineer in to fix a few minor issues and I saw him booting Ubuntu on his laptop.  He didn't have time talk me all through it this morning, but next month their is a full round of upgrades so he is going to take me out to show me what is what.  The company that provide this systm is called ACIS...

[http://www.acis.uk.com](http://www.acis.uk.com)
