### Post by tribaal on 2008-06-08
I have been carrying my 17 inches laptop around for a couple of years.

It is totally true - the weight makes a huge difference when it's on your back all the time.
Time has come for me to replace it with something new, and I'm getting a 13 inches.

I suggest you aim for a 15 inches or 13 inches instead if you plan on moving it around all the time.
17 inches is only worth it if your laptop is more of a transportable "desktop replacement" for you (i.e. it mostly stays in one place).

Hope this helps :)
