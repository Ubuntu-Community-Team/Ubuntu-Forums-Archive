### Post by oedipuss on 2010-05-04
There are handles for resizing the textbox, they're those empty rectangles and bars that appear in its corners or sides. They should highlight if you move the mouse inside one. 
The textbox must be selected though, maybe that's why they don't show up. Just click on your text with the text tool (not the selection or move tools) as if for editing. The handles should appear then.
