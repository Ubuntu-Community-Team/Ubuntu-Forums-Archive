### Post by Hwæt on 2011-05-30
When I originally installed my power supply unit, I bricked the onboard graphics card in my motherboard and as a consequence, the VGA ports quit operating. To get around this I had to install a new graphics card and stick a DVI to VGA converter into the graphics card's DVI port so that I could still plug my VGA cord into it.

I've also been known to get around my netbook's lack of screen real estate and still use its camera by plugging it into my 1680x1050 monitor.
