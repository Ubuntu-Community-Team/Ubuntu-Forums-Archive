### Post by earthpigg on 2011-03-10
> **leeshaub said:**
> not going to make one just wanted to know how much a good one cost.

20-40% less than a similarly specced pre-assembled machine from Dell or HP.

So, if you consider a $1400 Dell to be a 'good one', then a self-assembled computer would cost $800-$1100 - depending on how much hunting for sales and whatnot you are willing to do.

In the area of personal time investment, self-assembled computers are far more expensive. You must verify for yourself that parts are compatible, etc.

If time spent doing that is time that would otherwise have been spent playing video games, then it is probably worth it.

If time spent doing that is time that would otherwise have been spent bonding with one's children or something else of vital importance, then it may not be worth it.
