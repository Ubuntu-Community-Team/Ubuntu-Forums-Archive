### Post by nvteighen on 2007-10-11
My aim is to make a license intended to be used not only for software that includes protection against patent-agreements and also other possible legal agreements that might appear in future; this is made forcing people redistributing a work to ceade any legal right that interferres with this license's freedom and to give any legal documentation necessary to do it (but with one exception: rights derived from registered trademarks). Maybe this last "preventive" idea is its best feature.

But also, this is a license not really meant exclusively in software, actually, but also in other kinds of works. The original idea was to simplify GFDL using GPL concepts, but it derived into this.

Anyway, thanks for your opinion!
