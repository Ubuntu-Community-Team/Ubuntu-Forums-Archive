### Post by acl123 on 2008-05-16
I've seen quite a few people buy Acers (and similar brands), and they usually end up replacing them faster than people with 'better' brands (Asus, Sony, Dell). They usually go fine for 2-3 years before the lower specs and build quality bring you to replacing it. Personally I think that's a bit of a waste and you're better off saving longer and going with something better quality. Laptops are cheap nowadays and if you save your pennies a little longer you'll be able to get a nicer one that will last for 5 years+.
