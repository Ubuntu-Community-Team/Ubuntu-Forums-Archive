### Post by troymcdavis on 2006-12-07
I believe you're referring to "[Wii have a Problem]("http://www.wiihaveaproblem.com/")".

However, you're only going to have problems if you have extremely sweaty hands (in which case, you should know this and take appropriate caution) or you are the most idiotic of idiots. The pictures of broken walls, televisions, and windows do not justify avoiding the Wii. Those are all the result of user error.
