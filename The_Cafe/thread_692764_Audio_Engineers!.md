### Post by thepocketdrummer on 2008-02-10
I, like many others, use a home studio to record my band's records. Unfortunately, those of us who would like to use Linux to do such work are unable to do so due to hardware support issues.

I currently use a Mackie Onyx Satellite to record. Apparently, some manufacturers are less helpful than others (namely Mackie). The only way to encourage them to help is to show a demand for their hardware in linux.

So, if you are an audiophile or audio engineer or just want to help Linux become a better option for all... Urge Mackie, and other resistant companies, to support linux.
