### Post by Sporkman on 2011-03-28
A [good poem]("http://www.scientificamerican.com/blog/post.cfm?id=poetic-masterpiece-of-claude-shanno-2011-03-28") for your leisurely reading.

Here's a small excerpt:

> Rude folks might switch two tabs on thee,
The most unkindest switch of all,
Into insolubility.

In-sol-u-bility.
The cruelest place to be.
However you persist
Solutions don't exist.
