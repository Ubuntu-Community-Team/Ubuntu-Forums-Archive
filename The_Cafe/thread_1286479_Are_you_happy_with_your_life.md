### Post by LookTJ on 2009-10-09
Well, let's see

I have cerebral palsy, hard of hearing. I probably would have never learned sound if that one doctor didn't convince my parents to challenge the insurance to pay for my aural therapy.

In the first two and half years of high school, I was just so angry at the world for the body I've been given. but what does anger accomplish?  I just walked away from anger and depression.  6 surgeries is a lot and yes it is hard to recover my ability to walk but I had the determination and faith. My life isn't over yet, I'm a 18 year teen with a purpose to show the world that anything is possible no matter what limitations your body and mind give you.

So yes I am happy despite my life's circumstances

I know that I will probably get an infraction for this, but it's worth it. I believe it was God's will and purpose to put me on this earth with the gift and body I've been given. I also believe it was him who cleared my mind of the anger and stress I've been through the first 16-17 years of my life. It's not easy, but I focus on the positive things in life.
