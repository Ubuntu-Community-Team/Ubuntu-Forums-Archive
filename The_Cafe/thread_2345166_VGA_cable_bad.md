### Post by mcduck on 2016-12-02
Could be defective, but could just be slightly less well protected against outside interference. VGA is analog format after all, and especially on longer cables interference can become an issue. If the cable doesn't have any [ferrite beads]("https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ferrite_bead"), or only has one on one end of the cable, then adding one might help.
