### Post by IYY on 2006-12-04
I don't use a comic strip viewer. I just have a Webcomics folder in my Firefox bookmark toolbar, where the URLs or my favourite webcomics are. Each day I middle click on this folder, and they all open up in tabs for me to read.

This way, I actually visit the original site, and click on the ads to support the artists.
