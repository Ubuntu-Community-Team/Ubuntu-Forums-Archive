I don't know how many of you are familiar with the music streaming service, Spotify - [http://www.spotify.com](http://www.spotify.com), [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spotify](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spotify)

The service is a nice, fully legal way to listen to a huge selection of music, both mainstream and obscure.  In the UK the service costs 99p for a 24-hour pass or £9.99 for a monthly subscription (I'm not sure of the overseas prices).  There is also a free ad-supported version, but at the moment only those with invites can use the free service.

Unfortunately they don't yet have a Linux client, but the service is still in Beta so who knows what'll happen in the future.  For now, the client runs pretty well in Wine.
