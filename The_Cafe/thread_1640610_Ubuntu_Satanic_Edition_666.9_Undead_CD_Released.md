### Post by parker13 on 2010-12-08
Ubuntu Satanic Edition 666.9, codenamed Microsoft Massacre, is released as an Undead CD.

Based on Ubuntu 10.10, it has all the features of the standard distro, plus an even darker theme, selection of devilish wallpapers including some based on photographs by renowned artist Klemens (graemo), icons by Paul "Mattahan" Davey and an exclusive ray-traced screensaver.

Most importantly, it continues to promote freedom in music by including an EP of the heaviest and best metal music from around the world, all creative commons licensed. Tracks from up and coming bands Theorized from India, TANK and Lost World Order from Europe and thrashers Deathalizer from New York.

Run from the Undead CD or install over the top of Ubuntu Christian Edition. Most of all have fun and rock!
