### Post by az on 2008-02-27
The use of the word ubuntu in a domain name is covered in the Ubuntu Trademark policy:
[http://www.ubuntu.com/aboutus/trademarkpolicy](http://www.ubuntu.com/aboutus/trademarkpolicy)

Basically, you will need permission to continue to use that name anyway.  Most probably, you will eventually be asked to change it to a .org domain name if it for Ubuntu advocacy or related topics.
