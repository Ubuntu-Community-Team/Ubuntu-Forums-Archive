### Post by Cheesehead on 2012-06-07
Quite a bit of discussion and work happened in 2011 on this.
See [https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/foundations-o-debdelta](https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/foundations-o-debdelta) for the most recent Ubuntu effort.

It seems stalled a bit in 2012, as the developers involved have other tasks this cycle. However, the blueprint is still very good. If any community members want to pick up the dropped ball and run with it, everyone will benefit.
