### Post by imagine on 2006-05-14
Hmm, can't help you, but wouldn't it be easier to use a multi-protocol client and feed your MSN account data to your client instead to a jabber server?
And with a real jabber client it should be possible to use the transports feature of a server without having an account there.

The problem with MSN is, that Micros~1 doesn't allow any third party products to connect to their .NET Messenger Service. If you do that at home for yourself it's your own risk, but administrators of jabber servers usually don't want to risk a lawsuit because of that.
