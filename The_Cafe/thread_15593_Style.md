Yeah, if they are going to use the system there should definitely be a big bolded sticky FAQ/HOWTO for it at the top of every section.  I'm sure they'll have some explanation of it but  the feature *just* got activated it seems. I've been in a few other forums with this exact reputation system so here's a really brief explanation of it;

The reputation system is designed to be a marker of how helpful a person is. If someone helps you out you should give click the add reputation button under their name, eventually when enough people give some reputation they get another green dot.  More dots (more rep)  = "this person knows their stuff"...in theory. A lot of times people don't know what it is or how to use it so good posts go unrewarded and I've seen it degenerate into a popularity contest as well (people with more green dots are "cooler" than those without, childish stuff like that).  I'm not sure if it's active in this forum, but typically you can give negative rep points for trolling and generally unhelpful comments (like "rtfm n00b!!").  When a user gets enough negative points their green dot turns red.  Also if someone with a lot of green dots gives you a rep and you have only one,  you will get more rep points than if someone with one dot gave you rep, this is called "reputation power", esentially the more rep you have, the more your rep is worth.

As far as the distinguished road thing, it's just your status kind of,  if someone has tons and tons they are "A sight to behold"  and things like that.

There will no doubt be an official faq soon, but I hope I helped you understand it a little bit rather than confuse you even more :)
