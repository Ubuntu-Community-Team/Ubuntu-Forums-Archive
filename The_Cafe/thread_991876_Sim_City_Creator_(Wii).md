### Post by Dave.YNA on 2008-11-24
Its a great game. The control system is a bit rubbish to begin with (Which is shocking considering how easy it should have been on the wii) But once you get used to it, the general game is pretty good.

It has nice graphics and you can build curvy roads which really make the citys look more realistic and feel more unique. There are also nice seasonal effects like snow and night time views of the city. 

There are some extra additions like flight missions that feel a bit cheap and tacked on to the game. On the other hand there is also something called hero buildings which are historic buildings (Like Stone Henge) that shape the look of your city

I did not get to play much of sim city 4 so i dont know how it ompares to that but if all you have played it sim city 3 and below, I would recommend it!
