### Post by Hwæt on 2009-12-15
Why is it that the Romance and Germanic languages' words for "welcome" translate literally to Well + Come?

Germanic:

English: Welcome ("Well" + "Come")
High German: Willkommen
Dutch: Welkom
Danish: Velkommen


Romance:

Spanish: Bienvenido (*Bien* "good" + *venido* past part. of *venir* "to come")
French: Bienvenue
Italian: Benvenuto
