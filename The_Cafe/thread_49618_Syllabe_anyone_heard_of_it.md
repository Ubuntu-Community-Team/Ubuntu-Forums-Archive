### Post by HungSquirrel on 2005-07-17
Tried it.  Took [several days of tech support](http://www.syllable.org/discussion.php?id=837) on the [forum](http://www.syllable.org/forum.php) to even get the live CD to boot for me.  It's still in the very early beta stages, but if it works for you, give it a shot I guess.  It has a surprisingly good graphical interface and shell, and the graphics server (whatever it is) handles my monitor better than Windows does.  Just tell it the res and refresh rate you want, and if your monitor can handle it, it's done.  :)
