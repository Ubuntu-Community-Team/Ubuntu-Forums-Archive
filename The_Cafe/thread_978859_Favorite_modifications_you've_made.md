Heh, i though it would be another kind of modifications, so here goes. I like to modify some programs by changing the source code. For example, [pdf viewer evince (screenshot)]("http://vesso.marv.lafka.net/screens/screens24/mod_evince.png") and [archiver file-roller (screenshot)]("http://vesso.marv.lafka.net/screens/screens24/mod_fileroller.png") doesn't have menubars, and have some minor interface tweaks. In pidgin I removed the menubar and created a toolbar with buttons for my most used actions, including showing the menubar if I need it.
Some other programs I have tweaked a little are leafpad, gtodo, liferea and the murrina and aurora theme engines.
The latest and greatest modification I made is adding two finger scroll into the synaptics driver for my touchpad.
