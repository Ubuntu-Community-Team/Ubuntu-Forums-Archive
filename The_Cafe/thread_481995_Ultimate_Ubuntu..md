### Post by Garyu on 2007-06-23
While I would welcome any initiative like this... I would think you need to put a little more effort into it.

If you want to attract attention to your site, you should probably start the site with a lot of ready material. Like the boys at doc.gwos.org who has a lot of nice material. Once you got a good start like that, if you then also make it possible for people to add their own stuff... You got a good thing going. But you've got a long way to go before that. Also, remember to keep your guides up to date. Or start off now by categorizing guides by Ubuntu-versions (one section for feisty guides, one for edgy, dapper, gutsy, and so on). But most of all, you need to do a lot of work yourself by writing content. That will not happen by itself. This site is such a good resource, you're up against some heavy competition. ;-)
