### Post by frup on 2008-05-18
Here's what I did with mine:
[IMG]http://i21.photobucket.com/albums/b265/frup/100_1023.jpg[/IMG]

[IMG]http://i21.photobucket.com/albums/b265/frup/100_1024.jpg[/IMG]

First I scratched the ink off with a pocket knife. I did this quite carefully so the key had no scratches and was smooth. I then used a fine permanent marker, the type that works on CD's and finally sprayed it with fixative (hair spray would work) I let it dry for 30 mins.

Note, I pulled the key out to spray it.

With a black keyboard (like the one I am typing on now) I suppose twink or something like that would work but this one I just scratched it off and have a blank key.
