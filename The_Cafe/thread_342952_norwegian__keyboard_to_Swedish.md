### Post by mostwanted on 2007-01-20
Well, I just checked the layouts of both the Swedish and the Norwegian keyboards. The only major difference I can find is that æ is replaced by ä and ø by ö, so just change your keyboard layout from Norwegian to Swedish in System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Keyboard and you're set.

The two layouts:

no: [http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/KB_Norway.svg/540px-KB_Norway.svg.png](http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/KB_Norway.svg/540px-KB_Norway.svg.png)
se: [http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e0/KB_Sweden.svg/540px-KB_Sweden.svg.png](http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e0/KB_Sweden.svg/540px-KB_Sweden.svg.png)

I also compared the Norwegian layout to the Danish one and found that the æ and the ø had switched places. What a stupid design decision not to keep coherence when we're using the same alphabet...
