If you are one, you'll know what the word "conlanger" means. :P If your not, well, it's a noun portmanteau of the verb "construct" and the noun "language".  I've currently got one conlang in the works that I haven't worked on in a while.  You can find it [here]("http://conlang.wikia.com/wiki/Cinoktan").  I originally created it for a fictional alien race I was working on.  After I get some of my other projects worked on, I might get back to Cinoktan.  If I don't get back to Cinoktan, I might go ahead and make an artificial language meant to be used by humans; an auxiliary language, like Esperanto or Volapük, but it'll make more sense. :)

Soo...  Does anyone else on the Ubuntu Forums make up languages (complete with grammar, syntax, alphabet, and vocabulary) for fun?
