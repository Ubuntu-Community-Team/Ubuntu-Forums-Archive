### Post by Skripka on 2009-04-03
Well for Alpha software is QUITE good.


The Skripka Verdict?

Not So Fast.


Pros: Adblocking via Proxy is great and works like it should.  Flash works fine.  Speed at Google V8 benchmark is just as fast as Arora.  Takes native Qt widget theme fine-something Arora 0.6 cannot do until KDE4.3 comes out.  AND-Stop/Refresh buttons are ALL ONE ICON!!!! Something Konqueror still doesn't do.

Cons:  No restoring tabs after app crash...no session management at all-same flaws here as Konqueror.  *_**That is a biggie right there for me**_*.  Bookmark import/export is there-but buried.


A great start-if it had session management available-even restoring tabs after crash-I'd use it.
