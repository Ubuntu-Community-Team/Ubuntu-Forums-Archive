it's not that big a deal, but here's an example:

(nouns are subject)
jackie and i went to the movie.
[COLOR=Silver]jackie and[/COLOR] i went to the movie.

(nouns are object)
jackie watched the movie with me and bob.
jackie watched the movie with me[COLOR=Silver] and bob[/COLOR].

if the nouns are the subject, then the first person usually comes after the third person. if the nouns are the object, the first person usually comes before the third person. generally speaking, the third person noun should be at the start or the end of the sentence.
