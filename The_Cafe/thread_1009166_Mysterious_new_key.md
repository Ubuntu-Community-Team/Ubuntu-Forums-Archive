### Post by super breadfish on 2008-12-12
It's a standard keyboard button. Depending on keyboard layout the button left of the 1 will type:

Grave Accent **`**
Vertical Bar (hold Alt Gr) **|**
Broken Vertical Bar (hold Alt Gr) **¦**
Negation **¬**
Tilde **~**

It's not a well used key so it often gets moved or omitted. A lot of non-English keyboard layout replace it with a backslash key, or dead key for accents (eg German keyboards).

### Post by steeleyuk on 2008-12-12
[Negation]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C2%AC")

If and where you have this key depends on what type of keyboard you have, region etc.

UK keyboards, or mine at least have three actions with this key.

Pressing it produces a `
Shift + press produces a ¬
Alt GR + press produces a |
