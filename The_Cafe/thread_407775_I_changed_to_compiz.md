### Post by forrestcupp on 2007-04-12
I originally tried running nvidia-glx with aiglx and beryl.  I love beryl and all of it's features.  But everytime my boy played supertux, tuxracer, or childsplay my computer would lock up, and often I would have to do a hard shutdown and restart.
