The WRT54G Routers did use Linux Firmware...new version 5.0 uses a different firmware and less memory making them more difficult to flash and the inability to have as many features due to the lack of memory.  

Linksys released the WRT54GL (L stands for LINUX) so that people could continue to flash their routers with third-party firmware.  

I just took two WRT54GL's and sent them to a friend of mine with upgraded firmware.
