### Post by kspringer on 2008-07-20
Remember it well.  One afternoon, several years ago, after being informed I needed to upgrade my anti-virus, I attempted to upgrade my anti-virus. I was informed I had an illegal copy of Windows on my computer and I couldn't install the anti-virus.  I had a legal copy of Windows.  I called the toll-free number.

After a few hours on the phone, and being made to feel like someone who pirated software (because they couldn't fix the problem), and WITHOUT resolution, I ended up fixing the problem via an internet connection with Microsoft. I still don't know or understand what happened.

I threw up my hands, googled "linux", and discovered there were 200+ flavors of linux.  Took a test to see which one I'd like. Ubuntu was listed. I'd never HEARD of Ubuntu!

Went to the site and from there to the forums. The "Breezy" days. Saw immediately the value of this community.

I'm still here.

And I know it's a lengthy post for such a topic but it really is fresh in my mind -- a life changing experience.
