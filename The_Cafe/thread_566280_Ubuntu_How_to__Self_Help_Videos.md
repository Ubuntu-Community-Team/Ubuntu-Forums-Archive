I work for a company that promotes the use of Open Source and specifically the Ubuntu operating system. As one of our projects we made 20 English and 20 Dutch videos showing how to do some of the basic stuff like changing your homepage in firefox or adding a local printer. 

We want to share these videos with the Ubuntu community, and hope that new users and less computer savvy people will benefit from them. Any Feedback would be appreciated. I also hope this is the right place to share this.
