### Post by az on 2008-08-07
One resource is the software freedom law center.
[http://www.softwarefreedom.org/](http://www.softwarefreedom.org/)

By and large, legal action taken against GPL infringement seek compliance instead of damages.    Eben Moglen says on his website that he alone was able to handle all the FSF GPL infringement cases for ten years without any help.  Since the license is pretty straightforward, the lawsuits were simple to handle.
