### Post by oldfred on 2017-04-21
Are you really ripping that many CD when actually not at home?

I ripped my entire CD library using my desktop at home. (ok its only 10 CD & wife maybe had another 15). :)

I also am anti-laptop.
I realize some users need a portable system.

But laptops are not ergonomic. Screen is too small and low, keyboard is too high when on desk. And not as powerful as desktop, no matter marketing that says as powerful as desktop (optimized for low power consumption, not performance). 
Laptops really on work on lap, and then too warm except winter time.

But for your budget you can build a high end desktop with whatever options you want. And still have a good laptop, if needed to travel with.
