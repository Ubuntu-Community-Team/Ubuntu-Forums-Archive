### Post by mikodo on 2015-11-02
Background.

I'm 62. Have been sick for the last couple of years, with a wife who has faced 6 major surgeries in the last 6 years. Life has been and is still a challenge. I took some time off work, (I'm lucky I could do that). Time to prepare for going back to work.

I spent a lot of time (~ 7 months) cleaning up my poorly managed data. Why, I never thought of using folders in the past to categorize topics is, beyond me. I have never been as organized as I am now. Instead of just throwing everything in Documents and, hoping I can find it in the future, was nuts. I purchased and installed some new hardware. Made plans for my future Linux use, (I have an exciting folder called "New Projects") that, is going to be fun as I get to them. Have better backups, and plans for better ways to keep my stuff safe. 

I have really enjoyed reading Linux topics, while having no real pressing concerns telling me, I should be doing this or that instead. I guess, I gave myself a taste of what retirement might be like. Though, that is not in the foreseeable future. I'm rushing towards my second divorce and ex-wives cost money. That ought to keep me working for a while. Luckily, I come from a heritage where most have worked well into their 70's. I don't see myself doing any differently. Never really have.

When I look back to what my computer was like 6 months ago, and see how things are organized now, it gives me great pleasure. Clean, functional, organized, plans for future use well documented, with nothing that I can say is not well in its' place. It was time well spent. Something that will only continue to give me pleasure.

Now, for you folks. What can I say that doesn't sound like I am over-exaggerating. You have been a God-send as I faced some challenges. Reading and following your advice, has been a source of great solace. I mustn’t forget to make mention of how grateful I am for all the help offered. Your advice, work, altruistic leanings are evidenced every time, I turn on my computer. That can't be over-stated and will be witnessed daily as, I move on to what is next for myself.

I know, I will never forget this time. 

Not a good-bye post, just time to say something before, I move on to other things.

My best, to all of you.
