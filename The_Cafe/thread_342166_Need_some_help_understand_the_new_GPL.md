### Post by migla on 2007-01-19
One of the main points of gpl3 would be *internationalization,* meaning that the wording is looked over and adjusted to make sure that it says exactly the same thing in different parts of the world and that also other countries', apart from the US, copyright laws are taken into account. Internationalization is one of the non-controversial issues of gpl3, though.

[Floss Weekly]("http://www.twit.tv/FLOSS") podcast ep. 13 has an interview with Eben Moglen, General Counsel of the FSF.
