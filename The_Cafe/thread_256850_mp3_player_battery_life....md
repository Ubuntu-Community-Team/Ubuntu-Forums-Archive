### Post by jdong on 2006-09-13
Well, cheap batteries are gonna get you significantly shorter battery life than brand name batteries. You'd also be pleasantly surprised by the premium-grade brand name batteries, but they are expensive.

If your MP3 player has a backlight setting, make sure it's turned down to the lowest timeout, and don't keep switching songs back and forth when you don't need to, as that chews up more battery life.

My cheap mp3 players definitely get much more than 3 hours.... Maybe it's 9 hours, maybe more, but I've never really kept track.
