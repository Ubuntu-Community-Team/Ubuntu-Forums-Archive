### Post by PySky on 2010-03-03
I made program for Twitter account managing (HisTwi) and use it some time. But my opinion is biased. May need to add new features or change something? 

It my first open source program for Ubuntu, and I hope community help me improve it.

Now it allows:

[LIST]
[*]Download and save all your Tweets to Database.
[*]View and export all saved tweets to html or txt file.
[*]Compare your Following to Followers.
[*]Follow/Unfollow any your friends.
[*]Tracking new followers and unfollowers.
[*]Advanced Twitter Search.
[*]Managing multiple Twitter accounts.
[/LIST]

Ubuntu package (38 KB) are here - [http://originalcoding.com/histwi/]("http://originalcoding.com/histwi/")
