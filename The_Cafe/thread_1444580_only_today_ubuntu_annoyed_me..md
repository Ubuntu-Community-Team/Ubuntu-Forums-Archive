Your file migration speed is limited more by the USB version than anything else. No OS is going to be able to overcome the speed limitations of USB1.1 when compared to USB 2.0 or USB 3.0.

That said, 40 minutes does seem like a very long time.  I suspect your USB port(s) are v1.0 or V1.1 -- which are really, really slow.
