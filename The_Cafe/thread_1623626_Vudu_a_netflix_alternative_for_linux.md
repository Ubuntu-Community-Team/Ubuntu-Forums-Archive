### Post by forrestcupp on 2010-11-30
It's not a Netflix alternative, either.  You pay for each thing you watch.  A newer movie in Blu ray quality averages about $5.99 for one movie for 24 hours.  For $7.99, Netflix gives you unlimited streaming to everything they have available for a month.

That's not to say Vudu isn't awesome, though.  My blu ray player has an app for it, and we got a $10 credit.  We watched The Green Zone in blu ray quality, and I was amazed.  I can't believe they can stream that kind of quality without any hiccups, and it didn't even have to buffer.
