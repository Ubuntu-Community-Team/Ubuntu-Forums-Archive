### Post by 7G Operator on 2010-05-31
I highly recommend pSX as an emulator, as it faithfully recreates the playstation, without further enhancing it. It recreates it so faithfully that when playing games it will sound underrun a lot, this i finally diagnosed as being due to the fact that you need to run it at 480x640 res as this is the native playstation res. Other steps can also be used such as changing to an RT-kernel (not yet to my knowledge available for 10.04 though) or disabling pulseaudio, although to be fair the effects are negligable compared to simply changing screen res. Also for controllers, im using the original playsation controller, and playing via a playsation to usb convertor. Portable playtation netbooking man, its the way ;). Playing wipeout 3 at the mo! Hope this helps peoples. ;)
