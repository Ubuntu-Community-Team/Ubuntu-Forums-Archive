### Post by Lord Xeb on 2008-06-05
Here are a few tips to get more help:

* Do not panic
* Make the title a rough description of your problem
* Do NOT make it seem like your situation is so dire that it will end your life
* Give us time. If no one posts, it simply means we do not know so give some time for someone to post.
* Remeber KISS (Keep It Simple Stupid). Do not make you posts overly complicated. Give us as much info as you can while making it easy on yourself.



Thanks!

### Post by drs305 on 2008-06-05
And please mark your thread SOLVED if you have resolved your problem. You do this by clicking on "Thread Tools" at the top right of your original post and then selecting "Mark this thread as solved". Marking it solved allows those looking to help concentrate on unresolved issues.

Thank you.
