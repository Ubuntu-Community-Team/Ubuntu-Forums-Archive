### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-03-31
Ebay may be your best bet, perhaps only hope, of finding a laptop that cheap. It will be fairly old and out of date, meaning Ubuntu Studio may not preform as well on it. Watch pawn shops, and auctions in your area for computer stuff, though a desktop would probably be easier to come by at that price range.

Closest you could get new would likely be the Asus EEE. There is a model that is cheaper than the others, though it only has 2 gig's of space. (you could buy a CF card for the /home directory, but even then two gigs for root is pretty small.)
