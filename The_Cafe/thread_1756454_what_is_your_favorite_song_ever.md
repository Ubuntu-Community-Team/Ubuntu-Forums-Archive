### Post by linuxforartists on 2011-05-16
I think I was in junior high school when I first heard what became my favorite song.  Pure energy, made me want to bust out moves I didn't realize I could even do.  Totally cured me of shyness and got me on my feet.

It was played over a random MTV commercial with video clips of whatever pop stars were popular that year.  Forgot the commercial, but never forgot that song.  

Only years later at a university dance did I hear that song again.  I frantically asked every friend around me, "What song is this?!"  Finally one guy answered, "Oh, that's 'Renegade Master.'" ("Renegade Master '98" by Wildchild, Fatboy Slim's Old Skool remix)

What made it sweeter was this was one of the top YouTube results for searching that song:

[Various Anime Music Video - Renegade Master]("http://youtu.be/5mlw32jNrnY")

Sadly, although I've done a lot of clubbing around the world, I've only heard it played in a dance club exactly once.  In Taipei, Taiwan at Club W (a.k.a Club Wax).  I was so full-force into dancing to "Renegade Master" that people cleared space around me on the floor and cheered me on.  Easily one of my Top 5 nights out of all time.
