### Post by mamamia88 on 2010-07-26
Call me a naive college student if you will but why does our economic system have to function the way it does?  Why do people have to sacrifice their time and their happiness just to keep a roof over their heads and food in their stomachs?  You could say sure open a business and run it yourself,  But, still that is still trading your time for money something I don't think anyone should have to do.  I don't think I can ever be more than "content" with a job knowing that I have to pretend to be sick just to get out of work.  We say we are a free country but, when you can't be honest with your boss and just say, "i'm not coming in today i really don't feel like it" you have sacrificed your freedom and become a voluntary slave.  So my question is with all the innovations and technology created by human beings why can't we come up with a system that doesn't require people to be miserable for 8 hours a day 5 days a week just to enjoy the 2 days at the end of the week?

### Post by t0p on 2010-07-26
It doesn't *have* to be like this.  A few hundred years ago things were very different.  And in another few hundred years things will be very different.  So don't let anyone fool you into thinking that the way the world works right now is somehow the natural order of things.

If you don't want to wait a few hundred years, there are ways of changing the way we live right now.  But revolutions are messy and violent and you can never be sure how they'll turn out.  It's probably best to keep your head down, don't ask awkward questions, and die in your sleep in your 80s.
