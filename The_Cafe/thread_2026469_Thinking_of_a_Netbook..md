### Post by mr john on 2012-07-15
I've always been a fan of Asus netbooks. It's a bit tricky accessing the  hdd in some models but I've found the keyboards, speakers and screens to be good. I bought 3 of them for two people and myself people 2 years ago and with a bit of maintenance they are  all still working

My experience with MSI wasnt quite as good. I bought one for my girlfriend and the speakers were too quiet. The casing was also poorly built and started falling apart very quickly. My best experience has been with an Advent netbook. It has worked for nearly 3 years now and has never needed a hard disk replacement unlike the Asus which is now on HDD number 3. The Advent has taken a good beating over the years.

With regard to hard disks breaking it because my netbooks get bumped around alot. It might be better for me to one day change the HDD for a solid state drive because those are harder to break.
