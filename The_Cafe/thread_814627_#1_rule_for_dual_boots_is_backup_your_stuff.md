### Post by Lord Xeb on 2008-05-31
When you do a dual boot, make sure all of your stuff (you know: projects, financial records, pics, etc...) is backed up on a separate drive or on a CD/DVD. This will prevent headaches down the road. It is also BIG part of computer security and should be taken seriously. Even seasoned pros make mistakes, that is why they do backups too.



Enjoy :popcorn:

### Post by tamoneya on 2008-05-31
