Well, for starters, you could probably look into the ["Open Source Definition"]("http://www.opensource.org/docs/definition.php") for a look at the ideas that built the GPL (General Public License) license, which Ubuntu itself is mostly under. ### Post by spinlock99 on 2010-05-11
You should look into why Richard Stalman started the GNU foundation in the first place. Basically, he got mad at the problem of not "owning" the software that ran a printer (I think, be sure to double check my facts for your essay). This led him to create an operating system that users would truly "own" and be able to understand, modify, and - the real goal - use in an unfettered manner. 

Good luck with your assignment.
