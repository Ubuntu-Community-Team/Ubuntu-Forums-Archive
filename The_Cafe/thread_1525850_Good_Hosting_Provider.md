What price are you looking at?  I have a few pages hosted through [Lunar Pages]("http://www.lunarpages.com") which are okay, but they nickle and dime you on options, like SSH is $2/month and to get SSH you also have to get CPanel which is another $2/month.  One year of their '$4.95/month service' is like $150.  

Another option is [Linode]("http://www.linode.com/") which I'm probably moving our LUG to given I can get the group to help support the cost.  Their lowest plan is $20/month, but you have an entire VPS to yourself which is NICE!  

Unfortunately I haven't found any good gauge on how to find good hosting... with most hosts for every good review there's a bad one no matter how long they've been around.  Also setting-up a web host is so freakin' cheap, so you see LOTS of folks getting into the business and offering great service in the beginning but growing faster than they can keep-up.  Heck I read one blog the other day from a 13 year old kid who bought a server and is colocating it to do hosting.  You just never know who's maintaining the servers when you go with web hosting.

With all this being said, if you need dependable hosting I'd recommend spending the cash with either one of the larger named companies, or colocating your own box.  As I said before Linode has a good reputation and I've talked to them a few times about hosting, so running with a good VPS is a great option to get exactly what you want -- but again you'll pay alittle more for it.

Take care --

Sam
