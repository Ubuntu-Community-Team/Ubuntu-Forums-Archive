However I accomplished much more than I assumed I was capable of. Thus I decided to share my end result.

I give you.. Those Without Honor.

> Wayfarers in a timeless realm,
They find beauty that no song can tell,
As they sail beyond the mortal lands.


Silence.
Timeless mist of dreams,
Oceans grace of sleep,
A faceless death decends.

Faithless.
Betray the realm of gods,
Forsake the ancient laws.
The killing hand of man,
Builds a tomb of loss.


Dreams of the sea, and the north winds calling,
Eternal rhythm, rumor of dawn.

Cast aside your fear, the night-time longing,
Fair memories of times long gone.


Thus passes the wintry nocturne,
Memorial dawn in an unforgiving Eden,
A fair garden in this tempest of ice.


A ballad of winter, frost nightingale,
A frozen land, to thus we sail.

Wistful mist with the light of stars,
Aurora guide to save the lost.

Trembling ocean, mirror of dreams,
A voyage to forgotten seas.
