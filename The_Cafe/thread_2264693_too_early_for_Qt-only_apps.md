### Post by QIII on 2015-02-10
The idea that Kubuntu is monstrously resource intensive is generally blown out of proportion.

While it is heavier than, say Xubuntu, it is no more resource intensive than Unity.  My Kubuntu machine idles at CPU loads that barely register in my conky and the memory load idles at about 700MB.  CPU-wise it's a wash.  Memory-wise it's heavier but it isn't killing anything.
