### Post by mips on 2006-05-13
[QUOTE=Digidiz]hint here and its a very good one: 6.06, what that means is anyones guess[/QUOTE]

Ubuntu has a 6mnth release cycle for new versions. 

6 refers to the year 2006
06 refers to the 6th month of the year 2006.

Ubuntu version numbers are usually x.04 & x.10. This gives you a new release every six months. Dapper on the other hand has broken the 6mnth cycle so they  can deliver a polished product and is being released 2mnths late, thus the 6.06.
