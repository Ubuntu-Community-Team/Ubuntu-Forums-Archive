Hi all!

Here is the thing i have been wandering about for couple of years...a web service for finding the information about the particular song you remember only by melody.:) 
It occurred to me due to my specific handicap: i can easily remember songs by a melody, but much harder by text. There is actualy ton of songs pending in my memory from childhood up to now, which i cannot identify.:( Or you can pass by some cafe, listening to something beautiful that just caught your ear and...you want to know the name and author of that song. 
:guitar:
So what i propose now would be to make such a service that could make a signal maching, but the "relative" one (it is not expected one should pin down exact frequency to begin singing). The problem is if the song is protected by copyright. In such case, the comparison between your sample and exact song should be made available, without playback of the actual song (only to attain the needed information:P). Is something like this already existent?

Cheers!
