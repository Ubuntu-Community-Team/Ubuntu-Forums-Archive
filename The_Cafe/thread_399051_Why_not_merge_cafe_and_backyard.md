### Post by zenwhen on 2007-04-01
> **plb said:**
> Both seem more or less general discussion so why not merge the two?

The Backyard was more or less created so that people could discuss things that we (the staff) would rather not have discussed here at all. 

For example, religion and politics are topics that lead to very heated debates and do nothing but help to create divisions in a community that is based on neither religion or politics.

Open discussion of these topics creates more work for the staff than it does good for the community. The Backyard is a compromise.
