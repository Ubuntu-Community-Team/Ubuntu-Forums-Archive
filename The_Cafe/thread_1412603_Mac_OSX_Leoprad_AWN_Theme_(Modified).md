To download this is the link : [http://www.mediafire.com/file/nzmndynmtcd/Modified_Theme.tgz](http://www.mediafire.com/file/nzmndynmtcd/Modified_Theme.tgz)

**_How to Install:_**
Open the AWN manager, click on the **Themes** tab and then click on **Add** and then locate the file which you have downloaded. Apply the theme.
