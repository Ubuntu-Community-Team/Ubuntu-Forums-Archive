### Post by vexorian on 2009-11-27
HP's netbooks are among the ones with the highest failure rates. Acer is also high in that list but not that high.

Just wanted to mention that everything in the D250 works out of the box in karmic (you need to install the restricted drivers when asked for though) I don't know about the HP though, maybe it works OOB as well.

Something that I love about the D250 is that the screen has something different to other netbook screens, I can actually use it outdoors somehow. I remember that with my asus I needed a very dark location, not with the D250.

Edit: The D250 has 160GB of hard drive. It is nice to have space for ISOs and videos.
