### Post by undecim on 2010-04-16
Not an engineer or even a hobbyist, but I can tell you that yes, connecting two batteries in parallel will draw power from both at the same time. I.e., the phone will draw 1/2 its power from each battery, giving you twice as much battery life as compared to one battery.

### Post by gzarkadas on 2010-04-16
Since you intend to place the battery pack outside of your cellphone, you must ensure:
-- a good quality of the electrical connections
-- a good quality of the sealing of the connections and batteries from the outside world (especially from water / liquids and metal dust)
to avoid the danger of short-circuits, burns, explosions and the like. 

Also resist the temptation to use cheap no-name batteries, especially if you opt for the lithium type.

See for example [http://www.batteryuniversity.com/partone-5B.htm](http://www.batteryuniversity.com/partone-5B.htm) for more information.
