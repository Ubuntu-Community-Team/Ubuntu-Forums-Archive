### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-08-11
I been using photobucket for years (I forget when I started). Granted I don't do much hosting but it's always served my needs and I've never had a picture removed or had any other problem. I also never had a problem with the log in, almost every web 2.0 service requires a log in so how is that any different? As for the bandwidth limit, I dunno... I use them to host all the pics on my [guide]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=500020") and they've never gone down/limited it at least from what I seen.

So I recommend photobucket. Oh and yes, stay far away from imageshack... they take pics down like crazy.
