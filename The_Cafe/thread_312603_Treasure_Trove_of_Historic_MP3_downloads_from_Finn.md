I thought I would bring your attention to something I found a few days back on a "Classical Music Blog". 

Check it out. 

Finnish Radio currently has a large number of MP3'S of Performances of Conductors such as Yehudi Menuhin, Arturo Toscanini, Dinu Lipatti, Pablo Casals, Alfred Cortot, Kirsten Flagstad, and many more. 

The whole site is in Finnish, but navigation is intuitive. 

Just select the artist from the left hand side of the page, then select the Real Audio or MP3 hyperlink under the composition. 

Each MP3 download has a spoken introduction of around 20 seconds in Finnish, but the Music is really great. 

The Link where you need to go is: 

[http://www.yleradio1.fi/musiikki/musiikinmestareita/id5995.shtml](http://www.yleradio1.fi/musiikki/musiikinmestareita/id5995.shtml)
