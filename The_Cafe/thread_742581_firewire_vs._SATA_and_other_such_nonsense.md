### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-04-02
1. SATA is faster. Definitely. USB 2 is theoretically faster than Firewire 400, but Apple's USB drivers aren't the most efficient out there, and they reduce the speed to under Firewire's. Firewire 800 is definitely faster than USB 2, but still slower than SATA.

Be aware that Firewire has its own inherent security problems.

2. Mac users have been enjoying *draft*-N wireless, but so have many PC users. It's just that the PC people have been more wary of draft standards, as they are not finalised. I wouldn't buy devices based around specifications that have, and probably will, change. It's a potential compatibility nightmare.

N-wireless is fast, but with the rise of high definition video, it's already too slow.

3. Does iTunes have any lossless tracks? iTunes has mostly DRM'ed tracks. The iTunes Music Store has songs with DRM where the other stores have those same tracks without DRM.
