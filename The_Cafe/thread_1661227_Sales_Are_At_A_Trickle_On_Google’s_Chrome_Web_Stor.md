### Post by FuturePilot on 2011-01-06
Just some theories as to why.

[LIST]
[*]When people see what full-blown cloud computing is they finally realized it is not all it's hyped up to be
[*]Less control. As mentioned in the article you can purchase and "install" these apps meaning you're not really installing anything. Even when you purchase something downloadable you have at least a little bit of control and something slightly more tangible. So even though the apps in Google's Web Store are only a few dollars, people question what they are paying for if they have little to no control over the apps.
[*]Despite the hype, cloud computing probably only works well for a small niche set of users.
[/LIST]

tl;dr cloud computing isn't all it's cracked up to be.
