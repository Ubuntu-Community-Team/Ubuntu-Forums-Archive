### Post by McLogic on 2006-04-13
I would just remove the keyboard and plug in a $10 external keyboard. You may have to change bios settings to keep the laptop from halting on keyboard errors.  You will also want a new power adapter to keep your system reliable.

First you should see if your adapter was recalled:
[https://www.delladapterprogram.com/Main.aspx](https://www.delladapterprogram.com/Main.aspx)

Then you may have to buy a new power adapter:
[http://froogle.google.com/froogle?q=Dell+Inspiron+2600+power+supply&hl=en&lr=&sa=X&oi=froogle&ct=title](http://froogle.google.com/froogle?q=Dell+Inspiron+2600+power+supply&hl=en&lr=&sa=X&oi=froogle&ct=title)

The keyboard is here:
[http://froogle.google.com/froogle?hl=en&lr=&q=Dell%20Inspiron%202600%20keyboard&btnG=Search&sa=N&tab=wf](http://froogle.google.com/froogle?hl=en&lr=&q=Dell%20Inspiron%202600%20keyboard&btnG=Search&sa=N&tab=wf)

The manual will tell you how to replace/remove the keyboard:
[http://support.dell.com/support/edocs/systems/ins2600/en/sm_en/keyboard.htm#999636](http://support.dell.com/support/edocs/systems/ins2600/en/sm_en/keyboard.htm#999636)

The full man:
[http://support.dell.com/support/edocs/systems/ins2600/en/index.htm](http://support.dell.com/support/edocs/systems/ins2600/en/index.htm)

It should make a good machine. It is overkill, and you might save money by selling the laptop (for about $450) and buying a P3+ off of craigslist -- you see them selling for under $50.
[http://www.craigslist.org/](http://www.craigslist.org/)

I just got a gateway with a 500mhz P3 for $30! 
(plenty fast for ssh/ftp/print)
