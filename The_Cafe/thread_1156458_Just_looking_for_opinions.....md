This is by no means an attack, but if you want people to take you seriously you need to correct this.  I was able to take a solid guess at your age just by reading your welcome paragraph. 

Consider everything you put on a website like this way, would this be something I would send to the VP of a company, my boss's boss's boss via email?  If you are content with it than great, if not, go back to the drawing board and rewrite.

I work in a corporate environment, I have for years, nothing bothers me more than educated people who are smart in their field lacking at something as simple as writing a basic paragraph.  Based on that initial welcome paragraph I would not hire you, I would rethink the choice of words and get to the point.

Proficient means you are competent at completing a task, efficient means you can do a task in a timely manner.

My word of advice, KISS = Keep It Simple Stupid
