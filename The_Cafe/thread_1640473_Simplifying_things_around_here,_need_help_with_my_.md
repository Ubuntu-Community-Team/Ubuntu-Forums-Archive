### Post by 3Miro on 2010-12-07
I prefer .ogg files whenever I can, however, many people have to install extra software to read those under Windows. Also, many devices read mp3 only. The mp3 is the most commonly used format.

Video is more complicated and I know little about the different formats on it. There is an .ogg video format, but it may be even less supported then the sound format. MPEG, AVI and Matrioshka seem the most popular video formats, but I may be wrong.

For pictures, try .png or .jpg, I thinks there are some differences in quality vs size, but both should be OK.
