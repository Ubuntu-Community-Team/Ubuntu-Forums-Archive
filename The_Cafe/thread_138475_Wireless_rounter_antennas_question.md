### Post by jpkotta on 2006-03-02
The ones with multiple antennas are MIMO (multi-in, multi-out) devices.  Basically, they use the spatial redundancy of the antennas to get a better signal, which means more range and faster speeds.  

My personal opinion:  I would wait until there is wide spread adoption.  If you need speed, get a cable.  If you need range, may be it's worth it to go MIMO.
