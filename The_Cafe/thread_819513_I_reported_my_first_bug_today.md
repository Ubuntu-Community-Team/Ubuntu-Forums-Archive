### Post by Ozor Mox on 2008-06-05
I decided today that I should try and contribute more to free software by reporting bugs that I find, instead of assuming that they are already being worked on. I found that the button to refresh the list of online servers in [Freeciv](http://www.freeciv.org) didn't work, so I emailed the bug tracker and got a response a few minutes later to say a ticket had been created automatically. A couple of hours later I checked my email to see a developer had picked up the bug and confirmed it. Another few hours later, and that developer had created a patch and submitted it for inclusion in the next maintenance release! I couldn't (and still can't!) believe the speed of the response, and this isn't GNOME or the Linux kernel or anything, just an open source game...Amazing!

It's a good feeling when you've contributed something isn't it? I will soon be registering an account on Launchpad and getting ready to report some Ubuntu bugs.
