### Post by HappinessNow on 2010-06-17
[COLOR=#000000][FONT=Times New Roman][FONT=Arial][**AdBlock**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/gighmmpiobklfepjocnamgkkbiglidom") - Version: 2.0.2
AdBlock for Chrome! Blocks ads all over the web. 

[**Auto Replay for YouTube**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/kanbnempkjnhadplbfgdaagijdbdbjeb") - Version: 1.9.2
Adds an Auto Replay button in Youtube Page. 

[**Browser Button for AdBlock**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/picdndbpdnapajibahnnogkjofaeooof") - Version: 0.0.7
If you have already installed AdBlock and want a browser action button, install this too.

[**Craigslist Preview**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/jmcbgcbedienblgnfeecolmmcgocefnf") - Version: 1.0.14
Extension to prefetch Craigslist ad images

[**Easy Youtube Video Downloader**]("http://www.chromeextensions.org/music-videos-photos/easy-youtube-video-downloader/") - Version: 2.2
Easiest method to download YouTube videos in FLV, MP3, 3GP, MP4, 720p and 1080p FULL-HD video qualities.

[**Facebook. Themed.**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/fjnbbncopeekoioolgpfneccdgdnkfdg") - Version: 0.3.3
Who says you can't theme Facebook? By Dalton Gore

[**goo.gl shortener + share on twitter**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/fdndabnoclcmkbdgakdhdpjghagfnapj") - Version: 1.2
Shorten your URLs using goo.gl and share them on twitter.

[**Google Dictionary (by Google)**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/mgijmajocgfcbeboacabfgobmjgjcoja") - Version: 1.0.2
View definitions easily as you browse the web.

[**Google Voice (by Google)**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/kcnhkahnjcbndmmehfkdnkjomaanaooo") - Version: 2.2.1.5
Make calls, send SMS, preview Inbox, and get notified of new messages. (US only)

[**Send using Gmail (no button)**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/ahldefgplekckalfcolhhnljbbgaiboc") - Version: 1.11.4
Makes the Gmail webmail your default email application (original Google extension repackaged without toolbar button, safer links)

[**Sexy Undo Close Tab**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/bcennaiejdjpomgmmohhpgnjlmpcjmbg") - Version: 6
Undo your closed tabs (cleaner version) + new features

[**Speed Dial**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/dgpdioedihjhncjafcpgbbjdpbbkikmi") - Version: 2.0
Speed Dial for Chrome - replace Chrome new tab with your predefined visual bookmarks

[**Turn Off the Lights**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/bfbmjmiodbnnpllbbbfblcplfjjepjdn") - Version: 1.7.9.17
The entire page will be fading to dark, so you can watch to a video as if you are in the cinema

[**Yet another flags**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/dmchcmgddbhmbkakammmklpoonoiiomk") - Version: 0.6
Shows flags for the website in the location bar.

[**Gmail - No invites**]("https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/bdoeiplcjolkngkhhiejphomfhlmpkpc?hl=en") - Version: 1.1
Hide Gmail invites.
