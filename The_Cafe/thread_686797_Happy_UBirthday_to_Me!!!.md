### Post by whitefort on 2008-02-03
Maybe a self-indulgent post, but I just wanted to mark the fact that I've now been using Ubuntu for one full year.

Before that, in the past ten years or so, I tried Linux many times.  My longest previous Linux experience lasted about a month, max, before I got fed up with stuff not working and went back to Windows.

Now I have Ubuntu on 4 machines, and love it.  I retain 1 XP machine because of a couple of Windows programs I need that don't work properly under WINE (does anything?)

But 95% of my computing is on Ubuntu, and maybe another 1% on Puppy Linux which I keep on a bootable keyring-drive.

My relationship to Windows is like that of a schoolboy to a girlie magazine under his bed.  I may... uh, USE it from time to time, but I'm certainly not proud of it!  Ubuntu is the operating system you would take home to meet your Mother!

Anyway, here goes year 2 of my GNU/Linux experience.  Still LOTS to learn, but still havin' fun!

Happy UBirthday to me!
