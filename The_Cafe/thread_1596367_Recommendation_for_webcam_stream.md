### Post by aeiah on 2010-10-14
its probably easiest to look into something like [http://www.livestream.com/](http://www.livestream.com/)

an alternative approach would be to upload a snapshot from your webcam to a web server every minute, and display an auto-refreshing page that she can visit that displays the newest image. something like [http://devio.us](http://devio.us) offers free shell + web accounts, so you could set a cron job up to upload a webcam image every minute.

as for webcams, i think most will work now. just go to your preferred retailer, see what the 5 most suitable webcams are and then google to see if they are compatible.
