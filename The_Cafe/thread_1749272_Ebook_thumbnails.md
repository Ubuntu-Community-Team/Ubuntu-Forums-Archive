### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-05-04
I guess that this is due to the way that the Nautilus file manager works. It is able to read and display in a small image the first page of a PDF document but not able to access the first page of documents in those other formats. I do not consider this inconsistent. It is just a limitation of the program. The developers of nautilus may not see a need or may be too busy with other work or may not have access to the protocols of these ebook formats.
