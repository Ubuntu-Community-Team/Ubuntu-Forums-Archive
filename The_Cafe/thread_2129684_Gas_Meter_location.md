### Post by deadflowr on 2013-03-26
I was a gas pipping mechanic for a short time some time ago, and I don't remember meters ever needing support.
However, the rules aside from the general guidelines of the universal plumbing code, are made by place to place basis.

I remember, one town would require earthquake valves and the town next door would not.

But general consensus is that the meter is the property of the gas company, and shouldn't be your problem.
Everything after the meter is yours.

But being that you say this is Canada, what I know can go out the window.
