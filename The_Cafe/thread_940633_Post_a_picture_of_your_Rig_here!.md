### Post by geoken on 2008-10-07
> **Anurag_panda said:**
> I like your heatsink.

It's a Scythe. It's a really good cooler. It basically accepts any 120mm fan so you have the potential to make it really quiet. 

It also works really well in my setup because it gets push/pull. The cooler can accommodate more than one fan (you can put a fan on one side and another fan on the other side). This is considered a push/pull setup and significantly increases the efficiency of the cooler. Most cases probably don't have the necessary space for a push/pull setup (mine doesn't) but the cooler's proximity to the power supply fan and the rear case fan allows those two fans to pull some of the air.
