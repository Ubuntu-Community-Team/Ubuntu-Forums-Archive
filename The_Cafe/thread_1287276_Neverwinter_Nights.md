### Post by alphaniner on 2009-11-27

> Any suggestions? Sure hate to reinstall!

Your partition map is a bit messed up: your extended partition is sda3 and it contains sda5 and sda6 as logical partitions.  But sda4 is another primary partition and it is 'behind' the extended partition.  Point being, the remaining ~30GB on the drive is unusable.  [s]I don't think there's any way to fix this without deleting partitions 3-6.[/s]  There's a few ways to fix this, but every one would involve deleting at least one partition.  If you don't want to do that, you could probably get away with installing it on your existing /home partition.
