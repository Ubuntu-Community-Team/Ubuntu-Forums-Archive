### Post by Groucho Marxist on 2009-06-05
I'll get the ball rolling with a short poem I wrote about gambling :)

Aces, queens
Kings and jack
Hearts and spades
Red and black

Five card draw
Player trades;
Two of diamonds,
Three of spades

Move those chips
Across the table,
But only bet
If you are able

All players in
Will show their hands
Two shall fold, but
“Dealer stands”

Above all the noise
The man will shout,
"Play again?"
"Deal me out"

Your eyes are playful and your voice is sweet,
The God of heaven you will not meet,
