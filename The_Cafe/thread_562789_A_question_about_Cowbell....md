### Post by Wolki on 2007-09-29
Yes, you need to write something into the Album field, or cowbell won't know how to find the album art.

Cowbell IIRC only searches on Amazon US for cover art, so it can't find everything. There was a qt program called "Album Cover Art Downloader" that was nice for searching for covers, but the site seems to have vanished :-/ And that one didn't do tag editing, only cover art download.
