### Post by irv on 2010-06-16
How many times have you run across a good post or a howto that you wanted to keep in case you find a need for it later? I am one of those guys that loose thing all the time so I started look for an answer to this problem. Here is what I did.
I use gmail for my emails so when I find a good post or howto I send myself a link to it via my gmail account where I file it in my directory call “howtos”. This way I have one place to look for everything. This works good because if my system goes down I just jump on another PC and go to my gmail account and find the answer I was looking for.
It works for me!
