The First Amendment grants everyone in the U.S. the freedom to publish, express views, practice religion, assemble and petition government. Join news organizations, Google and others in celebrating the First on July 1. Join us at [1forall.us]("http://1forall.us/").
[LIST]
[*]Submit a 30 second video that demonstrates your freedom to speak, rock, or assemble.
[*]Winners will be featured on YouTube, TV, and at the Newseum in Washington D.C.
[*]All video entries are due by July 25th.
[/LIST]
1 for All is the joint effort of educators, journalists, and others who believe that the American public would benefit from a greater understanding of the First Amendment. Google and YouTube support 1 for All. Read about our participation on [our blog]("http://googleblog.blogspot.com/").
