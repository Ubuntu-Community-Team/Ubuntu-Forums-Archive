### Post by Colonel Kilkenny on 2009-09-10
Everything works just fine here. No problems.

But make sure that your browserjs is up to date:
Go Help -> Check for updates.
And after that visit: [http://www.opera.com/docs/browserjs/](http://www.opera.com/docs/browserjs/)
The page should say: "Current browser.js status: enabled. 
Target version and time stamp of the active browser.js file is Opera 10.00, Desktop, September 4, 2009 ."

Opera patches badly coded pages with browserjs and Facebook is one of the sites which has problems with Opera.
