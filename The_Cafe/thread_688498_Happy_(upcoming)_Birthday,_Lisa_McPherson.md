### Post by SZF2001 on 2008-02-05
Since I figured there may be a worldwide protest coming, and it's almost a  certain person's birthday, I figured this could be an appropriate time to bring up the life of [Lisa McPherson](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lisa_McPherson).

This is not a thread trying to promote "Anonymous", this is not a thread that should explode into a religious debate, this is not a thread that should become derailed or misguided - this is a story about the life of a young woman who just wanted someone to help her, someone who died at the hands of others who said they were 'caring' for her, someone who just wanted guidance and recieved a somewhat disturbed death.

 Maybe this thread could serve as a memorial, please take it very generally. I'll never forget her story - her life, and her death, and what she is still doing now (even as a dead person) to fight for a just cause - and hopefully some of you will be open and take into the accounts of people's past.

Happy upcoming birthday, Lisa McPherson, you shining diamond.
