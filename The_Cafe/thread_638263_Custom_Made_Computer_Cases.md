### Post by aaaantoine on 2007-12-12
Tom's Hardware reviewed a firm that lets you / helps you build a fully custom case right down to the blueprints.  But the price was insane.  I don't think it was $18,000 but it was definitely at least four figures.

My best advice to you is, search long and hard for a unique case you like.  Unless you're friends with a machinist or a metal worker, you'll have a hard time making a cheap custom case.

Edit: The company is [Protocase](http://www.protocase.com/), and the build that [Tom's Hardware](http://www.tomshardware.com/2007/01/25/making-a-custom-pc-enclosure-with-protocase/) came up with cost them $1460.
