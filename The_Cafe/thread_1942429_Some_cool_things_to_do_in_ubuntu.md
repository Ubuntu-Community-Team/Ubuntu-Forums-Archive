### Post by IWantFroyo on 2012-03-17
1) Poke around the Software Center and try things. Word processors, text editors, even games.

2) This may fall under appearance customization, but try new desktop environments. Give gnome-shell a spin, after all, you can always change it back from the login screen.

3) Do things on your computer. Take up blogging or programming, or just surf the Internet. Ubuntu is supposed to disappear into the background, so you can do what you enjoy.
