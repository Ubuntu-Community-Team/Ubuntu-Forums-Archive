### Post by HermanAB on 2007-08-04
The 'best' in terms of absolutely everything including the kitchen sink with lots of wizards for all the mundane stuff is Mandriva 2007 Spring Edition.  Mandriva is good for both servers and desktops and makes a great 'do everything' machine for a small business.

This is the only system on which you are allowed to handle multiple levels of classified data, although doing so is a huge pain in the *** and best avoided.
