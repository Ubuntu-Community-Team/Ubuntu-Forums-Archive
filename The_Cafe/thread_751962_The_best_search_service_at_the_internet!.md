### Post by AndyCooll on 2008-04-11
I've started using [www.zuula.com]("www.zuula.com").

It's a metasearch engine and hence compiles the results of others (e.g. Google, Yahoo! etc). What I like about it is that it brings back the results in the form of tabs, so you see the results from Google in one tab, Yahoo! in another. 

And you can choose the order of your tabs too. If you prefer the results  of Ask (for instance)  most of the time you can therefore make this the first tab.
