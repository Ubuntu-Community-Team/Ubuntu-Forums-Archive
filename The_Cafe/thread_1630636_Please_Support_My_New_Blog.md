some tips i can give you is to create good guides for beginners

make sure your pages show up on google when people search.

you can also give out your links in forums posts and IRC chat, if they're relevant and informative.

helpful and informative is the key. If not people wont go.

It takes time to build a good blog, do it because you love it.

If your blog becomes as good or better than most, people will follow and reward you :)

Dont worry if you get no visits at first. Keep posting guides. It can take weeks or months for google to mark your blog as "useful" and get your page rank up.

Once pagerank is up , lots of older guides will start receiving visitors.

if posts are too short, they may not even show on google

add pics too when you can and name them well

You can also create relationships with other blogs and guest post (not spam post).

well made Youtube videos may also get people to your blog

and best tip of all: no spam, or you will get punished by everyone and search engines
