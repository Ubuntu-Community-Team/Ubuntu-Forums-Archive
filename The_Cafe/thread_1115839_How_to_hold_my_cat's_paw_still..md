### Post by Dale61 on 2009-04-04
This may sound cruel, but I got this tip from a vet.

Grab two or three clothes pegs (the spring loaded type) and attach them to the back of the cat's neck.  Apparently, this puts them in 'kitten mode', and you can do almost anything when they are in this state.

I suggested that to my parents when they needed to de-knot my mothers Persian, and the cat didn't even give a whimper.  She just laid there.  It also works well when it's time to give the cat a bath.
