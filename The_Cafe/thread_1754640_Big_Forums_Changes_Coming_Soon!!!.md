### Post by Technoviking on 2011-05-10
The Ubuntu Forums will soon have a new look (see attached screenshot) and will be upgraded to this latest version of the vBulletin software [1]. If you want to check out some of the graphics for the forums, they maybe downloaded from Launchpad using bazaar[2]. We would love our forums community to contribute some artwork to the new look of the forums. More detail to follow on how to do this.

Thanks and more news on the forums upgrade soon.

Cheers,
T-V

[1] It was considered if the forums could be convert to an free web forums solution, but a way could not found to convert the database of this size from vBulletin to another web forums format.
