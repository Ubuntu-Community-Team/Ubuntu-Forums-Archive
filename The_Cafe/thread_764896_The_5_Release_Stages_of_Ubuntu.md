### Post by justin whitaker on 2008-04-24
After seeing all the "When will Hardy Release" Threads, I realized there are 5 release stages to each Ubuntu Release.

**Anticipation:** "Is it here? Not yet? How about now? No? How about now?"

**Revelation:** "It's here! We are saved! Linux will finally take over everything, including my girlfriend!"

**Acceptance:** "It's over and done with, and it's good! Really good. I gave up XP for it!"

**Anger:** "It didn't change the world! It broke my hard drive, overwrote Vista, took my lunch money, and stole my girlfriend!"

**Resentment:** Why isn't there more games for Ubuntu? When is X bug going to be fixed? Why is Ubuntu so buggy? Why isn't Ubuntu more like (insert pet Linux OS name here)?

Just trying to keep it in perspective.

Any stages I missed? :)
