### Post by tubezninja on 2009-08-03
> **mamamia88 said:**
> does flash work better in osx than ubuntu?


Yes. :D

Actually, it depends on the hardware you're using for the ubuntu box.  If you don't have a decent graphics card, expect performance to be flaky.

Mac hardware is more straightforward and certain configurations are expected, so Flash support is generally more robust and stable all around in OS X. The developers have fewer hardware "surprises" to contend with. But you can overcome this if your hardware under Ubuntu is up to par.
