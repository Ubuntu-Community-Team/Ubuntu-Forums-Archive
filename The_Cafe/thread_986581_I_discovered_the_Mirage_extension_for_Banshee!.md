### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2008-11-18
I was poking around installing Banshee again (now that 1.x is in the official repository!) and came across a young extension called [Mirage]("http://hop.at/mirage/") that comes from [a separate package]("apt:banshee-extension-mirage").

It does automatic playlist generation, like Apple's "Genius" thing but faster (it seems), without smothering all my system resources and with a cooler name. It also came first. So **hah**!

Oh, and it's really awesome software, to boot. Just drag a track into the Playlist Generator and a bunch of other similar tracks appear along with it. It's quite spooky. Combine that with the Last.FM-powered recommendations (view -> show recommendations) and we have something spectacular.

I can't help but wonder what would happen if someone pushed out a paper-thin iPod killer and integrated it with Banshee. Even right now it could work perfectly, since you can download tracks from last.fm and such.
All we need is a directory of podcasts and radio stations integrated with the program. Something like the [Miro Guide]("https://www.miroguide.com/") :)
