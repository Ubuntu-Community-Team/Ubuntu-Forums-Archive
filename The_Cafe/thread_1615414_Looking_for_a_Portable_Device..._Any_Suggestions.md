I believe the N900 is b/g only unfortunately.  But that's a relatively minor limitation in the grand scheme of the universe.

Anything in Nokia's N-series (N900, N810, N800) would probably suit your purposes.  I've never tried using Skype on my N900, but I think it should be possible to do.

For something a little more off-the wall, have you checked out the [Always Innovating Smart Book](http://www.alwaysinnovating.com/products/smartbook.htm)?  It's a combination netbook/tablet/handheld device that looks kind of slick.  It's not shipping yet (pre-orders are on now though).  I ordered the first-generation Touchbook a year or so back and barring my terrible luck with getting sent two lemons in a row, it was a pretty slick little device.
