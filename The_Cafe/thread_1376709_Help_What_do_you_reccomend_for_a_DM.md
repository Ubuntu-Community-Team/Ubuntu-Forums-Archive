### Post by RiceMonster on 2010-01-09
1) Log in and type startx (add exec startfluxbox to ~/.xinitrc)
2) Set X to start when you log in (check [this](http://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Start_X_at_boot) out)
3) SLiM
