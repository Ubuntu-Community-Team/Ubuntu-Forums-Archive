### Post by aidanr on 2007-08-25
GITanker, firstly you want to get multimedia codecs installed, so follow the instructions [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats")

Then you'll be able to download and watch any of the formats from the Dl.tv website, I recommend the h.264 version, it's the best quality

Also you can use a podcasting client to download the latest shows automatically, I use [Miro]("http://www.getmiro.com")
