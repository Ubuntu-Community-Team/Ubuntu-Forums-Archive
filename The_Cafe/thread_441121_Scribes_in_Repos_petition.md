### Post by PuleX on 2007-05-12
You can check what packages are available for Ubuntu or Debian at respectively: 
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/](http://packages.ubuntu.com/) 
[http://packages.debian.org/](http://packages.debian.org/) 
It's not available from either yet. 

**However**, do you know the[ getdeb.net Ubuntu software portal]("http://www.getdeb.net/")? They provide Sribes packages for Edgy and Feisty: 
[http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=Scribes](http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=Scribes) 

I just installed the i386 package for Feisty and it worked flawlessly.
