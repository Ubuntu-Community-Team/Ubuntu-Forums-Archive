### Post by fatality_uk on 2008-01-27
What's your budget?

Things to look for:
1) Dynamic Contrast Ratio - Should be over 10,000:1 Minimum. Otherwise Blacks just look like washed out grey
2) Resolution - Go for as high as possible (1920 x 1080 ) is good
3) HDMI - As many of these extras as possible

Samsung are doing great LCD's screen right now. LE46**M87**BD is a good model. The M87 is the part model number to look for as this is the latest on sale generation from them
