### Post by LaRoza on 2008-08-09
> **chucky chuckaluck said:**
> yup, this is trivial, but i don't know why it's considered bad.

It can be a sign of bad things, but on busy forums it happens a lot.

Valid reason for double posting:

[list]
[*] Replying to more than one post (often it is only possible to do them one at a time, because of the volume here)
[*] A completely new thought, where an edit wouldn't be logical
[*] To post your solution to your own unanswered post
[/list]

More exist, but those are ones I could think of.

Double posting is inappropriate for bean hunting and minor comments which should be edits.
