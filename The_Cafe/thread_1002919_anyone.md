### Post by Giant Speck on 2008-12-05
> **chips24 said:**
> difference between ionization and dissociation

Ionization is the process in which an atom loses or gains an electron, thus becoming an ion.

Dissociation is the process in which an ionic compound splits into smaller molecules, ions, or radicals.
