### Post by PapaWiskas on 2006-11-25
If your TV is set to use channel 3 or 4 (typically one of the two), make sure you have the frequency set to Cable and not Air.  If you have an option to choose STD, HRC, or some other cable frequency, try each one to see if that makes a difference.
Also, try leaving the cable box on, don't turn it off.  Just turn the TV off and on.
