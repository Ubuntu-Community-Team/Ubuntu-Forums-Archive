1cup chopped onion, divided
3cloves garlic, minced
1can (8 ounces) reduced-sodium tomato sauce
3/4cup water
1to 2 tablespoons chili powder
1tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
1to 2 teaspoons cider vinegar
1teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2teaspoon ground allspice
1/2teaspoon paprika
1bay leaf
1/8teaspoon ground cloves (optional)

8ounces hot cooked spaghetti
