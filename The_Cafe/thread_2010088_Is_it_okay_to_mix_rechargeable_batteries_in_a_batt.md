### Post by Redblade20XX on 2012-06-25
I believe that it will eventuall effect the load cycle of the batteries. Since each battery is at a different level when your recharging, each battery will degrade at a different point (each individual battery will lose there ability to hold potential at different rates). Therefore when you plug in two different potential held batteries your device may not respond correctly.
