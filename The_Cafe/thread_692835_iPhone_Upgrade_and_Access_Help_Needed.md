### Post by ntetreau on 2008-02-11
Hi, unfortunately, you'll need windows and itunes to do any kind of update/restore work as the USB driver is encrypted.  Unfortunately, this also means you won't be able to use vmware, virtualbox or wine to do this.

Once jailbroken, your iphone can be used over wifi with ubuntu to sync music (with album art), and a few other things like calendar, contact and notes to various degrees.
