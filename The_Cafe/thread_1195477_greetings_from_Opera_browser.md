### Post by dirtylobster on 2009-06-24
One thing that really bothers me about Opera is the inability to quickly switch tabs with the keyboard. On a laptop without a decent mouse it's very time-consuming to move the pointer around to click different tabs.

You can assign one-key shortcuts ('1' and '2') to cycle between tabs but that doesn't enable you to jump between them. Like if you have tab 1 open and want to open tab 4 you have to click '2' three times instead of jumping directly to tab 4 with Alt-4 in Firefox (and many other browsers).
