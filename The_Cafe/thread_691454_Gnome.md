Share Gnome Information:
> **Gnomes** are one of the humanoid [races]("http://witcher.wikia.com/wiki/Race"). Gnomes were the oldest sentient race on the [Continent]("http://witcher.wikia.com/wiki/Continent") and together with [dwarves]("http://witcher.wikia.com/wiki/Dwarves") and [elves]("http://witcher.wikia.com/wiki/Elves") are called the [Old Races]("http://witcher.wikia.com/wiki/Old_Races"). Gnomes are shorter and weaker than dwarves, but just as tough and more agile. They can be easily distinguished from dwarves and [halflings]("http://witcher.wikia.com/wiki/Halfling") by their long noses. 
Many gnomes live in [Mahakam]("http://witcher.wikia.com/wiki/Mahakam") along with the dwarves, although others inhabit the [Tir Tochair]("http://witcher.wikia.com/index.php?title=Tir_Tochair&action=edit") mountain range. They are excellent smiths and metallurgists and their [gwyhyr]("http://witcher.wikia.com/index.php?title=Gwyhyr&action=edit") [swords]("http://witcher.wikia.com/index.php?title=Sword&action=edit") are considered the best in the world.
