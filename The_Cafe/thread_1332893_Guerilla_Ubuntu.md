Well as the OP I have to say that I never did go the crazy way, I find old computers with windows on them, stick on the best Linux they can handle and just give them away for $20 to $40 depending on how nice they are. Most times it's some single mom looking for a computer for their kid. I leave a read me file on the desktop with password and user name (box set to auto boot) and some basic instructions with some links. They also get a copy of the OS and my email if they need help. I add restricted extras, DVD codecs and java with a note that if they decide to use them they have accepted the EULA, and if they don't they should boot off the disk and reinstall without them. It makes me happy thinking kids all around me are getting to do things they normally couldn't afford to do...And that parents can provide it...with a bit of help from me
