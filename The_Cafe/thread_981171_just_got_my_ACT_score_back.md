### Post by Viranh on 2008-11-13
The deadline for college applications is usually in the spring anyway, so just take it again. Now you know what to study. My English score was higher too, and I'm a mechanical engineering student. I'm much better at math and logical reasoning than writing and always have been. It's my opinion that the ACT is not a very good test.

EDIT: I see you're a junior. It's definitely not a big deal then. You've got plenty of time to take it again, or try the SAT. The tests were pretty different (at least as I remember them). 

My school requires:
1. a 22 on the ACT OR graduate in the upper half of your high school class OR have a 2.5 GPA 
2. an ACT math score of 18 or above or 4 years of high school math
3. an ACT score of 18 on the combined writing/English section or a 3 or better on the AP English exam. 

However, I personally know several people who did not meet these requirements and got in.
