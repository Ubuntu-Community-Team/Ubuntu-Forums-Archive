### Post by igknighted on 2007-07-25
I feel like it is the job of the Ubuntu developers, the PCLOS developers, and all the developers of desktop specific distros to either run their own tests and do some lobbying, or else form a committee to review the changes to the kernel and lobby for desktop related improvements.
