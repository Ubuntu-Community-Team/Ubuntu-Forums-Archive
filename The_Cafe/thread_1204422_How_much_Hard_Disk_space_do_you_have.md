### Post by Gizenshya on 2009-07-04
Similar to the other thread, but a **summation**, and not just your main drive.

Sum of all '**hard**' disk space.  Includes **magnetic** (standard) and **SDD** hard disks (and **flash drives** as well if you wish), but not **optical disks**-- even if you boot from them.

Do not include **tape drives**.

Include **external hard drives**.

Include **server** and **NAS** space, but only if you own it (not my 10 gig gmail account, or rented server space, for example).

For the purposes of this poll, use 1 GB as **one billion bytes**, not 1 **binary** GB.

Poll options will be added shortly.. just wait for your option to appear ;)
