### Post by Darkhack on 2009-07-21
It doesn't appear that way.  Etch was released in April 2007 and Lenny in February 2009.  Meanwhile the past two Ubuntu LTS releases have been in June 2006 and April 2008.  So they haven't even been in the same year.  Also, Ubuntu's packages are based on Debian's unstable branch.  That's true even for LTS releases.  So even if they did come out around the same time, it would be merely coincidence.
