### Post by shirshasin on 2007-12-08
Is your application vanishes after minimize ? Don't worry still they are running, just press Alt+Tab to see them again(it is a app switcher too). The root of this problem is that you accidentally deleted **Window List**t from Gnome Panel. Right click at any black space on the panel and choose Add to Panel option, now from the window opened - click on Window List and then click on Add button, you are done man ! Again you will see your running  applications as usual on panel.
