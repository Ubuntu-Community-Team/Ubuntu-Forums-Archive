### Post by lyceum on 2007-01-21
I got an Eddie Bauer backpack for around $30-$40. It was well padded, water resistant and a lifetime warranty. The warranty is great, my brother just to go backpacking with friends out in ID and dropped his pack (no PC inside) over a cliff. When they fond it, the pack was trashed, but he sent it in to ED and got a new one, no problems. He told them the zipper broke, which it did. My only problem with the pack is that it expands to carry too much stuff. I forget and put too many books in it and it gets really heavy.
