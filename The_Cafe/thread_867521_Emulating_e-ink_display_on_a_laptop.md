### Post by zmjjmz on 2008-07-22
The idea behind most eBook readers is that the use flash storage and a low power consumption ARM CPU.
So, if you were to replace the HDD and the processor with the above mentioned, disconnected the backlight (or take it out altogether) and cut out leftover casing, you could have a decent eBook reader.
