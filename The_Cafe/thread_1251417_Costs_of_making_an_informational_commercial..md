### Post by Groucho Marxist on 2009-08-27
> **dragos240 said:**
> How much money would it take to air a simple commercial, with just text, about the meaning of 'PC'. We all know what a PC really means, a personal computer. How much to just put it on the air, on a number of channels.

Just text, no narration or music would make for a difficult spot to capture the attentions of consumers. I would recommend drafting copy that would allow for at least one announcer and some form of musical accompaniment. However, licensing music, even for a short ad, is rather pricey, so I would suggest paying for the creation of a short piece of original music. I can talk with others in my career field {Broadcasting} who are specialized in music production if you are interested in an original score for this hypothetical advertisement.
