### Post by LowSky on 2010-09-10
Dragonbite, Verizon requires all blackberrys to have data plans, no way around it... I know from experience.

My BB charged just fine using any standard usb cable connected to a PC, no app required.

Many people forget that America has two different cell networks, CDMA and GSM, and CDMA doesn't use SIM cards, which Verizon network is based on CDMA.


your better off going over to Google, starting an account for Google Calendar and using that with your current phone, you can send updates for appointments by SMS and email if you want, and it can be synced with Evolution.
