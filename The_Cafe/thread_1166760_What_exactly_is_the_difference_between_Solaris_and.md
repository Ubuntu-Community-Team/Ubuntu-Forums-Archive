### Post by schauerlich on 2009-05-22
Solaris is Sun's in house, developed and supported enterprise level OS. OpenSolaris is an open source fork of that project that has been blessed by Sun. OpenSolaris gives a little more attention to desktop users, and several distributions of OpenSolaris strive to make it an every day OS. The googles will turn up more detailed comparisons if you're curious.
