Hey Guys,

I finally took the time this week to order a backup USB drive to backup everything on my laptop, and after all my stuff was safe and sound about an hour or so ago I blew away 9.04 and installed 64-bit 10.04.

I'm not digging the theme though.  The graphics are great, but I like the title bar and menu bar to be separate colors.  I'll be changing that soon.

I've also installed Skype and VirtualBox along with all the restrictive codecs for MP3, DVD, etc and also Flash (though it's using the crummy wrapper).  I'm now copying all my crap back over from the USB drive which will take a few hours.

But yeah I'm lovin' it thus far.
