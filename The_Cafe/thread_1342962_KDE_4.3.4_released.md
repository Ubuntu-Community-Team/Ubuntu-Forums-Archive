There is that.  But KDE is not posted on KDE's own repos, and therefore under their own rules.  It is up to your distributor, i.e. Ubuntu.  But Ubuntu official policy is that newer software will not be added to their official repositories after the freeze prior to release.  In Karmic's case, some time in early October, IIRC.
