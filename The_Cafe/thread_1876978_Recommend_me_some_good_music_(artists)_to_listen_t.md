### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-11-07
If you like world music, try the Narasirato Pan Pipers. They're very awesome and very easy to listen to - they fuse their traditional music and instruments with modern Western melodies.

Their website is [www.narasirato.com](www.narasirato.com) but it actually doesn't list their first album, which was great. Their second album seems to have fewer easy-to-listen-to tracks and more of the traditional sounding ones, which are difficult to listen to with Western ears.

If you like cover versions, take a look at Simply Three. They are a string trio who play some surprisingly good cover versions of well-known songs. Check out their version of Lady Gaga's "Paparazzi".
