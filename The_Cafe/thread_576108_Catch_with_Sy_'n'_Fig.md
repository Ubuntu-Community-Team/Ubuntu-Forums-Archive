### Post by Kumeelyun on 2007-10-14
This is a cartoon created in Ubuntu Studio (7.04). I started fooling around with Synfig and while there were many small demos and squiggles of what it could do, I wanted to see if I could create a whole cartoon with it. While Synfig was used for the whole of this cartoon's creation (including compositing the scenes) other programs were used for other tasks and it became a way to demonstrate the creative power of other open source tools.

[Click here for YouTube posting.]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bySt14ufRmU")
[URL="http://www.hoodyhoo.com/toons/syNfig01_final.ogg"]
Right-click and save here for an Ogg version.[/URL]

Programs used:
[Synfig]("http://www.synfig.com"): animation
[Audacity]("http://audacity.sourceforge.net/"): editing and creation of sound effects
[Hydrogen]("http://www.hydrogen-music.org/"): background beats
[Cinelerra]("http://cv.cinelerra.org/"): compositing video and audio and rendering
[Avidemux]("http://fixounet.free.fr/avidemux/"): video conversion for YouTube
[GnoTime Tracker]("http://gttr.sourceforge.net/"): for tracking how long I worked on this project. About 60 hours over a month.

Any feedback is encouraged! Hope you enjoy it!
