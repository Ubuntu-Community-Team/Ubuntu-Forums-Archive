### Post by Sableyes on 2016-09-17
Because laptops take the same or similar hard drive and ram connections. The motherboards need to be shaped to the size of the laptop and battery, and the processor and graphics card are usually integrated into that. So you cannot take a motherboard/processor/graphics card from one laptop and put it in another. The ram and hard drives though are more universal fitting.

### Post by HermanAB on 2016-09-17

Also bear in mind that many/most industrial 'embedded single board computers' are built from laptop parts.
