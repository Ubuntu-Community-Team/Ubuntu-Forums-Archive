### Post by Bachstelze on 2010-02-26
Also when you run a TOR relay, you can choose which ports can be forwarded through your machine, so you can restrict it to e.g. 80 and 443, which will keep you away from most potentially illegal stuff (mostly P2P, Tor already blocks port 25 by default).

EDIT: you can even not let any connections come out of the ring from your machine at all, and have it only forward connections to other relays.
