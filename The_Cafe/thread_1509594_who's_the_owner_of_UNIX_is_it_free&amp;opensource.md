### Post by Chesamo on 2010-06-14
UNIX is proprietary and closed-source. There are several derivatives that are owned by different corporations; Solaris by Oracle, HP/UX by Hewlett-Packard, AIX by IBM, etc..

Linux was written by [s]Linux[/s] *Linus* Torvalds, and while he technically "owns" it, it is a community project. It is not derived from any codebase, but its functionality is based on UNIX/Minix.
