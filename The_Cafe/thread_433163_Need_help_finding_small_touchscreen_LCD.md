What I have:

* 3 old computer cases. Plenty of steel to recycle into a custom chassis/lcd mount.

* Labtec speakers, $19 from Best Buy several years ago. They'll get disassembled/reassembled into the case, make it self-contained. The goal is aesthetics, not waking the neighbors.

* Mini-ITX board with processor and ram. Most have S-video/VGA/USB/Audio/etc. built in. The goal is compact.

* Metal mesh for cooling ports.
* LEDs for status.
* USB wifi
* Linux
