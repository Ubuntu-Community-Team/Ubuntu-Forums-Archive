### Post by NASA NICK on 2009-06-16
I ran my own service company for 24 years. I never needed any document, or contract for simple repairs. All I needed was my word it would be fixed or your money back. No one ever had to take me up on the offer. Just promise the customer you will do your best and never rest until the problem is resolved. In other words never let a computer kick your butt.

 For long term repair/maintenance contracts, YES. 

Good Luck.
