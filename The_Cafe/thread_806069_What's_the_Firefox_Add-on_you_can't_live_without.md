### Post by ice60 on 2008-05-25
i have loads i couldn't live without, i'd die without them. here are some that might not have been mentioned -


**Bookmark this page plus** - when you want to bookmark a page you navigate to the place you want the bookmark to go and click on the new option it puts in your bookmarks called 'Bookmark this Page...' (like in opera!)

**Paste and Go 2** puts a go option in the menu to let you paste a url and select go - right-click - paste - and - go lol

**second search** when you type something in the search box a second menu appears, you select the search engine you want to use for that search while keeping you default search engine for next time.

**Sort Bookmarks** you select in the preferences what you want it to do when you click the option it puts in the bookmark menu, i use it to put all my bookmarks in alphabetical order with folders first.

**URL Link** make urls clickable that aren't clickable.

**Toolbar buttons** adds loads of extra buttons you can drag to your toolbars to add functions.
