### Post by marx2k on 2007-02-05
Congrats on your first post. :) I have noticed that in every aspect of forums/newsgroups wether it's dealing with Linux, Horticulture, Emulation, Mathematics, Programming.. what have you... if you search hard/long enough, you will not need to make a post in most situations since you're re-asking the question others have asked before you.

A great search tool is google mated with ubuntuforums.org

So for all your searches, go to google and in the query field, put in "site:ubuntuforums.org <search term>"

Examples:
"site:ubuntuforums.org  dpmi"
"site:ubuntuforums.org installing wine"
"site:ubuntuforums.org xorg.conf"
