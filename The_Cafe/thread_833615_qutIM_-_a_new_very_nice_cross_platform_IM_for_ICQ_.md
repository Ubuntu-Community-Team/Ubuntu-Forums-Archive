### Post by toster13 on 2008-06-18
the 14th of June a new QT4 based IM called qutIM was released. It's small and fast. It's still developing. However it already has a many nice features - xstatuses, antispam bot, tabbed contacts, animated smiles, multiply icq accounts, different private lists and statuses and couple of extra things :)  It can send and receive files, as well. So, just try it and you will love it :)
The project site is [http://qutim.sourceforge.net/]("http://qutim.sourceforge.net/")
You can download qutIM from repository 
for Gutsy 7.10
deb [http://linux.intonet.ru/ubuntu/qutim](http://linux.intonet.ru/ubuntu/qutim) gutsy main

and for Hardy 8.04
deb [http://linux.intonet.ru/ubuntu/qutim](http://linux.intonet.ru/ubuntu/qutim) hardy main

Also you can download the fresh sources from SVN 
```
svn co https://qutim.svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/qutim/qutim qutim
```

And feel free to ask the questions on our forum [http://qutim.sourceforge.net/forum/]("http://qutim.sourceforge.net/forum/")
