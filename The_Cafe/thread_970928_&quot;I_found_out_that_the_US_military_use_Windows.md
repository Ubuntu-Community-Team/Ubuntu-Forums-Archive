He may have done it as a proof of concept, but it still was illegal as he was not paid to do so by the US government.

In my book, hacking is modifying a program to suit your needs. White Hat Hacking is when you are allowed to do so. Black Hat Hacking is when you are not allowed to.

Cracking is is when you make a "crack" in the security system and let yourself in. While this is not always "wrong" is is not something I would do without permission.

In a way, these terms overlap. But to keep myself sane, I choose to look at it this way. If the purpose is to use a piece of software on your computer, then it is "hacking". If the purpose is to gain access to files, or even to gain access to access to files, that is "cracking". 

I think this is right, but if it isn't please tell me how.
