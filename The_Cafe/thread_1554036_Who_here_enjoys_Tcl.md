No, I didn't make a typo in the title. It's Tcl, not Tlc ;)

Tcl, [according to Wikipedia,](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tcl) is a scripting language created by John Ousterhout. It's mainly used for [eggdrop](http://www.eggheads.org/) nowadays, but it has [many other uses.](http://www.tcl.tk/about/uses.html)

The reason I'm mentioning it is that I never hear anyone talk about it here. It always seems to be either Python, C, or some other language. Sure, Tcl isn't the most popular language, but there has to be others besides me interested in it?
