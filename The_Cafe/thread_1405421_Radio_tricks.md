### Post by sgosnell on 2010-02-12
The standard TV channels are in the FM band.  They can be received if you tune to them, although most FM radios won't.  I used to tune up TV stations on military FM radios.  It's nothing special, but you wont' find anything else.  Any standard FM radio or TV is designed to receive the entire band it's associated with.  You're chasing windmills, but have fun wasting time.
