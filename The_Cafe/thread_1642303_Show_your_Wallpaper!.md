### Post by Daniel0108 on 2010-09-16
[SIZE=5]Tutorial:[/SIZE]
[SIZE=4]How to attach images to your post:[/SIZE]
1.
Click on this icon [ATTACH]169612[/ATTACH]
2.
Look at the opened popup.
3.
Click on Browse, choose the picture(s).
4.
Click on UPLOAD.
5.
Close the popup.
6.
Click on the little arrow beside this [ATTACH]169612[/ATTACH] icon...
7.
Click on the picture, you want to insert into your post:
[ATTACH]169613[/ATTACH]
8.
Now its inserted into the post :)
9.
Watch your picture! :D

[SIZE=4]How to add thumbnails of images to your post:[/SIZE]
1. 
Click on this icon [ATTACH]169614[/ATTACH]
2.
Enter the URL of your image.
3.
The thumbnail of the image is now inserted into your post :)
4.
Watch your picture! :D

[SIZE=3]Hope this tutorial helped you,

Yours,
Daniel[/SIZE]
