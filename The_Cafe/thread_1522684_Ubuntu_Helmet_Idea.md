### Post by Roust on 2010-07-02
You got me thinking about this, and from my experiences messing around with electronics and gadgets, a really small scale build like you're planning will go alot better if you get most of it as pre-made as possible. 
 
Home tinkerers will have a very very hard time getting their pieces down to the scale needed.
 
If I were to do this I'd adapt your plan a bit. Instead of a generic mobo + HD + projector system I'd try hack and mod a psp or other portable device to fit my needs. I'm not sure how well the screen will work as a visor, but I think it will work better than a small projector would. 
 
Howto Install Linux on PsP;
 
[http://www.hacker.co.il/psp/bochs/](http://www.hacker.co.il/psp/bochs/)
 
 
Howto tear down PsP;
[http://www.llamma.com/PSP/PSPTearDown.htm](http://www.llamma.com/PSP/PSPTearDown.htm)

[IMG]http://wearable.bozonics.com/wearable-008.jpg[/IMG]

As for the screen, I see two options, either have a small one mounted on your forearm, or use a projector mounted either on the helmet or on your chest and project the screen onto walls.
