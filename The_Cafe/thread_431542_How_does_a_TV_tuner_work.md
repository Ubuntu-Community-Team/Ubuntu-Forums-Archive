### Post by DoctorMO on 2007-05-03
Er, tv isn't one thing. it's a whole bunch of technologies which you may or may not want to use.

I have a bt878 with on-board sound with a Philips analogue coaxial tuner; a frame grabber based pci tv card with an output of 640x400 perfect for PAL and NTSC traditional wide band transmitions of visual information.

The screw in kind are normally cable connections and a completely different kind of card would be needed to decode it.
