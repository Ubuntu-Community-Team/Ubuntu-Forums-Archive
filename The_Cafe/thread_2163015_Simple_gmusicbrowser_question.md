### Post by Toz on 2013-07-16
Not sure if this is what you are looking for, but in some layouts, for example "Lists, Libary & Context", you get a stop button. If you right-click the Stop button while the current song is playing, it will stop at the end of the song and not auto-advance. However, it seems you manually need to do this for every song you want to stop at the end of.
