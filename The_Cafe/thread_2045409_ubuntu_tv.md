### Post by jhodapp on 2012-08-23
The Ubuntu TV team is definitely in active development. We are currently in the process of getting Unity in line with the user experience that we expect. We have designs for most of the other components that make up a TV. You can expect some pretty exciting things in the next 2 release cycles of Ubuntu.

Thanks everyone for your interest in the product. Please keep your enthusiasm up and join us in #ubuntu-tv on irc.freenode.net. Also join us at the upcoming UDS-R in Copenhagen (or virtually online) and you can help influence the product. We expect to have many specific ways that a diverse set of skills can participate in relating to the project.
