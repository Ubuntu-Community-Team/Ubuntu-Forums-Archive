### Post by KiwiNZ on 2005-07-08
[QUOTE=lowlux]how are you guy on motorcycles? do you see a lot of people riding? 

where can i look up laws for NZ? gun laws motorcycle laws.... laws.[/QUOTE]

Gun Laws, if you have a criminal record you cant own one, Only persons over 20 can apply for and sit the examination to get a gun  licence, No hand guns are allowed except for sporting reasons and must  be sercured at a sports club. All privately owned rifles must  be securely  locked away in approved cases with the ammunition and fireing   mechanism in a seperate secure location. Tese are inspected by the Police. No Automatic weapons are permitted.

As for motor bike yep   no probs , you must have a licence , helmets are compulsory , you must ride with lights on and for the first 2 years you are limited in the power of the bike you  ride , that is 150cc.

Hope this helps
