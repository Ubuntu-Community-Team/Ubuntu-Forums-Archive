### Post by maniacmusician on 2007-01-29
Neverball is a pretty fun 3D game to play when you have the proper drivers. it's in the repos (sudp apt-get install neverball). It also comes with a game called neverputt, which is a 3d golf-type game with realistic physics. It's more fun than it sounds, but Neverball is better than neverputt.

Love the variety of levels in neverball, it'll take you quite a while to beat them all! so it will keep you entertained for some time. You can only play for an hour or two at a time, but that can span across two months or more if you play a few times a week.
