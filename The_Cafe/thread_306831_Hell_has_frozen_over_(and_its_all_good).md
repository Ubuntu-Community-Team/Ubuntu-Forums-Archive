### Post by Johnsie on 2006-11-25
These forums and the live support in Xchat were some of the deciding factors for me. It was very easy for me to get help and learn all the new stuff.

When switching over to Linux you might have to do some retraining of staff but I think maintenance costs would be lowered because of the stability and the fact that you don't have to pay for upgrades to Ubuntu. Windows is licensed for each machine and you need to buy a new license every time you upgrade to Windows+1 so in the longterm it is quite expensive. However, hiring Linux experts to do stuff can also be expensive as can getting enterprise level tech support. These forums are great but a large company cannot put trust in that kind of support, they need instant support from people who can be held legally accoutable.
