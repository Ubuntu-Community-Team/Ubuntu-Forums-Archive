### Post by TNT1 on 2010-07-29
I just bought a Labtec 2200 webcam, for ZAR69.99, so what's that, around 10 dollars US?

Anyway, It comes with cd's and drivers and all sorts, I figured, at that price, I'll just plug it in and see what happens. 

So I plug it in and nothing visibly happens. Go to skype, check  video options, and there is a webcam listed USB Camera at dev/video0. Run a skype test, works like a bomb. Took me longer to type this post than get webcam working.

Gonna take it with me when next I go to the office, and try it on a colleague's windoze machine. What's the bet I will need to load drivers, they will be the wrong ones, will have to break open the google, drink three cups of coffee, and it will still not be working properly;)

Ah well, simple story, but I dig my Linux for simplifying things like this...
