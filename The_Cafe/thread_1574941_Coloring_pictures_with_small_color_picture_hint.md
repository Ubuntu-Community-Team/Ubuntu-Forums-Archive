### Post by Legendary_Bibo on 2010-09-15
Color by numbers?

Or what you could do is take a coloring book image, add a transparent layer. Remove the white, make a new white layer and color in lighter pastel colors of the colors that go in each space on the white. Gimps' fill thing is weird. People told me you can mess with the threshold, but I have yet to find that. There's also the paths tool which can help to create your own image.
