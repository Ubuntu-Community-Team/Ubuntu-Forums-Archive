I was on the fence about this because typically, ultra wides and shorter (height wise), than "normal" monitors. I ended up buying this:

[http://www.dell.com/en-us/work/shop/dell-34-ultrasharp-curved-monitor-u3417w/apd/210-adtr/monitors-monitor-accessories](http://www.dell.com/en-us/work/shop/dell-34-ultrasharp-curved-monitor-u3417w/apd/210-adtr/monitors-monitor-accessories)

Not the best monitor for photo editing but I am not a professional photographer. I like this one because it is 3440x1440, which is actually taller than the 1928x1200 I replaced it with. I am happy and don't regret the purchase. If I had the desk space I would have gone bigger but I don't...
