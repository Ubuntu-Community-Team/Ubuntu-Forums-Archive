### Post by LinuxFanBoi on 2009-12-07
Okay,  So I'm back,  And yes my brand spanking new Toshiba laptop is fully functional with everything from touch pad to wireless operational.

What I learned.  Before pulling all your hair out trying to apply hacks and work-arounds to get your wireless adapter working, it's better to just e-mail the tech support guys at Realtek and politely ask them if they would send you a Linux driver. :D

Just be ready, because they will want to know if the driver worked,  and they will want you to share information with them so they can improve their drivers.  This shouldn't be an issue though as sharing is what Linux is all about.... right?
