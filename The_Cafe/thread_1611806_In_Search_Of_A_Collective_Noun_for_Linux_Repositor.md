### Post by msp3k on 2010-11-02
Hello fellow Ubuntineers,

I find myself in the position of managing multiple separate software repositories as part of my job.

This got me to thinking:  What would be a good collective noun for a group of Linux repositories?  You know, like "a murder of crows" or "an army of ants".  A friend once coined the collective noun for Unix sysadmins as "A clue of sysadmins."  :)

So I'm looking for people to complete this sentence:
A ____ of repositories.
