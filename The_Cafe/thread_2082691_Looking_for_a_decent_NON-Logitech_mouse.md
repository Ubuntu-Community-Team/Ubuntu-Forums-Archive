### Post by boogerama on 2012-11-10
I'm squarely on the Microsoft camp.  For me the fit is nice and they work as advertised.  

I'm currently using an MS Bluetooth Notebook Mouse 5000.  It's a little on the small size, designed more for travel, but I found the size does not impede usability.  If anything it it easier to use.  

I also have an MS USB wireless 3000.  It's a little bit bigger and also fits my hand nicely.  But the USB connector is full sized, not a mini, so for that reason alone I prefer my BT mouse.  It's just really aesthetics at that point.

Both have the scroll/click wheel but the 5000 has a click button on the left hand that forms perfectly with the design of the mouse.  You can't feel it when holding the mouse.  That button is ideal for going one page back in your browser.
