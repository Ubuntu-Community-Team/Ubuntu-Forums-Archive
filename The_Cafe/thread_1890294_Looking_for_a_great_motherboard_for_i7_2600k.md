### Post by Basher101 on 2011-12-03
go for the MSI Z68A boards. I got myself the MSI Z68A-GD65 (G3) for 140&#8364; 
it can handle up to 32 GB of DDR3 2133MHz Dualchannel memory, it is SLI ready and will give you one PCI slot when using SLI with two Dualslot cards. If you have the money go for the bigger brother MSI Z68A-GD80 (G3). 

I will use mine with a i5 2500k as the performance difference between it and the i7 2600k is not enough for me to pay another 100&#8364;....as a graphics card i will use a Gigabyte GTX 570 OC with their Windforce X3 cooler. Its much better than the stock card by nVidia..

p.s. the MSI boards with the (G3) at the end mark them as PCIe 3.0 ready for the next-gen Ivy-bridge processors by Intel and the newest upcoming graphics cards
