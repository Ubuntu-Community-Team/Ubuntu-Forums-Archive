### Post by mips on 2013-11-26
Just go read up on stuff osi model, routing, switching, mpls etc. Also know the comapny and what they do.

The assessment is probably something along the lines of aptitude & personality profiling. Did you ask them what the assessment is about?
They will probably put whomever they hire on a rapid development & training program to get them up to scratch anyway so I would not worry about prior experience/training as the program takes care of that if I'm not mistaken.

Good luck!
