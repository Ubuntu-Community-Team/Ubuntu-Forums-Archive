Hello Ubuntu fans

I've been working on my personal online portfolio in an attempt to gain employment after finishing university and part of the site is dedicated to showcasing my xhtml / CSS design skills. As a personal project I created a  propaganda web site for Ubuntu:

[http://theaccused.co.uk/sites/ubuntu/](http://theaccused.co.uk/sites/ubuntu/)

The site isn't intended for the general public but I would like your feedback whether such a site is a good idea?

I realise that Linux / Ubuntu isn't competing with Windows or any other OS, it just wants to be the best OS it can. However, I can't see the harm in some positive PR in the form of avocation sites. It worked for Firefox with their spreadfirefox.com site.

So what do you think? Should Ubuntu be pushed to the public more and is avocational [sic] websites the best way? What about viral marketing, or publicity stunts? Or will those antics only damage the Ubuntu image?
