### Post by RandomJoe on 2010-12-09
I haven't had or used an E-Machines PC in a VERY long time.  I've heard they are doing much better now than they used to.

However, the reputation they developed in their early years still lingers on.

Back then (around 2000) they were unmitigated disasters.  We used them for a short time at work, the salesmen were always looking for ways to reduce cost.  (For customers who didn't already have one, we supplied a computer with the software they used to control our HVAC control systems.  The computer was NOT a primary product.)  But we had such a high failure rate - especially with power supplies - that we were spending far more on the time we spent going to customer sites to repair / replace.  At one point we were even stocking spare E-Machine power supplies so we could reduce turnaround time.
