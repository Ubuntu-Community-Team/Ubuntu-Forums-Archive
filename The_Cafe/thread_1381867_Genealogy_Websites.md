### Post by obithius on 2010-01-15
I've used ancestry for a while,and it's pretty good.I've found things out that would have taken me a lifetime to otherwise unravel!
However,some words of caution.,,I only paid for basic membership,and you soon run out of things you can look at without having to upgrade; their automatic searches are great, and suggest loads of matches for you, but don't rely on them without checking yourself; and if you want things like birth certificates,you still have to pay for them and get them sent to you the old fashioned way.
Hope this is of some help.
Chris

### Post by Tristam Green on 2010-01-15
I use Geni.com - it's pretty nifty, and largely a "do-it-yourself" kind of site.  You make your family tree as large as you want, and it can suggest persons to connect to it.  It shows relationships like you just would not believe.
