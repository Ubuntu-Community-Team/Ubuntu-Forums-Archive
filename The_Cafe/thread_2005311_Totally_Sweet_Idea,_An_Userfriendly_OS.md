### Post by David Andersson on 2012-06-17
Efficiency. User friendly. Safety. These are sometimes conflicting goals.

Different Linux-distros make different trade offs. Any Linux-distro can be tweaked toward the user's preferences. You mention Mandriva. Surely you know of Mint that comes with more codecs preinstalled and have dvd install media.

If you have good ideas, or want to see other persons ideas, look at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Brainstorm/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Brainstorm/) . (Here some popular and very reasonable ideas: [http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/most_popular_6_months/](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/most_popular_6_months/) ). Be prepared to divide your vision into separate features that can be expressed clearly.
