Who do you have that's capping you?

In my area, there are no caps from the local high-speed providers, so I wouldn't be worried about doing a 700+ MB D/L. But obviously if there are caps by the providers in your area, that's a whole 'nother story.

You might try a local Starbucks' spot. You need to buy a gift card from them and activate it, but then that gives you (when last I was aware of the details) one hour free per day. If you could get it pulled down in an hour, that might work.
