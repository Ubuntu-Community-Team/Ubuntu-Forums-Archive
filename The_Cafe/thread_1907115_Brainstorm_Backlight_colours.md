Salutations!

So after some customisation and a little patience I've become used to the new Unity interface. However there are something's I'd like to see that would improve it drastically. I've made a entry on ubuntu brainstorm and I'm just trying to gather some support for it here.

[http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/29008/](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/29008/)

Basically instead of having backlight colours either all off, on when the program is running or worst yet always on (giving you a launcher will a horrible set of mish-mashed colour shortcuts) I propose having a changeable backlight colour for each of your shortcuts. Lets say you have loads of folder, application and utilty shortcuts. If you could assign all of the folder shortcuts say a red backlight colour and your application shortcuts a blue colour etc it would be much easier to find what your wanting at a glance.

Up vote please.
