### Post by ahaslam on 2006-07-29
I went to the motor show yesterday and was quite disappointed. I would like to know if any fellow petrol heads enjoyed the trip and what made it for you.

Here's my rant...

Few  British sports car manufacturers turned up. Only Lotus, Aston & TVR showed but you had to wait to get close.  I couldn't believe that Caterham, Westfield, GTM, Noble & Ultima were not there. 

Likewise there were other sport/super car manufactures missing from the event, like Ferrari, Lamborghini & Porsche. Popular main stream manufacturers such as Audi, VW, Skoda & Suburu were also missing. 

Even if a certain manufacturer was there, in a lot of cases they didn't have their whole range on display. An example of this was Vauxhall, having about everything bar their two sports models, the Monaro and the VX220. Also there was no Mustang or GT40  on the ford stand and no Viper in the Doge display. 

The list goes on... very disappointing.


Tony.
