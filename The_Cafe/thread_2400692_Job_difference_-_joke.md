[COLOR=black]    Job Difference[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]A Lexus mechanic was removing a cylinder head from the motor[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]of a LS460 when he spotted a well known cardiologist in his workshop.[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]The cardiologist was there waiting for the service manager to come[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]and take a look at his car when the mechanic shouted across the garage,[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]"Hey Doc, want to take a look at this?"[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]The cardiologist, a bit surprised, walked over to where the mechanic [/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]was working.[/COLOR]


[COLOR=black]The mechanic straightened up, wiped his hands on a rag and asked,[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]"So Doctor, look at this engine. I opened its heart, took the valves out,[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]repaired or replaced anything damaged,[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]and then put everything back in, and when I finished, it worked just like new.[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]So how is it that I make $30,000 a year and you make $500,000[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]when you and I are doing basically the same work?"[/COLOR]


[COLOR=black]The cardiologist paused, leaned over, and then whispered to the mechanic.....[/COLOR]

[COLOR=black]"Try doing it with the engine running."[/COLOR]
