### Post by uraldinho on 2008-05-26
have you installed KDE4? 

If you are running KDE4 and KDE3 from the same installation, you are very likely to have two versions of the same application. 

Go to the settings, and make sure your new ktorrent points to the old temporary directory. for example

/home/name/.kde/share/apps/ktorrent/

you might notice that in KDE4 applications the link is 

/home/ural/.kde4/share/apps/ktorrent/
