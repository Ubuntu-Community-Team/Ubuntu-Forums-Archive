In the last week, the following major bugs have been fixed on my system:

2) NetworkManager errors on shutdown.  Now gone. (This is due to the fact that I downloaded nm-applet 0.7.0 from the PPA, not an Ubuntu update per se)

3) Flash crashing sporadically.

4) JavaScript drop-down menus being drawn behind SWF objects.

With these major issues out of the way, I feel like I have a Linux distro that I can show off again. :)
