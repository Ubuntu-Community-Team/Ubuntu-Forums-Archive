### Post by schauerlich on 2009-08-23
If you want to have any chance of winning, play one of the use map settings games... Instead of playing a normal "here's your base, now fight until all their base are belong to you" games, you have special scenarios set up and you play in that context. Defense games are especially popular. Instead of having a "normal" base, you have one building unit (SCV or probe - although sometimes you get a hatchery for drones) and you build defensive buildings (cannons, bunkers, etc) to defend against waves of enemies that pass by without attacking.
