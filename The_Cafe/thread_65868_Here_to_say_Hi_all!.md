### Post by Brunellus on 2005-09-15
[QUOTE=CYHPT.net]Thanks... :P Please give me some useful tips if I needed..[/QUOTE]
 useful tip 1:  read all of aysiu's posts to noobs.

useful tip 2:  don't sell yourself short.

useful tip 3:  buy a good, non-distribution-specific Linux book, and read that.

useful tip 4:  when asking for help, GIVE US INFORMATION.  Hardware (specific makes, models, model numbers), software (version numbers)...if we have seen/can reproduce the problem, it's easier for us to come to a solution that helps *you*.

useful tip 5:  remember that any help you get here is offered in the spirit of ubuntu--humanity towards others.  Never, ever, make unreasonable demands on this kind of generosity, or you'll find yourself being (politely) roasted.
