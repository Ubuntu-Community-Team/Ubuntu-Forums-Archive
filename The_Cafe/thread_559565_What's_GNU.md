### Post by IYY on 2007-09-25
Gnu is an animal, but it's pronounced like 'New'.

This same pun is used both by the game ("What's Gnu?" pronounced like "What's new?") and the operating system, as Richard Stallman says in his talks. Back then, they used to talk about "The GNU operating system", that sounds like "The new operating system".
