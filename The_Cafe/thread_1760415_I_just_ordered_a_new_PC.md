The difference between nvidia and AMD (Newer cards = AMD, not ATI) is pretty simple:

ATI / AMD = Better at **Textures** (this often causes MMORPGs to play faster, etc. on most occasions.)

nVidia = Better at **Particle Calculations** (this means nVidia is better at rendering complicated systems with more special effects that are also 3D in itself. With its new tessellation systems on the 5xx (570+) series, it does this even better. When dealing with dynamic hair, etc. nVidia would wipe the floor with AMD.)

Their latest GPUs: AMD 6990 and nVidia 590 are both dual-GPU systems, and cost almost exactly the same price. (Nvidia's side: Sapphire's costs 699 USD as the cheapest.)
