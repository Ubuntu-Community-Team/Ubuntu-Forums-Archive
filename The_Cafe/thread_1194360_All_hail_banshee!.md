### Post by bryonak on 2009-06-22
I mainly use Audacious, but as far as "suites" go, it's Rhythmbox for me.

Banshee is very nice, but switching artists and albums is painfully slow with collections bigger than 20'000 songs. It also still doesn't have a genre browser, which is crucial if you listen to several categories of music.

Listen is very young and for me it shows... frequent crashes and UI bugs. And it's slow on bigger collections aswell. Still it's quite promising and I'll try it from time to time.

Exaile is cool all around, but I don't like the browser. Apart from that it's OK.

Songbird...too ugly for my taste, doesn't fit in my environment and has a sluggish UI.

Rhythmbox has it's share of flaws too... mainly weak tag editing and a limited (but still useful) feature set compared to the others. But it's the fastest of the lot and just gives me the least reason not to use it.
