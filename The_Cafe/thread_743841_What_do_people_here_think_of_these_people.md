### Post by Tundro Walker on 2008-04-04
Script kiddies are the necessary evil, the "trial by fire" for our modern technology.  Some may think they're stupid, and some may get ticked when they bomb someone's computer, but really, you're just getting angry at the messenger ... the messenger that's letting you know your OS sucks.

Bill Gates built up Windows in a bubble of pink skies and green fields, thinking everyone would just use it for good.  Then folks bomb the crap out of each other with it.

It's like how Richard Garriot, the creator of the Ultima series, was shocked when someone killed him during an early session of Ultima Online.  He not only shocked that he died, but more so that someone would have the audacity to attempt to attack his character.  The real world, of jerk kids and a-hole players had suddenly shattered the chivalrous do-gooder bubble he had long-developed his game worlds in.  Ultima Online then proceeded to be nothing more than gangs robbing people, bullies PK'ing noobs, and online megalomaniacs creating their own personal factions/fiefdoms.

We may not like it, but we need things like this to help stress test everything around us.  Would you rather someone bomb your butt on IRC, and that flaw get sorted out in the OS while it's still just a nuisance, or have that flaw still exist and someone bomb the OS while its in use at some mission-critical task, like air-traffic controlling or something?

These script kiddies play a vital role in society ... and they do it all for free.

Of course, that's not to say I wouldn't want to smack the little runts for being jerks, though. :)
