### Post by Mazza558 on 2008-09-10
[http://www.inference.phy.cam.ac.uk/dasher/TryJavaDasherNow.html]("http://www.inference.phy.cam.ac.uk/dasher/TryJavaDasherNow.html")

The idea of this program is to allow you to "type" with just the movement of a mouse. Imagine a sentence you want to type and follow the letters, one at a time, and the more common words will be easier to create. It's a very enjoyable way to type for some reason, perfect for touchscreen things. Give it a good try (yes, longer than 20 seconds).

You can change settings (like speeding it up to a usable speed) in the options at the top.

It's also in the repos, so just search add/remove for "dasher".
