### Post by marenum on 2010-02-11
You should be able to find guitar picks at pretty much any music shop, it doesn't have to be a guitar shop. They're really cheap too, now you just have to decide on a thickness.

I've played with the corner of a drivers license before. I didn't care for it but it could still work. Picks come in tons of different sizes, it's all about comfort and preference. If you're not sure, anything in the middle is a good starting point, I play with Fender mediums.
