### Post by KiwiNZ on 2007-02-08
Hi friends

My presence has been a lot less to nearly nothing over the last few weeks. 

As some of you know I have been confined to a wheel chair for a couple of years now. Well I decided that I have had enough of it and have been working on getting out of the thing.

So I have taken time out to try and do it 

Its been very hard and I have made some progress and can manage to walk short distances ,( I have reached 10 meters) unaided.

Small steps start any journey. Put enough small steps together and you complete the journey.

I still have my target to do this [http://www.cyclechallenge.com/](http://www.cyclechallenge.com/) . Its the Taupo Challenge , 160KM cycle ride , very hard very hilly. But its my goal .

So I havent forgotten Ubuntu Forums and I am still passionately behind this fantastic Forum.

So please forgive my absence and I will be back to full speed soon
