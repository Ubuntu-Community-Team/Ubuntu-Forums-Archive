### Post by Zorgoth on 2010-08-03
Discrete -

 1 : constituting a separate entity : individually distinct <several discrete sections>
2 a : consisting of distinct or unconnected elements : noncontinuous b : taking on or having a finite or countably infinite number of values <discrete probabilities> <a discrete random variable>
([http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/discrete](http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/discrete))

A discrete graphics cards falls under definition 1, as it is a graphics card which is separate from the rest of the system.


     Discreet -

 1 : having or showing discernment or good judgment in conduct and especially in speech : prudent; especially : capable of preserving prudent silence
2 : unpretentious, modest <the warmth and discreet elegance of a civilized home — Joseph Wechsberg>
3 : unobtrusive, unnoticeable <followed at a discreet distance>
([http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/discreet](http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/discreet))

There is no such things as a discreet graphics card.  Well, I mean, perhaps your graphics card doesn't bother you much or knows how to handle things quietly, but I don't think that's what people mean when they talk about "discreet graphics cards."
