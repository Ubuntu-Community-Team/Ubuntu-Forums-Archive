Having used a backpack exclusively for work travel the past year, I can say point 2 ain't happening.  Although, leather is better for that than nylon, as it absorbs a little, and generally stays cooler to begin with.

And I was going to suggest a Custom Hide leather backpack, but your budget pretty much killed that.  :P

Anyway, [this]("http://www.customhide.com/original_backpack_large.html") is the backpack I'm sporting now.  Built to last, and I think it could hold about 4 17" laptops.  It could definitely hold 4 15" laptops.  I can carry 2 glass gallon jugs in it, to give you an idea of the size.

Go leather, you'll thank me.  So will your wallet when those cheap backpacks split their seams.  :lolflag:

ETA: Leather isn't waterproof, but it can keep your stuff dry for at least a little while.
