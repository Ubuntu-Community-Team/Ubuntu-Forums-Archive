### Post by Swagman on 2015-10-30
Just about any dashcam will do.

Obviously you wont be able to use the software that comes with it unless it works under wine but it's the video footage that matters.
I bought an expensive BlackVue 550GW front & rear cams but to be honest, some of the cheaper cams record far clearer video.

Something like the [**Mini 805**](http://www.amazon.co.uk/E-PRANCE-Ambarella-6-glass-G-Sensor-Detection-Without-build-8GB-Memory/dp/B00SUKV6F0) is plenty good enough.


Here's some footage from my cams.

[**Daytime footage**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IpncYcM7Fyo)
[**Nightime Footage**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rsOtrBUZNwk)

Just pull the footage into Openshot or similar and upload the output to Youtube
