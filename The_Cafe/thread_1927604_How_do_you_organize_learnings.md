### Post by BeRoot ReBoot on 2012-02-18
I used to have trouble with remembering much of anything, so I've taken to writing down everything. I've kept an immaculate journal of everything that's happened in my life for the last five years. It's all plaintext (recently I've been using emacs orgmode) and easily searchable. I only keep it locally (and encrypted) due to the personal nature of most entries, however.
