### Post by papangul on 2010-03-26
Earlier nokia music store(India) was accessible on a pc only with the windows + IE combination. The downloaded files were in wma format, and there was a complex scheme of license check and other rules regarding ripping etc. 

Today the rebranded drm-free 'OVI Music Store' has been launched in India and it works great on Ubuntu. The downloaded files are in mp3 format.
*Screenshot*: [http://imgur.com/wFssb.png](http://imgur.com/wFssb.png)

The "Comes with music' service has also been rebranded as 'OVI Music Unlimited'.
Here is the related news story:
[http://news.softpedia.com/news/Nokia-s-Comes-With-Music-Becomes-Ovi-Music-Unlimited-138507.shtml](http://news.softpedia.com/news/Nokia-s-Comes-With-Music-Becomes-Ovi-Music-Unlimited-138507.shtml)
