### Post by stinger30au on 2008-12-29
Unfortunatley for me in not a programmer. Nor am i any good at creating amazing works of art in any kind of graphics program even as simple as Tux paint (my 3 year old does better drawings then me) so my contributions are limited to this community.

so, my contribution back to the Linux world is one of my Dads strawberry liqueur recepies, perhaps you could make it for your next Linux meeting in your hometown

[and in true linux style id like it to be covered by the linux gpl]("http://www.linux.org/info/gnu.html")

you will need
 
500 gram sugar
500 gram washed & hulled strawberries
600 millilitres of brandy or whiskey

put sugar & strawberrys in 4 litre  dish
stir well & cook on high for 15 min in microwave
stir half way thru cooking
remove from microwave & add brandy or whiskey
put in to air tight container or bottles .
store in fridge for 2 to 3 months
makes 750 mililitres

if u leave it for 12 months and have it for next christmas it will knock your socks off i assure you.

also goes real well as topping on ice cream
