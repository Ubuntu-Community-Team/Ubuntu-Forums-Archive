### Post by az on 2005-11-07
There is a bug report item in the menu.  Click on it.  It brings you to bugzilla.ubuntu.com

Go there and search for your bug.  Someone may have already filed it.  If that is the case, read it and add your comments to it.  If not, go ahead and create a new bug report.
