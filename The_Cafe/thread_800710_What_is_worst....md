### Post by RiceMonster on 2008-05-22
The worst I had with C was I just looked at what the program was supposed to do, not how the program was supposed to written, so I wrote this fantastic code that was really efficient and very small. So I showed my professor the day before it was due and she said "that's good, but I wanted you to do a two-dimensional array" so I said "WHAT!? but this way works so well with a one dimensional array!" and she said "maybe it does, but part of the purpose of this lab was to learn two dimensional arrays".
