### Post by p_quarles on 2008-03-27
There are a handful of Ubuntu developers who hang out in this forum, but the vast majority of us are simply users. 

From what I have observed, many open source developers participate because they enjoy programming and have an interest in improving large, collaborative projects. Some, but not all, join open source projects out of a political belief in software freedom. Many others are paid for their work, though this obviously does not preclude an interest in programming nor a belief in software freedom. 

The F/LOSS development community is huge, so the answers to your questions are going to be inclusive of virtually all imaginable answers, I think.
