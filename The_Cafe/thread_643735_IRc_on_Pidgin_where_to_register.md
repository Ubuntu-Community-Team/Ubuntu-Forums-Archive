### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-12-18
Hi. Open  Pidgin, go to "Accounts->Add/Edit", and fill it out like this:

* Protocol: IRC
* Screen name: (choose your nickname here)
* Server: irc.ubuntu.com
* Password: (Only if you registered your nick; [see here]("http://freenode.net/faq.shtml#registering") for details)

And that's it. Now to connect to a channel, type:
```
/join #CHANNEL
```
just substitute CHANNEL for the correct name. To find which channels are available, use:
```
/list
```
or go to "Tools->Room List" in the Buddy List window, and press the "Get List" button.

If you have questions, try #ubuntu:
```
/join #ubuntu
```
