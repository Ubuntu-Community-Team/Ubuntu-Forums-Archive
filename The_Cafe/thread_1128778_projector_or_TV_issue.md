### Post by RandomJoe on 2009-04-18
I prefer projectors, but I'm also not the typical viewer.  I rarely have the screen on, when I do want to watch I want it to look good and be BIG.  So bulb life isn't too much of an issue.  My projector is now three years old and still on the original bulb.

Of course, I have the living room to handle it - something like 16' wide by 36' long.  (That includes the kitchen - it's basically one big room.)  So my 11 foot screen on the wall looks nice!

For the money, I can get a much larger picture than with an all-in-one TV.  That's the original reason I went with it.  Right now I just have a standard-def projector, all I watch is regular DVDs anyway with the occasional OTA TV station through a converter box so it's fine.  It also makes playing games with the Wii a lot more fun too.  My nephews always want to come over for game nights.

Many warned I wouldn't like the brightness, but there again I'm happy.  The windows are at the far end of the room from the screen, so sunlight is rarely an issue.  I can have all the inside lights on and still see the screen just fine.  Still a lot better if the room is dark, though.
