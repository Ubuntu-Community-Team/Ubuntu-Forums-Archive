### Post by quinnten83 on 2008-06-30
i am not so crazy about tllts, I feel they blab on and on needlessly for over 2 hours. They may have news, but I don't have the attention span.
Also, the production quality is lacking. Because of the lag in VOIP software, you sometimes get akward silences, or everybody talking at once and it becomes hard to follow. And it often times feels like they didn't prepare their show properly and the sound quality is a bit subpar.

I do appreciate that these guys make the effort, cause lord knows, I wouldn't even know where to start.

As far as podcasts go, I like UbuntuUK a lot, it is very professional, the only thing they lack is the spontaneity of LUGradio, but otherwise extremely well done.
I also love linux outlaws, because the german accent is endearing and they post frequently..!
