### Post by kaldor on 2010-04-24
It's looking like a fancy goldfish to me. Hard to tell by your photos, but it looks to be the Oranda type. I could be wrong though. 

Also.. don't try the goldfish like crap by putting it a bowl lol. They can live up to 30 years under good care, and they can get quite big. They make a LOT of mess and require a lot of filtration in a large tank, and they need lots of water changes. If you are planning on plopping it in a bowl, or in another aquarium, I recommend you give it to somebody with more space/time to care for it. It's such a shame that people treat goldfish as creatures that should live in a tiny, crappy bowl and only live a few weeks. 

I'm a fishkeeper/breeder if you're wondering :)

Good luck!

Edit: from the looks of your pictures, it seems to be in an aquarium. I'd be very careful if I were you, because goldfish LOVE to eat live plants and can get a bit pushy and controlling to smaller fish during feeding. Not to mention they make gallons of mess.
