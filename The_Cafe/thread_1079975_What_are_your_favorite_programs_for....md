[font=arial][color=red][size=1]**&#8224;&#8224;**[/size][/color][/font] I list Banshee not so much because I'm really "in love with" it but because, of all the open-source audio players, it's the least cumbersome, both in terms of the UI and the back-end operations.

As with email apps, I look forward to the day that the open-source alternatives become better than the commercial equivalents.
