### Post by MikePnKY on 2006-10-03
But I'm hoping that the end justifies the means...

I've been holding off on posting this until I found out if it was going to work or not, because if it didn't, I was in for a major guilt trip.  My mother has embraced online banking, and bill paying, and shopping, and chatting and this, that and the other.  I had been trying to get her to allow me to switch her from XP to Ubuntu for the security aspect of it.  She was concerned about online security...but not enough to give up her windows.

So, I used printscrn to get a picture of her XP desktop....then used the picture as her desktop wallpaper, moved her taskbar to the top, and set it to "auto-hide".  When she booted up her computer, NOTHING that she clicked on would work.  After a couple of hours of reboots, she was so fed up that she was willing to try ubuntu.  

That was 2 weeks ago, and she's loving it.  She chats with her old AIM buddies on Gaim, has Thunderbird for her email and is just generally happy with the entire package.  

If you wanna flame me for being a bad, manipulative son, go ahead...but I feel like shes lots safer online now.
