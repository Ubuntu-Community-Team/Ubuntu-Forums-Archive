### Post by samalex on 2010-08-10
You guys named virtually every one of my fave movies/documentaries :)
- Revolution OS
- Pirates of Silicon Valley
- WarGames
- AntiTrust

Though not a film, if you can find the Nova episode 'The KGB, The Computer, and Me' ([IMDB]("http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0308449/")) from 1990, it's an episode about an astronomer named Cliff Stoll who spent a year tracking down a KGB hacker who was breaking into the LBL computers at Berkeley from Germany, all starting from a 75 cent accounting error.  It's based on his book The Cuckoo's Egg which is a great read!  

As for other films, though it's a kids book, the Percy Jackson books are great, and the movie is okay -- though the book was 10x better (movie was changed SO much from the book).

Hmm, others that come to mind:
- Daryl
- The Explorers
- Weird Science (someone else mentioned, but great flick)
- The Net
- Electric Dreams is great, though not about a hacker that gets famous.  One of the BEST soundtracks!
- Takedown (supposed to be about Mitnick -- not accurate at all, but a good movie)
- Freedom Downtime (neat documentary put out by the fine folks at 2600)

And of course -- Office Space!
