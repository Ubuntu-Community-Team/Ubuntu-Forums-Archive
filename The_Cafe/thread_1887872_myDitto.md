### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2011-11-28
The my&#8211;Ditto products are classified as a NAS device (network attached storage). Most NAS devices have a very simple computer that does one thing, make a hard disk network accessible. They range from a hundred dollars to thousands of dollars depending on what you need.

If you already have a server setup at home then a NAS would be a little redundant. The main advantage of a NAS device is simplicity and ease of use since you don't have to setup a server to use one. Certainly you could have a server and a NAS device but personally I'd just add hard disks to the server and share them on the network which would be more cost effective and flexible than a commercial NAS. Here's some more information on NAS devices in general.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Network-attached_storage](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Network-attached_storage)
