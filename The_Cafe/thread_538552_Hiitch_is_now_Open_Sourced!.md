### Post by Hiitch on 2007-08-30
We want to thank all those in the Ubuntu community who have provided us with your encouragement and feedbacks on our previous posting.

We are now officially open sourced. For those who have not read our previous posting, we are a social networking company that develops social networking products. We have developed a new social platform for the Linux desktop called [Hiitch]("http://hiitch.com"). We would like to invite all Ubuntu users to join us as part of our effort to provide Linux users with a unified desktop social network.

On top of the default network that we provide for free, Hiitch also allows you to hack on it to develop features that you desire so as to stay connected to you family, friends, company or organization. Check us out and if you are interested in what we are doing then help us spread the word.
