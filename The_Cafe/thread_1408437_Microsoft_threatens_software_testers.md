### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2010-02-16
I absolutely get what you mean, phantomjoker. It's really lame that they sucker people into testing their product for free. They've convinced many that it's somehow a magical thing to be running pre-release software, which is very, very far from the truth: [http://www.penny-arcade.com/comic/2010/1/25/](http://www.penny-arcade.com/comic/2010/1/25/)

Very common for their XBox games (oh-boy, some Halo game is going to be in public beta tests in a month!).
