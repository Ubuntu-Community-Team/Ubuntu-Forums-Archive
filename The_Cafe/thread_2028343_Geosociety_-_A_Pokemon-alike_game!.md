### Post by sharks on 2012-07-18
[INDENT]My friend launched a game  sometime back called "GeoSociety" - for iPhone/iPod Touch and I thought it would be fun to see what you guys think about the game.
I personally am a fan of the game and spend a part of the day on it to  capture monsters and to mutate it. And of course, to complete the  missions.

Here's a link to the game. [http://itunes.apple.com/app/geosociety/id441449501](http://itunes.apple.com/app/geosociety/id441449501) or [click here]("http://itunes.apple.com/app/geosociety/id441449501")

So if you are unsure about the game, you can see a [gameplay here or read review.]("http://www.slashgear.com/geosociety-for-iphone-review-a-perfect-pokemon-alternative-23214994/") Do let us know what you think!

This is the description from app store if you're willing to read without opening the link.
> A unique and traditional Monster Taming, Battling, Breeding and Hunting  Role Playing Game. Inspired by old school Monster Battling Role Playing  Games games like Pokemon, Dragon Warrior Monsters and Revelation Demon  Slayer.

GeoSociety Highlights:
&#9733; A Complete Storyline and World to Explore.
&#9733; Over 350 Monsters to Tame, Battle and Trade.
&#9733; A Unique 3 vs. 3 Time-Based Battle System.
&#9733; Monster Party of 9. 
&#9733; 4 Unique Mission Modes including Zombie Mode.

&#9734;  Storyline Introduction: Today is your first day working for GeoSociety,  You're not really the prime candidate one would expect for the position  - considering you dropped out of college and aren't qualified. The  GeoSociety is the organisation charged with the research and  preservation of the GeoRealm and GeoMonsters. Your colleagues at  GeoSociety make it known just how they feel about your new position, but  after seeing some familiar faces your ready to start on your first  mission.

&#9734; Monster Development: 
Every Time your Monster Knocks Out another Monster in Battle it will gain some Experience. 
With  enough experience a monster will grow levels which provides you with  stat points to allocate to Attack, Defence, Speed or Intelligence. 
Some monsters will even Mutate automatically when they reach a certain level.

&#9734; What Reviews are Saying:
"One  of the best games on the app store right now.  9/10 Deep featured game  play with new features on their way. Definitely a contender for best  game of the month." - Mctron proudly presents The Nerd Variety Blog.

"If  you ever played Pokemon on any Nintendo handheld, you should feel right  at home with GeoSociety. The recently released iPhone game offers its  own take on monster-breeding gameplay." - [www.cells2.com]("http://www.cells2.com")[]("http://www.cells2.com")[/INDENT]
