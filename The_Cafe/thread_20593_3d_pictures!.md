These things are great, just cross yer eyes until you get 3 pictures instead of two, and let your mind's eye resolve the middle one.  :D

There's thousands of em out there, so post your faves.

I`ll get the ball rolling with a 3d pic of the view I have from my desk at work.

[EDIT]

Better explanation:   Cross your eyes until you can see 3 pictures instead of just two.  They will all be blurry, but don`t worry, just try to just concentrate on the centre image - and try to relax your eyes slightly, without uncrossing them.  Eventually you will be able to focus your eyes in the middle image and your brain will interpret it as three dimensional.  The more you do this, the easier and quicker it becomes - I can view these almost instantly.

It is possible to reverse the order of the pictures and view them without crossing your eyes, but whilst the effect produces less eye-strain, it is much harder to accomplish - even with practice.
