neither.  buy a camera that records to a mini dv tape.

reason?  long after dvds are obsolete, which will be in a year, mini dv tapes will allow you to record at a higher video quality AND you can start creating videos for the new format, be it blue ray or hddvd.  mini dv tapes(taken from wikipedia): 

DV(Mini Tapes) uses DCT intraframe compression at a fixed bitrate of 25 megabits per second, which amounts to roughly 3.6 megabytes per second or 4 minutes per Gigabyte.

Typical data rates for DVD movies range from 3-10 Mbit/s, and the bitrate is usually adaptive.

*END WIKIPEDIA COPY*

You can turn your mini dv tapes into dvds, its some work, but possible, i do it all the time.  So I would recommend MiniDV, you also get more record time on a tape.
