### Post by utabintarbo on 2006-12-08
Just to establish my bona fides, I have worked in the auto industry in various capacities for ~15 years. 

I think it is probably possible to *design* a car using OS techniques, but the infrastructure to *build* it would cost millions. To build in quantity, hundreds of millions. Include testing and compliance, billions. 

The best that may come of this is that some part of this project (or a derivative work)will be incorporated into a vehicle produced by an existing auto company. Or (more likely) the project leaders and lead designers get a (better) job at one. 

Creating software is cheap. Creating cars, not so much.
