### Post by darkoptix on 2005-06-27
Alright... i'm a xbox modder, and you can use a xbox harddrive in your computer or any other device, it just has to be unlocked. Since from what you said, the drive is locked, I dunno if it would work. The only way I know of getting access to a xbox harddrive is to unlock it first, or use the xbox to boot, and unlock there, using config magic or something.

You can always try, but I think once you try the DBAN or whatever, I think you will see it has troubles reading the harddrive.

If you have the key or xbox eeprom.bin file from the xbox that the harddrive is taken from... you can use a nifty program from ldots called xboxhdm. A linux bootcd which has unlocking, locking and formating options for xbox or new harddrives.

For more info... check the source [http://www.xbox-scene.com](http://www.xbox-scene.com)
