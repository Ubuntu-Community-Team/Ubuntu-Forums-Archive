### Post by artilec on 2009-03-23
I once to thought this Mark76 but have to say I'm now inclined to disagree.  Why?  because Ubuntu always has evolved on a micro scale. 

To introduce a new color scheme would be like having a macro event thrown into the mix.  It could have a profound effect on it's user base, which is not always a good thing when trying to maintain a reassuring familiarity and unity, it's the spirit of Ubuntu.  The end users don't see the ever changing code but they do see substance with color (I think). I have always changed my theme which would seem contradictory to my point, but with every new release experience the desktop spirit which connects me to these forums and the people etc. 

A slow assimilation of user base helps with moderation in these forums, a huge influx would disembody, it's bad enough moderating oddballs such as my self, as it is!

Anyway I've just woke up so I may be odd-balling again!
