### Post by wolfen69 on 2011-06-11
While perusing Craigslist yesterday morning, I saw an ad for 2 computers. At the end of the ad it said "name your price". Ok, sounds interesting. The 2 computers were a HP Mini netbook, and a Compaq Presario 15" laptop. The netbook has 1 problem though, a completely toasted lcd screen. So I hooked up an external monitor to see if it otherwise worked good. It did, so no big deal to order a new one for $50.

The Compaq laptop is in like new condition, but the owner said it is REALLY slooooooooooooooow. No wonder, it had windows vista on it. :( No big deal, I can always reinstall an OS on it.

But here's the kicker: I only paid $80 total for both computers! The compaq has a 1.6ghz cpu, 1gb ram, decent intel video card, and 80gb HD. Not a speed demon, but more than enough power for basic usage. The netbook has similar specs.

She originally wanted $100 for both, but after I saw the broken screen on the netbook, I asked if she would take $80 for them. "yeah, no problem" she said. I couldn't believe it. :)

I wound up installing ubuntu 11.04 on the lappie, and it runs like it was made just for that computer. I have to order a screen for the netbook, which is about $50, and then plan on reinstalling win 7 starter and selling it for about $170. 

Not bad, huh? I'm so glad I saw the ad soon after it was posted, and was the first person to actually show up at the house.
