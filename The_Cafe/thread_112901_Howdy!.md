### Post by jeepmanjr on 2006-01-05
I'm new to the Ubuntu forums and new to linux/Ubuntu in general.  Wanted to say "hey" and thanks for having me.  ;) 

I've fiddled with various Linux distros over the years, RH, Mandrake, Mandriva, Suse, blah, blah, and eventually "stumbled" across Ubuntu.  Did some research, saw the reviews, did the DL and installed.  All went well, Breezy found my hardware without any issues and here I am.

Initially, the plugin/codec/encoder/decoder gig was kind of a hassle.  But, I did my homework, discovered Universe/Multiverse, and everything works now.  Out of all of the distros I've played with, Ubuntu was by far the easiest to set up.  The sudo thing threw me for a loop at the beginning, but easily got used to it.  Besides, sudo su is pretty much the same thing!

So there ya have it.  A Noob sets up a fully functioning desktop in four days with very little experience.  If I can do it, I know most anyone can.  Just gotta get past that Micro$oft mentality!!  

Ubuntu has renewed my interest in computing.  Life is good.
