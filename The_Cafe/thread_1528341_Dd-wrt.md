### Post by gletob on 2010-07-10
I love my new DD-WRT router.  For years I've been running 3 wireless devices almost constantly in my room.  My Desktop, Laptop (Which is more of a second desktop for me), and my xbox.  Now I don't have to deal with wireless dongles or my desktops wireless card over heating.  I was a little skeptical of the ebay auction that I bough this router from, and the router has no brand name but I love it so far.

I've got it set up in repeater bridge mode that allows me to connect the router to my current wifi network, connect clients wired to it and access my current network, and boost the wireless signal of my current network.  I love my new router, and am very thankful to the DD-WRT team, for creating such great firmware.
