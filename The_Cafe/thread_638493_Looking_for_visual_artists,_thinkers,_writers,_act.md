### Post by siciliancasanova on 2007-12-12
Hey everyone, about a year and a half ago myself and a friend started a magazine called "Culture Lounge".  Our magazine is now online only and we are looking for individuals who would be interested in a project in their spare time that runs on the basis of creativity.

Maybe this is spam?  I don't know, we don't make any money from this.  We just do it for fun.  In house already we have a philosophy, visual arts, politics, and financial editor.  We are looking for anyone who can bring something creative to the table.

You don't have to do anything if you don't want to but if you would like to be a part of it or just hang out for a while and feel it out I will set you up on our site as a contributor and when you log in you can submit an article if you like.  If you have any artwork, writings, thoughts, send them my way to be reviewed.  Now that we're online only and have unlimited pages we really have room to put as much in as we want.

Our site is [http://cultureloungeonline.com]("http://cultureloungeonline.com")
