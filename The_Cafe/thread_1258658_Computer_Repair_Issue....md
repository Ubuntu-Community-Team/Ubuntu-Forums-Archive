### Post by sydbat on 2009-09-05
Check all the connections. Something might have gotten loose. 

Also, disconnect everything, then connect one item at a time and try to boot. (that is, only one item connected at a time for each boot attempt)

Finally, is there any burnt electrical smell? That would be a bad thing.

If everything seems OK and you still can't boot, you might have a fried motherboard, or a bad power supply, or the processor died, or, or, or (you get the idea)...
