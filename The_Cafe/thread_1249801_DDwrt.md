### Post by achianese on 2009-08-25
I just installed it on a linksys WRT54GL. It gives you tons of options that the default firmware doesn't. A very common reason people use it is to use their router as a bridge (to plug in wired components in another room). You can monitor bandwidth, change QOS settings, or see who's on your wireless network and what signal strength they're getting, for example.
