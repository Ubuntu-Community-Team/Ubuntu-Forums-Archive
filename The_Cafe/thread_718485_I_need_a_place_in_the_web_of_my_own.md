There are loads of webhosts out there...  I suspect that you will be inundated with suggestions.

[http://punbb.org/](http://punbb.org/)

### Post by ellis rowell on 2008-03-08
The trouble with most of these free hosting sites is that if you move anywhere else you then have a different address. The advantage of a paid for sight is that the domain address is your own, you can move it if you want. I don't see a lot of point in changing hosts, all my domains are on one host except for the latest which is a backup domain for one of the others and is on another host.  I have been with the original host for seven years. The only crashes have been caused by me and their service restoring the site has been very prompt.

### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-03-08
> **ellis rowell said:**
> The trouble with most of these free hosting sites is that if you move anywhere else you then have a different address.

Not strictly true.

Certainly, JWN allows personal domains to be hosted for free (although you do have to pay for one independently).  I think websitenetworks does too.

In fact, you can use a free redirect service if you want to maintain an independent url that you can move with you:
[http://www.uni.cc/](http://www.uni.cc/)

As an example - one of my own:
[http://www.epconsult.uni.cc](http://www.epconsult.uni.cc) - redirects to: [http://www.ugm6hr.jwnmedia.com](http://www.ugm6hr.jwnmedia.com)

As for PunBB - it is good.  I just experimented a little with an installation, but never took it any further than that.  It is easy to do though.
