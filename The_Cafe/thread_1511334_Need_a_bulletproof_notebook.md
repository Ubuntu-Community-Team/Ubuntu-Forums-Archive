### Post by ddrichardson on 2010-06-16
We use CF-29 and they're rock solid. I've knocked one of the top of a aircraft and it survived. Mind you, someone I know lost his temper and threw a blade pin (they're quite heavy) at the screen - it didn't survive that.

The ones we use on aircraft use Windows 2000 and run reasonably fast but they're very stripped down. There are CF-28 around that we use for aircraft paperwork and they run Redhat - they're perfectly usable although the trackpads are terrible.
