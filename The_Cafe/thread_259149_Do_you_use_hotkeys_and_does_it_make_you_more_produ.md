### Post by Bloodfen Razormaw on 2006-09-17
Using a mouse doesn't mean you can't use hotkeys.  I am more of a mouse person, but I still use the left half of my keyboard for shortcuts.  If you use KDE check out its mouse gesture features.  You can get the same effect as hotkeys (by translating gestures into key combinations, or even more powerfully into DCOP commands) in any application.  Perfect for mouse people.
