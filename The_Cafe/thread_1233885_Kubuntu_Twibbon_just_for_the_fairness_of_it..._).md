### Post by philip5 on 2009-08-07
Twibbons are a way to show support for a cause you believe in when you use Twitter. When I made a search for Kubuntu Twibbons I couldn't find any but there where several Twibbons for Ubuntuists. Just for the fairness of it all I made a little Kubuntu Twibbon and of course because that I'm a Kubuntu user my self... :)

If you also want to show the world that we (the kubuntu users) are out there then add the Twibbon to your Twitter avatar you too.
[http://twibbon.com/join/Kubuntu]("http://twibbon.com/join/Kubuntu")

A Twibbon is just an effect that layers over your Twitter avatar as long as you have joined the Twibbon cause of your liking and when/if you part from the Twibbon then the effect disappears and your avatar looks like normal again...

Make a little gesture and show the world that we (the kubuntuists) are out there!
