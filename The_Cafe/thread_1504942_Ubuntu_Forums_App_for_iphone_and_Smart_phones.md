A mobile theme suitable for mobile phones is a great idea. You may wish to check this out [http://www.vbulletin.org/forum/showthread.php?t=151186]("http://www.vbulletin.org/forum/showthread.php?t=151186"). On the other hand an app for Ubnutu forums that is subject to the approval processes and censorship of Apple or Microsoft is a terrible idea. Let us not forget that Ubuntu is a competitive product to major offerings from both of these companies. A well designed mobile version of Ubuntu forums will work with the existing mobile browsers in both these closed ecosystems without having to submit this site to a **competitor** for approval.

By the way Ubuntu forums as is actually works very well on my Android smart phone.
