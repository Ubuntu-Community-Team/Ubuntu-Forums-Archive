### Post by jatos on 2008-05-12
I am not going to saying for certain that you will be able to go out and buy a network and it will work, but from experience, I have not yet found a PCI card that does work out the box on Linux. The worst problem I have had is Linux not automatically loading the drivers when putting a second card in an existing setup.
