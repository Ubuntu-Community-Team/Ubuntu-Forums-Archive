### Post by airtonix on 2008-10-09
i wouldn't trust the ext drivers in windows with anything you dont want to loose, i tried them out a while ago and i lost a lot of data via corruption.

I also dont trust the ntfs drivers in linux for the same reasons.

the best most sure fire way is to use a usb stick, external harddrive formatted to fat32 or some form of network storage that has both NFS and SMB access.
