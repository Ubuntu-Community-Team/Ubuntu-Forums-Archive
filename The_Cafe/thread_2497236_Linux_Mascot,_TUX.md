Please forgive me if this is not the place to post my discovery.
Perhaps someone could advise me where to post my findings.

I discovered that CIBC is using Linus Torvalds Trademarked Mascot, "Tux"  to advertise their products.
I took a picture of Tux with the CIBC logo and the product they are advertising, "Vantage" embossed on Tux's chest.

In my mind, this is sacrilege because it goes completely against the principles of Linux.
