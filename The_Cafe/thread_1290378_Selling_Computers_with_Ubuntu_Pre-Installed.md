### Post by Dragonbite on 2009-10-13
> **ModelM said:**
> About a year ago I bought a Dell computer with Ubuntu installed instead of Windows. I think Dell still sells Ubuntu machines.

Dell, System76 and Empire Linux I think are the 3 biggest computer sellers with Ubuntu installed. Dell and System76 has their own forums here in Ubuntu Forums for their products specifically.

AFAIK, the GPL does not keep you from installing Ubuntu on a computer and selling it, you just don't get support with Canonical unless you pay for it yourself.  You could argue that the selling price is for the hardware and the service of installing it so that Ubuntu is technically "free".

The sticky point will be the restricted codecs and proprietary blobs, if you were to install them yourself before selling the computer then you are the one that has to ensure their legality.
