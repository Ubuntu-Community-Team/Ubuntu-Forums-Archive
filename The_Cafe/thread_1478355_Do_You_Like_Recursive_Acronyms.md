### Post by duanedesign on 2010-05-09
> **NMFTM said:**
> Are there any non-open source software projects that uses recursive acronyms.

Recursive acronyms are not limited to computing terminology. For example:

    * TIARA &#8212; TIARA is a recursive acronym

Some organizations have been named or renamed in this way:

    * BWIA &#8212; BWIA West Indies Airways (formerly British West Indian Airways)
    * GES &#8212; GES Exposition Services (formerly Greyhound Exposition Services)
    * LINK &#8212; Link Interchange NetworK, the UK ATM switching organisation.
    * Saab &#8212; Saab Automobile Aktiebolaget, formerly part of Svenska Aeroplan Aktiebolaget, also known as Saab.
    * VISA &#8212; Visa International Service Association
    * ADAP &#8212; ADAP Discount Auto Parts
    * Heil &#8212; Heil Environmental Industries Limited, maker of garbage trucks
    * KLuB &#8212; Klub Linux Bandung (Linux Club of Bandung city)
    * RAS - RAS Acronym Syndrome, the recursive version of RAS syndrome; in which the last letter is repeated as its own word.
    * OIL &#8212; Oil India Limited

*source -[ wikipedia ]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Recursive_acronym")
