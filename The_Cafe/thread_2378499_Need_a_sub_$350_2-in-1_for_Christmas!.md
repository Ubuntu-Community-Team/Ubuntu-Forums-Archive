The first thing I did was to change out the hard drive for a SSD (64 gig for about $20) and put in a $7 wifi card with bluetooth. Next I added more memory. (4 gig to 8gig) for about $50. I then installed Ubuntu Linux on it and it is running great and boot up in about 15 seconds. So if you total it all up I have $277.00 in a nice little 11" 2 and 1 laptop with over 10 hour battery life.
