### Post by Psumi on 2010-04-06
Percentage Of Yes = (Y/(10+M)) * 100

Where M = Amount of Maybes/Ask Agains and Y = the amount of yeses. The number under the Y is the amount of times you have to do the question. If you get a maybe/ask later, you have to add one more asking.

Use this with it: [http://web.ics.purdue.edu/~ssanty/cgi-bin/eightball.cgi](http://web.ics.purdue.edu/~ssanty/cgi-bin/eightball.cgi)

See if you can't muster up some luck with asking some "dangerous" questions *cough*

This formula is meant to "Acurately Determine" how likely your question will be yes.
