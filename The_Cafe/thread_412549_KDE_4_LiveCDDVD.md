### Post by GeneralZod on 2007-04-18
Just to save people the bandwidth - it's only worth downloading if you 

a) Plan to develop a KDE4 app/ port an app to KDE4; or 
b) Plan to contribute to the KDE core libraries; or 
c) Are morbidly curious :P

For a) and b), you should really be building from SVN, anyway.

Last time I looked, KDE4 had no "desktop" (just a blank, icon-less void where the wallpaper would normally go) as it is being phased out for KDE4, and the kicker may or may not be anywhere near functional (it, too, is being ditched for KDE4).  Things like KDE Games, Dolphin and (more often than not) Konqueror should work reasonably well, though.
