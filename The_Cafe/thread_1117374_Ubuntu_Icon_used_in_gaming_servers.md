### Post by kostkon on 2009-04-05
Yes, the *Ubuntu* logo is a registered trademark of *Canonical*, the company behind *Ubuntu*.

For more info check [here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/aboutus/trademarkpolicy").

For example, quoting this from the above page:
> Canonical owns a number of trademarks and these include UBUNTU, KUBUNTU, EDUBUNTU, and XUBUNTU. The trademarks are registered in both word and logo form.
