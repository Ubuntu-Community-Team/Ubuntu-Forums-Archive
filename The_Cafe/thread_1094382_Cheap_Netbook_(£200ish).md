### Post by Coreigh on 2009-03-12
My favorite source of cheap notebooks is a company who handles the leasing for large organizations. I found the company who handles the hardware sales for our local hospital and get used and off-lease items at about half of what they would cost of used-retail pricing.

So like the hospital gets 20 new notebooks or tablets and the old units go back to the lease company who sells them off to outlets and such. I am able to buy one off them for what the outlet would pay. I got a 3 year old HP tablet / notebook convertible for $240 (US). Similar used were $400 and up.
