### Post by Lux Perpetua on 2010-04-13
The arcade version of Gradius 2 is the most addictive shoot-em-up ever. It feels like a 16-bit game. It's incredibly hard, though, especially the last stage. 

IMHO, the NES port of Gradius 2 is overall a better game, though. It's 8-bit, obviously, but it's really good for an 8-bit game. (The weapons are slightly better, which generally makes things more fun. The music is almost universally better. And the rotating options are so cool!)
