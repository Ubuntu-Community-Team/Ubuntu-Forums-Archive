### Post by fidelandche on 2010-11-29
Hi all,
 
Not been about for a while, but I thought I would pop in to promote a UK based PAYG mobile company that is a lot cheaper than the rest. What you get is 4p p/m calls and 2p per txt. If you top up £10 once every three months and you have someone else on the same network your calls, txts and video calls are free, with no cap on usage. They also do these things called goodybags, which is a mix of minutes, texts and free internet if you buy the £10 bag. When I say free internet, I mean free internet there is no cap on usage. It runs on the O2 network and the sim cards will work in any O2 phone they do not have to be unlocked, other phones like the Iphone needs a bit of playing with to get the correct settings same as a blackberry, but most smartphones work. Also the network is only run by about 14 people so like Unbuntu they use a internet community to help sort out problems. if you take part in the community you can earn cash to put credit on your phone, have for yourself or give to charity, but the choice is yours ( I have only been on the network for a month and I will be getting £15 in December!!!) Also if you get sim off one of the members when you activate the sim and top up £10 you will get £5 free credit. In case anyone is interested the company is called GiffGaff
