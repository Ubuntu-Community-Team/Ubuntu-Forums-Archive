### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-03-16
The position of the buttons is clearly to:

a. Differentiate Ubuntu from OS X and Windows (OS X has Close on the outside left, and Windows has it on the outside right)

Having said that, I'll stick with them in the top-right corner... I didn't even know until recently that the order could be changed in Metacity, so back in 2005 I just got used to the Ubuntu default.

I do wonder what the interesting button will be. Probably one that pins the window on all workspaces and keeps it above all other windows; pretty useful.
