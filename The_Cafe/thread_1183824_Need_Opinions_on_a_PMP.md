### Post by Vostrocity on 2009-06-10
I would stay far away from another Archos player unless you really need high capacity since they have a bad business of model of selling addon packs that should be included in the first place (as you said). Between the S9 and the O2, definitely get the S9. They have similar specs, but the S9 has an AMOLED screen instead of a plain LCD and a more premium feel. You should also look at the the Samsung P3. If you still want WiFi in your PMP, take a look at the Walkman X (which is $100 more expensive than all the other players here) and the Touch (if you don't care about Linux syncing).
