### Post by vikramaditya on 2008-09-24
**All-American breakfast**
First, I slather lumps of lard into my electric deep fat fryer, sufficient to render approx 1 half gallon liquid.  While the lard is heating to 325 fahrenheit, the fry basket is filled with thick-sliced bacon, sausage (patties & links), diced potato, & chopped onion.  Once the lard is hot, the basket goes in for about 6 minutes, then 6 unbeaten eggs are added for the final 2 minutes.  The cooked items are then artfully arranged on a large platter & hot lard is generously drizzled over everything, followed by salt, pepper, garlic powder, and grated parmesan cheese.  This exquisite "feast for 1" is gobbled with heart-stopping haste & sluiced down with the tasty, leftover grease from the deep fryer.  Finally, I dial 911 and order a paramedical rescue team to my residence, then slip into a cholesterol coma.
