### Post by chucky chuckaluck on 2008-10-30
a typing keyboard only exists for the purpose of typing figures, like letters. a piano can be played without any association with letters (there are thousands of examples of keyboard players who never learned to read music). in fact, a piano's association is with pitch, while notation is simply a visual convenience. so, letters in music is a secondary concern while letters in typing is a primary concern.
