### Post by 3Miro on 2011-09-02
The most important thing about buying RAM is to get the right type for your model. Most RAM manufacturers have RAM finders on their web-pages that lets you search for RAM depending on the Motherboard (in your case it should be laptop model). Once you get the right RAM type, you can then buy it from newegg or any other place.

I have good experience with both Kingston and Corsair RAM, but that is on the desktop, I have not used RAM for laptops.
