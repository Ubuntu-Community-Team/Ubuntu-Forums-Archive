### Post by Jucato on 2006-06-27
Well basically, all bookmarks in Konqueror are visible in the bookmark toolbar. Go to the Settings menu, Toolbars and Enable Bookmark Toolbar. (or right-click on any toolbar, and you'll get the same options).

### Post by Jucato on 2006-06-28
Not an exact equivalent, but a sort of workaround.

Why not put all the bookmarks that you don't want to see in a single group/folder, so that they will be grouped as only one item on the toolbar? The bookmarks that you do want to see will be left as individual bookmark items on the toolbar. 

You can basically arrange the bookmarks like a directory, with folders/groups and stuff like that. Play around in the "Edit Bookmarks" option in the Bookmarks menu.

### Post by asimon on 2006-06-29
[QUOTE=Castar]Yes... I'm already aware of it. I was kind of hoping to have only a special folder with just the bookmarks I want for quick display... :roll: 
[/QUOTE]
Bookmarks->Edit Bookmarks, there choose your special folder with just the bookmarks you want to appear in the bookmarks toolbar, then in the menu choose Folder->Set as Toolbar Folder
Only the bookmarks in this folder will now appear in the bookmarks toolbar.
