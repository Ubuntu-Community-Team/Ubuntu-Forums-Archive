Hello folks

Some news from finnish Ubuntu site.

We have planned to break user record in our site on 27th July 12.00-13.00 (CET). The idea is to show, that there are plenty of Linux users and show it on publicity.

There is also one philosophy behind this. By visiting on the site, you will support the request for equal and technologically independent digital rights and services. The message is that technology should not set any restrictions for equal digital rights.

If you want to support this idea, please visit one of the following sites on 27th July 12-13 CET:


[http://virtuaali.bileet.be](http://virtuaali.bileet.be) 

[http://forum.ubuntu-fi.org/](http://forum.ubuntu-fi.org/) 

Everybuddy is welcome :) Just one click on right time and you're in! :KS
