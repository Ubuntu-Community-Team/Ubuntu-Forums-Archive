The sad truth about spam email and scams like this is that it's not your computer's fault and it's probably not even your email provider's fault. If anybody in your friend's list or contact list has their email hacked, your address is out there. They say a chain is only as good as it's weakest link. In this case your Internet security is only as good as your most computer illiterate friend. That's a pretty frightening thought, too.
