Hi there...

I would like to show something to all of you, and explain what is here for...

about 2  years back, I started living with a friend of mine in Kortrijk to cut down on rental-prices... We opened up our appartement to everybody who wanted to join in... Thus 'Teh Lair' came to be.

I'm a collector of gaming consoles, at the moment, I've got 24 machines :-)  Now, as we all know gaming is great! And its even better when you can play multiplayer-games cause then you can talk smack :-) 'Teh Lair' became a refuge for the students of Kortrijk, they came to play and get away from whatever it was they were 'running' from, school ( :D ), family, girlfriend etc etc... And we ( me and Undergod ) provided a place for them...

[PICTURES]("http://users.skynet.be/mistic/tehLair/")

Last year, me and 4 of my friends collected 60 PC's and transported them to India, where we installed them in a very poor school, we installed Linux on them and thought them how to use it... While I was there however, Undergod found a girlfriend and decided they wanted to share the appartement together and kindly asked me to leave... I didn't want the concept of 'Teh Lair' to disappear into oblivion, so I rebuild it, better then ever... 

This time however, I want to make this 'child' of mine grow, as big as it can get... Therefor, I need your 'help'... I've created a document aka 'teh Lair deal' that clearly states my intentions...

> **'Teh Lair Deal']
