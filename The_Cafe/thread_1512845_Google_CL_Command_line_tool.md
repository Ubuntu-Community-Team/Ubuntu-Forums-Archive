> Now you CL addicts have even less reason to use innovations from the past 30 years. 

[http://google-opensource.blogspot.com/2010/06/introducing-google-command-line-tool.html](http://google-opensource.blogspot.com/2010/06/introducing-google-command-line-tool.html)

CLI is pretty much the only feasible way to automate things efficiently. Thats why some folks love it!

For example
add this first line in the examples to crontab

$ google picasa create --title "My album" ~/Photos/vacation/*.jpg 

You automagically have a backup of any .jpg you put in your Photos/vacation directory. You can have it run as often or as little as you like.
