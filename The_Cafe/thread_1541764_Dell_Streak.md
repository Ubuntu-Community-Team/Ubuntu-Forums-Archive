### Post by brsmits on 2011-02-28
Ok, 2/28/11 here.

I have had the Dell Streak for about a month now, here are a few thoughts:

..........AWESOME!

All in all I absolutely love it.  Very quick and snappy with everything.  The touch screen is great and the capabilities are fantastic.  Strangely enough it does not seem to want to hook up to my tower though.  Don't know why.  I'm running 10.04 with all the updates and I can't seem to see the drive on it.  Oh well.

You will need to find a way to root it if you want to have full use of SFTP and SSH capabilities.  The terminal is fairly straight forward.  The touch keyboard takes a little getting used to. I came from an iphone 3g and I keep mis-typing.

All in all, for paying about 115 for it (upgrade through AT&T and being buddies with the guy at Best Buy) I am super happy with it.

If requested, I will do a full in-depth review on it.  Not just pre-coffee morning ramblings.
