> On a different note, I am on windows right now and the bottom bar has frozen on me. I wish I could open up a terminal and type in "killall windows-taskbar" or something and have it restart ;)

Typically Ctrl-alt-del will get you to the task manager and then you can kill explorer.exe.  If you really want to kill things from a command line then you can [download pstools]("http://www.sysinternals.com/Utilities/PsTools.html") and do so.
