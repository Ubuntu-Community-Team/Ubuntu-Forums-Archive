### Post by .Maleficus. on 2009-04-18
Easy and perfect are too different things.  For me, Ubuntu is very easy but far from perfect.  Arch to me is easy, and closer to perfect.  NetBSD was both hard and not perfect.  It's all how you look at it.  If you aren't happy with Ubuntu anymore, try something different.  Give Arch a shot, and if you can handle compile times give Gentoo a shot.  After trying those, you should have a good idea of whether or not Ubuntu is right for you.
