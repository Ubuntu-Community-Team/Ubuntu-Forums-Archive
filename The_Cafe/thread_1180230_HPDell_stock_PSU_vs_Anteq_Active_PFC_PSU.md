### Post by Dimitriid on 2009-06-06
With my compaq I had to change the PSU for my GPU requiements ( nvidia 7600GT ). I was also not happy that while this computer specifications were listed everywhere as mini-atx the PSU form factor is slightly different so I had to force the new PSU a bit to get it to fit. The main issue I have with a lot of prebuilt systems is an insufficient power supply unit. Most users would have little problem but I tend to upgrade my system a lot.

One vendor I've looked at in the past few days is ibuypower.com since its one of the few places that gives you brand choices for the PSU and the prices seem to be reasonable ( i.e. you can easily build a gaming system for around 600-700 )
