### Post by Welly Wu on 2015-07-26
I have an Amazon Kindle Unlimited subscription and this book is available to read for free with my subscription. I've read the preface and long essay in which the author self-critiques his book in 2009 years after its publication and how the open source and GNU/Linux ecosystem have evolved since then. This is a good history book to learn more about how GNU/Linux, free software, and open source evolved up until 2000 and it is important because I see that GNU/Linux is now being courted by hardware manufacturers and commercial software companies to sell more products and services. I'd like to read a new book about the commercialization of GNU/Linux in today's world, but I have yet to find such a book.
