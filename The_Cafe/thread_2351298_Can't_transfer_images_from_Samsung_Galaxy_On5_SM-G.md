### Post by einfeldt on 2017-02-01
Hi, I was having trouble transfering pictures from my Samsung Galaxy On5 SM-G550T1, which I got free with a sign-up on MetroPCS.  I had turned on the phone; attached it to my Lubuntu 14.04 machine; and got this error:

error initializing camera: -1: unspecified error.  

At no point did the phone ask me to allow it to attach to my Lubuntu computer.  Here is the solution:  turn off the phone while it is attached to the machine; turn it back on; and then the phone will ask you to allow the computer to connect to it.  Here are the specs on the phone:

[https://swappa.com/specs/samsung-galaxy-on5-metropcs]("https://swappa.com/specs/samsung-galaxy-on5-metropcs")

This problem occurred after I had updated my phone.  It had never happened before that time.  I am currently running Android 6.0.1 on the Samsung Galaxy On5, which is the Android version I was running when this problem showed up.

By the way, I had been using Verizon Wireless, and their prices went up and up and up.  I asked them for a discount in light of the fact that I was a long term customer.  They said no.  I went to MetroPCS, and now have a decent calling plan for 2 phones for $50.00 per month, and the Galaxy On5 works just fine with Lubuntu 14.04.
