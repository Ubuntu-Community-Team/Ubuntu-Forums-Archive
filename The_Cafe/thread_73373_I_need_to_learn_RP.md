### Post by Sirin on 2005-10-09
[QUOTE=Luke Redpath]What is an RP accent?[/QUOTE] 
Received Pronunciation (RP) is a form of pronunciation of the English language, sometimes defined as the "educated spoken English of southeastern England". It is the accent of British English most often taught to non-native speakers, and represented in the pronunciation schemes of most British dictionaries.
