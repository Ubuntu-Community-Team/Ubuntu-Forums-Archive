### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2009-03-23
Things that the original series predicted and got right for the most part:
Portable Data tapes (a good 10 years or so before they were invented)
The desktop computer (sort of because Kirks office computer was tied into the Enterprises main database)
The flip top Cell Phone (communicator obviously)
Tech beyond transistors (the microchip has taken over for old style transistors for the most part these days)
Silicon chips are actually very close to isolinear chips found on TNG too when you think about it, the tech is very possible within our timeline.
