### Post by 23meg on 2008-03-28
Cairo is a vector graphics library, SVG is a vector graphics format. What they have in common is that they both deal with vector graphics, and that Cairo can output to and render SVG. One is not based on the other.

Clearlooks has also been using Cairo for a while.
