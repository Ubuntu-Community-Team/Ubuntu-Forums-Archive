### Post by Derek Djons on 2006-01-08
I totally agree as some people mentioned the 'message' aspect.
For now it's difficult to advertise in a 'Linux-way' of being. Advertising is actually for profit-making companies since they need to make their product more known to an audience. Linux on the other hand doesn't want to achieve any profit goals so making commercials about the 'spirit' of Ubuntu would not be understand correctly by the audience. The terms ubuntu will be converted to: "Oh hey guys, listen up... there's this cool new OS and it's free. You don't pay shit!"

Advertising now would be actually suecide in my opinion, destroying your message which already found so many people without tv / website adds.
