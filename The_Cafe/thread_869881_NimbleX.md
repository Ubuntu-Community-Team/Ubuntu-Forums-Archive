Hi All,

I just ran across Nimblex and its great. Ck it out at 
[http://www.nimblex.net/index.php](http://www.nimblex.net/index.php). I have ubuntu installed on a virtual drive and like it fine.

The best thing I like about Nimblex though is the ability to customize your ISO at their site. You can choose the basic 200 meg install on up . From starting the burn to using the live cd and booting to the desktop took a total of 13 minutes.

The feature I really liked was the usb installer in the home folder. Takes the heavy lifting out of setting up a usb drive.

Very sweet distro.
