### Post by ddrichardson on 2009-09-13
I have an 8Gb SSD Acer Aspire One, which aren't so easy to get hold of now - at least not in the shops.

Runs fine but has some issues, most noticeable is battery life of around two hours. You can buy a 6 or 9 cell battery but they double the weight and are comparatively expensive.

I also had to spend some time making adjustments for the SSD's slow write speeds. There is some software which doesn't play nice with SSD from a performance point of view - Firefox 3+ in particular and Adobe Flash to name two.
