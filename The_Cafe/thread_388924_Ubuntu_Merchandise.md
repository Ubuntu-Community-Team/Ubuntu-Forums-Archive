although you can't get a grant from canonical to make these, I'd recommend you make a poll in the cafe asking if people would buy them. If you find that 30 people would buy an Ubuntu lanyard, it'll start being worth it to have them made - it gets very cheap when done en masse (says the guy who's printed thousands of [stickers]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=2335813") and hundreds of [posters]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1739055") ;)).
