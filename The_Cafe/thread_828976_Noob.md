### Post by p_quarles on 2008-06-15
The traditional difference between a newbie and a noob:
**newbie:** An inexperienced user
**noob:** A user who appears to deliberately avoid learning anything about what they are using, or who puts up a pretense of being more knowledgable than they actually are.
