### Post by grossaffe on 2008-08-02
Not that this thread hasn't been done before, but in the genre of metal, I like:
Axenstar
Avantasia
Dragonforce
Edguy
Freedom Call
Galneryus
Kamelot
Power Quest
Rhapsody of Fire
Yngwie Malmsteen

Most of those are power metal, but Rhapsody of Fire can be classified as Symphonic.  Yngwie Malmsteen would be Speed.  Kamelot is Epic, Avantasia is... well its Avantasia.  They sound different album to album as they are a concept band.

Of those, I highly reccomend Edguy to fulfill your metal needs.  Kamelot is great if you like a nice strong and powerful band.  Rhapsody if you're looking for something more Grandiose.  Dragonforce is a great band if you like their style of music; they seem to be a very polarizing band.  Avantasia is a great band if your looking for something different.  The band is made up of members of a bunch of other metal bands; they also get special appearances from big names such as Alice Cooper.
