### Post by K.Mandla on 2008-04-25
Thanks to everyone who has been offering their opinion, or soliciting opinions, on the new release. 

Please remember that we have a subforum specifically for relating your experience with Ubuntu. You can post your testimonial in the area called, of all things, "[Ubuntu Testimonials and Experiences]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?s=&daysprune=-1&f=103")."

Also please be aware that, as with every new release, we occasionally get trolls or flamers who aren't really members of the community, but just want to stir up opinions or cause trouble.

Remember in those cases to report threads you think suspicious, or to ask that they be moved to a more appropriate area. You should never engage anyone in a battle of words; that wouldn't be very ubuntu. ;)

Cheers and thanks! :)
