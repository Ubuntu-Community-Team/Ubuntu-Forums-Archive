writing skills weren't really under thread. IM and e-mail are different from more traditional means of communication like letters and essays, so people write in a different style. Shorter sentences with a lot of abbreviations are more convenient for IM and e-mail, but this doesn't imply that writing skills are deteriorating. most people who use slang on the internet are still capable of writing good texts (except for some rebellious youngsters maybe who use their slang as a provocation). 

blogs are yet another medium, so i think people use a different writing style.
