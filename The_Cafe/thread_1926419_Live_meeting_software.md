### Post by toddkaufmann on 2012-02-17
[http://incubator.apache.org/openmeetings/]("http://incubator.apache.org/openmeetings/")

> OpenMeetings is a free browser-based software that allows you to set up instantly a conference in the Web. You can use your microphone or webcam, share documents on a white board, share your screen or record meetings. It is available as hosted service or you download and install a package on your server with no limitations in usage or users.

I haven't had a chance to try it, but it looks very promising.

See a video here, at the old home:  [http://code.google.com/p/openmeetings/](http://code.google.com/p/openmeetings/)

Look pretty cool, with ability to record, and VOIP/SIP integration.
