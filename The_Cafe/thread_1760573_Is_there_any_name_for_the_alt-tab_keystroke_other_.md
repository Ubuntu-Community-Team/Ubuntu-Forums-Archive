### Post by sv87411 on 2011-05-17
Alt-tab. Maybe Switch app? Switch Window. Tab Window. 

It's been around since Windows 1.0 and I'm pretty sure even before that. I'm not sure if its ever had a definitive name. 

Its one of the whole suite of keyboard shortcuts that have been around for years and pre-date the mouse (the mechanical kind :) ) Allowing for navigation in a window/pane application environment. 

Generally the 'Alt' key modifier acted on the window and the 'Ctrl' modifier acted on the pane.

Alt-tab switches between windows. Ctrl-Tab switches between panes in windows.

Alt-F4 closes the window. Ctrl-F4 closes the pane in the window.

I'm sure with a little Googling you can find all the others that mostly still work in windowed environments. :D
