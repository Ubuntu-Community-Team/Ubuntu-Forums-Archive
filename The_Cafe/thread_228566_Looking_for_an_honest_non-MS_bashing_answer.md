### Post by xtoph on 2006-09-11
i know why you could connect to the internet and she couldn't.
the problem is, those dsl modems have NO switching or routing capabilities.  if you connect to one port or the other, everything is A-OK (theoretically).  if you try to connect to both, it will serve the ethernet and not the USB.  i am currently at this point with my landlord.  whenever they want their USB internet to work, they have to unplug the ethernet to my wifi router.  less than ideal.
so that is why you got internet and she didn't.  now if she still can't connect when you're not around, or has to reboot etc., i have no idea about that.
