### Post by UniverseA7X on 2008-08-16
I know a few people on here are learning a new language, I remember someone posting a thread about wanting to learn Japanese, LaRoza is learning Hindi, and I'm going for German myself.

This website seems to look promising, [www.livemocha.com](www.livemocha.com), There's several courses that take on college naming (English 101, 102, etc.) and each course has a wealth of information. You get to write, read, listen, as well as speak for others on the website to help and correct you where you might be wrong. You can also help others with languages you know or are a native tongue. 

How many have used this, and what are your opinions of it? It's kind of helping me stay on a routine, studying for a little more than an hour a day, going back if I don't remember something.
