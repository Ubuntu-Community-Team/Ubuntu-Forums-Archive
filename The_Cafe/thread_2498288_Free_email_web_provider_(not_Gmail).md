### Post by currentshaft on 2024-06-07
I don't use Proton nor Tuta, so I can't answer those questions. In general, you do not need an app to get time-based OTP tokens for MFA. There are software solutions you can run locally on your computer, including in some password managers, to manage that credential.

I've not had email issues like you describe, sorry. But in general, I have found other email providers do not treat me like an idiot in the way Gmail does.

As for choosing an email domain, I would recommend instead purchasing your own name (gipsyshadow.net or whatever) and setting up email to that. This way, you can easily switch providers while keeping the same address.

Hope this helps.
