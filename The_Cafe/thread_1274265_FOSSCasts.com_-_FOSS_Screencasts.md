I just wanted to announce that a project I've been working on, [FOSSCasts.com]("http://fosscasts.com"), has launched.  At least every two weeks there will be a new screencast covering something Open Source.   I'm aiming these at the beginner/intermediate level FOSSer and at a variety of topics.

The inaugural episode is "Easy Nginx and PHP".  Future episodes I have planned include Zsh, into to load testing with JMeter, "stupid command line tricks", and others.  

Comments/Suggestions are welcome :)
