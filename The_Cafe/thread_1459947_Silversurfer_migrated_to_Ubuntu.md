### Post by ade234uk on 2010-04-22
Was helping one of my dads old friends. He is 70 years old. Well I installed Windows for him about 5 months ago, and it was just hassle after hassle with him calling me asking questions. 

In the end he got a virus so I thought sod this. 

Last month I asked him if he fancied trying something different, so I installed Ubuntu on his machine.

Sat down with him, had a cup of tea and was so surprised how quickly he got to using the system. In fact the only thing I need to show him was how to login. 

So it's 1 months in now, and yesterday I asked him what he thought of it. He said its great and still running fast. He said its easier than that other one you put on for me. Its very simple.

So there you go another win for Ubuntu.
