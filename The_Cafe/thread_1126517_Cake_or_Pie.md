### Post by Simian Man on 2009-04-15
> **Penguin Guy said:**
> Cheese**cake**.

It's not quite so simple as that I'm afraid.  Cakes are usually much more bread-like than Cheesecake in addition to not needing a separate crust.  Just looking at a cheesecake you will it has more in common, superficially at least, with pies than cakes.

Most consider cheesecakes to be in the tart family, which include other open-top pies such as Key Lime and Coconut Cream.  However, the name cheesecake implies that there was a looser definition of "cake" than the one in common usage today.

You can see that I take cheesecake seriously :).
