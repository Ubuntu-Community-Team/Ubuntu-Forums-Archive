### Post by Kai69 on 2010-06-02
The official [[COLOR=#0645ad]mascot[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/wiki/Mascot") of GIMP, *Wilber* was created by Tuomas Kuosmanen (*tigert*) at some point before September 25, 1997; since then Wilber has received additional accessories from other GIMP developers.[[SIZE=2][COLOR=#0645ad][37][/COLOR][/SIZE]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/#cite_note-gimp-linking-36") Accessories for Wilber can be found in the *Wilber construction kit*, included with the GIMP source code.[[SIZE=2][COLOR=#0645ad][37][/COLOR][/SIZE]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/#cite_note-gimp-linking-36")
Wilber is also used as a free software cultural reference outside of GIMP, for example Wilber can be played as a racing car driver in [[COLOR=#0645ad]SuperTuxKart[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/wiki/SuperTuxKart") along with other free software cultural icons.[[SIZE=2][COLOR=#0645ad][38][/COLOR][/SIZE]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/#cite_note-37") In addition to being referenced in other software projects, Wilber has been shown on the side of the [[COLOR=#0645ad]Bibliothèque nationale de France[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/wiki/Biblioth%C3%A8que_nationale_de_France") as part of [[COLOR=#0645ad]Project Blinkenlights[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/wiki/Project_Blinkenlights").[[SIZE=2][COLOR=#0645ad][39][/COLOR][/SIZE]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/#cite_note-38")
