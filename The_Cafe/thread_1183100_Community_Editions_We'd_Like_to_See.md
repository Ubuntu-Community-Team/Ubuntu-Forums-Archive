### Post by CharmyBee on 2009-06-09
> **xuCGC002 said:**
> I feel urged into making this. IceWM has a Win95 theme, but the window border could be redone.

- Middle click doesn't paste. It just causes a scroller to appear (Mouseware-style) or do nothing.
- Win key support
- Cascadable windows
- 32x32 default icon size, 16x16 for small icons (taskbar, menus, window titles)
- Ability to load and use raster fonts in .FON format
- YMF-262 OPL3 FM MIDI playback (emulated, or passthrough to actual card; use custom timbres from file) as default midi device 
- A terminal that has a classic "C:\" like mount-point structure and case insensitivity
- low-color PNG wallpapers shipped, with mostly small tiles
- Ships with Firefox with a very low color theme, new 16-color icons. Or Seamonkey.

This wouldn't just be Ubuntu with a new retro theme. It'd have to cover from boot to shutdown.
There's so many XP/Vista/7 imitators and so little 9x. It wouldn't be about just saving memory, either.
