### Post by alphacrucis2 on 2011-05-26

In 11.04, Unity is just a shell on top of gnome 2. In 11.10 there will be no gnome 2, hence they are migrating Unity to run on top of gnome 3. You will have a choice of running either the gnome 3 shell developed by gnome, or the unity shell developed by ubuntu. Both will run on top of gnome 3. As you might expect, Unity will be the default for Ubuntu.
