### Post by macgyver2 on 2005-09-17
[QUOTE=dunja]how can one find one's own threads, other than by going to advance search and searching for one's own name?

also, is there a way to "watch" a thread.[/QUOTE]
Another way to find your own threads is to look at the list of active users at the bottom of the page, click your name, and look on the left-hand side.  You'll see a couple of links about all your posts and just posts you've started.

You can watch a thread by subscribing to it.  You can subscribe when you're replying to a thread (full reply...look under the text box), when you're viewing a thread (look under Thread Tools at the top), or you can set an option to subscribe to every thread you post in (look in your account options).
