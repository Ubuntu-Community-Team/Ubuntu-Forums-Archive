### Post by guitarmaniac on 2007-04-14
I use a lot of Firefox extensions (probably too many).
Heres the list of extensions I use followed by a short description.

Adblock Plus: duh... blocks ads.
CustomizeGoogle: gets rid of google ads and a lot of other cool things.
DownloadThemAll!: A download manager
Fasterfox: Prefetches links in webpages etc to speed up Firefox. I know I shouldnt use this (but I do have it set to couteous) 
FEBE: Makes a backup of my firefox extensions.
Flashblock: Blocks flash objects on webpages (If you want too see the flash object just click of the "F" symbol). Good for people like me with a low bandwidth.
Foxmarks: Backs up my bookmarks to a remote server (good for setting up multiple computers). 
StumbleUpon: Choose topics that interest you and "stumble upon" web pages that fit the chosen descriptions.
Tab Mix Plus: Advanced oftions for tabs.
Ubuntu Forums Menu: Adds an item in the menubar with all the Ubuntu forums categories!
VideoDownloader: Good for downloading youtube videos etc.

Oh boy. I use quite a few here goes, with links:

**Extensions:**

_Security:_
[*No Script *:]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/722")Blocks Java script and cross-site scripting at the source.
[*Ad Block Plus:*]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1865") Fairly self evident, snap to filtering of ads and other assorted things.
[*Cookie Safe:* ]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2497")Snap filtering of cookes and management.

_Multimedia:_
[*Downthemall:*]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/201") Excellent download manager.
[*Ook? Video Ook! : *]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2584")Downloads flash videos.
*[Media Player Connectivity: ]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446")*Allows for selective streaming of media embeded into any client of your choice on Desktop.
*[Download Statusbar:]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/26")* Downloads appear in the status bar at bottom rather than separate screen, GREAT!

_Miscellaneous: _[URL="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3414"]
*Dictionary Switcher:* [/URL]Switches dictionaries, I use french and english.
*[Ubuntu Forums Menu: ]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2302")*Gives a menu for browsing and moving to all forums at a click.
*[Tab Mix Plus: ]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1122")*Provides excellent additional functionality and control to tab mix plus.
[U]
Optional (I don't always use these, some might like them):[/U]
[*Customize Google: *]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/743")Allows for customization of google services and pages.
[*Googlepedia: *]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2517")Cuts your page when searching in half and loads google results on left, wikipedia on right.
*[Fasterfox: ]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1269")*Contains network tweaks and performance changes, some find improvement with it.

That is it. I use a lot of extensions, I have more but those are the more important ones I have. Enjoy! :D.
