### Post by WRDN on 2010-08-11
If you want private hosting, then, for a long time, I used [www.unfuddle.com](www.unfuddle.com). The free plan they offer allows 2 users (2 public ssh keys) to access the repositories. The repositories are linked to projects, so you can track bugs etc. You get 200Mb of space free, and can use it for subversion and git repositories.

I have also used indefero.net when I had projects with more than 2 users, but there free plan only gives you 10Mb storage (20Mb when I used it).
