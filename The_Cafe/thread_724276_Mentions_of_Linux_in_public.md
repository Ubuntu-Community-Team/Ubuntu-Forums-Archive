### Post by Mazza558 on 2008-03-14
This thread's about any encounters (direct or indirect) with people in public (or just in your family), who have mentioned Linux. You might have overheard a conversation on a bus, for example.

This is what I heard today, a conversation between 2 kids from school:

[B]
person A: what operating system do you use?[/B]
person B: what do you mean?
**A: like, XP, Vista...**
B: Vista.
**A: vista is really bad.**
B: I like it.
**A: have you heard of Linux?**
B: nope.
**A: it's an operating system, made by the Swedish...no, I think it's Norwegian people, but they release it for free. It's amazing, it's really good.**
B: How do you know?
**A: I saw it on someone's laptop. It's like a Mac...**

It made me smile - although it's good that Linux is slowly spreading, it's fun to hear people's misconceptions about it.
