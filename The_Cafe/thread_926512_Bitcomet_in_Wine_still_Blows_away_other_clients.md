### Post by NoReflex on 2008-09-22
I don't think that there are programs that download faster than others...it depends where you are downloading from. I use uTorrent in Windows and Linux, Bitcomet in Windows, Transmission, uTorrent + Wine and Deluge in Ubuntu and I must say that all reached the same max download speed (the same torrent file was used). BitComet is not really a good choice:
> Quote:
BitComet was banned from KickAssAnime because of the following reason:

"ok here is exactly why. BitComet reports false data to the tracker and to other peers. it reports that it is sending out way more data to peers than it actually is. so by banning it, and forcing the leechers to use clients that dont cheat, we get a healthier torrent. because (not sure if everyone knows) a healthy torrent works on the rewards system most of the time. meaning its like "ok ill send you a piece of this torrent, then you tell me when you sent it to someone else, and ill send you another." Bitcomet, as soon as it recieves a piece, wether it sends or not, reports back to the client that it recieved it from that it sent it to another peer. by removing the leechy client by nature, the torrent gets healthier, and the people who use real clients, and try to go 1:1 on the torrents get the complete torrent faster."
