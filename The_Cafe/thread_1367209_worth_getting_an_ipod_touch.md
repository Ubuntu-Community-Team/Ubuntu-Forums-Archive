### Post by bashveank on 2009-12-29
I've found the iPod Touch to be the most useful device I own, and I have an Android phone!
The media player is par none, the hardware is very well designed, sturdy and stylish. What puts it over the top, though, are the apps. I have 9 or 10 games that I play regularly (all free or under $5), one of the best GTD clients I've ever used, an ebook reader that syncs with my Kindle account, an encrypted vault of my passwords and personal info, etc....
I would love it if my Android phone could supplant it, but even when there are equivalent apps for both devices, the iPod's is always more polished and more pleasant to use.
