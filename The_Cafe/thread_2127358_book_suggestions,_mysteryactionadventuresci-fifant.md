### Post by aspergerian on 2013-03-20
A sampling of mysteries: I've read all of CJ Box & Robert Crais & Louise Penny & Michael Connelly & William Kent Kreuger.  If you want a glimpse of social ironies in late 1800s and early 1900s England, try Anne Perry, post-WW-I glimpses of England in Jacqueline Winspear's books. Read each author in order-of-publication if you can. Each writes well, and characters develop from one novel to the next.
