all the pink dots will slowly disappear, and you will only see a green dot rotating

### Post by manmower on 2007-02-08
Looks to me like a demonstration of perceptual filling in + apparent motion. Nicely done though, the filling in (what makes the dots disappear when you fixate ) is usually not that strong in other demonstrations. Screen resolution and viewing distance play a great role in the effect too. The visual angle between the fixation cross and the dots is crucial to the effect. So if you can't get it to "work" try sitting closer to / further from your screen.

The green colour IMHO is the result of blending between the pink colour and the grey that appears when the dot shortly disappears.
