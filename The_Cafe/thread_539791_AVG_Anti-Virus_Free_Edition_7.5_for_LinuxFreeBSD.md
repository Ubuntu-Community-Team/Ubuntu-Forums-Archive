### Post by stmiller on 2008-08-23
Before the confusion goes further, know that this (like all anti-virus software for Linux) is for detecting Windows viruses.

Unless you are running a mail server scanning windows attachments, or have NAS for windows users, you don't need this...

Chances are very low that you will send someone a Windows virus. You would have to manually attach a virus as an email attachment. And even if by chance you deliberately do (from a Linux box), mail servers on both ends (yours and the receiving server) have AV scans in place to block it.
