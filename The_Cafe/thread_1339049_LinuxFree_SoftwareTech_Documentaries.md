### Post by gnomeuser on 2009-11-27
RIP: A Remix Manifesto

Not technically a tech documentary but it does deal with the technology that enables remixing and features interviews with people famous in tech circles such as Professor Lawrence Lessig.

[Code Rush](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Code_Rush)

About the early days of the Internet, focused on the fallout of the Netscape/Microsoft browser wars. It follows the process of Netscape in opening their code base and giving birth to the turd we know as Mozilla. Recently the movie was released under a creative commons license.

*edit*

I decided to edit wikipedia and add code rush to the tech documentary category. I would encourage others to do the same for existing movies not listed but confirmed as being tech docs.
