### Post by t0p on 2009-08-03
A good webmail service is provided by the [sdf.lonestar.org]("http://sdf.lonestar.org") folk.  I dunno if it's open source, but it's pretty ethical and run by people with a good attitude concerning the internet.

If you want to use the webmail service, you have to sign up for a free shell account on one of their wonderful NetBSD servers.  You can find out how to do this if you go to [sdf.lonestar.org]("http://sdf.lonestar.org"), or telnet or ssh to [email]new@sdf.lonestar.org[/email].

You don't get a huge amount of storage space.  But the service is free, there are no intrusive ads and no data-mining.  And there's a lot more stuff on offer other than webmail.  Check it out!
