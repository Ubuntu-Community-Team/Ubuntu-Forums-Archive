[I]Skeleton case main specs include:

[LIST]
[*]Application: ATX and smaller form factors derived from this one.
[*]Material: Steel.
[*]Power supply: Doesn’t come with the product.
[*]Available colors: Gray on black.
[*]Dimensions: 13” x 14.8” x 16.5” (33 cm x 37.6 cm x 41.9 cm) (H x W x D).
[*]Net Weight: 15.5 Lbs (7 Kg)
[*]Bays: Two external 5 ¼” bays, two internal 3 ½” bays and four external 3 ½” bays.
[*]Expansion slots: Eight.
[*]Fans: One 250-mm on the top (on/off switch and speed control: 400 rpm/79 CFM, 600 rpm/118 CFM and 800 rpm/158 CFM) and one 92-mm on the hard disk drive cage (1,100 rpm/15.6 CFM).
[*]More Information: [http://www.antec.com]("http://www.antec.com/usa/productDetails.php?lan=us&id=15125")
[*]Average price in the US*: USD 180.00.
