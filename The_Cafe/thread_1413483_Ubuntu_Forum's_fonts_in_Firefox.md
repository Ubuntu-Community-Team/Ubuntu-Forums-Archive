### Post by mcduck on 2010-02-22
Well, all the font definitions in the forum stylesheet are actually using Microsoft fonts (Tahoma, Verdana, Arial, Trebuchet MS) so it's no surprise if the forums look bad without these fonts installed.

The bigger question is why the forums aren't designed to be viewed with fonts that Ubuntu would have by default, or why such fonts aren't even included as alternatives in the CSS styles...

What comes to generic font rendering, that's quite often a question of personal taste. I mean that some pople even prefer the aliased fonts old windows versions had! (Not to mention that unless you have set the correct DPI value in your font settings I'm not even listening what you have to say about how your fonts look, since there's no way how they could be rendered correctly.. .;))
