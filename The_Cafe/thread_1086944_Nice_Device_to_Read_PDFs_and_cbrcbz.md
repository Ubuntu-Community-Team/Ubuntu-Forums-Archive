### Post by trlkly on 2009-03-05
The DS has a comic book reader, at least, called ComicBookDS (surprised?). But it requires you to convert all your comics to a special format. Fortunately, both the converter and the reader are available at the [website](http://cbds.free.fr/)

A place called [ClickWheel](http://www.clickwheel.net) both creates and repurposes comics for the iPod. [ComicZeal](http://www.bitolithic.com/ComicZeal/comiczeal.htm) can read comics on the iPod Touch or iPhone.

You could always just run [Linux]("http://www.ipodlinux.org/") on your iPod, of course.
