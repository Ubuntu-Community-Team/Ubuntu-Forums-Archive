### Post by BrokenKingpin on 2010-10-24
I would like a few recommendations for a Media Center app on Ubuntu (10.04). I have already tried the following, but did not work out for me:

**XBMC**: I really like XBMC, but I am having an issue where videos are choppy for the first 5 seconds. I am not sure why this is as the PC I am running on is quite powerful, with a decent NVidia graphics card. All other video apps do not have this issue. After using this for a few months, I just found this too irritating.

**Boxee**: At first I found this absolutetly amazing, especially for the ability to stream a large selection of TV shows, and also did not have the issue I had with XBMC. Unfortunately, the amount of bugs was just too much. When streaming shows it would hang, scanning my video library never finished, and when trying to close the app it would completely hang.

**Moovida**: The main reason I ditched this was lack of features, mainly not keeping track of what I watched. It has been about a year since I last tried this, so maybe it's feature set has grown since then... is it worth another shot?

Any suggestions on what to try from here would be greatly appreciated.
