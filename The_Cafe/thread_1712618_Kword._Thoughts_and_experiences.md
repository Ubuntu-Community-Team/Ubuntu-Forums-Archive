### Post by StephanG on 2011-03-23
Actually, I do use KWord on a regular basis, believe it or not :D.

I love how snappy it is on my PC.  LibreOffice feels faster than OpenOffice did, but the entire office suite still seems to feel bloated, and unnecessarily huge.
