### Post by drawkcab on 2012-07-24
Well one idea would be that the kernel featured in Ubuntu LTS is not yet abreast with the latest hardware.  

In the past I've bought the latest and greatest whitebooks from taiwan.  Although I was able to get Linux running on each of them, I often had to wait until the next release to get some crucial bit of hardware properly supported.

Nonetheless I see your dilemma and sympathize.
