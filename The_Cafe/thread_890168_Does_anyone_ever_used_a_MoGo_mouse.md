### Post by pp. on 2008-08-30
I have seen product descriptions of that thing before. After some reflection, I decided that it wasn't for me, for those reasons:

I don't think I'd be comfortable with a mouse that small and that flat. If I have to use it for a few minutes, I can manage with the touch pad, and for longer periods of intense use I'd prefer a larger one, I think.

While not in use, the mouse would be stored in the PC card slot. Since I usually turn the PC off when it's not in use, the mouse wouldn't get charged at all. That, of course, applies doubly when moving from place to place.

No go for me.
