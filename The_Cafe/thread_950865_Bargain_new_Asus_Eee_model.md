### Post by gn2 on 2008-10-17
Asus have released yet another Eee variant, the Eee 900A.

It's an Eee 900 with the Atom CPU from the 901, but the best bit is the price, in the UK it can be had for [**£175**]("http://www.morecomputers.co.uk/extra.asp?pn=EEEPC900A-BLK001L"), seriously undercutting the lower spec Celeron 900, and a massive £100 less than the very similar Eee901.

This new Eee 900A has just knocked the Acer Aspire One from the top spot of the UK's VFM Netbooks.
