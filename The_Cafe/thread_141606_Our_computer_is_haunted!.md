### Post by bonzodog on 2006-03-08
lol...
Seriously though, it sounds like a wake-on-lan type signal is being sent to the PC, and that you aren't switching off, but suspending the computer at night. You might find it does no harm to just leave it on, and make sure all vital ports are closed like SSH, FTP, etc. Computers use very little power if left on, and the most modern PC's have power controls available. My system turns the HDD off after an hour, and I always just switch the monitor off. It also turns off the CPU fan , and as I have cool'n'quiet AND powernowd available, my PC consumes lightbulb levels of power at night.
