### Post by kurt18947 on 2013-03-27
I assume you're talking about USB external?  I bought a 'generic' optical drive from Amazon and it did not work with Brasero - didn't know about k3b at the time.  I returned it and bought a Samsung SE-S084D/TSBS.  I only see that model  drive used and for $69.  I paid $39. The Samsung has been very good, works with Brasero and k3b. Optical drives are pretty mature technology so I suspect any drive from a reputable manufacturer that has a good number of positive reviews should be fine.  Reviews mentioning linux are a +.
