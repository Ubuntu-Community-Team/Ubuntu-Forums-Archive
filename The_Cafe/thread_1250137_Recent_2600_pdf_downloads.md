Asking for downloads of copyrighted material is not appropriate.  2600 does not advertise.  There is no invisible money flowing into their pockets to subsidize printing (as their recent printing woes indicate).  Yes, there are repos of backissues. Edit: For anyone who is unfamiliar with 2600, they are not only a magazine, they serve as a legal barometer in the U.S. and have been sued for publishing completely legitimate information.  This is not similar to asking for free downloads of mainstream publications as 2600 does not print advertisements for anyone but their own subscribers.  Mainstream publications actually get bumps in ad rates from anything that suggests people are seeing the ads - 2600 gets nothing.
