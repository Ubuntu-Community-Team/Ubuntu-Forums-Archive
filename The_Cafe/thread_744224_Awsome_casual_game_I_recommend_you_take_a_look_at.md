### Post by Ender Black on 2008-04-03
It is called Glass Beads.  Basically it is a board game where each player is given  glass beads with a subject matter on them...musical score, piece of art, historical figure...whatever.  Each player puts the glass beads on the board, the twist is, when you put a bead down, you must connect it to another bead using logic and creativity.  You're opponent then get's to accept your logic and the rounds repeat until the first player runs out of beads and is therefore the winner.  

It is a web based game with no client to download and everyone I have played were exceptionally friendly and helpful...a lot like the ubuntuforums...

here's the link: [http://glassbead.net](http://glassbead.net)
