### Post by ChuangTzu on 2017-11-22
All depends on the size of the team, and their dedication as well as the size of the community and their dedication.   Slackware for example has a small team that is very dedicated, and a large community that is very dedicated so it has remained for 24 years....  Crunchbang on the other hand had a small team that became less dedicated and a decent size  community that was dedicated so it crashed and burned but was forked by the community.  Corporate backing helps, but dedication from team and community is more important I think.
