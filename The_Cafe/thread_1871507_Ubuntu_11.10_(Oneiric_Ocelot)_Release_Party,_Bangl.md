### Post by ashickur.noor on 2011-10-29
After a long waiting It really happens. I was so worried about the party. After all the party was full of Ubuntu lovers and maximum is new users, which I wanted to do.

Party was started around 3:45 PM in the selected location, coz the location was a open place so we have to look after a suitable place where we can sit. After searching We find a place and put our banner there and start to gather one by one it takes about 1 hour to gather all in the place. In that time percipients where talking with each other after a little introduction of every one. There were about 62 participants.  

Around 5 PM the cake was open then every one become busy to take picture of the cake. Then two most junior participants who read in class 9 aged around 15  and Shariar Tarik from Ubuntu Bangladesh LoCo team cut the cake then it was served among the participants, though it was self service. There was some snacks and soft drink available in the party as refreshment.

Every body enjoy the party very much and most of them where new faces to us. After all it was full of fun.
