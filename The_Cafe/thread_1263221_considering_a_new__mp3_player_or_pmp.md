### Post by akiratheoni on 2009-09-10
I got a Cowon S9 16GB for about $215, although it's $200 now. There's also a 32 GB model.

It's amazing; it works amazingly with Ubuntu since Cowon tends to NOT require software with it. The computer just recognizes it as a flash drive and you just drag and drop to transfer music files.

The AMOLED screen is absolutely beautiful. Also it is extremely resilient to scratches; there's a video of a guy taking an XACTO knife and trying to scratch the screen, to no avail. I wouldn't do that with my S9, but someone took a toothpick to it and there was no sign of a scratch.

Also the battery life is amazing. I haven't had to charge mine in nearly two weeks.
