### Post by mharrison on 2010-01-10
So, I noticed today that my Ipod syncs faster running Windows 7 in VirtualBox on 9.10 than it does when I am on my actual Windows 7 system.  And it is not just a tad bit faster but insanely faster.  On my Win7 box it takes anywhere from 15 minutes to an hour to sync the 4 gigs of data I have on my Ipod, but on 9.10 with Virtualbox it takes around 1 to 5 minutes.

I'm not sure if I am missing some great thing to explain why this is happening, but I like it.


Edit:

Actually I found out much later that the problem is with Windows 7 and one of it's USB 2.0 drivers.  If you disable it in Windows 7 the Sync works just fine.  But since I just use XP in VBox now no longer an issue or concern.
