### Post by juliux on 2006-12-14
Hi folks!
Since there are hardly any ubuntu-shirts available in Europe, the [URL="http://verein.ubuntu-de.org"]German ubuntu 
association[/URL] has produced such. Two different kinds of shirts are available: Polo-shirts and t-shirts.

Details
Both, polo and t-shirts are provided with a embroidery (4,5cm x 4,5cm) of the ubuntu logo. See [http://ubuntu.juliux.de/polo.jpg](http://ubuntu.juliux.de/polo.jpg)
All shirts are available from sizes S, M, L, X, XL, to XXL.
The color of the shirts is navy blue. The weigth of the t-shirt is 180gram/sqm, the poloshirt has 220gram/sqm.

Finance
Production are 8€ for the t-shirts and 11€ for the poloshirts.
You can get a t-shirt for donations of 10€(+shipping) and a poloshirt for donations of 15€(+shipping).
The entire profit goes to the German ubuntu association. The association supports 
ubuntu activities in Germany, like ubuntu booths at expos or German ubuntu flyers for instance.
Therefore the profit returns to the ubuntu project.

If you want to get a shirt please contact me via e-mail(juliux@ubuntu-de.org).

cheers

julius
