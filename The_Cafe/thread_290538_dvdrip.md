### Post by forezt on 2007-12-08
From the dvd::rip website on 2007/08/05:

> 
Obviously many people use dvd::rip on smaller screens (widescreen notebooks and stuff ;) but the rich GUI doesn't fit very well on them. This release adds a "small screen size" option, which puts the notebook tabs to the left and adds scrollbars, so every bit of the dvd::rip GUI is reachable even on the smallest screen now ;)


So, you need to get dvd::rip .0.9.8 or above if you want to use dvd::rip on a small(er) screen. Currently, this version isn't in the gutsy repository, but hopefully it will be very very soon.

EDIT: If you're anxious like I was, then you can get dvd::rip .0.9.8 .deb packages from [http://www.debian-multimedia.org/pool/main/d/dvdrip/]("http://www.debian-multimedia.org/pool/main/d/dvdrip/"). They were compiled for Debian lenny, but they worked on my Ubuntu gutsy system. Use them at your own risk.
