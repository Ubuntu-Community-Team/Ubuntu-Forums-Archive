### Post by Vadi on 2008-08-03
Read the news article here: [http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=linux_opengl_prime&num=1](http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=linux_opengl_prime&num=1)

I haven't seen the Lightsmark demo apart from the screenshots, but I can confirm the Unigine one looks simply *amazing*. While you're at it, check out other tests that Phoronix Test Suite offers too (there's like... 60, and they do a lot of different things)
[CENTER][[IMG]http://img140.imageshack.us/img140/4299/screenshotmd3.th.png[/IMG]](http://img140.imageshack.us/my.php?image=screenshotmd3.png)[/CENTER]
There's no GUI yet (that's being worked on. If you know gtk and php, feel free to join in), but the command to run after [installing the .deb]("http://www.phoronix-test-suite.com/releases/deb/phoronix-test-suite_1.0.5_all.deb") for the Lightsmark test is this:

> phoronix-test-suite benchmark lightsmark

And the Unigine demo isn't yet integrated with PTS, so wait a bit :)

Digg link: [http://digg.com/linux_unix/OpenGL_Benchmarking_On_Linux_Reaches_New_Heights](http://digg.com/linux_unix/OpenGL_Benchmarking_On_Linux_Reaches_New_Heights)
