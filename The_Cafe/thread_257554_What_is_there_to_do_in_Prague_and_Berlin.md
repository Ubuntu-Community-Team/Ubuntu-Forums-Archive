### Post by punkinside on 2006-09-23
Seriously, in berlin. Take the S-Bahn to Alexanderplatz, see the Fernsehturm, the red town hall (rotes rathaus), Keep walking down that street in to whats left of the Palast der Republik, the Museums insel, later walk down Unter den Linden and see the Staatsoper, Neue Wache, etc until you reach the brandenburger tor, then decide to go left to the jewish memorial and potsdamer platz or right to the reichstag and the government quarter. Get a guide to read about what youre seeing in this walk. Anyhow, there are great tours leaving from the Mc Donalds in front of the S-Bahn station Zoologischer Garten I dont know the times but they cost 10$ a day and last up to 5 hours. If you cant drink then I would not recommend going on a pub crawl but if youre willing to risk it theire GREAT.
