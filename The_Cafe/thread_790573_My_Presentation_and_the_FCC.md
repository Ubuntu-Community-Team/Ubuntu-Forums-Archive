### Post by mips on 2008-05-12
> **b3n87 said:**
> So how come my iPod has a FCC logo on it? That doesnt have any wireless communications on it does it? :/

Thanks for the input btw :D

That is because the FCC certified your iPod not to cause radio interference which is something that falls in their domain. 

Most electronic goods radiate energy at some frequency or the other. The design of these devices usually have some form of shielding to minimize electromagnetic radiation so it does not interfere with stuff like your cordless phone, tv, radio etc and the people that control this is the FCC.

[http://www.fcc.gov/oet/ea/procedures.html](http://www.fcc.gov/oet/ea/procedures.html)
