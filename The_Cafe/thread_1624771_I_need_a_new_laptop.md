### Post by 3Miro on 2010-11-18
Get a 15.6 inch laptop with Nvidia video and i5 CPU. The rest depends on the things like Battery life (Nvidia GTX460 will burn way more than a 330, but the former will be considerably faster) and of course money. You can go with i3 or i7 CPU as well, I wouldn't go for Core 2 Duo unless money is really tight.
