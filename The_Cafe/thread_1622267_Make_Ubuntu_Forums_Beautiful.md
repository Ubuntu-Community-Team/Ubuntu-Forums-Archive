### Post by TiBaal89 on 2010-11-15
It's funny that purple is associated with the newer Ubuntu look... there are really only two elements that have that color as far as I can tell - the stock wallpaper and the default terminal background.  I use my own wallpaper and changed the terminal to black - the purple is decidedly absent on my system.

What I like very much about the new look is the use of the much darker gray tones for the panels, windows, etc.  It's not quite "gray" - I don't know what to call it, it feels warmer than just a dark gray.  It's very nice and easy on the eyes (literally! I stare at for long periods of time daily).

I'm not sure how that would translate to use on a forum... for the time being though, this one is one of the better looking forums out there IMO.
