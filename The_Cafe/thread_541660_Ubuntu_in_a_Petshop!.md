### Post by Lord Illidan on 2007-09-03
This is incredibly cool..
My uncle wanted to buy some fish from a petshop, and I went with him to help him out. When we arrived, I see a big projector screen, a wireless mouse, a keyboard..and what OS is running on the screen?

None the less than Ubuntu 7.04 with Firefox in a large icon on the desktop.

Wow, I said, but what is this doing in a petshop? The owner replied that he got it for people who might want to look up a few things about their chosen fish, do their homework, etc.

Ubuntu is spreading! Watch out!
