### Post by davebgimp on 2007-02-28
I've used serverpronto for about a year with Ubuntu.

It's fine. They work well and are cheap.

My main complaint is that I'm stuck on the 2.6.12 kernel with them. They give me some talk about how the later kernels don't work with the nic card...I don't know.

The point is, I've had a fairly steady experience with them. Just know that the reason it's so cheap is that once you get your server, you're on your own.
