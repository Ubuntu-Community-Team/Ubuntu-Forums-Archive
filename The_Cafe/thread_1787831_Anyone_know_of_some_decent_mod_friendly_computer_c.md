### Post by Lucradia on 2011-06-21
> **3Miro said:**
> Buying a case usually costs a lot in shipping. Check out craigslist.org cases are a low risk purchase too.

I got a very decent Cooler Master HAF 922 and there are cheaper ones too.

HAF-X has no shipping unless you're in the three states listed as taxable in NewEgg. Since Wisconsin is not listed, I got mine with free shipping at the time.

The HAF-X has 3 200mm fan slots, one optional, hard to screw in unless you have a superthin phillips however. HAF-X also sports 140mm fans in the two other places: Front and top back, power supply mounted on bottom, dust filters everywhere, and wheels!

The problem is: Finding a motherboard that can control them all, or a good fan controller. (My motherboard is the new Crosshair V Formula, with about 6 fan controllers in all, and a southbridge and northbridge heatsink.
