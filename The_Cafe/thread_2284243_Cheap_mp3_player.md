### Post by CantankRus on 2015-06-28
For anyone who is interested I just bought 2 cheap mp3 players off ebay.
Both use sd cards and have clips.
I wouldn't recommend these for large music collections as there is no way to search or shuffle songs.
To listen to a few songs or podcasts is ok though. Just cycle through you files.

_No sd card or headphone included. No display. AU $1.79_
[ATTACH=CONFIG]262895[/ATTACH]   


_sd card and headphones (crap sound) included. Display. AU $13.85_
[ATTACH=CONFIG]262896[/ATTACH]

Both work and sound ok with my own earphones.
Plugin to PC usb and the sd card mounts.
Just drag and drop your mp3 files to the card.
Supposed to be good for about 3-4 hrs use.
Good as second backup mp3 player.
