### Post by leg on 2008-10-06
Depending on what your requirements for a site are you may want to drop back to drupal 5.x. Some modules have not been updated for version 6 yet. Of course this only becomes a problem if you require a module for the site you are producing.

As for themes you should work through creating your own. It is not particularly difficult but could potentially (read more than likely) involve html/css/php work on your part.

[drupal 5 theming]("http://drupal.org/theme-guide/5")
[drupal 6 theming]("http://drupal.org/theme-guide")

As you noted Drupal is a great product but you will only realise its full potential once you start creating your own themes/content types etc. Most of the time an understanding of php is required.
