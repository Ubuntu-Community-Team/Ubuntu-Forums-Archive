### Post by moeFinley on 2007-05-26
I glad this is getting such a great response.

I've still to hear back from Tony but I think we could be waiting some time.  So maybe we should get things started without him.

I've been speaking to some of the Devon and Cornwall LUG members and they have a pretty active community.  I don't see why we don't tag along with them.

For example I'm suggesting that we use their site and resources and even go to some of their bigger events (annual barbecue etc.).  However their next normal meeting is 7:00pm on a Thursday in Paignton.  Being mid week I presume for most people in Plymouth it would be a pain to get to (I know it would for me).  So we'll do a local pub meet instead.  This gives us the best of both worlds.  What do you think?

I'm open to suggestions as to where's best to meet and what time?  At the moment I think the Britannia near Central Park because most of the interest seems to be in that area and it's a nice enough pub (with WiFi).

The numbers are growing thanks to contacts at Plymouth Uni and within the D&C LUG.  I'm hoping the LUG will help people find out more about Linux and what people can do with it and just be fun.  Maybe we could even organise some Nexuiz matches?

Hope everybody likes the sound of it?  Get back to me with your comments or questions.  If all goes well we get a red dot over Plymouth in Linux Format magazine's LUG section, that alone has to be worth it  :)
