### Post by bash on 2007-09-21
I just read an article on spiegel.de (The news page of one of Germanys most respected news magazines) that stated that at the Intel Developer Forum, all the prototypes for so called "Mobile Internet Devices" ran a special version of Ubuntu. I guess it is the new embedded version that is currently developed within the Gutsy release.

I think that is great news. So for whoever is capable of speaking german, I would recommend to read this article:

[http://www.spiegel.de/netzwelt/tech/0,1518,506853,00.html](http://www.spiegel.de/netzwelt/tech/0,1518,506853,00.html)

Also there were some pictures of the prototypes included on which you can more or less see how the Ubuntu embedded looks like ("weiter" means next picture, "zurück" means go back a picture):

[http://www.spiegel.de/fotostrecke/0,5538,24965,00.html](http://www.spiegel.de/fotostrecke/0,5538,24965,00.html)
