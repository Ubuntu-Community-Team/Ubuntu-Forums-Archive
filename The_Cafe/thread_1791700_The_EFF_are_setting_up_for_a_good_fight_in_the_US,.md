### Post by handy on 2011-06-27
A very important battle is currently being waged, & the EFF (with others) are attempting to get the rights of "First Sale" clearly sorted out by the Supreme Court, so that software & other media producers can't use "magic words" in a license, to take away the ability for people to actually own a product & thereafter lend it, sell it, or travel overseas with it.

It is a long read, but well worthwhile as the argument is very well presented (as it certainly needs to be):

[http://www.groklaw.net/article.php?story=20110622225806671](http://www.groklaw.net/article.php?story=20110622225806671)
