What is it?

> Join us this Today at 1800 UTC for Ubuntu on Air

Last cycle we decided to just hang out after a release, and we had such a good time and learned so much from our guests that we&#8217;re doing it again!

Hosts: Jose Antonio Rey, Jorge Castro, Benjamin Kerensa, and Alan Pope

Guests:

Barton George from Dell will be dropping by to talk a little bit about Project Sputnik, Dell&#8217;s new developer laptop initiative.
Steve Langasek from the Canonical Foundations team will be answering secure-boot related questions, and explaining what&#8217;s going on in this space.
Leann Ogasawara, Canonical Kernel Team &#8211; For all your kernel-related questions.
Chris VanHoof, Canonical Hardware Enablement Team &#8211; Chris will answer all your hardware questions, including how we roll with nVidia, Intel, and AMD.
Robert Carr, Canonical Web Applications Team &#8211; Robert will be answering all your questions about web applications.
Brian Murray, Ubuntu Defect Analyst, Foundations &#8211; Brian will be available to talk about how the Ubuntu teams manages your bug reports.
Michael Hall from the Ubuntu Community Team is going to talk about application developers, the application contest, and what it takes to judge 140+ new pieces of software for Ubuntu.
&#8230;. and of course Jono Bacon, the Ubuntu Community Manager will drop by to answer general questions you might have about Ubuntu and his latest project, Ubuntu Accomplishments!
