### Post by RAV TUX on 2007-09-28
I wanted to start a cafe thread on open discussion about Enlightenment 17, this thread is not a debate thread about whether Enlightenment 17 is better then or not better then other options.

This is simply a open discussion for active Enlightenment 17 users or those interested in using Enlightenment 17.

Please do not bash Enlightenment 17 here, if you do not like it simply don't post. Simple enough.

Please post about your positive experiences with Enlightenment 17 or if you have general questions, not necessarily technical.

I myself am a big fan of Enlightenment 17 and find that it is the best Desktop Shell(Environment) that I have found. 

Once you become familiar with it you will find that it is the most easy Desktop Shell(Environment) to use.

I find it uniquely Linux and beautiful, you can customize it to make it as simplistic or as blingtastic as you choose. Ultimately Enlightenment 17 is what you choose to make it, this simple truth is one of the beauties of Enlightenment 17.
