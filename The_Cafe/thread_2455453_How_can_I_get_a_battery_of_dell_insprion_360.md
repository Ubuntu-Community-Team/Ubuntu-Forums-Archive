### Post by grahammechanical on 2020-12-20
The User Guide will show you the layout of the motherboard. It should show the location of the battery and provide details of the kind of battery it is. Desktop machines have different batteries to laptop machines. If your machine is a desktop machine you may also find that the battery is a common type of battery that can be purchased from computer stores or even from stores that sell a range of stuff including computer cables and batteries.

Laptop batteries are of a completely different order as they do more than keep an internal memory chip powered up. Laptop batteries power the machine when not connected to the mains electricity and so please follow the advice provided by the earlier posts.
