### Post by HappinessNow on 2009-07-31
Caution! Beware! Microwave Fire Hazards!

I once worked with a girl who started a fire in the microwave by boiling a cup of water, the cup had metallic paint on it ~ don't put metallic paint objects in the microwave!

If I wrap a paper towel around a frozen habanero to defrost in the microwave the habanero stem will ignite the paper towel after about ten seconds ~ don't wrap a paper towel around your frozen habanero when defrosting your frozen habanero in the microwave!

Today I caught a bag of microwave popcorn on fire in the microwave, the popcorn manufacture sold a smaller version of their microwave popcorn, I didn't notice this, thus the one touch setting for popcorn cooking on the microwave caught the bag of popcorn on fire ~ beware of new packaging sizes by microwave popcorn manufactures!

Make sure you have fire insurance whether you rent or own!
