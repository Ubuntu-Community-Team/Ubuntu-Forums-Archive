Okay so here are the real equivalents after my knowledge was corrected:

amd sempron = intel celeron
amd athlon xp = intel pentium 4
amd athlon 64 = intel pentium 4 (with 64-bit extensions)
amd athlon 64x2 = intel core duo
amd turion = intel pentium M or Core Solo

intel core 2 duo = faster than any amd processor
