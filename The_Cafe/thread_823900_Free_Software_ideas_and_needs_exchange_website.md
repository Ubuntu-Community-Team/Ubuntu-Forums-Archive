Hi there,
Some time ago I finished working on the ideas and needs exchange website which has to help Free and Open Source software to evolve, expand and be more competitive to proprietary software. 
This is not the Ubuntu Brainstorm page, it may look similar, but its main goal is to exchange ideas on what applications users need and what can be done to make more and more people use Linux, this is also Ubuntu :) Please submit your needs and ideas and join Free Software development.

Please visit [http://flossforge.net/](http://flossforge.net/) and enjoy! :)

If you find the website interesting and worth visiting please spread a word among your friends.
