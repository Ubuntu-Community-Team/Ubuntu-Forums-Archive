### Post by KiwiNZ on 2012-08-16
> **mips said:**
> If it was me I would buy plasma before lcd/led.

It's horses for courses, if you have a bright room you will be very disappointed with a Plasma unit.

If you are connecting peripherals such as Game Consoles, PC's etc again you will be disappointed with a Plasma unit.

If you have a darker room and not connecting those peripherals then Plasma is fine.

I have a Plasma and a LED TV, one lounge is bright due to the amount of windows, the other lounge is darker, I have tried both sets in both rooms and can verify that the Plasma is best in the dark room and the LED is best in the bright room.
