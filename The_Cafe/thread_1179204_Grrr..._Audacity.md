### Post by liamnixon on 2009-06-05
Yeah, Audacity may be easy to use, but it's way destructive.

There's a bit more of a learning curve, but I **highly** recommend using Ardour (Jokosher is supposedly good and easy to use, too) instead for this very reason.  Plus, only being able to apply effects directly to the clip as opposed to it just being a plugin is disaster waiting to happen.  Especially when you want to remix something later, but you can't remove the compression/reverb/etc.  It's made me want to end it all a few times. ;)
