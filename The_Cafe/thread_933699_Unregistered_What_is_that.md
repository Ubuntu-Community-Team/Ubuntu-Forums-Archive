Just a quick little story.

I was over at a friends place and using some kind of music program on his Windows XP box. Sometimes a box popped up that said "Unregistered" and I, who have been using Ubuntu for years, suspected that it was some library or something that was not correctly registered.

It wasn't until I asked my friend about those boxes I realized it was because it was a trial-shareware-software I was using and it wanted me to register the program. It was such a weird feeling. Amazing how quick you get used to free software :)
