### Post by gradinaruvasile on 2010-10-06
I use Opera - the mailer is embedded in the browser with no noticeably overhead ( i have 2 IMAP accounts with a few thousands of mails).
Plus it has a nice tray plugin too (that is missing from Evolution/Thunderbird).
I also liked Seamonkey (that is the old Netscape = Firefox+Thunderbird, with a slightly more minimalistic interface, very stable).

Other than that there is Sylpheed and its relatives. Very lightweight, but had poor html rendering of mail with embedded stuff (images etc).
