### Post by cardinals_fan on 2009-01-17
What does GNU stand for?  GNU's Not UNIX.  What does the "G" stand for?  GNU.  What does GNU stand for?  GNU's Not UNIX.  And so on and so forth, ad infinitum.

This is called recursion.
