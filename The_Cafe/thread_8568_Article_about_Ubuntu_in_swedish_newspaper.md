### Post by Botsinge on 2004-12-18
There's an article about Ubuntu in the culture section of todays Dagens nyheter (Sweden's biggest daily newspaper). Under the title "Software for everyone" Stefan Helgesson describes Ubuntu as "one of the latest and most exciting parts of the world of open source". 

Nothing really new is touched in the article, just the ordinary about Linux and free software together with a short description of Ubuntu's origin. But, Helgesson ends the articles rather poetical with the words, "But as a  limited example of ubuntu is Ubuntu beautiful. Maybe the next IT-revolution will be African."
