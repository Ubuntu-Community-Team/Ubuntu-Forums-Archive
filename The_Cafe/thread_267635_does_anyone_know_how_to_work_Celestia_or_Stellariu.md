### Post by nalmeth on 2006-09-29
Hey, that task isn't too tough.

Just fire-up Celestia, hit ENTER to bring up a command prompt at the bottom.

Enter pluto, and then hit G to goto pluto.

Zoom in/out as you please with home/end. To increase time to show the orbit movement, hit 'L'. 'K' will slow down time. 

Hold down right-click on the screen, and move the mouse to get the right angle you want.

'Y' will syncronize the camera movement with the orbit, which may be useful for this purpose.
