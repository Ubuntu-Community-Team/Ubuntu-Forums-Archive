### Post by aysiu on 2013-06-14
Two things:

1. I believe when you add him to your family members group, he should be limited in the same way your other family members are.

2. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy**, and you should see there an option **Limit Past Posts**.

Also, if you go to **Timeline and Tagging** there's an option to **Review what other people see on your timeline**, and you can **view as** that new family member.
