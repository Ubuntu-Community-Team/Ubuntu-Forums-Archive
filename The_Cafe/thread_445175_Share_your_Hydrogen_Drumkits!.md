### Post by luxorvan on 2009-02-11
All the available drum kits I use are from hydrogen's drum kits hosted at sourceforge: [http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=24662&package_id=290536](http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=24662&package_id=290536)  With a total of 22 I'd say it would be a very great start for any beginner! I saved them all to a folder named drum kits in music and imported all of them into hydrogen. It isn't hard at all! But there is plenty of others just waiting to be found by searching the term "hydrogen drum kits" at the ubuntu home page (google) and theres a total of ten pages available in the search!
