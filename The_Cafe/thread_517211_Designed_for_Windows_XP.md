### Post by stalker145 on 2007-08-04
That shined up nicely. 

I removed my stickers completely and replaced them. There's a product that they sell (depending on where you're from) called "Goof Off" which removes the gunk left by the glue on the back of the sticker.

I've gotten several comments by people that have used my computer and noticed that my stickers are not the standard Windows load.

These are available on EBay (where I got them, anyway) and you can probably find them elsewhere. I think they're pretty good quality metallic stickers for the price (about $10USD for 2 sets).
