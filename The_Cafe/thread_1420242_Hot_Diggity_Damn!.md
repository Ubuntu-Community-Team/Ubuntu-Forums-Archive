### Post by Ozymandias_117 on 2010-03-02
If you disable auto mounting of drives most any media player will work already. just go into gconf-editor apps -> nautilus -> preferences and uncheck "media_automount" The only problem is you will have to click "Places -> The_Drive_You_Want_To_Mount" to mount anything. Also, make sure the media player is closed before you mount your ipod. The only problem I still have with ipods in ubuntu, is the album artwork never wants to copy properly.
