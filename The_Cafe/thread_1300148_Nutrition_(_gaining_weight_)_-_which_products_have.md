Some good advice here.  

I'm goin to tell you to hit the weights too.  The squat rack is key.  Supersquatting is when you take what you would squat for 3 sets of 8 reps and instead just kick out 20-25 reps.  Basically the idea is that you want to put your body into panic causing it to release human growth hormone.  After your supersquat set, continue a well-balanced workout for no more than an hour and a half.

Third, eat foods that have plenty of protein.  Keep eating all day.  The experts say you should just keep sipping protein shakes and the like until you get a gram of protein for every pound you weigh.  It is a lot, but it works.
