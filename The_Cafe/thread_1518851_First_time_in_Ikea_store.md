### Post by PartisanEntity on 2010-06-27
It's cheap and designed nicely. But often the quality leaves a lot to be desired.

When buying a piece of furniture from IKEA, really look at the piece and make sure it is good quality and will not peal or fall apart a few years down the road.

We have some IKEA furniture, in our home office, a desk and some shelves, as well as our kitchen and two cabinets. But all the IKEA pieces we bought, expect for the pieces in the kitchen, are made from real wood and not compressed wood.

Much better quality IMO.
