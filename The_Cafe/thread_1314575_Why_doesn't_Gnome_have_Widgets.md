### Post by Xbehave on 2009-11-04
> **koleoptero said:**
> Plus conky works the same on all systems so it's not gnome's.
plasma should work on any desktop (just dont add panels and you won't even kown it's kde), conky does have the advantage of not using any toolkits though.

I think:
[google gadgets]("http://code.google.com/p/google-gadgets-for-linux/") (gtk **or** qt) [is opensource]
[screenlets]("http://www.screenlets.org/index.php/Home") (gtk)
[gdesklets]("http://www.gdesklets.de/") (gtk)
conky (no toolkit)
plasma (qt)
**added:**
[aDesklets]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adesklets") (lightweight toolkit, Enlightenment based Imlib2)
[cario dock 2]("http://www.cairo-dock.org/") (openGL based, but works with all drivers)
[superkaramba]("http://utils.kde.org/projects/superkaramba/") (old and has kde dependencies, but will work under gnome)
all work with gnome, try them and see which one does what you want.
