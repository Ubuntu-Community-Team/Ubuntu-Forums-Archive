Switching distros/OSes is something...I stray away from now.

mint, is cool. flash, mp3, dvd, xvid all works out of the box. though, compared to ubuntu, it's a little slower in terms of boot up and shutdown.

sabayon, i got hang ups after about 5 minutes of running. don't know what's the cause. no messages after all. just purely hang where pressing the reset button is only the solution.

with kahelos, im stuck with installing packages. was waiting about 30 minutes and nothing happens. don't know if its downloading something or what. no error messages.

with sidux, it's not as easy as it was before. smxi is no longer available. installing nvidia needs some manual configurations. not like before, there's just one command to do so.

back with ubuntu. why? because of it's community. because of this forum. and, it detected my printer. which is a plus.
