### Post by mcreskiller on 2010-06-05
I decided i would get a taste of ubuntu because on my netbook i was sick of xp and 7 was a bit sluggish, Im starting with a wubi install and if it keeps up this good im gona switch for good on my netbook atleast.

Things that i love : Full driver support, i swear better support then windows 7 had. It detects that my touch pad is multitouch and allows both horizontal and up and down 2 finger scrolling something windows 7 and xp didn't, the overall speed also i love, i can run many applications and it works perfectly with no lag or wait times,  compiz speeds up multitasking nearly 10 fold and allows me to have 4 fully usable desktops at once which is just so much fun!

Dont like : I get a bit less battery life and i would still have issues with it on my desktop because A: ati graphics card and B: i game a fair bit on the desktop
