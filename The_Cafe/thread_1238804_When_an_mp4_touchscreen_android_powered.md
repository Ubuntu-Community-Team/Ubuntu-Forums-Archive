### Post by .Maleficus. on 2009-08-13
Yep, Zii is a Creative project.

As for releasing mass product, I have no idea.  It's actually a really good deal right now though - $400 may sound steep, but you aren't actually paying for the device at all, you're paying for the Plazsma SDK.  They're giving you a free Zii Egg for purchasing the Plazsma SDK, which will still be $400 when the device comes out.  Of course, you don't actually need the SDK, unless you want to develop Plazsma apps, but since I would want to it's not a bad deal.

NOTE:  Plazsma is NOT Android, the Zii Egg can run vanilla Android and Zii's Plazsma OS.  They aren't charging $400 to develop Android apps.
