### Post by Technoviking on 2009-09-14
1. Getting connected to the Ubuntu Community: Creating Launchpad and forums accounts, and learning about Loco. For very new users.

2. In Search Of ... Help: How to solutions for tech questions in Ubuntu community maintain support options.

3. Switching to Ubuntu: Learning how to use Ubuntu to it fullest after coming from a Windows/OSX environment.

4. Reporting Bug: Basics and Reporting Bugs: Debugging: The first session is how to report bugs back to the developers and the second is how to get proper debugging information to them.  

5. The Ubuntu Development Cycle: An explanation the 6 month development cycle for Ubuntu. What does feature freeze mean, how do I get my ideas into Ubuntu, etc...
