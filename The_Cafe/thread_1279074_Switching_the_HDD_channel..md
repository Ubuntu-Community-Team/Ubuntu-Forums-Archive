### Post by v8YKxgHe on 2009-09-30
There is normally a jumper (looks like a bit of plastic, say 1mm by 3mm) on some pins at the back of the hard drive. Depending on the drive, you can put it in multiple modes such as Primary, Master, Cable Select. You'd need to set that to what ever you need, then set the other drive which I assume you're going to add in, as primary.
