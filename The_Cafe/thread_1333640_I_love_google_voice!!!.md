> You can also use Google Voice for regular VOIP through Gmail, so as long as you have a fast Internet connection, there's no need for an actual phone. Well, you do need a microphone and speakers, though.

you can link GV to an IPKALL free phone number. IPKALL is then linked to a free voip provider like localphone or iptel, etc...
Then using a configured voip spa 2102 etc,.. ATA device which is registered on the voip network, plug in a phone and make and receive free calls using the normal standard handset phone.
you can recieve calls to the IPKALL number, but to make calls you use GV which calls your IPKALL number and yopur destination number and then links them together.
It is about as free as you can get money wise.
