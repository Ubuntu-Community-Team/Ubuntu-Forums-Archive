### Post by DeadSuperHero on 2008-02-09
So, my buddy (and fellow forumer) sjsayers and I (and another guy) have been working on developing a nifty new site. It's still in its infancy, but hey, we have to start somewhere.

[http://www.teenlinuxlounge.com](http://www.teenlinuxlounge.com)

Basically, we felt that not enough teenagers know about how great Free Software is, and we would like to start by sharing our love for Linux. We are aiming for:
-A regular blog that is written by me, sjsayers, and Brandon (the other guy)
-A regular podcast where the three of us talk.
-A monthly eZine.
-Eventually, we'll have forums, we're kind of pacing ourselves right now.
Anywho, feel free to check us out!
