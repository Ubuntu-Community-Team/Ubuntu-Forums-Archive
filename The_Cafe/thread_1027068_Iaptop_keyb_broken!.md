### Post by gn2 on 2009-01-01
Often if laptop keys become detached they can be popped back in place.

If the key is damaged and won't fit back in place, sometimes you can get a single replacement key from Ebay if a keyboard is being sold on a per-key basis.

Ebay is the place to look for a replacement laptop keyboard, usually you can get a complete unit for a reasonable price.
They're not difficult to fit yourself.
