### Post by Paqman on 2009-05-30
> **Warpnow said:**
> Any idea if this company would consider me even though I have no work experience?

If they put "experience not required" in the JD, then that's what they meant.

For positions like that an employer will be much more interested in someone who is keen to learn and has a good attitude than masses of technical skills. Even for jobs later in life, a candidate that creates the right impression in an interview will get the job over one who's got a smokin' CV.

Presumably the job is customer facing? Your employer is going to want to think you'll be good with customers as much as they want technical skills.
