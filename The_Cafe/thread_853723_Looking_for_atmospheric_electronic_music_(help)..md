### Post by Spr0k3t on 2008-07-09
Almost anything by DJ Tiesto.  Some of the early works of Moby are very atmospheric.  Paul Van Dyke's originals can be spacey at times.  Banco De Gaia has some organic atmospheric progressional techno (check out Drippy to see what I mean).  Adam White Feat. Martin Grech - Ballerina (Matt Darey Remix) is quite superb.  Armin Van Buuren has some good ones.  Charlotte Gainsbourg does an amazing downtempo release of AF607105.  Dave Ralph has premiered at a Love Parade, some of his concepts are borderline Tiesto.  Dumonde's original "I Feel You" is a good one.  Some of the mid point Juno Reactor is good, some of it can be a little heavy though.  I absolutely love Thievery Corporation for atmospheric.  Most remixes done by Gabriel and Dresden.  Carl Orff (the one who remade Carmina Burana into techno) has done some great albeit bizaar releases.  Mark Snow (famous for the X-Files theme) has some very trance like tracks.  Music from Delerium is always good.  DJ Keoki has some great mixes that are atmospheric.  Ferry Corsten or Freddy Fader are a little heavier than what you may be looking for, but some of their non-released tracks could hit the mark for you.  Goldfrapp can be good, but I like the remixes better.

No collection is complete without Giorgio Moroder's "The Chase".  One of the techno songs that started the whole atmo themes.

I've got tons more I could list, including the darker or lighter side of techno.

Ack, I forgot to mention Robert Miles, look that one up on youtube... several songs/vids for him.
