### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-05-21
I made my Firefox faster and much less memory hungry by changing two little settings.

In the URL box, send the browser to "**about:config**"

Then look for the setting called "**browser.cache.memory.enable**" and toggle it to '**false**' (just double-click it).

Find "**browser.sessionhistory.max_total_viewers**" and set to '**0**'.

Helps alot on older hardware, speeds up Firefox on my Ubuntu machine!

-Robin
