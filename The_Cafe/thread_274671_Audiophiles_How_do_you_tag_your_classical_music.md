### Post by henriquemaia on 2006-10-10
I put like this:

**title**: 
work (with opus number or other catalogue, if available) - movement
eg: *String Quartet No. 8 in B flat major, D. 112, op 168 - Allegro ma non troppo*

**artist**:
Composer's name
eg: *Schubert*

**Album**:
Record name (or recording location)
eg: *String Quartets, Vol 4*

**Genre**:
*Classical* (as broad as possible)

**Comment**:
Recording performers and location (if available).
eg: *Quarteto Italiano*

I fill the rest of the tags if I have access to the information, like **Year**, **Disc Number**, and so on.
