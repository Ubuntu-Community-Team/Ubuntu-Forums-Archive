### Post by chessnerd on 2009-09-07
Speaking as someone who has upgraded both ways twice I would have to say that a fresh install is a much nicer experience for a few reasons:
1. Seems faster
2. Your system will run faster after a fresh install, while an upgrade seems to slow it down
3. Cleans up hard drive space because you don't have to reinstall programs and files you've found that you don't use
