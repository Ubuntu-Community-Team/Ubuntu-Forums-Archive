### Post by gn2 on 2008-06-27
If you are considering a netbook, the Atom will easily do all that you need a netbook to do.
My current favourite is the Linux Acer Aspire One due on sale in the UK soon for £200(ish)

If you want to run a Virtual PC or heavily multitask or edit video etc, and portability is important, get a 12" screen Core 2 Duo laptop. (£500-ish)

### Post by LMP900 on 2008-06-27
Netbooks are typically used for internet browsing and light word processing. The Intel Atom should be more than enough for either task.

Just as **gn2** suggested, if you plan to do anything intensive, then I would suggest getting a proper laptop.
