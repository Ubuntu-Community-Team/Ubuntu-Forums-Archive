### Post by Kobalt on 2009-12-08
I'm testing Emesene right now and I'm pleased with what I see. It also supports audio/video chatting with my msn contacts, out of the box (on my eeePC 1005H). The GUI has a nice look and feel with Tango icons so it's not that badly integrated into Gnome.
The good thing is that it seems to handle http connection to msn servers far better than my Pidgin does (and indeed Empathy...). We have a web proxy that seems to kill the connection after a while using Pidgin, and I didn't notice this with Emesene yet.

Great little piece of software, looking forward to v2.0
