### Post by seeker5528 on 2009-11-11
When I first heard of Pandora I went to check it out and the combination of their music submittal process being a hurdle to those who create music as a hobby and don't want to mess with having CDs made, and the indication that even if the artist/record label gets a CD to them the people at Pandora that review the submissions may reject it based on their own opinion that it is crap.

Last time I checked they did not have a submission process listed, indicating that it was in the process of being updated, and there was indication that artists/labels would be able to upload music, but I expect the part about possibly being rejected because some reviewer at Pandora thinks it's crap will still apply.

Last FM has a mix of commercial and independent artists, makes it relatively easy for artists/record labels to upload, and as far as I can tell never reject/take down anything unless some legal issue comes up.

If you create a profile, it's up to the scrobbling and derived statistics to determine neighbors you should be paired with, recommendations, etc... 

[http://www.last.fm/user/seeker5528](http://www.last.fm/user/seeker5528)

Takes a little while to build the statistics and you will probably have to ban some stuff you don't like, but relative to the amount of stuff I have listened to I have not banned much, usually choosing instead to wipe tracks from my profile and usually it does pretty good at playing songs I like and providing recommendations to things I like.

The usage of arbitrary tags helps too if you find one already in use that has stuff you might like:

[http://www.last.fm/tag/theyre%20coming%20to%20take%20me%20away%20hehee](http://www.last.fm/tag/theyre%20coming%20to%20take%20me%20away%20hehee)
[http://www.last.fm/tag/meaty%20beaty%20big%20and%20bouncy](http://www.last.fm/tag/meaty%20beaty%20big%20and%20bouncy)
[http://www.last.fm/tag/awesome%20guitar%20jams](http://www.last.fm/tag/awesome%20guitar%20jams)
[http://www.last.fm/tag/tasty%20guitar%20licks%20and%20riffs](http://www.last.fm/tag/tasty%20guitar%20licks%20and%20riffs)

If you can find your way to some groups dedicated to things that interest you that can help too, you can listen to group radios without being a member of a group:

[http://www.last.fm/group/BLUES](http://www.last.fm/group/BLUES)!
