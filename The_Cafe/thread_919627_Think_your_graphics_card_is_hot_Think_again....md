### Post by Vadi on 2008-09-14
After running the Unigine Island benchmark, which my 8600GT scored 8fps average @ 1440x900 resolution, I was sad. The benchmark has awesome graphics but my fps is so low!

If anyone wants to try their card, install phoronix test suite: [http://www.phoronix-test-suite.com/releases/repo/pts.debian/files/phoronix-test-suite_1.2.2_all.deb](http://www.phoronix-test-suite.com/releases/repo/pts.debian/files/phoronix-test-suite_1.2.2_all.deb)

And then paste this command in the terminal (PTS doesn't have a gui yet, will soon) to compare against my laptop:

> phoronix-test-suite benchmark vadi-28416-17373-7833


Oh, and screenshots of the benchmark: [http://www.phoronix.com/vr.php?view=12835](http://www.phoronix.com/vr.php?view=12835)
