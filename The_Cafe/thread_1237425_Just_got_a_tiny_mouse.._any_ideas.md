### Post by afroman10496 on 2009-08-14
I rescued a deer mouse when it was young ([http://animaldiversity.ummz.umich.edu/site/accounts/information/Peromyscus_maniculatus.html](http://animaldiversity.ummz.umich.edu/site/accounts/information/Peromyscus_maniculatus.html)), and it was a pain but worth it.
