### Post by Nick_Jinn on 2010-07-01
Once the initial novelty wore off I realized that I actually prefer using a mouse. Its easier to have my hand resting on the table than to touch the screen constantly, unless its a phone with small space.

Now there are some cool things about touch TABLETS, or touch laptops that fold into tablets but retain the ability to use a mouse, like you can use multitouch and play musical instruments. Thats awesome. But if it cant fold into a tablet, I dont think I would pay any extra money for touch. Also, I dont care for tablets unless they have a keyboard, so the only ones I really like are the laptops that have the keyboards that fold under.
