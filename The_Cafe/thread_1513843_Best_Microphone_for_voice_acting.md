### Post by Redo on 2010-07-14
Loads of options, for voice acting only it's hard to go wrong with any professional mic. Any quality condenser mic with a pop filter will do you good. Even a standard dynamic mic will work wonders.

The issue is more what mic pre-amp you choose. That will determine how well it amplifies your voice without distortion or how well it voids background hiss. The pre-amp will also decide what operating systems it is compatible with (that is if you want it to be USB or FireWire interface).
