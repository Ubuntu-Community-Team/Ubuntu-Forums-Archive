### Post by mystics on 2015-11-04
If I remember correctly

**Green Circle**: They are on Facebook at the moment or at least have it open in a tab. I believe this also includes being in the mobile app and/or Messenger.

**Phone**: They are not on Facebook, but they have Messenger running (though not open) on their phone.

Both symbols mean that you should be able to reach them with the possibility of an immediate response. People with the green circle might not need to rely on a notification, though.
