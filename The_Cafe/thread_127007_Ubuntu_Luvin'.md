### Post by WelterPelter on 2006-02-07
Switched to ubuntu about a week ago, after years with Mandrake. I can't believe how much I love this distro! First of all, half the stuff I could never get to work in a stable way on Mandrake (like k3b) is just rock-solid in Breezy. 

Secondly, I much prefer apt-get to urpmi -- with urpmi, I spent half my time hunting down sources, or working around broken source links, etc. A total hassle. So far, loading up the machine with new software has been a breeze. 

Thirdly, my usual all-thumbs installation process hasn't gotten any better, but the ubuntu forums have managed to solve virtually every problem within a few hours. That's really the coolest part -- the fact that I actually feel like I'm getting support in the support forums. You can rot forever waiting for somebody to help you on the Mandriva site. 

So, thanks, ubuntu and community. There is something very right going on here. I look forward to many years together. *toasts*:KS
