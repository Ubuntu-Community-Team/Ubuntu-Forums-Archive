### Post by decoherence on 2009-08-28
I built my own desktop!

"Which desktop has the most features?"

My desktop supports most features you'd expect to find on a desktop and what you do on it is really only limited by your imagination and available resources.

"Which has the least?"

Features can be easily added and removed from my desktop via drag and drop. With no features, my desktop uses very few resources and pretty much sits there doing nothing.

"Which lightweight environment has the most features?"

My desktop is extremely lightweight. As I said above, it supports near limitless features, within resource constraints. I haven't even tried to put all the supported features on my desktop!

"Which do you feel is the most usable?"

Usability is a very personal thing. Perhaps my standards are lower than some people's, but as long as I can store objects on my desktop, open and read them and move them from one place to another, I'm pretty satisfied.

"Any to avoid and why?"

Please avoid mine. Because it is so lightweight, I had to sacrifice durability.

"What is your favorite and why?"

Mine of course, but seeing as I built it, I might be biased.

I recommend everyone build their own lightweight desktop at least once. It is a fantastic use for styrofoam and packing boxes!
