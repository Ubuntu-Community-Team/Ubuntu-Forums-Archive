### Post by Endolith on 2008-02-03
> **banjobacon said:**
> Do you mean autoscroll, where you middle-click, and then push the mouse up or down, and the page will move in that direction until you center the mouse again?

No.  To make the scrolling iPhone-like, you'd repeatedly use the scroll on the right-hand side of your touchpad, for instance, from top to bottom and then from top to bottom again, and the screen would scroll continuously at roughly the same speed, because it would still be moving from the momentum of last time you did it.  This would be very useful for touchpads, and might actually be a feature of some touchpad drivers or programs?  Rather than the window manager?
