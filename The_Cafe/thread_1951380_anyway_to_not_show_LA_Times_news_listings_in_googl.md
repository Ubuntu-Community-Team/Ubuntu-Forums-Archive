### Post by samalex on 2012-04-02
Click on the Personalize Your News icon, which is a cog icon on the right, add the source you want to remove, LA Times in this case, then move the slider to the left for Never.  I had to do this with Fox News and some other Conservative rags last week when it became too unbearable.
