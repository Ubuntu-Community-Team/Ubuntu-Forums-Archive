### Post by cbennett926 on 2013-04-03
Well everyone, I found the best coffee shop on earth and I was wondering what your best coffee shop you have been to was/is? 

Allow me to talk about mine!

I came upon the greatest little place on earth today, a local coffee shop. Now you may be wondering what makes it so great? Well for starters everything is free! You can get any espresso drink, coffee, syrups, sauces you name it they make it, for free! What's the catch you ask? Well technically nothing! The coffee shop in question is called Second Wind and they are a non-profit organization that runs off of donations, so you come in take a seat and they come to you and ask what you want, and you can give them a couple dollars in a jar or even better they now have a card reader for debit/credit transactions! This place is wonderful! Free wifi too!
