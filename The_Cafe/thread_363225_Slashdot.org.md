### Post by PurplePenguin on 2007-02-16
I got bored of it a while back.  Although some comments are quite informative and helpful, too many are written by people who haven't even read the article in question (and their comment's topic is usually covered in the article).  Then there's the other large percentage of posts that are just stale old jokes that people use to show that they're part of the Slashdot culture (eg. "But does it run linux?", beowulf cluster, etc. etc.). 

I check it out about once a month, then just get frustrated and forget about it for another month or so. :D
