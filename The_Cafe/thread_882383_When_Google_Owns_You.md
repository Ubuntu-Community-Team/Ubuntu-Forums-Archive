### Post by mrgnash on 2008-08-07
Read Google's user agreements carefully before using any of their services, and you will see that the information they collect is strictly **anonymous**, and only used for Adsense, and other related products in much the same way that Last.FM monitors the music you listen to, in order to determine what else you might like. Moreover, all your data is encrypted, and whether or not other individuals can access said data is under your control. 

Personally, I am not at all worried about Google compromising my privacy/security -- although I might feel differently were I residing in China ;) -- but I definitely would be inconvenienced, not to mention irate, if my account was suddenly suspended.
