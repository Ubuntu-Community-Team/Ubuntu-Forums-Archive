### Post by p_quarles on 2007-10-09
Seriously? Not a very good dictionary, then.

It's one of those words that's so common that it's hard to explain. It's a nominal form of "to refer," meaning "to point to another resource." A referral, in the US at least, is what your primary doctor would give you if you needed to see a specialist.
