### Post by Rhapsody on 2006-09-13
Bi-arch support would certainly be nice, but I don't see it as too pressing right now. It'll be needed in the future though.

Boot time isn't much of a concern for me. I can wait a few more seconds.

Synaptic isn't something I use since I'm on Kubuntu here, but Adept seems to work well as it is.

Eyecandy is at the bottom of my concerns really. I feel that my GeForce4 MX would be screaming at me if I tried some of the more fancy Xgl effects. Maybe I'll feel diffrently once I get a better PC with PCI-Express support and a better graphics card.
