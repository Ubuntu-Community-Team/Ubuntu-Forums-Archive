### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-01-22
> **.Maleficus. said:**

Sorry for the threadjack, but quick question to KDE users - You guys know how when you have a window open in another workspace, the window still appears on the taskbar (no matter what space you're in)?  Is there any way to turn that off like the behavior in Gnome?

Yes.
Assuming you're using KDE 4, you have to unlock the panel, then go to the task manager properties (hit the "cashew" on the panel, then right-click on task manager).  Choose "only show tasks from current desktop".
