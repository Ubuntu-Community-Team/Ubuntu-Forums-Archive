### Post by alphabet on 2005-03-14
Oh man, thanks. This operating system really saved the day. I use my Laptop as a multimedia hub using s-video out to my TV. I recently had a major fail with Windows (browser jacks despite countermeasures and a weird problem with the keyboard sending information to the OS) and so I went out in search of a Linux distro that could fulfill my needs. I did the Fedora Core 3 thing and it didn't want to recognize my S-Video port at all, so I went o Mandrake with whirred twice and then broke, I was downloading debian when I remember that, in my search for x.org configuration information, I had found some distro called ubuntu. I grabbed the install cd, burned it and watched the download magic. The text installer made me all nostalgic like, and good times were had by all. I threw in the needed information for XF86 and rebooted X, grabbed atitvout (which was much simpler with apt-get than rpms, in my opinion) and tested it out, holding my breath the entire time. BAM! TV Out and happiness were mine!

So, thank you Ubuntu. You saved the day for my fun-o-tainment experience.
