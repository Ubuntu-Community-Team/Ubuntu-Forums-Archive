### Post by dravine on 2006-01-28
Aside from a lot of gramatical errors, I have to disagree with you on a few points. Namely the use of openzuarus as your basis of review. The current version is not a stable release, many aspects of it are still in development so some quirks are to be expected. Sharp has discontinued new releases, however there is a fairly recent rom V3 available on [http://www.myzaurus.com/downloads3.asp](http://www.myzaurus.com/downloads3.asp).  It's much more end user oriented than openzaurus is, and would be more in line with what you were expected from an end user standpoint.

It seemed to me that most of your complaints were with the default PIM applications, which I won't dispute, most of them have issues, however there are a ton of replacements available for them if you know where to look. [http://killefiz.de/zaurus/](http://killefiz.de/zaurus/) is probably the best source I can think of.  In the PIM section they have 107 applications. There's 222 apps listed in games.

Personally, I've been very happy with my sl-5500 for the past year and a half that I've owned it.  My only complaint is the battery life, which as you noted can be resolved with the higher capacity battery.
