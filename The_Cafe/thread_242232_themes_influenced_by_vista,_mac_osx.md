### Post by bonzodog on 2006-08-23
Don't think of linux having copied windows or mac, it's actually the other way around. 
MS got their theming ideas for Win Xp done by the people behind Windowblinds. The people behind windowblinds started out theming Linux desktops. 
So, in a long sort of way, windows Xp was influenced by the eye candy available in linux. 
Some Linux themes have been influenced by mac themes, and vice versa - it seems that the eye candy explosion for OSX took place at roughly the same time as the really good KDE eye candy was coming round.
