Someone who turns a monitor on and off when a computer freezes up shouldn't be using a computer at all--it doesn't matter what operating system is on that computer.

3. Someone who insists on using a Windows-preloaded computer with Linux-incompatible hardware and yet wants to install Linux on that computer and *not* buy new hardware.
