### Post by t0p on 2009-12-14
Another /. story: Some of you may remember I told you about Shannon's attempt to get into a Kindle's operating system in [episode  605 of Hak5.]("http://www.hak5.org/episodes/episode-605").  Well, a team of hackers [have done something similar]("http://hardware.slashdot.org/story/09/12/14/1515215/BampN-Nook-Successfully-Opened?art_pos=7") to the Barnes & Noble ebook the "Nook".  They managed to get root access to the machine, and the result is a miniature computer running Android and with free cellular connection to the internet.  Their progress is documented on their [wiki]("http://nookdevs.com/Main_Page").
