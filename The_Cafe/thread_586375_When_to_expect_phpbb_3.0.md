What do you mean hundreds of options? I'm using it as well on one of my forums ([http://revive.anime-illusions.net](http://revive.anime-illusions.net)) and I've got no problems at. Its very easy and simple to understand. Please take some time to read the documents and manuals that came with the installer.

Administrative options? Do you know how to get into the ACP (Administration Control Panel)? 

Once you are in the ACP, click on the "Users and Groups" tab. Then on the navigation on the side under "Groups" click on "Manage Groups" then under "Pre-defined Groups" click on "Global Moderators" or any groups you have created. Once you are there there is an option to add a user to the group. Everything else is pretty straight forward. ^^

It pays to read the manual. Like you I'm a first time user of phpbb but I've gotten everything much figured after visiting their forums and going thru the documentation. 

Hope this helps.
