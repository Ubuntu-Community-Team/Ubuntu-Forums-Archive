### Post by Mez on 2005-06-01
I'm upgrading now

For those of you who dont know how to upgrade do the following

```
sudo echo deb http://download.kde.org/stable/3.4.1/kubuntu hoary-updates main >> /etc/apt/sources.list
sudo apt-get update
sudo apt-get dist-upgrade
```

You can find alternative mirrors at [http://download.kde.org/download.php?url=stable/3.4.1/](http://download.kde.org/download.php?url=stable/3.4.1/)
