### Post by Maverick Meerkat on 2013-06-15
Have fun shopping!
I'm from the United States and shipping wasn't cheap, but it didn't stop me.
My advice to you, or anybody for that matter, is buy everything you think you might want, at once.
If you see a pen, or stickers, lanyards, mouse pads... add them to your order, it won't add too much to shipping.
Also, ask your friends about placing an order with yours, then share the shipping costs with them.
I have done this several times.

I love their stuff, and you will be advertising for Ubuntu wherever you go.
I get comments and questions, it's cool.
Everyone at my work knows I'm into Ubuntu now.

Another tip, if you see it and you love it, get it!
They may never offer it again, in your size, or that color.
Be sure to look at their sale items section as well, those items are going away!

Rick ( a walking, talking Ubuntu billboard! )
