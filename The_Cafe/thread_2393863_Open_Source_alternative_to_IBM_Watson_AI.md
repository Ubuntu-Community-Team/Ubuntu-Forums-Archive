### Post by grier-devon on 2018-06-13
I don't know much about Watson AI per-say, from my understanding this is being developed more for AI in big enterprise (but could be wrong). There is Mycroft which claims to be AI and does quiet a bit on both the software and hardware side of things.

I think the big problem with AI being open source is the issues with the open source philosophy. Not that it is bad, but in order for a machine or software to demonstrate that it has artificial intelligence you have to feed it data to work with. In order to have data you have to collect it from somewhere.  I am one that has no problems giving away my data to ethical companies especially if it is for the greater good but, I know a lot of people appose it at all cost.
