### Post by BuffaloX on 2006-07-14
This may be just a setting in your Volume control.
I have VIA soundchip also, and had to change some mixer settings.

Check if these are present:

Right click volume control.
Select Open Volume control.
Select Options.

If you have a setting called deemphasis turn it off.
Set all channels to PCM.

NOTE:
Other settings may apply for your system.
