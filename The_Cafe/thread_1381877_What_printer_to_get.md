### Post by humphreybc on 2010-01-15
Hey! I'm also a poor student and I bought a printer the other day.

I was told to get an HP, so, I did. And it is MARVELOUS!

I bought an "HP Deskjet F2480" and downloaded the hplip drivers from the net. It was a simple .sh executable file and I ran it, totally set up my printer for me with scanning and everything. 

The printer itself is actually very nice, it's a scanner/copier/printer and the quality is high - the scan I took of an image I drew was good, and I printed out a couple of documents and they came out nice and sharp.

I haven't tried printing a photograph, not really my thing. Home printers aren't meant to print photographs - being a photographer I usually go get stuff professionally done.

Anyway, overall I am very pleased. Go HP, you guys rock!
