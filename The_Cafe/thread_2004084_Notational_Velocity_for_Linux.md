### Post by moodle on 2012-06-15
Calling all lovers of Notataional Velocity, the open source note taking software for OSX.

A version of NV is now available for Linux. You can find the download links and instructions here:

[http://pypi.python.org/pypi/nvpy](http://pypi.python.org/pypi/nvpy)

Works perfectly for me in Xubuntu 2012!

Syncs with SimpleNote, and includes many features of Notational Velocity and fork projects nvALT and Resoph Notes.

Enjoy!
