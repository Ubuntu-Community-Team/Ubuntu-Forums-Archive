### Post by ashickur.noor on 2011-12-31
After some discussion in mailing list and local forums, we have organized a Ubuntu users Meet Up in IER, Dhaka University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. The program was open for all to participate. About 30 people where present there. There where new and old Ubuntu users. 

There were two session regarding FOSS and how to communicate in the community regarding FOSS. The first session was presented by [[SIZE=1]Aniruddha Adhikary[/SIZE]]("https://launchpad.net/%7Edigitiger1"), he discuss about FOSS and how a open source project earn money to continue the project. 

Second session was presented by me, I discussed about community communication, there where why and how to use forum, mailing list and IRC. This session was held coz most of our new users doesn't know where to get help. It was the main thing of the Meet up, to teach new user about the community.
