### Post by Queue29 on 2010-06-08
Chromium is open source and is always in beta, so it will break from time to time, but you get all the new features as soon as they come out.

Chrome on the other hand has Google's seal of approval for stability and is more so the 'official' browser. However, it also includes closed- source changes that only Google knows about.
