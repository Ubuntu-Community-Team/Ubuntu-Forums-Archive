### Post by ankspo71 on 2009-12-26
Hi,
[Debian forums]("http://forums.debian.net/") might be able to help you, or at least give you a secondary place to search for some answers. That is the only other larger forum I can think of that that might be able to assist with Ubuntu/Debian matters since Ubuntu is based on Debian.

Laptop users don't 'have' to post in the laptop forum, but it is a good place to keep laptop/netbook specific questions in. The 'absolute beginner' forum and the 'general help' is where most people get answers the fastest. Although, the threads there get moved down the line the fastest too, because it's high traffic.

If you ever see your post slip off the first three pages or so, 'bump' your original question with some sort of valuable info that might be able to help someone else help you better/easier, or maybe just rephrase your original question.

Also, provide as much information as you can with your questions so that people know how to help. *ubuntu version, computer hardware, the things you already tried that didn't help, the last thing you did before you started having problems, etc.

Hope this helps.
