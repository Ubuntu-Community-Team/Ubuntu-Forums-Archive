### Post by castrojo on 2011-09-23
There's currently a manpower shortage for Chromium, but Micah is working on it and it should be updated soon.

(Chromium in all versions of ubuntu has a micro release exception and is normally kept as up to date as possible but the former chromium maintainer has moved on and it's taken a bit to get it updated)
