### Post by costales on 2018-02-02
Hi everyone!

We would like to announce the next Ubucon Europe 2018.

[IMG]https://preview.ibb.co/emDmr6/ubucon.jpg[/IMG]

This event is just for you! ;) You don't need to be a developer, because you'll enjoy a lot of talks about everything you can to imagine about Ubuntu and share great moments with other users.
Even the language won't be a problem. there, you'll meet people from everywhere and surely someone will speak in your language :)

It will be in the [Antiguo Instituto of Gijón/Xixón, Asturias, Spain]("http://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=14/43.5404/-5.6684") the 27th, 28th & 29th April 2018.

There will be 3 parallel talks in 2 conference rooms and 1 workshop room, and we'll have a few stands as UBPorts, Wikipedia, Slimbook, Libremanuals...

And we'll have a lot of social events too:

[LIST]
[*]The Friday:
[/LIST]

[LIST=1|INDENT=2]
[*]Ubuntu & Beer: Good beer, music and company.
[*]After that, we'll have a geek music in a night pub.
[/LIST]

[LIST]
[*]The Saturday: An awesome asturian traditional Espicha. [More info here]("http://ubucon.org/en/events/ubucon-europe/social-event").
[*]The Sunday, we'll finish with:
[/LIST]

[LIST=1|INDENT=2]
[*]Cultural visit to the city: Termes Romanes Museum.
[*]Ubuntu hour with Pizza & beer.
[/LIST]

We would love to meet you in the next Ubucon |o/ You have to Register as attendee in [http://ubucon.eu](http://ubucon.eu)

If you want to share your ubuntero knowledge, the [Call for Papers is open]("http://ubucon.org/en/events/ubucon-europe/call-for-papers/")! |o/

Remember that you have [discounts for the hotel and transport]("http://ubucon.org/en/events/ubucon-europe/travel-and-accommodation/") (bus or train).
