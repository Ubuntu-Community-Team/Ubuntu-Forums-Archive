### Post by floke on 2007-02-26
Last night I had a dream, or rather a nightmare, about Vista.

I dreamt that someone gave me a copy of it, and against all my better judgement I decided to satisfy my curiosity, so created a spare partition on my laptop and tried to install it. Upon so doing, Vista greedily devoured my entire hard drive, wiping over my Edgy and Feisty partitions, and destroyed all my data, which I hadn't bothered to back up. To make matters worse, try as I might, I couldn't remove Vista from my drive at all, and nor could I shrink it to get Ubuntu back. I was completely stuck with it. And worse still, it was complete rubbish.

I can't tell you how the dream ended since my dog woke me up wanting to go outside for a dump.

Not funny.

It was 5:30!
