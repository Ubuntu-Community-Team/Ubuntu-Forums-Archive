### Post by eragon100 on 2008-06-24
Partioning is done automatically, you don't have to do it. Just insert the ubuntu cd in your drive while windows is running, and use wubi (an ubuntu installer from inside windows) to install it to the harddrive with a few mouseclicks. No partitioning involved, since the installer automatically creates a virtual hard drive in a file on your harddrive (you can select the size). When you turn on your laptop, you choose wether you want to start vista or ubuntu :)

5 mouseclicks and you have ubuntu on your laptop :)
