### Post by w0Rm210 on 2010-06-04
Well considering i just went on a HUGE wild goose chase to discover this i figured it out eventually and i'd like to show you all how.



If you use AWN (Avant Window Navigator) and want to remove the top panel in ubuntu 10.04 (Lucid Lynx)

First minimize this window (don't worry you will have it back in a second.)

Then click randomly on your desktop somewhere then (on your keyboard) hit 'ALT-F2'.

Then i want you in the box type in gconf-editor click on desktop then Gnome-sessions and find panel double click the key and make it empty then reboot and your done yes DONE (note after you do this you will not be able to do the 'ALT-F2' shortcut an longer after this)

Well that's all folks enjoy your new sexy panel known now as AWN xD
