Back in the 1970s/80s, Marvel sold the movie and tv rights under ridiculously lax contracts and the resultant products were terrible.  Anyone remember *Spiderman* back then?  Awful cornball 'kung-fu' movies with some dumb fool in blue tights.   Movie makers had no idea how a comic book works, so didn't know how to reach the comic-reading audience.

Then Marvel tried to get smart, and made sure they had a role in the actual movie making.  Sometimes it works ok (*Spiderman* was not too bad) and some just stink.  *Daredevil* stinks.  But not as much as *Elektra*.  Now *that* was terrible...
