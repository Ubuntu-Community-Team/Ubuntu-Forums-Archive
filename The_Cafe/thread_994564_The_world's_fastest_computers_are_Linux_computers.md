### Post by I-75 on 2008-11-26
The world's fastest computers are Linux computers

[http://blogs.computerworld.com/the_worlds_fastest_computers_are_linux_computers](http://blogs.computerworld.com/the_worlds_fastest_computers_are_linux_computers)



Linux is now present in 469 of the supercomputer sites, 93.8% of the Top500 list. This represents about 10 more sites than in November 2007, when Linux had presence in 91.8% of the systems. In fact, Linux is the only operating system that managed gains in the November 2008 list. A year ago, Linux was the OS for 84.6% of the top supercomputers. In November 2008, the open source OS was used in 87.8% of the systems. Compare this to Unix, which dropped from 6% to 4.6%, mixed-OS use which dropped from 7.2% to 6.2% and other operating systems, including BSD, Mac OS X and Windows, which were all down this year from the November 2007 list."
