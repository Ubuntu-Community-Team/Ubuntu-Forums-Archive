Then again, I have always been weird that way, putting technical merits over personal taste.
What was it in particular that you liked about the original style?

Two possibilities I can think of:

You could change your GTK theme; Firefox will folllow. There are indeed GTK theme engines which can look similar to Firefox's style. Murrine springs to mind, and Clearlooks 2.22 isn't too far away. (Quite an attractive theme on its own merits, anyway).

I think it also cooperates if you change Firefox's own theme to a custom one. There's probably a Firefox Classic theme somewhere, for example.
