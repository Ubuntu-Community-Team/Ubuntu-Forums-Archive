### Post by 23meg on 2007-09-08
[Rafael Proença]("http://www.cypherbios.org/blog/") has [just introduced]("http://www.cypherbios.org/blog/?p=47&language=en") Ubuntu Search Beta, which is a Google CSE (custom search engine) that searches official Ubuntu resources. After entering a search term, you can narrow the results down to forums, official docs, community docs, wiki, Launchpad, etc.

[http://us.cypherbios.org/](http://us.cypherbios.org/)
