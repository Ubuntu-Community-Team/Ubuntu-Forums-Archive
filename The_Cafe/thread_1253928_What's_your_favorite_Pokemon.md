### Post by Methuselah on 2009-09-01
The main single player games are designed to be accessible not hard so it's usually a stroll.

The elite four, for example, is hyped in the games but they're not designed to be extremely hard.
All their teams have glaring weaknesses as they tend to use one type and you are free to level up higher than they are, etc.

However, there are *many* possibilities inherent in the game's design that other people (and to some extent computer controlled 'battle tower' opponents) exploit.
So it's really after the game's officially finished that you get to try some of the deeper stuff like the battle tower.

Still, Pokemon is mostly fun for people who like to explore and collect.
Definitely not everyone's cup of tea but it's good for a few million.
