### Post by jbcfarina on 2010-09-30
I've just developed this plugins, I hope someone, apparts from me and my girlfriend, finds them useful. They are based on[ rhythmbox open folder plugin]("http://www.infinicode.org/code/rb/"), so works similar, adds an entry in the right click menu in rhythmbox.

[LIST]
[*]Search the artist on the wiki
[*]Search the cover online in Google
[*]Open the current cover
[*]Saves the cover in the origin folder
[*]Saves the cover embedded inside the mp3 (needs python-mutagen for work)
[/LIST]
All of them are in English (sorry for my bad English) and Spanish. All works for me, in lucid.
You have to extract them on ~/.gnome2/rhythmbox/plugins/

Enjoy!

PD: If you could see the cover in rhythmbox but you can't save it, just drag & drop the cover inside rhythmbox, worksaround for DesktopArt also, :).
