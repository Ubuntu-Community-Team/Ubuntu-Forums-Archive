### Post by KiwiNZ on 2011-03-27
At the end of this month I am stepping down as Administrator and Forum Council Member after 8 years.I wanted to take this opportunity to say why and to say thanks

As many of you know I have suffered a severe disability for the last 7 years plus following rescue work during the 2004 Boxing Day Tsunami. I medically retired from my employment to concentrate on my rehabilitation. Well I have finally got on top of that disability and have finally managed to fight my way out of the wheelchair and no longer require any walking aids etc.

Following recent devastating events in my own country, the Christchurch Earthquake, and in Japan, the Earthquake and Tsunami I made the decision to return to the Search and Rescue Roles I had been involved in prior my disability
and will be concentrating my efforts in Strategy, logistics and Coordination.I am unable to return to a active USAR role which is frustrating but the greater good is what is important.

I will be staying on as a Staff member of the Forum for the medium term at least however I am unable to say how active I will be as my availability will be some what out of my hands depending on where I maybe deployed.

It has been a great pleasure and privilege being part of the birth and growth of Ubuntu Forums and I met many genuinely good people on the way. I have enjoyed working with the Staff who are a great friendly dedicated team of volunteers. 

I would also like to sincerely thank all the members for the support they have given Ubuntu Forums over the years and their HUGE contribution that has made it the success that it is.
