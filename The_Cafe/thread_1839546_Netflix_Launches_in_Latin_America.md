> Netflix launches in Brazil and that over the next week, Netflix will launch throughout Latin America and the Caribbean – some 43 countries and territories in all. 

I was so excited, but looks like the service is the third-world version of Netflix. The title availability is not terrible, but I probably have already watched most of it. They don't have any recent titles. They have TV series, but just a few and only old seasons. According to some news I have read, the delay between the release in the theaters and the availability on Netflix will be about 1 year. This is a joke right?

Netflix will not deliver DVD's in Brazil. The service is VOD only. According to some press releases, they do not intend to compete with cable TV or traditional DVD rental services. They claim the service is a complement to traditional DVD rental. This is a joke right?

In addition, no Linux support as we all expect.

It was too good to be true. I guess April Fools' Day moved to September.
