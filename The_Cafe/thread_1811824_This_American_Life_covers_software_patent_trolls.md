### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-07-25
Surprisingly relevant - Public Radio International's This American Life did a piece on patent trolls yesterday, "[When Patents Attack!]("http://www.thisamericanlife.org/radio-archives/episode/441/when-patents-attack")," primarily on software patent trolls, and had a very unfavorable view of software patents in general while presenting the trolls as a "protection racket." A lot of interesting input from developers, legal experts and the trolls themselves, basically making the case that the patent system has stopped working and never really made sense for software, anyway.

The mp3 is free this week.
