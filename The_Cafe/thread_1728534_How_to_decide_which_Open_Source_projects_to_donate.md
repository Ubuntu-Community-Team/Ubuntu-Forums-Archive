### Post by tjwoosta on 2011-04-13
> How to decide which Open Source projects to donate to?

Which projects do you use the most? Of those projects which ones do you think would benefit the most from your donation?

I like to donate to smaller/lesser known projects that I think have a bright future. I figure lots people are donating to the larger well known projects, so my contribution would make more of a difference for the little guys.
