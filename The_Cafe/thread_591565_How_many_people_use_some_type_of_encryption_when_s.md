### Post by Irihapeti on 2007-10-25
Apart from the SSL connection to both my email providers, I don't use encryption.

I'm rather wary about what I send by email, anyway. It's not just a matter of who might happen to see it while it's in transit. There's also the question of what happens to it when it gets to its destination, and no amount of encryption is going to do anything about that. 

Many computer screens are in locations that make it easy for someone else to just happen to see what's on the screen. A message can get forwarded to just about anyone, either on purpose or by mistake. It can get printed out and a copy given to someone else, or dropped in the street or left in the local library. 

So, if I want absolute security, I use another method to communicate.
