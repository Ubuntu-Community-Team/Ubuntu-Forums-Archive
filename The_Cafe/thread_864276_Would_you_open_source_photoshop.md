Plugins would take the form of interpreted bytecode. This would allow third parties to port the base Photoshop package while still keeping compatibility.

I view it differently.

There are businesses who require features and updates, they pay the licenses for those. 

Unless there was a fork (which is a lot of effort) Adobe would still remain the primary developer of Photoshop for example.
