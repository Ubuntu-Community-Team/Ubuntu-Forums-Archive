### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-11-21
The good thing about this forum (I have found) is that you can guess! You just may guess rightly.

You have some experience in messing up your system. Did you not learn from those experiences? I know that I have learnt from the times I messed things up. Use your experience to pass on knowledge.

Pick a certain type of problem that you see some as having. Notice the suggestions that others give. Build up a collection of commands and directions that others use and use them yourself.

When people respond to a suggest to run a certain command and then post the results, I run the command on my system ans I compare my result with what the Original Poster got. I then try to identify the difference between my working system and the OP's faulty system. I just might spot something that could point the really knowledgeable ones among us in the right direction. Try to make some kind of a post every day to gain confidence.
