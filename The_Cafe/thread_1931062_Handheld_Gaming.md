### Post by TheNerdAL on 2012-03-04
I'm starting to get into gaming more. So I own both the 3DS and the PS Vita. I have owned the Original DS and the 2000 Series PSP.

The 3DS is fantastic. The 3D is amazing on certain titles like Super Mario 3D Land. The graphics are better on the PS Vita, but to me, the best games are on the 3DS. Nintendo always had great games. 

Which worries me about the PS Vita. It needs great games to do well. The PS Vita is an awesome console. I got it because of the great features, I mean, an OLED screen. A quad core processor. Two touch panels. It's like a high end phone, but for the price of $250. Great deal. The only thing that makes me mad is the cost of the memory. 32 GB will cost you about $100. I got the 4 GB with the starter pack. 

Also, I don't like the rear touch panel. It's gimmicky to me. And the screen is kind of too big to type with two thumbs. And I have big hands. I can type, but it gets uncomfortable. It'd be worse with the Nerf Case. :P 

The games I got for the 3DS are Super Mario 3D Land and Pokemon Heart Gold for nostalgia because I loved Pokemon Gold as a kid. 

For the Vita, I got the Uncharted game. I must say. GREAT GAME. Never played an Uncharted game before. 

Overall, I think the 3DS is a better console. Because it has great games that are already out. It can use Standard SD cards AND it comes with an SD Card inside, unlike the Vita. 

I hope The PS vita gets good games though. 

Also, I really want more apps on both. Like Youtube. I wish Youtube at least worked for the Vita, since it has an awesome screen. Both have Netflix, so that's a plus for me.

### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-03-04
3DS and smartphone owner here.

Phone games suck. You can play a tower defence game or an Angry Birds ripoff, but anything that requires precision or fast action will be too frustrating. Finger touch is unsuitable for most games.

I love my 3DS. I play Mario Kart 7 and it is a killer game. I also enjoy PES and Pilotwings Resort - the latter has excellent 3D. The Virtual Console is a great 3DS feature so you can play old school games, and it is backward compatible with DS games too so you can play CoD or any one of a thousand DS games. The only let down is the 3D camera which is just too grainy and low res.
