### Post by jdong on 2005-11-22
In case people don't know this yet:

apt and aptitude both have easter eggs. Most are familiar with apt-get's easter egg:

```

jdong@shuttle:~$ apt-get moo
         (__)
         (oo)
   /------\/
  / |    ||
 *  /\---/\
    ~~   ~~
...."Have you mooed today?"...

```

Fewer people know about aptitude's easter egg:

```

jdong@shuttle:~$ aptitude moo
There are no Easter Eggs in this program.

jdong@shuttle:~$ aptitude moo -v
There really are no Easter Eggs in this program.

jdong@shuttle:~$ aptitude moo -vv
Didn't I already tell you that there are no Easter Eggs in this program?

```

aptitude's easter eggs keep on going if you append more v's, but I'll leave it to you guys to find out how it ends ;)
