To my mind, the threshold for editing is much lower than 2000 (if it exists at all). If you have more than 2000 rep, your edits don't need approval. If you have less than 2000 points, two people with sufficient rep should approve your proposed edit.

You get 2 points per approved edit and can earn a max of 1000 points (IIRC) by editing provided your overall rep is less than 2000. That, of course, leads to a lot of needless editing ;)

Bottom line ... @ineuw, you're actually encouraged to edit whatever you want there provided you adhere to the conditions. I'm assuming you have an account there.
