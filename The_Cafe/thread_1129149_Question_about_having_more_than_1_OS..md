### Post by Paqman on 2009-04-18
> **Newuser1111 said:**
> Can it play games in VirtualBox?

Short answer: no

Long answer: no, unless they're really old games. VMs have onlu just started doing hardware acceleration, and it's still a bit flaky. So you won't be getting much out of your graphics card. Not to mention that you've effectively cut your processor and RAM in half.

If you want to play Windows games, install XP dual-boot.
