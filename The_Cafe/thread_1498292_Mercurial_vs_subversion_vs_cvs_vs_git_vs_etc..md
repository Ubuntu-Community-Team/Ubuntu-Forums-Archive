### Post by Simian Man on 2010-05-31
Back in the day there was CVS and that's what most people used (for open source at least).  Then two things happened, first people realized that CVS sucked and began switching to SVN which is extremely similar but much improved.  Second people began investigating distributed version control.  This resulted in a lot of competing projects since the area was so new.  Eg mercurial, darcs, git and so on.

I think it will settle down to most people using SVN or Git since those are, in my opinion, the best and most used centralized and distributed VCS systems respectively.
