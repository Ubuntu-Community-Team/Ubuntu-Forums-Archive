### Post by IHeequ5i on 2011-04-07
This is what I came up with - which may not be what you want:

With your cable plan, you pay $14.99 a month.

If you talk for 30 minutes, each minute costs you $0.50.
If you talk for 40 minutes, each minute costs you $0.37

The Tracfone is a flat $0.17 per minute ($19.99 divided by 120).
