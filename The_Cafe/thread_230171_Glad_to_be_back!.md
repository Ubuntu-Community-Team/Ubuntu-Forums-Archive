its been a long time. whats changed? well, canonical has gone into liquidation, qt has now gone public and abandoned kde because there is more money to be made from its mobile platform, microsoft has now released its own linux distribution called MSux, and linux's market share is now 46%. :p.

no, seriously. not a lot has changed. a tip for when you install dapper: when you are doing the partitioning, DO NOT backtrack(ie press the 'back' button). try to go straight through it without needing to go back and make any changes. and under no circumstances, do any resizing of current partitions. otherwise, it will give you a trace back error and screw up in the middle of the partitoning. thats how i lost the whole contents of my home partition. so be warned.
