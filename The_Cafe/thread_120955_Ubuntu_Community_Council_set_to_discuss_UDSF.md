### Post by matthew on 2006-01-23
The [Ubuntu Community Council]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CommunityCouncilAgenda") is going to discuss the [Ubuntu Document Storage Facility]("http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Main_Page") at the next meeting (tomorrow...see link). If you would like to contribute to the discussion (without being inflammatory, rude, childish, weird or otherwise unhelpful) please take a look at the [preliminary discussion here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UDSF/talk") and come to the meeting (to listen...unless you are a council member it's really better to make your contribution on the preliminary discussion page). 

Please remember to do everything in your power to show these fabulous forums in the best light by representing the atmosphere here accurately through being polite, kind, civil, and listening intently before you say anything. I probably didn't need to say that, but just in case...obviously I'm not forum staff in any way, but I would love to see the Community Council get a taste of how wonderful and unique these forums are in the computer world. 

Hey, who knows, maybe they can be overwhealmed with so much good vibes that some of them begin to spend some time here on a consistent basis.
