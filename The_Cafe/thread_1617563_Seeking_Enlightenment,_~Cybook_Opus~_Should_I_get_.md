### Post by Jazzy_Jeff on 2010-11-18
The question you should ask yourself is, do I want a e-book reader or a multi use device that can read books. The Sytec has an LCD screen and would put strain on your eyes when reading just like on a pc. The e-ink displays are just like reading a actual book. There is no back light so you would need to use a light to read in the dark. Battery life is also a concern. If you go with an LCD reader you would only get around 8-10 hours of continuous use. My Kindle gets around 3 weeks of use per charge.
