### Post by jflaker on 2007-12-08
probably the best option would be a PHP and MySQL hosting with osCommerce as your cart.  osCommerce can communicate with paypal and track your sales........


os = Open Source.......Powerful and effective app.

Consideration would be to keep track of your traffic and adjust your hosting plan as needed.  If you opt for a basic plan with limited bandwidth and you get popular....the overage charges, much like cell phone services, can be alot more than just buying extra bandwidth.

### Post by KCPokes on 2007-12-08
Depends on whether you are building the site from the ground up, in which case OSCommerce will work.  If you have an existing site, using CSS, you'll find that Zencart is much easier to work with, in my opinion.  I've set them both up and the amount of layout flexibility with OSCommerce just wasn't what I needed.
