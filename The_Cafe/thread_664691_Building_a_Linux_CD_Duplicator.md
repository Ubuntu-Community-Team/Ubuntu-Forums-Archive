### Post by mips on 2008-01-11
You could use a script to control cdrecord or you could simply use a gui to control cdrecord.

[http://turbojet.sourceforge.net/](http://turbojet.sourceforge.net/)
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=443164](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=443164)
[http://www.cs.wright.edu/~pmateti/Courses/199/PM/CDdup/default.html](http://www.cs.wright.edu/~pmateti/Courses/199/PM/CDdup/default.html)

Edit:
A PII might not cut it depending on the amount of drives you are going to use. Might want to consider getting a good PCI sata controller for sata cd writers. Dunno what you budget or exact intentions are.
