> Hey 

Thats really cool. Just a quick question where did you get the alternate icons? and can you restore your system to the default icon-set?

Just go to [IconFactory]("http://www.iconfactory.com/freeware") and download various iContainers to import into CandyBar 3.0. Some of the newer iContainers have Dock graphics as well. Also, you can import your own Dock graphics. See [mine]("http://www.jamesnrhodes.com/personal/macosx/files/") for tons of those... :)

Yes, you can restore the system default Leopard icons, folders, and Dock too. :) It works that well and is worth every penny IMHO.
