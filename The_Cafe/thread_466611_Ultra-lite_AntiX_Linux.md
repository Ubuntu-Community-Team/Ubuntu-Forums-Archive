### Post by MyronTany on 2007-06-13
I have loaded it on my old Gateway laptop, and I think it's already one of the best light Linux distros out there. I liked Xubuntu, but the latest version of it will not install on my laptop. Antix loads quickly, has lots of applications and has better multimedia support (e.g. I can run Flash and MP3 files). There are still things I am trying to figure out. I can't access Firefox with Flux, so I have to switch window managers to IceWM  to surf the Internet. Then when I wanted to play an MP3 file, I couldn't find XMMS in IceWM and switched back to Flux. No doubt most of my problems come down to not being a Linux expert.
