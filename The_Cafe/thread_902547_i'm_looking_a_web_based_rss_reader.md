### Post by kaivalagi on 2008-08-27
If it's more for your own website, many of the open source CMS packages out there support rss aggregation. You can have content on your website to provide links to various rss feeds of your choice.

An example of such a tool is Drupal which I am using currently, but there will be more. Joomla must do it too, I am sure. Take a look at the "Software Updates" on my website here: [http://www.kaivalagi.com/aggregator/categories/2](http://www.kaivalagi.com/aggregator/categories/2)

If you aren't running your own site then go with google :)
