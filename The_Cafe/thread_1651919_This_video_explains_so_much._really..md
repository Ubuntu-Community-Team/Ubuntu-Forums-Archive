### Post by NMFTM on 2010-12-24
I recently graduated from college with an associates degree in network management and I would often browse 4chan while in school and do my actual work at home. I found that when I did my work in class I was often distracted by the set time limits and while I did manage to get the assignments done. I didn't learn learn as much as I did when I was at home. At home, it usually took me 2x as long to get something done. But I had more time to read up on stuff, poke around with different options in the software, etc. Wheras in school it was a rush to get something done and turned in whether I fully understood what the actual assignment was about or not.
