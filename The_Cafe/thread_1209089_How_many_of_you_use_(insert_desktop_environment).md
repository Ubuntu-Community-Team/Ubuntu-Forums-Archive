### Post by AoSteve on 2009-07-09
I was just wondering how many of you are like me.   Over the last two weeks I've been experimenting with the "big" three desktop environments in Ubuntu's repositories.   

Xubuntu is pretty neat.   With a fresh reboot, I get to about 148m of 2gb of ram used at my desktop.  It lacks effects but is very friendly to the "power" user like myself.   I like it, just a heavy lack of eye candy.   It works especially good for my schooling!  :)  

Gnome..   I love gnome...   It's so fun to tinker with and the compiz effects are just awesome since I first saw them in 7.10!!!  Even with my low end chipset on this laptop, they are great to see!   Gnome is my home and my home is Gnome!  LOL

My new "toy" is the Kubuntu desktop known as KDE.   It's quite amazing for the eye candy, and despite the so called extra resource eating, I like it.  Plasma is just an awesome ability and I don't think I'd ever want to change it!   It's not bad at all!   It does seems to add some stress, but the eye candy is slick for "Show off".    And the widget system is absolutely perfect in my opinion!

So now I have three DE's that I use for different things.   Used to be on my laptop here using three gnome accounts for different "operations".   Now I have one for each that are totally different!   

So what do you use?   More then one?
