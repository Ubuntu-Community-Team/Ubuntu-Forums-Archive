### Post by koenn on 2008-11-01
> **~LoKe said:**
> 
What can I do with a UNIX box that's not connected to the internet (but networked with everyone else's) that will keep me occupied/entertained?
check if it has talk installed and chat with other users on other boxes (man talk)
or send messages to all users (man wall)

classic : send something like "format c:\  [YN]?" or pretend you're a virus.


use standard network tools (ping, traceroute, ...) and see if you can map out the network from behind your keyboard.
See if you can log in on any of the other machines remotely and look around a bit. 

take up ASCII art

write a board game in shell script and see if you can win it.

If it's a good game, figure out if you can transfer it to other computers on the network. Maybe figure out how to play the game over the network. 

If you happen to stumple upon a computer that seems to enjoy playing games, log out immediately and inform your commanding officer that you may have triggered  WW III.
