### Post by FuturePilot on 2009-12-13
AFAIK there's no way to do that. And even if you could it would be a false sense of security unless the emails were actually encrypted on disk. Someone could easily just go into your Thunderbird profile and read your emails and completely bypass any password. Best advice: lock your screen when you're away and don't let other people use your computer logged in as you. Have them use the guest account.
