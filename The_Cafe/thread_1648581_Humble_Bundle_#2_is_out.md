I don't know if its already been posted (I didn't see it on the first couple of pages), but the Humble Bundle #2 is out, it includes Braid (a native Linux version if finally out!!!), Machinarium, Osmos, Cortex ***mand, and Revenge of the Titans.

There's only two days left so get it while you can.

For those not familiar with the Humble Bundle, it's basically a pack of indie games that you can choose how much you want to pay for them. You could pay nothing, or as much as a million bucks if you want to. Me personally, I paid $20, I felt it was a good amount, and a good amount I could spare.

Right now Windows is contributing the most to the revenue, but Linux users are paying the highest average. In fact our average is about double of what Windows users are contributing. Who says we're all a bunch of freeloaders who wouldn't pay for software. :P

You can find it [here!](http://www.humblebundle.***)
