### Post by Ntacman on 2009-02-16
[Hackwars!]("http://www.hackwars.net/")The web-based mmorpg game where you "hack", for the ones that really can't ;]. Known about it for a while, played it back on my old windows comp. when making your account, you can even set your own "Custom IP". You make money by hacking other players' virtual computers, or open up a store on your own IP, and sell virtual parts and scripts you make with the ingame scripting/programming language.
