### Post by Sushi on 2006-05-22
The device was just delivered, and it's charging as I type this. I'm at work, so I can't do an extensive testing, but here are few initial impressions:

- the device is VERY sturdy! It feels really solid when you hold it. It does weight a bit, but not too much. That weight give it an addition feeling of quality.

- I paired it with my Nokia 9300 Communicator, and it seems to be working very well. Web-browsing through the communicator work without a hitch.

- The screen is VERY good. It does show fingerprints easily though.

- When the device was turned on, and left idle so that the screen dimmed (but was still on), it seemed to flicker a bit. Not really an issue, since when you are using the device, there is no flicker

- No problems with performance. App seem to load in reasonable time.

More comments and pictures to follow later :).
