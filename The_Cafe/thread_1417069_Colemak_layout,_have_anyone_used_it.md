### Post by Kdar on 2010-02-26
I think few days ago, I saw a thread on Dvorak, and I decided to gave it a try. I liked it, liked how my hands didn't have to move all over the place, that I could write so many common words just by having my hands on homebar. But problem that I found with it is that all key-maps got moved, for example combination of Ctlt with one of those: ZXCV.

Then on other forum I was reading about Colemar, a keyboard layout for English which was developed very recently. It was based on QWERTY, but it shifted about 18 keys around, but keeping comas, punctuation and common key-maps in original place.

Have any one used it?
