I wanted to create a single location for documentation on ALL linux/open source programs through the communities joint effort. What do you guys think?  I made a simple free wiki at ([The Syntax of it All]("http://thesyntaxofitall.wikispaces.com/")) if you wanted to see (its REALLY UGLY)
