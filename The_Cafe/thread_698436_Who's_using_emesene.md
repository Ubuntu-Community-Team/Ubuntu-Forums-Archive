### Post by oomingmak on 2008-02-18
I'm based in the UK and out of everybody that I know, not one single person uses AIM or Yahoo IM. They all use MSN / Windows Messenger, so MSN only protocol is fine for my situation.

I'm also not that keen on multi-protocol IM clients. In principle they're nice to have, but in reality you really have to know what your doing design-wise in order to come up with a client that's flexible enough to adequately cater for all the differing protocols while at the same time not becoming overwhelmingly complex. 

Emesene is just so easy to use and customise. It has a clean aesthetic look to it, and the plugin architecture means that it has room to grow feature-wise (while still giving the user full control over what they chose to run).

For such a new product, it certainly shows a lot of promise.
