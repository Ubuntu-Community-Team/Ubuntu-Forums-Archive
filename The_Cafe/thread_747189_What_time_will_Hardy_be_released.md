### Post by 23meg on 2008-04-06
Take a look at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ReleaseProcess](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ReleaseProcess). There are too many variables at play to give a specific hour. Just assume that it will be released sometime within the day. And the release date can be pushed back by a day or two, due to possible last minute problems.

Whatever you do, don't download Hardy before the release announcement. Yes, it will look like images are available somewhere under cdimage.ubuntu.com, but if you download those before the announcement, you'll end up with a half-synced, broken image, and slow down the syncing of the mirrors, thus delay the release, and then go around complaining "Hardy sucks!! Worst release ever!!111".
