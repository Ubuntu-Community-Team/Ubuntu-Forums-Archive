### Post by PriceChild on 2007-09-15
Latest ipods have some sort of hash in the itunes database which the ipod checks... if it doesn't exist or isn't correct (ie made by anything other than itunes) then it won't load the database.

See #gtkpod on irc.freenode.net for work getting around this. You might even be able to help them with disk images of your new ipod or testing.
