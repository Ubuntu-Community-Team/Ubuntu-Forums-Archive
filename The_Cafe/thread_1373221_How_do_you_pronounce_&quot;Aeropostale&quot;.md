### Post by t0p on 2010-01-05
> **koenn said:**
> it's a French word, meaning "(by) air mail"

Air mail envelopes have *par avion* printed on them.  Aéropostale was [a French aviation company that ran an air mail service during the 1920s]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/A%C3%A9ropostale_%28aviation%29").

Note the acute accent over the first *e* in Aéropostale.  It's pronounced AY-ro-po-STAL (the final *a* is  as in "cat").
