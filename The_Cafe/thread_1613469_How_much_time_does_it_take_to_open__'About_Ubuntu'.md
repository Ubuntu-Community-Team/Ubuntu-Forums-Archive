### Post by NMFTM on 2010-11-04
'About Ubuntu' takes 14 seconds to load.
Software Center takes 5.
OpenOffice Writer takes 8.

I was very amazed at how long it took to open About Ubuntu considering my system specs. I have a Phenom II X4 965 clocked at 3.4Ghz, 8GB of DDR3 (4x 2GB) at 1333 data rate, fake RAID0 at 140MB/s average read time, and a GTX 260.

I don't think the GPU would really make a difference. But I know that Seti@Home can utilize your GPU to speed up calculations. So, for all I know other apps might do the same as well. But I'm just shocked at how long it takes.

So I tested it out three times and got the same result every time. Even after rebooting my computer, letting it sit there for a few minutes so that any possible silent background startup processes would finish, and setting all four of my cores to performance (max Ghz) mode before clicking it.
