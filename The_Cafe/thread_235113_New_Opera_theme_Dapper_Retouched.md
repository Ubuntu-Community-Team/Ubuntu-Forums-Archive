### Post by picpak on 2006-08-12
Here's a quick theme I just made: it's based off of the Opera Tango and Dapper themes, with Human icons mixed in.

It blends real good with Ubuntu's Human Firefox theme (sudo apt-get install firefox-themes-ubuntu to see it).

Here's a screenshot:

[[IMG]http://img236.imageshack.us/img236/1063/dapperretouchedshotsj3.th.png[/IMG]](http://img236.imageshack.us/my.php?image=dapperretouchedshotsj3.png)

Download:

[http://rapidshare.de/files/29194158/dapper-retouched-1_0.zip.html](http://rapidshare.de/files/29194158/dapper-retouched-1_0.zip.html) (save it to ~/.opera/skin).

Enjoy!
