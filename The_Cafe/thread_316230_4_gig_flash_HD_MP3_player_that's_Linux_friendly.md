### Post by banjobacon on 2006-12-11
I kind of want to replace my iRiver H120 with a Sansa, but two things are making me hesitant (besides the fact that that I don't *need* it):

-Lack of Vorbis support. All my ripped albums are in Vorbis files. I could rerip them as MP3s, but this would be a pain. I also like supporting free formats. There is a Rockbox port in development, but it's unknown how long it will take before it's usable. 

-Proprietary USB cable. The Sansa does not use a generic USB cable, but rather a Sansa-exclusive one. If you lose it, you can't just use some spare USB cable you have lying around. Also, this cable is required for charging, as there is no seperate charger.

I guess I can wait a while and see what happens with the Rockbox port, or just give in and use MP3s like everyone else.

(Or I can be happy that my iRiver still works as well as it ever has.)
