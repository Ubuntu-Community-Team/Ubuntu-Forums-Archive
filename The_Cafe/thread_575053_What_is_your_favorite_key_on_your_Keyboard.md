What is your favorite key on your Keyboard?

Mine is the "[**Tilde**]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tilde")"(AKA: swung dash, twiddle or the squiggle):

```
~
```> **Other uses**

 [Computer programmers]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_programmers") use the tilde in various ways and often call the symbol (as opposed to the diacritic) a **squiggle** or a **twiddle**. According to the [Jargon File]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jargon_File"), other synonyms sometimes used in [programming]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Programming") include **not**, **approx**, **wiggle**, **enyay** (after *[eñe]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%91")*) and (humorously) **sqiggle**.
