### Post by tonderai on 2005-10-30
Gthumb or F-spot.

Gthumb has quite traditional folder-based navigation, whereas F-spot is more innovative - with a rather swanky slider to skim through photos by date, and export to Flickr etc. I'm still exploring it myself. Both do camera import and export to CD.
