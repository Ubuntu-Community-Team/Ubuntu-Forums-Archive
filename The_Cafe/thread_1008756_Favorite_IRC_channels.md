### Post by happysmileman on 2009-01-17
> **abhilashm86 said:**
> how do u become a owner of a channel?like creating a group called apghats for example,i tried /join apghats,
then untill log out,it'll there,then it just vanishes,there are group of my friends,we just want to have group chat.

"/msg chanserv help" will give you list of commands, I think to register a channel it'll be something like "/msg chanserv register [#roomname] [password]", you generally need a registered nick and ops in the channel (and it can't already be registered).

Then every time you join the channel Chanserv will automatically give you ops, you can also set ops with chanservs access levels, so you can have a few people who are always given ops, which would be useful if it becomes a big channel where 1 won't be enough.
