### Post by 1roxtar on 2010-05-10
As of Friday (5/7/10), I installed Ubuntu 10.04 on my best friend's desktop computer.  He started out with WinXP and I had "updated" it to Win7, as per his request. He later reverted back to XP and now has thrown his hands up.  I go to his house almost weekly to fix one thing or another.  

I have been using Ubuntu around him on my previously owned netbook and now my new laptop, for the better part of a year now.  I have raved on and on about Ubuntu the entire time.  He knows how much I now loathe anything that is MS Windows.  He told me that he wanted to try out Ubuntu on his computer, so I took a LiveCD over to his house.  I explained that the cd would run slower than if he had it installed to his hard drive.  

Nevertheless, I told him to try it out simply to see if he could feel comfortable using Ubuntu.  After about 30 minutes of trying it out, he slapped the arm of his chair and said, "Put this on  my computer and take off this Windows %$#@!!!!  It runs faster from the damn cd than Windows does on my computer".  I was more than glad to press the "Install" button.

He even told me, "I'll give you the honor of tearing off that Windows sticker off", which I did with an Oh, so evilish grin!!!!
