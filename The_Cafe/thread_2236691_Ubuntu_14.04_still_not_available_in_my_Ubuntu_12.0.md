### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-07-28
You'd probably need to contact the mirror operators to determine what their update schedule is, and if they're currently having problems of some kind.

You could change to a different mirror. If you launch the "Software & Updates" application, you can click on the dropdown arrow at  "Download from ....". Next, click on "Other..." and you will be able to select a new mirror. You can do that manually or click "Select Best Server" to let the app rank mirrors based on response time (can take a few mimutes). Note that this seems to select a server based on how fast pings return, not on how uptodate its packages are.
