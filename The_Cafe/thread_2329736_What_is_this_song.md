### Post by coffeecat on 2016-07-04
The whole of that video consists of two tracks from the soundtrack for the movie Interstellar. IMDb page for Interstellar: [http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0816692/?ref_=nv_sr_1](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0816692/?ref_=nv_sr_1)

The first 4 minutes (approximately) consists of the track "No Time for Caution". Your link starts 2 minutes, 26 seconds into that track.

After the 4 minute mark, there is about half of the track "S.T.A.Y." which is faded out before it finishes.
