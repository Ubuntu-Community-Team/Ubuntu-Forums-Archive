### Post by polardude1983 on 2011-07-25
Its quite simple in Cell C1 type =SUM(A1/B1)

Then select C1 hit copy or CTRL+C

Then select cells from C2 to C30 or whatever and hit paste or CTRL+V

It will copy the formula from C1 into the remaining cells you selected.
