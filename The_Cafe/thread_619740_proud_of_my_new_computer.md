### Post by gn2 on 2007-11-21
With your graphics card it's going to be warm in there, so make sure you have two 120mm case fans in a push-pull configuration.
(by which I mean one sucking in at the front and one blowing out at the rear)

You would be advised to use a better heatsink for overclocking by that amount, and don't do it unless you can afford to buy a new CPU if it goes horribly wrong.

A Thermalright Ultra120 Extreme is a good choice if it will fit:
[http://www.thermalright.com/a_page/main_product_ultra120_extreme.htm](http://www.thermalright.com/a_page/main_product_ultra120_extreme.htm)
