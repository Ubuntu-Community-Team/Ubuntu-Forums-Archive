### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-11-22
> **smartboyathome said:**
> You mean Compiz or Metacity, or Emerald or GTK Window Decorator (the former are window managers, while the latter are window decorators).

Actually I think they're all one in the same. Emerald and the GTK Window Decorator will actually replace whatever non compositing window manager you happen to be using. Metacity, XFWM, Kwin etc. For example, the GTK Window Decorator will provide the same, if not  more, functions as Metacity such as double click to shade. So the GTK Window Decorator is actually a window manager.
