### Post by cra1g321 on 2010-08-05
Sorry, guess i should of explained how i fixed it. 

No in the endup i didnt have to :p Im running libimobiledevice 1.0.1 
What i done was i restored the ipod touch again in itunes which installed 4.0 again.
Then i transferred a few songs over to it then i jailbreaked the ipod again. 
Im guessing it didnt work last time because i didnt activate the ipod with itunes again or i didnt have something installed in ubuntu.
All working as it should with rhythmbox now \\:D/

You will need to add the libimobliledevice ppa in Synaptic manager 
ppa is here [https://launchpad.net/~pmcenery/+archive/ppa]("https://launchpad.net/%7Epmcenery/+archive/ppa")
then after adding it in synaptic package manager, click the "Reload" button and then the "Mark all upgrades" button then click accept or yes to the popup. 


Yeah the new jailbreak is super-easy, even easier than the spirit jailbreak:twisted:
