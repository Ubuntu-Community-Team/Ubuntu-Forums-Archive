### Post by earthpigg on 2010-08-24
keyboard+mouse is *far* superior to any console controller. 

in FPS games, the experience is simply better. for most. regardless of the fun of the experience, it's common knowledge that the best FPS players on the planet use keyboard+mouse and not a joystick. a mouse allows you to turn your character around at superhuman speeds - literally. joystick players are limited by whatever the game makers decided ought to be the maximum speed. in addition, mice are far more accurate than joysticks at aiming (aiming and shooting is exactly the same procedure as pointing and clicking).

in strategy games, keyboard+mouse is the most viable. alternatives are possible, but awkward. check out 'freeciv' in the repos for an example.
