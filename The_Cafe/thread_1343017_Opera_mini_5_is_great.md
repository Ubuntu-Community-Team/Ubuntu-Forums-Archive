### Post by papangul on 2009-12-03
For those who don't know this already: Opera mini is the thin client and all traffic is routed through opera's servers, while opera mobile is the full fledged opera browser adapted for mobile use.

File sizes of mini & mobile are about 300kb & 5mb respectively.
