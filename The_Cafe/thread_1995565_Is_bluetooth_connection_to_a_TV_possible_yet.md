### Post by layers on 2012-06-03
Audio/Video Distribution Transport Protocol (AVDTP)

you can code it yourself, but is not industry specific.

Actually, it is being done (sort of) - WiFi from the iPad and the router, which goes to Apple TV, which goes to your TV. WiFi(IEEE 802.11) and Bluetooth are similar. Apple are by far not the first ones to do so - smart TV's date to 1993 in France. Also, all streaming media devices work through WiFi (RCA Wi-Fi Streaming Media Player).
