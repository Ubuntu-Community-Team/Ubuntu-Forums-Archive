### Post by Shibblet on 2010-11-25
Great phone.  One of the best Droid phones available.  

Got it on Tuesday, and immediately love this way more than my iPhone.  Took a bunch of photos at Thanksgiving, and hooked the phone right up to Ubuntu, and it mounted like a charm.  No fuss, no muss.

I do only have one issue.  When I am at home, I have no trouble connecting to my Wireless router with this phone, however, at work, I can connect to my network, but I can't get internet access to the phone.  Now, this is something the iPhone had no trouble with, but apparently this is a Droid OS issue.

After searching the net for quite some time, I came up the subnet mask being altered?  Some people have fixed this by rooting the phone.  I don't quite understand what they are claiming, but it is supposed to be fixed in Froyo 2.2

Anyway, Droid fans, this is the phone for you.
