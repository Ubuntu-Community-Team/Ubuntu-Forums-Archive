### Post by icecruncher on 2008-05-16
Hi, 
I'd like to announce the opening of the [Bearcave bookclub](http://pclinuxos-bookclub.forumsland.com/index.php). Created by two members of the PCLinuxOS community, it is now open to all book lovers. Membership is free.
An IRC channel is also open at #pclinuxOS-bookclub on Freenode.net
Please feel free to join and discuss. 

***Note that we have some genres like Tech and biographies. These are for discussion of the books and their presentation of content. these categories will be watched for going offtrack and focusing on the subject of the book rather than the book itself. ( for example, posting about a Linux tech book does not mean an argument over Linux Distributions is acceptable, etc...)***

thank you,
icecruncher
