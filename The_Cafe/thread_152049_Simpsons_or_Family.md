### Post by RS3York on 2006-03-29
Between the two of them...I'd select The Simpsons over Family Guy.

Fox has really only had 1 cartoon for the past too many years, The Simpsons, everything else has been too derivative.  The Simpsons 1000 years in the future? Futurama.  The Simpsons as "urban African-Americans"? The PJs.  The Simpsons with an unwavering attempt to offend any group who's fought for recognition in the last 200 years?  Family Guy and now American Dad.  

Of the cartoons, Family Guy is the worst relying far too heavily on "The Man Show"-type humour...defaulting to lesbian jokes when the writers are too tired to play up yet another stereotype.

But some people like to see that sort of thing, if they didn't Fox wouldn't have uncancelled the show.  I guess there's a market for brash-"I can't believe they said that, because I think the same while giving lip service to the opposite"-type humour.
