### Post by Uncle Spellbinder on 2012-11-12
The charge shows in both your Paypal account and your bank account. Chech the "payment" option in your PayPal account. Then click "details" for the payment in question. It should say pending or completed. If your payment was withdrawn from your bank account via PayPal for the transaction, it HAS to show on your bank account as a payment made via PayPal. That's how it's always been in my experience. And it's never taken more than three days to show up as a charge on my bank account (not counting weekends, of course).
