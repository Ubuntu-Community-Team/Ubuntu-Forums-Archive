### Post by Viva on 2009-06-03
I always thought the supposed increase in download speed by splitting files is marketing propaganda by download managers. I was downloading a large file from sourceforge and changed the option in d4x to split the file into 5 chunks just to check whether it works. The download speed did multiply by almost four times. Stopped the download, changed it back to one chunk and the speed dropped again:shock:
