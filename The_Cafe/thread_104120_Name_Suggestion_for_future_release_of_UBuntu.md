### Post by captainpotato on 2005-12-21
[QUOTE=blueturtl]
Hmm.. or maybe it should be something more suited to the spirit of Ubuntu :D[/QUOTE]

I vote for 'Tipsy Tapir'. A tapir is a *good* animal that fits the spirit of Ubuntu perfectly. They are important in the distribution of seeds in rainforests and provide a good indicator of the general health of the area - a rainforest that has tapirs (assuming that they live there to start with) is a healthy one, and a rainforest that has no tapirs any longer is not. They also happen to be very sweet-natured and gentle (despite their size and strength). Indeed, I suspect that tapirs think that they are about the size of mice ;)

As for the 'tipsy'... think of a tapir that has come across a crop of over-ripe bananas that have started to ferment. Given the love for bananas that tapirs have, it wouldn't be long until it had stuffed itself and become a little tipsy :)

If Tux hadn't become the official Linux mascot, a Malayan Tapir would have been a perfect alternative :)
