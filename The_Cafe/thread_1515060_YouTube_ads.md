I had the same problem yesterday and I finally found the solution!!!

1) On Firefox, go to options> add-ons > Get add-ons and search for adblock plus

2) Once Adblock plus is installed, right click on the Adblock plus logo and click on preferences

3) A new Window will open. Click on Filters> Add filter subscription> From the list select "Easy list English" and click on add subscription. 

4) Click reload 

5) Enjoy your add-free youtube experience!!!):P
