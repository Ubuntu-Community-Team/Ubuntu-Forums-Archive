### Post by Iceni on 2008-02-22
Look into X10 type hardware. This can be controlled from your computer and in turn control every power-using utility in your home. 

For example you can carry a small chip somewhere on you and lights will turn on and off according to where you are in your house. You can send a message to your computer with your cell or from another computer to heat up a certain part of your house befor you go home,etc.

I would like to help more, but truth to be told I only know it exists and have a vague idea of the possibilities.
