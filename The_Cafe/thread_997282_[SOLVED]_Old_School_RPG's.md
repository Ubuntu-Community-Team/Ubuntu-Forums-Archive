### Post by jmorth on 2008-12-29
I think you are thinking of text adventures. Programs like Zork, Hitchhikers Guide to the Galaxy, and the like. Am I right?

If I am, then you'll need a program called frotz. It's in synaptic under text adventures. For stories, check out The Interactive Fiction Archive ([http://www.ifarchive.org](http://www.ifarchive.org)).

As far as playing online, if that's your thing, you'll want to find some MUDs.

Hope this helps.
