### Post by kebes on 2007-05-12
Not sure if I understand your question...

Are you asking for a listing of all open-source software that exists? To my knowledge, no complete listing exists. However, a very large percentage of open-source projects are hosted or affiliated with Sourceforge:
[http://sourceforge.net/](http://sourceforge.net/)

Like I said, Sourceforge is the largest repository of open-source software, but many projects (especially some of the larger ones) are not hosted there. For Ubuntu-only software, you can search the full listing of repository packages here:
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/](http://packages.ubuntu.com/)

Hope that helps! (Sorry if I misunderstood your question.)
