### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-09-07
MacBook Pros probably blow both away.  Depends on the model of the HP and the ThinkPad.  There are better and worse models in each line.  Better to google for consumer reviews for each model and you willl get a subjective feel as to which is better.

I would opt for a used, professional ThinkPad than a new HP.  Better a used BMW than a new Nissan.

The aluminum case of the mac takes quite a beating.

Unless you spend 4 hours in a coffee shop or under a tree (where Mac users like to work), battery life is less important.  For a cross-country flight, I carry a spare battery which can be swapped while the computer is asleep.  On a powerbook, with Tiger OS X, you can close the lid, wait 5 seconds for it to go to sleep, swap the battery, then lift the lid and be working again in 4 seconds.  Try that in any of the new computers.
