To those who are new just like me....

1.) Start at Absolute Beginner Talk page.

2.) Look for Forum Tools on the right side of the page and click on it.

3.) Next click on "Post A New Thread" this will get you posted so others can "see" you.

4.) Select the Prefix: of "All Variants" to get to a wider audience, then with whatever suggestions given, you can narrow down your field of inquiry.

5.)I have found the posts "Graveyard" of unanswered posts. Pages upon pages of Absolute Beginner Talk requests for help that have been viewed by many but gone unanswered. So I consider myself fortunate on this day to have gotten the responses that I have gotten.

I have not got mine functional to the point of Internet ACCESS which is KEY. Am using my Windows machine to find answers and to apply them to the Ubuntu machine.
