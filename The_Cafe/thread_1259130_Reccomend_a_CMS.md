first which cms's you tried?

for me drupal rocks, installation, community and all modules are free/open. Has few cons (probably on all cms): lots of people use the same default theme (maybe not a con..), uses a few tech terms (you get used to it later) and they support 2 versions (the older 1 is the safest choice as some modules take time to get ported to new release.)

xoops comes second probably, but i haven't tested it enough

moodle (targeted at education sites)

joomla 

mambo

phpnuke

personally those are the best and have the biggest communities + plugins.

test their admin panels and get a taste, see which one makes your life easier and which one has the modules you need.
