### Post by GeneralZod on 2010-07-14
> **cj.surrusco said:**
> Is it easier to learn a second programming language when you already know one, or does it get confusing?

Generally, much easier - but if you switch between the two often, it can be a little confusing at times :) 

The main mistake people make when learning a second language is to keep on using the idioms from the first language, even if they are not the best way of doing things in the second language (or are outright bad ways of doing things).  Being aware of this pitfall and deliberately trying to learn the "best practises" of the second language are a good way of minimising this, though :)
