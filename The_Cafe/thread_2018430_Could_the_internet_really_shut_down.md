### Post by forrestcupp on 2012-07-06
I think a lot of people don't understand what's going on here. The internet is not going to shut down.

About a year ago, some scammers released a virus that directed all infected computers through their servers to access the internet. When the FBI shut them down, they realized that completely shutting them down would shut a lot of people's internet access down. So rather than trying to get everyone to clean up their computers and risking them losing access, they replaced the malicious servers with clean ones, so now anyone with an infected computer is still routed through those servers, but they are clean now.

So now, they're finally pushing for people to clean up their computers because on Monday, they have to take down those replacement servers to service them. Only people with the virus will be affected, and as far as I know, it will only be for a day. If you don't want your internet shut down, all you have to do is make sure you don't have that virus. If you're running Linux, you don't have much to worry about.
