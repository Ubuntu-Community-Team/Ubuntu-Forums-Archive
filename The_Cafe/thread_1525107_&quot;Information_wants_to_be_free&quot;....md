Regardless of how you feel about the issue of intellectual rights, I think everyone can agree that breaking a promise; being dishonest, is wrong. 

And in the large majority of these cases, you are breaking a promise by sharing the file.  You see, the files come with license agreements, which you have to agree to before installing the file.  Whether or not those agreements are immoral makes no difference, as you consciously agreed with them, or didn't read them before agreeing with them, which is pretty bad.
