> Overview
The Mozilla Seabird, part of the Mozilla Labs&#8217; Concept Series, is an experiment in how users might interact with their mobile content as devices and technology advances. 

Drawing on insights culled from the Mozilla community through the project&#8217;s blog, a focus quickly developed around frustrating physical interactions. While mobile CPUs, connectivity and development platforms begin approaching that of desktops, the lagging ability to efficiently input information has grown ever more pronounced.
