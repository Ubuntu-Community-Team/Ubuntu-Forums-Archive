### Post by coffeecat on 2013-03-23
A new “Ubuntu, Linux and OS Chat” sub-forum will shortly be opened. This will be a place where any non-support discussion about Ubuntu, Linux or other operating systems and their software can take place. The [Mobile Technology Discussions](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=426) sub-forum will remain a place for mobile tech discussion. The old Testimonials and Experiences sub-forum will be closed and those who feel the need to post their testimonials may do so in the new sub-forum. The Cafe will then become a place for non-tech topics. As before, religious and political discussion will not be permitted in either sub-forum, but discussion about the politics of open source will be allowed in the Ubuntu, Linux and OS Chat sub-forum. We hope that people will understand the distinction and stay off broader political debate, which always causes problems.
