### Post by kvvv on 2011-11-12
Hello,

A German bank, DiBaDu, is currently awarding 1000 Euros to the 1000 most voted clubs or organisations in Germany that have registered for this. KDE started off at the top spot, but has been falling in the last few days, and falling fast.

If KDE were to win this award, it would use it to fund a developers sprint.

If you are a user of KDE, or if you support free desktops in general, do spare about 3 minutes to give a vote for KDE. Click on the link below:
[https://verein.ing-diba.de/sonstiges/10115/kde-ev](https://verein.ing-diba.de/sonstiges/10115/kde-ev)

The page is in German, so click on the orange Stimme Abgeben button. You can use Google translate to read it in English. It will ask you for your email address, after which it will send you a mail with the final voting link. 

Cheers!

 :)

PS: It was ranked 1007 a few hours ago, but it's now 815.
Also, there are only 3 days left before the votes are locked.
