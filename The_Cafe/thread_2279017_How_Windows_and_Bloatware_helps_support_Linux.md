### Post by SeijiSensei on 2015-05-20
Most estimates I've seen indicate that the various trial software on Windows machines can earn the OEMs about $25-50 per machine.  That's as much or more than the cost of the OEM Windows license itself.  It's also a major reason why even though Linux is free, shipping machines with Linux pre-installed can be more expensive than shipping a Windows machine that is subsidized by these third-party programs.
