### Post by original_jamingrit on 2008-07-13
One thing to check out is zenmap, the GUI frontend for nmap.  It will do port scanning and give you advice.  It should be available in the repos.  A good tool for beginners and experts alike.

For more information and news on security and stuff, here's a couple good places to look.

[http://www.grc.com/default.htm](http://www.grc.com/default.htm)
[http://www.grc.com/securitynow.htm](http://www.grc.com/securitynow.htm) --regular podcast, but be sure to listen through the archives as well.
[http://www.gnucitizen.org/](http://www.gnucitizen.org/)
[http://insecure.org/](http://insecure.org/)  - a little creepy, but informative. :-P
