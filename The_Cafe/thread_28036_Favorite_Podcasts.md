### Post by heimo on 2005-04-21
Just to clarify your description of podcsating - it can be listened with any player (typically mp3, rarely ogg), no need for portable players, definitely no need for ipod. It's fun, it's fresh. 

I enjoy my favourite weekly podcast at Spacemusic hosted by TC -  lots of Spacemusic (his own too!) and soundscenes. Very ubuntuish podcaster, in my opinion.
[http://www.spacemusic.nl/](http://www.spacemusic.nl/)

 [http://www.lugradio.org/](http://www.lugradio.org/)
