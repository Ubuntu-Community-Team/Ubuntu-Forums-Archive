### Post by igknighted on 2007-04-17
I have read so many threads recently, mostly from new users, where the most difficult concept about linux to grasp is its purpose.  So I want to pose to you all: what is linux?  Is it a product to be used, or an art to be enjoyed... or even something else entirely.  I say we are artists.  We make out masterpiece and let you enjoy it... but it isn't about you, the user.  And for this reason it is not a product (IMHO).
