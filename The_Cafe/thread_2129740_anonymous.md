### Post by coffeecat on 2013-03-27
@Cakardic, it depends what you [mean by hacking]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hacker_definition_controversy#Hacker_definition_controversy"). If you define a hacker as "someone who loves to program or who enjoys playful cleverness, or a combination of the two" as described in [this wikipedia article]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hacker_%28programmer_subculture%29"), then if you describe more clearly what you want to achieve, I am sure people will be able to help.

But if by hacking you mean maliciously subverting computer security, be aware that this is not supported here. Please see the forum [Code of Conduct](http://ubuntuforums.org/misc.php?do=showrules).
