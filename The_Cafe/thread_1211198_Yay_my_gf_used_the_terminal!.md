### Post by stwschool on 2009-07-12
My girlfriend is not a geek. However, she loves ubuntu. It does all the things she needs such as playing sims 3, web browsing, ms office, art stuff, etc. She can install software through synaptic and if I tell her what to do she can find me her IP address. However, she's definitely not a nerd.

So.. she needed to convert some pdfs to jpg files. Now of course, the logical course of action is *convert -quality 100 -density 150 from.pdf to.jpg* though first you have to navigate all the way there with cd and ls commands to check where you are.

Anyway, I'm really proud of her, I gave her the commands and told her what they did, and she did them all herself. Of course she's denying now that she's ever been near the terminal but I also got her to install cowsay. I think I'm turning her into a geek!
