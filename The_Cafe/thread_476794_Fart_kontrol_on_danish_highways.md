### Post by BuffaloX on 2007-06-17
Don't laugh now, this is very serious. #-o

Denmark takes CO2 control one step further and introduce "fart kontrol" on highways around Copenhagen.
See for yourself if you don't believe me.

[http://www.sandgreen.dk/bill/billeder/fartkontrol-crop.jpg]("http://www.sandgreen.dk/bill/billeder/fartkontrol-crop.jpg")

Exceeding fart limits is considered very serious, the police are allowed to make these controls on all roads, and the fines are steep.

For a long time we have had special cabins you can enter, and activate the "I fart" sign.
This warns others that the cabin is in use, and it may be wise to wait a little.
Some cabins gives a bell (not belch) sound when they are ready for the next user.

[http://www.sandgreen.dk/bill/billeder/i_fart.jpg]("http://www.sandgreen.dk/bill/billeder/i_fart.jpg")

Probably Copenhagen is the best smelling Capital in the world, and also the one with the lowest CO2 release.
