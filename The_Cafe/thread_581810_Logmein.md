### Post by macogw on 2007-12-05
Try using Bomgar ( [http://bomgar.com](http://bomgar.com) ).  They advertise Linux compatibility.  It's certified to work where the person doing the controlling (giving help) is on Windows or SuSE, but they say it works fine with other distros.  It can be used to control Windows, Mac, Ubuntu, SuSE, and a few other distros officially (again, with "and other distros will probably work, we just don't make a habit of doing a full-certification test" type of add-on).  It works like LogMeIn where it goes through the browser and port 80 so you don't have to try to explain port-forwarding to someone who's already clueless.
