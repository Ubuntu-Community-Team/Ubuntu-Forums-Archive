### Post by matthew on 2005-06-30
I am using a Palm m130 with Evolution. The following sync perfectly: calendar, address book, tasks. Everything else goes into a folder in my Home directory called MyPilot.

It took me a bit of fiddling around with the PalmOS Devices settings available in the System-->Preferences menu to get things to sync up the first time, but ever since it has worked beautifully.

Addendum: I forgot to mention, I used the same PDA with Outlook for two years before switching to Ubuntu and can honestly say I have the same functionality with Evolution...at least for the things I use. I guess there may be something I haven't used consistently in Outlook or tried with Evolution, so I should be careful not to make a blanket statement.
