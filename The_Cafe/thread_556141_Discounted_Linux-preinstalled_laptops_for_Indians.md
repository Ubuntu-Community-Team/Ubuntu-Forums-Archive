I just thought I should share this for those who don't read the newspaper. ELCOT, our government owned electronics retailer is selling preinstalled Dell and Lenovo laptops with SuSE installed. Sure, it's not Ubuntu, but if they're putting SuSE on it, I'm sure the hardware is supported. The paper says the laptops are 40% discounted. 

They were forced to remove the brand name 'Lenovo' because the Lenovo retailers complained, but seems to be the same.
