### Post by linuxforartists on 2010-11-21
I'm a staff writer for a travel blog, and I usually get photos off [Flickr Creative Commons]("http://www.flickr.com/creativecommons/").

Users upload their photos and allow others to use them for free.

IMPORTANT: Users give different levels of permission.  I stick to photos with the **"Attribution License,"** where all you have to do is give the photographer proper credit.  Otherwise, you're free to use the photos any way you want. 

Other licenses restrict for commercial use, making derivative works, etc.  Choose wisely.
