Hi everyone! My name is heimo, and I have a confession to make. I'm ubunut. I haven't always been, it started just five months ago. It's causing me some social problems, all my new friends are a little bit ubunuts themselves. We apt-get dist-upgrade together, talk about patches, run Breezy... all that stuff, you know. But I haven't told about this to my girlfriend, she's XP user and ... I kind of feel that we should have more things in common - like same code base, compiler flags and editor settings - but she's more like Word kind of person and I believe she's infected. I know I'm clean.

I know I was infected too, but I've been clean for about seven years now. At first, it was very hard to admit. I was on dual boot treatment for couple years, on and off. I was lucky enough to have some good friends who helped me if I couldn't compile kernel or configure firewall. I admired my friends, they were just so well in charge of their lifes. Every single day was hard for me.

Then one evening I just kind of slipped back to Windows, just to feel what it was like and I got infected in minutes. My self-esteem was about to collapse and I knew that if I didn't stop using on that exact moment, I'd be gone forever. So I finally took the step and got rid of that filthy stuff. It was year 1998. That's the best decision I've ever made in my life.

I kind of feel obligated to give back to those who have helped me. I realise that it hasn't been easy - this work never ends. The way I can contribute to the community, is to help others stay on the right track, keeping control of themselves and their systems, understanding the beauty of life and freedom to choose, humanity to others.

I feel relieved. It's good to be home. Thanks, mates.
