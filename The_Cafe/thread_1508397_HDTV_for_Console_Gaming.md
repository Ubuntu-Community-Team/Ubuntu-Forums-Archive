### Post by Legendary_Bibo on 2010-06-12
So you want to research something, but you want the research done for you?

Oi, allow me to educate you on basic things to look for in a TV that I remember looking through the Playstation forums

there's i and p TV's, as a gamer you want p, so instead of 1080i you'd want 1080p
i is interlaced
p is progressive
I'm not explaining them though

Generally you only need a TV with 120Hz, it has to do with the refresh rate. If it's lower you'll have some video tearing. I have a 60Hz and the "tearing" isn't that noticeable. It's not worth the extra bucks. I've heard of weird things happening for games with higher hertz.

You want a TV that supports HDMI 1.3 standards because that's what game consoles today use.

Higher contrast ratio is better

Plasma TV's no longer have burn in issues, and have great color, but aren't widely made anymore

Look for a lower mms response time.

There's more, but I don't feel like typing anymore.
