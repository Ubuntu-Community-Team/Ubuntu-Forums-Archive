### Post by yamfox on 2008-06-10
[SIZE="5"]**Gamerbuntu.**[/SIZE]

A fork of Ubuntu that is both slimmed-down and maxed-out at the same time.

A fork of Ubuntu designed for gamers, this sleek OS with a dark skin comes with tons of games, as well as including Wine and the Gstreamer restricted plugins out of the box. AbiWord replaces Openoffice.org as the word processor, and VLC replaces Totem. For the GUI a mix of Gnome and Openbox is used to improve performance. To be involved, you can contact me at [email]yamfox@gmail.com[/email] about new features you think are worthwhile adding, and/or file bug reports to help make this distro an enjoyable experience for me to make and for you to use. Remember, no downloads yet, not until I get a Sourceforge project :)
