### Post by fowl on 2010-05-08
I would just like to take a moment to say thank you to all who have dedicated hours into the Ubuntu project. I have used Ubuntu off and on since i first heard about it a few years ago. I am an average nix newbie and have had a few issues overtime with both Ubuntu and Kubuntu. I am not a genius by far haha but i have been able to work out most of my problems with a trusty internet connection and the guides and forum posts i have seen.

The latest version 10.4 Lucid Lynx has met every expectation i have ever wanted out of an operating system. I can now easily stream video and music files all over my house. I have a PS3, Xbox, Laptop, Modded Xbox and my trusty Desktop all in different rooms. The integrated (Looks to be to me anyway) Facebook, Yahoo and the like into the OS from boot is nice. I do not have to load anything. I am sure there are others out there that are FB junkies like myself.

There are too many things i use and have fun with to list. I will simply say that with all of the features and ease of use finally poking in (like running something haha, you have no idea how hard it used to be for me to figure out!) and the great customizations of the desktop environment itself.

Hats off to you all and i truly thank you,

Jason Fowler
