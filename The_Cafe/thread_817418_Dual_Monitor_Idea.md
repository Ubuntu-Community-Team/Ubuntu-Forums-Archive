### Post by _DD_ on 2008-06-05
I have 3 monitors (4 when I dock my laptop and use synergy) and have never found myself lost, so for me this wouldn't be very helpful.
To be honest I don't even think about which screen I'm on - I just fly between them - and so having it highlight it every time I move my mouse between them would just p*ss me off :)!!

You cursor should be following the path of your attention, not the path of your attention chasing after your cursor :D

When using multi-monitor, don't think of them as separate screens which you 'switch between'; rather, think all the monitors (it doesn't matter how many - 2, 3, 4, maybe more :p) as one big screen which is just helpfully divided so you can quickly place something different on each one.

If you mean getting lost when you sit back down at your computer, it helps if you lock your screen. Then (when you're prompted to log back on) the background is black and so its easy to spot your cursor.
