### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-06-15
If someone really has lost your account info, they won't have your email address in the first place.

*Any* unsolicited email claiming to be from *any* business or site that has even a piece of your financial info should be ignored.  *Never* click on a link that's been included in such mail. 

If you think a business actually needs your attention, go to their site via a a known good URL you enter manually or have bookmarked.
