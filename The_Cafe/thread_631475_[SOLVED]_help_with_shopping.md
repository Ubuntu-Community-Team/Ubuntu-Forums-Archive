### Post by sethvath on 2007-12-04
Set out on a list of components you need, check the price on newegg.com and the likes and adjust for the parts should you find say a particular site offering a deal for the motherboard, cpu etc. 

Personally I prefer to get all my parts from one place, they ship together and you save a little and they also arrive at the same time. Waiting for a crucial part that went missing on route is not enjoyable, especially when you are hoping to build that system over the holidays.
