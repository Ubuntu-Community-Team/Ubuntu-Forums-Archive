Hi folks

I found a new feature in Opera a couple of weeks ago that I really like.  If you're browsing e.g. pictures that are numbered in sequence, you can click the mouse buttons 4 and 5 Opera will increment or decrement the number automatically.

e.g.  i am viewing:
      [http://www.somesite.com/photos/1.jpg](http://www.somesite.com/photos/1.jpg)   

Every time i click mouse button 4, it automagically loads:
      [http://www.somesite.com/photos/2.jpg](http://www.somesite.com/photos/2.jpg) 
      then ./3.jpg 
      then ./4.jpg 
      etc 

Does anyone know of a Firefox extension that can replicate this? Its a really cool feature.
