### Post by rosswmcgee on 2017-07-30
My first smartphone was a Blackberry Torch, my second was a samsung S3, my third was a samsung S6. The S6 is truly a remarkable phone no doubt. So I used it a lot, so much
that the constant tapping on the screen to open apps and read and text and email, caused me to get severe pain in my wrist and right arm. What to do?  I bought a wrist brace, two
different ones. I began wearing a cut off sock on my right wrist to apply gentle pressure. My pain kept getting worse, so I took a risk and went retro, and purchased a BlackBerry Classic
which uses BB 10.3 OS. Within one day my pain began to ease, and after five days, not wearing sock or using wrist brace. I am enjoying the Blackberry Hub, the keyboard and other
small innovations, that Blackberry uses to simply operation.
