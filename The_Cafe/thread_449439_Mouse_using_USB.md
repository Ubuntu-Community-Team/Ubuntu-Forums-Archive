### Post by Ender Black on 2007-05-20
There is no advantage.  The USB came after the fact and is the leading connector so you don't have to have 100 different ports on your computer.  Plus, USB is powered unlike PS/2 connectors so it opens up additional abilities for the peripheral designers.
