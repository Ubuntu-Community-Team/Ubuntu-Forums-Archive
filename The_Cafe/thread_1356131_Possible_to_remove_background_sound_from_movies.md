### Post by Shibblet on 2009-12-15
If this is a movie that is done in 5.1 Dolby Digital, you would need to find an audio editor that can strip out the center channel track independently.

Either that or reduce the volume of the Front Left/Right, Rear Left/Right channels, and increase the volume on the Center.
