### Post by lewisgoddard on 2013-01-24
As many of you are probably aware, GetDeb and its sister site, PlayDeb, went down a while ago, and now it has [been announced that it is to be discontinued]("https://plus.google.com/+getdeb/posts/FF1gRuZN7pM").

[GetDeb: Phoenix]("https://plus.google.com/communities/107220800493234933713") (as you can probably guess) is currently just a Google plus community with the aim of re-hosting GetDeb in a new and improved form (GetDeb v3, as v2 was the one that went down).

Any help (especially with Phython and Packaging) would be appreciated by everyone.
