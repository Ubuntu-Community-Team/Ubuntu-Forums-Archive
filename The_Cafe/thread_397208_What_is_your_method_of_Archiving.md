### Post by justin whitaker on 2007-03-30
External harddrives, to which I back up regularly, and make sure that they are ext3 formatted and disconnected from my running system, and usually powered off. 

I used to do the whole DVD thing, but with some 50gb of MP3s, I have lost discs, had cyclic redundancy errors, and all other sorts of issues with questionable burns. Try explaining to your wife that you can't get at your wedding pictures because a DVD failed. :(
