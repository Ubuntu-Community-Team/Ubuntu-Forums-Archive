### Post by Hendrixski on 2007-10-31
This hit slashdot not too long ago, the OpenDocument foudation has dropped support for ODF.

So what does this mean, who is the OpenDocument Foundation?

They are not the foundation that makes the standard, that's OASIS.  They were formed in order to support the adoption of the ODF standard, and now they're moving away from it in favor of something called CDF because it's more compatable with OOXML.

So in reality, this means that they were probably bought out, and that Sun Microsystems, IBM, and the open source community at large will CONTINUE to use, support, and spread the ODF standard. :-)  and so should you.
