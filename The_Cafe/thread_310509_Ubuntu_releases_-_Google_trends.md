### Post by Mathiasdm on 2006-12-01
This is the second time I've made this post (I think the last time was around the Dapper launch).

A very interesting thing to see, after every Ubuntu launch, is 'Google Trends'.

It shows how popular Ubuntu has been around every release.

[http://google.com/trends?q=hoary%2C+breezy%2C+dapper%2C+edgy%2C+feisty&ctab=0&geo=all&date=all](http://google.com/trends?q=hoary%2C+breezy%2C+dapper%2C+edgy%2C+feisty&ctab=0&geo=all&date=all)

[img]http://google.com/trends/viz?q=hoary,+breezy,+dapper,+edgy,+feisty&date=all&geo=all&graph=weekly_img&sa=N[/img]

I believe Feisty and Feisty+1 will (like Edgy) cause a slight increase, while Feisty+2 (the next LTS version of Ubuntu) will be another jump.

I love graphs like this, they're very interesting:) 

Oh, and do remember: this is only an indication, there are no exact numbers.
