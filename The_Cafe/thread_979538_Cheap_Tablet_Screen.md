### Post by tom66 on 2008-11-12
You'd probably have to go to the manufacturers of the screens, and I doubt they would send less than orders of 100. 

Of course, you could cheat; if you know the row and column of a touch, you can figure this into coordinates on the screen. I would suggest an array of infra-red diodes around the top and left sides and then another array of receiving IR photodiodes. Trouble is, any battery will have some trouble, and you've got limited resolution, unless you can include analog signals somehow, which just makes it even more complicated.
