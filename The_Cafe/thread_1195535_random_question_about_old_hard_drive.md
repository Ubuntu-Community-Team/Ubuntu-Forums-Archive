### Post by sharathpaps on 2009-06-24
@mctwists

Hey, what you need to get from the store is an "external case for a 2.5 inch IDE hard drive" . In India, I get it for Rs 200/- thats roughly 4 $ U.S . Once you have it, its simply a matter of connecting your drive to the pins on the case and screwing it closed. It's a 'no brainer' really.... You will not need an external power supply because the drive will draw power from the USB port itself. Once you buy the case, you can have it working in under 5 minutes.
