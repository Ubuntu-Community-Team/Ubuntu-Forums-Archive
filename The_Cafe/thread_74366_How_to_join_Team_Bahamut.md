The team system that is being introduced is a way to reward users who have shown a commitment to Ubuntu and the forums. We keep a careful eye on users here, and those that distinguish themselves in some area or another are asked by Team leaders to join whatever team us moderators believe they can help the most.

Its a volunteer position, but it is also a position that one must be asked to join. If you want to be on a team and help the forum grow, the best way is to do your best to help new and old users on this forum in whatever area is your expertise. We will pay attention to all good deeds done here, and if you prove your worth we will invite you into a team.

Unlike the old VIP system based on post count, the new system is a meritocracy much like many important projects in the Linux community (the kernel for instance).
