### Post by linux18 on 2010-08-22
Wine 1.2 sure has a lot of program compatibility, and a few minutes ago I decided to test the idea of defragmentation on linux. Yes, yet-another-defragmentation-thread, but this one has a definitive answer. It can't possibly get more definitive than that. I believe I have solved, using wine, the long questioned claim of linux and fragmentation. screenshot: [http://www.mediafire.com/i/?cs090nlei9hueaa](http://www.mediafire.com/i/?cs090nlei9hueaa)
