### Post by PapaWiskas on 2006-09-08
Tovid....hands down....easy to use...command line interface just works great, the gui however is buggy.

I use Tovid for transcoding, makemenu to make the dvd menu, makexml to make the require .xml file for the makedvd command I use.

All the DVDs I make use the terminal, its easy and it just works.

There are other programs out there that do the menu thing etc...but I could never get any of them to reliably work.
And when it comes down to it, a fancy menu does not interest me, I just want to watch the video.
