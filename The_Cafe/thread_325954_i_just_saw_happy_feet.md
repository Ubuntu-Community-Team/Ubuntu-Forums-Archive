### Post by Polygon on 2006-12-26
and i loved it :D

What are your opinions on the movie if you saw it?

For me, i personally enjoyed it very much. The animation was really freaking good, Pixar quality, and in some places, even better. The plot was intresting, but unique and generally enjoyable. This movie does have some realistic elements to it, such as how the penguins lived  and some other things, but it also had a unrealistic side, of course with penguins singing, talking, and dancing.

The plot itself made sense overall, (if you use suspension of disbelief of course, and forget there is no such thing as singing and dancing penguins) and the only real complaint about it that i had was the end. The end seemed like they were running out of time and or budget, and it just seemed rushed. It did finish the plot that they had been building up the whole movie, but they could of elaborated and explained what happened a lot better. There was also some deeper meaning, like how humans are destroying the enviroment and we have to stop.

The singing and dancing was great, and i constantly noticed my self keeping the beat by hitting my leg with my hands =P


btw this movie got 77/100 (an average rating from a bunch of reviewers from like newspapers and such) on both metacritic and rottentomatoes, which translates to "generally favorable reviews" and "fresh" (as opposed to rotten)
