### Post by aysiu on 2010-11-28
> **Oxwivi said:**
> 1. If you're not gonna use it for a long time, discharge it completely before keeping it away.
2. Completely discharge at least once or twice a week.
Actually, no.

If you are going to be plugged in for a long time, discharge to about 40-50% and then store the battery in a cool, *dry* place.

Do full discharges only very rarely (once a month at the most) to recalibrate the battery reading. Full discharges actually decrease battery capacity.

For more details, read [How to prolong lithium-based batteries](http://batteryuniversity.com/learn/article/how_to_prolong_lithium_based_batteries)
You can find a shorter version at the Apple website: [http://www.apple.com/batteries/notebooks.html](http://www.apple.com/batteries/notebooks.html) (yes, non-Apple notebooks also use lithium ion batteries, so the tips apply to all current laptops).
