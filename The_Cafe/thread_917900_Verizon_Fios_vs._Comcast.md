### Post by Nopposan on 2008-09-12
I know a linux fan who's using Verizon FIOS near Washington DC.  He says it's working just great for him.  I've got Comcast and I must report that although the technician who came to my house to fix our connection issue was very patient and helpful, their customer service is generally slow and less helpful than Verizon DSL's.  Furthermore, the cable connections in my neighborhood are a mess; some of the cables are exposed above the ground now and I'm just waiting for some kid on a mountain bike to knock out all the cable connections in the whole subdivision!

BTW, I was having a similar problem to yours and the Comcast tech who came out to our house showed me just how many cable connections are in line behind my cable modem; it's amazing, and I'd bet that the possibility of any one connection going wrong is somehow positively correlated with the number of connections! :-)  As it turned out, we just had to change out one of the cables that's in our house.  Maybe you have a similar problem; until you get FIOS try tightening all of the connections you can find, and then if that doesn't work, call Comcast to come test the cables and connections.

Cheers.
