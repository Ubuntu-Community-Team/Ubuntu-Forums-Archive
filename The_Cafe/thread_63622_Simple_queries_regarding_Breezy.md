### Post by az on 2005-09-08
It is a moving target.  As pakcages are changed and bugs are fixed, other may crop up.

If you have no important data to lose, and you are interested in identifying bugs for others to fix, then you can upgrade to breezy and play around with it.

It happens to run perfectly fine on my machines.

You can always create a small (3 gig) partition on your hard drive and install it there.  You can dual or triple boot while you try it out.
