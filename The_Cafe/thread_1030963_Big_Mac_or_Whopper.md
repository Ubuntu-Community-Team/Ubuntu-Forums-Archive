### Post by Swagman on 2009-01-05
Actually Hungry Jacks are NOT owned by BK.

In 1971 a bloke wanted to open up a burger bar in Western Australia. There was no McDonalds presence in WA at that time. He approached BK and asked for a Franchise but BK thought it would be a disaster so they refused to allow him to go under the BK brand name but allowed him to set up as Hungry Jacks using their products with his own finance. (No risk to them at all)

It was a runaway success.

BK then thought.. "Hang on a minute,  we want in" and tried to pull the brand off him but the Australian law courts said "go swivel"

So BK had to setup and compete against their own product.
