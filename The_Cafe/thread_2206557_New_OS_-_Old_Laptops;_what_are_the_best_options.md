### Post by papibe on 2014-02-19
Hi D_H.

It all depends on the hardware.

My a rule of thumb (IMHO):
```

pentium 3  (>= 128 Mb RAM) -> Lubuntu
pentium 4  (<    1 Gb RAM) -> Lubuntu
penitum 4  (>=   1 Gb RAM) -> Xubuntu
dual core  (<    1 Gb RAM) -> Xubuntu
dual core  (>=   2 Gb RAM) -> Ubuntu LTS
```
Regards.
