### Post by t0p on 2009-08-24
I see relatively few trolls in these forums.  Sure there are users like me, ocasionally annoying and (unintentionally) rude at times.  But true trolls rarely pop up.  Whether this is due to the hard-line, take-no-prisoners attitude of the mods, or whether they find Ubuntu too boring a subject to troll about, I don't know.  But you will find plenty of "anti-Linux" trolls on other technology-centric web sites and Usenet newsgroups.  In fact, if you want to examine the troll in its native habitat, you should fire up your newsreader and check out the newsgroups!  "Anti-Linux" trolls are an interesting breed.  You just need to remember not to take them too seriously.  They are aiming to get a reaction, that's all.  They don't *really* want to fsck your mother! ;)

[I've put the phrase "anti-linux" in quotes because many "anti-Linux" trolls actually *like* Linux.  And many more may not particularly like Linux, but they don't actively *hate* it either.  They just want to wind you up.  If you get all offended, they have won.  This is why many folk say "Do not feed the trolls."  Though I am aware how hard that can be sometimes.]
