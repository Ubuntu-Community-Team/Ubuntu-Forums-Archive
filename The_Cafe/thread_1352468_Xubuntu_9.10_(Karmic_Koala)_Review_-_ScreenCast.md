### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-12-12
I like the idea of introducing people to Xubuntu that way... I think part of the reason it seems so random is that you were using Xubuntu for the very first time as you were making the video.

I might suggest that you get to know it, and show newbies how to set up a custom panel, change the wallpaper (and perhaps how to *add* wallpapers), then give a quick tour of the applications.

Also if it were me I would tell them *how* and why Xubuntu is different from Ubuntu. No PulseAudio, for example; no Mono. Explain what some of the default applications do (For example I had to choose an app to open pictures with, and I didn't even recognize several of the names because I'm new to Xfce).
