### Post by cespinal on 2012-01-27
Yes. Kubuntu is just Ubuntu with the KDE environment. Yes, some times compatibility issues may differ between ubuntu and kubuntu, but this cases are not frequent. 

I had experiences in the past where kde behaved better than gnome/unity and vice-versa. 

Today KDE is VERY solid. It used to be a tradeoff for me between love for KDE and its little bugs, but now I can hardly detect any annoyances. 

Also remember that in KDE, you can change almost everything, including the location of buttons you don't like. Or you can remove those buttons for good, or add more... 

You can see Kubuntu might be a little less popular, but there are always people willing to help. 

Hope you enjoy it.
