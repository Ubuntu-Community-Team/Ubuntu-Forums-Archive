### Post by Keyper7 on 2008-11-16
Okay, starting today I'll play a new, personal game, inspired by the Ubuntu Developers 5-a-day bug hunt. I wanted a structured, systematic way of helping people here instead of randomly keeping track of the posts I replied to, so I thought "what the heck". :)

These will be my personal rules in the forum from now on:

1 - I just searched the forum for five threads asking for help, which I could somehow give, and tried to help.
2 - I'll re-check those threads everyday and keep trying to help until the OP states that the problem is solved or I can conclude that it cannot be solved or worked around. I'll check them more than once per day if possible, but at least once I will.
3 - For each solved thread, another one will take its place. Always 5 threads, each day.
