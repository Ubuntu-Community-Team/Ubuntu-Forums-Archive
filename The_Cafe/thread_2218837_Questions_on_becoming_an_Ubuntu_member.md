### Post by bapoumba on 2014-04-22
Moved to the Cafe as this is not a support request.

Re: Ubuntu Membership via the ubuntuforums, please have a look at [http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=404](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=404) and in particular this sticky : [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1750642](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1750642) and this wiki page : [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Forums/Membership](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Forums/Membership)
You can have a look at other's applications and at their wiki/launchpad pages. So yes, you can get Ubuntu Membership if you contribute to the forums. 

Re: Ubuntu Membership, please have a look here [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership)

I do not think there is an age limit, or that you'll have any kind of bonus being from a particular area or another :p
