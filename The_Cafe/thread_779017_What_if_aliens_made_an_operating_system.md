### Post by PrimoTurbo on 2008-05-02
Sometimes I try to imagine an advanced alien civilization and how their operating systems and computers would work and look. My first guess is that they would have no need for monitors or probably any external hardware, their displays would be integrated into their mind. So their brain would be able to project an image in any format, shape or size anywhere. This ability would be shared and they would be able to view things with other beings at the same time. Furthermore they might have figured out a way to share emotional states or frame of mind. A frighting concept that can have a great positive impact, imagine relationships based on that level of understanding. Perhaps they have been able to genetically modify themselves to such a degree that they are born with integrated biological computers embedded in their minds. Computers that further evolve as their technology progresses probably at an incredibly fast pace. Or maybe they reached the limit of technology, any calculation is possible including infinite ones? No limitations perhaps, sounds a little  out there but according to my understanding of physics you could technically have more than one infinite.

I also wonder if they had an older human made computer, they would be able to design the most efficient operating system for it in a blink of an eye. Perhaps your Pentium1 would be able to run all the most recent games, and have much more storage with no hardware modification but some type of incredible software. One that takes everything to the limit.

Insane, I know. What are your thoughts?
