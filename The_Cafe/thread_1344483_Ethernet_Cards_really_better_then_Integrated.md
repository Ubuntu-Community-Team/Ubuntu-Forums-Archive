Two questions:

A.  How fast is your local network (100Mb/s, most likely, if not faster)
B.  How fast is your Internet connection?

If A is substantially greater than B, then don't worry about it - the Internet connection is the bottleneck, and changing Ethernet devices won't have any effect.

If B is close to A, then *maybe* a better Ethernet card would make a difference.  But in that case, you'd want a high-end Ethernet card (one that's basically a computer in its own right), which your old Ethernet card probably isn't.

Lloyd B.
