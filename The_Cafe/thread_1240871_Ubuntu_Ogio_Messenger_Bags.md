### Post by tuskenraider on 2009-08-15
i have the blue version..  its a great bag.. i love it...  tho it says it fits a 17 inch lappy and it will but its SNUG! so snug infact that it took a little while for the fabric to stretch so i could fully zip up the bag..

The only thing i dont like about it .. are the clips at the ends of the strap... on both sides..  they are rectangles, and what ends up happening is the strap and the connecter fabric on the bag itself become bunched in the ends of the plastic rectangle.  its slightly annoying.. but other than that... great bag... lots o space lots o pockets. 

I can fit.. my 17inch acer 1040z laptop with cord trackball pens pencils a thin book and misc odds and ends in it...  

i very much suggest getting it if your getting a messenger bag.
