### Post by ExSuSEusr on 2007-09-07
I have to say I am thoroughly impressed with Ubuntu "Fawn."  I am a former SuSE user and after getting tired of needed a couple of days to get everything working in SuSE, I wanted to try something new.  What would have and usually took a few days to get done in SuSE took me a few hours in Ubuntu.  

It's just amazing how user friendly Ubuntu is and THAT is exactly what Linux in general needs.  While the other distros are great most people don't have either the time or knowledge to configure everything to the point they no longer need that 'other' bloated memory drain of an OS (I won't mention any names :) ).  Enter Ubuntu.  

Finally, a Linux distro with a learning curve for the average PC user!
