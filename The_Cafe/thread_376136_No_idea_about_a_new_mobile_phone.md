### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-03-17
What carrier are you with? Thinking of switching any time soon?

If you're happy with your carrier, I suggest going to the local department store and buying a prepaid mobile pack. These things are roughly $90 in Australia ($70 US) and contain a mobile phone and SIM card with some credit on it.

Make sure you buy the prepaid pack for the carrier that you're on - for instance, if you're on Virgin Mobile now, buy the Virgin Mobile pack. That way, you can just put your current SIM card into the new phone. The phone is locked to the network, but that doesn't really matter if you don't want to switch carrier, and it's significantly cheaper than buying a new phone on its own.
