### Post by oldsoundguy on 2010-09-16
Every outfit has a "sound library".  And, to keep things under budget, they will utilize that.  Problem is that it is a UNIVERSAL and relatively FREE library and many do not go out and record the world to augment that library .. cost of sending out a couple people with a quality microphone and a Nagra tape recorder is beyond their budget.  Forget sitting down and actually creating effects on a computer   !

Most do NOT have the budget of a Lucas or Coppola production, so you get what they have .. be it games, a TV show or a movie.  

Time spent in the Foley room and on overdubs of effects to the sound track are considered "a waste" by some companies that are run by VISUALLY oriented producers/directors.

Academy Awards are given to those that DO spend the time and effort!!
