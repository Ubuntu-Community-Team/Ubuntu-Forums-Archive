### Post by DeadSuperHero on 2009-11-23
I've finally crossed over the borderline of sanity once again. I've decided life is worth living. Why, do you ask?

Well, Google's open-source Chromium browser is now able to access Yahoo Mail. No longer do I have to deal with the agony of using the clunky old YM interface, this browser now has first-class representation.

More importantly, though: Chromium works with MathXL, a math-homework service my college forces me to use. I can now do homework with everything else in this browser.

For the longest time I had to use Firefox for homework, which is resource-hungry on my machine. It was an arduous process of opening up another browser, doing my homework on something that Flash more often than not froze up, etc.

There goes a huge source of my rage. I can finally uninstall Firefox. I'm so happy!
