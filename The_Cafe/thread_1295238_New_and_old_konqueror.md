### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-10-19
Interestingly, there used to be complaints, or rather comments, that konqueror was far too bloated in it's kde3 versions, being a file manager, web-browser, media-palyer with embedded plugins, etc etc.  Presumably the current complaints are at least partly the result of the removal of file manager as one of its main purposes.

I have to add that I no longer use kde as my DE, but have moved to gnome.  I still use one or two kde packages, however, such as k3b, digikam, kmymoney, as I can't find alternatives that are as good as them for my purposes.  Just be thankful for the choices we all have.
