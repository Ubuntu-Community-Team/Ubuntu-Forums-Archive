### Post by lyceum on 2007-10-01
> **RAV TUX said:**
> Sorry for my lack of knowledge on this one but can someone please tell me what the primary benefits are to 'slab' and 'banshee' are?

Slab just looks nice. I am not a fan of it myself. I think it is too much. Banshee is great for iPod users, not as good as Amarok, but it work very well. I really can't list all of the benefits, as I started using Banshee to long ago to remember, but I can tell you I found it more convenient to use that Rythmbox. It just did what I wanted the way I wanted it. I also recommends other artists that are like what you are lstening to. Other than that it does the standard rip and write for CDs, it shows album art, plays songs, all the nice standards. I think it can also change ogg to MP3 and MP3 to ogg and the like. I do like Amarok better though. I keep both on my system, as Amarok will freak out on me from time to time and I use banshee when I don't feel like dealing with that. In the end, I think it is just what does what you want the way you want it to.
