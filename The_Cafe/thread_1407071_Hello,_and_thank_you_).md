### Post by gordintoronto on 2010-02-14
If your new computer has more than 3 GB of memory, go with 64-bit OSes.  

There's a great web site for people who build computers, called PCMech.  If you propose a configuration to the folks there, they will tell you to get a better (not bigger) power supply, and also point out any configuration errors.
