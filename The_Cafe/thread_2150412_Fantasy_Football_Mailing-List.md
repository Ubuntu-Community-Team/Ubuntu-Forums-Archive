### Post by Noah0504 on 2013-06-01
Something else I enjoy just as much as computers and Linux is fantasy football (the American variety).  I wouldn't say I'm obsessed, but I do love to play and discuss it.

I enjoy email discussion-lists and have searched the Internet far and wide to try and find an existing list geared towards fantasy football and keep coming up short.  Because of this, I figured I would try to start one.  I did a little research on the best way to start a mailing-list, but most hosted options seemed a little pricey for something that may not take off.  I finally stumbled upon Nabble ([http://nabble.com](http://nabble.com)), which allows one to create, among other things, a mailing list.

I've gone ahead an created a mailing-list (named Man's Game) and decided to create this post in hopes of drawing some interest in it.  Since this is my first time using Nabble, I'm not sure if it is the best method, but it seems relatively simple.  You can easily subscribe to the list from the link below and either post to the list directly from the website or  using email (it will give you an email address to use, however, it seems like it is different for each user... not sure how well that will work out in the long run).

If you have any interest, please subscribe and maybe we can make it a great forum for fantasy football or anything related to American football for that matter.  Also, if you have any advice on a better way to setup a mailing-list, I'm all ears!

You can check it out or subscribe here: [http://man-s-game.50877.x6.nabble.com/](http://man-s-game.50877.x6.nabble.com/)
