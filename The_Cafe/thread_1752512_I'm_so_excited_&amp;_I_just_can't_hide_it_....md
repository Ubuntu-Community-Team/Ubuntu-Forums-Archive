### Post by Fedz on 2011-05-07
Howdy!

It's 3am here & I'm so happy whoop! [-o<

I won a Packard-Bell Diamond 2400 Plus Flatbed Scanner on eBay (pick-up only) for only GBP £5 but, wanted it as it was silver & flashy looking :D

I've spend 6 hrs trying to get it working & finally did it by 'mistake' lol

Scanner not recognised no matter what I did ](*,)

What I eventually did after trying to 'override' via the .conf file is copy the scanners .usb file from the drivers CD & renamed to Xsane's default (?) of PS1fw.usb & copied to /usr/share/sane/gt68xx then chmod a+r /usr/share/sane/gt68xx/PS1fw.usb

Plugged in scanner & like Ubuntu magic it sprung to life - I didn't know whether to laugh or cry :cool:

Xsane calls the scanner 'Mustek Systems, Inc. BearPaw 2400 CU Plus' but, doesn't matter as it works bang-on perfect \\:D/

I eventually found **_[Ubuntu Documentation > Community Documentation > ScanningHowTo](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ScanningHowTo)**_ (after 6 hrs) which led me to do what I did - nice 1 & ...

Happy scanning :p
