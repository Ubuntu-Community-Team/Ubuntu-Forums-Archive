### Post by wolfen69 on 2012-02-03
Actually, I think that they look kind of cheap also. So I thought of a way to show my ubuntu pride while still showing some class. (see attachments) 

I went down to the local arts and crafts superstore, and picked up these raised metallic letters for like $5. They are non-permanent, and self-sticking. (Thickers, if you need to know the name)

They have different kinds of "metal", such as gold, silver, sparkle, and different colors. I think it looks much better than some cheapo stickers that fade and peel.

Btw, they look much better in person than in the pics.

I used a T-square to keep them in-line, and didn't press them down until I had them lined up just right.

Just thought I'd throw this out there for anyone looking for an alternative to the usual ubuntu stickers. Hey, come up with your own ideas, and share. Cheers.
