### Post by melopsittacus on 2008-04-18
Hello!

I was wondering if you do something to take care of your health while working during a longer period with the computer.

What I do is that I have a brake reminder enabled (Typing Monitor in Ubuntu and prevRSI for Win) and (if I can force myself to do so), I stand up and move around and do some stretching exercises when these programs tell me it's time to do so (I've set a 10 minutes brake for every 50 minutes)

The other thing I do is that roughly half of the time I sit on a ball instead of a chair, which is a little less comfortable, but it makes my waist move so that it does not get stiff.
