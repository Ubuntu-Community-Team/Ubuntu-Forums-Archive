**US Teams Project: Request for Leaders.**
*19 to be exact....*

 This has been a very exciting year for LoCo teams in the US. There have been many new teams created, and a lot of State LoCo's get approved.


 In an effort to accomplish our goal of having a LoCo team in every state by the end of the year, I am going to put the list of States who have no LoCo team at present:


 [Alaska]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"),  [Delaware]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Hawaii]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Idaho]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Iowa]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Louisiana]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Michigan]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Minnesota]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Mississippi]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Nevada]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [New Hampshire]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [North Carolina]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Rhode Island]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [South Dakota]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Texas]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Vermont]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [West Virginia]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Wisconsin]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3"), [Wyoming]("http://www.ubuntu-us.org/node/3")
 (all these states are grey)
 [IMG]https://wiki.ubuntu.com/USTeams/TeamList?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=map.png[/IMG]
 If you are interested, please stop by #ubuntu-us on freenode, or contact us via our [mailing list]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us"). There is someone on the channel who can help you get started setting up your team and the resources you will need for your team.


 Starting a LoCo team is not easy, but it is fun and very rewarding. It's also a great way to help contribute to the Ubuntu Community.


 See you at [OLF!]("http://www.ohiolinux.org/")
