### Post by waffletten on 2009-11-23
I found the box in a antique store in Weimar, Germany. It was an old microscope objective holder from Karl Zeiss and I loved the patina (it is about 50 years old). It has the old style weight warnings on the side and beautiful steel fittings and I couldn't bring myself to sand and refinish it as the lovely old patina would have been ruined.
 
Glad you liked it. I had to rout a strip in the bottom front of the box on the underside so air can enter and put some little feet so it is off the surface about 1/4 inch. Intel only gave me the space for a few pics so I couldn't really show all the little details.
 
It was a lot of fun and I was surprised it even made it to the finals! I thought that my little atom230 would be excluded with all the watercooled i7's.
