So far I've seen

[LIST]
[*]osCommerce, open source and with a long tradition, though possibly a bit dated now?

### Post by samalex on 2010-08-26
I've been using osCommerce on one of the sites I maintain for some time now.  It's pretty simple to setup and use, and it has LOTS of features.  If you're working with PayPal it's a breeze to get going, but I think most of the others are also now'days.

I've tested Magento but the community version is too limited.  Plus looking at the comparison between editions, what's up with 'Indemnification Not Included'?  Indemnification from what?  Not to thread hijack, but do vendors need to indemnify their users from lawsuits now?  

At any rate I've always liked osCommerce and the community support behind it.

Take care --

Sam
