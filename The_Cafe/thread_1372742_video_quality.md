### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-01-04
Depends on the video format of the input and output. For example if you have an original high quality wmv with 1280x528 and wants to make a decent copy for CD storage (700 Mb), then transcoding it to mp4 (h.264) with a lower resolution wouldn't be dumb at all. Besides, sometimes you don't have a choice, specially considering hardware restrictions (DVD player, Xbox, PS3...). 

That being said, since they are both lossy, you shouldn't make multiple linear transcodings, which will inevitably result in quality loss.
