### Post by k2t0f12d on 2008-02-15
We are not aware of *any* driver that works with Linux that will operate a Canon printer.  The reason for this is that in order for any developer, that is, free software *or* proprietary, to write a driver for a hardware device, its specifications *must* be known in advance.  Proprietary developers do not have any advantage here at all, and in fact are probably disadvantaged by *not* sharing code.  That being the case, the likelihood of Canon making specs available to the Linux kernel developers is more likely, or at least not less likely, then them ever giving them to proprietary third-party developer.
