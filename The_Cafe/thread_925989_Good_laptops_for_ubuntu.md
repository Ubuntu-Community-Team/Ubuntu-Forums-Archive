### Post by jespdj on 2008-09-21
You are not the first one who asks this question; try searching the forums and you'll find some of the previous topics about the same question.

Find a laptop that you like and make a list of the hardware components. Then do a search on the forums for those hardware components to find out if there are specific components that could give you trouble.

Some general hints: Intel and nVidia graphics generally work better than ATI graphics (because the drivers are better). Intel WiFi cards should usually work without problems (but there are some people complaining about the Intel 3945 WiFi cards giving them trouble on Dell machines). No-name WiFi cards might use chips from Broadcom, which are not well supported on Linux.
