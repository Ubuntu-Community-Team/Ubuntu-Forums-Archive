### Post by rewyllys on 2013-05-09
I recently decided to add a news aggregator to my Linux Mint Nadia laptop.

Having found an enthusiastic recommendation of RSSowl, I considered installing it.  But I found that various suggested installation procedures for it looked both rather complex and also somewhat conflicting.

Finally, I wound up installing Liferea, which has received many 5-star ratings in the Software Center.  The layout of its GUI strikes me as pleasing, and I've found it very easy to customize to my interests: e.g., mathematics, statistics, physics, and general science.  It is easy to identify sources of RSS feeds on various subjects and add them to Liferea.
