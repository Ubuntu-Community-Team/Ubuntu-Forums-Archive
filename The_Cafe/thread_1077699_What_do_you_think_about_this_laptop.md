That looks like a fairly decent laptop and a good price for the specs. The only thing you have to remember is that Video card will probably TurboCache(Or is it Hyper Memory?) where it takes RAM and adds it to the Video Card.

The wireless does appear to be Intel as it's a PM45 Chipset and doesn't mention any other brands by name. 

I'd say give it a whirl, it's a steal at that price.
