### Post by purgatori on 2009-09-10
Like most punk music, the appeal of the Misfits is completely lost on me. Their structure of their songs show little variation either within, or between, each composition, they aren't especially technically adept with their instruments (perhaps as a result of spending more time working-out/beefing up, rather than practicing), and their lyrics aren't exactly overflowing with profundity or imaginative wordplay. For some, perhaps a band need not possess any of the aforementioned qualities, but I am afraid that for me, they are the minimum prerequisites.
