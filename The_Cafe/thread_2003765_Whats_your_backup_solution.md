### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2012-06-14
For my home computers, I don't use online or cloud storage for backups. 

I use rsync for servers and grsync for desktops. I store the data on shared USB hard drives. I have two USB hard drives. I alternate between them. The one that is not in use is kept in a safe that is rated for security and fire. I have other uses for the safe; therefore, I already have it anyway dispute it costing hundred of dollars or euros. The safe is large, bolted to the floor, and rated to protect the contents for 1 hour at 1000 degrees Celsius. If I have a fire that intense in my house; then, I will have a lot of other things that are precious to me that will be destroyed. 

Everyone has to evaluate what backup system they choose. They need to look at their risks, value of their data, trust in a storage provider, privacy concerns, their ability to afford the backup system, and so fourth.
