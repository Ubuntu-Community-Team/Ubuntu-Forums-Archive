### Post by Niksko on 2008-11-24
Just got my free copy of Intrepid Desktop Edition in the mail. First off, it was exactly 3 weeks from when it was sent to the shipping company, to when it arrived. 3 weeks for shipping to Australia is pretty good by my standards. Secondly, kudos to whoever designed the packaging. Much nicer than the Hardy packaging in my opinion. Brown gives it a warmer feel, and makes it feel less surgical, which is what the red and white Hardy packaging felt like to me. The logo is less prominent, but this isn't necessarily a bad thing, as it is still on there, and it allows the "Ubuntu" and the "8.10" be more prominent.

All in all, a great job - and the operating system isn't bad either :wink:

Thoughts?
