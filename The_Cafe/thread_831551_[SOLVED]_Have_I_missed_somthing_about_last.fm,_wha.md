### Post by RiceMonster on 2008-06-16
No you don't need the last.fm software. Banshee will submit your tracks to last.fm. If you want to see your play history, just view your last.fm profile at [http://www.last.fm/user/YOUR_USER_NAME_HERE](http://www.last.fm/user/YOUR_USER_NAME_HERE). The last.fm software gives you a few more features I think (none of which I ever used), but since I switched to Linux I haven't used it because all the audio players I used have built in last.fm support or a plug-in for it.

Also, last.fm will build weekly, monthly, and overall charts for artists, albums and songs. Once a week (Sunday), you'll get new recommendations from last.fm based on what you listened to that week.
