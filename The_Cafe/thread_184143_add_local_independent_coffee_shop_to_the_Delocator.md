### Post by RAV TUX on 2006-05-29
This is a great tool to help independent coffee shops, go to the Delocator site and add your favorite independent coffee shop, available in in the USA and Canada,
 Delocator is open source  so if you are not in the USA or Canada you can start one in your home country.

Also if you want to find a local independent coffee shop in your zipcode, go to the homepage link and type in your zipcode.

Have fun and enjoy the coffee.

home page
[http://www.delocator.net/index.php]("http://www.delocator.net/index.php")

faqs
[http://www.delocator.net/faqs.htm]("http://www.delocator.net/faqs.htm")

get the code:
[http://www.delocator.net/toolkit.htm]("http://www.delocator.net/toolkit.htm")
