### Post by BWF89 on 2006-06-27
So these people are making this game thats kinda like Rez for the PS2 and Dreamcast. Only its for your PC.

I played the Dreamcast game that this is trying to clone and its really psychedellic, it reminds me of tron. It's a shooter on rails where your flying through space and you have to destory all these enemies that come onscreen and when you destory one it makes a sound and the sound goes with the backround music. Oh, and the levels change colors depending on what kind of music your playing. It's hard to describe, but very cool.

The project has kinda stumbled as of lately so send the guy an email and tell him you want this game. It's for Windows only now but "a Linux port isn't out of the question".
