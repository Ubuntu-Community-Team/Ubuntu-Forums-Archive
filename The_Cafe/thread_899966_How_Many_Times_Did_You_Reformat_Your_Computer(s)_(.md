### Post by aaaantoine on 2008-08-25
Please define the scope of reformatting.  I have only once reformatted the entire drive of this computer, in early November 2007, when I broke my partition table.

But I have several times resized and reformatted individual partitions.

- Sometime between January and August 2007: Converted my FAT32 partitions to NTFS partitions.
- August 2007, First foray into Ubuntu: sized down one of my Acer half-partitions to make room.
- October 2007, When Gutsy came out: sized it down some more to make more room.
- November 2007, I broke my partition table trying to make more room for Gutsy.  So I took the process to its logical conclusion, installed Gutsy, and just ran Windows in a VM.

So that's 5 times Since January 2007, if the first one even counts.  I think I also had a few incremental partition resizes in there.
