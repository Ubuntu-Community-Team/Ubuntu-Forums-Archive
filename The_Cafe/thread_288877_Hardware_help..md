I am thinking of getting a new computer case... but I never have brought one myself, and am wondering what some of the jargons mean.

- 10 Drive bays
- 4 x external 5.25"

- 4 x internal 3.5"
- Hidden drive bays

---

### Post by DoctorMO on 2006-10-30
5.25" means 5 and a quarter inch the same size as the old floppy discs or cd rom drives.

3.5" means 3 and a half inch. the same size as a hard drive, newer floppy disc or internal card reader device.

---

### Post by hkgonra on 2006-10-30
And internal would be for hard drives since you have no need to "access" them from outside the case. External bays would be for cd drives , floppy drives, card readers, and even removable hard drives.
