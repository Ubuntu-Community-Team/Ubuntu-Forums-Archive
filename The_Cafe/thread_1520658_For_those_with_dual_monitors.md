### Post by McRat on 2010-06-29
> **mamamia88 said:**
> how do you have them setup so you aren't turning your head 24/7 and don't lose focus on your main task?  it seems like 3 monitors would be better just so you could face main monitor head on.  please post pics if possible

For me, the "big" monitor is the one I focus on the most.  The one to the right will normally have a reference document (text) open, and perhaps the email as well.  90% of the time, the right monitor is not used, but for that 10% of the time, it's really handy.

While I do this for CAD, it's really sweet for software development.  You keep the output screen to the right, and the code window in front of you.
