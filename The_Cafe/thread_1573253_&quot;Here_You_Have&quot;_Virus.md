### Post by TheNerdAL on 2010-09-12
So there is a new virus going around the online world hitting a lot of people and organizations and companies. I think it even hit Google and Homeland Security. 

It's called the "Here You Have" virus. 

It's an email that has the subject line as, you guessed it, "Here You Have"

In that email contains a link and when you click the link, it takes over your computer and sends the email to your contacts in your address book as yourself. 

[IMG]http://www.symantec.com/content/en/us/home_homeoffice/images/misc/hereyouhave.jpg[/IMG]

I think this is a Windows only virus. 

So if you see an email like that, just delete it. :)

More info can be found here: [http://abcnews.go.com/Technology/virus-mail-spreads-online/story?id=11596433](http://abcnews.go.com/Technology/virus-mail-spreads-online/story?id=11596433)
