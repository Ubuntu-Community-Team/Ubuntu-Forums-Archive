### Post by Grant A. on 2009-06-16
If you want to archive them on your own computer, go to the private message folder that contains the messages you wish to download, and at the bottom right hand corner of the page, hit, "Download Private Messages As..." and select the format of your choosing.


Keep in mind, that sharing ones containing another person's replies may be illegal in your jurisdiction (such as in the United States).
