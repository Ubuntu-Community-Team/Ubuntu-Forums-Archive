### Post by kidux on 2009-02-25
I've had mine for over a month now with no issues. My wifes had to be taken back for a firmware upgrade, but it works beautifully now. The clicking isn't noticebly loud on mine, so I don't think you'll have a problem. The only thing to remember is that it's suretouch, meaning you have to highlight the button before pushing. Once you get used to it you can type pretty fast. The only other thing to warn about is that the keyboard is sensitive to the position of your fingers, so try not to type on the left side with your right hand, and vice versa.
