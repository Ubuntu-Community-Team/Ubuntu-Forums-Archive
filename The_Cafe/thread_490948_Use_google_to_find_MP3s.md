In Firefox, you can make it a bookmark to make searching a lot easier.

Click Bookmarks->Organize Bookmarks.  Then in the Bookmarks Manager, click New Bookmark.  In the window that comes up for the new bookmark, give it a name, then give it a keyword such as "music" without the quotes.  Then in the location line, enter:
```
http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&q=intitle%3A%22index.of%22+%28mp3%7Cogg%29+%s+-html+-htm+-php+-asp+-cf+-jsp&btnG=Search
```
Then click OK.

Now in the address bar (not the google search bar), you can type something like:
```
music baby.got.back
```
Separate the words in the title with dots.  Entering this will automatically bring up the search results and filter out everything except indices that have the music files without the need to remember any code.

Just like the first disclaimer **this is only for searching and not for the illegal download of copyrighted material.**
