### Post by simonn on 2006-05-25
Won't that be a bit noisy?

For a media PC you may be better off getting a slower CPU and spending the money on high quality and quiet heatsinks, fans and PSUs.

I have a Sempron 64 3000 (cheapest readily available CPU at the time), with 1Gb (was 512Mb) with the cheapest nVidia card I could find. 

It can play anything with much capacity to spare. 

I only upgraded the memory because I had a spare 512Mb simm kicking around. Most of the cost of the PC went in the case (Antec P150) and PSU (Antec Phantom 500).

Maybe even look at a via board?
