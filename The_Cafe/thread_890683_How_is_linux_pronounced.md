### Post by klange on 2008-08-15
According to Torvalds, it's "Lin ecks". I believe this specific pronunciation comes from "Minix".

(And it's "ooh boon too", btw)

@abgemacht: Interesting article, I'll have to find my source...

As a note, it's a *short* ee sound, which isn't well represented in the English language. The short i, as in 'in' or 'it' is the best replacement, and also best fits the spelling. Just like Unix, Minix, etc., the x is usually pronounced completely.
