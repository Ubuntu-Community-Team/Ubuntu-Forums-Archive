### Post by Ariya243 on 2012-08-13
Take away the Mint repository.

Update manager; you simply uninstall the Update manager or delete all the files in /usr, /etc... And reinstall update manager from Ubuntu.

Awn panel: check this [http://www.tuxgarage.com/2012/05/script-for-installing-awn-in-precise.html](http://www.tuxgarage.com/2012/05/script-for-installing-awn-in-precise.html) All you have to do is log out and log into Awn. You can, of course change that name too, if you want in the xsessions and gnome-sessions.
