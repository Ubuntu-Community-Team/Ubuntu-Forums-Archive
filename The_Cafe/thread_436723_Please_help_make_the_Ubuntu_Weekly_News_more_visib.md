### Post by Jenda on 2007-05-08
Hmm...
It's possible... I guess I can do it if you really want it.
In fact... if you really want it... you do it! &#12484;
Here's how:
1) download [http://koltsoff.com/pub/reformat.py](http://koltsoff.com/pub/reformat.py)
2) open up the page of the UWN issue you wish to do and click edit
3) open up gedit, copy the page into gedit, save as UWN23 or something like that
4) run 'python reformat.py UWN23 UWN23F' (change the filenames to whatever you like, but please include the issue number in them
5) once you're done with as many issues as you feel like, send them all to [email]jenda@ubuntu.com[/email]

**The reward to whoever does this is for all the past issues... a big thank you* in the Ubuntu Weekly News section... and 10 case badges/laptop stickers of your choice (Ubuntu/Kubuntu/Xubuntu/Edubuntu).**

*not really that big, but it'll be mentioned on the top of each of those posts.
