### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-10-01
I have a set of Senheiser 414's that I have had for over 20 years.  Best stereo headphones ever made and they are "open air"  meaning that if the doorbell rings, I can hear it, if the dog barks and wants to go out, I can hear it. Of course, such will not come cheap, even on eBay.

Your problem with the iPod earbuds is that those things are engineered to fail after so many miles so that Apple can make bucks on the replacements.

IF you need small light weights, a set of Sony's at any stereo shop should get it for you.  Or maybe a set of Koss HP-1's?.  Neither will break the bank, and for most will work just fine.  You aren't requiring sound isolation for mix down and having to resort to a set of David Clark cups with inserts.  You are just listening to music or that sound track.
