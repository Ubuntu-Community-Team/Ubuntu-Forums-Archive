There are standard rudiments to learn for complete stick and rhythm control, you can find the rudimentary patterns and practice methods in many basic study books. Good Luck

Edit here is a sight where you can download a pdf of the rudiments and it has a sample section so that you can hear what they sound like.
[http://www.pas.org/Resources/rudiments.cfm](http://www.pas.org/Resources/rudiments.cfm)
Here is a wikapedia that explains the importance of being able to play these rudiments, if you become efficient with these exercises you can play and teach any western music style, from rock and roll to jazz to classical and everything in between.
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paradiddle](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paradiddle)
