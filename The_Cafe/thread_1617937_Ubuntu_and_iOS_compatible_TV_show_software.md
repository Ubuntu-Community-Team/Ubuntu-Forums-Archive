### Post by LowSky on 2010-11-10
Why do quesitons like this come up all the time?

You purchased a iPad/iPhone. You need a PC with OS/X or Windows to get exactly what you want. You can try to run iTunes on Linux but the results are far (very very far) from pleasing. You can install Windows in a VM so you can use iTunes. Not ideal but will work.

No site is going to sell you TV shows without DRM. Not one exists that is legal. Because of that you're limited to streaming options like Boxee, Amazon, Hulu, and Netflix (works on your iOS devices not Linux). I get the frustration but we can't give you advice that tells you to break the law.

Your only real option is to go out buy a TV tuner card and start recording shows using MythTV. For past seasons buy the DVD's and rip them to a hard drive. Then transcode them to play on your super awesome Apple devices.

If you want instant downloadable content that works on all anything, well it doesn't exist. You have to make compromises (running Windows).
