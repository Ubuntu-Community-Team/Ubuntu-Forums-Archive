The other good one I did was years ago when I worked in the Accounting Department.

One other person and I had PCAnywhere installed so we could remote-control our computers from home.  At one point she left her computer open for some reason I cannot remember now so I set her PCAnywhere software to run on start up minimized but without any indicator other than in the task bar (I couldn't hide that one).

So on April Fool's day I fired up my copy and connected it to her computer and left it for a while.

Later, when I know she was on the computer, I would switch to her computer and just start moving the mouse!  Or she would leave and find Notepad opened with some message about being possessed.

One time she called me over, since I was the techy of the department.  So to make it not look obvious that the mouse stops when I come around, I balanced my ruler on the stapler and put my mouse (still remote-controlling) on the ruler. So while I was coming over (she could hear it) her mouse was moving ON ITS OWN!

I had her going for a while until she saw the icon in the taskbar,
