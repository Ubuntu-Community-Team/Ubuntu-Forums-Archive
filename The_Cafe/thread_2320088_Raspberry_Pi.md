### Post by mastablasta on 2016-04-11
I bought a package with case and PSU and HDMI. we use it as OpenELEC media center. the power usage is extremely low. I think for my version (the old one) it's around 5 W max, and 1-2 W idle. so it should be like a TV when switched off, but not unplugged.

it's not exactly a PC. think of it more as a phone without the screen and with PC components on it :). it also has I/O pins so you can connect various things to it.

when I have more time and a bit more money I plan to do a simple robot with my kid who is crazy about robots.
