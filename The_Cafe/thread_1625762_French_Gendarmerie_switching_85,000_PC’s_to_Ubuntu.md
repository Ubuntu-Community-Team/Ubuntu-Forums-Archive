### Post by 3Miro on 2010-11-19
85,000 computers with an average life span of 8.5 years (which is high for a computer), this means that on average you need 10,000 computers each year. At the rate of 200 Euros per Windows licence you get 2,000,000 Euros per year. Basically:
```
 (yearly cost) = (cost of windows license) * (number of computers) / (life expectancy of a computer) 
```

As for the Updates, there is a difference between remote installation, remote update from SP1 to SP2 (how many remember how painful that was) remote update of 3D party software and ssh.
