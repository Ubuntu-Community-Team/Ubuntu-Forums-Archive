### Post by bryonak on 2009-03-12
> **smartboyathome said:**
> Where are the years located? I didn't see any years on the SVG.

The font only shows if it can fit into the box, which is not the case if you don't have Blue Highway installed, since Inkscape's default font and font size is too big.

You can either select the whole layer (unlock, then Ctrl+A) and make your font smaller or download the Blue Highway font from dafont.com (dump it into your ~/.fonts folder), which is recommended anyway.
