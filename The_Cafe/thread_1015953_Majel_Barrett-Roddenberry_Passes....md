### Post by MikeTheC on 2008-12-19
Ok, so this isn't exactly technology-related, but given the fact that so many of us tech geeks are also sci-fi fans, I thought it appropriate to mention this.

Majel Barrett-Roddenberry, wife of the late Gene Roddenberry, creator of Star Trek, died today in her home of leukemia.

Link: [http://www.trektoday.com/news/181208_01.shtml]TrekToday.com: Majel Barrett Passes](http://www.trektoday.com/news/181208_01.shtml]TrekToday.com: Majel Barrett Passes)

I actually got to meet her at a Star Trek convention that my local Star Trek group had organized and run. She was such a very warm and personable lady, and I don't think there was a person in attendance who didn't like her. Everyone has their time, I know, and we can't hold onto everything and everyone forever, but it really sucks when we loose the nice ones.

Rest in peace, Majel.
