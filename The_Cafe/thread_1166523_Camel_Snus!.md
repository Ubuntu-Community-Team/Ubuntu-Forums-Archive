### Post by ratcheer on 2010-05-11
I just started trying snus about a week ago. I started with Marlboro Rich, then Camel Frost. i like the Marlboro pretty well, but it is too sweet. The Camel Frost just tastes like intense peppermint, to me (think Altoids). They are also too sweet and nothing in the flavor is even remiscent of tobacco. They are not terrible, just not what I want.

So, Saturday I went to a cigar store and they had a nice selection of General snus imported from Sweden. I tried to buy some, but the man at the store insisted on giving me a trial sampler for free, then come back later to buy. So far, I have only tried the White Portion, which is a plain tobacco flavor. So far, these are my favorite (over the Marlboro and Camel). Also in the sampler are Mint, Dry Mini Mint, and Wintergreen. I am most looking forward to the Wintergreeen, as I used to love Skoal.

BTW, I don't currently smoke or use any other tobacco products.

Anyone else enjoy snus?
