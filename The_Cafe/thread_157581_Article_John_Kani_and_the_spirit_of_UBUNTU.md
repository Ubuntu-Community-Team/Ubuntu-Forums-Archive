John Kani and the spirit of UBUNTU

> [I]...how do we explain UBUNTU? ....It does not translate 
into English. The closest is "Menswaardigheid" which is 
Afrikaans. It is the value of the human being, it is 
humaneness, it is kindness, UBUNTU is tolerance, 
sensitivity, and respect. When you put that together you 
find UBUNTU as the lifeblood that pumps through your 
veins and informs the brain to think correctly, to think 
emotionally correct.[/I]
