### Post by aaaantoine on 2011-05-01
1. I'll let someone else answer.  There may be an indicator for that.
2. You can customize these things at a very granular level with ccsm (CompizConfig Settings Manager).
3. No but you can add your own shortcuts to the Launcher.
4. Yes.  Click & Hold will allow you to drag the applications on the Launcher.

### Post by beew on 2011-05-01
1. Don't know about this. Maybe you can find something here[ http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2011/01/the-omg-guide-to-must-have-indicator-applets/]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2011/01/the-omg-guide-to-must-have-indicator-applets/")

2. Install ccsm (compiz configuration setting manager) and enable effects from there, though I remember wobbly windows was enabled by default in Unity, maybe I remember wrong.

3. You mean creating icon launchers?  Just use the "main menu" like in gnome 2. You can use the search bar to find it.

4. You can drag the icon out of the Unity bar ( a little to the right) and put it back into the Unity bar in the position you want it.
