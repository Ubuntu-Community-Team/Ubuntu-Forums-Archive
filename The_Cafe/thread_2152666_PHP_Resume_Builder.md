### Post by sandyd on 2013-06-10
> **yazmonium said:**
> What I really want is a website that I can build to prsent my credentials to prospective employers in a more interesting way than a text document can convey.  I am not interested in adding my information to some website where I lose control.  CareerBuilder and Monster already exist for that.

And there is one of your problems. Sites such as Monster and CareerBuilder exist so that Resume Scanners/PR Scanners (which is the software that most companies that are larger than 300-500 people use today to sift through thousands of prospective employees) can easily scan them. They essentially present information in a way that resume/PR scanners can electronically read. Most online work profiles and resumes go through this process, and are rejected/accepted according to the info scanned. There is no "1 big pile of resumes vs 10 PR Agents/Managers" anymore, as all of this can be done more efficiently and cheaper with resume/PR scanning software.

If your website is not compatible with the resume scanner, then the correct keywords that the scanner is looking for wont be picked up, and you wont be given an interview just because the resume scanner failed :|
