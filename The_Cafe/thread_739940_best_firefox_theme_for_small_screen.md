### Post by Tomone on 2008-03-31
I don't have a small screen, but I like to see a lot of webpage at once. I use the theme [miniFox]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/adhttps://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/607")  and the extension [Hide Menubar]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4762"). I also hide the bookmarks toolbar and all of the buttons (e.g. back, reload...)
