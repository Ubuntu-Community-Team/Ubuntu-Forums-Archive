### Post by chimera on 2005-12-29
I believe what you're looking for is gDesklets. You can find the package in Synaptic (System->Administration->Synaptic package manager, use ctrl+f to search, search for gDesklets and install whatever it throws at you). You can now start gDesklets from Applications->Accesories->gDesklets. You can get additional desklets (such as weather displays, system load displays, uptime displays and such) at [http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/](http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/) and [http://www.gdesklets.org/](http://www.gdesklets.org/)
