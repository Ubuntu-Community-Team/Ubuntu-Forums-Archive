### Post by saulgoode on 2007-02-05
Have you looked into [Google's Summer Of Code]("http://code.google.com/soc/")? It is not really "hands-on" but it pays students $4500 for successfully completed projects and while it is for the most part directly computer-related, there are some unique projects such as coding for the NASA's Mars Rover (you can also propose your own project ideas). Your location might offer some unique opportunities for getting a project approved.
