FYI 

I see those make reference to the LPI.

Might be worth noting that if you take LPI you just get LPI, if you take CompTIA Linux+ you get Linux + and LPI and NCA.

So for the person seeking certification it is better and more cost effective to take the CompTIA route (they are all the same exam) and then tick the box during start to allow information to be passed on and you will get 3 certs for the price of 1.
