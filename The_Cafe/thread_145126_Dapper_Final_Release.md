### Post by bonzodog on 2006-03-15
I have gone to the logs of the TB meeting today, and it looks like Dapper final release is 1st June, there are to be no more added features apart from (possibly) WPA support, and it will be all bug-fixing from now on, as there is a *huge* backlog of bugs to fix and they want to see how many of them they can iron out, and they have to fit in debconf in May on top of this. Debconf is a debian developers conference, and quite a few of the Ubuntu developers are also Debian Developers.
