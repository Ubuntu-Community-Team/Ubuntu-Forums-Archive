### Post by bmac on 2008-08-30
Someone once told me if "you ask if your crazy - then your probably aren't". Most all people exhibit some form of stress related dis-functionality. You are not alone,,,, Should these symptoms continue on a regular basis, you might wish to visit a professional and get some support.

I truly believe social stress has increased significantly over the last 20 years and much of this is directly related to increased communications. Seems we have less and less time to relax and enjoy a peaceful moment. Without releasing my age - I can recall when people would spent the day picnicking and just watching the clouds or something similar. Believe it or not the time spent enjoying this activity is extremely important to individual mental health. I think it provides us with  a comforting and relaxing escape from our normal stressful routine. To this day my wife and I still endeavor to schedule time for stress reducing activities. Non-competitive of course and without cell phones or any other electronic devices (usually not even a radio).... It really does help.....
