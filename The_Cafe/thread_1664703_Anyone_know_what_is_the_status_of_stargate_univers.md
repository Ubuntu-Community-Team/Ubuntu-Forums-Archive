### Post by WorfSOM on 2011-01-11
I feel for the people who will miss the show but I really disliked it.

I wanted to like it, but try as I might I just couldn't. I think the biggest problem I had with it was that it felt nothing like Stargate what had come before. It was almost as if everything from SG1 and SGA was changed just for the sake of changing it.

I could understand that they wanted a darker show, but they went about it the wrong way. It is one thing to have flawed characters, but the producers seemed to think that flawed= unlikeable characters.
The vast majority of the characters were totally unlikeable in my opinion, which is shocking since I think that this was one of Stargates strengths upto this point. When you don't like the vast majority of people in a show you have a serious problem.

DS9 proved that you could have a darker show based on a series that was lighter in tone without having to change every single aspect of what made the previous series so successful.
