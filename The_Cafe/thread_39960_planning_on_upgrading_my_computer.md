### Post by Dylanby on 2005-06-07
DVD burners are cheap & can double as CD burners and give you more flexibility. 
If you're not going to OC your ram then go for a low latency value ram kit.
New video cards are going to be announced soon, that will hopefully drive down the prices on current cards. Don't skimp on the power supply. I recommend one with active PFC.
Just personal preference, but I never cared for Maxtor too much.
