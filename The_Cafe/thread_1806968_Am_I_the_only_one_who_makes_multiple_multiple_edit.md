### Post by koenn on 2011-07-18
> **MasterNetra said:**
> Why not? 
it breaks the flow and coherence of the conversation - or it *can* do that; depends on the edit.

If you post something, people reply to it or comment on it, and then you edit it, those comments and replies may look silly, inappropriate, stupid, nonsense, ... compared to the edited post, while they perhaps were perfectly reasonable, adequate, insightful in relation to the unedited post



editing in in the update/change and mentioning it in a new post (making it obvious where in the conversation the edit occurred) is a reasonable workaround
