### Post by coldfire1784 on 2015-01-26
So this came from an indiegogo campaign ([https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/the-ubutab-a-1tb-ubuntu-tablet-for-media-lovers](https://www.indiegogo.com/projects/the-ubutab-a-1tb-ubuntu-tablet-for-media-lovers)) that reached about 80% of it 36000 dollar goal. I contributed to the campaign and as such have already bought one. I still don't know if it's real, but she does still give updates and put of the website after the indiegogo campaign. It will be a one shot deal. They aren't going to be making them again. I paid considerably less than the price on the website, so it was worth the chance for me.
