### Post by Therion on 2009-06-11
> **praveesh said:**
> Moreover with same softwares installed  I feels that his' computer boots and runs faster than mine.
Lots of things can affect boot speed and overall system responsiveness.  Amount, *and type*, of system RAM, FSB, hard drive access time to name but a few.  

To compare one CPU to another you'd need to do a head-to-head comparison using identically built and loaded PC's, or the same PC in which you swapped out the processor only.

Furthermore, Intel **Core 2** Duo processors are next gen processors from Intel built on the 65nm platform and developed from Ground up with new architecture called *Core*&#8230; They are whole new Processors just like the jump from the Pentium 2 to the Pentium 3 or the Pentium 4.  Core 2 is, simply put, a whole different animal.
