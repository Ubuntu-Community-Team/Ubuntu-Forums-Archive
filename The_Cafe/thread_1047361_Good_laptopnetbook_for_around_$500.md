### Post by noremac on 2009-01-22
I am really happy with my Asus netbook. 

I have owned a 701 and now 1000HA. The 701 was obviously extremely portable and light weight. But the speed and display got to me rather often. I knew what I was getting into when I purchased it, so I was never unhappy with it, but I wanted something a little bigger. It was my first laptop to own so I did not know how much I would use it. Turns out I use it quite a bit more than I expected and ended up wanting something bigger and more powerful. 

So I stepped up to the 1000 series with the 10" screen. Much more useful screen resolution. Rarely ever now do I find a website that is even remotely unusable at its size. The multi-touchpad is great. Keyboard size is perfect. Battery life is over 5 hours with wifi on. I can't complain to that at all.

If I were to use it as my only computer, I may have problems. But I still have my desktop that I use more. So I don't know how well a netbook would work as your primary machine.
