### Post by JECHO on 2009-08-21
> **mess110 said:**
> Ok I just bought my very own underwater housing for my digital camera.

Definitely keep it on. that will ensure that the watertight seal it uses stays tight. Only remove it after your dives to clean out any salt/debris that may interfere with the seal or with the stability of the O-ring.

### Post by Paqman on 2009-08-21
+1

Seals work best when they're left in place as much as possible. Do make sure you clean it and if you haven't used it in a while, inspect the seals before use.
