### Post by coffeecat on 2015-05-31
There's some weird stuff going on there. It may be you are logged in and the wiki server is telling you that you aren't. Try this:

Go to your linked page and log in. It'll take you to the wiki home page. Now go back to your link. It still tells you that the page is immutable. Click on the Login link. The next page (should) show your name and have the Edit and Info links at left. You could click on edit, or...

Click on Info. Now click on view for the latest revision. You should see the page in your logged in state, and as editable, but with the "Revision 24 as of 2013-12-14 04:53:46" message. If you click on clear message you are taken to the immutable page again. :big_sigh: But you can click on login to get back to the edit link again.

If I click on edit I see the editing page but I haven't tried to save any changes. For that, over to you. :)
