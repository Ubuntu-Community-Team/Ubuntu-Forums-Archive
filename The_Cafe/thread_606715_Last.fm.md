### Post by MattBD on 2007-11-08
What do people think of Last.fm? I've been on there since June this year and I love it. There's loads of stuff I've discovered through there that I wouldn't have found otherwise.
I particularly love the fact that they not only provide a Linux client, but they have a .deb package specifically for Ubuntu, and they now have their own repository for apt-get so you can get new versions as they become available. It's rare that a website like that supports open source so well.
The only thing it really needs is a way for me to sync my iPod with my profile in Ubuntu (but that's not really their fault).
Any thoughts? Or are there competitors that you like better?
