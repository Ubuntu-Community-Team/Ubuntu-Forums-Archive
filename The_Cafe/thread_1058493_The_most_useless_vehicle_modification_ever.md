### Post by blastus on 2009-02-02
The most annoying and useless vehicle modification is a gigantic exhaust...

[http://www.xtreme2k2.com/images/funny/ricer.jpg](http://www.xtreme2k2.com/images/funny/ricer.jpg)

It's called a fart can, fart cannon, or just coffee can. You can hear them coming/going from over 2 full city-blocks away. They are usually put on small Honda's or Toyota's. It basically consists of a tin can which you can probably get for under $100 and you slap it onto your exhaust. It doesn't do anything for performance and only serves to make the most annoying loud noise ever. There's no point in having an exhaust that is larger just at the end. It is illegal to have an enlarged exhaust where I live but the law is not enforced. 

If you can fit your fist in your exhaust or it sounds louder than your engine you have a fart cannon. I've seen 4" exhausts on small cars. I've even seen people put decals on the back that point out their exhaust as if someone might not be able to hear the terrible noise coming from it. If you are going to modify your exhaust at least do it properly. I have Magnaflow on my Mustang GT and it not only sounds harmonic but gives me about 8 hp more. But if someone pulls up beside me in a 4-cylinder Honda with a 4" tin can on the back, their exhaust drowns mine out.
