### Post by mips on 2007-05-21
[COLOR=Red]40C[/COLOR] is the highest recorded

28C is average daily maximum
11C is lowest minimum which makes for pleasant winters.

The thing is though the humidity here is pretty high so those temps feel even higher, what we refer to discomfort index. Temp might be 30C but in actual fact it feels like 37C due to the humidity which is not measured.

**To convert Fahrenheit temperatures into Celsius:**

  [LIST]
[*]Begin by subtracting 32 from the Fahrenheit number.
[*]Divide the answer by 9.
[*]Then multiply that answer by 5.[/LIST]  
**To convert Celsius temperatures into Fahrenheit:**

 [LIST]
[*]Begin by multiplying the Celsius temperature by 9.
[*]Divide the answer by 5.
[*]Now add 32.[/LIST]
