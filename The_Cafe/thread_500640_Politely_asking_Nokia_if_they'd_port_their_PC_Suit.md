### Post by Footissimo on 2007-07-14
Possibly the most short-lived and pointless thread ever, but it's worth a try.

Basically, I want Nokia to port over their 'PC Suite' to linux so that we can get their phones to sync properly and firmware updates are possible (without finding XP or a shop).  It is possible to get them to sync through opensync and a load of fiddling, but it's a messy procedure and it seems a little unfair as Mac users get the basic sync through [iSync](http://europe.nokia.com/A4405892).  =(

Anyway, so time to contact Nokia and see what they're doing about it!

I went through their [customer service email page](http://www.nokia.co.uk/email):

> Hi

Are there any plans to support linux (or the opensync project) in the future. I'm a big fan of the N-series (3 and counting), but getting them to sync with linux is a MAJOR pain and firmware updates require me to use a friend's computer!

Many Thanks

..and woke up to this:

> 
Dear       ,

Thank you for emailing Nokia Care. 

In response to your enquiry, you can install the Nokia PC Suite software on a compatible Windows XP with supported service pack but not on a Linux operating system. 

However, I will definitely forward your feedback to the relevant department for future consideration.

You may like to visit our website at [www.nokia.co.uk](www.nokia.co.uk) for more information and support for your Nokia device. If you have further enquiries, please write to us again or contact Nokia Care at 0845 0455555. We operate between the hours of 9.00am and 9.00pm, Monday to Friday (except bank & public holidays).

Kind regards,


Nokia Care

Very nice but missing the question somewhat =(...so another email:

> Hiya!

Thanks for replying so quickly!  Of course I realise that PC Suite is
only meant for Windows XP, but that wasn't really my query - basically I
wondered if there was any plans to port the PC Suite software over to
linux...or at least to make it so that there is 'official' support for
synchronising like their is for the Apple Macs (e.g.
[http://europe.nokia.com/A4299040](http://europe.nokia.com/A4299040) ).

Many Thanks
