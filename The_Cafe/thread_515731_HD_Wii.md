### Post by super breadfish on 2007-08-02
I really doubt it. The HD Wii will be the Wii 2 which will come out along with PS4 and Xbox 720, ergo the next generation.

Full hardware revision like that don't really exist in games consoles, there are aesthetic revisions, such as DS Lite, PSone, PStwo etc, and sometimes minor new functionality like better screen brightness in the case of the DS Lite, but rarely major internal changes to boost specs.

Even expansions to existing hardware have had little success in the 15 - 20 years, just ask Sega.
Nintendo too suffered this way having twice failed to produce a successful disk drive expansion for their consoles (SNES CD and 64DD).

Consoles aren't like computers, where hardware is constantly improving. Each console generation sets the technology and no-one expects it to change until the next generation.

Especially for a company like Nintendo, who is trying hard to paint gaming as easy, accessible and mainstream, creating a Wii 1.5 would be a risky business indeed.
