Here is an interesting article from Linux Journal about Gnome and Ubuntu:

[i]"Jeff Waugh is an employee of Canonical Limited, the firm behind Ubuntu Linux. In his spare time he works on the GNOME window manager program. Jeff formerly was the release manager for GNOME.

On November 7, 2005, Jeff Waugh was far away from his native and current home in Australia. He was at the University of Toronto in Ontario, Canada, as part of his BadgerBadgerBadger tour. His talk was made possible by the organizing efforts of the University of Toronto graduate student Behdad Esfahbod."[/i]
