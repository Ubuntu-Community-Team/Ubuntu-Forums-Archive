### Post by Lucradia on 2011-05-18
Does *anyone* know about Bcc? I swear.

Also, if you're forwarding to a GMail account, and the GMail user clicks the "Show original" link, they will see all headers, including debugs (IE: "User is neither authotised nor blocked from using this domain"), as well as the original server and IP and sender.

Battle.net spam can only be found out by showing the original and looking at the headers.
