### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-07-10
The handbook for my old CRT monitor gives this pin out info

> pin 1     Red video input
pin 2     Green video input
pin 3     Blue video input
pin 4     Indent output - connected to pin 10
pin 5     Self-test input
pin 6     Red video ground
pin 7     Green video ground
pin 8     Blue video ground
pin 9     Not connected - no pin
pin 10  Logic ground
pin 11  Indent output - connected to pin 10
pin 12  Indent output - not connected
pin 13  Horizontal Sync
pin 14  Vertical Sync
pin 15  Not connected
Regards.

P.S. If you are worried about missing pins then you could always get a new 15 pin D-Sub connector and solder the wires of the cable to the correct pins. I had to do this when I had broken wires in the monitor cable.
