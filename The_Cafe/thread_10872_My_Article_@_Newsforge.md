### Post by ralph_ubuntu on 2005-01-13
Hi, a large amount of the posts revolve around problems people have with doing what you suggested. For example the first discussion "Noch nie so einfach..." is about problems some people encountered when they actually tried to get involved in translations. The bottom line is that many think an easier way to actually get involved is needed and of course [http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/RosettaReleaseAnnouncement](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/RosettaReleaseAnnouncement) is mentioned as a step in the right direction.

Then there's a discussion about "dressing up the penguin", just like here. :-D

Next up there's a discussion about the problems people who don't speak english well enough have with fileing bug reports.

Finally someone is making a reference to the KDE Quality project as a possibility to easily get involved in a big open source project.

Bottom line, they all seem to totally agree with you and are focusing on the practical problems involved with doing what you suggested. These problems might actually be something to take up for a follow up article, imho.
