### Post by 1clue on 2011-01-16
I use a pair of 24" 1920x1080 monitors.

I keep wishing for a bit more height.  I think the 1920x1200 would be better.  I have one of them on a swivel so I can tilt it if I have an especially long method/function/procedure (depending on your language) to look at.

24" is a nice size, it's easier on old eyes and still small enough you can fit 2 of them on the desk.

1920x1080 has gotten really cheap for pretty good monitors because of the 1080p TV format.  If I could swing the 1920x1200 ones I think that would definitely be worth it.

I contemplated the super-huge Apple 30" display, 2560 x 1600.  Thing is, I would have to bump the font size up a bit on that because the dots are so small.

Whatever you use, get a completely digital signal path.  The old-style analog monitor plugs are a killer on your eyes.
