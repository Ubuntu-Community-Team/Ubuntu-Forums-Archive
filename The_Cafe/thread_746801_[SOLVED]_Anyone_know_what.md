### Post by finer recliner on 2008-04-05
looks like the system monitor item for GNOME panel.

to get it yourself:

right click on a GNOME panel
click "add to panel..."
find "system monitor" drag and drop the icon to where you want it.
right click on the system monitor icon and go to peferences. you can add/remove other monitors from here (CPU, memory, network, etc)
