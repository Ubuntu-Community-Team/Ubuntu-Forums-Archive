### Post by Foster Grant on 2008-04-13
I use Nextel ... er, Sprint. :D

Best audio quality I've found, possibly because the iDEN format pushes less data than the channels can hold so the extra space between bands can be used as "guard space." And the two-way radio feature is a sell point, for me &#8212; the whole family uses Nextel and when one of my older relatives got sick a couple of years ago, it's a handy way to get in touch quickly.

Down sides: Nobody really knows what Sprint's future plans are for iDEN, but there are multiple companies offering that network in the USA. And I believe service was better before Sprint bought out Nextel.
