### Post by Sporkman on 2008-01-02
Caveat:

> 
Net Applications’ monthly surveys do not measure market share in terms of computer systems sold. Rather, they sample data from visitors to some 40,000 websites operated by the firm’s clients. As such, the findings are probably better described as a snapshot of installed base taken from a less-than-random sample. But the results tend to correspond well to domestic market share as measured by more traditional market survey firms like IDC and Gartner.
