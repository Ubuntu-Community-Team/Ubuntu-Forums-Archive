hey all
thought i'd start a contest for an email signature that would promote the use of ubuntu. the goals are:

* has to be short (1-2 lines, though if the artist in you /really/ demands 3 lines, that's cool too)
* has to be intriguing to the reader, to entice a person unfamiliar with it to find out more about ubuntu
* would be nice if it were fun/amusing/witty

to start things off, this is what i came up with on short notice (stole first line from the ubuntu poster :) ):
> The best things in life are free. Use Ubuntu Linux. Find out more at [http://www.ubuntu.com](http://www.ubuntu.com)

think you can do better? go ahead, give it a shot. 

if there's demand, then once we have a few hundred entries, i will choose like 10 that  i (and everyone else seems to) like best and post a "summary post" for easy reference. otherwise, you can just trawl through the whole thread and choose one for yourself. then we can all stick one of them into our email sigs to promote ubuntu.  it worked for hotmail back in the day, why not for ubuntu? :)
