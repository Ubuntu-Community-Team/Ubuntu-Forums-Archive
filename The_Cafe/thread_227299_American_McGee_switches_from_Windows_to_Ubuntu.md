### Post by Blondie on 2006-08-01
Game designer American McGee of Doom and Quake fame has switched from Microsoft Windows to Ubuntu, according to his [blog]("http://www.americanmcgee.com/wordpress/?p=171")

The reason? Windows Genuine Advantage.

"This isn’t the first time I’ve attempted a switch to Linux, but it is the first time that I’ve made the switch and am going to stay switched." says McGee.
