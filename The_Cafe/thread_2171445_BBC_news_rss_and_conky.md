### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-08-31
The BBC seems to have dozens of RSS feeds.  This is probably a good place to start: [http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/10628494](http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/10628494)

I don't know what an "rss.ect" file is.  I've never come across one. For an RSS feed, you're looking for a URL that ends in XML, almost always "....rss.xml". 

Don't click on the link. Just copy it (usually "Copy Link Location" shows up with a right click) and paste it where you need it.

Sites sometimes have a feed and don't advertise it.  View the source, in that case, and scan for the RSS URL.
