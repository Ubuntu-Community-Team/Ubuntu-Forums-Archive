### Post by RAV TUX on 2006-07-22
2006-07-20         [Development Releases: Ubuntu 6.10 Knot-1]("http://distrowatch.com/3581")                       [[IMG]http://distrowatch.com/images/icon-large/ubuntu.png[/IMG]]("http://distrowatch.com/ubuntu")         The first development CD image of [Ubuntu]("http://distrowatch.com/ubuntu") release 6.10, code name ["Edgy Eft"]("http://distrowatch.com/weekly.php?issue=20060424#1"), has been released for Intel x86, PowerPC, and AMD64 platforms: "Welcome to Edgy Eft Knot-1, which will in time become Ubuntu 6.10. Knot-1 is the first in a series of milestone CD images that will be released throughout the Edgy development cycle, as images that are known to be reasonably free of showstopper CD-build or installer bugs, while representing very current snapshots of Edgy. You can download it here, for Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Edubuntu and Xubuntu respectively." Read the full [announcement]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-devel-announce/2006-July/000164.html") and visit the [Edgy Eft]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/EdgyEft") wiki page for more details. Three types of CD images (also BitTorrent files) for all three architectures are available from [cdimage.ubuntu.com]("http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/edgy/knot-1/"); here is a quick link to the [edgy-desktop-i386.iso]("http://nl.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu-cdimages/edgy/knot-1/edgy-desktop-i386.iso") (679MB, [MD5]("http://ftp.heanet.ie/pub/ubuntu-cdimage/releases/edgy/knot-1/MD5SUMS")).
