AS we all know. the Distrowatch numbers don't count installations, downloads, usage, or anything similar.  They count clicks on links at Distrowatch. (Does anyone know if those numbers parse out referrals?)

Also, the Mint number include all of the different flavors Mint releases -- Cinnamon, Mate, KDE, XFCE, and LMDE-- while Ubuntu and the other offical spins are broken out separately.  So, to get a fair comparison of click numbers, we need to add to the Ubuntu number the numbers from the other official spins and then see how that total stands against Mint's total.
