### Post by dyous87 on 2009-11-21
Last year the FBI teamed up with Fordham University to launch the first annual International Conference on Cyber Security. It was a great success with more than five-hundred people from forty countries around the world attending. 

Now, the Federal Bureau of Investigation and Fordham University will partner again to host the second annual International Conference on Cyber Security (ICCS 2010) in New York City, the information center of the world.

This year I have the honor of sitting on the board as the web design and marketing sub committee chairperson for ICCS.

Emergent cyber threats have reached a critical juncture, driving comprehensive security analysis and solutions. I invite all of you to join in following all ICCS 2010 developments. We will be blogging and tweeting information on cyber security and intelligence. ICCS will feature a first-hand opportunity to discover and share critical intelligence on issues shaping the future of cyber security.

Our new website will be launched very shortly with further information and the finalized dates for ICCS 2010.

For now I invite you to take a look at last years website here:
[http://www.iccs.fordham.edu/](http://www.iccs.fordham.edu/)

### Post by dyous87 on 2009-12-20
The ICCS 2010 website is now up:

[http://www.iccs.fordham.edu](http://www.iccs.fordham.edu)

Last year more than 500 professionals from 40 countries around the world met at the International Conference on Cyber Security 2009 to discuss the worlds greatest emerging cyber threats. This August 2010, the Federal Bureau of Investigation and Fordham University will partner again to host the second annual International Conference on Cyber Security (ICCS 2010) in New York City, the information center of the world. ICCS 2010 will be another unparalleled opportunity that will bring together global leaders in emerging cyber threat analysis, operations and enforcement. With the number of cyber threats escalating worldwide, the need for comprehensive security analysis, assessment, and actions have reached a critical juncture. Join us at ICCS 2010 for a first-hand opportunity to discover and share critical intelligence on issues shaping the future of cyber security.
