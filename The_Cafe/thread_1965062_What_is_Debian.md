### Post by Lucradia on 2012-04-25
Debian can be made to be even lighter than the lightest Ubuntu GUI. Because there's one thing that Debian does that Ubuntu doesn't, and that's **_*not*_** forcing gnome on certain packages.

Exa: [http://forums.debian.net/viewtopic.php?f=20&t=50703](http://forums.debian.net/viewtopic.php?f=20&t=50703) (xmessage shutdown / logoff won't work because your user has to be given shutdown / logout rights instead (via a special file) so you don't have to give it root/admin rights. Since xmessage won't work with sudo.)
