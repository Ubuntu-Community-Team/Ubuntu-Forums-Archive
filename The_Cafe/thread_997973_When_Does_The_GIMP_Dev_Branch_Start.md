### Post by saulgoode on 2008-11-30
The first revision was made [two days ago]("http://svn.gnome.org/viewvc/gimp/trunk/"), although it could have happened any time after the [2.6.0 stable branch]("http://svn.gnome.org/viewvc/gimp/branches/gimp-2-6/") was created. New features will be added to "trunk" (which is henceforth the 2.7 development version), with bug fixes being merged over to "gimp-2-6".
