### Post by GMU_DodgyHodgy on 2008-01-06
If you are looking for a money replacement - two come to mind:

1.) GnuCash
2.) JGnash

GnuCash has all the functionality of Quicken/Money and more.  it has funcitonality that would support small businesses. However, you enter transactions on an electronic version of a greensheet.

JGnash has a little less business functionality, but a Quicken/Money like interface.  That has worked well for me.
