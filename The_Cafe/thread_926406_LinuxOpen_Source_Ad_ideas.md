### Post by Ellipsis on 2008-09-21
Many of the user created "ads" for Linux really seem anti-Microsoft/Apple/proprietary but I would like so see a "TV-style" commercail and really like the new "I'm a PC style" of Microsoft. So here are few of my ideas:

"I'm a person counter-campaign"- I know I said that Anti-Microsoft/etc ads are bad but I thought I would relieve myself of this first. 

Basically a large group of Linux/OSS users...dont care what distribution because all the AD will show would be the people. This Ad would be done pretty simply by indivuals each doing the clips, a editor putting them together and a final "Community, more than technology"..."Linux/GNU, Open Source, **Community **(left flashing on a white/black screen).

Of course the clips would have to be taken to certain standards and would need a 30 second/ 1 minute/ Youtube 10 minute version and a "full version" featuring everyone who submitted their videos (a hell to edit).

"SHARE"- Close up on kid in a cafeteria, the kid has a sandwich split in halfs...the kid sees another kid without a lunch beside him/her, the kid gives half to that kid. A kid across the table sees this act and shares/trades with another kid and so on rapidly moving the camera until you zoom out to the full cafeteria in a Buzz. Whiteout and a blink "SHARE", "Linux, OpennSoure, SHARE" (This Ad could be done with a voice over but I think it is pretty simple

"FREE"- Zoom in on a man finding a guitar (old broken/in the trash). Voice over: Some people say Free doesn't work. Move  the Camera out of the store show the man walking home with his find. Show the man fixing it/strumming in his apartment through a window.Voice Over: Some people say Free is impossible. Spin the Camera show the man on street corner playing with a crowd and accidentally getting some money (you know in the hat kinda thing maybe in a Red Fedora ;)). Show the man as a rock star on stage (hinting that he his making millions). Show him leaving and than giving a kid his guitar. Voice Over: I see things differently. White out to "Linux/GNU, Open Source, FREE (Left blinking on the screen).  (This ad isn't meant to be anti-proprietary as it shows the guy earning money)

Anyway what are your ideas? Please don't go overboard with the comparison ads. Show what Linux/OSS have to offer not Apple/Microsoft's mistakes. These are just brain storming ideas...go crazy no budget no limits.
