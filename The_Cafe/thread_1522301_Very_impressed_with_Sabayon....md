### Post by kaldor on 2010-07-02
After a bit of distro hopping I am settled on Sabayon 5.3 GNOME. What a wonderful OS!!!

I have no problems... it's the first time a Linux distro worked so well for me. It's REALLY fast and runs Flash videos on HD without a hitch. Nvidia drivers installed by default as well. I had *no* configuring to do !

The source-based distros have always scared me off a little, but I think Sabayon builds the bridge between source-based and other distros. 

I feel by using Gentoo/Sabayon stuff I'll learn more about my OS and computer as well. It's great. I encourage everyone to give it a spin!

I think I'm staying on Sabayon for a nice long while now. Ubuntu is no longer my "fallback" OS. Thanks to whoever it was who recommended Gentoo in my "Ubuntu is going to sh**" thread :)
