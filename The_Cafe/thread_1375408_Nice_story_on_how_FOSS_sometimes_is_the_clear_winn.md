### Post by user1397 on 2010-01-07
So my dad had been complaining how adobe acrobat (on vista) wasn't letting him make PDFs.  So I looked at it, and he was trying to convert an MS office 07 word document (.docx) so naturally I thought that maybe this version of acrobat hadn't added support for that format yet.  So we tried an office 03 file (.doc), and even that didn't work!

Now I know that adobe acrobat works for a lot of people this is just one case.

So I downloaded and installed openoffice.org, opened the .docx file, went to file > export as PDF, and it isntantly made a PDF copy.

My dad said "that's it?"

To which I replied "yup, isn't that cool?"
