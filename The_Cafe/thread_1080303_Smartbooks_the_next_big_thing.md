### Post by BuffaloX on 2009-02-25
There are many stories around the net recently, about Asus developing a sub Netbook based on Google Android OS.
The advantages are many, it can run on very cheap Arm processors, it needs less RAM than even netbook Linux distros, it requires less power than the Intel Atom, it uses touchscreen so we can loose the keyboard.

These gadgets will probably be marketed at even lower price point than Netbooks, and make for a seriously handy and versatile PDA replacement.
