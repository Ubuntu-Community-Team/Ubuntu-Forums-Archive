### Post by t0p on 2012-05-20
There used to be a similar thing going on at shipit.ubuntu.com.   But that's ended now due to the near-prevalence of quick download connections.

Of course, you could set up such a site, if you think the idea's viable.  The Gnu GPL license allows you to sell Linux OSes as long as proper attribution is made.
