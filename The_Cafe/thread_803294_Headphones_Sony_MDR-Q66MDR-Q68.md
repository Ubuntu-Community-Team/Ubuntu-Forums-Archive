### Post by Abeeeee on 2008-08-24
I own both pairs of headphones and I am pleased with their performance. Sound quality is clear and the bass isn't bad considering the price of the headphones. The reason I got the MDR-Q68 is because the MDR-Q66 right headphone stopped working after about 2 reliable years of working just fine. You are completely correct, they are virtually identical. As far as I know and according to the sony website, all the specs for both are pretty much the same. The only differences are that the Q68 has a sensitivity of 108dB/mW and the Q66 has 104dB/mW (I have no idea what that means), and the Q68 is a tad lighter than the Q66. From what I understand, the Q68 is just an updated version of the Q68. Though the Q66 may be a bit cheaper because it is an older model, I recommend purchasing the Q68 because it is easier to find in stores.
