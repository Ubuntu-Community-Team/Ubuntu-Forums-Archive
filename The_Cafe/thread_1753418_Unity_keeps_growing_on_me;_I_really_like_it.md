### Post by nrundy on 2011-05-08
For most of February, March and April I planned to transition to Gnome-Shell. But after I tried Gnome-shell, I was pretty disappointed. Among other things, it wastes an enormous amount of vertical space.

So I tried Unity and pretty quickly realized I loved it.

The more I use Unity, the more I like it. Even things that initially I kinda didn't like I'm getting used to and am realizing they aren't that bad. I just needed to be a little patient and learn a new way. When I do, it's quite refreshing. It is such a huge improvement over Gnome 2x.

I'm so glad that Ubuntu released Unity! :popcorn:

I know from some posts that not everyone feels the same way. Hopefully Unity will grow on you too if you're patient. Otherwise hopefully you can find some kind of solution to fix whatever you don't like. In the meantime though, you can just keep using Gnome 2x.
