here's something I got from the xfire community, shows a little more as to why my GTX 470 is performing better than most people expected...

> [quote="zen1aserman"]That 470 is easily capable of blowing up that PSU. Just run Furmark or turn off Vsync and idle on certain game menus and that 470 will run full bore, drawing up to 300 watts by itself. Which is probably more than your 400 watt PSU's 12v rail can deliver, especially if it's hot. If your CPU is also fully loaded, you will be overdrawing on that PSU and it will quickly begin to heat up.
