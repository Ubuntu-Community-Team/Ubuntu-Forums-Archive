### Post by shivling on 2006-06-06
Dear Linuxers,

HDFC Bank and its associate website [www.hdfcsec.com](www.hdfcsec.com) play a major role in online stock broking in India. 

The problem with the two sites is that they are not Mozilla-friendly and cannot be accessed using Firefox or Opera. One has to have IE installed and running on a Windows environment to trade on these sites. Naturally, day traders and the rest working via HDFC's sites stay away from Linux and other open source alternatives.

I have therefore set up an online petition for linux enthusiasts and the open source community to persuade the senior officials of HDFC Bank to make their website Mozilla friendly. Hopefully the two sites will be opened up for access by Firefox and Opera in the near future.

Please sign the petition on:

[http://www.petitiononline.com/freehdfc/petition.html](http://www.petitiononline.com/freehdfc/petition.html)

Also forward this message to other open source enthusiasts so that enough 
pressure can be built up on HDFC Bank and its associates.

Regards
Shiv Kumar
