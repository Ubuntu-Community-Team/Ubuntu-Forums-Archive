### Post by Oxwivi on 2010-10-22
When opting for one-to-one support for an issue, there might be time differences which might cause inconvenience to the one giving support. So rather than asking for their country of residence, ask for the UTC (GMT) time of their region, then click on the clock on the panel and then location. I discovered it shows where the sun is up with shaded and brightened areas, so with a vague idea of the UTC time, you can figure out when to lay off pestering them.

Some of yo might be opposed to the very idea of one-to one support, but the more people trying to help on the same problem, the more of a mess it becomes.
