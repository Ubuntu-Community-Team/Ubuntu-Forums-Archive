You've probably heard about the [No Software Patents Movement]("http://www.nosoftwarepatents.com/en/m/ev50/index.html"). Its founder, Florian Müller, has been nominated for the title of European of the Year. I voted for him. If you think this is a good idea, go to [this page]("http://www.ev50.com/poll/") and vote for Florian Müller in the "Campaigner of the Year" and "European of the Year" categories. Please read the [voting rules]("http://http://www.ev50.com/noflash/votingrules.asp?id=12") first, to make sure your vote won't be disqualified. You don't have to live in Europe in order to be able to vote.
