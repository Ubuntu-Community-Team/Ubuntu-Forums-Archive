### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2010-06-22
Flash was great at the time when released because there was nothing else like it available. Though for the Internet to evolve it has to be free of proprietary clients and authoring environments. The goal of HTML5 is to basically replace the functionality of Flash (and similar like Silverlight), but natively in the browser and as an open standard so anyone can implement a client (in this case a browser) or authoring environment for it. :guitar:
