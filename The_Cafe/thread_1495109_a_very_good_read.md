Excellent Review. Very informative. I agreed with just about everything the guy said. Especially the "over-hyped October release" point. I wasn't nearly as excited about 10.04 as I was about 9.10. "I can't get Gnome to load, help!!!"
"Gnome Shell slow in 10.10"

"Maverick Meerkat == FAIL!"

Please, Ubuntu developers, read this article and take heed. I don't want to see another Karmic. Either mark 10.10 as "Not Intended for Production Use" or don't make so many changes. I'm tired of undercooked October releases.
