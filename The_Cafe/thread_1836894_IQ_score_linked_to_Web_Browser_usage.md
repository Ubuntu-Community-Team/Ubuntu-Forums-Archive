### Post by Paqman on 2011-07-31
Hmm. Seems like every news agency under the sun is flying this one under the headline "Using IE means you're dumb!". Tbh, it's a great headline, and very tempting to use (I did it myself on my blog...)

However, it's a bit misleading. The point of the study wasn't really to find out the link between browsers an IQ, it was to find a link between refusing to upgrade/switch software and IQ. Browser choice was used as a proxy for that. What they were trying to uncover were the reasons why people use default software, and never upgrade it. 

I think that's quite a pertinent question for the Linux desktop. It certainly adds a lot of firepower to the argument that for a significant segment of the potential user base defaults do matter, as they will be used regardless of quality.
