### Post by nicolasdentremont on 2024-10-04
Is the back of the fridge open to the outside? The heat coming out of there is probably coming from the fridge compressor. Some fridges need a gap around them for air movement otherwise the compressor stops being able to move heat.

If you want to improve the energy efficiency of an RV, insulation, draft sealing and high efficiency windows and doors are usually the first places to look. Otherwise heat gets in and your AC unit needs to run constantly to keep up.
