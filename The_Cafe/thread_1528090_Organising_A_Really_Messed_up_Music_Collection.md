Hi all, 
        this may seem like a fuss over nothing, but I have OCD, and have spent two weeks arranging and rearranging my Music collection, and I still can't find a satisfactory way of storing it.

Does anyone have any suggestions?? I would be most grateful for ANY suggestion.

### Post by Paqman on 2010-07-10
> **gallifrey said:**
> The problems arise when I try to organise songs which come from incomplete albums, or compilations. 


EasyTag is a great app for getting the tags sorted. I don't bother too much about folder hierarchy. As long as the tags are good then your player kind find everything. That's probably not satisfactory if you've got OCD though...

You can tag a compilation as such, there's a category for compilation artist (as distinct from the artist of that track).

### Post by koenn on 2010-07-10
How about this:

go by Artist/Album/song
for each Artist, have a subdirectory 'singles' for the songs you have not part of an album, one hit wonders, ...

"Best Of" Albums and compilations with misc. artists: treat as a random bunch of singles -> each song goes in the appropriate Artist/Singles subdirectory -> use playlists in stead of an arbitrary collection compiled by the marketing dept. of a music company if you want to keep them together somehow. 

That's probably how I'd approach it if I could be bothered to manually organise a music collection.

An other option I'd consider would be totally ignore any file system hierarchy and completely rely on tags.
