### Post by zenwhen on 2004-12-06
[QUOTE=saBrEwolf]I'm on the net quite often, but don't have much experience with IRC.. How do I set up Gaim to automagically login to #ubuntuforums for me? I heard it can do IRC somewhere, but not sure where..

Any help would be greatly appreciated[/QUOTE]

Look at this image:

[http://zenhardwhere.com/images/xchathowto.png](http://zenhardwhere.com/images/xchathowto.png)

1) Click X-Chat and select Server List.
2) Highlight Freenode.
3) Click Edit. 
4) Select "Auto connect to this server at startup.".
5) Put #ubuntuforums and any other channels you need in there seperated by commas. 
6) Hit close.

When you restart Xchat the channels will load. :)
