### Post by matthew.ball on 2010-10-25
You could probably use an MA in Psychology to clean a few windows (after that it would no doubt tear up and be useless).

That said, what do you do with any Master's degree? In the case of psychology, unless you have 1st class-honours, a MA might get you access to a PhD program. Presumably you would need PhD (or above) to be a practising psychologist (I don't understand why you'd want to do a MA in psychology unless you wanted to be a psychologist).

Don't know what undergraduate degree you're talking about, but BA is just a Bachelor of Arts.
