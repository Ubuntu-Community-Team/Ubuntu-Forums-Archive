### Post by harisund on 2006-04-23
For all those people who are stuck in a Windows world, and terribly miss Linux, might I suggest 2 excellent resources? 

First, USE CYGWIN. This totally rocks. I get complete Linux power. Well, almost complete. And with a X server, I can SSH and have nearly all linux applications. With XDMCP it is even more awesome !

Second, use coreUtils from GNU ([http://gnuwin32.sourceforge.net/packages/coreutils.htm](http://gnuwin32.sourceforge.net/packages/coreutils.htm)) This is terrific, and adds a whole bunch of terrific Unix command line utilities to your DOS prompt.
