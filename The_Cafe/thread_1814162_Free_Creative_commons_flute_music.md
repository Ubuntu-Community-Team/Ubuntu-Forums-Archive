### Post by cloyd on 2011-07-29
You might look at Celic music. Some of the slow airs played on a flute or whistle (especially the low whistle) are haunting. To get an idea, you might check out some of Davy Spillane's music on the flute (won't be creative commons). Yet there are a ton of airs out there played on the whistle or flute.

Another to check out for the songs themselves and how relaxing they can be is Joanie Madden of Cherish the Ladies. Many (I'm sure not all) of the melodies she plays are public domain. Nor do I think that her recordings are free of copyright . . . but much of the music itself is traditional. Of course, since she is plays traditional Irish music, her music is full of jigs and reels. I know that jigs and reels are not what you are after. But there is some good stuff there. A You Tube search for Joanie Madden will reveal some great music.
