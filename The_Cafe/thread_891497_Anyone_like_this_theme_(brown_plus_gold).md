### Post by Giant Speck on 2008-08-16
This theme isn't the worst I've seen.  What you could try doing is make the orange and gold not as loud.  Because the coppery brown you already have is loud enough and should be complemented with neutral or subtle colors, such as tan and beige.

So, keep the coppery brown, but lighten and slightly desaturate the orange color and lighten the gold text just a tad.

Oh, and try some fonts.  You could make the most dazzling desktop theme ever, but by sticking with default fonts, it waters down the artistic effect.
