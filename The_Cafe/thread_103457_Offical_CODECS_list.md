### Post by gil-galad on 2005-12-14
Well its a bit complicated.  

Some are open source and completely free. (Legal)
         -Ogg.  

Some are open source codecs yet they may be covered by patents.  (Possible patent infringement) 
          -Mpeg 2, Mpeg 3, Xvid/Mpeg 4.  

Some are open source versions of closed source codecs.  (Possible patent infringement)
          -Open source versions of quicktime and wmv.  

Some are closed source yet work with mplayer and xine.  (Illegal due to copyright/software license)
          -Windows codecs, quicktime.  

Some are encrypted and it is illegal to break the encryption by using it.  (Illegal in US due to DMCA)
          -DVDs, protected WMV/WMA
