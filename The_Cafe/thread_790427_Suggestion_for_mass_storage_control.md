The low level of control for mass storage devices should be moved out of the OS and onto the storage devices themselves.  This would relive the OS of a huge amount of processing.  It would also enable the movement of mass storage devices from one platform to another with absolutley no detectable difference by the users.  

I have gone overboard and written a significant web page on this topic here: [http://www.bkelly.ws/software/os_and_hard_drive.html]("http://www.bkelly.ws/software/os_and_hard_drive.html")

This is a long term concept, but there is a much better chance of Linux leading the way rather than Microsoft. Let me know what you think.
