### Post by dannyboy79 on 2007-08-15
I am pleased to annouce that Mark Sobell is currently writing a new book . It will be on the latest release (to be released in October), Gutsy Gibbon. It is called "A Practical Guide to Ubuntu Linux".
It will be available for purchase in December. I have been working with Prentice Hall as a Technical Reviewer to ensure the book is as accurate as possible. I reviewed 16 chapters and it's a very helpful book to anyone, whether you have no experience in Linux (Ubuntu) or if you're like me and have 2 years of experience. Everything is in the book from configuring a LAN (Local Area Network) to setting up an NIS network (Network Information System).
I hope other's are excited about it as I am. Happy reading.
