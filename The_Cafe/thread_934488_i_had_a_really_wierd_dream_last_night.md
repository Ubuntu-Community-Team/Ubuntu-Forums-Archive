### Post by mike1234 on 2008-09-30
I have disturbing dreams all the time. I wouldn't get too hung up on their "meaning". There are scores of books on this subject. Most of it is B.S. Designed to sell books (imagine that). Examine your dreams, write it down on paper if you can, and you'll find it's just your subconscious replaying video snippets of your day back to you. With a little drama thrown in such as your fears over jobs, relationships, etc. It's really kind of simple. But anyone who chooses to can subscribe to the melodrama. I found it's just a waste of time.
