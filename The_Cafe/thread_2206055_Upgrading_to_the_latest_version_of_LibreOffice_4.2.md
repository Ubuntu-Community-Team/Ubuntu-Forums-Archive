### Post by rewyllys on 2014-02-17
Upgrading to LibreOffice 4.2 could hardly be simpler.

Go to [this page of the LibreOffice Website]("http://www.libreoffice.org/download"); select the appropriate version (e.g., 32-bit or 64-bit) of the download; and download the Installer and the Help files in the order prescribed on the download page. Then let the Installer and Help downloads do their work on your computer.
