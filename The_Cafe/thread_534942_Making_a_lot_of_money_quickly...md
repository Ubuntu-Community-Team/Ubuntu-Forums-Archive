### Post by dasunst3r on 2007-08-25
Yes, you have to work hard for your money.  I am currently studying engineering, which would start me with an annual salary of $55K.  The road there is extremely difficult, and I am anticipating many pitfalls awaiting me.  With that said, if you want to get rich, start keeping tabs on the stuff you spend and see whether you can cut down on a few things and save up for others.  For example, I am much happier buying a $300 camera I worked hard for than I am with some little thing I buy.

If anything, don't fall for those pyramid schemes.  I've had a few people fall into those schemes and not get any money in return.
