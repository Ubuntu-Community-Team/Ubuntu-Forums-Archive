### Post by Mazza558 on 2008-06-20
I've been getting lots of requests for wallpapers recently- asking where I got them. This was mainly before the Futurelooks pack, where I packaged a few of my favorites with my theme. So, without further ado, I'm providing links to what I think are the best wallpapers on the net... enjoy!


[B]
My first port of call... InterfaceLift[/B]

[IMG]http://interfacelift.com/wallpaper/previews/01584_ladybughdr.jpg[/IMG][IMG]http://interfacelift.com/wallpaper/previews/01583_rangitotodawn.jpg[/IMG][IMG]http://interfacelift.com/wallpaper/previews/01568_greenparadise.jpg[/IMG]

This has always been a favorite for me, with wallpapers mostly consisting of fantastic photography of all kinds, and being regularly updated.

I recommend not just visiting this place once, but checking back every few days to see what new stuff is out. Here's the link to the latest wallpapers:

[http://interfacelift.com/wallpaper/index.php?w=&h=&sort=date&id=&page=1]("http://interfacelift.com/wallpaper/index.php?w=&h=&sort=date&id=&page=1")

For a list of the highest rated wallpapers on InterfaceLift, go here:

[http://interfacelift.com/wallpaper/index.php?sort=ratings]("http://interfacelift.com/wallpaper/index.php?sort=ratings")


**Quality over quantity - Desktopography**

[IMG]http://desk08.customize.org/thumbnail/thumb/57.jpg[/IMG][IMG]http://desk08.customize.org/thumbnail/thumb/63.jpg[/IMG][IMG]http://desk08.customize.org/thumbnail/thumb/26.jpg[/IMG]

A website I discovered very recently, which releases their wallpapers in annual exhibitions (a total of 4 including 2005). Most of their wallpapers are edited in some way, which creates some very arty designs. Also has some wallpapers which are suitable for dualscreen monitors. Impressive stuff.

On the link below, scroll down to find links to the various exhibitions.

[http://www.desktopography.net/]("http://www.desktopography.net/")


**Too much to look at... Deviantart**

[IMG]http://tn1-4.pv.deviantart.com/fs25/150/i/2008/147/8/b/A_moment_by_NestR.jpg[/IMG][IMG]http://tn1-5.pv.deviantart.com/fs30/150/f/2008/144/f/5/Lac_Seul_Wallpaper_by_TheAngelsrafel.jpg[/IMG]


This website will suck hours out of your time if you're not careful, providing thousands of wallpapers in the various sections of the site. You can either search for the type of the wallpaper/image, or narrow it down using the "categories" menu and then choosing "customisation", then "wallpaper".

[http://browse.deviantart.com/customization/wallpaper/]("http://browse.deviantart.com/customization/wallpaper/")
