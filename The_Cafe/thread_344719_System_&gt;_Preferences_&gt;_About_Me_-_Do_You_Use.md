### Post by LKRaider on 2007-01-23
There are some bugs associated with exactly of not making it clear what that applet is for. [[#80899]]("https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/control-center/+bug/80899") [[#3574]]("https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/control-center/+bug/3574")

On [Gnome 2.12 - What's New For Users]("http://www.gnome.org/start/2.12/notes/en/rnusers.html") they write:
> About Me

GNOME now has a new About Me control panel in which you can enter your personal details, such as your telephone numbers, address, email address, and instant-messenger IDs, instead of repeating the information in several applications. You can also change your password here.

So it is supposed to be a hub app that contains your info to be used by other apps, although I think it isn't very much in use right now.
