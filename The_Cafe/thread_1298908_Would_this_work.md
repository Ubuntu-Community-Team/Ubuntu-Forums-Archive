### Post by forrestcupp on 2009-10-23
It's been done many times before, and it doesn't work.  There aren't enough people to make it worth their while to spend the extra money on Linux development.  A big part of it is that a lot of them go with DirectX, which takes too much to port.

But there *are* some game studios who are already sympathetic to the Linux community.  You're better off rallying together to show *them* your gratitude.
