### Post by reyfer on 2011-07-13
Google is offering a new service, What Do You Love.

According to the official Google Blog:

> A while back, a few of us wanted to make a little tool that we could use to show just about anybody more of what Google makes. That led to some simple ideas, and then a few more ideas and ultimately, to a challenge: how we could connect people to products they might not know about and may find useful, but make the discovery relevant to them and keep it fun.

Playing about with that challenge produced a website&#8212;What Do You Love?&#8212;that we hope meets at least some of the challenge by demonstrating how different Google products can show you different things about any particular search query.[http://googleblog.blogspot.com/2011/07/what-do-you-love.html?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed%3A+blogspot%2FMKuf+%28Official+Google+Blog%29]("http://googleblog.blogspot.com/2011/07/what-do-you-love.html?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed%3A+blogspot%2FMKuf+%28Official+Google+Blog%29")

What it does is, it takes your query and searches inside all different Google products with just one click...try it, I did and I'm loving it
