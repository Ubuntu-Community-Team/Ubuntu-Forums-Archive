### Post by Paqman on 2009-11-14
Android phones sync themselves automatically. It all gets synced to your Google accounts (calendar, contacts, email, etc) so as long as you're using apps on your Ubuntu machine that sync with Google then you're sorted. 

There's no such thing as syncing over USB, because the phone syncs with the Google servers, not your computer.
