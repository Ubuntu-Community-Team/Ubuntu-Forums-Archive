### Post by Mr-Biscuit on 2009-05-22
"Everyday somebody wakes up. I just wish it didn't have to be me." 

If I know my limits and can act within those limits,
then I am aware.
If I know my limits and push them,
then I am learning.
Let me learn my limits and exceed them each time,
then I will know what it means to be free.


Love that has fear is not love at all.

Respect due to coercion is anything but that.

I know my mortality and I know that I can die.

Death is not what I fear,
not being able to right what I did wrong is.
