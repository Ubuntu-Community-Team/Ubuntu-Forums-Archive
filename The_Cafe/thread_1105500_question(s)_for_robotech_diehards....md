### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2009-03-25
Robotech was composed of thee series:
1st series: 
Macross:
Macross is the foundation of Robotech's storyline and the setting of its first generation.
Macross in its original format had around 38 episodes, during the import process Harmony Gold the company behind the Robotech project originally wanted to air Macross on its own, but for syndication at that time you needed over 60 episodes.
Macross is the one with Rick Hunter, Lisa Hays, Roy Fokker and the Zentraedi.

2nd series:
Southern Cross:
Southern Cross is actually the most edited of the Robotech franchise, originally set on another planet with 2 moons called Glorie Harmony Gold had to make tons of edits as there were a lot of scenes showing the 2 moons.
Lasting 25 or so episodes it composes the second generation of Robotech.
Southern Cross is the one with Dana Sterling the daughter of First generations Max Sterling and Mirya Parino a Zentraedi, Zor and the Robotech Masters.

3rd series:
Genesis Climber Mospedia:
The one with the scene with the leaches and the cyclones.
Mospedia makes up the final half of the original Robotech saga, lasting the same amount of episodes as Southern cross.
Starring Scott Benard, Marlene, Annie "Mint" LaBelle and the controversial Lancer/Yellow dancer (he was a cross dresser)
