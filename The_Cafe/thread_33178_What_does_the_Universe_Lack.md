### Post by az on 2005-05-11
[QUOTE=Ubunted]**JAVA!!!**

Yeah I know, legal implications and all, but maybe the Blackdown stuff?[/QUOTE]

Blackdown java is subject to the same licencing issues as Sun's java.

You are not allowed to distribute any other type of java environment if you distribute theirs.  This is why the proprietary linux distributions (Xandros, linspire, Mephis can ship with the official java since they chose not to ship the free implementations (and thus they do not contribute to the development of those free implementations - you are getting my opinion at no extra cost, here)
