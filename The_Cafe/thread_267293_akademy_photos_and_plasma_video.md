### Post by GeneralZod on 2006-09-28
My video stopped playing at around 22:30, but from what I saw, it was an interesting talk.  No screenshots were provided - Aaron claims that he gave these in earlier talks to non-developers, but he thought that a focus on the technical aspects would be more suited to aKademy.

Basically, it all seems rather evolutionary rather than revolutionary, although I suppose the same could have been said of Firefox's extension system (which has some fairly strong parallels with parts of Plasma), and we all know how that turned out :).  The technology and design itself is underwhelming (though solid) but maybe third party developers can make it live up to its potential.

Hopefully this video will help cool some of the hype and speculation that's currently flying around.
