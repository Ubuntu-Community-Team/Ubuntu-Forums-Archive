### Post by siciliancasanova on 2007-12-15
Let's see, domain name you want a known registrar even if it costs a couple extra bucks.  Sketchy cheap registrars will cause you weeks of frustration if you need to transfer the domain later.

I use godaddy.com you can find promo codes on google to take a couple bucks off

For a web server, if it's a relatively small site, with relatively small traffic you should not spend more than $10 a month for any host.

I do offer hosting myself, and I charge all my clients $9/month and they are probably the same size as your set up.  I wouldn't go with any host, again, that is more than $10/month.
