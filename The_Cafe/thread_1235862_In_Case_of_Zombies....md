### Post by EnergySamus on 2009-08-09
Hi!
Well, many people have seen the "Break Glass In Case Of Zombies" image online. It is a promotion for the game 'Dead Rising' If you haven't here it is:

[http://ui10.gamespot.com/1641/incaseofzombiesbreakglass_2.jpg](http://ui10.gamespot.com/1641/incaseofzombiesbreakglass_2.jpg)

Well, I decided to create my own version of this image just for fun... And here you go: 

[IMG]http://i581.photobucket.com/albums/ss253/EnergyXP/IMG_1463.jpg[/IMG]


I personally like mine much more than the other one, although that just *might* be a biased opinion :P .
It was quite easy to make as well. I used regular wood and sliced it up with a automatic rotating saw. A pane of plastic, a lock (seen on the right side), red paint, black paint, and deep red paint for the writing.
The Gun is a Crosman Pro77 C02 BB gun w/ a BSA Laser Sight attached (it is unloaded). The Knife is a Smith and Wesson M&P. And the book is "The Zombie Survival Guide" by Max Brooks.


Hope you like it! If you have any suggestions, throw them out.

EnergySamus
