### Post by K.Mandla on 2010-06-27
There are also some multiplexers or "window" managers for the console environment.

[vwm]("http://vwm.sourceforge.net/")
[twin]("http://linuz.sns.it/~max/twin/")

[screen]("http://www.gnu.org/software/screen/")
[tmux]("http://tmux.sourceforge.net/")

[dvtm]("http://www.brain-dump.org/projects/dvtm/")

They're not "classical" window managers, but they will do the job.
