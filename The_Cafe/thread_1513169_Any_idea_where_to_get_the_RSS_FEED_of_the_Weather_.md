### Post by Ebuntor on 2010-06-19
[yr.no]("http://www.yr.no/") 
A website by the Norwegian Meteorological Institute and is, as far as I know, the most precise weather website there is.

Do a search for where you want info about the weather and there should be an RSS link at the top-right side.
