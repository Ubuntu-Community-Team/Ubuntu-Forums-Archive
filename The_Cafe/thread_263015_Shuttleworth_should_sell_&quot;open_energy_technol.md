### Post by newbie2 on 2006-09-22
> Ubuntu and open source - not only for Shuttleworth's Linux
Date: Fri 22 September 2006
Category: Energy
Issue No:

Mark Shuttleworth should sell the idea of non-patentable shared "open energy technology" to world leaders as its potential to have a profound impact on the reduction of the greenhouse gases is enormous.

Developing a shared open energy technology development platform, similar to SourceForge, would accelerate the growth of cleaner energy and fuel technology more than any other initiative, and thus impact significantly on efforts to deal with climate change.

Mark Shuttleworth, who is currently taking on Microsoft's domination of the operating system market through the development of the open source operating system Ubuntu Linux is the idea person to convince influential players like Bill Clinton, Al Gore, Richard Branson and international bodies like the United Nations of the potential for this intiative.
