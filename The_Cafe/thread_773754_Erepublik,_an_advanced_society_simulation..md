I've been playing this browser based online game called [erepublik]("http://www.erepublik.com") for a while now, and I figured that I'd give you a chance to join in. It's an invite only game (still in beta), so PM me your email address and I'll send you an invite.

Basically you play a role as a citizen of any given country in the world (well, not really. But a lot of countries are represented) and you yourself can choose what to do with your life. You can either decide to just be your average Joe Sixpack and do nothing but work, eat and work out. Or you can choose to pursue a career in politics, or even in the military. Like I said, it's still in beta, so all features are not present yet. New modules are constantly being added to the game - the newest one being the war module.

Sound good? Then shoot me a PM and I'll send you an invite. Note that I only have 4 invites left, so first come, first serve.
