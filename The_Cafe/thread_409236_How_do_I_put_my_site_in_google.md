### Post by ronniet on 2007-04-14
Best way is to get links from ssites that are already listed in Google and get recognsed that way.

Failing that, sign up for a [Google sitemaps]("https://www.google.com/webmasters/sitemaps/") account and submit a sitemap of your site for Google to crawl.

### Post by Polygon on 2007-04-14
you dont even need to do that

if you are really lazy, you can use this:

[http://www.google.com/addurl/?continue=/addurl](http://www.google.com/addurl/?continue=/addurl)
