### Post by JlyGrnMigt on 2010-05-17
I bought their Terra netbook (which they no longer sell) around Christmas 2009.  I'm running UNR on it, and I've had a slew of problems.  Some are definitely linux-related, and others may or may not be hardware. I'm 80% happy with the response I've gotten from the company, though.

My main issue is with the touchpad.  The click will stop working sometimes, and no solution has been found on the forums. My workaround is to plug in a USB mouse before booting, and to disable the touchpad immediately upon logging in.  If I don't disable it and accidentally touch the pad, I can't click with either mouse because it thinks I'm dragging.  It's bizarre.

I also had brief trouble with the battery not wanting to charge sometimes, which was fixed after I re-seated it.  It recurred for about a month, and hasn't been an issue since.

My power cord is also sliding off the wires at the outlet end.

But I'm using it now, so it still functions...
