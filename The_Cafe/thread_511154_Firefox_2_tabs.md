### Post by PatrickMay16 on 2007-07-27
And here's another thing I just found; go to about:config and change **browser.tabs.closeButtons** to **3**, and this will change the tabs so there is only one 'close tab' button which is placed at the far right of the tab bar, just like in firefox 1.5.
