### Post by AndyCooll on 2008-11-25
I like Linux Format too. It depends on what you're looking for in a magazine but it seems to me that Linux Format seems to strike a good balance between articles for absolute beginners and articles for users who are more experienced but still wouldn't consider themselves as technically minded. For those who are looking for an in-depth technical Linux magazine then Linux Format probably isn't for you.

IMHO the other Linux magazines on the WH Smiths shelf whilst not necessarily being American tend to have an American "feel" to them and are for the more technically advanced readers.
