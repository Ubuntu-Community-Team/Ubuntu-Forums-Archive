### Post by herbster on 2008-06-23
Is this going to be your everyday desktop machine? What kind of video editing? And that 8400 does not have any capture capabilities, you will need a separate capture card, like a Hauppauge (I personally have a PVR-150mce I use with MythTV on my server).

That PSU is excellent, it's the exact one I have on my machine, but it's definitely overkill for you. It won't hurt by getting it, but if you can find a 300w-350w PSU that's cheaper enough it provides decent savings, go for that. Your desired specs won't be going anywhere near 500w at any time.

You forgot the hard drive and case, unless you already have either/or component(s).
