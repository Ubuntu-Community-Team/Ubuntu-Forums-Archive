### Post by aysiu on 2007-04-13
There's a free PDF magazine that just got started. Search the forums for Full Circle.

Hard copy mags--your best bet is *Linux Format*. It's not specifically about Ubuntu, but it's a great magazine. The price varies, depending on what country you live in. In the US, it's $16 per issue. It's cheaper in the UK.
