### Post by robsoles on 2012-02-10
The only tip I can suggest for anybody thinking of giving any file hosting/sharing site money for extra/better service is to try to research their customer complaints using Google.

If half of what you can find in Google about them, in terms of 3rd party review or complaints, does not put you off them and the other half doesn't have seriously meaningful flags to you then they are probably at least trying to be legit with at least remotely reasonable servers behind them.

If you can't find mention of them at all or all you can find is "beware" type evil warnings etc etc then it is either too undefined (no results!!???) or too clearly defined as to why you aren't going to give them any payment details whatso-ever.
