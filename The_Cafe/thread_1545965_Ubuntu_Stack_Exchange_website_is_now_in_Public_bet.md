### Post by arch0njw on 2010-09-05
> **fluteflute said:**
> I don't think integration is planned (although possibly the same log in details).

The idea is that the Stack Exchange website cuts out the discussion and forums, and makes it easier to find the actual answers.

It is a great idea, but I've seen this sort of thing before.  There will be answers in both places and it actually makes it harder to find an answer.  Ultimately it isn't a lot harder, but having more than one place to look at is the start of a more complicated problem.  I have faced this in content management when companies keep their documents or content in more than one location.  It creates a division and confusion as to where one can find "the real information".

This isn't to say that it isn't a good idea or that this should not be done.  But some kind of cross-references would go very far to help in preventing the problem of dispersed information.
