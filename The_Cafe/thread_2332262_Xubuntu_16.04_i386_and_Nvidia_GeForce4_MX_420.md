### Post by Xubuntist on 2016-07-29
I upgraded today from an onscreen prompt from Xubuntu to move up to 16.04 (i386). When the PC rebooted after the upgrade all I got was a black screen with a cursor flashing. I suspected the original gpu drivers weren't compatible with 16. After fighting to get the onboard graphics drivers working, I gave up and inserted an Nvidia GeForce4 MX 420 card from an old donor machine. This time I got some results but only at 800 x 600 at a displayed refresh rate of 0.0 Hz (not a misprint!). I really struggled with this until I found a closed thread on here which saved my bacon and left with with a lovely smooth graphics performance. I want to thank user [**[COLOR=#DD4814][B]Bashing-om**[/COLOR][/B]]("https://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=1111508") for the help he unwittingly gave me to solve my problem [in this thread]("https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2229183&page=2").

Thank you :)
