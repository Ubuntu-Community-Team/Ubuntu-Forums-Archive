### Post by DarkN00b on 2006-10-19
For those of you who haven't seen it yet, *Linux was featured in an article in MaximumPC magazine's holiday 2006 issue! Although Linux in general was the topic of the article, Ubuntu was chosen to give a demonstration of installation. The article focused on the availability of Linux distros in liveCD form. Gentoo, Knoppix and Ubuntu were given as examples with a little background on each provided.

BUT -- When they chose a distro to demonstrate how to install, they chose Ubuntu! They talked about how the shining point of Ubuntu was the package management system and automatic background updates.  It was a great article.

I'm going over to [www.maximumpc.com]("http://www.maximumpc.com/") and try to find a way to e-mail them and thank them for such a great article on Ubuntu.

Seriously, if you don't have a copy of the holiday 2006 edition of MaximumPC, get one. Let's show MaximumPC how much we appreciate good press from such a (normally) Win-centric magazine. \\:D/
