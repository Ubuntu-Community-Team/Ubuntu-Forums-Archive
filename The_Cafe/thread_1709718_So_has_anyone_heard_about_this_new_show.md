### Post by Legendary_Bibo on 2011-03-18
It's called My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, and yes it's a cartoon.

Now, I know most of your first thoughts are "that's a show for little girls, I'm a man, I would never watch it GRRRR!!!" as you take a swig of nuts and bolts out of a tin can.

That's what I first thought, but the thing is that it's actually a good cartoon with antics on par with classics such as Bugs Bunny. It also has a good voice cast, and animation. Also, it frequently has new songs in several episodes.

The show was created by Lauren Faust (Powerpuff Girls, Foster's Home for Imaginary Friends), and her husband. Her goal was to make show with a target audience of young females, but without making it stupidly girly so that even their parents could enjoy it. She succeeded beyond expectations, and most of her audience are males in the 20-34 age group. I've become a fan of the show myself, and I figured some of you guys would enjoy it. Also, a lot of people recommend to at least watch the first four episodes before making a judgment.

I worked on this yesterday, I'll probably write up the README tonight, and reupload it for people who aren't sure on how to install it. Basically as root just extract the folders into /usr/share/backgrounds, and in Appearance (System -> Preferences -> Appearance) click on Add, go to the directory, click on the drop down menu on the bottom right that says 'images' and click all files, then click the .xml file. I have them set to cycle every 5 minutes, but in the README I'll describe how to change how long it takes for them to change.
