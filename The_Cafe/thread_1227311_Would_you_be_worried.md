### Post by aysiu on 2009-07-30
I'll be perfectly honest (but I can't speak on behalf of any of the other mods or admins)--if **I** look at your profile, there's a reason for it, and it doesn't usually mean something good.

It doesn't necessarily mean something bad, though.

The primary reason I look at profiles is to look at what other threads the user has started. This could be because I think she's created duplicate threads that need to be merged or deleted. Or it could be that I suspect the user of being a troll.

And if I suspect someone of being a spammer, I look at the post count. Since some people (like me) hide the beans, I have to then look at the profile in order to see the bean count.

If someone just joined yesterday and posted only threads linking to something external, I'm generally going to think that person is a spammer.

In the old days, you could click on the username and then see all the threads that user started. Now, you have to click on the username and then click through the profile and then statistics. Or do an advanced search on the username.

So, I don't know about the other staff, but if aysiu looks at your profile, it's not usually a good thing. It doesn't mean you've done anything wrong, but you're probably under suspicion of something.
