I used the hot swap method where you open the case and switch the ide cables to your computer 

Here is the guide I followed PM me if you have questions about Hot Swapping
[http://forums.maxconsole.net/archive/index.php/t-13629.html](http://forums.maxconsole.net/archive/index.php/t-13629.html)
