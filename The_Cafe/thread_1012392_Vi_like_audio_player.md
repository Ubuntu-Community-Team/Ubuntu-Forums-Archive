### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-12-16
The Sox package comes with a little program called "play" that plays any audio files you pass to it.

Press Control-C to go to the next song, press Control-C twice to quit. The usual "suspend job" and "resume job" keyboard commands in Bash work to pause and resume playback.
