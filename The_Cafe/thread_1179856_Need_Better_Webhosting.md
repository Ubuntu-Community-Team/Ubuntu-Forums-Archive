### Post by MrDelish on 2009-06-11
I was with Hostgator for a year but didn't try to contact support until I wanted to cancel. The chat tech didn't seem too bright, but that was based on just one experience. I never really paid attention to the uptime, email reliability and so on - it was for a project that didn't take off. Their prices seem pretty good.

Full disclosure: I do tech support for BlueHost. I've worked in other tech support environments and think they are VERY support-oriented. The last company I worked for started outsourcing their support and the customer experience went even further down the toilet. Your mileage always varies with the tech you happen to get on the phone or in chat (I, for the record, am extremely helpful over the phone :KS), but we have plenty of folks go out of their way to say how pleased they are with BlueHost's service.
