### Post by HermanAB on 2012-01-21
That is not really an error message.  It means that there is no valid device on that ATA port.

---

### Post by Lucradia on 2012-01-21
If there is a drive there, and it's giving that message, it usually means the MBR is corrupt, (this is fixed by using DBAN to wipe the entire drive completely, until it's fixed, Windows won't even install) or you accidentally punched the part of the laptop that has the harddrive. (Even if the harddrive looks physically fine, the harddrive will never work again.)
