### Post by bsharp on 2009-01-30
I *just now* picked up Slaughter of the Soul, and I am completely blown away. I thought I had heard the perfect album when I got Wintersun (by Wintersun), but this predates it by 8 years! How could I have been so blind?

The sound is unmatched, this 1996 album could have been recorded yesterday. :guitar:

Fans of melodic death or thrash metal, buy this album as soon as you can!

Favorites so far (they are all my favs, these are the ones that stand out):
1.Slaughter of the Soul
2.Blinded by Fear
3.Into the Dead Sky
4.Suicide Nation
5.Under a Serpent Sun
6.The Flames of the End

Any other people here have/interested in this album?
