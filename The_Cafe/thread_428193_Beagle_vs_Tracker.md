### Post by ButteBlues on 2007-04-30
They function with two generally separate concepts. Tracker wants to be the main backend of everything and search the indexes that way.

Beagle wants to let individual apps handle the backend indexing, and keep its own index of those files for searching.


I prefer Beagle for having more features and faster development.
