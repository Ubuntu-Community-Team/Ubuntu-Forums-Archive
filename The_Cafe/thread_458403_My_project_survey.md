### Post by Das Oracle on 2007-05-29
Hello, I just finished drafting an online survey for a project. Before I post the link let me at least give you a little background. The project name is Veso, we are a group of students dedicated to producing an electric vehicle prototype. The end result of our project is to actually make a production model for the consumer. After a few meetings of deciding where our project should go we had many items that found were inconclusive. We have devised a survey to hopefully answer some of our questions. If you are wondering why this is posted in UbuntuForums the answer is simple. One of the features we want in the prototype is a built in computer system that runs off of Ubuntu. The exact details of what it will be used for are not to be disclosed at the moment but when we begin our prototype more details will be posted. Here is the link and the more honest responses we can get the better solutions we can prototype. Thank you for your time.


[http://vesoautomotive.com/survey/public/survey.php?name=Consumer_Survey_1]("http://vesoautomotive.com/survey/public/survey.php?name=Consumer_Survey_1")
