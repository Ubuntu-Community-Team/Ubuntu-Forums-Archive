### Post by macewan on 2005-12-08
Has anyone tried [gSpace]("http://www.rjonna.com/ext/gspace.php") yet? Below is an explanation from the site:
[INDENT][[SIZE=2]Gmail                  File Space (gspace) v0.1:[/SIZE]]("http://www.rjonna.com/ext/gspace_v0.1.xpi") This extension allows you                  to use your Gmail Space (2 GB) for file storage. It acts as a                  remote machine. You can transfer files between your hard drive                  and gmail. This is similar to "Gmail Drive" on windows                  platform. Your gmail account looks like a FTP host and you can                  upload and download your files. After you install, you get an                  option called "GSpace" in your "tools" menu                  clicking on which opens the window for transfer of folders/files.
