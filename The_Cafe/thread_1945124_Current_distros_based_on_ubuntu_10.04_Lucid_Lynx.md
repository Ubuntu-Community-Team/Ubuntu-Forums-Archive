### Post by papibe on 2012-03-22
Hi icett.

Take a look at [Bodhi]("http://www.bodhilinux.com/"), which is based in the latest Ubuntu LTS.

However, I'm not sure if you are looking more of a distro that is based on the interface of 10.04 Lucid Lynx. For example, traditional menus, a lower bar for managing your open windows, etc (basically a Gnome 2.x style).

If so, take a look at [Cinnamon Mint]("http://cinnamon.linuxmint.com/").

Also note that the incoming 12.04 release will have a way of 'converting' the interface as the one on 10.04 (read about it [here]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2012/03/gnome-classic-in-ubuntu-12-04-its-like-nothing-ever-changed/")).

Hope that helps.
Regards.
