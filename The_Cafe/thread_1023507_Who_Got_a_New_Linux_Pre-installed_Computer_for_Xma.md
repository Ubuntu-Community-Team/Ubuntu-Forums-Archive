I did not, but I did install [**[COLOR="#66BB00"]openSUSE 11.1[/COLOR]**]("http://www.opensuse.org/en/") on to my laptop.

The best part I liked about it is that the WPA/WPA2 personal wireless worked flawlessly from the Gnome liveCD, and so I decided to install it to the hard drive.

It tends to hang during boot, and I have to keep tapping the touchpad or a key like the backspace or the spacebar keys to have it continue to boot though.
