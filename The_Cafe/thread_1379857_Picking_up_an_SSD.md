> **blazemore said:**
> Does anyone know where (or even if!) I can purchase a small (~32GB) but FAST (None of these netbook ones) SSD?
[Here?]("http://ncix.com/products/index.php?minorcatid=1275") Many other computer stores as well.

> And would putting my / partition on one make a difference in terms of speed?
Bootup (all after the BIOS screens) is fast, all programs stored on the drive start almost instantly (even porky, slow Firefox starts and shuts down fast). Basically anything that loads off the drive is virtually "instant", and writes to the drive are very quick. Installs (after the download part) are very fast.

About a week ago I put in a [G.SKILL Falcon II 128GB Indilinx]("http://ncix.com/products/index.php?sku=46520&vpn=FM-25S2I-128GBF2&manufacture=G.Skill") unit in my #1 box and installed Ubuntu 64-bit (which is *still* pretty flaky - I'll be going back to 32-bit soon) on it. I'm lovin the SSD. Big bang for the buck. :D
