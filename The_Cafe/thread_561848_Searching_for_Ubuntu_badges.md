### Post by welkin on 2008-01-29
I know this thread is really old, but I thought I'd post just in case someone is still looking for ubuntu pins. I know that my university has a store that will make pins for you at a cost of about 30 cents (CAD) each if you buy 20 or multiples thereof. I'm sure there's a store in your city that will do the same thing for you - you just provide a high quality image of what you want on the pin, and they press it for you. The store I used even let me do a bunch of different pins in small amounts for the same price (I did 10 each of six designs). It's a good way of getting lots of custom pins.
