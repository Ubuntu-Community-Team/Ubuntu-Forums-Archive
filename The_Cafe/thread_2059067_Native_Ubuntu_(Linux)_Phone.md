### Post by forrestcupp on 2012-09-17
As far as I know, you're not going to get a major US phone that will run Ubuntu natively. Until Ubuntu for Android comes out, the best you're going to get will be to hack your Android phone to run Ubuntu on top of Android. Any powerful Android phone with root access can be hacked to do that.

[Here's a tutorial](Here's a tutorial) explaining how to do it. The downsides are that it's a pain in the backside, it's risky, and it's not native so your phone isn't really made to do that.
