### Post by porkrind on 2006-05-22
Just wanted to let you know that I'm putting together a free conference - The Ubucon (rhymes with Rubicon) - for Ubuntu users, developers and admins. Many many thanks to Google for letting me use their space. A huge debt of gratitude goes to Chris D. (Summer of Code guy) and Leslie over there for helping with this.

The Ubucon will be held August 18 and 19, the days immediately following 
LinuxWorld San Francisco.

Here's the conference wiki so far - [http://www.linuxpip.org/ubuconwiki/](http://www.linuxpip.org/ubuconwiki/)

I put up a pointer at [http://wiki.ubuntu.com/TheUbucon](http://wiki.ubuntu.com/TheUbucon)

I don't really care where the wiki lives, just as long as it lives somewhere :@)

As you can see, the schedule's not filled in yet. With your help, however, that will change RSN. 

There is a mailing list - [email]theubucon@linuxpip.org[/email], and you can subscribe at 
[email]theubucon-request@linuxpip.org[/email] or go to 
[http://www.linuxpip.org/mailman/listinfo/theubucon](http://www.linuxpip.org/mailman/listinfo/theubucon)

I'd love to get your feedback and ideas about what the conference should be.

Edit: On yeah, and I started a blog - [http://theubucon.blogspot.com/](http://theubucon.blogspot.com/) - let me know if I can add you as a blogger.

Thanks!
John Mark
