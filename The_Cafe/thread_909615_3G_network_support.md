It means network manager will be able to connect to 3g network if you have a 3g network card. 

While that might not be useful for desktop users, it is for notebook owners.

In theory you could get 3g access just around everywhere you are in your country. This means you have high speed internet access just about everywhere. So you won't need to be surfing on dodgy open wifi networks or pay to access them when you travel.

If you have a decent 3g supplier, you could get good speeds and no caps.
