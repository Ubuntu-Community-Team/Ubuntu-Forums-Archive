### Post by arzali on 2011-11-20
I got this new design automatically but not on all my accounts and here is a way to get it for you too:

[IMG]https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/-YN6vn3jBMxA/TshaRTysQSI/AAAAAAAAMdQ/hWRcZ5Wob3o/s1024/youtube_new.png[/IMG]



1. Go to [http://www.youtube.com]("http://www.youtube.com/")
2. Press Ctrl + Shift and J in Chrome to open the Developer Tools
    Press Ctrl+Shift+K in Firefox
3. Click on the "Console" Tab and enter
document.cookie="VISITOR_INFO1_LIVE=ST1Ti53r4fU";
4. Reload the website and enjoy the new layout.

I first got  SyntaxError: illegal character with firefox.
To fix it try document.cookie='VISITOR_INFO1_LIVE=ST1Ti53r4fU';

for Opera:
Settings > Preferences > Advanced > Cookies >Manage Cookies
search VISITOR_INFO1_LIVE
press edit
set Value to: ST1Ti53r4fU
save and reload youtube

Source: [https://plus.google.com/117596712775912423303/posts/K9bQqW9LRci](https://plus.google.com/117596712775912423303/posts/K9bQqW9LRci)
