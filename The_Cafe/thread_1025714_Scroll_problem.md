### Post by MaxIBoy on 2008-12-30
Type in "about:config" into your address bar. Take a look through it. Anything you set as a user should be in bold. Look for anything that might be related to your problem, and look up what that option does on Google, but **DON'T TOUCH ANYTHING UNTIL YOU KNOW WHAT THE OPTION DOES!**

Until you get this fixed, use the pageup and pagedown keys.
