**A wallpaper pack you say? What does this consist of?**
*A wallpaper pack should consist of images suitable for any range of common resolutions on a monitor, anything from 800x600 to 	2560×1600(or above!) . Just make sure that you keep the dimensions to those specific parameters, other dimensions look distorted, and just don't quite cut it.*

**Well awesome! Now, how can I get these wallpapers out of this file?**
*Simply use the system archiver tool(you may have to install the RAR tool) and unrar the walls to a specified folder. Then preview and choose them from the background selection screen*

**What can I do if I want to upload my own wallpaper pack?**
*Simply put your favorite wallpapers into a archive you create and upload to any sharing sites. Below I have included a list of commonly used sites.*

[http://rapidshare.com](http://rapidshare.com)
