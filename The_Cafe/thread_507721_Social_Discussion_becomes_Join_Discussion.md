### Post by Old Pink on 2007-07-23
[B][Announcement]("http://joindiscussion.com/forum-feedback-help/5675-social-discussion-now-join-discussion.html#post80270")

[/B]> There are some exciting changes in the works!
 
For starters, our forums have a new name, "JoinDiscussion.com." Watch for new graphics, and a new logo. Please be sure to update your bookmarks from socialdiscussion.com to joindiscussion.com as soon as possible so you don't miss any of the fun.

What is causing all this? Well, the forums have been sold. Don't worry, we aren't going anywhere, but things will change a little bit here and there. None of the staff have changed, and we have no plans to make any staff changes at this point. We do have one new admin, [sdkub]("http://joindiscussion.com/members/sdkub.html"), who you may see around occasionally. His main focus will be behind the scenes.
 
Just to give you a little bit of a "heads up" as to what to expect, here's a list of items that are slated for some adjustments. We plan to:[LIST]
[*]simplify the category listings a bit
[*]focus primarily on providing a place for discussions, both serious and fun
[*]change the forum skin a little and
[*]create a menu, with list of all available forums accessible from all pages[/LIST]
Well, it looks interesting. The change left us with a nicer layout, but a worse name and less activity, in my opinion. The most important change is that Ubuntuforums owner ubuntu-geek (or brandx) no longer owns the forums. 

It's a shame, the new admin won't compare to ubuntu-geek, and he's already said he won't be a posting admin, just a techie. But hopefully we'll see more of ubuntu-geek over here. :D 

Anyway. Congratulations on the sale. :)
