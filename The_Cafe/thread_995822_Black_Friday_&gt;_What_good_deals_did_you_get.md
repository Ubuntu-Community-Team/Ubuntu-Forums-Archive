### Post by klange on 2008-11-28
**Why is the day after Thanksgiving called "Black Friday"?**

It's a simple answer. Before the holiday season, most store chains are at a loss - selling in the "red". Despite the seemingly lower prices and abundance of deals, the massive sales achieved on the day after Thanksgiving actually bring chains out of the red and into the "black". Hence, "Black Friday".

***And now you know!***
