### Post by steveneddy on 2009-02-25
My issues are as follows:

I am unemployed at the moment and I had two instances of FF open simultaneously with one instance User Agent Switched to Windows Vista and IE7 and the other on Default.

I found that this confuses the FF rendering library somewhat and I could not type anything in an e-mail reply box, Ubuntu Forums reply box or even Google's search box.

Once I closed one FF and switched the open one to Default and not User Agent Switched, everything went back to normal.

Also Gmail did not look correct and Google maps simply would not function.

The second post of this thread was just me testing to make sure that I had the correct fix.
