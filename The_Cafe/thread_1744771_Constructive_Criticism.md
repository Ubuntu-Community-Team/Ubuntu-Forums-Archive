### Post by Richard Carlson on 2011-04-30
11.04 Unity is a good start but by far incomplete and need of reconstruction IMHO. Left hand menu is not the place to scroll through hundreds of Icons for programs installed and most commonly used based on peoples preferences and tastes. 

My criticism will be aimed only at the 3D version (using Nvidia and ATI video) cards and enhancements. 

I wish the developers would have only used the left hand margin for fix stationary Internet and Utilities most commonly used in all of the categories below in a hierarchical order. 

Left side fixed menu categories: 

1) Home folder
2) Internet Browser
3) E-mail
4) Cd/Dvd  burner utility
5) Gnome-Commander/Krusader Utility with built-in 'Terminal'.
6) Calculator
7) Printer Utility
8) Internet Video Camera Utility
9) Scanner Utility
10) Dictionary
11) Office Interface Menu
12) Photo thumbnail-browser Utility
13) Music Utility
14) Chat Utility
15) Bit-torrent utility
16) Software Update Utility
17) Software Installer Utility
18) System Moniter
19) Trash

The top of the screen should have what it has already but also in addition to compiz (slide or cube) workplace switcher Utility. Additional plugins also such as weather, program-kill button for lockups etc.) 

Bottom of the screen should have Docky with intellihide for all of the additional custom programs people would like to add and use. 

The rest could stay the same. I would also improve on the application category and and icon viewer of all installed programs and get rid of this button in the left side menu bar as it exists in the top left screen corner area.
