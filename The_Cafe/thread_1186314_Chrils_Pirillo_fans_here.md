### Post by Darkhack on 2009-06-13
> **MikeTheC said:**
> Who is Chris Pirillo?
He's a tech commentator.  He had his own TV show for a while and now blogs/streams online.  His nickname (and name of his website) is Lockergnome which comes from the fact that he could completely fit into his locker in high school.  Even as an adult, he is only 5'3 (160 cm).  Wikipedia has an article about him.  [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chris_Pirillo](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chris_Pirillo)

Chris is a really nice guy but I no longer listen to him for two reasons.  The first is that he tends to target the less geeky and less skilled computer user.  If you're an Ubuntu user and know your way around computers, you'll likely get bored listening to him.  The second reason is that Chris takes an incredibly long time to spit out what he's trying to say.  I've seen him make ten minute YouTube videos which have about two minutes worth of content.  He has a really hard time getting to the point.  It makes listening to him annoying and even stressful at times.  He really is a super nice guy though.  Sometimes he tries too hard to be funny, but I forgive him.
