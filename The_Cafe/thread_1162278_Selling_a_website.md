### Post by sydbat on 2009-05-17
15,000 to 20,000 hits/month is nothing. I run a small site that gets 50,000 - 75,000 hits/month from search engines alone. The other ~250,000 hits come from (hopefully) real people.

So, I would not expect you to get much money for your site.

Also, it is not the content of your site you are selling, it is the domain name. Content, unless it is completely unique, is not a selling point. If your site was for a company that sold items/services, it would be different.
