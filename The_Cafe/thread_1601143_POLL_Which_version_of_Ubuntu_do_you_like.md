### Post by aust77 on 2010-10-20
Basically an explanation of what each one did for me.

9.04: I used this on both my laptop and desktop. It was fantastic and very stable and I loved Firefox in it for some reason. I "fell back" to this version numerous times on my desktop.
9.10: Xorg constantly crashed due to my intel integrated graphics card on my desktop so I switched back to 9.04. I used 9.10 on my laptop up until 10.10 and it worked great.
10.04: The installer wouldn't even work on my laptop, so I kept 9.10 installed. My desktop experienced crashing once again but I was able to stop it using a number of temporary fixes.
10.10: Has worked excellent on both of my computers so far. By far my favorite, and only the second to work successfully on both my computers.
