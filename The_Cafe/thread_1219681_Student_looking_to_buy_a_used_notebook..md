### Post by richg on 2009-07-22
A few months ago I picked up used off of ebay a Acer Aspire laptop that comes with Vista Basic for $325.00, free shipping. These laptops were being sold at Walmart for $348.00 somewhere in late 2008 I believe. My stepson picked one up there new back around Jan 2009. I bought mine a couple months later. The exact model number is 5515-5879. There are still some on ebay the last time I looked. They give the specs there. I know they have a 160gb hard drive which is plenty for my requirements.
My Acer now runs Mint 7 with no issues. Sometimes I like a larger display. I still have a Asus mini laptop that is easy to use but the display is rather small.
Your mileage may vary.
