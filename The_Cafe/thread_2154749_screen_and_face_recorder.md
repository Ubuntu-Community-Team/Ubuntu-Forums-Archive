### Post by pqwoerituytrueiwoq on 2013-06-16
I suggest using [kazam]("apt:kazam"), you can record the webcam by having a viewer open on the screen and making the window always on top, you can open /dev/video0 in vlc smplayer totem etc. with some webcams, you can use actual viewers like [Cheese]("apt:cheese"), [Camorama Webcam Viewer]("http://camorama"), or [GTK UVC video viewer]("apt:guvcview")
