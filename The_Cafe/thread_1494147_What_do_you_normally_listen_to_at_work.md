### Post by samalex on 2010-05-26
Hey Guys--

I spend most of my day in a cubical, so to avoid going mad I have headphones on listening to a variety of content.

I have my normal podcasts I try to catch:
Monday - This Week in Tech, Science Friday (from prior week)
Monday or Tuesday - This Week in Amateur Radio
Wednesday/Thursday - Off the Hook/Off the Wall
Whenever they come out - Retrobits, FLOSS Weekly, and many others

I also stream SomaFM or various NPR stations that do Jazz or Classical, plus I love Magnatune.

And if there's a tech conference that's streaming, I try to keep it on in the background as much as possible.

Plus I have my laptop full of MP3's, though I rarely listen to them.  Mostly when I do it's classical, jazz, or the various eclectic or techno groups I like.  I rarely listen to pop or popular music (from any era).  

What do others listen to when at work?
