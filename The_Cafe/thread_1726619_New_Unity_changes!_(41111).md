### Post by Cam! on 2011-04-11
Unity got an update. Here are some observations I've seen.

[LIST]
Animations are much more smooth.
When clicking the Apps or Files/Folders icon, the launcher becomes monochrome. The same goes for clicking the Ubuntu logo and searching for something.
The Home Folder icon now looks like the original (default) one.
 When the launcher is hidden, try minimizing a window. It "peeks" out of the launcher and wiggles as a notification as to where it is.
[/LIST]

If thee's anything I missed, please say so!
