### Post by buddyd16 on 2011-08-23
Will this be for cable television, satellite television, or over the air/broadcast television.

**For Cable:** 
Is the tuner in the PC a Cable Card tuner?

No and Family wants HD - get a pre-packaged dvr from your family's cable provider

No and Family doesn't care about HD - stick with windows media center as it provides the guide information for free and setting up an ir blaster is a bit simpler and can be redone through the media center settings if something breaks
**A cable box from your family's provider is required as well as an IR Blaster to send the channel change singals from your computer to the cable box**

Yes - stick with windows media center

**For Satellite:**
Family wants HD - Get pre-packaged DVR from their provider

Family doesn't care about HD - See response for cable without HD
**A satellite receiver from your family's provider is required as well as an IR Blaster to send the channel change singals from your computer to the receiver**

**For OTA/Broadcast:**
**The tuner in the PC will need to be a digital tuner (clearQAM/ATSC)**

Stick with media center for the free program guide but get tied into the closed recording format

Use MythTV with the annual fee for the program guide and have more freedom over the recording format

If the tuner in the PC is an Analog tuner then you will need to either purchase a digital tuner or get a pre-made DVR that accepts digital clearQAM/ATSC signals
