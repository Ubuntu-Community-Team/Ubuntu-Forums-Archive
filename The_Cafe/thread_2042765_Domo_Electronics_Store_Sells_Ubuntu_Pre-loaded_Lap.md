I noticed the Domo electronics store sells Ubuntu pre-loaded laptops in their store. The store is a mainstream electronics store and the laptops are sold in full view of the general public- they also provide support. We're not dealing with Internet only sales in specialist Linux vendors anymore- we're dealing with normal consumer stores selling their products in malls. The store I visited was located in Romania, although I think the store might also have centers located in other countries as well. The laptops itself were of the common 350 pound type segment, pretty much mainstream- not inaccessibly high end or extreme low end.
