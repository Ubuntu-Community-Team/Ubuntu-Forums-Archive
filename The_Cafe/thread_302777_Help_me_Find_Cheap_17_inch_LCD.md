### Post by Bezmotivnik on 2006-11-19
For the past few months, Hanns-G has had various 19" 8ms LCDs for around $100-$110 after $100 rebate.

I got one for $104 after rebate from OfficeDepot.Com a few weeks back, and Woot.Com had one for $109 after rebate yesterday.

Rebate takes about 10-12 weeks to get, according to other buyers I've read.  Mine's been "accepted" and awaiting payment.

These are perfectly OK monitors, but like all LCDs, *they require configuration at native resolution for good visual clarity*.  I find that 1280x1024 is too big for efficient browsing, but that's life. :-|
