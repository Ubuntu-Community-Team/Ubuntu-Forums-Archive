In any case, the syllogistic logic that you are applying to this situation is irrelevant to actual fact. Syllogisms that are contrary to fact can be true according to deductive sentential logic, but that does not make them pertinent. For instance:

According to this equation, either one or both of p and q have to be true. Therefore the syllogism is valid.
