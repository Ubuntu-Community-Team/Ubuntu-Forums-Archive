### Post by mancha on 2009-06-06
A few years ago I had the chance to visit the Appleby Horse Fair as a spectating day visitor, Not being a Gypsy myself the experience was a complete and utter eye opener. To see young kids riding horses with no saddles and impeccable horsemanship was extraordinary. Hope to go again before I die (maybe 2010)
I know in most towns that Gypsies have a dreadful reputation but I did'nt let that preconceive any ideas of what the fair would be like and yet found them to be friendly and welcoming. Horses everywhere, thousands of them.
