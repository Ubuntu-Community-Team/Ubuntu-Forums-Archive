### Post by TBOL3 on 2009-04-13
Blender, it's amazing.

Oh, and one thing you'll want to watch out for is frame rates.  Make sure that you have blender set to import the video at the correct frame rate.  (For example, I shot some footage in NTSC, and imported it at 25 fps, and it was too long).
