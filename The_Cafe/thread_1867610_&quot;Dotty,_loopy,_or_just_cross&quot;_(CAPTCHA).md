### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-10-23
An interesting article from *The Economist* shows that [current vision-based CAPTCHAs are about to be cracked by automated bots]("http://www.economist.com/node/21532396").

I used to administer a forum for a friend, and we tried all sorts of CAPTCHAs. In the end, the one that stopped automated bots cold was the type that Ubuntu Forums uses, i.e. a text-based question such as, "What is 1+1?" or even, "To help stop spambots, please type the word **bat**."
