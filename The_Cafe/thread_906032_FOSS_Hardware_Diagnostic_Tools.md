### Post by DemonBob on 2008-08-31
What do you mean when you say freaking out? 


For Ram, since you already getting different results fromn 2 Memtest runs. Try downloading the memtest only cd from [http://www.memtest.org/#downiso](http://www.memtest.org/#downiso) and run it. Best 2 out of three pretty much. 

Also if you know what type of HDD you have, i.e. like Seagate/Westren Digital/Maxtor. 

You can goto the maufacture site, and usually they have downloads for diagostic's cds to check the HDD for errors. I.E. [http://support.wdc.com/product/download.asp?groupid=701&sid=30&lang=en](http://support.wdc.com/product/download.asp?groupid=701&sid=30&lang=en) for westren digital. 

Overheating could also be an issue, is the laptop hot when it starts freaking out?
