### Post by Maheriano on 2009-09-15
Yes you can but as soon as you pick one component, the other components you can choose to fit/work with it are immediately limited. Most boards use built in video which is either nVidia or ATI and don't offer you the option of both. And if you find a board which accepts a video card, you need to find get a case it'll fit in that allows for the insertion of the card.....then if you want bluetooth/Wi-Fi then you have to either find a motherboard with them built in or one with enough space where you can add them as cards. It's not like a desktop where you can keep firing PCI-e cards onto the board, space is limited so that's your biggest challenge.
