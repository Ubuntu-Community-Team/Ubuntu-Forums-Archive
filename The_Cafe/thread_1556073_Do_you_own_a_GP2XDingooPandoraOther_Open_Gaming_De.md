No, it's one of the originals and doesn't have enough power; the newest iteration of the GP2X, the Caanoo *still* doesn't have enough power for N64 from what I've read. CPU isn't good enough.

To be honest the underlying hardware (by which I mean CPU & RAM) hasn't changed much from the first GP2X to the Caanoo; the Caanoo finally got more RAM (128MB vs 64MB) and others things have been changed/added like touchscreens and accelorometers, though.

The OpenPandora project was for the most part started as a result of the shortcomings of the GP2X handhelds, because GPH wasn't really listening to its community. (TBH though, I doubt they have anyone with a firm grasp of English judging by their promotional materials. Maybe they aren't getting many complaints in Korea and there's a language barrier between GPH and its international customers.)
