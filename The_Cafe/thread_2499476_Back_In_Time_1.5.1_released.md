### Post by buhtz on 2024-07-29
Hello,

I am glad to announce the release of version 1.5.1 of [Back In Time]("https://github.com/bit-team/backintime") today.
If everything goes as planed it will hit the upcoming [COLOR=#333333][FONT=&amp]Ubuntu 24.10 "[/FONT][/COLOR][Oracular Oriole]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OracularOriole")" in October this year.

- This release increase the Qt version used from 5 to 6.
- As a new feature SSH proxy (aka jump host) can be used.
- EncFS is marked as deprecated ([#1734]("https://github.com/bit-team/backintime/issues/1734")). Looking for contributions to replace it.

See the [changelog]("https://github.com/bit-team/backintime/blob/dev/CHANGES") and [release section]("https://github.com/bit-team/backintime/releases") for more details.

Several new contributors where involved in this release. Thank you to
all of them, several issues could be fixed and the code quality
increased. Also several new translators and former translators updating
their translations to fit to the latest development. Thank you to them
the [increased to 82 %]("https://translate.codeberg.org/engage/backintime"). And thank you to the users for reporting issues
and giving feedback.
