Probably an orphaned inode, which suggests you may have filesystem corruption. And if you have filesystem corruption, that might explain the crashing and the freezing. Or if there is another reason for the freezing, that might explain the filesystem corruption and the orphaned inode(s). Either way, if you want help with this, you are very welcome to start a thread in a support section. But if you do, please ease up on the negativity. 

For what it's worth, 17.04 has been snappy, stable and reliable for me, but I don't have any orphaned inodes - or diodes for that matter - on any of my systems.
