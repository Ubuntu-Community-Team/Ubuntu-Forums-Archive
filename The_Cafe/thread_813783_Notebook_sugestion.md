### Post by teet on 2008-05-31
I've been looking for new notebooks lately as well.

I noticed that this laptop has a T8300 (2.4 ghz, 3 MB L2 cache) processor.  For me, I would try to get a T9300 (2.5 ghz, 6 MB L2 cache) processor.  I would probably be hard pressed to tell a difference in performance...it's more of a mental thing I guess.  If I'm gonna blow a bunch of money on a laptop, I might as well blow a little more and get the better processor.

Also, I would try to find a laptop with 1 gb of memory...then go to newegg or someplace and pick up 4 gb's and upgrade yourself!

Do a google search for "dell laptop deals".  They're offering 25% off coupons for orders over $999 and 30% off for those ofer $1299.  They were also offering free shipping a while back.  I configured a pretty decked out laptop for ~$800 the other day (shipping and tax included).  Add another $70 or so for RAM and I could have had a really nice laptop for < $900.
