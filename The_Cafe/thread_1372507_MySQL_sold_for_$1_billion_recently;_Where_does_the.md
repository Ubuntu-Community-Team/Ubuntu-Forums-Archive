### Post by koenn on 2010-01-04
> **whiskeylover said:**
> For the brand name?
If this is about Sun buying "mysql" - the actually bought the **company**, MySQL AB, so they bought whatever that company owned at the time :   the MySQL trademark, the copyright to the MySQL server and other components, MySQL AB's customers contracts, the core developers work contracts,  other assets (eg leases or ownership of buildings, hardware, ...)


Where did the money go ? to the ownners (share holders) of MySQL AB, obviously.


So how can *you* make money on open source ?  
Write a decent piece of software, build a company around it, develop your company and your software for about 12 years, make your software an essential part of IT infrastructure such as the WWW - then wait 'till someone offers to buy the company for 1 billion $.
