### Post by Lucradia on 2011-05-25
If you want one of those, it comes down to a few things:

1. Does it need to be in Full HD? (This includes 720p << Note the "p") and 60 fps (1080p/720p/60p)?

2. Does it need to have Low-light capabilities?

3. Does it need a touch screen?

4. How much internal space? Or none at all, and just have the SDHC?

5. As above, don't trust names you don't know. I'd swear on JVC, as they were the first to offer consumer VHS Recorders.

Toshiba and another brand are known for locking their camcorders to only allow transfer of video files via software that they give you with the camcorder.

Unsure? put the model into youtube, usually a review is lying around. It's what I did with the HAF-X case I plan to get.
