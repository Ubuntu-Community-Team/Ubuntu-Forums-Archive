In November 2006, Microsoft and Novell agreed to pursue a collaboration agreement that would make it easier for customers to achieve interoperability in their mixed IT environments. To further ensure interoperability, Microsoft and Novell also agreed to provide customers with patent coverage. The technical collaboration is focused on four areas:

   1. Virtualization
   2. Standards-based Systems Management
   3. Directory and Identity Interoperability
   4. Document Format Compatibility

Dell is excited to take part in this business collaboration. Customers expect technology vendors to put their needs in the forefront, and by partnering with Microsoft and Novell, Dell will simplify customers' IT operations by focusing on three things:

   1. Interoperability workshops
   2. Migration proof of concepts
   3. Migration services
