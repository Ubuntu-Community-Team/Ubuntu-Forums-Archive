### Post by billgoldberg on 2008-09-22
I can suggest bmpx.

I especially like its shoutcast features (thousands of online radios available from within the player).

And it looks pretty good also.

--

I myself use Exaile.

It has an easy normal library (unlike Banshee!). And the playlists are easy to make.

I also don't like Mono so Banshee is a no go for me.

--

If you are looking for something different (and with that much harder to set-up, but easier to use), take a look at this article I wrote.

[http://linuxowns.wordpress.com/2008/07/27/setting-up-mpd-locally/](http://linuxowns.wordpress.com/2008/07/27/setting-up-mpd-locally/)

--

Some people might suggest Audacious. I find it a horrible player. 

If you need something like that, I suggest you find a .deb for xmms.
