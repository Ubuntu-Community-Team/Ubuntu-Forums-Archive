### Post by Bungo Pony on 2008-02-22
Get yourself an old component tape deck at a thrift store ([like the one in this pic]("http://www.saturn-sound.com/images/pioneer%20-%20stereo%20cassette%20tape%20deck%20-%20ctf-7171.jpg")). Connect the microphones to the two microphone jacks (you may need adapters), and the line out on the tape deck to the line in on your sound card. Get a cassette with the record tabs still intact (or cover the holes with tape), put it in the deck, hit record & pause. Adjust the microphone input on the tape deck as necessary.
