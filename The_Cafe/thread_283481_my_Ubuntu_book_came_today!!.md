### Post by Not the man I once was on 2006-10-24
Greetings All,

I've been reading The Official Ubuntu Book for a month and I can honestly say that it's one of the best book bound purchases I've made in some years, and I'm not one for buying books regularly either, I usually have no interest with them but come last month I thought I would purchase it. And I don't regret it. It's clear, it's concise and it's perfect. Every term is well explained, and layed out in a logical and easy-to-use fashion, highlighted with explainatory images where applicable and an excellent source of information for anyones needs.
**taurus** is correct. After you've read the book, you'll be able to help others with ease.

Installation issues? Resort the the book. Kubuntu query? Again, the book is there to aid you. Ubuntu Server update issue? Your book is your best aid. This is the most definitive and easy-to-follow Linux book I have ever read. I demand a sequel!

Purchase this book, it's a must for all!

:mrgreen:
