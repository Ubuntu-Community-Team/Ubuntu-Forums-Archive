### Post by AGNKim on 2010-10-07
I was a recruiter for the Navy. The ASVAB isn't a required test by any federal or state law or mandate, but schools can require students to take it. If they are having you take it, then they (not the military) mandate that.

Also, being a vet, it disheartens me to see someone say they don't want to be in the military because they "hate war". Who doesn't? I like to think it is our military that curbs wars, not produces them.
