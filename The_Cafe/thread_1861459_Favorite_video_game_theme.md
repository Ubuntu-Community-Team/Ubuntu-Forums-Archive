### Post by Quadunit404 on 2011-10-21
Rediscovered this gem recently: [the main theme from the Commodore 64 game Monty on the Run.]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=93nIljXpqUY")

Even though the game is notorious for being extremely hard, it is known for its catchy theme song, which is said to be one of - if not THE best - video game song ever composed. Seriously, it's practically a legend among fans of 8-bit music and fans of regular video game music alike - so much so it got remixed numerous times and even received an [orchestral treatment]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gPYFkXHOulo") from the Commodore 64 Orchestra.
