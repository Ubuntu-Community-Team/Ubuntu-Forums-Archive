### Post by popch on 2008-01-10
On the other hand, I have experienced times when the browser hung while waiting for stuff to arrive from the web. 

You can gain some insight into what is happening by observing FF's status line which shows what it is doing, and by watching the system monitor's display of network traffic.

Some of the times when FF hung, it was waiting for google.analytics, other times for ubuntuforums.org. It is quite possible that the reason for those stalls are bottle necks in the servers which occur more often at certains times of the day as at others.

In some cases, I could 'unstall' Firefox by clicking on the 'stop' button, then on the 'refresh' button.
