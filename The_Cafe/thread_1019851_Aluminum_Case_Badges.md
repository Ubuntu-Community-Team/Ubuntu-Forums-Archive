These have been selling on ebay for at least a couple years.  I have some nice silver onces on my laptop.

Tip:  Never stick anything to the label (like a piece of tape).  The laminated coating that goes over the silver backing will peal off and you'll never get it to stick back on.  You'll end up left with a blank piece of silver label with no text on it.
