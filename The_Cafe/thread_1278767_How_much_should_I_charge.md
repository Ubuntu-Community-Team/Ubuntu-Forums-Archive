### Post by handy on 2009-09-30
Start cheap, increase your rate by a small amount every year.

That way you will gain more customers quicker, you will also gain word of mouth advertising (the best kind) if you are any good. Your customers will also stick with you if you are any good, & as your prices increase slowly you won't be scaring your clients away.

Basically, tempt them with value, prove yourself over time to gain their loyalty; in the process gradually increasing your remuneration.  If you have any kind of contract, include the annual increase in your rates if it is appropriate.
