### Post by Superkoop on 2009-08-14
The thing that you put around a neck is not a good idea at all for people, because people's necks are much weaker than most animals necks. However, a harness type of thing would work; it's not going to hurt the child, and it will keep the child in plain sight.

Sometimes it's tough for parents to carry their children everywhere, because children are heavy, so having them walk along is the best option. Although, little children will be little children, they will wander off because of being distracted. It doesn't matter how well you discipline your children, some children have a personality and character that makes them more inquisitive and more likely to run off. All children are different, and some are naturally more naughty than others; how well behaved a child is is not a fair representation of the quality of discipline they've had. 

So what will a caring parent do? They will do what they have to to keep their child safe. If that means using a harness, to keep them from wandering off in a store, from stepping onto a busy highway, or whatever the situation.

Usually parents just hold their child's hand, however sometimes the parents have to use both of their hands and holding onto another hand is not going to work. So you can simply attach the leash to yourself and the child, and the problem is solved. 
The neck is not a good idea though, it's too dangerous.
