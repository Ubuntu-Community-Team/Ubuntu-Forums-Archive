### Post by linuxforartists on 2011-03-15
I'd spend some time looking up comments on [Web Hosting Talk]("http://www.webhostingtalk.com").  They verify the domains of people who post, which cuts down on spam. Generally the reviews on there are reliable. 

Don't bother doing a Google search for "web hosting reviews." Nearly all of those are spam websites.  

For what it's worth, here's what I discovered after spending time on WHT:

--[GoDaddy]("http://www.godaddy.com/") gets pretty bad reviews for web hosting. They seem to be okay for registering domains.

--[HostGator]("http://www.hostgator.com/") often gets mentioned as a good web host, among the major providers.

--If it matters, I went with [HawkHost]("http://www.hawkhost.com/"). Lots of positive reviews on WHT. They're new, though. So I prefer to recommend HostGator.
