### Post by ayaruane on 2016-03-24
Hi everyone :) I used to post (a while back) as karimabunuh, or karimruan I honestly cant remember. 

I am a huge elder scrolls fan, though I admit to having only played Arena, daggerfall and morrowind. I mucked around with oblivion and skyrim a bit, but felt they really stripped the RPG elements out of them. Anyway, I also looooove text based games: roguelikes, text adventures, muds.... etc.

I have started a project called TES Adventures, a play on The Elder Scrolls and text adventure. But I didn't need to tell you that. I guess I also looooove saying unnecessary things. I am writing it in Python 2.7

I honestly dont have a lot of time, but I would love to get a few people willing to contribute at least some code to the project. You know, to help things move along and to provide a great game.

The goal is to have the whole world of Tamriel explorable with the same open ended, sandbox type of gameplay. All playable without X, that is the goal. I would love to eventually implement a tiled version, similar to the way Gearhead 1 & 2 do it. 

So, if you love Linux (and I guess you wouldn't be here if you didn't) AND the elder scrolls, if you wanna help out the check out the sourceforge link below :)

My sourceforge project can be found here [https://sourceforge.net/projects/tes-adventure-elderscrolls](https://sourceforge.net/projects/tes-adventure-elderscrolls)

### Post by ayaruane on 2016-03-24
Honestly I don't know. Its a completely open source project, and as far as I know isn't anything more than fan fiction, just a bit more technical I guess. There are DiabloRL, DoomRL and AliensRL, none of which have direct, explicit permission to use those brands. Plus, bethesda won't really care. It's free advertising and they honestly won't ever hear about it most likely. Seeing as the above mentioned RLs were made for  7dayRL quite a while ago, it seems like there is no problem. I did a quick search on google and found a lot about fair use and fan made creations being OK. I also couldn't find anything from any US gov source saying that it isn't allowed. I think its a grey area thing. Don't know about outside of the US though.

By the way, DiabloRL is awesome. That is actually how I pictured this project in the beginning, like Elder Scrolls RL. But, I can only make RLs using qbasic lol. Not the most relevant. I also feel like libtcod doesn't have the look I like. It looks too, modern I guess. So I opted to use the text adventure format with ascii graphics for each location.

If you find anything about the copyright issue let me know. I know bethesda owns Diablo and DiabloRL didn't seem to have any issues. As long as it doesn't end up better than what their markets gets i guess lol. IMO roguelikes are better than any game made in today's world.
