### Post by Ebuntor on 2008-08-17
It is indeed an interesting project. I installed it but it is just a nice toy IMO. I can't see this becoming an replacement for drop-down menus. There's too much cursor movement needed before you can select the desired app. Also if a sub-menu is too large the adjoining circle isn't completely visible. The text describing the menu or item is too far away from the circles. 

It's nice eye candy and looks high-tech in a way, reminds me of the [LCARS]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LCARS") computer interfaces from Star Trek. 
However compared with a simple list, like a drop-down menu, and circular menu I'll go with the former.

EDIT: I set the icons too small but the circular menus were still too large to fit completely on screen and they overlapped potions of my panel making the icons hard to make out.
