### Post by Hman242 on 2010-06-13
I meant to post this weeks ago when it happened, but I forgot. During a conversation at my cousin's place the topic of him getting a new computer came up. He said he might get a Mac because of them being virus resistant and it comes with some audio effects software. I told him he shouldn't spend all that money and he could just use Ubuntu. He asked, "What's Ubuntu?".

I told him is was kinda like a Mac, keeping it simple because he isn't the most tech savvy. I also told him it was free and that he could download plenty of free software. That made him want to try it out. I downloaded a the desktop .iso and put it on a USB using UNetBootin and booted it up. He liked it, so I partitioned his hard drive and installed Ubuntu on a small partition. I put Docky on it since I know he liked the look of Mac. After upgrading and restarting I showed him the software center. I told him just about any programs he wanted should be in there. I told him if he had any troubles he could just text me. I haven't got a text from him since I was there.
