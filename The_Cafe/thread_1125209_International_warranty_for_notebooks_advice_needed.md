### Post by Pasdar on 2009-04-14
I advice you to buy a laptop well-known for its good service world-wide. I have an ASUS laptop and it has a 2 year standard warranty (you don't pay for it). If something is wrong with my laptop they basically come and pick it up and a I get a replacement laptop while I'm waiting for mine to get fixed.

If you want to buy a different brand, make sure they have offices/dealers in your country/city.

### Post by kpkeerthi on 2009-04-14
