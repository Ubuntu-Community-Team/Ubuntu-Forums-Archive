### Post by buzzingrobot on 2016-08-28
The e-reader market is small. Other than Kindle and the Nook, there is the Kobo brand. Searching on "e-readers" will turn up reviews, etc.

If you have a phone or tablet and are comfortable reading on it, you might give it a try. 

Also look at the range of books available for a device. No use having a beautiful device if it limits you to inadequate sources.
