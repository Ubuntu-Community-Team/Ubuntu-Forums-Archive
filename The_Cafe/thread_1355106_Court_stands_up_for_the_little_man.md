Have you seen [this]("http://news.slashdot.org/story/09/12/14/1847256/Microsoft-Fined-In-India-For-Using-Money-Power-Against-Pirates?art_pos=1")?  Apparently a judge in India has rapped Microsoft's knuckles over the corporation's attempt to use its financial might to harass defendants.

It would be nice if this caught on in other countries.  In some places, the record industry extorts money out of alleged file sharers because said file sharers can't afford to contest a claim in court.  Unfortunately, I can't see a UK judge doing this.  Judges, like the governments they work for, tend to be very much in love with entertainment industry bigwigs.
