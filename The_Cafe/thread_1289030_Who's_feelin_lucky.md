### Post by Frak on 2009-10-12
> **hoppipolla said:**
> Kind of, but sometimes you don't have an enter button, like on a phone or a Wii or a PSP!

The server does checks to see what browser is being used. The Wii and PSP use much different browsers than say a normal desktop computer would, so when the headers are sent to the server, it knows what contents to serve. Besides that, I think the buttons are being hidden via JavaScript, and therefore, since many mobile devices don't have full (enough) support for JavaScript, the JS is ignored and the buttons stay on the page. It's the same for the fade. The elements are being hidden by JavaScript, but if there's no support for JavaScript, the elements are never hidden in the first place. The browser just ignores it.
