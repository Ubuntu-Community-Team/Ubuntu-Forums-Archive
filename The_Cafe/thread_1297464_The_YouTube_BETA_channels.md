### Post by Squonk07 on 2009-10-21
Yes, yes, a thousand times yes! I'm all for innovation, except when the end result is not as functional as the previous one and is forced on me arbitrarily. When I search through a person's channel, I would like it to default to Grid View. No dice. I have to set that option *each and every time* I view a channel. And if I left-click on any video--poof! Back to Player View (instead of opening in a separate tab). If I click the Back button when I'm done watching, I get shot back to the top of the page again. If a channel has over 400 videos and I wanted to watch through a series near the middle, I have to scroll down again and find it.

The only way around it is to right-click and open in a separate tab. Normally I give new things a chance and I don't have the knee-jerk reaction some people do to change, but the YouTube Beta channels are IMHO a PITA. I wouldn't mind so much if there were ways for me to customize behavior, but there aren't. I can make my OWN channel default to Grid View, but not other peoples'.

Incidentally, anybody notice lingering annotations? (i.e. Though a new video is playing, the annotations from the previous one appear--this only seems to happen in the Player View.)
