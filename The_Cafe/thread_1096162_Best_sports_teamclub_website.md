### Post by Bart_D on 2009-03-14
Sports teams don't play 365 games a year.  "In-between" games, fans often visit their favorite sports team's website to keep themselves up-to-date on club affairs.

Exclusive content to members(in some cases, free) can be quite good. Plus, websites give a behind-the-scenes look at the club activities(training, etc.) between games. I think that most sites contain these two features...to varying degrees though. 

I guess I find them useful when looking for club specific information(especially leading up to big games), beyond what is offered on the usual TV websites(bbc, espn, foxsports, setantasports, skysports, etc.)

Which team has the website(ex: nice flash, links, a lot of interesting content) that you have seen?
