### Post by Healey on 2008-06-19
Hi

I found Sipgate to be good because it offers free geographical numbers and there is a lot of chioce - I live in a small village and was still able to get one nearby

I have used it via Ekiga on feisty not tried it in Hardy

I mainly use it via my VOIP Router where the sipgate settings are held in the router, that way you can plug in a regular phone and use it without the PC being on - So it works like an additional telephone number. Adapters are also available but I have not tried them so dont know much about them.

Please remember though, that VOIP is not 100% reliable there will be occasions when the quality will not be great - BUT having said that there are times when you can not tell the difference between VOIP and a regular phone company

Hope it helps - Good Luck

Healey
