### Post by Lux Perpetua on 2008-09-07
I have polarized sunglasses, and I think the problem is that LCD screens give you polarized light. If I rotate my head just right, then my LCD screen looks completely dark, even if it's turned on and I'm facing the screen directly. If I turn my head 90 degrees away from that position, then it looks fine. From a normal viewing position, it's in between the two extremes: annoyingly dark but visible. (This isn't a problem for me, since I don't use my computer outside, and I don't wear sunglasses inside.)
