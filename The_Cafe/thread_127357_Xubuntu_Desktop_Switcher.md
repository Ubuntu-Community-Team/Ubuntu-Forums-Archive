### Post by 5-HT on 2006-02-09
Briancurtin pegged it down. 

XFCE has two default pagers. 
Seems like your graphical pager  (shows those nice representations of the windows open on each desktop) may have gotten replaced by the desktop switcher (numbered).

You can just delete the desktop switcher, add the graphical pager, and hopefully things will be back to normal.

HTH
