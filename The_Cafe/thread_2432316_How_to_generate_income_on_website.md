### Post by sdsurfer on 2019-12-03
Monetizing web sites alone has been dying a slow, flaming death for years now. But all hope is not lost. Your best bet is cross channel marketing (that is probably not the right name for it.) By that I mean, look where most people spend their time online. Most of it is on social media sites and video sites, the average time on a web site is probably three seconds or less (some research will prove that out.) So if you wish to drive valuable traffic to your site and monetize your services, you need to market on these platforms.

It's a fairly complicated question as to **how** you do that, it depends on your niche. Start following TechLead on YouTube - he is hilarious (in a techie, nerdy way.) He is an ex-Google, ex-Facebook tech lead, and considers both of those jobs "side jobs" because he has made his real money in developing his own apps and mostly monetizing his videos. In one video he shows he is collecting around 30K a month just on YouTube marketing and affiliate programs (and calls it "beer money." :-D ) He has a lot to share if you "get" his presentation. :-D
