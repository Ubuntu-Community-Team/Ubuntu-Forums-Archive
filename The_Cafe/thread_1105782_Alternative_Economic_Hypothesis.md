### Post by ZarathustraDK on 2009-03-25
We sort of have that kind of system in Denmark.

The challenge is to adjust the model just right so that you keep the rich (the ones who pay a lot of tax) from fleeing the country due to the higher tax-pressure, while maintaining an acceptable level of welfare.

It's a neverending seesaw where people constantly argue about give'n'take from the rich/poor, which is by no means bad; some people just get tired of it and wants a "one size fits all"-solution to stuff (and then mistakenly assume "if it's good for me, then it must be right").
