### Post by calvinpriest on 2007-03-20
Just thought I'd post this for folks who are currently bemoaning the lack of music sites (I'm not getting a kickback or anything)...

There is a great music download site called [www.allofmp3.com](www.allofmp3.com).  It has a very complete catalog -- seems to be comparable to amazon.com in terms of music availability.  And it's very cheap, about $2.50 for a complete album.  My understanding is that it's cheaper because it's based in Russia where they have different laws regarding music re-sale.

You can even choose your mp3 quality.
