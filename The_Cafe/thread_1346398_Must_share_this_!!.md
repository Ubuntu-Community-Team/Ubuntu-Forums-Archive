### Post by sigurnjak on 2009-12-05
Every once in a while i come across folks expressing their frustration with scrolling in Linux Firefox . 
Well , finally i have come across final solution , until FF does something about it :
 [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5846](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5846)

It just amazing how much difference it makes . Here a also some setting i copied from one user :


Here are my settings for perfect scrolling:

BROWN
(slow, for reading long text)

Stride Size of Scroll: 70
Smoothness - Start of Move: 77.0
Smoothness: 77.0
Acceleration Sensitivity: 100


GREEN
(medium, for everyday use)

Stride Size of Scroll: 125
Smoothness - Start of Move: 20.0
Smoothness: 80.0
Acceleration Sensitivity: 200


BLUE
(fast, for scanning through long text)

Stride Size of Scroll: 250
Smoothness - Start of Move: 50.0
Smoothness: 70.0
Acceleration Sensitivity: 350

Enjoy!
