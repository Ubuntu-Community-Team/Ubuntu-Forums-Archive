> **stmartin said:**
> Please tell me how to analyze this log? What means what?
The number under "total" is the total amount of physical memory in the computer that the operating system can use (in my case 3961 MB). "used" is how much physical memory is in use, and "free" is how much physical memory is free. "shared" means nothing (according to the man page of "free" it is obsolete). "buffers" and "cached" indicate memory used by the Linux kernel, for disk buffers etc. "swap" is swap space on disk.
