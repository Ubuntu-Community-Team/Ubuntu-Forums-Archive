### Post by Bou on 2007-04-19
Well now that Feisty is out I began thinking about the great new things it has, and the great things it could or should have, but it doesn't.

First thing that came to my mind is [Mathusalem]("http://tw.apinc.org/weblog/2006/08/22"). It was such a great idea, having all the information about ongoing processes together in the notification area where they belong, out of the way. So nifty, so elegant.

[IMG]http://pollycoke.files.wordpress.com/2006/07/taskman-mockup.png[/IMG]

Then I realised that there haven't been any news on Mathusalem for half a year, and started wondering if it will ever come true. It makes me kinda sad to see such good projects go to waste, but I guess those things just happen. So, what was the app you were excited about but never saw the light, or never was as great as it could have been?
