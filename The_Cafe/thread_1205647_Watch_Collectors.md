### Post by ohbuntu on 2009-07-06
I'm an amateur horologist and have 3 good watches in my collection (Omega SMP, Sinn U2 and a TAG Carrera) along with a Movado Museum and a couple of Seikos and G-shocks amongst others. These days I'm sporting the Sinn. Like most cheap, poor and penniless collectors, I can only dream about owning a Rolex right now. 

Keghead, are you a member over on the WatchUSeek forums? There are tons of like-minded horologists on there and with the hundreds of daily topics and posts, there's a lot of opportunity to learn about watches from lesser known manufacturers that you might not have considered in the first place. I barely knew the difference between two different movements until a couple of years ago but can gladly say that I know now, thanks to such forums.
