### Post by Patrick K. on 2006-11-19
I'm hooked on new keyboard feeling. You know how you love the new car smell? For me it's the sound and feel of a brand new light weight keyboard. The smaller, the better.

I just bought a brand new keyboard that has the most softest keys you ever touched. In fact they don't even make any noise.

My new keyboard is a line of thick plastic with little rubber things the size of dimes for each key between the rubber and a plastic deal that has a little wire in it. You can see all the way through the keyboard. It's no thicker then a half dollar and bends like a leaf. It even glows green, red, white, or blue by the touch of a button.

No more clacking, just thumping of my fingers as if I was trying to type on nothing.
