### Post by murderslastcrow on 2010-07-18
Maybe you could be smoking one of those Blu electronic cigarettes? XD

Nah, I personally wouldn't find it offensive, but that brings us to a new question. If what you're showing us isn't explicitly offensive, or too much for the code of conduct, then showing it is making it more likely for someone else to understand or show it somewhere else in the future, and make it even less questionable.

So, do you want cigarettes to become more acceptable or less acceptable in forum images? That's really where you'd be going with this since it doesn't seem to be something that would get you banned, and I'm sure you'd receive a warning at least if it were inappropriate.

Of course, this doesn't mean you're encouraging smoking, and there's also the possibility of creating an avatar that includes a man smoking without a neutral perspective on smoking, but a negative one that would reduce its popularity (like a skull with a fedora smoking a cigarette).

But yeah, if you wanted to draw a picture of you smoking a cigarette, I wouldn't assume you want to encourage smoking, since that's just plain stupid. The very small opinion I'm giving is that it might make it more likely for someone else to post a similar picture on the forum, which would not inherently promote smoking, and if it did probably wouldn't have much of an impact.
