### Post by BrokenKingpin on 2011-10-24
I have found a solution that works for me. Kontact/KDE-PIM with Akonadi was just too much hassle, so I switch to Thunderbird.

I switched to using Thunderbird with the following plugins:
- Lighting --> for calendar
- Google data provider --> for syncing calendar to gmail account
- Google Contacts --> for syncing contacts to gmail account

This syncs my Thunderbird data with gmail, so all my machines will have the same information.

For syncing notes and other data I am just going to use SpiderOak (similar to Dropbox).
