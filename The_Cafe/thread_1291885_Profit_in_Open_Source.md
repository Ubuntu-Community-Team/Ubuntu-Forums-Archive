### Post by benj1 on 2009-10-15
well there a few one man shops managing to scrape a living, redhat, sun etc

you generally wont be able to make money just from software sales, although there isn't any reason why you can't.

most businesses are based on support for an open source app, either technical support or through being commissioned to add features someone in particular needs, if you are the author of the software you are in a prime position to take advantage of these markets.
i suppose you could include companies that use open source as a USP so you could include computer retailers such as zareason or system 76 or websites such as google.

then there are those companies that use linux to cut costs or for competitive advantage but dont necessarily contribute back ie amazon

so to answer your question, yes
