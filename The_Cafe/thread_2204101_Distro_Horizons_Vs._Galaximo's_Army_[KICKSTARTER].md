### Post by Kitatus_Studios on 2014-02-06
Hey guys 'n galls,
My team and I are currently working on a collectathon game much like the classic N64 platformers. We've been accepted on Desura and are in talks with GoG, but we need as much help as possible getting the Kickstarter the attention it needs to help us get funding to create the game for Linux, PC, Mac, Ouya and Wii U.

We've got a Linux demo over on the Kickstarter if you want to try out the project yourself (It's in a very early alpha state at this moment in time)!

**Here is a little information file I've come up with so you guys 'n galls can have an insight as to what Distro Horizons is:**


[Distro Horizons Vs. Galaximo's Army]("http://kck.st/1i5jonC") is a 3D Collecathon platformer, designed as a love-letter for fans of classic N64 platformers such as Conker's Bad Fur Day, Banjo Kazooie and Super Mario 64. 


[IMG]http://gameclassy.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2014/02/1280-x-720-1024x576.jpg[/IMG]


Planned for release in the Summer, We're creating Distro Horizons for Linux, PC, Mac, Ouya and Wii U!


[IMG]https://s3.amazonaws.com/ksr/assets/001/542/101/b3a02d786247a8a47352a055743dad1f_large.jpg?1390164584[/IMG]


Taking place over 9 different dimensions, Distro Horizons will have you travelling to many bizarre and unique dimensions in your quest to put a stop to Galaximo's evil schemes! You'll be traveling to jungle dimensions, haunted dimensions, video-game dimensions and many, many more!


[IMG]http://media.indiedb.com/cache/images/games/1/30/29083/thumb_620x2000/vlc_2014-02-04_20-11-15-83.png[/IMG]


We've just been accepted on Desura and are currently in talks with Nintendo & GoG. We're actievely developing for Ouya too, but we need your help! Distro won't be able to live without your help, so be sure to check out our Kickstarter and help give Distro a heart-beat!


[IMG]http://media.indiedb.com/cache/images/games/1/30/29083/thumb_620x2000/vlc_2014-02-04_20-30-58-93.png[/IMG]


So I hope you can check-out the Kickstarter, and hopefully you guys 'n galls are looking forward to Distro as much as we are.
