### Post by roderikk on 2007-09-07
I just read this press release from the dutch consumer organisation (consumentenbond) [in dutch]:

[http://www.consumentenbond.nl/actueel/persberichten/Persberichten_Overzicht_2007/153470](http://www.consumentenbond.nl/actueel/persberichten/Persberichten_Overzicht_2007/153470)

It talks about how 59% of respondents have one or more complaints on windows Vista. The best part however is that it ends such:

> Bovendien adviseert de bond consumenten om ook eens te kijken naar alternatieven voor Windows zoals Mac OS X van Apple en het gratis Linux Ubuntu.

Which means something like: Besides this the Dutch consumer organisation advises consumers to also take a look at the alternatives for Windows, such as Mac OS X from Apple and the free Linux Ubuntu.

This was also featured on some Dutch news sites!
