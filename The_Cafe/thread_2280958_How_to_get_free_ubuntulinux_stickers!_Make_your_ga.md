### Post by t0p on 2015-06-03
Assuming you have a printer (or access to one): double-sided sticky tape is your savior! Do a search (I use Google - other search services are available duh!) for "ubuntu stickers" or "linux stickers" or even "cute penguins"!  You're bound to find *something* that'll suit ya!  Print out the image(s) you like.  Then use double-sided sticky tape to affix the pic to your laptop/gadget/whatever.  Ta-dah!  You have a sticker that shows the world you lurve Ubuntu/Linux/penguins, that will cost very little to create (yeah, the tape isn't free, the ink/printing isn't free, but it's near as dammit free so quite moaning and decorate your laptop!  My laptop currently wears a variety of Discordian stickers, but I've got together a bunch of Linux pix that will declare to the world that I'm a "freetard" Linux Ubuntu fan!  There are so many suitable images out there waiting for a make-over: I've attached a few, but if you exercise your fingers and do your own search you're bound to find a pic that says you love Ubuntu and you're proud of said love).

Do it peeps! Make your laptop easily as must-have as Gollum's precious!  Yeahhh!

t0p

One little tip; doesn't matter whether you print with plain paper or photopaper.....to make them waterproof, splashproof, etc,, after you've attached them to the double-sided tape, put a layer of single-sided clear tape on top. Then, stick 'em where you want 'em.

Makes them a wee bit more hardwearing!
