Since nowone has yet commented heres a recipe I'm thinking of doing for a stout:
 
3.5kg Pale Malt (Maris Otter)
.5 kg Chocolate Malt
.5 kg Crystal (caramel) Malt 
.5 kg Flaked oats
 
Bittering I was going to use a mix of Fuggles and Challanger with a late Fuggles to get about 100 EBC, and a touch of Irish moss for the last 15mins of the boil.
 
Yeast wise I was thinking of using my favorite Muntons Premium Gold as I always tend to get good results from that ;)
