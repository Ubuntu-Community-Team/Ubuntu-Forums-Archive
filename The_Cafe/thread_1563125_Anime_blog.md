### Post by FlameReaper on 2010-08-29
Hrm... Let's see.

1. The banner's design, layout and its composition on your blog's layout could've been a bit better. If you need someone to design a better one, you can ask someone to help ;)

2. I think you can make do with a minimalist background rather than the blurred landscape there. Like a blank patterned background... or possibly total white. So that people can be focused on reading what's your content all about.

3. No problems with pictures of the anime-related stuff (like putting posters up), but being an anime blog it'd help if you have some screenshots as well and make a comment of it in the caption (exercise your best witty lines whenever possible! XD). No need to put so much, just a few will do so that people can get an idea what's up in the series.

4. It'd be best if you don't put the flash fish at the bottom :\ For most readers it is a pain they have to wait your page to load just because of the flash down there...

I guess that's that for now...
