### Post by TheHimself on 2012-04-04
Not very long a go I found an app for  my Galaxy cellphone that enables it to take HDR photos by taking two consecutive photos and superposing them; something that my Canon powershot can not do. Then I realized how good an "open source camera" can be. In fact digital camera companies, similar to Microsoft, take good advantage of making their cameras' internal software unmodifiable. 

Searching the internet I found Stanford's 
[OpenCamera]("http://graphics.stanford.edu/projects/camera-2.0/"). It's still in infancy but looks promising.
