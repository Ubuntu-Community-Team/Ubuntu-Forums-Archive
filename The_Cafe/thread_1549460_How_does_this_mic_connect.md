### Post by Wee_Guy on 2010-08-09
To record with that mic you'd need an audio interface/sound card with at least 1 XLR input. You should also be aware that cheap sound cards might not sound as good as more expensive ones due to the quality of the analogue to digital converter inside them, so be careful you don't end up with something that doesn't sound very good.
