Look into SIL Open Font License.

To add:
If it were me doing it, I'd look into contacting the Debian font team,
[https://wiki.debian.org/Fonts/PackagingPolicy]("https://wiki.debian.org/Fonts/PackagingPolicy")
as if you get it into Debian, it'll get pulled into Ubuntu and then all subsequent distributions.
And then maybe other non-Debian based distributions might also look at including it.
