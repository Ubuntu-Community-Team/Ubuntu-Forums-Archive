### Post by whiskeylover on 2010-01-11
Go ahead and write "Statement of Purpose" at the top. They wont deduct any points if you do.

Your name should be enough at the bottom. 

There isn't a fixed format for writing SoPs. They're not like formal business letters. An SoP is supposed to tell them a bit about your background, and what you'd like to do if they accepted you... etc.

Here are some samples. 

[http://www.statementofpurpose.com/essay_samples.html](http://www.statementofpurpose.com/essay_samples.html)
