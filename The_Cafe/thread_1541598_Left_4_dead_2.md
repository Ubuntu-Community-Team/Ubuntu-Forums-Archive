### Post by mengfei on 2011-04-04
That's simple. First off, you don't have a Pentium D, it's a Pentium Dual-Core. Big difference.

Your integrated Intel graphics chipset offloads some of its work to the CPU, and in your case, your Pentium Dual-Core isn't as powerful as your friend's CPU, so performance suffers.
