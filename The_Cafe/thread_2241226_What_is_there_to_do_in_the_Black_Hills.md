### Post by MoebusNet on 2014-08-25
Nighttime:
Deadwood, SD - Old Wild West town, casinos, saloons, etc.

Hot Springs, SD - Spa baths, restaurants, movie theatre.

Rapid City, SD - big city type entertainments: shopping malls, upscale restaurants, occasional concerts & plays.

Daytime:
Sturgis, SD - Harley-Davidson museum

Rapid City, SD - Air Force museum
