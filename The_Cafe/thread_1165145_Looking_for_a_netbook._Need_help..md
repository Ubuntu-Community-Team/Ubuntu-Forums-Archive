### Post by Seishuku on 2009-05-20
My AA1 is pretty awesome--it runs better than my antiquated desktop.  Hopefully you don't plan to use it for gaming, at least not with Ubuntu.  There's an issue with the BIOS in these computers, it seems to give out after a few months...?  It happened to my mom's and my netbook, but it was easily remedied by a BIOS recovery that's easily found on the intrawebz.

Anyhow, the keyboard on these is the best.  It's bigger than in the other models, 80% of a normal keyboard.

Edit:  If you plan to only run Ubuntu on it, the linux versions of netbooks go for way cheaper.

### Post by ssam on 2009-05-20
just be careful not to get one with an intel GMA500 graphics. it's not actually designed by intel, so the specs are not open. this means there is no open source driver. for the foreseeable future it will be a big pain to use anything other than the preinstalled distros on netbooks with a GMA500.

that page does not seem to say what graphics it uses, so you will need to do more research.
