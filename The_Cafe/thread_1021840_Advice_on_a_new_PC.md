AMD processors work just as well as Intel ones. Intel does give better performance & lower power consumption/heat at the moment. Go for Intel right now if you are not on a tight budget.

For GFX cards stick with nVidia for now as their drivers are just great. AMD is getting better but I would not buy an ATI card right now as their drivers are still not on par with nvidia right now.
