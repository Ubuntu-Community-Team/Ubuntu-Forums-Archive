### Post by p_quarles on 2007-10-21
> **Ireclan said:**
> Ever heard of MESSENGER Pidgins?
No need for the sarcasm.

Obviously it's a pun on the idea of a messenger pigeon (note the spelling), but it also refers to "pidgin" language (again, note the spelling) which is a kind of regional or cultural dialect based upon another language. For instance, Jamaican English is often referred to as "Pidgin" English.

The joke is that IMers themselves have developed a fairly extensive pidgin language -- lol, ur, iirc, and so forth.
