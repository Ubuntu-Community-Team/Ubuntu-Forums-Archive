### Post by monsterstack on 2009-05-28
I like the Replace Text-type scripts, such as [this one]("http://userscripts.org/scripts/review/41369") [userscripts.org]. You can set it to replace text on specific sites, too. You can do things like translate web shorthands into English:

```
'im*o' : 'in my opinion',
'wtf' : 'what the ****',
'lmao' : 'That is funny',
'lol' : 'I am done typing my sentence now.',
'+1' : 'I agree with that.',

```

Or anything else your heart desires.
