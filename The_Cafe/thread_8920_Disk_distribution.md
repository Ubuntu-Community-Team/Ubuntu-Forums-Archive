I just received my 10 disks that I ordered.  I announced on the "LInux in Libraries" list that I am on that I had them for anyone that wanted them.  I now have one left and have ordered 50 more.  I really like the concept behind the Ubuntu distribution.  Other distributions seem to be driven by people who know Linux laready for people who know Linux already. Ubuntu seems to be much more about getting it out there to all including the curious with little to no linux experience.  

I just installed it on a very old 380XD IBM ThinkPad that was my first laptop.  It runs great and is fast compared to Windows 95 that was on the machine inititally.
