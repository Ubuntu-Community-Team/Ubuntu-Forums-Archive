### Post by bonzodog on 2007-03-19
Some of you might already be aware of this project, but, I feel now is the time to remind people of it's presence.

**What we do:**
We basically fetch how-to's and tips from the [Tips and Tutorials Section]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=100") of the forums, and wikify them. 90% of the information on the wiki is from that section of the forums.

In recent times, however, attention to the UDSF has slipped, and we need dedicated people who can spare 1 or 2 hours a week of their spare time to help us keep it up-to-date and maintain it.

I am one of the UDSF's key admins, and we have a couple of other people on here who help us out. 
We are after people who really feel that they can spare sometime on a regular basis to help us maintain it, and not people who might do one item then lose interest.
If you are genuinely interested in helping out, please message me or [Frodon]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=23970").
Please do not ask why there are two ubuntu wikis - this issue has been discussed in depth many, many times before, and does not need discussing any further.

The Wiki can be found at:
[http://doc.gwos.org](http://doc.gwos.org)
