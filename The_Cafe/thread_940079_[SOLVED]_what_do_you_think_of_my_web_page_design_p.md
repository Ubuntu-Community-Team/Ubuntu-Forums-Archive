### Post by ju2wheels on 2008-10-06
You should really learn how to use a color palette, there is way too much orange. Install Agave, it can help you with this.

Secondly, the layout could use some more work and thought. There is alot of "white space" or space in general where there should not be any. For example, use the layout to control the placement of images and text such that most of the empty space forms borders on the left and right. Also, for some pages your menu has buttons that disappear.

Last, your banner looks like it was made in MS Paint. With Linux having GIMP, there should be no reason to have artifacts and halos or pixelation in your images, unless you are inexperienced at using it of course.

Overall, I think it looks like a website that belongs in the 90's.
