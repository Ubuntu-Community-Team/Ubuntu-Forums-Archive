### Post by Vadi on 2008-09-19
I game across a google ad for this site & program: [http://simpleuninstaller.com](http://simpleuninstaller.com)

What stuck me was that it offered to uninstall Ubuntu. Odd. I doubt it would do the partitioning and everything, it probably only uninstalls wubi.

But for Ubuntu to be actually listed? I wondered if it was such a popular item... so I did some research.

Going to the Google Keyword Tool ([click]("https://adwords.google.com/select/KeywordToolExternal")), I searched for "ubuntu". The word "uninstall" didn't even come up in the list that practically covers everything. Same for vice-versa.

Very strange I thought, and checked out Google Trends. But that doesn't show much confirmation either ([click]("http://www.google.com/trends?q=uninstall+ubuntu%2C+install+ubuntu&ctab=0&geo=all&date=all&sort=0")).

So, oddly enough (and probably happily), apparently uninstalling Ubuntu isn't such a popular item... I suspect they just used a bunch of keywords of the most popular programs for SEO optimization.

Just a heads up to anyone who runs into this ;)
