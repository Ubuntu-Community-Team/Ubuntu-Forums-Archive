### Post by Giant Speck on 2008-10-24
In December 2001, when I was in the eighth grade, someone broke into our middle school and set the auditorium on fire.  The fire completely destroyed the interior of the auditorium and caused smoke damage to the entire building.

There weren't any buildings large enough to accommodate all 500 or so students, and the new middle school was still under construction at the time of the fire. So the school board split us up by grade level and sent each grade level to the educational buildings of three separate churches in town.  We studied there for about a month before they had cleaned enough of the smoke damage to allow us back in the school.

The auditorium was completely unusable, and most of the school still smelled burnt.

They finally completed the new middle school the following autumn, and soon after, they demolished the old middle school building.
