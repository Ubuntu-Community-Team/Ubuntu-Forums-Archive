Don`t know what they use in Britten but here in Britain we use ponds and stones for weighing ourselves but kilograms for everything else.

We do distance/speed in miles/mph, but do temperature in centigrade.

We measure our height  in feet and inches, but everything else (except mountains and altitude) in metres.

We drink water in litres and beer in pints.
