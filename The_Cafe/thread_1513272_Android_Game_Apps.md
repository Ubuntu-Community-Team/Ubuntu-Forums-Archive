### Post by HappinessNow on 2010-06-19
What are your currently installed/favorite Android Game Apps?

here are my 'five star rated' games:

[LIST]
[*]**tairu** - by Synthetic Reality - "if tetris and rubik's cube had a baby it would be tairu"
[*]**Enjoy Sudoku** - by Jason Linhart - "If you really enjo Sudoku this is the app for you" has Kangi and Hanzi digits also!
[*]**Smiley Pops** - by Boolba Labs LLC - a pop the smileys game; insanely addictive with awesome music.
[*]**WordWrench Full** - by snctinSoftware - "6 letter anagram game" 
[*]**Jewellust** - by Smartpix Games - puzzle gem game
[*]**Super Froggy** - by spwebgames.com - think Frogger
[*]**Develry Huntress** - by Smartpix Games - think Jewellust Halloween style
[*]**Solitaire MegaPack** - by Tesseract Mobile - 90+ great games to play
[*]**Doodle Jump** - by GameHouse - tilt action jumping game
[*]**Radiant** - by Hexage.net - "arcade/action space shooter"
[*]**Tetronimo** - by Mahoney - The best Tetris-style game I have found yet for Android
[*]**Fish Food** - by Tom Moor - fast action fish eat fish game
[*]**Super Tumble** - by Camel Games - Steam Punk-like Physics Game
[*]**WordPops** - by Larva Labs Ltd. - "cross between Tetris and Scrabble. Spell words with your fingers to stop bubbles from filling the screen"
[/LIST]
