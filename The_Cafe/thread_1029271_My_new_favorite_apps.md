### Post by agim on 2009-01-03
I have just started using [Songbird]("http://getsongbird.com/"), a music player that recently went 1.0. It is a web browser, gives me recommendations from last.fm on similar artists, shows me media content (like youtube videos) from whatever artist I am listening to, and lets me know if that artist is playing a concert in my area any time soon. And its open source!

Also, I am using google desktop, which unfortunately, is not open source. Its similar to beagle, it catalogs my desktop and emails, and has a hotkey to run searches either through my desktop or over the web. It is very clean and efficient. So far, I am a fan.

Anyway, just thought I would pass these along... Def check out songbird!
