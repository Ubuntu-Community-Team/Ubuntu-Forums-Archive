Just wanted to extend an invitation to [SlaughterInc.com]("http://www.slaughterinc.com").  This my website and I have just developed it using all open source software.  Right now I have opened the site up in BETA version and would love it if the Ubuntu Community would come and test it out.  Here's a few features that we offer:

- Create your own Blog.
- Access to wide variety of Image Galleries that you can add images to.
- Forum Access (currently not up and running but soon it will be ).
- Upload videos, images, and audio files.
- Post videos from other sites such as Google and Youtube.  And share your thoughts.
- Customizable Profiles.
- Send Messages to other users.
- Add friends to your buddylist.
- Login using your OpenID.
- Submit content found here to sites like Digg, reddit, Facebook, del.icio.us, StubleUpon, Windows Live, and more.


We hope to have more features up and running and any bugs that we may have.
