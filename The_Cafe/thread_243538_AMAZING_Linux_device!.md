BTW, it has Envy24/ICE1712 audio chip.

PCI based Envy24/ICE1712 soundcards are at least the following:
M-Audio Delta 1010, M-Audio Delta DiO 2496, M-Audio Delta 66, M-Audio Delta 44. These are professional sound cards and have 100% Linux support.
