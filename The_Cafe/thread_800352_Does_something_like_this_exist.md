### Post by FyreBrand on 2008-05-19
You could have a Google or Windows Live shared calendar and allow each other to update that.  You can view them with a web browser on your pda/laptop/desktop.  There are other more integrated solutions for enterprise applications such as Outlook connected to an Exchange server or Novell.  Outlook will also import snapshots of Google or Windows Live calendars, but they aren't real time and I find it really clunky.  Shared calendars are easy and work.
