While I was hanging out with some friends last night, I noticed that the guy whose apartment we crashed had his music running through the average WinXP / IE6 / Winamp system, with Kazaa as the main "music store" :)

- Windows XP
- Mac OS X
- Linux source
- Ubuntu/Debian

This little brownie we love to call our OS is really becoming a force to be reckoned with in the FOSS world, it seems :KS
