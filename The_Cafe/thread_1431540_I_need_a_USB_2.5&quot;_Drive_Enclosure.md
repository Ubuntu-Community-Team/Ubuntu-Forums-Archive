### Post by tgalati4 on 2010-03-16
I had a maddog usb enclosure that had trouble with a WD320 gb drive.  I had to power it externally to get it run properly.  This was with my t43p.  I would guess that your t41 will have similar experience.  Some enclosures have a dual usb cable, one grabs power, one is for data.  That gives you 1 amp at 5 VDC, which is still marginal for spinning up drives.

Of course, I bought it from radio shack on clearance for $7.

If you have trouble with something from Best Buy you can always return it.

Antec is better known for their power supplies.  So if you can't borrow one from your local linux user's group, then go with that one.
