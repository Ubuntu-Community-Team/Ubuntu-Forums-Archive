### Post by alindgr1 on 2010-04-23
My favorite website of all of them to get space pictures is Astronomy Picture of the Day ([http://apod.nasa.gov/apod/](http://apod.nasa.gov/apod/)).  

As the title indicates, the picture changes daily, and I have never seen any watermarks.  It has been going steady since June of 1996, I believe, and has been my home page since about 1997 or 1998.  They have several sections on the site where you look for pictures of a certain type of space object, and go through whole site by day, etc.
