### Post by pelle.k on 2010-09-24
From what i can gather, swedish patent laws says "programs for computers shall not be regarded as inventions", which in turn is based on the european EPC patent convention, that say pretty much the same thing. So, in principle, no.
However, a good lawyer could probably turn that around in the court of law, given enough time and resources?
