### Post by mridkash on 2008-04-09
The email sender can know that you opened the image, because the image is downloaded from the sender's server, unlike the email text, which is downloaded from your email server.
Spammers can use this information to confirm your email address.
I'm not 100% sure about this, but I think this is why an email client blocks only 'remote' images, and not the ones coming from email server.
