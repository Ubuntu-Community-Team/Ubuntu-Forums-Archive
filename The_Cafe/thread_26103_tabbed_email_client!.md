so my request is: can you recommend me an email client that works with tabs the way i mentioned above? i know that Balsa has tabs, but it uses them just to manage different mailboxes, not individual messages and composition windows. i know that i can use Opera for Linux, but i'm sure many of you know it's a total nightmare under Gnome, and i fancy a change as well ;)
