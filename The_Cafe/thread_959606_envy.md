### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-10-26
Hardware Drivers only uses whatever drivers were in the repos at the time of that release.  Envy uses the very latest drivers from nvidia and ati.  Alberto has recently worked with the Ubuntu devs to make envy more compatible so that you can do distribution upgrades without crashing X.

So, yes.  Envy is still a viable choice.
