### Post by sh4rkbyt3 on 2010-02-07
Most of the 5 hr energy drinks as was stated, are nothing more than an overabundance of B vitamins (B6 & B12 usually). Since we quickly burn through B vitamins and store almost none it is a viable alternative for a quick pep with little to no real consequences if used sparingly.  For the person who claimed to have kidney pain I would seriously question the validity of the pain. B vitamins are extremely water soluble and are quickly burned up and evacuated with almost no side effects. Flushing of the skin and a warm sensation are normal (temporary) side effects but painful kidneys? All in all, the 5 hour energy drinks are in fact safer and more healthier than the sugar bomb drinks like Amp, Monster and a few others. Still, I would only use them in emergency situations and I prefer to use a sub-lingual B drops that I buy at K-Mart pharmacy. Even though that 5 hour bottle looks small it doesn't mean you have to drink it all at one time. Use it sparingly if you use it at all.  Better yet eat a normal diet and get plenty of rest (if possible) and avoid using any of them at all.
