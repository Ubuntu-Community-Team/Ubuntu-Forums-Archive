### Post by forrestcupp on 2009-12-30
> **Psumi said:**
> Any idea on how to turn on the system to use 480p?

I have up to 1080i (720p) on my TV =/ Is it easy to change the progressive setting?

Go into your Wii settings and go to Screen->TV resolutions.  If you don't have the optional component cables, you can only choose 480i.  If you have them hooked up, you'll be able to switch it.

Edit:
You get to your settings by clicking the circle that says Wii in the bottom left of the screen, then click the Wii Settings button.
