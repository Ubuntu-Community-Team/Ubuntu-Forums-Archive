### Post by Untitled_No4 on 2010-09-05
KDE 4.5 looks very nice and has some nice improvements. I really like the new Notification Area or the new System Tray that doesn't expand. Unfortunately, I have two issues with 4.5:
1. The Blur effects. Way too resource hungry. Yes, I can turn it off but then some widgets are almost completely transparent which makes it hard to make out what is in the foreground and what is in the background.
2. KDE 4.5 introduces a new feature for people with dual monitors: once you disconnect a monitor all the panels that were on that monitor migrate to the still-existing monitor. Previously you just wouldn't see those panels until the second monitor was reconnected. Unfortunately, now panels cover each other and don't migrate back. Don't know, the bug report says it was a popular feature request, but why would anyone want panels to cover each other is beyond me.

I can live with #1 but don't want to live with #2, so until they fix this feature (if ever), I'm staying with 4.4.5.
