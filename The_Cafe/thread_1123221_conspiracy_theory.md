### Post by alternatealias on 2009-04-13
> **chris200x9 said:**
> a new one will come anyday now oh yea I heard that before duke nukem forever anyone?

The difference between Moonlight and Duke Nukem Forever is that Moonlight is Free Software developed in public. So if you cared enough to look, you would see that Moonlight is under heavy development to reach a 2.0 working version.

Also, as someone else pointed out, your "theory" would only work if Silverlight was the dominant player in the 2D/MediaPlayer space on the web. But it's not. No doubt Microsoft would love it to be, but it's not and likely won't be anytime soon (if ever).

The Flash programming model targets artists/designers (e.g. people who are not first-and-foremost programmers) while Silverlight targets programmers.

The point is that each of these plugins targets a different set of developers. This means that some places are going to prefer Flash because they are mostly artists/designers w/o that aren't comfortable with the Silverlight programming model, and you'll have other places that are more comfortable with the Silverlight than Flash.

Just like there will always be people who prefer an SUV over a sedan and vice versa.
