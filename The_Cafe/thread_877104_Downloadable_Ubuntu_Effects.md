### Post by eragon100 on 2008-08-01
Yes, go to add/remove and install"Advanced Desktop Effects settings".

Then, go to system -> preferences CompizConfig settings manager.

There you can enable / disable all kind of effects:

Drawing fire on the screen, making it rain, using desktop cube, have your windows folded into an airplane and fly away when you close them, etc ...
