### Post by TheFu on 2020-02-03
[https://www.meetup.com/ALE-Atlanta-Linux-Enthusiasts/](https://www.meetup.com/ALE-Atlanta-Linux-Enthusiasts/) is the ALE meetup page.  Check the calendar/Events.  Very active. I attend the NW meetup many weeks. That's Cobb near I-75 (east of it) on Windy Hill. That sub-group is more Ubuntu slanted than the others, but all flavors are welcome.

About 50% of the people who show up do not register through meetup - only 5 showed up yesterday, but it was beautiful outside and Superbowl. 
Sign up for the ALE email lists here: [https://ale.org/?page_id=5](https://ale.org/?page_id=5)  Usually less than 5 messages a day.  Linux sorta just works at this point, so questions are non-trivial.  ALE has been around since the 1990s; over 1400 members now, but only about 50 don't just lurk.
