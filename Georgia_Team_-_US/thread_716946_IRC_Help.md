### Post by JReagan1990 on 2008-03-06
Danielle,

To get into IRC, click on your "Applications" menu, and go down to the "Internet" menu.  Next, click on the Pidgin Instant Manager Application.

If you have never used Pidgin before, it will ask you for a new account.  Click the "New Account" button, and select IRC from the drop-down chat types.  Then, add a username, which can include numbers, caps, and small letters, but it can not include spaces.  Do not worry about adding a password.

You will see a window open that says you are connected to the freenode network.  Select "Join Chat" from the buddy list window, under the "Buddy List" menu.  In the "Channel" box, type "#ubuntu-us-ga" without the quotes.  Do not enter a password.  Click "Connect" and you should be in!

If this does not work, feel free to either post back here or to the teams mailing list (you will need to register first).

Jon

### Post by JReagan1990 on 2008-03-10
You can access the IRC protocol via Gaim.  Pidgin is a new version of Gaim, they just changed the name.

For the protocol in a new account, select IRC from the drop-down list.

Next, add a username.  You do not need a password. 

For your alias, you do not need to put anything there if I remember correctly, but you can just put your IRC screename in that box.

That should do it, and you should be able to connect via the "Buddy" list in the "Buddies Window" by selecting the "Join chat" menu option.
