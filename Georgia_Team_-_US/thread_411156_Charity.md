### Post by sciurus on 2008-01-11
[Free IT Athens]("http://freeitathens.org/") has done work in Atlanta before. We provided [computers]("http://movementphotos.org/main.php?g2_itemId=7120") to the US Social Forum last summer, for example. Anyone who'd like to volunteer with us or just donate some hardware should send an email to [email]freeitathens@gmail.com[/email]

Of course, there's a huge need in Atlanta that we don't have the manpower or logistics to meet. It would be wonderful if a group of Atlanta techies could organize and try to address that.

Anyone who is primarily motivated by the idea of offering low cost computers and doesn't mind a Microsoft environment should contact [http://www.techcorpsga.org/](http://www.techcorpsga.org/)
