### Post by JReagan1990 on 2007-07-10
There are new job positions in our LoCo that we need people to volunteer for.  These jobs are for the two new sub-teams that were introduced on the July 8, 2007 IRC meeting.  A detailed description of the jobs and teams can be found here:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/GeorgiaUSTeam/Meetings?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=Sub-Team+Proposals.odt](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/GeorgiaUSTeam/Meetings?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=Sub-Team+Proposals.odt) 

The first team is the Marketing Team.  The positions we need to fill are listed below:

**- Marketing Team Manager**
This position requires working with all those who have CDs and other promotional material to plan new campaigns and manage our supplies wisely.  This position also requires the designing of new material  to be used in future campaigns.

The second team is the Support Team.  The positions we need to fill are listed below:
   
**- Support Team Manager**
This position requires the creation and monitoring of the support email for new support requests, and the fine-tuning of the current support system.  This also requires checking the forums for new support requests, as well as idling on the IRC channel for instant support.

*note: you will have people help you with the tasks included with this position*

If you want to sign up for these positions, please send an email to [EMAIL="jreagan1990@gmail.com"]jreagan1990@gmail.com[/EMAIL]
