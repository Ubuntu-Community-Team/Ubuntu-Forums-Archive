### Post by 73ckn797 on 2012-01-02
Welcome to Georgia. All that's been said about Savannah is true. It is 250 miles from where I live in Newnan, Ga. but I have enjoyed my trips there. 

The nice part of Georgia is the diversity. There has been a saying about there being 2 Georgia's. There is the Metro Atlanta area and then there is the rest of Georgia. You have the mountains which are an easy drive away and then the coast, which is where you will be. There is a lot is history here, Savannah being about the earliest area.

The mosquito's here, if viewed under a microscope, are nothing but teeth and wings, beware! You will also encounter the "dog day afternoons" (picture an old hound dog with it's tongue hanging out and panting in the heat) in the summer, but that is just how it is.

I do not know of any Ubuntu groups around but I know there are a few users very close to where I live. I found them here on the forums.
