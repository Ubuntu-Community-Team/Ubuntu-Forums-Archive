### Post by collinp on 2009-06-28
The Atlanta Linux Fest will be held Saturday, September 19th, 2009, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. This coincides with the 2009 Software Freedom day, so come show your support for free software! The Atlanta Linux Fest is a free grassroots conference for the GNU/Open Source Software/Free Software community to gather and share information about fun and exciting existing and upcoming Linux and Open Source Software. Attendees of all skill levels are invited to share their experiences to benefit all users of Linux/Free and Open Source Software. Atlanta Linux Fest is the place to learn, make new friends, and have fun! Many Ubuntu presenters will be speaking, including Rick Clark (manager of the Server Team), Pete Graner (manager of the Kernel Team), and many more. We will also be having many other fine presentations about other open source projects such as Pulseaudio, SugarCRM, etc.

More information on the Atlanta Linux Fest (presentations, schedule, directions, etc.) is posted on our website at [http://atlantalinuxfest.org/](http://atlantalinuxfest.org/) . Hope to see you there!
