### Post by coffeecat on 2023-04-23
By posting in this section of the forum, you are applying for Ubuntu Membership via Forums Contributions. See here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Forums/Membership](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Forums/Membership)

From that link:

> Qualifications for membership

You need to:

[list][*]have been active on the forums for at least six months.
[list][*]As a guideline, you need about 1,000 posts in support areas. [/list][/list]

You do not fulfil the criteria for Ubuntu Membership via forums contributions.

There are other avenues for applying for Ubuntu Membership described here - [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership/NewMember](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership/NewMember) - but please be aware that ["Official Ubuntu Membership means recognition of significant and sustained contribution to Ubuntu or the Ubuntu community." ]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership") If you have significant and sustained contributions in other areas, then another avenue would be best for you. If you are new to contributing to Ubuntu, then I suggest you persevere in your efforts and I wish you well for the future.
