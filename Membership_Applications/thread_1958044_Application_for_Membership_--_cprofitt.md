### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2012-04-13
> **cprofitt said:**
> I am already a member, but would like to get membership via the forums... while they are basically the same it is my understanding that there are some differences. I did this after consulting with bodhi_zazen.

The difference is that not all Ubuntu members are active on the forums.

All Ubuntu members are eligible for identification as members. Those ubuntu members who are active on the forums are eligible for Ubuntu Forums Membership.

Although the details are under discussion

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ForumCouncilAgenda](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ForumCouncilAgenda)

It is likely that future staff will be selected from Ubuntu Forms Members and UFM will likely help elect staff and FC members. If you are interested in the details, please try to attend the FC meetings or read the logs. You are also welcome to discuss the issue with the community on irc (#ubuntuforums).
