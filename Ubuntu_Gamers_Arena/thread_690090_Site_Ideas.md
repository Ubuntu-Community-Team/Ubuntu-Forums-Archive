### Post by compiledkernel on 2008-02-07
Speaking as the Project Founder Ill try to touch on all your points. 

Firstly, we are in a redesign of the game list page. AI is working quite feverishly on this, and our model currently is seperation of free vs commercial. Hard to setup a top 10 list, I guess I can query the access.log to determine how many of the games list pages get hit and order them in that fashion or something. 

If you want to submit a game review send it over to one of us, we will get it put up for you. See here -- [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=394135](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=394135)

I admire the HappyPenguin guy, have talked to Bob on a few rare occasions. He has his motivations, and we have ours. There is a mutual respect there I think. As far as Linux-gamers goes, I was pretty much down talked for my duplication of effort when I attempted to speak with them about colab, so im likely not going to offer any kindness (nor do I intend to be any further unkind as well) in that direction. 

[Install guides here]("http://gaming.gwos.org/doku.php/guides:start"), well our howto page is quite extensive and relatively easy to read and understand. If you dont see a game install there and you want one, specially if its native one, let us know and we will get you running. Automatix in our humble opinion isnt a solution to the installation problem, proper education is. As such we do not endorse it, nor strive to be like it.
