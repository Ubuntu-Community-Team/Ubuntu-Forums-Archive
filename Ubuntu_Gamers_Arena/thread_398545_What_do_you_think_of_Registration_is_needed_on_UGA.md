Greetings fellow gamers!

Just making a survey on "If UGA need a making an account to post, vote and comment". The reason I ask is that the web is flooding with spam bots and people which purpose in life is to spam (what I wouldn't give to get my hands on such persons), which makes it a tedious job to maintain a non-registration free for all posts site.
If we had such system would you make a registration?

1) Comment on the news
2) Give your votes on our guides.
3) Much less annoying spams
4) The admins can use the extra energy on something useful.

Downside;
1) It takes less then 2 min to make an account.


What do you think? Just have in mind it's just a survey to see what our user think, we know people think it's annoying to create an account, but it helps to keep things clean.
