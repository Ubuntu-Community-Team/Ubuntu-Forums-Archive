### Post by Mindless1 on 2008-02-02
I have been an Ubuntu user for a while now, though switching from windows, I found it hard to find a decent selection of games.

Over the past few months, I have put together a comprehensive list for all of the people who love Ubuntu as much as I do, but also enjoy playing a few games, or ALOT!:)

The site is 

[http://www.playubuntu.com]("http://www.playubuntu.com")

My site has a list of over 1800 Games that will run on ubuntu, they are catagorised in an easy to search function, and I have also placed an easy to install guide link for install some of the trickier games through the terminal.

I think this could be a great help to those ubuntu users like me who are looking for a wide selection of games to play on their machines.

This information could maybe be more accessable if maybe a link was added at the bottom of

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Games](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Games)
