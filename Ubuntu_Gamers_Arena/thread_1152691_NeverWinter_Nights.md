### Post by Carpentr on 2009-05-08
Neverwinter Nights has two main expansions (Shadows of Undrentide and Hordes of the Underdark). There are also a few premium modules you can download on the game's official website for a price; then there are numerous player made single-player and multi-player modules available at various places.

As for the price, I got Neverwinter Nights Diamond Edition for $20 and it came with Neverwinter Nights, both expansion packs, and the Kingmaker premium module.
