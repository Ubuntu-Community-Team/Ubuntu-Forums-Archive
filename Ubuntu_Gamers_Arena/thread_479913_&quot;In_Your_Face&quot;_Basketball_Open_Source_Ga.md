### Post by tretle on 2007-06-20
[In Your Face]("https://projects.warp.es/projects/inyourface/") is an open source basketball game currently in the early development  stage. Its written by a guy named [EMAIL="isaac@warp.es"]Isaac Clerencia[/EMAIL] as a project for his final year university degree. He states himself that the gfx to the game look crappy and suggests that this may be down to him being the worst graphics designer out there... Whats interesting about this game is the engine. He is making huge advances in the physics and si department and if people start contributing graphics(2d or 3d)  to this game may end up being the best open source sports game out there. I am definitely bookmarking this one, if it turns out well it could mean great things for the open source sports genre.
