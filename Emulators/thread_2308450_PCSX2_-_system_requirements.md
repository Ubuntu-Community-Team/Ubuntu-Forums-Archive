### Post by deadflowr on 2016-01-04
> **michael-piziak said:**
> PCSX2 - system requirements ?

What should one probably have at minimum before even attempting this emulator?

Michael

A four core processor (probably a minimum 3.0 ghz speed), and a really decent graphics card. 
4 gigs of RAM should suffice, so anything around that or more should be good.

You will need some hard drive space as the recommended method to run games is to copy them as iso's (and it is much faster, by a mile)

Also good to have durable cooling system of some kind, since it'll most likely be running at full-to-near-full throttle for most of the time you play it.
Those are few of the things I can think of.

The minimum they list as having a dual-core processor simply gets the games to run, but frame rates and such are horrendous, imo.

But...
even with the best specs there will still be a lot of games that have terrible performance issues.
So there is that.
