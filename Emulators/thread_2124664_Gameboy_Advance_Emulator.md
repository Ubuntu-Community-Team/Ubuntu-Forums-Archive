### Post by Samuel_Knight on 2013-09-23
I agree with geoaraujo. I've been using Linux for about two years now, and I do a lot of gaming. Mednafen is the best for running GBA games.

When I first got the Mother series up and running on my laptop, I used ZSNES for Earthbound, Mednafen for Mother 3, and VirtuaNES for Mother 1.

If you want to customize the keys in Mednafen (which I recommend) or you just want to know the keys you can use, go [here]("http://mednafen.sourceforge.net/documentation/09x/mednafen.html#Using+Mednafen%01Key+Assignments") for keyboard shortcuts. For customization, it should be under the second table.
