### Post by LillyDragon on 2013-10-05
I have a similar problem with Regen's native port, but I use VBA-M (An infinitely better edition of VBA.) outside the repos for GBC games, so I didn't know that had the same issue.

The only remedy I had was using QJoyPad, and its 32-bit package can still be found through Synaptic, I do believe. Not only would you be able to mimic similar keyboard commands, but you could also navigate the cursor with your controller as well, so it's perfect for couch play on the PC, or playing games that otherwise have no joypad support of their own.
