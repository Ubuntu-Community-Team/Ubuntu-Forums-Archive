The reason why mupen64plus from the Ubuntu Repos is kinda slow is that the Version in the Repos is outdated. Get the newest mupen64plus from [playdeb]("http://www.playdeb.net") and also install cutemupen.

cutemupen is a GUI for mupen64plus as it doesn't have one out of the box.
