### Post by General_Faliure on 2012-09-11
Hello.

Mame is for the old arcade games (pacman, Donkey kong etc, there are thousands of them).
Mess is for emulating old (game)computers like colecovision, bbc micro, Sinclair ZX Spectrum and lots of others.
Neither is more advanced, they just have different functions.
The newest Mame can be found in the repositories.
For Mess you have to add the repository from this page: [http://sdlmame.wallyweek.org/](http://sdlmame.wallyweek.org/)

Have fun
