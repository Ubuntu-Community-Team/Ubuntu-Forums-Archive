### Post by deadflowr on 2013-05-22
If you dig around the web, you'll find some psx linux plugins. Usually in a zip file.
Just download 'em and extract into the hidden .pcsx folder, or epsxe folder(whichever). The folder will have the bios, patches, plugins, etc folders within it.

At least with pcsx, when the files are in there, when you run the program, the dropdown menu option 'configurations' has the first item as plugins and bios. You can change the plugins here.
