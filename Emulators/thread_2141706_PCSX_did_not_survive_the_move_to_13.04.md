### Post by p7X7eRab on 2013-05-19
Make a backup of your memory card folder, and save states. Along with any configurations you want (this can be redone though). 
Now uninstall the emulator completely (including mem card files, and save states, keep backups in a seperate file). Now update system, and install any package upgrades. Reinstall. Reconfigure, and see if your iso files play properly, just copy mem card folders, and save states back into their respective folders. I do not have 13, just 10.04 never had a problem, only when windows did a similar thing.
