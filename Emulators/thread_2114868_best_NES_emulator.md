### Post by ZarathustraDK on 2013-02-25
Depends on your definition of best.

If you have a powerful enough PC, then BSNES is probably the best in regards to fidelity. Some of the other emulators pull some tricks in order to speed up emulation, at the cost of "true" emulation, which may be evident in screen-artifacts, ridiculous speeds and the likes.

It may not matter to you, but part of the retro "thing" is replicating the slowdowns when the screen is busy, it tickles the childhood-muscle :)
