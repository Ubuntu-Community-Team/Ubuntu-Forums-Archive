### Post by Bölvaður on 2012-11-26
In gaming, if you and your friends join the same server, you will be able to play together.

If you are meaning that you are hosting the server, it means you and your friends have to join the server on your machine. The ip which you join is not going to be the local ip and they will have to connect to your external ip, the ip of your router. They will therefore connect to a port on your router. Which means you will have to configure the router's port to be _forwarded_ to the correct port on your computer (local ip). Just look up *port forwarding* on google for further information.
