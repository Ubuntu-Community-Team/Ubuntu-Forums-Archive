### Post by BigSilly on 2012-05-13
Just discovered this absolutely excellent ZX Spectrum emulator - [FBZX]("http://www.rastersoft.com/fbzx.html"), that seems as though it's still being maintained and loved by the dev (last update was only December 2011). It's the same chap who brought us the tremendous DeVeDe that I still use to this day, so its a trustworthy program too.

I've just had a play with it and I much prefer it to FUSE. This handles fullscreen better and just feels easier to use imho. The fact though that it is still being maintained is a real big plus for me though - it works great on Precise 64 bit.

Give it a go. Personally I'd love to see it make the main Ubuntu Software Centre so more people could get their hands on it. :)
