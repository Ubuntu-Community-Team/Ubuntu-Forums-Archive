### Post by deadflowr on 2016-01-20
Not sure about getting a fixed frame rate, but you can probably get better performance by running a lighter desktop.
One without 3d-accel features.
lxde or xfce (lubuntu and xubuntu's desktop, respectively) come to mind.

Even lighter is something like a plain-old window manager; your namesake (awesome) is one.
Fluxbox, or openbox are others.

Pcsx also comes with two graphic plugin options.
Try using the Xvideo plugin over the Opengl plugin.
The graphics will be crude, but the graphics for PS1 games were already crude...
