### Post by Dinosawer on 2013-08-24
Tip: instal dbgl ([http://members.quicknet.nl/blankendaalr/dbgl/](http://members.quicknet.nl/blankendaalr/dbgl/)), which is a GUI for dosbox, download their Daggerfall package (somewhere halfway the page, don't extract the archive!), import it in DBGL with file->import...  and you can play without mucking about with command lines.
(You might have to deinstall your current installation of dosbox if you get an error while trying to play)
