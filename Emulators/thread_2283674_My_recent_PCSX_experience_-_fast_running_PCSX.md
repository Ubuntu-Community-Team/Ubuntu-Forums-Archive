### Post by michael-piziak on 2015-06-23
Yesterday, my PCSX emualtor was running at an extreme fast speed.  Perhaps an update caused this, I don't know.
I eventually solved the problem by selecting & turning on "Use FPS limit" in the graphics configuration.  Previously, only "FPS limit auto-detection" was turned on and worked for a very long time.
Anyways, I thought I'd share this info if any of you are getting a really fast running PCSX all of a sudden.
