### Post by Gina on 2008-10-26
Can you tell us which graphics card you have and what type of screen.  What screen resolution are you getting and what should it be - likewise what refresh rate.  A refresh rate of 50Hz or above is usually OK for TFT screens but you'll need a higher rate for a CRT screen.  You may need to install a proprietry driver for your graphics card.
