### Post by the real omni on 2008-10-01
Updated from 8.04 to 8.10 alpha 6

In 8.04 RealVNC worked with no flaws as far as I could tell, except that it would take a loooong time to draw the desktop in vnc-viewer.. Once the desktop had been drawn, it would operate pretty quickly but while the desktop was drawing initially it wouldn't even respond to input.

When I first updated to 8.10, vnc-viewer just plain wouldn't work. As soon as I tried to connect, the window would just close itself.

I don't know what kind of magic you devs did overnight, but today vnc-viewer works perfectly AND it draws SUPER SUPER fast!

So it went from working slowly to not working at all to working faster than a speedmonkey!

At the risk of being sued by McDonalds, "I'm lovin' it!"
