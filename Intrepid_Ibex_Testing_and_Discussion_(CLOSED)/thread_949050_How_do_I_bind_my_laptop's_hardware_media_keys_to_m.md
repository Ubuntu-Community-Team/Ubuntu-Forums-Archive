### Post by damis648 on 2008-10-15
Go to System>Preferences>Keyboard Shortcuts. Scroll over to "Increase Volume" and "Decrease Volume". When selected, it will say "Press a key". Press your volume up button and your down button on the respective items. Good luck!:popcorn:
