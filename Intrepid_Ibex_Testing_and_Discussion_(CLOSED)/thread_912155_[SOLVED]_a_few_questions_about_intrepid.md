### Post by mudfly on 2008-09-06
From the X.org 7.3 release notes,

"Input hotplug: Input hotplug allows hotplugging of input devices, and also adds enhanced support for touchscreens and tablets, through either HAL or D-Bus."

[http://www.x.org/wiki/Releases/7.3](http://www.x.org/wiki/Releases/7.3)

Sounds like it supports hotplugging which means you will not have to restart X after plugging in your tablet as long as its supported by HAL or D-Bus.
