### Post by LoneWolfJack on 2008-10-06
issues with audio appear to be a common problem... had my share of them as well.

when you click on the sound mixer thingy icon in your taskbar, are the speaker symbols below the volume bars crossed out/greyed out? this would indicate that there's a driver issue of some sort.

AMD64 is still a common term because AMD was the first to introduce processors capable of 64bit operations to the consumer market. you got the 64bit version installed, so it will work fine with your CPU. otherwise ubuntu would have told you about incompatible architectures previous to the setup process.
