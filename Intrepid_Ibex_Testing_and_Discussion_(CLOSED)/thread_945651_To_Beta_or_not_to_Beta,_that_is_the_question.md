### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-10-12
The beta releases are for testing. If you are willing to test and put up with bugs and bug reports go for it. Support will not be as good and you may need to re-install or wait for the developers to fix any particular bug (I have had to re-install betas to fix bugs in the past).

If you *need* some new feature and just can not wait, again go for it.

If you expect a stable system with minimal bugs wait for the release.

Otherwise this thread seems to be a matter of opinion and you should take care that all these "works for me" posts are not an indication it will work for you.
