### Post by 23meg on 2008-06-19
After some discussion and feedback on the mailing list and at the weekly [meetings]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/QATeam/Meetings"), the QA team's schedule for Intrepid Ibex, which outlines the specific areas the team will be focusing on during different phases of the cycle, was announced.

You can view the schedule at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/QATeam/IntrepidSchedule](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/QATeam/IntrepidSchedule), and ask any relevant questions you may have in this thread. Hopefully this will be useful for everyone who wants to [help with bugs]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HelpingWithBugs") and perform various kinds of [testing]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Testing") in getting coordinated with the wider community of QA contributors.
