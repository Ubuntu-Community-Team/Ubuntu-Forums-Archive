Does anyone know where to find more? (Most everything at gnome-look.org is ironically enough made for KDE, or just a loose collection of sounds rather than a real sound theme in the sense of the new specifications.) Any tips would be appreciated.

### Post by shane19174 on 2008-10-22
You just have to put the folder with the index.theme file and the subfolder with the sound files into usr/share/sounds. Then you can change themes under System > Preferences > Sound.
