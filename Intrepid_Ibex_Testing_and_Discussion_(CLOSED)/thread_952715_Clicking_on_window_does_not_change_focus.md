### Post by mer on 2008-10-19
As the title suggests, after upgrading from hardy to intrepid, the focus of a window does not always change when I click on it. For instance, if firefox is open and a gnome-terminal is open, clicking on the gnome-terminal will bring it to the top of the z-order, but focus will not change to it and all keyboard actions will be sent to firefox instead of the terminal. I can change focus using alt-tab or using the window list applet. There are exceptions to this. For instance, a maximized firefox will gain focus when I click on it, but not a maximized compizconfig will not. Furthermore, when I enable focus change with mouse hovering, the focus changes as expected. A further, perhaps non-related problem, is that I cannot alt-drag windows.

Any ideas?
Thanks in advance

SOLUTION: Ok, so this in technically not an acceptable solution, but logging in and out seems to solve the problem. However, you need to repeat this every time.
