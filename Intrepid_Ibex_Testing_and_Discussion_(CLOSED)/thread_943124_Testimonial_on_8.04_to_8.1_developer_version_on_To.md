### Post by hari_home on 2008-10-09
I want to congratulate the team who improved wireless and Audio support.

On Toshiba A215-S7416 AMD64 Dual-Core laptop, I had 32-bit desktop x86
version of Ubuntu 8.04. Everything was working fine except the internal
wireless card and audio recording. Just for that reason I tried
update-manager --devel
to upgrade to 8.1 and I was pleased to see that the internal wireless card is recognized and works. Also, audio recording works fine from the front microphone jack using external microphone. Now I can do skype Audio.

Looking forward to 802.11N support soon.
