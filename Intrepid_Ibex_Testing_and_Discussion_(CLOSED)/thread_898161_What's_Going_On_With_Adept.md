### Post by beartard on 2008-08-23
Adept's beta is replacing the old kde 3.5.x versions in the repositories, slowly but surely.  The two can't co-exist on the system, so in order to upgrade to the kde4 version, it's removing all the related adept packages from kde3.  I believe that adept and adept-notifier are available now.
