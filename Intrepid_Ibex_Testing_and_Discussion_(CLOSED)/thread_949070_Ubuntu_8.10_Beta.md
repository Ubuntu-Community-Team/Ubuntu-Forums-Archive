### Post by haydnc on 2008-10-15
This is dead easy to do and they actually provide some instructions on how to do it on the Ubuntu website if you dig around a bit.

That said, the important part of the page - aside from the warnings not to do it on a production machine because 8.10 is still in beta and therefore might break - is this:

> To upgrade from Ubuntu 8.04, press Alt+F2 and type in "update-manager -d" (without the quotes) into the command box. Update Manager should open up and tell you: New distribution release '8.10' is available. Click Upgrade and follow the on-screen instructions.
