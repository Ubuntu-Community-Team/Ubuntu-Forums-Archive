### Post by dualscreenman on 2008-10-23
You'll have to stick with Hardy, KDE3 as a desktop environment is gone in Intrepid.

However, so solve some of your issues:

-Intrepid has the fuzzy clock installed by default. To get this in KDE4 in Hardy install kdeplasma-addons and log out/back in.
-You can drag launchers from KMenu to any panel you'd like.
