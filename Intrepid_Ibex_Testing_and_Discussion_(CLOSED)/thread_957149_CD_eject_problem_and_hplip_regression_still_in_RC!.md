First of all I love Ubuntu! Really! That's why I find it sad that an RC shows such problems. 

Of course I'll file bug reports as I always do, but it's absurd that the CD does not even eject properly. The CD retracts so quickly it effects the function of other things - like Brasero being able to auto-check the integrity of a burn! And it's downright "fiddly".

Then I'd hoped they'd do something about the regression in HPLIP support. The new gui interface is great, but it was even greater to just have printing work! My Deskjet 5440 worked out-of-box with both Gutsy and Hardy but now I have to spend 20 to 30 minutes to DL the newest HPLIP driver to get my printer working!

To me these are minor issues, but when I think back to the first time I tried Ubuntu - about 9 months ago - these would have been the kind of things that pushed me to try other distros.

These would be "deal killers" for total noobs!
