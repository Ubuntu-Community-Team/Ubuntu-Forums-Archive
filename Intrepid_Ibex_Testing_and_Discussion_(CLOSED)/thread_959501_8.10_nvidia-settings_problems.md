### Post by dwoodwar on 2008-10-26
Bug #286424 has a couple of work arounds to solve this issue.  Appears that the lack of a ServerLayout section is the cause.

Link to Bug is [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nvidia-settings/+bug/286424](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nvidia-settings/+bug/286424)

to get this to work i did:

sudo nvidia-xconfig
sudo nvidia-settings

Was then able to save the settings.

Hope this helps

Thanks

Dave
