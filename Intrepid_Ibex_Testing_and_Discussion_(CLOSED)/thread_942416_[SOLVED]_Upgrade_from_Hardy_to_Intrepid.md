### Post by amauk on 2008-10-09
just as a side note,
more technical than before, but if you move your home folder to a separate partition you can do what you want to the root partition and all your personal settings (everything, inc. email) is kept intact

you can format your root partition, reinstall the OS and apps, and have them all back to how they were instantly

you can even switch OS's
(wipe Ubuntu and install Fedora, say)
keeping all preferences and settings
