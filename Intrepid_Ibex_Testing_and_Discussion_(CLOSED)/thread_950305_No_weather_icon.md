### Post by KStorm on 2008-10-24
In previous versions of Ubuntu, the clock applet weather icon wouldn't be displayed if there was a discrepancy with the time zone you chose when you installed Ubuntu and the time zone you chose when you configured clock preferences -> weather.  The time zones must match in order for the weather icon to appear.  You'll have to go to system -> administration -> time and date to change your original time zone.
