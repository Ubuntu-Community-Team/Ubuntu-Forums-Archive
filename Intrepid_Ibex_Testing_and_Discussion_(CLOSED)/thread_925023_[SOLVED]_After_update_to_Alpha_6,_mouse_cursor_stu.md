### Post by darkxman on 2008-09-20
After I updated my Hardy Heron install with Alpha 6, I can't move the mouse cursor very far before it snaps back to a spot on the middle of the screen. 

It happens as soon as the login screen pops up, and it doesn't seem to matter what mouse I use. I've tried my USB Logitech MX1000 and my ps2 Logitech mouseman. I've even booted the system with no mouse attached at all and as soon as the login screen comes up the mouse cursor is still wobbling.

I've been playing around with the xorg.conf, did an update/upgrade from terminal but no success so far. Anyone have any ideas?
