### Post by bash on 2008-10-23
If you really can't afford losing the data or have no means to back it up, then I would recommend you to stay with Hardy. There is never a 100% guarantee that everything will work peachy even if it should. On a more general note, regular back-ups should always be made. I had my fair share of painful experiences where my HDD went borked and all my files were gone. That has at least taught me a lesson to always back-up my data ;)
