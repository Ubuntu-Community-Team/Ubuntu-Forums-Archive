### Post by Therion on 2008-10-08
When you burn the disc, are you burning an .iso image?

Put your CD or DVD in the drive and let it spin up.
Now go explore the disk using Windows Explorer.
Do you see one file on the disc you burned, or do you see several files and maybe a few folders as well?

If you see only ONE file on the disc (with an .iso extension) you've not burned the disc correctly.
