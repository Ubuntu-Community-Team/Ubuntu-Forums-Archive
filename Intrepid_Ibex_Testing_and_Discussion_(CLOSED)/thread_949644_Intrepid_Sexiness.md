### Post by WiFi Ed on 2008-10-16
Shut down times are noticeably quicker in II than they were in HH. Boot up times are a mixed bag (for me anyway). I turned off the splash screen/progress bar and it appears that the loading of the various modules/objects/files, whatever is faster than it was in HH, but...after I get to the login screen and enter my info the time to get to a stable desktop seems a tiny bit longer than it was in HH. In particular, a Power Management app or service that appears in the lower panel seems to drag things out.

Rhythmbox has improved IMHO, as has the file management stuff. Wallpaper got better too, recently...
