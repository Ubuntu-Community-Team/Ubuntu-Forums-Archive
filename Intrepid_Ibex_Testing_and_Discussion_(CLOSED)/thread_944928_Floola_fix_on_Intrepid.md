Just encase anyone is interested here is what I did to get floola working on Intrepid. Go to Synaptic and install libstdc++5. I had the behavior where when clicking on the floola executable nothing would happen, not even an error message. When I tried to fire it up from terminal that is when I found this dependancy was missing. Not sure if this thread should be in the Intrepid section, I just thought it might be relevant to other Intrepid testers who like this app...
