### Post by bwallum on 2008-10-11
> **petri said:**
> In Nautilus choose Bookmarks in menu and you'll see Edit bookmarks or something similar. There you should be able to edit Nautilus bookmarks.

Hole in One!

To open Nautilus (for anyone following) go to Places and left click on Documents.
From top menu in Documents - File Browser window, left click on Bookmarks and Edit Bookmarks.
The Edit Bookmarks window appears and allows changes to be made.
