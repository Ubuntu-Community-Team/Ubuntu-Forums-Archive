### Post by Rinnan on 2008-10-13
Warning...

My wife and I use OpenOffice very seriously in our day-to-day work life and we use every feature.  Our work depends on it, so we aren't ordinary users.

I tried out a beta of 3.0 about a month ago and discovered that if you load and save document files (whether in .doc format or OOo's native format), and are tracking changes, and edit a file back and forth between OOo 3.0beta and 2.4.1, it will start to "lose deletions", which will cause the internal representation of the edits to be very messed up.  So, deletes become insertions, and everything gets "shifted back one step", but wrongly.  It creates total spaghetti out of the file.  I am now wary of the release and will read reviews about it from aggressive power users in the months following the 3.0 release before satisfied to upgrade.
