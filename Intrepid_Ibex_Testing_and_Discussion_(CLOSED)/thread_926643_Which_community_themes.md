### Post by *g!t5^_)*(H on 2008-09-22
Why don't use a completely black theme ? 

Because shadows around windows doesn't work on black interfaces, they look great even over very dark grey but not on completely black surfaces. 

If this is the situation, you loose the 'over' effect of the shadows, and this situation is even worst when the contents of the application (for example one black website) is also black, then you don't know what's the begin or the end of windows and menus (for example 'File' menu). 

New wave is all right in my opinion (For usability, even if I don&#8217;t like those window buttons).
