### Post by BackwardsDown on 2008-09-10
If the livecd works on your computer as expected, a HD install will problably work fine too.

If the live-cd won't boot, gives the wrong resolution, etc. Then you have a reasonable chance that it will work when it's installed on your HD.

If you can miss the gigabyte's I would recommend installing it, because that is also what you want to do with it when it comes out, and thus need to be tested.
