### Post by Sand &amp; Mercury on 2008-09-19
A while back I made a thread about Metacity and its compositor in Gnome 2.22, complaining of general jerkiness and slowness despite its stability. I upgraded to Intrepid yesterday and while the lack of fgrlx prevents me from using Compiz at the moment, I have to say the compositing in Metacity has much improved! Much smoother and even a bit more presentable now, and it works even just using the Mesa video drivers.

I'm just starting this to see what everyone elses' experiences with it have been.

(You can turn it on by running "gconf-editor", then navigating to apps>metacity>general and setting "compositing-manager" to true)
