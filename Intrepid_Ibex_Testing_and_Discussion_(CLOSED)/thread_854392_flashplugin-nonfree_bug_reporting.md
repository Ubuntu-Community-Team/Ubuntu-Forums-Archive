### Post by plun on 2008-07-09
Its difficult to define a right way for nonfree stuff.

I have reported directly to Adobe with this form.
[http://www.adobe.com/cfusion/mmform/index.cfm?name=fp_beta_feedback](http://www.adobe.com/cfusion/mmform/index.cfm?name=fp_beta_feedback)


But in your case with a crashing fox it must be a bug filed also to Launchpad

[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/flashplugin-nonfree/+bugs](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/flashplugin-nonfree/+bugs)

But again probably better directly against Firefox...

[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/firefox-3.0/+bugs](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/firefox-3.0/+bugs)


Then it can also be a good idea for some bugs to file directly to
Mozillas Bugzilla

[https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/](https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/)

[https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/buglist.cgi?query_format=specific&order=relevance+desc&bug_status=__open__&content=flash](https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/buglist.cgi?query_format=specific&order=relevance+desc&bug_status=__open__&content=flash)


Mozillas devs talks with Adobes devs and thats maybe important.:confused:
Ubuntus or Debians devs maybe talks with Mozillas devs....:)


So right or wrong....???   In your case probably only Launchpad and a Firefox bug.
