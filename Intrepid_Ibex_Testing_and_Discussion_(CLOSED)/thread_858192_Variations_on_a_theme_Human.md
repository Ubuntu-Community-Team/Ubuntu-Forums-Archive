### Post by mejy on 2008-07-13
I've always been a big fan of both the murrine engine and dark themes, so was very pleased to see the move in this direction in the first Intrepid alpha release.  While it is obviously still pretty unrefined, I feel it has a huge amount of potential (despite some of the criticisms it has received) and this is a fairly small modification which I hope captures some of that.

I've played around and tweaked many aspects of the theme, and have created two similar version - the inventively named 'NuvoHuman' and 'NuovoHuman2' - the former with a similar toolbar style to NewHuman, and the latter with something closer to what's seen in Clearlooks.  I currently prefer the latter style, particularly in apps like nautilus where the numerous toolbars can appear fairly overwhelming without some sort of visible division into sections.

This is just a first attempt, and if there's any interest I would hope to spend some time refining this modification - a process which would certainly be made easier by suggestions of what you feel would improve the theme, and which of the two versions you prefer.
