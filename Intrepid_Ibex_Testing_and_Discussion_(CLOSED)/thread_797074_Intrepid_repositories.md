### Post by macaholic on 2008-05-16
Unless you want to do a full on upgrade, it's not a good idea. Intrepid isn't even at Alpha yet, for more info see the [Intrepid development board.]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=346")
Also, if you want newer software, you could always try installing their source packages from the project website. Or, sites like [getdeb.net]("getdeb.net") have software built into debs that is sometimes more current than the stable ubuntu packages.
