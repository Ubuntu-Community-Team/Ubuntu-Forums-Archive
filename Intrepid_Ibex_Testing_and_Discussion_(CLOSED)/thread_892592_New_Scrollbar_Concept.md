### Post by thorwil on 2008-08-18
ronacc: even if you really don't interact with the scrollbar, it's still usefull because it shows where you are and how much of the document you see. Here my concept has the advantages of reducing clutter by not showing the arrows upfront and giving a better overview by using the full available height and allowing the indicator to shrink down to a single pixel to be as accurate about the view/document ratio as possible.
