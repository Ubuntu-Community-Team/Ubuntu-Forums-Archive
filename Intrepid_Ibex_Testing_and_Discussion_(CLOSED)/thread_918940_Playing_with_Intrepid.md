Yes, install emerald theme manager. Then goto [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) and click on "beryl" on the left. Browse for whatever theme you want with transparency (I like "DarkLight"), save to a folder in your home path, then go into compiz config settings manager, then under the effects section go into "window decoration".
Add this into the command box

/usr/bin/emerald --replace

and restart. Then once your loaded back up go click on the emerald icon under your preferences menu and you can install all the themes you downloaded and apply them instantly to see what they look like.

Once you get a theme that you like you can customize it even further.
