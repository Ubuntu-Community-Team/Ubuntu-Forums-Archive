May be a bit off topic, but why is the transparent panel being forced on the default Intrepid desktop? It's badly implemented, as it's not part of the Human GTK theme itself (and due to the pseudo-shadow at the bottom, it doesn't fit in with workspace switcher or window list applet).

Not everyone knows instinctively that you must edit the panel's configuration if you're planning to use any other theme besides Human.

It's not a bad idea to include panel backgrounds, but they shouldn't be forced by default. They should at least be implemented in Human's gtkrc, so that they are removed when the user switches to another theme.
