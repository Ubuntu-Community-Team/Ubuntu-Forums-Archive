### Post by RIchard James13 on 2008-08-09
I would try
1. Click on the plasma button on the right of the menu
2. Click on add widgets
3. Scroll down to Task Manager
4. Click on the red bar so that the Task Manager is removed
5. Add the Task Manager again using the same menu
6. Move the Task Manager back into position*

*To move things around in the panel you just need to click the plasma icon. Then if you move the mouse over objects in the panel you should see a a icon with four arrows. Click on that and then move the mouse to position the widget. Then click again when you are happy with the positioning.
