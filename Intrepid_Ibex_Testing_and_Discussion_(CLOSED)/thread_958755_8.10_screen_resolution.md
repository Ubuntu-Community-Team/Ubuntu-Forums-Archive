### Post by mrjohnston on 2008-10-26
Do you have any type of adapter between your monitor and vid card?  It sounds like EDID (what tells the card what the monitor can do) is failing.  install xresprobe and run ddcprobe to see if at the bottom it says edid fail.  If it is remove the adpaters, try a different monitor, or you need to find the custom lines for your monitor and place them in your xorg.conf manually (or get an xorg.conf from a working installation of linux and save that for the future just in case).
