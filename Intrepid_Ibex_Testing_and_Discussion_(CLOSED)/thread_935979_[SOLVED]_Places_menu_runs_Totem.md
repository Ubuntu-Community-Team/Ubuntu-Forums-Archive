### Post by Gina on 2008-10-02
This is a strange one...  Clicking on any of the top section of the Places menu in Intrepid (from Home Folder to Videos) launches Totem instead of nautilus.  Other items seem fine.

Anyone else get this or do I have a setting wrong somewhere?

---

### Post by ssam on 2008-10-02
right click on any folder in nautilus.
go to properties
have a look at the 'open with' tab.

if at some point you tried to open a folder with totem it might have stuck.

---

### Post by Gina on 2008-10-02
That was it - thank you very much :)  I thought it might be something simple!  My home folder was set to open in totem - put it back to "Open Folder".
