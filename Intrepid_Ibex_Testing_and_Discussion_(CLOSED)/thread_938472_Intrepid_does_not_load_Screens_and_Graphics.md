### Post by Kow on 2008-10-05
displayconfig-gtk is no longer in the repositories (ie: it's not longer available with ubuntu.) The idea is Xorg 7.4 will auto-configure everything Screens & Graphics (displayconfig) did minus the screen resolution in use. BTW, Preferences-> Screen Resolution is probably what you are looking for if you want to change the screen resolution.
