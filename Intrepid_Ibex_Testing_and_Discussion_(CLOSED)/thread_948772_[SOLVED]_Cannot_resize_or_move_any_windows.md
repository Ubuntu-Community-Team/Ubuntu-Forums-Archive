### Post by TerpInHotLanta on 2008-10-15
Turn off Compiz (right-click on the background, Change Desktop Background--> Visual Effects--> None) and see if that fixes the problem.

If so, when you turn Compiz back on, you'll need to go to the Advanced Desktop Effects Settings (System--> Preferences) and turn on Window Resize and Window Move.

Hope this helps.
