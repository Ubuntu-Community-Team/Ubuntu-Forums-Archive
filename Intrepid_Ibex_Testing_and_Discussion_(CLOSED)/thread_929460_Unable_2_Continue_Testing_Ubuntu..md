### Post by houstonbofh on 2008-09-25
The Comcast thing is bad.  For one thing, they 250gb is not a hard limit.  You can be "shaped" well below that.  Also the rules keep changing with each press release, so you can't trust them.

Some solutions;
1) Start tracking your use, and logging it daily, if you can.  MRTG is good for this.

2) Get with your LoCo to share downloads.  A copy of /var/cache/apt/archives/ on a thumb drive can help a lot on heavy days.

3) Shamelessly abuse feww wifi hotspots.

4) Call your city and see who regulates the cable franchise.  If you have good logs, you can prove how Comcast is messing with your legal activity, and that the charter should be reviewed.

Also, consider a business class account.  It is way more expensive, but they jack with your traffic almost not at all.
