### Post by Sef on 2008-10-11
> Have not been able to find any information about how large a partition I should create, except for really old references to how small a linux installation can be =).The partition should be 8 - 10 GB.

From [Partitioning your disks]("https://help.ubuntu.com/8.04/switching/installing-partitioning.html"):

> The root partition is akin to the "C" drive in Windows. You will need at least 4GB, double if possible. This will allow you more space to install programs.
