### Post by samsimba on 2010-11-05
Hi fellow Kenyans,

I know downloading the Ubuntu OS in Kenya can be a pain in the :guitar:, coz of the internet cost and connectivity issues. 

However you can get a free CD mailed to you via mail, by placing your request on [https://shipit.ubuntu.com](https://shipit.ubuntu.com), Remember to select whether you want a Desktop (The Default option), Notebook or Server Edition.

You can thereafter make online upgrades easily, to be at per with the most current Ubuntu releases.

Please make a donation to Ubuntu to promote more shipments if you can.
