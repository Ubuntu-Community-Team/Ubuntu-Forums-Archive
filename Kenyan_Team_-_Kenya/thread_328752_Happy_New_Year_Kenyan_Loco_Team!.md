### Post by EdThaSlayer on 2007-01-06

> hello and happy new year to you guys out there. good to know that finally ubuntu is catching on at home.

Go to :
1.Applications
2.Add/Remove Programs
3.Search for "codec"
4. Check GStreamer extra plugins and GStreamer ffmpeg video plugin
5. Click on apply and etc..

EDIT:you might want to check the "show unsupported applications'
