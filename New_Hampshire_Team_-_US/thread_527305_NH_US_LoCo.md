### Post by drdabbles on 2007-08-16
The NH LoCo is starting to get off the ground! We would like to call for any interested Ubuntu users and forum members in New Hampshire to add your name to the NH LoCo members page at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NewHampshireTeam/Members]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NewHampshireTeam/Members").

Our initial goal is to get a core group of interested people together so we can start building the resources the community will need. As of now, we are simply emailing each other and using the IRC channels. Please feel free to contact us and, again, add your information to the members page!
