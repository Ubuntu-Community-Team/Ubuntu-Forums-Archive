### Post by E-werd on 2005-05-09
I found a solution to a problem I've been having since I installed Ubuntu (using hoary preview).  In some games, namely UT2003 Demo, the sound would be sped up and all gargled.  I tried everything I found on the net, but to no avail.

There is one thing I haven't tried: the latest alsa release.  I went to the alsa site and downloaded the 1.0.9rc3 development release and everything is fine now.  Perhaps this should be packaged for easy update through the Ubuntu Updates (even though its a dev release).  I also followed directions on these forums (for which I will provide the link).  Hope this helps anybody who has problems with their sound.

[HOWTO: Hear multiple sounds at once](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=8882)
[Advanced Linux Sound Architecture - ALSA](http://www.alsa-project.org/)

For reference purposes, here is are my system specs:

AMD AthlonXP 2200+
512mb DDR PC2700 memory
ATI Radeon 9000Pro 128mb DDR
80gb Western Digital HDD
rtl8139too onboard network
via8233a onboard sound

Good luck.
