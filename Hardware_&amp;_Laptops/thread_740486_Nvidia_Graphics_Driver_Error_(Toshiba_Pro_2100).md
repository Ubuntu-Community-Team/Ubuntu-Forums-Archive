### Post by mdlueck on 2008-03-30
You seem "so close".

By chance have you taken a look through /etc/X11/xorg.conf ?

Specifically the "screen" section. It has "hard coded" the possible resolutions that will be displayed in the Screen Resolution applet.

To get laptop's with a wide screen to have all compatible possibilities, it was necessary to use the nVidia applet that you pictured to look up the possible resolutions (X Server Display Settings \ Resolution drop down pick list), and hand add them to the xorg.conf file. Then reboot and the Screen Resolution applet should have additional choices.
