### Post by Sef on 2008-03-12
> ati usually works well with ubuntu intell on the other hand doesn't

Intel works just fine with Ubuntu.   ATI's restricted drivers are more of a problem than NVidia's.

> A few days ago I installed Ubuntu 7.10 on my notebook and as the system rebooted to finally launch Ubuntu it freezes

1) Did you md5sum the download? (Checks to make sure it is good.)

2) Did you burn the iso at 4x or less? (More can cause a bad burn.)

3) At the start/install menu, did you go down to 'Check Disk for Errors'?  Maybe you have a bad disk, and it did not install correctly.)
