### Post by angryhomer17 on 2007-04-16
> **Landy Mann said:**
> Hi 
I have a Toshiba Satellite s3000-x4 (930mhz, 40gb hd, 394mb ram, 8mb vidio ram) witch i am going to install ubuntu 7.04 possibly as a daul boot with win xp would ubuntu or xubuntu be better

I would suggest xubuntu because you have very little ram for graphics (sounds much too low) It sounds more plausible that you would have 8mb of actual video ram, 512mb of ram (128 shared for graphics). Anyway whatever the case, I still would suggest xubuntu. It is less resource hungry, performs better on low to mid range computers, and is overall cleaner than gnome or kde. 

If you are just begining with linux you may want to use ubuntu (gnome) or kubuntu (kde). They provide more graphical options than xubuntu. I actually started my linux experience with gentoo and xfce (what xubuntu uses). Ubuntu is much easier than gentoo, but xfce is a great window manager for any linux distro. 

Xubuntu will run faster and look cleaner. Ubuntu (gnome or kde) will give you more graphical options, but will be slower. Hope this helps.
