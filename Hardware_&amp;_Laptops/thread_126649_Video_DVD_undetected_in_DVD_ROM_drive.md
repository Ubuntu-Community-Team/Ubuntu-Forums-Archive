Until recently, the drive is not able to detect video DVD with certain region code. I don't know what region code are those disks for sure but I believe them to be region 1 disks.

I have since reflashed the firmware of the drive and disabled the region code thingie. Windows and other utilities shows that it is not region locked. However, whether it is unlocked or set to whatever region (including region 1), it does not affect the outcome at all.

On the contrary my DVD writer drive is shown as region 1, but it play all my disks which I believe from different regions, just as the DVD ROM drive had been in the past.

I am not sure what triggered the symptoms, below are what I have done to the system recently:

- I installed Windows and PowerDVD on this PC.

- I updated my BIOS. I have reverted to earlier BIOS but it doesn't help (though not the same BIOS as my original BIOS is not available for download).

Below are the less likely cause:

- I meddled with hdparm so that my harddisk would turn off itself when not accessed.

- I installed some fans that tapped power from the DVD ROM drive, At first I suspected that it is underpowered and behaving erratically. Disconnecting the fan doesn't help, I wonder if the lack of voltage permanently affected it.
