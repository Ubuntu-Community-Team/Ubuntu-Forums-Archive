The little drive activity light on the front is red, instead of the usual green on nearly every floppy drive I've ever had.  It blinks while reading to/writing from floppies or flash cards.

This little sucker came in very handy last night... my brother and sister in law came in from San Diego with their two kids.  They brought their digital camera and like five full SD cards of pictures that they wanted to share (ranging from 32Mb to 256Mb).  In addition, my mother in law has a digital camera which takes CompactFlash cards, and she wanted to give some family pictures to them to take home with them.  I simply grabbed both cameras and all the cards, took them home (just a couple blocks away) and copied all of the pictures to a folder on my desktop, and burned them both some picture CDs in Gnomebaker.

Anyway... two thumbs up for this little miracle of a drive.  Most places sell it for $29.99, but tigerdirect.com is currently selling for $19.99.
