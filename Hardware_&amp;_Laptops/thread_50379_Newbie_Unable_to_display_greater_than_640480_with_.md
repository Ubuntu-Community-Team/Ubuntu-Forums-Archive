### Post by Rusty Age on 2005-08-07
Well hello again forum:

After searching through the internet (Google AND these forums) I finally managed to get my display up to 1024*768 PLUS get all three choices in my xorg.conf file  :smile: 

Here's how I did it:-

Step 1
I manually entered my HorizSync and VertRefresh rates according to my monitor specs (bit of Googling here.)

Did not work at first. However, I made no further changes to my xorg.conf file.

Step 2
After seeing somewhere on Google that x requires a bit more VGA memory, I entered my bios and found that the VGA display memory allocated was 1MB. I changed this to 8MB (the only other upward choice available), rebooted and presto! everything worked smoothly.

Two questions: How come I didn't have to do this in Windows? (which works perfectly at 1152*xxx, 1024*xxx etc)? And should this not be documented somewhere in Ubuntu's help? Such a simple documentation would have saved me 8 days of searching and sleeplessness!  :) 

Here's hoping this post helps somebody else.

Regards,
Russel
