### Post by Mygly on 2006-05-21
These posts are old so you may have figured it out by now. Restart and go into your BIOS. There should be an option somewhere in there to enable legacy USB keyboard and mouse support. Enable that option and your troubles should be over. Hope this helps if you haven't figured it out already.
