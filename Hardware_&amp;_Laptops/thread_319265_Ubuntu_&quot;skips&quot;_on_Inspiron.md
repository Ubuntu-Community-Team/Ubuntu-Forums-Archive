Fairly frequently (but only, at most, once a session), Ubuntu will start "skipping." By "skip," I mean it freezes for a second or two, then goes on, then freezes for a second or two, then goes on, etc. for about a minute or two. After that, it'll be fine for the rest of the session.

I've investigated the "skipping" with System Monitor, and it DOES NOT report jumps in CPU usage.
