### Post by L473ncy on 2008-03-21
Intel Graphics = Integrated. If you want any good performance then get an external GPU.

As for the "restricted drivers" they're not restricted in the sense you can't use them just you probably shouldn't be using them. (If you catch my drift).

I installed restricted drivers for my 7600 GS and the desktop effects work fine but I'm not sure maybe your graphics card doesn't support the desktop effects. I'd suggest that you get an external GPU (ie a nVidia 6600 or 7600 or x1600)
