### Post by solracarevir on 2006-12-11
in order to use the internal wireless you need to insert \\Option		"DisableIRQ"\\ on the device section, for example

```

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"VIA Technologies, Inc. S3 Unichrome Pro VGA Adapter"
	Driver		"via"
	BusID		"PCI:1:0:0"
#the next line is the one you need to add
Option		"DisableIRQ" 
EndSection

```

on my averatec (av3715) you have to keep the wireless switch on the "off"  position for the wireless to work propertly. After instaling ubuntu on mine I was connected to my wifi on less than 5 minutes!:p
