### Post by grobar on 2007-08-04
I have a standard mic plugged into my sound card (don't even know which one I have lol)

To install it use this procedure. It was a pain in the *** to find this, you don't need alsamixer and things like that...

1. right click on the sound icon (it's near date and time in the upper right corner)
2. chose open volume control and then go to edit---->preferances
3. click on CAPTURE (not line-in capture or cd capture just CAPTURE)
4. in "volume control" you see two tabs (playback and recording). In playback tab uncross the MIC (click on it) and put mic on max.
5. In recording tab put CAPTURE to max and uncross the icons below.

6. HAVE FUN
7. in skype you have to click CTRL+O (that's options and chose in sound devices for all 3 options hw1 or hw 0 and make a test call)
8. go to application----sound and video-----audacity to record something for fun :)
