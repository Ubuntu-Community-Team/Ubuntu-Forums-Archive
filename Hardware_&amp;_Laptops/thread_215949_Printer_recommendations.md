### Post by Kiddalee on 2006-07-14
Hi.  Have you looked at the documentation yet?  There are lists of printers with ratings of how well they work on [the Ubuntu Wiki]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsPrinters") and [LinuxPrinting.org]("http://www.linuxprinting.org/printer_list.cgi").
