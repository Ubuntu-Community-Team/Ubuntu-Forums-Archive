### Post by coolclassic on 2005-08-11
The printer is supported, in kde you would go to:
system>settings>Peripherals>printer

or you could go to [http://www.turboprint.de/english.html](http://www.turboprint.de/english.html) and install there software it is excellent and gives you an easy gui to work with which even shows inklevels.
