### Post by lonce on 2006-10-13
You dont want to change your warning temp.  That could smoke your processor by accident.  On a laptop there are very low tolerances from what I have seen.  Is it new or been used a while.  Make sure the fan is kicking on full speed.  You would be suprised what a can of air can do for you also.  If its getting that hot there is a problem that needs to be solved.  One time it might overheat and not start because the processor is smoked.  If its new talk to where you bought it from and maybe exchange it.
