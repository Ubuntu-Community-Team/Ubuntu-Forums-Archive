### Post by remin8 on 2005-07-02
I think that the problem of reading your pdf xls and doc files lies in your floppy drive not, ubuntu. I frequently use ubuntu to read pdfs and cread and read xls and doc files from winXP systems. I think you need to either format your disks in ubuntu then use them in windows (which really shouldn't make a difference), or get a new floppy drive. There is no real reason that I know why such a failure should occur other than hardware. I use my floppy rarely in ubuntu and it works. I would suggust getting a flash drive instead of a new floppy, everyone knows they are the coooooolest! :)
