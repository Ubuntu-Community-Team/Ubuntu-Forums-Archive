### Post by poe503 on 2007-09-13
Its probably your computer power supply.  Some supplies have "over voltage" protection and it can go south and give the kind of trouble you are describing.  I have only seen that in supplies that don't have a power switch on the back but have a green light.  I don't know of any fix other than getting another power supply.  Not hard to replace that.
