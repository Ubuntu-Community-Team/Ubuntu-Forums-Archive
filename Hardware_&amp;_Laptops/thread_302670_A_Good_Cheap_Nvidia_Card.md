### Post by marcozs on 2006-11-19
> **esaym said:**
> So what all higher end nvidia cards do you think could be had for under $50 used??

There are a few things you should consider:
1) Resolution you have on your screen (especially if it's an lcd)
2) Are you gonna use AGP or PCI-E?

I would suggest a Geforce 6600GT with 128Mb: you might find it for 50$ on ebay (I'm in Italy, saw one on ebay for 50 euros), it's a good card if you wanna play games at 800x600 or 1024x768 with decent details and you can use beryl/compiz without any problem (I tried it cause I had one) if you want some eye-candy desktop :D
