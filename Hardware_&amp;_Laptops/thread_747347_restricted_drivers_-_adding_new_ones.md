### Post by odin1965 on 2008-04-08
I think it only shows drivers that work on detected hardware in your computer. Try checking to see iff all repositories are enabled in Synaptic, especially the 'restricted'. Also, newer, less tested drivers will show up in 'backports' or 'proposed' repos, so you could add these as well. You could also hand edit your sources list, look here;
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=739119](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=739119)
