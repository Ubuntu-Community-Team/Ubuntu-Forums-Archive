### Post by fluffnik on 2006-08-17
ThinkPads from the T22 to T30 are old enough to be cheap but modern enough to live with and mostly JustWork(tm).  The three button nipple mice are the most X friendly pointing devices fitted to any laptop and the docking stations are excellent and dirt cheap second hand.

You can download excellent workshop manuals that make maintainance and upgrades a breeze - yes you can sensibly upgrade them.

ThinkPads in general rock and the splendid [ThinkWiki]("http://www.thinkwiki.org") will help you iron out any wrinkles.
