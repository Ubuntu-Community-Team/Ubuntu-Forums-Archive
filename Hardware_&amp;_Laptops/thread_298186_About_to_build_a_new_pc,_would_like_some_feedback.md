For one thing, not all games gain performance using SLI.  In fact, you have to switch out of SLI in some games to keep from losing performance.
In addition, you can pretty much count on each new generation of GPU being around twice as powerful as the preceding one(nVidia's new 8800 series for instance).  Therefore, I believe one is better off buying one card at a time but upgrading more often.:-k
