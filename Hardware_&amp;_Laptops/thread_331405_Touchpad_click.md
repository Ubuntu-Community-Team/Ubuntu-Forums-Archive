### Post by tkjacobsen on 2007-01-04
Go to xorg.conf
sudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf


And add the line:

        Option      "MaxTapTime" "0"

EDIT: Just corrected typing error above \EDIT

in the section that starts with something like this:
Section "InputDevice"
        Identifier  "Touchpad"
        Driver      "synaptics"
