### Post by t4thfavor on 2007-07-10
Intel or Nvidia graphics
3com or realtek nic (wired)
Atheros (wireless)
hp Printer (good linux driver support) psc-1210 works for me and its a scanner/printer/copier
USRobotics serial modem (sportster or v.everything courier) 
Stick to those guidelines and you shouldnt have much trouble.
