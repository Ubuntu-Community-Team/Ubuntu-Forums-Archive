### Post by Old *ix Geek on 2007-12-27
> **dawg said:**
> I don't know if you're aware of this, but if you install Ubuntu or anything else but VISTA you void your waranty.Not true, at least not in my experience.  I've owned nothing but HP (and now Compaq) for a long time now, and wiping windoze off a new system is the first thing I do.  It hasn't affected my warranties with HP in the slightest.  There is a catch though!

If you're going to send/take in an HP for repairs, be forewarned that "restoring the hard drive to its factory condition" (or something like that!) is part of what you're agreeing for them to do.  *I* refuse to let them do that, since that would mean having 'doze put back on it.  Most recently, my brand-new laptop (a dv6000) had a power-related failure and I told HP that I REFUSED to send the laptop in with its hard drive, because I didn't want them screwing up my Linux installation.  I shipped the laptop, sans hard drive, and they fixed the power problem and shipped it back--all under warranty. :)
