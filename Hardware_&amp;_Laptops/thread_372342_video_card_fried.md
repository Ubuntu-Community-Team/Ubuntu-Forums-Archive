### Post by Daveth on 2007-02-28
so, you forgot to connect the heatsink fan header to the motherboard but did connect the card power supply to the psu? I think the game playing episode may be bad news as that will have stressed the card a lot (when I got my 7900gs I tortured it to see the nice effects!). Were you playing long?
I think it depends a bit on how well your case cooling works, but if you gamed for some while, even this will not have shifted heat out from the gpu cooler. It's probably not too happy.
To be fair to you, I forgot to connect the power supply to my card when I put it in, so it tried to power itself from the sli socket- which is rubbish! So, easily done.
