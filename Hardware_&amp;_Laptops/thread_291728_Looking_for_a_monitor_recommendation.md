### Post by wererabbit on 2006-11-03
> **Cynical said:**
> Dell monitors are equal if not better than their Samsung equivalents. The only reason they are more expensive is because they include a 3 year warranty. /snip

NO!!! They are not.  I have done tech support for Dell boxes and monitors for several years now.  I've seen WAY too many problems with Dell LCDs to recommend them outright.  They are nice to start with but then they have a nasty habit of dying or developing pixel deaths.  They also tend to fade sooner than other LCDs do.  They charge you for the warranty, but the problem is that you may actually need it.  You'd be better off with either a Samsung, an NEC, or a modern Viewsonic lcd.  And if you want to be certain that the monitor doesn't have dead pixels, purchase it from a store like MicroCenter or Best Buy where you can try it out first and if you don't like it can then take it back.
