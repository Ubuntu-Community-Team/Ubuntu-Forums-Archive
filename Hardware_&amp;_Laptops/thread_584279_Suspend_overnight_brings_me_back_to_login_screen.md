I have a strange problem with my laptop and Gutsy. I use suspend through out the day with no issues (well mostly). But, on a few occasions I've left my laptop suspended overnight. When I open the lid in the morning I see the login screen. It's definitely coming out of suspend and the battery still has a decent charge on it, so I know it wasn't running all night.

This also happens whether it's plugged in or not.
