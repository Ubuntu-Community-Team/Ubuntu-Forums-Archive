### Post by casperiv on 2008-02-06
The problem is that the system you are using is very different than what most people would be overclocking.  Normally, you achieve your overclocking via adjustments to the voltages, multiplier, etc, in BIOS.  Since you don't have any of those functions, the next suggestion would be a utility, but due to oddness of the machine, you might be hard pressed to find any.

Do you have the documentation for the system or motherboard diagram?  Those switches do not sound like adjustments so much as setting for different processors.
