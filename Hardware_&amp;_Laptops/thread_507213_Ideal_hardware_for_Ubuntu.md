### Post by brent113 on 2007-07-22
NVidia has the most mature linux drivers, and Beryl and NVidia get along very well.  A few ati cards work, but for best compatibility, use NVidia.

Pretty much everything else, use whatever you want.  Ubuntu has excellent driver support right out of the box, and all major chipsets are supported.

I have an Intel motherboard, Intel 820D, NVidia 6800GTS, and a Seagate 500Gig 7200.9
