Hello all, to all the owners out there with laptops with Atheros wireless cards.

It was the first time I had actually used the built in wireless card since I bought the thing ( I deleted Vista as soon as I got the laptop).  I don't broadcast my SSID and there were some difficulties getting the wireless card to acquire my router, but I will attribute that to being a beta release.  I am sure that the final release will be better.  

I am going to just wait another month and get the official release of Feisty and be done with it.
