### Post by erfahren on 2007-12-30
you *could* try another bootable CD to see if it works. the GParted LiveCD is pretty small ~(50megs I think). 

as for your question about the possibility of a (partial) hardware failure but still being able to boot the Windows CD, I'm no hardware expert but I'd say that possibly could happen. I think when those CDs boot they first load a generic CD-ROM driver (don't they?). Maybe the driver on the Windows CD is a little better suited for the CD-ROM.

I'd definitely say that you'd tried enough burns to rule out it being a bad burn UNLESS the only ones you've tried are ones you've burned just recently. Maybe your CD-ROM is not burning correctly.
