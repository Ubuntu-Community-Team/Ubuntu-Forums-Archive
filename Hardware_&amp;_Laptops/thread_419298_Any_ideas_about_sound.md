I am fairly new to Linux and had a sound issue on my Dell XPS. Try Right clicking on the sound applet on the panel. Choose "Open Volume Control." Under "Playback" make sure the Master and PCM are turned up. For some reason ,mine were turned all the way down after I upgraded to feisty. Good Luck
