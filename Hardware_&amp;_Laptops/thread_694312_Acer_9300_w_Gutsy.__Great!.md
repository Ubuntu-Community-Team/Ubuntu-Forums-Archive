### Post by stevejesus on 2008-02-11
Alright, I just bought a new laptop.  The Acer Aspire 9300 series performance laptop.  Here is what it has;

AMD Turion X2 @ 1.8Ghz
1 gig of RAM
160 gig disk
DVD Burner

It has a 17inch widescreen and GeForce 6100 GO Graphics.

This is the third Acer laptop I've had.  All three have had the Atheros wireless chipset.  The first laptop (breezy) required NDISwrapper in order for the card to function.  This was my first experience with NDISwrapper...  Awful...  The second laptop (feisty), NDISwrapper again, but much less of a pain in the ***.
Finally, the atheros chipset in supported by default.  So, with that said, let me tell you what works on this laptop.

EVERYTHING!  Even the orbicam attached to the display.

This is a perfect Ubuntu laptop. Highly recommended\\:D/
