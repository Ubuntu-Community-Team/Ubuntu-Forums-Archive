### Post by hardyn on 2007-04-08
it sounds like you have answered most of your own questions...

keep an eye on what chipsets will be supported with what motherboards when selecting one... im sure that somebody is maintaining a list of supported network cards, i would have a look at the sound blaster, you bet nvidia, and you are on your own with the raid.

as a general rule of thumb, linux is not going to support the brand brand new stuff... do your homework and find out what will work... it might not be the newest on the market, but a fully functional older piece of hardware is better than a brand new device you cant use.

(what is purpose of a raid for a desktop system, just sounds like more to go wrong to me)

good luck.
