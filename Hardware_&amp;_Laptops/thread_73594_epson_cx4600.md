### Post by David Haas on 2005-10-09
danelb--I too see no driver for the Epson CX4600 in Ubuntu Hoary, either in ./gimp-print/4.2 or ./foomatic-ppds/Epson. A search in past Forum postings shows that others have had your problem. But, I see that Epson offers the driver for downloading. This is worth a try, though Ubuntu may not have the required dependencies. The site URI is <http://www.avasys.jp/english/linux_e/dl_spc.html>. I don't see an available driver at TurboPrint <http://www.turboprint.de/english.html>. I also don't see the CX4600 listed in the Linux compatability database at <http://www.linuxprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=Epson>

One person in a past forum indicated that the driver (PPD file) for the CX3200 worked for the CX 4600. This could be tried.

David
