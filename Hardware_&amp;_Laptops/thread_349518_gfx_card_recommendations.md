### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-01-30
If you want a PCI specifically, the "last" strong PCI video card was a 6200 made by Jaton -- a 338PCI. After that, I think everything went PCI-Express.

[http://db.jaton.com/VGAProductDetail.aspx?P_ID=84338KL-T256](http://db.jaton.com/VGAProductDetail.aspx?P_ID=84338KL-T256)

I had one for a while and really liked it. It's great if you don't have (or want) a good AGP solution.

If you want an AGP card, almost anything is an option. 6800s are cheap now. 7800s are coming down. ... I used to have a 7800GTX in a laptop, and it was an incredible card. 

Just some ideas. :D
