### Post by kacperpl1 on 2008-02-15
No atx mobo's for those cpu's but u can buy small form factor one's. The bes one is IWILL ZMAX-D2
IT has NF4PRO2200 up to dual opterons 285's and sli and nice integrated sound device but it isnt cheap. see this [http://www.bit-tech.net/hardware/2005/05/25/preview_zmaxd2/1](http://www.bit-tech.net/hardware/2005/05/25/preview_zmaxd2/1)
Also if you decide to buy it notice that u will have usb wifi adapter if u're in wireless network and sff boards arent good for normal series of opterons so check if yours are HE or OSP series OEM's and make sure your graphic cards are fitiing in the small form factor case and are using only one slot if u have two of them for sli mode.
