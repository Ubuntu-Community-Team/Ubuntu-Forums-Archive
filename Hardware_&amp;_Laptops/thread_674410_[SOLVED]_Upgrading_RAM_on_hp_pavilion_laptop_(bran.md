### Post by Existentialist on 2008-01-25
Did you replace all of your ram or did you mix some of the original ram with the new stuff?  If you are running the new and old at the same time try running the comp with just the new stuff, as BIOS may not be setting the timings correctly between the different modules.  It is possible you received bad ram and may have to exchange it, but if it fits in the slots you should have the right type of ram.
