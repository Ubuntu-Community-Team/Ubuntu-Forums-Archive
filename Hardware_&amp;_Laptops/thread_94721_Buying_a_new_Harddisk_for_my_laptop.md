### Post by hw-tph on 2005-11-25
First you need to check what physical sizes your laptop supports when it comes to hard drives. There are currently two standard heights in 2.5" disk drives - 12mm and 9.5mm. Most laptops will accept 12mm and thus be able to fit both sizes but a few laptops - mostly notebooks and ultra-portables - will only fit a 9.5mm drive.
