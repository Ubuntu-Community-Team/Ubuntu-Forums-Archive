I am very happy with my brand new ThinkPad R61. It is a sturdy machine, love the conservative looks.

I have the 1280x800 x3100 screen and card. It is not working with compiz yet, and sound is dead. I suppose I will get these things fixed, or the next release of Ubuntu will fix'em.

If you can live with a little buggyness in the beginning of your machines lifespan, I say go with the R61! It is a great machine, sturdy as hell, and guess what - you can spill water on the keyboard.

If you get one, keep me posted on you development with the flaws.
