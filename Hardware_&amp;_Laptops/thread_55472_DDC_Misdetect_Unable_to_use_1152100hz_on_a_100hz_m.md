Hey Guys, 

The problem I'm having is that with my philips 109B monitor, X seems to default to 85hz at 1152X864 no matter what I do. 

This problem appears related to DDC, as I have the same problem under windows, unless I untick the "Use DDC information" in the ati driver control panel, after which it works perfectly at 100hz. (confirmed by the OSD.)

Under Ubuntu, however, I have tried several things all to no avail. 

First, I tried adding Option "NoDDC" to the device section of /etc/X11/xorg.conf. 

This forced made my X start at 640X480, 60hz. Fair enough, I still need to define the Hsync and the Vsync.
So I also added this to the Device section:
	HorizSync	30-97
	VertRefresh	50-160
(values taken from monitors pdf manual)
