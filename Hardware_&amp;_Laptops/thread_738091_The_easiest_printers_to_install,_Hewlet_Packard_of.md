### Post by peterthewolf on 2008-03-28
This got my C5180 working as a network printer and on another PC as a usb printer.

Go to the site below and download the  HPLIP Self Extracting Archive With Installer and then just follow the instructions on that page and of course the ensuing screen directions.
Because it is easy to overlook make sure that before you start you DISABLE the CD in synaptic repositories

[http://hplip.sourceforge.net/downloads.html](http://hplip.sourceforge.net/downloads.html)

I presume that the installer will work for most HP printers although I cannot try it out as I only have the one.

HP are to be congratulated for providing this sort of support and when buying a new printer the linux community should support them.:)
