### Post by Mithrilhall on 2008-02-27
There are two schools of thought when it comes to that question.

1. Reboot puts more wear and tear on your drive than leaving it running.
2. Leaving your computer on brings down the lifespan of your drive.


[http://wiki.answers.com/Q/Should_a_computer_be_shut_down_when_not_in_use](http://wiki.answers.com/Q/Should_a_computer_be_shut_down_when_not_in_use)
