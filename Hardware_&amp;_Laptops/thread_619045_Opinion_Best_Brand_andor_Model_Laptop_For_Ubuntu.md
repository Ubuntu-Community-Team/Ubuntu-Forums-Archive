### Post by jespdj on 2007-11-21
I don't know if you'll get any valid results out of this poll, because most people haven't tried a whole list of different laptops from different brands to see how well they run Ubuntu.

I can only tell you that it runs great on my Dell XPS M1330. Everything works with Ubuntu 7.10 out of the box (including wireless networking and the webcam) except the internal microphone, and that problem was easily fixed with a [patch](https://bugs.launchpad.net/dell/+bug/147682).

If you want a laptop that is guaranteed to work well, buy a Dell or System76 which comes with Linux pre-installed.
