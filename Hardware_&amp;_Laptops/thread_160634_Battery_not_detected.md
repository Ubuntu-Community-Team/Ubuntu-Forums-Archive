### Post by YuHoo on 2006-04-17
apm and acpi are only management so I'd rule those out for now. It sounds like your battery is the problem. When your computer boots up, I want you to enter the bios. It's when you usually have a splash screen of the logo and maybe some numbers. It will say something on the screen like "press del to enter setup" and it is usually very quick at the boot up. There may be battery options as well as some controls or readings. If those show it with 0% charge, it is your battery that is the problem.
