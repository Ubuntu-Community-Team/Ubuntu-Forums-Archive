### Post by stuporglue on 2005-10-29
Go to the System-->Preferences-->Keyboard preferences. 

In the "Layout Options" tab, open the "CapsLock key behavior" menu. I'm not sure the *exact* function you're looking for, but "CapsLock acts as Shift with locking." might be it.
