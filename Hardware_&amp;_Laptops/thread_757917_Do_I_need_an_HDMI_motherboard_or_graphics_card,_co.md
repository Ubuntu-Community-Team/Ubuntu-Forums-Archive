You are the only one who can answer this.  No one here knows at all if you have any need for HDMI.  If you need HDMI, then either of these should work.  HDMI on the motherboard is the same as on the Video Card, since the only thing HDMI is used for is Video, it would be coming off the motherboard's Video Card if it was on the motherboard.
