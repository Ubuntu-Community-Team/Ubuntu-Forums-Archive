### Post by a.buck on 2008-02-25
The Microsoft Fingerprint Keyboard with Fingerprint reader and the USB Fingerprint readers are based on technology designed/developed by Digital Persona.  ([www.digitalpersona.com](www.digitalpersona.com))

In looking at Digital Persona's support pages, it appears that their readers are only supported under windows operating systems.  

There are two pieces to the digital personal solution.  The driver, and the software.  Even if a linux driver was written, that still leaves a gap regarding the software.  Both pieces would need to be ported.

I don't think this is going to happen........as much as I want it to.

Hope I'm wrong.
