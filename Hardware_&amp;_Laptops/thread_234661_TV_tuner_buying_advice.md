### Post by futz on 2006-08-12
I've owned 4 different (all non-HD) TV cards. Hated every one except my current Leadtek TV2000 XP Expert w/Remote. Best TV card I ever used! Even comes with a reasonably useable remote control, unlike most other cards I owned (most ship with junk remotes).

The Leadtek is in my big Windows box, so I don't know how well it works in Linux. I have another one of these cards, but haven't installed it in a Linux box yet. You may want to google around and see how well the Expert works with Linux.

I also have an MSI card in my Fedora box that was hideous to use in Windows, but in Linux it's quite good. The hardware is fairly decent, but MSI's Windows software is pathetic - incredibly bad! Once I got it working in Linux the card is quite nice to use.
