### Post by dedmonds on 2008-04-18
David,

I have the same problem on a T61. I found out that I could change the brightness by pressing Ctrl-Alt-F2 to get to a console, then pressing the brightness buttons to change the screen brightness, and then pressing Alt-F7 to return from the console. It might be worth a shot on your Z-series.
