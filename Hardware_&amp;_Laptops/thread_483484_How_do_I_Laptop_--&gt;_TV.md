### Post by Glenhawk on 2007-06-24
Use an S-Video cable to connect your PC to the TV (cable that looks like it has ps2 (keyboard/mouse) plugs on each end) and an audio cable with RCA plugs on one end and a headphone plug on the other (headphone plug into the laptop, RCA into the TV)
