### Post by finni on 2008-03-14
Using the intel drivers created system-wide hang ups when screensaver were activated or laptop's lid was closed. See more info on the matter at the launchpad; [https://bugs.launchpad.net/xorg-server/+bug/138256](https://bugs.launchpad.net/xorg-server/+bug/138256) (In short: By adding a PipeA-tag  to the xorg.conf helped to fix these hangup issues, but that also adds to power usage of the laptop, which means less battery life)
