### Post by o_fortuna on 2006-01-19
[QUOTE=Noremacam]Is it possible to get middle mouse button scrolling, as one does on windows? I'm talking about when you click the middle mouse button(assuming you're not over a link), and then move the mouse up and down to scroll. Is there a way to get a feature like that on ubuntu?[/QUOTE]
At least for Firefox, go to Edit-->Preferences, click Advanced, and check the "used autoscrolling" button. This will allow middle-button scrolling in Firefox. I've yet to figure out how to do this with all applications though.
