### Post by Espionage724 on 2007-04-27
Here is how I got sound working on the Acer TravelMate 2480-xxx Laptop
I assume this will work also with the sound cards Realtek HD and Intel HDA

*Clean installed Kubuntu 7.04 (Doesn't matter which distro you install)
*When to mixer settings
*Turned on all sounds (3D, Surround, etc) and maxed their volume
*Thats it
When ever you first install ubuntu your volume is muted for some reason. There is no need to install any other device.
