### Post by er-ku on 2005-12-17
well.... 
[LIST]
[*]if you SEE Ubuntu working, it means, screen works, hence, no driver is needed
[*]If sound works, you don't need the sound driver
[*]If modem works or you don't use it, you don't need the modem driver.
[*]same applies for everything else...
[/LIST]
Generally, forget these drivers. Drivers for most of the new hardware that works fine with Linux are distributed along with Ubuntu itself. Something might not work, but often it's just a matter of configuration rather than the matter of missing drivers. If, however, something won't work for you, don't hesitate to check these forums and post a new topic with more specific question here.
