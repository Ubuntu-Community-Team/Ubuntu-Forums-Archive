### Post by K.Mandla on 2006-05-13
If it's not turning on at all, it's probably hardware related. I believe system fans should come on at startup, regardless of what software you're using.

If I were you, I wouldn't turn it on until you can get a replacement fan and at least test it. If a new fan doesn't come on, think about testing the motherboard. But be careful. Processors will turn into smoke in a matter of seconds without proper cooling.
