### Post by aggiechemist on 2005-07-22
I'd suggest making a live CD. Obviously you will need another computer to burn the live CD, but if you can get to one then that is a great solution. Boot from the live CD, then try and mount and inspect the hard drive. A live CD from any distro would do.

Hope that helps.
