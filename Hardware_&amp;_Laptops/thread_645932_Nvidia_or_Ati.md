### Post by jespdj on 2007-12-22
The drivers for nVidia cards for Linux are in general much better than the drivers for ATI cards, so go with an nVidia.

WIth ATI cards, you're much more likely to get display driver problems, have desktop effects not working, etc. AMD/ATI has promised months ago that they would improve their Linux drivers, but at the moment it looks like they still do not work really well.

My laptop has a 8400M GS and it works well. The 8600M is a step more powerful than the 8400M.
