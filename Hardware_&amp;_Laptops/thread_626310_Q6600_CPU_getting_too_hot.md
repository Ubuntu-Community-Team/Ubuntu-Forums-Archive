### Post by Tlon on 2007-11-29
I'm running a QX6700 at 2.95 GHz (over-clocked one multiple), and it runs 25C idle and 50C max load (Crysis at high settings under XP).  This is with a Zalman proc fan.  I do also have 2x12cm and 1x8cm case fans though.  Still, even with stock parts and a crappy case, there's no way your chip should be getting that high.  Re-seat and re-grease the sucker.  It won't last long at that temp.  I'm actually kind of suspicious that it's even functional... maybe your sensors are what's wrong.
