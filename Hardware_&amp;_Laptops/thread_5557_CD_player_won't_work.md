### Post by bob k on 2004-11-19
I have found that  if you synaptic the  xmms modules and set the alsa driver in the perf screen  to digital then the sound will work.

You may have to play with the settings to get the snap crackle pop, out of the audio. but once that is done. It is done.

Oh I forgot once you get it working you have to make xmms the default audio player. There is one problem I have found if you use xmms, is ejecting the CD. That is the only reason I still have CD-Player on my system
