### Post by sillyxone on 2008-02-28
When suspend, all off except the memory (RAM) so an average laptop can only sleep for a few days. When hibernate, everything from RAM is written to HDD then power off, so no power consumed.

From my experience, most rechargeable batteries discharge themselves over time (even if not in use), except Alkaline one (very slow or none discharge)

Can you specify more how you figure out that 7% lost? Gutsy sometimes showed my laptop battery at 100% although the charging light was still on for another 5 minutes. Dell sent me a new battery after the first year (can only last 20 minutes at the time). Now after 2 months, the new battery stays at 95% (reported by Gutsy) when fully charged.
