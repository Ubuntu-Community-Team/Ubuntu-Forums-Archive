### Post by athertek on 2007-08-22
I am trying to build up a hardware compatibility list for Ubuntu at [www.ubuntuhcl.org](www.ubuntuhcl.org).

I was motivated to do this about a month ago when shopping for a new computer. I wanted something that would work, out of the box, just like Windows. I must have spent 15 to 20 hours researching compatibility on the internet, it was a real pain. And at the end of it I came out with a good idea of what I wanted, but still not even perfect. I've had the computer for a few weeks, and I still haven't chosen a keyboard and mouse.

So please visit today, [www.ubuntuhcl.org](www.ubuntuhcl.org). This is a community driven project so we need the community to contribute! I've posted about 19 reviews on there so far (my username is samb0057). We have a ways to go but the site is actually starting to pick up some hits, I'm starting to see daily hits from google and netscape.
