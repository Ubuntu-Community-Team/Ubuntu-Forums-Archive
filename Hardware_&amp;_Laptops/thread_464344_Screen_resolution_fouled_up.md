### Post by LeeM on 2007-06-04
Just found the cure, although I still don't know what happened. I found the backup version of /etc/X11/xorg.conf, copied the Monitor and Screen content out of it into the current version, crossed my fingers and restarted.  Voila! High resolution (actually 1280X1024) again!
