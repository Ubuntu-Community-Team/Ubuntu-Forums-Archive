### Post by zgoda on 2006-07-10
If you are running on 686 kernel, try with 386 version. Anyway, I couldn't get beyond 40 minutes with my HP nx6110 (compare this with about 3 hours with Ubuntu Breezy 5.10).
I think it's a matter of some bug introduced in recent kernels, specially visible in 686 versions. These issues have been reported in Malone and we wait for fix. See [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=204100") to get more information.
