### Post by Griff on 2007-12-07
I got a HPTX1205US from circuit city (i know, but it was cheaper here than newegg, tigerdirect, etc) and it runs flawlessly now.  Installing the 32bit wireless drivers was easy with ndiswrapper (no wireless issues).  Compiz-fusion runs super smooth with no lag.  There's also a thread here on the forums that help you quickly get a couple minor issues fixed.  It might not fit you definition of 'super compatible', but with minor tweeking you have a fully operational machine.  And having a AMD Turion 64 dual core with 1GB ram and nvidia graphics (go6150) on a 12.1" widescreen is pretty sweet for $850 bucks.
