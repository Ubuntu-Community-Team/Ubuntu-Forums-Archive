### Post by mattmyers83 on 2007-05-30
I have a p105-s171 In order to get my sound working I had to physically remove my modem card from the bottom.  Then I had to do the ubuntu updates and install the latest kernel.  Then my sound worked.  This is the not so spanish way of doing it.  By default with Ubuntu 7 automatically thinks your modem is your default sound card.  Removing the modem and updating the linux kernel fixed this issue for me.
