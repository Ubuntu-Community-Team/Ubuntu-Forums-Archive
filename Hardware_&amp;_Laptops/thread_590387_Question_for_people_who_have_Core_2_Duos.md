I'm running 7.10 with relatively little problems.  One thing I did notice though is that my CPU is seems abnormally active, even when I am doing nothing (or at least I think I am doing nothing).  

I have an Intel Core 2 Duo @ 2.66 GHz.  When I check the system monitor, one of the cores will be at 80-100% while the other is at around 20-30% and it constantly fluctuates.  Is that supposed to happen?

### Post by rsambuca on 2007-10-24
Turn on your System Monitor (System -> Administration), and click on the 'Processes' tab.  It will show you what is running, and how much of your cpu it is using.

### Post by rorestuff on 2007-10-24
I have Core 2 Duo and I think this is fairly normal. Most apps can only use 1 of your cores at a time, so if you have a CPU intensive app (eg. Firefox), this will happen.
