### Post by JurB on 2006-11-23
Mythtv might be overkill if you just want to watch tv.
Tvtime is the best quality tv app for ubuntu, and it's in the repositories.
You can open synaptic and search for the "tvtime" package or type```
sudo apt-get install tvtime
``` in the terminal.
