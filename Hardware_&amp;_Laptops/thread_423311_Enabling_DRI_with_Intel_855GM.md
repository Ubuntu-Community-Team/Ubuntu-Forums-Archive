### Post by Dr. Z2A on 2007-04-25
Hey all.  I'm currently attempting to get DRI to work with my graphics card (which is an Intel 855GM), but have been having trouble with it.  I checked and I have a "load    "dri"" line in my xorg.conf and have "mode 0666" in there as well, but when I run glxinfo I get "direct rendering: No".  Does anyone know how I would enable DRI?

EDIT: found solution.  I had to add these lines under the device section in my xorg.conf:
>        
 VideoRam        65536
Option          "NoAccel"       "false"
Option          "DRI"           "true"
