### Post by cosmiccharles on 2007-10-25
Ubuntu and my Dell work GREAT together. I actually have the same exact model laptop. I got it a little under a year ago. It came stock with Vista home edition which totally sucked. I tried Ubuntu, Feisty Fawn and I have just recently upgraded to Gusty Gibbon and everything seems to work better than ever. The key with installing or dual booting Windows on your machine is that it has to be setup and partitioned first before you ever think about setting up Ubuntu. Once you partition and format your Windows you are set to install Ubuntu with ease, just remember to allocate space for both and you are set. Happy installing!
