### Post by arvevans on 2007-06-12
My Tecra 8100 laptop has an internal 56K modem.  The phone jack is on the right side near the back.
There is a USB 2.0 jack on the rear, near the right corner.

With no docking station I don't have any Ethernet port so I use a PCMCIA Ethernet card in one of the two PCMCIA slots.  The docking station contains an Ethernet interface.  This gives me two Ethernet interfaces and allows me to have the internal PCMCIA one configured for the office network and the docking station one configured for my home LAN.
