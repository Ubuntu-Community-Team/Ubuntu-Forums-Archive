### Post by zgornel on 2007-04-30
It should all work but you never know ... Anyway, that's why the forum is here ;) The things you should check after the install are: wireless functionality, full suspend/hibernate functionality, dma support and system responsiveness, native screen resolution support, audio quality and special buttons behaviour/support.
