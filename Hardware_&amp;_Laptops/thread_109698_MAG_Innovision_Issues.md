### Post by freddy33 on 2007-11-02
Thanks a lot, I had some resolution problem with my Mag screen and you gave me ideas.

I look into Mag specifications of my screen (BP2219W) and found that I need for all Wide Screen and non wide screen resolutions 1680x1050:
Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"Mag Innovision"
	Option		"DPMS"
        HorizSync 30-80
        VertRefresh 55-75
EndSection

And I finally managed to get full wide screen working properly.
So, from reading your xorg.conf I found some strange things:
1600x1200 is not wide screen and makes my screen flash from time to time.
Your vertical frequency (50-160) sounds like a big range for me. At 1600x1200 I doubt you can still do 75Hz (like in Windows).
So, is your Mag a wide screen?
If yes remove the 1600x1200 mode at the beginning of your mode list.
Hope it helps, and thanks anyway.
