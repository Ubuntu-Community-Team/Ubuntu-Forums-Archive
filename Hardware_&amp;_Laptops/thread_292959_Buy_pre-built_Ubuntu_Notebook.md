### Post by Joe_CoT on 2006-11-04
[System 76](http://www.system76.com/) Sells Ubuntu laptops, as does [Linuxcertified.com](http://www.linuxcertified.com); System 76 has a support forum on these forums.

If you're willing to install it yourself, and are comfortable without commercial support, you might be better off just buying a laptop and installing it afterwards; there's numerous online resources for installing linux on laptops, and numerous resellers who sell no-OS laptops. You tend to pay a premium on hardware with linux already installed.
