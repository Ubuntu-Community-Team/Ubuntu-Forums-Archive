### Post by UnderMine on 2007-11-06
I have just got an Alto delivered today. I am currently using Fedora on this laptop but I have already noticed my typing speed is increasing as my fingers get used to the normal sized keys.

Initial impressions are good however as expected the multimedia keys are not detected correctly by default. However they are detectable and just need key-mapping to the appropriate functions as opposed to my acer laptop that needs a kernel driver :S

The Alto is reasonably light and I must say I am getting very used to it.

The only possible issues are if you need access to any ports on the front of your laptop i.e. headphone, line in, memory cards etc. there is very little clearance but a right angle 3.5mm connector should solve this.

Just my thoughts.
