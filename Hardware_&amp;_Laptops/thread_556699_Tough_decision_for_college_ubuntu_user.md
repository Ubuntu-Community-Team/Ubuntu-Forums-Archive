### Post by strabes on 2007-09-21
You're not going to get good speakers inside a laptop, so forget about it. The Dell Latitudes (the business laptops) are more rugged than the Inspirons. The Latitude D630 has a 14.1" screen and be powered up pretty good for under $1500.

I have the Inspiron E1705. I bought it the summer before this past one and am really happy with it. The only thing I'd absolutely say is to get the CompleteCare warranty. Especially since you're carrying it around to and from classes. (I also do this) The CompleteCare warranty protects against accidental damage so if you drop it and it explodes you can get a new one for free in a couple days.
