Getting wifi to work in Ubuntu was a pain in the arse for me. First, what model and manufacturer is the adapter? You'll most likely have to use NDISWRAPPER to get it to work in linux...it basically uses the windows drivers IN linux. Do a search on whatever model it is and ndiswrapper...might find something to help.
