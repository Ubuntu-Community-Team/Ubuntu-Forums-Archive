### Post by howcome on 2005-07-26
Today I fixed two problems that have prevented me from using Hoary on my Thinkpad X30. The problems were a) that suspend did not work reliably and b) that the machine did not output external video through the VGA port, Both problems were fixed by setting options in  /etc/X11/xorg.conf. This is a snippet from the new file:

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Intel Corporation 82830 CGC [Chipset Graphics Controller]"
	Driver		"i810"
	BusID		"PCI:0:2:0"
	Option		"VBERestore"		"true"
	Option		"CheckLid"		"false"
	VideoRam 	131072
EndSection
