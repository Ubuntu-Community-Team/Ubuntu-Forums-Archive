### Post by vakont on 2006-12-03
Hmmm... my LG SATA DVD burner is VERY fast, it turns on and off during boot! Then it sleeps! Sorry, that was a joke.
I wish I could make mine to work to tell you exactly about SATA DVD burning speed.

What I can tell you though is that the speed differs, depending on what parts of the disc the burner is actually writing. It has to do with the mechanics and the disc layout.

Usually the maximum speed is achieved as the laser reaches towards the outter part of the CD/DVD itself, because the actual vertical-linear speed gets higher in those areas. So, there is nothing wrong with your drive, as much as physics and structure is involved. I don't know if there are other side-techs in this, but that's what I have observed through various burners.
