### Post by Sef on 2006-01-02
Since you've got a laptop, just get a pcmcia card that will slip in the slot.  

Will you be using it for dial up connection or broadband?

If it's the latter, get a broadband/lan pcmcia card.   I have a netgear card for it and never have had a problem with it.   If it's for the former, then get a pcmcia card for dial up,  if you are dialing up.
