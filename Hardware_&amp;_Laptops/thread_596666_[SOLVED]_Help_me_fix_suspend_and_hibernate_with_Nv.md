### Post by justsomedude on 2007-10-29
Ok, sorry I bump this, I just solved it myself and thought I let you know how in case anyone is interested or does a forum search for this problem:

Changes made to */etc/default/acpi-support*:

POST_VIDEO=false
SAVE_VBE_STATE=false

plus disabling VSynch in Compiz did the trick, I should have read the English Wiki before posting this thread. I read the German Wiki, the SAVE_VBE_STATE information is missing there... :oops:
