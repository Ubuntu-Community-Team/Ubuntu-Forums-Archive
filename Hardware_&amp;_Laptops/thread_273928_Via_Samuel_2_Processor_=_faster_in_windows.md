The processors are very complex you can not compare them just by their clock speed. It is like comparing two engines by their redline.
Your celeron is a much faster cpu on the other hand the VIA draws less power and does not dissipate as much as the celeron.

Even so the sluggishness more likely to be the result of the vga driver.
