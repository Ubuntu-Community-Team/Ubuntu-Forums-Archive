Well, I guess you picked up the X31 because its tiny? Well tiny comes with its consequences. I use a X31 with Feisty and I am very pleased with how it runs. Many things work outta the box, even the hot keys. However, this laptop lacks a optical drive so installing ubuntu on it (I had windows before) took a little research. As for battery life, I can't really tell you because this laptop is old and they probably came out with new batteries. But I can sit in a 1.5 hour class and type in MS office with wine and wireless on mute and lowest brightness and my battery will be down to about 10% when the class is over. That's because the laptop is small and the battery is small.
Over all, the X31 is very stable, low on specs (dont do linux gaming on it), but very portable.
Hope this helped!
