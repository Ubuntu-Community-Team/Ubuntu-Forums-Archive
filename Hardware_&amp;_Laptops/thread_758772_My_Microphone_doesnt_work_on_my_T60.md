### Post by pseudo-random on 2008-04-18
The default setup with regards to sound recording is pretty poor. Do this:
Double click your volume icon to open your mixer.
Enter the preferences and select EVERYTHING.
Now go back to your mixer and have a look at the Recording tab.
You need to experiment with selecting different capture channels until you find THE ONE.
Using your Sound recorder application try different inputs until it works.
You should only ever need to do this once and then its done forever (until you fiddle with it that is)
