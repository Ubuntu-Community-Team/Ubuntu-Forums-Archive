### Post by zonk on 2006-01-04
Hi peewee.

My girlfriend has a Lg Lw40 and I experienced the same problems on it. Battery emptys very fast, fn Keys not working, ....

I could maybe help you a little. For example if you turn of your wireless before shutting the computer down the lw40 turns off without problems. This is done by using the Fn-F6 key combination. (this one was working out of the box).

Sound really should be working with Alsa.

For further reading I would suggest to take a look at: [http://www.linux-on-laptops.com](http://www.linux-on-laptops.com)
