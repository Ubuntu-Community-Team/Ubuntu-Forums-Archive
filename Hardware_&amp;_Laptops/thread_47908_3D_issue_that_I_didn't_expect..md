To expand what this means in case it wasn't entirely clear to you.

Goto System->Administration->Users & groups

Find the user you wish to give access to the 3d card.

Click the user and then click properties.

Click the user Privileges tab at the top of the page.

Select use hardware acceleartion and then click okay until your out of User & groups menus.
