### Post by zxee on 2006-08-14
One way to approach this is to use the sane supported devices page:
[http://www.sane-project.org/sane-mfgs.html](http://www.sane-project.org/sane-mfgs.html)
And you can shop ebay or elsewhere to look what available and then check if it is supported. Many usb HP scanners are supported but be very careful to check out all of what sane tells about a potential scanner i.e "good" support is not the same as "complete".
