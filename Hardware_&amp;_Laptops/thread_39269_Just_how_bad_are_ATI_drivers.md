### Post by zlogic on 2005-06-15
Installing drivers is not more difficult than nVidia.
However, nVidia has better support for its products under Linux and it seems that they offer more freedom in using it (I remember when ATi drivers were only RPMs which trashed the whole Mesa opengl library and xserver on my Mandrake box). Or how the provided RPM trashed my Fedora xorg.conf so that I had to fix it by hand. Now ATi is improving, they're already offering a non-RPM specific version of the driver along with a GUI installer. Nice, but I haven't tried it because I've replaced my Radeon 9200 with an nvidia Geforce 4 MX. The main reason was bad driver support under Linux, but ATI are now definetly improving.
