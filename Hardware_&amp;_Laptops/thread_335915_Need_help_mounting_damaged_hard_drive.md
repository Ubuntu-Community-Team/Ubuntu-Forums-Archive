### Post by meng on 2007-01-10
Here is the first step:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MountingWindowsPartitions](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MountingWindowsPartitions)
Above all, take your time! If you aren't sure, ask! Try to understand what you're doing before you do it. After all, that data isn't going anywhere, and the last thing you want to do is screw it up. (Fortunately, Ubuntu doesn't have the capability to WRITE to an NTFS partition by default, so you'll probably be safe even if you don't know what you're doing.)
