A KVM switch, like mine, is purely hardware, the OS doesn't know about it.

Here is mine, [http://www.amazon.com/IOGEAR-GCS62-MiniView-Micro-Switch/dp/B00006IRR8/ref=pd_bbs_1/002-9379475-6117603?ie=UTF8&s=electronics&qid=1188932747&sr=8-1](http://www.amazon.com/IOGEAR-GCS62-MiniView-Micro-Switch/dp/B00006IRR8/ref=pd_bbs_1/002-9379475-6117603?ie=UTF8&s=electronics&qid=1188932747&sr=8-1)

Works in every OS I used it, although that isn't important, because it has nothing to do with the OS.

To switch, press Scroll Lock twice. Some have a physical switch.

If you have a USB mouse, you can get a USB to PS/2 converter, my mouse came with one. [http://www.amazon.com/Keyboard-Mouse-Adapter-Pin-Type/dp/B000CDFYLU/ref=sr_1_8/002-9379475-6117603?ie=UTF8&s=electronics&qid=1188932884&sr=1-8](http://www.amazon.com/Keyboard-Mouse-Adapter-Pin-Type/dp/B000CDFYLU/ref=sr_1_8/002-9379475-6117603?ie=UTF8&s=electronics&qid=1188932884&sr=1-8) (don't know about this one, you might want to look around and read reviews, but it should be fine.)
