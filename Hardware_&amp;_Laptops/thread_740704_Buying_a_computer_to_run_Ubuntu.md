### Post by CrimsoniteX on 2008-03-31
8 gigs of RAM huh? Makes me curious why you need that much :)

Anyway, I would recommend building it from scratch. It's a good learning experience, and not nearly as hard as it seems. Order all your parts from newegg.com as they tend to have the lowest prices. Here is a list of what you would need:

-Processor
-Processor fan (w/ mounting bracket)
-Motherboard (one that supports 8 gigs of memory)
-Video Card
-Sound Card (if not integrated into motherboard, most times they are)
-Hard Drive (try to stick with SATA)
-Media Drive (ie DVD Burner)
-Memory
-System Case
-Power Supply (at least 430 watts)
-System fans (one 80mm should do you fine for now)
-Thermal grease (may come with your processor)
-An extra SATA cable

If you would like help putting finding particular parts, let me know and I would be happy to help.
