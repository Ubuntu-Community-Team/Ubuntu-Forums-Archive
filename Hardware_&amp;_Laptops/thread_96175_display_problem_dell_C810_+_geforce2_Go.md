### Post by stults on 2006-01-24
Don't know if you found your answers yet, but I thought I would throw in my two cents.  For the screen resolution you can resize it.  On my Inspiron 8200 I press Fn+F7(Font) and the screen will adjust itself.  A very nice feature!

As for the mouse issue, you may not have the mouse properly configured you should try to either run the xorg config or use a different mouse driver in your /etc/X11/xorg.conf file.  Good Luck!
