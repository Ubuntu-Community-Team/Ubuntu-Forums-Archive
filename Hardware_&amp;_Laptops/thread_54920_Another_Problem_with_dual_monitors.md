### Post by foolsh on 2005-08-21
one thing I see is that in your xorg.conf the secondary device.....

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"deviceSecondary"
	Driver		"vesa"
	BusID		"PCI:0:5:0"
EndSection

is pointing to.....

0000:00:05.0 IDE interface: Silicon Integrated Systems [SiS]: Unknown device 0180 (rev 01)

an ide interface.......

on top of that it is unknown

try removing all pci cards and installing the pci video card in a different slot until lspci shows somethin like "*nnnn*:*nn*:*nn*.*n* VGA compatible controller: #your device#"
then add the info to your xorg.conf
