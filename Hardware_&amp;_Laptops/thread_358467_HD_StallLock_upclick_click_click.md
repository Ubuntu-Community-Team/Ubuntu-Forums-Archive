Take it out of your PC, wrap it in something waterproof, place it in you freezer for ~60 minutes, then take it out, leave it at room temperature for ~30 minutes and then put it back in your PC.

If - and this is still a big if - you can access data, copy it off as quickly as you can before the drive warms up.

Sometimes marginal read errors on a drive are readable when the drive is cool, so this procedure can help in that particular situation.
