### Post by allwin on 2007-02-01
Go see if you can find the volume control app and turn it up.  There's two, the master, and the PCM.  Master might be turned up high, and PCM set too low.  Turn PCM up to about 80%, higher if it doesn't sound distorted.  GNOME...you add the volume app to the panel, right click and select it.  KDE, I think it's called KMIX or KMIXER and you can just run it.

Forgive me if you've tried that already...
