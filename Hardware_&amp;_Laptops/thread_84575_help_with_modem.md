Many *nix users (yes, inc. *BSD) would probably recommend US Robotics External 56K modems.

They work 100%, as they are 100% hardware based - connected via your COM port at the back (/dev/tty).

They go for peanuts on eBay (I paid about £5 or something). You can even get them working under the command-line, with wvdial (I think).  I used one to build a Linux-based dial-up Gateway for my LAN, before we had ADSL.
