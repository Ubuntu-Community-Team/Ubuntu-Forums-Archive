### Post by EndPerform on 2007-06-13
Usually brands such as Dell / Compaq / HP tend to be a bit proprietary when it comes to board sizes and what they'll fit in.  My guess would be no, it's not going to fit properly.  As for power supplies, as long as it's a good quality power supply and has enough wattage to power everything, you should be ok.  But that brings up another potential issue.  The power connectors on the Compaq board might also be proprietary, and the standard connectors on the PS in the new case would not work.  Just a couple of things for you to think about.
