### Post by jimbren on 2006-11-07
The only app that I'm aware of for linux that will let you use the Zen for data storage is [Gnomad2]("www.gnomad2.sourceforge.net").

You can't just mount a Creative player as a USB mass storage device due to the closed-source filesystem that Creative uses.

Hope that helps...


jimbo
