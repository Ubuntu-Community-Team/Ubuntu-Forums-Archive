### Post by Herman on 2008-02-21
[UbuntuHCL.org]("http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/")                    is the new Ubuntu Linux Hardware Compatibility Site from fellow Ubuntu enthusiast samb0057. 
Take a look around in there and see if anyone has written a review on computer parts that interests you.
While you're there help your fellow Ubuntu users by entering details of hardware that you own that you know does work well with Ubuntu so others will know what to shop for.
If enough people join UbuntuHCL.org then it might help to put a little pressure on manufacturers to make hardware that's operating system independent. I think we're still only a tiny minority so far, but things are changing and people like you and I do make a difference.
