### Post by LazyTownRocks on 2007-07-02
I've been running an M2 with 6.06 Dapper-Drake for 6 months and it's fine with no over-temp or extra heat from the CPU or fan problems.... and my PC is on for days at a time. That said I'd personally be worried in any increase in heat with any O/S as the CPU temp should be the same regardless of O/S. Maybe go for a known Ubuntu on that model PC with good feedback (like I have experienced with Dapper-Drake) and see if the problem persists, likely it won't as it sounds like it may be an incompatibility with the Linux you're running and your Hardware management that controls your CPU temp/fan. Hope that helps.;)
