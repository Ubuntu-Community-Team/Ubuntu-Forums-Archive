### Post by thk on 2007-11-10
Many laptops have a built-in compact flash reader linked to the PCMCIA bus. These do not work in Ubuntu because the kernel maintainer chooses not to configure the kernel to build the ide-cs driver. I have filed a bug here:

  [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux-meta/+bug/161857](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux-meta/+bug/161857)
