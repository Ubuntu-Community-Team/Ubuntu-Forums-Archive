### Post by opscure on 2008-02-18
not really.  your wireless card runs at a much higher frequency than the FM band.  FM runs from roughly 88.6-107.9 mhz whereas your wifi card will probably run somewhere in the 2.4-5 ghz public spectrum.  There are methods of accomplishing this end result with separate devices like this:
[http://www.mobileblackbox.com/content/view/31/54/]("http://www.mobileblackbox.com/content/view/31/54/")
