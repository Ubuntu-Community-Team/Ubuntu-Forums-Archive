### Post by ModelM on 2008-01-21
Since a Mac Mini is just a laptop stripped of its keyboard & display, then folded into a box, I'd recommend just buying a laptop. I wanted a *quiet* computer & also was considering a Mac Mini but instead got a laptop with a dual-core 64 bit processor, 2 gig of ram, 160 gig hd, dual-layer DVD burner, 802.11g , modem, etc. all for $700. And, unlike a Mac Mini, since my laptop also has a keyboard & 15" widescreen display, I can take it with me if I choose. And it runs Ubuntu Linux just fine :)
