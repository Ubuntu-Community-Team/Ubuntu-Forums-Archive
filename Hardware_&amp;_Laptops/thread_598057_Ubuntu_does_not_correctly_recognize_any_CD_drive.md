### Post by louieb on 2007-10-31
> **JawsThemeSwimming428 said:**
> .... I don't really think it matters which one is Master or Slave as I will be using both, ... what do you mean "are they jumpered 'cs' " ? What is cs? ...

Master / Slave / CS is Cable Select, these are the 3 setting you can jumper on the back of the drive.  Sometimes we get lucky and it doesn't matter how there set it just works. To  be safe  Jumper one as Master and plug it into the end connector. jumper the other as Slave and plug it into the middle connector. A less sure method is to jumper both as Cable Select and let the hardware figure out which is which.
