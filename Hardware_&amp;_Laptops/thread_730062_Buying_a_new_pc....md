### Post by Existentialist on 2008-03-21
As I'm suer you noticed, it is a micro atx motherboard, and the 8800gt and 9600gt are both 9 inches long (229mm) so with a smaller case you may want to make sure you wont have any issues fitting theses cards in there.  But for the money I would still recommend the 9600gt since it is cheaper and consumes around 20-25 watts less power on average when running full 3d.  This could end up being an issue with the psu included in that bundle.  It seems like it may be a cheap psu that comes with the case, and often times the wattage meets the cards requirements, but the voltage on the +12 volt rails is too low to provide reliable power to the gpu.  I would consider calling the place before buying and asking them some questions about these issues so that if you do have to buy a new psu or something you know about it before buying.
