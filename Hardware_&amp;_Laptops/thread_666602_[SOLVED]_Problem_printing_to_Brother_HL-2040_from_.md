### Post by kostkon on 2008-01-13
I had the same problem and it is a *Firefox* related problem. I adjusted the margins and everything now is OK for me.

Why not try my own adjustments, they may also work for your printer just fine. :) In case you want to know, I have a *HP Deskjet 840C*.

You can find the *Page Setup* dialog at **File -> Page Setup** in *Firefox* and the *Printer Properties* at **File -> Print -> Properties**.

In *Printer Properties* you don't need of course to set the *Colour* to *Greyscale*. I have set it because I only have black ink in my printer right now.

EDIT: Just to add that *Firefox 3* is supposed to have a better printer support; I hope this is the case.
