### Post by tehBoris on 2007-05-31
You might want to try Kubuntu in this instance. It has a (similarly) fairly idiot proof interface for doing things with multiple monitors. Unfortunately if it fails then your kind of hosed lol.

Also, the graphics card wouldn't allow you to send a VGA signal out of a S-Video port. the idea is that the graphics card does all the hard work while the OS 'just sends it a picture to display'. You might have to tinker with resolutions and refresh rates to get it working proper though. At least this is a starting point if nothing else :P
