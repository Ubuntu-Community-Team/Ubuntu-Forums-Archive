### Post by Existentialist on 2008-01-25
Seeing how you have not got any response to this I figured I would at least give you something.  The 8800 series are great cards for the money especially the gts.  Only problem I'm having with my gt is that the linux drivers don't support fan speed control yet, and these fans are loud at full speed.  This may have been fixed by now though I haven't checked in a while.  As for you mobo, I have no personal experience but I did see that it has been tested for compatibility running linux here:

[http://www.linux-tested.com/results/asus_m2n-vm_dvi.html](http://www.linux-tested.com/results/asus_m2n-vm_dvi.html)

At the time the onboard lan did not work by default, drivers had to be manually installed.
