### Post by cyrusrayne on 2007-12-18
What Palm you get is by and large up to what you would like to do with it, I find some of the most popular are the Zire and more basic Tungsten models.  

For operation in Ubuntu, you can actually set that up via the PalmOS Devices options (System ->Preferences ->PalmOS Devices).  I cannot vouch personally for the functionality, but I trust that the built in software should serve you well.

Best of luck :)
