### Post by K.Mandla on 2006-11-17
I've moved this into the hardware area, where it's more likely to get attention. The Cafe moves so fast it's likely to get pushed down and away. 

I can think of about three things that might be happening.

[LIST=1]
[*]The motherboard was damaged when your PSU blew up. Frightening, but possible. 

[*]The new PSU is somehow improperly connected, or worse, is underpowering the board. 

[*]Finally, there's some sort of video glitch that makes you think it's not getting to the BIOS screen, when it has.
[/LIST]
My best suggestions are to swap out the power supply with one you know is working (borrow one from a friend, or get a box store model and take it back when you're done) and see if that helps. Worse comes to worst, you might have to spring for a new mobo.
