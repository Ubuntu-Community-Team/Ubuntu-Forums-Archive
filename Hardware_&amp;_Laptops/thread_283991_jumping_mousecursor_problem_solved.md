### Post by dannemil on 2006-10-25
I spent days online trying to find software and hardware fixes for my jumping mouse that had become completely unusable. It would jump randomly to the corner of the screen, run across the top, jump to the bottom, etc. all with a mind of its own.

After pulling my hair out on this one, I tried a simple solution: replace your mouse pad with a soft one with some texture and foam rubber backing. I was using some free, plasticized handout pad with some advertisers logo on it and apparently the mouse movements were not being registered correctly on this surface. PROBLEM SOLVED. No more out-of-control rodent wreaking havoc on my screen. The problem instantly went away and has not returned.

Some times the simplest solutions are the best. By the way, I was using an optical mouse.
