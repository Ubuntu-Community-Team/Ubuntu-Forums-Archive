### Post by kebes on 2007-01-07
Try going to the printer admin page:
[http://localhost:631/](http://localhost:631/)

Go to your printer and try to print another test page. Check the Jobs list and remove any that are stuck or blocking it somehow.

You can also try deleting the printer from CUPS and creating it again. When doing this you usually have a few options when it comes to drivers. You could try some of the alternate drivers.
