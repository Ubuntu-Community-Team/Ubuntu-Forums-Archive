### Post by scottro on 2008-03-10
Most of these Acers are using the AR5007EG card.  Search the forums, we have a thread going on it.  

The light will probably not work.  

The button makes it more interesting--you can't be sure you have the wireless on or off till you do everything that you'll have to do to get wireless working then, if it doesn't work, push the button a time or two and see if it works then. 

If there's a sticker on the bottom saying  Atheros AR5BXB63 then you probably have the 5007EG card. 

Acer sold these with Linux in various parts of Asia, then used a different wireless card when selling them here.
