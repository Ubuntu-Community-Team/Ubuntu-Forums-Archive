### Post by gavinjb on 2008-04-17
You can fairly easily enable the 3D desktop, but there is currently a bug in the driver which causes problems with Video while Compiz is running, so the Intel Chipsets have been BlackListed in Ubuntu.

To see how to easily enable use the following link

[http://theubuntublog.wordpress.com/2008/04/05/compiz-fusion-on-an-x3100-gma-956/](http://theubuntublog.wordpress.com/2008/04/05/compiz-fusion-on-an-x3100-gma-956/)
