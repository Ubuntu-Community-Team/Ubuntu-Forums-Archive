### Post by frankos44 on 2008-04-20
Yes I have.

Its a brilliant little board and it is silent.

I've tried it with Gutsy and Hardy Desktop. The only drawback is Ubuntu doesn't detect the graphics correctly and uses the vesa generic driver. 

Hardy "Release Candidate" version does not have "Screen and Graphics" in the menus and "dpkg-reconfigure" doesn't configure the graphics any more. I am hoping that there will something else in its place place on the final release.

The graphics acceleration seemed to work with Fedora 8 out of interest.

My application was to use it as a Ubuntu Server so graphics was not an issue anyway. 

Performance was exactly as I expected, Acceptable but by no means as fast as my normal desktop.  Boot speed was about 60 seconds, there is a pause for some reason of about 15 seconds but once up and running it was about 25% slower when initially opening a  large application such as openoffice.

Hope this helps.
