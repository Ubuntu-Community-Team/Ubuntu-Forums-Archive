### Post by mips on 2005-06-08
What resolution does the TFTP display support ??? The hardware will be the determining factor.

The chipset might support higer resoltions but the display can only do 1024x768. With an external display you will get higher resolution. 

It all depends on the spec of your laptop, most only do 1024x768, more expensive models support higher resolution. I have a hp nx6110 and it is spec'ed with a XVGA display 1024x768 and that is as high as it will go.

Go and read the manual for your specs (RTFM). If yours is spec'ed for higher resolution then you have a problem with your xorg.conf I would say.

cheers
