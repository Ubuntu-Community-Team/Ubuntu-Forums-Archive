### Post by thearthur on 2008-02-01
WORKAROUND:
  move the mouse to the upper right and click the "log out" button.
(you cant see it but you can still click it). then hit ALT-w as the keybord shortcut for switch users. this restarts compiz and recovers the rest of the screen. then his Controll-Alt-F7 to go back to your normal desktop.
