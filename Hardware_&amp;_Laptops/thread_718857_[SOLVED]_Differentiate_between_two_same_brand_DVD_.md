### Post by Pygi on 2008-03-09
Hey,

ofcourse you can. Go to Edit --> Preferences --> Devices. Click the name
of the device and change it. Make sure to tick off "Always scan for devices on
startup" or it'll overwrite your settings next time you start it.

Hopefully that helps.
