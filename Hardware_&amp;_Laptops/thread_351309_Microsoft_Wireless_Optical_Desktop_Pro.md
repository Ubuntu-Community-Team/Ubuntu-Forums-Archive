### Post by lhtown on 2007-02-02
I don't know about the product you mentioned. I would guess that it would work flawlessly for basic use although you wouldn't get any of the super advanced handholding/features that the 100MB undoubtedly provides. Also, some of the special keys might not work out of the box.

Personally, I am not convinced that keyboard ergonomics are all that important for most people. I am fine with a standard keyboard if it is good quality and a reasonable sized mouse. Handrests, screen and keyboard position, having a high quality screen and seating all rank well above the mouse and keyboard for me.

Since my last office chair broke, I have started using a "Fitball" exercise ball for an office chair. So far, I am pretty pleased with it although I would still like to have a super comfy chair for when I am at the computer for those really long sessions.
