### Post by gus3001 on 2008-05-29
Hi
I also have a Presario 1700 that overheated. The problem was that the fan simply wouldn't turn on when I booted up my xubuntu (hardy). I tried to install programs to control the fan but the notebook's hardware doesn't support it. 
Finally, I updated the bios (I was trying to avoid that because of the implications of something going wrong) and presto. Now my fan works regularly , and my laptop, although still kinda hot, doesn't crash anymore. :grin:

Heres the link for the official bios updater software. You'll need windows and a diskett.
