### Post by hw-tph on 2006-08-02
As long as they don't require special drivers to work in Windows 2000/XP, most USB harddisks will work flawlessly "out of the box" in Ubuntu, using standard usb-storage drivers. I have a [WD MyBook](http://www.wdmybook.com) and it works great, it even powers off when I unmount it. I also have a 40GB portable MP3 player (iAudio M3) which works equally well - both show up in the Places menu and on the desktop, but they are actually mounted in /media.
