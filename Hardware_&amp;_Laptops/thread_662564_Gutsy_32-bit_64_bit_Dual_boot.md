### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-01-09
We have a sticky in the 64-bit forum that answers your questions.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=368607](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=368607)

Yes. you can run both 32 and 64 at the same time with a common home partition. There's nothing silly about it if you want to compare two distros.

Who runs 64-bit? Anyone who has the hardware and wants increased performance, esp. in multimedia apps, boot time, and software building.
