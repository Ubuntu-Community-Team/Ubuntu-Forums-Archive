### Post by mocoloco on 2007-08-27
So you can connect to the internet with Ubuntu and want to download XP drivers?  Just pull up Firefox and browse to or search for the site you need the drivers from.  They'll download to the desktop, then go to "Places -> Computer" and double click on your hard drive.  Move the files somewhere there, reboot and install under XP.
