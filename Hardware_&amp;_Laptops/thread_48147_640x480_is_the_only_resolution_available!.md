### Post by varunus on 2005-07-11
I can get resolutions higher than 640x480 with the i810 driver on Ubuntu out of the box...try the following:

1.  Remove the HorizSync, VertRefresh, and Modeline lines from your xorg.conf completely.
2.  Change the Screen section and delete all resolutions except 1280x1024.

I can also remember a utility designed for i810 cards called 855resolution or i810 resolution or something like that that would let i810 users fix this problem.

You could always try compiling the new i915 driver from source.

Do **NOT** use these, as you will get better support with the drivers included in Hoary.
