### Post by styven on 2008-01-06
Hi all,

Thought I would let you know that an all in one printer I bought works out of the box in Ubuntu 7.10.

It is an HP Deskjet F390, Printer, Scanner, Copier.

Ubuntu sees it the moment you connect it and loads the relevant drivers, great.

Fire up Xsane (for scanning) and it too sees it.

The copier aspect is non pc based, so OS is irrelevant.

I have even had it set up as I network printer via a usb port on my router. Once I knew how it is very easy to do in Linux, still not got it working for XP as yet!.

Hope this will be of assistance to someone in the market for a cheap (GBP 29.97,  USD 59.12, EURO 40.09) fully functioning printer.

Steve.
