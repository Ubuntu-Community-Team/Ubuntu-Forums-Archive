### Post by jespdj on 2008-03-06
I don't have a direct solution to your problem, but I can tell you that the Intel 4965 AGN WiFi adapter is supported by Ubuntu 7.10. I have it in my Dell XPS M1330 and it works out-of-the-box (with a NetGear router), but not at N-speed (300 Mbit/s). Only G-speed (54 Mbit/s) works; as far as I know, the N-speed standard isn't yet supported by the Linux drivers.
