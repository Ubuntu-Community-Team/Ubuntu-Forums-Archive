### Post by iZm on 2006-12-12
I have a dv8000 and just this minute attached a firewire drive. The port in the laptop is fine and is discovered o.k. The drive on the other hand is not. It will connect and work via the USB port, but when connected to the firewire, it is seen as a network device! and the dhcp client is run on it!.

This is probably just a module thing, but I haven't chased it up. I only plugged it in 5 minutes ago.

The drive I have is a Western Digital My Book 250GB.

So if what you want is a firewire drive working out of the box, don' go for a My Book.
