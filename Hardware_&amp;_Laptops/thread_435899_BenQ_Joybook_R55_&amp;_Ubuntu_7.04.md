### Post by Josh1 on 2007-05-07
In February I picked one of these up for $1500 AUD, which had everything I needed. It came preinstalled which Windows XP Pro. The first thing I did when I got home was installed 6.10, but that didn't work at all (it wouldn't even boot into it after burning it 4 times).

So tonight I thought "Hey, since I have been using 7.04 on my server, why don't I try it on my Laptop", and I installed it after reading that networking had been improved.

I was not let down. I turned it on, fired up the livecd and then everything worked. It had the "restricted" thingy for accessing the 3925agp off the livecd, as well as my 7400 geforcego drivers! Wow!

So I installed it dualboot (I am a webdesigner, I need my tools which are windows only, I am also a gamer ;)), and I am typing this on my Laptop.

Thanks Ubuntu, you now have me installed on my Laptop as well as my server! :-)
