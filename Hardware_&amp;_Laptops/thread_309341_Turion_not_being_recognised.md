### Post by zgornel on 2006-11-29
The only "driver" would be support in the kernel for the optimisations (instructions) that the CPU and some ACPI stuff ;) . Practically Turion is just a Athlon64 - Mobile, they're quite compatible and I doubt problems should arise. Specific software always brings benefits so if you do find something Turion related and *need it*, install it.
