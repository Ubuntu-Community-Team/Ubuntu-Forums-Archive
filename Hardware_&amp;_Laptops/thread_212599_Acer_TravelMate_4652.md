### Post by obi-nine on 2006-07-13
Hi,

I'm using an Acer 4652LMi with Ubuntu Dapper (previously with Breezy) and my only real issue is with playing videos through the SVGA video out.

Everything else (that I'm using) works pretty much flawlessly, out of the box - sound, wifi, suspend, etc. etc.

When I hook up a TV through the SVGA though it will display the desktop and any application except for a moving video (eg. VLC movie). These only appear as a solid blue screen or window.

Also... google earth is a bit mangled but it's still a beta.

Over-all I would rate my Dapper/Acer experience quite high and I'm happy to show it to my windows-using friends so that they can see what they're missing!

If you run into specific problems with your install, let me know and I may be able to help out.
