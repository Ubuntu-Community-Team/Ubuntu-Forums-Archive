### Post by bobpur on 2007-09-20
With a computer that old, I wouldn't try a 6800 nvidia in it. Your card is an AGP 2X-4X or so. Newer AGP cards are 4X-8X. I.m not sure what the numbers mean. It might have something to do with the speed of the card. 
There are voltage requirements, too. The XFX 6800 XT I'm running has an additional power  plug from the power supply.
If your card is going bad some computer stores maintain (if they're smart) a supply of older used parts for people that  want to get that last ounce of use from their old machines. 
Also, check with [www.newegg.com](www.newegg.com) for a replacement video card. Their new AGP 2X-4X video cards are pretty reasonable.
Google your old card and try to get the exact specs for a good replacement.

                                                       Good Luck
