### Post by johnc4510 on 2006-06-13
You don't have to wipe out your partitions. However you should backup your home folder, if it is not on a separate partion. Just pop the cd in and install on the same partion you installed on before. If you home partition is on a separate partition, do not format that partition or you will lose all data.
