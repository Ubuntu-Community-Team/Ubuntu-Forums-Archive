### Post by Fire_Chief on 2008-02-27
The choke point for that setup will probably not be the processor speed as nearly all of what you are doing is going to be at the hardware level (passing traffic between the two NICs on the PCI bus). Most common proprietary routers don't use even that much CPU power although they do often have specialized chips to handle some of the traffic analysis and processing. I think your setup will perform well enough that you won't notice.
