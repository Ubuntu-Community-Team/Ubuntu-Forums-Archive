### Post by rcarroll215 on 2007-06-29
I have the same laptop.  Are you looking for drivers for Windows XP?  They are available for download on Dell's website.  You'll need the service tag number from the bottom of the computer.  Good luck though, Dell's confusing driver database along with their insistance on putting drivers in .zip files as executable programs files makes reinstalling them a nightmare.

I recently wiped XP completely off my computer and installed Feisty 7.04.  Wireless worked right out of the box through restricted drivers.  The only thing I needed to fix was the screen resolution by downloading 915resolution.

Good luck!
