### Post by cracker on 2006-07-07
You will have to look for AGP cards. All of the newest AGP cards will be 4x/8x, and are compatible with your motherboard. You should be able to get a card as recent as the 6800s in AGP, but probably not new, as all the new stuff is PCI Express. All of the 6 series cards should be around in both AGP and PCI Express, but the 7 series cards will only be PCI Express. Anything older than the 6 series will be AGP, and since yours is a GeForce 2, it's safe to say that anything in the middle will be either 2x/4x or 4x/8x, and will work. Your best bet is ebay.
