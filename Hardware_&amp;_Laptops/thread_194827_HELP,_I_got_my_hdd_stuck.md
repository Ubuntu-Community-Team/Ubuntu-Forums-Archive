### Post by musther on 2006-06-12
Is it in some kind of slot, as that's what you've made it sound like?  My best advice is to use something to lever it out, I'd use two small flat head screwdrivers, one on either side to pull it out, but of course be gentle.  If you don't want to risk it yourself you could always take it to a service center, although that'll cost.
