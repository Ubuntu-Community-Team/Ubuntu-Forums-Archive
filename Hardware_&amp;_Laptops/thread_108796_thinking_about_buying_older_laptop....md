### Post by ninotob on 2005-12-27
Well, I have an old Toshiba PIII 600, 256mb ram -- it's actually fast enough to use without going crazy, although I wish I could get to 512 ram.  By the same token, I have 466mhz G3 (color ibook ~5 years old).  Even with 576 mbs of ram, that thing is a slug.  It's current use is as a fancy wireless bridge.

Anyway, A PI or II would likely be annoying and you might find a low memory ceiling.

Oh, and the toshiba -- it rocks!  Sound came up without any effort and it's built in speakers are big and loud.  I was shocked at how much sound that thing will put out.

EDIT:  Also, the pointer (eraserhead) also worked without any configing.  But I hate eraserheads so I just plug in a usb mouse -- also worked w/o configing.
