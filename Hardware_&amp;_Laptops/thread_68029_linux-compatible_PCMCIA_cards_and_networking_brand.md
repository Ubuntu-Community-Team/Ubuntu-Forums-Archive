### Post by mlomker on 2005-09-22
> I have been a happy Linksys user for years, but their products (wireless) are crap with Linux, as they use the Broadcom chipsets that have no native drivers...


That may be true of their access points but not their cards.  Most of the cards are based on Atheros and have native drivers.  The USB-models are the major exception at the moment...they need ndiswrappers.

I've looked for compatibility lists on the `net but the are all so out of date.  You'll want to find a card that uses Atheros, Prism, or RT2500.
