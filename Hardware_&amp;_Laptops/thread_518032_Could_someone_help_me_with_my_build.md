some tips:
-Avoid ATI graphics (nvidia is much much better)
-don't get anything too bleeding edge
--wireless networking may be a sticking point (although support gets better with every Ubuntu release)
-you don't need a ton of ram (1 gb should more than adequate for average use--with vista this would barely be enough)
