### Post by Existentialist on 2008-03-14
I was going to recommend the 8000 series to you.  I have ran dual 1920x1200 off of an 8600gts with compiz on, so I know that works.  I would imagine that the 8500's should do fine too, but I can't speak for them.  With both the 8500 and 8600's, try to find one with the GDDR3 memory.  It is significantly faster than the GDDR2 which is actually just plain DDR memory not optimized for gpus.  Newegg usually has a few 8600's with rebates that make them pretty cheap.
