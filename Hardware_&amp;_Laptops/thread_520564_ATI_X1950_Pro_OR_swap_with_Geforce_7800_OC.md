### Post by EXCiD3 on 2007-08-08
Well if you want to use Beryl, games, or other fancy programs i would recommend swapping your card for the Nvidia one, however, gaming isnt too great in linux, so if you want your better card to use for gaming, and dont want to crazy of graphics stuff in ubuntu keep the ATI card.

Nvidia is tons better with linux drivers, and you can easily config dual monitors, etc. ATI has crappy linux drivers, but they are usable. You could go without gaming/graphics intensive apps in linux and dual boot XP/Vista and enjoy the best of both worlds. I do this with my Nvidia 7600 Go.... Just my thoughts on it....hope it helps you decide!
