### Post by tread on 2005-06-06
You may need to enter a modeline in the Monitor section.
go [here](http://www.d.umn.edu/~salu0005/XF86Config-4) to see where a modeline should go, and [here](http://xtiming.sourceforge.net/cgi-bin/xtiming.pl) to generate a modeline if you cant find one on the net for your model. Check out tuxmobil.org for your laptop .. maybe someone got it working using a different distro, in which case you could look at their modelines.
