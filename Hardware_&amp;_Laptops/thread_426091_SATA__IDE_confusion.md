### Post by kidders on 2007-04-29
Hi there,

The practical implications depend on whether your motherboard supports *both* IDE And SATA devices. Many do. Generally speaking, if you can find somewhere on your board to plug your old optical drives into, then they will work. If not, you have a problem.

I took a quick look at a picture of your motherboard, and I didn't see any IDE slots on it. :-( If there really aren't any, you may (afaik) need to buy a PCI IDE controller, to use your old drives. Don't do anything until someone else tells you the same thing, though!
