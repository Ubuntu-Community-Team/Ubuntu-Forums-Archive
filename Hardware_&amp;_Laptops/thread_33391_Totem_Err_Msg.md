### Post by AgenT on 2005-05-10
[UbuntuGuide]("http://www.ubuntuguide.org/") is your friend. Please read that, especially the section on playing DVD's. 

There are probably also sections about this on other parts of Ubuntu.org (wiki, etc.) so you might want to read up on those.
