### Post by skoal on 2005-04-11
If you're 'cheap' like me, your underwear is probably just an elastic band holding together a few threads of cotton and you use deer antlers mounted on your wall to organize computer cables.  If that's the case:

Find yourself an older Sound Blaster Live! on eBay for < $10.  I have the original one with the I/O card attachment.  It allows me to feed 5.1 surround to my receiver.  You'll be so impressed with the 'concert' like quality of your mp3's and streaming audio that you'll probably raise your hand and flick your lighter.

I've had this little gem of a card for over 7 years now.  In those years, I have yet to find one distro or linux module which doesn't support it straight out of the box...
