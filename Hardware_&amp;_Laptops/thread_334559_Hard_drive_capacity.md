### Post by jonfenton on 2007-01-09
When using NTFS hard drives I have kept about 20% free to allow defragging etc. On a SATA drive formatted with Ext3 what space ideally should be left free to permit efficient working?

---

### Post by 23meg on 2007-01-09
As you won't need to defrag the volume, and swap space will be on a separate partition, typically there's no need to leave a certain amount of space free.
