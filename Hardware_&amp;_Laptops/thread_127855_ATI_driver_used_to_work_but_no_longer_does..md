### Post by newuser111 on 2006-02-10
the problem is that you have  TWO drivers listed in the xorg.conf

> Section "Device"
	Identifier  "Radeon X800"
	Driver      "ati"
#	Option	    "UseFBDev" "true"
	BusID       "PCI:1:0:0"
EndSection

Section "Device"
	Identifier  "ATI Graphics Adapter 0"
	Driver      "fglrx"
	BusID       "PCI:1:0:0"
EndSection

note the identifier needs to match the "device" under "screen", i recommend you run sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg and select fglrx, it will create a brandnew xorg.conf

after that add these under the "device" section of your video card in your new xorg.conf to enable XV playback

	Option		"VideoOverlay"		"on"
	Option		"OpenGLOverlay"		"off"
