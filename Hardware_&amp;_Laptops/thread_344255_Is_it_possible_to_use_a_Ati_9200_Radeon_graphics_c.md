### Post by CowzRule on 2007-01-22
The 9200 series cards are pretty popular cards and should work right out of the box. I'm thinking you're having some other issue causing your "crash".
If you can't get the LiveCD to work, then you may have to use the "alternate install" version.
You could try this; when you boot up the livecd, hit F-6. Below the menu you'll see a command line pop up with a command in it. Delete the "Splash" and "Quite" from that line then hit Enter.
That will allow you to see what's going on rather than the Ubuntu splash image. That may give you an idea of what's going on.
