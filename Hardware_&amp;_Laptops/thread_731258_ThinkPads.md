### Post by happycoder on 2008-03-21
I can't compare the notebooks you are looking at. I just got a T61 15.4 X3100 graphics and preloaded with SLED10 SP1. SLED is fine for enterprise use, but I want more packages. Now running Ubuntu 7.1 with no problems except 3D driver is still blacklisted. I have the 1680 screen, it has some output falloff on the right edge, and other users claim it isn't very bright. It's a lot brighter than my 5 year old Sony, which was bright for its time. I don't do image editing, and in normal use the screen is fine and I really like the high resolution. I wish it had DVI out without requiring the dock. I'm old enough to have some trouble reading labels without reading glasses, but I can read the screen fine at equivalent font sizes. Build quality is excellent, fan is just barely audible with normal ambient noise, and system hardly warms up at all. I did manage to get the fan to kick into high gear last night, but it is still quieter than most laptops I've heard. The fan doesn't surge like a lot of laptops do, it stays at a consistent speed so it blends in better with ambient. CPU runs at 800Mhz most of the time. You'll have to tune the touchpad with synclient. Google for eCoupons to save 10% over and above the current sale price. Good luck with your decision.
