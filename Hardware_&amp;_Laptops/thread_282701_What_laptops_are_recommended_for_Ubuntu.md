### Post by gavin on 2006-10-25
If you want to be sure that your wireless will work:

Get a Centrino. 

Intel provides the drivers for the wireless cards that are built into centrino laptops. They even run a sourceforge project for this purpose. You do not need to use Ndiswrapper or a reverse engineered driver. It just works, and just about every recent version of Linux on your favourite distro will have the drivers available if detected on the base installation. 

If you are considering an Acer Aspire: Get the Edgy Eft. Dapper has sound card and ACPI issues with some models of Acer Aspire. 

Regards

Gavin
