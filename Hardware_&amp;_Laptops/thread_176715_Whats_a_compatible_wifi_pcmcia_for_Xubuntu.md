### Post by totfit on 2006-05-15
I had difficulty finding a card that worked easily. I found plenty of card names, but couldn't seem to locate one to purchase. Any card with an RT2500 chipset is good. I purchased one that is very inexpensive from: [http://newegg.com](http://newegg.com). The card is a Zonet ZEW1501. Good card and works out of the box with Dapper. The range is not superb, but is adequate and the price is great. I can't guarantee a network install, but I don't see why not. I didn't try an network install using the card.
