A friend of mine recently purchased a brand smacking new Apple G5, the one with the ppc64.

I put the ubuntu live disc in and booted it, just to show off how you can use your Apple without even touching the hard drive,
however shortly after booting the live disc, the boot loaded froze, i managed to divert my friends attention to something else while this happened, i quickly rebooted and inserted the installation disc, the installation went by nicely however ugly it was, after installation the machine rebooted and came up to gdm, at this point he was eager to login and start using it, butttttttttttt, it froze,
