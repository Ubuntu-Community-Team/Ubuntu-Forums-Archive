### Post by MikeBee on 2005-01-23
Hi all,

Just installed Ubuntu the other day. Have to say I am *most* impressed. It detected my madwifi wireless card automatically (after i selected no network during installation.... would have been nice to detect during cd boot install). my printer got picked up no probs. package installation is a dream and gnome 2.8 is delightful (came from yoper and kde 3.3, hardware support was sketchy).

Anyway. I had problems getting my USB Canon Digital Ixus v4 working. I'd plug the cable in and turn the camera on. Under Winslows it would get detected and appear as a drive. Not so under linux (yoper or ubuntu). I tried lots of things - hotplug config files, usb config files etc. My solution (stupidly) came from a simple BIOS upgrade. So anyone else having problems, head over to the Asus website and grab the latest BIOS.

Other than that, all my hardware is working spot on. Stability is great, gui is lovely. Dead chuffed.
