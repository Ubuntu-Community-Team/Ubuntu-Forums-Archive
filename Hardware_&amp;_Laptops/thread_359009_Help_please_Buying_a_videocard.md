### Post by RandomJoe on 2007-02-11
Those boards should work fine.  I am currently using two very nearly the same as the first one you list, by MSI: [http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.asp?Item=N82E16814127211](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.asp?Item=N82E16814127211)

I have them in the same machine, running triple/quad-head.  Work great!

The processor shouldn't matter - they should work as well on AMD as Intel.  (I have a Core 2 Duo system.)

I would be cautious if you look for a video card with built-in tuner.  The only ones I'm familiar with are ATI and aren't well supported in Linux.  Better to get a good video card (like you selected) then get a dedicated tuner card that uses a chipset well-supported by Linux.  It'll be a LOT easier to do that with discrete cards!
