### Post by K.Mandla on 2006-08-06
It'll work, but it isn't pretty. I put Xubuntu 5.10 on a Compaq Presario 1020 once, and it barely fit (it had a 1.08Gb hard drive, and needed every last bit). With only 48Mb of memory, it was sluggish, slow to boot and unresponsive. But it worked.

To be honest, for somehting that small, go with Damn Small Linux. DSL on that machine was completely opposite: Spunky, fast and functional. I hate to steer anyone away from Xubuntu, but DSL is your best bet.
