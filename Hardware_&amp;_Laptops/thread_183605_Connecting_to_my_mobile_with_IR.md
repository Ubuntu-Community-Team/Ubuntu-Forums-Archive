### Post by chrestomanci on 2006-05-28
Nokia PC suite does not support linux, so you will have trouble syncing contacts or the like.

It is possible to transfer files using OBEX. You need to install the OBEX tools (apt-cache search obex) and some drivers for you IR device (Is it USB, serial or directly connected to your  motherboard or laptop?)

Once OBEX and IR is working you should be able to send and receve files between your computer and phone. Anything more will be much harder.
