### Post by dermann on 2007-05-19
Hi guys and gals,

After having no sound in Ubuntu (Feisty and occasionally in Edgy previously) I discovered this fix for sound on my HP Pavilion dv2000 laptop by accident, but it works every time,

The problem seems to be an incompatability in the way Windows XP and Ubuntu use the sound hardware (I'm sure Ubuntu's is better). Anyway, with dual boot, after running Windows and then restarting, and booting to Ubuntu the sound would then be completely absent after having previously worked.

**The FIX**: After running Windows, shut down. Unplug laptop from power source, *and* pop out the battery. 
Replace the battery, plug back in, boot up to Ubuntu and sound works perfectly!

This seems to reset the hardware completely, erasing the mess that Windows created.
Hope this helps!

HP Pavilion dv2000 (dv2025nr) with Feisty.
