### Post by pmj on 2005-07-29
How long a burned DVD lasts depends on the manufacturer, how you store the disc and the burner you use. It could be many years or only a few months.

If you really want to be sure that you get the best possible burner and media, you should probably take a good look at the [CD Freaks forum](http://www.cdfreaks.com/) and other CD/DVD communities and see what they use and recommend.
