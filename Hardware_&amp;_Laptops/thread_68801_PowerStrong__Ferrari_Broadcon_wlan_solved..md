### Post by NaitoNeko on 2005-09-24
Most of new 64 Bit Laptop come with broadcom wireless card.
The problem is that the drivers are 32bit, so you need to find  64 bit drivers for ndiswrapper.
Assuming you know how to install ndiswrapper, and instal the drivers i will post only the driver that you need for most of the 64bit laptops.

You can get the driver from rapidshare [here](http://rapidshare.de/files/5476414/WL_T60H906_8.0.10.0_XP64_logo_.tar.gz.html)

I hope You Enjoy it. 

PD: Try to use the last ndiswrapper from sourceforge.
