### Post by Melcar on 2006-09-08
I'm running Ubuntu on a 200M based laptop, and in my opinion runs perfectly; never had any problem with ATI divers and even ran XGL nicely (took it off because so much stress on a laptop kills your battery life).  The way I see it both nvidia and ATI are equally good at this point in driver development, so it really comes down to the laptop itself (CPU, hardware, etc).  No point in getting a laptop that costs 50% more just because the other one has an ATI GPU.  However, I have been using Linux for less than a month now so don't take my words as gospel.
