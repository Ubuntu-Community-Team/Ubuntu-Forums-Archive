### Post by Xizorbg on 2006-10-22
I found a partial solution:

1)  The media was bad of the DVD+RW

2)  I had to go into bios, select the DVD reader as the primary drive, and disable the RW from being in the boot selections

BUT

the Pioneer now will not mount while in a regular HD-based session of UBUNTU.
