### Post by neurostu on 2008-02-08
The best way to do this would be to transfer a file over your firewire. You can look at the file info to determine its size and then just time how long the file took to transfer or write a simple shell script to time the transfer.  Then look up on google what the average FW800 and FW400 transfer speeds are and you should be able to tell with that.

I know that this isnt' what you were asking for but its a really simple math problem  

rate mb/sec = (file Size in MB) / (transfer time in sec)
