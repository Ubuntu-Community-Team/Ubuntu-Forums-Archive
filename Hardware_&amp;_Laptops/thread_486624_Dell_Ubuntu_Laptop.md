### Post by dardack on 2007-06-28
I just recieved my Dell Inspirion E1505 Ubuntu laptop a few days ago and I love it.  Just wanted to post in case anyone else was thinking about buying it.  I upgraded to the Nvidia Graphics Card, 1gig of ram and the Dual Layer DVD burner, and came to $768 before tax.  Everything seems to be working flawlessly.  I haved issues with my network drivers on my desktop, and some Soft Bug Lockup CPU at boot, so was kind of nervous about this.  But everything has been great.  I can even RIP DVD's at the same time as being on vent, listening to amarok, and playing WoW with 30FPS in the wild (60 in town).  I am very pleased, and happy that a vendor is supporting this operating system like this.
