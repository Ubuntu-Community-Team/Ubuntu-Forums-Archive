### Post by rogerdean on 2007-08-23
hi folks

it went well. or at least, it did until the power cable blew on 10 seconds. hp got another one to me in a couple of days, and we were off.

connected through a wired dhcp network, the hplip driver works fine. printing, scanning, copying is great. i couldn't transfer photos from the card reader to file on a computer, but didn't really expect that. didn't try fax.

i guess hp assumes that anyone who has linux doesn't need any linux doucmentation, and should be able to work it all out for themselves. this aint really good enough, imho, and takes the edge off the joy of having such great drivers. anyway...

all in all, recommended. hope this helps someone
allbest
