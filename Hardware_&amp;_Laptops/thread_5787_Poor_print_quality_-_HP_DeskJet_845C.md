### Post by Joakim on 2004-12-20
Over at linuxprinting.org's HP-forum Till Kamppeter came with [this helpful suggestion](http://www.linuxprinting.org/pipermail/hp-list/2004q4/006048.html) :

[I]Update GhostScript to the current ESP GhostScript and HPIJS to version 
1.7.1 (or HPLIP) and you will have KRGB which should improve your 
printout quality.

    Till[/I]

I haven't got the time to try it out yet, but thought there might be others interested in this tip, here at the Ubuntu forums.  ;)
