well there are not that many applets out there for the g15 in linux...well at least not as many as in windows. the clock applet is the only thing that comes with the g15daemon and there is also an applet for amarok to display information on tracks currently playing (applet can also be found in the post above). Also check out the sourceforge page where you downloaded the g15daemon, there are a couple applets available there too ([http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=172261](http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=172261)).
