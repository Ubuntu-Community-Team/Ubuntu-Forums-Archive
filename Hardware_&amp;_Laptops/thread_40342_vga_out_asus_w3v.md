### Post by ming0 on 2005-07-03
Jeremy,

I just got a w3v, and mirroring the computer in TV/LCD out mode means that one of the displays *must* show an incorrect resolution. The easiest thing to do is to set the LCD to 600x800, and then you can see the whole thing on the TV and it just looks a bit distorted on the laptop. 

The only other option would be to make a hack that would put black bars on the sides of your laptop display (making it the same dimensions as the TV), but I don't know enough about X11 to do that :)
