### Post by Yoooder on 2007-03-01
I had bizarre keyboard problems with the 6.10 LiveCD, I'm not sure if they apply to your problem--but the fix is easy!

When booting the LiveCD my keyboard worked through the boot-menu (where you have the options to Run/Install Ubuntu, do a memtest, etc...) but by the time the system was loaded my keyboard & mouse (both USB) stopped working entirely (the NumLock light wouldn't even turn on).

I rebooted and was curious if it was failiing at the beginning or ending of the load, so I tapped my NumLock key all the way through the boot process waiting for it to stop responding--but it never happened.  Basically when running from the 6.10 LiveCD I just keep tapping on those buttons and it works like a charm.  Once I have Ubuntu installed the problem goes away entirely.
