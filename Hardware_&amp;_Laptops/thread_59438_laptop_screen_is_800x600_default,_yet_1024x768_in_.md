### Post by yokirb on 2005-08-24
I found another post on the 4090xDVD, and changed DefaultDepth from 24 to 16 in /etc/X11/xorg.conf. Then entered ctrl-alt-backspace to reboot X. It's all good. I have 1024x768 now. (the other post suggested using a different driver and adding "Virtual" entries, which I did not need to do).
