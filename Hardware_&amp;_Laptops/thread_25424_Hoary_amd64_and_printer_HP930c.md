Well I would say you should lodge a bug report since you have followed all the advice on linuxprinting.org and it is supposed to work. One thing you could try is to see whether it works with the turboprint drivers (much is not disabled with the free version) It is listed as supported there:

[http://www.turboprint.de/english.html](http://www.turboprint.de/english.html)

BTW my problem was solved with these drivers: I was trying to print out my Tax Form (1040) today and it failed under the standard hp driver but worked very nicely under turboprint.....
