### Post by tgoose on 2007-08-05
Unless you feel you're missing out on anything, you may as well leave it how it is. Installing them probably gives better 3D performance, but unless things have changed since last time I checked they're closed source and hence unsupported. By the time Gutsy comes out, it might be better to have the specific drivers because of Compiz and suchlike, but I think the Gutsy installer will do this for you if you want.
