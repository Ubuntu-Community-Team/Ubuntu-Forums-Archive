It seems to be a good graphic card, the 6600 chipset is well know and supported by ubuntu, you shouldn't have any problem with this card. 
The price looks quite good, latest chipsets like 7600 or higher are too expensive with AGP interface because the new standard is PCI-express now and the latest chipsets are less expensive in PCI-express therefore i think that  6600 chipsets have a good quality/price rate with AGP interface.
