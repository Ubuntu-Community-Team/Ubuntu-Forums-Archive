### Post by jml on 2006-06-09
The tricky part about wireless, is that its not the brand of the card that matters, but the brand of wireless chip set the the card uses.  Manufacturers do not go out of their way making this information easy to find.  That being said, cards that use the Atheros chipset seems to work well with Ubuntu.  I agree with the comments about USB  wireless adaptors, they are a real pain to get running.  You might want to check out the hardware compatibility section of this site.  Sorry I don't have link.  Just search on hardware compatibility and it should come up.
