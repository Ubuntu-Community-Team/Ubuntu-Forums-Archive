### Post by mickv on 2007-10-20
This is really information for anyone else stuck, with get 1280x800 working under Gnome.

I have an Advent 8111 with Intel 945 video chipset.

I have recently upgrade to UBUNTU 7.10 and was put back to only having 1024x768 regardless of what I did. resolution915 -l and updating xorg.conf made absolutely no difference.

Whilst I was about to give up I decided to take a look at KDE so installed KUBUNTU-DESKTOP and as if by magic I now have 1280x800 in BOTH KDE and also Gnome.

As I've rebooted the PC several times I'm pretty certain it's something that's been installed as a result of putting KUBUNTU in.

Hope this is helpful.
