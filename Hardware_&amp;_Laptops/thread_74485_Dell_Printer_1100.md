### Post by Emerzen on 2005-10-11
**If you want to try the Samsung driver, I believe you do the following:**

System>Admin>Printing

Double click on "new printer," which will bring up a dialogue.  If your printer is plugged in it should be listed under "detected printers."  Click "forward" and look for Samsung ML-1000, select it and hit "apply."  Then try a test page.
[B]
If you want to try the Lexmark driver, follow this thread:[/B]

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=49714](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=49714)

To follow this thread, open a terminal and cut&paste the directions into the command line.
