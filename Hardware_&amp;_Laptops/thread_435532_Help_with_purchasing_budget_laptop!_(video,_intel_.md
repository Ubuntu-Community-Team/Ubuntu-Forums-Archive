### Post by antonc on 2007-05-07
I picked up an el-cheapo Acer 3680 a few months ago...

I was initially a bit skeptical, but the build quality seems pretty good, everything works out the box (with Edgy), only minor glitches have been with the BCM43xx wireless drivers (no WPA support). 3D acceleration works fine, Beryl works (quite snappy, in fact), and ACPI works great (Haven't tried suspend to disk, but suspend to RAM works perfectly). S-video out works, DVD playback (using VLC) is great, ummmm... that's about all I can think of.. :-)

So - if you're on a budget, I'd say go for the acer. Just keep an eye on the wireless chipset (although with ndiswrapper pretty much anything should work).
