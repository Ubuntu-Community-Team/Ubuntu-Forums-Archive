### Post by RebounD11 on 2008-01-17
As far as my experience in switching from single (AMD Athlon 64 3500+) to dual-core processor (AMD Athlon 64 X2 4800+) (under Ubuntu ) is concerned I had no problem in terms of running the system as it was before the upgrade. However there were some inconsistencies: gDesklets showed 1 core, Karamba (I installed KDE also) showed 2 cores (only I had the feeling it showed only 1 twice) and Cedega was having problems detecting the exact type of processor. I can't tell you for sure if it was running in dual-core or single-core mode but I can tell you that after a reinstall (I reinstalled for other reasons - new hard drive a week later) everything was fine and everybody got along with the new dual-core :D
