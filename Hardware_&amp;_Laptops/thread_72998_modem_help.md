### Post by yaye on 2005-10-07
Hello,

Take a look at this link for information about PC Tel compatibility with Linux:

[http://pctelcompdb.sourceforge.net/display.php](http://pctelcompdb.sourceforge.net/display.php)

If you can't get the PC Tel to work, see if there is a Compaq or HP dealer in your area.  Our HP and Compaq computers each have a 56K PCI modem based on the Agere/Lucent chipset and this is known to work with Linux.  The HP/Compaq dealer may have one in stock or they may be able to order one for you.  

Good Luck!
