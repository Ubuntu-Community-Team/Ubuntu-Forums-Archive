### Post by grj on 2005-02-03
Drivers for SB cards are probably loaded. Double-click on the volume icon in the tray (upper right). Click File->Device, make sure your card is selected. Then look at Playback tab and adjust the volume for Master. Also, make sure there is not a red x below the sliders. If there is click on it and it should go away.
