### Post by sharpycore on 2008-04-05
When you delete something in Ubuntu, even on something like a USB drive, the file is not completely erased. It gets moved into a 'trash' folder. To empty this and free up the space on your USB drive, plug the drive in and go to 'Deleted Items' where you can click 'Empty Deleted Items' to free up your space.
