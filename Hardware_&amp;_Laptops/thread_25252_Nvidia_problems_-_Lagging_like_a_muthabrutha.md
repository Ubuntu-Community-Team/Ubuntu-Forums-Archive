### Post by skoal on 2005-04-09
If you want to keep your old 5200 information, I would suggest commenting it out to avoid confusion.

* It just looks like your 5600 device entry is still using 'nv' instead of 'nvidia'.

Although I haven't seen any confilicts while keeping the 'dri' stuff in there, I comment out any mention of DRI as well (per the NVIDIA README).  You don't need any mention of that in your X config as the nvidia driver doesn't rely on it.
