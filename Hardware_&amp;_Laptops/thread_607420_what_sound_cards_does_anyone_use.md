### Post by kyphi on 2007-11-08
The Creative Soundblaster Live 5.1 is an inexpensive card that works without any problems in the various releases of Ubuntu.

Just remember to go into the BIOS and deactivate your onboard sound chip to avoid conflicts.

BTW, it sounds good too.
