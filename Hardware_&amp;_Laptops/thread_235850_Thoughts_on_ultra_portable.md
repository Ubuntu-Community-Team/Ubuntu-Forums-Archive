### Post by Titus A Duxass on 2006-08-16
I'm running a JVC XP3210DE, not sure what processor 600 or 800.
It has 2 USB, ethernet, PCMCIA slot so I can use my Linksys wlan card.

It has the extended battery which gives it about 6 hours life.
There is also an external CD rom drive through the PCMCIA card.

The only thing that doesn't work is the built-in card reader.

Dislikes:
Narrow keyboard which makes touch tpying difficult.
No ability to connect to a monitor without a special adaptor.

Likes:
Super clear screen.
Weight (just over a kilo with extended battery).
Size (not much bigger that a paperback book).

Running xubuntu at the moment mainly for surfing, emails and as a jukebox.
