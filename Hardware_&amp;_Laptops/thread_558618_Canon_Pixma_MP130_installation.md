### Post by gautada on 2007-09-24
The Pixma MP130 does not seem to even be listed in the [Open Printing Database]("http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi").  Not sure why that is but your issue seems the be a miss identified printer.  You have something line a MP110 driver instead of the appropriate MP130 driver.  Make sure that it is an MP130.  Then look specifically for a linux driver provided by cannon as there is not one listed in the database.
