### Post by jotagab on 2006-06-27

You don't provide much info about your laptop or your Ubuntu configuration...
Anyway I recently installed Ubuntu 6.06 on my Centrino 1.5GHz with an ATI Mobility 9700.
It was also running much hotter than Windows, even with the CPU frequency monitor applet showing that the cpu was being scaled down to 600MHz. I solved it by installing the binary ATI drivers, instead of using the default Ubuntu driver. And yes, the difference in temperature is huge! Hope this helps...
