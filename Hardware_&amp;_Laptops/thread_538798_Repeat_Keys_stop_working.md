So, here's my problem:

I actually LIKE the repeat keys.  I like being able to hold down, say, the backspace, and have it delete a lot of stuff until I tell it to stop by releasing it.

So, I go into System > Preferences > Keyboard ....    and ensure that the box marked "Key presses repeat when key is held down" is CHECKED and that delay is set to a little above 'Short' and that speed is almost set all the way to 'Fast', and everything's good... at least for a little while.  

After varying lengths of time my Repeat Keys just stops working. BAM!  I'm holding the key down, and NOTHING (except of course the very first firing of the event, whichever key it was).

So then I go back into Keyboard Preferences and play with the sliders, and then we're happy again, at least for a little while.

But this happens several times a day!  An _inordinate_ amount of times a day... 

Does anyone have a clue how to fix?
