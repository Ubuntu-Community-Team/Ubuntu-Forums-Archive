### Post by localzuk on 2006-06-05
According to the x.org website, the Ati Xpress 200M was supported in xorg7.0 ([http://ftp.x.org/pub/X11R7.0/doc/html/radeon.4.html)](http://ftp.x.org/pub/X11R7.0/doc/html/radeon.4.html)). I am not too sure about the official, proprietory, drivers from ati though ([https://support.ati.com/ics/support/default.asp?deptID=894&task=knowledge&folderID=27](https://support.ati.com/ics/support/default.asp?deptID=894&task=knowledge&folderID=27))

The TV out should work fine, just do a search on this forum for 'tv out ati' or similar, and you will find a wealth of information.
