### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-02-10
This is a personal opinion, but I have an aversion to using USB mice and keyboards.  Simply because UNTIL the USB loads on boot, you have NO KEYBOARD OR MOUSE.  It may be old school, but I plug my wireless receivers into the PS2 ports and never have a failure because they load on POST (because mouse drivers vary, the mouse may not load but the keyboard WILL).  Sometimes old school is still GOOD.
