### Post by lt_kije on 2005-06-06
There are a couple possible problems...

[1] Can you play files other than .mp3s? Due to copyright and IP restrictions, Ubuntu cannot ship an MP3 player in the default distribution. It's not difficult to get MP3 playback working, though; see [http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/RestrictedFormats](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/RestrictedFormats)

[2] Have you adjusted your mixer settings? Check that the mixer setting is at an appropriate level.

If neither of the above solve your problem, it could be a hardware issue. Feel free to post back here if your troubles continue.
