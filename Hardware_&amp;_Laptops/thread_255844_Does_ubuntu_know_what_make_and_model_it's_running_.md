### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2006-09-12
I don't think there's a way to do that, and I don't think it would be very usable: Manufacturers often change the their hardware without changing the model numbers (and sometimes change the model numbers without changing anything except the chassis...). 
 A hardware test should test available hardware, not some "un-scientific model number". Adding the make and model as additional info as a kind of double check would be great of course, but I believe the easiest way to get that info is asking the user.


EDIT: just to make sure: you know about [http://hwdb.ubuntu.com/](http://hwdb.ubuntu.com/), right?
