### Post by tmeier on 2006-12-08
I have a small lab of 15 thin clients running off of a debian server which then boot VMWare Player and run Windows XP.  The thin clients are NTA Virtual Office machines, I then purchased 17" Flat panels and USB keyboards and mice.  The system works well once loaded.  Boot time is a little slow at the moment, but do not have a Gigabit Switch for this lab yet.  System works great.  Sounds like a good idea for a small business solution, just make sure to have gigabit networking to decrease boot time.
