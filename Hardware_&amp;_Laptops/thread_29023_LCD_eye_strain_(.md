### Post by crs on 2005-10-16
i also think that the font rendering is causing more eye strain than microsoft's cleartype rendering.

cleartype is based on a mathematical model of human vision to increase font quality and uses information about the currently used display/resolution:

[http://research.microsoft.com/~jplatt/cleartype/](http://research.microsoft.com/~jplatt/cleartype/)

i'm not sure how the current subpixel rendering in ubuntu compares to that..

you could try to turn the brightness of the display down, that helped me a lot, or use darker themes as discussed in this thread:

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=44610](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=44610)
