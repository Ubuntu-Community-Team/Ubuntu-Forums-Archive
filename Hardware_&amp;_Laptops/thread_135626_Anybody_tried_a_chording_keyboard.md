The website [gkos.net]("http://gkos.net") says that they work under win98/2000 but no mention of linux.

I'm currently considering whether or not to buy the BAT keyboard ([http://www.infogrip.com/](http://www.infogrip.com/)). Sad, but apparently true. I'm looking forward to carrying my keyboard as a small, battery-powered bluetooth palm-grip device that I don't even need to take out of my pocket to use :) I must be a geek.
