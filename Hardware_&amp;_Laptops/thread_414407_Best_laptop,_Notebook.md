### Post by ltmon on 2007-04-20
Three useful links for laptop stuff:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam)
[http://www.linux-laptop.net/](http://www.linux-laptop.net/)
[http://www.thinkwiki.org/](http://www.thinkwiki.org/)

My personal advice is to get something that is Intel all the way through - motherboard, processor, video card, audio and wireless.  They produce quality, open source drivers for pretty much everything.

Anything with an ATI card is still problematic.  They have produced better drivers over the past year, but the quality still isn't there for Linux.

If you want powerful graphics, Nvidia cards are currently becoming more common in laptops than they have been in the past.

Cheers,

L.
