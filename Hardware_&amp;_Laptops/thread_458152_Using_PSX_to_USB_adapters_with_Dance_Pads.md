### Post by mrpeanut on 2007-06-01
Most psx->usb converters have something called "dance pad mode."  To activate this it might vary from converter to converter, but I believe the most common key combination is to hold up+start+select for 3 seconds.  If you still have your retail packaging check that, or maybe google your model information.
