### Post by kostkon on 2007-10-30
Your camera is using the [PTP]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Picture_Transfer_Protocol") protocol to connect to the computer so it's not mounted as a drive. Also, I think *gThumb* is the program that imports your photos, not *F-Spot*.

Most cameras offer you the option to make them to use [PTP]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Picture_Transfer_Protocol") or to work as a drive. Thus, check the preferences of your camera, maybe there is such an option for you to choose.
