### Post by nrune on 2007-10-23
Thought I would close this thread out with the solutuion. 

Apparnetly Toshiba laptops only accept drives in a master setting condition.  The compal drive I have is in a cable select condition.  There are no switches or togles to change it to a master drive.  So what you have to do is short pins. 

Here is the guide I followed and got my drive working just fine.
