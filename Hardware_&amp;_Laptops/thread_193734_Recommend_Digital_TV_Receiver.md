### Post by _simon_ on 2006-06-10
I can't really help with recommendations but I struggled along with a normal tv tuner card for about 2 years under windows - picture window always in the way, too small/too big, crap sound, crap signal etc. Before you spend on a tuner card, be sure it is what you want.

In the end I got a 15" LCD tv which I have on the same desk as my pc screen and got an AV sender (one of those wireless dooda's) and hooked it up to the sky box downstairs, and for audio as the tv speakers were shit, I have that coming through a sony hifi I had sat around doing nout so now I have sky digital on it with decent audio - best bit is I don't have to fiddle with settings in an OS to get it how I want.
