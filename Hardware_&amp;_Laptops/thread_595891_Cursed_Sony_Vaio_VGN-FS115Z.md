### Post by bubbalouie on 2007-10-29
In Fesity I had to remove *sonypi* to make smartdimmer work, though, in gutsy the two get along, it may help you though.

From memory, smart dimmer will not work if you do not have the nvidia binary driver installed.

My volume and mute buttons just worked, S1 & S2 I have never needed, the Fn Key, well I use the windows key in it's place and have made a few shortcuts which link to hot keys (KDE feature).
