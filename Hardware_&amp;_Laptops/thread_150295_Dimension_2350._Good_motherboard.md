### Post by Bartender on 2006-03-25
Weasel -
Dell places a big old land mine in their PC's.  They've been doing it for years.  You know the big 20 or 24-pin plug that goes from the power supply to the motherboard?  Although Dell's plugs look the same as everyone else's, they're not.  If you jack a Dell PSU into any one else's board, or plug a standard power supply into a Dell motherboard, you'll have sparks, smoke, and gnashing of teeth.

If you replace your motherboard, get a new PSU also.  Everything else will be O.K.
