### Post by tambooki on 2004-10-29
This isn't a particularly technical answer, but I had similar problems w/ my Artec e+48U. What worked for me was to tell XSane to "Calibrate before next scan." This option appears for me on the "Standard Options" window. Hope this works for you, too.

### Post by kamstrup on 2004-10-31
I solved the problem. It was related to the electrical adapter. It turned out that I had to use the very adapter that came with the scanner. If I use any other (same specs of course) I get all sorts of weird noise in the image.

It must have something to do with the scanner being really sensitive to small fluctuations in the current or something.
