### Post by JLB on 2005-06-13
Start all over again :)
Take your disks you ordered from IBM when you bought the laptop (you did order them in the 30 day timeframe didn't you? you get em free then)

Repartition the drive leaving unpartitioned space for your future linux installation
Reinstall from the setup disks
tweak XP to you liking and if you want verify the rapid restore stuff. 
Now install linux as usual using the unpartitioned space on the drive.
You should now have everything working fine again.

This is off the top of my head. it has been a year or so since i did this to my wife's R40.
