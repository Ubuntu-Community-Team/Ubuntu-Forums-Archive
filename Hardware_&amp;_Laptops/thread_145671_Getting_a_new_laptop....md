### Post by jimrz on 2006-03-16
Consider any relatively recent IBM/Lenovo ThinkPad. They are excellent machines, very linux friendly and there is quite a lot of info available here + ThinkWiki.org and many other places. The intall on my T42 (Pm1.8G/1Gb ram/80Gb hd/ATI 9600 vid (128Mb)/802.11 a b g wireless/Gigabit e-net/bluetooth went quickly and flawlessly with nearly everything (and certainly all the important stuff) working right out of the box.

   I know they use ATI video but it works straight away although without hardware acceleration. I am not a big gamer and do not use the lt for gaming. Therefore  I have not bothered to get that running, but I have seen quite a bit of instruction out there on how to sucessfully get it running.

   Older TP's like my 600x that preceeded the T42 were much more difficult to get going correctly, although this too could be done.
