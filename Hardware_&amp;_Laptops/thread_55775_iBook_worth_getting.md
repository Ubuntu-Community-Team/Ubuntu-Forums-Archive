### Post by costoa on 2005-08-10
Wives can be a pain can't they? =)

Mine just bought a new 12" Powerbook. iBooks and Powerbooks are beautiful machines. Nice, very solid feel and Mac OS X is extremely stable. Long story short, for $200 more you can get a new **G4** iBook with a much better video chipset, 802.11b/g, bluetooth, a bigger hd, more RAM, etc. For many apps, especially Apple apps, a G4 is much faster. Plus the new iBooks have OS X 10.4, which would cost you $130 to upgrade an used system.

Buying used in this case IMO isn't worth it.
