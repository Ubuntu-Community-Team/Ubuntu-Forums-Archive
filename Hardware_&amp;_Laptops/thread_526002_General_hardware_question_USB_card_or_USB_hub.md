### Post by fredj on 2007-08-15
The front usb ports probably don't work because they haven't been connected. A usb hub will 
give you more ports but they won't be the same as fully powered ports. So if you want to use
devices that take their power from the usb port then a hub is not ideal. If your existing ports
are not usb2 ports then adding usb2 via a card is a necessity if you want to connect things
like disk drives etc.
