### Post by thedaylights on 2008-01-27
Microphone may be defaulted to mute. There is a simple fix for that.

Right click on the volume control icon on the top menu bar, next to the clock.
Choose "open volume control". Go to Edit --> Preferences. Check all the boxes that are unchecked, close that. Now on the volume control panel you should see microphone with a little crossed-out speaker on the bottom. Click that speaker to unmute, and raise the volume of the microphone. You can test it with Sound Recorder.

Hope this works for you.
