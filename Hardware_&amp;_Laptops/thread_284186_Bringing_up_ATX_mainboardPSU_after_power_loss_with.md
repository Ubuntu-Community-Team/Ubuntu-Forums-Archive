### Post by Dr. Nick on 2006-10-25
The "paperclip trick" involves putting a "jumper" on the green and black psu cables to start the psu without hooking it up to a computer, that may be what your thinking of.

My system has a bios option to change the default power state after a outage, may look around in yours for one similar.

If thier is a spot on your mobo to plug the power button into then 
it may be possible to hard wire that into the on position, not sure exactly how you would do it though, and it could possible be risky to the hardware.
