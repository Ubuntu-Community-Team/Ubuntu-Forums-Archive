### Post by reesh on 2007-01-21
sounds to me like you have a core hardware problem, motherboard, ram or video card. try taking them out and replacing them (ram and video card). if you don't get any motherboard info when u boot up it's one of those components. also listen out for any beeps that your pc makes when booting, they could help a lot. 

it's not a data problem, so you can forget hard drives and boot cd's for now. concentrate on getting the mobo info on your screen, then continue from there
