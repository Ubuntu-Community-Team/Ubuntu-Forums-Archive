### Post by rsambuca on 2007-02-20
There is a good How-To written by Kilz on getting Flash to work using 32-bit Firefox on the 64 bit system.

This question has been asked ad infinitum and everyone has their own preference.  Yes, with 64-bit there is a bit of effort to get it set-up, but it should only be once, and then you are good to go.

As far as performance, it depends on what type of applications you are running.  If you are doing just everyday word processing, spreadsheets, email, web-browsing, etc. then you will hardly notice any difference.  If you are doing things like video transcoding, 3D rendering, or even large photo manipulations, then you can notice upwards of 30% increase in speed.
