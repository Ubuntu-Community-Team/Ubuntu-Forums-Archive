### Post by BoneKracker on 2007-04-18
It's probably not a good idea.  Most cases these days wouldn't bear the weight of a CRT on the side of case for long without bending and possibly damaging the components inside.

It's especially not okay to do this with the motherboard side of case on top, where the bending is likely to cause the most damage.

If the case is extraordinarily strong and seems like it can hold more than the weight of the monitor without ever bending, then it might be okay.
