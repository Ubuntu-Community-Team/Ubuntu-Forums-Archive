### Post by amo-ej1 on 2008-01-12
if you are sure the fan it broken, and are not entitled to having a new card due for warranty (i do think warranty should cover this) I would stop at a local store where they sell electronics components and see if they can provide you with a similar fan. But keep in mind that it doesn't _have_ to be the fan that's broken, it could also be temperature controller, some logic or a faulty wire. The sure thing to do is send it back to warranty.
But if this is not an option you can still play with it yourself. (And keep in the back of your head that you'll end up buying a new one ;-) )
