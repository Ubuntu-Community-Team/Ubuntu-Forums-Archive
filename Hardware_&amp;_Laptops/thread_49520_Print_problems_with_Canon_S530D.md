### Post by David Haas on 2005-07-16
After I installed my HP Deskjet 5740 several months ago, it printed perfectly  in Open Office and gThumb, but not The GIMP. But when I made some changes in printer settings that seemed illogical, it printer perfectly. However, I can't recall what these changes were. But I can say that in the "Setup Printer" in The GIMP, my printer model is set as "Postscript Level 2". I selected this. The printer name is Deskjet 5740--selected by the program. So, perhaps you should select your model this way.
