### Post by pbrane on 2008-02-04
I have run Fedora and Ubuntu on IBM Thinkpads and Dell Inspirons with little trouble. My Broadcom ethernet and wireless works fine. I had to get the firmware for the Broadcom wireless. Intel, nVidia, and ATI radeon graphics cards work fine and can be hardware accelerated with the manufacturer's drivers. ACPI on some Thinkpads requires a kernel option sometimes.

Ubuntu should be your choice of OS if you want to use a laptop. It really does "just work"
