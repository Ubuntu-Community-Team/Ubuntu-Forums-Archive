### Post by dannyboy79 on 2007-03-13
if you're not a gamer than stick with an existing agp card and get a board that has both ddr and ddr2. the ASRock 775dual-VSTA. It works with the Core 2 Duo. I got the E4300 and am going to overclock it and out perform Intel's Core 2 Extreme X6800 -  by 3% (sited here: [http://www.anandtech.com/cpuchipsets/intel/showdoc.aspx?i=2903&p=2)](http://www.anandtech.com/cpuchipsets/intel/showdoc.aspx?i=2903&p=2)), why spend money on a chip when you can do the same thing with an overclock? The only thing the E4300 doesn't have is VT. VT is just Intel's virtualization technology built into the chip.
