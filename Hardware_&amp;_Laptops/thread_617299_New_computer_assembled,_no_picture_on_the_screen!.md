### Post by jespdj on 2007-11-19
A lot of things can be wrong. Maybe you have not connected everything properly. Check all the connections. Do the fans start spinning when you try to switch it on? Do you hear beeps, which are an indication that the BIOS finds that there is something wrong?

Find a friend or other local computer guru who has experience with building computers and let him/her have a look at it.

### Post by Mithrilhall on 2007-11-20
Take out everything minus the memory/cpu.

Toss in a PCI gfx card and see if you get anything on the monitor. If that works or if you don't have a PCI card around try your PCI Express card again. Make sure it's seated properly.

Do you get any POST beep when booting up?
