### Post by Dana on 2005-03-12
I've been running Warty on my A22M Thinkpad for a month or two now. Failed to get my winmodem working but have a pccard modem so all was not lost. Had some other fun along the way. Up until now I had not tried to print and wasn't looking forward to setting up my printer, a Lexmark Z51. Imagine my surprise when I clicked on System Configuration -- Printing -- New Printer, selected Lexmark -- Z51... and it just worked. Careful Linux, making things easy could lead us down the road to Hades.   :D 

Glad I bought the Z51 a few years ago. As far as I know it was the last Lexmark inkjet that worked with OS/2, which is why I bought it. Happy that with Ubuntu it was no problem at all.
