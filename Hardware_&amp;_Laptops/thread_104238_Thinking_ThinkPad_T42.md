### Post by spark888 on 2005-12-15
Hi,
I've been using my T42 for a year now and I'm VERY happy with it. I have triedseveral notebook pc brands - toshiba, fujitsu, compaq, acer but so far, this thinkpad is my favorite. It has a lot of excellent features but what i like the most is its rigid construction (and looks). 

My unit fell from about 5ft. to a concrete pavement, bounced on it's edge (infrared port side) and slid for about a meter on its lcd cover -- short of getting ran over by a minivan. The Thinkpad was on sleep mode when it  happened. I asked my wife to check it out for me as i was too scared to do it myself :). To my relief (and surprise) the unit is intact, save for some scratches on the rubberized lcd cover and a small dent on the left edge (infrared port). 

However, if you are concerned about issues regarding t42 and ubuntu. Better check out the link on lambert's post.
