Here's the page you need. Sounds like you did plug it in correctly.
[ATTACH]38774[/ATTACH]

> if it is the power connectors are there any alternative ways to start the computer with out the power button?
You can unplug the power switch and VERY VERY VERY CAREFULLY briefly short across the the two JP3 PWR-BTN pins (be sure you have the correct pins) with a screwdriver. If the machine starts up, then your power switch or wiring is bad. If not, look elsewhere.

Are you sure you have the power switch plugged onto JP3 PWR-BTN, and not JP4 Reset?

Try another power supply. Most builders have a few spare junkers kicking around.

Recheck all wiring and installation. Be sure main power supply to mainboard connector is fully seated.
