### Post by nic2 on 2005-08-07
Read the Unofficial Ubuntu Starter Guide at [http://www.ubuntuguide.org/](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/) and when you are ready do the installation.  Most things should work fine under Linux provided that you are prepared to read a little about it and ask some questions along the way in order to configure it.  This forum, the mailing list, and other community sites/venues offer support for getting help when you get stuck. 

You should expect a couple of things to be different from Windows but with a basic understanding of Linux and some help from this forum, you should be able to troubleshoot almost anything that goes wrong.

Good luck
