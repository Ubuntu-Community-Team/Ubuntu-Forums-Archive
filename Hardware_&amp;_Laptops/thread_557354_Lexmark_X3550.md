### Post by phidia on 2007-09-22
Generally speaking lexmark printers are not supported in linux-any distro. Some may be-your model isn't even listed at the linuxprinting database though.
You can get a non-free printer driver called turboprint but i don't use it so I'm not sure if it would work for you.
Before buying a printer it's a good idea to check [here]("http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi") for compatibility
