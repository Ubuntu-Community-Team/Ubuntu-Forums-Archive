I finally got my new work laptop.

Quadro FX 1600m with 512MB
HDMI outs
Bluetooth 2.0
Firewire
6x USB ports
802.11 a/b/g/draft n
Fingerprint reader
17" WUXGA
160GB internal disk
Lightscribe dvd+/-rw
Docking station
integrated smart card reader

Kubuntu 7.04 (64-bit) installed like a breeze on this machine on this machine.  Even the touch sensitive volume controls were recognized and working.  The only thing that didn't work from install was the fingerprint reader, and there seems to be a problem with the 64-bit source code from what I've read.  

Chalk up another performance laptop with great linux (*ubuntu) support.  Its not just for economy machines. ;-)
