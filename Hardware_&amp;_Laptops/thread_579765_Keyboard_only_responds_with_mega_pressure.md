### Post by evets on 2007-10-18
**SOLVED** I was just trying things, and held a key down using only normal light pressure, and it registered. So, I repeated it on several other keys, and they worked. Now, I knew they worked without being hit, but there was a delay. So I started looking for settings having a delay. 
I found it in the Universal Access>Keyboard Accessibility menu. Somehow this option became selected, and I de-deselected it. It works as advertised again.
