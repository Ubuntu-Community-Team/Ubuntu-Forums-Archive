Here's an interesting problem. I have a Sony VAIO FE880E and it's an allright laptop. It comes with a card reader (built into the laptop). Now in Ubuntu the card reader works. When I insert a memory stick pro into it, it can download the images off of it. When I put my SD card in there, it also detects images.

Ok..but when I try to download the images.. Well they're not there! The folders that they're located in when the camera takes the pictures are showing up. But the pictures themselves are gone! Or invisible. I try opening the location up in the Nautilus and the images are still gone. Even show hidden files does nothing.

I eject it and put it back in my camera. Yup the images are here. 

So so far I've been downloading images off my SD card in Linux by attaching the camera to the laptop with a USB cable. Which works (it detects it and can download the images).

So why can't it do the same for when the SD card is inserted? It detects images since it greets me with the "do you want to import these images prompt" and the folders they're in. Just not the images themselves!
