### Post by someonestolemyname on 2008-04-09
I believe there is very good tablet support. I have a tablet pc (laptop with tablet built in), and all I had to do was uncomment three lines in /etc/X11/xorg.conf (where it says UNCOMMENT IF YOU HAVE A WACOM TABLET). IT works perfectly. 

So hopefully pretty much any Wacom tablet would work.
