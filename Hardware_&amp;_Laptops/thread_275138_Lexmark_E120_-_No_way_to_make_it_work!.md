### Post by fabiosato on 2006-10-14
Hi!

I managed to make it work with my Ubuntu (Dapper).

Just add the printer via "System->Administration->Printing", choose "New Printer" and select "Lexmark Optra E lj4dith" driver.

Good Luck!
