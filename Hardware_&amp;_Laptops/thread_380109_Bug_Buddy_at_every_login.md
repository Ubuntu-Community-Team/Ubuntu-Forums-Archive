### Post by MarzoRules on 2007-03-12
This happened to me when I went over to the Xfce desktop, which I much prefer to Gnome. I simply wanted to get rid of Bug Buddy every time I logged in and you can do this simply by going to Applications>Settings>Autostarted Applications and unchecking the Evolution Alarm Notifier. I don't use Evolution and doing this doesn't appear to harm anything and I don't get the bug report flashing up now.
