As far as I know, satellite and cable providers will always require a special set-top box to decode HD signals. pcHDTV is just for receiving off-air HDTV broadcasts. I think the newer models also receive unencrypted Cable HD channels. But all the satellite systems I've seen require a set-top decoder box. If the set-top decoder box has an ATSC output, you could connect it to the pcHDTV card to use your computer for PVR functionality. But I don't know if any current set-top boxes do this.
