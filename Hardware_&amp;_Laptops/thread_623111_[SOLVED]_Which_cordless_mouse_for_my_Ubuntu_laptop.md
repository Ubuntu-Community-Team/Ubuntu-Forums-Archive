### Post by chaosrl on 2007-11-29
Just last weekend, one of my friends bought the Microsoft mouse and I picked up a VX Revolution for myself. I must say, both are very nice. bntx is actually quite easy to use; with the walkthrough online, it only took about 10 minutes to get up and running. The mouse itself is solid and wonderful to use.

The Microsoft one is just as good to use, and it's MUCH cheaper to buy. You only get one other button, but to be honest, I wouldn't miss the extra features on the VX too much. They're neat to have, but not essential.

The biggest difference that matters to me is the size of the receiver. I wanted something that was small, but not too hard to pull out (VX Nano?). The Microsoft receiver is notable larger than the Logitech one; I don't know if that matters to you though.
