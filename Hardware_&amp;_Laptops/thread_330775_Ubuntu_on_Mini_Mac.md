### Post by meltingrobot on 2007-01-10
Runs well, could probably run quite a bit better with more RAM, I'm probably going to jump both mine up to 1GB.  It's not bad, but it is noticeable when you get several apps running that more RAM is needed.  That's the same as any other pc though.  Oh, if you haven't looked, unless it's changed since the last time I looked, you do lose the internal speaker as there is not a driver for that yet.  Plug some speakers in or headphones and that works though.  Also, I haven't attempted to do anything with the remote yet.
