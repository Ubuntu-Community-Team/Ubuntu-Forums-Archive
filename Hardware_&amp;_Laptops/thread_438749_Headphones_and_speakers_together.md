### Post by blueturtl on 2007-05-10
Do you mean you can make the line-in function as a line-out in Windows?

Depending on your card that might not be available in Ubuntu.
You can try searching for the option in the mixer (just double click the speaker icon in the upper right corner of the screen).

Then from there click on the Edit menu and select Preferences.
Here you can find things that can be displayed in your mixer.
Try and see if you find the appropriate option there and check it.
Now when you go back to the mixer the options you have selected should be there.
