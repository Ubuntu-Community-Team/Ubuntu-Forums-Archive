### Post by Corey on 2008-01-24
I have a logitech QuickCam Ultra Vision Special Edition. It is one of the more espensive logitechs so I don't know if what I say will hold true for the lower end ones. Its image is great and I get compliments from all the people I skype with on the image quality. I would say it is really worthwhile to get a webcam that supports **true** 1.3 megapixels.
