I'm not sure about reliability, but keep in mind that if an external HD uses standard usb, it will be slower to transfer files, etc. I have a 220Gb external HD and I love it, but it is not good for streaming high quality movies, for example, because of the usb "bottleneck".

On the pro side, external HD's are portable and great for storage.

I personally would (and did) go for the external. But you'll have to weight pros/cons and cost effectiveness.
