### Post by The Avatar of Time on 2008-02-11
I use SATA DVD burners and hard drives and have had no trouble with either. I bought 2 ASUS DVD burners from newegg. Not sure which ones now but in the 30 dollar range, they have both been great. If you plan to do much copying getting 2 is definately worth it. I noticed the one you are looking at is OEM which is fine just bear in mind that means that it does not come with any cables. So you will need a sata and a sata power cable. Or they make a combo cable. Also I am going to assume that your motherboard works with SATA. Not being a jerk just making sure you don't get something that you can't use. But yes I highly recommend going with SATA drives, they are nice. I don't know about performance because I have never had anything other than a SATA so can't help you there. Good luck.
