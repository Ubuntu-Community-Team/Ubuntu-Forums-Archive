### Post by pollock.tar.gz on 2006-07-26
Ok, so I installed linux only 2 days ago (ive had it before), and I had my Dell AIO A960 hooked up to it. It didn't work, as expected with Dell printers. So I decided to scout around my attic and find something that might work. 

Enter: HP - The Abandoned Printer

 So I saw the thing, old, dusty, abandoned for about a year (my brother said it didn't work). Thinking I had nothing to lose, I took the thing down, hooked it up, and within 2 minutes the HP was up, running, and printed the test page perfectly..I could never expect that from my Windows installation.

 I'm amazed at this, and now I fully reccomend HP to anybody that wants to print with linux. 

HP/PWNED/DELL
