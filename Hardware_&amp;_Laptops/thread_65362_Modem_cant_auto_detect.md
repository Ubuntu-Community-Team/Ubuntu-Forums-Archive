### Post by mlomker on 2005-09-13
Laptop modems are difficult to get working under Linux because they are 'software modems' and not real modems, so to speak.  If you purchase Xandros or Linspire the modem will probably be automatically installed.

Otherwise, you are stuck figuring it out on your own.  [This website](http://linmodems.org/) is dedicated to the topic.

good luck.
