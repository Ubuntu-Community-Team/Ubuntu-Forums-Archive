### Post by mrpeanut on 2007-10-07
Any converter should work, because xbox controllers are basically usb devices.  You could do what I did and buy one of those breakaway cables for $3 and a usb extention for another $3 and then cut them both in half.  Once you've got that done just match up the colors to the two new halves that you need and you'll be back in business.
