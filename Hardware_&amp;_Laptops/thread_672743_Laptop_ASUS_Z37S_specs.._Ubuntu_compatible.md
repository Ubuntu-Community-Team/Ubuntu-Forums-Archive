### Post by disembodied on 2008-01-20
I can't offer sure advice, but I run ubuntu on an ASUS S96J, which uses similar stuff and here is what I can tell you:

the intel abg wireless should work just fine, but the wireless-n may have problems.

the nvidia card should work just fine, even for desktop effects but you may have a problem with suspend/hibernate. My ASUS laptop is running ATI graphics (if i would have only waited a little longer I would have got nvidia, darn) and it is unable to suspend/hibernate but it does run compiz fusion fully.

ASUS, overall, seems to work pretty well
