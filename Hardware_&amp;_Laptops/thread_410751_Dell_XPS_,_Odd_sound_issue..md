### Post by mac.ryan on 2007-05-25
I am not on ubuntu right now, so I can't double check the name of right menus. However it should be something like: System -> Preferences -> Sound -> Predefined Mixing Tracks.

Then you can select what tracks you want to put under the control of the master volume.

Shweet, that was so easy

I opened up System -> Preferences -> Sound and then held in Ctrl and selected Master and Master Mono

Now when I use my laptops Volume + and - buttons, both Master and Master Mono sliders in the sound mixer control panel move at the same time.
