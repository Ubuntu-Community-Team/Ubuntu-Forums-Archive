### Post by lisati on 2007-09-09
I use Ubuntu 7.04 on my Toshiba a100 laptop without any major hassles. It needed a little work to get the sound to go (an easy fix, by using "automatic updates"), to play videos (a little more work at the terminal but achievable in a reasonably short time) and to make sure that my USB mouse and USB keyboard didn't lock up after a few minutes (also a fairly easy fix)
