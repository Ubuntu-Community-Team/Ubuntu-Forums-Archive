### Post by EXCiD3 on 2007-05-10
I got a sweet deal when i purchased my laptop. Either an $80 printer for free or $80 a $100 printer. I got the more expensive one. It is an HP C3180. Here is a link: [http://www.shopping.hp.com/product/printers/all_in_ones/1/storefronts/Q8160A%2523ABA](http://www.shopping.hp.com/product/printers/all_in_ones/1/storefronts/Q8160A%2523ABA)

Its really nice, except for the fact that it uses the smaller sized print cartridges which need to be replace more often...Over all it works great. HP is the way to go since they are pretty supportive of Linux. I like my printer, mostly I use it in WinXP, but it does work in Linux. I haven't tried anything but printing a test page on it yet.

Hope you find a good printer.
