### Post by jtibau on 2006-05-13
I think that ubuntu, or more generally linux, has pretty good hardware support when it comes to external modems, like modems and such.

If you want to try first, donwload a ubuntu live cd, and have your DSL modem plugged before you boot, it should work right away. :D
