My main system failed on me yesterday and I decided to build a brand spanking new system from scratch. Recently the Intel Core 2 Duo CPUs seem to be the clear winner in terms of performance, although Linux/Ubuntu support for the required motherboards (especially those based on the P965 chipset) looks problematic. But, according to this wiki entry [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Core_2_Duo_Support]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Core_2_Duo_Support") some success stories were recently added which seems to suggest that most (if not all) of the problems were fixed once and for all with the latest Edgy ISOs from [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily/current]("http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily/current"). I would like to know if anyone has successfully installed and used a Core 2 Duo based desktop in the past few weeks. TIA.
