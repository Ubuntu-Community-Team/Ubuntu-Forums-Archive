### Post by philidox on 2008-04-16
Well after days of searching I found the answer to my question.  Ubuntu Mobile is meant for MID'S(Media Internet Device) and UMPC(Ultra Mobile Personal Computer) which are basically the next level of computers.  Laptop can be kind of a pain to carry around, but these MID are meant to combine laptops, mp3 players, pda's, smartphones, etc all into one.  Ubuntu Mobile are for these devices not mobile phones per se.  The processor meant for Ubuntu Mobile to run on could be in a mobile phone but thats not a reality as of right now.  My hope of putting Ubuntu Mobile on my iPhone clone are long gone.  O well
