### Post by swudee on 2007-11-23
Yes the power supply's are mostly generic.
The main thing to look for, apart from physical dimensions and  plugs of course are the power ratings. These will be on the side of the supply. Depending on the age most will be something like 350 - 450 watts. The other thing to watch is the 12 volt current.
As long as these match or exceed the original you shouldn't have any problems.
Some brands are better than others. I was looking at this site last night, you might find interesting.
