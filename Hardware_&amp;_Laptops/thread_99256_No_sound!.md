### Post by wbiggers on 2005-12-05
I had a similar problem after purchasing a soundblaster card for an older computer.  Then I discovered that there are several different audio channels and you can control volume for each one.  Try opening the audio volume control (double click or right-click>properties) the speaker icon.  Raise the volume for all channels.  Then you can play around with each channel to determine which one your speakers are connected to.
