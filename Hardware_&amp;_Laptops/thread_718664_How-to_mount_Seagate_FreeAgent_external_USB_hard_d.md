### Post by snjokaggl on 2008-03-08
Well, I like many other people have been in trouble getting my FreeAgent drive to mount with Ubuntu. I've searched alot with Google but never found any real answers except to format it from ntfs to ext2 or something similar. But I have alot of data on my drive so that wasn't an option, soooo... If you have any computer still running Windows or something hat still mounts your drive, mount it and DELETE Ceedo (a program that comes pre-installed on your Seagate FreeAgent drive) and the autorun file. I did that and still have all my data and it mounts easily with Ubuntu now. Hope this helps :D
