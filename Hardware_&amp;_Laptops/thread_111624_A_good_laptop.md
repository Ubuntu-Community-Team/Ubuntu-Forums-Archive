### Post by redsmoke on 2006-01-03
I have an Acer Aspire 5002 it is running great under 64-bit ubuntu it meets your spec requirements. and it was only $650US. The wireless didn't work out of the box(but from what i have read about laptops and linux most don't) and the ACPI(Battery Meter) didn't report correctly. I had do some searching on the forums to get both working but it only took a few hours to get it figured out.  There is an entry in the wiki on getting ACPI running and this thread will tell you how to get the wireless working [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=110749](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=110749)

So after getting those two going only real issue which really isn't an issue is the sound the actual volume is actually PCM and master will not adjust the volume.(mildly anonying but really how often do you adjust the volume)

I am pretty happy Acer Aspire 5002 user it is a nice slim wide screen 64-bit laptop at a price that is to good to pass up
