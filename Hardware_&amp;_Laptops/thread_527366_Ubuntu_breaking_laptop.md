### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-18
No, you can't physically break your laptop by installing Ubuntu, or any otherOS, for that matter.

I suggest a few  things.

1. You may want to download the gParted LiveCD and burn the iso to a CD and use it to completely re-format your HDD for NTFS.

2. When you do re-install Windows, make sure that you do it by the numbers and not just quickly click through things.

3. Do you have the driver disk that came with your laptop? That will almost certainly have the drivers for your wireless, screen resolution, keyboard setup, etc.

If not, go to your laptop manufacturer's website and download the CD for your particular model.
