### Post by francesco_m on 2007-04-20
you need to install the restricted modules for your kernel version
because of the nvidia video card

open a terminal:
sudo apt-get install linux-restricted-modules-2.6.20-15-generic

every time you update the kernel remember to follow this step.

Francesco.
