### Post by gavin on 2006-10-24
I have a an Acer aspire 1642 which is famous for its Realtek ALC883 sound card, not supported by the version of ALSA in Dapper. 

In the latest version of Alsa driver, 1.0.13 at time of posting, the problem has been fixed. 

So all I need to do to fix my sound card is ... update to Edgy. 

But apart from the sound card, everything works beautifully in Dapper. 

So decision, decision. 

The question is which version of alsa is present in Edgy? If its 1.0.13, I upgrade right now :)
