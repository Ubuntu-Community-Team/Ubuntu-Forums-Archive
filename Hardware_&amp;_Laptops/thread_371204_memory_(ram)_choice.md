### Post by jotagab on 2007-02-26
When you say at most 1GB you mean in total or per slot?
If 1GB is the maximum amount possible then I'd buy 2x512MB. If the motherboard is smart enough it can access both of them at the same time, which would give you a (hardly noticeable...) speed increase. Besides you won't have "all eggs in one basket".
If it's 1GB per slot then I'd go for the 1x1GB, so that you can add more later.
