### Post by johannes on 2005-11-22
I wrote [this]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=504374&postcount=2") in another thread. I'm not sure if it works on your computer, but I don't know why it shouldn't. It will set up your irda port and tell how to send files to/from your computer using the console.

Also, I don't have a Nokia phone, and the phone I use doesn't have the option to sync calendar stuff, and can only send one contact at a time, so I don't know about that stuff. If you want a GUI and a simple way to transfer, you may want to have a look at [gnokii]("http://www.gnokii.org/") and [gnocky]("http://www.linuxsoft.cz/en/sw_detail.php?id_item=2950"). Gnokii is included in the repos, but not gnocky. The latter you'll have to complie yourself. Again, I haven't used those, so you'll probably have to read the documentation.
