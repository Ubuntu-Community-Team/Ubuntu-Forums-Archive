### Post by trwrt on 2008-04-21
In Windows you can just make a folder on the drive, right-click on it, and pick "Sharing and Security".  From there you can select to share the drive with other machines.  On the other machines you go into "My Network Places" and you should be able to see it.  Of course this would mean you'd have to leave the desktop running all the time.

You could also put the drive into something like this and then just plug it into your router:
