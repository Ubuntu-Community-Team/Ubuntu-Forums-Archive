### Post by lexarrow on 2007-05-27
maybe this could help:

1.click anywhere on your desktop (except an icon) then press Alt+F2
2.type in: gnome-terminal
3.type in (each line separately): 
```
sudo -i
gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf
```
now, everywhere you see a horizontal list of resolutions add in front of the list your desired resolution (in quotes)
4.restart x server: press ctrl-alt-backspace 
5. change your resolution
