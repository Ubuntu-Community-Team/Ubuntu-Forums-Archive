### Post by vicG on 2005-10-02
AFAIK, there are no manufacturers that design for linux.  That would probably be financial suicide, since 90% of the planet runs M$.
There are vendors; however, who sell linux notebooks, such as Emperor Linux, and maybe you should check them out.  I saw Emperor Linux at the LinuxWorld Conf in NYC a coupla years back, and they had the latest/greatest Sony notebooks on display -- all the little Sony gizmos were working perfectly under Linux, the built-in scrollbars, the wireless, the 7-in-1 reader, the screen fade, hibernate/suspend, etc, etc.
[http://www.emperorlinux.com/](http://www.emperorlinux.com/)
PS No, I don't work for them, I've just been in your boat, where you just don't have the time to spend getting everything tweaked.
