### Post by entropic_existence on 2008-02-06
Quite sure that for hardware problems the OS won't matter in terms of warranty. If you live in the US you are covered under the same laws concerning warranties that they introduced about cars and that means that unless they can directly link the problem to a part that you replaced yourself (if you upgraded RAM, installed a new HDD, etc) or to the OS they cannot legally refuse to honour your warranty.

Although you may need to remind them of that fact if they say otherwise. The laws were brought in because otherwise you experienced vendor lock-in which produces de facto monopolies which are of course illegal under US law.

You should be ok but if you want to play it safe throwing Vista back on or calling and checking first are options available to you.
