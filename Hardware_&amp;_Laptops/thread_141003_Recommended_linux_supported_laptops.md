### Post by K.Mandla on 2006-03-18
I've had pretty good luck with Ubuntu on nearly every laptop I've worked with, so long as it wasn't more than eight years old or so. There seem to be some hardware issues, particularly with PCMCIA ports, on machines that predate P3 chips when they're combined with the 2.6-series kernel. It's frustrating, but so long as you're not looking for something that old, it shouldn't be an issue.
