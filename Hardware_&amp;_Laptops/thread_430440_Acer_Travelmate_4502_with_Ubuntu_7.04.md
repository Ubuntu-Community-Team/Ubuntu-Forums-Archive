Just wanted to inform those who have this computer and that are curious on switching over to Ubuntu that it supports Ubuntu very well.

I just installed 7.04 on a friends Travelmate 4502 and I had no problem at all. I got Beryl to work too with no trouble. The builtin wireless works too.

The guide I used: [http://www.ubuntuguide.org](http://www.ubuntuguide.org)
