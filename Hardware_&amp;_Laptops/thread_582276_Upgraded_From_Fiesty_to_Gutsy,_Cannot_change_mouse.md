### Post by lilalmond on 2008-03-11
Hi there

i had problems changing my mouse them as well since im a noob at linux but i finally got it to work
The easiest way to do it
Downaload the theme you want (i downloaded mine from gnome-look.org)
Once you downloaded it on your desktop dont even unpack it just go in system/administration/synaptic and look for the name of the theme you just downloaded and install it from there.
Then simply right click on desktop and select change background, then go in mouse pointer and your new theme should be there.
Worked out perfectly for me
Hope i helped  :)
