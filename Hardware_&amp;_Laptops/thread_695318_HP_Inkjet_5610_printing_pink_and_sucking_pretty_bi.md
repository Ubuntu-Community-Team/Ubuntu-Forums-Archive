### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-02-26
My brother blamed a Windows update for bad printing on his two Epson units.  Turns out a thorough cleaning fixed the issue.  Just cleaning the cart with the utility does not get it sometimes (I have 3 HP printers .. one a laser)  You have to open them up, remove the cartridges, and clean the contact points in the carriage using swab/alcohol or even swab/Windex.  I print a LOT so I clean mine every time I put in new ink carts. ALSO, your color cart could be bad or nearly out of ink .. they do not run out uniformly when it comes to the color cartridge.  That depends on what you are printing.  
In the US, Walgreen's drug stores refill and TEST carts .. 10 for black and 15 for color and they guaranty their work.
