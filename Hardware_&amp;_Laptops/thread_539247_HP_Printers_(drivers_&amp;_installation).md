### Post by johnmadson on 2007-08-31
HPIJS, as far as I can tell, is a newer version of HPLIP (which may already be installed on your system). To install your printer, I highly recommend you go to Synaptic and install HPIJS. Now the  application "HPLIP Tools" should be installed.  Open it and follow the printer installation wizard. Most HP printers are supported, and they even have support for scanners.

Also see [http://hplip.sourceforge.net](http://hplip.sourceforge.net) for more details.

Note that you may already have HPLIP (version 1.73) installed, but installing HPIJS (version 2.7.2) would extend your printer support.
