### Post by zuccs on 2007-08-04
UPDATE: To anyone with the same sort of problem, I think the problem was actually with the PCI --> SATA card, even though the hard drives were recognised in Ubuntu. It didn't like going through the PCI to SATA interface, and then through the other interface to the hard drive.
