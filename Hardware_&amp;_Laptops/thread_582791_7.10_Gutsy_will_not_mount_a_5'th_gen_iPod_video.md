### Post by vinnn on 2007-10-20
iPods don't use the NTFS filesystem.
They (as most MP3 players do) use FAT32.

Have a look at what is happening when you plug in your iPod using the Gnome system log viewer or the **dmesg** command.
i.e.
[LIST]
[*]Open a terminal
[*]Run **dmesg**
[*]Plug in your iPod and wait about 10 seconds
[*]Run **dmesg** again, notice you have some new messages on the system log.
[*]Not working? Post the new messages.
[/LIST]
