### Post by walmartshopper67 on 2008-03-26
I know this is an old thread, but... I have an Everex Stepnote - and, well for the price it's great. BUT, it's got a few quirks. I was running Feisty on it, then switched to Gentoo at the moment, but the problems carried over - switching to a virtual terminal from X gives you a goofy looking black and white gibberish thing, VERY close to the "Matrix" screensavers, just scrolling horizontal, and in B&W.  The headphone jack doesnt work, you'll have sound coming out of the speakers, plug your head phones in and the sound will stop coming out of your speakers.....but nothing comes out of your headphones. The screen dimmer thing doesn't work at all. I get the same goofy Bios error as Arkscott, but it still boots up.


Other than that it handles both Gentoo and Ubuntu very well, it's smooth and responsive, and the screen is very crisp. The wireless card is an Atheros so it isn't really too bad to get it to work, not like a broadcom-type nightmare.

The greatest thing about this thing has been the support though. The 3rd night of using it - it started acting weird, blank screen on bootup with BIOS beeps. We tried everything we could think of, and then realized they had 24 hour support. I called the Everex people up and 2:30 AM (EST) and the guy walked me through it (had to re-seat the RAM, which is behind the "Warranty" seal, so call them first before you open it), and after maybe 15 minutes it was back. And, they DIDN'T care that I had Linux on it, they actually had a little thing in their touch-tone jail, push 2 if you're running linux).  If you don't mind some goofyness, it's not a bad laptop for ~$400 with some great service.
