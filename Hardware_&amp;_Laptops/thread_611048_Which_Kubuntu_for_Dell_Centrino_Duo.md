### Post by reckless2k2 on 2007-11-13
open box browsing with i386 is pretty simple. you have to do more tweaks to get 64 working with browsing. things are coming around but that is just one of the most obvious since everyone is concerned about browsing. nothing is really taking advantage of the 64bit bus anyway except high end servers. you won't be using any applications taking advantage of it in general. unless you are doing some crazy scientific calculations and such, just stick with 64bit. if you want to "play" with it, then go right ahead but you will run into issues that you will not be able to circumvent or will require quite a bit of tweaking to work. if you want to be a 64bit bug tester, go right ahead. if you are concerned about being productive without much hangup, then stick to i386.
