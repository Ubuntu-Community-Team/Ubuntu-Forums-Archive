### Post by Penguin_Newbie on 2008-04-18
A friend suggested this:

> Also, some people have reported that dropping to a virtual console (Ctrl+Alt+F2) allows them to adjust the brightness with the Fn keys. You can then bring it back up out of console mode by pressing Alt+F7.

And it worked, brightness is now back up to proper levels. Hope this helps others with similar problems.
