### Post by amgra on 2006-12-09
I just wanted to share with you my experience, I am very newbe to ubuntu, and so far I am enjoying to discover how to do things here :)

I have a Dell 1700 Laserjet Printer, and a Dell 3300 wireless print server, I wanted to setup it in my ubuntu 6.10 and the first thing stopped me was I checked the list of available printers on System=>Administration=>Printing=>Add new printer and there was no Dell 1700, after a research on the web I found this very useful page [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsPrinters](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsPrinters) where they explain I had to configure my printer as a Generic Dell PCL 4. Because I am very stuborn, I tried PCL 6 (the latest version I had in my list) and it caused every time a "Paper Jam" error after the first page. Than I tried Postcript and only trash came out of my printer, so I just left it at it should work Dell 1700 Laser printer ===>>>  Generic Dell Laser PCL 4

By the way my dell 3300 wireless printer, works great as an HP JetDirect on the IP of my print server and port 9100
