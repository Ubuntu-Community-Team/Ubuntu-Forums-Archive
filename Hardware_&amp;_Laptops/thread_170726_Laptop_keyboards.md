[QUOTE=adam.tropics]In Breezy, the keyboard showed up as  Generic 104 US/Int or something like that, now I am using Dapper it shows up as 101.[/QUOTE]

An ordinary keyboard (US style) has 104 keys. 101-key is its version without Win/Menu keys (remember the old days?).

European layouts usually have one more key, which is placed between the left Shift and Z keys. So, European keyboards are 105- or 102-key keyboards (I have the latter here).
