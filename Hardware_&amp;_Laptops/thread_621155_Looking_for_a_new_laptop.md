### Post by catfishk on 2007-11-23
i have had good luck with Thinkpads.  There is a Wiki devoted to them that has been quite helpful over the course of 3 machines here: [http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/ThinkWiki](http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/ThinkWiki)

 most any laptops will give you some gruff with either wireless, video, or obscure hardware drivers.  your best bet is to find a machine with common hardware that is well supported.  if you select a few models you might like, and check the following boards, you can get a good idea as to what issues have arisen for people with each hardware configuration

  [http://tuxmobil.org/mylaptops.html](http://tuxmobil.org/mylaptops.html)
  [http://www.linux-laptop.net/](http://www.linux-laptop.net/)
 and, of course
  [http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Category:Models](http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Category:Models)

 good luck!
