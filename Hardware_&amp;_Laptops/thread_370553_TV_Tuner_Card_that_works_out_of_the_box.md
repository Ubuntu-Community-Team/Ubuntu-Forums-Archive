### Post by newlinux on 2007-03-29
I believe in Feisty you will have many options for tuners that work out of the box:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MythTVTeam/MythTVHardwareSupport](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MythTVTeam/MythTVHardwareSupport) 
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=390776](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=390776)

However for edgy, the one I know that worked "out of the box" for me was the Fusion 5 series (specifically for me the fusion 5 RT lite, but I don't think that one does video capture). I was able to tune NTSC signals without doing anything. Are you looking for hardware encoding of NTSC signals, ATSC, QAM? The Fusion 5 does NTSC/ATSC/QAM but does not do hardware encoding of NTSC. I believe the fusion 5 gold has an input for capture, but I can't guarantee that functionality works out of the box...

And it these work with myth well... Just a little overpriced if you ask me, compared to other cards that do the same thing.
