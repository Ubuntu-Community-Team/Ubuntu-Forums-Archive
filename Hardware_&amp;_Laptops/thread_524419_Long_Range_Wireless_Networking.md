### Post by insane_alien on 2007-08-13
lineof sight is not all you need to worry about.

there is signal strength and signal to noise ratios. both of these get worse with distance.

you'll want to use directional antennas and have the router at the hill. how far appart are the places as even directional antenna signals degrade overdistance quite rapidly.

### Post by Blindraven on 2007-08-13
Get yourself a Parabolic dish and a directional "cube" antenna and route the ratio's in to the one feedback.

You should find you get your city blocks signals, but only the directional cubes position strength.
