### Post by mgmiller on 2006-10-02
I had a similar problem with my scanner (Epson Perfection 3200 photo) and it turned out to be a USB port issue.  With the scanner plugged into the pci card USB ports, it had this problem, when I plugged it into a USB 2.0 port built into my motherboard, the problem went away.  I then replaced the pci USB card with a new one and that one worked properly.  The old card that did not work, had an ALI chipset M5271 A1.  This was weird because everything else that I plugged into that card worked fine.  Just my scanner refused to work right.
