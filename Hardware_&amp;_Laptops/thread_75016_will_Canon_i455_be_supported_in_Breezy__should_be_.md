### Post by donar73 on 2005-10-14
Here's how I did it:

1. select "new printer" in your system-settings
2. "Canon i455" will be recognized automaticly, click "next"
3. in the driver-selection page select "BJC 7000" (the one where it says "gimp-print") and click on "Use" or "OK" (I have a german Breezy here, don't know how it's named in english...)
4. you now have a new printer named "BJC-7000" in your "Printer"-window, right-click on it for it's properties
