### Post by doctoss on 2007-05-18
My compaq 1600i was stuck at resolution of 800x600 and I could not change it. I finally read the instructions on the xorg.conf file and typed at the terminal "man xorg.conf" and just changed the driver from vesa to neomagic and the selected the resolution, rebooted and voila. I could not use the laptop with that 800x600 resolution.
