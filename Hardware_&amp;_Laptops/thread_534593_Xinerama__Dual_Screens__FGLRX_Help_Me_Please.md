### Post by fuzz500 on 2007-04-15
Wuhoo!... found this [http://www.cuberick.com/?p=48](http://www.cuberick.com/?p=48)
Worked for me... it looks like you have to add this to your xorg.conf

Section "Extensions"
        Option      "Composite" "disable"
EndSection

Note: on my nvidia setup composite had to be enabled, so this must be a quirk of ATI cards... 
Hope that helps, lads.
