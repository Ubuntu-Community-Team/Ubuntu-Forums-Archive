I'm currently using the HP PSC 1610  If your running it under windows, the software it comes with will slow your bootup slightly.  If linux, HP Linux printing and imaging system works perfectly.

AS far as the printer itself, the ink cartridges are fairly long lasting (the set that came with it lasted about 3 months).  The scanner, once perfectly silent, is now makes a small noise, but works fine, so nothing too bad.  I also use the scanner heavily (Every important paper I get i.e. exam/quiz/homework is scanned and stored in a triple encrypted volume (gotta love truecrypt).  

The pictures it prints are as good as you can get with inkjet.
