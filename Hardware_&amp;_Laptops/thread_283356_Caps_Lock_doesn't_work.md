### Post by Spear on 2006-11-01
Here's how i made it finally work :
Went to (in my french Gnome desktop) System/Preferences/Keyboard, then i chose the third tab (options), there you choose the third option, about Caps lock, and then the eighth (the last one in fact).

It now works, but the led indicating wether Caps are on or not doesn't work anymore ... but i heard about a litte program that could indicate if it is on or not, haven't tested it yet, and don't know if it would fixthat new issue.
