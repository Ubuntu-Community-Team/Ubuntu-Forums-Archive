### Post by emteeoh on 2007-06-26
I've installed Ubuntu 7.04 on a P5020D, with only minor problems:

1) to get the native 1280x768 resolution, I had to install the "915resolution" package. As soon as I did and rebooted, it naturally went to that resolution.
2) The atheros wifi was detected just fine, but I had some problems configuring it. In my home, I use WEP with a 128 bit key. If I tried to do a "manual configuration" it did not work. If I just selected my wifi network from the pulldown  and right-clicked on it and entered my key, it worked fine.
3) Wifi does not recover if I hibernate the machine.

Wired ethernet, modem, and battery are all correctly detected etc.
Sound works perfectly, so does the touchpad, including the scroll button.
CD and DVDs play fine, but I have not yet tried to burn.
I have not used firewire, SD, PCMCIA  or CF. I have also not tested the vga out.


This is a huge improvement over basically all linux distributions I have tried in the past, although I need to admit its been over a year since I last tried. Previous attempts were bad for the blood pressure.
