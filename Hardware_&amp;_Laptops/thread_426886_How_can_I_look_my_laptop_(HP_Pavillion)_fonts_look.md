### Post by dizee on 2007-04-28
By default Ubuntu's font rendering is pretty poor, but you can edit it to get great looking fonts by going to System>Preferences>Font. Under "Font rendering" click the "details" box and play around with the settings. On my laptop the settings I use are Subpixel smoothing, Slight hinting, and RGB subpixel order.
