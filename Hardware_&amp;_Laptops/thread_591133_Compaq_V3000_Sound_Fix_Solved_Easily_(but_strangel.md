Ok for those of you that installed ubuntu but the sound doesn't work right away

Plug in a USB headset and see if you get sound through that, if you do then continue with this method....

Unplug the headset, restart the computer, and your sound should work!

Warning, Hibernating the computer can cause it to stop working, so i dont recommend it.

My laptop: Compaq V3016US
Sound card:  82801G (ICH7 Family) High Definition Audio Controller
Headset: Logitech USB headset
