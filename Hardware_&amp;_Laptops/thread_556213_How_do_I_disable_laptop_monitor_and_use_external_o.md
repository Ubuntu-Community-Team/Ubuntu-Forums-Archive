### Post by mlissner on 2007-09-21
The greatest tip I ever learned in regards to external monitors is this: BEFORE ubuntu boots, while the bios is running, hit the Fn + F2 key (or whatever key it is that switches monitors on your laptop). This will switch things in the bios until you reboot, and essentially takes the software side of things more or less out of the equation.

Admittedly, this is a hack, but in a few more months we should have a new version of X that can handle this kind of task.

Hope that helps, if not, I hope you're better at solving this problem than I was.
