### Post by jnorthr on 2008-04-20
Have you ever been able to connect to your ISP before ? Have you ever been able to do it on the same system using windose ? Do you have any security provisions enabled on your wifi ?

My guess is the blinking light is cos the wifi is searching the airwaves for different frequencies/ networks to join cos it's not happy with what it's found so far. It may do this for quite a while until it finds a network to connect to, so you are correct it will not respond much while it does the 'hunt'. The other possibility is that your system may be near other systems and they are causing interference to you system, in which case your system, 'by design' will reduce it's signal rate until it can 'talk' to another system, so this might give you a slow network connection.
