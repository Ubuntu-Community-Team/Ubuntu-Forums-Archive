### Post by vyruss on 2007-03-09
Why don't you install the driver from the repositories? Just go to **Synaptic**, **Settings -> Repositories** and make sure **restricted** is ticked. Then click **Reload** and choose to install the package **xorg-driver-fglrx**. **Apply**, and you should be all done.
