### Post by Ptero-4 on 2006-03-28
To be 100% sure that your box is going to work in ubuntu you need to ensure that your mobo is either:
1) Made in 2001 or earlier (2002+ models have TCPA), or.
2) The same brand/make/model of the one inluded in the PowerMac G5 or earlier model (iMac/eMac/Mac mini and portable Mac mobos are based on mini-ITX ones which you probably aren't powerful enough for your needs and MacTel ones may contain TCPA hardware).

P.d: you don't need to get yourself a Mac, only the mobo that Apple normally puts in their PowerPC PowerMacs
