### Post by em3raldxiii on 2008-02-10
Yup, for any multimedia creation, higher processor speed will make a noticeable difference (although price is definitely going to be a factor).  If you are going to get really hard into multimedia creation (such as using Audacity), you might consider a 0-latency kernel.  You might also want to check out ubuntustudio.

On the other hand, I have 0 experience with midi on my own Linux box, so that may be a factor.

For a jump from 1.6 to 2.0 alone, I dunno ... maybe worth a couple hundred bux ... max. Unless the machine comes with other upgrades too.  Just my opinion, of course :D
