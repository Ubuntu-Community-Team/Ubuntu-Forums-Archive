### Post by Qrk on 2005-12-06
USB modems are still hit and miss, but breezy has made huge strides in supporting them. I have not used your exact modem, but I've installed breezy on two machines that used USB ones. Both worked out of the box. 

My suggestion is to try the LiveCD. Someone here might have your modem, and could give you a better answer, but the LiveCD is the best test of hardware out there.
