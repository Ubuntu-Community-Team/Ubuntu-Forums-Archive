### Post by luisromangz on 2008-01-30
The differences are that AIGLX is integrated with the real X server, so you get all the features of a normal X server. You can activate and deactivate compiz, lets say because you want to play a game, and then activate compiz again without logging out.

AIGLX is the real deal, XGL is a fix for those who couldn't use AIGLX, because of drivers (like the older ATI drivers) or other reasons.
