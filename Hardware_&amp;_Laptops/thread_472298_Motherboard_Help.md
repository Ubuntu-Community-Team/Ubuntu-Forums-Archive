### Post by jbag on 2007-06-14
Hi anonymous131

I would check with Compaq (now part of HP), to find out about how proprietary the motherboard is.

I have found that in my travels as a computer tech that most name brand clones such as Compaq, HP, Dell & others are proprietary to the point that the motherboard is built to fit in the case it came in & will not fit in a standard ATX case. This also holds true that other name brand (MSI, ECS, Asus, Biostar, etc.) standard or mini-atx boards may not fit in the Compaq case you currently have.That, unfortunately is the fact of buying one of these clones. 

I am not saying motherboards are absolutly not compatible, but they may not be.

Check first before you spend any money.

Hope this helps

jbag
