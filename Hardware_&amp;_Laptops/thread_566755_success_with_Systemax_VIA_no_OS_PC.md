Hello, World.  I am a North American Newby who had an easy time installing Fiesty Fawn on a new Sytemax VIA no O/S PC.  It cost $279 plus shipping on Oct 2007, from Tiger Direct.

It's CPU is a 1.5 giga hertz VIA C7.   My keyboard, mouse, printer and internet connection are all working fine.  The VIA C7 CPU may sound slow, but I chose the system because the CPU runs on less than 20 Watts and is supposed to generate less heat.

I also bought a book called Beginning Ubuntu Linux version 2 from Borders Bookstore for about $30.
