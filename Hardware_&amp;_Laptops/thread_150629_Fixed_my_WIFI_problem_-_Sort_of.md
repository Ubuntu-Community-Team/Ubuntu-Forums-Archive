So,
dont give up all you newcommers this is my tale of woe.

After 3 days of installing, re-installing 4 different versions of linux, trying ndis wrapper and dozens of drivers. Even managed to compile some stuff from source - wow! 3 different wifi cards, swapped around with the XP machine before and after each install to make sure I hadn't broke them.

I was ready to give up..... Problem was it is a second PC, used to learn (!) linux,  and the only place I'm allowed to put this PC by the wife would have to be wireless as I was not allowed to lift floor boards and chip away walls/ re-decorate etc.

Then it came to me. A wireless bridge! use the wired card with this! (the products 'usually' advertised to get Xbox or PS2 games machines up and running on the net).

I live in the UK and got one from [www.ebuyer.com](www.ebuyer.com) for less than 20 quid. Believe me that so cheap considering I spend about 20 hours on this bloody think.

Man was I happy.
I hope to get a proper wifi card working, but man this is a cheap option, in time and money, considering.
