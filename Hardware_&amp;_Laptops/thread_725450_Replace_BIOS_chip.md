### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-03-15
Unless you actually KNOW what you are doing, you should never flash ANYTHING as it makes a permanent change if it works and turns it into a brick if it doesn't work.  Without taking it into a tech and having them look at it and give a prognosis, no way to venture just how much damage was done. And no way for you to replace a bios chip either in a laptop or a desktop unless you are a real expert at soldering.
