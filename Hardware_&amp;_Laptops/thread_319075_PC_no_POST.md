### Post by hkq35 on 2006-12-15
Follow up for future references .

Upon closer inspection of the graphics card it seems 2 of the electrolytic capacitors have bulged , swollen and popped open, releasing a flux like fluid.
The same thing happened on my last motherboard, which resulted in random shutdowns- increasing in frequency over time.
If you have to troubleshoot without being able to swap things around, check the capacitors.
Look for bulging capacitors, they should be perfectly flat on the top and well sealed.
