### Post by CrimsoniteX on 2008-03-31
Ubuntu comes standard with 'System Monitor', which should cover everything you need.
System -> Administration -> System Monitor

If you want to keep an eye on your CPU clocks, Right click the top menu bar, select 'Add to Panel...' and select 'CPU Frequency Scaling Monitor' under System and Hardware.
