### Post by strategischen on 2006-11-14
I had the same problem in the past.

Random restarts are very difficult to solve.

check that your power supply is working as expected, you have good power cables and very important that you have the latest BIOS.

you can put on google random restart and you will find out that it's really nothing very easy to solve.

If everything still wrong then I'll suggest you to try to unplug different components of your hardware.

And yes once you put the live CD on your computer if you have internet by DHCP you can even surf automatically the CD has a lot of drivers and get them to put you on the internet.


In my case I found out that it was my power supply who was generating this errors but options and answers can be many.

Good luck!
