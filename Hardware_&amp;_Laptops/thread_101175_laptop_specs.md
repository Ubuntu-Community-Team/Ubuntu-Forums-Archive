### Post by quietglow on 2005-12-09
Be careful which onboard video you use! Anything based on the Intel 915 chipset is hit-and-miss right now--they are usually called Intel Media Accelator 900 or something similar. Older 815 onboard video is supported well.

I know that a stock Edubuntu install--and it has a whole lot of additional software--is about 6.5 gigs. I ran Hoary on a 20g laptop drive and it seemed like I always had plenty of room for d/l stuff etc.
