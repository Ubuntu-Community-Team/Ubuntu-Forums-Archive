### Post by l.tambiah on 2006-05-26
You dont need one, wireless works out of the box in most cases, the IBM Thinkpad R50e has an Intel Pro Wi-Fi wireless technology built inside to connect to 802.11b and 802.11g wi-fi networks. I use wireless all the time on my IBM Thinkpad R50e at home and at my University where I work. I'm glad to say it works really well. 

I'll also add that the latest release candidate for the Ubuntu dapper release is looking very stable now and is running perfectly on my IBM Thinkpad R50e. Im very impressed with dapper and have to say well done for everyone involved. It's amazing to see the difference in 7 and a half months (including the delay), this is a great achievement and is an example of how rapid development of top quality software is produced by the free software and open source communities.
