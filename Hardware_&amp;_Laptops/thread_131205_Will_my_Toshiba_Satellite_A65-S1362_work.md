Hey there,
The best and the easiest way to find out is by Downloading a Live CD and trying it out. By using a liveCD you can run the operating system without having to install it on the hard drive. That way... you can easily find out if your hardware works with  Ubuntu or not...

I use Ubuntu on my laptop (Compaq presario) and My touchpad, ATi Radeon IGP 320 graphics card and my Broadcom Wireless card work. Well..the first 2 worked out of the box and my Wifi card took abt 10 mins to tweaking.
