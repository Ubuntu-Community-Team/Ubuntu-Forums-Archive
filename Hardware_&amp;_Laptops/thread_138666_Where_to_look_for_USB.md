### Post by prizrak on 2006-03-02
It should automount it for you and place a shortcut on the desktop. All your mounted drives go into /media. If you want to find the device itself (w/o mounting) it will be in /dev. Try this site [http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Main_Page](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Main_Page) it has the info on mounting drives.
