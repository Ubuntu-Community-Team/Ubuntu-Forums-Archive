The AMD64 3700+ will be noticeably faster -- more cache means the processor wastes less clock cycles waiting for the main RAM. It also runs 200 MHz faster. It will also fold proteins better ;) (see my sig).

All that aside, just ask yourself if the performance increase is worth the cost. If you are just compiling, you probably won't be stretched for CPU power, and the Athlon64 3200+ is fairly quick anyway (although there WILL be a noticable performance difference).

BTW, [http://www.tomshardware.com/]("http://www.tomshardware.com/") has great CPU charts where you can compare benchmarks on two different CPUs.
