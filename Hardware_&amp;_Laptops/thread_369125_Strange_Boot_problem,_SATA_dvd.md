### Post by robenroute on 2007-02-24
Could be any number of causes:
- bad cable
- faulty BIOS
- faulty SATA drive
- motherboard

.... to name a few. Start with simpe things: e.g. replace the cable and/or upgrade to the most recent BIOS. You could also give your drive a spin in a different PC, to see whether that makes any difference.
