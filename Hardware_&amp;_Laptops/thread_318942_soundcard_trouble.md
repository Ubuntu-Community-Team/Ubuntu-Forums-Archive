### Post by dmizer on 2006-12-14
double click on the volume icon in your tool bar (if it's not there, right click and add it).  check to make sure that the "pcm" volume slider is turned up to at least 80%.

for some reason, if you type something while the xine window (or a few other windows) has keyboard focus, the pcm volume gets turned off.  no idea why.
