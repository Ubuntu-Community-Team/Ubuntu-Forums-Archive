### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-04-05
Both of those are 3rd party versions of some of the newer models that ATI and NVidia make.  I know I've seen people have trouble with the Radeon HD cards, but the radeonhd driver should take care of it.  The newer nvidia card will require the restricted drivers as well.
I can't say for sure if these cards will work for you, but you can always try.  The biggest concern would be that they are 3rd party modifications of the standard cards.
