### Post by futz on 2006-04-08
For my old Ubuntu box (before I built a new one with native USB 2.0) I went to my favorite local computer shop and bought a "AP USB 2.0 (6 Port) PCI Card" for $17 (in Canada). It gives you 4 ports in back and 2 inside the case.

The sticker on the card says, "ALI 6 Port USB".

It was autodetected with no problems and works great!

Other brands will work too. They're cheap, and any computer store will have them in stock.

EDIT: I just read a post over on [http://linuxquestions.org](http://linuxquestions.org) and a guy there says his Adaptec works fine in linux.
