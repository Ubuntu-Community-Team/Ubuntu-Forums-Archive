### Post by UltraMathMan on 2006-08-03
I just bought a Dell Inspiron E1505, of the following specs:

-2.0Ghz Intel Centrino Duo T2500
-2GB DDR2 SDRAM @ 533Mhz
-15.4" SXGA+ widescreen
-100GB hard drive @ 5400rpm
-Intel Pro 3945 a/b/g wireless internal
-Dell 350 Bluetooth wireless internal
-256MB ATI MOBILITY RADEON X1400 HyperMemory
-Integrated Sound Blaster Audigy HD
-85 WHr 9-cell Lithium Ion Primary Battery

Wireless worked without any config other than selecting my network and entering my WEP key through System->Administration->Networking. Installed the fglrx video driver no problem and Compiz/XGL went on without a hitch (other then a strange low click when I use my scroll bar). Had to reconfig the xserver to get widescreen resoultions, but all in all it has been an easy, and certainly doable process.
