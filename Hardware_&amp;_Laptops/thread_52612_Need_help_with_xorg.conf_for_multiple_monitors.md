### Post by autocrosser on 2005-07-28
I would first try to turn Xinerama "on" (you can use "true" also)-- ATI cards are kind'a "fun" with Xorg-- The other option is to use FBMerged--I would like to see your xorg.conf to find out what it has set-up.. I run a dual set-up with a PRO 9600 & Rage 128--I ended up with Xinerama (slower, but more accurate)--I'll post mine so you can get the feel of it---
