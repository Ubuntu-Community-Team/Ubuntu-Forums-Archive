### Post by xmastree on 2006-02-09
A couple of questions:

Is the machine hooked up to the net? If not, that's why you get the error. It's trying to sync the time but can't connect. Ignore it.

Second, what kind of mouse? Serial, PS2 or USB. If it's serial (not many are these days) it may not be detected and you have to manually edit a file. I'll not go into details now, just in case it isn't... Meanwhile, I'll look for the thread where I helped someone with that problem previously...
