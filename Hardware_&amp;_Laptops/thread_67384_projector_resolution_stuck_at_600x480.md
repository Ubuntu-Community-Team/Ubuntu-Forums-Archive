### Post by mlomker on 2005-09-22
That's a difficult question because it is going to vary depending upon the video card, the driver that you are using, and the capabilities of the specific laptop.  

Generally laptop video outputs match the on-screen resolution.  If yours isn't doing that then it may be challenging to figure it out.  I always recommend that people search good with the name of their machine and the word **linux**.  Odds are someone has written a how-to web page with a lot of useful information on it.
