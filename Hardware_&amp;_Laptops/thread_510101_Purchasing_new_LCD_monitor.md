### Post by MrHippocampus on 2007-07-26
Any monitor nowadays should be fine, most problems with monitors occur when the montiors don't give out correct EDID information, which shouldnt happen with a new monitor, especially one which has DVI.

On the point of configuration tools your just going to have to settle for anything that nvidia-settings can do, which should be enough.
