### Post by mpettitt on 2005-10-17
Depends on the type of ink. If it is a normal water-based felt tip, a very slightly damp paper towel will do the trick (turn off monitor first, obviously!). If it is a CD marking pen, you may not be so lucky...  Try some dilute window cleaning solution or white vinegar.
