### Post by klugja on 2007-03-16
I have swapped DVD drives with my windows machine, because Linux constantly reported that the tray was open when I used the Liteon SHM-165H6S.

I replaced the drive with an LG GSA-H22N.

The Liteon machine could not do packet writing to a DVD-RAM in Linux.  It could write an entire image at once using growisofs and the -Z option.

With the LG device, packet wring now works.

And of course the LITEON machine works fine under windows XP.

I don't see DVD-RAM devices listed in your compatibility list.
