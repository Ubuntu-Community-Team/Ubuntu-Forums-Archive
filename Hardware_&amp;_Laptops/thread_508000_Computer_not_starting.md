### Post by geraldm on 2007-07-24
Some very old computers have leds on the motherboard that show the post code.
Also, when the boot fails, some give signals through the speaker -- short, long,
which allows a look into its manual for the point of failure.

Gerald

### Post by fredj on 2007-07-24
It could be either that the power supply has failed or the motherboard has failed, difficult to say which one it 
is without proper testing. Try local component shops or look online for a cheap power supply tester.
If you know the power supply is working then it is probably the motherboard.
