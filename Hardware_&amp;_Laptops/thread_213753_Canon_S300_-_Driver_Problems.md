### Post by Rodneyck on 2006-12-18
None of the drivers (using Edgy) work well with the Canon S300 printer, including the S800 drivers as reported above.  The Ubuntu guides are for dapper and it appears some things have changed in Edgy for this printer.

I downloaded the TurboPrint drivers and they are excellent, works perfectly, just like in Windows XP including nozzle cleaning and ink monitoring. The problem is that you have to pay to remove the logo at the bottom of the page. What a shame.  For those with the same printer and not wanting to pay to use it in Linux, one word... amule.

[http://www.turboprint.info/](http://www.turboprint.info/)
