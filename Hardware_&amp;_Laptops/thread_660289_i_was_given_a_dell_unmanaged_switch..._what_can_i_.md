### Post by KCPokes on 2008-01-06
To clarify, as the use of hub and switch is being intermixed, a hub and switch are definitely different.  A hub splits the incoming connection, say 100MB connection, into the number connections into the hub; a 4 port hub splits the connection 4 ways, sharing the same broadcast segment as well.  A switch will create separate broadcast segments, thus far less collisions.  There are numerous other differences, but the one other important difference to note is that you cannot stack hubs, meaning you cannot connect a hub to a hub, whereas you can with a switch.
