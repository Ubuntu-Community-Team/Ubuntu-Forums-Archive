### Post by PhrankDaChickenGeek on 2006-12-25
On ubuntu, install gparted
Plug in your hard drive, open gparted, and select your external hard drive from the list.
You can then create a new partition and format it as FAT32

[http://gparted.sourceforge.net/index.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/index.php)
