### Post by jazzgossen on 2007-05-17
I'm planning on building a VIA-based small and silent box, primarily for use as a PVR, with MythTV. But I'm wondering which motherboard is best suited for this?

The EX-10000EG is 1.0 MHz, but has "advanced system media processor with enhanced audio and video technologies". The EN-12000EG doesn't have that, but has a higher CPU frequency. 

The EX has better ouput connections (component video and SPDIF audio). But disregarding that, which board would give me the best playback and recording quality for HDTV video?

---

### Post by Ken_Lewis81 on 2007-05-17
Of those two, go with the EX.  It has dedicated hardware to accelerate video decoding and better outputs.  But it might not be up to HDTV quality, so considering something like a ULV Core2 Duo with a board that has built-in HDMI should have the performance you want.
