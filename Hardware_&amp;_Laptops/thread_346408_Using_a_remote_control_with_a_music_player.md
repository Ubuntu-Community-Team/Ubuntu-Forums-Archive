### Post by feed_sparky on 2007-01-25
I was trying to figure out how to make the remote that came with my laptop (HP dv5215us) work like it did in windows for the last few days. Well today I was sitting across the room wishing I could change the song and BAM! It hit me to try to use the keyboard shortcuts option in SYSTEM > PREFERENCES > KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS . I went to the SOUND part and selected the desired shortcut option and just pressed the corresponding button on the remote and IT JUST WORKED!!!!! Sorry if I seem too excited with this little deiscovery but I just started Linux about 2 weeks ago and I am still figuraing out the little stuff. I'm sure this is already known by alot of people, but if there are other people out there that don't know this maybe it will help them out. :guitar:
