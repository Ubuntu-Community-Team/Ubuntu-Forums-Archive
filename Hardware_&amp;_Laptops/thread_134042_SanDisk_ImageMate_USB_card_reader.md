### Post by unperson on 2006-02-21
I have the SanDisk ImageMate 12-in-1 USB flash card reader.  When I first connected it up to my Ubuntu system it worked like a charm.  I just plugged it in, plugged my card in, and the flashcard icon popped up on my desktop.  I opened it up and moved stuff two and from.  Recently, I've had an issue where I can no longer write to either of my flash cards (see [here](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=133785) for details), but I'm not sure if the problem has anything to do with the card reader itself.  I haven't had an opportunity yet to clock the transfer rates yet, so I'm not sure exactly how fast it is.
