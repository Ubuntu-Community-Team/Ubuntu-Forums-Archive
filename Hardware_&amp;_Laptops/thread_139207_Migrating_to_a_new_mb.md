### Post by nickle on 2006-03-03
If you are lucky i.e. all the drivers are available for you mobo and everything on board, you should simply have to install all the hardware and boot up. This worked for me recently moving from one socket A mobo to another.
I see no reason why you should loose data. In any case it is always best to do a backup beofre you start.
