### Post by Se[BBB]e on 2007-05-12
Go into volume control. Go to edit>preferences and enable all controls. Turn "HPhone" to highest and mute "CD". This solved the issue w/ background noise and low level on my laptop.

Also, if you want sound out of all ports (I only got out of one before), go the options tab and select 6ch as channel mode.

### Post by sanctusconsilium on 2008-07-18
This may help, I was having the same issue. Everything I would play, cds, mp3, dvds, video games, everything was very soft. 

I went into the volume controls, then went to edit>preferences, like the above post said, in my case I clicked on "PCM" to make it visible.

I then cranked up "PCM" to max and all the sudden I had good volume level. I don't know why it was set so low, or even why it would get set so low all of the sudden.

Hope that helps. Heh, I don't even know what "PCM" stands for, but it must have to do with the volume coming out of the back of my comp.
