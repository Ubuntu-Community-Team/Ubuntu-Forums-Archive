### Post by xtknight on 2007-10-14
Check out Ubuntu Gutsy.  My Plus Series 3 works here with a little coercion.  It is autodetected as generic text but you have to give it the Kodak EasyShare Gutenprint 5.0.1 driver (included with Gutsy CUPS).

Here's my entry: [http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Kodak-EasyShare_Printer_Dock_Plus_Series_3](http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Kodak-EasyShare_Printer_Dock_Plus_Series_3)
