### Post by ryanVickers on 2008-01-03
I would bet that GRUB is on the external hard drive.  I would get a copy of "Super Grub Tool" (free - bootable disk/floppy) and try and install it/move it to the internal one, then, you will boot windows with just the laptop, but if you have the external HD attached, you can pick the linux option and boot it as well :D
