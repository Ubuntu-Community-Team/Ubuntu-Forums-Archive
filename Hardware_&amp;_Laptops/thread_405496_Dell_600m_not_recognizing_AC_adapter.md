Just want to see if anyone has figured out a way around this problem. I received a dell Inspiron 600m that didn't have a working battery but worked fine on the ac adapter. Figuring I wanted a laptop not another desktop, I installed a new hard drive (the orginal died, hence I got the laptop) and a fresh install of edgy. I also bought a new battery (an actual dell replacement battery not a generic one). When I went to charge up the new battery and boot it up for the first time I got a strange warning, saying that the computer couldn't recognize  the AC adapter, which meant I couldn't charge this new battery I just shelled out money for, plus a bunch of other problems.

Either way I updated the Bios (9 revisions behind) and tried a different ac adapter, neither of these worked. I'm coming to assume that it is the systems board but just want to put this problem out there in case anyone has any ideas, that don't involve buying a $400 systems board.
