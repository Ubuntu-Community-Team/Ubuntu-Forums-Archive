Hello!

I wanted to switch off the touchpad of my laptop, but after two days of work I gave up. :(
**Without synaptics** I was not able to switch it off.
**With synaptics** it was OK until I started to use the keyboard, then synaptics hanged due to a synchronisation problem and the touchpad kept working again.

I reported the bug concerning the synchronisation problem (Bug #116251):
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-input-synaptics/+bug/116251](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-input-synaptics/+bug/116251)

There you can find all the versioning information and error messages too.

Thank you!
