### Post by Redeyes_Gambit on 2006-12-08
Acer 2423WXCI.

This little laptop rocks my open source world! In Dapper everything worked great OOTB (Out Of The Box) with the exception of my Wlan. All I needed to get the Wlan working were some kernel headers and WEP instead of WPA encryption. Wired ethernet works perfectly.

Touchpad has a slightly different "feel" then in windows but after a while I have come to like the Ubuntu feel better.

The intel i915 graphics worked beautifully in Dapper with hardware acceleration OOTB and is now even faster (also OOTB) in Edgy! Can't complain there. I currently have Beryl installed and get to enjoy all the sweet eyecandy with minimal lag or loss in performance (Though it should be noted that I upgraded the RAM to 1.2 GB.)

In Dapper hibernate worked fine with a few bugs here and there, but in Edgy hibernate is somewhat dangerous. Last time I accidentally hibernated I lost my swap partition and had to modify my fstab, among other things, to get it back. This is a bug that needs to be fixed post haste! 

The lid switch works fine and is currently set to suspend the computer. The volume and brightness buttons work perfectly and I can assign the custom buttons to do just about whatever I want.

Sound works perfectly. I haven't yet tried the modem or the PCMCIA card slot.

Overall I would highly recommend this laptop for it's open-source and low config friendliness.

Edit: Oh, almost forgot, there are two buttons on the front that don't work; the Wlan and Bluetooth enable/disable buttons. Of course the bluetooth button doesn't actually have any PC guts behind it anyway :P .
