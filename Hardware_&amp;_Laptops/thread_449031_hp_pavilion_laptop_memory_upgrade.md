### Post by DarkN00b on 2007-05-19
You need DDR2 533 MHz ram in any size that your laptop can utilize. That machine should be able to handle a couple of 1 gig sticks easily. It mainly depends on whether your BIOS and motherboard supports the size sticks you want to buy. Brand doesn't matter as long as you go with good quality ram. Unfortunately the cheaper stuff is just that - cheap. There's no telling how long it will last.

That said, I put a cheap stick in my HP ze2000 and it has been working just fine.
