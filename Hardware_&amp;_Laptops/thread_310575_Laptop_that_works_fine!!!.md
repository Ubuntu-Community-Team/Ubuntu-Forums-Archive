### Post by serentaius on 2006-12-01
Hey I noticed a few threads where people had asked about laptops. I'm using a Dell Inspiron 6000 in a Dual boot with XP pro and Ubuntu and it works great. Ubuntu has native support for the sound,video, and networking drivers! the only thing i've found that doesn't work is that the "wi-fi" card light on the top of the laptop doesn't come on when it's on, but the network status in the top right hand of the screen shows when it's on and not so it's not a big deal. So, in short if you're looking for a laptop that works with Linux Ubuntu right out of the box I would go with the inspiron 6000.
