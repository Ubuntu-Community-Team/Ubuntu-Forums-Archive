### Post by zubrug on 2006-04-01
I got my A620 working in both Dapper and Breezy. It works in Gthumb etc. Reboot with your camera plugged in via usb, turn it on in the preview images mode and not picture taking.(the slide switch on rear) no reboot required after that, just mounts. I have to say that this has worked out of the box in Dapper, gutenprint also renders prints than cups. I have found dapper to be very stable, faster and more hardware friendly.
