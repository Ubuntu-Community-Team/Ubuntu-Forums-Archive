### Post by mibikerguy on 2006-10-31
I just upgraded my Dapper to a 250 Gig Western Digital SATA HDD SE16; 7200 RPM; 16 M cache and was not able to make it be recognised by my BIOS in a VIA SP1300 mini-itx MB. Here is the solution which worked for me.

Though not specified in the WD instructions, I installed a jumper on pins 5-6 on the back of the drive. Though it worked for me, on this specific drive, no guarentees granted or implied for anyone else, so use with caution on your drive.
