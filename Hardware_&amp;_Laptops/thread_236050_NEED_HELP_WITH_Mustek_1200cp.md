### Post by zxee on 2006-08-14
You may be in luck, since the sane supported devices search engine lists your scanner here: [http://www.sane-project.org/cgi-bin/driver.pl?manu=mus&model=1200&bus=par&v=&p=](http://www.sane-project.org/cgi-bin/driver.pl?manu=mus&model=1200&bus=par&v=&p=)

But you will probably have to download the backend for your scanner and set it up. Parallel port scanners are not well represented in off the shelf releases., but they can be made to work. 

Go to their docs page [http://www.sane-project.org/docs.html](http://www.sane-project.org/docs.html) and be prepared to do some reading. Hope that helps.
