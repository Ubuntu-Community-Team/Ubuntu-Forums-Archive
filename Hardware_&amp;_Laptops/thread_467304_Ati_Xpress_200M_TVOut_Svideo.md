### Post by holodad on 2007-06-08
Hello Everyone.
I just resolved the problem...
Here is how it works:
Just add the line Option"EnableMonitor" "tv" in section "device". Then when you need to switch Laptop screen > TV: just plug the svideo and "ctrl+alt+back space" to restart xorg
When you need to switch on the Laptop screen:  Just unplug the svideo and "ctrl+alt+back space".
