### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-01-03
> but which one of the 2gb modules do you think is the best?

They're all DDR2-667, rated for CAS5 timings and 1.8V. Theoretically, they should all work identically. I wouldn't bother paying extra for a heatspreader. I've never seen a test that shows they make a significant difference unless it's one of those fancy heatsinks or water cooling blocks and the RAM is being pumped with extra Voltage and/or OC'd.

Go with the cheapest one.
