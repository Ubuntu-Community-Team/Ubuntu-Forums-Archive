### Post by aamod on 2007-12-07
I own a Lenovo N100 laptop.
Many users of Ubuntu are surprised to find that sound does not work from external speakers, but they do run from their connected headphones.

All you must have is your internal modem switched ON in BIOS setup. If disabled, sound wont work. I dont know why this happens?

But, its sure that external speakers of Lenovo work only when you switch on the internal modem from BIOS setup.
