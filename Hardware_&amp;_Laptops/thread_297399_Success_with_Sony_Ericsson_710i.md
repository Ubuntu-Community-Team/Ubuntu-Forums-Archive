### Post by essexman on 2006-11-11
I am using Ubuntu Dapper and have been Windows free for over a year.   My girlfrind been wanting the [Sony Ericsson W710i]("http://www.sonyericsson.com/spg.jsp?cc=gb&lc=en&ver=4000&template=pp1_loader&php=php1_10463&zone=pp&lm=pp1&pid=10463") and waited 3 months before they rolled out in the UK. I warned her that It would be unlikely to work on Linux, but the radio and general style were of more interest.  last night on our first free evening for ages (steady!!), we gave the phone a try on my PC.

No luck with the software on wine.  But when we plugged in the usb (supplied with the phone), instant success, no tweeking necessary.  Dapper immediatly asked if I wanted to import photos, which worked perfectly.  the Phone Card Icon appeared on the Desktop and Media stick came up on Computer.  Nautilus showed all the folders which Sony Ericsson have set up in a very simple fashion and loading MP3 files was a simple drag'n'Drop process.

Sony Ericsson W710i is the first Mobil Phone to wow me.  it is comfortable, stylish, fast, simple to use, good value, and works with Dapper out of the box.  I have always been a nokia man but have recently considered Motorola because of good reports of Motos working with Linux.  But I think my next phone will be a Sony Ericsson W710i.

We haven't tried the Contacts yet; I may report back later.

Jenny is thrilled and has gone off to the Cologne George Michael concert with a phone she loves, [George Michael]("http://www.georgemichael.com/") Mp3 ringtones for all her friends and a [Tango Milonga (Se Dice De Mi)]("http://www.planet-tango.com/lyrics/se_dice_de_mi.htm") Ringtone for her main Squeeze.
