### Post by Monstroxus on 2006-12-19
I couldn' get the sound working on my Lenovo 3000 C200 laptop. I found an easy (hardware) solution. Considering that the sound from the internal speakers of a laptop is mostly very poor, this "solution" is well worth considering. I simply bought a nice pair of sleek and portable USB powered speakers, AND a special USB plug thingy which has a tiny internal soundcard inside, plug it in one of the USB port, (put the male plug of your USB powered speaker in first)... and you will have sound, although not through the laptop's speakers but through the external speakers. It will also solve any mic in problems, because it will offer you that option too.

Or... you can just find an USB powered AND USB audio build in speakers, so you will not need the USB plug thingy. (which will costs around 15 USD)

I hope this information will be of any help.
