### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-04-18
an old time mechanics trick.  Take a wooden stick and place one end on the bone behind your ear.  Take the other end and prod around the computer until you reach the loudest point..  that is the general location of your problem. now days they use a stethoscope with a prod to do the same thing.
(but, from your description, it sounds like a fan somewhere has a bearing going south.  Canned air .. blow the dust bunnies out of the fan on your processor, video card, power supply, and any others that are in a confined position.)
