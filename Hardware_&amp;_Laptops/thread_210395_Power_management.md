### Post by jko21 on 2006-09-08
Hi,

I am not sure what you can do to make your battery hold longer, but as for the inaccurate time remaing, you can make it only appear the percentage of battery life. Maybe this way it will stop the warning messages. In Ubuntu (with gnome) it's easy to do that. It's in the :
Configuration Editor->apps->gnome-power-manager->use_time_policy

I haven't seen a lot of Xubuntu, but maybe it's something relative.:)
