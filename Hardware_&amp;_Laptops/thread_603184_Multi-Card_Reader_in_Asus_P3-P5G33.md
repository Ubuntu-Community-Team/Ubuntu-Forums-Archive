### Post by Difranco1911 on 2007-11-13
My Asus barebones system has actually been great.  Myself and/or many of my friends have used those or the Shuttle systems with great results over the years.  Small has been sought after because most met each other at LAN parties.

This particular model has been absolutely stable, I ordered my parts from newegg.  Assembled and installed Gutsy right away without any issues.  Bios was already up to date when I unboxed it saving me a step.

I upgraded from the onboard video to a PCI Express Geforce 8400GS, a half hieght (low profile) card with 256MB ram from PNY.  This allowed for DVI output as well as a significant increase in video performance. 

This system is also much more quiet than my last system which makes me happy as well.
