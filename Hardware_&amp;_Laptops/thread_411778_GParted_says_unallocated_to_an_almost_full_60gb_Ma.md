### Post by kidders on 2007-04-18
Hi there,

If a hard disk makes clunking noises and won't spin properly it's a paperweight really. :-( To be honest, I wouldn't have put it in my PC to begin with, personally ... the data on it must be pretty important!

If I were you, my first step would be to try my best to take a clean image of it, and then plug out the disk. The filesystems on it may still be unrecoverable, but at least you'd get a chance to play around, without risking further damage to them ... or your PC.

> What would happen if I'd open the casing and see if there is something stuck?The hard disk would be completely unusable ... unless of course you know exactly what you're doing, and have done it before.
