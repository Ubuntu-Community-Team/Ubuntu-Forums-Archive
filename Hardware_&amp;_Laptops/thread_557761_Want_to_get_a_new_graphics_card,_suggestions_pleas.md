### Post by bobpur on 2007-09-23
This thread gets answered a lot in this forum. Anything from Nvidia is good. For $200.00 you could probably get one of the weaker DirectX 10 cards in the 80 series if your dualbooting Vista. If you're not worried about DirectX 10 and Vista you can probably get something in the higher 70 series cards. As long as it's got an Nvidia chip it doesn't really matter. 
There are, also, ATI cards out there but they are not supported in linux as well as Nvidia.
