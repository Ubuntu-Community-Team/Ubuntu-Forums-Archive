### Post by anil_robo on 2005-12-22
XGA screens: Okay (used for internet, email, word processing etc)
XGA+ and SXGA screens: Good (used for games, powerpoints etc)
UXGA screens: Best (used for advanced gaming, graphic design etc)

A "W" (e.g. WXGA) denotes widescreen.
