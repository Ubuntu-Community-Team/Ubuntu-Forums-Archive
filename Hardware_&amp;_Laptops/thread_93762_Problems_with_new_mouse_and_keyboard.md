### Post by Sp@z on 2005-12-27
I just got the Logitech G15 gaming keyboard and had the same issue. I had to go into my bios and enable Keyboard USB support and USB mouse support. The mouse was a lil trickier, I had to plug my old one back in and reinstall the drivers AFTER the USB support was enabled then it worked like a charm. Well the mouse and keyboard work but only as basic devices. I have the copperhead as well but no "on the fly" sens switching or anything. The LCD and G keys don't work on the keyboard but all else does. I was 99% Ubuntu till I got these items, now I am 99% windows again, support for these two items seems pretty weak, prolly because linux isn't a "Gamers" choice OS.
