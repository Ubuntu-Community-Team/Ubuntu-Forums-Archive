### Post by David Haas on 2005-07-09
Hi--You must mean Nikon Coolpix ? Here is the way I download photos from my Nikon Coolpix 3700. First, I had selected the PTP transfer protocol in the USB section of the camera. Then I plug in the camera via the USB connection and turn on the camera. Then I open gThumb Image Viewer and select File>Import photos. gThumb then lists my camera model and shows thumbnails of all the photos in the camera's memory. If I select Import, all the photos in the camera are immediately downloaded into my Home directory. Of course, the first time one does this, the camera model must be selected in the program. gThumb asks for this and presents a list of Camera makes and models. i hope this helps.
