### Post by calraith on 2007-05-08
I would imagine any would be usable.  For what it's worth NVidia graphics cards seem to behave better than ATI.  However, for reasons that would probably bore you if I elaborated, it's probably safer to avoid getting an nForce board.  Suffice to say that manufacturers sometimes recycle buggy BIOS code, not realizing it since Windows doesn't seem to care about the bugs, and it's best just to avoid getting a board with a buggy BIOS if possible.
