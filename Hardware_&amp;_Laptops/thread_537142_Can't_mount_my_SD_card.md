### Post by frith on 2007-09-13
Well, your case might be different to mine, but on my toshiba m200 there is zero support for the internal SD card reader. It just doesn't work at all, there are no drivers and likely never will be.

Your laptop is different, so there is hope, but google around a bit and I'm sure you will find out very quickly if it will never work.

As for the SD card not being read by anything else, most cameras have an option to reformat the card. Can you do this? Also, you should be able to reformat the card in windows. If neither of these things work, there may be a problem 'hardware' wise - I have a few memory cards and usb sticks that only work in particular computers and not others...

Hope this helps,

Frith.
