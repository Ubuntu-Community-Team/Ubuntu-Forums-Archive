Hi jocejas, first, I'd prefer if you asked this in the forum so that everyone can see the solution, but I will readily answer you.

First, enable the additional repositories by cruising over to System -> Administration -> Software Sources.

Check everything in the first tab, then click Close. It will then reload your apt configuration.

Second  you install it from synaptic by going to System -> Administration -> Synaptic, enter your password and search for gnomad2. Double click it, and click Apply.

Once everything has been installed it can be found on the menu through Applications -> Multimedia - Gnomad2. Plug in your Zen Micro and launch the application. It will detect your mp3 player and show you your music. Then you can use the arrows to either put music from the player on the pc or to put music from pc on the player, as you wish.

Goodluck! And if you need any more help, please ask.
