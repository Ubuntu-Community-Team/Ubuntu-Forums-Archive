### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-03-29

Hard drives, cases and monitors are pretty much independent of OS.

I don't know of any desktop processors that won't work with Ubuntu.

For laser printers - Samsung budget printers tend to be well supported, and HP for higher end.

The sound, graphics and any card readers tend to the main potential problems.
