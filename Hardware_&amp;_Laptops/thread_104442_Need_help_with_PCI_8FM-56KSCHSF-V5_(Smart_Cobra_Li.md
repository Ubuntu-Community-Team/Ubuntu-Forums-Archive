### Post by localzuk on 2005-12-16
According to the linuxant website, you can download the driver for HSF modems here: [http://www.linuxant.com/drivers/hsf/downloads-installer.php](http://www.linuxant.com/drivers/hsf/downloads-installer.php).

The advice I would give, though, is that you should buy an external Serial modem and use that. You will, usually, get better connection speeds and reliability through them.
