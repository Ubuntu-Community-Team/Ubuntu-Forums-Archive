I use eBay.   

Bought 8, 7 good deals and 1 duffer with a no quibble refund when failed to perform as specified.  Several friends have bought on eBay too, all are happy with the experience.

Make sure you get the seven character type/model code before buying and check the spec against what IBM says.

The model codes are more important than what the badge says - a 2373-??? badged T40 will cost you less than a 2373-??? badged T41 or T42 but might have an identical or even superior spec. 

It's often cheaper to buy a WiFi ready 'Pad - pre-fitted antenna - and a miniPCI WiFi card with the IBM firmware separately and it only takes about 30mins to fit to a T4x or X3x machine following the instructions in the excellent downloadable Hardware Maintenance Manuals.   

Remember to calculate the total cost including tax and shipping when comparing deals.

Good Luck!
