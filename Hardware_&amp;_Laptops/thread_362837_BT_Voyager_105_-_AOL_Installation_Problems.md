### Post by Daveth on 2007-02-16
searching the web suggests that the 105 is a usb modem, right? Tricky to set up in linux, though I recently was chided for bypassing the challenge of getting them to work, I still maintain an ethernet router is the best way forward for you. You just jack that into the ethernet port round the back of your pc, do a bit of browser-based config work and off you go- not a usb driver in sight. I use the BT 205 ethernet router which works just fine- has the advantage of giving you a nice firewall to hide behind.
[http://corz.org/comms/hardware/router/bt.voyager.205_router.how-to.php](http://corz.org/comms/hardware/router/bt.voyager.205_router.how-to.php)

has lots of ethernet how-to stuff and is well written. I'm no help with your usb problem, but this is 1 way forward.
