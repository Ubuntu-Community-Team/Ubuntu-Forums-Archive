### Post by rjpilla on 2007-11-03
SOLVED!  After weeks of working on this it appears it really isn't a driver problem. I noticed upon entering Ubuntu from Grub there was a PCI/PNP problem which quickly flashed up and dissappeared.  So I installed the driver with Envy and then entered CMOS and reset motherboard to default values (KN8 Asus motherboard and MSI GeForce 5700 LE video card). Rebooted and voila 3d back.  So if your having problems getting the nvidia card accepting the driver it may be worth a try to set your motherboard to default values and work from there.  Good luck.
