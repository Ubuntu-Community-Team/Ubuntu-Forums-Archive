### Post by Ciego on 2006-08-15
I had a similar problem when installing.  I was installing on a desktop so this may not apply to you, but I figured out my problem was that I had two different brands (and sizes) of memory chips.  Apparently Ubuntu did not like one of them because after removing it, the install finished successfully.  I tried to put the chip back in but it then caused the system to crash after a few minutes.  I took out the chip again and it all runs fine now (although a bit slower).  

I do not use laptops so I do not know if this will apply for you, but I hope it may help.  Good luck.
