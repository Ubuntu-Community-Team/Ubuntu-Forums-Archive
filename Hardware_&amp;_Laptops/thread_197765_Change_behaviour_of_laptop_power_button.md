### Post by kreso on 2006-06-16
I'd like to change the behaviour of my power button from displaying the shudown menu to hibernate. How can I do that?

---

### Post by reztho on 2006-06-16
You need to use the gconf-editor. Run it with ALT+F2 or search for it in the gnome menus (apps>system tools? I don't know exactly since I have my system in Spanish). And go to: apps > gnome-power-manager > action-button-power. Change it from "interactive" to "hibernate".
