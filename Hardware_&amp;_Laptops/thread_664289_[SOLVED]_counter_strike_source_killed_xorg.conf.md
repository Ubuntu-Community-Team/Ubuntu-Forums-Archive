### Post by Z_o-s-o on 2008-01-11
Have you tried resetting Xorg using:

sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg  ?

Just make sure you dont have too many custom settings in there that you dont need to keep because this wipes the current xorg.conf and you give it new settings to use.

I've got CSS and HL2 episode 1 and 2 , and also steam running in wine and they all work great.
