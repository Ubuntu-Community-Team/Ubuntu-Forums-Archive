### Post by deadgobby on 2006-06-05
I seen a handful of post on people getting no internet connection when they boot up Dapper. Here is a simple solution to try out. 
1. Power down your Dapper PC.
2. Turn off/unplug your cable modem and if you have a router. Turn it off too. Wait for at least on minute and turn back on the modem and/or router. 
3. Turn back on the Cable modem and/or Router. Let the Cable modem do it self test and same if you have a Router with a cable modem.
4. Keep the modem on and boot up the Dapper PC.
 Dapper should connect after this simple solution.
  When I boot up my Dapper PC and do not have the cable modem on before I boot up. I will not get an internet connection. If I have my cable modem on while Dapper is booting. Dapper finds the signal and surfing away like a hack. 
 Just remember to have the cable modem on before booting up Dapper.
