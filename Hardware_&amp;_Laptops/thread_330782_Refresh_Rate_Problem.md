### Post by meng on 2007-01-03
See the section "monitor"? That sets a limit on your vertical refresh rate of 64 Hz. No wonder you don't get the chance to set it above 60. You could edit the xorg.conf (with superuser privileges) and then restart X, or for a safer way you could do the following:
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
