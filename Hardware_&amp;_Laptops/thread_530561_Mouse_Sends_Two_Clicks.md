### Post by Dave54 on 2007-08-21
Does it send two clicks sometimes, or EVERY time?
Did you do anything software related before it began?
Is it just the left mouse button, or every button?

I had a GE 5-button mouse, and one day it began to send two clicks every now and then. As time went on, it happened more and more. Eventually I opened it up and re-soldered the connections to the left mouse button. The fix worked, but a few months later it came back. I kept fixing it for about six months 'till I finally got a new mouse.

If you have a different mouse, plug it in and see if it sends two clicks. If you have a different computer, plug the original mouse into the other computer and see if it sends two clicks.

Good luck.
