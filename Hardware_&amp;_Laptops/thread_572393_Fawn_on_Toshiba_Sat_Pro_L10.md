### Post by loza on 2007-10-10
Hi everyone...

I'm really happy with the amount of 'stuff' I've got working on my laptop. I used to use Suse 7.0 many many years ago and it was a bit tricky but I got most things working on that. But Fawn is just so easy to get 'stuff' working.... However there are just a few things I'm finding running Fawn on my Toshiba Sat Pro L10...

Things that work... in  a fashion
--------------------------------------

1. Touchpad

It seems that when trying to move focus to another window or object in the GUI sometimes the pointer doesn't click objects e.g. moving and clicking the scroll bar on a window, or dragging a document from on window to another, it seems the pointer doesn't pick it up first time. Yet if I connect a usb mouse, it behaves perfectly. Something to do with the hardware on the touchpad perhaps?

2. (Some) External disk drives and ntfs-3g

I have several external disk drives. I have a lot of data! Unfortunately I have to use ntfs as I share these drives with MS Windows PCs. I use the ntfs-3g driver and my two Parkard Bell Save and Store 3500 drives work perfectly. However I have a 2 and a half inch 520GB FREECOM Mobile Drive drive which is powered through the usb port. It was also a ntfs format drive but for some reason I seem to have a lot of trouble getting the drive to mount. Dialogs would appear and state the drive was dirty and so I could force the mount under 'sudo'. Eventually I got fed up of mounting it this way especially after trying to mount under MSWindows it reported as corrupt, even though it was hot pluggable. I managed to get my data off under Fawn and have now formatted it to FAT32. This mounts no problem under both systems now.

3. TFT screen

I seem to be stuck in 1024 x 768 screen resolution mode. Whilst this is not a show stopper it does make life a little crowded at times. I know I could use multiple desktops but I have enough trouble working on one! :) I would be sure to forget I have application open on a hidden desktop somewhere and would loose my work. I'm like that! Are all other laptop users stuck with 1024 x 768 mode I wonder?


'The' Thing that doesn't work
-----------------------------------

1. Siemens 108 usb wireless stick

I almost got this working once. But I use a wire now. I loaded the drivers from XP and it would hang only when I tried to access the network. I find this amazing how another OS can load drivers from another OS and attempt to work.:lolflag:

2. Hot Laptop!

This is a little worrying. I have noticed that the laptop get very hot. One day the whole thing over heated and had a nap for 15 mins until it had cooled down enough to let me back on! This only seems to happen when running Fawn. I think under MSWindows I have some utility that drives the fans to keep the laptop cool.


Things that work really well!
----------------------------------

1. Sony Erricson 880 Mobile Phone

This connects via usb and the memory stick appears ont he desktop with no problems.

2. Epson 1290 Colour Stylus

This works absolutley fine.

3. Fuji fine pix 304 camera

Appears on the desktop and can drag the pictures off the card with no problems

4. Corsair Flash Voyager stick

Works perfectly.

All in all I am very happy with what Fawn has got working on my laptop. I know its not a great mobile monster laptop (although I do have to admit its got a 160GB HD and 1GB of RAM), but it has enough power for me to type stuff, which is all I need really.

See how I ended on a happy note there....? :)

If anyone does have any pointers to my little problemettes I'd be very grateful, but if not, i'll live! :)

loza
