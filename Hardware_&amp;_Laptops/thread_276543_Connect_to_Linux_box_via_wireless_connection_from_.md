### Post by jhofmann on 2006-10-13
I can't begin to give you a step by step guide, but I can provide some experience.  Of course you can connect to the internet through your linux box from your laptop.  You must first establish a connection from the laptop to the linux box.  Can you ping your linux box from your laptop?  Can you login to your linux box?  If not, that is the first thing to do.
