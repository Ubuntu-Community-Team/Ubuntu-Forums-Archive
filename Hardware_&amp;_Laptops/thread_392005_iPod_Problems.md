### Post by fender1212 on 2007-03-24
alright, i finally fixed this, here's how i did it.  I booted into windows, updated itunes, still couldn't read it.  booted back into ubuntu, ran banshee, which told me that my ipod has been upgraded to a new version of itunes that it couldn't recognize and it gave me the option to "rebuild ipod" or something like that, i clicked yes, and after a long wait, voila, my ipod was back to normal :)
