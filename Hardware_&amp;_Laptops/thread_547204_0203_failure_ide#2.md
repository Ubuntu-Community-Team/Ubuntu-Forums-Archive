### Post by geraldm on 2007-09-09
Look at the IDE drive.  The proper setting is printed on most IDE drives, and there are
pins and a jumper that must be set correctly to function.  Other than the switches,
the cable must be properly installed and correctly seated.  The needed information
is almost always printed on the drive.

Gerald
