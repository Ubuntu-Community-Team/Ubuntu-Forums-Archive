### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-03-12
What you need to do is to download the iso image of the Live CD or the Alternate CD and burn the image as an iso. If you just burn it as a data disk it will not work.

Most software programs for burning CD's has the option buried away in a menu somewhere for burning an iso image.

There are two VERY IMPORTANT things to remember when burning iso images:

1. Burn them SLOWLY. No faster than 4 X

2. Use high quality media. Budget media just does not "cut the mustard" for iso's.
