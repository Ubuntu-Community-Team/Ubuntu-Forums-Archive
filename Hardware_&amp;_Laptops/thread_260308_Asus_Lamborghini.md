### Post by apelete on 2006-09-18
Hello there !

I have an Asus V6Va, and it's basically the same expect for the new design (Lamborghini), the new Core Duo processor (I have an Intel Pentium M 760 inside mine), the new graphic card (new Geforce 7400 vs "old" ATI X700) and the bigger hard drive (160 GB vs 100).

Ubuntu does just run HELL GREAT on my V6V !! Almost everything was working right out of the box (wifi, most of laptop shortkeys, ALL activity leds...etc). So I think it should run just as great on the Lamborghini version of the V6 (it's actually called "VX1" instead of V6).

Nonetheless there's one thing you should be aware of...
This beast does...heat...a lot. I mean, it is not "overheating" since the laptop just seem to run fine and does not hang or freeze, nor does the fan always kick in (in fact it rarely kicks in). But it's just that the right palm rest does heat a lot since it's where the HDD is (and the top right side near the power button does also heat, but you won't notice that much since you're hand won't be there that often :D ).
In fact I think it's normal for the inside case to heat since it's made out of aluminium (or magnesium maybe ?), so it tends to conduct more heat than a plastic case...

I think it's all you need to know O:) 
Feel free to ask more questions...
