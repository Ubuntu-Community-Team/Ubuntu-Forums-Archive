You can get these for $25 but then you have to spend $10 on a USB cable, and $40 on a set of a black and color inkjet cartridge (2 cartridges). They give you a color one with the printer but it's half-filled and lame.

I can't get it to print and so I followed the advice of trying other Lexmark Z* (and, hell, any Lexmark) printer drivers and they all seem to fail. I also tried using the HP Laserjet 2 and 4 drivers, and I also tried using Generic Postscript sent over CUPS as well as sent over LPR. I even rebooted a few times, powered off the device a few times, and bounced cupsys a few times. Nothing seems to work and all I get are either:

* Queue fills with request and then request is removed with no printout or error.
* Queue fills with request and sits there. After about 10 minutes, it bails, shows no error, and an empty page comes out.
* Queue fills with request and pulls down paper. It sits there 2 minutes, ejects a blank sheet, and removes item from queue.
* Queue prints a blank page and removes item from queue.

I highly recommend you don't purchase this printer from Wal*Mart until there's a Linux printer driver out for it. If anyone has any more advice, I'm game. I really find it a bit of trouble to box all this up and see if Wal*Mart will take it back.
