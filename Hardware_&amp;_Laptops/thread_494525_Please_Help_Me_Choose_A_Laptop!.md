### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-07-07
Given they are similar, I would say the most important thing is for you to like the feel of the keyboard.

Laptop keyboards vary quite a lot in their feel (some have very spongy key-strokes), so just have a type on the keyboards and pick one.

The other thing to ensure is wifi chipsets if you're looking for Ubuntu to run - some are a lot easier than others.

That said, I'd personally go for the HP if you're going to be doing any gaming.
