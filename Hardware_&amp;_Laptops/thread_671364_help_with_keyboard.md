### Post by leonidas666 on 2008-01-18
Do you have some usb-keyboard or mouse attached to your computer? Try disconnecting those, or, if you don't have one, add one one and try typing there. 

Do you have settings in your bios which work with usb-keyboards? E.G. "enable usb generic input device" or something like that? Try chagning that setting.

Also, in my experience, the connections from the keyboard to the mainboard sometimes get loose. Then also strange effects can occur (to check that you'll have to disassemble your keyboard). Although, if windows works, then the connection should be good.
