### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-04-08
Do yourself a favour and pay a few extra dollars for the HP Laserjet 1022.

The reason? The 1022 has 8MB of RAM compared to the 1020's 2 MB.

I have read where the 1020 has problems with graphic images because of its' small amount of RAM.

This site : [http://www.linuxprinting.org/printer_list.cgi](http://www.linuxprinting.org/printer_list.cgi)

says that the 1020 works "mostly" under Linux, but the 1022 works perfectly.

The 1022 has an inbuilt print server, also.

The old addage "You get what you pay for" comes into play here.
