### Post by localzuk on 2006-04-07
Having looked at what is actually supported by X.org and your card, it seems that you can have 3d acceleration but only in 16bit.

Take a look at [http://ftp.x.org/pub/X11R6.9.0/doc/html/i810.4.html](http://ftp.x.org/pub/X11R6.9.0/doc/html/i810.4.html) and [http://ftp.x.org/pub/X11R6.9.0/doc/html/i810.html](http://ftp.x.org/pub/X11R6.9.0/doc/html/i810.html) for more information.

On further reading, it appears that Xorg 6.9.0 doesn't support 3d at all (contradicting the first page above) [http://ftp.x.org/pub/X11R6.9.0/doc/html/i8108.html#8](http://ftp.x.org/pub/X11R6.9.0/doc/html/i8108.html#8)

So, as you are using XR6.8.2 it is not possible, it seems...
