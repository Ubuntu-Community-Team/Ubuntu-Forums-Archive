### Post by raxmox on 2006-09-13
Hi,

I just bought a T60 after I had a R51 so I can compare both:

- nice form factor: small and beautiful!

- keyboard: very good IBM quality -- but feeling is a little bit different from R51 

- screen: it's not as good as the R51 FlexView display I had before (viewing angle, colors are a bit blueish)
but since IBM/Lenovo don't have a FlexView (best display on the market) of that size I preferred 14" over 15" FlexView

- hardware support by Ubuntu Dapper (6.06.1): excellent
I never got a machine up faster: SATA disk, CDROM, X, net, sound, wlan, X accelearation)

OK -- after some hours working with the T60 display I must say it is not good enough :frown:  ! The viewing angle is too small and the contrast width is also too small -- compared to the FlexView display it is a big step back!

You can not only see the difference with photographs but also when working with Sub Pixel Rendering Fonts which are otherwise very nice to use with a quality LCD display: the characters in the middle of the screen look great but on the bottom of the screen (viewing angle plus some degrees) the characters get from black to grey and on the top of the screen the "subpixels" get darker which results in a crippled font display.

](*,)  So I will now revert back to the 15" Flex View R51!

cheers!
