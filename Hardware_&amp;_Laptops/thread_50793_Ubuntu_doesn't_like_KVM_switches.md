### Post by centremco on 2005-07-21
I have used KVM at home for a while and I have dealt with them at my work.
My experience with them is that is has to be good quality.
They need to simulate the existence of the keyboard and mouse very good when not having the port selected.
Is it like that from the beginning? Or does is stop working when you switch to another port and back.
I know the real professional 19" rackmountables (professional prices) will work very well. 
I think it's the kvm.
Try connecting your keyboard video and mouse directly, while running disconnect them and connect them, then see if they will stil work. If not, it's probably the KVM.
