### Post by GOwin on 2006-07-21
I'm posting this for the sake of others who may be in the same quandary.

In dapper drake, look under generic and select ESC/P. Do not use the recommended driver but use the epsmid or epshi (check out which one is best for you)

Other Epson dot matrix printers could also working use the generic ESC/P driver.
