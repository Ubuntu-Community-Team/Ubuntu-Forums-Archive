### Post by Declan on 2005-03-03
Just to let anyone interested know....
I was having that difficulty with getting any sound from Warty (with AC97 sound card).  I had posted about not being able to get any of the alsaconfig etc. things to work, and the that it did not recognise any valid device.

I applied some of the advise about adding certain arguments to the boot line.
I tried a few things.

Reluctantly (though not very, since it's extremely good too), I downloaded SimplyMepis 3.3 with a view to changing to that, since sound works for me on Mepis.

As a last effort, I decided to try to upgrade to Hoary.

It works!
I still have to kill esd to get it to work, but I know that I can work around that.

Now, I urge caution.  Just because it worked for me doesn't mean it'll work for anyone else.  And I really never figured out what kind of problem there was, and why Hoary doesn't have the same problem.   So I'm not much wiser.

But it worked for me.  

Just to pass that on, to anyone interested.

Declan
