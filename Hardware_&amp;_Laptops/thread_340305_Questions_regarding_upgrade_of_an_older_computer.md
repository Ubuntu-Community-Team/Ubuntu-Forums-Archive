### Post by maxamillion on 2007-01-17
The interchanging of the two video cards shouldn't bother anything as long as they have the same interface (AGP 4/8x or PCI for hardware of that age).

For setting up the older one wireless ... here is a link to docs [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs)

For printers:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Printers](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Printers)
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsPrinters](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsPrinters)
[http://linuxprinting.org](http://linuxprinting.org)

And just in case you need to share a printer with a windows box:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NetworkPrintingFromWindows](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NetworkPrintingFromWindows)

Hope this helps and that I covered everything, if you have any more questions feel free to post back.
