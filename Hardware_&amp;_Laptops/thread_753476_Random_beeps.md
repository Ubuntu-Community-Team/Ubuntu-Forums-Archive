### Post by prshah on 2008-04-12
> **RyanNutt said:**
> (it's been running for months) it's started beeping somewhat randomly.  Sometimes it beeps once.  Sometimes it alternates between a higher and lower pitched beep.  


Check your internal fans; the beeping may be a warning of overheat due to dust clogged heatsinks and/or failed fans. Especially a ululating siren sound indicates an overheating problem.

A good blast of compressed air (or a vaccum cleaner SET ON BLOW) should take care of your problems.
