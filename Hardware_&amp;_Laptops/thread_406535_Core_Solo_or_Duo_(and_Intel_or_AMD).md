### Post by amohanty on 2007-04-13
Quite frankly not that many applications use both cores in a manner that will be visible. Try profiling some of your regular (non-gaming and non-server) apps on a single core and a dual core and you will be surprised to see how little the performance changes :). 

On the other hand bump up your RAM to 1.5 - 2 G (if you can spare the money to ECC registered) and you _will_ really notice the difference.
