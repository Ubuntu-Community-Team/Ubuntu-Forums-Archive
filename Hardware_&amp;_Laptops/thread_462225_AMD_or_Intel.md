### Post by Jeanpaul145 on 2007-06-02
Before even attempting to answer the queston, know that what I'm about to say is my opinion, which MAY be biased by several factors. There is no objective answer to your question, really.  
While I don't want to risk an AMD-versus-Intel-fanboy war here, I do think that there is a good reason that AMD is forced to cut their prices as heavily as they are.
That reason is (imho) that Intel makes (for the time being) a better (read: faster) chip than a comparable chip from AMD.
Amd on the other hand has cheaper chips.
So the real question is: Are you willing to pay a little extra for extra performance?
