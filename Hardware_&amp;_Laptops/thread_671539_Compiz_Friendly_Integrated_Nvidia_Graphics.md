### Post by sowelie on 2008-01-18
You definitely want to go AMD / Nvidia.  It is very easy to get compiz set up on an nvidia card.  Pretty much all nvidia chipsets are supported, even as far back as the nvidia 2 series.  ATI and intel are not as well supported.
