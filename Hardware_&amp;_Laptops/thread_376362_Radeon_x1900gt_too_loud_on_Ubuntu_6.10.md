### Post by mibs on 2007-03-04
I have a Radeon x1900gt and I installed Ubuntu 6.10 running gnome and dual booting with Windows XP.  One of the first things I noticed when I booted Ubuntu for the first time is that the fan on my video card runs very fast as if the card is under a heavy load. This can get a bit annoying as the fan gets quite loud on this card. I went through a lot of posts on the forums here and I'm fairly confident that I installed all the drivers for the card properly. Does anyone have any idea why it runs as if it's under such a heavy load? While I'm using Windows it's virtually silent. any ideas? thanks.

### Post by 67GTA on 2007-03-11
I had the same problem with my x1600 and Intel 945GZ board. I installed  cpudyn from the package manager and the fan speeds dropped significantly. Acpi is supposed to control this, but if you have certain Intel boards it is noy recognized. Not sure what motherboard you have, might give it a try.
