### Post by scottro on 2008-03-08
I ~think~ it has the infamous AR5007EG network card--I remember when I bought a laptop, going into BestBuy (they have several stores near me) and looking at the specs using the Windows Device Manager. 

However, I'm not positive, and you are no doubt discovering, it can be difficult to find out by googling.  They seem to change cards as often as we change socks.  

Sometimes, if you call a store, they'll actually go and look for you.  Despite all the horror stories about BestBuy, their salespeople, at least around here in NYC are usually quite nice and helpful.  

Now, if it is the AR5007EG card, as long as you stick with 32 bit, you probably won't have that much trouble using the patched MadWifi snapshot (which has worked much better for me than has ndiswrapper--although if you search around these forums, you'll see people saying ndiswrapper is the way to go.)  :)
