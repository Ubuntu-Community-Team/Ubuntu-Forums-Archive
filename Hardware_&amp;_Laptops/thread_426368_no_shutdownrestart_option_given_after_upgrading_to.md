### Post by obrient on 2007-04-30
I haven't upgraded to Fiesty, but I know that I had a problem in Dapper related to this. It had to do with the *Login Window Preferences*. If you open this by going to System>Administration>Login Window you want to make sure that the **Show Actions** box is checked. This should then give you the ability to shutdown or restart your computer.

Hope this helps.
