### Post by fredj on 2007-08-10
The new Intel 6850 cpu (dual 3G) is a bargain, much cheaper than the 6800 it replaces.
A good but cheap motherboard for it is the Abit IP35 (solid state capacitors instead of 
electrolytics around the cpu) and with the right memory can be overclocked. I'm running
mine at 3.3G and it's very stable. This motherboard will also be able to take the new Intel
45nm cpus so it's upgradeable. It has six sata ports, lots of usb and firewire (don't get the
IP35-E which is  a cut down version).
I'm located in the U.K. so I can't recommend suppliers.
