### Post by rsambuca on 2007-03-01
If you are looking for something quick and painless, the Hauppauge PVR 150 cards are cheap Hardware encoding cards and work very well with linux using the IVTV drivers.  Install is pretty easy.

(In Feisty, the IVTV is pre-installed in the kernel, so just plug and play!)
