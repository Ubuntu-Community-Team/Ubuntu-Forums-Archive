### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-03-08
I have a hp psc1350 and it was detected automatically, HPLIP was installed, along with CUPS.

All functions worked straight-up.

Quite often you won't see your specific printer listed, it will just list the series.

All you have to do is this:

1. Click on System>Administration>Printing

2. A window will open. Double click on "New Printer"

3. Another window will open. You should see your printer listed, if it is plugged into your PC and powered-up.

4. Click "Forward" and just continue to follow the prompts.

HP is one of the best-supported printer companies under Linux. The psc series works fine.


As I said, mine is a 1350, but the correct driver for it was the 1300 series driver.
