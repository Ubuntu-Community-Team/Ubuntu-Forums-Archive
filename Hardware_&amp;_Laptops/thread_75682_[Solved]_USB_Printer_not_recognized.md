### Post by robenroute on 2005-10-14
I had a problem with my Canon iP4000 USB printer (see [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=74289](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=74289) ): BB wouldn't recognize it as a printer connected to my laptop. Bob (rplantz) came up with a solution. However, I had to switch off the printer (and leave it off) after having it deleted (and not thrown out ;-)), then shutdown my laptop, switch on the printer and then reboot my system. A minor detail, but it was the only way to get my printer working. Following Bob's procedure just wouldn't cut the mustard.

Thanks a lot Bob, you made my life a little more comfortable.

Thanks again!
