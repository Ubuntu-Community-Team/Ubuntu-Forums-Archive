### Post by warwickmon on 2008-05-09
I have a Slimpro SP635. I have it with a 320 G disk and 2 G of memory and Core 2 Duo processor . It is a triple boot setup. W2000, XP, and Ubuntu 8.04. I didn't do the install but the Linux went amazingly smooth. I highly recommend you do your own installation. Most of the problems we had were from Unicomp (CappuccinoPC). Granted triple boot is not your average setup but that is what I needed. So far so good. And I bought the dual VESA mount kit so I could mount the SP635 right behind the monitor. Slick.

Great machine but bush league operation. They offer all these Extended Desktop features but that only works with a DVI out (not VGA). But they don't even offer a monitor with DVI ports! And all these machines have PS2 mouse and keyboard. WHich is nice because it won't tie up your USB ports. But they don't offer PS2 mouse or keyboard. They claimed they could get me the PS2 keyboard. Then they sent a USB keyboard anyway. Stuff like that. Great machine though.

Warwickmon
