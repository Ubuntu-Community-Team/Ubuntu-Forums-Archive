### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-03-30
Unfortunately, Lexmark do not produce OSS drivers and are often difficult to get working under any operating system, besides Windows.

This site [http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x2350](http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x2350) confirms what I have said and refer to it as a "paperweiight".

Sorry, but it looks like you need to select another printer.
I would suggest Hewlett Packard, as they actively support Linux.
