### Post by Belak on 2008-02-27
On the desktop, the current mounted drives should be shown. Right click on your external HD and go down to unmount volume.

Done.

Also, if you're in nautilus, I'll show the drives on the side and you can right click those as well...

Note: It will still show the HD, but if it doesn't give you an error, it's been 'safely removed'.
