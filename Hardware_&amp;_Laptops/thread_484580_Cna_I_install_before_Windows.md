### Post by Scunizi on 2007-06-26
You probably can but you'll want to create and reserve a partition for windows.  After installing windows you probably won't have an option to boot into Ubuntu.  For that you'll have to fix grub.. There's lots of threads on that.  Fixing grub will bring back a menu when you boot and give you the option of choosing WinXP or Ubuntu.  This would all be true on a PC, and may be on a Mac.  I'm not an authority on mac's.  Maybe someone else will chime in and validate what I suggest.
