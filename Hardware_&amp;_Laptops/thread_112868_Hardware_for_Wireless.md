### Post by Steve S. on 2006-02-17
It's all good!  We now have a wireless connection, a few rooms away from the router!

For future reference, here is what I used:
A Linksys 802.11b router
A Linksys 802.11g/b wireless card
[http://www.maplin.co.uk/Module.aspx?ModuleNo=43481&&source=14&doy=search](http://www.maplin.co.uk/Module.aspx?ModuleNo=43481&&source=14&doy=search)
A Cantenna
[http://www.cantenna.com/](http://www.cantenna.com/)

Put it all together, plugged it in and then configured ra0 (the wireless interface) via System-->Administration-->Networking as it had immediately picked up the new card.

Very cool.
