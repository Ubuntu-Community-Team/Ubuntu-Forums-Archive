### Post by kidders on 2007-02-07
Hi there,

Linux doesn't have any concept of a "drive" (it's an idea that is peculiar to Microsoft operating systems). All non-Microsoft OSs gain access to filesystems by "mounting" them. There is some help on doing various related things at [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy), or in Google.

I hope that helps.
