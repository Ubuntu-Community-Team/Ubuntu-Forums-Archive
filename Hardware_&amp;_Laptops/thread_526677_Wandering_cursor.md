### Post by bluedragon436 on 2007-12-22
Alright well as far as the bottons and everything being gone, can't really help you with that, but as far as the wondering cursor....I had this issue with my laptop, and did some searching for a fix.  Once you have the laptop up and running, try and apply pressure to the palm pad towards the top of the trackpad, on either side of it, and see if this will either cause the cursor to take off or stop moving all together.  If it does, then what I did to fix it, and have done on a few other Dell laptops with the same issue is to pull the top plastic sorround off the laptop, and pull the trackpad apart, and cover all the metal "brackets" with static free plastic, and put it all back together...and this has worked many times for me...some people on here have been able to fix this issue by either turning down the sensitivity or just cutting off the built in trackpad...but this was not an option and it wasn't that hard to do it this way...  But that is just my two cents worth..  Here is the link that I followed for the idea..and would apply to you I believe...
