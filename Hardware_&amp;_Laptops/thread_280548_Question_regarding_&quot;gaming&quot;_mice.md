### Post by emarkay on 2006-10-19
I have a Logitech MX-310 with 7 buttons - all of which work in Windows (I use it for browsing) extra buttons do "back" "forward" and "open new window" - and don't use #7).  There's some ways to get them to work in Linux, but preferably, look at the manufacturers website and see if they offer a native Ubuntu/Debian/Linux driver!

For now, it's a 3 button mouse with wheel, but it's a good mouse.
