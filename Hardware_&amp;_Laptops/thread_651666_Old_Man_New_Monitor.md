### Post by irv on 2007-12-27
Had a nice Christmas. The kids got the old man a new 22” Samsung LCD Wide screen Monitor. I hook it up to my laptop, loaded the driver, and set the resolution to1400X1050 and it works great. Now here is where the problem comes in. I had to set the resolution back to 1024X768 to take my laptop on the road. This worked OK until I got home again. I set the resolution back to 1400X1050 and I got a messed up screen on the laptop and no signal to the Samsung Monitor. I finely had to do a 
> sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg 
to reset the resolution right so I could get my Laptop screen to come up. I reset the resolution back to 1400X1050 and everything is back working again. I hope I don't have to go through this every time I go on the road?
Irv
