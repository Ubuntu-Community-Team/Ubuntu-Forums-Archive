### Post by mlomker on 2005-10-04
> I want to upgrade to Hoary 5.10 without lossing my data, I don't want to reinstal the whole ubuntu again. 


Upgrading is pretty much the same as installing everything over again.  You'll definitely want to back up all of your data because the upgrade doesn't always go well.  I'd very much recommend that you wait until it is formally released.

There is a forum for Breezy and quite a few posts on [how to]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=70948") upgrade.  If you're going to do it over the Internet then you'll want a fast connection or it'll take a long time.
