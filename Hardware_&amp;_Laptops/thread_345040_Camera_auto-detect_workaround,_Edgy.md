### Post by DavidFourer on 2007-01-23
Problem: Can't upload photos from my camera.  Since upgrading to edgy, I plug in my camera and nothing happens.  
Simple work-around:  (some of us didn't know this)
Launch "gnome-volume-manager-gthumb" manually.

plug in camera and then
Command:  /usr/bin/gnome-volume-manager-gthumb

So I don't have to repeat this, I put an icon on my launch bar.
Open Nautilus from menu:Places>computer
Find directory /usr/bin
Sort the icons by name: menu:view>sort>name
Drag the icon gnome-volume-manager-gthumb to your launch bar at top of desktop.  
Dialog pops up
View and select icon
Hit GO.  You should have an icon for loading photos from your camera.
