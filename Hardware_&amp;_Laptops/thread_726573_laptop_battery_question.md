### Post by Existentialist on 2008-03-16
The power runs from your power cable to your power supply which delegates power to the computer or battery as necessary.  This is why you can use the computer plugged in without the battery.  I have read the same thing about batteries.  There are a lot of theories, but the only disadvantage of running the computer off of AC power without the battery, is that if you accidentally unplug the cord the computer will shut down.
