not even a fan comes on?? this is because the motherboard is coming in contact with something metal, it's shorting out so it won't turn on and fry itself, which is a good thing. this happend to me when I switched from a microATX to a normal ATX, I had accidentally left in one of the stnadoffs and for the life of me I couldn't figure out why the thing wouldn' t power on. If I were you, make sure nothing is touching the case regarding the motherboard. Also, this can mean that you have tightened the cpu fan to hard into the cpu, this is the other very common casuse, don't push the "push-pins" to hard, just one click should do it. (if it's a 775 that is) check these 2 things, if those are good, you can take out your power supply and take it to a local pc shop and they can test it for ya. Since I work on computer, I bought a cheap tester for like $20 bucks, check it out: [http://www.directron.com/ps124.html](http://www.directron.com/ps124.html)
it's really cool, it can check 20 and 24 pin, it can check floppy, sata, ide, 4 pin, 8 pin.
