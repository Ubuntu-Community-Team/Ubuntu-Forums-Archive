### Post by phylae on 2007-10-15
APC is the best UPS manufacturer. For something like this, reliability is really the most important thing.

I live in a basement suite with old and unreliable power. My computer used to just shutdown randomly, even if power didn't 'officially' go out. I got an APC Back-UPS XS1200 and haven't had any problems since. It is perfect. It has lasted me through tons of little drops in power. The few times that power was out for a long time (hours) it initializing the shutdown as it should.

I just bought another APC UPS (Back-UPS RS1300) for my new server.

To me it just isn't worth the few dollars that I would save to get a cheaper brand.
