### Post by kleeman on 2005-02-18
According to linuxprinting.org your printer should work (more or less) with the s600 driver:

[http://www.linuxprinting.org/canon-faq.html#s_25](http://www.linuxprinting.org/canon-faq.html#s_25)

So got to Computer--> System Configuration -----> Printing and install the
attached printer using the s600 driver (double click on new printer icon and follow your nose ;-)

Also turboprint offers a commercial driver for the s330 specifically

[http://www.turboprint.de/english.html](http://www.turboprint.de/english.html)
