### Post by theox26 on 2007-07-01
Actually I do have this phone, and i love it.  The way I've found to sync it with Evolution is kind of round about but it works very well.  

I got an account with [www.scheduleworld.com](www.scheduleworld.com) which will sync with your phone from the OTA settings.  After than I sync ScheduleWorld with my Google calender and with evolution. 

Like I said, it may not be the most direct and beautiful method, but if no one else has another option, it works and it works well.

Good Luck
