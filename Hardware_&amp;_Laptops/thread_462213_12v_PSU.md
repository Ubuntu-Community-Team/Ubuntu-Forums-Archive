### Post by Titus A Duxass on 2007-06-03
There is no easy (cheap) way around this.

An alternative, though not very pretty, would be to use a DC to AC converter.
You can get them for about 30€.

I am not sure if a cheap one could run a PC + Monitor.

Your normal PC usually needs +12vdc and +/-5vdc.

You could build a 12v to 5v converter for not much money and run the 12vdc direct from the battery. You would have to remove the standard PSU and connect direct to the mother board and peripherals.

Not a task for the faint hearted.
