### Post by evilghost on 2005-09-15
With nvidia-settings you may be able to monitor your GPU temp.  This exists in 7676, just run "nvidia-settings" from the terminal.

Your card may not support temp probing from nvidia-settings so you may want to try nvclock.

I don't know if your card has a thermal sensor on it.
