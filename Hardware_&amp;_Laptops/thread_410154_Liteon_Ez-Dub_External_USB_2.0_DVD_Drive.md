### Post by eric_poulsen on 2007-08-02
Short answer: "Yes, with a 'but, ...'"


Long answer:

Yes, but the "File" and "Dub" buttons on the top won't do anything.  In a Windows environment, these buttons launch special Lite-on software that you've already installed.  Fortunately, these buttons are simply unnecessary bells and whistles.

For use as a CD/DVD reader/writer in Ubuntu, it works fine.  When you put a blank disc in, the popup for writing to the disc appears as expected.
