### Post by lenwood on 2006-06-21
[QUOTE=Paracelsus]how do I get a battery icon on my taskbar[/QUOTE]

*_EDIT: I just noticed that you're using xubuntu. I'm using Ubuntu, so this may not be accurate._*

Click, in this order...
System / Preferences / Power Management / General (tab)

And finally, select "Alwasy display icon" and then close. Ba-da-bing, its next to the clock all the time. Mine is pretty accurate.
