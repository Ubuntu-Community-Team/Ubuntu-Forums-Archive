### Post by MonsterDog on 2006-10-03
Hi,
If you really want budget I recommend Dell Outlet.  I bought a refurb Inspiron 1300 not quite a year ago for about $500 US, incl. shipping.  Everything worked just fine for me.  I had to use ndiswrapper to get my bcm4318 wireless card working and 915resolution for the widescreen.  The only issue I have left is that it won't hibernate properly, but it does suspend.

The new dells seem to have an intel wireless card, hopefully that card would work with the native drivers.  Otherwise, I've been really happy with this laptop.

Dell Inspiron 1300 (aka B130, ME051), 1.4G Celeron (I did say budget), 512MB RAM, 40G HD, CDRW-DVD-ROM, Dell 1370 wireless](*,) ,15.4 inch WXGA + Works/Word.  Total price $526.  It's almost cheaper than the 3 year warranty and I won't feel *too* bad when I drop it.

FYI- Dell Outlet is kind of funky.  You have to read all the details, sometimes it seems like two computers are the same but one might come with a better battery or other options that aren't immediately visible.  Don't shop when the US is at work, 2 AM Pacific time is pretty good.  Outlet's warranty is only 90 days, but they are generally pretty good.  My HD fried and they had a new one here in under 36 hours.
Despite what you may read around here getting Dapper up and running on this thing was, well, breezy.

Have fun.
