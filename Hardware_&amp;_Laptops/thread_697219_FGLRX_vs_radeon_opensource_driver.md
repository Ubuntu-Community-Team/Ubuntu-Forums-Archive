Well, that's an easy choice. ATI haven't supported anything older than radeon 9500 the last one and a half year, so for 7500 or 9000 "radeon" is the only driver that will work.
PowerPlay is only available on notebook gpu's and Radeon HD3800, so there would be no way to make use of that feature on a Radeon 7500 or 9000 (unless you're thinking of bying an old laptop with Mobility Radeon 7500 or 9000?).
