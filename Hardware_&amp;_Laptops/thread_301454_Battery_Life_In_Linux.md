### Post by lixy on 2006-11-17
You could always complain to the computer manufacturer about that matter. It clearly derives from the effort component makers don't put into tweaking Linux drivers. That won't fix your problem right away, but just might for future purchases.

Also, knowing that most energy's spent on display these days, reducing the brightness from the BIOS leads to substantial battery life gains.
