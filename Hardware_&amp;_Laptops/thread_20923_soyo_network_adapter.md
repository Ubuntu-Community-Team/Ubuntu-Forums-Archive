### Post by harryc on 2005-03-19
After a bit of research, I can't locate the chipset for that adapter and find no mention of it being used in Linux, no less Ubuntu. I'd recommend that you return it and look into a PCI adapter. The Dlink DWL-G520 seems to work out the box on Ubuntu. See the HCL linked here. There are some other adapters mentioned that work as well. Get one. You'll need an open PCI slot in your desktop. 

[http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCards](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCards)
