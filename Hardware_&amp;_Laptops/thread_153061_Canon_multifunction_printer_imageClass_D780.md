### Post by idefis on 2008-02-07
I would recommend anybody to contact Canon technical support to show your interest in Linux drivers. This is the only way Canon will hear the Linux community.

I just want to remember that Dell preinstall Ubuntu on some of their PC since IdeaStorm: [http://www.ideastorm.com/](http://www.ideastorm.com/)

You can show your interest for Linux drivers for instance here:

[http://www.canon-europe.com/Support/Software/Linux/registration.asp](http://www.canon-europe.com/Support/Software/Linux/registration.asp)

or in any Canon support site. Even if you receive the answer that they do not support Linux drivers, you let them know that Linux exists.
