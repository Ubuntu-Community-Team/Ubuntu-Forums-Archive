### Post by wouterommeslag on 2006-04-16
I own a HP nx8220 laptop. Most of the hardware worked out-of-the box (wifi, volume buttons , sound etc). For my graphics I installed the new ATI drivers and that did the trick. 

Now only 1 problem left:

At home, I always use my wifi connection. Now, a few days ago, on location, I had to use my wired cared because no wifi available. I just plugged in the cable and turned the laptop on. Then I configured the card in Network manager (DHCP and so on and so forth). 

Now, back home, when I boot, It takes a few seconds more to boot, because he hangs at something with network. I guess it has something to do with the wired card. Can I speed things up again?

---

### Post by mscman on 2006-04-16
If you disable that wired card, it should skip trying to configure it at boot.  Just disable it in the network manager.

### Post by wouterommeslag on 2006-04-16
Thanks, it worked!
