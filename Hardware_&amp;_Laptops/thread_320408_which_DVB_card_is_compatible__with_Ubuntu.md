### Post by luria on 2007-01-15
That page is outdated and hasn't been updated since 2001.  Go to linuxtv.org and look at the DVB wiki for updated information.  Most DVB-S cards work fine in ubuntu and most other distributions with a fairly recent kernel. The dvb mailinglist has some recent information: [http://www.linuxtv.org/cgi-bin/mailman/listinfo/linux-dvb](http://www.linuxtv.org/cgi-bin/mailman/listinfo/linux-dvb)

I use two Skystar 2 DVB-S cards - they work flawlessy in both dapper and edgy.
