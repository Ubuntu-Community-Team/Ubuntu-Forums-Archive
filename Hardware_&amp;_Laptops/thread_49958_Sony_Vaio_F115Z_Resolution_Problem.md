### Post by sbucky on 2005-07-21
Whilst looking for something else I came across the page..

[http://web.tagus.ist.utl.pt/~ricardo.cruz/sonyDebianHowTo/debian-vaio-fs215s.htm#Section_III](http://web.tagus.ist.utl.pt/~ricardo.cruz/sonyDebianHowTo/debian-vaio-fs215s.htm#Section_III)

All I needed to do was make the Monitor section in my xorg.conf file look like this:

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier 	"Generic Monitor"
	HorizSync 	28.0 - 110.0
	VertRefresh 	43.0 - 90.0
	Modeline 	"1280x800" 80.58 1280 1344 1480 1680 800 801 804 827
	Option 		"DPMS"
EndSection

Worked a treat.
