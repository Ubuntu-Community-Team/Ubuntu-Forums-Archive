### Post by spd106 on 2007-05-12
I'm afraid that the bcm43xx version in Feisty (and Edgy) is limited to 11 Mbps. The driver devs have pushed it up to 24Mbps, but it's not stable enough yet. Fingers crossed for 54Mbps in Gutsy.

If you want it now then you'll have to go with ndiswrapper instead or buy another card.
