### Post by minktoast on 2005-10-14
I have just installed Breezy on my Acer 4651 LMi.

I needed to type "linux noapic" to get the installer to run.  After that everything worked just fine.  As far as I can tell (so far) it has detected all my hardware except the card reader (I'm sure I can get this working - I'm just reporting what the installer did without any assistance).  This machine has the Nvidia 6600Go which it detected and also the installer correctly identified my 1400 by 1050 display without me having to do any tinkering (a first for me with Linux).

In answer to the original question, the Intel Wireless card has been correctly identified and is working perfectly.

First impressions of Breezy are superb.  Many slick improvements over Hoary - well done to the Ubuntu team - this is impressive stuff.
