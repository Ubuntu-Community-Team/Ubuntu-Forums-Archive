Yep... sounds like the laser crapped out, which is why Nautilus kept asking you to insert a disk ( because the drive can't see that there is one in there ).  It happens sometimes.  How old was the drive?  If it is still under warranty, send it back and get a new one.  If not, well, it served well during its time, but now it's time to buy a new one.  They are dirt cheap these days anyhow.  I had my old one do the same thing a few months ago, though it was only the red laser that died ( the one that reads cds ) but dvds were fine.  I picked up a new one that's better than my old one for like $40.
