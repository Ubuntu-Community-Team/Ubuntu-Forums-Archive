### Post by NotJustANewbie on 2005-12-15
The best thing to do is to turn your computer off - POWER OFF.
Unplug your DVD Drive and plug it back in (internally) making sure that the connections are properly connected.  Then boot up Ubuntu and hope it recognises it. Type "dmesg" to see what the output of your computer is for the hardware.

Try using a live CD to check if the drive works and its not the hardware that is the problem.
