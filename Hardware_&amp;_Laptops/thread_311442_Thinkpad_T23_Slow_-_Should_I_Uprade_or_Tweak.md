### Post by mattotoole on 2006-12-04
I'm using a T20, a little older and "slower" than the T23.  I have no speed complaints.

Make sure you're using the 686 kernel, instead of the 386.  If not, it's easy enough to upgrade with Synaptic.  You'll have to reboot for the change to take effect.  I don't know why, but sometimes the installer will choose the 386 kernel, sometimes the 686.  It makes a big difference on my machine, and probably yours too.
