### Post by buntunub on 2007-08-20
I have a dv2415nr with Feisty running on it now. Everything works including the builtin webcam mostly out of the box. Minimal tweaking for the wifi (ndiswrapper) and that works flawlessly including WPA via gnome network manager. I had originally had alsa issues the first try with Feisty, but I later found out that was probably because I still had Vista on it. Once I got rid of that piece of garbage and reloaded Ubuntu, all was well.
