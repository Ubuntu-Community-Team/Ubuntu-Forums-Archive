### Post by ahallubuntu on 2006-07-27
Did you try going into Preferences and trying to change Screen Resolution?

If there's nothing there higher than what you have, several possibilities:

- Ubuntu didn't detect all the modes of your video card during install.  Or when you installed and you were asked(?) which resolutions you wanted to use for the display, you chose modes that are now too small for a 20" monitor.  You can have it try to re-detect them. (I forget how, but you're basically configuring Xorg again.)
- Your laptop's video card is limited to its resolution and Ubuntu may already have detected the maximum resolution.
