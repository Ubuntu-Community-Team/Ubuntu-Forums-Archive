You might get a good deal on nvidia video cards that support directX  9.0 (in Windows) as directX 10. is coming ouit and everyone is cutting prices on directX 9.0 cards.Check out the sites mentioned above for video cards also.
You might look at sites that sell linux compatible desktops for specs. You might even buy one and save yourself the hassle of building one. Check out [www.distrowatch.com](www.distrowatch.com) for links to linux computer sites.
These are sites that I use in my research and they are pretty decent.
