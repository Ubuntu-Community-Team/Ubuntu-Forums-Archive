### Post by daengbo on 2007-11-18
If you are using straight Ubuntu, the iRiver Clix is a pretty decent deal. It uses the MTP protocol to do file management, so it used to be a PITA. As of Ubuntu 7.10, though, Rhythmbox handles MTP through a plugin, so you can just drag and drop from Rhythmbox to your player.

If you want to go really cheap, I suggest one of the players based on an SD card, like the [GrandTec MPG-1000]("http://salestores.com/grmpgrmp3mup.html"). $30 + a 2GB SD card for another $30 and you're ready to go. They're guaranteed to have no problems because you just load the songs through a card reader. [These]("http://www.nextag.com/GENERIC-Folding-USB-2-574714305/prices-html") sound cool, too.
