### Post by Existentialist on 2008-03-16
For $250 you can get the 8800gt, which will perform almost identically, if not better than the 9600gt.  You can even find the 8800gts's 512mb for under $250 now, which will outperform the 9600gt's and these run fine with current drivers and kernels.  In terms of performance, you will not be able to beat the 8800gts 512 until the new 9800gtx is released later this month.  These will also cost $350-400+ so for now I would go with the 8800gts.  I just upgraded my 8800gt to a gts due to the unimpressive preliminary tests of the new 9800 series being released this month.
