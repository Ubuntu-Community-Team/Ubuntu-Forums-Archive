### Post by dr_gonzo on 2007-06-17
Hey

Got this laptop yesterday. It's freakin sweet! I would thoroughly recommend it! It's cheap (my version cost €777, which is cheap in Ireland) and it completely works with Ubuntu. I'm just posting here so if anyone's looking for a good Ubuntu laptop, they should probably get this. 

Here are the specs:
Core2Duo Processor T5300 (1.73GHz,533MHz)
15.4" UltraSharp Wide Screen WXGA+ (1440x900) TrueLife Display
Memory Dual-Channel 1024MB (2x512) 533MHz DDR2 SDRAM
Hard Drive 120GB Serial ATA (5400 RPM)
DVD+/-RW (Read/Write) 8X Fixed Internal
Integrated Intel Media Accelerator 950
Software Integrated Sound Blaster Audigy Advanced HD Audio
Intel&#174; Pro Wireless 3945 802.11a/b/g Mini-PCI Card (for Core 2 Duo Processor)

The CPU is detected and CPU frequency scaling works.
The GPU is properly detected and after installing 915resolution, runs at full resolution.
Desktop effects works smoothly except video playback doesn't work. Need to find out about that...
Power management (hibernate and suspend) works perfectly.
Sound works.
Wireless card is detected (haven't connected to a wireless network but there isn't a reason why it shouldn't work).
