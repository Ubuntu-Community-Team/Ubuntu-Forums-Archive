### Post by naked on 2006-03-12
If you laptop supports 'speedstepping', meaning it will slow down the processor when not needed, this makes a huge difference.  Go to your gnome panel properties and select add applet to panel.  Look for the CPU Frequency Scaling Monitor.  This is just a monitor, but will let you know what is happening.
