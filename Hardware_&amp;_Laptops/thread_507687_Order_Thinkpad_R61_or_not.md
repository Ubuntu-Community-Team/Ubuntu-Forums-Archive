### Post by argantel on 2007-08-01
I bought a R61 some weeks ago.

I Like it very much though I haven't managed to get the graphics working yet (800x600 hurts when you know there is so much more). Sound isn't working as well.

But don't let that bother you, I have started using Ubuntu (Feisty) only some months ago and due to total lack of time am not very experienced yet.

The laptop doesn't get very hot, even when run for hours. The place you put your hands gets warm, yes, but not as much as to be uncomfortable.

The adapter gets hot as well but still touchable.

The R61 is rather quiet. The vents only get to work when necessary. I have had no time to play anything yet, thus I cannot tell you about gaming performance. 

Unfortunately it is sold with Vista preinstalled. I got rid of it very soon...it takes about 30GB of HD!!..... plus I wasn't very convinced of the system having experienced some of Beryls functions...

I keep XP on the laptop as an alternative. Until I'm better used to Ubuntu :)
