### Post by w4ett on 2007-09-23
Pretty good advice on the 6200 card....make sure you get one with the NV43 GPU. (not the 44).. The 43 is capable for massive overclocking and the pixel pipelines are unlockable which will effectively give you the power of a 6800 card at 2/3 the cost.
 
I have one myself and it works great.  You can find them well under $50
