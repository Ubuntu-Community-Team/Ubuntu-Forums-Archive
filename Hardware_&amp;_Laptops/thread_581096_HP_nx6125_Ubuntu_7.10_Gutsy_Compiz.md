### Post by dantum on 2007-10-19
Hi Everyone,

Just wanted to share my early experiences with 7.10 on my HP NX6125 laptop with ATI Xpress 200M Graphics card.

Compiz works. I enabled restricted drivers and installed xserver-xgl package. After reboot it worked and seems stable so far. The is no need for creating new sessions anymore, login as normal and it kicks in. 

Compiz was a major problem with Xpress 200M  card before as xserver had problems with xgl and ati drivers. It worked once after a lot of hacking but broke and never came back even after clean installs.

Wireless worked as well with my access point. I haven't tried WPA or WPA2 though, but will give it a go later and try to report back.

My thanks to Ubuntu and Debian Teams for getting a very polished product out to us. Keep up the good work.
