There is very limited support for Brother. Go to [www.linuxprinting.org](www.linuxprinting.org) and see if there is any support for that model.

HP and Epson work best in Linux. (have the most supported models) I have an HP 5550 and a new 4214 multifunction which both work on Linux. (scanning is a little dicey)
