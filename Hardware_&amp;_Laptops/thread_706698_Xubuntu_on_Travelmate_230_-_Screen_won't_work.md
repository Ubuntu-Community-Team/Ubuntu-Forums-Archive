### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-02-26
I think the bad V_BIOS error is because the chipset does not have enough system memory. Go into your system setup (probably by pressing 'Del' when you start it) and make sure at least 8MB is allocated to the video device.

Also, add "1024x768" to the Modes line in xorg.conf

EDIT: If that doesn;'t work, you might also try changing the Driver line from "intel" to "i810".
