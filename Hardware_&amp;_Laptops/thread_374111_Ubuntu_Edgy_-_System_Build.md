### Post by dburnett77 on 2007-03-08
For those who might be curious, I went with the following system:

Motherboard:  Gigabyte GA-945GM-S2
Processor:  Intel Pentium D 915
HD:  Western Digital Caviar SE SATA, 80GB
RAM:  Kingston DDR2 PC2-4200, 533MHz, 512MB

Ubuntu loaded in about 30-45 minutes, and updated via cable modem in about 45 minutes.  Don't see any problems yet.  Don't really expect any, either.
