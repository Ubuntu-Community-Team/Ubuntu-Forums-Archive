### Post by elemal on 2008-05-29
Make sure u have g15macro running, press the MR-button on your kbd, insert a key combination, for example ctrl-z, press one of the G1 - G18, and u should have a macro bound to the G-button chosen.
When u are done assigning macros to G-buttons - end the app with ctrl-c - this will make g15macro to write your macros to /home/ ~ /.g15macro/g15macro.conf
