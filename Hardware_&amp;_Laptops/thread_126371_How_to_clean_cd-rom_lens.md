### Post by gitfiddler on 2006-02-06
[QUOTE=UphillSkier]
Is there a way to clean the cd-rom lens? 
[/QUOTE]

Yes, but if you mean some "automatic" method, like inserting a disc and running a program, the answer is "no".

The only possible method involves removing the drive, and wiping the (extremely fragile) lens with a lint free swab. In my (many years of) experiece, if the lens is clouded, there are other elements of the optical system that are bad, and you will be better served by replacing the unit.
