### Post by rklauco on 2007-06-25
The MS sucks on software, but I was very happy with the HW from them. I had a desktop elite set (wireless), working smoothly in Ubuntu (to be honest, even better than in MS Windows).
The only thing not working was the battery indicator (in MS control panel you could see how much battery left).
But I can live with that.
I get rid of it after almost 3 years just because it got too dirty ;-)
And, the battery in keyboard lasted for more than a year, in mouse aprox 6-7months of daily usage.
As I said - crappy software, but reliable hardware.
Anyhow, for example Logitech also has a very good set of wireless desktops (set of keyboard and mouse).
