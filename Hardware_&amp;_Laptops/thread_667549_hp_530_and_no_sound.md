### Post by statto1977 on 2008-02-26
I had no sound on my 530 initially. On about the third reboot I miraculously got sound (without having done anything). The fix mentioned elsewhere sorted the 'muting speakers when headphones plugged in' issue.

Sound worked fine for a week, then I had my computer hang when working within a VirtualBox. When it rebooted I'd lost sound, as well as my email account (all my address book and emails were still there, just the account settings were lost). None of the things I tried sorted out the problem, but three or four reboots later it once again miraculously solved itself.

I have no rational explanation for all this, but my advice would be that if you have this issue and you've tried all the other suggestions to no avail then try a few reboots.
