### Post by Eystein Bye on 2007-10-29
You did not say what type of digital signal you want to view? T, S og C
(t = terrestrial), (s = satelitt) or (c = cable)

For a list of supported DVB hardware go to:
[http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Supported_Hardware]("http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Supported_Hardware")
Choose T, S or C then USB

Hope you find something you can use.
