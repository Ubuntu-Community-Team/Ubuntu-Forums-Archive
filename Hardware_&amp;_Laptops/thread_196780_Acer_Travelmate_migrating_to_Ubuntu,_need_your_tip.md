### Post by simon360 on 2006-06-14
The really good thing about Ubuntu is it's Live CD. If the Live CD works on your computer, Ubuntu will work on your computer. And anything that doesn't work, you can just ask about here, and we'll tell you if it'll work, and what you'll need to download. Everything (almost, anyway) is downloadable via apt-get, so there isn't too much configuring involved. 

So, go ahead and download the Live CD, if everything works (check your internet, too), just double-click Install from the desktop. Ask here if anything doesn't work, even do it from within the Live CD; it'll save you time.

Good luck!
