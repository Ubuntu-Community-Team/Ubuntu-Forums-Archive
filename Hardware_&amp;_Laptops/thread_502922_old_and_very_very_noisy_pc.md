### Post by DrMega on 2007-07-17
The spec is fine, but compaq evos can be noisy (we use them at work). Is it a desktop or a tower? If it is a tower you might be able to get a really silent CPU cooler from your local computer shop. Basically, it consists of a huge circular heat sink with a larger than usual fan. The fan spins slower than the standard one thus making less noise.

Your other problem is going to be the power supply fan and case fan. In the case of the former, you can buy silent power supplies and in the case of the latter, again a larger case fan with a lower RPM might help.

Another thing to consider is the mounting of the base unit. It may be that the noise is worse than it needs to be purely because it is vibrating whatever it is stood on. If this is the case then simply cushion it somehow to absorb the vibration. Maybe some corrugated cardboard (a bust up cardboard box) or if you want to be posh, go to your nearest audio shop and get some rubber stick on feet intended for speakers, and stick them on the bottom of your PC.
