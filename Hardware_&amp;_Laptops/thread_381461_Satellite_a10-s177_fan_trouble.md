### Post by rpaar63 on 2007-03-11
Hi, I have a Toshiba A35-S159 laptop. My fans run alot less under Ubuntu than when i'm running Windows XP . I was a little worried at first but checking the vents once in awhile i realized it just runs alot cooler with Linux for some reason.
If they do start running alot or you feel alot of heat coming from the vents take a look at the heatsink. If it's like mine the heatsink gets clogged every once in awhile. If you search for Toshiba overheating problems on the internet you'll find it's a common problem with some models, it sometimes shows up as the computer just shuts off for no reason. I kept having that happen until i realized the heat sink has a tendency to clog. i pull the cover of the cpu and clean it, and  my son laptop does the same thing his isnt as eesy to get to so we spray a little  compressed air into the vents as gently as possible and it takes care of it for a few months
 just thought i'd give you a heads up, does'nt hurt to keep an eye on it.
