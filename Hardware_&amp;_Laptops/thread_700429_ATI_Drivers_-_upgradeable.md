### Post by OffAxis on 2008-02-18
> What I want to know is are the drivers in the restricted manager the proprietary drivers from ATI?
yes. 

> Would downloading and installing the drivers from the ati site effect this in anyway?
most definitely.

> Will I lose all my effects or run into issues?
Probably and definitely.

By installing the driver manually from the ati site you'd also need to manually update (uninstall the old and install the new) later or manually remove it and change back to the open source or 'restricted' driver.

If you're looking for a slightly more up to date driver try using envy to manage the most recent version (essentially another restricted driver manager).

[http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html]("http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html")
