### Post by mikesfx on 2007-11-28
I use a Sil 680, it's kinda crappy but it gets the job done. Other users on the forum may have other oppinions, but one of the things you may want to watch out for with this card is that there is a special workaround you have to do to get your system to boot after installation. I'm actually searching for that workaround now which is how I stumbled onto your post.

If you're doing anything critical with the PC you're installing the sil680 on you may want to choose another brand. But if you just want to use a raid 0 or 1 implementation for either mirroring or the extra speed of striping, in my experience this card does work.
