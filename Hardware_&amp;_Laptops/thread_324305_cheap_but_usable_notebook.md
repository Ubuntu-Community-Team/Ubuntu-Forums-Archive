### Post by crispy_420 on 2006-12-23
There are many places to buy used ones for less than $600. The main thing I would look at battery warranty and "grade".

Many places will not give you any warranty on the battery and just as many will tell you outright that they will not hold a charge at all. That is something to look out for since a new battery, if you can find one for your model, will add another $100-150.

"Grades" is a whole other issue. Look at how each company defines grades. They can mean small things like scratches and missing bay covers all the way up to cracks (in screens and cases) to dead pixels.

Also look at included parts like a default OS. Some sellers will not include an OS or even if they do it will be "pre-installed", so if you mess up there is no going back.

Just my two cents.
