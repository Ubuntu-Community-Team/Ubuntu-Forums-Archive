### Post by chicagoD on 2007-09-25
if when you plug in your headphones and there is no sound...

the problem might be simply to right click on the volume control at the top right hand corner, click 'open volume control', 'edit', 'preferences'.  then click on everything having to do with the mic- mic, mic boost, front mic boost, etc.

mute those, or turn those volumes down low.

make sure PCM and master (if applicable) are turned up high.

this should take care of it.
