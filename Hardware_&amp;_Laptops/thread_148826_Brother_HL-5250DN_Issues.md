I've got a Brother HL-5250DN connected to our LAN, and the Brother drivers work fine in one-sided mode. From OS X it works fine, and I figured it worked by a hardware switch--but apparently it's a driver issue. If I want a different first page I have to put in into the main tray. I have tried all the paper-tray options in the driver.
