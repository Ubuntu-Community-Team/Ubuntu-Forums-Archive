### Post by tommcd on 2008-04-04
Set the horizontal and vertical refresh rates as per your monitor's specs. The refresh rates should be in your monitor's manual, or you can google them. I have noticed that the nvidia-settings utility gives slightly different values for vertical refresh rates than the "settings > preferences > refresh rates" in Ubuntu's menu.
Try setting the resfresh rates as per your monitors specs and that should be optimum.
