### Post by reuben on 2005-10-15
If you just want a basic digital camera (self timer included) I recommend the Canon Powershot A20. It's "only" 2MP (1600x1200 resolution) but is excellent quality, fast to startup, and you can probably find one on ebay for less that $50. 

I'm currently also using a Canon Powershot S2, which is amazing, but unless you want to go semi-pro you won't need most of what it offers. 

Digikam supports both and is generally excellent.
