### Post by MrFSL on 2007-11-13
Earlier today I search around trying to find a decent cheap webcam that worked out of the box with Ubuntu Feisty.

I wished to take advantage of the new video option in Skype for Linux. Ultimately I was disappointed. I just couldn't find and good and recent information.

On a whim I purchased a webcam and it worked perfectly out of the box. I thought I would share the info here for others. The video quality was excellent and I did side by side comparisons with several more expensive webcams.

The camera was a Logitech QuickCam Chat. The manufacturer SKU in 961462-0403 and it can be purchased online for around $25 (American).

Hope this helps someone out.
