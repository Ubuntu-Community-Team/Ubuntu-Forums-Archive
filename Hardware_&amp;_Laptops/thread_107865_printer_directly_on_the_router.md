### Post by DFreeze on 2005-12-26
Some more reading on the forum gave me a few ideas. Using the JetDirect option in the Printer-settings dialog got the printer to output something (an error message, but non the less, something had been communicated). 

So I switched the driver to the first one below the recommended driver and I had a good testpage!

If you can't get your network printer working as well, try searching on JetDirect. You'll find enough info here!
