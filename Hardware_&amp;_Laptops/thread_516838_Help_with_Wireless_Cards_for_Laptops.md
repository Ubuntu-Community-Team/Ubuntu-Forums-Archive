### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-08-05
You need to state what kind of laptop card you want.

If it's a mini-PCI card - then an Intel 3945 is probably the best option.  But be aware that some laptop manufacturers have a restricted hardware policy (IBM, HP, Compaq...) so you can only use their own branded stuff.

For PCMCIA - do a bit of research:
Hopefully this will get you started with some options:
