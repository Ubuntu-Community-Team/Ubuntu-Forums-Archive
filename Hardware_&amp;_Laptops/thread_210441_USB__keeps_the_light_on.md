### Post by cracker on 2006-07-07
This is a feature of ATX power supplies and motherboards. They leave power to USB devices, network cards, and a few other things, so that things like PDAs and MP3 players can charge while your PC is off, and so that you can configure 'wake on lan' features for network cards.
