### Post by Mimsy on 2006-10-24
I have a laptop of the exact same model, (BIOS start date is in January 2002) and the battery worked out of the box with Ubuntu. It does have a very short active life, but it was just as bad under WinXP.

Someone on the forums suggested this:

1. Completely discharge the battery
2. Put it in a ziploc bag and deep freeze it for at least 24 hours
3. Take the battery out of the freezer, and *let it get back up to room temperature before doing anything else with it*
4. Charge the battery fully, without using the laptop while you do
5. Run the battery flat again
6. Repeat the process at least once

I did that to my laptop battery, and its active battery life increased from 55 minutes to 85. Wohoo! :)

/Mimsy
