### Post by René Reichelt on 2008-03-12
Hello masi0,

with linux the LIFEBOOK C1320 has seperate volume controls for internal speakers and headphones (which can be quite useful). To solve your problem, change the default volume control to "PCM". This will affect Headphones AND internal speakers. At least, this solution worked for me.
