### Post by librarybaldy on 2008-06-11
From the **System** menu, choose **Preferences** --> **Sound**.  In the **Sound Preferences** window, look to the bottom in the section labeled **Default Mixer**.  In the box below it, you will see a selection for **Master**, **PCM**, **Front**, **Surround**, etc . . . choose **PCM**.  

A better, illustrated guide can be found at [http://linux.dell.com/wiki/index.php/Ubuntu_7.10/Issues/Built_In_Analog_Mic_Does_Not_Work](http://linux.dell.com/wiki/index.php/Ubuntu_7.10/Issues/Built_In_Analog_Mic_Does_Not_Work)  

It worked for me in 8, I hope it works for you in 7 :)
