[/QUOTE]

I have a T43p which I previously had running 5.04 Hoary with working audio but no DVD video or DVD/CD-RW burning capability.  I just upgraded to 5.10 Breezy this past week and it is significantly better; I have audio working great, the DVD drive is completely functional for video playback and for recording with GnomeBaker, and I've even gotten the finger print scanner (the 'p' part of the T43p model name) to work with pam.d

This site has a ton of info regarding linux on thinkpad hardware and has proven very valuable: [http://www.thinkwiki.org]("http://www.thinkwiki.org")

Breezy actually uses the full features of the SATA bus at full speed which significantly improves disk i/o.  I'm just loving it...
