### Post by Rob500 on 2007-10-10
I have pretty much the same laptop as you; mine is a TravelMate 2410 :D

It's possible to upgrade the RAM like that so it would be 1.25GB, although it would be more beneficial to buy two identical sticks of RAM as your laptop is probably DDR2 RAM, and hence supports dual-channel, which gives better performance if two identical sticks are installed.

MIne currently runs 2x 256 sticks to make 512MB. If you want 1GB, I would buy 2x 512 sticks, as then you will get the benefit of dual-channel i.e. better performance than standard 1GB.
