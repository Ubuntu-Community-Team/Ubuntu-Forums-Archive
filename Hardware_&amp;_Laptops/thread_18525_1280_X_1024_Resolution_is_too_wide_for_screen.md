### Post by carlc on 2005-03-07

When selecting this resolution, the display is too wide and long and will not fit the monitor screen. I am currently running 1024 X 768 which works fine but I would rather use 1280 X 1024. Any ideas on how to get it to work would be greatly appreciated. I would rather not adjust the monitor settings because then I would have to readjust them when booting other os's.
