I do not know how to guide you with the specifications you provide. What I will tell you is what I did in my situation.
First of all in order to hear sound you have to install some codecs. Automatix is one good solution. Synaptic package manager is another. 
After that if you still don't have sound type alsamixer and confirm that the options you want are enabled. I hope that these will help.
