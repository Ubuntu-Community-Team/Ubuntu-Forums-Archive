### Post by heimo on 2007-05-13
If you see 1GB in Ubuntu, then all your memory is in use.

In Windows, you may have mixed total memory (or what-ever it's called) which includes also swap / pagefile and physical memory, which is just the amount of RAM. My experience is that dual channel memory is reported just like single channel memory.
