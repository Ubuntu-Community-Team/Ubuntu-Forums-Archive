### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2007-02-06
I have a fanless small form factor machine with a 800MHz VIA processor with 0,5 GB of memory. Web/email/word processing is fine. My machine has a laptop hard disk, so can't comment on the external HD aspect. I installed using netboot and this was relatively painless.

About the fanlessness -- my box is practically one big (relatively speaking, that is) aluminium heat sink and seems to spread the heat pretty well. No problems running 100% CPU for hours.
