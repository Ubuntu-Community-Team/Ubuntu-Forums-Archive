Webcam support in Linux can be a little challenging at times.  I believe your camera uses UVC the driver located here:

May work for you, it will require some effort to get it working.  I have a Logitech Quickcam Notebook Pro (2006) that I never could get working reliably, I finally went out and bought a Creative Livecam Notebook Pro and it was plug and play.  I use it  with WengoPhone for vdeo chat.
