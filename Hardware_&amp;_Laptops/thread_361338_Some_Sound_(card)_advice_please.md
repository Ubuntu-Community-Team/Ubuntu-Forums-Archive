### Post by Daveth on 2007-02-14
I have an Creative Audigy 2 which worked out of the box on Dapper, though I had to hunt out alsamixer from Synaptic to get my 5.1 speakers rocking. Might be worth moving from Breezy up to Dapper, as support for all sorts of hardware improves with upgrades. The stunning Creative X-FI card series does not currently have alsa support, though this is alleged to be fixed in mid 2007. The sound advances are meant to be amazing- might be worth saving up!
