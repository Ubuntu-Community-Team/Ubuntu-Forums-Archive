### Post by nobodie on 2008-02-02
I bought the Epson RX590 (from Singapore, probably not available elsewhere but the RX line is what i am talking about) and have been moderately satisfied. I am running 7.10, fully updated, but the major problem is just permissions for the scanner module, nothing too big but for a newbie it might be frustrating. 
The big advantage is the 6 color system they have, individual cartridges for each color and outstanding color quality. This is a photo quality printer and is worth the extra trouble for that. It doesn't have the fax, but you might find an RX with that, but if photo quality printing is your need then the Epson is outstanding. 
Here in Asia I can also get an "Inktank" system that allows me to bypass the cartridges and use bottles of ink. Thus the cost is way less than it would be buying cartridges.
