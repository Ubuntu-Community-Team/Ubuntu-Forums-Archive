### Post by TheMono on 2007-04-26
No. Not at all. It is only worth it if you plan on running windows as a virtual machine to get that application.

Even then probably not. Don't worry about it.

Edit: To clarify what I mean, for what you are doing, I'm not convinced you need more than 512MB. I use 1GB simply because I do a lot of media recoding in the background, but if I am just doing what you do my RAM usage doesn't come near 1GB, and to be honest, doesn't really come near 512MB either.
