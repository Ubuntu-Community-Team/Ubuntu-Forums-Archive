### Post by orionjoe on 2008-02-16
Well no one's answered in a long time but I'll post an answer just in case anyone else has this problem.

I had the exact problem on my Acer Aspire 3000, it was working fine for the first month or so of using Ubuntu then the battery wouldn't charge and I was getting messages saying my battery was old or broken. I downloaded Kpowersave and everything is ok now.

You can get it from the synaptic package manager, just do a search for "kpowersave".
