### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-11-07
The fanless VIA Epia series mini-ATX computers use between 12 and 18 watts of power for a 1 GHz processor (roughly equivalent to a Celeron 1 GHz performance).  Do a google search and you will find a power consumption calculator for these minicomputers.  Lots of development is going on in this area, including Walmart's Everex gOS $200 green computer.
