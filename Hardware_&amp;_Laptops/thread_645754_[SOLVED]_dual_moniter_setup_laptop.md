### Post by perixx on 2007-12-20
Hi!

I'm not very experienced with dual-screen setups, not to speak of laptops...

But this link got me on the right track to get it to work in the first place! 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1773624]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1773624")

You'll need to specify one monitor as TFT, the other one as CRT (your CRT monitor, that is) and if you use the accellerated drivers from ATI, you must edit the 'driver' section and insert e.g. 'radeon' (perhaps best to first download and activate it, then you'll have it already in your /etc/xorg.conf) - depends on your grahpics card. 

To be perfectly honest, I really have no idea if you can activate true dual-monitor output on a laptop, but you should try it, of course.
