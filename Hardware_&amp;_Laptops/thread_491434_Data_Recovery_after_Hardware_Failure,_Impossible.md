### Post by CrispyFried on 2007-07-03
the click of death can mean it cant load its OS (drives are computers themselves and have some OS code stored on the platters in a special manufacturers area).. or the drives board is damaged or as you say maybe the heads. or frozen bearings.

 if its the board and you have an identical drive you can swap boards and see if it comes to life. or try the "freezer trick" which is put it in a baggie and put it in the freezer for a few hours. the contraction from cold can unstick frozen bearings or balky electronics. its a **very** long shot but you have nothing to lose. it wont make things any worse anyway.
