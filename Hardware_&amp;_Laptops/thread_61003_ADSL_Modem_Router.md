### Post by tehwa on 2005-08-30
If you mean to say "Will this work straight out of the box *with Ubunutu?*" then the short answer is: Yes, this device supports DHCP, and Ubuntu works with devices that use DHCP. It's as simple as configuring the Modem/Router and then activating your network card in Ubuntu to "This device is configured" and "Configuration: DHCP".

With regards to whether it is worth purchasing, the reviews on the site say good things, but you should always google the produkt and look for lots of user reviews to see how it performs.

### Post by tehwa on 2005-08-30
Oh yeh, USB is a pain in the bum. Any modem/router box that has an Ethernet plug will work with almost no configuration with Ubunutu, granted you have an ethernet card.

Any other PC connected to the router will not affect your Ubuntu machine, since they all run as independent devices connected to the common gateway (in this case, the router).
