### Post by Chris Cromer on 2005-11-22
Nevermind, after some reading on these boards... and some "experiments" with different things I found out how to get it to work. I knew that I used the nvidia graphics card in my laptop since that's what was installed in windows for my new monitor. So I installed "nvidia-glx" which made it so the monitor wasn't jumbled... then I played with some resolution settings since my new monitor doesn't support the same resolution as my laptop monitor.

Looks good... and it's now time to watch a DVD.
