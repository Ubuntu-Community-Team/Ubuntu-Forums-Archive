### Post by hardyn on 2007-03-29
The ram can only be accessed in parallel if the motherboard is dual channel compatible.  and being pc100/133... it is not.
you can always go faster with ram, just not slower. eg. 133 will be compatible with 100 and 66, but not vice versa.
