### Post by rillip on 2007-04-20
I think most of the touchy issues about the wireless can be traced to the card drivers.  My understanding is Broadcom wireless adapters don't have very good support yet because they don't open source their drivers.  See if you can avoid one of those, but most likely anything you get will take some fidling, signifigantly more than your desktop did.  You might try out System76.  They're a company that sells preinstalled Linux laptops with tested compatibilities.  They have a third party forum here.
