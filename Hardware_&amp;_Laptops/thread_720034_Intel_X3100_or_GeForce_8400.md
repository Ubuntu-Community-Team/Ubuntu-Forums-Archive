### Post by sdennie on 2008-03-10
It's a hard call.  I have a Dell with the 8400M and, as long as you are willing to put in some work to get the card running well, it's a great card.  However, with the X3100 you will get MUCH better power savings due to the fact that you essentially have to run the 8400M at full power at all times to make it usable.  Also, at the moment, the X3100 has problems running xv accelerated video under compiz (there are workarounds though) so, that's another thing to consider.  

I would say if you can afford it and power conservation isn't your most important critirea then I'd go with the 8400M.  In reality, both cards have drawbacks and will require some work to get compiz running well, video working, etc.  I would take a few hours and google things like "nvidia 8400 compiz", "X3100 compiz", etc and decide which one is going to be the least painful for you.
