### Post by Astinsan on 2008-04-03
Crossfire is supposed to be supported with ati driver 6.9+ on linux kernel 2.6.18+ for x1000 x1300 x1600 x1800 series. 

The thing that your friend should be told is that this isn't windows.  If this is the setup he/she is going to use and it isn't for 3d modeling or something simalar. Windows games may not work as well with this setup on wine. Most of the calls to directx are supported but not all.
