### Post by LuisGMarine on 2007-05-03
This might be a stab in the dark, but have you tried going to System > Preferences > Keyboard?  Once you are in there mess around with different layouts  under the 'Layouts' tab, and you should find something in there.

I had this problem back when I was using my old HP Laptop, and messing around in there with different layouts seemed to fix the problem.  Also try looking at this, and see if you find anything helpful,

[Ubuntu Guide - Num Lock]("http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_turn_on_Num_Lock_on_GNOME_startup")

Good Luck
