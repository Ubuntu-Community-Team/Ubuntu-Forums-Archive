Previously when I installed Dapper I was able to find the correct (secret) key combination on the internet; this time I cannot locate it.

### Post by Jeff G. on 2007-07-09
Okay, I think the problem is solved.  It was not related to the bios boot order setup as I suspected, but the issue was related to my cd/optical drive.  Apparently on this QSI drive I can copy the ISO image, but it cannot read what it has written.  I even cleaned it, still no success.  Today at work I burned the ISO image from one of our company computers, and now my QSI can read the disk.  I will attempt to install Ubuntu Feisty tonight  and will post the results.

Thank you for reading, and I want to make it known that I will think twice or perhaps thrice before I purchase another Sony laptop.
