### Post by mips on 2006-03-11
The A8N-SLI does NOT support AGP. You need a PCI-E bus type video card.
[http://usa.asus.com/products4.aspx?l1=3&l2=15&l3=148&model=382&modelmenu=1](http://usa.asus.com/products4.aspx?l1=3&l2=15&l3=148&model=382&modelmenu=1)

Rather have a look at one of the following:
[http://www.xfxforce.com/web/product/listConfigurations.jspa?seriesId=42&productId=94](http://www.xfxforce.com/web/product/listConfigurations.jspa?seriesId=42&productId=94)

Only look at those with **Bus Type: [COLOR="Red"]PCI-E[/COLOR]**

The 6600GT give a good price performance ratio. Please note the differences in Memory types
