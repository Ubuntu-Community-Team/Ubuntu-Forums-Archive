### Post by er4z0r on 2006-04-11
Well, at least I could solve my problems with Fn-F7 not working:

[http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Additional_options_for_the_radeon_driver#External_Monitors]("http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Additional_options_for_the_radeon_driver#External_Monitors")
There I found the solution:
```

        # use bios hot keys on thinkpad (aka fn+f7)
        Option          "BIOSHotkeys" "on"

```

Note besides:
This is for the radeon and the ati driver.
I am NOT using fglrx, since its not working for me. Propably my old hardware...
