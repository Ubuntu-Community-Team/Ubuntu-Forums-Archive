### Post by cambie on 2007-08-23
You're going to have to open up the case, find an open IDE channel and molex power connecter, hook them up to the drive, and then find a place inside the case to mount it.

Then, it's a matter of setting up the parition table on the drive, and giving it a filesystem. I'm a command line guy, so I'm sure someone else around here will have great GUI instructions for you.
