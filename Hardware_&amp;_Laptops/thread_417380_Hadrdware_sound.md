### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-04-22
It is the CPU making the sound. My Core 2 Duo T7200 does the same thing if I do something processor intensive. In most cases, it is nothing to worry about. I've heard that it might be the frequency scaling to automatically turn up the speed of the processor and emitting a sound, although I'm not 100% sure.
If the performance of your computer is affected by it, you might want to take it to a hardware center and see if anything is wrong with it. But, it should be normal right now, and nothing should be wrong with the noise.
