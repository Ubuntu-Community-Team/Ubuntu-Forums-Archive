### Post by Howler9443 on 2007-07-03
Ubuntu has support for SMP (Symetrical multiprocessor) turned on in the Linux kernel by default. Four cores should work just fine. If I recall correctly, and this may have been resolved a few years ago, but I think there was a 16CPU limitation at one time...or perhaps not a limitation so much as a bug. I really don't recall.

You should be just fine with a quad-core CPU. Ubuntu sees the CPU and all SPUs on my cousins PS3 as well...so life should be good for you!

Enjoy!
