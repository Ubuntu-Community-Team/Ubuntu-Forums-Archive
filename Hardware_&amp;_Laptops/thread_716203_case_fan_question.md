### Post by Whiffle on 2008-03-05
You can get a wire that has a female molex on one end and two male molexes wired to that, which you could plug your fan into.  I don't see many other choices than that given your situation.

like so:
[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16812183147](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16812183147)


Make sure its not a SATA one, those are different.

### Post by Whiffle on 2008-03-05
Yep.  You're just adding another device to the circuit in parallel.  You woudn't want to add too many devices this way, but fans really don't draw much power at all so it will be okay.
