### Post by derekco on 2007-10-21
I just bought the Epson Stylus Photo RX595 and had some driver issues.  The Linux drivers were not available from Epson (although they were supposed to be).

My workaround was to install the latest version of Gutenprint (a package of Linux drivers).  5.0.1 and up has RX580 and RX590 drivers (works for RX595).  This version of Gutenprint might be available in Gutsy Gibbon by default, but I am not sure as I am still a Dapper user.

For details on the problems I faced and  how I installed the drivers from source, please see my post at [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=3594776#post3594776")

Hope this helps in your decision.

As always...Linuxprinting.org has the best info on what printers work with Linux.
