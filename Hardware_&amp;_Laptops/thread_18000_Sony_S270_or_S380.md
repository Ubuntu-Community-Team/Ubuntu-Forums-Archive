### Post by jliedeka on 2005-03-09
I just got a 270 with the ATI 9200 chip.  Graphics performance wasn't a major factor for me.  I'm running the Xfree driver only.  With a standard battery, I'm guesstimating that I could watch a DVD before running out of juice.  That would be with wireless on.  I would guess one could hope for 2.5 to 3 hours for less power intensive applications.

One thing you might want to know, Sonys have a rep for not being able to play audio CDs under Linux.  Someone suggested that there is no analog connection from the CD-ROM to the sound card.  I was able to play CDs using XMMS with the CD input in digital mode.
