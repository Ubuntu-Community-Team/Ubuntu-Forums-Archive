### Post by mdbarton on 2007-12-13
I also have this laptop.  I use this as my main computer and have been running Ubuntu on it since I bought it early 2007.  I've now installed Gutsy (64bit) and am v. impressed.  Everything works fine with the exception of the card reader and the hardware keys (volume, play, etc).  The webcam works fine with Camorama (although you need good ambient lighting) but does not work with Skype 2.0 Beta (yet).

Got commercial DVDs playing by following these instructions: [http://limulus.wordpress.com/2007/12/10/playing-dvds-in-ubuntu-gutsy/](http://limulus.wordpress.com/2007/12/10/playing-dvds-in-ubuntu-gutsy/) but didn't installed VLC (installed gxine instead).  However, viewing in fullscreen on Totem player and gxine crashes the PC.  I don't really watch DVDs on the laptop (what's home cinema for?) so this isn't a problem for me.

Wireless and 3D graphics worked out of the box with Gutsy.
