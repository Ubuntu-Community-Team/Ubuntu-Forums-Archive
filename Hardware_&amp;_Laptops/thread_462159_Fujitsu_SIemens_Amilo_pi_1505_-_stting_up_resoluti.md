I am a newborn linux user (Ubuntu 7.04 Feitsy Fawn).

I installed Ubuntu on laptop - Fujitsu Simenes Amilo pi 1505.

As many of you I also had some problems with setting the resolution to fit my wide screen.
I found hard to get the right info on how to set this up... so here I am passing the instructions for all you new to Ubuntu...

If you use that laptop, you do not need 915resolution... (!)

edit: ...and hit Return (Enter)

all you need is to open Terminal and type in:

[FONT="Georgia"]sudo apt-get install xserver-xorg-video-intel[/FONT]

After the process is over just restart your computer.

P.S. I tryed to install OpenSUSE 10.2 on this laptop... save your self the time and even trie to do the same :)
