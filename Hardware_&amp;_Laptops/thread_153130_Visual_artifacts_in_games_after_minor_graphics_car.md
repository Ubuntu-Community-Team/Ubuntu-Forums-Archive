### Post by M4av0 on 2006-03-31
Downclock it. I had the same problem with my (now old) ati radeon 9500 pro. But in my case the problem was not gpu clock, but ram clock. Play a little with various clock configs. If you don't see artifacts on your desktop it doesn't mean they won't appear in 3d apps, when a card is more stressed.
