### Post by Kapre on 2005-11-05
[QUOTE=bmbeeman]How do I do that?[/QUOTE]

During the boot-up of your laptop after the POST (Power-On-Self-Test), you either press the Delete Key or F1 (look into your lappy during boot up and look for "Press ___key to enter BIOS setting". Change the boot sequence from A,C to CD-Rom, C).

K
