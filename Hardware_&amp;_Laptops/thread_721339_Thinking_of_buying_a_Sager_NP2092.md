### Post by obinine on 2008-03-26
I just received one of these. The 32bit version of Gutsy installed fairly smoothly but I decided to go for the full enchilada, so I installed the AMD64 version of Gutsy then immediately upgraded to Hardy.

It's taken me a full day and I've definitely had to jump though a few hoops but so far I have pretty much everything I want happening on it. The remaining issues are having sound continue after suspend and that the built-in mics are too quiet. I'm confident I can sort out both of these and it's quite possible that they will be fixed by the time Hardy is released.

Even 32bit apps have installed pretty smoothly (Flashplayer, Skype, Google Earth, etc.) I can definitely recommend this machine for the money. The power is roughly equivalent to a new Macbook Pro for less than half the price.
