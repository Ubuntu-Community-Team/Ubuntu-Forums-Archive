### Post by skoal on 2005-09-09
Well, for compatability, reliability, and featurabiity (<- is that a word?), I would find yourself an older Sound Blaster Live! (pci)

0000:02:0c.0 Multimedia audio controller: Creative Labs SB Live! EMU10k1 (rev 05)

I've been using this little gem in Linux for over 7 years now!  It's got the I/O extension card so I can pipe AC3/Dolby 5.1 to my external receiver/amp.  You just can't get any better for cards which just work "right outta the box" in linux...
