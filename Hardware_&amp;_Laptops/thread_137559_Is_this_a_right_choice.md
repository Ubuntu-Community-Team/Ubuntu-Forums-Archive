### Post by chele on 2006-02-28
A good price for a laptop? Depends on Weight, Battery Life, Screen Resolution.

Personally, I would avoid anything over 5 lbs, under 4 hours, less then 1400x1000.

I have a 1.5 GHz cpu that never uses more the 10%, so the cpu is a none issue. Ram is good though 512MB or more should be fine, unless you are editing HD video.

Don't know if the graphics chip you indicate has good free drivers though. What does xorg say?
