### Post by Crowhurst on 2005-12-04
Fixed it -

Followed the link regarding creating S09startupscript. Just downloading 855resolution from apt-get install did not do it.

I then had to follow the chmod a+x startupscript. I did not do rcconf, i just renamed startupscript to S09startupscript and put it in /etc/rc2.d/

Now it boots into 1280 x 768 mode - I also had added to device Option "videoram" "65536".

var/log/xorg.0.log was very helpful in determining that the system was looking for 1280 mode but there was none available.
