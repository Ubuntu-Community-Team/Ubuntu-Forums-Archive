### Post by Sai-me on 2006-03-14
There are a few threads with how to mount the floppy drive.

I just join to tell others what I just found out without wanting to mount my floppy drive. Okay. Here it goes.

Insert a floppy. Open beep media player. Make sure your playlist is open. Click the add button then the add files... option and double click the floppy drive. It should mount the floopy drive. you should be able to explore you floppy files from there. That how I'm mounting my floppy drive now. ;) 

I'm sorry if this doesn't help you. I mount it even though I don't use it that often. Make sure to do a search and type mount floppy. Lots of threads about that topic.
