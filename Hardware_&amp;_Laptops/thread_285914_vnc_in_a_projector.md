erbedo, what do you mean by "my Xsession and the one projected separated each other" ?

What you basically need to do is to physically connect the VNC client to your projector and project whatever you can normally view in a VNC window (or full screen if you switch to that mode).

You can always switch back & forth between the VNC window and your client's normal X session.
