### Post by apos on 2007-07-12
Hi Tachyon,

unfortunately this problem will stay. I am running Feisty 7.04 and SuSE 10.2 on an IBM TP R31 (same as yours: VGA compatible controller: Intel Corporation 82830 CGC) and 3D is running no way. 

Activating "dri" (direct rendering) in the "/etc/X11/xorg.conf" will freeze the whole system cause the "i810" module of the xserver for this paticular chipset  is very bad.

The DRI feature in "X11" is dead for this type of chipset since xserver version 6.2 or 6.1 (years ago, when I remember that right). 

Probably you can post your "xorg.conf" (device section !), it might be interesting to see, why you obviously can run DRI without freezing your system, even if it won't run with full speed.

Be glad you can activate DRI and run it.
