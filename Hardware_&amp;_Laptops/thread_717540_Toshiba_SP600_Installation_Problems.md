### Post by jonesjm3 on 2008-03-11
In case anyone is interested or experiences similar problems in the future i have found a workaround for this.

I booted into the existing windows install on the laptop (that I didn't want to keep) and downloaded unetbootin and installed ubuntu 7.10 this way. 

This worked perfectly and now have a pretty quick laptop which previously was stuck in the cupboard not being used.
