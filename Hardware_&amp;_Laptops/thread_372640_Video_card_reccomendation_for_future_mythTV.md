Any one of those would be awesome.  I use a 5200 128M pci (non-express) and it works fine for the display.  If you don't mind proprietary drivers, nvidia cards are definitely very easy to make work.

Another option is to get a pvr-250 or 350 which I believe include a tv-out feature, and Im sure myth tv supports it pretty well.   My specs for my myth box are:

a hauppage pvr-150, p4 2.8ghz, 1gb of ddr400, and 6 hard drives in linux software raid, and a streamzap usb remote.

my setup is pretty snappy most of the time, but when doing multiple tasks like recording while watching something else can make it a little sluggish at times.  It does improve as mythtv gets better and better however.  

GL w/ myth :)  its awesome.
