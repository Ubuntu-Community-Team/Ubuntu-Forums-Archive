### Post by dave_euser on 2004-12-28
This isn't really a question related to Ubuntu....but most laptops have a key that you can press that will activate the standard RGB/VGA output on the rear of the laptop. This is the best way to connect to a projector....you should not have to enable anything in linux to get this functionality (the video bridge should be able to be activated externally).

If this doesn't work, you have two options: post here what video card you have, etc., or you can try turning off your laptop and starting with the project connected and turned on. Usually the computer will activate the VGA port if it detects something attached at power-on.

Good luck!
