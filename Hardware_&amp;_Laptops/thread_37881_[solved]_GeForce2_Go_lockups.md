### Post by usergentoo on 2005-05-29
For over a year now Ive been looking for a fix for my laptop Gateway Solo 9550 GeForce2 Go 32mb PIII 1.2ghz.

I had the hardlockup when trying to get the nvidia drivers running.. After trying everything I seen where people where having luck hooking up their monitor to the laptop, and it worked. So I tried it. No luck but I went into the bios and found the setting for crt enabled that, rebooted and it worked, only if your using the crt. Now Im making headway after messing with that awhile, I give up. So rebooted and forgot to disable crt view in my bios. Going back into my bios I found lcd and crt view had both listed. So I tried that and to my surprise it worked with that enabled and with no extrenal crt hooked up to it. Ive tried several livecd from different distros and they also work. I DID NOT touch ANY config files. It worked out of the box. All the live cds picked it up and it ran flawless. I tried out severl opengl apps and they worked very good as well.

The only thing I can think was when I using my laptop testing monitors about a year ago and changed the setting. Around the same time I updated gentoo and thats when I started having the complete lockups.

I hope this can maybe help some of you.

I talked to my friend and he has a Dell with the GeForce2 Go also and have had the same problems.I tols him how I fixed mine and he checked his bios and had a similar setting and that fixed his as well.

If this helps any post the brand of laptop. That way others will know as well.
