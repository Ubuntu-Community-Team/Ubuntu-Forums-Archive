### Post by MrElephant on 2006-12-09
I have a T40, and while yes, you can do this, I am looking for a better way.  I have  a mini-dock, not a port replicator, so this might not work.

It seems that in 6.10, the thinkpad volume buttons change the speaker volume as well as the system volume.  So, if you hit the volume down button until it is silent, then go put your system volume back up, the thinkpad's speakers will be off, while the output through the port replicator should be just fine.

I would prefer that the volume buttons did not affect the system volume, but I only just installed 6.10 yesterday and haven't gotten around to figuring out how to disable that yet.

Hope this helps.
MrE

### Post by MrElephant on 2006-12-11
I wound up uninstalling a package called hotkey-setup.  The only thing it seemed to do for me was cause my volume keys to control the system volume, which I do not want.  This way, I can easily have speakers plugged in without any sound coming from the laptop itself.

Woo.
