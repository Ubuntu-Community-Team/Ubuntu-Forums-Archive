### Post by frantic on 2006-06-30
Can somebody help me to set up wifi and card reader on Acer Aspire 5030? Thanks.

By the way, in order to have proper shutdowns and restarts on this laptop (without hangs mentioned in various posts) you have to boot Ubuntu 6.06 with acpi=force argument. If you use acpi=off, then you'll be able to boot the system, but shutdown and restart will not work (computer will hang at the end of shutdown process).
