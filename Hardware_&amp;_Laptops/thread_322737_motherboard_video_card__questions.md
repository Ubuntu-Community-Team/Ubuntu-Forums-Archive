### Post by tommcd on 2006-12-21
Here is a guide to upgrading from onboard video to an nvidia card:
[http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Nvidia_Intel_Integrated](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Nvidia_Intel_Integrated)
Get an nvidia card, better driver support than ATI.
For a socket 754 motherboard, I have this one:
[http://www.amazon.co.uk/ASUS-K8N-Motherboard-Ethernet-8-channel/dp/B0006IGKWI](http://www.amazon.co.uk/ASUS-K8N-Motherboard-Ethernet-8-channel/dp/B0006IGKWI)
Everything works out of the box. Motherboard has no performance or stability issues at all. It has an AGP slot though, not PCI-express.
Check your motherboard manual, you may need to switch the video from onboard to PCI-express in the BIOS.
