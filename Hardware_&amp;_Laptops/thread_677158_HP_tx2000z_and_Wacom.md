### Post by TorchlightJay on 2008-02-15
I got everything working but the Wacom. I found the reason why the wacom won't work of course. This wacom device is not supported because it is a USB device. Currently there is no Linux support for USB based Wacom Devices on Tablet PCs. No worries, it appears that they are already working on it so hold on tight boys and girls, we'll see full linux wacom support on this device soon.

All that aside, everything else works like a charm it seems.
