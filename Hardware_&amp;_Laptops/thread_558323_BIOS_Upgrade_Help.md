### Post by geraldm on 2007-09-23
Flashing a new BIOS is quite special.  On some computers, if you start, and fail to finish,
the computer becomes unbootable.  If I were you, I would take your situation to
customer service at Toshiba and ask them about a better tool to work with.
They may have a way to use available ram, or use a cd/dvd drive for the upgrade file.

Gerald
