### Post by az on 2006-01-19
The number on the chip is just probably the model of the eprom and not the bios version.

You really need to use the bios for your motherboard.

When you boot, try to jot down some of the numbers displayed - usually at the bottom.  Google those numbers and your manufacturer name and try to find a current bios.
If you flash the bios from another motherboard, you will not be able to boot, not will you be able the flash it back!
