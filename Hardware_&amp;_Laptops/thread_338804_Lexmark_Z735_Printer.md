### Post by blackened on 2007-01-15
I have an Officejet 7210 All-in-one and I love it. Granted it's the first HP I've ever owned, but everything worked out of the box with Ubuntu (including the card reader). You can find full lists of compatible printers at [http://www.freestandards.org/en/OpenPrinting]("http://www.freestandards.org/en/OpenPrinting").
Specifically compatible HP printers are at [http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=HP]("http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=HP").

Since you said you don't need an All-In-One, then you could go for a dedicated printer from one of those lists and I'm sure it would work just as well, and be much more economical.
