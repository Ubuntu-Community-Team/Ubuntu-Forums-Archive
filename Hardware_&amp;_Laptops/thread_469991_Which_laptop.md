### Post by silkstone on 2007-06-10
I bought an Acer 5612WLMI last year from Laptops Direct. It has T2300 Core-2-Duo, 1GB RAM, 100GB disk and - whoopee! - an Nvidia GForce Go 7300. Screen is 15.4" 1280 x 800, and that seems fine as far as text size etc is concerned.

The graphics card is nothing special and doesn't have much onboard memory, but Feisty installed with no problems and it recognised both the graphics and the wifi cards. I don't use the Desktop Effects but I have tried them and they work fine.

This model will be superseded now, but it may be worth checking the current Acer range. The prices are pretty good and the quality is decent.
