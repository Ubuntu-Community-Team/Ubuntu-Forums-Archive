### Post by laidback on 2007-03-14
I've cloned my hd and replaced my current hd with the cloned version, it worked. I've cloned my hd and put it into another pc, it worked. I'm using Dapper, IDE drives, Athlon XP 1800 and Duron 1300. Both older style machines, socket A's. I have no experience of Raid or LVM. But I have read another thread where the correspondent quoted success with Raid. It was somewhere in the General forum. I say backup everything and give it a go. Have you anything to loose?
I have to say that I didn't expect my clones to work, especially when I moved the hd to another pc, which had different ram, cpu, video etc. But it all worked, including the web and email. Fantastic, don't try this with Windoze!!
