### Post by jml on 2005-12-13
I'm not at my Linux box so this is from memory.  Go into Administration--> System--> Netwoking.  You will be asked for your password.  Type it in.  Select your wireless card by clicking on it.  Then click on activate.  It may take several minutes, but you should get a dialog box that says something to the effect that "device activated"  Click on OK, then close out of the networking application.  You should be good to go.
