Hello,

I have a Nec versa Note notebook, and sound did not work  with Unbuntu 5.04 Hoary, but it worked fine with Warty 4.10.

The sound card on my laptop is an ESS Solo 1 ( es1938 ).
When ubuntu start, I got an error like :
Invalid card number
amixer <option> ... and --help of amixer.

After reading and trying lot of solutions, I found why sound doesn't work ...  :grin: 

Just disable power saving in the BIOS of notebook an the sound will work fine.
