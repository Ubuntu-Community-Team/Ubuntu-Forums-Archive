### Post by Tsen on 2007-06-29
Just don't buy an ATI, they have horrible Linux support.  And buy online, you'll save yourself tons of cash.  An Nvidia FX5500 (a mediocre card) is $80 at the Wal Mart here, but online it goes for $35.

What I'd do is look at the games you're likely to want to play soon, find their graphics requirements, then go buy a card that's slightly better than the recommended.  That way you're set for games in a year or two, but you won't blow your budged on it.
