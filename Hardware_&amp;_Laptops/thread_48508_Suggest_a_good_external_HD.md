### Post by dave9191 on 2005-07-12
Any external harddrive should work with ubuntu. I picked up a 200GB Lacie usb external drive as a backup for serveral machine. Works likes a chram with windows and ubuntu. plug it in, flick the switch and away you go. Its  resonable in size and looks good :) Its a desktop sized one, so it needs a seperate power supply. you might want to get a laptop sized one if you want to take it with you to places. Laptop ones run of the usb power supply, but they are slower. 

I picked Lacie because it had the best reviews, other external drives I was looking at had people complaining about them failing.
