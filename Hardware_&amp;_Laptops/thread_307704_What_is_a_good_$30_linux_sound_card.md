### Post by Peepsalot on 2006-11-27
I think you can find a Sound Blaster Live! 5.1 for around this price.  It is what I use.  Most of the older sound blasters are supported very well in Linux as far as I know.
Can't say the same for the latest hardware.  I recently made the mistake of buying the latest X-Fi sound card from Creative Labs, only to find out there are no drivers for it.
