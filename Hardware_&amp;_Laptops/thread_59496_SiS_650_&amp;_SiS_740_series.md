### Post by s_p_a_r_k_y on 2005-08-24
you need to edit your xorg.conf file in /etc/X11/ to use the sis video driver.

Look for this
Section "Device"
and edit the Driver line to be 
Driver "sis"
save your work
and from the command line type sudo /etc/init.d/gdm restart

if that doesn't work and you don't have X anymore change it back to what you had in there. What exactly is the problem, you just say you don't have the "drivers" but can you get into graphical?
