### Post by peterx14 on 2007-06-29
I've just installed a Wacom Volito 2 on a fresh Feisty (7.04) installation and had the same issue. Despite what [the wacom community page](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Wacom#fnref-de5d43802e5d3359778eb5d3ea0e7eea32bcfb51-1) says at the start, you do need to tweak your xorg.conf file so that references to /dev/wacom are changed to /dev/input/wacom so follow the notes on the linked page anyway!
