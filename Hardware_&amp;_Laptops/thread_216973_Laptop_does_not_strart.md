### Post by bluecherry on 2006-07-16
I don't know a solution to your problem when it comes to fixing "sleeping-mode" because on my laptop it never worked.

But I can help you to prevent your laptop from going into this mode, because that's what it will do to save power once you don't touch it for x mins.

First of all, if you ever have this problem again you can always restart your laptop the 'cold' way. Removing the battery for a couple of seconds will REALLY shut the system down (when you remove the power supply the battery takes over and nothing happens!).

Solution: 
Right-click on the battery icon in the notification area and select Preferences.

First tab: "Running in AC"
1. Label "Put computer to sleep when it's inactive for:"
Move the bar completely to the right, it will say "never".

2. Label "When laptop lid is closed:" select "Blank screen"

Second tab:"Running on battery":
1. & 2. 
3. Label "When battery power is critical:" select "Shutdown"

Third tab:"General":
1. "Sleep type when inactive:" select "Do nothing"
2. "Sleep button action:" select "Blank screen"

That should prevent you from ever getting your laptop in sleep-mode.

Hope this helped.
