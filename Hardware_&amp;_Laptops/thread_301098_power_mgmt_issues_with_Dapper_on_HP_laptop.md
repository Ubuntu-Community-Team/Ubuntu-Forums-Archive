### Post by igeterrors on 2006-11-19
Here is a way I discovered to work around (not solve) this problem.  In power management preferences, on the "running on battery" tab, I had "suspend" as my choice for "when laptop lid is closed" -- because that was what I wanted.  So if I change that to "do nothing", I can remove the power cord without the computer going into suspend.  

I guess Ubuntu thinks that the power cord coming out is the same as the laptop lid being closed.  Now if only I could get my computer to suspend under battery power when I close the lid.  I suppose I could do it manually before closing the lid...
