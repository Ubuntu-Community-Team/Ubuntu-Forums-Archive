### Post by mgmiller on 2006-10-02
I have not had any real sound hardware detection problems with Ubuntu.  The only times I found that sound was not working "out of the box", the only thing wrong was the mute button was on for some reason.  You should have a small speaker icon in your top toolbar.  If you right click it, you will see the mute option.  Try clicking that and see if your sound starts working.  Also check that all the volume sliders are up.
