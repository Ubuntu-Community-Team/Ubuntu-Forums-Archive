### Post by coffeecat on 2006-12-25
You would help yourself if you were to state which version of Ubuntu you are using and also say what hardware you are using. This means that other members can give relevant replies. You can set the Ubuntu version in your user profile, and knowing what make/model of laptop you are using, owners of similar ones can tell you their experience.

That being said, if you are using Ubuntu Edgy (6.10), there is a well-known bug which affects touchpad scrolling in Edgy but not in Dapper. Search these forums using the words 'edgy' 'touchpad' and 'scrolling' for more details and a somewhat unsatisfactory workaround which may or may not affect touch-tapping depending on your laptop. You could try 'synaptics' instead of 'touchpad' - it may throw up different threads.
