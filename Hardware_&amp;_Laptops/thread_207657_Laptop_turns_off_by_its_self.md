### Post by WhiteStool on 2006-07-02
I had this same problem. My ram slot (DIMM slot B for me) was bad. Try moving around your ram, and if you have more than one card try taking one out. I had to buy new ram, but my computer worked.

This may not be your problem, but it very well could be.

### Post by gmertr on 2006-07-03
I had a similar problem. The issue was over-heating. I first vacumed as much dust as I could, and then took a leaf blower and blew out the dust. That fixed it for me.
