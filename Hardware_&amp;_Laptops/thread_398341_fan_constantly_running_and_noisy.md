I don't believe that installing Edgy on your computer has anything to do with your fan running constantly.

You should be able to adjust the fan speeds in the BIOS.  My fans are set to start at 25C and go to full at 50C.  My CPU fan goes to full at 60C.

If you set temps similar to these and it still runs full tilt, then you should assure that you have good air-flow.  Good air-flow is very necessary to help your fans function correctly.

Since the computer is new, you may want to have a tech check to see that the fan is not faulty.
