### Post by adpatter on 2007-10-06
Information about the type of wireless card can be found in the output of dmesg.

Open a terminal window.

Type the following:

dmesg

See [http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Category:X40](http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Category:X40).

After following directions linked to the above website address the wireless card in my X40 functions.
