**No CD player access on Toshiba A200 with 2.6.22.9 kernel !**
If you are the happy possessor of a Toshiba A200, beware ! If you want to upgrade your kernel from 2.6.20 (Feisty) to 2.6.22.9 generic, you will have problem with your DVC/CD player which acquire an erratic behavior and doesn't write CD or DVDs at all. I hope this bug will be corrected in Gutsy... I deleted this kernel after the upgrade.( I hoped to solve my hibernation and webcam problems).
