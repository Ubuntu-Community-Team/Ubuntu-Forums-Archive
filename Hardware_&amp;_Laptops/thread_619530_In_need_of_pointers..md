### Post by colo on 2007-11-21
Last time I checked (~1 month ago) there weren't any VDR-compatible PCI-Express based tv-cards whatsoever. Can't tell about the PCI card, thogh - check Linuxtv.org's hardware compatibilitylists and the Wiki over there.

For the new graphics card, there are numerous models to choose from. Free drivers are in development right now, and will be kicking in a few weeks to month.
