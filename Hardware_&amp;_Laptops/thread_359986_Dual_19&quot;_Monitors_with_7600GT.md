### Post by daou on 2007-02-13
I have an XFX 6600 GT, dual DVI, that is rated at 2048X1536. I'm running two 1280x1024 screens, so that would be 2560x1024. So the horizontal res is higher than the supported one, but max pixels is less than what is supported. And it works fine.

The same might apply to your case. I can't say for sure.

So now I have a 22" + 19" with 1680x1050 + 1280x1024 resolutions (both DVI). Which totals to about 2960x1050. And this is with my 6600 GT (2048X1536). I asked one of the workers if it will work.
