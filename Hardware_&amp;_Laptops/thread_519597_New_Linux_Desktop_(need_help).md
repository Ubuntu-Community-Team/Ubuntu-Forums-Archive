### Post by dabl on 2007-08-07
My only concern would be that motherboard -- I seem to recall there have been some issues with the integrated Nvidia video system, when a second video card is installed.  If you could find a motherboard that has a decent integrated sound system, but no integrated video, you would probably lower the risk of some kind of conflict to be resolved.  You might want to search this forum or google for video conflicts on motherboards that have an integrated video system, when the user added a graphics card. :)
