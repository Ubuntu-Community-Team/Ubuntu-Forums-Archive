### Post by Bigbluecat on 2007-07-17
This is part of Intel's Menlow platform push using their new Silverthorne processor. Menlow was announced at the April Intel IDF and is designed to bring mobile devices smaller than UMPC's but larger than PDA's running a full OS.

As th OP said the devices will be similar to Nokia N800 but manufactured by many companies each with their own slightly different take on the platform.

Interesting to watch how this develops
