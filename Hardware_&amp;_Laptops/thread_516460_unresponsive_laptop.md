### Post by pbcartwright on 2007-08-03
my laptop was taking 15 seconds to get to the OS boot selection screen ( grub). I went into the BIOS and found that it had the floppy drive selected as a choice for bootup. I don't have a floppy drive, so it took 15 seconds to time out.
if you select HD as the first choice it may start faster, or at least look at the options, and also look at  the POST ( power up self test) options, it may be set to thorough.
