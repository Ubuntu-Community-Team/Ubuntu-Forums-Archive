I just want to inform everyone that I successfully installed EDUBUNTU 6.06LTS on my very old HYUNDAI LIBERO SLIM laptop.  I was just curious about how Edubuntu would behave on a very low-end laptop.

Specs? Intel Celeron 433 Mhz, 128MB RAM, 7GB HDD, 24X CD-ROM drive

Here are my observations:

GOOD POINTS:
1. Well, Edubuntu successfully installed itself.
2. I was able to run the applications included in the distribution without hassles.

BAD POINTS:
1. The installation went on for 2 hours...God it was slow!

So far, those are my observations.
