### Post by taurus on 2005-11-17
Is your network printer a static IP or a dynamic IP?  You can configure it using System --> Printer.  Then click on "Add Printer" option.  You should choose the second option from that window, Network, and pick LPR from the drop down menu.  Then, type in the IP address of your printer and lp in the second field...
