### Post by reacocard on 2008-02-19
if it was just a cheap ($5-$10) cord, then you have a cord that requires your computer to support embedded TV-out in your VGA port. Not many systems have this however. Check your system manual or tech support to find out if it does. If it does not, You'll have to buy a more-expensive ($60+) VGA to SVideo converter.
