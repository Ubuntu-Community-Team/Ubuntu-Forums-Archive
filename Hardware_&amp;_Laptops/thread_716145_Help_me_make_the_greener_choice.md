### Post by morticul on 2008-03-06
heheh cool thinking ;)

From my point of view, the greenest solution is to stick with the computer you already have.  (building a computer in itself consume a big amount of power and other ressources). As for choosing between a p4 and p3, it might be tricky. A p3 might consume less power but will take longer to accomplish a task. On the other hand, your cpu might be idle most of the time. To my knowledge the cpu that consume the less amount of energy per gigaflops is the cell processor of the playstation 3. 

To be accurate, you could order this small device :
[http://www.thinkgeek.com/gadgets/travelpower/7657/](http://www.thinkgeek.com/gadgets/travelpower/7657/)
and evaluate the full power consumption of each computer for a specific task. 

To save further energy, you can play with the acpi, to put some cpu throttling (if available), close your screen as soon as possible, lower the brightness....  

My server is an old P4 1.8 GHz with 1GB of ram and it is doing a really good job. However, for reason I don't understand, firefox is damn slow and make web browsing really painful.
