### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-01-13
[http://cups.org/ppd.php](http://cups.org/ppd.php)

HP printers are well-supported but quality level of newer printers is generally poor.  Your best bet is to do a google search and look at customer reviews.  Choose the printer that has the least problems, greatest customer satisfaction and also has linux drivers.

That list is small.
