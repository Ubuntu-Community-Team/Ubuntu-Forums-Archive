Just to make it clear, 'win modem' is a modem that doesn't actually work itself, but uses computers CPU to do part of it's job. Most cheap modems sold are like that, as new computers have enough prosessing power. But these modem's are no good without their windows drivers.

You can also buy 'real' modem, that really has all hardware it needs to eork, and so it doesn't depend on windows doing part of the job. Thes modems often cost a bit more.
