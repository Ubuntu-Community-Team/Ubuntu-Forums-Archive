### Post by John Bennett on 2007-09-28
I'm currently researching a similar box.

I don't want the expense of building a system that can play games.  I just want an Ubuntu box to sit next to my recliner so I can check email and surf the web.

I want it to be small, quiet, energy efficient   ...and cheap.

Unfortunately,  most forums where one could acquire system configurating advice are populated by gamers.  They are not interested in discussing low-end systems.

I'm thinking:

* MicroATX motherboard
* Integrated Nvidia graphics
* Some type of small Shuttle-style case
* cheap harddrive, 1/2 a gig of RAM
* LCD monitor with itegrated speakers on a microphone stand next to my recliner.

Problems:

- small cases are more expensive than mid-towers
- integrated Nvidia graphics mATX mobos are few and far between
- newegg and zipzoom fly don't keep older, cheaper tech in stock long
