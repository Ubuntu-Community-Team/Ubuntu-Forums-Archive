### Post by daveshields on 2007-08-21
I'm running a Leadtek TV2000XP/32510072 PCI Interface TV/FM Tuner Card on Ubuntu 7.04 desktop using kdetv. I haven't tried it with MythTV. I just use it to watch cable TV broadcasts.


Sound didn't work right off the bat, so I plugged a separate speaker into the audio output on the card.

I bought this card three years ago so it may not be available now. You can probably find a newer card that works via [http://newegg.com]("http://newegg.com") . Search on "TV tuner cards." Read the customer comments and look for mention of Linux, Ubuntu or Debian.
