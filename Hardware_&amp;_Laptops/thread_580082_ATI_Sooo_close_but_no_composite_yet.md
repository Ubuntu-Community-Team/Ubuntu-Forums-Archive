### Post by UCAP on 2007-11-03
I have to same GPU in my laptop and received the same error messages. In the end I decided to use an older fglrx driver instead of the brand new one - and low and behold - it worked.

I use the 8.40.4 right now which you can easily install by installing ENVY first ([http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html](http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html)). Once ENVY is installed you can opt to "install ATI driver manually". Pick "8.40.4" and you should be good to go. Of course xserver_xgl needs to be installed as well.
