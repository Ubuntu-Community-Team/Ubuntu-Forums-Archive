### Post by taurus on 2007-10-25
I bet you will get an error message about your ntfs filesystem doesn't shutdown down cleanly when you try to mount it by hand!

```
sudo mkdir /media/sdb1
sudo mount -t ntfs /dev/sdb1 /media/sdb1
```
