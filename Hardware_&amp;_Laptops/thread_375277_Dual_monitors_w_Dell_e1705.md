### Post by zoink on 2007-03-03
Well I finally got both monitors working with KDE, it was easy with gnome...but a pain with KDE.  I was using my xorg.conf that was working in gnome when I switched to KDE, both displays would work on the login screen, but as soon as I typed in my username and password the external screen would shut off. (I'm using a laptop + LCD via DVI).  Basically what I did is played around with the options in the "Monitor & Display" config until I got them both to turn on.  Then I fiddled with my xorg.conf until I got my resolutions correct.  Now the only problems are that windows will initially open up in my external monitor but I want them to open up in my default display....and I think the refresh rate is set incorrectly  because when  I play videos and the camera pans faster than a certain rate there will be a noticeable horizontal line in the video and there will be some delay between the top and bottom half (this only happens on the external display).  Also when I try to get into my "Monitor & Display" settings it says 

"The module Monitor & Display could not be loaded
Possible Reasons
-An error occurred during your last KDE upgrade leaving an orphaned control module
-You have old third party modules lying around.

 also, after I login and it shows the little splash loading screen, the backgrounds on the ions are black...it wasn't like that before I got both monitors working.
