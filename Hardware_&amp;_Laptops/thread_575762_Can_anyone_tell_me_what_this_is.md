The item in question is a 2.5" hard drive caddy. Ussally used to hold the hard drive in place when installed in a laptop. 

You would place the hdd in it in the the caddy in the proper position so that the rear of the hdd connector lines up with the laptops internal connector.

Also most oem drives will ship bare with out any mounting device. The end user usually can reuse the caddy. Unless it's missing from the laptop in which case they are left to search one out.
