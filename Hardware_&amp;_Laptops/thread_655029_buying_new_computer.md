### Post by intel on 2008-01-02
For ubuntucompiz/vistaAero RS690/X1250 is MORE that enuff !,

if ur getting 1GB DDR2 1 stick, it would be better to get 2 sticks of DDR2 512MB,
and put that in dual channel configuration, much faster than 1 stick of 1 GB.

on a side note, RAM is cheaper than grafix memory, & grafix memory is faster than RAM for grafix applications.

If you want to know about performance of RS690/X1250, you may browse the following site [https://groups.google.com/group/x1250](https://groups.google.com/group/x1250)

If you are not currently gaming heavily, onboard 690 is more than enuff,
if you decide to start heavy gaming later, you may buy a better gfx card at that point in time.

Asus Vs Gigabyte with regards to RS690/X1250
Gigabyte should be cheaper(consumers pay a premium for ASUS brand),
Plus gigabyte offers some cool overclocking options(few, better than none at all).
