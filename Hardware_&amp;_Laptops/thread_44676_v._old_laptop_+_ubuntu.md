### Post by Frogger on 2005-06-27
[QUOTE=wdso]Have you tried Damn Small Linux? Very fast indeed![/QUOTE]
 Some words of experience, I have a Moblie Pentium 266 w/ 32megs running slackware.
I kept getting a critical temp error Ubuntu's install, disabling ACPI stopped that but I had other problems.
With 32megs do not, I repeat, DO NOT run KDE, it will take hours (1 1/4 on my machine) to initailize and even moving the mouse will be jerky.  I run gnome (and like it, sorry KDE folks).
