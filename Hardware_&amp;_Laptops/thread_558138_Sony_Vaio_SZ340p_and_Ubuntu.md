### Post by l33g10n on 2007-09-28
just installed 7.04 on my sz340p, and so far so good. using about 2.4 gb of hdd space, wifi is working, function keys are working, all video drivers are installed (had to connect to the net to get the nvidia one, and it was automatic). even my wireless mouse worked off the bat. when i upgraded to vista i had to d/l most of my drivers, ubuntu only needed to d/l one. i'm a very pleased to far, but i've got about 45 minutes of experience with linux, so we'll see what happens in the days to come.

my opinion, go for it. it was a pain on my toshiba notebook pc, but it's been an easy transition on my vaio. i have a 2gig mem upgrade coming via newegg next week early, so i'm interested to see how ubuntu reacts to it.

have fun and good luck, hope this post helps!
