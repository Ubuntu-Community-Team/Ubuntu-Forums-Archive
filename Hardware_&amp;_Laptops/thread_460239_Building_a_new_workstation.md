### Post by rufius on 2007-05-31
Well under the assumption you're at a roughly 800 dollar budget this is what I recommend:

Mobo: [Asus P5LD2](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16813131048) ~ $85
Processor: [Intel Core 2 Duo E6420](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16819115016) ~ $186
Memory: [Corsair ValueSelect 1GB 240 pin DDR2](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16820146580) ~ $60 (2 x $30)
Sound Recording Card: [M-Audio Delta 1010](http://wwwmusiciansfriend.com/product/MAudio-Delta-1010-LT-PCI-Digital-Audio-System?sku=701376) ~ $200
Harddrives
     - Primary: [http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16822136054]Western Digital Rapter WD360ADFD - 36GB @ 10000 rpm](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16822136054]Western Digital Rapter WD360ADFD - 36GB @ 10000 rpm) ~ $100
     - Secondary: [Western Digital WD1600AAJS - 160GB @ 7200 rpm](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16822136075) ~ $53
Video Card: [EVGA GeForce 7300GS 256MB](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814130270) ~ $54
Power Supply: [Rosewill Stallion PSU](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16817182044) ~ $50
Case: [Rosewill Case](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16811147063) ~ $18

Grand total: ~ $806 + shipping, keyboard/mouse/monitor, etc.

You'll notice the most expensive thing was the sound card which is the most important part. You don't want to skimp on your recording card, you want to make sure you take advantage of it. The reason I put the WD Rapter for the primary drive is it will improve performance of the box since its 10k instead of 7200 RPMs, this can greatly improve the quality of the recording because the drive will be able to read/write faster overall. Another suggestion is to NEVER skimp on your power supply, its very important that you get a good quality one. This link is a good start for building this kind of workstation, but this is the budget setup I've made for you:
[http://www.newsforge.com/article.pl?sid=04/10/20/1418231](http://www.newsforge.com/article.pl?sid=04/10/20/1418231)

Hope this helps :)
