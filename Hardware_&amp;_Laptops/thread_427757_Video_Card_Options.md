### Post by mrpaco on 2007-04-30
This doesn't relate specifically to ThinkPads, but I have a Sony with an Intel GMA950.  3D worked out  of the box and Beryl worked fantastically (burn effect FTW!).  My mom has a Gateway with a Mobility Radeon 200M.  Getting 3D to work was an absolute chore (as it is with all ATI cards in my experience), and Beryl ran like garbage (Compiz ran fine though).  Long story short - go with the Intel.
