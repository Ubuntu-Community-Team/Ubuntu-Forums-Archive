### Post by reckless2k2 on 2008-01-08
512MB will be fine for Ubuntu. 

as far as transition tips, try to make it look similar if not just like windows (the desktop) and put icons they need or use right on their desktop. you can rename them to EMAIL, WEB, etc... things like that to make it easy. i've actually converted more than a handful of people and find it easy to make it very friendly like that. because linux is so customizable, it's very easy to do.
