Hi, 

If you are having trouble getting the Alcatel speedtouch USB modem working with Dapper, you may find it useful to do the following:

**sudo cp speedtch* /lib/firmware/**

where "speedtch*" are your firmware files. This is different from previous versions of Ubuntu.
