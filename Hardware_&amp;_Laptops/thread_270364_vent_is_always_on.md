### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-10-04
Do you mean that your fan is constantly running?  If so, and on certain processors and especially on laptops, you want the fan running as much as possible to keep your system cool -- laptops are notorious for overheating...just stay plugged in and you won't need to worry about the fan draining power from your system.  If you think your fan sounds too loud, there may be programs out there (I'm sure there are) that can slow it down, or power-manage it.  Check your power settings and monitor settings to see if there is an option that's built-in.
