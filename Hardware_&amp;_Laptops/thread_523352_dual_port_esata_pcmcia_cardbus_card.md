### Post by lamborghiniwang on 2007-08-28
Just to report that the Rosewill RC-604 PCMCIA eSATA card works fine in my Feisty/Windows XP   Thinkpad T60. Feisty recognize the card without any problem. 
I had some problem when using gparted to devide my external HDD into two partitions (one FAT32 and one EXT3 partition). I don't know if I did something wrong. But I just boot into Windows, and used Windows to do the partition part, and then reboot into Ubuntu to format the HDDs. After that, it seems everything works fine. The external HDD is recognized as /dev/sdc (I have two internal HDDs installed in my T60 as /dev/sda and /dev/sdb).
