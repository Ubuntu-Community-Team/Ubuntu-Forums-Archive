### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-05-18
Intel based laptops seem to have good support. Nvidia cards are said to work better then ATI, and stay away from broadcom wireless. Intel wireless cards, such as pro/wireless 3945 ABG or similar, work out of the box in most cases.

### Post by leona on 2007-05-18
you got a budget in mind?
For example, I have 7.04FF running on an old IBM Thinkpad 600x and it runs ok ,not lightening quick, but ok for net surfing, its a  P3-500mhz has 256 Mb Ram and a 20gb h/d. :)
