### Post by Ocxic on 2006-07-22
dude, just run "sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg" after pluging in your new monitor, (X11 will prolly give an error durin boot and drop to a shell) and it will go through all of you video options.
Change what needs to be changed, save and reboot.
Pluging your monitor in befor hand should allow "sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg" to detect the new monitor


Be sure to backup you current xorg.conf
