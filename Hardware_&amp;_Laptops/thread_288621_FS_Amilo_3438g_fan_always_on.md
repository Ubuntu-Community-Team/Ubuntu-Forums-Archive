### Post by Z(L)o on 2006-12-06
I had the same type of problem with FS Amilo VS 2055. I was searching around a lot, but what did the trick for me (and for Amilo) is changing the BIOS setting for fan. I don't remember the exact setting, but it should read something like TEMP CONTROL - HIGH NORMAL. changing it to normal did the trick - no more CONSTANT fan noise.
hope it helps
