### Post by sawjew on 2006-09-10
I have an Evo N400C which had 128M of ram.  I added a further 256M and rebooted no problem, everything was recognised and just worked.  I am running Xubuntu Dapper and dual booting with Windows 2000 and Xubuntu just flies compared to Win2000.

I have heard that the old Compaq laptops are very touchy with ram and only like to use genuine Compaq or HP ram.  The ram I bought isn't genuine but it was guaranteed to work with this model and a bit dearer than the usual generic ram.

I have had no real problems with this laptop except the battery.  My old battery wouldn't last for more than 10 minutes any more so I bought a new one and it's no better.  Have you had any problems in this area?
