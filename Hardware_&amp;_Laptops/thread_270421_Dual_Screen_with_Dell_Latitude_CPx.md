### Post by MrPedagogy on 2006-10-03
Try your function buttons with the function key held down and then pressing F4 or on some notebooks, F8. You may have to press it two or three times until the dual monitors work. It effectively forces the signal to the second monitor. 

I use this daily to force the signal to a data projector. I have to hold down function and press F4 three times and it set to both the notbook and the projector.

Hope this helps.
