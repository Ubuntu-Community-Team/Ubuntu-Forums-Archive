### Post by dubrict on 2007-04-26
I have an asus motherboard with an nvidia chipset, two nvidia graphics cards, an amd athlon 64 processor, a sound blaster audigy 2 sound card, and a serial ATA hard drive.  (nForce controller.)

Hardware detection and stability have been absolutely flawless in every distro I've installed.
