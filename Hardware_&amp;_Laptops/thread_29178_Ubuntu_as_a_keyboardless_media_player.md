### Post by andvaranaut on 2005-04-23
Hi Owen,

Ubuntu will probably let you into Gnome with no problem. There is an option in the display manager (the login screen appearing when your PC has booted) to automatically log in a given user. You can use that to make Gnome start automatically without your intervention.

As for the keyboard, the Gnome accessibility does have an on-screen keyboard. It's called **gok**. So I think you are in good shape ;)
