### Post by pentium on 2006-09-04
I have had a problem like this before. I was ready to burn a cd and when the drive began burning the entire system shut down hard. If this is a custom, you probably got the wrong power supply and when the drive begins burning the power consumption skyrockets and overloads the power supply. Double check that all your current draw adds up to below the PSU's max output. don't forget that usb and firewire also draws power.
