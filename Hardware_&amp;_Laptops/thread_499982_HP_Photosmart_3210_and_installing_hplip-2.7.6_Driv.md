If there are any faults I can see with running Ubuntu it would be the lack of instructions for us Newbies and trying to do things. So hopefully some where down the road leaving this post will help another Ubuntu Newbie whom is trying to break free of the Microsoft clutches.

# 1

Download driver hplip-2.7.6

Go to [URL="http://hplip.sourceforge.net/"]Sourceforge
[/URL]

# 2

Start download

Just click on the [COLOR="Blue"]blue [/COLOR]download button found just down a bit and right of centre page

Followed by [COLOR="Blue"][SIZE="4"]Download the self-extracting Archive With installer[/SIZE][/COLOR]

# 3

If using Firefox your browser should automatically download to the desktop.  Be sure to double check that it is being downloaded to desktop this is easy to do just look at the bottom of the download window and you should see "Desktop" there. 

# 4

This is where things got confusing for me.

Open the terminal
> 
Application > Accessories > Terminal

Type or Copy/paste            cd ~/Desktop

Then Paste                           sh hplip-2.7.6.run

After that just follow the directions on the terminal when it is done it will ask you to restart just press Y
Do not be concerned when it seems to pause for a long time it will keep going then. 

Hope this helps some one out there. basically the part that screwed me up, I didnt know to put in cd ~/Desktop. I am sure this is somthing every one should know but being the newbie that I am I dont even know to do that let alone how to do it :lolflag:
