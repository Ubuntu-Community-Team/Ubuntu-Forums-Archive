### Post by wastedfluid on 2007-06-23
I can't really recommend anything except for the Cooling Pads that you can buy for your laptop.  They'll make a small difference.  In my opinion, the end result is, laptops run hot.  My laptop runs about 100-145F on Ubuntu.. and the bottom has always been hot.  The lst two laptops I have had (Dell, Sony, THEN Acer) were all extremely hot, as well.  It's just something you can't really beat.  Try out the cooling pad, see if it helps.  It'll help, but I doubt it'll help to your satisifcation level.  Plus, the cooling pads draw power usually from a USB port on your laptop.. thus, zapping out more battery power.  It's a lose/lose situation, kind of.

Good luck.
