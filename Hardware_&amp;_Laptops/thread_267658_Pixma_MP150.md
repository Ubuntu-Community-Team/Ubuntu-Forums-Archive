### Post by mgmiller on 2006-10-02
I have a Canon i960 printer and tried many different things before I got the turboprint driver.  It works great and is worth the money.  There may be Japanese drivers available for your printer, I had heard of them for mine, but couldn't make them work.  I was finally wasting so much time on this, that it was cheapest to just buy Turboprint.  When it's set up for maximum resolution on good photo paper (I use Kirkland Professional glossy paper), the prints look better than what I get from windows.  It works perfectly with all my printing software and works fine with Picassa for linux.
