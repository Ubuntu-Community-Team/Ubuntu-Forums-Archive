### Post by arvevans on 2007-03-11
What type of laptop are you using?
Is that 70 watt unit the correct power supply for your particular laptop?
[INDENT]It sounds like your laptop may be detecting that you have a same manufacturer power unit, but that it is the wrong model for your particular laptop model, and thus unable to power the unit.[/INDENT]

Has the laptop worked before with this particular power unit?
Does this happen only with Ubuntu Linux installed, or does it also happen with other OS installations?
Have you tried re-running the original manufacturer's Systems Disk with your laptop?
Have you recently performed an upgrade on the CMOS firmware (possibly to the wrong firmware version)?
Have you tried re-booting with all USB extension equipment unplugged (the USB power load could be too great)?
