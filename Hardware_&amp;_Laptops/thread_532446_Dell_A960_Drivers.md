### Post by tturrisi on 2007-08-25
Dell does not make their own printers.  Dell has a deal w/ Lexmark, Dell is a reseller of Lexmark printers.  They put the Dell label on them and the printers often have some subtle changes made to them such as cartridges, labels, power supplies, etc., but all Dell printers are really Lexmaek printers from 2002 forward.

[http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Dell-Photo_AIO_Printer_960](http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Dell-Photo_AIO_Printer_960)
