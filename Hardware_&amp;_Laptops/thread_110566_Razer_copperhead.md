### Post by Sp@z on 2005-12-31
Was playin with my new Razer copperhead mouse this evening to learn that the settings are stored within the mouse (up to 32kb) so when you load into Linux OS the settings you have programed into your mouse will follow you. Not all settings work, like "on the fly" sensitivity, but the senstivity and dpi and buttons programmed (in the mouse) will follow. Pretty nifty for gamers with this mouse, like me. Just a lil FYI for you folks.
