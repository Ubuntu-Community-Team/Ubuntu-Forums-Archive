### Post by MrHippocampus on 2007-07-31
I would go for an nvidia 8600GT at the top end of your buget, or an 8500GT at the lower end (always shop around for graphics cards, you can get really good deals if you spend a decent amount of time looking).

One important point with those cards is that they don't provide XvMC (in this case that means hardware MPEG2 decoding) which takes a big load off the cpu when decoding anything in MPEG2 (DVD's/Digital TV being the main examples). If you do want XvMC then you should probably go for a 7 series like the 7600 at the lower end or if your lucky you might be able to find a 7900GS at the very top of your budget.
