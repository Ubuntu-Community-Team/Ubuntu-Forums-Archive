### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-01-11
As far as Function keys go, I have an HP multimedia keyboard with 23 extra keys and a volume dial.  I was able to get them all to work with some tricky scan codes and xmodmap.  Search tgalati4 and multimedia and you may find some pointers.  I can't imagine that Function keys would be that hard to program.  Now if you are trying to get the special functions to work, they are controlled by the BIOS and IBM generally supports Linux so it's a matter of finding the magic modules to load.
