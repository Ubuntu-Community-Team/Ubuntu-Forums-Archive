### Post by kurotenshi on 2008-01-17
Well, the luxpad is one of the new touchpad technology from synaptics

And here is were I have to say sorry and make another thread or edit this one, wile searching for the site that explains what is the luxpad I've found that actually what I have is only called a DUAL MODE touchpad, not luxpad.

From what i've understand the luxpad technology is only an illuminated touchpad that can be used for a lot of stuff (not interested in it right now but here's the link [http://www.synaptics.com/products/lux.cfm]("http://www.synaptics.com/products/lux.cfm"))

Now the dual mode functions like this, it has 8 differentiated areas drawn in it. In normal use you can only see clearly the little square on the top right corner that, when touched, activates the dual mode. When the dual mode is active, a backlight glows on the touchpad making visible (in my case) the Toshiba logo, 3areas on top that activate web browser, network preferences and photo gallery. On the bottom it has 3 custumizable areas that can be assigned to lunch any software and finally on the right it has an area that turns the volume up and down by sliding on it. (of course this is what it does on Vista).
For more info [http://www.synaptics.com/products/dualmode.cfm]("http://www.synaptics.com/products/dualmode.cfm")
