### Post by toupeiro on 2007-12-12
another thing (most annoying)  is that newer keyboards have a button called F-lock.  if you F-lock is off, your function keys do other windows system actions.  If your Function lock is on, they behave as regular function keys.  May be worth scanning your keyboard for an F-lock key, hitting it if you have one, and trying again.
