### Post by CrispyFried on 2007-06-08
you could look into a pocket PC, I have a Dell X5 Axim that I use mainly for ebooks with MSReader.. it gives about 4 hours read time (more if the backlight is off) and there are extended batteries for it that will give 6+ hours. you can find pocket PCs cheap on ebay. I paid 50 bucks for mine. only drawback is the screens are small but then again so is the unit :) pluses are color, SD and CF slots (store a million books) plus music and video playback etc. some pocket PCs can also run linux, but support is spotty.
