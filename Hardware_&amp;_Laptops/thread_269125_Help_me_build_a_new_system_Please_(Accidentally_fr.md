### Post by mips on 2007-03-08
> **Raavea said:**
> 

 Anyhow, I have been planning.. I will be trying to get a liveCD onto the thing to see if it's my HDs that were killed. Or just one of them.. Or the mobo herself, or the whole kit and kaboodle. I have a vague recollection that she still goes 'beep' after a few moments of power, which used to mean that the system was about to boot.

Best bet is to disconnect everything. Then only connect power supply, motherboard, ram and video card if not onboard. Check if you get a bios screen and it boots to that point.

If it gets this far then it's going to moan about a lack of boot device like a HD. Connect one at a time and see what happens after you connected each one and booted up again.

The fact that you get the normal single beep sounds good. If you got no beep or multiple beeps you might have to worry.

You should be able to figure out what went 'pop' if you do it one step at a time.
