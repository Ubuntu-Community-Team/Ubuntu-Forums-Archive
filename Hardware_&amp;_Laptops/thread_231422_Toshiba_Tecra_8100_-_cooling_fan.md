### Post by pvdg on 2007-01-04
I guess you must have found an answer elsewhere by now, but here it goes anyway.

My own Toshiba Tecra 8100's cooling fan worked under 5.10 and now 6.06. I did notice, however, that the laptop heated considerebly before the fan started. I read somewhere that this didn't harm the machine, being merely uncomfortable for the user.

Ther is a package available in ubuntu repositories - toshset - that allows you to manually set fan speed, among other parameters, in Toshiba laptops. I've been using it for months.
