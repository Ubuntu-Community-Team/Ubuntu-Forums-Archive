### Post by gcranston on 2005-06-08
in /etc/X11/xorg.conf   Under modules, did you comment out

Load  "GLCore"
Load  "dri"


and add in 

Load  "glx"

this could be your problem.  

Also check 
/usr/share/doc/NVIDIA_GLX-1.0/README.txt   
It has a lot of info in it that may help you.

Good luck.
