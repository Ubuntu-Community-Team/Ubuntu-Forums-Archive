### Post by AndrewTheArt on 2008-04-17
In the graphics card realm, it's safe to say that proprietary Linux drivers are better than open source Linux drivers in most instances, especially with drivers provided by ATI and NVIDIA. (I think NVIDIA is a little better at providing half decent Linux graphics card drivers, though). This is simply because reverse engineering a driver to work with Linux  or creating one from scratch is extremely difficult. Honestly, I wouldn't waste your time looking for open source drivers - I'm willing to bet that the proprietary one is better.
