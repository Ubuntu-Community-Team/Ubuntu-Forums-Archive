### Post by vadriaan on 2007-03-15
One of the great things about Ubuntu is that the installation CD let you boot the machine and try it before you install it. This is when you can identify what works and what don't. It is even better than Knoppix (which does not recognize my pcmcia wlan card).

Be warned i have read about various makes of laptops here Ubuntu 6.10 hibernate works but not totally. In my case when I come out of hibernation, the swap partition is deleted, so next time I want to hibernate, i have to make and activate my swap partition again (and again).
