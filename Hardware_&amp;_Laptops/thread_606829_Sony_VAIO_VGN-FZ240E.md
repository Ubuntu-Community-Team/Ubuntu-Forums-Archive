### Post by mpcd on 2007-11-16
I've got the VGN-CR220E which I believe is pretty much the same but 14" instead of 15.4". Its got the some of the best out-of-the-box support I've seen so far in Ubuntu. The X3100 works beautifully with the built-in intel video driver and compiz is smooth. 

The 4965ABGN works very well, however I haven't figured out how to connect with N speeds instead of G speeds (always shows 54Mbps even though I have an N router - Vista connects at 130Mbps). That being said, NetworkManager works well, all networks in the area are found, it gives good readings for signal strength, and connects quickly.

The webcam is detected as a webcam however I've not gotten it to work yet. I've found this thread that seems to link to a driver but I haven't installed it yet. [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=289836](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=289836)

The brightness buttons on the keyboard don't work but the brightness applet for gnome works so you can use the slider to adjust brightness. The default touchpad settings are marvelous, significantly better than in windows. 

If you're looking for a good Ubuntu laptop, I don't think you'll be disappointed.
