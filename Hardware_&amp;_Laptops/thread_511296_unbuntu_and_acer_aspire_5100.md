### Post by Paul820 on 2007-07-27
You just put the CD in a leave it to load. Do you have your laptop set to boot from CD first or your Hard drive? If you don't know, take the Cd out and reboot your computer, press f2 to enter the bios and look for boot priority, set the first device to your CD drive and second to hard drive the save and exit. Put the Ubuntu CD in and it should load. I have an aspire 5100 aswell and that's all i did to get it to load.
