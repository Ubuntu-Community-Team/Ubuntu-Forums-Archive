### Post by David Haas on 2005-09-22
modestmelody--Although I'm not informed enough to help with your problem, I can recommend a good book for Linux novices and beyond: "How Linux Works" by Brian Ward, published in 2004 by "No Starch Press." It's not huge, like some others--it's 329 pages--and it offers clear explanations of Linux processes, besides giving a good introduction to Linux commands.
