### Post by aysiu on 2007-05-13
Of course there's an advantage--the hardware is guaranteed to work with Ubuntu. You also usually get pretty good support should anything go wrong.

You can read some reviews of System76 (an American vendor of Ubuntu-preinstalled laptops) [here](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=343798).

You can get a comprehensive list of Linux-preinstalled vendors [here](http://lxer.com/module/db/index.php?dbn=14).
