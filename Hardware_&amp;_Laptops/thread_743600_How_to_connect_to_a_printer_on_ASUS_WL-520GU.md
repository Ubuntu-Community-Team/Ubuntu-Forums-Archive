### Post by withaar on 2008-04-02
The WL-520GU is a router with wireless. It also has a usb port to connect a printer with. The print server uses the LPD/LPR protocol. The URL to use to connect to your printer in cups is:

 lpd://192.168.1.1/LPRServer

It works very nicely.
