### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-01-15
The flash drive could be broken, shorted, fried, or otherwise inoperative.  Because they contain a file system, problems in reading them can cause an unresponsive system.  You system is probably not frozen, just waiting for I/O.  You can reset it by typing alt-printscreen R-E-I-U-S-B is succession waiting a couple of seconds between presses.

Try on another machine, windows or mac and see if you can read it and/or reformat it.
