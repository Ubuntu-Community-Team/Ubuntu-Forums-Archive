### Post by khowe on 2007-06-11
The main hardware differences are as follows:

The XPS 410n has...

Expansion Slots
PCI: 3 Slots
PCIe x1: 1 Slot
PCIe x16 (Graphics): 1 Slots
PCIe x4/x8: 1 Slot

Integrated 10/100/1000 network interface

375 Watt DC Power Supply (handles video cards that draw more power)

3.5" Bays: 4 bays (two external; two internal)

The two internal hard drives mount side by side which may be cooler when two drives are installed in the 410n.

The e520n has...

Expansion Slots
PCI: 2 Slots
PCIe x1: 1 Slot
PCIe x16 (Graphics): 1 Slots

Integrated 10/100 network interface

305 Watt DC Power Supply

3.5" Bays: 3 bays (one external; two internal)

Integrated Intel®  Graphics Media Accelerator X3000 (Intel®  GMA X3000)
