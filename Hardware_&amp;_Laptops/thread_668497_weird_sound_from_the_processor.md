### Post by ARhere on 2008-01-15
there are a few things that will make a humming/buzzing sound.

1.  Speaker  (duh!)
2.  Saturated Inductor
3.  Loose part.

> every time it starts the humming sound i just touch the fan for a sec and it stops
The processor itself (physical hardware) cannot make noise.  So I would suggest looking on the heat sink bracket for a screw that is not tight.  Possibly the metal plate on back of the MB used to tighten the heat sink?

The second thing I would test, see if the noise occurs when the processor is in heavy use, verses sitting idle.  Try turning on your PC and pushing the 'PAUSE' key while the POST is occurring.  (*in other words, when the memory test is occuring at first boot, push the 'PAUSE' key and it should stop the system.*)  Then report back here.

-AR
