I recently purchased an MSI K9N Diamond (nForce 590 SLI chipset) motherboard for a future upgrade. I have scoured the net in search of information regarding support for this motherboard/chipset in Feisty and have turned up very little. This post [http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?t=73925](http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?t=73925) led me to the Linux platform drivers section of Nvidia's site, but it appears that the drivers only support nForce chipsets up to nForce 4 430. Creative's site doesn't have any Linux drivers for the Audigy SE. Anyone out there have any experiences/advice on the K9N Diamond or nForce 590?
