### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-03-28
> **grizdor said:**
> In windows when I do "safely remove" on my external hard drive, the fan and light in it turn off. Is there an Ubuntu equivalent to this?

Yes.  It is called "unmounting".

If you haven't changed the defaults, the drive icon should appear on the desktop.

Right-click it and select "unmount drive".

If you have done a lot of writing, it may take a bit of time before telling you it is safe to disconnect.

You can remount by selecting (double-click) the drive in "Computer".
