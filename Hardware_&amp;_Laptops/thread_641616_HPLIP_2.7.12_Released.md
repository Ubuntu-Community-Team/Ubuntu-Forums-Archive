### Post by noynac on 2007-12-15
Of late, a number of people have reported having problems with their HP printers. I'm not certain if this will be of help, but HP released version 2.7.12 of HPLIP yesterday. The download site is:

[http://hplip.sourceforge.net/index.html](http://hplip.sourceforge.net/index.html)

The HPLIP installer works well with Gutsy and can be downloaded from:

[http://hplip.sourceforge.net/install/install/index.html](http://hplip.sourceforge.net/install/install/index.html)

The release notes are at:

[http://hplip.sourceforge.net/release_notes.html](http://hplip.sourceforge.net/release_notes.html)

This release fixes bugs and adds support for a few new printers. It also seems to spruce up the interface a bit.

As an aside, the HPLIP version in the Gutsy repo's is 2.7.7, which was followed by 2.7.9, then 2.7.10, and now 2.7.12.
