### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-01-31
Just from a quick look that seems fine.

I would look closer at the components on the motherboard to check what network chipset it's using, what audio, what northbridge controller.

An easy way is to just do a search on the internet for your_motherbroad + linux (or ubuntu), and do some more research about what chips are on your motherboard and what their compatibility with ubuntu / linux is.
