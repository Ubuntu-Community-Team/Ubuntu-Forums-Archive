### Post by kodoku on 2007-10-28
If you're going to use Windows, then what model of a webcam really depends on how much you want to spend and what features you need.  You can get ones as cheap as $30 (Microsoft LifeCam VX-1000, or Logitec Skype Quickcam), or in the hundreds.  There are new high resolution webcams (look on logitec's website) that can stream high quality video.

Do you really need a recommendation for a microphone?  Are you going to record music or something?  If not, then ANY microphone that plugs into the recording jack will do.  (Also, many webcams also have built-in microphones, meaning the cam doubles as a voice recoding device)
