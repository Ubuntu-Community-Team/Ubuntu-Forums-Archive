### Post by David Haas on 2005-10-08
conjurer--Others have installed the Canon i250 in Ubuntu. Instructions are available at a prior forum posting at <http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=29255&highlight=canon+i250>. You can read other postings in the Forum repository by doing a search at the search "button" in the upper brown bar. Just click on search and enter "canon i250" (without the quotes). At the least, this will give you some experience. If it doesn't work out well, you could buy a driver from TurboPrint at <http://www.turboprint.de/english.html>. These have a solid reputations. However, if the i250 is an inexpensive printer, spending for a driver may not be cost effective, especially since you can purchase an HP for from ~$35 (low-end) to ~$90 (mid-range, like my Deskjet 5740).
Good luck,
David
