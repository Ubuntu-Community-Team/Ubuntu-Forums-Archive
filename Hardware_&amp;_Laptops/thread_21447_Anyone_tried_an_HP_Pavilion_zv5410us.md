### Post by MesOblivious on 2006-11-08
Hi everyone. I have a HP Pavilion zv5410us, and I am completely new to Linux and, thus, new to Ubuntu. I tried just installing a few other distros of Linux on this laptop and I was unsuccessful, i beleive because I am a total n00b and I had no idea how to do it. Thankfully Ubuntu exists and I was successful installing Ubuntu 6.10 the first time, and surprisingly I had most of the basics working automatically after the auto-install from disk. The touchpad mouse and touch pad scrolling, nVidia widescreen, and sound all worked worked right away. Then I did a little research and got the 3D-nVidia video working and also I finally got Flash working properly thanks to the HowTo Flash guide here. So, the only device lacking so far is the Broadcom 4306 802.11g wireless card, which I know many people have had difficulties with. So if anyone wants advice on how to get these working, let me know. I would be glad to share my success to help them, because so many have helped me on here, and in turn if anyone has advice on getting the wireless to work on this laptop, hit me up. Thx!:D
