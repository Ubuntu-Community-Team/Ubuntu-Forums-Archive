### Post by TaNeK on 2005-01-21
Going socket 939 athlon64 is the only way to go atm, imho. The most-for-them-money AGP gfxcard nowdays should probably be Nvidia 6800le, or so I've heard(sone people say nvidia is better for linux too :D). I would suggest that you get 1*1024 of ram instead, as getting 2*512 may work faster now, due to dual-channel but soon you'll find that 1GB ram might just not be enough for playing the latest games (which I'm assuming you'll be wanting to do, as you're gonna have windows :D). 

Well, for more specific data try to find a hardware/overclocking-forum, people at places like that tend to be quite good at knowing what is the most you could get, still not having to sell your home or car ;).
