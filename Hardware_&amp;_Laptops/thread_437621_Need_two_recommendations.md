### Post by joft on 2007-05-09
I had several problems with "kind of cheap" laser printers ... have a look at [http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting/Database/DatabaseIntro]("http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting/Database/DatabaseIntro") before buying one.
Note that there are many "GDI/windows only" printers out there, which AFAIK only run under Windows. Buying a printer which supports PostScript or PCL5/5e/6 or any other "open" protocol should be save - but I doubt that there are PS-able printers for less than 100$.

For the scanner question, hmmm, I have a LiDE 50 which works just fine. Have look at [http://www.sane-project.org/sane-mfgs.html]("http://www.sane-project.org/sane-mfgs.html") .
