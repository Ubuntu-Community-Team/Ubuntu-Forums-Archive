### Post by idrailgag on 2007-10-24
I have an HP Pavilion DV2000 (dv2125nr) that dual boots Ubuntu and Windows Vista.  The sound in Ubuntu looks like it works perfectly, everything shows up, I can adjust the sound, and the music players act as though they are playing.  However, most of the time, no sound would ever come out of my laptop.  I do not know to what extent of hardware and versions of Windows this applies to but hope this helps:

[B]If I shutdown in Windows with my computer on mute, the sound will never work in Ubuntu.
Solution: always unmute Windows before shutting down.[/B]

This problem took me months of frustration to realize, so I hope this helps as many people as possible.  I tried so many "solutions" on these forums and nothing worked.
