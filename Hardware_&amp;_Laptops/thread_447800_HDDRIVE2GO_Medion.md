Hi there I'm not an expert or anything but I would say that the tray itself is broken, so what I would do is try update the drivers of the hardrive by going to "My Computer" and then right click on the drive icon and then "Properties", then go to "Hardware" and then "Troubleshooting" and try to identify your problem. Another way is try to contact Medion Help Center for more information, or even sending the computer to them so they may have a look if there is a hardware or software problem.
Good luck fixing your problem.:)
