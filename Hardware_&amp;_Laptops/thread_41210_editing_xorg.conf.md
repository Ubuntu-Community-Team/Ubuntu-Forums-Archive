### Post by skoal on 2005-06-12
Hmm...

that all looks correct, and the "hsync: 49.68 kHz" from your modeline falls well within your Monitor specs.  Try these settings (in the appropriate places) instead:


Section "Screen"
[...]
DefaultDepth 16
[...]
Depth 16
Modes "1200x800_60.00"  "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"
[...]
EndSection

And if that works, then you can try out a depth of 24 and make the appropriate "Modes" changes to that setting as well (as I did for the 16).  If that don't work, then your "sis" card may not support it - although it should for such a low freq.

* At least doing it this way in your 'xorg.config" file, you are assured that X will use the explicit modeline setting you made first, then fall back to lower 1200 settings (if possible).
