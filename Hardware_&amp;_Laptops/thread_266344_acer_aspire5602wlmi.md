### Post by cruiseraddict on 2006-09-27
gingerj
I do not have the 5602wlmi but do have the 5102wlmi. it is the little brother as it does not have the tv accessories, firewire or bluetooth(only one i miss) I loaded Ubuntu Dapper Drake on teh laptop about 1 hour after purchasing it. Everything went smoothly and have only booted into windows 3 times sice installing Linux. There are a few things to watch for. 1. Windows is installed into a virtual partition for some program that acer loaded with Windows XP MCE. I would make your backup disks for windows before starting load to make sure you can load windows if you ever need to again. 2. The video camera and card reader do not work in linux just yet. There are some threads on here to working on getting them to work. As far as teh basics. Yes wireless works with the madwifi that Dapper loads and the graphics card work just fine. 

The best way to test. load the live dvd for dapper and see if you like it.
