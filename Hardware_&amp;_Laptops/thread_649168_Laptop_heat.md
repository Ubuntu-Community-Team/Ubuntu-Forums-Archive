### Post by MRCeltic on 2007-12-25
I was having this problem also.  My Laptop would get extremly hot.  Didn't know what was going on.  I happened to stumble upon this thread that said it had something to do with the indexing service.  I went and disabled the indexing service >System>Preferences>Indexing Preferences and unchecked both boxes on the first tab and haven't had a Problem since.  I can actually sit with my laptop on my lap again where it belongs.
