### Post by SSzretter on 2006-11-01
Ok, I found a solution - in the BIOS, I set the mouse to disable the stick, and use only the track pad, and changed the setting to 'both' for if you have a external mouse plugged in ...
interestingly, my external mouse no longer works, but i am guessing if you play around with the settings in there, you could probably get both to work.
