I can't necessarily help you in terms of compatibility of specific cards, but I would recommend that you look for a higher-end card if you're interested in playing Battlefield 2.  Both the 6200 and especially the 5200 are significantly lower-powered 3D cards than the 9800 you're looking to replace.  I'd suggest looking into at least a Geforce 6600 if you want to be playing modern 3D games.  I wouldn't expect there to be a difference in compatibility between the lower-end and higher-end cards of any particular model line (6x00, 7x00).

Also, TV-out is relatively standard on video cards these days, so you shouldn't have any problem getting that in a cheap or medium-priced card.
