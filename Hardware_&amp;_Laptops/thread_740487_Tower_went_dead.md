### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-03-30
Grab a voltmeter and check the voltage of your Yellow-to-Black (12V) and Red-to-Black (5V) on a spare power connector while booting.  Check your 3.3V on the ATX power connector (orange-to-black).  Look for any bulging or leaky capacitors on the motherboard.

Check your BIOS for voltage/temperature monitor if it even boots to BIOS.

AMD's aren't known for their overtemp capability.  They tend to self-destruct at thermal shutdown temperatures--which kind of defeats the purpose of thermal shutdown.
