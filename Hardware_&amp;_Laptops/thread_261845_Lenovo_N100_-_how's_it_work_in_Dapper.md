### Post by smylie on 2006-10-01
There's a large number of N100 series laptops  - all slightly different.
They all seem to fairly well supported, with the same few issues:

[LIST]
[*]Sound - works, but doesn't mute automatically when inserting a headphone jack. Can be muted manually, but this affects the sound quality. Maybe alsa project will eventually get this fixed.
[*]Wireless - just works. It is intel. Dapper detects this out of the box.
[*]Web Cam - doesn't work. No drivers available.
[*]Fingerprint Reader - doesn't work. It's not the same model reader as the thinkpads' (which does work), and i'm not sure if it will ever work 
[/LIST]

Other than that, pretty much every thing seems to be pretty good.
