### Post by chavo on 2006-10-24
I'm running a 6600GT here and it absolutely flies with beryl-xgl. They are under $100 dollars now and I think are one of the best cards for the money ever. I also do a little gaming, and for the games I play the card has performed very well. This particular one is a PNY.
So if you're not really a bigtime gamer and want to save a few bucks I highly recommend this card. Remember Beryl != Vista, you don't need a top of the line card to run it. It runs on this laptop with an integrated Intel 845GL. Now it's not exactly fast on here but vanilla compiz works fine.
