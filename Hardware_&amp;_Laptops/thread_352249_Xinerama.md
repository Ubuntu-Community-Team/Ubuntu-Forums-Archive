while using the same descriptor Screen X, are referring to two different things.

In the ServerLayout section, they refer to virtual screens.  Every screen on the system is numbered consecutively.  However, in the Device sections, they refer to _physical connections on that specific card_ - so if you have two separate cards in the machine, then _both_ Device sections will use "Screen 0" (or, you shouldn't even need them).

So, I would at least change the second Device section's Screen to 0 or just take the Screen line out of both Device sections.

This would account for the "no matching device for PCI:1:0:0.

What I'm not sure about is why you are getting the same thing for the first line. (Note the use of a colon for both separators, instead of a period.)  You might try that, see what happens...
