### Post by Peferling on 2005-08-27
XFree86 supports the NM2200 chipset:

[http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/html_single/Hardware-HOWTO/](http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/html_single/Hardware-HOWTO/)

Heres an example config under XFree86 that I found on the web as well:

[http://www.cse.ucsd.edu/users/kube/linux/XF86Config](http://www.cse.ucsd.edu/users/kube/linux/XF86Config)



I had a problem just the opposite with a Dell Lat C400 not being supported by MEPIS using XFree86, and so here I am with kubuntu using Xorg.

However, you can replace your Xorg with XFree86 and stay with Kubuntu, if you feel up to the challenge.  It's installable via Knaptic/Synaptic.   Do a search on the wiki or forum here for specifics, or if someone whose done this wishes to chime in...
