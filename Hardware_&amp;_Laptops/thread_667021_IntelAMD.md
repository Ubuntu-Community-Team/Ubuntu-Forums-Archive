### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-01-14
They're both very viable platforms.
Intel tends to have better performance/watt and is just flat-out faster than AMD at the top-end. They also have more OC'ing headroom if that's your thing.

AMD has better performance/$ and tends to undervolt better if that's yourthing. Unfortunately, you have to roll your own kernel to get custom dynamic undervolting. There's no easy tool like with Windoze.
