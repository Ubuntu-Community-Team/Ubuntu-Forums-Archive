### Post by arvevans on 2007-06-29
Not getting the startup "beep" would seem to indicate a major hardware failure, not a software problem.  Probably neither Microsoft or Linux are at fault.

When you reset the BIOS, did you remember to put the jumper back in the non-reset position?
If you have a digital voltmeter you could check the power supply output voltages as a first step in troubleshooting.
