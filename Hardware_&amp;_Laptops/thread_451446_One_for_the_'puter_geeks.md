This is a question for all the hardware geeks out there;)

I have an MSI K7N2 Series mainboard. The onboard video chip fan is starting to pack it in and making one heck of a racket. I have an FX5200 video card pluged in and am not using the onboard video anymore. Can I safely unplug the onboard chip fan so my computer room can be quiet again????

Edit:  I solved the problem......I took the little sticker off the back of the fan and put a small drop of oil in the hole. Quiet as a mouse now:D
