### Post by tobibobi on 2005-09-28
By reading your config file, i believe that you have forgotten to comment out the two modules [FONT="Lucida Console"]GLcore [/FONT]and [FONT="Lucida Console"]dri[/FONT] (i think it's [FONT="Lucida Console"]dri[/FONT])

All specs i have read so far states that these two can not be placed along with the [FONT="Lucida Console"]glx[/FONT] module.

But if you keeps getting problems, try to disable the [FONT="Lucida Console"]glx[/FONT] module as well - remember if you are tired of rebooting each time your screen gets blocked, you an always try to ssh to your computer from a different machine.

And i dont know if its right to State that VideoRam should be 32. As far as i recall, [FONT="Lucida Console"]VideoRam [/FONT]is stated in KBytes and you should have more than 32 KBytes on your card (i hope ;-) )

I know this is an somewhat old thread, but i always believe those should be complete.
