### Post by Unbound Spectre on 2007-04-23
Your current drive is using a [Molex]("http://www.ag.ndsu.nodak.edu/agcomm/accs/support/maintenance/images/molex.jpg") power connector, not a [SATA]("http://eshop.macsales.com/images/Items/sata/sata15pin.jpg") power connector which is what you need for most SATA drives.
