### Post by jeremy on 2005-04-14
I recently bought a Safecom 3.5 portable HDD box (model No. SUSB2-35CAF) at a computer fair in London.
Works perfectly with warty and hoary (just plug it in and an icon appears on the desktop) with no need to configure, made of aluminium, and cost about 20 quid.
