### Post by dproffessa on 2006-01-28
Hey

Chances r u probably have a WINMODEM. Do a search on the internet (somehow) for a program called scanmodem and follow directions and read the log generated after, if it says anything like controllerless modem, HCF, DCF or HaM then u have a winmodem end need to get a Linux driver for it, check the manufacturer, or u can always buy a real (hardware) modem ie #com USRobotics 3cp5610 costs a bit more but it works.
