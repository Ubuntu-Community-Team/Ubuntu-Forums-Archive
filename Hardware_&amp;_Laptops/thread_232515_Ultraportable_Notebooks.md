Macbooks are far too large to be ultraportable. Ultraportables are more the size you are talking about. Wikipedia, for example, defines them as usually having a sub-10" screen and a weight of less than 1 kg. Most ultraportables I have are significantly smaller than a piece of A4 paper, and less than an inch thick. The lightest Macbook weighs 5.2 pounds, is slightly over an inch thick, and is nearly 13" by 9".
