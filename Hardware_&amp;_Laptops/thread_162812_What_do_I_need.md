### Post by Tom Tiger on 2006-04-20
If you want to connect directly without the need for a USB modem then an external Ethernet ADSL modem is the best way to go IMHO.  

You do need a networkcard in your pc to connect to the modem.  

But the fun part of an Ethernet ADSL modem is that you can connect multiple pc's (or notebooks) to it.  Either trough a switch connected to the modem or directly to the modem (multi pc modem for adsl)  the latter usually comes with 4 ethernetjacks (so connect your desktop, your laptop ect....)

My config for my homelan is, one cisco modem ADSL (multipc) connected to an eight port switch and on that switch are my two desktops, my laptop, my test sytem and my test laptop..... eh.... and my satelite receiver :)
