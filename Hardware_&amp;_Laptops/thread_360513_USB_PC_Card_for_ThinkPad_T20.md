### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-02-20
Did the T20 come with USB 2.0?  As I recall, it came with one USB 1.0 port.  The cardbus slot probably runs at pci speeds of 33 MHz which would roughly equate to 33 megabits per second.  My guess is that you are getting 480 megabits between the device and the card and 33 megabits per second between the card and the computer.  What benchmarks are you using?

What is the speed factor difference between using the built in 1.0 USB port and your Zonenet port?  High speed is marketing hype.  True speed depends on many things including the motherboard architecture.
