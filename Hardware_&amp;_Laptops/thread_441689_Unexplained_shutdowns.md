### Post by trak87 on 2007-05-12
Check for overheating. Modern systems will shut themselves off if they get too hot. Cleaning out the dust and checking to see if the fans are still spinning is something everybody needs to do. A fan should spin easily with the flick of a finger. I often reseat the CPU and memory as well.
