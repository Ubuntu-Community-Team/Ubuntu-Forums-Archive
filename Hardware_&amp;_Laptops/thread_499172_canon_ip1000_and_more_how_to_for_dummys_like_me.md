### Post by dragonwings on 2007-07-12
how to install 
Canon Pixus iP3100 / iP4100 / iP8600 (and Pixma iP1000 / iP1500) Drivers

1/ left click on "system" (at toop of desktop)

2/ go to Administration then left click on "Synaptic Package Manager"

3/ left click on "Settings" and left click on "Repositories"

4/ left click "Third-Party Software

5/left click "Add". now copy and past the below into box ALL OF THE LINE BELOW!!!!
deb [http://mambo.kuhp.kyoto-u.ac.jp/~takushi/ubuntu](http://mambo.kuhp.kyoto-u.ac.jp/~takushi/ubuntu) ./

6/left click "add Souce"

7/ open terminal and copy and past the below
   sudo apt-get update

8/open terminal and copy and past the below
  sudo apt-get install libcnbj-2.5 bjfilter-2.5 pstocanonb

NOW JUST INSTALL PRINTER AS NORMAL BUT THIS TIME YOU WILL
FIND THE IP1000 ON PRINTER LIST.
