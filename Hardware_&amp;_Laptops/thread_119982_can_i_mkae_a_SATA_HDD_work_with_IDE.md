### Post by wrtrdood on 2006-01-20
A SATA drive needs a SATA controller and it uses a different power connector as well as the small blue data cable.  Your system would have to specifically support it and yes, it will work just fine along side an IDE controller.  Still, the system must support it and you might even need a BIOS upgrade too.

### Post by audax321 on 2006-01-20
Yup, if your motherboard has SATA connectors you can use a combination of both IDE and SATA drivers. Although, there is an issue with power connecters. SATA drives sometimes take a different kind than the 4 prong most IDE drives take. But you can find drives that have both types of power connecters on the drive and that is what you should look for if your power supply doesn't have the SATA power connecters.
