### Post by odinb on 2007-09-07
For Kubuntu, you can go to "system settings" and "regional & language" and "keyboard layout". Add the languages that your 2 keyboards have, and you will get a country-flag in the lower right corner. Klicking that flag will swap between the language alyouts you have installed.
