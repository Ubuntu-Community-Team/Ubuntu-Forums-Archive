### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-15
I'm sorry to hear that you are having such a bad time with getting printers working.

However, did you do much checking for compatibility, before purchase? 

Did you post on this, or any other forum about this printer?

Remember a couple of things about drivers for any piece of hardware.

1. An operating system can only support a piece of hardware if there is a suitable driver. If the manufacturer has not released a driver, the hardware will probably not work. This is the case with any OS, be it Linux, Windows, OSX, etc.

2. Often new models are not immediately supported, as manufacturers usually provide Windows initially. Sometimes it can take a few months for a Linux or OSX driver to become available.

Driver issues are not something only encountered within Linux. Have a look on the net and see how many people are having a lot of problems finding Vista drivers for their hardware.

Summing-up I can understand your frustration, but direct it where it should be pointed - at Epson for not yet releasing the correct driver.
