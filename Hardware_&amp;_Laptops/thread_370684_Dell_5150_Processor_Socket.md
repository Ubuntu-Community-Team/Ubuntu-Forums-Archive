### Post by m3741 on 2007-02-28
It's a socket 478. Watch it though, putting a bigger proc in there. Cooling is always an issue.

Also, standard disclaimer, laptops are VERY tricky. If you have never taken one apart before, DON'T DO IT! I can't tell you how many I have repaired from inexperienced people attempting upgrades/repairs. As always, if you would like to learn, go buy an old clunker and practice on that instead. Enjoy! :)
