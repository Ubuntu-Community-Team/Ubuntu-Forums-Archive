### Post by mlampard on 2006-11-22
Hi.  Chances are your insert key works just fine - the vim editor just doesnt use it - if you want to insert some text, press i or a to enter edit/insert mode.  i inserts typed text before the cursor, a inserts it after.  Pressing the escape key once or twice will get you back into command mode, where you can save the file.
