### Post by shad0w_walker on 2007-08-14
Ubuntu doesn't have that limitation to my knowledge, that was a purely Windows problem. I have a 500Gb SATA drive sat in my server right now, worked perfectly with out any effort so you shouldn't have any problems with Ubuntu.

Not to cast a shadow on your plans but if there is a problem supporting the larger hard drive it would most likely be with your BIOS/Motherboard, you could get lucky with the updated BIOS, might not, its very hit and miss. Either way, you are fine with Ubuntu supporting that hard drive size.
