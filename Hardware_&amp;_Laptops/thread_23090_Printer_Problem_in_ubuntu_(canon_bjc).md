### Post by m4ng0 on 2005-03-31
I have a Canon BJC 3000 too. I had not your problems, but my printer had a very bad resolution and dropped some ink stains on the paper. I installed cupsys-driver-gimpprint and changed the driver for the printer with the one provided by this package for BJC-3000. After that everything went fine. I don't know if this can help you too, but you can give a try.
