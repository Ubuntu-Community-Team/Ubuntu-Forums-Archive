### Post by DeadToad on 2007-04-25
Wow!  I fixed it!
Feisty Dawn does not like my Kogi  L7EH-TA 17" TFT LCD Flat Panel Color Monitor.
I disconnected the Kogi Flat Panel and attached a KDS Xtreme Flat 17" CRT Monitor.
Now, Feisty Fawn loads and installs without any problems.
