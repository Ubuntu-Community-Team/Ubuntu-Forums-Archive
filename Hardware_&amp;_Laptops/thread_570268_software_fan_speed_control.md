### Post by JBAlaska on 2007-10-08
The problem with that idea is, The fan in your laptop is thermostatically controlled so if it's spinning at top speed that means your CPU is getting hot. If you limit your fan speed, your CPU will overheat.

Best to get a replacement Fan or even use a exacto knife and try to trim the flashing so it won't contact.
