### Post by sublime on 2006-04-21
youre talking about linux here.  theres a way to change how everything works, and yes it does take a little getting use to.  if you find them slow youll have to change the mouse acceleration as well as the sensitivity.  theres a gui for all of that though.  you can just mess with it till you get it the way you want it.  also dont be afraid to mess around with the built in scrolling on the touchpads.  i use to hate it but now i use it all the time.  i dont plug a mouse into my laptop unless im playing a game or something.
