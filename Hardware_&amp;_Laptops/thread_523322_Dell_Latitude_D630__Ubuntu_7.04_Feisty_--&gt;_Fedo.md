### Post by biancoscuba on 2007-08-11
Hi all,

After struggling w/out much progress to install Ubuntu 7.04 Feisty on my new Latitude D630, I found out that **[COLOR="Red"]Fedora Core 8 - test 1[/COLOR]** (that is FC 7.90 Rawhide) was out:

**[COLOR="Red"]https://www.redhat.com/archives/fedora-announce-list/2007-August/msg00001.html[/COLOR]**

I downloaded the Live CD ( about 700 MB, since I don't have a DVD writer), slotted it, and it installed without a glitch out of the box (I have broadband and 1 GB RAM, the latter is required).

Video works out of the box.

Sound works out of the box.

Wireless: for my [COLOR="Red"]**Dell 1490 wireless card**[/COLOR] I had to go and install the [COLOR="Red"]**bcm43xx**[/COLOR] driver following this procedure: 

[COLOR="Red"]**[COLOR="Red"]http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.p...ight=dell+1501[/COLOR]**[/COLOR]

Life is good, finally!
I'll try out Ubuntu again when 7.10 comes out.
