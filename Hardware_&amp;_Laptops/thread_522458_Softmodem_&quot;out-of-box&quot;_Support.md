### Post by kidders on 2007-08-12
Again, most Linuxes will find out-of-the-box support for software modems problematic, for obvious reasons. If you want to use a modem with an OS other than Windows, I suggest choosing a proper hardware one.

The best way to find out what works and what doesn't is to do a few web searches, but I would be surprised if you had problems with a hardware modem.
