### Post by DarkN00b on 2007-01-08
The i945 uses the same i810 driver I use in my i855 chipset. Open Synaptic and make sure you have the Intel video driver installed then change
```
Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Generic Video Cad"
	**Driver		"vesa"**
	BusID		"PCI:0:2:0"
	Option		"UseFBDev"		"true"
        Option  "XAANoOffscreenPixmaps"
EndSection

```
to
```
Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Generic Video Cad"
	**Driver		"i810"**
	BusID		"PCI:0:2:0"
	Option		"UseFBDev"		"true"
        Option  "XAANoOffscreenPixmaps"
EndSection
```

Edgy correctly identified my video chipset and installed the [COLOR="Red"]xserver-xorg-video-i740[/COLOR] and [COLOR="Red"]xserver-xorg-video-i810[/COLOR] drivers. I have no idea why it installed the i740 one. Anyway, make sure you have the driver installed [COLOR="Red"]before[/COLOR] you change your xorg.conf.

Good luck.
