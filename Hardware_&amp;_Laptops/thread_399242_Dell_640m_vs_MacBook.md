### Post by rye_ on 2007-05-02
Hi,

I own a dell 640m and have had the opportunity to  play with a couple of macbooks recently.

If you opt for the intel 4965 AGN card then will need ndiswrapper ( this with few problems).  As for noise the fan & hard-disk are extremely quiet (almost silent).  My only criticism is the noise for the dvd drive, but even this can be controlled by limiting the drive speed.  All hotkeys are detected by ubuntu, even the front media keys.

Also the screen resolution, weight and performance of my 640m are excellent

Macbooks; very nice, extremely quiet and not too overpriced (you expect this from apple).  The only true advantage I see macbooks as possessing is its operating system.  Whilst I love linux (particularly ubuntu and fedora) I still must acknowlegde the superior (much) capabilities of the apple mac os.  Since you intend to use ubuntu (not a bad choice), I see no substantial reason to choose apple over a pc laptop.
