Why is it that you are not open to software raid?

The only major failing of a hardware raid is that if the adapter breaks you generally need to get the exact same (or very similar) card to replace it. To repair a software raid, you just need another of whatever broke. Also The built in Linux Raid software is very efficient, I use it every day (raid 5 and raid 1, but I have experience with 0, and soon hopefully six as well), and it generally performs flawlessly, and Linux Software raids perform better than many hardware raids(when I last check about a year ago :? ).
