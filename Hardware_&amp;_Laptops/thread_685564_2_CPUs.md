### Post by patryk77 on 2008-02-02
Laptops generally don't have 2 CPUs. There are some more recent ones that have Dual-Core CPUs, which are considered like 2 CPUs by Operating Systems.

Hyper Threading is also displayed as 2 CPUs, although it really is a single CPU.

This is not what x86_64 or IA64 programs are for... these are for 64 bit processors.

System shutdowns are often caused by overheating.
