### Post by joao82 on 2007-07-25
problem 100% solved. Thank you w4ett and myoungf1.
After reading your files I realized my error was changing the Identifier name to "ati". So I went to the live cd, copied the real identifier to paper, then edited the xorg.conf in rescue mode through vi. It allowed me to start Kubuntu graphically, although the image wasn't perfect. So I found in the X11 folder a file called xorg.conf.2 with the old settings. Then I just had to delete xorg.conf and rename xorg.conf.2 to xorg.conf.
presto :D

thanks again
