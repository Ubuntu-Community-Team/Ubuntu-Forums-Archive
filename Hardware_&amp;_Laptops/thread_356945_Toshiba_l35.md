### Post by Awalton on 2007-03-11
I also have a Toshiba Satellite L35 (S2171) on Edgy; sound works fine through the onboard speakers with both Linux 2.6.15 and 2.6.17, but headphones work with neither. However, headphone detection (mutes the audio when you plug in headphones) works with 2.6.17.

I haven't tried a newer kernel yet, but I intend to upgrade to 2.6.20 to see if there have been any fixes yet. It's not a major annoyance for me, but it's something I would like to have running before I get on a plane.
