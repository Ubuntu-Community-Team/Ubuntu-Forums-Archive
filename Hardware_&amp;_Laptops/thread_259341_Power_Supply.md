### Post by kidders on 2006-09-17
Afaik a 500W power supply would only be required if you were planning on plugging a hell of a lot of hardware into your system (ie more than 4 hard drives, plus more than two optical drives, along with maybe some UV lights and such).

If all you're planning on is, say, a DVD burner, one hard disk and a graphics card with a bit of welly, then 400W or less should be more than sufficient, imho.
