### Post by shijirou on 2007-06-05
Both actually, but you have enough memory, if you want more detailed textures and that extra "oomph" get a more powerful video card.

Regarding your video card, for starters it sounds like you are on AGP. If your motherboard supports PCI-E then that would be th way to go to, otherwise you are stuck with an AGP Video card. Try to look for an Nvida 7300 GT 256mb ddr2 128bit video card. That should be reasonably priced.
