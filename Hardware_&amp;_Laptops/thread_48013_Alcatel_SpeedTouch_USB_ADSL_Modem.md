### Post by dyadya_sam on 2005-07-12
As far as I know, the LiveCD currently does not give you the option to install itself to the harddisk (although it is planned in the future). The LIveCD's basic function is to give you a look around and allow you to make up your mind about installing and no, you can't install firmware. 

What I would suggest, is to get hold of the proper install CD and dual-boot with Windows, and if you don't like ubuntu or can't get your modem up (which you should, anyway, if you follow the HOWTO) you can just go back to Windows or what else have you.If all of you hardware **except** the modem got detected, you're most likely ok and can go ahead.
