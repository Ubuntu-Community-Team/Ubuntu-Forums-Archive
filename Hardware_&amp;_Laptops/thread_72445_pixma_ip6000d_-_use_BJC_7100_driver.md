### Post by jakobc on 2005-10-06
Hi, I got a Canon Pixma ip6000D printer and I found out that I could use the BJC 7100 driver. I have not used it for pictures - but for text documents it works OK.
When you see the list of Canon drivers there are two BJC 7100 deivers:
BJC 7100
BJC-7100
Select the first one (the one without the dash).
I changed the resolution to 1200x600 DPI
I have it configured with my wireless printserver (Dlink AirPlus G)
If you need to do that you must select 'network printer' and 'Unix Printer' and specify your host IP address and the Queue is the port on your printserver where the printer is connected. In my case PS-266F5C-U1
Good luck,
Jakob
