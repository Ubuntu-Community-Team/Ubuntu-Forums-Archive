### Post by overdrank on 2007-12-31
HI and after doing some searching some users have had luck with changing the default depth to 16. 
Example
Section "Screen"
Identifier "Default Screen"
Device "ATI Technologies Inc Radeon Mobility M7 LW [Radeon Mobility 7500]"
Monitor "Generic Monitor"
[COLOR="Red"]Defaultdepth 16[/COLOR]
SubSection "Display"
[COLOR="Red"]Depth 16[/COLOR]
Virtual 1600 1200

And you may find some more help here 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=584439](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=584439)
Along with the previous link for resolution. Good luck!
