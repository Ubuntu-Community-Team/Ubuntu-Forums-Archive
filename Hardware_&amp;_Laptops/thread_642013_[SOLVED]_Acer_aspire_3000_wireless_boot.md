### Post by Presto123 on 2007-12-19
The broadcom 43x is much easier to get in 7.10. It will be listed under restricted drivers. Be ready with a wired connection, and after it loads the stuff from the net, it will give you two options: use an existing file, or download from internet. The internet download will automatically select the driver and install it.

Worked for me, so, I'm happy.
