Yes I would choose the nvidia even if you were asking about a 6200.  There are alot of reasons to not choose the ati card.

1.It's new.  It was just added in the fglrx drivers.
2.ATI has classically had poor open gl performance
3.They don't care about linux users
4.XGL performance is slow and buggy
