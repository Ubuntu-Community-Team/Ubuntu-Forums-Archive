### Post by Onurzaim on 2007-11-06
I am using Pinnacle PCTV Hybrid PRO and DVB channels are just doing great. Regular tv channels arent showing up nice because of the digital tuner but its fine aswell.

The problem you'll experience is you wont get as much comfort as MS operating systems during installation but at the end you'll feel watching tv in linux is much stable.

My other problem is I cant record pal tv shows. I can only record DVB channel shows via Kaffeine.
