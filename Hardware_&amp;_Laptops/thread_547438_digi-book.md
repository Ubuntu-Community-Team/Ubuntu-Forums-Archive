### Post by cubytes on 2007-09-10
I have designed a product to revolutionize the mobile device industry

even before the iphone had come out I knew that touch screen was the way to go, that is why I designed my mobile device with a multi-touch interface well actually a touch screen interface, I had no idea how efficient multi touch gestures could be until the iphone came out!!

my digi-book design is unique because it willl have two displays each about the size of a DVD case, except a lot thinner. The really cool feature I made sure to include was swivel screens so that the device can go from tablet to book/laptop mode.

I originally designed the device to replace text-books, and be the holy grail for mobile e-readers that is because I did not have a clue about multi-touch gestures. even so the more I worked on the design the more I realized that this device can excel where the UMPC has failed.

for starters I want the device to be entirely driven by an open source OS
I want it to be unlocked but definitely carried by a network so you can surf the internet at anytime
 I would suggest Alltell or a third party network of my own design that can piggy back on multiple networks and give free access to any public hotspot for a low monthly rate all data driven with out voice, but I did imagine a VOIP type of service, and of course it will have blue tooth support, edge and quad-band technology as well as the latest and greatest 802.11 standards

the reason I choose the multiple screens is because that is the nature of reading a book, when you read a book your are given two pages to read from left to right, its was just that simple.

I have also designed all kinds of applications for this device all I need is money and I belive this device will be the next big thing since the ipod.......................
