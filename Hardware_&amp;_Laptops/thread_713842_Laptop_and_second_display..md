### Post by Arthur Archnix on 2008-03-03
If you're laptop is anything like mine, then heat will tend to dissipate through the keyboard. I'm not sure how to do what you're seeking to do, I just wanted to warn you to keep an eye on your temperatures if you actually succeed.

### Post by mkammerer on 2008-03-03
Oh, thanks for this warning.

Edit: Problem solved! Just select in the Nvidia settings "Resolution: off" for the lcd panel. Now i can even leave the panel open to prevent overheating.. Very good :)
