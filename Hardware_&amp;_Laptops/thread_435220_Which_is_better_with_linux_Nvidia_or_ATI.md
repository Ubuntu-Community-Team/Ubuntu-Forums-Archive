### Post by jespdj on 2007-05-07
The video driver that ATI has for Linux lacks some features, which makes it difficult or impossible to get things like 3D accelerated graphics working. I recently read an interview somewhere (sorry, don't remember the URL...) with someone from AMD/ATI who explained that they are working on completely rewriting video drivers for Windows Vista, XP and also for Linux, and in the future the drivers should get better, but it didn't say anything about when those new and better drivers would be available.

For now, you'd better get an nVidia graphics card if you want something that works well on Linux.
