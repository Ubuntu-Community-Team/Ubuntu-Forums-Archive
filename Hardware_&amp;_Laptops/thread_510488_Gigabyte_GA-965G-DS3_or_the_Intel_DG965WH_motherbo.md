### Post by tlunde on 2007-07-26
I've just bought a C2DQ6600 and now need to buy a motherboard for it.  I think that I've got the choice down to either the Gigabyte GA-965G-DS3 or the Intel DG965WH boards.   They're both about $115, so price is not the issue.  Both are full ATX motherboards:  I'd like to have as many PCI slots as possible and it's going into at least a seven bay case, so space isn't an issue.  

Can you offer any advice or warnings?

Gigabyte
I've seen warnings of lots of DOA boards and (more worringly) significant issues with RAM.  See, e.g., [http://www.newegg.com/Product/ProductReview.aspx?Item=N82E16813128018](http://www.newegg.com/Product/ProductReview.aspx?Item=N82E16813128018)

Intel
I've seen lots of reports of audio problems with the chipset on this board.  One even claimed that Intel told him that it was a known issue with this board.  

If you have one of these boards, can you tell me if you've been able to get 5.1 audio output from the optical or coax connections?  


If someone thinks I'm being stupid and they have another board to recommend, I'd like to hear about it.  I have a strong preference for Intel integrated graphics for this system (open source drivers and lower heat in the box being the two reasons).  It needs to be able to address as much memory as possible (which is why I'm avoiding the 945 et al chipsets).

If you have either of these boards, I'd be especially grateful for an "everything works fine" or "xxx is still broken" post.  

Thanks!
