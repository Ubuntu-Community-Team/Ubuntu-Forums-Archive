### Post by TechSonic on 2006-01-28
The card you're using will work fine on ESD.  That old of a device had full duplex drivers already setup. No need to worry.  Unless you're on an SB Audigy or SB Live! 24bit, then you may have problems.  But I believe you have SB Live! 5.1 if I'm reading the EMU10K1 part correctly.  Although you could be using Digital (Dell) or standard, not sure.  But far as I see, you're ready to go.  Try playing music from two different applications, such as Totem and XMMS at the same time.. If you hear from both, you're sound is configured correctly.
