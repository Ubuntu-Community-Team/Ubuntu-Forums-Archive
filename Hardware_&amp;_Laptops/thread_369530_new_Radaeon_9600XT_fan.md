### Post by Daveth on 2007-02-24
Both Zalman and Artic Cooling make good gpu coolers which you can fit to your card instead of the stock cooler it came with. This should be quieter too. I fitted a Zalman onto my Nvidia which both dropped the temp and dramatically reduced the noise. 

You might, however, also consider upgrading your case fans and checking the airflow paths inside the case, all of which can crank up the temps if things are not too good inside.
