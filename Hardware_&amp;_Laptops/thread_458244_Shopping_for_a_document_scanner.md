### Post by mujalan on 2007-12-18
I'm trying to go paperless at my office also.  In fact it is almost a necessity.  I have a fujitsu 5110 EOX which was terrific with Windows, and is supposed to be workable with Linux.  I have so far not had success with it on Ubuntu.  PCLinux OS did pick it up and install it so that it works, but it seems pretty slow on PCLOS so far.  For the time being I have resorted to an HPLaserjet 3050 multi-function.  It is not quite as fast as the scansnap and does not do doubleside scanning (so far as I am aware).  It does work pretty well with my Ubuntu/Mint and at a reasonable speed, but "reasonable speed" is of course what is reasonable to me.  I do not personally care for PCLOS for other reasons so I would like to see some improvement in the Ubuntu scanner situation.
