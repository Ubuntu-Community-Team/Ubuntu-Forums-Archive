### Post by mcduck on 2007-01-19
The estimated lifetime for flash memory varies between 10000 and 100000 writes. When used as portable storage that's more than enough, but in hard disk use it's not going to last for more than couple of years. Remember, that this is not the lifetime for the whole disk, but for individual data blocks and even only one block failing means that you loose some data..
