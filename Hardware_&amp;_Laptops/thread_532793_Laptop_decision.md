### Post by tturrisi on 2007-08-23
I just ordered a Dell D830 for $1300.00:
2.0 GHz CPU
2 GB ram
nVidia
80 GB 7200 rpm hd
Dell 1390 wifi

I chose the 1390 wifi because I will be running straight Debian and I never had any issues getting the Broadcom 43xx chipset driver to work.  I'll likely swap out the broadcom for an atheros mini-pci anyway.  On Ubuntu, you may want to get the Intel chipset wifi.

If order a Dell, go to their main page and then select laptops > small business section.  You DO NOT need a business account to purchase from the small business section!  You can purchase as GUEST.  After you config your system & purchase, call the toll free number & select "change order".  Then ask the salesperson about your order and specificall ask if there are any other discounts that were NOT shown on the Web page.  I did this and added an addition 1 GB of ram and ended up paying $140 less than the original purchase!  (she dropped the S+H fee and gave an additional $100.00 discount!)
