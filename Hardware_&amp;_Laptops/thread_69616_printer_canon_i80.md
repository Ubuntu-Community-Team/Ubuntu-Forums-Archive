### Post by David Haas on 2005-10-07
I see no driver for the Canon i80 in Hoary at either the foomatic or gimp-print/4.2 directories. If you can't locate an open-source driver, you could obtain one from TurboPrint. Go to <http://www.turboprint.de/english.html> and select "supported printers," where you'll see the i80 listed.
