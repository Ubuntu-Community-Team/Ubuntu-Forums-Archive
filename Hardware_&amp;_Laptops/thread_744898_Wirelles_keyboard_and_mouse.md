### Post by chewearn on 2008-04-04
> **Kevbert said:**
> How do you set this up ?  I'm using a logitech wireless mouse and keyboard in Gutsy.
Thx in advance.

There is basically no set-up.  You just have to turn on the notification icon, via Preferences > Power Management > General (tab) > Notification Area, select "Always display an icon".

Then, on the Notification Area, left click on it, the battery status should be there.
