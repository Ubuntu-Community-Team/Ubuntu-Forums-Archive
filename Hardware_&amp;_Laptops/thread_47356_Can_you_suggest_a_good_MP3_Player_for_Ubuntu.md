### Post by dataw0lf on 2005-07-08
I'd suggest looking at a sound quality.  For mp3 players, I'd go with the Creative Zen Touch.  HOWEVER, ensure that you don't get one with the MTP firmware update, otherwise you won't be able to use it in Linux.  
iRivers aren't too bad either (and have ogg support, which is my main complaint with the Zen Touch).
