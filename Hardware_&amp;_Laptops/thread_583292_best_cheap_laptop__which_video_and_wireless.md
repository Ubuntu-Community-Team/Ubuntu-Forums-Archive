### Post by Lil on 2007-10-21
I wholely recommend a ThinkPad T40, T41 or T42 as these are now cheap.

I run a now 4 years old vanilla T40, nothing special, no dual core, just a simple Mobility Radeon 7500 and a 'low resolution' 1024x768 screen.

But i tell you, it's a very nice laptop. Slim, durable and fast. Ok, if you're going to do 3D rendering and so forth, maybe not. But for office applications, internet, 2d graphics work--I am not even considering remotely upgrading. Best of all; they work great on Ubuntu.

I would recommend a T40/41/42 with a Radeon 7500, and any of the Intel wireless cards (likely a 2100B or 2200BG) for Ubuntu as this has worked out great on my laptop and many others too. They also have Pentium M processors, mine has a 1.5GHz processor and I have yet to think, "Gee isn't this slow!"
