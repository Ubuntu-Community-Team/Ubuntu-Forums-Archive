### Post by blair on 2007-12-02
I purchased a new HP Pavillion S3220N. A very nice system because it is about as large as a hardcover book. Of course it comes with Windoze preinstalled but my plan is to fix that problem.  I booted off of a Gutsy Gibbon 7.10 Live CD and the mouse and keyboard are completely undetected when using the PC's PS2 mouse and keyboard ports.  I tried several different PS2 mouses and keyboards to no avail.

Fortunately, the PC does have multiple USB ports and I did have USB mice and keyboard.  USB connections worked fine.  So I have a working system, but was suprised to find Gutsy does not support the PS2 peripherals on this brand new PC. Obviously, HP is not without blame here either, as they could have included this in their device testing and contributed to the open source community as necessary to ensure PS2 peripherals work across their current product line. Shame on you HP for not including Linux in your QA process.
