Greetings ECted,

Since you didn't provide a link to the HP9000 thread, I can only make two suggestions at this time. If you provide more info, I may have more ideas:

1. Disable legacy USB support in your computer's bios. Back in the early days of Fedora Core Linux (several years ago), I had to disable legacy USB in order for my HP laptop to even load the installer.

2. Try the latest alpha release of Ubuntu 7.10 (Gusty Gibbon). You can find that [here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/testing").
