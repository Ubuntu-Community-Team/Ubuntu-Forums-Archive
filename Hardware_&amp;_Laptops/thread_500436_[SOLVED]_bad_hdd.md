### Post by w4ett on 2007-07-13
Sounds like a hardware issue...try changing out the ribbon cable (I've had them go bad, or be DOA from the package.  Does the Bios on your motherboard Have S.M.A.R.T. capability on bootup?...If it's disabled, turn it on and let it test your drives.

Intermittent faults can be from a failing Motherboard or Controller Card too..don't overlook these possibilities...Also check the Power Supply....could be the +12V supply is running on the low side....

Intermittent faults are the worst to troubleshoot.....sometimes it takes swapout of the items to find it....

There are some Linux based testing suites out there:

[http://ltp.sourceforge.net/tooltable.php](http://ltp.sourceforge.net/tooltable.php)

Any other questions...just ask...Good Luck!
