### Post by Intertricity on 2007-03-07
If you're looking to upgrade the RAM on your laptop, or even find out what ram you're using, you may wonder why there's only one RAM stick on the bottom of your computer.

It turns out that there really are two RAM sticks in the zv6000, but the second one is somewhere else. I came cross this handy website to get to the second RAM stick, which is located under the keyboard, so have a screwdriver ready.

[http://www.dinarius.com/060913pavil.html](http://www.dinarius.com/060913pavil.html)

Also, when putting RAM in, if you're are new to laptops as I am, remember that you push in at a diagonal angle and then bring it down. I was using this computer with half my ram for weeks without realizing I only had 256mb of memory.
