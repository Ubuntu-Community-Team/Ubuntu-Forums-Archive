### Post by slush1000 on 2007-07-01
Any MATX power supply will do. I would go with one that is minimum 350w. You shouldnt need anything higher then 450w since this is a mid-end machine that wont be running any additional high-end video cards. So the higher cost of higher wattage is not justified for your purposes. Depending on what you want you should be able these for between $25 and $60.
In the past I have just bought a $50 tower with a power supply, never had a problem yet.
