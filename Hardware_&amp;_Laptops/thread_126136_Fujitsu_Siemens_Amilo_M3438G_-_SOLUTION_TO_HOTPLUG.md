### Post by UK31337 on 2006-02-05
I found a good workaround on an Asus support page regarding certain laptops hanging on "Starting hotplug subsystem..." when starting Ubuntu 5.10

The Asus solution works on the FS Amilo M3438G also (I've got this laptop, I've tried it and it works):

When "Starting hotplug subsystem..." appears, press Alt-SysRq-E once.  The harddrive light will light for a few seconds.

Press Alt-SysRq-E again.  The HDD light will come on again, and after a few seconds, the Ubuntu logo will disappear, a blinking cursor will appear in the top right of the screen, and the machine boots as normal.

Hope this helps - it's been a big problem for a lot of people, including myself.
