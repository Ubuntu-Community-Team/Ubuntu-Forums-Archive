### Post by FuturePilot on 2008-03-29
I used to have a freezing problem on my old laptop. Turns out it was caused by some issue between the Nvidia driver and powernowd. I disabled powernowd and the freezing stopped. Apparently the Nvidia driver was causing lockups when the CPU would switch frequencies. With powernowd disabled I get no more lockups, however my CPU is now constantly running full throttle. :neutral:
