I assume you have Ubuntu 7.10?

Try going to System -> Preferences -> Sound -> Default Mixer Tracks. Select whatever the Intel option is (unless you have some other soundcard but most laptops don't) and highlight PCM. Then, open the Volume Control by right-clicking on the speaker icon on the right side of the top panel (menu bar). Make sure Speakers is among the checked items.

If it isn't a driver issue this could be the problem.
