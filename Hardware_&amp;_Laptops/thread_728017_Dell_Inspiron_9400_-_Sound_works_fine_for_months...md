### Post by kerekesb on 2008-03-24
You do not have to upgrade or reinstall anything. I had the same problem... Just go to "Volume control" and select SigmaTel device in "File" -> "Change device settings". Check the SigmaTel device preferences. In my computer the main volume control had different settings in left side and right side.
