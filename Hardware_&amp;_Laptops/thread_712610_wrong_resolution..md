### Post by Bri0 on 2008-03-02
Go edit the xorg.conf file and add you res manually.

1.  In Terminal:  sudo gedit  /etc/X11/xorg.conf

3.  Where you see the line that looks something like this,  

Modes		"1440x1440"	"1440x900"	"1280x1024"	"1280x960"

4.  Remove all resolutions except your res.

So this is what the line should look like after but with your res in the " "

Modes		"1440x900"

5.  Save & Restart
