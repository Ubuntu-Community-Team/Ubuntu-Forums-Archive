### Post by jinx099 on 2006-10-29
I ahve the Smasung ML-2010 and I can say that it works great in Ubuntu and all recent distros I've tried.  The only thing that does not work in linux that does in windows is the double sided printing.  But it does work right out of the box in both dapper and edgy.  

The only weird thing is that in dapper (and some other distros) you have to pick the ml-200 driver.  I assume this is a typo, or that they mean ml-200X series or something.  However in Edgy, the driver is recognized as ml-2010.
