### Post by bookofjoshua on 2007-03-09
I purchased the 6233se when they had it for 700 ALMOST the same laptop.  What you need to do is right click on My Computer and select manage then select hardware and find the webcam.  Right click on it select uninstall and when is done reboot.  it will load the correct drivers then.  It did the same thing to me and at one point I had one in the device manager list that was working fine and another that had a issue.  Now I have no idea how it came to recognize two cam but I unistalled the one and it worked fine after that.  Good luck.:guitar:
