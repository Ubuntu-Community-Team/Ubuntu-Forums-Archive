### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-04-12
Your motherboard supports wake on usb power.  To disable the lighting you have to go into the bios and turn the feature off.  One problem with that, some manufacturers remove that specific feature from the motherboard.  If that's the case, a solution would be to connect the keyboard and mouse to an unpowered hub and just disconnect the hub from the computer.
