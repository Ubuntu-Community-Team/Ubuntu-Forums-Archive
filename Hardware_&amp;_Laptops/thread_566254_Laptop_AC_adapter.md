### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-10-03
You need three things to match:

Voltage:  14.4VDC or 12VDC or something else?  Should be same or slightly higher.
Amperage:  2 amps or greater?  Should be the same or higher.
Plug:  Make sure it fits and has the proper polarity.  Plus on the inside and Negative on the outside--or is it a reverse polarity plug?

Your old one burned out, so 1.5 amps is not enough.  Find something with 2 amps or greater.
