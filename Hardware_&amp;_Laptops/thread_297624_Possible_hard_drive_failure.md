### Post by jhenager on 2006-11-20
If it is a Maxtor or Western Digital, both those companies offer a free diagnostic program for their drives. The WD utility (Data Lifeguard Tools) will work with other manufacturers, but they have a disclaimer to warn you that YMMV.
I would start there. If problems exist at the hardware level, all the software in the world won't fix it.
