### Post by ltmon on 2006-06-16
Most laptops have the GPU soldered directly into the mainboard.  Even though it is a dedicated unit, replacing it is nigh impossible.  Some large desktop replacements allow it, but mostly not.

You may look at replacing your motherboard, but with a laptop that is still a task best undertaken by a professional.
