### Post by Borbus on 2008-01-09
The Nvidia linux drivers list the 7100 GS as supported: [http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86_64/169.07/README/appendix-a.html](http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86_64/169.07/README/appendix-a.html) I think the drivers support all Nvidia cards anyway.

The Realtek audio and ethernet will also work fine with no extra configuration needed in Ubuntu.
