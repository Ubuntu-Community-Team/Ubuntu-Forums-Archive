Have you tried cycling the battery down to empty, then recharging it (with the laptop off), then repeating that? I've done that in the past with some machines and it seemed to help, but I don't know that it would work.

You might also poke around in [this forum]("http://www.notebookforums.com/showthread.php?t=71850&highlight=PCG-FX215") to see if anyone has power supply or battery issues with that model. I was troubleshooting a friend's Vaio for a while that had memory issues, and I found a solution through there.
