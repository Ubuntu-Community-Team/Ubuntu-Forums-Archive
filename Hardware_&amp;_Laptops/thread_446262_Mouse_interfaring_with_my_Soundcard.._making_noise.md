### Post by Ken_Lewis81 on 2007-05-17
This is noise from your mainboard which leaks into the sound.  It happens to pretty-much all on-board cards and is the primary reason for getting a separate sound card.

You can ususally drop it to undetectable levels by turning everything down a lot. Muting the sound channels you aren't using right away should also help.

Take care.
Ken.
