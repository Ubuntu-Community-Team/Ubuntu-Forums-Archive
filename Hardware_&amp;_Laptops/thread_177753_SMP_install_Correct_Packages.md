### Post by MystaMax on 2006-05-31
Ok, it ended up having less to do w/ the kernel and more to do w/ the video drivers for my Radeon 7000 VE card. I changed the following in my xorg.conf file:

Section "Device"
        Identifier      "ATI Technologies, Inc. Radeon 7000 VE (RV100 QY)"
**         Driver          "ati"**
        BusID           "PCI:11:13:0"
EndSection

[SIZE=4]TO

[/SIZE] Section "Device"
         Identifier      "ATI Technologies, Inc. Radeon 7000 VE (RV100 QY)"
**         Driver          "vesa"**
         BusID           "PCI:11:13:0"
 EndSection


I believe this will not allow me to use 3D acceleration and things of that nature, which is ok, b/c the server primary role is a VMware server. 

Here is a link to [launchpad.net]("https://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-driver-ati/+bug/16873") where someone submitted a bug w/ breezy and the Radeon 7000 card.
