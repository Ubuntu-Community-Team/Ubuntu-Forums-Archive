### Post by David Haas on 2005-06-16
According to Linuxprinting.org, Hewlett-Packard and Epson printers are strong considerations for Linux users. I intalled an HP Deskjet 5740 in Ubuntu Hoary  a month ago easily through the Gnome Printer Manager. It's a current model, selling for about $90 in the U.S. I can't compare it to other brands/models of similar price, but I'm happy with it, for it prints black text quickly and quietly and also prints color photos quickly and satisfactorily for me on plain paper.
