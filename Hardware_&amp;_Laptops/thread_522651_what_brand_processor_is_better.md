### Post by davidsrsb on 2007-08-11
There is no single answer
I have both Core2Duo 2x1.86GHz and an AMD X2  2x2.6GHz desktops

Under both Ubuntu and XP the AMD is much faster for general purpose software.
The Intel is quieter
Intel runs multimedia programs faster

What matters far more for laptops is the graphics card and Ubuntu compatibility of built in peripherals like Sound, WiFi and card readers
