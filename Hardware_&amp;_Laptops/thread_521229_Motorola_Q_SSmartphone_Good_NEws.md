I wasn't real sure where to post this but its 6 Am and I'm tired. After weeks of looking for ways to get my motorola q up and running on my linux box. and fighting with synce. I decided to go back to square one with maybe addons to my phone. I came Back across a little cab file called WMSTORAGE. which can be found at 

[http://www.modaco.com/WM5torage-t238545.html](http://www.modaco.com/WM5torage-t238545.html)

Basically what it does is makes your Moto Q readable as a flash sd card instead of a smart device. 

No extra drivers needed no synce software nothing I plugged it right in and shazam it showed up on my desktop.
 
I am using Gutsy at the moment so I'm not sure if it woulda worked on my edgy but I dont see why not.
 you must have an sd card for it to work my phone plugged in as a 2gb thumb drive. Hope this is helpful to some people.


Side note 

I'm not sure how the Q is setup from other carriers but I am a sprint Tech  in the call center and I will tell you this much you will need to go to your Settings menu then to connections and then USB to PC and makwe sure the box is UNCHECKED.

STAR>>>SETTINGS>>>CONNECTIONS>>>USB TO PC>>> BOX MUST BE EMPTY

if it isnt the ubuntu box will read it as rndis and not usb  and it MUST be usb

EDIT i forgot t omention this works for all WM5 devices.
