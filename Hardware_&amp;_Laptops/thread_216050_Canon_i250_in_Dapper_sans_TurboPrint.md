### Post by Jesterday on 2006-07-14
I just got my Canon i250 working in Dapper without using TurboPrint. I had all of the dependancy problems everyone else ran into, but I just downloaded all of the necessary packages from [http://www.debian.org/distrib/packages](http://www.debian.org/distrib/packages)

I got all of the necessary packages from there, except xlibs. so I downloaded and installed the xlibs package from Breezy, and Voila, it works...slow as all hell, but it works.

I followed the How-to from [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=126063&highlight=i250](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=126063&highlight=i250)

And used some of my very basic knowledge and now it works.
