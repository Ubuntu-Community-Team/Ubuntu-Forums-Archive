### Post by lucy_liu_lover on 2007-08-08
When I installed my Dell 24" lcd screen, even with the Nvidia driver installed I couldn't get my monitor's native resolution. I edited the xorg.conf file and added my monitor's resolution (made a backup copy first, of course!)

In each Depth just type in your monitor'r resolution (in my case 1900x1220)

_________________________________________________________________________________

Identifier	"Default Screen"
	Device		"nVidia Corporation NV18 [GeForce4 MX 440 AGP 8x]"
	Monitor		"VM71RDA"
	Defaultdepth	24
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth	1
		Modes		"1900x1220"    "800x600"	"1024x768"	"640x480"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth	4
		Modes		"1900x1220"    "800x600"	"1024x768"	"640x480"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth	8
		Modes		"1900x1220"    "800x600"	"1024x768"	"640x480"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth	15
		Modes		"1900x1220"    "800x600"	"1024x768"	"640x480"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth	16
		Modes		"1900x1220"    "800x600"	"1152x864"	"1024x768"	"640x480"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth	24
		Modes	        "1900x1220"      "800x600_75.00" "640x480_75.00" "832x624_75.00" "1024x768_75.00" 
	EndSubSection
EndSection

______________________________________________________________________________

Save the new file and re-start x-server.

### Post by milton1 on 2007-08-08
You don't need to change the identifier, as long as the references within xorg.conf are consistant. However, you may want to make sure the HorizSync and VertRefresh values are correct for your new monitor.
