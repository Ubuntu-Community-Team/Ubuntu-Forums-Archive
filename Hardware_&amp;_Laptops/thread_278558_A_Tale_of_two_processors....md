### Post by David Corrales on 2006-10-16
Up until the core 2 duo, AMD is ahead of Intel in similar processor lines. Also, AMD chips require a lot less power, therefore they run cooler.
Ghz speed doesn't matter a lot these days. For example a core 2 duo with 1.86ghz can beat that AMD easily. Regarding both processors you're interested in, the Athlon kicks the crap out of the Intel one.
