### Post by Chipmaster on 2007-12-22
I own the aforementioned laptop.  With Gusty, there are two main problems wireless and display.  I have made a post that shows my process for getting the wireless to work here:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=3994989](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=3994989).  So there's one down.

 I'm still looking into the video problem, which really isn't crippling.  I have screenshots of the problem posted at the link above.

Other than that, I have had no problems with it.  Sound worked by default along with the buttons on the laptop.  The Fn shortcuts seem to work.  I did mess with the BIOS before installing, but I'm not sure it was necessary as I didn't try before I tinkered.

The laptop is decently powered and if I can get some help fixing the display issues, it should be a great linux machine.
