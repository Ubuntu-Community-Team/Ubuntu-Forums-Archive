We'll I've "fixed" my wireless wo's.  I say "fixed" because I made a mistake when I ordered my wife and my laptop and accidentally got the 1390 mini-wireless card.  I played around with this card for a little and got it working with wpa but wasn't very happy (especially with the range with my D820).  So I ordered 2 of the Intel 3945 cards like I should of done from the beginning.  Install of both of these only took about 15-20min total from start to finish.  I  install edgy (k)ubuntu and working 100% right out of "the box".  I haven't gotten into wpa yet but no security is working just fine.  Plus when I was in there I noticed the black wire that connects to the wireless card was almost off....hence poor reception.  I haven't gotten around to installing linux on my wifes machine but this card has already proved itself much better than the 1390.
