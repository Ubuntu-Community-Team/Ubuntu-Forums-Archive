### Post by Bartender on 2007-12-24
I know what you mean.  It's scary to plunk down your money without knowing whether it's gonna work or not.  Keep a close eye on this forum - there are some threads with reports from owners.
After watching this forum for a long long time, I bought an Acer 5920 (not the "G" version) a coupla weeks ago because it was on sale at Circuit City and I'd read one or two posts saying it worked.
Not quite true.  The 5920 comes with a new Intel wireless chipset (4965) that is not fully supported in 7.10.  Apparently connect speeds can be slow, and sometimes the device disappears from the "Network" list entirely.  
Only the right hand laptop chassis speaker works.  Left channel dead.  Headphones sound fine but don't kill the chassis speakers.

I can try to fix these problems with the help of forum members or wait for next version of Ubuntu, which will undoubtedly address the wireless and probly the speakers. 

Check out the [Laptop Testing]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam") pages
