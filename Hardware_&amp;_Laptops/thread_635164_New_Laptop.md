### Post by FreewheelinFrank on 2007-12-08
You could always take a live CD into the store to try out- make sure the sound card works and that that the laptops shuts down and turns itself off. (Mine doesn't).

Do some research on the graphics card before you buy- you should be able to find out if it works with Ubuntu.

Same goes for the laptop and model- if it's a major brand and has been out for a while, Google should tell you if it runs Ubuntu or not.

Basically, anything with those combinations.  I've always had great luck with Dell's lineup of laptops, btw.
