I have an answer to question number 2. I had the same problem with my keyboard. Check out System->Preferences->Keyboard Shortcuts, you will see that under the "Sound" Portion on the left, there are the functions that you would like to get working. Just click next to them in the shortcut portion of the window then press your desired keyboard button. You can also have functions with a series of buttons pressed down at once if you would like as well. 

Hope this helps, good luck.
