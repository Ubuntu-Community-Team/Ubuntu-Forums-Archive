### Post by josephus on 2007-04-06
I run a Thinkpad R51 on Edgy Eft since it's release, and ran a T30 on Dapper from July through September when I dropped and killed the laptop.

Looking at the specs of R40 (and depending which model you buy), it looks like we are running similar hardware.  On the R51 I'm running
Pentium M 1.5
Radeon Mobility 7500
IBM 11a/b/g Wireless LAN Mini PCI Adapter

I haven't run into any issues with either system.  The graphics card is not the best but has not caused me any pain. 

[http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Category:R40](http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Category:R40) is a good source of information for thinkpad and has a lot of linux related information.

If you can, try to get a Pentium M cpu rather than a celeron or pentium 4 M.

### Post by battyice on 2007-04-13
I run Edgy on a thinkpad R40 with a 2.2g pentium 4m, and the 1024x768 15" LCD.

Everything worked perfectly right out of the box.  The built-in wireless works, volume control buttons work, suspend works, and the default xorg.conf worked fine.  

Power management seems to work great also.  Mine was an ebay purchase, and I couldn't be happier.
