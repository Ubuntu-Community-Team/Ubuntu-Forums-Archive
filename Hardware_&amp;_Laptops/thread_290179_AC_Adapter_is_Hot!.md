### Post by blind0wl on 2006-10-31
AC Adaptors are designed to remove heat, sometimes dependant on the weather and where your actually sitting will determine how effective the adapter is at removing heat.  As a test, you can check the heat on the AC Adapter over an hour period running Ubuntu...switch to Windows and run the same test for an hour after letting the adapter cool.

You may also find that your doing more intensive CPU activity and if your laptop can change CPU speeds as part of its power saving, it may be drawing more power during these times.
