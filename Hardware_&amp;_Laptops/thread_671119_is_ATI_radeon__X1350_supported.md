Well it is fully supported with 3d acceleration with highest resolution and all the other common goodies yes. However, it causes your computer to lock up and freeze when you try to sleep or hibernate the laptop. And the crappy thing is that the highest resolution you can have without using their restricted drivers is a 1024x768 resolution (as far as I can figure out how to do on mine anyway) so I opt to just "blank screen" instead of suspend on lid down and stuff. And I power off instead of hibernate because it is just annoying to have the system lock up because of that video card driver.
