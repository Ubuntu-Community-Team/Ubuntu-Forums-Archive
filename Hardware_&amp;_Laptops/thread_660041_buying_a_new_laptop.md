### Post by bowenmark on 2008-01-06
Bought an HP Pavillion DV9000 with Intel that meets those specs closely (5400rpm drive, 250GB with dual core etc.) for $849 on Xmas 2007, new models are out from HP after the holiday iirc...everything works except for some multimedia (DVD, mp4 etc. is "choppy" still, Flash is okay on the web) but should get that sussed out with some more digging around on this forum.

Big gotcha though, even in Windows (am dual booting vista) media would not play and first had to [start up once and configure the "Quick Play" program]("http://blogs.technet.com/robert_hensing/archive/2008/01/02/random-stuff-from-the-last-2-weeks.aspx") so would not recommend this machine for anything but a dual boot unless you have the knowledge to flash the bios.
