### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-03-22
It arrived! And wow, its everything I expected and more. Quite unbelievable for the price...

I need to tweak my conky colours a bit and maybe the fonts, but all I did going from CRT to TFT was to comment out my refresh rates in xorg.conf, uncomment Option "DPMS" and added 1440x900 to my screen section. If you are looking for a cheap widescreen monitor in the UK, I can heartily recommend this monitor. I got it from PC Global Direct for under £125 including delivery.
