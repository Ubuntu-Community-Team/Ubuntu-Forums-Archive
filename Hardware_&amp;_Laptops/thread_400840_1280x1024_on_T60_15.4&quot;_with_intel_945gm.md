### Post by jjordan on 2007-04-04
1280 X 1024 is a 5:4 resolution NOT widescreen (except on some funky plasma tvs).

A quick look at the data sheet for the T60 indicates that there are two different 15.4" displays
WXGA (1280x800) resolution and WSXGA+ (1680x1050) resolution.  If you have the 1280X800 model then it will look best at 1280X800 and you don't need to do anything.  If you have the 1680X1050 model you will need a combination of 915resolution and modifications to your xorg.conf file to get it working.
