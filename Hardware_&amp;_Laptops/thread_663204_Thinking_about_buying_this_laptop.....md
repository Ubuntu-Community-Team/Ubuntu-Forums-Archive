### Post by jimrz on 2008-01-09
neither your link nor the product page @ toshiba.com specify which atheros chipset, but toshiba's does mention Super G which means it is probably the Atheros 5212. You might want to check further and see if you can nail it down.  I have 2 laptops using this chipset (one onboard + 1 pcmia card) and it works "out of the box" with ubuntu and handles unsecured/WEP/WPA connections without problem. Though a lot of products with this chipset do not mention this, it also supports 802.11a.
