### Post by ShamusPatt on 2007-08-22
If you're using Ubuntu, and not Kubuntu, then you might prefer the Gnome conduits that come with gnome-pilot .  The Gnome conduits can also tie into Evolution so you can keep your contacts and calendar synchronized between Evolution and your Palm. It's not as well integrated as I'd like - it doesn't associate categories, for example - but it mostly works.

You just need to put the pilot applet onto the Gnome panel somewhere (right-click, Add To Panel, look under Utilities for Pilot Applet). The applet should lead you through the setup. 

Some of the setup can also be found under System / Preferences in the Gnome menus. Look for PalmOS Devices and Removable Drives and Media [PDA] tab.
