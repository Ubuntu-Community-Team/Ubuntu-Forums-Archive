### Post by ernestoongaro on 2006-05-01
I bought this laptop for under $1000 and it works perfect with Ubuntu Breezy and Dapper. 
WLAN works, video works, Cpu scaling works (just tpye in sudo modprobe p4-clockmod), sleep works (no suspend but looks easy to fix), great battery life, sound works, touch pad works. Everything works great out of the box!
If anyone is interested in getting a linux supported laptop the VAIO VGN-FS810 /W is a pretty good choice.
