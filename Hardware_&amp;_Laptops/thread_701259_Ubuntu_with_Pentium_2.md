### Post by HereInOz on 2008-02-19
If you use Xubuntu, and you are patient, then it will work.  However, you would be better with something like Damn Small Linux, or even Puppy Linux, on a machine of that specification.

Another 128MB RAM would help, if you can get it in there, but Damn Small Linux and Puppy will do OK with the specs you have.
