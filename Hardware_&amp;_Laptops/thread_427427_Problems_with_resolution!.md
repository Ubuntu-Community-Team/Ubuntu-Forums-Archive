### Post by gjohn2 on 2007-04-29
have you tried changing the drivers? my resolution was fixed when i switched to the 'intel' driver for our video cards.

i would try changing to the 'intel' video driver through synaptic then
edit you xorg.conf (backup it up first of course.) and change

Section "Device"
Identifier "Intel Corporation Mobile 945GM/GMS/940GML Express Integrated Graphics Controller"
Driver "[COLOR="Red"]i810[/COLOR]"
BusID "PCI:0:2:0"
EndSection

to this 

Section "Device"
Identifier "Intel Corporation Mobile 945GM/GMS/940GML Express Integrated Graphics Controller"
Driver "intel[COLOR="Red"][/COLOR]"
BusID "PCI:0:2:0"
EndSection
