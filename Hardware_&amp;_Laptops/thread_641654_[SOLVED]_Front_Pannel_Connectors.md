### Post by TheLions on 2007-12-15
power switch put jpf2 on 5 and 6 (polarity not important)

power led put on jpf2 on 2 and 3 (+ on 2)

restart on jpf2 on 13 and 14 (polarity not important)

hdd led  on jpf2 on 1 and 8 (+ on 1)

sleep switch ??? never heard about that!
