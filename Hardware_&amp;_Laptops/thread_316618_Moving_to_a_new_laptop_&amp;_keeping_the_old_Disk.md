### Post by kingcharles1666 on 2006-12-11
Best thing you can do is to backup your complete home folder and resore that on your new laptop. Just make sure that your user id is the same on both.

i would advise installing from scratch first. make sure you create a seperate partition for your home folder. You will need to re-install any non standard packages though. Automatix will help there.

Alternatively, if you currently have a seperate home partition you can re-install the root system (after physically installing the drive in the new machine). But maybe a fresh drive is nice also. these laptop drives do take a beating in their life.

good luck charles
