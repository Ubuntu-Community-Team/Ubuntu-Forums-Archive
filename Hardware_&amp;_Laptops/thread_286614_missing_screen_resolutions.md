### Post by draeath on 2006-10-28
Problem resolved. The refresh timings detected by X were completely wrong. After fighting will Dell (because for some reason my screen doesn't have a model number) I finally found the information.

FYI, if anyone has a dell 17" CRT, its likely an E773c


Correcting the refresh timings did the trick.
