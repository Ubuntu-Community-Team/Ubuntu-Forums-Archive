### Post by moore.bryan on 2006-10-12
*i would suggest using gparted... livecd is wickedly good, but the run-of-the-mill one in gnome should be fine for your external.  just make sure the external is connected, run gparted, and in the top right-hand corner will be all your connected devices.  choose your external (usb's are usually sdx, where x is a sequential letter) and you'll be given a bar graph of the drive now.  right-click that and you'll see some straight-forward choices.  if you get lost, post back for help...*
