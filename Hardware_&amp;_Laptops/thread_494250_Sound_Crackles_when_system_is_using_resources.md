### Post by spamking2000 on 2008-05-05
Hey - Don't know if you ever fixed this or not, but I noticed the unanswered thread from long ago... as it turns out for me, mine was due to my TV card picking up static from all the various electronics in the general area (especially my HDD). That had a line going from the TV card externally from the case to the Line In on my sound card. Once I muted that, the problem was gone.
