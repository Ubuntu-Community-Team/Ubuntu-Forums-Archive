### Post by tomdkat on 2008-01-12
Well, it turns out the problem I had booting Ubuntu 7.10 on this laptop was due to bad RAM.  Once I replaced the RAM, Ubuntu booted fine as did Knoppix 5.1.1 (consistently).  Ubuntu even supported the wireless adapter without problems!  There were some video issues but since I was running the live CD, I didn't care much about them.  :)
