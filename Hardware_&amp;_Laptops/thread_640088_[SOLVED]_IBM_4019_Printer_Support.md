### Post by Jason.Kenedy on 2007-12-14
Sef,

Thanks a million, that had the answer.

As a shortcut for anyone else posed with this unique challenge, use the recommended Ubuntu driver (IBM 4019 Foomatic/laserjet) but when powering on your printer, you MUST hold down the blue "Font" button on the front panel to force the print into HP emulation mode.  As long as you remember to do this, you're in good shape.

Not ideal, but extends the investment in a great old workhorse of a laser printer. :)
