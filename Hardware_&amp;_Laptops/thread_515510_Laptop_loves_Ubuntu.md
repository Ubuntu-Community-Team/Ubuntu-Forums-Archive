### Post by waldorsockbat on 2007-08-02
I have read a lot of post about how laptops hate Ubuntu which is why I decided to post this.
My last post was the day after Feisty came out I think. since then I have converted my wifes Toshiba Satellite A105-1014  to Feisty/Xp dual boot but she only uses XP for a couple games that wine just wont run, she is booted into feisty 95% of the time and she" loves it like a fat kid loves cake":lolflag:  The only problem I have had so far is getting compiz running because of the ATI mobo in this lappy but even that is working perfectly now, and for some reason feisty was trying to use the modem as the sound card but after running the updates that fixed its self.Wireless worked out of the box, with envy ATI driver was no problem. Everything just works like it should with way less problems than XP. I have also set up my old P4 2.4ghz desktop for my daughter that is Feisty only and my water cooled gaming behemoth is now a XP/Mint dual boot on separate hds that I still have to be in XP most of the time cause there is still no X-fi driver yet.. any way enough gushing about Ubuntu, I will be happy to help anyone with a simmilar laptop that I can

P.S. Keep your head up Ubuntu will run on lappys just fine with a little effort here and there.
