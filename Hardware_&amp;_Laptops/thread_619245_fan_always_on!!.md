### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-11-21
With the temperature you are getting even with the fans on, it would be suicide for your mainboard to turn off the fans, you can do that through the BIOS, but I believe that option kicks in only when the CPU and other temperatures are at a low enough temperature. 

In any case, DO NOT turn off your fans unless you use a better a cooling system such as a liquid cooled one instead of the conventional air types.

There isn't much of a tool for this from my knowledge, but a good way to ensure that the CPU temperature remains quite stable is by keeping all of the vents free of obstructions and dust.

### Post by smartbei on 2007-11-21
I had a similar problem. In the bios there is an option that is something like "Temperature to turn on fans." On mine this was too low (40) so my fans were on a lot. Check it out, perhaps that will solve your problem.

If you want to monitor more closely the speeds of your fans, temperature, voltage, etc. You may want to install lm-sensors. Search the forums - there are HowTos floating around.
