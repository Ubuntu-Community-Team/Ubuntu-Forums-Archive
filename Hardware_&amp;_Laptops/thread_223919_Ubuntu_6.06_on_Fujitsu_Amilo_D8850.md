### Post by Jaxilian on 2006-07-27
I got Ubuntu 6.06 Dapper to work on a Fujitsu Amilo D8850.
The computer runs very fast with Ubuntu. There is some tweaking to make all of it work, but it's easy with the guide on: [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Dapper](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Dapper)

Here are a few things that I thought is important to get working.

*3D acceleration on ATi Radeon 9000 mobility - works
*Sound - works
*DVD playback - works
*MPEG/AVI playback - works (divx/xvid)
*USB support - works

*Burn CDs - not tested yet (will test tonight)
*TV OUT - not tested yet (need to buy a cable) 

If any have the same exact model and wanna add something, please feel free to do so.
