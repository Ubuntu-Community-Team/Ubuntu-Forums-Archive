### Post by daniels on 2005-02-28
I have the A8N-SLI Deluxe and it's very nice, except the onboard sound is total crap.  Buy it and an Audigy as well, or something.

The standard ALSA driver doesn't initialise SPDIF; the hacked-up ALSA driver Asus provide to fix SPDIF doesn't work with SPDIF either, and even under XP, the SPDIF output is a direct mirror of the front analogue channels.  So if you have everything set to 5.1, and you start playing HalfLife 2, the speech will cut out when you tilt your head 1 degree.  Lame.
