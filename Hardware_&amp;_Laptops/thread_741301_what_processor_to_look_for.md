> **android6011 said:**
> I'm going to be getting a laptop, and as far as the processor goes what specs should i try to stay above? Any specific processors to look for? I'm going to be in the 500-750 range for this particular computer.

    Well Ubuntu will run on any decent hardware. I would say if you can go for Intel Core 2 as reviews show it to out shine AMD counterparts. Next I would say go for 2GB of ram as it is dirt cheap nowadays.  Next make sure whatever graphics card you get is going to work well with Ubuntu. Some work right away while others you have to play with to get your native resolution. Last but not least and this has always drove me crazy is the WIRELESS thats built in to the laptop. Check with other users here to see what is compatible. Very few laptops have just worked out of the box "wireless" with ubuntu for me.  But for the price you mentioned I'm sure there is multiple options. Good luck and let us no what you get!
