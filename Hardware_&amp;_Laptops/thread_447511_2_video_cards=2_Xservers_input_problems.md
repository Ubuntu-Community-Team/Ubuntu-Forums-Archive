### Post by lej on 2007-05-24
hi labatazza,

sounds like an interesting project!  i don't know exactly what your problem might be... but just a few words of advice.  you've probably already looked at this page, i imagine:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MultiseatX?highlight=%28multiseat%29](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MultiseatX?highlight=%28multiseat%29)

In it, a "dummy" X server is recommended.  Another recommendation is to keep the xorg.conf as bare bones as possible--adding to it a little at a time to see where the problem is.  Finally, i was running multiseat on 6.06 with no problems and then started encountering problems when i upgraded to 7.04 (not sure why?).  So, i've since gone back to using 6.06.  good luck!
