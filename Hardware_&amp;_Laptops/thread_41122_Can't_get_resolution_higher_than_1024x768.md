### Post by Xian on 2005-06-12
[QUOTE=Psycs]Section "Monitor"
Identifier "SyncMaster"
Option "DPMS"
EndSection[/QUOTE]
This is a problem. You don't have any sync/refresh rates in that section.
Look up your monitor's specs and add them manually.
You might also include the display size. Here's a [How-To](http://www.advogato.org/person/robertc/diary.html?start=35)

Here is my Monitor Section:
```
Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"COMPAQ 7500"
	Option		"DPMS"
        HorizSync       30-70
        VertRefresh     50-140
        DisplaySize     312 234
EndSection
```
