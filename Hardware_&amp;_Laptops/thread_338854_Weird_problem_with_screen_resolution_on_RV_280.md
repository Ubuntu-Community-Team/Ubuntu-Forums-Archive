### Post by Troels Nybo on 2007-01-15
I have got a really weird problem with the screen resolution in Ubuntu 6.06. When I first installed it on this particular computer with ATI RV 280 (Radeon 9200) I could not get the resolution any higher than 800 x 600. I looked for drivers offered by Ubuntu and found and downloaded one for ATI video cards.

This did indeed help! I was able to raise the screen resolution to 1024 x 768 which was enough for me. But the next day the problem was back! Since then I have not been able to raise the screen resolution to more than 800 x 600.

I have tried to uninstall and reinstall the driver a couple of times. Didn't work.

---

### Post by Troels Nybo on 2007-01-17
Seems that I solved the problem without really knowing how, or understanding the exact nature of it. During physical maintenance I unplugged several cables. After reconnecting and restart the high resolution worked again!
