### Post by l.tambiah on 2005-05-16
Hello,

I am currently using Soundblaster Audigy2 ZS sound card using 7.1 sound. I have not tweaked anything, and all was working from install.  I'm glad to say the sound is excellent quality.

I'm using XMMS and Xine which are both outputting 7.1 cinematic sound. Just make sure before you listen to sound  type "alsamixer" into the console and unmute all of the channels. Also ensure all the channels are up to a reasonable level. This is required as Alsamixer is usually muted by default. Hope this helps..... :wink:
