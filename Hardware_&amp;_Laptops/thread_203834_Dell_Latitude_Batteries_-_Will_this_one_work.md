### Post by Peter Cannon on 2006-06-26
Whats the MODEL designation of your Dell?

Do you have a removable drive? You should be able to fit battery 'S' in where the CD/DVD/FDD bay is then drop into the BIOS and see if;
1. Its recognised as a second battery.
2. If it’s charging.

If its the same size, shape and has the same connector it may well be that its point of manufacturer/supply is different from your Laptop I.e. Laptop=UK Battery S=America
