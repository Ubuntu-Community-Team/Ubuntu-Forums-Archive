### Post by vanadium on 2007-06-03
It is a bad idea to improperly shut down a system because it can create great havoc. That is not Ubuntu specific. I recently witnessed great damage with a laptop running XP. XP shut down because the battery was low. The people turned it on again, but then, the sytem fell out because there really was no current anymore, and the whole system was badly damaged and had to be reinstalled from scratch. I am afraid your only option now might be a fresh install.
