### Post by ltmon on 2007-02-13
I've had great success with an Oki B4100.  Oki are more known for their industrial level printers, but this one is a small consumer model.

It's about the cheapest B&W laser printer I could find (note: I'm including running costs here!!!!).  They also don't play any nasty tricks, such as only supplying half-full toner catridges out of the box (I'm looking at _you_ HP).

In any case, whatever you choose, check it out on [http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting](http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting) first.
