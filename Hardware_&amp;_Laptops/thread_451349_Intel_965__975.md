### Post by kyphi on 2007-06-30
I hope that I am not too late with this response.

You are quite right in your discovery that the 965 chipset works OK if you use SATA optical drives.  It will not work at all with PATA optical drives.

Also, the ethernet onboard chip will not work in Ubuntu.  You would have to install an ethernet card.

I understand that the 975 chipset does not have any of these troubles but would urge you to further research this before making any choices.
