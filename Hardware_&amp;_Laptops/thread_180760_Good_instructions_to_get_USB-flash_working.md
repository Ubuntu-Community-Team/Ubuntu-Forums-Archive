### Post by jonrkc on 2006-05-22
I installed a USB flash thumb drive today, and as with most USB devices the computer automatically saw it and acknowledged it, but I had no idea what to do after that.  After some searching, I found very clear instructions at

[http://www.ibiblio.org/pub/linux/docs/HOWTO/other-formats/html_single/Flash-Memory-HOWTO.html](http://www.ibiblio.org/pub/linux/docs/HOWTO/other-formats/html_single/Flash-Memory-HOWTO.html)

and within 15 minutes I was able to use the thumb drive for file storage and retrieval.

Some parts may be slightly out of date, but the essential part of the instructions is valid, and should be useful to anybody needing to use a USB flash drive, regardless of distro version.

I hope this speeds up some other user's installation time!
