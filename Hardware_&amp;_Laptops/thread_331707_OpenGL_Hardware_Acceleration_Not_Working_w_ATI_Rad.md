### Post by aberry9036 on 2007-01-15
the reason it slows to a crawl ( i have the same problem with my x800) is that you are not using hardware acceleration, you're running off your cpu. The fglrx drivers in the synaptic repositories are broken in the sense that they don't provide acceleration, you will have to get the drivers from the ati website.
