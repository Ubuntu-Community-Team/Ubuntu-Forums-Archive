### Post by Equiflux on 2008-04-10
Plus, I don't think ATI even has Linux driver(s) for the 3870 yet. 
[http://ati.amd.com/support/driver.html](http://ati.amd.com/support/driver.html)

I have a 9600GT and NVIDIA does at least have a beta driver out for the 9600GT, and so far I've had no problems with it at all. The only "con" would be: The boot-up splash doesn't display; Nothing is displayed on normal boot-up until the system goes into run level 3 and the driver/X-server starts.

As for which one is better...
9600GT - [http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814130345](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814130345)
HD 3870 - [http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814102732](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814102732)

Take a look at the specs and decide for yourself I guess. However, I have seen a few reviews here and there showing that the two cards perform about the same. One is slightly better at certain tasks/things and vice versa. Also, the 3870 has a faster core clock speed by 150mhz but the 9600GT has a faster memory clock speed by about 700mhz. 

But I guess, since NVIDIA has Linux drivers for their 9600GT and ATI does not have any for their 3870, you might wanna get the 9600GT ;P
