### Post by tommyx53 on 2006-09-23
I've had problems with systems not booting as well as the onscreen arrow pointer jumping erratically over the screen even when I didn't move my optical mouse. The Micro Innovations optical mouse, purchased from Wal-Mart, has a light underneath instead of a trackball, and I think the sensor became confused by the different color patterns of the mousepad. My solution: throw out the mousepad! And everything works dandy! If I had known this three weeks ago, I wouldn't have: blamed my 11 year old son for crashing SimplyMEPIS Linux, taking my computer to CompUSA three times, upgrading the hard drive, resetting the BIOS to poll the CD-ROM first instead of the hard drive, vacuuming inside the computer, switched to Ubuntu Linux (which I like a LOT), and pulling out more hair!
