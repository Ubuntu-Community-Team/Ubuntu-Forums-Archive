### Post by ubuntu-geek on 2004-10-15
Most basic printers should work ok. You could goto Computer &gt; System Configuration &gt; Printers &gt; Double click on new printer &gt; click next &gt; and then look for supported models and brands this might give you a base idea.

Hope that helps.
