### Post by Cal on 2005-04-25
I wouldn't suggest ubuntu.  Its a great linux distro, but your hardware constraints are all together to low for a full GNOME or KDE desktop based distro.  I would suggest DamnSmallLinux .  It has a rep for working well with low end hardware.  It runs a custom fluxbox desktop, which is much lighter and quicker.  It is also debian based and comes with GTK pre installed so you will be able to easily run almost everything ubuntu can.  If you ever deside to run linux on more modern hardware, go for Ubuntu you won't regret it.

Until then [http://www.damnsmalllinux.org/](http://www.damnsmalllinux.org/) is your friend.  Let me know how it goes.
