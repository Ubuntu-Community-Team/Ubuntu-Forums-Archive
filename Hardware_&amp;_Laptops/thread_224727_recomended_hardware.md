### Post by zxee on 2006-07-30
There are many sites offering the information you want. I tend to like this one: [http://www.linuxquestions.org/hcl/index.php](http://www.linuxquestions.org/hcl/index.php)
I've built a few computers starting about 6 years ago with an athlon cpu and more recently semprons. If you stay with well known brands and not the latests and greatest you'll probably be ok. When I'm in doubt about any specific piece of hardware I do a search on the component and include the word linux. Hope that helps.
