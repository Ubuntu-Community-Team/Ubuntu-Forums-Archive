### Post by gspr on 2007-10-25
Try installing 915resolution and letting it do its automatic magic (/etc/init.d/915resolution start with X closed). Also, if you decide to upgrade to Gutsy at some point, the driver named "intel" included in Gutsy no longer needs 915resolution and the likes of it for the correct setting of modes.
