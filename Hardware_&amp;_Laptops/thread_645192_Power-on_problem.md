### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-12-20
With the 24-pin power connector plugged into the motherboard, carefully take a paperclip and short the green wire and black wire.  That should wake up the power supply.  It's possible the power-on transistors/relays are bad.  I have two identical computers that lost their power-on capability.  I simply soldered a switch in between the green and black wire.  As long as the machines are plugged in (and on an UPS) having the green and black wire shorted allowed the power button to function again.
