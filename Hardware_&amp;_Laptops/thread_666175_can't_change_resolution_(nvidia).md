### Post by Minilek on 2008-01-13
I have resolved this issue by adding the following option under the "Screen" section of my xorg.conf:
```
Option "UseEDIDFreqs" "False"
```

This worked for me, but from what I understand you should not use this option unless you're sure your monitor can handle all the resolution/refresh rate modes you've entered.  Otherwise, doing so may permanently damage your monitor.  I believe this option essentially says "ignore what the screen says it can handle and instead use the resolution and refresh rate I gave".
