### Post by Richard Kut on 2007-02-27
Hello juice99, and welcome! The printer you reference is currently not supported. See here for details:

[http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x5250]("http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x5250")

The folks at linuxprinting.org tend to recommend either Epson or HP printers for the best experience. I would check there first before going out to buy a printer.

As for your scanner question, I cannot say because I have no experience with printer scanner combo setups like yours. However, I have successfully installed and used an Agfa scanner with my Ubuntu laptop with very little trouble.

A point of reference, in case you weren't aware, but scanners in Linux typically use SANE and XSANE software to work. Maybe this will help you to track down a scanner that will work with Ubuntu.

Don't despair. With a bit of research you will certainly find the hardware bits that work for you. Good luck!
