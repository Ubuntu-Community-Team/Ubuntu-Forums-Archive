### Post by Benanov on 2008-03-09
Can you tell me which driver you're using?

I have an 1100 here in front of me (has the common feed issue, but the part is on order and the printer still works.)

On Dapper I'm using the hpijs 0.9.7 driver and it's slow as molasses, but things do print. Might also be related to the fact I need a cable extension but it does print.

On Gutsy I'm using the Foomatic ljet4 driver and it's fairly reliable, and reasonably speedy.

You may also have a defective cable (Rule #1: check the cable. Rule #2: CHECK THE CABLE. It's the cheapest point of failure) however since the cable is proprietary you may not be able to easily find another one.

I'll subscribe to this thread to hear back from you.
