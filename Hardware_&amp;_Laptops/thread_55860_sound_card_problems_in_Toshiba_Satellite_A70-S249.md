### Post by varunus on 2005-08-12
These commands, when typed into a terminal (which can be found from Applications->System Tools->Terminal) will give us an easy to read, easy to copy and paste text based output of:
- all the devices you have in your computer (lspci)
- all of the drivers you have loaded (lsmod)
