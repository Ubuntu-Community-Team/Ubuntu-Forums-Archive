### Post by ericlin0122 on 2007-06-23
Hi:
I am running ASUS w7j notebook, which has build in volume switch on the side.
But when I press volume up, it does not change the "actual volume" but the "line in" volume.
Any idea how to re-assign that hardware key to switch volume up and down?
Thanks for your help

---

### Post by 5-HT on 2007-06-23
Easy way: 
            If you right click the volume applet on your panel (or Applications -> Sound & Video -> Volume Control), select preferences, and change the track to control to master instead of line in, does that take care of it?
