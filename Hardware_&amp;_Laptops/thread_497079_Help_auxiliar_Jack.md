### Post by fredj on 2007-07-10
You can connect your powered speakers to the auxiliary jack if it is a line out jack and not a line in
jack. You will then have to set the appropriate volume control in the alsa mixer use Edit - Preferences
to add a volume control for the auxiliary if its not already there.
