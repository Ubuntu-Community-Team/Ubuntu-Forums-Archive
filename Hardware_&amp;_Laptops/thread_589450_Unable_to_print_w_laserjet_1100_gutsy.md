### Post by Mundu on 2007-10-24
Try unplugging the printer USB cable, then plugging it back in. Or power off the printer first (unplug power cable). 

I used an HP Laserjet 1000 in the past with Linux and found that the printer ROM was downloaded to the printer from the computer when the printer powered up!

---

### Post by lennyharris on 2007-10-24
Mundu, I unplugged the power cable and replugged it, and now the printer works.  Just as you suggested.
