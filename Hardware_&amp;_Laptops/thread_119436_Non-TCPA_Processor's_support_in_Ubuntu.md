### Post by 23meg on 2006-01-19
The short answer is yes, but you'll most likely have compatibility problems here and there.

Side note: with Linux if you want to opt out of TC completely, you can just unload the TPM kernel module and it won't be utilized on your computer. As long as your OS and your apps don't utilize TC, hardware support means nothing.
