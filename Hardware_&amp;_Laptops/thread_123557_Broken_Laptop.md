### Post by mips on 2006-02-01
Ok, the intermittent nature indicates you might have had a dry solder joint. Try reheating the solder again or replace with new to ensure a better join between pcb & connector pins.

Alternatively the actual connector could be broken (hope not). If this is the case then you can buy a new one for about $10 on the net. Google **Inspiron Latitude CPx CPI XPI DC Power Jack** and just verify that it is the correct connector.

You'll probably need a solder sucker for the above procedures.

Something else to check but I doubt it from your description:
[http://forum.us.dell.com/supportforums/board/message?board.id=insp_power&message.id=34124](http://forum.us.dell.com/supportforums/board/message?board.id=insp_power&message.id=34124)
