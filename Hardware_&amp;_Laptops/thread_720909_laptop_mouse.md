### Post by justsomedude on 2008-03-10
This is intentional. 

tap with one finger = left click
tap with two fingers = middle click
tap with three fingers = right click

If you want to change this behavior, take a look at your xorg.conf. The setup is different for different kinds of touchpads, so you will have to read the wiki.
