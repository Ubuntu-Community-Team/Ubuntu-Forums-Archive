### Post by roachk71 on 2007-11-30
You might have to look up the monitor's horizontal sync and refresh rates, pasting or typing them into xorg.conf. Simply add these to the end of the Monitor Section (HorizSync and VertRefresh, and not as options.)

Here's an example from my monitor:
```

Section  "Monitor"
              Identifier            "HannsG HS191"
              Option                "DPMS"
              [B]HorizSync            30-80
              VertRefresh         56-75[/B]
EndSection
```
