### Post by x64Jimbo on 2007-05-14
First, wireless interfaces are not expensive. Most can be had for less than $30. If you must use your ethernet port and a converter, you can mod a linksys wrt54g router with the DD-WRT firmware to make it a wireless network bridge device, but that will cost you a lot more than a wifi interface would in the first place.
