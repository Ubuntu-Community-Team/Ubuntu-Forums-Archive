### Post by jespdj on 2007-10-19
Some hardware works better than other hardware - mainly because some manufacturers are more friendly to Linux and open source software than others.

For graphics, Intel and nVidia generally work better than ATI (altough AMD/ATI has announced that they will improve graphics drivers in the future).

For wireless networking, Intel is good; avoid Broadcom chipsets.

Ubuntu Gutsy works great on my Dell XPS M1330 (with nVidia 8400M GS and Intel 4965AGN).
