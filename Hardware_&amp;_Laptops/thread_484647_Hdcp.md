### Post by benanzo on 2007-06-26
I can pretty much guarantee that Ubuntu and Linux in general will have a very difficult time supporting the kind of kernel-mode DRM that HDCP/AACS requires to watch "premium" content.

The only reason to consider an HDCP-compliant monitor would be if there's a chance you'll want Vista in the future or if you plan to plug an HD-DVD/Blu-Ray player into it directly.
