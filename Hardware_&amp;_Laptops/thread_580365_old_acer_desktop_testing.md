### Post by jonathanmotes on 2007-10-18
My brother once installed XP on a computer with a Pentium I but I think he upgraded the RAM to 64MB. If you want to try Ubuntu, I would suggest trying Xubuntu (a lightweight version of Ubuntu that uses less RAM and other resources) after upgrading the RAM. In my brother's experience, he had to have at least 192MB to get the Ubuntu live disc to work, but even then it was really sluggish. I think Xubuntu requires at least 128MB of RAM, so you could try to find two 64MB cards that would work.
