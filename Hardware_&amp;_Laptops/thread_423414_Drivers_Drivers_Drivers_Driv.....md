### Post by teryan2006 on 2007-04-25
When you d-click a .run file, the default action for ubuntu is to open it in a text viewer. To actually run it, you need to open it in terminal. This is noted on the ATI driver website where you downloaded the driver. There a quite a few guides regarding how to do this, such as [this one]("http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Feisty_Installation_Guide"). 

Regarding your sound issues, realtek really should work out of the box (or out of the disc). Exactly which Realtek codec chip is on your laptop?
