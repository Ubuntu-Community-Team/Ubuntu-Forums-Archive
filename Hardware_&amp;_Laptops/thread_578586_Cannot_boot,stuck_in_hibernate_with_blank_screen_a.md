### Post by cubeist on 2007-10-17
If you hold the power button down for 5 seconds it will completely turn off the computer.  Then you should be able to get back to the grub menu and either try to boot normally or enter the recovery mode...   On the other hand, if your computer is off and when you turn it on you can't even get into the bios, well then you probably have a serious hard drive failure...
