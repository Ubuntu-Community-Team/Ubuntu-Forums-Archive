### Post by kevinrs on 2007-06-20
I have a Samsung SCX 4100 copier/scanner/laser printer.

It has no wireless connection just usb. Its good value. I got mine from PCWorld in the UK for £70.00

The bonus is you can get a Linux printer driver from Samsung. If you want colour then its not for you. However its a great product and cheap to run. I buy a refill kit off Ebay when I need more toner .
