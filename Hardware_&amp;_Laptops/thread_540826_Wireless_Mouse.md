### Post by viergeame on 2007-09-02
I started using a wireless mouse a few weeks ago, and everything was going well until yesterday.  Yesterday morning I grabbed my mouse off it's cradle and started my normal morning computer routine.  After about five minutes the mouse suddenly stopped working.  I resynched it and it worked for a few more minutes then it stopped working again.  I thought that maybe when I put it on the cradle the night before that I put it at a weird angle and it didn't recharge, I have done this a few times.  So, I put it back on to charge.  A few hours later I decided to try again.  I took it off the cradle, resynched it, and started to go about my browsing.  Again, after about five minutes it stopped responding.  I also noticed that both times the red optical light on the bottom wouldn't come back on, and the green light on the cradle that flashes when the mouse is in use was no longer working.  The first time I just assumed that this was a sign that the mouse was in desperate need of charging.  One other thing, normally the mouse doesn't need synched every time it comes off the cradle.
