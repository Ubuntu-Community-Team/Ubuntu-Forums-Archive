### Post by renzokuken on 2007-03-05
well according to your xorg.conf you are not actually using the fglrx driver at all, you are still using the old radeon driver

go to the Section "Device" part of your xorg.conf and change the line

Driver   "radeon"

to

Driver   "fglrx"

restart your xorg using Ctrl+Alt+Backspace and see what happens
