### Post by jml on 2007-08-20
A clicking sound usually represents the hard drive's stepping motor trying to move the read/write heads accross the hard drive.  It also means that there is a high probability that your hard drive will fail.  At the very least back up all of your critical files.  

You may also want to begin looking for a replacement hard drive to replace your current one.  It should not be too expensive to replace a drive for an older laptop.  I replaced my ThinkPad's original 30 gig hardrive with an 80 gig one for about a hundred dollars US.  I'm sure you could get one for a lot less if you shopped around.
