### Post by huygens on 2006-11-17
For me your xorg.conf looks fine. Did you try switching to another resolution for your desktop to see if it's work?
Just go to the System menu and under Preferences select "Screen Resolution". Change it to 800x600 and restart your session or even X server.
If it works, you know that your X server is properly configured.

By the way, I'm pretty sure that Starcraft runs in 800x600 and not in 640x480. But that's more than 8 years I did not play with it...

### Post by gejr on 2006-11-17
After endless xorg.conf-modifications, backuping, reverting to backup and about 100 ctrl+alt+del  I've finally made everything run perfectly. Battle.net included :)

I ran dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg and changed the default refresh rate from 32-65 or something like that to 10-70. That gave me all my desired resolutions available in the gnome Screen Resolution thingy and X managed to change to 640x480 when running games needing that.

Hope this change doesnt affect anything else.. Everything looks fine this far. Beryl and all...but time will show.
