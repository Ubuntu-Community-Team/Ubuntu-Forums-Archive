### Post by jnorthr on 2007-08-16
in my previous experience, taking a printer than is a 'close' match to a printer is good enough as it will often have most of the same options as your printer, at least that's my experience using HP printers. Why not try taking a close cousin and using that just to see how close you can come to making it work. If you can get that far, you may decide it is not worth the trouble to mess with it any further, or you can uninstall it.
