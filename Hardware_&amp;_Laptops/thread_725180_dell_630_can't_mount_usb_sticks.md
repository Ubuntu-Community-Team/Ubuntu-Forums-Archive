Hi,

I have a Dell 630 and for some reason USB sticks and readers won't mount.  I've tried several and none of them seem to work.  The exact same USB devices automounted fine with my old IBM T42.   I'm sure the physical USB port is good, because I can plug a USB mouse and/or keyboard just fine.  I'm running Gutsy.

Any ideas?

Thanks in advance.

---

### Post by giants_fan on 2008-03-26
Solved my own problem.  Apparently, the USB ports on the Dell are a little to "long".  The physical contacts on several of my USB sticks weren't totally making enough contact.  A USB extension cord thingee solved the problem.
