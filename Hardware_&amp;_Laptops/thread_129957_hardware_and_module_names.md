While looking for a module for my raid controller I found this useful page.
[http://kmuto.jp/debian/hcl/index.cgi]("http://kmuto.jp/debian/hcl/index.cgi")
One have to copy & paste the outputput of lspci -n which 
shows PCI vendor and device codes as numbers and gives information about the hardware support in a GNU/Linux debian system (ubuntu sould work as well) and also gives the name of the module.

And not to forget to mention the page [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport")

Sometimes I found it hard to get the name of the needed modules. This makes it easier.
