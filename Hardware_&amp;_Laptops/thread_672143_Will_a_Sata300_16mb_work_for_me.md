### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-01-19
No, it shouldn't be a problem. Good south bridges that only support SATA150 (like Intel ICH5) will auto-negotiate the speed to SATA150. Even with crappy south bridges like VIA8237 that only take SATA150 drives, you should be able to set a jumper on the drive to force it into SATA150 compatibility mode.
