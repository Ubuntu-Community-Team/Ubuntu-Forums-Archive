### Post by martinw89 on 2007-04-15
Ah, Antec makes good power supplies, you should be fine quality wise.  Check to make sure that you don't have the processor and the graphics card on the same rail (it probably is laid out so this can't happen).  Also, make sure there isn't something preventing your motherboard from POSTing like a RAM stick half-in or something like that.  Another thing I can think of is to make sure you don't have any of the 4 pin molex connectors backwards, causing some 5V component to get 12 volts or visa-versa.
