### Post by evilghost on 2005-08-21
No, since 1024x768 is the native resolution of that LCD.  It will do lower, such as 800x600 or 640x480 via LCD stretch.  Using a higher resolution will result in a "Virtual Desktop" where 1024x768 is displayed and the screen will scroll right/left/up/down upon mouse movement.
