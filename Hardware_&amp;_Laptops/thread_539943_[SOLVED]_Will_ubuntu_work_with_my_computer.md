### Post by Pumm4 on 2007-08-31
Hello **xxhaloownerxx**  ...

Recommended system requirements are:[LIST]
[*] 500 MHz x86 processor
[*] 192 MB of system memory (RAM)
[*] 8 GB of disk space
[*] Graphics card capable of 1024x768 resolution
[*] *Sound card*
[*] *A network or Internet connection*[/LIST]So don't worry about that.

As for Wireless support, use a well known card and you'll be fine... (more **[COLOR=Navy][here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported")[/COLOR]**).

For 6.06 version is **[COLOR=Navy][Belkin card]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsBelkin")[/COLOR]** a great choice ... (**[COLOR=Navy][Their homepage]("http://www.belkin.com/")[/COLOR]**)
