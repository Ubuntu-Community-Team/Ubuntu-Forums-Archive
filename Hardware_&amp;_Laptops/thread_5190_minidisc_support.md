### Post by dataw0lf on 2004-11-16
As far as playback is concerned, I suggest trying [http://www.pdr.cx/projects/gnetmd/main/#top](http://www.pdr.cx/projects/gnetmd/main/#top).  It's an xmms plugin that will allow you to playback any tracks on your minidisc player.  However, as far as downloading a music onto a MD, this is more problematic.  Without Sony's support, any reverse engineering of the USB protocols and encryption they use would be a DMCA violation.   I'm on freenode, I can give you more indepth information on there if you wish.
