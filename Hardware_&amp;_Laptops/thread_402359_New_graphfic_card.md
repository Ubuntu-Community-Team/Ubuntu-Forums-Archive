### Post by kidders on 2007-04-07
Hi there,

My advice would be to go for a commonly used manufacturer. For example, ATI and Nvidia cards are used by an *awful* lot of people, so (at least in theory) you should get good support, reliable functionality (games, etc.), and plenty of HOWTOs for doing specific things.

Many agree that, of the two, Nvidia would be the company to go for, but opinions differ.

It's worth mentioning that Beryl is _not_ stable ... don't upgrade your graphics card just for that.
