### Post by Paul Moir on 2008-01-13
What trouble are you having?  I believe support is in the restricted fglrx driver if you want 3d.  To enable that have a look here:
[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Main_Page](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Main_Page)
Unfortunately, ATI's linux support has historically been poor.  That does seem to be changing though.

You are aware that most drivers are built right into ubuntu, so it should just work right?  The issue with graphic drivers is that the manufacturers are inclined to keep the way they work secret, and so release these proprietary drivers.  There's a similar problem with wireless cards but for the most part everything else just 'works'.
