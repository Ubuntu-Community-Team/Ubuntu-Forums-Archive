### Post by Archmage on 2008-03-05
Not formating your /home-Partition and attaching it later at /home will leave all the things that are in the /home-Partition intact.

In most of the cases this will be all settings and data from all the programms. Unless you did store it elsewhere.

However some program might have problems runing with a configuration that is from a newer version.
