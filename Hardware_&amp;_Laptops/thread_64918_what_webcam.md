### Post by sethmahoney on 2005-09-12
There are lists available on the internet.  I'd do a google for "webcam support linux" or something.  But there's another catch: There's not a lot of software support in linux for webcams.  gaim, for instance, doesn't support webcams at all yet, though this is being worked on.  There are ways around that, though, like installing a copy of Windows in Ubuntu (there's a HOWTO around here somewhere for that) or dual-booting with Windows.  But native webcam support is very much lacking in linux.
