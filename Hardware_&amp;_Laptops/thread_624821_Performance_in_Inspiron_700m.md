Hi,

I was wondering if there are people out there using Gutsy Gibbon (or Feisty Fawn) in their Inspiron 700m machine. The reason I'm looking for such people is that I want to know the performance of gutsy gibbon or feisty fawn in that machine. I tried both feisty fawn and gutsy gibbon and I'm experiencing terrible performance in my laptop.

Assuming that you got your Inspiron 700m loaded with XP, can you compare the performance of your laptop with XP with its performance with Ubuntu?

My laptop has 1.6Ghz Pentium M with 512MB memory. The graphics is Intel shared memory. My swap file is 1 GB size and I use compiz.

Gutsy Gibbon installation I have is unresponsive and sluggish. Especially when I use Firefox and Thunderbird at the same, the system is very slow and unresponsive. There's one more case, when I use Synaptic, during search or package installation, machine is completely unresponsive. Opening an 0ffice document with Open Office takes very long time as well. When I was using XP, the system was more responsive.

So, other 700m users, are you experiencing similar performance problems? Is there a problem with my installation OR are all 700m's experiencing unpleasant performance?
One solution is to increase memory but I'm doubtful that it will resolve the issue.

Any suggestions / comments / support ?

thanks..

### Post by btlee on 2007-12-17
I also use inspiron 700m with 1.8GHz cpu and 2G ram oon Gutsy Gibbon.
It runs greatly and smoothly.
I have only several minor problems.
For example, gnome-power-manager malfunctions for suspend due to acpi bug.
Another problem is that I cannot use SD card reader.
I think that your problem is not due to small memory.
Actually 512 MB should be enough for your usage.
However, I have no suggestions for you, because I don't know your settings.
Would you let me know your specific settings or system information in more detail?
