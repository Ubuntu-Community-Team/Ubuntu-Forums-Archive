That's a logical trick : the more your desktop color average turns to white, the more energy is consumed. Consequently it's better to use Dark Wallpaper, and dark color theme.
The autonomy which can be saved with this is consequent. You'll like it.

### Post by chewearn on 2008-02-01
Only CRT power consumption is affected by black or white desktop.

Unless you have a super-old laptop that still runs from CRT (has there ever been such a device??), you would do significantly better power saving by dimming your LCD backlighting.

In which case, to reduce strain to the eyes, a whiter desktop is better.
