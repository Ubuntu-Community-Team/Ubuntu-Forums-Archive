### Post by kiplingw on 2007-01-04
I've experimented with aticonfig, but I can't seem to get the desired effect. I am using a laptop with an ATI mobility x1600 video card (16:10 ratio) using 1440x900 resolution. My external LCD (4:3 ratio) I would like to have set at 1024x768. I'd like it so that the LCD to the right of my laptop behaves like an extension to my main one, or even a seperate desktop space for all I care. 

What I have now appears to be just a clone of my laptop screen, but with automatic horizontal / vertical scrolling. I've tried...

sudo aticonfig --initial=dual-head --screen-layout=right
sudo aticonfig --dtop=horizontal --overlay-on=1
sudo aticonfig --resolution=1,1280x1024,1024x768,800x600

I restart X and I get the effect I described above.
