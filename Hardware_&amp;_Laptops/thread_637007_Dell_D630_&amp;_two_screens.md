### Post by public_void on 2007-12-11
I decide to edit your xorg.conf based upon my working one. But be warn, **check it first for mistakes**, or integrate with your existing one. I added a new Device, Monitor and Screen section. I've left comments in to help. Also refer to the xorg.conf manual (man xorg.conf or [here)]("http://www.x.org/archive/X11R6.8.0/doc/xorg.conf.5.html")

```

    FontPath    "/usr/share/fonts/X11/75dpi/:unscaled"

    Option        "XkbOptions"    "lv3:ralt_switch"

    Option        "CorePointer"
    Option        "Device"        "/dev/input/mice"

EndSection

    Option        "Device"    "/dev/input/wacom"
    Option        "Type"        "cursor"

    Driver        "intel"
    BusID        "PCI:0:2:0"

EndSection

    Identifier    "Generic Monitor"
    Option        "DPMS"
EndSection
```
