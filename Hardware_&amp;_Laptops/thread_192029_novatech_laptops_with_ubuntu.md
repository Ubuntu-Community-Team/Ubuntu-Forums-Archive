### Post by Greyhair on 2007-09-14
Last month I decided it was time to retire my old trusty Compaq Evo N400c laptop.

Not wishing to pay Windows Tax, and because Dell only offered one laptop model with Ubuntu in the UK;  I gave Novatech a look.
I finally bought their Santa Rosa model  (now renamed the Rasor) with no OS installed \\:D/

Using Ubuntu 7.04 alternate Desktop CD, I only encountered two problems. Intel Wireless 4965AGN, and Intel GMA X3100 Graphics.
Hopefully these will be resolve with the next release.

But thanks to the stirling efforts of the unsung heroes of this forum, I was able to cure these problems within one hour. I now have a machine that  'flies' when compared to my old Compaq.

### Post by FRuMMaGe on 2007-09-22
I purchased the Revo Max Turbo from Novatech and it was amazing!  Rock-bottom prices!  And as most of the internals are made by Intel, they were very compatible.
