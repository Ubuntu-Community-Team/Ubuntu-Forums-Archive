### Post by stoeptegel on 2006-02-26
With AMD you can't look to the Mhz when you want to compare them to intel, you have to look what the title says:  i.e. AMD64 3200+ 
This says that it is as fast as an p4 3200 MHz or faster. (and that's true)

The same applies to people thinking that mp3 with 128 kbps is a way of measuring the quality of the audiostream, but in reality there are LAME, Xing, blade. fraunhofer etc. encoders with all different outcomes. 

Bottomline is that AMD is per clock-cycle much more efficient in handling tasks, which makes AMD needing less Mhz for the same speed.
