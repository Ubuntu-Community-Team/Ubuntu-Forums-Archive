### Post by bradford72 on 2007-03-01
Okay, not used to posting my own replies, but if this can help anyone else out, here it is...I went to Synaptic, and downloaded all the CUPS and gutenprint packages there.  I uninstalled and reinstalled the printer, and chose the gutenprint(expert) driver.  After that it was just a matter of running a LOT of cleaning cycles (my heads were dirty as dirt) and voila!  I'm now printing just fine!
