### Post by Sunflower1970 on 2007-03-01
Have you tried repositioning the wireless...controller-thingy in different areas around your desk/computer area? 

One of my computer setups has a wireless keyboard and also a wireless mouse. They were not bundled together, I bought them separately. I had trouble with the wireless mouse for a while. It would start off fine, then just slowly begin to die. What I discovered was the wireless...what's it called...a router? radio control? Anyway, that...thing...was too close to the one that was controlling the keyboard, and I also had set it on top of the computer's case (which is metal). Once I moved it as far away as possible from the computer case and wireless controller-thingy for the keyboard all worked well, and still does :)
