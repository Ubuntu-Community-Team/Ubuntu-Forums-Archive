### Post by ubuntu_demon on 2005-10-19
this will reconfigure your xorg.conf and create a backup of your old xorg.conf afterwards :

```

$ sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg

```

When you are unsure about a question just press enter. Also I always do the simple monitor configuration.
