### Post by kwacka on 2007-11-02
Install gparted Uuse synaptic)

Run gparted, ensure that you choose the usb disc rather than your harddrive and click on 'Format to' then apply.

If the disk shows up on your desktop you will need to unmount it before starting gparted.
