### Post by Fire_Chief on 2008-01-10
The Nvidia cards are definitely better supported and easier to get working in Linux. However I think ATI cards produce a better quality S-video output. And I'm not certain how well S-Video out is supported in the current binary ATI driver. I'm actually in a similar situation where I have an Nvidia GeForce 4MX for my TV out card in an older Myth system. I think though that I don't have enough cpu power or RAM (Athlon XP 2200 / 512MB) to cleanly handle the playback or live TV (can see some pixelation on my screen when fast motion actions are on the screen). I wondered about changing over to an ATI card to see if it improved the output.
