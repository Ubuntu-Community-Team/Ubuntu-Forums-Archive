### Post by ahaslam on 2008-01-18
The nvidia 8800gt is one of the best value performance cards around atm. Unfortunately there's a fan speed issue with its current driver. This can be resolved with the use of nvclock  but a new driver release is expected soon to fix the issue.

A much cheaper option is the well proven 7600gt which should fit your needs perfectly & there's a choice of drivers that work well otb ;)
