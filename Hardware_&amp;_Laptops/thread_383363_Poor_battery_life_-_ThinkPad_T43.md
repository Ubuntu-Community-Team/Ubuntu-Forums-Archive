### Post by xyz on 2007-03-13
Try and read up on it [HERE]("http://www.nabble.com/thinkpad-x60-Battery-Life--!-t1971716.html")

I also read somewhere that "not all batteries are born equal".
Once in a while, if you can, let it empty out totally and recharge.

In Dapper, it was easier...for instance, to adjust brightness! Now it seems it's full blast all the time.

Go into Power Management and set "Put display to sleep..." to lowest and check "Prefer power savings...".
