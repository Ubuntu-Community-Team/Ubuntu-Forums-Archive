### Post by chewearn on 2007-10-21
Do you have another computer, where you can plug it the drive to verify?  Either it has short-circuited, or maybe your PSU is fried.  My hunch is, it might be the latter, as the PSU usually has higher failure rate compared to the HDD.

Another option, if you have one of those USB HDD enclosure. stick the HDD is there and see if it can be mounted.
