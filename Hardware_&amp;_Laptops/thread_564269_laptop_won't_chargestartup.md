### Post by brad103 on 2007-09-30
If you don't get any feedback that you've plugged in power (i.e. no charging or power lights on the laptop), and you're confident that power is coming from the power supply (remember that current is just as important as voltage), then it may be a loose connection on the laptop's power socket. If it's an old laptop and you're feeling ***very*** brave then you might open it up and see if the socket's loose. This comes with a number of risks so think hard before trying this.

Again, if you get no lights then IMHO it can't be an OS problem.
