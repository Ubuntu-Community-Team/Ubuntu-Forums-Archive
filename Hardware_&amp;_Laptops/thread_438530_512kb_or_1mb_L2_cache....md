### Post by EXCiD3 on 2007-05-10
OK L2 Cache is MUCH MUCH MUCH faster than RAM. It is used to store temporary commands that go to the processor. The more L1/L2 cache you have the, your processor will have a higher maximum speed. L1 cache is more important than L2, but you still want to get as much of that as you can, so long as it is still in your price range. Does taht help much?
