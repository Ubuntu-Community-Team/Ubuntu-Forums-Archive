### Post by p110011 on 2007-01-20
If you have any CATV splitters, try eliminating other outlets. Another component could have a bad ground.

Also try switching the AC plug 180*. Some old houses can have neutral and hot crossed.

If it's coming from the Cable Co., you and your neighbors may see a white horizontal bar slowly rolling down your tv screen. 

Hope that helps, Gill
