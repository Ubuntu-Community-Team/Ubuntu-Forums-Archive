### Post by darkstar8 on 2007-11-22
I bought a Kodak Easyshare v1003 a few months ago. It's 10 MP and very easy to use. It was $225 canadian (I think).
F-Spot works very well with it, especially with the docking system you can also buy. Just plug it into the dock and press the button, F-Spot will open up and start downloading (you don't need the dock, but it makes it much easier).
I had to order it online, though, and I still have yet to see it in stores.

As an aside, my family and I have been using Kodak cameras ever since they switched to digital-only and we've been very pleased with the picture quality, ease of use, and (especially with my newest one) the amount of features.
