### Post by cgbier on 2007-03-22
Did you try to use a card reader instead of connecting the camera?

Olympus was known (at least their E-1 DSLR) to have some issues with a direct connection to the computer under Windows, due to some driver problems, so I don't think it runs better under LINUX.
Camera makers love to put some proprietary stuff into their USB protocols.

Using a card reader is also more secure in copying. when your battery dies during the transfer, you might corrupt images.

Any $5.95 reader from Walmart will do.
