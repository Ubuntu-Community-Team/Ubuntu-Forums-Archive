It might be a better idea to "support" it anyway. Just make sure you've got a supported modem in that computer and put notes on how to configure it on their Desktop + configure it for them in initial install. You never know when someone will decide to use dial up or not...

I would go with an alsa-capable smartlink modem (a winmodem/softmodem -> see second link in my signature in section "alsa compatible blabla" which describes sl-modem-daemon), or an internal hardware modem (hardmodem). 

Second option would be much easier on both you and them.
