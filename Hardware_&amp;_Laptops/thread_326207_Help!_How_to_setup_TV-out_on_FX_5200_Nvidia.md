### Post by wjston on 2006-12-29
> **1ns4nity said:**
> 
Im using a Nvidia FX 5200 with DVI VGA and TV out. VGA works fine but when i plug the tv out into my tv, I get no signal at all.


Are you trying to set up your output using S-Video to S-Video or S-Video to RCA on the TV? 

If you are going from S-Video from the FX 5200 to S-Video on your TV, then you should be changing your xorg.conf file to S-VIDEO. Otherwise, it should be COMPOSITE if you are going from S-Video from the card to RCA on the TV. If this is setup incorrectly, you will only get a black screen once X takes over after the initial boot screen disappears.
