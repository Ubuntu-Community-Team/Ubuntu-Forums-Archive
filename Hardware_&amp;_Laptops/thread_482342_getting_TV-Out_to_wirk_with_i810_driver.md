### Post by tpg on 2007-06-23
Try adding
```
Option  "Clone" "true"
Option  "MonitorLayout" "CRT,LFP"
```
To the "device" section of your xorg.conf. Restarting X should do the trick. This won't allow you to switch the VGA out off easily, although it may be an improvement than no output at all!
