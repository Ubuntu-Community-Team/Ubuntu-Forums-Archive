### Post by nova on 2005-05-29
Well i can at least answer the part about the weird virtical lines. That is because ubuntu is running in a resolution that your secound monitor cant handle. While the res is most probably fine where the isue will be is with the refresh rate. Youll want to lower it by 1 step at a time. If for example its currently on 100 for both monitors drop it to 90 (if you have it) or 85. Still no good drop to 75 70 65 etc. If by the time your on lowest refresh lower screen res by one setting max refresh supported by your primary monitor and repeat the above steps.
