### Post by wvslkr on 2005-07-16
I believe the problem is the horizontal and vertical sync.  Add the values for your monitor so that your xorg.conf contains something like this:

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"Generic Monitor"
	Option		"DPMS"
	HorizSync	28-49
	VertRefresh	43-72
EndSection

Use the values for your monitor from the manual or website.
Hope that helps. GL :)
