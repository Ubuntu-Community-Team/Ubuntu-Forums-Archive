### Post by Mack Mirillo on 2007-04-11
Hey, I've got an r40 thinkpad here, too.  It has a softmodem, of course.  Softmodems are notoriously difficult to get working under linux because manufacturers have proprietary drivers and don't like to share the info on their drivers, or make drivers for anything other than ms windows.  BUT, you might be in luck, because IBM is one of the best companies around for being open and providing support, so check it out, very first hit on google for "r40 thinkpad": [http://www-307.ibm.com/pc/support/site.wss/MIGR-46024.html](http://www-307.ibm.com/pc/support/site.wss/MIGR-46024.html)

They  DO provide drivers for linux :-).
