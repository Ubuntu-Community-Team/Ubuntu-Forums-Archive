### Post by Jorge on 2007-10-01
Try SAM Linux, a derivative from PCLinuxOS. One important difference between them is that SAM uses XFCE as desktop manager, which makes it lighter and faster, specially in old machines. You will be amaze how well an old computer behaves with this distro. Otherwise, try Xubuntu, to stay with the much loved (*)ubuntu family.
