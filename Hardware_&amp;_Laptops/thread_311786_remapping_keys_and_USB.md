### Post by kobewan on 2006-12-03
Look into xkeycaps, should be in Synaptic.

You could also take a look at using xbindkeys with xvkbd. I warn you though, they are a little buggy and setting it up to run perfectly could take a couple of hours. Still, they work fairly well once set-up, and I use them for all my extra mouse buttons.
