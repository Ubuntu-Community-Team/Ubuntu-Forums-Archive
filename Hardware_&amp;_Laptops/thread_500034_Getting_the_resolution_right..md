### Post by w4ett on 2007-07-13
> **JumpyLINUX said:**
> First I get the grub menu where I can choose ubuntu or windows. And if I choose ubuntu the loading hangs after the progress bar thingy and the screen is black except for one _

From the Grub Boot menu, select recovery mode...

Will it completely boot to a command prompt?


if it asks for you to log in, type your user name then <enter> and then type your password (you won't see any characters echoed on the screen...just type it in and again press <enter>

then  type :  sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg


this will give you the semi-graphical interface to configure your xorg.conf

the controls for this app are up and down arrows move cursor, <spacebar> selects options and <enter> confirms options.

for the driver, scroll through the options and select 'vesa" and press <enter>

next, are the resolutions, press <enter> to select the defaults....complete the selections for your refresh rates, plugging in the values for your monitor from my previous post.

complete the application without changing your mouse or keyboard values.

when asked save your changes.

when back at the command prompt type:  startx

this should get you into your HDD install with a GUI.
