### Post by drummingpariah on 2007-05-31
Got it.  Because of the keyspan, it was never mapped to an event.  Instead, ttyUSB1 was where the input was going, and I hadn't even checked the USB devices.  It's working now, with sensitivity.  I just need to disable it as a normal non-gimp mouse.
