### Post by cjmct on 2006-12-29
I have a nc6220 and had the same problem.  If Volume Control is not available install it through Add/Remove Programs, then enable the Headphone Jack Sense.

Applications > Sound and Video > Volume Control > Edit > Preferences > Headphone Jack Sense.
