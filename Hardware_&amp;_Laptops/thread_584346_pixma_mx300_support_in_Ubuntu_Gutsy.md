### Post by k4sgt on 2007-12-03
Just happened to me too.  I found a fix, though!  Simply use the already installed "Canon PIXMA MP150 - CUPS+Gutenprint v5.0.1" driver.  It seems to be printing fine.  You can copy and fax stand alone still on the unit itself.  I guess that we will have to wait for a real driver to take advantage of the onboard scanner.  Hope that this helps!

Bump...I just bought my MX300 from Walmart.com on sale for $55.  Seems like a good deal...and it DOES WORK with Linux.
