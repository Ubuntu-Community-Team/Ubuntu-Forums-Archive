### Post by Whiffle on 2008-01-29
Theres alot of good info at battery university.  [http://www.batteryuniversity.com/parttwo-34.htm](http://www.batteryuniversity.com/parttwo-34.htm)

I don't go through the trouble of taking my battery out, its not hard but I use it quite often (usually with the computer just suspended).  I'm going to setup charge limits sooner or later, to keep it from charging fully, which is better for the battery.  Also, don't deep discharge it, that really really kills them quick.  I try not to run mine below 40% if I can.
