### Post by Namain on 2008-01-23
Hi there,

In order to see more information about what is going on, when you are turning off your computer, and you see the Ubuntu splash screen with the bar unloading, hit Ctrl+Alt+F1.  This will change your view to a console, showing exactly what command the computer is freezing on.  If it just shuts down normally after doing so, then it is probably a problem with the splash screen.
