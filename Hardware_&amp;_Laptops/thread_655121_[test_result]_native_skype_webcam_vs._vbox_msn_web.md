### Post by bittergourd on 2007-12-31
i have a logitech quickcam for notebook and for curiosity i tested the CPU (T7300) usage when my webcam is turned on in native skype and in vbox. 

what i found is that in vbox, the cpu usage is in average 33% (of the two cores, i.e. 60%-70% for one core) when the webcam is on.  the frame rate has visible pauses; by my estimate, it's around 10 fps.  the exposure time was set to 1/30s, therefore it should be very smooth as shown in native windows.

but in native skype, i got strictly 50% cpu usage, and one core is totally busy.  the frame rate seems quite smooth though.  there is no adjustment at all as in the beta version.

i was a little disappointed by the performance of the native skype.  no idea why they made it take so much cpu power.  hopefully in the finally version, it would give somewhat better performance and the freedom to adjusting image.
