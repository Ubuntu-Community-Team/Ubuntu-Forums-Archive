### Post by bonestonne on 2007-07-27
you could:
1) test with a digital multimeter to see if its putting out what it should
2) try a different outlet
3) your laptop might be getting old, and the power inverter on the mainboard could be losing its touch.

the screen flicker is normally due to a lack of amperage, not voltage [however lacking voltage has effects too].

id suggest making sure you're battery isn't the issue, and also check the power adapter with a multimeter to see what its real output is.
