### Post by RandomJoe on 2007-01-15
It's been a long time since I bought it, but I have an Adaptec DuoConnect that has both USB2 and Firewire/IEE1394.  Looks like it's still available, found it on [Newegg.com]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.asp?Item=N82E16815152011").  Works very well for me, been in use for backing up a server to USB/FW drives for several years now.  Mine has an NEC USB chipset.

But I've never had a USB2 card that _didn't_ work (thus why I didn't answer your other thread) regardless of how nice or cheesy they were...!  I also have sitting here an IOGear card, model GIC220U-BIO, that was left in a machine someone gave me - it works as well.  (Ah, found it on [Newegg as well]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.asp?Item=N82E16815104309").)  It's USB only, and considerably cheaper than the Adaptec card.  I never really used it for much, so can't say to how _well_ it works, just that it does work.  It isn't installed at the moment, but the chip on the card says it's an ALi M5273-A1.
