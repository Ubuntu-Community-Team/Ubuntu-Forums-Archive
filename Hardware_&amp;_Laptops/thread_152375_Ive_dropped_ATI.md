### Post by gregh on 2006-03-29
Well, I have been researching my new laptop and will be getting a HP 5129 Core-Duo.

The deciding reason? It has Nvidia graphics and not ATI.

They will not respond to support requests, they treat Linux like someone elses problem, so I have had enough. At least Nvidia *attempt* to appears as they are supportive to the linux community.

My last laptop was a Toshiba that had an IGP Radeon 9000 that *never* was supported by their official drivers, and I never got the performance that I could get when running winblows, sp I am not  taking that risk again by choosing ATI.

Bravo ! to HP for using Nvidia as another option in some of their laptops, other manufacturers should take note.

Until ATI offe proper support for linux with their drivers I will *never* buy another laptop with ATI graphics.
