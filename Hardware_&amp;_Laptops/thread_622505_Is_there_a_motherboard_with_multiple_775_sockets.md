### Post by jespdj on 2007-11-26
There are server motherboards that take two [Xeon](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xeon) processors (which is the server version of the Core 2 Duo). There are computers for sale like the [Apple Mac Pro]("http://www.apple.com/macpro/"), which has such a motherboard in which you can plug two quad-core Xeon processors.

You don't need the skulltrail platform. That's something Intel made especially for gamers and it's supposed to be expensive and exclusive. I remember reading somewhere that Intel was only going to make a couple of thousand Skulltrail motherboards. Just get a regular board on which you can put Xeon processors.
