### Post by eprebys on 2005-12-16
In terms of functionality, I want everything that a PVR supplies:

- A catalogue showing the radio shows available.

- The ability to specifiy recording based on show, not based on time.

- The ability to specify shows on multiple different channels.

- The ability to control playback with a remote control, skipping commercials and fast forwarding through songs that I don't like.

This is like the distinction between hooking up a vcr to record tv, and having a PVR. The show-based mode of recording is indescribably better than time based recording.
