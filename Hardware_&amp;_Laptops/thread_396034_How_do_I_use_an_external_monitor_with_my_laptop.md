### Post by moephan on 2007-03-29
Typically laptops have key combinations that tell the video driver how to output. For example, on my dell, the F8 key says "CRT/LCD" in little purple type. This is how I know that holding down the Function key and tapping F8 cycles through the output modes. There should be some similar hint on your Compaq.

Cheers, Rick

### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-03-29
Sometimes external video needs to be activated in BIOS.  Try bringing up the BIOS during boot (del, tab, esc, F2, F10, F12, whatever works) and look for external video setting.
