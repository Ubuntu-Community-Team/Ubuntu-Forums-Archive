### Post by FurryNemesis on 2006-12-10
I had this problem with my Toshiba Satellite 5200-701 about a week ago. A reboot of the xserver (ctrl+alt+backspace) fixed the problem temporarily, as did a total reboot. May I suggest some compressed air down the fan vents, or failing that, check the fan for gunk? My eventual problem was a hair wrapped around the fan spindle. Degunk the fan bay and you should be ok. I was.
