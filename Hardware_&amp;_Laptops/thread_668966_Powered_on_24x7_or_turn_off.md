### Post by anachreon_ on 2008-01-16
Personally I think it is unethical to leave your computer on and unused 24x7.  If you're running distributed computing, for example, that's one thing.  But just wasting that much energy is absurd.  I recently calculated the greenhouse gas emissions my law firm emitted by leaving all of their computers on overnight : the technology people there were floored when I told them the results.  It amounted to several hundred thousand pounds of C02 per year just for our New York office - and we have dozens of offices globally.

Turn off your computer.  You can make a difference.
