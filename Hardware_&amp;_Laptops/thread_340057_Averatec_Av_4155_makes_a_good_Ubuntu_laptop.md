### Post by hardyn on 2007-01-16
*opinion warning*

my experience with averitecs' has not left a good taste in my mouth, i have never owned one, i have elected to pass them over.  There are a few fan sites on the web, and a few really scary photos, but every manuf. has examples of such.

in my experience they are not that much cheaper than a more recognised name brand. esp. with the cost of macbooks, and the LGs right now.

the big thing is to go through the specs, and pay attention to hardware specifically, wireless board, audio, video etc.  go for compatibility. that way you will know that you will not have to worry about compatibility.
