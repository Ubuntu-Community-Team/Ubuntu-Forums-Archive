### Post by sgleo87 on 2008-02-11
I would say most laptops are fairly well supported now but there are so many different ones out there that it is hard to say which one would be the best for you, it's a lot about personal preference.
I would suggest that you first find a laptop that you like and that fits your specifications/needs and then check online to see if there are any issues with it in Ubuntu. Besides googling "[your laptop] ubuntu" and checking the Ubuntu forums you can also check at [www.linux-laptop.net](www.linux-laptop.net) and [http://tuxmobil.org/mylaptops.html](http://tuxmobil.org/mylaptops.html). 
Also if you want Ubuntu preinstalled with everything working you can check out the Dell laptops that come with Ubuntu or System76 laptops.
