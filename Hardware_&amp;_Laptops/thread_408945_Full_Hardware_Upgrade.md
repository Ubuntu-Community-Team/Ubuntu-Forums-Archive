### Post by Tanker Bob on 2007-04-14
I just built  a new PC, only preserving my previous hard drives, DVD writer, floppy drive, and Zip drive.  After assembling the hardware, I cranked up my primary Kubuntu OS.  Kubuntu found the new LAN chipset, etc.) changed hardware (Intel Dual Core 6600, MSI P6N Platinum motherboard, NVidia 8800GTS video), and finished the boot effortlessly.  I had issues with the 8800GTS driver installation, but the generic video worked.  My hearty congratulations to the entire Ubuntu team for their robust design and implementation.

BTW, I tried to crank up my WinXP dual-boot setup, but it has yet to complete a boot-up, even in safe mode.  It keeps restarting my PC.  There goes another Windows myth...
