### Post by rkh on 2007-03-16
black & white is called "lineart." You should see it in the same dropdown menu as color and greyscale. 

As for other options, you might check out kooka -its in the repositories. Since my machine can make copies directly, I don't need a copy feature in my scanning program. With that in mind, I'd also suggest you check out gscan2pdf. There is currently no copy feature -although you can, of course, print out your scans manually- but it is very quick and easy to use. Check it out here:

[http://gscan2pdf.sourceforge.net/](http://gscan2pdf.sourceforge.net/)

Another plus is that the developer is an active participant in the forums.  Check out this thread for more information:

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=62636&highlight=gscan2pdf](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=62636&highlight=gscan2pdf)
