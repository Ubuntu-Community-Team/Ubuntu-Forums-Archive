### Post by lsutiger on 2007-06-09
I have a Dell 1501 (AMD Turion X2, 1GB, Broadcom Wireless, ATI Xpress 1150 video). It took a little work to get everything like I wanted, but just about everything worked out of the box. Wireless will take some work and if you want 3D desktop, Beryl works. The ATI Restricted drivers provided by Feisty work with a few mouse clicks.
I learned most of this from[ this guy's site.]("http://ubuntu1501.blogspot.com/") Also the best bang for the buck. $630 (US) on eBay!
