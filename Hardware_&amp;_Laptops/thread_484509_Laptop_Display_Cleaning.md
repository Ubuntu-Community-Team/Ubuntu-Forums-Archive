### Post by Greydog on 2007-06-25
Sorry to bring up an old thread but, I thought that there may be some folks wrestling with a dirty display wondering how best to clean it.

First, I am no expert and you may not have the same good results that I have had. 

For the last year I have been using Invisible Glass (tm) and those really great napkins from Logan's Steakhouse (tm) (order hot wings and ask the waiter for extras!) 

I spray the I-G (available at Auto Zone) on the napkin and gently wipe the screen, flip the napkin over and lightly buff dry.

Invisible Glass (tm) is safe for use on tinted windows (contains no amonia) and, so far, has worked really well for my laptop, desktop LCD monitor and LCD TV as well.

BTW, I-G works great on windows (no tm), too!

I hope that this is the proper place to post.

Greydog
