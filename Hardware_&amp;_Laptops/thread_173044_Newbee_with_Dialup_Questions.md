### Post by K2712 on 2006-05-10
You do not need to be a programmer to use Ubuntu.  I have absolutely zero programming knowledge yet Ubuntu has still become my primary OS.  Some (basic) knowledge of the command line is necessary to perform certain tasks within Ubuntu, but most procedures are clearly outlined either here in the forums or in the wiki.  If you need help with specific topics, such as your modem & floppy drive issues, it helps to be more specific when you ask your question, such as including your hardware information and error messages, that way people can help you.  Try reading these threads:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=35457](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=35457)
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=142716](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=142716)

[http://ubuntuguide.org/](http://ubuntuguide.org/)
