### Post by Golyadkin on 2007-08-26
Try putting the monitor on a higher refresh rate (frequency)   85Hz and up is flicker-free. However, your monitor has to support that for it to work. If you have an older monitor, or a 17" monitor, it may be limited to 60Hz which is horrible to look at.
