I can't speak for other but one reason that i haven't responded so far is the lack of information you have given.  Particularly where you are so we can see whether you need PAL or NTSC.  This link will help you though.

[http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)

For PAL I'm using a DVico USB2 tuner and am very happy with it.  However be aware that many USB tuners need 500mA through the USB port and with some motherboard chipsets this is a problem as they are not up to specification.  Further for HDTV you must have USB2 on your pc.
