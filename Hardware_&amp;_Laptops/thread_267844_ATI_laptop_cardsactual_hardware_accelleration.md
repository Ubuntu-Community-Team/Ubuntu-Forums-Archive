I've always used nvidia desktop cards in the past, and just recently bought a laptop with an ATI x1400.  ATI's proprietary drivers are pretty good.  I haven't had any problems with games, or XGL and Compiz.  I've used both the .26 and .27 drivers with success.

### Post by Melcar on 2006-09-29
ATI drivers are getting better, but they still have a ways to go. I have a 200M using the 8.29 drivers and I can run XGL rather well.  You may face problems with ATI drivers like the drivers "dying" on user logout (a common problem I hear).  No point in breaking the bank trying to get an nvidia based laptop if you can get a comparable ATI chip for less.
