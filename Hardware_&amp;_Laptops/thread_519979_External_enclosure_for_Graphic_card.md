### Post by DrPppr242 on 2007-08-07
I've never heard of such a thing but if it does exist I'm not sure it'd be worth your money, the reason video cards attach to the motherboard is because the speed of pci express or agp is so much faster than that of firewire or usb most agp slots run at 8x which has a speed of approximately 2 Gb a sec so if you were to put your new card over a firewire or usb bus you wouldn't get the advantage you paid for, so rather than making external enclosures for agp cards manufactors make pci, not pci express versions of their slow cards for people with no agp slot.
