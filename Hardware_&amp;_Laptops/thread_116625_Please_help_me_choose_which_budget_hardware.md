### Post by handy on 2006-01-15
The AMD64 CPUs have proven that their intended design - to be a 64bit CPU that is fully 32bit compatible - works very well indeed.  No ifs or buts about it!  Just look at all the 32bit winxp's out there running as well as they can (poor things:rolleyes: ) on 64bit AMD CPUs

The nVidia GPUs have the best drivers, are the easiest to install, & you won't find one post here in the forums where someone is going to, or has traded, (down)graded from an nVidia graphics card to an ATI.

There are lots of posts, where ATI users are cursing their ATI graphics card & wish that they had, or are about to, or allready have changed to an nVidia card.

If you go Intel or ATI, you have no one but yourself to blame.
