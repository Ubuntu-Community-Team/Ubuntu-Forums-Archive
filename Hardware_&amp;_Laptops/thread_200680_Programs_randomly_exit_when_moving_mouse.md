This happen both when I use the trackpad on my laptop or a USB mouse connected to my laptop.

I have seen this happen using on the following programs.
firefox
gnome-terminal
rhythmbox
gnome-panel

My laptop is a Toshiba Satelite M30. I have not seen this happen on my other Ubuntu boxes.
