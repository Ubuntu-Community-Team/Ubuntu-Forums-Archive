### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-02-11
Opterons will be faster than their X2 counterparts at the same clock speed in applications that can take advantage of the Optys' cache (e.g. gaming). They're also exceptional OC'ers (though the current X2's do well there too). Only you can decide whether that's worth the extra money or not.
