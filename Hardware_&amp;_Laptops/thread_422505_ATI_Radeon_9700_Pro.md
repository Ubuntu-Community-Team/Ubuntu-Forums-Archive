### Post by strabes on 2007-04-25
The 9700 pro does not support the open source driver. (from [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto))

You'll have to use fglrx and that means no composite desktop support (AIGLX/beryl/compiz). Buy the nvidia.
