### Post by wallijonn on 2004-12-29
With Windows XP you can set the Audio to Digital sound whereby it uses the ribbon cable to route the sound signal. With Linux you need to use the Analogue sound cable (small slim 4 pin cable) between the sound card and the back of the CD & DVD players. It can play .mp3s, though, just not regular CDs without the slim cable.
