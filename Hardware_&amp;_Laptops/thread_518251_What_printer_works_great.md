### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-08-05
Look for a used officejet 7110.  You can override the ink settings and refill the ink wells.  You can add a network card and a duplexer to make it more versatile.  The 7210 has built-in ethernet, but reliability is poor.  I know two that have crapped out in one year.  Also they changed the cartridge configuration and the cartridges now expire and can't be refilled effectively.

In general HP's have good support under Linux, but they are expensive to print with and reliability is generally poor on the newer printers.  Old Laserjet 4's are good for black and white printing--for coding.
