### Post by thetazland on 2008-03-29
I went through a lot of time google serching this and spent much time in the forums to resolve this issue. I had two LG drives on the system, one  LG CD rewriter and one LG Super Multi Drive. For some reason Ubuntu Gutsy would see the CD rewriter but not the Super Multi drive. The original hardware was setup with the CD rewriter as the Master and the Super Multi as the Slave. Finally I switched the CD rewriter to Slave and the Super Multi to Master and there it was, problem solved. I can't explain why but that was the only thing that worked. :-)
