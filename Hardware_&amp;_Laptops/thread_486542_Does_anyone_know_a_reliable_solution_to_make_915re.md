Thanks for your rant 101, but not needed.  If you want someone to help you will have to be a little clearer as to the specifics of your situation.  I am not familiar with your hardware so maybe you could start by telling us a little bit about it; specifically: what video card does it run and what are the resolution specs you are expecting, and what all you did to get it to the point you are at.

I have 2 laptops, one wide screen on which ubuntu/915resolution automatically sized to it's proper dimensions after reboot.  915resolution, if you read the description, is for the intel chipset, including the 845G,
855G, and 865G, as well as 915G, 915GM, and 945G.

If you are running nVidia, or ATI that's another story and you would not use 915resolution and there are plenty of tutorials and wikis available on this forum if you search for it.
