### Post by djgenesis on 2005-12-22
In your conf
```

Section "Device"
Identifier "ATI Technologies, Inc. Radeon 7000 VE (RV100 QY)0"
Driver "ati"
VideoRam 32768
**BusID "PCI:1:0:0"**
EndSection

Section "Device"
Identifier "ATI Technologies, Inc. Radeon 7000 VE (RV100 QY)1"
Driver "ati"
#VideoRam 32768
**BusID "PCI:1:0:0"**
EndSection
[code]

the lines in bold define the BusID of your cards. I may be wrong but you are telling it to output on the same adapter, the primary "BusID "PCI:1:0:0"
Probe your PCIs with [code] lspci 
``` and copy the BusID of your secondary adapter to device: **Identifier "ATI Technologies, Inc. Radeon 7000 VE (RV100 QY)1**.
