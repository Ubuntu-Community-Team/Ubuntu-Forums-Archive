### Post by locutus42 on 2008-02-11
let me get this straight, you are on a Ubuntu forum so I would think you are talking about a Ubuntu host with a MS Windows VM under VMware. Now, you want to update your hardware/computer BIOS from Windows running inside of VMWare?  I would not try this unless you hear directly from VMWare on how the VMWare drivers access the BIOS.

Best to post on one of their forums unless someone here knows the internals of VMWare. Do they not have a DOS update version instead of Windows? If they do, you should be able to download any of a dozen or so DOS CDROM images and use that to install your BIOS update.  Good luck.
