### Post by ijason on 2007-12-26
almost all computers are compatible with ubuntu. you'd have to do a device by device comparison with the approved hardware list to make 100% sure than everything will work 100% well, but ubuntu is very good at working on every platform. 

that said, why are you particularly looking at this laptop? you can get a new dell for the same price with similar hard-ware specs. then you get a one-year warranty and new equipment rather than refurbished.

i just say dell because that's what i have, i'm sure that HP or other vendors have comparable machines as well.
