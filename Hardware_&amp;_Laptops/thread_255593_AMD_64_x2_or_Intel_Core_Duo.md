### Post by frank_costanza on 2006-09-13
I went with the Turion x2.

Turion is 64-bit and I wanted the ability to mess around with 64-bit OSes.  Core Duo is 32-bit, so that was out.  Core 2 Duo is 64-bit but the machine was a few hundred dollars more with that configuration.  Since I turn over laptops every few years, I figured the Turion's performance was good enough to last a few years.

Power consumption wasn't a big factor for me since I'm usually plugged in anyway.
