### Post by Whitewolfe on 2005-06-12
Not sure about your brightness issue.  However, I've had excellent results using ndiswrapper with my own unrecognized wireless card.  What you need to do is to find out what sort of wireless card your machine has.  Then get the windows driver files ( {drivername}.inf and {drivername}.sys).  Once you have those, installing the wireless is very very easy.  Feel free to PM me if you need more help.
