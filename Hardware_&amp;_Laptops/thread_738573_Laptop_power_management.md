### Post by Resurrection on 2008-03-28
Check your BIOS settings on startup.  You may be able to control power management or fan settings from within BIOS.  Make sure you have the most up to date BIOS for your laptop from Toshiba installed.  

I have a Toshiba Satellite which had the fan spinning quite fast and loudly when I first did a recent install of Gutsy on it.  After updating Ubuntu with the latest updates, the problem stopped.  

Sorry I can't give more specific help, but basically make sure you have the latest updates installed, then the latest BIOS from Toshiba, then check BIOS settings if the problem persists.  Maybe someone else can give more specific guidance.
