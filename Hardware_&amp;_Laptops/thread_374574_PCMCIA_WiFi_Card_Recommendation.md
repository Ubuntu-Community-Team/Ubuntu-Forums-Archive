### Post by bwiewiora on 2007-03-03
I know what you mean--I spent many confusing and frustrating hours trying to get a wireless card working, and then gave up and bought one that is listed as working.  The one I bought is a Netgear WG511T.  It wasn't the cheapest (around $50), but it did work right out of the box, so I felt it was worth it.
