I got ltmodem (for which search this forum) working with wvdial for the modem. At home I use my wireless to router to cable modem, However when I travel, I sometimes find locations where dial-up is the *only* option. Works perfectly on my T23.

Great lappy for the money ($435 at Ebay), robust and capable.
