Hello all,

After over a year I discovered the fix to when you insert your headphones into a Toshiba laptop the speakers mute.  It actually auto mutes the speakers when you insert headphones.

Follow this:

[http://www.stchman.com/feisty_tips.html](http://www.stchman.com/feisty_tips.html)

Refer to the mute speakers when headphones are used.

It works beautifully.  My laptop has an Intel HDA type sound card made by Realtek.  I just needed to update ALSA to 1.0.16.

Hope this helps.
