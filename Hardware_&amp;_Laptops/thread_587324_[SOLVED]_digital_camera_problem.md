I was fooling around with my camera and i found a temporarily solution, when i connect it to the usb port it has three options: PicBridge, PTP, Normal. It's in normal by default, but just to see what happened i changed it to PTP and PicBridge, and ubuntu recognized the camera with both options. Now i can import photos from my camera.
