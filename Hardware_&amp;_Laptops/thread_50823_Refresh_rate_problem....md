### Post by DancingSun on 2005-08-23
Try commenting out the refresh rates in the monitor section, and changing the Identifer to your monitor's name:
```
Section "Monitor"
Identifier "LG F900P"
Option "DPMS"
#HorizSync 30-111
#VertRefresh 50-200
EndSection
```
