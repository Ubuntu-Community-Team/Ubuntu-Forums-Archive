### Post by eggdeng on 2006-11-18
Hope I'm not insulting your intelligence, but the first thing to do is check whether your user is a member of the audio group. I'm sure you know how but just in case you don't: System->Adiministration->Users and Groups. Select your user and rightclick for properties. On the priviliges tab, make sure Use Audio Devices is enabled.
