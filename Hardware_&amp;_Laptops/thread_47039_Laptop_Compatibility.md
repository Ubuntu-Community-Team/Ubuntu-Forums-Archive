### Post by nocturn on 2005-07-07
I think this is internally the same as the HP zv6000 series, although I have to tell you that there are numerous problems with it.

If you run a search on zv6000, you'll find a thread about them.

The issues seem:
- the ATI drivers
- Problems with the realtime clock, need a patched kernel or 2.6.12 kernel.
- Broadcom Wlan needs Ndiswrapper (should work though)

I was considering the zv6000 series too, but I looked arround and ordered a zv5000 instead (R3000 series), even though it cost 200 € extra (for an older machine).

I hope you get it sorted out.
