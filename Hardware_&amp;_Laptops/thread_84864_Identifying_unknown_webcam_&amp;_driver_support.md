### Post by jdawdy on 2005-11-01
In my experience, Linux doesn't do webcams very well.  Even if there is a kernel driver and linux recognizes the webcam, I still have a lot of problems with the applications accessing the webcam.

This goes especially for wierd/generic webcams.  However, the chipset may be supported under linux.

Start here [http://www.linux.com/howtos/Webcam-HOWTO/index.shtml](http://www.linux.com/howtos/Webcam-HOWTO/index.shtml)
