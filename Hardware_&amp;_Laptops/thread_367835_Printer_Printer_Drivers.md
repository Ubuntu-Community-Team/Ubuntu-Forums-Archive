### Post by gnoel on 2007-03-13
The pricing structure for inkjet printers (cheap hardware / expensive consumables) plus some peoples' infatuation with new photo printers has created a weird marketplace for used printers.  I was able to replace my clogged-up Epson with an older HP model by buying used on Craigslist.  The price I paid was 1/3 the cost of the new black and color ink cartridges that were included in the deal.  Plus, the old HP printers have high capacity ink cartridges and work great in Linux :) 

Why buy new?
