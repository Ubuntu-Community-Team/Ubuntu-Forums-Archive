### Post by georgeg on 2007-09-05
I don't think you'll find a scanner that works out of the box with Fiesty or even Gutsy.  Most all of the scanners supported by the SANE project still fail in Fiesty due to a bug in the core program.  Some people have been able to get their scanners to work but most have not.  At the moment, this is the biggest drawback to Ubuntu and its derived distros that I know of.
