### Post by xyz on 2006-10-10
In the event that you use gnome:

in the menu go to Preferences -> Sessions.
On Gnome Session window go to the tab 'Startup programs', press "Add" and add the start-up program.
> numlockx on
OR the wiki:

[http://easylinux.info/wiki/Ubuntu_dapper#How_to_turn_on_Num_Lock_on_GNOME_startup](http://easylinux.info/wiki/Ubuntu_dapper#How_to_turn_on_Num_Lock_on_GNOME_startup)
