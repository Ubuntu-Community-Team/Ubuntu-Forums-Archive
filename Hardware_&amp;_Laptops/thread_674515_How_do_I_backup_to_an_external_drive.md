### Post by nick_h on 2008-01-22
The first thing to look at is System->Administration->Simple Backup Config
There is a guide on [Ubuntu Geek](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/backup-and-restore-your-ubuntu-system-using-sbackup.html).

Or you could use tar - there is a howto on these forums - [ Howto: Backup and restore your system!](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=35087)

Or you could use rsync.  Have a look at the manual page for details.

Or you could just copy files and directories.
