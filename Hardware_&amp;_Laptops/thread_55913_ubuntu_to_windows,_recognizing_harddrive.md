### Post by varunus on 2005-08-10
A couple of things:

Is the windows HD external or internal?  If its USB external, just plug it in and an icon will appear on your desktop; drag and drop.

If its internal, its a little more difficult, but not hard.  Follow the instructions here:
[http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#windows](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#windows)

This will let you mount your windows HD.

If you just want media files, you can mount it as mentioned in the UbuntuGuide and then just have your programs play your music from the Windows HD, that's what I do.
