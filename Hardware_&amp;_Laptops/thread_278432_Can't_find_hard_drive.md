### Post by ajgreeny on 2006-10-16
Sounds rather as if your hard disk has died completely.  Perhaps try Knoppix to see if it is detected, or even download Gparted LiveCD on another machine and burn the image to a CD and try that.

I suppose it could simply a bad cable connection, but how you deal with that in a laptop I don't know.  If it was a desktop it is simple to un-attach and re-attach the ribbon cable but perhaps more difficult with a laptop.  Have a good look at your laptop manual for hard disk upgrade info - it may show you how to remove it and then put it back.
