### Post by Fire_Chief on 2008-04-02
Hi Larry,

Unfortunately, you haven't stated what result you are seeking so we don't have a solid direction to pursue in helping you. But I'll get the ball rolling...

1. Is the touchpad disabled in the Thinkpad BIOS? (I usually do this as I prefer the "eraser head" pointer over the touchpad.) If it is not disabled, is it physically broken or just not recognized in Ubuntu?

2. Since it's a laptop, the odds are that the USB port is soldered directly to the motherboard. If that is the case, then the most likely cause is the soldering is breaking loose. You could try re-soldering it but I do not recommend this.

I assume you are experiencing problems using Ubuntu on the laptop as a result of these two conditions. Any additional info you could offer would be helpful. :)
