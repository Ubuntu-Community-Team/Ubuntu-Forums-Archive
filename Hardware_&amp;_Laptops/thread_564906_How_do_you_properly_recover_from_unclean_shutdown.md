### Post by Linicks on 2007-10-01
Tough question.

First, always look to see if HD led is on after a crash - sometimes that is bad news and a hard reset can corrupt disc - but sometimes LED is stuck on and drive is not being used/held open etc. even though it might seem to be.

From experience, if the machine comes up OK after a reset, I let it all load and leave it for a few minutes (without doing anything), then shutdown cleanly and turn off.

Wait 1 minute.

Then bring it back up again. Hopefully nothing on the drive is damaged.

If it doesn't reboot after a crash, then you have other issues which need to be addressed when that happens (obviously).
