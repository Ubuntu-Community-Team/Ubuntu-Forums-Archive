### Post by CC_machine on 2007-05-29
quite simply, anything nvidia should work great. I know 6600s can be dodgy as I have one and it often defaults to 1280x1024 85hz which my monitor cannot handle well as it has to downscale the image. What's your price range? I'd recommend a 7600GT or 6800GT if you can afford either, or a 7300GS as tgm4883 says if you're looking for a more budget card.

Note: be sure to get a card with the right interface! if your motherboard is AGP you'll need an AGP card, PCI-express will need a pci-express card etc.
