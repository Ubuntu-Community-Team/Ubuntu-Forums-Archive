### Post by marfal on 2008-07-21
**[B]Solved**[/B]

What I did:
Download "xfree86-driver-synaptics" from
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/xfree86-driver-synaptics](http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/xfree86-driver-synaptics)

uninstall "xserver-xorg-input-synaptics"
install the "xfree86-driver-synaptics" deb package and reboot.
You should have a working scroll wheel again.
