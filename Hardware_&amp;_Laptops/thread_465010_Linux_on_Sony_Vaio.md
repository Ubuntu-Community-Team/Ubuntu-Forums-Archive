### Post by Death_Sargent on 2007-06-05
Take this with a grain of salt. 

it will run faster than windows because it makes use of its resources better.

For about a week until you finish tweaking it you may notice it being a bit buggy. 

Graphics will be like crap until you install 915video (for an intel card) or switch up to proprietary drivers for your video card. 

You don't own a very good laptop so nothing to teribly complicating will work any better. 

You will notice a slight increase in battery life (maybe ten or twenty minnutes if you don't use fiesty)

Finally if you have a built in card reader it might not work out of the box and your wifi may require some slight configuration though i doubt it.
