### Post by MrHippocampus on 2007-07-31
You probably have your lineIn/microphone volume up too high, try running "alsamixer" in a terminal and turn down/mute (mute by pressing m, a volume bar will display MM at the bottom if its muted and OO if it isnt, not all bars can be muted) anything which looks like its for recording (you may have to press tab to be able to move through all of the bars).
