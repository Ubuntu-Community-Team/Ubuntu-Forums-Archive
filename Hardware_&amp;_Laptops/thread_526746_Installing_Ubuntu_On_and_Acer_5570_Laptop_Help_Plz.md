### Post by logos34 on 2007-08-15
C: is your vista boot partition/drive with system files and installed programs; D: has your saved files and is probably considerably larger.  Use the vista disk management utility to shrink D:, or let the Ubuntu installer do it for you (using 'guided install' option).  Defrag it first.
