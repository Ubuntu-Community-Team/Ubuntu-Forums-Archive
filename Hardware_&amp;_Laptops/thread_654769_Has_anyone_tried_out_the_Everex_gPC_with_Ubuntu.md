TigerDirect is selling these; you can read some comments from people who've bought them on their comment page.

apparently the windows "press" people, the reviewers etc, really hate this machine - they equate it with a yugo.

however, at least one comment said that you could load linux (ubuntu?) on it, and it works  fine.  the Tigerdirect commenters have a lively debate there that you can read.
