### Post by ROBarber on 2007-11-11
it seems that on /dev/sdb where you have installed ubuntu is not a master HDD. also you don;t have a boot flag on your sdb1 partition where the system files should be.

i recommend you to make a fresh installation of your OS after making the HDD master (the one where you want to install the system).

when you'll have to make your partitions, mount one for / (10 GB I think will be enough) one for swap (around 1Gb) and the rest of the space you want to use mount it to /home, do you'll be able to use it properly..
