### Post by tas50 on 2006-04-12
A lot of laptops have issues with resizing the screen.   Usually in the BIOS there is an option to stretch the image to the entire screen.  Otherwise it will just occupy a small box containing 800X600 pixels.   You will drop quality quite a bit when you have it strech 640X480 or 800X600 to 1024X768.
