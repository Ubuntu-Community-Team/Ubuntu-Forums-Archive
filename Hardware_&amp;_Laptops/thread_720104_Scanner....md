### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-03-09
I can recommend a few Epson Scanners that work very nicely in 32bit Ubuntu (not 64bit unfortunantly, due to epson using propietary 32bit binaries).

These are:
Epson V200, V350, V700, V750.

For more info about how to get them working etc, visit this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=627650](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=627650)

Many HP scanners also work, but the most recent scanners as sold by HP may not work with Ubuntu.
