### Post by Sef on 2006-07-26

### Post by rmjb on 2006-07-26
Sorry, I got this resolved. In the Printer Options page in CUPS the 2nd section allowed me to choose the default Printout Mode and from there I chose 300 dpi Color + Black and now things work correctly.

For some reason I was not seeing that option at all when I first installed the printer.

Thanks for your help though.
