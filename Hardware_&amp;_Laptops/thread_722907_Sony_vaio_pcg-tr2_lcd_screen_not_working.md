### Post by jwedekin on 2008-03-19
It is black from startup? If you don't see the "Sony Vaio" splash screen or have the option to enter bios, then it is a hardware problem. Ubuntu will have no effect on the POST of the laptop. 

One suggestion, if it is hardware, try removing one stick of RAM at a time...and try using different RAM slots. Sometimes one of those old Vaio RAM slots will go bad and keep the laptop from POSTING.
