### Post by CompShrink on 2006-04-16
On the top panel, go to System > Administration > Synaptic package manager. Install multisync and libmultisync-plugin-all.

Then you can go to System > Preferences > Removable Drives and Media. Under the PDA, check the second box and it should sync automatically when you connect it.
