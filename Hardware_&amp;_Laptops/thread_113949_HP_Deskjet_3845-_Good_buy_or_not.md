### Post by OneEyedMan on 2007-01-18
I bought one a while back for my wife to use for her home business. So far it's done pretty well. It's simple, decent print quality, and seems okay on ink usage. I haven't tried it for photo printing, but for a small home office it does everything she needs.

The only drawbacks I've seen so far is the small paper tray means you refill the paper a lot, and the front-ejecting output can occasionally overflow and dump to the floor if you're printing a lot of stuff. But for the price, we've really found it a good deal.

Keep in mind, she is using it on a WinXP mahcine.

Hope that helps.
