### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-12-04
Hi. After copying something, if you want to replace whatever is in the address bar, then double click the URL to highlight it and paste using right-click, not middle click; the middle-click will paste whatever's highlighted at the time you press the middle mouse button, since that's what stored in the video buffer memory. So, again, use right-click to paste after highlighting the url you want to replace.
