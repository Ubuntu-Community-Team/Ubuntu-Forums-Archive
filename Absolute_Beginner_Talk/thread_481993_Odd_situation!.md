### Post by Inxsible on 2007-06-23
When you moved to Ubuntu, did you remove Kubuntu or simply installed the ubuntu-desktop ?

Thats probably why you start off with Kubuntu splash screen and then since you might have the gdm (as opposed to kdm)as default it logs into Ubuntu. If you havent removed Kubuntu, then on the log in screen if you click on Options, there should be a choice to log into kdm - which is the K Desktop manager and you will log into Kubuntu.
