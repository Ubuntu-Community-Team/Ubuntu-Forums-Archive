### Post by crossedout on 2007-02-28
Open up your Home folder, press ctrl+h.
Navigate to .icons/<THEMENAME>/<SIZE>/apps
Paste your icon into that directory and name it distributor-logo.png

That's assuming you have installed a theme of your own and are not using the default icon theme.  Its much easier to install a theme and then modify that to your liking.  [[COLOR="DarkOrange"]gnome-look.org[/COLOR]]("http://www.gnome-look.org") for new themes.
