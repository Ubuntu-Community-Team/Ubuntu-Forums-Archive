### Post by katiad on 2006-09-26
If you're happy with what you've got, and you want nothing more, I see no reason to upgrade. However, that said, Dapper will have long term support, whereas Breezy... not really so much. If you /are/ going to upgrade, I'd upgrade to Dapper, not Edgy - as Dapper's been out for nearly six months and thus, will naturally have more *resolved* bugs than Edgy - and stability seems important to you.

However, upgrade at your leisure. If you're happy, keep it. :) I have known many a Windows geek to be perfectly happy with Windows 98 - the newest edition of whatever OS/software is not always the right one for everyone.
