### Post by user1397 on 2006-04-25
Not to disappoint, but you could have gotten that jpeg image out of there easily.  You could have just right-click on the panel, properties, background, and choose solod color instead of background image!

As for the bugs, I don't know - sorry! :(

Just as a suggestion, always look for solutions- right-clicking is one of the best options for anything, as it'll give you many options usually.  In other words, don't be so drastic and delete anything that *seems *to have gone haywire.

Also try adding "window list" and "notification area"

Maybe also "clock" and "volume control"
