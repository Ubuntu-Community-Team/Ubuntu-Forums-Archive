### Post by wirelessmonkey on 2008-02-25
Try reseating your RAM, and blowing out all the dust and whatnot with compressed air.  Then run memtest again.  IF you still get errors, and are having significant problems with your installations, you may have to replace your RAM.  Most RAM errors cannot be fixed, re-imaging would do no good whatsoever.
