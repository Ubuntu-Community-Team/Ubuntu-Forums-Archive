### Post by angry_johnnie on 2008-03-17
In order to have a different wallpaper for each one of the cube's faces, you had to tell Nautilus to stop drawing the desktop.  That's why you're not seeing any icons.  It's not an error, and there's nothing you can do about it, really.  If you want your icons back.  You'll have to settle for just one wallpaper.
