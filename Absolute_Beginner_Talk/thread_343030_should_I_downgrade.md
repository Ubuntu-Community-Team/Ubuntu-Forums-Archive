### Post by euler_fan on 2007-01-20
I have a similar conundrum: I am currently running 64-bit Edgy and can't get the wireless working (Broadcom 4318 card, no surprise there). ](*,)  In Dapper or 32-bit Edgy I would say go for ndiswrapper, but it doesn't work with the 64-bit kernel. Hopefully they fix that one of these days.

I am going to agree with the others that you should downgrade or dual-boot with 32-bit Dapper or Edgy. I haven't tried 64-bit Dapper.

I can say unless you need the power, there is not much benefit from running 64-bit and you'll probably get more bang for your cycle by learning a lighter windowing system rather than running 64-bit.

However, I encourage you to keep 64-bit around somewhere to increase the demand to get some of the issues in 64-bit Edgy addressed (even if it is in the next release) ;) 

Good Luck!
