### Post by fyleow on 2006-07-18
Is there a way to get thumbnails for the icons of video files that are on network drives?  I have some files on my other computer that just have the default regular icon but when I drag it to my desktop it will load with a proper preview.

I tried following the instructions in the wiki [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SolvingGnomeThumbnailsTroauble?highlight=%28thumbnail%29") but no luck

---

### Post by reacocard on 2006-07-18
open a nautilus window. go to edit -> preferences. under the preview tab, set show thumbnails to always.
