### Post by IYY on 2007-01-30
Ubuntu is an African philosophy with several meanings:

The simplest translation of the word is "humanness" or "humanity". Other translations are "humanity towards others", "I am who I am through other people".

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu)

Recall that Ubuntu the GNU/Linux distribution was started and still has its home in South Africa, so the reason for the name is fairly obvious. Also, the idea is to make an operating system "For Human Beings", meaning that it has an active community, user-oriented interfaces, and a friendly, natural look.
