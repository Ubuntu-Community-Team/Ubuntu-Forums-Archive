### Post by Presto123 on 2008-01-27
If you are running it from the live disk, you probably wont be able to do what I have following. If you have it installed onto your system, this is what you can do:

Connect your computer to a wired connection first. Then go into "Restricted Drivers Manager" under System/Administration. When that pops up, it should list the WIFI device as "Firmware", if not don't go any further and report back here.

If it shows, click on "Enable" and a popup will, umm...pop up. Just click on the selection that allows you to pull from the internet. It will automatically find the driver needed and install and your light/WIFI should be enabled.

Hope that works for you. :)
