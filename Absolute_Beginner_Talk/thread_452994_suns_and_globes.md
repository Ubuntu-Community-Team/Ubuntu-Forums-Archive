### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-05-26
Hi. Those symbols are ways to contact that person: the butterfly is MSN, the face is Yahoo! messenger, and there are more, like Skype, Jabber, ICQ. You can configure those in your account's control panel, so they show up for you too (click on **User CP** near the top of the page and select "Edit Profile").
