### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-05
Well when I go to psychocats it looks plain black to me, maybe my comp is slightly different. Anyway, I know what your referring to...

Open Firefox, go to Edit > Preferences > Content Tab. This is where your font is set, you can choose your defailt font and size here in the basic pane. Then click advanced when your happy, in the advanced tab there is a button that says "Allow web pages to choose their own font, instead of my selection" Uncheck this. All done :)

That should always force the Text to display as your preference.
