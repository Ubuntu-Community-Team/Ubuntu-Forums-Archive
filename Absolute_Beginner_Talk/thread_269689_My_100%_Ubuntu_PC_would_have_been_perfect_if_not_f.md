### Post by Sef on 2006-10-02
Your printer should work just fine.  If you can't get it going under System > Administration > Printing, then check out [LinuxPrinting.]("http://linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Epson-Stylus_C45")

Your scanner is unsupported at the moment, though there are plans to support it in the future.  See this page at the [Sane-Project.]("http://www.sane-project.org/cgi-bin/driver.pl?manu=Genius&model=ColorPage&bus=any&v=&p=")
