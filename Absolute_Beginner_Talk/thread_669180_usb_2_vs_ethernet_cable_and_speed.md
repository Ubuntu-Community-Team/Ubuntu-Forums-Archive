### Post by ARhere on 2008-01-16
> **arvindenrique said:**
> which one is fast , reliable and best? USB or ETHERNET?

I think the first two replies said it best.  USB and Ethernet were engineered for two completely different purposes.

The CAT5, 5e, 6... cables for Ethernet were designed to carry signals at medium distances with a LOT of research going into twisted pair and common mode rejection.

The USB connector standard was engineered for ease of use for an end user for master to slave communications.

I think I know what you are asking about though.  There is a lot of Ethernet devices that allow you to plug in a USB cable instead of a CAT5e, like the new Linksys cable/DSL modems for internet access.  In that case, stick with 'Ethernet'.  Less driver/software complexity and faster.
