### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-07-19
If it doesn't appear under the Applications menu somewhere, you can open the terminal and type 
```
whereis *appnamehere*
```
Then you can create a launcher on the desktop, in a menu, or on a panel.
Programs are typically installed to /usr/bin or just /bin
