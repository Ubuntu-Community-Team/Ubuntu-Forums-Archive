### Post by carlosfocker on 2007-02-08
Heres an example of what my users needed.  The call center accesses a web app that provided customer info but in a format that is very tall.  Turning a wide screen monitor sideways and pivoting the screen allows them to have a very tall rather than wide monitor to few customer info in one glance rather than scrolling up and down and up again.
