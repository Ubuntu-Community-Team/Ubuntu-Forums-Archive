### Post by cozmicharlie on 2008-02-06
> **dynamethod said:**
> Dont you have to forward that port on your router?

Yes you do.  Go to [http://www.portforward.com](http://www.portforward.com) and search for your router.  It walks you through the process.  Once done you can check in Azureus if you are connected - in the menu go to tools>NAT/firewall test and run the test.  If it says OK you are good to go.  IF not then it is your router or you are firewalled.  

Good luck and post back if you are successful.
