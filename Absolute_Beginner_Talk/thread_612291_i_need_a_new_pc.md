### Post by white_sox_fan_82 on 2007-11-13
Hmm, I've always found New Egg to be great price-wise...

In terms of the motherboard/processor fitting, the mobo specs will tell you the types of socket it has on it.  Just match this to the type of processor that would fit.  You can also find this info in the details of the processor description, just do your research.

For the case, again, you have to look at the details of both the case and the mobo, which will give you all the info you need.

Barebones means a system that has only the parts to get it to run, nothing fancy, just a working machine.

Hope this helps...
