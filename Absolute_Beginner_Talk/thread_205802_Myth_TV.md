### Post by Titus A Duxass on 2006-07-02
If you are, as you say you are, a total beginner then the mythtv install may just be beyond your present capabilities.

If you follow Hyam's MythTV How-To it does work.  However I would recommend setting up a separate machine just to practice the install.

My tip: Do not just blindy cut+paste the instructions from the how-to into a terminal, type the commands in and use the tab key to complete them for you.  That way any variations will be automatically dealt with.

### Post by redelephant on 2006-07-03
If you are a beginner and have a separate box, you might want to look into [Knoppmyth]("http://www.mysettopbox.tv"). You can do an auto-install where you basically just have to answer some questions. If you are installing to a SATA drive, I think you will have to do a manual install.

I think the Hyams guide is just about the best guide for MythTV on Ubuntu. If you follow that one closely you should be okay.
