### Post by emarkd on 2008-02-04
Those are generally referred to as Virtual Terminals and all you've got running there is your login shell, which the Ubuntu default is BASH.  You can do a little customizing there, such as the type of prompt shown, etc, by editing your .bashrc file in your home directory.  A search for .bashrc examples would probably reveal lots of information.
