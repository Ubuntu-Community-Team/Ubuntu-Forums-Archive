### Post by mhl12 on 2006-02-12
you first have to burn the .iso image using a image burner such as Nero or Alcohol 120%. Make sure you select the image burning option.

Next make sure that your computer is set to boot off the cd. When you turn on your computer. Press F12 right when it turns on to load the bios options. Press enter again on the first menu and keep on pressing the right arrow key until you get to the list of which boots first. The instructions on the screen will tell you how to change the order. Then put your boot cd in your cd drive and restart your computer.
