### Post by madcow72 on 2006-09-21
I just finished a rapid flurry of different Linux distro installations to compare Ubuntu/Kubuntu/Suse to do a comparison.  I have now uninstalled Suse 10.1 from both of my machines and replaced 'em with Kubuntu 6.06.  In my opinion, Suse 10.1 was much slower (Yast took up to 3-4 minutes to open!), although it seemed to have excellent support for drivers.  For example, my video card, wireless card, and TV Tuner card worked right off the bat.  However, Ubuntu has much better community support than Suse and the Adept/Apt-get package managers are way way easier to use than Yast. In my opinion.  I'm sold on (K)Ubuntu.
