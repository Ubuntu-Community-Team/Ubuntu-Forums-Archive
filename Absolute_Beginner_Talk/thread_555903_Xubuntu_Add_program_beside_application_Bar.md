### Post by kerry_s on 2007-09-20
> **Orbital75 said:**
> I'd like to add ThunderBird and XMMS to my top panel.
Firefox comes already there by default but I'd like Thunderbird
and XMMS to be there as well.  Could someone explain how to
add them and get the correct icons as well.  thank you.

This is easy in Ubuntu but is a little different in Xubuntu because
of XFCE.

right click on the panel> add new item> drag & drop launcher where you want.

now right click the launcher icon> properties

now open xfce4-appfinder> drag & drop your application to the big window on the left in the properties window, use the minus(-) sign to delete the empty launcher.

hey what do you know, no typing what so ever.

xfce4 is very drag & dropable, you can do all kinds of things just by drag & drop, even changing the icons just by dragging & dropping from thunar to the icon pic in properties, it will auto put the path.
