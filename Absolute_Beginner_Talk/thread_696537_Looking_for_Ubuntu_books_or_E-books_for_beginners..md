### Post by pedro_orange on 2008-02-14
If you want to buy a book about Ubuntu, then go ahead. 

But with Operating systems, things change quite fast. Buying a book, will no doubt be out of date within 6months/ a year. 

What I would recommend is using forums, and wikis to find out info you need to know. Then make a note of it yourself. I have a wiki on my local machine that I use to keep all the info I learn in one place. 

Also, just play around! You're not going to break your computer if you're not in root. So don't worry!
