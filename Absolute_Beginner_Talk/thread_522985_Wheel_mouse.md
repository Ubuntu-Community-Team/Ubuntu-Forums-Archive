### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-08-11
> **philinux said:**
> Have you clicked on system > preferences > mouse to see whats available there?

I can assure ya, said feature (assigning multiple functions to middle click) is not there. Mouse Properties only offers basic customization with regards to sensitivity, mouse appearance and click preferences. Don't let that stop you from looking though, always good to tweak your mouse feel just right.

If you really want the software, you may want to consider writing an email to the support of logitech and ask them why they don't support the OS of your choice. Other than that, I'm afraid your out of luch with regards to that specific feature (barring a custom xorg write but wouldn't give you same functionality).
