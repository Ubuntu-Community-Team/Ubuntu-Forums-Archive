### Post by adwait on 2006-11-22
Here try this: [http://ale.freeshell.org/articles/hotkeys/hotkeys.html](http://ale.freeshell.org/articles/hotkeys/hotkeys.html)

It worked for me. Don't create the initial scripts though. Just follow the instructions to assign extended F- keys to your multimedia keys (eg: F13 F14 F15 F16 etc). After that you can set the short cuts like you normally do in KDE.
