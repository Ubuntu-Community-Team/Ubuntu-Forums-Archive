### Post by kudu on 2005-10-17
Yes it's possible. You need to change your sources.list to point to Breezy instead of Hoary but I HIGHLY recommend burning a 5.10 iso and installing fresh from the CD. I think most of the trouble upgrading to Breezy is due to not installing fresh.

If you want to go the upgrade route check the starter guide for a copy of the Breezy sources.list file.
