### Post by Nano Geek on 2007-12-13
As far as I know, you cannot do that in OOo. Sorry

As for hibernate and suspend, that's a tricky issue. Unfortunately, every computer vendor does it differently, and while Windows gets all the information from the vendors, Linux has to try to do it on it's own. Because of this, hibernate and suspend work on some machines and don't on others. So the best advice is to be patient and hope that the next release will make it work on your computer.
