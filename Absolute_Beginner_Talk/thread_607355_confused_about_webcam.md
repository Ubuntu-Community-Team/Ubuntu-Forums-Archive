### Post by victorgreen on 2007-11-21
Most/all of the longitech webcams that work with ubuntu are listed here:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsMultimediaWebCamerasLogitech](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsMultimediaWebCamerasLogitech)

Some longitech cams are known to work for a short while and then stop working. Its obviously driver related, linux webcam drivers arent all that brilliant, but hey at least they exist now! I do all my shopping based on whether or not hardware is included on the supported hardware lists on the ubuntu wiki and other random places. New driver releases happen periodically though, so there is some hope.
