### Post by Maestro23 on 2007-02-20
First thing you need to do, is go into your BIOS and change the boot order.  You want your machine to boot from its CD/DVD drive first so it can detect the Ubuntu CD.

Depending on your machine, to get into your BIOS will be either the ESC key or one of the F1-F12 keys.  Look in your machine's documentation if you don't know.
