### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-09-13
In your CD-burning program, you clicked a button for "Bootable CD". In most popular CD burners, this adds Dr-Dos to the disc.

You must instead just burn the disc image as a disc image. It already contains everything it needs to boot up. Try looking in your software for a setting called "Burn Image to Disc" or something like that.

As long as you still have the ISO image file on your hard disk, you do not need to download it again.
