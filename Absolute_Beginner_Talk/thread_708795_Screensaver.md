### Post by tylo42 on 2008-02-26
Here's what I do:

First set up the F-Spot Photo Manager (Applications->Graphics->F-Spot Photo Manager), It comes with the Ubuntu install. You can import pictures from a folder. Once your pics are imported choose the ones that you want in your screen saver by right clicking and and going to attach tag->favorites.

Then go to System->Preferences->Screensaver and choose the F-Spot photos Screensaver Theme.

All the pics that you choose as favorites will come up.

Hopefully this works for you.
