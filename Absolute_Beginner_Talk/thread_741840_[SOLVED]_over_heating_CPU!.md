### Post by Xiong Chiamiov on 2008-04-01
> **hellomoto said:**
> 
I took the side off whilst on and all 3 fans are running

Just a side note: taking off the side of your case won't necessarily help anything, as it'll screw with your airflow.  The key to a good air-cooled computer is lots of air in the front, over the hot stuff, and out the back.  I know that we're talking about relative differences, coming straight from Windows, but do remember that there is more to cooling than just open spaces and many fans.
