### Post by Rinzwind on 2006-06-03
If your system crashes and your filesystem gets corrupted or you have places on your disc that are busted there will be files in there. Those are oprhans that lost their place in line next to their dad and mum so be gentle with them ;) 

If you get them you need to perform a filesystemcheck (fsck) (Ubuntu default does the fsck every 30 reboots so it's automated for you).

Oh and if you get lots of files in that directory it might be a good time to start to backup your files and look for a new HDD :)
