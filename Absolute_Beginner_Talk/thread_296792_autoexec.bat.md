### Post by 23meg on 2006-11-10
You can put things in a startup script, or your desktop environment's list of startup programs, depending on how exactly you want them to work. For the first, add them to /etc/rc.local . To do the latter in GNOME you'd go to System / Preferences / Sessions / Startup Programs .
