### Post by suRoot on 2005-12-01
No mail client really works with OWA as it's just a web frontend to your exchange mailbox.

If your administrator has POP or IMAP enabled on the exchange server, you just need to setup thunderbird to access the server via one of those protocols (I highly recommend IMAP).  Feed it the same username & password you normally use and it should be fine.

If they won't enable POP or IMAP on the server, you're more or less stuck using Evolution (which sucks, sorry) or OWA.
