### Post by viciouslime on 2007-06-03
Actually it isn't possible to play DVDs in totem at the moment. If you put a DVD in a drive totem will be able to play it via autorun, but you can't open the disc, nor any given vob file, ripped to hard drive or otherwise. This is because of a lack of support for DVDs in gstreamer at the moment.

I think they're hoping to add this to gstreamer in time for the next ubuntu release, until then it's impossible.... unless of course you switch to totem-xine.
