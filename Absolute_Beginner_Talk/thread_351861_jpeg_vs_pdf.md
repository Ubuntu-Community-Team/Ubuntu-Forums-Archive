### Post by Rui Pais on 2007-02-02
since your are mixing an image format with a document format, i wonder if your questions is not if you should put your docs as pdfs or images?...

if you put them as pdf they will be downloadable but not direct visible (on browser) for those who don't have reader plugin installed. In that case, images of it (maybe with alternative link to pdfs) could be nice. 
But, and the point of my post, jpegs are terrible to do that.

jpegs are for photographic images, and are able of great compression, keeping the image nice That characteristic will not prevail if you use it for docs (line, text, B&W graphs...) The compression will destroy the information on your pages make it unreadable for most cases. 

try png instead. it won't compress so much (larger sizes) but it's a format generally good for line/BW image. Another alternative is old gif, svg  is good too, but have the same problem as pdf, some browser will not show it directly (only download)

hth.
