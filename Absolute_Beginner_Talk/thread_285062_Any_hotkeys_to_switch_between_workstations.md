### Post by Henry Rayker on 2006-10-26
I use a package called brightside. It allows you to just move your mouse between them. If you ram into the left side of your workstation, you'll go to the workstation to the left etc.

I think you can also navigate between them with a ctrl+alt+up/down/left/right (left to go to the workstation to the left, down to go down etc.)
