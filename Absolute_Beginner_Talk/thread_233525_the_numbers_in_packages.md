### Post by zba78 on 2006-08-10
It refers to different processor architectures.

i386 = intel 80386 processors and compatible/above
i486 = intel 486 processor and compatible/above
i586= intel pentium proccessors and compatible/above
i686 = intel pentium pro/ intel pentium2 compatible
x86 = familyname for 286, 386, 486... processors
x86-64 = 64bit-version of i686 (amd64, em64t)

You ca look on wikipedia for an explanation of each type (i386, i586, i686 etc.)
