### Post by catlett on 2006-04-06
I don't know the particulars of your hardware but have you run the Live CD? The Live CDs seem to be a pretty good barometer of how your hardware will be confuigured during install. I would try the Live cd first, see if your computer boots the disk, how your screen looks and if you have an internet connection. As long as you can get your monitor up and an active internet connection you'll be able to work out any other bugs(that is if there are bugs, there might not be). Not having a workable monitor and internet connection really puts you at a disadvantage. And the Live CD will definately answer those two compatability issues for you.Good luck.
