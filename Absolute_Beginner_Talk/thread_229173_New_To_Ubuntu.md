### Post by anaconda on 2006-08-04
go to "System/administration/Synaptic paccage manager"

select "search" and type the name of the game you want. eg. supertux
click on the box and select apply.

after that supertux is automatically downloaded and installed, and added to your "applications/games"

if your system can't find supertux, then you havent updated your sources.list repositories list.. search for instructions to do that if necessary..

another choice is to make the klik system to work, and installing those games couldn't become easier..
