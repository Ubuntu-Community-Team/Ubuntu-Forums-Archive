### Post by PriceChild on 2006-10-29
Yeah first just plug it in and see...

If that doesn't work... then check the bios for settings to turn onboard off and use your agp/pci card.

And finally, if you can't do that, then check your computer's documentation to see if there is actual physical "jumpers" that you need to use on the motherboard.

P.s. Nvidia's work better on linux that ati... but don't let that put you off or spend lots of money.
