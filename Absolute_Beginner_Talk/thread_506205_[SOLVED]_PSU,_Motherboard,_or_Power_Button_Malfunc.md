### Post by totheresq001 on 2007-07-21
If this is a new motherboard check to see that the "clear cmos" jumper is not set.  Often boards are shipped this way and it can be a common "Gotcha" the cmos jumper is usually labeled "jp1" or somthing and is near the on board battery
