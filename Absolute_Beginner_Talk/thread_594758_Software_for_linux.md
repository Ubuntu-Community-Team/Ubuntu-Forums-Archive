### Post by linuxlizard on 2007-10-28
click the applications menu in the top left corner of the screen then at the bottom of the menu that opens you will see add/remove applications listed. Click on that and you will see hundreds of free software available with a couple of clicks arranged neatly into groups like internet, games, sound/video, etc. that you can choose from.

Also [http://www.getdeb.net/](http://www.getdeb.net/) has a lot of the nicer apps and games, and the list is constantly growing.
