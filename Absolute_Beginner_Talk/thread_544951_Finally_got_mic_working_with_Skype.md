### Post by cc6000 on 2007-09-06
I have been unable to use my mic with Sound Recorder. I have tried different versions of SB Live!, SB Live! Value, and SB Audigy cards.

I have messed with alsamixer, and volume control settings for hours and still unable to use the mic with Sound Recorder. Actually, I really just wanted to use the mic with Skype and thought if the mic does not work with Sound Recorder it would not work with Skype.

Anyway, I was wrong. My mic works great with Skype but Sound Recorder is still dead.

To get the mic working with Skype, go into Options-> Sound Card and make sure the devices used is not "Default Device."  Select the first device listed for all 3 sections. Of course, make sure the mic capture, mic boost, and other settings are set correctly under volume control.

I have Ubuntu installed on an old Gateway P4, 1.3 GHz PC.

Anyway, I am a happy camper!! \\:D/
