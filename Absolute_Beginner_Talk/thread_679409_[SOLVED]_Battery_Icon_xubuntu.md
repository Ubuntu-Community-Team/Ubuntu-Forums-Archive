### Post by OZFive on 2008-01-26
you can add a battery icon to the xfce-4 panels on the top bottom or wherever you want to put them.  Right click on a panel likethe one that runs along the top of the screen (or bottom) and select +add.  This will bring up a listing of handy little programs and accesory info stuff.  Battery is the top listing I think, you can select it and modify it to your prefrences.
