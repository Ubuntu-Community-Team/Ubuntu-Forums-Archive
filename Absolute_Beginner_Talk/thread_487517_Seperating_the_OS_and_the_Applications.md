### Post by niteshifter on 2007-06-29
Hi Matuku,

Not really. You could repartition, splitting things out from / - but you'd need to separate out nearly everything (and I'm not sure you can keep under the partitioning limit), and you would still get jammed. Where package X from A puts it's parts isn't necessarily the same as what Package Y from B does.

Not being snide here, I sympathize, really I do as I've bombed many a system over the years – but you've just had a valuable object lesson on system backup and getting religion about it :)

Research the various partitioning schemes and backup methods. Work up a scheme and methods you can live with – and this part's the important part – be regular with the backups, both full and incremental.

Good luck!
