### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-20
To my knowledge all DSL and Cable (ethernet based) boxes do work with Ubuntu, and will automatically configure long as you have DHCP on them (or the router you connected to).

If you mean dial up, you may have a bit more trouble... you'll need to search for a guide for dailers.

Internet is pretty well supported though by Ubuntu, even wireless seems to work somewhat better now in Feisty. :)
