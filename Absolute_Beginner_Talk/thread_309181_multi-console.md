### Post by LLRNR on 2006-11-29
Those are called "tty"s and you can access 6 different ttys through Ctrl+Alt+F1...Ctrl+Alt+F6 indeed; to switch back to your GUI you just have to hit Ctrl+Alt+F7 :) 

The good part about this is that you can, for instance, allow different users to login at different ttys. I use ttys mainly for "troubleshooting"... errr... when something goes wrong with my GUI I switch to a tty and fix it from there.

Cheers,
