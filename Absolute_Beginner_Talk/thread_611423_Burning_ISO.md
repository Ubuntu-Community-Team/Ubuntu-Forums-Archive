### Post by Madpilot on 2007-11-12
If you're already in Ubuntu, ISO burning is easy.

Stick a blank CD-R or DVD-R in, then CLOSE the popup "what do you want to do with this blank disc" window that pops up.

Find your ISO file, right-click on it, choose "Burn to Disc". Easy as that.
