### Post by jagwah on 2006-12-25
Hi, I just started using my ipod with kubuntu today, I am using Amarok with the 'ipodslave' plugin. It seems to work fine. At first it did not seem to detect it, but I went to Settings/Configure Amarok/Media Devices, then tried the 'autodetect' button, didn't seem to do anything, then the 'add' button, anyway all of a sudden I got a popup window asking what I wanted to do, 'open in a new window' etc, from then on it's been fine.

You can check in Start Menu(Kmenu)/System/Kinfocentre/USB devices and see if your ipod is detected there. 

Best of luck with it.
