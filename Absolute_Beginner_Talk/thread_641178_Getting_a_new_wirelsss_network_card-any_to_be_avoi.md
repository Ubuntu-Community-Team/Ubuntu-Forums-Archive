I just bought an "Ativa Wireless G USB Adapter" - CHEAPEST ONE I COULD FIND - $38 at Office Depot.  It works perfectly with either Fiesty or Gusty - plug and play.  On Gusty be sure to disable "Ipv6".  BTW, I have the same router.  Now, the USB adapter is inexpensive because it has limited range (only about 200 ft.)  I set-up with WEP, and don't know if WPA will work.  Good Luck!.
