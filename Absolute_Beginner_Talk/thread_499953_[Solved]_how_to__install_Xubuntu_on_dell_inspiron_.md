Celeron 600Mhz CPU | 128M Ram | 4.2G hard drive | a floppy drive

[COLOR="Red"]**w00t!!!!!!!!!!!**[/COLOR]

ok after some great support from the people in this forum
and abit of web searching i have worked out how to install
not ubuntu (this requiers more ram that the system has) but
Xubuntu with out a CD-Rom Drive


**[COLOR="Green"]STEP 1)[/COLOR]**

download the torrent iso (will require utorrent: [http://www.utorrent.com/download.php](http://www.utorrent.com/download.php) 
or likewise bittorrent client)

[http://torrent.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/releases/feisty/release/alternate/](http://torrent.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/releases/feisty/release/alternate/)
(xubuntu-7.04-alternate-i386.iso)

**[COLOR="Green"]STEP 2)[/COLOR]**

download the floppy installer and accompanying files (requires 2/3 disks)

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromHardDriveWithFloppies](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromHardDriveWithFloppies)


now i am a super muppet when it comes to things like this and before today,
i was n00bsvilletastic in the world of linux but, it works so take your time and 
read through the steps very carefully and you will be fine :)


these links will also help but are not part of the installation process:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam/DellInspiron3800](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam/DellInspiron3800)

[http://www.math.ucla.edu/~jimc/inspir3800.html](http://www.math.ucla.edu/~jimc/inspir3800.html)



take it easy and have fun with your new linux machine :)
