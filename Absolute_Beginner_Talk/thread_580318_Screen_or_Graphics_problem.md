### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-10-18
It *could* be permanently damaged, I think, if the problem really was long exposure to a speaker's magnet. The speaker issue with monitors only affects CRT monitors, so if your monitor is LCD, then the cause would probably have something to do with the video card, though I haven't seen a video card do that.

If you've had speakers next to the monitor until recently, it depends on how long they were there.

### Post by jbush82 on 2007-10-18
I would recommend doing two things.

1) Degauss the CRT.  There should be an option to do this within the monitors configuration options (setup/menu button on the monitor)

2) Try disconnecting the cable from the back of the monitor (if possible) and from the back of the computer and plug it back in.
