### Post by Naegling23 on 2006-10-13
What you are looking for is any player that is UMS.  There are also MTP players, these carry the plays for sure logo.  MTP players do not work under linux.  There are patches to address this...libmtp, but I have never been able to get it to work.  Just make sure that the player you buy is not a plays for sure device.
