### Post by OU812 on 2006-07-24
Were you able to access the internet with the live cd? If so, maybe try system>admin>network tools. Click the device you want to get working, and make sure it shows up as the default device in the drop down window at the bottom. Click properties and make sure the enable this connection is checked. Back in the main window, click activate device (you may have to click deactive device first). That's all I got.
