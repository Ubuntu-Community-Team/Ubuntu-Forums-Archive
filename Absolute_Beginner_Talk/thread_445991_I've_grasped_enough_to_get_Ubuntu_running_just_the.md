### Post by apunc1 on 2007-05-16
do you have a separate /home partition? if not, make one [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome)

This will keep your settings, files, and other personal things separate so if you need to do a fresh install, or if your install won't boot for some reason, you can still retrieve data on your /home partition. not exactly a backup, but a nice idea.
