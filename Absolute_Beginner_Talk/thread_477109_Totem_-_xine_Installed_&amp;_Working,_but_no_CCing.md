Hi. According to [this site]("http://diveintomark.org/archives/2006/07/23/video-howto#vlc"), Neither Totem or Mplayer support captions included in the video, only from separate files, so I suggest you give VLC a try; seeing as you're running Dapper and taking into account the author's comments, you have at least two choices: either install VLC through Synaptic (if you don't plan on viewing H.264 videos with it), or download a newer version from [VLC's site]("http://www.videolan.org/vlc/download-ubuntu.html") if you do. Hope this helps.
