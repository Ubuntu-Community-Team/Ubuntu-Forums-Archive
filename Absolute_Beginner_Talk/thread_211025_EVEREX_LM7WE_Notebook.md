I would go for the Dell since you will have the option to upgrade the battery to a higher capcity for about $15 which is well worth it since it will almost double the run time.

Even using a laptop around the house (cooking recipes etc) good battery time is very helpful.

I have bought quite a few of the Dell B130 (well Inspiron 1300 in the UK) for customers and they are very nice. Not as clunky as you first think. Also quite tough so will survive a small banging on the table. Unlike my Acer did, plastic broke by the screen hinge.
