### Post by catlett on 2006-06-02
You can also subscribe to threads through the top of the page under "user cp" and then "edit profile". I do this (but I do not subscribe to the email updates) because it puts a clock icon in front of a thread I posted in or posted. So when I go to the forum page I can easily see if I posted in any thread.
If I want to find an older one I go to the top of the page (ubuntu forum page) hit on "quick links" then I hit on "my profile". On your profile page ther or two options in "forum > info"
 Total Posts: 605 (7.71 posts per day)
Find all posts by catlett
Find all threads started by catlett I hit on one of those and it lists all my posts or all my threads I started. So if a post is to old to be seen on the first couple of pages on the forum I don't hunt around for the post in back pages, I go to my profile and search my posts.
