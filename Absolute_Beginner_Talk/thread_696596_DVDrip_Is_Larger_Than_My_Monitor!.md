### Post by bumanie on 2008-02-14
Your upper and lower panels are moveable. You can either grab and hold and move them to the side of the screen (ie 45 degree flip) or you can right click on them and choose properties. In properties you  have the choice of auto hide, (which works much like windows) or show hide buttons which allows you to click the panel to move it out of the way.
