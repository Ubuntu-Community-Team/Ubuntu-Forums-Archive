### Post by stalkingwolf on 2008-02-26
IMHO, you never Have to quit being a beginner.  Once you master the basics, there are countless
areas that you can try ( ie : a beginner ) until you master them. And with every upgrade, be it kernal
software,hardware,or use, there are new things to learn and ways of doing them.

And if you get bored learning things to do with Linux you can always try to figure out ways you
can use what you have learned to help out your windows using friends.  That provides endless
opportunities and challenges.

So being a beginner is not a bad thing.  It just presents opportunities that are only limited by your
time and desire.
