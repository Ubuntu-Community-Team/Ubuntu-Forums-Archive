### Post by az on 2006-10-31
> **Mazeralis said:**
> Can you actually format your hard drive and erase the evil Windows from your system and be completely funcitonal with Ubuntu or Kubuntu?

I know a lot of people have told me that they are not sure if erasing windows from their computer is legal!

Talk about not knowing your rights...

Ubuntu is a fully-functional OS.  It is free of charge and free to use and redistribute.  It depends in no way on any other operating system to run.  In no country on the planet is it illegal to install and use Ubuntu.

Now, there are some multimedia codecs that are not licenced for redistribution.  You can use these codecs if you like, but you have to install them yourself - Ubuntu does not ship them.  

There are also some hardware vendors who do not release specifications nor open-source drivers for their products.  Usually, these devices have reverse-engineered drivers which work fine in linux.  Occasionaly, they may not work.  You can often go to a big computer store and specify that you want linux-friendly hardware.
