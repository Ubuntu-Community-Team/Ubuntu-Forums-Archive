### Post by lordofchaos1984 on 2007-12-05
Just a little idea for those who like to customize...

First, disable the default logon sound  system>preferences>sounds

next go to the Services dialog  system>administration>services

click add in the window that pops up and type into the command area "/usr/bin/totem /absolute/path/to/your/chosen/sound_file.(ogg or mp3)

for best results, choose a small sound file (:30 seconds or less)

log off and re logon to test. should open totem AND play your selected file.

I personally use a "we are the borg" type of file for my system.
