I inadvertently deleted the bottom panel on my destop.  Now when I minimize a program I have no way of retrieving it.

### Post by FreewareFan on 2008-04-11
Right click on your top panel, and select 'Add New Panel'.  You can choose the properties then, like where to place it, and what you want on it..  Things like a Windows List, that show the programs you have running, and when they are minimized, and other things to suit your particular needs.
