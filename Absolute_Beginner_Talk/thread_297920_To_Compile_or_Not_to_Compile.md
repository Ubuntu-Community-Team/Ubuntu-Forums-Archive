### Post by digby on 2006-11-11
The only possible reasons to download and compile source are:
1) You absolutely must have some feature in the newest bleeding edge release.
2) You hate life, have nothing but free time, and think headaches are fun.

In all serious, if you can install from the repository via apt-get/aptitude, I would suggest it.  You'll be much happier in the end.

In terms of performance, you'll never notice a difference.
