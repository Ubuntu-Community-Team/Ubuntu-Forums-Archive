The version of Ubuntu you should go for should be 7.04, the latest release, 7.10 isn't due for release until next month.

### Post by Warren Watts on 2007-09-10
You might search the forum for references to your particular wireless card to see if anyone had had any good/bad experiences with it.

A google search for your wireless card & Ubuntu might yield some interesting information as well.

You can always download the Live CD, boot it up and see if it recognizes your wireless card.  The live CD isn't going to install anything on your hard drive, and you can get a feel for how well Ubuntu is going to handle your hardware.
