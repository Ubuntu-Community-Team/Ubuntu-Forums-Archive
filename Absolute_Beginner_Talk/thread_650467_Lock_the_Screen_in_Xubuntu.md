### Post by plucky on 2007-12-26
> How do I lock the screen in Xubuntu

**Right click** on the shutdown button and go to **Properties**

A **Panel Actions** GUI opens and the choices are **Quit,Lock Screen,Quit+Lock Screen**

See which option works best for you!!!

Good Luck
