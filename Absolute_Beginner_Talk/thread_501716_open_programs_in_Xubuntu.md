### Post by Kralizec on 2007-07-15
On a clean install of Xubuntu, there is by default a top panel and a bottom panel and the bottom panel has buttons just like in Windows that you can click on to bring up your applications. If you don't have anything like that but there are panels there, right click on one of the panels, select Add To Panel, find Window List, and click-and-drag it into the location on the panel that you want. However, If both of these panels are gone, I'm not sure how to get them back.
