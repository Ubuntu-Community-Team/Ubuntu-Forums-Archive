### Post by Golem XIV on 2008-04-08
In my experience the problem lies with panel applets that change their size.  For example, my CPU scaling monitor will change between 800 MHz, 1.2 GHz and 1.4 GHz.  Each of these states will have a slightly different width and sometimes the applet will relocate to the corner.

I have solved the problem by placing applets such as these between separators, and giving them enough space to expand as they need (they usually need only a few extra pixels).  This is not foolproof but it reduces the hassle to a manageable level (i.e. once in a couple of months instead of almost every day).
