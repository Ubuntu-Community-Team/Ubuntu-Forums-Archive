### Post by jimbren on 2006-11-10
There are lots of problems still being reported with upgrading to Edgy, but that has a lot to do with unique hardware and software issues.  I personally upgraded to Edgy without any major issues, though I did (do) have some minor ones.

If you just do an upgrade of Firefox, your bookmarks and settings should be preserved.  If you're really concerned about your bookmarks (and I can understand if you are), I would export them before you upgrade the browser.  If they are lost, you can import them and all will be well with the world.
