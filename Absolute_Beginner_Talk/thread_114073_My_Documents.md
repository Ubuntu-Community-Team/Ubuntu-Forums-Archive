### Post by mistergq on 2006-01-07
Is there any way to make a My Documents similar to the way it works in Windows?  I have a directory in my user directory called Documents and I have made a shortcut on the desktop to it, but it would nice if I could have the option in a drop down menu when I am trying to save various documents, etc.

---

### Post by kairu0 on 2006-01-07
You can add it to your Places in Gnome. Just open the folder in Nautilus, add it to your Bookmarks, and it should appear in the left side of those Save File... dialog boxes.
