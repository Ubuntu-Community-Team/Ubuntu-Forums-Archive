Can someone explain what the different is between GTK and Metacity please?

---

### Post by Knome_fan on 2005-10-16
Metacity is the window manager, so metacity themes will change the ways the window decoration looks (that is, the thing around the app where you find the close and maximise buttons, for example).

GTK is a toolkit and gtk themes change the way the applications themselve look (the stuff that's in the window, like toolbars, scrollbars, etc.)
