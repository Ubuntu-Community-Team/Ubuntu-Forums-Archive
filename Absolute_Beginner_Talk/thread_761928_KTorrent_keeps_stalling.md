### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-04-21
since some isp's throttle connection speeds for bittorrent, do this: go into preferences and change the default ports to 50100 and 50108. then find where you can enable (check box) "encryption" or "encrypted connection" or something like that, *and* enable legacy connection. these things may help.

also, let me know if you would like to know how to use peerguardian blocklists in deluge. hope it works for you.
