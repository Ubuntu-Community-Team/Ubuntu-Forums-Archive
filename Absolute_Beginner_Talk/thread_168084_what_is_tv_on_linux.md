### Post by Kethinov on 2006-04-29
You can buy PCI cards called TV tuners that can receive a cable signal from your local cable TV company, among other providers. Programs like MythTV allow you to watch TV on your computer along with doing advanced things like record episodes and so forth. With such a setup, your computer basically becomes a very powerful DVR.
