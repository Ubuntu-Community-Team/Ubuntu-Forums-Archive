### Post by varunus on 2005-07-14
Resizing NTFS is very easy from the ubuntu installer.  I did that myself; I had a 60 GB NTFS partition, and I resized it in the installer.

To do this, boot the Ubuntu installer, and when it asks you to partition, choose "manually partition."  It will list your drives, and you can resize.

There are step by step instructions on what to do (with pictures!) here:
[http://www3.telus.net/linux4u/ubuntu.html](http://www3.telus.net/linux4u/ubuntu.html)

Good luck.  Ubuntu is a very enjoyable OS to use, and installing it isn't as hard as it seems.
