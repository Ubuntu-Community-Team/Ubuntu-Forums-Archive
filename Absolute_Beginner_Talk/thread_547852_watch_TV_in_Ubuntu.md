### Post by svalding on 2007-09-10
You should look into MythTV. It is a full featured application used to watch, and record live tv straight to your hard drive. You can download the program guide content from practically any cable provider. It even has some support for overseas (not USA) channels. 

Check out [www.schedulesdirect.org](www.schedulesdirect.org) for more information on how to get the data feed to your computer. MythTV also provides many addons to the standard installation, including MythWeb, and a really neat ROM application, allowing you to play all those old NES, SNES, and Genesis games you know and love!
