### Post by Sef on 2006-04-10
To get the printer running:   Systems > Adminstration > Printing  >Click on New Printer > First page (set local or network, and how connected) > Forward > Second page > select HP (HPLIP), then select HP Photomsmart 3200 > Apply > Follow directions to finish.
