### Post by ph1 on 2008-02-26
I'm guessing that your window borders are controlled by Compiz, which actually has its own (seemingly disconnected) cursor settings.  Open the Compiz Configuration Settings Manager, click on General Options, and specify exactly which cursor scheme you want.  Hopefully this will take care of things.
