### Post by mocoloco on 2007-09-05
Hit Alt-F2 and type "xterm".  This is another, less fancy terminal window.  You can just use it instead if you want, but to trouble shoot your issue type in "gnome-terminal" and it should give some output as to why the gnome-terminal can't open.
