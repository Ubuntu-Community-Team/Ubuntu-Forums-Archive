### Post by lamego on 2006-06-29
I am assuming your wife is more familiar with Windows.
If you want to keep away from technical descriptions just tell her Gnome is just a graphical style of working with the computer, the same way Windows 98, Windows 2000, XP have their own. 
Nautilus is a program that allows you to browse, open or manage your files, like windows explorer does for Windows.
