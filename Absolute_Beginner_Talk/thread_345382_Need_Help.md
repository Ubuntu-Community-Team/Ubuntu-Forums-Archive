### Post by renzokuken on 2007-01-26
i could be wrong here but the error says its expecting the i915 driver (your first post), but according to your xorg.conf you are using the i810 driver.

i dont know much about intel chipsets (i'm better with nvidia or ATI) but for now, it may be worth changing 
```

Section "Device"
Identifier "Intel Corporation 82845G/GL[Brookdale-G]/GE Chipset Integrated Graphics Device"
Driver "[color=blue]i810[/color]"
BusID "PCI:0:2:0"
VideoRam 64000
EndSection
```

to the following, and rebooting

```

Section "Device"
Identifier "Intel Corporation 82845G/GL[Brookdale-G]/GE Chipset Integrated Graphics Device"
Driver "[color=red]vesa[/color]"
BusID "PCI:0:2:0"
VideoRam 64000
EndSection
```
