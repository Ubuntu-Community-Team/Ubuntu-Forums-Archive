### Post by Ambimom on 2006-10-04
Download and install the following:

[http://www.izarc.org/download.html](http://www.izarc.org/download.html)

Okay, once it's loaded, go to the place where your rar files are.
Right click on the rar file and you will see a menu item for IZARC.  Move the cursor over Izarc and tell it to "Extract"

You can either extract the image where you are or to another location.

Make sure that you un-rar the rar file that is numbered first.

When IZARC finishes, you will have an ISO.

Now take your favorite burning software, Nero or Roxio and burn it as a Disc Image.

Here's a helpful video which will show explain the process...[but there's about five minutes of "artistic drama" at the beginning....wait, the info is there.]

[http://www.adventuresinlinux.tv/main/component/option,com_frontpage/Itemid,1/](http://www.adventuresinlinux.tv/main/component/option,com_frontpage/Itemid,1/) 

Good luck!
