### Post by petersjm on 2007-04-03
I think you mean the dock? At the bottom of the screen where all your program icons are? You mouseover and they get bigger and you click them to launch the app? Yep, there're plenty of ways. Try KXDocker, or KibaDock, or GnomeDock (I think). I use KoolDock - it's basic, but does the job and doesn't need XGL for transparent background. Just google for any of them.
