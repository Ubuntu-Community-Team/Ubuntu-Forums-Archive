### Post by GreenHawkIA on 2006-12-06
Is it gone completely, or just not functioning?  If it is gone completely, you probably just accidentally removed the applet.  Right click on a blank area of the panel you want it on and select "Add to Panel."  Then in the first group select "Clock" and hit "Add" down at the bottom.  It should pop up on the bar.  If it is not quite where you want it, right-click on it, and select move to move it where you want it to go.  Also, check out some of the other cool things in the "Add to Panel" dialogue, they can be a lot of fun & useful too.
