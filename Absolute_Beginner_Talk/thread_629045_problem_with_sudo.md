### Post by eolson on 2007-12-01
It won't display your password.  It will appear blank.  Just type in your password and hit return.  It's a security feature.  No system that is secure will show your password when you type it in.  Might show some xxx's or ****'x,  but no passoword.  Totally blank is best.
