### Post by frank26080115 on 2007-08-27
It's annoying, every time I switch user, and then log back in, I have to go to my screen saver, after moving the mouse, it locks the screen and begs for the same password. How do I avoid typing my password twice like that?

---

### Post by cawill on 2007-08-27
press ALT + F2 and enter 'gconf-editor', then go to Apps > gnome-power-manager and gnome-screensaver have some options that can be unticked to disable asking for a password.
