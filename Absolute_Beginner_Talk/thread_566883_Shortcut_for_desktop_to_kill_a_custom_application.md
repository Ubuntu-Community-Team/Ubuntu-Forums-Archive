### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-10-04
[COLOR="Blue"]Right click[/COLOR] on a blank space on either the top, or bottom taskbar.

Click on [COLOR="Blue"]Add to Panel[/COLOR]

When the window opens, scroll down to [COLOR="Blue"]Desktop and Windows[/COLOR]

Select the [COLOR="Blue"]Force Quit[/COLOR] item

Click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Add Button[/COLOR] at the bottom right of the window

Click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Close button[/COLOR]

A new icon will appear on your taskbar and when you want to terminate an application, just click on it and follow the instructions.
