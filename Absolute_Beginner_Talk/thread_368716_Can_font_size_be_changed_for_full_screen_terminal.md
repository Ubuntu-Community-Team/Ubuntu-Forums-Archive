### Post by lucas42 on 2007-02-24
I'm not sure about the Ctl+Alt+F1 terminal, but if you open terminal within gnome (Applications>Accessories>Terminal) then you can by right clicking on the terminal and going to  to edit profile.  From here you can change text color, size, font, backgournd color, scrolling and a few other things.  To get this terminal full screen you just have to hit F10
