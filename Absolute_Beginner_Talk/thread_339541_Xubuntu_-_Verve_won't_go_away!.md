On a default installation of Xfce, right-clicking the desktop gives you the desktop menu, right-clicking an item in the panel gives you the Maximize, Send To etc menu, and right-clicking a blank space on the panel gives you a menu listing Customize Panel,Add Items, Quit, Restart, and About the Xfce Panel. I mention all three because finding the blank space to click on is extremely difficult, and it's easy to get one of the others by mistake. I find it easiest to right-click on the extreme end of a panel.

Once you get this menu, you can use the Add Items entry to remove the one you don't want.
