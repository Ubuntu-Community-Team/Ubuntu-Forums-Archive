### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-04-16
Right click on the bottom panel, click [COLOR="Blue"]Add to Pamel[/COLOR].

The Add to Panel Window will open.

Scroll down to the section Desktop and Windows

Click on [COLOR="Blue"]Window List[/COLOR]

Click the [COLOR="Blue"]Add button[/COLOR].

Close the window.

Viola! You can now minimise apps and they appear on the taskbar!
