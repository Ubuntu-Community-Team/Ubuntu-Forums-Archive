It could work, though I'm not 100% sure. 
But beware : updating your bios may mess up seriously your hardware configuration if something goes wrong. Read the more you can on how to restore your system if the bios is messed up, prepare a B plan (like a reinstall of your previous bios version) and more than everything : backup everything you consider important.
