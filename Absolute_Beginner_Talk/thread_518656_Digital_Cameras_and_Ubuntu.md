### Post by serfcx on 2007-08-06
Most cameras will be auto detected and mounted.
Ubuntu default photo handling software is Fspot which will import your photos to your PC.
Alternatives are Digikam which you will need some KDE libraries but can be installed easily through the software installer synaptic.
Play around and see which you prefer.
