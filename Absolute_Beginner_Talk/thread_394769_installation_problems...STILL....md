### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-03-27
You typically delete the highest resolution values from the mode lines:

Modes "1280x1024" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"

becomes

Modes  "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"

To lock in your special resolution:

Modes "1680x1050"

Of course this may not work, but it's worth a try.


From a terminal:

sudo -B /etc/X11/xorg.conf

Control-O and Control-X when finished editing.  Control-Alt-Backspace to restart the xserver

Good luck!
