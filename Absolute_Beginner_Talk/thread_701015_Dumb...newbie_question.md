### Post by steveneddy on 2008-02-18
Most desktops and laptops can have bluetooth added to them even after the sale.

If your model of laptop was offered with bluetooth, then it can be added.

If you have a desktop with an extra PCI slot, you can buy a card and have bluetooth.

I even believe there is a USB stick available that would give you bluetooth capabilities.

[TigerDirect has a few]("http://www.tigerdirect.com/applications/SearchTools/search.asp?cat=909&keywords=bluetooth&mnf=") and[ this one]("http://www.tigerdirect.com/applications/SearchTools/item-details.asp?EdpNo=3399853&CatId=909") is discrete as it sits close to the body of the laptop so it is unobtrusive.
