### Post by bignickel on 2007-04-22
You can upgrade with the CD.  Once it's burned just put it in the drive and a dialog will come up asking if you want to upgrade.  Still a good idea to back your home directory up first.

There are instructions on the [official feisty upgrade site]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/upgrading").
