### Post by BaffledMollusc on 2007-04-18
> **ddrplayer512 said:**
> How do you enable mp3 playback in Ubuntu?

That's just a burning question inside of me because I have a lot of mp3s and I would like to listen to them.

Ubuntu 6.10 does not ship with the codecs to play MP3s already installed, due to legal reasons. To get around this install either EasyUbuntu or Automatix - these applications allow you to install the codecs for a large range of multimedia formats.

Alternatively you could wait until tomorrow and download Ubuntu 7.04 instead. I believe that has automatic codec installation for things like MP3s.
