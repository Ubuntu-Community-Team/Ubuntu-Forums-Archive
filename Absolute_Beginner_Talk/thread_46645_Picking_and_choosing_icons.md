### Post by gammyhand on 2005-07-05
How do I pick and choose icons to use? For example... I want to use 90% of icon theme a and 10% of icon theme b...where do I choose unique icons?

---

### Post by Xian on 2005-07-05
The way you pick and choose icons is to first install a theme, and then tailor it by right clicking on any icon that you want to alter on your desktop, panel, or in the file browser; select 'Properties' and then click on the icon box within the window that appears. What you choose with remain even if you apply other icon themes.
