### Post by RayArdia on 2008-03-11
Just how does a thread or post get its [SOLVED] marker? I have a thread in Networking which didn't get any replies:( but to which I have now found the answer. Can I cancel the thread or mark it solved?

---

### Post by kesman on 2008-03-11
There's a drop-down list called "thread tools" right in the upper right corner of the thread. It's there.

---

### Post by scragar on 2008-03-11
thread tools > mark thread as solved.
