### Post by DemoN3x on 2007-08-22
I had problems with this as well.  The easiest way I found to do it way:

Go through your applications menu and find the app you wish to add.

Right click on the app and choose > Add this launcher to desktop.

Goto your desktop and drag and drop the launcher onto kiba.

There is probably an easier way, but this is easiest way I found.
