### Post by asmoore82 on 2007-08-22
the 2 situations are un-related... neither Graphical tool is the true location of Trash.

most likely the "move to trash" icon is greyed out on/in a location you don't have permissions to  change.

P.S. true location of Trash is "$HOME/.Trash" which is usually the same as "/home/$USER/.Trash"
