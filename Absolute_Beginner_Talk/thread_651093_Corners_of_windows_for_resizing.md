### Post by jkblacker on 2007-12-27
I'm not sure about making the edge more sensitive, but I can hit Alt+F8 as a shortcut to resizing - after that, the window should get the blue transparent layer over it and just move your mouse in the desired direction (ie toward the corner) and it will resize that way.

(You might need the compiz manager installed, not sure).

Hope that helps!
