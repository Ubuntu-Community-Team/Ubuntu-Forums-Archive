### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-02-17
Installing won't slow you down.
However, extra memory will be used if you **run** amarok in gnome because you will have to load up QT widgets and GTK widgets.  

Will it slow you down?  No, but will you have as much free memory as if you had chosen Rhythmbox or something GTK based?  No.

That said, I'd say go for it--it runs great for me even with only 512 MB of RAM
