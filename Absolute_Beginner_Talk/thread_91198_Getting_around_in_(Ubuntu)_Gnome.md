### Post by dabear on 2005-11-16
disable bootup services: Find and install BUM (located somewhere on these forums).
Control center: alt + f2 , write gnome-control-center and press enterr. You could also make a launcher by right clicking the desktop->create Launcher. There you would type in «Launch Gnome Control Center» after the label «name:» and «gnome-control-center» after the label «command:»
