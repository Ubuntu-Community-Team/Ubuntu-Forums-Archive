Just for the record, you can also place them on the bottom panel, or whereever you want really.

There are a lot already included with ubuntu, and there are a couple more. You can search for "gnome panel applet" in synaptic and it will show you some.

Personal highlights incluse:
- Music Player Applet: Control your favorite music player directly from the panel.
- Teatime: Tea timing applet, of course you can use it to time other things as well.
- Netmon: Show the current speed of your network connection on the panel.
- Sensors: Hardware temperature and similar things

And some favorites of the installed-by-default ones:
- Deskbar: Search everything. And do various other things
- Tomboy: Wiki note taking with cool features
- Wanda: Oh so deliciously useless. Plus, it has fortune cookies. :)
