### Post by rosieg on 2006-11-16
Update: Problem sort of solved.

On my computer fn F3 is meant to mute, fn F4 in conjunction with the arrow keys is meant to do volume down.

What I now have working in Kubuntu (and I don't understand this at all):

fn F2 = mute
fn F3 = vol down 
fn F4 = vol up

This is different to how it works in Ubuntu.
