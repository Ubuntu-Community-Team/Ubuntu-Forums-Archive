### Post by HornedBeast on 2007-05-14
Quickest and easiest way is to remove the .gaim or .pidgin folder from your home directory.

HOWEVER. Only do this if you need to as it removes all your settings for the program such as plugins and sound settings.

Try and your own risk I guess.
