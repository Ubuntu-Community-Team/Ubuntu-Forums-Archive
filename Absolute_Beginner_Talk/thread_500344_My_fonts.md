### Post by Corvias on 2007-07-13
Start Here

[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_improve_sub-pixel_font_rendering_for_Feisty](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_improve_sub-pixel_font_rendering_for_Feisty)

And then:

Since you've already installed the MS core fonts, in ff go to Edit -> Preferences. Click the Content section and click the Advanced button in the Fonts and Colors area. Set your Serif font to Times New Roman, and your Sans-Serif font to Arial, Verdana, or Tahoma. You should experiment with the sizes to find something that is comfortable.

Good Lock!
