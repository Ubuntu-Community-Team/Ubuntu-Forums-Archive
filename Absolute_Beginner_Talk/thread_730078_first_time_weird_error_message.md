### Post by OffAxis on 2008-03-20
Sounds like your BIOS lost its multiplier.

Reboot, hit DEL to enter the CMOS setup and adjust the CPU multiplier. You might be able to get away with selecting 'Optimal Performance' setting on the opening menu.
Save and Exit.
