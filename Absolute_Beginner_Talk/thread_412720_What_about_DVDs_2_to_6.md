### Post by freebird54 on 2007-04-18
Under Edit->Add CD ROM - is the entry within Synaptic.  All the stuff you need is right there!  Saves you the download time.  First thing I would suggest is to put in the update DVD, and give that a go.  The others are there whenever you wish to add something else to the system - perhaps a different Word Processor, an encrypted filesystem, a music player, a game for your kids, a programming language, a bit of extra beauty for your desktop.....

Actually there is lots already installed - so it can wait a while before you need any of those others!
