### Post by oodledoodle on 2008-01-21
right-click on the volume icon and select preferences. you should be presented with a list of choices as to which device the volume button will control. one of these should be your speakers! there's probably a really quick way of telling which one you should set it to, but in the end i just used trial and error until i found the option for my speakers.
