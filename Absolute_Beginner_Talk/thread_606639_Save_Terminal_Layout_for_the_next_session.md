### Post by Schalken on 2007-11-08
If your using plain Ubuntu and not Kubuntu, then your terminal will be "Gnome Terminal". I am not sure whether Gnome Terminal provides the functionality you describe.

However, "Konsole" does, under Settings >  Save Session Profile. You can install Konsole with the command "apt-get install konsole", or search for it in Synaptic, however as it is part of Kubuntu (KDE) and not Ubuntu (GNOME) it will bring pieces of KDE which it depends on along with it, and will likely look and feel out of place on your GNOME desktop.

But other than that, Konsole should suit your needs fine.
