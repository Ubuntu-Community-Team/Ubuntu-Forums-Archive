### Post by rickycodie on 2007-04-28
as far as the temp goes, try stopping by best buy or newegg and picking up some arctic silver. it's a silver thermo conductor that goes on top of your processor. only try this on a desktop, open your box and remove the heatsink. then **_GENTLY[U][B]**_[/U][/B]REMOVE THE OLD STUFF. replace with about a dime size of arctic silver, and re-install the heatsink.
