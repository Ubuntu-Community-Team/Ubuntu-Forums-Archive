### Post by High Camp on 2007-09-16
Are you talking about the task bar or icon box?

The task bar will not show all applications, only the ones that are designed to show an icon in the task bar (Firestarter, Skype, Exaile, Desktop Search are the ones I have).

If it's an icon box then you have the option of showing only minimized programs or not and only the programs from the current workspace. Those options may have something to do with your problems. 

I'm only a noob so please don't blame me if I'm wrong,

EDIT: The way to change these options on the icon box is to right click on it and select properties. I find it hard to right click on sometimes, you may have to get the mouse over the "handle" which is on the left side and it's a small thing with 4 horizontal lines on it.

### Post by High Camp on 2007-09-16
If you press Alt+Tab does it show up in that window?

If it doesn't then of course you must be closing the window or something.

When you right click on the task bar does a right click menu come up? At the top of that menu does it say Task Bar?

Next I would try selecting "remove" from that right click menu. Then right click again and select add and then add a task bar again. You also have the option to restart the panel from the right click menu, which may change things. 

Do you have the stock Xubuntu setup or have you changed anything in regards to the panels or workspaces?

Let me know,
