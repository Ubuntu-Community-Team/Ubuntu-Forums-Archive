### Post by SenseiJonny on 2007-05-25
The skinny:  Call Samsung for a free replacement (very painless process).

The story:

Well, I have had the same issue for a few months and I was unable to find any answers on the web.  My 931BF would randomly open the monitor's menu, it would constantly auto-adjust, and it would sometimes lock the menu.  After getting a second one of these monitors for a dual monitor setup, I got around to some troubleshooting.  Guess what?  The first monitor with the problem was the only one that had the problem, regardless of what computer it was connected to and what OS was running on that computer.  Well, an easy 10-minute call to Samsung and I was all set.  After I explained the problem to the Samsung customer care representative, he instantly knew that I needed a replacement and set it up for me.  Here's the good part: as long as you are within your 3-year warranty, it is completely free, including free shipping.  Samsung had the monitor in stock, so it shipped out the same day and I received my replacement 2 days after placing the call.  I have not had the problem since.

So, go forth and give Samsung a call.  Even if you are having some other issues, they seem really helpful.  It's worth a shot.
