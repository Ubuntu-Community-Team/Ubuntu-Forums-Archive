### Post by igknighted on 2007-03-14
This should be required reading for all new users.  Especially the bit about culture shock.  Even people that expect a different OS, the cultural differences between the windows crowd and the linux crowd are great, and often unexpected.

I think the one point he missed is this: While linux users don't feel like we need to convert users to our OS, we are immensely proud of it.  More so than Windows users, and right there with Mac users.  This is the reason we want to show you linux.  We love it, and we want you to as well.  If you don't take a respectful attitude about it, well, don't expect much help.  But come with an attitude that suggests you want to learn and at least somewhat respect our beloved OS, and we will jump through hoops to help you, more so than ANY tech support for a commercial company will.
