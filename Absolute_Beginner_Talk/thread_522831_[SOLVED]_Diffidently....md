### Post by Bofur on 2007-08-11
If you want the panel back, right click your top panel then click new panel. A new panel should appear and when you click and hold it you can move it wherever your please. When you right click the panel and click add to panel you can re add everything that was on your previous panel like windows selector, your trash can, workplace switcher etc etc.. Hope that helps!
