### Post by imrumpf on 2005-12-14
Correct me if I am wrong, the only impast would be the size of the ISO image. The way the Live Cd works is that the programs are only loaded into the memory when needed, so having 10000 aplications or only having 5 would make no difference because they are not loaded until needed. 

For the live CD I am guessing that the Ubuntu devs said "put as much on as possible, make it the best and greatest live CD ever, and let's utilize the CD space to it's maximum potential"

In short: Size? Yes. Speed? No
