### Post by mjm115 on 2006-04-28
By default, Ubuntu doesn't come with any services or ports open to the outside world, but there's no such thing as too much security.  A very good firewall is [Firestarter]("http://www.fs-security.com/").  Just enter this command in a terminal:

> 
sudo apt-get install firestarter

### Post by Spinelli on 2006-04-28
