### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-06-17
It can be done like this:

Sudo apt-get update

Then

Sudo upgrade

And finally

Sudo dist-upgrade

Be adviced though that Ubuntu 7.10 is stil an infant no older that Tribe 1, at least last time I checked. 

Why not just upgrade to 7.04 which is the official release at the moment and wait for a month or so before you upgrade to 7.10. I'd hold my upgrade til at least Tribe 2 is out, maybe even longer. My 7.04 runs perfect so why risk it on a not yet finished distribution update?
