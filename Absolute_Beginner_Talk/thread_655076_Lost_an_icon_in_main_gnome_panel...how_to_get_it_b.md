### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-01-01
Hi. If running nm-applet from the terminal didn't bring Network Manager back, then most likely it's already running but unable to show up due to a missing Notification Area; right-click on an empty part of the top panel, select "Add to Panel..." and look for Notification Area (should be at the bottom, in the "Utilities" section.
