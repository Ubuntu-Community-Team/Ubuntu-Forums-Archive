### Post by mivo on 2007-10-16
Nautilus is what you get when you select *Places -> Home Folder*, for example. It can be configured to behave in the way you want, and there are even extensions for it. :) You can see screenshots [here]("http://www.gnome.org/projects/nautilus/screenshots.html").

### Post by FreddyGill on 2007-10-16
Nautilus just allows you to see what's stored on your computer. It let's you view all of the files and folders on your system (and thumbnails of images also). It allows you to copy, move, or rename a file or a folder. It's very similar to "Explore" in Windows systems.
