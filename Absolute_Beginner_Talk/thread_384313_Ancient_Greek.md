### Post by simosx on 2007-08-08
There is an issue with Greek Polytonic when your system locale is not el_GR.UTF-8.
More and workaround at
[http://simos.info/blog/archives/639](http://simos.info/blog/archives/639)

Regarding fonts, Ubuntu comes by default with Greek Polytonic support (DejaVu font).
You can also use Gentium (packaged in Ubuntu) or even try the GFS fonts, at
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greek_Font_Society](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greek_Font_Society)

All these are free and open-source fonts :-)
