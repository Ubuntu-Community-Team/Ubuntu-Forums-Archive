### Post by monktbd on 2006-09-10
what's wrong with 2.14?

easy upgrade to the new gnome would be to upgrade to edgy eft by changing the repositories.
that will give you an unstable system at times though.
edgy eft is still in testing.

of course you could try to compile gnome yourself but that is not an easy process.

i will stick with 2.14 until a new stable version of ubuntu is out having 2.16 (i.e. edgy eft is released).

updating such a big part of the whole distribution is not easy and mostly not worth the hassle.
