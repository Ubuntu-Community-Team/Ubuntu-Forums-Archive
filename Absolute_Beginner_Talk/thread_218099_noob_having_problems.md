### Post by adam.tropics on 2006-07-18
caseyaberhart, HereInOz: Hey, I realise that at least one of you are on dial up, but just in case you change that, and just in case HereInOz doesn't know already, a tip! If you happen to use Big Pond  broadband, you will be given a monthly bandwidth maximum. This can be very irritating when downloading for example the latest Ubuntu discs and so on. I am not quite sure of the motivation, but they (and maybe other providers I don't know)offer use of a [file server]("http://files.bigpond.com/index.php") that **doesn't** count towards your monthly allowance. They currently have Dapper disks, and you are able to make requests too. As an example I recently requested the [Ubuntu Plus Add-ons]("http://files.bigpond.com/library/index.php?go=details&id=23335") cd. Just thought i'd share!!
