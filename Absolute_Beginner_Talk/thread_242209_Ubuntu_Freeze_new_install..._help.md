### Post by Druidor on 2006-08-23
pop the side off the PC & have a look at the fan on the CPU to see if it is clogged with dust & spins, 

Get the vacume cleaner out & use the upholstry brush to go over the fans to get the dust out.

also check that the cables, cards & memory are still firmly fitted as this can cause some weird problems too.


It could also be a power supply problem as you said it seems to do this when you are loading the machine more.
