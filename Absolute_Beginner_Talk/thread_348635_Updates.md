### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-01-30
Updates are indeed important: they solve bugs, provide more functionality and stability, and improve security; however, having said that, only you can decide to update or not; they are not mandatory, your system won't stop working if you choose not to update. But it **is** recommended since you'll have the latest version of the software.
