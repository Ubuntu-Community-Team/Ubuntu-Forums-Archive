### Post by dmizer on 2006-09-27
compared to normal email (text only) puctures take up a huge amount of space.  so email's probably not your answer unless you want to send the entire cd a few pictures (2 or 3) at a time over the course of several days.  most likely, they'll have to check their email every day and delete your previous email before you send the next batch.

a full cd (for example) can be around 700meg.  but most (not all, but most) email accounts have a maximum size limit of 100meg or less.  which means you'd fill up an account 7 times over by sending a single email.  and that's if they have no other email in their account at all.

so ... by doing it slowly, a piece at a time over email would take longer than it would to simply copy the cd and drop it in the regular mail.

there are other options for you, but if you're asking about how to send pictures, i'm not sure they would be good options for you.

if you want to do some research and tinker, some of these options might be:
set up an ftp server
set up a webserver to host your pictures from gallery
upload them to someplace like image shack
