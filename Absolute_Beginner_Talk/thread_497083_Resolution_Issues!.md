### Post by sab0403 on 2007-07-09
Oh, sorry, didn't see you posted your complete xorg.conf file... :oops:

So it's an ATI... hmm... have you tried adding your monitor's Horizontal Sync and Vertical Refresh specs? To do this, find out what they are (googling your monitor's model and "Horizontal Sync" and/or "Vertical Refresh" usually does it, or consult your documentation) and add the following to the "Monitor" section of the xorg.conf file: ```
HorizSync      28-61
VertRefresh    48-75
``` with your own numbers, of course.
