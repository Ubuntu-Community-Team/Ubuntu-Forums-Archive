### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-04-18
Open the control for the Menus with System> Preferences > Menu Layout. Adding programs is fairly simple, I'm sure ya can do it without any real trouble :).

Have fun!

### Post by zvacet on 2007-04-19
Just follow starcraft.man advice and when you are in menu layout you will see on the right side **new item**.Click it and launcher will pop up.
1.name = name of your app
2. command =you will see search bar on the right.Most of executables are in usr/bin so go there with search.If you find your app that is the path you need.
3.icon = usr/share/pixmaps
