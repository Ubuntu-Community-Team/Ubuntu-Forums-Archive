### Post by tuebinger on 2008-03-22
Although I don't know if it really matters, when I went shopping for a new router recently I was  drawn to the ones that mentioned Linux among the operating systems they support. I went with a Netgear router that specifically stated on the side of the box that it could be setup with Linux.  The other routers, like Linksys , only mentioned supporting Windows and/or Mac in their specs, and worse yet some had Vista-compatible stickers.  Naturally, everything's working swell with our new 'Linux-compatible' router!
