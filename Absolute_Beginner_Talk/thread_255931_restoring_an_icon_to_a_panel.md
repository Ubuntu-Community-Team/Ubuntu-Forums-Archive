### Post by risbac on 2006-09-12
Right click on the panel, then "add to the panel" (or so... I'm using a french Ubuntu, I just translate quickly). Then you will find it in the list of items to add to the panel.

### Post by ruedu on 2006-09-12
I think you just need to get the gnome-volume-manager running again.  If you click on the Actions menu (I believe it's called that, it's the one to the right of the Places menu) and choose Preferences->Sessions you can add in a start up program called gnome-volume-manager.  Then, log off and log back in and your volume controls should appear again.
