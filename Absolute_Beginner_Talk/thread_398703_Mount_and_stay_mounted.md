### Post by taurus on 2007-04-01
Please look at my previous post, #6. 

1.  Try not to mount /dev/hda1 to two different places/ media/hda1 & /media/window.
2.  You don't have /dev/hda5 so that entry shouldn't be in /etc/fstab.
3.  Don't use "nls=utf8,umask=0222" for ext3 filesystem.  If you want to write to it, use "chown" to change the ownership from root to your login name.
