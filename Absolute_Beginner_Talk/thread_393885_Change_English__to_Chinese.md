### Post by arochester on 2007-03-26
(When I answered a post like this, to try things out, I changed my computer to Cyrilic Russian - which I neither read or understand! ) 

According to "The Official Ubuntu Book" - "...you may want to change the language at a later date. To do this click System>Administration> Language Selector."

And "...When you see the login screen, you can use the Language button to choose a language for that specific login session. When you select the language you are asked if you want to make it the default language or use it just for that specific session."
