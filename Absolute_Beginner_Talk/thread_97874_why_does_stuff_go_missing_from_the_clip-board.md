### Post by blastus on 2005-12-01
From my experience, the clipboard in KDE (and I know GNOME has issues as well) is quirky. Sometimes it works sometimes it doesn't.

In KDE I find that if the application with the clipboard source is closed, the clipboard contents may be destroyed. I believe KDE has a unified clipboard architecture but GNOME has two different clipboard architectures--I could be wrong.
