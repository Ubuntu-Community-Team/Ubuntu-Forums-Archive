First off, Eclipse is an IDE, it might be a bit too powerful for what your trying to do.  If you want a text editor, gedit is in gnome, or kedit in KDE (ubuntu and kubuntu repectively) OR you can do it in a shell, using Nano, which is very straight-forward.
