### Post by solar george on 2007-04-24
I assume you mean either the login sound or the login window sound (the first thing I do is turn sounds like that off)

For the login window sound you need to use the login window option in the system/administration menu, the sound settings are listed under accessibility.

For the login sound the options are in system/preferences/sound.
