### Post by Daveth on 2008-03-07
open up Synaptic, System/ synaptic, and type in alsamixer into the search box. You should have both alsa-utils and alsamixergui with nice green boxes i.e. installed. If not, install them. Puts it nicely into sound and video, from whence you can tweak till your hearts content.
