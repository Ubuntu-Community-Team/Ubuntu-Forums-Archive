### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-04-09
You could try downloading from a different server.

When you open Add/Remove click on the Preferences button in the bottom left hand corner.

It will ask you for your password. Enter it.

When the preferences window opens, click on the Ubuntu Software Tab.

About midway down, there is a box that allows you to change servers.
