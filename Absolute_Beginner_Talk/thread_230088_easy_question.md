### Post by Indras on 2006-08-05
If you're already using Ubuntu, just open nautilus, go to Edit->Preferences->Behavior and click the check box next to "Always use text-entry location bar" and click close.

Now, in your nautilus window, there will be a text bar to enter locations in, and type the url to your ftp site you wish to log into:
ftp://ftp.<sitename>.com

This will open the FTP site in a nautilus window, allowing you to drag-and-drop files between your filesystem and the site.
