### Post by daimaru on 2007-06-01
well all i had to do was copy my thunderbird folder (with the profile in it) from windows and paste it into the .thunderbird folder under my home directory. you can see your hidden files by hitting ctrl+h or selecting it from the menu-->views-show hidden files . you probably should only copy the relevant files so backup your old .thunderbird folder before u do it just in case.

edit: i think the thunderbird profiles were under documents and settings - your user name - application data - mozilla - thunderbird or something. (@ least not the one under your program files folder)
