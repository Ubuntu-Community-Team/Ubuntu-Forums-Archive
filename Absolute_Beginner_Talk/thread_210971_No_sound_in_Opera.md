### Post by xael on 2006-07-07
If you love the Opera browser but are reluctant to use it, because it doesn't play any sound in Ubuntu, this little tip's for you: 

Open Opera Prefences, open Advanced tab, click "Add", click the blank next to "Protocol", type "mplayer", click "open with other application", type "/usr/lib/mplayer", next make sure sound is enabled in the content section, hit "ok" to exit preferences. Now you can open any webpage like "google videos" and Opera will play sounds in any video. Enjoy.
