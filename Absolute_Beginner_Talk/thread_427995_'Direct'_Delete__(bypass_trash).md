### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-04-29
Open any nautilus viewer, home folder for example.

Go to Edit > Preferences > Behaviour, then look at bottom of properties and tick "Include a Delete command that bypasses trash". It will appear right under the trash bin in the context menu.

That should do it :).
