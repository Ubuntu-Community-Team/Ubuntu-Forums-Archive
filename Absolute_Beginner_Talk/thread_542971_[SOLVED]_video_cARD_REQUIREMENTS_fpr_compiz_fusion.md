### Post by m9ke on 2007-09-05
I bought an ASUS N6200/TD/128 GeForce 6200 128MB 64-bit DDR AGP 4X/8X Low Profile Video Card for 37 bucks at newegg.com

Note this is an AGP card, not PCI or PCIe

Anyway, I installed this card, went to Restricted Drivers Manager, installed the nvidia glx driver and it just worked.

Edit:  LP reviewed this card on 9/4 on newegg's site:  "Pros: Silent and works out of the box with Ubuntu (both the open source and the proprietary/restricted drivers). Runs my monitor's 1680x1050 without any issue."

I won't vouch for LP's statement, YMMV, so check out the review on newegg's site, and decide if you can use this card.
