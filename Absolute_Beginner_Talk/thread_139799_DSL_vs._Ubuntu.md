### Post by pbransford on 2006-03-04
not to be rude, but he BEST place to get help/info about DSL is the DSL forums... only because the people there tend to primarily use DSL (rather than Ubuntu, as here) and they can give you more specific support.

[http://damnsmalllinux.org/cgi-bin/forums/ikonboard.cgi](http://damnsmalllinux.org/cgi-bin/forums/ikonboard.cgi)


Out of personal experience... DSL makes no writes to your drives unless specifically told to do so. It will not load such changes on boot either without explicitly specifying to do so (and where).
