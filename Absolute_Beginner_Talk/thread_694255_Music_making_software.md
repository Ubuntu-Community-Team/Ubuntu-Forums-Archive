### Post by linuxmagick on 2008-02-13
On the Gnome Panel choose **System** => **Preferences** => **Main Menu**.  In the Main Menu window, on the left hand side, choose the category for the shortcut.  For LMMS I would recommend **Sound & Video**.  Next, on the right side, click on the **New Item** button.  **Name** can be anything (this is what will appear on the menu).  Where it says **Command,** type in **lmms.**  Click **OK,** then **Close** to exit out of the Main Menu dialog.  Now when you click on **Applications** on the panel, and  go to **Sound & Video**, you should have a shortcut that will launch LMMS.
