### Post by Quicky on 2006-12-12
Adding text to video in Kino is pretty easy. If you click the FX button, in the Video Filter tab is a tool called ‘Titler’ (which sounds like a Nazi porn film if pronounced incorrectly). You can add text to the video from here as well as edit the font, size, colour, alignment and fade options of the text.
