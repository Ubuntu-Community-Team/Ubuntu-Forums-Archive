### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-09-08
The Live CD I think is shipped from Canonical, UK. It's perfectly safe for you to use. The Live CD is run from your system memory, RAM, and does not install anything on your computer until you double-click the install icon on the Live CD desktop. It does not alter your BIOS and do not harm any other OS you have installed on your disk(s). You can simply use the Live CD to get the hang of Ubuntu and install when you feel ready.
