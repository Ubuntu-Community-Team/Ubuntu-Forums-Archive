### Post by zephyrcat on 2008-02-28
I would recommend using Disk Usage Analyzer (Applications -> Accessories -> Disk Usage Analyzer) which will give you a graphical representation of where all the stuff on your hard drive is. That should make it obvious where the backups are stored and then you should just be able to delete them.
