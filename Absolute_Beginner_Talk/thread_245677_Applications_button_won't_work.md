### Post by amo-ej1 on 2006-08-28
you can switch to the text console by pressing ctrl+alt+f1 (f1 -> f6 will give you difference consoles). But you can also try to restart your x-server and restore your session (pressing ctrl+alt+backspace) will bring you back to the graphical login prompt. This will 'refresh' your session. Using the mouse it's also possible to create a launcher to a terminal on your desktop ...
