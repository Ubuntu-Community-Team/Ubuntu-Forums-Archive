### Post by rsambuca on 2007-09-22
You have to enter the system bios to change the boot order.  The method of entering the bios differs from manufacturer to manufacturer.  Some machines it is 'Esc', others it is F1, F6, etc.  Unless you can enter the bios to boot from the CD, there is nothing we can help you with.  I suggest you go to your computer manufacturer's website and find the manual.  Usually the method of entering the bios is in the first few pages.
