### Post by stuporglue on 2007-03-07
If it's *really* brand new, you should probably have restore disks, right? 

Or if it's an HP, you may have to burn your own restore disks like I did.  

If you've got restore disks, just backup any files you've created -- word documents, photos, music. There's no need to back up all of Windows since the restore disks have that huge chunk of data covered.
