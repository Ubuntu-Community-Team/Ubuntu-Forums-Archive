### Post by justin whitaker on 2007-09-03
> **HunkieChan said:**
> i was wondering if there's anyway to install any/every pc games on linux . thanks !!

Check the Game forums here, as well as the WIne HQ database, as well as Cedega's forums...basically, to answer your very broad question the answer is: maybe.

If there was a linux installer or port for the game, then, yes, it will run. If not, Direct X 7 and 8 titles seem to be pretty well covered by WINE, and Direct X 9 is certainly coming along. Direct X10 doesn't even run properly on Vista, so we will forget that for now. :)

It also depends on how much work you want to go through to get games to run. Some troubleshooting, even of titles that are "supported" is the norm. Don't expect to drop in the discs, fire up wine (game executable).exe and be playing in 10 minutes...although that does happen from time to time. 

So, maybe. That's the answer.
