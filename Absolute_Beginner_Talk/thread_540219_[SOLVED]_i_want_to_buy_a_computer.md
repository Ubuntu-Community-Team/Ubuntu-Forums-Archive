### Post by Dark Hornet on 2007-09-01
Hello--

From personal experience, DO NOT buy an ATI card (radeon is ATI)--their driver support for the Open Source Community is not nearly as developed as the drivers are for nVidia.  As far as the rest of your set up goes, it looks just fine--personally I would go with one higher end nVidia card than two running in SLI, as you won't see any performance differences running under the driver provided for Ubuntu....Hope this helps, and good luck.
