### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-09-11
Does your router or network have a firewall?  If so, check the ports that GAIM uses (should be in preferences, connections or somesuch - not in front of my Ubuntu machine at the moment) and make a note of the port(s) that it uses to connect.  Then, you'll need to set your router to open those ports.

For specifics and how-tos, search the forum for "open ports" and/or "port forwarding"
