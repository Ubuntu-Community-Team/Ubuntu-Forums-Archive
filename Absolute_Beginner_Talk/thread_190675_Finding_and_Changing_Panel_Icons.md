### Post by ruffneck on 2006-06-06
Try: [http://search.deviantart.com/searchcraft/?cmd=1&offset=0&search=firefox+icon](http://search.deviantart.com/searchcraft/?cmd=1&offset=0&search=firefox+icon)
or 
[www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org)

Save downloaded icon to somewhere in you're Home folder. 
To change  firefox icon, right-click the icon on the panel --> properties --> click on the icon and browse to where your new icon is and select it.

Note, that when changing themes the the firefox icon will remain the same and you will have to manually change it to the new theme's firefox icon.
