### Post by bernied on 2007-11-28
Are you sure that your pc can boot from cd and that you have made boot-from-cd a higher priority than boot-from disk in your bios settings? Can you check this with another live-cd such as, for example, a windows recovery disc?

Did you burn the cd yourself and if so, did you burn it 'as an image'? Can you look at the contents of the cd in your other (normal) operating system? Is there just one file, or many? If there is just one file, then the cd has been incorrectly made.
