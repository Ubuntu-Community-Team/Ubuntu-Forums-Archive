### Post by jbiskupiak on 2007-09-18
The .docx files are from Office 2007.  All the Excel, Powerpoint and Word files end in x (for xhtml).  There is a converter program available from Windows to convert Office 2007 files to Office 2003 files which would enable you to read them in Open Office as well.  The converter can be downloaded from the following site:

[https://www.microsoft.com/downloads/details.aspx?FamilyId=941B3470-3AE9-4AEE-8F43-C6BB74CD1466&displaylang=en](https://www.microsoft.com/downloads/details.aspx?FamilyId=941B3470-3AE9-4AEE-8F43-C6BB74CD1466&displaylang=en)
