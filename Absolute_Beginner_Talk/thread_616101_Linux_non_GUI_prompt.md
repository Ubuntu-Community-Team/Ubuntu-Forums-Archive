### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-11-17
I believe Alt+PrntScrn(Hold them together while continuing the rest of the sequence)+R+S+E+I+U+B(In other words Raising Skinny Elephants Is Utterly Boring;)) is what you want. This sequence safely restarts your PC after all the drives/devices are safely unmounted or stopped, this is particularly useful if the OS stops responding.
