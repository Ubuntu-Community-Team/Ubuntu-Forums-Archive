### Post by pveith on 2007-03-04
This could be a problem of the 3d chip / radeon driver. It supports a canvas of 2048x2048 in both Screens together, which means you can't do dual Monitor beryl with this configuration, which either have a bigger horizontal or vertical resolution (both screen added together). I am not sure if i got the facts right (have not found a cite for this), but for me beryl doesn't work beyond 1024+768 on both screens. Haven't put much energy in this for some time now, though...

Is there an in-between resolution which works for you? like 1280x800 (or an other widescreen resolution)? A friend of mine has also difficulties with setting different resolutions to the screens and haven't found a working solution for this, sorry...
