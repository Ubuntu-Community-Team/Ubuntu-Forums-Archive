### Post by jasay on 2006-06-04
Most of the drivers (with the exception of some wireless and graphics drivers) are included with ubuntu.  My suggestion is to try the [livecd]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download") (I think they renamed it "desktop cd" in the latest release, Dapper Drake).  It runs ubuntu from a cd and doesn't touch your harddrive at all.  That way you can see if everything works without any worry about messing up your system.

This used to be the bane of a Linux install years ago, finding drivers (or something that partly worked) for each piece of hardware. Nowadays most things are recognized on install, especially with Ubuntu. I would not be concerned about your DVD player, it almost certainly will be fine! Even NVIDIA cards will not require you to download separate drivers, Ubuntu will configure it for you (choose the package nvidia-glx).
