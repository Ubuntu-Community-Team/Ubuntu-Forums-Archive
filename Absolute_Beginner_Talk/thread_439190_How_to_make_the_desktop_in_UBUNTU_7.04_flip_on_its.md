### Post by lazyart on 2007-05-30
For the record, it isn't so much of a Windows thing as it is a hotkey included in Intel video drivers.  I'd flip screens at work to mess with people's heads.  One guy did it by mistake.  When I got to his desk because a call had come in, he had turned his LCD upside down so that he could work.  It was funny.  Control-Alt Down was all it took to fix it again.
