Hello people,

I needed the ability to play streaming Real video, so I installed Real Player 10 in my U7.10B. Wasn't that big of a problem, and it even worked, ie. the videos were playing. There was just one small problem and that was the lack of sound.
Seems that's not all that uncommon, and as I was reading about it I realized that maybe I should check if my sound in general was working. So what to do? Kill my installation, put a fresh copy on the hd, and then think of updates as The Enemy and not install any until the next official version comes out?
