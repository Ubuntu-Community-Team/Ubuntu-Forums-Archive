### Post by mlentink on 2007-06-16
By closing the application do you mean clicking on one of the three little buttons on the top right of the window?
Or byt clicking on the secondmost  button on the lower left?

If all else fails, you can always kill it by going to System>Administration>System Monitor
Select the "Processes tab" and look for "Gnome-app-insta". Right-click it and then choose to option to kill it.

Untidy, and not preferred, as you will not find out why you cannot close it.
