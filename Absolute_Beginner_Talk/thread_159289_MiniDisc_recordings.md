### Post by xyz on 2006-04-13
-Connect stereo cable to "line out" (phone) in MD
-Connect the other end of that cable to "line in" (MIC) in your computer
-Applications>Sound and Video>Audacity and Volume Control
-In Volume Control:click on "Capture" and unckeck "Microphone" and "Phone"
-In Audacity: set to "MIC" + level (not too high) "Output/Input Volume"
- Click on "Pause Still" (blue button) and "Rec" (red round button)
-Start MD to check "Recording Level" (for digital recordings, don't record to 'max level'
-Click on "Pause Still" to begin recording
-Click on MD's "Play"
-Finally, Audacity>File>Export as Wave on Desktop or /home/user
Wave files work with any OS.
