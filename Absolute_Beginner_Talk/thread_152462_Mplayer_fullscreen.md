Whenever I try to play a video in fullscreen in Mplayer it never actually stetches the video out to full screen... it just stays the same size but with a nice big black border.

---

### Post by jrib on 2006-03-29
Go to preferences and choose 'xv' as the video output

---

### Post by taurus on 2006-03-29
Or put "zoom=yes" in ~/.mplayer/config...
```

echo "zoom=yes" >> ~/.mplayer/config

```
