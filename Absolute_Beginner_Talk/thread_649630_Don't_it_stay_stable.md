### Post by Yoke &amp; Chung on 2007-12-25
You want to share your system specs? 

I find Gutsy a lot better than Feisty, at least it can load onto my Dell Notebook and configures everything automatically.

I have so far installed Gutsy on 3 systems and non give me any major issues:

1. Dell Inspiron Notebook, duo core 2Ghz, 2 GB RAM, nVidia 8400M graphics, in fact Ubuntu runs better than Vista on this notebook. Ddual boot Vista & Ubuntu, hardly use Vista, but since I have already paid for the license, might as well install it.

2. Pentium 3 - 800 Mhz, 20GB HDD, 256MB RAM (upgraded to 384MB later), 32MB Riva TNT graphics (changed to FX5500 with 256MB later for some games). Slow at times, but generally ok. (Purely Ubuntu)

3. AMD Sempron 1.6GHz, 1 GB RAM, on board nVidia 6100 graphics. This is the only one that gives me some problems during the installation, mainly due to conflict of SATA & IDE drives, and some display issue with onboard 6100 graphics card. After some playing around, it is now running Xubuntu Gutsy like a charm. Dual booting Xubuntu & Windows XP, XP is there purely for games.
