### Post by brady618 on 2006-05-12
N00b here too, but I was having the same prob when I first got xcfe.  I have Drapper, so I don't know if it's different, but here's all you have to do.  Right click the toolbar, click add, click launcher, under command, type whatever command it is to launch whatever program you're trying to get.  Most likely it'll be /usr/bin/(whatever prog you want), or at elast that's what it is for all of my shortcuts.  Hope this helps.  Peace.
