### Post by maol62 on 2006-01-01
In k3b, you should use the New video-dvd project (you find it under File-New Project in the menu). It could be named something slightly differnet because a dont use English as my standard language in Ubuntu. Then you get, in the bottom of the screen, the two folders, AUDIO_TS and VIDEO_TS, that a standard DVD consists of. Now you just need to drag and drop your TS and VOB files into the VIDEO_TS folder and burn and it should be ok.
