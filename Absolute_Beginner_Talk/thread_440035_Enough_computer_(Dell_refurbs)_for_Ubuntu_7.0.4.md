### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-11
Ummm, I don't have any experience buying refurb from dell, I've always made custom or bought new. I don't think I'd recommend getting a p3 when you could get an M for a few hundred dollars more though. You probably could get ubuntu (or at the very least Xubuntu) on it, but it would probably feel very slow doing some things... so if ya have the cash you should go for the M/or even a p4 (I'm still on my 5 year old P4 :D).  Make sure your PC comes with an NVidia card btw, just makes it easier and if you want wifi easily, avoid Linksys...

Hope ya get a good one :)
