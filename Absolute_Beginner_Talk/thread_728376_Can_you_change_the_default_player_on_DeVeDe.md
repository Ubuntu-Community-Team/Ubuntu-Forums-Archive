### Post by bumanie on 2008-03-18
As far as I know, mplayer is the player needed for devede as it is one of devede's dependencies. What is causing you problems with mplayer? Mplayer had bugs in feisty and gutsy, mainly sound. There is a wor around for this problem. On gutsy all you have to do is enable backports and if sound is your problem, enabling backports will fix it. System --> Administration --> Software Sources --> click updates tab and enable backports.
