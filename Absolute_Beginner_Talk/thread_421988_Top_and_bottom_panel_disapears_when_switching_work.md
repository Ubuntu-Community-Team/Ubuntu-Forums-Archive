### Post by nanotube on 2007-04-25
try pressing ctl-alt-f2, (that will switch you to a virtual console), and then ctl-alt-f7 (that will switch you back to the gui), and see if that restores the panels.

i know this is not a "fix", but if it works, at least you don't have to restart X all the time :)
