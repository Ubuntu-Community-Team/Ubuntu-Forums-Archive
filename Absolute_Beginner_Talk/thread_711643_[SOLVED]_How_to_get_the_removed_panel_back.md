### Post by Presto123 on 2008-02-29
A lot of people who delete that main panel wonder where to get back their battery meter/network monitor as well (I did once too).

If you have that issue just right-click on the panel and select "Add to Panel" and you will get a list of applications. Select "Notification Area" to get it back.
