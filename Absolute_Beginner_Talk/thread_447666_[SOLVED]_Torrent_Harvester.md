### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-18
I don't really think ya need to search across tonnes of sites, I've tried searching the same things across many of them and find that quite often the search results are very redundant(i.e. same torrent files are indexed with slightly modified names but same tracker/files) with only a few differences... I recommend ya just use isoHunt or Mininova, or a combination of both. I use only those two and I've never failed to find what I was looking for. That said, if your aiming to get something less than legal maybe your preferred place would be piratebay...

I don't know of any desktop program that pulls searches from all of the top sites... I don't think its that hard to search the three of the ones I mentioned, I doubt theres much missing from them given their enormous user base.
