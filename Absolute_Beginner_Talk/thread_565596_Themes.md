### Post by Incense on 2007-10-02
> **clancymf said:**
> Thanks for the info ( I had the same questions), but after you download these how do you apply them.  Is it through Emerald or is there an easier / more effiecent way? 

Thanks and regards,
Mick

Click System, Preferences, Theme. Then you simply drag your theme in the Them Prefs window, or click "Install Theme" to locate it on your hard drive. You can even click customize and mix a match installed themes, or to set a different icon theme. 

Emerald is what you would use for Beryl, or Compiz themes if you have it set up that way. You can drag Emerald themes into the emerald themes window if that's what you downloaded.

Hope that helps.
