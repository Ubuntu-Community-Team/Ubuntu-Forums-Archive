### Post by dvarsam on 2006-04-04
If you want to share files between your Ubuntu & your Windows, do NOT forget to make a FAT32 Partition.

You should have 3 Partitions (or Hard Drives if you prefer):

1. EXT3 for your Ubuntu
2. NTFS for your Windows
3. FAT32 to move files back&forth from Your Ubuntu to Windows.

To setup your own keyboard language with your Ubuntu, look at the "Customization Tips&Tricks" page (in this Forum).
I have made a Tutorial on this.

To open "Open Office Org's Writer" files in Window's "Microsoft Word", you MUST Save the Files created on your Ubuntu in the ".doc" format. 

You MUST also learn how to "mount/unmount" Partitions.

Good Luck!
