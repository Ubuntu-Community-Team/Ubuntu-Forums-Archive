if you have enabled hidden folders/files to be shown, you will find a fonts folder in your home directory. just put them in there. alternatively, you can go to 'fonts' in your main menu, go to the font folder, and drop the fonts into there. either way, the result is the same - the fonts available to you (and only to you) go in /home/<username>/.fonts.
