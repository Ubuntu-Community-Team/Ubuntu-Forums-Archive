### Post by nereid on 2007-03-12
The system load and the CPU usage aren't hurting your PC. If the CPU is occupied some things may take a little bit more time but that's it. The percentage is relative to the amount of programs you've running on your pc and what they are doing.

The memory managment is different in Linux and in Windows. Windows doesn't use the whole physical memory and starts swapping right after you've logged in. Linux uses the whole physical memory, that is also the reason why your RAM is always full ;), but this is a Good Thing (tm).

Don't worry about it as long as you have not changed your processor speed manually or something like that.
