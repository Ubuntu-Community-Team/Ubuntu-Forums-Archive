### Post by wheredidrealitygo on 2008-01-07
There are three types of XP discs, OEM, Retail, and VLK. If you got XP with your computer, your copy is OEM, and only another OEM disc will work with your product key.

Also, some OEM discs are remastered, and programmed to only work on the computer they came with, so you may find that other OEM versions won't work on your system. Your computer manufacturer will be able to (and are legally required to) be able to provide you with replacement discs, although maybe not for free depending on your manufacturer.
