### Post by Therion on 2008-02-12
I think the closest thing to a "Program Files" that Ubuntu has would be either /usr/lib or maybe just /usr.
As I recall though, most of those files will be hidden, so you'll need to select "View Hidden Files" from the menu.

### Post by Het Irv on 2008-02-12
I know that some of the files are kept in your home folder, but they are hidden.  These are your personal settings
Press ctrl+H while in your home folder to see them.

The actual program will be somewhere in /usr, files here are owned by root.
