### Post by Belathor on 2007-09-01
That is the driver for the wired connection. If you have an X41, you should have an intel 2200 (B/G) or an intel 2915 (A/B/G) wireless chipset. You shouldn't need either of those drivers in the links if you can use a wired connection with your tablet.
