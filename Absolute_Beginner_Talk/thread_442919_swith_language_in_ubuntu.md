### Post by swisscow on 2007-05-14
Hi there

In KDE system settings, select language/region. At the bottom of the page choose new language install, select the language you want, adept will fire up and install it. Once it's finished, just use the move up, move down buttons on the right of the language/region setting page and then logout, log in. Works for me with English and German - not sure about Chinese or Japanese displays though.

Good luck
