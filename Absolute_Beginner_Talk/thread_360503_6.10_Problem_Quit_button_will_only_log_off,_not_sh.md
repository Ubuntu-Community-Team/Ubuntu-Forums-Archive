### Post by IndyBart on 2007-02-13
Crooksey,

Thank you for your help. I have resolved the problem. The "Ask on Logout" option in the Sessions Preference was deselected and "Show Actions Menu" under Menu Bar in the Login Window Preferences was deselected. Reselecting both has me back to where I want to be (and I found out what GDM is!). Thanks again.
