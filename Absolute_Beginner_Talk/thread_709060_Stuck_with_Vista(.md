### Post by desertboy on 2008-02-27
1. Load Vista, right click my computer select properties, select device manager (Might be under a hardware tab been a long time since I used windows).
List the video card, sound card (Probably not needed) the network card and the wireless chipset, here.

2. Also when your on the boot menu for the Ubuntu Livecd press f6 and add irqpoll to the line of text and press enter. (This is needed on 1 of my machines for it to boot.) 

3, Try the Hardy beta cd's as well they're in the dev section.

4. As soon as the livecd starts booting (IE you selected something from the bootmenu) press ctrl+alt+f1 this bypasses the splash screen I think I needed to do this to get it to boot. (You can also change this at the same point as step 2)


I'm pretty certain it'll work with a bit of tweaking I got my Pavilion working.

I'm betting it'll be an ati mobility if it's a laptop with a broadcom wireless chipset which should work out of the box.
