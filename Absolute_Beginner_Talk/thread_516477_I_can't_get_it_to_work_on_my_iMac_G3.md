### Post by pandan on 2007-11-02
Hey there,

I've gotten previous version of xubuntu (6.06) working on a G3.
Ok. So what I've learned.

1. The old tray loading CD-ROM drives are more sensitive about the media you use.
     Burn slow (<16x) on CD-R media that's designed for audio.  It should say 'Compact Disc Recordable' and not just 'CD-R' on it.

2. You have to press and hold 'C' with the CD in the drive just before and during the startup chime.

3.  If your firmware is old (e.g. last OS is Mac OS 7.x) step 2. may not work.  You can update it by installing a newer version of Mac OS 9, which, I believe will update the firmware.
