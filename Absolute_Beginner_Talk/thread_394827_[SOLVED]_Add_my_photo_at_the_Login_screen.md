### Post by jeffathehutt on 2007-03-27
I don't know about edgy, as I don't use it.  Under dapper, you should go to System->Administration->Login Window and select a theme with a face browser.  Then, go to System->Preferences->About Me.  There should be a little square button in the upper left.  Click on that and select a picture.
