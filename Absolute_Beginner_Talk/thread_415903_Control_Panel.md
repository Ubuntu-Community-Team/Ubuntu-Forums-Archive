> Wasn't Feisty supposed to have a new "Control Panel" ??  I had seed it on earlier betas.  :confused:

You have to make it show it....
go to system->Preferences->Main Menu

In the left panel, select "Preferences" and put a checkmark to the "Control Panel" thingy.
It should now show in system->Preferences->Control Center
