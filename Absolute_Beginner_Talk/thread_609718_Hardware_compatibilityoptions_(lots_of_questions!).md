### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-11-11
I personally would suggest that you get an Nvidia 6200, it's not that expensive(Maybe about 70$), it works perfectly with Ubuntu 7.10 and it gives you the full benefits of the effects and 3D. And don't worry about the pins, PCI-Express is PCI-Express, there aren't any derivatives for now, so all you have to say is "Give me a PCI-E Nvidia 6200";).

About the RAM, 512 Mb is enough for the stuff you want to do, but it wouldn't hurt to get an extra 256 or 512Mb RAM module, about the speed, as it is indicated as 667Mhz, do not go above it as it could be incompatible with your system which may mean the death of the RAM module or of the main board itself. About the pins, once again, DDR2 is only DDR2, so don't worry overmuch about, just make sure you say DDR2 instead of DDR or DDR3 and you should be safe:).
