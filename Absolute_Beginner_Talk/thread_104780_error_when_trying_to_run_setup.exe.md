### Post by ninotob on 2005-12-16
The easiest thing to do is just throw a router between your cable/dsl modem and your computer.  This has the added benefit of making it possible to share your connection with other machines -- many new routers have an automatic network sensing option when you first hook them up.  I recently added a wireless netgear router (though I don't think I'd suggest it because as far as I can tell, you can't turn off the ability to admin the router from the wireless network) and it automagically configured everything.  For my desktop, I just set it to get an address by DHCP and plugged into the router.
