### Post by groggyboy on 2006-11-21
There's a program called [streamtuner]("http://www.nongnu.org/streamtuner/") in the repositories, which lets you browse several streaming internet radio directories in one place (SHOUTcast, Live365, xiph.org, etc).  You'll need a media player as well.

There's also an app called [Songbird]("http://www.songbirdnest.com") which is a mashup between firefox and a media player.  You can  play any mp3 or other audio files that you find as you surf the web.  It has an excellent list of bookmarked music blogs.  It's still in testing however, and likes to crash.  Its a really neat program.  You can find an installation script for Ubuntu [here]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/songbird").
