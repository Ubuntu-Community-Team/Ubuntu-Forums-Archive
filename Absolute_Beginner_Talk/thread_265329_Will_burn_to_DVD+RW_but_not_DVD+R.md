### Post by bbukh on 2006-09-25
If you have a dual-boot system, try booting into the other OS and use the DVD-burning software that comes with that OS. If that works, it is a probably software problem.

Check whether your have DVD+R or DVD-R and whether your DVD-writer supports writing to them (by looking up the hardware specs for your DVD-writer).
