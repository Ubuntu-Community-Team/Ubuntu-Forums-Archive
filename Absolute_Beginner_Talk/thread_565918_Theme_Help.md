### Post by tlink on 2007-10-02
the "~" denotes your home directory, so it would be /home/<username>/.themes/Samui-2.0/gfx/

Also note that the "." infront of themes denotes it as a hidden direcotry, so use Ctrl-H in nautilus to show hidden directories, or ls -a from a command line
