### Post by DSn0wMan on 2006-07-16
You can manage most of this through System -> Preferences -> Themes. You may try downloading some new themes from gnome-look.org, or kde-look.org depending on what desktop you are useing.

To change the color of panels, simply right click the panel and select properties.

You may also find this thread helpfull. [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=77694](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=77694)
