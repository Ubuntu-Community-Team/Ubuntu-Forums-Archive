### Post by NICU on 2007-05-04
Hi, the menu bars are very versatile in Gnome and XFCE (probably KDE too I haven't tried there).  You can modify the bars that you have by adding and removing items to the bars themselves.  I would suggest creating a new menu bar before modifying the current ones.  I made a mistake and erased all my menu bars on my first try :-(.  

Here's a pretty common tutorial to make Ubuntu look like OSX [http://www.taimila.com/ubuntuosx.php](http://www.taimila.com/ubuntuosx.php).  It includes the centered launch bar on the bottom.  You don't have to follow the rest of the steps (unless you want to).  After reading through and experimenting you'll know enough to do most of what you want.
