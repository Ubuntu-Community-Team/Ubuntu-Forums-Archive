### Post by rplantz on 2005-11-09
This thread reminds me of a book I once reviewed. I was a computer science professor, and publishers sent me books all the time, hoping I would use theirs in my classes.

One of the things I did was to open a book in the middle and start reading. I figured that if I, already knowing the subject, had trouble reading it my students wouldn't stand a chance.

Well, about every fourth word on one book as an acronym. I soon got stuck on QAD. After a lot more searching through the book, I figured it out: Quick And Dirty. I did not select that book.

Professors need to be careful what we say in the classroom. So I told my students that RTFM = read the goshdarn manual.
