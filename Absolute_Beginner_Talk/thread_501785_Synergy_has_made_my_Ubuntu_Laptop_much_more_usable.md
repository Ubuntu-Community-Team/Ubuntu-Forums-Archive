### Post by TheTaylorEffect on 2007-07-15
I recently installed Ubuntu 7.04 on my Sony V505 series notebook.

The V505 series machines are ultra portable (12" screens, and 4lbs), but sometimes when I'm trying to type on that little keyboard I feel as though I'm wearing boxing gloves... [Synergy]("http://synergy2.sourceforge.net/") helped solve that problem!

So, I installed SynergyClient my notebook Ubuntu notebook, and the SynergyServer on my dual screen Windows XP desktop .

Now its like having a cheap alternative to a docking station.

I've never used my notebook this much before, and I'm learning TONS about Linux becuase of its accessibility. My notebook is running all the time - and I can access it whenever I need with a flick of the wrist.
