### Post by Nano Geek on 2007-11-10
You might try going to **Systems=>Administration=>Screens and Graphics** and seeing if you can change it from there. 

About the GIMP. You are correct, there is no quick Red Eye remover. You can do it manually, or you could use F-Spot which does have a Red Eye tool. It's under **Applications=>Graphics=>F-Spot Photo Manager**.
