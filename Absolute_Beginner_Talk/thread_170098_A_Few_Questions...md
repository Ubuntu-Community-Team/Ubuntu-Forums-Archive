### Post by nalmeth on 2006-05-04
You might have had a corrupted disc.
I don't know why your windows would have been corrupted like that, but glad you were able to fix it!
Yeah, I recommend just going with the regular i386 ubuntu. The AMD64 is good, but you'll have to put some effort into a work-around to get your required 32-bit apps going. It's not very difficult at all, but as a self-described absolute beginner, I suggest the generic kernel.
Check that you have a good disc image, and try to burn the CD at a lower speed.
Good luck with attempt #2!
