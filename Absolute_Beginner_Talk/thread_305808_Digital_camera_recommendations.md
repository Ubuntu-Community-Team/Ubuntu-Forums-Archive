### Post by livinginx on 2006-11-25
I work in a camera store, very fun job.  Casios are great for features, pictures aren't as good as a Cannon, Fuji, or a Sony as far as Point and Shoots go.

If you are looking for a dSLR, I would recomend a Nikon or the Sony A100k.

Basically, it boils down to what you want from a camera and what you want the camera to do for you.  Most all cameras out there have 2 usb modes.  PPTP (or something like that) which is used for Direct Printing/Camera Control, and Mass Storage which acts like everybody else was talking about. 

If you are buying a new Camera, don't go to a box store like Best Buy, go to an actual camera store.
