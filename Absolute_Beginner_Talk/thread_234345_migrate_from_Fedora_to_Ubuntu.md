### Post by msak007 on 2006-08-11
It's not really possible to "upgrade" FC to Ubuntu, as they are different OSes. However as was already stated if you have a separate /home partition, it may be possible to wipe everything else out and keep that partition when you do the install. I would just do the complete wipe since you don't have much to back up. As far as the Firefox extensions go, you don't have to reinstall any of them. When you're backing up data, back up the **.mozilla** folder in your home directory. This is the folder used for Firefox settings, and it includes your profile's folder which has any extensions / themes you've already installed. I believe for Thunderbird, you need to back up the **.thunderbird** folder in your home directory as well (sorry can't verify don't have Thunderbird on my laptop and don't have access to my desktop at this time).
