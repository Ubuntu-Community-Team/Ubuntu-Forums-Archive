### Post by orb9220 on 2007-06-09
Fotofox 1.0.9.2 extension for Firefox

Easily select, tag, title and upload pictures from your computer to an online photo service. Works with Flickr, Tabblo, 23hq, Smugmug, Marela, and Kodak EasyShare Gallery...

Allows me to drag and drop from file manager,assign tags and shows me my upload limit left.
Works great on whatever OS you are using.
