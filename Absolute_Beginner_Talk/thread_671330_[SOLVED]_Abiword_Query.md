### Post by Frem on 2008-01-20
The first poster had it right, you're in overwrite mode. Look at the bottom right-hand side of the AbiWord window; a little "OVR" is displayed in the status bar. The cursor is also red to alert you to this fact.

You probably just hit the "Ins" (Insert) key on your keyboard by mistake. Locate it and press it again, and all will be well.

By the way, OpenOffice.org also supports overwrite mode, and reinstalling AbiWord won't fix anything. This ain't Windows, fortunately. ;-)
