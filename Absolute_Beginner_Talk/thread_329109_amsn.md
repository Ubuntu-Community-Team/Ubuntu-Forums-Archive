### Post by jem7v on 2007-01-01
While I would say that Gaim is definitely less bloated, it doesn't support tasty tasty custom emoticons, which are sometimes vital to those of us who are obsessive about sending tiny, silly pictures of dancing platypusses to our friends.

k! To make it remember your username and password, at that massive Log In dialogue click the Add New Profile button.  Then follow the instructions.

To make it log in immediately when it opens up, go to Account -> Preferences -> Session and choose Sign In Automatically when ... launched.

And to make aMSN NOT SUCK (i.e., to make its text and icons anti-aliased instead of jagged and gross) install it with this script instead of the usual way: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=297676](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=297676)  It works really well.  Took maybe 15 mins to compile, aMSN works like a charm for me now and looks much prettier than it used to.
