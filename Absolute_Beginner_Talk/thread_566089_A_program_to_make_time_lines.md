### Post by Goliath! on 2007-10-08
I think exactly what you're looking for is available open source from MIT: [http://simile.mit.edu/timeline/](http://simile.mit.edu/timeline/)

I have been looking for a timeline generator myself, and haven't found exactly what I need.  MIT Timeline is the closest I've found but I need to print and Timeline doesn't seem to do that easily.

With Timeline you have to create an xml file of your timeline events.  If that's too much for you, search lifehacker.com for timeline - there's a post from Gina about Timeline and she provides an xml generator.  Cool!

BTW, if you find anything better, please let me know!
