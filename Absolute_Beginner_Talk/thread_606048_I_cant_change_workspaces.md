### Post by thisbeadam on 2007-11-07
same issue.
Although, workspaces are not a big deal to me, but the closest I have got to fixing this is by this.

On the bottom right hand of the screen, right click on the workspace icon(s).
Preferences, then on show workspaces, have it set to one row. And one workspace.

Then go into compiz settings, general options, desktop size tab, then slide the bar to the amount you want.

This SHOULD enable workspace ability through compiz.
