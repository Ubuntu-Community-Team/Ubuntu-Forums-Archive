### Post by chiefofthejojos on 2008-02-01
Just right-click on the AWN dock and select preferences.  Then go to applets and select what you need.  For Network manager, you will need a systray, but you have to make sure no other systray is working before you add it or it won't work.  Then just make sure nm-applet runs at startup and it will show up in the systray.  AWN has a volume applet, but I can't get it to work. :(
