### Post by nanotube on 2006-05-15
all the suggestions made above are correct. i just wanted to note another neat feature - if you place your mouse cursor over the workspace switcher, and scroll the wheelie, it will change desktops.

also, i have set up ctl-alt-1 through ctl-alt-0 to switch to my desktops 1-10, very convenient. (unfortunately, no convenient shortcut to switch to desktop 11 and 12, but oh well...) 

in general, if you explore the keyboard shortcuts panel that aysiu has mentioned, you will find some nice stuff.
