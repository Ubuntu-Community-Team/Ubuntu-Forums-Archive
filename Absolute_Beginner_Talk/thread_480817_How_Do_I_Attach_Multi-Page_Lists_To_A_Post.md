You probably want to put CODE tags around the text. Type the left bracket '[' followed by the word 'code' followed by the right bracket ']'. Then paste the output of your command. Then to enclose the area, type left bracket '[' followed by a slash '/' followed by the word 'code' followed by a right bracket ']'.

Or just press the "CODE" button in the message editing toolbar (it's a pound sign '#').

To attach a file scroll to below the message editing box, where you see the  "Additional Options" headline. from there click "Manage Attachments".
