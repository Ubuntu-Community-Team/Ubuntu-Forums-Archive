### Post by akniss on 2006-08-09
To speak from experience on this... I've been using the MS Exchange plugin for Evolution for about a year now.  It does seem to work well most of the time.  However, it only interfaces with the 'webmail' portion of the exchange server.  This makes it s...l...o...w... but useable.  The only thing to make sure of is to be sure when you put appointments on the calendar, you put them into the exchange calendar rather than the 'personal' calendar or else you won't have the info when you need it. (i learned that the hard way)
