### Post by ReaderRat on 2006-11-25
No, I don't believe you can install a printer using the LiveCD. Accordingly, LinuxPrinting.org says your printer works "Perfectly" with Linux.See [**[color=blue]HERE[/color]**](http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=HP-DeskJet_5740) for driver particulars.

EDIT:  Your printer will probably be recognized and configurable after Ubuntu is installed. If you have other peripherals to install, you may check some of them in the Hardware Compatibility List (in my signature).
