### Post by Lil_Eagle on 2007-02-05
There are plenty of books that you can read online.  There are plenty of sites that cater to new linux users.  In my opinion, [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php) is one of the best.  

There are plenty of others.  Just look at the taglines on the some of the postings in this forum.

Linux isn't unix, but the commands are VERY similiar if not exact in most cases, so if you have experience with unix, you should feel pretty much at home on the command line.

Don't forget that every command has a man page (for example: [COLOR=Red]man cp[/COLOR], [COLOR=Red]man ls[/COLOR], etc ) and most have info pages as well, which are a little less terse.
