> **4Orbs said:**

Well, you could copy and paste the text into something like Babelfish or Google Language Tools:

[http://babelfish.altavista.com/](http://babelfish.altavista.com/)

[http://www.google.com/language_tools](http://www.google.com/language_tools)

But any automatic translation is going to be fairly haphazard.

And unless you know the sender -- which you presumably don't, otherwise you could ask them to re-send in a language you can read -- you might likely have spam there, anyway.
