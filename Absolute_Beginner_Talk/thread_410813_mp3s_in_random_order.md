### Post by TheLive1 on 2007-04-16
Not sure of this but..
Perhaps its the naming scheme of your files, like how files were named when ripped, and then load when you drag a folder in to your mp3/mp4 player on your computer.

For example if they are named:
1.Song NumberOne.mp3
2.Song NumberTwo.mp3
11.Song NumberEleven.mp3
I think it will load 1.Song NumberOne.mp3 first and then 11.Song NumberEleven.mp3

And also remember Linux is case sensitive, where Caps are aligned first and then lower case.

I hope that made sense
