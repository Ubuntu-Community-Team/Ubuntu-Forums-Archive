### Post by Tabenx on 2007-08-30
Go to System-Preferences then click on Volume Control. On the Volume part it should have a speaker with an X through it at the bottom. Simply click on that speaker and the X will be removed then raise the Volume (if it isn't already) and then it should work. Post back if it doesn't.

### Post by ThyPiGuy on 2007-09-01
I was having an issue thats almost identical to yours just a few minutes ago (on Window none the less).

The mute button seemed to  be "stuck on" on my HP latpop.

Googling my problem I came across this forum -- which didn't help me, but I felt obligated to share how I fixed it.

I have multiple sound devices -- one the main sound card, another one was bluetooth audio.  It was set to bluetooth for whatever reason.  I changed it back to the normal sound card, and now it works.

So make sure you have the correct sound device selected to use.

Cheers.
