### Post by NiBu on 2007-04-25
Normally you should be able to see all the stuff of a such data-cd in /cdrom - Folder with nautilus or in terminal.
Just start gnome-terminal and type:
cd /cdrom 
after it :
ls -a 
So you should be able to see all your files and folders.

Or you can go to the "Places"-menu -> "My Computer" and after it you should be able to see all available devices, for example your CDROM! Just click on it.
