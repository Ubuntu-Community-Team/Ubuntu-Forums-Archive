### Post by tiggsy on 2008-06-20
Here's the thing. When I loaded up Sound Recorder, on my way down the list, I saw another useful looking little thing, Recording Level Monitor. So I loaded it up.

When I started Sound Recorder just after booting, it was working UNTIL I STARTED RECORDING LEVEL MONITOR, when it failed just like before.

I closed Recording Level Monitor, and now it all works!!!

Hope this helps someone else.
