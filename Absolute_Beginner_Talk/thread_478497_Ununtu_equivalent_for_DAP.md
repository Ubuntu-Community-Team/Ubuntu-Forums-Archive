### Post by p_quarles on 2007-06-19
The "X" in "Downloader for X" refers to X11, which is the windowing system that underlies all Linux/Unix desktop environments. 

There's no difference between a download manager and a download accelerator. They both work by breaking up the download into sections, and d4x does this very well.

### Post by p_quarles on 2007-06-19
Essentially, yes. Nothing can increase your raw downstream bps except for getting a better broadband service. Download managers make things more efficient by breaking large downloads into sections and retrieving all these sections simultaneously. I promise you, DAP is not performing magic, it just makes things more efficient. And d4x does exactly the same thing.
