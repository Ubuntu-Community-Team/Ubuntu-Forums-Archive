### Post by kwaanens on 2007-03-20
iRiver E10 is an ogg-capable player that I got for my girlfriend, and which was so nice that I ordered one for myself too. Just drag and drop files, and then use [pmplib]("http://pmplib.sourceforge.net/") to build database.

I used to have an iAudio X5 60GB, which is now more or less bricked. (I used rockbox on it) 1 year, 3 months after purchase battery life is down to a little over 1 hour. R.i.d.i.c.u.l.o.u.s!
Moral is: if you get an iAudio X5, get the 30 gig "L" version which has waaaaaay more battery life.
I myself am waiting for huge flashdisks before I buy a new big player. Give that a year or two.
