Well, if you've had other problems with this install of Dapper, I guess a reinstall couldn't hurt and is the easiest way to fix it. I would just go download the alternate (or live) cd of Edgy (or if you want newer Feisty) and then install that. You shouldn't get any problems with it after that. The book doesn't talk about corruption of linux because its a theoretical analysis of a real thing, in real life Murphy's law is what applies, not the words in a book :p.

Yes, you do have to reinstall your old applications. Back up important data or move it all to home folder and reinstall. I'd recommend clean install rather than an upgrade, since your having trouble as it is.

You can get Edgy or Feisty [here.]("http://releases.ubuntu.com/") Edgy is no longer on the front page.
