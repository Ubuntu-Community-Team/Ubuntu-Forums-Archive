### Post by FM1 on 2008-01-20
Start Nautilus, go to Edit > Preferences. Click the Behavior tab and select "single click to open items".

If you mean not having to double-click to open something, then that'll do it, but if web-style mouse means something else, I'm not familiar with it.

Not to do what you asked. Otherwise, depends upon whether everything works properly or not. :)
