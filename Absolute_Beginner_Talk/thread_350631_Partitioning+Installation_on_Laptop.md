### Post by jimrz on 2007-01-31
> **Northsider said:**
> With Partition Magic do I select install another OS?

NO.  Just re-size your Win partion to create room for your Ubunto install ***Important*** defrag your win drive at least a couple of times (back to back is ok) BEFORE your attempt to resize. (If you do not already have Partition Magic, you can download the G-parted live cd which is free and at least as good as PM and will get the job done.) Then do your Ubuntu install as a separate operation.
