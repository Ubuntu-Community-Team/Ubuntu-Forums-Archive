### Post by reckless2k2 on 2007-05-04
I ran Ubuntu 5.04 through 6.06 on a T42 with MUCH success. All of the buttons worked fine including the ligth and such. There are a few sources online that talk about installing on the T42 and T43. 

As far as VRAM, any of them up to the T60s will have sufficient VRAM for desktop affects. 

I'd suggest that you make sure the wifi is the Atheros chipset though. Those worked out of the box for me on the older Ubuntu's. I know the intel wifi chipsets will not work so well on 6.10 and below. I couldn't comment on 7.04 but supposedly that intel chipset is supported in 7.04. Either way, Atheros is open source friendly and you are better off across most if not all distros using that chipset with wifi. Especially if you feel the need to learn to hack a little and have to inject and sniff at the same time.
