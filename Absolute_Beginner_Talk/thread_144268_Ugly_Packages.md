### Post by Sutekh on 2006-03-14
[http://gstreamer.freedesktop.org/documentation/]("http://gstreamer.freedesktop.org/documentation/")

They are the plugins required for playing mp3s.  The ugly comes around because they could present distribution problems as opposed to gstreamer0.10-plugins-good, which contain codecs licenced under the LGPL

Then there is gstreamer0.10-plugins-bad, these are not up to the quality of the -ugly and -good plugins.

[Ubuntu Packages - gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/dapper/libs/gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly")
