### Post by Hygelac on 2006-02-09
For general softmodem issues, [www.linmodems.org](www.linmodems.org) can be useful.  This site provides a utility called 'scanModem' that will tell you what chipset your modem uses, and what Linux drivers it needs.

Once you have that working you'll need to configure the connection.  Various ways of doing that are described here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DialupModemHowto](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DialupModemHowto).  I used 'pppconfig' myself.

Good luck! :D
