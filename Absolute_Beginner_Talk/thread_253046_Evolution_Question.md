### Post by TwoWordz on 2006-09-07
Hi Tom,

The easiest way I found to transfer emails to linux is to find a free imap provider (i used [www.bluebottle.com]("http://www.bluebottle.com")) and setup Outlook to use it. Transfer all your emails to this account then set it up in evolution. You can then download all you mail to your regular unix mbox format.

### Post by jediborger on 2006-09-07
Yes there is a very easy way, first open Outlook and export your contacts to a .csv format file. Then open Evolution go File-Import Then Forward, slect import a single file, Forward, then point it to the .csv file and under the drop down menu select Outlook CSV and hit Forward. You should now have all your contacts imported correctly.

Any more questions, post back.:)
