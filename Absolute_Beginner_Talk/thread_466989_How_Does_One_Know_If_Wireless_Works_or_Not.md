> So my question is: is there a way to verify that one's wireless is working (even if I can't connect due to the WPA issue)? 

The answer is yes. You should be able to see wireless networks in your neighborhood, regardless of which encryption they use (if they broadcast their SSID, that is). NetworkManager should show them, but there&#347; also WifiRadar
