### Post by Mike_Longbow on 2007-02-16
If you got your wireless device working properly, you can use [network manager]("http://www.gnome.org/projects/NetworkManager/") to conect to access points or routers.
To install it just do:
```
sudo aptitude install network-manager network-manager-gnome
```
Then you must log out from gnome and log in again, and you will have a little applet in your panel.
