### Post by ieee488 on 2007-09-14
I have a Toshiba Satellite 2065CDS. I added more RAM to max it out.

No internal ethernet so I had to buy a PCMCIA ethernet card. Neither of the 3Com NIC I bought would 
work with Windows 98 that came on the laptop even after downloading the 3Com drivers. The same 
cards worked in my Thinkpad 600.

I tried Ubuntu 5.04, but the PCMCIA was a problem again.

There's something about Toshiba's PCMCIA implementation.

I then tried the latest PuppyLinux and now the network works. I think you should give that a try.

I also tried Damn Small Linux, but it wouldn't work. I'm guessing the older kernel is the difference.
