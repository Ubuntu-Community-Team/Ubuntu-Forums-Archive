Go to [www.oreilly.com](www.oreilly.com) and buy everything they have, but especially *Running Linux *by Dalheimer and Welsh.  There is a pretty good (albeit very basic) book on Ubuntu, and it's installed on your system.  Go to System-->Help-->Ubuntu Book Excerpt ..
