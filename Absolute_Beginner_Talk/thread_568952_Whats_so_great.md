### Post by aysiu on 2007-10-06
> **mbt12 said:**
> I just cant figure out how to download and install any of the themes
Go to [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org) and look for GTK, Metacity, or icon themes. Download them. Drag them to the theme manager window (System > Preferences > Themes).

That's it.
