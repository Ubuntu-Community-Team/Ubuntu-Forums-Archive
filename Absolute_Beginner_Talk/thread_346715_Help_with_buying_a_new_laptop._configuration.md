### Post by cosborn72 on 2007-01-26
Under the main support catagory of the forums there is a subcatagory called Hardware and Laptops.  Check out the sticky topics at the top of the forum- they are not the easiest to read, but they can provide you with a lot of specific information about what works.  

Specifically, look at the Hardware Compatibility topic.


BTW, I run a HP dv-series laptop with a Intel 945GM graphics card and an intergrated intel PRO wireless 3905ABG card.  The wireless card works great out of the box. I had to install the 915 resolution packet from synaptic to get a decent screen resolution.  I have had some problems partitioning the SATA drive, but it definitely works...

i would recommend it.
