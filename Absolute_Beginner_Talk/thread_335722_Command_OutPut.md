### Post by meng on 2007-01-10
At the command line (open a Terminal or Console/Konsole), you enter the command.
Whatever the computer spits out at you is the output.
If you ask for help here, you may be asked to enter a command at the prompt, and then post the output, i.e. copy the computer's output to a post in this forum.
