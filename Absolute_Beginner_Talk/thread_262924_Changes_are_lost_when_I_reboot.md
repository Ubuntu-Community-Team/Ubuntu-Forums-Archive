### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-09-22
When logged in, go to your System menu > Preferences > Sessions and click the "Automatically save changes to session" checkbox.

As for your DNS settings - after entering your settings and getting back to the main Networking window (where it lists your interfaces), at the top is a drop-down menu marked "location".  Click that and choose to create a new location.  That will save your settings.  (Be sure to set up your connection before creating the location, or the settings won't save!)

Hope that helps.
