### Post by Leif on 2005-09-16
ok, don't get frustrated, it's just a new way of doing things that you need to get used to. with ubuntu, 99% of the time you don't need to search the web for programs to install, they're included in the distribution. 

click system->administration->synaptic, seach for gaim, then install it. after that, it should pop up under the applications->internet tab, or just do applications->run application and type gaim.

good sources for information are [www.ubuntuguide.org](www.ubuntuguide.org) (must read!) and [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UserDocumentation](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UserDocumentation). just ask and people will help !
