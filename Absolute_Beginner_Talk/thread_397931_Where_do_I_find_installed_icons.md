### Post by ComplexNumber on 2007-03-31
> **miggols99 said:**
> I am looking for the icon sets I have installed. Where are they? I want to see the individual files.
if you installed them via synaptic, they are in /usr/share/icons

if you installed via the gnome-theme-manager, they are in /home/<your-username>/.icons. files preceded by a dot are hidden files. to make hidden files and hidden directories visible, go into 'Edit' in naultius menu, the select 'preferences'. tick the box 'show hidden and backup files'.
