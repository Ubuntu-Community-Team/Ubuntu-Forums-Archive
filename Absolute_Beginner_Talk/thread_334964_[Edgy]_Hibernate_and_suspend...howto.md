### Post by brazzmonkey on 2007-01-09
hibernate and suspend are 2 different features :
suspend to disk and your computer will poweroff : you need to switch it on again (i.e. push the power button) aftwerwards.
suspend to ram and your computer will be in standby : in this case i believe any key you press will wake it up.

### Post by mlissner on 2007-01-15
To suspend to RAM, you have to turn on the suspend feature in the Power Management settings. 

Go to System > Preferences > Power Management, and turn on Suspend in one of the settings. I think it'll give you a crazy message about how the feature is experimental, and after that, I believe the option will appear in the Quit... menu. 

And for the record:
Suspend --> Saves to RAM, wakes up at touch of button.
Hibernate --> Saves to HD and shuts down. 

Also for the record, neither of these works worth a *&( on my system.
