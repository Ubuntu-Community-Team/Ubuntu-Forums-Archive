### Post by php_penguin on 2007-06-24
open up the "Applications" menu, and click on "Add/Remove Programs", 
now, type in "kopete" into the search box, and hit enter

select "kopete" in the results (should be no.1 of 2), and then hit "apply". tthis will install automatically, without any intervention

finally, go back into the applications menu and under "internet" will be Kopete.

If you want to make shortcuts, hold down Alt and drag the shortcut wherever and select "Link here".
