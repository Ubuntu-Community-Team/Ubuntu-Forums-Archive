---

### Post by 67GTA on 2007-03-11
Go to SYSTEM>PREFERENCES>REMOVABLE DRIVES AND MEDIA >MULTIMEDIA tab and put the command for mplayer in the spot for (Play video DVD discs when inserted). I believe the command for mplayer is gmplayer.
