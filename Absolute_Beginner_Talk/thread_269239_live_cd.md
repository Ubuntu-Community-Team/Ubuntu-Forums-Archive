### Post by KrayzeKaliber on 2006-10-01
To my knowledge, a live cd IS basically just to try it out. Let's say you may want to recommened it to someone...instead of them having to install an entire ubuntu OS, they can just test it out from the cd.

Usually you can also install ubuntu strait from the cd, which is great because if the person enjoys their experience on the live version so much, they may just want to download it on the spot.

I'm not too certain about the space though...since it's only on the cd, I assume that most of the programs aren't the full versions or something of that nature.:-k 

Live cd's help spread the vast greatness of Linux to everyone!:D
