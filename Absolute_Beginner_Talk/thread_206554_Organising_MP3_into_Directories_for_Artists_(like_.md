### Post by Jucato on 2006-06-30
You can get Amarok to arrange your music files by artist/album/year/etc. But the files must be included in Amarok's Collection, similar to iTunes Library.  Once they're in the Collection, you can arrange them anyway you want. Amarok gets it's data from the MP3 tags.
