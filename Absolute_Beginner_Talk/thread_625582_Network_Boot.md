### Post by mellowd on 2007-11-28
hmm, it sounds to me that your CD is not working correctly. My guess is that its trying to boot off the cd, then the network, then the harddrive.

It's not finding or reading the CD so goes off to the network. It find no bootable network config so it boots off your harddrive, therefore going into windows.
