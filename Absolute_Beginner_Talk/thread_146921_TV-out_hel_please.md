### Post by mlind on 2006-03-20
Hi there, I got exact same gfx card as you do, so I'll post my xorg.conf here for
reference. I use official Nvidia drivers.

There's few problems with the current nvidia drivers tho. If your primary monitor is
connected using DVI connector, all virtual terminal screens are displayed on TV,
not on your TFT monitor. There's a [thread]("http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?t=54638") about Nvidia Linux forums about the issue.
It looks to be a driver bug.
