### Post by Niniel on 2007-10-21
This is a common issue and most likely due to two things - a) the downloaded image is corrupt. Make sure to check the checksum after the download (look for a program called md5sum to help); b) the burning wasn't perfect and there are errors on the CD. Use a CD/R and don't burn at max. speed. I had success with 16x, but even then it took a few tries. Some people recommend going even slower, "as slow as possible".
