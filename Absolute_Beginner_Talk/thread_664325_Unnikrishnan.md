### Post by balaknair on 2008-01-11
Did you try just going to System menu>Administration>Printing on the system the printers are connected to and enable sharing?
Then use the client machines and select the printers and test printing.
Configuring it shouldn't be too difficult if you aren't too particular about setting custom permissions
I've had to do absolutely no configuration for either of my printers- just plug them in- Ubuntu(7.04 and  7.10) just detects them and they're ready out of the box)
Epson printers and some models of Canon have been  known to have issues however.
You might want to check out the hardware forums [http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=135](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=135) with your particular printer models if you still have trouble.
Be sure to post the printers make and model when you ask for help
