### Post by benuski on 2007-01-03
A lot of people are going to tell you Amarok, and the new version is a great program.  But, especially on Gnome, I find it a little clunky and memory intensive.  The two that I really like are Banshee, which is available from the repositories, and Exaile, which is available from its website.  Banshee gives me a lot of features I like... its like a well thought out version of Rythmbox.  Exaile is an Amarok clone for Gnome, and its still a little rough around the edges, but is a solid player.
