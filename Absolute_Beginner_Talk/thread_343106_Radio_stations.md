### Post by Sef on 2007-01-21
> How do I find additional stations and add them to Music Player?

I go to [Mikesradioworld.com]("http://mikesradioworld.com").  When I find a station, I like, I right click on download link.  Next I right click and if Rhythm box is not the default, I choose open with or open with other application.  Once I click on Rhythmbox, it is automatically added.
