### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-07-16
The .amule directory is hidden. Hit Ctrl+h to unhide it. You can change it from Preferences>Directories

---

### Post by manuleka on 2007-07-16
if its hidden... how can i see my download files? thats funny if its hidden by default

---

### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-07-16
Funny or not, hidden it is. You can easily unhide it permanently by renaming .amule to amule in both amule and your home folder. The files you download can be seen it amule's window.

---

### Post by Hallvor on 2007-07-16
You can also just change the download directory to something like /home/amuledownloads to make everything visible in your home directory.
