### Post by Dr Small on 2008-01-28
Accessing the BIOS Setup utility is different with every motherboard and BIOS.
There is no plausible way to determine how to access it without knowing what your motherboard is.

Your alternative would be to press F1, F2, DEL and different keys to see if you can access it. Maybe it says it on the screen, so pay close attention.

Dr Small
