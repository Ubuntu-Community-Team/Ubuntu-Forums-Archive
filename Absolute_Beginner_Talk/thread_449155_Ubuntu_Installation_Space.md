### Post by bobplano on 2007-05-19
after you partition your hard drive you go to a screen where you choose which partitions mount to where. the three most common partitions are /, swap and /home and you just choose the partitions that those will mount to, or where the files those carry, go.
10gigs is usually more than enough unless you are planning to install more than 10gigs of software
