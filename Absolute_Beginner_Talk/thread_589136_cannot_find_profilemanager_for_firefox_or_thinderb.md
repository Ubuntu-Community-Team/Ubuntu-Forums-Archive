### Post by alienexplorers on 2007-10-24
The -profilemanager is what is used to create your profiles.  It is not a directory or  file it self.  Your profiles are /home/.mozilla and /home/.mozilla-thunderbird. These two directories are normally hidden directories.  In these directories you will find your extensions and plugins and all other files needed to run firefox and thunderbird.
