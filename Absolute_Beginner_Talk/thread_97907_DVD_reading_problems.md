### Post by Gamabunta on 2005-12-01
You need something called libdvdcss2 to play DVD's in Ubuntu. It is illegal in the US due to the DMCA, but probably fine in Canada.

To get it, visit here: [http://wiki.ubuntu-fr.org/doc/plf](http://wiki.ubuntu-fr.org/doc/plf)

and follow the instructions. On that same site you can get codecs necessary to play Microsoft media files and a convenient Java package as well.

If playing DVD's is slow follow the instructions here:

[http://ubuntuguide.org/#speedupcddvdrom](http://ubuntuguide.org/#speedupcddvdrom)
