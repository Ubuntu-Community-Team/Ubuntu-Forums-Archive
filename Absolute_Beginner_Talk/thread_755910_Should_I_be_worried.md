### Post by tgm4883 on 2008-04-15
While that is pretty warm (it's about 50C), most processors will top out at around 70C(150F).  I don't like it when my system gets above 60C(140F) and will take preventive measures from letting it get this high.

My recommendation is to shut it down if it gets to 140 for any length of time.  You may also want to check and see if your processor is throttling back correctly.  Just for a comparison, my laptop (Dell Inspiron E1505) when doing normal office stuff sits between 40C and 50C.
