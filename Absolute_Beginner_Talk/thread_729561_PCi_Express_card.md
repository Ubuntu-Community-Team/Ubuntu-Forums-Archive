### Post by Moop on 2008-03-20
It's a pci-e slot and you either have one of those or a agp slot.  You can still buy agp video cards but the pci-e is newer. 

Install sysinfo from the repos if it's not installed. Run that and it will tell you what graphics card you have. 

Wikipedia has pictures of a pci-e x1 and a pci-e x16 slot.
