### Post by Chuck_W on 2006-04-10
There may be one problem not covered in other posts. I have a Dell Optiplex GX200 that does a pretty good job of running Ubuntu. Video is the only issue I can't get past. It has onboard video that is going bad. (This is a known issue with this model.) When I ran Windows I put a PCI video card and everything was ok. Ubuntu is different. It seems to ignore the PCI card and try to use the onboard circuit even when the monitor is connected to the card. If your BIOS allows you to actually turn off the onboard video you may be ok. Mine only allows for "auto" and "onboard". good luck.
