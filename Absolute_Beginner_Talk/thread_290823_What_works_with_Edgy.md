### Post by jpeddicord on 2006-11-01
Edgy wireless support is very bad at the moment, espically for Atheros (shows up as ath0 or similar) based cards. Connections drop, timeouts occur, and it seems very slow. I had to downgrade my system to Dapper, because I cannot live without wireless.

If it is an Intel-based card, then it should work fine, since the drivers are open-source.
Broadcam cards were reported to work somewhat, although I don't know much about that brand.

Jacob
