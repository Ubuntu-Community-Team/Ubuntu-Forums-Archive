### Post by atoponce on 2006-07-03
[quote=erik1397]just right-lick on the bottom panel and select "New Panel"[/quote] 
After creating the new panel, right-click and select properties.  From there, you can place the position (back at the top) and the size (among other things).  After that, right-click again, and add the application launcher, and everything else to get it right back to how it was before.
