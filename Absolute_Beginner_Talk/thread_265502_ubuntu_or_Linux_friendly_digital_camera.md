### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2006-09-26
Anything that supports PTP (Picture Transfer Protocol) is Linux friendly, but many proprietary protocols work too, see [http://www.gphoto.org/proj/libgphoto2/support.php](http://www.gphoto.org/proj/libgphoto2/support.php)

Canons Ixus-line is probably not the cheapest, but they are pretty good with regards to picture quality, user interface and size.
