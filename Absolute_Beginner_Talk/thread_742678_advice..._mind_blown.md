### Post by Tatty on 2008-04-01
These forums are probably the best place you can learn about Ubuntu.  Ask for help and read otehr articles, and over time you will begin to get it.

Also, at the top of the page go to Support->Documentation.  This will give you both the official ubuntu documentation (good for starting out) and the community supported wiki documentation which will give you most answers you will seek.

In terms of distribution-upgrades, they should generally upgrade withotu problems, but as you are altering an entire operating system (a big change to your computer) sometimes problems can occour.  The best way to upgrade without running into problems is to simply install it from scratch rather than updating.

Do you have any specific problems you need help with?
