### Post by CaptainInsaneO on 2007-09-17
Go to terminal from there and type this:

```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```

That will allow you to reconfig your xorg.conf and make your gui work.

As for your question about video cards, I don't know about ATI but I use an 8800GTX from evga and it works great! I use Envy (as noted above) to install the drivers and it's very hassle free.
