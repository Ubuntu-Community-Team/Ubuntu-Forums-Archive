### Post by KLineD on 2006-05-04
To configure your router you should read the manual for it. Most of the routers I've seen have a web based configuration so just point your browser to the ip address of the router.

Look for port forwarding or virtual server. With my particular router I have to set my nic with a fixed ip and then tell the router to forward bittorrent port to that IP.

Good luck with that.
