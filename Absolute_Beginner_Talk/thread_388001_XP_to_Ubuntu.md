### Post by Ozor Mox on 2007-03-19
It's taken me exactly two weeks to become comfortable with my new Ubuntu operating system. I made a full on switch from XP to Ubuntu by making a complete backup of my files, erasing XP entirely from my hard drive and installing Ubuntu. The important thing is, my two weeks would have been just a few days had my wireless card worked from the start. I thought about switching back to Windows twice. Once when I was enjoying little success with my wireless card, the second time when the entire operating system collapsed on me.
