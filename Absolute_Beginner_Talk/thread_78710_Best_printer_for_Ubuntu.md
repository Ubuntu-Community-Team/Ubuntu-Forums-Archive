### Post by Goober on 2005-10-18
HP Printers work very well.  My HP PSC1410 was automatically recognized by Hoary, and I mean it automatically detected the printer, and chose the right driver.  In Breezy, well, apparently Breezy didn't like the USB port that the printer was connected in, but once I got Breezy to recognize it, then it automatically detected it.  From what I have read, this is the same for other similar models of HPs.

HP printers are very good for Ubuntu.  There is an article in the Wiki 'somewhere'.

[Wiki Article](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HpPscHpPhotosmartSeriesAllInOnePrinters?highlight=%28Printers%29%7C%28HP%29)
