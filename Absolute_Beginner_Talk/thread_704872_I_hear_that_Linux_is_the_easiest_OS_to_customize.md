### Post by PeterJS on 2008-02-22
The most of the element to customize the look of ubuntu can be found on gnome-look.org. The splash screen during the boot up is called Usplash, the login screen is called GDM, window widgets are controlled by GTK, and the borders around windows are controlled by Emerald (if you're using compiz), or Metacity (if you're not).
