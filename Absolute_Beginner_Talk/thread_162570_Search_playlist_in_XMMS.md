### Post by aktiwers on 2006-10-23
Heh I just found out. Maybe a little late but here is how to use it.

After doing the 
sudo apt-get install xmms-find

Go to "Options" => "Preferences" =>  Pick the "General Plugins"  => Pick " XmmsFind 0.5.2" and hit the small Enable Butten. Hit Apply and Ok.

Now when you press "J" you are able to search!
