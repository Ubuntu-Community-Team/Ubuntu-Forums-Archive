### Post by e_james on 2007-07-23
It has taken me months to figure out ways to find helpful information about linux. The ubuntu wiki probably have most of the information you need, but how to find it. I just discovered that the keyword "howto" is a good starting point. If you use more specific details as keywords you tend to get hundreds of irrelevant answers. Starting with "howto" I found this page

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs)

which may have all you need. After you have a look if you come back to this thread with more details of what you tried and the results, I expect that there are many people willing to provide any further help you need.

I have deliberately written this reply in a general manner for the benefit of other people who may find this thread when they are searching the forum for help for their own problems.
