Evolution has more features by default and is closer to "Outlook" although its not the same.  I think the closest in terms of functionality to Outlook is Kontact (the KDE PIM).  Thunderbird is like what outlook express should be... stable and safe.  It can use extensions like Firefox to get more functionality, but isn't quite as fully featured as Evolution or Kontact.  It can also team up with Sunbird to give you calendering functions.  Many people find evolution really buggy, so I would only go that route if you need its features.
