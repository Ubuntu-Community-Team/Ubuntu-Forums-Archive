### Post by mbs348 on 2007-04-12
So since I just broke Feisty, (it was bound to happen) I will just wait to reinstall after it is released. Can I reinstall from the beta live disk I already have, and then just update to the released version, or do I need to make a new live cd?

also, if i used a home partition, I simply just point to it as my home partition during reinstall to insure it does not overwrite my files?

---

### Post by machoo02 on 2007-04-12
Yes, you can just use the beta disk, and upon first reboot all of the software update will bring you to the released verion.  And yes, you can just point the installer to your existing home partition.  Just be sure to uncheck the "format" box and your files will still be intact.
