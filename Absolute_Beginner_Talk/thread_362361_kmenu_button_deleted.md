### Post by MartinJ on 2007-02-15
Ok, on the taskbar: right click and select Add Applet to Panel.  (You may have to unlock the taskbar first, but you'll be given the option if it is locked).

In the Add Applet window select 'K Menu', about 1/3 of the way down.  Then hit Add to Panel.  The K menu should now be back, but in the wrong position.  You will have to right click on the new K menu and select Move to rearrange it.

Hope that helps...
