### Post by Algus on 2008-02-07
Once you get a LiveCD, either by buying it, requesting a free copy, or downloading the ISO and burning it to a blank CD, you can stick it in your machine, reboot, and then run the OS straight from the disc.  This is a great way to see how Ubuntu acts with your hardware before you commit to installing the OS.

You'll find [this]("http://apcmag.com/node/5162/") to be a useful read if you want to keep your Windows install
