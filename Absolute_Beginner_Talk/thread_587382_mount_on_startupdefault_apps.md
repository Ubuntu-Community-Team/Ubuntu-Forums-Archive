### Post by Dr Small on 2007-10-22
I do not know how to automount a partition (never fooled with any of that, but I do believe it involves fstab), but to set VLC as the default player for avi file types, Right click on one of your AVI files, Select Properties, Open With, and choose what you want it to open with.

This will change it for every file of the same filetype.
