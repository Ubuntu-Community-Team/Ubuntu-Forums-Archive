### Post by imhdd on 2006-08-08
In the back of your power supply there is a fan. If your power supply is working this fan will operate. If the fan is not operating it can be because the power supply is dead OR one of the circuits to some hardware is shorted, keeping the power supply from coming on. 

If you are mechanically/electronically handy, there is a simple test that you can do to test the power supply. It's outlined on one of my Web pages. But be aware, you can totally destroy your power supply/motherboard if you make a mistake. I don't recommend trying this if you don't feel competent.

[http://www.imhdd.ms11.net/COMPUTER/dead_computer.html]("http://www.imhdd.ms11.net/COMPUTER/dead_computer.html")
