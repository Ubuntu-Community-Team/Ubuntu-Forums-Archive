### Post by Dragonfire on 2006-06-08
This website is currently under devolpment, and is aimed to helping with problems, questions, and open source conversation.

[http://www.linuxconversionteam.tk/](http://www.linuxconversionteam.tk/)

We are currently scripting Version 2 of the site. The one you see, is the current Version 1 site, and is only meant as a place holder.

We are looking for some HTML scripting guru's, and some troubleshooting professionals to come and help us with our venture.

We are also programming and designing new features, applications, and plugins for Linux distributions.

Our current goals are to help in the conversion, and post-conversion stages of windows switchovers. With this, we hope to expand the Open Source community, and spread the joys of free, and fully customizable OS's, Apps, programs, and plugins.


We at the Linux Conversion Team hope that you will Join us in this venture, and help bring our forums to life like these very forums!
