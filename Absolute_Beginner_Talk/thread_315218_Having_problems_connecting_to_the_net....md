That's what I did too. Found an external Zoom 56k modem on ebay for a dollar (and the serial cable for another dollar or two at my local St. Vincent De Paul) and that's my Ubuntu internet connection. I didn't even try messing with my winmodem. I would also recommend installing the Gnome PPP package from Synaptic or Automatix to simplify the process (of getting online.)

With respect to PeoplePC, I think you will need to have them give you a phone number to dial into and just configure your connection manually with either the package I mentioned above or through the "Networking" entry under "System" and "Administration." If they won't do this, you will probably have to find an ISP who will.
