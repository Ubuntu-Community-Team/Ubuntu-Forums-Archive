### Post by scottro on 2008-03-04
Many times, if you can't see a page correctly, it's because the encoding is wrong.  Usually firefox (and opera, and yes, even IE) do a good job of automatically detecting the language and giving the correct font, but not always.  

Go to View=>Character encoding and see if playing with those settings will help you view the page.
