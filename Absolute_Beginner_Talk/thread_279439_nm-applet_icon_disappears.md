### Post by analogdevices on 2006-10-18
I can't get the nm-applet wireless network controller to stay in my task bar. A day ago my friend clicked 'remove from panel' and now all I can do is run it through the terminal, but when I close the terminal window I lose the icon. How can I get it to stay?

---

### Post by mcduck on 2006-10-18
Go to System/Prewferences/Session and on the 'Startup Programs'-tab click 'Add' and add command 'nm-applet --sm-disable'. Next time you log in the applet should start.
