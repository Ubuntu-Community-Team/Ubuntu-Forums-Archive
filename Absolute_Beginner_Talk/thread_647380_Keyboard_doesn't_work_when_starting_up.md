### Post by mellowd on 2007-12-22
The bios is the code that allows your software to speak to your hardware. It's located on your motherboard.

You need to get into it when your pc first gets switched on, before the OS loads, before the post has finished. Most of the time you either need to press DEL or F2 or F10, it depends. When you first switch your PC on you should see a message saying press del to enter setup or something similar. That's when you need to press it.
