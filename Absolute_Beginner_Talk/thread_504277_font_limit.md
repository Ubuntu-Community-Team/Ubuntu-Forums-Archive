### Post by BirdZerk on 2007-07-25
My mother owns an embroidery company and she uses a lot of fonts, some where in the neighborhood of 6000+.  The OS is not the problem, it is the programs that use fonts and preloads them.  The best thing to do is have a folder with all the fonts in them and then move the ones you want into  the .fonts folder.  This has worked well for my mother and fewer headaches for me.  Remember the more fonts that a program loads in the slower everything will run.
