### Post by laptoplinux on 2008-01-25

But which pdf reader works best often varies from document to document.  Why is there such a strange and inconsistent variation of display quality among these readers, and what can be done to improve the font quality?

### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2008-01-25
I think that they all use different rendering engines, hence the variations.  When in doubt, Adobe's rendering is the correct one.  (by definition ;))  If it doesn't look right in Acrobat, (on both Linux and Windows), then it is the PDF creator's fault.  :D
