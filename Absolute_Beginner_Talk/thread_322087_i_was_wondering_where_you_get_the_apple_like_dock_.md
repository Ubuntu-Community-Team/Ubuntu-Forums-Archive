### Post by d3v1ant_0n3 on 2006-12-20
There are quite a few docks available for Ubuntu- some are easier to setup, some are prettier, etc.

These ones are available in the repositories:

Kxdocker
Kooldock
Starter Bar (included in the gdesklets package)

Of those, Starterbar is probably the easiest to set up and use.

There's also KibaDock, which requires Beryl (or Compiz), and does lots of utterly useless but cool effects like bouncing (REALLY bouncing) icons, dragable icons etc.

Cairo-Dock is (IMO) worth a look at, but not really suitable for fulltime use as a dock at the moment- it's still very much work in progress from what I've seen.
