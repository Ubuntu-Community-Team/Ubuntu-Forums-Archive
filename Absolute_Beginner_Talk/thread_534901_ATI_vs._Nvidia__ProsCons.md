### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-08-27
I have a ATI Radeon 9600 256 MB graphics card in one of my computers, and a nVidia G-Force TI4200SE 128 MB in the other. I use the one with the nVidia card for my every day work and find it very pleasant to work with. My ATI card has too many issues and are hard to set up and awkward to work with. To enable Compiz and Beryl I need to disable the restricted driver, to play a game like Open Arena I need to enable the driver once more. 

I have a friend who has a Radeon mobile and he has the exact same issues with his card. This is in both 7.04 Feisty and 7.10 Gutsy. I keep going back to my old nVidia card simply because it's easier to install and it runs the graphics just perfect. I'm no big gamer, just like my system to work without too much workaround.
