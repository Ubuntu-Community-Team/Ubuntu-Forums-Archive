### Post by joshrobinson on 2006-04-14
[QUOTE=yozef]:confused: I'm not sure I just want to back up the whole system on CD's not sure what that prompt is about?[/QUOTE]

you can only back up data, such as music, movies, pictures, word documents, and install files

that error msg looks like its for a program that you install to automatically backup, or something along the lines of a one button backup system

just drag and drop your files onto your hd or burn cd's as someone said above

if you were to try to backup your whole c:\ it would be useless.. your better off just having everything you want to reinstall if something were to go wrong

you have to actually install windows, not just copy it to a cd/dvd and flop it on your HD 
due to your MBR and booting, and system files in general.. and your registry might not copy

so just backup your files like i listed above, pictures, music, word documents, installers etc
