### Post by zirtik on 2008-02-13
Hi, 

I have been looking for a download manager that is capable of downloading files from Rapidshare servers with a premium account, just like Flashget does under Windows. I have found out that aria does the job.

sudo apt-get install aria

Install flashgot extension for firefox and configure it to work with aria from the options menu. 

Launch aria and go to the options->Authentication menu and enter your Rapidshare username and password and save.

Enjoy!

### Post by Che.SSL on 2008-03-05
There is another simple solution if you use Firefox:
1st, login to your Rapidshare account, choose "options" and enable direct downloads.
Then, optionally install an add-on for Firefox - "Download Manager Tweak". With Download Manager Tweak you can put download links in a queue and determine how many downloads should be run simultaniously.

That's it! Start downloading.

NOTE: here's the important thing: when you login to your RS account, Firefox stores a cookie with your account data. As long as that cookie is on your harddrive you can download from RS as usual. Now it's your choice whether to set up Firefox to keep cookies on exits, or just to login to RS before downloading.
