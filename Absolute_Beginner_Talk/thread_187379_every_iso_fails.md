### Post by BoneKracker on 2006-06-03
The CD's can take weeks to arrive.

If the ISO passed checksum, but the CD fails its integrity check, then I would say the problem is in your burning of the CD.

The best advice I can give is to do what has worked before.  You should use a combination of cdwriter, medium and speed (and other settings) that you've successfully used to burn an ISO before.

Other than that:

Check your other settings.
Try an even slower speed.
Try a different medium (especially if you're using cdrw - they do wear out)
Make sure your firmware for your cdwriter is up-to-date.
Try a different cdwriter.

(Maybe you can get a friend to burn it for you on their machine?)
