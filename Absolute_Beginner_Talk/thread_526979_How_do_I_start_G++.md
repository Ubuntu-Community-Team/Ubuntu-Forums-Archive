### Post by petit.padavoine on 2007-08-16
"man" is your ticket to understanding (more or less) every command in Linux.

In a terminal,
```
man <mycommand>
```,
in your case
{code]man g++[/code]
Will give you lots of info about g++.

P.S. To get out of a man page, press "q".
