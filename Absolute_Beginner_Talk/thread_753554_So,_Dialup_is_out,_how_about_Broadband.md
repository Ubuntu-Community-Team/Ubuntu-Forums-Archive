### Post by arsenic23 on 2008-04-12
If we're talking about dsl or similar, then you may have one and only one problem, which should be easy to get around.

Some ISPs only give instructions for setting up their modems using a windows only CD.  You'll just need to look up how to setup your modem manually.  Or your ISP may not have this problem.  After that you should just be able to plug in your ethernet cable and go, you won't have to do a thing in Ubuntu.
