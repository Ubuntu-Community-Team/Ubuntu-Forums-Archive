### Post by Pumalite on 2007-10-11
You should probably use Gparted:[http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php) to erase the Ubuntu partition. Then restore Vista MBR with your Installation CD. Then re-install Ubuntu in your 2nd drive.
