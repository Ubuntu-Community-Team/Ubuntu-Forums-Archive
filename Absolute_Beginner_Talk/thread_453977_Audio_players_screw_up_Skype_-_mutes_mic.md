### Post by Cloudedbrains on 2007-05-25
On one the taskbars you will see a speaker symbol
right click it and open the volume control
if the mic is listed with a red cross underneath it
click the red cross and that unmutes it

if mic isnt listed go to preferences and tick mic and do as above
