### Post by illu45 on 2006-08-28
Hello all,

I've recently installed two games on my Ubuntu PC (NeverWinter Nights and Enemy Territory) and I noticed that I could not get any sound for them. However, this morning, I tried switching my speakers from my main sound card (Creative Audigy 2 ZS) to the inegrated one, and voila! It works! The only trouble is, I would really rather use the Audigy Card, as the integrated card lacks quality (or a microphone port). 

Most of the sound (media files, startup sound etc.) is played by the Audigy card, it seems only to be games that use the integrated card. 

Any advice would be greatly appretiated,
illu45

### Post by davebgimp on 2006-08-28
Had the same problem. Since I never used the integrated sound ever, I found disabling it in the system BIOS fixed it for good. Now, (K)Ubuntu just sees one sound device (my soundblaster card) and uses it for everything with no issues.
