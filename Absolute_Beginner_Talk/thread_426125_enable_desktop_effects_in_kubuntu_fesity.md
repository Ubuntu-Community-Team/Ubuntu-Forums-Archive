### Post by adelie on 2007-04-28
What sort of effects are you looking for? Are you looking for Beryl-type 3d effects or the basic KDE menu effects?

There are some great beryl how-to's out there, like this one:
[http://wiki.beryl-project.org/index.php/Install/Ubuntu]("http://wiki.beryl-project.org/index.php/Install/Ubuntu")

Other than that, you can look in the System Settings, under 'Appearance", you can find some effects under the different categories like style, and also under "window decorations" you can find some different effects. 

Hope that helps.
