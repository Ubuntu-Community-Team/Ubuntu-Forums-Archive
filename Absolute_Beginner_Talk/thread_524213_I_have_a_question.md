### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-08-12
If you mean the Ubuntu logo, just be sure to follow the trademark guidelines:
[http://www.ubuntu.com/aboutus/trademarkpolicy](http://www.ubuntu.com/aboutus/trademarkpolicy)
Similar terms may apply for other OSS logos, you'll have to check up on those.

Basically, if you are worried about trademarks, just keep the logo as-is on your site. You won't run into any problems.
