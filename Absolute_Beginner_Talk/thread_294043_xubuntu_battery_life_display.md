### Post by gn2 on 2006-11-06
> **neo_weapon said:**
> Hi all,

 I just did a fresh install of xubuntu on my portege 7020ct and loving it. I was wondering how I can check my battery status when I'm using my laptop and how to change its brightness. Thanks.

Right click on the top panel, "add new item" and battery monitor should be in there.

There are screen gamma sliders in Applications>Settings>Display Settings which helped me get a better looking screen, brightness slider is in Applications>Settings>Desktop Settings

Hope that works :)
