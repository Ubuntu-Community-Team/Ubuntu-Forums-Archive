### Post by tashmooclam on 2007-08-07
This isn't a question, but for an absolute beginner maybe a tip.
Right click in the open area of the "menu bar". 
Properties>general>show hide buttons.
There will be some little buttons you can click to make the menu bar slide over to the right, giving you another 1/4"+ of space. 
Of course you can play with the "autohide" feature too.
