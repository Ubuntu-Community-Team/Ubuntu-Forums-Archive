### Post by Sef on 2007-01-23
Three questions:

1) Did you [md5sum]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM") the download?  If not, it may be corrupt.

2) Did you burn the iso at 4x or less?  If not, it may have been corrupted during the burn.

3) Did you burn the iso as an iso and not a file?  No cost [iso burner]("http://isorecorder.alexfeinman.com/isorecorder.htm").
