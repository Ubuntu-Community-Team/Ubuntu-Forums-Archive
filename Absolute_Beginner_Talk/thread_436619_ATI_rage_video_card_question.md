### Post by jiminycricket on 2007-05-08
ATI problems are only with cards that aren't supported by the open source drivers, a Radeon X850 and up.  I would buy computers with Intel or nVidia in the future.

Anyways, your card is old and doesn't seem to have 3d support by either (no beryl, no 3d games)  Yet normal operations will be fine.  [http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Ati_open_source_Driver](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Ati_open_source_Driver)
