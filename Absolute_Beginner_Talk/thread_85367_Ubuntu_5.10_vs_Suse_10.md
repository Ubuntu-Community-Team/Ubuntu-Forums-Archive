### Post by 23meg on 2005-11-02
They're both good, but have some essential differences. Suse is rpm-based whereas Ubuntu prefers apt. Suse comes with lots of software choices (5 CDs) whereas Ubuntu ships with its selected defaults but has huge repositories.  [The Linux Distribution Chooser]("http://www.zegeniestudios.net/ldc/") may help you decide which is more suited for you.
