### Post by kebes on 2007-01-25
You can use the "Quick Links > My Profile" and then see what threads you've started and what posts you've made.

An even better solution, however, is when you find a thread you like, you use "Thread Tools > Subscribe to Thread". Then you can go "Quick Links > Subscribed Threads" to see your subscriptions (threads with new posts are shown in bold at top).

There is also an option in the user preferences so that you automatically subscribe to threads that you post in. That's very convenient because it means you can easily keep track of discussions you are involved in. To activate this, go "Quick Links > Edit Options" and set the "Default Thread Subscription Mode" to whatever you want. ("No email" means you subscribe automatically but don't receive an email notification, etc.)


I hope that helps.
