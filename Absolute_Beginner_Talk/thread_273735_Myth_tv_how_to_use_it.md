### Post by tagra123 on 2006-10-08
the mythtv command is only one of several parts of mythtv.

You can view recorded programs by typing mythtv filename.nuv or watch live tv by typing mythtv.

The backend needs to be setup, and yes you will need a tvcard on the computer with the backend.

Once the backend is setup you can watch tv and recording on other computers connected to the backend.

google for -- mythtv ubuntu  -- you'll find all kinds of good info.
