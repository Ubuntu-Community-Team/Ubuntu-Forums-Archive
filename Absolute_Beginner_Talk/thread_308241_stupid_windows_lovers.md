You'll want to share folders on your network. You have two options that are easy to use: FTP or smb, the windows sharing protocol. Both are already installed on Ubuntu by default. I'd recommend smb.  Just create a directory where the documents will be stored. Open System/admin/shared folders and then follow the GUI.

BTW, conversion comes best with friendly experience.
