### Post by papatrpt89 on 2007-12-09
Hey man.  I love openarena!  Its a fun little FPS :)

Anyways, I think I can help.

./openarena/baseoa refers to a hidden path within your home directory.  The full path to that folder would be /home/YOURUSERNAME/.openarena/baseoa

Open up your home directory in nautilus, open the view menu, and select show hidden files.  Alternatively, you press Control + h when inside of nautilus for the same effect.

You will notice that there are many folders that start with a . (this hides them from view).  They are typically created automatically and store user-related settings or content.

So, once you see all of the hidden files, there should be one called ./openarena, with a folder called baseoa inside of it.
