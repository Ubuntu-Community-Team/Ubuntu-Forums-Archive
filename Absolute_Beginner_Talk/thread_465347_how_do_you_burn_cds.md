### Post by viciouslime on 2007-06-05
Not quite all, are you trying to burn an iso, files, or music? If you want to burn an iso, right click it and click burn to disc. SImple. If you want to burn files, you can put the blank cd in and you will be given the option to burn a cd. Upon doing so a blank window will open, drag your files here, then press burn. To burn an audio CD you'll need to use Serpentine Applications/Sound/Serpentine or for something more powerful, try brasero or gnomebaker. The last two will let you burn all sorts of discs with a whole host of advanced options :)

### Post by Znupi on 2007-06-05
Just insert a blank disk and click "Make Data CD" in the dialog that pops up.
Also, you can go to **Places -> CD/DVD Creator** or **Places -> Blank CD-R Disc**. :)

After that, just drag and drop files and folders to the disk and click **Write to Disc**.

Edit: of course, it has some limitations. For one, you can't leave a session open.
