### Post by phread59 on 2008-04-07
70 Plymouth, I was just in this situation.  I had a thread going "physicly cannot install ubuntu".  Solution was that you burnt the image you downloaded as a data file.  You have to burn it as an image.  Go to [www.psycocats.net/ubuntu/iso](www.psycocats.net/ubuntu/iso), there you will find the instructions and a list of software to use.  My copy failed the checksum but I burned it anyway.  Worked just fine.  Look at psycocat's site there is a ton of good info in there.  I hope you can sort out your problem.
