I tried using WINISO but again the same  problem nothing to copy/burn or other errors. Can someone please give me detailed instructions on how to copy/burn ISO files. Thanks in advance.

Start Nero StartSmart

Select Copy and Backup

Click Burn Image to Disk

Find the downloaded .iso file, select it and click open

Then you need to click next a few times and the burning process should start.


If it still don't work, maybe you downloaded a corrupt image, or its blank?
Try mounting it on [Daemon Tools](http://www.daemon-tools.cc/dtcc/announcements.php) and see if its fine running under a virtual CD/DVD Drive.

### Post by taurus on 2006-08-30
Fire up Nero, Nero Burning ROM, scroll to the bottom and highlight "CD-ROM (UDF/ISO).  Click New and browser to where your iso image file is and drag it over to the left window.  

Or you can click Cancel at the opening screen and click Recoder -> Burn Image and scroll to where your iso image is!!!
