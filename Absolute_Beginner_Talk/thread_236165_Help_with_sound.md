### Post by rck_hitokiri on 2006-08-14
You might have your PCM in ALSAMIXER way to high for your speakers. Try to run alsamixer on terminal then lower your PCM just to be sure. If that doesnt change the problem try to post your soundcard stats over here and we'll see whats up. 

Apps > Accessories > terminal.

code:Alsamixer.

cheers.
