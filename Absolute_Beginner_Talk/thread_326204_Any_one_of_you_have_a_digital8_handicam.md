### Post by ieee488 on 2006-12-27
With transfering video, you need to know about your camcorder and you need
to know about your OS. It isn't enough to worry only about the PC side.

Does this camcorder have a Firewire port?
This is the preferred way to get video to a PC.

If the camcorder doesn't have Firewire, you will need to buy a TV tuner card with RCA composite inputs.
