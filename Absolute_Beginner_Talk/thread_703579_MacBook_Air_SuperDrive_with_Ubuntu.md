### Post by metallicamaster3 on 2008-02-21
The MacBook Air has an external USB SuperDrive that is available from Apple for $99. I know, I own one.

All worked well for me when I installed Ubuntu to a 10GB Partition to the 80GB PATA. (7.10 Gutsy Gibbon)

It booted from the disk from the external SuperDrive just fine, and after a little tweaking with the settings for some trackpad functionality, Ubuntu was just fine on it.
