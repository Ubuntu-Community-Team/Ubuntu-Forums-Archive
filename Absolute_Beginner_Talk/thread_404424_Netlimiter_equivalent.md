### Post by sammy23 on 2007-05-03
There are plenty of options out there, but they all range in cost and effectiveness. If you're looking to shape traffic with just a few users, one of the cheaper (or free) solutions may work. But, if you're looking to shape for an entire network, you'll probably have to pay for something that will really work. I've used netequalizer and it did great for 100-200 users. Those start at around 2K. On the high end, a packeteer can cost 10s of thousands of dollars.
