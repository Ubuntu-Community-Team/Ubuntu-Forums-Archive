### Post by Scunizi on 2007-10-04
If you don't want to see programs in the menu's that you're not going to use, go to Applications/Accessories/Alacarte Menu Editor and remove the check mark next to the programs you want to hide.

The problem with deleting some of the "stock" programs is they are integrated into the system.. for instance Evolution.  I use it but it's not my favorite program.  The calendar is integrated into the time/date in the top right corner.  Just try clicking on the time/date after inputing a scheduled activity in the calendar for today and see how it's displayed..

You could probably get away with removing Rhythmbox.
