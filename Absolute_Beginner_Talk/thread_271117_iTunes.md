### Post by Ambimom on 2006-10-04
From your Ipod? Assuming you installed your Ipod to Windows...

First, download Yamipod ([www.yamipod.com)](www.yamipod.com)). Important: use the Windows version of Yamipod.  [There's a Linux version too, but you can't use it on a Windows' installed Ipod because I tried and my Ipod forced me to restore it and I lost everything.]

In Windows, connect your Ipod cable but don't open Itunes, open Yamipod.

From Yamipod, export your music to your hard-drive, or external drive.  IT EXPORTS IT ALL, including the purhased tunes.

I burned the exported music to a DVD as data. (A handy backup of the Ipod with everything intact and believe me that comes in very handy).

Then when you boot into Ubuntu. From those backup disks, you can either copy your music onto your Linux partition drive, or listen from the disks.
