### Post by skymt on 2006-08-30
Most things don't come with menu entries. It's just something someone has to do, and most of the time, nobody does. Many Ubuntu packages have menu entries, but they're usually made by the packagers.

It's partly the assumption that anyone who can compile software can find and run it. It's also the fact that many programmers program for themselves, and they don't personally need a GNOME menu entry in the source package.
