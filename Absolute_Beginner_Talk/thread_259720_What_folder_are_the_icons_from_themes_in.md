### Post by crossedout on 2006-09-18
The icons from installed icon themes can be found in home/.icons/<themename>, ctrl+h to show the hidden folders.

For gdesklets icons, just right-click on the launcher and choose 'Edit Starter'.  Click the icon image and navigate to the folder that has the replacement icon, click 'Open' in the bottom corner then choose your icon.
