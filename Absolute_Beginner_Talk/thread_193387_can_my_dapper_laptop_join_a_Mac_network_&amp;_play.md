### Post by LKRaider on 2006-06-10
I don't know how mac's share files, but you could try going in Network Servers (under the 'Places' menu, or in a Nautilus window) and see if the macs show up.

About sharing music, try installing the Banshee player. It seems to have a DAAP sharing plugin that works with iTunes and the like. ( [Banshee Review]("http://www.desktoplinux.com/articles/AT7150747782.html") ). Just do a search on Synaptic for 'banshee'.
