### Post by Austin_KW on 2007-08-26
The different OS's have nothing to do with it. The problem is on the XP setup and its connection to the router. I dont have this combination so I am not sure how but you probably want to manually configure the wireless on XP. This usually entails using the wireless manager, selecting your network and entering your password/key. You should be able to find/change the key by connecting to the routers web interface.
