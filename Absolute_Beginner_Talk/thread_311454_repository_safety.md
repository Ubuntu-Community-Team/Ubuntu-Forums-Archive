### Post by CatKiller on 2006-12-03
You might find [this thread]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=297814") highlights the issues involved quite well.

There's no real reason why you would need to keep a repository selected once you've got the software you want from it. You could comment it out from your sources.list or untick the box in Synaptic, until you know from other sources that there's an update you want.
