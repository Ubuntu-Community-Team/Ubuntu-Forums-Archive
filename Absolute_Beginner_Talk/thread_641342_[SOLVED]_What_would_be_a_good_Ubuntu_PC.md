### Post by bobpur on 2007-12-22
AMD 64 x2 5600+ socket AM2 CPU, Asus M2NPV-VM Micro ATX mobo, XFX 7600 GT (Nvidia) video card, Creative Labs Audigy 4 soundcard, 1 gb Kingston RAM, 2 500 gb WD SATA hard drives, and an Apevia cube case.

This little machine was built by me for a lot less than you'd think. The case was used (A display) as was the sound card. I don't, usually, advocate used parts in a machine unless you know where they come from. Also, deals can be had if you keep a miserly eye open for them. The video card (7600 GT) is at the end of its run so the price is down from what it was and it's still a good card. [www.newegg.com](www.newegg.com) is a good source of discounted parts. Don't worry about going old tech. Linux loves old tech.
This machine is really light as the case is aluminum and plastic. A fact you might appreciate if you have to lug it around much. It's got a fold-away handle that shouldn't be trusted.

Good Luck.
