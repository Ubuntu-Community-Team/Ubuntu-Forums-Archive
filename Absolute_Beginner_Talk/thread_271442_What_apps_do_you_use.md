### Post by ewl1217 on 2006-10-04
**Web Browsing - Konqueror**
I use Konqueror here because of the brilliant KDE integration. If you use GNOME, Ubuntu's default desktop environment, you're probably better off with Firefox or Epiphany (the GNOME web browser). You might also want to give Opera a look, even though it is closed source.

**Music - Amarok**
Amarok is my choice here. It's much more advanced than Rhythmbox and handles larger music collections better, at least in my experience.

**Video - Kaffeine**
I only use Kaffeine because it's the default with Kubuntu and KDE. I don't watch much video, and Kaffeine's never given me any trouble, so there's no reason for me to switch.

If you couldn't tell, I'm fairly biased towards KDE apps. As a KDE user, that's kind of expected. I recommend you just try out a bunch of programs and then see what works for you. That's one great thing about Ubuntu (and other Linux distributions): the amazing selection of programs.
