### Post by artcancro on 2007-11-16
Zimbra, Scalix, and OpenXchange are all proprietary applications that have a crippled open source version.  If you are looking for a truly free (as in, the entire system is GPL) then check out [Citadel.]("http://www.citadel.org")  It is probably the "open source Exchange killer" you have been looking for.  Go to [http://www.citadel.org](http://www.citadel.org) to check it out.  It's got email, calendars, address books, instant messaging, a gorgeous ajax-based web client, and there's even an Outlook connector currently being developed.  Don't waste your money on Zimbra or Scalix when you can have the full version of Citadel for free.
