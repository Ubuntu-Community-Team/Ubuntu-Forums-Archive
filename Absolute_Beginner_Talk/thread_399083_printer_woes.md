### Post by mathewb on 2007-04-01
Have you tried sharing it through the printer manager? To do that, go to the System menu at the top of the screen, open Administration, and click on Printing. When the Printer Manager opens, click on the Global Settings menu and select Share Printers. It will display a warning about potential security risks, just click ok.
