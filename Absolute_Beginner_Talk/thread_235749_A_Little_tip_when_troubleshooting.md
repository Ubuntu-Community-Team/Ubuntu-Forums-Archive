### Post by Stranger678 on 2006-08-13
Let's just say for the sake of argument that you are running Ubuntu and you are a real rookie to the linux world, Here's a little tip for you. If your internet browser suddenly stops working, rather then say.....reconfiguring EVERYTHING related to networking and then breaking it even worse and eventually having to reformat and reinstall the entire OS just to get it back to it's defaults.

Walk over to your router and make sure that it's still plugged in. 

Just a friendly tip.
