### Post by oliviacond on 2006-11-28
How to disable a feature that always display song information on the panel when the next song start playing?

---

### Post by inabsolutes on 2006-11-28
Right-click on the Rhythmbox icon (the musical note) in your notification area and uncheck "Show Notifications."
