### Post by Onay on 2007-10-24
My recommendation is this...

Make another ext3 partition for 7.10, install it there, then boot between the two until you get your desired settings. It is the safest way. Besides, using the official upgrade method has a higher change of ruining your install, rendering it impossible to be able to mimic your settings on another settings.

You'll be taking a gamble if you use the official upgrade method, but it if works out 100% (so far I haven't heard of one doing so) then feel free. It's just that if you have wireless, programs, tweaked settings, compiz/beryl, etc. working, then something will probably go wrong. It only took me about 2 or 3 days to "copy" my setup onto Gutsy, though I downloaded and installed only Gutsy 7.10 approved programs.
