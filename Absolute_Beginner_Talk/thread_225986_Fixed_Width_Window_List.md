### Post by MikeMLP on 2006-07-30
I'm not sure if this is exactly what you're looking for, but if you look down by your window list on the left hand side (by default) there should be a space between the "show desktop" icon and the window list.  if you look in that space you should be able to see two vertical rows of dots (tiny).  (if it isn't there then look on the right side - it boarders your window list in any case)

right click the dots

select preferences

go to "size" tab

adjust until you find something you like :)

hopefully that offers enough adjustment for your tastes.  If it doesn't... I'm not sure where else you should/could look... perhaps someone else might have something to add.  But hopefully that will get you started.
