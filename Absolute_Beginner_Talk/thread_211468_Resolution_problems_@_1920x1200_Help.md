### Post by shdgrao on 2006-07-08
You can try to erase all the modes that you don't use. For example I have 1400x1050 and I have in my conf file:

SubSection "Display"
		Depth     1
		Modes    "1400x1050"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth     4
		Modes    "1400x1050"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth     8
		Modes    "1400x1050"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth     15
		Modes    "1400x1050"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth     16
		Modes    "1400x1050"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth     24
		Modes    "1400x1050"
	EndSubSection

When I have all the modes, like you have, I had the same problems that you described.
