### Post by Ptero-4 on 2006-04-20
Right click your bottom taskbar and select "new panel". It will be created at the top. Then right click your newly created top taskbar and select the stuff you want in it.

### Post by johnc4510 on 2006-04-20
Right click on an empty part of your bottom panel and select new panel.
It will probably put it on the side but if you right click it and choose properties, you can then orient it right, left, up or down. Then you'll have to right click on it again and choose add to panel to start adding back everything you had before.
Good luck
