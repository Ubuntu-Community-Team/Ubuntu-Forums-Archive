### Post by chggr on 2007-02-27
Right click on the battery icon and choose "Information"
On the "Device Information" tag, click on "More" and see what the capacity is. This is a measurement of how good your battery is. If it is too low, then your battery will not last for a long time when you unplug your laptop.

If you want to change the brightness, you can use the Fn key combination.
You could also right click on the battery icon and choose "Preferences". There you can change your laptops settings when it is running on AC or when it is running on Battery. Screen Brightness is one of those settings.
