### Post by kelbizzle on 2007-02-22
Okay then your probably not gonna want to hear this... But I'm telling you some good stuff here. 
I know you probably have at least once in your long use of Microsoft products have clicked on the Infamous 'Help and Support Center" when in need of help or just a little more explanation on on a certain topic. Only to find **Nothing** to aid you in your time of inquisitiveness. Not to mention the the stupid point and click troubleshooting wizards that help you out tremendously at doing nothing. 

Well If you haven't already you need to navigate your mouse to...
```
System -> Help -> System Documentation
```

Notice how much more organized the information is? Thats because the Staff at Ubuntu wants to help you help yourself.  More than you want to help yourself even! If your new to Linux your going to check out the help pages. As cliché as it sounds. Theres a great deal of good information. But not so much as to overwhelm you. Thats the best part. Just the right amount of information to help you enjoy your first cup of Ubuntu.
