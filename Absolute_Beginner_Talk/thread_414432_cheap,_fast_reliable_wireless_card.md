### Post by DeeTee on 2007-04-19
My choice is the D-Link DWL-G122. Works plug and play with Ubuntu and is pretty fast.

The only downfall is that its range isn't particularly amazing. However if you just need it for inhome networking it works perfectly.

[http://www.dlink.com/products/?sec=0&pid=334](http://www.dlink.com/products/?sec=0&pid=334)

Its really cheap as well. I got one for 25$ Canadian.
