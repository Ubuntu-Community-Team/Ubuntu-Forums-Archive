### Post by LaurelLynn on 2007-04-05
Be careful m47h3us,

Connecting to someones network without permision is illegal, even if they have left all the doors and windows open.

Its easy to do by accident, and your controller will keep trying to go back to the same access point. But note that the access point has your MAC address, so the men in black could track you down in the middle of the night.

Am I paranoid or what?

But seriously, under System->Administration->Networking your controller´s properties should let you pick one to connect to, just make sure it´s kosher.
