### Post by maniacmusician on 2007-02-24
> **kittyhawk63 said:**
> Looks like I'm headed to Nvidia. Any suggestions on what card to buy?
It depends on your budget. An averagely-priced card (I have this one as well) is the GeForce 7600GT. It'll probably cost you around $130 US, and it'll take care of all 3D needs you have, and then some.

If you plan on doing some gaming, this card will still be fine. If you want to game while still in 3D mode, you could buy a better card. Again, it all depends on your budget. Nvidia is definitely the way to go, though. 

Oh, and also, Beryl is **not** alpha software anymore, it hit Beta a little while ago. It's perfectly usable. It's a little unstable sometimes, and you may experience a few crashes if you get the SVN version (which is updated several times a week; completely cutting edge). However, Beryl isn't really necessary to your computing needs. I have to say, it's a nice addition. Whenever I'm without beryl, I feel myself missing it. 

Also, you may want to wait till Feisty Fawn (7.04) before doing all the beryl-ish stuff, it'll be less painful than installing edgy + programs + beryl and then upgrading. That is, if you're planning on upgrading. If you want to stick with Edgy Eft, I guess that's fine too.

PS: I think it's really cool that you're willing to buy a new video card to get the full experience out of Linux. I applaud your persistence
