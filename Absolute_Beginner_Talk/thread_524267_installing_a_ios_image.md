### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-12
OK, I assume that you burned it as an ISO image, not just as a data disk?

I also assume that you burnt the CD at a slow speed, no faster than 4 X?

If the above are OK, you probably need to ensure that your BIOS is set-up to boot from the CDROM, before the hard drive. This is usually done by intervening by pressing one of the function keys, when the PC is being booted. A menu will then open allowing you to make system changes.

Once this has been done, you just insert the LiveCD and re-boot and your PC should boot-up into a LiveCD version of Ubuntu.
