### Post by Marsolin on 2006-10-06
Some apps you should take a look at include: [LabPlot]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/labplot"), [kst]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/kst"), [KMPlot]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/kmplot"), and [Gnuplot]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/gnuplot"). Gnuplot is a command line only program. The others have graphical interfaces.
