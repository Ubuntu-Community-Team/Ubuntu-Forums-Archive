### Post by Witling on 2007-11-13
I don't know about adjusting the fan speed but let me venture a guess. You've owned this laptop for some time. If I'm correct, you've probably accumulated a lot of dust in the air ducts. The best thing to do would be to take the laptop apart and clean it. If you don't feel up to that, get a can of compressed air at an office supply store and try cleaning the ducts by spraying the compressed air into the ventilation ducts while the computer is running. Best wishes.
