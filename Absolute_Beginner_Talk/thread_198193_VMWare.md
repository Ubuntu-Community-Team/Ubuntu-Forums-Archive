### Post by jeroen2 on 2006-06-16
Bochs is a full IA32 emulator which doesn't do the instruction translation trick that vmware does. Therefore it's slower, but more robust. 

[http://bochs.sourceforge.net/](http://bochs.sourceforge.net/) 


Also, the Xen HyperVisor could be usefull for you if you know what you're doing:

[http://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/Research/SRG/netos/xen/](http://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/Research/SRG/netos/xen/)
