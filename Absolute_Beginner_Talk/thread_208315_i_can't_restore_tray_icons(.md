### Post by Jagot on 2006-07-03
For GAIM, when you're in the program go to Tools, Preferences.  Now go down to  Plugins and tick 'System Tray Icon'.

For the separator, right-click on the panel and click 'Add To Panel'.  Now go down to the 'Utilities' section at the bottom and the very last thing is the separator.  Highlight it an click 'Add'.  Once it's on the panel, right-click on the separator and you will see the option 'Move' so you can put it wherever you like.
