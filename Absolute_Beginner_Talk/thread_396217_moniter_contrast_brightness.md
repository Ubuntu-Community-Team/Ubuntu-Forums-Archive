### Post by Jonam on 2007-03-30
Is your PC video card setup correctly? You should not have to run at 100/100 for contrast and brightness.

Try downloading monica from:

[http://www.pcbypaul.com/software/monica.html](http://www.pcbypaul.com/software/monica.html)

Follow the instructions to extract and run the binary version. This will at least calibrate your video card so that your colours are balanced and therefore you should not need to run your monitor so hard.
