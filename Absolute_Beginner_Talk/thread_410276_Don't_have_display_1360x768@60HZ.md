### Post by locke.dragon on 2007-04-23
ok.  click system up at the top.  hit administration>>synaptic package manager.  wait for it to load.  then hit settings>>repositories and make sure the top four boxes are checked.  hit ok and wait for it to re-load.  now look for 915resolution.  it should be right there.  click on the check box next to it.  hit mark for installation, and then apply at the top.  wait for it to finish installing, then restart x using ctrl>>alt>>backspace after closing everything you're doing.  let me know if that fixes it!
