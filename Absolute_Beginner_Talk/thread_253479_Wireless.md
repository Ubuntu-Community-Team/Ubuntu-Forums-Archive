### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-09-08
If your network card was detected out-of-the-box, you'll just need to go to your System menu > Administration > Networking

If you don't see your wireless device listed as an interface there, the configuration will depend on what kind of card you're using.  Post as much detail about your setup (is it PCI, USB, PCMCIA, what brand, model number, chipset) and we'll point you in the right direction.  :)
