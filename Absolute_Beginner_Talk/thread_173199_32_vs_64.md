### Post by nanotube on 2006-05-10
according to your kernel version, you are running 32bit ubuntu, not 64bit. while your computer is "capable" of running 64bit, given your processor... i would really encourage you to stay with 32bit, as 64bit still has some problems with software availability. switching over to 64bit would not really be a walk in the park.

so if you want to save yourself some headaches, just get 32bit nvidia drivers.
