### Post by benuski on 2006-08-07
The traditional method of doing it is to put the disc in the drive, and then reboot your computer and tell it to boot from the cd instead of from the hard drive.  Now, being a lifetime PC user, I don't know where you could change that option.  On my Dell box, and almost all PCs, there's a button, usually F2 or F10 or ESC, that takes you to a one time boot menu, without changing your BIOS.  If you could find that on a Mac, thats what you want to do.  I would reccomend putting it in the drive, rebooting, and looking for that boot menu button, or seeing if it just boots itself up.

If you need more help, I'll try and help, but I bet some Mac users woul be much more helpful.
