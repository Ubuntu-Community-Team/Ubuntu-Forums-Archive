### Post by Deadmode on 2007-10-23
Can you give us a better idea of what your connection settings are for ktorrent?  Do you have your ports forwarded for ktorrent, if not go into ktorrent settings click on the UPnP tab and scan for ports to be forwarded, then click on forward ports.  This is only a temporary port forward, you will need to go into your router settings and forward those ports that you saw in UPnP.  If this still doesn't work try to restart your router.  If restarting your router doesn't work then test your bandwidth speed, perhaps there is a more serious problem with the router.  Also, try asking ktorrent forums.
