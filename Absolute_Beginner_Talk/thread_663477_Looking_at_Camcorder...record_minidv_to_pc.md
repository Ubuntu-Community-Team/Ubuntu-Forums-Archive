### Post by skullmunky on 2008-01-13
for usb, it's hard to say.  it might work or it might not - there might not be linux drivers that'll support webcam mode for it.  (these are called "video4linux" drivers, try googling that with the camera model to see what comes up).  

another way to do it would be with FireWire, aka IEEE1394.  higher quality video and more likely will be supported in linux.  if you're looking to shoot video and then edit it,  this would be the way to go.  Kino is a very easy-to-use video editing program; another one that I like that's a little more sophisticated but not as finished is Kdenlive.
