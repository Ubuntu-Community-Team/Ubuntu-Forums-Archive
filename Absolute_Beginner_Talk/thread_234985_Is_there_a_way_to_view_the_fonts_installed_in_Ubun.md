### Post by coffeecat on 2006-08-12
Just to carry on from where MikePnKY left off... You can also get the Font Preferences Windows that                               gnome-font-properties gives you by going to System > Preferences > Fonts. Once you've got that open, click on the 'Details' button. In the new window click on the 'Go to font folder' button. You'll then get a Nautilus window showing you all the fonts installed in your system. The samples only show 'Aa' but you can right-click and open with Gnome Font viewer to get much more.
