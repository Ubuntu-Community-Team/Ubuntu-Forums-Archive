### Post by meng on 2006-06-19
I suggest that /home be on a separate partition. Makes for easy upgrades without losing data if you need a fresh install. You don't need to defrag in Linux, you may already know this. Try to minimize spaces in file names. Linux handles this most of the time but some commands are finicky.
