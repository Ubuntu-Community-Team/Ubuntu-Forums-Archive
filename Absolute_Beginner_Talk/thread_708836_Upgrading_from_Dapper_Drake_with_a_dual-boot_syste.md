### Post by ctswhole on 2008-02-26
Have you already tried the Gutsy livecd?  It's been quite a while since I last installed Ubuntu on a dual boot system, but if memory serves correct (it doesn't always though), I think you will have the option of installing the Gutsy sytem over the existing Dapper system you currently have installed.  If you put in the Gutsy livecd and boot into it, you can click on "install" and go through the steps of installing it and see what the partitioning options are.  Nice thing about doing that is it won't change anything on your computer until a few steps after the partitioning phase, so you're in no danger of wrecking your computer.
