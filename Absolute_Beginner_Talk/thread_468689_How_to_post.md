### Post by Inxsible on 2007-06-09
you can add [quote]  [/ quote] around the line that you want to quote or [code] [/ code] around your code lines without spaces. If you see the box where you write your post text, you will notice a bunch of icons over on the top of that box. Explore all of them. You can simply highlight the line and then click one of those buttons to quote the line, or enclose them as code or a link etc.
