### Post by linuxwizard on 2007-09-27
The printer is compatible to the Epson Stylus Photo 915, so choose this model (using the Gimp-Print driver) as long as the Epson Stylus Photo 900 is not explicitly supported by Gimp-Print.

[http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Epson-Stylus_Photo_900](http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Epson-Stylus_Photo_900)
