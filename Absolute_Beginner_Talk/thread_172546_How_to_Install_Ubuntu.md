### Post by user1397 on 2006-05-08
[quote=Clay85]This is my download site: [http://www.ubuntu.com/download]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download")
I can see that it is an .iso file, but it is saving as a .rar file. Should I just burn the .rar file to disk?[/quote]
No that won't work. Unrar it, and you should have a .iso file.  Using whatever burning software you have, make sure to burn image or burn iso image to disc, don't do burn data to disc, that won't work either.  Then you can change your bios settings on your harddrive, (usually by pressing Esc, or one of the F-keys, it'll probably give you options) the bios settings can be opened in the screen that has your computer's name on it, usually the first one after you turn on the pc.  In bios try to find the part where it talks about priority of boot or something like that.  select cd-rom as first.  Then save, exit, and stick your ubuntu cd in there and have fun!
