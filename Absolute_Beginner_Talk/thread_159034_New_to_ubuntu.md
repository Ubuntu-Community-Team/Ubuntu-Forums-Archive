Or, more accurately, it stands for Wine Is Not an Emulator. Gotta love [recursive acronyms]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Recursive_acronym"). :) It's a [compatibility layer]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compatibility_layer") rather than an emulator.

### Post by taurus on 2006-04-12
The first time you run gdesklets, nothing displays because you haven't included any desklets for it.  However, you should see an icon on the panel in the upper right hand corner, next to your clock!  Now, head over to [http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/categories.php](http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/categories.php) and download some nice desklets for your system then...
