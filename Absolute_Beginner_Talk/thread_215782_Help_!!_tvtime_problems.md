### Post by bojzi on 2006-07-14
No, don't reinstall!

The sound not turning off is acutally the normaln behaviour.
You see, the sound is heard because the Line Out exit of your sound card is still turned all the way up even if you exit TVtime.

To solve it do this little trick go to this thread -> [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=7754](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=7754) and look at the 2nd post. That's all there is to it. ;)

Happy Ubuntuing! :)
