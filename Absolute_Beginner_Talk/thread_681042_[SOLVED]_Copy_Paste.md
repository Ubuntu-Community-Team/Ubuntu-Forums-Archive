Copy with the left button and paste with the middle button (or the little scroll button) or with both left and right at the same time if you have only two-button mouse.

### Post by mocoloco on 2008-01-28
FYI, there are *two* clipboards in Ubuntu (and any Linux/Unix os with X) that work independently, the ctrl+c/ctrl+v clipboard, and the highlight/middle-click clipboard.
There's a great clipboard manager app called [glipper]("apt:glipper") that keeps track the last several items you've had in your clipboard.  It shows you at the top in bold what's in the middle-click clipboard, and the rest shows the history of the other clipboard.
Gee, did I use the word clipboard enough :D.
