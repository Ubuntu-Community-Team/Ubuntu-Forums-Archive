### Post by user1397 on 2006-06-22
well, after all my trials and tribulations, i finally installed FC5 on a seperate partition on the same hard drive where dapper is installed. 

I gotta say, FC5 has very pretty splash images, but their default icon setup is not at all as pretty as dapper's (IMHO)  Also, all of my hardware was detected, just as in dapper.  But, FC5 did bug me in two major ways.  

First of all, i don't like the whole root password thing instead of sudo, it seems a lot more dangerous and plus i have to remember two passwords (user and root pw's), not one like in dapper.  also, and this is the major thing that bugs me, updates and generally adding/removing applications seems a lot slower, and sometimes, it'll even freeze.  also, i tried to install kde through the add/remove program, but at the end, i got some dependency problem, so i was pretty pissed off at FC5 for a minute.  it took a hell of a long time juts to download the packages, and plus it didn't work.  

So, basically, i think dapper is overall a better distro than FC5.  of course, all of this is IMHO.
