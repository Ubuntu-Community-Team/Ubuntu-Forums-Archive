### Post by klausner on 2007-12-15
I've been through lots of threads here on problems getting video to render properly under Gutsy. Solution range from enabling DMA to diddling with Xgl to changing drivers. But for many, there is a simpler solution. Go to System->Preferences->Appearance->Visual_Effects, and click on the option for None. This seems to solve it for most systems.
