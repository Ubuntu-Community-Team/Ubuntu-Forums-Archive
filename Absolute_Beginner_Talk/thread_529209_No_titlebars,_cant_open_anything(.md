### Post by sumguy231 on 2007-08-19
Just for interest, open the run dialog (should be Alt+F2) and try running 'metacity' if you can. See if that brings your window decorations back. If you can get to a terminal, you can find out running processes with either 'ps -u <username>' or 'top'. If you can't get to a terminal from the desktop, you can hit Ctrl+Alt+F1 to get to one. And you can hit Ctrl+Alt+F7 to get back.
