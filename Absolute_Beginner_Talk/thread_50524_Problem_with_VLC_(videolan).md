### Post by kspr on 2005-07-20
hi,

1: in the nautilus filebrowser, right click on a movie, choose open with other program, then dont click on the vlc icon, instead click below the icons where it says something like: choose a command ...or someting (i dont have english version so not sure what it says in english)    then enter vlc in the upcoming prompt, press open....  

2:
now close vlc. right click on the movie again and select properties ->  open with -> vlc


enjoy :)
