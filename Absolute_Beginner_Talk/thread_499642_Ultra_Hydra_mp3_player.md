### Post by Archoniam on 2007-07-13
Wait, uh.. Oh crap. Look, all you have to do is go to (top bar)
System > Preferences > Removable Drives and Media.
Go to tab Multimedia.
Set that program to open to...well... whichever program you're using. Then, close your music program and eject and mount your player. If it doesn't work, i really don't know. And if ubuntu still won't let you access it, you are most likely not admin. Become admin or make it so you can access your removable drives.
