### Post by Circus-Killer on 2007-11-05
personally, i never recommend shipit, only as a last resort.

the best option is to download, if you have broadband.

if you do not have a broadband connection, then i recommend that you try locate your nearest locoteam, you can find a good list of all the official loco teams [HERE]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LoCoTeamList"). once you find the nearest one to you, you should be able to make contact with the team and request a cd from one of the members of the team. this will cut down on the wait big time.

finally, if neither options work out for you, then yes, try shippit. but as i said, ordering from someone in your local community should take much quicker.

hope you have better luck.
