### Post by orb9220 on 2006-07-25
Nero does vob's. No one I know calls a dvd video vobs. It is called burning a 
dvd movie which is composed of two folders audio_ts and video_ts. There is nothing in the audio folder,  Just create two folders in the bottom half of nero one called audio_ts  and video_ts then grab all yer vobs and drag them there.

Now if it is a proper movie copy there will a bunch of other files with besides the vob's that are for menu navigation and etc,, be sure to get them all. The easy way if to just drag the audio and video_ts folders to the bottom nero window then you don't have to worry about creating the folders.

Hope this helps
