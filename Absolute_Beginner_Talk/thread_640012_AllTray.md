### Post by Dawa on 2008-01-25
"for dummies" instructions:

Go to System Menu > Preferences Menu > click on Appearance

Appearance Preferences window should open.

Click on Visual Effects tab > Click on "None" > Click Close button

AllTray should now work just fine!  Window effects will be less flashy, however.
