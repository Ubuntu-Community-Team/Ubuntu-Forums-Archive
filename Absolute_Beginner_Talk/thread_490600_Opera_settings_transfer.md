### Post by MyR on 2007-07-03
in your ubuntu home folder your opera settings are in the folder .opera
copy over everything in the skin, sessions, and widgets folders
bookmarks are stored in the file called opera6.adr
not sure about the rest of the data..

as an easier approach, i would back up everything in .opera and then overwrite all of it with your C\Program Files\Opera files (while opera is closed of course), and see if everything works.
