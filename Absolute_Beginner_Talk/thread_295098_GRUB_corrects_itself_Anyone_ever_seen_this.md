### Post by pbaehr on 2006-11-07
Sounds like your hard-drive may be on its last legs. Or, it could just be a one time drive error. It may even be what locked up your system in the first place.

I'd make sure I had any crucial files backed up and keep an eye on the drive.

From my experience, hard drives which give an error like that from time to time fail a month or two after. Then, it could also just be a one time fluke. I wouldn't be overly concerned unless it becomes a more regular occurance.
