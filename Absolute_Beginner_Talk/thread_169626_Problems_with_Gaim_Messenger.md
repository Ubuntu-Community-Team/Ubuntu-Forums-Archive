### Post by yoink23 on 2006-05-03
If you mean the **buddy list**, go to tools...preferences...interface...buddy list and uncheck "Raise window on events."

If you mean the actual **conversation windows**, go to tools...preferences...conversations and uncheck "Raise window on events."
