### Post by teatimebing on 2007-06-18
So you have used fireworks but you can't get on with Gimp?!? Have you tried inkscape? Whilst it's mostly comparable to something like freehand or illustrator it is still very good for image editing.

Depending on what you want cinepaint branch of Gimp which split off long ago, whilst it is mostly aimed at film and high colour formats and matte painting, initially it has a much "simpler" interface than The Gimp. THere is of course Amazon Paint, comes in at aroudn $3000 excluding plugins.

I would really suggest you invest your time in Gimp, it will be worth it.
