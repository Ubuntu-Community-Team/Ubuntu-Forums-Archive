### Post by geekphreak on 2006-02-01
Natively Linux can not fully read NTFS partitions, I believe. You can download special drivers that would allow read/write, but as far as I know, I/O goes really really slow. 
Take a look at [Captive/]("http://www.jankratochvil.net/project/captive/") and [Linux-NTFS]("http://www.linux-ntfs.org/")
