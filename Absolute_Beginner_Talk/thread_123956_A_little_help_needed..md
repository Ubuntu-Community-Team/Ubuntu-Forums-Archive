### Post by matthew on 2006-01-31
This is intentional...it's a bit confusing at first for people coming from other operating systems, but this is quite standard for Unix/Linux type systems. When you input the password nothing is shown on the screen...a person looking over your shoulder couldn't even guess how long the password is. Try typing it in anyway and hitting <enter> and see if it works. If it doesn't let us know and someone will try to help you out, but I bet that's all that's going on.
