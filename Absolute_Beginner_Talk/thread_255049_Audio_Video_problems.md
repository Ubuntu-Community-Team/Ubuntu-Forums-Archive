### Post by xyz on 2006-09-11
Hi,
Did you enable universe/multiverse repositories?
If not, open Synaptic, click on Settings and Repositories.
Finally, click on Add and check Community maintained (Universe) and Non-free (Multiverse). And then click again on Add.

There are several ways to deal with Audio/Video. Go and visit these 2 links and see which suits you better. Feel free to come back and ask questions:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)
[http://www.getautomatix.com/](http://www.getautomatix.com/)

Good luck.
