### Post by deadgobby on 2006-10-17
You can install Automatix or Easyubuntu. It will load up all the codecs and mplayer as well. It may fix some things you are getting fluster with.
[http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org/get.html](http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org/get.html)
[http://www.getautomatix.com/](http://www.getautomatix.com/)
 To uninstall a program. Go into synaptic, do a search and select uninstall. If it gives you a warning. Do not uninstall. What ever you do, do not do a complete uninstall. You may end up breaking the system/kernal and then you'll be not all happy and screaming my name.
