### Post by benuski on 2007-01-03
I've had this problem before as well.  Sometimes it just took my cable modem a day or two or three to really realize that there was a computer attached to it that wanted to get to the outside.  Another fix that my uncle has had work for him in the past is to find a cheap router, and just plug in your modem to that, and then your computer to the router as well, and then his connection worked just fine.
