### Post by mcduck on 2007-12-05
Just go to System/Administration/Software Sources, and on bottom of the 'Ubuntu Software'-tab you can disable the CD. This will make Ubuntu always download packages from the web repositories, and never ask for the CD.

### Post by asmiller-ke6seh on 2007-12-05
PROBABLY: Somewhere along the line SYNAPTIC was told that there was a Repository on a local CD. Check your list of repositories in SYNAPTIC, and delete the reference to the local CD drive.
