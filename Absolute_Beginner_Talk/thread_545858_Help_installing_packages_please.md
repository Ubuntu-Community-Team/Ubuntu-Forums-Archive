### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-09-08
Many websites have repositories that can be added to your package management system; but be careful which ones you enable, as a malicious repository can potentially do anything to your system.

There's also the excellent website [Ubuntu Click And Run]("http://www.getdeb.net"), which creates and hosts real packages for Ubuntu. They've got the latest version of Pidgin there too. It's not a repo, just an ordinary website (similar to download.com).
