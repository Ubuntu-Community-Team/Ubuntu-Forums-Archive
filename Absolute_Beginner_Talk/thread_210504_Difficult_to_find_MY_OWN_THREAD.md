### Post by Dr. Nick on 2006-07-07
Subscribed threads are the way to go.

Hit the side bar "[Edit Options]("http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editoptions")" then go to "         Messaging & Notification" and "Default Thread Subscription Mode" and set that to something other than "do not subscribe" then you can hit "list subscriptions" on the side or get to it through the "quick links" up top. It will bold all the threads youve posted in, not just created, that have new responses
