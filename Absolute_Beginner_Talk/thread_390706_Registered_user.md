### Post by daynah on 2007-03-22
You're not blind at all! There is not official registering of Ubuntu or Linux. We're anti-establishment!

Well, maybe not all of us.

Anyway, what you may be looking for is the Ubuntu or Linux counter. There are two projects that are not related to GNU or Canonical that are right here:
[http://ubuntucounter.geekosophical.net/](http://ubuntucounter.geekosophical.net/)  (ubuntu)
[http://counter.li.org/](http://counter.li.org/)  (linux)

These are private projects (because there is no official project. We're all open source hippies here) that are keeping a count of how many people use Ubuntu or Linux if you let them count you. :) If you'd like to be counted, just sign up. I don't think you have to put your name or anything. Many people on the forums put in their signature what Linux user number or Ubuntu user number they are. :)
