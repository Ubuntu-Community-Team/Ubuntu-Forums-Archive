### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-02-07
I don't think it's possible to have a video background with other things on top of it.  You can click 'Insert' then 'Movie and Sound' to put a video in your frame, but I can't figure out a way to put text or anything over the video.  You can resize the video to take a smaller portion of the frame and have text, etc. in the unused part of the frame, but I can't figure out a way to put it on top of the video.  If you can't find a way to do it, your best bet might be to use a video editor like kdenlive to edit the video and superimpose text or other things on top of the video.  Then you could render it to a new video file and insert that into your Impress slide show.
