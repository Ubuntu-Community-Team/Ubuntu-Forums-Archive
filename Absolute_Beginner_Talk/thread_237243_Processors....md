### Post by ash211 on 2006-08-15
I assume you're talking about the kernel.  With your amd64 processor, you can choose to either run the 32 or the 64 bit versions of the linux kernel.  

If you want maximum compatibility, choose the 32-bit.  If you like tinkering with your system a lot and want to try to squeeze every last bit of performance out of your processor, go with the 64-bit version.

By the newest version, you mean Dapper Drake 6.06?  Or the point release (6.06.1)?
