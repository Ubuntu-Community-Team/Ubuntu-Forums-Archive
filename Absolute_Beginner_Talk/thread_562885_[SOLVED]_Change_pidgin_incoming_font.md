### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-09-29
In the conversations tab of Pidgin preferences is a tickbox for "show formatting on incoming messages."  This should take care of that for you.  Otherwise, in the plugins menu is a conversation colors plugin that allows you to change text color.
