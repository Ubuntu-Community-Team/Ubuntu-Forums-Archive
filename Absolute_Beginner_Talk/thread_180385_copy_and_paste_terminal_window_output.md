### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-05-22
Easiest is to use the scroll bar to scroll back to the start of the text you want to capture, and **click** at the **starting point** but don't drag. Now use the scroll-bar to scroll to the end of the text you want to capture, and **shift-click** the **ending point**.

Bingo!
