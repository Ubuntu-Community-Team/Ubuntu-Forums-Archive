### Post by SpiderGorilla on 2008-02-23
Okay. Try this. When your system is logged in, go to:


System >> Preferences >> Sound

In here, click the tab labeled "Sounds", which is wedged between "Devices" and "System Beep".

Try changing where it says "Log in: Login" to "No Sound". That'll just disable it out-right. You can attempt another sound there, which may tell you if it's an issue with your soundcard or drivers, or possibly the ALSA mixer. You can also try unchecking "Enable Software Sound Mixing (ESD)" if yours is set up like mine.

Try that, let me know how it goes.
