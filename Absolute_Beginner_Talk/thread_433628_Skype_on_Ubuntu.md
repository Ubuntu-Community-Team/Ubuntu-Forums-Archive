### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-05-05
If you have a slow Internet connection, sending the file to you as an email attachment will not help, for two reasons:

1. The Skype Debian file is 9.6 Mb. Whether you directly download it or someone attaches it to and email, it is still 9.6 Mb and downloading it as an email attachement will be no faster.

2. Very few email providors allow the attachment of such large files.

You will either need to go to a friend who has high speed Internet and burn it onto a CD, or go to an Internet Cafe to download it.
