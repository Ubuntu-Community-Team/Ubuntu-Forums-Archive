### Post by lemmy999 on 2006-01-30
I dont think there can be any taxes or duties payable on the software. 

Any such duties would have to be calculated on the commercial value of the goods. As the goods in question have no monetary value then no charges can be levied. Certainly that is the situation in the UK. There is also a de minimus level under which low value gifts/purchases are exempt from all such charges.
