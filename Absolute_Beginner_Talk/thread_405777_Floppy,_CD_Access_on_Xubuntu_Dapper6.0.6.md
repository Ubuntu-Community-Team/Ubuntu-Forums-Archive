### Post by dstew on 2007-04-10
On my setup, the CD drive appears on the desktop when I put in a CD. I did not do anything to set that up, it just worked from the time I installed it.

I think that any drive mounted in the /media directory is supposed to appear on the desktop, but I am not sure. You might look at your /etc/fstab file to see if the Cd mount point is in fact in the /media directory. I am not at my Xubuntu machine now so I can't test it. Oh, and my USB drives appear on the desktop when I plug them in also.
