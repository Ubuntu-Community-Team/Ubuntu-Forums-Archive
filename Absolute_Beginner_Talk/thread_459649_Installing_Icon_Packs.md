To install, System > Preferences > Themes.

Push install, and direct it to the archive with the icon set. Then you can customize any theme to use em with the customize button and going to the fonts tab. 

Sometimes its easier to just get whole themes (GTK 2 packs) from gnomelook though :).

### Post by isaacj87 on 2007-05-30
Oh I understand what you're saying...

here I'll give you a step by step with pics :)

I have fiesty but I'm sure it's not too different

1. go to system->preferences->themes

2. save the icon pack to the desktop (preferably)

3. within the themes preferences click on **install theme **(see pic, it's at the top right)

4. choose your icon pack from the desktop (see pic, the pack i'm choosing is OSX3.3.tar.gz)

5. all done! it should ask if you want to use it.
