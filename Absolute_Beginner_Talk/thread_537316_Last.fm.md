### Post by Butthead on 2007-08-29
Amarok plays Last.fm streams just fine. :KS

As a matter of fact, I'm listening to their Heavy Metal stream as I type this. :guitar:

Just set up an account on their webpage, and then add the same username and password under the "configure last.fm" menu thingie under the configure Amarok top menu. =D>

Then it's just a matter of picking what stream you want to listen to. \\:D/
