### Post by rhodry on 2007-10-01
I think you will find the previous posts are discussing the older Metacity way to add desktops. I note you say you are running Gutsy beta which, as I understand it, is running Compiz by default. Therefore your number of desktops is a function in your Compiz Settings Manager.

I have gone back to Feisty Metacity myself for a while until Gutsy settles in, so I can't remember the actual command. I do remember though, you go into the Compiz Manager and (I think) in the General Options you will find an option that says 1 Desktop and 2 Horizontal Spaces ( or something like that). Change to 2 to 4, or whatever.

A bit vague I know but I hope this helps?!

Rhodry.
