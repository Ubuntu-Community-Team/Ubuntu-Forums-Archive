### Post by benuski on 2006-09-21
I would try restarting your computer, then when the BIOS page is up, before it starts to load windows, hit either Escape or F12... usually one of those two buttons is the Boot Menu button.  It should say somewhere on the screen, but since its only up for a few seconds, you might not have seen it.  It should pop up and ask what you want to boot from for that time only.
