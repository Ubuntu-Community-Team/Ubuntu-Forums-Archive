### Post by gwilson on 2007-05-30
If you are having trouble getting your wireless card working with ndiswrapper, I would suggest that you compare whatever procedure you are using to the one in the following link:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Device/TRENDnet_TEW-421PC_H/W:B1_(ndiswrapper](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Device/TRENDnet_TEW-421PC_H/W:B1_(ndiswrapper))

This may not be specific to your hardware and situation, but it may show up some sort of missing step or show you which questions to ask. I have used this procedure with several different wireless cards making changes here and there as necessary with great success.

Otherwise, try searching for posts by FrodoB (who wrote the how-to in the link) because he seems to know what he is doing and is able to communicate well.

(Thanks, FrodoB)
