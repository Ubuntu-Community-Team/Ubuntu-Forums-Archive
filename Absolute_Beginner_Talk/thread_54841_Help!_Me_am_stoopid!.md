### Post by Knome_fan on 2005-08-06
1. Back up your important data and especially your wife's important data!
This will save your marriage and your live if something goes wrong, which isn't very likely but of course possible.

2. Back up your important data and especially your wife's important data!

3. Back up your important data and especially your wife's important data!
You get the drift.  ;-) 

4. As you are a hardcore gamer, definately keep a windows partition. There are some games for linux and you can even get quite a lot of windows games to run on linux, but as nearly all games are targetting windows you should definately keep it.

5. Yes, dual booting is no problem.
