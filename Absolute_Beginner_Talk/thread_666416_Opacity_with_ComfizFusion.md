### Post by dje on 2008-01-13
For the terminal open up gnome-terminal (i assume that is what you use) and right click in it and select 'Edit current profile'. On the Effects tab on the window that appears select transparent background. As for the opacity settings of other windows have a look [here]("http://www.ubuntu1501.com/2007/12/transparencies-and-opacity-in-compiz.html"). Hope that helps

### Post by Udaho on 2008-01-13
To make your Terminal transparent open it up and go to Edit > Current Profile. On the Effects tab click Transparent background and use the slider bar to set how transparent you want it to be.

To make the taskbar transparent right click it and choose Properties. Select the Background tab, click the Solid Color button, and use the slider there.
