### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-08-06
If you take a look at:  [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org) you'll find thousands of different themes for your desktop/Icons/Beryl/Emerald and so....

Installing the themes are straight forward. Go to System>Settings>Themes and when you have the themes manager on your screen you simply click: Install, then you surfe to the folder you have downloaded the themes files to, mark up the one you want and click ok. Theme manager will do the install for you.
