### Post by kpkeerthi on 2007-04-19
Alternate CD cannot be run "Live" and has textual interface. Mainly targeted for PCs with less than 256 MB RAM.
With the desktop CD, you can run off the CD and has a better graphical interface. As for the features, they are much the same.
