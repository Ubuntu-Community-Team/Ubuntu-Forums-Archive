### Post by rgsproductions on 2006-09-22
I run the outputs from my Behringer 24 channel board into the line in on the sound card.  Through your software mixer, select your line in input.  If you are running a low end sound card, you will need either a small mixer or direct box, if you want really good sound.  A regeular mic or electric guitar 's signal is not hot enough.   I use a presonus tube preamp for my acoustic and either a mic off my amp or straight out my pedals into my sound board for the electric guitar.  I have had great success with the above.  Ardour with the jack programs work great.  Read the forums on Ubuntu studio.  They help alot.
