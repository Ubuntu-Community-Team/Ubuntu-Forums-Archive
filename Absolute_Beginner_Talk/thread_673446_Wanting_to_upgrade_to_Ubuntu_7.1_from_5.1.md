### Post by Shazaam on 2008-01-20
Gutsy (7.10) fits on a regular cd. Download the version you want and burn it SLOWLY (4x or less) on a good quality cd.
IMHO upgrading is a long process with a lot of pitfalls. I recommend a clean install. BUT, some people have had success going the upgrade route.
