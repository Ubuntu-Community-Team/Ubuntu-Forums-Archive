EldaraIts called the Sky Dome plugin

System>>Preferences>>Advanced Desktop Effects Settings>>Desktop Cube>> Appearance Tab
At the bottom expand the Sky Dome and check mark it. Also add any wallpaper that you like in Skydome Image and then reload compiz
