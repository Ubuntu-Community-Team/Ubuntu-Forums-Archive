### Post by Iceman17 on 2008-01-24
I have Ubuntu 7.10 installed under virtual pc.  When I go to change my visual effects level to normal I get an error reading: "Desktop effects could not be enabled".  Is it possible to change themes under virtual pc?  If so, how?

---

### Post by PeterJS on 2008-01-24
Yes it is possible to change themes look under System > Preferances > Apperance. Enhanced desktop effects however can not be used on a virtual machine. The virtual video cards just don't support the 3d acceleration needed.
