### Post by Kobalt on 2006-08-07
Hello,
7.5Gb should be fine for Kubuntu systems, if you use your 25Gb partitions to store your personal files. 
Otherwise, you can resize and move partitions with a tool on a LiveCD of (K)Ubuntu. I know it's Gparted with an Ubuntu LiveCD, but I'm not sure what's that with Kubuntu. QTparted comes to my mind, though it might be something else, I'm no KDE user. Anyway, start a LiveCD sessions by reboot your computer with the LiveCD in you CDrom Drive, and when you're logged in, go to System > Administration and you should find the too to edit your partitions.

Cheers !
