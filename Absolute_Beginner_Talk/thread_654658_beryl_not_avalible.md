### Post by Ub1476 on 2007-12-31
Compiz-Fusion ("new" Beryl) is already installed in Ubuntu now. Go to System>Preferences>Appearance>Visual Effects to enable it. To customize it like Beryl, install "Advanced Desktop Effects (compizconfig-settings-manager).

### Post by mattgaunt on 2007-12-31
Basically people worked together on compiz, then there was some sort of dis-agreement, so they forked off and created beryl, then after a while they joined back together and created compiz-fusion.

So while compiz-fusion isn't beryl, there is no further development on beryl and most effect on beryl are available with compiz-fusion
