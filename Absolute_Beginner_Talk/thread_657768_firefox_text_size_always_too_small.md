### Post by Casual Fan on 2008-01-03
Go to Edit>Preferences>Content>Fonts & Colors>Advanced

Then set the font sizes you want and the minimum font size allowed.

Then UNcheck the box that says "Allow pages to choose their own fonts, instead of my selections above."

Click OK. Click Close.

That should do it!

---

### Post by HermanAB on 2008-01-03
Here is the secret:
Preferences, Content, Advanced, Minimum font size = 12 and also UNselect Allow pages to choose their own fonts.
