### Post by kyphi on 2008-02-23
Do you have a spellchecker installed?

Open Writer, go to Tools-Options-Language Settings-Languages and find the caption Default Languages for Documents.  Select your language and make sure that it has ABC and a tick in front of it.  That is your spellchecker.

Also go to Writing Aids (just below Language Settings) and place a tick next to Spellchecker in Available Language Modules.

That should fix it.
