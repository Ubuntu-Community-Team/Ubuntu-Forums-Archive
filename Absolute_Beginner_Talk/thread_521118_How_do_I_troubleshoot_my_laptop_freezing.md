### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-08-09
Go through all of them and post anything that looks unusual.  Sometimes the battery contacts get dirty.  Use an eraser to clean them.  Sometimes the internal power regulation hiccups causing random errors.

Try reseating the memory.  Clean the memory tabs with an eraser, but ground yourself while doing it.

I repaired a laptop with random freezes--the video cable was shorting through the hinge.  Too many hinge cycles for the metal braid and it wore though the insulation.  A bugger to diagnose.

If you are running Damn Small as your profile says, then you won't get much in the way of error reporting.  Besides hardware freezes aren't captured well in Linux.  You will get all sorts of errors but not really the source of the problem.
