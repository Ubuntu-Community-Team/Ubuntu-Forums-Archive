### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2007-06-05
utorrent does not need the "installer"

Just download the exe :

[http://download.utorrent.com/beta/utorrent-1.7-beta-2407.exe](http://download.utorrent.com/beta/utorrent-1.7-beta-2407.exe)

And copy it to c:\Program\ Files

run with

wine ../Program\ Files/utorrent.exe
