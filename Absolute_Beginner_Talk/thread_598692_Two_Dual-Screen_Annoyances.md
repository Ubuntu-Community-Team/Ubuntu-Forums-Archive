```

your settings should stick after you make changes.

### Post by Inxsible on 2007-10-31
The problem is that your second screen is not defined in the xorg.conf file. 

At the very bottom in the Screen section, you only have the DFP screen listed. Add the CRT screen there and both your screens will be active when you boot.

I have the same nvidia card as yours. I always maximize windows and they only take the screen they are in, leaving the other screen free for me to use.

I am also looking for a way so that, when I disconnect the external monitor, my Twin View should automatically recognize it and bring all the windows to the primary screen. No luck so far :(
