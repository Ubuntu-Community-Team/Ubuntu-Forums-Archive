### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-04-24
Yes, your printer will work fine: Check out this link:

[http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=HP-PhotoSmart_2575](http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=HP-PhotoSmart_2575)

If you install it with HPLIP, you will have full control of functions and be able to monitor your ink levels in a GUI. 

By the way, HPLIP requires the python-qt libraries, which are not installed by default. (I don't know why as HPLIP will be and won't work without them)
