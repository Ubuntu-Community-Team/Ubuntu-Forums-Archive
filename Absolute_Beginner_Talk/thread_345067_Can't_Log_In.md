### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-01-23
Try rebooting to rescue mode, and when you have the root account command prompt, enter 

```
passwd user-name-account
```
where "user-name-account" is the name of your old account. It will ask for the password and confirm it, and then you can reset it.

Reboot again, and you should be able to log in normally.
