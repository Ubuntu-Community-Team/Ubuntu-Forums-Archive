### Post by Nythain on 2007-07-01
In a nutshell... Desktop Environments and the software to go with them

Ubuntu - Gnome and general gtk apps
Kubuntu - KDE and a whole suite of kde/qt apps
Xubuntu - xfce and the xfce suite of apps, with some gtk thrown in there
Edubuntu - i believe is Ubuntu with a bunch of Educationall software pre bundled
Fluxbuntu - ROX and Fluxbox with a minimal amount of and light weight apps

It all boils down to preference and what your machine can handle... older boxes should probably hit up xubuntu and fluxbuntu... if you have a machine made in the last half decade you could hit up ubuntu or kubuntu...

i was personally a kubuntu fan untill i discovered fluxbox...
