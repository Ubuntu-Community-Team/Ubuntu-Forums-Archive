### Post by kernel tom on 2007-10-24
well the program is located in your "bin" folder... so let's say you download a torrent, and you want to "open with" a program via firefox.  You would select open with, then go browse starting at the highest level, select your "bin" folder, and find rtorrent in the list and select it.  Also press remember this application.  then your torrents should automatically open with rtorrent.  Furthermore, if you have downloaded torrents already, you can right click on them and to "open with" the same way as i said earlier
