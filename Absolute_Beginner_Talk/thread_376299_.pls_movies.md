### Post by freeflyer57 on 2007-03-04
do you have the playlist (.pls) downloaded? If so find the stream address. 

Example:
[playlist]
NumberOfEntries=1

File1=[COLOR="Red"]http://streamexample.com:80[/COLOR]
Title1=My Favorite Online TV Show
Length1=-1


Version=2

add the address to your media player. I know that gXine and VLC can play streamed media.

In VLC: File->network stream-> In MRL type: stream address

gXine: Media->add media mark->add MRL address and name it
