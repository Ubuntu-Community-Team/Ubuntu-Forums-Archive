### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-02-29
go to system>preferences>main menu and on the left you will see the internet category.(highlight it) check off bittorrent(on the right) to add it to applications>internet.

btw, deluge bittorrent is a much better full featured client. [http://deluge-torrent.org/](http://deluge-torrent.org/)
