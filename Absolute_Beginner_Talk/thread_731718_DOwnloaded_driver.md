### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-03-22
Go to your Advanced Desktop Effects settings and click on General Options.  Click the 'Desktop Size' tab and change the 'Horizontal virtual size' to 4.  Then click the 'Back' button in the lower left and make sure the boxes are checked next to 'Desktop Cube' and 'Rotate Cube'.

You use it by either ctrl+alt+left or right arrow, or you press ctrl+alt and drag your mouse with the left button for a free rotating cube.
