### Post by PatrickMay16 on 2007-10-05
My friend, there are many ways to open a terminal window. Choose which is handy for you.

A)
Choose "terminal" from the Applications menu: Click 'Applications', then 'Accessories', then terminal.

B)
Press ALT+F2, then type this:

gnome-terminal

then press enter.
