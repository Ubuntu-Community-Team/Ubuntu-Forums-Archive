### Post by Tux Aubrey on 2007-08-02
I have had no problems at all with Feisty - but you may be better going for a fresh install rather than the upgrade route.  You would have to upgrade to edgy and then to feisty and the downloads would be bigger and longer, and the risks higher, than a clean install from a Feisty CD image.  You will then have the joy of upgrading to Gutsy when its released (or sooner if you are a risk taker).

Back up your /home (and any other work you want to keep).

Good luck!

### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-08-02
I think to upgrade to Feisty, you first have to upgrade to Edgy.  Gutsy is due to be released in October, so maybe you'd just like to wait until then and do a fresh install of Gutsy.  Dapper is an LTS release.
However, if you want to experience Feisty, you can certainly go ahead with it, esp. if you don't have anything to risk in the upgrade (like losing important files).  You should always keep good backups anyway, like on a flash drive or external hard disk.
