### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-11-27
No one can be really sure unless of course they went to the houses and offices of every Linux user and asked them what they were using:D or you could just setup a poll asking Linux users to mark what they were using, but both of these are either inaccurate or not feasible. You could check out the ratings of every Linux distro according to the amount of hits concerning their searches at Distrowatch.com, but this is also very inaccurate but would give you a rough idea of what Linux distro is used the most.

But basically it comes down to one thing:-

Use the Linux distro that fits you best and stick with it as long it does suit you regardless of how famous or infamous it may be.
