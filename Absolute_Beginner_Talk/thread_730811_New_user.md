### Post by drascus on 2008-03-21
OK well to jazz up the desktop get compiz enabled or get beryl there are good posts in the forums on how to do that. Also check out [http://gnome-look.org/](http://gnome-look.org/) or [http://art.gnome.org](http://art.gnome.org) these sites have great wall papers and everything you could imagine for your desktop. Don't forget to check out themes for firefox. [https://help.ubuntu.com/7.10/config-desktop/C/index.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/7.10/config-desktop/C/index.html) has good info on how to do all this stuff...
