My monitor goes into a sleep mode,  after first going into screensaver when the PC is not used for a while.  When I press any keybord key it wakes up normally.  How can I prevent the monitor from going to sleep please?

### Post by mcduck on 2006-12-14
Open System/Preferences/Power Management and slide the controller for display sleep to far right so that it says 'never'.
