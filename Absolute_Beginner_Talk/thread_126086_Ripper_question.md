### Post by Terry of Astoria on 2006-02-05
For me sound juicer can create oggs which are about the size you specified. (small)  A CD can only hold 700 Mb so you are ripping wavs, probably. 
Try in Sound Juicer:
Edit > Preferences > at the bottom choose Ogg Vorbis from the list

Oggs are better than mp3s and it's an open source codec. You can burn a music CD with them easily. Most windows users will have to download a codec to play them but Ubuntu is good to go, as are most Linux distros.
