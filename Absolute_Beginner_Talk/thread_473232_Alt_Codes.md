Is there any possible way to do alt codes on Linux?
On Windows I could go to Wordpad and press, <ALT> 21 and it would insert a symbol.

### Post by shirilover on 2007-06-13
Yes, there is.
The convention will either be:
Ctrl+Shift+<character code> ; must keep Ctrl and Shift held down
or
Ctrl+Shift+U+<character code> ; again Ctrl+Shift must be held down
