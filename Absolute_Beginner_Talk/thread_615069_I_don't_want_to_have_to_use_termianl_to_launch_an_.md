### Post by cjm5229 on 2007-11-16
System>Preferences>Main Menu Click the category You wish to put the Launcher in. (Internet for Thunderbird) Then click New Item, In "Command", browse to the folder where the launcher is and click it. Enter the name of the application in "Name" somewhere in the  Applications files you should be able to find an icon you enter where it says "Icon" Click the Little square and browse to the icon.
