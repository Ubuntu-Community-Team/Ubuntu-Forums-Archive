### Post by JohnLM_the_Ghost on 2008-02-12
Well I can think of only few reasons why would you have this kind of problems. For me it went pretty smoothly.

Few would include:
[LIST]
[*]It is always best to install a fresh Ubuntu. Updating Major Ubuntu version is somewhat buggish. (In case you updated)
[*]Sometimes ATI video cards have trouble installing. (It is not a big problem, have ATI myself)
[*]Two or more Sound Cards require special configuration.
[*]Newer sound drivers can have problems with older sound cards.
[*]I don't do wireless, but i hear they have problems.
[/LIST]

This may sound as if Ubuntu has many problems, believe me, it really isn't that way. I only listed everything what i had in mind for this matter.

You could post what hardware you have problems with, so we can be more specific.

### Post by lswest on 2008-02-12
that's the thing, the "working" drivers were probably updated, meaning they possibly stopped support of older cards/chipsets, or the new features in those drivers are unsupported by the hardware in your laptop.
