### Post by irish_flu on 2007-05-28
A few thoughts:

-getting 75 GB out of an 80 GB drive is normal.  You lose it in formatting, and (as somebody said earlier) the difference between GB and GiB (1000mb vs. 1024mb).

-Any chance that other data is stored in "hidden" files (in other words, have you turned on "view hidden files" in the "view" menu)?

- Just FYI, you do indeed have 1 "partition" on that drive.  Without a partition, there's nothing to format.
