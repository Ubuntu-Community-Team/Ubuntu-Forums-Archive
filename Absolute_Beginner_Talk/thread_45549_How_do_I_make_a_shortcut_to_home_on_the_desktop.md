### Post by marsian on 2005-06-30
[QUOTE=gammyhand]I can't work out how to make a shortcut to home on the desktop :([/QUOTE]
 on the desktop, click on the right-mouse button. Then select the third item in the contextual menu (it's "créer un lanceur" in french... should be something like "create a launcher" in english... sorry for not being more accurate): then just fill the form with the elements you want:
Name -> name than will appears under the shortcut icon
URL -> type "/home/<your login>"
Type -> choose "Link"
Icon -> choose a cool icon
... and that's all !
When you will click on the created icon, nautilus will be launched and it will open your homedir.
