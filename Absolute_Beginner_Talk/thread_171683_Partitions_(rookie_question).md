### Post by Rinzwind on 2006-05-07
Yes it can.

But preparing upfront with Partition Magic is less risky if you never ever did it before cuz you then have the option "use the empty space". 
The slightest mistake during repartitioning during install and you lost your XP.
Oh and for things like this I(!) think it's perfectly acceptable to find a version of PM and to try it out and see if it does what you want. 

EXT3 is the default and general FS at the moment.
