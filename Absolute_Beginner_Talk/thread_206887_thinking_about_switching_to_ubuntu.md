### Post by uzi09 on 2006-06-30
openGL issue:
i think it supports it quite well. if you'd like, there are live cd's are available for ubuntu to try it out on your system without making any changes to the harddrive. just burn the cd, pop it in to the coffee mug holder (;)) and restart.

basically, i'd recommend a dual boot...
