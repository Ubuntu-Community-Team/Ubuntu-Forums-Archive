### Post by drummer on 2005-05-21
If you're looking for preferences.. click on 'System' in the top panel on your screen, mouse over 'Preferences' and a list of apps comes up. Third from the bottom is 'Sound' and there is also 'Multimedia Systems Selector' which has some audio settings as well. Have a look in 'Sound' first though.
Most of the general settings can be found in this menu (Preferences).

If you already know about this menu but can't see 'System' in the top panel towards the left.. you might have removed it by mistake. To add it again, right-click in a blank space on the panel, click 'Add to panel...' then scroll halfway down the list, select 'Menu Bar' and click 'Add'
