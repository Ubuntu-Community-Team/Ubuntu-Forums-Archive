### Post by shoujulei on 2006-10-16
There is such a thing, and it's called superkaramba.  The weather widget you probably are thinking of is called liquid weather.  Various other widgets, including the cpu monitor (and more) can be found at kde-look.org  under the category of karamba.  (You can apt-get install superkaramba from the terminal, or from a gui package manager like synaptic).  Hope this helps.
