### Post by Sklasko on 2006-12-09
Me, I prefer Gnome. ### Post by NZ-Wanderer on 2006-12-09
I started out with Ubuntu, but just recently switched to Kubuntu...

After running both of them, I must say I personally prefer Kubuntu, as it got lots more bells and whistles in it, which makes things a lot easier to do..

Just as an example, I upgraded from dapper to Edgy on Ubuntu and had no end of problems, yesterday I upgraded (using update manager) Kubuntu Dapper to Kubuntu Edgy, and everything so far is as smooth as silk, my biggest problem in Ubuntu Edgy (SMB) didn't happen in Kubuntu Edgy, and works like a dream.

Maybe it's just my system, I dunno, but for me it worked.. - So as of writing this, unless something goes horribly wrong, I think I will be sticking with Kubuntu.
