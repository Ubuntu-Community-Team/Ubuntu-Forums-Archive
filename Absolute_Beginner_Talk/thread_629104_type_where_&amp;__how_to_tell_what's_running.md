### Post by PointyWombat on 2007-12-02
Applications -> Accesories -> Terminal.

You may want to put it on your top panel as you'll find you'll probably use it a lot.

On seeing what's running, in the terminal, type "ps -ef" or "top". For help on most commands, type in the terminal "man <command>",ie: "man top"
