I'd like to create a launcher to go directly to google calendar. How can I do this?

---

### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-11-19
Create a launcher; and in the command section put something like
```
firefox www.google.com
```

Obviously substituting your browser of choice for firefox and your website of choice for [www.google.com](www.google.com)
