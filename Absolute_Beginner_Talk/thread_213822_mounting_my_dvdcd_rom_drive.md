If it plays, it is mounted..your fstab file (shown on your previous post re mounting partitions) has an entry to mount the cdrom.
It will usually mount as soon as a disc is inserted. Some of the behaviour can be controlled from 
System>Preferences>Removable drives and media
And if you then go to Nautilus and right click on the cd rom icon(or press the eject button on the drive) and eject the media it will umount
