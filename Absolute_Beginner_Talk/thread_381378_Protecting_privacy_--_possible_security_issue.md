### Post by russell.h on 2007-03-10
They get that information from your IP address. Theres not much you can do about that, except maybe use [TOR]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tor_(anonymity_network)"), which probably isn't worth your while. At any rate those things aren't particularly accurate. For example I live in Anchorage, Alaska, but for the longest time they IDed my IP as being in North Pole, Alaska.

So I would get all these ads that were like "Meet single women in North Pole, Alaska!" then they have some picture of a chick in a bikini. Needless to say, even if that village of less than 2,000 people has any hot chicks, they are *not* hanging around in bikinis.
