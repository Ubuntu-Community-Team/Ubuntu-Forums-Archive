### Post by jaytek13 on 2008-02-06
Bumping is replying to your own thread. When a thread gets a reply, regardless of who made it, it goes to the top of the forum.

And there isn't necessarily "nothing that can be done." Often times when someone makes a post no one will be on the forums who knows the answer, so bumping a thread 24 hours later will bring it in view of some people who perhaps hadn't seen the thread before.

Although a lot of people do browse the "unanswered posts" option and try to answer the questions that haven't been.
