### Post by Kurt` on 2006-08-02
Ahhh, shared video memory. :(

See if you can't find another 128mb stick of sdram to throw into that machine.

Then it will be perfect. :)

As for the cd-rom just spinning and spinning, try to burn the ISO again at a slower rate, like 20x or something (I'm assuming you downloaded the livecd/install cd).

Alternatively, try booting off the disc in another computer. If it still doesn't work, then it's the cd. If it does work, maybe the cd-rom drive is going on the Compaq.
