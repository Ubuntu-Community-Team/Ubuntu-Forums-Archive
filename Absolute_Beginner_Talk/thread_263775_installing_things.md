### Post by RobsterUK on 2006-09-23
The most important thing to realise is that unlike windows, most software you might need in Ubuntu (including flash) can be downloaded and installed automatically by going to Applications > Add/Remove where you will find a list of many many applications.

So whatever you are looking for, look for it there before going for the method of visiting the software's website and downloading.
