### Post by billgoldberg on 2008-02-03
Put in the ubuntu cd.

In the installer, one of the options will be : use entier disk. Then select partition b. (it could be called another name).

This will erase gutsy from b and then put a fresh gutsy on b. Nothing on a will be touched. And the grub should still work.
