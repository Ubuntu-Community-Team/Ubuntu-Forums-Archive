Well, in my limited experience...DO NOT DEAL WITH BROADCOM CHIPSETS!!
They are a pain in the a$$.

Just do some research and find out which cards use the prism or Atheros chipsets.  They are the most linux frindly.  

I just bough a D-Link DWL-G650 108Mbps wireless card which uses an Atheros chipset and it was nothing short of plug-and-play.  Ive heard that Prism based cards are the best but just do some research and see what you can find.
