### Post by in_flu_ence on 2007-02-17
If the wireless is built-in in your laptop, most likely it should work. At least, I have tried my compaq and lenova laptops and they work fine for me. USB is probably abit risky if u are going for that option but there are ways to get around with it.

I would foresee that the SD/MMS/CF card reader (if there is one) not able to function which happens in my compaq laptop.

Widescreen might need some tweak to optimise its performance at time (but I got mine pretty straight forward). Usually u don't need to do anything unless the screen fails to please u.

Hope it helps. but network wise, laptop might be easier to get things configured if they are built in.
