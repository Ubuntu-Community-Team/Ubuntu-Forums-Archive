### Post by taurus on 2006-06-27
That thing, " user name"@desktop #, is your prompt.  There is no password and you are currently log in as root when you choose the recovery mode!  You can use the recovery mode to fix your machine...  Otherwise, do a normal boot and log in as a regular user.
