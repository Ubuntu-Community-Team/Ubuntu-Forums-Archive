### Post by Mad_Gouki on 2008-02-24
I know of Dgen and gens
Dgen:
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DGen](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DGen)
wikipedia says that Dgen is no longer maintained for any system other than the psp, but that may be incorrect.
also there is dgen/sdl
gens:
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gens_%28emulator%29](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gens_%28emulator%29)
I have used gens before in windows, and it ran everything I had fine, haven't had the chance to try it in ubuntu
[http://gens.consolemul.com/](http://gens.consolemul.com/)

dgen is available in the multiverse repository, gens is available at [http://sourceforge.net/projects/gens/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/gens/)
it seems that you have to compile the source for it, I don't know what the dependencies for it are.

hope this helps!  also, you could try running any win9x genesis/megadrive emulator with wine, hopefully one that is not based on DirectX or anything like that tho.
