### Post by Ripfox on 2008-03-30
You can uninstall TB through synaptic, then go into your home folder and click "view" then "show hidden files" and delete the TB folder, but beware you will lose ALL settings and saved items for TB. I do this all the time when I mess up a program like TB, just to get rid of all the traces before starting over.
