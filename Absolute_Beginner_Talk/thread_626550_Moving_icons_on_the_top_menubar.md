### Post by CatKiller on 2007-11-29
The network tool that appears on the panel is there because that application has put an icon in the Notification Area. (Personally, I hate this, and have removed it from the startup scripts). Look slightly to the left and you will see vertical bars, or dots, or somesuch (depending on your theme) that represent the Notification Area's control. Right-click on that and you can move it around in the usual way.

Since you'd not found the Notification Area previously, it probably still has the "Lock to Panel" attribute set, which is why you can't drag any other icons past it. Clear this attibute and you should be able to put the power applet where you want.
