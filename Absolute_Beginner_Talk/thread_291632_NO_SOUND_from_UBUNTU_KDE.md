### Post by NeoLithium on 2006-11-02
One of the first things you can do to check, is if alsamixer is muting anything that might be causing that.  In a term window: ```
alsamixer
``` and turn the volumes up in there, and ensure nothing is muted; and see if that might have been the cause.
