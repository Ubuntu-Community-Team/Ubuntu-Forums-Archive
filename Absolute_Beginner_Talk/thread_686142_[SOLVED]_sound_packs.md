### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-02-02
> **TechDragon said:**
> the system sounds, like the boot up and shut down sounds.

I'm not aware of any packages for system sounds, but you can put .WAV files in **/usr/share/sounds**, then open 'System->Preferences->Sound' and in the 'Sounds' tab you can select them. You can also change the login sounds in 'System->Administration->Login Window' by going to the 'Accessibility' tab.

EDIT: The package that provides the sounds is **ubuntu-sounds**; there's also **ubuntustudio-sounds**, but I don't know how different the sounds are, and it would require the installation of **ubuntustudio-desktop**.
