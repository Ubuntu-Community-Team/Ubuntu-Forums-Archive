### Post by jargoman on 2007-05-05
I don't know if only your taskbar is missing or the entire panel so I'm going to assume the worst and you don't have a panel at the bottom of your screen.

fist you need to create a panel hopefully you still have a panel at the top of your screen. 

Right click on that then select "new panel." 

you can then click and drag this new panel to the bottom of your screen.

right click on the new panel and select "add to panel.."

a window will pop up and under "desktop and windows" you'll find "window list" select that then click add. 

voila
