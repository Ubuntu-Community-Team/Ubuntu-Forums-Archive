Click the "edit" tab in the Firefox window, then click on "preferences". Afterwards choose the "downloads" tab, then click on "view and edit actions". A window should then open displaying files and their defualt programs. Choose Utorrent for torrent files. 

This doesnt always work depending on your browser version and configuration, but its worth a shot. Clicking on "other" should allow you to navigate to the Utorrent executable using a GUI, but that doesnt seem to be working.
