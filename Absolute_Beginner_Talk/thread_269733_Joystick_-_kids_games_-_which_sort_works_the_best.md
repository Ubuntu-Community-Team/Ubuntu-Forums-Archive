### Post by bluefrog on 2006-10-02
I would say that most of the games which are using arrow keys might be not suited in fact for your 4 years old kid.

My 5 years old kid had problem playing such games last year (when he was 4 then). I kept telling him to use games of his age instead of those "grown-up" games.

I put him on supertux then. I had to play quite a while with him as  combination key (ctrl to go fast and arrow keys were a killer for him at the beginning) so I was asking him to push on ctrl while I was manoeuvering. then I was letting him had a go for a while on its own without telling anything except now and then.

In the end he managed to master (yes master) the keys in no time. Some other games are still hard to get a grip on for him especially the "stars" like game where the ship is travelling a bit everywhere on the screen. but in no way am I pushing him on those games as I think (maybe wrongly...) that there are no for his age yet.

I am afraid that a joystick might be even tougher than arrow keys.
