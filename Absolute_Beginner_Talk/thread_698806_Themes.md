### Post by drdrewdown on 2008-02-16
if you want emerald to load upon startup add the following to your System>Prefs>Sessions

create a new item 

name:   Emerald Themes
command:  emerald --replace

& as long as you have emerald installed & working, it will load emerald at startup

if that doesn't answer your question, be more specific...
