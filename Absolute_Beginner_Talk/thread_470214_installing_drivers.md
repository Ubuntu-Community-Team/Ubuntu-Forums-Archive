### Post by Pixtu on 2007-06-14
There is no reason why you can't search for and download required drivers from any system - the Web is the same for any system.

However, getting them to work is not always straightforward and can involve downloading more files, or at least more info/instructions.:-( So, it is often a good idea whenever possible, for the system that you want to be setup to be connected at the time.

I don't know about your Scanner or DVD, but the iP4300 is certainly not a simple exercise. Some people apparently have it working fine, others are or have struggled.:( It may also be that some people that think they have it working properly are not aware of how it uses the different inks.

I for one am still in the struggling to set up mode but am ever hopeful that I will resolve the problems.:-?

Search the forums for iP4300 and you will see what I mean!
