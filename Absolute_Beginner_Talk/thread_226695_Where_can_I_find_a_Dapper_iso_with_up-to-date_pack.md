Good Morning,
This may help with a fresh install. What I do is to copy everything I have in cache. I use a second hard drive for backups. I guess a person could use a cdrw. Anyway I copy everything from the apt subdirectory and after the install I copy the files to the new install. After reloading the package list I update the files and no more downloads are needed at that time. This saves a lot of downloading time and I have used it many times. I hope it helps.
