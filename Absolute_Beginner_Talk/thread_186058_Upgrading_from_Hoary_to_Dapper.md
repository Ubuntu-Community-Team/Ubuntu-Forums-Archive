### Post by Breuls on 2006-06-02
[QUOTE=Iarwain ben-adar]How come it would brake your system?


Iarwain[/QUOTE]
Because you're missing one step: Breezy Badger. The Breezy upgrade is suitable for Hoary, the Dapper one is suitable for Breezy.

I think it would be okay to upgrade from Hoary to Breezy, then from Breezy to Dapper. But, if you're able to do a fresh install, I'd recommend doing that. It will leave you with a slightly cleaner system.
