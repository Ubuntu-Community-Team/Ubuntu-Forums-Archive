### Post by Valhalla on 2006-08-02
This sometimes happens if there is a glitch between what is called the framebuffer and the videocard.  Stuff is still happening but you just can't see it.  This actually happened to me about 1/2 way through the install on a Dell E1405.  What you can do is just wait a little while and then hit the enter key a few times.  If the disk pops out and the computer reboots then all is well.  If that doesn't happen, wait a little longer and try again.  Obviously, if you have been waiting 4 hours and nothing happens, something went wrong, but until then don't abandon hope.  If you happen to know what kind of videocard is inside the computer, that might help explain what is going on.

Good Luck
