### Post by unfair on 2007-03-27
I am happy to report I solved the overscan/underscan issue - I haven't seen any dual monitor configs dealing with overscan, so here's mine in case someone else needs a reference.

The only difference between this and my previous is changing 1808x1016 to 1808x1016_60.00 in both the Modes and MetaModes section.

Beware, when you use two different resolutions together, it picks the biggest side of each to show in the resolution list (System > Preferences > Screen Resolution)

Example:  I used a monitor at 1808x1016, and one at 1680x1050 - the resolution in the list will be 1808x1050 - the largest width and the height.  It uses the proper w/h on each monitor though.
