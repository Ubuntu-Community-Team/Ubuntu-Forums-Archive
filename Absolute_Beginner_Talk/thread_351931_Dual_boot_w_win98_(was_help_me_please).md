### Post by IYY on 2007-02-02

To install Windows 98, you could boot from floppy, but I think that you can also boot from the CD as well, provided that the CD is at the top of the boot order in the BIOS.

A relevant thread title lets people who know how to help you know that they can help you.  Information is also likely to get you better technical advice than begging.

You want a dual-boot system.  Generally, it's advisable to install Windows before Ubuntu, because Windows' boot loader overwrites Ubuntu's bootloader (an annoyance--MSFT doesn't want more than 1 OS booting on your machine at any one time, imagine).
