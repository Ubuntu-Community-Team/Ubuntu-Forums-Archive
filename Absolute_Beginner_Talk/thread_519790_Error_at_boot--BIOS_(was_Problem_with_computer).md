### Post by misconfiguration on 2007-08-07

Depends on what BIOS version you're using, some are f1, some esc, some f10, f12 or even delete.

### Post by thefoolisme on 2007-08-07
Although BIOS is different on every motherboard, it is usually accessed by hitting <DEL> right after the initial scroll through the RAM, and telling you what drives are on your system.  It usually says something along the lines of "Hit <DEL> for setup".  Sometimes it can be one of the F-keys instead.
