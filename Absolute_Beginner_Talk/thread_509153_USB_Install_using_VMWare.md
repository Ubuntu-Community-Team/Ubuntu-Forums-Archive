### Post by benx009 on 2007-07-25
well, you can install ubuntu to the vmware disk on your external drive if that's what you mean.  however, in order to boot it, you will have to have vmware running.  now if you are just looking to boot ubuntu from your external hard drive right after you turn on your computer, you can install ubuntu to your external hard drive (w/o vmware that is) and then have the dual-boot option available to you at your pc's start-up (ubuntu will let you choose whether you want to boot up linux or windows).  if you have any troubles, post back here.
