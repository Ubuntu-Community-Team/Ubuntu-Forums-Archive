### Post by MrHorus on 2006-08-10
At the login prompt, there should be a menu on the bottom left of the screen marked "Session" or "Sessions" - something like that anyway.

In the menu you can select your window manager and you can change it for the current session only or for all future logins.

That should do it for you :)
