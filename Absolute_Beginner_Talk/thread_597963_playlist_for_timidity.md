### Post by NewDisciple on 2007-11-04

found in synaptic.  It is GUI based karaoke/midi player.
Off topic a little bit, most likely when you requested a response there was no one in that section of the forums who
could answer your question.  You will find that patience, respect and less attitude are virtues which will achieve better

enough for you try Google.  There is a ton of information out there if you are willing to look.
