### Post by daryll on 2007-10-27
Connect to [http://127.0.0.1:4080](http://127.0.0.1:4080)

Click Servers -> Import server.met.

Paste the following link:

[http://ed2k.2x4u.de/48tmaugh/min/server.met](http://ed2k.2x4u.de/48tmaugh/min/server.met)

You can also manually add the servers into the mldonkey gui client.

Also in the settings for mldonkey network in the mldonkey gui:

Select / enable:

update_server_list_client
update_server_list_server
update_server_list_server_met
