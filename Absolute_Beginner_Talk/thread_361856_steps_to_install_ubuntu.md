There's a wealth of information at psychocats. However, here's something short and sweet. 

Firstly, you need some unpartitioned space on your hard drive. Free space in your Windows partition is not enough. Either that, or you need another hard drive.

Go to Ubuntu.com, download the distribution, and burn it to a CD. Insert the CD and install.
