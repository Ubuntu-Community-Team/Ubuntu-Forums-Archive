### Post by 23meg on 2006-11-24
> what exactly is beryl?
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beryl_%28window_manager%29](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beryl_%28window_manager%29)

It's a window manager that makes use of compositing (a compositing manager as well). GNOME and KDE are complete desktop environments with their own window managers.
