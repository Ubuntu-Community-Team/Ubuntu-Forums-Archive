### Post by freebird54 on 2007-05-05
When you ran the dpkg-reconfigure, the last thing it will have done before writing the 'updated' file, is to make a backup copy of xorg.conf.  The name of this file will be similar to

```
/etc/X11/xorg.conf.20070316075924
```

Where the number at the end will consist of the date/time of creation.  If you look in there you will find out what settings were in place OTHER than the touchpad settings.  From there you can remove your 'changes' in the original, or update the 'new' file with the video settings you need.

This should be the easiest route back to full function.  IF you need specifics on how to accomplish this=- just repost with your questions :)
