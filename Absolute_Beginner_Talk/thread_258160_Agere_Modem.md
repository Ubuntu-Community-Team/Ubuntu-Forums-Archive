### Post by ieee488 on 2006-09-15
It sounds like your PC is a fairly new one.

In all likelihood, the modem is a Winmodem and won't work with Ubuntu.

My suggestion is that you buy an external SERIAL modem. Brand new they are about $40US. There are used ones on eBay for about $10-15US.

There are also some internal modems that work with Linux. My old PC that I bought in 1999 had one of these. They also come up for sale on eBay from time to time.
