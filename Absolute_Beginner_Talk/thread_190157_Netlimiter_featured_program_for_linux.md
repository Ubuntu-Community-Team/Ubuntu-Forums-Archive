### Post by Flyn on 2006-06-06
As far as bandwidth shapers go in linux, you have trickle, which is program specific and can't be adjusted after the program is run, and wondershaper, which is interface specific, and will most likely be of less use to you. You can find plenty of network _monitors_ in the repos, just look them up.
 
 
A word of caution, trickle, at least on my system, makes programs more likely to lock up frequently.
