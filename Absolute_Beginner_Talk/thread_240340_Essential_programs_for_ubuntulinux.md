### Post by Kilz on 2006-08-20
Do yourself a favor and take your time installing things. Learn a little. Setup scripts are nice, but you may end up with a lot of things you dont need or will never use.
Things I find I use a lot are

K3b - buring software
screem - for writing php
k9copy - for backing up DVD's
vmware - its alwasy fun to mess around with other Os's without commiting to installing them to hard disk. Its also great for testing out software.
