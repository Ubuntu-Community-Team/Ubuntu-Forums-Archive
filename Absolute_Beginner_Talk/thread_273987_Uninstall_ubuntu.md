### Post by wirelessmonkey on 2006-10-09
You probably don't actually have 2 installs, you probably have 2 kernel configurations... one (probably the default) is your main install, the other will be a safe configuration that allows you to access your machine if something horrible has happened in your main installation. There won't be 2 copies of all applications, just kernel stuff, which doesn't take up enough space to worry about.


Good Luck
