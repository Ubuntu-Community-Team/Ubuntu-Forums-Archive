### Post by mostwanted on 2006-08-22
Metacity themes, Gtk+ themes and Icons: open [COLOR="DarkGreen"]*System &#8594; Administration &#8594; Themes*[/COLOR] and drag and drop your theme file onto it [COLOR="Red"]without unzipping them first[/COLOR]! ### Post by aysiu on 2006-08-22
Be sure not to unzip those .tar.gz files before dragging and dropping them.

Drag and drop them as is (exactly how you downloaded them).

Do not do anything to the files beforehand. Do not double-click them. Do not run any commands on them. Just drag and drop them to System > Preferences > Themes
