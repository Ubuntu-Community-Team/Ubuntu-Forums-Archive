### Post by o_fortuna on 2006-07-12
The way to remove the games is to go to System--Administration-->Synaptic. Then, look for the games you want to remove.

If you want to remove the games from the menu and not from the system entirely (they don't take much space anyway), you could just use the menu editor (Applications--Accessories--Menu Editor) to remove the entries for the games and utilities you don't like.
