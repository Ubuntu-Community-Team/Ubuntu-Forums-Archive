### Post by hajk on 2006-10-13
> **mendingo84 said:**
> what's the role of this partition?

Holds any stuff in memory when your installed memory isn't enough.
The swap space may be in its own partition (default in Ubuntu), but can also be a file.

Note: if you have lots of memory installed, you may not even need swap space. In my case, with 2GB memory installed, I haven't ever used the swap space, not even when doing some heavy compiling in rival Linux distribution Gentoo.
