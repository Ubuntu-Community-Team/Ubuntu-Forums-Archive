### Post by Kobalt on 2007-03-16
I never ever heard of any spyware on Linux, and very few viruses that were "actual viruses", not just proofs of concept...
If you don't touch any IPtables (the Ubuntu firewall) settings then you shouldn't need such clam av softwares. 
The main point over security on a Linux box is the strength of your passwords.
