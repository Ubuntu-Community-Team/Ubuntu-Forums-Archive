### Post by davideckels on 2007-10-12
The cellphone has a MicroSD card built in: that's what is detected as a mass storage device. Unfortunately I don't have a USB cable to directly replicate your results.
I do know:
1. How to connect via bluetooth for SMS and quick file transfers to/from the phone such as camera pics, etc. Setup the phone this way [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=547349](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=547349)  
 and you'll be able to do those things.
2. Using a MicroSD to SD adapter and then an SD card reader, you should be able to move anything you want directly onto the flash card, thence into the phone. The only limitation will be whether the phone knows what to do with it.

Hope that helps.
