### Post by janascii on 2007-05-28
If you really want an anti-virus you can check out AVG... I believe they have a linux version of their software.  There is also a program liscensed under the GPL called [Clam AV]("http://www.clamav.net/").  If you have automatix installed you can install it and a firewall as a package.  Typically what I have found is that there really aren't a lot of viruses you come along during normal use.  Seeing how you are using linux, I assume you understand safe browsing habits.  I typically just watch what I'm doing and don't bother with the anti-virus.
