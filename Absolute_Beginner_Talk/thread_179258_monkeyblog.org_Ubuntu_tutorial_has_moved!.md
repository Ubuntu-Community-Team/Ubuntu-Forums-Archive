### Post by catlett on 2006-05-20
[QUOTE=mostwanted]It's just a post to move the topic to the top of the list.[/QUOTE]
To further explain. When threads are posted in the forum they are set up in the order of newest reply or start of a thread. If noone responds to a thread it will eventually end up a few pages back or more. If you want to get the thread to the first page again (to bump it up) you  enter a new reply. You have to enter something for the reply to occur. You can't submit a blank reply. So "bump" has become the status quo word to enter to bump your thread up to the first page,
In this case mostwanted wants people to be informed about the link moving. Most people only look at the first few pages. If it goes past 2 or 3 he can bump it up to the front page so people can see it and be informed.
