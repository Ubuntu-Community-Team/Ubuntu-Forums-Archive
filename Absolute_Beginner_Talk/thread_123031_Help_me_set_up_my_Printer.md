### Post by cmongini on 2006-01-29
i just setup my printer ( took me two hours)...canon has linux drivers for japan that you could adapt...i suggest you read [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=38995](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=38995)
i got mine (ip3000) installed by following the instructions. 
instead of downloading the driver from the recommended links ( your printer seems not to be one of the three listed) go directly to [ftp://download.canon.jp/pub/driver/bj/linux/](ftp://download.canon.jp/pub/driver/bj/linux/) and look if you can find the files relative to your printer. then follow the instructions in the thread and good luck!
