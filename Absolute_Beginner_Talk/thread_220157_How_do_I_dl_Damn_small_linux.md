### Post by richbarna on 2006-07-21
I use Damn Small all the time, it's my favourite micro-distro. When you get the iso, right click on it and select "open with" and choose your burning software. Burn the iso at 4X if you can or if not 8X, the slower the better for iso's.
Shove it in your cd-rom drive and shut down the computer.
Reboot and Damn Small should start. I've got the BIOS to start from CDROM first, you may just have to press the "c" key. Then just follow the on screen instructions.

You may want to have a look at Dyne:Bolic as well, it's pretty slick for a micro-distro. Easy to find, just google Dyne:Bolic.

Good luck:)
