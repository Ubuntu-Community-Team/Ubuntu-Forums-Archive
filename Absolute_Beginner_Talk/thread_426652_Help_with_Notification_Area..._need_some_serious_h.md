### Post by kyphi on 2007-05-04
All items on the panels are movable after you have unlocked them (right click on the icon and scroll to "lock to panel" to remove the checkmark.  Don't forget to lock them in position when you have decided where they should stay.

If you have lost icons from the panel, you can right click on the panel, left click on "Add to Panel", select "Notification Area".

Does that cover it?

---

### Post by kyphi on 2007-05-04
One more thing:

If you want to remove the blank space between the left and right top panels, right click on the top panel, go to "Properties", "Background" and check "None (use system theme)" and your panel will be in one piece again.
