### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-05-03
To start off, you'll need another panel.  Right-click your bottom panel and select New Panel.  This should place the panel at the top (if not just drag it up there).  Then we need to add the applets.   Right click on your newly created panel and select Add to Panel.  We have to add the quit button, clock, network monitor, volume control and menu bar applets.  I believe thats the correct order from right to left.  To add application launchers to the panel, you can drag and drop them from the menu to the panel.

Good luck!
