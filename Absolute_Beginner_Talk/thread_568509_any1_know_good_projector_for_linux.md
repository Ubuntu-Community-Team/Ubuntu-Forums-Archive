### Post by HermanAB on 2007-10-05
Pretty much any projector, since they come with a separate remote control.

Light Processing devices are brighter and will probably last longer.  LCD units are less bright and cheaper.  However, look at the cost of the replacement bulbs.  That is far more important than the projector.  A cheap projector with an expensive short lived bulb is somewhat like buying an ink jet printer...

Cheers,

Herman
