### Post by benuski on 2006-11-20
I like using Banshee with my iPod.  When you have all the right codecs installed, all the ones that are necessary just to play the music, it can take you files and transcode them as you put them onto your iPod.  This allows me to store all of my music on my hard drive on ogg vorbis (the open source audio format) and then when I load it onto my iPod, Banshee automatically converts it into mp3, so it can be playable.


(Actually, thats what I used to do.  Now I just use Rockbox, which is FOSS firmware that basically replaces Apple's firmware.  Its a little buggy still, but that doesn't bother me)
