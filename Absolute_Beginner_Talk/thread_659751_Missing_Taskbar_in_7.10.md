### Post by TBerben on 2008-01-06
Assuming you are in Gnome just right click the remaining panel and select "add panel" from the menu. Once the panel is back in place right click it, select "add to panel" and add the "show desktop", "workspace switcher" and "trash" buttons and it should look like before.
