### Post by ChrisTheGeek on 2005-12-02
Hi,

This page will explain how to get the extra codecs you need for audio/video working in ubuntu.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats)

As for MSN, any recent version of MSN (i.e. the last couple of years) will store all the contacts on the server.  This means that when you add the account to GAIM all her contacts should be available since they are read off Microsoft's servers.  If they aren't showing up, perhaps it is because of a setting in GAIM.  I  believe by default, offline users aren't shown.  You can make it always show all contacts if that is preferred - just look in the program's settings dialog.

Hope this helps,
Chris
