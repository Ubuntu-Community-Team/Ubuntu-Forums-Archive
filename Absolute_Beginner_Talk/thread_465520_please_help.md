### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-06-05
I see. What you want is called Remote Desktop Access, where you can control her entire machine from a connection secured over the net. I'm not really an expert at this, I've only done it a handful of times and all over windows with basic software. 

I can point you in the right direction though, there are two major protocols for it [VNC]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VNC") and [RDP.]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Remote_Desktop_Protocol") RDP is only for windows I think via microsoft services, but you can get programs on *NIX machines that interact it with it methinks.

[The list of apps are here. ]("http://linuxappfinder.com/internetandnetworking/remoteaccess")You'll have to research them on your own, I can't really be more helpful than that. I do all my helping of people via the forum, and when needed I use screenshots to make a point :).

If you have a windows machine and need a quick solution, I think I've heard good things about[ logmein.]("https://secure.logmein.com/home.asp") There is a free service, I believe its only windows though.

Have fun, hope it works out :). Oh and I hope I was helpful.
