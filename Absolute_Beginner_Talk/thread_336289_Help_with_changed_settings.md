### Post by MontanaMax on 2007-01-11
While I don't know how to get the icon back on the screen for you, the keyboard has all the shortcuts you need to get around and between desktops.

I think the ones you'll be most interested in are:

    *  Rotate cube 

    Ctrl+Alt+Left/Right Arrow Switch desktops on cube; 
    Ctrl+Shift+Alt+Left/Right Arrow Send the active window to the left/right workspace 
    Ctrl+Alt+Left-click and grab Rotate cube manually 

The full list of shortcuts is at [http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Tips/Default_Commands](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Tips/Default_Commands)
