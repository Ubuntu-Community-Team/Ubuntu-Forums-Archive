### Post by qamelian on 2006-12-30
> **Christopher said:**
> I was wondering IF i can change the default sounds with other wave files i downloaded off the internet & IF so where do i put them?:confused:    Thank you in advance.

Right-click on the gaim icon in the notification area and click on Preferences in the menu. Go to the Sound tab of the Preferences dialog. In the section labeled Sound Events, select the sound you want to change, then click on the Choose button to open the file selection box. Locate the sound you want to use and double-click on it. Repeat as necessary.
