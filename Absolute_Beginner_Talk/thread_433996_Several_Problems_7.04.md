### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-05
> **RogerDavis said:**
> I just did a fresh install of 7.04, and there are things happening that I don't know how to approach.

1) No icons/launchers appear on the desktop at all, even for added programs

2) I can't find a file manager 

3) How do I install Seamonkey for all users?

1) Ummm, you have to manually make a shortcut/launcher appear on the desktop. By default it only goes in your application menu. Click applications, find the program you want and then right click on it and click "Add this launcher to desktop". Alternatively, choose add to panel and it will appear next to the evolution and firefox icons in the task bar at top.

2) To browse your files just click places and home folder, then use the up and back keys to browse around.

3) [Here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=186011&highlight=seamonkey") you go.

Have fun with Ubuntu, I prefer firefox :)
