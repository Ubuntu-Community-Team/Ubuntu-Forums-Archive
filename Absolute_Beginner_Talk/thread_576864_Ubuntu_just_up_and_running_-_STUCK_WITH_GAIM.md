### Post by Samhain13 on 2007-10-15
The "local alias" is only for replacing your username with an alias in your conversations window. Say if my Yahoo ID is "fgafgas.asdgasdg" but I had a local alias "Pedro", my conversations will appear as:

(04:24:59) Pedro: Hey! How's it going?
(04:25:10) My_Chatmate: I'm fine.

If I don't have a local alias:

(04:24:59) fgafgas.asdgasdg: Hey! How's it going?
(04:25:10) My_Chatmate: I'm fine.
