### Post by BandD on 2008-03-31
Try double-clicking on your volume control in the upper panel (the little speaker icon).  On the PLAYBACK tab look to see if you have a microphone setting. If you do, make sure it is muted.  That should solve the problem of you hearing your self.  

Then look at your RECORDING tab and make sure the levels are all reasonably high on any option you have there.

You can also enable more options by going into Edit-->Preferences and checking all the things that apply to you.

Hope that helps!
