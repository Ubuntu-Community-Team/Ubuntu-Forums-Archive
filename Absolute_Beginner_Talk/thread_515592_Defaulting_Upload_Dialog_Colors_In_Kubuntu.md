### Post by apswartz on 2007-08-02
Did you use your System Settings to change the colors?

Go to the **Start** Menu and select** System Settings **(pic 1)

Under** Look and Feel** select **Appearance** (pic 2)

Select **Colors** and choose a theme and press **apply** to fix the colors (pic 3)
