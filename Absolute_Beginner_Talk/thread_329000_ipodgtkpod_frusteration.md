### Post by Bright Hammer on 2006-12-31
Hi i had the same problem. I will tell you what i did however i am not sure if this will work for you.

1.Dock your ipod and sut down the default handeler if it is not GTKPOD.
2.Start GTKPOD
3.Highlight the ipod on the left menu bar.
4.Use the buttons at the top add the files or directory where you have your music. (Ogg wont work)
5.When your done hit the sync button.(This takes a while because it has to build the playlist db. Look in the lower left to see what GTKPOD is doing while you are syncing)
6.Undock and play!


I hope this helps. I just moved over to Ubuntu this week. I have to say it is pretty sweet. 

P.S look into installing frostwire ( Automatix is the way to go)
