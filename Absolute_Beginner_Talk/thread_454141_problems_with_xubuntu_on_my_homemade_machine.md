### Post by dstew on 2007-05-25
Wait a bit before you give up. When you boot, you said you see the screen, with lots of messages and [ok] marks, correct? If so, you might only have a graphical user interface configuration problem.

When you get to the black screen, have you tried ctrl-alt-F1? Also, at the grub menu, have you tried to boot in recovery mode? These things should get you to a command-line interface.
