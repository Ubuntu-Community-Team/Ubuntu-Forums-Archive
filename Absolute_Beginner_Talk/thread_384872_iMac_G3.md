### Post by ShareBuntu on 2007-03-19
Finally received my iMac G3 today. Like most eBay purchases there were a few bumps. Instead of 128 MB of ram, it only contains 64. Rather than having a 10 GB hard drive, it only has a 7 GB hard drive. However, despite these shortcomings it was still a bargain.

So, I booted it up to check if everything was running okay. Tried Mac OS 9.x and wasn't too impressed at all. I restarted and booted from the Xubuntu 6.10 Alternate CD. Installation went fine except for a part where it hung on trying to configure anthy. I killed the process and the installation completed without any problems. The entire process took about an hour and a half to complete.

Xubuntu is really impressive and everything seems to be working just fine. I'm updating to Feisty at the moment and will report back on my experience. So far I'm a very happy camper. :)
