### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-01-28
I believe it can take a few weeks or even more sometimes to get a cd.  If you can download everything, I would request that you don't order copies because they already struggle to keep up with demand, and the discs should go to those who can't get them otherwise.
A new version of Ubuntu is released every 6 months, so having a ShipIt disc doesn't do you much good anyway because by the time you get it, it's almost (or is already) outdated.
They will ship to wherever you have them ship it, so if you get your mail in a mailbox, that's where it will go.
