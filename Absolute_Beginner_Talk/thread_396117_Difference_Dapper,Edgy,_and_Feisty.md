### Post by kahrytan on 2007-03-29
6.06 LTS has Long Term Support so it will continue to have security updates until June 2009. 


Edgy and Feisty Fawn are newer but lack LTS. They do however have upgraded software that is unavailable in Dapper.

From personal experience, I experienced a performance upgrade when I went from Dapper to Edgy.  Dapper was sluggish while Edgy wasn't.

### Post by insane_alien on 2007-03-29
ubuntu uses names for versions it also has numbers based on the release date.

dapper is 6.06
edgy is 6.10
fiesty will be 7.04

the differences are that the later versions have updated packages and extra features. they also tend to be a tad faster.

If everything works fine for you then you should probably stay where you are. upgrading could possibly lead to problems although it is unlikely. if your going to upgrade to feisty,  you should probably just do a clean install(do you have a separate /home partition? its easy if you have this.) its quicker than upgrading through edgy.
