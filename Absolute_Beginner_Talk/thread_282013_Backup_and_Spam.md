### Post by aysiu on 2006-10-22
My guess is the Evolution itself has spam filtering built in, but if it doesn't, use Thunderbird--that has a junk mail filter you can train, and it works pretty damn well. It takes about a week to train it (marking messages as junk/not junk), but after that, it's golden with few exceptions.

For backup, look into *rsync*. I've never used it over a network, but I believe it has that capability.
