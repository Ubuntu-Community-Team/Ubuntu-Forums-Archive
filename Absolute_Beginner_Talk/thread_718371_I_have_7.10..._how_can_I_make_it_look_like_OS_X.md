### Post by seagullplayer77 on 2008-03-08
You can install an icon dock called Cairo Dock, and you can customize that to look like a Mac OS X dock. There's a really nice tutorial on how to get it installed here: 

[http://thedailyubuntu.blogspot.com/2008/02/cairo-dock-animated-launch-bar-for_03.html](http://thedailyubuntu.blogspot.com/2008/02/cairo-dock-animated-launch-bar-for_03.html)

After you get everything installed OK, you've just gotta mess around with it a little bit and you'll get something pretty cool. I would recommend updating it after you download it, too. Hope it works out for you!
