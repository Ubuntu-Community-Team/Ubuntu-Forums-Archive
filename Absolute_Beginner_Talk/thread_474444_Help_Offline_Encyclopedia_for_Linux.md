### Post by ADT on 2007-07-17
The full wikipedia site is about 15 GBs. Too heavy for home offline use in my opinion.

However there is a light version of the wikipedia website suitable for children with high quality articles that you can get from SOS Childrens website:

[wikipedia-for-schools]("http://www.soschildrensvillages.org.uk/charity-news/wikipedia-for-schools.htm")

It's about 2.5 GBs and once extracted is easy to use, you just click on index.htm.
