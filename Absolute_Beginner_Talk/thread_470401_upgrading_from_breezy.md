### Post by Seisen on 2007-06-10
You can upgrade from breezy if you have a fast connection and a lot of patience. You can do a dist-upgrade from breezy>dapper>edgy>feisty in that order. These guides will help you out.

[http://www.cyberciti.biz/tips/howto-upgrade-from-ubuntu-linux-breezy-to-dapper.html]("http://www.cyberciti.biz/tips/howto-upgrade-from-ubuntu-linux-breezy-to-dapper.html")

[http://www.debianadmin.com/upgrade-ubuntu-dapper-to-ubuntu-edgy-eft.html]("http://www.debianadmin.com/upgrade-ubuntu-dapper-to-ubuntu-edgy-eft.html")

[http://www.ubuntugeek.com/upgrade-ubuntu-610-edgy-eft-to-ubuntu-704-feisty-fawn-2.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/upgrade-ubuntu-610-edgy-eft-to-ubuntu-704-feisty-fawn-2.html)

I personally have updated from dapper>edgy>feisty in a whole day and had no problems, but this doesn't mean that something won't go wrong with the upgrades.
