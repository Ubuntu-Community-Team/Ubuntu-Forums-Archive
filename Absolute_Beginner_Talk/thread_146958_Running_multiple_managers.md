This has probably been asked before but is there a way to switch between kde gnome and xfce safely on one system?

---

### Post by bluevoodoo1 on 2006-03-19
Yes, with the gnome-display-manager aka gdm (or kdm/xdm if kubuntu/xfce are your flavors of choice). When you login, you can choose which session you want to start, either gnome, kde or xfce. I've had all 3 desktop environments on my system, logging into them with the gdm with no problems.
