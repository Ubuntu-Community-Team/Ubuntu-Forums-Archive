For what it's worth, I'm using a Winmodem and dial-up right now. In Australia, most winmodems are based on either  "Lucent" or "Conexant" chipsets. Drivers abound for Lucent, but until very recently, if you had a Conexant based modem, you were SOL.
All fixed now, thanks to Dell putting the Conexant drivers on their site.
<http://direct2dell.com/one2one/archive/2007/07/17/21325.aspx>
