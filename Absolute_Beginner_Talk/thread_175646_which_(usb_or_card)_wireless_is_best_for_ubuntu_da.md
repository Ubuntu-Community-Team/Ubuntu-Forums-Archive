### Post by igknighted on 2006-12-10
As a general rule, USB wireless and linux do not get along well.  I just bought a Belkin F5D7010 (ver. 5001) and it works absolutely perfectly with ndiswrapper.  If you get a card with an Aetheros chipset there is the madwifi drivers you can use, and there are some (rather hard to find) cards that have native (and open) linux drivers, so if you look really hard you could find one.
