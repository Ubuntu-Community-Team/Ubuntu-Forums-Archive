### Post by Charlie Chick on 2007-04-23
Hello Everybody,

I have a strange problem with gtkpod. When transferring tracks into a playlist the first lot goes in in the correct order. If I then transfer another lot of tracks into that same playlist, they go in in reverse order. I have to manually re-arrange the tracks and, in a long opera, that's a pain in the ****. Can anybody suggest a way of making all the tracks transfer in the correct order, please?

Thanks.

---

### Post by lluisanunez on 2007-04-23
Did you check the right sorting options? They are at Edit >> Sorting
you need to select a sorting field or else "auto store". When the playlist is in the right order, do Edit >> Save displayed track order (Shift+CTRL+S). Then sync
