### Post by mattismyname on 2008-01-18
In my experience there are far more problem reports on the forums of NVidia cards than ATI.  I personally have an older NVidia card and I can say the drivers are definitely not perfect, but they do work most of the time for compiz and some 3d FPS games.

For CPU, Intel dual-core would be the way to go.  They have the price/performance lead right now for most of the upper end.  AMD can be decent if you have a specific need for fast floating-point performance, but most apps do not benefit from FP speed.
