Wow! you're doing the homework yourself and you have a really good attitude.. nice. I wish everyone would do that.

I use Ubuntu Dapper 6.06.1 LTS on 11 computers - 5 are servers. the only problem I had was how to change the main menu icon. This is the one Linux distro I have seen that is truly stable and problem-free. I didn't have such success with Edgy so I went back to Dapper. I always recommend the following to any user:
