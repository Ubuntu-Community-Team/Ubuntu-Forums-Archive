### Post by macogw on 2007-10-08
System > Preferences > Keyboard and enable the US International with Dead Keys.  Then right click on your panel and add the keyboard indicator.  When you need funny characters, click on the keyboard indicator in your panel and your keyboard layout will change.  To get á type AltGr (the right alt key) +a.  É is AltGr+E.  í ó ú all the same way... ñ is altgr+n....  ¡¿ are also altgr + their right-side-up versions.
