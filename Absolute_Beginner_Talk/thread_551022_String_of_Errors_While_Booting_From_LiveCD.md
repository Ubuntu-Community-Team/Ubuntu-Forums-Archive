### Post by CaptainInsaneO on 2007-09-14
Go to the Ubuntu download page for directions on how to verify the md5 checksum on your iso. If that is good, reburn another copy of your disc at the SLOWEST burn speed your cd writer supports. If the md5 sum doesn't check out, download another copy of your .iso and burn it.
