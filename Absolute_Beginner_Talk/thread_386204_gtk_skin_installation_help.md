### Post by Kobalt on 2007-03-16
You have to place it in the ./themes folder inside you /home. Can't see it ? Of course, it's a hidden folder :) Press Ctrl+H to see hidden folders, drop your theme (as a folder) inside ./themes and then press Ctrl+H to hide them back. 
And voilà, your new theme is available in System > Pref. > Themes 
(you may need to press Custom to be able to chose your theme)
