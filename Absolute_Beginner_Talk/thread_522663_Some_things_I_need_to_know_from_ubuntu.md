### Post by original_jamingrit on 2007-08-10
[http://gparted.sourceforge.net/larry/resize/resizing.htm](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/larry/resize/resizing.htm)

This might help.  I suggest trying it out on the livecd if possible.

In gparted, (as long as you don't touch the flags), you can fiddle around with partition sizes without making any permanent changes.  You use gparted to queue up your changes, and then press Apply.

You may want to back up important stuff first, though.  Better safe then sorry.  And also, make sure you have emptied your windows partition accordingly and then defrag it before resizing.
