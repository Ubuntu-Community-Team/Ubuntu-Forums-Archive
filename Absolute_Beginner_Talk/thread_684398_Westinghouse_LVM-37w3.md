### Post by chewearn on 2008-05-14
I found out that in most cases of nvidia driver and EDID problem (that I have seen), adding the appropriate parameters for horizontal and vertical frequencies into xorg.conf solves the problem.

Here is a sample from my xorg.conf:
```

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier  "Monitor0"
    VendorName  "Unknown"
    ModelName   "LPL"
    HorizSync    30.0 - 70.0
    VertRefresh  50.0 - 60.0
EndSection

```
