### Post by NewJack on 2007-04-05
Hey Gif,
  
  I am new to Ubuntu also and decided to use Dapper LTS for now until Feisty comes out in a couple of weeks.  

  I would say for a browser go with Firefox.  I've been using it for a while now (even in XP) and have no complaints.  There are tons of add-ons to help customize your browsing experience.

  As far as a word processor goes, I recommend either Abiword or OpenOffice.  The OpenOffice suite is installed by default in Dapper (I'm sure it is in Edgy and Feisty too) and is comparable to MS Office, with a spreadsheet, draw and presentation programs.  You can open MS Office files in it also.  If you don't need a full office suite, just a word processor, then Abiword is a good way to go.

  I also recommend going to this site: [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php) and make sure to check out this page on the site to plan your partition scheme, [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partitioning](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partitioning)

Good luck and enjoy!
Joe
