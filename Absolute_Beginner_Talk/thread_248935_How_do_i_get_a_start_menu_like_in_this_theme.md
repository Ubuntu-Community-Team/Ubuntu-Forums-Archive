### Post by jordanmthomas on 2006-09-01
If you are referring to the panel background, you can get it [here]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=44425")

Download it and extract the image somewhere.

Just right click on the panel and go to properties.  Click on the Background tab and you will be able to select an image to use as the background on the panel.
