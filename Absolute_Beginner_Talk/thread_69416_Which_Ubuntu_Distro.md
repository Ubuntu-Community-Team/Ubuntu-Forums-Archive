### Post by doobit on 2005-09-27
I just tried Breezy Colony 5 and it works great with only minor bugs. I think the Breezy final release will be available in a couple of weeks. The 64 bit version is well supported, just doesn't have as many apps in 64 bit, but the AMD chips run most 32 apps just fine with little to no teasing, even with the 64 bit kernel. You will need all the 32 bit libraries. 

BTW, if you have installed the Breezy preview, the repository upgrades will bring you into the release without needing to reinstall.
