### Post by grsing on 2006-06-09
RAM can write to a DVD, ROM can only read them.  There isn't really any difference between + and - (I'd just get one that does both, most do these days, and then you can just buy whichever disks are cheaper at the moment).  They should all work fine; is the ISO image a CD image or a DVD image (simple question, I know, just making sure).  Just to clarify, RAM, +, and - are all different formats of recording to DVDs, just make sure you get the right media for your drive.
