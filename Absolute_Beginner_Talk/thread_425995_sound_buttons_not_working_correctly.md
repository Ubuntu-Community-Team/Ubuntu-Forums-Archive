### Post by DezSP on 2007-04-28
Go into System -> Preferences -> Sound, then select PCM (and PCM only) in the list near the bottom of the page.

Edit: Oh, and you can right click on the volume button in the tray and go to Preferences to set that to PCM too (I don't think it defaults to that -- could get confusing).
