### Post by crawfjs on 2006-06-08
I'm rather new to linux, as well as, Ubuntu.  My question is, where do I find the Syslogs?  I installed Ubuntu on my Inspiron 9300 and I saw a blog that stated that after installing on their 9300, the person had problems where the cd rom kept polling, which ended up causing errors and filling up the syslogs.  I was just curious to make sure that this wasn't happening to me.

Thank you.

---

### Post by darkali on 2006-06-09
It's on Applications > System Tools > System Log.
Then you will see various logs. Including 'syslog'.
