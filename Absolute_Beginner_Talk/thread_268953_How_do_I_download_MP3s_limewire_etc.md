### Post by Hospadar on 2007-08-07
I would suggest using bittorrent, You can get at the same kind of content as limewire, but it's a lot harder to trace, and often you can find more obscure stuff.
I use azureus for this ```
sudo apt-get install azureus
``` then you'll have to go to a site like the pirate bay to find torrents, download the .torrent files you want, open them with azureus.

Depending on your network setup though, bittorrent may be a little finnickey, if that's the case you will probably have better luck with limewire.
