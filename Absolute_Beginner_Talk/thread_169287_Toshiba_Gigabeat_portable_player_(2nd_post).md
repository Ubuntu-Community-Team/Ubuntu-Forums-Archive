### Post by Afkpuz on 2007-11-16
My advice is to switch to rockbox on your gigabeat.  If you have any audio already on the gigabeat, read my post here to pull it off.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=358663&highlight=gigabeat](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=358663&highlight=gigabeat)

Then, goto the rockbox site to install

[http://www.rockbox.org/twiki/bin/view/Main/WebHome](http://www.rockbox.org/twiki/bin/view/Main/WebHome)

Once you get rockbox set up, linux can access it just fine.  and don't forget to download a theme for rockbox.  By default, rockbox is just text.
