### Post by Paqman on 2007-10-30
Your problem is most likely due to a known bug in the Nvidia drivers. There's a few threads around dealing with this, such as:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=585714](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=585714)

If you can install Envy you can get the latest beta driver, which may solve the problem. As a practical measure in the meantime you can also edit your xorg.conf file to switch drivers from nvidia to nv. 

```
sudo nano /etc/X11/xorg.conf
```

You can do that from the command line in recovery mode if it's really bad. It'll disable your desktop effects and reduce your screen resolution, but it should give you a stable platform to work from.
