### Post by tchoklat on 2007-01-31
you could start by looking in <System> <administration><device manager> and look for the Ethernet card 

Then go into <System> <administration><network settings> to see if your modem is configured. If not configure it, if it is, unconfigure it and then reconfigure it

That is what I did when I had satellite broadband connected recently
