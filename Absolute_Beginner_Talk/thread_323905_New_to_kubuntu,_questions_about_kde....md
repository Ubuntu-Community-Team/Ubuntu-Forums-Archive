### Post by msak007 on 2006-12-22
Welcome to Kubuntu! I've never used irssi, but I can help you with the toggling of Desktops.

1. In your KMenu you'll have an entry for System Settings, which is Kubuntu's customized interface for KControl. Launch it and you'll see a module for "Keyboard & Mouse". 

2. Once the module loads, in the left hand sidebar click on "Keyboard Shortcuts" (at the top in the "Global Shortcuts" tab). The entry you're looking for is "Walk Through Desktops". Highlight it and click on the button in the "Shortcut for Selected Action" section at the bottom and assign it the key combo you want.
