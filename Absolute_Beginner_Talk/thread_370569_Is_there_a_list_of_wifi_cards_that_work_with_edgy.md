Suggestion:  Look into a solution such as this:

[http://www.dlink.com/products/?pid=292](http://www.dlink.com/products/?pid=292)

This is the Dlink DWL-2100ap.  Similar products may be available from other companies.

It is a multimode wireless box.  Set it to Client mode.  I use these with my desktop systems.  I never have to mess with wireless drivers.  It connects to your NIC port via included cable.  The only driver you have to worry about is the one for your NIC.  Linux won't even know it is working via wireless.  You control wireless via a web page.  Works very well here.
