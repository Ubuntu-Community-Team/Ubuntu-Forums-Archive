### Post by Junglizer on 2008-03-26
Slackware: 'powerful' and very stable. Use ReiserFS and recompile to remove unneeded modules and build everything in. [http://www.slackware.org]("http://www.slackware.org")

Or Gentoo: (I just use a knoppix cd for the minimal install, or the minimal install cd) write everything by hand and compile your kernel as you see fit. [http://www.gentoo.org]("http://www.gentoo.org")
