### Post by morphodone on 2006-12-10
If your burner can handle both then don't worry about which one.

I have used mainly +RW but I don't know of any reason one is better 
than the other.

The only problem that can occur is burning movie DVD's and having
them play in a DVD player.  One format may be better in that case.

Not for linux DVD's though.
