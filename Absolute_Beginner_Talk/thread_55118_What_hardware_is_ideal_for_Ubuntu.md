Both are good. What matters more is the sound card (creative stuff works good, I recommend and old cheap sound blaster live), serial ATA controller (I can give you a link to a perfect one if you want for around $20), motherboard (I think the VIA are the worst), network card (most work, the cheapest are the best), modems (win modems suck in Linux, you have to get a REAL external modem), wireless card (hardest thing to shop for, but I can help if you want), webcam (none I have found work without a lot of trouble), and tvtuner cards (there is a list I can get if you want with compatible cards).

By the way, there is no competition to the 6600 GT. Its cheaper than any ATI card in its class, and it BLOWS AWAY ATI cards in Open Gl (all Linux stuff is done with open GL). It just might be the best computer part I ever bought (for the money).
