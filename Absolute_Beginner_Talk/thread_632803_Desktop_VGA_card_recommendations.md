### Post by bobpur on 2007-12-05
Stick with nvidia cards to save yourself some hassles. About any nvidia card will do you a good job. 
I deal with Newegg also. The 6 series cards are getting scarse so you will have to go with a 7 series card. I can attest to the 7600's doing a good job. I have several GT's in different machines. You might check out the 7300's but I don't know much about them.
Before you order find out if you have an AGP slot or a PCIe slot. you will have one of these two slots on you motherboard. the cards will not interchange.

Good Luck!
