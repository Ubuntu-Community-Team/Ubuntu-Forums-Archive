### Post by mkoehler on 2008-02-25
Various wireless signals have different ranges, based on the technology.  For example, 802.11 a/b/g are accessible (inside) from ~30m.  On the other hand, the newer technology, 802.11n, outputs signals that travel ~70m inside.  It really depends on the obstruction.  However, it is not legal to use other peoples' wireless internet, even if they are dumb enough to leave their network unsecured.  In order to legally get wireless internet, you will have to visit your local internet cafe or other shop with wireless internet available.
