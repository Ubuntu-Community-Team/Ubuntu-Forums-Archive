### Post by gent_k on 2006-12-01
Hey, I managed to install the latest Nvidia drivers (from nvidia.com) today, which means I won't have to go through all the trouble with setting up xgl and all.

Anyway, tried compiz and wow... this thing is fast.

Seriously though, thought there would be a catch due to the eye-candy and stuff, but moving windows around (the "drawing" of windows) and menus and such are actually more responsive that when not using compiz at all. Absolutely love this (even though i don't enable some of the useless effects) and am very impressed how this thing improves productivity, and there is absolutely no hit in performance as well.(to the contrary, moving windows around and such is snappier as I mentioned)

Anyway, thought I'd share my joy. :)
