### Post by bigboy_pdb on 2007-10-22
If you want to make your panel centred and you want it to expand as it needs more space then do the following.

Right click on an empty space in the panel and click "Properties" (you will know you are clicking in the right area if the first option is "Add to panel..."). If there is no empty space then you may need to close some windows or you can try right clicking on the panel on the next desktop (provided it has empty space). Uncheck the box labelled "Expand" which is under the "General" tab. Your menu should now be centred and it should only be using the space that it needs.

If you want to change the panel back then since there will be no empty space (unless all windows are closed), you can right click on left or right edge of the panel in order to see the "Properties" menu option.
