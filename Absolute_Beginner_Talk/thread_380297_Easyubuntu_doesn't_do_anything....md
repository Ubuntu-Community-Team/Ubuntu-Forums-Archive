### Post by Kobalt on 2007-03-09
I know there will be some people falling on me, arguing I keep on bashing these tools, but I still dislike such things as Easyubuntu or Automatix... From what I have come to read they create just as much problems as they solve.
On the other hands Aptitude is going to get you the job done, fast and easy, clean and smoothly. 
I guess what you're trying to install is the w32codecs package, or something like that... Some repositories exist, such as [Medibuntu]("http://medibuntu.sos-sts.com"), which will provide you with these packages. You will be able to install them easily and safely with Aptitude (or Synaptic if you don't like the command line). 

I suggest you take a look at Medibuntu first, and then get back here if what you search isn't inside these repos.
