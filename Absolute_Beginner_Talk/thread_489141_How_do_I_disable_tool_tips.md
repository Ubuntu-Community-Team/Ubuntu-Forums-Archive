### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-07-01
Hi. Open a terminal (Applications-->Accessories-->Terminal) and enter **gconf**; in it go to  "apps-->panel-->global" and almost at the bottom there's an option called "tooltips_enabled". Just uncheck the box.
