### Post by Carbonfish on 2006-08-26
Hi Cathy,

I agree with jimrz you won't go too far wrong if you buy a used IBM  ThinkPad. I am running Dapper on a 3 1/2 year old T23 (see my signature), and everything worked pretty much out of the box once I found out which PCMCIA Network Adapter had the chipset Ubuntu likes without having to configure anything (Just so you know, Belkin F5D7010 Wireless G Notebook Card "just works").

Ubuntu loaded perfectly, recognized all of my hardware without any problems, and the laptops are bullet-proof.
