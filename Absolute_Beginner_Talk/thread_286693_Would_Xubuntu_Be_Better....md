### Post by seshomaru samma on 2006-10-28
Xubuntu is somewhat faster than Ubuntu
Please remember that the live CD is meant to test whether your hardware is supported , it is not an indication of the speed it will run once installed to the hard drive. In other words , after you install it ,it will be MUCH faster than the live CD.
Ubuntu will be fast on 600MB memory , you dont need Xubuntu. If this is your first experience of Linux I suggest you try Ubuntu first. Xubuntu, though faster , is much more difficult . If after a while you still feel Ubuntu is too slow for you (unlikely with 600MB memory) then you can download the XFCE desktop and turn your Ubuntu into a Xubuntu.
