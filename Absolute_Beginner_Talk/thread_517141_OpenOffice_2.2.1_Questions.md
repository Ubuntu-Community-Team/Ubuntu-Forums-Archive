### Post by tgoose on 2007-08-06
StarOffice was bought by Sun a few years ago, but some time in the early 2000s (?) Sun open sourced it all and that is the version included in Ubuntu. While it is open source, it's not really a community project, since practically everything is done by Sun devs still (I think.)

Star Office is still available, but it's basically OpenOffice.org with a few bits added.

OpenOffice.org does not yet support .docx files (perhaps the Novell fork does,) but that will come soon in the Sun version, I assume.

I don't know about the Ubuntu community as a whole's stance on Novell-MS, but I know Mark Shuttleworth has on a number of occasions said that there will be no such deal for Canonical. But there is Novell software in Ubuntu (like Evolution) from many years back.
