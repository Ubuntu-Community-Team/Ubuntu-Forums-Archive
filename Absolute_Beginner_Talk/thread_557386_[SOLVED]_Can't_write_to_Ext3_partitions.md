### Post by lennartack on 2007-09-29
> **mysticmatrix said:**
> Hi I have just installed fresh 7.04 on my PC. Problem is I cannot write to my ext3 partitions.
I know I will have to change some permissions but which???

I guess you can't write to your partitions /media/games, /media/media and /media/movies. Maybe this qill help: Open a terminal(Application - Accessories - Terminal) and type(replace USERNAME with your username):
```
sudo chown -R USERNAME /media/games
```
Do the same for /media/media and /media/movies.
