### Post by CCNA_student on 2008-01-23
Did you install the proprietary drivers for your video card? You can go to System>Administration>Restricted Drivers Manager to check. Also, I would suggest using the x86 version of Ubuntu since there are far fewer programs available for the 64-bit version. If you want help with the 64-bit version of Ubuntu, look here at [http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=134]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=134")
And part of why Ubuntu may be running slower is that the features in Ubuntu 7.10 are newer(like Compiz-Fusion) than many of those in Windows XP, so they make the system work harder. And your English is fine. Some of the native English speakers have such horrid spelling and grammar that you cannot understand their questions in the forum. But your question was easy to understand. I hope that you enjoy Linux.
