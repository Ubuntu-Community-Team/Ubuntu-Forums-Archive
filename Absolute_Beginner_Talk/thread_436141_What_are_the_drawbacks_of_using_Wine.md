### Post by Belyel on 2007-05-07
> **suki said:**
> What are the drawbacks of using Wine?

The biggest drawback is that some of your programs will not work under Wine.  There are other application layers (Cedega and crossover office) that are for (games and ms office) specific applications that do a better job.  Wine doesn't do a super great job working with 3D graphics and sometimes has problems with sound, and hardware in general.  I've gotten a few things working with wine, but many times it's a lot more trouble than just finding a linux alternative and learning how to use it.
