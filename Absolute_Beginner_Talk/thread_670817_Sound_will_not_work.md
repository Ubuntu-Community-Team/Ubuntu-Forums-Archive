### Post by Redache on 2008-01-17
Have you tried disabling the integrated Sound Module in the BIOS?

To do this you need to enter the BIOS and find your soundcard in the list and disable it. Save the settings reboot and see if it'll work then.

This could be caused by an IRQ conflict between both soundcards and the crash in Rythmbox just brought it to a head.
