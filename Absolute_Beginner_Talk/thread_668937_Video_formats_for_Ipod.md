### Post by kostkon on 2008-01-15
The problem with the black lines maybe is caused by *Vive* and it's not related to the iPod screen resolution. 

EDIT: Videos with 640x480 resolution are supported by your iPod. The gain from making 640x480 videos is that you will get better picture quality, but bigger file sizes.

I use *FFMpeg* with the *WinFF* frontend, and I don't have any problems with the videos I convert (although I have a 5.5 gen 80GB iPod Video).
