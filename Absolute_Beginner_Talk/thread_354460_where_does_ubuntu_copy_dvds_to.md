### Post by irish_flu on 2007-02-06
Did you already look in your "home" folder?  I'm not totally sure where it would put that, sorry.

As for the difference between "rip" and "burn"-
"Rip" usually means to take information *from* a CD or DVD, as in "I just ripped all the songs from this Beatles CD so that I could listen to them in my MP3 player".

"Burn" usually means to put information *onto* a CD or DVD; as in "I burned these Beatles mp3s to a CD, so I could listen to them in my car's CD player".
