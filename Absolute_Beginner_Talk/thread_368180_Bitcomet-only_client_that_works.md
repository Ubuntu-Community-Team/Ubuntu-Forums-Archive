### Post by capdog on 2007-02-24
Port shaping is when certain ports on their network get less priority than others. Often, when bandwidth is an issue, http and mail ports get high priority, whereas everything else gets lowest. 

If it's not the network's fault, and it isn't a setting inside one of the torrent clients, then I can't help I'm afraid.
