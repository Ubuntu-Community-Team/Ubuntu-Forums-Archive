### Post by annda on 2007-03-26
most current touchpads are made by synaptics. if yours is one of them, install the gsynaptics driver (from the repositories).

EDIT: there are also ksynaptics and xsynaptics available. search your package manager (like synaptic or adept) for the right one. then configure them to your liking via command line or GUI. in gnome it is system > preferences > touchpad.
