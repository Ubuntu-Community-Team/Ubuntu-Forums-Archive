### Post by freebird54 on 2007-06-15
They only have your address if you gave it to them.  Probably they recognize you again by means of cookies - which are controllable through your browser (Edit->Preferences->->Privacy->->Cookies if Swiftfox/Firefox).  In general, if you don't want them to have information - refuse to give it :)

Hope this helps..
