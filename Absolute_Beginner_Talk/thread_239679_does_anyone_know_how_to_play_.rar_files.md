### Post by Emerzen on 2006-08-19
In other words, a .rar extension is another form of "zipping" a file or a .zip.  To uncompress the .rar file, right click and use the extract option.  Once extracted you should have a file in a typical music format .mp3, .wma, .rm, etc...
