### Post by gn2 on 2006-09-27
6.06 needs 256mb of RAM to install.

I got Ubuntu installed on my pentium 3 500mhz laptop with 192mb RAM by installing 5.10 then updating to 6.06 via downloading updates.

The laptop has selectable processor speed in the BIOS, either 500 or 233(I think) Anyway, at the lower setting 6.06 boots and runs fine!
