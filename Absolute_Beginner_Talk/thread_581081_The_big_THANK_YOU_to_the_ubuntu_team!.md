### Post by aschaetter on 2007-10-20
I've been running Gusty beta on my Toshiba Satellite A150-S4656 for a quite some time now. I've been pretty pleased, but I've spent months aggravated over the fact that my speakers wouldn't turn off when I use my headphones. After ALSA v1.0.15 came out, I almost cried when my headphone problems persisted. I read through the forums and behold... I found the fix!! All i had to do was add "options snd-hda-intel position_fix=1 model=lenovo" to the config file.

Sooo, a special thanks goes out the the forums and thank you, Ubuntu, for giving me a fully customizable, stable, and resource efficient OS that isn't leaving my laptop for a LONG time.
