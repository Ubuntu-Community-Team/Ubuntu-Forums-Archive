### Post by FleetAdmiral74 on 2007-07-27
If your laptop crashes due to critical temps, that means your fan is not working. Usually. Is your laptop hot to the touch (hotter then it should be anyway)?

Is it crashing a few seconds after boot up, or minutes or hours?

That's not entirely true, though is quite possible.  In my generation of laptops, the airflow design was rather poor and overheating is a serious issue.
