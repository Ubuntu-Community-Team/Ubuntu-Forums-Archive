[MS Publisher Wiki]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Publisher"):

> Publisher's proprietary file format is, with the exception of Adobe PageMaker, unsupported by most other applications. Markzware's PUB2ID (Publisher to InDesign) plugin will convert Publisher documents from versions 2002 through 2007 to Adobe InDesign v4 or v5 (CS2 and CS3). Publisher supports numerous other file formats, including the EMF (Enhanced Metafile) format, which is standard[citation needed] and supported across many applications and platforms. Publisher 2007 can also export in the industry-standard PDF format.
