Yes, there are still a few missing pieces to the 64-bit puzzle but then again, it's twice as many pieces as the 32-bit, right ;) 

64-bit will require some extra tweaking and twisting but there are some good how-to and manuals that will help you out nicely. Check out the sticky threads in the 64-bit forum. Once the app's are installed, they won't require any more of you (at least to my experience).

Personally, the only thing that really bugs me is that I can't get java and flash with my 64-bit firefox (I have it in a chrooted environment but another, and more common, option is to install the 32-bit version directly in your 64-bit OS) but most app's are right there in the repos for you.

My advice is to go for the 64-bit. Why would you want to be a pedestrian all you life? ;)
