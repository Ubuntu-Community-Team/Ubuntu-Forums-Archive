### Post by rmockler on 2007-11-01
When you boot from the live CD, your intent will be only to use it to resize a partition.  You will be running Ubuntu from the live CD, not your installed version on the hard drive.  Because you are running from the CD, your partitions on the hard drive will not be in use, and you will be able to do the resize.  Just make sure when you are finished resizing that you don't INSTALL.  Then when you shut down, remove the CD from the drive when told to, and reboot.  You will come up in your installed system with the resized partition.   I did that exact same thing a while back, and it worked beautifully.
