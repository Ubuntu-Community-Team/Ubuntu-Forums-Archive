### Post by flameproof on 2008-03-10
I installed an English 7.10 to a new PC. Now I want to change the GUI language to Chinese, how can I do that?

When I do System>Administration> Language  the only options I see is different variations of English, but no other language.

So how I can I get to Chinese menus and help files?

I have another PC in the office were I also installed English 7.10 and later Chinese, but I forgot how.

---

### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-03-10
```
sudo apt-get install language-pack-zh language-support-zh
```

That will install the Chinese language for you.
