### Post by engla on 2006-02-21
I'm not sure this is a very easy issue to troubleshoot. Basically support for laptop suspend and hibernation is quite variable -- I was happy when the Breezy LiveCD made suspend work with my laptop (Hoary didn't work).

It could be that your laptop is unsupported by the kernel or perhaps Ubuntu doesn't bundle the neccessary daemons to take care of it. 

What can you do? I don't know. Perhaps a newer kernel has better support for your computer model (solution: Wait for next release of Ubuntu in April or compile a newer kernel yourself). You can try googling around for "hibernation linux <your computer model>" and see if someone can define what the problem really is.. if you're lucky the problem is well known, but perhaps not in the comparatively small Ubuntu community.
