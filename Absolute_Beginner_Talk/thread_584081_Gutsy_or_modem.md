### Post by mivo on 2007-10-20
ADSL2 and ADSL2+ are different modulation types. If your modem does not have a setting for this, you will need a router. I bought a DSL router/modem/switch combination this week (D-Link DSL G684T) that supports numerous different modulation types, and it was under $100. So just replacing your modem with a device of this type may also be an option.
