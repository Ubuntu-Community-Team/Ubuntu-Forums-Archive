Once running, everything is running well and I am almost to the point of removing Windows from the computer.  However, there is one annoyance.  It usually required me to boot the computer three times to get everything running correctly.  On the first boot, I have no sound and the background picture doesn't take up the whole screen.  The background picture is 1600 x 1200, the same resolution of my monitor.  Initially when displayed the picture fills the screen top to bottom, but there is a couple of inches on each side with only the background color displayed.  The tool bars go completely across the screen.  Then I reboot and the display corrects itself and finally on the second reboot the sound comes back.  From then on everything runs great!  Any ideas on how to correct this problem.
