### Post by patrickfromspain on 2008-01-05
it seems you are missing some fonts I would say... also aMsn from the ubuntu repos doesn't support antialiassing.

For the fonts, open amsn preferences and in the first tab, click on the latest change font button. There select sans 11 (or Nimbus sans, or Deja Vu sans or whatever sans you see). You should now be able to read them better

For the antialiasing stuff, so that amsn looks better, have a look here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=649364](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=649364)
