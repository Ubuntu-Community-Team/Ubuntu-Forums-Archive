### Post by Hallvor on 2007-07-31
It all depends on what material you are looking for. 

Large and popular files: Bittorrent/Bittornado/kTorrent/Deluge, etc.
Large and/or rare files, ebooks: aMule

If you are just looking for single mp3s, it is probably better to use Frostwire.

Whenever I want to download the newest and most popular stuff, I use a bittorrent client. (However, torrents tend to live violent and short lives.) If I am looking for older and/or rare stuff, like a movie from the 1970s, chances are that I can only find it on aMule. It may take a while to download, but if you are patient you will be rewarded.
