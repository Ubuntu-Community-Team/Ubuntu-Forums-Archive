### Post by mgmiller on 2006-11-22
If you are looking for a snazzy looking player that handles your files, mplayer is skinnable.  You can make it look like almost anything.  They have skins that look like Os/X and lots of other things.  Just go here:  [http://www.mplayerhq.hu/design7/dload.html]("http://www.mplayerhq.hu/design7/dload.html")
and scroll down to the skins section.  Read on the same site how to install them, it's easy.
