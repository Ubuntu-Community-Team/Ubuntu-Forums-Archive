### Post by brownknight on 2008-03-31
> **KevinD_Atl said:**
> [FONT="Verdana"]I accidently deleted the toolbar which my minimized windows would sit in, like the MS taskbar.  I can create and add icons but I can't get the minimized windows in them to sit. I know this is easy... but am missing it[/FONT]

Just right click on the panel > click Add to Panel > select Window List. That's it!
 You might want to pull it on the left most side and then click "Lock to Panel" so you dont accidentally move it around.

### Post by DingsBumps on 2008-03-31
If you completely removed the bar at the bottom, right click the bar at the top and say "new panel". A blank new panel will appear at the bottom of the your screen. 

After that, you can follow brownknights instructions.
