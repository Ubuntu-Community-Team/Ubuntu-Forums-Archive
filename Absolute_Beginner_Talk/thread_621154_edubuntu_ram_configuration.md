### Post by ofb on 2007-11-23
You'll need a little more than 2GB to reach minimum for 15 clients.

> Installation requires at least 2.5 GB of disk space, a CPU of at least 1 GHz, and 256 MB of RAM plus an additional 128 MB of RAM per thin client (e.g. a server driving two clients should have at least 512 MB RAM).

That info hasn't been updated since 7.04. Ubuntu 7.10 requires more RAM, so wouldn't be surprised that the minimums for Edu would climb too.

After that, yes, more RAM means faster. 4 gigs would mean roughly twice the minimum specified for 7.04 clients, which is probably a good idea. You might try the Edubuntu mailing list for people with direct experience.
