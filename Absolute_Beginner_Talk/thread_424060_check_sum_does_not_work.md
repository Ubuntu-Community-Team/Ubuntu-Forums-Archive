### Post by NewJack on 2007-04-26
Ok, here is what you need to do.  Find your ISO file and right click on it.  There will be an option in the Send to WinMd5Sum.  A new window will open and it will have a Checksum in the top box.  Then go to here:  [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes) and copy the hash for the version that you downloaded and then put it into the COMPARE box and then click on compare.  Hope this helps!
