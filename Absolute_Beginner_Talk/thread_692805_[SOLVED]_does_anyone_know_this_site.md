### Post by randy78 on 2008-02-10
This is the type of stuff that gets regular Windoze users into trouble...

If I were you, I'd stay FAR away from places like that.

If a game has a copyright and does not have a reseller's agreement in place with the site that is charging you a "Membership" fee, then it's not legit and later on, you could actually be charged with willfully facilitating and encouraging copyright infringement.

If you want games, do a Google search for Ubuntu Games or Linux Games, hit up Download.com, but try to keep away from places that don't have a solid reputation, because you don't want to be the test subject for the newest exploit on the market!

Hope that helps!
