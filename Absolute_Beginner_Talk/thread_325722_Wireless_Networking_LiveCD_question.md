### Post by bscbrit on 2006-12-26
The answer to your primary question - why doesn't it work? - is that the manufacturers of the wireless modems and cards very often do not provide software for linux.  Have you sent an email asking your modem manufacturer for help?  He probably won't be able to / wish to, but if enough people ask he might decide it worthwhile to help in the future!

I found that the easiest way to set up my wireless was to use a conventional (ethernet) modem to maintain WWW connectivity while I experimented with my wireless card.  I solved it in the end but I accept that this solution might not suit everybody's temperament or wallet. :-D 

Hopefully, someone will come up with a more practical solution to your problem.
