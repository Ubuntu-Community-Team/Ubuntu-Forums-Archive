You have to be careful when mentioning these cards as working out of the box. It depends on which chipset they use. Belkin have used different chipsets with the same wireless card ...I know to my cost! I have three, two with one chipset which works out-of-the-box, and one which requires tweaking to get it working. Great card, can highly recommend them, but check the chipset carefully.
