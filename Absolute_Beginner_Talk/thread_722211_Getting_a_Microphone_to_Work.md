### Post by Sceiron on 2008-03-12

>  I am using the gnome desktop. I have loaded skype and I can connect to the caller but they do not here my voice.

Hello.
Have you enabled the microfone in ALSA?
Is this a integrated mic, or is it a USB-mic?

If you rightclick the loudspeaker symbol (if you have one) on your taskbar and choose "open volume control",
it wil open up a new window. In this window choose File->Devices. Here you should be able to see if your microfon is detected by your system.
