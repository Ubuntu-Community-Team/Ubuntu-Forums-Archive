### Post by Origin415 on 2008-02-13
If the laptop is fairly old (at least several months with normal use) the fans could be blocked by dust and such.

I would suggest opening it up (as long as you dont have a warranty to void anymore) and cleaning it out. Then again, if you DO have a warranty to void, then you should look up if this is covered -- it *is* a hardware problem.

edit -- you could also go into your laptop's BIOS menu (by holding F4 or something similar when it is first booting up -- before grub), many times there is diagnostic information to tell if your fans are okay.
