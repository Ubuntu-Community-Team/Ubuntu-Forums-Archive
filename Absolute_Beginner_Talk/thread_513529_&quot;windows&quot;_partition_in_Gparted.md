### Post by Inxsible on 2007-07-30
you can actually mount your windows drive anywhere you want. 

I, for one, have mounted my c: drive @ /windows/sda1 and my d: drive @ /mnt/Windows Data.

So to answer your question, no you do not *have* to select /windows. But you can, if you want to :)
