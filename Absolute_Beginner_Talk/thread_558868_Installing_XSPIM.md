### Post by Maestro23 on 2007-09-24
That's a tarball.  You will have to extract it and compile from source.  Take a look at the section in the Feisty starter's Guide: [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#Handling_.22.tar.gz.22_.28Tar.2FGZip.29_Archives](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#Handling_.22.tar.gz.22_.28Tar.2FGZip.29_Archives)

To extract: Navigate to where yo downloaded the tarball. Your Desktop?

> cd /home/username/Desktop (Captial D) then

tar xvzf package.tar.gz


When you extract it, it will create a folder.  Inside that folder should be a README/INSTALL doc with instructions.

Post back if you hit a roadblock.

Good luck.
