### Post by Lanky Juggler on 2007-05-11
I think there are some threads floating around on this website about a problem where it freezes up as soon as you click the Launch button on steam.  Try running a search for those threads.

As an afterthought, though i would really try to find those threads first, its possible that you don't yet have your video card set up for linux (it still needs the right drivers.  To know which ones to get, you'd need to know what video card you have).

I forget how to check what your video card is (aside from digging around in the information you got with the computer), but you can check your version of wine by opening a Terminal (Applications > Accessories > Terminal) and typing in ```
wine --version
```

Good Luck!
