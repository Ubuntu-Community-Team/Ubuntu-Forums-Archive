I just started playing with 6.10 and have the newest Firefox on my XP system.  I did an export of the bookmarks to a floppy disk and want to import them into Firefox on 6.10.  How do I go about doing that?  I tried a couple of times with no success so I am sure someone else has done this without a hitch.

---

### Post by chukcha on 2007-03-12
In Firefox, Go to "Bookmarks" menu then choose "Bookmark manager", then inside manager goto "File" menu and choose "Import". Then choose "from file".

Hope it helps.
