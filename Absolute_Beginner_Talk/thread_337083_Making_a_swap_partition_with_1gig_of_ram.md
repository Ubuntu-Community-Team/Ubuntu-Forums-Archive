### Post by Modernity on 2007-01-12
Some ppl don't run a swap partition, but it is always recommended. In most cases you don't need anything over 2 gbs. Even if you are running a server. I wouldn't make it over that unless you want to and anything over 3 gigs is wasted entirely. The only rule of thumb I can suggest is anything from 2x to 4x your RAM.
