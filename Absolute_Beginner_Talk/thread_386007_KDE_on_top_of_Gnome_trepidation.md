### Post by Songwind on 2007-03-16
You will end up with all the apps visible in both menus AFAIK.

However,  you could go into Synaptic and install, for example, KOffice.  It will add all the qt/kde libraries that you need without actually installing the KDE desktop environment.  That way you can just put the KDE apps that you like on there without messing around with two DEs and all their associated additional baggage.
