### Post by altonbr on 2007-10-22
> **Sef said:**
> If you do that, you will just mess up your system.  Feisty and Gusty repositories are not compatible.  There were changes made to Gutsy that make this a bad idea unless you want to end up reinstalling Gutsy.

I agree this WILL mess up your system but you can download the .deb from Ubuntu's package list: [http://packages.ubuntu.com/feisty/net/gaim](http://packages.ubuntu.com/feisty/net/gaim). Click on 'i386', 'amd64' or 'powerpc' (whatever you are) and find a mirror to download it from.

There is always this HOWTO for installing from source, however I DO NOT suggest it: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=558492](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=558492).

The reason why I'm giving you all this information is because I feel it's more fair to say "here you go" and let you decide for yourself (with my opinion on the side of course).

Good luck.
