### Post by Malibu Illusion on 2007-07-02
If you installed an icon theme, go to System > Preferences > Theme and click the "Theme Details" button.  Select the "Icons" tab and then choose the icon theme that you installed from the list.  This'll use the new icons with your current controls and window borders settings.  

Take care.
