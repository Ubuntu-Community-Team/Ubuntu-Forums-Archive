I am using Ubuntu Feisty and currently Adobe Acrobat Reader is the default application for opening pdf files, I installed xpdf and I want to make that the default pdf viewer. How do I do that? Thanks for your help in advance.

---

### Post by felicity on 2007-07-13
You can right click a PDF file and choose Properties, then under the Open With tab, choose xpdf if it's there, or if it's not listed click the Add button and if you don't see it on that list click the Use a Custom Command and type xpdf.
