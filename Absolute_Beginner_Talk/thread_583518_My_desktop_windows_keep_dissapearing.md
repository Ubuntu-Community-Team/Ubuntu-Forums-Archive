### Post by Songwind on 2007-10-20
I am 90% certain I know what this is.

One of the desktop effects that is enabled by default is the viewport switcher.

Scroll-wheel on the background makes you change virtual desktops.  Your windows aren't disappearing, you are changing to the other desktop.

### Post by Ulfursson on 2007-10-20
So, how do I get the windows back, and how do I turn this off?

---

### Post by Songwind on 2007-10-20
Scrolling on the background again should bring you back to your first desktop.  If you have more than 2 desktops set up, you may have to do it more than once.

You can install *compizconfig-settings-manager* from Synaptic to get at the advanced settings.

Once that is installed you will have an option called "Advanced Desktop Effects Settings" in Preferences.  In that screen you can uncheck "Viewport Switcher" to turn that off.

Or, just set your desktop effects to "None" in the Appearance dialog, if you don't want any of the glitzy stuff.
