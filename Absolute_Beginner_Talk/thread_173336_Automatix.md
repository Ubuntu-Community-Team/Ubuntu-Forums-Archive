### Post by Borniet on 2006-05-10
Are you aware of the possible dangers of Automatix?
Because it installs software next to the normal system (apt-get), and doesn't use the systems 'inventory' database of installed software, your apt-get might get very confused.
I tried Automatix myself a while ago, and was very pleased by the way it worked, getting alot of stuff installed automatically (hence the name! No kidding??? ;-) ). But afterwards I tried updating my system, and apt-get went berserk. Also some software didn't work anymore afterwards. I managed to fix almost everything, apart from some errors in apt-get.
