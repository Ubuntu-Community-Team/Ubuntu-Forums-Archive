### Post by tylersticka on 2007-07-25
There are way more Firefox extensions than spell-check and Google search, just look at Mozilla's [add-on directory]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/").

I came to Firefox because of how open, lean and irrefutably usable it was. Although it has bloated quite a bit, I still stay because of the wonderful extensions. I'm a web developer, and tools like the Web Developer Toolbar, FireFTP (which I designed the icon/logo for) and Firebug have completely changed the way I create web properties.

That being said, I currently have a very low-powered laptop running Xubuntu and I've found that Opera or Epiphany are the smoothest browsing experiences on lower-end machines. As I said before, Firefox is a joy to use but suffers from serious bloat, and this has become less than tolerable for me on certain setups.
