Uh, LOL! Update nuked your trash bin... maybe its a sign Ubuntu wants ya to keep your drive with less clutter and just enable the delete command? :D

Anyway I recommend the delete command, means things don't sit around in the trash bin till you delete em. Just open up nautilus (open any folder), go Edit > Preferences > Behaviour > "Include a Delete command ...".

Its for the best, I think your OS is giving ya a sign :p.

Other than that, I dunno why it disappeared, maybe if you deselect the option, reboot and reenable (only do this after mike's suggestion)? I vote delete command in the right click menu.
