### Post by purpleturtle on 2006-01-19
Woohooo... Just though I'd share my joy with the rest of you..

I have been running Ubuntu for about a month on my "experimental" machine to see how I got on with it, and to help me decide if I wanted to install it on my main machine.   Well, to cut a long story short, I've now taken the plunge and installed it on my main machine!! (dual boot with Windows).  And this post is being typed in from Ubuntu!!

The deciding factor was discovering Automatix - a simple, but wonderful idea - a script that just "sorts out" your machine by installing the commonly required pieces of software.   I wish I'd found Automatix sooner - I was trying to install a lot of this stuff by hand (which took at least 2 days of messing around) and none of it worked quite right (whilst grumbiling to myself "why can't this stuff just work... Grrr.. Every user must have to go through this agony!" :mad:  ). 

The other major factor is making me choose ubuntu over other distros was ubuntuforums.org.. Wonderful, wonderful...   (Please don't now flame me for my pointless post !! :smile: )
