### Post by e^(i*pi) on 2007-10-23
Every time someone makes a post, the thread they posted in is moved to the top.  The farther down thread is on the list the less likely it is that someone will see it and respond.  Typing "bump" has the effect of bumping your thread back up to the top.  You could post anything you like to move yours back to the top, but bump is the word that is conventionally used.
