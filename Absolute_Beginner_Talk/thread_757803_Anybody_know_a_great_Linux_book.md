### Post by Hendrixski on 2008-04-17
Seriously though,
There's a few books on "Linux hacks" that you might enjoy.  Like "Linux multimedia hacks" it's published by O'Reilly.  and you can tweek your computer to do some pretty jaw-dropping stuff.

I would REALLY recommend a book not about the linux technology but rather the Linux ideology.  A book about how Open Source works, and about why linux is important ... including what the future of software development looks like in a world that has been shown the light.  For that I would recommend
"Cathedral and the Bazaar" by Eric Raymond

and "The success of open source" by Webber.
