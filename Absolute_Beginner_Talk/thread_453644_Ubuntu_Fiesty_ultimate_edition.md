### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-24
There really isn't any reason to wait/get the Ultimate edition, it is simply a prepackaged install of things that are left out of the regular Feisty CD, plus a different theme. I certainly wouldn't say its an official distribution, nor do I believe the creators frequent here...

Anyway, just get the cd off of Ubuntu.com and then install it via that. You can get their theme if you like it from [Gnome look]("http://www.gnome-look.org/") I believe. As for installing things, look [here.]("http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty")

Thats about it, if you really want ultimate, I guess you will have to wait however long it takes them, if they get to it... just doesn't really seem worth the wait to me when all you need is to install a few programs to get the same thing.
