Just so you are clear - Ubuntu6.06 / Ubuntu 6.10 / Ubuntu7.04 is like Windows 2000 / XP / Vista, and Windows does a really bad job of automating those *upgrades* (and - from what I gather, Ubuntu is only marginally better).

If you are thinking of *updates*, Ubuntu6.06 is the *Long Term Support* version, so will continue to receive updates for another 2 years, before it needs to be *upgraded*.  

*Updates* are automated in Ubuntu (at least they are in 7.04 - someone else might confirm re: 6.06) - just go to System->Update Manager.
