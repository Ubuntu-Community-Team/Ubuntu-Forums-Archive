### Post by bwhite82 on 2007-04-19
Pretty sure this question was asked not even a half an hour ago, but anyways. Type "about:config" into the URL box and hit enter, once you're at the options page, in the "filters" box type in "Backspace", right click on the option that appears and change the 1 to a 0.

### Post by jimrz on 2007-04-19
> **mojo123 said:**
> In firefox  when you press the "backspace" button the page just scrolls up, how do i make it so you go to the previous page?

open a new tab in FF2x, type in about:config and enter, type "backs" (without the quote marks) this will bring you to the backspace line where you chang the value from "1" to "0", close the about:config tab and your backspace key will now do what you requested.
