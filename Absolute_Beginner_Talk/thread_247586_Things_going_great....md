### Post by jpeddicord on 2006-08-30
Just a warning... you will have to wipe your entire hard drive to do that, since most Linux partition filesystems (ReiserFS, Ext3, XFS) cannot be moved. Once you delete Windows, you will have to backup Ubuntu and create a new partition to cover the whole drive. Make sure your backups are safe before doing this.
