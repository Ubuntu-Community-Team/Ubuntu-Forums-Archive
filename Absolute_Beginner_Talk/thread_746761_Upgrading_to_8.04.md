### Post by granny6989 on 2008-04-05
I'm pretty sure that by doing an upgrade you will only be upgrading what you already have. Which ever flavor you have installed just gets upgraded to the next version. Depending on the stability of the beta version, you may want to install it to a separate partition or something to keep your system safe. There is some risk to fully installing (or upgrading) a beta version, but if you are willing to accept the risk and want to give it a go, then upgrading will keep your current 64bit, it will just get you running on the new beta version.
Good luck with it.
