### Post by llamakc on 2007-11-06
[http://www.opencompositing.org/](http://www.opencompositing.org/) will explain the differences better than I can.

Put your mouse on the Desktop. Are you using GNOME? Roll your mouse wheel. Did the cube spin? That's it.

If not, go into your menu and hit: SYSTEM | APPEARANCE | VISUAL EFFECTS. Make sure it's at least set to "normal".

You can install the package compiz-config-settings-manager from Synaptic to fully customize Compiz-Fusion effects. It will show up in the SYSTEM | APPEARANCE menu as "Advanced Desktop Effects Settings".

Answer us this: what sort of video chipset/card are you using?
