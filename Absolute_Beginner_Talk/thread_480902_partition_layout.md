### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-06-21
Well, all depends on what you like, but I have mine, though it's not 80GB total, it's 60, set up as this:

/  - 10GB
/home - 10GB
SWAP - 512MB *  Equal to my RAM
/storage - remaining 30 something gb and change.

You might want something like your / and /home and swap on one drive, and the whole other drive to be one solid storage as well.  That's what I'd do, anyway.
