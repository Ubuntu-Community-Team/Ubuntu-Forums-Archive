Sounds like your hard drive is failing, if it has not failed already.

However, it *could* also be:
1) A faulty or improperly seated cable going from the motherboard to the hard drive(s).
2) A faulty power connection and/or power supply.
3) Improper settings in the BIOS

Try downloading a hard drive test utility from the manufacturer of your hard drive, but be warned, if the drive is teetering on the brink of death, the drive test may in fact put the drive into it's grave. Either way, you've got some homework to do and decisions to make. -Remember, Google is your friend. And I sure hope you've been backing up all of your important data.
