### Post by seetho on 2007-05-15
To anyone still interested in a simple solution to this  problem:

It is a known bug with the ATI driver and the Human themes.  Just simply switching to another theme solves the problem.  I switched to clearlooks and the buttons now render correctly.  The colour scheme doesn't match now but I can live with that too.
