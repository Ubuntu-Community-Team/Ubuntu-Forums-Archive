### Post by HermanAB on 2007-12-22
If the dialogue is *not* maximized, then pressing the Alt key and doing a mouse left click close to the border, will allow you to move a window, without having to grab the title bar.  

The trick is the little square button next to the X button on the top right.  It must be in the right position else the window will refuse to slide.

So with a little fiddling about, you should be able to slide the window through the edge of the screen to expose the hidden buttons.  

'Hope that helps!

Herman
