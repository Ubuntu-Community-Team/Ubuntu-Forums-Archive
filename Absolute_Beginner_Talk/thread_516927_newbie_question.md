### Post by Scunizi on 2007-08-03
Well if there is a little white verticle bar either on the left or right of the screen at the bottom, you may have just shrunk the bar to one side.  Click the white bar and it will appear.

If that's not the case, point your mouse to the upper bar that should still be there and right mouse click, choose add panel.  It may stick the panel vertically on the right, maybe left or above or below the upper panel. Where ever it lands, click it twice.  On the second click drag it to where you want ie bottom.  Now you have to add back the things you're use to looking at there.

Right click the panel again and choose "Add to panel" then in the new box pick "Trash", "Workspace Switcher", & "Show Desktop" & "Window List".  Arrange them in the order you want and voila! you're done.
