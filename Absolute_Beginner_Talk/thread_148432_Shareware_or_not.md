### Post by Jason_25 on 2006-03-22
They are referring to free as in freedom,  as in the source is released under a non-restrictive license.  

(Almost?) Everything you find in Synaptic is free, as in you don't have to pay for it.

If you are using breezy, you want unrar-nonfree, if you are using dapper you want unrar.  Both are the nonfree versions.   The free versions are limited to unraring files that were rar'd using free raring methods.
