### Post by jISh on 2006-09-26
You don't need virus/malware scanners. You have very little to worry about regarding those issues.

Right click on an MP3 file, select to "Open with other application..." and pick XMMS out of the list. Remember to tick the little "Do everytime(or whatever it says) box. And hit OK, and you are done.
