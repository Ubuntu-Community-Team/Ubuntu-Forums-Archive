### Post by NCFC on 2007-09-13
When deciding on a CPU, you should be considering what are going to use the computer for.  There is no sense in getting the biggest and the fastest if all you are going to do is basic computing.  With that in mind you should be able to determine what CPU is best for you and price accordingly.  Also, being a notebook user, the battery drain should also be a concern in picking a CPU.

Finally, I wouldn't disregard AMD.  They make a fine product and the price cuts recently make them a great choice.
