### Post by defrex on 2007-11-02
Funny that they didn't go into the menu when they came from synaptic. Either way, look in the folder /usr/bin for the name of the game. Then, right click on the menu and go to 'Edit Menus', and then, under games, click 'add item'. Where it says command, but the name of the game exactly as it spears in /usr/bin. You can then click on it to launch the game.
