### Post by Brunellus on 2006-06-22
gdesklets' transparency isn't a "real" transparency--it's a "pseudotransparency"--they simply take on the pattern of whatever you background is.  So if you bring a desklet up to the "surface", it'll show its own border and the background, NOT the background of the window that it happens to surface on top of.

Not a bug;  it's in the design.  We'll have to wait for projects like XGL/Compiz and a new X server to get real transparency.  (Some of us are running testing versions of this, and it's pretty cool, but not really ready for prime time yet)
