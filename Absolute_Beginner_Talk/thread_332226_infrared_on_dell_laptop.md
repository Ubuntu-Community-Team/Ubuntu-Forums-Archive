### Post by ubundude on 2007-01-22
Let me start by saying this is my first post to this forum. I haven't even installed ubuntu yet! However I do know a thing or two about the DELL C600. The default setting in the BIOS for the infrared is disabled. To enable it re-boot and press f2 to go to setup, alt-P to change to page 4 and the infrared is the third setting from the top. I hope this helps.
