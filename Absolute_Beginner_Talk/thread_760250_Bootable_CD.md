### Post by indiecast on 2008-04-20
The CD/DVD should automatically be bootable

however, you may need to set your system to boot from a cd/dvd in the bios

to access the bios press the button as directed by the inital start up screen

ex. "for setup, press F1"

if your computer does not have an inital start up screen look up the model number or your compy or motherboard on google.


once in the bios, change your boot order to boot from the CDROM first

### Post by ad_267 on 2008-04-20
