### Post by Sef on 2006-11-10
**Back up** your files first.  Anytime you are installing, you run the risk of losing your files.  To back up your files, get a [Knoppix]("http://knoppix.com") CD and a usb drive.  Once Knoppix boots up, simply click on the hard drive and the usb drive, then transfer the files from the hard drive to the pen drive.  (I used this method to get files off a failing hard disk, which wouldn't boot up.)
