### Post by ssadhale on 2008-03-15
aah ... not sure if its too late! But right click on the toolbar. A small dialogue will open. Then click on "Customize Perspective ...". Then click on the commands tab. This is where u will find checkboxes to either add more icons or removing the existing ones...hope that helps. If you are on Europa then u will also get "hide toolbar" option when you right click on the toolbar. If you click on that, the entire toolbar will disappear! I spent half an hour hunting for an option to get it back. Its available under "Window" menu. Hope that helps.
