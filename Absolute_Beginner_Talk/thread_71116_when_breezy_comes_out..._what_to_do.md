### Post by Goober on 2005-10-02
I just installed Breezy last night (Ok, this morning) on a different partition, and it immediately recognized and loaded my Hoary partition, allowed me to simply click and drag all my important files over.  I assume this works for the Stable Release as well.  Then you could keep your Hoary partition, or put a different version of Linux on it.

For all the programs you have installed, yes, I believe you will have to re-install them.  Although there is apparently an easy way of installing programs, which I've yet to find.  Apparently you can just click and select in a graphical way what programs you want.
