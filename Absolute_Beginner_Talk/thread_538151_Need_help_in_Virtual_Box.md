I'm having the same problem with my top panel still visible and AWN dock still visible. I've gone into the properties of the top panel and checked "show hide buttons", and now when I switch to that side of my cube, i just click one of the hide buttons for the top panel and I close AWN.  It would be much better, though, if all that needed to be done was to flip from one side of the cube to the other (the way that parallels can do it).

Edit: I used this conversation to get a workaround when using beryl: [http://www.virtualbox.org/ticket/138](http://www.virtualbox.org/ticket/138). However, I use compiz fusion, but it worked just the same. If using compiz fusion go to CCSM (compiz manager) > click Window Mangement > click Extra WM Actions > enter your own command for Toggle Fullscreen. Now, with your VB already in fullscreen, but with the gnome panels blocking, use your command for Toggle Fullscreen.  You only have to do it the first time and you can switch from that side of the cube to another and back again, albeit you must click ctrl_r before using the cube.
