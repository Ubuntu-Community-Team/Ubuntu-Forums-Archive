Rich,
As a noobie myself --Ive found the book "Ubuntu Hacks" by Jonathan Oxer et al to be a great help.  It lists in some detail what you are asking for:  Moving from Outlook to either Thunderbird or Evolution.  Also how to connect with your Palm device and much more.  

I like Thunderbird, I use it in Windows as well as Ubuntu.  Now Thunderbird allows for an integrated calendar (Lightning) to be added. This has room for to do lists but no room for "notes" yet -- of course this is just being developed (current version is 0.3.1) and should mature in the future.

Many here like Evolution which has pretty much all that you are asking for.
