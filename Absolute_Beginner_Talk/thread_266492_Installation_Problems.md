### Post by CatKiller on 2006-09-28
It might be worth trying to spot which BIOS that particular motherboard uses. Or it may be listed in the manual. But there should be a logo at the top of the screen as the very first thing that you see - or second if the graphics card displays its own BIOS information first. Phoenix BIOS and Award BIOS are both commonly used, although its possible that this one is something else. Armed with the name of the BIOS, it will be easier to google for a guide to show you around as to what each menu does.
