1 Download packages to Laptop; Connect Laptop to PC transfer files to PC, double click files to install 
2 Or burn to CD, transfer to PC etc.

You can 'mirror' your Laptop but you would need to set up a utility to do it - and it is a longish process. Much better to download the packages (don't auto-install) and then install them on both systems.
