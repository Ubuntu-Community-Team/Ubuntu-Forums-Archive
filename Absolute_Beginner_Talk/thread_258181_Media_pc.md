### Post by Brunellus on 2006-09-15
> **haxer said:**
> But is knoppmyth an os? But theres tons of tv cards out there wont anything work?
Knoppmyth is an OS, yes.  The issue with dapper and MythTV has less to do with TV cards and more to do with MySql, a database package on which MythTV depends.  Dapper's MySql packages don't play well with the MythTV packages in the repositories--result:  nonfunctional MythTV.  It can be made to work, but requires more time/expertise than I'm betting you'd be willing to devote to it.

Hence, Knoppmyth.
