### Post by felicity on 2007-07-31
You can download the .tgz of AIM 1.5 [here]("http://ftp.newaol.com/aimgen/380469/aim-1.5.286.tgz"), extract it, and copy the sounds from inside the extracted folder named /usr/lib/aim/sounds to your /usr/share/sounds/gaim (or pidgin) folder. The sounds may be named differently, so you may need to manually tell gaim or pidgin which sounds you want to use for alerts inside the gaim or pidgin preferences dialog. 

Another option is to copy the sounds from an AIM installation on a Windows computer if you or a friend has one. I only mention this option as I'm unsure if the sounds have changed any from AIM 1.5 to newer versions.
