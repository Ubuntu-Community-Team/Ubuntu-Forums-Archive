### Post by Vague on 2007-08-12
Well, I can't say I really like your thread title, but I do have some advice.  Wine is not tremendously easy-to-use.  If you want to use it, you'll definitely have to get used to doing some tweaking.  Some things work quite well out of the box, but many things do not.  If you haven't already, you should look at the WineHQ AppDB entries for programs you want to use.  You can find that here: [http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/)  Steam, in particular, is often not the easiest thing to get up and running.
