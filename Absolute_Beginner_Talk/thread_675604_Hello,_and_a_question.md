### Post by roboxking on 2008-01-22
Hm, are you just trying to make Ubuntu recognize your Zen? If that's the problem, then, I have a simple answer (as I just had the same problem earlier today.) First, make sure that you have the Rhythmbox Music Player installed. Then, plug in you Zen, or not, doesn't matter. Then go to Edit-->Plugins. Then enable the Portable Players - MTP, and it should recognize your player. I haven't quite gotten the ability to edit the contents with just the foler system, only with the music player though. Hope this helps.
