### Post by maddog39 on 2006-11-25
Just go to:

Applications->Add/Remove...

Then search GStreamer and check off the first 2 results that come up, the GSteamer extra plugins, then search VLC and check off VLC Player and hit apply. That does it for me, gets all my multimedia working perfectly. :)
