### Post by Patrick K on 2007-04-18
I can't say if the Lexicon will work but M-Audio stuff works very well, at least the PCI card I have does. ALSA (Advanced Linux Sound "something") has very good M-Audio support. I suggest going to the ALSA site (I don't have the URL but Google does) and taking a look around. It's not well organized but you can find your way around. 

All that said, Ubuntu has, so I've heard, excellent USB support. You can probably try it with the liveCD. 

Two DAW's you can look at are Audacity and Ardour. Audacity is simpler to use but Ardour has more features. I use both. 

I've heard good things about GNUCash. I have tried it, though (I still use a DOS version of Quickbooks and will likely continue to. It's more hassle than it worth to transfer 15 years of data).
