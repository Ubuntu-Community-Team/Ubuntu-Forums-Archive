### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-10-04
No, problem!

[COLOR="Blue"]Navigate to :

System>Preferences>Sound[/COLOR]

When the Sound Preferences window opens, click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Sounds Tab[/COLOR]

You will now see a list of sounds for each system notification, etc.

Next to this you will see a large, rectangular box with up and down arrows. [COLOR="Blue"]Click on the box[/COLOR] and another menu will open. Select the [COLOR="Blue"]Select Sound File[/COLOR] option and fill in the location of your custom sound file, when prompted.
