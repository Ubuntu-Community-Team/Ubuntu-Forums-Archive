### Post by jken146 on 2007-11-21
I would try deleting the .openoffice.org2 directory in my home folder.  To do this, first navigate to your home folder in Nautilus, then press Ctrl+H to show hidden files.  Next, make a backup of the existing .openoffice.org2 folder, easiest way is probably to rename it .openoffice.org2.backup or something.  Then restart open office.  I hope this helps you!
