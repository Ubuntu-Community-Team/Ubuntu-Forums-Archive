### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2008-02-16
> **Joeb454 said:**
> It could just be somebody has got into the email server you connect to...not your actual machine...unless they are both the same thing ;)

Like I said no-one needs "get into" any machine. Anyone can forge e-mail headers so that the email looks like it's from some-one else. That is just a feature of e-mail.

And yes, I originally meant that the only way to prevent e-mail forgery is to not use e-mail at all (or use crypto signatures, but that's another story).

EDIT: btw, this doesn't necessarily mean your email account isn't compromised, just that there is no proof of something like that happening: changing your password may still be a good idea.
