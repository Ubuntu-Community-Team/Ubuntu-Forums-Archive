### Post by Marsolin on 2006-09-30
You can use [K3b]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/k3b") to burn any files to data CD's or DVD's. If you mean can you create a VCD or DVD to play in a DVD drive without a computer then you will need some other software first. [Tovid]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/tovid") is one option for both of those. Other DVD authoring apps can be found [here]("http://linuxappfinder.com/multimedia/dvdauthoring").
