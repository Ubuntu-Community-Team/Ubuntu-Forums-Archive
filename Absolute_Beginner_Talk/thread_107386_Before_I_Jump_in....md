### Post by Kurt Dodrill on 2005-12-22
if you want to play it safe, just wait until you get that extra harddrive. thatll be the easiest way to since you wont have to resize anything. also, if youve never done this before, UNPLUG the drive(s) that you use for storing valuable data. this way you can be sure youre installing to the right drive and not formatting the drive with your lifes work. i have 5 harddrives in my computer which holds together almost a TB of data and its real easy to make a mistake if you dont know EXACTLY what you are doing.
