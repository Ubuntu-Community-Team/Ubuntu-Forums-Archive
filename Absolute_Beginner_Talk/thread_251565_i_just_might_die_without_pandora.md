### Post by KrayzeKaliber on 2006-10-07
I had the same trouble with Pandora while running Opera in Windows.  I thought the solution would be some tedious, lengthy procedure but all I had to do was this:

---

First go to Pandora.com

If you still have the standard menu bar at the top, go to Tools > Quick Preferences > Edit site preferences.

Then choose the "Scripting" tab, and then choose "Enable JavaScript" to disable it.  Choose ok, then refresh the page.

---

If you might also use **www**.Pandora.com, you'll probably have to repeat these steps because Opera registers these two sites as different, thus different settings.

Hopefully it works out in Ubuntu. Good listening!
