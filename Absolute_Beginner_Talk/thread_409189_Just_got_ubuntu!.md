### Post by caffienefree on 2007-04-14
Open synaptic (system>administration>synaptic). Click on settings>repositories. Click the third-party software tab. click the add button, and type:

**deb [http://ubuntu.beryl-project.org/](http://ubuntu.beryl-project.org/) edgy main**

then go back to synaptic, click reload, and search for beryl.

You might also want to install emerald-themes along with it.

As for your wifi problem, please peruse this guide.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessTroubleShootingGuide](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessTroubleShootingGuide)
