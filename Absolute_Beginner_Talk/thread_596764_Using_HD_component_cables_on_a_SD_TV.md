### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-10-30
You can use a component video cable, plugging green into yellow, blue into white. But you probably won't see any improvement in quality. And for best results, you should get an adapter that plugs into your white (mono audio) port with red and white audio ports on the adapter, otherwise you might only get sounds coming from the left channel on your TV.
