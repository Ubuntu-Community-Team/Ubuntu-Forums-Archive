### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-06-01
Hi. [Beryl]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beryl_%28window_manager%29") is a window manager that let's you enable 3d effects on your desktop (some are just eye-candy, while others are very useful). There's a very comprehensive sticky thread on this section of the forums, near the top of the page (or just press [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=349732")). However, if you're just beginning to learn about Linux, I would advise you to wait until you become more comfortable with it before trying it out, since Beryl, pretty though it may be, is still beta software, meaning it has bugs and is not quite stable yet (it does crash), and can lead to other problems with your display.
