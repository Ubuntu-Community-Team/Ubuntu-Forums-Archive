### Post by tocleora on 2007-02-09
Right now the volume control next to the date is controlling "headphone", which is fine when have speakers or headphones plugged in, but when I don't (which is more often) I have to double click that icon to control my internal speakers. Is there a way to either change that control to internal speakers or have a second volume control for it?

---

### Post by gh0st on 2007-02-09
Yeah I had this problem a while ago so you're not alone :) 

You can do this quite easily, 

1. Right-click on the volume control next to the clock

2. Choose "Preferences" from the menu

3. In the pop up window you should see a list of different outputs in a select box, you can scroll up and down to find the one you want, the one that's highlighted is the one that is controlled by the little speaker symbol next to the clock.

4. Scroll up or down until you find the one called "Master" and highlight it. Close the window and you're done.

You can now control the master volume from the main fader. That should control the headphone and speaker output for you in one go.

Hope that works, let me know if you have problem :)
