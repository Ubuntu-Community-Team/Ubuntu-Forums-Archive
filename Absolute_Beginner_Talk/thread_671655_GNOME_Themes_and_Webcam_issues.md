### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2008-01-18
> **BandD said:**
> For the webcam you could try a program called "Camorama" you can find it in both Add/Remove Programs and Synaptic.  Give it a shot.

To get a full theme, you have to download a few packages.  To install, just drap n' drop them into the System > Preferences > Appearance window.

GTK 2.x - buttons, widgets, colors, etc
metacity - window borders
icons - icons

Camorama is in the repos, you just may not have all your repos enabled.

Go to System > Administration > Software Sources and check the boxes for Universe and Multiverse.  Click 'yes' to reload your software sources when prompted.  After that, you should be able to find Camorama.
