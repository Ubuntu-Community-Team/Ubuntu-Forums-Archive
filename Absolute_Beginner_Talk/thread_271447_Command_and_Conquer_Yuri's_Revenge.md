### Post by furiousV on 2006-10-05
You might want to consider [Cedega](http://www.transgaming.com/).

It's basically a commercial fork of Wine but focuses on Games based on DirectX.

You can check if Command and Conquer is supported at the [Cedega games database](http://transgaming.org/gamesdb/games/alphabrowse.mhtml?letter=C).

But to use Cedega, you have to pay a monthly subscription to download and get updates, though its a very small price. Something like £3/$5 month + VAT(Tax) and merchant cost(debit/credit card processing). I paid about £10 for my 3 months, but never got BF2 to work :( though my friends did.

Hope you have better luck than I did.
