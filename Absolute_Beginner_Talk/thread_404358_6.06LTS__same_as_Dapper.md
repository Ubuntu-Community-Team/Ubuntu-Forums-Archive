### Post by laidback on 2007-04-08
Once you're 6.06 is online you'll be invited to date it to the current 6.06 LTS via synaptic. Ubuntu keeps you informed when updates are available. It's recommended that you just accept all updates, I don't bother to try and work out what they are for, when I'm ready I just click update and off it goes, fabulous. Others may employ a different tactic of course, but if works for me and was suggested by the Ubuntu guys at the last London Linux show.
