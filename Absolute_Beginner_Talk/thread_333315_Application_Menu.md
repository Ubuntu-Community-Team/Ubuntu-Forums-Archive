### Post by justifier on 2007-01-07
the image is stored in /usr/share/pixmaps/  the easiest way is to change your image, i find, is to look in their find the image that is your menu image and change the name, for example put a 1 after it, bring in your new image, call it the origonal name and refresh your toolbar, you may need to restart GNOME, and that should be it done.
