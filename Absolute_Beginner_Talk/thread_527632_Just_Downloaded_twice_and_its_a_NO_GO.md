### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-16
Afew things to heed, when dealing with iso images:

1. You must burn ANY iso image SLOWLY, no faster than 4 X for trouble-free operation.

2. Use high quality media. Cheap CDR's often cause problems with iso's.

3. Of course, remember to burn it as an iso image, not a regular data disk.
