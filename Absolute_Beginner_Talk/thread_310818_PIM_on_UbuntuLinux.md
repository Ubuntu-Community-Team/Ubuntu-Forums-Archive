### Post by ewl1217 on 2006-12-01
I recommend that you try both Evolution and Kontact to see what works for you best. Different people like either one for different reasons, and it really comes down to a matter of preference. Some may say Evolution if you're using GNOME and Kontact for KDE, but I think that's more a cosmetic issue. Remember that mixing programs from different desktop environments will pull in extra dependencies, but that's not really an issue unless you're really cramped for disk space or RAM.
