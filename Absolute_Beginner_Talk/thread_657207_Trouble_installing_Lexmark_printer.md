You are a victim of the "Lexmark does not want a whole lot to do with Linux" syndrome.

Your printer works partially according to open printing.

I will never buy a Lexmark product until they start supporting Linux.

Get yourself a nice HP printer preferably a Color laser.  If you want an all in one there are numerous HP models that work well with Linux.
