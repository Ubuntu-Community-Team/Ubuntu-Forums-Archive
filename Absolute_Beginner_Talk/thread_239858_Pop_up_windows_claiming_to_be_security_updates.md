### Post by tribaal on 2006-08-19
I get some of thoses as well, of course.
You can safely close theses pop ups, as they are only commercials.
I usually laugh out loud before I do so. Every time I see one it reminds me what a beautiful OS we're running: security updates are almost automatic. :)

If you *really* need updates and use Ubuntu (I'm not sure how it looks like in Kubuntu), you'll get a little pop-up from your panel telling you "there are updates available!". But anything in a firefox window can be closed without remorse.

I suggest you turn on the popup blocking feature of firefox, it'll weed out most of theses nuisances (Edit > Options > contents > tick "enable pop up filter").

Hope this answers your question :)
