### Post by xdwmx on 2007-11-20
I'm having a small problem with my laptop's touchpad.

If I am on a page with a vertical scroll bar (ie a long web page) and I touch or press the touchpad on its far right hand side, I can scroll up and down pages by moving my hand up or down the right hand side of the touch pad. This is quite cool but I sometimes accidentally tap there and I start scrolling all over the place.

"Tap to click" is disabled.

I don't know if this is an intentional feature. Does this happen to anyone else?

Ubuntu 7.10 AMD64

### Post by xdwmx on 2007-11-20
I solved my 'problem!'

System >> Preferences >> Mouse >> Touchpad >> uncheck "Vertical Scrolling"

D'oh!
