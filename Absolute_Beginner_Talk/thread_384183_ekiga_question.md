### Post by Kobalt on 2007-03-14
You can use ekiga to call any phone line, but you will need to configure it to use a [Voice Over IP]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voip") protocol, if you've got one from your internet provider for example...
Otherwise you can 'simply' call on [SIP adresses]("http://www.gnomemeeting.org/index.php?rub=3&pos=0&faqpage=x62.html#AEN83").
