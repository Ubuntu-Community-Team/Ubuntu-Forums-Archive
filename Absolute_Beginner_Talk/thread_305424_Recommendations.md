### Post by Bartender on 2006-11-23
The Lenovos seem to be highly recommended.  HP seems to work pretty good but the sound is weak for some reason and this is apparently nearly universal with HP lappies.  Acer and Toshiba come up fairly often, though some Toshibas apparently have trouble with the CPU fans - they turn the fans off or way down, then the CPU overheats.  There are ways around it but I can imagine it might be hard to do with the thing shutting off while you're trying to tweak on it!

Look for Nvidia or Intel graphics, and true Intel networking chips, not Broadcom or some of the others.

If you look in the "Hardware & Laptops" section you'll find more posts on laptops.
