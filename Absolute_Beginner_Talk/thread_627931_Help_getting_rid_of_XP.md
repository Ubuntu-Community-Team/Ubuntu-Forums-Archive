### Post by jfinkels on 2007-11-30
Go to the BIOS configuration by pressing <Del> or <F2> or <F12> or something as soon as you turn on your computer. In the BIOS configuration, find the section that says "Boot order" or something similar, and make sure your computer tries to boot from the CD-ROM drive before the Hard Disk Drive. Also, make sure you burned the installation disc as a .iso image instead of data. For more information on burning and installing, see [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso)
