### Post by Inxsible on 2007-06-23
Open an OO application. (I used Writer) Go to View -- > Toolbars -- > Customize. Navigate to the Toolbars tab.

On the right side you will see a button called New. Just below that you will see a drop down named Toolbar. Click on it and it will give you the options of Icons Only, Text Only and Icons and Text.

Select the Icons and Text and you will get what you want. I think you may have to do this in each of the OO app individually.
