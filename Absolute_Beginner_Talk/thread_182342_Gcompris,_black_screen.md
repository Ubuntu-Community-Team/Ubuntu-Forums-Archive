### Post by az on 2006-05-26
Gcompris works fine for me.  I beleive that it switches down to a resolution of 800x600.  Some video cards and LCD monitors sometimes do not support some combinations of resolutions and refresh modes (it would all be better if linux had vendors' support - the drivers are often reverse-engineered)

Can you try a different refresh rate?  Maybe that is a workaround?

You can also install childsplay (and the plugins package).  Same principle as gcompris, but written in C.  But Gcompris is much more extensive.
