### Post by Mathias-K on 2006-06-04
To get Ubuntu 6.06, visit [http://www.ubuntu.com/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/download)

Choose your country and then click:
*ubuntu-6.06-desktop-i386.iso* to download it in your browser or
*ubuntu-6.06-desktop-i386.iso.torrent* if you use BitTorrent.

You can burn the ISO file as an image to the CD as detailed here --> [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BurningIsoHowto](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BurningIsoHowto)
