### Post by Spaniard on 2007-04-21
When I burned the iso's for Dapper, Edgy, and Feisty Beta, I had read somewhere that if you were having problems, reduce the burn speed, so reduced my 52X burner to 20X and all worked fine.
Feisty would not burn at 20X.  4 CDs went down the crapper before I figured that out.  I reduced my burn to 4X and it worked.
Hope this helps someone.

### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-04-21
Actually, an even better idea is not to go over 4x to reduce every possible chance for errors.
