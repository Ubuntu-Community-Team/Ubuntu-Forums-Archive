### Post by Efwis on 2007-03-20
after you ran automatix for the first time, you have a backup copy of your original source.list.

for example in your case I would look for something similar to 

sources.list_backup_200704201057

This is just an example, your file **will** have a different set of numbers at the end, the numbers represent the date and time in the following manner  year/month/day/time. This corresponds to the day and time you first ran automatix and it changed your sources.list file
