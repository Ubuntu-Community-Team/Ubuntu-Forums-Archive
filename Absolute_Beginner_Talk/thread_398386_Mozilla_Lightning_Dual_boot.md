### Post by jcdeprez on 2008-02-17
The crazy part is that if you simply uninstall and reinstall the lightning extension (xpi file) each time your change boot then you can see and share all your data from one boot to the other. 
So the simple solution is to keep the xpi intaller for lightning somewhere on your disk and uninstall tehn resintall but it quickly become tedious.

However, I imagine that reporting this to an ubuntu forum is not going to get it solve.
If you have the time I suggest that you report the issue to the
