### Post by aysiu on 2007-09-30
Sometimes the .tar.gz files you download from Gnome Look are themes, but sometimes they are bundles of several different themes. When they're bundles, you can't just drag and drop them to the theme manager window. You need to unzip them first and possibly manually copy the unzipped folders to the appropriate places.

### Post by aysiu on 2007-09-30
Well, it depends, again, on how the unzipped file is organized. If the bundle is a bundle of unzipped themes, then the theme manager won't know what to do with them. But if the bundle is a bundle of zipped themes, you can point the theme manager to them.
