### Post by mrazster on 2007-10-04
Right click / add new item / dubbelckick on launcher

In the 2 top lines add name an description...i.e  *name= Thunderbird,  description= Mail Client*
In the third add an icon, either choose one from the drop down menu or add one of your own choosie. 
In the last line add the command for starting your application...in your case, simply add:
```
thunderbird
```

Then just close...and click away my friend :)
