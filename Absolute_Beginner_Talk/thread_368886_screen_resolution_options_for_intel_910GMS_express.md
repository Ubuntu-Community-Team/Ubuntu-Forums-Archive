### Post by featherking on 2007-02-23
Install this attachment

The i810 driver pulls its possible resolutions from the video bios, the 1280x800 is a nonstandard resolution so this program changes one of the modes in the video bios to be 1280x800, then it runs during startup everytime you boot your computer to make sure the resolution is available when you get to your loginscreen.

Having said that, its been a long time since i first used it and i dont remember if you have to do anything fancy after installing..

While im thinking about it, the link in my signature that refers to svideo on the i945 chipset also works on the 915. Just in case you ever wanted to use your svideo or external monitor hookups..FYI
