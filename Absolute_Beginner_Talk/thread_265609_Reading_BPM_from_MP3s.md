### Post by JAwuku on 2006-09-26
In Gnome, if you right-click on the MP3, go to Properties, and click the rightmost Audio tab, the bitrate should be listed there.

### Post by Oysterville on 2008-03-13

Maybe in the future someone will have the same question and, as this thread was #1 in my search, they will find this answer:

Amarok has the ability to show beats per minute.  Right click on the header column and in the Show options choose BPM.
