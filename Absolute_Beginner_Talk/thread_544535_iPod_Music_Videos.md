### Post by kostkon on 2007-09-16
I understand your problem. I had the same problem with [*gtkpod*]("http://www.gtkpod.org/") for music videos and also TV programs. I wanted them to be put to the right category but [*gtkpod*]("http://www.gtkpod.org/") could not do this.

By using *Floola* I managed to solve this problem. When you upload something to *iPod* with [Floola]("http://www.floola.com/"), you are able to select the category that it belongs into (movie, tv series or music video).

[*Floola*]("http://www.floola.com/"), although not Open Source, it's a very good application for *iPod* management. You can [download a binary directly from its site]("http://www.floola.com/modules/wiwimod/index.php?page=download"), or just [get a deb package from *getdeb.net*]("http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=Floola").

I hope I helped you somehow.
