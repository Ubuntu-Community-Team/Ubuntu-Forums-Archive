### Post by mgmiller on 2007-03-07
I have been reading about myth tv and Ubuntu and I agree it seems to be a daunting task.  There is another way to do myth tv that is supposed to be much easier.

I haven't tried this yet, but it is supposed to make a myth tv setup "trivial".  It is it's own distro, not Ubuntu, and it's major purpose is to install a linux/myth tv setup as painlessly as possible.

There are a couple of choices.  Knoppmyth which is Debian based and Mythdora which is Fedora core 5 based.

[http://www.mythpvr.com/mythtv/distribution/knoppmyth/info.html]("http://www.mythpvr.com/mythtv/distribution/knoppmyth/info.html")
