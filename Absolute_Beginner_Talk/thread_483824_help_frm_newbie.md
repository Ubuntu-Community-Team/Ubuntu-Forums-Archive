### Post by Happy_Man on 2007-06-25
If you did not make a separate /home partition when you first installed, all your files will be erased when you install the 7.04 ISO. Which, by the way, I recommend over upgrading twice. IMO, upgrading is far more trouble than it's worth. If your files are stored on a shared FAT32 partition or similar, then they will be OK. So I recommend making a /home when you install 7.04. Just my 2 cents.
