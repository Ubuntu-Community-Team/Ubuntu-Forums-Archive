### Post by Paqman on 2007-10-20
> **intense.ego said:**
> I want to upgrade to gutsy gibbon, but I'm scared that there may be too many bugs. 


There aren't that many actual bugs, you can see them all in [Launchpad](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/gutsy/+bugs). People are just striking the normal sorts of problems involved in upgrades or reinstalls. It just seems like a lot because they're all doing it once.

If Feisty installed fine on your hardware and you haven't been doing weird things to your system since then there's no reason why the move to Gutsy shouldn't be flawless.
