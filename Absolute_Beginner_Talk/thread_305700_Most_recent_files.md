### Post by wombat20 on 2006-11-23
I recommend (again) the Deskbar with Beagle.  Right-click on the panel at the very top of the screen and select Add.  Pick the Deskbar and press Add.

Right click on the newly added deskbar and select preferences.  Scroll down the list and make sure that Beagle is checked/ticked plus anything else you want.  If you can't find Beagle, search for it in Synaptic and install that way.  

When you start a Beagle search through the deskbar (for anything to start with), it will then give you a window with > Select more options.

One of those options will be Date Modified.  Hope that solves it.
