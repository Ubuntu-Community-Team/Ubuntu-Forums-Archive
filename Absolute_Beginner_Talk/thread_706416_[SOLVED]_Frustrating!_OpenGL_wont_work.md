2. while running X under nv or vesa drivers,
re-install nvidia drivers with the "Restricted Drivers Mananger," restart.

If you have used any 3rd Party "helper" Apps for graphics drives, you may have to purge
and reinstall the mesa3D packages as well *before* installing nvidia packages.
