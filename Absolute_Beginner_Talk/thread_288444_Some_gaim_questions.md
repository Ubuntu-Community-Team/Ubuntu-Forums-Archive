### Post by meng on 2006-10-29
In order to sign into multiple accounts simultaneously, first you have to setup each account in gaim and then have them connect. (Go to Accounts, Add/Edit and then add a separate entry for each protocol - MSN, yahoo, AIM, etc). Gaim 2.0.0beta3 is the version I have installed on Dapper, from repositories.
