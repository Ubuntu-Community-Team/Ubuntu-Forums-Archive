### Post by sumguy231 on 2006-10-02
When you boot from the LiveCD, you're running a complete Ubuntu system, but it is no installed on your PC and will be gone when you reboot and remove the CD. If you run the LiveCD installer, it will format your hard disk (or only certain parts, if you need two operating systems or need to keep data) and when you reboot and remove the CD you will have a full Ubuntu install on your system.
