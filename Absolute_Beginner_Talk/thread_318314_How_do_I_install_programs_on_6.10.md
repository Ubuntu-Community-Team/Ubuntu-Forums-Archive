### Post by jmomandia on 2006-12-14
1. Try clicking on Applications, then Add/Remove. Another window opens. 

2. On the left pane, click on Internet. 

3. On the right pane that appears, scroll down and look for Gkdial ISP dialer (PPP dial-up configuration and dialing tool).

4. Check the box.

5. Click Apply, then OK.
