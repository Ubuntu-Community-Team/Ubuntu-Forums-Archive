### Post by Bölvaður on 2008-03-25
(this is not how to unlock the icons)

Some of the icons are locked.
But you are able to add new ones and remove the locked ones.

First step:

Right click on the panel
Chose "Add to panel..."
Pick Trash (or what ever you want)
Push Add

Second step:

Right click your new icon
Choose 'Move'
Place it where ever you want.


Third and the final step:

Right click your old icon
Click 'Remove From Panel'



You might want to add some extra icons you find there. Some of them are fairly useful.
