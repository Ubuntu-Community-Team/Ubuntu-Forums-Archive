### Post by picpak on 2005-05-14
Ubuntu is fairly screwy when you start to play an mp3 when you hover over it. It skips over random parts, still runs after using Ctrl-Alt-Backspace, and makes it impossible to shut down. I have to press the power button. None of the other distro's I've tried (SuSE, Mandrake) have done this.

Is there a way to disable an mp3 from playing when you hover over it? Or, at least a solution? :-|

---

### Post by Stormy Eyes on 2005-05-14
Open up the **Preferences** dialogue in your file browser, switch to its **Preview** tab, and change the "Preview Sounds Files" option to **Never**.
