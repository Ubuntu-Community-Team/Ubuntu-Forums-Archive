### Post by bumanie on 2008-03-16
May be you need the alternate cd which is text based. Also it is worth keeping in mind that hardy is still in its alpha stage and it may be that there is a conflict between your machine and hardy at this stage of hardy's development. The beta version is about a fortnight away and should be more stable. At that point no new things are introduced, but final bugs are ironed out. If you really want to try hardy now, I suggest the alternate cd may be the way to go, it generally will install onto machines that have similar problems to what you are describing.
