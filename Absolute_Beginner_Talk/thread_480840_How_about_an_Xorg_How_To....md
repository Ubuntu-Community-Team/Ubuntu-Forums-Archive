### Post by reset3x on 2007-06-21
I want to preface this by saying I'm no guru... And I hope I'm posting this in the right place....

I feel we're in dire need of an xorg-config how-to sticky... The beginners forum is plastered with posts from new users having a nightmare with configuring X. And as a result they are turned off by Ubuntu and some never come back. I know there are too many variables for every person to have a perfectly wonderful out the box experience. I've had my fair share dealing with X since I've started using Linux. I'm probably more determined than others. I've managed to get by by going here and there and putting the pieces together to get things working. I've done this on many different configurations including nVidia, ATI and Intel graphics components. Like I mentioned above, I humbly admit I'm no expert. But I'm willing to collaborate with other users who are knowledgable  with the inner workings of X and put something together. A little while back I attempted to help someone get things working ( [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=472188](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=472188) ) and they gave up. Maybe we can prevent this from happening again. Maybe the instructions I gave could be used as a starting point. I have no problem with what I posted being (constructively) picked apart. There's no ego issues here.

Thanks for reading this...

EDIT:

This issue appears to be addressed in the release of Gutsy in October:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/GraphicalXConfiguration](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/GraphicalXConfiguration)

[https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/bullet-proof-x](https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/bullet-proof-x)
