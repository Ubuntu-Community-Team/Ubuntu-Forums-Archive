### Post by louis_nichols on 2006-05-14
well, screenshoots are the best way to explain what desklets are.

[here]("http://adesklets.sourceforge.net/screenshots.html") you can find screenshots of desklets provided by adesklets. Then you may also want to visit their [homepage]("http://adesklets.sourceforge.net/"). As you can see in my signature, I recommend them, because they are the least resource hungry and will work in any desktop environment.

Other desklets are [gdesklets]("http://gdesklets.org/"), native for gnome, but VERY resource hungry. I find it pretty weird they have no screenshots on their homepage...

The KDE native desklet app is called [superkaramba]("http://netdragon.sourceforge.net/ssuperkaramba.html") and is pretty cool too, if you wanna use kde
