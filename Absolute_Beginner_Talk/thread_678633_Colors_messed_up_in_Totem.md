### Post by Zars on 2008-01-28
I had this problem. A good way to find out if it is a package problem is to install VLC and try running the DVD in there.

I prefere totem as VLC doesn't work with my media buttons (on a Dell 6400n), but this is a good way to check.

 I have adjusted the setting under Edit > Prefernces in the Display Tab to make the video look near perfect. Its mainly the contrast (set to about 30%) and the saturation (25%) thats the problem.
