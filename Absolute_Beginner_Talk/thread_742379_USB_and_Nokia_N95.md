### Post by bobnutfield on 2008-04-01
The N95 normally comes with a phone to USB cable.  If not they are available and most PC supply stores.  When you connect it, it will recognize it as a mass storage device (Like a camera.)  You will not be able to access the phone's ROM data (except when using Nokia's software for Windows, but you can transfer photos and music that you have saved to the storage card in the phoe.  To my knowledge, things like address books and message history is stored in the phones memory and not the storage card.

Hope this helps

Bob
