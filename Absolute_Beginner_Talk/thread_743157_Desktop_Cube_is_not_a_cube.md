### Post by sayakb on 2008-04-02
Ctrl+Alt+Left/Right won't zoom out the cube. Have you set zoom parameters under Rotate cube in ccsm? I've set mine to 0.4000. Click and hold the middle mouse button or both button 1&2 on the desktop to zoom it out at rotate it freely.

 EDIT: Plus, goto Desktop Cube -> Appearance Tab in ccsm. There, enable skydome, add a skydome image (make it large: about 4 times the size of your screen resolution, or the maximum your graphics card could support) and Enable the Animate skydome option for a better 3D experience..
