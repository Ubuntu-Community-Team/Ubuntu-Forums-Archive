### Post by towsonu2003 on 2006-09-24
try the same thing with "reformat" options (first screenshots) checked. most distros have a hard time installing to a partition they did not format.

but beware, **formatting** will **erase** all data (your files) on that partition.

tip: you could tell the installer to mount your windows partitions. for mount point, write something like /media/windows or similar. do NOT check the reformat option for windows partitions. 

to double-say: **formatting** will **erase** all data (you'll lose your files)

also, please backup your windows files. just in case...
