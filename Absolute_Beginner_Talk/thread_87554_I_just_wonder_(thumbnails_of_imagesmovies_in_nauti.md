This isn't critical.  I just wonder.

You know how nautilus makes thumbnails of images/movies in any folder that has image/movie files in it?  Do these thumbnails get saved anywhere, or are they discarded when you leave the folder?

I just wonder because these thumbnails could come in handy for say, web design.

---

### Post by matthew on 2005-11-08
They are saved in /home/<username>/.thumbnails
(notice the dot at the beginning of the hidden directory) 
In Nautilus you can go to your home directory via Places->Home 
and then click View->Show Hidden Files to see it
