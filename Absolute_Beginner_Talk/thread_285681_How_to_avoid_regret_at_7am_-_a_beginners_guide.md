### Post by unlokia on 2006-10-27
Hello everyone!. I woke up, bright and early this morning, with a kind of spring in my step, and proceeded to execute the ```
rm -rf /
```command on an old Mandriva install that is unused... and totally forgot to remove my EXT3 usb hdd :( :(.

Now suffice to say that there was nothing much on the 15gb partition that cannot be replaced, but it is a pain having to download Edgy Eft i386 DVD *ALL OVER AGAIN*, wasting 2 hours!.

I am such a complete plank at times - and I even thought, before typing those dreaded commands "I *MUST* remove my external drive" - evidently I learned the hard way, and after setting d4x to re-download the ISO, I proceeded downstairs, to sulk over my Weetabix and toast.

Just remember - UNPLUG EXTERNAL DRIVES BEFORE DOING ```
rm
``` commands!!! :):mrgreen:
