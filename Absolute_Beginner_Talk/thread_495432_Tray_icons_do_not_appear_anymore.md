### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-07-08
If I understand you correctly, you may need to do the following:

1. Right-click on a blank section of the bottom taskbar.

2. Left click on "Add to Panel"

3. When the window opens, scroll down to "Utilities"

4. Select "Notification Area"

5. Select "Add"
