### Post by nitricacid on 2005-10-07
Is there anyway to disable the balloons that pop up everytime you mouse over an icon in the task bar?

---

### Post by aysiu on 2005-10-07
Are you in Kubuntu? Go to System Settings (or Control Center), Panels, Appearance, Enable icon mouseover effects, and uncheck the box.
