### Post by abrahamwolk on 2007-03-19
If you use mplayer, you can write:

mplayer -speed 0.5 <filename>

and it will slow it down to half the speed. It will also lower the frequencies of course, but with exactly one octave (as we lower them exactly to half their value), so it should still be possible to listen and learn the tune without problem, simply playing one octave higher.

If anybody knows of an audio-player that keeps the frequencies, please tell!
