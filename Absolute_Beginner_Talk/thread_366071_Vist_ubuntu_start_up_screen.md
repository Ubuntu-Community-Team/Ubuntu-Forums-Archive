### Post by Quicksand on 2007-02-20
You should be fine.

I just did a 64-bit Ubuntu install with 32-bit Vista already present, and what resulted was a Grub menu with Ubuntu as default and Vista (referred to as Longhorn) as an alternative selection.

Just select the Vista entry, and away you go . . . you shouldn't get a secondary bootloader menu after that.
