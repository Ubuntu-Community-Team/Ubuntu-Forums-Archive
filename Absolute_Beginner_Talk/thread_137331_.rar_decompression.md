### Post by taurus on 2006-02-27
Have you tried this version from [http://www.rarlab.com/](http://www.rarlab.com/)?

[http://www.rarlab.com/download.htm](http://www.rarlab.com/download.htm)

Click on "RAR 3.51 for Linux" and save it somewhere.  Then from a terminal, type

```

tar xvzf rarlinux-3.5.1.tar.gz
cd rar
./unrar <path to your rar file>

```
