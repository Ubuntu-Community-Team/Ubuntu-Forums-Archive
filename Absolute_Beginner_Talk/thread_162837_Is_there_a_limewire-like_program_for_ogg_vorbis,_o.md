### Post by colo on 2006-04-19
You can buy music in OGG Vorbis format at allofmp3.com, for example. There are no clients for p2p-networks spcifically crafted to fetch OGG Vorbis, OGG Theora, FLAC, Musepack and other sorts of patent-unencumbered-encoded files, but you'll find some via Bittorrent, edonkey2000 and the like as well for sure.
