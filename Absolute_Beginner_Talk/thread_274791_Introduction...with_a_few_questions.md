### Post by jordanmthomas on 2006-10-10
I have an integrated sound card (Intel) and it works fine in Ubuntu.

Yes, there are plenty of ways to control your CPU's speed.  By default, it is throttled all the way down until more juice is needed, but it is easy to change.

Installing AIGLX / XGL should be no problem right now, but installing compiz (or beryl), which actually give the effects seems to be down right now.  

Check over at [http://wiki.beryl-project.org](http://wiki.beryl-project.org) for install instructions (go with beryl)
There are plenty of other easy-to find guides.
