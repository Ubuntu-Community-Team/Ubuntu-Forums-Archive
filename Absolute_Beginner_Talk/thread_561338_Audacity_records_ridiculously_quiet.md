### Post by ofb on 2007-10-03
Quick question: Have you checked the Volume Control panel? Right-click your usual volume control icon on the tool bar (if you've put one there), and select Open Volume Control. You'll get a set of sliders for different functions. On my machine, the microphone and line-in were muted at default.

### Post by hfw on 2007-10-05
When checking the volume control you need to bring up the preferences menu from the edit dropdown.  In there select Mic Boost (+20db).  I also checked microphone capture, but not sure if that helped or not.  I think it was the db boost.

Not sure about the MP3 export.  I ran across this post looking for that answer.
