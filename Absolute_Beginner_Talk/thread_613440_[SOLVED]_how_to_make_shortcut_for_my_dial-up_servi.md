### Post by natehatewindows on 2007-11-15
what dialer are you using? if you are using gppp or kppp you can get a icon on your task bar you can click on. just right click the task bar and select "add to panel" . then when the window comes up you can press the "application launcher" button and then drop down the internet one and select gppp or kppp and click "add".
