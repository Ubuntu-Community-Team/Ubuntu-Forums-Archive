### Post by njKeever on 2007-12-10
First, make sure you have 1 row by 4 columns of workspaces. If you just have the cube enabled, press ctrl-alt-up, then while holding the first two buttons drag your mouse past one of the edges of the screen. If you have the cube rotation also enabled, press ctrl-alt-down and while holding the first two, move the mouse around.

P.S.- I may have switched the up and down, try both.

### Post by Law506 on 2007-12-10
To actually see the cube in action just push ctrl and alt at the same time and then left click the screen and move the mouse.  The screen should zoom out and make a cube of the desktops.  Be aware that **if you have only 2 workspaces then you wont get the cube, need to set it to 4.** The image is based off of the # of workspaces, 2 of them gets you a 2 sided plane, 3 gets you a triangle, 4 gets a cube, and so on.
