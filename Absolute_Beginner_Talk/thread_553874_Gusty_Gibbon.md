### Post by Samuelh3 on 2007-09-20
I installed Gutsy Gibbon after running Feisty Fawn for maybe a week or two. This is after not even touching a Linux box for years. I've had no real major issues with it. Getting the Compiz to work with my ATI Mobility Radeon x1600 was a breeze compared to Feisty (main reason I took the chance). A few apps lockup every once in a while, but coming from a Vista machine, it no real disturbance. I've been getting updates like 3 times a day and they are definitely making an improvement on the reliability. Firefox used to lock up on youtube occasionally but it doesn't anymore. Only major issue (if you can call if that) is that my pidgin has no sound. 

In short, Gutsy Gibbon has been a very pleasant experience so far. (knock on wood)
