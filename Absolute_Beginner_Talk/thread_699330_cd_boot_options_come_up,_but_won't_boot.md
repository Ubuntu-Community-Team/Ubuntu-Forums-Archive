### Post by bumanie on 2008-02-17
What speed did you burn it at? The slower the better to reduce the chance of any burning errors. Many users believe 4x speed a good speed to use. You also need to ensure that you did not burn at a speed higher than what the disk can burn at (I once did that by mistake and the cd only worked for a few seconds and then just a fuzzy screen as the optical drive could not read anything).
