### Post by TrendyDark on 2006-03-01
You should see a screen with a status bar that says something to the effect of "partitioning drive hda2 with ext3".

If you're not seeing that screen one of two things may be happening.

One, and the most likely problem, is that your disk may be corrupt and not functioning correctly. Check the disk and reburn the Ubuntu ISO if needed.

Two, Could be a hard drive problem.
