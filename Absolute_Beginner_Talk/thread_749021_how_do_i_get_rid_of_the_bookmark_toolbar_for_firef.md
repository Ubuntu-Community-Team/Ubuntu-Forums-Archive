### Post by jw5801 on 2008-04-08
Right click on blank space in the toolbar anywhere and uncheck the "bookmarks toolbar" box.

---

### Post by rjmoerland on 2008-04-08
To get rid of the bookmarks toolbar folder in your bookmarks as well, go to the bookmarks menu, select 'Organize Bookmarks' and select a folder of your own bookmarks as the bookmarks folder: Edit/Select as Bookmarks Toolbar Folder. Then you can delete the original Bookmarks Toolbar Folder.
