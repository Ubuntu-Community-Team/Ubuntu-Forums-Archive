### Post by Niniel on 2007-12-26
Hello,

a) ATI is owned by AMD, not Nvidia,
b) the current high-end ATIs are somewhat cheaper than their Nvidia counterparts (but you don't need to get the most expensive card/s for a nice gaming experience). 

But what are you trying to find out here? You start asking about graphic cards and end up talking about motherboards and CPUs. Also note that this forum is primarily about Ubuntu Linux, not setting up a Windows gaming rig (although I'm sure people can help you with that as well).

Regarding graphics cards, why do you want a new one? Is your current graphics controller not running your games? If it is, and you don't plan on getting any new games any time soon, there's no need to plug in a (new) card.
