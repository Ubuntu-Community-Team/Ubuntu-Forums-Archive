### Post by pmshirey on 2008-02-06
Here are some things to do.

The easiest thing to do is just turn the input part of the plug in end of the speakers some, if that doesn't work look for water in the input. 

The out of wack thing to do would be open up the computer (if it a laptop please have a professional do it) and make sure all the connections for the sound card are complete. If the sound is on the on the motherboard, try blowing the dust out. 

Last and most hardest, make sure all the drives are installed.
