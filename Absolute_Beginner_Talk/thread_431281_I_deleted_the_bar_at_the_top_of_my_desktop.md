### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-02
Geez, so many people are doing this maybe soon we will just have to declare open season on taskbars :p  

Seriously though, if you still have your bottom panel just right click on it and select new panel. Then if its not on the top, right click and select properties then make orientation read "top". Then you just have to readd all your "things", just right click on the panel at top again and select add to panel. Then you'll see a screen and you can select what to put back on it, however you had it configured. Like clock, main menu... take your pick.
