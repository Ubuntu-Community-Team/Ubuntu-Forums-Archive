### Post by A4006 on 2008-03-07
Vista has nothing to do with setting the boot device priority.  Go into your motherboard BIOS by pressing Delete or F1 when your computer first turns on (when it shows the manufacturer name i.e. Dell) and find "Boot Device Priority" or something similar, and set your DVD drive first on the list.
