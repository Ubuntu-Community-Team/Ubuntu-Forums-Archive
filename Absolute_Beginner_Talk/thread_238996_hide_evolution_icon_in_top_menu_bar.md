### Post by jem7v on 2006-08-18
meng is right. Just right-click the icon and choose "Remove From Panel." If you decide you want to put other things there instead you can just right click somewhere on the menu bar and choose "Add To Panel..." to open a dialogue of handy things to add. You can put pretty much anything wherever you want, within reason... To move them around you just right click, turn off "Lock to Panel" and then right click again and click Move. Then move it where you want it and click again when it's in a good spot.
