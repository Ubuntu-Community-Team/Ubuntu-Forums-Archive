### Post by Najand on 2007-05-18
The support Ubuntu 6.06 LTS (Dapper Drake) would be available until 2009 (3 years from the date of release) and the support of the newest release of ubuntu (ubuntu 7.04: Feisty Fawn) would last until 2008 (18 month from the date of release). You would be able to upgrade to a newer enterprise (long term support) release when a newer enterprise one releases. For upgrading to Feisty Fawn from Dapper Drake you need to first upgrade to Edgy Eft and then upgrade to Fesity Fawn.
