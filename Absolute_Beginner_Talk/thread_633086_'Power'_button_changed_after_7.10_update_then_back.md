### Post by GGLucas on 2007-12-07
The "green running man" is from the any of the other (non-default) themes, you can go to System->Preferences->Appearance and select a theme there, the default one is "Human", of course, it could also be that there's just something screwed up aside from the themes, but changing the theme to "Glossy" or "ClearLooks" (or "Human", if you want to get rid of it) is something to try first :P
