### Post by shearn89 on 2007-06-23
i'm not certain, but this is how i think it works:

GTK is the GNOME desktop, so when you download themes for that, it changes your GNOME toolbars and panel. 1/2 is just the version.

Not certain about what metacity actually does..

I know compiz and beryl are other window managers - they appear in the available "sessions" when you log in, and you can choose beryl or gnome (or others). They are sort of like the difference between GNOME and KDE.

I may be totally wrong, but thats JM2C...
