### Post by anjilslaire on 2008-01-26
Ubuntu, like most distributions, is inherently fairly secure. Ubuntu does not come with any ports open by default.

Sending info over the web or by mail is a completely different subject, though. Once it leaves your inbox/browser/whatever, the question becomes "how secure is my method of transmission?" There are encryption methods for email, as well as IM, not to mention creating VPNs, etc.

How you send your information is separate from your OS of choice, in many cases.
