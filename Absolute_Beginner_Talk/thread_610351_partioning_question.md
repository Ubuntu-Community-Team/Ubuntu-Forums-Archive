### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-11-11
You may want to create separate partitions for your operating system and data you want to keep, for example.

Say I have a bunch of pictures I always want to keep no matter what.  If I choose to install a new OS, I have to go through the trouble of backing them up somewhere, right?  Now, say I kept them separate from the OS...I could just delete the OS and install a new one in its place, leaving my pictures alone.  

Many people suggest creating an extra partition for your home directory so if you ever reinstall Ubuntu, all your settings will remain intact.
