How can I easily copy the inbox and sent folders from my evolution e-mail client to the desktop or pen drive? Thanks, Tony Johns

---

### Post by yaztromo on 2008-04-11
If you just want to back your mail up you can copy the entire evolution folder.

```
 cp -r .evolution/ Desktop/
```

Will copy the evolution folder to the desktop.
