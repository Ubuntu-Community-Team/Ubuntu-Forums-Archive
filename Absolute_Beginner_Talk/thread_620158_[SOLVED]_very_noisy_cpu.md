### Post by minisubwoofer on 2007-11-22
Hey there,

If you have a relatively new computer you will probably find that on high processor usage your computer will up the speed of your CPU fan to bring the temperature down. So when an application hangs or you are doing something intense it will get louder.

Best thing you can do is clean the CPU heat sync (involves taking off the side) or otherwise have a careful poke around in your BIOS to find some fan controls.

Hope this helps
