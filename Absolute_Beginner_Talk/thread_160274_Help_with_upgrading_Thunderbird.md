### Post by arnieboy on 2006-04-14

aysiu: a necessary correction needs to be made to the above statement and all future ones regarding Automatix.
Automatix **DOES NOT** replace the sources.list **unless it has the explicit permission of the user**.

Denial of permission by the user does not affect the operation of automatix in terms of softwares installed.
