### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-02-06
The new (as of 9/07) ipod was specifically designed NOT to be compatible with linux. They encrypt the music directory on the ipod, so you can download music to it, and even play music from the ipod when the ipod is connected to Ubuntu.

But as soon as you disconnect, no music will show on the ipod directory.

[http://www.boingboing.net/2007/09/14/new-ipods-reengineer.html](http://www.boingboing.net/2007/09/14/new-ipods-reengineer.html)

There are reported work arounds, but I elected to return the ipod. There are many other options in terms of mp3 players, including cell phones, which are both cheaper then ipods and linux compatible out of the box.
