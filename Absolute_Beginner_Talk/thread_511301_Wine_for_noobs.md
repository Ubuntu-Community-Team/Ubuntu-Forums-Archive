### Post by cwm33 on 2007-07-27
Easiest of the easy would be going into the Applications menu, then into Add/Remove Applications, then do a search for Wine.

A list with a few entries should come up, with Wine Windows Emulator being near, or at the top.  Select it, hit apply, and then you should be off to the races. :)
