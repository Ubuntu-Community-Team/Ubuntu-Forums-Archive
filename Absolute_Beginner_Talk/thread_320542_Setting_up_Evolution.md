### Post by BlingStudly on 2008-05-19
Sympatico Tech Support was amazing - even though they do not support Evolution or Ubuntu, the support person actually did some research on her own and then helped me to set up Evolution.  

The trick for me was to get the right POP server for my account (there are several choices), use my full email address as the user name instead of my normal Sympatico "B1" number, and choose "no authentication" for both outgoing and incoming mail servers.

If all else fails, call Sympatico Tech Support!
