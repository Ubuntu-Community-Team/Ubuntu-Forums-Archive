### Post by victorgreen on 2007-11-21
Ok, by the sound of it you're on a laptop. What kind of laptop. What version of Ubuntu are you using? Go to system ==> preferences==> sounds, to the bottom where it says device, your laptops sound card should be there as the device, under that there should be a list of stuff like pcm and microphone. Make sure pcm is hilighted by clicking on it. Close the sound preferences window, and see if the volume keys work. 

Thats what solved my volume button issues on a Lenovo x61 running fiesty 7.04. My volume keys seem to work out of box in 7.10.
