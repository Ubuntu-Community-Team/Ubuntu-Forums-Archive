### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-07-30
You can check your BIOS settings to see if there is an option to enable USB devices earlier in the boot sequence.  Not all BIOSes have this, so if you can't find it, you're pretty much out of luck.  The only other option would be to use a USB to PS/2 adapter.  A lot of mice and keyboards comes with them, they tend to just be little green things.  See if you can get your hands on one (you just need it for the keyboard).

EDIT: Err, that is if you can actually get to the BIOS.
