### Post by Papa-san on 2006-09-22
I know there are several threads in this forum that have this information. I think it would be a tedious search, but it is there...

To post output, copy it to clipboard. (right click > 'copy')
Then you type ( Replace the * with an o) [c*de] at the beginning, and [/c*de] at the end. you get this:
```
 blah, blah, yada, yada 
```
