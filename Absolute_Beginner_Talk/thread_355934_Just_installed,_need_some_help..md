### Post by lhtown on 2007-02-07
1. You can try searching in Synaptic (System>Administration>Synaptic Package manager) for "midi". I use a program called Timidity. Your mileage may vary depending on your soundcard.

2. You should choose a password that is difficult to guess. Some of us run a software firewall. It really isn't strictly necessary with a default installation though. You should still avoid opening spam and going to the sleazy parts of the internet.

3. In most cases, advanced video drivers aren't strictly necessary although they can improve performance. I don't know about your card.
