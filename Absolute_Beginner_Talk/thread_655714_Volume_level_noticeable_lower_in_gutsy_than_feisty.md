### Post by rivalarrival on 2008-01-02
Have you tried adjusting the "PCM" slider? 

 I had the same problem when I first installed Ubuntu, the maximum volume it would output was much lower than the max I could get from Windows, and turning up my speakers, I started getting distortion and hiss. 

If you don't have a PCM slider, hit Edit>Preferences and tick the PCM box.

[https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+question/15876](https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+question/15876)
