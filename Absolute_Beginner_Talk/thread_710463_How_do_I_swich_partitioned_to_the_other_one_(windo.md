### Post by Het Irv on 2008-02-28
When Ubuntu Installed it should have installed GRUB, which will show up first upon turning on your PC.  When Grub comes up you should see 4 or 5 options some of which say Ubuntu, the one near, if not at, the bottom should say either "Windows XP/2000/ME" or Windows Longhorn/Vista".  These options will boot you into Windows.   To get to GRUB from Ubuntu you need to restart your computer.
