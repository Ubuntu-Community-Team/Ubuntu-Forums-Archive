### Post by Sef on 2008-01-06
System > Administration > Language Support > click on Korean 

If you don't have Korean yet, then do this:

Systems > Preferences > SCIM Input Method Set Up > Hangul > Keyboard > Click on the button with three dots > Make sure Hangul_Hangja is highlighted > click ok.
