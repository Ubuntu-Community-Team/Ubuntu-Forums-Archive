### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-08-28
Do you know how to check MD5sums?  If so, did you check the MD5sum for the ISO you downloaded before you burnt it to CD?

Also, when the CD's contents were displayed in Windows, was it a series of files and folders, or just one large file?

If the answer to the first question is "no" or the answer to the second question is "one", then [view this page]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso.html") for instructions that may help you.

Otherwise, try re-burning the CD at a slower speed, preferably 4x or slower.  Installation CDs get kind of dodgy when burned at higher speeds.
