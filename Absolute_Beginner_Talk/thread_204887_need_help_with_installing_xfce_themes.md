### Post by aysiu on 2006-06-27
Copy and paste it into the /home/choness/.themes folder.

.themes is a hidden folder.

So go to /home/choness first. Then press **Control-H**. This will show hidden files and folders.

Double-click into .themes and paste your untarred folder in there.
