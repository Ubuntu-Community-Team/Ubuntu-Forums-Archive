### Post by jken146 on 2007-12-26
Yes, try recovery mode and see what sort of error messages you get when booting up.  In future, though, when something crashes, it is almost always recoverable without doing a hard reset.  Typing **xkill** into the Alt+F2 box makes your cursor kill off a window, and the kill and pkill commands can be used from the Ctrl+Alt+F1-6 terminals.
