If they are in standard MS Office formats, no conversion is necessary unless you use macros. OpenOffice will open these documents just fine. If you do use macros, then you will likely need to re-write the macros in OOs StarBasic macro language as OO doesn't understand VBA macros unless you are using a build of OO that includes the new patches from Novell to handle some of the VBA functionality.
