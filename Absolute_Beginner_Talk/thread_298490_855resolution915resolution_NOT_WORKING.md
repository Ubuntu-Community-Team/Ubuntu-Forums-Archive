### Post by crypticlabs on 2006-11-12
I got it to work! Here is what I did. I first made a backup of my xorg.conf file. Then I simply copied the settings (the ones pertaining to the monitor, resolutions, etc...did not change anything regarding fonts or input devices). I saved the file, did a Crtl + Alt + Backspace and voila! I am in 1600x1200 mode. I didn't even install/apply the 855/915 resolution patch.
