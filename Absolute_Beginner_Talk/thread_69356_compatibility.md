### Post by mlomker on 2005-09-26
A lot of people boot multiple operating systems.  I'm not sure what happened with your installs but it isn't normally a problem.  I recommend installing Windows first and then installing Ubuntu.

To answer your question, no, nothing would improve with two drives.  The MBR is always on the first one, regardless of how many drives you have.
