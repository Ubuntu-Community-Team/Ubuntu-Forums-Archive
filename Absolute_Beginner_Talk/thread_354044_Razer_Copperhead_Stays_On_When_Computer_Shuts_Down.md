### Post by Madpilot on 2007-02-09
This is a known bug deep in the current generation of Linux kernels that Ubuntu is using. It affects a lot of USB devices, not just your specific model of mouse.

I get the affect with my basic Microsoft Optical mouse, too, and sometimes from my USB key, if I leave it plugged in.

The only fix I've found is to cycle the power on your PSU - flip the main power switch off for a few seconds, then back on. It's irritating, but I do it because the PC is in my bedroom these days, and the mouse light is distracting when I'm trying to sleep...
