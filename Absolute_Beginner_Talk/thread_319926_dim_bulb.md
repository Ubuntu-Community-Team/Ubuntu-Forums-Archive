### Post by Shatrat on 2006-12-16
As far as winmodems go, I don't have much experience with them but if you get one working it will probably stay working.  I don't think you'll want to upgrade your kernel on a dial up connection, or need to.
I couldn't live without broadband and it definitely makes any computer much more powerful to have fast net access.
Definitely get a modem that outputs to ethernet, then you can connect it to a router and connect the router to your computer in case you get a second computer or network attatched storage or somebody comes over with a laptop, et cetera.  USB devices more complicated than a mouse and keyboard are generally a bad idea in my experience.
