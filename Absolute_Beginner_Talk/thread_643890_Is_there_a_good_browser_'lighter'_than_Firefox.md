### Post by eldepeche on 2007-12-18
> **markyb86 said:**
> isnt swiftweasel just FF removing the copyrighted images?

Iceweasel is Firefox with branding and automatic updates disabled. Swiftfox is a proprietary fork of Firefox optimized for specific processor architectures. Swiftweasel appears to be a free software attempt at recreating Swiftfox.

ALSO, Epiphany's address/search bar can be supplemented by adding smart bookmarks. Anytime a search query is included in the URL, replace it with %s and bookmark it. Then when you type something in the bar, a pop-down list appears with all those smart bookmarks listed. I've got Google Linux, Wikipedia, eMusic, the Gentoo Wiki, and a couple others in mine. You also don't have to tab over from the address bar.
