### Post by IYY on 2006-10-09
Is a voice recorder just a program that records sound from a microphone, or audio-in in your sound card, and convert it to MP3 or OGG? If so, the program for you is Audacity. It's in Ubuntu's repositories.

```
sudo apt-get install audacity
```

### Post by Marsolin on 2006-10-16
Does your voice recorder have a headphone jack? If so you can buy a cable from a Best Buy, Fry's, or any other store that sells audio components. You would plug one end into the voice recorder and the other into the microphone port of your laptop. You should be able to use Audacity or any other recording program in Linux to capture the input.
