### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-11-29
Upgrading to Ubuntu 7.10 is not a must, if you feel that Ubuntu 6.06 is good enough for you then stick with it. An upgrade could break your system instead of making it, so the best options in your case would be to either:-

1) Do a clean install of Ubuntu 7.10.

2) Keep Ubuntu 6.06 which still works perfectly for you.

I believe you know what choice to take;).
