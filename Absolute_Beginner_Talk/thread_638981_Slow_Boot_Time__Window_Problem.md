### Post by sirgogo on 2007-12-12
Hello coolmatt,

Heres my two cents:
Boot-up time: This can be related to the devices connected to your computer, or perhaps the fact that your graphics card isn't properly installed. I have experienced some boot-up issues with my graphics card when it was not installed properly, including long boot-up times. One thing I might suggest is having your graphics kernel recompiled (xserver thing).So you might want to check that out.

Apps Opening:This can be a real hassle! So, thats why Ubuntu integrated Compiz Fusion, among other things, to make it easy. To change your settings go to
--> System --> Preferences --> Advanced Desktop Effects Settings --> Place Windows.
There you should be able to change the setting.

Concerning the lseep function... I'm not sure.

Good luck, and tell me how it goes!
