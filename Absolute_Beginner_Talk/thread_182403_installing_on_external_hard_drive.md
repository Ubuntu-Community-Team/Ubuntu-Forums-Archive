### Post by aysiu on 2006-05-26
Not to sound too discouraging, but going from OS X to Ubuntu will be tough for the following two reasons:

1. PowerPC has fewer available packages--especially some key ones like Flash.

2. The documentation for PowerPC is scant. Most people are using x86 because they're switching to Ubuntu from Windows. You can ask questions, and people will try to help you, but sometimes you'll have to really scrub Google to find the answers you're looking for.

It can happen. You can do it. You just have to be determined.
