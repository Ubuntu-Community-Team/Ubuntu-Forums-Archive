### Post by jordank on 2007-11-13
what he's saying, is that he's on a laptop.  Most laptops have mouse touchpads.  These touchpads generally have scroll bars on the side of them, so you can take your finger and move it up and down along this scroll bar on your touchpad.  Because this acts the same way as a mousewheel (can move up and down websites, etc) it also makes you switch to your next workspace if you do it on an empty part of your desktop.  He wants to disable his scrollbar from changing workspaces.
