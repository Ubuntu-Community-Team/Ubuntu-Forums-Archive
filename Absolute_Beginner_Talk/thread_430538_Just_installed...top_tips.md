### Post by deathbyswiftwind on 2007-05-02
Congrads and welcome to ubuntu!

As sef said always back up your data. Also read my friend read. Read as much of the forums as you can. That way if you ever do get into a problem with anything you might just remember reading about it from someone elses posts and probably already know the answer!

Also dont ever be scared to ask a question. This forum is a lovely place and no matter how silly your question seems ask anyways. ### Post by doomster on 2007-05-02
welcome to ubuntu

top tip 1 : AS a biginner you will have issues on installing programs... NOT :P

use these commands :
apt-cache search ** program** <-- searches a huge database of programs  for word "program"
sudo apt-get install **programm ** <--- installs this program

in order to make this huge database , even bigger you should  go to 
System>Administration> Software sources and tick all   (main/universe/multiverse/restrected) repositories.
