### Post by macogw on 2008-02-08
Do you by any chance have insanely high amounts of dust/dirt/grime in your CPU's heatsink?  I saw a Pentium 4 that took aaaaaagggeeeeesssss to load even to the menu (on either Ubuntu or XP....ended up going with XP because her dialup modem is only supported in 2.4 kernels) and then just sat after getting there.  The trouble was that there was so much gunk clogging the heatsink (and the thermal paste was dried up too) that it was overheating *really* bad.  To avoid overheating, the CPU kept scaling itself down lower, and lower, and lower, til it was practically not doing anything at all.
