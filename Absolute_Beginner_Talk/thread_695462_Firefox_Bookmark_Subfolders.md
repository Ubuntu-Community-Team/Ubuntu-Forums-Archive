### Post by rkd on 2008-02-13
Under the Bookmarks menu, click "Bookmark this page". To the  right of the "Create in" selection box is an arrow (NOT the arrows in the selection box. Click that arrow and the dialog box will change to one that includes a "New Folder" button.

### Post by IanW on 2008-02-13
If you don't want to bookmark your current page, simply click on "Organise Bookmarks", also in the Bookmarks menu.
