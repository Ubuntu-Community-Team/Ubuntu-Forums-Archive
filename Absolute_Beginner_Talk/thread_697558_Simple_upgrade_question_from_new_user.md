### Post by neurostu on 2008-02-15
No you will not, that is the advantage of using th upgrade manager to switch,  all your settings and customizations should be migrated to gutsy. I've seen upgrade manager work several times flawlessly on really specialized systems.  I trust it completely. 

One piece of advise is run the upgrade and then walk away. It will take it 30 minutes to a hour (maybe longer depending on your internet connection).  If you get impatient and start messing with stuff during the upgrade you could do some serious damage (I'm speaking from experience here).
