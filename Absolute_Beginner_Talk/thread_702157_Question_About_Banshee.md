### Post by marufaberlin on 2008-02-21
Well... your music files must be stored somewhere, be it on your disk, on cds or on external devicesl. What I would recommend you to do is this, if you have not done it already:

Organize all your music files into subfolders in one music folder. Then you can simply copy the whole folder onto some other medium - and there you have your backup. You can rename all your files however you want - Banshee just shows the ID3 Tags of the files - if you don't use some weird format, like wav or something.:)

What Desktop Environment do you use? I know that Konqueror (KDE) asks you if you d'n'd into a folder. But seeing as you use Banshee, you probably should delete the music files you removed from your library from your music folder.

Why don't you rename those files that have the same file names? If they are different songs, then they should have different file names - keeps things cleaner.
