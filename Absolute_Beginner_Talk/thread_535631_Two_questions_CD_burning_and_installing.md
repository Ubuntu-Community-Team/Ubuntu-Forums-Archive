### Post by Hobo Joe on 2007-08-26
On the first question, there should be an option that just says 'burn ISO image', you don't want floppy or hard disk, but I don't know about 'no emulation'.

As for the second question, no, you do not need to reformat. However, you will have to partition it, but, provided you have some free space on the drive, that will be easy.

And yes, you can run on an external drive, but it's not necessary.
