### Post by Joeb454 on 2007-12-22
gksu is for graphical apps in Gnome (normal Ubuntu) basically any app that is under the Menu's (Applications / System etc.)

kdesu is for graphical apps in KDE (Kubuntu) for the same thing

sudo or su is for most other things.

Though be careful using them, you can cause a lot of damage, hope this helps :)
