### Post by smartboyathome on 2008-03-09
These are called "Virtual Desktops". They are used to better organize applications. To switch them, click on the panes at the bottom right of your screen, and it will switch to that one. You can also do control+alt+left/right arrows to switch to a different desktop. To move a window to another virtual desktop, you can either use super (windows key) + e and drag the windows from one desktop to another, or control+alt+shift+left/right arrows.
