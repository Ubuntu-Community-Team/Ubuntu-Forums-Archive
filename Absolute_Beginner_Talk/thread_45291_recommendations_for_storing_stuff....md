### Post by sonny on 2005-06-29
If by apps you mean the installers, then the answer is /home/username, your home directory is the only place you SHOULD store anything not belonging to the system configuration or something use by the OS. You can create some folders call "My Downloads" "Movies" "PDF" etc... do you get the idea?

The only one thing I ALWAYS recommend is to have your /home directory within a separeted partition, that way you can mess your system, if anything goes wrong, then do a complete reinstall without having to backup your files, or loosing them.
