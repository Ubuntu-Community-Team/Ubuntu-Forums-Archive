### Post by MikeSTL on 2005-06-24
To the whole Ubuntu team:

I spent the day today trying out every distro that sounded interesting...FC 3, DSL, Vector, Xandros, Slack, and Ubuntu.

Without a doubt, Ubuntu has won me over! I come from years of being an admin for MacOS (9 and X) and Windoze environments. I hate to say it, but I have been searching for a simple to install, use, and update distro that would compare to MacOS and Windoze, and not require Linus' phone number for tech support. Ubuntu is it for me. It does the job perfectly.

Since I settled on Ubuntu this afternoon, I have spent the day installing everything from Scribus to MySQL. Every install went without a hitch...this is unbelieveable to me. The last distro I seriously used for any ammount of time was RH5. Boy have things changed!

Keep up the good work, and thanks again to everyone.

Mike
