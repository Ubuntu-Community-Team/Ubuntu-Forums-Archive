### Post by Penguin Power on 2008-03-26
It's a mobile core2duo, so you can install either IA32 or IA64. There are some performance reasons for choosing IA64,but for compatability (particularly with flash) you are better sticking to the IA32 edition of Ubuntu Gutsy. 

It's time-consuming and not completely effective to try and work out whether everything will work before you install Ubuntu. Just burn a liveCD and give it a go. Most hardware is compatible, but if you use wifi you may want to check that your wifi chip/dongle/adapter is going to work; it's something which is often incompatible.
