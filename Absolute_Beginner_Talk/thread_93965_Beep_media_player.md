### Post by bored2k on 2005-11-23
[LIST=1]
[*]Go to **$HOME/.bmp**
[*]Create a folder called **Skins**
[*]Extract your skins and place the Skin folder there.
[*]Go to BMP and select them in your Preferences menu.
[/LIST]

[LIST]
[*]Auto-presets? You could save your presets from Winamp and load them in BMP.

[*]DFX or similar? Not to my knowledge.
[/LIST]
