### Post by Krydahl on 2008-01-24
I'm pretty much a noob. I installed Feisty and then did a reinstall of Gutsy a month or so after it came out.

I found the differences between the two (in terms of how to set up and configure) minor and usually improvements. They certainly caused me no troubles.

I know some folk have had trouble with Gutsy and it depends on your hardware, I guess, but I'd say if you want to end up with Gutsy on the machine, go for it from the start. After all, if it all goes horribly wrong, you can still drop back to Feisty.
