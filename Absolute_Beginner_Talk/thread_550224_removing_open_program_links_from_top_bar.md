### Post by rsambuca on 2007-09-13
On the top panel, you will see a vertical row of 3 dots that indicate the presence of an applet.  Right click on the dots and select about.  If it says Window List, then that is the one you want to get rid of.  Right click it again and select Remove From Panel.
