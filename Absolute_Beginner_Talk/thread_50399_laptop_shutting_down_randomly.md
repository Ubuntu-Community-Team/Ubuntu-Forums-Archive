### Post by rsay on 2005-07-20
Tanix,

I have a Dell Inspiron 5100 and I was having the same problem a while back. I called dell tech support and they had never heard of such a problem and quickly blamed linux. I later went to one of their web forums and found several long threads that explained that certain of their laptop models have overheating problems that cause them to shutdown. 

I now take canned air and blast the ventilation ports on the bottom, back and sides of my laptop about every six weeks and my problem has gone away. You'll know when you get "the right one" when you see the big cloud of dust come out. If you search google with: Dell Inspiron overheating   you can get more information about this well known problem.
