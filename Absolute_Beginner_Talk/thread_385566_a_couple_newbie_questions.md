As far as backing up see my last post. The big difference is whether you want to just save files or an image of you computer at its current state. 

By other distros you mean Redhat(Fedora), Debian, Suse. Its really just the way the OS is setup. Filesystem, default Desktop (GNOME,KDE), packages included, etc..

As far as which one is for you.  This is really up to your preferences. Ty Ubuntu, then maybe try Fedora Core, but try to avoid Gentoo (If you really want to use it go ahead but be warned  it is manual install) if your new to Linux and computers. People will tell you that one distro better than the other but it's really what you like that matters.

If you mean Xubuntu, Kubuntu, Ubunntu, edubuntu

As for which files to backup first are all documents that u have generated over time (.doc, .xls, .pps etc), pictures etc. Hopefully they are all centralized like in "My Documents".
