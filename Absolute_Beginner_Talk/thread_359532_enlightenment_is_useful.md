### Post by Sunflower1970 on 2007-02-12
I had enlightenment installed for a time on a PII 400 Mhz machine, and although I did notice a bit of speed difference, in the end, it wasn't speedy enough for me. I ended up with IceWM on it instead.

Enlightenemt is pretty--lots of eyecandy, my only problem is it didn't seem intuitive enough for me...and I had a few crashed with the taskbar at the bottom of the screen if I played around with it too much. But, one feature I loved with it was how I could use my mouse to go through each desktop. I took it all the way to the right of the screen it allowed me to scroll through each one.
