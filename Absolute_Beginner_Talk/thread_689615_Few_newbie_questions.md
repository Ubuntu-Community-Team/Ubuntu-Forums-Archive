### Post by jakelute on 2008-02-06
Reply from another newbie: I strongly recommend the Live CD. You can run Ubuntu off the Live CD (though it will of course run more slowly than it would if it were installed) to see if everything on your system works in Ubuntu, and the Live CD won't touch your XP installation (unless you tell it to).

### Post by MariusSilverwolf on 2008-02-06
In addition to the other suggestions of running from the LiveCD first, you can also run a search of the forums for your PC model number.  There's a decent chance of finding others who have had problems and getting the information on how to fix troublesome issues before you reach them.

If you choose to install the OS, look for some How-To's on installing in a dual-boot format before you take that leap.  They can be instrumental in helping you avoid any data loss on your XP partition.
