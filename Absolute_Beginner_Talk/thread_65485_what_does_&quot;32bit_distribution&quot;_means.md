### Post by endy on 2005-09-14
It sounds like you have downloaded the wrong version for your type of processor. If you have a regular Intel or AMD CPU, or if you're sure it's a safe bet you need the "Intel x86" version which you can find here:

[http://us.releases.ubuntu.com/releases/5.04/ubuntu-5.04-install-i386.iso](http://us.releases.ubuntu.com/releases/5.04/ubuntu-5.04-install-i386.iso)

Burn that to CD and try again :)
