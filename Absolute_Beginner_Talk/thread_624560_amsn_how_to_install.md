### Post by wheredidrealitygo on 2007-11-27
In your system menu, go to administration, and then synaptic package manager.

From there it will ask for your password, enter it, then select "amsn" from the list of packages there, right click it, click "mark for installation", and then at the top of the screen click "apply" and it will install :)
