### Post by CatKiller on 2006-11-01
> **Wikzo said:**
> Then it works with laptops (not Mac's) or not?

Yes. 6.10 and 6.06 will both work on laptops.

If you have a laptop with not very much memory, you might consider Xubuntu, which is less memory-intesive.

There's not a huge amount of difference between Dapper and Edgy - Edgy has software versions that are four months newer, but Dapper has been tested more thoroughly. Some people have reported that it is easier to get some wireless networking set up with Dapper.

### Post by PriceChild on 2006-11-01
Dapper Drake (6.06) is a LTS (Long term release) version.

It will be supported with security updates and bugfixes for the next 3 years on the desktop(/laptop) and 5 years on the server.

Edgy Eft (6.10) is "edgy"... not as stable as Dapper. There's also been a few regressions because of the new untried technologies being incorporated ready for Feisty Fawn (7.04 due out april next year)

As a beginner i STRONGLY suggest you get used to Dapper Drake :)
