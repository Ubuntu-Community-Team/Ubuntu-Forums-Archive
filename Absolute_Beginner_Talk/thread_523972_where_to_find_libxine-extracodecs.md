### Post by linuxwizard on 2007-08-12
You will need to enable universe & multiverse repositories.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/6.06/kubuntu/desktopguide/C/codecs.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/6.06/kubuntu/desktopguide/C/codecs.html)

Look down page about half way down will give you step by step to enable the extra repositories    (look for Edgy Eft (6.10) and older)
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Kubuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Kubuntu)

Once enabled you will be able to get what you need
