### Post by mc4man on 2008-02-29
K9copy can reauthor the title to a movie only very easily.
After loading the dvd in k9 on the left side is a tree view of title. Find the titleset/title  that has main movie and check the video, audio stream(s) and subs you want. On bottom right side is 'keep original menus' - make sure it's unchecked.
Pcgedit can ( re)name subs (or audio)  (theres a linux version or win version in wine)
After loading dvd find the VTST that is the movie, highlight it, and under the Domain tab pick domain stream attributes. In front of each subpic listing is a box for language codes - add or change as needed. For example english is en, french is fr, ect. There is a listing tab of all iso639 codes at bottom of dialog box.
