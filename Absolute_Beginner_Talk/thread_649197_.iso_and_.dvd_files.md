### Post by asmiller-ke6seh on 2007-12-24
TO backup my (commercial) DVDs, I use K9COPY. It will transcode DVD-9 (dual layer) down to DVD-5 (single layer), which saves money. For DVD-9s which I can't successfully transcode, I simply use K3B to duplicate DVD-9 to DVD-9 through a straight duplication. There has been only one DVD that I couldn't backup using either of these two methods. (sure beats trying to watch a DVD that the kids have used as a skimboard across the rumpus room :))
