### Post by ryth on 2008-04-22
Just a heads up but I installed that passive cooler (with the turbo pack addition) the other week and it works like a dream.  I did a few tests and idle it was running at 51oC without the turbo pack installed, with it was 46oC.  Straining the video pumped it up to 58/49.  

To compare with the stock fully active (and loud as hell) fan it would run at 65 idle and 90+ when worked.

I should mention I don't have the best ventilated case, but...

Oh, and did I mention that even with the turbo fans it is essentially silent?

Best $30 i've invested in a while.
