### Post by mdsmedia on 2007-10-06
In a perfect install you won't lose anything. If you don't backup, though, and a mistake is made in partitioning, or any other possible error in resizing, you MAY lose data.

If you don't backup before partitioning you always run the risk of losing data.

Defragmenting XP is a good idea before partitioning, to get a clean area of your disk for partitioning. As previously stated, defrag a couple of times and delete temp files to allow maximum space for partitioning.
