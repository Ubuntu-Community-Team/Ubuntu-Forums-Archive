### Post by T700 on 2006-07-07
Assuming you burned a Live CD, you put it in your computer and reboot.  If the PC is not set to boot from a CD, you must go in the BIOS and change that.  How you do that depends on the make and model of the PC--check the documentation.

### Post by Jagot on 2006-07-07
