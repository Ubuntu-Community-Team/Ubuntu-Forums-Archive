### Post by kostkon on 2008-03-30
Do you mean that you have sound but you just lost your speaker applet that was on the panel? If that's the case then just right click on the panel, select *Add to Panel* and add the *Volume Control* applet.

Then, right click on the speaker (that has obviously appeared), and select *Preferences* to choose which sound device volume level you want it to control. 

If you want to access the volume levels of all your sound devices, just right click on it and select *Open Volume Control*.
