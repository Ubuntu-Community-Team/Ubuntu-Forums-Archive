### Post by PointSource on 2007-01-31
The easiest, but most disabling pre-emptive solution is not to lock the screens.

when and if it does hang, then CTRL+ALT+BACKSPACE should restart the X server, but all your applications, etc related to the locked session will be lost.

(hotkey only tested by me on kubuntu)
