### Post by Alterax on 2007-05-18
Glad to see that you're not afraid of the terminal.  You'll find there is a LOT that you can do with it.

My recommendation is to take a trip to the bookstore.  All of the commands used in Ubuntu are really nothing more than gnu/linux commands.  They are pretty similar across distributions; distributions tend more to differ on where they put the different programs.

The books I recommend are the O'Reilly's pocket Linux guide (invaluable).  It's basically the manual pages printed and bound together.  Add a book on linux certification if you want to know more about what is going on under the hood.  But if you REALLY want to do well with the terminal, try Unix Power Tools.  (Yes, that is Unix, with a 'U'--the commands are very similar).

Best of luck!

--Alterax
