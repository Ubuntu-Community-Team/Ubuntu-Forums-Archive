### Post by kolinab on 2007-03-13
Have a look at:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam/Acer](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam/Acer)

Have you opened up the volume control applet in the notification area and diddled around in there yet? You might have to go to Edit . . . preferences . . . and check all the boxes so you can see the input settings for your mic. In there you can look to see if your microphone is muted (the little box under the level control). Turn the input levels all the way up (for testing), select 'mic' as the input source. The details of what show up inside this volume control program may differ a bit from machine to machine.

How are you testing your mic? I have had good luck testing mine with the little sound recorder program under Applications . . . Sound and Video.

Have a try with those 'first steps' if you haven't already and let us know how it goes - we can roll up our sleeves if we need to!
