### Post by az on 2005-10-06
On a pentium 350 MHz, with 128 megs of ram, things run well.

Any lower than that will become too sluggish for me, but your mileage may vary.

You will be able to install with 32 megs of ram, but you will not be able to run X.  You need a minimum of 128 megs of ram and perhaps more if you do a few things at the same time.

You need a slightly larger than 2 gig hard drive to fit everything.
