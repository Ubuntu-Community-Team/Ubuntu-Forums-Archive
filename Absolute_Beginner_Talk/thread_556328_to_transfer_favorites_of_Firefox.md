### Post by DapperMe17 on 2007-09-21
There are all kinds of ways!


Easiest would be to simply e'mail them to yourself.


Go to Bookmarks, then Manage Bookmarks, then Export....you will them choose to copy to your Desktop.


Now, you have a file to e'mail. 


From the other PC, you would follow the same step, only you will Import from the Bookmark Manage section of Firefox on the second PC.
