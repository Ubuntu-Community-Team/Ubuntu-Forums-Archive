### Post by jaytek13 on 2008-02-13
I've experienced this same problem. The high pitched hum was coming from my monitor. When I would turn the monitor off, it would go away. At first if I left it off for a few minutes then turned it back on, it would take care of the humming for a little while, but eventually come back. It finally came to the point where nothing I did would make the noise go away. But, I discovered it actually had to do with my resolution. I was running at 1280x1024, which I had been doing ever since I got the monitor without incident. But after this humming started, I ascertained that by reducing the resolution to 1024x768, the humming would go away. 

Eventually I replaced the monitor because I hate 1024x768, but for the time being it was better to reduce the resolution than listen to that high pitched squeal that hurt my ears.

I guess with a laptop it might not be the same issue, but they do sound very similar. I'd try reducing your resolution.
