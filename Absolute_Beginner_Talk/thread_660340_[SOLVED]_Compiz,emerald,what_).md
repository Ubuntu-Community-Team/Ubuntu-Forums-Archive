### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-01-06
well emerald needs compiz to run.
basically compiz by default uses metacity as a backdrop, but if you like crystal themes and such then emerald might be your answer.
if you are seeing no effects then most likely you turned compiz off, to turn it back on just right click on your desktop and select "change desktop background"
the last tab is what you want, just click it and if it says "no desktop effects" then you can re enable it by using the selections given...
just select what you want and you are running in effects again

also you can remove emerald easily if you dont like it, just go into the package manager and remove it...
if you need a tutorial I can give one if needed
