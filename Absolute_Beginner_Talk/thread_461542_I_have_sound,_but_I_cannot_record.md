### Post by aktiwers on 2007-06-01
Double-click on the sound icon in your panel (the one you can use to turn on/off sound). I think microphone is disabled by default. Remove the red cross there.

If that doesnt work, go to Edit => Preferences and look if something could be missing.
I have ticked "Mic Select" in there, because I connect my Mic from in front of my PC. This way, I can select "Mic 2" and then it works.
