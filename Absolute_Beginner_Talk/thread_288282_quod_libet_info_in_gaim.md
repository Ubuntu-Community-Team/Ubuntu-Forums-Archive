### Post by tronica on 2006-10-29
i think you just download the file, and extract anywhere

then lets say you extracted it to your desktop

> cd /home/yourname/Desktop/gaim-ql.tar.gz_FILES

> sudo mv gaim-ql.so /usr/lib/gaim

then go into gaim and enable it in your plugins. It worked for me.
