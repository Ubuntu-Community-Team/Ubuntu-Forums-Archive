> **Xalan said:**
> heh on that note i'm running 6.10 here just in case it might be in different spots

I'm not sure where these controls are located when using the KDE interface, however in Gnome they are located under System>Preferences. There you will find the Screensaver tab as well as the Power management tab. It is the latter which is shutting your monitor off. Just uncheck the option which instructs the PC to power the monitor down after a certain period of time if you wish it to remain on indefinitely.
