### Post by old_geekster on 2007-06-22
Hi Folks,

Here is one for the record.  I am using Feisty with all of the updates.

I have a "Lexar SmartMedia Reader".  For those of you who don't know the product, it is about as cheap as you can go and still expect it to work properly.

Well, for the long and short of it, I placed my SmartMedia card in the reader and it actually worked without any tweaking.  I was able to upload the pictures on the card without any problems.

Now, to me, this is a great achievement on the part of the "Ubuntu Development group".  If this little known card works out of the box, they have done something right.

Kudos Ubuntu Devs!!
