### Post by xopher on 2007-04-08
You have to set your BIOS to boot from CD, or if your BIOS supports it, press a key to enter a 'boot menu' - from where you select the appropriate device.

Enter your BIOS by pressing eg. DEL or F2 "when the computer calculates the available RAM" - if you know what I mean, when you see the first text at boot. The button varies on different BIOSes, and it tells you what button to press too.. 'Press xx to enter Setup/BIOS'.
