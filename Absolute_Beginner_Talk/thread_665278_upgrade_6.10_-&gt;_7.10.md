### Post by finer recliner on 2008-01-12
backup everything in your home directory (the hidden files/folders in particular). applications store personal settings in this manner. for example, the email client Mozilla Thunderbird stores all its settings and configuration in ~/.mozilla-thunderbird.

i suggest upgrading to 7.10 (by reformatting) and then restore all the personal settings files and folders that you backed up to your new home folder. this will restore you personal settings

hope this helps
