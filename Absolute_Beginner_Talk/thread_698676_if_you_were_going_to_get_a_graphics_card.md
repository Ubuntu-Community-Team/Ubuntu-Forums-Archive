### Post by Presto123 on 2008-02-16
Absolutely, Nvidia. It is much easier to work with (at least from the problems I've seen with ATI).

I would stick with the higher end of whatever you decide to get like 6800 instead of 6200 (6200 is my card and at the lower end of cards). It all depends on what you want...if you want to run Vista games, go with 8 series for the DirectX10 support...if you are solely wanting XP and/or Ubu, you could stick with the 6 and 7 series. 

If you're strapped for cash on this, go to [http://www.newegg.com](http://www.newegg.com) and they will have some really good deals. I think 256mb's are running about $20-30 right now.
