### Post by Marsolin on 2006-08-20
For stand alone tag editors, I'd try Audio Tag Tool, EasyTAG, or Kid3. Additional options can be found at the following link: [http://linuxappfinder.com/multimedia/audiotageditors](http://linuxappfinder.com/multimedia/audiotageditors)

Rhythmbox does have some tag editing capabilities of its own too.
