### Post by spacefreak86 on 2008-02-19
As far as the start menu, right click on the panel on the top, click properties, change it so that its on the bottom. Then click "add to panel," put the main menu where the "Start" button usually is, customize it from there.

For the desktop, click on System Tools -> Configuration Editor. Go to apps -> nautilus -> desktop. Click on computer_icon_visible, home_icon_visible, and trash_icon_visible. Unselect volumes_visible, as these will be in the computer icon on your desktop. Close out of there 

Then go to System -> Preferences -> Keyboard Shortcuts. Scroll down to "Show the panel menu", click on the space to the right. Press the left "Windows" key. This will enable it so that when you press the windows key, the menu bar comes up. Hit close, and then test it to make sure it works.
