### Post by useResa on 2007-06-01
I assume you are using Ubuntu with Gnome Desktop.
If you like to take a screenshot of a menu, you first select **Applications --> Accessories --> Take Screenshot**. Select to take a screenshot of the Entire Window and set the delay to -- for example -- 3 seconds (see attachment screenshotDelay.png) and click on "**Take screenshot**".

During the delay open the menu you would like to have a screenshot of and keep it open until the screenshot is taken. Next save the file. As an example see attachment partialMenuExample.png (only part of the screenshot attached)

I hope that this answers your question.
