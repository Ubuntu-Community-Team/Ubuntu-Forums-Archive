### Post by techno-mole on 2007-07-12
ok, ive had this problem aswell.
firstly id suggest going through the how to on multimedia on these forums.(its at the top of the absolute beginner forum)
then id do sudo apt-get install vlc, or install it through synaptic.
for xine id go to xine hq (search for it in google if you like) or you can install xine from the repo's ?
vlc or xine should play most dvds, provding you have the right codecs and such like installed.
if you open vlc, you should be able to just click on open disc and then click dvd (one along from dvd menus) and then click ok, and it should play.
for xine you should be able to just click on dvd once its installed properly.
