### Post by atomic0x on 2005-12-28
Did you add the window list panel applet back to the panel?  You won't be able to see the tasks with out it.

Right-click on the newly created panel and choose "add to panel..."
type "window list" in the search box, choose "window list" icon, click on add.  

Hope this helps :)
