### Post by rjmoerland on 2008-03-30
I had something similar, with a terminal always opening eventhough it was closed when I rebooted. In my case, this was due to the fact that I once had the option to save the session for future use on, and rebooted while I had the terminal open. Then, after a restart, I unchecked the option to save the session. Nevertheless, the old session was still used after a login, giving me the unwanted terminal. 

The solution was closing the terminal (and all other programs), check the option to save the session, reboot, and uncheck the 'save session' option again. 

However, in my case it was a sure-fire thing, not on rare occasions. HTH though
