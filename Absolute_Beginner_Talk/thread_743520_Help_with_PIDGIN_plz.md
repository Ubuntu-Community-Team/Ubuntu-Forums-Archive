Hi!
As far as I know, you can't see the e-mail address as an option (someone please correct me if I'm wrong ;) ). However, you could set **Aliases** for your contact. Just right click on the contact and select **Alias...**. Then, instead of seeing those horrible nicknames (which I hate too, I never know who I'm talking to), you'll see the **Alias** you set. You could just type the e-mail address if you want. The only problem is that you'll have to set them one by one, what may take some time, specially having 200 contact...
Good luck! :KS
