### Post by laidback on 2007-05-11
If you have the speaker symbol in the section at the top of your screen, right click it, then select Preferences. This allows you to choose which control option the volume slider operates. Try PC speakers (or any others) in an attempt to find one that works for you. When I use skype I need to turn off my speakers leaving the headphones working, that's how I do it.
