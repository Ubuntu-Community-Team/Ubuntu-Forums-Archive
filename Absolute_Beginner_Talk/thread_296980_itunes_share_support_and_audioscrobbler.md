### Post by taelatus on 2006-11-10
After a day of trying out new media applications (Banshee, Listen, Exaile, etc.) my rhythmbox is starting very slowly.  It hangs for about 15 seconds now before the inferface shows up now.  I've noticed that I have two new features under my rhythmbox preferences: itunes share support and audioscrobbler support.  I don't need/want either of these.  Does anyone know which packages are responsible for the two listed features?  Does anyone know a simple way to get rid of those features?

---

### Post by taelatus on 2006-11-11
Solved.  I had to remove Avahi packages from my system to prevent Rhythmbox from searching iTunes shares at startup.
