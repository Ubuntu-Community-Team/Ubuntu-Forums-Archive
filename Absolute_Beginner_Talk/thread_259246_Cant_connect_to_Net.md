### Post by coffeecat on 2006-09-17
You'll find a howto for the Speedtouch modem [here]("http://www.linux-usb.org/SpeedTouch/"). A word of warning. It does work but as you are new to Linux you may find it daunting. A word of explanation. The Speedtouch is popular but is really designed for Windows. And it's a usb device and can seriously reduce your broadband speed. (USB was not designed for networking.) You would be much better off getting yourself an adsl modem-router which can be configured with a web browser. Apart from the fact that it doesn't matter which OS you use on your computer you get the added security of a built-in firewall in the router and NAT.

As far as your ATI card is concerned, [this link]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto") may help, but to be honest I have no experience of ATI cards. I use nvidia which are more Linux friendly. If that link doesn't help, try a forum search.
