### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2006-11-12
> **unrealmstr said:**
> im not retarded]

No one said you were :p 

I think you will be able to set up dual monitors if you read a little and spend 45 minutes or so at it.

FYI: After you edit xorg.conf you need to restart X for the effects to take effect. Ctrl-Alt-Backspace ...

If you are like me you will not get it right on the first try. In this event X will crash and you will be stuck with a CLI only.

If you can vi or nano, fine. If not:```
sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf.bak /etc/X11/xorg.conf
```Will restore X, but you will lose your work. Perhaps:```
sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.conf.work_in_progress
```Before you restore xong.conf from backup.
