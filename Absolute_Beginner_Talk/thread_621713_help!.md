Help is available.  One source is the [**_XChat Howto_**]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XChatHowto") which will help you set up XChat.

In addition, the server usually used for help with Ubuntu is irc.freenode.net.  Also, in addition to the #ubuntu channel, which can be very busy at times, there are various LoCo Team channels available.  See [**_Ubuntu LoCo Team Forums_**]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=183") for a list of available LoCo's.  LoCo Teams vary as to how active they are on channel, and what their expertise is.  However they can be an alternative source of information for some problems.

Whatever way you choose, I wish you the best of luck.
