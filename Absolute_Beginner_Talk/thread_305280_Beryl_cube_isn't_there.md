### Post by rigol on 2006-11-25
As to the actually visible cube: fiddle with the settings in the Beryl Settings Manager, ecspecially  Rotate Cube --> Numeric Values --> Zoom. 
Plus, you can see the cube with ctrl-alt-holdleftmousebutton and moving the mouse.
