Suspend and Hibernate do not always work for all machines in Linux. They are also not well supported because most times the hardware manufacturers only test their hardware and drivers with Windows, because that's what the machine is shipped with.

Having said that, many have found success by using uswsusp. Give it a search in the forums and you shall find a bunch of posts on it.
