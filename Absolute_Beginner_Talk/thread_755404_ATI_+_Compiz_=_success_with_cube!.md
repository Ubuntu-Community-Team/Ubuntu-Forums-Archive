### Post by anewguy on 2008-04-14
If any of you have seen my previous posts regarding video card versus motheboard compatibility issues, I just wanted to post that I finally succeeded!  I bought a used ATI9250 off of EBay and installed it today.  Since it is a brand new installation of Ubuntu Gutsy, rather than mess with getting drivers working I just reinstalled Gutsy.  Nothing could have been smoother!  It installed with the ati driver and xorg configured beautifully.  I installed the compiz settings manager, turned on the cube, made 4 desktops, and enabled advanced settings and have the "jumpy" windows thing enabled (pretty technical, eh?).  At any rate, I have the cube, the bouncy windows, etc..  Now for the best part:

If you have an older machine you can still get compiz going.  I have an old Biostar M6TZA motherboard, slot-1 PIII-500, 512mb of memory and the ATI 9250 video card.  This is an old, and usually fairly slow machine, but Compiz and the cube run great!

My thanks to EVERYONE who has tried to help me the couple of frustrating weeks!  Also, a big thanks to those who wrote the tutorials on using Compiz and the cube!
