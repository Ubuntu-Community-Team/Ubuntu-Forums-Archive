### Post by p_quarles on 2007-06-05
Dunno who manufactured your computer, but here's how to boot from CD on the three biggest makers:

Dell: Hit F12 during the initial startup; make sure CD/DVD is the first option

HP: Hit ESC during startup; ditto

Mac: Hold down the "c" key during startup
