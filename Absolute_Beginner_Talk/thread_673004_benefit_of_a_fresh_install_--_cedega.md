### Post by ChaosParser on 2008-01-20
If you're replacing your mobo, a fresh install to redetect the hardware would be beneficial.  Just click view and the show hidden files and backup your entire /home directory.  

In the future, /home on its on partition is lovely.  You can reinstall the OS to your hearts content and never loose configs and data.
