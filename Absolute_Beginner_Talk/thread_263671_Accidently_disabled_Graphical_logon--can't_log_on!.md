### Post by samma on 2006-09-23
I'm using Ubuntu 6 and I was unwittingly messing with the settings and disabled graphical logon.  immediately after I did so, it switched from GNOME to text mode.  I rebooted and to my relief got the graphical logon screen.  After I typed in my user name and password, it switched to text mode for a second, and then put me right back at the logon screen!  How do I fix this so that I can continue using GNOME and Ubuntu?

---

### Post by bulldog on 2006-09-23
Take a look in your sessions menu and see if you have ticked in the first tab the lowest option.
Something like keep the changes made in this sesion [have a dutch version]

Untick it and logout CTRL-ALT-BACKSPACE and login again.
 Now it should be normal again.
