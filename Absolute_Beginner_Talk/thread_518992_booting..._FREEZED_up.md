### Post by basketballer on 2007-08-06
Check your hardware.. The minimum requirements are:

      300 MHz x86 processor

      64 MB of system memory (RAM)

      At least 2 GB of disk space (for full installation and swap space)

      VGA graphics card capable of 640x480 resolution

      CD-ROM drive


if all are fine with you, then check the CD you're booting from.
