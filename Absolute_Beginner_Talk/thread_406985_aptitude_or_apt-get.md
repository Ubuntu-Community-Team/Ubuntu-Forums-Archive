### Post by aysiu on 2007-04-11
*aptitude* isn't superior to *apt-get*. *aptitude* tries its hardest to resolve dependency issues. Sometimes it's very good at doing that. Sometimes it fails miserably.

*apt-get* is pretty straightforward at adding and removing stuff. There may be *some* situations where *aptitude* will handle things better, but if you're using 6.10 (Edgy Eft) or 7.04 (Feisty Fawn), *apt-get*'s probably the way to go.
