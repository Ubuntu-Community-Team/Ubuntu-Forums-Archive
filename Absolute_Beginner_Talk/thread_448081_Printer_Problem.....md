### Post by Sef on 2007-05-18
Read [LinuxPrinting]("http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-E232").   It says and gives directions on how to get your printer work under GNU/Linux.   You are lucky because in general, Lexmark printers do not work under GNU/Linux.   Note:  That applies to their consumer printers;  the business printers (Optra) do usually work under GNU/Linux.
