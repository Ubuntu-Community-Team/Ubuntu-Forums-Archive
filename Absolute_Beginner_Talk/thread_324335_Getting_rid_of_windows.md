### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-12-23
Is your puter able to boot other CDs? If so then the CD you burned was probably burned wrong. When you burn the image to the CD, you need to find an option that says to burn the **image**. If you burn it as an image, when you look at the CD contents afterwards, you will see lots of files, not just the .iso file. In this case, the CD should also be bootable.
