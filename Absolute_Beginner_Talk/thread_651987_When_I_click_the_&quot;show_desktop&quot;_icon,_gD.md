### Post by dtgolder on 2008-02-21
I have the same problem here, and it's supposed to be with compiz enabled. Try this--it worked for me (but it is still a bug):

Go to:

System...Preferences...Appearance

Click the "Visual Effects" tab

Turn off all visual effects

Click close, and reboot. Should now keep your gdesklets on the desktop, but you don't get the cool compiz window effects (too bad--guess it depends on what's more important to you)
