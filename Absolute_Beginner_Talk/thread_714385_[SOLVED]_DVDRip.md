### Post by Arthur Archnix on 2008-03-03
First confirm that you can play encrypted dvd's. If you cannot watch them in totem or mplayer then you cannot rip them either. VLC doesn't count as it has its own codecs built in.

If you can't head over to [medibuntu]("http://medibuntu.org/") for instructions on installing dvd playback support and it might fix your dvd problem.

Here's more info from Ubuntu on [restricted formats]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs").
