### Post by MisterD on 2007-05-27
It's not really "corrupting" the line, it's just parsing it as a smiley, you can use some vB code to tell it not to parse your text in certain areas to avoid this, as so...

```
[noparse][noparse]about:plugins[/noparse][/noparse]
```

Which will make it appear like this in your post...

[noparse]about:plugins[/noparse]

Voila, no more smileys, unless you really want them :)
