### Post by Gyrotwister on 2006-07-27
It is possible to get Conexant internal modems and other makes of internal modems working in Ubuntu without the 14.4 Kbps handicap.  I managed to get mine going by following this howto thread by Matchless.  

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=190728&highlight=conexant](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=190728&highlight=conexant)

Unfortunately you need to have a working internet connection to gather all the files and packages needed to compile a driver.  I purchased a slowish external modem off ebay to do this.  I've also learded that I have to recompile the driver whenever the kernel is updated.  Hopefully this won't happen too often.  

It would be nice though, if there was an internal dialup modem which was known to be Ubuntu friendly right out of the box.
