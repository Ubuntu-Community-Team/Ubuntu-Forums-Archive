### Post by spur on 2007-08-13
I would suspect that you need to select 'gnome' for your session. To do this log out and choose options and gnome as your session. Then each time you  start it should be chosen auto.
Disabling the 3d effects does not then choose your session manager which is why you can't control things the way you are used to.
