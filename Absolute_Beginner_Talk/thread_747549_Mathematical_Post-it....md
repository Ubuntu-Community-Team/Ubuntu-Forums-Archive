### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-04-06
You can put liitle post-its on your desktop by using the Sticky Notes applet.  Right click an empty space in a panel and hit Add to Panel.  Then select Sticky Notes and hit Add.  Then you can right click the sticky note applet and create a New Note.  You can write in it whatever you want, including your favorite math formulas.
