### Post by jordanmthomas on 2006-09-09
Go to System --> Preferences --> Keyboard
Go to the Layouts tab
Try to change the Keybord model to something more appropriate.

(To get all my keys to work I had to choose a model other than what mine was, even though my keyboard was listed!)

In the meantime, you could always copy and paste spaces.  lol

### Post by jordanmthomas on 2006-09-09
