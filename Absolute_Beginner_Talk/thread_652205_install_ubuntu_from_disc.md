### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-12-28
I would strongly recommend you read about Ubuntu and how it works before doing anything.

This is brief and very helpful:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/)

After that, then your idea of just putting the CD in and booting **from the CD** will be pretty straight forward.  If you want to keep anything on your hard drive - make sure you back up before starting.  If you want to keep Windows, then do more reading about dual-booting before going any further.  If not - just follow instructions.
