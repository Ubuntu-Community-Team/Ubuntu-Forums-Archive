### Post by WaySensei on 2008-01-22
After hunting around a bit more, I stumbled across a fix that I'd like to share. 
All I had to do was type: 
```
sudo depmod -a
``` into the terminal, reboot, and VOILA! everything was back to normal. I feel stupid that the answer was so easy :/
