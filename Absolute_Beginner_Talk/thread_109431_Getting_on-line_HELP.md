### Post by bonzodog on 2005-12-29
The BT voyagers are a known problem in linux. They are USB only 'soft modems'. We generally recommend that it is worth considering obtaining a good ethernet router and modem, which will rob you of a small bit, but you will get a guaranteed connection. I was lucky enough that my ISP gave me a good ethernet router free with my connection.
