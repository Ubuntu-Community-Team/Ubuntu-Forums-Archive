### Post by Brunellus on 2005-11-22
Brian Ward, *How Linux Works: What Every Superuser Should Know* (No Starch Press).

Very engaging.  Explains without insulting your intelligence.  Very thorough grounding with the command-line;  good explanations of networking.  Some advanced topics;  a good reference to keep by the computer.
