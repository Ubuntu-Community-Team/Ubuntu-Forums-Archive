### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-03-18
Check out [Ogre3d](http://www.ogre3d.org/) and [Crystal Space](http://www.crystalspace3d.org/main/Main_Page) for a good 3D engine that is cross-platform.  I've messed with Ogre3D a little.

They are only 3D engines, so you'll have to use SDL or something for input and sound.

Blender is good for creating models and stuff.

It's a lot of work.
