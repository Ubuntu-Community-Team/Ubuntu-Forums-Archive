### Post by CheeseEatingBulldog on 2007-02-12
After installing kubuntu-desktop, it will grab all the KDE libs. (you could also install xubuntu as well).

Next, of you want to chose on login, go to System-->Admin-->login window Here make sure you don't have auto login, as it will then bypass the login screen at start and go to GDM on default.

Then logout and under the 'sessions' button at the login window you can chose KDE or Gnome etc etc. 

If you open a Kubuntu application under gnome it will open as well (it just takes a little longer than native gnome aps as it is loading the KDE libraries to do it.)
