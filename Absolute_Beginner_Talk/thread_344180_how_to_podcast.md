### Post by smiley2billion on 2007-01-25
Sounds like 90% of the work is done already then.  Once you get it uploaded head over to feedburner.com and give them your URL.  They offer tracking and allow people to play the file directly from that site.

Unless you're needing help with the whole getting it ready to upload process.  Which, in that case, I would use something to encode it to an MP3 (LAME encoder works well) and you'll be all set.
