### Post by Sef on 2006-03-19
To Uninstall:

Applications > Add Applications > Sound & Video > more programs > uncheck real player > apply > apply again > will uninstall it.

To install:

Applications > Add Applications > Sound & Video > more programs > check real player > apply > apply again > will install it.
