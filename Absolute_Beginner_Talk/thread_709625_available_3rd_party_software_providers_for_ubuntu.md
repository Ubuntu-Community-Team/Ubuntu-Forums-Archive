### Post by PeterJS on 2008-02-27
There is no master list of third party repositories, the number of them is simple to big, and there's no centeral authority they are required to, or even can, report to. Get-deb.net has a pretty good listing of individual third party applications. Some specific products provide third party repositores, but trying to catalog them all would be nearly impossible. Is the something specific you're looking for?

Have you enabled Universe, Multiverse, and Medibuntu?
