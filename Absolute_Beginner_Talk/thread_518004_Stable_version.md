### Post by fimbulvetr on 2007-08-05
The current stable version is 7.04.

Go to ubuntu.com, click "Download now" it's in the big area in the middle of the screen.

Alternatively, you can go to ubuntu.com, hover "Products" with your mouse in the upper right corner, and click "Download" when the option appears.

Then click "Desktop - 7.04", choose the type of computer and choose which location is nearest to you, then go to town!
