Well, I haven't used the migration assistant but I guess it failed. Not difficult to fix, you'll just have to manually get your bookmars/emails.

So, go into XP and under firefox, bookmarks > Organize bookmarks > File > Export. Save it to your desktop and drop it on a cd.

Then to get your emails open up thunderbird and I think ya want to follow this [guide.]("http://www.mozilla.org/support/thunderbird/profile#backup")

Sorry the migration assistant didn't work perfectly for you, no technology is fullproof. Hope that helps get ya up and running.
