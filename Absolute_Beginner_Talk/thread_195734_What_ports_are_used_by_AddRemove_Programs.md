seems localised. That is, it is a fix number of applications.

System > Administrator > Synaptic Package Manager

That is playing it safe as a lot of other programs may cause problems.

When you say you cannot connect to any servers, can you surf the web? That is all that is needed to find linux files, but as I said, synaptic is quality assured by ubuntu. Everything else is a risk.
