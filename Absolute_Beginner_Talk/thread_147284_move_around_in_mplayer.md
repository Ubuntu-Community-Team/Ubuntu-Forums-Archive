### Post by celloandy on 2006-03-20
Alternatively, the back and forward arrows will move you forward or backward in the stream.  A few other keyboard commands: "p" is for play/pause, "f" is for fullscreen, and "q" is for quit.  Running "man mplayer" at the terminal will give you a manual page that contains, among other things, a complete listing of the keyboard shortcuts mplayer recognizes.
