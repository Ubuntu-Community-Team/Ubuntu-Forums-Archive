### Post by aysiu on 2006-12-20
Right-click on the panel and select **Configure Panel** or whatever the *configure* option is (I don't have KDE in front of me right now). Then, go to **Appearance** and go to **Advanced Options** and choose to show the Applet Handles.

Then you should be able to drag the clock around.
