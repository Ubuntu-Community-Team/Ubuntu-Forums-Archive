### Post by Nobel on 2007-05-08
Don't quote me on this.

As far as i've heard, there is no composite support for the offical ATI drivers (Because AMD/ATI er slackers :) )

I think there are some open source drivers out there, made by reverse engineering, but i'm not sure how to find or install them, or if you they even support your cards.

You SHOULD be able to get 3D support with your card tho, for 3d apps and games. Have a look at :

[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Feisty_Installation_Guide]("http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Feisty_Installation_Guide")

For installation instructions.

Furthermore, i heard good things about the[Envy]("http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html") script. Again, i have had no luck installing these drivers myself, but that's just because i have an odd video card :)

Good luck!
