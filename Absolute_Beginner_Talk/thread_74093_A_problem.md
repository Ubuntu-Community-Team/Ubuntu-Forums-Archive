### Post by mlomker on 2005-10-10
> Ctrl+Alt+F2

Linux is multiuser--you could actually run more than one copy of your desktop if you wanted to.  Ctrl-Alt-F2 brings you to one of the 6 command prompts that are available.  Your regular gnome/kde session is actually on screen 7 (Ctrl-Alt-F7).  The next time the Ctrl-Backspace thing doesn't work then try switching to screen 2 and see if it is alive.  If you can't then that usually means a hardware problem...if you can switch to a terminal then it's just a gnome/kde issue.
