### Post by fresnofred on 2007-10-22
I could not get sound on my pc with ubuntu no matter what i did!  Here is how i fixed it!  I had my speaker, mike, line in plugged into my installed sound card.  I unplugged them and plugged them into the onboard sound card that came with my motherboard and now i have beautiful sound!
