### Post by Delkster on 2006-10-07
If you don't plan on gaming much, I might suggest that you just stick with the built-in radeon driver that comes with Ubuntu. It should provide some 3D acceleration (although it's not fast) and is generally quite stable and hassle-free.

ATI has dropped support for these old R200-based chips in their latest closed-source driver releases anyway.
