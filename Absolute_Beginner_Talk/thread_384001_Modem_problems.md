### Post by orkim on 2007-03-13
Usually your first stop with any modem related issue will be to check [http://linmodems.org/](http://linmodems.org/)

You can find most modem manufactures there and their associated models.  Typically if your modem is listed as a 'winmodem' you are most likely going to have some troubles.  In a sentence: 'winmodem' means that many of the features that were hardware only were implemented in software drivers.

There has been limited success with winmodems under Linux however.  You may want to search this forum for winmodems or Google with your specific model and Linux for further assistance.

I hope that helps!
