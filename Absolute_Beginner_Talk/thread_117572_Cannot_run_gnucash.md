### Post by cstudent on 2006-01-15
[QUOTE=saltscar]Yes - Great, many thanks, but can I get an icon on my desktop?[/QUOTE]


Goto Applications>>System Tools>>Applications Menu Editor. Select a main group you want to place the Gnucash item in. Click the New Item button and fill in the edit boxes with Name, Comment and Command. Command would be gnucash. You can select the icon. It is located in /usr/share/pixmaps/gnucash/gnucash-icon.png.

### Post by 23meg on 2006-01-15
You can also put a launcher on your desktop or Gnome panel. Right click the desktop, hit "create launcher" and enter "gnucash" as the "Command" string.
