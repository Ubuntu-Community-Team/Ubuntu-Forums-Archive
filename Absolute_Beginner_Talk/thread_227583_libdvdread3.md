### Post by GreenHawkIA on 2006-08-02
First off, IANAL.  I am not a lawyer, so that is important to understand.  This issue is on that requires more expertise than just me.  First off it depends on where you live.  It is certainly legal in some parts of the world, and is not in other parts of the world.  If you, like me, live in the US, the answer is most likely no, due to the anti-circumvention provisions of the Digital Millenium Copyright Act.
For more info:
[http://thomas.loc.gov/cgi-bin/query/z?c105:H.R.2281:](http://thomas.loc.gov/cgi-bin/query/z?c105:H.R.2281:)

If you live somewhere else, maybe some other user can help you.
