### Post by LowSky on 2008-03-17
For te most part UK English and US English are nearly the same (US english does change the spelling of few words but nothing too drastic).

As for update names being confusing, that isn't really a translation problem, but a communications issue. You see geeks and nerds use numbers and shortened names to label programs names. Nothing new and has been done for decades. What you really need to look into is creating better descriptions of what each update is for.
