### Post by gitfiddler on 2006-02-23
I don't have an answer for you, but I will say that based upon my experience, you will need to be patient. If the card is not automatically configured by either distribution, the process of manually configuring a new TV card is quite involved. Until someone else installs exactly the same card and take the time to work out the correct parameters, you are on your own.

Having said that, have you tried installing TvTime? You might be one of the lucky few who's card "just works"*.
