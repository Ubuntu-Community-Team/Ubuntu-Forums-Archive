### Post by LowSky on 2008-01-04
1. Europe uses PAL instead of NTSC which is only a setting while in the program and easily changed, also the US will be going all digital next february. Which means most computer tuners wont work without a converter box (this might not effect Satelite TV, but check with your provider).  

2. You can probably record to you computer but you will need a satelite box connected to the computer, withthe tuner tuned to a certain station, or used throught Video in port on a graphics card. Cable TV is alot easier with a computer tuner box.

3.the  issue is the power supply, I know europe uses different power prong than here in the US. They also use differnet rated power lines. I would look into that more.

4. LOL buy a cheaper case, as much as i like them silverstones they are EXPENSIVE, checkout [www.newegg.com](www.newegg.com) for cheaper cases. I just bought a Rosewill case for $30 shipped, and its a decent case. My regular case is a LianLi, which are really nice but also very expensive.
