### Post by qamelian on 2006-11-11
> **skowsa said:**
> Don't understand what that would have to do with it. I think the ISO took aout 3 minutes to burn at about 32x - honestly don't pay attention to the burn speed.

Ian
When you burn discs at higher speeds, they tend to be harder to read. Also, the faster you burn a disc, the less likely it is to work on other drives. It could just be that the PC is having to read and re-read the disc to get at the data. I've seen this a lot. As a result, I never burn any CD at fast than 4X if it might be used on a drive other than the one on which I burned it.
