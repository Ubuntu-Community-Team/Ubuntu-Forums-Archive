### Post by Neobuntu on 2006-10-09
Yeah, the thing is, one has to use a fair amount of restraint with their freedom in open software and stay in the mostly stable (but still new stuff in wonderful x/k/ubuntu) and not get too jiggy with the beta stuff.

With that said. If you can make it with your Edgy for month or so, it will then be the stable release and you can happily hit the upgrade button to keep all of it current. Only when the release after yours goes stable, then you could switch sources over to it and upgrade release levels. There will no doubt then be even easier methods to do this and it will be well tested.
