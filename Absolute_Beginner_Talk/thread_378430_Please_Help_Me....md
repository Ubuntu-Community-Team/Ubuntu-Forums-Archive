### Post by buuntuu! on 2007-03-07
welcome!
well, if you installed your driver you should be only a few clicks away from your first print :)
click: "System" > "Administration" > "Printer"
the "printer" window should pop up.
click "printer" >"add printer"
this opens a three step setup
step 1. select local printer (should be selected by default) and click "forward"
step 2. select your printer model and the driver you installed, and click "install driver". click "forward"
step 3. click "proceed" (i think you can leave "description" and "place" blank)
this should be it, i think.
please don't panic if the buttons do not show the exact words i used, my system language isn't english, so i had to translate...
