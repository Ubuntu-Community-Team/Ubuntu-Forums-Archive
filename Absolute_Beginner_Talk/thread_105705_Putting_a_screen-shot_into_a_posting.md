### Post by Sutekh on 2005-12-19
When you post, scroll down and click on the Manage Attachments button, then you can upload your screenshot.  You will get a nice Thumbnail when you submit the post.

Alternatively you can upload it to the Forum Gallery or any other site, and then include the link in your post.
