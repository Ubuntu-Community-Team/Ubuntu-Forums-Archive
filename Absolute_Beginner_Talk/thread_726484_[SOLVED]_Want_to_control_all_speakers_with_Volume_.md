### Post by AegisTalons on 2008-03-16
So I have 2 Front speakers and 2 Surround Speakers. On the volume control they are controlled by Front and Surround respectively. I have a multimedia keyboard with volume keys and mute keys. I want to hit the volume up key and have it affect all the speakers not just the Front Speakers.

I think I need to control PCM with the volume keys but I have no clue how to do it. Please help.

---

### Post by kostkon on 2008-03-16
It's easier than you think. Just go to *System -> Preferences -> Sound* and at the *Default Mixer Tracks*, select (or press the Control key to multi-select) all the devices you want your multimedia keys to control the volume levels of.
