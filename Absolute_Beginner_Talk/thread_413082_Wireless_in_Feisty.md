### Post by Kobalt on 2007-04-19
You need to compile ndiswrapper and use windows drivers in order to get that card working under Ubuntu. 
Here is how to compile ndiswrapper : [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper?highlight=%28ndiswrapper%29#head-c1ebf95637d110c5f01b9a1383d137f79d8cbddb](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper?highlight=%28ndiswrapper%29#head-c1ebf95637d110c5f01b9a1383d137f79d8cbddb)
And here is where you can find drivers for your card (if you don't have them) : [http://ndiswrapper.sourceforge.net/mediawiki/index.php/List](http://ndiswrapper.sourceforge.net/mediawiki/index.php/List)
