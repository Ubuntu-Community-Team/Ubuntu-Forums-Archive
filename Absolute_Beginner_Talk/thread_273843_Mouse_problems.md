### Post by Madpilot on 2006-10-08
Linux uses the middle mouse button (wheel) as a text paste tool - highlight while holding down the left button, then click anywhere with the middle button/wheel and it'll be pasted.

That sounds like at least some of the problem you're having - as for the Firefox double tab thing, that's a bit of an oddity. Middle-clicking on a link in FF (or Opera) will open it in a new tab in the background, but it shouldn't be opening two tabs with one click...
