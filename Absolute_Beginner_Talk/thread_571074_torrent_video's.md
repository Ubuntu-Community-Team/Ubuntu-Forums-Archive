A torrent program does not split a movie (or any file) apart. If the file you downloaded is in multiple pieces this usually means its been compressed, just right click one of the files and choose extract here. 

As far as the speeds, that means you are downloading at 20(proabably kbs), and uploading at 2-3 kbs. There are alot of reasons for this. First of all your internet connection will have far less upload capacity then download capacity. Torrents work by having everyone upload/download from eachother, so it could just mean lots of people are uploading, and so your uploading isnt really important. Really dont worry about it.

And oe last thing, always try and share 150% of what you download (IE a ratio of 1.5), give back to others what you have received. :)
