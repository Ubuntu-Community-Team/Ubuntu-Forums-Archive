### Post by cam_tram on 2007-06-25
Try this site for the Brother drivers: [http://solutions.brother.com/linux/sol/printer/linux/lpr_drivers.html]("http://solutions.brother.com/linux/sol/printer/linux/lpr_drivers.html")

As long as your printers are shared on windows and your firewall isn't being too restrictive, they should show up in your Ubuntu printer dialog as Windows shared printers.
