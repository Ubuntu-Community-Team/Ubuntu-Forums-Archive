### Post by Hallvor on 2007-08-29
They should be in a folder called Incoming in your .aMule folder. The aMule folder is hidden in your home directory. From the desktop, press Alt+Home to access the home directory, and then Ctrl+h to view hidden files. Click the .aMule folder and then the folder called Incoming. Your files should be there.

Or.. from aMule click Preferences and then directories to see where they are saved.
