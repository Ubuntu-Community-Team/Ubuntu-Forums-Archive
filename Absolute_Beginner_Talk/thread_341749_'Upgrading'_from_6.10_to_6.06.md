Everything is possible, but NO don't even try. Much better to make a backup of your home folder, and then do a clean install.

If you like to downgrade then do like this. Boot from the installer alternate cd (not the desktop cd). Then do a install of the os *don't touch the partitions* only mount them to it's locations like '/' "for root/boot"  '/home' for your home folder, if it was on a separate partition.
