### Post by clayt0njknight on 2007-12-29
iPod yes.  iTunes, no...  at least, not natively.


there are other ways to run iTunes in linux using something like WinE, Codeweaver's Crossover, or even setting up a vmware session.  HOWEVER...

I recommend against it and just download Exaile and use the iPod plugin.  In fact, most media players, at least the bigger ones (Banshee, Amarok, Exaile) support iPods.
