### Post by yuku-aki on 2007-09-23
First off, I'd try another live CD, just to make sure it's not the CD rather than your machines that has the problem.  Could be an easy fix.

Did you have any version of Ubuntu on these machines before?  Which one?  Which machine?  Etc?

If it's a hardware problem, you should have at least seen some error messages.  There are Nvidia drivers available now, but they're not on the disc, I don't think.  

Did you accept the use of restricted software upon installation?  Many times hardware drivers are proprietary or restricted, and you don't get those if you select only community maintained repositories and initial installations.
