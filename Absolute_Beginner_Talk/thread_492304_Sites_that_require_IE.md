If the website uses ActiveX there isn't much you can do as spoofing the user agent won't accomplish anything. You can try to install IE on Linux, but if it's just one website it's not worth the effort. 

What kind of website is it? If it's a government or important commerce website like a bank, telephone, power, etc... I would complain to someone important in the agency or business that maybe can bring the issue to light. If it's a personal or some non-essential commerce, you could raise the issue with someone there, but you can always take your business somewhere else since they obviously don't care about your business.
