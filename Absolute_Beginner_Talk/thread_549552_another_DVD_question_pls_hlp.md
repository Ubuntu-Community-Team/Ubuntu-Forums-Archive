### Post by Hospadar on 2007-09-12
If it's a copy protected dvd, you'll need libcssdvd or something like that.  You'll probably need to get the medibuntu repositories and get it from there.  
Have a look here:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs?highlight=%28dvd%29](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs?highlight=%28dvd%29)

also here:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu?highlight=%28medibuntu%29](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu?highlight=%28medibuntu%29)

these are some good guides to getting encrypted dvd's to work.  I've gone through some similar steps and have encrypted dvd's running fine.
