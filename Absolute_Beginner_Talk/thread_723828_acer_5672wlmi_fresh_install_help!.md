### Post by drascus on 2008-03-13
yes the best way to learn Ubuntu is to live with it that's why I have learned so much in such a short period. you might want to plug in your computer to your router for the ttenime being just so you can get the files necessary to get your computer running right. When you plug in go to System --> Administration --> Restricted Drivers manager. see if there is any Drivers that need to be installed in order to get your Graphics Card or Wireless Card running. If there is something there you can just tick those boxes. It will ask for your password and then it will download and install those drivers for you. if your graphics card was in there you might now be able to change you Screen resolution by going to System --> Preferences -->Screen Resolution. A good place to start to learn the basics of Using your Ubuntu System is through the Help menu on the panel that little blue question mark has more information then you might think. Other than that keep searching the Forums and Asking questions. Google is also a Valuable Resource. Search for your computer and model and Ubuntu see what pops up people might have already solved your issues. Half the fun is trying to find the answer and fix problems. You will be happy when you realized how much you learned and that you did it yourself!!
