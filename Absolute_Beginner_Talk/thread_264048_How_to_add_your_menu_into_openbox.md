### Post by siacs on 2006-09-24
There are a couple of ways, you can use [Menumaker]("http://menumaker.sourceforge.net/") which will import KDE menu entries automatically or you can use [Obmenu]("http://obmenu.sourceforge.net/") to manually edit. You'll probably want both, menumaker for the intital menu creation and obmenu to touch it up.

### Post by fuscia on 2006-09-24
the debian menu appears in the openbox menu by default. with obmenu, it's wicked simple to edit.

edit: you can also use kicker in openbox. just open up a terminal and enter kicker and it will appear in the location you have it in kde. if you want to change it, you can also open up kcontrol from the terminal.
