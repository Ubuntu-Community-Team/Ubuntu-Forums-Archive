### Post by Cypher on 2007-04-24
When you re-installed XP, it overwrote the GRUB installation which was managing the dual-boot for you. You can use the LiveCD for Ubuntu and re-install GRUB to get things back.

Please search for "GRUB re-installation" either on this forum or on Google, I'm sure it's documented well enough..
