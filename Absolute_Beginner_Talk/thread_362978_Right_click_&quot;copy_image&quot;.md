This depends on where you are copying from. It could be locally or on the net. Either way the answer is Yes and Yes.

For Firefox or any other browser, right click and select "save image" to where ever....

In Nautilus, or Konqueror, once again Righ click and just selct copy. The same procedure as winblows.

Or you could just select the image and press "control C" to copy and "control V" to paste.

It's that simple.
