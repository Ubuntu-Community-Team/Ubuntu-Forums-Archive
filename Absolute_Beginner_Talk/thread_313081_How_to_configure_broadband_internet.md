### Post by IMELucky on 2006-12-05
Have you tried using System->Administration->Networking?

It will ask you for your password, but once your in you can select your ethernet card (probably something like eth0, or eht1) and click on properties.

You will get a dialog box that will ask you for your internet settings.
Change your configuration from DHCP to static and enter your ip settings.

Good Luck!
