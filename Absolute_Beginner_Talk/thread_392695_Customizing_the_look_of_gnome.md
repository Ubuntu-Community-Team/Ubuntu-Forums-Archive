### Post by freebird54 on 2007-03-24
Well  -it is hard to know where to begin.  For starters, enter gconf-editor in your terminal - lots to play with there.  Right click on a panel to change things there.  System->Preferences->DEsktop Background /  Theme / Windows / Sound all have lots of options.  And of course there are replacements like Beryl available with their own theme managers (like Emerald) if you want eye candy as well..

Hope this helps..
