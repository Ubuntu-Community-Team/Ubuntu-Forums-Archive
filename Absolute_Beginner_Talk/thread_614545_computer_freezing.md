### Post by oscarthefish on 2007-11-16
I have since been told to press: Ctrl + Alt + Backspace. I found that by pressing Ctrl+ Alt + Backspace it throws you back to the Ubuntu Login Window.

Another method you can try is to right click on either the top or bottom taskbar and then select "Add to panel". When the new window appears look in the "Desktop & Windows" section and highlight "Force Quit" now click on "Add".

Once installed you just click on the icon and then click on the frozen window to clear.

Hope this helps
