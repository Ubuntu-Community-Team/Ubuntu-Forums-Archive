### Post by kyphi on 2007-05-26
On the top panel you will find a loudspeaker symbol.

If you left click on that you can adjust a slider to increase the volume.

If you right click on it and choose 'open volume control' you can play around with the sliders until you get your sound just right.  Start with the Master and PCM adjusters.
