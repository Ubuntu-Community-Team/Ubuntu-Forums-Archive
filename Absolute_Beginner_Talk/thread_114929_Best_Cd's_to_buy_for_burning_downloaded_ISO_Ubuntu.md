### Post by etc on 2006-01-09
1) Don't buy cheap media, sorry but I don't know what company to suggest.

2) Download from any US mirror, or better yet use Bittorrent and it will make sure your iso isn't corrupt.

3) A mirror is just a (synced)copy of whats on the other main server, hence mirror

4) IE and Firefox render things differently, but you have Opera and Firefox for both Ubuntu and Windows though.
It's just browsers.
