### Post by SamH on 2005-11-29
I don't have an iPod so I can't help there.

As far as ripping goes, yes, you can rip to Rhythmbox.  Use grip or Soundjuicer.    You can rip from CDs to a directory of your choice.  There is no reason you can't designate a directory you have Rhythmbox "watching".  After ripping, I think you will need to tell Rhythmbox to reload that directory for the newly ripped songs to appear in your playlist.

Hope that helps.
