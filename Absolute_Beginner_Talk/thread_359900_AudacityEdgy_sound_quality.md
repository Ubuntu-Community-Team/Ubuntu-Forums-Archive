### Post by go2dell on 2007-02-12
Most of your problem is likely due to using the sound hardware on your motherboard.  It's a common problem, since there's lots of electrical interference flinging around in there for the sound hardware to pick up and pass on.

I notice a huge difference when I go into the volume control (not in Audacity) and mute the CD input, since a whole lot of electrical interference is picked up in my system by that little cable running from the CD drive to the mobo.

While you're actually recording, try muting everything else (even the outputs) and see if that helps.  Just don't forget to turn the speakers back on when you check the results. :-)
