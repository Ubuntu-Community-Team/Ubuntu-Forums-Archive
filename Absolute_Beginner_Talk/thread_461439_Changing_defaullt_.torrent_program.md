### Post by CaptainInsaneO on 2007-06-06
Do this:

Right click your torrent file.

Click "Properties" in the menu that appears.

Click the "Open With" tab in the Properties window.

Click the radio button next to "Azureus".



Let me commend you on a fine choice of a bittorrent program. Azureus is my favorite!
