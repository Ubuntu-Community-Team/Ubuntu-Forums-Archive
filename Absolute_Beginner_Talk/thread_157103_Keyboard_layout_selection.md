### Post by ncappel1 on 2006-04-08
I switch back and forth between keyboard layouts too. 
right click on a panel (I chose the top, but it doesn't really matter) and click "add to panel." Select the "Keyboard indicator" icon under the Utilities section.

Now you should be able to quickly switch between keyboard layouts by just left-clicking the new button on the panel!

### Post by gdgardnerw on 2006-05-07
From SYSTEMS>PREFERENCES>KEYBOARD choose the LAYOUT OPTIONS tab. Click on the GROUP SHIFT/LOCK BEHAVIOR option. This lets you choose the keyboard option you would like to switch layouts right from the keyboard.
