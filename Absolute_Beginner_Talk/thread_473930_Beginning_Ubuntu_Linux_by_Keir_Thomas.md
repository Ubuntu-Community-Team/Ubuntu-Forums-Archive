### Post by Papi-KB7VGW on 2007-06-14
Hi all
     I just checked this book out of my local library and while there seems to be tons of online  videos, How To articles, good general documentation and this fantastic community, I would like to recommend this book to all noob users.  It was written for Edgy 6.10 but easily applies to Feisty.  It includes step by step instruction with pictures.  Tips are highlighted and helpful.  In the 4 months I have been using Ubuntu I mostly searched the web and forums for info.  While I didn't have the problems some folks do, this book would have answered most everything I have seen asked.  I don't remember seeing it listed as a reference but maybe it should..:D
