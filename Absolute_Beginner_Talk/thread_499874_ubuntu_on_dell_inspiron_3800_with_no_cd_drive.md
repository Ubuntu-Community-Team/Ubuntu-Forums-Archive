hi, just a suggestion, but have you had a look at puppy linux? it is a small distro (80MB approx), but a full desktop, you can install it on a usb drive and use a 'find pup' floppy' which will direct your bios to load it from usb (specially for pc's with no usb boot option), you don't even have to install it to your harddrive if you don't want to, it loads completely into ram and is really fast because of this. it may be worth a look:
