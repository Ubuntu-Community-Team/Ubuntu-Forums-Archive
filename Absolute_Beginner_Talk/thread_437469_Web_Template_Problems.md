### Post by lyceum on 2007-05-15
When you get a template play with it to make sure it works the way you want it to. Also, they can come with several files. You have to put any pictures on your server and re-write the links, like so:

   > <img src=[COLOR="Red"]"http://whitetreeevolution.com/pictures/WTLogoFlat.jpg" [/COLOR]
    width="60%" 
    height="60%" 
    border="0" 
    alt="Welcome to the Evolution" 
    align="middle">"

This way the browser knows where to find the pictures or what ever is there. Most of the time templates seem to have things like this to make them look good. (At least the ones I have seen, I normally wrote my own.) I made the part you would change red so you can see it easier. Use Bluefish to do your edits. 

Hope this helps.
