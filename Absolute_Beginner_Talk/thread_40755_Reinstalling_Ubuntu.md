### Post by aysiu on 2005-06-10
[QUOTE=zaxer]Hi guys.

After having some problems with apt-get (it stopped working dont know why) Ive decided to reinstall my ubuntu partition.

Whats the best way to do that?

Formatting the whole partition and doing a new install or is there any other way?

Thanks guys.[/QUOTE]

The best way to do it is to copy everything out of your home folder onto some kind of backup (a CD, a USB key, an MP3 player, an external hard drive, even floppy disks). Make sure you copy all your hidden folders as well--these are where all your settings reside!

Then, just reinstall Ubuntu from scratch. Once it's installed, download all the applications you had before. Then, copy your home folder back-up to the new home folder.
