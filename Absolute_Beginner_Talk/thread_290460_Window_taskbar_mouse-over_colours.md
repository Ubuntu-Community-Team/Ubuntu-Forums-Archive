### Post by d3v1ant_0n3 on 2006-11-01
Right click on the panel. 

Select 'Configure panel'.

Select the panel you want to change the look of from the drop-down.
 
Click the 'Appearance' button

Options are in there. I have 'enable transparency' checked.

if you look at the Advanced Options there are- unsuprisingly- more advanced options for changing the look of the panel.

Hope that helps.
