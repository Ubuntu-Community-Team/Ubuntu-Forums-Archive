### Post by jfinkels on 2007-08-10
If you don't need a fancy schmancy GUI with your media player (which in most cases, you don't), then use mpd (Music Player Daemon) with mpc (Music Player Daemon Client) or ncmpc (Ncurses frontend for mpc).

Try it out:```
sudo aptitude install mpd mpc
```

Just direct mpd to your music folder, build the database, then do:```
mpc ls | mpc add && mpc play
``` and your music will play. Read more about it after installing it here:```
man mpc
```and ```
man ncmpc
```

As for PC to phone calling, again, I direct you to my previous posts about Ekiga.
