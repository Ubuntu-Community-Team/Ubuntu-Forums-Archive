### Post by emarkd on 2008-02-04
This is usually a graphics card issue.  Try [downloading the alternate CD]("http://releases.ubuntu.com/7.10/") and installing from there.  It's text-based so the graphics problem goes away.  After the installation, you'll have to enable the restricted drivers for your video card.
