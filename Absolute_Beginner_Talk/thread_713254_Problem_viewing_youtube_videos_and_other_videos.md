### Post by TheThingfish42 on 2008-03-02
I actually was just toying with this myself, My youtube videos and flash based websites always freaked out, a little googlin and I found a simple solution, go into Synaptic, and completely remove Flash Player and Gnash. Go to youtube and it will direct you to to download Flash, and send you to the .tar.gz version of flash, scroll down on the page and you will get friendly instructions for installing it over again, but it managed to fix my problems, I believe (and someone correct me if Im wrong) that Flash and Gnash were conflicting. I promise nothing, but I dont think you can do any damage with this solution. At worst, you have the newest version of Flash installed :)
