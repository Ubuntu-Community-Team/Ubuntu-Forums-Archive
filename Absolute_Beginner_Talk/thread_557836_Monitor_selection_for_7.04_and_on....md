### Post by mcduck on 2007-09-23
Operating systems couldn't careless what the monitor is. If the display adapter is supported everything works, if not, you get no picture. The OS only communicates with the display adapter. From the computer's point of view it makes no difference what the monitor is or if there is no monitor at all.
