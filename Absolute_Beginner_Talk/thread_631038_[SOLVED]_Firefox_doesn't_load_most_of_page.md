### Post by cinematography on 2007-12-04
Try clearing the cache under Firefox > Tools > Clear Private Data. If that doesn't work, you could delete the settings folder so a fresh, default, one could be created in its place. But..  BE WARNED: Doing this will also delete all of your bookmarks and cookies. If you want to try this option, close Firefox, press ctrl-h in your home folder to show the hidden settings folders, and delete the folder named .mozilla. Or in your case it may be called .swiftfox. Then reopen Firefox.
