Easy, you don't have to install anything.
Go to System->Preferences->Keyboard

Click the LAYOUT tab.

Click "Add..."

In Layouts -> select Russian
In Variants -> select your variant

i have Default, Ossetian, Phonetic, Tatar, Typewriter, Winkeys

Ukrainian, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Kyrgisistan, Kazachstan, etc. all have a seperate Layout, with other variants...

If you right click on the gnome-panel and click add panel, and select "Keyboard Indicator", you can switch between the layouts you have installed, convieniently, just as in windows.
