### Post by ron999 on 2006-10-01
Hi Bush - PPAAUULL beat me to it, he must be a faster typist than me!:) 
This is my suggestion:-
If you just want to import your bookmarks from the Firefox that you use with Windows into the Firefox that you use with Ubuntu then you can do this...

With your Windows Firefox click on Bookmarks>Manage Bookmarks...>File>Export...

Then export them onto a floppy disk or a USB stick. They are exported as an html file. You could even email it to yourself as an attachment.

Then start up Ubuntu and with Firefox click on Bookmarks>Manage Bookmarks...>File>Import...
and import the file.:cool:

### Post by mjpatey on 2006-10-01
If you need to get your bookmarks from Windows IE to Ubuntu Firefox (or Swiftfox)...

In Windows, download and install a copy of Firefox.  Tell it you'd like to import your IE bookmarks.  After the install, export all your bookmarks to something portable (even thousands of them would probably fit on a floppy, I'd think).

In Ubuntu, open Firefox and import the bookmarls from your portable media.

Hope this helps!
