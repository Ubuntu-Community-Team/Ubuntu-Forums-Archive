### Post by w4ett on 2007-07-14
from the terminal type:  

sudo dpkg-reconfigure xorg.conf


This will open a semi graphical application to reconfigure your xserver

what we're going to do is select additional resolutions from the options provided.

The up and down keys meove the cursor, <spacebar> selects options and <enter> confirms selections.

you want to select  1600x1200 from the available options

complete the selections and save.  

Restart X by <Ctrl> <alt> <backspace>

the new resolution should be available .

Try it and check back with results
