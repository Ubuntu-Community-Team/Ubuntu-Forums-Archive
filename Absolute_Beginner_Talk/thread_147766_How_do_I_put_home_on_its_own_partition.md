### Post by cilynx on 2006-03-20
You can always reinstall if you want to go that route, but more fun in my opinion is using gParted.  It lets you resize / move around / splice and dice partitions.  Be careful as it can nuke your setup if you're not careful.  But if a reinstall is a reasonable option, you're probably not worried about that.  You may as well get the experience with a neat product that can save you later.

If you need more of walkthrough, let us know where you're at and exactly what you're looking to do and why.
