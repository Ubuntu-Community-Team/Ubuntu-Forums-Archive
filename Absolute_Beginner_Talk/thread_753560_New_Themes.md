### Post by fredbird67 on 2008-04-12
There's also [http://art.gnome.org](http://art.gnome.org) and [http://www.ubuntu-art.org](http://www.ubuntu-art.org).  Ubuntu-Art is run by the same group that also runs Gnome-Look (and KDE-Look and Xfce-Look, for that matter).  Of the three, I personally prefer art.gnome.org because it's moderated and they don't allow anything offensive on there.  The other two seem to be unmoderated, and therefore, while most of what you'll find on the other two is OK, there can be something offensive on there every once in a while.  Unfortunately, art.gnome.org don't allow anything distro-specific on there, either, as the only branding allowed, if present, is of GNOME itself, since they want to keep things on there distro-neutral.
