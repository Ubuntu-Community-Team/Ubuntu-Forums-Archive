### Post by taurus on 2007-02-24
You first need to convert .avi to iso before you can use k3b to burn it.  You can use either devede or tovid (and others) to convert your .avi to .iso first.  Then, use k3b to burn the .iso as an DVD image file.

Devede should be in the repos.
