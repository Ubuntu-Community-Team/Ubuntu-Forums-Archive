### Post by hartleyshc on 2007-08-25
> **bloor75 said:**
> I have a firewall in my router how do I open ports in it

look over at dslreports.com for your specific router, and how to do what is called "port forwarding"
youll want to take whatever port 16234 for example, and forward the port to the ip of your local computer *often 192.168.x.x* that means that when data comes in on port 16342, the router knows what computer to establish the connection with.
or simply visit the website of your router manufacturer, all the info should be there.
