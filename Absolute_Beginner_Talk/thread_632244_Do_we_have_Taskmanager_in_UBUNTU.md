### Post by ryth on 2007-12-05
For easy reference in Gnome you can also right click on your top or bottom bar, select "add to panel" and then choose "System Monitor".

This adds a small real-time "CPU Monitor" icon onto the bar, and if you click on it brings up the "System Monitor"/task manager.
