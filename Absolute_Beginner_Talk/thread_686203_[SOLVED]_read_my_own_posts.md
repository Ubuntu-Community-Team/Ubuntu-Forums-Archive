### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-02-03
For future reference, you can enable auto-subscribing to threads you post in (or create).
At the top of the screen there is a gray area with menus
User CP->Edit Options->
Then scroll to Messaging & Notification
and for Default Thread Subscription Mode, choose an option like No Email Notification.
Then you can check your subscriptions by going to User CP->Subscribed Threads
