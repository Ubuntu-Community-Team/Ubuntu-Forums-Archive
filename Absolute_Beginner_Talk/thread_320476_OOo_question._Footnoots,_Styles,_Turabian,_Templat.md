### Post by turbotuba on 2006-12-17
I figured it out.\\:D/  Yeah, it was real easy...
I read the instructions in the OOo help.:oops: 

For a Kate Turabian style format:
1. Download this [.stw file]("http://documentation.openoffice.org/Samples_Templates/User/template/turabian.stw")
2. In OOo click file--->Templates--->Organize...
3. Right click on "My Templates" (or any template category)
4. Click "Import Template..."
5. Format--->Styles and Formating
6. In Styles window click the "New Style from Selection" button in the top right corner
7. click "Load Styles"
8. Select "My Templates" (Template Category) then "Turabian" the Document 
9. Then click OK, and you're done.
