### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-07-08
I hope by albums your meaning albums you already own or which are free. Most of the time a torrent is slow because there aren't that many people seeding it. Torrenting isn't like an http download, thats guaranteed and eats bandwidth like crazy. I'd say go to where you downloaded the torrent (site you found it) and check how many people are seeding, if its less than 500 (ideally 1 to 2 thousand or more) then there probably just aren't that many people seeding.

If its high, I dunno. Try ktorrent, I've always found it to give me great speeds even on less tracked torrents. You might also want to consider port forwarding, for that please do a search for IPtables and your router separately, you'd have to do it for both.
