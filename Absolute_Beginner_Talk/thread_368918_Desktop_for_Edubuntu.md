### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-02-23
I like these sites to get wallpaper:
[http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org)      (click on wallpapers on the left sidebar)
[http://www.interfacelift.com](http://www.interfacelift.com)

Just save them wherever you like and then follow the instructions Kateikyoushi gave you.
In the wallpaper chooser, click on 'Add Wallpaper' and then navigate to where you saved your new wallpapers and double click on it.  Poof!  It's installed.
Or, you can just drag and drop the picture onto the wallpaper chooser and it will be installed for you as well.
