### Post by JesterDev on 2006-12-10
The CD is bootable yes. Unless you downloaded another which I am unaware of (I'm new too Ubunto, but not so much to Linux). I'm not sure what program you used to burn the CD, but personally I use Nero under windows and it hardly ever fails me. 

Just because the CD burned OK does not mean that it's still good. You could try burning it again. I suggest getting Nero ([www.nero.com](www.nero.com) - for which there is a free trial version) and ticking off the option to verify written data. It will let you know if the CD is good or not.

Also you might want to try updating the firmware for your CD Burner. Just a suggestion, but may not have any effect at all.
