### Post by Bartender on 2006-03-13
like2...
You didn't mention any specifics about your external modem.  Many externals aren't full hardware modems.
Did you go into Administration>Networking and enable the modem?
What did you mean when you said the "actual program put on my computer screen"?
One of the modem programs, called Modem Monitor, is available by clicking on the top or bottom panel, clicking Add, then dragging the red phone icon to your panel.  There are a few different ways of getting modems to work...if you do some Search'es on it you'll find lots of discussion on the subject.
Dial-up is not one of Ubuntu's strong points.  Good luck with it!
