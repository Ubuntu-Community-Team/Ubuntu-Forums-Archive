### Post by ajgreeny on 2005-11-09
At the botom of your Monitor section in xorg.conf put two extra lines with the resolutin info according to your monitor specs.

it should look something like:-

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"Generic Monitor"
	Option		"DPMS"
	HorizSync	30-70
	VertRefresh	50-160

You should now be able to set your resolution and refresh rate as you want from a selection shown in the desktop configuration screen.

Good luck and enjoy Ubuntu
