### Post by MasterJS on 2008-03-19
Ubuntu has already provided a general driver for your video card, hence why you can log in and still use it. Now, if there is a proprietary driver for your card, you can check and enable it with the restricted drivers manager (System > Administration > Restricted Drivers Manager). If not, then i'm not sure what you can do. Some cards are just not able to use the desktop effects.
