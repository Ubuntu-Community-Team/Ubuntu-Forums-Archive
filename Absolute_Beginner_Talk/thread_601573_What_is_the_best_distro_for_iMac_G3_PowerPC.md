### Post by Hendrixski on 2007-11-03
:-) you'll find that asking "which distro is the best" will get you a lot of meaningless answers.  It's like asking a group of Ferrari owner which model is best suited for a certain stretch of road.

My suggestion.  Try a few.  It's called "distro hopping" and everybody does it, it's a LOT of fun.  You get to see a lot of interesting things that the world of Linux has to offer.  You'll also so see that they all pretty much work on the same hardware because the kernel and the drivers are the same across all distro's :-)

If you're coming from the world of station wagons (I.E. Windows) then you're probably thinking "oh no, I have to try all these different operating systems"... but no, it really is ike test driving different models of Ferari's... and that's why we're so passionate about our operating system, and the different distributions whereas station-wagon owners just kinda settle for less.

Enjoy the journey.
