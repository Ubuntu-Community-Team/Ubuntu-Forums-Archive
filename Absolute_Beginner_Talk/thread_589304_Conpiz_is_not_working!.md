### Post by sujoy on 2007-10-24
search in synaptic for compiz and install it along with compizconfig-settings-manager

you will have a new menu at System->Preferences->Advanced desktop effects settings

from there configure your compiz settings

and for your NVIDIA card try the restricted drivers manager for any proprietory driver that you may require
