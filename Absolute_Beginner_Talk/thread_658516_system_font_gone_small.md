### Post by kavoura on 2008-01-04
A short while ago my system fonts in some programs have gone very small. In File Browser, Firefox and Thunderbird they have stayed the same. But in OpenOffice and Konsole the menu fonts are very small, as is the text in Konsole. The text on the icons is normal, as is the font on the page, in OpenOffice.
What happened to cause this, how can I fix it?

---

### Post by cypherzero on 2008-01-04
(I assume you are using KDE)
Start control centre (Start : System Settings), under the Appearance tab is a section called Fonts, you can control the master font size here.

---

### Post by kavoura on 2008-01-04
I am using Gnome, but I found the Appearance settings under System/Preferences.
The font size listed there for everything was 10. I changed one of them to 12, and fonts changed and were too big (in Firefox), and then back to 10. Now all programs seem to have the correct font sizes for their menus again.
