### Post by harlan on 2007-11-29
System takes that icon from /usr/share/pixmaps/xmms.xpm , so if you put the icon you want
in /usr/share/pixmaps/ and name it xmms.xpm the icon will be changed.

---

### Post by master_kernel on 2007-11-29
You could also right click on it, go to Properties, click on the icon and change it to whatever you desire.
