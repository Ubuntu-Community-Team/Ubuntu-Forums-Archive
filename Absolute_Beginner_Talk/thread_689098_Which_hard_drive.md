### Post by techno-mole on 2008-02-06
ive used a few brands of hard drive for years now.
seagate, western digital, samsung and maxtor.
from my experience you cant go wrong using samsung or western digital (very quiet and reliable) i have gutsy on a western digital sata drive and it works like a charm.
i have windows xp on a samsung drive and that too works like a charm.
i have had many problems with seagate drives, and as a result stopped using them a few years ago, and the same goes with maxtor.
just for the record if you have a motherboard with an nforce chipset you may well have problem using maxtor hard drives (sata or ide) there are compatibility issues.

I have a main board with an nforce 3 chipset, and it will not accept any maxtor hard drive, i also know that the later nforce chipsets also have the same trouble (nforce 4 and nforce 5) although i think this issue may have been improved upon in later versions.
just my 2 pence worth, but if given the choice id go western digital of samsung.
cheers
