### Post by nanotube on 2008-04-17
sure, you could just connect to your xp machine and use it over the net.
but linux also supports multi-disk drives, in the form of lvm (logical volume management). 
for more info:
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lvm](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lvm)

so... yes, you can! :)
