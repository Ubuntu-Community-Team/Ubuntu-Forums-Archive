### Post by phr0ze on 2007-06-20
> **crazydiver said:**
> build a computer... way cheaper and you have the freedom to customize it!

Its not really cheaper to build your own if you are buying a budget system.

I found Dell's cheapest system (non-ubuntu) for $499 without using coupons.

The same system off new egg will cost at least $457. And the dell system includes vista, not that anyone cares but it does have a market value to consider when comparing prices. Dell also has 1 year in home service. Its also hassle free. Lets assume it takes you 1 hour to build the system, how much is that worth to you. Finally there is better quality in the dell system than the bargain system I put together on newegg just to compare prices.

Now, if you are adding upgrades to dell's system, then dell charges about 2x more than what the upgrades are worth. You will quickly enter an area where newegg will be cheaper unless you find a dell coupon.
