### Post by Sef on 2006-05-18
You would want to do a manual install.  Here is an excellent tutorial on how to dual boot. [http://users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/]("http://users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/")

If you do the default, it will erase all your data.  By doing a manual partition, you can save your data.  One Caveat:  **back up**your data first.  At times, the install does fail and you can lose all your data.
