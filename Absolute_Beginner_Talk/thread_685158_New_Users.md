### Post by bluewraith on 2008-02-01
System > Administration > Users and Groups
You'll be asked for your password
Click "Add User"
Next window, type his user name in the appropriate box.
Decide what his password will be at the bottom.
Click on "OK" and hes all set.

As for your files vs. his files, you will each have a separate Home directory. Now, whether or not he can view your files I'm not sure of. I only have one user on my PC.

### Post by wesleybailey on 2008-02-01
1. Navigate to your home folder in the file browser.
2. Right-click folder.
3. Click Permissions tab on the top
4. Change the Group/Other Folder access permissions in the drop down box to "None".
5. Click close
