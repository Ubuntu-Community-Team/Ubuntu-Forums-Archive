### Post by Saint Angeles on 2008-04-22
when you see the white screen, type alt+f2... then ```
metacity --replace
```
don't worry if you can't see it. hit enter after it and you will see your desktop. basically the white screen tells you that you are trying to run compiz but you don't have direct rendering enabled.

can you give us some info about your video card and driver?
