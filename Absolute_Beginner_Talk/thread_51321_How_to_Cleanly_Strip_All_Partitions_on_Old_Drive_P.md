### Post by az on 2005-07-23
Here is how you do it.

1- Enter your Bios.
2-  Set it to boot from CD.
3-  Insert Ubuntu Installer CD.
4-  Reboot
5- Keep hitting enter at all the yes/no questions.
6-  Enter name and password when asked.
7- When your computer shows you the Ubuntu graphical login screen, you are done.



By default, the installer blows away the first hard drive it sees.
