### Post by BoneKracker on 2007-04-21
If they are themes that came with the distribution, they should be in a folder called "themes" somwhere in /usr/share.

If they are themes that you installed, they should be in a folder called .themes in your home directory.

Just delete the ones you don't want to keep.
