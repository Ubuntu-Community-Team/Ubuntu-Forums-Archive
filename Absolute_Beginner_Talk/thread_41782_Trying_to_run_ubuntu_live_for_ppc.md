### Post by Steele on 2005-06-14
You should really look at the Wiki before posting this kind of thing.  It will save you time in the future.  There are two things that you need to know about Macs and booting from a CD.  The first is that there are two different macs.  They are split between the Beige G3 and the Blue and White G3.  Anything before and including the Beige G3 is considered "Old" and after and including the Blue and White G3 is considered "New".  If you have a "New" mac then you should be able to turn off your computer, but make sure you have the Live CD in first, turn on the computer, and hold the "C" button.  The C button will allow you to boot from the CD.  If you have an "Old" mac then you should google about how to boot from a CD.  It is quite a bit more work for "Old" macs.  Good luck, but remember about the Wiki.
