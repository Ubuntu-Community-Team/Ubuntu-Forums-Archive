Unfortunately, I can answer only one of your questions. [QUOTE=b-dizzle]
2. Some people on another forum have said that out of the box, Ubuntu is not very slick and eye-candylicious, but with some tweaking, it can be.  Are all the themes and such available for Ubuntu in the System>Preference>Themes menu, or are there other websites I can check out for these?[/QUOTE]  Yes. Go to [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org) and look at Metacity, GTK, and icon themes.
