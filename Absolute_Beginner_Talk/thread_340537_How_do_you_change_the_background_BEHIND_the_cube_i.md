### Post by igknighted on 2007-01-17
> **SMS said:**
> hey i was wondering if it was possible to change the colour behind the cube and if it is posible, the how can it be done
thanks alot

If you open the beryl-manager (ie, click the red gem in the system tray and select beryl settings manager) then scroll on the right until you see the desktop cube plugin.  Click that, and there should be a setting to enable the skydome (background image), and then on another tab there is the option to upload your own image to use as the skydome.
