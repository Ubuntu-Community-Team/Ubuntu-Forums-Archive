### Post by llamakc on 2007-11-17
It's a theme for GDM.

Download it, save it to your Desktop, and click

SYSTEM | ADMINISTRATION | LOGIN WINDOW

Enter your password.

When it opens click on the LOCAL tab. Choose ADD from the right navigate to your theme.

Then you have to choose it.

Logout and enjoy.
