### Post by Sprogg2001 on 2006-08-28
Phantom784  is correct, this is not something the operating system linux or windows can do as you need to have a network setup to share your internet connection the easyiest way to do this is with a router, check with your Internet Service provider (ISP) to find out which routers they support (they will usually have some to sell on their website) this will replace your usb adsl modem and also allow you to plug in multiple computers to your ADSL Line.
