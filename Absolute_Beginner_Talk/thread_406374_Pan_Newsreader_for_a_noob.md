### Post by john.nicholls on 2007-04-11
When you first run the program, it should come up with a wizard that will ask you for the details of your ISP including the name of your ISP's news server. Failing that, you can go to Tools | New Servers to enter the information.

Once you've done that and are online, go to Servers | Get list of all groups to download the list of all groups available from your ISP. You can then choose which ones you want to subscribe to.
