### Post by mofokuban on 2006-11-21
I opted out of using 64-bit at the moment because coding for 64-bit simply has yet to tap into the benefits.  It's a very minimal gain if you're going to use a 64-bit OS in every day computing.

If you're a power user on the other hand, then yes, you'll notice a difference.

But again, since programmers have yet to tap into the potential, there are less programs, because the 64-bit market hasn't hit the mainstream quite yet.
