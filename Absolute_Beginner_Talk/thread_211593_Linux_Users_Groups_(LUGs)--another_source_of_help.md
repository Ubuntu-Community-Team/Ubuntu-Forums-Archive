### Post by editrix on 2006-07-08
This forum is a wonderful site, but I suspect many newbies don't know about Linux Users Groups, which have the advantage of being in (or near) your community and can often help with info on local ISPs and where to get good hardware--and often have used hardware they're flogging cheap. As well, they hold meetings and tutorials. There are about 700 of them in 90 countries. You can find a list here:

[http://lugww.counter.li.org/](http://lugww.counter.li.org/)

Or google your nearest town/city and "Linux Users Group."
