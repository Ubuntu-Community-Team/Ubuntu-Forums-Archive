### Post by cfaulkner on 2007-10-22
Depends on what Flash drive you have.  If you have a cheap USB drive, the first 128mb (or less) is gonna be snappy, and the rest of it is gonna be slow access...  

Keep in mind that the bus speed of USB 2.0 is 480mBITs per second, as opposed to SATA's 3.0gBITs per second..
