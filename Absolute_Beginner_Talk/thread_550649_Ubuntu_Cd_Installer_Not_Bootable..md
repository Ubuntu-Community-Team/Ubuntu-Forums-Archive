### Post by indytim on 2007-09-14
2 basics (there may be more things to "discover"):

1.  Are you sure your changes "stuck" on changing the bios boot order?  If you have another known good boot disc from another app... try that just to verify that bios is in fact set to boot from the optical.

2.  Failing that, I'd recommend re-burning the cd from the iso and try again.
