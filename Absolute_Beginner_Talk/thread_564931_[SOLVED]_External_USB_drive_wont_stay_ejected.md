### Post by dimbulb1024 on 2007-10-01
I have a USB external drive that is powered by the USB cable. When I highlight the desktop icon and select eject, at first it ejects the drive and then remounts it right away. It won't stay unmounted/ejected.

I have two other external drives that mount through USB though they both have their own power supplies and do not use the USB port for power. They dismount just fine.

Also, my camera, phone and iPod all mount and unmount without a problem.

I'm not to worried about it I was just wondering if anyone has any ideas?

---

### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2007-10-01
Go to System > Preferences > Removable Drives and Media and uncheck the box(es) "Mount removable ..."
