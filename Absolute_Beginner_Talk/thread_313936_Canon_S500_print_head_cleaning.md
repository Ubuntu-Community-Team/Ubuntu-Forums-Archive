### Post by GuitarFingers on 2006-12-06
Canon are notoriously slow with drivers and options available for printers in Linux although you can generally get most of their printers to at least print, but anything of the cool softweare based stuff you can do in other operating systems can be problematic. I haven't checked for some time but last time I looked there was no software available from them to do this.

I do know that with all Canon printers theres a button combination that you can press on the printer itself to do both an align and a print head clean so until/if Canon improve their driver support for Linux thats really your main option. 

I have a HP Photosmart 2575 that works flawlessly. When your Canon gives up the ghost you may want to look at HP instead, their support is a bit better.
