Are you a developer?  Do you understand howto use configure->make?  if Yes then you will find the[ path fairly straight ahead]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/KernelCustomBuild") (you still have much to learn).  If No, you have a very,very long way to go.  The beauty is that the community is here to help and nothing is out of reach.
