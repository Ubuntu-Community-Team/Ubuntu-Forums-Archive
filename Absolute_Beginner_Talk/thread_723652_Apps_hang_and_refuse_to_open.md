you might try going to your home folder and typing ctrl+H go to the folder .mozilla and try deleting that folder then go and launch Firefox. Make sure you back up your bookmarks before you do this. What this does is delete Firefox's Configuration files and gives you the default settings back. It has helped me solve similar problems in the past.
