### Post by dwhitney67 on 2007-08-31
I do not believe the the 4.6 version is available yet in the repos.  Therefore, if you want to get the latest, try to obtain the source package from a mirror site.  But from what I can tell, it is not available at the present moment and will be available "shortly".

Btw, you probably wouldn't see a different regardless of what version you use.  For now I would remain with the version that ships with Ubuntu.  There are easier ways to protect your system than having to worry about which version of openSSH you are using.

Here's the official site for [openSSH]("http://openbsd.rt.fm/openssh/").
