### Post by Ziox on 2006-07-22
when you computer is boot, like right after you turned on the power, it should display the logo/name of your computer's manufacture.  At that point, there should display something about how to enter setup, such as pressing a key. if there isn't any message, then try pressing either ESC, F1, F2, or F12. If they don't work then, just try all the keys. :)

one of the keys will bring you to the setup screen, and look for option boot or boot order...there you should be able to change the boot order.

Now every company's computer has a different setup screen, so i can't tell you the exact method, but what i gave you should get the job done. If not, then post your computer model, and perhaps someone can help you out better than I.
