### Post by Optimus Prime on 2006-09-11
Banshee is an excellent iTunes alternative. Its built-in iPod libraries let it sync your computer's music database with your iPod, and you can use it to easily copy your music from your iPod onto your computer.

I've used gtkPod and Rhythmbox and I've found Banshee to be the best at syncing and using the iPod. (I have a 5G)

If you are using Kubuntu, then use Amarok.
