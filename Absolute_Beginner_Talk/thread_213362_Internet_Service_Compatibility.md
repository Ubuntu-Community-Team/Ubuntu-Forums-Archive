### Post by RRS on 2006-07-11
Don't worry about it.

I have ATT Yahoo (formerly SBC) and Ubuntu has no problems.

I did order their modem with my initial sign up because I didn't really know my options and it was basically free as part of a low cost promotional package.

Besides the usual ignorance from tech support the only Linux problem is the install cd they send with the modem for windows. 

Even in windows the connection and setup can be done without the cd and when/if you call tech support don't bother to tell them you have linux, just open Firefox when they tell you to "click on IE" etc.

It's also quite likely that whatever modem/router hardware you're using now will work just fine. All you really need is the physical activation of DSL service in your phone line and a loggin/password.
