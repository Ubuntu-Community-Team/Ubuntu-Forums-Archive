### Post by skip910 on 2006-10-28
I suggest just making CheckGmail start on startup.

System -> Preference -> Sessions
Click "Startup Programs" on the top right tab. Click "Add" and in the input box type in "checkgmail" (without quotes).

And logout and log back in!
