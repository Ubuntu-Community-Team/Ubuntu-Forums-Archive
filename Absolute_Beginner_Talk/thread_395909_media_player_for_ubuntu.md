### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-03-29
my favorite personally is xine, with libxine-extracodecs and w32codecs installed.  Works on just about anything.  I do encourage that you try a bunch, just so you can find your favorite, although you seem pretty proud of vlc already :-)

Also note that just about any player can handle the different codecs out there, its just a matter of knowing which libraries to install
