### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-05-13
Welcome Aboard!

OK, here we go!

Click on System>Administration>Networking

You will then be asked for your Administrator (login) password. Enter it.

The Network Settings window will now open.

Click on the button in front of the desired connection method.

Then work your way through the tabs Connections, Genderal, DNS, Hosts.
