### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-08-25
1.  Love the title of your thread
2.  try this:

press Alt + F2
type
```
gconf-editor /apps/nautilus/desktop
```
and hit enter

In the right pane there's a check box for **trash_icon visible**
Check it, and enjoy.
