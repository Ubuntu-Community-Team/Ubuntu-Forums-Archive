### Post by jlambeth1 on 2008-02-19
I have the latest Ultimate on my Dell Latitude D630 and it is pretty good.  I started off using Gutsy and then Mint but then I heard about Ultimate and am giving it a go. Mint is my favorite by far and I was going to wait to go back to it until a new version comes out, which I assume will be shortly after Ubuntu 8.04 is released.  But the amount of crashes that I have experienced might make me go back to Mint sooner than I expected.
