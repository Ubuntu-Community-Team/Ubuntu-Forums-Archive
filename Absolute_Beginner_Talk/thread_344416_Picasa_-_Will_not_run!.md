### Post by C-A on 2007-01-24
I had the same problem. However, I had also installed incorrect drivers for my nvidia card. I realized this and installed the drivers correctly. Later, I tried running picasa again and it worked. I can't say that the drivers were the cause of the problem but picasa did run after I installed the drivers correctly. I assume that a needed resource was not available for picasa when the wrong drivers were installed.
