### Post by ITdrummer on 2007-07-06
Hello,  
This is not a post for an error or a problem.  This is just a post to let people know about a wireless card i bought that could not have been any easier installing.  I was getting frustrated trying to get my broadcom chipset Belkin Wireless card working in Ubuntu, never with 100% funtionality.  

Luckily, i decided to buy a new wireless card.  D-Link DWL_G650 108mbps wireless card.  It was as simple as plug and play.  Seriously, I installed no drivers, no NDISwrapper, no fwcutter, NOTHING.  Just Plug-n-Play.  Now this card might not work as seamlessly on other machines, but i figured i would send a recommendation for a good wireless card for ubuntu/Linux.

P.S.- the performance is spectacular also.
