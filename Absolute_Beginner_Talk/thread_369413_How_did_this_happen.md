### Post by Ek0nomik on 2007-02-24
**What is Edubuntu?**

Edubuntu is a free/libre/ open source GNU/Linux operating system. And if that didn't make any sense to you, we hope that it soon will. In simpler terms, Edubuntu is a free software system designed for school environments, and should be equally suitable for kids to use at home.

Edubuntu is based on the "ever-popular" Ubuntu operating system, and has many contributors, many being volunteers. 

[http://www.edubuntu.org/faq](http://www.edubuntu.org/faq)

You can download Ubuntu 6.10 Edgy here:  [http://www.ubuntu.com/products/GetUbuntu/download#currentrelease](http://www.ubuntu.com/products/GetUbuntu/download#currentrelease)

Boot into the Live CD, and choose install.  You can knock out your current Ubuntu and go right over it.  I would reformat the partitions, assuming you have backed up all your needed data.
