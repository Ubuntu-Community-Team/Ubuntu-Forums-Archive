### Post by chewearn on 2008-01-10
Usually it's pressing the delete keyboard button, or one of the function key (liek F2 for Dell computers) during POST (boot-up BIOS screen), will bring you into the BIOS setup.

If you are not confident about changing stuffs in there, then don't; just look for the one for the USB keyboard.  If you made a mistake and change something, but don't know how to revert, just escape from the BIOS menu without saving, and it should be fine.
