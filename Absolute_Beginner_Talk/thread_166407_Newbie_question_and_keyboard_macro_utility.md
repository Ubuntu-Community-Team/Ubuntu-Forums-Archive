### Post by pbaehr on 2006-04-26
Data belongs in your home directory /home/[your user name]. You can make subfolders in there to suit your needs.

As far as applications, when you install them they will put themselves where they need to be. Largly they will fall in various ./bin folders like /usr/bin or /usr/local/bin. Just let them go where they want to and they'll be fine and organized.
