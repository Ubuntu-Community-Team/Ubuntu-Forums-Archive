Does it help if you unplug and replug it (preferably different port)? I had this a fewttimes with my MX revolution. Unplugging it and replugging the receiver it solved it, and the problem has dissapeared since.

All I can think off is that its related to a problem with  USB. The ports might be overloaded if you have lots of USB devices, or it could even be a PSU problem. Try connecting the devices to a powered hub, and/or check your voltages in the bios
