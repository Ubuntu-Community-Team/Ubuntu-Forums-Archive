### Post by louieb on 2006-11-18
The only sure fire way to keep the Win 98 install from eating Ubuntu is to get a second hard drive.
I known this isn't the answer you were looking for.
Don't know where your at, but here in Texas at the local swap meet or mom and pop computer store, I can get a small 5 - 30 meg used hard drive for about a dollar a meg. 

Its not hard but there are a few thing you will need to know. 

Setting a hard drive drive for Master, Slave or cable select and what the difference is.
Which way to plug the ribbon cable in.
Which way to plug the power cable in. 
My favorite hardware books are by guy named Minasi. 
Or there is plenty of stuff on the net about hard drive installation.
and plenty of post on this forum on how to get Ubuntu and windows to work together.
Good luck and happy Googeling.
