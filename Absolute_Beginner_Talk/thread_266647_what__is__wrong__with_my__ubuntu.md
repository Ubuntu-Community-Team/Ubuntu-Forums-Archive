### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-09-27
Okay, I think I see the problem.  Just unpacking the ISO directly to your hard disk won't give you a useable operating system.

When you download the ISO file, you have to [burn it to a CD]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso").  Then reboot.  If it doesn't boot from the CD, you'll have to change your computer's BIOS settings (or hit F12 when it first comes on) to boot from the CD-ROM drive.

That will load the Ubuntu LiveCD, and from there you can install the system to your hard disk.
