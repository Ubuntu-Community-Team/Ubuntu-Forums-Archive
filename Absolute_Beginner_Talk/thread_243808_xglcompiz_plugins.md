### Post by Crosbie on 2006-08-26
You'd be mad to install miniwin - it's not being supported anymore AFAIK.

I believe the plugins come packed-in - use gset-compiz to select which ones you want active. (Gset-compiz appears under Applications > Accessories for me, you may have to install it via Synaptic first.)  Only plugins in early/experimental stages are downloaded separately - again, the Compiz Forums are the place to find out how to do so, but I suggest you leave it to the hackers. :)
