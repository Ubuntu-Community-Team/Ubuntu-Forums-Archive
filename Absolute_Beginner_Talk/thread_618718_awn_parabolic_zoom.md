yes. (if you mean the fish-eye effect that you get on OS X.) You can change the behavior in the Awn Manager.

System>>Preferences>>Awn Manager. Select General on the left navigation menu. On the right side under "General" tab, Select Custom under the Icon Effects drop down.

I have configured the Zoom on Hover which gives a fish eye effect. (Not exactly, but kinda :D)

There are a lot of options there with spot light and 3d turns and such. play around with them. I have set different option for all categories :)
