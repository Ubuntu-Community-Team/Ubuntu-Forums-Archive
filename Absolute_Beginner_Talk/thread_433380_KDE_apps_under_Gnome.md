### Post by deanjm1963 on 2007-05-04
KDE apps run just fine under Gnome. If you've got a little disk space to spare and don't mind a bit of KDE installed on your system, install kcontrol, with that you can change how your KDE apps look, e.g. colors, icons, fonts, styles, etc. If you do install it, just open a terminal and type kcontrol, and you're up and running.
