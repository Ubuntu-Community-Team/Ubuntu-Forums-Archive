### Post by jhenager on 2007-03-23
I also have an older ATI card (a PCI All-in-wonder) on one of my systems. It works fine as a VGA card, but doesn't show up as having 3D acceleration, nor do the TV out features work. It does have a respectable number of screen resolutions available. I think I settled on 1280x1024 with 24 or 32 bit color.
Your milage may vary, but I would not expect all the bells and whistles to work.
