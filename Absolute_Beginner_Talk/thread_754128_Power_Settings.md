### Post by supertails on 2008-04-13
What does it mean when you put computer to sleep. Is that like a shutdown?

---

### Post by lswest on 2008-04-13
sleep is putting the computer into standby, whereby all the data of what programs are open, etc. are bundled, compressed, and saved into RAM.  The computer is then put into a minimal power mode, so that when you open the screen/hit the power button, it then goes back to the state it was in before (e.g. no boot, straight back to where you were).
