### Post by kitty500cat on 2007-06-30
Go to System->Administration->Network.  Click on the DNS tab.  Click the Add button.

Enter the following on the line:

**4.2.2.2**

Press Enter and close the window.  Then you should be good to go.

Regards :)

if kitty500cat's way wont work, turn your computer off, unplug it from the wall, wait 3 seconds, and plug it back in.
