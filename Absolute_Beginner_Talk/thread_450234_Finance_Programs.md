Hi Kristian.

There was quite a long thread about this recently, mentioning lots and lots of programs,

In Windows, I'd been using MS Money, and (sorry everyone!) I found that for my purposes most of the Linux finance programs were quite poor alternatives.

Then I discovered Kmymoney.  It may not be right for you, because I think you need a lot of KDE components installed (I have both Gnome and KDE set up on my PC), But I personally thought it was by far the best of the bunch, and now I actually prefer it to MS Money.
