### Post by landotter on 2005-11-21
Sound-Juicer and ripperX are both fine, I use both--you need to tweak your CD drive first. If it's less than three years old, enable DMA (be aware that this isn't 100% safe), you can find a how-to on the forums.

With Sound-Juicer, reduce the "paranoia" settings, by running gconf-editor, searching for sound-juicer, and reducing the level from four to one or zero. If your rips have skips in them, you'll need to move the paranoia level back.
