### Post by Nano Geek on 2007-11-20
If you do a lot of sound editing and music composition, then I would say that you shouldn't replace Windows with Ubuntu. Ubuntu still has some trouble with advanced sound hardware. 

But why don't you try Ubuntu in a [Virtual Machine]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_machine"). There are several programs that will let you do this. A few are [VirtualBox]("http://virtualbox.org/"), [VMWare]("http://vmware.com/products/server/"), and Microsoft's own [Virtual PC]("http://www.microsoft.com/windows/products/winfamily/virtualpc/default.mspx").I use VirtualBox, but you can use whatever you want.
