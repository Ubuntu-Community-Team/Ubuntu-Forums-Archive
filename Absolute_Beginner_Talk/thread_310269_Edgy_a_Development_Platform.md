### Post by IYY on 2006-12-01
The goal for Edgy was to try and insert many many fundamental changes under the hood. When changing the OS so drastically, things can always go wrong on certain machines. This is why Dapper, the last stable long term support edition of Ubuntu, is still recommended for machines where stability and reliability is particularly important.
