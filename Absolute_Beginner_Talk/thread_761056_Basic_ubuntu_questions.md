### Post by wormser on 2008-04-20
You do not log into root with Ubuntu.  Use sudo and gksudo (for GUI apps) to get root privileges.  Read why [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RootSudo").

Services are a bit different in Ubuntu.  There is System> Administration> Services.  It controls some start up services.  What are you trying to do?
