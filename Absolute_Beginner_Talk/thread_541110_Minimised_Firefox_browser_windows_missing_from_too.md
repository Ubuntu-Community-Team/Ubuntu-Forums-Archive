### Post by *Zebedee* on 2007-10-10
Hi all...  :-)

I tried the following with Dapper Drake 6.06 and it solved the
'finding minimised windows issue'!

Right mouse click on empty part of bottom panel next to workspaces.
Select 'add to panel'.
Scroll down and select 'notification area'.
Click on 'add'.
Done!  :-)

Although it will not necessarily seem as though anything has changed on the panel, this is because an invisible 'notification area' has been created.
When you now minimise a window, a blue globe icon will appear within the 'notification area' in the bottom panel, click it to minimise or maximise each window.

Hope this helps  :-)

Zebedee
