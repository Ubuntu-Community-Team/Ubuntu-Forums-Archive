### Post by cairnsww on 2006-11-02
Before I upgraded to Dapper I was running 5.10 and used the rather nice feature of being able to maintain my desktop between shut downs - for instance I run Evolution in my second workspace and all I had to do after rebooting was to go to WP 2 and there it was.

I can't figure out how to do this in Dapper.

---

### Post by Joe_CoT on 2006-11-02
I believe this is accomplished with sessions; go to System -> Preferences -> Sessions. If you check "automatically save changes to session", that will basically have the effect you're looking for.

### Post by cairnsww on 2006-11-02
Thanks - this is exactly waht I was looking for. (Previously it was on the logout screen).

### Post by Joe_CoT on 2006-11-02
In that case, I believe you can separately check "ask on logout" for that effect, though it's obviously overwritten by the "automatic" option. ;)
