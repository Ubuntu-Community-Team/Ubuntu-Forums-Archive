That may or may not improve once Ubuntu Linux is running, either off the liveCD or after installation.

Until you get that far, I suggest:

After booting the CD, you should see a number of options along the bottom of the screen. Try and select VGA or one of the screen resolutions. Also try and press F1 for help and study the options given there.
