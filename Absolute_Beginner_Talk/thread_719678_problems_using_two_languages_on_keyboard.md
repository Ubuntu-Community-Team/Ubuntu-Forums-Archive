### Post by thaibuntu on 2008-03-17
Hiya,
        I think I can help you with your keyboard issue. I just figured it out yeasturday. My wife is thai too, and of course she wants to IM her friends. Anyways to the point, just make sure you have the multiple keyboard layouts listed (System > Preferences > Keyboard > Layouts), then go to one of the panels that you have either top or bottom doesn't matter and right click it and click ADD TO THIS PANEL. A list will appear, at the bottom you should see one called KEYBOARD INDICATOR. Click that, then click ADD. You should now see a keyboard indicator on your panel. You can toggle keyboard layouts by simply left clicking it once. Click it again and it moves to the next on in the line. Mine has three layouts because I chose the USA layout and two variations of the thai layout.
       Hope this helps.
