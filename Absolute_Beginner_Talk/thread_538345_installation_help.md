### Post by p_quarles on 2007-08-29
Like the previous posters said, you just need to restart your computer to boot from the live disk.

If you do install, though, you should take a few precautions first:
1) Back up any important data that you have in Windows.
2) Defragment your hard drive twice. 

By doing this, you are much less likely to encounter any problems you can't solve.
