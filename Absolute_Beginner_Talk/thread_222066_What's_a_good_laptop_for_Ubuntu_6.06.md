### Post by Zaskoda on 2006-07-24
I have an Inspiron 630m which is now called something else at Dell.

Ubuntu is fairly well supported.

I've got a wide screen, so I had to do the 915 resolution hack. 

My 5 in 1 card reader won't work until I get a newer kernel. I haven't attempt to update it myself yet.

Modem doesn't work, and I haven't attempted to research it as the only times I've tried it were due to not having any other net access.

Some of the media buttons don't work, I've read they can be mapped but I haven't tried setting that up as the volume controls work and that's mostly what I use.

The light for the wireless card doesn't come on when wireless is turned on. It's just costmetic, but it would be nice if it worked.

That's about it... everything else seems rock solid.
