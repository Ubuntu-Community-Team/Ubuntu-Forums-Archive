### Post by paydaydaddy on 2008-03-06
Last things first: Ubuntu is not so amazing that it works with almost any software. There are alternatives for most microsoft programs, but not all. Some are not as full featured. Ubuntu (any linux distro) is not for everybody, but it is a viable alternative for many. Expect to struggle with it in the beginning. As for the install, you can (and many have) accidentally overwrite windows. Do plenty of research and back up everthing important before proceding. If at all possible, start by doing your first install on a seperate hard drive. I will leave it to others to chip in with further advice on the install, but that is my two cents worth. Oh, hope I don't sound negative, I just urge caution to the beginner. I love Ubuntu.
