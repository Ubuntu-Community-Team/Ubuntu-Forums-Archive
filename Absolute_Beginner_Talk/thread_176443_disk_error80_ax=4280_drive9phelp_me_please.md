### Post by catlett on 2006-05-14
It took me a couple of weeks. But if the midsum5 checked out then the disc should be good. Have you ever had any trouble with the cd drive? I had a bunch of trouble installing Mandriva and it ended up being my cd drive. I had 2 drives (a rom and a burner) I switched the master/slave setting around and used the other drive. The install went through.
I wouldn't get another drive for this but if you already have a second drive switch it to master and try it.
The last thing would be a problem with your hard drive. I doubt it if your not having any other problems.
