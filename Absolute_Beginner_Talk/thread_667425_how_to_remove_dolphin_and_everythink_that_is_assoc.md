### Post by harold4 on 2008-01-14
1.) Right click a folder, select Properties
2.) Click button next to "Type: Folder"
3.) Set konqueror as default and apply.

Unless you're hurting for drive space, it can't hurt to leave dolphin installed.  I didn't like it at first, but the more I started using it the more I found that konqueror and dolphin both serve a purpose.
