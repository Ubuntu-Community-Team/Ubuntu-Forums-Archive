### Post by Paulmd on 2007-08-25
> **nonewmsgs said:**
> yeah dsl with a frugal install or win95

win95 is signficantly faster with lower requirements than win98 but crashes more often.

He's got 160mb of ram in these things, i wouldn't bother with win95 unless he had 8-16mb ram. 

There's a few good reasons for 98se over win95.

1) as you say, it crashes less.

2) It supports ie6, which can view more websites than the ie5.5 (the last ie that installs on win95) [firefox is too resource hungry for that class of computer]

3) support for higher versions of many, many programs.

4) with that kind of ram, he's not likely to see any performance difference between 95 and 98.

5) much better built-in driver support.

6) USB actually works in 98. Wireless is right out for 95, but is a viable option in 98se.
