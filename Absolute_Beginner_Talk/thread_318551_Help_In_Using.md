### Post by Bachstelze on 2006-12-14
Well anyway, sice the autorun starts when you insert the CD in Windows, I guess it's properly burned, check your BIOS.

Just when you power your computer on, you should have some text saying "Press F2 to enter SETUP" or something similar. Press the mentioned key, look for something like "boot order" and put the CDROM first (or at least before the HD).
