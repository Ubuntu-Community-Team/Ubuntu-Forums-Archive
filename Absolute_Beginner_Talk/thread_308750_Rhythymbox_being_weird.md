### Post by turbojugend_gr on 2006-11-28
Codecs are needed to play media file types. From what I understand you need a some help on getting familiar with ubuntu. Please read these guides, mainly the parts of them you find interesting, theywill solve most of yourt problems.

1. Official [Ubuntu Desktop Guide]("https://help.ubuntu.com/6.10/ubuntu/desktopguide/C/index.html").
2. [Ubuntuguide.org's guide]("http://www.ubuntuguide.org")
3. [Ubuntu Document Storage Facility]("http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Main_Page")
4. Some great how-to's/guides from asyiu, on [psychocats]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php")

In your case, visiting the first guide and selecting to view the multimedia codecs page, should do it. Alternative choice is always visiting th official ubuntu wiki, the instructions on how to install multimedia codecs are here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)
