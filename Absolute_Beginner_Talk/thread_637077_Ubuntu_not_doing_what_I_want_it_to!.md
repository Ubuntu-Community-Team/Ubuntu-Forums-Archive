### Post by Lucifiel on 2007-12-10
Some suggestions:

a) Try shutting down the computer to let it rest for about 30 to 40 mins, once every few hours. 

b) Also, if your casing has some "holes" or "openings" on either the right or left side(not the back part of the computer, though!), try blowing a fan in. That ought to keep the computer cool. 

The longer you keep your computer on, the more heat it'll produce. This means that the computer fans will have to work harder to keep it cool and thus, the various components(sound card, graphics card, etc.) will draw more power from your PSU(power supply unit).
