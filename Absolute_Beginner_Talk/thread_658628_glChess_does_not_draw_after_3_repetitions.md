### Post by parhelia on 2008-01-04
Just as the title suggests ;) glChess does not recognize the threefold repetition rule or the 50 move rule. (For new players: the game is a draw  if the same position occurs 3 times, or nothing is captured/no pawn moved within 50 moves.) Personally I think these rules are as important as any other and should be implemented into Gnu Chess - or do I have a strange program setup?
