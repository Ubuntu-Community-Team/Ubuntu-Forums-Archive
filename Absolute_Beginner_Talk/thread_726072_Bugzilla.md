### Post by cmnorton on 2008-03-16
I believe by default Bugzilla uses MySQL, and it's not fussy about MySQL version. 

The problems I've had in the past configuring Bugzilla are with getting Perl modules configured. 

Once you get past configuring Bugzilla, it is quite good, especially given its price.

Here is a place to start: [http://www.bugzilla.org/docs/tip/html/configuration.html](http://www.bugzilla.org/docs/tip/html/configuration.html)
