### Post by adamklempner on 2007-08-20
When you drag-resize the image, try holding ctrl, shift, or alt.  One of those modifier keys maintains the aspect ratio.

---

### Post by vanadium on 2007-08-20
The "keep ratio" checkmark will make sure the height changes with the width in the Picture dialog. However, it won't affect the behaviour of dragging. To change the behaviour of dragging, use  modifier keys as mentionned by adamklempner.
