### Post by LHZ on 2005-10-15
This is actually normal behavior. When you type confidential information, such as your password, the terminal doesn't show anything. This keeps people looking over your shoulder from reading it. The bad news is, you'll have to type your password 'blind'. The good news is, you get good security (and nothing happens if you type it wrong, so you can just try again).

Also, the clipboard in Ubuntu (or Linux in general) is a bit less universal than in Windows. Usually, you can copy/paste to your heart's content within one application, but exchanging 'clippings' between different programs doesn't always work.
