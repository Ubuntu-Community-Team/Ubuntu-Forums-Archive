### Post by wpshooter on 2006-12-13
I think this is simply computer speak for "you don't have some of your e-mail parameters setup correctly".  Open evolution, then go to edit and the preferences and then check all of the setup parameters, especially your ISP e-mail server names for the incoming and outgoing mail and also your user name and password for accessing your ISP.  Something is either not filled in or something is probably simply misspelled.
