### Post by healthpellets on 2007-01-20
We have a winner!!!!!

First, Kubuntu does NOT by default allow you to swtich workspaces with any key combinations.  To correct this (imo, error) do the following:

Open up the Control Center
Search Key Bindings
Click on Keyboard Shortcuts
Scroll down to the 'Desktop Swtiching' section
Assign your desired key combination.
***Note*** If you're coming from Gnome, the Ctrl-Alt-Left/Right combination is avilable to you.

Done.
