### Post by nicedude on 2008-04-21
One thing is to boot your PC and enter bios and check settings for FAN CONTROL and see how it is set. I don't have a T60 but I think there is such a setting.

I looked at the Thinkpad forums and saw there is allot of problems people are having with them regarding heat, mostly looked like high temp on the GPU (graphics processor). Some people were talking about redoing the heatsink and applying thermal paste and new heatsinks, But I wouldn't attempt such a mod unless you have worked on laptops before as they are much harder to take apart than a desktop due to the small parts and a billion screws. actually they are harder to put back together :-)
