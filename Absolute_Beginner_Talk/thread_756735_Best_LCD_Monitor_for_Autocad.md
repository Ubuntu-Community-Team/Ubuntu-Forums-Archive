### Post by OffAxis on 2008-04-17
The make/model (as long as it's a decent one) doesn't matter nearly as much as VGA versus DVI; make sure it has (video card, too) and she uses the DVI interface. It's significantly clearer and sharper than the same monitor using the VGA cable.

By 'decent one' I mean:
low video response time (less than 8 ms)
high resolution (no smaller than 1280x1024)
minimum of 19 inch

If you want to go with dual monitors (one for the toolboxes one for the main drawing pane) even better. In that instance, they don't need to be the same size.

I'm a fan of Viewsonic and Samsung.
