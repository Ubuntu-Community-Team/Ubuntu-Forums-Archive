### Post by Hospadar on 2007-08-13
Well the notification area is the system tray,
Try restarting gdm with ctrl-alt-backspace and see if it loads up your system tray (this will log you out, so save anything you have open).

if and when you have updates available, they will show up here, but you may just not have any at the moment.

you can always go to System->Administration->Update Manager if you want to manually check your updates.
