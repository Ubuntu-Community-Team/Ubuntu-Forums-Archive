### Post by mac.ryan on 2007-04-26
You have to set up the order of booting devices from your bios (normally hitting "del" or some other keys at the very first moments after you power up your system (before it starts loading the OS....). Just make sure your CD dirve is checked **before** your HD.

### Post by mjitkop on 2007-04-26
When your computer is just restarting, press either F2, F8 or F12. You may have time to see the BIOS boot screen with some text telling you which keys to press to enter the setup screen. Form experience, it's usually one of the 3 keys I mentioned.
