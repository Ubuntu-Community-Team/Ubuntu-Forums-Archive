### Post by iPirates on 2007-06-19
Download Beryl/Emerald themes for the look of your windows/menus.  
Download GTK2 themes for the look of your panels/color of menus.

GTK2 themes are imported in the Theme manager in Menu/System/Preference/Theme (as stated above)
Beryl/Emerald themes are changed in Beryl itself (right click on the diamond in the taskbar, emerald theme manager).  Just import the theme from wherever you saved it at.
