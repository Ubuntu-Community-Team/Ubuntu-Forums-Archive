### Post by Keith_Burton on 2007-10-15
Mine are in /usr/lib/win32.

Other possibilities are /usr/lib/codecs, /usr/local/lib/win32, or /usr/local/lib/codecs/. Different players sometimes expect them in different places so try the other choices if /usr/lib/win32 doesn't work.

Hope this helps!
