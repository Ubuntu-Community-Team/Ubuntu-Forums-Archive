### Post by aysiu on 2006-01-14
Go to Applications > Utilities > Disk Utility > Images > Burn and find the .ISO
If you want Ubuntu completely free (not even paying for the blank CD), go to the [https://shipit.ubuntu.com](https://shipit.ubuntu.com) website and order your free PowerPC CDs. It'll take a couple of months, but even the shipping is free.
