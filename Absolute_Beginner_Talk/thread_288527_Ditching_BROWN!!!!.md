### Post by land0 on 2006-10-30
Go to System--> Administration--> Log in Window enter your password
Below the GDM(Log in) themes you will see Background Color click on what looks like a button to the right select the blue/orange/white that works for you. ;)
As for the Log in Splash go to System--> Preferences--> Splash Screen
If you do not have any splash screens installed you can open Synaptic go to Settings--> Repositories--> make sure all of the extra repositories are activated Universe etc... Then reload and do a search for gnome-art. after you have installed that go to System--> Art Manager and download all the splash screens backgrounds you can handle.

I hope this helps
