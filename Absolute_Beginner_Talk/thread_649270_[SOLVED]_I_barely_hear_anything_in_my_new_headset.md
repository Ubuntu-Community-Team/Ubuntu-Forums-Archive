### Post by Shazaam on 2007-12-24
Have you ran "alsamixer" in terminal yet? If you see anything with a MM at the bottom it is muted. To unmute select the control and hit the "m" button on your keyboard. Use the up and down arrows on your keyboard to adjust higher or lower. Make sure you scroll to the right as there can be many more  settings depending on your souncard.
