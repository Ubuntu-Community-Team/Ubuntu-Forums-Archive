**In the Compiz Config window there should be "Window Switcher," and if you go to its settings you can give it a different shortcut key. I use Alt + Tab (out of habit).**

How do u change your skydome and put in something like this.  [http://compiz-themes.org/content/show.php/Lines+Skydome+II?content=63947](http://compiz-themes.org/content/show.php/Lines+Skydome+II?content=63947)

**Go to General Options in the Compiz Config window and go to the Skydome tab. You can enable it and change it there.**
