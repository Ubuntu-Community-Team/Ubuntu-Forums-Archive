### Post by jazzmuzik on 2006-04-28
A general solution is to clear your cache. In Firefox, do Tools, Clear Private Data, and check Cache and Authenticated Sessions. You could also select Cookies as well if you don't mind losing some of that data.

Another solution is to log out and then back in. It might just be a temporary glitch after upgrading or something like that. newegg.com works fine for me.

Are you using a proxy? Any installed plugins that might be filtering the web stream?
