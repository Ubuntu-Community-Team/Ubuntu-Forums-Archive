### Post by AAM on 2007-07-12
get music off CDs with "Sound Juicer" - may find it under **Applications > Sound & Video > Sound Juicer CD Extractor**
if not there, you can get it off the package repositories

later ... I tries the formula editor in OOo. Have a go, it doesn't seem to be too difficult. s=ut+1/2at^2 didn't cause much grief.

much later ... I opened a document, typed some rubbish that you can see on the screen capture PNG, placed the cursor at the place to insert and the chose **Insert > Object > Formula** and the lower window opened up. How did I know what to type in? well, if you select a 'New" formula you will see the entire formula selections. Right clicking on the lower box will give you these options also. From then on it was just experimentation!
