### Post by Old Pink on 2006-10-07
Yes, click System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts and link Ctrl+Alt+Delete to run "gnome-system-monitor"

Or hold Alt+F2 and type/paste: gnome-system-monitor

Or, get [Automatix]("http://www.getautomatix.com") and click the Ctrl+Alt+Delete option. :)
