### Post by sciencectn on 2008-01-29
That means there's no driver for the printer. I tried looking up your printer on the Linux openprinting database, and got nothing. Here's the list of Dell printer drivers with Linux on the site:

[http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=Dell](http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=Dell)

Generally, I wouldn't even bother trying to get a Dell printer working with Linux, because of their low quality Linux support. It looks like you'll have to get an HP (they have very good Linux support) or use it with windows only. There may be a way to get a driver for Linux, but that's beyond my knowledge.
