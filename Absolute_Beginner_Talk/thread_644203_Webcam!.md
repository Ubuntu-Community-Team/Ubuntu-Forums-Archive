### Post by bcrom on 2007-12-18
First see if you have anything input from your webcam at all.  An easy way to check is to open up Ekiga and click on the webcam icon.  if you see yourself, your webcam was automatically detected.  If not, open up Edit-->Preferences and scroll down to Video Devices.  Play with the settings there and see if you can get your webcam to display something.  If that works, then move on to Pidgin, Skype, and whatever else.
