### Post by aysiu on 2006-09-18
email lives in your /home folder. It's in a hidden folder within /home/wirelessman

For example, I use Thunderbird (the one from Mozilla--not the Ubuntu one), and my emails are in the /home/username/.thunderbird directory.

If you had Evolution, yours would be in /home/wirelessman/.evolution

To see hidden files, press Control-H in Nautilus.
