### Post by kevinatkins on 2007-12-11
Hi,

I sympathise. Actually, Totem is rubbish for playing DVD's, it really is... A far better alternative would be either gXine or VLC media player - both are available from the repositories. If you want to play most commercial DVD's, then you'll also need libdvdcss - again, it's available from the (multiverse) repositories but be careful: it's illegal to use in the USA; I'm not certain about other territories, apart from the UK where we're OK (at the moment.....;-)
