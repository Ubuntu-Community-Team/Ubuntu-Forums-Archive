### Post by Prodromoi on 2008-01-20
I've no experience of the Audigy, but for what it's worth I would recommend *against* a Creative Labs X-Fi card.  I had one in my machine when it was operating Windows, but when I migrated over to Linux I found that it had no proper driver support for Linux.

Apparently there is now a 64-bit driver for it, with no plans for a 32-bit driver.  Even so, the 32-bit driver reportedly gives sub-standard output.  [*Link to relevant thread.*]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=571656")
(I ended up taking the X-Fi card out and putting it into a Windows machine, at least until better drivers come along.)
