### Post by zourcast on 2006-03-25
Problem Solved.
Reinstalled the printer, but instead of using de default stc680p.upp drivers, I installed the GimpPrinter drivers.
Now I have a lot more options to chose from, including a lot more resolutions, both lower and upper resolutions.
Just in case someone has the same problem, Epson Stylus Color 680, is the European version. In the US, this printer is the Epson Stylus Color 777.
