### Post by kwacka on 2008-02-12
If you click Systen --> Preference --> Appearance--> Visual Effects ensure 'Extra' is selected.

Alternatively type 'ccsm' in a terminal.

The CompizConfig Settings Manager will open - choose what effects you want.
