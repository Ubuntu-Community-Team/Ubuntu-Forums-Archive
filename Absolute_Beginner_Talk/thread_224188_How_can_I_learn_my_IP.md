### Post by jimmygoon on 2006-07-27
depends on what network interface you need

"ifconfig eth0" should give you the ip address of your wired connection
"ifconfig ath0" should give you the ip address of your wireless connection

[www.whatismyip.com](www.whatismyip.com) will give you your external ip address

also prefixing the above commands with sudo will give you more information on them
