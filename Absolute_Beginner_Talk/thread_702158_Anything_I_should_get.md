### Post by mkoehler on 2008-02-20
There is iPod support in amarok as well as a large number of other media players.  Amarok is relatively nice, but it doesn't index your music library in a way that makes searches pain-free.  For example, I have 7700+ songs on my music server, and carrying out searches on it is painful (even after switching to MySQL from SQLite).  For my purposes, I like banshee.  It's clean, simple, and does what I need it to do.
