### Post by Lambert on 2005-12-19
There are three known different chipsets in these devices so the version needs to be identified. That link will only work if you have v2.6 and as the opening line states, it's an incomplete guide. You can also search the forums for wusb11 as there have been posts about this device before.

More then likely you will need to use an app called ndiswrapper to get your device to work. There's a link in my signature you can follow.
