### Post by catlett on 2006-09-04
Rerun ```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```unless you have a wireless keyboard, the computer will autodetect the typre without a problem. Just accept the defaults by pressing enter. The same thing with the mouse. You can actually go threough the whole thing by selecting the default option given and have pretty good results.
If you do a little research on your monitor and video card and find their specifications, you will have better results.
One "tip" is to select all the resolutions they give you when you run dpkg-reconfigure. By selecting all resolutions the system goes into a default  where it will offer you every available resolution.
