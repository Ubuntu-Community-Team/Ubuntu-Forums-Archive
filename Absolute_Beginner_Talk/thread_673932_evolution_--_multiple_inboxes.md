### Post by RudolfMDLT on 2008-01-23
It seems that you are going to have to use Thunderbird if you need complete separate in boxes for syncing purposes or privacy.

What you could try, I just did this and it works pretty well, is create two folders under your global Inbox called for example Gmail and Ananzi. Then, rightclick on one message from your first account, click "Create Rule From Message", "Filter On Recipients". Make sure that in the filter textbox, only your email address is entered for the first account. 

Do the same for the second account. 

Now, select all your messages and press "CTRL + Y"

In my Inbox, all messages from my Ananzi account are now in one folder and all the messages from my Gmail account are in one folder.
