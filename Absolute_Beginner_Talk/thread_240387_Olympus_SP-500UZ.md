### Post by Bartender on 2006-08-20
I just wanted to post that an Olympus SP-500UZ digital camera works like a champ with Dapper.  You do the exact same thing with the camera that you do with Windows - plug the camera in to a USB port and turn it on.  The LCD on the back of the camera lights up to a configuration screen.  You toggle down to "PC" and click the "Enter-OK" button.  The camera talks to the PC and Dapper places an icon on the Desktop.  I don't remember what it called the camera; a USB something or another, but the point is it worked just as easily as with that other OS.
Of course, nothing on the Olympus CD will work in Ubuntu, but Ubuntu has GIMP and the default image viewer(s).
