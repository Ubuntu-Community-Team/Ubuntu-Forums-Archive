### Post by seijuro on 2006-10-03
You may find something interesting for MMORPG or eyecandy 3d multiplayer games [here]("http://www.crystalspace3d.org/tikiwiki/tiki-directory_browse.php?parent=1") but there is a lot more to games than eye candy imo so here is a list of other free multiplayer games that I like.

Gtetrinet - Multiplayer tetris over internet or lan
Netpanzer - RTS like game w/o having to micromanage resources
Globulation2 - VERY COOL RTS with a different spin on resource management
lbreakout2 - nice sdl breakout game with multiplayer over lan/internet
Xpilot - multiplayer space shooter
BZflag - multiplayer tank game mentioned above.

there is tons and tons of stuff available it really depends on what interests you I would HIGHLY recommend browsing the games section in your package manager after you have added the universe and multiverse repos and updated.
