### Post by ewtesterman@cox.net on 2006-02-17
You will get this message every time you do a major update. This will update the kernel you are running, this is the main part of the Operating System. It is okay, and recommended that you update. After updating you will need to restart you will now have two different kernels in your grub boot menu, so if your machine does not perform well under the new one just reboot again into the old one and use synaptic to remove the new one. Hope this helps
