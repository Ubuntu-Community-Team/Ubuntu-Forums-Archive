### Post by stump138 on 2008-01-24
the trick I have used with some success in the past with cd swapping issues in wine is to manually un mount the cd, put in the next one and manually mount it.  Sometimes you have to go around wine and make it happen for yourself.

When wine prompts you for the next cd try:
```
sudo umount /media/cdrom
```

eject the cd and put the next one in.  You may have to manually mount it, but sometimes you can get away with just pressing "ok" when you have the next cd in
