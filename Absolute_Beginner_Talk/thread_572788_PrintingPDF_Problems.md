### Post by *Zebedee* on 2007-10-10
Hi rlj999,

Having recently been in a position where I wanted to produce the document in a large version over many sheets of A4, I found I had to alter the 'canvass size' of the document.
So it is possible that the canvass size you are working on is much bigger than you anticipate.

Try looking for 'canvass size' or perhaps 'page size' in the menus of the programme where you created the pdf.

If you are printing in A4, they should be set to 209.97mm x 297.01mm.
They are usually pretty easy to change, just click and add the correct numbers.

Hope this helps.  :-)
