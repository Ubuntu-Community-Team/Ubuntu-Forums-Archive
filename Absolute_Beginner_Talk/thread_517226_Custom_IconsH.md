### Post by felicity on 2007-08-04
I don't use KDE so I can't help you there, but in GNOME you right click the icon/folder you want to change, choose Properties, and under the first tab, the Basic tab, you should see the icon currently being used. If you click the icon it will bring bring up a dialog box to choose the location of the new icon you want to use.
