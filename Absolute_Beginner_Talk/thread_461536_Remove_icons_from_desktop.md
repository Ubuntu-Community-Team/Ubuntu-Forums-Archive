### Post by dpar on 2007-06-01
If you want to remove all the icons for drives off the desktop, then do this......
In terminal type gconf-editor
when it pops up, click on apps
scroll down to nautilus and click on it
Then click on desktop
On the right you should see volumes_visible
uncheck that
you should be good to go:D
