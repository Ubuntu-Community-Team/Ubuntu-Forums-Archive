### Post by stchman on 2008-04-04
> **NR1224 said:**
> Can i add a new IDE hard drive without rebooting? just wondering

NO, IDE deives are not hot swappable.  USB and SATA are not IDE.  If you add an IDE hdd to a system when it is running you could ruin the PC and the hdd.
