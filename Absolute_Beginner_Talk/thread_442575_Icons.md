### Post by Happy_Man on 2007-05-13
Ok. First, open up System --> Preferences -->Themes.
Then, there will be a row of buttons on the right, click "configure."
Navigate to the icons tab, and click install.
Find the downloaded .tar.gz (don't extract it), and hit enter.
Your icon theme is now installed! Congrats!
This will work for pretty much any icon theme.

### Post by Znupi on 2007-05-13
That is not an icon theme! It's a **whole** theme. To install it just go to
**System -> Preferences -> Theme** and click on **Install Theme** and choose the downloaded file. As Happy_Man said, don't extract it.
