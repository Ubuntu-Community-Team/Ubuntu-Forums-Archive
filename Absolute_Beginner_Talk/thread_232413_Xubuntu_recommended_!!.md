### Post by jordi_rc on 2006-08-08
Hi to all people!

I am using XUBUNTU from a week ago and I am really happy with it!

[http://www.xubuntu.org/](http://www.xubuntu.org/)

It's really easy. I installed it alone in the hard disk of a barebone (XCube, sempron 3000+ 1 gb ram , onboard audio and onboard video, onboard LAN ), and it was installed in 15 minutes with very few questions. I got a graphical instaler from the beginning that made me very calm about it was going to work well.

I didn't have to configure nothing about graphics card, it is OK from the beginning.

After some hours of use, I got to install xampp, run it and host my website to see it in my local host. I am not already connected to dsl, but this has been very easy.

The look and feel of Xubuntu is very nice. It is easy to change all to your desire, but the default style is great.

I recommend you to use Xubuntu, and download the ISO from the main mirror, burn the cd at low speed (4X), then make the checksum and when installing, make sure the cd is ok by the option of the installer.

You will like it!

And it runs very quickly!

I tested Debian before, that worked really slow, and was so difficult for me, and detected badly my video card.
Then I downloaded Ubuntu but I got a corrupt ISO and it crashed all my hard disk data.
After that I discovered Xubuntu and now I am really happy that all that happened, because I think I ended with the best distro.

Jordi
