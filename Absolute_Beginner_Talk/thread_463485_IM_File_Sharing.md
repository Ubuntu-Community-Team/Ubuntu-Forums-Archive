### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-06-03
I would not recomend that you try that - What I'd do in your shoes is to download vsftpd and install it. Then make a list of your bodies as ftp-users on your system. This way you only get thrusted users on your list. You could make a common list like an account for dudes and the give the password to the boddies you thrust.

Get vsftpd by:

sudo apt-get install vsftpd

And then correct the file /etc/vsftpd.conf to disable anonymous login through your ftp.
