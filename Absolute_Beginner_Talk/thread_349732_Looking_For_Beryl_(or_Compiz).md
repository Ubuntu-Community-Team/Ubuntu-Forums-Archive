### Post by Brunellus on 2007-02-21
Attention, new users:

I know many of you are keen to try out the pimptastic shininess that is Beryl.  Understand this:  

[SIZE=6]**Beryl & Compiz are BETA SOFTWARE**[/SIZE]

It is in no way guaranteed to be stable or reliable.  It is not included in default Ubuntu for a good reason.  Therefore **Beryl, Compiz, and all references to Beryl or Compiz, are off-topic in this section. **


If you want to try Beryl out without affecting your current install, try [Sabayon Linux]("http://www.sabayonlinux.org").  That will give you Beryl on a Live CD.

**If you want to walk on the cutting edge, you must understand that you might bleed. You have been warned.**

If you have read all this and STILL interested in installing Beryl on your ubuntu system:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263851](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263851)

Is a thread on the topic.


General advice: learn to be comfortable in Ubuntu & Linux first before you go off beta-testing.

If you DO decide to install Beryl or Compiz:

1) Keep careful notes, especially noting WHOSE directions you are following so that you can give people that try to help you an idea of what you've done and what might have gone wrong.

2) Do not expect it to be worry/bug free.

3) In the event that it does crash, file useful crash data and forward this to the developers.
