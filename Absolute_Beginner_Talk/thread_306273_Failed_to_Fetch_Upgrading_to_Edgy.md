### Post by taurus on 2006-11-24
Going from Breezy to Dapper, you just have to replace breezy with dapper in /etc/apt/sources.list and then run "sudo aptitude update && sudo aptitude dist-upgrade".  However, you can't do that with Dapper to Edgy.  You need to use the official steps.  

And since you plan to install Edgy, I highly recommend you have the installer to reformat your / unless you have a separate /home.  Then, don't format /home but mount that partition to /home.  If you don't format your / when you install Edgy, you still have stuff left over from Dapper and that could spell trouble later...
