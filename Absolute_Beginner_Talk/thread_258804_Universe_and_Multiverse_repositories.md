### Post by Marsolin on 2006-09-16
Open Adept and select Manage Repositories from the Adept menu. Right click on any of the grayed out lines that say universe or multiverse and select Enable. Click Apply when you are all done and Fetch Updates.

Hopefully that helps.

### Post by Marsolin on 2006-09-16
> **That Guy X said:**
> O and im useing Synaptic not adept.

For Synaptic select Repositories from the Settings menu and click the checkbox next to any lines that mention universe or multiverse.

The other option is selecting any that have main listed as a section and change it to "main restricted universe multiverse" instead.
