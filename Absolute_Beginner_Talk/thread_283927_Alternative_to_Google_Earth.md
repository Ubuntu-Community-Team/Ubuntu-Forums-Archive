### Post by bobpur on 2006-10-24
From November issue of Maximum PC

NASA Takes on Google Earth.
Nasa's new app, World Wind, will look mighty familiar to Google Earth users. However, instead of using the low-resolution satellite images Google purchased on the free market, World Wind's imagery comes from Nasa's satellites, and includes complete maps of the Earth, the Moon, and Mars. Plus, you can even overlay current weather conditions and cloud positions.
Get it at [http://worldwind.arc.nasa.gov](http://worldwind.arc.nasa.gov)

This web site also uses terms like "Open Source" and "Linux"
