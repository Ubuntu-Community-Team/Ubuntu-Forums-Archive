### Post by kyphi on 2007-09-13
If your attempt to install Ubuntu to your second hard drive failed, then you had better start again.
Ubuntu will recognise your second hard drive.

If you are worried that Windows cannot see your second hard drive, you have been looking in the wrong place, perhaps.  Right click on your Computer icon on the desktop and select 'Manage' from the drop-down menu and then go to 'Disk Management'.  Then you should see both your hard drives.

Linux isn't hard really, just different.
