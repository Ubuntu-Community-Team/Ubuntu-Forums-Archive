### Post by Malta paul on 2008-03-24
You could try setting your 'Keyboard Preferences'
Go to System>Preferences>Keyboard. Then slow down 'cursor Blinking' try typing in 'Test settings' and see what happens , you can click 'Mouse Keys' then click 'Accessibility for  mouse preferences and settings, and slow the cursor down if necessary.
It may also be an idea to set your visual effect to 'None'   System>preferences>Appearance>visual effects,Just in case this is messing things up.
Have fun:)
