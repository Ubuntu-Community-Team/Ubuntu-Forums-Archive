### Post by huygens on 2006-10-24
Which version of flash player are you using?
To know it, just type "about:plugins" in the address bar (where you would usually enter a web address just like [www.ubuntu.com](www.ubuntu.com))

The flash version 9 for Linux (and also for windows) is still in beta, and it is available here: [http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer9.html](http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer9.html)
However, myspace cannot use flash9 as it is not out yet. They are using flash8.
