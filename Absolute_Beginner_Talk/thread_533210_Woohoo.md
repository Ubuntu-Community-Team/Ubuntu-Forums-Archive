### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-08-23
Hi and welcome to Ubuntu.

Your access to the NTFS drive/partition from Ubuntu has no effect on your files on NTFS since you have read only access. This of cause, if you didn't install NTFS read/write tools from Programs/Add/Remove/System tools and enabled the access.
