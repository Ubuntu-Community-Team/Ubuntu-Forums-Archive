### Post by oakgrove on 2008-03-03
Maybe go from the opposite direction and copy your profile in /home/user/.mozilla/firefox/yourprofile/ to /home/user/.mozilla/firefox/yourprofile.bak, delete the contents of your original profile that is still there because you copied, not renamed, and start fresh by adding one extension at a time until you have what you want.  Then you can just import your bookmarks from your old profile.
