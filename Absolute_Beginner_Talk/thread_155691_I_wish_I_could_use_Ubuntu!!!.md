### Post by Papa-san on 2006-04-05
The wireless on my Dell Lattitude did not work with Ubuntu. I struggled with it for weeks. There are many posts that deal with it, and some wiki pages that will help. Under the wiki, search for wireless troubleshooting. You will need to find the PCI ID for your particular card so that you can get the right drivers. That wiki will help. If you can hook your system to your router with a cat 5 cord, I would suggest installing and running Automatix. (Takes a while, but is SO worth the wait!) 
yoy will want to select and install ndisgtk from the list. this will almost do it for you, but you will need the right drivers first.
