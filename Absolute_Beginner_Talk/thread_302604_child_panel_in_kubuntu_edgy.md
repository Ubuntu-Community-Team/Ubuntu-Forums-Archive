### Post by ewl1217 on 2006-11-18
If the panel is locked, you'll see the options "Unlock Panels" and "Help" when you right-click it. If that's the case, choose "Unlock Panels". After that, right-click the panel again, and choose "Add New Panel > Panel" (You can also choose anoter custom panel if you want.). Just make sure you click on an empty spot on the panel. If you don't, you may not get the right menu, or you may have to go to "Panel Menu".
