What is packet sniffing? and how can it help me with security?

---

### Post by cgjones on 2006-05-14
Packet sniffing is basically just watching the traffic over a network using a tool like tcpdump, Ethereal or Snort. It usually is used for auditing purposes, or in the case of Snort, it can be used to filter certain types of traffic.
