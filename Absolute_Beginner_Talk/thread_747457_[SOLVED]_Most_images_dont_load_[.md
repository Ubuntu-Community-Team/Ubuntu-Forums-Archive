### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-04-06
1. Backup your bookmarks from the Bookmarks Manager (Export)
2. Delete the .mozilla directory in your home folder. Open your home folder, go to View > Show Hidden Files, and find and remove the .mozilla folder.

That should clear up a botched profile. It will create a new one, so if there is anything you need to keep from it, make backups.
