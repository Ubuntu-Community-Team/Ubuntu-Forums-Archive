### Post by siccness on 2006-06-24
You'll have to enter your BIOS and change the boot sequence of your devices.

For most computers, press the Delete key when it's showing the list of IDE devices/memory scan at startup.

Note: Press it repeatedly to ensure you get into the BIOS. Nothing more annoying than missing it :|

---

### Post by Winblowz on 2006-06-24
You need to load your motherboard bios when the computer boots. Once you are in your bios, edit the device boot priority  and make the cd-rom drive the number one priority.
