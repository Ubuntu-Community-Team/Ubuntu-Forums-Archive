### Post by harold4 on 2008-02-28
Not a problem.  

As a side note, you could make directory ~/bin, copy mppdec to that dir and add ~bin to your $PATH.  Doing this will give you access to whatever you put in there, but not every user on the machine.

Probably not necessary for mppdec, but good form to know for future reference.
