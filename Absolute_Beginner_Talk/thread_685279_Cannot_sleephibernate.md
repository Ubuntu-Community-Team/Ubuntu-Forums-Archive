### Post by pmshirey on 2008-02-06
This error will actually have to be resolved in Windows. Go into windows, and right click into properties, then go to you power savings area and make sure your computer will allow this setting. After this you may need to do some research on your computer power supply and motherboard and try to see if the power supply will allow this with the motherboard you have.
