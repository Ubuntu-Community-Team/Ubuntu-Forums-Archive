### Post by cykotiktek on 2008-02-12
I was able to ventrilo to work under wine.  It took me a couple of times uninstalling it and reinstalling it.  The key to that was doing regedit and searching for Ventrilo and deleting all entries.  

What finally got mine to work was actually the input and output devices in settings 

I have also read that there is a addon that you can use that will key the mic if ventrilo is behind another window.  I run dual monitors i am able to keep mine up on one monitor in front of any windows and play WoW and still be able to key the mic.  My mic key is left alt which if i am clicking on something in WoW while i am talking it activates wobbly windows on WoW so that is another issue i have noticed with it.  Good luck it isn't impossible and the voice quality is still better on vent then it is on teamspeak

Also the ventrilo linux version has been in development since like 2006.... I am not holding my breath on it being completed anytime soon.
