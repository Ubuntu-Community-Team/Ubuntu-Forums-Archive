### Post by gpilkay on 2008-03-08
If it's a question about hardware compatibility, you can also use a virtualization application, like VMWare or VirtualBox.  I use VirtualBox on a Dell laptop that didn't have supportable hardware.  It works just fine, cntrl-F gives me full-screen virtual Ubuntu laptoppy with XP invisible in the background. Not the ideal solution but it's better then nothing.  Plus I can play Pingus when I'm supposed to be working.
