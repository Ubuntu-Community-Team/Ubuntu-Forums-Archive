### Post by damianubuntu on 2006-06-06
You'll hear some people say that getting away from Windows is not a valid reason for moving to linux, and that you have to realise that they are different tools for different jobs, etc.
personally I generally disagree, with a few caveats.  I think if you keep dual boot as a reserve option, and get happy with using the forum and wiki here for help, you should have no difficulty making the switch.  Plenty of people here have done it, and they are not all geeks.
Go for it, and be happy that time you invest will be part of a longterm solution not repeatedly firefighting against viruses, spyware and crap software!

All the best.............
