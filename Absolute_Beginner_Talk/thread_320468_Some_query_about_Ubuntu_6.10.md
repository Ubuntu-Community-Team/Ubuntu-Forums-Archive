### Post by deadgobby on 2006-12-17
For your Mp3 and codecs is a simple one. You can either install Automatix, Easy Ubuntu, or Install them one by one.
[http://www.getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation](http://www.getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation)
[http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org](http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org)
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)

 Ubuntu does not come the codecs and you need to install them yourself. 

As for antivirus. You really do not need one. Most virus's are made for Windows and for you to get a virus working in Linux. You have to loged in as root and invoke it. There is like 45 know virus's for Linux and your changes are very low. Like 1 out of 4000. I been using Linux for over 2 years now and yet to get one.
