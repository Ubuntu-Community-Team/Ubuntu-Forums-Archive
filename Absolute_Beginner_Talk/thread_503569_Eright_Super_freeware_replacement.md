### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-07-18
How funny, I was just looking up Super yesterday.

You could try installing gTranscode from the repositories, and/or do a search for "mencoder frontend" on Google, but there's no hard-and-fast answers yet. Transcode, Mencoder, and ffmpeg GUI frontends come and go all the time unfortunately.

I just converted some videos to MPEG using Konverter ([http://www.kraus.tk/projects/konverter/packages/konverter_0.93-1_i386.deb](http://www.kraus.tk/projects/konverter/packages/konverter_0.93-1_i386.deb)). It's a bit buggy but it works.
