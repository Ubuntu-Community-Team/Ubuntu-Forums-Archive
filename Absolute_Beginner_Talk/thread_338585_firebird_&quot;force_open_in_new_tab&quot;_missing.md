### Post by NT4usB on 2007-01-14
fwiw...
Things come and go in the Firefox GUI. The contributors sometimes move or remove settings.
You can bypass the GUI and edit the settings directly my typing ```
about:config
``` in the location bar. There you can edit (most) everything that's configurable in Firefox.

More reading:
[http://kb.mozillazine.org/About:config](http://kb.mozillazine.org/About:config)
[http://kb.mozillazine.org/About:config_entries](http://kb.mozillazine.org/About:config_entries)
