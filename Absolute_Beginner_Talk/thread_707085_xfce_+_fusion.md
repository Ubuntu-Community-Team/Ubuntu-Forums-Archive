### Post by mali2297 on 2008-02-25
> **frito said:**
> does installing fusion to xfce completely defeat the purpose of having xfce vs gnome?

No. Compiz Fusion replaces the window manager Xfwm4, but there are other parts of Xfce4 that are lighter than their Gnome counterparts. For example, the file manager Thunar will still be faster than Nautilus.
