### Post by kyphi on 2007-06-10
There is nothing wrong with Rhythmbox.

However, if you really want to use another programme to play your music files, do the following:

Right click on any music file, go to Properties, then to 'Open With' and click on the programme of your choice.

Enjoy.

### Post by ThinkBuntu on 2007-06-10
I used to be quite anti-Rhythmbox, but really it can do most of what Amarok can do, with a couple extra nice features. I find the Rhythmbox UI a little more intuitive, especially with an iPod connected (no separation of the collection and playlist view, much like iTunes). One nice thing, if you like to shuffle, is that, like iPod or iTunes, Rhythmbox can go both back and forward without losing your upcoming shuffled songs.

### Post by ncappel1 on 2007-06-10
You could try disabling the keyboard shortcut and entering a custom launcher in Applications-->Configuration editor. Navigate to the "metacity" tab and add "amarok" as the value for keybinding_commands_1 and then under global_keybindings change the value of command 1 to the keystroke(s) of your choosing. It should be availbe for use immediately!
