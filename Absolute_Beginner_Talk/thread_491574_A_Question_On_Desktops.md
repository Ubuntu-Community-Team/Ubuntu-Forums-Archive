### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-07-03
You bet. That will allow you to choose between whichever one you like.  Just log out, click Sessions and select what you want- Gnome (Ubuntu) KDE (Kubuntu) or XFCE (Xubuntu) and it will take you right in.  It's a great way for those who aren't sure which desktop they'd like, to pick and choose without downloading multiple CDs.
