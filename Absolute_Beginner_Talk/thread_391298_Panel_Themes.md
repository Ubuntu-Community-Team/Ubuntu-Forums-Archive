### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-03-23
Right click on it and go to properties.
You can play with it there some.

Also, check out gnome-color-chooser
[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=47349](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=47349)
It provides a GUI to editing .gtkrc-2.0 which you can use to edit the widgets in your panel.
