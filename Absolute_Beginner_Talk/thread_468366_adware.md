### Post by az on 2007-06-08
> **abu-fatu said:**
> how is it that i was re-directed from the page i was looking at to their page?

It's called phishing.  Users can be misled via email, instant messaging or popup ads.

Since you can use standards-compliant email, IM or HTML to mislead people, phishing is OS-agnostic.

You could have been running from the live cd, with a read-only filesystem and you would still have been told that your computer was scanned and contained adware.  

I doubt that your computer was actually scanned.

Just ignore it an remember that the owners of that site do business with less-than-scrupulent businesses.
