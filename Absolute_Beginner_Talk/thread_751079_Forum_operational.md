### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2008-04-10

### Post by Kebabman on 2008-04-10
If you click on 'Search' from the top menu, then on 'Advanced Search' from the drop down menu you can enter a username to search for on the right hand side. This should bring up all posts by a specific user.

Once you have done this remember you can subscribe to a thread by clicking on 'Thread Tools' -> 'Subscribe to this thread' whilst viewing it. This will then allow you to quickly go through your subscribed threads in your user control panel.
