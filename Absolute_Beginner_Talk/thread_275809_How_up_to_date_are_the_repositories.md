### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-10-11
The repositories tend to go into "freeze" shortly before the distribution's release, and the only updates are essential bugfixes and security patches.

However, many people make .debs for new programs, so you can just install them with one click. For instance, Dapper comes with Gaim 1.5, but someone packaged Gaim 2 beta as a .deb, which I've installed.
