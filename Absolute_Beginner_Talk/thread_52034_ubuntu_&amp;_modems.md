### Post by psyched321 on 2005-07-26
If your friend has a Conexant modem, or one with a Conexant chipset, forget about it.  The drivers provided by Linuxant are the only ones available and they're commericial.

If he has a 3COM/US Robotics modem, a Sportster like mine, you'll run into problems.  I tried setting up the modem using gnome-networkconfig with no success and alot of frustration.  I didn't try the guide linked to above, it may be useful.

I hope that a simple way of setting up hardware modems is provided in Breezy Badger!  My experience is that it's not very user-friendly.
