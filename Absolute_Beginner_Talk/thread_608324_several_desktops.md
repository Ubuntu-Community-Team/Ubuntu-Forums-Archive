### Post by PurposeOfReason on 2007-11-09
It's really simple. I'm assuming you have a fresh install. In the bottom left corner is the workspaces applet. You can just click it to navigate between the two. You can right click it and select preferences to change how many there are. You can also you Ctrl+Alt+Left(or right) to move between them.
