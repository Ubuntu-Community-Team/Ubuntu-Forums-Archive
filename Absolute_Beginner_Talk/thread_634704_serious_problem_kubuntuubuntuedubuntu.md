### Post by ectospasm on 2007-12-08
It sounds like you're having serious hardware problems.  Could be a flaky HDD controller, maybe bad RAM, etc.  I'd suggest running memtest86 for several cycles, then maybe getting a utility CD to check out your HDDs...  [Ultimate Boot CD]("http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/download.html") has a lot of free utilities that you may find useful.
