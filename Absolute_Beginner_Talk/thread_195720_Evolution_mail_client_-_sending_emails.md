### Post by The Cosmic Hobo on 2006-06-13
Some web-based email services have restrictions on what you can do using email client software, if they allow it at all.
Yahoo, for instance, allows POP3 connections to both send and receive, and full details can be found under "Mail Options -> POP Access and Forwarding", including links to the correct settings and a help page.

Which web-based service are you using? This is the first thing everyone's going to ask... probably the second will be what are your Evolution settings? If you can post both of those, I bet it won't take long to get an answer.
