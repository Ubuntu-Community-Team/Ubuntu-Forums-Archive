### Post by nsilva on 2007-12-08
Another way is to reset your gnome settings to the original settings when you installed Ubuntu.

Remove this folders in your home directory. Dont be worry Ubuntu will create them again to the original settings

```
rm -rf  .gconf .gconfd .gnome .gnome2 
```

Read more in
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=89250](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=89250)
