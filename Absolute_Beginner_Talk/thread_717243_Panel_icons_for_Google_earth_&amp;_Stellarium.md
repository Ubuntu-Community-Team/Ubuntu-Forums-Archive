### Post by coolbrook on 2008-03-09
Right click on the icon.
Go to properties and then click on the Gnome Icon.
Go to Browse and then look for the folder where you know the icon is (even if it doesn't show up) and then click OK.

It should refresh and list image files including your icon.  Select it then click OK.
