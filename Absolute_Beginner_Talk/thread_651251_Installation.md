### Post by dstew on 2007-12-28
Usually this means the LiveCD is having hardware incompatibility with your computer. You can use different boot parameters when you start the LiveCD. Often it is the fault of the video system, and you can try the boot parameter **nosplash**. You press F6 I think at the first screen to enter the boot parameters.
