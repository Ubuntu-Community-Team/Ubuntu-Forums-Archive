### Post by Malta paul on 2006-12-30
I 'lost' my trash can some time ago because, I was trigger happy and right clicked the mouse > Remove from Panel instead of Empty Trash!
To restore right click on on the lower panel>add to panel, then drag icon back to lower panel, move to the position you require then right click Lock to Panel. Thats it - hope this helps :D
