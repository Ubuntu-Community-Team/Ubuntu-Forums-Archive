### Post by kebes on 2007-12-17
Yes, you can.

Just don't enter any settings for the capture card, and the rest of MythTV will still work fine. You can put videos into the video folder and put music into the music folder, and MythTV will be able to stream them to any frontend that connects to it (whether on the same computer or another computer on the LAN).

I don't know if the DLink DSM-320 is compatible with MythTV, though.

Good luck!
