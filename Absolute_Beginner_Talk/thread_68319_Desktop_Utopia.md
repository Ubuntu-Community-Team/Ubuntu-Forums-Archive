Some of your minor questions:
utility icons: standard gnome
application icons: garGANTuan or just GANT
widget: gdesklets (most likely)
background image: ... most likely you'll have to ask the author for that one.

Now for your overall question.
Some of these items can be grouped together: icons, window decorations, and controls.  Most people do not release that kind of "meta-theme" however, and instead, choose to release elements... and then people take those elements - tweak them, add other components, and in general, play around until they like what they have.

Mostly, the only time you'll get a "fully customized theme" all packaged together is by a corporation or other large entity.  (Ubuntu ships with it's own "complete" theme.)

So - how to do it without putting it all together yourself - you can ask someone for the finished product that they are showing in a screenshot.  But there isn't an application (that I know of yet) that puts it all together into a package (so sometimes getting a finished theme from someone may be difficult.)

Does that answer your question?
