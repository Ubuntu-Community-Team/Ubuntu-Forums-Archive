Well, one way is to open Synaptic and search using the installed applications filter. When you see the ones with the green check box listed then right click on the one you are interested in, and choose properties to open a tabbed window, The tabbed window has one tab that will help you to see all of the places that the parts of that application were installed.

### Post by Kobalt on 2006-09-08
Hello,

One thing you can also do after installing an app from Synaptic is to restart your Gnome Panel to make the program appear in the menu 
```
killall gnome-panel
```
And if it still does not appear, you can launch it by pressing Alt+F2, then give the name of you program. With this you can make yourslef a launcher by right cliking on your desktop.
