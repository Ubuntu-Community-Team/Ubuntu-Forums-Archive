### Post by Redlance on 2006-09-28
First of I must say as far as Ubuntu goes its a real nice distro.
However it suffers from what most linux distros have in common.
Driver support from manufacturers.
I was left very impress linux has matured a bit since my last foray. (Knoppix) 
Synaptic is nice
user howtos are excellent. actually far better than any I have seen.
Is Ubuntu for me? Not yet but its close. Why is this? Well my Radeon x850 pro modded to 16 pipes hates the ATI drivers.
tried many approaches and hit the virtual wall with it.
Is this the fault of linux or Ubuntu? Of course not.
Has Ubuntu made My linux foray easier? yes. It was all smooth sailing till i tried to get X to use ATI and with 3d support.
will I try again. Of course. Much room needs improvement on manufactures recognizing and SUPPORTing linux. The rest was very impressive. I am very much a linux newbie i know some commands in CLI but not enough to yet to fix a problem in a few minutes. I will try edgy eft when it comes out in final.
