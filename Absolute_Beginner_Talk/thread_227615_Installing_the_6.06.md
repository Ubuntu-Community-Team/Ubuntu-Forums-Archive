### Post by wieman01 on 2006-08-02
ISO is an CD image. Although you can open it with WinZIP this is not the purpose of the installation image. Burn it on to a CD/DVD ("burn ISO function") and boot from it when starting up your computer.

That's it. No unpacking or anything like that. Simple ISO image burning.
