### Post by whoosh on 2006-06-05
I have a problem where the sound randomly works on Ubuntu.  I think it might have to do with the fact that I have two sound devices on my computer (one on my motherboard, and another on pci).   Is there anyway to maybe disable one of the sound devices so I just use one of them?

---

### Post by someusernoob on 2006-06-05
Check in your motherboard's bios if the onboard sound is disabled.

If not, disable it. Then it should automatic pick up the pci sound card.

---

### Post by darkali on 2006-06-05
Try changing the 'default sound card'
To do this go to 'System' > 'Preferences' > 'Sound'
A window will pop, so just change 'Default sound card', for your other sound card. BTW it is a good idea to untick 'Sound for events' here, to make it more compatible.
