Is there a way to BCC someone in Evolution without having them in your Address book? Its so frustrating...been searching for a long time...:mad:

---

### Post by useResa on 2007-04-11
Create a new mail message (or pretend to do so).
From the menu click "View"
Now you can mark the fields that you want to see when creating a mail (bottom one is Bcc field).

Hope this is what you meant and that it solves your issue.
