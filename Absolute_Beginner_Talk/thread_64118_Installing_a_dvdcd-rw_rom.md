Welcome to Ubuntu! Just add your DVD/CDRW drive and upon re-start, it will be detected. As for the software that came with the drive you won't need it (coz it will be just drivers and OEM software). You can just install K3B or GnomeBaker for your burning app (you can get it from Synaptic (if your using Gnome) or Kynaptic (if your using the KDE desktop).
