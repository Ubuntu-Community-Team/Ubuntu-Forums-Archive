### Post by izmaelis on 2006-11-17
Click right mouse button on the top panel, select *Add to Panel...*. In *Add to  Panel* window select *Window List* (under *Desktop & Windows*) and click *+Add* button. Repeat these steps for *Show Desktop*, *Wastebasket* and *Workspace Switcher*. After you've finnished click *Close*.
Now click right mouse button on the bottom panel and select *Delete This Panel...*. Drag top panel to the bottom of a screen after the bottom panel is deleted.

### Post by Seq on 2006-11-17
By default, each control on the panel is locked in place to avoid accidental moving. Right click on things you want to move (If it has one, you usually you have to do this on the little gripper found on the left) and uncheck the Lock option. You can then move the control around the panel, or to any other panel, by click-dragging with the middle mouse button.

Also, right-click on the panel and take a few minutes to play around with the available applets in the add-to list. There is quite a lot of extra stuff there, including a condensed menu applet.
