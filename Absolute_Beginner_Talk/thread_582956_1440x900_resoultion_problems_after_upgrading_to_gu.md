### Post by AlCarmon on 2007-10-20
I have nVidia FX5200 card, ASUS vw192T monitor, had same problems.
Before you do anything go to System-Administration- Restricted Driver Manager and check what it says   again    just to be sure.........
Most likely has to do with the Monitor section
See thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=454217](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=454217)
scroll down to **Adding custom modeline**
There are several sites with modline scripts.
Different scripts work differently with monitor/video card combo's.
the sites also sometimes have good info - I think one has blurb on anilog-digital differences

I double checked my monitor specs before entering info, forget which script I ended up with.......
