### Post by neurostu on 2008-03-06
I can answer a couple of your questions:

1 - No you do not need the internet to use ubuntu (not sure what you mean by "efficiently"). Of course if you don't have an internet connection you won't get security updates and such, but you probably won't need them as your computer won't be exposed to the internet. Also if you are going to be completely without an internet connection I would recommend getting a copy of the Repo CD's this way you can install software from the ubuntu repos without an internet connection.

2. for your wireless card look here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=297092](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=297092)
a simple google search for "Dell 1505 wireless ubuntu" yielded this

3. Not sure what to do about your speakers try googling "Dell Vostro 1400 Ubuntu Speakers" or something like that

4. I'm sure there is but I'm not aware of any

5. GTKPod is considered to be the best software for managing your ipod in linux. When it comes to playing mp3s on your vostro there are a lot, check out these posts:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=714998](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=714998)
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=711483](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=711483)

Hope this helps!
