### Post by stefcep on 2008-03-05
I just tried this off Australian PC User magazine cover disc.  The mag created its own lean and fast xubuntu-based distro.  For anyone interested in running linux in 256 meg or less this is great.  Just download libdvdcss and you get vlc configured to play dvd's out of the box, mp3's play out of the box, video editing out of the box.  nvidia drivers already there.  you get web browser, email audio, word processor, spread sheet.  dial up didn't work for me (it never has with all ubuntu-based distro's for me) i needed gnome ppp with its patch but it really flies, fast booting, and no crashes, nice control center.  i added shorewall for firewall.
