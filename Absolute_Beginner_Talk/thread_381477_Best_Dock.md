### Post by igknighted on 2007-03-11
I like kooldock myself.  Kiba does all kinds of weird physics stuff, which while fun, really annoys me.  Kooldock acts much more like the OSX dock, and is the smoothest at zooming that I have found.  Also, a normal gnome panel can be made transparent and also be made to zoom (see dreamlinux for an example of this), but I do not think it can highlight selections and bounce.  Also be aware than many docks (like kiba) require beryl or compiz to be running or else they won't work.
