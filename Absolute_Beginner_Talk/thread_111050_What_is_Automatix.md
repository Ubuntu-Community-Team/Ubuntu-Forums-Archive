### Post by Mustard on 2006-01-01
Its a script that automatically installs a number of popular applications, codecs and such, thus making it easier from some people to get there systems up and running, without having to learn the ins and outs of installing stuff on an ubuntu system.  Personally I prefer doing it the hard way. :)  I learnt a lot more that way, and I can install all parts of my system from scratch, without the need for any 'handy scripts'.

For those that are simply baffled by the linux experience and just want it 'working now' its an option they can use.
