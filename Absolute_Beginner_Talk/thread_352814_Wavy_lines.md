### Post by ed-j on 2007-02-03
Morning!

If the lines are transparent and not solid colour, they are possibly Moire. If you access the screen functions for your monitor (the buttons on the monitor, not buttons in your software) you should find a function to make some alterations to one or two of these Moire Patterns. There should also be a function to Degauss or Demagnetize. If there is, doing this a few times will also help.

You may find that the effects are not the same for different resolutions. Hope this helps.
