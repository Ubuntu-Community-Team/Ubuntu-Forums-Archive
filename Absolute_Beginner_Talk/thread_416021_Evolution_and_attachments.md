### Post by PACSFerret on 2007-08-24
It is indeed a problem many people have and frustrating too.  Save your winmail.dat to a local folder.  Install (in Add/Remove...) package KTnef.  That will appear in your Accessories menu.  Open your winmail.dat with KTnef & you can get your attachments. 

Its a bit of a pain, but it works.

Note If your using Ubuntu rather than Kubuntu the user interface is a little different than normal but it does the job.
