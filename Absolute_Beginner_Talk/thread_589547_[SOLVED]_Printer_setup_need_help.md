### Post by Sef on 2007-10-24
Your Lexmark is likely a paperweight.  

HP's [HPLIP]("http://hplip.sourceforge.net/supported_devices/index.html") works with most of its printers.

Note:  Ubuntu's HPLIP is 2.77; the most current version is 2.79.
