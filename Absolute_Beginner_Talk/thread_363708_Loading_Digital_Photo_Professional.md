### Post by xmastree on 2007-02-21
I can't really help with running the Canon program, but have you tried **dcraw**?

> This utility converts the native (RAW), format of various digital cameras into netpbm portable pixmap (.ppm) image.  Supports the following models: Canon, Kodak, Olympus, Nikon, Fuji, Minolta and Sigma (see [http://www.cybercom.net/~dcoffin/dcraw/](http://www.cybercom.net/~dcoffin/dcraw/) for full list)
Note: This utility does not read directly from the cameras, only the files after they have been downloaded, use gphoto2 for that.
