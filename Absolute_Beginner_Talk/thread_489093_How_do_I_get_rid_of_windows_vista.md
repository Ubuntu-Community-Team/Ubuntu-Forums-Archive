### Post by chrome saint on 2007-07-01
Just to further expound on the above..

1- Boot from the Live CD you made
2- Once at the GNOME Desktop go to **System**>**Administration**>**GNOME Partition Editor**
3- You can see all you HDD space and are free to do what you'd like

Just curious though if you boot from the Live CD and choose the "install" link on the desktop there should be a choice at like step 3 or 4 that says "Guided-Use entire disk" or something to that effect that basically will wipe the selected drive and install GRUB and Ubuntu over whatever is on it.
