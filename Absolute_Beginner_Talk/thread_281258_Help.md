### Post by ReaderRat on 2006-10-20
This is off-topic, but fits...

When you find printouts that you want to Copy, do so by:
1-Put your mouse pointer at the start of what you want to Copy, press the left mouse button, and hold it in.
2-While it is in, move the mouse over the area you want to copy, and the page will turn a different color behind the printing.
3- Stop when you have everything that you want to Copy highlighted and let go of the mouse button. The text should remain highlighted.
4- Go to the Edit menu near the top of the page and click on it.
5-Then click on Copy. This will Copy the text to the computer's memory.
6-Next, go to the place where you want to Copy to and click on the spot where you want the Copy to go.
7-Then go to the Edit menu again and click on Paste. This will "paste' your copy of the text from the computer's memory to the place you want to put it.
8-Easy, isn't it?
