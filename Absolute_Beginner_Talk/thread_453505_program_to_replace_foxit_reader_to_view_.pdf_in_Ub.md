### Post by Skrynesaver on 2007-05-24
Acrobat is available for Linux and should be in the default install. You can also use kpdf and many others, try going to System>Administration>Synaptic and searching for pdf, take a look at the descriptions of the returned applications and install a few you like the sound of, then when you've picked a favorite uninstall the others.
Hope that helps ;)
