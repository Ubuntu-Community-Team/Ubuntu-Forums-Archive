### Post by Paul133 on 2007-08-19
Two options: 

A. Click places and drag the computer icon from the Places icon to the desktop.

B. Alt +F2 and type 'gconf-editor' in the dialog box. Go to Apps -> Nautilus -> Desktop and on the options on the right, check "computer icon visible"
