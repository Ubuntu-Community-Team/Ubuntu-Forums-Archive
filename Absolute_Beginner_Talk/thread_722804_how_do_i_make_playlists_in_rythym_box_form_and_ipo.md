### Post by owbizi on 2008-03-12
- Open the program, look at your left -- no, not *your* left exactly... the *minimenu* of Rhythmbox.
- On the playlists section, right-click on a blank space >> new playlist.
- Choose a name to the playlist.
- Look at your right -- no, not *your* right and blablabla -- and select the songs you want on the playlist.
- After, look at your top -- again, haha... -- and click on edit >> add to playlist >> the name of the playlist.

It's done! :)
