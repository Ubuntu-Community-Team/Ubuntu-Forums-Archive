I'm not sure what you mean. On star what?... what is that?

The error 17 is a GRUB boot loading error. Which means that somewhere your files got changed , moved or removed and grub doesn't recognize the partition you are trying to boot from. 
[B]
You may need to use a live CD[/B] like **Knoppix** or **Mepis** to see the GRUB files in your partition. If you are little shaky about doing that then your only other option is to re-install.

It's happened to me, I opted for a clean install. *(I needed it anyway)*
