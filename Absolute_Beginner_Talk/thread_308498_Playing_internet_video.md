### Post by LLRNR on 2006-11-28
Hi there. You'll have to go through a series of stages to set up some things to run properly. 

There are 2 ways to do this:

1. You need a tool called **Automatix2** that will install for you everything you need and you won't need to bother too much. You can get it from [www.getautomatix.com](www.getautomatix.com)

or

2. To do everything manually, you'll have to go through [this](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Dapper) and [this](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats), mainly.

Good luck!
