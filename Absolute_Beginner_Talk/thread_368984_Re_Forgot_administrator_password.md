### Post by aysiu on 2007-02-24
Reboot into recovery mode (usually the second boot option--right above *memtest*). Then, type ```
passwd udkushwaha
reboot
``` That assumes your username is *udkushwaha*. Substitute in whatever your actual username is, of course.
