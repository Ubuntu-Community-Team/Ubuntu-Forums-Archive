### Post by compmodder26 on 2006-09-29
I have a T60.  I've had Ubuntu on it since May and am quite satisfied.  Everything works fine on it except two things.  Suspend is very buggy. Sometimes it works, sometimes it doesn't (although I haven't tried it in the last 2-3 months, maybe it's been fixed).  The other thing that doesn't work is HDAPS (Hard Drive Active Protection System).  The 2.6.15 kernel series doesn't support HDAPS with the T60.  The 2.6.17 kernel does, but I haven't been ambitious enough to compile it.  Everything else works out of the box or is fairly easy to get going (Fingerprint Reader).
