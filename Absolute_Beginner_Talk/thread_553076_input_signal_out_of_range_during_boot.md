### Post by Shazaam on 2007-09-17
Just a few questions that might help others help you.
First, can you post your xorg.conf for us here? Have you investigated the settings (OSD) of your monitor? What video card are you running and do you have the correct drivers installed for it? "Out of range" usually means that you are trying to use a refresh rate and display mode (1280x1024 or higher) that your monitor or video card can't handle.
Sometimes you can add a vga entry to your kernel line in grub that might help. I don't know the correct entry to use but others might.

### Post by yannoo on 2007-12-25
The specifications in the user manual of the HW173A mention the following specs:
                     H-Frequency 31KHz – 80KHz
Video
                     V-Frequency 55 – 75Hz
Display colors             16.2M Couleurs
Max Resolution                 1440 X 900 @75Hz

This means that it will work by setting your xorg.conf to:

Section "Monitor"
Identifier "Hanns.G HW17"
Option "DPMS"
HorizSync 31-80
VertRefresh 55-75
EndSection

I read from [other posts]("http://forum.ubuntu.org.cn/viewtopic.php?p=313907&sid=f0874db9a8a3c3321b14450a8e89ba22") that you should use something like:

Section "Monitor"
Identifier "Hanns.G HW17"
Displaysize 408 255
HorizSync 30.0-80.0
VertRefresh 55.0-75.0
Option "DPMS"
Option "DDC" "false"
Modeline "1440x900" 106.5 1440 1520 1672 1904 900 903 909 934
EndSection 

but for me it already works with the refresh rates setup as above (athough not as 1440x900 just yet).
