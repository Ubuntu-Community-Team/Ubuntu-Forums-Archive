### Post by MasterOfDisaster on 2007-02-18
If your xorg.conf says that the driver is 'radeon' then that is the open source driver with 3D support (I believe).  While slower that of the ATI proprietory driver (fglrx) it supports more cards.  To install the fglrx (binary blob) driver, look here:
[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Main_Page](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Main_Page)
