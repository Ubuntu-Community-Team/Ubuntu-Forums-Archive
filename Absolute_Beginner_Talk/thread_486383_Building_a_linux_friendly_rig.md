### Post by thornomad on 2007-06-28
I have had a hard time, myself, finding a simple resource that shows what is "linux friendly" in terms of hardware -- but one way to test your current/old graphics card would be to run the LiveCD on your current system and see that it works.  If it did, I don't see why it wouldn't on your nex machine.

From my (limited) experience, most graphics cards work basically -- if they have special restricted/proprietary features they may or may not function.  I have built machines and, so far, haven't worried about hardware compatibility with Linux and have been fine.
