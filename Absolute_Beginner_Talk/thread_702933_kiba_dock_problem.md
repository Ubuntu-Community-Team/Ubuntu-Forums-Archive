### Post by Presto123 on 2008-02-20
Kiba cannot just be dragged to the bottom, you will need to basically "walk" it to the bottom of the screen by dragging it to one side and then down that side and then across to the bottom. Unfortunately, I don't know how else to explain it.

To get the dock to boot at startup, have it running, and then navigate to System/Preferences/Sessions and in the Sessions screen, click on the tab marked "Session Options". Click on the button marked "Save the Current Session" and then restart to check if that worked.
