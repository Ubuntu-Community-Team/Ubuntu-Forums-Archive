### Post by mitch.c on 2006-09-12
The command line in *nix is a shell and is accessible via a terminal. If your using Gnome, click Applications, point to Accessories, and then click Terminal; or to get out of the GUI completely, <Alt><Ctrl><F1> and login.

As for dial-up, someone else will have to help.

Good luck!
