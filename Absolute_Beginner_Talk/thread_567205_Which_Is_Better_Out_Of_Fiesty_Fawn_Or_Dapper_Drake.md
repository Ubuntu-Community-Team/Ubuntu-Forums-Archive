### Post by shad0w_walker on 2007-10-04
Dapper is supported for 5 years after its release, mostly for company reasons (No point in having to upgrade every couple of years when the system works fine)

Feisty has a shorter support cycle (Updates cut off sooner) but it has alot of newer packages and improvements to things (Refined interface, etc) so if you are going to do a system for personal use, not a server then I would go with Feisty.

If you have no rush, wait until the 18th and get Gusty, it has a lot of improvements for the everyday user and the newer kernel will help with improved hardware support. If in doubt, get Feisty and upgrade to Gutsy when it comes out.
