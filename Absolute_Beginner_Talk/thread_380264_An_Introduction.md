### Post by igknighted on 2007-03-09
Well, I learned by hanging around these forums and picking up tips.  Then after a while I'd jump in and help some people, and as time when on I just picked stuff up.  Then when I was moderately comfortable, I started trying other Distro's.  Doing the gentoo install taught me sooooo much, even though I was just following the manual step by step.  I had enough linux experience to understand why I was doing things.  When you get a little more comfortable in Ubuntu, branch out and try a few other distro's, it's a great learning experience.
