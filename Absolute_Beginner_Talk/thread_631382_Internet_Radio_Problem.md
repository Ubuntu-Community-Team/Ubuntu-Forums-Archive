### Post by TekNullOG on 2007-12-04
Internet radio is kinda my specialty. What radio station are you listening to?

It works differently depending on the encoders they use (mp3, ogg, wma, etc...)

Also, are they sending you a direct link or ip to the radio server or is it a .pls or .m3u file?

Personally, my software by choice for listening to radio is BeepMediaPlayer (XMMS). It works with most radio similarly to Winamp.

If you want radio that works out of the box, visit [Techno.FM - Electronic Radio]("http://techno.fm/#channels"). (that is if you like electronic music)
