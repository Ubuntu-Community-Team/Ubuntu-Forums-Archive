### Post by Niniel on 2007-10-03
From my limited experience, I have to say that xUbuntu will probably not be a good idea for your machine, at least the current version 7.04. I tried it on a PII/333 with 384 MB of RAM, and it is very slow, not to mention the desktop didn't load right (no taskbars at the top and bottom). 
On the other hand, I have found that Grafpuppy and Slax run very nicely on my old computer. But I have the machine on a LAN, so I don't know how well they work with (old) modems. Since these are lightweight distributions (~ 100 MB) that may be an issue. But I think it's worth a try.
