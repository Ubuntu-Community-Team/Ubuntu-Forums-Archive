### Post by John.Michael.Kane on 2007-03-15
You can only upgrade to 6.10 "Edgy Eft" from Ubuntu 6.06 ("Dapper Drake")

So you will have to run this in two parts,and it's not guaranteed to work fully so backup you important data.

Step One Upgrading to Dapper:
[**DapperUpgrade**]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DapperUpgrades")

Following that guide,and provided everything goes with out issue.

Step Two Edgy Upgrade guide.
[**EdgyUpgrade**]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EdgyUpgrades")


**Short way backup your data download the edgy iso install it..**
