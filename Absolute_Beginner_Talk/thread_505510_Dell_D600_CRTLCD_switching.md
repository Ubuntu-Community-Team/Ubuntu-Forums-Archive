### Post by BlahMan_of.Doom on 2007-07-20
A laptop usually contains a port on the back (usually VGA only) that can hook up to a monitor. Hook it up to the monitor. The laptop should have a button that has a monitor switch button. For example, it's Fn+F7 on mine.
