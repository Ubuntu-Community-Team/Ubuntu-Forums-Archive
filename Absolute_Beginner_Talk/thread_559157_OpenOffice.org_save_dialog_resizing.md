### Post by kyphi on 2007-09-26
Assuming that you are referring to Open Office Writer, go to Tools -> Options -> Open Office org -> General and place a tick next to 'use OOo dialogs' (that really should read dialogues in English).

Now when you press 'save' or 'save as' you will have a larger screen that you can resize and it will retain that size.
