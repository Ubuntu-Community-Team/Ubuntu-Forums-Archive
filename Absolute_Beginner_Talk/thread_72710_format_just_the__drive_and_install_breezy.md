### Post by nitricacid on 2005-10-07
Goto System settings>BlueTooth
Under the Bluetooth Services>Device Discovery there should be a line of txt that says:
Addistional Search Options for non-discoverable devices
Click that thenclick the ADD Device button.
it should find your fone or a set of number&letters (don't forget to have bluetooth turned on on your phone).
Once it finds your phone you should be able to send items from your phone to your computer.
Just use the Bluetooth OBEX Client to send files to your phone.

Hope this short guide help (whether it be in Hoary or Breezy).
