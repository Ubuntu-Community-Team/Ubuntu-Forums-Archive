### Post by wheredidrealitygo on 2007-09-17
sorry, I keep forgetting it's not included in with compiz fusion in general, it really needs to be.

[this post]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=3163821&postcount=8") has the .deb for the compiz fusion icon, works perfectly.

it'll end up in your 'system tools' section (underneath sound & video) on your menu.

click it, and compiz-fusion should load right up (if it isn't already).

the parts you want are under 'compiz options' when you right click the icon after it's in your system tray.

also integrates with emerald theme manager if you have that installed.
