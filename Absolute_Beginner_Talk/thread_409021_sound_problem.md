### Post by jfinkels on 2007-04-14
Are your speakers plugged in, turned on, and working? Do you have a laptop? Is the GNOME volume control applet working? Is it muted? Double-click on it and change the sliders. Right-click and select preferences and enable all tracks and change all the volumes to see if that has any effect.

### Post by OU812 on 2007-04-14
If you get sound at start-up, then it's probably a settings problem. Launch your audio and video applications and look for the preferences option (usually edit>preferences). Almost always there is an audio tab. Choose the right option(s) and you'll be fine.
