### Post by bulldog on 2006-10-09
Download the themes of your choice and open your thema application located in   Preferences Themes.

Then drop the downloaded themes into your theme-manager and it should work.

They should be listed in your themes-manager.

Or you could choose the install themes button and point out where you have them on your hdd.
