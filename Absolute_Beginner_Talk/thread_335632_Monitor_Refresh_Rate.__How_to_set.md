### Post by alienexplorers on 2007-01-10
Try using the gtf MODELINE creater.  It is located at [http://www.sh.nu/nvidia/gtf.php](http://www.sh.nu/nvidia/gtf.php)
and you just enter your data in where stated and it will create a modeline.

Take the created MODELINE and insert it in your xorg.conf under the monitor section as in the following example.

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier     "Generic Monitor"
    HorizSync       30.0 - 68.0
    VertRefresh     50.0 - 85.0
    Modeline "1024x768_85.00"  94.39  1024 1088 1200 1376  768 769 772 807  -HSync +Vsync
EndSection

The sync rate should then show up as 85hz at 1024*768..
