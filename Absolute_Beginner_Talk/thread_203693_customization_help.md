### Post by Solver on 2006-06-25
First, take a look at [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) . That will give you an ample selection of Themes. Installing Themes is fairly simple. You go to System->Preferences->Theme in Ubuntu, and press Install Theme. Then you just select the theme file you downloaded from a site such as the one above, and the theme is installed and available.
