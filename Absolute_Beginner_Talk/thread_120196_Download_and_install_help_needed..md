Unless he's running the 64-bit distro..... i found that out the hard way....

The simplest way to enable Nvu is to go to **Applications** *(should be on one of the panels, with the Ubuntu symbol beside it)*, then **Add Applications**.  Go to the **Programming** menu, and check **Nvu** *(you may have to click on the "more programs" sub-menu to see Nvu)*.  When you click on **Apply**, it should automatically install it for you.

As for adding it to a panel at the top, open the **Applications** menu again, go to the **Programming** section, and simply click and drag the **Nvu** icon onto the desired panel.

Hope i helped, i'm still a newb myself.
