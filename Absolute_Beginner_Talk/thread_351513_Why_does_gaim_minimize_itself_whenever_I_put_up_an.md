### Post by macogw on 2007-02-07
Because it's an updated version of Gaim.  The Gaim devs probably decided that if you're away, you don't need it up, so they made it minimize.  You can click on the Gaim icon in your notification area to bring it back.

---

### Post by V-600 on 2007-02-07
You may have "iconify on away" checked. Go to tools>plugins and untick it if you don't want gaim to minimize.
