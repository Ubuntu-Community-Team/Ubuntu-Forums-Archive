### Post by taurus on 2007-03-24
When you first turn your laptop on (what manufacture anyway), you will see a logo of your manufacture so you need to press one of those special keys:  F2, Del, or even F12 (it would say it on the screen) to get into the BIOS.  If you still have the manual, it would tell you which key to press.  Otherwise, look it up at the manufacturer's site.
