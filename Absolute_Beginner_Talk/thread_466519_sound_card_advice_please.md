### Post by aeiah on 2007-06-06
try the creative labs one. if its not really ancient it should work. if its soundblaster 16 compatible there shouldnt be a problem.

as for new soundcards, well, [the cheapest one at novatech is linux compatible](http://www.novatech.co.uk/novatech/specpage.html?nov-sc51n&P=1), as are most of the other ones (i think there's problems with the creative X-FI range but then they're probably out of the range of what you want for an old box or two)

if you're on the other side of the pond then newegg.com probably has something low cost that'll suit. but try the old creative first, id be suprised if it doesnt work.
