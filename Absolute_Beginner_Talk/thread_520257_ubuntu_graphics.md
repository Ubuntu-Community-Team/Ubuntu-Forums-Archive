### Post by amazingtaters on 2007-08-08
It would definitely speed you up. You would free up RAM that is being used for graphics at the moment for other things, which will mean a general increase in performance. Don't expect too much though, I mean a 60 dollar card isn't going to move mountains or play a new game at full, balls to the wall settings, but it would help. As far as finding a card, start [[color=red]here[/color]]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsAti") for ATI cards and [[color=red]here[/color]]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsNvidia") for Nvidia, then go to newegg.com or some other retailer to find a card that's supported and in your price range.
