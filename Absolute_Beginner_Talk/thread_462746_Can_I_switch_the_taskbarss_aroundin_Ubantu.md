### Post by pappapump on 2007-06-03
An even easier way is exactly the same as the Windows way.
Click the taskbar and hold down the mouse button.
Then drag the taskbar to the left or right then to the bottom and your'e done.
No muss, no fuss!
To change where your icons are located simply r-click em and uncheck lock to panel, then r-click again and choose move and put the lil buggers wherever you want.
The same options are also available from the applications button.
When you want a shortcut on the desktop just r-click it and choose show on the desktop, or the taskbar.
