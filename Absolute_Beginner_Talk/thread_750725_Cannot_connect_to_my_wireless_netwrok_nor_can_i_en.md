### Post by warbread on 2008-04-10
Welcome to the forums.  In the future, when asking for support help, please supply more detailed information.  What wireless and graphics cards you have, for instance.  You can easily find this information by plugging your laptop model number into Google or, if you can read through some more technical garbage, open up System> Preferences> Hardware Information.  

Also, what is it that your computer isn't doing?  I assume you're using the default network manager.  Is it not showing any wireless networks at all, or is it not letting you choose?  For the graphics problem, have you installed the drivers using the Restricted Driver gui, found in System> Administration?
