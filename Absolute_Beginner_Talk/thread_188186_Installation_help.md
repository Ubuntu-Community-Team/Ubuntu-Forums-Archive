### Post by catlett on 2006-06-03
It's not that obvious. You have to change the boot sequence for your computer from within the bios menu. The bios is accessed from the splash page with the image of your computer maker's logo on it. It usually says to press F10 or F8 for setup or for menu. Sometimes it doesn't say anything and you have to try all the F buttons until you get it.
When you get into bios it will be a text based menu. Use the arrow keys to navigate to "boot" or "boot sequence" something like that. Not all computers are the same.
What you want is to make sure the cd drive is before the hard drive in the boot sequence. If the cd is after the HD then your computer will boot into the HD and windows will start. If cd rom is first then the computer goes to the cd drive and the dapper live installation will start.
