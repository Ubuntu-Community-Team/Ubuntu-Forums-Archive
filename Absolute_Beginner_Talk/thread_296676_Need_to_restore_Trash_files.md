### Post by sideshowb0b on 2006-12-02
Ahh - Amarok comes to the rescue!

All i had to do was move all the deleted files out of the trash and let amarok add them to its collection. As long as the files have tags, then amarok will sort them in the collection for you. As if this wasn't helpful enough, you can then get amarok to move the deleted files into directories based on their tags - leaving you will a very organised file structure. Nice!
