### Post by rharris on 2007-04-01
VLC has the items you mentioned. Click settings then select the extended GUI. There you will find three tabs named Video, Equalizer and Audio. Tweak to your hearts content!

Also located under settings is the preferences selection. There you may tweak VLC even further. One of the first selections that comes up is the default volume for VLC.
