### Post by Tux Aubrey on 2007-01-18
The official answer, I'm guessing, would be wait.  Neither Compiz nor Beryl are beyond the early testing stages and many people do have bad experiences - to be fair, I think Beryl is usually considered less stable (but more ambitious) than compiz.

On the other hand, if you are willing to take that risk, are prepared to fix things that don't work for you or if you have a machine that is not critical to your life/work, you might have some fun.

I have installed Beryl (on a version of edgy I am willing to break) and have not had many problems at all (nVidia card and driver and 1 Gb Ram).  

Sorry if this sounds like a bet each way, but whether to install beta software is really a personal decision based on your particular level of knowledge and willingness to test it.
