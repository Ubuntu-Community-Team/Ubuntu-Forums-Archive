### Post by w4ett on 2007-12-29
To give a simple answer, No,  Mfgs do not normally list Linux compatibility on the box.  One of the best sources for up to date info is maintained by the Linux Foundation at [www.openprinting.org](www.openprinting.org)  For Example, your present printer is listed here:

[http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x5250](http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x5250)

Also there is an ongoing project to build drivers for the all in one printers (HP is probably best supported in Linux, btw) so a little research goes a long way when deciding on the right printer to purchase/use.

Good Luck!
