### Post by sargeras on 2007-04-04
When you get to the select model, choose canon in the left window, then BJC-1000 in the right window.  When it asks you for driver selection, I would probably use the bottom one (Foomatic + bjc-600).  The bottom one is for black and white only.  If the second one is causing problems try using the second one.  After you click next, you get a chance to test the printer.  Hopefully this helps.
