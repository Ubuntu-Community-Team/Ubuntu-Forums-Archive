### Post by ginkhao on 2006-12-26
You are in the right place, just go a bit further:

Go to ubuntu.com
-> Click **download**
-> Choose your version (6.10 or 6.06LTS)
-> Choose your location
-> Choose **Other installation options**
-> Choose **Server install CD**
