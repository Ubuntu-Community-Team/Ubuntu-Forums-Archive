### Post by Nythain on 2007-07-15
first thing i found myself doing was famliarizing myself with the directory structure of linux and some of the more important files such as
/etc/X11/xorg.conf - thats your main x file, responsible for a bunch of stuff, usually drivers
/etc/apt/sources.list - thats your apt source list, anytime you want to add or remove a repo, they are stored in there

then you could google xfce look, i think the site will be called [www.xfce-look.org](www.xfce-look.org) (dont quote me on it, but most de's and wm's have a -look site)

you could also just start reading random threads on this forum, most of my linux adventures have been inspired by randomly stumbling upon something i read here

any other questions we are more than willing help otu with
