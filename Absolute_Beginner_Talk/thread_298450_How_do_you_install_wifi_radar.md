### Post by Marsolin on 2006-11-12
[WiFi Radar]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/wifi-radar") is available in the universe section of the repositories. You can enable that section through [Synaptic]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/synaptic"). After you do that and click reload, you can search for wifi-radar to find the package.
