### Post by Daveth on 2007-09-15
that looks as if your repositories are not ticked or selected. Go to system/admin/software sources, enter your password when prompted, and look in your sources, especially under 3rd party tab- you will probably see something like Wine. You need to check those boxes so the servers can be connected.
