### Post by mcduck on 2007-09-02
Of course. Just right-click in any empty space in your panel or desktop, create a new launcher, use the command you want to run and fill the other fields as you wish. Or right-click the Applications-menu and select 'Edit Menus' to open the menu editor so you can add your program into Gnome's menu.

If you use Compiz all the time you could also just add it into your session startup programs, go to System/Preferences/Sessions, and on the Startup Programs-tab click the "New"-button.
