### Post by LaRoza on 2007-04-30
It would be a simple matter of copying and pasting the cookie files, which are plain text; however, the site most likely used session variable and session cookies.

If you want to preserve setting, use a portable browser, like me. 

For windows, [http://www.portableapps.com](http://www.portableapps.com) has all you need.

For linux, try installing Firefox to  a flash drive. Firefox is portable, it is just not advertised.

(A cookie has a: name, value, and expiration time, a session variable can reside in a session cookie or on the server, a session is closed when the browser is closed, and are not reusable)
