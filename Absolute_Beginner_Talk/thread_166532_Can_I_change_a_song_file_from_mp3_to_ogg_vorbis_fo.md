### Post by chrishack14 on 2006-04-26
This has always been sort of a pain in my opinion, as there are several programs out there that will do things like this, but I have had trouble getting most of them to work properly.  PAC (for Perl Audio Converter) is very versatile and can convert between a lot of different formats, though.  It's a little awkward if you want to convert a ton of songs all at once, but other than that it works pretty well for me.  You can download the script from Sourceforge, but you'll also need to make sure you have the appropriate libraries installed for encoding/decoding mp3's and ogg vorbis (which are available through apt).  If anybody knows of better tools, though, I would love to know about them.
