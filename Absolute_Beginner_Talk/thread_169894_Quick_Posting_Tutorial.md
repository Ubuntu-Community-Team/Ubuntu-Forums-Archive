### Post by cgjones on 2006-05-03
If you go to User CP, then Edit Options, down toward the bottom you can select what type of editing controls you will see when you post. Then when you are posting click Go Advanced. This will bring up a new page with more options. Click on the pound sign (#) for code, or the little dialog balloon for quote.

Or, it looks like you can just click Go Advanced, and skip all the other stuff.
