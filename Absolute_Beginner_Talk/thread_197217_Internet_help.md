### Post by PhoenixP3K on 2006-06-15
When I wireless connection just stops working it might be that you are asking for a too high number of simultaneous connections. With my D-Link I had to configure my BitTorrent client to make sure it did not make too many connections. If it's your neighbors and he is doing heavy Internet usage you might be asking too much from his router.

As for reconnection to the network it might be that the router is restarting or you could try to enable it from the Terminal, lookup in Ubuntu Help for network, there should be a walk through there.
