### Post by bored2k on 2005-05-13
Indeed you will learn. Those messages will probably get hidden by a cute image in one of the future Ubuntu releases, but for now, get used to it, it is very informative :D. 

If you want to read more slowly what It said, right click on your desktop and select open terminal, and type
```
dmesg |more
```That will display it again slowly. YOu need to press space for it to continue scrolling if you do ;).

BTW, you could jump start your terminal window use reading a little message I posted [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=169881&postcount=20](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=169881&postcount=20) . It may be confusing at first, but eventually you will learn how to use most of them and will use them regularly [because its faster than doing things graphically].
