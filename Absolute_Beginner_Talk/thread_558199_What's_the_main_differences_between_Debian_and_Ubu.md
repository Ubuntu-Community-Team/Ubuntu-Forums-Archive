### Post by AndyCooll on 2007-09-23
You don't need all 5 DVD's,  four of them will just be additional software and the different architectures. As mentioned above the netinstall CD will be enough.

Essentially Debian is the parent, and all other distros which use Debian as the base (including Ubuntu) are modifications.

Ubuntu takes a snapshot of the Debian unstable branch at a particular point, modifies it and adds a bit of extra polish (e.g. the restricted drivers app). So when you have a look at Debian you'll see plenty of familiarities.
