### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-07-24
Hi BKK,

WiFi Radar won't work for a wired connection.  Good news, though - there are built-in tools in Ubuntu to set your IP and such.

Go to System > Administration > Networking.  This will bring up a window showing your network devices.  Click on Eth0 (Ethernet connection), and click "Configure".  There is a tab where you can manually enter the IP address (as you need at school).
