### Post by schwascore on 2007-03-25
To reduce an image in GIMP, do the following:

On the menu bar above the image, click on Image --> Scale Image

In the menu that opens up, select the desired width/height and hit the Scale button.

### Post by schwascore on 2007-03-26
Try increasing the scan resolution.  For example, my LIDE 60 defaults at 300 dpi, but I can increase it much higher than that.  That should result in a larger (size and pixel dimension) image.

Hope that helps!
