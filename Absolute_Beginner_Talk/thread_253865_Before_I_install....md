### Post by YeahBuntu on 2006-09-09
You need to go into your CMOS and change the boot order.

This is a pretty simple task.

When your computer boots up it normally has a splash window screen saying something like 

AMI BIOS 







press F2 to run setup



Sometimes the command to get into setup is not F2... we'd need to know more about your computer.

Bascially you are going to tell your computer to attempt to boot from the CDROM first .. and if that fails .. THEN boot from your hard drive .. or other device .

### Post by Cynical on 2006-09-10
