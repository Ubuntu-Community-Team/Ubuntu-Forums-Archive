### Post by Grey Box on 2007-11-28
This could turn out to be a bit of a complicated problem for you, I'm afraid. What you describe usually describes a failed suspend. There is something running on your laptop (often some piece of hardware, actually) that refuses to let you go into suspend mode. As a result the system wakes up (it fails safely, so be thankful for that!).

What I would do is go to linuxlaptops.org or some other such website and see if there is a HOWTO for your model laptop and how others overcame your problem for your specific laptop model. Actually more specifically, the graphics card your laptop has on it is most likely the problem. You might need to get the proprietary drivers for your laptop if it has, for example, an nVIDIA or some kind of fancy intel accelerated graphics card etc.

Otherwise if you can gather some error messages and so forth that would be a great help.
