Hmm not sure if you mean a full backup, or just all your local settings. If the latter, go to your home folder in nautilus. Press ctrl + h (also under the view menu) to show hidden files. Folders beginning with "." are hidden, and hold your personal settings. Like .mozilla, holds your firefox settings...

My favorite option is keeping /home on a different partition, so even on a fresh install, just install your apps, and everything is setup as you like...

With that, search around the forums to see various backup methods... There are different tools, or some simple commands (like backing up the home folder). :)

Hope this helps you find the best solution for you...
