### Post by RedRat on 2008-04-19
Hey I too have connected by older Zen and it can be done. I wrote Tech support at Creative about this and here is what they replied:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Dear Customer,
In most cases, not direct support for Linux.
But we try to keep some links to sites that may get you what you need.

SEE: [http://connect.creativelabs.com/opensource/Wiki/Media%20Player%20Support.aspx](http://connect.creativelabs.com/opensource/Wiki/Media%20Player%20Support.aspx)

As for Linux and the Zen Vision:W, this is an MTP device, and each OS can make MTP support.

I suggest the Gnomad2 Project
[http://gnomad2.sourceforge.net/](http://gnomad2.sourceforge.net/)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

What is at play here is that the in actuality, the file system they use is really a linux file system.  Check the above url for more info. I got mine working and I can move files to and from my Zen.
