### Post by houstonbofh on 2007-04-07
To test sound, use a known sound file.  Click on Places --> Home Folder.  Go into 'Examples' and click on 'ubuntu Sax.ogg' and see how it sounds.  (Or you can just hover the mouse over it without clicking.)

Note that Ubuntu will update drivers allong with all other software if you stay in the repositories.
