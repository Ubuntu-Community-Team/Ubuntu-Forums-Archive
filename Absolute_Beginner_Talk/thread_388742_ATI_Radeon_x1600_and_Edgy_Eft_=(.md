To enable dual monitor  the option to add are :

in device section

        Option          "DesktopSetup"  "horizontal"
         or
        Option          "DesktopSetup"  "horizontal,reverse"
        and
        Option          "ForceMonitors" "tmds1,tmds2,notv"   # TMDS and NOT CRT
