### Post by skewer on 2007-07-08
I installed the ubuntustudio files on top of a standard Feisty 7.04 installation and I found that the login screen's shutdown button caused the system to hang. I am not sure if I got round it with Ctrl-Alt-Backspace or a hard reboot, but after removing the files it worked fine. 

I can't say if it would be the same on a fresh ubuntustudio install, or on someone else's machine, but since you can get the software by adding repositories anyway I wouldn't recommend ubuntustudio to (m)any individuals who already use (k)Ubuntu. 

Multiple installs in a production environment or a way to entice a multimedia enthusiast into Linux would make sense, but I was seduced by the slick black appearance and found it a little buggy...
