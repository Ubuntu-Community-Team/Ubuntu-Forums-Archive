### Post by ron999 on 2006-11-18
Hi Felix Jay
Right click on a torrent link that you've saved.
Then select "Open with other application".
Then select Azureus for this and other Bit Torrent seed files.
Next time when you click on a link it will open with Azureus by default.:cool:
