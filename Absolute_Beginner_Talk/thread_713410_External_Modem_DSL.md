### Post by spiderbatdad on 2008-03-02
It's likely to take a little work, at least. I would recommend finding out if the company supports linux at all. You're likely to have an easier time of it with a usb modem than with an internal, so that's a plus.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UsbAdslModem](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UsbAdslModem)

[http://www.linux-usb.org/USB-guide/x332.html](http://www.linux-usb.org/USB-guide/x332.html)

I would also recommend trying a frontend like kppp or gnome-ppp if you're not well versed at manual configuration of devices.
