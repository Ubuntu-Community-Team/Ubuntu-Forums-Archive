### Post by livesys on 2007-04-29
Ubuntu comes with xorg 7.2 which should work with the ATI Radeon X800.  You don't have to use ATI fglrx.  You have to  [specify the correct timing]("http://xtiming.sourceforge.net/cgi-bin/xtiming.pl")
for your ViewSonic VX2025wm wide-screen LCD.

You can try these for your xorg to see if it work:

```


Section "Device"
	Identifier	"ATI Technologies, Inc. R420 JK [Radeon X800]"
	Driver		"radeon"
	BusID		"PCI:1:0:0"
EndSection

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"ViewSonic VX2025wm"
	HorizSync       30-82
	VertRefresh	50-75
	Option		"DPMS"

Modeline "1680x1050@60" 154.20 1680 1712 2296 2328 1050 1071 1081 1103
Modeline "1680x1050@75" 210.42 1680 1712 2504 2536 1050 1070 1083 1103
EndSection

Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"ViewSonic VX2025wm"
	Device		"ATI Technologies, Inc. R420 JK [Radeon X800]"
	Monitor		"ViewSonic VX2025wm"
	DefaultDepth	24

	SubSection "ViewSonic VX2025wm"
		Depth		16
		Modes		"1680x1050"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "ViewSonic VX2025wm"
		Depth		24
		Modes		"1680x1050"
	EndSubSection
EndSection



```
