### Post by Chayak on 2007-11-15
The XO is an interesting little machine thought with only 1gb of nand memory and 256mb of ram it isn't going to be a general use laptop for you.  It is pretty specialized towards it's designed purpose and while the ones you will get for the US will be unlocked so you can install whatever you want on them keep in mind that you'll still only have 1gb of memory on it.  You can use an external drive with it.  They are neat to play with and over open ground the wifi has a range of about 2km and the mesh networking is very cool as well as what they've done with the harware itself.  The laptop will literally sleep between keystrokes riding that line of human perception and uses a framebuffer to keep an image on the screen so you don't even realize it is.  A cool piece of engineering that other laptop designers could take some lessons from.

If you want a project to play with get one but it's not going to be a good alternative for P2P, movies, and music due to the lack of storage and if you did use an external all the time it kind of defeats the purpose.

I have one to test to try out various methods of powering it in the field.  I've been using a PowerFilm foldable solar panel and while more expensive than the laptop it gets the job done.  I have a 10w and a 5w rollable panel on order to try out.
