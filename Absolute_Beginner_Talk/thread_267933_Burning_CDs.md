### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-09-29
I would say the easiest is nautilus, the file manager. Click Go->CD/DVD Creator. Drag files in there from another nautilus window and click Burn.

I would say that k3b is the most trustworthy. In the past (not Dapper) I have had to use k3b because nautilus and gnomebaker didn't work reliably. k3b also has the most options - more than I can cope with, but the defaults are fine.
