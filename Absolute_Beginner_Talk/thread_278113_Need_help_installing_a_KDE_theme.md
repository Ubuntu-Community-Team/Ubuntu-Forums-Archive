### Post by shoujulei on 2006-10-15
You're in luck; there's a very easy way to do this: [HTML]http://netdragon.sourceforge.net/[/HTML]Superkaramba.  Probably best to install this through apt-get/adept/synaptic (your choice of package manager), then launch superkaramba and find the (now un-tarred/un-gzipped archive)theme and have superkaramba install it.  Be warned, though, that not all themes play nice with the latest iteration of the kernel, as some of them were written two or three years ago or even longer. You can find even more themes at kde-look.org, under the heading karamba (on the left border).  Good luck!
