### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-11-24
If you are happy with your old Ubuntu system then why do you want to upgrade? Usually upgrades can break a system instead of making it better, so if you are happy with your current Ubuntu system then an upgrade is not really necessary. If you aren't happy with your old Ubuntu system and is willing to give the upgrade a try, then you may do so. Otherwise if you don't want to waste time on taking a chance with an upgrade, you can do a clean install of Ubuntu 7.10 which will have a much less chance of having problems than an upgraded 7.10 system.

On the methods concerning upgrading or a clean install, **make sure that you backup all you important data before doing any one of them**.
