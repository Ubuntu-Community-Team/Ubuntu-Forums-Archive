### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-12-27
Hi. Most likely the icon is in Wine's folder, hidden in your home directory; open Nautilus and press CTRL+H to see the hidden files and folders, then go into the **.wine** directory and find where Dreamveaver is installed. The Icon should be there. Then you can use that path to tell Alacarte where the icon is.

EDIT: You can also drag the already existing launcher to the desktop or to either panel.
