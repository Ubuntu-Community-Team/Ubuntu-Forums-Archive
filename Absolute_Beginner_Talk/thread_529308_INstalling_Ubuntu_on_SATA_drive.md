Just for Info. I use both a SATA and an IDE HD without problems. Ubuntu is mounted on my SATA drive. You may have to arrange you BIOS to boot from your SATA drive. Also check to see if the live disk recognizes both drives, if not you may have to set your BIOS 'Integrated Peripherals'
