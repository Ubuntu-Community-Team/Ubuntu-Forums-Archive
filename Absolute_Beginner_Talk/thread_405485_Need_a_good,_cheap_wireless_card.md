### Post by ffxr on 2007-04-09
any card with a ralink chipset is usually recommended... preferably the rt2500 chipset.. i got a edimax EW-7128g  (or something) for 10 british pounds and it works fine.. do a llittle research into what chipset  is on the cheap NIC your thinking of buying.. this should help you become a little bit more familar with 'how things work' and hopefully make you a little more confident when dealing with network hardware drivers.. or any other hardware drivers for that matter..
