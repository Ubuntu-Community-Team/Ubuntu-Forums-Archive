### Post by Afkpuz on 2008-02-26
If your laptop has a vga out, then there is probbaly an option somewhere to change outputs.  First, check your function keys.  Most, if not all lappys have a function key.  Look up at your f1 keys and numbers.  Do you see anything that resembles a monitor?  On my lappy, I have to press Fn+f4 to change to the vga output.  Also, there is a setting in my bios.  It doesnt give me the option to turn off the lcd per se, but it changes the output.  My choices are LCD, CRT, LCD+CRT.  See if either of those options is present on your laptop.
