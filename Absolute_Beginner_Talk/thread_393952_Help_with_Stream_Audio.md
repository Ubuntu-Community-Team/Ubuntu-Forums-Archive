### Post by chocbar31 on 2007-03-26
Try Songbird.

[http://www.songbirdnest.com/]("http://www.songbirdnest.com/")

Plus, when you can add this link [http://www.g2p.org/](http://www.g2p.org/) into Songbirds address bar. When you perform a search, Songbird will extract all audio and video from webpages  and create a list for you to either listen, bookmark, or save the media to your computer.

And you can add the plugins for ITunes and other apps.

This is by far my favorite!
