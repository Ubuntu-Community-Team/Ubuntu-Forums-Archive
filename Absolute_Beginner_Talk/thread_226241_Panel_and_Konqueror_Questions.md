### Post by croak77 on 2006-07-31
Right Click on the new panel and select 'Add Applet to Panel' then double-click 'Taskbar'.

As for flash, when you select Setting -> Configure Konqueror, scroll down to the bottom on the left panel. There is a section call 'Plugins'. Hit the 'Scan for New Pluins' button and the check the 'Plugins' tab to see if it is listed.
