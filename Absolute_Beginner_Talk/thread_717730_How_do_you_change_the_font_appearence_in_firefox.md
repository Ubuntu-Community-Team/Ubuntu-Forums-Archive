### Post by Hospadar on 2008-03-07
you can change the default font in Edit->Preferences->Options also you may be zoomed in or out too much, try doing Ctrl - (the minus key) or Ctrl + (the +/= key) to change the page size.

Barring that, are you sure your monitor resolution is set correctly? also if you are connected to an LCD monitor with a vga connector you may need to do an auto-adjust on the monitor.

### Post by herbster on 2008-03-07
Yep, check the Font preferences in FF, and if some tinkering doesn't seem to do it, hit System > Preferences > Fonts. If you have an LCD, you can choose the appropriate setting.
