### Post by ubuntuuser on 2006-03-17
I haven't yet found THE book on Linux for all skill levels.
Most linux books I have are in German, and the few English books I have are quite specific, but I like the O'Reilly books a lot, so I recommend checking their site. A very good, yet specific book is "Practical Unix & Internet Security" by Simson Garfinkel & Gene Spafford, published by, you guess, O'Reilly. 

You could also have a look [here]("http://www-128.ibm.com/developerworks/linux/lpi/index.html"). These are tutorials from IBM.

I also have some books published by No Starch Press and like them a lot, so I recommend checking their site, too.

Good luck.
