### Post by xmastree on 2005-09-02
Right click on the panel, 'add to panel' 
Select 'Custom Application Launcher'

Name, Opera's probably a good choice
Generic name leave blank
Comment, whatever you like. That's the one which shows when you hover over it
Command: opera
Icon: see if there's an oera icon somewhere and browse to it.

That ought to do it.

### Post by xmastree on 2005-09-02
Smeg is for editing the menu which shows up when you click Applications. That's slightly different from putting a launcher on a panel.

Think of the panel as Windows' quick launch thing on the taskbar, and the menu is, er, the menu.  :)
