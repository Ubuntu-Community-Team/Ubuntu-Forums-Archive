### Post by techno-mole on 2007-07-19
sorry for not getting back sooner.
the difference is the basically the way the card slots into the motherboard, and then theres the difference in terms of performance, pci express graphics are the newest way of doing things, and therefore you get better graphics power with pci express.
to tell if your motherboard has an agp or pci express should be easy enough, did you buy you pc pre-built ? if so do you have any paperwork for it ? manuals and the like, these should tell you what sort of graphics card you have.
if you know the make and model of the motherboard (ecs.msi etc etc) you can look on the website of the company who made the board and get a set of specs.
and you can always open up the case and have alook to see what type of interface there is.
cheers.
