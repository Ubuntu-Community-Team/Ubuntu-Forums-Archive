### Post by mcduck on 2006-11-27
To create a desktop launcher right-click on desktop, select 'create launcher' and now you can enter your command (or click 'browse' to find your program). You can just drag&drop image files to the 'no icon' button, or click it to browse your icon image.

Note, that you can also drag programs from menus to desktop, and between desktop and panels..

### Post by Ecthelion on 2006-11-27
> Once you have a file on the desktop, how do you associate that particular file with a particular icon?

To give an icon to a file you just need to right-click, choose properties, click on the icon left of "name" and select the icon you want it to have.
