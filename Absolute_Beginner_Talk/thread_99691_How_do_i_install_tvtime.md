### Post by cjm5229 on 2005-12-06
Hi,
 I'm New to Linux also, I have found though that if you study these forums a bit, you will get most questions answered. Now, to install Tvtime; throw away your download, (This is Ubuntu not Windows) Go to System/Administration/Synaptic Package Manager. Click on search, enter Tvtime, "Look In" Name. Click search. Mark Tvtime in the window, click "apply". Sit back and enjoy. Believe me once you get the minor issues worked out you are going to really like this. I have had some hardware issues also, but once I solved the problems things started getting much easier. Sometimes you may have to dig a little to find the right apps for the job but you have to do that with Windows also, and here you don't end up downloading viruses and spyware in the process. Keep trying and good luck.
