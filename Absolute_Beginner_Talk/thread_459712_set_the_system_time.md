### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-31
> **leesway said:**
> Ok this should be easy.......how do I set my system time to us central time? I cant find a time setting for the panhandle of Florida. When I manualy set the time it will reset the time one hour ahead.

Uh, right click on the time in the upper right and select "adjust date and time". Time zone option should be right at the top. Then just pick your time zone from it, it doesn't have to be in florida exactly just pick another city in the same time zone. You can of course then just manually configure the time or synchronize with a time server.

Doesn't that work? If it keeps resetting do you have a dual boot with windows, if you do have windows installed on another partition and boot into it, right click on the time and select preferences, then uncheck UTC.

That should be it, enjoy :).
