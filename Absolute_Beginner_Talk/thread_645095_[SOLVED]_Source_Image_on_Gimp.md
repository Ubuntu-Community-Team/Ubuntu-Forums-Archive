### Post by legend2440 on 2007-12-20
> **WarMonkey said:**
> I want to use the clone tool on the Gimp (but use an image I drew). When, under source, I select Image and click on the image that's currently open, it says in the bottom right corner "set a source image first". How do I do that???:confused:

Hold down the control key and click the mouse with the mouse over a section of the pic you want to clone.
Release the mouse then press mouse again without pressing ctrl this time, and start brushing area of pic you want to fill
