### Post by mostwanted on 2006-07-24
Go to Synaptic, search for the apps you just installed, righ-click and view their properties, check the tab that says "installed files" or similar, look for files in the /usr/bin directory.

Aas far as I know, menu-xdg is the Debian menu. That thing has always been mysterious to me, never know when that thing shows up.
