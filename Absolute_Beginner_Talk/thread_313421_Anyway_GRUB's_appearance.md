### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-12-06
You can try your hand at [GfxBoot]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=208855&highlight=gfxboot") or you can add a splash image to your GRUB like this: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=228104](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=228104) or this: type in **grub image** in the search box at the top right of the page, here on the forums.  There are other ways to do this as well (get an image working in GRUB...some are modifications, some are replacements.  In the end, it depends on how much you want to work at it :-)
