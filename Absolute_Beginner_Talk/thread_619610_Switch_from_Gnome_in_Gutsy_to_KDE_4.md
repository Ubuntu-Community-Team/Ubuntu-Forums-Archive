Listen (or read XD) my advice:**Stay away from kubuntu KDE 4 packages, they suck.**

KDE 4 isn't out, RC 1 has been released, however, you should wait until KDE 4.0, the shiny RC 1 doesn't have dialogs for setting up trivial things (but they will be there on 4.0, don't worry ;) ), Plasma is still work in progress, it's the only part of KDE 4.0 that hasn't been freeze, so it'll change eventually.
