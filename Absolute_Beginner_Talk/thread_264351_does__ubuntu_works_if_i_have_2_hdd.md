### Post by æþeling on 2006-09-24
Ubuntu works completely fine if you have another OS installed next to it. You'll just have to select which OS you want to boot, when you turn your PC on.

Oh, and you can't install Ubuntu on fat32, you'll need to re-format that disk with another filesystem (preferably ext3, but you can still keep a smaller fat32 partition on it to share files between windows and Ubuntu).
