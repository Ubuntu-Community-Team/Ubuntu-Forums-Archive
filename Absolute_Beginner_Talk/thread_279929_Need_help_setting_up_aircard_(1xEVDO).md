I am attempting to put together a howto for UMTS Sierra Wireless Air Cards on the wiki with information from various sources. See:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AirCard8X0](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AirCard8X0)

Unfortunately lots of manual intervention is required to configure these cards. Your best bet is to formally complain about official support for Linux directly to your provider and to the card manufacturer. If enough people do that, they will probably provide official .deb packages for Ubuntu and other packages for other distributions or better yet, have them commercially packaged and offered by Canonical or else.
