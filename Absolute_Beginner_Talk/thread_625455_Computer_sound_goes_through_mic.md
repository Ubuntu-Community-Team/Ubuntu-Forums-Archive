### Post by riven0 on 2007-11-28
Double click on the sound icon on the right-hand side of top bar of the desktop. Make sure there's an X mark on the mic section. If it still doesn't work, go to Edit > Preferences. Check mark the **Microphone**, **Microphone Capture**, **Mic Boost** and **Mic Select** then click OK. 

You should see some extra tabs on the volume control now, one labeled **Recording** and the other labeled **Switches**. Check both and make sure the mic is not on. It should work.
