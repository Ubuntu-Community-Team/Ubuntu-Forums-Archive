Sounds like your minitor needs the knobs adjusted.  The view area being diisplayed by the tubes hits the glass in different places based on refesh frequency, color depth and resolution.  Every different combination will hit the monitor at a little but different spot, sometimes a radically different spot.

Find a resolution you like, and then adjust the monitor settings with the knobs or buttons on the front.  (This sort of thing happens regardless of what OS you are running.)
