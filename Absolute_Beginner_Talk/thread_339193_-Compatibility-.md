### Post by bobbob94 on 2007-01-15
You'll be lucky to get every little thing working on a laptop (because manufacturers often don't make linux drivers for their hardware or provide information for the community to do so)- to be honest if the only thing that's not working straight off is the card reader you should consider that a very good result! It might be possible to get the card reader working with a bit of tweaking, but you'd have to search the forums for help with that. My laptop's card reader doesn't work either but I never bothered checking out how to fix it as I have no use for it. Which isn't much help to you, but I'm sure someone can be more useful!
