### Post by Max Luebbe on 2007-11-02
If you are on a laptop, why not just shut the lid?
Almost every laptop in the world has a hardware switch that turns the screen off when it is shut.

Tweak the settings in Preferences->Power Management to ensure your system does what you want it to when you close the lid, and you should be good.
