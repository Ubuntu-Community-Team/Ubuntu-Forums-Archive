### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2006-10-29
Sure, just create a username for yourself, and make it and give it adminstrative privileges.  Then, from that new username account, delete your friends account, and also delete any other accounts you wish to get rid of.  

You can do all of this via Users and Groups (System > Adminstration > Users and Groups).  Don't try changing names of any users.  Just do as I said by creating a new account.  It's not like your friend's personal files will have any meaning to you anyway.

Deleting his account should get rid of his home directory as well.  Get back to us on the results.
