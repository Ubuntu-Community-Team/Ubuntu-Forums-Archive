### Post by amsterdam on 2007-06-20
is there some way to use this camera with ubuntu?

Probably, try looking through the camera menus for 'usb mode' or something to that effect. Tyr setting it to MSC mode if possible.

Can I save, and put my photos on disk so that a windows based program can read/ use it? 

Are you asking how do you get your photos from linux to windows? Or once your photos are on linux are they readable by windows? For the former, you need to check out 'samba' (search the forum, there are great HOW-TOs). For the ladder, yes. 

what is the best photo editor available thrue Ubuntu?

Take a look at GIMP (again, search). It is very powerful.

Does HP in any way supply programs to linux for this product the way they support it for Windows?

I doubt it. However, anything the software from HP can do there is most likely a linux alternative that is just, and often times better than the manufacturer. 

Hopefully this will help.
