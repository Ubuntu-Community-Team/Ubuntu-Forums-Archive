### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-12-13
There is also a more developed Flash called [Gnash]("http://www.gnu.org/software/gnash/") which is open-source when compared to Adobe's Flash which is proprietary.

You can also install it automatically on Firefox as an alternative to Adobe Flash whenever you visit a site that contains flash content.
