### Post by eentonig on 2007-05-26
I personally don't recommend using automatix.

I does indeed help with automating a lot of basic stuff. But as a new-comer, I would recommend you to follow the howto's Irish_flu proposed.

- They're dead simple, and you get to be familiar with how Ubuntu allows you to install software and such. You'll need those skills in the future anyway.
- Automatix worked fine for me in the past, but when I wanted to upgrade a few months later, I got screwed because off changed repo's. Took me a while to figure out. Much more then if I altered them myself.
