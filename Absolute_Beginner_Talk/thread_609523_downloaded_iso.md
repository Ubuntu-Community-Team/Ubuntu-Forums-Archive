### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-11-11
One thing about Nero, it is not very reliable, I assure you. 

Once when I wrote a CD before a Windows format(Typical:)) I did not check it to see if the files were written on it(This is a decision I sorely regretted as it contained one of my mom's most important data), and when I put the CD into the  CD-ROM, presto, the files are not there, when I had a look around using a CD recovery software it told me there weren't any files on it. 

I then looked around the internet and found out that Nero has a reputation of saying that the files were burned when in actuality they weren't burned at all. So I would recommend another CD burning software, one (Free) software would be:-

[http://cdburnerxp.se/](http://cdburnerxp.se/)

though I prefer Sonic the best.
