### Post by diggity on 2006-11-12
I recommend buying an external hard drive case and use either an existing hard drive or a new hard drive. My problem with external hard drives is that they become obsolete very quickly because they fill up. When they do you have to buy an entirely new external hard drive. When mine fills up, I simply buy a larger hard drive and replace the one in the case. It will save you money in the long run.
