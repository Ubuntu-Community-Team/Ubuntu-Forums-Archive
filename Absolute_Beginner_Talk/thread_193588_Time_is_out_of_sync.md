### Post by garner_nc on 2006-06-10
You're in South Africa so you're 2 hours ahead of Greenwich England ( or UTC) time.

Right click on the clock at top right, select preferences, and see if the UTC box is checked. If so, uncheck it. This should fix your problem. I had the same issue at UTC -5 hours.
