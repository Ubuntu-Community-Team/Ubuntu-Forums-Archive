### Post by mgranet on 2008-02-24
You should install ScanModem to see what hardware you have before we can help you.

[http://linmodems.technion.ac.il/packages/scanModem.gz](http://linmodems.technion.ac.il/packages/scanModem.gz)

You'll want to extract this to your home directory, then go into a terminal and type:

sudo sh scanModem.sh

Post what it says here. Keep in mind that Winmodems can be VERY tricky to get working. It took me about 6 years to find a solution.
