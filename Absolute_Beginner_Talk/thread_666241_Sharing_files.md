### Post by dustman on 2008-01-13
check this out : [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SettingUpSamba](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SettingUpSamba)

You can see the other computers in your network by opening Nautilus, then click on the button on the left("Toggle between button and text based location bar") and then in the address bar enter : ```
network:///
```
