### Post by Pragmatist on 2006-02-09
For future reference, this is a decent resource for hardware compatibility, although it is sometimes outdated:

[http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/Hardware-HOWTO/](http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/Hardware-HOWTO/)

Also you can google with "hardware compatibility list" or similar keywords.

The good news is that your card is supported.  The bad news is that you need to download and install the driver!  Creative has a linux driver!!!

[http://www.alsa-project.org/alsa-doc/doc-php/template.php?company=Creative+Labs&card=Sound+Blaster+Live+5.1.&chip=emu10k1&module=emu10k1](http://www.alsa-project.org/alsa-doc/doc-php/template.php?company=Creative+Labs&card=Sound+Blaster+Live+5.1.&chip=emu10k1&module=emu10k1)
