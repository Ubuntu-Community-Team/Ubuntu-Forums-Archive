### Post by bbqsandwich on 2008-02-14
For Gmail Notify, all you have to do is download & install it (Applications menu, Add/Remove, search for Gmail Notify), and launch it (Applications, Internet, Gmail Notify). 

The first time you run it a box of settings will pop up. Put in your username and password and leave everything else at the default setting. If you want, you can also tick the box to remember your password. It should  then work fine, resting in your top panel and letting you know when you have new mail. To change your settings, right-click the icon in the panel and choose Configure.

If you use Google chat frequently, I would recommend Pidgin, the IM program. Under your account settings you can tick on New mail notifications and it will tell you when you get new gmail. This is what I use; then it's one program running vs. two.
