### Post by Dynaflow on 2007-12-17
What you probably removed was your Notification Area.  Remove the stuff you tried to re-add from the panel.  Then add Notification Area to the panel (it's in the Utilities section of the Add to Panel menu).  Log out, log back in again, and everything should be how it was.
