### Post by lackofcreativity on 2008-04-07
Look at the sticker on the computer that says "Intel Inside" or AMD. If it's AMD and has a 64 on it, it's 64 bit. If it's Intel and you bought it in the last 2 years or so, it *probably* is. I'm pretty sure all Core 2 Duo and Solo are, but I would check the Intel website.
