### Post by tagra123 on 2007-03-10
I didn't follow the link above but if you download the firefox-2.0.0.2tar.gz file from firefox's site you can extract it into your home directory.

Using nautilus you can open your home directory and then open the firefox-2.0.0.2 directory.

Then right click and run    run-mozilla.sh

if you want an icon on your desktop edit -- create a launcher and enter the path and filename to the run-mozilla.sh
