### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-13
Two things that may be of assistance

1. Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]System>Preferences>Font[/COLOR] and increase the font sizes.

2. Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]System>Preferences>Accessibility[/COLOR] and see if any of the features there are of help.

### Post by anaconda on 2007-08-13
And more importantly go to:
System>Preferences>Screen resolution

and select a smaller resolution from there. And everything will be bigger.
