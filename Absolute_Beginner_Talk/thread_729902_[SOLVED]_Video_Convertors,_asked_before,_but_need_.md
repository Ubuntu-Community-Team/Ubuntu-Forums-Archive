### Post by bumanie on 2008-03-20
Mandvd and devede both have menus where you can increase or reduce the bitrate. The lower the bitrate the smaller the file and also the lower the quality of output. Hope this is what you are looking for.

### Post by sleepingdragon on 2008-03-20
+1 for Avidemux, it allows very fine control of resolution, file size and bitrates. As long as those PDAs can handle .avi or .mpg, then you should be fine. Avidemux can take a little practice, but a bit of effort can lead to excellent results. So far, I've been able to create an .mpg 2hr film with acceptable quality/sound on a PDA (Palm Tungsten) in as little as 240Mb, that's with 320x240 resolution, 15fps and 112Kbps on audio.
