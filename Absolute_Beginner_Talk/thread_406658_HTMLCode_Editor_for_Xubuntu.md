### Post by akao79 on 2007-04-11
I've been using SciTE for the last few months and have enjoyed it.  There's configuration and syntax highlighting for just about every language under the sun, including php.  It's lightweight and i've had no problems with it so far on xubuntu.  

What i would really suggest though is just downloading a few editors through the add/remove prompt.  It is to use and if you have a decent connection it won't take up too much time.  Really the best way to find out which one is best for you is to just try a few out.  That's what lead me to SciTE at least.
