### Post by Gadren on 2005-09-05
I can scroll just fine with the scroll wheel on my mouse (it's an HP mouse, by the way), but I would also like to be able to click down on the wheel and move the mouse the direction I want to scroll (I can do this in Windows).

Is there a way to do this in Ubuntu?

---

### Post by xmastree on 2005-09-05
It can be done in firefox, although it's a function of the browser rather than the OS.

Edit>Preferences>Advanced>Browsing and check 'Use autoscrolling'

Other programs may use the middle button for something else. Nautilus for example uses it similarly to right click dragging in Windows. Drag a file with the wheel (rather awkward I find) to another folder and you get asked whether you want to copy/move/link etc.
