### Post by renzokuken on 2007-08-23
press Alt+F2

 type

```
gconf-editor
``` in the box at the top next to the 3 cogs/gears

press enter, now gconf-editor should show up, in the tree view on the left go apps>nautilus>desktop and do what the info in your pics says
