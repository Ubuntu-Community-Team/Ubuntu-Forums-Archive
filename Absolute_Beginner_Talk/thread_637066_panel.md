### Post by xhie on 2007-12-10
Right click somewhere on an empty area of your top panel and select new panel. That will give you a new blank panel. 

Then Right click on that new panel and select Add to Panel. In the window that opens up go down to the Desktop & Windows section and select Window List and click the add button. That list you speak of will now be in the new panel. From that same window you can add lots of other stuff, set it up however you want and close that window. Move the new panel to where you want by dragging it.

### Post by Flyingjester on 2007-12-10
Right click on an open area of your top panel, choose new panel, now right click on that panel, choose properties, make it's orientation bottom, now you'll have to right click on the panel and do + add to panel to get the applications that were on it back.
