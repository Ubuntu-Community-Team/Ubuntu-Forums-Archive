### Post by RaiSuli on 2007-06-25
Just one: Don't panic. :)

Well, maybe a few more:
- search the forums for answers to common questions.
- if you get stuck, ask. Somebody here will most likely be able to help.
- don't be afraid of the command line. It's your friend... really, it is.
- depending on how much work you do on your computer, it might be a good idea to dual boot Windows and Ubuntu at first (if you're coming from Windows). That way, you can still get your work done, should you run into problems.

Hope that helps.
