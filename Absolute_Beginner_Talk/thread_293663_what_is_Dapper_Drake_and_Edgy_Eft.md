### Post by jeffathehutt on 2006-11-05
Ubuntu releases are scheduled to be 6 months apart, so ideally every 6 months there will be a new stable release.  Each of these releases are supported for 18 months (1.5 years).

LTS releases are made occasionally, probably with 4 non-lts releases in between.  These releases are supported for 3 years (5 years on the server).

Hope that helps to clear it up some.
