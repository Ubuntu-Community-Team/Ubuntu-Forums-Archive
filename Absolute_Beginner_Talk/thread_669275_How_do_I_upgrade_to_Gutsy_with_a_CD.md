### Post by Sef on 2008-01-17
> got my ubuntu cd its in the cd drive how do i go about upgrading to gutsy now?

1) Is the disk Fiesty or Gutsy?

2) If it is Gutsy, you will do a clean install, i.e., reformat your hard drive's root partition.

3) If it is Fiesty, then take out the disk and go to System > Administration > Update Manager.
