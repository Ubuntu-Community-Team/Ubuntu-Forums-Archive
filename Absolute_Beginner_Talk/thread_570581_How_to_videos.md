### Post by jd65pl on 2007-10-08
Hi,

I have been introducing some of my friends to ubuntu, a few are very keen to learn yet do not have much experience with using computers. I have been making screen videos with "istanbul" to show them how to do things in their new OS. 

I am going to start a web page which will provide new linux users videos on how tasks can be performed. If anyone would like a video on how to do something or would like to contribute please let me know.

I have uploaded a few on how to install applications through various methods and how to find the terminal. Remember these videos are intended for those who don't know too much about computers, they may not know how to do something even if you do!

Here is a link [http://jamiedumbill.com/index.php?page=linux_howto_vids](http://jamiedumbill.com/index.php?page=linux_howto_vids)

Thanks

Jamie
