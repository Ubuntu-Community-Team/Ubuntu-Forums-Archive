### Post by LaRoza on 2008-02-19
Opera isn't open source, so you might have little recourse here.

It is the browser I use, and I love it. I do agree that its saving of web pages is less than ideal. I have since learned that wget is much better anyway, and use that instead.

You can block content, right click the page, and go to "block content". You have have it automatically stop scripts under Tools->Quick Preferences

For the transfers, there is a "View" option in the transfers window. (Upper Left), it has the ability to change what happens during downloads. Change it to "Show Transfers in Background".

### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2008-02-19
Go to Tools -> Transfers to open the Transfers window.
Click the view button and select "Show transfers in background when starting download"
or "Never show transfers when starting download".
