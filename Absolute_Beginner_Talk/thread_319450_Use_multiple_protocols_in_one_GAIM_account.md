Sure you can do that.
You only need 2 accounts.

You say you have a jabber account already, so the only thing you have to do is to make a second account for your msn. ( "Accounts > Add / Edit > Add" and then give your msn account info etc)
If you set both accounts on line at the same time then your contacts of jabber an msn will be in your list. Clicking on one to sent a message will make you send with the right account.
