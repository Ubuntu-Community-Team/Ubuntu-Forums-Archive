### Post by Hendrixski on 2007-06-07
oh... I didn't finish reading your question....
yes, it should work on your computer.  It's a modern operating system so it'll run a little slower than let's say a Windows 95 or a Linux from 1995 would run on that computer ... but it will run.

I recommend xubuntu because it uses less resources.  There are also micro-distributions like Puppy Linux, or DSL (damn small linux) that can run on a 500 meg hard-drive with a 386 processor, and they'll run a bunch of new software like abi-word (for word processing)


Hey, if you have more questions then don't be affaid to ask here.  We LOVE Ubuntu, and we're very willing to help people out who are interested in trying it... just like when you read a good book you want to share it... we do too.  Welcome to the community. Also check out #ubuntu on IRC if you want to chat about it with a real person, or look up the Ubuntu LoCo team for your city, or even a LUG (linux users group) in your area.  you'll be glad you did.
