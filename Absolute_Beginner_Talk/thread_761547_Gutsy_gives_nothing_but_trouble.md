### Post by ByteJuggler on 2008-04-21
This does sound like hardware issues tbh.  The fact that it's new kind of strenghtens that suspicion for me.  

Have you tested your ram with the memory tester? (Have you checked that your RAM is actually running with the correct voltage in the BIOS?  Many motherboards will undervolt RAM by default, causing corruption and instability.) Have you done an extended stress test on your CPU?  (E.g. with Orthos) What's the temps like?  In short, have you made sure your system is stable?

Also, you must note down the exact error messages you receive in order for us to try and help you.

Aside: Although quite rare and therefore unlikely, power spikes (sometimes goes together with power outages) can damage hardware.  Do you have a voltage regulator/spike/surge protector for your PC?
