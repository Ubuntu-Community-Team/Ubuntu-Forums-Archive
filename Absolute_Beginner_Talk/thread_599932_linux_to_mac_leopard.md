### Post by It_Was_Luck on 2007-11-01
Okay:

Theme: This can be found in Mac4Lins huge package, there is a theme folder somewhere once you untar it, upload that.

AWN skin: Also in Mac4Lins package you'll fins an AWN skin this can be installed via drag and drop into awns setting manager or by clicking install from the settings manager.

Icons: The icons I have are custom icons... my computer has over 500 mac icons stored and I only use about 10 of them total, oh well its good to have a big selection... Anyway most of the icons I found can be downloaded via >> [www.deviantart.com](www.deviantart.com) << They even have a category dedicated to "DOck Icons" 
Other other icon stuff you can pick a full Leopard icon set from going to your appearance settings, after you downloaded and installed Mac4Lins Leopard theme. 

Fonts; Fonts aren't huge but a Mac's font base is Lucida (I think thats it) if you want to edit your fonts to that go ahead.

Top Bar: I didn't like the transparent look so I made my own "Tiger" version of the bar in gimp. I manged to fund a couple links. Depending on your screens resolution... Just save the image somewhere then find it then drag and drop it onto the bar.
Image for 1024 x 768 >> [http://www.taimila.com/files/osx-top-bar-1024x768.png](http://www.taimila.com/files/osx-top-bar-1024x768.png)
Image for 1280 x 1024 >> [http://www.taimila.com/files/osx-top-bar-1280x1024.png](http://www.taimila.com/files/osx-top-bar-1280x1024.png)
Image for 1366 x 768 >> [http://www.taimila.com/files/osx-top-bar-1366x768.png](http://www.taimila.com/files/osx-top-bar-1366x768.png)

Background: Theres a bunch out there that can just add the mac effect my favorite is the grass one, the one I use is one for a 4:3 moniter, the actual Apple one is for a widescreen. You can just google those and I'm sure you'll find them just like that.


Anyway thats just small, if you need any help/ links just ask.
