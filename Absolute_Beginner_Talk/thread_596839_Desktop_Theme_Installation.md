### Post by pgvoorhees on 2007-10-30
Actually, it's a bunch easier that that. 
Click **System -> Preferences -> Appearance** 
The first tab is the "theme" tab. Just click the "install" button and go to down, you don't even need to extract it.

---

### Post by joeycbulk on 2007-10-30
Go to System > Preferences > Appearance. Drag your <theme>.tar.gz package on the appearance window or click the install button and browse to your package. If your theme does not appear in the Theme choices click the customize button.
