It may not be your speakers, but your sound card.

Are you using onboard sound, or an add-on sound card. If you are using onboard sound, try getting a sound card, then install it as far away from other PCI cards as you can. But if you have a sound card, disable it and try your onboard chip, if you have one.

Check out this article:

[http://www.desktoplinux.com/articles/AT8018846552.html]("http://www.desktoplinux.com/articles/AT8018846552.html")

It's about Mandriva, but the issues are cross-distro.
