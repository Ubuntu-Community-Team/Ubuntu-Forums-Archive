### Post by elvis on 2007-09-29
Transgaming Cedega is a project aimed at Windows refugees wanting to continue playing "Windows-only" games under Linux.

I's based on WINE code, but with a simplified installer, batter management tools, and a focus on games.

Product page:
[http://www.transgaming.com/products/cedega/](http://www.transgaming.com/products/cedega/)

Game Database:
[http://games.cedega.com/gamesdb/](http://games.cedega.com/gamesdb/)

You can download Cedega and compile it manually, but it's highly recommended that most pay the subscription fee which includes a simple installer and support from Transgaming themselves.

When you consider the financial cost of Windows Vista, Cedega makes a very attractive alternative.
