Evolution does suck.  The Kontact suite is pretty good, you might try that.  Its more fully featured than the others (like Outlook).  If you can get by with it Thunderbird is the best mail client.  Not as many features (although with extensions you could probably get almost all outlook functionality), but very easy to use and not very buggy.

I would avoid wine at all costs.  Apps look terrible running through wine (like back in the win95 days).  Plus, the native apps will perform better and be more stable.  If you NEED outlook, check out a virtualization solution, vmware is the most popular.
