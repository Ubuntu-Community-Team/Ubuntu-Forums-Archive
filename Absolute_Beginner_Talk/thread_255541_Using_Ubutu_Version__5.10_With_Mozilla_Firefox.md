### Post by ieee488 on 2006-09-11
Firefox will connect **if** you can log in to your ISP.

I am using Ubuntu 5.10, and I am able to surf the internet just fine with Firefox and with Opera. I am on dial-up.

It isn't the browser you use that matters. What matters is getting your modem detected by Ubuntu and configuration of that modem and password and username for your ISP and all that stuff.

If you search this forum, you'll see plenty of posts about people needing help to get online. It is not always a slamdunk.
