### Post by lamalex on 2007-12-24
**Step 1: **System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts
**Step 2: **scroll down to the "Desktop" section
**Step 3: **4th option down is "Home Folder", click where it says "Disabled", click, and bind to desired keys.

---

### Post by Maxtorm on 2007-12-24
Actually, you can do it without the Configuration Editor. Go to System -> Preferences -> Keyboard Shortcuts. Scroll down to the Desktop section and you'll find Home Folder. Click on it then type your new shortcut.
