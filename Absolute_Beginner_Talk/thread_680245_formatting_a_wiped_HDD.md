### Post by qpieus on 2008-01-27
Yes, the installer will give you the chance to format the drive.

Also, there's really no need to wipe the disk beforehand, since the formatting during installation will, in effect, delete everything on it. Unless you have some super top secret data on there, I wouldn't bother with the wipe.
