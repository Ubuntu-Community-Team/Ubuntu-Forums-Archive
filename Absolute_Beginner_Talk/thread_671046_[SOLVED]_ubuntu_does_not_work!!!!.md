### Post by seanmiller on 2008-01-18
Can you post what version you are using (7.10, presumably? 32 bit?) and the specifications of your machine.  It may be that if the machine is older having to boot into a Live CD (memory-hungry etc.) is ust too much for it and you'd have more success using the alternative installer CD which doesn't try to boot into a Live CD but rather does a text-based install.... there's a checkbox at the bottom of the download page at ([http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)) to select that one instead.

And, of course, as BillDozer pointed out it could be that the CD is duff and that's your issue...
