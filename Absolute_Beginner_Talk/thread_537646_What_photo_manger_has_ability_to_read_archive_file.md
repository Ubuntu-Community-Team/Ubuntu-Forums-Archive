### Post by russell.h on 2007-08-29
Is there a particular reason you can't just un rar your photos? Assuming they are jpegs I wouldn't think you would get any meaningful compression anyway. A lot of comics aren't jpegs (hard lines don't lend themselves well to jpeg compression, its designed for photos which have softer lines), so are stored in lossless archive formats like .rar, which is why more comic viewers can do this than photo programs.
