### Post by Baby Boy on 2007-10-31
1.)Right-click on *Applications* and select *Edit Menus*

2.)Right-click on a category under *Items* and select *Properties*

3.)Click on the icon in *Directory Properties* and enter the path to the folder where your desired icon is

EDIT: Sorry, I misunderstood what you were saying. But maybe you can scale those icons in GIMP once you find them using the method I suggested.
