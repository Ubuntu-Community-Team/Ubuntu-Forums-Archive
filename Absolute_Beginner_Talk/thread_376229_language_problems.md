### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-03-04
To change the system language into English, go to System->Preferences->Language Support and enable/install English and choose it to be default. Keyboard settings are in System->Administration->Keyboard Layout, so enable the ones you need. I hope I got that right, because I dual boot and am writing this from XP.
