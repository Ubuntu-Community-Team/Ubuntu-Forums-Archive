As far as I know you need to use your Launchpad page to change/update your email address.

Go to [http://launchpad.net](http://launchpad.net) - top-right corner there's a Login link. Use the same email address/pw that you use to login to the wiki. You should be taken to your LP user page, and on the righthand side column of that page one of the entries is "Edit Email Addresses". You can add your new address, and set it as your primary/preferred one there.
