### Post by petersjm on 2007-07-01
That will happen when Amarok crashes. It's built-in crash handler detects the crash and tries to send a crash report to the Amarok developers. I'm assuming you don't have KMail or Kontact; Amarok will look for KMail and when it doesn't find it, it spits out that message you got. Click okay and the app closes. There must be a way around it, changing the email handler or something, but I'm not sure how. What you need to determine now is *why* Amarok is crashing.
