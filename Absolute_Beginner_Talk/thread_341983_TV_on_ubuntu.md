### Post by edward4130 on 2007-01-19
Depending on your capture cards, you could be in great shape.  There are quite a few of us on here using Ubuntu and MythTV.  It is not as easy as setting up WinMCE, but in the long run much more featured and cleaner running on slower machines. 

My suggestion, get ahold of a IDE drive that is clean and do a instal of Edgy and folow the packages to support mythtv, this way your original can be put back in to be back where you started.  If it works out and your cards are seen, and other items are comming together.  Buy a big harddrive and strt the official build.

Me... I'm 6.10 edgy and mythtv with a pvr-500 dual encoder card and a PentiumD-at3.2gh . I can be recording two shows at a time, while ripping a DVD, and I still can surf the internet and stuff while all is working.  If I was on MCE it would be jamming up and crash hard!

Good luck, this is a great place to figure out the kinks, it is linux so you have to earn the cool stuff, but it not overly frustrating if you like computer-technical projects.

-Edward
