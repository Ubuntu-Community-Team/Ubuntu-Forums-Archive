### Post by siciliancasanova on 2007-04-19
How much patience do you have?  Backing up your data, installing feisty over edgy and restoring your data is the best way to do it.  

If you have little patience to get to 7.04 but want to risk data loss then just click the "Upgrade to 7.04" button.

As far as hardware goes, that will always be relative but in my situation I had more things compatible when I installed Feisty a month ago than I did when I installed the official release of Edgy.
