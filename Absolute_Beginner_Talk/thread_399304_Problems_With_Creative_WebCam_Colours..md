### Post by cvmostert on 2007-06-24
I also had the same problem but used a filter to correct the colors (swapping red and blue)
do this:
Ctrl+E to view effects, right click and add filter -> Color Correction.

worked for me. It seems that the frame rate is affected a little, but at least the color is corrected.
