### Post by dbott67 on 2006-12-27
1. Right-click on the tool-bar panel & select **+ADD TO PANEL**
2. Scroll down to the **UTILITIES** section
3. Drag & drop the **MENU BAR** icon to the panel.
4. Right-click on the old one & select **REMOVE FROM PANEL**.
5. Right-click on the new one and move it to where the old one was.
