There are NO silly questions except for the ones not asked, and thus un-answered...

I understand the "User CP" here may be a bit confusing at first so I completely understand;)

The display image for your  profile is different from your  "Avatar" which is what is displayed here in the forums...

follow this directive:

user CP>Edit Avatar>(select)Use Custom Avatar

here you can upload or paste a url for the image you wish to be displayed here in ubuntuforums.org

select: "save changes" to set your avatar image.

Have fun and enjoy!
