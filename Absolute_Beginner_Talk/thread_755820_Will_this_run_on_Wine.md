### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-04-15
When you need to see if something will work with Wine, the first thing to do is search the [app database](http://appdb.winehq.org/) over at Wine's web site.  If your app is listed, people will rate how well it works and also post any workarounds they have found to get it to work.

If it's not listed, your best bet is to just try installing it and see.
