### Post by indytim on 2008-01-15
Not knowing what your present h/w configuration is, the probable answer is Yes.  This is especially true for the graphics cards.

My recommendation is, that since you are 75% there in a new pc purchase, hold off until you can bundle your purchase to include a new case, power supply and cpu, etc..  With the items you have already outlined, you will have a new pc.

Lot's of people go the upgrade route only to find out that soon after the upgrade, the remaining components start to die off.  If your existing system is in the 4-5 year old range, you are probably coming up on the life expectancy of things like the power supply etc.

Just a thought for your consideration.

IndyTim
