### Post by mysticrider92 on 2007-11-11
You could leave the resolution at 1280x1024, and set the font size (DPI) larger. To do this, go into System>Preferences>Fonts and click on Details. From here, you can control the rendering and font type. Raise the DPI to somewhere around 100-125, or whatever looks good to you. For the icons, I am not quite sure, but the DPI might help them some.

If you want to use 1024x768, there might be a fix, but I don't know for sure. Monitors will always look much better in their native resolution.

Upgrading to Gutsy might help some, but I can't say as I use the native resolution on my monitor with regular fonts.
