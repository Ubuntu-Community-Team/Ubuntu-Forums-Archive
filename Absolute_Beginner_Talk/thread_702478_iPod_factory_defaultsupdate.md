### Post by mrsteveman1 on 2008-02-20
I have a 3G nano and when i want to restore it i just format its data partition. When it starts back up it reinitializes the filesystem and acts like its brand new.

Be careful though, some ipods keep their firmware on a separate partition that is accessible from the OS, so don't write over that one.
