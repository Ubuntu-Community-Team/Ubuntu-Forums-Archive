### Post by expatCM on 2007-09-11
I have a very different view ......   allow your child total and free access to the Internet.

Period.

However, before doing that run through a scheme of education, tell your child what to do in certain situations, how to recognize inappropriate content, why keeping personal information private is important and how to generally manage the on-line experience.
