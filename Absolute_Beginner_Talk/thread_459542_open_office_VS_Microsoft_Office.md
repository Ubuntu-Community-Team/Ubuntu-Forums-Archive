### Post by EagleRock on 2007-05-30
The two are inter-compatible, as that was what OpenOffice was designed to do.  MS Office is ultimately more powerful for the very high-tech stuff (such as advanced macros, etc.), but for basic needs, the two are interchangeable.  The only difference is the price (obviously) and the fact that OpenOffice does not have a MS Outlook comparable program.  However, that is what Mozilla Thunderbird is for.  :-)
