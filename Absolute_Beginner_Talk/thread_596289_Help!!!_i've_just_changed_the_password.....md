### Post by petit.padavoine on 2007-10-29
Reboot, and at the GRUB boot menu, instead of choosing the default topmost listing, choose the one which says recovery mode. It will automatically log you in as root (full administrator priviliges). At the command prompt, type "passwd [youruser]", without the quotes, replacing [youruser] by your username. Then type in a new password.
