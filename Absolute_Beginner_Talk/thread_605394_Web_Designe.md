### Post by TeaSwigger on 2007-11-07
Agree, try KompoZer. Any web design will take learning, but that's pretty much the easiest route there is in Linux, Mac or Windows.

If you can't get that to work, don't despair. There are other approaches depending on what you are wanting to put up. For instance if it's mosty text pages, I think you can make pages in Zim (a desktop wiki of sorts) and it can export it as web pages for you, which you'd just upload then. Or look into the OpenOffice Suite, which may offer something along the general angle of front page. You know the saying, there are ways and ways. Good luck.
