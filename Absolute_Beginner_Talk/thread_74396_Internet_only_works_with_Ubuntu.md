### Post by shandar on 2005-10-11
I don't think that your problem has anything to do with Ubuntu since you were running the Live CD which does not access the harddrive or windows in any way. Have you checked everything in windows to make sure that, for example, your firewall hasn't started to block all programs? It actually happened to me once, the settings got screwed up for some reason with the result that I couldn't access the internet at all. Took me quite some time to figure out what had happened...

Another thing I came to think of is whether you are using DHCP to configure your network settings? Have you checked your router (if you have any) to see if your computer is identified correctly and given the correct IP?
