### Post by emarkd on 2008-02-12
You may be able to disable your on-board audio in the BIOS, but having two sound cards in Ubuntu is not a problem.  You can select which card to use in the System > Preferences > Sound panel.  My laptop uses both the onboard Intel audio as well as a PCMCIA Sound Blaster Audigy2 card with no problems.
