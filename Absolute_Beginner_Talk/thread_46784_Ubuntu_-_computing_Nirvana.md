### Post by nelso on 2005-07-06
Hi,

Ubuntu user for 48 hours and loving it.

I have toyed with Linux since 1998 (RH5.2) and since then have not found the right distro.

Nearly went SuSE, mostly used RH/FC but I was after something for a server that gave me the consistency and structure of FreeBSD (the ports collection).

After much reading looked at Debian. I was apprehensive as it has the reputation of not being good for beginners. Then I saw apt and was convinced this was for me.

What I needed then was a desktop version of Debian so i could walk away from Windows at home and I think I have finally found it - Ubuntu.

From the moment I saw the brown and gold login screen I was hooked. Couldn't get my Logitech webcam to work so I googled, got this forum, ran an apt-get blah and away it went. Sold!

I am a total convert and will now be moving to Debian GNU/Linux at home for good.

I'm loving it 

Cheers from Oz,

Nelso
