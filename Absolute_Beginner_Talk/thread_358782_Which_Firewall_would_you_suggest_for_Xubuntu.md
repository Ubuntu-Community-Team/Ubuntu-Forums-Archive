### Post by jml on 2007-02-11
If your computer is connected to an "alway on" internet connection like a DSL, or Cable connection, I would also recommend that you purchase and utilize a simple router, like the Linksys BEFSR11 even if you have only one computer.  The router acts as a very basic hardware firewall, showing its IP address to the internet while shielding your computer's IP address.  Any packets attempting to reach your computer that were not actually requested are usually blocked.  Not perfect protection, but pretty good.  Just be sure to change the administrator password as soon as you set it up.  Just my two cents worth.

Joe
