### Post by spiderbatdad on 2008-03-05
This sounds like a problem with your internet service, not a problem with network-manager. If the ready light on your modem is blinking or cuts out...you have lost service, albeit temporarily. Could be bad cable connector, bad splitter, kinked cable...etc. Your modem is a gateway to the internet. It's readiness is determined by it's ability to receive data upstream...if thats fails...the modem is not synced and ready for use.
