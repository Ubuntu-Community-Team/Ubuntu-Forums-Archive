### Post by hw-tph on 2006-08-21
I'll stick my chin out and say that [Quod Libet](http://sacredchao.net/quodlibet) is the best audio player out there. Very configurable and lots of nice plugins (QL Scrobbler is fun, Download Album Art (from Amazon) are fun).

Make sure to update to the latest version though - currently 0.23. Changes happen fast and are often quite big or at least useful.

Håkan
