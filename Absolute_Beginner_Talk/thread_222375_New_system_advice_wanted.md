### Post by herrdoktor330 on 2006-07-24
To throw in my 2 cents:

I'm with Sef. Go with 2 GB of RAM. You won't regret it.

As far as the whole dual core thing goes, from what I understand in a previous post you can take advantage of that with an SMP enabled kernel. As far as the 64 bit instructions go, that's another story.

for your reference, check this thread:
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=214812]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=214812")

But other than that, it looks pretty boss to me. If you want to save a buck or two, try to get your hands on a socket 939 and get regular DDR ram. The performance you get from DDR2 is negligable.
