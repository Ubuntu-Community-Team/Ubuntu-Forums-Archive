### Post by machoo02 on 2007-04-11
Well...the first thing that you could do is to download and use the LiveCD...think of it as a way of "test driving" Ubuntu to see if your hardware is compatible.  If you happen to know more specifics about your hardware (i.e., who makes your wireless card, video card, etc.), you can easliy search the forums for any potential issues.

In general, all of the volume buttons, etc. should work without a hitch, movies and movies will take a little bit of installing codecs but that's no big deal, and wireless (depending on your card manufacturer) should work fine.
