Rneelis - I would assume you're using Firefox...If you want to speed it up you can use the extension called "Fasterfox". On your Firefox Tools menu, click on Extensions and a new window will open and on the bottom right corner of that window will be "Get More Extensions", clicking it will take you to the mozilla site where you will get fasterfox.
