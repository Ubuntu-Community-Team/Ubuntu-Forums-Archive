### Post by Samurai Penguin on 2008-03-16
If you are using 56K and can't connect with a PCI WinModem you have two choices:

- spend days trying to get a Winmodem to work (good learning experience though)
- buy a hardware modem

Hardware modems are getting difficult to find but you just pop one in,
boot your box, enter some info and you're done. The code is:

PPPconfig

and when done execute this code:

pon

in Terminal and if the info you entered was correct (ISP phone #, etc) your box WILL connect to the net
unless there's a hardware incompatibility or something like that.
