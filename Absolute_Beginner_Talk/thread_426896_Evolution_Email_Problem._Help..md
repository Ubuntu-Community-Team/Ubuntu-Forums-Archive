### Post by freebird54 on 2007-04-29
I am not quite sure what you're after here.  If you want a folder, under 'On this computer', to hold all incoming mail from a specific place, just right click on 'On this computer', and New Folder.  CLick on "on this computer' again (to say where to put it, enter a name (GMail?) and click on Create.  You might want to create one for spam (or bulk) or whatever while you're there.

Now, select an exanple mail of the type to put in there, and select Message->Create Rule.  Hopefully sender is the way to distinguish these, just fill in the forms to have them sent to GMail by default.

I HOPE that's what you're tying to do!  :)
