### Post by m9ke on 2008-01-16
A workaround for driver problems might be found in connecting your ethernet port via a cable to some sort of wireless bridge or router.

There was a post from someone who used an inexpensive bridge for this purpose in the forums but I searched like heck for it and  can't find it.

My own experience: I have my Ubuntu box connected to a Linksys Wireless-G broadband router via an ethernet cable and my wireless is rock solid, even for streaming music from my linux box to remote Squeezebox music players.

Even if your ethernet port is otherwise occupied, there are 4 additional ports on the backs of most of these routers that you could hook up to whatever.

Hope this helps,
m9ke
