### Post by kintsa on 2006-10-28
*buntu 6.06 is a long-term support version and is thus very stable, where as the 6.10 version is meant to be cutting edge.

There are known bugs in Kubuntu 6.10 and I am sure the developers are working on fixing it.
[https://wiki.kubuntu.org/KubuntuEdgyKnownProblems]("https://wiki.kubuntu.org/KubuntuEdgyKnownProblems")

My advice for you is to go ahead with 6.10 if you are not upgrading and its a clean install. Most of the people with issues are ones who are upgrading.
