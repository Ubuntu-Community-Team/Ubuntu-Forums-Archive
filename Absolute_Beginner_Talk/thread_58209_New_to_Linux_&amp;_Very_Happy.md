### Post by coxc24 on 2005-08-19
Just thought I would say how impressed I am with Ubuntu!

I had always been a Windows user until 4 days ago when I descided to jump into the Linux world. I actually started with Debian on the command line on an old box, and then a collegue told me about Ubuntu.

4 days later, I have installed all the equivelent software that I used in Windows without any issue onto my main PC and been able to sync my IPOD for the 1st time in 2 months!

Very impressed and the Ubuntu developers have got my praises! A Superb operating system!  \\:D/
