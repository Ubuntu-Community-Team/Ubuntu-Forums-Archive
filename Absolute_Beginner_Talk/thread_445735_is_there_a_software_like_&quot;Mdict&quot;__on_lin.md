### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-16
I don't know about a dictionary program that will support your .mdx file format, I did a quick google and ubuntu forums search and didn't find anything...

Ubuntu does have its own programs for dictionaries. For instance one is loaded by default, Applications > Accessories > Dictionaries will give you access to online databases.

There are also extra programs that you can look at on [linux app finder]("http://linuxappfinder.com/utilities/dictionaries").

JaLingo is cross platform and understands multiple formats, maybe it will understand them? Anyway, good luck with that.
