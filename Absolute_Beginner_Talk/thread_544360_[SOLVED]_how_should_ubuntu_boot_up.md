### Post by raijinsetsu on 2007-09-06
On most systems, you'll see a text "grub will boot in 5,4,3,2,1..." looking thing... And then the Ubuntu splash. If you don't get a splash, then usplash is probably not setup for your video card and monitor (lookup usplash).

Grub is the standard boot loader. You can use the NT Loader, but I wouldn't suggest it. Also, I'm not sure about Lilo's compatibility with Windows...
