### Post by CJ56 on 2008-03-05
If you go to a folder and click on the Edit button on the top bar, then click Preferences, you'll get a menu of options - you can choose to display the items in the folder in various ways, including most recently modified, sort folders first etc.

There might not be everything there that you want, but it's a start...
