### Post by p_quarles on 2007-06-26
You can look up specific hardware compatability here:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport)

Generally speaking, wireless is the main problem with laptops. Intel and Netgear cards work very well with Linux, but other vendors are spotty. 

The other thing to bear in mind, unfortunately, is that your battery life won't be as good under Linux. This is very high on the list of things the developers want to fix, but still a lingering problem.
