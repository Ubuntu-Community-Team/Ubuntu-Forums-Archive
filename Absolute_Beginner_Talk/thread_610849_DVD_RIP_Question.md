### Post by x64Jimbo on 2007-11-15
The only limitation that I have found to k9copy is that it will not rip ARccOS and RipGuard protected discs. For these, you can use RipIt4Me - a front-end for DVDShrink & DVDDecrypter that combines both of them. It's a Windows app, as are both DVDShrink and DVDDecrypter, but if you run into problems with k9copy, then you might need to hack around a little bit with Wine. Not the most ideal solution, but better than losing your only copy to scratches, eh?
