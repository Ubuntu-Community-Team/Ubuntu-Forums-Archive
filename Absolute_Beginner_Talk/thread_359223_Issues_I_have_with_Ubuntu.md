### Post by DerArzt on 2007-02-11
I always had that sound problem after I would use XP (dual boot). This is how i fixed it. Open volume control, go to edit-->preferences, check external amplifier. Then click on the switches tab, and uncheck external amplifier. hope it works ;) I never used a microphone but it worked for me with headphones.
