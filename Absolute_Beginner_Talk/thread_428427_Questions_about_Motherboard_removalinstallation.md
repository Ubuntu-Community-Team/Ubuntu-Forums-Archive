### Post by BHelts on 2007-04-30
the answer to your second question is the AMD is faster.  the answer to your first question is a bit tougher.  you absolutely can change your motherboard and processor.  some things to look for.  you first want to make sure that the motherboard you get will fit your case.  so you want to be careful because a microATX mobo will fit in a full size case, but an ATX mobo will not fit in a small form factor case.  you will also want to make sure the mobo has the correct slot/socket.  you may also need to replace your ram if, for example your old mobo used sdram and your new mobo needs ddr.  that being said, if you do your homework and get it right, the physical replacing is pretty easy, just make sure you use the standoffs.  good luck, and good for you.
