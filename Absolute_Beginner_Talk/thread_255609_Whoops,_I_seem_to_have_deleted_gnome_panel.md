### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2006-09-11
If you delete a panel, there's nothing to worry about because you can add another panel.  Right click on another panel, and select "New panel."  The new panel will be empty so you'll have to add whatever was there before to it.  Do so by going back to "Add to panel."
