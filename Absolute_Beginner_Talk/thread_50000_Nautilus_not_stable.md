### Post by super on 2005-07-18
first of all yes Nautilus can be a bit unstable at times, i sometimes get crashes for no aparent reason. if you want you can try rox-filer instead.

second, you can turn off the spatial browsing feature (why it open seperate windows) by following the instructions here: [http://ubuntuguide.org/#openeachfolderssamewindownautilus](http://ubuntuguide.org/#openeachfolderssamewindownautilus)
this will give you a more consistant feel. or you can try to get used to the spatial browsing  :)
