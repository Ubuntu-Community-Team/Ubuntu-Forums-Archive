### Post by aysiu on 2006-02-26
To move the asterisk focus, press <Tab>
To select an asterisk or deselect it, press <Space>

As far as I know, you need to specify a save-to device during the PartImage setup process.

Their homepage has screenshots and documentation:
[http://www.partimage.org/](http://www.partimage.org/)
