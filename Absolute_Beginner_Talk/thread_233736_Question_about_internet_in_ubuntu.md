### Post by Arisna on 2006-08-10
You can disconnect manually by going to the System Menu, going to Administration, and choosing "Networking."  Click on your modem and then click deactivate.

There is also a panel object available that provides essentially the same functionality as the tray icon in Windows.  Right click on the desired panel, select "Add to Panel," scroll down until you find a Modem Monitor choice, and drag the modem monitor to your panel.  You can then right click and access activation/deactivation menu options.
