### Post by viciouslime on 2008-02-18
you can download them from here: [http://packages.ubuntu.com/](http://packages.ubuntu.com/)

You'll want the following:

gstreamer0.10-plugins-bad
gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly
gstreamer0.10-plugins-good
And any dependencies listed.

Then installing them is simply a case of double clicking and pressing the install button :)
