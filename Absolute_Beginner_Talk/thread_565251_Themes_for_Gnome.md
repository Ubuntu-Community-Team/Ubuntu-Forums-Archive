### Post by misfitpierce on 2007-10-02
Metacity is window border. GTK is another option for full themes but gtk2 engines may be needed from synaptic package manager. Metacity border and looks are easiest to install from [http://art.gnome.org](http://art.gnome.org) where you can click window border or look and just download .tar.gz and open theme manager under system - pref - theme manager and click install find .tar.gz you downloaded and install. Simply put.
