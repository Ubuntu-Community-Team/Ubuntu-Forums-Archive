### Post by aysiu on 2006-07-02
While an upgrade can break your system in some way--possibly making X not functional (so you won't have a graphical environment) or making some programs crash--I've **never** heard of an upgrade actually erasing or corrupting personal data (docs, pics, etc.).

That said, meng is right--it's **always** a good idea to back up your data, even if you aren't making major changes to your system. I back up every week.
