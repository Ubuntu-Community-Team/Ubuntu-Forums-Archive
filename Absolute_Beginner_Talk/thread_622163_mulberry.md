### Post by finer recliner on 2007-11-24
i've used mulberry before. but it was years ago, back when the company went bankrupt. and i know why they went bankrupt. their email client just couldnt keep up with the with the changing pace of the internet. it looks old and doesnt feel well rounded or stable. 

anyways. that was years ago. as of last year, someone has taken on the challenge of developing the client again and making it opensource. i havent taken the time to check it out yet. 

if you're having problems checking email automatically in TB, try these two things:

go to account settings. click on "server settings" for the account you're having trouble with. make sure that "check for new messages is checked"

secondly, if your account's inbox has subfolders, make sure you right click on each subfolder and go to properities. there you will see an option to check this folder for new messages at startup. make sure to check that box off (this may be only relative to IMAP accounts, not sure about POP)

hope this helps you
