### Post by the_darkside_986 on 2007-10-05
[http://www.opensuse.org/](http://www.opensuse.org/)

It has a base cd and packages on another CD. Its package management system lets you install stuff from the CD.

It is also the only distro that I've seen that actually has a decent NTFS-resizing tool that works without destroying the data. But that is beside point.

It is trying to be user-friendly but it might be a little rougher around the edges than Ubuntu. At least it was when I used 10.1. I guess they've improved it since two releases.
