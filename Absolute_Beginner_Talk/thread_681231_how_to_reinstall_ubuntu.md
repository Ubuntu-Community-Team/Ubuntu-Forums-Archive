### Post by mikewhatever on 2008-01-28
Reinstalling is pretty similar to installing, with the exception of partitioning stage. You do not need to create more partitions, but just specify root and swap. The rest is all the same. Note, your root partition will be formatted, so back up anything you need.
Why do you think reinstalling should make your drivers work? If they didn't after the initial installation, I doubt they would at all.
