### Post by pommattski on 2008-04-01
(I'm using GIMP 2.2)
If you resize the image by going to:  Image > Scale,  you also have the option of adjusting the resolution.
If the image is only to be posted/viewed on the web, once you've adjusted the size, you can safely reduce the resolution to 72 (pixels/inch).

### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-04-01
When you go to "Save As", in order to find out what the finished file will be in terms of size, you need to check the "Preview" checkbox. Then you can move the quality slider and it will tell you how big the finished file will be.
