### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-08-21
Or you can post a list of hardware here and let people comment on it.

Without seeing your hardware list, I can say that the most likely problem areas, because the makers don't release enough hardware information, are wireless adapters and USB modems. PCs with a mixture of SATA and IDE drives are sometimes problematic too.

Your drivers on the CDs will be windows drivers, and probably not much use on Linux, although some people have got wireless cards running with windows drivers and a utility called ndiswrapper.

The more detail you can post on your hardware, the more likely you are to get accurate answers here.
