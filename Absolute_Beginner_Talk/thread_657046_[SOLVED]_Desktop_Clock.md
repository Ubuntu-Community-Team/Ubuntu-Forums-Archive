### Post by Rui Pais on 2008-01-06
> **unknown user said:**
> **[COLOR="Green"]This looks good, but I have noticed that when I reboot the clock does not come up on its own, without me having to go into Applications and clicking on it again.  Is there a way I can get the clock to come up on its own after a reboot?[/COLOR]**

On menu go:
System -> Preferences -> Sessions
On the window that appear add 'gdesklets' to Startup Applications.
