### Post by cwaldbieser on 2005-10-28
[QUOTE=Darrin]Thank you.  fonts:/// . What exactly do the three slashes mean anyway?[/QUOTE]
protocol://server/path
server is optional in some cases.
E.g.
[http://www.example.com/path/to/file.html](http://www.example.com/path/to/file.html)
file://192.168.0.2/home/guest/README.txt
file:///home/guest/NOTE.txt
