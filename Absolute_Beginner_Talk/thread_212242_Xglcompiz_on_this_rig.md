### Post by richbarna on 2006-07-09
Yes you can, but bear in mind a couple of things.
People are still having problems with ati cards on basic usage. 

The last thing is, I had xgl/compiz installed with an nVidia card, very impressive, very pretty, and it's great to show off to your friends with.
After a week I got rid of it because the novelty soon wears off. Also it really messed up my keyboard settings.

Yes you can make it work, yes you can solve all the problems that come with it, you just have to decide if it's really worth it.
