### Post by u.b.u.n.t.u on 2006-06-12
To change individual icons, like those cool large icons in the panel or on the desktop, simply copy short cut links of what you want to those locations. Then right mouse click on an icon and select properties.

Next click on the icon in the popup window. You well see a collection of icons. Change the path to where your customs icons are located, eg /home/name/my cool icons and you should see your icons. Find the one you want and click it.

Sometimes you see a visual list of the icons and sometimes a text list with a window where the icons appear as you click on the name.
