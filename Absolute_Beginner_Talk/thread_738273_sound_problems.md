### Post by Daveth on 2008-03-28
you probably need to enter the laptop bios and disavble the internal sound chip. On boot you will get a message about hitting a key to enter the bios, then navigate through the menus, usually with the poage up and down keys, till you find some motherboard info, then disable the internal soundcard option. That will then force all sound through your external system- or should do!
