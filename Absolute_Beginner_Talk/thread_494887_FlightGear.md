### Post by p_quarles on 2007-07-07
The basic installation actually comes with several aircraft, but they are all launched using different commands. You can do this from the command line, or create launchers for the aircraft you want to use. 

A couple links that give more info on the commands, and where to get additional aircraft and scenery:
[http://flightgear.org/Docs/getstart/getstart.html](http://flightgear.org/Docs/getstart/getstart.html)

[http://www.jp.flightgear.org/Docs/InstallGuide/getstartli2.html](http://www.jp.flightgear.org/Docs/InstallGuide/getstartli2.html)
