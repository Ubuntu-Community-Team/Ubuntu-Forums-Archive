### Post by agatha on 2007-09-27
I have created a number of launchers on a laptop that start streaming audio from radio stations with vlc; eg command "% vlc mms://media3.abc.net.au/classicfm".

This works OK, and the launcher icons are always on a panel when I boot.

HOWEVER, when I create exactly the same launchers on my desktopPC the launcher icons disappear every time I boot. They work OK as long as I don't reboot. I have tried to create the launchers on the desktop first and then moving them on to the panel or just used the 'add to panel' command in the context menu. All works OK until I boot.

Any help that explains why things work on one computer and not on a nother are appreciated.

BTW, both PCs use same versions of Ubuntu (Feisty), gnomepanel, vlc etc

### Post by notwen on 2007-09-27
After you add your launchers to your panel, try going to System--->Preferences--->Session  and save your current session.  You may also want to close any open applications prior to saving your session, unless you want said applications to be launched every time you boot up your desktop. =]
