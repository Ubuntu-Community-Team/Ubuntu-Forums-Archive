I am not an expert on these matters, but....

It looks like there is a hardware problem with your floppy drive or the floppy drive controller.  If you leave the process running (for a good 30minutes+), it will probably eventually load.

However, you will get a slow boot each time after installation too, since the problem with the FD will still be there.

I think the best solution is to unplug your floppy drive and then try booting from the LiveCD again.

As mentioned, this may or may not work.
