### Post by daageep on 2006-04-21
i've been using debian with my notebook for most of my undergrad years in math/econ, so i would say its pretty safe for you to use ubuntu only.

the only things to worry about are (i had to dual boot windows until i solved these issues): 
1) software. are there lawschool-specific programs that are not available in ubuntu? i know openoffice doesn't cut it for my problem sets sometime
2) printers. is your printer working perfectly in ubuntu? 
3) internet. does your wifi card work perfectly with campus/coffee shop wifi?

these can easily be solved if you dual-boot windows, but that would defeat the purpose of saving money with a no-OS laptop. =)

edit: [www.system76.com](www.system76.com) sells laptops that are ubuntu-ready
