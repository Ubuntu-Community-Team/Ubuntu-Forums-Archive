### Post by Wangsta on 2006-10-26
All of a sudden, my xfce4-panel turned off. it's happened before, but the first time, i just used the command line to restart it.

but now, everytime i close the terminal i used to turn the panel back on, the panel just goes away again.
Not having a panel makes it really annoying to check battery life etc.

how do i keep it from turning the panel off?

---

### Post by Super King on 2006-10-26
You may be running the panel 'in terminal', meaning when you close the terminal you're effectively closing the panel. Try right-clicking on the desktop and choosing 'Run Command.' There's a little checkbox that says 'run in Terminal'; leave it unchecked. Try running xfce-panel from there.
