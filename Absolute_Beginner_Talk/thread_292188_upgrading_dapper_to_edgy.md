### Post by Sef on 2006-11-04
> should i upgrade it to edgy?

Dapper is stable, so few problems with software not working correctly.  However, the versions are not the newest.  Support till May 09 for the desktop.

Edgy is testing, so packages are newer, and therefore more likely to have problems with software not working correctly.  Support till May 08.


> what happens to my repos?

Your repos will be Edgy repos.
