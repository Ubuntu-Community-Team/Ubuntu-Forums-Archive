### Post by LordVector on 2007-08-16
It's not particularly obvious, but this link should help you get to the "Removable Drives and Media" dialog in Xubuntu:

[http://thunar.xfce.org/documentation/C/using-removable-media.html](http://thunar.xfce.org/documentation/C/using-removable-media.html)

Once you get to the "Removable Drives and Media" dialog, go to the "Multimedia" tab.  You should be able to disable autoplay from there.
