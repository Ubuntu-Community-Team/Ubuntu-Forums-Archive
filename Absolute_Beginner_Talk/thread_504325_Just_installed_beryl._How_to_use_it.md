### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-07-19
First of all you go to System>Administration>Restricted Drivers Manager and install the driver for your graphics card. Activate it and reboot your computer.

Your next step will be to go to System>Settings>Desktop Effects and enable the desktop effects, remember to markup Desktop as a cube. Then you go to Programs>System tools and start up Beryl. Next to the clock in your upper right corner there'll be a diamond shaped icon. Right-click on this and choose Beryl Manager in order to load the program manager. Here you can setup Beryl to your likings. To activate the cube you hold down Ctrl+Alt and rotate your mouse. 

Please remember that Beryl is beta software and may crash.
