### Post by Kobalt on 2006-07-07
The WiFi should work on Xubuntu : I actually have my Linksys WPC54G card working really easily... But it'll be different if you use the LiveBox (a sagem modem used in France) usb dongle given with France Telecom internet connections... As you may have guessed Akash799, I live in France as well. To get your Xubuntu CD, if you can't get it from a friend or relative that would d/l and burn it for you, you could order it for a cheap price on [distrowatch.com]("http://distrowatch.com") .
