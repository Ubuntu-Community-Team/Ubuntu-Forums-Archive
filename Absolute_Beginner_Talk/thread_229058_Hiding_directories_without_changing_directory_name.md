### Post by hasimir44 on 2006-08-03
this is posted in the [frostwire forums]("http://www.frostwire.com/forum/viewtopic.php?t=188&highlight=incomplete"): 
> Your incomplete folder automatically follows your share folder. It is created automatically wherever the share folder is and cannot be moved independently. Whether this is the primary share folder or the audi share folder is a matter of slight debate. Your share folder can be put pretty well anywhere but NOT in the Frostwire Application folder or the Preference folder, as It if likeley to be lost if you have a problems with either.

You can change the location of your share folder in Frostwire>preferences > sharing>share folder. but isf you move it your current incompletes may be lost.
