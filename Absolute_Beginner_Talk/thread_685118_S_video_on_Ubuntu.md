### Post by JoshuaRL on 2008-02-01
Well, it all comes down to if the driver supports it for your video card.  Some don't and you have to look no farther than the device manufacturer for the issue.  Nvidia and Intel work great, but ATI is pretty spotty.

But to see if it does, go into Settings->Administration(or Preferences, I can't remember because I'm at work)->Screens and Graphics.  With it plugged in, see if it will allow you to switch over to S-Video out.  Play around with the settings in there and see what you can come up with.
