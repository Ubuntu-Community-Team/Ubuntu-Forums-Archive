### Post by p_quarles on 2007-12-11
Many laptops have brightness adjustment keys built-in to the keyboard. On mine (a Compaq), it's the FN (function) key + F5 or F6. These keys have a brightness icon as well as up and down arrows. Yours is very likely configured differently, but you will probably find something very similar.
