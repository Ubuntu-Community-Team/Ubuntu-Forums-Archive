### Post by meierfra on 2007-07-18
I'm using Feisty, but it should be the same in edgy: You can add a "force quit" button to your main panel:

Right-Click your main panel.
Select :Add to panel"
Scroll and click on "Force Quit"

This adds a new button  to your main panel. To "Force Quit" a window, click on the button and then on the window you want to close.

(If your are using kubuntu, there is different solution: Press Ctr-Alt-Esc on the keyboard. You cursor turns into a cross-bone-skull. Click on any window to kill it)
