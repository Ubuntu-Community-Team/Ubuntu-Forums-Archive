### Post by Cypher on 2007-07-06
Yes Adept and Synaptic will give you the option to upgrade to the latest distro when it is released and everything resets. Once the security timeframe for a particular distro elapses, all work is stopped on it and you will have to update to the newer distro to continue to get security updates.

It is usually advisable to stay up-to-date with the latest distro anyway, unless you have a very specific reason to remain on an older distro. And even then, Ubuntu will support you for a little while, but if you're going to stay with Breezy Badger, for example, when Gutsy Gibbon is released, then you're on your own..
