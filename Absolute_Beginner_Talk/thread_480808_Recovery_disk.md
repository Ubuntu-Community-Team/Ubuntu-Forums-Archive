### Post by jfinkels on 2007-06-21
> **Ssj3_man said:**
> I did its still boots 7.04 the one i have installed already.

And i thought it would be that easy but no...

You must configure BIOS options to allow booting from CD ROM drive before hard disk drive. When your computer is booting up, press F12 or Delete (might be a different button, there should be a message saying "Press F12 to enter setup" or something similar). Once in setup, find an option that says "Boot Order" or something similar. Make sure to move your CD drive to a higher priority than your hard disk drive. This way, next time you boot your computer with a CD in the drive, the system will attempt to boot from the CD before trying to boot from the hard disk.
