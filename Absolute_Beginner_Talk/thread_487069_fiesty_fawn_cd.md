### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-06-28
When you download the ISO, there are a few things you have to do before you can use it:
First you should check the md5sum to make sure the images is OK:
[HowTo]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM")
[Hashes]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes") to check against

Then you can burn the .iso file to a CD as an image - do not burn as data.  You should burn at a slow speed and on good quality media to prevent writing errors (4x is a recommended speed).

After this you can boot from the live cd, you may have to configure your BIOS to read from the CD before the HDD.
