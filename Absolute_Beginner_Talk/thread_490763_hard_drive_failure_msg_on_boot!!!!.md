### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-07-02
> **the.phantom said:**
> i don't know if Ubuntu supports smart disk ?
I think SMART is built into the hard drive and loads with the initial hardware scan, not the OS.

As far as backing up your stuff goes, just remember where you store stuff - if you stick to the convention of using your home folder for your files, you should be OK.  You can always backup other directories, but I don't see a need - you can always reinstall everything else.  You may want to consider backing up the hidden files in your home folder though (CTRL+H in nautilus to view or ls -a in terminal), just for safety.
