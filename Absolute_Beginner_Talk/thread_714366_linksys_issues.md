### Post by Miljet on 2008-03-03
Go to Network Manager, click on wireless network, then Properties. Look closely at your password. You won't be able to read it since it will be all dots, but if it looks unusually long, delete it and re-enter it. Wait for it to re-configure, then close the window and try to connect.

I have to reset mine every time I reboot. I found a thread discussing this problem a few weeks back. I followed it for two pages and it seems to be a common problem. Nobody ever did come up with a solution and I have since lost the thread and have been unable to find it again.
