### Post by tatimmer on 2007-03-30
You might fire up your editor and look at the following files:

/etc/ppp/peers/providerTicon
/etc/chatscripts/providerTicon
/etc/ppp/pap-secrets 

Instead of 'Ticon' the name of your isp should appear. I had to edit these files.  See the tip file on my website under "modem".
