### Post by Bartender on 2006-11-01
Sharon -
Modem problems have been covered a million times.  Type your model of Dell into "Search" along with 'modem' and see if you get any hits.
Most laptops (all laptops?) come with modems that rely on the Windows OS to work.  Slang term for these is "winmodems".  Some can be hacked to work in Linux, some can't.  
You took a step in the right direction with the external modem, but USB externals are often winmodems too AND going thru the USB interface causes complications.
If you have a serial connection take a look at old US Robotics externals on ebay.  Another option is getting a Linux-compatible PCMCIA modem.  I know nothing about these so can't offer specific suggestions.
