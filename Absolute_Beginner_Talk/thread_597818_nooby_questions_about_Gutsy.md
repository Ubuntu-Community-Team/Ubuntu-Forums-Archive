### Post by rudeboyskunk on 2007-10-30
1) ctrl+alt+backspace brings you to an alternate command line view.  The best way to get back to your screen is ctrl+alt+F7.

2) Go to System>Preferences>Appearances and Desktop Settings (or something similar to that title), and you'll be able to adjust ALL compiz settings, including desktop cube.
