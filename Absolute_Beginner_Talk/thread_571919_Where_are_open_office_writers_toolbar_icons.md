### Post by rubbsdecvik on 2007-10-09
Ok... here's how I can do it.

Open Writer and do the following:

View-> Toolbars-> Customize. 

Then make sure you are in the toolbars tab.  In this tab is drop down menu under the button that says "new."  This dropdown menu is labled toolbars as well.

in that menu is the option to turn the buttons to text, icon, and text & icons.

Hopefully that works,

Let me know.
