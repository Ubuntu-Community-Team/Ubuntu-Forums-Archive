### Post by engla on 2006-04-04
Whatever the technical differences and similarities are, just pick a few distros and try them, repositories is not all but it's the whole configuration -- and in your case it just seems to be the monitor configuration.

Anyway, find a couple of live cds or so from different distros, Ubuntu and Mepis have those and others probably have too. Try the livecds, and see which distros manage your hardware best.,. make a simple list, and in the end compromise and choose the one with best support.

If you're just trying around, be sure to try the daily dapper live cd too, for comparison. It might be that the next release charms you.
