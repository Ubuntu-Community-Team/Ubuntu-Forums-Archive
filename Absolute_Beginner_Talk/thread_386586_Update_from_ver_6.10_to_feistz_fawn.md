### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2007-03-17
First Welcome.

There are a lot of resources available to you to help you transition to Ubuntu, including the beginners team :) . However, you should not expect a drop-in replacement for windows XP.

It will take you a few weeks or months to learn a new OS.

My advice to you is to go ahead and install edgy (6.10) if everything is working.

Here is an install guide : [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installing](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installing)

In terms of upgrading to 7.04, well up to now most users are reporting breakage when they upgrade from Edgy to Feisty. We will have to wait and see when the new release is available.

Best to use Edgy for now, do not customize too much ...

Then, in a few weeks, if you like, once it is released, just install Feisty over the top ...

You can make a separate /home (or a data partition) if you wish to save you data ;)
