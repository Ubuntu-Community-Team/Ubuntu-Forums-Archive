### Post by The Cog on 2008-03-26
gedit does this, and I guess other editors might too. It's a backup of the previous version of the file, and is intended as a safety measure. To disable it in gedit, go to the menu Edit->Preferences, Click the Editor tab and un-check the option to make a backup before saving.
