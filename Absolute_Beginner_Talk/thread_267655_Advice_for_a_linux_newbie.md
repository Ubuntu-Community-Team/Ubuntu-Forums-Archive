Hi Brendan

Wine will have problems playing those games. The [URL="http://appdb.winehq.org/"]wine application database
[/URL] says Fable wont run. BF 1942 and Farcry have mixed success. But BF 1942 and Far Cry are featured games of [Cedega]("http://www.transgaming.com"). They should install and play with it. The only problem is that Cedega isn't free. Its $15 bucks for a 3 month subscription and $5 each month after that. During that time you can download the program and updates that add new games. The good news is if you let the subscription run out it still works. You just will have to renew the subscription if they add any new titles to cedega that you want to play.
I paid for cedega, it was worth it for me to be able to just click install and play instead of the headaches that wine can give you. But its up to you.
