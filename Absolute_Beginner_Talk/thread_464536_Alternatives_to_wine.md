### Post by Eddie Wilson on 2007-06-05
As far as I know The Wine Project is the only one that is open and free. CrossoverLinux by Codeweavers is very very good but it is a paid for program. Those are the same people who run the wine project. You have to pay one time for CrossoverLinux. Cedega on the other hand is a monthly payment thing. I used it for a while but it really isn't worth the money plus they don't give anything back to the community. Dual booting, (you have to own MS Windows) or using Wine is your only free options. Like I said thats all I know about.
Eddie
