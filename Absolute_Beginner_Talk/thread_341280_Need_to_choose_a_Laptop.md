### Post by houstonbofh on 2007-01-21
HP, Compaq, andIBM all have a BIOS whitelist for mini-pci WiFi cards.  That means you can not swap out the internal WiFI for better suported cards.  Dell, on the other had, allows this with no problem.  I have installed Ubuntu on several Dells.  The only problems were some WiFi cards (replaceable) and some internal modems.  You could take a LiveCD to the store with you.
