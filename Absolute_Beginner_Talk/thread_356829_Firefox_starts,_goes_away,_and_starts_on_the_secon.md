### Post by spin2cool on 2007-02-08
start it using "firefox -p" and try a clean profile.

If it starts fine using the new profile, one of your extensions probably hosed things.  Copy your bookmarks over to the new profile and re-install extensions one at a time until you figure out which one screwed it up.
