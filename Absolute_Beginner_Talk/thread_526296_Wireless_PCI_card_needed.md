### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-08-15
The Netgear WG311T is a good choice for a PCI card for an Ubuntu desktop.  Other people have said it works fine, and as far as I know, there is only 1 version (with Atheros chipset).  And it is sold by this company as Ubuntu (Feisty) compatible:

[http://efficientpc.co.uk/components/wg311t/](http://efficientpc.co.uk/components/wg311t/)

Have a look for it locally.

Here is the guy who got it recently: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=508440&page=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=508440&page=2)
