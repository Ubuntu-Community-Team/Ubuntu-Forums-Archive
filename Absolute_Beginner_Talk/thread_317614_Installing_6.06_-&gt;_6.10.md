### Post by kevinatkins on 2006-12-12
I've upgraded 6.06 to 6.10 on my main desktop machine - not too bad, but the end result isn't too much of an advance over 6.06, and I felt 6.06 was slightly more stable (well, OK, Firefox 2 seems rather more crash-prone than 1.5; rest of the distro is OK). I'm not so sure Edgy is a worthwhile upgrade, given the effort involved.

### Post by maxamillion on 2006-12-12
the upgrade nightmares that have been happening have to do with update-manager, which is a well documented bug and is in the process of being resolved but the upgrade from dapper to edgy via apt-get or aptitude should be smooth.

more info on the update-manager problems can be found here [https://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu/+source/update-manager/+bug/68027](https://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu/+source/update-manager/+bug/68027)
