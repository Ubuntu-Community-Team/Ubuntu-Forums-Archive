### Post by defishguy on 2007-04-01
To re-mount the drive just unplug the sansa, wait just a second or two, and then plug it back in.  It should mount again automagically.

When you deleted stuff on the sansa you may have just "moved it" to the .trash file on the sansa itself.  Once it's mounted again you should see stuff appear in your trash can if it's displayed on the desktop.  Just empty the trash with the sansa plugged in.

I do know that sansa players work with amarok but I'm not going to be much help because I don't use it personally.

BTW - be sure to open the "Computer" window and eject the sansa by right clicking and selecting eject before you unplug the sansa from the computer.

Best of luck!
