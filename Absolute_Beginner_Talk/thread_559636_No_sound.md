### Post by SendDerek on 2007-09-25
I believe you have to mute the amplifier within the ALSA sound menu. 

You need to goto the terminal and type "alsamixer" and MUTE the External Amplifier all the way to the right side of the screen by hitting "M" when "External Amplifier" is highlighted. That may take care of it!
