### Post by Old Pink on 2007-09-09
Incorrect, an iPod will charge when plugged in any powered USB socket.

All I can suggest is your socket is not powered, and charges slowly when iTunes is open due to being in use.

What you need is an AC to USB convertor. So you plug this into your wall and then your iPod cable into the convertor, and finally your iPod into the cable. Or try another slot on your PC.

Example:
[http://pocketpccentral.net/mp3c/images/accys/boxwave/ipod_wall_charger.jpg](http://pocketpccentral.net/mp3c/images/accys/boxwave/ipod_wall_charger.jpg)

You can get iPod specific ones, but they're more pricey. As long as it feeds 12V into a USB cable you'll be fine.
