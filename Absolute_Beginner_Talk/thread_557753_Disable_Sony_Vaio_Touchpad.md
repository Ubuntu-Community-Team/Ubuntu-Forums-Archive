### Post by startherepodcast on 2007-09-23
Try to delete this text:

Section "InputDevice"
Identifier "Synaptics Touchpad"
Driver "synaptics"
Option "SendCoreEvents" "true"
Option "Device" "/dev/psaux"
Option "Protocol" "auto-dev"
Option "HorizScrollDelta" "0"
EndSection

From your Xorg.conf and restart X (CTRL+ALT+Backspace). I like my touchpad though, it works like a charm!
