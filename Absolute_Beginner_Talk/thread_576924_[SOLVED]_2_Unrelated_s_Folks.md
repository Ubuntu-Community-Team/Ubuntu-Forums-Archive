### Post by Keith_Burton on 2007-10-15
I've seen sites freeze like that because of a broken banner ad. Turn on your browser's status bar, and check what it's saying when the site freezes.

"Looking up" or "resolving" means the browser has asked a DNS server to translate a name to an IP address ([www.google.com](www.google.com) to 64.233.169.147)

"Connecting" means the browser has requested to open a session with a site, but the site hasn't responded yet.

"Waiting" means the site's responded and a session is open, but the site hasn't sent any data to your browser yet.

"Transferring" is moving the actual data.

Hope this was helpful
