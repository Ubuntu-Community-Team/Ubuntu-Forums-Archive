### Post by skippy81 on 2006-05-06
just installed it to test for you.

from terminal type > aegis-anti-virus &

you can easily add an icon for it to reach it easily:

1) right click on "Applications" menu.

2) select "edit menus"

3) Click on accesories on the left panel and click on "new entry".

4) Give it some text and an icon, in command enter "aegis-anti-virus".

Hope that helps you.
