### Post by Incense on 2007-10-11
The applets are locked. Right click them all one by one and unckeck the Lock to Panel Box, and you should be able to put whatever you want there. To add new ones, right-click on a blank piece of the panel, click add to panel, select what you want, then right click it once it's added, select move, and place it where ever you like.
