### Post by oldsoulsong on 2008-04-21
right click on your top panel, click on new panel and it should make one on the bottom, then right click on the bottom panel you just made and click add to panel, then scroll down to window list and click it , then you should be back to normal

### Post by Jon Monreal on 2008-04-21
> **TolTime said:**
> i deleted my bottom panel and now my windows don't show up on the bottom bar.  I have been using AWN and using alt-tab to get around but i am wondering how to set it up so that the windows are accessible from the bottom panel.

You can right-click on the menu at the top and click New Panel.

Then, you can move that panel to the bottom, and click Add To Panel. You can then add Window List, Workspace Switcher, and more.

If you need more help, please feel free to ask.
