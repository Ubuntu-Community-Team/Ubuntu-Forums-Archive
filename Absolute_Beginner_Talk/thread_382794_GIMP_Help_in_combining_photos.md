### Post by Ek0nomik on 2007-03-12
If you go to Image/Canvas size, you can change the size of your image.

What I would do is this:  Open 1 of your images.  Go into Image/Canvas Size, and double the width (not the height).  Than, I would load your second image.  Copy it, and paste it into your new blank space that you made by changing the Canvas Size.

Note, this is only the best option if both images the exact same height and width.  Making it look good is up to you.

That will do the trick though.

Cheers!
