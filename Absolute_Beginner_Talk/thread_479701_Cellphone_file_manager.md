### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2007-07-03
Hey, I have the same phone (w300i), but I am selling mine soon!  

You actually don't need anything "special" to put MP3s, or any other files (games, themes, etc.) onto the phone.   Use the USB cable that came with the phone to connect it to the computer, and Ubuntu should automatically pick up the phone (the phone also automatically picks up on the connection).  Now that the phone is connected, drag and drop the files you want into the phone directory (there should be a new disk like icon showing up on your desktop - that is the directory).  Go to the appropriate folder (for games, songs, etc.), and drop whatever you want into the right folder.

Once you're done, right click on the desktop icon, and select something like "eject" or a similar phrase for ending the connection.  A moment aftwards, remove the USB cable, but not right away. 
For games, you're not done.  You'd have to go into the phone, and install the game.  

For any other questions, let me know.
