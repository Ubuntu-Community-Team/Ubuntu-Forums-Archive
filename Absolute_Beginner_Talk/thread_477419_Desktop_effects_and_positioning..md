### Post by ThinkBuntu on 2007-06-18
It should still do this, except along the screen edge (because the workspaces are shared). I believe there's a setting for this in the gconf-editor. Access it via F2: gconf-editor and find the compiz entry. You'll discover all sorts of settings you never knew about.
