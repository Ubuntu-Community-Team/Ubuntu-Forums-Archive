### Post by seoatway on 2006-03-21
The 54Mb/s speed is between you and the Library access point. The 90Kb/s is the speed you are getting between you and wherever you are downloading from which is limited by (amongst other things) the internet bandwidth the Library has, the upload speed of the site you are downloading from, and general internet congestion. You will never get 54Mb/s throughput from the internet at a publi access site.
