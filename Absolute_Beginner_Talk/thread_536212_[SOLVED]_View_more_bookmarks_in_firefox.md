### Post by felicity on 2007-08-27
Try using [del.icio.us]("http://del.icio.us/"), it's neat for lots of bookmarks! 

If you still want to use firefox for bookmarking, you can seperate them into folders, or you can try viewing them in the firefox sidebar.

---

### Post by felicity on 2007-08-27
Oh, I forgot to mention you can add your bookmarks into folders in the Organize Bookmarks dialog, then drag the folders into your Bookmarks Toolbar Folder. Then the folders will show up on your toolbar and you can click them for a drop-down menu of the bookmarks contained in that folder!
