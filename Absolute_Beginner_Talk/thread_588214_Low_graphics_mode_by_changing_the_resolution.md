### Post by mikeyphi on 2007-10-23
Open the Screen & Graphics GUI - check that the correct monitor is selected. Also check that the correct graphics card is selected. After that you should (!!) be able to select any resolution handled by your system.
It's worthwhile also confirming that the same resolution is selected under the 'Screen Resolution GUI.
