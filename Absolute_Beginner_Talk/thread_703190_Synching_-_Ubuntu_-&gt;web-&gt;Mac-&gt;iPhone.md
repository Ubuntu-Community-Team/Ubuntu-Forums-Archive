### Post by ntetreau on 2008-02-23
Hi, I won't be able to answer all of your questions directly but i'll try.  I use evolution you see, but I use syncevolution to sync it's calendar and contacts with the scheduleword web service.  Scheduleword and google calendar are kept automatically in sync.  On the iphone, I use the Funambol app which syncs my contacts with the scheduleword web server.  I sync my calendar directly over wifi but not going through scheduleworld yet.  Notes are synced with Tomboy.  In order of effectiveness and ease of use, I'd say contacts are 80%, calendar is 60% and notes 40%.
