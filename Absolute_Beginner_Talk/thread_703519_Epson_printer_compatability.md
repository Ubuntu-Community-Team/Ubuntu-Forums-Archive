### Post by cotcot on 2008-02-21
Have you tries pipslite from avasys-epson ? (see [[COLOR="Red"]here[/COLOR]]("http://www.avasys.jp/lx-bin2/linux_e/spc/DL2.do")) 
Unfortunately pipslite will give you not the full support (printing to envelopes and stuff like that is missing)

You have to download the .rpm and convert it with alien to .deb.  Then install the .deb (right click on the file will give you the package installer)

Have you checked gutenprint 5.1.6 (developer version !) ?
