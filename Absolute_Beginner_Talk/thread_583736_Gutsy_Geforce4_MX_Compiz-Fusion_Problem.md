### Post by bumanie on 2007-10-23
All I can suggest is that the combination of your motherboard, CPU, graphics card and amount of ram is not enough to run the compiz-fusion. Beryl was a stand-alone app. that is no longer supported. Beryl was merged with the compiz program (hence the fusion part of the name). Gutsy is meant to enable the full blown eye candy only if your hardware is capable of supporting it. I assume your hardware is not able to support it. Beryl on its own may have been less demanding. You could try installing beryl on gutsy - just insert gutsy where you see feisty. Not sure if this will work, but ma be worth a try.
