I am very new to Ubuntu, and as yet, the only thing that I have really managed to learn for myself is how to set my screen resolution and refresh rate. This was a journey of epic proportions for me, as I decided that I would not ask for any help on the Forum until I had achieved this and I could help someone else first. This has been done on 2nd hand Hardrives from 4 to 20GB.

If you are a Complete Novice as I was, my only important suggestion is to acquire Bootable CD's for your first attempts and get hold of a cheap 2nd hand/user Hardrive. This will very likely add to your stress, but, it will save your data! Hope this helps!
