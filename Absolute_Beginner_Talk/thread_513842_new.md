### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-07-31
Boot Camp is for the new Intel Macs. It's not relevent for anything with a PowerPC or 68k processor.

For starters, what sort of Mac is it? The model number would help; if there's no model number, you could start by telling us if the case is beige, or if it's something more interesting (like metallic or white/colourful plastic).

For all PowerPC-based Macs made since late 1998, you can boot the Ubuntu CD (PowerPC version) by putting it into the drive and holding down the C key while starting up.
