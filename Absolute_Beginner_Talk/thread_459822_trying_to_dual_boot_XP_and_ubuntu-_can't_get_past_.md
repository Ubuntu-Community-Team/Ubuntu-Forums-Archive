### Post by candtalan on 2007-05-31
Sometimes The burning speed is abit too fast for the system and possibly the media being burned to.

My burner drive is rated at 52x speed and the cd media I use is rated 52x speed also. However, I carry out all my data or ISO burning at 24x speed because experience has shown that - for whtever reason - I can get errors (sometimes) idf I use faster speeds, and 24x never gives problems.

btw
I always use md5 checks on download images and also following burning. 
On rare occasions - after a higher speed burn - the md5 has checked ok but still a CD has given trouble when being read in other drives.
