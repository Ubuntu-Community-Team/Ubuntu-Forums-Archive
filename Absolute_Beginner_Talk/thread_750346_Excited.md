If you installed everything from the Synaptic Package Manager you should be able to just click the UPGRADE button when you get the notification.  It won't hurt you home folder or destroy your files.  That said however, you should always make a back up copy of your home folder and put it in a safe place just in case something major goes wrong like a power outage or hardware failure during the upgrade process.  This way if you must do another clean install, you can restore your home folder to previous condition and not skip a beat...
