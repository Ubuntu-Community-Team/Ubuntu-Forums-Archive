### Post by northern lights on 2008-04-16
mkey. While I doubt Best Buy is anywhere near your best option to ask for advice at, yet the guy is probably right.

How does your sister connect to the net? Is her com the only comp in the household?

If she's on a wired connection and her comp is the only one, you likely don't have a router. If there is another comp in the house and both simultaneously connect to the web, you likely have one already, most European ISPs supply one nowadays. Most common are 4-port routers, so check whether you can plug a wire in at least. Obviously you'd know, if there was wireless internet in your place already. Just in case there is, you need nottin', as your laptop will come with a wireless card for sure. If there ain't no wireless yet, you should indeed get a wireless router. It can either be put between the dsl/cable modem and your sister's comp, could replace an existing router (all wireless routers come with 4 wire ports also), or simple be connected to an existing one via an uplink.

The operating systems of the machines in the (W)LAN are completely irrelevant.

And since your getting a pre-installed Dell, the wireless will work out of the box 100%.
