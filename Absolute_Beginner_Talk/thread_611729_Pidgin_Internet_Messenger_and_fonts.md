### Post by mcduck on 2007-11-13
> **kaidenet said:**
> I tried to enter the information in the Pidgin, but I think i entered one of the fields wrongly, so now my Pidgin cannot load properly. Can anyone gimme directions on how to reinstall Pidgin?

No need for reinstall. Open the file manager, and in your home directory press Ctrl-h to show hidden files. Find the directory called .purple and delete it to remove Pidgin's setting files and reset it to default settings. Then start Pidgin again.

Anyway, I can't imaghine what setting you could possibly enter that would stop Pidgin from loading at all. You should be able to just go back to Pidgin's settings and change whatever you did. Jsut right-click Pidgin's icon in notification area, select Accounts, your MSN account and then click the Modify-button..
