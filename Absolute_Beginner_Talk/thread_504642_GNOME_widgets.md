### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-07-19
Yep! The package "gdesklets" provides this functionality, along with some built-in widgets. You can find it in the Add/Remove programs dialog.

### Post by Kobalt on 2007-07-19
You can either use GDesklets, that are the easiest way of getting widgets (directly through Add/Remove) or the Screenlets. They require a composite manager though (like Compiz or Beryl).
