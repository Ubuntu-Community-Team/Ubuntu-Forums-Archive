### Post by deepwave on 2006-10-25
Should be fairly straight forward.  Just run gksu update-manager -d or sudo apt-get dist-upgrade.  In update-manager you should get a notice about a new distribution being ready.

After you do dist-upgrade, just check for any necessary updates.

Personally, the updating Dapper to Edgy was straightforward with a few exceptions.  Updating to Dapper from Breezy should be easier.
