### Post by o_fortuna on 2006-09-20
Wine doesn't like USB (ie, it wouldn't be able to see your calculator, most probably). The TiLP will probably do the job - you can find it in Applications--Add/Remove, I think, as long as you enable unsupported applications.

Also, you only need TI-Connect if you want to transfer stuff from your computer to your calculator. Your calc will work fine without it.
