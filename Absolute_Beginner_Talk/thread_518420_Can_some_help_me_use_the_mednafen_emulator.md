### Post by optimarcus_prime on 2007-10-08
Mednafen is a multi-game-system emulator, for various platforms, including emulation of the following systems:

    * Atari Lynx
    * Famicom
    * GameBoy (Color)
    * GameBoy Advance
    * Neo Geo Pocket (Color)
    * NES, both NTSC and PAL
    * PC Engine/TurboGrafx 16 (CD) and SuperGrafx
    * PC-FX
    * WonderSwan (Color) 

[Taken directly from the Mednafen documentation]

Mednafen doesn't support SNES.
