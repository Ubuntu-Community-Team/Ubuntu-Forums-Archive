### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2007-02-20
Faster than your hand calculator. I bet it can add numbers faster than a snail, too!
I've even seen it outrun a garden gnome once or twice :o

It's faster than Gnome for some people (though come to think of it, Gnome is being a lot faster these days!). If you want really fast I recommend Xubuntu, which uses a really simple and light-weight desktop environment that is easy to learn, too!
(It feels a lot like regular vanilla Gnome-powered Ubuntu).

If you are just starting out here, I suggest you try standard Ubuntu and then if you want you can always install the others from there (by installing the xubuntu-desktop, kubuntu-desktop, edubuntu-desktop, etc. packages). I am a bit biased, of course!
