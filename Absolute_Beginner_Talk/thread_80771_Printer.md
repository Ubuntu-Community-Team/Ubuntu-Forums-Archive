### Post by Herman on 2005-10-23
I have Epsom Stylus C43UX, and I just set mine up by clicking 'system', then 'administration', and then 'printing'. A window should open with an icon in it titled 'new printer', and you can click on that icon, and click 'add'. By the time you get to that stage you'll be pointing in the right direction enough to figure the rest out by yourself. Ubuntu has drivers for just about any printer in the world, all you have to do is find yours on the list. Bye, (Herman).
