### Post by T700 on 2006-07-07
I just randomly checked several community colleges near me (East coast of the US) and every one of them offers one or more classes on Linux and UNIX. I'm not surprised that there are not (yet) Ubuntu specific classes, but that isn't really necessary until you get to the server admin level.  If you want to learn to drive a car, you don't look for a Ford class or a Chevy class, you just learn to drive a generic car.

Honestly though, it isn't really that tough.  Read, search the net, buy a book or two, and just experiment.  That's how likely 98% of the Linux users learned their way around.  It works and it works well, just give yourself some time.
