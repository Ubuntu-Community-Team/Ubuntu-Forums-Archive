### Post by GreyFox503 on 2005-10-06
If you look in the manual that came with your motherboard, it will tell you what key to press to access the BIOS.  It usually says during the POST, but sometimes it flies by so fast you can't see it.  Also see the manufacturer's website if you can't find the physical manual.

Once you find the right key, press the power button on your computer and just repeatedly press it (once a second or so) until the BIOS screen comes up.

EDIT:  Common keys are delete, escape, or one of the F keys (F1, F2, F8, F12)
