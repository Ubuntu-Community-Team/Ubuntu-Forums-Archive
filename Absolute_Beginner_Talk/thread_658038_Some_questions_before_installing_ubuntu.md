### Post by sumguy231 on 2008-01-04
Ati graphics cards work, the drivers just used to have a bad reputation for quality. They claim the new drivers are better, but I don't have one to verify that.

I have an Atheros-based PCI wireless card which works fine in Ubuntu out-of-the-box, so it's certainly possible. Your mileage may vary though. It depends on the restricted kernel modules, so don't remove those.
