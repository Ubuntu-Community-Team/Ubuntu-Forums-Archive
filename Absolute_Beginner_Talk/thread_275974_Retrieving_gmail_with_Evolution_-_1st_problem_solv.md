### Post by jrz on 2006-10-12
I finally got Evolution to retrieve my gmail. It turns out you have to login to gmail, click on settings in the upper right corner, and the the Forwarding & POP tab, where you must enable POP download. 
Now if I can get Hotmail and Yahoo to work also I will be happy.
