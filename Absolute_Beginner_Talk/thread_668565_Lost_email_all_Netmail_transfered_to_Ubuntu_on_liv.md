### Post by red_Marvin on 2008-01-15
The email is gone from your computer, since the live cd does not touch the hard drives of the computer unless you explicitly tell it to by mounting them (that's the point of the live cd after all). (AFAIK)
So if the mail is recoverable depends on if verizon somehow keeps a backup or moves it to a 'read mail' folder on their servers.

I'd suggest that you contact verizon and say you accidentally deleted your mail locally and wonder if you could recover it. (Personally I would try to not mention linux + live cd since it *could* end with a "oh, but we don't support linux, sorry", even if it has little to do with if the mail would be recoverable from their servers or not)

If that doesn't work out and you remember who sent you mail you could always mail them and ask them to resend.
Good luck.
