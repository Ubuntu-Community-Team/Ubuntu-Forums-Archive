### Post by LeeDavis on 2007-11-09
I recommend doing both :) (VMware & HD Partition) as you may find a differences.

I tested Ubuntu for 3 weeks in VMware and loved it, Vista fubar so I just reformatted with Ubuntu.  This is when I realised how the VMware differs.  

Firstly was the bridged Networking, which wasnt too much of a problem, just needed the WPA key (and tbh my wireless is much more stable in Ubuntu than Vista).

The second thing to be 'Bridged' was the sound.  This was a must for me (I live in single room with no room for TV etc so my laptop is the hub for all entertainment).

Suffice to say I have a dual boot with Vista until I can figure the sound with Ubuntu.
