### Post by JoshuaRL on 2008-04-06
It won't show anything, but it's recording your keystrokes.  Just type in your password and hit Enter.  It should work.

sudo from the CLI won't have any dots, stars, or anything else saying how many characters you entered.  That's a security measure.
