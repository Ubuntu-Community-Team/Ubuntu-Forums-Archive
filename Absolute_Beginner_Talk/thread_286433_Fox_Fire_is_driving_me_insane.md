### Post by nalmeth on 2006-10-28
I like stellarium

Have you installed any extra drivers for your video card? That may be the problem.

By default, ubuntu uses open-source video drivers (nv for nvidia, ati for ati, i810 for intel)

Find out which card you have, and installing the proprietary video driver may fix the problem.
