### Post by mickmade on 2007-06-27
Hi, did you give a try to SCIM? I am a Chinese/English user. Once I install the language support for Chinese (mostly I used English for menu and anything) I have the input method for Chinese. By pressing CTRL+ENTER, SCIM pops up for my Chinese typing; and pressing the same sequence again, it brings me back to English typing. I guess you can use it for Spanish too.
