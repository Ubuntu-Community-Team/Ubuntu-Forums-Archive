### Post by adam.tropics on 2007-03-22
Well, I doubt this is the 'ideal' solution. I can't find one of those! But there are more file managers than just Nautilus. Unfortunately, it seems the only ones that do what you require are kde based. However I can recommend Dolphin. It may not look too pretty in your Gnome setup, but you don't need it all the time do you. It's in the repos, (sudo aptitude install dolphin) and on install will appear in you Applications-->System Tools menu. I was thinking you could do something like a panel shortcut to ~/.Thumbnails using Dolphin as the app. I'd probably stick with Nautilus for everything else though.
