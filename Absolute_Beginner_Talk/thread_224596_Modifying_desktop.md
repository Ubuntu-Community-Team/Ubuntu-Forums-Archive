### Post by hw-tph on 2006-07-28
There are loads of already written themes for the window manager (Metacity themes) and applications (GTK2 themes). You can find them at places like [art.gnome.org](http://art.gnome.org) and [Gnome-Look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org).

You can also roll your own themes but that is actually quite tedious.
