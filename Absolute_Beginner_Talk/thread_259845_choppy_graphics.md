### Post by WalmartSniperLX on 2006-09-17
See if you have an ATI or Nvidia graphics card, you can install the drivers very simply by using the synaptic package manager. They may not be the latest drivers but they work well. Just open the manager and search for 'nvidia' or 'ati' (whichever you have) and read the discriptions on your results. Whatever ever one mentions being a driver, get it. If you have an older card (nvidia) get the legacy driver
