### Post by p_quarles on 2007-06-21
MP3s are a proprietary format, and therefore Ubuntu does not include codecs for them by default. You can get info for enabling them (and other things) here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)
