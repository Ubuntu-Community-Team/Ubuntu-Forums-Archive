### Post by dmizer on 2006-10-02
couple of things could be going on here.

first of all, i'm not entirely sure that winme will work in qemu.  so your choice of guest operating systems is probably questionable.  i'm sure that win98 will work.  i've personally used win2000 in the past, and there's plenty of howto's for xp, but i really don't know about me.  if i recall correctly, me can't see the cdrom drive correctly because of how qemu emulates the cdrom drive.

anyway, you're better off using vmware now anyway.  it's faster and much easier to configure because it's a gui interface.

you can use this howto here: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=183209&highlight=howto+vmware](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=183209&highlight=howto+vmware)
just because it's written for xp doesn't mean you can't adapt it to me or any other os you desire.
