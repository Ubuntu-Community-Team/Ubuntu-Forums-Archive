### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-05-28
> **edgarpole said:**
> My question is - if I purchase a linux compatible PCMCIA wifi card for my laptop (a TP-LINK TL-WN510G for example) will I be able to  access the internet on my laptop via the existing Airport Express network?


Compatibility is required in stages:
The PCMCIA card must be compatible with your laptop (highly likely)
The PCMCIA card must be compatible with Linux / Ubuntu (reasonably likely - but may require work for some)
The PCMCIA card must be compatible with the Airport Express (highly likely)

So, in short, if the card is Ubuntu compatible - the answer should theoretically be yes.  Unfortunately, computers being what they are, the only way to truly find out is by trying.
