### Post by MHlavsa on 2006-08-24
I think what you're talking about is the starter bar in OSX where you click the icon, it bounces and opens.  You can come close, as far as I know you can only have icons to start, you can't have the right side of that where it shows what's open.  If you install gDesklets, under Starter Bar you can put that onto your desktop.  You also have to add gDesklets to start when Ubuntu loads.
