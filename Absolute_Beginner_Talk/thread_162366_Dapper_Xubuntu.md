### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-18
I don't think it matters whether you install Dapper and then add Xubuntu or just go straight for Xubuntu. Both will be the same in the end.

And, yes, if you keep apt-get updating and apt-get dist-upgrading, you'll be beta before you know it.

Lastly, I always admire new users who want to get to know the terminal, but any apt-getting you can do in the terminal can also be done graphically through Synaptic Package Manager.
