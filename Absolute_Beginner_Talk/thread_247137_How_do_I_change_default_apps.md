### Post by bruce89 on 2006-08-30
To open a file in another app, just right click on it and use Open With.  You can change the default with right clicking>Properties>Open With.

### Post by benfindlay on 2006-08-30
I you download a torrent file anywhere on your pc (Desktop is good), then right click on it and choose "properties". In there, choose the "open with" tab, and from there you can click "Add" and it will display a list of apps on your pc.

pick the app you want, and click OK, then on the "open with" tab, click on the little circle next to the icon of the prog in question to set it as default.

hope this helps!

### Post by John.Michael.Kane on 2006-08-30
To change default app:
Right click on the file/torrent. Select "Properties" and choose the "Open With" tab. select the program you want the file open with.
