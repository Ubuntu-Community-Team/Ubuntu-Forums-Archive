### Post by ubuntu-freak on 2008-02-25
Go to Places>Computer and click on it. Then on the left will be the Windows partition with whatever name/label it has, click on it and enter your Ubuntu password. Navigate to your documents and settings folder, then your account folder. Your desktop folder should be in there along with your files, just copy or move them to your Ubuntu home folder.
