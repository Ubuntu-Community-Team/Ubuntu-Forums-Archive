### Post by Brunellus on 2006-09-22
subject line changed to better reflect help request.  If you're posting here, we presume you're a new user.  Help us help you by using a subject line that lets users who know what help you need to give you the help you need.

Your biggest problem is likely going to be the wireless network card.  My advice would be to use the live CD (aka "DESKTOP" CD), boot that in your T20, and see if it works. That way you will have an idea of what works and what doesn't without changing anything on your computer.

There are certain quirks on the T20, though.  I would NOT, for example, instruct ubuntu take over the whole hard disk, lest you lose the hibernation partition.
