Like any other piece of software, anything can go wrong  when in the real world (especially when your not OSX which only needs to work on apple hardware/software). From all things I have heard though the Migration assistant has been very helpful for people (I myself used the alternate CD from the beginning so I never used it) and has been for the most part reliable (have heard a few cases of it failing to move data). I'd say it's highly recommended to back up all your vital data, if you have lots of DVDs I'd just back everything up. Then should your drive ever fail you'll have a spare back up for a while.

Oh and I'm fairly sure it will work with spanish, the assistant isn't dependant on language to find the files I don't believe...

Hope it all works out and have fun with Ubuntu :)
