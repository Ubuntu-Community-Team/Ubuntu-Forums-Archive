### Post by doctapeppa on 2008-01-18
An old compaq will almost certainly not have anything in the bios that will allow you to overclock your processor, Hell, I don't even think the newer ones do.. Some older computers had jumpers to overclock them and some you would have to solder either the actual processor or some points on the motherboard. 

What is it exactly that you are trying to do with this computer? What kind of file server and what flavor of linux are you putting on it?
