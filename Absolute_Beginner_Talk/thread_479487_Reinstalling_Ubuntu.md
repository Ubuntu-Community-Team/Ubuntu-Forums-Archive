### Post by finer recliner on 2007-06-20
when your going through the ubuntu installation, eventually you will get to a screen that will ask you which partition you want to install to. choose the partition that has your current ubuntu OS installed to and check the reformat box. you will lose any data that was on that partition. your other partitions shouldnt be harmed. this will effectivally overwrite your current installation.

and of course match up your swap partition and other linux related partitions you may have made.
