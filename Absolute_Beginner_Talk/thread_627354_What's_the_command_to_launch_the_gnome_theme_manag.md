### Post by erfahren on 2007-11-30
for future purposes, you can easily find the command for items in the menu by going to the menu editor (System > Preferences > Main Menu), right-click on the item in the right pane and select "Properties".
(Or just right-click on the item in the menu to create a launcher to it and look at the properties for it).
