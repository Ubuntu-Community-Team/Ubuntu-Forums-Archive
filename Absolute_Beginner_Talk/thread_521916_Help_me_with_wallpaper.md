### Post by kyphi on 2007-08-10
Perhaps you have too many files stored and that is why they keep disappearing.

If you want to find the file where they are stored, under 'Places' in your menu on the top panel you will find a 'Search for Files' icon, set it to 'Look in Folder'  and search the 'File System'.

Cheers
