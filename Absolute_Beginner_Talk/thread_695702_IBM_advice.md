### Post by sigge on 2008-02-13
The service partition usually contains the install-media for windows for your computer. Lieve it lying there, to restore your computer to windows if you don't find Ubuntu/Linux good enough.

It is also likely that your grub will recognise this partition as an OS, and hence allow you to boot from as usual, by selecting the right menu item. :)
