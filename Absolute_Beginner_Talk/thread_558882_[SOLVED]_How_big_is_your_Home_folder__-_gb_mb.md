### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-09-24
Not quite sure what your question is really asking or what the reason is for asking it.  Everybody's home folder will be a very different size depending on what they have stored there.  Mine is 21.8 GB as a result of thousands of photos and several videos and music files, plus all the usual Open Office docs, etc etc, all of which quickly add up to a very large size.

If you want to make a separate home partition, allow up to 10GB, maybe a bit less, for root, and let the remainder of the available size be for /home.
