### Post by Xian on 2006-03-11
From your current panel: Right-click > Add New Panel
A new panel should appear that the top of the screen.

From the top panel: Right-click > Add To Panel > Window List

From the bottom panel: Right-click on task bar divider > Remove From Panel

You can drag panels around with left button, and you can drag panel items with middle mouse button. If something doesn't move, right-click it, and unselect 'lock to panel' & try again. If you don't have a wheel mouse/3-button mouse you can right-click an item and select 'move'.

edit: 'task list' is called Window List, 'systray' is Notification Area. Menu Bar is normal gnome menu with Applications/Places/System, and Main Menu combines all three to one single icon.
