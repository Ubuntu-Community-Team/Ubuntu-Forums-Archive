### Post by u.b.u.n.t.u on 2006-06-18
Click on any image. "Eye of Gnome" is the default image viewer.

Click the tab at the top "Image" and then click "Set As Wallpaper".

Edit: You cannot layer your wallpaper, if that is what you are asking. You will need to make a new wallpaper with Gimp.
