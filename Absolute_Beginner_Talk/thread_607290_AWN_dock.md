### Post by Barge on 2007-11-08
Assuming you've already added the 'Launcher/Taskmanager' to applets as mivo suggested (which had me tricked a while), I was having a similar problem with certain launchers, Terminal comes to mind... I'd set them up in the Launchers panel and get nothing on AWN. 

I found that by grabbing Terminal (or others) from their apps menu and either dragging and dropping if they would agree to be dragged or right click "Add this launcher to panel" and then dragging from the new panel entry if they would not that it would work for me. The app would show up on both the bar and the launchers menu from where I could move it where I wanted it. Then you can remove it from the gnome panel again...

### Post by mivo on 2007-11-08
