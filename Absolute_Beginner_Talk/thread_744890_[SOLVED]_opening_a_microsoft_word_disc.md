### Post by tamoneya on 2008-04-04
Ubuntu will be able to handle anything your teacher throws at you.  PDF -> evince.  .doc -> Open Office.  The only thing that may give you a bit of trouble is docx, the new microsoft format that is the default in office 2007.  If you get that then you should first ask for it in a more standard format and second you can get it converted with a variety of online conversion services.  the chances of docx though are rather small.
