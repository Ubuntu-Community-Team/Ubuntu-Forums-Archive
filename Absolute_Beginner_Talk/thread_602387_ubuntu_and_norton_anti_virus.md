### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-11-04
If you are concerned about viruses then you can be very happy with Linux since Windows viruses do not work on Linux and there are virtually no viruses for Linux. So unless you dual-boot Windows, it really is just a waste of resources to use an AV guard on Linux. By the way, Norton does not have a Linux version and anyway is a bloaty AV guard which would just gobble up your resources for no reason.
