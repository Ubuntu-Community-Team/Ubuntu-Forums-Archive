### Post by riven0 on 2007-01-31
Can you make sure you selected the right sized paper for your printer? Try going to System >> Administration >> Printer. Then right-click on your printer and choose Properties, and under the Paper section, make sure you've got A4 selected. Also, check the PageRegion under the Advanced section and try printing out a test page.
