### Post by thefoolisme on 2007-04-28
I can't believe he was running XP on only 256mb :-)  It ultimately depends on what he uses his system for.  My guess is that he's not a hard-core gamer, or video/audio/graphic editor, or else he would have had to upgrade the RAM previously.  Based on the assumption that he uses his pc for the internet, email, and document preparation, he should be fine.  The OS doesn't require all of the extra RAM, the need for greater speed when playing games, or manipulating graphics is really what calls for extra memory.
