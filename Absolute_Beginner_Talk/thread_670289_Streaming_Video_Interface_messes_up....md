### Post by toddward on 2008-01-17
Sounds like you might be having an issue with your flash plugin install.  If you can, try reinstalling the plugin for your browser.  I'm assuming the fan on your computer is speeding up due to increased CPU usage...which might be because the plugin isn't operating correctly.

The 'repos' basically have the varying software titles available for apt-getting.  I won't go into the specifics of it.  If someone asked you to add a repo to your system, the easiest way is to do it is to go through System-> Administration -> Software Sources.  Under the tab *third-party software* you should be able to add your repo there.
