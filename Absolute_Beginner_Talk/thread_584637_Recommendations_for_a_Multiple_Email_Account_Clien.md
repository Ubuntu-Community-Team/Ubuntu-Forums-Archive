### Post by Romin-1 on 2007-10-21
Thunderbird! It is included in your distro. You can have mutiple accounts including imap.

On the server settings screen click on Advanced and check "Inbox for this account". You can tell it to save sent mails in the accounts Sent Folder.

T-bird has no calendar though. Maybe an extension.
