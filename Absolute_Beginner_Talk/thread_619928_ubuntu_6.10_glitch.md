### Post by jfinkels on 2007-11-22
When in doubt, you can restart the X window system by pressing <Ctrl>+<Alt>+<Backspace> (this will log you out of your current session). If you need to get to a terminal, the virtual terminals can be accessed by pressing <Ctrl>+<Alt>+<F1> (or F2 through F6). The graphical window system can be accessed again after that by pressing <Ctrl>+<Alt>+<F7>.
