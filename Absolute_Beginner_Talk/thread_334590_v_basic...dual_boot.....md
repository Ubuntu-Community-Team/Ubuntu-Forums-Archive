### Post by Rhubarb on 2007-01-09
Please be careful and pay attention when you're installing Ubuntu.
Make sure you defragment your drive (in windows) first, then when it's time to install Ubuntu, make sure the "Resize existing partition" is selected (I think it's step 5 of 6 in the installer).
Then everything should be ok.

I also recommend you backup all your documents in windows before installing Ubuntu, otherwise you may well end up in this situation:
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=334542](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=334542)
