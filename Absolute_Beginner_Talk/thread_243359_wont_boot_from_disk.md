### Post by meng on 2006-08-24
Your BIOS may not be set to check the CDROM drive first before the hard drive. The key to edit BIOS at startup varies from machine to machine, could be F1, F2, DEL, ESC. You may see a quick message flash up immediately the machine is turned on, telling you which key to press.
