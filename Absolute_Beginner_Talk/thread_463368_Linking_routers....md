### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-06-03
I believe [this is what your looking for.]("http://www.dd-wrt.com/wiki/index.php/Wireless_Bridge")

It's written for DD WRT firmware, but I think most routers support this. Follow it carefully. If you do find you need to install the DD WRT firmware (cuz your router's firmware lacks the support) [here is the wiki for it.]("http://www.dd-wrt.com/wiki/index.php/Installation") Read it carefully.... flashing firmware can turn routers into bricks (the useless kind) if done wrong.

Best firmware I've ever put on my routers, I'm happy with it :).

I do agree with Blackened, the ideal solution is to have cards for all comps and a dedicated router. Choice is up to you what you want to do.
