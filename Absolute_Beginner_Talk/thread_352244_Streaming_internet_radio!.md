You can find StreamTuner in the repositories or use Synaptic. It is specifically for tuning internet radio stations and I believe will open channels in XMMS by default.

Also, you may or may not be aware that rhythmbox has a radio option as well on the left side. I am partial to [SomaFM]("http://somafm.com/") (which will give you the option to open with XMMS). Have fun!
