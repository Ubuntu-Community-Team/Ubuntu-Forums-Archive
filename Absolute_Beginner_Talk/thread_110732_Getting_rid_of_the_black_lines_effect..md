### Post by prizrak on 2005-12-31
The lines are drawn by your Window Manager (WM) which in Gnome is called Metacity. The only way is to replace the WM itself. Customization section of the forum has guides on how to replace WM's and there are a few to choose from. The most popular one on Gnome is Openbox, for eyecandy Enlightenment is the best, there are a few others that can be used. Poke around the forum see what stuff they got since it's a bit different for different WMs.
