### Post by stoodleysnow on 2007-06-18
Go to system, preferences, keyboard and check the typematic rate setting (keyboard repeat rate or letter repeater or something like that) if it is switched off... switch it on. If it is on and you still can't get it to work, try checking your PC's bios (press del or f2 to enter setup at start). There could be a disabled typematic rate setting in there. When you've reactivated it, press f10 and select yes, then press enter. The pc reboots. See if you have fixed it then.:)
