### Post by FreewareFan on 2008-03-15
If you are hearing a thud sound, it is the result of some mechanical movement.  There are only two possibilities, to my knowledge, that will produce a thud.  One is your hard drive, or one of your hard drives if you have more than one.  The second possibility is a cdrom drive or floppy drive if you have one.  

Can you be more specific about where the noise is coming from?
