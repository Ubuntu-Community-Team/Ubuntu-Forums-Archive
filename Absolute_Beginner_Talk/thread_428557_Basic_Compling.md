### Post by annda on 2007-05-01
hi!

i believe you were a little confused by the format themes are distributed in: tar. it is just an archive, meaning a bunch of files put together with the directory structure preserved.

source files (that must be compiled) are archived as .tar too, because they need to preserve the directory order.

if some of you themes are not working correctly, it is most likely that you need the right GTK engines for them. a lot of those are installable from the repositories via apt-get/aptitude or synaptic. look for gtk-engines-* and gtk2-engines-*
