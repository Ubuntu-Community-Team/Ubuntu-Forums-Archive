### Post by timcredible on 2007-11-16
is it dsl?  if so, browse to 192.168.1.1 and see if you get to the modem.  if that works, you may have to enter a username/password (might have to do a google search to see what the default is), and then you may have to enter your virgin media username/password, which virgin media will have to tell you what it is.  if it's not dsl, just ignore all this, but this is what i had to do to get my verizon dsl to work.  the windows cd that they give you just puts a bunch of ads on your pc, changes your homepage, etc.  it's basically a virus that also does 30 seconds of configuring on your router.
