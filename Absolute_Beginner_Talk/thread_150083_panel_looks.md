### Post by predaeus on 2006-03-25
As a start you can right-click anywhere on the panel to get the pull-down-menu with "Add to Panel...". "Properties" and so on. Then select "Properties" from that menu and after that the "Background" tab in the appearing menu. If you tick "Solid Color" instead of "None (Use System Theme)" and pick white in the color box, then you get quite the result shown in your screenshot.

If this is not sufficient, you might want to look for another theme at [http://art.gnome.org/](http://art.gnome.org/) or [www.gnome-look.org/](www.gnome-look.org/) or somewhere else.
