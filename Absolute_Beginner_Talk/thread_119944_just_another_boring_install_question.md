### Post by az on 2006-01-20
Boot the installer
Let it detect your hardware and stuff
It will ask you about partitioning, pick "manual partitioning"
pick your thrid partition (the one you mentioned)
delete it
it will then show you two partitions and FREE SPACE
scroll down to "guided partitioning"
It will suggest an ext3 partition and a swap, made out of the free space
Say yes, make it go.
Go make yourself a coffee (or any beverage of your choice.  You can omit the beverage in favor of a snack or a nice phone call to a friend.  Whatever.)
