### Post by ridgeland on 2007-08-23
Just another comment.
With my old Gateway qarecord recorded what I was listening to.  No problem.
With my new Dell-Ubuntu qarecord would never record what I was listening to.  
My solution was to take the sound card out of the Gateway and put in the Dell.  This gave meant Ubuntu used a different driver and now its fine.
The Dell has on-board sound, which I disabled in the BIOS settings.  The card I used was just a Sound-Blaster Audigy (from around 2002).
