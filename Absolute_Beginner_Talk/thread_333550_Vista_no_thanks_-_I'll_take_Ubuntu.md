### Post by getaboat on 2007-01-07
After getting fed up with trying to sort out a recurring fail to load W98 PC I overwrote it with Unbuntu.

That was over a week ago. Since then everything I have tried just works, Easy load, easy to sort out resolution probs with the Voodoo 3dfx card, straight on the internet with a wired network, mail worked straight away, there are my Windows PCs (Wk2 Pro, XP Home, W98) (not XP-Pro yet but that sits outside my home network) - the shared drives and printers just work.  Installing software with synaptic a doddle - and software updates just work.

When I have had problems (minor) these forums heve been great even with my thin Unix knowledge.

I dabbled with Ubuntu some 18 months ago but could not commit to it, Now I am amazed at how usable and stable it is as a real desk top PC and not just something to be played with. Big changes ahead for how I organise my personal computing. 

Next tasks are VPN (a la Cisco), wireless, webcam, proper multi user set up and backups (- so I may be back with some problems (!) - but until then a *big* thanks to all of you who have made Ubuntu what it is today.

Getaboat
