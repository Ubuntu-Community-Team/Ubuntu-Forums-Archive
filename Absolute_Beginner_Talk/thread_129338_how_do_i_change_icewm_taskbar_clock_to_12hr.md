### Post by byen on 2006-02-13
what you need is "icepref" program available in synaptic.
After Install Run icepref>Format>Clock time format> I think you might want %X or %I:%M:%S
Where %I denotes the hour in a 12 hour format.
Hope that helps. Goodluck.
