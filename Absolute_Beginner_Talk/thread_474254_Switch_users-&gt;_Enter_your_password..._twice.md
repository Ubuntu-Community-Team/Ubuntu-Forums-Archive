### Post by louieb on 2007-06-17
Try using Ctrl+Alt+F7 to switch to the 1st user. Ctrl+Alt+F8 to switch back to the second user. Will have to enter password the 1 time you switch back. Then password-less switching after that. Works great in Dapper. My PC sometimes will lock up when switching this way in Feisty.
