### Post by kidders on 2006-09-11
A set of utilities called BlueZ is the place to start. Have a read, and see what you think!

Once the basic bluetooth infrastructure is up and running, there are various phone-related apps you can download, such as gnokii, many of which are in Ubuntu's package database :-)
