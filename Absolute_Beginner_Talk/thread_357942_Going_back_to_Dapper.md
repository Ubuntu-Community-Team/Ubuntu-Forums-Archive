### Post by jml on 2007-02-10
By design Dapper was designed to be a more stable distro than Edgy.  That was felt to be necessary sind Dapper was designed to have Long Term Support (the LTS in its name) Edgy's name was chosen intentinally to acknowledge the fact that it was going to use more cutting edge, (and therefore, potentially more unstable) applications.  Without specific details, I can't offer an opinion as to the cause of the crash.  

But to answer your question, yes you can reinstall Dapper, but I would recommend doing a clean install, rather than "downgrading" to etch.  So back up whatever data you don't want to loose and simply reinstall Dapper over edgy.

Joe
