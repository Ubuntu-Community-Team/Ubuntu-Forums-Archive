### Post by mssever on 2006-10-18
I don't know how much effort HP puts into Linux compatibility in their non-printer products, but I've never had problems with HP hardware in Linux. (OK, the Broadcom wireless card in my HP laptop took quite a bit of work to get working, but my friend's newer HP laptop had working wireless out of the box.)

I do know that HP dumps quite a bit of money into Linux development. And at one point, HP decided to offer Linux pre-installed on some of their computers in Europe. I don't know if they're still doing that.

At any rate, since I've had good success with HP stuff working in Linux, and since HP supports Linux development, I try to buy HP hardware whenever practical.
