### Post by logos34 on 2008-01-13
yeah, Xubuntu or the like...you need a lightweight wm like fluxbox or xfce with those specs.  ~384 mb ram should be plenty, but you'll need more if you want to run desktop effects with ubuntu. (and a new video card).

Personally, I'd run it as is without sinking any more money into graphics or ram. (but that means no compiz-fusion cube stuff). On the other hand, for around $100 you could get a new processor and mobo (with things like ethernet and nice integrated graphics, and possibly hd-audio).  Look for combo deals at newegg.  I'm sure you could find a stick of 512 mb ram on rebate sale for ~$30usd.
