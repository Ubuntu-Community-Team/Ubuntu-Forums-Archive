### Post by bumanie on 2008-04-15
You can recover files via a live cd and copy them to another drive. To retrieve your password you need to boot in safe mode and type > passwd into terminal, then you can change it.

---

### Post by master5o1 on 2008-04-15
another way is to boot the liveCD, press CTRL ALT f1 to open the tty1 and from their mount your drive.

Chroot to your mount point and run passwd there.
