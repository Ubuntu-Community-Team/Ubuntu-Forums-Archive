### Post by oilchangeguy on 2008-02-19
life is short. don't waste alot of time trying to get a modem to work. most distros of linux don't play well with internal modems. if you must have dial-up, i'd suggest getting an external serial modem. ebay would be a good place to start your search. also if it's available in your area, cable, or dsl is a much better choice than dial-up. most cable and phone companies have several plans available. with pricing at or near that of dial-up. but many times faster.
