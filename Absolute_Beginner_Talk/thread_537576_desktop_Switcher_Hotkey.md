### Post by JimmyBEng on 2007-09-07
Take a look in the System->Preferences->Keyboard Shortcuts menu, like xtapolapaktl suggested.  He just wasn't thinking of the particular shortcut entry you were.

The entries you are interested in are in the Window Management Section:
-Switch to workspace on the left (default is Ctrl+ Alt + Left)
-Switch to workspace on the right (default is Ctrl+ Alt + Right
-Switch to workspace above this one (default is Ctrl+ Alt + Up)
-Switch to workspace below this one (default is Ctrl+ Alt + Down)
