### Post by solitaire on 2008-04-16
Sounds like a short is causing things to Heat up. Or really bad cooling design..

As it's an Acer i'd suspect it's a bad design of the cooling system. But a short might still e the case.

There's not a lot you can do apart from opening the Laptop up and making sure there is not Dust/ Gunk/ Fluff around the cooling bits... Also check that the fans are running.

You should be able to install a prog to check the system temps. Laptops should run between 40c (with no load just sitting Idle) - 65c (CPU at 100% running a 3D game)
with an average temp of 55c when surfing and watching Movies. (well that's what my old Dell Insppiron 8200 runs at).
