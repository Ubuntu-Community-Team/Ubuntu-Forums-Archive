### Post by aidanr on 2006-12-22
ok, in the network list, scroll down to "**freenode**", select it and hit "**edit**", check "**auto connect to this network at startup**", and in "**channels to join**" type in "**#ubuntu**", then hit "**close**" and then "**connect**"

all without the quotes by the way


also there a [_quick start guide_]("http://www.xchat.org/docs/start/") on the xchat homepage
