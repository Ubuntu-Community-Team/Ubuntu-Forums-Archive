### Post by alexm on 2006-06-14
Hey guys/grrlz -  

If you would like some free, one-on-one, real time help with Ubuntu issues, I'm now listed on the [Ubuntu area of Qunu]("http://alpha.qunu.com/search/ubuntu"). 

These guys have a new twist on the old free ask-an-expert service - they pop up help requests from their site directly in the Jabber-friendly IM client of experts like me, which makes it super convenient to provide real time support.

FYI, from the site: "Qunu uses Jabber instant messaging to connect -- in real time -- people who have software or tech-related questions with experts who are passionate and willing to help."

Anyway, so I'm hanging out in the [Ubuntu]("http://alpha.qunu.com/search/ubuntu") area .. feel free to rock on over and request a chat. And if I'm not there, there are most likely going to be other experts online available to help with your question immediately. See you there!

If you're a passionate Ubuntu expert and have a jabber friendly IM client, then maybe consider donating a help session or two as well? You tag yourself with your areas of expertise and go from there. 

You can read more at their site: alpha.qunu.com

[http://alpha.qunu.com/search/ubuntu](http://alpha.qunu.com/search/ubuntu) !

Alex
