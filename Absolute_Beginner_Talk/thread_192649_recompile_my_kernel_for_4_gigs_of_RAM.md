### Post by Dragonfire on 2006-06-09
Okay, have you ever flashed your kernel before? or created a back up copy?

it would be best to flash your system's kernel, then recompile it. and should the kernel be damaged, you can repair it from a live cd, or with dapper the new Live/Install CD.

You just move the backed up kernel to the damaged kernels place, reviving your system, and allowing you to try it again.
