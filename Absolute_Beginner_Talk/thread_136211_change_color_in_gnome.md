### Post by gunksta on 2006-02-25
I'm not sure what you mean, but let me see if I can help.

The color of the panels (the ones at the top and bottom) are initially set by the gtk theme.  The background color of the "Applications", "Places", and "System" are also determined by the gtk theme.

To change the color of the panels themselves, right click on the panel and go to propertis -> Bakcground.  There you can select color or a background image.

To change the color of the actual menus, you will need to change your gtk theme.  Go to System-> Preferences -> Themes to do this.  By default you won't have too many installed.

You can get more themes via synaptic or:

[http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/)

[http://art.gnome.org/](http://art.gnome.org/)
