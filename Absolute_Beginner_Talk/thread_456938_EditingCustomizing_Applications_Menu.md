### Post by stevezygote on 2007-05-28
Is there any way to edit or customize the Ubuntu Applications menu, or other menu items? I have a HUGE Applications Menu and would like to group the programs by category. Thanks.

---

### Post by compmodder26 on 2007-05-28
Right click the applications menu and select "Edit Menus"

edit: Alternatively go to System->Preferences->Main Menu

---

### Post by ep2011 on 2007-05-28
Yes, as compmodder26 said, you can right click the applications menu and click "Edit Menus". This will open the menu editor, and you can easily group things, hide things from showing, and even add new menus.
