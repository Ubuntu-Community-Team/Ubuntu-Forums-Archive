### Post by indytim on 2007-07-04
One (of many) approaches is to install and use Partimage.  It's in the repositories.  It will allow you to take an image of your ops partition.  It includes capability to restore your partiion.  It runs from a shall invoked through the terminal. Hint do the $sudo partimage to launch.
