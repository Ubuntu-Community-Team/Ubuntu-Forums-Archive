### Post by *Zebedee* on 2007-10-10
Hi JW  :-)

Try this my friend..

Position your cursor on an empty bit of bar where 'applications' etc used to live.
Left click mouse button.
Select 'add to panel'.
Scroll down and click left mouse button on 'main menu' and 'menu bar' to highlight them.
Click right mouse button on 'add'.
You should now have 'applications' etc back where they belong!  :-)

Hope this helps.  :-)
