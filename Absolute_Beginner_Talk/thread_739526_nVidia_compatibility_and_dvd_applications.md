### Post by ggaaron on 2008-03-29
NVidia releases only binary, not free drivers, so it is not awesome. It's binary driver is better than ATI's (at least less people seem to report problems with it), but ATI released it's documentation and free drivers are being developed from some time. So my answer to your question - NVidia's GNU/linux support isn't a dream, but it shouldn't work worse than on Windows.
