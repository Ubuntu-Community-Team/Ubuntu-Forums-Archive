### Post by prcm311 on 2007-03-14
Right click the volume icon next to the date/time and open volume control and then edit >> preferences.  Add in sub or surround controls, you'll notice they are all the way down, turn em up.  This worked for me and my Audigy 2 card.  Enable Surround (front, rear), Center (center), and LFE (sub).
