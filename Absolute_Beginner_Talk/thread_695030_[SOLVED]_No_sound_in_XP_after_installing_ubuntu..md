### Post by zozobra on 2008-02-12
Why we're supporting Windows on the Ubuntu forums, I don't know but.....

Boot into Safe Mode using F8 on startup. Right Click My Computer and choose Manage. Go to device manager. Go to Sound Video and Game Controllers. Find the driver for your sound card. Right Click, Uninstall. Reboot Windows. Download and install the correct driver for your sound card. If you continue to have issues, you may have IRQ issues.
