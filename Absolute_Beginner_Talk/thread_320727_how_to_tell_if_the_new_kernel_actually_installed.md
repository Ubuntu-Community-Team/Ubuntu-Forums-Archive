### Post by Travisty on 2006-12-17
type [COLOR="Red"]uname -a[/COLOR] in your command line. This will tell you your kernel version and the date it was installed.  

example:
travisty@kyoto:~$ uname -a
Linux kyoto 2.6.17-10-generic #2 SMP Tue Dec 5 22:28:26 UTC 2006 i686 GNU/Linux
