### Post by Monster_user on 2006-04-11
You downloaded, and installed it with Synaptic.

Now, run 'winecfg' to set it up.
Press Alt-F2m then type it in, and press Enter.

Don't worry about most of the settings, just check to make sure that a C: is under drives, and that it detected your CD/DVD drive (D: ).

Then go to the sound section, and run "Autodetect".

Finally, find any Windows *.exe, and right-click on it. Open it with 'wine'. Your done.
