### Post by omegamike3 on 2007-07-22
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper)

there's the good ol' documentation. the key, once you get it ndiswrapper up and running is finding the right .inf and .sys files for your card. but if you had windows installed, then it shouldn't be too hard for you to find them, since you'll probably know where to download them. once you've downloaded the installer for windows, just rip open the executable with a package handler and the files you're looking for should be inside, might just take a little work to figure out which are the right ones to use ;)
