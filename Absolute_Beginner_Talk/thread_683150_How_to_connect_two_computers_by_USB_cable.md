"Someone" sounds like they don't know what they are talking about.

If you want to get data off a PC that doesn't even boot up, you will have to remove the hard drive and connect it to a working PC (you can get adaptors to connect 2.5" laptop drives to "normal" 3.5" hard disk interfaces).

If your laptop is that far gone you should take it to someone who knows what they are doing, otherwise you are not going to get very far.

If the laptop is booting, but the screen is broken, you should try connecting an external monitor to it and seeing if it is actually working.
