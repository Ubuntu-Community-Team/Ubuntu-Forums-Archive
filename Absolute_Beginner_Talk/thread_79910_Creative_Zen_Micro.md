### Post by LHZ on 2005-10-21
There is a program to get Creative players working under linux, it's called Gnomad2 (you can get it through Synaptic). It's been reverse engineered though, so you'll miss some functunality. 

If I were you, I'd do complex stuff (like making playlists) on Win XP, but you can move music around without problems through Gnomad.
