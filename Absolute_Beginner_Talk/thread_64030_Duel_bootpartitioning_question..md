### Post by wvslkr on 2005-09-09
It is doubtful that you have two drives.  The main drive is probably partitioned to hold the recovery partition.  
If you wipe that partition you will not be able to reinstall without cd's.  There may also be distributer specific software that you would have to track down to get the same functionality back. 

I would recommend shrinking the c and repartitioning.
