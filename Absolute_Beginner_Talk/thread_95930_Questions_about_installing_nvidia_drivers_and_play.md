### Post by super on 2005-11-27
[here is the link to the latest nvidia drivers howto thread.]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=75074")

as for the resolution you can set it in gnome by clicking system>>preferences>>resolution on the gnome-panel. if that doesn't work you can force the resolution used by editing /etc/X11/xorg.conf.

to play divx movies under linux you need the divx4linux codec. it is available at the divx.com site. i think it may also be in the universe repos.
