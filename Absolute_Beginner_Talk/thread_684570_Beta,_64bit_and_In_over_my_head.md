### Post by chewearn on 2008-02-01
If you are just playing around, instead of needing a stable system to work or play, then by all means, jump right in.  The best way to learn is to go into the internals, mess around and fix things.  But be prepared with an open mind and be willing to trash stuff if it breaks beyond repair.

But, if you need to get to the good useful stuffs right away, and don't want too much trouble, current Gutsy 7.10 is the way to go.  32-bit or 64-bit, my opinion is they are pretty much at the same level in terms of stability nowadays.

Welcome to the fast lane.):P
