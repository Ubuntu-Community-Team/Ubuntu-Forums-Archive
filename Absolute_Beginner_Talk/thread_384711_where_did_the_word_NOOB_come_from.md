### Post by caligula2day on 2007-12-15
Just to clear up one thing, noob and noobie have two different meanings. 
back in the late 80's and early 90's I remember playing games across a Telnet system, we would often refer to new people as newbies and we would call asshat lamers that couldn't get along with other people
nobies. As time moved on and TelNet dropped off the grid the two words moved on to a younger generation the true meanings got lost. Nobie used to stand for no body.
1. Player Asshat23 is a nobie, screw him we will go help the newbie.
some were down the line the younger generation changed the spelling and lost the true meaning of the two words.
