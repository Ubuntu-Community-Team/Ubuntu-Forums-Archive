### Post by Blutack on 2007-12-02
Click Places --> Connect to server...
Choose private FTP and enter your details.  An icon will appear on your desktop representing your FTP connection.  Click on it and you can then modify everything through nautilus.  The only caveat to be aware of is that you cannot directly edit and save things to ftp - you must first copy them to your computer, edit them, then copy them back.
