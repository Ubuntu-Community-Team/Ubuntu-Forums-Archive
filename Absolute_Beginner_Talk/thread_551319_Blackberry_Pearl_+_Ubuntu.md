### Post by aspen_dv on 2007-09-15
Ok I like my blackberry pearl (BP) and I love Ubuntu. Over last 2 weeks trying to load mp3 and photo to the phone thru VMWare. It sucks, the software that came with the phone sucks so bad. It's slow and annoying. 
I just found an simple way to do this, so just want to share with the community.
First, on your BP , assume that you have a media card installed.
Settings->Options->Advance Options->Media card: 
Mass Storage Mode: On
Auto Enable Mass Storage Support: Yes

Then connect to your computer and Ubuntu should regconize it just like a USB drive.
Now you can just transfer file back and forth to the appropriate folders.(music, photos, videos)

Finally, make sure you eject the drive the correct way: Right click->eject. It will finalize the transfer. (not just unplug the cable,  else your mp3 and photo won't play on the phone).

It's just simple but some people at work asked me today, so I thought I could share this.
Thanks
