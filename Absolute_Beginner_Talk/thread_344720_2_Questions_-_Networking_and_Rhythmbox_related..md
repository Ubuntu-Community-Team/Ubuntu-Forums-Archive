I only know mp3 solution, which is the easiest. You need codecs installed to play mp3s, DVDs, Xvid and whatever else. See this page for help [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)
You can also try installing EasyUbuntu or Automatics2. They can get the codecs and a lot more for you.
