### Post by Fully216 on 2006-12-13
Generally it is okay to bump when it has been a long time, not a few hours.  It makes you seem like you feel that only your problems are important or pressing.  And you are right, it is not okay for some to do it and not get chastised, but such is the forgiving nature of most people.

It just might be the possibility someone is trying to figure out how to help you and has had time to post.  Instead of waiting around, wouldn't you rather try to help yourself?

I have heard that installing XGL on Dapper and now Beryl on Edgy will help with the issue, making the screensavers work 10 times better (possible workaround).  It definitely has to do with the ATI proprietary fglrx drivers, being that ATI does not until recently even support linux.  

If you don't want to install beryl, I would try messing with your video setting, setting direct rendering to yes (assuming your graphics card is beffy enough).
