### Post by taurus on 2008-01-26
1.  You did change the boot order in the BIOS from harddrive first to CD-ROM first, right?

2.  You did burn the ISO image as an image, not a data?  In other words, if you look at the content of the CD in windows, you should see a bunch of files and directories, not just one large file.
