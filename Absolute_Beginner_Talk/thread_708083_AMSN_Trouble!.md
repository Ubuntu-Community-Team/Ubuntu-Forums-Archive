### Post by Claus7 on 2008-03-07
Hello,

I do not think that you should delete everything about amsn. I think that if you open a terminal and type 

```
cd .amsn
```

(the dot means that it is a hidden folder) you will find another folder that has the name of your email that you are using in amsn. There you can find your discussions as well as your pictures and also your friends pictures. Go there and delete the picture in question and see what will happen.

(In order to open a terminal Applications->Accessories->Terminal)

Another way will be to open your home folder and go to View and check the option Show Hidden Files (if you have ubuntu and gnome). Find then the .amsn folder. That way you can navigate more easily on the files you want.
