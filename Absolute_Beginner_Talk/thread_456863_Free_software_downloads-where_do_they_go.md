### Post by petersjm on 2007-05-28
Most apps you download through Synaptic will go into your menus (top left of screen). You can hit ALT+F2 and type "airstrike", which will start the app, assuming it's installed correctly. If it isn't in your menus, you can right-click on "Applications" and select edit menus, then create your own launcher for it.

### Post by Znupi on 2007-05-28
For installing new applications, it's better to use **Add/Remove...**, it can be found in **Applications->Add/Remove...**. Synaptic is better for installing plugins/packages/libraries. At least that's what I use them for :).
