### Post by me1on on 2006-11-01
Sign into your gmail account, click "Settings" (in the top right corner), click the "Forwarding and POP" tab, then check the "Enable POP for all mail (even mail that's already been downloaded)" box. Now go open Kmail and check for new mail. All the mail from your gmail account should be sent to Kmail (all read and unread messages). 

Hopefully that works, it worked for me. :)
