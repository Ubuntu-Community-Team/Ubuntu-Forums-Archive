### Post by aysiu on 2006-06-22
I don't think if it matters whether or not Ubuntu is easy to use. It's different, and that's what throws people off.

Some things that throw people off will be because of deficiencies in Ubuntu's interface (clipboard contents disappearing upon the source window closing, for example), but some will just be because of a different approach (installing software from the repositories instead of a setup.exe).
