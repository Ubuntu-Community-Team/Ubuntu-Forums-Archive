### Post by dfreer on 2007-08-12
You can't drag and drop mp3's onto a regular ipod (you might be able to with the ipod shuffle, I think). Even in windows you can't do this. You have to *sync* the files into it, using rthymnbox, amarok, or gtkpod. 
I'm not quite sure why rthymnbox is giving you that error, you might want to try one of the other players I mentioned above.
