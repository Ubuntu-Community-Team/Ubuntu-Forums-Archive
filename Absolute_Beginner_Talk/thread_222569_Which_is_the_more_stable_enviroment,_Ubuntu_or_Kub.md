### Post by T700 on 2006-07-25
Which is more stable, KDE or Gnome?  The answer is:

**"Yes"**

You're going to get tons of people posting in here about how, "Dude, I've had Gnome lock up on both my machines--KDE rulz!" and "Listen to me, I know since I've run Linux since 1946, Gnome never crashes 'cause KDE is just bloatware."  The real answer is, both are excellent desktops and it is 100% your personal choice.
