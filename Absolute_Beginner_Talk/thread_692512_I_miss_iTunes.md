### Post by wigglydiggly on 2008-02-09
I use gpodder.  Its a seperate application just for caching podcasts.  Works with both download and bittorrent feeds.  Uses other players for audio or video.  Syncs with ipods and flash-based players.  The one in repositories is a little old, but search for gpodder and download .deb for current version.  You might like it you might not.
