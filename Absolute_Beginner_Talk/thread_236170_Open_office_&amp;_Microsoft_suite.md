### Post by Bloch on 2006-08-14
Specifications for  .doc .xls formats of MS Office are not released: they are a trade secret. The programmers of OPenOffice have to reverse engineer from the documents to see how the formats work. They do an excellent job, but as you can imagine the "inner engine" for Word and Excel are huge - and have bugs too, of course. Creating a programme to behave exactly the same way in all circumstances is well nigh impossible.

I use OPenOffice for commercial translations and rarely have problems.
 What version of MS Excel do you have? Is there a choice to save in MS Office 97 version? I believe that is more compatible.

Here's a couple of links that examine compatibility issues. But bear in mind that compatibility is solved in the long term by using opendoc formats.
