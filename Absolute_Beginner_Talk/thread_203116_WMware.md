### Post by Sye d'Burns on 2006-06-24
I use vmware workstation and I'll agree, it works perfectly for windows xp though I prefer win2k for lighter system requirements. I'm sure vista would work on it as well, though I'm inclined to think the eye candy might not work so well with it (due to lack of good 3d support) and heavy ram requirements. I honestly can't speak to how well it'd work in vmware though.

It also works great for testing various linux (and yes) BSD distros. You can even download appliances (there are several bsd based appliances out there) to make your life even easier. 

All in all it's one of the niftiest things around.
