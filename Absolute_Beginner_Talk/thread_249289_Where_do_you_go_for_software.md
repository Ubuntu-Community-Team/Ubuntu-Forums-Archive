What programs do you have in mind because chances are they are available in the repos.  You just need to edit your /etc/apt/sources.list and remove the # in front of lines with universe & multiverse...  Then, just use apt-get/aptitude/synaptic to install the extra stuff!

```
gksudo gedit /etc/apt/sources.list
```
