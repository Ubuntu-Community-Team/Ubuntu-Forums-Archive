### Post by julian67 on 2007-10-28
I'm not sure how it's done in Gnome but in Xubuntu (Xfce) Settings Manager>Sessions and Start Up you can choose to have any KDE or Gnome services loaded at startup or not.  From memory I believe that in Gnome the KDE libs won't be loaded until you actually start an application or service that needs them.
