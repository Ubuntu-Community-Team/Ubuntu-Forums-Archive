### Post by akiro.yamamoto on 2006-03-25
Right click on the panel at the top of your screen.... select New Panel.
A new panel should appear... right click on it select Add To Panel....
Then add the following...
1) Show Desktop
2) Window List
3) WorkSpace Switcher
4) Trash
See my pic...
You can move the different components around on the panel, until your saticfied  ;)
