### Post by GhostZrydA on 2007-06-02
Try this link [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/10](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/10) which will take you to the firefox adblock install.

Then once installed you will have an adblock feature at the bottom righthand side of the browser, use that to block the flash ad from loading on that site when you visit it.
