Kerberos[/color][/size][/font]
An [authentication]("http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/K/authentication.html") system developed at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Kerberos is designed to enable two parties to exchange private information across an otherwise open [network]("http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/K/network.html"). It works by assigning a unique [key]("http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/K/key.html"), called a *ticket,* to each user that [logs on]("http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/K/log_on.html") to the network. The ticket is then embedded in messages to identify the sender of the message.
