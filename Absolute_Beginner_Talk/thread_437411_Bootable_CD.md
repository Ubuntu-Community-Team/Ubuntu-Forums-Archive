### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-08
> **ubu_n00b69 said:**
> How do you burn the ISO to a Bootable Disk using Dapper?
Thank You for your time.

The ISO (I assume Feisty CD) is a bootable disk, all you need is to burn a data disk. The Ubuntu folks were kind enough to image a disk that was already loaded with the bootable files :)

Just use whatever program you use every day... (gnomebaker, k3b, etc...) and make a data cd. Burn at a LOW speed... lots of people get errors when they do it too fast. 4 or 8 X whichever is your lowest.

That should be it, then drop it in and reboot. Have fun :)
