### Post by thelatinist on 2007-12-09
Check to see if your laptop is on the compatibility lists at[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam") and find out if there are any issues specific to your machine.  If you can boot from the LiveCD, play around with it and see if your fan works.

As for malicious commands, be sure not to copy and paste code you don't understand into terminal.  If you don't understand something, please ask: nobody on Absolute Beginner Talk will mind explaining exactly what commands do if you don't understand them.
