### Post by kingbilly on 2006-06-04
Hello my friend!  Same problem here, I always forget on a new install.

Press Alt + F2 to bring up the run dialog.
Type gconf-editor and press enter.
You probably know the rest, but just so you don't have to search through the options its
Apps>Nautilus>Desktop
