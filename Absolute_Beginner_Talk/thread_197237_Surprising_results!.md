### Post by klato on 2006-06-15
Well I just downloaded the Ubuntu 6.06 ISO yesterday and burned me a CD...and I was quite pleased with the results!  I rebooted with the live CD, and everything loaded without a hitch. I must say, that's a significant step up in other distros I've tried in the past (Red Hat, Mandrake, Knoppix)...most of the time these 2 items are messed up.  Now I just have to see if CD burning works, if my HP PSC 1410 works, and if I can access my iPod =) I've got a Dell Inspiron 4150 if anyone's interested (it's about 3 years old I think).

The only thing that bothered me was the fonts...they looked sort of low quality to me...kind of jagged edges everywhere (and my resolution was 1400x1050)

But anyways, just thought I'd report on a very good initial experience with Ubuntu. :)
