### Post by Hobbsee on 2005-10-05
you're going into bios and changing the boot order?

To get into bios, press ESC as you boot up the computer (ie press it from when you hit the power button).  After that, when it tells you, press F1.  

Then change your boot order to boot from cd-rom first, and save and reboot.

At least that's what you do on a Toshiba A10 Satellite - I doubt it would be terribly different on your machine...
