### Post by Rocket on 2006-11-10
**"[COLOR="Red"]Error while Storing folder Inbox.Summary and folder mismatch, even after a sync[/COLOR]**"

I to had been getting this error frequently. I haven't had this re-occur since I have changed each account to "manually send/receive mail". 

I realise this may not be suitable for everybody. 

For those who may be rusty, this is done by: *[COLOR="YellowGreen"]edit > preferences & select account > receiving options tab & deselect "automatically check for new email every 'x' minutes".[/COLOR]* Do this for each account that you have.

Hope this is of use,
