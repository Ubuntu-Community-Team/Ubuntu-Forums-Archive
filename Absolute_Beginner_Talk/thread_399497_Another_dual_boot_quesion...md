### Post by Foxmike on 2007-04-02
As for what I have read, resizing a partition is possible after several disk defragmentations (optimization) but it is still risky.  Backing up data and considering to make a clean re-install of windows would be good ideas.  I know that reading an NTFS partition is possible, though you will need reading and learning about how to manage partitions.  [Search for NTFS read/write]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/search.php?searchid=17320181") should give you answers about NTFS reading.  As for photos and music, as loing as you get proper CODECS, it is easy to share.
