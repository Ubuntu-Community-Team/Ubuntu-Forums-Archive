### Post by fineas on 2007-12-07
Go to System>preferences>keyboard> third tab. First option is "adding the eurosign to certain keys". I chose the E key.Then, there is an option "Third level choosers". I chose 'Press Right Ctrl to choose 3rd level".
With these settings, I get the eurosign symbol bey pressing (right Ctrl)+ E. Of course, you can have different settings; if you 're running compiz, avoid the combination Super+E or Alt+E, as they enable some compiz effects 

Good luck
