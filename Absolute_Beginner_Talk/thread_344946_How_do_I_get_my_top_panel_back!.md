### Post by Jamesbowden on 2007-01-23
Right click, click "add to panel" then you can pick what to add from there, i think by default the top panel has (in order, left to right.)

- Menu Bar
- Windows list
- Notification Area 
- Volume Control
- Clock
- quit.

that should set you up, just play around with things to add untill you like the look and feel of the panel.
