### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-03-01
> **silverhair said:**
> :confused:gutsy dapper etc what do they mean

As mentioned, they refer to Ubuntu versions.  The names are used to refer to the Ubuntu versions pre-release.  Lots of software companies use codenames pre-release (including Microsoft: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Windows_Vista](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Windows_Vista)).

Officially, you should use the numbers to refer to Ubuntu after release, but the names and numbers tend to be used interchangeably.  If you notice the ubuntu website does not refer to these names at all.

As a general rule of thunb, they are alphabetical (since Ubuntu 6.06 = Dapper)
