### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-15
Without a helper program (like Klipper), text copied to the clipboard will disappear once you close the source application.

For example, if you do this:
Open Adobe Reader.
Highlight text.
Copy text.
Close text.
Open GIMP.
Try to paste text.

You'll find there's no text to paste because you closed Adobe Reader.
