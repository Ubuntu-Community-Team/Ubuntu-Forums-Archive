### Post by falcon15500 on 2006-03-13
Is your modem internal or external?  If it is an internal card, chances are it is a winmodem.  Basically this is pseudo modem that uses software drivers to control its modulation functions.  They can be a bit of a pain to get working in GNU/Linux because the drivers are generally for win only.

Try this site for more info:  [http://www.linmodems.org]("http://www.linmodems.org")
