### Post by baeckel on 2008-02-19
This worked for my Ubuntu version:

1: Right click on the Panel that you want the "2 squares" (workspaces) to be on

2: Select "Add to Panel..."

3: In the 3rd section down you will see "Workspace Switcher", highlight it and click the now available "Add" button

4: Now you have "1 square" (workspace) on the panel, you can right click on this workspace, select preferences and edit the number and arrangement of the workspaces to your hearts content.
