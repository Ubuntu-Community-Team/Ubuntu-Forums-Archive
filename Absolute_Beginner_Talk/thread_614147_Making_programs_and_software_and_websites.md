### Post by eDRoaCH on 2007-11-15
actually re-reading your post again I am thinking you want to start off with ubuntu? If you dont have an extra pc and are afraid to take the plunge and format, google up 'wubi' it is a really excellent piece of software allowing you to dual boot to ubuntu without having to format or anything overly dangerous. Best of all if you break ubuntu you don't break windows, and you can just go an uninstall it from add/remove programs in windows.

Note it will run slightly slower than a "normal" ubuntu install.

There are also virtualization programs such as virtualbox that you can use to run ubuntu in a window on your desktop. The virtualization subforum here has lots of great tutorials for it.

wubi is probably the most dead-simple way though!
