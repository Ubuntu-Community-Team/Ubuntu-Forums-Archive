### Post by robinl on 2006-11-30
1. 184MB is a bit low for normal Ubuntu. You might want to try Xubuntu which is based on the Xfce desktop environment and have a lower meory requirement than Ubuntu which uses GNOME as the desktop environment.

2. Sound and video should work out of the box. Unless you want 3D acceleration (which a TNT don't have much compared to modern ones anyways) or have a wireless card you don't really need to install any driver on most cases.

3. Just try the LiveCD and see for yourself. Everything should just work out of the box.
