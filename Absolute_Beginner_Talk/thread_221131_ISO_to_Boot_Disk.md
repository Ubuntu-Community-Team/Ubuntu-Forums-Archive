### Post by richbarna on 2006-07-22
You need to burn the iso then boot from it.
To burn the iso to disk (I assume you are using windows) just right click on it, select "open with" and choose your burning software.(eg Nero)
Make sure that the burn speed is 4X or 8X if 4 isn't possible then click burn.
Once it is burnt, leave it in the cdrom tray and reboot. It should start to boot on startup.
If not, you may have to change your BIOS settings to boot from cdrom before looking for the C drive.
