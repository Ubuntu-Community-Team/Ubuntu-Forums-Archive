### Post by blakeg on 2007-10-13
RideP2, I also have this issue. I don't know exactly what causes it. i don't believe it has anything to do with the Microphone settings.

I have a SoundBlaster Live SE card and I use Alsa to configure it.

The only way I have seen to fix this issue is to run the sound level at 3/4 or below and use the sound levels on the speaker(s) to compensate for a lower volume.

Since using this work around, I have not heard any "canny" noise coming from my speakers.

BlakeG
