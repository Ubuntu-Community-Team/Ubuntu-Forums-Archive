### Post by meborc on 2007-07-04
so you are using a cd-rw? i have no experience with those...

but if you get a permission error, it usually means that either the location you try to copy to or from is not accessable with your user...

try ```
gksudo nautilus
```from termial, this will open nautilus with root privileges... probably the location you are trying to copy the files to is not owned by your user (some ntfs partition may be)
