### Post by Marsolin on 2006-09-04
[Denemo]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/denemo") is a Gtk front-end for [LilyPond]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/lilypond").

You can also try [NoteEdit]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/noteedit"), [Rosegarden]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/rosegarden4"), or [Brahms]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/brahms").
