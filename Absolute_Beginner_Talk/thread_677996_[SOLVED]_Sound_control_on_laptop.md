### Post by ynef on 2008-01-25
Bring up the mixer, and see if your buttons are manipulating the "Master" slider or "PCM" slider. Perhaps your sound card requires you to modify the latter, since I think the buttons normally only affects the former (and that clearly isn't working). Once this has been verified, perhaps we can find a solution.
