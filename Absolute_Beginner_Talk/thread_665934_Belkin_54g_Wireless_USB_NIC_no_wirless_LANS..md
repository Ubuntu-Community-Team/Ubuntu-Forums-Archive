### Post by anewguy on 2008-01-13
I'm going to assume a few things:

(1) When you say it works when you boot from the CD, I assume that means that your wireless works when you boot off the LiveCD

(2) When you say that when you install Ubuntu you mean you followed the installation procedure after you booted the LiveCD

Okay, now exactly what type of Belkin USB Wireless G adaptor are you using?

I have a Belkin USB Wireless G adapter that I bought at Wal-Mart and it works fine as long as you:

(1) Install ndiswrapper and it's utilities

(2) Use ndiswrapper to install the Windows version (not Vista - preferably XP) of the driver for the adapter



If you could clarify what you did to install ndiswrapper and then what you did to use it to try to get it working, I can help you from there.:)
