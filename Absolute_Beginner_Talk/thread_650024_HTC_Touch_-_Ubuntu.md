### Post by tienhn on 2008-07-24
I am using one of two way:
1/ If it is a simple task such as accessing the internal storage disk then I just plug my HTC Touch into my Ubuntu and then select "Disk" mode on the phone dialog. Then Ubuntu see it as an FAT32 drive. There I can manage all of my HTC files.

2/ For calendar, contact sync stuffs, I have VirtualBox installed so I can just turn on the USB port where the HTC plugged into and install ActiveSync from the HTC CD. The HTC must be in "Normal Serial mode", not "Advance USB mode".

Hope this helps.
