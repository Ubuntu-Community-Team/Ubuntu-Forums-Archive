### Post by bcrom on 2007-11-08
Go to System->Administration->Software Sources and check everything under "Downloadable from the Internet" and uncheck "CD-ROM with Ubuntu" if you don't have your installation CD in your drive.  Also check everything under the Third-Party Software tab and the Updates tab.  Do a  sudo apt-get update to update your sources.  Updates should now install.
