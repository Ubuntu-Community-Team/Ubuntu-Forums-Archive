### Post by Dr Small on 2008-01-04
CTRL + ALT + SHIFT + BACKSPACE will Kill your Xsession
and ALT + F2 > xkill > click the problem application, will also kill a applicaion.

### Post by seventhc on 2008-01-04
Well if you want to end a process you can go to System>Administration>System Monitor and you can end processes there, and sometimes if your all locked up you can do ctrl-alt-backspace which will log you out of the xserver and then you can just log back in. Better than doing a hard shutdown.
