### Post by Seine on 2007-01-09
Ensure when you save the file as a particular type you give the filename the extension required.

Windows wont recognise a Word document unless it ends in .doc. Similarly a .rtf, .htm (.html) etc.

Your friend can just take the RTF she's already received and rename it to *whatever*.rtf and it should work.
