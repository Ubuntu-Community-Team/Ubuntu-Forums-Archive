### Post by aysiu on 2007-04-10
It's normal. You won't see anything.

Just type the password and hit *Enter*.

Your password is being accepted even though you will receive no visual indication that it is.

[It's a bug that will never be fixed](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=214393&highlight=password+terminal+feedback). Unfortunately, you just have to get used to it.
