### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-02-08
I've only ever found one place that required Windows, and that was to setup the users account with a proprietary application. If that doesn't work, buy a router and configure it.  I recommend a router anyway as they generally offer a hardware based firewall to begin with making your connection to the internet more secure.
