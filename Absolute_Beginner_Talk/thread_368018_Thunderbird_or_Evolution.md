### Post by jml on 2007-02-22
It really depends on what your requirements are.  Thunderbird is a great e-mail client.  But that is all it does.  

Evolution is very similar in functionality to Outlook, an application which is found in Microsoft Office.  It includes not only an e-mail client, but a calender function, a contacts manager, and it will synchronize with certain models of the Palm PDA.

So if all you need is an e-mail client, Thunderbird is the way to go.  If you need the functionality offered by Microsoft Outlook, or if you want to sync with your Palm device, then consider Evolution.  The good news is that since both applications are open sourse, all you are spending is you time, and not your money.  :)
