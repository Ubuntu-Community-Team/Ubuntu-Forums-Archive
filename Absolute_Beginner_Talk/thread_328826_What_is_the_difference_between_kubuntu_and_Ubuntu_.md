### Post by benuski on 2006-12-31
Ubuntu uses the Gnome Desktop environment, while Kubuntu uses KDE.  KDE is the pretty one, but personally I prefer the simplicity and ease of use of Gnome.  KDE does some things that I think are counterintuitive such as defaulting to single click to open things and their menu setup.  But, the best way to figure it out is to just download both livecds, if you're able, and try them both out, and then install whichever one you like better.
