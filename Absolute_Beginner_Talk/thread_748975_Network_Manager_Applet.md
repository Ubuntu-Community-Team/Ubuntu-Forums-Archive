### Post by autocrosser on 2008-04-08
Firstly: Network-Manager goes in the Notification area--did you remove it? (you can tell because you won't get update icons, etc). If this is the case, reinstall it & logout/login & you will get it back.

Secondly: Network-Manager should be listed in your sessions startup (Menu>System>Preferences>Sessions)--make sure that the box is checked for Network-Manager Applet under the Startup Programs tab.
