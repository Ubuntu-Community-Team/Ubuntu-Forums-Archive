### Post by Ann667 on 2006-07-28
You may have removed something.  Right click on the bar, choose “Add” and you will be able to add things to your task bar by dragging and dropping.  (There are a bunch of icons listed.  You’ll recognize things when you see it.)  There is one, I forget exactly what it’s called, I’m at work, not on my computer, but it shows the minimized windows in the task bar.  There are two, one that shows them individually and one that is just one icon and stacks them.  I think it has “window” in the title.  (I know, I’m probably being confusing. Sorry.)  I hope that helps.  It's really easy to do, and if you add something you don't want, right click on the icon you want to remove and choose remove.
