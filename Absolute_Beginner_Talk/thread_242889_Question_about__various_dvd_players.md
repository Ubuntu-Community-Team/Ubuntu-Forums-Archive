### Post by LadyDoor on 2006-08-24
I know that **xine** has this option. Start xine and then press g to bring up its panel (pressing g again will banish it). On that panel are slow and fast motion buttons. I believe the package to install is **xine-ui**, which will also install the xine core as dependencies.

I hope that works for you!
