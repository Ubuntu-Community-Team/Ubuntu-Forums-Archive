### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-02-13
Unfortunately, you are correct - there is no official Flash player for PowerPC.

However, you may want to look for a project called Gnash. This is an open-source Flash player that can handle some Flash movies (including Flash 7 movies). It can be compiled for PowerPC. Do a search through the PowerPC forum for Gnash instructions and you'll be set.
