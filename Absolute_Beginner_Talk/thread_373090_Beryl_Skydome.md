### Post by mcduck on 2007-03-01
> **kinematic said:**
> a skydome has to be 2:1 or 4:1(1024X512 or 4096x1024) as far as i know.No, is Doesn't. But the sizes must be powers of two, like 512, 1024, 2048 etc. so 1024x1024 is fine, 512x4096 is fine but 2000x1000 won't work.

Also the max size depends on how large textures your graphics card can manage.

When having problems it's best to try first with a 1024x1024 PNG image, that should work in about every case.
