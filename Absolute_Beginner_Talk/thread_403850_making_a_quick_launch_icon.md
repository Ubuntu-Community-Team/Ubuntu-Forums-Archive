### Post by Wicca on 2007-04-07
> **mdknaebel said:**
> OK, but how do I get it to work for, say, VLC?

Put in at command: **wxvlc**

If you already have a launcher for VLC in the menu (which you probably have, see Applications -> Audio and Video) you can simply rightclick on it and choose 'put this launcher on the Desktop'.

> **mdknaebel said:**
> And, how do I make it so it can be on the menu bar?
After you have put it on the Desktop, simply drag it to the menu bar and release the mouse button.
