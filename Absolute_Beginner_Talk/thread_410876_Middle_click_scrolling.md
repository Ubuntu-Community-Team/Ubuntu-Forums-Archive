### Post by LMP900 on 2007-04-16
For Firefox:

1. Edit > Preferences > Advanced
2. Select "Use autoscrolling"

I'm sure someone else can point you in the right direction for Opera, as I don't have it installed at the moment. :)

I just found the way for middle click scrolling in Opera. Hold down the shift key and press the middle mouse button. It will give you a dialog prompting the user what to do on middle click.
