### Post by drs305 on 2007-03-11
Are you asking how to move the panel from the top to the bottom? If so, just right click on it, select Properties, and select bottom in Orientation. Launcher buttons can be added to that panel by again right clicking and selecting add, then putting the command to launch the program in the command line.
