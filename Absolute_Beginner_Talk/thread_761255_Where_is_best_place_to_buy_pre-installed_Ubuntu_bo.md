### Post by skymera on 2008-04-21
The PC i have now all the hardware is supported.
It is a Dell, nothing special as standard.

Intel G965 Chipset - works on the i810 or intel driver.
Intel High Def Audio Integrated Sound - works straight away - Sigmatel/Alsamixer thing.
Intel E6300 C2D - No problems there
Belkin Wireless Desktop G F5D7000 - Works out of the box (rt61pci module)
My mobo: Dell 0WG864

Try to avoid ATi, alwyas have problems, the drivers are useless.

Nvidia works well, ive had no problems, my 7900GS worked well and my current 8800GT works well.

The Belkin Wireless G USB adaptor works out of the box, i have that for my laptop, its product name is F5D7050. It works on the RT73pci module.

If you go with Intel or mainstream hardware you'll be safe :)
