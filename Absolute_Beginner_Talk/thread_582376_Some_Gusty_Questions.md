### Post by Anztac on 2007-10-19
> **JasonBourneLinux said:**
> 
and- how do we play around with desktop effects?


Go to Add/Remove in the applications menu and install " compiz-configuration-settings-manager ".

You can also install VLC this way, though you may have to tell it to show you All Software.

You might want to read this blog post, I found it really helpful: [http://lunapark6.com/ubuntu-710-gutsy-desktop-edition-review.html](http://lunapark6.com/ubuntu-710-gutsy-desktop-edition-review.html) .
