Hello. I strongly suggest that you completely remove the Marrillat source. Its is intended for Debian and everything it has that is important is duplicated in the backport repo (sooner than later). The backport repo has NEVER broken anything for me and I have never had a problem upgrading with it.  

Using the Marrillat repo is a standby from the old days of Ubuntu when we lacked our own repo and we had to borrow Debian's. We've moved past that. Its caused breakage for me before.
