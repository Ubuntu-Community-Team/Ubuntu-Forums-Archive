### Post by Malibu Illusion on 2007-06-30
Suspend mode shuts off your monitor and stops the HDD from spinning, saving power and your HDD from unnecessary rotations.

Hibernation powers off your system completely, saving its current state to disk which is restored when you boot back up.

Shutting down, obviously, just shuts off the system entirely.
