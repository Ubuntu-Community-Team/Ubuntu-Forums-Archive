I play DVDs with Xine. Totem opens every time I put in a DVD. Is there any way to get it to stop opening  up?

### Post by joselin on 2006-02-04
You must go to System/preferences/removable drives and media preferences. Then, select multimedia label and uncheck the option play video DVD disks when inserted.

Regards.
