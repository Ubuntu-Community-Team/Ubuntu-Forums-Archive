### Post by mcduck on 2007-07-31
I've been very happy with my Asus laptop. The build quality is the best after old IBM Thinkpads, everything is well placed and solid enough to hammer nails into walls. I've carried my laptop around Europe in my backpack and handled it pretty roughly and there still no signs of any wear or loose parts.
