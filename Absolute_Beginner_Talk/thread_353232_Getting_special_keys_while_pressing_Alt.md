### Post by kregg on 2007-02-04
In windows, if I wanted to get the character à or á, I would press **Alt** and then press 'a'. Can this be done in Ubuntu? I tried having a go and now if I press Alt and 'a', I get 'æ'. How do i get it back to the 'á'?

---

### Post by c_forq on 2007-02-04
In Ubuntu if you switch your keyboard to ´international english (with dead keys)´ you can do characters as follows:
á = tap ´ than tap a
à = tap ` than tap a
ã = tap ~ than tap a
ä = tap ¨ than tap a
to use `´¨ and ~ just hit them twice.
