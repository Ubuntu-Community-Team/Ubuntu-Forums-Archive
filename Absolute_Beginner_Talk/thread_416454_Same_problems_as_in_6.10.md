### Post by jiminycricket on 2007-04-21
There's a laptop testing team here, specific models listed: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam)

Dell, HP laptops are supported OK, but not optimally (i.e., include Broadcom wireless which isn't OOB, and ATI/nvidia)

IBM laptops are very well supported, esp. if it's an old ATI card or Intel card.  The Intel wireless card depends on whether your distro includes the firmware, which I think Debian and Ubuntu do.
