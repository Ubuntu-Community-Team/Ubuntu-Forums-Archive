### Post by chimerical_brio on 2007-10-18
I agree with wpshooter. The problem is that the OS will be configured for the hardware on the machine you installed it from; if you try to use it on another computer, you'll need to totally reconfigure the hardware.

That said, I would recommend formatting the hard drive for transfering your files, and just transfering your files back and forth. Personally, I know I don't need a lot of specialized settings; things like bookmarks in Firefox are pretty easy to transfer over, and don't change that often for me (I use del.icio.us). Then, you can just brin gall your files with you wherever you are.
