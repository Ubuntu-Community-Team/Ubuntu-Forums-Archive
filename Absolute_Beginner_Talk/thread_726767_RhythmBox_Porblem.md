### Post by mozetti on 2008-03-17
Check in the notification area - there should be a tiny off-white rectange. That's rythmbox. Just right-click and choose "Show player" or something along those lines. By default, Rhythmbox skips the task bar and gets minimized to the notification area. You may be able to change this behavior in the preferences, but I'm not sure.
