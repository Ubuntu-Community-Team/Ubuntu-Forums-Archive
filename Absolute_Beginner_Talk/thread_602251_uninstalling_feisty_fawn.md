### Post by haldean on 2007-11-04
You don't have to uninstall. You just run the upgrade manager instead.
You can do this by pressing Alt+F2 and entering
```
gksu update-manager -cd
```
Near the top of the window will be a message that says "A new version of Ubuntu is available" or something like that. Click the button next to it and follow the instructions.
