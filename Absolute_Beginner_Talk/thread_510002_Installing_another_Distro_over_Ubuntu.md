### Post by eric_son on 2007-07-26
I just replaced my openSUSE 10.2 installation with Ubuntu 7.04.
After backing up all my essential files, I deleted the openSUSE partitions from WinXP.  Afterwards, I proceeded with installing Ubuntu on the freshly cleared partitions.
The new installation replaced the old Grub loader..so I didn't experience any problems.
This might not be the best way to do it...but it sure worked for me. :)
