### Post by mcduck on 2007-06-14
Right-click in any empty space in your remaining panel and select 'New Panel'. Then drag your new panel where you want it, right-click it and select 'Add to Panel'. You'll get a dialog with loads of different panel applets, and you can just drag&drop the ones you want into your new panel.
