### Post by BarfBag on 2006-12-19
Are the jumpers on the back of the drives in the right places?  You have to make them both Masters.  If one of them is a Slave, the computer will think less of it and boot the Master.  Another thing to do is look in your bios.  I believe there's a Master/Slave option in there too.  Check the jumpers first, though.
