### Post by adwait on 2006-11-17
Well, start Synaptic from the System menu. Search for the package you want, in this case AMSN. Then click on install. Click apply, and you are done! 

You might need to enable extra repositories to do this. Here's how: [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_add_extra_repositories](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_add_extra_repositories)
