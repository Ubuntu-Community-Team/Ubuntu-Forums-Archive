### Post by elpichi on 2007-09-19
If everything is installed correctly you press Ctrl+Alt and drag the mouse with left click and you should see the 3d cube. make sure that the window manager selected is beryl and not metacity when beryl is running(right click on the beryl icon to change this option) it should be set in beryl as default when you open the program though

### Post by nb123 on 2007-09-19
hi, okay, press ctrl + alt + (mouse click button 1) and drag. If this doesn't work, right click on your beryl manager and click on the beryl settings manager. Under the Desktop tab --> Rotate Cube ---> Shortcuts ---> Bindings ---> you should see a setting for initiate rotation. Change that to what you want and you're good to go.
