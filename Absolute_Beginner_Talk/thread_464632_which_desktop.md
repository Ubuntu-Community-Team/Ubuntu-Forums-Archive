### Post by jakev383 on 2007-06-04
Most likely you're seeing the Beryl videos - the ones where they roll the desktop around in a cube. I have it running on mine as well. Really nice, and makes all the Windows admins I do work for jealous. The wobbly windows get hard on the eyes after a while though.
In Beryl, you'll need to enable the 3D-cube to get that functionality. Then just hit CTRL-ALT-LEFT[arrow] to roll the desktop to the left.
