### Post by IYY on 2006-08-18
Xubuntu is faster because it runs XFCE instead of Gnome. XFCE has a more efficient window manager, and runs less services. The programs that are included with Xubuntu also tend to be either more efficient, or not as feature-complete. 

There are no Xubuntu "developers". Neither XFCE or Gnome are made by the Ubuntu/Xubuntu guys.

The most important resource that Xubuntu saves is RAM. If your machine is low on RAM, Xubuntu will be much faster than Ubuntu.
