### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-02-07
They blink because you are using the fglrx drivers and you have Desktop Effects turned on.  It's a known problem with ATI's drivers that I hope they will fix soon.  Videos blink too.

To make them stop blinking, turn off your effects while you are playing the games.
