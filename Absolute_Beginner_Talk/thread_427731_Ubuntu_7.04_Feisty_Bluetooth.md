### Post by Roner on 2007-04-29
Search synaptic for "gnome bluetooth" and install all that you can find, especially gnome-bluetooth and bluetooth manager. After you do that, a bluetooth icon of the manager should be in your system tray, if your computer's bluetooth adapter is recognized.

For the second question, edit the desktop theme. Look for the "theme" entry in the menu.
