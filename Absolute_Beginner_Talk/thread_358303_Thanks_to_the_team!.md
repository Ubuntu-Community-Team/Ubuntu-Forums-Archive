### Post by Captain Kirk76 on 2007-02-11
There's not much to share!

just type ```
 sudo sed -i -e 's/dapper/edgy/g' /etc/apt/sources.list
sudo apt-get update
sudo apt-get dist-upgrade 
```
into Konsole or Terminal,  Ubuntu begins to download its files, and prepare them for install, and it all installs, no restart or anything till its done, i could even continue using my Ubuntu whilst it installed. Very Easy!
