### Post by blueridgedog on 2007-12-31
Hmm.  I use esnipe. [http://www.esnipe.com](http://www.esnipe.com).

Ebay is not like an auction in one important way, it has a set end time.  As such, it pays to not bid until the end, lest you encourage others to bid higher.  So, a computer can be used to bid within seconds of the end.  People's bids are still automatically bid up to the highest that they stated that they wanted to pay when they placed their bid, so the auction is fair, but it prevents people from seeing your bid and reconsidering their maximum.
