### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-10-02
For music, it's simple:  Run Banshee, Rhythmbox, or Frostwire.  Set Music Sharing On in Preferences.

On the Mac, go to iTunes and your music shares will show up.  Don't get excited, iTunes will not share with Linux (since iTunes 7.*)

To share photos, you need to enable sharing on the Ubuntu directory that contains Photos.  You may have to load SAMBA onto your system (through Synaptic).

Another option is to load FUSE onto your Ubuntu system and FUSE onto the mac.  This is a new form of file sharing that I use and works well.  It's a little more involved to set up but there are tutorials in the forums and on the web.  Just do a search.
