### Post by fandeboris on 2007-12-19
I am a little new here, but registered to say I had a similar problem. I found out if your settings on the Show tab are "Unread Messages" You can click on an email and it will show a blank email. 
You can still see them in the preview panel, without a problem. I think this may be a bug, I haven't read very much documentation on Evolution, So I couldn't tell if this was mentioned or not

Just check you SHOW tab and see if it has "unread messages" showing. Change it to "all messages". 

I hope this helps
