### Post by popch on 2007-12-16
It's the same as with the key for the digit 4.

Simply pressing the key gives you one character
Pressing Shift and the key gives you another one
Pressing 'alt gr' and the key gives the third one

'alt gr' is the key immediately to the right of the space key.
