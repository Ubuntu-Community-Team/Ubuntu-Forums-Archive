### Post by gila_monster on 2006-01-03
You should be okay using OpenOffice, which comes with Ubuntu. OO can open MS Office formats, and can usually save to them as well. (There are a few OO features that won't save properly to Office formats.)

For most Windows programs, there is a Linux equivalent, sometimes several. You may have to play around a bit to find the one you like the best. Like you, my laptop is used for general work, presentations, and the like (no games), and Ubuntu has served me well in this regard.

Note that your mileage may vary depending on what HW your laptop uses.
