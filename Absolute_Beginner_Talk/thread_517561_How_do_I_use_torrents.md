### Post by ron999 on 2007-08-04
Hi
Most Ubuntu users probably use ktorrent or azureus, but transmission will probably be OK.

First you visit a website such as thepiratebay.com or torrentspy.com and search for your file.
Then you download the torrent (this is just a small file) onto your desktop.
Then you open up your client (transmission) and point it to the torrent that you've downloaded.
That's it.
