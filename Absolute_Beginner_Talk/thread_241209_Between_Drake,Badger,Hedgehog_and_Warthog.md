### Post by Josh_b on 2006-08-22
My advice is install the newest (Dapper Drake) and if you have any problems, then try the previous ones.

The reason I say that is, often people have an old version of something, update it and then find that it doesn't work, but if you have the newest version workly correctly, you are less likely to have that issue. Also the newer version has (generally) less bugs and such and is more likely to work. For example, I tried Warthog, and the GUI crashed on startup, I downloaded Drake and it works perfectly.

Hope that helps
Josh
