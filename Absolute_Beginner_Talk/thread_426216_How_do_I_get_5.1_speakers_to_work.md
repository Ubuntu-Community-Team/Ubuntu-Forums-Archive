### Post by ripleyscat on 2007-04-28
Right click on the speaker icon (on your panel) then open volume control, select edit from the volume control sub-menu which will appear after the right click. Then select preferences: in the volume control preferences you can choose which switches will be shown on the menu when you open it,, (in your case surround, and center etc should be shown. Select the switches you need from that menu. Then sit back and enjoy the surround sound goodness!
