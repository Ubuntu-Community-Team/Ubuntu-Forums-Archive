### Post by BarfBag on 2007-04-18
AMD and Intel both have excellent Linux support.  It doesn't matter.  Buy which ever one you get a better deal on.

I (and probably every one else) wouldn't touch an ATI card with a ten foot poll.  The drivers are terrible.  nVidia is the way to go.  It sounds like a GeForce 7600 GT would be perfect.  It matches your preferred specs.  Also, NewEgg has it in both AGP and PCI-E.  I have the AGP version (I know, living in the stone age).  Even with AGP, it's an amazing card.
