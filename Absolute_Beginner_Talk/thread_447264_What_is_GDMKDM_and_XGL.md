### Post by ComplexNumber on 2007-05-17
GDM and KDM are the gnome and kde login manager, respectively. after you've booted up, you will see a dialogue that asks for your password and username. thats the login manager.
XGL is the technology that enables the use of graphics acceleration( for 3D cubes etc etc) from graphics cards.
