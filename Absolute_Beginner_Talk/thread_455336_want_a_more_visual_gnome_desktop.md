### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-26
> **JParkus said:**
> I dont know what i can change. I like the desktop I have beryl just want the menubars to look a little better

Menus are skinned via GTK themes (they change icons, font and windows too). Beryl only changes how your windows display, its only a window manager. [Gnome look]("http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=100") has lots of GTK 2 themes to choose from, have a look.

You then apply them by System > Preferences > Themes > Install, then point it at the tar/archive. Thats it.

Have fun playing with themes, they can be manually customized too if you click customize.

### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-26
> **discmaster said:**
> Download theme to desktop? I did that, but what to do from there? It does not show up in the Theme GUI...

You push the install button on the GUI for Themes, then you navigate to the archive (it can be different formats, should be tar though) and then click it and open. It installs automatically, then scroll down the left menu until you find the new one (it has same name as archive usually). Click it and it applies, close when your happy.
