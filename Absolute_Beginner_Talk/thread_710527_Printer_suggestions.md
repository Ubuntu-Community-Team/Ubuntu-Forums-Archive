My recommendation is to steer clear of ink jets.  They are cheap to buy but drain you of money with ink refills.

Take a look at the HP Color LaserJet CM1015 MFP.  It is a scanner, copier and printer.  It is a color laser and it works perfectly under Linux as it is PostScript.

You can also look at the CM1017MFP which adds a built in print server.

Pretty much any HP printer works with Linux.  The CM1017 runs around $400 off the net.  Yes color lasers are more than ink jets but the toner cartridges last way longer and don't dry out like ink.
