### Post by hashimoto on 2006-11-17
Two things I would check:
1. Monitor refresh rate. Are you sure you use the same refresh rate  in Windows?
2. Check System > Preferences > Fonts and try different rendering options. At least with LCD display I needed to tick the subpixel alternative to get better fonts.
