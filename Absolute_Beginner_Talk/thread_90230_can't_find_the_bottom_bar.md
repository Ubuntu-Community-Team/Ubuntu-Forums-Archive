### Post by mcduck on 2005-11-14
Right-click on any empty space in the existing panel, select 'New Panel' and drag it to bottom of screen with middle button (or right-click it, go to properties and choose where you want it). Then right-click on the new panel, select 'Add to Panel' and from the window drag 'Window list' to the bottom panel. Feel free to add other useful applets to your panel too :)
