### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2007-05-08
You're facing two problems.  Please use two separate threads.

For mounting, see [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Mount](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Mount).  If you have further questions about mounting, please do a forum search.

To get printers to work, please use to search function to locate threads on your printers.  If the search function is not useful, please create a new thread (don't combine both printers into one thread, use two separate threads for each printer) in the hardware forum.

You can install XP using VirtualBox, but I can't tell you whether or not you'll be able to play a certain game.  Do a search on Battlefield 2 to see what others have said.  To install VirtualBox, use Automatix (getautomatix.com).

As far as your ATI card, you'll likely have many problems with it on Linux.  I am not ATI expert, but lots of ATI card owners report problems.  For any video card problems, do a forum search on your particular card.  If you notice strange problems, your ATI card is likely a suspect.

### Post by Saphira on 2007-05-10
I strongly suggest that you do NOT use Automatix to fix your problem. Automatix is a waste of your time and energy. Refer to these sites for more formalized documentation -- [http://wiki.ubuntu.com](http://wiki.ubuntu.com)  [http://ubuntuguide.org](http://ubuntuguide.org)  [http://doc.gwos.org](http://doc.gwos.org) for better help. 

Automatix is known for breaking systems and you'll find that you really don't need it. It may also make upgrading your system later difficult.  With Feisty most of the stuff you'll use Automatix to install is already a one click install either via add/remove applications or by referral to one of the above sources. Do NOT be fooled by those that support Automatix.
