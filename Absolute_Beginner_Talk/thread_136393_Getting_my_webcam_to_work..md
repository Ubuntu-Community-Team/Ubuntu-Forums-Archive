### Post by AndyCooll on 2006-02-25
You need to be a bit more specific about what webcam you are using. Exact details of the model please.

Gaim doesn't currently support webcams (though I believe Kopete and aMSN do).

You can quickly check if your webcam is working "out-of-the-box" by opening up Gnomemeeting (Applications-->Internet-->Gnomemeeting). It will open up and display video output from your webcam if it is. Mine does and I haven't needed to install any drivers or anything.
