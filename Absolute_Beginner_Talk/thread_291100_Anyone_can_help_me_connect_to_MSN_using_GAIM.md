### Post by jm2003uk on 2006-11-02
How do you connect to the internet? Do you use dial-up, broadband (modem or router) or some other connection?


And just to make sure you have the right information in the gaim accounts screen (scroll down to view image):

Make sure the protocol is set to MSN (simple i know, but could be missed)

The 'Screen name' box will contain your msn email address (yourname@hotmail.com or whatever address you used previously)

The 'password' contains your password. Make you you type it exactly and check you don't have caps lock on (might give you wrong password).

The 'alias' box only affect gaim at your end (i believe). When your typing a message to someone, you will see your 'alias' on screen instead of your email address. I never put anything in here.

[ATTACH]18731[/ATTACH]

To change the name that other people will see (in their contact list) go to 'Accounts' and hold your mouse over your account and select 'Set Friendly name'.
