### Post by Sef on 2006-07-21
> On that note, how does Ubuntu deal with Viruses, Spyware, Malware, Worms, and all the rest of that icky nonsense?

Linux itself is very secure.  The problems that will be experienced will be through 3rd party apps, e.g. flash, firefox, etc.

Also you should have a firewall installed. And an anti-virus if you run a mail client, because even though window viruses won't affect your system, then can ride on it and infect your window-based friends.
