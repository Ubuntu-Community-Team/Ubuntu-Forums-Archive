### Post by equal on 2007-02-10
you can't install linux inside windows. It's a separate operating system. You need to boot your computer from the CD. Generally speaking, you have to hit F2 or F10 while the computer is booting, and then tell it to boot from the CD/DVD drive first.

Look for a how-to on the forums if you want to dual boot windows and linux.
