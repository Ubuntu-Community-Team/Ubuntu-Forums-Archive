### Post by chillin on 2007-05-08
I was getting the same exact error with gmail and Thunderbird. Everything worked peachy for weeks, I was sending email like a low-key spammer... then, with nothing changed, suddenly and without warning, I get this error and can't send email. Or so I thought.

Is it Google? Turns out it was me.

Check your recipiant's address closely. In my case, the address had more than one dot in the url(someone@users.sourceforge.net) and Thunderbird stuck the "someone@users.sorceforge" on one recipiant line and the ".net" on the next. Easily corrected, and away the mail went.
