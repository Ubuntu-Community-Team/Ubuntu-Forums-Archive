### Post by julian67 on 2007-09-15
> **Specter043 said:**
> I am currently using Xfce, and would prefer to just use the trash can in the lower right corner.  I also would prefer home folder and disk to not show up as desktop icons.  does anyone know how to hide these/delete them?

Settings>Desktop Preferences>Behaviour

Uncheck the boxes for home and filesystem and trash. You might need to log out and in again, or you can uncheck the box "Allow Xfce to manage the desktop" close the pop up, close the Prefences dialogue. Then, again, Settings>Desktop Preferences>Behaviour and this time check the box "Allow Xfce to manage the desktop" and close and close. You should now have an empty desktop that only shows you an icon when you plug in a removable drive or load a CD/DVD.
