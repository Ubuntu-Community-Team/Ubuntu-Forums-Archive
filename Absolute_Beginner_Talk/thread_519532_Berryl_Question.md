### Post by atomkarinca on 2007-08-07
> **Vuto said:**
> How do I stop berryl from making the desktop change when i move me mouse to the side?

beryl settings manager > desktop > rotate cube > behaviour > edge flip, uncheck edge flip pointer
> 
And Also

How do I stop this transparent thing fro mhappening and showing all my desktops at once

desktop > desktop cube > transparency, uncheck transparent cube.
