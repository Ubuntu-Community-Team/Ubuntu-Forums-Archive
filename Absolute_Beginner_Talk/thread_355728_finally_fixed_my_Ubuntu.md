### Post by DSn0wMan on 2007-02-08
In the device section try changing ...

Driver "ati" to Driver "radeon"

and since you are enabling dri add an option to the device section

Option "DRI" "true"

I beleive the BusID line is optional, I would just remove it because specifying the wrong BusID can have less than desirable effects.

Here is an example:

Section "Device"
Identifier "ATI RADEON 9600XT"
Driver "radeon"
Option "DRI" "true"
EndSection
