### Post by Native Dialect on 2007-03-11
I just done setting up a dual boot for my own computer. But I have the advantage of two drives, so I just kept XP on the master drive and I wiped my slave drive (after backing up my files of course). Since you aren't specifying the problems with your live CD, i'll just go ahead and suggest two things

1)You can actually run a CD check when you insert the Live CD. It is a 10 minute scan that makes sure that the disc is functioning.

2)If your disc just isn't loading, or is showing up as an invalid system disc, then that means that you just copied the ISO to the CD, rather than actually burning the image. So make sure you burn the image and not just the ISO. 

Good luck
