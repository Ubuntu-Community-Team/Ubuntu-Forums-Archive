### Post by andypaxo on 2006-09-28
Truetype fonts are just regular fonts.
I think you may mean sub pixel anti aliasing - smoother font rendering for LCDs...

For this, go on your main menu to System -> Preferences -> Font

Then choose 'Sub pixel smoothing'. Your fonts should now all look more smooth.
(If this makes weird colors appear around black text, you may want to choose 'Details...' and try a different pixel order)
