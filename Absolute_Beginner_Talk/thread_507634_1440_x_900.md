### Post by benx009 on 2007-07-23
well, first off, go to **System -> Adminstration -> Restriced Drivers Manager**,  and enable the nvidia driver (if you haven't done so already).  After restarting your PC, go to System -> Prefences -> Screen Resolution and change it to 1440x900 from there.  If you don't see that option, you may have to manually edit your /etc/X11/xorg.conf

enjoy your Ubuntu install!
