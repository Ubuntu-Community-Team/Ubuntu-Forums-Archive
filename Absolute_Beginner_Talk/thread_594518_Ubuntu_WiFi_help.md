### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-10-28
If your network has a password on it or is hidden it will not connect in Roaming Mode.
Left click on the Network icon by the clock and select Manual Configuration. Select the Wireless Connection and click Properties. Uncheck Enable Roaming Mode. Then just fill out the info.
