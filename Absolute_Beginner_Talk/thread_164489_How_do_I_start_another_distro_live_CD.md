### Post by catlett on 2006-04-22
Once you burn that image to a cd you then have to restart your computer with the cd in the drive. If your computer is set up correctly in Bios your computer will boot to the cd.
This will start the Scientific session. Just follow the prompts. There shouldn't be too many questions. You can usually just press enter at the prompts.
Then the session will start and Scientific Linux will be running your computer from the cd.
