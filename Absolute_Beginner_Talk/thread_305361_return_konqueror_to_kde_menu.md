### Post by Bachstelze on 2006-11-23
Konquerr should be in your K menu, in the "Internet" section, if that's not what you meant, please be more precise.

To add the Taskbar to your panel, right click on it, choose "Add applet to panel" and you'll have a list of all installed applets, search for the taskbar and double-click on it to add it to your panel.
