### Post by jayson.rowe on 2007-12-18
To start, open up your termnial and go to Edit -> Profiles, make sure 'Default' is selected and click Edit, now, you can go customize your heart out! Also, rather than editing 'Default' you could simply make a new profile and edit that.

One customization I always make is adjusting the size of the initial terminal window when I click the launcher on my panel - if you want to do that, first, make a launcher on your panel the right-click it and go to properties, and in the command box you can add:

```
--geometry=110x35
```

Of course, you can adjust those values to what you desire. I use 110x35 on my laptop's 1024x768 display, and I use 120x45 on my Desktop's 1280x1024.
