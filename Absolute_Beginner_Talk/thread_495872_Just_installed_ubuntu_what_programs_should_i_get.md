That all depends on that kind of things your interested in. Get Automatix2, its gives a list of programs and easily installs them. For eyecandy, get Beryl with Emerald theme manager (you'll have to activate your graphic drivers, the way you do that depends whether you use Nvidia or ATI). For writing I use GnomeBaker. ### Post by p_quarles on 2007-07-08
Just my opinion, but no one should be telling an absolute beginner to install Automatix and Beryl. These are programs that cause a lot of problems on many machines, and they are not necessary for any kind of functionality. The Synaptic package manager -- as well as the light version in Add/Remove Programs -- is more than adequate for installing anything the average user needs, and they're both very easy to use. 
[/2cents]
