### Post by orb9220 on 2006-09-03
Well I have a fx5200 nvidia card which has a crt and a dvi out on it, So I connect the dvi out with an adapter that came with the card which allows me to have two monitors connected.

The card must have two outputs on them crt,dvi crt,crt more rare to see. Or having two seprate cards which is more of a bear to config.

Then you have to install the specific drivers nvidia or ati which are more problematic.

Then you have to modify your xorg.conf file to enable multi monitor support.
