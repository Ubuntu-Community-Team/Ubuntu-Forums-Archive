All these 3-4 factors combined have made it impossible to startup Firefox or even IE at all in 6.06.  Now I reckon I will have to wipe out my entire drive and do a full clean install all over again :mad:

---

### Post by adam.tropics on 2006-11-28
You can find the file in:

/home/<*username*>/.mozilla/firefox/<*someoddstring*>.default/bookmarks.html

save a copy somewhere and restore when needed!
