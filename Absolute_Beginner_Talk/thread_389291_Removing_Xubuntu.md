### Post by annda on 2007-03-20
insert your ubuntu install cd, and when you get to the point of partitioning, choose the manual option. then delete the linux partitions and after applying changes, reboot.

the last thing you will have to do is boot from your windows cd, go to recovery console and type fixmbr. this will remove the grub bootloader.
