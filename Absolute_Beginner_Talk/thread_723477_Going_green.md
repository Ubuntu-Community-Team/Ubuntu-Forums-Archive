### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-03-13
You can't suspend your computer and still have access to the internet since standby turns off most I/O devices, and puts everything into a low power state.  You can have your monitor turn off when it's not in use, use speed scaling if your processor supports it, and keep other peripherals off when you're not using them, liike speakers, printers, external disks, and mp3 players.
