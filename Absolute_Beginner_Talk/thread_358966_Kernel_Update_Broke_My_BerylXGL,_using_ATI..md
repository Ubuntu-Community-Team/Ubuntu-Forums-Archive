### Post by Migs on 2007-02-11
Yes you will have to reinstall it and in the future again if you update your kernel. It has to do with manually installing drivers for your ATi card. Everything which comes standard in the kernel doesn't have to be reinstalled, everything that doesn't needs to be reinstalled.

For more info see this link:[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Edgy_Installation_Guide#C](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Edgy_Installation_Guide#C)

Good luck!
