### Post by knightnet on 2007-05-15
LVM is great if you may have several disks and may want to extend partitions onto new disks or otherwise rearrange your space.

However, it does need some careful thought as to the layout of partitions and is not especially easy to set up.

For the non-experienced, I would say avoid it for now.
