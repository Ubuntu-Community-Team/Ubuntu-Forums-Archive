The gOS is a good idea. But it's not ready for general use at this time. The companies that have used the gOS in their recent products have made a huge mistake, IMHO. Are you using a gPC, Cloudbook, or other PC? Since you're new to Linux, I'd recommend that you switch from gOS to "real" Ubuntu. First of all, gOS is still rather unstable. You can expect frequent system crashes, etc. Second, the control panels in Ubuntu are much easier to find and use than in the gOS -- especially gOS Rocket. You may check the hardware requirements for Ubuntu by clicking [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements/GutsyGibbon").
