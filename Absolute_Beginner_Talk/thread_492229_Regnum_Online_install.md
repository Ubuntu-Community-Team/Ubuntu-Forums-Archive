### Post by ben-g on 2007-07-05
Okay, first you need to make an account, which you have to do at their website. Then when you've downloaded the launcher file extract it. After you extracted it launch the run file.   It'll be in Spanish but just enter your username and passworld and hit enter.  On the next screen you can open the option menu by clicking the middle button on the bottom of the page. Then just change it from Espanol to Ingles. Now  close the options  menu exit the program and open it up again. It should be in English. 

The game starts downloading once you hit play so don't plan on playing right away it'll take some time.  Hope this helps.
