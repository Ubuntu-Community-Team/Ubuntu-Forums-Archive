### Post by rillip on 2007-05-03

If you're in the add/remove programs section, there's a list at the top that lets you chose only KDE, other packages, proprietary and unsupported.  If you check them all, you'll see a ton of stuff.  I believe only the KDE ones are enabled by default, but they will all install and work, including Gnome apps.
