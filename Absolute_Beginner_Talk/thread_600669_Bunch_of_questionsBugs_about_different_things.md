### Post by shad0w_walker on 2007-11-02
I can help you with the first two or at least half of the second.

1. Right click on a file (Of the type you want to change) and select properties. In the properties dialog click the 'Open with' tab and select you program.

2. Right click on the volume manager icon and select 'Preferences'. Highlight which ever volume you want to control with it. Ctrl/Shift click to select multiple.
