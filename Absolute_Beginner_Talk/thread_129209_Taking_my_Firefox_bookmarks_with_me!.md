### Post by mcduck on 2006-02-13
You can easily save yoyur bookmarks to a file on your desktop. Open firefox, and go to bookamarks/manage bookmarks. Then select File/Export and now you can choose name for your bookmarks file. Easy.

You can import bookmarks the same way, only choose Import instead of Export and find your bookmarks.html file ;)

Many FF extensions also allow exporting their settings. At least Adblock and Tab Mix Plus have this option.
