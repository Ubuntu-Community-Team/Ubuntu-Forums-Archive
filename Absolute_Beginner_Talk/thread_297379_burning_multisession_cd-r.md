### Post by balaram on 2006-11-11
I use K3B. I'm not sure if it automatically loads upon installation but you'll find it in the synaptic package manager. Anyway when you choose BURN a window will open and there's an ADVANCED tab near the lower half of the window, click it and you'll see a START MULTISESSION box, check mark it and your all set to go.
