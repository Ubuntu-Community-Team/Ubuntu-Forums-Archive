### Post by Chew55 on 2008-03-02
There was a thread about this not that long ago.

The new generation iPods have a check sum or something in them which means that when a program that isn't iTunes does something to the file it effects the check sum and the iPod can't read the files anymore.

This happened to me and I had no back up.  However, I viewed my iPod in Rockbox and from there copied all my music back to my computer, then went into windows, used iTunes to restore the iPod database and used iTunes to copy all my music back on to the iPod.  Now I just save all my music on to a common drive and just open windows and use iTunes to put music on my iPod.
