### Post by thelatinist on 2007-12-10
For a beginner I would suggest:

1) Applications > Add/Remove...
2) Click Preferences, then in the Ubuntu tab check all the check boxes except "Source Code." Close.
3) If you are asked to update sources lists, do so.
4) In the Show dropdown, select "All available applications."
5) Browse or search for the software you want.  You can search for "torrent" to bring up a list of available torrent-related applications with their descriptions, for example.

Note: in the future all you will have to do is open Add/Remove... and do your search.
