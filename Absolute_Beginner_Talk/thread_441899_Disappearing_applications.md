### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-05-13
Hi. Once you open applications and minimize them, does **ALT+TAB** let you cycle through them and bring them to the front again? If that's the case, you lost your window list. To recover it, right-click on the bottom panel (somewhere to the right of the "Show Desktop" button (the little desktop on the bottom left) and select "Add to Panel..."; from the applications it shows, choose **Window List** (next to the Trash applet).

### Post by heimo on 2007-05-13
Is your Window list missing? Try right clicking on a panel (upper or lower area / bar where all kinds of menus, clocks and icons live), select Add to Panel and add Window List.
