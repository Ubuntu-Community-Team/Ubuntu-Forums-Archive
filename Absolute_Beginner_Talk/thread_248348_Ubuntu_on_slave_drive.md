### Post by click4851 on 2006-09-01
You should be okay, I have dual booted linux/winxp in this manner for some time. The installer is very clear about which hardrive it will install to, its even easier if the hadrives are different sizes, its a dead give away then. The installer then installs grub, which when the computer boots, lists your linux os and should list any windows os. I did just this, this morning with a dapper 6.06 dvd on a 80gig master, and winxp on a 20 gig slave.
