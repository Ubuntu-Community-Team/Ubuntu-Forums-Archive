### Post by Indras on 2006-09-21
I just put my swiftfox icon there (find it in the Applications menu under Internet, then right-click->add to panel) then right-click and remove the firefox icon.  I personally think the globe icon is pretty ugly, but if you like it you can just change the swiftfox icon to it, it's located at /usr/share/pixmaps/mozilla-firefox.png

Edit: Or, something I just thought of... right-click the globe icon, go to properties, and where it says command (should contain "firefox %u") delete it and type in "/opt/swiftfox/swiftfox" instead.  That should do exactly what you're asking for.
