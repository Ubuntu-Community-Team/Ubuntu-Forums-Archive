### Post by technikalKP on 2007-06-27
I don't pay for hotmail and have no problems accessing it through Evolution using Hotway's POP3 access.  It depends on how long you've had your hotmail account.  It you've had it for years, they seem to 'grandfather' in your ability to access it via email clients. 

That said, Thunderbird's Web-Mail settings work better for me with Hotmail than Evolution+Hotway. I prefer the way Thunderbird allows me to retain messages on the hotmail server, but still pushes through Deletes so I can get rid of junk mail.  I don't know if Thunderbird's implementation has the same dependency on how long you've been a hotmail user as the Evolution solution I was using.
