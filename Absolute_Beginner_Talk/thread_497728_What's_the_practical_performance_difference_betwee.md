### Post by stalker145 on 2007-07-10
> **Ian-on-the-Trent said:**
> ***Should I spend the bucks on a dedicated video or just use the MB's video?***  This is for office applications not gaming.

If it's going to be mainly for office use and you're on a tight budget, I would suggest that you remain with the onboard video until you have the finances and the desire to get a dedicated card. It will work fine, I'm sure. Just make sure that the video chip is Linux friendly.

The main advantage to having a dedicated video card is that you are not using your system RAM to run the video processes. This would give you more available RAM for doing your work.
