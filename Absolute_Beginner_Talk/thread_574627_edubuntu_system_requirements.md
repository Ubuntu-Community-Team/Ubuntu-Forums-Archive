> System Requirements:

Edubuntu is available for PC architectures.

Installation requires at least 2.5 GB of disk space, a CPU of at least 1 GHz, and 256 MB of RAM plus an additional 28 MB of RAM per thin client (e.g. a server driving two clients should have at least 512 MB RAM).
