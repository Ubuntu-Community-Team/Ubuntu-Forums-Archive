### Post by freebird54 on 2007-04-19
It may not be ready for primetime yet - but it doesn't screw up very often, and it fails gracefully (just lose the effects - not your session) when it does go out.

Install it as a separate X 'session', so you can choose on boot up whether you want it that time, and all will be well. :)

You can switch the session by <ctr><alt><backspace> - and hitting F10 at the login and following the  prompts once you have installed everything.

It *IS* handy for workspace switching.  It is incomparable for showing that the WOW is not on Vista :D
