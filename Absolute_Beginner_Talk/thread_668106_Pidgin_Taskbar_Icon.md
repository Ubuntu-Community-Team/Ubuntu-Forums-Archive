How do I make it so when I click the "X" on the Pidgin window it minimizes to the taskbar. Right now I do that and it just closes, no icon on the taskbar.

### Post by SanskritFritz on 2008-01-15
Why do you want to do that? Close is close, minimize is minimize. Just press the minimize button, if you want to minimize pidgin. BTW, on close, you can hide the window into the notification bar. That is a a setting in Pidgin, go to preferences.

### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-01-15
HI. Are any other applications showing up in the system tray? if not, you might be missing the Notification Area; right-click on an empty space in the top panel, select "Add to Panel..." scroll all the way to the bottom and double click on "Notification Area".
