### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-09-11
Alt Gr + 4 works for me too (I'm in the UK).

As a last resort:
    Accessories->Character Map
    Search for "euro" - then search again (the one you want is the second find)
    Double-click the symbol to put it in the text box
    Copy/paste into your document

### Post by secret_force on 2006-09-13
lol i found out, using the euro sign is not automatically enabled.

You need to change it in system settings --> regio/accesibility --> in the left column you choose keyboardlayout (don't know if's called that way in the english edition, i'm using the dutch version) --> xkb-options --> scroll all the way down to "adding eurosign to certain keys".

probably the way to add the eurosign to keys can be optimised and simplified for edgy, it's rather difficult to find, and many people need it.
