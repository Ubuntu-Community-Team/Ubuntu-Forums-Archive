### Post by land0 on 2006-10-28
Openoffice.org relies on myspell dictionary for it's spell checking features. Using Synaptic make sure the proper dictionary is installed for your language. Then in OOo in the top menu go to Tools--> Options--> Then expand the +sign next to **Language Settings** and make sure the language/dictionary you installed is checked. There are also some additional settings you can tweak to your hearts desire. Hope this helps.
