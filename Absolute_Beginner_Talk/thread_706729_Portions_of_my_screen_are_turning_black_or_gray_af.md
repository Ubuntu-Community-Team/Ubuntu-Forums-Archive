### Post by RedRat on 2008-02-24
Open Synaptic and search for Wine and Crossover. Click on Wine (which should have a small green box next to it. Right click and chose completely remove. Same for Crossover. That should un-install them.

If you want to stop either one while they are running, go to"
System>Administration> System Monitor. Go to the Processes tab and scroll through there until you find Crossover or Wine and click on them then right click on them and click the kill process in the context menu. 

Hope this helps. Maybe somebody else has a better idea.
