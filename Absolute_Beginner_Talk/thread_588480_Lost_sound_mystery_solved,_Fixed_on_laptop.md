### Post by tashmooclam on 2007-10-23
This may be repetitious and useless, I apologize in advance.
Sound worked on my HP zt1000 laptop since installing 7.1 
Then, it stopped working. I didn't know what I had done, since I had opened the volume control and unchecked the mic box and pc sound. 
After a day, I "lost" my sound.
I could faintly hear the sound with my earphones plugged in. 
Clicking the mute button on the laptop brought back the sound! I actually had never touched it so I didn't remember it was there.
What is odd is I realize that now the mute button was actually "on" when I started up the laptop, somehow this was by default "muted", There is a little led next to the button and this was on the whole time. Now I manually clicked it "off". The led is off. Sound is back.
To those who have laptops and who have lost their sound, hit some buttons before trying other things.
Why the button was selected by itself is a mystery, but that's what happened.
