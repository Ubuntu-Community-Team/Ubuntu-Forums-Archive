### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-10-01
> **Papapishuman said:**
> should i be sure to make a backup?

Yes! Although the plan is that the windows install should be left still working, it is possible that an error will scramble it (happened to me once out of many installs). You should make a backup, but you are unlikely to need it. You don't get extra points for being brave, and don't get points deducted for being careful.

Good luck, and enjoy Ubuntu.
