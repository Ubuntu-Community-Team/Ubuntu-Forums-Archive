### Post by crispy_420 on 2007-01-06
I would disable the onboard video in BIOS for starters.

As far as MythTV and any non-hardware rendering card, don't count on recording and doing anything else at the same time. All the work will be on your CPU, destroying your performance.

Would it be an option to use Intel video and ATI tv, I don't know but it may be a viable solution as a Rage card is redicuously outdated, no offense.
