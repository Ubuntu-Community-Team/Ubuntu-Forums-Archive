### Post by Sef on 2007-04-03
Your low ram will prevent many distros from installing.  Also you could have a chipset that is not supported by a number of distros.  With your ram, I would look at installing a light weight distro, like [Damn Small Linux]("http://www.damnsmalllinux.org/").

### Post by NICU on 2007-04-03
I would suggest [Puppy Linux]("http://www.puppylinux.org/user/viewpage.php?page_id=1") over Damn Small Linux - the UI is better and with your old ThinkPad it should run pretty quickly.  I have an IBM T20 (700mhz - 256MB) which runs Xubuntu, Damn Small Linux, and Puppy Linux.  Puppy seems to be the best compromise of speed and looks.
