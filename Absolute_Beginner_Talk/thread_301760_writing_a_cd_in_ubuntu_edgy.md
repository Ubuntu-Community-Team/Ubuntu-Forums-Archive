### Post by wpshooter on 2006-11-17
Ridgeback:

Go to the Add/Remove section of Ubuntu and go to the sound/media section (I think it is) and find and install the K3b CD burning application program.

I recommend that you use CD-RW media instead of CD-R media.  There is an erase CD-RW functions in the K3b program.  Use it to erase your old CD-RWs and use them again and again and again.

Good luck.
