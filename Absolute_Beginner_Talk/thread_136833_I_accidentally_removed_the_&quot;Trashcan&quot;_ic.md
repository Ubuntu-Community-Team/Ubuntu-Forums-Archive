### Post by WackToMack on 2006-02-26
LOL!  I did that the other day... took me FOREVER to figure out how to get it back... all you have to do is right-click on the bottom panel, then click ADD To Panel.  Find Trash somewhere among all those Add To Panel applications and ta-da!  Instant trash can.  (In order to get it back to the spot where it used to be, right-click one of the Workspaces on the bottom panel and click Remove From Panel.  Then right-click your Trash and click Move.  Move it to the same spot it was before.  Then right-click Trash again and click Lock To Panel.  Then right-click the panel and click Add To Panel and find the Workspace Switcher.  If that is not positioned correctly either, right-click it and click Move.  Move it to the correct place and right-click again and click Lock To Panel.  Done!) :D
