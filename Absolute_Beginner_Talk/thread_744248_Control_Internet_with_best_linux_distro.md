I would suggest Linux Mint if you are looking for something that just works.

Of course you can find a program to control the internet access, but I will give you a good reason why not to. I explored the Internet quite a bit when I was younger, and my online experiances helped to shape who I am today. For example, my sense of humoIr wouldn't be half as good if my access had been restricted. I understand why you would want to have a level of control over your children's Internet access, but in my experiance it just frustrates and annoys the people who have to suffer from it.
