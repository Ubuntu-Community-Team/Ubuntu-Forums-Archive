### Post by Paul820 on 2007-10-23
I have just sorted mine out. It was taking 2:48 to boot. What i did was download startupmanager from the repos. I had a look at it and found my resolution was set to 640x480, thought it was weird, so i set it to something similar to my display 1280x800. Rebooted and got my boot-time from 2 minutes 48 seconds to 26 seconds. Enclosed some screenies.
