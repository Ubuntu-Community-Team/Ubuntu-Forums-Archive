### Post by afilonov on 2007-03-27
You can save your documents on SD card in Excel or Word format and then use card reader to copy files and open them using OpenOffice.
Reverse process works too, the only problem is that DoToGo doesn't understand all Excel and Word formatting, so keep your documents as simple as possible.
