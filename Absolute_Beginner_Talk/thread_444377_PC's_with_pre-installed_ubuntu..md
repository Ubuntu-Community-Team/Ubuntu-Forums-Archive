If you want this to really take off, make sure that you only use hardware which works out of the box on Ubuntu. The last thing a new windows convert wants is hardware that needs to be coaxed into working properly. Also, if you pre-install ubuntu on your computers, remember that you are the only one in your area who knows anything about Linux... You will be tech support for the whole island. If you are not prepared for that, I would include the cost of canonical's tech support in the cost of the computer, and sell it to your customers as software technical support for their Linux operating system.
