for session cookies it would be the same as choosing " I close firefox ". what i do is chose the always ask option, then go to the sites i need cookies on and only accept the cookies i need for that site, then i just turn off cookies completely as there not needed for general browsing, if you come across a site where you need that cookie it's easy to turn it on accept the cookie and turn it off again.
