### Post by Najand on 2006-09-15
Oh, Can't you see it?
Well, then "Right Click" on the Ubuntu Sign on top left of the page. And Select "Edit Menu". Wait for a moment until Menu editor loads. Then Click On the "Internet" on left and check "Bittorrent" then Click "Close".
Now you are able to find it at Application -> Internet -> Bittorent.
