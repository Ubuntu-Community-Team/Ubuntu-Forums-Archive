### Post by Capricori on 2007-12-28
Just one more thing, I don't think it's mentioned on the psychocats page.
When you're planning where stuff is going to go on your hard drive, and how to partition it etc (and it is best to plan first), make sure that the Windows stuff is on the first partition on the master hard drive (most probably the C: in Windows). Windows has been known to get moody if its not at the beginning :)

Have a read of the psychocats page, and then come back here and ask if you have any questions. It's better to spend half an hour talking to people and then get it right, than it is to jump straight in and have to repeat the process three times :)

Regards,
