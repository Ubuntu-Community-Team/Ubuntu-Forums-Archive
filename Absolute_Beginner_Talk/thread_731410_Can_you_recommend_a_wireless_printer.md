### Post by PilotJLR on 2008-03-21
Very few printers are (or have an manufacturer option to become) wireless. Instead, just buy any USB attached printer you want, and then add a wireless print server:
[http://www.dlink.com/products/?sec=1&pid=482](http://www.dlink.com/products/?sec=1&pid=482)

There are similar products from other home networking vendors too.

For printers themselves, you can't go wrong with an HP Laserjet! Unless you want photo inkjet, which is good with HP and Epson (in my experience).
