### Post by AtrejuT on 2007-03-28
what don't you like about the new settings?
It should not be much of a problem to restore the panel to what it was before:
- add a panel by right-clicking on an existing panel and select 'add panel'
- right click on the new panel, select 'add to panel' and add:
   - Show Desktop
   - Windowlist
   - Workspace Switcher
   - Trash
- right click on the items, select 'move' and arrange them as they were before.

Or do I missunderstand your question?
