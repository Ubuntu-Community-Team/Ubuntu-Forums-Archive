### Post by eolson on 2008-01-05
If you can find an old distro,  it would work fine.  I used to dual boot Redhat 7 and Mandrake (I think 8) on a system much like that except I had a 6 gig hard drive.   It won't be as touch feely as the new stuff,  but you'll learn a lot.  Both of them booted to the command line and you needed to startx to get to the gui.  How well the sound etc will work is pretty iffy.  But at worse you'll have a stand alone computer to play with.
