### Post by Big_Croc7 on 2007-05-16
To answer your second question:
You can put Ubuntu on your 30Gb partition - installing it will involve reformatting the partition, so you'll lose any data on it.  You can't run both simultaneously that way, but you can 'dual-boot', so you can have other operating systems on the other partitions and choose which you want at start-up.  The Ubuntu installer will do this automatically for you.
