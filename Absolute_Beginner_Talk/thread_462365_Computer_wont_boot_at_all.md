### Post by Stew2 on 2007-06-02
Clicking sound? Is it the drives? If it is not the drives perhaps there is a short between the case and the motheroard... maybe a standoff in the wrong place or a loose screw behind the mobo. Obvious things I know but they are always the first things I check :). Also are you getting any BIOS beeps? If you are you can check the mobo manual and see if the beeps tell you what the problem is. Not much I know but just a few of the simple things I can think of. HTH
