### Post by mcduck on 2006-01-03
Alt-F2 opens 'run command' dialog.

Ctrl-Alt-Backspace restarts X-server (your graphical desktop)

I like to add a shortcut Alt+Enter to maximize windows, it works with all apps and maximizes them without titlear or any other window decorations.. (It's also the only way I get some apps to fullscreen)

Ctrl-Alt-F1..F6 toggle between virtual terminals (command line)
Ctrl-Alt-F7-> to toggle between X sessions 

Ctrl-Alt-Tab toggles focus between gnome's panels and stuff so you can open things even if your mouse stops working.
