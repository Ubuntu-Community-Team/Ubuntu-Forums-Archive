### Post by benfindlay on 2006-09-12
Linux doesn't use .exe files like windows does. If you want to install drivers for your wifi card, I'd suggest installing ndiswrapper using Synaptic Package Manager. You could also try searching [http://sourceforge.net/]("http://sourceforge.net/") for linux drivers for your specific hardware

As for accessing other partitions, did you choose to mount them during the installation? Are they ntfs? Are they fat32?
