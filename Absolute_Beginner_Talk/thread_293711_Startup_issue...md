### Post by lodravah on 2006-11-05
The answer is: There isn't, as far as I know.. What I did find out, was that the "automatically save session" was checked, and that skype, for some reason, had five running processes every time I closed Xfce. So naturally, since the processes were running, and I apparently had told my pc to save my settings, the processes ran every time I started the damn thing.. What I did was just to end all the processes that was not supposed to be there, and then save the session at rebooting. 

Hey presto...
