### Post by janrinok on 2005-12-12
geor1712 - I'm a bit confused by your hardware.  Your hardware (Netgear WG311 Wireless PCI card) will connect to another wireless card or to a wireless router.  So either the computer containing the 2nd wireless card or the router must be connected to your ISP somewhere.  You cannot simply insert a wireless card and pick up instant internet access. (Well you possibly can if someone near to you is providing accidental wireless access, or you have placed your computer in a wireless hotspot, but I guess that is not the case in this instance).  So we need to know more about how you connect to your ISP.  Do you have a modem or is this computer part of a larger wireless network, in which case please provide details?  Jan
