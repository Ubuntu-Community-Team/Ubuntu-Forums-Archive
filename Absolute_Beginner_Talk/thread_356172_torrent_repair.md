m0rph's Boggy Step 1 - Torrent Repair/Resume

After using the default Bit Torrent you only have the option to pause or close your torrent.
Whats happens if you haven't saved the torrent link but chose to open the torrent and you accidentally closed the window or like i just did, rebooted.

Step 1: - Backup your 1/2 download file somewhere safe
Step 2: - Go to your history in your browser and this time save the same torrent link 
onto your desktop
Step 3: - Start this download from the saved torrent link.
Step 4: - Saved to any location.
Step 5: - Now close the torrent once it has started.
Step 6: - Now copy your Backup over the top of your new download file
Step 7: - Run the torrent link on the desktop.
Step 8: - All is forgiven and it will continue on.
