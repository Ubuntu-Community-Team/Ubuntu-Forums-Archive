### Post by seijuro on 2006-11-20
I've run Mandrake in the past everything I can think of off the top of my head I have running in Kubuntu. (K)ubuntu is a mega paired down install compared to Mandrake however any extras you need are easy to obtain from the repository. I'm not sure about your applications like Maya and such perhaps someone else may know. Same deal with the codecs you will have to download them from a repo.
