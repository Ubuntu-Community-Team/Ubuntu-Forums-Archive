### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-08-31
You'll need to set the nautilus preferences to show previews of those file types or type.  What are they, bmp, ico?  Whatever they are, set them to display previews and the nautilus icon view will then show your icon thumbnail, very small, depending on your icon size preference settings, but a thumbnail nonetheless.
