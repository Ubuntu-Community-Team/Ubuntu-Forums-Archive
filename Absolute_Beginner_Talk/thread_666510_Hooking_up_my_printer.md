### Post by Victormd on 2008-01-13
I have a Canon MP460 and had the same problem. If you open the printer properties under System>Administration>Printing, you can change your printer driver. That's what I had to do. I had to downgrade the driver to get my printer to work because MP460 did not have a native driver and was setup as a "text only printer." Check to see if the same shows up for you and if so, try selecting other drivers under "Make and Model". I took a quick look and saw that there is a driver for canon i560, not i550. You might want to try to select that driver instead and see if it can print.
