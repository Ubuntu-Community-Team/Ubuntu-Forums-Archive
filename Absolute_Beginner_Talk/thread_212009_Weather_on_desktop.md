> **cyberlite said:**
> Is there any progam to get the weather on you desktop??? i saw one in linspire that was very good.

I know of a few options. In gnome there is a gnome-panel applet that will report the weather to you. right click on a panel and select "add to panel". It is listed under accessories

Another option is by using gdesklets, also in gnome. I don't use it but I am certain that there will be one in there, it will sit on your desktop above the background and look funky, but will drain resources.

KDE is a different kettle of fish, but I havent dipped myself into KDE in a while so I cant help there either, although from memory there was a similar weather app in the KDE panel applets.
