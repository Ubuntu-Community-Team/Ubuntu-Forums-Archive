### Post by poofyhairguy on 2005-06-18
[QUOTE=bloodpuppy]what's the difference between official and unofficial packages? is it like stable and beta releases?[/QUOTE]


No, not really. Official packages are maintained by the Ubuntu developers. Within the set of official packages (only the main and restricted are official, the Universe and the Backports-the one that gives you that message- are not) is the base Ubuntu system. The developers have made the divide because they sell their support for Ubuntu, but only for the official packages. Unless you plan to buy support from them, its ok to to whatever.

The package you are trying to install that gives you that error is maintained by Jdong, not a paid to do it developer. I assure you that jdongs work is of the same quality.
