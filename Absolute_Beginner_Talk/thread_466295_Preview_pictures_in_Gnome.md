### Post by steeleyuk on 2007-06-06
In Nautilus (Gnome) you can have icons, thumbnail view or list view. You can choose list or icon/thumbnail using the drop down box in the top right hand corner.

To switch between icon and thumbnail, you need to disable (or enable) previews in Nautilus preferences.
