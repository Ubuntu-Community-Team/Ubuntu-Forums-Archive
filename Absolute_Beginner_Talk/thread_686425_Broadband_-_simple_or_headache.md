### Post by bumanie on 2008-02-03
Most users have trouble free connection, although there were some who were plagued by an issue with the ipv6 stack. If ipv6 is causing connection problems, it can easily be blacklisted. It is unfortunate that your query can't be answered with 100% certainty as there are sometimes unforseen hardware conflicts that are not apparent until something different is tried/installed. All you can do is try and see what happens. If you have problems, post here, there is usually someone who can help.
