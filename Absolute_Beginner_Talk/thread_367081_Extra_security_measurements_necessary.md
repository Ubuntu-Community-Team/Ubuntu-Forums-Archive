### Post by PurplePenguin on 2007-02-21
I've never heard of people getting spam from using online banking, but you definitely will get a lot of spam if you use your email address for stuff like contests, registration at sites for various companies, or even if your email address is written in plain view - spammers do have ways to scour the internet for every email address they can find.  

You won't need any anti-virus software unless you've, say, got a network with a Windows machine connected.  This way, you can scan the other machine from linux, or maybe you use your linux box as a fileserver, etc.

The site that 23meg gave is a really good one to check out... very informative.
