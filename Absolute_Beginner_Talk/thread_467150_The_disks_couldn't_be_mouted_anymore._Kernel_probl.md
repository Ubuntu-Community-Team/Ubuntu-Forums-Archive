I too had similar problems but was using the Automatix Auto-mounter for NTFS drives.
(Automatically makes NTFS partitions read and writeable)

Fixed by un-installing and re-installing the Auto-mounter utility.

[www.getautomatix.com](www.getautomatix.com) NTFS automounter is available in "Miscellaneous" category after you've installed Automatix.
