### Post by aysiu on 2006-10-17
Why should you use it? I don't know.

Not everybody should.

Why do *I* use it? 

1. It does what I want it to. 
2. Program installation is easy (for the programs I want, anyway)
3. It's free.
4. It will always be free.
5. Upgrades are free.
6. There's no activation key to lose. No activation, as a matter of fact.
7. Less (or no) spyware, viruses, adware.
8. Easy customization (don't give me bull about LiteStep--I've tried it... I've tried *everything* people have suggested) built right into the OS itself.
9. Better documentation
10. Live CDs that can install, preview, and rescue
