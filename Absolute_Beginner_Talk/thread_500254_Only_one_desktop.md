### Post by pbaehr on 2007-07-13
1) The window borders disappeared because there was a problem with your Beryl settings.
2) To get your other desktops back, right click on the switcher icon (lower right hand corner by default) and go into the settings. You should be able to set the number of desktops there.
3) Before you get your 3D cube switcher working, you'll need to get Beryl working properly. I'd focus on that, first.

Good luck!
