### Post by m.musashi on 2006-02-01
[QUOTE=earobinson]I searched the forums for [pronouce ubuntu] and found [this]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=10216&highlight=pronouce+ubuntu")[/QUOTE]

The above forum link shows the wide variety of pronunciations. I think people from different countries tend to pronounce foreign words according to their own native language rules. Despite these local variations, there is a "correct" way to say Ubuntu or any foreign word and that is the way those who speak the original language would say it (of course they don't always agree either).

For the most "official" pronunciation however, visit this page on the Ubuntu site. It's kind of buried but here is the link:

[http://www.ubuntu.com/support/faq?action=show&redirect=FAQ](http://www.ubuntu.com/support/faq?action=show&redirect=FAQ)

Hope that helps.
