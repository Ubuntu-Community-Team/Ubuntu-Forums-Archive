Just fire up synaptic and then hit search, enter in gnutella.  Then install it.

Here are some ones I recommend (in order of preference):

bittorrent:

ktorrent (much like utorrent but native to linux, uses a little more resources than utorrent)
utorrent (using WINE, works well, very lean)
azureus (resource hog)

gnutella:

gtk-gnutella (very smooth, but tends not to find as many files, restrictive)
apollon (pretty much perfect, but uses mroe resources than gtk-gnutella)
frostwire (resource hog)
phex (kind of a unknown choice)

ed2k:

amule

Good luck!
