### Post by Malibu Illusion on 2007-06-24
I have experience with [FDCServers](http://www.fdcservers.net/).  Not saying they're the best or cheapest, but they offer what you're looking for at good rates.

If you only need access to Linux while you're away though, and can't install anything on the machines you do have access to, have you considered/would it be viable to boot from a LiveCD and use Linux with that method?  There are also distributions of Linux, such as Damn Small Linux, which fit and will boot from a USB flash drive.
