### Post by ibutho on 2008-04-13
Its possible. This [article]("http://www.gentoo.org/doc/en/articles/prompt-magic.xml") may help you with that. If you are starting bash using gnome-terminal or konsole, they both have built in schemas in their preferences that change various aspects of the terminal including colors.

### Post by finferflu on 2008-04-13
Yes it is possible. Just for you to know, that console is called TTY. Anyway, this is [a guide](http://www.ibm.com/developerworks/linux/library/l-tip-prompt) for changing the text color in bash. It's not so hard, but it might be a bit confusing, with all those special symbols. 
Notice that this will change colors both for the terminal emulator (the GUI one) and the TTY.

Good luck ;)
