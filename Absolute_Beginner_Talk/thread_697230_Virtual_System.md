### Post by hyper_ch on 2008-02-15
I prefer vmware over virtual box.

Add the Canonical Partner repos to your sources list and you can then download and install vmware-server through APT.

All you then need to do is to go to [www.vmare.com](www.vmare.com) and register yourself to get a working serial.
