### Post by Madpilot on 2006-03-04
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AzureusHowTo](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AzureusHowTo) for your first question.

For your second, how Gnome handles themes is actually a bit odd until you get used to it - but it's very flexible.

To find the new stuff you're installing, hit the "Theme Details" button on the right of the Theme dialogue box.

Look thru the three tabs there, find the new stuff, and assemble your own themes from them and the components Ubuntu comes with.

So if you like the icons that Ubuntu ships with, but not the window borders, you can just change the window borders and create a new custom theme.
