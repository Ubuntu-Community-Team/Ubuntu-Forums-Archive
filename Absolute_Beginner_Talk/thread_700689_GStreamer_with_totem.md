### Post by spiderbatdad on 2008-02-18
just open synaptic package manager...scroll down to totem....uncheck totem-xine--mark for complete removal...check totem, totem gstreamer, totem mozilla, mark all for installation...apply.

### Post by stoneybroke on 2008-02-18
Hi if you go to APPLICATIONS>ADD/REMOVE APPLICATIONS select SHOW ALL in the top left hand box then enter GS in the search bar you will get the list of codecs from gstreamer select the 1st 2 and install these plugins and totem will play all your video amd music files.
