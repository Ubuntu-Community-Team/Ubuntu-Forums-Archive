### Post by Naegling23 on 2006-09-01
mythtv= good
setting up mythtv=bad

You wont be able to control your cable box, as far as I know, from your computer.

If you change the channels using your TV remote (not the cable box) then your computer will work fine controlling the tv.

If you use digital cable, you would need a TV tuner card that can decode a digital signal(or an external cable box).  Im not aware of any, but that doesnt mean that they are not out there.
