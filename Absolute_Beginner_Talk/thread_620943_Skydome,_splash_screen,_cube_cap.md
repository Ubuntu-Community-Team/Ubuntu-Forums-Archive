### Post by ubukool on 2007-11-23
In the context of Compiz Fusion, the splashscreen, if selected in the Compiz options, will display a Compiz fusion logo when Compiz fusion is selected as the window manager.  This logo will remain on the screen for a couple of seconds and then disappear.

It has no purpose other than to tell you that compiz fusion is now running - Oh, and to look cool of course!! :cool:

### Post by ubukool on 2007-11-25
You don't need to install the splash screen, just tick the right option in compiz manager.  Go to System--->Preferences----->Advanced Desktop Effects Settings.  Under 'Extras' you'll see 'splash', just tick the box next to this and voila....your splash screen will appear in all its glory!
