### Post by jhenager on 2008-02-26
> **Kilz said:**
> You may have to enter the bios and tell your computer to try the cd in the boot sequence. There may also be a message when starting up that says to hit a F key to enter the boot menu.

Setting the BIOS to boot from CD-ROM first, and HDD0 second is what I usually do. That way, if you don't have a bootable CD in the drive, your normal OS boots up.
For many computers, you hit DEL right at the first screen (POST). For Dells, F2 to enter Setup (BIOS).
