### Post by Inxsible on 2007-06-08
I am not currently on the Ubuntu machine. So the instructions might not be 100%. But everything that you need to do is in one place..so you will see what I mean.
 
If you dont have a bottom panel already
 
1) Right click top panel --> Select Add Panel.
2) Put the new panel on the bottom.
 
**Start here if you already have a bottom panel**
1) Right Click the Bottom Panel and Select Properties.
2) Deselect the "Expand..." property.
3) Increase the size to 50 (I think its defaulted to 25 - i might be wrong)
4) Now add the launchers that you want to the panel.
 
I keep the trash icon, the window list, the terminal, firefox, thunderbird,opera and of course the home folder. You can add additional ones you want.
 
I shall update this -- if I am wrong on any instructions. But seeing that its so easy, I am sure people will figure it out themselves ! :D
