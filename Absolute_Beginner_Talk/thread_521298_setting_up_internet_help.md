### Post by deadgobby on 2007-08-09
Is your wired  a cable modem or DSL? A cable modem should work right off the bat. Just turn on the modem before boot up and should be good to go. Ubie will auto detect it and same with most DSL. However, some broadcom users do have some difficulty on getting on the net. A wired router should work  the same way. I have a Cisco cable modem with Cisco wired router and fly like the wind. I had to call up my cable provider because I changed cable modems and they had to reset the signal.  So depending on your ISP is the main thing.
