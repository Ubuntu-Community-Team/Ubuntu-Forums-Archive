### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-12-12
I often find it easier to do a google search but specify the domain for ubuntuforums.org only.  You can do this by typing this in the google search bar:-
**search words etc site:ubuntuforums.org**
It usually comes up with appropriate answers quicker than using the forum search system, and is often much more useful.  Give it a try!
