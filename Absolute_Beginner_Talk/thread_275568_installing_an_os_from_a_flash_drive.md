### Post by rlozano on 2006-10-13
im not sure if there's a way of using just your flash drive to boot ubuntu but there's a way using the combination of your LiveCD and flashdrive.

during boot up menu of LiveCD, press F4 or F6 then a sort of a command line will appear. add a space and the word persistent. you should be able to save the changes you made in youe USB flash drive.

or, if your flash drive is big enough, say 2gb, u can actually configure your PC to booth from a USB device, if your BIOS will allow it.
