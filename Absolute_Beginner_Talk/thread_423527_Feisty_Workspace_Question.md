### Post by jfinkels on 2007-04-26
Right click on the panel, select "Add to desktop..." and select "Workspace switcher".

When Beryl is not active, it will look like your standard workspace switcher, but once Beryl turns on, it will be 4 workspaces wide (to account for the four faces of the cube).
