Not only do mounted vfat partitions seem to randomly appear and disappear from your Places menu, they on rare occaisons they will also appear on your desktop for one or two resets, before disappearing again. Virtually mounted network drives  (i.e. ones you 'connect' to using the 'Connect to server' option on the menu) also seem to exibit the same strange behaviour...

As for having the partition appear in Places, within the same Create Launcher display, I simply selected browse, then found my partition and selected Add.
