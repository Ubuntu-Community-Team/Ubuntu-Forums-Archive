### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-04-27
Yup there is.

System > Menu Layout

That lets you move anything around anywhere, drag and drop at will. As you see on right menu = folder, item = program not listed and seperator is just a line. Have fun :)

Edit: Forgot, right click on the applications menu, it sends you straight to layout editor.
