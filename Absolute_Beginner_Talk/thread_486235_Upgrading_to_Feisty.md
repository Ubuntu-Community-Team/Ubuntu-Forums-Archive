### Post by k33bz on 2007-06-27
> **meanburrito920 said:**
> When you upgrade to Feisty, will all your old files and progs be saved and still be there, or do you have to reinstall everything?

yes, when you upgrade all your files and programs will remain intact, however some programs might not work. In rare cases, such as mine, you would have to do a clean install. (just in case you happened to be one of the unlucky ones that comes across that option).

so feel free to upgrade, but do make back ups on files, as well as a list of programs you have installed, again it is rare that you might have to do a clean install, very rare.
