### Post by Mimsy on 2006-11-10
Welcome to Ubuntu! You will be assimilated. :)

First off, back up everything important, or better yet, don't keep anything important on the machine until you feel more comfortable with Ubuntu. It's hard to break things, but it is fully possible - I break stuff on my Ubuntu laptop all the time. Luckily, it is usually very easy to fix the broken stuff, and these forums are a big help.

And check out the Beginner's Guides link in my signature, it has some very good basic how-tos on getting things set up, plus explanations, and links to other helpful resources.

Automatix is also a nice thing to chek out for beginners, if you want help installing mp3-functionality and flash, and stuff like that.

/Mimsy
