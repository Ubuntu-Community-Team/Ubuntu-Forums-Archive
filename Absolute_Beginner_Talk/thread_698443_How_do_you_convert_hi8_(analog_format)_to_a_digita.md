### Post by bumanie on 2008-04-04
You will have to get a video capture card that will convert your hi8 to digital format. After that you can try the various ubuntu video tools to make a dvd. As far as I know, support for most video capture card chipsets are included in the kernel. But you should double check before buying.
PS: Here is a good website to start with re which card to buy.
