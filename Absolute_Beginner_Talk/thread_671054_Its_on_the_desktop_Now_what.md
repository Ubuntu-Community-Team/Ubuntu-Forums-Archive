### Post by crjackson on 2008-01-18
> **Zackie said:**
> this is for the new Songbird?   and btw... DEB means "Auto Install?

Click on the link and look at the version.  DEB doesn't mean AUTO install, but that's pretty much what it IS.  DEB is short for Debian.  Debian is the basic flavor from which Ubuntu was given life.  DEBIAN is both the Linux TYPE and it's name. Debian is a conjunctive word from the common names of Debbie & Ian.

Ian was the creator of this twist of Linux, Debbie was his then Girlfriend.

When you see DEB files, consider them the same as an Microsoft EXE file.
