### Post by pragmatine on 2007-05-24
you can select it, and then it is available in the general X buffer, which you can paste from by middle clicking (ie. click your scroll wheel). Or once you have selected it, you can hit CTRL+SHIFT+C and that will copy it to the normal clipboard, which you can paste from (back into the terminal) using CTRL+SHIFT+V - AFAIK there is no way to drag text from it the way you want.
