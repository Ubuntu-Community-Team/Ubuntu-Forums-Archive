### Post by GMachine_24 on 2007-09-01
If there are truly no device drivers for your hardware, you have two choices:

1. - Check the list of Liinux-compatible hardware and find another network card.

2. - Write a Linux driver for your hardware - this would take a fair understanding of the Linux kernel, C programming and Linux driver writing and file/kernel set up.

I'm guessing finding a Linux-compatible piece of hardware is your best bet.
