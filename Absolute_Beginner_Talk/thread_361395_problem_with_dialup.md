### Post by oilchangeguy on 2007-02-14
first, go to system, administration, networking. and see if your computers modem is listed as active. if not it may be a headache to get it recognized (linux is not a fan of modems). second, i doubt the aol disc will work in linux. and from what i understand, in windows you have to have aol's software, you can't just get the local access number and create a manual connection. if you live in an area where highspeed is available, check prices. some low end packages are available at or near dial-up prices with approx. 10 times faster connection speed. (300-500kbs vs the maybe 50kbs)
