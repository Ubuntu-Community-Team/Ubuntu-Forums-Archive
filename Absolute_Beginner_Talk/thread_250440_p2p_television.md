### Post by kostkon on 2006-09-04
I had used Peercast in the past in Windows but I know that there is also a Linux version of this app. Check [this forum post]("http://peercast.org/forum/viewtopic.php?p=12068") at peercast site for more options. Maybe some of the other p2p TV apps have linux versions.

So, I think you can have p2p TV in Linux with a little effort.

P.S.: the next version of Ubuntu, Edgy, will have the gnome version of Peercast in its repositories, so it will be just a snap to have it installed.
