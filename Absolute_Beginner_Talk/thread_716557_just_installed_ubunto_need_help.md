### Post by talsemgeest on 2008-03-06
So you are saying that you have just installed it and it plonks you in front of a terminal after you reinstall?

If that is the case, then be glad because everything has worked.

However, supposing you want a graphic user interface, you can install xfe on top of it: ```
sudo apt-get install xubuntu-desktop
```
Once that has finished, you will have a nice gui to your server.

There are some nice how-tos in issues 9 and 10 of the free full circle magazine: [http://fullcirclemagazine.org/](http://fullcirclemagazine.org/)

Hope this helps :)
