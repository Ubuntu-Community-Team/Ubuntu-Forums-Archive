### Post by blendmaster on 2006-10-28
Well, If it's from shipit, it's going to be a live CD.

You should test it out to make sure that it works though, and don't forget to change your BIOS settings to read from the cd drive first.

When you run the live CD, you can partition your drive directly from it (you have to partition if you're going to dual-boot ). It's a really easy process!

Enjoy your time with Ubuntu!
