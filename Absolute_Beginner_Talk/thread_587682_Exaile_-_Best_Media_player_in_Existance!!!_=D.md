### Post by ryanVickers on 2007-10-23
I've tried them all, iTunes, Amarok, Listen, etc. etc. but this one stands out above the rest! (and when*** I*** say it beats an apple app, you know it's good! ;))

Some things I like about it:

-Fastest startup ever! (in about literally 1 second, I can kill it and have it up again!)
-Super intuitive!
-Tons of nice features (especially the tabbed play lists :))
-Graphical Equalizer!!!! (I dare you to tell me one other thing (besides XMMS ;)) for Linux that's got that :p)
-it closely resembles the 2 best runners up - Amarok and iTunes :D
-it's for gnome ;)

and so much more, but I don't want to, or can't explain it, it's just got a great feel! Fast, intuitive, extremely feature rich, someone should *seriously* consider replacing Rhythmbox as the default ;)


The only things I think could be improved - it uses between ~32 and 55MB of RAM, and the OSD is rather annoying, so I keep it off ;)
