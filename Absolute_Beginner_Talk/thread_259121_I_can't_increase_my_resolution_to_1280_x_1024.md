### Post by kpham.p on 2006-09-16
Menu:  System->References->Screen Resolution.

BTW: If by edit the "screen" section didn't work--check your monitor manual book for correct HorizoZync and VertRefresh value and add this two line in the monitor section:

Section "Monitor"
........
HorizSync 30-70
VertRefresh 50-160
........
EndSection

After edit the xorg.conf you can use Ctrl+Alt+Backspace to restart the xserver without restart the computer.

goodluck.
