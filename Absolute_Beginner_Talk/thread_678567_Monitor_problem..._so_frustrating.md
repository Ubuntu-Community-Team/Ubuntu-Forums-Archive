### Post by A4006 on 2008-01-26
Use Envy [http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html]("http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html") to install your video card's drivers along with Nvidia's Control Center or ATI's Catalyst.  Be warned what you will have to disable the proprietary drivers for your video card first otherwise you could end up running in low graphics mode at 600x800 resolution.
