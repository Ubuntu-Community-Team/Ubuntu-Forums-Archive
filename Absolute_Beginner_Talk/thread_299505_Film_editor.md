### Post by Marsolin on 2006-11-14
There are quite a few. [Cinelerra]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/cinelerra"), [Jahshaka]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/jahshaka"), [Avidemux]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/avidemux"), [PiTiVi]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/pitivi"), [LiVES]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/lives"), and [KDEnlive]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/kdenlive") are some of them. Another option is [Kino]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/kino"). It works best when capturing video from the digital video camera.
