### Post by rodtempleton on 2007-08-24
Whenever I plug in my 2nd gen iPod Nano, Rhythmbox comes up.  Only problem is, I don't use Rhythmbox for managing the Nano, I use gtkpod (mainly because that's what I've been able to get to work).

Is there a way to specify that I want gtkpod to open when the system detects that the iPod is plugged in?  

Any help that anyone could provide would be greatly appreciated.

Cheers,
Rod

---

### Post by Inxsible on 2007-08-24
Go to System --> Preferences -->Removable Drives and Media. Select the Multimedia tab and then change the application that opens when you connect an iPod. you can have gtkpod open up, or if you leave it blank, then nothing will happen.

---

### Post by wormser on 2007-08-24
I do not have a device to test but this should do the trick.  System> Applications> Removeable Drives and Media> Multimedia tab> Portable Music Players> change the command to gtkpod.  Post your results.  :)
