### Post by Carbonfish on 2006-09-06
Thanks fiver22,

I appreciate your taking the time and providing the links. I started experimenting while waiting for a reply and stumbled on a "fix" that seems to have worked. 

I case anyone else has the same issues, I went to Tools>Options>Language Settings>Languages and from there changed the defaults (which thought that I was Canadian because when I did the initial Ubuntu set-up I chose Vancouver, Canada as the closest time zone) to English (USA). I then followed the same path to "Writing Aids" and made sure that the dictionaries that I needed were checked. After making the changes a dialog box opened telling me that the changes would take effect the next time I opened OpenOffice.org Writer.

That was it. Now everrything seems to be hunky-dorey.

Thanks again, and if anyone else jumps in to help, thanks to you as well.
