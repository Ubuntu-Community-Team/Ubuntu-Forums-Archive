### Post by Skrewdriver on 2006-03-29
sorry about the delay in replying.
You need to download, compile and install the iBurst drivers. 
It's not as hard as it sounds. Have a look at the [iBurst PCMCIA how-to]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=135057"), the process is pretty much the same (make, install) except you don't have to configure any PCMCIA options.
Note: The connection will be called ib0
