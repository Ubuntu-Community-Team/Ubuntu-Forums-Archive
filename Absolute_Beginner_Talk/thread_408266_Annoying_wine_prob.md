### Post by david_kt on 2007-04-13
> **Doug11 said:**
> I just compiled wine from source. The annoying thing is if I have an .exe file on the desktop, you can usually just right-click and choose "install with wine. But I don't get that option. I have to install through the terminal with > wine *whatever*  <  Anyone know a way around this?

Right click your exe file, choose property, and click "open with" tab, add program, custom command, and type wine there.

After that, you should have a choice to run it with wine when you right click any exe file.
