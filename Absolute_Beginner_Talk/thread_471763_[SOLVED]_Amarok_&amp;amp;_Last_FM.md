### Post by Sethalos on 2007-06-12
I'm a huge Last.FM user, and yes you can play the radio stations through AmoroK,  you can simply click on Playlist - Add Last.fm stream, and I generally choose Neighborhood.

AmaroK also keeps track of the music you are playing you can see this by going to [www.last.fm](www.last.fm) and clicking on your profile settings. You'll see it listed there.

I have noticed that sometimes AmoroK can be finicky when I try to get the radio station to play through my player. Sometimes takes a few tries.
