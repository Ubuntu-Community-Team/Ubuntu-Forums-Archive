### Post by mali2297 on 2007-10-11
To copy/paste: 

Highlight the area you want to copy. Then move the cursor to where you want to paste, left-click  and  *middle-click*.

For useful keybindings in the terminal, see here:
[http://linuxhelp.blogspot.com/2005/08/bash-shell-shortcuts.html](http://linuxhelp.blogspot.com/2005/08/bash-shell-shortcuts.html)
