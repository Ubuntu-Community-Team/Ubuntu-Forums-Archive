### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-31
A few initial questions:

1. Did you check out the integrity of the CD first, by confirming that the MD5 checksum was correct?

2. I assume that you burnt the CD as an iso9660 image, not just a regular data disk?

3. At what speed did you burn the iso? Anything faster than 4 X for any iso can cause big problems with errors.
