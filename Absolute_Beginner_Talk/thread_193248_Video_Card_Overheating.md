### Post by u.b.u.n.t.u on 2006-06-09
Are you using the BIOS for the temperature readout or a program? No 2 programs agree on temperatures. You may want to VERY carefully clean your video card fan (only if you know what you are doing or ask someone who does). I don't know how much you know so I need to state that to be safe.

What is the ambient temperature? 60oC when it is 20oC is different to 60oC when it is 40oC. You may want to add an extra case fan. Blowing air cools, sucking air does not cool. So you want a fan to blow air into the case. I have a fan on about a 90 degree angle to my video card blowing air in.

I have other boxes with the sides off, as I am always playing with the hardware like a lego set for kids lol.

Video cards do get hot, some models don't provide adequate cooling. What card do you have?
