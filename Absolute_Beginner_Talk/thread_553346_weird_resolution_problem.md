### Post by Ashrael on 2007-09-17
When you go to a lower resolution it moves them to keep them visible, it just forgets to move them back again.....

Right click the thing you wanna move and deselect : LOCK TO PANEL...
Right click again and MOVE and enable LOCK TO PANEL again...

It will forget again if you go to a lower resolution.....


Hope this helps...
