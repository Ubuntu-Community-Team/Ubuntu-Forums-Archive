Well, since you're downloading from work, I'd reckon the broadband system is configured by your employer's IT person/staff. As bittorrent is also widely used to distribute illegal content, most businesses do not allow bittorrent traffic over their connections to the internet through the use of a firewall of some sorts. The general idea for businesses is to be as secure as possible, and only allowing what's necessary (web & email sums it up for most businesses).

> Right, after my ramblings :) My actual question is... do I have to keep downloading over and over again till the md5sum finally matches?

Yes. The system will not boot or install properly otherwise.

Also, try a different mirror. It's entirely possible that the mirror has a corrupt copy of the ISO or that there's some flaky router along the path that introduces subtle errors in the bitstream.
