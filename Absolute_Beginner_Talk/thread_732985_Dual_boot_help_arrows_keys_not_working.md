### Post by torgrot on 2008-03-23
Go into the BIOS and look to see if there is an option for legacy keyboard support.  Change it to whatever it isn't.  Sometimes my wireless keyboard on a usb port isn't initialized in time for grub to recognize it.  Sometimes just going into the BIOS and exiting is all it needs to get the keyboard recognized.
