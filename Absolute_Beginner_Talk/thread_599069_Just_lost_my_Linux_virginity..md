Hello, uh... new to the neighborhood and all so I thought I'd drop by and say hi.

I started with MS-DOS, moved up the Windows chain from 3.11 to XP, and now I've finally given Linux a try with Ubuntu 7.10.  I've always liked the idea of an open source OS and was finally sold on it after working with apps like Blender and Open Office within XP.  They really hammered home the fact that high quality projects can  be created and maintained by community effort.

I set my laptop up as a dual boot; the installation wasn't too bad.  Most everything works well, aside from some sound issues which will hopefully be worked out (audio stutters except when I make use of my usb mouse or keyboard).

I like it.
