### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-03-26
I believe somebody forgot to change the title page for the Disctree application.

If the title of the disc in My Computer includes "6.10", then it's definitely Edgy. If you boot up from it and you get a progress bar that starts by going backwards and forwards like a Cylon's eye, then you've definately got Edgy. If it just goes left to right with only a couple of shades of yellow and orange in the Ubuntu logo, then you've got Dapper.
