### Post by Bachstelze on 2007-01-21
Ctrl+1 (or Window > Show preview) should give you the preview window, which has an "Acquire preview button (see [screenshot](http://img167.imageshack.us/img167/7757/xsane9wn.jpg), the window on the right). Then it should show you your whole image and let you choos ewhich area of it you want to scan.
