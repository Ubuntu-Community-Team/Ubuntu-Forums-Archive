### Post by jvc26 on 2007-01-29
A typical vision of a computer architecture has a series of abstraction layers: hardware, firmware, assembler, kernel, operating system and applications. The kernel is the central component of most computer operating systems (OSs). It is responsible for, amongst other things, managing the system's resources and the communication between hardware and software components. For more information you could check out the wiki kernel article.
