### Post by EFobes on 2007-11-18
Actually the problem was not the color but the fact that the screen was frozen... I couldn't reach the desktop.

I did solve it myself without really knowing how. I managed to reach the desktop in "failsafe mode" and then found that there were some GNOME updates to be downloaded. After, downloading them, restarting and logging in normally, everything was back to normal. 

Perhaps the problem  had something to do with some updates that I downloaded before the probelm first appeared... although they are theoretically supposed to all be reliable.
