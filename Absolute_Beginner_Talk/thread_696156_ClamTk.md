Hi Europa7!
The thing is that when you click on the **Scan your home directory** icon, it scans all files inside your home directory, but not the folders or the files in them. To scan all your home directory, you have to click on **File/Recursive Scan** and then select the folder you want.
It should now take a little longer.
Cheers!
