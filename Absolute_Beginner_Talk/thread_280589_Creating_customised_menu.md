### Post by CatKiller on 2006-10-19
The menu is defined by the ~/.config/menus/applications.menu file. You can modify this directly if you want ultimate control over your menu.

I'd advise you not to bother. It's natural to be unsettled by the fact that Linux behaves differently to Windows. Wait a while before you try to re-design the way your computer functions, relax, and soon it will sink in that Linux is different to Windows and what you're used to isn't necessarily any better just because you're used to it.
