### Post by phr0ze on 2007-09-25
The usual answer is this section is wrong

Section "Monitor"
Identifier "Mecer"
Option "DPMS"
HorizSync 28-50
VertRefresh 43-75
EndSection


You need to look up your exact monitor model and find the max HorizSync and VertRefresh. Until these are correct you can not use the higher resolutions.
