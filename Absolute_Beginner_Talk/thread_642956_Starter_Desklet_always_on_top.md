### Post by liveandletlive on 2007-12-17
I am trying to customize the starter-desklet. I want it to be always on top. I found a way of doing that by modifying the source. 

Right click on starter-desklet and click on view source.

Find the following line

[COLOR="DarkRed"]<display anchor="center" window-flags="below, sticky"[/COLOR]

Here if you change from [COLOR="DarkRed"]*below*[/COLOR] to [COLOR="DarkRed"]*above*[/COLOR] it does the trick. 

You would need to restart the desklet. 

However, when it stays on top, there is a big border surrounding this desklet. It comes on top of every window.

Is there any way to make it less obtrusive?
