### Post by mrtaber on 2005-05-14
I have installed Ubuntu Hoary successfully on VMWare 4.5 and 5.0.  You might choose "Custom Virtual Machine" instead of "Standard" and there choose an "IDE" instead of "SCSI" hard drive for your virtual hard drive.  See if that works--it did for me.

Hope this helps,
Mark :)
