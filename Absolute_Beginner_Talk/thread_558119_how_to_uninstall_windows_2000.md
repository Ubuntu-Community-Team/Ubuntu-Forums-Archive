### Post by st33med on 2007-09-23
Go and download and burn the [Gparted LIVECD]("http://sourceforge.net/project/downloading.php?group_id=115843&use_mirror=easynews&filename=gparted-livecd-0.3.4-8.iso&58372976").
You might want to back up your data, just in case.
Boot from the CD.
Delete the NTFS partition and expand EXT3 (or whatever filesystem your Ubuntu uses) to fill up the gap. Continue and enjoy a Windows free computer! :)
