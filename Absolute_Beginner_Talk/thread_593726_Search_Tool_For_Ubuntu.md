### Post by nhandler on 2007-10-27
Right click one of the panels and select "Add to Panel".
There should be something called "Deskbar". Click and drag it to your panel.
Click on it to launch it (The default keyboard shortuct is Alt+F3).
You can then enter your search in the box. You can also use this to search the web for your search term.

The Deskbar is on the top panel by default in Gutsy

Tracker is the search tool in Gutsy.  (Applications>Accessories>Tracker Search Tool) I have used both Beagle in Feisty and Tracker in Gutsy, Tracker works better IMHO, it seems to be a bit lighter on resources.  You need to tell Tracker which drives to index System>Preferences>Indexing Preferences and you can set the Tracker preferences there, it may be giving the error if it has no indexing preferences set?
