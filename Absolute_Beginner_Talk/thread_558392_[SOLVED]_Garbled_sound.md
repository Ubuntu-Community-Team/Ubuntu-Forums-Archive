### Post by energiya on 2007-09-25
Do you have by any chance an onboard card? (maybe AC97 ?) If so, open alsamixer, go to the last switch (press right button) and lower the first VIA DXS to about 74. Play with them until you get a clear sound.
If it isn't AC97, post the card type. Someone might now a solution.
