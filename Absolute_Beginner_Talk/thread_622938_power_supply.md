### Post by jingo811 on 2007-11-25
If it behaves uncontrolled like that then you should start thinking about getting a new PSU.  Not buying the cheapest one usually saves you some headaches.  Buy some mid-ranged-price or expensive PSU that will ensure you getting a quality PSU.

I think I had a similar Antec PSU it didn't respond when pushing the On button I had to press it for 5 minutes before something happened.  Then it sparked when going on like it wanted to explode and burn up anytime.  Get rid of the Antec and get something that didn't come with your chassi.  I bought a Zalman for replacement it's stable.
