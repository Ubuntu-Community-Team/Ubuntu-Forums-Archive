### Post by bren on 2007-07-14
if you are up for it you could investigate long distance WIFI using simple cheap parabolic transmitter/receiver dish for your wifi

you will need line of sight to a friendly neighbour with WIFI who is between 0 and 40km away according to various links I have seen

you would then just agree a tarrif for accessing WIFI from your neighbour and get a copy of his WPA key to login.    Or you could put your own WIFI ADSL router at your neighbour's house and keep independent that way.

anyone else doing this kind of thing here?
