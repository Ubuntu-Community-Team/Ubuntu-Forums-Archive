### Post by Joe Dinmore on 2007-12-31
Recently I have noticed that my modem dials out and attempts to connect to the internet whenever I boot into Ubuntu. 
What could be causing that behaviour, and how can I stop it? I want to connect to the internet only "on demand", not whenever Ubuntu starts up.

---

### Post by Techpenguin on 2007-12-31
You could try clicking the network icon and selecting manual configuration then double click on the modem connection and uncheck 'enable'
