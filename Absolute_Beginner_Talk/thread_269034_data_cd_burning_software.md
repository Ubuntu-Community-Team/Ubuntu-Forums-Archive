### Post by sbergman27 on 2006-10-01
The built in Gnome file manager, Nautilus, will do simple CD burning.

Places->CD/DVD creator

Drag your files into the window. And then File->Write to disc

For .iso images, it is even easier.  Just rightclick on the iso and select "write to disc".

It does not do music cd's, however.  Though it can, of course, make mp3 or ogg vorbis cds.

For more complex stuff, gnometoaster or gcombust are good.  But I rarely need anything but nautilus.
