### Post by pveith on 2007-03-09
Don't want to start a flame. But IMHO you are better of getting a nvidia card as their driver offers better support (like switching Screen-Layout on the fly [from single monitor to xinerama dual monitor without Xorg restart]). And nvidia provides a directly supported rendering-platfrom for beryl, which works like a charm for me. For ATI cards you might need to install XGL instead of Xorg, which requires you to go through  another "howto"... But that are just my biased 2 cents.
