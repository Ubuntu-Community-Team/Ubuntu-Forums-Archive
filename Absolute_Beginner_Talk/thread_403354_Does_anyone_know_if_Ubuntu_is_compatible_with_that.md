### Post by igknighted on 2007-04-06
Card reader may or may not work, those are allways oddball devices, you can't be too sure... but I would say with 90% surety it will work.  The wireless might also be an issue, there is no reference to what type of wireless chip it has.  You will definitely be able to get it to work, the question is with how much work.
