### Post by p_quarles on 2007-10-02
I second the recommendation of the O'Reilly Linux series. One in particular, _Ubuntu Hacks_, came in really handy for me. It covers the basics of networking and scripting, as well as all the little ways in which Linux works differently from Windows. 

It's also really helpful to have a guide to Bash commands handy -- I use Scott Granneman's _Linux Phrasebook_.
