### Post by ReaderRat on 2006-11-05
> **compaq_xp said:**
> How do i make a boot disk for the LTS verson of Ubuntu?
Download the .iso file. Burn it to a CD as an image file (.iso), not as a data file. Do _not_ have the burner make it bootable. Burn it at a slooow rate (4X), and you should get a good copy. Place it in your CD drive, Reboot, and change the BIOS option to "boot from CD"., and off you go.

For help:
ISO - Downloading & Burning
          **[http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso](http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso)**
          **[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)**
