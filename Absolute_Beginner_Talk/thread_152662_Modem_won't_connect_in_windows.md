### Post by Bartender on 2006-03-30
Hi, kayla -
Sorry, I don't have specific directions to offer.
Is it a brand new modem?  Do you have any reason to think it's broken?  Did it come with directions for those pesky little dipswitches like my USR 0701 has or does the newer modem dispense with those?
You mention that your other computer dials in "just fine with same connection settings".  I'm assuming this is a Windows box.  If so, does this PC have a serial connection?  You might want to try installing your external to the other PC and seeing if you can get it to work.  I installed the 0701 to my Windows PC.  It was educational to do that.  The modem tried connecting at 9600 until I told it to go faster.  Of course, you'll also have to go to "Modems" in Control Panel and select the serial modem.  But it did work, and that at least told me the modem wasn't broken.
