### Post by gingermark on 2006-02-12
If your PC doesn't boot off the CD automatically, then you'll need to change the boot order in your  BIOS.

You can usually get into your BIOS menu by pressing "Delete" just as your computer starts up. You'll then have to rummage around the menus until you come accross your boot order. Make sure your optical drive is first, save and exit and you should then be able to boot off the CD.
