### Post by zxee on 2006-09-04
You laptop's modem is in all likelihood a winmodem i.e a modem that relies on windows or the operating system to function.
You can go here: [http://linmodems.org/](http://linmodems.org/) and get their scan modem tool and see if your modem can be made to work in linux.
