### Post by Berean on 2008-02-24
It's been suggested there are 60,000 viruses for Windows, but only about 40 (not thousand) for Mac, and 40 for Linux.  To this end, in my opinion you don't need AVG.  But I would advise using a firewall - Firestarter is what I prefer - but also NoScript which I think I'm correct in saying stops sites communicating with your computer with/without you knowing about it.  There is a fantastic thread that suggests what to use for security, but I'm having difficulty finding it at the moment: I'll post again if I find it.  Incidentally, as part of the learning process (and not wishing to sound patronising) find out what Sudo does, and what it is.  Hope this is of some help.
