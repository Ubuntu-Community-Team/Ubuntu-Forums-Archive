### Post by Kuoi on 2007-05-07
Hello ,
I was searching for a replacement to Roboform as a form filler in Firefox, and came on this one ...
[Iopus Imacros]("http://www.iopus.com/imacros/firefox/?ref=fxhome")

You can "record" all you fill in , and save the macro.
Then later , all you have to do is doubleclick the macro in the Imacros toolbar ( wich you can hide or show easely with a button ) , and all goes 'automatic'.

I edit the macros all by deleting the following sentence :
"TAB CLOSEALLOTHERS"
Otherwise if you doubleclick a macro , all other tabs will be closed first.

After some issues with "formfill" , I was looking for something else , and I'm happy I've find this one.

Greetings, Kuoi

It may be a great form filler, but there are still plenty of flaws for now.
