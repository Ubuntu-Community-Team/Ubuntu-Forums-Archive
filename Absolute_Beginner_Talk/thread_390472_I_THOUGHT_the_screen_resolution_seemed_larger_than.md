### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-03-22
System --> Preferences --> Fonts
You can either change the DPI to 75 or so or you can lower the font size.  
Smaller fonts will help make the windows seem smaller as well.

This may be enough for you, but if it isn't there are other things you can change to make things smaller as well.
