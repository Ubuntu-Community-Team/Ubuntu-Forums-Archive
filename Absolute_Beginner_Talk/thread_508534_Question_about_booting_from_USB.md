### Post by Orochi on 2007-07-24
If your USB drive is a flash drive, then keep in mind that flash media has a limited number of writes. Meaning if you use it as a boot drive, it'll stop working much more quickly than if you were simply using it to transfer files. If your USB drive is a regular hard drive then there's no such problem. The Zen Vision M I believe has a regular hard drive, so you should be ok.
