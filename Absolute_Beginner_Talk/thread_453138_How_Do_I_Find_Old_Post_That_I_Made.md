### Post by Arndt on 2007-05-24
> **drakebarimore said:**
> I have posted a few times in here and I never know how to go back and see the replies to my posts.  I usually just wait around to see what people have to say and then when I have to leave, I never am able to find those posts again.

Is there a way to keep up with the posts whenever I return to the forum after having been gone for a long time?

Under "User CP", go to Edit Options and set the Default Thread Subscription Mode to something other than "Do not subscribe". Then posts you make add you to a subscription to that thread, and you can find new posts in those threads directly by using User CP -> Subscribed Threads.
