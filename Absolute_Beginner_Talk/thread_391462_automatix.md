### Post by PartisanEntity on 2007-03-23
Their repos are offline very often. And when that happens you have many new users posting here asking why they're are getting *apt-get update* errors.

Also, it is not a good idea for newbies to get used to automatix, you should learn how to install software without some helper script, otherwise you will never really understand how the OS works.

Finally, based on my experiences, installing applications manually is by far better and they are less prone to errors and problems.

Just an FYI....

Feisty has made it easy to do all the important stuff automatix did....manually - and it's way more stable than having a 3rd party app "modify" your /etc/apt/sources.list file.
