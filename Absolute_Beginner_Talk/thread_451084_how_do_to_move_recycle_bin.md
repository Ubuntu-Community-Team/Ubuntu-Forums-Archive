### Post by gradedcheese on 2007-05-22
Run "gconf-editor" (Alt+F2 gets you a "run" box), in there go to apps->nautilus->desktop and checkmark "trash icon visible", then close the editor.

The reason for this is how nautilus gets configured, and the Trash icon in the panel is a separate little applet.
