What's the difference between "Applications >> Add/Remove" and "System >> Administration >> Synaptic ..." ??

---

### Post by aysiu on 2006-08-16
Add/Remove has fewer applications, most of which are point-and-click applications, and its give you fewer options for searching.

Synaptic has all available applications, some of which are terminal applications, and it gives you more options for searching.
