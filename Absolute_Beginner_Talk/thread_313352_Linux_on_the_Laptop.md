### Post by thepaul on 2006-12-05
I decided to see if Ubuntu would work on my Dell Inspiron 1000 laptop.  At first the installation stalled so I had to reboot my computer.  However the second installation worked and I was able to boot to Linux.  :cool: 

I plugged in the ethernet cable and was on the Net.  I changed the wallpaper and downloaded a few programs, including easy Ubuntu. 

After a few hours, and reading the documents about Wifi, I plugged in the wifi card and unplugged the cable. The lights on the card blinked, became steady and I opened up firefox.  I clicked on one of the links and I was online.  

So, I can surf, listen to mp3's, watch DVD's and am going to have a great time.
