### Post by perce on 2007-11-19
What have you tried so far? for example, have you tried to connect your printer (I assume it is a USB printer), turn it on, and go to System > Administration > Printing > add a new printer? you should see your printer and the program should guide you through the configuration. If it happens that your printer is not in the list of supported HP printers (it happens to me for example) then try the model with closest name (for me it works).
