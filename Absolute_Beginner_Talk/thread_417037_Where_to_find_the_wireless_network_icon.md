### Post by nickpaton on 2007-04-21
Could be worth trying Gnome panel at the top of the screen.

Hover mouse over the top panel,, right click > Add to Panel > System and Hardware >Network Monitor.

This gives a wireless icon with a level indicator, but possibly not the stepped one you're talking about.

Could be worth looking elsewhere in the Gnome Panel.
