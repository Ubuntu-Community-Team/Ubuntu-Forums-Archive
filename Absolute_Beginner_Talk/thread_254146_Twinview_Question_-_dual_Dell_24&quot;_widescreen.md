### Post by thexaspect on 2006-11-25
I've had a similar problem, but with two 20" dell's and a dual head 7600 GT. You need to change your display settings here in xorg.conf:
> **alkith said:**
> 

SubSection "Display"
Depth 16
Modes "1920x1440" "1920x1200" "1856x1392" "1792x1344" "1680x1050" "1600x1200" "1440x900" "1400x1050" "1280x1024" "1280x960" "1280x854" "1280x800" "1280x768" "1200x800" "1152x864" "1152x768" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"
EndSubSection

And change it to something like:

SubSection "Display"
Depth 16
MetaModes 1920x1440,1920x1440 1920x1200,1920x1200 
etc etc
Just keep backups, and fiddle. But that's what I did with metamodes, and it works great =)
