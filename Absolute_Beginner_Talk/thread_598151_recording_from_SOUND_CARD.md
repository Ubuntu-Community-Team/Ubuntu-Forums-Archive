### Post by srhlefty on 2007-10-31
use a male-male 3.5mm cable to pipe the sound that would be going to your speakers, to your line-in port on your sound card.  you can then record the line-in channel with any recording software.

of course, then you can't hear what's playing on the speakers.  to get around that, get a 3.5mm cable splitter, preferably 1 male to 2 females.  you then hook the male end in the sound output of your computer, and plug the speakers into one female, and the cable mentioned above into the other.
