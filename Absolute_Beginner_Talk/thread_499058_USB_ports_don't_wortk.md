### Post by Al3xK3aton on 2007-07-12
Make sure the USB device is plugged in all the way. If it's only partway in, it won't make a complete connection. Once it's in, wiggle the cord. If that helps, the problem is that you need a new cord. Otherwise you may have to install a new USB card.

Have you tried other devices, such as a thumbstick, or a USB flashlight or a USB mug warmer?
