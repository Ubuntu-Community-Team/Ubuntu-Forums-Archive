### Post by Arthur Archnix on 2007-11-17
Well, you could just tell your music player to monitor the limewire folder for new files and add them to the library. That would probably be easiest. You'd still need to know where download folder is.

Alternatively, you could also mv or cp the files from the download directory to your music library. The general form is cp /path/to/file.mp3 /path/to/library/
