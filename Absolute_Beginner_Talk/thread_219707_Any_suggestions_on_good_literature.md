### Post by avtolle on 2006-07-20
I can recommend a fairly new book: *The Linux Bible* by Christopher Negus. It is relatively elementary, but contains much good basic general information. It also comes with a CD and DVD with various Linux Distros thereon, together with at least one "floppy" rescue distro a user can make from the CD. In my case, I checked it out from our local library, and I have been going through it, prior to making a purchase decision. It, too, is available from Amazon.com.
