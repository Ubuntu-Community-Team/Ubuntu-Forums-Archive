### Post by Ampersand on 2005-10-08
1) Warty, Hoary and Breezy were all released 6 months apart, Warty was in October 2004, Hoary was in April 2005 and Breezy will be released on the 13th. Each one has more up to date software than the previous, but they all get security updates for 3 years after they were released.

2) These are for different processor types. IA32 is 32bit intel/amd (the most common), IA64 is 64 bit Intel (I've not seen this used much) and AMD is 64 bit amd  .
