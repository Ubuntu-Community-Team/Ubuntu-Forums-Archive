### Post by mark_g on 2006-11-14
It really depends on your use. If it's a work-critical pc, you might want to stick with Dapper.
If it's your home pc, you're interested in the improvements and you don't mind reinstalling Dapper IF (not very likely) something really did go wrong, I'd go for Edgy. I personally find the new fonts very nice and a good reason to upgrade.
You could also try to search for your "hardware + edgy" on the forums or google to see if other people have any complications. Especially for your graphics card, that's known to be a toughy sometimes.
Good luck.
