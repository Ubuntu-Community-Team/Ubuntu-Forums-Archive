How do I add a folder to the "Places" menu? ('Edit Menus' doesn't show "Places")

### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-12-18
Hi. You have to bookmark it in Nautilus first; then it will show up in the "Places" menu. To bookmark a location in Nautilus, just navigate to the desired directory and press CTRL+D, or go to "Bookmarks->Add Bookmark".
