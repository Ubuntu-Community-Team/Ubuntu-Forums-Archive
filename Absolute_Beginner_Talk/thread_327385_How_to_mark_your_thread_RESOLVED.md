### Post by 2CV67 on 2006-12-29
For dummies like me who would like to signal when their threads are RESOLVED but could not find how to do it, or could not get it to work, here is the method:

[LIST]
[*]Go to your FIRST post in the thread
[*]Scroll down to the BOTTOM of that post
[*]Click on "EDIT"
[*]Click on "GO ADVANCED"
[*]Put something in "Reason for editing" (like "Resolved"...)
[*]TICK in "Thread Resolved:    Check box if your thread **IS** resolved. "
[*]DO NOT click on "Preview Changes" (it would remove your tick...)
[*]CLICK on "Save Changes"
[/LIST]

Voila! You're all done!

But you might like to check by revisiting the forum page where your thread title is listed to make sure it appears with [RESOLVED] after it - try "Reload current page" if not there immediately.

Thanks for helping to sort Resolved issues from Open issues!
Should make it easier for others to find good answers & for helpers to concentrate on unresolved issues.
