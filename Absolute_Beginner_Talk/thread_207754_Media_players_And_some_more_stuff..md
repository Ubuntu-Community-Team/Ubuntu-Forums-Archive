### Post by mrvw0169 on 2006-07-02
For streaming media, I found the mplayer plugin to be able to play videos and audio (real, wma, wmv, avi, mp3, etc.) in firefox well...

```
sudo aptitude install mozilla-mplayer w32codecs
```

For Beep, you can download skins at [www.winamp.com](www.winamp.com)

Personally, I have totem (gstreamer), gxine, mplayer, realplayer, and vlc all installed. My favorite interface for videos though is totem because it is simple and it has fullscreen controls that look nice. The rest are for in case totem won't work since they all have not as "polished" interfaces...

As for audio playing, I use Rhythmbox because I am lazy. I really like amarok though (with its uber features - most I don't need but really nice like lyrics/wiki) but rhythmbox suits me fine... Listen doesn't look nice to me with that bar in the middle (if that were changeable to tabs or something like amarok...)... And Exaile is really new and it shows but I eagerly wait till it matures as it seems the best organized looking amarok lookalike =P
