### Post by Hospadar on 2007-11-09
If you deleted them with the delete key, they probably just got moved to your .Trash folder.

Try having a look by either clicking on the trash icon in the lower right corner of you screen, or open up a file browser window, and either type "~/.Trash" in the navigation bar, or click "trash" in "Places" on the left side.

See if it's in there.

If you used a command like "rm" from the command line, the files would actually be deleted, and are much harder to recover (if it's possible at all)  I believe there are some data recovery tools for linux, but I'm not really sure what they are.

Probably though your stuff just got moved to .Trash
