### Post by bored2k on 2005-07-06
[QUOTE=bk452]after installing rar and unrar-nonfree, in a terminal type rar e tcm-e101.rar ***

Will that give me a movie format? Thank you for being patient.  I'm new at movie stuff.[/QUOTE]
 The .rar file is just a compression format like .ZIP. The .rXX files are files made to make smaller files out of a BIG one. After you extract them, you get a single file of what the creator wanted. In your case, yes you would most likely get an AVI or an MPEG or WMV.

Note: this is not a Linux problem, it's the same everywhere ;).
