This is a guide on how to install ubuntu by Goat Spirit.
(This is the way I installed)
**Requirements:**
-One ISO or CD of Acronis Disk Director
-One ISO or CD of Ubuntu Dabber Drake
-A general or low knowlage of computers
-A brain
or
-A brain

**Step one:**
Alright after you have burned your Acronis ISO to a blank cd or have bought the product, put the cd inside your cd rom drive.
Restart the computer.
If using windows, I think you press F12 or F8 to get into the BIOS. (May also be DEL or other for different computers)
Find the setting for boot-up order, put cd-rom drive as 1.
Save and exit, the nwait for it to restart.
Acronis **should** boot up, if it didn't then check ther settings above.

**Step one and a half:**
After Acronis gets to the boot up screen push "manual".
Find your hard drive and right click each partition=>delete=>select clear all data.
After you have deleted all your partitions, click the flag which means commit. It will take a few, after it is done click file=>exit.
Then when it restarts your computer stick the Ubuntu disk in your cd rom drive.

**Step two:**
Wait for Ubuntu to start, it should take at least 5-15 mins depending on the computer.
After everything is up, click the "install" icon on the desktop.

**Step three:**
Do all the settings, when you get to the partition manager click the option that has use "hard drive name" and overwrite all data.

**Step four:**
After everything is done, click the power button (located in the top right corner of the screen).
Then click restart.
It should eject your cd when you arte finished.

Then BOOM you now have a freshly installed copy of Ubuntu!
