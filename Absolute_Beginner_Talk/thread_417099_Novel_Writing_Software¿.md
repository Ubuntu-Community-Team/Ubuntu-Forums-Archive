### Post by caffienefree on 2007-04-21
You could theoretically use a wiki, and use categories to create associations. For example (this isn't the actual syntax, just a representation), adding category:characters to all character pages would give you a list of characters, category:chapter1 would associate that element with the first chapter. Wikis are pretty extensible to your needs.
