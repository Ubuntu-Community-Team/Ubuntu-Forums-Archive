### Post by mcduck on 2006-08-29
GDM themes are for the login screen. Install them in System/Administration/Login Screen (click the 'Add' button)

GTK themes change how your apps look like, buttons, sliders etc. (in Gnome)
Metacity themes are for window frames in normal Gnome
Icon themes obviously are for icons.
These are all instlled by opening System/Preferences/Theme. You can just drag&drop the theme package to the windo to install them. Click the 'Theme details'-button to change various parts of your theme.

CGWD themes are window frame themes for Compiz. Open Compiz Themer (I'm not sure where it is by default) and click 'Import' to add your own themes.

You'll find wide variety of themes from [http://www.gnome-look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org")
