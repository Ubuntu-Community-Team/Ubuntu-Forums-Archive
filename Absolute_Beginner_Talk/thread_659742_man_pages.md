### Post by nick_h on 2008-01-06
> I am trying to get to know my Command line

The man page for bash is good but long.  You may find it useful to use the yelp help viewer as follows:
```
yelp man:bash &
```

Just listing the directories /bin, /usr/bin, /sbin and /usr/sbin will show you a lot of available commands.

Also have a look at [http://www.linuxcommand.org/](http://www.linuxcommand.org/)
