### Post by Hobo Joe on 2007-08-15
This is possible.

On the install disk, when it asks you what partitions you want to use, just select the one for root, and(optionally) the one for /home. Then press the 'format?' button.

DO NOT PRESS FORMAT ON ANY PARTITIONS BUT THE ONES YOU ARE INSTALLING TO
