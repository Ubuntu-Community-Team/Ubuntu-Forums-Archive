### Post by vikramsharma on 2007-11-30
You can turn off/on the Visual Effects by going to the Ubuntu Menu System_>Preferences_>Appearance.  In Appearance click on the last tab "Visual Effects" from there you should be able turn off/on the Visual Effects.  Blender crashing may not have anything to do with Ubuntu's stability/instability, but might be a bug in the app Blender itself.  Hope this helps.
