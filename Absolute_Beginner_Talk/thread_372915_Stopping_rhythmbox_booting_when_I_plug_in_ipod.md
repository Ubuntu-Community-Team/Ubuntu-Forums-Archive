I'm not very familiar with using Amarok to sync my ipod (I usually use GTKpod myself) but for the connection thing, try going System>Preferences> Removable Drives and Media. ### Post by qamelian on 2007-02-28
Go to 
System > Preferences > Removable Drives and Media. Click on the Multimedia tab and remove the check mark from Play music files when connected under Portable Music Players. That should do the trick. Alternately, to have a different application start, replace the command line with one to start the app of your choice.
