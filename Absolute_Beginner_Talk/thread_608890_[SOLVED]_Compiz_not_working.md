Just in case you don't know this.  I didn't at first.  If you are having a hard time seeing text on the screen in Firefox all you have to do is hold down the control key and hit the + key to make it bigger, the more times the bigger it gets, to make it smaller control - key.  This doesn't actually make the text bigger if you are making a post, it just displays it bigger on screen.  I don't have a lot of trouble most of the time, but if I've been online for hours I have to zoom in a couple of clicks.  Hope this helps.  Later... cos
