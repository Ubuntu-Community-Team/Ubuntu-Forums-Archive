### Post by koconnor100 on 2007-05-01
> **kirfreak said:**
> why r there no viruses in ubuntu? so many people r using it, so why hasnt anybody designed any?

Notice how you are limited to your own little area , the home folder for example. You can't do anything outside of that, including install programs, without typing in your super user password. 

Imagine a virus designed to trash the operating system. It comes in on the browser fire fox (some how ... I think this is actually impossible for fire fox but we'll let that go for the moment) , and it's only got your access rights. It want's to over write the operating system.... 

... up pop's a box asking for your super user password !

You're on a web site looking at porn , and this password box comes up from linux... bound to raise suspicions. Most users are gonna click "cancel"

... and the virus goes no where. 

Why are there no virii in Linux ? 
Because the designers knew better.
