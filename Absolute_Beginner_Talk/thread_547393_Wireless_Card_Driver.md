### Post by BrendanM on 2007-09-10
You'll probably need more specific information than that. Linksys makes a lot of different cards. Try googling for the model number of the card, or searching with the word "chipset" in the string to find out what chipset that card uses.

You can also use the command **lspci** to list devices attached to your computer and try to find the wireless card's info there.
