### Post by spankminister on 2007-07-28
Hey, I'm the project maintainer for Icepodder =)

For Icepodder, just hit the green plus sign.

For gPodder, go to podcast, and add new subscription.

The URL you want to copy and paste is the RSS feed, for example, [http://leoville.tv/podcasts/mc.xml](http://leoville.tv/podcasts/mc.xml)
It'll probably end in xml.

### Post by expatCM on 2007-07-28
The Twit site(s) appear very poor in what they present for subscription information.  

Most sites will give you a string and all you do is copy and paste into the application.  However with Twit this is way off the mark....  they do not give the information and assume that the application will be automatically populated.  

The way I did it with Twit and Security Now for gPodder was to use google to find a directory of podcasts where Twit was listed and then look down the list to find out what the strings were.  Cut and paste to gPodder and it worked.

### Post by philidox on 2007-11-15
I use micro guide and gpodder.  Its the bomb!  Just go to [https://www.miroguide.com](https://www.miroguide.com).  In the search bar just type in what your looking for in terms of channel.  Like cnn, adult swim, etc...  (They have over 2500 channels so go nuts.) Click on the green plus sign with "Add" to the right of it.  A pop up will happen just ignore it.  All you have to do is copy the url right under where its says "now subscribing to:".  And paste that to the podcast url list.
