### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-05
Heheh, EVERYONE wants a dock :D

Short answer, they are unstable at worst and at best mildly buggy and unreliable. I've yet to really find a stable one that I can firmly recommend, as such I'd tell ya to just use the basic panel at the top of your desktop, it will never crash on you. :)

Just right click on it, and select add to panel, then push the application launcher on the top left.

If you must however try one... here are two options.

[AWN]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=385981")

[Kiba]("http://www.kiba-dock.org/components/com_mambowiki/index.php?title=Installing_Kiba-Dock#Ubuntu")

I don't take any responsibility if you crash your Ubuntu with either of those two, nor do I take responsibility if they suck your CPU cycles :p
