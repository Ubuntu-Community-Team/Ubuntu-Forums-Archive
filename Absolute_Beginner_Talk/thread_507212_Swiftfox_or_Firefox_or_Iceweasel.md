### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-07-22
> **tcoffeep said:**
> Whats the difference between Swiftfox and Swiftweasel?

The person doing Swiftfox didn't want to give back the changes he made to the community (he literally didn't show what changes he made in the source code).  The MPL forced him to, but people seem a bit cautious about his software now...

Swiftweasel doesn't have that problem.  Same idea, but implemented by well meaning people.  

I've tried swiftweasel, and I thought it actually ran slower on my machine.  I tried swiftfox before, and I didn't notice a difference.

If you want a speed improvement, I'd suggest simply not using firefox.  Seamonkey is faster, as well as epiphany, galeon, opera, ...
