### Post by John E on 2006-11-23
I know the feeling...! If you open a terminal window and type:-

**sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg**

you'll be asked a load of questions about your monitor, keyboard & mouse. If you answer them all correctly, you'll probably end up with better resolution, assuming your video card is capable of more!! (I did, anyway).
