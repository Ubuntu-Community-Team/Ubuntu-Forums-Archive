### Post by netyire on 2007-06-23
Compiz may come installed by default, I think it works with metacity. Click System->Preferences->Desktop Effects and then enable it. However, you may encounter the following problems:

1) The window borders disappear.
2) Cube effects stop functioning.

For possible solutions, check:
1) [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=455735&highlight=desktop+effects+no+borders](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=455735&highlight=desktop+effects+no+borders)
