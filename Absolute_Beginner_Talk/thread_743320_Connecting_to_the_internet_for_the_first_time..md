### Post by Calash on 2008-04-02
That will depend on your setup.  Wired or Wireless?  What type of card?  Modem or Router?

In many cases Ubuntu will already have what you need loaded and ready to go.  The best thing to do before you start is load the LiveCD and try to get that connected to the Internet.  If that is painless (it was for me) then the settings carry over to your install and you will be good to go.
