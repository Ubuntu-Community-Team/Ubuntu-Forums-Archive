### Post by aJayRoo on 2007-09-11
I think kdebase is the smallest meta-package for installing KDE however I have always gone with kde-core for a minimal installation. I'm not sure that kdebase will give you a usable desktop environment as it is without many 'core' packages (hence kde-core). Have a look [here](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InstallingKDE) for descriptions of the KDE meta-packages.
