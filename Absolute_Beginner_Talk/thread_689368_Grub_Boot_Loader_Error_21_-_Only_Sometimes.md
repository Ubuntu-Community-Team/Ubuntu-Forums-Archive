### Post by airbornemist6 on 2008-02-06
You may have a wirebreak in your IDE/SATA cables (or they could just be loose). If I remember right error 21 is a missing harddrive or partition, so if that's the case check your cables.

---

### Post by dstew on 2008-02-06
Yes, this sounds like a hardware problem. Try a new cable as airbornemist6 suggests. Maybe a drive is not responding quickly to the BIOS for some other reason, and when it warms up a bit it responds.
