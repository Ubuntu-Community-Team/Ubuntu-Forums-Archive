### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-07-13
The floppy Formatter isn't in the menu by default so you'll have to add it.

Right-click the menu and choose "Edit Menu". Under System Tools, tick the box for Floppy Formatter

That'll show your menu item, and now you can format to your heart's delight.
