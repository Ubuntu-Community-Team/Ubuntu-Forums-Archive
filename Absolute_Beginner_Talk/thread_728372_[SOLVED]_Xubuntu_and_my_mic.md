### Post by TornadoWarning40422 on 2008-03-18
Fixed, after I went into applictaions>multimedia>Gnome Alsa Mixer. If you don't know how to install that, just go to applications>system>Add/Remove and under the multimedia section look for "gnome Alsa Mixer". After installing, I turned off "headphone jack sense". then i could hear it wonderfully! Still wondering how to get it in audactiy, tho.
