### Post by Technogenius on 2008-03-05
I think your hardware is unable to run two screens at once. I have a Latitude LS and it doesn't let me enable the second monitor either. What you can do is plug in the second monitor and then press Fn (the blue key at the bottom of your keyboard) and then LCD/CRT. This is F8 on my laptop, and should be under the key's main label in blue font.
