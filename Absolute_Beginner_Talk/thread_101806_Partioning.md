### Post by az on 2005-12-10
By default, the breezy installer will see if you have enough space and offer to split your windows partition for you.  All you have to do is say yes and tell it how big you want the new partition to be.  It does all the rest and when you boot your computer, you chose which OS to go into.

The partitioner is very safe, but it is smart to backup important data.

Earlier version of Ubuntu did that too, but you had to do it by hand.
