### Post by ruibernardo on 2007-11-08
Opensuse is free, it's Suse that is not. 

If you buy Suse, you should have direct support from Suse and all the benefits of their agreements with propriety software companies that distribute closed-source software with Suse and also benefit from agreements made by Novell with Microsoft.

Of course, after this you will be supporting closed-source software...
