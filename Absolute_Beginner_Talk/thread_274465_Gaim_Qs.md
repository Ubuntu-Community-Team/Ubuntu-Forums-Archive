### Post by kkellner on 2006-10-09
buddy pounces are sound alerts you can set when a specific person signs online or comes back from away, etc.
to change your buddy icon, open gaim and go accounts - add/edit, pick the account you want to set the buddy icon for, and click modify.  at the bottom of the modify screen should be an option to open a picture for your buddy icon.  probably has to be a 50 by 50 pixel image under a certain file size.
I'm not sure about themes in gaim 2.0beta3. hope this was helpful.
