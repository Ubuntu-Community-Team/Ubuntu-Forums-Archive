### Post by sancho panza on 2008-03-31
Before you go ahead, just make sure to back up your home folder and the /usr/local folder, just in case anything goes wrong. If you do that, and anything goes wrong, u'll just have to reinstall the programs thru add/remove or synaptic. All your personal files/ settings will be in the home folder.
