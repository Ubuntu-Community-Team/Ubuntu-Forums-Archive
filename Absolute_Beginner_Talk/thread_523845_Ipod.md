### Post by kostkon on 2007-08-12
I initialised my iPod under *Ubuntu* with *gtkpod* successfully (formatted as FAT32). I don't know if this error free and will work in all cases, so *I cannot recommend doing this*. I suppose, the most secure solution would be to initialise it in *Windows* with *iTunes*.

After a while, I stopped using *gtkpod* and now I use [*Floola*]("http://www.floola.com/") which is a very good replacement for *iTunes* and can work in *Ubuntu*, *Windows*, *Mac* and I assume even *Linspire*. Although, *Floola* is not a full featured media player like *iTunes* is. If you are looking for this type of application, under *Ubuntu* (and maybe *Linspire*) you can use *Amarok*, but even *Rhythmbo*x or *Banshee* will suffice.

[*Yamipod*]("http://www.yamipod.com") is another good option.

Anyway, I believe the choice of the application to use depends, also, of the type of iPod. For example, if it is an iPod video and you'll need to transfer videos to it, then the number of applications that can do this shorten to two: *Floola* and *gtkpod*.
