### Post by astoltz on 2007-12-22
Compiz is installed by default on in Gutsy (7.10).  Click on System -> Preferences -> Appearance.  Then Click on the Visual Effects tab and select Normal or Extra.

There are a couple of prerequisites:  You have to have a video card capable of handling compiz and you have to enable the restricted drivers for the card.
