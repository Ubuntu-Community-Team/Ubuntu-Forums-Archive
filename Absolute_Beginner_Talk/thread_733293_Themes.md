### Post by CesarHideyuki on 2008-03-23
Just download a theme (you can try websites like [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) or art.gnome.org). I like to save it on the desktop :D
Then, on the desktop, right click and change desktop background. Click on Themes and click and drag the theme you saved on desktop to the window. It will automatically install it.
