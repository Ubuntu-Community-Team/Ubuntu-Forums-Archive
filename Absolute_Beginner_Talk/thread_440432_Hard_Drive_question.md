### Post by Happy_Man on 2007-05-12
Dude...I think you installed Ubuntu to the wrong drive by mistake. Keep the second HDD out, and boot from the LiveCD and reinstall. Afterwards, ONCE YOU'VE BOOTED FROM THE FIRST HDD AND MADE SURE EVERYTHING WORKS, put your second back in and reconfigure the samba share. Hope this helps!
