### Post by aeiah on 2007-06-19
the IM client gaim should be installed by default. this can be used with msn accounts, amongst others. the new version of gaim is called 'pidgin', due to some issues with aol and aim, and you may want to give this new version a try. there are others, but see how you get on with that.

you can grab pidgin here:[http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=Pidgin](http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=Pidgin)

or use gaim by going to menu button > internet > gaim
