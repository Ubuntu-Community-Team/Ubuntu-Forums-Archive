I finally did a complete new installation of "Ubuntu".  When it wanted to update my system, I added KDE.  There were a total of 132 updates to download and install. I will eventually learn the commands, but for now the name of the game is --- get up and running with little effort.

My dad told me that a "lazy" man will always find the easy way to accomplish a task.  This is what I attempt to do.
