### Post by ayenack on 2007-08-01
This is what you need to do. Got to menu at top of screen and click

**Systems>Administration>Printing**

This will bring up Printers window double left click on

**New Printer**

Make sure Local or Detected Printer is selected and click 

**Forward **

Then

**Manufacturer**

Select

**HP**

Then Scroll down to your printer and select it and

**Forward**

That should do it. Simple eh.

BTW your printer is already supported so you didn't need to download the drivers.
