### Post by nexu56 on 2007-08-14
~ just means your home directory.

So if you are logged in as "joeuser", you can just type "cd ~" instead of "cd /home/joeuser".

---

### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-08-14
~ is a shortcut to your home directory.  For instance
```
cd ~
``` will bring you home
```
cd ~/Desktop
``` will bring you to your desktop
