I don't quite understand your question . . . what are you specifically trying to do? What's not working?

EDIT: I think I got it. Do you mean that it keeps asking you for your password after you've entered it? That's what it's supposed to do, and that keeps your system more secure. After you enter your password, you can use the "sudo" command to perform root activities for the next fifteen minutes, and then it will ask for your password again. A little bit of a hassle, but it's really worth it for the security it provides.
