### Post by taurus on 2006-11-05
> **hiffenscgaupher said:**
> How do I qand check the bois?
When your computer boots up, press Del or F2 to get into BIOS.  However, your mobo's manual should tell you which key to press to get into the BIOS or there would be a message either at the bottom of the screen or the upper right hand corner telling you which key to press.  Then, look in there for the boot sequence section and make sure you move CD-ROM to the top of the list.  Save it and reboot with the CD-ROM (burning it right this time) in the drive...
