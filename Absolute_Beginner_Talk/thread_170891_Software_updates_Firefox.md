### Post by aysiu on 2006-05-05
No.

If you followed that guide, you have two separate installations of Firefox that are independent of one another.

The Wiki just adds in a separate pointer so that when you type in the command *firefox*, it'll launch the Mozilla version instead of the Ubuntu version.

By the way, update your Firefox--1.5.0.1 is old. 1.5.0.3 is the most current version.
