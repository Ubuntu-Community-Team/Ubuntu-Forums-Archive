### Post by Qrk on 2006-04-17
Yes, most certainly.

For the Gnome terminal, open it and simply select Edit->Profiles->Default->Edit->Effects->Transparent Background

---

### Post by 23meg on 2006-04-17
In Gnome Terminal hit Edit / Profiles, go to the "Colors" tab and choose "Transparent background".

Also see [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=81727").
