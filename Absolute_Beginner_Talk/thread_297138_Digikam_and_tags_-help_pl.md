### Post by Marsolin on 2006-11-10
[F-Spot]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/f-spot") and [jBrout]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/jbrout") are supposed to save tags to the images and not to a database.

### Post by Martin-Herrmann on 2006-11-27
Another tool for tagging and managing pictures is [Mapivi]("http://mapivi.de.vu").
It supports all IPTC tags, EXIF data and JPEG comments. A powerful search feature, hierarchical keywords and batch tagging are also available.
