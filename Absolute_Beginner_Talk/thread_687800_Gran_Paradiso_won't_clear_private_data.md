### Post by Blutack on 2008-02-04
It's an alpha, you might be better off filing a bug with mozilla.  With FF2 you can do it manually by hitting Cntrl-H and going into the .mozilla /firefox folder and deleting history.dat and anything else that looks roughly relevant.  For FF3 you will need to find out which hidden folder the data is in, shouldn't be too hard.
As for the second part, you could just use a livecd.  Or look into stopping gnome saving document history, there will be a gconf thing for it somewhere I'd imagine.
