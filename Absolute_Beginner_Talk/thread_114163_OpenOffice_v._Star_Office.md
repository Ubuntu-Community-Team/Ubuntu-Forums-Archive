### Post by Azriphale on 2006-01-08
OpenOffice.org is an open source project that partially has the backing of Sun Microsystems. 

I used to use OpenOffice.org 1.1.2, and also Sun StarOffice 6 (or was it 7? whatever) and there really is no material difference. Sun uses OpenOffice.org as their code-base and supposedly adds stuff to it, and they provide enterprise level support for the product. I don't think its worth paying for it, there is plenty of (very good) community support, and I can't see any difference in the products. I think the main thing is businesses wanting somebody they can blame for stuff and the like, so they purchase the support packages.
