### Post by Hospadar on 2007-08-13
The standard gnome desklet does support your city and if you give it a click it will give you all the more detailed info like pressure and windspeed.

Right click you panel and choose "add to panel" and add the weather one.

Then right click the icon on the panel and go to preferences, from here you can select your location.

Not sure about the network tool.  You might look into firestarter, it's a frontend for iptables (the linux firewall).  It's not as convenient as a desklet, but it does give a lot more options and graphical interface.  It may do what you need.
