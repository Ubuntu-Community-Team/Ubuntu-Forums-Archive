### Post by dptxp on 2008-04-09
In Ubuntu you can run KDE programs in Gnome and Gnome programs in KDE, the dependencies shall be automatically downloaded and installed.

You can add kde-core or kubuntu-desktop to Ubuntu OR gnome to kubuntu, even more Desktop Managers as XFCE,ICEWM, Fluxbox etc and select when you log-in. You can set any as default.

You can remove what you do not like.

Best is to try and feel. Gnome is easy to navigate and use, KDE gives you more control. Fluxbox and ICEWM are light, give better speed and are preferred for older machines.
