### Post by aysiu on 2005-10-31
People don't have specs memorized for which hardware does and does not work with Linux. There are hardware compatibility lists out there, but they're not comprehensive. Your best bet is to try a live CD--it'll run off of the CD and RAM completely. It won't touch your hard drive, and it'll give you an idea of how well Ubuntu works on your new computer.
