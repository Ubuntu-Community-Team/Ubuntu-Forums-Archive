### Post by mikeserv on 2007-11-10
First you have to get a real theme file.  Look at [gnome-look.org]("http://gnome-look.org").

After you download the theme, leave it in the .tar file.  Then open *System > Preferences > Appearance*.

Under the first tab (*Theme*) select *Install*.  Then point the installer to the theme file you just downloaded.  That should do it.

-Mike
