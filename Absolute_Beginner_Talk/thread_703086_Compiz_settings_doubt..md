### Post by chewearn on 2008-02-21
If you add the settings that way, you will "unfold" the cube, when you either press CTRL+ALT+Up, or press Button1 (not both).

But "unfold" is to open up the cube into a long strip of four faces.  I'm sure you are not looking to unfold, but to get the cube.  For this, the setting is under "Rotate cube" plug-in.

Also, you might want to try Button2, Button3, etc; if Button1 does not do anything.
