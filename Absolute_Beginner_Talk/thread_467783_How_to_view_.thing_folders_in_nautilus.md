### Post by DarkGob on 2007-06-08
Ctrl+H toggles show hidden files on and off.

Clicking show hidden files does work, although apparently not evenly.  After selecting it, I closed the folder, then reopened  it to find hidden goodies.  After that, selecting and deselecting it changed the viewability of the hidden files in real-time as you'd expect.  Strange...
