### Post by joao82 on 2007-10-03
dude, it's almost impossible to make a usb modem to work on linux. I only got mine to barely work because someone made a script and hosted it on a website with instructions. and after that i couldn't update my kernel, or the modem would stop working.
you really need to know exactly how a usb modem works to make it work. the easiest way is to buy an adsl modem/router with an ethernet exit and just plug it in to a LAN port, it's about 80 euros.
I can be wrong and maybe there are people who can setup a usb modem easily, but from my experience I got this point of view and found too many frustrated people with these usb modems made for windows who just couldn't install it.
