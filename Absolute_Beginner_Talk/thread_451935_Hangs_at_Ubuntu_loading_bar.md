### Post by Repabil on 2007-05-23
To get more information about what fails you can as you choose what to boot in grub press E as the one failing is marked. Then from the line with saying "kernel" (access it by pressing E) delete the word "quiet" press ENTER then B to boot it. As you boot now you should see messages instead of a loading bar. Write down what fails and post it here for further help.
