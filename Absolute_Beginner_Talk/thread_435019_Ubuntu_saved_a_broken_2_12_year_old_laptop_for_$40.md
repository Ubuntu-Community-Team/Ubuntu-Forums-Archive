### Post by mistergq on 2007-05-06
So I have a 2 1/2 year old gateway laptop.  The hard driver controller failed in November '06.  I purchased a new laptop.  However, I have been frustrated because other than the hard drive controller, it is a good shape laptop.  

I just installed Kubuntu on the new laptop.  I decided I wanted to see if I could give life to my old laptop and I created the Kubunut live cd on a 1 gig flash drive.  However, I wanted to be able to install plugins for flash and java script. I could not get any of the programs or follow the how tos to make my own live cd.  

So I picked up a $40 4 gig Thumb Drive and installed Kubuntu on it.  The installation took over 2 hours. :popcorn:   I actually thought the installation process failed and was stuck because it seemed like it would not move past 78%.  Once it was finally installed, I added Firefox, Flash, and Java Script.  My wife is now using the laptop and is impressed and happy.

Thanks Ubuntu for bringing back my 2 1/2 year old laptop!  :D
