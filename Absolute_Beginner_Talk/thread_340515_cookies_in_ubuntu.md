### Post by MellonCollie on 2007-01-17
If you're looking for the actual file, it's in your Firefox profile folder: /home/yourusername/.mozilla/firefox/profilefolder/cookies.txt

(You'll need to select View>Show Hidden Files, or Ctrl+H to see the .mozilla folder in your home directory)
