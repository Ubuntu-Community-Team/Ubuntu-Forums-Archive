### Post by compmodder26 on 2007-05-11
There are other wallpapers and color schemes included.  You already mentioned that you can change the wallpaper.  In doing so, you'll notice that there are some other wallpapers included.

For customizing the theme, go to System->Preferences->Theme.  There you can change to different preset themes or customize your own.

To use a theme from gnome-look.org, download the theme you want then go  to System->Preferences->Theme.  In there, click the button that says "Install Theme".  Navigate to where you downloaded the theme, highlight it, then click Open.  Your new theme should now be installed.
