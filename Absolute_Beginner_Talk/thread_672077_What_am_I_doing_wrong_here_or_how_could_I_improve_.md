### Post by Vadi on 2008-01-19
The LiveCD does not alter your system in any way. As soon as you shut down your computer, it'll never know the LiveCD ever existed - because the LiveCD does not save your data anywhere.

Is there any reason as to why you can't install Ubuntu on your harddrive? Then all your settings, data, and changes will be saved.
