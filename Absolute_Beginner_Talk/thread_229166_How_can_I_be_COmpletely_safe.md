### Post by jeffmetal on 2006-08-04
Its probably a very bad idea connecting through a proxy since your going to be sending all your bank details through that proxy even if it is encrypted.

best way to stay safe is to make sure you always type the url of the site in yourself. **Never** follow a link from an email to the banks site. Chances are it is fake.

Check the certificate of the site by double cliking the padlock that gets displayed. If there is no padlock displayed which means its not using https:// then dont use the site at all.

oh yeah and make sure you tell firefox never to remember the details and password for the banking sites.

I really cannot think of any way connecting anonymously to your banking sites will help with security.
