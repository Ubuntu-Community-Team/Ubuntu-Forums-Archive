### Post by etank on 2007-02-07
Do you know what kind of wireless card you have? Also what is the chipset on the card? One of the easiest ways to connect to a wireless LAN is to use network-manager.

*** I am assuming that you are using Gnome here ***

```
sudo aptitude install network-manager network-manager-gnome
```

That gives you an icon next to the clock where you can select the network you want to connect to.
