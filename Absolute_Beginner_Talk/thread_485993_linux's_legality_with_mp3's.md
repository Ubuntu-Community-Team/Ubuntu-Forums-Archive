### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2007-06-27
> **ktown said:**
> can someone briefly explain what the deal is with linux and mp3's and why they don't "mesh"

Well, there is actually nothing illegal about playing MP3 per se ...

The mp3 codecs are patented and not free (both in terms of $$ and Open source) , so not maintained by Ubuntu/Linux.

If you pay the appropriate fees it is legal. The illegality comes when people want to play mp3 but not pay the associated fees.
