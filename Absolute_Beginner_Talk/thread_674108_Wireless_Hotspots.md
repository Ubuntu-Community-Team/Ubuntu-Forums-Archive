### Post by bobdebouwer on 2008-01-21
Do not connect to any unsecured wireless hot spot unless you know who is providing it, as they could be run by unscrupulous people just waiting for you to access you online banking. Because even 128bit encryption can be broken in minutes and keyloggers and other things could be uploaded to your laptop.

Make sure you use good anti virus, firewall and spyware programs.
