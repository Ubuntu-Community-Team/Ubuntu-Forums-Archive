### Post by BobCFC on 2007-06-25
I can recommend the 320mb 8800GTS for your main monitor.  It is the cheapest of the next generation cards so you get the best bank for your buck. Works great in Beryl etc because Nvidia are good with regular Linux drivers and you have DirectX 10 support for games.   It is a good match for Core2Duo.

The only issue is if you have a 30" monitor or something and need superhigh resolutions.. you will need more video memory.  I run no higher than 1680x1050 with all details on max for new games like Infernal, R6 Vegas and Fear Combat.
