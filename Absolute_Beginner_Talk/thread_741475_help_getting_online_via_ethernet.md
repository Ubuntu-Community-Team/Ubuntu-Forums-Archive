### Post by bumanie on 2008-03-31
Try this is may or may not help. It's what I had to do to get gutsy to connect to the internet.
Open firefox. In the address bar type about:config
In the filter type ipv6 On the line that appears, right click and then left click toggle in th e drop down menu to change the value to true. Log out and back in and see if you can connect. If that doesn't work, you can reverse the process by toggling the value back to false. 9x out of 10 this works with gutsy if your NIC is OK and your connection is OK.
