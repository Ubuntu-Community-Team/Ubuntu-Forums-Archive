### Post by Jest3r on 2008-04-09
**Paste to Putty**

_Windows as host OS_
Document
  Crtl+C  - will copy
 in putty, 
  Shift + insert   - will paste OR middle click

**Paste From Putty**
  In putty
   Hightlight what you want to copy
   in Document use the normal paste. ie, Ctrl V
