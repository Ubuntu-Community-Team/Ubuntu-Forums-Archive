### Post by emarkd on 2008-02-13
Your password is not stored under your home directory, so that is not an issue.  You can simply create one large tarball and copy it over the network, or burn it to a disk, or copy it to a usb drive.  You can do that in Nautilus by right-clicking on your home directory.  Then untar it on your laptop overwriting your home directory there.  All of your settings will be transferred that way.
