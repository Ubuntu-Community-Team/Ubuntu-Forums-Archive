### Post by Judicata on 2007-09-07
For the latter Q - right-click on the window bar (not just the task bar in general - try the far right side where the window list starts), click "preferences" then the "show windows from current workspace" radio button. (I'd also select to "never" group windows together - but that's just me).

Can't help on the first Q. I've had to follow a guide to uninstall/re-install alsa before, for a different problem, but there is probably a less dramatic way.
