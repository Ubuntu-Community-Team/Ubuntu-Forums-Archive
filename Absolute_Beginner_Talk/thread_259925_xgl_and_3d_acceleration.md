### Post by RoosTa on 2006-09-18
First you'll need to install your graphics card, then you can install XGL/Compiz through Synaptics.

Here's how I got mine to work: [http://www.compiz.net/topic-389-howto-compiz-gnome](http://www.compiz.net/topic-389-howto-compiz-gnome)

And here is one for NVidia: [http://www.compiz.net/topic-652-compiz-ubuntu-gnome-with-nvidia](http://www.compiz.net/topic-652-compiz-ubuntu-gnome-with-nvidia)

Hope this helps

PS Follow the "second howto". This way you can always go back to normal gnome, incase things don't work out. CTRL-ALT-Backspace will restart your XServer, click setting at the bottom left corner, sessions.. and select Gnome or XGL.
