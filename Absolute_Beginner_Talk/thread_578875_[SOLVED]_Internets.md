### Post by CSMatt on 2007-10-17
Right-click "Applications" and choose "_E_dit Menus."  Right-click "Internet" in the "It_e_ms:" list (*not* the "_M_enus:" list) and choose "_P_roperties."  If you have Compiz or Beryl on (Desktop Effects) then the resulting window might appear in the background.
