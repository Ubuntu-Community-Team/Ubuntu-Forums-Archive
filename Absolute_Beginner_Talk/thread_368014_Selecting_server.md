I was wondering how you change what servers you use in dapper, because my downloads are very slow (30-50 kb) when on edgy they are up to 600 kb per sec.

---

### Post by Bachstelze on 2007-02-22
I assume you're talking about the repositories where apt gets it's packages from. If so, open */etc/apt/sources.list* in your favourite text editor and choose a mirror near your. For example, instead of [http://archive.ubuntu.com/](http://archive.ubuntu.com/), I would use [http://fr.archive.ubuntu.com/](http://fr.archive.ubuntu.com/)
