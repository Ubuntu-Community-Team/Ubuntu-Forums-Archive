### Post by davmac on 2006-02-14
I don't use any spyware removal apps at all and haven't found a problem at all. I suspect most are written to hit the most common platforms (Win and IE) The Linux security model and Firefox make it much harder to infect your machine anyway. I also use AdBlock and Filterset.G with Firefox. There is also a discussion about some other steps you can take at [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=110440](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=110440)

For setting up your Ubuntu box as a gateway, have a look at [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=107292](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=107292)

Hope this helps,
