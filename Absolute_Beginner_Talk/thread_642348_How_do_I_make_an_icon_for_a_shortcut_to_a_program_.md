### Post by mister_p_1998 on 2007-12-16
Pretty easy,
Right click on your desktop and select "Create Launcher"
Give it a name
browse to it (if you know where it is!)
its probably in /usr/bin/
click on the no icon button, 
choose an icon from the defaults
or if you have a picture handy for the icon, browse to this
all done!
This will create a shortcut on your desktop
