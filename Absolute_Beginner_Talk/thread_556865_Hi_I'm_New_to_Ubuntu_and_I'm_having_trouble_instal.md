### Post by Brightbelt on 2007-09-22
My experience has been that if available networks do not show up in Network Manager, then my wireless has not been configured properly. 

Once you get your wireless configured, your available networks will automatically show up in Network Manager, at which point you merely have to choose which network you want to join and enter the appropriate password if necessary. 

I hope that helps, ....
