Hi there folks

Just a quick note to say howzit and rave about Ubuntu. Ive been out of the Linux scene for some time now and although I am not at all an expert I have always enjoyed Linux and the learning. I was thinking I should check out what distros are out there these days and at first I had a look at Gentoo and Knoppix but they didnt really suit me, I then saw this word Ubuntu which is quite familiar since im a South African :) 

I installed on VMWare without any probs and WOW from the first boot it just seems great!  While I was playing around I thought where is the name Ubuntu coming from and where is the connection and how did it come about, I then saw the credits and there it was: Mark Shuttleworth  :razz:

Now I am sure there are alot of people working developing, contributing and I take my hat off to all you guys  :cool:  But when I saw Marks name I couldnt help but feel proudly South African!!!

Go Mark Go!!!

I am sure alot of you know who he is but for thos who dont,  he is the founder of Ubuntu and also the first African in space: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Shuttleworth](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Shuttleworth)
