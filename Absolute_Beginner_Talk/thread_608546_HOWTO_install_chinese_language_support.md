### Post by Malta paul on 2007-11-10
Hi, When you go to System>Administration>Language Support. In the 'supported Language' Box tick Chinese then The apply box will light up -click this and Ubuntu will download your language for you. Then select Chinese in the 'Default Box', and apply. .
Hope this helps :)
