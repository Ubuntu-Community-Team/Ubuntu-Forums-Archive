### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-03-14
Are you asking about port forwarding on your router?  Or on Ubuntu?

Cos Deluge has "Plug-n-Play" port forwarding, so it should automatically open the port in iptables (Firestarter can confirm this).

Modern routers also auto-port forward (my new one does :)

If you need to manually set port forwarding, then you can choose any name.  It is the port number that matters, not the name you assign to it.  The name is for your information, so you know what it is for when you turn it on or off.
