### Post by ckuka on 2008-01-12
Well, you could simply move the applications button on the top panel to the bottom left, and you can add programs to the menu. Otherwise, and I have to admit this is something I have not tried, you can download a windows theme clone from beryl.org. Here is a link I have found:   
[http://www.beryl-themes.org/content/show.php/Windows+XP+Clone+Settings+(Plus+Vista)?content=71377](http://www.beryl-themes.org/content/show.php/Windows+XP+Clone+Settings+(Plus+Vista)?content=71377)
Apparently, this is not a visual emulator, but more a way to change the behavior of the desktop environment to act more like windows in terms of desktop shortcuts and the like. 
I hope this helps. Good Luck
