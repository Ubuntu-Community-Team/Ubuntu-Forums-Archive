### Post by PmDematagoda on 2008-01-09
Ubuntu 7.10 is a normal release with 18 months of support after the date of it's release and Ubuntu 6.06 is an LTS(Long Term Support) release with support for 3 years after the date of it's release. 

Also since Ubuntu 7.10 was released much later than Ubuntu 6.06(more than a year after), Ubuntu 7.10 is more updated and may(in some cases) be even faster than Ubuntu 6.06 so Ubuntu 7.10 is good for those who want to stay up-to-date and want the OS to be flashy. Ubuntu 6.06 is more matured and is more stabler than Ubuntu 7.10, so it is recommended for those who do not want flashy and up-to-date stuff and just want the OS to work reliably without any unexpected problems.
