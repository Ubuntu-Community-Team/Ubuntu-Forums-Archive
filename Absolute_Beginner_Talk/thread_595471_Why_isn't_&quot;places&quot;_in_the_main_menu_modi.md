### Post by macogw on 2007-10-29
To remove an item, open up a Nautilus window, and in the pane on the left, right-click whatever you want to remove from the bottom half (the bottom half always matches your Places menu) and choose "remove".  You can also drag a folder into that left pane and it'll automatically be added.

EDIT: oh, "recent documents" isn't removable that way..just normal folders are.
