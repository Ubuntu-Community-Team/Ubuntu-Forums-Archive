### Post by Henry Rayker on 2006-10-18
It can save to .xls, I'm pretty sure it'll save .ppt too. I've noticed a few small problems with .xls support, but it's kind of uncommon...if you have more than 256 rows, and you put a chart below the 256th line, save and re-open, your charts will get crammed up to the 256th line.

I sort of recall having the same problem in excel, though, so it might just be that they copy the format a little too closely.
