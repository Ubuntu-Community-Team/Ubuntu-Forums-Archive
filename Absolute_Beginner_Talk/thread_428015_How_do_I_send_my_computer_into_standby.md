### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-04-29
Ok, I think this is my last answer of the night.

I believe that all computers have to have the right 3D drivers to get standby working. For that I send you [here.]("http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html")

Read the page and download the 0.92 version. Install the debian, go to Applications > System Tools > Envy.
Then automatically install your nvidia or ati driver. Say "y" to any requests to install. Say yes to reconfigure xorg, say yes to reboot and you should be able to suspend when you reboot.
