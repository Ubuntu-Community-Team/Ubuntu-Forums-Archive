### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-03-24
Right click an empty space on the panel you have, and choose the option to add a new panel.  Then right click the new panel, and choose the option to add something to it.  Then in the window that gives you all of the things you can add, just add all of the things it had.

That's pretty much what you have to do.
