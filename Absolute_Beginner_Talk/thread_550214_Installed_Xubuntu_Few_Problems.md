### Post by Haus Neuerburg on 2007-09-13
Hi k5ks,
welcome to Xubuntu!
I can't answer all your question but hope to be helpful on some: #1 the xubuntu desktop IS blank, well blue w/o any icons. If you want icons you can place them on the desktop, I never had any on the windows desktop so I don't miss any. But you can also create starters on the top or bottom bar with all the programs you want. Just rightclick on it and click "add new object", select starter and fill in the pop-up with the program details. Most programs can be found in /usr/bin....or you copy/link the files from there to your desktop, whichever suits you more.
#2: no idea, FF2.0.0.6 runs fine here.
#3: try: applications =>settings=>all settings=>Display (hope the translations are ok, that part of my Xubuntu actually speaks German) and choose the correct resolution settings
Hope to have been of some help
Haus
