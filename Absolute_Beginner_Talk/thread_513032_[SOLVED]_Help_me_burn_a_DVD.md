### Post by jackmc on 2007-08-02
After a lot of guess and check, hope, and a big stack of CDs, I have a solution.

In** k3b**, when burning a DVD, set write mode to "**DAO**" (ie Disc at Once - if you want to try it in other programs) and **speed to "Ignore"**. Using these settings I was able to burn 2 DVDs - one on a DVD-RW and one on a DVD-R. Both were successful.

Other Info:

DMA is off
I did not have to stop HAL for it to work
I ran standard k3b - not "sudo"

I think that is it. I hope my pile of coasters benefits someone else :D
