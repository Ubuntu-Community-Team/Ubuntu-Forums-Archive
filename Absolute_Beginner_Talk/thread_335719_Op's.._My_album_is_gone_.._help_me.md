### Post by meng on 2007-01-10
If all else fails, it may be that a data recovery expert (someone you have to pay) could help out. If you haven't got your old photos stored somewhere else, then take this as a valuable lesson that you should not rely on a multi-session CD (whether burned with Linux software or using ANY OTHER OS) as the sole means of storing sentimentally important data.

You should have a backup of all important data at all times. And whenever you do something particularly important (e.g. repartitioning, upgrading your OS, or using a piece of software you've never used before)  you should repeat your backup.

I do hope you recover your old photos, but that you still learn from this so that it doesn't happen again in future.
