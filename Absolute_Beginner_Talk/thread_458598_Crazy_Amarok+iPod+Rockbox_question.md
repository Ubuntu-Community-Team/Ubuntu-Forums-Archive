### Post by undertherift on 2007-05-30
I've found it to be similar to iTunes.  Basically, you leave your music in the folders they are already in.  But then add them to the library (database?) and then drag and drop them into your ipod (which should automatically mount in amarok).   Your music will stay where it is and you can access it via the database, or double-click on a file, like you are used to doing.

I do't know if thats any different with rockbox on the ipod.  I didn't need to use rockbox with my ipod to sync it w/ amarok.
