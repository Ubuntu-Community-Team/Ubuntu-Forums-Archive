### Post by PDXlewis on 2005-12-30
I have forced myself to use Nvu. If you are used it Dreamweaver it will take some time to adjust.  I have used it on all platforms, XP/Linux/OSX and it works great. just like anything there are new ways of doing things you have to get used to and I am hoping for some good updates. If you are willing, it is great. 

If the page is done with Frontpage, save yourself some trouble and just redo the page. 

I understand the votes for bluefish and I am in source view half the time. But I have to say, wysiwyg is hard to go without and is a big minus for me on bluefish. I'll put up with some minor tag issues.

There is even a portable NVU USB utility out there but is just for windows right now with hopes of multi os.  I recommend Nvu to anybody that has asked about trying out html editing and even some of the html oldtimers. I think with a little encouragement Nvu could turn into something pretty nice.
