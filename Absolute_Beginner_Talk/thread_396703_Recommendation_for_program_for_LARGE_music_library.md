### Post by Rizlaw on 2007-03-29
I have a music library in excess of 25,000 tracks and I am reasonably happy with "gmusicbrowser" (a Gnome centric program). It works quite well in Ubuntu 6.10 and can be installed through Synaptic Package Manager once you add the "gmusicbrowser" repository to the "Third Party" section in Synaptic.  

Had you performed a search on this topic you would have found previous posts on the subject and my answer with the necessary links to "gmusicbrowser."
