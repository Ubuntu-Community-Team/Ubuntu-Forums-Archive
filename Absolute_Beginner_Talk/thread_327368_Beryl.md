### Post by maxamillion on 2006-12-29
AiGLX hands down, it uses your gpu to offset the overhead of the rendering, i know a guy running beryl with aiglx on a 350mhz amd k6-2 with an nvidia card ... said it only loses him about 20mb extra ram and minimal cpu time because of the aiglx support in the new nvidia drivers (but i have been told the intel open source drivers provide this same support)
