### Post by battlecry on 2007-11-05
This is a solution especially when going from Windows XP to Ubuntu 7.04 or 7.10 using a Westell modem or  router(DSL) with a BellSouth or Verizon system:

Go to [http://www.dslreports.com/forum/remark,17554389](http://www.dslreports.com/forum/remark,17554389) and click on Connection under Expert mode and change to Manual at the bottom of the screen. You will get a manual connection in Ubuntu and you will need to turn it on.
