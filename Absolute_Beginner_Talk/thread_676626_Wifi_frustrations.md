### Post by mandrill on 2008-01-24
wpa psk? you should be fine. if you go to synaptic (activate all your repositories)
make sure you have madwifi installed, then go to restricted drivers (System>Administration>Restricted Drivers, and activate the Atheros HAL. Click on network applet at top right of screen and connect to your network.

Post back what happened. Good Luck!
