### Post by Bloch on 2006-04-23
There might be overload on your soundcard. Go to
Applications --? Sound and Vision --> Volume Control

and set the PCM volume at a lower level. I had problems with the sound suddenly becoming just a crackle. It might just as suddenly snap out of it to clarity again.
 Is the system sound also a crackle? i.e when you open the waste bin or delete something?
