### Post by drummer on 2005-08-20
I think that the DVD is just the Live and Install of Ubuntu in one. Also, all the packages wouldn't fit on just one DVD (Debian comes on 2 full DVDs) and many couldn't be included because of legal reasons (ie. different licence, propriatry, etc.). This is why the Universe repository is disabled by default on a fresh install. I think some extra programs are included or installed from the CD/DVD, eg. OO.o, xChat, gaim, etc., and some others not installed by default, but nothing from Universe will be on there.

Also, you can get Ubuntu CDs shipped to you for free if you go to [http://shipit.ubuntu.com/](http://shipit.ubuntu.com/) (they can take a long time to arrive though)... or of course make good use of a friend's (or school's ;) ) cable internet connection.
