### Post by kpkeerthi on 2007-12-06
Launch nvidia-settings from the terminal and choose the resolution from the GUI. If you are able to change the resolution, open nvidia-settings with gksudo

```

gksudo nvidia-settings

```

and change the resolution and choose "Save to Xorg.conf"

**NOTE: This may not work if you have ever manually added "ModeLine"s to xorg.conf or made some customization with respect to resolution. **
