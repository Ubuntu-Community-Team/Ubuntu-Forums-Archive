### Post by drs305 on 2007-11-28
They are stored in a variety of folders. If you right click on one of the icons, select Properties, then click on the icon the folder in which that particular icon is stored will open up. From there, you can navigate to another folder or note the location to open up the image from a graphics program to modify them.

I use gimp to modify lots of icons. I merely save them as png files and then use the same method described above to open the icon setting. I then just point the icon setting via the browse button in the upper right corner to the folder where I keep my modified icons.

### Post by CatKiller on 2007-11-28
They are largely kept in /usr/share/icons, and they are theme-dependent. If you just flat-out don't like those ones rather than having something particular in mind, just change the theme.

There are many available through Art Manager (in the repositories) or places like gnome-look.org
