### Post by airtonix on 2007-09-02
what is your problem in the first place?

where, how, why, and when  did you download gstreamer.(ie: did you apt-get it )

what did it look like when you downloaded it

why are you trying to tell it wher to download to?

why are you trying to install a sound server?

have you found the applications menu?

in that menu you will find "add/remove programs"

also further in the system menu you will find "synaptic package manger"

the command line can be invoked like this : 

1 - press alt+f2
2 - type "gnome-terminal" or "terminal"
3 - press enter

tada terminal.

or you can go to the applications menu and then accessories and then terminal.
