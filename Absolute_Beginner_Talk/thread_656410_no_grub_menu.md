### Post by cecure on 2008-01-02
Sounds like your second hard drive is not booting

When your computer starts up go into your bios setup (immediately after your computer starts it will show you on the splash screen which key to press).  Find the area where it says your boot device order and make sure that your second hard drive is set to boot first.  Save your settings and reboot.
