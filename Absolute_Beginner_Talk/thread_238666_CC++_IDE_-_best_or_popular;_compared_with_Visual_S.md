### Post by wapowell on 2006-08-18
Hi,

When I started my transition from MS to Ubuntu I went through the same thing when I was looking for an IDE (in my case for PHP).  I got all kinds of responses ranging from commercial apps on the linux platform to vi.  May I suggest something?  Since you have used VS before why not approach it like this... What are the features of VS that you love, and what are the needs that you have (or anticipate having) as a developer?  If you have this check list, then you can approach an IDE and see how it compares to your needs and wants.

What I found was that there are TONS of IDEs out there for the linux platform, with each one being slightly different here and there (from functionality to look and feel).  If you know what you personally are looking for in an IDE, then it may be easier to settle on one particular one based on hot it fits YOU and your needs.

I'm a little sleepy, I hope this made sense.
