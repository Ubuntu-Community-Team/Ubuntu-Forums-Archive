Going back to itunes, burning the songs to CD and then re-importing them into linux (as ogg/flac/mp3/whatever) will do the trick.  There is some loss of quality if you go from m4p to another lossy format like .ogg or .mp3, so flac might be your best bet.  But as long as you are not a major audiophile you probably won't notice the difference and ogg/mp3 will work fine.
