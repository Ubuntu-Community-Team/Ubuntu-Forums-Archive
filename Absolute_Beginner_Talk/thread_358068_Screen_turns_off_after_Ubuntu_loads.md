### Post by jexdawg13 on 2007-02-10
To enter BIOS (Basic Input/Output system) you need to enter a certain "hotkey" while your computer is starting. There are much better ways to explain it, but thats about the best I can do. Heres what I do: Shutdown the computer, and then turn it back on. While it's starting, you have to enter that certain hotkey. My computer is either F12 or Del... I'm not sure which, so I just pound both repeatedly until I get into BIOS, haha. Anyways, yours will be either Escape, any of the Function keys (F1 - F12), or Del... unless your manufacturer is really random and decides to make it something odd. Here is a (short) list of manufacturers and their respective hotkeys: [http://www.computerhope.com/issues/ch000192.htm](http://www.computerhope.com/issues/ch000192.htm)

If those don't work, research your manufacturer's site and they'll most likely have it posted somewhere. Goodluck to the both of us.
