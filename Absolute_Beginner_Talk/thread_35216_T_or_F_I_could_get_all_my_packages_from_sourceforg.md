### Post by Burgundavia on 2005-05-18
Basically, sourceforge is most just source, raw uncompiled code.

Synaptic has compiled code, which is built for Ubuntu systems, and had many tweaks applied to it.

Think of the Ubuntu stuff as mortar between the bricks of the source from sourceforge/other places. Your house stays together with mortar than without.

Thus, stick with synaptic.
