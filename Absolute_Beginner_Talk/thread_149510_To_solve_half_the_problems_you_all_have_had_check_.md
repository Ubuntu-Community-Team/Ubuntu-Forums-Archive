### Post by jlm81387 on 2006-03-24
Most of you all are clueless ex-windows users like I am. It took me forever trying to install most of the programs I need (limewire, acrobat reader, realplayer,) because I didn't know what to do and everyone wanted me to type in a bunch of commands in the terminal. i was told to check out this program called Automatix. It installs most of the software you need to happily use ubuntu. It also fixed my sound card and a bunch of other things. 

Type these lines in ONE AT A TIME in the program called terminal under the accessories menu:

wget [http://beerorkid.com/automatix/automatix_5.7-2_i386.deb](http://beerorkid.com/automatix/automatix_5.7-2_i386.deb)

sudo dpkg -i automatix_5.7-2_i386.deb

After it is done go to system tools under applications and open AUTOMATIX

It will open a window titled autoscript and do a bunch of stuff. Just have patience!!!!  eventually there will be a checklist of many programs that people struggle to install that will open up. Check in all the programs you want from this checklist.

Click apply and let the program do its work for you.

Enjoy!!!!!
