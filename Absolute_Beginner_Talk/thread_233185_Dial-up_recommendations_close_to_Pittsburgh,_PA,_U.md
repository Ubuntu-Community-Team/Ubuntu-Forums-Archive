### Post by MethodOne on 2006-08-09
If it works with the standard Windows dialer, you can use it in Linux without much configuration.  All you need is your user name, password, and access number.  Avoid ISPs, like NetZero and PeoplePC, that use proprietary dialers and encrypt your passwords.
