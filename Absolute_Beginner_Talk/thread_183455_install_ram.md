well what i usually do is:
1. turn the pc off.
2. disconnect ***_all_*** cables from pc.
3. remove the case, and touch something metallic (static electricity can harm computer parts, so you need to discharge yourself of it by touching metal)
4. find where the ram goes (probably your manual is a good place to check out first) and insert it until it clicks, and close the little pins
5. put your case back on, reconnect everything, and turn on your pc, and voila you have just installed RAM!!!!!!!!!1
