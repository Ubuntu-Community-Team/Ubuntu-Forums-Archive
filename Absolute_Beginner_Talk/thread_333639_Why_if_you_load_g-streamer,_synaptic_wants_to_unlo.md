### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-01-07
GStreamer and Xine cannot coexist on one system. If you want one, you must remove the other.

Totem has two versions for compatibility of both:
[LIST]
[*]totem-gstreamer (recommended)
[*]totem-xine
[/LIST]
Install whichever one you wish.

Jacob
