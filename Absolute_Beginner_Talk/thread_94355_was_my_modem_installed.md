### Post by Grimoire on 2005-11-23
Go to

System>Administration>Networking

Your modem should show up on there and if it does then simply click on it and then click properties to configure it.

Once you have configured it with your ISP settings etc. then simply activate your modem by selecting it on the same networking screen and clicking Activate.

That should be all there is to it!
