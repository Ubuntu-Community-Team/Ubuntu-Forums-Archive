### Post by Arthur Archnix on 2008-02-29
Generally speaking everything you see should work in gnome too. That's why a lot of the time people give you terminal commands, because they'll pretty much work in Ubuntu, Kubuntu, or Xubuntu. You'll just need to remember to change 'gedit' (gnome's text editor) to 'kate' (kde), or 'mousepad' (xfce).

When you see instructions for gnome like >apps >keyboard  I'm afraid you'll have to take a look around to find out how to do that in KDE. Or, head over to the irc and ask there, either #ubuntu or #kubuntu
