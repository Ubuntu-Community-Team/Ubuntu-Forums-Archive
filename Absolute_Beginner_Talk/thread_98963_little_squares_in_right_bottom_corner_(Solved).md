### Post by kalos on 2005-12-04
I'm guessing that you accidentally did a Remove from Panel.

[LIST]
[*]Right clicking on the bottom Panel.
[*]Click Add to Panel
[*]Under "Desktops & Windows", click Workspace Switcher
[*]Click Add
[/LIST]

-kalos
