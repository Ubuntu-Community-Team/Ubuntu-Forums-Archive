### Post by zanglang on 2007-06-27
Just blocking senders won't be effective enough, because most of these emails have randomly-generated sender addresses. Try these threads to set up spam filtering in Evolution:
Howto: Get Bogofilter working in evolution [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=354040](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=354040) and
Howto spam filtering in Evolution with bogofilter and spamassassin [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=99603](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=99603)

Or if like me, you have an Gmail account as a primary, forward all emails to it. Gmail does a great job in detecting and removing spam.

Or, try Thunderbird. It has a pretty decent spam filter.
