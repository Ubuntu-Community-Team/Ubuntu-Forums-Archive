### Post by shaft350x on 2006-12-03
I had been playing around with the Add/Remove programs a bit since coming from XP and in Ubuntu one can actually Add programs through it.  However I removed a couple games that I didn't really like and their icons have remained in my game menu.  Clicking on them acts like it's going to open a window then an error comes up, since I removed the program already.

How can I remove these orphaned icons in my games menu?

(Specifically "Falcon's Eye" and "LinCity-NG")

---

### Post by jamyskis on 2006-12-03
Right click on "Applications" and select "Edit menus". Select games and right click the entries you want to delete, and click on "delete". If you can't delete them for whatever reason, just uncheck the box next to them to hide the entries.

### Post by ron999 on 2006-12-03
Hi

What jamyskis says above is true for Dapper.
It's different for Edgy:-
System>Preferences>Menu Layout
Then do as jamyskis says.
