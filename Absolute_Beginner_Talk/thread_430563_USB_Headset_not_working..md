### Post by phuck_windows on 2007-12-30
I'm sure by now you have probably fixed this problem.  Others however may be having the same problem.  Assuming you were savy enough to go into options on skype and selectted usb headset as sound in and sound out that should of fixed your problem.  For me, my problem was that it worked fine in Skype but nothing else (movies, music, webpages, etc.).  What I did to fix this problem was open my symantic package manager and search for the "asoundconf-gtk" package.  Now under system, prefences I have "default sound card".  When I want to use my usb headset I first plug it in, click system, prefences, and default sound card.  From there just select headset and click close.  That should solve your problem.
