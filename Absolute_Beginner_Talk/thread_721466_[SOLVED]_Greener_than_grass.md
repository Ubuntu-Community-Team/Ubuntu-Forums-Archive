### Post by arron on 2008-03-11
1.  Most laptops run quite well with Ubuntu.  Search for your vid card on the forums here, and im sure you will find some help.

2. When I did my switch from windows, i did a dual boot.  This gave me the chance to do things i knew in windows, and learn Ubuntu.  But I was able to do all my stuff in Ubuntu and withen a month or 2 i removed windows completly, and have never looked back.

3. Destroy?  As in the computer self implode?  No, not at all.  Worst case is you lose you info.  If you are really worried back up any data of yours in windows on cd/dvd just in case.

4.  Most games work in wine.  You can check the wine site to see if yhe games you play are supported.  As well linux has a lot of games for itself, just take a look in add remove programs, or search the web.  The list is long these days.

Good luck, I think you will be quite surprised in the change and options.
