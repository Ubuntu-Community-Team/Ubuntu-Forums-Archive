### Post by sancho panza on 2007-10-26
Welcome once again...
I'd like to clarify. It is not true that Linux cannot be corrupted. A better way of saying it would be that Linux is fundamentally designed so that its harder for viruses to cause damage, and also that as of today, Linux viruses are very rare. So in effect, the chances of Linux getting infected are very very low compared to a microsoft windows machine.

And yes, a thread is just a topic and the related responses posted by members of a forum like this one.
