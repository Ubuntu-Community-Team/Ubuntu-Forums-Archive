### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-04-18
Did you burn the image as an iso, or a regular data CD?

If you didn't burn it as an iso image, it won't work.

Most burning software has the option to burn as an iso, just look in the menus.

Also, you MUST burn an iso image [COLOR="Red"]SLOWLY[/COLOR]. No faster than 4 X, or you're headed for trouble.

Another tip. Use high quality media for iso images, budget media doesn't "cut the mustard"
