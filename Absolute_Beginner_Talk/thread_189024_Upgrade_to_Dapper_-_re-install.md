### Post by aysiu on 2006-06-04
It's *likely* that everything will work out perfectly.

However, it's *possible* that your installation will get hosed.

If I were you, I'd [use PartImage](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partimage.html) to back up your Breezy installation as is. That way you can always restore it to just the way it was before should something go wrong with the Dapper upgrade.

By the way, I prefer this method of upgrading:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/dapper](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/dapper)
