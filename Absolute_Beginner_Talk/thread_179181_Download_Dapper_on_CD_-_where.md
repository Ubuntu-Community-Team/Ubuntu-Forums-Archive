### Post by gr0kzer0 on 2006-05-19
incidentally, [http://shipit.ubuntu.com](http://shipit.ubuntu.com) are now taking orders for Dapper CDs.  You can order one or more CDs, and they'll be mailed to you absolutely free of charge.

I used shipit to get Breezy. And, as I have a very slow internet connection and downloading Dapper would take a very lont time, I've just ordered Dapper CDs from shipit.  

The site says it takes 4-6 weeks for CDs to arrive.  My Breezy order took only 2 or 3 weeks.  But I'd imagine that shipit are knee-deep in Dapper orders already, so it could take longer than 6 weeks for it to arrive.  Still, from what I've heard, it's gonna be well worth the wait!
