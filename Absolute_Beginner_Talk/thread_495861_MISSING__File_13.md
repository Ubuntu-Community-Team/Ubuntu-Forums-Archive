### Post by jgoguen on 2007-07-08
Right-click an open area on the task area, then choose Add to Panel...

Then in that window, scroll to the Desktop & Windows section and click the Trash icon, then click Add.  If you want to move it, right-click it and choose Move, then move it to where you want it.  Then right-click and choose to Lock it so it doesn't get pushed around :)
