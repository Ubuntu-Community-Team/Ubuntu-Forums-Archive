### Post by Henry Rayker on 2007-09-06
When your laptop is plugged into the AC adapter, it is running off of the AC. However, "wearing my battery out" is a loaded phrase. If you leave your laptop plugged in all the time, you will run the risk of lowering your laptop battery's life. You should make sure you discharge it every so often.
