### Post by agapito on 2006-01-28
I think you can get them working with Cedega (a.k.a. WineX, a.k.a. transgaming)... But it's not a free application.

You can compile their CVS version for free but then you don't get Point2Play and you have to crack no-cd all the games, cause they won't give you the Cd support for free... I did that once, a long time ago, and it was a pain... It messed up my wine installation.

You can see their site [here]("http://www.transgaming.com/") and  check out the compatibility list database [here]("http://transgaming.org/gamesdb/").
