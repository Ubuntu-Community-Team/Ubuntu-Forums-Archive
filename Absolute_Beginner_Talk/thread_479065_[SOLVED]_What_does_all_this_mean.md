### Post by Tyke91 on 2007-06-19
KDE and Gnome are different companies, they just determine how your desktop looks. KDE tends to be a bit more flashy, Gnome is a bit more stripped down and fast, i prefer gnome, but it's really up to you.

Ubuntu - basic ubuntu install, uses gnome

Kubuntu - kde optimized, uses the K desktop environment

Xubuntu - uses the Xfce environment (another environment, even more stripped down than gnome)


warty, breezy, edgy, dapper, fiesty, and gusty are all codenames for the next version of ubuntu (i.e edgy was 6.04, dapper was 6.10, fiesty is 7.04 etc...)
