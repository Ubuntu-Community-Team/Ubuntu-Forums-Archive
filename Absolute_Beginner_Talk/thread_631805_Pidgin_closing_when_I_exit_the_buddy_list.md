### Post by kelbizzle on 2007-12-04
Did you remember to add a notification Area? Thats where the little icon goes when you minimize. Also check in the preferences On the first page under System Tray Icon

### Post by JillSwift on 2007-12-04
BTW, Pidgin 2.2.1 *does* quit when you close the buddy list window. I'd call it a minor bug. To get rid of the buddy list window and leave Pidgin running, click the notification icon.
