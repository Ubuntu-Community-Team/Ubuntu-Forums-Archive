### Post by uberlube on 2008-03-05
Welcome to the community and I wish you good times in your endeavor to learn Linux. If you have any questions regarding what happend with your installs that wouldnt work I would be happy to guide you through as best I can. Also here are a couple of websites you may find helpful:

1) Install Anything in Ubuntu
[http://monkeyblog.org/ubuntu/installing/](http://monkeyblog.org/ubuntu/installing/)

2) Ubuntu Team Wiki
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/)

3) The Ubuntu Guide
[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Gutsy](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Gutsy)

4) GetDeb.net (Installs alot of cool programs with 1 click)
[http://www.getdeb.net/](http://www.getdeb.net/)

5) Click n' Run (Same as #4)
[http://www.cnr.com/](http://www.cnr.com/)

6) Softpedia (warehouse of open source software and alot of other cool stuff)
[http://www.softpedia.org/](http://www.softpedia.org/)

7) Sourceforge (same as #6)
[http://sourceforge.net/](http://sourceforge.net/)

8) Distrowatch (a list of all the best distros out there, if Ubuntu doesnt have what you need then this is where you go to find what does)
[http://distrowatch.com/](http://distrowatch.com/)


And if you have any more questions dont hesitate to ask. :)
