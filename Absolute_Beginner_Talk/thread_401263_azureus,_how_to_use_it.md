### Post by Abbobo on 2007-04-04
I think you are asking where to get the torrent files from in the first place right? If so, you just visit a torrent site, such as [www.mininova.org](www.mininova.org) (type download torrent into google to find more). Search for what you want to download, and pick the torrent you like. Seeds are people with the completed file, and leechers are the people who are downloading from the seeds. So ideally you want one with a few seeds and leechers. When you have picked your torrent, download it (should only take a few seconds). Then open the .torrent file with azureus, check where it is downloading to, press ok, then wait. 

To keep up the file sharing community, try to upload as much as you download. I try to keep a share ration of 1.5 - 2, so i am uploading more than i download. You can check your ratio in azureus by double clicking one of the blue arrows in the bottom right of the azureus window. this brings up the statistics tab. under statistics, select trasnfers, and it will show your ratios.

hope that helps some
