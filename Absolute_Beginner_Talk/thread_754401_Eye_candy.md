### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-04-13
First, you should tell us what kind of video card you have and if you installed any restricted drivers.

Try going to System->Preferences->Appearance.  Click on the 'Visual Effects' tab and choose anything other than None to see if it works.  If it works, then you just need to learn how to use the effects.  If it doesn't work, we need more info to get it working.

Also, those effects are called Compiz/Fusion.  If you read that on the forums, that is what they're talking about.
