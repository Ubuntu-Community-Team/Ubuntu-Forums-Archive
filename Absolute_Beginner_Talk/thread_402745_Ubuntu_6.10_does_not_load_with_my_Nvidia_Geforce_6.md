### Post by Spike-X on 2007-04-06
**BACK UP YOUR XORG.CONF BEFORE YOU GO CHANGING IT!!!!!**

TO BACKUP XORG.CONF

sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.conf_backup_<<date ie 20070213>>

**write down the name of the backup file you created!!


TO RESTORE XORG.CONF FROM COMMAND LINE

sudo cp /etc/X11/<<name of backup file>> /etc/X11/xorg.conf
