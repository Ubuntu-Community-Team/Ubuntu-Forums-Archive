### Post by mykalreborn on 2006-12-14
you should download automatix, because it makes the whole codec thing much easier:[http://www.getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation#Installing_on_.28K.2CX.29Ubuntu_6.06_i386.2Camd64_.28Dapper.29](http://www.getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation#Installing_on_.28K.2CX.29Ubuntu_6.06_i386.2Camd64_.28Dapper.29)
after installing open it from applications>system tools and in the program select multimedia>mplayer and ffplugin. this will make your browser - assuming you use firefox 1.5,2.0 or swiftfox - open just about any type of embeded media you might see including quick time or windows media player.
also in multimedia select aud-dvd codecs and multimedia codecs and you're good to go ;)
