### Post by batty_whol on 2006-12-05
Look for the software package 'gnokii' available through apt from Ubuntu's repositories. This is in development (version I have is 0.6.12.

Don't get your hopes up too much (check the website for docs on whether it supports your phone) as I haven't got it working for my phone yet (Nokia 6021 see other post 'Can't connect Nokia 6021 with irda and gnokii'). However, having read numerous forums on the topic, there are certainly quite a few people that do get it working. Certainly more over USB cable or Bluetooth than irda (just my luck).

Good luck!
