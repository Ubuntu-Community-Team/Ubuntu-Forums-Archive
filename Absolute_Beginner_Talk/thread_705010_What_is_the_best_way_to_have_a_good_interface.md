### Post by Tyke91 on 2008-02-22
ya you should already have compiz-fusion on your comp (compiz-fusion is the junction of the beryl and compiz projects)

to get more options for 3D and such do this

```
sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager
```

then go to System>Preferences>Advanced Desktop Settings 

in order to configure your shininess.
