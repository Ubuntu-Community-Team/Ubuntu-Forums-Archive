### Post by jdong on 2005-06-15
Yes, there is. It's called "g++", and is one of the most standard-compliant and powerful compilers available. To install, pull up Synaptic, and mark "build-essential" and "g++" for install.

If you'd like an IDE, it's called 'anjuta', also available via Synaptic.
