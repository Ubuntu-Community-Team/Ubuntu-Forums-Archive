### Post by sewlua on 2006-09-06
I had a similar problem with the Live CD not booting. Then I purchased a DVD±R DVD Burner with 5X DVD-RAM; and now it boots fine. 

First check your boot options in your bios, CD drives should boot before the hard disc.
 
My problem could have been that my old setup had a broken DVD-ROM drive as primary and CD drive as secondary and it didn't bother to boot from the CD drive.
