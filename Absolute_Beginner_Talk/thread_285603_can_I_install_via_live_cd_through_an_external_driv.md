### Post by DarkN00b on 2006-10-27
You need to check your BIOS to see if it supports booting from external media. Is it a USB or firewire device? Most modern BIOS allow booting from USB, and I have heard of someone booting from a firewire connected device. Just check your BIOS and have a look at your boot options. Most computers allow you to access BIOS by pressing the Delete key or F1 or ESC before the computer starts booting from the hard disk. There is a possibility that a GRUB boot floppy would let you boot from an external device, but I really don't know. Someone more knowledgeable than me would have to answer that one.

Good luck.
