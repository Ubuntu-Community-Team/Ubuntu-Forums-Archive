### Post by njparton on 2008-01-23
Are you using a router?  Try forwarding the relevant ports in your router to your PC.  Sometimes different bittorrent clients have varying upnp abilities and may not be able to do this automatically.

Look in the ktorrent options for the ports it's using.

### Post by nemilar on 2008-01-23
Make sure that the port you have forwarded in your router is the same port being used by your torrent application.  If it isn't, torrents will download at really slow speeds (around 4KB/s).
