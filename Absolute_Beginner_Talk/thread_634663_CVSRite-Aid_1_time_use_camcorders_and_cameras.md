For those of you who may be interested, or those of you looking at Linux but not wanting problems with using your hacked "1-time use" CVS/Rite-Aid camcorders and digital cameras in Linux, help has arrived.

Working of the open source projects of avidownload (SaturnNights) and pv2fetch (BillW), I have created a version of avidownload for Linux that will download both your videos from the camcorders and the stills from the digital cameras.  This means a single program for both of these cameras, and it WORKS in Ubuntu!!  (I use Gutsy 7.10).

The source and executable have not been posted to sourceforge like the other 2 mentioned apps are, but that is because it is a new meld of the 2 and I would rather have 1 of those guys post it if they want.

however, the source and a binary executable are available from me if you want it.  Just reply/message/email me and I'll get it off to you.  It is not a GUI'd app, so it runs from the command line (or desktop launcher) and is less than 42k is size.
