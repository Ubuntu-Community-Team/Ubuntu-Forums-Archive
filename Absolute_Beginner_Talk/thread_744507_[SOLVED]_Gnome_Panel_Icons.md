### Post by Therion on 2008-04-03
> **kelnage said:**
> Sounds like you've changed the appearance. If you go to System -> Preferences -> Appearance ...
Go there... Choose your preferred theme (e.g. Clear Looks) and then click on the "Customize" button.  Click on the "Icons" tab at the top and choose the Icon set you like best.  Save the customized theme and you're good to go.
