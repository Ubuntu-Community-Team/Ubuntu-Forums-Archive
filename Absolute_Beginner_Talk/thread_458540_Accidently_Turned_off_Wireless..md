### Post by greybirds on 2007-05-29
Try this.

If you restarted with the live cd, remove it and reboot normally. Log in.
Select **System > Administration > Network** (not Network Tools -- that's different).
You will have to enter your password.
A window should appear with a list of all your networking cards and modems. 

Is your wireless card listed there?

If it is, is the box next to its name checked? If not, check it. You might have to restart Firefox (my experience is it's a little uncertain about the network going away and coming back but that might be me). But your wireless networking should be back.

If your card isn't listed, or if something strange happens, please let us know! :)
