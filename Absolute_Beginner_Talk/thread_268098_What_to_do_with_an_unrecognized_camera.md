### Post by pseudonym on 2006-09-29
Has it got a memory card which you can plug into a USB card reader? If so, you just treat the reader like a USB flash drive - plug it in, mount it, copy your photos/movies to your hard drive, unmount the reader. I have an OLympus mju-720 and this is how that works.

Other makes and models use a different method of interfacing with the PC. If your camera is not supported by usb storage, you need to check on support at [http://www.gphoto.org](http://www.gphoto.org) .
