### Post by bone2006 on 2007-09-11
get envy [http://tinyurl.com/yhxnpm](http://tinyurl.com/yhxnpm)

It will install and ask you if you want it to automatically configure xorg.conf, which I normally do automatic.  

The truth is that I've use envy before and had it automatically configure xorg for me, but when I rebooted I couldn't login.  I booted up the liveCD, took the xorg.conf from the liveCD and replaced the one in my HD and seems to be working just fine for me.   
I've had better results with envy than anything else.
