### Post by MethodOne on 2007-12-16
Rhythmbox can't play MIDI files because there is no GStreamer plugin for MIDI (that I know of).  If you want to play MIDI files, follow the instructions at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MidiSoftwareSynthesisHowTo](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MidiSoftwareSynthesisHowTo).  I recommend [KMid]("apt:kmid") for a decent graphical player.
