### Post by LaurelLynn on 2007-04-05
Dear pimpaulogy,

Many DVD-R drives leave sessions open on their disks until they are either full, or you finalize the disk from one of the menu options. How they leave it open varies from recorder to recorder, so computers can´t read a disk until it has been finalized.

That may be where your problem lies.
