### Post by aysiu on 2008-02-27
I don't see much information about 1363LC, but looking at [the issues present in 1362LC](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam/AcerAspire1362LC) might give you a sense of the problems that you'd run into.

If you're going to run Ubuntu on a laptop, I'd take a look at [the Laptops-that-work-perfectly-on-Ubuntu thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=427464) or buy a Ubuntu-preinstalled laptop from ZaReason, System76, or Dell.

And, no, it won't matter what version of Ubuntu (Kubuntu, Xubuntu, Ubuntu, Edubuntu) you install. Hardware detection happens at the kernel level, and they all use the same kernel.
