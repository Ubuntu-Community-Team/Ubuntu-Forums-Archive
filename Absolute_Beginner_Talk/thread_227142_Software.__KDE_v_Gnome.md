### Post by mjm115 on 2006-08-01
Your best bet would be to search around.  There really isn't a 'there's more apps for KDE than for Gnome', or vice versa.  I recommend that you check out [http://www.kde-apps.org](http://www.kde-apps.org) and [http://www.gnomefiles.org](http://www.gnomefiles.org).

Personally, I have found that most multimedia applications-Amarok, K3B, etc.- are more polished in KDE, but productivity applications such as Evolution, Firefox, GAIM, and Openoffice (all GTK apps) are more useful than their KDE counterparts.

Remember, this is **personal opinion**.  The best thing to do is to try them for yourself.  There really is no such thing as 'which is better' but more a 'which works best for you'.

Hope this helps.

Kopete and KDevelop mainly, and after I started using KDE I've been using Amarok and KTorrent too.
