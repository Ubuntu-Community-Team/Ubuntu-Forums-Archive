### Post by Forrest Gumpp on 2008-02-21
Click on your own userID.  It gives you a drop down menu, with the last option being 'show all posts by<userID>'

Selection of this option takes you to a page with all your posts shown with the most recent at the top.  The summary shows the name of the thread (a clickable link), the opening words of your own post,how long ago you made it, and how many posts and views have been recorded on the thread.

This feature seems to record your total posting history.  If you click on the userIDs of some of the long term members of the Forum you will see many pages of their posts listed at the bottom of any such page, just as in the Forum question index pages.

I thought it was just me when I could not find this feature at first.  I no longer feel so alone.

Its an excellent feature for keeping track of a particular thread in a fast moving part of the Forums like Absolute Beginner Talk.
