### Post by memyselfnie on 2008-02-09
Playing around with what I assumed was fairly worthless software , I discovered that the Maxtor Safety Drill disk included with my 500 Gb  external drive IS a Knoppix live CD.  Nowhere in the documentation is that mentioned, I haven't noticed anyone in the Ubuntu forums talking it up, yet it 
seems like we SHOULD be!  It's included as a rescue boot disk, but the backup software documentation claims it's only good for backing up Vista and XP:lolflag: When booting, it was clearly 
linux, after a little tinkering to get by the backround I was shocked to see an apparently fully functional Knoppix.  I realize it's not Ubuntu,  but it is a brilliant piece of software that could certainly help out lots of folks (around here) IF they even knew they had it.  
There's more than one way to skin a cat, an I'm just letting this one out of the bag   :)
So, who else knew?!
