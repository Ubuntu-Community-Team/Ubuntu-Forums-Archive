### Post by lightstream on 2007-12-01
Just to say that once you reboot after enabling the restricted nvidia drivers, you can check they are installed and operational by going back to restricted drivers and checking that it now shows a green light and the words 'in use' alongside a tick in the 'Enabled' checkbox
