### Post by YuHoo on 2006-04-04
check out [www.notebookreviews.com](www.notebookreviews.com)
I bought the Dell XPS M140, with full backlight, several apps running, wifi, and basically all the bells, whistles, and everything else, and I still get at least 3.5 hours. Turn down the brightness and I can watch 2-3 movies before I ought to recharge since I can get up to 6 hours with the standard battery. You do need to get the 915resolution drivers and the stable release of ipw2200, but now that I have figured it out, you can be up and running in under an hour with it. Plus the wifi card on it outperforms my brother's stellar powerbook. As for breakability, it has ABS casing, but the hinges are metal and are directly connected to the main computer frame which is metal. No wobbling, really quiet, and quite cheap. The outlet store has deals where you save over $300 on a laptop so you can go get that nice extended warranty. Wait for their laptop sales and save more. There are many inspiron sales, and if there isn't just go to the small business site and get the 630 (exactly the same but for less). I spent $700 for a 60gb hdd, gig of ram, dvd+rw, and the truelife screen. If you have any more questions PM me, I've used several laptops in the past year, but only one with linux (sadly).
