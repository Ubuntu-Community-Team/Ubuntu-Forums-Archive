### Post by JayTee on 2007-01-29
The 3D desktop you are referring to does not come with Ubuntu by default. There are two 3D desktop enhancements you can choose from, Compiz or Beryl. Compiz has been around longer and Beryl is a fork of Compiz with more special effects such as Burn (Window burns up) and Beam (Window teleports out of site or teleports into view). I'm running Beryl and you can get that from several repositories. Here is a link to a HowTo to install Beryl.  
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263851](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263851)
You'll need to make sure your video card is supported and can do 3D compositing.
