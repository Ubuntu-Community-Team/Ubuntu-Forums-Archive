### Post by Daveth on 2007-02-17
well, it is made by Nvidia for starters. Your old card looks "old", so is it AGP, or PCI, or do you have a hybrid motherboard with both types of graphic slots?? If it is PCI then you have a much wider choice as few AGP cards are made nowadays. The 6600 series had good reviews a while back, though you can pick up 7900GS for the same sort of price new and they whizz along. Ebay usually has lots of this sort of stuff for sale. Not got into Beryl yet so cannot vouch for the 6600- my 7900GS would eat it for breakfast no doubt.
