### Post by louieb on 2007-06-03
The fact that this has happened with multiple distros lead me to believe  that your hard drive may be dieing.  Go to the mfg site and see if they have a diagnostic utility, most of them do. If thats the problem, new hard drives are cheep enough and easy enough to install, you'll just have to decide if the computer is worth fixing.
