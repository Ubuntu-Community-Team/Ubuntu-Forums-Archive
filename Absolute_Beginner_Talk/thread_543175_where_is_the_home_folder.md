### Post by dptxp on 2007-09-05
If you have not made a separate /home partition, it is in root partition.
Places>computer>. Click on File Systems on left. It is inside File Systems(/ partition).
You will see all your folders in /, including home.
You can also use Places>Desktop>.

### Post by southernman on 2007-09-05
Regardless if you use a seperate partition for /home or not it's in the places most of the people have suggested already.

Top Toolbar:
go to Places > Home
go to Applications > Accessories > Terminal and it dumps you into your /home/username directory
go to Places > Computer > Filesystem > home > your username

As you can see, there are *_at leas_*t three ways to skin that cat!
