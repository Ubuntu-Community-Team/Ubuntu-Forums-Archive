One easy way to do this is using zamzar.com. You just enter the url of the youtube video in the box. Then you select the audio (or video) format you want. It will then email you a download link.

### Post by bluepowder7 on 2007-11-25
go to your /tmp folder, and copy the file from there to your own folder (either in /home or some /media folder you made).  if you just want the audio, you can use ffmpeg to extract it i think.  or use ffmpeg to convert it to an avi format.  VLC will play the youtube FLV format just fine, though.
