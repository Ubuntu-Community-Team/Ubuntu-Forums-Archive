### Post by inferiorwang on 2008-04-21
Compiz and Beryl are indeed still being developed independently.  Ubuntu comes with compiz-fuzion which is the merger of the two.  Get compizconfig-settings-manager to adjust all the settings.  It will show up under System->Preferences->Advanced Desktop Settings.  Compiz and Beryl themes have both worked fine for me with compiz-fuzion so far.
