### Post by TheThinker on 2007-10-02
Gxine allows you to configure the de-interlacer through the same tab that it's placed in. You can select and add de-interlacers and other plugins that help to reduce these issues you're facing. Just read carefully to choose the plugin that suits you before enabling any options. My preferred plugin is Greedy since video playback doesn't take too much system resources and looks much better. Just be careful not to activate more than one deinterlacer together; chances are that gxine will crash.

Don't forget to look at the preferences section to see how you can tailor gxine to match your needs. A lot of options are there to help you experiment. It should be located in the "Edit" tab.
