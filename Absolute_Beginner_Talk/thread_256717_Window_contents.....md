### Post by skymt on 2006-09-13
Hit Alt+F2 and enter "gconf-editor". In the left pane of the Configuration Editor, choose Apps > Metacity > General. In the right pane, turn on "reduced_resources". Note that this turns off several other eye-candy-type-things, including several animations.
