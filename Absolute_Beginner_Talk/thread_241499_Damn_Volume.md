### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-08-22
Menu System->Preferences->Sound to make sure you're driving the correct sound card (e.g. built-in or PCI).

Double-click the speaker icon to get a larger mixer desk. Try and see if any of those sliders work for you. 

Right-click the speaker icon and choose Properties to select which slider on the larger mixer desk (above) the panel slider actually controls.
