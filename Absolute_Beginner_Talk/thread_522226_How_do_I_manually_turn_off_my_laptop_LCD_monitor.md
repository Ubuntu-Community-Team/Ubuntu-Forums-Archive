### Post by porcorosso on 2007-08-10
It sounds as though your "blank screen" is the blank screen screensaver. You're right. That doesn't turn off the panel's backlight. I think you need to check out what's available to you in System -> Preferences -> Power Management. The "Put display to sleep when inactive for:" setting should get you what you want.
