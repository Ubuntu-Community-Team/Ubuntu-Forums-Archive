### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-02-07
Sounds like you might have the alternate install cd which is just a text based installer without a live session.  You can download the LiveCD at [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)
Make sure the checkbox at the bottom is NOT CHECKED, otherwise you will have the alternate cd again.
