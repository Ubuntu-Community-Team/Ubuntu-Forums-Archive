### Post by tread on 2005-06-06
If Quickbooks is a Windows only software, it wouldnt work on Linux. You may be able to get it working using wine (windows emulator) or crossover office (similar project) 

I checked the quickbooks website, they seem to have a Mac version. It might be a good idea to ask them about it.

Also, there are a number of free financial softwares for Linux, gnucash comes to mind. Take a look at that and see if it satisfies your needs.
