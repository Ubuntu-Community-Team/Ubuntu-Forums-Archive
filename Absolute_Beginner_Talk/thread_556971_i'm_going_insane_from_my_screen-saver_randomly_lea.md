### Post by alienexplorers on 2007-09-22
My computer did the same thing.  I found out it was running open-gl screensavers and bombing out.  This caused it to go to the screen you are seeing.  I fixed it by removing my graphic drivers in ENVY and then reloading my graphic drivers using ENVY.
