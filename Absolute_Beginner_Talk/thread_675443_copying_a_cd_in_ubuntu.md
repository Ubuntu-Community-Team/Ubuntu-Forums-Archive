### Post by crjackson on 2008-01-22
> **VoXoM said:**
> Cool that worked, thanks.

Somehow now, after I've gotten the CD out, it won't reopen. Is this a problem that can be fixed somehow or is it a hardware problem? (the drive always worked well before I installed Ubuntu)

It sounds like you may have a hardware problem.  Power off your system.  Reboot.  While in the BIOS screen (before Ubuntu starts to load) Hit the eject button.  You may have to do this a few times.

If it won't eject at this point, you can rule out the operating system, it's hardware.
