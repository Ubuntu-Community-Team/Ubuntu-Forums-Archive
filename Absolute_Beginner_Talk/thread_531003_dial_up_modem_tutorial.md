### Post by plucky on 2007-08-22
> what port does an internal modem use?  


That depend on the driver you install. I have an Intel536 soft modem and the port was 
> /dev/536ep0

And on another machine i have a Coexant V90 modem and the port on that was 
> /dev/SHCF0

Luckily i don't need them now for internet access as I have broadband:)
