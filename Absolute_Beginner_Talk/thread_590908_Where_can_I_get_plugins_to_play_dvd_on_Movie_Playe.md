### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-10-25
Hi. It seems that in Gutsy, enabling DVD playback is easier than before: go to "Applications->Add/Remove", in the drop-down "Show:" menu select "All available applications", and search for **Ubuntu restricted extras**.

Note: I didn't notice that earlier and enabled DVD playback the usual way: by adding the [Medibuntu]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu") respository. Please post back if you're still having trouble after that.
