### Post by twin_57103 on 2008-01-09
Ubuntu is much more stable than Windows - you will rarely need a full reboot, which is what I guess you're  getting at for ctrl-alt-del.  Ctrl-alt-backspace resets your desktop, in essence, a lightweight reboot.  Another useful trick that I found only recently is that if you highlight text, middle-click will paste - a shortcut for Ctrl-C and Ctrl-V.

Be sure to check out the how-to's and wikis for the various tasks you're trying - they are very helpful and will make the learning process a little easier.  Most of all, be patient with it and you'll get the hang of things quickly.
