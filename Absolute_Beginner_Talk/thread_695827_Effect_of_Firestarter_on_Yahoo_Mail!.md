### Post by bump_ on 2008-02-13
AFAIK, Firestarter is not a firewall. Ubuntu comes with iptables which you modify to open and close certain ports. All Firestarter does is give you a nice interface to modify those iptables. Once they are modified by FIrestarter, they will stay that way whether Firestarter is on or not. 

So there is really no reason to keep Firestarter running.
