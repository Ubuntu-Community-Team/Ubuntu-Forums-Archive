### Post by multi on 2007-10-04
I ran into the same problem today, and when I was searching on the forum I came across your thread. Since I am now able to play DVD's from a mounted image I am happy to share that with you. 

First i installed xAcetoneISO2 from [http://www.acetoneteam.org/acetoneiso-gnome.html](http://www.acetoneteam.org/acetoneiso-gnome.html)
I installed the beta4 xAcetoneISO2 instead of the regular one, because I use Gnome and not KDE. Also be sure to read to notes and check for dependencies before installing.

When you have xAcetoneISO2 up and running install VLC-player if you don't already have it. Now when you open xAcetoneISO2 choose the tab "play". After that choose "Play DVD movie ISO". Don't worry you can also mount .img files with it, and pick your .img from you harddrive. Now a popup appears asking for program to playback the video in, choose VLC-player and the movie will start playing.
