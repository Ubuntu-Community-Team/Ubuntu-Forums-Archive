### Post by band-aid on 2007-06-11
The intel IPW3945 is well supported, worked right after install for me. As long as you stay away from broadcom it will be pretty easy to get one that works. Don't be afraid to do a bit of configuring, linux is a learning experience and its not going to kill you. As for large antennas for maximum range look at the ALFA 500mW wireless. It has a RP-SMA connector so you can put some huge antenna's on it. It does require a lot of power though, you have to plug it into two usb ports. Another incredible card is the Ubiquiti SRC, it has an external jack for an antenna.

Neither of these cards are going to be cheap, but you definately get what you pay for.
