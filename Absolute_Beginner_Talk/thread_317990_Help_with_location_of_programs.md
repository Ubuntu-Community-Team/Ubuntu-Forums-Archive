### Post by taurus on 2006-12-13

### Post by thomasaaron on 2006-12-13
A couple of ways to do this:

1.
Go to a terminal and type:  tuxpaint

2.
Right click on your desktop and click "add a launcher"
Type tuxpaint in the command field

3. Click Applications>A la Carte Menu Editor> Games
and create an entry for it in your games menu
