### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-11-10
There used to be a program called SharpeMusique that allowed you to download from the iTunes music store, but apparently it's old and outdated.

If you really want to do this, you can try using wine or crossoveroffice, download itunes for windows, and proceed that way--don't know if that works or not.

You could also go to a Windows machine, and download your song that way.

Keep in mind, all music bought and downloaded form the iTunes music store is DRM'ed and will only play on Windows or selected iPods.

For the future, you will have much better luck with an open music standard.  Try Rhapsody or another music service that is not "locked down".
