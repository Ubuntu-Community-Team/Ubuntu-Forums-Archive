### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-05-28
aMSN will work fine under Ubuntu.

However, I will put a caveat on that.

If you are using ADSL, behind a firewalling router, you will need to open up the ports necessary for aMSN to work. There is information on how to do this on the aMSN website: 

[http://amsn-project.net/](http://amsn-project.net/)

If your isp provides a dynamic, rather than a static ip address, there could also be problems. There may be a work-around for this, but I haven't seen one.
