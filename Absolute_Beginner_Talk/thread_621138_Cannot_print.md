### Post by jken146 on 2007-11-23
First, switch your printer on.  Usually your printer will be added automatically with no fuss, within a minute or two.  If not then go to System > Administration > Printing and attempt to add your printer that way.  If you still can't do it then post some more information such as the make and model of your printer, and how exactly it doesn't work.  Hope this helps.
