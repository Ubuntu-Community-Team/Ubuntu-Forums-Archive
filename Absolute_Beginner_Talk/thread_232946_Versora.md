### Post by x64Jimbo on 2006-12-11
From a PM that I got regarding this matter:
[quote=ubergoods]I unfortunately took the plunge into versora without realizing you could do this on your own. Can you refer me to some good resources and/or guides for migrating settings from microsoft email and we browser programs to linux? I am new to linux so the dummed down guides would be most helpful to me.[/quote]
As far as settings and such go, there's not much to it - just fire up your new software and go into the options menus and set it up the way that you like it. With files, put all your stuff on an external HDD or a thumb drive, then install your new OS and copy all the stuff into your home folder. 
For Microsoft email (I assume you mean Outlook) there's a tutorial here about how to convert an Outlook .pst file to a format that's usable in Linux: [http://lists.suse.com/archive/suse-linux-e/2002-May/3229.html](http://lists.suse.com/archive/suse-linux-e/2002-May/3229.html)
As far as your browser goes, just export your bookmarks in IE to a file, and then tell Firefox to import bookmarks from that same file.
