### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-11-12
Wine really doesn't work with hardware drivers and stuff. If it's an MTP device (which most Zens are) you probably want gnomad2 which will let you access the device. Amarok will also let you manage your songs on the device and both are in the repos. Though currently there is no way to browse the device through a file manager.
