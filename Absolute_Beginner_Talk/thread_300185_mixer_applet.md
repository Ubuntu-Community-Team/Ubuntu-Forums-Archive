### Post by jordanmthomas on 2006-11-15
Right click on your panel and go to Add to Panel
Add a Volume Control to your panel
Right click on it, and open the volume control

Once in here, you may need to go into its preferences and turn on all your audio channels.

Alternatively, from a command line, you can type
```
alsamixer
```
to edit your volumes via the command line.
