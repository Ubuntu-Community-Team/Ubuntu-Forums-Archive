> **eulallia1 said:**
> are you sure about deleting my hard drive upon boot wolfen? wont that make my computer dead? like nothing can run, not even a cd?




The button 'delete' will bring you into the BIOS menu, although my BIOS key is either F2 or Esc.

From there you look for boot up, and you will see both your HD and CD/DVD, and of course, your HD comes up first. So you drop HD, and raise CD/DVD up.
