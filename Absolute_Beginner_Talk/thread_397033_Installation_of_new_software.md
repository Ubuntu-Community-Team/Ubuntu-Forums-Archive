Well, in linux things are different... not necessarily more difficult... you (probably) have been using windows for years and you have learnt a lot of things there... things that you just take for granted and you may think why isn't it done the same in linux and hence you may think linux is difficult..
However if you keep an open mind you will eventually find that some things are more easily accomplished in linux than in windows... some things remain more easily accomplished in windows than in linux :) each OS has its strengths and weaknesses :)
