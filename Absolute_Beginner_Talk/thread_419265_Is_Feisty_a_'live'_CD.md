### Post by jfinkels on 2007-04-22
> **L Campbell said:**
> I'm using Dapper and have downloaded the Feisty .iso file, checked it with MD5SUM and burned a CD.

However, I assumed it would be a 'live' CD but when I insert the CD and do a re-boot (it is set to 'boot from CDROM) I am still in Dapper.

You may have to change your boot order in your BIOS setup so that your system tries to boot from the CD drive before trying to boot from the hard disk drive. Usually, you press <F2> or <Delete> as the computer is booting up to enter into your BIOS setup.
