### Post by aysiu on 2007-02-14
> **jtmedin said:**
> have no idea how to get into nautilus or windows behaviour?
Nautilus is the file manager. It's how you look at your files and folders usually. Try going to **Places** > **Home Folder**. This will open up Nautilus (the file browser).

From there, in Nautilus, select **Edit** > **Preferences** > **Behavior** > **Single-Click to Activate Items**
