### Post by mcduck on 2007-01-14
Screenshot captures your desktop to an image. Easiest way to take one is to press the Prt Scr key on your keyboard.

Alt+Prt Scr takes a screenshot of the currently active applications window instead of the whole desktop.

You can also go to Applications/Accessories/Take Screenshot
