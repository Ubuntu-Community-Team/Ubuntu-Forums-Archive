How do you assign the startup and shutdown sounds. They use to work but I changed the sounds for them and they quit. ### Post by subpar on 2007-11-25
System>Preferences>Sound

Then click on the sounds tab, and you are able to set sounds for multiple system events.

### Post by Dragons World on 2007-11-26
I have changed them in the Sounds tab. I finally got them to work. It's Ubuntus verbage in their OS. Logon really means startup and logoff really means shutdown. There is another area in the ubuntus OS where you can actually change the logon and logout sounds if you want but not here.
