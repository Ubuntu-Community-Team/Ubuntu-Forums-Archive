### Post by oilchangeguy on 2007-01-23
most versions of linux will make a computers network connection work right out of the box, however this cannot be said for a computers modem. if your friend is dead set on using dial-up, then he might be better off sticking with windows. yes it's possible to get the modem to work, it's just not worth the headache. i've read that external modems are more linux friendly than internal modems are. but with phone companies and cable companies offering lower levels of high speed at near dial-up prices why bother with the modem. upgrade your connection!
