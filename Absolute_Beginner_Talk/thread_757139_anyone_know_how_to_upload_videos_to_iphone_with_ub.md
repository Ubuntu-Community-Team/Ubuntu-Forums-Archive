### Post by st33med on 2008-04-16
The AVI format is not supported on iPhone. The only formats that work are MPEG-4, Quicktime, and H.246 videos. If you want to play those videos, you need to convert to one of those formats. Unfortunately, I don't know any codec converters. Search for them on synaptic, or have someone else tell you one.
