### Post by ThinkBuntu on 2007-04-24
Forget how it looks. It takes about one minute to turn MEPIS into a default KDE, and another to make it look as you please. MEPIS is a solid distro, but in my opinion it's a bit slow. However, if you're not using a laptop and can leave your machine on all the time, you'll probably enjoy MEPIS a great deal. I liked it, but I went with Mint afterward because it's much faster.
