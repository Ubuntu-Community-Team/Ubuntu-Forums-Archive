### Post by kernel tom on 2007-06-06
NO DRIVERS NEEDED!!!

This is especially for the LG Chocolate and similar lines of phones.

If you want to add media to your phone via the SD card, simply insert it into ur adapter or straight to ur computer if you have that option.

It should mount automatically, if not, mount it your self

open amarok, and go to devices, and configure it as a Generic Audio Player.

the path you wish to add files is as follows...

Song Location:
/my_music/%filename.%filetype

this is the only way the LG Chocolate will be able to load the files when you put ur card back in your phone...

took me a while to figure this out, hope it helps somebody out there.

regards,
tom
