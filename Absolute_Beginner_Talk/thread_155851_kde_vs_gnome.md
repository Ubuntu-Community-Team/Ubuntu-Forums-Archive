### Post by dermotti on 2006-04-05
KDE feels alot  like windows. KDE has alot of customization options. Ide say it has too much customization to the point it gets frustrating. Alot of times i have broke KDE customizing things. 

Gnome to me, is like a hybrid of windows and apple. It has a decent amount of customization, but not so much to confuse and frustrate. I have never broke gnome. Its definatly my second choice.

XFCE, which i currently use. It is fast, sleek, simple and very solid. Not the most customizable, but very simple and easy to use. It may be a little difficult at first, if you are expecting it to be like windows.

So in my mind

XFCE > Gnome > KDE


If you are used to windows,

KDE > Gnome > XFCE.
