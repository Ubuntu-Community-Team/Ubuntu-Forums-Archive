### Post by bluepowder7 on 2007-11-21
i've got CompizFusion running mostly fine on my laptop, which has on-board graphics and only 1gig or ram.  the onboard graphics don't even have their own memory - they "steal" some of the system ram, around 256megs of it.  runs mostly fine, but some fancy effects are a bit slow.
