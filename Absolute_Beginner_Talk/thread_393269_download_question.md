### Post by PartisanEntity on 2007-03-25
Which 'file' are you downloading? If you mean the Ubuntu .iso (CD image file) then yes of course you need to download it first before you burn it to CD.

In order to find out whether Ubuntu will be fast than your Windows 98 it is important to know what your computers specifications are: what is the processor speed, how much ram do you have are the bare minimum to know.

When people mean "you need to type things" it means that sometimes (not always) you might need to enter a string of text into a window in order to carry out certain actions.
