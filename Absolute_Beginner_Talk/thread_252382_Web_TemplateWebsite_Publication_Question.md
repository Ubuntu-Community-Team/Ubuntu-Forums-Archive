### Post by jordanmthomas on 2006-10-08
Well, it might take a while to learn GIMP well enough to actually be productive, but basically you just open it, switch to the layer with the text, select the text tool, and change it.  Then, you save each layer as a separate image to use on your webpage.  It's kind of complicated, but if you play around with it you should be able to get the hang of it.

But, yeah, I'd go with some of the other suggestions you got.
