### Post by Pumalite on 2007-09-29
Try:
Ctrl+Alt+F1 to get a command line.
At the command line:
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg, accept most defaults, choose 'vesa' as the driver, then: startx

(Personally, I think upgrades are messy. I usually save data and do a clean install)
