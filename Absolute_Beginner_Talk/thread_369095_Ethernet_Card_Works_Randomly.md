### Post by nu2this on 2007-02-24
This may sound crazy but I'm serious, this happened to me & this worked. I got it from the Suse forums.
1)turn off your PC
2) unplug the PC's power cord from the rear of the PC
3)press the power button on the PC, you may see a flash of light on the switch if you do it 's OK even if you don't it's OK
4) replug your PC back in & see if this problem persists

If it does go to your windows partition call your ISP tell them just what 's been happening & ask about forcing an IP address. They'll likely want to do other things 1st go along with it eventually they will do it. After they have it should be fine. 

Go on back to your Ubuntu side& about once a week do the steps above. Better yet if you have surge protector click the switch on it once a week do steps 1-4, you should have no problems that way. Since I started doing that I've had no problems 

Should this problem still persist then it's your ethernet card replace it.
