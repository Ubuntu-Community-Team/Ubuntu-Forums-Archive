### Post by Crosbie on 2006-08-26
A blank screen appears titled 'Downloads' with All files downloaded to... (some location) at the bottom?

What is the location?

To re-download, go to the menu bar at teh top, choose System, then Administration, then Synaptic Package Manager.  You'll need to type in your passowrd.  Then choose Search, and type firefox.  A page of packages should appear, including firfox, with a tickbox in green.  Right-click that tickbox and choose 'mark for reinstallation'.  Finally, 'Apply'.
