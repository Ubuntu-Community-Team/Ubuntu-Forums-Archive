### Post by llamakc on 2006-10-05
Oh, the bittorrent client shipped with Ubuntu doesn't do searches. One acquires the torrent files with other methods and then runs it.

An example would be at a torrent site and you click on the torrent to 'download' it, and Gnome will ask if you want to open it or save it. If you open it, the Gnome front end for bittorrent starts.
