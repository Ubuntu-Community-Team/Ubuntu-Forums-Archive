### Post by sebbouckaert on 2007-05-03
Not that I want to continue the everlasting KDE/Gnome war - both have their ups & downs and everybody's free to choose whichever they want - but: after  switching to Ubuntu 7.04 (freshly installed) I moved (back) from KDE to Gnome.

### Post by Metacarpal on 2007-05-03
The root of this matter doesn't lie in whether Gnome is better than KDE.

What it comes down to is, Ubuntu is the primary project, and the devs for Ubuntu work on the Gnome-based distro.  Kubuntu is a separate project and, in this release at least, appears to have lagged a little behind, so it came out a little choppier.

It's not a Gnome/KDE superiority thing.  It's a Feisty release schedule thing.
