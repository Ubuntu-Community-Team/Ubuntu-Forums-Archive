### Post by felicity on 2007-08-02
You mean [qbittorrent]("http://qbittorrent.sourceforge.net/download.php")? On the download page it gives directions for different versions of Ubuntu. Try following the directions for your Ubuntu version and you can install it from the added repository easily instead of compiling it from scratch. You may also want to try [Deluge]("http://deluge-torrent.org/") as an alternative bittorrent client.
