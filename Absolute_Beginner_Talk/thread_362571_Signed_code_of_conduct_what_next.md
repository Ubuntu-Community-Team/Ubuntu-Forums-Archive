### Post by jamesstansell on 2007-02-26
As far as I can tell Lauchpad doesn't know about your PGP key.  You may have to try step 2 again.

Once you've uploaded a code of conduct signed by a key that launchpad recognizes you'll get a "Ubuntero: Yes" on your main launchpad page.

If it still doesn't seem to work for you then keep asking for help.  The code of conduct is so important to the Ubuntu community that I'd rather have someone who wants to sign it ask 100 times than give up.  Also, if you're having problems then other people probably are too.
