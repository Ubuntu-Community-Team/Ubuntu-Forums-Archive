### Post by plucky on 2008-03-20
The question is how do you know what Feisty updates are required? Just because Gutsy Ubuntu has an update,doesn't mean that Feisty Xubuntu does.

Have you thought about creating a small partition on your desktop and installing Feisty Xubuntu?

Then all you have to do is boot into Xubuntu occasionally and updating from the Feisty repositories ,before creating an AptonCD CD to transfer over to the laptop.

Also some of the applications in Ubuntu and Xubuntu are different and the ones in Xubuntu would not get updated if you just used Ubuntu for updates.


Hope this helps
