### Post by sameoldude on 2007-06-04
hi, i'm a giant noob,
o.k, this is for the people who have had trouble with getting their sound to work on ubuntu. i tried many of the fixes that appeared on the forum with no success. i had the same trouble with pclos2007, so i thought maybe the same solution would apply.

here's what to do:

1. right click on the speaker icon, choose Open Volume Controls
2. now that you're there, click on Preferences (on the menu)
3. look for the square next to External Amplifier, then click it
4. exit that tiny window
5. in the second tab of sound control now you can see the external amplifier was on, but you might not have one and that's why we ain't getting any sound, let's turn it off
6. try opening some sound file
7. done! (hopefully)

i hope this little fix works for some of you noobies that have updated alsa and kernels and whatnot and still get frustrated because this beautiful OS has no sound.
