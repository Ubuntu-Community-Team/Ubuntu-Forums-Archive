### Post by 5-HT on 2007-04-26
Absolutely! 
'Inactivity' for gnome-power-preferences includes the 'idle' time in gnome-screensaver.
If you'd like to put the display to sleep after a couple minutes of inactivity, you'll need to reduce the time required for the computer to be considered idle in the screensaver preferences to one minute.

Minutes required for inactivity (power management settings) = Minutes required for idle (screensaver settings) + 1

Hope that helps.
