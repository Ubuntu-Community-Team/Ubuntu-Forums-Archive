A simple question, I hope:
 How can I remove the text descriptions that pop up every time I hover over an icon on the top panel ('Firefox Web Browser, Browse the World Wide Web' etc).  I've tired tweaking various settings in gconf-editor but to no avail.

### Post by gazj on 2007-05-01
ok

open gconf-editor

then goto apps > panel > global

then untick tooltips enabled.

Hope that helps.
