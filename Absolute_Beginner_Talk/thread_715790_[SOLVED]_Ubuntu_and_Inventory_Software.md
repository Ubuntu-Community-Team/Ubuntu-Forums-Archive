### Post by webdr on 2008-03-05
For simple asset tracking, you can use your barcode reader and a standard example database from the open office wizard. Click applications, office, office.org database. Create new, follow prompts, select business, then asset tables, and oo does the rest. 

Also, if I remember correctly, WASPNEST stores in MSAccess tables so you may also want to look into some cool import export features that are available in Oo for MSA data.

Hope this helps.
