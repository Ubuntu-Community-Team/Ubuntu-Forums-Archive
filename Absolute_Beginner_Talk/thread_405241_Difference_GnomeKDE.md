### Post by ardchoille42 on 2007-04-09
KRecipes is a kde app and you seem to be running it in gnome with no problems.

There are no problems with running kde apps in gnome, and vice-versa. The only thing you may notice is that, when running a kde app in gnome, the kde libs need to load to be able to use a kde app and this may lead to kde apps in gnome being a little slow.
