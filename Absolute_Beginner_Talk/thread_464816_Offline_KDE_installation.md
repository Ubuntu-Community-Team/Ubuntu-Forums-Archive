### Post by pbw on 2007-06-05
It'd be quite simple if you had a kubuntu cd, and a spare cd.


Use apt-move to place kde 3.5.7 on your spare cd.

Add your kubuntu cd to your sources.list and install kde.

Then do the same with the 3.5.7 cd to upgrade.
