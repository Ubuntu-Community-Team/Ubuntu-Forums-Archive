### Post by stalker145 on 2006-11-22
1. 6.06 has the most stable programs for your computer while 6.10 stays on the 'edge' with the most recent stuff (hence the name Edgy Eft).

2. LTS is Long-Term Support. The distro will be supported for 3 years.

3. The CD's are 100% free. I received 15 CD's a couple of months ago to distribute to friends/family and didn't pay a dime. No postage, no CD fee, nothing.
A word of caution, though: I believe some localities *may* have to pay some sort of a crazy import tax or something. If I recall correctly, most of that info is on the Ship-It site.
