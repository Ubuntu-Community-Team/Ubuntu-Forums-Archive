### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-04-08
Best to backup all your files when hardy comes out in final form and then do a clean install but this time keep /home as a separate partition.  Then if you ever need to update to a new version or simply reinstall because you've broken something, which I did a few times when I started with linux, you won't have to worry about your personal files and settings.
