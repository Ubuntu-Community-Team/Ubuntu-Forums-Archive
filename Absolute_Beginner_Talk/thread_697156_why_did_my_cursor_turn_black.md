### Post by linuxmagick on 2008-02-14
If you're using Gnome as your desktop manager, then click **System** in the panel, then **Preferences,** and choose **Appearance.** On the **Themes** tab, click the **Customize** button (it's near the bottom).  Select the tab called **Pointer** and then select the type of mouse pointer you want.  That should do it.  Let me know if you still need help.
