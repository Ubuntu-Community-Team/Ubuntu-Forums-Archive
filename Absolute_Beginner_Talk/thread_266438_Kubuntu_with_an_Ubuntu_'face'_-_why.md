For the time being you can change the desktop background to anything you like by selecting System>Preferences>Desktop Background. You can even load material from pictures you have downloaded as long as they are in the "png" file format. 

As for what your desktop currently is at the moment, I cant say. Someone more experienced will have to answer. I will say that KDE is rather buggy, however, and you are really better off with Gnome, which is much more stable. Some people interface much better with the KDE gui, so if it works better for you, then your choice is clear.
