### Post by jken146 on 2008-02-07
> **Cariro said:**
> Actually iTunes is for Mac OS.X
And that makes it even better, cause Mac OS.X and Linux is both based on the UNIX core, so in wine i think it will work pretty good.

Wine is a *Windows* compatability layer.  It is used for running Windows binaries in linux, and so no Mac programs will work in Wine.


[http://winehq.org/](http://winehq.org/) will tell you about the various versions of iTunes that have been tested under Wine, but don't hold your breath!  I haven't heard many all-out success stories.

That said, there are native Linux programs that can sync with most iPods, such as gtkpod.  If you want to buy music from the itunes store, you're probably out of luck.
