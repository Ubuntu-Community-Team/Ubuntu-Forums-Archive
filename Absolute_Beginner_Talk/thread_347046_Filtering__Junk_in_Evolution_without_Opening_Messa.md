### Post by bustanil on 2007-01-29
Sure you can, just add a new filter (Edit > Message Filter > Add) for **incoming mails**, then set the criterias which identify your junk mails, then add new action (in the **Then** section) to "Set Status" "Junk". You can even colorize your junk mails by using action "Assign color" and pick your favorite junk mail color :D
Hope this help. :D
