### Post by DracoPsycho on 2007-06-13
Yup, Pidgin is just new version of Gaim. They had to change the name because developers of AIM claimed that its name is too similar to their product. SO right now it's Pidgin and that's all ;) Main change which bothers some people is the fact that there's no protocol icon next to your contact name in contact list, there's only icon showing status of the contact. Personally I don't see any problem with that ;)
