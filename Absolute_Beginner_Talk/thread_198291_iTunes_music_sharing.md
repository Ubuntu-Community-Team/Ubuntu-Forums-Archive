### Post by Vurius on 2006-06-17
I know for a fact that Banshee with the DAAP plugin allows streaming to iTunes.  I have Banshee up and running on my desktop computer with my collection of ~17,000 songs around the clock.  Whenever I want to listen to music on my Macbook Pro, instead of copying music over, I just open iTunes and click on the Banshee music share, which is found automatically for me.  It also works in reverse: I can see my iTunes share (with a grand total of 3 songs) from Banshee.

If this isn't the type of sharing you are talking about, I'm sorry for wasting your time.  However, if this is what you desire, give Banshee and its DAAP plugin a whirl.
