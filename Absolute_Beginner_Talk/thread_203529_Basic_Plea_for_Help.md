### Post by therunnyman on 2006-06-25
There are a couple cool alternative system monitors, if you feel like popping Synaptic open.  Conky is all the rage, but I like GKrellM.  The former has the benefit of being really light weight, resource-wise; the latter has all kinds of wacky and cool plug-ins, and a zillion skins.  There are also desklets that'll do what you're looking for, but they tend to be resource-intensive.
