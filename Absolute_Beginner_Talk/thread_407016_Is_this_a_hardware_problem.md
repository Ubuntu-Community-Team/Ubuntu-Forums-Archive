### Post by jem7v on 2007-04-11
Did you plug your headphones into the same jack your speakers are usually plugged into?  Or onto the headphone port on the front of your speakers?  Or is it a laptop and you haven't tried plugging in headphones or speakers before? (Long question short: What's your computer like, and where did you plug your headphones into it?)

The only place I can think to check software-wise would be Applications>Sound and Video>Volume Control.  Once the volume control panel comes up, go into Edit>Preferences and uncheck anything related to your speakers, then look to see if there's any left/right discrepancies there.
