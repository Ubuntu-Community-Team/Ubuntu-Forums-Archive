### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-09-21
If you're installing a different distro (like SuSE, RedHat, etc), then they should both show up in the GRUB loader.  If they don't, and it boots straight into one, pop in here and we'll help you get it sorted out.

If you want to install another flavor of Ubuntu (like Kubuntu or Xubuntu), then you would be able to switch between them (Gnome, KDE, and XFCE desktops, respectively) at the login screen.  Just use "select session"
