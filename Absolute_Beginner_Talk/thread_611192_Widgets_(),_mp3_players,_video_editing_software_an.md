### Post by mtn on 2007-11-12
> **mexicola said:**
> Hello!
First of all, the widgets (or what they're called). I'm talking about that line at the bottom of the screen with the fancy icons on it, how do I get them?


I think you are referring to the panels at the top and bottom of the screen? To add "widgets", right click and click "Add to Panel"

> **mexicola said:**
> 
 And also, how do I zoom out and watch the entire Cube spin? I want the stuff that guy on youtube have!


I think you need to add the Beryl manager through Synaptic. From the main menu go to System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager. Once Synaptic is open search for Beryl.

> **mexicola said:**
> 
Mp3-player. I want one with decent media library and cover art, but all I can find is standard text-players.


I think Exaile is good but have a look at this article with lots of screen shots for a good run down of what is available: [http://onlyubuntu.blogspot.com/2007/03/media-players-available-for-ubuntu.html](http://onlyubuntu.blogspot.com/2007/03/media-players-available-for-ubuntu.html)

> **mexicola said:**
> 
Finally, are there any good photo and video editing tools for Ubuntu?!

Have you tried the GIMP? Applications>Graphics>GIMP Image Editor.
