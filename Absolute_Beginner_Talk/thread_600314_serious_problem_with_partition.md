### Post by Officer Dibble on 2007-11-02
When you mention "to a previous state", what was that previous state? Was that "previous state" an image of the entire hard disk before you partitioned it?

By restoring this partition it is quite likely you have overlaid what was current with what was previous because it may have restored not just the partition but the entire disk.

Depending how important those files are to you I would suggest that you don't write to the disk any further at this time and get a knowledgeable friend to have a look at it for you. However, there are applications you can get to retrieve data that hasn't been overwritten.
