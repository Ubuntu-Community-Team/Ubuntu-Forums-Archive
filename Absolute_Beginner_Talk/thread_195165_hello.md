### Post by someusernoob on 2006-06-12
Depends on you!

- Do you want to play games? Then you need Windows (yes, there are _some_ games for Linux)
- Do you like to produce music on your computer? Then you need Windows, or even better buy a Mac and use OS/X.

And so I can make a whole list of things that do not work very well with Linux yet but do work with Windows or OS/X.

Those are the reasons that I didn't dump Windows yet. I like to play a game once in a while, and i like to play with Propellerheads Reason. 

And if it will be better in the future depends on both the users (do they ask for things) and developers (do they want to develop the things users ask) and for what i've seen untill now, they want to develop!! So I think (maybe not the near future tho) that in the future games and other non-linux-yet-programs will be less a problem on Linux then it is now. Or at least I hope so.

And once we can do all those things we used to do on Windows, there is a change for Linux to grow bigger and bigger, but do we really want that?
