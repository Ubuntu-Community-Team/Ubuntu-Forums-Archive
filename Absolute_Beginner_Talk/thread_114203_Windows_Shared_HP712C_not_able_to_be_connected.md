### Post by Mustard on 2006-01-08
I've noticed this post slowly dropping down with no responses, so I thought I would throw in a suggestion.

There  is an IRC support channel for ubuntu, as well as the forums.  If you have the time and inclination, you might like to try to find someone in IRC who is familiar with printer sharing and samba who can assist you.

The X-Chat client is installed by default in Ubuntu and can be found in the Applications>>Internet menu.  When you go to choose a server to connect to from the Server List, you will see 'Ubuntu Servers' listed and that should automatically connect you to the irc.freenode.net network and put you into the #ubuntu channel.  From there I am hopeful that you will find some assistance with your problem.  If you try it out, just dive in a try to give as much detail as possible about your problem.  Just as in here, the participants in IRC are just volunteer community members, so there is no guarantees that anyone will be able to help, but its certainly a way that many people find solutions.

Don't stop trying in here of course, but its just a suggestion for another avenue of finding help while this thread seems to be getting limited attention (probably due to no-one being around atm who knows how to dea with the issue). :)
