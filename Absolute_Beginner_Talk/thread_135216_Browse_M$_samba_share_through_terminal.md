### Post by Robgould on 2006-02-23
First I would mount the share with a mount statement.  You can read about that here:
 
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=131561]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=131561")
 
Make sure you read on down the post to the part about mounting without your fstab.
 
Then navigate to the share like you would any other file or folder from the terminal.
