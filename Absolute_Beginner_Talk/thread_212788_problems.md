### Post by Brunellus on 2006-07-10
Ubuntu does NOT, repeat NOT, ship with proprietary media codecs for legal reasons. Out of the box, you can only play things in Free formats (ogg vorbis, ogg theora).  

For help on this:

[http://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats](http://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats)
