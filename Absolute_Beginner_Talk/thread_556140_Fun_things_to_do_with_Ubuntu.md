### Post by PreviousN on 2007-09-21
Things to do:

Ubuntu has a great imaging editor by default: GIMP. Play around with it, I hear that its as powerful as photoshop.

Currently I use ubuntu as a server for jinzora2 ([http://www.jinzora.com/](http://www.jinzora.com/)) it allows me to stream my music anywhere in the world. I went to japan about 6 months ago and was streaming my music from my server in cali. Pretty chill.

Also you could set up your own mediawiki. I use that for notes (I'm a college student). Its got a mysql backend so it might be over your head, but its searchable and fast. Easy to print off cheat sheets too.

Also, just use it for everyday use. Try wine/cedega to get games working. Wine is pretty f*cking awesome. Its not an emulater, but rather the windows api implimented in linux...So some programs even run FASTER.

Also, try amaroK. Thats a great music player the equivalent of itunes.
