### Post by xpod on 2006-09-14
As i just said to someone else...try installing after booting into "safe graphics mode" at startup.....failing that you might need to use the alternate cd......it dont use all the resources that the gui live cd does hence it installs easier on those tempremental old machines.

Ive had to use the alternate text installer version and its even simpler than the live cd plus you can skip any problem parts and sort it later but it should just fly through the install....

Failing THAT...you might need to take a step down in size to either kubunto or even xubunto......both good for older machines....

You`ll get one of them on.....it`s just a case of trying each and taking a step down if ness...i.e Ubu live to Ubu alternate.THEN kubu live then kubu alternate and finally xubunto live to xubunto alternate.

THATS how i found the one that would work on my kubu machine.
Just using "safe graphics mode" worked for this here ubunto though
