### Post by kyphi on 2007-11-06
First of all, Welcome to Ubuntu and the Ubuntu Forum.

To put a launcher from your menu onto your desktop, just open Applications, Programming, and drag the icon to your desktop.

You can then start an Icon file in your home folder and place that icon in it.

If you want to add an icon to your panel (top or bottom), right click on the menu entry and choose 'Add launcher to panel'.

Right click the icon on the panel and choose Properties.  Left click on the icon and then Browse to your newly created Icon folder in your home directory and select your favourite icon.

Easy, isn't it.
