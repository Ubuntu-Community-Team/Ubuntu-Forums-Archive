I am trying to make my server for building a community.
I have XAMPP working ok. Now I am testing CMS systems. For those who may not know, CMS are a system to administrate and build webpage to do forums like this, for example, and administrate them in a visual way. That makes having a community easier.

There are also smaller CMS that work for a single purpose, like having a Blog. I am interested in BIG systems, but if you want to serve your own blog, this is valuable for you too.

I am focused in GPL content manager systems (CMS) for liberty and  costs reasons.
I have found these useful links for people like me:

[http://www.opensourcecms.com/](http://www.opensourcecms.com/)

This has demos that can be visited and administered to test the different CMS that have GPL license.

[http://www.cmsmatrix.org/](http://www.cmsmatrix.org/)

This is a quick way to choose a CMS, since it can compare any of them. You can also use a form to mark some caracteristics of what you seek for and it will give you the names of what CMS has what you want.

I finally selected 4 CMS:
XARAYA
XOOPS
TYPO3
TIKIWIKI ( also known as TIKI or TIKI CMS )

TYPO3 seems to be too had to understand and manage. XARAYA is a bit confusing but feels good and not too complex.
The easier are: TIKI and XOOPS.
I printed the comparative that I got in cmsmatrix.org between the 4. 

I want to ask if someone has test Xoops, Xaraya or Tiki and may give me some advice on what is easier to deal with, wich is more powerfull and full-featured. 

If any, I also appreciate any comments about anything that I said, or new suggestions.

Thanks to all,

Jordi
