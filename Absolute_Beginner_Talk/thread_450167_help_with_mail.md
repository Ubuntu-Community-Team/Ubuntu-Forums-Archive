### Post by houstonbofh on 2007-05-21
All of your settings for any program are in your home folder.  Usually they are named .evolution, or whatever the program.  The leading . makes the file hidden. In Nautilus, you just need to click on "Show Hidden Files" and it is there.  Delete your .evolution folder, and your will run Evolution "for the first time" all over again.
