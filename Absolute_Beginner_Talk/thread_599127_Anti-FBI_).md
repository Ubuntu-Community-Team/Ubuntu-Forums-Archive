### Post by Colro on 2007-11-01
Kind of a stupid title that might get moderated, but nonetheless:

Go to system -> administration -> login window preferences. Then click on the "Security" tab. There's a place where you can set the delay between allowed login attempts. Keep in mind that it's kind of pointless unless you're in the immediate danger of being brute forced, though. If the FBI takes your computer, they're going to mount your hard drive on another machine and have full access to it regardless.
