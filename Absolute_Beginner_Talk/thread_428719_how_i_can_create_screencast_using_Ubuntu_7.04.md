### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-04-30
Applications > Acessories > Take Screenshot.

Choose your options, its easy enough. Choose your options on first screen, then pick where to save it (default is desktop) on second screen.

BTW, Print Screen will take a snapshot of your current screen (All windows) and let you save it anywhere.

Have fun taking screenies :D
