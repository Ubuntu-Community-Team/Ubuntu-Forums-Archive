### Post by Bartender on 2007-11-07
Late reply -
As you've probly already figured out, the chances are about zero.

If you can find one, pick up a used serial external hardware-based modem and the necessary cables.  The most reliable way to get online at someone else's house, although you'll face other hurdles like their ISP's settings, and possibly no serial connection on the PC.  The Sabrent serial-to-USB cable works fine with latest Ubuntu.
