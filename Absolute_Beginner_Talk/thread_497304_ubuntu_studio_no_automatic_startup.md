### Post by steamshooter on 2007-07-10
I'm with you on liking Studio. It's great!

Automatic login:

Go to System>Administration>Login Window (here you're asked for your password)
Click on the Security tab and up at the top is Enable Automatic Login. 
Check the box and you're all set.
