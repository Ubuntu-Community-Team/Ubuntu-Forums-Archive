### Post by davmac on 2006-12-06
Have a look at [http://parker1.co.uk/mythtv_ubuntu.php](http://parker1.co.uk/mythtv_ubuntu.php) and there's a good guide for getting mythtv up and running with freeview. The author uses a pair of Hauppauge Nova-T DVB-T Cards (one with the Phillips chipset one with Conexant). You can see the full details about his hardware at [http://parker1.co.uk/mythtv_silent.php](http://parker1.co.uk/mythtv_silent.php)

I've had an external Twinhan USB DVB-T lying around (its a laptop so no room for a card) and had time to get through the early steps and was successfully able to scan for channels. I couldn't get a picture in mplayer or gxine and ran out of time, although I suspect it was a problem with me rather than the box. I'll be having another go this weekend and will update the thread if I find out anything more.

Hope this helps,

Dave Mac
