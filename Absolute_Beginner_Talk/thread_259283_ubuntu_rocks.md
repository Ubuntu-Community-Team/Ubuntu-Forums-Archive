I just wanted to see a positive thread title after all the "I'm going back to Windows" threads.

I was actually quite shocked when, at the start of this year, I loaded [COLOR=Lime]Suse[/COLOR] Linux 10.0 onto a new desktop to discover how easy it all was to install. It actually came up into a graphical environment and had all the office tools, browser and email packages.

I am agog that all this functionality, the effortless installation and ease of use, all of it is delivered on a CD completely free. Wonderful. Much kudos to everyone involved in the ubuntu project. I'm a big fan. =D>=D>
