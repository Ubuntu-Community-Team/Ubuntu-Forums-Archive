### Post by scotttkd on 2007-04-20
unfortunately there seem to be a lot of video related problems and just as many possible solutions.  I have similar problems and no one solution I have found in the forums seems to work for me.  

there is a plugin from the mozilla site that some swear by (search for "mediaplayer" and you should find it) but it did not work for me.

 Automatix is another fix that works for some, (google for it and there is a download for specific ubuntu flavors) just install the codecs and plugins section and it seems to fix for many.  For me the adobe flash driver tries to take over for wmv files and even though I set mplayer or an equivalent in file type handling,it gets ignored and flash still tries to play (audio, no video) the only way for me to get flash to let go is to uninstall.  

 I lost video compatibility when firefox updated from 2.02 to 2.03 and have had little luck in getting a complete fix.

keep searching the forum and someones solution will probably work on your set up.

good luck
