### Post by sprinkles on 2007-09-18
The problem is Myspace's uploader is  looking for files with specific extensions. It specifies jpg as one of them,  however most cameras save the extension as .JPG (uppercase). The problem doesn't arise on Windows, because filenames are case insensitive, but on Linux, it means you can't see the files.
