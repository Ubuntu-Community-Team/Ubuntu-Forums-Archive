### Post by rickycodie on 2007-08-19
heres how i do it (i just explained this to my boss too so your not alone). take the cared out of the phone and stick it in the adapter that you should have gotten with the card (if you don't know what i'm talking about you probably don't have it) it looks like a sd card with a slot in the back. then stick that in your sd card reader in your comp.  open the disk file that appears on your desktop, and drag n' drop the appropriate files in the appropriate folder. stick back in your phone and the songs should show up.

to use wine, go to
applications>add/remove programs

and ensure that you have show all available apps selected from the dropdown in the upper right-hand corner. search for wine and tick the box. then hit apply. it will ask for confirmation give it till it installs. then once installed, put your disc in and look for the .exe file on it. double click it, and wait for around for a while if it doesn't install, sorry, if it does good for you. 

some windows programs work and some don't. 

oh you could also try installing amarok and see if that will recognise the phone as a media device.
