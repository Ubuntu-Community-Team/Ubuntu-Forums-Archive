### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-06-28
To my knowledge (I could be wrong) the image viewer is just that, a viewer. It doesn't edit pictures in the least, simply displays them and as you found it can rotate the pictures. If you close them after rotating it doesn't actually change the orientation, it just saves how you liked it displayed for future.

Open the thing in GIMP, the actual picture. In the window with the image, go Image > Transform > Rotate X direction. Then save the picture out after its suitably rotated. That is all.
