### Post by Sef on 2008-01-05
With the cursor on the top panel > right click > Utilities > click on Keyboard Indicator.

That will allow you to choose which keyboard you want.

### Post by siretfel on 2008-01-05
Ok got it! For those who had the same problem this is it:

Go to top panel-->right click-->add to panel-->Keyboard Indicator

this should give you the language you use.

Go to system-->preferences-->keyboard-->go to Layout tab and add your language-->go to Layout options and go to Group Shift/Lock behavior-->check Both alt keys together change group and Alt Shift changes group

This should work

That worked for me
