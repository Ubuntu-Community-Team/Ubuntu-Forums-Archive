### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-03-07
Your PC is only a little better speced than my machine.
Why limit your PC to 32bits when 64bits would speed up number crunching tasks such as BOINC work units, encoding videos, and in some cases playing games?

Your PC can never be too powerful running Ubuntu, it's what applications you choose to run and what you want to do with your PC that decides.
