### Post by Stealth on 2006-04-28
Yes...

1. Find your drivers, maybe even get [this CD]("http://www.downloadsquad.com/2006/04/04/virtually-all-current-xp-drivers-on-one-cd/")
2. Then if you get that CD, I'm pretty sure you can just burn the ISO by right clicking on it. Or if you d/l the drivers yourself, throw in an empty CD into your burner and you should get the dialog asking what you'd like to do. :)
3. Burn
