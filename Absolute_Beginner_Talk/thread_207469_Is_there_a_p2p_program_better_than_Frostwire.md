### Post by GTvulse on 2006-07-01
Your question is confusing. What are you talking about, p2p networks or gnutella servelets?

If the first, there are **better** thought out p2p networks with more efficient use of network resources and better overall privacy, but about the contents I cannot comment. If you are talking about gnutella servelets, there are at least three other gnutella servelets available in the Ubuntu software repositories: mutella, gtk-gnutella and ml-donkey.
