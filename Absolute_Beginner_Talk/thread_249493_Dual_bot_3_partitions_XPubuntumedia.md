### Post by cotcot on 2006-09-02
ext3

Install in your XP (supposing your windows is XP) a driver for accessing ext2/3. You can download the driver from [www.driver-fs.org](www.driver-fs.org) and install is very simple with a couple of clicks.

Fat has the disadvantage of limiting the size to maximum 4 GB for a file. You get this easely if you work with video capturing.
