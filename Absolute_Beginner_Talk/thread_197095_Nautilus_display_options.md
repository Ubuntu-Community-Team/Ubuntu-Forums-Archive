### Post by mcduck on 2006-06-28
If you go into Nautilus settings, you'll find options for showing additional information under each item. You can choose 3 types of info (size, type date etc.), and how many of them are displayed depends on how big you have scaled your icons. By default, at 100% (normal size) no additional info is displayed, as you increase the size more info will appear. Just put 'size' in the first available field (empty by default)and you'll get file size under all files and amount of files under every directory.
