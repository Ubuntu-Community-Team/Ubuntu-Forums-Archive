### Post by tehwa on 2005-10-02
[QUOTE=Drifter]I used firefox[/QUOTE]
Within Firefox, click through the 'Edit' menu and click 'Preferences' (alternatively, press <Alt>+E, then 'n' to open it).
Click on 'Downloads' in the left panel, the first option will be Download Folder. This is where downloaded files are saved, and it's likely that it says Desktop.
If the files have not appeared on the desktop, try opening file browser, then browsing to ~/Desktop and then refreshing the folder.
