### Post by Denis on 2006-05-21
Right click on the panel where you want the icon to be displayed. Chose "add to panel". You'll get a dialog with everything you can add. Scroll down until you get to utilities. Select "Notification Area" and press the Add button. 

You will now see a line or something similar. Normally your Skype icon will show up there when you run it. You can move the notification area around (on your panel) by dragging it.
