### Post by bwallum on 2007-10-19
Hi Bruised

Thanks for your help. I followed your advice, went to linuxprinting.org and found lots of info. I decided to try the Minolta 'legacy' driver mq3100np.pdd.

It downloaded as a tar file, was recognised by Gutsy; on right click,open, a simple dialogue enabled me to unpack it. I then went to System>Administration>Printing and when prompted 'browsed' to the unpacked file on my desktop. It loaded instantly, I couldn't believe anything had happened until I noticed extra tabs had appeared. One of the tabs, 'Installable Options'  enabled me to select an additional multipurpose tray. Absolutely brilliant and so painless!

I can now select a tray when printing from most apps, at the print dialogue. I cannot do this with Firefox for some reason but I am working on it.

You wouldn't believe the pain in setting up this printer in XP (and it was marketed as a Windows printer!). I am chuffed to bits with Gutsy.

Thanks again for your help, it is really appreciated.
