Have you tried the system monitor? I think that's what it's called, anyway. 

Right-click on a bar, add, and find system monitor. Once you have the single window up there, right-click one that (it's your CPU monitor) and add a networking window. 

It's not real-time, but it's not bad and you don't have to install anything.
