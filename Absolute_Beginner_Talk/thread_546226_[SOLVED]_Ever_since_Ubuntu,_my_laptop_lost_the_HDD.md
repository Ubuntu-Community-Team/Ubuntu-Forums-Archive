### Post by schorsch on 2007-09-08
You could check in the BIOS if the hard drive is properly recognized. When starting the machine there should be some hint which key to press to enter the BIOS. It should be something like "ESC, DEL, F2" or something like this. But don't change anything in the BIOS right now and save it as this can leave your machine unbootable! Just take a look ....
