### Post by Paul820 on 2007-11-15
To make a good selection you should really zoom in as far a possible and do the selection that way. If the selection is still too jagged you can blur the edges by right clicking on the layer in the layers dialogue and do an alpha to selection, shrink the selection by two pixels and then invert it. Then go to filters->Blur>Gaussian Blur and blur it by a couple of pixels. 

If you want a more precise way of selecting something and cutting it out there is this tutorial that teaches how to make a perfect cut. [http://www.gimpguru.org/Tutorials/SimulatedDOF/]("http://www.gimpguru.org/Tutorials/SimulatedDOF/")
