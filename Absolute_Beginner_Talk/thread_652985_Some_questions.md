### Post by kestrel1 on 2007-12-29
To enable WiFi in Ubuntu right click on the network icon in the panel (top right) & select enable Wireless. To connect to a wireless network, left click on the same icon & select the network you wish to connect to.
To boot into Windows at startup have a look at this link: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=426954&highlight=bigbluecat](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=426954&highlight=bigbluecat)
