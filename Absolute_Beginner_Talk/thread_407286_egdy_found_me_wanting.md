### Post by tipsqueal on 2007-04-12
Customize the hell out of it. Do like I do and make your own backgrounds, make your own tile for the top/bottom shortcut menu (mine is a nice slab of mahogany). Do what you can to make it run faster (If you dont know how then look it up and then do it). Clean up your Xorg.conf, Get bluetooth working (it requires a $5-10 usb Bluetooth stick), try out the gazillion media players there are, take over the world, help other people new to Ubuntu on these forums (I try that as often as I can), go read through synaptic and find some new programs to keep you entertained (I love the Hydrogen drum machine), etc...
