### Post by annda on 2007-10-12
personally, i would wait for the official release. i did betas with edgy and feisty (both a few months before releases) and was pretty happy. in my experience and with my hardware gutsy seems to get unpleasant bugs with updates. they get fixed pretty quickly but unless you have another install or distro (or OS/computer) to fall back on for a few hours or a day, i'd wait.

i'm using gutsy now, but there were days where i had to manually tweak things or switch to gentoo or wolvix to finish some work because gutsy refused to cooperate in some areas. but in general, it's great.
