So I have a DVD made of Feisty and I was just running it by way of the LiveCD and was thinking about installing it. If I were to install Feisty over my Edgy, would it replace everything I have on my Linux partition and install Feisty fresh or would my documents/software remain intact?

### Post by BKonkle on 2007-05-05
From my past experience, Feisty does not like to be installed over an "unclean" source.  If you didn't separate your systems into multiple partitions when you first installed Ubuntu, then it will probably erase everything because it will need to reformat the partition.

If that is the scenario you are in, and you feel adventurous - try resizing the partition that you installed originally and moving your /home directory to a new partition.  There's a tutorial on how to do this here: [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome)

This way, you can save your documents all in your /home directory, and when you reinstall the operating system or upgrade, you can leave your /home directory intact.
