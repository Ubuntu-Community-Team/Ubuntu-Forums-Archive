### Post by chggr on 2007-03-07
Well I think you aren't doing anything wrong.
The password ubuntu asks for is the one you created when you firstly installed Ubuntu.

The first thing that comes to my mind is that you should check whether you have CAPS LOCK on or you are entering your password in another language. It has happened to me many times to enter a password, ubuntu to reject it and then to realize that the greek characterset was on while my password has only latin alphabet letters in it.
