### Post by freebird54 on 2007-07-04
The question is whether it is off by a consistent amount - or completely random.  If the first is true, the amount it is off will be by a constant number of complete hours - and is caused by settings differing between CUT (Greenwich mean time is the old name) and local.

If is completely random, then you probably have a dead battery protecting your BIOS settings, including time.
