### Post by catlett on 2006-08-19
It's just how the panel applets are loading. For some reason your power and clock applets are starting in a different order occasionally. If you want proof you are not loosing your mind :D  Right click on the top panel and select "add to panel" add a few applets to the panel. When you restart, most likely they will not be in the same order when you restart.
The one unusual part is the recurrence. Normally an order is worked out after you restart and they don't change order again.
Keep the new ones there for a few restarts and see if it occurs with them as well.
