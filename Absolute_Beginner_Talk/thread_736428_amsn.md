### Post by smurphy_it on 2008-03-26
In your home folder/User profile (whatever your userid is) is where you will find the files.

amsn_received will be your downloads.

.amsn/displaypic is where your cached user's display thumbnails are
.amsn/logs are of course the logs

etc.

.amsn is a hidden folder so when you are in your file manager viewing your files hit Ctrl+H to show hidden files (otherwise in file manager choose view, then click on "Show Hidden Files".
