### Post by Ben Sprinkle on 2006-10-07
Time format codes:
HH - The hour according to a 24-hour clock, using two digits (00 to 23).
hH - The hour according to a 24-hour clock, using one or two digits (0 to 23).
PH (uppercase &#8220;p&#8221;) - The hour according to a 12-hour clock, using two digits (01 to 12).
pH (lowercase &#8220;p&#8221;) - The hour according to a 12-hour clock, using one or two digits (1 to 12).
MM - The current minute using two digits (00 to 59).
SS - The current second using two digits (00 to 59).
AMPM - Either &#8220;am&#8221; or &#8220;pm&#8221; depending on the hour. Useful with PH or pH.
