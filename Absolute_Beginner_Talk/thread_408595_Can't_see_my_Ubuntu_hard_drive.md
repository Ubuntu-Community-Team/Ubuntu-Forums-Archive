### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2007-04-13
> **a.v.l said:**
> When in Ubuntu Edgy I can see my XP Hard drive on the desktop and in (places/computer) but not my Ubuntu Hard drive why is that?

The file system is a little different.

Under "places" you have your home directory. The rest of your root partition is needed only for sys admin. So, essentially, Home is your Ubuntu HD (unless you are doing sys admin).
