How do you connect to the Internet? If you use broadband/LAN, just plug the Ethernet cable into the PC before booting, and you're on.

If you use dialup, it's more complicated, and I suggest you take a look
at the Wiki ([https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UserDocumentation](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UserDocumentation)) where you can
find answers to many of the questions you may have.
