For some reason any apps (terminal, firefox etc) I open show up at the top and bottom panel , can I remove the apps at the top panel and just show them at the bottom panel?

### Post by Xian on 2006-03-16
Right-click on the top panel at the small divider where your apps begin to appear when open. Choose "Remove From Panel".
