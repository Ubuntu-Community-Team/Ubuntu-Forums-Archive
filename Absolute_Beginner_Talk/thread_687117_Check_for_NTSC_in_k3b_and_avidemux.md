### Post by fiddledd on 2008-02-04
I'm not sure if this is what you are doing, but, Your DVD Player won't play MPEG files (at least none that I know of) and Avidemux will convert to MPEG1 or MPEG2 but won't author the DVD. You also need to load the MPEG into a DVD Authoring program, then Burn to DVD. You can try one of the following:

DeVEDE
Tovid
KDE DVD Authoring Wizard

There are others also.

As for NTSC/PAL, I don't know your location but in the UK all DVD Players I've used/seen handle both NTSC and PAL. You just need to match the framerates of the file you are converting etc, for example if it's 25 FPS that's PAL.
