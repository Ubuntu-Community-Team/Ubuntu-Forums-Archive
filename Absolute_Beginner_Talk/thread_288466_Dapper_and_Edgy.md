### Post by WiseElben on 2006-10-29
Yes, its possible to dual-boot both. When you get to the partition part of the Edgy install, you will have a choice to use empty unpartitioned space or manually edit your partitions. Of course, if you don't have empty unpartitioned space, then you would have to resize an old partition using gparted, which comes with the Edgy LiveCD
