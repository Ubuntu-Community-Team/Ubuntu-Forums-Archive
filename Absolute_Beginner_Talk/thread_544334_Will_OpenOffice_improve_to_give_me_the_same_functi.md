I have found there is one thing that Open Office doesn't seem to do, at least I haven't found the feature, that Word does do, and that's vertical select.  

So, fans of Open Office, here's a test for you.

Imagine you have a piece of text like this:

aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaabbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbb
aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaabbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbb
aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaabbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbb
aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaabbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbb
aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaabbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbb
aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaabbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbb
aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaabbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbb

Now, with the mouse, select the text area on the left - in other words only the 'a's, on all 7 lines.  Or select the area on the right, only the 'b's, on all 7 lines.

Word does that very easily, by pressing 'alt' as you select with the mouse.
