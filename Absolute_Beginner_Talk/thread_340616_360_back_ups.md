### Post by crispy_420 on 2007-01-18
on most console games there is copy protection at the disc level that can't be copied. most often you will need a mod chip in console to play "backed-up" games. this has been true for the original xbox, ps2, etc.

just do a google search for mod chips for your 360 if they are even made yet. just keep in mind installation will involve taking apart your console and voiding any warranty.
