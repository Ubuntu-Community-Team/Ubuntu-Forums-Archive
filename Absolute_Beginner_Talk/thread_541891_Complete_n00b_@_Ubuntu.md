### Post by pacsum on 2007-09-03
Ignore all those "strange names" for now. It is easier and friendlier to install than any Windows.
Just burn the iso to data cd, and then restart your pc with the disc loaded, (on your pc boot menu choose boot first from cdrom drive), then choose from the disc menu the first option, (install or run ubuntu) this will load ubuntu (the operating system) temporarily on your ram memory. Then choose the option on the desktop that says install. If you have a doubt, you can use mozilla firefox while you are installing the system.
