### Post by pauljwells on 2007-02-02
For 'quick switching' between OSX and Ubuntu you can try mac-on-linux (MOL). There are lots of howtos in the wiki for this, but frankly, you're better off living with rebooting to get the full experience..

For wireless, the airport extreme cards now work using bcmcutter and a firmware download. I got mine working with WEP at least.

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=185174](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=185174)

I actually used an old 11g usb wifi dongle before the bcmcutter stuff worked and it was fine, a Trendnet TEW229UB.
