### Post by HereInOz on 2006-07-17
There have been a few issues with my upgrade from Breezy to Dapper, but nothing over the top.  I had to re-configure the playing of MP3s and things like that, but most of the information was available in the wiki.

Dapper probably works a little better than Breezy overall, but I have to say that, from my point of view, it is an upgrade, rather than a "wow, this is really different".

So if you have spent a lot of time getting your Breezy installation to where you want it, and can't be bothered sorting a few things out after the upgrade, stay where you are until you feel the need.

I tend to upgrade and then sort things out later, but that may not be what you want to do.
