### Post by tbrminsanity on 2007-10-23
There are two options that I know of:

1. Create a new panel and make it transparent (ie the Google Sidebar), then add the panel apps necessary to get everything back you had on your Google Sidebar (I think there is a RSS feed reader in there)

2. Gnome Gadgets:  These are small apps that do thousands of things (report the weather, RSS feed reader, search computer, etc).  It should be under Synaptic.
