### Post by aysiu on 2005-11-30
I agree with Invalid.

I've generally found that people who have one thread with six or seven different questions tend to get fewer responses.

Usually folks get intimidated by very long posts. People may also feel (for some strange reason) that if they can't answer all your questions, they may as well not answer any of them.

Split up your questions. Find a good subject title ("How do I mount my Windows partition?" instead "I need help please") and post it in the appropriate forum. Better yet, search for the answer. This forum is full of good answers.
