First off, I hope you burned the file as an iso image and checked the md5sum before doing that...

Secondly, make your bios to boot from CDrom as first alternative. Then put the CD in and reboot your computer. I'm not quite sure what you have done, but you should not run/start any exe-programs from the cd. Just place the CD in and boot to run Ubuntu.

Thanks for your quick response, which has sorted things out for me - the problem was due to the way in which I had used Nero to burn my CD.  (Don't forget - I did say that I am a newbie!).
