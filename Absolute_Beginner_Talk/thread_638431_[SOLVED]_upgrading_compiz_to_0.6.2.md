### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-12-12
If you're using Gutsy, enable the Gutsy Backports.
System>Administration>Software Sources. Click the Updates tab and check off "Gutsy Backports"
Click close and then Reload the sources when prompted. Then you should get an update notification after a few moments.
