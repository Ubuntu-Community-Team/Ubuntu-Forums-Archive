### Post by medad on 2007-04-23
There should be an icon on your screen next to the clock.  It appears to have two monitors, one right in front of the other.  If you left click on it, it should let you see your options.  You should be able to select your router and go from there.  You will need to do some tweaking if your router has some kind of security activated.  There are some other posts on how to do this, but I don't currently have any to link you with.
