Its listed in WIne db as working perfectly, I guess all you have to do is go to the terminal and type in 

```
wine setup.exe
```

Where setup.exe is replaced by the path to your installer .exe. (for your desktop for instance, it would be /home/username/Desktop/installer, where username is your name you login with, and installer is the folder/installer).

I do think you should seriously consider looking at the GIMP, 4.0 is extremely dated and in all likelyhood (i haven't used 4) lacks many significant features of recent years... GIMP isn't so painful, try it, you may prefer it :)

edit: I concur with oilchangeguy, try gimp first, its native and works well.
