### Post by jvc26 on 2007-01-23
6.06 is LTS (long term support) which means that it will be supported for a period of (if I remember rightly) 3 years. It is more stable than 6.10, and has been recommended as a better way of getting used to ubuntu than going straight to edgy. However, I have found either to be stable for my uses (unless I customise it to a point which breaks it or use alpha software which tends to break regardless ;)) You can upgrade from dapper to edgy, or stay with dapper, its entirely up to you :)
