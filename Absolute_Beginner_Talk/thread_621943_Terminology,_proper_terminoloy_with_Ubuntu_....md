They are called panels. You can have as many as you like, and on the sides of the screen as well if you like. Panels can also have different applets, and you can drag applets between panels if you wish.

The menu (with Applications, Places and System) is the Menu Bar applet, the one that shows you all your open programs is the Window List applet, and the 'system tray' is Notification Area applet.
