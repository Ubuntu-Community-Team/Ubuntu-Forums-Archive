### Post by paydaydaddy on 2008-04-08
Have you enabled all repositories in synaptic? Open synaptic and click on settings. Choose "repositories" from the drop down menu. Tick the boxes for "main", "universe", "restricted", and "multiverse", then close and search for 'wine".
