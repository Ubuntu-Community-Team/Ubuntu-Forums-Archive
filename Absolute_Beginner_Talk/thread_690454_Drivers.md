### Post by emarkd on 2008-02-07
Windows drivers don't work in Linux without a wrapper, but the Live CD won't use anything that's already on your harddrive.

Problems depend on your hardware.  Some manufacturers are Linux-friendly; some aren't  I've never had a problem but some people do.  You may find some information [here]("http://hardware4linux.info/"), but the best thing to do is try the Live CD.
