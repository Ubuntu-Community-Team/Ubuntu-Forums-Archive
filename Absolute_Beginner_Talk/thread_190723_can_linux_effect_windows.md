### Post by Lord Illidan on 2006-06-06
Linux live cds are generally used for repairing Linux partitions. What you can do, though, is use the live cd to mount the windows partition, and recover your valuable data and put it on a usb drive if you can.

But no, it shouldn't affect your harddrive, unless you really knew what you were doing.
