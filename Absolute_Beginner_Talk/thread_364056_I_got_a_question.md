I got a question. In windows there was a "My Documents" folder. Where I kept my documents and stuff. I am not sure, but in Ubuntu, is the Home Folder used for this?

---

### Post by meng on 2007-02-17
A common place to put data is in the home folder, e.g. /home/username, otherwise known as ~
But you can place it in /home/username/Documents, or /home/username/documents (remember Linux is case-sensitive) or wherever you like really! (just as you can customize the default location of saved files in Windows)
