### Post by Najand on 2006-09-17
Gnome is the default.. Average Speed... Having enough graphics for newbiew.
KDE is the slowest... With detail graphics and a lot of tools.
XFCE is the light... Has a reasonable numbers of tools and easy to use...
WindowMaker is lighter than XFCE but difficult for newbies to configure...
Fluxbox is the lightest... Fast... Not many tools...
