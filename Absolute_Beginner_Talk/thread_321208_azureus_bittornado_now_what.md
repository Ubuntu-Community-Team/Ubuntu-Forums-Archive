### Post by benuski on 2006-12-18
What you have to do is find a website that has a ".torrent" file of what you want.  You can open that file from where it is, you don't have to save it to disk, and then it will open your bittorrent program, and ask you where to download the whole thing.  Then it will start downloading itself onto your computer.  [This]("http://netforbeginners.about.com/od/peersharing/a/torrenthandbook_4.htm") website gives another good explination
