### Post by robkeys on 2008-02-20
Just so you know: You pressed CTRL+ALT+F2. This will take you to one of the many terminals that are open alongside Gnome as it is running (the same command with F3, F4, etc will take you to the others). You may want to stay away from this keystroke at first, however, as getting back can be problematic.
