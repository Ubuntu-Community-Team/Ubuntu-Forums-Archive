### Post by johnny4north on 2007-03-31
in ubuntu 7.04 you would click;panel,system,administration, then Removable drives and media preferences.  click multimedia, then go down to the video discs area.  edit command: VLC [your cdrom or dvd]dvd://  you might have to figure out where your optical drive is. most of the time its dvd://    read the how-to areas and do searches for "default vlc" in the fourms and read others answers.  thanks ubuntu

### Post by locke.dragon on 2007-04-06
since i was working with a fresh ubuntu installation, i really only had to remove totem movie player.  but if you have more that you installed later on, browse over to a file you'd like to play with vlc and right click on the file.  uninstall whatever program it says next to "open with..." and keep doing this until it says "open with vlc".  that's all i did and it works perfectly.  just keep in mind that beryl and vlc don't mix well.  :P
