### Post by msak007 on 2006-08-17
One thing you can do in your User Control Panel is to enable the option that automatically subscribes you to any thread you post in (including ones you start) without email notification by default. This way, any thread you post in will show up in your User CP any time there is a new post. It makes it easier to find them.

Also, an alternate way to do what kpkeerthi suggested is by clicking on "Advanced Search" in the bar across the top - there are 2 options at the very bottom of the list titled "Find All Your Threads" (for threads you started) and "Find All Your Posts" (for any thread you've posted in).
