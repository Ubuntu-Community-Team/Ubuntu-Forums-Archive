### Post by costre on 2008-04-04
Updated:

I got it working. Flash now works perfectly in Hardy Heron.

**Step one**: Uninstall firefox 3 beta 4, ia32-libs, and flashplugin-nonfree + nspluginwrapper.

**Step two**: Install Firefox 2 through Synaptic

**Step three**: Download and install ia32-libs_2.1ubuntu3_amd64.deb

**Step four**: Install flashplugin-nonfree + nspluginwrapper through Synaptic

**Step five**: Go to youtube, google video or jokeroo and watch your brains out :)
