### Post by Bachstelze on 2007-05-26
There is no CD labeled Gnome because the "standard" CD1 installs Gnome. And you guessed right, all the CDs are just additional packages, useful if you have no Net connection but if you have one, you can just download the CD1.

The businesscard CD is an even more minimalistic CD then the netinst, it is designed to fit on a business card-shaped CD, thus it's name.


By the way, if you're just trying it in vmware, you don't need to burn anything, just use the ISOs directly ;)
