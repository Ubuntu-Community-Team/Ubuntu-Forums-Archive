### Post by tc101 on 2007-01-11
I figured out how to make the change I wanted.  

I went to System>Preferences>Keyboard

I selected the Layout Options tab

I selected Miscellaneous Compatabiity Options

I checked "Shift with numpad works as in MS windows"
