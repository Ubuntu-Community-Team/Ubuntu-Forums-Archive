### Post by ArieT on 2007-03-27
You can't play mp3 by default. the mp3 format isn't free. See

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/MP3](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/MP3)

and

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)

for making it work. It's pretty easy.
