As Aysiu said, 5.10 is completely unsupported and it's repositories have been gone for a while. Beryl wasn't written until about the 6.06 was released and wasn't in common use before 7.04. You really should upgrade to at least 6.06 as it's still supported (and will be through 2009), but I would really really highly recommend upgrading to the current version (7.10).
