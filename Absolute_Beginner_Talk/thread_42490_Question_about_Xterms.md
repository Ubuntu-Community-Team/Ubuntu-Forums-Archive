### Post by intangible on 2005-06-18
You can do transparent terminals with gnome-terminal (which is installed by default with Ubuntu).
For gnome-terminal:
Right-Click on background in terminal, **Edit Current Profile->Effects** Enable Transparent Background, 50% opacity seems good.  I also changed the color scheme to white on black.
