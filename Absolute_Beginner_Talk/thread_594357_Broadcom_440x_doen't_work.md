### Post by Alex Sotelo on 2007-10-28
Hi Will.

After installing Ubuntu Gutsy in my Dell inspiron 1521, the “Restricted Dirvers Manager” automatically installed the Broadcom 43xx firmware. After that, the wireless was recognized, but I still could'nt connect to the WiFi, so I installed “WiFi Radar” with “Synaptic Package Manager”. I solved my problem connecting to wifi via “wifi radar”.
