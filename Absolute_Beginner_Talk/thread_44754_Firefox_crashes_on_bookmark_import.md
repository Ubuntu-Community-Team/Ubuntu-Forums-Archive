### Post by aysiu on 2005-06-27
The cleanest way to reinstall Firefox is to back up your bookmarks and make a note of what extensions you use, then completely remove your settings (/home/username/.mozilla) and uninstall the program. Then, reinstall it.

If you're having trouble with the apt-get version of Firefox, you may also want to try Firefox straight from Mozilla's website.
