### Post by heimo on 2007-05-17
> **AlecWest said:**
> Any hints on how I can keep the smaller display print ... or at least get down to the point where I can "Continue" on to step 2?

Try changing resolution using ctrl+alt+numpad plus or minus. Or if that fails, keep left alt key down while dragging installation program windows up and down as needed to access buttons - with alt down, you can drag window from any point, not just title bar.
