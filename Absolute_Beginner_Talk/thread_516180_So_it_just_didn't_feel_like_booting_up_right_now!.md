### Post by Yookji on 2007-08-02
I use wubi on my desktop downstairs and the same thing happened after the power went out while running Ubuntu. Because of the nature of wubi, doing a hard reboot can damage the system. I ended up just reinstalling Ubuntu since I didn't have anything very valuable on the partition. Downloading the latest wubi installer might help in the future. Since you just installed this morning you probably don't have anything valuable; you'll have to ask in the wubi forums if you want to recover something from XP.

If I were you, I'd just boot XP, uninstall (saving downloaded files), and reinstall wubi.
