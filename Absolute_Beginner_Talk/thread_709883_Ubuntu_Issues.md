### Post by Origin415 on 2008-02-27
Dont worry too much about the graphics issue, after you install you will have to do some manual setup, but thats about it. The sut of range error normally means that the frequency is set too high for your monitor, and thats easily changable in a real installation.

For your wireless card, the DWL-122 is based on the RT73 chipset, which is supported under linux -- and Ubuntu with a bit of hacking. There is an indepth guide here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=400236](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=400236)

So try installing Ubuntu when you have some free time and access to a wired network.

Good luck.
