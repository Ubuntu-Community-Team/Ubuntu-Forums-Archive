### Post by Bartender on 2007-06-03
Here is the U.S. the method most likely to result in success is to buy a used USR Sportster external serial modem.  Then you have to find your way thru the obstacles put up by Ubuntu - creating the /etc/resolv file, fumbling with wvdial or somehow downloading then installing GnomePPP.  Then you need a cooperative ISP that won't cut you off after a coupla hours.

I've read that full hardware external modems are insanely expensive in other countries.  Is that your situation?
