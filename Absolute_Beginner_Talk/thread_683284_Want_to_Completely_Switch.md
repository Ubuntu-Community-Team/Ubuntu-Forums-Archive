### Post by emarkd on 2008-01-30
You won't be able to play 3D games in VMWare.  Remember that virtualization software virtualizes your hardware.  In other words, Windows in VMWare doesn't really "see" your high-dollar video hardware, so it just runs like it's on a generic video card.  Many current games use Microsoft's DirectX to render, so they're tied to Windows.  Anything else runs great in VMWare. 

 iTunes also doesn't work but there are other softwares that work with your iPods and such.  Other than that, burning CD's HD video, etc all works fine.
