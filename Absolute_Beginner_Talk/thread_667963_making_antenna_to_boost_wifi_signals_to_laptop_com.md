### Post by crjackson on 2008-01-14
You should have gotten a driver disk with the USB Dongle your purchased.  If ubunt has no built in drivers for your device, you can install ndiswrapper and lode the windows driver from the supplided disk.  If you got no disk at all, then yes, you need to download the drivers for that WiFi device.

You shouldn't need to disable your internal card, it's just that you can only connect to a signal with one at at time.  Once properly installed, you can go to your network manager and select which card you wish to connect with.  Just look to see which one shows the strongest signals and use that one.
