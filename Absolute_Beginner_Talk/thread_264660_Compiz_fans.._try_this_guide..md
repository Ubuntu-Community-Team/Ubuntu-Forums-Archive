### Post by kpkeerthi on 2006-09-25
Are you a compiz fan and love its cool animations? Try this guide and make your animations perfect. 

Follow:
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=1398328&postcount=1](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=1398328&postcount=1)

for: 
1. Elimating 'tearing' in fullscreen DVD playback (in totem).
2. Elimating 'tearing' in cube rotation and wobbly.
3. Smoother edges of cube.
4. Smoother edges of dragged windows (wobbly).
5. Smoother animations (minimize, unminize, wobbly and all).
 
I wrote this guide to fix some DVD playback issues I have been having. I have had compiz removed for quite sometime due to some dependency issues I had. But after upgrading compiz (a **huge** improvement) to the latest version and (re-)applying the tweaks from the guide I found big visual improvements in every aspect. 

**NOTE:** The guide uses tweaks like full-scene antialiasing/anisotropic filtering/vsync which could possibly impact performance if your graphics card is not fairly latest.
