### Post by ORBiTrus on 2007-09-08
bugs.ubuntu.com

They'll forward it upstream if it's valid.  However, as you are using Compiz fusion, which is not yet part of the official Ubuntu, there may be other people who have reported similar bugs.  So make sure you search first.

And narrowing it down would help - is it related to flash, video, certain compiz actions, what?  They'll also want to know what video card you have, I suspect, and what drivers you are using.
