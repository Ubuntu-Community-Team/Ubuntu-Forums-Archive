### Post by anaconda on 2006-06-19
Well You want the whole CD-image to the USB-stick, so it should be bigger than 700MB 

If you want to install DSL, then you only need 64MB USB-stick  (DSL is Damn Small and FAST)  And come to think about it.. Your 64MB of ram isn't wery much.. Maybe you want DSL or puppy-linux? they would be fast with your laptop..

Yep, And I still dont know how to install ubuntu from USB (knoppix can be installed)

Go to your PC:s Bios.. If there is a setting for boot from USB-HDD or Boot From USB-ZIP then your computer (propably) can boot from USB. (Some older computers claim that they can boot from USB, but they cant..)

Do you still have a working floppy-drive in your laptop? If you have, then you can boot from boot-floppy, which boots from your USB.. That is what I had to do.
