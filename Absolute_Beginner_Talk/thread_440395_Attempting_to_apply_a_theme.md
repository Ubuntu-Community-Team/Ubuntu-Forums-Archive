### Post by ItaniKnight on 2007-05-15
I think I see what you're getting at here. When you say panels, I'm assuming you're talking about the ones along the top and bottom of the screen (by default, anyway). If so, they're not too hard to fiddle with (just right-click and select "properties" or "customise panel") and you're fine there. So, theoretically, you could delete the top panel and fix the one on the bottom edge to do everything if you like windows, or keep the top one if you're more familiar with Macs.

The desktop works like most other systems, and a quick look in the settings should find you the "desktop settings", where you can change your desktop to pretty much any image you like.

But note that I'm using XFCE at the moment, though I do know enough of GNOME to know what I'm on about ;)
