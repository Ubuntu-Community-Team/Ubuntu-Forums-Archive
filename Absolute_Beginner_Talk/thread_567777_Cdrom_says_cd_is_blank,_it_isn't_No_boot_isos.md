### Post by Trance Gemini on 2007-10-05
Booting from bootable cd cannot be affected by the previous installations of any OS, so first I recomed you to check bios setting regarding booting sequence - be sure that the first boot drive is set to CD.
Also, if this happens on the notebook, they usualy put that kind of bios, that prints you something like that when you turn on your pc: "press F12 (for ex.) to enter boot menu" nad there you chan choose what to boot from.

So, none of ubuntu or mint are the black spots in your case. Be sure that you burned bootable cd correctly, check the booting sequence, and off you go! :)
