I'm sorry to hear of your troubles - it sounds like it was a difficult situation with several opportunities for exclaiming, "What the....?"

Short of doing a fresh install, there is an option on the "alternate" CD to help "fix a broken system" - at [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download), just below the "Start download" button there's a checkbox for "Get alternate CD"
