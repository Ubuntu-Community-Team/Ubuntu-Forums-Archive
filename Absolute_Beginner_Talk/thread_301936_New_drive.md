### Post by taurus on 2006-11-17
The first drive in your computer with XP and Ubuntu need to set to master and your new drive need to set to slave.  You can do the settings on the back of your drives with the small plastic cover jumpers.  There should be a picture on top of each drive showing you how to set the master, slave, or single.  Otherwise, look in the manual that comes with each drive.  And if there is still no info about the settings, go to manufacturer's site and look it up.

Remember, the master drive should be at the end of the ribbon cable while the slave should use the connection in the middle of the cable.
