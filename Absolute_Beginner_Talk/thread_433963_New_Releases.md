### Post by silkstone on 2007-05-05
You can't upgrade from Dapper direct to Feisty - you need to go Dapper > Edgy > Feisty. Better still, save all your data and do a clean install.

I'd be a bit cautious about upgrading just now. The forums are full of people with Feisty problems, including quite a few for whom Dapper or Edgy worked fine. Maybe wait a while until there's a kernel update or something?
