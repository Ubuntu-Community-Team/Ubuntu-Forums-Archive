### Post by 23meg on 2007-01-23
If you're likely to upgrade to Feisty once it's out, and keep upgrading to the latest stable releases whenever they're out, Dapper's long term support doesn't mean anything to you. If you'd like to pick one version and stay with it for years, let that be Dapper. Otherwise, if Edgy is working for you, stay with it, especially if you'll keep upgrading.
