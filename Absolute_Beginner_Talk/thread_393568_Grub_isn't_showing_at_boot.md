### Post by xXx 0wn3d xXx on 2007-03-25
Try rapidly pressing Esc after your Bios screen. With luck, your grub config is just corrupted/damaged and you might need to change the timeout. If this does not work, you may need to re-install grub/fix your MBR (master boot record).
