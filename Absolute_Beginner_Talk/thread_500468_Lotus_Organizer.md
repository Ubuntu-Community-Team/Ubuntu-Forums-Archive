### Post by mdsmedia on 2007-07-16
Without any knowledge its ability to import from Organizer or anything else, I had been looking for something similar to Outlook from a PIM point of view.

I came across Kontact which, although it's not perfect, does a great job of organizing my life. It also has a Journal module for notes etc, a news reader, email, calendar, task list, and maybe other functions too. I don't use the email module.

Evolution also has a PIM which I might have used had I wanted its email client, but I found it a little buggy and the email client didn't work the way I was used to in Thunderbird.

In Kontact you can use whichever modules you want and discard the ones you don't want through the menus.

By the way, although it is a KDE package, I use it in Gnome without problem.
