### Post by Globulator on 2007-04-29
I too had the Adept programs grayed (greyed) out.
I'd installed Kubuntu from the live CD just before - so my first boot into the new system.

I simply could not get adept to accept I wanted to do anything, the ADEPT tab just have the Exit option in it - no way to specify repositories.

The cure?

It's on a laptop (HP nx9020) so I changed the default lid-closing action to 'suspend' (not hibernate) - just as that's what my powerbook does.

On closing the lib the system when to sleep. On opening again it all woke up again. I started up Adept again to look at the grayed out items again - and they were all selectable!!

Weird but true - I don't know how, but if you have Adept problems (and there was a similar thing with being unable to look at Notification properties), click on the battery/charging icon, click on 'Suspend' option when the lid is closed, close Adept, Close the lib, wait a bit, open the lid, then run Adept again. In my case, the items were selectable again.
