### Post by Zeroangel on 2006-04-20
What are your issues, basically? Is it the speed that's bothering you? If so you should know that dapper drake uses a faster version of nautilus (and other gnome components). And, if that's too slow for you you can always switch over to XFCE (like gnome/kde only faster and lighter with some features stripped down).

Upgrading to dapper drake has a small chance of breaking your installation, or breaking somewhere along the way as it's still in testing for its june release.
