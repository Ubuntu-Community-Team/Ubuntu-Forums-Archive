### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-12-04
From what you are saying I think you burned the CD too fast.

Keep this in mind. **The ISO has to be burned at a speed of 4X or lower otherwise the Live CD will get corrupted causing the problem you are having now.**

EDIT:- But before we think about the CD, lets just make sure that the BIOS is setup properly.

What kind of PC do you have? Usually the BIOS can be brought up by pressing Del or F2 just after the PC starts booting.
