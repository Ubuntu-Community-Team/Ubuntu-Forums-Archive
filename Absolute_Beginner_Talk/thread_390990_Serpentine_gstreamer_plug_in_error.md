### Post by Muzik83 on 2007-03-23
Hey

Seeing as nobody has replied in 10 hours, this is partially a bump up higher, and partially a "try this".

I really like K3B for burning discs... its a KDE application, but if you dont mind installing a bunch of dependencies (ie you have a lot of disk space for K3b) then I recommend k3b.

In K3b if you want to put mp3s on a cd to play in a normal cd player, you will also need the package libk3b2-mp3
