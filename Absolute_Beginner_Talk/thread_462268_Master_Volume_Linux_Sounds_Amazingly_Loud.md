### Post by Doc.Caliban on 2007-06-02
I'm on a laptop with a subwoofer and ubuntu boots up with that thing roaring and no sound at all coming out of the regular speakers.  What I had to do was open the volume control and add the "master mono" slider via Edit/Preferences and adjust that along with the master volume to get the subwoofer to turn down.

Something for you to look at anyway.
