### Post by Sef on 2006-11-10
> now i have a printer canon pixma iP1600

[LinuxPrinting]("http://linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-PIXMA_iP1600") says that your printer is a paperweight.  It won't work.  If you want to get a new printer that will work, read [LinuxPrinting's Suggested Printers]("http://linuxprinting.org/suggested.html").  Generally HP and Epson work best with Linux.
