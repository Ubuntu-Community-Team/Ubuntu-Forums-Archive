### Post by Hagar Delest on 2008-02-12
Go to the Gnome menu System>Preferences and change the fonts used for the applications. OOo (Ubuntu version) has some problem with that setting sometimes.

You can also try to remove the openoffice.org-gtk package IIRC (not really sure about that one).

### Post by jordanmthomas on 2008-02-12
Also a way to fix the fonts in OpenOffice is this:
Go to Tools -- Options --> fonts
and use font replacement to replace "Andale Sans UI" with a font of your choice.

I do this to fix the menus sometimes and it sometimes works.
