### Post by rune0077 on 2008-04-20
I think that's not possible, since your keyboard will be bound to VirtualBox rather than to your normal Ubuntu-desktop. The only obvious way, is to run VirtualBox in a maximized window, rather than as full screen, that way you can "leave" the window and spin the cube when you need to (or just change viewports from the panel).

There may be some smart way to accomplish what you're looking for, but off the top of my head I can't come up with one.

EDIT: actually, you may have luck just pressing the right Ctrl button, then spin the cube with Ctrl+Alt+left or right key (the right ctrl button should "unlock" the keyboard from VirtualBox).
