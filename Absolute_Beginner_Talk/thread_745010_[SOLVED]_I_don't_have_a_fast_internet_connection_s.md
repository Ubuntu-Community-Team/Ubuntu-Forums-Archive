### Post by talsemgeest on 2008-04-04
Go to /var/cache/apt/ and burn everything in it to your cd (like Places>Burn cd/dvd).

When you want to restore, just copy the files back, update your repos and it will install the updates from your computer instead.

Hope this helps :)
