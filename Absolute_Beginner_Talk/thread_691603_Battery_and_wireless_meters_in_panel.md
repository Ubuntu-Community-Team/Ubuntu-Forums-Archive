### Post by Presto123 on 2008-02-08
Hey there. I was smashing my head with this a few weeks ago and finally realized how to fix it. LOL

Right click on the panel you want it back on and click on "Add to Panel" from that drop-down box. When that applet comes up, select "Notification Area".

### Post by bvmou on 2008-02-08
You can get the network manager back with the command 'nm-applet'; I have also had weird misses with that applet.  The battery is available through the "add to panel' dialogue after right-clicking where you want the icon.  If you want to totally refresh the desktop you can execute "sudo /etc/init.d/gdm restart' (to restart gnome.)
