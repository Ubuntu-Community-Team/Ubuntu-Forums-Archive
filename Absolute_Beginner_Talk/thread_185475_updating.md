### Post by macdo on 2006-05-31
Updating shouldn't normally change anything on your system - it won't uninstall programs, codecs or libraries that are in use, and doesn't change any preexisting config files.

If you're upgrading to a whole new release, however, that's a bit different. I suggest that you back up your home directory and your etc directory, so that you're sure that you keep all your config files.
