### Post by Squizz on 2007-12-15
[http://www.speedtest.net](http://www.speedtest.net) will determine your actual speed.

Try and take into consideration what time it is - is it peak time?  That could be making it slow.

Take regular readings with speedtest.net, and then send the data to your ISP complaining about it if it's not acceptable.  They're less likely to fob you off if you're keeping track.
