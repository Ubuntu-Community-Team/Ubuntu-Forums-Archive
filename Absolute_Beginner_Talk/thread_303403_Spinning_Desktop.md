### Post by michaeltrainor on 2006-11-21
I've gotten the 3D desktop to work.

Search the forums for XGL Beryl.  There is NO easy way to click and install this feature.  Top on your list should be configuring xorg.conf correctly for your card.  The hardest part is installing the drivers for your video card.

**HOWTO: Beryl with latest nvidia drivers and EDGY's built in AIGLX (no XGL)**
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263851&highlight=beryl]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263851&highlight=beryl")

I have an ATI card and used this fairly simple tutorial to complete my setup.
[http://lhansen.blogspot.com/2006/10/beryl-and-xgl-on-ubuntu-linux-with-ati.html]("http://lhansen.blogspot.com/2006/10/beryl-and-xgl-on-ubuntu-linux-with-ati.html")

As always...BACKUP everything before you commit changes.

I started as a complete noob and have now completely switched to Ubuntu both server and client.  I'm a 3D Animator using Maya and Blender.  I have never been satisifed with the performance in window$.  Ubuntu CAN replace absolutely everything on your windows box.  You just need to be patient and keep asking questions.
