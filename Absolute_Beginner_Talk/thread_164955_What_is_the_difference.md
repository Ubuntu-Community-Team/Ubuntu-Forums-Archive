### Post by Sef on 2006-04-23
> WARTY__HOARY__BREEZY__DAPPER

All are ubuntu? Then what is the difference between these and why should one chose one over the rest?

They are all different versions of Ubuntu.  Warty is 4.10; Hoary is 5.04; Breezy is 5.10; Dapper is 6.06.    Breezy is the latest stable version; Dapper is beta, i.e. testing.  It is scheduled to become the next stable version on 1 June.  

It would be best to use Breezy until Dapper comes out.  Then you can decide to upgrade to Dapper or not.

### Post by catlett on 2006-04-23
Linux/Ubuntu are not like windows where it is years before a new version comes out. Linux distros schedule regular upgrades of their OS. That list is a list of the last two releases, the current release and the next release that is in testing. Warty and Hoary are the two older versions. Breezy is the current version and Dapper is the next version that is in testing now. 
The distro will upgrade and the Linux kernel will also release new versions of the kernel.This is one of the finer points of Linux. You continually get the newest version for your system.
