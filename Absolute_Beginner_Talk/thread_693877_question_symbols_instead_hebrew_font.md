### Post by ubuntu27 on 2008-02-11
Go to

"System menu"-->"Administration"-->"Language Support". Mark "Hebrew" as your desired language, and choose to "Apply" the changes. 
This will install necessary locale data as well as do any configuration needed to put the new locale into effect. After you finish it's recommended you log out and re log in to GNOME to have the new locale settings take effect.

Let us know if it works. There might be some other things to do.
You might also want to read this wiki on "[How to have Hebrew localization"]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HebrewLocalizationHowto")

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HebrewLocalizationHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HebrewLocalizationHowto)
