### Post by Brightbelt on 2007-06-15
Hi,
First you must have your accelerated graphics card driver installed. Then check the System Menu/Preferences/Desktop Effects and enable them.

You may also need to install Beryl but I'm not familiar with that part as much. 

As for your graphics driver, check the System Menu/Administration/Restricted Drivers manager. That will tell you whether you have an accelerated driver that has not yet been installed. 

Good Luck, Frank B.
