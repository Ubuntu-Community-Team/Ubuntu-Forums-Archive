### Post by Rhubarb on 2007-01-03
A 700MHz AMD chip is NOT 64 bit.  It's a standard 32 bit chip.  Download the regular i386 version.

> secondly we want to dual boot so to do that you choose " resize partion and install in freed space" then grub will take over booting yes?
Yes, that's it exactly.  The installer will let you choose how much you want to shrink the existing windows partition down by.  It defaults to around 50% to 60%
