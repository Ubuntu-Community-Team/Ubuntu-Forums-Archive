### Post by kpkeerthi on 2007-12-24
gparted should do the job. I suggest you use the [gparted live cd]("http://gparted-livecd.tuxfamily.org/") as it is more up-to-date than the version shipped with Ubuntu. 

Make sure you defragment (atleast twice) your windows partition before you shrink it to minimize chances of data loss. And always.. backup backup backup. Good luck.
