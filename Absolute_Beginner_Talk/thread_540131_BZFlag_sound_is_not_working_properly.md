Hi, 

The sound on my Ubuntu machine seems to be working properly. However, the sounds in the game BZFlag are not playing properly, it looks like they're playing too fast. Does anyone have a similar problem? 

I noticed that under the 'Audio' settings the 'Driver' and 'Device' entries are empty. 

Does anyone know how to fix my problem?

Regards, BigPilot

---

### Post by bigpilot on 2007-09-01
Solved it. Changed the setting 'Device' to 'hw:0' and 'Driver' to 'Alsa' and now it works perfectly. You need to restart the program for it to work.
