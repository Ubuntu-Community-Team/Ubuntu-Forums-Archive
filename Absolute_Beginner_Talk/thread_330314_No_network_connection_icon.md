### Post by oilchangeguy on 2007-01-02
go to system, admin. networking. you should see your nic-etho1, wireless if avail-wlano, and maybe a modem. highlight your desired network connection, click properties, and make sure the box is checked to enable this connection. then from the icon near the clock select the connection from the dropdown menu.
