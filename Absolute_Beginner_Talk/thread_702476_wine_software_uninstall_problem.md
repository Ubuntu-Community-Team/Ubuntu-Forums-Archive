### Post by kevindubrow on 2008-02-20
You could try to go to Wine's virtual C drive and delete that program's system files if you no longer need them. It may get rid of the menus.

You get to that by clicking on Applications->Wine->Browse C: drive (just in case you didn't know)

Good luck!
