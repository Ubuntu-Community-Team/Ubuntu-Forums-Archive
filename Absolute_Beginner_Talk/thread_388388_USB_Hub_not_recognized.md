### Post by groeswenphil on 2007-03-19
I've gone through three USB hubs over the last few years.

When they start playing up, you get all sorts of strange things happening.

Just check whether you can get one USB device recognised with a direct connection.
That way you can make sure that it isn't a faulty hub.
