### Post by paulozzzz on 2007-02-25
I finally have my graphics card working.

Steps:

1) Upgraded to Ubuntu 6.06.
2) Installed the fglrx driver.
3) ran **sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg**, disabling kernel framebuffer (graphics card directly under the control of the X server).
4) reboot.
