### Post by hamburglar6 on 2007-08-02
do you get any error messages ever?  also, does the sound work only in specific situations (like you hear sound at startup but not if you try to play a song) or does it cut in and out (like you were playing a song and the music would periodically come on and off)?  if it is the former, make sure you have all of the correct plugins installed.  to do this, on your desktop go to applications > add/remove.  in the upper right of the add/remove applications window, choose 'all available applications'.  then, in the 'other' section, choose the 'ubuntu restricted extras' and 'macromedia flash player' packages.  in the 'sound & video' section choose all 4 gstreamer plugins, and the 'xine extra plugins'.  if your problem is the latter, you probably have some loose/faulty cables or connections and some jiggling is on the menu.
