### Post by baldy1324 on 2006-09-16

``` then scroll down to the part where it has the resolution. on the line with 24, add the resolution your monitor has in front of the others.

### Post by DavidW2 on 2006-09-16
FWIW.  A couple of things I had to do to make my resolution 1280x854. I have a NVIDIA gpu in my laptop but the standard install notes only got me to 1280x800.  I got it up to the right resolution by using the [Modeline Generator]("http://xtiming.sourceforge.net/cgi-bin/xtiming.pl").  The results of this are put in the Xorg.conf file at the end of the Monitor section.  Also after using the Generator I found out my HorizSync values were slightly off.  In my notebook manual the rating is 29-53 but the Generator said they should be 29-54.  So I change the 53 to 54 and voila!  Some things to try/think about.
