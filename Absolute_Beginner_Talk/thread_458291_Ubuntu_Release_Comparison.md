### Post by mikewhatever on 2008-01-23
You can find the comparison of most common packages here (middle of page) [http://distrowatch.com/table.php?distribution=ubuntu](http://distrowatch.com/table.php?distribution=ubuntu).
The newest features in Gutsy and Feisty are directed to user-friendliness, and they are: better hw detection (wireless drivers are preinstalled and nvidia/ati ones offered after installation) and codecs' installation is semi automatic. Compiz-fusion hit the desktop in Gutsy and the rest of software is of newer version.
