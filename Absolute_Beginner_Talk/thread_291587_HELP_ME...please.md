### Post by mattskr on 2006-11-02
Your PC should boot to the CD. Your BIOS is probably booting to the hard disk first, thus bypassing the CD. You need to go into your BIOS (Usually press DEL when first booting) and find the boot order. Just move CD-ROM above the hard disks. If there is no bootable CD-ROM in the drive it will boot Windows like normal.
