### Post by mivo on 2008-02-25
Do you have a separate home partition?

It's generally better to just backup your files and do a complete re-install, unless you have a separate home partition, though even then I would delete the Gnome configuration files. I've had some trouble with importing settings (application settings usually do work just fine).
