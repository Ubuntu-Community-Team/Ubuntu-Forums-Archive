### Post by TeaSwigger on 2007-11-02
Hello, Welcome and congrats on your great experience. All that beauty and brains too - oh and incidentally it asks nothing from you. Tho' of course you're encouraged to "give back" to the linux community in whatever way you might be able to help. :)

Yes there are a number of splendid setups out there. ubuntu may be the easiest. Puppy Linux may be one of the lightest, fastest and most "portable." Perhaps a more technically inclined one of remarkable quality is Gentoo. Since Gentoo oft focuses around KDE as desktop environment, if you want to transition to it gradually you could always try Kubuntu, which is ubuntu focused around KDE instead of Gnome. Another ubuntu-based version you can try is the recently unveiled Fluxbuntu, featuring the lighter & faster but also lovely text-file configuration based Fluxbox on the "desktop." There are many projects for you to try and enjoy. 

Oh and have fun exploring the myriad apps in the ubuntu repositories too. :)
