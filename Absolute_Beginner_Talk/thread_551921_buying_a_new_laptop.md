### Post by kebes on 2007-09-15
If you want a system pre-loaded with Ubuntu (and full hardware support), check out [System 76]("http://www.system76.com/"). (There is also a [support category]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=158") for System 76 that is monitored by employees from that company. You can ask any questions you have about their systems there.

Other options for pre-loaded Linux (therefore good Linux hardware support) are the pre-loaded Dells (already mentioned) and [Emperor Linux]("http://www.emperorlinux.com/").

Hope that helps.
