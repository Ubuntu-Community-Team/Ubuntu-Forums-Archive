I'm not sure which small selection box you mean but you can add Opera to your
Applications>Internet menu by using the Menu Editor

Under Applications go to System Tools>Applications and open Menu Editor and highlight
Internet from the left pane. Your current menu items will appear there. 

Click New Entry and in the box that pops up Enter 'opera' under name and command. To get your opera icon click on the icon box and navigate to
usr/X11R6/include/X11/bitmaps. select opera.xpm from the list and it should
appear in your Applications>Internet Menu.
