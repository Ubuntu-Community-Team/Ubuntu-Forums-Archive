### Post by matrooswolf on 2006-06-24
Newsreaders read articles from Usenet (or newsgroups).  You have to connect to a news server (maybe your ISP has one for free, mine has), or you can subsribe to one (newshosting e.g.).

Nowdays people use newsreaders also to share and download (legal and illegal) movies, music, etc ...
