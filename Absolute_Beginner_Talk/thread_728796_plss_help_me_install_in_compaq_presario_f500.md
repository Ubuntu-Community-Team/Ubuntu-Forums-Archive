### Post by Sef on 2008-03-21
1) Did you [md5sum]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM") the download?  (The check makes sure your download was good.)

2) Did you burn the iso at 4x or less? (Faster may cause a bad burn.)

3) Did you check the disc for errors? (Instead of start/Install, go down the list to 'Check Disk for errors' or something similar.)
