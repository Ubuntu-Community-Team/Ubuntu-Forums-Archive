### Post by that guy on 2007-03-10
Hmmm, personally I would get a copy of Edgy 6.10 which you can find here:

[http://www.ubuntu.com/products/GetUbuntu/download#currentrelease](http://www.ubuntu.com/products/GetUbuntu/download#currentrelease)

And try installing that.  If you have an older model, there is usually pretty good support out of the box for those.  I have an install on a 6 year old Dell laptop that worked perfectly out of the box; video wireless and all.

Also, when it comes to typing stuff in the terminal go to:

Applications>Accessories>Terminal

That will get you a terminal window.  When you are doing critical stuff it will require you to use 'sudo' before the command and then enter your password.  This is Ubuntu's way of preventing you from doing, or at least making you think twice, about doing anything that might screw up your system.  Its a pretty foreign concept for Windows users.
