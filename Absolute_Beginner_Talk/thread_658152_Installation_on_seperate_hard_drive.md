### Post by PPAAUULL on 2008-01-04
Well I would say that you should buy a new Harddrive and install your Ubuntu on that then you can use the old one with your info on it as a secondary and you can pull you info off it as you please. I would say that that is your best bet.

The plus to buying a new Harddrive is that you will always have your old windows stuff backed up on the old hard drive and you can pull it off when ever you needed it.
