### Post by tgunner on 2006-10-17
Yes, that sounds like it could be the issue, but another thing to consider: does the video card share the system memory? If it is a laptop, the answer is almost certainly yes. If so, you are starting out with less then 256, and the LiveCD won't work. If this is the case, you could download the alternate installer version, which uses the old text based installation, which will work with 64mb of RAM I believe. Did you format the CD-RW's first?
