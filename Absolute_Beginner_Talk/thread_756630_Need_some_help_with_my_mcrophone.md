### Post by PhilCash on 2008-04-16
A bit of a coincidence here. I was having the same problem and found your help request in my search for an answer. I've just resolved my problem by trial and error and offer the following suggestions:
Double click on the volume control to access the full volume control mixer settings.
Use Edit/Preferences to ensure that all of the settings are ticked to make them visible.
In the Playback tab, ensure the microphone is not muted and set the volume at maximum.
In the Record tab, set the volume to maximum.
In the Switches tab, ensure that Microphone Capture, Microphone Boost, Stereo Microphone, and V_REFOUT Enable are all ticked.
I have no idea what V_REFOUT Enable means, but this was the box that when ticked enabled my Joybook 7000's microphone.
Also check the Options tab for the Mic setting, you may need to try Mic1 or Mic2...

Cheers, and good luck,
Phil
