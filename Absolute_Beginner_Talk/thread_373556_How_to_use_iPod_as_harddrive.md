### Post by ohzopants on 2007-03-02
When you plug your ipod in, Nautilus should open automatically to your ipod.  From there it should be a simple matter or dragging the files you want onto the ipod.

By default my ipod is mounted to /media/ipod, so if nautilus doesn't open automagically you could try browsing to it.  Also, in my experience with transferring files with an ipod, directories don't seem to matter; I have a directory on my ipod that I cleverly called "Stuff" and I just throw files in there without problems.

Good luck
