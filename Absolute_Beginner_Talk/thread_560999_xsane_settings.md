### Post by gdi2k on 2008-06-28
Yes, the xsane UI was designed by sadists. I found this thread looking for the same answer, but didn't find it. For those that come looking after me, here it is: 

In xsane, choose Window, and enable "Show Preview" if it isn't already enabled. 

On the preview window, below the image display area, you can choose paper size from a drop down (A4 = DIN A4 port), US users will want legal or letter. 

Press "Acquire Preview" to see if you've got what you want, then press "Scan" (in the main window) to actually do the scan. 

Hopefully [Gnome Scan]("http://www.gnome.org/projects/gnome-scan/index") will provide a better interface in the future.
