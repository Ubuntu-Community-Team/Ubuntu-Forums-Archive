### Post by dmizer on 2006-07-14
first of all, a short lesson:

gaim is not a messenger, it is a program which can connect to messenger services (like yahoo, msn, icq ...)

so, if you are choosing which service you want to use, take a look at which client most of your friends are using.  once you have chosen your service, then you can choose which program to connect to that service.

gaim (among others mentioned above) can connect to multiple services including yahoo, aim, msn, icq, jabber, irc.  as far as i'm concerned, it's the most convenient because it comes pre loaded on your system and it takes almost no effort to configure.
