64bit Ubuntu is nothing like 64bit Windows. There is no difference in available packages according to launchpad (the ubuntu development site). Gaming is the same on the 32bit as on the 64bit in my experience. Most games are still windows based on both Ubuntu versions and running games involves wine (free but hard to setup games)  or cedega(costs $15 , but its easy to setup games).
I would second the waiting, its only 2 more days and you will have less chance of dealing with any development problems.
