### Post by vladan.b on 2007-11-10
I don't think you need drivers for ADSL modem, it should work just like it did before, so as long as it still has your login/pw in it, you should be getting an IP for your Ubuntu.

As for Add/Remove... I never ran a dual boot, and most of the time I used apt-get, and you might want to as well, you will save yourself a lot of time.  Most of your codecs and players you might want to install/update can be obtained through apt-get.  

Apt-get page I used when I switched (cold turkey) to Ubuntu is at:
```
http://www.debian.org/doc/manuals/apt-howto/
```

Also, do a search in the forums, as someone else might have answered some of your other questions.

Hope this helps a little.
