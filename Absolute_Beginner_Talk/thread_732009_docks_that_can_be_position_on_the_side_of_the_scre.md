### Post by fela on 2008-03-22
Docks on linux tend to be really buggy and use lots of system resources, so my suggestion is that you just have a gnome-panel on the side of the screen. (and you can uncheck 'expand' so it doesn't fill the whole side of the screen up, more like a OS X dock)
