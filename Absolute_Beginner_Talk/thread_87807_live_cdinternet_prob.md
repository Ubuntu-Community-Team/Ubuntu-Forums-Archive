### Post by LHZ on 2005-11-09
I'm on DSL and the LiveCD detected my connection just fine.

You mentioned you use USB for your connection. Does this mean the modem for your internet connection plugs into your USB port, instead of your ethernet card? If that's the case, the Qwest people are right: Linux will need drivers to be able to communicate with the modem. Driver availability is a bit random: some are made by the company, some are made by volunteers, and some are never made at all. All I can advise you is to search around the forums, and/or post the name/type of your modem. Someone might be able to help you out.

Hope this helps.
