### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-11-25
You can change this in the move plugin's settings in ccsm.
Look for "initiate window move"

You do not have to hold down alt to move a window if you grab it by its titlebar no matter what though.

Keep in mind that if you take off alt from this option, you'll no longer be able to drag and select text or drag and select anything for that matter.
