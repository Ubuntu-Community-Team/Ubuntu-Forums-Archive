### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-01
Hmmm, ok... Ultimate Ubuntu is really just Ubuntu with a different splash screen and wallpaper and lots of preinstalled packages.

So, i assume then that you simply got the Iso then. First step is you need to burn that to a CD or DVD (I dunno how big it is). Then you have to make a partition with vmware, click add new OS (not sure the exact name). Follow the steps and make a new file, it should be at least 8 GB to get the ubuntu experience :).

Then just pop in the CD/DVD and push the start button on vmware and the screen will boot into your virtual machine. Follow the prompts and make sure to make two partitions (1 6 GB root directory (mounted to "/") and 1 2 GB swap file).

That should be it, have fun with vmware.
