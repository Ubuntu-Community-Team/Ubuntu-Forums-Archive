### Post by skymt on 2006-09-07
Well, there's Kopete. Also, both Yahoo and AIM have official clients for Linux.

[Meebo](http://www13.meebo.com/) is a web-based client that works with both Yahoo and AIM.

[Qnext](http://www.qnext.com/index.php) has a bunch of other features in addition to IM-ing on the major networks.

If you like simple, there's [SIM IM](http://sim-im.org/wiki/Main_Page), or Simple Instant Messenger... Instant Messenger. I guess.

There are also various clients for the command line, I'm guessing you don't want that.


I just use Gaim.
