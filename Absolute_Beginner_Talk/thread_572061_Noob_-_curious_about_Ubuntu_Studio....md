### Post by Orbital75 on 2007-10-10
Hi, Welcome to Ubuntu..... By looking at your PC specs ( Compaq Pentium II ),
I can tell you right now that most flavors of Ubuntu will not run your machine properly 
and will be very very slow. I attempted Ubuntu on a similar machine and it's mind
numbingly slow. As far as Ubuntu Studio, you'll find that Distro is meant for Musicians
and meant to replace some of the windows tools that they use for recording and mixing.
It's more of an industry type of distro. You need a Linux that is specificly written for older 
machines, any other recent Distro such as Ubuntu, Kubuntu, PClinuxOS will not run the way
you would like and will be irritatingly slow. If you do want to attempt to put a flavor of linux 
on this machine, I'd recommend Puppy Linux or Xubuntu.  Both of these Distros are meant
for older PC's  that you wish to save from the land fill. I tried Xubuntu and like it a lot.
It's very easy to use and pretty fast.

Don't worry Linux is really user friendly now and you will have no trouble
at all with support.  Everyone on this forum will be happy to help you with
any situation that may come up.  Just ask and we'll all help out one another.

Good Luck choosing and welcome to the Linux community!!!!  :KS
