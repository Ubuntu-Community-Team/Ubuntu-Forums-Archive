### Post by spiderbatdad on 2008-02-21
It sounds like you are talking about "panels." They are highly customizable. The simplest method is to right click on the panel and select properties>>background. choose solid color...from there you can add degrees of transparency or change the color completely. Then, if you like, you could look into .gtkrc-2.0 files, which can be placed in you home folder for even more unique effects. Note: most themes will also offer changes to the panels. If you navigate to Preferences>>Appearances>>Customize...the input boxes for background and active can be changed in color...this would affect the colors of the window list viewer...if you have this enabled for your panels.
