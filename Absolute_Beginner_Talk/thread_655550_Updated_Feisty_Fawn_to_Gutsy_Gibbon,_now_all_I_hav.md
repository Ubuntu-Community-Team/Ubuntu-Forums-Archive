### Post by taurus on 2008-01-01
Are you thinking reinstalling Gutsy from fresh?  If you are, you don't have to remove Ubuntu before you reinstall it.  Pick the Manual option when you get to the partition part during the installing process and tell the installer to mount which partition to / and it should pick out your swap partition right then.  Don't forget to format it.
