Hmmm...... I would suggest that the first thing you do is wait for the CDs. Then, run a live session (means that the CD boots up in such a way that it runs a full session off of RAM, meaning that your Hard drive is untouched unless you ask the CD to). Play with it for a week or so, doing everything you normally would, tweaking, whatever. See what works, and what doesn't. Test out everything. See whether your Internet works. Just use the live session like a normal installed session. If you like, then go ahead and install. If you don't, well.... we'll cross that hurdle when we get to it. Hope this helps!
