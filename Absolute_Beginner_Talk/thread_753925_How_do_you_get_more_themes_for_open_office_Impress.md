### Post by erginemr on 2008-04-13
Please visit the OpenOffice extensions gallery at:
[http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/most_pop_ext](http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/most_pop_ext)

and download the "Professional Template Pack" extension.

You can install it by following OpenOffice Menu -> Tools -> Extension Manager...

P.S. While you are on it, you can also have a look at "OxygenOffice Extras - English Templates" and "OxygenOffice Extras - Gallery".
