### Post by HereInOz on 2007-05-13
Download the GParted Live CD iso from [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/)

Burn that iso to a CD and boot the machine from it.  It will allow you to move and resize your partitions.  May take a while though, so don't get impatient, and back up all you can before you do it, just in case something goes belly up.
