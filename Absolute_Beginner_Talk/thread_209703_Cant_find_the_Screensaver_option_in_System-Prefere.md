### Post by MaximB on 2006-07-05
if you don't see a screensaver option you can add it.
just right click on the upper panel and chose "edit menus"
there on the left side chose system - preferences and on your right side chose all the applications you want to appear on your panel.

if you don't see the "edit menus" option when you right click on the upper panel you will need to install "alacarte menu editor" from the add/remove programs.
