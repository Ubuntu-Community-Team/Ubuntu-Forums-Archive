### Post by philippe_carlo on 2006-07-24
Best way to go: go to an internet cafe and apply all updates. In any other case: no update is crucial, as your system already runs. The update system is not very dial-up friendly. Don't be afraid to apply isolated updates, the package manger will decide for itself what it needs and will download it along with the rest.
