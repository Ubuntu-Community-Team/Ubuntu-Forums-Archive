### Post by Shatrat on 2007-01-03
Well, if you have beryl running and you have the box next to the Desktop -> Desktop Cube checked in Beryl Settings then there are a few ways to rotate it.
CTRL + ALT + left or right arrow will rotate the cube, or you can hold alt and click on the desktop and drag it around.

---
