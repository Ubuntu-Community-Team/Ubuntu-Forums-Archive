### Post by Wiebelhaus on 2007-09-08
This is a defiantly a hardware issue , I'd check the S.M.A.R.T Status of the HDD with manufacturers diagnostic software , Also checking the rest of the IDE 1&2 Chain , Making sure that all devices connected are functioning properly and that all jumpers are installed properly and while your at it you should run full diagnostic test on everything else , [UBCD]("http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/") "Ultimate boot CD" has allot of tools in one location as well as running manufacturer specific diagnostics would be choice.

Good luck.
