### Post by kc0dhb on 2006-10-29
I hope you figured out how to customize katapult...But in case you didn't.

Do the Alt-Space keyboard shortcut to bring up the Katapult display, then do the Ctrl-C keyboard combination to bring up a contextual menu, then you will be able to click on Configure Katapult.  This might not be exactly what you're looking for...do some searches for Katapult and plugins.

Another thing you could look at is creating shortcut keys for apps you use frequently (like ctrl+alt+f for Firefox).  You can do this by opening kcontrol (alt+F2 then typing kcontrol) and searching for keyboard shortcuts and going to Command Shortcuts.  Another fun thing to do is assign global shortcuts (kmix and assigning something for master volume is a favorite of mine).
