### Post by jem7v on 2007-04-04
Most of your programs will retain their old settings, but a lot of the non-default programs you have installed MIGHT get uninstalled.  That happened to me when I made the move from Dapper to Edgy.  If you reinstall them, though, they Should have the same settings as they did before because most of the time the user settings are saved in your /home directory.

You'll probably have to reconfigure some things, yes.  Some things might not work until you fix them.  But it'll probably be less configuring than you did when you first set up your system.
