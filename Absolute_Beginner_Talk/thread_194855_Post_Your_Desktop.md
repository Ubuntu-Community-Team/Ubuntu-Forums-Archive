### Post by maskd on 2006-06-12
I'm not really into desktop customisation, I enjoy the default human theme enough. The things I do however, is reduce the size of both panels top and bottom by 1 pixel and change the background to something a little nicer. This one is included with Ubuntu and I like it.

On the left is where I keep all the places that I store files, they're both FTP servers and SMB shares. Terry is a paritition on my hard drive, JACOB is the name of my ipod and Lupin III is a japanese anime from the 80s.

I change the workspace switcher to 6 workspaces because I like to keep everything maximised, and switch between workspaces with alt + s and alt + d, so i can change desktop and alt+tab quickly. I also delete the desktop button on the bottom left because I hardly look at my desktop, and when I do I just look at an empty workspace.

The icons on the top of the screen include firefox, terminal and eclipse.

Not impressive I know, but it's what I'm comfortable with.
