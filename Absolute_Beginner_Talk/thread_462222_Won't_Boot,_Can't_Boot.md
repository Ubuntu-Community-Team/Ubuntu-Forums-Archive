### Post by candtalan on 2007-06-02
I believe you have a RAID system with your two mirrored hard drives from the sound of it.

I have not used raid myseelf, however, I see that the live CD installer does not seem to handle raid installs - I see the following for the Alternate (ie non live CD) installer:

The Desktop CD runs a live desktop directly from the CD and can install to your hard disk. The Alternate CD includes a text based installer for low memory, LVM or RAID installs.

Your DVD question - I do not think it matters as long as you burn the 'image'  - making the media bootable - I am not totally sure though.

The Alternate CD can be downloaded and the image burned similar to the live CD.

You may like to see an example of a search I did
[http://www.ubuntu-in.org/wiki/SATA_RAID_Howto](http://www.ubuntu-in.org/wiki/SATA_RAID_Howto)

(you may find other infformation also)
