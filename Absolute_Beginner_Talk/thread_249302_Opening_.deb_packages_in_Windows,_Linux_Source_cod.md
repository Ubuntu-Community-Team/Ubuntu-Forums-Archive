### Post by skymt on 2006-09-02
.deb packages are just special .tar.gz archives. Rename it to packagename.tar.gz and you're good to go, assuming you have a Windows app to open those. If you don't, go download [7-zip](http://www.7-zip.org/) (which, I just noticed, decodes .deb packages anyway).
