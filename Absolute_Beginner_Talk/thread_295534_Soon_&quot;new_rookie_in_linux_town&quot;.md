### Post by goatflyer on 2006-11-08
At the time of performing the install, you will be prompted to create a swap partition.  Generally this should be about twice the size of your physical RAM.

Kubuntu/Ubuntu depends on what kind of user-interface you prefer:

Kubuntu: flashy, lots of controls, power-user...

Ubuntu: more sedate, intelligent under-the-hood defaults, normal-user...
