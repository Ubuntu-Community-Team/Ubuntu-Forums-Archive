### Post by bro on 2008-03-19
You are in no real need for the commandline / terminal. 

As reported most things can be installed via Synaptic, no building / compiling. Just clicking and automatic downloading and install. Beryl is no more. You need compiz-fusion. 

Most likely you already have compiz-fusion. If your graphics can handle it, 7.10 (Gutsy) enables Compiz by default. Go to System > Preferences > Appearance choose the tab 'visual effects' and pick one like 'extra'. 

For more howto of different kinds (installing or whatever) you might want to look at [this.]("http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Gutsy") Lots of commandline stuff, but no explanations about what it is though. If you actually want to use Ubuntu instead of Geeking around :lolflag: you don't really need to know the commandline.
