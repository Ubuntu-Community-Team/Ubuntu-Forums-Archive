### Post by shen-an-doah on 2007-07-26
Really, if you want to be assured of 100% compatibility, you can't go wrong with one that is basically a hard drive + an mp3 player interface. Then you just plug it in and it'll be seen the same as a usb key and you can drag and drop files as you usually would.

### Post by str8line on 2007-09-02

The new iRiver players and the Cowon players are both solid contenders and are quite affordable.
