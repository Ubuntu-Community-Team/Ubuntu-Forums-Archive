### Post by rancid98 on 2007-06-08
I'm not sure if this is where to post this, but hopefully someone will direct me where to post it, or move the post to the appropriate directory.

I installed Ubuntu and got it running well with the help of a few folk in this forum.  I was working in open office writer, and noted that the spellcheck was not working, and the autospellcheck was not underlining misspelt words.  After a bit of searching on the net, it appears that if the dictionary you are using is English-Australian there may be some problems with the spellcheck.  I installed the Australian dictionary thanks to some help I found in another forum via Google.  The thread I found is at this link [https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/openoffice.org/+question/1872]("https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/openoffice.org/+question/1872").  I went to the link suggested by justlocal in the response at [http://www.dictionary.JustLocal.com.au.]("http://www.dictionary.JustLocal.com.au.") and everything worked great after that.  As I had a bit of trouble finding out out to fix the problem, I thought I would post what I found to help others, hopefully to give something back to the community that gave me so much help when I needed it. :)
