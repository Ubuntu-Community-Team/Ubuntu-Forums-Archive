### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-03-10
No prob
right click on your menu and select edit menus
go to the internet category and click on the "new item" button
For the name just put in pidgin
for the command go to browse and make your way into usr/bin by going into filesystem, then to usr then to bin and look for pidgin.
once that is done go to open and you will have a link and possibly a icon to go along with it.
If not go to the icon box and click on "browse"
this time go to filesystem then to user, then to share, then to icons then to hicolor and to 48x48 then to apps.

there just go to close and you will have your icon back, then if need be click on the box next to your newly created pidgin icon and you will have a link :D
