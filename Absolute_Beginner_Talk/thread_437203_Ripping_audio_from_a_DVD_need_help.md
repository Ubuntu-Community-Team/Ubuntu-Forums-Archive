### Post by hitokiri0kenshin on 2007-05-08
I'm not sure if there is any programs that can do this. I do know that you can use VLC Media Player to save audio and video streams of whatever you are watching. I did this once and managed to pull a section of audio off of a Power Rangers episode, but it took some work and had to edit it with an audio editor afterward. I'm no expert on how to do it but when you open something with VLC click the Stream/save button and set up the settings the way you want. 

[http://www.videolan.org/vlc/download-ubuntu.html](http://www.videolan.org/vlc/download-ubuntu.html)

Hope this helps :)
