### Post by Countryboy123 on 2007-07-01
Have you been working with your fonts?  >system> preferences> font 
(The > means goto/ click on. )

I had to reduce my Sans font from a 10 to a 8 under " Applications Font " to get my Right corner back in my Evolution email application. The rest of my applications were just fine.

Just so I can say I said it. This is a change for all applications not just a specific application change. I have not found a way to change fonts for specific programs, yet.

(Just for the record , I looked it up, on a 800x600 resolution, Its supposed to be a known bug in Evolution.  It is  # 5684, if your curios. )
