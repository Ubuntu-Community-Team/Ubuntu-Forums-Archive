### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-05-14
If the "check the disc" option doesn't work, it may be that the .iso (CD image you downloaded) is corrupted or incorrect.  Sometimes there are hardware compatibility issues, so it may just be that the Live CD won't work for your computer (although it is unusual to not even get past that first page).  Worth trying the disc in someone else's computer to see if it gets any further.  Otherwise, download the .iso again, and write to a fresh CD, and try again.
