### Post by heimo on 2006-01-25
Nice to hear these success stories too! :) Thanks.

[quote=Sbarton]. As my adsl usb modem does not support linux I shall be getting another.[/quote]

If you already have ethernet card / integrated ethernet in your computer, I'd definitely choose adsl "router", perhaps with a 4-8 port switch, box that attaches to your phone line and you can attach your computers to the ethernet ports. Most of the time these devices are easier to install than USB Adsl modems (if that's what you were going to get). Network card support is much better than usb adsl modem support in Linux and this gains you the advantage that you can set up a home lan later and share the connection easily, if needed (laptop for instance).
