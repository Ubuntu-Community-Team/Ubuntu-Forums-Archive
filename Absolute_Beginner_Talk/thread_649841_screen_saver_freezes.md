### Post by CharlieFoxtrot on 2008-01-08
Although this thread is two weeks old, I thought I'd put a note in here for anyone searching for information on this problem in the future. I found the thread after searching for posts on screensaver freezes after experiencing the same problem. That is, the Braid screensaver freezing. I have my screensaver set to "random". I noticed that whenever the screensaver froze it was on Braid. This appears to be another Compiz conflict. Disable Compiz and Braid runs normally. Enable Compiz and Braid chokes.

This is now the third Compiz conflict I've run across. The other two are white-screening when user-switching and the Xsane scanned image window "taking" over the entire screen.
