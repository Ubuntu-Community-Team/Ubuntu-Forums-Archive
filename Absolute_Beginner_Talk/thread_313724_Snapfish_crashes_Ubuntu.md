### Post by bulldog on 2006-12-06
Works without a problem over here,but that's not what you want to hear,I suppose :D 

You can try to make a new profile,but before you do make a backup of your bookmarks.
A clean new profile means you start from scratch with firefox.

To do so,go to your /home and go to tab view and check show hidden folders.
Find the .mozilla folder and rename it what ever you want.
Restart firefox and a new profile is created.
