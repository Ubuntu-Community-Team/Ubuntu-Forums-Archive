The way I worked around this was that I created an icon folder in the kxdocker folder and stored the icons I wanted the docker to use there (I used 128x128 or scalable) . . .

I then opened configurator -> rolling icons and edited each of the programs to use the icon in the folder I created . . . save the configuration and restart kxdocker . . . hopefully this works for you . . .
