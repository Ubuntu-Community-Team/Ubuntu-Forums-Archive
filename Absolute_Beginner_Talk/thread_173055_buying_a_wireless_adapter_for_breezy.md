### Post by nickle on 2006-05-09
Using Ndiswrapper should support most cards. However for native support cards with atheros chips are well supported. Mine was recognized in Breezy and dapper. Either search the site here or do a google to find the card names with these chips. I know there are several sites with this info, but I don't remember where...

### Post by garner_nc on 2006-05-09
I recently bought a Zonet ZWE1501 wireless cardbus card. Plugged it into my Breezy laptop, went to System>Adminstration>Networking and saw the system found the card. It works very well.

   Nextag.com has (had?) these for USD 18.99. It's a great deal.
