### Post by DreadPirateRoby on 2007-03-28
I've never tried this in linux and my current install doesn't have open office so I don't know if it will work on there to, but in Open Office for windows holding **Ctrl** and pressing **Z** just after the break will give you back the "-----" you originally typed. Ctrl+z again will undo the break.

The additional lines could just be artifacts on your screen, look at the print preview and see if they show up there as well.

If nothing else works you could use abiword instead.
