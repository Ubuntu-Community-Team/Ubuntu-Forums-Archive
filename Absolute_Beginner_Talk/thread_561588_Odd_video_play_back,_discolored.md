### Post by tbroderick on 2007-09-27
Try changing the various video out drivers for xine. There should be a bunch: x11, xv, gl2, etc.

There should be an option in whatever text editor you are using. In gedit it's;
Preferences->Editor->Create a backup copy of files before saving.
