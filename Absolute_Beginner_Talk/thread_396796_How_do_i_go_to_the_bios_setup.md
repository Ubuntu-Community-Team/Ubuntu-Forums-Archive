### Post by qpwoeiruty on 2007-03-29
At boot, you'll have to press and hold a key or key combination to get into the BIOS.
Common ones are: del, F10, tab, esc, Ctrl+Alt+Enter, F2

Then, you look for the appropriate setting and change it :)

### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2007-03-29
It will vary depending on which motherboard manufacturer produced your motherboard and possibly even the specific model of motherboard you have.  Usually, you hit a specific key when you first turn on the computer.  There is usually a message on the screen that tells you which key to hit.  Sometimes it is the escape key, for others it is delete, I've even seen some that use F1.  You need to read either your computer manual or the manual that came with your motherboard to find out which.
