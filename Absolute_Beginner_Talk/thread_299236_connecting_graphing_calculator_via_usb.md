### Post by b_martinez on 2006-11-13
I'm likely way off the wall on this, but Ubuntu works well with a usb hard drive, 2 digital cameras and also a pen drive that I have. Try to just plug it in to a handy usb port. It may show it as removable storage, and pop up a desktop icon for it. Right click on the icon,click on the 'OPEN' menu selection, and if you see it's contents, you are in business. It may not work, but it's worth a try. The current modules and programs for loading removable media are really good.:mrgreen:
