### Post by Cypher on 2007-12-26
As you have noticed the posts in this forum do move very quickly, so posts that are an hour or so old do fall from the front page, but most regulars do peruse multiple pages to see the topics that they might be able to help with.

So definitely update your existing post with any new relevant things that you've tried or learned, but don't unnecessarily bump your posts..

The other option is to directly PM people you felt were knowledgeable in your post and hopefully get some help that way. If you do PM, be sure to update your post with whatever info help your resolve your issue so that others can search for it later on and benefit as well..
