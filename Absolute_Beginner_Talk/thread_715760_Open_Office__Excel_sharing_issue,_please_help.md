### Post by neurostu on 2008-03-05
The feature you are talking about is track changes (I think).  Which I am pretty sure that Open Office has not yet implemented.

The best solution would be to buy CrossOver linux which allows you to install MS Office (up to 2003 I think) and will give you 6 months of support. Or you could try wine and figure it all out yourself.

But in a buisness environment I'm sure that having your computer be the one that doesn't work probably isn't the best idea, thats why I would recommend getting Crossover and installing MS office.
