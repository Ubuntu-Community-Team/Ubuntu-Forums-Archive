### Post by esoterica on 2007-05-18
I just found this and thought it was so cool! 

Add it to the list of awesome things you can do with Ubuntu.

I haven't tried it out yet, but it's certainly on my list of fun Linux projects I want to try.

It lets you set up your own WiFi hotspot and have total control over it. Anyone thinking what would you do with that, the answer is simple.

What a great way to get your neighbors to pay for your internet access from your house (if they live close enough). If you charged just 3 neighbors $10 a month for access they'd save money and you'd get your own access for free (paid for by them).

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/ChillispotHotspot](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/ChillispotHotspot)
