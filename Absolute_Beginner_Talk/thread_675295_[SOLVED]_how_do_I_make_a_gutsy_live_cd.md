### Post by irish_flu on 2008-01-22
Conveniently, the 7.10 Desktop CD (but *not* the alternate CD) is already a "Live CD".  You can boot right to it and play.

Caution, though; as with all Live CDs, performance running from the CD won't be as good as the performance you get when (any OS) is installed on your hard drive.

### Post by Techwiz on 2008-01-22
You mean a live CD, as in one to boot in your computer?
To make in Ubuntu:
Download to ISO from the website; [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu") (I think)
Put file in CD burner directory on your computer.
Push burn.
Choose make image from file.
Wait for it to burn.

In (ahem) Windows:
Download InfraRecorder here: [http://infrarecorder.sourceforge.net/?page_id=5]("http://infrarecorder.sourceforge.net/?page_id=5")
Download Ubuntu ISO (see above)
In InfraRecorder choose the burn image option from the Actions menu
Select the ISO and the choose burn to disk
Wait for it to finish
DONE!
hope this helps. :)
