### Post by Presto123 on 2008-02-22
Post help threads in Absolute Beginner's Thread. ;)

Hopefully you will not need any kind of code, all you will need is the driver for the ATI card. It will be located in Add/Remove in the main menu on your panel under Applications.

Click on the Add/Remove and make sure your top-right drop down menu shows "All Available Applications". Type in the search box "ATI".
