### Post by glennric on 2008-03-11
However, if your screensaver is set to "Blank Screen" you may thing your display monitor is going to sleep, although it is not really.  Some monitors may actually go to sleep when they are given a blank screen for long enough.  It is also possible that you have the bios set to turn off the monitor after a given amount of time.
