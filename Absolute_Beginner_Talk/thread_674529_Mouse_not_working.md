I have run into issues with USB mice freezing the Anaconda installer in LiveCD mode. I never figured out why it happened, I just swapped the mouse for a PS2 and moved along. If you have access to a PS2 mouse, or another mouse in general, try that. Otherwise, as meborc suggested, you can try installing the OS in text mode and fixing your mouse later.
