### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-02-07
You can check both right from System > Administration > Networking. If your card is installed and initialized properly, it will show up there as a wireless connection. If not, then your card might not be supported natively, but might be available through an application called Ndiswrapper.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper)
