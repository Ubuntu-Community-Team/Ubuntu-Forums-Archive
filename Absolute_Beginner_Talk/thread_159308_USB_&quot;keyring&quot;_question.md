### Post by geeknation on 2006-04-13
It all depends on what you want really. For speed then a flash drive and card is the way to go. But for portability, as in sticking the thing in your pocket and going somewhere else to use it (a friends house maybe), then i'd go for a usb pen drive.

If it's mass storage you want then have a loot at some external hard-drives, they can be quite cheap. Having said that these can be horribly slow at times.

If push comes to shove...use a tonne of floppy disks! :twisted:
