### Post by odinb on 2007-09-04
I am doing that at work. 
For mail, you just have to make sure that you have the fully qualified domain name(s) for the mailserver, i.e not smtpserver0123 but rather smtpserver0123.domain.com (and then ofcourse your user&pass on the domain).

For browsing, you need the proxyserver-address and portnumber (if applicable).

If you also have shares you want to access, smb4k is an excellent tool.

Good luck!
