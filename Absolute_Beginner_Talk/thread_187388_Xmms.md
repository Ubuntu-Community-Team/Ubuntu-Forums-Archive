### Post by kingbilly on 2006-06-03
You will have to install a plugin for that feature. 

[http://packages.debian.org/stable/sound/xmms-status-plugin](http://packages.debian.org/stable/sound/xmms-status-plugin)

Head over there for a nice .deb package.  Install.  Open XMMS and press Ctrl P.  Choose the General Plugins tab, select Status Docklet Plugin and then check the enable box on the bottom right of the preferences window.
