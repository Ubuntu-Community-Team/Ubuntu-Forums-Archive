There's another app called [PyMusique]("http://drmnews.com/pymusique/") not sure if this is what you are looking for but it makes it possible to download from iTunes store (without the DRM I think) and play on a GNU Linux media player. Another thought comes to mind why not use VLC media player to stream the music/video to you Ubuntu box and then use Streamtuner or something like it to rip the files at the other end.
