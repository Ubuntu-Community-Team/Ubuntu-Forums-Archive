### Post by wormser on 2007-05-09
Those are differnt systems you can boot into.  Typically there will be 2 for ubuntu.  One for regular use and the other for recovery.  Sometimes you will see more ubuntu lines.  Those are older kernels.  You can boot into them.  It is best not to delete one of them incase you have issues with the latest kernel.
