### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-08-03
The option to enter the BIOS appears at the beginning of the boot sequence, you usually only have a second or two to hit it.  It should display on the screen, but common keys are F2, F8, F12, and DEL to enter the BIOS.  You can probably check any computer manuals you have or look up online which key needs to be pressed to access BIOS.
