### Post by Famicommie on 2006-12-15
> how do i chat... because it asks for the java runtime enviornment plugin but when i try to install it i can't..

What client are you trying to use to chat? As far as java, did you install Automatix like bulldog suggested?

> also how do i login as the main user such that i dont have to change the permissions of stuff coz im not good at it...

Logging in as root is disabled, and for a good reason. You could very easily break your OS if you make a few wrong moves as the root user. Trust me, stick to sudo and you'll have fewer headaches.

> i tried to get limewire for linux from their site but i have absolutely no idea how to install...

Until you're more familiar with linux, I suggest using Automatix to get amule (it's kind of like e-donkey).

> also my comp keeps downloading new updates but how do i actually use the updates..

They're automatically installed. No need to worry about that.

> how can i play good games likie fps or strategy or sumn like that...

[http://gaming.gwos.org/index.php](http://gaming.gwos.org/index.php)
Make sure you have the proper video drivers installed for your system, too.

> can i play vcds and dvds on my laptop also?
If you have a DVD drive for the DVDs, almost. Most DVDs are encoded, and in order to play them you have to use codecs that are illegal to download in the US. Automatix can install those codecs, but don't download them if you're from anywhere within the USA.</disclaimer>
