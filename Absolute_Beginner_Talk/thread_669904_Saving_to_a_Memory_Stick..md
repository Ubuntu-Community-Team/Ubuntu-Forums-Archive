### Post by eolson on 2008-01-16
The best way is to save them to your desktop where they are always handy.  If foresee having a bunch of them, make a folder for them under places > home folder.   That way you've always got them on the system.  Then you can just drag and drop them to the usb stick.  That's the way I do the 6 or so that I have (mostly spreadsheets).   Quick and easy.
