### Post by JonathanD on 2008-02-11
I use mp32ogg and it works great. That script can convert a whole directory tree recursively and it will also transfer the meta information (artist, album, track, song title, etc). It is easy to use and has other nice features. Details here: [Convert your MP3 files to Ogg Vorbis with mp32ogg]("http://blog.goodcamel.com/?p=3")
