### Post by rockerphil on 2008-04-15
it sounds like you don't have all your repositories enabled. so open up Synaptic, click on the button that says "settings" up at the top and click "repositories" and make sure all the boxes are checked., click "ok' then when the repository window closes click "reload". all available programs should be accessible once you do that
