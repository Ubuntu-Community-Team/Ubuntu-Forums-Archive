### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2007-04-17
This particular printer will definitely pose a problem, as almost all Canon printers will.  As other threads on this messageboard have indicated, it will take some effort.  As a mattter of fact, I don't know of anyone who has been able to get it to work, but as reported by linuxprinting.org, it should work.  Your printer should work, but other things may not work.

This is why it is ideal to buy a printer that is recommended from linuxprinting.org.

Some have reported getting the scanning part to work with SANE.  Others use Turboprint for the printing part.  You may be lucky with gutenprint since LinuxPrinting.org says the printer part should work with gutenprint.

Let us know how things go.

By the way, doing a search on Google.com/linux was pretty helpful.

Edit:  You may need this link: [http://home.arcor.de/wittawat/pixma/](http://home.arcor.de/wittawat/pixma/).
