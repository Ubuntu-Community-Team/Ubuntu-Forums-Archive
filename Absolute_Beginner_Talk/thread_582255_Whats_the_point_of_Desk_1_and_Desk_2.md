### Post by wheredidrealitygo on 2007-10-21
go to "advanced desktop effects" under your preferences, in the system menu.

this is what compizconfig-settings-manager provides. then click "general options", and under the "horizontal desktop size" string, move it over to as many as you want.

make sure you have both "desktop cube" and "rotate cube" plugins for this to work.

Spinning an octagonal prism is fun ;)
