### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-01-08
Hi. In Synaptic, got to 'Settings->Repositories' and on the 'Ubuntu Software' tab check the first four boxes. Close that window and click the blue 'Reload' button. [MythTV]("http://www.mythtv.org/") and [Elisa]("http://elisa.fluendo.com/") are in the repositories; aside from those, there's also [LinuxMCE]("http://www.linuxmce.com/").
