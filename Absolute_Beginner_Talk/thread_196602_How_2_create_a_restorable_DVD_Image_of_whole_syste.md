### Post by The Cosmic Hobo on 2006-06-14
I should suspect it's possible - at least in theory.
I don't know precisely how you could achieve it, but assuming you have a suitably large spare hard disk on which to store the image, a Norton Ghost-like application, the means to create a bootable DVD which includes both the Ghost-like application and the image, and a BIOS which supports booting from DVD... well, I don't see a reason it shouldn't work.

There's probably a better way than this to achieve your objective - I'm just speculating, based on some experiments I did with Windows 98 and a bootleg Norton Ghost provided by a friendly computer engineer; the restore CDs for my laptop (Toshiba Satellite A10 Pro) actually work pretty much the same way, booting into Ghost and copying a Ghost image to the HD. Anyone with a little more practical experience than me care to chime in on the subject?
