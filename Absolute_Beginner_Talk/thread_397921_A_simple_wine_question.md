### Post by pollywog on 2007-03-31
I don't know about that particular program, but if you need to access the fake "C" drive that wine creates, the open your home folder, select "view" up top, then click "show hidden files". Then go to wine>c>program files, or wherever you would go if you were in windows. I hope this is what you needed to know- good luck -P.W.
