You don't have a DVD burner? If not, consider it the best 30-40 dollars you ever spent, it is very useful for last minute recovery and there are lots of isos that don't fit on CDs (Ubuntu studio being one). I'd strongly recommend ya go get one. And yes, you need more than 4 gigs for Ubuntu.
