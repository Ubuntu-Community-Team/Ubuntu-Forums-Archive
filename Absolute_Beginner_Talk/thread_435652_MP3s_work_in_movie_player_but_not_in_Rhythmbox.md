### Post by r3bol on 2007-05-08
Making Rythmbox find music, try this.
Stick all your music in a folder like /home/you/music
Open: Rythmbox>Edit>Prefs>library tab
Browse the folder you stuck your music in.

Next to the library tab is also a podcast tab. Setup there they are too.

Oh, and it might take a little time to organise.
