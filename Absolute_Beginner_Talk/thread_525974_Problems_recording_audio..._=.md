### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-14
Check out the following settings:

1. Right-click on the [COLOR="Blue"]speaker icon[/COLOR] in your taskbar and click on [COLOR="Blue"]Open Volume Control[/COLOR]

2. When the mixer opens, click on [COLOR="Blue"]Edit>Preferences[/COLOR]

3.Generally, it doesn't hurt to put a tick in every box (to be sure).

4.Click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Recording Tab[/COLOR] and move the recording sliders to maximum, making sure that the small microphone and speaker icons below the sliders are not X'd out.

5 Click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Switches Tab[/COLOR] and click on the captures source that you want. (Microphone?)

Close and try again
