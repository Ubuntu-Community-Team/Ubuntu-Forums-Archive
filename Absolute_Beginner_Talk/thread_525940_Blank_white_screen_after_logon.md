### Post by some_random_noob on 2007-08-14
No, Ubuntu isn't for tech-heads only. As for the white screen: pretty visuals have no 100% guarantees - they won't always work. When you get the white screen, try CTRL+ALT+F1 and see if you get a command prompt (you could then try to 'apt-get remove' the visuals you installed, although its not too likely).

So, you either screwed up when you installed the visuals, or they just screwed up themselves. Those visual effects aren't always stable... do you know exactly what set of visuals you installed anyway? 

You should consider reinstalling your OS and then avoid experimental/unstable visual effects software in the future.
