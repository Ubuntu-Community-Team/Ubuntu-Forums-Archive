### Post by zettberlin on 2007-10-25
The Delta is supported by Linux since many years - you need a special mixer for it. Open the Synaptic-Packagemanager and search for envy24control.

I got a MAudio Audiophile that works like a charm with Ubuntu (and any other recent linux) only the GNOME-Desktop mixer applet cannot handle such beasts so alsamixer or envy24control is needed.
