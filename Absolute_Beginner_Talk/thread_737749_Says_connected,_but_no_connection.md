### Post by cisforcojo on 2008-03-27
What kind of WEP key are you using? 64/128bit and HEX/ASCII? This can affect your password entry.

I'd also suggest for simplicity's sake FIRST removing your password from your wireless and see if that works. Then add on the password protection. THEN scrap WEP and use WPA ;)
WEP protection is kinda like having a fake rock outside your door, everyone knows there's a key under it and can still get into your house despite the lock.
