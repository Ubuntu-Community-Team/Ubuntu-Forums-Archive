### Post by Ribs on 2007-10-22
A lot of sites don't bother to configure their colours properly and just stick to whatever defaults the browser gives them.

You can change these defaults by going to the Edit menu, Preferences. Click the content tab on the new window which opens, and there is a option to change your colours on the right hand side.

Hope this helps.
