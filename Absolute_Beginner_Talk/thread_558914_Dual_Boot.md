### Post by Dr Small on 2007-09-24
Ubuntu will install GNU GRUB, the bootloader at Installation, if you choose to leave your current installation of W2K, and use the rest of the free space on the Hard drive.
Then when you boot up your computer, GNU GRUB will load, and you will be able to choose between W2K and Ubuntu.

Dr Small
