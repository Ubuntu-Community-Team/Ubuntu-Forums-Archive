### Post by p_quarles on 2007-09-12
I would need a little bit more detail to be sure what you're asking . . . but I *think* this is what you're looking for:
1) Right click on the area of the task bar that that you want it to appear in
2) Select "Add to Panel"
3) Scroll down to the bottom of the menu that pops up
4) Select "Notification area" and click "Add"

If that's not what you're looking for, try to be a little more specific.
