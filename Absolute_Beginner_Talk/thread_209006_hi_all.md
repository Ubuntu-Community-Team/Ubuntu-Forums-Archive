### Post by Raavea on 2006-07-04
Whilst Linux is inhospitable (not immune) to malware, and there are no 'wild' viruses on the net for linux, it's a good idea to have the firewall there just in case.

 I use Firestarter - you'll find it in Synaptic. If you want to see just how awesome linux's natural defences are, go to [https://www.grc.com/x/ne.dll?bh0bkyd2](https://www.grc.com/x/ne.dll?bh0bkyd2)  and test your system. :D

 PS: If you intend on using bittorrent or azureus, make sure firestarter is on. :)
