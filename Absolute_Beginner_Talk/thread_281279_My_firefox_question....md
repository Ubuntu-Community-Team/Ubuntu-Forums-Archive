### Post by localuser on 2006-10-20
If the Firefox window is open and you have access to the menus, then go to Tools | Add-ons, highlight the add-ons that you recently added (one at a time), and click on uninstall.

If its the fault of the add-ons, then after you close down Firefox, then you should be fine when you restart it.
