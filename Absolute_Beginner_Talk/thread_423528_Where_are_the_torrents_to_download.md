### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-04-25
Well, if you have a client installed (I like bit tornado) then all you need to do is find the tracker file to download from a site or search for it on a site like [isohunt]("http://isohunt.com/") or [mininova]("http://www.mininova.org/"). Download it to your desktop, then right click on it and open it with your preferred client, it asks you where to save it and browse to the place and then download begins. Be sure to forward a listen port per your client's instructions, makes your download faster. Hope thats the answer to your problem, you were just a wee bit vague...
