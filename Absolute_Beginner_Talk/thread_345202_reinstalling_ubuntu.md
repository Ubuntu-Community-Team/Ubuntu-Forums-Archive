### Post by Quillz on 2007-01-24
Be sure to enable hidden files, then just copy everything from /home/username to an external hard drive, etc. Then, just copy them back over after re-installing Ubuntu and all your settings, passwords, etc. are intact!

### Post by Sef on 2007-01-24
Just make sure that you only format the root (/) partition and swap.  When reinstalling, know which partition is your home partition and leave it alone and all shold be well.  But be sure to back up your files first.  Nothing is 100% guaranteed.
