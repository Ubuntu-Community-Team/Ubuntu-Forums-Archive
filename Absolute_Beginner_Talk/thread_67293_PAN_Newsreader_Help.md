### Post by mneptok on 2005-09-19
[QUOTE=ubienewbie]It says it is trying to connect to news.localdomain, port 119 and is failing. What do I need to change to be able to browse some newsgroups and continue my re-education in computers? Thanks in advance.[/QUOTE]

You are trying to reach a Usenet server on your own machine, and your machine is not a server.

You need to have a Usenet server to which to connect. Most ISPs run Usenet servers for their clients' use. Check with your ISP to see what Usenet server you should use.
