### Post by adam.tropics on 2007-12-24
If your home directory is /home/spydon, then go to /home/spydon/.config/compiz and you will see a compizconfig folder. Rename or delete it and restart compiz and you will be back to default. Note: for me this doesn't restart the window decorator. I had to manually do that the first time.Also, for individual settings, within the settings manager itself you will see little brush buttons next to each value, those will restore defaults for individual settings.
