### Post by Inxsible on 2007-05-21
If you downloaded it, most times when its in .r01 etc format, its a DVD Rip and not the entire DVD. so you probably wont be able to make the DVD menus for the movie.

What size is your download ? Complete DVD's are around 3.5GB to 4GB in size.


If the size is right, use the File Roller on the .r01 file and open it. In it, you should see the DVD files and all you have to do is simply extract it. Once you extract it to a location, you should see all your files there and you can burn them accordingly.
