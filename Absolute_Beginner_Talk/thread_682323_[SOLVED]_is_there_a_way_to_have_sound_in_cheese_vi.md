### Post by thebestofall007 on 2008-01-30
i have found out how to get sound from the built in mic. what i did is i double clicked on the volume control icon in the panel to open up the hda volume control menu and enabled the mic in the controls. next i clicked edit.preferences and enabled the  capture option in the record tab and turned the mic all the way up and the audio from the mic all the way down (so you dont hear yourself in the background).
