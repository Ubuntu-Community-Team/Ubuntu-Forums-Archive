### Post by Sef on 2006-07-17
How much memory do you have?  Ubuntu (or Kunbuntu) are full flavored distros really needing at least 256 memory.  

Xubuntu is a lighter weight distro needing really 128 memory.

Both can run on less than those, but tend to be slow then.

### Post by Sef on 2006-07-17
2-3 years old, it should have at least 256 mb ram.  If you got windows on there, you can right click on the my computer icon (i believe it is that one) and then click on properties....that will tell you how much memory you have.

All 3 are good distros. the best one for here is Ubuntu since it is the most widely used, thus there are more people to answer questions that you may have.

The difference between Ubuntu, Kubuntu and Xubuntu really comes down to a matter of taste unless you don't have much memory, in which case Xubuntu is better.
