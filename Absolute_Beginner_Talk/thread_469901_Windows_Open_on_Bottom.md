Ah, I believe I know what this is. Ran into it when trying out Tilda, it refused to pop over my active windows.

Right click on the Beryl gem and open the Beryl settings manager. Make sure your on the general options tab and then scroll down to the entry that reads "Level of Focus Stealing Prevention". Select "None" in the drop down. Thats all it takes to fix. Push close and your windows should pop ontop again without fail.
