### Post by Apple 101 on 2006-09-06
That depends on your hardware setup, not just the CPU.  In my case, I connect to the LAN with a WLAN card.  This, as I found out, after installing 64-bit Ubuntu only has 32-bit drivers.  So I had to install the 32-bit system.

I would recommend installing the 32-bit operating system as it will have better compatibility overall.
