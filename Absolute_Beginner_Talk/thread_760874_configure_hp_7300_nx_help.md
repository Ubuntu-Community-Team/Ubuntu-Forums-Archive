### Post by A4006 on 2008-04-20
What exactly is it that you want to configure?  Ubuntu should have all the drivers required for your hardware, and will work fine from you first boot.  If you are inquiring about video card drivers or codecs, video drivers can be easily installed using Envy [http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html]("http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html") and most codecs can be found using Ubuntu's Add/Remove programs feature.  If you have all that, I'd consider your laptop pretty much "configured".
