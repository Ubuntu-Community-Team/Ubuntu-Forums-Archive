### Post by monktbd on 2006-09-02
There is nothing wrong with that.

All images that are thumbnailed have its thumbnail in .png format. the original image can be of any format.

Just look into the thumbnails and watch out for an image you have normally stored in jpeg or some other format and you will find a png thumbnail of it.
