### Post by freebird54 on 2007-06-08
The probable cause of excessive slowness is the CD drive itself.  Given a slow drive, there is not much the OS can do about it.  If you can show her on another machine, or talk her into letting you have 10Gb at the end of the drive to play with - the installed system should be fast enough!

Good luck...

(I have seen CD drives in recent laptops as slow as 4x - shudder)

### Post by Kobalt on 2007-06-08
The LiveCD requires at least 256Mo to start but a Live Session can use much more memory. If you 'only' have 512 then the session will never be as responsive as a real install.
If you really want to show Ubuntu off to someone then I'd suggest a real install.
