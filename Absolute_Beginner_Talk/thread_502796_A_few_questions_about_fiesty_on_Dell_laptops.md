### Post by Steve Fisher on 2007-07-17
I'd wait.

I've got Ubuntu installed here on an old Latitude laptop, and everything worked straight out of the box, so to speak. But that's just because it *is* fairly old, and the hardware is compatible.

If I bought a brand new laptop from Dell now, I wouldn't expect it to work very well; certainly not as well as it would with Vista or XP. As you've pointed out, some of the hardware looks... dubious.

However, I do believe that Dell will start producing laptops with Ubuntu installed on them available in the UK, but I wouldn't be surprised if the hardware was subtly different to the "mainstream" laptops - using parts known to be compatible.

In short, if you fancy the challenge - go for it. If you just want something that works, hang on a while (or import one from the US!:))
