### Post by thephantomlinguist on 2006-09-29
The trick is that PowerISO is a windows-only program.

You can use the trial version, but I think it limits you to only 300MB images and won't let you mess with anything more than that.

My recommendation would be to find someone you know that has a registered copy (or that you can convince to get a registered copy) and have that person convert the daa to an iso or a bin/cue for you that you can hold on to. Or, you could buy the program yourself. But if you're only going to use it once, that seems like a waste.

Hope this helps!
