What did you enter as the POP server? The incoming should be pop.mail.yahoo.com and the outgoing mail should be configured as SMTP, and the setting should be smtp.mail.yahoo.com. No encryption boxes should have been checked. To change your settings, open up Evolution, go to Edit at the top, then click Preferences. Single-click on the account you want to change, then click on Edit in the bar on the right. Use the tabs to navagate the menu and find what you want to change. Hope this helps.
