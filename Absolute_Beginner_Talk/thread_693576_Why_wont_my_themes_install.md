### Post by aeiah on 2008-02-11
manually place the files in your .themes folder (its a hidden folder in your home directory). if the themes come in tar.gz files, unpack them by opening them up and extracting them to your desktop, then move them over to the .themes folder. that should work, if they have the proper structure. this is assuming the themes are valid, and you arent trying to install kde themes to a gnome desktop etc
