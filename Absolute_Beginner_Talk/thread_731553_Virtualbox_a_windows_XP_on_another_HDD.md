### Post by ByteJuggler on 2008-03-22
If you want to permanently convert as a once off, then VMWare has a converter tool that will convert from a physical machine to a virtual machine for you.  Needless to say, once done, you can't go back. (IIRC however VMWare's commercial offering allow virtual->physical conversions.)
