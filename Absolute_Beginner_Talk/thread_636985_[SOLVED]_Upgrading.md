### Post by rsambuca on 2007-12-10
There is nothing that says you have to upgrade from Dapper.  It is strictly your personal preference.  Dapper is a Long Term Support version, meaning it will continue to receive security updates until the middle of 2009, so you are safe for quite a while.

If you do decide to upgrade, make sure you back up your sensitive data first.  I would then just do a fresh install of the newest version.  If you want to try the upgrade path, you would have to go from Dapper to Edgy to Feisty to Gutsy.  It would make for a lot of downloading, a lot of time, and it may not work anyways.
