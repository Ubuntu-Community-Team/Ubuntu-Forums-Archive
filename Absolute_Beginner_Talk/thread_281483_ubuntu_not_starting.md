### Post by Kurt` on 2006-10-21
You need to enter your BIOS setup (usually you press F1, F2, or Delete after turning on your computer) and change your "boot order" so that your computer will look for bootable media in the cd/dvd drive BEFORE booting off your hard disk.

Consult your motherboard/manufacturer manual
