### Post by Arthur Archnix on 2008-03-08
That's because, again, it's not called alacarte in the menu, it's called "Main Menu". I agree, that's a poor name that doesn't tell you much. Perhaps it should be called "Main Menu Editor", the good news is that if you go through your menu, >system  >preferences  you will see >main menu, which will allow you to change that silly name to something else. Or rearrange them.

If you still cannot find it, press Alt+F2, then type: alacarte  and hit enter.
