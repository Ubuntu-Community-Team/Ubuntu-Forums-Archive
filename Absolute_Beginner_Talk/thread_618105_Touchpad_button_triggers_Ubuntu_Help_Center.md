### Post by Inxsible on 2007-11-20
Its funny !!

I never gave it any thought until now. It seems the solution is easy.

Go to System>>Preferences>>Keyboard Shortcuts

Then Under Desktop, look at the first entry
Click on Launch Help Browser and then hit 'Backspace' (to Disable it) or rather change it to Fn + F1 (since the F1 key has a small ? mark sign which means its for help )
and voila !!!!
