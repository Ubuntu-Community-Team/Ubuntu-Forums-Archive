### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-08-15
Right click the Panel and select Add to Panel.  Under Desktop & Windows there is the Trash applet.   Add it :)

---

### Post by Hobo Joe on 2007-08-15
Right click on the bottom bar, and press the 'add to panel' option. Then just find the trash icon and drag it over onto the taskbar. This can be done for top and bottom bars.
