### Post by wreti on 2007-06-12
For the most part, file sharing takes up a lot of bandwidth so you should only see your internet connection slow. As far as viruses, be sure you trust the source before you download anything. The most common sources of infection are from careless users.

### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-06-12
No, people won't be able to send you viruses, however sharing may or may not slow down your internet connection, depening on how many people are downloading the files that you are willing to share.  I wouldn't worry about it slowing down your computer though. Once frostwire is up and running, unless it's piecing together files for you after a download, it remains rather quick and in the background, compared to most tasks.
