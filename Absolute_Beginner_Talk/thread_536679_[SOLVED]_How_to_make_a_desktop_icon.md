### Post by jw5801 on 2007-08-28
What happens if you open up a terminal and simply input "bitpim"? Without the quotation marks of course. If that runs the program then just right click on your desktop and go "Create Launcher" and in the command section there put in bitpim like you typed in the terminal. Otherwise report back and we can give further advice!

### Post by wormser on 2007-08-28
A 'short cut' in Linux is called a link.  Right click on the file and select Make Link.  Then you can drag that link to your desktop.

### Post by eentonig on 2007-08-28
Right click on a free space on your desktop and select 'Create launcher...'

A new window will open.
Choose 

Type "Application" (Default
En a descriptive name
enter the location for the command to start your app.
