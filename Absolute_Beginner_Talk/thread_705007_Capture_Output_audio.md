### Post by lingnoi on 2008-02-23
> **AmanoAcid said:**
> Is there a way to configure the sound settings so that I can record what I hear through my speakers? (output)

Applications -> Sound & Video -> Sound Recorder

In "record input from" select the channel the sound output is coming from and hit record.

This might need some fiddling around with the settings. If your output channel isn't listed then just try them all and see which one works for you.
