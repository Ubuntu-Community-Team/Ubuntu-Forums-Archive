### Post by Rovdjur on 2007-08-07
Splicing can work if you know which wires are positive and which are negative. I have never personally tried this with broken headphones, but it technically should work with your crappy headphones jack theory.

If you are lucky they could be color-coded. Red for positive, black for negative. If they are not, then guess. Just keep trying until you get it right! It should work eventually. When you find the correct combination, twist them together well and use some electrical tape to tape it up.
