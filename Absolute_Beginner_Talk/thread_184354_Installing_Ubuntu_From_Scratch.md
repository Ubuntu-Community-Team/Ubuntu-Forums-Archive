### Post by mlind on 2006-05-29
[QUOTE=camRW]I don't think I can even get to BIOS.[/QUOTE]

Yes you can. Usually by hitting 'delete' key after your computer has booted, and is
doing its POST stuff. The key or keycombination to BIOS usually flashes when
RAM is beign counted on POST process.

If you can't find the shortcut key(s), consult your system's motherboard manual.
