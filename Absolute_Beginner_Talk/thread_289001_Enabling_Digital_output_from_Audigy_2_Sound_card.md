### Post by cyriq on 2006-11-01
double click on the volume icon, then edit -> preferences. Now you got a list, make sure you have selected audigy analog/digital output jack. Now when you still have the window for the volume controls theres 3 tabs, choose the one in the middle and choose the audigy analog/digital. That should enable the digital output only. And about the tabs, I'm using the swedish version and dont know the english names for the 3 tabs :mrgreen:.
