### Post by solar george on 2007-04-29
You could do this using the standard gnome panels, right click on an existing panel and choose 'New Panel',
Now you can add launcher 'menus' to it using drawers, right click on the panel and choose 'Add to panel' then select drawer, then press add.

You will see a little icon of a drawer which, when you click on it, will open a little menu, you can then add launchers to it as you would to a normal panel.
