### Post by hassan_saeed on 2006-09-19
First make sure you have a modem. :)

Then it is more important to make sure that your modem is supported by linux and is properly installed.

Now goto system --> administration --> networking.

Here you will see a similar to windows (if you have used this thing) dialup dialogue box.

Click propertis of the ppp connection. click enable
then goto modem tab. click autodetect. hopefully it will work.

then type your user , pasword and phone no. then click activate after clicking okay. 
if you want to save all these numbers etc , you have to click create new locatin after making all these changes.

wish you good luck.
