### Post by halfvolle melk on 2006-06-29
Because of [restricted formats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats). The link will provide a way of setting up mp3 support.

However, you could also install XMMS, which can play mp3 right away. As for encoding, ripping to mp3 is possible, but I would recommend ripping to ogg which is an open standard (but can't be used on an iPod unfortunately).
