If needed you can try the vesa drive for the install.

Hit Ctrl-alt-F2 to get to a console 

Enter nano /etc/X11/xorg.conf

scroll down to > Section "Device"
	Identifier	"nVidia Corporation C51G [GeForce 6100]"
	Driver		"nv"
	BusID		"PCI:0:5:0"
EndSection

change to ```
Section "Device"
	Identifier	"nVidia Corporation C51G [GeForce 6100]"
	Driver		[color=blue]"vesa"[/color]
	BusID		"PCI:0:5:0"
EndSection
```

Scroll to the screen section enter your desired resolution

Now restartx

Ctlr-alt-F7 to get to the gui, ctrl-alt-backspace to re-start the gui
