### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-03-27
What is the specific model of your nVidia card?

Have you tried to reverse the order of the screens:  Screen 1 leftof Screen 0?  Perhaps there is a buffer overflow problem.

Also try "topof" and "bottomof" to see if you get correct orientation with these other modes.

It's possible that you are running out of video memory to support your particular configuration.  If that is the case, remove the highest resolution from your mode lines and control-alt-backspace to restart the xserver.

I'm not sure what this mode line does:

Section "Modes"
    Identifier         "MyModes"
    ModeLine     "1280x1024" 116.0 1280 1312 1752 1784 1024 1045 1055 1076
    ModeLine     "1280x1024" 116.0 1280 1312 1752 1784 1024 1045 1055 1076
EndSection

Try removing it and see if you get better twinview response.
