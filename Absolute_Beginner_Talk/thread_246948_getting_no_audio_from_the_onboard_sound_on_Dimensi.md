### Post by snapcase16 on 2006-08-30
I had a similar problem when I first installed Ubuntu. If you go to System/Preferences/Sound you'll be able to see if the computer is aware of the sound card. My computer was confusing my sound card with a webcam I had plugged in through a USB port. I unplugged the camera, selected my sound card, and plugged the camera back in. I haven't had a problem since then. I hope that might help you some.
