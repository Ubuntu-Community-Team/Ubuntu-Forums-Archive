### Post by jimrz on 2007-09-13
[[COLOR="Sienna"]**_NetGear WG511T_**[/COLOR]]("http://www.amazon.com/Netgear-WG511T-Super-http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16833150006&Tpk=wg511tMbps-Wireless/dp/B0000") (the "T" is importanat as other 511 series use different chipsets and some have issues) uses the Atheros 5212 chipset / madwifi driver and has been working beautifully in my old TP 600x from breezy through gutsy tribe 5. I also have a friend who recently purchased one and is getting good results, also. The only small thing is that if you need to use WPA encryption the firmware that the card ships with does not support WPA, downloading the current firmware from the Netgear site and intalling it is quite simple and painless and cures the possible issue. the price in the link is at the top end of your range, but you may be able to find better with a bit of shopping.

EDIT: sorry, mis-read and thought you wanted pcmia, but still look for something with the Atheros 5212 and you should have no issues
