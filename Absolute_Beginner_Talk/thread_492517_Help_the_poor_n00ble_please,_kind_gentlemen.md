### Post by Nekiruhs on 2007-07-04
Its not a "Nightmare" per se. But it is more difficult than an nVidia.You'll have an error to over come with the X series card, but there is a Sticky on that here. And you'll have to use the proprietary ATi Driver via Restricted Drivers Manager (Literally a 3 click process). And you'll have to use XGL for Beryl/Compiz/Compiz Fusion. None of these are insurmountable, or even challenging. Of course the Forum Community is amazing and can help with most problems too. 
Bottom Line: Its harder than with nVidia, but not a nightmare.
Just try the Live CD and see if you can fiddle with it, if not, make a new thread, and we'll be glad to help.
