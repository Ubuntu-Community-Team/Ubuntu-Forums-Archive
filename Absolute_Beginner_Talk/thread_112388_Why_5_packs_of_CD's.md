### Post by AndyCooll on 2006-01-04
The minimum order you can ask for is 5 packs. This is for postage reasons (eg. to qualify the package as a certain type of postal material, and also because it is just as cheap to send one as it is five when posting around the world).

The idea is that you use one pack and pass on the rest to your friends for them to try!
