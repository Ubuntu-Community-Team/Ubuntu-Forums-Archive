### Post by Jucato on 2006-02-25
that little empty box that becomes a chain when clicked?
It's like a chain/link tool. here's an example on how ou use it:
1. Open a tab in konqueror, for our purposes, displaying a directory
2. Split that window/tab. your choice of left-right (ctrl-shift-L) or top-bottom (ctrl-shift-T).
3. in one of the panes, right click the status bar (of course, you should have the status bar displayed) and choose "Lock to current location". This will be your "reference" pane
4. now, in both panes, click that tick box so that both will have chains.

Now, whatever you click in the "reference" pane, the changes will be displayed in the other "linked" pane. You can do this for directories, files, and, yes, even web pages. Kinda nice huh?
