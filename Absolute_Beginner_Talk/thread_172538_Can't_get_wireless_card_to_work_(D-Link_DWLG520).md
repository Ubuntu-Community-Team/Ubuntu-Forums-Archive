I just put this same card in my pc.  I am a newbie too so I may not be the best to answer this question, but mine is working right now, thanks to wireless card you are reading this post.  When you click on system > administration > networking, do you see a wireless card there?  Right now mine says "Wireless connection"  "The interface ath0 is active."  If you see it but it isn't active you can click on the "wireless connection" and then on the little button to the right that says Activate.  Also click on properties and make sure the card is enabled.  Maybe you've done all this, sorry if you have.  I would also have the router turned on.  What kind of router do you have (brand and speed)?  With mine I had trouble getting the security to work.  I had to turn off all security on the router's settings to get ubuntu to activate the card and for me to browse the web.  Let me know how it goes.  Again, I am new too.  But I'd love to help you get your card working.
