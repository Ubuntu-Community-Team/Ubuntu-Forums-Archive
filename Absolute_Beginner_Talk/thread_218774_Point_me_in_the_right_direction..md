### Post by molly_001 on 2006-07-19
First, you've made the right choice in choosing Ubuntu as your virtual dip into Linux waters.  It is incredibly easy and intuitive to use.  As an experienced PC user of any operating system, you won't have any trouble navigating first use.  I can also predict that your Windows machine will see less and less use as you become accustomed to the clarity, control and efficiency of a Linux desktop distribution.

My recommendations:
(1) Do not hesitate to jump in.  Burn the Ubuntu Dapper 6.06 LiveCD, and you will see all (albeit at a slow CD-operated pace) before you.
(2) Install it and start asking questions in the forums.  Every question has been asked before, but that exponentially increases the number of thread readers who will thus know the answers.
(3) Go to getautomatix.com and your desktop distribution will be smoking in a half-hour.
