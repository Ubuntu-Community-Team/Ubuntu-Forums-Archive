### Post by Inxsible on 2007-06-25
10 GB -- /
20 GB -- /home
1GB -- swap
rest of the space -- /shared data for your music movies that can be read from windows too (if you plan to have a dual boot)

### Post by Smandola on 2007-06-26
That'll work... as you will find in a number of the posts, its recommended you make your swap twice the size of your RAM.  I've done this, and usually never use more than 15% of my swap space.
