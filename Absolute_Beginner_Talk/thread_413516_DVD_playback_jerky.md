### Post by Geoff@CRAFT on 2007-04-19
Just a suggestion for those, like me, who have spent hours trying to get Edgy to play DVD's without them being all jerky.  I tried all the proposed solutions  on various forums (different players, libraries and DMA etc. etc...) but to no avail.  I then tried with only the DVD drive connected - previously it had the DVD as master and a CD drive as slave (both on the second IDE channel).  It suddenly decided to playback perfectly!

Don't ask me why this should make a difference, but it did.  It's a Dell Optiplex 700Mhz celeron with 256Mb RAM.  Any ideas why this should happen?

---

### Post by ruibuntu on 2007-04-19
That happened because many MB don't work very well with CD/DVD drive in the same IDE slot. 

Perfectly it should have a CD/DVD drive per slot.

---

### Post by klytu on 2007-04-19
To add to RUIBUNTU's post, also many older motherboards (like mine :-)) use the playback speed of the SLOWEST drive when two are connected to the same IDE cable. When I put my DVD and CD drives on separate IDE cables, a number of annoying problems disappeared.
