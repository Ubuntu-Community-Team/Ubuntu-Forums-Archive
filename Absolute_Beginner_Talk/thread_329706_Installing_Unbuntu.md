### Post by Sef on 2007-01-02
No. It should not take that long.

1) Did you md5sum the download? (If not, then the download could be bad.)

2) Did you burn at 4x or less? (More may corrupt the burn.)

3) If the answer is yes to both questions, then you might want to consider installing with the alternate cd.  It can work where the live cd fails.
