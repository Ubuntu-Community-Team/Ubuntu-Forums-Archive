### Post by Slychilde on 2006-08-26
When I plug my receiver into my sound card, via a mono -> stereo converter, I get the buzzing you are referring to - I believe it is due to a 'dirty' connection through the cabling.  This is most likely because some form of metal is touching the contacts somewhere on the line.  As funny as this sounds, try rotating the jack in your sound card while the volume is high and see if any position gets rid of this.  It does it for me sometime, at least temporarily.

Another thing you may try if you are 100% sure it isn't a physical connection issue is type "alsamixer" into a terminal and set your PCM down to ~75%.  My sound was heavily distorted at all times and this fixed it - it may bring the buzzing down to a non-audible level.
