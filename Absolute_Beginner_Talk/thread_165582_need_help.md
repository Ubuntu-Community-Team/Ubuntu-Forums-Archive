### Post by gingermark on 2006-04-24
The easiest way to remove ubuntu is to format the partition it's on.

To change the amount of hard drive space allocated to each partition you can resize them. I'd suggest deleting the ubuntu partition, resizing the windows one, then reinstalling ubuntu.

Make sure you back up your data though - the process isn't fullproof, especially if you are unsure of what you are doing, and could damage your windows partition.

You could also download a Live CD with a graphical partioner such as QTParted or GParted.
