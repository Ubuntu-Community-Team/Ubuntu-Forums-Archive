### Post by Mr Frosti on 2006-10-05
You can change your saved password in Mozilla Thunderbird the same was as with Mozilla Firefox. Click on "Edit" -> "Preferences" and click on the "Privacy" tab and then "View saved passwords". You then have the option of removing the old password in this view. Once it is removed, the next time you connect to your account, it will ask you to enter a password again.
