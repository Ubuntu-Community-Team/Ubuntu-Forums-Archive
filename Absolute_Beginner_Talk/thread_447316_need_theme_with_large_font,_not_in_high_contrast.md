[Gnome look]("http://www.gnome-look.org/") is your friend for finding themes/fonts that are easier to read, GTK is system themes (affects panels, windows, and fonts), theres also a font section if your hunting for something separate. You can manually change the Font used for the panels and system from System > Preferences > Fonts though, there you can make it anything/as big as you think is needed. Oh and GTK themes from there can be installed from System > Preferences > Themes. 

Hope that helps :)
