### Post by OffAxis on 2008-03-24
> I rebooted PC but now my Local area connection has vanished
from where? The system tray?
Start-->Control Panel-->Network-->Local Area Connection-->Properties-->Show Icon in Notification Area When Connected

Otherwise you can go Start-->Run-->ipconfig /all
to see your settings.
From the 'Network Connections'  in the control panel you can try creating a new network connection, but if your card isn't listed you may need to reinstall the driver for your network card.

> did system restore to a date when it's was working
Never use 'System Restore' when trying to eliminate spyware; a lot of spyware nowadays will attach itself to any restore points that it finds in the system.
