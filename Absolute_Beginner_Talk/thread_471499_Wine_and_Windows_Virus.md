### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-06-12
A Mac OS X virus was discovered and isolated. The person who isolated it put a copy of it online, for security researchers to study.

The next thing you know, there's a second outbreak of this virus. Mac users had found the download link and run it out of curiosity, because they believed that their operating system was completely invulnerable to viruses; even OS X viruses apparantly.

The moral of the story is: Do not EVER try to run malicious files on your real operating system, even if you believe they will not harm your computer! NOT EVEN in a virtual machine unless you are a security researcher and you have checked and double-checked the VM settings. Judging by your question, you are not a security researcher.
