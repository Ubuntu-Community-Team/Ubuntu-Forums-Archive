### Post by viciouslime on 2007-06-22
If by "non copyrighted" you mean it is NOT a commercial DVD, i.e. you or a friend have made it yourself from a collection of family photos, or home videos or something, then you put the disc in, when the icon for it appears on the desktop, just right click it and click copy. Simple.

If you're actually trying to copy a commercial DVD, i.e. one you've bought for errr.... "backup" purposes, or otherwise, then the process is a bit more complicated.
