DRM:  Yes it's locked to iTunes.  Options--Use QTFairUse or Hymn, or burn to cd option and then copy to computer (tedious), and don't buy low quality drm-locked music anymore :P For MSN Music/Janus DRM I think there are some options there but it's a bit more complicated to unlock your files, definitely google this.
