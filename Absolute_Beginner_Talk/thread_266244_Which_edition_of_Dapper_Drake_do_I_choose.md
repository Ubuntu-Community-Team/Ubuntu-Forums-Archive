### Post by petri on 2006-09-27
You can choose both. If **64-bit PC edition** does not work the **PC edition** (386) will.

The 64-bit will give you more speed with your CPU but most of the programs has been written to 32-bit. Take a look at the **64-Bit Processor Users** -forum at here [http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=134](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=134)
