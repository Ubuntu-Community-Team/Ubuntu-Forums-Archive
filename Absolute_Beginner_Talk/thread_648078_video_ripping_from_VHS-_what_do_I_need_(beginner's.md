### Post by bumanie on 2007-12-23
Can't give you lots of specific information, however, I can tell you;
1) You need a video player (obvious) with computer connectivity (av cables, s-video or whatever other connections you may have on your video player and motherboard
2) You need a pci video capture card to convert the video from analogue into digital
3) You need to look at all the ubuntu dvd authoring programs and see which one you would like to use.
I fear this may take you a while to work out. You will have to search the internet and/or forum to find which capture cards work best with ubuntu and then obviously purchase one.
Once you have converted the video it will be a matter of compiling it into your preferred dvd authoring program and convert it to mpeg 2 (dvd) or mpeg 4 (divx/xvid).
Is quite possible to achieve what you want, but I think it will take a bit of searching and trial and error before you get everything working properly.
