### Post by Hallvor on 2007-01-10
Hi, there!

It is impossible to say what is best for you without more information. The protocols are different and the number of files and content differ. Gererally speaking, if you for the most part download large and popular files, I`d stick with Bittorrent. But if you like rare files, like old movies or ebooks, I`d try aMule (with several million users). I use Bittorrent for popular files (fast download), and aMule for rare content I can`t find anywhere else. Downloading files on aMule can be slow, but it you are patient, you will be rewarded. :)

Both apps perform a lot better (faster downloads) if you open the right ports in your firewall.

I personally like bittorrent. In a terminal you can type:

And it will download in a neat clean torrent window.
