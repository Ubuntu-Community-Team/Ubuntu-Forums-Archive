### Post by dwally89 on 2008-02-07
Hi

I upgraded my RAM today from 1GB (2x512MB) to 2GB (2x1GB).

Since then I have noticed that in GParted, it shows all of my hard disk as being unallocated.

How can I make it show all my partitions again?

Thanks

EDIT 1:

Using TestDisk I have found that two of my partitions have a space conflict. How can I resolve this?

EDIT 2:

Turns out Dell puts a MediaDirect partition on certain computers, and after I installed the new RAM, the computer tried to boot using the MediaDirect partition, but failed, so I booted into the MediaDirect OS again, and when I booted back into Ubuntu, tada, GParted displays the partitions :-)
