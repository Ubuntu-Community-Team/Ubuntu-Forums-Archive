so ive finally got my computer to recognize my new hard drive, but now im trying to partition it and its asking me which one of the two partitions do i want.. primary or extended.. which one should i use? i was thinking setting it up as a primary partition, but i dont want to mess anything up :|

### Post by taurus on 2007-04-18
You can have up to 4 primaries.  If you want more then, you need to make one of those 4 primaries as an extended partition.  Then, you can have more logical partitions under that extended partition.  Therefore, use primary if you have less than 4 partitions.
