### Post by Sidewinder1 on 2008-03-30
Welcome CoCo! Yes, it's definately possible. Just right click on you're desktop; than left click on 'change desktop background; if the pic you want is not shown, simply left click on "+add" and navigate to the folder that has your pics and double left click on the one that you want. Then click on it back in 'background' and close. You're done.
HTH
