### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2007-06-20
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/AAC](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/AAC)

Basically, install gstreamer0.10-plugins-bad and gstreamer0.10-plugins-bad-multiverse.  You will need to enable the universe and multiverse repositories.  You can enable them in synaptic by going Settings->Repositories.  After you enable them, you will have to hit the reload button.  Then those two packages should show up.
