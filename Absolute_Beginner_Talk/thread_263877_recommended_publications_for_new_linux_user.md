### Post by ronmarley1 on 2006-09-23
For beginners: "Tux Magazine" at [http://www.tuxmagazine.com/](http://www.tuxmagazine.com/)
It's sent electronically, but prints nicely for offline viewing.  It's free now, but they are gonna start charging for it early next year or something like that.  Once they start charging, I won't hesitate to pay for it.  You can download all previous issues for free.

After you get a handle on things, "Linux Journal", sister publication of "Tux Magazine" at [http://www.linuxjournal.com/](http://www.linuxjournal.com/).  Costs money, but well worth it.

Good luck, and welcome to Ubuntu and Linux!
