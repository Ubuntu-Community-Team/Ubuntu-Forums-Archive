### Post by NilsE on 2008-01-04
As far as the mouse goes, I had both a Logitech and a MS USB mouse and they worked fine. I vaguely remember that it would not work until I was up and running then worked fine on reboot after that.

However, I think the solution applies to both the keyboard and the mouse and that is to get it working after you are up and running then it should work on reboot.

Plug the USB adapter in before booting up then after you are up and running press whatever connect buttons there are on both the mouse/keyboard and the adapters until then synch. Concentrate on the mouse first then the keyboard the next time.

The bottom line is they will work but you may have to play with the USB until it does. Search on "USB mouse" with the quotes and you will find all sorts of recommendations.
