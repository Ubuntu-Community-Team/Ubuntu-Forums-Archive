### Post by benerivo on 2007-08-06
Oh yeh, definitely number 1, i'm not sure what you mean by downloading 7.04 and using an archive manager. I would only suggest that if you have your /home directory on it's own partition that you keep that partition as /home and do not format it during the reinstall. Otherwise back up all your personal files to CD/DVD/USB, or you will loose them all. If you have too many personal files to backup (ie. you have no DVD burner), and your hard drive is large enough, then you may want to try gparted to make a new partition and put them there before reinstalling.
