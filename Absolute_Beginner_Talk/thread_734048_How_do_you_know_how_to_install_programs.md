### Post by Whiffle on 2008-03-24
The difference generally lies between drivers and regular software.  Drivers (like ndiswrapper), like you said, require some configuration to work.  Others, like firefox, just run.  The easiest way to get to know it is to follow and pay attention to some howtos, they generally will show you what all needs to be done to accomplish a task.
