### Post by Pelham123 on 2008-02-12
You are saying that this message is generated by your screen, rather than by the OS? The figures relate to Horizontal and Vertical sync - basically so your screen displays properly. It would help if you could post your xorg.conf file -> type in terminal please...

cat /etc/X11/xorg.conf > xconf.txt

This will generate a file 'xconf.txt' in your current directory and copy the contents of xorg.conf to it. You can then add it as an attachment to your reply, as its usually quite long.
It would also be helpful if you could get a make and model for your monitor - take a look on the back of the monitor by the serial number.
