### Post by jkeyes0 on 2007-04-23
Looks like a fairly decent setup so far. The nVidia video card will make your life a lot easier (ATi cards are notoriously difficult to setup/install). The Intel Wireless card will make things easy as well, as Intel has always been excellent to work with in Linux. 

Most people really only have hardware problems with video cards and wireless (just from personal experience and reading the forums for a while), so it looks like you've covered most of your bases already. Feisty should run very happily under that hardware.
