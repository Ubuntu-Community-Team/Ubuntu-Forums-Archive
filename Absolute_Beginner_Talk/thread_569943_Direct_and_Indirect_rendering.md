### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-10-07
Direct Rendering means that all of the onscreen rendering is handled by the GPU (your graphics card). Direct Rendering is good. 
Indirect Rendering means that all of the rendering is passed off to the CPU. Indirect Rendering is not so good because it clogs up you processor and can slow things down.
