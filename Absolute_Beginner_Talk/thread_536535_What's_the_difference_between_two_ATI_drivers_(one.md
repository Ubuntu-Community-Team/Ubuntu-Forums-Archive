The drivers from ATI have much better performance for me and most other people.  They can be VERY HARD to install correctly.  I have 7 machines using ATI from laptop models to various ATI desktop models.

I hate the ATI drivers during the install process, but love them when it's all working correctly.  I love the repro drivers during the install process, but hate them when it's all over.

Roll up your sleeves and put on your learning cap.  If repro drivers do what you want then leave it be, if not then go to the ATI website and provided they have the driver for your card.....  Well, it's get your hands dirty time at the command line interface (CLI).
