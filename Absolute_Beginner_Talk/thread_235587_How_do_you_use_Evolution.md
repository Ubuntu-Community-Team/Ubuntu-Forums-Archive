### Post by blackened on 2006-08-13
Generally, especially with broadband, but also with dial-up, your isp will allot you an email account, and sometimes sub-accounts. These accounts are accessed by connecting to a pop server the address of which depends on your particular isp. 

The easiest solution would be to contact your isp or go to their webpage's support section (if they have one), and get the server address.

The pop server is for receiving mail. In order to send it you will also need to know the smtp server address. 

ex. pop.sbc-global.net
ex. smtp.sbc-global.net

Hope that's helpful.

To connect to your (I assume) Gmail account you need to open Gmail and go to Settings -> Forwarding and POP and make sure POP is enabled.
