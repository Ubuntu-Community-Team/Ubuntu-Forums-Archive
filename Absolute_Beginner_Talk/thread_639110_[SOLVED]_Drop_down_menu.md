### Post by Bakon Jarser on 2007-12-12
I just did a reinstall of gutsy and have a menu that I created under applications for all my common apps.  I use to have a shortcut to this on the panel next to my firefox quicklaunch.  It showed the folder there and when I clicked on it it would drop down and show the contents of the menu.  I can't figure out how to get it back.  Any ideas would be appreciated.  Thanks!

### Post by rune0077 on 2007-12-12
Try right clicking on the panel, select "Add to Panel", and it should be somewhere on the list.

---

### Post by thelatinist on 2007-12-12
1. Applications > [Your Folder]
2. Right-click anywhere in this menu and choose Entire Menu > Add this as menu to panel.
