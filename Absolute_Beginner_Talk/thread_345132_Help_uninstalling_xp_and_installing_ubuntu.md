### Post by Happy_Man on 2007-01-23
Well, it seems that your immediate problem is the CD not being recognized. Did you reboot with the CD in the drive and set your BIOS to boot from it? That may be what is tripping you up... 
If not, though, simply go through the install, and eventually the installer will ask what you want to do with your hard disk. Simply select the option to use the entire hard drive for Ubuntu, and that will erase your windows automatically!

More then likely there is a splash screen with what key to press at the very bottom.

Anywhoo once in BIOS, there should be the option of change boot order and that should do you right
