### Post by catlett on 2006-06-07
[http://mirror.cs.umn.edu/ubuntu-releases/6.06/](http://mirror.cs.umn.edu/ubuntu-releases/6.06/) Scroll down to "Alternate Install CD"  That is the one that installs without going through the live cd boot. The "pain in the neck" part is your going to have to download another iso. The live cd and the alternate cd are 2 different isos.
