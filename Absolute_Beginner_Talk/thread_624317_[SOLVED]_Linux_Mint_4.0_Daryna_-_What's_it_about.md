### Post by Caffeine_Junky on 2007-11-27
What's it about? ... It is Slick!

[_Mint 4.0 Daryna (Main):_]("http://www.linuxmint.com/")

Well, I DL'ed it about 2hrs ago, from the Torrent link on the Mint Site, ...I cheat when it comes to Torrent DL's , ...I use UTorrent on my windows xp box :D ..... I always use Ubuntu to write my ISO's to Disc tho, ...it is so easy!

The DL speed was great! (plenty of seeders) ...

The install was basically the same as Ubuntu, and went off without a hitch!

The initial setup was very simple, ...mp3 playback is available "out of the box", the Restricted Driver for my nVidia 6100 card was offered to me straight away (same in Ubuntu), ...my internet connection is adsl-modem (I don't have wireless) and was no problem (same with Ubuntu), ... my HP 2110 all-in-one printer/scanner/copier was detected (altho there is no notification icon in the task-bar to show it's connection status), ... my usb thumb drive mounted and opened when it was hot plugged, ..basically the same as Ubuntu on all these items.

The Default Green & Blue theme that you are presented with is as ugly as hell, haven't the dev's at Mint ever heard the saying "Blue & Green should **never** be seen"!?

Compiz-Fusion kicked in as soon as I installed the "Compiz configuration settings manager", I did not even have to open the config' settings and check any boxes, ...accept for wobbly windows, gotta love the wobbles!

I have never liked the "Slab Menu" style (I first came across it in OpenSuse and Mint 3.0 in my first few days on Linux) ...but I am finding it very easy & fast to navigate.

I have **not** found any **bug's**!, it seems to be very slick'n'stable at this point!

I'm very impressed with "Mint Daryna", out of the box the desktop feels/looks more refined than Ubuntu (imo), once I got rid of the Blue title bars that is, ... 

So, if you know your way around Ubuntu you will have no prob's with Mint, ..it must be one of the best Linux distro's going around at the moment I think, **All thanks to Ubuntu **
