### Post by mph66 on 2006-10-20
Your best bet at learning about Ubuntu is right here.  I'm still quite a n00b, but I can tell you that the folks here are really helpful, as are searches of these forums.

Also, get your hands on "Ubuntu for Non-Geeks" by Rickford Grant.
