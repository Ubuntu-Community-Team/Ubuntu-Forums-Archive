### Post by cynvision on 2006-12-30
I believe I have researched an answer.

Go to the big K Menu. In System Tools go to the Accessibility option.
In the "Keyboard Filters" tab, uncheck the "Use slow keys".
In the "Activation Gestures" tab, uncheck the "Use gestures for activating sticky keys and slow keys" and "turn sticky keys and slow keys off after a certain period of inactivity."

check the "Modifier Keys" tab and make sure "Use sticky keys" is unchecked just in case that got turned on. 
Apply changes.

Accessibility is a good thing, but I somehow unintentionally activated slow keys with a gesture and thought KDE was a bust for my computer for a whole week.
