### Post by thomasa93 on 2006-12-30
In the address bar in Firefox, type :  about:config
In the filter bar, begin typing Ipv6 and disable it.  Restart FF and see if your speed is better.  

You can check your speed at:  [www.speakeasy.net/speedtest](www.speakeasy.net/speedtest)

Read in [this]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202838") post on how to disable IPv6
