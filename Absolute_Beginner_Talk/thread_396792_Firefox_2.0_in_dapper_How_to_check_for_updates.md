### Post by david_kt on 2007-03-29
For some of you that has upgraded from firefox 1.5 to firefox 2.0, you will find that you could not check for updates automatically.

The solution is very simple, just open a terminal and type:

[INDENT]gksudo firefox[/INDENT]

key in your password, and you can activate:

[INDENT]help> Check for updates[/INDENT]

But it is not a good idea to surf it this way, as firefox has access to your file system folder. So, after check and updates completed, just close your firefox, and launch it normally. Make sure "check for updates" option is grey out so that your browser could not alter your file system

In windows, this option is available all the time, right? Very convenience..... I mean for hacker and viruses.
