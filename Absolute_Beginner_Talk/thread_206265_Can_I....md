### Post by woedend on 2006-06-29
yes, copy your home directory.(full path is /home/yourusername/
your home directory is full of hidden files and folders that store all of your settings.  Every setting that a program/app saves/remembers...will be here.  If you were to reinstall, then move your home folder back, all of your settings would be there.
As far as apps, I would just backup the .deb files, not the actual applications(dependency tracking would be a PITA this way)
The packages/apps you install should be in the folder
/var/cache/apt/archives
you can copy the debs to an external HD, then in the future just double click them to reinstall them.
