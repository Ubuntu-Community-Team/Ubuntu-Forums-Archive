### Post by Bartender on 2008-02-18
Well, if you don't know what it is, I certainly don't. :)

Right-click on your junk mail folder.  Go into Properties.  Click on the "Retention Policy" tab.  Tell it to "Always delete read messages".  Click OK.  Close Thunderbird, come back, see what happens.  
If that doesn't work, go back to "Retention Policy" tab and look at the "Delete mail after X days" setting.  Look at the weird stowaway e-mail and find out what date you got it.  Do the math, set the "Delete mail after X days" so that it should delete the mail tomorrow.  Click OK, wait and see.
