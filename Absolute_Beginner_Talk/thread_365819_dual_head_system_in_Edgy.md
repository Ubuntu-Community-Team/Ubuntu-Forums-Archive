### Post by dkohen on 2007-02-21
I don't have a ATI, mine's an NVIDIA but I managed to get it to work (and it breaks after every upgrade!).

Looking at the xorg.conf file i think at least part of the problem is you [screen] sections. 

This is what I have it that piece: 

Section "Screen"
    Identifier     "Default Screen"
    Device         "NVIDIA Corporation NV34 [GeForce FX 5200] 1"
    Monitor        "SONY200sx"
    DefaultDepth    24
    Option         "NoLogo"
    Option         "RenderAccel"
    Option         "TwinView" "true"
    Option         "NoTwinViewXineramaInfo" "false"
#    Option         "MetaModes" "1158x1024,1158x1024;1280x1024,1280x1024; 1024x7
68,1024x768; 800x600,800x600"
    Option         "MetaModes" "1152x864,1152x864;1280x1024,1280x1024; 1024x768,
1024x768;800x600;"
    Option         "TwinViewOrientation" "RightOf"
    SubSection     "Display"
        Depth       24
        Modes      "1152x864" "1280x1024" "1024x768"  "800x600" 
    EndSubSection
EndSection

As you can see I only have one screen and it has meta modes that define the total resolution of both the screens together. 

I hope this puts you on the right track.
