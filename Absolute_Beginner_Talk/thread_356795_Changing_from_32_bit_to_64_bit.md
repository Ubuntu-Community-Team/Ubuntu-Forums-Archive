### Post by hawksbill on 2007-02-11
Like Pay said you would have to do a dual boot since you cannot mix up 32 and 64bit packages. I think your best bet would be to back up and then do a clean install. You might want to consider that although 64bit Ubuntu runs well, you may run into a few problems here and there. As well unless you need 64bit specific applications there isn't that much of a performance gain to justify doing a new install.
