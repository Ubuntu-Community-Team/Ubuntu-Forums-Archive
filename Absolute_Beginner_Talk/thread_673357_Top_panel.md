### Post by mnemosyne on 2008-01-20
Right click the panel and click 'Add new item' or something similar. Drag an item named 'System Tray' to the top bar. If an item called system tray is already there (usually it will just display network status), you don't have to do anything. At least that's how it works on Xubuntu.
