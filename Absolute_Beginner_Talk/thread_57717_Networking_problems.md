### Post by nad on 2005-08-19
The first problem is solved with a "One Port Switch", or a crossover cable. Both readily available. Just make sure that both computers are on the same subnet and have a blast.

A common denominator of the second bunch is smb/cifs networking.

Please see [www.samba.org](www.samba.org) as well as dozens of threads here (the guide has already been written as well as hundreds of books on the subject).
