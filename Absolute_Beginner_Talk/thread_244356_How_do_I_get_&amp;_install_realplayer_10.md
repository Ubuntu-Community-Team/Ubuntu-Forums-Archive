### Post by BlueLava on 2006-08-26
Hi thibeaz, Thanks for replying,
I didnt expect such a quick reply & found another way to get it.
For anyone interested I clicked on support at the top of this page, on the page displayed click on 'Documentation' - next page click 'ubuntu desktop', then at the top of that page choose 'community docs' page - under the heading 'Installing & using software' click 'multimedia' - next page click 'restricted formats', then under the heading 'real media' click 'realplayer' then 'deb package. This downloads & installs realplayer 10 & it now appears in the synaptic package manager.
Maybe this is a long way of going about it though.
