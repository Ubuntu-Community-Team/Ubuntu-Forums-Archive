> I guess i just want information on process of installing softwareWell, most of the time you don't actually need to download and install programs. There's a huge list of programs already lined up in the repositories, ready to be installed with Synaptic.
Take a look at this site:
[http://www.ubuntuguide.org/](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/)
and in particular, look at the extra repositories.
There's a 5.10 version out there somewhere, but I forgit the url. :roll:

What is it you're trying to install? If it's not in the repos, you need to either compile it from source (not recommended for new users) or download a .deb package and use dpkg to install it.

But look in synaptic first.
