### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-07-22
I think you made a mistake with the title...

Anyways, his wireless probably isn't supported out of the box (a common issue unfortunately).  If you can get info on what kind of wireless your friend is using, you can post it here and also check the list of supported wireless cards for a thing called ndiswrapper [http://ndiswrapper.sourceforge.net/joomla/index.php?/component/option,com_openwiki/Itemid,33/id,list/](http://ndiswrapper.sourceforge.net/joomla/index.php?/component/option,com_openwiki/Itemid,33/id,list/)

You can also try to install the driver yourself [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_install_Windows_Wireless_Drivers_.28Ndiswrapper.29](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_install_Windows_Wireless_Drivers_.28Ndiswrapper.29)

It probably looks daunting but we can help you through it :-)  So be sure to ask questions if you don't know what to do/where to begin
