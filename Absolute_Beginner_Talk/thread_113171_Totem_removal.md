### Post by Turtle.net on 2006-01-05
To change the behavior of your system when you insert a DVD just go to System->Preferences->removeable media (I'm not sure of the exact title since my system is in french...but you will find :))
Then in multimedia change totem by gxine (i've done the same think with vlc, worked like a charm).
Removing totem will not change this action for you, you have to do it manually.
