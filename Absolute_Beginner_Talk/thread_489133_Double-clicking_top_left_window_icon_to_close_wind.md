### Post by nanotube on 2007-07-01
here are a few ways you can close a window (probably there are some more i don't know about...):
alt-f4
ctl-w
alt-space, then 'c' (alt-space opens the window menu)
right click /anywhere/ on the task bar, and select close from the menu
and finally, 
click on the little x button on the top right hand side.
i hope one of these will make you happy. :)
