### Post by matthew on 2005-08-17
A GDM theme is to customize the login screen rather than the entire desktop interface.

I put all my login themes in one folder in my home directory, all my desktop themes in another, all my background images in another. They don't take up a lot of space, so I figure I will just keep them all.

Good place to find all these:
[http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/)
