### Post by paydaydaddy on 2007-02-18
From your xorg.conf info came this:

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier     "Generic Monitor"
    HorizSync       28.0 - 51.0
    VertRefresh     43.0 - 60.0
    Option         "DPMS"
EndSection

In that section you need to make sure that the values listed for HorizSync and VertRefresh are the exact values that are listed for your monitor. Find the correct values in your monitors owner's manual or look them up online. Sounds like you know how to edit so I won't go into that.
