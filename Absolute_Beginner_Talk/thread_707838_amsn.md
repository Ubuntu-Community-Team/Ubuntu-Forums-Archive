### Post by lswest on 2008-02-26
By default amsn groups tabs in windows according to what group the contacts are in within your contact list (e.g. say you had a group of "Friends" and a group of "Colleagues")  Well, every chat you open with people from "Friends" would be in one window, and the other contacts in another window.  However, to disable this you can go to **preferences** and under the **"Session" tab** you can (once you scroll down)  you'll see a section "**select how to handle multiple message windows**" and select "one window for all contacts in the contact list" instead of the "**one window for all contacts from the same group**" or "**normal windows without tabs**" (my preference :P).  Hope i cleared it up.
