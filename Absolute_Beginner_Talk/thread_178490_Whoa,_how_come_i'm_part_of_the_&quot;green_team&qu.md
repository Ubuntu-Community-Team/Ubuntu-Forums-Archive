### Post by kassetra on 2006-05-17
[quote=erik1397]I never got a message or promotion or something in the forums, i'm all of a sudden on the green team! i mean, don't get me wrong, i think it's cool to belong to a team, but someone please explain to me what it is!?[/quote] 
LOL I knew that would happen.

The way the green team is set up works like this - 

When you work very hard in the beginner talk area, you are noticed. The top three people every three months get promoted to green team, similar to how beanies of the month work. Semi-rotational. Now, if you want to stay a green team member, you just have to keep on working hard! :D

At least, that's the current scheme of the green team. The idea being that green team people help introduce new users into both Linux and the forums, and who best to do that than the people that are helping out like crazy-go-nuts! :)

*p.s. green team is the only team that works like this, because it's designed to give all new users the chance to be new, learn, and then help other in return, and be rewarded for doing so.  :D  The other teams all have very broad duties, but the green team is very specialized.  :D
