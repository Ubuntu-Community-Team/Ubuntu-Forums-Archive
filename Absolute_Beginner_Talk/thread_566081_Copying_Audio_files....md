### Post by dwhitney67 on 2007-10-03
Niloc,

If I were you I would use SoundJuicer to rip MP3s of the tracks on the CD that you wish to store on your PC.  MP3 files occupy less space than the uncompressed music files on a CD.

GrumpyBob is right -- you can configure SoundJuicer to specify which format you want to rip your tracks into (default is oog), where to place the ripped tracks, and which tracks you want.

I think it is a great tool because of it's simplicity and easy of use.

P.S.  You might need a plugin for SoundJuicer to rip MP3 tracks.
