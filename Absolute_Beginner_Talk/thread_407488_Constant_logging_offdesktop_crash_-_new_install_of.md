### Post by Brendantb on 2007-04-12
Hi, 

I am a linux newbie, like yourself, but I think that the constant crashing may be due to the lack of adequate RAM. I am running Xubuntu 6.10 on a laptop of much the same spec as yours, and it runs well on 196mb ram. You can get a second hand stick of RAM on ebay for your machine for about one pound plus postage, so it would seem silly not to buy some. 

There are other linux distributions for low specced systems, I'm thinking of Puppy Linux, or Damn Small Linux. But these are not as popular as the Ubuntus, and thus there isn't as much support. They also require more linux know how to configure, and there are fewer made up packages of software. Xubuntu is a great newbies choice. 

Sorry I can't be of any more immediate help...
