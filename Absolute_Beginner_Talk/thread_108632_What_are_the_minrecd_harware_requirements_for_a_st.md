### Post by az on 2005-12-26
It is pretty low-end.

You would benefit from a big performance improvement by adding more ram.  The minimum recommended ram is 128 megs.  

You need at least 2.2 gigs of hard drive space and processor speed depends on what you can tolerate.  I find 300-400 Mhz to be the tolerance threshold.  YMMV.


You can install icewm or xubuntu-desktop to try lighter desktop environments.

(install icewm and menu, since icewm is pretty useless without menus.  Xfce (xubuntu) has it's own menu system)
