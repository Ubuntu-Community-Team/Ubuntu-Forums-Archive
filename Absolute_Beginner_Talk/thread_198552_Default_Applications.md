### Post by m_pav on 2006-06-23
Right click the file you want to play, click on "Properties", then the "Open With" tab, select your preferred application if it's already there, or click the "Add" button to select it from a list, then make sure the radio button for your preferred application is checked and click on Close.

Have you tried vlc for your vids?
