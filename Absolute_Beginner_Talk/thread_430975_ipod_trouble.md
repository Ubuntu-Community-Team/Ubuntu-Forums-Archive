### Post by misconfiguration on 2007-05-02
Plug your iPod in, it should automagically mount to /media which will display on your desktop.

Open GTKpod and it should ask you to setup your iPod, point the default mount DIR to the directory of your iPod if you select 'browse' you should see the iPod in the menu you may have to go to the dir of the actual iTunes DB file but I didn't have to do this.

Select your iPod model, on the back of it in very small print you'll see it, make sure you select the proper one, it's crucial. You should see everything start to mount, depending on the size of your iPod DB, this could take up to several hours. 

Another way you can do this is, click on the button 'ADD DIR' and locate your mounted iPod this was as well. Then point to the proper DIR within the iPOD. I accept emails so check my profile and shoot me a message if you need more info (I'm at work right now so this is all from memory).
