### Post by Sef on 2006-04-10
To have your live cd boot, you'll have to go into your BIOS and set it to boot from the cd rom first.  To go into the BIOS, you need to press usually, either F2, Delete, ESC or sometimes other.   When you first boot up, it will say to go into the BIOS, push x.  

Don't change anything else, as your system could end up being unable to boot up.

Also you may have a button for a one time boot.  If so, push that and highlight the cd-rom. (On my Dell laptop, it is F12.)
