### Post by qpieus on 2007-10-26
Yes, rockbox is linux. I put rockbox on my ipod a month ago and love it. Yes, there are lots of themes, games, etc. And it can play lots of formats, as you mentioned. I does use just a regular filesystem structure, so you just have to copy songs into a folder and you're good to go. The other nice thing about it is that you can load the rockbox firmware without overwriting the apple firmware - so you can boot into the apple firmware later if you want.

I only have a couple of complaints about it. One, battery life is definitely not as long as with the apple firmware. Two, it acts weird when I use the AC adapter to charge it. Under the apple firmware, I can browse the menus, play songs, and it will shutdownand just take the charge  if I don't touch it. Under rockbox, when charging with AC, the ipod continually reboots and won't just shut down. It'll still take the charge, but I don't like that rebooting business. So I just charge it through the computer usb connection.
