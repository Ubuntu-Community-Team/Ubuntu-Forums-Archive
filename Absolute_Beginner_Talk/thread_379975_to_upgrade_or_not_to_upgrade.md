### Post by Famicommie on 2007-03-09
If you already have everything the way that you want it, there really isn't a reason to upgrade *just yet*. Edgy should be just as secure and have just as good a repository as Feisty will be so far as I'm aware.

However, Edgy isn't a LTS, or Long Term S-omething release. That means that they will stop supplying Edgy users with updates after a particular time period.

Dapper will continue to be maintained until 2009. Edgy will only be maintained until April 2008. So, no need to worry about updating until April 2008, which should be the month that a brand new LTS release will come out anyways ^_^
