### Post by lisati on 2007-07-06
First question: are you having trouble on receivng or sending email?

Fore receiving, perhaps you need to reset the "authentication type" for your email account. From within Evolution, choose Edit->preferences. Then click on the email account you're having trouble with, and click on "Edit".  Under "Authentication Type" on the "Receiving options" click on "Check for supported types."

For sending, you might need to uncheck "Server requires authentication" - some ISPs require it, others don't
