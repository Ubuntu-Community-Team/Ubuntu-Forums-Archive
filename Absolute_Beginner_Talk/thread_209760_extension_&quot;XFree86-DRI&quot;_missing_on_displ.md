### Post by richbarna on 2006-07-06
Have you edited your xorg config file?

You do this by opening your terminal and typing:-
> gksudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf

Below is an example of a xorg file with "dri" enabled, I have highlited 3 parts, It's not the same card as yours but you can compare it with your xorg.conf file.
