### Post by MyrddinWyllt on 2008-02-20
Eclipse is one of the big ones. I think KDevelop is also a big one, and may be installed already (or easy to get with your package manager).

You may have g++ installed, which is simply the C++ compiler (and if I recall correctly, the compiler on which Dev C++ was built).  If not, it should also be fairly easy to get with Synaptic. Grab the compiler, write your program in your favorite text editor, then pop into the CLI and let g++ do its work.

The term you are looking for is IDE (Integrated Development Environment, I believe) you can pop on google and look up Ubuntu C++ IDE if you don't get a suggestion you like here.

Welcome to the Linux world!
