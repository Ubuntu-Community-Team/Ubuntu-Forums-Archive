### Post by BobCFC on 2007-06-23
100% go with NVIDIA.   They actually help you with regular new Linux drivers .

I can recommend the 320mb Geforce 8800gts as a good bang4buck card.  I play Fear, Infernal, Rainbow Six Vegas on max but then I don't go above 1680x1050.   If you want super-high resolutions you will need more video memory

Works flawlessly with Beryl etc.  im even using twinview on gnome with different resolutions on each monitor - works great I can wobble a moving video from left to right screen with no slowdown.  New Linux drivers every few weeks.
