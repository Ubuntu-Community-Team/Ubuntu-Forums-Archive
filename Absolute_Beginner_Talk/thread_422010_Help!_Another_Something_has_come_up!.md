### Post by Sef on 2007-04-25
Abiword could be able to do it.  When installing, you have to check it to read Claris works.  When installing, it asks you about file types, just click Claris, and see if that will work.

To get Abiword:  Applications > Add/Remove > search > type in Abiword > check Abiword > click apply > click apply > follow the directions to finish.

If it does not ask you, then follow TimelessRouge's directions to get the Claris plug-in.  Once you are in synaptic > search abiword > click on the plug-ins.
