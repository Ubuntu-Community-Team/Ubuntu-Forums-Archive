### Post by erginemr on 2008-04-04
The fellows who are having problems with Flash, how did you install it? The first time you try to visit a Flash enabled page, Firefox interrupts with a message and suggests installation of either Adobe Flash or Gnash (OpenSource alternative to Flash). The latter is still being developed and may not perform well if installed.

Besides, if you have installed Flash from this dialog or from Synaptic, you may also consider installing it directly from Adobe web site to see if it makes any difference.
