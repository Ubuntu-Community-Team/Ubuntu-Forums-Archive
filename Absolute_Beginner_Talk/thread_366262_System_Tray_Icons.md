### Post by mahiyar on 2007-04-05
Once my gnome panel went berserk and I did this to get the default gnome panel. Remember that this will do away with all the additional shortcuts that you might have put.
 rm -r ~/.gconf/apps/panel  in a terminal or move it to another folder, log out of gnome "Alt + BKspace". Relog in gnome and you will have brand new default panels.
