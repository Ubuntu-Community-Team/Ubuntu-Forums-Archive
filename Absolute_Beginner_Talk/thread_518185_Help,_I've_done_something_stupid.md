First: it wasn't you. It's a 10 year old machine that gave its last. (although I suspect the problem is 'just' the DC jack)

Second: if it's just  the files you want, you can always pull  the hard drive out and connect it do another PC. (Requires either a cheepie IDE pin adapter, or a more robust 2.5" USB enclosure). You can pick up one or the other locally.
