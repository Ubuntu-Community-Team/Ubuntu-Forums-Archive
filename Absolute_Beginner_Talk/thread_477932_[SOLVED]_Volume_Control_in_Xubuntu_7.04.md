### Post by wormser on 2007-06-18
If you are running Gnome then in the upper right hand corner next to the clock there is a little speaker icon.  Click on it to adjust the volume and double click on it for the mixer controls.  For Xubuntu check near the clock.

Another method is through the command line.

```
alsamixer
```
