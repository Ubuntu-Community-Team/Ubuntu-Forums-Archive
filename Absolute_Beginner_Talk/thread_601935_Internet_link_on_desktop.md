### Post by eqqfooyoung on 2007-11-03
This works for me:

[LIST=1]
[*]Right click your desktop
[*]Choose "Create Launcher"
[*]Type = Application
[*]Name = gmail (or whatever you want)
[*]Command = firefox [www.gmail.com](www.gmail.com)
[*]Click OK
[/LIST]
