### Post by tesserhex on 2007-11-26
Hi,

I'm assuming this is in OpenOffice.

Go to (in Word Processor)

[INDENT]Tools> Options[/INDENT]

In Language Settings, select Writing Aids

Select the dictionary you want to work with and click 'Edit'

Select the word you want to delete, then click the Delete button.

Confirm and exit...

Should do it.
