### Post by ambre on 2008-02-06
Hi Marius,

Just a word from a fellow 'sufferer'.

Think back to the time when you saw/used Windows for the first time.  Frustrating or what? Inadvertant mouse clicks, things disappearing, blue screen of dead, system reboots - well now you are more advanced than that and once your system settles down to the way you want it, it will run for years without any intervention.

LINUX is very different to Windows and unfortunately there is a steep learning curve.  The Terminal IS as scary as DOS but it will become your friend.  You will be amazed at what control you have over the system from there.  GUI's are getting better, but still not up to Windows standard (IMHO).  The big plus for me is that the foundations are right.  Windows for the masses - Yes, but the foundations are flakey.

Folks around here are helpful and friendly.  They don't always have the answers, but then who does.

Don't lose too much sleep - life's too short.  Stick with it - if Windows can do it, LINUX will do it better.

Excuse the rant,
