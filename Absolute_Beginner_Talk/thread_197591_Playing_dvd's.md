### Post by Arnaud_B on 2006-06-16
u could just use mplayer or xine-ui it works great and is quite light after for playing dvd you need libdvdread3 (should be installed already) and you do :
$sudo /usr/share/doc/libdvdread3/examples/install-css.sh
check [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats) for more info 
Good luck!
A.
PS: if you want it very light you can just use mplayer in the command line and it will open a window to play dvd, you just have to know a couple of shortcut after but it's quite simple and so much faster than any other player :-) 
for instance to play a dvd:$mplayer dvd://1
