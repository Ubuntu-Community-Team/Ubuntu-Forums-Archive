### Post by crispy_420 on 2007-04-17
I like Linux Format. It is out of the UK so it does cost a good chunk of money here in the US. But I think it is worth it. It has good reviews in the front, both software and distro. In the back it has nice tutorials.

As far as the latest distro/news. Kind of hard for a magazine and the ever changing face of linux. Even what you read right now is old news in a few days. And magazines are always a month behind.
