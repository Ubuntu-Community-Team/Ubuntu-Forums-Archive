### Post by Cresho on 2008-02-21
it never left your pc.  it is in /usr/share/backgrounds if you hover your mouse long enough, it will tell you what the file is and tell you where exactly the location is.  so when you go to that directory, just drag and drop it into the appearance preference under background and you restored your lost wallpaper
