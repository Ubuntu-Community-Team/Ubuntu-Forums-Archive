### Post by annda on 2007-06-02
check all the capture/record options in volume control.

you can see if it's working by trying to adjust the mic level in playback. if it's ok, you can disable it again and play with the capture sound level for mike. make sure you have the mic capture switch enabled - if it is relevant for your sound card. ALSA has different options for all the sound cards, so my advice is based on guessing your settings...
