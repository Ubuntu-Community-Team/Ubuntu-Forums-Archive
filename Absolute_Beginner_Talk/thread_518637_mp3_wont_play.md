### Post by 505 on 2007-08-06
You need the correct libraries to play MP3 files. Because of licence restrictions there are not on the CD-ROM. So you need to find a computer with Internet access, [download the files](http://packages.ubuntu.com/dapper/libs/gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly) and transfer them to your computer. Once installed, you can play MP3's. Also check if you have all the packages that depend on this file. You need to download and install these first.
