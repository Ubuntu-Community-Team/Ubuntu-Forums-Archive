### Post by jordanmthomas on 2006-12-10
I don't even know that there exists a current distribution that will run on 640 kb of ram.  Certainly any mainstream distro will have a kernel that won't even be able to load in such a space.  Good luck, but you might be better off installing DOS rather than trying to find some *very* old version of linux.
