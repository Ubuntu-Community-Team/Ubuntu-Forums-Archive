### Post by spiderbatdad on 2008-02-22
Are you saying that The desktop loads with no login screen, and you would like to set a login screen, or do you want to change the password of your user account.

In the first case, navigate to System>>Administration>>Login Window>>Security...uncheck the automatic login box.

In the second case, open the terminal in your account and enter:  passwd
