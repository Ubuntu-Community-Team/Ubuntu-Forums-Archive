### Post by pondochris on 2007-10-16
I had the same problem I tried and tried to make the background black but it wouldn't change. In skydome settings you probably have a skydome image selected. If you disable the image you should get the desired result.

### Post by Yexo on 2007-10-16
Thx for your answer. I finally found the solution: I had to disable "reflection for windows" under the reflection plugin.
