I don't know of any software but can recommend YouTube as well as Google for finding lessons and instructional vids...

The most important thing to remember is that it's not going to happen overnight, and that you should put in at least 2 hours each day, 5 days a week for optimum results.

Try not to get caught up in the "Speed" lessons and those other tricky scams, because in the end, all they're doing is showing you how to play a certain scale a certain way, and they don't focus on the fundamentals that will allow you to create your own style.

Take it slow, read, listen and practice as much as you can and pick 3 songs you'd like to have completely down in 6 months as a goal.

Take Care and Good Luck!
:guitar:

P.S... Get a metronome!
