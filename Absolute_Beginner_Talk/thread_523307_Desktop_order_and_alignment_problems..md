### Post by p_quarles on 2007-08-11
I was able to achieve a customized alignment pattern by tweaking things until it looked right. The same method might work to get the spacing correct on your desktop.

What I did: unchecked "Keep Aligned," and then moved the icons as close as I could to where I wanted them. Then I turned the alignment back on. It took a few rounds to get it exactly how I wanted it, but it worked.
