### Post by ThinkBuntu on 2007-06-10
Use the [gparted]("http://gparted.sourceforge.net/") LiveCD (about 50MB) to safely resize partitions. Read over their guides (or skim over...it's fairly intuitive anyway) and you'll be set. I can say that I've had success with GParted, resizing partitions while preserving my data and rearranging partitions. It's a great piece of software.
