### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-11-09
When you save the file in Firefox, there will be an icon on your desktop with the same name underneath it.  Move your mouse cursor over this icon and depress the button.  While holding the button down, move your cursor onto the appearance properties window.  Once there, let go of the mouse button.
