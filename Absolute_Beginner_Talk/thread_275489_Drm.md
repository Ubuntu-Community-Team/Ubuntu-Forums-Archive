### Post by daxelrod on 2006-10-11
Just keep in mind, the files you bought from the iTunes music store are [AAC files]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Advanced_Audio_Coding"), not MP3 files.

That said, the probably least murky thing to do legally (at least in the US) would be to use iTunes to burn everything to Audio CDs, and then rip those to whatever format you like on your Linux PC. (Note that this may result in a slight loss of quality, depending on how you rip them.)

However, the usual disclaimers apply. I am not a lawyer, this is not legal advice.
