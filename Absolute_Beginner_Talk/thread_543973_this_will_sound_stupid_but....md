### Post by badguyanil on 2007-09-05
For the icons on your desktop..........
1. Press alt+F2. 
2. Type "gconf-editor"
3. in the configuration editor browse to apps > nautilus > desktop
4. Select the check boxes on the right side pane of all icons you want on your desktop!
