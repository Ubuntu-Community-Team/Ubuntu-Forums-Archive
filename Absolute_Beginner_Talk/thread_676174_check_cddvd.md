### Post by gvartser on 2008-01-23
point you web browser to .

[http://support.dell.com/support/topics/global.aspx/support/product_support/product_support_central?~ck=ln&c=us&l=en&lnki=0&s=gen](http://support.dell.com/support/topics/global.aspx/support/product_support/product_support_central?~ck=ln&c=us&l=en&lnki=0&s=gen)

The service tag is found on the bottom of your laptop.

When you found your model it will show you what kind of HW it is made of.
