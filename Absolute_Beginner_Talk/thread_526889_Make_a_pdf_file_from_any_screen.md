### Post by xminion on 2007-08-16
imagemagick has a program "convert"


[http://imagemagick.org/script/index.php](http://imagemagick.org/script/index.php)

you may have it on your computer already

command:
convert yourimage.jpg topdf.pdf

thats it.

also for many pics to one pdf do:

convert *.jpg big.pdf
