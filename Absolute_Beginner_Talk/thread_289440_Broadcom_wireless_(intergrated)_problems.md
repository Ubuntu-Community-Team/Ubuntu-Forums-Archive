### Post by Paul133 on 2006-10-31
OK. Then you have Dapper, since Edgy's not out yet. I think you have a Broadcom 4318. To check, go into system - administration - device manager and look around for something that says 802.11 or Broadcom or wireless and it should give you your chipset, but I think on a 7330GZ, it's a Broadcom 4318. Search the forums for "Broadcom 4318" and you should find something. Good luck. For the record, I have a Broadcom 4306, and I used this tutorial: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=201902&highlight=broadcom+ndiswrapper](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=201902&highlight=broadcom+ndiswrapper)
