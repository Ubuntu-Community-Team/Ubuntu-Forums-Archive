### Post by Chris S. on 2007-09-11
Do you have the proper ttf or xfonts package installed?  Open up Synaptic and search for "arabic" in "name and description".  Among the packages found should be ttf-arabeyes and a few xfonts packages (xfonts-intl-arabic, xfonts-efont-unicode, xfonts-efont-unicode-ib).  I'm thinking you'll need one or more of those xfonts packages.
