### Post by fedira on 2007-09-24
First right-click on your Home folder, and go to "Share Folder." Then change it to "Do not share" in the dialog box. 

Then right-click on your Pictures folder, right-click, and go "Share Folder." Select "Windows Network (SMB" in the dialog box.
