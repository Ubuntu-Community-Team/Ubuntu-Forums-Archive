### Post by RobMunda on 2007-07-29
I'm somewhat new to Ubuntu myself. Since I switched over from Windozen't XP, I have tried gaim, kopete, gyachi, and a couple others I think. With all of them, I found that file sending to friends of mine who were using the Windows version of Yahoo IM was not possible. I could receive files from them, but sending always failed.

However I tried Pidgin IM, which is what gaim has graduated to (if I understand correctly). The file sending works just great for both Yahoo and AIM. (I haven't connected to the other chat networks, so I can't give you any info about them.) 

So, in other words, if you don't send files to your friends, any of the above will work just fine. If you are planning to share files with your friends you should give Pidgin a try. You can find it at:

[http://pidgin.im](http://pidgin.im)

Hope this helps.
-Rob.
