### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-03-12
When you are running the Live CD it is an operating environment all on its own. The reason that you can't get access to your hard drive is that it is locked, to protect your current installation on the hard drive from being damaged.

I suggest that you don't try anything fancy to try and access things, or you may hose your current installation.

The Live CD is just a toe in the water look at Ubuntu and should be used that way.
