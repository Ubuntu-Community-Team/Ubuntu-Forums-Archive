### Post by mscman on 2006-07-20
Not sure about the Wobbly effect, since I don't use it (too much for me). For the darkening desktop/blurry windows, your problem lies in the Scale plugin. Open gset-compiz and click the 'Plugins' button. Then click on the 'Scale' tab. On this dialogue, you want to uncheck all of the boxes under "Select hot corners". That should fix your problem.
