### Post by ryanVickers on 2007-09-28
You could try manDVD ([getdeb.net]("http://www.getdeb.net/")).  I've never used it because it's not what I've wanted for my projects so far, but it has come pretty good features.  Also, try QDVDAuthor.

manDVD and DeVeDe all convert the video into the format (thus reducing the quality), but QDVDAuthor simply makes VOB files out of it (but it doe not need to convert some how :)), and it's quite capable of lots of good features, like subtitles! :)
