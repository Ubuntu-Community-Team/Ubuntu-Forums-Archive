### Post by benfindlay on 2006-09-01
If it is powered but doesn't have a drive letter, try going into Management (right click on my computer and choose Manage, then go to Disk Management. Your cd drive(s) will be listed at the bottom of the right hand section in here, and you can assign a drive letter to them.

Or is it not powering up at all? (i.e. does the drive refuse to open/close when pressing the eject button?)
