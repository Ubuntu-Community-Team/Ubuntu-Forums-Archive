### Post by mackinax on 2006-07-02
Whenever I connect my USB DAP, rhythmbox automatically starts and reads the contents...

How can I disable this ''feature'' ?

---

### Post by Jagot on 2006-07-02
System -> Preferences -> Removable Drives and Media

Then go to the Multimedia tab and untick the Portable Music Players box at the bottom.
