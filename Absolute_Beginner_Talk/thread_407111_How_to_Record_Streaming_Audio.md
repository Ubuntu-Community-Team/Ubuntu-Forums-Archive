### Post by jmrichky on 2007-04-11
I use Streamtuner and Streamripper.  It works beautifully.  All you need to do is: 1. install them from the repositories  2. start streamtuner and select the stream of your choice  3. click the record button in the top left of the window.  The console will open and display exactly what Streamripper is doing.
