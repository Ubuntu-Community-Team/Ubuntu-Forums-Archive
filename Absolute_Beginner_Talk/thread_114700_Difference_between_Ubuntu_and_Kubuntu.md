Both are different engines. While ubuntu, kubuntu and xubuntu, like all versions of linux, use the linux kernel, kde and gnome are different desktop environments. while you could probably skin gnome to look like kde, or kde to look like gnome, they are both built on different toolkits. That's why skins from one desktop environment are not usable in another...
