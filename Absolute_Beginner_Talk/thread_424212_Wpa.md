### Post by ronmarley1 on 2007-04-26
> **Tucatts said:**
> Anybody had any luck with WPA encryption for their wireless router in Feisty?
Yes.  We have Cisco Aeronet here at my school.  I just clicked on Network Manager and Connect to Other Wireless Network...  Since the SSID is not broadcast, I just typed in the network name, selected the security type (WPA2 Personal), typed in the password given by our network admin. and selected the type (TKIP).  The Keyring manager asked for my password, I entered it, and I was connected a few seconds later.
