### Post by houstonbofh on 2007-02-08
System --> Administration --> Software Sources
Click on the Internet Updates tab.  You can set what you want to see, and how often it will check.

However, what you don't see here are the many people who have no problem with updates.  I am checking Security, Recommended, and Backported updates daily.  The only ones I hold back a day or so are kernal updates.  All of the major issues have been kernal updates on binary video drivers, or compiled software.
