### Post by testube_babies on 2007-07-18
I think everyone would agree with dougfractal on this one for many reasons:

1) Most of the 5.10 repositories are down, so you won't be able to find the linux-restricted-modules package to begin with.
2) 5.10 is nearly 2 years old; most Ubunteros have been using Network Manager for quite some time, and probably won't remember how to set up networking the "old" way.
3) You can get version 7.04 shipped to you for free if you can't download and burn it: [http://shipit.ubuntu.com](http://shipit.ubuntu.com)
4) EVERYTHING is more user-friendly in 7.04, and it looks better too :)
