### Post by 23meg on 2007-03-25
What exactly is the problem you have? The default driver ("nv") is the Free one, but the proprietary driver made by Nvidia supports your card as well. You can download it from their website and install manually, or use Envy, a script that makes installation easier for beginners; do a forum search and you'll find it.
