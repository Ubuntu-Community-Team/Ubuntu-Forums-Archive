### Post by w4ett on 2007-07-17
Well, I believe you might be expecting too much from that legacy video card.   System response is directly related to the following factors:

CPU Speed
Ram Installed
Video Card VRam/Speed
IDE Speed ie: 66,100 or 133
Available space in swap.

You might be just bumping your head at the highest capabilities of your system.

Recommendations:

1.  Consider using XFCE as your desktop manager.
2.  Upgrade your video card
3.  Add more memory
