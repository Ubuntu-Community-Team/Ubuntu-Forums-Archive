### Post by tubegeek on 2007-04-27
I made a Perl script that does something Golden-Section-related that I'd be happy to share with you. (But it's not a graphics tool.)

The script calculates successive pairs of Fibonacci numbers and displays them, as well as the ratio between them and the difference between this ratio and the irrational Golden Section ratio. The Fibonacci ratio converges on the Golden Ratio pretty quickly and alternates between too high and too low, and this makes an interesting demo. The script will keep outputting numbers to a terminal window as long as you run it; when you stop it with Control-C you will have a terminal window full of the successive iterations which you could cut and paste into a spreadsheet or whatever.
