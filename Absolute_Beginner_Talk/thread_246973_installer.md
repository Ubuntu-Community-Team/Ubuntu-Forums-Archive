### Post by monktbd on 2006-08-30
The file to download should be called something like:

kubuntu-6.06.1-alternate-i386.iso 
ubuntu-6.06.1-alternate-i386.iso 
xubuntu-6.06.1-alternate-i386.iso 

depending on the flavour you want.

depending where in the world you are you can get it from
[http://ubuntu-releases.cs.umn.edu//kubuntu/6.06.1/](http://ubuntu-releases.cs.umn.edu//kubuntu/6.06.1/)
or go to
[http://www.ubuntu.com/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/download)
to select your area.
