### Post by LLRNR on 2006-12-01
Hi Mac,

I really don't think Beryl has "minimum" system requirements since I got it perfectly running on a P3 computer (1 GHz CPU and 384 MB RAM) with an old nVidia graphics card of 64 MB. On Edgy, yes, but it's incredibly smooth. And a lot more stable that I expected...

For Edgy, I used [this](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/BerylOnEdgy) and it runs great.

For Dapper, I'd recommend you take a look at [this](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/EyeCandyDapper).
