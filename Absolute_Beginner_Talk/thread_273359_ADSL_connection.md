### Post by sylc on 2007-01-11
As far as I know, the Dynalink Home DSL Modem (ALE800) is an external winmodem that will not work with Linux. See: [http://www.linux.org/docs/ldp/howto/Modem-HOWTO.html#toc2](http://www.linux.org/docs/ldp/howto/Modem-HOWTO.html#toc2)

Also have a look at the modem's specs on the Dynalink website: [http://www.dynalink.com.au/modemsadsl_dis.htm?prod=ALE800](http://www.dynalink.com.au/modemsadsl_dis.htm?prod=ALE800) where it states that the modem is "Win 98/98SE/ME/2000/XP compatible".

And then I found a compatibility table at: [http://www.dynalink.co.nz/support/broadband_download_au.htm](http://www.dynalink.co.nz/support/broadband_download_au.htm) where it receives an N/A.

Also, finally, I have the same modem and I'm facing the same problems.
