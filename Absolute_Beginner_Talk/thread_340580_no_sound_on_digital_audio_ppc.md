### Post by Sandlst on 2007-01-17
Hmm are you able to access volume controls without getting an error? (double click on the volume icon in the top right corner of your screen if using Gnome).  If you can get here, it is a safe bet ubuntu has detected and configured your audio card correctly, however sometimes the default volume control doesn't work.  Try going into prefrences (edit/prefrences?  Im not at my comp right now) and checking some of the unchecked boxes to make other volume controls come up, and then turning those up in the volume control window.  Note that changing volume doesn't make a sound, so you must try some different method for testing.  Hope this helps, post back if you have any problems.
