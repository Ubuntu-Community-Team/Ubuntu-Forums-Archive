### Post by Sinkingships7 on 2008-02-18
you should definitely go with an nvidia card, as they have the best compatibility with ubuntu. you're almost guaranteed to have no problems. next, i would think about getting a card that isn't too good, since your ram won't allow you to play graphic intensive games very well anyway, and be sure that it's all onboard memory. you don't want it trying to feed off of your limited system ram.

a 128mb or 256mb card with 64-bit support should be just fine. great for compiz.
