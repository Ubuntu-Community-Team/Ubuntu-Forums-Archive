### Post by lazyart on 2007-06-06
It's an organizational thing.  You may have a pile of documents on your real life desk that are related to one project and a stack of papers regarding next week's company function that you are helping to plan.  This lets you switch from one stack to another without muddling the two.

If you just want one desktop then that's all you should use.  If you want up to 32, well you can have that as well (and it looks pretty neat in beryl but that's another story).
