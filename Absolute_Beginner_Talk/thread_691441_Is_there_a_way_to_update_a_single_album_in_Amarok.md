### Post by CupofDice on 2008-02-08
I don't think there is a way to just update one album specifically, but you can just choose Update Collection from the tools menu instead of Rescan Collection. Of course it seems like you have amarok set to "Watch folders for changes". You can change this by going to Settings/Configure Amarok/Collection, and uncheck that option which will mean you will have to manually update your collection when you want to. Is this what you are talking about?
