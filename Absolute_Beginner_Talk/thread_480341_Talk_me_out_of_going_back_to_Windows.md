### Post by maddog39 on 2007-06-21
Well for one thing, your on 64-bit and that tends to be quite tricky just for the mere fact that adobe doesnt have x86_64 binaries and you have to hack at your system to use the 32-bit version in a 32-bit mode. Also, the Desktop Effects have a big disclaimer on them that says that this is highly experimental and they mean it. You dont have a mainstream graphics card so they will not work. You would need some sort of nvidia or ati (be warned that ati graphics cards have had many problems with their drivers that ati provides) graphics card in order to enable those effects. Pretty much anyone you can still buy will work just fine. My system has a GeForce 6500 which is like $30 now.

[Edit]
The simple solution is to use Ubuntu 32-bit and since your on a laptop you cant upgrade the video card, so you cant use a 3D accelerated desktop (aka desktop effects).
