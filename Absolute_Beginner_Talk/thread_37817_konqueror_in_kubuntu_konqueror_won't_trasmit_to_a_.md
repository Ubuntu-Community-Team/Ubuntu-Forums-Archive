### Post by SGC on 2005-05-28
Klipper (KDE's clibboard tool) doesn't collect the copied text only, it also collect the highlighted text. 

In order to make it collect just the text that you copied, right click on klipper icon on the system tray and choose “configure klipper” from there check “ignore selection”.
