### Post by paker on 2007-02-14
If you are a newbie just like me, consider installing automatix first. It takes care of some plugins for Firefox (web browser) and a few other important things, including nvidia driver and cd/dev codecs. I like this approach for I don't have to manually select the correct nvidia driver. You don't even have to worry about adding repositories. Automatix automatically changes repositories.

[http://www.getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation](http://www.getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation)

From the link, select the right file. Double click on it, and follow the instruction. Once autmoatix installation is complete, Applications>System (?)>Automatix. Check mark "nvidia driver" and click on Install.
