### Post by Dr. Nick on 2006-07-07
You dont change it in windows, You use the BIOS. The BIOS is on any PC no matter the OS. However different Companies have slightly different BIOS so you cant give step by step instructions. 

When you turn your computer on press the "delete" or "F10" or Escape, F1, F2, F12 to get into the BIOS, those are what most use. Or look for a message saying "press ... to enter setup" which should appear on the first screen you see when booting. 

Then look under the menus for boot order
