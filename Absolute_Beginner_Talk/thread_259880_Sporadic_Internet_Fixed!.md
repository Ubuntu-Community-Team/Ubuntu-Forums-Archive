### Post by underwaterbob on 2006-09-18
I thought I'd post this since it was driving me nuts and I just figured it out and I'm pretty ecstatic about it.

My internet connection was very sporadic and I could not figure out why one minute it was working and the next it wasn't.  I have DSL connected through ethernet to a Samsung Sens p28, the laptop also has a wireless network card.

Anyhow long story short the wireless card was set as the default networking device and occasionally picking up very weak signals from some wireless network around here that I evidently could not access the internet via.  So when my computer was detecting the wireless network I didn't have internet while when it was not detecting the wireless network my regular ethernet/DSL was working fine.

Any other newbies have a similar internet problem check this out.  Yay!  Now if only I could get my network settings to stick (setting my ethernet as default using the Administration->networking settings does not seem to take as when I open the settings again the wireless has gone back to default).  For now I have just disabled my wireless card.
