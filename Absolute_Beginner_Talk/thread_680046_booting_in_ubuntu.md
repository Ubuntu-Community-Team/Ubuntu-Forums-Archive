### Post by alwiap on 2008-01-27
When you are running Ubuntu from the LiveCD, there should be an option on the desktop that says 'Install'.  It will run you through the installation process, so you won't need a CD anymore.

If you want to erase Windows, and make the entire drive for Ubuntu, then in the installation process, you will come to a selection where you can click 'Use entire disk' or something to that effect.  Keep in mind you won't have anything in Windows anymore, so be sure to backup your data and documents you want to keep.
