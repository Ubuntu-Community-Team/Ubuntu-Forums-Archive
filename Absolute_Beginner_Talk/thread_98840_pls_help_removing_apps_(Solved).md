### Post by Lambert on 2005-12-04
That's the best way or via synaptic.

remove will remove the app from the system  but keep config files so you can reinstall later and keep your set up. If you want to completely remove these apps then apt-get --purge

In synaptic you'll see dual options, remove; remove completely
