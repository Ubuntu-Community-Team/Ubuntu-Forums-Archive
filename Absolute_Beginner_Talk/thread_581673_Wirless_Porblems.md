### Post by Joakim Stokland on 2007-10-19
Hi, and welcome! :)
What distro did you install? 7.04 Feisty or 7.10 Gutsy or...? And what brand is your card?
Basically you should be online by following these steps:
Click System > Administration > Network. Enter your password when prompted.
You should find your wireless card in the list showing up. Click it and select properties. Here you can enter your settings, or selecting Roaming mode if you want to select which network to connect to from the "tray icon". Click OK. Now uncheck (if already checked) and re-check the check-box in front of your wireless card, to be sure that all changes are applied.
You should be online now.

Some hardware requires third-party non-free drivers, and require you to install further their respective drivers in order to make the hardware work.

Good luck!
Best regards, Joakim
