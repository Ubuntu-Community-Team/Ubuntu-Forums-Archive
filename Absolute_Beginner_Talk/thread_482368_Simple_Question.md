### Post by qpieus on 2007-06-23
Open Office can save documents in MS Word format. In my experience MS word will open and display OO-created documents just fine. If you use weird fonts or complicated formatting, Word might have trouble properly rendering your document. I wouldn't expect a resume to have any kind of those issues though, so you should be good to go.
