### Post by Nano Geek on 2006-06-11
Go: **System**, **Preferences**, **Theme**. You can also find more themes at [art.gnome.org]("http://art.gnome.org").

### Post by Nonno Bassotto on 2006-06-11
If you just want to change one icon, then open the properties of your file and click on the image of the icon in the properties dialog.
