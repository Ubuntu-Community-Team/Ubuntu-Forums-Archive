### Post by aysiu on 2006-02-11
I'm kind of surprised that XFCE ran slow on 128 MB of RAM. I tried it on my 128 MB RAM computer, and it seemed just fine (granted, it has 766 MHz processor, but that shouldn't make that much of difference... should it?).

The distro isn't really important.

What matters most is how many services you have running and what desktop environment/ window manager you use. XFCE, though light, is a desktop environment. Maybe you need just a window manager like IceWM, Fluxbox (what Damn Small Linux uses), or Blackbox.
