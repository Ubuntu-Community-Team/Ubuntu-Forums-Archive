### Post by stalefries on 2006-03-10
Are you thinking that BitTorrent is a p2p client? It is, but not in the way that LimeWire or Kazaa. You have to download a file that points to who has the file you want, then it will get it.

### Post by bored2k on 2006-03-10
BitTorrent is a special kind of Peer 2 Peer client. Instead of connecting you to a download server, it simply requires for you to load a pointer file (the .torrent file) for it to connect **that ** file's trackers, peers and such. That is what keeps BitTorrent (the software) legal.
