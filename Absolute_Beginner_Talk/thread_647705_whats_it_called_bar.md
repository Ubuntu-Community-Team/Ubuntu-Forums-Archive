### Post by t0p on 2007-12-22
The "bar" is called a panel.  This has happened to my desktop before.  What I had to do was delete the panel that moved, then create a new panel at the top of the screen.

Delete the moved panel by right-clicking on it and selecting Delete This Panel.

Now, right-click on the panel at the bottom of the screen and select New Panel.

You can put your menus etc back on your new panel by right-clicking on it and selecting Add To Panel.
