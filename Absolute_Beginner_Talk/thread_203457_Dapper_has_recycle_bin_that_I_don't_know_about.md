### Post by allix on 2006-06-25
If you are using the default panel layout in Gnome, there should be a Trash can icon in the lower panel to the right. Otherwise just right-click on a panel, choose "Add to panel" and select a Trashcan.

### Post by bukwirm on 2006-06-25
You can add the recycling bin to a panel by right clicking on the panel, then choosing  "Add to panel". You will see a 'Wastebasket' icon in the second section.
