### Post by adamklempner on 2007-07-01
It might have something to do with hardware limitations.  I have that problem with my on board sound, but when I use my Sound Blaster Live! card I can have many apps making sound at once, Teamspeak included.  

A long time ago (several years) I researched this problem myself.  I don't remember the details about what I found out, but I ended up buying this "Sound Blaster Live! Value" card and now everything works perfectly.

Search around for posts with your particular sound cards and maybe someone has posted a software workaround.  If you can't find anything, consider a more Linux friendly sound card.
