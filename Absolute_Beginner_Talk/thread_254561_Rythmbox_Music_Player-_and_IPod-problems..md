### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-09-10
I think it's just that loading iPods with music is unreliable, full-stop.

When I bought an iPod, iTunes failed to correctly load it with music, and GTKpod worked. I recently deleted the music off it and tried to load more on with GTKpod, and it didn't work. I tried AmaroK and that didn't work. I finally tried iTunes on Windows and it DID work this time.

What I do know is that if you format an iPod under Mac OS X, you can't read it on Windows.

I didn't figure out how to put music onto an iPod with Rhythmbox; maybe you can't actually do it. Try using GTKpod, if you can stand the interface :-)  Or try using AmaroK, if you can stand the lack of feedback (when you hit the Transfer button, it immediately says that the songs have been transferred - in reality, Linux is still copying them in the background).
