### Post by Bachstelze on 2007-02-26
cdparanoia is a command line ripper that can do it.

It will give you a WAV file, you can then use your favourite program to encode it to MP3 or OGG or whatever the hell you want to encode to

You can also use abcde to do it all in one step - actually abcde is an encoding suite that uses cdparanoia to do the rippoing.
