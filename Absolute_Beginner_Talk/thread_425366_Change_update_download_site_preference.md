### Post by PaulFXH on 2007-04-27
OK, I've found it and I've changed to the main servers and it's made an enormous difference.
Many thanks for your help.

For anybody else who may be unclear how to change the download location, it's here:

System>Administration>Synaptic  Package Manager>Settings>Repositories>Ubuntu Tab>Download From
