### Post by yamfox on 2007-12-27
:lolflag:
Hi, everybody, mind if I share my Ubuntu story? Well Okay...
The thing that got me started in Linux was The GIMP. I needed a good photo retouching program to use, and using Google, I came across it. I was amazed. Everything I could have got out of photoshop, for free!! I wanted to know more about the project. It said it was licensed under something called the GPL. What?? I looked it up, and it told me all about Richard Stallman, Linus Torvalds, and the open source act. But what really got me going was this OS called Linux. I loved the fact that this guy was offering everything M$ was offering for free, $400 less!! I wanted to try it out, so I looked up the most popular and easy-to-use kind. The answer I got: Ubuntu. I went to the Ubuntu site, and guess what: Free CDs!!
I registered with shipIt, and got it 2 weeks later. I tried the live CD. I was amazed!! But I was scared to make the switch. I didn't know why, but I was. Ubuntu was out of my mind until Christmas, when I got a $40 Newegg.com gift card. This is my chance, I thought to myself. I bought a 120-gig Seagate portable hard drive that was on sale, and now I'm using Compiz, rockin'  Amarok, and lovin' the Tux. I will be checking the forums everyday. Thanks for freeing my PC, Ubuntu!!!
