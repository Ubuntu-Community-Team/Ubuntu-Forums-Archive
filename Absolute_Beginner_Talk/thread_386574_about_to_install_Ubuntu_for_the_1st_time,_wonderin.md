Just install with dual boot if you have not removed VISTA or XP. If you got a spare partitioned drive of 10 or more, use 4 to 8 GB for root, 2 GB for swap, rest for data. Ubuntu will also read from FAT32 partitions.
Live CD is too slow. Live Feista CD also mounts your drives (after clicking on the drive at 'PLACE'), have not checked with EDGY.
I am using 64-bit EDGY.
