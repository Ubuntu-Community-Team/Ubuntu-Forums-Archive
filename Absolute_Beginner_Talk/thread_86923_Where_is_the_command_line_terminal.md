### Post by Kvark on 2005-11-06
You also have 6 full screen terminals on the F1 to F6 keys, you can switch to them by pressing Ctrl + Alt + F1-F6, these come in handy if your graphical interface freezes. To get back to the graphical interface you press Ctrl + Alt + F7.
