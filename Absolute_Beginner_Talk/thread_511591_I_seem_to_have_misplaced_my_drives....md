### Post by Malta paul on 2007-07-28
Check to see if your icons are in "Places". If not check in Places >Computer.  If found clicking on the icons should mount them. To mount them on your desktop at boot-up use: ' sudo mount -a ' in the terminal.
Check out [http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/index](http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/index) and [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Edgy](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Edgy)
