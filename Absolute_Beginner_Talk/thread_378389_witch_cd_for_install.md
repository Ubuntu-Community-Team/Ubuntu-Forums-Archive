### Post by andreas64 on 2007-03-07
Hi,

you can use both (LiveCD or AlternateCD) for installing Ubuntu.

The AlternateCD has a text-only-installer, wich gives you more Options during the installation.
Most people don't need this and uses the LiveCD for installing Ubuntu.

During the installation process, Ubuntu will detect your Windows and will add it to the GRUB bootmenu automatically.
