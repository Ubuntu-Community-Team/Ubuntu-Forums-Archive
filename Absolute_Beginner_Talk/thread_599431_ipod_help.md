### Post by justsomedude on 2007-11-01
I use Banshee to subscribe to podcasts and sync music, works like a charm. 

However, syncing videos, contacts or pictures is a different story. The only two applications I've tested that are able to sync videos are gtkpod and floola. Both apps are broken in Gutsy. Floola might get fixed soon, gtkpod (the aac version) has to be compiled from source for Gutsy, as mp4 support is broken. 

If it's just music, players like Banshee and Amarok will do a good job...
