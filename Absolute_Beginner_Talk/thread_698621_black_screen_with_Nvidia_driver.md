### Post by jecker on 2008-04-09
I had the same problem until today. After doing some playing around and looking at the /var/log/Xorg.0.log, I found the Nvidia driver is interpreting the display device incorrectly. In my case the Nvidia driver defaulted to a CRT instead of an LCD screen. To fix this problem I added
Option "UseDisplayDevice" "DFP" in the screen section. Other options can be found at [http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86/1.0-9755/README/appendix-d.html](http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86/1.0-9755/README/appendix-d.html)

Hope this helps...
