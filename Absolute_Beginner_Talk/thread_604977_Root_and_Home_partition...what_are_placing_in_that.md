### Post by It_Was_Luck on 2007-11-06
the root partition is pretty much where the OS files will go. all those folders that you've never heard of that have all the info you need to keep your computer going.

the home partition is strictly your files. no hidden stuff, just documents and photos and videos you've stored in the folders within that partition.
