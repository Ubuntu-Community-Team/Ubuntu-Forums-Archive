### Post by Viewtifulj on 2007-08-28
So i had totally and completely given up on any hopes of having OpenGL. After trying pretty much everything under the sun, i just gave up and accepted the fact that i was going to have to purchase and Nvidia card if i wanted to get the most out of Linux. To that end i went ahead and installed the Tribe 5 of Gutsy (Kubuntu) and went about my merry way. For the last 4 or 5 days i have been using it pretty much error free. I installed some updates today after receiving a notification in my system tray. After the reboot, i was going to change my resolution when i went and check the info in the ATI control center. And then i saw it:

OpenGL Provider:  ATI Technologies Inc.


Man i was shocked. The last thing i had tried, as a last ditch of course, was to use the Envy installer. That didnt work as far as OpenGl was concerned, but i was getting better performance than when i went with "vesa", so i had decided to keep it. I'm glad i did. I would like to think of it as God blessing me for having patience and not giving up like i did a few months ago. It feels like once i decided to let go of OpenGL it actually came and found me.

The question is:

Has anyone else experienced this or something like it??
