### Post by herbster on 2008-03-17
There's different ways to do this. I think the most basic way is on your menu Places > Connect to Server, select Windows Share and put the ip and user/pass if necessary.

You can also go Places > Network and you should see Windows Network, just double-click and proceed to the workgroup and then the shares.
