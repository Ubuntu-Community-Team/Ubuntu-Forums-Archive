### Post by szf on 2006-09-27
The [Official Ubuntu Book]("http://fridge.ubuntu.com/node/504") was published by PHTR this summer. They've also been a sponsor of Bruce Perens' Open Source series, so with a little effort and/or luck you can download entire chapters for your perusal [shared under creative commons license].

[Bookpool.com]("http://www.bookpool.com/sm/0132435942") (online technical bookseller) has had the book available for $18.95 USD since pre-release (Aug. 2006)... not bad for a dead-tree copy.

You can also use [bookburro]("http://bookburro.org/") to find a book to purchase or available from a local library, assuming you're in a major metropolitan area.
