### Post by Kapre on 2005-08-23
[QUOTE=Rick069]Is there a way to be able to use the floppy drive after the computer boots automatically? As opposed to  manually mounting from a command line after the computer boots?[/QUOTE]

The floppy drive in any linux system is automatically detected and it will be mounted automatically when you insert a disk on it. Just click on the drive icon (if it is not shown on your desktop, you have can make it visible by adding it to your panel)

Just right click on your panel (up or down panel bar and add the Disk Mounter)
