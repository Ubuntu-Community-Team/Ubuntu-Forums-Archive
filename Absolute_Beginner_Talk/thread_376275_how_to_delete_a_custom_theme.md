### Post by aysiu on 2007-03-04
Assuming you're using Gnome, go to your home folder, press Control-H, find the .themes folder, go into that, and delete **anything** in that folder. Log out. Then log back in again.

You can also go to System > Preferences > Themes and select the default theme (Human).

### Post by aysiu on 2007-03-04
Control-H toggles the hiding and showing of hidden directories.

In your home folder, there are a whole bunch of hidden directories. One of them is called .themes

In order to see that folder, you press Control-H.

See the attached screenshots for more details. The one with the blank space marked in green is pre-Control-H. The one with the .themes folder marked in green is post-Control-H.
