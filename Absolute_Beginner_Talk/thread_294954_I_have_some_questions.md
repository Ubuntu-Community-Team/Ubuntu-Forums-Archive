### Post by AmandaKerik on 2007-02-03
There are instructions on the site, hun. Just follow them step by step.
I'd give them here, but why repeat what they say?

Tip: Set up the ports in the extension FIRST
Click on these in order: Tools > Extensions (or Add-ons) > Webmail > Preferences > Servers (first tab) > Ports
Set the pop3 to 1111 and the smtp to 1112 
Save and reload Thunderbird.

On the Hotmail site you don't need to do a thing! It's all in Thunderbird!
