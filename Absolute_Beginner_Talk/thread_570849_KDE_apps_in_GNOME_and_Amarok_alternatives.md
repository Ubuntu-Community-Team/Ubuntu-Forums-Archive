### Post by mivo on 2007-10-08
Amarok works fine in Gnome. The start-up time may be a tiny bit longer because Gnome does not load KDE libraries at launch, but it's more or less a non-issue. The QT libraries are GPL'ed for non-commercial projects, so by installing KDE applications you are not downloading non-free components. However, you will need the codeces to play MP3 files, so you will end up with "restricted" libraries no matter what you do, but there is no need for extreme idealism here. :)
