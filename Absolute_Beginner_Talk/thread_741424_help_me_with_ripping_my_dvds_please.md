### Post by Hospadar on 2008-03-31
You should google "medibunto", check out their "repository howto" to add their repositories.  they provide some software packages of questionable legality in some regions. (i.e. dvd decoding).  then you can install "libdvdcss2" from synaptic, and grab dvd::rip from Add/Remove programs.  you can also use the kde ripping/burning program (k3b I believe?) it's another good choice if you don't like dvd::rip.

This will allow you to rip all dvds, including those which are encrypted.
