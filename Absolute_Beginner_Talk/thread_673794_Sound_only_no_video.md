### Post by Iehova on 2008-01-21
What graphics card are you using, and what video driver? Do you have compiz running?

I had this problem on Kaffeine and Mplayer with an Intel X3100 gfx card running compiz because the video driver was set to xv - changing it to xshm and X11 respectively fixed the problem. If you don't have that same graphics card, though, and you aren't running compiz, then it's unlikely that that's your problem. If you are, then I can provide further information as to changing your video driver settings.
