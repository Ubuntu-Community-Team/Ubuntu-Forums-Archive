### Post by rsambuca on 2007-12-06
> **sox fan Matt said:**
> My question is simple I think..Is it wise to keep Firefox 2.0.0.11 and the Beta FF 3 (cause It hasn't broke on me yet) and Id rather only have one Firefox installed, or is it necesscary to have both installed?

The default Firefox (Currently 2.0.0.11) is a core packge of the Ubuntu desktop.  It is tied into the help screen system, and if you try to remove it, you will also remove ubuntu-desktop and a number of other packages.  While it won't destroy your system, I would recommend leaving it installed side-by-side with the 3.0 Beta so your future upgrade potential is less troublesome.
