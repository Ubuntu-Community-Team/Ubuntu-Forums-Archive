### Post by po0f on 2006-10-29
voodoonirvana,

If you live in the US, it would be either just "en" or "en_US".

Programs use localization to make the program more familiar to the user.  Language is probably the most important one of localization's aspects, but there are other details as well.  Date/time format, currency symbol/placement, thousands separator (some countries use periods to separate large numbers like 1.000.000 and use commas to separate the decimal like 1.000.000,00) come to mind.
