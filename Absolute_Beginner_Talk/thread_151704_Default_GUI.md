### Post by christhemonkey on 2006-03-28
At the login manager,
select to log into GNOME,
then login,
it should then bring up a dialog saying "would you like to make GNOME your default session".  
Say yes.
Have a cup of tea and pat yourself on the back.
(if any problems, let us know)
