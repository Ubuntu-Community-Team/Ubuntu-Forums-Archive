### Post by rockinlinux on 2007-12-10
it seems like your best option would be to just backup your files you want to save on a cd or dvd or external HD and do a fresh install. Some settings and things are stored on your /home. if you want it to look like a new install then i would just do a new install. you could also just make a copy of your /home partition and move it you the "2nd HD" then after you do the fresh install mount that partition and copy your files to your new /home.
