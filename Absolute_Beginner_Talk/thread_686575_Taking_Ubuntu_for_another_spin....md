### Post by jeffus_il on 2008-02-03
You're on the right track, I would make 2 partitions on the hard disk, One for Vista and one for Ubuntu. Keep a viable Vista installation until you have Ubuntu running to your satisfaction. Dual booting with both Operating systems is easy. Use the 32 bit Ubuntu not the 64bit version.
