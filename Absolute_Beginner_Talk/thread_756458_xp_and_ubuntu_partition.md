### Post by bill516 on 2008-04-15
The very best discussion of partitioning strategies I have seen for Ubuntu (or any Linux, I think) is at:

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu)

This is done by A. Y. Siu, who also is writing in the Ubuntu forum.  I had many questions about how best  to partition and I found nothing better than this site.

Let me also suggest using Gparted, which is a self-booting Linux application.  It works nicely and, if you do a Google search, you will find a home page where it is explained very well.

Take some time to study and your problems will be fewer down the road.  I am dual booting Ubuntu with WInXP and having no problems at all.

I have to admit I am using XP less and less.  It may not be long for this laptop.

Good luck!
