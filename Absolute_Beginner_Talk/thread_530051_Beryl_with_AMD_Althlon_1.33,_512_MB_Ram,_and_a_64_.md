### Post by daradib on 2007-08-23
Not much different between Compiz and Beryl. They both do the same thing. Compiz was originally released by Novell. Beryl is a fork of Compiz that supposedly has more features and higher degrees of customization, but is sometimes said to be less stable and require more processing power. Compiz is included by default in Ubuntu 7.04, but not enabled by default (Desktop Effects must be enabled.). However, both Compiz and Beryl have merged together to form Compiz Fusion.
