### Post by codenametiger on 2007-07-18
Ok, there may be many answers to your problem:

1. I would recommend re-formatting your hard drive. It will make things a little easier.
2. Before you reformat it though, reinstall Ubuntu from the website. One of the possible problems is that you have an incomplete version of your OS. On the website, there is a check-sum. It is called the MD5Sum.  Here is a link about how to do it. [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)
That site can explain it better than I can so be sure to take a look!
3. Check to see if your hard drive is actually connected. (either take apart your PC or use Windows to check. 

That is all I have for now. If you need some more help, you can e-mail me @ [email]mattjack53@comcast.net[/email]. Thanks and good luck!
