### Post by ajgreeny on 2006-03-08
Assuming the monitor is supported you need to add "1280x1024" to each of the figure parts of the xorg.conf.  You can do it for each depth entry or just the default of 24.
You will now (or you should have) a choice of resolution when you right click on the desktop under kde or reconfigure desktop under gnome.
All of this presumes your video card is able to provide the resolution, of course, which I presume it is.
