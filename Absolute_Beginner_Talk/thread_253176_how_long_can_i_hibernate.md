### Post by manojvekaria on 2006-09-07
Hibernate, normally works best when left off for less than a day.
In windows XP if you leave it on more than a day, some applications face some problems.

In ubuntu however, not many problems in the application.


I recommend you shutting it down once per day.

TIP: hibernate everytime, so it shuts down and starts faster, but shut it down at night, when you leave!!
