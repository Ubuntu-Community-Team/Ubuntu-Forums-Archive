### Post by Dmize on 2006-10-27
I found a resolution, thanks for the help though. For future reference to those who may encounter the same issue, here is how i fixed it.

1) Go to System -> Preferences -> Sound
2) Click on the "Sounds" Tab
3) Make sure the correct sound card is selected at the bottom. 
4) Press "Close" and give it a shot!

Thanks again for the help,

Derek.
