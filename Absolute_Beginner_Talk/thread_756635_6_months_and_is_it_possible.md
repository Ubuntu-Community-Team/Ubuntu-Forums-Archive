### Post by kpkeerthi on 2008-04-16
I suggest you read on in the following order. 
1. Get some familiarity with linux commands from [http://www.linuxcommand.org](http://www.linuxcommand.org)
2. Read the [official ubuntu documentation]("https://help.ubuntu.com/"). This is a must to know your way around in ubuntu.
3. Read about cool things you can do with ubuntu from 
[http://www.ubuntugeek.com/](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/)
