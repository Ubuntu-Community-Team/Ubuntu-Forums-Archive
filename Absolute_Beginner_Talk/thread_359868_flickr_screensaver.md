### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-02-12
You cannot directly use Flickr photos as a screensaver, but if you download them as an album, you can import them into F-Spot (Applications > Graphics > F-Spot). You can then pick F-Spot as your screensaver to display your photos. It's a bit tacky, but it works.
