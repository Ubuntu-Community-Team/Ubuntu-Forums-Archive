### Post by mcduck on 2007-04-13
1. Right-click in any empty space in your bottom panel
2. Select 'New Panel'
3. Drag your new panel to top of the screen if it didn't go there by itself.
4. Right-click on the new top panel
5. Select 'Add to Panel'
6. Drag&drop all applets you want back to panel. I think at least Menu Bar, Notification Area and Clock are there in default setup.
