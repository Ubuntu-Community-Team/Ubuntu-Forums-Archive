### Post by aeiah on 2008-04-11
older howtos should work. your conky config file is in your home folder and is called .conkyrc i believe. its a hidden file, so in  nautilus hit ctrl+h to see your hidden files first or open a terminal and do
```
gedit .conkyrc
```
