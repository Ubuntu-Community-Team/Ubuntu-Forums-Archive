### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-12-07
Avant Window Navigator requires [COLOR="Blue"]Compiz Fusion[/COLOR] to be running.

Have you enabled visual effects?

Navigate to : [COLOR="Blue"]System>Preferences>Appearance
[/COLOR]
When the window opens, click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Visual Effects Tab[/COLOR].

Select either [COLOR="Blue"]Normal[/COLOR], or [COLOR="Blue"]Extra.[/COLOR]

If this doesn't work, it probably means that you haven't loaded the video driver for your card, or chipset.

Hope this helps,
