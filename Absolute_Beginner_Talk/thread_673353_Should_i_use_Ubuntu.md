Your hardware should work fine in Ubuntu. And as far as I know most sound cards are supported in Ubuntu. My Soundblaster Live 24-bit from Creative instantly worked. I just had to disable the on-board sound card in the BIOS. Ubuntu kept wanting to use the on-board sound card.
