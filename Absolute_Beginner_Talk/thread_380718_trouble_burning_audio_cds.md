### Post by igknighted on 2007-03-10
Gnomebaker and k3b are the two best programs for writing CDs.  I would try with these.  In general, the linux philosophy is to make one program do one task as well as possible, and not overlap functionality.  Therefor, your media players are not where you burn cd's from (or in most cases where you rip from), which is a bit different than windows.
