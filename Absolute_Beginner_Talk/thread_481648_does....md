### Post by jfinkels on 2007-06-22
> **Ssj3_man said:**
> Does Ubuntu 7.04 - the Feisty Fawn have a music libary like windows media player where i can store my music and bring it all up when I launch the media program.

The default GNOME music library manager is Rhythmbox. It's under "Applications > Sound & Video". You might also want to give some other music players a try, including Exaile, Listen, Banshee, Xmms, mpd + (nc)mpc, or Amarok (if you're on Kubuntu).
