### Post by sbergman27 on 2006-10-04

Welcome!

I am a long time imap user.  I've used a number of imap clients, including Evolution, Thunderbird, and Kmail.

For imap, I would strongly recommend Mozilla Thunderbird.  

Evolution is workable, but clunky and slow, and a bit buggy, for imap accounts.

You can get it at:

Applications->Add Remove Programs->Internet->Thunderbird

When you first start it up, it will lead you through setting up your imap account.  You will need the name or ip address of your imap server.

I think you will find it easier and better for imap.
