### Post by macgyver2 on 2005-08-31
[QUOTE=fartbarker]fartbarker@ubuntu:~$ uptime
 17:55:32 up  1:07,  2 users,  load average: 0.23, 0.27, 0.26
fartbarker@ubuntu:~$ users

Is this right? Why am I listed as 2 users?[/QUOTE]
Every terminal you have open shows up as a user.  So, one terminal open, 2 users show up (your login is the first one).
