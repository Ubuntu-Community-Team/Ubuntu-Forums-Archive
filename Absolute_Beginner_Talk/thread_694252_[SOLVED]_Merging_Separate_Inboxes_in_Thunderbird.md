### Post by jrc on 2008-02-13
Problem solved.  Aysiu, I thought a little more about what you said and then came up with a workaround solution that didn't require me to mess with trying to merge one Inbox file (in mbox format) with another Inbox file.  I simply created a new account in Thunderbird, then moved the Inbox mbox file into the new account's folder so the emails would show up when I opened Thunderbird.  I then dragged the emails to the place where I wanted them and deleted the new account.  Thanks for putting me on the right track.
