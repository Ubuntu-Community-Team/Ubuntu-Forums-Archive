### Post by legzelda on 2008-02-17
After you insert the DVD, an icon will appear on your desktop.  Right click this icon, then click Copy Disc.  In the window that comes up, click the drop-down selection box to write the disc to a file.  This will allow you to create an .iso of the entire DVD structure, which you can then burn to another disc as you see fit.  If you do not have a dual-layer DVD writer or discs for those discs that are dual-layered, you'll want to check out DVDShrink for Linux.
