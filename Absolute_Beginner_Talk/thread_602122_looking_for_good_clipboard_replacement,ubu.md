### Post by nomasteryoda on 2007-11-04
And if you did not know already, Linux has a simple copy method... Just highlight the text you want to copy then middle click the space you want to paste it... Very simple. 

The Klipper (in kubuntu -kde) or glipper (in ubuntu - gnome) work well with this too. Just install the one for your desktop of choice, launch the program with a quick "Alt + F2" then type the name of your new program and hit the enter key.. You should now have a nice new clipboard icon in your tray.

Rightclick this icon and choose the stuff you copied to...
