### Post by kyphi on 2007-09-25
Take heart, SabrinalovesUbunt2, here is the solution:

Remove the 'Dictionary Look Up' icon from the top panel.

In the Applications menu, under Accessories, you will find 'Dictionary' which you can customise and resize to your heart's content.  Left click and drag to the top panel and lock into position.

Enjoy.
