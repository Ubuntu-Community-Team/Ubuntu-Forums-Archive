### Post by JacquesBouman on 2008-06-02
Hi,

The following should work : 
-Open the volume control.
-Edit the preferences.
-Check the mic boost and the Capture options.
You now should have a tab named Recording.
- On that tab disable the speaker and enable the mic.

This should do the trick.
