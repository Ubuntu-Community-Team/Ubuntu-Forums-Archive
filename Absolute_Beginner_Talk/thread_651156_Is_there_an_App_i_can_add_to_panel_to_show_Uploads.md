### Post by c0met on 2007-12-27
Hi Hopelessone,

When I installed the program, it appeared as an item in the menu for "Administration" as a link to " Network Tools". Check that this is activated by doing the following.

Right click on the Ubuntu icon that lists the menus for Accessories, Games, Internet, etc. Choose "Edit Menus". Now scroll down to the Administration menu and click on it. Make sure that "Net Tools" has a check-mark against its name. Only those programs that are marked will appear in the menu list.

Regards,
c0met
