### Post by Bartender on 2008-02-20
The most straightforward thing to do at this point would be to try and ascertain the EXACT settings for the IMAP servers you're trying to use.

If you get one thing off in Thunderbird it won't work.  Such as "username" instead of "username@ISP.com"
Or the wrong port.
Or the wrong TSL/SSL setting.
Or "Use username and password" if the server doesn't.

Whose IMAP servers are you trying to talk with?
