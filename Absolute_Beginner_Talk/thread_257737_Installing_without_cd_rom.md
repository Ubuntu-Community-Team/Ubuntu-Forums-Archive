### Post by alanmzifa on 2006-09-15
Perhaps the easiest way (particularly if you are new and perhaps uncomfortable with doing command line instructions etc.) is to buy a 2.5" to 3.5" HDD adapter. They can be bought for as little as $2/$3. Simply remove your laptop HDD, plug in the adapter, swap the laptop HDD for a desktop HDD and go through the normal install procedure from there.

I had the same issue to deal with on a laptop a couple of weeks ago. Thread [here]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=247565").

And whatever way you decide to do it remember to allow a couple of hours to defragment your hard drive first.
