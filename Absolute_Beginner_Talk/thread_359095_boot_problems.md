### Post by alextj on 2007-02-11
I used to have similar problem, when I had Windows installed. Dirty workaround was to make your Linux hard drive bootable in your bios settings. :)
This way you should be able to get into your installed Linux system.

Then check out [this guide]("http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_restore_GRUB_menu_after_Windows_installation"). Not exactly your case, but might help.
There are instructions oh how to setup GRUB with Windows using your livecd.
