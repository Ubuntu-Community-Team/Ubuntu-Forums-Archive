I would like to know what Get & Hit mean on updates and how to make Hit  Get. Please explain

---

### Post by Brunellus on 2006-09-22
"hit" means that the package manager accessed the repository.  "Get" means that the package manager is downloading updates. If it hits, and doesn't get, then there are no updates to get.
