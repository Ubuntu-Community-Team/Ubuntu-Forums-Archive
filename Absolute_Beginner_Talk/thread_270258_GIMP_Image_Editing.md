### Post by zerodogma on 2006-10-03
If you were using "Paint", I take it you have never used Photoshop, PS Elements, or the like (maybe I am wrong).  Raster image programs such as The Gimp and Photoshop use -layers- for collages and image blending.  If you have a photo on a layer, simply choose "new layer" from the layer TAB, open or drag another image into that layer, and *poof*.  Any PS layer/collage tutorial on the web should help... just apply the technique to GIMP.

Hope that helped...?
