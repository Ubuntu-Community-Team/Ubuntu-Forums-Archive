### Post by Mustard on 2008-02-26
I would just consider them as superfluous error messages that you get when the operating system is 'talking to itself' as I would call it. When the OS is talking to itself it often displays more information about the processes than is necessary for the average user.  I've had similar 'assertion' errors appearing but I never pay much attention to them.  As long as it boots up and runs fine. :)

The way I read these assertion errors is that the system is making an assumption, attempts the assumption, fails, and then tries another assumption.  Eventually it hits the right assumption and proceeds.  Thats my layman's take on the messages anyway.
