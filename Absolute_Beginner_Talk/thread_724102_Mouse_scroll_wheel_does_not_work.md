### Post by alphaniner on 2008-03-14
Is your scroll wheel a button?  If so you can go to edit -> preferences -> advanced and check 'use autoscrolling'.  That way when you click the scroll button, it will change the pointer and you can move the mouse to scroll.  Not what you are looking for I know, but I think you will find it is better than nothing.
