### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-02-08
Hi and welcome. Whether or not Vista is as good as Ubuntu is up to you to decide ;) If you want to try Ubuntu, you'd do well to inspect the hardware on your new laptop and make sure it will play friendly with Linux. Do you know which model or brand you're getting? Can you give us a link?

Ubuntu does its best to move away from the command line; whether or not you use it or need it is going to depend on the circumstances. My parents have a laptop with Ubuntu on it and never use the command line, even when backing up their entire hard drive or reconfiguring their wireless network. Myself, I like the command line better, so I use it. It's a matter of preference.

Sorry, there's no DX10 patch for Linux, although both wine and Cedega are very good at what they do (for what I've heard. I'm a straight Linux-native gamer ;) ).

And Ubuntu will only format your hard drive if you tell it to. A lot of people dual-boot Windows and Ubuntu, and how much space one or the other takes up is for you to decide.

Cheers!
