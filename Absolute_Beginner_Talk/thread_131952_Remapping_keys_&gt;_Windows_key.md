### Post by heimo on 2006-02-17
Probably. It should be easy as launching *System->Preferences->Keyboard Shortcuts*, selecting *Run a terminal* and pressing the key you want to assign to launch terminal. :) Works for me. (And I already had configured the right Windows/menu key to launch terminal.)
