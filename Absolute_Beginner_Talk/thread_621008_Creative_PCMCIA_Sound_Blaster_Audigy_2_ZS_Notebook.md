### Post by TekNullOG on 2007-12-04
Yes, a few people have (including me).

This is how it works for me and a few other people:

- Line-out: [INDENT]works great[/INDENT]
- 5.1 sound (different from Line-out): [INDENT]works great but I think it is stereo[/INDENT]
- Line-in/mic-in/optical-in: [INDENT]DOES NOT WORK[/INDENT]

I have been trying to get the line-in to work for 1 year without any success. It is the only reason I still boot in Windows. I never manage to put money aside for a new sound card.

I suggest just pluging it in and trying. It worked for me. 

Now since it is a laptop and you probably remove that card from time to time, you might have difficulties with Ubuntu managing the priorities of the sound cards. For example, sliding it in to you pcmcia will work right away for Movie Player but Firefox and XMMS won't automatically detect the change even if they are set to detect Default.

This thread helps a little: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=436522](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=436522)

Good luck
