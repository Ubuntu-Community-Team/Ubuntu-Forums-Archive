### Post by smartboyathome on 2008-04-12
To change your repo to one which is faster:

1. Go to System > Administration > Software Sources.
2. Click on the "Download from:" drop down menu, and select Other.
3. Click the best server button and it will scan for your fastest server.
4. When done, click Choose Server, and then click Close.
5. When asked to reload, do so.
