### Post by spd106 on 2006-11-03
Most people seem to be using beagle integrated into the deskbar. You can add it to your panel as an applet. Just right-click the toolbar and select "add to panel..." then add the deskbar applet. I think beagle is included with edgy, but it's been in the repos for a while now. It requires mono.
