I had this problem as well with my Logitech 5.1 speakers on my Dell E520N with an Intel soundcard.  I went to the alsa website [http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Main_Page](http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Main_Page) and downloaded the latest driver, utility, library as well as the latest plugins and compiled them all individually with my soundcard hda-Intel.  After compiling it defaults the speakers to mute.  After a reboot I had to unmute the speakers using sessions -> sound and made sure the surround text was highlighted.  I then ran the speakers test and sure enough I had 5.1 surround sound.
