You're probably using Flash 9. I've noticed that it's buggy. Oftentimes when I had a page open with Flash content and switched to another tab the Flash content on the previous tab will just render as a empty grey box when going back to it.

I've since experimented with a setup where I use Flash 7 with Opera and Flash 9 with Firefox (for the sites that require it). It seems to be working.

Flash 7 is much more stable with Opera.
