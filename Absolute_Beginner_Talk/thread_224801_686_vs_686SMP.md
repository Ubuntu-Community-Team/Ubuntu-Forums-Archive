Quick question: how do I determine whether or not I need the SMP kernel and modules?  All I know for sure is that it's a Pent-III.

---

### Post by 5-HT on 2006-07-28
Dapper's 686 kernels include SMP support, there is no need to download a separate 686-smp kernel.

Unfortunately,  PIII's cannot take advantage of SMP (but may benefit from a 686 kernel as compared to the 386 that would have been installed by default).
