### Post by Parama on 2007-09-21
If I were you I would firstly download the drivers from [ati.amd.com]("http://ati.amd.com/support/driver.html") for your graphics card. If that installation doesn't do it I would upgrade to Gutsy where you don't have to make use of those session scripts to enable compiz.
To my experience Compiz in Feisty is rather unstable and I kinda gave up on it. My last 3 weeks with Gutsy (amd64) however have been a very neat tour into the wobly and semi transparent world of compiz with almost no hassles :)
