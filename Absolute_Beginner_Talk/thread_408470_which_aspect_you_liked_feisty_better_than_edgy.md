### Post by mesh_ok on 2007-04-13
I do recommend Feisty because it has some significant (to my view) improvements such as 
packages' sources included by default, so you don't need to blow your mind with finding and installing, for example, beryl, anymore, or videodrivers, media codecs, your favorite programs.
