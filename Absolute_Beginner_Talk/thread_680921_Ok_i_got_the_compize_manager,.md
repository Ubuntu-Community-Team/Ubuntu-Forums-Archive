### Post by mocoloco on 2008-01-28
Right-click on the workspace switcher (bottom right, next to the trash), and click **Preferences**.
Change the number or workspaces to 4.  Now you'll have a cube.

---

### Post by emarkd on 2008-01-28
Two desktops is the default.  In the config manager, click on the General Options button and select the Desktop Size tab at the top.  Move the Horizontal Virtual Size slider to 4 (or whatever you want) and save it.

---

### Post by lswest on 2008-01-28
also, if you wanna move the cube, ctrl+alt+left mouse button does it, or ctrl+alt+left/right, and ctrl+alt+down pulls the cube apart and gives you a line of the workspaces.
