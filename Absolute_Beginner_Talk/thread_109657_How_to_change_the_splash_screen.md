### Post by mcduck on 2005-12-29
If you mean the one after you log in, then what you need is in System/Preferences/Splash Screen. Open that, press the 'Install'-button, find the image you want, press 'Open', and then select your image and press 'Activate'.

---

### Post by Perfect Storm on 2005-12-29
The Splash screen application is not installed by default.
You need first to enable universe in your source list. Then:

```
sudo apt-get install gnome-splashscreen-manager
```
