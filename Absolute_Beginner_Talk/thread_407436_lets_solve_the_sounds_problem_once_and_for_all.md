As someone mentioned above, we all screw things up and have to reinstall.  Maybe the fix you need will be in the newer version.  From the link you gave it seems as if you are running Dapper.  Have you checked to see in the forums and wiki if folks running Edgy or Feisty have the same problems?  If they do not, maybe an upgrade will help.

What would I do?  The first thing I would do is check at skype forums and site and see if the issue you have is easy to fix with skype. Then, try to reconfigure from there.  

I do not think ditching Ubuntu and trying Debian is the answer though.  Debian is not quite as friendly as Ubuntu and many of the packages will be the same anyway.  It won't get you what you think.  I really recommend you work through the problem.  It is great because you will get satisfaction when it is fixed and you will learn a great deal along the way.
