### Post by TVu on 2006-08-11
Well,as a beginner as well, I know only little, but basically every file have an owner who have full rights to the file. For security reasons it is not intelligent to give out permissions to files without any control (i.e. everyone could access them), hence groups. By defining a group of specific users, you can create shared folders among other things.
