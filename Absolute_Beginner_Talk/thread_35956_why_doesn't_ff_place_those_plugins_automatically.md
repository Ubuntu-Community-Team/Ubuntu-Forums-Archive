### Post by poofyhairguy on 2005-05-21
[QUOTE=newbie2]why doesn't firefox place those plugins automatically in ubuntu like in window$ ??[/QUOTE]


The reason is that unlike Windows (which has a very common base) the pluggins do not know what is own your computer. Programs rely on other programs, and the different kinds of Linux are so separate that  you can not assume anything about Linux except its heart-the kernel (and even then its not always the same model heart). To get around this, Ubuntu has its own way of installing these pluggins to make sure that what they need are there. If Pluggins installed the Windows way often it simply wouldn't work. The ubuntu way has always worked for me on every computer I've touched.

Try either the ubuntuguide:

[www.ubuntuguide.org](www.ubuntuguide.org)

or this zip file/CD:

[http://www.mrbass.org/linux/ubuntu/](http://www.mrbass.org/linux/ubuntu/)

to get pluggins to work easily. Sorry it isn't easier, but its what you get for free.
