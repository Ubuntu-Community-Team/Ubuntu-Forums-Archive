### Post by rharris on 2007-07-10
Download the m3u and save it to a location of your choice. Open Nautilus and browse to that location. Right click on the m3u file and select "properties". Once there, click the "open with" tab and select XMMS.

Once this is done, m3u files will then be opened with XMMS directly. Assuming you are using Firefox, the option will appear giving you a choice to play with XMMS or download the m3u after this is done.
