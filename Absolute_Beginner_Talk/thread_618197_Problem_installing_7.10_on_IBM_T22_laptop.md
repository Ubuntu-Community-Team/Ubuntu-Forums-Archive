### Post by Arthur Archnix on 2007-11-20
Trouble installing could be a number of different issues, graphic cards being not the most or least likely. You can certainly rule out 'Grub', since if you haven't finished installing it, then grub hasn't been installed yet. And if you can boot to the desktop of the live cd, then the bootloader (grub) is doing a fine job. 

The link I provided is probably your best bet at ruling out problems and finding a solution.

If you're confident that the problem is your graphic card then simply download the alternate cd and use that to install.
