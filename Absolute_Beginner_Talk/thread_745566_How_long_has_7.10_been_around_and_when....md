### Post by Tatty on 2008-04-04
8.04 will work pretty much the same as 7.10.  There will of course be changes, hopefully for the better, but the majority of things will operate in the same, or at least similar, way.

When it comes out you can jsut do a distro upgrade from the update manager to update your 7.10 to 8.04.  It will probably take a couple of hours (theres a lot to do!), depending on your internet connection speed of course.

But before you do that, i would definately reccomend backing up any valuable data you cant afford to lose... whilst unlikely to go wrong, a distro upgrade is a big change to your system so its always best to be careful.
