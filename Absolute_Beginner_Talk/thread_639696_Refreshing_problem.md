### Post by p_quarles on 2007-12-13
I've seen that happen a few times with Flash, where Firefox will go away after I've killed the process, but the Flash portion of the window stays on the desktop. The only solution I found was to restart the desktop (not the whole computer): ctrl-alt-backspace.
