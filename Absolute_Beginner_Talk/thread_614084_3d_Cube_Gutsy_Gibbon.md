### Post by porschify on 2007-11-15

### Post by jordank on 2007-11-15
You have rotate cube on, but do you also have "desktop cube" enabled aswell?
And yes, you're going to need 4 workspaces, which can be configured at the bottom right of your screen by default. right click > preferences i believe.
if you don't have 4 workspaces, you will be able to rotate other shapes. for example, 2 desktops will be a flat surface, with a workspace on either side. 5 workspaces is a pentagon, etc.
Set it to 4 for the cube.
Don't forget to add a skydome for cooler background effects aswel.. And check out cube caps.
