i dont know why this happened, but after i did an upgrade off the net from the repos, my volume on my pc after i restarted it went very low. i had my speakers cranked up flat chat and could hardly hear a thing.

i went hunting and i double clicked the speaker and found the "PCM" volume was very low. i dragged it up via mouse and all is well again.
