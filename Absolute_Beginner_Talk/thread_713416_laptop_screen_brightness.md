### Post by BandD on 2008-03-02
Did you make sure that "Dim Display when Idle" in the power preferences is un-checked?

Go to System-->Preferences-->Power Management and look near the bottom of both the "On AC Power" and "On Battery Power" tabs.

If this isn't the problem, then there is probably something you need to change within your HAL configuration.
