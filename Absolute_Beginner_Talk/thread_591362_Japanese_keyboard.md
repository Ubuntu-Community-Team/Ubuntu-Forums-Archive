### Post by Joe88 on 2007-10-25
you can change the keyboard setting by:

1.click on the system menu in the top bar of your desktop
2.move the mouse over preferences
3.then click keyboard
4.a window will then be displayed, click on the layout tab
5. click the add button
6. find the keyboard settings you want from the list, then click add
7. finally click the 'default' circle next to the keyboard settings you want to activate
