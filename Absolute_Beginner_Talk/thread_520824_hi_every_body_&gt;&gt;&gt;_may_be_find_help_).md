### Post by phr0ze on 2007-08-08
You will need about 256MB of ram to install with the standard method. 128MB and smaller is possible. Just about any x86 compatable CPU will work. You don't need much hard drive space but I personnally like to have at least 10GB to play with.

The download link is here [www.ubuntu.com](www.ubuntu.com)

Please also see if there are forums in a language you are more comfortable with here. [http://www.ubuntu.com/support/community/webforums](http://www.ubuntu.com/support/community/webforums)

We'll be happy to help you here, but if it's too difficult to understand you, you may not get responses.
