What are you trying to accomplish? [MySQL]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/mysql-server") and [PostgreSQL]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/postgresql") are two of the popular databases to use. Both work well for a web site.

If you are looking for not just the database, but also a front-end (similar to Access on Windows), there are a number of options [here]("http://linuxappfinder.com/databases/editorsinterfaces").
