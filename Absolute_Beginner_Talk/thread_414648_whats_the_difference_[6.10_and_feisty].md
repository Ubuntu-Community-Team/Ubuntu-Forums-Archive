### Post by freebird54 on 2007-04-20
Short answer - probably not.  Not because it isn't better, but because it is too hard to get to it till the rush subsides!

Most of the changes are aimed at a better initial experience,  and thus there is no WOW when you first switch.  It uses a newer kernel, and enhancements have been made elsewhere as well.  Wait at least a week for things to settle - keep an eye on the forums for problems if any, and enjoy!
