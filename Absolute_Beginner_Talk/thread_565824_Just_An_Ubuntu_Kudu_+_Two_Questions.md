### Post by mysticmatrix on 2007-10-02
Firewall, its inbuild although not visible. you can install a GUI fronted(I guess it's named firestarter). Check add/remove

Regarding Updates: Ubuntu updates both the software you installed as well as various needed security updates. Security updates and bug fixes are impotant. They are shown as seperate section.
You may choose not to upgrade your applications.
Upgrade is even more powerful Soon you'll be able to upgrade your entire OS, to ubuntu 7.10, simply through update manager. So in short, nothing except security updates is required if you can live with old software.
