### Post by avik on 2007-08-02
Right click and uncheck Show Hidden Files.  Yes, these folders are normal, but because they start with a dot, they're supposed to be hidden.

And yes, I believe the screenshot issue is because of compiz or beryl.

Just press Ctrl-H to toggle visibility of hidden files. I think it's in the right-click menu also. (and yes, they are all supposed to be there).

Tiltle bars and window borders are not included as you are taking a screenshot of the running program and window borders are not part of it. I think if you use Metacity and take the screenshot using Applications/accessories/Screenshot instead of just hitting PrtScr key you have the option to include window borders and titlebar.

Also Beryl had this option somewhere, at least at some time, but I newer got it to work.

Just take a full-screen screenshot and crop it with Gimp..
