I have been reading this forum and noticed that (some)  people are using bigger than 32GB fat32 partitions!! (60GB 300GB etc...)

You shouldn't do that!

32GB fat32 partition is the maximum size that windows 2000/XP can make..

I have had problems with bigger than 32GB fat32 partitions.. Sometimes like after a month or few, windows 2000 automatically tries to FIX those partitions, and it ends up corrupting them... automatically.. isn't that nice? got to love automation...
Well actually I have been able to rescue the files from those FIXED partitions, but it isn't fun....
