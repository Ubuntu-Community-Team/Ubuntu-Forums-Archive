### Post by heimo on 2007-05-17
Directories beginning with dot are hidden. Open nautilus and hit / key. Then write or copy paste ~/.mozilla-thunderbird/ to the location bar and hit enter. You're there.

---

### Post by Vixis on 2007-05-17
You may also hit ctrl+h to unhide files.
