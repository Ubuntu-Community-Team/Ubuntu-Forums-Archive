### Post by Mogurijin on 2008-04-01
I personally wouldn't really bother, you can just use the Ubuntu live cd to adjust partition sizes. Or you can just wipe your whole hard drive, partition it as you like and re-install Windows (do this before installing Ubuntu so you don't have issues with Grub). The first option might be quicker and easier. Also, if you do it your self you have more control, and can easily make adjustments until your happy. Especially if you just playing around. But that is just my two bits. For purposes of playing around Ubuntu does fine on 10 gigs.
