### Post by phr0ze on 2007-06-23
For each mail service you have to look up their custom settings. Some mail services wont allow you to check the mail with any other client and you must use their webpage. To look up the settings just type something like Yahoo mail settings in google. You'll have to do this for whatever services you are using.  It usually doesn't matter if the settings are for outlook or not, they should be the same.
