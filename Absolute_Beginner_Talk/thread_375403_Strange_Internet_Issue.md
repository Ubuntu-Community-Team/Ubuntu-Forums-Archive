### Post by tbodine on 2007-03-03
Oddly enough I had this problem not too long ago -- I could get to Google, use XChat for IRC, and Gaim for IMing, but nothing else, and I also have a Linksys router.
What I did was merely unplug the router and modem, wait about a minute to give them time to reset, and plug them back in. Make sure everything comes up, and you should be good to go -- it worked for me anyways, hopefully you have the same luck.
