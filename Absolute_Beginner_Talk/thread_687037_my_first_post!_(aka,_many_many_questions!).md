### Post by randy78 on 2008-02-03
For security related questions, refer to this great guide by bodhi.zazen:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=510812](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=510812)

Always remember that security is a mindset and that you should always take the necessary steps to ensure minimal exposure to potential threats.

I recommend enabling the Firestarter firewall:
sudo apt-get install firestarter

Here's a quickstart guide:
[http://www.fs-security.com/docs.php]("http://www.fs-security.com/docs.php")

I wrote a tutorial on how to set up right click virus scanning with Avast anti-virus (some people will tell you to use Clam, but it is completely up to you)
[URL="http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=685514&highlight=avast"]
http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=685514&highlight=avast[/URL]

Another useful guide is the Ubuntu Training Guide... A great guide to get you started, written by the generous and kind folks of our community:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Training?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=student.pdf]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Training?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=student.pdf")

Check out [http://ubuntuguide.org]("http://ubuntuguide.org") as well.

Welcome to Ubuntu!
