### Post by bobpur on 2007-08-18
Stay away from the Soundblaster X-Fi cards by Creative Labs. At least, for right now, as they are not supported by linux.
I have a machine that has an X-Fi card in it. It works great for Win XP. 
When I want sound on Ubuntu. I pull the jack from the X-Fi card and plug it into the on board sound card built into the motherboard. The on board sound is disabled in Windows because of the X-Fi card but works OK for Linux.
