### Post by vayde on 2007-03-08
Skins:  open your tarball of skins and put them somewhere.  

How? Double click on the tarball (tarball is general term for tar.gz or .tgz file) or type 'tar xzvf <filename>' from a command prompt.  Right click on player and choose Options->Skin Browser.  Point resulting screen at wherever you opened the tarball.

Playing streaming stuff:  Right click & choose Play->Location

Good luck.

### Post by bonzodog on 2007-03-08
For xmms skins you do not need to unpack them.

Simply place them in your /home/$USERNAME/.xmms/skins directory.

Xmms can read winamp 2 skins as well.

As for radio, go to the link for the stream on the website, if its mp3 or ogg, thats good. Realaudio or aac might not work.

Right click the link in your browser and simply choose open with...

Xmms might already be an option. If it isn't then you need to browse to the location of the xmms binary, which is /usr/bin.
