### Post by sardion on 2006-12-03
Programs live in /usr/bin (well most of them).

To run geekcode, just open up a terminal and type

geekcode

and press return.

Or, you can put in your menus or make a launcher (done differently depending on how you have your desktop set up).  But the path is /usr/bin/geekcode.

Hope this helps.

---

### Post by sardion on 2006-12-03
By the way, anything in the ubuntu main repos will pretty much automatically show up in your gnome or kde menus but sometimes the stuff in universe doesn't (such as geekcode).  If after installing something you don't "see" it, try just typing the command name in the terminal.
