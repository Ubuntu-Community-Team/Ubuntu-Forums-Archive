### Post by hw-tph on 2006-05-03
It's called font anti-aliasing. ClearType is a Microsoft trademark (they picked up on it a couple of years after Apple and the Linux community...).

You can disable it in System menu --> Preferences --> Font. To me, using a laptop and a desktop with TFT, subpixel smoothing looks very well.
