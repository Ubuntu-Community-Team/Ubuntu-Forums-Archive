### Post by rsambuca on 2007-09-06
Go with Feisty, unless you want to be testing alpha software.  Feisty is  very stable, as is Edgy, as is Dapper.

Keep in mind you can't go directly from Dapper to Feisty via the upgrade method.  You will have to go to Edgy, and then Feisty.  Another method is to back everything up and just install Feisty overtop of your existing Dapper.

More info is [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes").
