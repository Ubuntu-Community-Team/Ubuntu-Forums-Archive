### Post by gingermark on 2006-06-27
Don't extract them.

Press Alt+F2 and type 'kcontrol' into the box and press enter.
Under "Appearance and Themes" you will find the "Theme Manager". Load that up, click "Install New Theme", and then select the theme you want to install.

Of course, if it's an icon theme or a colour theme etc. then go the relevant section and do the same there.
