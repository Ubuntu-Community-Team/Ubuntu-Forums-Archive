### Post by aktiwers on 2007-07-17
Right-click on the panel and pick "add to panel". Then navigate down and find the Trash under "Desktop and Windows".

The trash is located at /home/username/.Trash

Yes with that dot infront of it. and "username" is your username. :)

To see it from filebrowser, go to your Home Folder and press CTRL+H. 
(Folders with a "." are hidden folders.)
