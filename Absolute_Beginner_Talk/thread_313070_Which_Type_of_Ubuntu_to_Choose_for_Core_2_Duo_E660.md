### Post by Bachstelze on 2006-12-05
Hi and welcome to Ubuntu :)

"x86_64", "x64" and "amd64" are the same thing. On Intel "Core 2" chips, the technology is called EM64T (as opposed to IA64, which is used on Itaniums) but if you wand 64 bit suport in UBuntu, the "amd64" is for you.

But beware, some non-free apps you may want might not be available on 64 bit.
