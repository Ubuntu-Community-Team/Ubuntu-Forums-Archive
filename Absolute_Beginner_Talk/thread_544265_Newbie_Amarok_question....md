### Post by orange2k on 2007-09-06
Well, you can enable the NTFS read/write tool - its available in the add/remove programs list.
This way, the music that you have on your XT partition will be accessible at all times...

And yes, Amarok is one of the best music players available (not only on the Linux platform).
But you also might want to try Exaile, Beep Media Player (BMP) and many others...
