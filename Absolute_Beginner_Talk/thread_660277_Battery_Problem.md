### Post by Toxicity999 on 2008-01-06
It's most likely the battery itself, or odd behavior in the percent calculations, which happens often enough. Try unpluging it and see if it actually dies at 0. Your battery could also likely not retain a full charge in reality, such as it charges as full as it can "100%" but is only a physical value of X%, see what I mean? I neither case it's not really something you can just fix, so study it and adapt. If it's the actual battery, then work on replacing it.
