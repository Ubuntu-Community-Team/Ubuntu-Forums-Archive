### Post by MartinG on 2006-02-06
No, totally different.  Synaptic maintains your system and is the normal route for adding software. Automatix is designed solely to add software not in the default repositories, by enabling extra repositories and other trickery.

You can have Synaptic without Automatix and do things done for you by Automatix in the messy way, but you can't use Automatix to replace Synaptic.
