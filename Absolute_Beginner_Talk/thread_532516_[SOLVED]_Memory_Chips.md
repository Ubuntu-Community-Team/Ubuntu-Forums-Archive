### Post by w4ett on 2007-08-22
My favorite suspect is always the PSU.....Beg borrow or steal a tester and check it....also do a close visual inspection of the motherboard capacitors for signs of leakage or the tops 'popped' up....run your finger over the top of them if you have to.....hopefully if the PSU is bad you can RMA it under warranty.
