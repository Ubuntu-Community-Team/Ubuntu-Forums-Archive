### Post by dondad on 2007-12-27
> **Hospadar said:**
> you can change that stuff with compizconfig-settings-manager, install it from synaptic.

After you install the configuration manager, open it, select the cube and in the appearance tab there is an item called skydome that allows you to put a picture of your choice behind the cube. The fish swimming in the cube is a plugin called atlantis, but I don't think it is in the normal setup. There is one called cube gears that will put a geartrain in the cube. There are also settings for transparency of the cube and also what you have for the endcaps.
