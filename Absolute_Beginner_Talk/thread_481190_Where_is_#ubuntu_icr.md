### Post by 5-HT on 2007-06-22
#ubuntu is an [irc]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_Relay_Chat") (internet relay chat) channel on irc.freenode.net
It's not a website, and you'll need an irc client to use irc. xchat is in the repos and is a favorite of mine, but there are many others. Just do a search for irc and install what strikes your fancy. 

To join, connect your client to irc.freenode.net and enter
```
/join #ubuntu
```at the prompt. The client's help system will get you going on the irc commands.
