### Post by mscman on 2006-06-01
I think it's a good idea to have your home directory on a separate hard drive, particularly if you are planning on storing most/all of your data there.  Your system drive shouldn't take up near that much space, and that way if you have to reinstall, your settings and data in your home directory won't be affected.  Due to limitations on my computer, I'm unfortunately unable to use this method (only one hard drive will fit currently.  I'm working to fix this problem...), but hope to be able to do this soon (reinstalls are on occasion unavoidable...)

I would stay just stick with what you have, sounds like a good setup to me!
