### Post by rubbsdecvik on 2007-04-02
When the video is playing, you can right click on the video, and one of the menu items is "Deinterlace."  There a few different settings.  Sometimes finding one will help get rid of jumpyness and random lines.  I tend to use the "blend" mode.  It can help a little.  I"m not an expert though.
