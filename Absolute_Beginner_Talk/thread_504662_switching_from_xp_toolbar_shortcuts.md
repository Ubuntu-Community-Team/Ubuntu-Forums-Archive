### Post by kyphi on 2007-07-22
There is a toolbar at the top of the screen.  Each entry generates a dropdown menu.  Your personal files all get placed in your Home folder under the Places entry accessible by two mouse clicks.

You can even access your system files from there using the up/left arrows.

You can generate an icon for your Home folder on your desktop and you can even increase or decrease the size of that icon.  That would save you one mouse click.  Personally, I prefer the clean and uncluttered look of the Gnome desktop and keep it icon free.
