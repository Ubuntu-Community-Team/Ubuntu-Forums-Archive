### Post by Tux Aubrey on 2007-06-12
Gutsy is very alpha.  Don't go there!  It will break.

Download the 7.04 (Feisty Fawn) live CD (Desktop) - get the 32 bit version "Standard personal computer " from here: [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download) (64 bit works but you will not have great support for some programs and the improved performance is marginal anyway).

Burn it at no more than 8X speed.

Boot with the Live CD and test, play explore.  I'd suggest you do this for at least a few days to see if you like it and want it.  It doesn't suit everyone - mind you I installed 6.06 after five minutes and haven't looked back.

Before you even consider installing it read [THIS SITE]("http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php")  - there is a lot there about preparing your computer and partitioning.  Then come back to the forums to see what sort of issues new users have.

I am advocating a cautious approach because I see so many people diving in and having problems.
