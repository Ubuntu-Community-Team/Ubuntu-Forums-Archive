### Post by teet on 2007-01-13
Plug it in :)

I can verify that MY Sound Blaster Audigy SE worked "out-of-the-box" on my Ubuntu 6.10 Edgy Eft system.

The only drawback of the card is:  it doesn't have a PCM so gmplayer freaks out and won't work.  I could probably fix it somehow, but totem works well enough.  Also, I have my gnome keyboard shortcuts set up so I can just hit Alt + A or Alt + Z to increase or decrease the main volume, but it wouldn't work after I installed the new soundcard.  Besides those two minor things I didn't have any other problems.
