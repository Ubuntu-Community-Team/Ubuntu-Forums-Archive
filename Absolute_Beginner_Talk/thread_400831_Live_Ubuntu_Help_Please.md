### Post by Dual Cortex on 2007-04-04
Most likely CD/DVD drive is not the first in your boot sequence.
Try pressing F12 (this is the common key to bring up the boot menu) almost immediately after turning on your computer. Then if the menu is brought up, choose your optical drive to boot.

Otherwise, if your PC is a bit old, you'll have to enter the BIOS (press F2, or DEL) and change the boot sequence.
