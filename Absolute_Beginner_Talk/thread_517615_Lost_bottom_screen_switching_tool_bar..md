### Post by deew on 2007-08-04
1. Right click on the top panel and select "New Panel".
2. Left click and hold down on the new panel and drag it to where you want it.
3. Right click on the new panel and select "+ Add to Panel".
4. A dialog box will pop up with a long list of things you can pick from such as "Trash" or "Workspace Switcher".

There you go.
