### Post by rubbsdecvik on 2007-10-01
When you add the custom bookmarks and entries, you are forcing the system to look in many different directories where the entries point to.  In the default configuration, all the directories are very close.  Your custom entries are forcing the system to do a lot more searching.

I could be wrong, but I'm not sure that you can do a whole lot to fix it, other than get rid of some of your custom entries.  

Someone correct me if I'm wrong.
