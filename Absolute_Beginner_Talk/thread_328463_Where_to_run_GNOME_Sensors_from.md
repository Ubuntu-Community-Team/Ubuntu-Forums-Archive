### Post by Malta paul on 2007-01-01
Hi 
Regards Gnome sensors you can find a help document at:
/user/share/gnome/help/sensors-applet/c/sensors-applets xml

To use the applet:
Note: The 'Control Panel' is the 'Line' above or below your screen (ie where the trash can is)
Right click on the panel>Add and remove - this will bring up a pop-up screen, go to System Hardware area then drag the 'Hardware sensors Monitor' icon to the control panel, and position where you want you can then right click on the icon and click 'Lock to panel"

Well hope this helps ;)  Have fun:D
