### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-09-11
Hi

Presumably, you can get online from another computer, thus your post...

It is possible to download the packages, put them on a USB jump drive or burn to CD, then install them on your computer.  Be aware, however, that if these packages have dependancies (other packages they require to run), you won't be able to install them without internet access... or making note of the listed dependancies, going back to the other computer, downloading more...

[Here is a page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats#head-dd75cf9cf440f1782515e6df311684e6f981b4e4") that will give you the names of the packages you're looking for.

Here is where you'll find the [totem-gstreamer codecs]("http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/pool/multiverse/g/")

Here is where you'll find the [xine codecs]("http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/pool/multiverse/x/xine-extracodecs/"), in case you prefer the totem-xine setup over totem-gstreamer.

And last but not least, here's where to find the [w32 codecs]("http://packages.freecontrib.org/plf/pool/dapper/")
