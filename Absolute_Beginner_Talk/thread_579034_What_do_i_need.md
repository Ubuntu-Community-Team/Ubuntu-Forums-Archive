### Post by Xenaco on 2007-10-17
I have a Micron Transport XKE laptop with the same specs as yours.

I loaded Xubuntu 6.10 and it ran satisfactorily.

I just loaded [[COLOR="Blue"]Puppy Linux[/COLOR]]("http://www.puppylinux.org/user/viewpage.php?page_id=1") which runs all in memory from the LiveCD.  It's a very small
distribution of Linux and it includes everything you need for browsing the Internet.

I use Ubuntu or Xubuntu for everything else but for this limited hardware Puppy 3.00 has the best performance.
By the way, Puppy will co-exist with any other OS on the hard drive and run just fine from the LiveCD or if your computer supports it, a Flash drive.
 You need not take Ubuntu or Xubuntu off of the system before trying out Puppy.
