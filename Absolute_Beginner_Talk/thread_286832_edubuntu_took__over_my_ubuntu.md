### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2006-10-28
For me I like some of edubuntu's artwork but thats about it.
I just took the other way of getting edubuntu looks on my computer by going
[here]("http://packages.ubuntulinux.org/")
downloading [this]("http://mirrors.kernel.org/ubuntu/pool/main/e/edubuntu-artwork/edubuntu-artwork_0.1.0-32_all.deb")
extract it with File Roller as opposed to installing it with gdebi
after that I went inside the extracted folder, then extracted the "Usr" zip file.
after that went into said extracted "user" folder, then went into "share"
then "themes"
then I turned the folders inside into zips by using file Roller (right click, move down and select "create archive")
after I did that I opened up the "theme" option in system > preferences
dragged the two zip folders I mafe into the themer and BAM!
Got myself the edubuntu themes without the edubuntu hijack!
(I had this happen to me when I wanted the edubuntu theme on my system as honestly I like the theme minus the cartoony icons)
