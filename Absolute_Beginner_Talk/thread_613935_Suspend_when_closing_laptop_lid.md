### Post by victorgreen on 2007-11-21
Suspend to ram takes all the programs you have open and saves em to ram and has the battery provide only enough power to keep the information in the ram and the entire rest of the computer shuts down. 

You can tell the computer what you want it to do when you close the lid in the power manager. [system==> preferences==> power management]
