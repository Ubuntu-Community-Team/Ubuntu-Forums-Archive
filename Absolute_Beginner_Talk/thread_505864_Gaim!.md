### Post by srunni on 2007-07-20
No, it doesn't support webcams. While support is planned in the future (I think), there is definitely no support right now. If you want to try to make your webcam work, I know that Kopete, the KDE IM client has at least partial support. Though it's a KDE application, you can run Kopete in GNOME (the default Ubuntu desktop environment) if you want.
