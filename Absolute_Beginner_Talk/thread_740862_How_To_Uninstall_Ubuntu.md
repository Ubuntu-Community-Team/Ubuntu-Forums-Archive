### Post by abhiroopb on 2008-03-31
Couple of questions:

1. Are you dual-booting? Sorry if this is stupid but I can't make out from what you have written, I assume you are.

2. WHY exactly would you want to uninstall ubuntu? I mean leaving aside the fact that its a solid OS, is there any particular reason? Because if you want to do it to simply upgrade then you do not need to uninstall it.

Finally:

If you DO want to "uninstall" ubuntu without disturbing windows. The easiest (and probably safest) is to go into your windows install. Use a disk partition manager such as [http://www.partition-manager.com/](http://www.partition-manager.com/) (or the one installed in windows). And simply format the Ubuntu partition.
