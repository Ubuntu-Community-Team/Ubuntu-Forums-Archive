Just click on the K menu, go to Internet and Firefox should be there.
You could also right click on the panel, select add a new application (or something like that, I'm in gnome right now ;-)) then go to Internet again and choose Firefox and you should now have a Firefox icon on your panel.

Oh and to really answer your question, I think you can simply drag the Firefox launcher from the menu to your desktop, but again, I'm not totally sure.
