### Post by gupta_sumesh63 on 2007-11-19
In compiz setting manager double click on > desktop cube. Then the > Appearance tab.
For a image behind the cube add the path of the image to > skydome.
uncheck > animate skydome.
close the manager.
You should see the image when you rotate the cube.
I hope you meant to have the image there.
