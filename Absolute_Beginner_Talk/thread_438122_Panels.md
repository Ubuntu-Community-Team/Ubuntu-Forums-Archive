### Post by NilsE on 2007-05-09
SyL hit on it.  Other than what some themes can and will do to panels you can modify the panel yourself by right clicking on the panel and selecting preferences.  You can change the colors, change the height, or add a graphic to apply.  

As far as a graphic goes you can create one in gimp or steal one from elsewhere.  Remember, it will tile left to right so make sure it does not have edges or it will look funny.  Also, make the graphic either the same size as the height or larger or it will also try to tile top to bottom.
