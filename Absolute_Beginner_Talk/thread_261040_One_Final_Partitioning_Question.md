### Post by xpod on 2006-09-19
If your partitions are already made and you have already installed XP to the first part of your drive(unless you have more than one hd)You should then be able to fire up the installer once the Ubu cd is loaded and when you get to the partitioning stage choose the "create partitions maually" option..

It`ll then show you the partition table with your XP partition at the top of the list and your ubunto "/" and "swap" below that.(as long as you have created them)Make sure the XP box is not checked to be formatted then off you go.

Its all as simple as that if you`ve done your partitions beforehand.
