If you haven't already done it, if you do a fresh install, set up a separate partition for your /home directory. That way if you ever need to reinstall again, or move the drive to another machine, you can redo the OS and keep all your data. I think I am a charter member of the "if you break it, you get to keep all of the pieces" club and having a separate /home partition has saved me from myself more than once. ;-)
