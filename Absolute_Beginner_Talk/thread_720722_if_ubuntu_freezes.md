### Post by Shazaam on 2008-03-10
VT means Virtual Terminal.
If your Ubuntu freezes you can do this=
Hold down the Alt+Sys Rq (print screen) keys and type in R E I S U B
This will gracefully shut down your pc. See this....

[http://fosswire.com/2007/09/08/fix-a-frozen-system-with-the-magic-sysrq-keys/](http://fosswire.com/2007/09/08/fix-a-frozen-system-with-the-magic-sysrq-keys/)
