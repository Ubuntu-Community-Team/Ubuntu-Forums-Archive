### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-04-17
you can get it in the repositories as long as you enable the partner repositories, just go to system>administration and click on synaptic package manager.
under settings there is a selection called repositories, here you can edit where you get stuff.
make sure to enable every repo except the last one on the first tab by clicking on the boxes, and in the second tab make sure to select the first partner repo, not the source partner repo.
