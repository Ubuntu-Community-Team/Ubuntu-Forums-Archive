### Post by dstew on 2007-05-18
beijingjj: I installed scim for Chinese character input and it works well. My co-worker uses it to input Chinese characters in Firefox web pages to email her sister in China. I used [this wiki]("http://www.scim-im.org/wiki/documentation/installation_and_configuration") to guide my installation. On the wiki, you have to read and follow the instructions in the All --> System Configuration page as well as the Ubuntu/Kubuntu page. You have to pay very close attention to setting up the environmental variables. Do not skip that part.
