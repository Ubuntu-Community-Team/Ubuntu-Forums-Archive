### Post by Kobalt on 2007-05-26
Hello,

you will find many themes on [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org). To install a theme, go to System > Pref. > Themes and add them from here. 
The things you call 'ribbon' are actually called dock. You can find many over these forums, but my pick would be [Avant Windows Navigator ]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=385981")(the closest to Mac OS X dock, in terms of look and functionalities).
