### Post by smacattack on 2007-11-16
What you can do is... if the radio station has a .asx link on their website, you can go to the 'Radio' tab in Rhythmbox, rightclick and click 'add new station' and paste the .asx link in and it will let you stream it whenever you want from rhythmbox, rather than through the browser.

I'm not sure how many websites carry such a think but I often listen to national news broadcasts from around the world (Canada, Sweden, Netherlands etc) and they all have this feature.
