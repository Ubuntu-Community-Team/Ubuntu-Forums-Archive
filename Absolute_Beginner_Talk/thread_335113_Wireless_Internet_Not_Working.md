### Post by Atomic Dog on 2007-01-11
Try opening up system-->administration--> networking.  Click on your wireless properties.  Enable it, enter something like linksys for an ssid.  Close and then activate it.  Now go in and disable it and deactivate it.  NM will probably now offer your wireless again.  Possibly have to restart -I don't remember, but this happened to me and that's what I did to get it working again.
