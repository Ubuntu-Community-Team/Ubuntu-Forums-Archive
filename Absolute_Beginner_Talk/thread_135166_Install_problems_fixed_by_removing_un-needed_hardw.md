### Post by TrevorNet on 2006-02-23
About a week ago, I dropped Ubuntu on my home machine (after giving up on Gentoo) and have become a rather happy camper. Over the past few days, I have been trying to install Ubuntu on an extra computer at work. I'd get all the way through the install... When everything completed, and I expected to see the log-in screen, I'd find myself with nothing more than a blinking cursor in the upper left-hand corner of my screen. No matter how many forum posts I looked through, nothing solved my problem. In a last ditch effort, I removed an old video card, a un-used USB2 card, and the modem... Everything is alove and happy now. :) So, if you are experiencing difficulties with your install, and you have un-needed hardware, try removing it. Worked for me.
