### Post by menancito on 2008-01-19
Ahh, don't you just love the world of Linux. 10 guys, 10 different opinions.

Here's mine:

I wouldn't use wine for a p2p or torrent client, because there are many linux programs which are simply better or at least on par with the Windows ones.

For torrents I use Azureus, 2.5.0.4. The programs is very customizable including many options for seeding and managing connections. It's JAVA, but hey, nobody's perfect.

For p2p - Amule. The linux version of eMule. The downloads are not extremely fast (like in ares for example) but the program is good all around and some of the servers are full of interesting stuff :)

Hope I helped.
