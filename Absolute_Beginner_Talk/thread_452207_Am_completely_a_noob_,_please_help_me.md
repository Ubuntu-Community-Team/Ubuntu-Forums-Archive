### Post by rickycodie on 2007-05-23
ok the way to get all that stuff is to first install your video drivers. if you have a nvidia or a ati driver then go to
system>administration>restricted drivers 
select your driver and you're good! if you don't have those drivers then don't worry.

then go here
[http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu_Feisty_with_XGL](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu_Feisty_with_XGL)

afterwards go to 
[http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/)

and download more eye candy.

to install icon themes go to system>preferences>theme
then click on the customize button, then on the icons tab. drag and drop you new theme into the window and your done!

as for codecs the easiest is automatix
but be careful! don't upgrade gibbon (the next ubuntu) with it on your system!! it will break your upgrade!!
the site is here
[http://www.getautomatix.com/](http://www.getautomatix.com/)

the other way is to do a manual install instructions here
[http://feistyguide.blogspot.com/search/label/Fiesty%20Fawn%20Intro](http://feistyguide.blogspot.com/search/label/Fiesty%20Fawn%20Intro)

have fun i switched and will never go back!!!
