### Post by Niniel on 2007-12-12
I don't know what people have against ATI, I have an HP z5000 notebook with an ATI mobility Radeon 9600, and it works, better in fact, than another desktop computer with an nVidia Ti4200 card. 

Stay away from IBM/Lenovo with their TPM chips, that stuff is the new devil. :)

The computer you seem to like is not that great, I would advise against it. 
First off, it has only a Celeron M processor - I'd go for a Core2 Duo with a new PC. Second, it has only 512 MB RAM, which is ok with Ubuntu, but if you want to do a lot of graphics work you'll need more, especially since you don't have a graphics card on that system, instead, it uses some of your RAM for graphics, decreasing the amount that's available for applications.
Get AT LEAST 1 GB, more would be better.
