### Post by coffeecat on 2007-05-26
What do you mean by 'I configured it to start'. It's not clear what you did. If you've installed Firestarter from the Ubuntu repositories with Synaptic or apt-get, you can start it from the Gnome desktop from System > Administration > Firestarter. You'll be prompted for your user password and then the basic configuration wizard will start. After that you can do more detailed configuration from the Firestarter window.
