### Post by Malta paul on 2007-01-01
Totem will not play your DVD as is. But will pay it if you configure it with Xine.
Go to App>Add/remove>search Xine, tick box>apply. It should now work.:) 

As 'Pissedoffdude" says VLC is a great player and will play more formats than Totem.
You can install the same way as Totem but this time Search for VLC.
