### Post by canadianwriterman on 2007-04-19
Go with Feisty. The applications are more up to date, hardware support is better and there is the transition wizard to pick up bookmarks and address books from your prior installation (yes, even Windows). But, most importantly, there are the wizards to install codecs for playing certain media files that normally take quite a while to get installed and working without the wizard. You can use Automatix2 to do those things, but some people prefer to use the "factory installed" process.
