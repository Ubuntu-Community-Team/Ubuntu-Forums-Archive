I would like to buy a LCD monitor, but I don't know what kind of interface my desktop uses. How can I determine it's interface and whether it's digital, analogue, etc.? 
FYI, it's a DELL dimensions 4550.

---

### Post by BHelts on 2007-07-01
if the plug you use is blue, it's VGA.  - analogue
if the plug you use is white, it's DVI. - digital

it looks like the Dimension 4550 came with AGP video card, so it's probably VGA.

a good way to go might be to buy a DVI monitor and then if you need, you can get a DVI to VGA adapter.
