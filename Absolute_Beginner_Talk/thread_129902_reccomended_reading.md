### Post by Madpilot on 2006-02-14
Another good O'Reilly book is the [Linux Cookbook]("http://www.oreilly.com/catalog/linuxckbk/index.html") - lots of short pieces on doing specific things. Flipping through it is a education in all the many things you can do with a Linux box.

For online command line stuff, linuxcommand.org has already been mentioned; there's also the Ubuntu wiki's [Basic Commands]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BasicCommands") page for starting out. (it's got links to other tutorials as well)
