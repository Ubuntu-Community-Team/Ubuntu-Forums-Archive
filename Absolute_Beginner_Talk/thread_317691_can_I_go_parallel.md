### Post by neemz on 2006-12-12
The installer will attempt to repartition your harddrive none-destructively, it usually works fine however I also do it by using Partition Magic in windows to create some free space first, then installing ubuntu is simply a matter of pointing it at the free space you created.

If you don't have any partitioning tools on your XP install (which most people don't) then backup any important files, take off your shirt, tie your shirt tie arround your head, place stones arround your computer in a perfect circle and try the installer :p
