### Post by joewhite on 2006-04-06
When I play games and I need to hold on to the keys Ubuntu generates annoying beeps. I tried muting my PC speaker but that hasn't stopped it. Any ideas?

---

### Post by GaFFy on 2006-04-06
Try opening up the Sound configuration dialog (under System->Preferences, and under system bell, uncheck "Sound an audible bell". Hope this helps. :-k
