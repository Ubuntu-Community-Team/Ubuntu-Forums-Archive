### Post by Shatrat on 2006-12-15
Converting from one lossy format to another is generally a bad idea because you end up with something with the worst of both formats.
When I rip a CD I rip it to ogg, but if you get something as an MP3 just leave it as an MP3 and get the right codecs so that you can play it in any of the media playing programs.
Obligatory Restricted Formats reading:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)
