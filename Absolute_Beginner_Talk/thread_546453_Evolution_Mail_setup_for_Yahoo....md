### Post by JReagan1990 on 2007-09-08
For Gmail:

The pop (incoming server) is pop.gmail.com

Check the "SSL" encryption box

The smtp (outgoing server) is smtp.gmail.com

SSL must be checked for this one as well.

The servers will automatically find the right port number.

For you sign-in info, use your username (not full email!) and password.

This should give you the ability to send/receive email through Gmail.

I do not know Comcast's server, and I think to get yahoo on an email client on your desktop, you must be a paying customer.

I hope this helps!
