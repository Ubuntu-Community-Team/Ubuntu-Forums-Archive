### Post by digitalbenji on 2007-12-13
Have you checked the trash can?  It will be very easy to restore them if you have not yet emptied the trash.  It will be named .trash<something> in your home folder.  You should also be able to find an icon for it in one of the menus or in the Nautilos explorer under Go->Trash.
