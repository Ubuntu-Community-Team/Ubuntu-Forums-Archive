### Post by Sef on 2007-03-19
Watch out for automatix.  It can mess up your install.  Flash is easiest to install this way:

1) Open the terminal (I forgot what it is called in Xubuntu. But the rest will be the same.) 

2) Add [Universe and Multiverse]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu"), if you haven't already.

3) Applications > Add/Remove > in search type in Flash > check Flash box > click apply > follow the directions.  (There will be a licesnse that you will have to click on yes to get it to install.)

If you have any problems then ask here.
