### Post by localzuk on 2006-05-16
If you sign up to the announcements list, yes you will get information about new releases (lists are available here: [https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo))

Second, it doesn't have 'its own' media player but comes with various players such as Totem - with many others available for download from the software repositories. In order to play mp3's you would need to follow the instructions to do so at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats) - as native mp3 support is not included due to patents and other legal issues.
