### Post by bapoumba on 2008-03-18
Please look for a "language Support" menu (I do not recall where it is located in GNOME) and tick French, then apply. It should install a bunch of packages. Then choose French as the default language. Log out your session and log back in. Voilà !

### Post by FM1 on 2008-03-18
Select French from the menu in System > Administration > Language support. You should get a prompt to download packages and may need to restart to apply the changes.
