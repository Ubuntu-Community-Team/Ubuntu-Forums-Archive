### Post by DarkN00b on 2007-04-04
Do you have the Window List applet on your panel? If not, then add it and see if that helps.

Right-click your panel, click Add to Panel, drag the Window List applet to your panel and close the Add to Panel window. All programs you open now should now appear on your panel.
