### Post by ewl1217 on 2006-07-21
From the menu, go to "System > Networking." From there, open the "Connections" tab, select "Modem connection," and click the "Properties" button. Check the box to enable the connection, then fill in the rest of the information as given by your ISP.

This should work, but I can't say for sure because I'm not a dial-up user. Let me know what happens.
