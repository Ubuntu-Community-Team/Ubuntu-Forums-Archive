### Post by Hospadar on 2007-11-12
Have you tried qdvdauthor?  Also I'm pretty sure devede will go straight from mpeg to burning a dvd (or at least making an image).

Also consider that there are tons of different options when transcoding video, so to get the best quality, often the default options just don't cut it and you need to use something more complete (like avidemux) to get the kind of transcoding that you want.

In general, I use avidemux to get exactly the video that I want, it has some good auto options with a lot of good and easy to use filters.  Then I use qdvdauthor to make the menus and author/burn the dvd.  I know that qdvdauthor *can* do transcoding as well, and it does have some gui stuff to control that, but I've always had really good luck with avidemux, and not so much with qdvdauthor transcoding (although all the other parts of qdvdauthor work well for me)
