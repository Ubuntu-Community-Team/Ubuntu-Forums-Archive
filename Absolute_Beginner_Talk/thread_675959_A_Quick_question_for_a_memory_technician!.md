### Post by bumanie on 2008-01-23
I'm not a technician (yet), but I believe, for value for money anything over ddr2 800mhz at this point in time is a waste of money. You will find ddr2 1066mhz is not much faster than 800mhz. ddr3 is very expensive and will only give a few % increase in speed. As things get refined and further developed, this will change, but at this time, ddr2 800mhz should be your best buy and give decent speed.
