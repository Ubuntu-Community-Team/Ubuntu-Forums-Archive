### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-02-22
> **carrizz said:**
> Can anyone tell me where book marks are stored for Opera and Firefox so that I can back them up.
Thanks

Sure, first keep in mind that they are in hidden directories so to unhide them go to the view menu and click it with your left mouse button and then click on the box/circle/whatever next to "show hidden folders"
or just hit ctrl+H

For firefox the bookmarks are located in:
/home/yournamehere/.mozilla/firefox/xxxx.default (this folder usually has some crazy number that always varies, but it almost allways ends with .default)

For opera:
/home/yournamehere/.opera
the file you want is listed as opera6.adr
