Most of the books on Ubuntu are fairly out of date with the GUI (which changes fairly rapidly), so don't bother with those. I was looking at the one's in my local bookstore last night and they referred to 6.10 as new stuff, so they're a year old(and came out in 2008 funny enough).

I'm currently writing a GUI-based book for Ubuntu/Kubuntu, but until then, online is your best bet, along with the official Ubuntu book. That's probably the only physical book worth buying since it's all relevant stuff with the GUI.
