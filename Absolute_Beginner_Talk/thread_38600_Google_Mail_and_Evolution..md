### Post by ~nix on 2005-06-01
Firstly you are required to enable POP retrieval from within the GMail settings. After you have done that, visit this link: [Settings](http://gmail.google.com/support/bin/answer.py?answer=13287).

All the settings required to setup GMail with Evolution are there :)
