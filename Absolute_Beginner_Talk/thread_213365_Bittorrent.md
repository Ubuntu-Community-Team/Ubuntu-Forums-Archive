### Post by HereInOz on 2006-07-11
You should have BitTorrent listed in your Applications > Internet menu group.  

As an aside, I personally prefer BitTornado - the BitTorrent client in Ubuntu is a bit dodgey - BitTornado, also available with Synaptic, is, in my opinion, much better, more flexible, and gives you much more information about the torrent.
