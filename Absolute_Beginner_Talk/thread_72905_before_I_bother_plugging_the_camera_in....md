### Post by wylfing on 2005-10-07
Ubuntu will automatically detect USB mass storage devices and will even display a little icon on the desktop for you. If it's a camera with photos on it, you will be asked if you want to import the photos. All very easy.

Now what you may or may not be used to from Windows is that you must explicitly unmount the device before disconnecting it. Right-click the icon that appeared on the desktop and choose Unmount volume. Then you're safe to disconnect.
