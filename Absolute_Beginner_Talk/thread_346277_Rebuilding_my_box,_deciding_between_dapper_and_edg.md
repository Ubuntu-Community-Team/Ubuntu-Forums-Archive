### Post by Kobalt on 2007-01-25
Ubuntu is not windows, you don't need a 'stronger' PC for each new version, Ubuntu actually gets faster with time. 
In terms of reliability some would advice you Dapper, as it's Long Time Support. I like Edgy better as well, especially thanks to Gnome 2.16 (as opposed to 2.14 of Dapper), and I never had any stability issue. Hardware support (especially for laptop user like me, for their battery) is also better in Edgy... I would advice Edgy.
