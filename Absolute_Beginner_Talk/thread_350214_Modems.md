### Post by ieee488 on 2007-01-31
The easiest is to buy an external SERIAL - not USB - modem. You can probably find one for a decent price on eBay.

There are PCI internal modems that are recognized by Ubuntu. You'll have to search and do more digging on eBay for those.   [www.linmodems.org](www.linmodems.org)
