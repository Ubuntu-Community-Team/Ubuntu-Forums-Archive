### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-06-22
Make sure all of your sub-channels are turned up as well.  Sometimes there are 2 stages of amplification that can be controlled.  You need to turn up both to get maximum volume.  Sometimes there's a switch to control this and you have to activate the switch so the slider appears.

Double click on the speaker symbol in Gnome and Edit-->Preferences and turn on all of the channels--everything.  Then go through each one to see its effect.
