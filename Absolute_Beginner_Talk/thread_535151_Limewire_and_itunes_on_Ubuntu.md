### Post by laidback on 2007-08-26
Frostwire is in the repositories so can be installed via synaptic. I have installed it on both my desktop and laptop with no difficulty.

For ipods...Amarok...reads and writes to my ipod with no problems, others report success too. Use:-

Settings>Configure Amarok>Media Devices choose plugin ipod from the drop down at the top of the page, now press Auto-detect Devices, when finished press OK. Your ipod needs to be in the USB socket throughout.
