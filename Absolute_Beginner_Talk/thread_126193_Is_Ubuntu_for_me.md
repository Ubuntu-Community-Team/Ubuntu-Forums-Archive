### Post by Mission 10 on 2006-02-05

1. Automtaix is Awesome- This will install alot of the codecs that you need for media. On top of that will get you up-to-date on programs, tweaks and other usefull stuff. It's a sticky at the top of the forum.

2. P2p I use the bittorrent that comes by default on Ubuntu and works well for me as that I don't download that frequently. But there are other posters that will be more into that than me.

3. Anti-virus. I haven't had a virus yet in 4 months of using Ubuntu. When I get one I'll reseach it. You'll find that virus' are not very common on *nix. You're more likley just to get a security vunerablilty, and those are resolved through periopdic updates.

4. Music mixer- Audacity is really cool and fun to use and I use it to record jam sessions on my guitar and export to mp3. But there are probably many mroe programs for that. 

5. Don't have dial up but other posters will give you advice. I know it runs fast on Ubuntu.

6. You can dual boot Ubuntu which means you don't have to ditch windows. Which is good if you are an avid gamer. But I'd try  Ubuntu first because the forums are excellent and you will really learn a lot here as I have.
