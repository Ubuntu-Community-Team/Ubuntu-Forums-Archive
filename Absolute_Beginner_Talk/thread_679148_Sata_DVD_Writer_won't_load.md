### Post by techstop on 2008-01-26
Your hardware might just plain not support SATA optical drives. I just tried installing a SATA DVD combo drive, and my motherboard does not detect it and there are no BIOS updates released to add this support. This is on NF7-S Socket A board. Check the support pages on your motherboard manufacturer's web site.
