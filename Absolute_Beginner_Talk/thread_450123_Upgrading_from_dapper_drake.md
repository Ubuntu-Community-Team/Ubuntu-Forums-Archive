### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-05-21
To upgrade from Dapper to Feisty, you'll need to follow a long route: Dapper>Edgy, Edgy>Feisty. [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/?action=fullsearch&context=180&value=upgrade&titlesearch=Titles](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/?action=fullsearch&context=180&value=upgrade&titlesearch=Titles)
I'd recommend against it. You'd most certainly have to redo the nvidia driver installation.
There is a way to write to ntfs on Dapper, so you do not need to upgrade.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MountingWindowsPartitions/ThirdPartyNTFS3G](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MountingWindowsPartitions/ThirdPartyNTFS3G)
