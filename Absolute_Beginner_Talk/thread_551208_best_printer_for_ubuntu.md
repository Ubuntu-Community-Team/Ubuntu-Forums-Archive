### Post by nomasteryoda on 2007-09-14
I just upgraded from Epson Stylus 900 and 960 to one HP-4260 for $70 US. The cartridges are standard HP found almost everywhere those things are sold.... Local corner pharmacy, department stores, ... 

This printer is great but I need to find the printer profile files for it to work correctly with GIMP. ... I just use Krita or Gnome Photo Printer tool for photos... This thing prints fast and matches my photos.

I just recommended yesterday that a friend get HP printer and HP scanner because they just work. Be sure to stay away from combo Fax/Scanner/Printers...  Why? Well what happens  when the Printer starts failing (4 yrs or so under normal use)? You have a big brick with a working scanner... No receiving Faxes ... as you have no printer to print them with... And, they take up more space. 

Good luck and hope this helps your decision. Either way you go, the OS is certainly the best decision you made :)
