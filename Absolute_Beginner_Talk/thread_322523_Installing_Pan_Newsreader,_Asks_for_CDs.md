### Post by rattking on 2006-12-20
Hi
I think I know whats up
in Synaptic go to Settings > Repositories
and uncheck the one labled cdrom in the URI section
and then make sure you have some other repositories enabled 
[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu#How_to_add_extra_repositories](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu#How_to_add_extra_repositories)
has a list of repositories for Breezy
and make sure to reload Synaptic after changing any repositories
