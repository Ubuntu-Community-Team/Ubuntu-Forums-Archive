If you have no sound at all this is the right place for you.

Try this: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HdaIntelSoundHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HdaIntelSoundHowto)

**NOTICE:**When compiling remember to CHANGE ALSA-14 TO 15 FOR ALL OF THEM (DRIVER, UTILS, LIB). For example while doing this 
 cd alsa-driver-1.0.14 change the alsa-driver-1.0.14 to alsa-driver-1.0.15, so you are basically changing the .14 part to .15 for ALL OF THEM.
