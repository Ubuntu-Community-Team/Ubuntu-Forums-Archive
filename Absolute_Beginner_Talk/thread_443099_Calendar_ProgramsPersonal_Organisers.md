### Post by Martin on 2007-05-14
Depends what you want. Evolution which is part of the basic Ubuntu install and is very similar to outlook in features and functions. Sunbird is a standalone calender prog. from mozilla which is quite nice, there's a calender extension for Firefox. there's a whole range. I like Sunbird because its simple and standalone and can take feeds from my google online calenders ( this is not exclusive to Sunbird, many calenders can sync with google etc.)
