### Post by louieb on 2008-01-28
How old a laptop? Could be the CMOS battery.  A dead battery will cause the PC to forget all it setting after being powered off.  

Most computer use some type of lithium battery. I've seen them last for 10+ years but eventually they do go out. 

I've found the easiest way to tell what battery you need is to go to ebay and  search for  your computer model + battery.
