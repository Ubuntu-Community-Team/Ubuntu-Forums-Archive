### Post by tyggna1 on 2007-08-21
you can subscribe to a thread at the top of each one by clicking on thread tools. . .that way you can access the thread from your user CP drop down menu later. That's the fastest way I've found.  It'll also let you get notified by e-mail if anyone posts on a particular thread if you so choose.
