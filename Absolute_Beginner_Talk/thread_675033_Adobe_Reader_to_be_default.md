### Post by uarale on 2008-07-06
Right click a pdf file > Open with Other Application... then choose Adobe Reader. Next time, you double click a pdf file, Adobe Reader will be the default program for opening it.

I don't have Adobe Reader on my pc, but i use the above method to switch beetwen VLC and Totem for the default mp3 player.
