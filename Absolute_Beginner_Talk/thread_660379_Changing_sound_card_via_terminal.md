### Post by Redache on 2008-01-06
You'll have to enter the BIOS and disable the on-board sound card.

Step to step you need to:

1) Turn your computer on and press DEL or whatever to enter the BIOS setup (It should have a message saying press etc. to enter BIOS setup
2)Find the part of the BIOS setup that houses your sound card.  Just go through the menu's until you spot it.
3)Disable it by changing the selection and save and exit the BIOS. Now Ubuntu should only see your Audiophile.
