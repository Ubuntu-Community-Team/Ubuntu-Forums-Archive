### Post by blastus on 2007-07-04
Take a look at your X.org log. The file is /var/log/Xorg.0.log. It provides a wealth of debugging information. Specifically take a look at the lines that are prefixed with NVIDIA as they will tell you how X.org is using your NVIDIA video card based on your /etc/X11/xorg.conf file.

Why do you have the BusID commented out? I'm quite sure that is necessary to identify the video card. If you have an onboard video card, have you disabled it in the BIOS? I would also default back to the open source NVIDIA driver. Change nvidia to nv. Test it out, and see if it works. Finally, do you have Windows on that machine? If you do I'd test your video card there as well.

To rule out the possibility of a faulty video card, you need to do some investigation first and eliminate some scenarios.
