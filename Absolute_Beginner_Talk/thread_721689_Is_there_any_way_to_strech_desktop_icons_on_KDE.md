### Post by tyler_roach on 2008-03-14
you can't stretch individual icons as in Gnome, but you can change the size of all your desktop icons by going to System Settings >> Appearance >> Icons >> Advanced (Tab), and changing the size of the Desktop/File Manager icons. I think this also makes them bigger in konqueror, too.
