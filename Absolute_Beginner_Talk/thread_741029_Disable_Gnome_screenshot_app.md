### Post by Alekth on 2008-03-31
Is there a way to disable the gnome preinstalled app for taking screenshots?

[This thread had info on completely disabling the app]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=624332"), but unfortunately it also disabled Print Screen taking a screenshot at all. Is there some other way to make Print Screen take a screenshot (and just have it in the clipboard) while the game saves it itself.

A second option, if that's not doable, would be to somehow me the screenshot app capture its screenies and stay in the background.

### Post by Nameless_one on 2008-03-31
Go to System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts and find the row that says "Take a screenshot". Click on it and press Backspace. That should work.
