### Post by 505 on 2007-08-02
It's on by default, and I think most keys are the same as on windows. Alt+tab switches the application. Alt+[letter] opens a menu in a application. Alt+F1 opens the application menu, and with the arrows you can go to places and system. Enter is the same as double click, and even the menu button (right to the right win-button) opens the left-click menu. You can even switch desktops, thats ctrl+alt+[left/right arrow]
