### Post by ComplexNumber on 2007-01-20

But when i click on it, the volume is on full, and music plays so it is obviously not on mute.

the reason why its showing muted is because your slider control must refer to  something thats muted. if you right click on the volume applet, then select 'preferences', you can select exactly what the slider controls. you obviously have it selected to something thats been muted. for example, you may have treble selected as being what the actual slider controls. select 'volume from the list instead.
