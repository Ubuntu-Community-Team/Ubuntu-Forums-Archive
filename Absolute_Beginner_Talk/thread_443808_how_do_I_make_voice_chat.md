### Post by CasperRed on 2007-05-15
My first post on the Ubuntu Forums is an answer? :shock: 

Or maybe I should call it a "clarification".

acpsiddhartha, are you looking for one-on-one chat or are you looking to chat to a group of people at once, like a group of people in a game?

If you are looking for one-on-one chat, you probably need to look into Ekiga Softphone or Skype, or one of the IM (Instant Messaging) programs out there. If you are looking for group chat, it would be hard to beat TeamSpeak. And TeamSpeak is an easy install for Ubuntu. 

Go to "Applications" -> "Add/Remove Applications". In the upper right corner, set it to show "All Available Applications". In the left window, select "Internet" and then in the right window scroll down to "TeamSpeak" Put a check in the box and click on "Apply". 

There will be a warning window, and I hove no idea if the program is legal for you to download, but it installed just fine on my system and works for me as well as it does in Windows.

You will need to access a TS Server, but a little research should help you there.

Hope I've been helpful.
