### Post by old_geekster on 2007-03-29
Install "Alsamixer" and check to assure that all of the necessary boxes are checked.

I had the same problem.  Plus, I wasn't getting sound from my rear speakers.  So, I installed "Alsamixer" and checked "Stereo and Duplicate front" --- let there be sound and there was.

Also, make certain that PCM, Master or another necessary option is not muted.
