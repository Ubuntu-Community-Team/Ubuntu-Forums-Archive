### Post by Holmes89 on 2007-07-31
I have a keyboard that allows you to control audio levels by increasing, decreasing, and muting. Now the computer recognizes that these are shortcuts and when I click them a picture comes up showing an increase, decrease, mute, but the the volume doesn't change. Does anyone know how to fix this so my audio will be changed with these controls?

---

### Post by lisati on 2007-07-31
It is possible to associate specific keys with specific actions. From the "System" menu, choose "Preferences", and from there, choose "Keyboard shortcuts". On the list there will be an entry for volume up and volume down.

### Post by spam10707 on 2007-07-31
I had a similar problem when I first installed.  It turned out to be a matter of having the wrong device selected.  Try selecting another device by going through System-> Preferences-> Sound  and then checking to see if another device is available.  Once you've selected that your buttons should work fine.
