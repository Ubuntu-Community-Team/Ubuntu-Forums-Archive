### Post by bumanie on 2007-10-30
I believe a program called devede will do what you want. The only problem is that for feisty and gutsy, part of the package had a bug that caused a hissing sound over the audio. The good news is that there is a fix on the download page at [http://www.rastersoft.com/programas/devede.html](http://www.rastersoft.com/programas/devede.html)
You can get devede from the repositories and the fix from the above website.
Using it is fairly self explanatory once it is installed and you see the GUI. It outputs iso files that can then be burnt by right clicking on the iso and choosing the burn to disc option.
