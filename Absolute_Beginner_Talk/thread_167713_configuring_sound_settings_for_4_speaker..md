### Post by _simon_ on 2006-04-29
um well I have an audigy2 Zs and 5:1 surround and all I did to get them all working was start alsamixer and:

Enable "audigy"
Set "surround" volume
Set "center" volume
Set "front" volume

Oddly front volume does my rear speakers even though everything is wired correctly.

I notice there is also a "audigy c" volume but it doesn't seem to do anything when i try it.
