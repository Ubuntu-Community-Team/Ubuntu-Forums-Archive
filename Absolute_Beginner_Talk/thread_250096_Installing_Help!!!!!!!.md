### Post by Episteme on 2006-09-03
I don't know if this will help, but I had a very similar problem on my first instal.

The problem was that my monitor (ok, projector actually), wasn't syncing with the resolution/Hz ubuntu selected for my video card - and the top and bottom of my screen weren't visible to me (all I could see was the background and my mouse).  I hit r-sync on my projector which then sync'd with my output and I could see the top and bottom pannels. From there I installed automatrix (which installed the latest Nvidia drivers for me), and changed my resolution and Hz to match my projector's native resolution [system - preferences - screen resolution]. 

Hope this works for you too.

Sean
