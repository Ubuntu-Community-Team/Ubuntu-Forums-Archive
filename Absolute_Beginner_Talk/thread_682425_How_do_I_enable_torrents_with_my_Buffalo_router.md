### Post by ajgoyt on 2008-01-30
Ok this is the instructions for Utorrent - Opera really isn't that much different you just need to know the default port that opera needs to be forwarded in the router - Then you go to the homepage of the router from either Opera or Firefox or Internet explorer log into you Home page of your router then go to ((((( Click the Internet Games {Port Mapping} )))) make sure you enter the port on both UDP and TCP.  Follow the instructions at that page or below.......


Address where i got this info  [http://www.portforward.com/english/routers/port_forwarding/Buffalo/WHR-HP-G54/Utorrent.htm](http://www.portforward.com/english/routers/port_forwarding/Buffalo/WHR-HP-G54/Utorrent.htm)



Open a web browser like internet explorer or Netscape. Enter the ip address of your router in the address bar of your browser. In the picture above the address bar has [http://www.google.com](http://www.google.com) in it. Just replace all of that with the ip address of your router. By default the ip address should be set to 192.168.0.10.



You should see a box prompting you for your username and password. Enter your username and password now. By default the username is root, and the password is blank. Click the Ok button to log in to your router.



Click the Internet Games {Port Mapping} link near the left side of the page.
