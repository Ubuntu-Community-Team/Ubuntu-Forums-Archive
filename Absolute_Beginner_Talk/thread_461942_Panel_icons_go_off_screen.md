### Post by Brightbelt on 2007-06-02
Right-click on the power button (or close enough hopefully to where it catches it) and select 'move'.  You may see a check mark by 'lock to panel'... If so, right-click and choose that first in order to Un-check it, then do right-click move.

I hope this helps, Frank B.
