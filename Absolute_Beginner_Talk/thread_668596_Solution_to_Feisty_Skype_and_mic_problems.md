### Post by Michl on 2008-01-15
I struggled for two days to get a mic to work in Skype on a Feisty desktop.
What irked me most was that skype and mic worked fine running Feisty on a laptop.
What confused matters more was that Feisty mics and recordings do need some debugging.
Suddenly I had a big idea to compare the versions of skype I had been running,

And lo, the one that didn't work was a package in synaptic from medibuntu I had seen,
While the one that worked was installed from skype.com, version 1.4.0.118.
So quickly to the Feisty desktop I returned and uninstalled the rotten skype
And installed the latest version that took away the reasons I had for this gripe.
