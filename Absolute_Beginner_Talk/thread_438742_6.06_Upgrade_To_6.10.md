6.06 Upgrade to 6.10 Team:  Hi Team,

When I installed Dapper Nov 28, my first Linux o/s, I really liked the LTS part. But last weekend I decided it was time to upgrade to Edgy before I was left behind. I did all my backups plus a Mondoarchive so I would be able to recover from a complete disaster. 

I decided to use the Official Upgrade Method cited by PWill in the first Sticky on the Installation & Upgrades Forum (first intelligent decision for at least a week). It was simply  $ gksu "update-manager -c"

That's it!   Absolutely no problems. Of any kind. It was a long download because of MY CATV connection, but the download and installation of Edgy was totally problem free.

My Congratulations and most sincere Thanks to the Dapper  to Edgy Upgrade Team!
   


My System:
Dual-boot XP/Ubuntu 6.10 (50/50) on a HP nx9010 notebook with a 40GB HDD and an Iomega 80GB removable usb HDD. ATI Display: PCI Bridge [GP 340M] Radeon IGP 330M/340M/350M RS200/RS200M AGP Bridge [IGP 340M] a HP Deskjet 5740 printer and a HP Scanjet 3770 flatbed scanner that only works in Windows.
