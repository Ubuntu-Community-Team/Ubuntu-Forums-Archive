### Post by Cato2 on 2008-02-03
You need to call your Internet Service Provider (ISP) - whoever provides your ADSL - and ask for this. The jargon is VCI and VPI - these are parameters that must be set identically in your ADSL modem/router and in the ISP (technically it's the 'DSLAM' node.)
