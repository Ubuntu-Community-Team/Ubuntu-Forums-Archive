### Post by freesitebuilder on 2007-08-15
The TurboPrint driver supports Canon IP1600 [http://turboprint.info/](http://turboprint.info/)

I use it with my Pixma IP3000 - you can try it free (which puts a logo on your output), and then buy it if it suits (about 30 euros, I think). I've found it OK for docs and graphics, supports double-sided, but I haven't tried photos or printing on CDs.

Used it with Dapper and now with Feisty.
