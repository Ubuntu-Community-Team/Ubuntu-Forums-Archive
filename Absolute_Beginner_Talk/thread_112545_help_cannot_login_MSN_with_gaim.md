### Post by thekiller on 2006-01-04
In GAIM, go to Accounts, then Modify your MSN account. On the pop up window, go under MSN options, make sure your login server is messenger.hotmail.com and Port is 1863

Under proxy options, Use global proxy settings.

Its working fine for me right now and im connected. Another thing to make sure is that you are entering the correct password :)
