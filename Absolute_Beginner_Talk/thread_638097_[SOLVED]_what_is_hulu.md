### Post by forrestcupp on 2007-12-18
It has shows and movies from the NBC and FOX channels.  Hulu is in beta testing stages and it is by invitation only.  Basically, you sign up on their website and wait for them to invite you.  It took about a month for me to be invited.  I just got my account yesterday.

I can verify that it does work with Firefox in Linux.  The videos are Flash based.  The only thing that doesn't work is full screen mode.  But they also have a mode called "pop out" that gives you a pop up window that shows it in full screen.  This works in Linux, but you have to set Firefox to allow pop ups.

If you don't feel like waiting, check out [OPENhulu](http://www.openhulu.com).  Hulu allows you to embed their videos on your website/blog.  Some guy set up a website and embedded *all* of Hulu's videos so people don't have to wait for an invitation.
