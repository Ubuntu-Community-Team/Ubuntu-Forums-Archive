### Post by CroEragon on 2007-01-22
I dont know about first question but about 2 question:
In your place, i wouldn't Install Fiesty on business laptop. I assume that you use it to do some serious work on that laptop, you do not have time to play with it, then i would even suggest dapper. Let me explain. Fiesty is in early development stage, i wouldn't be surprised if it wont install on some machines at all. It is full of bugs, and might be even dangerous for your computer. It is not even beta. Even when it gets out i wouldn't suggest upgrading straight away. Even when final product is finished it still might have some problems.
When every release is out it is of course stable, but to some extent. For example Edgy is stable release, but if you need solid and stable OS that just works then use Dapper. I dont say that Edgy doesnt work (i use it) but it is not what is Dapper in terms of stability. Of course Edgy with all current update is very stable OS but even with it it can't compete with Dapper that have much more fixes, testing and it has been built in longer period of time. Fiesty is in no way redy to be used as main os.
