### Post by cymen on 2007-05-30
Hmm, are you behind a strict firewall or something? I've seen people fix this sort of thing before by opening up their list of repositories

```
gksudo gedit /etc/apt/sources.list
```

and replacing every "http://" with "ftp://" which I suppose is worth a try. If it doesn't work switch 'em back to "http://" and maybe someone else can think of something.
