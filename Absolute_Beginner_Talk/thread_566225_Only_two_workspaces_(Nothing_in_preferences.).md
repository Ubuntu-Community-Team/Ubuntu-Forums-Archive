### Post by Jake90 on 2008-01-19
Hi, I think I had the same problem as you, and someone helped me resolve it. First try turning off compiz by going to System>Preferences>Appearance click on the visual effects tab and select none. Then try going back to preferences and you should get another menu.   Then switch the amount of spaces to however many you want. Then try turning visual effects back on. If that doesn't work then go to System>Preferences>Advanced Desktop Effect Settings. Click on general options. Then click on the desktop size tab. Set the Horizontal Virtual size to however many spaces you want going across. and the vertical to however many you want going from top to bottom. Hope this helps.
