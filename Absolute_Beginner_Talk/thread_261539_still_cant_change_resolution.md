### Post by alienexplorers on 2006-09-20
If you know the refresh rate you want to run along with the resolution you want you can you the modeline program to generate a line with your resolution and screen refreshrate rate to add to your xorg.conf file.  The gtf program is located at: [COLOR="Red"]http://www.sh.nu/nvidia/gtf.php[/COLOR]
