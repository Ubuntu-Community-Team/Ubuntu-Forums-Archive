### Post by Oldsoldier2003 on 2008-03-18
right click a header (like date) and select customize view


a window called "customize current view" will pop up 

click the sort button
sort items  from (chose ascending or descending)

thenby:
select date and descending

click ok on the "sort" window

click apply then ok  in the "customize current view" window
