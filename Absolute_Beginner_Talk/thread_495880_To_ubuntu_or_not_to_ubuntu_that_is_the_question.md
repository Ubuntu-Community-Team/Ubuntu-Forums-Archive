### Post by psyopper on 2007-07-09
All of the games listed either work wondwerfully in wine (WOW) or the developers have expressed dedicated support for Linux. Unreal3Engine will work in Linux, as has Quake since 3. If the engines work under Linux it's only a matter of the game developers/producers supporting Linux. And if they don't there's a good chance someone will come up with a workaround.

Spend some time on the game forums for the games you want to get and start asking about Linux support. The problem is that only 50% of the gaming quality video cards are supported in Linux at gaming quality capabilities right now. That would be the Nvidia half... ATI's 3-d drivers for their top end cards are non-existent under Linux.
