### Post by hw-tph on 2006-09-20
Of course you can cluster (K|Ed|X)Ubuntu installations. However, depending on what you're looking to do Ubuntu is most likely a lot too big - imaging Ubuntu installs just to run a single scientific application, for example, is a big waste of bandwidth and time.

Read more at the [Linux Documentation Project](http://tldp.org) and see what articles [LinuxJournal has to offer](http://www.google.se/search?hl=en&q=cluster+site%3Alinuxjournal.com).
