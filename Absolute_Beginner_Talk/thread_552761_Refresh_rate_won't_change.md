### Post by alienexplorers on 2007-09-19
You could try adding a modeline to your monitor section of xorg.conf file. > # 1600x1200 @ 60.00 Hz (GTF) hsync: 74.52 kHz; pclk: 160.96 MHz
  Modeline "1600x1200_60.00"  160.96  1600 1704 1880 2160  1200 1201 1204 1242  -HSync +Vsync
You can then use;
> gksudo nvidia-settings
to setup your graphic card.
