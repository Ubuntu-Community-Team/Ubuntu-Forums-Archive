### Post by AndyCooll on 2006-12-01
As far as I'm aware ogg codecs are installed by default anyway.

You can also convert mp3's to ogg if you wish (remembering that each conversation of a media file reduces its quality).

mp32ogg can do this for you, as can Audacity.

And yes all media players that come with Ubuntu can play them.
