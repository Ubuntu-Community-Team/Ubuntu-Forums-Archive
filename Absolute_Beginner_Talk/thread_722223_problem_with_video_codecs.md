### Post by Bölvaður on 2008-03-12
Totem (which many of the video clients are built upon) is doing that. Will probably be fixed by open up (Totem) Movie Player (or any other for the matter I think).
Click edit
Click Preferences
Display
Change Saturation accordingly.
