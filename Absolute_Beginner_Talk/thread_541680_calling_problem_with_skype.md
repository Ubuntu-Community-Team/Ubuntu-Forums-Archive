### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-09-03
I've been using Skype for Linux for some time, without issue.

It sounds like you need to set up your sound correctly.

Right click on the [COLOR="Blue"]speaker icon[/COLOR] in the top taskbar then click on [COLOR="Blue"]open volume control[/COLOR].

When the window opens, click on [COLOR="Blue"]Edit>Preferences[/COLOR]

When the window opens, just put a t[COLOR="Blue"]ick in every box[/COLOR].

Now make sure that the s[COLOR="Blue"]liders for microphone are at full.
[/COLOR]
Click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Recording Tab [/COLOR]and make sure the sliders are also at full.

Click on [COLOR="Blue"]Switches Tab[/COLOR] and make sure that [COLOR="Blue"]Microphone Capture is ticked.[/COLOR]

Hopefully it all should now work

By the way, Skype have not yet implemented video in the Linux version of Skype yet.
