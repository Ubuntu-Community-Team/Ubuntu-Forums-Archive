### Post by Jinx-Wolf on 2008-02-27
Finally figured it out...

I ran it with wine:
```
wine "/home/wolf/TransGaming_Drive/Program Files/Diablo II/Game.exe" -w
```

When i click out of it this time, it leaves a small window in the corner. Just click the window and hit: ALT+Return
