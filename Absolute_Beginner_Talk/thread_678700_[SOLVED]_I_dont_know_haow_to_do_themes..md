### Post by kpkeerthi on 2008-01-26
Open System -> Preference -> Appearance. Drag and drop the archive to the window and select it. With a gtk theme selected, click on Customize and there you can change icons and window borders.

### Post by Moop on 2008-01-26
You don't need to extract the theme files. Just go to System-Preferences-Appearence and then click install and browse to where your downloaded themes are. 

You can also install Art Manager from Add-Remove Programs. Then you can browse themes and install.
