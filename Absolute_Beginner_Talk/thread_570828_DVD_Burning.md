### Post by rscott5 on 2007-10-08
You should be able to tell it to copy the disc, and put the original into the DVD-RW. It will then copy the files onto your hard drvie and when it finishes it asks you to put in the blank disc. Burning in real time can be risky and most people end up with a lot of coasters. It's much safer to just use one drive.
