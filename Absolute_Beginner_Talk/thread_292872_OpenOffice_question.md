### Post by shaft350x on 2006-11-04
That will change it once, for whatever document you are currently working on.  If you want to change it so it is your default you will have to change the template.

The help files in Open Office will tell you how to go about doing this.  It involves opening a new file, making your desired changes and then saving the blank document as a template.  Then you make that template your default by going to templates > organize.  This is just a quick summary of what you would be doing, like I said the help in Open Office will tell you better than I just did how to do this.  Default Template were the keywords I used to get the right help file.
