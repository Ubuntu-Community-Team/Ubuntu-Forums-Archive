### Post by vayde on 2007-03-12
I hate to be the one to tell you this, but that printer is best used as a paperweight under linux.

Lexmark doesn't release specs on the internals of their printers, and therefore drivers can't be written for them.

check out [www.linuxprinting.org](www.linuxprinting.org) for more information.

[http://www.linuxprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=Lexmark](http://www.linuxprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=Lexmark)
