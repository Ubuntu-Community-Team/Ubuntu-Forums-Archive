### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-08-09
If you're more comfortable right now in a GUI, then by all means, use Synaptic over Add/Remove.  The Add/Remove panel is new and buggy.  Synaptic is clear, consise (we can debate this one), and to the point (was that redundant?)  Anyway, dependencies will always be met through the Synaptic if you use it correctly.  Read up a bit more about it and you'll find it to be a happy companion.  Okay, maybe not a happy companion, but at least it'll download things for you ;)
