### Post by Tomosaur on 2006-08-03
Steam will run under Wine, which you can install using Synaptic.

I'm not familiar with that printer, so I can't really comment.

Gaim can use the MSN network, so you can still talk with your friends very easily. You may also want to take a look at aMSN, or even kopete. aMSN is, I think, specifically designed to be an MSN Messenger/Windows Messenger clone, while Gaim and Kopete have their own style, but can connect to the MSN network.

Regarding your printer - hardware support out of the box in Ubuntu is extremely good. I really can't stress how much of a myth it is that getting hardware working is an endless struggle. Sure, you may need to fiddle to get your settings just right, but your printer should work straight away.

The normal Ubuntu install CD is a LiveCD, which means you can try it out and test your hardware without the need to install it first. It also has a bunch of programs which are popular open-source variants and clones to common closed-source/propietary software.
