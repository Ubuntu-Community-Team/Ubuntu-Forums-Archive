### Post by Soybean on 2007-05-25
> **discmaster said:**
> What to do? I guess I could shut beryl down when I am visiting those types of sites, but that's not really very convenient...there must be something else to fix it - no?

Eh, not really. Like the sticky says ([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=349732](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=349732)), Beryl is beta software, and instability is to be expected. That is, it's beta software in the traditional sense of the word, not the way Google uses it. ;) If stability is important to you, you probably shouldn't be running Beryl. :(

On the other hand, it depends how frequent the freezes are, and how much they annoy you. Even with the banking thing... it's not like your money is just going to vanish because your computer froze... either the transaction will complete or it won't, banks are pretty good that way. It can be a big hassle sometimes; the question is whether the jiggly windows are worth the hassle to you or not.
