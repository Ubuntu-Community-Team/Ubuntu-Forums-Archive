### Post by kernel tom on 2008-02-15
sure is

there should be a menu somewhere for Removable Drives and Media...
in Xubuntu you get to this by:
Applications>Settings>Settings Manager>File Manager Preferences
Click the "Advanced" tab, and then click the "Configure" link

now once in the Removeable Drives and Media menu, click "Multimedia" tab

Check the box for play audio CD's when inserted.

and for the command something like:
amarok cdda:/
