### Post by dfreer on 2007-03-19
> it all works fine through the music player but only plays downloaded films through the laptop speakers.

Try going to System > Preferences > Sound. Tell it to use USB Audio instead of your default Sound Card, and try rebooting. The sound *should* come from your speakers instead of from your laptop. 

> is there any good movie players that you can recommend that i might try instead?
I prefer Xine the best, although most people recommend Mplayer as the best movie player for linux. I normally install Xine, Mplayer, and VLC... and whenever I have problems playing a particular file on one, I give the other 2 a try. Normally at least one player can play the file.
