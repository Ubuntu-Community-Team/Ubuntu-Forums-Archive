### Post by dulbirakan on 2007-08-24
Workrave is a great program with body exercises and dynamic calculation of worktime based on keyboard and mouse activity but if you are just looking for a typing break then just do:

System>Preferences>Keyboard

Then on the window that pops up select "typing break" tab from the tab.

And that's it...
