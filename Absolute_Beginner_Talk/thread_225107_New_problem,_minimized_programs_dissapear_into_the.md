### Post by Indras on 2006-07-29
Yep, a friend of mine had this same problem, he was rearranging his task bars and deleted the bottom panel and tried putting it back, but it doesn't have the window listing.

Easy fix: right click either your top or bottom panel (or side one, if you've put one there), and click Add to Panel.  Scroll down a little ways to "Window List" under "Desktop & Windows" and drag it onto one of your panels.
