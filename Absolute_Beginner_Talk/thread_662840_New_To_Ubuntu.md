### Post by laidback on 2008-01-09
See here for video cards:-

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsNvidia](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsNvidia)

Motherboards:-
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsMotherboards](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsMotherboards)

but if yours isn't there it doesn't mean it wont work as sometimes these refs can be a little behind the times. Only deduce that it doesn't work if you find a report to that effect.

The way to try it for sure is to get hold of the Live CD and run that. It doesn't harm you hdds at all but does give you a preview of Ubuntu on your hardware.

And of course welcome to the wonderful world of Ubuntu.
