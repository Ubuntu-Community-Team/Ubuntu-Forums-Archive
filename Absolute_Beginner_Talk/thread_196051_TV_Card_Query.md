### Post by catlett on 2006-06-13
For what it's worth.
I use TV Time. It is pretty basic but it runs fine for me. It is in the repositories/synaptic. I don't know much about the menu's etc because I run the coaxial cable from my cable box to my tv card. So I only have TV Time on channel 3 and when I change channels with my remote control on the cable box the channels change in TV Time. 
I'm in Boston so I don't know about any where else but if you just connect your cable to the tv card you don't get many channels (maybe 30) You need the cable company's box to decode the signal to get the 200+ stations. So instead of just plugging the cable straight into the tv card I connect the line to my cable box and instead of connecting the cable to my tv I connect it to my tv card in my computer. Then I just set TV Time to channel 3 and I get all my 200+ channels.
