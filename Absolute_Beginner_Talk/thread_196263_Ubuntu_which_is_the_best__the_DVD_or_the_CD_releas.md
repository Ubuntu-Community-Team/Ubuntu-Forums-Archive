### Post by AndyCooll on 2006-06-14
Depends on your Internet connection.

The CD is what is sent out via ship-it and is the standard install. It provides a good basic set of applications. However it assumes you have an Internet connection if you want to add "extra" programs. Updates etc, and many of programs will require downloading from the Internet. The DVD will probably be for those without an Internet connection.
