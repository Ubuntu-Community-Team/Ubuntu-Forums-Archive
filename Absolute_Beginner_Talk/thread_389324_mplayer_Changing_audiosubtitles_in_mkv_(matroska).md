### Post by sagara on 2007-03-21
Turns out you can do it but you have to use your keyboard.  The are no fancy menus to click on.

- To change subtitles **j**
- To switch audio track **#**

You can find a full list of hotkeys here: [http://wiki.multimedia.cx/index.php?title=MPlayer_FAQ](http://wiki.multimedia.cx/index.php?title=MPlayer_FAQ)
