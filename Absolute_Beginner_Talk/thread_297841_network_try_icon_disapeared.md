### Post by bswilson on 2006-11-11
> **Mazen said:**
> hi all,
my wireless network tray icon disappeared and it gave me a message if i wanted to reload it and i answered no....so i restarted my laptop and it still didn't appear...any suggestions?

Right-click on the top panel, and select **Add to Panel** from the menu.  Then find **Network Monitor**, and click **Add**.

> **Mazen said:**
> and how can i remove the icons on my desktop such as the hard drive icons?

Click on **Applications** > **System Tools** > **Configuration Editor**.  Then navigate to **apps** > **nautilus** > **desktop**, and uncheck the **volumes_visible** box.
