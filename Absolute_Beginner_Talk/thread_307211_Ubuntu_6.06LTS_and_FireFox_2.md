### Post by durant1958 on 2006-11-26
There is a kludge to get FireFox 2.0 on Dapper LTS.

The maintainers claim that there are too many issues in dependancies for them to be willing to backport FireFox 2.0 onto Dapper LTS.

To put it into plain language, they don't want to be bothered with it.  It looks too much like work.  

Since Dapper is the Long Term Support release you would think that it would continue to be actively developed and not go static. 

My advice.  Keep asking, squeak the wheel as loud as possible.  Don't take no for an answer.
