### Post by mivo on 2007-10-11
Try this:

In Evolution, go to *Edit -> Preferences* and select "Mail Accounts" on the panel to the left, then highlight your account and click on "Edit". Go to the "Defaults" tab and set "Sent Messages Folder" to an existing folder. It should probably be INBOX/Sent, unless you want the sent mail saved somewhere else, or it is a remote IMAP account.
