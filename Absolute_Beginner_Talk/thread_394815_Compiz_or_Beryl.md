### Post by compmodder26 on 2007-03-27
It's not on by default.  You have to go into System->Preferences->Desktop Effects.  Click "Enable Desktop Effects" and make sure the cube option is checked.

*Note - there was a bug where the cube didn't work properly.  It apparently hasn't been fixed yet.  To fix it yourself, use the instructions at the bottom of this post: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost...62&postcount=1](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost...62&postcount=1)
