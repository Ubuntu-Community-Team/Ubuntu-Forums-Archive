### Post by cubeist on 2007-09-24
I am not sure about the radio issue.  Perhaps if you tell us exactly which stream you are trying to play we can tell you which plugin you need.  As far as having rhythmbox play your MP3's follow these easy steps.

1st.  Place all your mp3's in the same folder, such as /home/Username/Music

2nd.  Open rhythmbox and go to Edit:Preferences.  Under the Library tab you can browse through your home folder to find the folder where you placed all your music.  It should all show up in your library after this.
