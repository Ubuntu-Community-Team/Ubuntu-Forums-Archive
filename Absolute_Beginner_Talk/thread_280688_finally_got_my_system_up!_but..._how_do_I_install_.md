### Post by Bartender on 2006-10-19
Some stuff is incredibly simple.  Take a look at Picasa for Linux on the Google website.  Find the Ubuntu version, download it to your desktop, double-click on it, it installs to your "Graphics" menu and drops a quick-start in the top panel; couldn't be easier.  When Picasa first starts don't ask it to collect all the image files!  It'll round up every single scrap of imagery on your PC and make hundreds of folders.
