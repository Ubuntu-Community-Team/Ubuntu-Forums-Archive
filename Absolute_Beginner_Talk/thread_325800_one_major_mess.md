### Post by bulldog on 2006-12-26
Which email program are you using?

Let's say you use Thunderbird.
Got to your /home folder and under tab view check show hidden folders.
Now find the folder .mozilla-thunderbird and copy this folder to another location.
In this folder are all your mails and settings stored so you can use it again by copying it back.
