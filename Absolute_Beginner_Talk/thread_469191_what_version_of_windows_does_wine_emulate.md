### Post by machoo02 on 2007-06-09
To change the version of windows that wine is pretending to be, open up a terminal and enter```
winecfg
```On the 'Applications' tab, at the bottom, you will see a drop-down list titled "Windows Version".  You can change this on a global or per application basis.
