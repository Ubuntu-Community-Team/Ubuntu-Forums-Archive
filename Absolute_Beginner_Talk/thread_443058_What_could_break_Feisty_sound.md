### Post by whitefort on 2007-05-14
Thank you, thank you!!!

I got my sound back.  I tried selecting another device (which didn't actually work), but when I switched back, I noticed that the 'PCM' setting in the control panel had been turned off.

I think Ubuntu (or some app) MUST have done this all on its own, because I didn't even know I could open that panel - or that it existed - until I read your post.  And my wife wouldn't have clicked it, because all she does on the PC is browse and do emails.

Thanks again!!
