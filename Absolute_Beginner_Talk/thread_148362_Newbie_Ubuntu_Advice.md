Just a short note for those starting out--Stick with it!

I just put Ubuntu on 1/2 of a 250GB drive with XPHE on other 1/2 on a 1st build PC and all is well. Thanks to Canonical for free discs and forum members for help...with time maybe I'll be answering questions, not only asking!
