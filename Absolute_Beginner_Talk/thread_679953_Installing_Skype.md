### Post by Hospadar on 2008-01-27
Hi, you can install skype from the medibuntu repositories and it'll take care of any dependancies like that. 
First, add their repositories as described [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu").  then use synaptic and search for "skype" and install.
It's also in general a better way to install, and then it will get updated automatically by the medibuntu people
