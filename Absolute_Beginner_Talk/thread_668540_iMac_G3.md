### Post by davescafe on 2008-01-15
> **GunsAndRoses said:**
> Where can i find PowerPC LiveCD can u give me link?

**Short Answer:**
Download the "Mac (PowerPC) desktop CD" from here: [http://releases.ubuntu.com/6.06.1/]("http://releases.ubuntu.com/6.06.1/")




**Long Answer:**
Your G3 iMac has a PowerPC chip. According to the Ubuntu download page ([http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download")) the PowerPC chip is not supported with the most recent releases of Ubuntu:
> Note to PowerPC (PPC) users: The PowerPC platform of computers is not supported by the newest versions of Ubuntu. However Ubuntu 6.06 is still supported and available for your machine. Please use the link above to view the complete list of download locations to choose a location near you.

Any of the Ubuntu download mirrors that host 6.06 or 6.06.1 will work. 6.06.1 is the maintenance release, and is probably the most up-to-date version. Here is a link to a description of 6.06.1 and some download locations (mainly in France):
[https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-announce/2006-August/000088.html]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-announce/2006-August/000088.html")
