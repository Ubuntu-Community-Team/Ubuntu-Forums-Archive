### Post by dfreer on 2007-03-24
On any blank space on the panel, "grab" (Left-click and hold) and drop the panel back in it's original position. You can also right-click on any blank space, click "Properties" in the context menu. Then, under the "General" tab Change the "Orientation" from "right" to "Top".
