### Post by Mark_in_Hollywood on 2007-08-31
I have a D-Link WDA 2320. It is the Atheros chipset and in Feisty the drivers are in-built.

Do you have the Gnome network manager icon on your panel? If yes, and you left-click does it bring up a list of available wireless access points?

If you do get an access point that isn't secured, try connecting to that for just a moment. The icon should spin and 2 points should turn green one at a time, just before connecting.

If you right-click the icon, do you have both network enabled and wireless enabled? If you have not enabled wireless, do so and in System/Administration/Restriced Drivers Manager, make sure your card is "seen". It should say you are using a "restricted" driver.
