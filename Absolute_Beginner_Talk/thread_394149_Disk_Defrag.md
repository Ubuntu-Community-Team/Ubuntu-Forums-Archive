### Post by kostkon on 2007-03-26
Yes, you will not normally need a defragmentation tool. The filesystems used by Ubuntu, in most cases ext3 (but also ReiserFS, XFS, ext2) do not suffer from high fragmentation of files. I regularly used my ext3 filesystem for two years and I got only 3% (!!) fragmentation!
