### Post by nvteighen on 2007-08-11
> **SOULRiDER said:**
> You CAN have KDE and GNOME, so dont worry.

Yes, you can, but it can destroy your font configuration and make them look too small/too big. From what I have researched while looking for a solution, it seems KDE's Qt engine **sometimes** takes over GNOME's GTK and corrupts it in a non-trivial way... and I (and some other user) had to reinstall Ubuntu. Look [this (me)]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=508513") and [this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=498402").

If you want to switch to KDE, do it, but then remove (and forget) GNOME.
