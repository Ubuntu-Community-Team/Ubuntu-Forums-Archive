### Post by DerArzt on 2007-02-16
just for reference, if your computer locks, even hard locks, and you get no mouse and seemingly no keyboard response, hold alt & SysReq and then type "r s e i u b" without the quotes. Leave a few moments of time between each, after typing the last letter the computer will restart. i dont remember what each letter stands for, but this ends up properly dismounting your drives and everything (that is a reason why it is optimal to wait a moment between letters, to let the drive(s) properly dismount).  The way to remember this is Raising Skinny Elephants Is Utterly Boring.
