### Post by jken146 on 2007-12-03
Go to System > Administration > Printing on the box your printer is connected to.  In Server Settings tick 'Share published printers connected to this system'.  Then, having installed your printer, click on your printer name and in the Policies tab, tick 'Shared'.

Then go to your other computer, open Printer Configuration in the same way there, and click on New Printer.  You should then be able to add the shared printer over your network.

N.B. The printer might need to be switched on through all this!
