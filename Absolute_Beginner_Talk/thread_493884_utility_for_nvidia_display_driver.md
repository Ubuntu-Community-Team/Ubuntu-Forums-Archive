### Post by buzz40000 on 2007-07-06
[http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html](http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html)

Application called "Envy" will automatically detect your card and install the latest stable NVidea driver (it upgraded me from the nv driver to 'new nvidea' driver.

After installation, you will have a new application under <Applications><System Tools> called "NVidea Settings" which I believe is what you are looking for.

I just had to doubleclick on the debian package on the page linked above, and it automatically started the install script.  Really easy.
