### Post by Durden10 on 2007-07-12
Considering your first question; in order to make an qualitatively high recording, you need some additional hardware.  Depending on your audio source, which means if you want to record several sources (using a microphone or various line-ins) you'll probably need an external soundcard or mixer . In the worst case, you could use a simple line-out source from the DJ's equipment and plug it into your onboard soundcard, which will reduce the quality.
