### Post by konungursvia on 2007-04-08
Monitor cables are mainly analog, and your default video card driver may not have been the most suitable, so you were probably picking up electrical interference from an improperly configured wireless card or electrical power cables, a problem made more visible than usual by an imperfectly configured monitor refresh setting. Just my guess.
