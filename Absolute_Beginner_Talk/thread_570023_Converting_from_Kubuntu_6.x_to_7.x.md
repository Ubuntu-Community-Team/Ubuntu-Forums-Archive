If you have Kubuntu 6.06 (Dapper Drake) or 6.10 (Edgy Eft) you can upgrade. But if you have Dapper you will first have to upgrade to Edgy, then to Feisty. If you are planing on using Gutsy, I recommend waiting until after its released, its in beta testing now.
 The upgrade from Dapper to Edgy can have problems. You may just be better off doing a clean install. In that case, if you have room, install to a new partition. That way you can keep your working install and boot into it if you have problems. You could also mount it in the new install and transfer all files. Then when you are done, and everything is working for you, you can delete the old install.
