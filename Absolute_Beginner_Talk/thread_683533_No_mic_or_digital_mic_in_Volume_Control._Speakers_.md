### Post by c0met on 2008-01-31
I'm not sure which lot of "preferences" you used as you haven't said.

If you double-click on the speaker icon (near the log-out icon), that will open up the volume control. Under "Edit" in this control there are preferences that will allow you to make a number of sound card aspects visible. (This is not the same as the "preferences" that you get by right-clicking on the volume icon.)
