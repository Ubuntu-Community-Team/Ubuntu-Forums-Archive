### Post by mlentink on 2007-06-23
And please be aware that Wine will not run each and every windows application, sometimes only with quite a bit of tinkering. Do not feel disappointed if your particular application does not run, especially if it concerns games.

Take a look at [winehq]("http://www.winehq.org/") to see what runs without problems, and what doesn't.
