### Post by maruchan on 2007-01-31
Your xorg.conf doesn't mention your monitor's vert. and horiz. settings. This means it doesn't know how to work your screen.

Example:

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier     "DELL 2407WFP"
    [b]HorizSync       30.0 - 81.0
    VertRefresh     56.0 - 76.0[/b]
    Option         "DPMS"
EndSection
