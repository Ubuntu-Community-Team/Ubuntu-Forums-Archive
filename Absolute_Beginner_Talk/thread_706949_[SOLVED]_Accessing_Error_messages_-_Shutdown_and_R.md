### Post by Hospadar on 2008-02-24
they might be somewhere in /var or /var/log something or other.  you might try using the "pause/break" button on your keyboard to freeze them, or if you can read a couple words of them try searching for those words on your hard drive (Places->Search and then make sure you search the whole filesystem, not just your home drive, and search within files)

Do these error messages seem to be causing any trouble? somethimes there can be minor hardware issues that throw some errors but don't actually cause any problems.
