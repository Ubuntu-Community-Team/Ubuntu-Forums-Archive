### Post by ed-j on 2007-02-25
If you are familiar with the Terminal, type [COLOR="SeaGreen"]sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg[/COLOR] this will give you a list of options for you for your *lovely* CRT. As you are going through the list of options if there is any thing you do not want to change, press [COLOR="SeaGreen"]esc [/COLOR]and keep going. When you have finished the options, type [COLOR="SeaGreen"]exit[/COLOR] and then restart your machine.

I've got a Tatung 19", build date Nov 2nd 2000! The picture is really good quality on Ubuntu 6.10 and KDE: 1600X1200@75Hz and 1280x1024@85Hz.

I hope these tips are helpful, apologies for taking so long to reply, post again if you have any queries. All the best!
