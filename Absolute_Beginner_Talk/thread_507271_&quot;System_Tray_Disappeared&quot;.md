### Post by AndyCooll on 2007-07-22
Well, in general you can create a new panel by right clicking on your taskbar and choosing "add new panel". You can then drag this to the top of the screen. You can then add various functions to this new panel (e.g. menu, clock etc) by choosing "Add to panel". Not sure what the equivalent for xfce is.
