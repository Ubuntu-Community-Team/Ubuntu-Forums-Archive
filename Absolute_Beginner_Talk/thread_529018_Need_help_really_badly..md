### Post by gn2 on 2007-08-18
Open Synaptic Package Manager, click the search button and look for ttf fonts.
.
There are quite a few extra ones to install, just mark all the ones you want for installation and apply.
.
You can also install Fontforge and create your own fonts.
.
You can also change how Firefox uses fonts and choose which ones it uses or let sites select their own.
In Firefox Edit>Preferences>Content>Advanced
