I suspect your keyboard layout is set to the wrong region (if that's the right word). For example, whenever I install Ubuntu on my machine (here in the UK), It often defaults to the US keyboard layout' The difference being @ and " are swapped, as are £ and #. In your preferences menu, look at Keyboard Settings.
