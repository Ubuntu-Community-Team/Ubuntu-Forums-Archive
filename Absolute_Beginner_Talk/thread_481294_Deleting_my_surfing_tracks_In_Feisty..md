### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-06-22
Or if you are totally totally paranoid you could use:-
**cd .mozilla/firefox/*alphanumeric*.default/Cache && shred *.* && shred .mozilla/firefox/qn4bdmr0.default/history.dat**

This will completely overwrite all the files in cache and your history of sites visited with random data 25 times and make it totally non-recoverable ever again.  I'm sure that is overkill, but thought it worth mentioning for people who have never seen this command.
