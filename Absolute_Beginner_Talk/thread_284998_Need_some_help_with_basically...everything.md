### Post by Kouki on 2006-10-26
I'm pretty much a newbie too, and all I can do is recommend automatix2, this sorted out all my graphics problems, it's absolutely brilliant.  Give it a go and see if it sorts you out.

As for your sound, click system, preferences, sound and have a look at what your default sound card is set to.  I had no sound at first and I found that the default sound card was something random.  I changed this and got sound.  Also right click your little speaker icon, click preferences and change it from line in, to one that actually has a master sound control in it.  I found that the volume up the top was only changing my line in volume which obviously wasn't helping.

As I say, I'm still getting to grips with ubuntu, so if this seems like telling you how to suck eggs, I'm sorry #-o
