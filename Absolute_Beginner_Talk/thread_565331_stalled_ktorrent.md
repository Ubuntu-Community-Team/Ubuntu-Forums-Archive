### Post by NovaAesa on 2007-10-02
In KTorrent, stalled is a possible status of a torrent that is downloading (or would be downloading if it wasn't stalled). It basically means that there are no seeds of leeches that have the part(s) of the torrent that you are trying to get.

You can simply leave KTorrent open and hope that someone who has the pieces you need opens their bit torrent client (this is often the best option), or you can alternatively choose to use another torrent (if there are no or very few seeds).

Hope this answers your question.
