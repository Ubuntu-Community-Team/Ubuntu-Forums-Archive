### Post by SpiritIsReality on 2007-09-29
oh! ok. I love teamwork. the light finally came on here. I can be a real mule sometimes. takes a lot of prodding to get ...
what I did was
move mouse pointer onto the panel at the top of the screen, on an empty space.
right click. click Add To Panel.
click Application Launcher
click the > in front of Internet (the icon that looks like the planet earth)
scroll down, or drag the scroll bar down or click on the down arrow...
until you see KTorrent.
click on KTorrent.
click on +Add.
click Close.
the KTorrent Icon should be in the top panel now. green,red arrows icon.
you can drag it where you want it. right click it for menu.
