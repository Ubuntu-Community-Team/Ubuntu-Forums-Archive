### Post by FrozenFox on 2008-04-13
1) open the terminal and type alsamixer. Press f3 and f4 to switch between output & input. There are ~15 settings per screen, I'd guess. Use left/right to change the section, up/down to change the volume, M to toggle something on or off. Some odd things may be linked to your microphone's operation, like analog something for me, so just try turning a lot of stuff on in addition to what you might expect (mic boost on and mirophone volume).

2) in vent, go to settings, and disable direct input/output.
