### Post by ajgreeny on 2006-09-02
Rather than just have the one desktop, why not have both uf your hard disk is big enough?  Just install *kubuntu-desktop* with aptitude (or apt-get or synaptic) and you can then chose between the two when you boot.  If you stick with *gdm* and not change to *kdm* as your manager (you'll also get this option) you will be asked if you want to change just this time or make the new desktop choice the default.  It doesn't matter which you do as you can easily change again if you want to.

Give it a go!  It's great having both available to show off to people who think linux is just for geeks, nerds, or people who don't want a graphical desktop.
