### Post by ph1 on 2008-02-19
Martym,

Nothing to worry about.  What happens is when your screensaver comes on, your screen locks.  There's an option on the locked screen to leave a message for the user when he/she returns.  When you log back in, the message pops up in that little message bubble in the bottom right-hand corner.  (You can test this by locking your screen, leaving yourself a message, and then logging back in--your message will appear to you.)  It's a neat little feature of Ubuntu.

I believe, then, that the culprit is apparent, and that it is not your operating system.

Best,
ph1
