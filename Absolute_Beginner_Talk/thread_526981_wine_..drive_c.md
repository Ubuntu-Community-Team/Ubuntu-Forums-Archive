### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-08-16
Hi. Wine makes a hidden folder in your home directory; to view it, open your home folder in Nautilus and press CTRL+H to show the hidden files and folders. You're looking for a folder named **.wine** (notice the dot before the name); go inside, and there should be another folder called **drive_c**, I think.
