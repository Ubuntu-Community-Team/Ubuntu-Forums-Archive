### Post by Chimera76 on 2008-02-17
You need to go into your BIOS settings (at startup you press Esc, F# or Ins/Del depending on what BIOS you have) and configure the drive with the live CD as your primary boot device :)

### Post by argraff on 2008-02-17
Check your BIOS to make sure that your computer checks in with your CD ROM for an OS before it checks your harddrive. It goes in a specific order (usually, floppy, CD, harddrive).

To get into your BIOS, watch your screen when you first start up. It usually says something like "Press F2 to get into BIOS" or something similar.
