### Post by kyphi on 2008-01-03
Have you disabled your motherboard sound chip in the BIOS so that your Soundblaster Audigy is the only sound device?

That the sound deteriorates after five minutes when components heat up seems to indicate that there is either a bad connection or a faulty component.

Remove the sound card and put it back in.  If that does not resolve the issue you might have to try a replacement card.
