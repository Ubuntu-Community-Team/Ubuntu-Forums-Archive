### Post by dca on 2007-10-12
That could also relate to the optical disk being written using Packet Writing a'la InCD or something...  Packet Writing gives you the ability to use an optical disk like a floppy.  Nobody really uses that anymore on Win machines because of InCD or something similar needing to be on the system you give the optical disk to.  Most people just use the full burn of CDs & DVDs...
