### Post by cptjaben on 2006-08-17
I'd say it depened what OS you think you will be using more often.  I'd go 2/3 for my main OS and 1/3 for my secondary( that's 2/3's and 1/3 of the whole hardrive).you could also make 2 small partitions for Ubuntu and Windows and a third larger partition in which you can store files on that both Operating systems can read/write. I'm sure there are other ways as well.
