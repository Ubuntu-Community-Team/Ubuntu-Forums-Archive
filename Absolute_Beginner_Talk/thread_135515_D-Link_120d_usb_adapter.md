### Post by dralaroc on 2006-03-18
Holy Cow!!!

After 3 weeks of hogging my family computer room trying to get dapper online I finally prevailed.  I did about 10 installs and all kinds of other stuff and I finally got my wireless to work!!  The key to to getting it up and running was, and yes I am kicking myself in the rear for not thinking of this earlier.  Use a older driver...I was using the most current Xp driver and I was catching all kinds of grief.  Then just for the heck of it, cuz I was about to give up, I decided to download and install the Win2K driver.  I did that and "sudo ndiswrapper modprobe" restarted my pc.  I also unplugged the adapter during the boot process and re-plugged when I was all signed on and BAM!  Both lights lit up, activated and here I am.  Now my wife and kids can have their space back, cuz daddy is going to his hole upstairs \\:D/
