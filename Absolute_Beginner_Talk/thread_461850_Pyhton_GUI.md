### Post by freebird54 on 2007-06-02
Gedit is the text editor (GUI based) that you find under Applications->Accessories.  Or by typing  **gedit** ***filename*** in the terminal.  There are LOTS of alternatives, but gedit is surprisingly useful.

To look for alternatives you could try Add/Remove Programs under programming - or Google a bit and see what other recommend - the see if it is in the repositories (probably is).  Hope this helps!
