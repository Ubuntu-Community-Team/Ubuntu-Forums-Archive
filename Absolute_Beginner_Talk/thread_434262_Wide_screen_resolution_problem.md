### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-05-05
If you are using Feisty 7.04, don't use 915resolution.

The updated Intel driver is available on Synaptic and works better.

Just open a terminal and type in sudo apt-get install xserver-xorg-video-intel

After the install has finished, just re-start your PC and all should be a source of joy.
It should correctly identify your optimum resolution and aspect ratio and start-up in that mode.

The other resolutions should also be available in the resolution menu.

Roger 8)
