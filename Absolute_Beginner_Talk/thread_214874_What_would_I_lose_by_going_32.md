### Post by johnnymac on 2006-07-13
Right now - you don't get that much of a benefit from 64-bit.  Full 64-bit mode operations can have all sorts of benefits for math intensive applications and for addressing tons of RAM.  Outside of that you probably won't see the difference unless doing some intense benchmarking.  Right now the 64-Bit issue is one of "I have it and I'm doing it."
