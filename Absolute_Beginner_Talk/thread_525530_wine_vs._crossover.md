### Post by Hospadar on 2007-08-14
If you can't run the latest flash or dreamweavers on linux (I hear cs2/3 doesn't work so well with wine, I don't know about codeweaver's proprietary stuff like crossover though)  You might want to look into running a virtual windows machine.  It's slower than wine, but you don't run into the same compatibility issues, look into vmware or xen.

Also of course you could look into some open-source replacements for that stuff.  Since flash is proprietary you will be out of luck there, but there are some good replacements for web authoring like n|vu, bluefish, and quanta.  They may not be as powerful as dreamweaver (I don't really know) but they might suit your needs.
