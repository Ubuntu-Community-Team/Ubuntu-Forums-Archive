### Post by kyphi on 2007-07-13
I have noticed a difference in behaviour of icons when placed on the desktop and dragged to the panel and those created for the panel.  I do not know why it is so.

CrossOver creates icons on the desktop automatically.  Rather than dragging the icon to the panel, I have found it best to delete the icon and create a new one by right click on top or bottom panel, Add to Panel, Application Launcher, scroll to Windows Applications, Programs, Quicken, Quicken.

Don't forget to lock the item to the panel.

You could also leave the CrossOver created icon on the desktop.

BTW - the name CrossOver Office was abandoned last year, it is CrossOver Linux now.
