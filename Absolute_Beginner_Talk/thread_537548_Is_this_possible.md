### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-29
You can't partition a mounted drive.

It's like trying to change a flat tyre, while the car is still being driven.

The best thing to do is to download the [COLOR="Blue"]GParted LiveCD[/COLOR].

It is an iso image that you burn to a CD and then you boot from the CD.

You can get the GParted LiveCD from here: [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/)

Don't forget to burn it as an iso9660 image and to burn it SLOWLY, no faster than 4 X.

Regards,
Roger :cool:
