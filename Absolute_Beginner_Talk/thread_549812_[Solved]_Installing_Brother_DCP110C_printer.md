### Post by tonymoloney on 2007-09-13
Well, after a couple of weeks of mucking around, following false leads and imploring help from both this forum and the Brother web site, I've finally got my DCP110C to work.
And even though part of the solution came from the above help, no-one actually gave me the final step, which was so easy.
So, I'd better tell you all what I did.
First of all, following everyone's advice I went to [http://solutions.brother.com/linux/sol/printer/linux/cups_wrapper_install.html](http://solutions.brother.com/linux/sol/printer/linux/cups_wrapper_install.html)
and downloaded the LPR Driver and the Cupswrapper (see step 1 on the page).
Then after following all sorts of false leads and actually getting a test print into the queue without it printing, I removed everything and started over.
This time, all I did was right click on the LPR Driver file and select Open With/Gdebi Package Installer.
Then, when that finished, I did the same thing with the Cupswrapper file.
Job Finished!!!!
(now, why didn't I think of that sooner)
