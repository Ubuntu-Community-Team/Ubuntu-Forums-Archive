### Post by Q4U on 2006-03-02
Guess you know this already, but try [http://www.edubuntu.org/tour.html](http://www.edubuntu.org/tour.html) for a general look at the various apps.

The backbone of Edubuntu are apps from KDE Edu [http://edu.kde.org/](http://edu.kde.org/). If you follow the link from this portal, you'll have a brief description of the application, history and a few other things.

However, there is nothing that I know of about the suggested age etc. Unfortunately the usability reports seem to be at an early stage. I suggest you subscribe to their newletter. 

Q4U

PS If you produce a presentation with original content, post it online and to the attention of Edubuntu and KDE Edu, so that your work may be re-used by others in your situation.
