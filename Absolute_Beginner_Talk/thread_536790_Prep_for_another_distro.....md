### Post by Malibu Illusion on 2007-08-28
You'll likely be able to delete/reformat the partition using the PCLinuxOS's partitioner during the installation.

I'd still recommend checking out the LiveCD of [GParted](http://gparted.sourceforge.net) though as it's a useful stand-alone partitioning tool.

Take care.
