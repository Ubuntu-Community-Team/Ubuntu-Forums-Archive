### Post by Qrk on 2006-05-21
You may want to have two partitions, but make sure that the / partition isn't less than five gigs. You can make the rest home.

Either method works, it is a matter of preference. Some people like having a home folder that stays with them when they reinstall, others would rather have more options with space.
