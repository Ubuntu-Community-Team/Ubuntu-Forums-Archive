### Post by matthew on 2005-09-18
[QUOTE=braveerudite]PS:  Would someone plz explain what's the whole point of realesing a "Preview Version"[/QUOTE]
A preview version allows those who enjoy testing the newest software an opportunity to do so even before the whole package is officially released. There may be bugs in the setup and it is implied that this is not the final edition--that work is still being done on stabilization and features. It is called a preview so that people who value stability and certainty in their software can be advised to wait a little longer until the actual release is complete and issued.
