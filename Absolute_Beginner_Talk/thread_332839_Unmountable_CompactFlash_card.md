### Post by Phophos on 2007-03-16
Problem resolved - CompactFlash card was in fact faulty. Both the vendor and the manufacturer, however, refused to give me any service for this situation, caused entirely by dodgy hardware I'll warrant.

I did find the useful piece of software testdisk, available through Synaptic, which can restore filesystems and partitions deleted from all kinds of hardware. Quite useful, but unfortunately there's only so much you can do to a CF disk with a bad superblock (i.e. destroy it)
