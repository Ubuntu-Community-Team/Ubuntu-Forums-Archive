### Post by aysiu on 2006-01-29
Extremely easy.
Boot into Windows and go to the Control Panel > Administrative Tools > Computer Management > Disk Management.

Right-click on the second hard drive and format it as FAT32.

Then, use the fourth link of my signature to mount the drive in Ubuntu.
