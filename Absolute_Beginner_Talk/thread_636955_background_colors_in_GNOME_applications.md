### Post by vasa1 on 2007-12-11
Background colors:

Thanks to reply from GeorgiaBoot!

Solved for Help 2.20.0 and Nautilus 2.20.0: From the desktop, System > Preferences > Appearance > Theme tab > “customise” button > “colors” tab > click on “background” rectangle of “Input” to get background color (select from color wheel or type in hex code #ffffff being “white” and #a9a9a9 being “grey”). You can also change the text color here. Hex codes and the colors table is at [http://www.w3schools.com/default.asp](http://www.w3schools.com/default.asp).
