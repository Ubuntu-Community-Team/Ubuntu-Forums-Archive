### Post by jbonll05 on 2007-07-06
A fairly recent version of HPLIP for HP printers is in Edgy 6.10 and Feisty 7.04. HP printers are usually recognized and hplip is recommended. HP scanners and multifunction printers are usually also functional. I use a HPpsc1401 and HPpsc1510. They print, scan, and copy since 12/05 with Ubuntu. I also have an Epson cx3810-multi that works.  As long as you do not buy the latest and greatest they should be plug and play. It take a while for the open source drivers to be written. HP has a Linux Printing website. JB
