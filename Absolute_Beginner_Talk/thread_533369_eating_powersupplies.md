### Post by SPQQKY on 2007-08-23
I wouldn't put that POS power supply near my system. What is the model of the Asus, how much memory? Asus do not like cheap PSU's, even some of the older Antec (which are considered good) were having issues. I would not use anything less than a mid range Enermax w/ about 480w or more. Some motherboard manufacturers will (like DFI) will not even warrant your motherboard unless you use a PSU on there recommended list and for good reason. The PSU is THE most important part of your system, bottom line. You may want to consider a Corsair 520 or 620w. For an Asus motherboard, I would only choose a Corsair, Enermax, Seasonic, newer Thermaltake or maybe a FSP with a minimum of 450 watts. Yeah, they're about $75 to $100 more than the one you have, but like I said, being the most important part of the system, it's worth it. I wouldn't use the one you have for anything but a paper weight.
