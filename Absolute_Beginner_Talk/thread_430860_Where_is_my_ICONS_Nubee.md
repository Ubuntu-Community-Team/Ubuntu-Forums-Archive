### Post by lluisanunez on 2007-05-02
Sometimes the programs don't show on the apps menu, but they are there. Usually at /usr/bin
Do this: 
Alt + F2 (run application), enter the name of the program, Noteedit, 7zip or whatever, and see if it runs. You can edit your apps menu by right-clicking on it and clicking "edit", and then add launchers for your programs.
