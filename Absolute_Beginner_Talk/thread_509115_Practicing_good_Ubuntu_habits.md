### Post by Raineer on 2007-07-25
The most important security habit is to not use root unless you need it.  Use "sudo" only when you have to, like seen in [this]("http://huntersdad.com/?p=22") comic.

All kidding aside, Ubuntu is very stable and just spend your first few days playing around in the menus and browsing Synaptic for software you might like.  Also keep spending time here on the forums and you're sure to pickup tricks and hints you didn't know otherwise.
