### Post by warbread on 2008-04-08
It's hard to say exactly without knowing what monitor you're using, but access the monitor's menu using the buttons on the case.  These could be on the very front, the bottom and sometimes the sides.  Monitors will have a couple buttons, usually marked with a + and -, or a ^ and V.  Use these to move up and down the menu.  There should be at least one more button, besides the on/off switch.  Press that to confirm you selection on the menu.  It's hard to understand without playing with the controls.  Press buttons until you find something like "color correction", or perhaps just "color".  The rest of the controls should be displayed on the screen.  You can reduce the red tint, or up the blue and green.
