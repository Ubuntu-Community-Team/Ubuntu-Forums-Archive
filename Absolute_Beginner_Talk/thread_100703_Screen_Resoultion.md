### Post by AndyCooll on 2005-12-08
[QUOTE=lesansley]Thanks, how do I boot into command line?[/QUOTE]

Linux actually gives you more than one screen, usually 7. So, once the pc has booted up use one of those and work from there, e.g:
ALT+any one of the the first six function keys (eg. ALT+F2)
ALT+F7 is the gui (which is usually the default) and the one you can't currently read :)
