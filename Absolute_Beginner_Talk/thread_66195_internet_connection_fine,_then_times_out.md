### Post by pompeyjohn on 2005-09-16
running installed. just worked it out!

Solution:

1. Type about:config in location bar
2. Find(scroll down to) network.dns.disableIPv6
3. Right click on above item
4. Choose toggle

that seems to have sorted it out
