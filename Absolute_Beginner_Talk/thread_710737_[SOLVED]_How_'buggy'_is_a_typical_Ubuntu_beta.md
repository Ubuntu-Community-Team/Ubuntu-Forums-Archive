### Post by shad0w_walker on 2008-02-28
The betas are normally fairly decent, they don't tend to have many show stopper bugs I have seen (Run 7.04, 7.10 and currently 8.04 alphas and betas) 

I would however stick with the latest official release for anything important. If you need the laptop for school I would lean way from using the beta, never know what might happen and when. that said, if you feel confident you can try, just do so with a word of warning and backups (Especially of school work, etc) I remember the bug in 8.04 alpha 3 (or 4 can't remember) that accidentally delete the contents of the home folder.

Edit: The second post is a damn good idea, you can use a shared home partition and if something does go wrong with 8.04 you can just reboot into 7.10 and carry on until you have time to fix it.
