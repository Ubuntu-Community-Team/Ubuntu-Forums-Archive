### Post by JoshuaRL on 2008-02-02
That's the size of the cache for your processor and the chipset/Front Side Bus.  Not that important for this here.  

I would suggest getting the Alternate CD.  When you go to the download page  [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)  then make sure you hit the checkbox at the bottom right under the Download button.  

That will make sure you get the Alternate CD that uses a text installer instead of the Live CD environment.  That should fix your problem.
