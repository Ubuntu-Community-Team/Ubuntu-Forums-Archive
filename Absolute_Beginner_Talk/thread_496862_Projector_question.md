### Post by Toufik on 2007-07-10
I think CTRL+ALT+BACKSPACE may work but I cannot guarantee --> Advice: come in advance to try!! you always get surprise!

But you'll probably have to change your resolution to fit something the projector is able to handle. Have a look at this [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=411674](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=411674)

PS: to backup a file ```
sudo cp /ect/X11/xorg.conf /ect/X11/xorg.conf.back
``` and the opposite to restore
