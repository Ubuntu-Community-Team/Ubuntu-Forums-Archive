### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-29
Flash ads are annoying, and you can block them if you install this extension:

[NoScript](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/722/)

Ostensibly, it blocks Javascript on sites you do not explicitly whitelist. You can also enable an option to block Flash, too.

However, if Flash is crashing your Firefox, you may have a corrupt profile. If so, try [this fix](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=103930).

A) Do one or two

1) click on extensions and see if No Script is still listed under the top downloads.

download it.
