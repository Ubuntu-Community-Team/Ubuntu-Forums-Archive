### Post by Lord Illidan on 2007-06-27
It can be done.

E17 has animated wallpapers, and even animated logins, I believe. Very naff, but you need a powerful cpu and graphics card. E17 is a WM like KDE/GNOME, but I don't think it is released yet. Still under heavy development.

Also, you can use xwinwrap for doing movies or even screensavers on your desktop. Nice tutorial here : [http://www.robbief.com/content/view/35/36/](http://www.robbief.com/content/view/35/36/)

However, it's not perfect..and not many of us like the idea. I did it myself, and while it was cool to have a *Matrix* background initially, it interfered a lot.
