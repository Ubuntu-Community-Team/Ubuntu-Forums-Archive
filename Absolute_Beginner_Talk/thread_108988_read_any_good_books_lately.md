### Post by TeeAhr1 on 2005-12-27
Once upon a time (okay, last month) I asked the same question on these forums.  I am going to tell you what a wise, wise sage told me.  Before you go shelling out any of your (presumably) hard-earned money on a book (though there are great ones), go to $TORRENT-SEARCH-OF-CHOICE and search for "linux books" or "bash books" or variations thereof.  The wealth of free, quality stuff out there is baffling (some of it pirated, but there are also some great GPL licenced ebooks for people just like you and me).  I know not everyone has free/cheap access to printing/binding (I happen to work in printing), but if you do, it's well worth the time you'll spend.
