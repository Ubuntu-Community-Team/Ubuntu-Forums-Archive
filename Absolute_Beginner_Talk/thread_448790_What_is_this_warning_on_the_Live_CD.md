### Post by Outrunner on 2007-05-19
No, that definitely shouldn't happen. You should check the .iso you burned to see if everything is alright([https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes?highlight=%28hash%29](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes?highlight=%28hash%29)). If it isn't, redownload the .iso and burn it at a slow speed(x4 or x6)

And yes, the LiveCD is slow, but when(or if) you install to the hard drive it will work normally.
