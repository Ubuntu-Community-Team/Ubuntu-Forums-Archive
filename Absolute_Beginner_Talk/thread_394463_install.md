### Post by octoberdan on 2007-03-26
Steps to Install Ubuntu:
1. Visit [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)
2. Fill out form and click "Download" to download the cd image
3. (Windows) Follow steps outlined [here]("http://www.petri.co.il/how_to_write_iso_files_to_cd.htm") to burn the image to a blank disk
3. (Mac OSX) [http://www.tech-recipes.com/apple_mac_tips1002.html](http://www.tech-recipes.com/apple_mac_tips1002.html) or [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/maciso](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/maciso)
3. (Linux) [http://www.linuxdocs.org/sln/cdcommands/](http://www.linuxdocs.org/sln/cdcommands/) (see Writing an ISO)
4. Put the cdrom in the cdrom drive of the computer you wish to install it on.
5. Boot 'er up!
6. The proccess is self explainatory at that point, but if I'm wrong just let me know and we'll try to help you from there. Good luck and happy geeking to you!
