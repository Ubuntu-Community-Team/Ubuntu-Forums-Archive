### Post by be4truth on 2007-07-22
I don't have access to a Xampp installation right now but you can go to the Xammp main page, then go to phpMyAdmin. On the left side choose the database you want to backup. Then in the middle choose 'select all" which selects all fields of that database. At the bottom of this side check 'save as file' and give it a name. This will backup your database but not you configuration files nor your webpages.
