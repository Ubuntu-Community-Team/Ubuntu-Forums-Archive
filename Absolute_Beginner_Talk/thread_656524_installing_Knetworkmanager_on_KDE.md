### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-01-02
Hi. Are you trying to establish a wired connection, or wireless? you can get [.deb packages from here]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/"); you need at least these two (32-bit packages):

* [network-manager-kde]("http://mirrors.xmission.com/ubuntu/pool/main/k/knetworkmanager/network-manager-kde_0.2ubuntu1-0ubuntu5_i386.deb")
* [knetworkmanager]("http://mirrors.xmission.com/ubuntu/pool/main/k/knetworkmanager/knetworkmanager_0.2ubuntu1-0ubuntu5_all.deb")

Please note that network-manager-kde has many dependencies--[see here]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/kde/network-manager-kde")--which may not be satisfied at the moment, so you would need to download additional packages as well.

If you are setting up a wireless connection, the easiest way to get it going is to first have wired internet and let Adept download the additional packages.
