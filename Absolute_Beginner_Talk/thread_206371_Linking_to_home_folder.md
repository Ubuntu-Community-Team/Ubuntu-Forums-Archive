### Post by zluka on 2006-06-30
hi there

the answer is yes, and it's quite simple actually ;)

first edit your menus and make the "configuration editor" visible in your "system tools" menu, and then run it. go to"apps > nautilus > desktop" and click in the checkbox on the right side of "home icon visible".

or (just checked it :cool: ) open your "places" menu and drag the "home folder" icon to the desktop. ;)
