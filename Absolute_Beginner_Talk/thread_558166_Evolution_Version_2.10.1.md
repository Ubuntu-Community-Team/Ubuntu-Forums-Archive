### Post by kyphi on 2007-09-23
There are two ways to make adjustments to the calendar view as well as any other view.

1.  If you click on Edit in the top bar and then go to Preferences you can set various parameters.
2.  You can drag both horizontally and vertically any of the fields on the Evolution screen.

So, if you want only to see the calendar, drag the Summary screen all the way down and the hourly schedule all the way to the left.
