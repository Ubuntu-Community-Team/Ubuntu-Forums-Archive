### Post by Sef on 2007-02-07
Most likely it is not going to work.  Lexmark is known for not releasing drivers or help for Linux.  Only their Optra line which is made for business do they make drivers for.

Check [Suggested Printers]("http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting/Database/SuggestedPrinters").  Best printers for Linux are HP, Epson, and Brother.
