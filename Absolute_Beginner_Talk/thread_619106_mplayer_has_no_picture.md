### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-11-21
If you're running compiz, you may need to set the video output to x11.
In the firefox plugin, right click it and go to properties.
in gmplayer, you can right click the player, go into its preferences, and then to the video tab to change it to x11.

Not sure about totem, I hate that garbage program.

If you're not running compiz, disregard what I've said here.
