### Post by JayTee on 2006-11-11
You can adjust brightness in Totem through the menu. Click on Edit, Preferences and then click the Display tab. It will let you set brightness, contrast, saturation and hue. I've been using Totem-xine with Dapper and most all of my movies play with normal brightness and I haven't experienced any issues with it changing on me. Do you have the latest lib files and version of Totem? It also might be a twitchy video driver, I had issues with brightness in general before I upgraded my nvidia driver. Are you running Dapper or Edgy? Are you running Beryl or Compiz on XGL or AIGLX?
