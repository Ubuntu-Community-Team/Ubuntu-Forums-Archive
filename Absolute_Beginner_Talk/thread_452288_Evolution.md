### Post by gary0 on 2007-05-23
Go into your home dir, select view --> show hidden files. Copy the .evolution dir to usb or cd or whatever, set up evolution on the other pc (username, servers etc), go into home on that pc, view --> show hidden files, rename .evolution to .oldevoultion or something, copy the .evolution dir from your usb, cd or wherever you saved it into the home dir on the new pc. Voila! all your email will appear in your new installation of evolution. Works for me.
