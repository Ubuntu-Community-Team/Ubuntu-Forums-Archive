### Post by starcannon on 2008-04-13
Open Emerald Theme Manager 

System-->Preferences-->Emerald Theme Manager

Click on Import in upper right corner

Browse to location of the theme file, click affirmatives

Back in the Emerald Theme Manager double click the Theme of your choice.

Close Emerald Theme Manager.

CTRL-ALT-Back Space

Login

Open a window, and if all went well you should have a newly themed skin on it.
