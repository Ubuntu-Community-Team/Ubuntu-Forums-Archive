### Post by capdog on 2007-02-23
ATI's fault! This is such a huge issue and has been debated so much you're bound to find hundreds of articles online about it. Essentially they don't devote very much time to developing drivers.

The whole problem as I understand it, is that card manufacturers would like to release open source drivers, but they can't because their drivers use libraries of code that they have purchased from third parties. So they simply cannot do it.
