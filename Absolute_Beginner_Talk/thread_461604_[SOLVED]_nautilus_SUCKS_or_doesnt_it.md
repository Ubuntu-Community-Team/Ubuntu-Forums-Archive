### Post by pbw on 2007-06-02
I'm not a fan of nautilus at all, but others are i'm sure or it wouldnt be gnome default.. Anyways, there's a ton of alternatives. Here's a few you can check:

thunar (gtk so it would integrate well)
pcmanfm (same as thunar basically only it supports tabs)
rox (lightweight and flexible)

My fav by far, konqueror.. kde based, but very powerful, as is krusader.
Dolphin is QT as well and kinda sucks imo.

loads more, i'd reccomend trying out konqueror, krusader or pcmanfm. Easy to install any of them so try a few out and see what suits you best.

I'm sure people will post with different/conflicting opinions now, but again. Just peep a few and see what you like.
