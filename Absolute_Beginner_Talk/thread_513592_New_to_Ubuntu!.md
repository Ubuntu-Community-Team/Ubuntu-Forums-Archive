### Post by p_quarles on 2007-07-30
The advice I've given to people curious about Ubuntu is to begin by dual-booting it with their existing OS. This will allow you to experiment and figure out how to do things and how well you like Linux, without making you ever feel locked out of your computer. 

There are many tutorials to be had for the searching, but feel free to post any questions about dual-booting. The two most important things are 1) defrag your drive before doing anything and 2) make sure everything is backed-up.
