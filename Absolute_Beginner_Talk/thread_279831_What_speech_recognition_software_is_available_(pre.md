### Post by rvk on 2007-02-12
Your best bet at the moment is likely to use windows software under wine. The open source community is working on speech recognition (SR) software, but still has a lot of work to do. SR is very complex and people with the required knowledge rare.

To speed up this process we, normal users, can contribute 'our voice'. That helps in developing the necessary 'Acoustic Models'. If you want to help out go to [http://www.voxforge.org/]("http://www.voxforge.org/") and find out how (quite easy).

I am Dutch too, but voxforge.org is for now focusing on English (sad, but understandable). The more people join, the sooner other languages can be added (Nederlands!!!).
