### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-10-05
How about installing a second disk and putting Gutsy on it.  That allows dual-boot, with access to data files from the Dapper drive.

As problems get ironed out with Gutsy, you still have a working Dapper boot.  Over time, you will use Gutsy more and more and the Dapper partition can be expanded as a data partition.
