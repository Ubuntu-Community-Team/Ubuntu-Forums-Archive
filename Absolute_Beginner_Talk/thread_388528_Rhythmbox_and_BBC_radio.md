### Post by Kobalt on 2007-03-19
Have you installed the appropriate [multimedia codecs]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats") to listen to the BBC radio stream ? I don't know which format that is by the way...
If you installed them, open up Rhythmbox and click on Music in the Rythmbox menu, and select New web radio. Copy/paste there the address of the BBC stream, and you'll be able to listen to it from the Radio menu, in the left panel of Rhythmbox.

Oh and by the way, good choice :)
