### Post by darksizzle on 2007-12-02
Have you installed emerald? Download an emerald theme, if it's in a tar.gz format or something of the like extract it in fileroller.  Once you get it into a .emerald format open emerald.  Click import in emerald, and select your .emerald file.  Apply the theme and enjoy the eye candy!
