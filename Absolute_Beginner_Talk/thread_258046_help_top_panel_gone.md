### Post by richardward101 on 2006-09-15
It really does sound like your clicking on a panel applet rather than an empty bit of the panel, but i'm prepared to be wrong. If you have made no alterations to your panels that you want to keep, you can simply delete all their settings and they'll reset to default.
1) close all programs
2) open up your home folder, press CTRL+H and navigate to .gconf --> apps and delete the 'panel' folder.
3) press CTRL+ALT+BACKSPACE, and log back in. with any luck you'll have your panel back
