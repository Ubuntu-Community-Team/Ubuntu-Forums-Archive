### Post by Golyadkin on 2007-10-10
About the optimized version swiftfox versus normal firefox: I tried both, and there is no single difference in performance. Nothing the browser does could be any faster, it is instant. The only limiting factor ever is bandwidth, and that has nothing to do with the browser. So my suggestion is to simply stay with Firefox unless you have a very old slow computer that might benefit from the optimizations.
