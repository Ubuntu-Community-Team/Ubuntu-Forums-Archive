### Post by dasunst3r on 2007-09-19
I've had that problem myself, and I would probably blame it on AIM's servers.  In any case, when you do get stuck, do this:
Disable the account: Accounts -> {AIM Account} -> Disable
Enable the account: Accounts -> Enable Account -> {AIM Account}

Hopefully, the second time around will be better.
