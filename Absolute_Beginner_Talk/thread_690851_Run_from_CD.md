### Post by Blutack on 2008-02-07
You need to change your bios to ensure your computer tries to boot from the CD first.
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installing](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installing)
It should be reasonably obvious, somewhere in your bios menu will be a list of all your bootable devices, you need to move the cdrom drive to the top.
