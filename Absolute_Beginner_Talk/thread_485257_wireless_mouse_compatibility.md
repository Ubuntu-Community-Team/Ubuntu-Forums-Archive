### Post by Teh Dust on 2007-06-26
We use this mouse on a work computer that runs windows. My only complaint is that with the computer just to the left of the screen, mouse on right, and the small usb signal receiver in the attached to the back of the computer, it is sometimes hard for it stay connected on anything lower than 50% battery power. 

You will more than likely have problems with the side buttons, search, and tilt wheel, but there are plenty of topics on how to fix that.
