### Post by arsenic23 on 2007-06-30
Looking at benchmarks for those two CPUs, it would seem that the E6420 wins out over the X2 5600+ by about 5-25 seconds on nearly every benchmark.  So if your looking for every grain of performance you can get, go with the Intel, otherwise just buy the CPU of the company you wish to suport.

I'm an AMD fan myself, but I admit if I had to buy a machine today I'd get one with an Intel E6600 in it, perferably running in a newer P35 board.
