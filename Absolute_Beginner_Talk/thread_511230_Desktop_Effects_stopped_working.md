### Post by st33med on 2007-07-27
I think I know what happened.
Check your little desktop changer. Is there only one desktop? If so, do not add any more desktops (yet). Make sure the desktop cube is disabled. Now, change the amount of desktops to four. Switch on Desktop cube. It should now work.

---

### Post by MD40 on 2007-07-27
Great, thanks for your help, man!

Had to try several times though.
At first, I completely disabled desktop effects, set the number of desktops to 4 and enabled desktop effects again. Obviously NOT the way to do it ;)

Then I just un-ticked the cube option, but left the desktop effects in general enabled, set the number of desktops to 4 and re-ticked the cube option -  works perfectly now :D


Again, thanks for your help,
