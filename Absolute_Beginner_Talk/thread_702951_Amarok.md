### Post by ace007 on 2008-02-20
Under config just tell Amarok where the music is.  It will not copy or organize the music like iTunes does.  As far as playing the iPod, just select it as the source and play away.  There might be an option to copy music from the iPod, but unless Amarok can rename the files as they're copied, they will be in some jumbled Apple naming scheme.  Regardless, the ID3 tages will be intact, this is where all the data about artist, album, title is stored.
