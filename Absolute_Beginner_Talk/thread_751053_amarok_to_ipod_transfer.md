### Post by Tom--d on 2008-04-13
Locate sound converter in the repos and also install Ubuntu restricted extras (allows .mp3 decoding for sound converter. 

After its all installed go to

Apps > Sound & Video > Sound converter >> edit > properties > Check the mp3 box. 

Drag your songs over and convert then :)

Easy as that!
