### Post by Burgundavia on 2005-05-18
There are 2 classes of packages in Universe:
1. Those that come from Debian. These have a Debian maintainer, who may or may not keep on top of security issues. But several eyes have seen the package before it gets into Debian.
2. Those that come from Ubuntu. These are checked by at least 2 experienced packagers and possibly the security team.

Think about what you are installing, and what it does. Things like don't need sudo/root are not major risks.

Thus, the universe stuff is mostly low risk. 

Hopefully, that answers your question a bit better.
