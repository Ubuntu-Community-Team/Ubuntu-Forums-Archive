### Post by scrattox on 2006-10-02
If you're using Firefox, the option you want is found by opening the "Preferences" dialog (from the "Edit" menu).

It is in the "Advanced" section under the "General" tab and is called "Use autoscrolling".

Tick that box, and you should be good to go!  :)
