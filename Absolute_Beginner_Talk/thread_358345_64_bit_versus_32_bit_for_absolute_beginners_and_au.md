### Post by DerArzt on 2007-02-10
in general 32 bit works better because it has far better driver support. As to updating to 7.4, it should work relatively seamless with Update Manager. Some people reported problems going from Dapper to Edgy, so it isn't guaranteed. In any case, you could stay with Edgy until a few days after Feisty is released, and see on the forums how well the update has worked for others.
