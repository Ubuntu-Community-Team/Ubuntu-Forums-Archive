### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-01-23
Most DVD players can't play standard .avi files, although some of the newer machines can play divx / xvid encoded discs.

If you want to ensure that they can be played anywhere, with a menu to select each file, 2 programs stand out:
DeVeDe: [http://www.rastersoft.com/programas/devede.html](http://www.rastersoft.com/programas/devede.html)
ManDVD: [http://www.getdeb.net/app/ManDVD](http://www.getdeb.net/app/ManDVD)

I have personally used DeVeDe, and can recommend it.
