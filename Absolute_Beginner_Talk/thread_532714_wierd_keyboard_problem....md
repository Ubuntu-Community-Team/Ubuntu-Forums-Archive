### Post by Chris S. on 2007-08-23
Depending on how and where you are logging in, it may or may not show that you are typing in a password.  Since I don't know all the details, I'd suggest you try typing in the password anyway and see if that works ... unless you've already tried that.  You sort of imply you don't have this problem with your laptop.  Is there a difference between the computers, the installation, or how you are logging in? (edit: is there a significant difference between the computers, not just that one is a laptop and the other isn't :D)

Typically, password prompts at the terminal or the command line will not echo the password characters at all, while graphical interfaces will.
