### Post by NightwishFan on 2008-04-13
You can use apps meant for Ubuntu in Kubuntu I believe. It should work fine. Installing Kubuntu in Ubuntu is possible, but is a little untidy, as you will have kde apps in your gnome menu and gnome apps in your kde menu, which can get cluttered. It should not effect performance however, and you can use either ones apps in the other.
