### Post by Jeff24K on 2007-04-29
I believe they both use the Gecko engine so "under the hood" they should be similar. The same things that break using Firefox will break using Epiphany, in theory.

The big difference is the numbers of bells and whistles available for FIrefox. There's plugins for everythign under the sun, and community support is a lot easier to come by. Epiphany is lean and mean though, some people prefer that.

My personal preference is Opera. :)
