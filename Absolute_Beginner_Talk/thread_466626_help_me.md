### Post by ryanVickers on 2007-06-06
So, you don't like the resolution and you want to change the monitor? :p (you didn't actually specify your problem or what you want to do, but I'll assume that you've found your desired resolution not listed in the menu and want it fixed!) :)

Try installing the drivers for your video card in System > Administration > Restricted drivers Manager

That will add all of the extra options, but if fomr some reason it breaks something (some people's drivers don't work) then try adding manually an entry in /etc/X11/xorg.conf of what you want.
