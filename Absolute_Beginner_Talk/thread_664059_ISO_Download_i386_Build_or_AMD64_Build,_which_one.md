### Post by Emblem Parade on 2008-01-10
I agree with the suggestion for AMD64. With Feisty, I would not have recommended it to any but the brave and persistent. But with Gutsy, almost everything worked immediately.

Because many more people are using it now, that means that there is more and better support. The Ubuntu Forums are full of very simple and successful how-tos for getting some of the more obscure things to work properly.

For example: Skype 2.0 Beta was just recently released, and within a week or two there were already great how-tos on getting it installed on AMD64, and getting it to recognize and use webcams.

The AMD64 is noticably faster, especially in things like encoding and compressing.
