### Post by endersshadow on 2005-11-23
GDM is your login screen.  Go to System>Administration>Login Screen Setup

Click on the tab, "Themed Greeter," and then on the button "Install New Theme."

Select your theme to install, and hit "Open" or "OK" (can't remember which right now), and then check the radio button by the theme you want.

When you go to log onto your system again, you'll see this theme :-D
