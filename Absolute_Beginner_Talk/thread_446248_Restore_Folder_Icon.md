### Post by vtel57 on 2007-05-16
In your Gnome Theme Manager... Main Menu --> Desktop --> Preferences --> Themes...

You'll see the Theme window and to the right you'll see a Theme Details button. Click on that button. You can modify the base theme that you picked by clicking on the different tabs along the top. One of those tabs is Icons. Click on the Icons tab and choose your favorite Icons. You probably had Ubuntu Human originally... the glassy orange folders.
