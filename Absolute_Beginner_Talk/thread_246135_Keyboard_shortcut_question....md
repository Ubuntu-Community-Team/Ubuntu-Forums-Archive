### Post by aysiu on 2006-08-28
In answer to your first question, there's just no consistency in design.

Tagtool, Epiphany, and Nautilus behave one way.
Gedit, GIMP, and A la Carte behave another way.

Yes, they're all GTK apps, but that doesn't force their developers to work together on a keyboard shortcut standard.
