### Post by yogo on 2007-08-23

### Post by raijinsetsu on 2007-08-23
Personally, I only use verify when making a bootable OS disk, or backups of some data (like when I burn off ***_legally_*** imported Anime to DVD).
Even if the burner does not report an error during the burn process, there can be single and multi-bit errors on the disk which may or may not affect you when you try to read it. If it's an audio CD, don't bother. Game CD's, enh... You'll know if you get an error, and you won't get an error most of the time.
