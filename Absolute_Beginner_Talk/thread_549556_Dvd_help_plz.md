### Post by Hospadar on 2007-09-12
what sort of files are you starting with?  and what exactly are you trying to do with devede? just transcode some movies or also create the .iso?  do you have all the needed codecs installed?  Also, at what stage in the process does devede fail, does it happen immediately, and are there any other error messages?

If you can't/until you get devede working, you could try using qdvdauthor, it's a little more complex the devede but still very easy to use.  

I personally use avidemux to transcode the files (it has a nice auto dvd option, all you have to do is make sure the output video has the correct framerate and resolution for NTSC or PAL, have a look [here]("http://www.avidemux.org/admWiki/index.php?title=Converting_to_DVD"))  And then I pull those transcoded files into qdvdauthor and make a nice little menu (although you don't have to) and then qdvdauthor takes care of all the dvd filesystem and burning quite nicely.
