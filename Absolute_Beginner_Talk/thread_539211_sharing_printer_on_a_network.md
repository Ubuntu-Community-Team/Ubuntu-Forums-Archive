### Post by chipper_30 on 2007-08-31
I have the same situation. I don't think what you want is possible, however here is what I have done:
On your brother's computer (XP), first add a network printer when you're in ubuntu (I think you have already done that). Then reboot your computer in XP, and on your brother's computer instead of changing the configuration of the network printer, add a 2nd one. That way your brother will have 2 network printers, and just needs to know if you're in ubuntu or XP to choose the printer to use.
