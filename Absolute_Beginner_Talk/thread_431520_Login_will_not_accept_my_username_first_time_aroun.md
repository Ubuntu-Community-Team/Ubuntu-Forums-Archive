### Post by freebird54 on 2007-05-03
I have certainly never seen this on Ubuntu - but I did run into a similar thing with a Win installation once.  This is not to say it **IS** your problem, but it **COULD **be...

What was happening to me is that I had a 'sticky' key - and what I was typing (very fast) wsa NOT what was showing up.  A slower (making sure) second attempt always worked.  The sticking key problem can be a shift key, or a doubling of some other key.  Check out this possibility before worrying too much, OK?

Hoping that's all that's happening...
