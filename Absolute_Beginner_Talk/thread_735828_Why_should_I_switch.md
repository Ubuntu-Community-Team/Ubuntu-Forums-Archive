### Post by CJ56 on 2008-04-02
Speaking as a real fingers'n'thumbs computer user & generally totally apprehensive about screwing up my system, I set up a dual-boot with Xp about a year ago. Windows was already installed on the machine so I took the easy path & just carved out a partition for Ubuntu & let the install disk do the rest. GRUB seems to take care of the set-up pretty well.

It's not only a wonderful operating system, but a the whole Open Source philosophy becomes more compelling the more you get to know it -

Once you get it up & running, you'll never look back... ;)
