### Post by justin whitaker on 2007-04-27
Step 1: Under system>administration>system monitor, there is a tab which gives you information about your hardware. 

Step 2: use one of four tools to install the driver:

add/remove
synaptic (make sure you have the restricted repositories enabled if it is ATI/Nvidia)
automatix2
envy.

Links to automatix2 and envy are all over these forums.
