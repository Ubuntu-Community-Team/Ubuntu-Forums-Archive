### Post by Bagnaj97 on 2006-09-11
I dont think you can allow vnc and ssh access again without connecting the monitor etc. Once you have connected everything I know you have to unblock port 22 for ssh access and I believe you have to unblock ports 5800 and 5900 for vnc.
