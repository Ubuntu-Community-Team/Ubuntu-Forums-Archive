From a pure CPU standpoint I don't think it makes any difference.  I have installed Feisty on Athlon 64, Athlon XP, Pentium III, Pentium D, Celeron M, Core2Duo, with nothing CPU related being a problem.  Both Intel and AMD frequency scaling are supported under Feisty as well.

Now as far as ATI hardware, with Feisty the restricted drivers should work for all but the absolute newest ATI cards.  I know that the 8800 series of Nvidia cards don't work very well on Feisty.

Either CPU would be a good choice.
