### Post by kvonb on 2007-07-16
Where did you plug the headphones into?

You may have got the wrong hole, it's easy to do especially if you have multiple sound cards.

If you are sure you have the right socket, have a look under the Skype menu under Tools->Options, it gives a drop down list of all detected sound cards.  You can switch between them if needs be.

Also, are your headphones/mic actually working?  You could try using "sound recorder" from your main menu to make sure :).
