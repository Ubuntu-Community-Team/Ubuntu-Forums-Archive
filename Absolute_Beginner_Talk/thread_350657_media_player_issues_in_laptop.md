### Post by Cherry Cotton on 2008-05-15
> **Sklasko said:**
> On another note, I would recommend checking out [Automatix]("http://www.getautomatix.com/") or [EasyUbuntu]("http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org/") to help ease the process of making these restricted formats playable.

I would strongly discourage this. [Automatix has been shown to be actively dangerous to Ubuntu systems]("http://mjg59.livejournal.com/77440.html"). I'd recommend Taurus's wiki link above. If you're comfortable with third-party repositories, you can also check out [Medibuntu]("http://www.medibuntu.org/") for things that aren't in the official Ubuntu repository. In general, though, you should stick with the official Ubuntu packaging system for critical elements of the operating system, and stay away especially from alternatives like Automatix that are seriously flawed.
