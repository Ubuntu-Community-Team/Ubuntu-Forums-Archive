### Post by Ateo on 2007-05-18
No version of Ubuntu comes with MP3 support due to patents. Same applies to mpeg. However, rest assured that you can get mp3/mpeg support after you install (applies to all).

> also has svg images of Memory Stick, SD card and IPod under the devices folder.

This should work regardless of which variant you installed. I have Kubuntu and those all appear as you state (when applicable). I think you might have configuration issues if that's the case.

But just because you saw a screenshot with all those goodies doesn't mean that it will work/install any different from your current (meaning it might not solve your SD issues). Never go by screenshots, you basically just have to try and find out.
