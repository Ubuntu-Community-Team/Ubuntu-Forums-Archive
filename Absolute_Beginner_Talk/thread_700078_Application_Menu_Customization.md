### Post by billgoldberg on 2008-02-18
Right-click you mouse above "applications" and pick edit menu.

Then pick a sub category, then you can add new programs and you can off course choose from where they are executed. You can also change the location of the executable if you right click the program and pick "properties".

I hope this is what you were looking for.
