[QUOTE=bluevoodoo1]Is there a difference between 'gksudo nautilus' and 'sudo nautilus'? I usually do the later.[/QUOTE] *gksudo* works even if you're launching it as a regular command. *sudo* will work only if you're launching it from the terminal. *gksudo* gives you a graphical "enter password" box before launching the application.
