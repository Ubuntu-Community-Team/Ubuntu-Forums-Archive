Ubuntu ultimate... Which version? Gutsy gibbon (7.10)?

I'll try to help with some possible solutions below (I used to have an Acer Aspire):

- Audio problems: You may need to update your ALSA drivers. I'm sure you've got a RealTek audio card in that Acer of yours. 

- Webcam: I'm willing to bet it's an orbicam - the type that can roll up or down. As far as I've searched, there's no support for it as of yet.

- Card reader: I don't know what brand you have, but the one that I had wasn't supported till Gutsy. If you're using Feisty or below, chances are that's why it's not being detected.

- As for your problem with your bluetooth... I really have no idea how to help you there... sorry.
