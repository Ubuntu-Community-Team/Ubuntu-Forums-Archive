### Post by bobpur on 2007-10-19
I don't have any experience with Vista but it looks to me like you could go to [www.distrowatch.com](www.distrowatch.com) and download and burn a Gparted cd. Use it to delete the linux partitions and resize the NTFS (Windows) partition to take up the space left by the missing linux partitions.
I never, really, liked the partitioning tools that come on some OS disks. That's why I suggested Gparted. To me it is simple and easy to use.
