### Post by lloyd_b on 2007-02-23
AFAIK, there's nothing in Ubuntu that can do anything with .lit files.

There *is* a tool to take .lit files and convert them to html, but you'll have to compile it yourself (the only binary available is for OS/X).

[[COLOR="Blue"]_www.convertlit.com_[/COLOR]]("http://www.convertlit.com")
