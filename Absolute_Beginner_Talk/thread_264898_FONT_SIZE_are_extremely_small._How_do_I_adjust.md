### Post by andypaxo on 2006-09-25
If you wish to change settings for the whole system, you can use:
System -> Preferences -> Font

You can then set various sizes, ('application  font' being the most useful for chaning your interface fonts).
Or you can choose 'details' and change the resolution setting which will affect every font on your system. (It sounds like this is the one you want)

Hope that helps!
