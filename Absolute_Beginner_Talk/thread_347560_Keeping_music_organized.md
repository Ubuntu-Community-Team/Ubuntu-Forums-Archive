### Post by agustin.g on 2007-01-28
I don't know about rythmbox, but when i add music into Amarok's library, it organizes them like this:

/[music_directory]/first letter of the artist's name/artist/album/track

you can alternately tell it to check specific folders for music and add them to the library. In that case it does not move the files.
