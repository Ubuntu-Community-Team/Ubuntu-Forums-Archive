### Post by catlett on 2006-08-08
The files are not always gnome themes. Sometimes they are border controls or icons etc and they are tarred but not installable. If you get an error untar and try again. If it installs it will say available in icons or controls etc.  Then you need to go into the theme details to choose them.
Also some themes are for the login. They are the gtk themes. They install by drag and drop but you have to open System<Administration<Login window. Then drag and drog and you can choose the theme for the login page.
