### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-25
> **Grifter said:**
> How can I take screenshots of a new Install?  I want to capture the images of screen as i step through the install of an Operating System.

Ummm, easiest way I know of is to install the OS into a VM product. That way you are inside of Ubuntu and can save screen shots to your disk. VMware is my favorite product, they even have built in video and screen capture in workstation. Virtual box is free, it will let you install into VM and you can then use Ubuntu to take shots.

I don't really think theres a way of taking screen shots of the alternate install unless its in VM since your not loaded into Ubuntu when installing. The live CD on the other hand you can take shots via print screen or Applications > Accessories > Screenshot. You will have to make sure you are saving them to an external source or seperate partition.

Lots of choices, have fun with it :).
