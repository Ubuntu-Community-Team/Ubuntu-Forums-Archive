### Post by irish_flu on 2007-05-18
Not sure what "it is that's saying permission denied?

Try this in Terminal:

gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf

type gedit and the path to the other file, too.

BTW is the *whole* machine restarting, or just xwindows?  Like, do you hear a POST beep and see RAM counting, etc as you would if you did a full restart?

If so, I kinda wonder if your PC is getting hot enough to shut itself down.  IIRC your motherboard (very similar to my own)  might be able to show you a temperature reading for various spots (chipset, processor, etc) if you look around int he BIOS a bit.
