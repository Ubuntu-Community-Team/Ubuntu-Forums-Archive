 I would think you should check your BIOS settings and find out what order the system is looking at the boot devices in.  You may find that the system is looking at your hard drive before your CD drive, therefore booting XP before it gets to check the CD.

You can enter BIOS by hitting your 'delete' button, on my computer at least,  when the BIOS screen comes up during startup.
