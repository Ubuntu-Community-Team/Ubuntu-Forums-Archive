### Post by confused57 on 2006-05-04
I bought a X2Gen TFT 19" LCD Monitor a few weeks ago, has buttons and works flawlessly with Ubuntu.  Really works the "Auto-Tune" switching back and forth from Ubuntu and XP.  It's analog, I've read DVI doesn't use "Auto-Tune".  Good to know about the "buttonless" LCD's.  You may want to check out LCD monitors at TigerDirect.com or Newegg.com, just to read some of the "Product Reviews", to see if anyone's tried Linux with the one you're interested in.
