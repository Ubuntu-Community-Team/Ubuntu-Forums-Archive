### Post by matthew on 2005-08-25
When you uninstall something in synaptic you are given a choice between "remove" or "remove completely". The first one just uninstalls the program but leaves the config files on your computer so you could re-install without having to download them again. The second removes everything. These files you see listed are what is left after uninstalling the first way. You can leave them there or remove them completely, whichever you want. They have been uninstalled but the deb is still on your hard drive in case you want to reinstall them without having to download them again.
