### Post by Mel_K on 2006-12-28
I think that this may be useful to new users.

By pure accident, I found that gedit can toggle case. For months, I have been irritated by open office's word processor only allowing changes to all lowercase or all uppercase. I always forget I have caps lock on and I hardly ever remember to capitalise the first letter of a sentence.

gedit can change text to all uppercase, all lowercase, invert and title case.8) 

Edit>preferences>plugins>change case

There are more plugins there, but this is my new best friend!
