### Post by Jake90 on 2008-01-21
> **ssteinbr said:**
> You can download Avant Window Navigator.  It's OSXish dock.  One of the applets that comes with it is Stacks.

Once you have Avant Windows Navigator (it is like the dock on a mac) working right click on it and click preferences. Then go to the applets section and move the applet called stacks to the left side (the active one) After you have that installed right click on it in the dock go to preferences>the stack layout tab and switch the layout to curved gui (this will make it look like macs' version) then drag whatver files you want ot it and you're set.
