### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-06-12
> **wreti said:**
> I suppose I should just try it, but it doesn't hurt to ask. Can KTorrent run in the GNOME environment?

Yes Ktorrent runs very well in the GNOME environment. In fact you can run **ANY** KDE app in the GNOME environment with no appreciable difference (and vice versa). All that is required is the libraries and other services that form the backend of the app, they are installed automatically from the repos. 

I also recommend Ktorrent. I think its my favorite client in a long while. It is very customizable, has one window for managing all downloads and has a nice tray icon with speed bars. Give it a try. Also remember that port forwarding a port for DHT and peer exchange will make you go faster most of the time... its up to you if you want that.
