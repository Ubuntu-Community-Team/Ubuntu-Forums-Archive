### Post by aJayRoo on 2007-10-16
Thunderbird uses the system beep as default since this works even for people who do not have speakers. To get thuinderbird to play a sound throough your speakers you will need to ask it to play a custom sound (wav file) on reciept of email. You can do this by going to Edit -> Preferences then clicking on the General tab. Here you should see a button labelled Advanced next to the play a sound tick box. This will allow you to choose a sound to play. Remember this can be anything you want but if you like the beep you can try and download or record your own beep to have thunderbird playback through your speakers when you get mail. Hope that helps!

Oh by the way, this is for Thunderbird 1.5. The same thing is possible in Thunderbird 2 but the buttons/menus way be in slightly different places.
