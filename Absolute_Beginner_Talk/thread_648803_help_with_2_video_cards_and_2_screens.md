### Post by CatKiller on 2007-12-24
The general process is to duplicate the Device and Monitor sections and change the information in them to refer to the other set of hardware, then define a second Screen section that refers to the new Device and Monitor sections, and then add a reference to this second Screen in the ServerLayout section.

**man xorg.conf** has more information, and if you make a backup of your xorg.conf before you change it then you can easily restore your old settings from the command line should something go wrong.
