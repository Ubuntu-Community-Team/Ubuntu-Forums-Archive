### Post by bonzodog on 2005-12-31
There are numerous scripts and plugins for xmms and xchat. most are perl scripts you place in your ~/.xchat2 dir, which are then executed with a command in xchat like /xmms. you will find a lot of scripts that do different things at: 

[http://scripts.xchat.org](http://scripts.xchat.org)

Come and join us in the forums channel: #ubuntuforums on freenode. There are plenty of people in there who would be more than willing to help you with little things like scripts.
