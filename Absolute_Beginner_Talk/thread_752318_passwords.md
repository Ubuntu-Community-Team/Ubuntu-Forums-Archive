### Post by milton1 on 2008-04-11
A great way to make strong passwords that are easy to remember and easy to change is to switch from passwords to key patterns. Pick a pattern you like on your keyboard that you can remember, perhaps holding/not holding shift at some points to get upper case and special characters. Once you know the pattern, you need only remember the starting key, and you can periodically change the starting key to shift the whole password.
