### Post by benx009 on 2007-08-11
believe it or not, i actually switched back to azureus, despite its many bugs, after using deluge.  my first experience with deluge was absolutely horrible, as i firefox loaded pages at unusual snail speeds while deluge (limited to half of my bandwidth) was running.  however, what really annoyed me was when my internet would randomly drop its connection, forcing me to manually restart my router.  when this first happened, i thought it was just some random occurence, but then it happened two more times, all while using deluge.  that's when i immediately "completely removed" deluge with synaptic and got azureus back again.  my internet has never had problems since i did that.  azureus may not be the best bittorrent app, but it sure beats deluge imho.
