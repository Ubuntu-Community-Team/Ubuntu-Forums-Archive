That 'hmmmm, click click' theme music is driving me nuts.  Everytime the Gnome desktop comes up it rattles on for 3 seconds too long.

---

### Post by aysiu on 2006-11-11
System > Preferences > Sound

Uncheck *Play System Sounds*

---

### Post by Drakkor on 2006-11-11
