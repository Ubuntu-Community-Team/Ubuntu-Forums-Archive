### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-08-27
Do you have a quickstart of OOo on your system that starts at bootup?  If so and you then have something that has changed your /.openoffice.org2 folder you can get this error message.  I've had it several times when changing the folder I mentioned above and have always clicked on "yes" to continue.  Once OOo has opened with the new version of the options in this folder all should be OK again.
