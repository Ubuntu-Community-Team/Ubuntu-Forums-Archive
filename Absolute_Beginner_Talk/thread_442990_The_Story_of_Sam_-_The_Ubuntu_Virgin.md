### Post by Sam Alan on 2007-05-14
Once there was a guy named Sam. 
Boring really. He used WinXp. 

He thought he was untouchable...the convenience, the speed, the easy to ignore yet persistently annoying error messages.... Until one day a nasty virus destroyed his WinXp. 

Sam's good friend - FatTux - told him about Ubuntu. Sceptically, Sam tried it out...and fell in love. But on bringing it home to his measly P4-3.00Ghz, 512MB DDR RAM, IntelGAV motherboard with onboard LAN, 160GB-HDD, abode until he realised they were from two different worlds.

He couldn’t get her to understand anything (how to install programs?)
He couldn’t connect her to his friends (how to network and get online?)
And he couldn’t improve her performance (how to install plugins and effects?)

After contemplating breaking up with her - FatTux - told him to try a relationship counsellor and directed him to the know-it-all gurus - ubuntuforums.

So now having told his side of the story, Sam can only pray that somebody helps him out for he would hate to have to go back to the cold heartless bitch that is WinXp.
