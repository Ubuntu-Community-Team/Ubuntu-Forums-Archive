### Post by Lord Illidan on 2005-07-21
Hi there, Dave..

It depends on what you want to use your system for.

For example, I have Ubuntu running with Gnome on an old Celeron 333 Mhz with 192 mb of RAM. It is not going to play 3d games, but for browsing, office work, etc, it works excellently, and I am happy with it.

Then, I also have a Celeron 2 Ghz with 512 Mb of Ram and an NVIDIA 6800 GT graphics card running Ubuntu which can play 3d games and everything else.

What specs do you have?

Also, remember, that don't get a fancy soundcard if you want it to work excellently under Ubuntu. All the extra features will probably be for nothing...
