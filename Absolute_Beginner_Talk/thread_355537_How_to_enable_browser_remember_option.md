### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2007-02-07
the configuration editor might not be there.
to make it visible:
first:
go up to your main menu (applications, places, system)
right click
select "edit menus"
this will launch the "menu layout" app
in here scroll down to "system tools"
click on it
from here look for "configuration editor"
here just click on the box next to it and this will enable it
close the app
and now the program should be listed in your applications> system tools menu
