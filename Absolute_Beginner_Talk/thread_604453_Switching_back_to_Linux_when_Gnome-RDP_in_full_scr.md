### Post by briwood on 2007-11-07
Don't know Gnome RDP specifically, but you might try Ctrl+Alt+Enter when you are in full screen mode.  That is how I get out of fullscreen using Terminal Services Client's RDP.  You should see your Gnome panel appear at top.  You need to click on that to get the focus away from the RDP window, then when you Alt+Tab you should see Gnome respond.

HTH

Brian
