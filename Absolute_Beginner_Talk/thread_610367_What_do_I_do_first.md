### Post by alienexplorers on 2007-11-12
First did you burn the .iso as a data disk or as an .iso image.  The file needs to be burnt as a .iso image.  Second, burn the disk at a speed of 4x or slower.  Third, verify that the disk is good if you can get it to boot.  In the startup menu there is an option to verify your disk for errors.
