I have an idea that I want to suggest to the community:  What about offering graduated levels of Linux certification (ala RedHat), but designed to be Ubuntu specific?  Start perhaps with a beginner certification (installation, basic tweaking, knowledge of common problems, etc.)  An individual with this level of understanding could then assist other beginners with their difficulties.  The system could then build on itself with each level of certification becoming more and more difficult.  I'm sure this forum could be gleaned for the "Top 100 Most Common Problems" with education modules written around them.  The test could be administered online.  I think a certification component of Ubuntu would add to the legitimacy of the distro (IMHO).    Your thoughts?
