### Post by annda on 2006-09-27
if your laptop is working fine, you have nothing to be concerned about.

ubuntu has its own drivers for your hardware and as soon as they are updated (for ubuntu i mean), you will most probably get them via update-manager (automatically), or find them in the repositories.

in short, ubuntu will take care of that for you. it really is very user-riendly in that respect.
