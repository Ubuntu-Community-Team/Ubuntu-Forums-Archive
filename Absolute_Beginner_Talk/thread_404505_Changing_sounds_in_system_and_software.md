Hello,
most of sounds played in applications such as evolution have to be in .wav (at least all the ones I know of). For evolution notifications, I just browsed from google and downloaded a .wav sound that I liked.
Or run from a terminal (> Applications > Terminal):
```
locate *.wav
```
and you'll get a list of the .wav files that are present on your system (most of them are in **/usr/share/sounds**).
