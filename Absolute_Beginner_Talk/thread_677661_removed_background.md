### Post by kplaxmaster on 2008-01-25
Sure there is a way!  You didn't really delete the wallpapers, but deleted the symbolic link to them).  So lets re-find those wallpapers for you....

Right click your background, on the dropdown select "Change Desktop Background."

From there, press the "add" button.  Go to the folder: /usr/share/backgrounds/

All the original background images are found there plus a couple others.  Add all of them or whatever you wish to have as an option.

Enjoy.
