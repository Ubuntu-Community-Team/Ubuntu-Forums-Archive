### Post by FleetAdmiral74 on 2007-06-09
Sorry to say, but I don't think a switch can actually share the internet between comps. Here is a quick breakdown, someone tell me if I am wrong.

Hub: Share files, network, but traffic only one way at a time.
Switch: same as above, but traffic can be incomming and outgoing (think DDR ram)
Router (what you want): share files, and distribute IP adresses and what not to share internet.
