### Post by asmiller-ke6seh on 2007-12-26
I had a similar problem - only had sound in my right channel.

Try this (if you are having the same problem that I, and others, had):

Open the mixer, and find the mute control on the FRONT or SPEAKER or HEADPHONE channel (try each one); click to mute, then click again to unmute.

When I muted, then unmuted, my FRONT channel, the problem was immediately resolved. Try this with sound active from your favorite application.
