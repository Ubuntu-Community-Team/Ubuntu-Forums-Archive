### Post by hartz on 2007-05-14
Have a look at Cinelerra and Kino.  (Kino is in the default repositories, look here for Cinelerra: [http://cvs.cinelerra.org/getting_cinelerra.php](http://cvs.cinelerra.org/getting_cinelerra.php))

You should also have the following utilities installed
ffmpeg
avitools
devede (Some really good DVD video conversion scripts in here)

For fancy audio and video stuff, eg 3D effects and sound effects, look at blender and audacity.

### Post by aeiah on 2007-05-14
if you just want to play video files on your dvd player, use tovid. ive got a python script set up to do it with a couple of button presses but there's a gui version too. it'll create a basic menu and allow you to add video files, and then it'll convert everything into an .iso image that you can then right click and select 'burn image...'
