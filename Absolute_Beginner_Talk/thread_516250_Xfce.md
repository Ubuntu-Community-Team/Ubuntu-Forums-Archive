### Post by kerry_s on 2007-08-03
just plain xfce4 has no setup, you got to do it, there should be the starter launcher in the upper right hand corner, right click on that> properties and set up the first application you want on the launcher, then right click it again, this time select> add new item and add what you want, depending on what xfce4 parts you installed.

tip:
grab the xfce4-appfinder
you can drag and drop app's from finder to the launcher properties window to make launchers.
you can also drag & drop from the add new item window, instead of clicking the + and then moving it where you want it.

right click is your menu.
