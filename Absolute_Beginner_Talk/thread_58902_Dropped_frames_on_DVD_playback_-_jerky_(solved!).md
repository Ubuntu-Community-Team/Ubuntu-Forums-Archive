### Post by aysiu on 2005-08-22
I wasn't sure where to post this exactly, but I was having a little problem for a while. I was playing DVDs, but the playback was always jerky, and many frames were dropped per second. I tried playing DVDs with Totem, with Xine, with Ogle. I installed Totem-xine.

Eventually, I figured it out, and it was the dumbest thing. Nothing was wrong. I just had a theme installed that was slow to render (RPanther for controls). I changed the theme to a "faster" theme, and all of a sudden my DVD playback was normal in Totem.

I'm an idiot.

Well, I'm not sure if that helps anyone, but there it is.
