### Post by Daveth on 2007-10-20
looks like you might have to use Synaptic to add Jack to your system. Never played with Ardour but  I note its a music multitrack bit of kit, and jack is 

"JACK Audio Connection Kit (server and example clients)
Low-latency sound server. JACK allows the connection of multiple applications
to an audio device, as well as allowing them to share audio between
themselves."

might be worth poking about in Ardour manuals to see what else you need- in Synaptic there is jackd, jackeq, jack-rack and jack-tools
