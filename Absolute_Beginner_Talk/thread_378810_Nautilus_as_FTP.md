### Post by gbutler69 on 2007-03-07
Simply choose, "Places" from your Panel Menu, then select "Connect to Server...".

When dialog appears, select FTP (or Anonymous FTP) bs fill in the hostname, user, and path....then enter password when prompted. You will get a "Mount" that you can drag-n-drop to/from like any other folder...

Hope that helps.
