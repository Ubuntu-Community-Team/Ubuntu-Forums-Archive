### Post by mikeyphi on 2008-01-22
You should be able to install a postscript driver - rather that the cups driver.....open System/Administration/Printing...select properties on your printer and under the settings tab it will give make & model and cups setup....click on 'change'...you will be presented with the list of drivers - choose the PPD for your printer.
