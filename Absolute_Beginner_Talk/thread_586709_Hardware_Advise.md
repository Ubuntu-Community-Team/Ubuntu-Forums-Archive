### Post by mivo on 2007-10-22
Definitely more RAM, preferably more than 1 GB of you use VMware  I'd aim at a minimum of 2 GB. Currently I would only consider a Nvidia card since the drivers are simply better. That may change in a year or so, now that ATI/AMD opened their specs, but at the time being Nvidia is the far superior choice. As for the model ... well, a top-end of the 6er series would do, and I would not go with something lesser than this (not with what you want to do with it). Better would be a 7950GT (there are also GX and GX2, but usually more expensive), which gives you a good price/performance ratio. If you aim at the 8er series, only the 8800 models will beat the 7950GT. Those are high-end cards, though, and you didn't post all of your computer's spec -- these cards may simply be too much for your system. You can look at a [comparison of Nvidia cards]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comparison_of_NVIDIA_Graphics_Processing_Units") here (look at memory bandwidth and tact).
