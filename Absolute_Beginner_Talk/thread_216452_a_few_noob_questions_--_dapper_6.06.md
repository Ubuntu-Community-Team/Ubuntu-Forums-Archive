### Post by MikeBenza on 2006-07-16
You can always go back to that place where you found OO.o Draw (right click Applications and go to Edit Menus) and click Restore.  That will restore your menus to whatever they were originally, and you can find OO.o Draw right back where it was: in Graphics.

Unfortunately I don't know what effect that will have on new programs that have been added to the menu.

What I meant by the click and drag bit was yes, to drag it to the section in the list that said office.  Make sure that OO.o Draw is checked, or else it won't show up in whatever menu it *is* in.
