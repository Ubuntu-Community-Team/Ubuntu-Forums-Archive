### Post by kolinab on 2007-02-15
There's probably a way to do it from the command line but on my system I can enable automatic login through . . . 

System . . . Administation . . . Login Window . . . 

Under the 'Security' tab check 'enable automatic login.' 

I tried it for a while and then decided that since I'm using a laptop that travels, I was happier with the security of having to supply a username and password when I fire up.
