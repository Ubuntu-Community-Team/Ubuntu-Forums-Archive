### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-13
OK,

Just do this:

Navigate to : [COLOR="Blue"]Applications>Add/Remove[/COLOR]

When the window opens, in the top right is a drop down options window, labeled [COLOR="Blue"]Show[/COLOR].

Click on the [COLOR="Blue"]up/down arrow[/COLOR] at the right of the box.

Select [COLOR="Blue"]All Available Applications
[/COLOR]
You now have access to all of the repositories.
