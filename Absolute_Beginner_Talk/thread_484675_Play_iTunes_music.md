### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-06-26
> **chris062689 said:**
> Can I access the music store on iTunes 6?

No. Unfortunately, the store itself is hardwired not to allow iTunes 6 clients. This is because Apple keeps modifying the Digital Restrictions Management encryption of the files on the music store, to limit the loss of profits caused by people cracking the encryption algorithm.

This is one of the reasons why the free software community shuns the iTunes Music Store - because the DRM encryption applied to those songs has just stopped you from being able to play the songs, even though you've bought those songs and they are completely legit.

You should burn your music to CDs from within Windows, and then rip those CDs in Ubuntu. There may be programs around to decrypt the tracks without recompression, but I don't know anything about them and they are probably illegal in your jurisdiction.
