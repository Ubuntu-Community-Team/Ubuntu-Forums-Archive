### Post by dmitriyp13 on 2008-01-11
If you're looking to just uninstall any trace of VMworkstation, do a search for vmware, vmworkstation (etc) and delete whatever comes up.  Do be careful when erasing to make sure you're not getting rid of anything else as this could ruin your day.  As a side note, when I first tried out vmware I had a few problems too, mostly related to performance.  Since, I've switched to VirtualBox.  Easy install and a breeze to use.  Good luck.
