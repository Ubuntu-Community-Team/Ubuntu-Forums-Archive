### Post by Mornedhel on 2007-07-28
After some googling, I can't confirm either possibility. Sometimes AV software will misidentify uninstalls as trojans. I'm guessing this is the case, the guys who made the CD probably got Blender straight off the Blender website, but just to be on the safe side, you should get a new copy of Blender from [http://www.blender.org/](http://www.blender.org/) . (Blender is also in the Ubuntu repositories in case you want to use it with Ubuntu.)
