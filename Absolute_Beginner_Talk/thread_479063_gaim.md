### Post by davidsiegel on 2007-06-19
I might be wrong about what is going on, but I think that the site you are using is somehow making calls to AIM through a scripting layer. However, since you aren't on Windows and you don't have AIM, these calls aren't effective. To change your buddy icon in Gaim, find the icon image file you want and save it to your computer by right-clicking on the image in your browser and choosing "Save Image as..." You can save it to your home folder, your Desktop folder, or anywhere else, but be sure to remember where you save it. Next, launch Gaim and click on the icon button in the lower right-hand corner of your buddy list window. This will bring up a window asking you to select a buddy icon. Find the image you just downloaded off the web and press ok, and your new buddy icon should be set.
