### Post by stengah on 2006-08-20
could be a couple of things I guess:

[LIST=1]
[*]The downloaded image may be currupted (do a md5 checksum)
[*]The cd has been burned at too high speed - chose the lowest speed possible
[/LIST]

I actually have had a couple of unfortunate experiences with downloading corrupt images off the official site. The burned CD did not pass the checksum check. Some say torrents are to blame... I dunno!

Anyway, thanks to another hint in another thread got at working image from here: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=233444](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=233444)
