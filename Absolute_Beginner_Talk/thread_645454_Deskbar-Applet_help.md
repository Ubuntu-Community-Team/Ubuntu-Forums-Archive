### Post by nowshining on 2007-12-20
if using gnome - u should of already had gnome-applets installed

right-click an open area of the panel and select add to panel a box wil pop-up choose what u want and select it and drag it to anywhere u want on the panel, u can also remove them by right clicking them and selecting remove from panel or even lock them in place and if u want u can re-move them either clicking (when locked is unchecked) on the middle mouse button and dragging it to where u wnt it to go and releasing the button (or re-clicking it - i forgot), and or right clicking the applet and selecting move and moving the mouse to where u want it to be.
