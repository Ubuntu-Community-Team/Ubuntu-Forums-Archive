### Post by crispy_420 on 2007-03-12
You need to look for some good guides on CUPS for the printer and SAMBA for the sharing. Just search the forums, you will find many guides.

CUPS = printer setup for linux
SAMBA = sharing with windows

They both tie in together for what you need. Setup cups to work in linux and then move to samba for windows share.

Good luck... you may not need luck as it is pretty easy.
