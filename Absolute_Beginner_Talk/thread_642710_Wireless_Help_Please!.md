### Post by anewguy on 2007-12-16
If by leaving it for an hour you mean that your laptop goes into hibernation, that would be your problem.  Hibernation seems to be a little tricky, and you will find MANY post both in the forum and just on the net with a search of "ubuntu hibernation problem".

Most people report that IF the laptop wakes up, most things don't want to work right - the biggest being wireless and sound.  You may even try killing network manager and restarting it to see if you can "wake up" your network, but it may be a hardware thing related to the hibernation.
