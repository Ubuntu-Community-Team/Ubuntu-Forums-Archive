### Post by pete_zahuat on 2008-02-17
What I did was hold down ctrl+alt+left mouse button+ and move the mouse down (while holding down all of those buttons!).

Also, be sure to do this: open   system--preferences--avanced desktop effects settings, then click the "General options" (should be on top of the list), then click the 'Desktop Size' tab, and make sure the horizontal size says 4 and the others say 1.

### Post by kryth on 2008-02-17
Make you have cube rotation enabled.
Defaults 
rotate cube alt+ctrl+left or right arrow key
int cube       alt+ctrl+button1 mouse

### Post by JoshuaRL on 2008-02-17
Yeah, in the Advanced Desktop Settings make sure you have both Desktop Cube and Rotating Cube enabled.  Then you can either click both left and right mouse buttons on the desktop to grab, or Ctrl+Alt+left mouse button.  Then you can move it around.
