### Post by whabo on 2008-01-15
well i wasnt sure where to post this, however after long hours of research i was able to revert my HP notebook dv6636nr to windows XP from windows Vista... now i dualboot with XP and ubuntu. No more VIsta for me. Here are teh list of drivers for XP on that particular Notebook. I hope some one would find it helpful. Please note, i am doing this out of good faith. I hope i can save someone's time. 

.Here is the link for the chipset driver for your notebook.
 [ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33001-33500/sp33411.exe](ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33001-33500/sp33411.exe)
 Please copy the entire URL and paste it in a new window of Internet Explorer. Then open the link from there.

. Here is the link for the video driver for your notebook.
[ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33501-34000/sp33537.exe](ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33501-34000/sp33537.exe)
.Here is the for the modem driver:
[ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33501-34000/sp33742.exe](ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33501-34000/sp33742.exe)
.Here is the link for the wireless driver:
[ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp36501-37000/sp36684.exe](ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp36501-37000/sp36684.exe)
.Here is the link for the media card reader :
[ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33001-33500/sp33413.exe](ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33001-33500/sp33413.exe)
.Here is the link for the webcam driver:
[ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33501-34000/sp33544.exe](ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33501-34000/sp33544.exe)
.For the audio of your notebook please download and install the Microsoft UAA driver from the below link: 
[ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp32501-33000/sp32646.exe](ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp32501-33000/sp32646.exe)
.After installing the UAA driver please restart the notebook and download and install the audio driver from the below link: 
[ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33501-34000/sp33543.exe](ftp://ftp.hp.com/pub/softpaq/sp33501-34000/sp33543.exe)


Thank you for your Time. Please leave me a comment if you found it helpfull. FOr assistance please contact me :) Take care now.
