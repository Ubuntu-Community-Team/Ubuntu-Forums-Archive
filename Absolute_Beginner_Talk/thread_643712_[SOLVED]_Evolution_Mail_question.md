### Post by unknown user on 2007-12-18
Guys, I have set up one of my accounts to use Evolution in Ubuntu and I must admit that I like it from what I have seen so far.  I am looking to do one thing though that would be the icing on the cake for me.  When I receive an email and then forward it on it is forwarded as a mail attachment, which you need to open.  However, in Outlook it forwards the email as an extension of the message, which can be seen by scrolling down the text of the message, rather than having to double click on the attachment to view it.  Is there a setting that I can change which will allow me to forward my messages without the recipient receiving them as an attachment of the email?

---

### Post by plucky on 2007-12-18
Try

**evolution > Preferences > Composer preferences**

Click on Forward style and select preference.You can also change the **reply** style as well.

Hope this is what you want

---

### Post by unknown user on 2007-12-18
Thanks for this, it is just what I was looking to do.  I selected inline in the forward style drop down list and it has done what I was looking for.  Cheers for this.
