### Post by mmillman on 2008-04-11
What video card are you running?

I run a 9600 gt and after updating i had to configure my settings manually from a tty with dpkg, xorg was defaulting to the "nv" driver instead of the "nvidia" driver and I had to change it to use the correct driver.

I believe it was something like:

```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```

or something similar

[Here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=690760") is the link to a thread with more advanced instructions

You just asnwer the questions the best you can and make sure it's using the right driver, and that should solve your problem
