### Post by AndyCooll on 2006-09-21
Unfortunately due to the fact that hardware manufacturers typically don't release a Linux version of their driver and then keep their software closed source, it usually means a delay before new hardware can work with Linux. 

Since one hasn't been made available by the manufacturer themselves the Linux community have to create their own by reverse engineering. And this can be time consuming ...hence the delay.
