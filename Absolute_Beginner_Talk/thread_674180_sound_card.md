### Post by Sandlst on 2008-01-21
Are you able to enter 'Volume Control'? (ie, double clicking on the volume icon at the top panel).  

If you can, Ubuntu does recognize your card, and the problem is volume-related.  If this is the case, try going to Edit/Preferences in the Volume Control window, and select all of the tracks to be visible.  Next, turn up the volume on all of the tracks, unmuting as necessary.  Also, try playing with the settings found in the 'Options' tab if you have one.

You can test sounds by going to (System/Preferences/Sounds) changing to the 'Sounds' tab, and clicking the play button next to log out/log in sounds.at the bottom.  Good luck, post back if you have any problems.
