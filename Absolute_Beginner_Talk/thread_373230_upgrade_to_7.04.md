### Post by calvmari on 2007-03-01
When the big event hits, you'll see the little icon appear in your icon tray saying: "New Upgrade ready!", and it'll say "Ubuntu 7.04 Feisty Fawn" in the upgrade list.

It'll upgrade your system without any CD burning. However, I highly recommend backing up your data before upgrading. Upgrade from 6.06 to 6.10 took me over 30 minutes, so if the computer gets turned off during the installation process by accident, or something else happens, it could be lights out for your system x_x
