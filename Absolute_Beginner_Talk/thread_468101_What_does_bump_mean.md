### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-06-08

> I see a lot of posts that just contain the word "bump."  What does that do?

Hehe, usually just means the person wants the post to get attention and its been left alone or hasn't been responded to in a while. So they make a post (which makes it rise to the top of the thread list in the selected section of forum) and instead of writing something in response to the thread they just say "bump". A bit of a lazy habit :).
