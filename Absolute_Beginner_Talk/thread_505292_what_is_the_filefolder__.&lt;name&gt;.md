### Post by mlentink on 2007-07-20
These are hidden files/folders. 
In Nautilus, your file-manager, you will not see them unless you specifically enable viewing of hidden files.

Linux does not have a specific extension for executable files. Even text-files can be turned into executables by changing a attribute of the file.

For a good expose on linux commands see [linuxcommand.org]("http://www.linuxcommand.org")
