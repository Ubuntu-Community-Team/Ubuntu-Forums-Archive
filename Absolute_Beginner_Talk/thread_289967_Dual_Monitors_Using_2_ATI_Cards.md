### Post by cjssmo on 2006-10-31
This is how I got my two ATI Radeon 7000 VE video cards to work (I have no idea what my monitor specs are).  I took a little different approach.  What I have is a Radeon 7000 ve AGP, and a Radeon 7000 PCI video cards.  I have a Compaq and a Dell CRT monitors.  What I did was to pair the Radeon 7000 VE AGP with the Dell monitor and the Radeon 7000 VE PCI with the Compaq monitor.  First thing I did was to backup my xorg.conf

sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.conf_backup

Then what I did was to place the pci Radeon in its pci slot and connected the compaq monitor to it.  Then I ran this command

sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg

to give me a fresh copy of xorg.conf that works with the Radeon 7000 VE PCI and the compaq monitor. I saved a copy of this xorg.conf to my home folder for later comparison.  Then I ran these commands to get my BusId

cat /etc/X11/xorg.conf | grep BusID

lspci -X | grep VGA

Then I shutdown my system and removed the Radeon 7000 VE PCI and the compaq monitor and installed the Radeon 7000 VE AGP card and connected the Dell monitor.  Started my system and run this command again to give me another xorg.conf for the Radeon 7000 VE AGP and Dell monitor

sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg

I also run these commands to get the BusID for the Radeon 7000 VE AGP and Dell monitor

cat /etc/X11/xorg.conf | grep BusID

lspci -X | grep VGA

I saved a copy of this xorg.conf file to my home folder.

Now comes the fun part.  You should have two xorg.conf files for the two different video card/monitor combinations.  Now open your current xorg.conf file and copy and paste the Section "Device", Section "Monitor", Section "Screen" from your other xorg.conf file.  Now what you should have is an xorg.conf file that has all the information you need to get going. 

In the Section "Device" I added the BusId for each device, make sure the Identifier are unique.

Then in Section "Monitor" I changed the Identifier to "Main Monitor" and "Second Monitor".

And in the Section "Screen" I changed the Identifier to "Main Screen" and "Second Screen".

Then I added this code before the Section "ServerLayout" section

Section "ServerFlags"
  Option "Xinerama" "true"
EndSection

My Section "ServerLayout" section looks like this

Section "ServerLayout"
	Identifier	"Default Layout"
	Screen		0 "Main Screen"
	Screen		1 "Second Screen" LeftOf "Main Screen"
	InputDevice	"Generic Keyboard"
	InputDevice	"Configured Mouse"
	InputDevice     "stylus" "SendCoreEvents"
	InputDevice     "cursor" "SendCoreEvents"
	InputDevice     "eraser" "SendCoreEvents"
EndSection
