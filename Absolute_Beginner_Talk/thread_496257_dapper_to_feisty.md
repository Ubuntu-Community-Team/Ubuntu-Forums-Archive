### Post by euler_fan on 2007-07-08
Unless you have done some major customization you're better off backing everything up you want (including a complete copy of your home folder), making a list of all the programs you want to make sure you remember to reinstall, burning a Feisty live CD and reinstall from scratch.

If you are dual booting just manually partition and don't touch anything just tell it to install over your existing Ubuntu partition.

If not, just let it wipe your drive.
