### Post by ZeroXR on 2007-03-17
Where's the .exe for Ultimate Bet Poker on your system?

Best recommendation is to place it on the desktop and (optional) rename it to something short like ubp.exe for example

Open up a Terminal window and do this after the above:

```
cd Desktop
wine ubp.exe
```

That should trigger the .exe to begin installing.
