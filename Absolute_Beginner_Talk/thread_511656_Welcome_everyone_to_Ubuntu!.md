I just wanted to let everyone know about a great extension for FireFox. The extension is Peekko and it basically makes a chatroom for every website you visit. So if you're looking over the forums here at UbuntuForums.org then anyone with the extension as well will beable to talk directly with you and help you right away. To get Peekko just go to the following link, click install, then restart FireFox. Happy chatting!

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1825](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1825)
