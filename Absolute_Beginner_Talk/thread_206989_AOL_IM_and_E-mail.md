### Post by dmizer on 2006-07-01
yes, gaim is installed by default.  it's under the internet section of "applications".  gaim can also do msn, irc, jabber, and yahoo im among others.

as for hotmail, you can use thunderbird with the webmail extension to have your email downloaded directly to your computer like it would with outlook.  it's a bit of a trick to set up, but i haven't had too many problems with it.

here's the link to the webmail extension: [http://webmail.mozdev.org/](http://webmail.mozdev.org/)
