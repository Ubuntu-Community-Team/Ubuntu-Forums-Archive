### Post by Marsolin on 2006-10-27
You could try [Clara]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/clara") and see if it works any better for you.  [Ocrad]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/ocrad") is another option.  I believe it can be used with [Kooka]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/kooka") as an alternative to [gocr]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/gocr").  The final program I am aware of is [Tesseract OCR]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/tesseract-ocr").  It is supposed to have very good quality.

Edit: It looks like Kooka recently switched to Ocrad as their preferred backend because of better accuracy.
