### Post by aysiu on 2006-09-23
I would just install *gnome-utils* and then use *gnome-screenshot*.

You can define keyboard shortcuts for the following commands:

*gnome-screenshot* (takes a picture of the whole screen)

*gnome-screenshot --window* (takes a picture of just the active window)

*gnome-screenshot --delay 5* (takes a screenshot that's delayed by five seconds--useful taking screenshots of open menus)
