### Post by monado on 2007-10-16
To minimize the forefront window (I guess any window) : Alt+F9
...to unmaximize (restore) a maximized window : Alt+F5
...to maximize a window : AltF10
...to move (still didn't get the hang of it) : Alt+F7

Hint: press Alt+Space to get a menu :)
