### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-02-21
Sometimes the camera will work when plugged in. Sometimes times, you may need a manufacturer driver. Other times it might not work at all.

Your best bet is to ask which webcams support Video for Linux (v4l). If you haven't tried the webcams yet, give them a shot. They might work! :)
