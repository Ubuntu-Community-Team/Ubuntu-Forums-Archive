### Post by Sykil on 2005-11-03
View > Toolbar (also Ctrl + Shift + O)

Edit: well, that gets rid of the entire toolbar.  If you want to just see text or something, go to System > Preferences > Menu and Toolbar Preferences and mess with the toolbar label settings.  This will make changes on all applications, though---or so I think.
