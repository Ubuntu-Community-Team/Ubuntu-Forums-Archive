### Post by gitano1 on 2006-11-02
I had a problem with the screensaver simply not coming on after having worked in perfectly in 6.06 and temporarily in 6.10. I attempted a number of uninstalls of the Screensaver and reinstalls. Nada. 
Today in a discussion with a student (middle school) of mine he suggested that it was a power problem, that screen savers pull a lot of power. The idea made sense. I came home and looked in the Administration area and found Service Settings. Under Power Management I found that Power Management (apmd) was checked. I unchecked it and rebooted. Lo and behold the screen saver came on when scheduled. I am not sure what the function of that particular service is, but I do know that it was interfering with the ability of the screen saver to work and thus far its unchecking has caused no further problems.:p
