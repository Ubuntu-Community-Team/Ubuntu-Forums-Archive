### Post by Austin_KW on 2007-08-21
Left click on the mini icon, or ctrl-L or F6, or double-click, or right-click (select all).

Or if your really want to change it

Type **about:config** in the url address bar
type **click** in the filter bar
See browserUrlbarClickSelectsAll (or some such) click on it until it reads **user set true**
restart firefox
enjoy
