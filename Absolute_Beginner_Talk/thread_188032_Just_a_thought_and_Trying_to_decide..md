### Post by bobpur on 2006-06-03
Dapper installed just fine on a 300 gb Maxtor SATA HD in my computer. However; I think you might want to stay away from RAID configurations.
R.A.I.D.= Redundant Array of Inexpensive Disks
Essentially, Two or more harddrives connected to the motherboard or RAID Controller so that info can be split between two drives for a performance boost. Or, copied or mirrored between two drives as backup. or three or four drives connected to do both mirroring and splitting. If your motherboard supports RAID you'll see three or more SATA connections  on it.
