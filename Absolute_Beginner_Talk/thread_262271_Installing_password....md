### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-09-21
That's for your login password.  Take note, though, that Ubuntu does not enable a root account by default.  Instead, it uses sudo (with your password) for administrative access.  [Here's more info]("https://help.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/desktopguide/C/linux-basics.html#root-and-sudo").
