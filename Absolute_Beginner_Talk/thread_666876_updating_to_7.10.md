### Post by meanburrito920 on 2008-01-13
technically you can, although I would highly recommend against it for 3 reasons. 
1. you have to update to every distro along the way, 1 by 1 until you reach 7.10, so lots of time
2. uprgrading so many times in a row can sometimes mess things up pretty bad
3. might as well clear out all the old stuff on your system you never use.

I suggest you back up your /home directory and make a list of all the programs you will need to reinstall, then do a clean install from disk.
