### Post by Schalken on 2007-12-03
I think it is possible given Sony once sold kits for people who wanted to put Linux on their PS2. So yeah, definately possible to get Linux on there.

However, it looks like the PS2 has a MIPS based processor, and Ubuntu only supports PPC and 32bit and 64bit x86. (the three most popular processor types on servers and desktops.) However, you may be able to get Debian, which is the next best thing from Ubuntu, running on it since it supports MIPS, however Gentoo supports MIPS as well.

Good luck. A PS2 with linux on it would make a nice media center :D

**Edit:** Google for it, you might find something useful. (my search results looked promising, at least)
