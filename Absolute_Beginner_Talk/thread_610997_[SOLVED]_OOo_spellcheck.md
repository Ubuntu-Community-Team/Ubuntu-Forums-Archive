### Post by jay1097 on 2007-11-12
I had a really similar problem as you. When ever i spelt something incorrectly OOo wouldn't underline it. I found out that it was because when i installed ubuntu i choose Canada as my country and it set the default language for OOo to Canadian english which doesn't have a spell check feature so I went to tool>options and i changed my default language to english US now it works fine. So if your default language isn't either UK english or US english chances are ur spell check won't work.
