### Post by Steveway on 2007-10-18
1. WebDav -no clue
2. zSnes -sure
3. HDMI output -dunno
4. SD/MMC slot -sure should
5. open/save directly from FTP to edit CFM files -no clue
6. Putty with SSH -sure
7. uTorrent (an acceptable client) -Deluge or Ktorrent
8. MIDI notation (similar to this) -no clue
9. Photoshop 6.0 (maybe with VMware?, I just can't use Gimp right now) -use Gimp2.4, far better
10. Cedega -sure, you could also try wine first before buying Cedega
And you shouldn't use Automatix, it is not needed on Gutsy.
