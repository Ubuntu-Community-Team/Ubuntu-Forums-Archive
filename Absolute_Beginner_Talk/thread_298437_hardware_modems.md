First--I'd search for dialup & modem--there are lots of threads about the topic--I used a SmartLink PCI modem I got from a vendor on eBay--was 9.99+shipping & was operational within 1/2 (installed drivers & setup)--I also have a backup Zoom 2986L USB hardware modem (no drivers--works very well)--I have tried Best Data modems & have never got they to work reliably--Some of the older Supra-Express modems work--In any case--take a look at these links:
