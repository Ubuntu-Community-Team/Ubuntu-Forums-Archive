### Post by BitTorrentBuddha on 2006-12-17
I would suggest beryl over compiz, it has a much more active development team, or so it seems to me. And if the effects are too gaudy for your tastes, you can always tone them down. As to your other question, both work equally well in either gnome or kde, however, beryl has heliodor and aquamarine, which use metacity and kwin (the default window decorators) respectively in place of beryl's standard emerald or compiz's standard CGWD. This essentially means that you can use standard themes to decorate the windows (window decorations are things like the titlebar and borders.)
