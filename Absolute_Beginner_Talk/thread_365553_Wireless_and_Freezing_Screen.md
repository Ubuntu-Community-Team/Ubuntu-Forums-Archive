### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-02-22
Wireless could be slow because of poor antenna placement.  Antenna should be on a high shelf away from other computer/phone equipment.  Check the signal level with another computer.  In the same room as the antenna you should register 4 or 5 bars out of 5 or 80 to 100% signal.  If not then either the router or wireless card antenna is out of wack.

The second problem could be anything.  If the computer is on the network, then try pinging it (ping 192.168.1.104 or whatever the address is).  If the computer has frozen, but you can still ping it from a remote machine, then you might have a graphic card problem.  Open the machine and reseat the memory and video card.  Clean the machine as well.  If it is a laptop then sometimes CPU overheading will cause it to freeze.  Use a vacuum to clean the openings.  Without any system information its tough to guess.
