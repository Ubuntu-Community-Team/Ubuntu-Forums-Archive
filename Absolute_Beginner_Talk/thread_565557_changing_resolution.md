### Post by skyjacker on 2007-10-02
Try this. it's the only way I could get mine changed and I have a Nividia driver.
open terminal and type [sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg]
Selest the resolution(s) you want by arrowing to them and press the space bar. This will place an X in the box to the left. When you are finished selecting press "Enter", and finish with Ctrl,Alt, Backspace. This will restart, and you will need to log back in.  Your new settings should be in the fonts drop down box.   Good Luck.
