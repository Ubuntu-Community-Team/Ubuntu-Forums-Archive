### Post by lazyart on 2007-05-28
you don't *need* it.  If you don't already, add the networking app to one of your panels (right click a panel, choose add to panel and find the network monitor).

once the monitor is there, click on it, select manual configuration and select the wireless card, then click on properties.  By network name, hit the drop arrow and it will show you what networks it can see.  Select one and enter the security key/password if there is one and click ok.  That should get you online.
