### Post by Kuoi on 2007-02-23
I use Ubuntu for about 3 weeks now , and wanted to let you know what I think about it.

Installation was a bit of a hassle , but that was mostly my fault.
The first installation  worked very well , but that one was on a partition on my Windoos hdd.

I've decided 2 days later that **I liked Ubuntu that much** , that I wanted to have my hole second hdd for it.
That installation was abit a hassle , but went well at the end ( and after 3 installations ) .
I didn't partitioned like Ubuntu wanted it seems.

After these 3 weeks of using , I have something I wanted to share with other Ubuntu users.

Ubuntu software is not as soffisticated as I've used to in Windoos , but that isn't bothering me , except for all the audio players software I've tried ( about 8 ) .
Songbird is my favorite in this one , but has to many bugs , and eats memory .
I've decided to use  "Listen" for listening to my wma, mp3 files on my Windoos hdd.

**Updating software in Ubuntu is almost impossible if it's not in the repos.**
I've tried to upgrade Songbird , Listen , Sylpheed Claws gtk2 ... nothing worked !
There seems to be always an error in the way for updates !

What I do like most in Ubuntu is that you can install and reinstall as much as you want , it's not slowing down Ubuntu !
As I love to test software , this is  good for me.

é-mails  , surfing on the internet , burning dvd's and cd's , .. I'll do everything with Ubuntu now.

I only need Windoos for all my Minidsc concerns , and editing my audio recordings , that's all !
Everything else will happen from now on with Ubuntu !!!!!! 

_My only issue with Ubuntu is "updating software"  , this is very annoying to me , but that's the only thing_. 

Enough said , greetings from a newbie Ubuntu lover , Kuoi ( from Belgium )
