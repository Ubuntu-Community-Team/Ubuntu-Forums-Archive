### Post by derspiess on 2007-04-27
I've been playing around with different Ubuntu distros for just over a year now & until recently it has been sort of a love/hate relationship.  I'm getting more & more confortable with terminal commands & whatnot, but I have to admit I'm still a bit of a GUI addict.  I'm still in what I would consider to be my newb stage.

I had Edgy installed as the primary OS on my 2nd computer & was generally happy with it.  I was never able to print to my Konica Minolta 1350W that was connected to my XP machine, and had all kinds of fits getting the Nvidia drivers to work (I always got the gray screen of death after installing & rebooting).  

Both of those issues (and probably some others I can't think of atm) went away with Feisty-- Ubuntu is quickly approaching that "sweet spot" for me.  I'm not ready to ditch XP just yet, but I think I may finally install Feisty on my main computer now that I've learned how to change the boot order in grub :) 

I just wanted to say thanks to everyone involved with the community/project, not only for an amazing OS, but for all the support you've offered me & other newbs who are trying to get a leg up.  I'm now recommending Feisty to everyone I've talked to-- I've tried several other distros & Feisty is ahead of them all :) 

Regards,
derspiess
