### Post by Terl on 2007-04-23
Here is the way I look at it.

I have WindowsXP; it came with my pc.  I still have programs that work on windows better than linux.  Since I have the license (valid copy of windows) and have the drive space, I just leave windows on the pc.  

I did install VirtualBox and I am trying out different things on it.  I also have Wine and will try VMWare.  Once I have everything I like tested and working in Linux, then I will ditch windows.  If something I need still needs windows I will just keep it.  

As for hooking to the programs you already have installed on your windows share, it may be possible but it may take more time than reinstalling the programs on the virtual box.  I would try installing a few and see how they work.  Remember though, not all of them will work like they do in your windows share.
