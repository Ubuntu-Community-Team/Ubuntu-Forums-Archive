### Post by 505 on 2007-06-20
You're talking about [this guide](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Beginners/Guide/Feisty/Multimedia)

To follow the instructions, do the following.
1. Press Alt+F2
2. Type 'gnome-terminal' and press enter
3. Copy the commands from the guide (those in the grey areas) and paste them in the program you just opened. Press enter after you pasted a command.
