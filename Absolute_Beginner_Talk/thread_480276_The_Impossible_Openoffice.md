### Post by AtrejuT on 2007-06-21
You might not like this answer but it is the only one I can give you ;-)
If you want to write your thesis properly, if you want to have a well-formatted document, if you want proper support for all kinds of references, beautiful formulas (in case you need those), and if you don't want to go crazy about crappy office programs (and I understand very well that you do) then:
you might want to consider writing your thesis in Latex.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LaTeX](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/LaTeX)

Yes you do have to learn it - but it really isn't hard. Took me exactly one day.

Cheers, and good luck with your thesis
