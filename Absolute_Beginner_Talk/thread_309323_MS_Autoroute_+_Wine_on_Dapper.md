### Post by AusIV4 on 2006-12-01
Check the WineHQ [AppDB]("http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iAppId=1071") to find out about compatibility. MS Autoroute is not very highly rated, (bronze for some older versions, garbage for the most recent).

When it comes to getting windows programs running, you need wine, and when it come to while, there's not much variance from distro to distro, so the first place to check is the App database.

Someone else may be aware of a native alternative, but I can't help you with that.
