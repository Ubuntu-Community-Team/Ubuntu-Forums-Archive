### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2007-03-02
You can get more colors and themeds for ubuntu here:
[Gnome look]("http://www.gnome-look.org")
To install a theme the normal method is to download one of the themes off of gnome look, then go into "system" then to "preferences" then to "theme"
click on it and a new window will pop up, just drag and drop your newly downloaded theme into the manager and it normally works.
There will be cases though that a theme is in a source package though, so be on the lookout.
