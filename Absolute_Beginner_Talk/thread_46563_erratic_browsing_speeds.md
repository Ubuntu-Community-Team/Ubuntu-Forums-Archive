### Post by varunus on 2005-07-05
This sounds like the IPV6 Firefox speed issue.  (At least, I have a cable modem, and firefox reached normal speeds again once I disabled IPv6.)

To do this. follow this howto: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=42949](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=42949)
Or at least the part dealing with IPv6.

Enjoy.
