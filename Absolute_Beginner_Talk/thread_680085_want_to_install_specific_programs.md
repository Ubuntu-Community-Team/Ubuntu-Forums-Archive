File system:
"Programs" are in** /usr/bin** normally
"My Documents" is** /home/username**
"My Computer" is **/**
Not much else to know that is important for most users.

Your ISP doesn't need to support Linux.  Your OS has nothing to do with them.  The fact that their support staff are too stupid to know that is irrelevant.  The only issue is *how you connect* to your ISP i.e. via cable modem-router, usb ADSL modem, ADSL modem-router etc.  If you configure the connection settings via a router in a webpage, there will be no problems.
