### Post by Inxsible on 2007-05-30
You might wanna put on a System Monitor for the processor load and check it when you copy/move files. That could give you more insight. 

Right Click on the top / bottom panel and select Add To Panel. Look for System & Hardware. 

Select System Monitor. Once it comes on your panel, you can right click it and select monitoring for CPU, memory, hdd usage etc. 

Before you perform a copy /move....see how much processor cycles are being consumed before / during / and after the copy/move.

It's strange that it should happen only during copy and not anything else.
