### Post by aidanr on 2008-03-26
/tmp is short for temporary, a folder that programs can put stuff in **temporarily**.
You should have selected "save to disk" instead of clicking "open" when you were downloading the file or at least saved it to your home folder from Open Office.
There's no easy way of retrieving it now. If it's really important you can look into [data recovery programs]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery").
