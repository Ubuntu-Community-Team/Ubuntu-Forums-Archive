[quote=racermike1967]
I accidently deleted my recycle bin from my bar on the bottom.  Is there a way to get this back?[/quote] 
Right click on the toolbar and click "Add to Panel..."  Find under "Desktop & Windows" "Trash," and drag the icon to where you want it.

### Post by ncappel1 on 2006-04-18
In a related note, you can also add a trash can to the desktop by typing in 
```
gconf-editor
```
and navigating to /apps/nautilus/desktop/ and checking the "trash icon visible" box
