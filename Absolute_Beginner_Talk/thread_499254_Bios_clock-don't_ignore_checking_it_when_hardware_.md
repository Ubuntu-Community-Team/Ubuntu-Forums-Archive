### Post by randytuggle on 2007-07-12
I have spent several weeks trying to get my laptop to work properly. I would get hard disk problems, system freezes, sound problems, etc... and memtest86 would fail at address 00000000000 all the time. I couldn't get anyone to determine the real issues with my system.

I checked the clock today while booting the system and I noticed my clock was 4 hours off. I corrected the clock in my bios and ran memtest86. It didn't issue a single error! A google search on that issue had mentioned that my Acer laptop was producing those errors due to a fault in the memtest program - yeah,right! LOL!

If your system is doing stupid things, please check your clock in your bios. It sounds so silly but I cannot believe what a difference that made in my system so far. Cheers!
