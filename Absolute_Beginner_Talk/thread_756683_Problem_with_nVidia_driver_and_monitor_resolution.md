### Post by Tomatz on 2008-04-16
This is how i done it:

Open a terminal and type:

**nvidia-settings**

select **server display configuration**

Change the resolution to the **resolution you want**

click **save to x configuration file**

Then **show preview**. **_DO NOT SAVE_**

Then go to the **"screen"** section and copy **subsetion "display"** e.g:

```
SubSection     "Display"
        Depth       24
        Modes      "1600x1200" "1280x1024" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"
    EndSubSection
EndSection
```

**_DO NOT SAVE JUST CANCEL_**

Then:

**sudo gedit /etcX11/xorg.conf**

Then paste the **subsection "display"** part over the existing **"display"** part in your own xorg.conf.

Ctrl alt bkspace

*It may be best you backup your original xorg.conf incase it borks*

This is the best way to do this as nvidia-settings detects your monitor well unfortunatly it nukes your x configuration so you have to only use the relevent part. ;)
