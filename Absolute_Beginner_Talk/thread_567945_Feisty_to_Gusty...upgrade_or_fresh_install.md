Yeah this is my viewpoint too. Though many people say upgrading works fine (and I have no reason to doubt their experience), I personally have always had problems. Therefore I prefer a fresh install. And since my data is stored on a server (or just as good is if you've created a separate /home partition) I don't lose my settings. If you haven't either of these but want to keep your settings then you'll need to backup your /home partition first.
