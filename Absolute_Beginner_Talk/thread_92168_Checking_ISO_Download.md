Hi!!  New to Linux and Umbutu!  Have been playing with these boxs since the Commadore days.  I read a post where the person had a corrupt iso download.  I'll assume she used Windows for her initial download or has access to one.  If you go to Nero they have a free 'checksum' verifier.  It checks the integrety of your download.  I've found it works on all downloads if you have a checksum (It's like the inventory number).  If you go to the site you downloaded from it should have a page with the appropriate checksum for your download.  Paste that number to the verifier (it's called the MD5 Verifier) and enter or browse to the file with your iso download.  It will check it for accuracy.  AND IT'S FREE!!!  Glad to be here and hope that helps.
