### Post by uzi09 on 2006-05-21
i installed on a inspiron 6400.

had a few problems:
1) breezy didn't work -- graphical problem with X. needed something called 915resolution which came stock with Dapper, so i decided to switch.
2) sound didn't work with one of the earlier versions of dapper (back in march). upgrade ended up working eventually
3) wireless was also a problem, eventually an update came out that fixed that issue to.

so far (with the latest updates) everything seems to be working okay. every once in a while, an update will come out that may mess something up, but the next day or two, it'll be fixed. it's still unstable, but don't worry, you don't have long before the final release date (june 1st) :D
