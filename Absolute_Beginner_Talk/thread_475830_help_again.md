### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-06-16
Hit either your Del-key or F1 or F12 key on your keyboard when your computer boots up. Then go to Advanced Setup and set First boot device to CD-ROM. Save and exit BIOS and reboot your computer with the disk in your CD-ROM drive
