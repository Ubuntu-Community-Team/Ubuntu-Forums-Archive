### Post by frodon on 2005-12-14
Ok, modify the monitor section of your xorg.conf like that and do a Ctrl+Alt+Backspace : ```
Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"SAMTRON"
	Option		"DPMS"
[B]        HorizSync       30-70
        VertRefresh     50-160[/B]
EndSection
```
