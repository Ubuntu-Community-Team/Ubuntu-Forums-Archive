### Post by jkndrkn on 2005-05-21
5- Your entire thunderbird profile can be transferred or even shared

Essentially, you need to locate where thunderbird stores your emails, copy them into a folder in linux, and then set up thunderbird so it looks for email in the folder you have just created.

If you seek to share the email between windows and linux, you will need to put the email folder in a fat32 partition, mount the partition in linux, and change your settings in both the windows and linux thunderbird programs to point to the same folder.

Works flawlessly.
