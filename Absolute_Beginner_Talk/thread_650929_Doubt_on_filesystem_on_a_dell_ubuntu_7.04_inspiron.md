### Post by bigtony on 2007-12-27
I don't exactly understand what you're asking about "leveraging", but without looking at it, I would assume it's Dell's on-the-drive restoration tool.  The Dellutility probably just reimages the drive using the image in the other folder.  It's easier and cheaper for computer manufacturers to do that than send the customer two reinstallation disks- one with the computer and one to replace the one the customer lost:)

The advantage of keeping these folders instead of deleting them is the image may already have configuration tweaked for your hardware.  I'm not certain about that, but it only seems like the proper thing for Dell to do.
