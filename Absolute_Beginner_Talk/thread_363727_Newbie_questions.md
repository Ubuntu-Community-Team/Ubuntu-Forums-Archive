### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-02-17
The resolution problem depends on your hardware and can be dealt with by reinstalling the graphics driver or by editing certain other setting. 

Ubuntu should be able to read files backed up on CD/DVD, but I would recommend dual booting just to make sure you it actually suits you. The zeal to dump Windows often wanes faster then expected.
