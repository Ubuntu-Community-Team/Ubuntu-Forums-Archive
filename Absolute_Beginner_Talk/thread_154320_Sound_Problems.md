### Post by R3linquish3r on 2006-04-02
When you boot your computer, open the BIOS by hitting the DEL key before the Ubuntu boot screen. Go into your periferals page (different from BIOS to BIOS so I can't direct you) and look for RC'73 (or some other type) Audio and make sure it is disabled.
