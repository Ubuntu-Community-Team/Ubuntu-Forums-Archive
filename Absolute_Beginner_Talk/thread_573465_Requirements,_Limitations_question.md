### Post by jingo811 on 2007-10-11
Whatever road you choose don't expect your wireless to work right away.  My personal estimation is that 80% of wireless devices out there won't work right away without some reading and tinkering.
It took me 2 years to get my Wireless USB to work :) but then the Wifi support today is much quicker than 2 years ago.

See if your Wireless device is listed in this database, that should give you a good time estimation as to how much time you'll need put down on troubleshooting things.
