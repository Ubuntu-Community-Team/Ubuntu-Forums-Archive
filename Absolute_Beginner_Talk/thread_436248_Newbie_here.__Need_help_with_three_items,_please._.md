### Post by freebird54 on 2007-05-07
tbolen - I think what you are thinking of is virtualization.  This means that you run one operating system inside another one.  This can be done either way around (with XP hosting, or with Linux hosting) - but there are more, different, soct-effective ways of doig it with Linuz as the host.  The two most popular systems for this are VMWare, and VirtualBox.  With them running, the 'other' OS is in a window on the desktop, or they can be hotkeyed between.  Depending on your hardware, this may be a bit tricky to set up - though I found VirtualBox to be pretty easy.

Hope this helps...
