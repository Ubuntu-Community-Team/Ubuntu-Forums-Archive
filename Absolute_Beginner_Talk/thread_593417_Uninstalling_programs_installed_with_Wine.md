### Post by Malta paul on 2007-10-27
Go to: System>Preferences>Main Menu, when there, go to 'menus' , click on wine then go to the 'Items' section, right click boxes and delete item, If all 'Item' boxes are deleted you can then delete wine in 'Menu' section.
Have fun :)
