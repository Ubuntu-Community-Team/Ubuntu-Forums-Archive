### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-02
If you right-click on the speaker icon, the second item down should be [COLOR="Blue"]Open Volume Control[/COLOR].
Select it.

When the mixer opens, move the sliders on the [COLOR="Blue"]PCM Control[/COLOR] down.

Hopefully, this will wind the level down for you.
