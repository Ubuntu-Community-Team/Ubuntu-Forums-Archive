### Post by eternalsword on 2007-07-02
In terms of recording from line-in, I use ecasound.  It's command line only, but works great for raw pcm or wav format.  you could then load the wav into a mixer program or instead of recording, playback the raw pcm directly to your soundcard if you desire that.
