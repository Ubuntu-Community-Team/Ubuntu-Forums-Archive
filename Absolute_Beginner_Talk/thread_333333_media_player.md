### Post by Scorpuk on 2007-01-07
As for media players the one I use with Ubuntu is Kaffiene.

Simple to use and will work with music files, dvd's, movie files, DBV (if you have a card).

I installed it through synaptic.


As for playing wma, I am not sure, but you could search this site for info on Automatix2 and see what it provides. Just be careful with the legalities of installing codecs, Automatix2 will prompt you about these. Something to do with America I think.


I would check my install, but i am not at home so have minimal access to my system.

Good luck.
