### Post by str8line on 2007-09-09
Installing printers is pretty simple in Ubuntu, there are no massive installs of drivers and software like with Windows.  If you click on Settings->Administrative->Printers it should open the printer window.

From there just click Add Printer.....this will open the Add Printer Wizard.  With a modern HP printer the drivers should be found automatically and in 3 clicks of the wizard should have a working printer.
