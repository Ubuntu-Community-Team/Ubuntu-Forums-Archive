### Post by clancymf on 2007-10-15
I feel like this is the first time that I may be able to help someone... Even though no one has asked=P~

I used synaptic to get bitpim and then spent about an hour trying to get my vx8550 to work until I realized that synaptic had a version that was about 4 versions old.  

I went directly to the website downloaded the deb file to my desktop
ran sudo dpkg -i debfile.deb

and ta da! my phone now works :)  

Hopefully this may save someones time in the future and I guess the lesson is when you are downloading "random" or seldom used software it is always good to check that synaptic has the latest version.
