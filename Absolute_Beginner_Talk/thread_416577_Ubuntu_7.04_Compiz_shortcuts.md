### Post by huelbe on 2007-04-24
Hi!

keywords: 7.04 Feisty Fawn Compiz AIGLX XGL Desktop Effects

After you turn on 'Desktop Effects' (using menu System/Preferences/Desktop Effects) you will immediately find out your windows will 'wobble' and 'fade-in/out' when moving and work with them.

But, wait, there is more! :)

If you use 'Alt-Tab' you will cycle through all opened aplication (even if it is minimized or hidden), each one will be showed as a miniature in the center of the screen.

Another shortcut I found was 'Control-Alt-UpArrow', this one will show you small screens of your opened windows (applications NOT minimized/hidden).

Does anyone know other shortcuts?
