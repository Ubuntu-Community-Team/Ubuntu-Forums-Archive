### Post by johnc4510 on 2006-05-08
Your Breezy version won't automatically update. As long as your repository list says breezy it won't use the dapper repositories. I am running dapper now and have been for about a month. ### Post by jasay on 2006-05-08
Actually, I think there will be an auto-update option.  Update manager has been expanded to do dist-upgrades.  I think what will happen is that you will be notified of update the same as always, but on the top of the update manager, it will ask if you want to dist-upgrade to dapper.  If yes, it will change the sources.list for you and upgrade.  If no, it won't bug you again.  At least that what I understand from the ubuntu_devel mailing list and this [wiki page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DapperUpgrades").
