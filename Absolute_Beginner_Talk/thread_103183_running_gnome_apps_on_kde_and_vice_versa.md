### Post by kairu0 on 2005-12-13
You can run both types of programs in both environments.

Gnome apps use the GTK libraries. KDE apps use the QT libraries. If you have the necessary libraries installed, your desktop environment (= Gnome or KDE) preference won't stop you from running your programs.
