### Post by punx45 on 2006-12-26
if there are more than 2 jacks on your sound card, check, then double check, to ensure you have the microphone in the correct jack.   then check the volume control panel - right click on the speaker looking icon in one of your toolbars, or press Ctl + o  - go to the capture tab and make sure the microphone is turned up and not muted.    finally in audacity, make sure that the microphone is selected for the capture device ( as you said you have).   the record level meter does not monitor levels unless you click on the drop down tab next to the microphone at the top right and then select "monitor input"   Of course, that is not necessary to do to begin recording.

if none of that helps, then there might be something wrong with your sound card or something else.
