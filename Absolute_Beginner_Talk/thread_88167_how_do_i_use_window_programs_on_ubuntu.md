### Post by Brian Levy on 2005-11-09
FWIW, I use OpenOffice 2.0 (final release) in a Windows environment and also have MSOffice.  To date the ONLY problem I have found with compatability seems to relate to .rtf files generated in Word. Rather weird.  As for .doc files, have not run into any problems. On Excel files, have found 1 formula that Excel can open that OpenOffice did not recognize. However, the formula was poorly written and not in a manner a reasonable DooDoo would have done. No problems with opening or generating PPT.  We just moved the AmCham Canada offices and converted from MS Office to OpenOffice and though a few oldtimers were upset for a couple of days, by day 3 no one was even noticing.  I did set the program up to save automatically as a MS file since many of these docs do go outside and need editing.  I suspect in the next year to 2 years though the org countrywide will be on OpenOffice.
