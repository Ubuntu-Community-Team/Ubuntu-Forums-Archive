### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-03-30
Hi, there! Welcome aboard!

What you need to do is to enable the extra repositories in Synaptic!

Here we go!

1. Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager[/COLOR]

2. It will ask you for your [COLOR="Blue"]password[/COLOR]

3. When Synaptic opens, navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]Settings>Repositories[/COLOR]

4. Put a tick in every box, except sourcecode.

5. Close off

6. You should now have access to a huge amount of software!
