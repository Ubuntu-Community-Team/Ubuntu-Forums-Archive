### Post by Juergen on 2005-07-21
If it's not automatically recognized, did you read this:
[http://tldp.org/HOWTO/Modem-HOWTO.html](http://tldp.org/HOWTO/Modem-HOWTO.html) ?

I'd think DSL modems have to be intelligent enough that they aren't win-modems, so you probably just have to search for your device (look at #2.9) and follow this document.

There may be a simpler way to set up modems in Ubuntu. If you find one just use the device you found via #2.9 in this setup.

[edit]
Found it. Of course it's in the menu System/Administrative Tasks/Network (or similar, I have a german menu)
I have an entry 'modem' there, you should, too. Click on 'Configure', activate it and enter your device on the 'modem'-tab
[/edit]
