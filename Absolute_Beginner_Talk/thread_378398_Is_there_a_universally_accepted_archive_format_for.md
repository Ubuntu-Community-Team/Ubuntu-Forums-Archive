### Post by compiledkernel on 2007-03-07
Phew...ok. 

mbox is the original standard as far as Ive known. 

Maildir and MH are probably the most widely accepted current formats. 

Maildir++ isnt really a standard by itself but merely an extension of maildir (written by the Courier guy if memory serves me correctly, to include thing like quotas).

Example: Mozilla suite programs use a variant of mbox I believe to store their mail.
