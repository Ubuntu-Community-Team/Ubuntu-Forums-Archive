### Post by TWO on 2007-11-01
I assume you mean that your mic doesn't seem to be working right?

I thought I had that problem once before. If you go to Applications -> Accessories -> Terminal, and type in :

alsamixer

It should present you with a screen showing various volume bars and an input source. I do remember reading that certain things a muted by default sometimes and all you may need to do is crank up the volume and make sure it isn't muted.

Another method is to  double click on Volume Control - on mine it's an icon next to the clock- click on Edit -> Preferences then Capture, click on the corresponding tab and make sure the volume is up and that it is unmuted.
