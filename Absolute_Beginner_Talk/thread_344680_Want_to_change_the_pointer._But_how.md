### Post by Wes24 on 2007-01-23
Go to System-> Preferences-> Mouse. In the menu, click on pointers.  There you can choose different x11 mouse themes.

Try [http://www.gnomelook.org]("http://www.gnomelook.org") for different themes. You can put themes in home/user/.icons.
