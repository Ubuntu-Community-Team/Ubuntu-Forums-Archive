### Post by Paqman on 2008-03-25
According to their website Gparted can shrink an HFS partition (but can't grow it) If all you want to do is shrink your HFS partition down to create space for Ubuntu then you should be able to let the installer do it for you. The Gparted LiveCD is a handy thing to have lying around, too.
