### Post by Bigbluecat on 2006-10-25
I have the Netgear router and it works fine. One thing I do suggest is that you connect via ethernet cable when your try the live CD or install. The wireless connection may not be configured when you first install especially if you have encryption turned on (as you should). 

With the ethernet cable I hooked up to the internet instantly from the LiveCD and on install so could get updates. After that I could configure wireless encryption at my leisure.
