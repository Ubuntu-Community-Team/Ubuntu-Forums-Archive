How do you install firefox skins? I'm trying to make my Ubuntu to look exactly like Mac OS X, and I found a skin that makes Firefox look like Safari.

---

### Post by Therion on 2008-02-07
Open Firefox.

Tools/Add-Ons/Themes/Get Themes.  
Find the theme you want to install.
Click on the green button that says "Install Now".
Go back to Tools/Add-ons/Themes and double-click on the theme you want to use.
