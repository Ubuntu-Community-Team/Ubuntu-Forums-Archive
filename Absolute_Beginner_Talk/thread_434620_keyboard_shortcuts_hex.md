### Post by Skrynesaver on 2007-05-08
Depends on your keyboard, they are the quirky keys added to some keyboards that are automatic hotkeys, eg. Raise/Lower speaker volume, open your email client, turn on/off bluetooth ...

### Post by Skrynesaver on 2007-08-21
If you go to system>preference>keyboard shortcuts and select an action then hit the key in question it's hex definition will appear in the New Accelerator space, if it is a known X-Windows hotkey it's assigned name will appear
