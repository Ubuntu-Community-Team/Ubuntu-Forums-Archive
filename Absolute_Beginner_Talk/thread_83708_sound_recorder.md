### Post by davmac on 2005-10-29
As a first step, in sound recorder, click on File -> Run Mixer. When the mixer opens, click on the "Capture" tab. You need to make sure that the little microphone icon, under the line in, doesn't have the red cross through it, and all the other channels do have the wee red cross.

You may also need to experiment with muting/unmuting the channel. Sometimes when it is unmuted you'll get feedback - sometimes you won't be able to hear what you're recording.

Hope this helps,

Dave Mac
