Novice Alert!

Of the command quoted above, I only know the command without the "gk" in front of sudo. When the computer is booting up, there is a 3 second window, by pressing Esc you will find a choice to go into recovery mode. If the command is typed in without the gk, it will take you into xserver-xorg without having to go through your GUI. You may be able to try some different options.

Unless you know the relevant command, you will have to Restart for any changes you make to take effect.
