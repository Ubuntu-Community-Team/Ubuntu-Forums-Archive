Is there a way to enlarge KDE Desktop Icons on Kubuntu 6.10?
I want them to be super-large.

---

### Post by igknighted on 2007-01-28
open KDE control center, go to 'appearances & themes' -> 'Icons' and then click on the advanced tab.  For the use of icons on your desktop/file manager you can increase the size from 32x32 all the way up to 128x128 I believe.
