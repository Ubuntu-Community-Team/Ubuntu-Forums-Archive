### Post by newlinux on 2007-08-29
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases)

May help. 6.10 (Edgy Eft) is an older release, and 7.04 (Feisty Fawn) is the current release, with 7.10 (Gutsy Gibbon) coming out in october. The number stands for the release date (6.10 is 2006, October, 7.04 is 2007, April, etc.)

the newer the release, the more up to date most packages, drivers, etc. are, and the longer it will be supported (with the exception of LTS releases).
