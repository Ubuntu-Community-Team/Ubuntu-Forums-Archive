Does the disc mention anything about how many **_minutes_** of recorded data it holds? According to Wikipedia, "By far the most common is 120 mm in diameter, *with a 74-minute audio capacity and a 650 MB data or an 80-minute audio capacity and a 700 MB data*" 

So if it mentions 74 minutes of audio capacity, it would be a 650MB data disc, I guess.
