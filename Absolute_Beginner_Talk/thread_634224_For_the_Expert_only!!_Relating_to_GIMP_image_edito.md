### Post by mali2297 on 2007-12-07
To change the background from white to transparent, do he following steps in Gimp:

1. Add an Alpha-channel to your image (using Layer > Transparency > Add Alpha Channel)
2. Select the white area using the Magic Wand
3. Go to Edit > Clear

---

### Post by HotShotDJ on 2007-12-07
1: Layer --> Transparency --> Add Alpha Channel
2: Select --> By Color
3: Click on the color that you want to make transparent in your photo
4: Edit --> Clear

This probably won't work real well on a photo, since the background isn't really a solid color, even though it all looks white to you.
