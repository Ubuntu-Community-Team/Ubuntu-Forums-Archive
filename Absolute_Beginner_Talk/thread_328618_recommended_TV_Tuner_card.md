### Post by pseudonym on 2006-12-31
I run 64-bit Xubuntu and my Kworld DVB card works brilliantly, although the video-in ports don't work as well as I would like (but I don't use them anyway). It's not the latest and greatest, so depending on your needs you might want to go for something else. There are plenty of cards supported these days in Linux, though.

The best place to check for hardware support is [Linux TV]("http://www.linuxtv.org/") . 32 or 64 doesn't matter - the hardware is fine on both. and because the streams are usually mpeg variants, you shouldn't have any software issues on 64-bit either. I use gxine to watch TV, and record on the command line by doing a stream copy and it all works perfectly.

HTH :)
