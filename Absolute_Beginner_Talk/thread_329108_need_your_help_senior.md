### Post by jem7v on 2006-12-31
For the sound, I suggest looking under System -> Preferences -> Sound.  See if experimenting with those settings will make a difference.  Also, check your volume control under Applications -> Sound & Video -> Volume Control.

Bluetooth: Can't help you there.

CAD software: From what I've seen so far, there are no NICE programs.  There are a few okay programs, such as [http://www.ribbonsoft.com/qcad.html](http://www.ribbonsoft.com/qcad.html) (a free version of which is under Applications -> Add/Remove Applications -> Graphics: QCAD), but nothing that compares to what I was using five and a half years ago under Windows.  You might want to try installing a Windows-based CAD program using WINE, but that might be a nightmare.  OF COURSE, there could always be something I have missed!  I'm not Completely omniscient.
