### Post by Jucato on 2006-09-23
they can be found in different places, depending on who installed them.

1. System/Admin installed wallpapers and icons would be in
/usr/share/wallpapers
/usr/share/icons

2. User added wallpapers and icons would be in
~/.kde/share/wallpapers
~/.kde/share/icons

Note that the last 2 folders would not exist if you have not installed or added any new wallpaper or icon set.
