### Post by taurus on 2006-11-25
6.06 LTS has a Long Term Support (you have three years of support on that) while Edgy is a cutting edge.  Something, a cutting edge can cause some problems on some machines so just because you use Dapper, 6.06, doesn't mean you run older version.  So, it's up to you if you want to figure out what's wrong with Edgy on your machine or try to see if you can boot from Dapper!  Also, burning at slow speed doesn't hurt either.
