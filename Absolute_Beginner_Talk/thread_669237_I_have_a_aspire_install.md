### Post by bcrom on 2008-01-16
Ubuntu comes with a couple drivers for webcamse, V4L (video for linux) and V4L2 (video for linux 2).  Try opening Ekiga and going to preferences->Video Devices and try different drivers.  If you can get the webcam working in Ekiga, you know you have the correct drivers.
