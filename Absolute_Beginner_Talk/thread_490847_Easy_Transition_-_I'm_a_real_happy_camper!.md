### Post by yeastpuppet on 2007-07-02
I finally took the jump from Windows to Ubuntu, believe me this was not a simple decision, but here I am.

Cleaned out a spare drive for Ubuntu, popped in the FF 7.04 distro, hit the install option and crossed my fingers.  

Unbelievable.

Ubuntu installs itself, GRUB and that's it.  

All of a sudden I have dual boot capability, but I'm sitting here listening to streaming Pandora radio, surfing in Firefox, and checking out my Excel spreadsheets in OpenOffice.  I got my all my pic and music files here with me in Penguin Land.  Do I really need dual boot anymore? 

Nothing is crashing, got three workspaces set up and humming and I just installed Ubuntu yesterday afternoon.  

I spent most of last weekend digging trojans, worms, and virii out of my brother in law's Windows box.  Guess what he's getting at the July 4th picnic???
