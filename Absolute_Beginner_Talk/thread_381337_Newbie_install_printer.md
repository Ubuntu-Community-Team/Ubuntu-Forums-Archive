### Post by Sef on 2007-03-10
Sorry to tell you this. but [LinuxPrinting]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x4270") says your printer is a paperweight.  Therefore you will need to get a new one:  HP, Epson, and Brother are usually the best.  Check out [LinuxPrinting's Suggested Printers]("http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting/Database/SuggestedPrinters").

(Lexmark does not provide Linux support for consumer printers. Hence the reason it won't work.)
