### Post by nickburns on 2007-08-25
I have a new 64 bit machine, with 4 gigs of memory. When I installed the 64 bit OS everything went just fine. But I can only utilize 3.2 gigs of memory? I thought 64 bit will let me go above the 3.2 gig limit?

What is wrong?

---

### Post by InGunsWeTrust on 2007-08-26
Consult the documentation of your motherboard. While lots of motherboards have enough slots to support 4 gigs of RAM some do not actually support that much RAM. It is also possible that you need to update your BIOS. These are the only things that can cause an underusage of RAM.
