### Post by Bartender on 2007-07-16
Does your home-buiilt PC have a serial port?  Old controller-based serial modems are the most reliable method.  You can pick up a new one without breaking the bank 
[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16825137104](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16825137104)
or shop for used ones on eBay or craigslist or garage sales or what-have-you (don't know where you live in the world; that will have some bearing on availability).  I've picked up 4 used ones that way - 3 US Robotics Sportsters and one Boca.
Here's an interesting new product for the USB port
[http://www.marketwire.com/2.0/release.do?id=749607&sourceType=3](http://www.marketwire.com/2.0/release.do?id=749607&sourceType=3)
Just released in the last few days.  Zoom claims it works in Linux.
