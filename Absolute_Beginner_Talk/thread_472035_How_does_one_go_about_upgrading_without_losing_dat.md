### Post by Tux Aubrey on 2007-06-12
This is all handled very elegantly when you upgrade.

When a new release is launched, the update manager will have a new button offering the upgrade online and you just click it. All your settings etc are retained.

Of course, you then miss out on any enhancements to the install process!

A lot of people (me included) tend to use an upgrade as an excuse to reconfigure and clean-up their set-up.  In that case, you back up your /home directory (or keep it on a separate partition) and do a clean install from the live CD.

Development versions (eg. the gutsy tribes, the beta and release candidate) can also be installed the same way, but you need to do that through a command.
