### Post by atomkarinca on 2008-03-28
Why should you bother with piracy when you can listen to those songs legally?

[Seeqpod]("http://www.seeqpod.com/") is a music crawler. You can listen to many songs using seeqpod or its linux client [LifeTunes]("http://www.bashterritory.com/lifetunes/").

Or you can stream many many songs using [Grooveshark]("http://www.grooveshark.com/"). And if you have music on your harddrive you can also make money off of 'em.

You can download [trade friendly]("http://wiki.etree.org/index.php?page=TradeFriendly") artists' live performances from[ etree.org's bt tracker]("http://bt.etree.org/") or [Furthurnet's software]("http://www.furthurnet.org/index.php").

At last but not least you can listen to *free* music. [Jamendo]("http://www.jamendo.com/en/") is like a heaven of free music.

So, why not give these a try?
