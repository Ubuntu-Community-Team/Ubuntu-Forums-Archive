### Post by A4006 on 2008-03-16
Applications>Accessories>Avant Window Navigator.  Since you'll want it to start automatically, go under System>Sessions, and under the Startup Programs tab, and click click "Add". The command is "avant-window-navigator". Name it whatever you like.
