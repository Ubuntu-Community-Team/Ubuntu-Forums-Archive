### Post by pppetter on 2007-04-21
hi there!

Wine usually doesn't show up in any menu, since it's not a regular application that you just run. Open up a Terminal or konstole and type this in:
wine /Location/of/windowsinstaller

Remember that Linux is case sensitive when it comes to files and folders, just as passwords.

Edit: You may of course choose the option "Open with" and just type "wine" in the box and push enter...
