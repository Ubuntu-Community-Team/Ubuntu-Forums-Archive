### Post by bobbybobington on 2008-02-24
Try installing **Advanced Desktop Effects Settings** from **Applications>Add/Remove...** menu. After it's installed you can find it under **System>Preferences>Advanced Desktop Effects Settings**.

You may have to go to The **Visual Effects** Tab under **System>Preferences>Appearance** and select the **Custom** radio button. Then you can customize the effects in the **Advanced Desktop Effects Settings** program.
