### Post by torost on 2007-03-07
I am using a Qtek S100, a Windows mobile 2003 device. I am synchronising calendar and adress book over Multisync and the Sync-CE plugin. The setup is not completely smooth - I failed with getting it to work over Bluetooth and finally succeeded using the USB cable after consulting the Ubuntu Wiki. Even then I am glad every time it works and I am carefully following the secuence of actions that I have once documented. The Multisync / Sync-CE is low activity projects. From what I understand the community is focusing on the Opensync which is going to replace Multisync with a more flexible/reliable/better design. So far Opensync does not have the functionality of Multisync, so the latter is the preferred solution. Unfortunately I find that this is a weak point of Ubuntu and Linux as a desktop system. I can imagine that for many people the failure to use one of their electronic 'gadgets' is enough reason for not using Linux as their primary desktop. To summarize I am glad that it works but the solution could be more stable. Torbjörn
