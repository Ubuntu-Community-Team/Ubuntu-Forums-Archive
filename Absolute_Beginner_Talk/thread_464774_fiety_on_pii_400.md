### Post by orb9220 on 2007-06-05
Not really you would need 192mb just to use the GUI installer on the liveCD for any ubuntu flavor.

The alternate.iso does a text based install of ubuntu. And I would recommend the Feisty Alternate of Xbuntu which has less demand for resources and is pretty good for older systems.

If you wanted Gnome I don't think you would have much of an enjoyable experience with less than 256mb.
