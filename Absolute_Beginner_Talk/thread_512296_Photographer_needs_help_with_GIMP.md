To scale an image:

Image ->Scale

To increase the canvas size:

Image-> Canvas size

This is the menu provided with individual images, not the one titled "The Gimp"


To scale individual layers or selections within an image, select the layer in question, use "The Gimp" menu, and click on the icon that says "scale the layer or selection (shift+T)" when you hover your mouse over it. It looks like 2 blue boxes with a diagonal arrow, and a third, barely visible box.
