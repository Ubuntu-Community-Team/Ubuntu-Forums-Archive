### Post by Charlie Chick on 2006-11-04
It seems that a lot of us have had difficulty in getting Realplayer to work on the BBC News web site after upgrading to 6.10. I got MPlayer instead. Re-installing Realplayer didn't work. However, I found that uninstalling MPlayer, MPlayer plugin for Firefox and MPlayer skins did the trick. The video part of the BBC News site now opens in Realplayer.

Hope this helps others who were struggling with this one.
