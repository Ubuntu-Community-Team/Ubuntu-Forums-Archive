### Post by lhtown on 2007-03-08
Installing anything on a disk partition that you are going to try to recover deleted data from is highly inadviseable since it will possibly overwrite the data that was marked for deletion. In short, you shouldn't be writing anything to this disk until you get your data off and figure out what you are doing.

Check out the Trinity Rescue Kit from this address:
[http://trinityhome.org](http://trinityhome.org)

There may also be other options, but I am not sure what they are.
