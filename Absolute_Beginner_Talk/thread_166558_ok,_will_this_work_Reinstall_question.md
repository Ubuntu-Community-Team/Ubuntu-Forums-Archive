with the rest of the installation in another HDD

### Post by pbaehr on 2006-04-26
Yes, you can.

In the options for partitioning in the install it gives you a chance to tell it where to mount each of your partitions. Set it to mount your other partitions to /home and /usr and format the root partition for the new install.
