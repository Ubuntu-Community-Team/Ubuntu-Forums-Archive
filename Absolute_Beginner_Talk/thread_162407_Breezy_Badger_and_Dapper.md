### Post by NeoGreen on 2006-04-18
What is Breezer Badger and Dapper?  I see them all the time on the forum, what are their purposes.  :(

---

### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-18
Breezy Badger's a nickname for the current version of Ubuntu (5.10). Dapper Drake is the nickname for the future version of Ubuntu (6.06).

---

### Post by NeoGreen on 2006-04-18
Oh, okay so it a version not a utility or application.:) How do I know which version I am using?  Is there a command I can type in the terminal that will tell me?

---

### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-18
Press Control-Alt-F1, take a peek at the version, then press Control-Alt-F7.
