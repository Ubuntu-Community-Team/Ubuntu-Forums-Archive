### Post by AndyCooll on 2008-04-18
Perhaps I didn't make myself clear. I'm trying to eliminate your broadband package as the problem. What I'm asking is what is your network connection speed, i.e. your bluetooth/mobile connection package?. I for instance have a 2mb broadband connection. At this stage I'm not asking what speed it's currently downloading at, but what speed you *expect* it to be able to download at.

If the bluetooth package you have bought only provides a slow download speed then clearly you are only going to be able to download a couple of pages at a time. If you have a fast connection but are actually only receiving slow download speeds then this points to some bottleneck, wrong setting or throttling somewhere.
