### Post by sysctl on 2007-03-03
Embedded mp3s fail to play for me with Totem and VLC plugins. MPlayer works though.
[list=1]
[*]Uninstall VLC plugin
[*]Quarantine Totem plugins (can't remove them as they are part of Ubuntu desktop package), remove (or backup) all symlinks: /usr/lib/mozilla-firefox/plugins/libtotem*
[*]Install MPlayer plugin
[/list]
