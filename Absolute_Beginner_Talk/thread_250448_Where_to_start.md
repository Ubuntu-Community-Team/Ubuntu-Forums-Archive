### Post by Amablue on 2006-09-04
Do you already have Ubuntu installed? 

If you don't, I recomend that you download the live CD so that you can give it a try. If you burn the CD and boot your computer off it you can see what Ubuntu looks like and you'll have a chance to play around with it a little. Unless you mess with the setup it won't make any changes to your computer. 

After you get the hang of the layout, take a look at some of the installed programs that come packaged with Ubuntu so you can compare them to the windows counterpart. OpenOffice.org has a lot of stuff that's good for people who like Mircosoft Office. It comes with a wide variety of games (some might argue too many =D you can never have too many games I say). 

If you have any other question feel free to ask here or search the wiki.
