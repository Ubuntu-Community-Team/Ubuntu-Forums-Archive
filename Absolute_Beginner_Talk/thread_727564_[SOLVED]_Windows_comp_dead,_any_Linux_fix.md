### Post by outofnicks on 2008-03-17
Not tto draw attention away from Ubuntu, but Puppy Linux is what I'd use. It's a mini-distro, live CD, quicker download at just under 100 mb. It has a mounting tool right on the desktop. Once the CD boots up, you can eject it as the distro runs on the computer's RAM. Then you can mount your hard drive, browse through your files and burn a CD of what you want to keep.
