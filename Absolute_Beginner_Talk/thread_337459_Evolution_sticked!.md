### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-01-13
I would check the permissions of "/home/symsr/.evolution/mail/local/Inbox". In Nautilus, hit Control-L and type "/home/sysmsr/.evolution/mail/local". Hit Return. Now find the Inbox folder, right-click and go to Properties. Check that your user account is the owner, and that you have write permission.
