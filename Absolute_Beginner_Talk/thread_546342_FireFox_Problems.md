What kind of system do you have? If it's a slow computer, or has low quality graphics, then all the transitions and loading of large animations (such as flash) could be causing it to bog down. You force-quitting the application doesn't change the fact that the computer is still trying to use it, resulting in you having you manually kill it. The same thing happens in Windows, meaning it's not a Linux-specific problem, and formatting will definitely not help you with a software issue like this.

Two easy options

1) Be patient

2) Get a faster processor / graphics card
