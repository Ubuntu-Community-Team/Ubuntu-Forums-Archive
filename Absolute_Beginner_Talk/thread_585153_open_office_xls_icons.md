### Post by mcallenSchmee on 2007-10-27
right click on the file and go to properties,click on the icon in the properties window, and select the icon you want to use. '/usr/share/icons/Tango/scalable/mimetypes/gnome-mime-application-vnd.lotus-1-2-3.svg' is the one feisty used.
