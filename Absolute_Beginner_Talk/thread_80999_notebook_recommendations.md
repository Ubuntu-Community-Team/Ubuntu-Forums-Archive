### Post by audax321 on 2005-10-23
I would recommend the HP DV1000. Everything on it works under Ubuntu. The only things I haven't tried yet is the card reader and bluetooth (bluetooth was detected when I used Kubuntu, so it should work as well). It also comes with a remote control that can be setup using xbindkeys, a brightview display, and with the 12-cell (bulges out the bottom of the laptop a little but gives it a nice ergonomic slope) gets about 6-8 hours of battery life. I don't know how big of a screen you want though. The DV1000 only has a 14" screen and is made to be extrememly portable. It's the best fit for me because I go to college and needed something I could drag around. And if you decide to get this, I recommend getting only 512 MB of memory in 1 stick and then adding a 1 GB stick after market. You can get one for about $120 from [www.newegg.com](www.newegg.com)
