### Post by vyruss on 2006-06-12
[QUOTE=fluffington]It's a gnome-pannel applet, so you should be able to add it to the panel by right clicking and selecting "Add to Panel...".[/QUOTE]

And you'll find it as: ***Display Geometry Switcher***.

In another note, if you want to find out where a package's files go when you install it, right click on the package in ***Synaptic***, choose ***Properties*** and look at the ***Installed Files*** tab.
