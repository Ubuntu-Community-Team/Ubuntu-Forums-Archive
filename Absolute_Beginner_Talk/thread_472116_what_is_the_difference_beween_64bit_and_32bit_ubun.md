### Post by CautionaryX on 2007-06-12
To clear up the hardware issue:

Any AMD processor on the market today that has a 64 in the name (Sempron 64, Athlon 64/FX/X2) will support 64-bit.

For Intel processors, I think any processors produced after the Pentium 4 Prescott will have 64-bit capability. However, to be safe anyone using a *non*- Core 2 Duo processor should use 32-bit Ubuntu.

I hope this clears the matter up. I know its really confusing, but I hope this helps.
