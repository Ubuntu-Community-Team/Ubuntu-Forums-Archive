### Post by wylfing on 2006-04-22
You could go to the archives themselves and poke around if you want, but I'll warn you there are something like 15,000 applications. If you want to see where the repositories are, take a look at the file /etc/apt/sources.list. You could also just browse through the list in Synaptic. It should show you everything that's available in all the repositories you've enabled.
