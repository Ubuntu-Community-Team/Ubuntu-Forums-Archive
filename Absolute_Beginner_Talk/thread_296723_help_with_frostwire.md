### Post by r4ik on 2006-11-10

Try,

wget -c [http://www.users.on.net/~stubby/FrostWire-4.10.9-2.i586.deb](http://www.users.on.net/~stubby/FrostWire-4.10.9-2.i586.deb)

In a Terminal.

Good luck !
