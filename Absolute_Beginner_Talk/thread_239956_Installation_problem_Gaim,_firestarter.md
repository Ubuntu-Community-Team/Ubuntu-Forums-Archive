### Post by win_zik on 2006-08-20
Sounds like you somehow removed the notification area applet from the panel.
To get it back, simply right click on the panel, choose add to panel and then choose the notification area applet (don't know what it's called exactly as I'm not on an English system, but should be easy to find).

You'll also find the "two comp screen" network indicator there and can also add it to the panel.
