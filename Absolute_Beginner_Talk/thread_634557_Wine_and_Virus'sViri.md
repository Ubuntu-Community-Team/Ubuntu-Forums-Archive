### Post by HermanAB on 2008-01-05
I haven't heard of any viruses that actually work on Wine.  Viruses are very specific in their code since they exploit certain imperfections in MS Windows.  Since Wine is coded different and doesn't have a standard registry, viruses tend to break.

Maybe one day when we get to Wine version 10.0, it will be so good that viruses will work, but at the present sub 1.0 level, they don't.  

So, if you want the full Windows experience, spyware, viruses and all, then Wine is going to be disappointing.

Herman
