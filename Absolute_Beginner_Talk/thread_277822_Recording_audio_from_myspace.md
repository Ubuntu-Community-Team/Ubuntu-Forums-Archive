### Post by dbd on 2006-10-15
If they don't have the download option then there isn't an easy way to do it, since MySpace may not want you to, for all I know it may even be illegal. 

There is a windows tool, which wine may, or may not, allow you to use:
[http://www.tech-recipes.com/rx/1640/myspace_download_any_song_for_free_again](http://www.tech-recipes.com/rx/1640/myspace_download_any_song_for_free_again)

Or alternatively you could just play the song and then get a sound recorder (such as krec or sound-recorder) to record what you are hearing. This way is probably easier, and will definitely work, but takes longer and you may lose a little sound quality.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2006-10-15
