### Post by tonyr on 2006-05-18
Short answer, yes.  **Aptitude** is a front end for **Apt** (clever naming, no?), as 
are **Synaptic**(Gnome GUI) and **Adept**(KDE GUI).   You just gotta 
know the **Apt** cli details and the **Aptitude** control details.
