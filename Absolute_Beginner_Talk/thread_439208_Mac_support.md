### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-10
Macs have moved to the intel chipset. Thus, they now can just use the x86 installer. That is why feisty was only coded for x86 and 64 bit. You can use the Edgy installer [here.]("http://releases.ubuntu.com/6.10/") I don't know if ya should update to Feisty via update manager though... I assume since feisty CDs aren't made for PPC neither are the update files. I don't have experience with the PPC chip, don't take my word on it.

### Post by Najand on 2007-05-10
PPC support is not supported by ubuntu anymore. However, it is now a community supported platform. Look at the following.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=427714](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=427714)
For the Feisty CD (PPC Architecture) can be found here (not on most of official mirrors):
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/feisty/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/feisty/release/)
Official Repositories hasn't changed though and are available through all mirror sites.
