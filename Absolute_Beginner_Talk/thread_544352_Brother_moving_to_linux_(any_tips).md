### Post by str8line on 2007-09-06
As was stated in previous posts, come here often.  Keep an open mind and welcome to open source.  Your brother has made the first step to lasting computer enjoyment.  Another good site for your brother to save some future headaches is [www.ubuntuguide.org]("http://www.ubuntuguide.org") which takes a great deal of the pain of tweaking Ubuntu to meet the needs of the individual user.  

A parting thought....a PC is a Personal Computer, no two are exactly alike and no two users are the same either.  If my wife (a hater of all things computers) can be comfortable with Ubuntu and my 7 year old can install, Trouble Shoot and repair her pc, then your brother will be able to get on just fine.
