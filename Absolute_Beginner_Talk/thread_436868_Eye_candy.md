### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-08
> **TurntableJunky-Xx said:**
> What can I do to give my linux some nice eye candy without beryl?

Easy!

Go to [GnomeLook]("http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=high&page=0") and search for GTK and Metacity themes. GTK (you want 2.0 if you using a newer version of Ubuntu) changes the way menus, icon sets, fonts, color schemes. Metacity is more focused on the window decorator, just the window itself.

Pick and choose which you like,  themes are installed via System > Preferences > Themes.

Have fun, and make sure to show all your window/mac fans how easy it is to change your look :p
