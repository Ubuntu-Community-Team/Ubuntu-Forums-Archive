### Post by machoo02 on 2007-04-12
Check out the [StarCraft page]("http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iAppId=72") over at the Wine AppDB.

Metaphor, WINE is **not** a [virtual machine]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_machine").  Think of it as a translator that will translate commands/calls from Windows-based programs into ones that the Linux kernel/programs can understand.
