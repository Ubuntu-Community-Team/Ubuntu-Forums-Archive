Yes.

1. Install the 'beagle' package.
2. Alt+F2 and run 'beagled'.
3. Right click on one of your panels, and choose 'Add to Panel...'
4. Select the Deskbar.
5. Right click the deskbar, and choose 'Preferences'.
6. Enable Beagle Live Search and whatever other options you may want.
