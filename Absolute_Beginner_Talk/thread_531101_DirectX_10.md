At this point in time no.  Wine and Cedega do not support DX10.  A dual boot setup as suggested above is your best option, at least for now.


To run Windows games you need one of 2 things.  Ehter Windows or a compatibility layer in Linux.  For compatibility you have a few options but the big ones for games are called Wine and Cedega.  Wine is free, Cedega is not but seems to be more compatible with games, at least for the moment.
