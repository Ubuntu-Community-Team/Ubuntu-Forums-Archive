### Post by jken146 on 2007-12-04
If you look in Advanced Desktop Settings, in General Options, in the Desktop Size tab, you will see that you have 1 desktop and a 'horizontal virtual size' of 4.  This allows the cube.  I guess this is why the ordinary desktop switcher does not work.  You can switch with ctrl+alt+<left/right arrow key>  or super+e or the mouse wheel.  Or ctrl+alt+mouse1 click and drag the cube.
