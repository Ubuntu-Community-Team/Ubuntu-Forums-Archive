### Post by Hygelac on 2006-02-06
When I had to connect with dial-up, I found this very useful: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DialupModemHowto](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DialupModemHowto)

I used the 'pppconfig' utility as is explained there (I found it easier to get working than the GNOME or KDE graphical configurations).  Not mentioned in there is the program 'GKrellM' (which is in the repositories), which I used to monitor the status of the the modem (and other hardware).  I hope that helps.
