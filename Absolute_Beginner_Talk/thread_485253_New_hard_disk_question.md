I think that any hard drive you buy should be able to write in any position, as long as you are not spinning it like a top or anything like that. Iv'e had to hang hard drives by the IDE cables in some computers and haven't had any problems.

As for a hard drive to buy. Im dont have much info on which brand name is best, but I am currently using a Western Digital and a Maxtor hard drive. From what I've heard, the two best brands are Seagate and Western Digital.

Your welcome.

Also, why not just buy a external hard drive? They are sometimes cheaper and since you will be using USB it doesn't make much sense to get a SATA hard drive with an enclosure, unless you already have it which I'm thinking you do. Just throwing out some ideas.
