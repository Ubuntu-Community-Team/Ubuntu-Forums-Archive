### Post by Phlosten on 2006-04-05
As much as I like the idea of a totally free software solution, your best bet might be Crossover Office for the commercial environment. From what I gather Crossover Office is in fact Wine anyway, just commercial developed and supported.

If support is important to you, and you are running business critical apps then a commercial solution might be better. They do have a trial version of the product ( [http://www.codeweavers.com/products/download_trial/](http://www.codeweavers.com/products/download_trial/) ), might be worth setting up a test run of it just to see it's capabilities and make sure it runs stable for what you need.
