### Post by raublekick on 2005-11-11
To reach the desired effect, right click on the top bar and select "Add to panel..."

Scroll down to the Desktop & Windows section and add Trash, Workspace Switcher, Window List, and Show Desktop. 

You'll probablyhave to mess around with the positioning to get it how you want it. Just right click one of the objects on the bar and select "Move" However some of the things like the Clock are stuck in place as far as I know.
