### Post by aysiu on 2006-07-15
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats) explains both why Ubuntu doesn't include them *and* how to install them.

If it's such a big deal to you, you might want to consider using Mepis, Blag, or PCLinuxOS. Those three Linux distributions come with a ton of proprietary software--NVidia drivers, MP3 codecs, Java, Flash, etc.
