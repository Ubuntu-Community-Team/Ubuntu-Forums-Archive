### Post by meng on 2006-08-19
LiveCD speeds are significantly slower than installed speeds. However, if you are near minimum specs consider using XFCE rather than GNOME. So long as you have enough disk space, it is relatively simple to try more than one desktop environment and uninstall later. So you're not locked in by your initial choice of X/K/Ubuntu.
