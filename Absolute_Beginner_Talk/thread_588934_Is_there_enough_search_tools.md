### Post by beesthorpe on 2007-10-24
You're forgetting the "find" "locate" and "whereis" commands, not to mention Beagle :)

 I'm no expert but as I understand it Beagle and Tracker are alternative implementations of indexed searching which should speed up searches enormously at the cost of the time the app has to spend actually indexing the files. They neither seem to be completely bullet proof yet which is why neither has been adopted as the default method in Gnome.  The way this is presented to the user certainly can prove confusing, indeed it's filed as a bug [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/beagle/+bug/52878](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/beagle/+bug/52878)
