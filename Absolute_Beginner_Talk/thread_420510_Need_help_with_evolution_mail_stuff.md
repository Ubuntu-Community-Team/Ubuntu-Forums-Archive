### Post by viciouslime on 2007-04-23
Go to edit/preferences/mail preferences/html mail then change the radio button to always load images from the internet.

To make it open in opera, you have to make opera your default browser, for this, go to the system menu at the very top of the screen on the gnome panel (next to applications and places) then go to preferences, then preferred applications, then select opera in the drop-down,
