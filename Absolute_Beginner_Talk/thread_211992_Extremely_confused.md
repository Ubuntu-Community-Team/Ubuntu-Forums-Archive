### Post by Rackerz on 2006-07-09
> I got the screen res working, but I had to run dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg.  I seem to be using the ati drivers.  If I change it to fglrx, I get errors and black screens.  Very, very annoying.

You get errors because 'fglrx' is what makes Xorg use the official ATI drivers and you probably don't have them installed. Unless you intend to use XGL/Compiz or play Games that need 3D Acceleration then 'fglrx' isn't really needed :).

### Post by GarlicFish on 2006-07-09
There are 3 drivers that I have seen for ATI/Radeon cards

ati
radeon
fglrx

I think the "radeon" driver may be a better match for your card.  just replace "ati" with "radeon" in xorg.conf (make a backup first) then run 
sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg
to set your resolutions 
if it doesn't help, revert to your backup.
