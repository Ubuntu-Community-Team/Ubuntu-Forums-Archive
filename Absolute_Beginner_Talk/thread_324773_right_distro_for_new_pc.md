### Post by meng on 2006-12-24
Ubuntu suits a broad range of needs, but what are your specific needs? If you use flash, java, multimedia codecs a lot then you may prefer something like Mint Linux or PCLinuxOS which has much of these pre-installed. Or you may wish to use Automatix to help install and configure these. Or (my own preferred method) choose Ubuntu and install those components yourself from repositories.

No offense, but I'm not going to assume that your MS-techie background will make the transition easier for you than for the "average user". It may help, it may not. Feel free to use these forums to ask other questions.
