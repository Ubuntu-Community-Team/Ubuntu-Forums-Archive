### Post by rai4shu2 on 2007-01-10
If the monitor it indicates is correct, the scan rates are wrong in your xorg.conf file:

```
Section "Monitor"
    Identifier     "Dell 1702FP"
    HorizSync       30.0 - 70.0
    VertRefresh     50.0 - 160.0
    Option         "DPMS"
EndSection
```

Horizontal scan range should be 31 to 80 and Vertical scan range 56 to 76.

[http://support.dell.com/support/edocs/monitors/1702fp/en/specs.htm](http://support.dell.com/support/edocs/monitors/1702fp/en/specs.htm)
