### Post by Redenbacher on 2008-03-13
Studio looks like gutsy with a few apps and some very nice skins pre-installed. Unless you wanted to do a fresh install, you probably could just customize your current install, and just download and  install the apps you want via synaptic. The ubuntu studio wiki gives you a list of all the packages included, so I would imagine you could do that.

If you were to install ubuntu studio from a liveCD, you would be installing all the packages that come with it by default. As far as the desktop and login themes, those would also come by default, but I'm not sure how an audio plugin would be related to them, unless you are talking about specific sounds related to login and actions on the desktop. In that case, if you found the right audio files you could customize that yourself too.
