### Post by twirp on 2008-01-24
You can go to [http://no-ip.com](http://no-ip.com)
Sign-Up, and then activate your account and login.
Then create a new Host (Add Host I think the link is called).
Enter in your desired sub-domain and select one of the free extensions.
The type should be an A Record.
Then enter in the IP address of the machine.
Click submit and it should hopefully be up soon.

There's also other services like cjb.net everydns.net and etc.

You might have to configure your router if you are behind one.
