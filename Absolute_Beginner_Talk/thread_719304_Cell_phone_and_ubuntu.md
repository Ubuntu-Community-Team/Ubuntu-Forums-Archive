### Post by netcrusher88 on 2008-03-14
Bluepad is obviously not what niko is looking for.

niko123, what you're trying to do is known as "tethering" your phone to your computer (even though it's on bluetooth, it's still called tethering).  You can't use your cell phone to dial normal dialup services though - you need to get the service from AT&T that allows computers to tether to the phone.  Smartphone connect, I think it's called.  If you search Ubuntu Forums for "tether" there's a bunch of threads about it - including some specifically for the Blackberry series.  You could ALSO try the instructions at [http://www.litljay.com/howtos/tether/](http://www.litljay.com/howtos/tether/) (I'm going to) but they're really written for SuSE, not Ubuntu. 

Or you could buy me a Smartphone Connect plan and I'll customize them for Ubuntu. :wink:

NOTE: your phone WILL tether if you don't have the proper plan, it just won't work.
