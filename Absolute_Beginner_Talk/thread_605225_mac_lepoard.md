### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-11-06
Dockwise, AWN will get you pretty close (probably not perfect, mind you) to Leopard's dock.  It has stacks and reflections.

If you don't like AWN there's umpteen million other docks that try to emulate it, but most have something funky with them.

For dashboard, look into screenlets.  With compiz, you can enable the widget plugin and only show them on a keypress like in OSX.

To get your panel to look like OSX, have a look at this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=241868](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=241868)

Also, to get some things to look *just* right you're going to have to be creative.  For example, there is no Finder clone, but you can get close.
