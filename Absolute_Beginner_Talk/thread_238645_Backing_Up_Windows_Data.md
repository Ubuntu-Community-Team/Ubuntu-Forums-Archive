### Post by Tom Brokaw on 2006-08-18
> **Seldoms said:**
> 
I am currently backing up my swsetup, WINDOWS, and system32 folder.


Those are Windows install/run files and are not worth backing up, as you'd need to reinstall Windows for them to work properly (I'm pretty sure).  Don't know what swsetup is, though.

> **manicka said:**
> The most obvious thing to back up is your own data in my My Documents, then I'd think about specific application data you might need in C:\Documents and Settings\*username*\Application Data e.g. your Firefox settings.

This is the stuff you want to back up: music, pictures, word docs, etc.
