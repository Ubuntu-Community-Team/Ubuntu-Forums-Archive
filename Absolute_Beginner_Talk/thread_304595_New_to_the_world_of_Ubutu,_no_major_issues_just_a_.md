### Post by jeremydigital on 2006-11-22
So I wanted to say to anybody that may be intimidated by the effort that it takes to install Ubutu, dont worry. Keep at it and you will become comfortable using this software. I have had it installed for 5 days, and I have to say I'm upset with myself that I waited so long to try to learn something new.

I do internet technical support for a cable provider, so I have to guide not-so-knowledgable people around windows XP/OSX all day setting up browsers, email, checking ip and tcp/ip configs, and NIC drivers. I myself used windows Xp, 2k, ME and 98 in my life. The common sense that went into this software is just a breath of fresh air. 

These forums and the load of links that are available with good, updated info are a major reason that this is not impossible for someone like myself. I don't program software or anything, but I am basicly trying to learn a new language of sorts. Already I have themes, web, email, ipod, login managers, etc.. Enough to feel like progress is being made.

I would like to see a few good *powerful* audio applications to sink my teeth into. Also I am interested in learining more about how the networking in handled. I'm sure I will find all of that in due time.

Just a few words after clicking links for the last handful of days. Cheers.
