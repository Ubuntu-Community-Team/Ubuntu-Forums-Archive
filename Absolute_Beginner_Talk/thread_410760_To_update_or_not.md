### Post by Tsen on 2007-04-16
I'm using the Feisty Fawn Beta right now, and it's perfectly stable for me.
If you're relying on your computer heavily and won't have time to troubleshoot should a problem arise, then I wouldn't recommend installing it quite yet.
Once it's finalized in a week or so, you should be fine to install.  There will probably be a few lingering bugs, but the bulk of them should be worked out.  Nothing worth waiting a few months to avoid, anyway.
