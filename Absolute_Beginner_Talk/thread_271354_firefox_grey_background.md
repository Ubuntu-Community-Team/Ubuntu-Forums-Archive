### Post by jorgen_s on 2007-05-22
This is more than 6 moths ago this post was posted, but I hope this can be useful for someone out there.
Worked fine for me in Feisty AMD64 with Firefox32 2.0.0.3

Howto change from gray to white default background color in Mozilla Firefox:

In Firefox write about:config in the url field and hit enter.

Check the value browser.display.background_color. Mine was #C0C0C0

Change the value til #FFFFFF

Restart your Firefox.
