### Post by crystal on 2006-06-30
I think your profile folder contains your settings. The [Firefox FAQ](http://www.mozilla.org/support/firefox/faq) links to a page on [locating one's profile folder](http://www.mozilla.org/support/firefox/edit#profile). Once you have found your profile folder, you can just take a backup of it, and restore after the install.
