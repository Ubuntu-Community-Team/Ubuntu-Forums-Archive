### Post by chili555 on 2008-04-21
I assume you are trying to get into the BIOS. Grub has not changed that at all. You have a limited amount of time to press F1 or Esc to go to the BIOS, or setup screens, just a few seconds. By the time you see Grub, the BIOS has already handed off control to Grub and it's too late. The answer is, as soon as you see any flicker of life from your Sony, start repeatedly pressing F1 and you will get to setup.

### Post by bwallum on 2008-04-21
I think you were accessing your bios to make the graphics change. You may have had a small programme that interfaced between the bios and you and that has been scrubbed.

Try to enter the bios setup, try Del. F1 or F10. From there see if you can tweek the graphics. Grub does not change your bios, it merely positions itself on the master boot record (mbr) to be booted first.
