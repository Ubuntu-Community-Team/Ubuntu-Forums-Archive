### Post by amingv on 2008-02-27
-Yes, upgrading your system is better (at least for me) than installing from scratch. If you have a decent connection it will take 3-4 hours to the most (took ~2 hours in mine). If you have a slow connection I think there are ways to upgrade from a CD; but someone else will have to elaborate on that, since I've never done it.

-Yes, if you copy the contents of /home/username you can paste it over a new install, effectively keeping most of tour settings. Just notice that some configuration files may not coincide with the new version standards (Another plus of upgrading vs reinstalling).
