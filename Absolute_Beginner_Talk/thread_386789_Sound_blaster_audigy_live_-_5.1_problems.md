### Post by nsn3 on 2007-03-17
Double click on the volume icon in the top panel.

Go Edit>Preferences,  tick all the options available and press OK.

Then go to Options Tab and put these settings (that's what I'm using, with the same soundcard):

Front Jack: Rear Output

Center/LFE Jack: Center/LFE Output

Rear Jack: Center/LFE Output

Or work with these three settings till you find the settings you like the most. But your problem should be gone using my settings.
