### Post by mostwanted on 2005-05-19
You need to have already registrered your email with a PassPort account with Microsoft.

Open Gaim, click the button called "Accounts" and add a new account. Add your email as a nickname and of course your password. To have Gaim automatically start, you need to edit your start-up sessions. Open the sessions setup application and add the command "gaim" to the third tab.

### Post by Zelut on 2005-05-19
Gaim is great :)  I've been using it for years in & out of linux...

If you open GAIM it should default to an 'accounts' window (if you haven't configured any accounts yet).  You can click 'add' & configure the protocol (ie; MSN, AIM, Yahoo, iCQ, etc), username / password & options.

If you've got a few kids that use seperate accounts you wont want to select the 'auto-login' option.  That'll automatically login to that account when GAIM is opened.  If you've created multiple accounts & not selected the auto-login you (or they) will be able to select their user from a drop-down menu to login to their account.

Its fairly simple & I'm sure you'll be able to get it done.  Let me know if you need any more help.  I'm a big supporter of GAIM and, of course, ubuntu :)
