### Post by freebird54 on 2007-04-08
If I understand you correctly, you are planning on hooking up by wireless to a router that is already hooked to an XP machine?  Provided the router is hooked to the internet, XP or not should be irrelevant.

That wiki entry is pretty old, and things seemed to work pretty well even back then.  I expect that edgy or above should have even better support.  As for the wireless - I would check that out by brand name/model before expecting easy success - there have been issues on that before - leading to some creative solutions.

Wireless support is one of the things that is better (more easily) supported on Feisty Fawn (7.04) release - coming on the 19th of this month.  You might want to try the Live CD of Feisty before deciding how to proceed.  (it runs right from the CD, without touching the hard drives - so you can test how well it recognizes things on your system.  It will be slower than from a hard drive - but if your internet just goes, you know you have a winner!
