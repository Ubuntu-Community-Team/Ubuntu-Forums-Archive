### Post by MrFSL on 2008-01-09
My apologies, I didn't think you were being inflammatory at all. It's just that for Windows and Mac there are vendor supplied drivers for webcams. I don't know of a single - not even one - webcam that has a vendor supplied driver for Linux.

The existing drivers are at best hacks (some good - some not so good) so the skype team has a pretty huge job making their software work. I am shocked that they have bothered with a Linux port at this early stage; but I, like you, am very glad they did.

I use mebeam ([http://mebeam.com](http://mebeam.com)) I use it for the video while using skype for the audio. It works and doesn't seem to suffer from the same issues with resolution on logitech cameras / gspca drivers.
