### Post by sleepingdragon on 2007-10-31
AOL UK do provide the settings to connect to their service if you have your own external modem/router, their software is not necessary.

You can find the UK help page [[COLOR="Red"]here[/COLOR]]("http://help.aol.co.uk/what-are-the-essential-settings-for/article/20060802092709990038"). I can't vouch for anywhere else, but try using "manual configuration modem" in their site search engine and you should find something useful.

I personally never trust any internal "winmodem", they're a pain to get working and make the computer do half the work. If you can, always go for a decent external modem.
