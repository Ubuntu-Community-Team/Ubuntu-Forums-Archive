### Post by ofb on 2008-04-17
So... what's the problem specifically? When you open K3B there's six big black buttons of common choices, and the two on the right are Burn CD Image and Burn DVD ISO Image.

Also you can use the Tools section of the Tool Bar to get the same choices. And of course there's the K3B Handbook under Help.

It's a pretty straightforward program. Experiment with an RW if you're feeling unsure, but you'll probably do it right the first time.
