### Post by twrock on 2006-11-21
I'm using Amarok 1.4.3 in Ubuntu Dapper. I had a weird thing happen after I imported my music. In the "collection" list, most all of the music is catagorized by Artist/Album, just like it says it should be. However, all of my "Greatest Hits" albums got catagorized together under the "artist" named "Various Artists", which is placed at the top of the alphabetical listing, above the A's. Other than each of the albums being named "Greatest Hits", there is no other apparent pattern (for example, Dan Fogelberg and Styx are both in there). Additionally, since it seems to be ignoring the Artist for these albums, the music is listed by track, all track #1 songs then all track #2 songs, etc. Has anyone else seen this? Any solution?

---

### Post by Bazzaah on 2006-11-24
Found this out this morning after importing some music which it didn't recognise - you can right click the folder and choose the do not show in various artists option, then it will file under the applicable artist.

---

### Post by twrock on 2006-11-26
Fantastic. That did it. I'm still quite curious why those albums were put there in the first place. But at least there is a solution. Thanks.
