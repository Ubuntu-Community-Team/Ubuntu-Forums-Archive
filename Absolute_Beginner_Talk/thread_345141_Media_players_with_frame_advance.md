### Post by sardion on 2007-01-23
Mplayer can do everything, so can VLC.  Mplayer is not exactly easy to use (to do all the stuff you'll have to use the command line).  VLC is also not exactly intuitive.  Off the top I don't know how to frame by frame with them, but I know they can.  Look at their sites and see if they're what you're looking for (both are in the ubuntu repos, either universe or multiverse).

As to flash... I think Adobe/Macromedia makes available a standalone player for linux as well as the plugin.  But flash9 for linux is beta right now so there may or may not be a standalone player.  Google for liunux flash9 beta and find Adobe's official releases, that's where it would be.
