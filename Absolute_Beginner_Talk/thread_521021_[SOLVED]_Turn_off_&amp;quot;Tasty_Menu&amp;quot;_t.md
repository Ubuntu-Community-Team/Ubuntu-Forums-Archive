### Post by ravenwere on 2007-08-09
Try this.

Right click on the toolbar menu.
Select panel menu
Expand then select configure panel...
Select Appearance  icon on left hand side
Under the General section at the top deselect the Enable icon mouseover effects button.

Hope this addresses the issue.

r
