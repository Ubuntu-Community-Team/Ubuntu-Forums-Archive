### Post by jimrz on 2008-03-16
you could, also, either: 

1- use alacarte menu editor to have it show in your menu (will be under System > Preferences)

                                                                       or

2- in your deskbar applet type "hplip", which will give several choices, select "execute" then this choice will remain in your deskbar for use anytime thereafter.
