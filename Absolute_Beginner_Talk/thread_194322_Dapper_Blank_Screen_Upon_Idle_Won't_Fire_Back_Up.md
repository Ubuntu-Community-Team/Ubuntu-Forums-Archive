### Post by lazyron on 2006-06-29
I saw something similar on my Dell E1405. Screen went black, no amount of key pressing or trackpad swishing seemed to get it working. Finally hit the 'esc' key and the screen came back up with the screensaver dialog lock box up. I had to re-enter my password and everything was back to normal.

So in addition to checking Power Management, check your Screensaver options as well.

Good Luck.

edit: Actually it wasn't 'esc', I had to cycle through to tty0 then back to tty7 for the screen to come up. :P
