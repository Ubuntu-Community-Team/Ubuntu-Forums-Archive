### Post by jimrz on 2007-10-31
> **dense said:**
> I have Ubuntu 7.10 new install and not liking icons on desktop, what is the sda1 on my desktop for and can i not have it on there? I know i am crazy but i just like a clean desktop with no icons.
Thanks :)

Run "gconf-editor" go to apps > nautilus > desktop and un-check the box marked "volumes_visible".   This will make the disk icons on your desktop go away.
