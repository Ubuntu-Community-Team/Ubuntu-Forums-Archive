### Post by shirilover on 2007-08-30
You would need to transcode the audio and video portions to DVD specifications(very time consuming).

My guess is that the source for the movie was probably a Blue-Ray disc, and so, the resolution(probably 1920x1080) of the file is much greater than a normal DVD(720x480 or 720x576).

The easiest solution would be to use your TV as a second monitor.
