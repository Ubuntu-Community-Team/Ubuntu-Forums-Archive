### Post by raijinsetsu on 2007-09-14
Turning off bluetooth won't really save you power. My bluetooth earpiece can run for 3 days before it needs charging. I really don't think having the transmitter active will drain your battery.

Power consumption (from greatest, to least)
3D Video/HDD, LCD, 2D Video, CD-ROM, WLAN, USB, BT

This is not scientific, but it is, to the best of my knowledge, close to correct.
