### Post by igknighted on 2007-02-21
It has always been 'safe' to upgrade to edgy.  I would follow these guidelines to determine whether it is right for you:

Upgrade if: You want the latest programs (ie, if you want FF2 then upgrade), If you don't mind doing an upgrade/reinstall every 6mo-1yr to stay current, or if you have any issues with dapper... I know on my system Edgy runs much smoother than dapper did.

Don't upgrade if: You value not messing with your OS.  If you want to install the OS once and forget it until 3 years down the road, go with Dapper.  Also if you try Edgy and find you have an incompatability, stick with Dapper (a good general rule for picking a linux distro in general... use the one that recognizes your hardware best).

Hope this helps, I personally think that Edgy is a far more advanced OS than Dapper (the new boot sequence is much quicker for one) and highly recommend it over Dapper, however if messing with your OS isn't something you want to deal with once a year to stay up to date, you are better off with an LTS version like Dapper.
