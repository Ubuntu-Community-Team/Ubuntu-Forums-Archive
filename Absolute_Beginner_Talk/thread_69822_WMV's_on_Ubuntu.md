### Post by GreyFox503 on 2005-09-28
I'm able to play about 95% of the .wmv's on my computer using xine and the codecs found the mplayer site.

The other 5% play Ok, but the audio doesn't work.  If I really wanted to fix it, I might try to do more work, but you can set up your computer to play most of them in a few minutes.  Not very difficult.

xine
totem-xine

are packages in the repository I use, and I downloaded from this site:

[http://www1.mplayerhq.hu/homepage/design7/codecs.html](http://www1.mplayerhq.hu/homepage/design7/codecs.html)

The codecs I needed to play Windows Media and Quicktime files.
