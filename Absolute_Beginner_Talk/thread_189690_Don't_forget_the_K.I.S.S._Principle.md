### Post by bobpur on 2006-06-05
I posted a thread earlier about my computer locking up at different times due to what I thought were Dapper, Nvidia driver, operator error (Not likely:)) or, maybe even, sunpot problems. 
After stumbling, falling, tripping and other newbie (Yeah, I am.) stuff,through the guts of my computer and Dapper OS...  I LOWERED THE SCREEN RESOLUTION. My computer has worked great, now, for about 2+ hrs without even so much as a hiccup. Life is good! 
Don't sweat the hard stuff until the easy stuff has been checked.
