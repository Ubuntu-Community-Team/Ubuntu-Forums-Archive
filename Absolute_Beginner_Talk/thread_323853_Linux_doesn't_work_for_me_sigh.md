### Post by msak007 on 2006-12-23
That's a very recent motherboard which may have some hardware that wasn't supported in older kernels. If you received an Ubuntu CD in the mail then it is most likely version 6.06 (Dapper Drake), which was running kernel 2.6.17 and some of your hardware may not be supported. I would suggest trying a download of the most recent version (6.10 - Edgy Eft) as it ships with kernel 2.6.19 and will probably have better support for your hardware. You can download it [here]("http://ftp.ussg.iu.edu/linux/ubuntu-releases/6.10/"). You can either download the ISO and burn it in Windows, or to save bandwidth and help others use bittorrent to download and seed it.

EDIT: You want the "Desktop" CD (not alternate or server) - it functions as both a Live CD and installer CD.
