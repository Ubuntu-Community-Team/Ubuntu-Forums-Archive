### Post by mivo on 2007-12-24
Are torrents legal? That depends on what they are for (it's just a way of distributing data, independent of the type of data) and where you live. I believe in Canada, for example, downloading commercial audio files is legal (distribution is not), but that is certainly an exception in the Western world. In general, commercial stuff, i.e. ripped DVDs or songs that you would normally purchase at iTunes or buy on an audio CDs, is not legal. iTunes doesn't seem to be available for Linux, as far as I know.

As for the PS3 question, perhaps [this page]("http://www.querycat.com/faq/769fcb2ec19fcd21b2a2407486eb19ac") is a starting point? (I only have a PSP.)
