### Post by bscbrit on 2005-12-22
Er, your last post is a little confusing - at least to me.  I would suggest that everything is _not_ fine if the printer is not printing!  But I think I know what you are trying to say.

Ok, lets go back a bit.  Are the symptoms the same as you first described? I.e. the file can be seen in the printer queue but there is no sign of life from the printer.  Do any lights flash to indicate any form of communication between the computer and the printer?

Check that the printer is selected as the default printer - i.e. to confirm that you _are_ sending files to it.  (SYSTEM -> ADMINISTRATION - PRINTING.  Right click on your printer and select 'Make Default').

Check that you are printing to a device and not to a file - it is the same dialogue for both.  Print using the FILE - PRINT dialogue and not by pressing Ctrl-P.  Check each field in the dialogue for sensible settings.

Check that the cable is plugged in and the printer is switched on!  (Please don't be offended - these things can be overlooked)

Let me know what you find.
