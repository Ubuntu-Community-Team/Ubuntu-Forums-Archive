### Post by molly_001 on 2007-06-23
The partition created to install ubuntu on my Pc will swipe the Windows XP partition correct?

If you mean swipe as in "wipe away" -- Negative.  Back up your XP data files as you wish, then use LiveCd of Gparted first, to shrink the XP partition.  Leave about 20 GB as "unallocated, free" for Ubuntu to slide in.


- How would i go about installing my mobo drivers (ASRock) and sound drivers (C-Media) along with the Nvidia GPU? is it from the driver CD or do i install them through ubuntu?

You'll find that the mobo drivers won't be a problem; the sound drivers probably won't be either if you use Ubuntu 7.04.  Cross that bridge when you get there, however.  If you have no sound after installing Ubuntu, folks here will help you acquire and activate such drivers.  For nVidia just use a snappy utility called enVy .




- What windows games are supported (if you know of a good site for a list)? i just mainly want Unreal Tournament 2004 and Unreal tournament 3 when its out.

Good luck.  The 6200 is very low end -- this is not an insult, but I assume you are OK with it in Windows games you have now.  I don't know of any list, but Google will.



- What do i do to get MP3 music files to work in Ubuntu? Do i need to re-encode them in XP first? or can i transfer them over to Ubuntu then re-encode them?

File format .mp3 immediately playable when moved to Linux Ubuntu desktop.  In fact when you hover your cursor over the filename.mp3, you'll hear Sinatra crooning softly, or whatever the song is you are hovering over.




- Do you know of any useful tools to keep Ubuntu running tip-top? as in tools like in XP (registry cleaner, disk cleanup etc.)

You're going to find that its speed blows away Windoze.  Defragmentation is never needed, etc.




- Is limewire supported? Ogg Vorbis, i have never seen that filetype in limewire... as in my 4th question, if limewire is supported, can i re-encode mp3 files to Ogg Vorbis?

Don't know, though I see some threads titled with Limewire.




- I connect wired to a D Link Router using Orange 8MB broadband, is it auto detect and connection like in XP or do i need to note the IP, subnet mask etc.?

Wired ethernet will connect very well for you in 7.04, it will just be "on" with your first login.



- Is the Install really THAT simple from Live CD ?

Yes.  In your case, after resizing your NTFS partition (Win XP) , please boot into Windows twice.  Then, install away on the largest free space of the disk - a vacuum which you just created.  



- I suppose my Ipod will become useless as iTunes wont work?

Good question.  



- Different themes? are they easy to install unlike patching the uxtheme in windows etc.?

Super easy, click, done.


- Is my current hardware specs compatible?:


Yes.
