### Post by DrMega on 2008-02-05
What sounds are trying? Sounds from games (installed from Add/Remove or Synaptic? MP3s?

The reason I ask is that the default setup of Ubuntu won't play MP3s. The good news is that that is really easy to change. If you go to your system menu and run Synaptic Package Manager, look for 'ubuntu-restricted-extras', tick the tick box and say apply.

If it is something else, then sorry I can't help you (but I'm sure plenty of more knowledgeable folks can).

EDIT: If sound is broke in Windows, you may need to reinstall the drivers that came with your soundcard/motherboard.
