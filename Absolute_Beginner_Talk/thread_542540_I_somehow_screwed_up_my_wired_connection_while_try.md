### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-09-04
Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]System>Administration>Network[/COLOR]

When prompted enter your [COLOR="Blue"]password[/COLOR]

[COLOR="Blue"]Un-tick Wireless Connection[/COLOR] and[COLOR="Blue"] put a tick in Wired Connection[/COLOR]

Provided you haven't changed any of the properties of your wired connection, your 100 Base T should now be active.
