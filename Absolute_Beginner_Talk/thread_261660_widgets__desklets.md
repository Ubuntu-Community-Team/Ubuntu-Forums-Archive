### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-09-20
Not sure about the system info, but if you right-click the Panel (top or bottom) and choose "Add", you can put a weather info applet into the panel.

After adding it in, right-click and choose (can't remember if it's Properties or Configure... stuck on an XP box at work right now :( )... then select your location.  I love that thing - it's how I get all my weather info.

No reason to beg for forgiveness; folks here are happy to help!  There are actually a lot of 'widgets' that come with Ubuntu that you might be happy with.  For example, look at the toolbar that stretches across the top of the screen.  Right-click in a blank area, and select **Add to Panel...** from the menu.
