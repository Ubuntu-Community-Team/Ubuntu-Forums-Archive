### Post by bionnaki on 2005-11-16
[QUOTE=rad_dreamer2kx]for music, movies, dvd burning, cd burning, browsers....etc. Im knew and have no idea what to use.thanks!![/QUOTE]

I dont know what the most popular are, but this is what I use and recommend:

music: beep media player for playback, nicotine and bittorrent 4.1.7 for p2p, crip for ripping to flac/ogg, easytag for ID3 tag editing, streamtuner for internet radio, audioconvert script for converting lossless to lossy (for my portable digital player)

movies: gxine for viewing, dvdbackup+wine+dvdshrink+k3b for dvd copying, tovid for creating dvds from .avi etc.

cd burning: k3b

browsers: firefox as primary, epiphany for backup.
