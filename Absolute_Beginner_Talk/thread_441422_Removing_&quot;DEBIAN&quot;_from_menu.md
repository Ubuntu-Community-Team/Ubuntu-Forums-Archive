Ah, I know what your talking about now with the Debian thing, it is the main menu, just not where your looking.

Go to the Main Menu editor. Look to the left of the list, select the top of the menu "Applications", it should have an arrow next to it, then look in the pane at the right. There is a debian entry, uncheck it. There isn't a way to uncheck debian from the debian entry if its empty.

All fixed :)
