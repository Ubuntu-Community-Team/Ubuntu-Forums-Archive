That works ok, but a better way to look at your cbr file is to get a comic book reader. A great one is comix. You can look for it in the repositories and instal it within a few clicks. Remember to edit your repositories if you don't find it at first. Or download the lastest and best version at [http://comix.sourceforge.net/](http://comix.sourceforge.net/)

Follow the install directions, its a breeze. You just have to cut and paste.

Enjoy
