### Post by CatKiller on 2006-10-22
By default, **Ctrl**-**Alt**-**+** and **Ctrl**-**Alt**-**-**, where the **+** (plus) and **-** (minus) keys are on the numeric keypad, will cycle through the resolutions defined in xorg.conf.
