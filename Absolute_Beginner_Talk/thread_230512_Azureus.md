### Post by Jagot on 2006-08-06
Azureus downloads to a hidden folder in your /home directory.  Open your /home and hit ctrl+h to show hidden files.  You should now see a folder (amongst a lot of others) called .azureus.  Double click and there should be a folder i nthere called torrents - this is where it downloads to.

And no - it doesn't work like Windows.  Take a look at this diagram:
