### Post by indytim on 2007-08-16
Carl,

I'll give you a partial answer on
> B-) want to move ones Ubuntu installation off of one PC and onto a newer PC

Unless your hardware configuration is the same from PC-a -> PC-b, you may experience problems with startup of your new PC-b.  If the pc's h/w was the same, you could make an image of your ops from PC-a via say, Partimage, and then use Partimage to re-install on PC-B.

IndyTim
