### Post by RoyArne on 2006-09-16
> **ron999 said:**
> Excellent work RoyArne

From the [http://www.radiofeeds.co.uk/]("http://www.radiofeeds.co.uk/") page, right click on one of the boxes that say "64K Stereo", "32k Stereo", etc. and choose "Copy Link Location". You can then paste the link with Control-V or clicking with the middle mouse button above the spot where you want to paste it.
