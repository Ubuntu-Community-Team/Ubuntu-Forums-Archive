Not to diss Ubuntu...

I am concerned when you mention "windows servers". It sounds as if they may be "mission critical" to you and I am not sure everyone would advise Ubuntu in this setting.

In my personal experience I would voice some concerns with the stability of Ubuntu as I lost my entire OS recently with no explanation. I now "dabble" with Ubuntu but do not use it as my primary OS. See also the recent debacle with X. Ask yourself if you can tolerate this in your setting.

On Distrowatch this week there was a conversation regarding this issue via some IT professionals. The consensus appears to be Debian (stable branch) or Redhat (Possible SUSE) for the type of set up you may be contemplating.

[distrowatch](http://distrowatch.com/weekly.php?issue=20060828&mode=1#comments)
See comments # 83 and 87.

I am not saying not to use Ubuntu, nor am I suggesting Distrowatch is a great source of information.

 I am advising caution if you are talking a Enterprise level implementation (of Linux).
