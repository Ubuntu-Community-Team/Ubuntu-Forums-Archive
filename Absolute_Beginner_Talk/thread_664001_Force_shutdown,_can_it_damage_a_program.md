### Post by houstonbofh on 2008-01-10
Depending on the program, there is a very slight possibility it could not gracefully exit from a write and corupt a single file.  An example is a Xcell spreadsheet in Open Office that got hung, and forcing a quit caused an issue with another native spreadsheet.  However, Open Office automagically fixed the broken file.  The MS Xcell marco viri was another issue. :)  All that said, that is about the only case I have personally seen, and I force a lot of shutdowns.  (Especially Firefox...)
