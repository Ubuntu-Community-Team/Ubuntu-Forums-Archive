### Post by TLE on 2006-12-07
First of all welcome on board as a translator!

You should really contact the team that heads the translations for your language. They can tell you all about how to do it and might also have some conventions that you should follow and might offer to read through your translations, as to ensure a higher quality.

Furthermore, many teams actually translates everything that is not Ubuntu specific upstream, meaning that e.g. GNOME it translated and uploaded to GNOME directly (and so might not even involve Launchpad), that way your translations can benefit other distributions also.

Each language should have a team page in Launchpad so you should be able to find a contact there. If you can't find the team or need me to elaborate on anything just write again.
Regards TLE
