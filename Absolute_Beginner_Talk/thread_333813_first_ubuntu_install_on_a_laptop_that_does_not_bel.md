### Post by solracarevir on 2007-01-08
I have been using linux since 2 years by now, and during all taht time, all off my friends seems to be scared about that weird OS that I use on my computers, But today one of those friends called me: "Carlos, I'm sick of having to reinstall windows, my computer just got another virus that i cant remove". I told him: ok bring the laptop and i'll try to remove it for you. "No!" he replied, I want you to install me that OS that you use. Ubuntu? I asked. "yes, ubuntu" he answered. he arrived an our later, and two hours later he left to his house with ubuntu 6.06 on his compaq presario laptop, everything worked as always, even the wireless with ndiswrapper. he didn't called me during the rest of the day, so i thought... everything is ok or he haven't used the laptop yet, so I decided to call him and (trying to hide the excitement) i asked: any issues with ubuntu? "no" he replied.. "I just have finished transferring my backup and everything is working great, it feels  fast and responsive, thanks you dude. but..(heart stops because of that word) i don't see any anti virus application.(heart starts working again).  "It won't be an issue, i haven't used one since i used linux and you really don't need it all" I replied. minutes later the conversation ends. I feel so happy! my friend is not a computer oriented guy and he wanted to have ubuntu installed on his computer! more than that, he wasn't scared because it looks different or behave different! I hope this to be the beginning of a trend with my friends so I will be happy to use my free time during weekends to install ubuntu on their computers!!!! I just wanted to share my happiness with the community!
