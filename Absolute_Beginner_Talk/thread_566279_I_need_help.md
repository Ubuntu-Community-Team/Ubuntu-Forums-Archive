### Post by goatflyer on 2007-10-03
Well, welcome to you, and its good you are patient as well as stubborn.

You will have to do this in stages.

Stage 1) get some version of ubuntu or linux working
Stage 2) Have or install proper graphics drivers for your graphics card for the 3D effects you want.
Stage 3) Install compiz or beryl

For Stage 1) versions of Ubuntu you could try are (from most recent to old)

Gutsy = (not officially released yet)
Feisty = 7.04
Edgy = 6.10
Dapper = 6.06 LTS

You don't want anything before that.

Try LiveCDs first.  If they don't boot up, and let you connect to internet, don't bother to install them - try something else instead. If you run out of Ubuntus to try, there are other distros, don't be shy.
 
You are better off with the most recent version which runs on your computer.

For stage 2)  You need to find out what graphics card you have: nvidia, ati, or integrated Intel (or even S3, Trident,  or something else).  Then you need to make another post telling us what kind you have.  Perhaps the default ubuntu graphics driver will be good enough, no one can tell you yet.

For stage 3) If you have chosen Feisty or Gutsy, I believe you have a preferences menu to select 3d effects,  For Dapper you have to install the compiz package from the repositories.

Anyway, proceed slowly, one step at a time, do not try for everything at once.

And again, welcome.
