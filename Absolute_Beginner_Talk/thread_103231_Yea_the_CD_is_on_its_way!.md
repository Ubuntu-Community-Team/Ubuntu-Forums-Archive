### Post by az on 2005-12-13
Ubuntu is generally faster than XP, but takes about 20 seconds longer to boot.  

850 with 192 megs of ram is fine.  The minimum would be 400 MHz and 128 megs of ram.  More ram is better.

The default install takes just over 2Gigs of hard drive space, including a swap space (virtual memory).  Give yourself at least four or five gigs of disk space if you want to play around with stuff and go crazy.
