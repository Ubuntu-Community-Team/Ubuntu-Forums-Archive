### Post by ahaurw01 on 2007-05-26
(If the panel happened to get deleted, you can right click on the one at the bottom of the screen and select New Panel.) Right click on the panel and select Add to Panel... and then add the Menu Bar. Not quite sure what happened to you, but its really easy to add/delete/customize Gnome panel bars with a little bit of right clicking.
