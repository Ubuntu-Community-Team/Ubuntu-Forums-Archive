### Post by bobbocanfly on 2007-11-11
I have a Linksys WRT54GS V.4 sharing an Internet connection between my 2 computers. It also acts as a wireless internet access point for my sisters laptop. I want the router to allow access to the FTP and Web Servers on 192.168.1.101 from the Internet. Can this be done with my WRT54GS (if yes, how?) or do i need to buy another piece of Hardware?

---

### Post by RichardIII on 2007-11-11
You'd have to access the router through your browser, your manual should give you the address (192.168.1.100?), from there you can set the "Port forwarding"; set port 21 (default FTP-port) to the address(es) you want the FTP-requests/replies to be forwarded  to. This is fairly simple and straightforward, but the most important part is: getting access to the router's web interface!
