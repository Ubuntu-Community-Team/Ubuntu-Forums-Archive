### Post by LukeyMI on 2006-10-15
Try doing this:

mv ~/.etwolf ~/.etwolf-bak

Launch ET and see if you still get those errors. It sounds like a file/folder permission problem to me. You could just try correcting the permissions too, but sometimes it's easier (takes less time) just starting fresh than deciding what you want to keep. Besides, if you play the same maps/servers pretty much constantly you might have alot of other junk pk3 files you don't need.
