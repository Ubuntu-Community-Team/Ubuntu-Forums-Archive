### Post by ed-koala on 2007-10-27
After looking thru the compiz setting, all I can suggest is - under Cube Caps - Appearance, delete the bottom image listed.  Then, under Behavior, uncheck Draw Bottom Face.  I'll check this on mine to see how it works.  Looks like you'll have to use an image for the top that is already low opacity tho.
