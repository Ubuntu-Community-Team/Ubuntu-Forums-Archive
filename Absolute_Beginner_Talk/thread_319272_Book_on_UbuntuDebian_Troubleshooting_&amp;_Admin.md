### Post by deadgobby on 2006-12-15
There are so many books on Linux/Unix either on Debian or Slax for sample. Buying books take up money and who wants a bunch of books that can serve as a cup coaster. Who has a bunch of books? Your local Library. Ubuntu is a Debian based and you might want to start there. At least when you go to your public Library you can check out mounds of books and return them. With no hassles of the return counter or perhaps a restock fee. They're books written in lawyer mode and there are books that are in simple lingo. So stop at your local Library and you may even find a good book to read to take your mind off of Linux/Unix.
