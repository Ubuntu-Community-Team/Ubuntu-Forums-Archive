### Post by st33med on 2007-05-28
Hello jcjenn. I assume that you want to use a printer directly connected to the computer. Go to System>Administration>Printing. A window should pop-up with an icon labeled "New Printer". Click on it. Now have Local or Detected Printer and Use Detected Printer dots on. In the Use Detected Printer section, select the printer you are using. Hit next. Now find your manufacturer and choose the printer you are using (in this case, Stylus CX3810) and choose the driver at the bottom. Now, go ahead and click next and enter any additional info you want into the fields. Hit Apply.

The printer should show on the Printers window. Right-click on it and click 'Properties'. Hit Print Test Page at the bottom. It should print.

Happy Printing:popcorn:
