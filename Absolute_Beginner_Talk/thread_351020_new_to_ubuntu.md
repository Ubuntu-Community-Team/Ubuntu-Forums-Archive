### Post by Coelocanth on 2007-02-01
Ubuntu gives you the ability to have multiple desktops. Just switch desktops and open a new app on it. (The default is 4 desktops, but you can add more. The icons for them are in the lower right of your screen).

I use this quite often: e-mail open on one desktop, browser on another, Open Office on a third, perhaps a game on a fourth and so on. It's very slick.
