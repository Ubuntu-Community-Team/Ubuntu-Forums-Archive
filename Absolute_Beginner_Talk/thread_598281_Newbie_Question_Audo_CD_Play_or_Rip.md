How can I make it so that when I insert a music cd it gives me the option to play or rip it to MP3?

### Post by zzztownsend on 2007-10-31
When you insert a CD, sound juicer should start. This has a play button and an extract button.

If sound-juicer doesn't start then check 'Removable Devices & Media' in the preferences/admin menu

Cheers

### Post by AndyCooll on 2007-10-31
And to continue on from what zzztownsend advises, Sound Juicer doesn't give you the option to rip to mp3 by default. However you can add that functionality by following this [HOWTO: Rip mp3 using Sound Juicer]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=295698")
