### Post by Henry Rayker on 2006-10-23
Having two browsers installed shouldn't slow the system down. I suppose if you ran both simultaneously, it would probably slow you down (because having a different browser would, in most cases, be more system-intensive than just opening a new tab).

So long as you use one at a time, though, you should be just fine.
