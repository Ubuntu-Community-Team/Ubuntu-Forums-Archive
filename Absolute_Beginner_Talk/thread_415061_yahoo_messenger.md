### Post by compiledkernel on 2007-04-20
1. the gaim messenger (now called Pidgin) that comes default with every ubuntu install can be set to use a Yahoo messaging account. Here is its FAQ ([http://www.pidgin.im/faq.php](http://www.pidgin.im/faq.php)) , should be able to setup yahoo that way. 

2. If your a KDE user, you can use Kopete in a similar way. Its Handbook is here -- [http://docs.kde.org/development/en/kdenetwork/kopete/](http://docs.kde.org/development/en/kdenetwork/kopete/)

3. You can go out to [http://messenger.yahoo.com](http://messenger.yahoo.com) , download the debian file for yahoo (if you want to use the official yahoo versioning supplied by yahoo -- though it isnt that featureful and one of the above two is more likely to be useful to you) and double click on it once it saves to your desktop.

4. If you want gyachi as the above poster recommends, grab its deb file here -- [http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/gyachi/gyachi_1.0.5-1_edgy_i386.deb?download](http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/gyachi/gyachi_1.0.5-1_edgy_i386.deb?download) , and double click on the package once its saved to your desktop.
