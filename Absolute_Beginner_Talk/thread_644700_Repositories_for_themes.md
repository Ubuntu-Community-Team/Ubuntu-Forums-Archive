### Post by Lord_Dicranius on 2007-12-20
There's a few GTK type themes in the repos:  blubuntu, peace, tropic, and some others I think.

Regarding making the panels transparent...
- Right click on a panel, select "properties"
- On the background tab, click on "solid color", then slide the style bar from transparent to opaque to find the right setting for you.
