So you don't know how to reconfigure the icon back to its original state?

EDIT:  I just figured it out.

Click on the network configuration icon.  Click on "Manual Configuration".  Click on the network you're connected to, and choose "Properties".  Then click to enable "roaming mode".  That does it.
