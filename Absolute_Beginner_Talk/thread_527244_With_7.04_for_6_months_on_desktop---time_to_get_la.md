The LiveCD contains gparted.  Just let the installer wipe the entire hard disc.  I recommend partitioning the hdd into 30GB for Ubuntu and the rest for whatever you want.

Leave the 30GB as free space and format the rest as EXT3.  Tell the installer to use the largest continuous free space.  Don't forget to hit apply after you make your changes.
