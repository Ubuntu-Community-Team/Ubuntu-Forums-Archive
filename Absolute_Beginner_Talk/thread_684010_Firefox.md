### Post by barrack on 2008-02-01
I think it should be clear on what the differences are between Firefix and IE just by using them. At work I use a Windows computer it has IE preinstalled. Using it is painfully slow. Firefox just runs faster. 

One of my favorite default features of firefox is the ability to highlight text and search Google on that text in a new tab.

For example, I get an email about a party and it gives me an address (you can try this):

7400 Sand Point Way NE
Seattle, WA 98115

I can just highlight it, right click and a new tab will appear with a map as a search result!

Or if I'm reading an article and there's a term I don't understand, I can look it up in the same fashion.

IE6 didn't offer this. I can't say the same of IE7, as I have used it but once on a friend's computer.
