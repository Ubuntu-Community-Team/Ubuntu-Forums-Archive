### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-06-15
Well, I use Magnatune.com and Jamendo.com (be warned that those are creative commons only, but they still have some great artists there) and I've heard great things about emusic.  You may be able to get very limited itunes support (I've heard that people have made programs to work with itunes, but they break easily when apple changes things).  I've also heard that amazon was doing (or going to be doing) the music downloading stuff, but I don't know if they require a non-linux client to do so.  Also check out last.fm if your interested in radio.
