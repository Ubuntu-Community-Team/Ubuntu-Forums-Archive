### Post by jakupl on 2008-04-11
when you pop in the live cd. and press "install" you will get some options. If you simply press "guided install... something" (I don't really remember, but I think it is marked by default). it will install ubuntu on a separate partition. Without touching Windows Vista.

However, though unlikely, Partitioning can always damage your harddisk.. you never know. It is always a good idea to backup your computer before installing.
But don't be afraid.
