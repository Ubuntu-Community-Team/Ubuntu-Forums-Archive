### Post by p_quarles on 2007-06-21
I've had some applications crash on me occasionally, but I haven't seen what you're describing on a regular basis. 

One thing you could try, though, is to right-click on one of your panels, click "Add to Panel," and then select the "Force Quit" applet. When an application crashes, you can just click on the applet, the click the unresponsive window and it'll go away. 

Another option is to try a different window manager.
