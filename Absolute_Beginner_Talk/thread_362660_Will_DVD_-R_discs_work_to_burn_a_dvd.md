### Post by shirilover on 2007-02-16
> **maximo said:**
>  It says please input a supported disc with at least 4582mb free. It should have right over 4700 free.

I expect that this is your problem.
While the media may say 4700 MB (here mega = 10^6), what you really can use is 4482 MB (here MB = 2^20).

So, try reducing the size of your compilation to something under 4482 MB.

Just a side note:
Media and hard drive manufacturers report the size in 10^x bytes. so, your single layer DVD can hold 4.7 x 10^9 bytes (4.7 GB).
Your computer uses size in 2^y bytes. So, your computer see the size of your blank DVD as 4.37 x 2^30 bytes (4.37 GiB, note the difference).
