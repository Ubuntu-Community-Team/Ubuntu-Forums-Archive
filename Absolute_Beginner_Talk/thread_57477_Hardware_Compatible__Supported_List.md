### Post by aysiu on 2005-08-31
I would just like to say that my eMachines T3065 computer is extremely compatible with Linux in general, but has worked especially well with Ubuntu. So if people are looking for a Linux-compatible computer, add this one to the list.

The CD-R/DVD-ROM drives are recognized and fully functional. Even the 8-in-one media card reader is fully functional. The video and sound cards work great. I had to do very little hardware configuring with this box. All the USB ports work just fine for automounting (external hard drive, external floppy drive, MP3 player, digital camera, iPod).

The only "problem" I had was with my ViewSonic VG150 monitor, whose HorizSync and VertRefresh rates Ubuntu didn't recognize, but once I entered those in the /etc/X11/xorg.conf file, everything was fine.

Anyone else have computer model #s they've had particularly good Ubuntu/Linux experiences with?
