### Post by ComplexNumber on 2007-01-24
1) find out where the application is that you want to make a launcher for (chances are, it will be in /usr/bin or /usr/local/bin)
2) go to your menu botton in the top left of your screen, right click, and click on 'edit menu'. 
3) add the details (ie the name of the application, any comments). add the icon. the icons are (mostly) stored  in /usr/share/pixmaps
