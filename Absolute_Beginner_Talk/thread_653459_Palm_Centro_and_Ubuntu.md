### Post by clayt0njknight on 2007-12-30
Beautiful thing about Palms, at least newer ones I've toyed with in Linux, you can mount the memory card just like a USB Thumbdrive and just drag and drop the PRC and PDB files to it.  Worst case scenario if you can't mount it in such manner or use JPilot to install files for you, memory card and card reader work wonders.
