### Post by CatKiller on 2006-09-21
> **alienexplorers said:**
> Where is the data for the weather icon on the panel stored.  My town of Hershey Pennsylvania is not listed and I would like to add it or at least add a town nearby.

As b_martinez said, right-click on the applet and select Preferences and then the Location tab. Harrisburg International Airport is listed, which is apparently "a short 15 minute drive" from Hershey, according to your tourist information people. Apparently it's 9 degrees celcius there at the moment, and mostly sunny today :p 

The reason international airports are listed is because they have weather stations. Other weather stations are listed too, of course. The long-term solution to getting your town in the list is to start up a weather station in your town :D Otherwise, I suppose you could edit the list so that one of the nearby weather stations were **called** Hershey, but I'm not sure exactly what you'd change, or what you'd achieve.
