### Post by reckless2k2 on 2007-06-08
yes you can go from 15 to 17 if you wait around for the next kernel fix. these are just bug fixes though and sometimes bug fixes might  cause bugs in other places. to track kernel progress, you should really consult kernel.org. then you can get a better picture of the true current kernel and bug fixes and or updates to older kernels.
