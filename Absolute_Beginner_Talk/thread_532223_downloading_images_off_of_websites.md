### Post by horned0wl on 2007-08-22
Mozilla Firefox has a host of _extensions_, between 50-75 items, to help you with downloading, image capture and management, and video capture and management.  Go there, and select what's right for you.

And, if you like to do video capture on the fly, once you've downloaded a video, then VideoLAN's VLC media player has both stop-action and on the fly video capture capabilities. The vid-cap pictures save to your chosen directory as .png files.

Both are free.
