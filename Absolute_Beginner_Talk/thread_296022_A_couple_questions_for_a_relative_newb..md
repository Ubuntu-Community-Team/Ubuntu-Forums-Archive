### Post by randomi on 2006-11-09
[QUOTE=sunexplodes]a) is there a good equivalent for Macromedia Dreamweaver? I've tried NVU and I don't like it.[/QUOTE]

Try BlueFish or JEdit. Both are pretty close to Dreamweaver from what I remember. It has been a few years since I've used Dreamweaver, but both BlueFish and JEdit are powerful little apps.

[QUOTE=sunexplodes]b) What is the best torrent app for linux? I used to use uTorrent in windows, and bittornado and azerus are weak comparitively.[/QUOTE]

Personally I use gnome-btdownload. It's the original bit torrent client, so it opens a new window for each torrent downloading/uploading. I just throw the windows on another desktop and use that. It works pretty well and has had better speeds then BitTornado and Azureus on my computer.
