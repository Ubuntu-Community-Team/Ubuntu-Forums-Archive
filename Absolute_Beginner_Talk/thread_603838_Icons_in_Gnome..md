### Post by kyphi on 2007-11-05
You can change any icon you want by right clicking the icon you wish to change and then selecting Properties from the drop down menu.

Then click on the icon that is attached to the programme and a screen will show with icons.

There are several icon files in Ubuntu and you can also start your own collection in a folder in your Home directory.

Remember that in Ubuntu you can change the appearance of anything to suit your personal taste and thereby make it uniquely your personal operating system.
