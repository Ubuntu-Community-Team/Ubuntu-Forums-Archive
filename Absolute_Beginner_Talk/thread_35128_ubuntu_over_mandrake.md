### Post by dave9191 on 2005-05-17
If you want to get rid of mandrake and everything thats on your computer, then you can tell the ubuntu installer to clear the disk for you and sort everything out. But make sure that you have your files backed up. The other option is to install it along side mandrake. In which case you want to make sure that there is some unpartioned space on the hard drive and tell the ubuntu installer to use that instead. 

To start the ubuntu installer, put the ubuntu CD into your drive and turn your compueter on to boot from the CD.
