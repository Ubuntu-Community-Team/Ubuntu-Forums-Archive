### Post by cincinnatus13 on 2008-02-09
I went through this same thing just yesterday. I assume this is for Gutsy (Ubuntu 7.10). What you need to do is slide it to the amount you want for your OLD partition. This means slide it to the space you want for your Windows partition. I would also recommend a 10GB partition for Ubuntu as it is more than enough. Ie. if you want 10Gb for Ubuntu and 50Gb for Windows, slide it to 50GB. Windows will use that 50GB and Ubuntu will use the free space (10GB in your case.)
I hope I explained it well enough.
