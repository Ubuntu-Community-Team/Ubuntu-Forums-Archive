### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-12-03
I usually just make a folder in my home directory called wallpaper.  I put all the pictures I use for backgrounds in there.  Then, right click your desktop and go to add wallpaper.  Navigate to your folder and select the one you want.  As long as you don't move this picture around, you can always go back and choose it again.
