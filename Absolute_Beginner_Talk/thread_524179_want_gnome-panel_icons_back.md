### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-08-12
Hi. It looks like you're missing the Notification Area; right-click in an empty space in the top panel, select "Add to Panel", scroll to the bottom and choose "Notification Area". Afterwards, you can move it to its original location by right-clicking on it and selecting "Move", and once it's in the place you want, right-click again and select "Lock To Panel".
