### Post by lisati on 2007-07-05
If you can play "OGG" files then you might want to check out "Thoggen DVD Ripper". You can find out if it's installed on your system by going to Applications->Add Remove programs, typing in "DVD RIP" (without the quotes) in the search box, make sure that "show" is set to "all available applications" 

Hope this helps

### Post by moredhel on 2007-07-05
Unless you use rockbox, iPods won't play OGGs. stupid firmware.

What I would do is use acidrip to rip the dvd to an avi, then use winff to convert to an mp4 with the right dimensions (it has a preset).

winff might be able to go direct from the cd, i dunno. You can find a .deb for winff on [www.getdeb.net](www.getdeb.net) ;)
