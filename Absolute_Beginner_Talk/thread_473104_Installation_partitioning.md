### Post by Morsolo on 2007-06-13
When you install, it has 3 options for 'partitioning'...

The 1st one (if you use windows) will take SOME space off your C drive...
The 2nd one will use your whole C drive and overwrite any Windows you have on it (re-format)...
The 3rd one, will let you choose how to do all this, but I find it doesn't work well...

I used the first one...
When you use it, it tells you how much you want to SHRINK your C drive to. Not the size of the new partition...

But, if you knew that, then the answer to your question is:
It creates A NEW partition and formats ONLY the new one! You keep your data, unless you did something wrong...
