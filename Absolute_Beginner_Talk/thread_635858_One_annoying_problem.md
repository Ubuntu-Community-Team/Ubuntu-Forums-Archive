### Post by HereInOz on 2007-12-09
If the problem is fixed with a reboot, but then occurs again after a time, then the first thing that I would suspect, and replace (it is also the cheapest to try first) is the network adaptor.

I have had numerous boards of late (manufacturer shall remain nameless) where we have had inexplicable flakiness with the network connection, only to solve it with a cheap network card.
