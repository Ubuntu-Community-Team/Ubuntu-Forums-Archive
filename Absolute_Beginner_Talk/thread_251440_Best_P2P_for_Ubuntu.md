### Post by toasterofirony on 2006-09-05
I only really do torrenting (and then only legal stuff!), but I'd put a vote in for kTorrent for it's friendly interface and built-in search engines for places like isohunt, mininova, torrentreactor et al.

Torrenting works on the idea of sharing packets of information from a file or group of files between a network of people who have parts of or the entire file or files. You have to open or save and then run a .torrent file in your app of choice. This is a tracker that effectively makes you part of the group looking for this specific file or files. The tracker allows you to see how many people are in the group and how many are "leechers" (people without the entire file who are mainly downloading from others) and "seeders" (people who have the entire file and are only uploading).

It is considered good-form when torrenting to leave the torrent running even after you have the finished downloading the file, to acheive a download: upload ratio of 1:1.
