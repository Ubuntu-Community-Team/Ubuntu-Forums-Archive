### Post by raijinsetsu on 2007-08-21
Ok... from the konsole, you type "sudo kate /etc/fstab"
You should see an entry for this partition that looks like:

"/dev/hda1 /media/windows ntfs defaults 2 0"

the "/media/windows" is the mount-point for the parition.
You need to insert ",users" after defaults. Don't change anything else.

Hope this helps.
