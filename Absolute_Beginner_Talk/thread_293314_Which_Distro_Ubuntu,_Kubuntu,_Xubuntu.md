### Post by aysiu on 2006-11-05
Xubuntu will always be faster than Ubuntu and Kubuntu.

Whether Ubuntu or Kubuntu is faster than the other is up for debate. You'll get people swearing Ubuntu is faster, and others swearing Kubuntu is faster, and still others swearing they can't tell the difference. There are also different tweaks or versions of KDE and Gnome that affect performance.

I think you should use either, both, or neither. Just play around! I use IceWM, XFCE, KDE, and Gnome. There's no reason to settle for one!
