### Post by maniacmusician on 2007-03-14
Hi,

Device for peripherals is still shaky at the moment; this is mostly due to manufacturers not wanting to provide drivers. So, Linux developers have to do clean-room reverse engineering, which takes a lot of time and effort; basically, more stuff is on its way to being supported, but it's slow work.

I researched a little and came up with some sources. You can try them out if you wish. There is a wiki on setting up palm devices here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PalmDeviceSetup](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PalmDeviceSetup)

If that doesn't work, perhaps try one of these other pages:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PalmZire31HowTo?action=fullsearch&context=180&value=Palm&titlesearch=Titles](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PalmZire31HowTo?action=fullsearch&context=180&value=Palm&titlesearch=Titles)

Or do some more research on your own. It's a bummer that your experience on Ubuntu has been bad thus far, but I hope  you can pull through and get along with it.
