### Post by ReaderRat on 2007-08-01
Amazing!!! This just happened to me.  I have an AMD 5600+ X2 processor on an **ASUS** motherboard with **OCZ** Plat. memory (2 Gigs). I tried everything suggested in the forums and nothing would work. 
 It was the RAM. It was not compatible with the ASUS board. All that work trying to install, and all I had to do was use one of the vendors in the Qualified Vendors List in the ASUS User Manual and buy compatible RAM.
 The mobo is a M2A-VM and the BAD RAM was OCZ dual-channel DDR2 667MHz.

Let me also tell you that you may have a problem with your EVGA card if the above does not apply to you.
