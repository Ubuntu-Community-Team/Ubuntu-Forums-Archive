### Post by kpkeerthi on 2008-02-04
Open a terminal, and run **alsamixer**. Make sure the sliders are all raised enough and the channels are not muted (MM). Use 'M' key to toggle mute (MM - Muted, OO - Turned on). Press 'Esc' to quit alsamixer.
