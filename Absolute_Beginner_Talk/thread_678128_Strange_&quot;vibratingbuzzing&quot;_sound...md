### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-01-25
Could be a fan that isn't quite in line and is just rubbing - you'd have to stick your head back there and look.  This happens when I boot my server computer, but disappears after awhile.  A little shake tends to help, too.  If you hear anything like scratching, that could be your hard drive failing which would be a serious problem.
The only moving parts in your computer should be fans, the hard drive, and a CD in the drive.

### Post by Cypher on 2008-01-25
A failing fan usually will be heard as a fluctuating noise, the speed will increase and decrease at some interval..if one of the ball bearings is shot then potentially it would be making that noise, but then I would think it'd make it all the time.

Open up the case, start it up and look around to see if you can "hear" any noise and the next time it does make the noise, open the case (if not already open) and locate it..
