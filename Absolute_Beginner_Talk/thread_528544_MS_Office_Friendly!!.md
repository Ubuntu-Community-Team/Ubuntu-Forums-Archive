### Post by aysiu on 2007-08-18
OpenOffice documents saved with the .doc extension will work just fine with MS Office. MS Office documents saved with a .doc extension may not work 100% well with OpenOffice, though--the formatting may be slightly off.

### Post by HermanAB on 2007-08-18
The important thing is to install compatible fonts.  Most people who complain about compatibility with MS Office neglect that simple step.  You can either get the Freedom Fonts from RedHat, or install the TrueType Fonts from Microsoft.  

Without the same size and shape fonts, documents will never convert properly - kinda obvious really!
