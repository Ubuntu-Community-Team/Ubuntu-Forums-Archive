### Post by Happy_Man on 2007-04-29
1. 7.04 is out of beta and ready to go! 

2. The alternate CD is equivalent to the server install off of [http://ubuntu.com/download](http://ubuntu.com/download)

3. There's a way, but 10 GB for Ubuntu is awfully small...you won't be able to do much with it. Try putting all your media on an external drive, and use that freed space for Ubuntu. 

4. You can use a 32 bit kernel with an x64 processor architecture, and in fact, I'd recommend it because there is far more stuff available in the repos for 32bit kernel than .64 bit kernel. 

Post back with any questions, and we'd be glad to help. ;)
