### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-05-28
OK, one nice thing about Linux, is that there is always more than one way to "skin a cat".

Go to [COLOR="Blue"]System>Administration>Login Window[/COLOR]

If it doesn't appear as a menu item, you may need to go into [COLOR="Blue"]System>Preferences>Menu Layout[/COLOR] first and then when the window opens, click on [COLOR="Blue"]Administration[/COLOR] in the left pane.

Then make sure that there is a tick in front of Login Window.

Once that is sorted, just go into Login Window and add the login screen that you have downloaded.

If you go to the gnome art website: [http://art.gnome.org/](http://art.gnome.org/) there are heaps of things to download.

Regards,
Roger :cool:
