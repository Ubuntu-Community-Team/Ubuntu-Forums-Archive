### Post by irish_flu on 2007-02-08
Yes and no.  6.06 (Dapper) is a release called "LTS" (for long-term support) and will get security updates and such for a much longer period of time into the future than Edgy (6.10) will.

Edgy definitely carries more "bleeding-edge" stuff (which, in theory, contain newer and more features), but isn't considered as stable as Dapper (though I've personally had no problems with it).

As far as worrying about updates and such: as with any operating system, you will have updates to worry about no matter which version you install.
