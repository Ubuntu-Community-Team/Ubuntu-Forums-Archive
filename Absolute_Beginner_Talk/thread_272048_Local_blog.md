### Post by Marsolin on 2006-10-06
[Here]("http://linuxappfinder.com/development/web/blogs") are a few good blogging apps.  One might work for you.

The Wiki options may be better. Click [here]("http://linuxappfinder.com/development/web/wikis") for a list. [Wiki on a Stick]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/stickwiki"), [doxWiki]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/doxwiki"), and [TiddlyWiki]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/tiddlywiki") are probably more of what you are looking for. Also note that if you click on one of those three there is an article from Linux.com listed in the Web Links section of the right sidebar that discusses them.
