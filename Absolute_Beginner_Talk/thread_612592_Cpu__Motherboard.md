### Post by Bartender on 2007-11-16
> **jpsimm said:**
> YI've found that on dial-up faster hardware helps a great deal.Thanks

Isn't that odd?  I can't prove what you say, but strongly suspect it's so.  A dial-up connection involves such a small amount of data processing that you'd think any half-decent PC could handle it with ease.  But that doesn't appear to be so.

I know for sure that PC's with ancient onboard video or even discrete cards have a heck of a time with websites that carry a lot of graphics.  Had an old PII that took several minutes to load a graphics-intensive webpage.  Put in an old AGP card with 32MB of memory and the same webpage loaded   much faster.
