### Post by Jerry N on 2007-07-10
DVD has capacity of about 4.7gb (unless it is dual layer) while CD-RW has a capacity less than 700mb.  You could convert your DVD video to video CD but it would look crappy.

### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-07-11
K9copy apparantly is able to convert DVD movies to DivX - tell it to restrict the file size to 700 megabytes, then you can burn it to CD and use it in any DVD player that can play DivX.

DVD burners are very cheap these days. If you have a spare drive bay or can take your existing drive out to replace it with a DVD burner, I'd really recommend that.
