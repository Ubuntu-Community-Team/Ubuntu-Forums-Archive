### Post by Raptor45 on 2007-07-29
That will install the 32 bit version of firefox I believe. If you're fine with that go ahead. Personally I prefer using the real 64bit firefox, and just using the pluginwrapper for flash. Why bother with a 64 bit OS only to use 32 bit software? But regardless, its your choice... and in the end you probably wont notice a difference

About automatix, theres a fair number of horror stories out there about broken systems and random crazy issues. Theres also a fair number of people who swear by it. Your choice. Personally, I dislike it because I think it breaks the ubuntu "philosophy" of the package manager to some extent, and I don't like random things installing themselves. Plus there is also a certain amount of knowledge and experience you get for doing it the "real" way. But again, its your choice.
