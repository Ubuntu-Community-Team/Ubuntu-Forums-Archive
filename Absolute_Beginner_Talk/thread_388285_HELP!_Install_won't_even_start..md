### Post by lawentzel on 2007-03-19
If all else fails, one of the guys who I work with had a similar problem.  He ended up running a boot & nuke disk.  For some reason, Linux when it installed over a previous version didn't clear everything out of the hard drive.  So, he kept losing drive space, and ultimately had things acting a little strange.  If you Google "boot and nuke" or "boot and nuke CD" you will find what you need to make sure your hard drive is wiped clean for a new load.  Just a suggestion.
