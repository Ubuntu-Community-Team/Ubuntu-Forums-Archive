### Post by shearn89 on 2007-08-04
If you find the folder where the theme is stored (either /usr/share/pixmaps/themes [i think] or ~/.themes), and open the folder of the relevant theme, you can replace all the pictures (which are the various patterns for titlebars etc) with your own.
