### Post by gitano1 on 2006-12-04
Adewale, you might want to keep in mind that Seagate and Maxtor are now the same company. If you buy either name you will be buying the same product. Hard drive failure is not terribly common in any of the major brands, but they do occasionally fail, usually in the first year, sometimes in the first month. I have drives that are 5 years old and still running. I have generally chosen Western Digital or IBM drives. However, I just replaced a 40 GB Western Digital drive in a friend's Dell computer. It was making funny noises, so don't assume that if you have a bad experience with one of the major brands that it is indicative of that brand. Maxtor has had a lousy reputation among many of my friends and associates, so you really can't judge by name.
