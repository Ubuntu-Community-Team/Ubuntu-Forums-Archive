### Post by flatwombat on 2007-09-10
Why pick only one?  I'd dual-boot, which most users initially do, and then just use Windows for games and use Ubuntu for work, surfing, etc.  Since you won't be going online much with Windows and will be checking your email with Ubuntu, your computer will be a LOT safer.

In time, you may find that you are seldom using Windows and may want to delete it from your machine.  There's also those game consoles instead of just using a computer for something it really wasn't designed for.
