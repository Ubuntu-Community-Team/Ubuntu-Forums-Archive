### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-04-30
You don't have to manually add pictures to that, put the picture you want your desktop as in the home folder of your account, then just go:

System > Preferences > Desktop Background.

Push "add wallpaper" and navigate to the picture, then select it and push apply. Thats the easy way :). 

Also a note, to the left of the add button you can configure how it is used, in the drop down.

Enjoy.
