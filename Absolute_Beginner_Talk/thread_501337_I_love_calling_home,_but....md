### Post by p_quarles on 2007-07-15
Go to System >> Admin >> Software Sources. There's a dropdown menu there that allows you to choose servers from different parts of the world. 

That said, you might not get any big boost in speed. The repositories are sometimes just slow, especially when there's a big update.
