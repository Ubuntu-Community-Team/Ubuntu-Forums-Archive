### Post by euler_fan on 2007-03-25
I believe what you are looking for is ntfs-3g. I don't use it personally, and the last time I heard it was still in beta and a bit sketchy for writing to the windows partition but could read just fine.

There are also drivers available to let you read and write from windows to your ubuntu partitions. I would suggest installing both and then reading from ntfs while in ubuntu and both reading and writing to the ubuntu side while in windows.

Is there anyone out there with more experience in this who could provide a second opinion? This is all from reading other posts on the same subject.
