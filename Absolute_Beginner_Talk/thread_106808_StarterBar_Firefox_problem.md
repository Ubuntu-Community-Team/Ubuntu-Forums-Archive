### Post by matthew on 2005-12-21
While I think it's cool that you are defaulting to my Alma Mater I feel your pain. Try this. Right click on the firefox starter in the starterbar and select "edit starter." Look and see what the command is that is being used. I seem to recall a similar problem that I had when mine had the command "firefox %u" and I removed the %u and haven't had any further problems. (Only it was sending me to the home of the Minnesota Golden Gophers...) :)
