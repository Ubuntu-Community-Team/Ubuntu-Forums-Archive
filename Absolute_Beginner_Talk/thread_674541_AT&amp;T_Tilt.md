### Post by snackbarber on 2008-06-28
Hey guys.  I too have a Tilt and am working on getting it to work with Ubuntu(Gutsy in my case).  First let me point you to [http://www.synce.org/]("http://www.synce.org/")  SynCe is a project aiming to replace ActiveStink for Linux users.  Check it out.  So far I am able to do an "ls" and view the contents of my Tilt.  I just started trying to figure it out though.  I am sure that copying files and moving files to/from my Ubuntu laptop and my Tilt is possible.  From the looks of it bluetooth syncing and syncing the email client on the Tilt with Evolution are both possible as well.  I do hope that this project gets integrated into the default Ubuntu install at some point.  Does anyone know if Hardy has any changes that affect the syncing of Windows Mobile devices?
