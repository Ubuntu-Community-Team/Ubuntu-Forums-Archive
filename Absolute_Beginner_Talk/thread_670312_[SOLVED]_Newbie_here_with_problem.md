### Post by toddward on 2008-01-17
If you don't have any information that'll be lost, the best bet to get your computer up and running might be to press and hold the power button for 4 seconds.  This will shut the computer down hard without actually running through a shutdown process.

Once you've started backup, disable the screen saver from running.  That way, if you want to lock the computer you can still log out...but you'll never start up the screen saver.

Once you have this work-around in place, research on your own computer (and do your school work) why the screen saver is making everything inactive.
