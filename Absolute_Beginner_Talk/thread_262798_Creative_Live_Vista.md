### Post by NiksaVel on 2006-09-22
since I am also recently converted from win, here are some basic tips:

in ubuntu you are a basic user... to execute a terminal command with "root" permissions (equivalent of admin group in win) you need to add a "sudo" in front of your command...

i.e.
instead of mkdir whatever, do a 'sudo mkdir whatever'....


hope that helps a bit...
