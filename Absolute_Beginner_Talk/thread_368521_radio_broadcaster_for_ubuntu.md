### Post by go2dell on 2007-02-23
No idea how involved you want to get with this, but here's a [wiki page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DJPlayWithShoutcastHowto") describing how to use one solution for streaming.

There's another [wiki page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MultimediaApplications") that explains many of the popular multimedia apps available in Ubuntu that will give you a good overview of what's available in this area.

While we're discussing streaming, remember that MP3 is a [restricted format]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats") in Ubuntu, so you will need to take additional steps if you're streaming existing MP3 content.  If it's going to be live streaming or new content, then you can safely use free formats instead.

I would also recommend searching the forums for "shoutcast."  You will probably find lots of information on the pros and cons of whatever solution you're looking at.

Good luck!
