### Post by eternicode on 2008-01-04
As a general rule, you should ask the original owner's permission before publicly posting a derivation.  If there's no contact information listed on the owner's site, you could probably get by with posting it with your contact information (email should be more than enough) and a line saying to contact you in case of issues.

Of course, I'm no expert on (or even signifcantly knowledgeable of) intellectual copyright laws, and I'm sure such laws vary from country to country.  If you want to be perfectly safe, either get the owner's permission before posting it, or don't post it.
