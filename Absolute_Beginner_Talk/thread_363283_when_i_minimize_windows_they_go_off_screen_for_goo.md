### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-02-16
To get your windows back, you can use alt-tab.

You have a few options otherwise.

Right click on your remaining panel.  Go to 'New Panel' to make a new one (if you actually want another one)
Then, right click on the panel you want to add your window list back onto and go to 'Add to Panel'
You can add either a "window list" or a "window selector"
Window list is what you had before, and a window selector is a button that shows your windows when you click on it.

Does this help?
