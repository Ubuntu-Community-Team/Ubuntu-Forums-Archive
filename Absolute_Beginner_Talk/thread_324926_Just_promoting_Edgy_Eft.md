### Post by Sef on 2006-12-24
> which dist is edgy? I'm looking to build a machine for my wife and wanted to try Ubuntu. Is Edgy the current version?

Dapper Drake 6.06 is the stable version.   That means that the packages tend to be older, but it is more likely to work on your machine without problems.  Edgy Eft is the unstable version.  That means the packages are newer and are more likely than Dapper to not work correctly.

1) If you are just starting out, Dapper is a better way to go.

1a) Longer support than Edgy: 3 years vs 18 months (June 2009 vs April 2008)

1b) You can always upgrade to Edgy.

2) Edgy is better if you want or need the latest software.

2a) Be prepared for possible problems that may not exist in Dapper.

2b) You can always install Dapper over Edgy if the latter does not work out.
