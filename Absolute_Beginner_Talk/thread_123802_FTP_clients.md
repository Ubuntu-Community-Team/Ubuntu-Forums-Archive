### Post by endersshadow on 2006-01-31
[QUOTE=NobleSavage]Ok... Duh.. you just answered the 2 qustions I just asked. :mrgreen:[/QUOTE]

Not only did he answer it, but there's also a link to [the Linux port which is still in development](http://filezilla-project.org/nightly.php).

You can even do this:

```
wget http://filezilla-project.org/nightlies/2006-01-31/FileZilla_3_i386-linux-bin.tar.bz2
tar xjf FileZilla_3_i386-linux-bin.tar.bz2
filezilla3/bin/filezilla
```

And voila. You have Filezilla as a native Linux port staring you in the face.
