### Post by Scorpuk on 2006-11-29
majority of bios's will tell you which key to press to go into setup, but maybe not long enough for you to see it.


Generally if you press the del / delete key while its booting up you will get into the bios.


Another key that is used is F2, or ctrl + F2.


If you only get a graphic boot up screen it may say something at the bottom of it.
