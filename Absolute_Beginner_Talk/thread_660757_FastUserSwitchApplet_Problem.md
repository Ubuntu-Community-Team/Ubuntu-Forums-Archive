### Post by Chriswaterguy on 2008-01-08
Not to answer your original question, but:

> i need Ubuntu installed on this machine...

Do you need *Ubuntu* installed, or *Linux*? It can be really hard to get a particular distro working on a particular machine, and the easiest way might be just to try a different one. 

I had a series of serious and minor problems with Ubuntu, even after using it for months, and couldn't get answers (from here or elsewhere), and eventually tried different distros. Things have been much better for me (I'm now using the Gnome version of Mandriva - not perfect, but it's working pretty well).

To help other newbies avoid the same problem I started a wiki page, [[COLOR="Blue"]How to choose a Linux distro[/COLOR]]("http://www.appropedia.org/How_to_choose_a_Linux_distro"). Hope that helps someone.
