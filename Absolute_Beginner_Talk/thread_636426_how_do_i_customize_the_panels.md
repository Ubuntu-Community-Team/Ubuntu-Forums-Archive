### Post by xhie on 2007-12-10
Go into  system-preferences-advanced desktop effects   and make sure window decoration is disabled, or in the very least make sure it does not have "emerald" in its command field. That is how you get compiz to turn "on" emerald, make sure its not there to turn it "off".

### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-12-10
In that command field xhie speaks of,
emerald --replace                   will give you emerald + compiz
gtk-window-decorator --replace      will give you metacity themes + compiz
