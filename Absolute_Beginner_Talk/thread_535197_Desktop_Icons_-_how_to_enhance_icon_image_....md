SOLVED.  You pointed me into the right direction.  I stated a Nautilus window by going to Places->Desktop.  Then I clicked on Edit->Preferences and selected Preview tab.  For "Show thumbnails", I changed it from "Local Files Only" to "Always".  Now the icons for my MPEG   files show the first frame of the video, and my JPG files show the image of the picture itself.
