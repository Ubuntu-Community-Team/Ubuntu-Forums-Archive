### Post by aysiu on 2007-11-16
> **sc2 said:**
> Would it be possible for you to explain to me the difference between Metacity and GTK2 etc..
Metacity is the window manager for Gnome. GTK is the toolkit for the widgets in Gnome and Xfce.

Metacity themes tend to be the window borders, and GTK themes tend to be the controls.
