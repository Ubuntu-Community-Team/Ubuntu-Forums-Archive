### Post by Malibu Illusion on 2007-06-20
To be honest, your question is very vague; I'm not entirely sure what you're asking for here.

Briefly, to connect to networks;:
/server network.address.com

To join channels:
/join #channel

To view a list of channels on a network:
/list

You can automatically set networks and channels to reconnect for you at startup in ChatZilla by right clicking their respective tabs and selecting "Open at Startup."

You can edit preferences for specific channels, or globally, by clicking "ChatZilla" in the top left-hand corner and selecting "Preferences."  You'll see a list of networks on the right that you've previously joined.

If you need specific help with any particular feature of ChatZilla, or IRC in general, then you should go ahead and make it clear exactly what you're looking for.
