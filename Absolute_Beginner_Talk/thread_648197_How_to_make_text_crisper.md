### Post by ed-koala on 2007-12-23
Go to your System - Preferences - Appearance menu and click on the Fonts tab. Either change the fonts to your liking, or make sure Best Shapes is checked (that makes my fonts look good).

Let us know if that helped, and mark thread as solved if so :)
