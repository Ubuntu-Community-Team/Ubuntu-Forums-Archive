### Post by mcduck on 2006-06-08
[QUOTE=uzi09]judging by their site: [http://raphael.slinckx.net/deskbar/index.php#installation](http://raphael.slinckx.net/deskbar/index.php#installation)

looks  like you just need to right-click on the top panel > add new panel > then select deskbar![/QUOTE]
almost right, but you might want to use 'add to panel' instead of 'add new panel' ;) Then you'll get a window with lots of different applets you can have in your panels. Find the deskbar, and drag it to wherever you want it.

'new panel' would create a new, empty panel.

By the way, you might also want to install Beagle to get the most out of your Deskbar applet.
