### Post by Chaotic Thought on 2007-08-18
There's probably a complete list somewhere on the system, but if you want to figure out what a few menu items are doing, here's the trick I use: right click on a menu item, for example, System > Administration > Shared Folders, and choose "Add this launcher to desktop". Then, right-click on the desktop icon, choose Properties, then choose Launcher.  In that tab it shows details such as the command to execute.
