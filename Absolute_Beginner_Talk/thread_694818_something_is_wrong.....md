### Post by dca on 2008-02-12
When it freezes are you able to click on [ALT]-[CTRL]-[BACKSPACE] which kills 'X' and brings you to terminal mode?  Or is everything dead?  If it lets you in, you can login there and type 'cat /var/log/messages' without quotes, check the times and see if any errors correspond to that time referencing your issues.
