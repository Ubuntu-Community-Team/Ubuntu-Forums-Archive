Unless you're dual-booting, there's nothing special you have to do to reinstall Windows. Just pop the disk in and prepare to click a lot of buttons.

As for the wireless, the live disk generally gives you a really good indication of whether the hardware will work. If it doesn't work on that, you'll still be able to get it running, but prepare to have your patience tested. :)
