### Post by benplaut on 2006-02-24
In ubuntu, there is a 'package freeze' during development. This means that programs won't be upgraded, except for bug fixes, until the next release. Backports brings back new versions from the development release for those of us who want things a bit more up to date (hell, i run dapper).

Some people have problems with backports, but it works for most.

By the way, why still use Hoary?
