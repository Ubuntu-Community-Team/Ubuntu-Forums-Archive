### Post by Zeroangel on 2008-02-04
gdesklets is kinda cool -- it lets you have little desktop widgets like clocks, calendars, gauges and stuff like that.

Google Earth is nice if you like to look up things (like addresses) on a 3D map.

Lbreakout is a game where you hit a ball with a paddle and try to break blocks. Theres lots of powerups you can get that will do new things for your ball like split it into 3 balls and set it on fire and things like that.

And of course your favorite emulators like Zsnes and ePSXe if you like to play your old SNES or PSX games.
