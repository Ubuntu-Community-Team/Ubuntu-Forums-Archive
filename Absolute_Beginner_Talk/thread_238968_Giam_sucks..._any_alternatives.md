### Post by Marsolin on 2006-08-18
Kopete and Gaim are typically the top two multi-protocol IM clients.  ScatterChat is another that is based on Gaim and is meant to be secure.

aMSN and KMess are options for the MSN network.

Psi and Gossip are options for Jabber.

Alicq for ICQ.

[http://linuxappfinder.com/communications/instantmessaging](http://linuxappfinder.com/communications/instantmessaging)
