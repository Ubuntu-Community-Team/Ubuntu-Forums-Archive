Is there a way to get back to a running gnome session after I press Ctrl+Alt+F* ?

---

### Post by Bliepo32 on 2007-10-15
You can do this with Ctrl + Alt + F7. F1 to F6 are the terminals, F7 is the normal Gnome session, and F8 to F* are for other users.

### Post by Dr Small on 2007-10-15
F1 - F12 are all virtual terminals, but F8 - F12 are empty by default. You could run different sessions on them if you wanted to.
