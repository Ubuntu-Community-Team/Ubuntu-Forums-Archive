### Post by BirdZerk on 2007-10-31
I build my computers, so when there is a new version I don't have any problems with it, in fact I can actually see the improvements.  So for me Gutsy is better than Fiesty which is better than Edgy.  I never upgrade an OS,  whether it by MS, Apple or Linux.  From the time you install the OS, to the time you want to upgrade, enough programs have been installed and enough modifications have been made that when you upgrade I can almost guarantee that your OS will be hosed.  The best thing is to have a dual boot system with your current version and the next version and then it makes it easy to get access to all your data in both systems.
