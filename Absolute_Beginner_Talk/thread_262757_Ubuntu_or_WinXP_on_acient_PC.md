### Post by Average Joe on 2006-10-20
Ok, it has been a while since I started this thread, but since dmizer asked me, I'll tell you how it went.

The PC I had in mind turned out to have a Pentium II 450 MHz processor, and 448 (256+128+64) MB RAM. A bit better specs than I expected.

It has two hard drives, one of 20 GB, that has Windows XP on it, and one 5 GB, that was empty. So I installed Xubuntu on the second hard drive, and that runs fine.

One minor thing is that the language support is not that perfect, since some of the stuff is still shown in English. It's my parents computer, and they are not very familiar with the English language (and computers). They will probably not make use of Xubuntu (yet). But it is nice to have a good backup OS on that PC.
