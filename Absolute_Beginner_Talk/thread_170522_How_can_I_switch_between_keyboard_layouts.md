### Post by xp_newbie on 2006-06-14
[QUOTE=Isoss]Any suggestions ppl?[/QUOTE]

In case you are still at loss, here is how to do that:

 Alt-F2 > gnome-control-center
  > Keyboard
    > Layout options (tab)
      > Group Shift/Lock behavior
        > uncheck "Both Shift keys together change group"
        > check "Ctrl+Shift changes group"
        > check "Alt+Shift changes group"

This will give you keyboard switching behavior similar to what you are used to in Virus XP. ;) 

HTH
