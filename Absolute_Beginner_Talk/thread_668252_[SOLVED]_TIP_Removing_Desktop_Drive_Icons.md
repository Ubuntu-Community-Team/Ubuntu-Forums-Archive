I'm posting this to the forum for 2 reasons.  (1) So that some of you may find this helpful, and (2) so that I have a place to find this info again for myself in the future.

How to get rid of the icons of other drives from the desktop:

Press ALT+F2 and in the "Run Application" type the command "gconf-editor" and then click "Run"
Navigate on the left nav bar to apps -> Nautilus -> Desktop, and there you'll find an option called "volumes_visible".  Turn it off.

This will remove the icons to other drives off of your desktop, but of course you can still find them in the "Places" menu on top (or in /media)
