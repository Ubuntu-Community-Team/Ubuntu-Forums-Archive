### Post by Wolki on 2005-07-15
[QUOTE=elmagozizou]Hi...I dont know why my xmms frezzes when y try to open a file, I reinstall it three time now and doesnt fix...What can I do?[/QUOTE]

It's easy.

Start xmms without pening a file. Go Oprtions ->Preferences->Audio I/O Plugins and change Output Plugin from OSS to ESD/eSound. Now everything should work.


With other misbehaving media players, the fix will probably be the same.
