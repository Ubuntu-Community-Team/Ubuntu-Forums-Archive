### Post by Robfuscate on 2008-07-13
I use Linux Mint on my ACER4310. I tried a wide variety of otrhers on my way to choosing that one. Some simply would not install; others did not have the basics I required - Open Office / Mozilla / DVD Player / MP3 Media Player (Amarok for preference). Mint 4 Daryna had all of these, installed easily and let me use the basics from the very first.

Issues included things like the headphones not turning off the speakers and ... well, nothing else really. Installing a driver for the dial up modem was easy once I did some research and used scanmodem and I don't need/use the wireless access so can't comment on that.

In fact, I liked Mint so much that when Elyssa 5 came out, I bought the CD instead of waiting for a freebie on some Linux magazine or other.
