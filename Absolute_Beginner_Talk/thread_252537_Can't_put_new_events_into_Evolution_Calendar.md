### Post by BungaMan on 2006-11-12
I had the same problem.  Closed evolution and in /home/<your user name>/.evolution/calendar/local/system I've renamed calendar.ics to calendar.ics.backup
Restarted et voila, you can add stuff.
I upgraded from dapper to edgy.  Perhaps this caused a little mallfunction.  Did you upgrade?
btw, this method of course makes it that you can't access your appointments previously made.
