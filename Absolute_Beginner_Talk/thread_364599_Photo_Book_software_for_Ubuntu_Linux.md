### Post by flyingsolo on 2007-02-18
Take a look at Bubbleshare.  [http://www.bubbleshare.com/]("http://www.bubbleshare.com/")
It mainly is a photo sharing site but is linked to a company that prints photos in books, T-shirts, cups, magnets etc.  I use the photo sharing part of the site which is very well made but I haven't bought books of prints.  It looks as though they just grab your photos from the Bubbleshare server to make the book without you needing to download special software and so is independent of the user's OS.

Thanks for this guys, I will follow up both links.

For information Photo Books are where you want your pictures printed directly into a book (rather then sticking the pictures in an album with glue).

You need software to enable you to pick which picture you want where and how big (you can often have between 1 and 9 pictures per page, with or without text)

Have a look at the demo on [www.bonusprint.com](www.bonusprint.com) for an example.  Most photo printing companies seem to do books, but I've only found windows software so far.
