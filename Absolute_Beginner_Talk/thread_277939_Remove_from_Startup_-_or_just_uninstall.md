### Post by bruenig on 2006-10-15
System>Preferences>Sessions
From there go to the startup program commands and find the one that relates to it. It will probably be pretty obvious and either delete it or disable it or whatever.

Edit: I assumed that was in the notification area, if it isn't, that solution above won't work, you will have to remove it from the panel.
