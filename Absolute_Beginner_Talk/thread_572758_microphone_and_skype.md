### Post by laidback on 2007-10-10
When I did this I found that I first needed to get the sound recording in Sound Recorder to work first, then Sype worked. Review File>Open Volume Control>Recording. I used Voice Lossy as my Record As setting. 

Also be aware that you need to Save As the recording before playback, don't use Save, it's faulty.
