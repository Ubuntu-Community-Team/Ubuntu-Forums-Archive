If you want a still screen capture, the Gimp allows you to take a screen capture after a timed delay of your choosing.  Open the Gimp and click File -->Acquire -->Screen Shot.  Click the Whole Screen button and set the delay for 3 or 4 seconds.  Do your "Ctrl+Alt+Mouse to show/rotate the cube and the Gimp will take a screen shot after the delay you select.
