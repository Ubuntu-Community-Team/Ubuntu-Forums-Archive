### Post by talking Llama on 2008-06-12
I have an iRiver Clix 2, yeah they're fantastic MP3 players. Here's a topic in the forum, well it's more like a kind of review I gues... - [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=690745](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=690745)

Also my iRiver is only 4GB big and that's fantastic enough for me. Though I do have to change some songs over sometimes but it's damn easy with the iRiver in either Windows or Ubuntu. I use Amarok and have my iRiver set to MTP then just easily transfer the songs over! Easy peasy. Gnomad2 I've heard is quite good, I don't use it myself but I've heard it suggested (like in the post above this one) to be quite good, although I find Amarok sufficient.
Just for reference UMD also works perfectly fine but I prefer to use my iRiver in MTP mode.
Great MP3 player though! Check out that other thread as well, lots of information on iRiver Clix 2 there. :D
