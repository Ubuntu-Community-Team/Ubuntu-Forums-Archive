### Post by Al3xK3aton on 2007-08-08
From within vista, copy everything on your NTFS partition over to a second drive. Use this second drive in a separate computer, and devote your first computer to Ubuntu only. Your second PC is now your Vista PC. Buy yourself a good router and some cat5 cables, and you should be able to use it as a network drive instead of a separate partition. Much easier this way, trust me.
