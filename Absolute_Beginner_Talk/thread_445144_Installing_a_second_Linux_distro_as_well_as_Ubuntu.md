### Post by Kobalt on 2007-05-15
The first advice I'd give you is to [make a separate /home partition]("http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome"), it will make it very easy and confortable (and safe as well) to switch distros... Other than this, you can try any distro you like. You should just document yourself a little bit [about grub]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GrubHowto") though, in case you need to reinstall it.
