### Post by coastdweller on 2006-12-07
You can use a program called ERD commander I believe and boot and do a full drive recovery. If you haven't written over the part where that actual place on the drive it was stored then its fully recoverable.

You'll have to obviously get the program, then run a full recovery (Scans the entire disk looking for anything and copies it to a folder full of other folders).

Example"

JPGS
DOCS
MPG
etc

Inside of those folders you'll have to scan to find the files it has found.

More than likely you'll have good success.
