### Post by shamrock_uk on 2006-09-02
> **Uncle_John said:**
> As the title says my question is if ubuntu needs any antivirus program or spyware?

Basically, no.

If you come into contact with lots of Windows files (eg in email etc) that are distributed amongst your friends you may wish to have one around purely to protect the Windows users, but there's no danger of one of them infecting you.

Linux viruses are practically unknown in the wild, although there are about 100 in the lab. It's difficult for them to spread due to the fact that users aren't given root access. 

However, regular and thorough backups are still advisable as a precaution.
