### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-06-13
Hi. Open Mplayer, right-click on it and select "Preferences", go to the "Video" tab, and choose the **xv    X11/Xv** driver. As for the media player classic thing, there's probably a skin for that in [Mplayer's site]("http://www.mplayerhq.hu/design7/dload.html") (look for **WMP6**).
