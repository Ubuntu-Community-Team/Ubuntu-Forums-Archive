### Post by justleen on 2007-08-28
At the very frist boot menu of the live cd, there are several options at the bottom.
Select the one for the resolution/refresh rate, pick a low one (800x600 @ 60Hz orso) and try booting with that..

I had similair probs on my laptop, and was able to boot the live cd that way.
Once installed, you can change to a comfortable resolution permanently :)
