### Post by BigDave708 on 2006-07-21
Another alternative would be to buy a router and connect that to your internet connection and then wire a connection from the router to your Ubuntu computer. Ubuntu should automatically connect to the router and if it doesn't, it would be an awful lot easier still than trying to connect to a USB modem.
