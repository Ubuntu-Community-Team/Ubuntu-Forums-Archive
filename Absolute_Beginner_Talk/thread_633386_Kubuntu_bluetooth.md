### Post by umuro on 2008-03-14
A lot depends on the configuration of the phone itself. Somewhere, in your phone find out how to make your phone "bluetooth visible" and "bluetooth discoverable". Also your phone might have an additional visibility toggle for each resource accessable.

For example, I turned on "bluetooth visibility" and then made the phone "bluetooth services discoverable". Then I was able to use obex file transfer services namely kbluetooth. Then I discovered that to get the files from the phone I had to turn each files bluetooth visibility on. So the story changes for each phone.

In fact, kbluetooth was just working out of the box. It was just the phone settings...

For security reasons, those options are "OFF" by default on any phone. So first check those also before looking at the linux site.
