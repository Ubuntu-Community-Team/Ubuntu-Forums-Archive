### Post by bsussman on 2006-11-05
When we threw out MSEverything, we found that OO Word could do everything that we were doing in Publisher.

We probably were not pushing the limits in Pub, but we were doing many standard advertizing format things and found that OO Word had all we needed.  It is significantly stronger than MSWord.  We just cut and pasted to convert, since there is no file conversion.

I expect we may have to graduate to Scribus but we have not been forced by some needed feature yet :)
