### Post by xshady87 on 2006-03-21
I guess this is a really dumb question, but how do I access my hard drive? I have files on a DVD that I would like to put in a new folder, not on the desktop. I was looking for a 'My Computer' type thing.

### Post by IYY on 2006-03-21
On the top panel, there is a menu called "places". From there you can access various locations on your computer. I'd suggest placing your files in your home folder.

If you want to make an icon for a location on your desktop, you can do so by dragging it from the "places" menu to the desktop.

---

### Post by pbaehr on 2006-03-21
Also, assuming they're just random files, you should probably put them in your home directory. (/home/your_user_name).

It's sort of like "My Documents" in Windows in that it's the place you should keep the random files that belong to you. Of course, you should ideally organize it into folders to keep sane.
