### Post by fmo on 2006-01-26
If you replace you IDE CD Burner by an IDE DVD Burner you won't need to do anything.

For the removal of the harddrive it should be ok as long as you have no important folder on it. If you plug it as an external drive it will be recognised as a new drive.

To do the things properly I suggest that you remove your harddrive from /etc/fstab.
