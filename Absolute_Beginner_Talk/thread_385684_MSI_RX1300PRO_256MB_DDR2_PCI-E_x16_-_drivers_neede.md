### Post by sunexplodes on 2007-03-16
Download a program called ENVY ( [http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html](http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html) ), and use that. it's a command line tool that makes it easy to install drivers.

Just figure out if your card has a nVidia or ATI chipset (it installs both) and follow the prompts.
