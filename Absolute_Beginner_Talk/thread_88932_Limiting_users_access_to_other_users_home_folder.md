### Post by discofro on 2005-11-11
Hi Everyone,

I have created two login accounts. One for me (admin) and then a guest account. When I testing the guest account, I found that I could very easily locate and open files that were in the admin accounts home folder. 

Is there a way to prevent this? I don't want the guest account to be able to see any files that are in the admit accounts home folder. 

Thanks

---

### Post by Kyral on 2005-11-11
While you are logged in as the Admin, right click on the home folder for it, go to permissions, and uncheck all the options for "Others"
