### Post by alexforcefive on 2008-04-02
To change your shortcuts you go to System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts

But first, you have to go to System > Preferences > Keyboard > Layout > Alt/Win Key Behaviour and change it to "super is mapped to the win-key"
