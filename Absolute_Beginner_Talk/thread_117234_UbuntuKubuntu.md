### Post by blair on 2006-01-14
The official Gnome site is: [www.gnome.org](www.gnome.org)

The official KDE site is: [www.kde.org](www.kde.org).

The look and feel of Gnome and KDE are completely different. Some prefer one, some prefer the other, some hate both, some like both. KDE has more market share. There are also some differences in the packages (applications and utilities) that are bundled in the packages. Gnome and KDE are not only different looking, but they are complete packages of desktop utilities and applets, e.g. notepad, post-it notes, email, etc. I recommend you burn a live CD of each, boot your machine up using each one and play with them to see which you like more. 

People have expended lots of hot air on which is "better" and "worse", but I find them both acceptable. I'd state my preference but that would almost certainly result in a flame war on this thread over why the other is better. Play with both. See which you like more.
