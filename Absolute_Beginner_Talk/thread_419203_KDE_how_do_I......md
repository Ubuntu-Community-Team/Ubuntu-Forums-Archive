### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-04-22
When you're logging in at the GDM screen, there should be some sort of button that says Options or something to that effect.  Within this menu, there is a "sessions" option.  Choose KDE or Gnome from this menu to switch.

Hope that helps.  I'm not sure of the exact name of the first button you need to click because I don't actually use GDM or KDM.
