### Post by stefangr1 on 2008-03-29
If you have installed the nvidia drivers, you can run the NVIDIA X-server settings utility from the task bar, or by the command /usr/bin/nvidia-settings.

If you go to the "X Server Display Configuration" tab, and press the "Advanced" button, can't you manually set the resolution to 1920x1200, and the frequency to 60Hz?

Otherwise, what happens if you manually put the desired display mode in your xorg.conf?
like:

    SubSection "Display"
        Depth        24
        Modes      "1920x1200_60.00"

Otherwise, here you can find a good tutorial on changing your monitor resolution and refresh rate: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=83973](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=83973)
