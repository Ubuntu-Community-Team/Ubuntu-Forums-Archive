### Post by kleo skywalker on 2007-11-18
> **art7jh said:**
> I want to instal Linux on a machine that is using old Bill's software, is it best to re-format and start from scratch, or load on top and wait for the crash?

If you want to keep Windows, or are unsure whether Ubuntu will meet all or most of your requirements, then dual-booting is a better option.
If you don't have those considerations, and especially if you have access to another computer in case anything goes wrong, just back up your data and start from scratch. Just download and burn Ubuntu, boot from the LiveCD and double-click install - simple as that.
