### Post by fiddledd on 2008-01-13
This isn't an answer, and from what you say in your post, you already know this, but for others who may not know and might be confused:

The OSE of VirtualBox in the repositories does not support USB, the Non-free version from the VirtualBox website (or by adding Vrtualbox's repo to sources) does. I've been using VirtualBox with an XP host for months and all my USB drives, Memory Sticks etc, work fine, with vbox additions installed on the host.
