### Post by BitTorrentBuddha on 2006-06-16
Browse to the mount point in Nautilus and just drag the folder icon over to the right (underneath the separator), from there you can rename it to anything you deem fit. It uses a pseudo bookmarking technique and it applies to everywhere, including an entry in Places ->, I find it useful to have /tmp located there.

NOTE: This will put the thing under the separator, not above with the rest of the things.
