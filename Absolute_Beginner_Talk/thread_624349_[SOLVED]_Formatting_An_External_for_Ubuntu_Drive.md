### Post by llamakc on 2007-11-26
Do you mean that if you bring over your external drive to their place and go to boot, will GRUB see Windows? 

No. Grub doesn't do live detection. One could have an entry for grub FOR Windows and edit the boot prompt command in the GRUB shell though.

However, if you're using the external to SHOW somebody Ubuntu, what need is there for GRUB to see Windows? You could just choose the external device in BIOS and boot into Ubuntu in that fashion.

Am I misunderstanding your question?
