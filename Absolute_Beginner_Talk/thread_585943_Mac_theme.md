### Post by richiewrt on 2007-11-04
You can also install art manager. It will automatically download and install anything from art-gnome.org  Just open add/remove programs then find art manager. Once installled go to system-->Preferences-->Art Manager

Once whatever you find in art manager is installed just right click on the desktop, change desktop backgrounds. From there you can change the theme to whatever you want. Not necessarily the MAC desktop, but with all the options out there, hopefully you can make it look even better.
