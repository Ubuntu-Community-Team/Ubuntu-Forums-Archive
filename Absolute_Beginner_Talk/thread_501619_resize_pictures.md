### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-07-15
Open your picture using GIMP (right click the picture and select open with "GIMP Image Editor"...or if it's not there, select "Open with other Application" and find and select GIMP)

Then select

Image-->--Scale Image-->....then adjust height and width...-->--press "Scale"


...then "Save as".
