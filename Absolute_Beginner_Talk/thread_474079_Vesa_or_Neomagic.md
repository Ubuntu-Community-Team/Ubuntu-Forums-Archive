### Post by kerry_s on 2007-06-14
You need to remove that>  Option "UseFBDev" "true" <frame buffer is slow.
here's the settings i use for mine->

```
Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Generic Video Card"
	Driver		"neomagic"
	BusID		"PCI:1:0:0"
        Option          "StrangeLockups"        "false"
        Option          "BackingStore"        "true"
EndSection
```

You need to look at your /var/log/xorg.0.log and look for the (ee) and (ww) those are errors and warnings.

gedit /var/log/xorg.0.log
