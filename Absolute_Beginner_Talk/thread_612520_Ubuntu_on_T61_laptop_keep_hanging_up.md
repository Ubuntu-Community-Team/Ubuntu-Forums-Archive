First, there is a communication problem here. Originally you said it was "hanging up," but in English "hang up" is what you do with a phone when you have finished a conversation. When a computer no longer responds we say it "hangs, " not "hangs up." But computers have modems, which work like telephones, and we do say "the modem hangs up." So some people might be confused about what you meant. (English particle verbs are extremely difficult for foreigners to master.)

Second, you need to give more detail. What are the error messages you get? What were you doing when the computer hung? 

Regarding the wireless network issues, I would suggest posting in the Networking and Wireless forum. Be sure to give the details of exactly what happens or doesn't happen.
