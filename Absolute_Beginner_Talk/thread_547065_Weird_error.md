### Post by Ant_Merlin on 2007-09-11
Hi, HAL stands for Hardware Abstraction Layer and is the layer that sits between hardware and software.

This is usually the layer between motherboard and Kernel, and in real terms it means the kernel doesnt have to worry about what make, model etc of motherboard you have.

I am new to linux but for windows getting this error meant either clean reinstall or a hardware (motherboard) fault.
