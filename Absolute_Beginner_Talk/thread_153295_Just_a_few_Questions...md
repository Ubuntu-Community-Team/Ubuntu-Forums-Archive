### Post by taurus on 2006-03-31
It usually takes about 4 weeks for the CDs to arrive!  You can change the look or theme of Gnome if you want to.  Just see which one you like and download it to your machine.  Then, apply the change...  And if you want to share files between four machines, you can use samba (if the other three are Windows) or nfs (if they are all Unix).
