### Post by red_Marvin on 2008-02-08
Well compiz and such things are loaded after you log in, and the flashier your desktop is, the longer it will take -naturally.
If the speed is so important you could always install a lighter window manager, like openbox or fluxbox which loads almost instantly, but at the cost of eyecandy.
