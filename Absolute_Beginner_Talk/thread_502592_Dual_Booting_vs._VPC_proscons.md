### Post by testube_babies on 2007-07-16
Your setup is beefy enough to run Vista satisfactorily under virtualization.  The problem might arise with your Windows license.  Not all licenses allow you to run Windows under virtualization.  In the worst case, re-using your Windows activation in a virtual machine will register with Redmond as trying to use it twice, and you'll be locked out of Vista on your VM.
