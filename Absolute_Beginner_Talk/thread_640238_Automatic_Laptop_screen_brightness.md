### Post by nudaz on 2007-12-27
This fixed my problem too,
but my problem was that every few minutes ubuntu would dim my screen.
I went into System -> Preferences -> Power Management and found the screen was set to full brightness on AC power and not to dim the screen.
But in the On Battery tab I changed it to not dim the screen and display full brightness,
and that fixed it, even though I'm on AC power.
Ubuntu 7.10 thinks I'm on battery power all the time.

I use an LG SA8X2A
