### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-02-11
It's not bad for the laptop, just uses up battery when not plugged in.  This doesn't seem to be a problem for you :)
Having the screen turn off when idle is a bonus, and you can tell it to spin down the hard drive, too, when idle.

### Post by Whiffle on 2008-02-11
I'd just shut it off when you're not using it...

But if you insist on leaving it on...
If it gets too hot it can hurt the battery, but other than that its not a big deal.  I wouldn't use a screensaver, just make sure it shuts off the screen when you're idle.  I don't know how well built your power brick is, but if its questionable it might not stand up to continuous usage and burn out eventually.  Make sure its not buried under anything such that it would get too hot.
