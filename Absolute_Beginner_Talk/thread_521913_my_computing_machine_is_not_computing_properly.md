OK: first please don't be too cute with the language. It makes people less likely to be helpful.

Anyway: I gather that ryhthmbox isn't working, and the internet freezes up (with firefox). 

Since these are 2 unrelated programs, I suspect hardware. Your machine is several years old (i think: you have 256mb of ram, and the newer, the more ram). There are several major hardware failure modes that could have happened 'instantly'. 

1) Hard drive could have developed some bad sectors. Seatools from seagate is the possibly best bet here. It is available in ISO format, which means you could put it on a CD.

2) CPU Fan failure: meaning overheating and instability. 

Check out that stuff and report back. Preferably in better English.
