### Post by Buffalo Soldier on 2006-04-29
[QUOTE=Kishore Sundara-Rajan]Hello,

I have been using Ubuntu for about a week now, and the sounds the system makes when opening and closing a window is driving me crazy. How do I turn it off?](*,) [/QUOTE]

System -> Preferences -> Sound -> (uncheck) Play System Sounds

To disable the sound heard during GDM login, go to:

System -> Administration -> Login Window -> Accessibility -> Sounds

[QUOTE=Kishore Sundara-Rajan]Also, the volume when playing my DVDs seems to be a lot lower than what it used to be in Windows. How do I rectify that?[/QUOTE]
Have you check your volume control?
