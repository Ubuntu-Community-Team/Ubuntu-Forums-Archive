### Post by cognaq on 2008-05-11
Unfortunately Creative Zen W recognises the file information only by its tags. It also sorts it this way.
When you turn on your Zen W you can select Artists, Albums etc, but you cannot have your own folders. That is why gnomad2 does not allow this.
