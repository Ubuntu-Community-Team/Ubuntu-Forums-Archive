### Post by fastpakr on 2007-08-27
There isn't very much that can't be made to work in 64 bit.  What isn't out there will be soon enough.  While I don't have 64 bit on my laptop, I do on the desktop (and have for almost six months in a single boot environment) with no regrets at all.  It runs Compiz, Flash, and everything else with no hiccups that I can blame on the 64 bit aspect.  As long as you're willing to look into the forums for help when you try to install something new, you'll be just fine.  I'd say it's definitely worth it if you are at all resourceful.
