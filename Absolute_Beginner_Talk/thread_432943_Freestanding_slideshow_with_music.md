Just scroll down to feisty and add the line to your sources list (to do that just run this command in terminal "sudo gedit /etc/apt/sources.list" and add the line at bottom)

Pentium 4 is for the x86 processors, otherwise if your running 64 you need the amd version >.>

Once you add the line to your repos, you can just install it :)

Have fun with it.
