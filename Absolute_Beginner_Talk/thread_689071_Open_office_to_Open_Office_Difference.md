### Post by mysticrider92 on 2008-02-06
So when you open a file on your desktop it is 90% of the length it is on your laptop? My guess would be that the DPI used for font rendering is different, but that would probably show up in other programs. It could never hurt to try though...

If you want to change the DPI (Ubuntu 7.10), right click on the desktop and select Change Desktop Background. Click "Fonts" at the top of this window, then "Details" at the bottom. It is at the top of the details window. A DPI between ~86-96 is usually good, I use 96 on a 1440x900 monitor.
