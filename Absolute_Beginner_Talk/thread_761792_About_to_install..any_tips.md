### Post by canthus13 on 2008-04-21
Verify the CD after you boot it.  It's a real pain to get halfway through the install and have it crash because there was an error on the CD, especially if you only have access to one computer.  It happened to me on several CDs.  I finally had to burn it at the slowest speed in order to get a clean burn.

Oh, and memorize the URL for this forum.  That's the only MUST. I personally didn't have any installation issues, but those who do generally get their problem solved within an hour or so. (Unless it's a really odball problem.)
