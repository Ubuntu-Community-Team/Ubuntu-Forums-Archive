### Post by jem7v on 2007-04-03
When you actually went through the install process did you choose to wipe the entire hard drive, or just resize your Windows partition?

If you haven't actually gone through with the installation process (i.e., double clicking on Install, going through all 6 screens of the install wizard and Reading What They Say, then waiting half an hour while it does its thing) then you haven't lost anything.

On the other hand, if you've installed with the dual boot option and you just don't know how to access your old information, read this: [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/mountwindows](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/mountwindows)
