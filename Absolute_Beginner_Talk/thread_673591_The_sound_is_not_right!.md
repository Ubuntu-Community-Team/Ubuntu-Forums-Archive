### Post by nethbar on 2008-01-22
From what you're describing it sounds like a hardware issue.  Most software problems have dependable, reproduceable symptoms but hardware can be very gremlin-ish.

- Have you had any other operating systems (or other versions of Ubuntu) installed on this PC in the past?  If so, did they have any sound issues?

- Try to see if you can reproduce the sound issue by booting off of a Live CD.  It might take a few attempts but if you can get the sound problem to come up on the Live CD, it is very likely a hardware issue.

- The sound failure on boot might come from the hardware not 'being there' due to the problem.

I have a lot of experience as a technician but little on Linux so I might be missing something.
