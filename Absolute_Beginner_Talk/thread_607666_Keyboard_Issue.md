### Post by Trebaruna on 2007-11-09
Is your keyboard USB?
If so, you might need to enable it in the BIOS.
Pressing esc, del, f2 or similar (should say on screen when you start your machine) will get you into the settings screen of your motherboards BIOS. Look for a setting about USB keyboard support and make sure it is enabled.
