### Post by Cappy on 2007-06-16
(System-->Administration-->Users and Groups). Click on your user name and click properties. Click the "User privlidges" tab. Make sure "Access external storage devices .." is checked.

Also make sure the first 3 check boxes of the first tab are checked in (System-->Preferences-->Removable Drives and Media)
