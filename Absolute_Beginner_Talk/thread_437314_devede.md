### Post by mcduck on 2007-05-08
Video CD uses MPEG1 codec, while Super Video CD uses better MPEG2 codec like DVD movie do. MPEG2 has better quality, but if you wish to play the disk on a DVD player you'll have to check which one it supports. Most DVD players support Video CD, but if yours can play Super Video CD then it's better choice. Super Video CD also supports menus and other features Video CD can't do.
