### Post by PCFascist on 2008-03-30
In Pidgin I went into preferences and the sounds tab.
I was then able to select ALSA and enable sounds ALWAYS.
You may also need to uncheck the focus box, as I can't test this.
Then make sure VLC is also using ALSA.
