### Post by [S|G] on 2007-04-29
The BIOS warning is ACPI-related. Older BIOSes did not really follow ACPI specs so sometimes they act weird, and Linux doesn't use their ACPI capabilities. You could try to turn off ACPI support in your BIOS and try to install again. By the way, booting from the live cd does take a while, so make sure you're not getting impatient and rebooting before it finishes to boot.
