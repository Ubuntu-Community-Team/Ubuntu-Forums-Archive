Are you talking about the menu that has the Ubuntu Logo, "Applications Places System" in the upper left hand corner (like the picture I attached?

[attach]36224[/attach] (Click to enlarge.)

If so, it's simple. Right click the panel, click "+ Add To Panel," scroll down to the last group and find "Menu Bar." Click on it to choose, and click the "Add" button.

Then, if it's not placed where you want on the panel, you can right click on it, click "Move," and slide it to where you want it to be.

Good luck!
