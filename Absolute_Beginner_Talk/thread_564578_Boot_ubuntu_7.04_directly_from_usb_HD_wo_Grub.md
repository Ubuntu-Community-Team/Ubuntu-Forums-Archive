### Post by Yoooder on 2007-10-01
you should be able to boot from the LiveCD, tell it to install to your external drive, and under the advanced options tell grub to install to your external drive (versus your primary internal drive).

You will need a bootloader like grub to boot Linux regardless of where it lives.
