### Post by equal on 2007-02-16
As far as I know, the installer program isn't setup to do multiple disc installations, they simply don't exist in Ubuntu. One thing you could do would be to download the Kubuntu installer CD .ISO, and then use the DVD as a repository source later on for installing programs (I'm assuming you want the DVD because you have a slow internet connection).
