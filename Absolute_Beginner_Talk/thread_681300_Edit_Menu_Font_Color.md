### Post by amaroKer on 2008-01-28
Click to System>Preferences>Appearance 
OR
> Right-click on the desktop
> Click on Change Desktop Background
> Click on the 'Theme' tab at the top

You may have perused the included themes as shown in this window.  You can select any of these, and customize them to your taste.
> Select a theme you wish to use or modify
> Click on the 'Customize' button

You can mix and match every _element_ of each theme.  Colours (2nd tab) are editable only on some Controls (1st tab).  The Colours window is pretty self explanatory.  If it's greyed out, you need to choose a different Controls _element_.
