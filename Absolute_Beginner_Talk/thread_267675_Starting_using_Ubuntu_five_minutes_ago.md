### Post by LouIII on 2006-09-28
I'm blown away that only five minutes after starting LiveCD for the first time I'm already online.  I was reluctant to try Linux again after a disappointing experience with Mandrake several years ago (couldn't connect to the Internet), but Linux has apparently come a long way.  Sweet!

An audio buff, I was concerned that I'd have a hard time getting decent sound.  As I write this, I'm listening to a sample track that sounds just as good as the music I hear on my current Windows XP system.  The more I dig, the better it gets.

I briefly considered buying Linscape or Xandros so that I'd have printed documentation and, I must admit, the corporate crutch.  But part of Linux's appeal to me is the free, communal philosophy.  I also like Gnome, because it gives me an alternative to the Windows-like environemnt.  I'm glad I chose Ubuntu.
