### Post by Scorpuk on 2006-12-01
Correct me if I'm wrong, but I think what you are trying to do is play a movie on a windows computer from your linux box and up until now you have been using a terminal program.


If this is the case then you can use samba servers on your linux box to allow windows machines to view the contents of your linux box's hard drives and thus play them in the native programs on windows.


I do this to play anything from my music collection to stored video files on my windows machine that are stored on my linux box.

hope this is of some help to you.
