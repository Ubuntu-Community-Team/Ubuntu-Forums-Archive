you gotta understand (and the linux junkies won't admit to this) that companies release drivers to microsoft before any other operating system. linux usually does a good job with hardware a few years old. but the latest and greatest it's a crap shoot. a normal mouse will probably work fine, but the ten button one, good luck. and the same with your printer, if it was just built last week instead of last year. but as a free choice to the ever rising costs of microsofts operating systems, ubuntu and linux in general is great.
