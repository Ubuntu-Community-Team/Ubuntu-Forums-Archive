### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-09-03
Wallpaper is generally seperate from themes. Splash screens are the screens that pop up while an app (like OpenOffice and GIMP) is loading.  A website like [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) will have everything you need for theming:

Icons
GTK2.x - controls the widgets, buttons, menubar/toolbar look, etc
Metacity - window borders
Wallpaper
