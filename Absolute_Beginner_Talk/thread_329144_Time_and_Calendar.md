### Post by riven0 on 2007-01-01
> **vivin_west said:**
> I set the time on Dec 23rd. Even today the calender shows Dec 23rd.It is JAN 1st today.I think I have missed some step in the setting. can you please help me by telling me the procedure?

Okay, so right-click on the time and choose Adjust Time & Date. Now you should get a window with the time, (in military format for some reason), a calendar and some options below. 

Hit the arrow key next to December to get January. Then click on the correct day. You also may want to select your timezone if it's wrong. Now hit Synchronize Now. 

AT least that's the way I do it. Hopefully everything will work.
