### Post by irish_flu on 2007-10-06
A PCIe-x16 card is not compatible with an AGP8x motherboard; the slot is different, as is the power supply needed (the PCIe video card needs an extra power connector from the power supply).  In order to use a PCIe video card, you'll need to purchase the card, a motherboard, a power supply, and possibly a processor (if there are no PCIe motherboards that can use the processor you have now).

Anyway, I use Beryl with all kinds of fancy eye candy turned on, and it works great with my old GeForce 4 Ti 4800 (128MB).
