### Post by chamberlain2006 on 2007-11-09
You can go to System>Administration>Networking and click on the wireless interface.  Uncheck "Roaming Mode" if it is checked, and then enter you network name in the appropriate box, as well as choose "DHCP".  The next time you computer boots, it will use that network.
