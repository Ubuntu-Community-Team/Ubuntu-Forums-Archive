### Post by beartard on 2008-01-20
It also depends on how much communication you need.  There's a smartphone app called WM5torage that basically turns your phone into a USB card reader so that you can transfer files back and forth.  There's also a PIMbackup utility that lets you make backups of your contacts and other info in csv form that can be imported into several applications.  As far as actual activsync functionality, there's SynCE, but you almost need herculean amounts of patience, alcohol, and experience to install it and configure it properly.
