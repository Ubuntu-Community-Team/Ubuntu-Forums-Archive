### Post by CCNA_student on 2007-12-16
There is a version of Ubuntu for the PPC architecture, but it is not used as often so you will get less support. I do not know if an Airport Extreme wireless card works with Ubuntu, but I will bet someone made a driver for it. Look in the Apple PPC section of Ubuntu forums. You should be able to get Ubuntu working on an old Mac laptop. Make sure that you download the PPC version of Ubutnu, the x86 or AMD64 architectures will not work on a G4 proccesor.
