### Post by fuscia on 2006-09-29
i'm listening to shoutcast on amarok and everytime the selection changes, amarok announces this fact in a billboard that screams across all desktops. how do i disable this lovely feature?

---

### Post by ewl1217 on 2006-09-29
In the Amarok options menu, there is a section for the OSD (On-Screen Display). Just go to that section and turn it off.

---

### Post by ash211 on 2006-09-29
Amarok: Settings->Configure->OSD->Use Onscreen Display
