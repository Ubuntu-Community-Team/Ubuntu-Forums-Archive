### Post by aysiu on 2007-08-18
**Some things that distinguish distros:**
* Funding source
* Release frequency
* Number of CDs
* Default programs and artwork
* Package Management
* Price (though most distros will have at least one cost-free version)
* Documentation
* Surrounding community
* Development model
