### Post by TeraDyne on 2007-03-10
Under your k menu (the main application menu that has the big K), choose the "System Settings". The system settings menu will show up.

Click on "Notifications". You can customize the sounds in this menu.

Everything that has a sound associated with it will have a little speaker icon in the fifth column on the first screen. Click on any of them to turnoff the sound for that notification.

There's also an option to "Turn Off All" and a menu next to it that defaults to "Sounds". You can use that to turn off all the notification sounds.
