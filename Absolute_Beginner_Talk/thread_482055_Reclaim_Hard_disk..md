### Post by molly_001 on 2007-06-23
Download the LiveCD version of GParted.

I recommend [version 2.5-2 from June, 2006]("http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=115843&package_id=173828").

You will burn an .iso (bootable CD or DVD).
Then boot computer with this CD media,
and you will see easily how to reclaim/resize.
