### Post by nalmeth on 2006-07-18
Ubuntu is developed by a company called Canonical, under funding from Mark SHuttleworth.

Here's the wiki link: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu_%28Linux_distribution%29](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu_%28Linux_distribution%29)

Nobody in this online forum makes money that's for sure.

Canonical makes money by offering year-round support for corporations who want it. The operating system, and it's applications are free.
