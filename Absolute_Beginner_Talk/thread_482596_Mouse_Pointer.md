### Post by isaacj87 on 2007-06-23
Do you already have a theme ready? If so, all you have to do is copy the theme into /home/<your username>/.icons

Log out and and log back in....

Then go to System->Preferences->Mouse-> and the pointer tabs and it should be waiting for you.

Let me know if this works for you.
