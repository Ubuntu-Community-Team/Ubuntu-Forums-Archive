### Post by Flying caveman on 2008-04-15
I was having a problem on one of my machines that wouldn't display the correct resolutions.  

After I tried dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg.    didn't work, I even edited my /etc/X11/xorg.conf but I kept ending up in low graphics mode.  800x600.  Finally I just commented out allow-failsafe in the /etc/X11/Xsession.options.  It worked fine after that, meaning I could choose and use all the resolutions my monitor was capable of.  I'm sure its using the VESA drivers instead of the nVidia ones on that machine, but I didn't need any fancy effects or anything so I decided to live with it.

I could raise the resolution all the way to 2048x1536 but I wouldn't be able to read anything without increasing all the font sizes.
