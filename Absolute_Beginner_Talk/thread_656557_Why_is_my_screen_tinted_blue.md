### Post by Delvien on 2008-01-02
Slip in a LiveCD and see if it still happens, It can be a number of different things, but it sounds like your monitor might be going bad....

IF the LiveCD boots up and you dont have that problem anymore, then it could be  something with the video driver you are using. If the LiveCD doesnt fix the problem, I'd say its your monitor. 

IF you can, try and replace the monitor, see if it still does it.
