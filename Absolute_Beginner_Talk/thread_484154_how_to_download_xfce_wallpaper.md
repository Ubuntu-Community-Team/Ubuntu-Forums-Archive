### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2007-06-25
As suggested by overdrank, save the image to your home directory.

You can then ```
sudo mv <image> /usr/share/xfce4/backdrops/<image>
```

OR

Set the backgroung with xfce -> on the little folder on the right -> navigate to the image in your home folder.
