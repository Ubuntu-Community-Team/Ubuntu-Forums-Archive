### Post by A$h X on 2007-08-25
You need an ethernet cable - they are very cheap and you sohuld have got one with your rotuer- see if you can find the box it came and get the cable from there. If not then ask friends/family if they have one otherwise get one from a store. Remember it's called an ethernet cable, they come in all lengths you just want one that will connect your router to your machine so a 1 meter cable will be fine.

After that, there will be a port on the back of the router labelled LAN 1 or just 1, and the end of the ethernet cable will fit into the port. Pluf it in, and then plug the other end into a port on your machine. Both ends are the same on the cable so don't worry.

Once plugged in, kubuntu should find the internet connection by itself. Let me know how you get on.
