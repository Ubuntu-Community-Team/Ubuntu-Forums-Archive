### Post by ffxr on 2007-04-14
you can run photoshop in virtualbox in a window on your desktop

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=224212&highlight=seamless+windows](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=224212&highlight=seamless+windows)

& yes you need samba to share files between your virtual xp, and ubuntu:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202605&highlight=samba](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202605&highlight=samba)


i have a similar setup and the guides worked well for me, and i agree using ANY version of photoshop under is far too buggy for daily use..

if you have a well spec'ed pc a virtual solution is best.. another idea to consider rather than the seamless windows & rdesktop is to use one side of a beryl cube for your virtual photoshop..
