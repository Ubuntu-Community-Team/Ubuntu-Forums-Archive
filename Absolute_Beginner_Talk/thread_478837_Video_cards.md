### Post by EagleRock on 2007-06-20
The NVIDIA GPUs do have a generally good convention on speed, as each series since the 6000 series has incremented by one.  However, note that some GPUs are low-grade, some mid-grade, and some high-grade.  An 8400 card will NOT be faster than the 7900GTX, for example, as they are two different tiers of GPU.

Here's a site that shows benchmarks for the mid-grade and high-grade cards worth buying:

[http://www23.tomshardware.com/graphics.html](http://www23.tomshardware.com/graphics.html)

In terms of a price range, this article will help tremendously:

[http://www.tomshardware.com/2007/06/11/the_best_gaming_video_cards_for_the_money/](http://www.tomshardware.com/2007/06/11/the_best_gaming_video_cards_for_the_money/)
