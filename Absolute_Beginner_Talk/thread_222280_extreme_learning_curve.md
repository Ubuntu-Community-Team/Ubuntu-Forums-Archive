### Post by T700 on 2006-07-24
> **tphartl said:**
> Thank you for the warm welcome.  So, how do I go about 'learning ' Ubuntu / open source coding?

Coding means writing a set of commands that are carried out by the computer.  Just like in the human world, there are many different languages such as Java, Javascript, C+, C++, and dozens more.  The choice of language depends on what you are trying to do and to some extent, the operating system you intend to run your program on.

Personally, I think teaching a newcomer Javascript is the way to go.  It is simple to learn the basics, provides instant gratification, and is widely documented.  Just do a Google search for "learn Javascript" and you'll find a wealth of guides.
