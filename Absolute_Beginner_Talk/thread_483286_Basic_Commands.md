### Post by caro on 2007-06-24
Like you, I'm still learning a lot about Ubuntu, and while I have used commands that people have given me, I want to understand what I'm doing.

One thing you can do is use the manual pages.  At the command line of the terminal screen, type "man" followed by the command you want to know about.  It will give you lots of specific information.

Also, here is a great resource that someone on the forum pointed out to me.  [http://www.linuxcommand.org/]("http://www.linuxcommand.org/").  

Good luck.
