### Post by aysiu on 2006-06-28
Use whatever repositories list you want to have updated.

If you have no idea which is best, I'd stay with the Automatix list.

If you really liked your original sources.list but just wanted to use Automatix for convenience (and you generally know what you're doing), I'd say it's a good idea to use your original list.
