### Post by aysiu on 2006-05-23
Even if you did everything correctly, there are a couple of things I can think of that may be hampering your Flash-viewing ability:

1. Flash is great for x86 computers, but if you're using AMD64 or PowerPC, it may not work so well. More details in the link Sef gave.

2. I know the Adblock extension can interfere with Flash if you have Obj-Tabs option enabled in the Adblock preferences.
