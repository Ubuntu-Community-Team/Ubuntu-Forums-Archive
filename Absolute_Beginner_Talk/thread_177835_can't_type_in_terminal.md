### Post by aysiu on 2006-05-17
When you're asked for your password, there's no visual feedback. Go ahead and type the password anyway and then press Enter.

By the way, you don't need to enable your root account to make root changes: [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/permissions](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/permissions)
