### Post by CloudFX on 2008-04-02
I wouldn't suggest upgrading to Hardy while it is still in beta mode.  It's best to wait the three weeks until the full version comes out.  I do have a couple procedures you can attempt to get your sound working though.

I'm running a Dell Inspiron 1521, and I too could not get my sound to work at first.  I thought it was working, but that was because I had USB headphones plugged in, which showed that the sound card was installed; just being a bother.

First things first, make sure sound is not muted on all other OS's.  Now open up Sound (System > Preferences > Sound), and under the Devices tab, change autodetect to something different.  What it is changed to did not affect me.  Now restart your computer, and it might work.  It did for me.  

If it doesn't, then I supposed your only option is Hardy.  But I still vote against upgrading while it is still in beta mode.
