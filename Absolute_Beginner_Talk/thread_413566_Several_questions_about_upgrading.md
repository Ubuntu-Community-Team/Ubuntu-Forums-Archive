### Post by houstonbofh on 2007-04-19
Nothing will move.  However, updates can "go horribly wrong" and leave you in an unusable state.  A good backup is recommended. (Of course I never do it myself...)  Also, look at you install to see what is likely to blow up on an upgrade.  Do you have any compiled drivers or applications?  Did you have to do special stuff to get WiFi to work?  Are you using special machinations to get Video drivers? (Envy or Nividia drivers)  The closer you are to a clean "Apt only" installation, the more likely you are to upgrade without issue.

However, if it blows up, you can still get to all your data with the live CD.
