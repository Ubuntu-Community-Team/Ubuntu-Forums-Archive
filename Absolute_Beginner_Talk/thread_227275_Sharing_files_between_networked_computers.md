### Post by bajara on 2006-08-01
In UBUNTU, the SAMBA protocol (the one that Windoze uses) is enabled by Default, just go to "Network" ---> Servers (i don't know if the names are right since my Ubuntu was in Spanish).

In Xubuntu (the one I use nowadays), the SAMBA protocol wasn't enabled by default, you have to download SAMBA apps in Synaptic.
