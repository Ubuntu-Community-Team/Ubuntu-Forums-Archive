### Post by Daveth on 2007-07-16
an easy one- right click in the middle of your toolbar, unlock, move, lock.
If things have dropped off your toolbar you can add them back by, for example, dropping down the applications menu, games, and right click and add launcher to panel for the application you want.
Just make sure you lock things back onto the panel. Resolution changes always seem to do that to menus, inc in windows.
