### Post by hardwarder on 2005-07-02
You can get the one from the repository. 2.6.10-686
It's a kernel image, you only need to download and install and reboot to go "into it."
It's recommended that you don't use the 2.6.11 images. They have problems with gamin.

Or if you prefer, you can compile your own kernel image from the vanilla sources with this howto [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=43065](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=43065)
Compiling gives you some knowledge about your kernel.
