### Post by maxamillion on 2007-01-02
If the ATI card you are running is capable of running AIGLX natively then your system shouldn't see much overhead when running beryl, I know someone who runs beryl on an AMD K6-2 333MHz with 192mb of ram because his gfx card is a nVidia GeForce 5200 (running nvidia-9xxx drivers) so majority of the grunt work is passed off to his gpu.

the link you need is [http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu) at the time I am writing this the site is under maintenance so you might want to wait a day or two.
