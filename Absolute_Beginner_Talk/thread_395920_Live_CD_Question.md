### Post by h0ax on 2007-03-28
Do not see your hard drives, do you mean to say they're external hard drives? 
If they're external, generally they will be detected on the install.  I have two WD External drives, both of which were detected.

Wireless is difficult sometimes and easy other times.  The thing to look for would be how well linux drivers work for your Wireless card specifically, not the model or series of the laptop.  Once you know the actual kind of wireless card inside, perhaps a forum search will yield some additional information.
