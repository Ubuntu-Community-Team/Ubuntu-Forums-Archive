### Post by Lucifiel on 2007-03-31
If you want to give Ubuntu a try, may I suggest that you simply boot from the Live Cd?

Yep, the cd image that you burn onto your cd = your Live cd. 

To boot from the Live Cd, simply leave the cd in your cd-rom drive and reboot. ;)

If you simply boot from your live cd, you're not installing Ubuntu onto your computer and so, you can play around with the programs to see if you like it. :) 

I have faith you'll like Ubuntu, though. ;)
