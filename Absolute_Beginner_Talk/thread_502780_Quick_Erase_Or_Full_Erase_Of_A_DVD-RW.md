### Post by Bofur on 2007-07-17
Quick erase works just fine if you need to get the files off the dvd and put different ones on there. If you are worried about someone somehow finding out what you previously had on a dvd rw then do a full erase, but as you said it's just for home use so a quick erase would work just fine.

On how long a dvd-rw will last part, it should be quite awhile. I've reused the same 10 dvd-rw's for the past year and I've had no problems whatsoever.
