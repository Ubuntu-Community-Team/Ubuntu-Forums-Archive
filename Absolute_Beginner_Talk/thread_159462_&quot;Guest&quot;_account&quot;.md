Creating a new user and not giving him access to "Perform Administrative Tasks", "Monitor System Log" etc. should solve it. I am not too sure about resetting everything once they log off though (if you mean resetting even the changes that they made to their desktop etc). Take a look at System-->Administration-->Users and Groups.
