### Post by pbaehr on 2006-10-16
For what it's worth, the lowest file size I could achieve without making your small text images unreadable was about 1.14 MB. That's still well over the 19.5 kb limit for the forums, but if it helps you any I could email it to you.

Other than that, it looks good. You obviously put a lot of time into it.

If I may make one comment from a professional designer's point of view I would suggest that you use a more readable typeface. For large portions of text that are meant to be viewed on the screen I would recommend a relatively plain sans-serif typeface.  While your typeface is perfectly legible, it is less of a strain on a reader to use a more "standard" face.

Just my two cents.
