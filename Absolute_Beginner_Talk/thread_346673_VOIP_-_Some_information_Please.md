### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2007-01-26
SIP and H.323 are the standards you want to know about, and Ekiga is the "bundled" software that supports them on Ubuntu. 

Practical examples: 
Windows Messenger==SIP, 
Netmeeting==H.323. 
Skype doesn't interoperate.
