### Post by RedSquirrel on 2007-04-17
Yes, pressing **q** will quit the man page. 

When you're completely done with the terminal, just can also press **Ctrl-d** instead of typing "exit". Just make sure your prompt has nothing after it:

```
foo@bar:~$
```
