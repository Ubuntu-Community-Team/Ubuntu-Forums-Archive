### Post by BHelts on 2007-06-27
in addition to what the previous poster said.  you can download themes from gnome-look.org and drag them into the system ~> preferences ~> themes and apply them from there.

gtk themes handle the way your menus and panels look.
metacity themes handle the way your windows look.
icon themes obviously handle the way your icons look.
all can be installed the same way.  Then you just click on configure to detail each individual theme.

this assumes (I know, bad) that you are using gnome.
