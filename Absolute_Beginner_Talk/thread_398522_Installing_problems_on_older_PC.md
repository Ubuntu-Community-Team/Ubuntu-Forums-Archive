### Post by Ubluedog on 2007-04-01
i have several laptops here , all old, only the upmarket ones actually work , apart from ram constraints the video drivers are usually the problem, same happens with minimal installs like puppylinux.
the compaq armada here for example had a problem in that it couldnt access admin mode even after 3 instals, it was after installing the alternate cd , then the main cd straight after i could access the admin mode, it kept saying it didnt have password authentication installed , but it works now.
dont even bother trying older gateway laptops, compaq armada basic versions, or old dells with low ram.
any DEC laptop will not work , due to video driver problems , i have 3 of them , none work. fortunately they are no longer made, but they had nice screens.
