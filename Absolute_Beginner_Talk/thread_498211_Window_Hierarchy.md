### Post by kidders on 2007-07-14
Hi there,

What you're describing is called "focus stealing prevention" ... at least I *think* that's what you're describing! Many window managers support the idea in variable degrees, so you can try to find a happy medium between "on" and "off".

### Post by freebird54 on 2007-07-14
ONe quick way in most managers is to enable "Sun mouse" that is to say have whatever the mouse is over be the active window (having focus).  If you leave your mouse at, for instance, the top left corner - and have your browser open a bit OFF the left edge, it won't take over your view/work....

The only other settings I know are in Beryl - but I am sure that lots of alternative methods are out there - this *IS* Linux!
