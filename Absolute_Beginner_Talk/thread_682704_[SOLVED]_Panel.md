### Post by seventhc on 2008-01-30
If there is no check mark next to 'lock to panel' then right click, choose move and just move your mouse left or right, it doesn't even have to be over the icon you are moving. Since the network icon is not moveable you can still place the ones that are moveable before or after it which in a sense will bump it over. (close to moving it, but in a different way.)
