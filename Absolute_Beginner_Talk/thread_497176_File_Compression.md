### Post by pyros on 2007-07-10
yeah, compression works best with text-based files, not datafiles like compiled programs, music, movies, pictures etc. Sometimes adding them to an archive will end up taking up more space.
You can try 7zip though, I think it has the best compression ratio available.

### Post by atria on 2007-07-10
Yep especially videos that are already encoded (compressed) with a codec like xvid. Compressing the compressed video/pics with another algorithm will not yield much results.

Also tar archives are for archival purposes since it does not offer any form of compression by itself.
