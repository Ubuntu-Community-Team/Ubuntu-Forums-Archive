### Post by Mustard on 2006-03-06
Try going into Synaptic and open up the 'Repositories' menu choice.  The one just under 'Preferences', then click on the 'Settings' button and see if changing the update interval setting in there helps.  I changed mine recently to 7 days and it seems to be working the way I want it to now.
