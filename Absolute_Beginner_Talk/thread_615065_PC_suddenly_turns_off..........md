### Post by approaching on 2007-11-16
something similar happened to me a while ago, before you go and buy a new power supply you should check to see that all the wires are plugged in good and solid into their respective jack. if that doesn't work, it would be a good idea to switch out the power supply with a spare one if you have one lying around. but yeah, that sounds like a hardware issue to me if it is just all of a sudden and without some cryptic writing before it goes down.
