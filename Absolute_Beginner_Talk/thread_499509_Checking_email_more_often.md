How can I set evolution to check email more often?

---

### Post by PurplePenguin on 2007-07-12
Easy... Edit->Preferences->(select whichever email account you want to modify)->Edit->Receiving Options.

You'll see an option there to change the number of minutes for checking email.

---

### Post by Al3xK3aton on 2007-07-12
There's also a button to check it manually. Just click this button as often as you want.
