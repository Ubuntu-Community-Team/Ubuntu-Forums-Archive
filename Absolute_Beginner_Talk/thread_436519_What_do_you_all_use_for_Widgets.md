### Post by shadowboxer_k on 2007-05-08

### Post by rygar on 2007-05-08
first i tried gdesklets, and it really wasn't all that good.  the desklets looks pretty primitive compared to the offerings of vista and macos x.  the control panel interface wasn't that great either.

next, i tried screenlets.  it's really good!  in terms of looks, its top notch.  much much better than gdesklets.  if you have beryl installed, use the widgets plugin for an unbeatable combo.  the only drawback is that there aren't too many screenlets available at the moment...  hopefully that will change in the future.

i have heard good things about superkaramba--it's been around longer, and has a better selection of widgets.

there's also jackfield, a developing product that allows you to run dashboard widgets on ubuntu.
