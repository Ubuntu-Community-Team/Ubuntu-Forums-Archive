### Post by geoffm33 on 2006-11-10
Click on the "Playlists" tab then at the top press Add > Podcast.

Paste in the URL of the podcast feed and you are all set.

If you are looking for more of a directory listing of podcasts like itunes then you might need another app for that. I usually just go to the website of the podcast I want, copy the link of the feed and paste it in.
