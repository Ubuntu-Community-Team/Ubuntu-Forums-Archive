### Post by invisage01 on 2005-12-22
you may run into problems with serving the public if you don't have a static IP address.. if it's dynamic you will have to give your IP address out to the family each time you get a new one.. 

setting up apache is pretty straightforward.. it's getting your routers.. IP's .. ect that make serving to people outside the LAN you have (or may not have) difficult.

Don't let it frighten you though.. it's a great learning curve!
