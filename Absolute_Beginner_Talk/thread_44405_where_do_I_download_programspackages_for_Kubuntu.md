### Post by orev on 2005-06-26
Go to the Kmenu (WIN=Start), then goto:

>System>Synaptic

or

>System>Kynaptic

The software packages are listed there are in different repositories (sections), check out the "Documentation" area of the ubuntu/Kubuntu website to learn what the repositories are.

This should get you started....there is a lot of software out there.

My favorites:

Open Office (Office Productivity)

Amarok (Media Player)

Firefox (of course)

Thunderbird (for mail)

Hope this helps...
