### Post by dmetzcher on 2006-08-27
I have a theme that appears twice in the Theme Preferences screen. How can I get rid of one them (I installed it twice, accidentally)? Also, how do you uninstall themes, in general.

---

### Post by Qrk on 2006-08-27
Select "Show hidden files and folders" in nautilus and find  the .themes folder in your home directory.

You can remove a theme by deleting it's folder.
