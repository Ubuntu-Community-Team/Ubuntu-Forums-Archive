### Post by an.echte.trilingue on 2007-02-28
No, for many reasons.  For starters, the library structure is different; further, OSX uses a different binary executable format (Mach-O) than Linux, *BSD, and the other Unixes (ELF).

That said, lots of things have been ported between the two OS's.  What are you looking to run?
