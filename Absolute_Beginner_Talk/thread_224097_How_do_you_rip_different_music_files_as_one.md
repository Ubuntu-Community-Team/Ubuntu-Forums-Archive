### Post by gingermark on 2006-07-27
Grip allows you to rip sections of a CD, but you have to specify the sectors that you want to rip, so not ideal.

You could just rip all you want to .wav, then use Audacity to combine the tracks, cutting out any silence, and then encode the resultant track.
