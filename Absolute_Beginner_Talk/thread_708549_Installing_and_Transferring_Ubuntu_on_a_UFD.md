### Post by mrsteveman1 on 2008-02-26
If you installed it according to the pendrivelinux link, you basically have a persistent live system.

So what you can do is, when you get the new drive, install ubuntu onto it the same way, and then copy the files from the 2nd partition on your old drive, to the 2nd partition on the new drive.

It should work just fine. :D
