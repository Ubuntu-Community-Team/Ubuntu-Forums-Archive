### Post by PreviousN on 2008-03-02
Likely not. Sounds like the cd you are using to install ubuntu with got scratched or something.

 Try redownloading the install cd, if that doesn't work, your computers hard drive may have been dying and you managed to find out when you installed ubuntu. 

Good news is that its** NOT** ubuntu's fault and that if it is a hdd going out, then you have enough time likely to save most of your data. 

Installing an os can't really brick your computer very easily, and I've actually NEVER heard of it happening to a laptop or desktop computer.
