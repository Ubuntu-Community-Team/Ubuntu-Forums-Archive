### Post by Anchovie on 2005-10-15
Chances are you will need to [install](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=75378) either the 8.16.20 version of the fglrx or the [8.18.6](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=76116) version of the driver. After successfully installed one of those you'll get an ATI Control Panel that'll let you switch between displays.
