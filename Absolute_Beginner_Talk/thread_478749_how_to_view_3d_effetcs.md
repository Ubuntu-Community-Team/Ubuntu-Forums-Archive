### Post by cogadh on 2007-06-19
Which effect do you want to see? Check the Beryl settings manager to see what your options are. Some of them will need to be turned on before you can see them and some of them are very subtle (like the shadows under menus). The big one (desktop cube) can be seen by holding down the middle mouse button on the desktop and moving left or right.
