### Post by GreenHawkIA on 2006-12-06
Any of the Intel cards - intergrated or external - are supposed to be very good for out-of-the-box linux support.  I have an integrated Intel ProWireless 2200BG in my laptop, and it kicks the *** of any wireless card I've ever dealt with and has always worked out of the box with Ubuntu for several versions now.
