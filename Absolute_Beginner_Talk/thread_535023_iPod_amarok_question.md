### Post by sumguy231 on 2007-08-26
Just drag a song from the list in the devices tab to the name of the playlist. Or, if you drag a song from elsewhere to the playlist it will try to transfer it to the iPod and add it to the playlist if it can. For some reason though, if the song is already on the iPod and you want to drag it from the collection to add it to the playlist, it just complains that the song is already on the iPod and does nothing.
