### Post by ReaderRat on 2006-11-19
> **akasixcon said:**
> It tells me to turn off pnp bios. What and how do I do that?
It's telling you to turn off pnp (plug & play) in the BIOS. When you first boot your computer, a screen comes up with a way to get into the BIOS (Basic Input/Output System). It will say something like "Press <DEL> for Setup" or it may say to press another key (like <F2> or <ESC>). Once you are in the BIOS, **[COLOR="Red"]Do not change anything unless you know what you're doing.[/COLOR]** If you can find the PNP entry, you may turn it off (and you might have to turn it back on later). Look around if you want to know more about your computer's "inner workings", but be careful. I hope this helps.
