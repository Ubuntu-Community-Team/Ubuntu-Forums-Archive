### Post by kassetra on 2005-05-12
Absolute Beginner "Talk" is intended, as azz et al. have already stated to help those people intimidated by the other support areas, and by the answers they may have found.

I think it is very important not to just shovel beginner tech questions into the appropriate "area" - but instead guide people just starting with Linux, Ubuntu, Computers and the like.  This is exactly the appropriate place to do this.

Now, to keep this area unintimidating and free of advanced topics, we mods will most certainly need to patrol here more often - but the majority of us feel that this area should be kept as a tech support area.

In the scenario of a brand-new-Linux user coming to the forums with an issue and not knowing where or even exactly *what* the real problem is - this forum will be their spot.

It serves no one if we restrict the talk here to non-support only; in fact, I believe that would be a detriment to our community.

As for right now, this issue has been closed.  The beginner area will remain a support forum for new and possibly intimidated users.
