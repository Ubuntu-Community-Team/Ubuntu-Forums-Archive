### Post by skippy81 on 2006-05-07
No need to compile anything mate :p 

Simply open up synaptic and download the packages.  ("system" > "administration" > "synaptic package manager") on the menu bar if your using Gnome desktop.

Just type clamav into the search (under edit) and download the package.  You will have to enable the file repositories though.  [http://help.ubuntu.com/starterguide/C/ch02.html#addinguniverse]("http://help.ubuntu.com/starterguide/C/ch02.html#addinguniverse")
just follow the instructions titled "install universe and multiverse repositories.  

Good luck :)
