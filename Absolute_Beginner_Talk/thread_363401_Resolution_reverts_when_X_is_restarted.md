### Post by taurus on 2007-02-16
Well, you need to add "1280x1024" resolution to /etc/X11/xorg.conf

```
gksudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf
```
and make your Depth 24 to look like this.

```

SubSection "Display"
Depth 24
Modes "1280x1024" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"
EndSubSection

```
Then reboot.
