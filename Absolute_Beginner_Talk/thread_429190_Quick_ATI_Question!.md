### Post by dtgolder on 2007-05-01
I'm struggling with an ATI 9250...it works well with Feisty as long as you don't need the TV out (which I do).

If you're using a monitor, you'll be able to use it. I did experience some inconsistencies with the video effects in Feisty (the wiggling windows and all). That would mess up the display on MythTV (which wouldn't display properly full-screen). When I turned off those "Desktop Effects" extras, display was back to normal.

So--if you're considering MythTV, I'd shy away from the 9250...I'm looking to replace mine with an nVidia card.

### Post by MikeEvans on 2007-05-01
I have a Radeon AIOW 9800 and had no problems with Edgy or Feisty.  The default driver works great.  I tried loading the ATI provided driver but its not compatible with VNC which I use daily.  With feisty the desktop effects work great, but I have to keep them off due to conflicts with VNC.  I have not tested video-in yet.  I have tried some basic 3D games like neverball and things look good.  I do get some static in the sound when 3D scenes are being rendered.  In windows that would mean IRQ conflict but I haven't researched it yet since I'm not gaming.  My understanding is that driver support is better for older cards so you should be fine.
