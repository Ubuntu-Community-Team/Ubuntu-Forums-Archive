### Post by Coelocanth on 2006-02-21
[Here](http://www.ss64.com/bash/) is a good place to start on the commands.

[Ubuntu Guide](http://easylinux.info/wiki/Ubuntu) (unofficial).

[Terminal for beginners](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=73885&highlight=linux+commands).

[Tuxfiles](http://www.tuxfiles.org/) (Linux newbie help).

[Linux Newbie Guide](http://www.linuxnewbieguide.org/chap10.php)

[Ubuntu Wiki](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UserDocumentation)

That should give you a decent start (these are among some of the many sites I've been perusing). Try a google seach and don't be afraid to follow links on the sites you find. That's how I found most of the stuff I've been reading.
