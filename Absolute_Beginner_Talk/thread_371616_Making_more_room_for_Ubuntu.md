### Post by actuary286 on 2007-02-27
Unfortunately, Vista is not well supported by third-party partitioning tools at the moment. The partitioning tool used by Ubuntu is [GParted]("http://gparted.sourceforge.net"), which is also available on a standalone liveCD. I'd keep an eye on future releases of GParted for full Vista support if Microsoft's built-in tool isn't cooperating.
