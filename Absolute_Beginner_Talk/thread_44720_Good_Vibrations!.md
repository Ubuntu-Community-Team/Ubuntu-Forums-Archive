Hello All,
I just had to pop in here and tell everyone Thanks for all the help the more seasoned Linux people give!
When I first delved into the Linux world I tried out Ubuntu...
I was able to most of the things that I wanted, but still wanted to see what else was out there so I tried out a couple of other distros, learning a little more along the way.
The last distro I ended up with was Fedora Core1, and I must say that through my experiences with other distros, that Ubuntu is the king (at least IMHO) :-P 
As soon as I got off my lazy butt and ran Cat5 to my linux box (it's a desktop PC), and quit banging my head at getting my USB wireless adapter to work, I have been extremely pleased!
So far I have had to modify my /etc/fstab to mount Remote folders from my XP box to listen to music, and to view my pictures...
My desktop background is even a picture that resides on my other PC in the other room, how cool is that!
This has allowed me to also use MPlayer to watch movies, streamed through the network!
I even set up a network printer that is connected to my XP box in another room of my house!
I am totally an evangelist now!  Everyone ought to try Linux!
I mean, why not?  It's secure, it's fast, it's free!

Thanks, everyone.  I couln't have done it without you! :grin:
