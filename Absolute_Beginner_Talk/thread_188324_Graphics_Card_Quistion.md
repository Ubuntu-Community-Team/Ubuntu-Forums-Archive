### Post by u.b.u.n.t.u on 2006-06-04
Don't get a Radeon 9550!!!

Get a Nvidia 6600GT!  Make sure it is GT. I had a Radeon 9600Pro before that and it died on me. Radeon 9550 is even older!

The Nvidia 6600GT is an excellent card for the price. Google the history. It was the all the rave when it came out. The Radeon 9550 was never anything special.

PS

Don't be fooled by the "256MB DDR" tag. The performance increase is small over that of a 128MB version. You need a whole other card to notice better performance.  The 6600GT leaves the 9550 eating dust and the price difference isn't much!
