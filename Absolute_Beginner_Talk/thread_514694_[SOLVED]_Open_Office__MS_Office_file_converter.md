### Post by tashmooclam on 2007-08-01
Openoffice can open anything and save as anything, it's great.
I suggest you look into (free) googledocs. 
You can upload the spreadsheet. Then go online and look at it from anywhere. Then you can download it anywhere if needed.
Or, one could simply create the thing in googledocs in the first place.
You can also share it with anyone anywhere. You can allow them to just see it or edit it also. 
Of course the same applies to text documents. 
I very often edit word documents for others. They create them in Word and upload them to their googledocs.
