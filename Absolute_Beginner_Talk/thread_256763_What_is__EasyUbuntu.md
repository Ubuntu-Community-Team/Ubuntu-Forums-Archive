### Post by Metacarpal on 2006-09-13
EasyUbuntu is a helper application created by Ubuntu users.  It can be used to quickly set up multimedia codecs (including mp3 and w32 codecs), DVD viewing, Acrobat Reader, Flash, Java, etc.  [Here's their website]("http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org").

[Automatix]("http://www.getautomatix.com") and [BUMPS]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=143") are two other apps to do the same, or similar, tasks.
