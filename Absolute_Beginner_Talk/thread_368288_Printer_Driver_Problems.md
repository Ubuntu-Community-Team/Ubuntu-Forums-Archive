### Post by houstonbofh on 2007-02-26
HP has the best Linux support.  Lexmark has the worst.  However, since they publish some drivers (that no one can get to work) they are able to keep up the lie about Linux support.

Also, all-in-one solutions have the most trouble.  Many of them only work as printers, or have reduced functionality.

Do your research at [http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting/Database/DatabaseIntro](http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting/Database/DatabaseIntro)
