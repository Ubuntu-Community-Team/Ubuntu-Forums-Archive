### Post by PaulFXH on 2006-08-02
Hi Stephen
I use the Alt-Shift combination to switch between my two languages (only because, this is what I too used in Windows).

What Spier described is correct.
Once you have the Keyboard Indicator on your desktop panel, you can assign a shortcut through System->Preferences->Keyboard->Layout options->Group Shift/Lock behaviour.
Only a limited number of KB shortcuts are available, but one of them is Alt-Shift.
