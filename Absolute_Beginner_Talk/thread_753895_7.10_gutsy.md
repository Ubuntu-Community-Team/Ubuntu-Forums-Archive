### Post by sayakb on 2008-04-13
Goto System->Administration->Software Sources and there, uncheck the  "Cdrom with Ubuntu 7.10 Gutsy Gibbon" checkbox.
But why do you want to remove that? Unchecking it might reduce the available packages through the repos and/or download every library that may be already there in the CD.
