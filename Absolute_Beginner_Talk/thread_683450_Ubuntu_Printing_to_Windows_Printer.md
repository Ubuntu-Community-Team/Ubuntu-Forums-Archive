### Post by Tenken on 2008-01-31
Go to System->Administration->click the "New Printer" button, under devices select "Windows via SAMBA" and click the "Browse" button you see by the smb://text box select your network, then the computer with the attached printer, then printer. Click OK, Click Forward, select your printer brand,click forward, select your printer model (and driver if there are multiple), click forward. Fill out the name,description, etc. Click Apply and try printing a test page.
