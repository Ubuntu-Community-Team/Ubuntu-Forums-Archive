### Post by jyba on 2007-04-12
Hi, in my experience, when it comes to bash there's no alternative than to chew your way through the Advanced Bash Scripter's Manual.

[http://tldp.org/LDP/abs/html/](http://tldp.org/LDP/abs/html/)

I've read through it three times now and it eventually gives you a lot of knowledge and power. Everything else really only teaches you tips and tricks.

There is also an excellent newsgroup called comp.unix.shell where a small hardcore of shell gurus wrestle irascibly with a tidal wave of googlers. It's entertaining and informative. There's an archive of their old messages here:

[http://www.mailarchive.ca/lists/comp.unix.shell/](http://www.mailarchive.ca/lists/comp.unix.shell/)
