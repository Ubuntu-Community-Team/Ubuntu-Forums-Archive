### Post by taurus on 2006-12-04
Yes.  You can use gparted on the LiveCD to resize your harddrive, leaving some free space at the end to install Edgy on.  And of course, you always need to backup your important files in Windows first before you plan to resize it in case something goes horribly wrong...
