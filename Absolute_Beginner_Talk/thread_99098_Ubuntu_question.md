### Post by Qrk on 2005-12-04
Depends on what you want. The server installation will install on stuff that should have been in the junkyard in 1998. 

For a desktop, I believe XFCE takes 500 mhz and 64 MG RAM. Gnome typicaly wants 800 mhz and 128 MB RAM. That said, I find Gnome quite usable lower specs. After it loads, its not too bad on a 233 mhz PII with 64 MB ram.
