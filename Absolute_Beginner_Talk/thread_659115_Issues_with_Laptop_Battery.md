### Post by shanepardue on 2008-01-05
Ubuntu definitely drains battery faster than XP, but Vista is pretty close to Ubuntu's drain 
time.

As far as a brightness adjustment, you can do it from a panel app called "brightness 
applet" or you can adjust it with your laptop's keyboard shortcuts. There should also be a 
brightness button on your keyboard somewhere that lets you decrease and increase the 
brightness. Most of them have it at least.
