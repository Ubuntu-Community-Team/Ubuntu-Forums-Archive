### Post by Hossie on 2007-06-17
The graphics card insinde your Virtual Machine is always an emulated card, no matter what virtualization software you are using. Except Mac's Parallels there is afaik no virtualization software that can offer you 3d support (and even that is very limited, so you won't be able to play Battlefield oder something...).
