### Post by ARhere on 2007-11-03
Messing with xorg.conf has caused me more headaches then I can count, but my past problems have revolved around incorrect settings.

My best advice is to vim /etc/X11/xorg.conf and make sure you monitor is identified correctly with the right available resolutions.  If not, the correct monitor should be listed under 
System > Administration > "Screens and Graphics".

Also, NVidia closed driver are not bad (this is arguable I know), you might want to uninstall your drivers and try reloading the recommended driver from nvidia.com.

Not sure if this helps, but try checking your xorg.conf settings.

-AR
