### Post by 23meg on 2007-03-05
> **old_geekster said:**
> Will the update process be something for we newbies to fear?:confused:

Theoretically, no, it's very easy to do. However, things may go wrong in the process, the most widespread causes being closed source drivers and careless use of third party repositories. If things do go wrong, you can find help here and most likely solve the issues. If you *have to* rely on the upgraded OS being functional immediately, without assistance though, I'd advise differently.

If you have a separate home partition, it's also very easy to do a reinstall and keep your application data intact. That method spares you the possible headaches of an upgrade.
