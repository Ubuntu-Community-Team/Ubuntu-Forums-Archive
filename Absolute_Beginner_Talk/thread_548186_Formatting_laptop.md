### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-09-11
You need to go into your BIOS settings and change the boot order, so that it will boot from your CDROM before your hard drive.

Most PC's use a function key that you press when the boot screen comes up, before your OS starts to load.

You may need to check your system motherboard manual to find out the correct key sequence to get into the BIOS settings.

### Post by jw5801 on 2007-09-11
Booting from a CD will work even if the harddrive is blank (ie. you have no OS installed)... It's completely irrelevant what's on your hard-drive if you're booting from something else. So no, Ubunutu is not stopping you from booting from a CD, chances are your CD drive is faulty if you can't read from any CD's before or after the OS boots.
