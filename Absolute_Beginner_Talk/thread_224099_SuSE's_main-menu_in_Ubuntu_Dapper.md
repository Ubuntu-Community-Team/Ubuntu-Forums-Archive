Hello,

I just want to share this info.

I have seen a practical new "main menu" in the recent SuSE Linux distribution. Take a look at [this picture...]("http://shots.osdir.com/slideshows/original.php?release=700&slide=6")

This guide shows howto install it in Ubuntu (thanks to Scott Angel, USA):
[http://angelicpenguins.blogspot.com/2006/07/sled-menu-for-ubuntu-uslab-now-in-repo_14.html]("http://angelicpenguins.blogspot.com/2006/07/sled-menu-for-ubuntu-uslab-now-in-repo_14.html")
Follow the installation instructions.

Then right-mouse-click on the Ubuntu's toolbar panel, select  "Add to panel..." from the menu and drag & drop the "Main menu" item onto the panel. 
Here is a picture:

[[img]http://bildr.no/thumb/7009.jpeg[/img]](http://bildr.no/view/7009)

This is how to add more applications to "Favourite Applications" list in the menu.

Launch the "Main menu" and push the [More Applications...] button.  A large, categorized table of apps appears. Select an item from the list and press right-mouse-button.  Clink on "Add to Favourites".
