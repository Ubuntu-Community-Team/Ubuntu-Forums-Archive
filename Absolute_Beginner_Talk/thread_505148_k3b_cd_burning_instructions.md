### Post by marcusplanet on 2007-07-20
Hello!

Grip is not a program directly related to CD burning. It is a program that rips and encodes audio CDs, turning them into a collection of files in specific formats.

From what you have told me, I believe you have on your hard drive a collection of files with the mp3 extension that you would like to burn onto CD-R's using a burning program such as k3b?

I would startup k3b specifying 'create data disk' and then drag onto the lower window the specific mp3 files you wish to burn.  Then click 'Burn'

If your burn is successful you should be able to play this disk in most CD players.

Is this information helpful?

Thank you for your question!
