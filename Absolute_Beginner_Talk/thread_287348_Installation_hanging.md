### Post by meng on 2006-10-28
Desktop has a live try-before-you-buy (er, I mean install) desktop, while the Alternate CD has an installer only. The installer on the Alternate CD is text-based, but is just as intuitive as the installer on the Live CD. The end-result after successful installation is the same.
