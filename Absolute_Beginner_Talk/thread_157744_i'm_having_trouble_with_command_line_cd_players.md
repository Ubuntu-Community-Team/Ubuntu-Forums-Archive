### Post by xmastree on 2006-04-10
xmms playing audio CDs, or mp3?
As dcstar said, some programs use analogue, some digital. The digital is via the ribbon cable, which must be there or the CD wouldn't work at all. The analogue, maybe not. MP3 playback will always be via the digital connection, as the computer has to decode it. Audio CDs can be played by the drive itself.

Does yours have the small cable fitted?
