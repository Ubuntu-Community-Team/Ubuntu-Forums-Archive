### Post by mcduck on 2006-06-28
It seems like you burned the disk the wrong way.

You need to 'burn image to disk', not 'burn a bootable cd' or anything like that.. Your disk isn't now booting Ubuntu at all, it boots DrDOS that your burning app put to the disk to make it bootable.
