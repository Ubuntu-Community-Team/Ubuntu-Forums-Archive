### Post by Atreus12 on 2006-11-29
I messed this up the first time, the way it is worded is confusing imo. You are choosing what size to resize your partition to. For example, you have an 80 GB hard drive with windows on it. You want to resize this to 20 GB and put linux on the rest (60GB). You would move the slider to 20GB.

Edit: Option 1 will happen.
