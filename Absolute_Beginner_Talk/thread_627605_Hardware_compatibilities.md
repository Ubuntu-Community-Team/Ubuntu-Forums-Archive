### Post by asmiller-ke6seh on 2007-11-30
Why not start by putting up your components list, here, and then see what people say. That might be easier. You will get all kinds of neat information like "Works out of the box," or "There's a loadable kernel module for that which I used to get it to work, and here's where it is and how to compile and load it."

You might want to choose a more descriptive TITLE for your post like, "Are these components Ubuntu compatible?"

You will find lots of folks here who have lots of experience with Ubuntu, specifically, and Linux, generally. You will also find (if you haven't already) that folks here like to help and the more information you can provide the more helpful assistance you can receive.
