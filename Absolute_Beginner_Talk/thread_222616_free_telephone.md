You can use voipdiscount.com for free for the first 10 or 15 calls.

Afterwards, you need to put 10$ credit in your account for calls to paying countries (50+ countries are FREE) (other countires are only 1ct). This credit is valid for 4 months. You get a free phone number where people can call you, it will ring on your phone or PC.

This means, that if you only call the free countries, you can call for as long as you want for 2,50$ per month.

I have been using them for several months, calling to the US, Europe and Asia. You just call how much you want.

You can also use your web interface to call you on any phone and connect the call to another phone. You can send sms too.

So yes, this is the best phone-to-phone/PC-to-phone/PC-to-PC service in existance. Skype and the others are much too expensive in comparison.

You have a problem with 2 and a half bucks per month to use the service ? I call this free.

If this is not good for you stop looking, because you wont find anything better if you want to call regular phones (and cell phones in many) for free in 50+ countries
