### Post by oodledoodle on 2008-01-21
d3d is directx, which will have some problems in wine as its primarily designed for use in windows. gl is the linux equivalent, and for most linux users will give the best performance results. along the line wine will support directx better, but for now gl is probably your best bet. sorry i cant get much more technical than this (im not a games/graphics expert) but when my brother was trying to get world of warcraft working he had to use gl just to get the game to be able to run.
