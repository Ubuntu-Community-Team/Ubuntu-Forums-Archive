### Post by jenred on 2007-07-09
It really depends on what sort of functionality you are looking for.  I recommend making a list of what you want from a CMS system and then poke around and see if Joomla or Drupal is a better option.  Also visit the forums to get a sense of the community.

I've run both Drupal and Joomla and don't believe there are any major differences.  It really comes down to personal preference with regards to extensions/templates/plugins that are available.
