### Post by Anonii on 2006-09-01
> **jesus of suburbia said:**
> like these ones [http://art.gnome.org/themes/metacity/](http://art.gnome.org/themes/metacity/) just that on that site people say they made them from GIMP ifnot how do you make buttons?
I'll show you how to install one in GNOME. I hope you have GNOME ^_^

Download this:
[http://art.gnome.org/download/themes/metacity/1276/MCity-Almond.tar.gz](http://art.gnome.org/download/themes/metacity/1276/MCity-Almond.tar.gz)


Then go to "System -> Preferences -> Theme"

and drag and drop the .tar.gz package you downloaded in that list. 
Now go to "Theme Details" find it, and apply it.
