### Post by Woodgar on 2007-01-03
From the command line you can type

which evince

and find that the main program is located at /usr/bin/evince

Or you could try

whereis evince

to find locations of config files/man pages and the like

Or you could go all the way with

locate evince

which will give you all references to evince that can be found.

Good luck. :)

or do as suggested in previous post by woodgar.
