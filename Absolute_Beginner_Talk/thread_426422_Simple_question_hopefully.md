### Post by irjason on 2007-04-30
In Firefox 1.5 I used the Smoothwheel extension. It does not work with Firefox 2. I found your post while I was looking for a solution. Then I found the answer for Firefox, so I thought I would share.

"if you want to increase the scroll speed in Firefox, point your browser to: about:config, enter 'mousewheel.withnokey' in Filter field, then double-click on 'mousewheel.withnokey.sysnumlines' to change its value to false and adjust the scroll speed by changing 'mousewheel.withnokey.numlines' to desired value (the higher the value, the faster the pages scroll)."

I changed it to 6 and it seems to work good for me.
