Oh. I misunderstood. I don't know of any way to do that. There is a good tag editor that lets you do batch processing which would make it a little faster than EasyTag by at least letting you do the common tags for all the songs off of one cd. It is called tagtool.
>  What are the relative merits of flac vs mp3 vs ogg vs wav?
flac is lossless compression. mp3 is the most common. ogg is similar to mp3 but free (as in freedom, no licensing required) and will compress a bit smaller. wav is lossless, but not compresses so the file will be really big.

Portable music players are likely to play mp3. Some will play ogg, and some wll play flac. wav is is too big to be a real consideration for that but is good for recording and editing and some other stuff.
