### Post by the.dark.lord on 2007-10-01
Ubuntu isn't something I would recommend for somebody without an internet connection. Codecs such as mp3 aren't supported by default due to the open source nature of this OS. I recommend that you download this player - [http://www.videolan.org/vlc/download-ubuntu.html-](http://www.videolan.org/vlc/download-ubuntu.html-) somewhere you can get the internet, burn it on a CD and install it on your comp.
