---

### Post by Hallvor on 2007-12-09
Slot allocation is how much bandwidth is directed to a single upload slot to a user. Say, if you have a bandwidth upload of 100 KB/s, slot allocation of 2 on each slot will open some 50 slots to upload to different users simultaneously.

I recommend setting the number to something like 15-20 in this case, though.
