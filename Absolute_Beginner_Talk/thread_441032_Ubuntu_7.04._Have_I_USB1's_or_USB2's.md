### Post by Roadrunner777 on 2007-05-12
I'm not much further along than you... I can answer the first part though.  Go to System, Preferences, and Hardware Information.  Scroll down the list.  I have USB 1 and USB 2.0 ports on my machine... The USB 1 is listed as just USB.  The USB 2.0 is listed as... USB 2.0.

I can take a wild guess at the second part... I don't think a USB 1 driver will work on USB 2 ports, so I am guessing that your ports are set up correctly and you have network driver issues.  That's a guess though.
