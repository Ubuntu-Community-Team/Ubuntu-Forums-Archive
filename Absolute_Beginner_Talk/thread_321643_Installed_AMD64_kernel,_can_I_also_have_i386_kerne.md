### Post by pay on 2006-12-19
You can dual boot with 2 installations of Ubuntu. Also you can install 32bit programs in an amd64 installation with dpkg --force-architecture my.deb. Look up 32bit chroot, dual booting amd64 and i386 and installing 32bit firefox.
