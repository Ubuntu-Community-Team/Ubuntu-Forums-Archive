### Post by jISh on 2006-09-01
Nice system :D
Well two obvious things come to mind here, first of all, when you burned the disc, did you select Burn Image to Disc from your software, or just copy the .iso over? You need to burn it as an image, so the actual contents of the disc are replicated on yours.

Next, check your BIOS and make sure the CD-ROM is first in the boot priority list. You can usually enter your BIOS by hitting Del or something similar right at the beginning of boot up.
