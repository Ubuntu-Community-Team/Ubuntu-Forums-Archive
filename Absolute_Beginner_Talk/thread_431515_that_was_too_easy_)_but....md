### Post by tact on 2007-05-03
I think he has already added the network monitor thing from "add to panel".  Thats not the one he is after.  (so its safe to remove that one)
You need to have the "Notification area" added to a panel to see the networkmanager applet (and some others too).   You add that in by right clicking on the panel >add to panel>notification area (its in the utilities section).

networkmanager displays in that notification area.   you might have to log out and log back in to see the result.
