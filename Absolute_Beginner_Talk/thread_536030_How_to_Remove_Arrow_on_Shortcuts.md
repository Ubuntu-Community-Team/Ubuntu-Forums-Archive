### Post by dwhitney67 on 2007-08-27
Out of curiosity, how are you creating your shortcuts?  When I create a (symbolic) link to another file, the arrow on my icon points the other direction.  Ditto when I drag-drop an icon from one of my gnome pull-down menus.

Anyhow, I can't help you remove the arrow, but I do know how to change the image on the icon.  Just right click on the icon and select properties.  Then within the dialog box that appears, select the icon box just to the left of the file name.  Then you can choose your own image or one of the countless ones available under the 'pixmaps' folder.
