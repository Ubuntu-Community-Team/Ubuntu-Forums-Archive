### Post by mahy on 2007-03-31
First of all, if you wanna use a mouse, you DON'T HAVE TO disable your touchpad AT ALL. If you absolutely need to disable the touchpad (for whatever reason), you can do it in the /etc/X11/xorg.conf. I've got a Synaptics touchpad and i know how to disable that one, but i've got no experience at all with ALPS touchpads. You'll have to google it, if no one else responds. Just forget about disabling it in BIOS.
