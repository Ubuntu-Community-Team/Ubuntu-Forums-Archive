### Post by philinux on 2007-11-01
I was browsing for kde sound and came upon this config file 

/home/philcb/.kde/share/config/knotifyrc

Contents
[StartProgress]
Arts Init=false
KNotify Init=true
Use Arts=false

I just changed the two Arts entries to true. Now full sound notification.  
This is an excellent IM with webcam support.
