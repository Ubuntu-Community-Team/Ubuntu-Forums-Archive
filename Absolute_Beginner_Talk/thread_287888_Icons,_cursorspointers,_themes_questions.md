### Post by catlett on 2006-10-29
To change pointers go to the top panel and enter System<Preferences<Mouse There is a pointer tab.
For themes, go to System<Preferences<Themes Here you can change icons, window borders and controls of windows.
There are 2 sites with themes etc for gnome. GnomeART [http://art.gnome.org/](http://art.gnome.org/) and GnomeLook [http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/)
