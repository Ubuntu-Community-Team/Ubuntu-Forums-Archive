### Post by emarkd on 2008-01-27
No, I mean to open it with GIMP.  That's a piece of software that's installed by default in Ubuntu.  When you right-click on your .gif, click on OPen with> and select "Open with GIMP Image Editor.

After it opens in GIMP, click on the File menu > Save as... and save it as a .jpg.
