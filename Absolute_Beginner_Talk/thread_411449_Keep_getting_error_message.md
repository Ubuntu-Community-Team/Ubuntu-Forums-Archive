### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-04-16
To disable automatic updating, open Synaptic (if the updater is not running) and click on "Settings-->Repositories" and on the second tab (Internet Updates) uncheck **Check for updates automatically** (or change how often).

As for broken dependencies, to list them I think you press the **Status** button on the lower left of Synaptic and there will be an entry for Broken Packages; to fix them, go to "Edit-->Fix Broken Packages"
