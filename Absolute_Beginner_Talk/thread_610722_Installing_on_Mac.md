### Post by Paul820 on 2007-11-12
When your computer boots up there should be some text appear telling you to press one of the f keys to enter your bios, usually f2, f11, or some other one, they are all different. When you get into the bios you have to look for the boot order and set the cd rom to the first one to boot from. Just move it up the list.
