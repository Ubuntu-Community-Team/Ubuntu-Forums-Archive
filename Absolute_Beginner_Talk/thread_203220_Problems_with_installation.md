### Post by siccness on 2006-06-24
[QUOTE=xaviusshadow]I really don't have a bank account to do that either. My friend said I could mount the iso on a virtual drive, but I don't know how to go about that.[/QUOTE]

You can only mount ISOs on a virtual drive in Windows, so when you go to install Linux the drive won't be there. So unfortunately, your best three choices at the moment are:

Ordering from Amazon
Ordering from ShipIt
Finding a friend with a CD-Burner, and burning it.

OR
Finding a linux user group in your city who are willing to burn a copy of Ubuntu for you, all you'll have to do is pick it up. (unless they're real nice and drop it off for you) :)
