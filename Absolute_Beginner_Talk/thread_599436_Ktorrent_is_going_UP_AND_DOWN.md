### Post by ddrichardson on 2007-11-01
It's most likely that you aren't hitting the "sweet spot". Every time you recieve a packet you upload a packet - there needs to be a balance between the two - in most cases this is typically around 25%: i.e. I use 111kbps download and 26kbps upload and get a consistent download speed.
