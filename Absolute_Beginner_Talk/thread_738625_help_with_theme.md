### Post by ph1 on 2008-03-28
Metacity, GTK, etc. all use "engines" to render your windows, as emerald does to render your window decoration.  You can probably drag and drop into the appearance window, if not, then you can probably click the "install" button and go find it.  Once you've installed the engine, then you should be able to (re)install the theme and use it perfectly well.
