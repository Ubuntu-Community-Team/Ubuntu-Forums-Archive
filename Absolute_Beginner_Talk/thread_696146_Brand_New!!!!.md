### Post by Vadi on 2008-02-13
Hey! Welcome to the easy world.

Forget about the downloaded file. Go to Applications - Add/Remove instead. In the top-left corner, make sure it says "All avialable applications" (change it if it doesn't).

Then, search for "banshee". The first thing that'll come up is the Banshee Music Player, I assume you want that. Check off the checkbox in there, and click on "Apply Changes". Banshee will be automatically downloaded, and installed for you.

You can then find it in applications - sound & video. 

Enjoy!
