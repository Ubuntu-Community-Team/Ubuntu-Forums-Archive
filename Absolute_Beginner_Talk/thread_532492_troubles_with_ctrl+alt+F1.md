### Post by rolf-c on 2007-08-23
Well, this is likely a driver issue.  Just for kicks, why don't we drop your resolution down a bit and see if that has any impact?  

Edit your xorg.conf file and change

```
SubSection "Display"
		Depth	24
		Modes		"1920x1200"
	EndSubSection
```

to

```
SubSection "Display"
		Depth	24
		Modes		"1024x768"
	EndSubSection
```

Now, this is going to be pretty brutal to look at but it won't tax your video like 1920x1200 does.  Worst case you'll get the same kind of result going in and out of X, and you can edit your xorg.conf file and set the resolution back to 1920x1200.  Your desktop icons may be out of place but you can rearrange them to your liking.

Make your changes (you must be root to edit xorg.conf so use sudo), restart X and let us know what kind of results you get.
