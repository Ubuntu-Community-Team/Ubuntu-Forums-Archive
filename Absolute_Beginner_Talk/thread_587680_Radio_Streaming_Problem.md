This is just a stab in the dark, but you could go into Synaptic Package Manager and install Streamripper and Streamtuner. They are listed one above the other. Also, XMMS to play the music on. When you check out XMMS, look on down the list for some additional add-ons for this player that may help you. Streamripper/tuner allows streaming audio on your computer and has a lot of streams to tune to.  It may provide the codecs to let you play the stream you want.
