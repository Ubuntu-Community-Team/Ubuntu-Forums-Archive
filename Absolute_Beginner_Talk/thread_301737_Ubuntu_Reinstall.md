### Post by nickpaton on 2006-11-17
For me never - except when I was learning, it was about 3 times a day!

But the last time was when going from Dapper to Edgy when it somewhat backfired on me.  I'll not bother now until the next release, but if the upgrade works OK, then not even then.

Linux Files don't suffer from bloat like Windows ones, so there's no reason to reinstall at regular intervals.

What may be a good idea is to create a new separate FAT32 partition, which you treat rather like Windows My Documents area.
So if something goes wrong, there's no danger of losing anything.
