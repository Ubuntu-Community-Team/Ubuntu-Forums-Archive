### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-08-22
Hi. Do as Dark Star said: open a terminal (Applications->Accessories->Terminal), in it go to "Edit->Current Profile->Colors", un-check **Use colors from system theme** and change **Built-in schemes** to "White on black".
