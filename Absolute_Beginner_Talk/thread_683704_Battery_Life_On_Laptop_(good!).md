### Post by LowSky on 2008-01-31
you seem to be having the oposite of what many ubuntu laptop users are saying. many have issues with battery time.

Some things to try are setting your BIOS settings to use a minimal of your processor (stepping technology), ubuntu I think has a  application that can be added to the panels to also watch how much of hte processor is bwing used. 

Also if you lower the contrast and brightness settings you will gain anywhere from 10-30 minutes more battery life. Also set your power management so that the screen and hard drive turn off when not being used.

Those are some quick things I can think of
