### Post by sayakb on 2008-04-20
GMA950 can run everything very smoothly. I have a laptop with GMA950 and another with nVidia 8600 M GTS. When equipped with the proprietary drivers (through the restricted driver manager), the 8600GTS works slower than the GMA950!! GMA950 will not give you 2 features: Decoration blur and reflection, and mipmaps. Everything else works very smoothly (specially on a 2GB RAM like you mentioned)
