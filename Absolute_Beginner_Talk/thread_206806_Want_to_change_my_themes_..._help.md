### Post by ahaslam on 2006-07-01
I found a rather nice theme on there, [have a look]("http://www.xfce-look.org/content/show.php?content=38852"). There's loads on there, I'm sure you'll find something to your liking.
Once downloaded, extract and place the folder in /home/username/.themes (it's a hidden folder). You should then be able to select your new theme within the theme manager.
