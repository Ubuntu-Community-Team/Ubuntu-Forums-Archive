### Post by Hospadar on 2007-09-02
You probably need to enable your universe/multiverse repositories, you can do this in synaptic, there are a few good pages in the community docs on how to enable these.  search for "enable universe repository" and you'll find em.

Also, the versions of wine in the ubuntu repos are usually a little older, I would suggest using the direct wine repos as directed by amneziac's link.

As for guild wars, have a look in the wine appdb here: [http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iAppId=2243](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iAppId=2243)

the appdb is probably the best place to start figuring out how to get something to work.
