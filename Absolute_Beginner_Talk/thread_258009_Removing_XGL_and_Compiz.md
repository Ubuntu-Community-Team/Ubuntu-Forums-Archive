### Post by bulldog on 2006-09-15
There's nothing wrong with your xorg.conf.

Well you removed your gdm.conf-custom so all your customization what's put in there is gone too.
You should make the changes again I think.

There's a hidden file called 'gdm.conf-custom~' in your gdm folder.
You could cp that.

sudo cp /etc/gdm/gdm.conf-custom~ /etc/gdm/gdm.conf-custom

This should bring an empty file back for your customizations.
