### Post by Terl on 2007-04-23
Open synaptic package manager.  In the menu there is a selection for repositories.  Open it and make sure they are all selected.  OK it all and then hit the reload button. 

It is not the "restricted drivers manager" as such but what I gave you will allow you to access the drivers.  You need to have all the repositories checked.  Sprok3t is correct as the nomenclature is new to Feisty.  It sounds as if you are looking for your video drivers though.
