There are many mp3player in the repos list but Ubuntu/Kubuntu comes with the default (Rhytym Box and XMMS). You can have the option of installing Beep Media Player or Amarok by installing it from Kynaptic/Synaptic.

The movie player that comes std with the package is Totem but you can also have the option of getting Xine, Mplayer or GXine (it will all go down to your preference - on what you want and what works well for you)

Getting K3b (not K3D) is thru Kynaptic (if your using Kubuntu). Just update your repo list and select if from the list.
