### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-04-19
No, iptables IS your firewall.

GardDog and other programs, like Firestarter are merely GUI's to enable easy manipulation of iptables.

If you are behind a router, you are far better off leaving things alone.

By default, all ports are closed and your system is safe.
