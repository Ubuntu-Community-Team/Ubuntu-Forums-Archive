### Post by taurus on 2006-12-31
You can use gparted from the LiveCD to erase the master drive first before you install it.  And once you've installed Ubuntu on the master drive, you can then format and mount the slave one later...

### Post by gh0st on 2006-12-31
There is a great program called Gparted you can get for free. It will do almost anything you want with your disks :) 

Just download it from here - [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php)

Boot the live CD and you use the inbuilt Gnome frontend to sort out your disks.

It's a sweet program :)
