### Post by Lanky Juggler on 2007-03-12
Is the stuff you're trying to play bought from iTunes Music Store?  They have DRMs (digital rights management, or a form of encoding they use to make sure no one doesn't use the file who isn't signed onto your account) and will not generally play in Linux.  Try playing a song that you got from a cd or is in .mp3 format.

If that doesn't work either, you probably need to get some restricted drivers (they'll help you play more kinds of files) [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats#head-99259e1841e1e1262f4f71e0c72d5a51b3fb69e9]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats#head-99259e1841e1e1262f4f71e0c72d5a51b3fb69e9")
