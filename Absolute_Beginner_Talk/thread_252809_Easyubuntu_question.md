### Post by jordanmthomas on 2006-09-07
EasyUbuntu doesn't install itself.  You extract what you download into a folder labeled 'EasyUbuntu' and inside lies a python script you run.  Look in your home folder and see if it's there.  If it's not, you can just follow the directions at their site again and rerun it then.
