### Post by thavard on 2005-12-17
Last week, I contacted this site about the fact the screen resolution of my old Toshiba 2655DX Laptop would only default to 800 by 600.. I followed all of the recommendations on the forum and wiki pages. But nothing worked.. Finally
I realized that by changing the depth from 24 to 16 bit. I was mimicking the Windows setup.. So if everything has been tried and and you cannot get the resolution right and theres no info from toshiba follow the wiki page on screen resolution and then set back depth..
