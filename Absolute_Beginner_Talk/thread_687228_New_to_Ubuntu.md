### Post by orange2k on 2008-02-04
A good way to start exploring Linux is to try some LiveCD.
Ubuntu may be a good choice, but there are many other. One of my favorite LiveCD-s is Knoppix.
Knoppix (especially the DVD-edition) comes with a LOT of programs you can try.
Ubuntu comes with a lot of software, too, but additional software becomes available only after  you install Ubuntu on your hard drive.

After you get a hang of it, you may want to learn something about the command line in Linux - you will come across that sooner or later. so its probably good to learn the basics: [http://www.linuxcommand.org/](http://www.linuxcommand.org/)
