### Post by Adamant1988 on 2007-04-20
Your issue is that there are no options available to you (channels to join etc.) am I right? 
This does not mean that it is not working.  When you're connected with the server a window should pop up (for instance from "freenode") this is your window to IRC.  From there you can type /join #ubuntuforums (or w/e) and start adding your channels so that they will show up in the GAIM menu. 


However, GAIM is a very substandard IRC client and I just would suggest you try a different one such as X-Chat or Chatzilla.  There are tons of clients out there, but the consensus is that GAIM and IRC do not play nicely.  Chatzilla is a FireFox extension, by the way.  

If you need me to explain anything further please IM me or PM me here on the forums!
