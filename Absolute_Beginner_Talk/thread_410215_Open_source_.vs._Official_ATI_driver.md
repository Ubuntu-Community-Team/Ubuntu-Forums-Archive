### Post by igknighted on 2007-04-15
Typically the 3d provided by the open-source driver isn't in the same league as the binary blob.  On older cards it is very close, but newer cards will struggle with the open driver.  In short, you will get better 3d performance with the binary, but there is no guarantee that is what is wrong with penguin racer.  I say use the open source driver unless there comes something your NEED the better 3d the binary driver provides, mainly out of principle.
