You need to do a lot more reading. The short answer is 'yes', you could save some settings so that next time you started up the livecd your settings were the same. However, it would not be easy. It sounds like you are a complete novice to the world of Ubuntu (nothing wrong with that; we all were at some point). It would not be easy to walk you through this. If you want to experience Ubuntu, while retaining your XP installation, your best bet is to install Ubuntu as a dual boot. The livecd will offer this to you as an option when you choose 'install'. However, you will have to pull the trigger, so to speak, and decide you are ready to commit to installing to your hard drive.

Read on if you're not ready for that yet. Ubuntu (Linux in general) is a whole new world. It depends on what you are looking for.
