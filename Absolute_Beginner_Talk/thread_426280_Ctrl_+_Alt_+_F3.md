### Post by hyperair on 2007-04-28
FYI: Ctrl+Alt+Fn where n is a number from 1 to 6 lead to 6 different terminal screens. Ctrl+Alt+F7 leads to the GUI.

It's excellent for when the Xserver crashes because you can still run so many terminals at the same time.
