### Post by ubnewbie2 on 2007-11-08
When you downloaded it (using Firefox?) did you tell it to save the file?  If you did, and it didn't ask you where to put it, then it went to the default set in Firefox.

Go to Edit>Preferences in Firefox, and make sure the "Main" section is highlighted.  In the middle is the part about Downloads, and you should be able to see where it puts all your downloaded files.

Open that place (directory) in Nautilus (use the "Places" menu in Ubuntu's desktop, and then right-click as I said before.
