### Post by gothelin on 2007-07-01
Sounds like the right way to do it.  One thing though, have you double checked where your templates go?  Check Tools -> Options -> OpenOffice.org -> Paths.  It might not necessarily be where you expect.

Also, even if you can't get Impress to see the templates for some reason, you can still use them.    If you simply open them from Impress, they will open as an untitled new document.  :)
