### Post by Malta paul on 2007-06-24
Hi, I assume you are using the Gnome desktop?
If you right click on your Button and un-tick 'lock to panel' then tick 'move' you can then slide the buttons to where you want. You can also right click on the bar and select 'add to panel' then drag any item you want to the bar.(panel)
You may also drag the bar itself to the side, top or bottom if you wish.
Have fun ;)
