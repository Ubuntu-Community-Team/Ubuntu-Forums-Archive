### Post by kostkon on 2007-10-31
What do you mean when you say that it does not allow you to unmount it? Does it show an icon on the desktop or at your *Computer* folder?

Since you say that *gThumb* asks you to import the photos from the camera, that means that the camera is connected to the PC using [PTP]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Picture_Transfer_Protocol"); it is not mounted.

The only thing you have to do is to turn off the camera and remove it from the USB slot.
