### Post by chrisbaume on 2006-08-19
Specifically, right click the title bar and select 'Special Application Settings...' in the Advanced menu.

In the Geometry tab, enable 'Maximised Horizontally' and 'Maximised Vertically', and select 'Apply Initially'.
