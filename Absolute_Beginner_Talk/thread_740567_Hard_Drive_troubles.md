### Post by Tatty on 2008-03-30
You need to set it in your bios setup.

There will be a hotkey that you need to press within the first few seconds when you turn your computer on.  It should tell you what it is on the screen while the bios is loading

common keys are:
delete
F2
Any of the F keys

When you are in the bios. You simply need to change your boot priority so your CD drive comes before your hard drive...  this way it checks the CD first to see if it is bootable before the HDD
