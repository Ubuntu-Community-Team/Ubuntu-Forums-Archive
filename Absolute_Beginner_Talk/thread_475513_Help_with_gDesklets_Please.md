### Post by jairtrejo on 2007-06-16
Go to System>Preferences>Sessions

Click in new

Fill the name: 

gDesklets

And the command: 

gdesklets

The command, gdesklets, should be all in lowercase

And that's it, you'll have gdesklets running on startup. Hope I helped you. :)
