### Post by Tyke91 on 2007-10-04
just put the blank disc in the cd/dvd rw drive and burn the iso to it (in k3b select "Burn Image") 

it should be bootable afterwards... although, you will want to burn it at the slowest speed you possibly can. the slower you burn, the easier it is for old computers to read it
