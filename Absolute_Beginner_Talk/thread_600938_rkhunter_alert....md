### Post by ofb on 2007-11-02
It means Don't Panic. ;)

That and a few other things rkhunter and chkrookit output are normal warnings for clean machines. The trick is do a search with the warning to find previous posts. When you don't find reassuring results for a particular warning, then you can be concerned.
