### Post by m9ke on 2007-08-30
Advice from a fellow newbie:

1.  Realize this takes time and patience.  You will learn a little at a time, and sometimes make mistakes.
2.  Everyone on this forum went thru #1 above, no matter how smart they sound now.
3.  You can do it too.
4.  Read.  Two books I found useful:  Ubuntu Hacks, and Ubuntu for Non-Geeks.  Both of these show you step by step how to do stuff, at a level appropriate for us newbies.

Welcome!
