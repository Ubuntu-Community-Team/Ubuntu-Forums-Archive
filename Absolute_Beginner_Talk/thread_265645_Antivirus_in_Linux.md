### Post by n0dl on 2006-09-26
Chances are you will not get any viruses on linux unless you are specifically targeted by a script kiddie, or guru you angered on IRC or some forum. The reason for this is because of the ELF binaries. Every OS reads binary differently therefore, a virus or some peice of malware that is compiled to run on windows computers (EXE binaries) have a low/no chance of affecting your precious files. However, if you are paranoid enough to think that you are targeted , or you have a windows partition somewhere you can always try ClamAV
