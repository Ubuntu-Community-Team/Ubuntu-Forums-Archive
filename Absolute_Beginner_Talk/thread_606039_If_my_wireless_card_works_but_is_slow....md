### Post by P4man on 2007-11-08
Are you sure its the wireless connection that is at fault, and not your access point or internet connection ? Try rebooting your router. Try moving closer to your AP, and see if that cures anything ?

If speeds are good with a wired connection, and bad with wireless even if you are sitting next to your AP, then, maybe its the drivers. Or maybe there is another AP on the same channel causing interference (try switching channels).
