### Post by Breezy Badger on 2005-10-29
Oh very sorry Tom

It was a rather silly oversight on my part, but here it is.

When you use GAIM you can go into your account and change your nickname (not sure exactly what field is called).  This does nothing but change the name that you see for yourself when your typing, really I think its rather useless as it does not change your name for others to see.

To change your name for others to see you must go into set friendly name when gaim is open, when you set your friendly name THIS is the name that others are going to see for you.

Rather silly for something that should be so simple, but anyone that is new to GAIM might overlook this, so here is the way to do it
