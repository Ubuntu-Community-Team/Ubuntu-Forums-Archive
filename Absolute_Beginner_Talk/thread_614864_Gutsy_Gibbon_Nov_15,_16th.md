### Post by Joakim Stokland on 2007-11-16
Also, the amount of updates depends on installed hardware. Several entries in the list of updates may belong to the same program, thus causing the list to grow. If you look through the list, you will also see that many of the updates usually are binaries, libraries (the ones starting with "lib").
The release of a new kernel often causes several updates to be released. Nothing to worry about :)
