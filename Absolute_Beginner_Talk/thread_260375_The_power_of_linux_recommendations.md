### Post by A. J. Rimmer on 2006-09-18
"The Official Ubuntu Book" is available from Amazon.  It addresses Dapper and includes the 6.06 LTS live/install DVD.  I would not say it is a "Power User" book but more of an introduction to Ubuntu, so is probably not exactly what you are looking for, although it does specifically deal with Ubuntu rather than Linux in general.  It also discusses the Ubuntu Community and gives some background on Canonical, Ltd.

I also have "Linux for Dummies."  I learned a bit from it, but don't think I would recommend it because it deals more with Fedora, Mandriva, etc.

### Post by fragos on 2006-09-18
Check out Ubuntu for non-geeks at [http://www.nostarch.com/ubuntu.htm](http://www.nostarch.com/ubuntu.htm).  Its available in print or PDF at a reduced cost.  The reviews are good and I liked the sample chapter I looked at.
