### Post by Michiba on 2006-04-24
Hi,

I have a sound blaster live, gave me all sorts of problems with sound and such. I'm no expert but what fixed it for me was changing the multimedia audio settings from ALSA to ESD. 

In GUI System/Preferences/Multimedia System Selector 

Change input and output from ALSA to ESD.


Like I said worked for me and worth a try.

Michiba
