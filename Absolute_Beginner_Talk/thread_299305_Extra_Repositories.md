### Post by CatKiller on 2006-11-13
There's some interesting stuff in there. If you're interested, you could browse packages.ubuntu.com to see what's in there. Or you could just enable the repositories and look at the New category. It's not like enabling them will use much extra hard drive space, and you can check to see which repository you're installing from if you become curious.

### Post by JayTee on 2006-11-13
You can enable them and disable them with a click of the mouse. Just click on the System menu, Administration and Software Properties. You can enable them to get what you want and then leave them disabled until you want something else. You won't get automatic updates for those repos tho but Synaptic will check for updates if you run it with them enabled.
