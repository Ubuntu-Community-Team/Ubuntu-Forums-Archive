### Post by freebird54 on 2007-04-20
The expert installation will allow what you intend - Ubuntu on the separate drive.  You can specify where the partitions and what Filesystems they use.  The multiboot is usually handled by Ubuntu, as Windows tends not to 'play nicely with others' on its own.

Near the top right of the forum page is a search feature.  Many people have asked about dual booting - so if you search for dual boot you will find lots of possibilities, and many set up guide links to assist you!
