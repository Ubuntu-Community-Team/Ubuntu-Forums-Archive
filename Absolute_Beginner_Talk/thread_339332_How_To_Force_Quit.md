### Post by ComplexNumber on 2007-01-15
right click on your top panel, select 'add to panel', then select 'force quit'. that will add the 'frce quit' applet on your panel.

press this whenever you want an application window to die for a misbehaving application (note that it sometimes only kill the graphical part of an application in circumstances when its the GUI that is causing the problem). sometimes its necessary to fire up the system monitor and look for the actual application to kill it.

### Post by alphaomega on 2007-01-15
put your mouse on the handles of your application panel, right click then click on add to panel.  scroll until you see the "Force Quit" icon and click ADD. It will be on your panel now. Anytime you want to force quit, click on it, then click within the window of the app you want to force quit.
