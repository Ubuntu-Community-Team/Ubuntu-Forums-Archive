### Post by Mark_in_Hollywood on 2007-07-26
I just went through a bunch of madwifi stuff.

Turn the computer off. Unplug the power cord. Open the case. Install the card. Close the case. Plug the power cord in. Power the computer.

It should "work", if you have done your homework and checked to see that the wireless card is on the "hardware compatibility list" or HCL. Google the model of the card and HCL. You may have gotten lucky.

As for the rest. Get the card running and then ask for so much help in smaller bites, if you please.
