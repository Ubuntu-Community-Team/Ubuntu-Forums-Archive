### Post by aysiu on 2005-10-09
When those freeze-ups happen, it's usually one or more of these problems:

1. The ISO disk image you downloaded somehow got corrupted (Do you know how to do checksums?)

2. The burn somehow corrupted the disk (Burning at slower speeds sometimes helps)

3. There's a hardware problem.
