The update program was new in Hoary, and it breaks for a lot of people. Maybe in Breezy it will be fixed....its not really needed though. I can't tell you how to fix it (if you were smart enough to debug code, you would be giving me advice) but I can tell you how to update your system. 

Open synaptic:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SynapticHowto?highlight=%28synaptic%29](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SynapticHowto?highlight=%28synaptic%29)

The hit refresh or reload or whatever. This hit upgrade and pick smart upgrade. Then hit apply. Perfectly upgraded system.
