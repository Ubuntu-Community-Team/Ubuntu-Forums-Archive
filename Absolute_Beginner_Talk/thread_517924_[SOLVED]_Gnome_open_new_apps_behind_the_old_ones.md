### Post by steve.horsley on 2007-08-05
Are you touching other applications in the meantime (while your new app is launching)? My understanding is that if you simply launch a new app, it launches in front. But if you click another application (or type on the keyboard or spin the scrollwheel) while waiting for it to open, it will open behind, to prevent the new app from "focus stealing" from the app that you are using in front.
