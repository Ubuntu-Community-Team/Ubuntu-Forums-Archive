### Post by aysiu on 2006-12-04
It doesn't really matter. KDE is KDE. You can customize it however you want.

Usually the difference between distros is just the default wallpaper, applications in the menu, and the applets on the taskbar.

You can change all of those. If you like Ubuntu, just use Kubuntu or *kde-core*.

### Post by Bachstelze on 2006-12-07
