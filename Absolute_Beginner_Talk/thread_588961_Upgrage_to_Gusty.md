### Post by Pumalite on 2007-10-23
Save your data and do a clean install of Gutsy. It comes with compiz-fusion working out of the box. If you have /home in a separate partition; better, you can use it in the new installation; just don't format it. You can also use this prior to installation:

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome)
