After my guess and check work I still havent found out how to make the input box/userlist transparent. I have found out  how to change the color of your message.
To do that just go to 
1.Xchat-preferences
2.Colors
3.In local colors change the #30 color to anything you would like. 
4. Click ok and you will see a result!
