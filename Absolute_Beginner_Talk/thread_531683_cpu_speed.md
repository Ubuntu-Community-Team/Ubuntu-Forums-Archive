### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-08-22
Right click on either the top, or bottom toolbar.

Click on[COLOR="Blue"] Add to Panel[/COLOR]

Scroll down to [COLOR="Blue"]System and Hardware[/COLOR]

Click on [COLOR="Blue"]CPU Frequency Scaling Monitor[/COLOR]

Click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Add Button.[/COLOR]

You now have a CPU Frequency readout on your taskbar
