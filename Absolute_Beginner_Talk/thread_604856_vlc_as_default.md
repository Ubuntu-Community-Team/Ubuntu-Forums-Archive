### Post by Dr Small on 2007-11-06
Right click and select Properties, on the audio / video files that you want to open in VLC, go to Open With and select VLC Player.

---

### Post by evilregis on 2007-11-06
Dr. Small is right.  If you do not see VLC in the list of applications (it will show you all manners of applications -- not just those for music playback) you can click "Use a custom command" and enter "vlc" (no quotes) and "Add".
