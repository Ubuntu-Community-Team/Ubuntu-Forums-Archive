### Post by jordanmthomas on 2007-11-19
Well, it's basically impossible to scale a 100x100 pixel image up to 1024x768 without it looking bad  (think about it and you'll be able to see why...you can't mathematically create details that aren't there while scaling).

You can, however, just center it on your desktop or tile the image.

### Post by PointyWombat on 2007-11-19
Like already mentioned,  low resolution wallpapers will ultimately look like crap, regardless of what you do with them... you should look for wallpapers which match or exceed your current resolution of 1024x768.

Search GIS for 'wallpapers +"1024x768"' for suitable images.
