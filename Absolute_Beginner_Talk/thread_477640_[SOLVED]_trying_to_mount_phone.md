### Post by mactece on 2007-06-24
Fixed it - kind of. After finding some other sources I realised that Ubuntu (or in fact Linux) cannot mount the K608i  as it presents itself as a modem (it appears in /dev as ttyACM0). I think there may be some kind of workaround using OBEXFTP but I don't have the knowledge to understand it.

Anyway I simply went out and purchased a bluetooth USB adaptor (8 british pounds) and was able to send the information that way. 

I was able to get WAMMU to swap information about contacts, etc. using the cable but could not get multisync to pass the information. 

I suspect that overall 'phones without memory cards are going to be a problem to connect by cable.

The end
