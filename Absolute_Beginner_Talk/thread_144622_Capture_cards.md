### Post by paulmilliken on 2006-03-14
[http://www.ati.com/products/catalyst/linux.html]("http://www.ati.com/products/catalyst/linux.html") states:
Q8:  	  Is v4l video capture supported for ALL IN WONDER cards?
A8: 	The ATI Proprietary Linux driver does not currently provide video capture functionality. Video capture support for most ALL IN WONDER cards should be available from the [GATOS]("http://gatos.sf.net/") GATOS project.

However, I'm not sure what the status of the gatos project is.  Hope this helps.
