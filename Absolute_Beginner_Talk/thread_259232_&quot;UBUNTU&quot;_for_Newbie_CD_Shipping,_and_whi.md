### Post by petri on 2006-09-17
**7mm** What is your computer specs? You will need an alternate CD if you have less than 800 MHz CPU (about) and/or 256 MB RAM (about).

An Ubuntu LiveCD requires at least 256 MB to load and an alternate requires at least 128 MB RAM (about) to work when installed on you harddisk.

EDIT: Yes, I do realize there are pc's running with less than 800/128 but thats just what I think the minimun needs are. I have tested alternate 6.06 with 200MHz/96MB and it runs. It takes about 3 minutes to boot, CPU hits the roof directly when something gets started and so on...
