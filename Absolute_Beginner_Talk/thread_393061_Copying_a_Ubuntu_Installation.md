### Post by martinw89 on 2007-03-25
You could use [partimage]("http://www.partimage.org/"), it's available on [SystemRescueCD]("http://www.sysresccd.org/").  However, this would require that the other computer has identical hardware, including a partition that is identical in size to the one on which your Ubuntu is installed now.  And it would still require some minimal configuration after that, such as changing the host name for the computer.  I personally would recommend just installing again separately, but feel free to look into your options.
