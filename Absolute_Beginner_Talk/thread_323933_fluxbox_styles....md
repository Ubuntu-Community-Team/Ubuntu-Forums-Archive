### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2006-12-23
Put them in ~/.fluxbox/styles

or 

/usr/share/fluxbox/styles

Make sure you have an entry in your menu something like this:

[stylesdir] (/home/user_name/.fluxbox/styles)

or

[stylesdir] (/usr/share/fluxbox/styles)
