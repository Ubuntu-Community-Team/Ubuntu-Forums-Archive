### Post by eldragon on 2007-11-26
what are you using to play your avi?

if you are using mplayer, you could add your screen's size to .mplayer/config

so that it calculates the aspect ratio:

monitoraspect=1680/1050

thats what i added to fix my problem.

### Post by rsambuca on 2007-11-26
Virtually all the players, whether it is the default Totem, or others such as VLC, have a setting called aspect ratio.  Just change it to the proper ratio of the video, or usually the auto setting works.
