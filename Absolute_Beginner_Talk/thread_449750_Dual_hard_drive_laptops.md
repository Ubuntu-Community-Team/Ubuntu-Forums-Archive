There was a model that had two internal disks, but I can't remember what it was off the top of my head, I'll do a bit of research and post back.

What you might want to do is check out the accessories section of most of the major manufacturers.  Look for something called a drive bay caddy.  It would replace your DVD drive and allow a second hard drive to be installed easily.
(IBM do this, I'm sure that dell used to, as well as HP.)

We use the modular drive bays for IBM T4x series laptops, as well as the newer T60 series, however the technology changed and we needed a Sata drive bay caddy when the T60 models came out.
