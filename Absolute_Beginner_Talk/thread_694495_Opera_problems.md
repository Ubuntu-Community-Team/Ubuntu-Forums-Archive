### Post by james_xxx on 2008-02-12
The easiest way for you to get your flash working again in Opera, is to go to the Opera download site, and download the Beta version of Opera 9.5 for the version of Ubuntu you are using.

After you have it installed, go into settings and tell it to look for new plugins.

You should then be rocking and rolling again.

The 9.5 beta has been working very well for me.
