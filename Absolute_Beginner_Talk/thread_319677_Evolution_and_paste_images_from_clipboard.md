### Post by ecoxmit on 2006-12-16
reply to my self: a better way to do this! :D 

Use Compiz/Beryl screenshot command: Hold Windows Button, Click and select screen area. This creates a screenshot of that area in the Desktop. Then Drag and Drop from Desktop to email body. 

(You can change the keys and directory configuration in gconf-editor and browse to /apps/compiz/plugins/screenshot)
