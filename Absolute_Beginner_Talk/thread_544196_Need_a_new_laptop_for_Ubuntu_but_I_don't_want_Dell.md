### Post by fastpakr on 2007-09-06
Having played a bit with both Lenovo and Compaq recently, there is one thing that would make me not even consider them as a Linux (or even Windows) machine in the future.  Both of them (as well as HP and possibly others) run a blanket blacklist of wireless devices in their machines.  The ONLY wireless cards you can use are ones that are specifically whitelisted by them, and these are frequently specific models of a common adapter with modified PCI ID's so that only the one manufactured FOR the OEM will work.  In my case, a Compaq F572US would not even boot with anything other than the factory Broadcom 4311 adapter in place.  My wife's Lenovo is exactly the same way.
