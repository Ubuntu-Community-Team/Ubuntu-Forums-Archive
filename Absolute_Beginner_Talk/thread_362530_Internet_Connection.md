### Post by benfindlay on 2007-02-15
First things first, I'd recommend going into System>>Administration>>Networking and checking that the ethernet is present AND that it is enabled. I've known ubuntu do daft things like disabling my sound when it worked fine on the live cd! Took me ages to realise that master-mono was muted! You could have something similar here so try that first!

Hope this helps!
