### Post by isaacj87 on 2007-07-30
Just like the title says...

Qunu, for those who don't know what it is, is one-on-one chat site regarding computing (among other things apparently). You can simply type in a tag (for example, ubuntu) and Qunu.com will list all the online users who can assist you in the area, plus how knowledgeable they are (newbie, user, guru...). You can then select a user and begin a real-time chat with them. Pretty cool stuff... 

If you think you know a thing or two about something found on Qunu.com, you can become a Qunu "expert" and assists others...Apparently, this site is dying. If we can breathe some new life into Qunu, maybe newcomers to Ubuntu who are having personal troubles can receive help in realtime...instead of posting and waiting on the forums only to bump their own threads...I've noticed that many common problems keep popping up on the forums (for example, sound not working, resolution is incorrect...etc...). Many topics keep on getting regurgitated over and over  again. 

I apologize if this is old news, but I know lots of people are trying/switching over to Linux and hopefully this could be helpful for them. 

So, check it out...I'm on Qunu.com as well...and will probably be on often under the screenname isaac_j87. My tags include: compiz, ubuntu, windows, hardware, customization, amarok, music, recording
