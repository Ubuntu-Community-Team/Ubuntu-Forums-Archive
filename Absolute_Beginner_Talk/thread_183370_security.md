### Post by Sef on 2006-05-27
> Do I need a firewall and virus protection? If I do where do I find them? How do I install them?

A firewall is good to have.  I use one.  Just an extra layer of protection.  If you are behind a router with a properly configured hardware firewall, then having firestarter is not really needed.

To download the firewall

First, you need to have the repositories added.   [Adding Repositories]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AddingRepositoriesHowto")

Second, to download firestarter: System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager 

As for virus protection, you don't need it, unless you have a mail client (e.g., thunderbird, Kmail, evolution, etc.)  Then you need it not for you, but for your friends.  A Windows virus won't infect your system, but your friends who have Windows could get infected from a virus sitting on your system.
