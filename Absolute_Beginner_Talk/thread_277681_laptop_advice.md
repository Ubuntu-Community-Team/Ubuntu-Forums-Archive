### Post by junglepeanut on 2006-10-15
All opinion below, sorry I do not have hard facts.

One save money by buying one with out windows installed so you do not need to  throw it away. 

This I am guessing, but stay away from the latest and greatest, and go simple (not a load of media stuff) and everything will be fine.

My gateway 8510gz which I bought a while ago I got cheap from bestbuy because  somebody bought it and returned minus the windows cds and powercord. (1800 dollar laptop went to me out the door for 1100 after taxes.) I have had no issues except the typical ati issues.

At one point I had media key issues but this was from switching to xubuntu where I had to xconfig them myself. They worked fine under kde and gnome.

Try sites like powernotebooks.com for deals without os.
Or a local shop may be able to work a deal for you.

I suggest also checking local fry's and bestbuys for returned items as they may be an awesome deal, for somebody willing to work.
