### Post by asmoore82 on 2007-08-23
I have known Dells in particular to take a _long_ time to bring up the BIOS settings
after removing the hard drive completely.

the BIOS waits for a complete timeout trying to detect the hard drive and even then when
you get into the BIOS screen it doesn't automatically mark the drive as gone,
it just says "unkown device"

turn it on, wait for the keyboard indicator lights to flash, and Hit F1 and F2 just once each
and then give it about 5 minutes to see if the BIOS settings will come up ...

also, if you have a video card make sure the monitor is plugged into the card
and _not_ the motherboard's VGA port.
