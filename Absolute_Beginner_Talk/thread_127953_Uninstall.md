### Post by Kapre on 2006-02-10
[QUOTE=Richa8]I have Ubuntu installed on a laptop and would like to uninstall it without deleting windows.  How do I do it?[/QUOTE]

Richa8 - I dont know why you want to remove Ubuntu but its your choice. If you're dual booting and you set GRUB to use MBR, get your windows CD (bootable) and fixmbr (if you have a bootable diskette - boot into it and type fix mbr). Then when you really want to erase Ubuntu (which I guess is on a different partition), just format that partition.
