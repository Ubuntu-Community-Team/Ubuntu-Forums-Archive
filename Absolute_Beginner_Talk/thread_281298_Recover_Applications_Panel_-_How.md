### Post by CatKiller on 2006-10-20
If you still have a panel at the bottom, you can right-click on it and select New Panel. You can then move the new panel to the top and add the things back in that you had before. The menu isn't especially special, it's just a launcher like all the others. Menu Bar, is the name of it, I believe.

If you don't still have a panel at the bottom... we'll have to think of a different solution for you...
