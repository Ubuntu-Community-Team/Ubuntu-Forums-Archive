First of all, thanks to everybody for Ubuntu, some guy uploaded a video to you tube comparing it with Vista, thanks to him I decided which distro to install and at least gave vacations to my XP, permanently.

I have checked some threats around and did not encountered something that can answer this little bug:

-Nautilus hangs when I try to open a folder, but it does it randomly (5 minutes, 2 hours...).

The way I solve it is trying to open another folder, in system monitor appears then another process of nautilus, but only one of them is working, taking about 50% of the CPU with it. Forcing to terminate that process will take all back to normal.
