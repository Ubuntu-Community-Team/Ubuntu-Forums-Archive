### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-02-19
Take it back to the store and ask them to put it into another computer they have there, and check that it's reporting itself correctly on another computer. It sounds very unusual for the drive to be reporting the wrong information, and to my knowledge the BIOS shouldn't govern whether the operating systems can see it as a burner or as just a reader.

### Post by bumanie on 2008-02-19
Go into hardware information and see what it says there. I don't know about your bios, but some only state that there is a cd-rom even when it is a burner etc. In essence, cd-rom in bios is really saying that bios knows there is an optical drive present. System --> Preferences --> Harware Information (I think is the path, I'm at work, can't check).
