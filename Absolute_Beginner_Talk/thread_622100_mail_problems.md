### Post by pajim17020 on 2007-11-25
I was having a similar problem. I am using evolution with embarq email and it kept saying that I had to log in when I tried to send an email. Go to edit-preferences-select the account. Then under the sending email tab. Under authentication type make sure "login" is selected. I had to enter my email password the first time. Then it worked. I hope this helps.
