### Post by HereInOz on 2007-01-26
I seem to remember that the support period for Dapper is three years for the desktop, and five years for server installations.

Given that the release date for Dapper was June 2006, I would be looking towards the next LTS release, if they do one, at about that time in 2009.  Just a guess, but it seems logical.  Obviously, you will be able to upgrade to any of the later releases, but I believe that the plan is to release a "LTS" release every 3 years.

However, 3 years is a long time in this industry, so do not be surprised if it all changes before then.
