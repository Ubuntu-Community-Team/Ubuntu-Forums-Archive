### Post by TheWizzard on 2008-02-22
relax.
there's no real difference between eMule and aMule. 
just start firestarter and click "stop firewall". if this helps, you'll need to configure firestarter. 
this is easy: when you have amule running, start the firewall and click the events tab. now allow connections that are obviously related to amule. 

if it's not your firewall, it's either your port forewarding or your amule server list.
