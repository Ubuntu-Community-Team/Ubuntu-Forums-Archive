### Post by aysiu on 2005-07-25
Not knowing what exactly happened, I'll still try to offer a few suggestions:

1. Completely uninstall through Synaptic. Then, reinstall through Synaptic.
2. Download the .tar.gz from Mozilla, extract it, then double-click on firefox-installer.
3. Try launching it in safe mode and uninstalling extensions:
[http://kb.mozillazine.org/Safe_mode](http://kb.mozillazine.org/Safe_mode)
