### Post by lamalex on 2008-01-02
Hardy will be a LTS but that doesn't really mean much other than it will be supported longer than the normal 18 months (it will be supported for 3 years). This is really only pertinent for enterprise/business users who upgrade much less frequently than desktop users do. For you, I'd say you're better off tweaking now and if you want to upgrading to hardy when it's released. Your tweaks for wireless should still work after the upgrade.
