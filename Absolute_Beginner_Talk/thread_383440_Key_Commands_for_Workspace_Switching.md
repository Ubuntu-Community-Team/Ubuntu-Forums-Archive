### Post by ardchoille42 on 2007-03-13
Yeah, the System>Preferences>Keyboard shortcuts dialog will allow you to do that. But, be aware that if you switch window managers in gnome, your keyboard shortcuts won't work.. you'll have to set them up in the new window manager.
