FYI, even if you spend 400 on a new motherboard, it has no relation to the cooling of your (potentially) over clocked cpu.

Even buying a retail packed cpu (comes with "stock" heatsink and fan) these are only "technically" rated for cooling a cpu at it's stock speeds. These fans will allow for slight OC'ing, but if you start getting aggressive about it, your gonna be buying another cpu to replace the ones you toast. dig it?

You don't need to worry about cpu envy. The best bet for you is to dive right into overclockersforum and start reading... then start asking questions.
