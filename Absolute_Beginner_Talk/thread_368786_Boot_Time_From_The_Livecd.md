### Post by Skidpad on 2007-02-23
Boot time from the Live cd is all going to depend on your system specs, provided your burn is good.  This ol' laptop (1.8 mobile celeron, 512mb) took at least 5 minutes to boot Ubuntu, but my desktop booted it much faster, just a couple of minutes.  The installed version of Ubuntu works just fine on this laptop however, and boots much faster than XP.

Put your Live cd in, and let it go, give it time, it won't hurt anything.  It's the only way to check out Ubuntu.  You'll be glad you did.

EDIT: just saw your post...the Live cd is definitely slower than a fully installed version; it's just a "test drive".  Let us know how you get on with it.  Welcome aboard.
