### Post by Kateikyoushi on 2006-10-04

### Post by meseleto on 2006-10-04
How do you turn on/off jack sensor?

---

### Post by Kateikyoushi on 2006-10-04
Type alsamixer in terminal, left right key selects devices.
You can mute and unmute with the m key.
