How to do it? have your cradle connected. Let me see (I don't have my ubuntu on at the moment - I'm figuring out how to go online with it), go to systems - preferences, and you should find something about palms. It will ask you a few questions, then plug your palm in the cradle and press hot sync. I think you will need Evolution to be open at the time, so that it will synch your palm data. 

Sorry if this sounds a bit vague, but I'm a newbie myself! Still, I managed to get it to work!;)
