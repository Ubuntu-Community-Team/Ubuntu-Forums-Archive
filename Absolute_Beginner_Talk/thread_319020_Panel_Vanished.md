### Post by Bakerconspiracy on 2006-12-14
If you can right click on the bottom panel, select New Panel, then a new one will be created for you. All you have to do is put everything you had back on it.

### Post by ButteBlues on 2006-12-14
1. Are you using Ubuntu or Kubuntu or Xubuntu?

2. Do you have a bottom panel as well, or was the top panel your only panel?

2a. If you answered "Yes" to 2 and are using Ubuntu/GNOME then you may have just accidentally deleted the top panel. Right click on a blank-spot on your bottom panel and choose New panel. Then you can add the applets and such you had before onto the new second panel.

2b. If you answered "No" and are using Ubuntu/GNOME, copy/paste the contents of "~/.gnome2/session" here.
