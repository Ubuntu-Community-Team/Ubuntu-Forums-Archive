### Post by cjhowe on 2007-05-07
I know this is a much delayed posting, but for the benefit of the community...

You may want to confirm your page settings (I had this same issue).  Printing A4 settings when your paper is letter size will print ~3/4 in lower than you would be expecting.

In America,
Letter Size = 8.5" x 11"
A4 = 8.25" x 11.75"
