### Post by bvmou on 2007-04-25
There is a free package for hp printers called HPLIP that I use and love.  On edgy I sometimes got a CUPS error message with a 5610 all-in-one -- In general I think HPLIP is better than straight CUPS for these printers and HP and Debian have a long relationship.  I think Dapper may have the most solid support if this is a big issue for you.  I have also printed things on unusual stock by just orienting the image on the page, taking dimensions and then placing the paper accordingly.  You can also use this technique to make Linux stickers by placing logos in the white spaces of UPS stickers and cutting away the excess text and images ;)
