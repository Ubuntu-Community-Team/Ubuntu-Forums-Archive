### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-08-26
**netstat -lutwn** will show all the listening internet ports.
**netstat -utwn** will show all the connected ports.

Remove the **n** if you want names instead of numbers.
Anything listening on 127.0.0.1 is listening on the loopback address for connections from within the box, and cannot be connected to fromn the internet so you can ignore those.
