### Post by steveneddy on 2008-02-05
the three main OS's are

1. Linux (many different flavors)
2. Mac (Apple)
3. Unix

Some people think that the toy from Washington State is an actual operating system, but in reality, it was only meant as a novelty and really doesn't count as far as operating systems go. This is why IBM never supported it after 1988 or so.

There are many OS's based on UNIX. Namely BSD and Mac. 

There are other industry only OS's that you will only find in industry that are loosely based on UNIX, but one may never encounter these unless in that business.
