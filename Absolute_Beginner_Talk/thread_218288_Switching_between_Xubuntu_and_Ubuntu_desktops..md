### Post by moma on 2006-07-18
1) Logout first via the System -> Quit menu or just 
press CNTR + ALT + BACKSPACE keys.

2) In the login picture.
Click the "Options" button and select "Select Session..." from the list.

Then choose XFCE as your new desktop and do login.
