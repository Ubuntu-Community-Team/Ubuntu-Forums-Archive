### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-01-12
When you install GPARTED, you will find it under [COLOR="Blue"]System>Administration.[/COLOR]

However, GPARTED has limited use when installed on your hard drive, as you cannot use it to make changes to any mounted drive. (like trying to change a wheel, while the car is still being driven).

A much better alternative is to download the GPARTED LiveCD iso image and burn it to a CD. You then boot from the CD and can make changes to your hard drive.

You can get the GPARTED LiveCD and CloneZilla from here:

[http://gparted-livecd.tuxfamily.org/](http://gparted-livecd.tuxfamily.org/)
