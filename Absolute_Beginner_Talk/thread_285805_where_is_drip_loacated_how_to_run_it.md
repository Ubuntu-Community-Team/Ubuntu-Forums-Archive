### Post by Marsolin on 2006-10-27
I wrote an article that you can find [here]("http://linuxappfinder.com/dvd_rippers1.php") comparing some of the available DVD Rippers. [K3b]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/k3b") and [dvd::rip]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/dvdrip") are my two favorites.

Regarding [Drip]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/drip"), there are two executables, drip and dripencoder. Both have man pages available.
