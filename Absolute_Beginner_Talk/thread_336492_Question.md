### Post by Cathead Fred on 2007-01-11
Hi vexripper,

	Sorry to hear about your problem. I believe you can download software to a thumbdrive from one computer and install it on your computer.  Search the forums on how to do this. 

	 If the Ethernet jack on your laptop does not work anymore there are other ways to get an Internet connection. One way is to buy an Ethernet PCMCIA Card and plug that into your laptop. They go for about 20 dollars.  Or you can try a USB to RJ-45 Ethernet Adapter. They go about ten to fifteen dollars. You can find these cards at computer stores, Amazon, or on Ebay.

Good Luck and post your progress.

Cathead Fred
