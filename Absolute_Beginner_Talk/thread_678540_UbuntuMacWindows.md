### Post by JoshuaRL on 2008-01-26
Well, there's Mac4Lin.  But I haven't used it personally.  And you can make Ubuntu look like both Vista and XP with very little tweaking.  For XP just go into the Window Manager and mess around with the different settings.  I think one of the themes is called Redmond.  :p  For Vista enable transparency and get gDesklets.  Use the sidebar widgets in that app and you're set.
