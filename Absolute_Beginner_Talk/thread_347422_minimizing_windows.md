### Post by jamiehd on 2007-01-27
You can find them again by pressing Alt-Tab

To have them along the bottom like in windows; right click on the menu at the bottom and click add. Then click and drag the window selector where you want it. :)

### Post by 23meg on 2007-01-27
You may have removed the Window List applet from your panel. Right click empty space on your panel, choose "Add to panel" and then "Window List" to add it back.
