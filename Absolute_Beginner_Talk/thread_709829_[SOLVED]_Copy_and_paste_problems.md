### Post by incen on 2008-02-27
Mainly you can do this:
Select the text you want to copy and it will copy for you automatically in Linux, at least Debian, Ubuntu, and Xubuntu. And then press the middle button (the wheel in between) to paste.

However, this is not going to work in gedit, in which what you do is the same as what you do in Windows. Ctrl + c to copy and Ctrl + v to paste. :p
