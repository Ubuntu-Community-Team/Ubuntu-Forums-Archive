### Post by greybirds on 2007-06-01
The transparency is simple. 

Right click on an empty part of the panel you want to be transparent and select "Properties" from the menu that pops up. On the Background tab, make sure "Solid Color" is selected and slide the "Style" slider all the way to the end labeled transparent.

### Post by greybirds on 2007-06-01
Sorry, I misunderstood.

The general tab of panel properties has an "Expand" checkbox that will make the panel stretch the entire screen width/height or just take up the amount of space listed in the "Size" text box. Turn off expand and your panel will shrink to the smallest it can be. You can then drag them around to the sides of the screen with the left mouse button.
