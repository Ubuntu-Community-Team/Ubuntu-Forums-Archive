### Post by 1/0 on 2007-04-05
What desktop manager are you using? It depends on which. KDE, Gnome, XFCE, Fluxbox, WM...

Gnome is standard for Ubuntu. I think its the same as for me in XFCE. In that case you right click on an empty space at the panel and choose properties. There should be an option about the size or width of the panel there.

Each desktop and window manager provides a more or less good documentation. Just check out their homepages. 

Gnome's guide:
[http://www.gnome.org/learn/users-guide/2.14/](http://www.gnome.org/learn/users-guide/2.14/)

### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-04-05

> By default, a panel expands to the full length of the edge of the screen where it is located. A panel that does not expand can be moved away from the screen edges to any part of the screen.
