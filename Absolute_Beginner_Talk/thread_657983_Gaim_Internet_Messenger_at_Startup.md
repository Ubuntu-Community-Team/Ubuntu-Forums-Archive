### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-01-04
Hi. Go to "System->Preferences->Sessions" and on the first tab (Startup Programs) click the "Add" button and fill it out like this:

* Name: Gaim instant messenger
* Command: gaim
* Comment: (you can leave it empty if you want)
