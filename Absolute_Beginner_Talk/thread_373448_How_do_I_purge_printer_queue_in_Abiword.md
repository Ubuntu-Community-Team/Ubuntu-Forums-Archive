### Post by Kobalt on 2007-03-01
Don't you see a printer icon in your notification area (top right of your screen) ? If you do, click on it to see your print queue and delete works from here. 
If you don't see any printer, click System > Pref. > Printing, click on your printer and you should access the queue...
