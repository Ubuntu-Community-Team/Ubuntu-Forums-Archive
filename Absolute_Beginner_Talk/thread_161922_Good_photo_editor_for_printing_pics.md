### Post by Madpilot on 2006-04-18
Have a look at gThumb, it's a photo organizer/viewer app. that prints using the regular Ubuntu printer setup, not GIMP's own internal printer setup. It does resizing, basic editing and a bunch of album/organizing stuff too.

gThumb also has really good help files, which is rare and should be appreciated! :p
