### Post by catlett on 2006-07-29
HP printers have linux drivers and work fine in linux. Ubuntu should set it up for you.
First go to the top and enter into Systen<Administration<Printing
This will bring up a window. If your printer is plugged in there should be a printer icon that says new printer.
At the top menu select "add printer". This will open another window with choices about that printer.
Just select "use detected printer" and keep it selected as "use local printer".
That is the 1st of 3 steps. With an HP just follow the prompts and just select your model when the list of models appears.
After that the document in abiword will just print.
There is even an HPLIP Toolbox which is like the HP program that installs from the HP cd in windows.
Just go to System<Administration<Printers and get started by selecting add printer. If you have any problems, post them.
