### Post by weverjames on 2007-07-15
I am a current ubuntu user.  I went from windows, to never go back.  However I found there are so many distro to try.
I got interested in linux when I accidentaly saw an add of dell with ubuntu.  I was looking for buying a new desktop but I never was satisfied with windows.  It always crashed, and not for free.  I found irritating how every 2 years MacOS and windows launch new and bulkier OS that leave your pc obsolete.  And using those OS in the new PC's with more RAM , faster processors does not mean better performance.  Basically you have to invest more money to support Bill Gates OS/fortune.
So when I saw there was a viable option, I jumped to it with my eyes closed.
Ubuntu is a great OS.  I which it had a more modern look , but it is good enough for me.
I recently installed PCLINUXOS, cause I wanted to try the second most popular distro (and the most popular in the las 2 months).  It is not bad at all.  However:
1.  It does not get updated automatically.
2.  You don't know when ther next version will be launched.
3.  The community is less organized.
4.  It is less oppen to begginer (although here you can also get a hard time sometimes asking basic stuff)
One thing that I noticed is that in PCLINUXOS the desktop effects work flawless.  I tried to activate them in my ubuntu, I cold not get them to work.  I hope the next version 7.10 could fix this.
