### Post by gn2 on 2007-11-19
> **aweqawrd said:**
> Why the preference for the Core 2 Duo E6750 over the Core 2 Quad Q6600?


Much cheaper and better performance. [http://tinyurl.com/2tmbra](http://tinyurl.com/2tmbra)

Lower TDP 

TDP is the maximum amount of heat given off by the CPU, the E6750 is 65w, the Q6600 is 105w.

Less heat = cooler running = quieter. Also more potential for over-clocking.

Also uses less electricity so more economical to run.

Not a lot of software available that takes best advantage of the four cores.
