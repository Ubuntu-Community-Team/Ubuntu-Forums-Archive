### Post by mgmiller on 2008-04-19
You can use synaptic to actively select the best server for you at the moment.  

In synaptic, go to Settings > Repostories and in the "Download From:" area click to open it and select "other"  Then click on "Select Best Server" button.  

Be patient, :popcorn: it will scan every Ubuntu mirror everywhere and set you up with whatever is fastest at the moment for your location.

Then try the upgrade again.
