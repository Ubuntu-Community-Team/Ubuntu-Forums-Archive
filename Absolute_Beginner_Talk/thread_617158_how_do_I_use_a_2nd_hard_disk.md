This might help you to connect the second hard drive:
[http://tech.yahoo.com/gd/adding-a-second-internal-hard-drive-to-your-pc/2988](http://tech.yahoo.com/gd/adding-a-second-internal-hard-drive-to-your-pc/2988)

Then if all has gone well you system should boot to your first hard drive (Master) gusty or Feisty what ever one you installed on your first  Hard drive. You might have to fix up the grub, I am not sure. 
To setup grub look here:
