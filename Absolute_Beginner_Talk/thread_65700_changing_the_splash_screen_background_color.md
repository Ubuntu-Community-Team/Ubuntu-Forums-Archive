### Post by endy on 2005-09-14
[QUOTE=nighttime]How do I change the splash screen background color from yucky ubuntu brown to black?[/QUOTE]

I believe you can change it by editing the preferences of GDM.

System > Administration > Login Screen Setup

Then open the "GTK+ Greeter" and on the bottom right you can click on the colour button and change it to black. Log out and see if it worked :)
