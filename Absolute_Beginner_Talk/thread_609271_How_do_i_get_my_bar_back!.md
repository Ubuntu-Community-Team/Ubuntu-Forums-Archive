### Post by badguyanil on 2007-11-10
right click on the bottom task bar and say "New Panel". Another bar appears. then right click on the new top bar and click on add to panel. A window appears with lots on addons. Keep adding each one and place them where you want to on the bar.... you will have your "bar" back...
