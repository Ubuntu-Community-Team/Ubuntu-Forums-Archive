### Post by happy-and-lost on 2007-03-21
If you open up one of those .tar.gz files, you'll see that they only contain resources, such as images and a couple of text files which pass the GDM instructions as to what to do with the resources. That's the beauty of open source, if you did distribute something nasty, it would be identifiable in its code. AFAIK, everything on G-L is good and clean. Enjoy :)
