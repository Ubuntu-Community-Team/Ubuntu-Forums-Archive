### Post by Tatty on 2008-04-16
All computers crash.

It really depends what you are doing.  If you are trying to use all the latest and greatest software all the time then it is more likely to crash as it will have been tested much less.

Linux itself is very stable.  Linux is the kernel at the core of your GNU operating system and its used in many highly critical applications all around the world.  

If you run less-than-stable software on top of that, then your software may crash.  Although due to its modular construction, that crash is very unlikely to bring down the linux kernel.  You may need to restart some other component, but linux itself usually keeps on going quite happily.

Another thing to consider is wether your hardware is stable.  A cause of a great many software crashes is poor or damanged hardware.
