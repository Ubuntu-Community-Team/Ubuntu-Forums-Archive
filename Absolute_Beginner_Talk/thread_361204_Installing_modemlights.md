### Post by go2dell on 2007-02-14
It's part of the "gnome-applets" package, which should already be installed.

You should be able to right-click on the bar at the top of the screen (it's a "panel" in Gnome), then click **Add to Panel**.

Under the *System and Hardware* section, you'll see *Modem Monitor*.  Click it, then click the **Add** button.

You'll get a new icon in the panel.  You can right-click it to move it to a better location on the panel, configure the properties of which modem it controls, and use "Lock to panel" to make it stay put where you want it.

You could also put the Modem Monitor on the panel at the bottom of your screen, or even create a new panel for your own uses.  If you want to move things around, just remember you'll probably have to Unlock them before you can move them.
