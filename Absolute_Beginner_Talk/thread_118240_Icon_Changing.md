### Post by jasay on 2006-01-16
2 and 3 I'm not sure.

For 1: open the configuration editor (main menu > system tools > configuration editor)
Navigate to panel options on the left(/ > Apps > panel > objects)
Scroll through the objects until you find one that lists "main menu" under the "tooltip" on the right.
Enter the path to the icon you want to use in "custom_icon" and click "Use_custem_icon"

That should be all.
