### Post by sjh on 2006-02-08
I upgraded some hardware in my current box and found Ubuntu was not that good at detecting new hardware changes.  After fiddling with it for a while, I just reloaded the OS.  Since I had already figured out how to load Foxfire and other packages, it went much quicker.  So I guess I am saying a fresh install is quicker.

My 3.06 gig Celeron D with 512 ram, case and 450W PS cost me close to $200.  The case has extra fans to cool the Celeron and the bigger PS is needed to run it.  You didn't say what all comes with your deal.

Can't help you with your video questions.

Hope this helps,
