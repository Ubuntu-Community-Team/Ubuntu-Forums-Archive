### Post by eentonig on 2007-09-13
As long as your router works with standard IP protocols, it will be compatible.

Your router is something completly seperate from your computer. He couldn't care less if you were using Ubuntu, Windows, Mac, .... whatever. As long as the network protocol you use to talk to him is a standard, he'll be more then happy to work for you.
