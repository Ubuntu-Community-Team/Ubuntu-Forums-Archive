### Post by Tsen on 2007-04-14
Probably an HP.  [LinuxPrinting]("http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting") has an index of printers, so check through it to make sure it works before you buy it.
Whatever you do, do NOT buy a Lexmark.  Their office printers tend to work perfectly, but their home printers use the most backwards, obscure and annoying as hell drivers, and aren't in ANY way standards compliant, so printing is damned near impossible.

Also, so long as you're not printing photo-quality stuff, just buy a cheap printer.  No use spending a ton of money on a printer, since most don't last more than a year or so, and ink will run you a ton on top of whatever you spend.
