### Post by squidward_tentacels on 2006-11-27
The extra ram will certainly help system performance overall, but the biggest difference in getting Beryl to run smoother will be the video card you bought (Hopefully Nvidia or ATI). The intel xtreme graphics2 3d is kinda really low-end and can only render the most basic of 3-D animations. These are really common on budget systems. I couldn't get beryl to run for *crap* on a Dell with the same card (also had a gig of ram). Unfortunately on that machine there is not even a PCI express slot to plug a better card into, if your system has an available PCIe slot, that will accept a better card your Beryl will work beautifully.
