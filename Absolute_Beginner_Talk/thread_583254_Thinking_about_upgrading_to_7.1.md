### Post by Hospadar on 2007-10-20
there's quite a few new features, most notably display stuff and the integrated compiz window manager for nice 3-d effects.

I would suggest getting the livecd and seeing how you like it.  That's really the best way to tell if you want to upgrade or not.  If everything is working fine and it's not a big deal for you you could always just stay.

Also be sure to back up your home directory before upgrading as sometimes upgrading will break systems.
