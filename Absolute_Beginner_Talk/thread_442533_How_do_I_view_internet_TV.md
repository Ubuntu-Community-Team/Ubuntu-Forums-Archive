### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-13
Hmmm, well two steps to check then:

First) Have codecs installed, follow [this]("http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_install_Multimedia_Codecs") and use their commands. Make sure you have the extra repos on, if not follow the link at the top back up.

Second) Download and install this [plugin for firefox]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446"). Then configure it to load all its media on Totem (except real, that should go to real if you have it) and poof, you got a stream :)

I just watched the stream so I know those two steps are the only things you could be missing :)
