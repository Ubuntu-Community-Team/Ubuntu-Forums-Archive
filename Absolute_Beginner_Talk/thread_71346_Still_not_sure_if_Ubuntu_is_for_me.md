### Post by 23meg on 2005-10-03
[http://www.linux-laptop.net/](http://www.linux-laptop.net/) is a good resource for checking out how well certain laptops will run with Ubuntu and other distros. like you, i just use ethernet at home, but my wireless interface, the ipw2200 got detected without any need for configuration and i've used it mainly at school and in various other places with no problems (wifi radar and gtkwifi are two great tools for easily switching between wireless networks; do a google search and check them out). in general the intel wireless adapters (mainly the ipw2200 in the centrino configuration) are known to work well, and the brand you should definitely avoid is Broadcom.
