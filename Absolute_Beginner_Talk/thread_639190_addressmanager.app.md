### Post by Fawlty Towers on 2007-12-15
:popcorn:
I figured out why I could not get addressmanager to run.  In Synaptec Package Manager it showed that addressmanager was installed, but there were 3 files that were not yet installed...  addresses.framework, addressview.framework, and addresses-goodies-for-Gnustep.  After I installed them, AddressManager ran.
