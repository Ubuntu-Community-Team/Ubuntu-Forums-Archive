### Post by anaconda on 2007-06-18
with any luck the motherboard and soundcard will be recognized and the drivers will be installed automatically during the install.

The free (nv) drover for your NVIDIA card will also be installed during the installation of ubuntu, but it doesn't support 3d-acceleration (yet), so you will need to install the restricted drivers yourself. Easily  from System>Administration>Restricted Drivers Manager
