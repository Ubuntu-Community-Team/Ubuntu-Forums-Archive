### Post by AmandaKerik on 2007-07-05
May I suggest an add-on that lets you quickly and easily switch video players?
"MediaPlayerConnectivity" - [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446)

It's main purpose is to load web videos in external players; this has at least two side benefits: easy full-screen and image adjusting.

VLC is handy for this - fast to load, customizable, plays almost anything. You probably already have it on your computer.

This may or may not help, but it's an option...
