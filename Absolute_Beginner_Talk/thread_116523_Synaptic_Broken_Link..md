### Post by jklick on 2006-02-10
Try this:
1. go into Synaptic
2. click on Custom (bottom left)
3. click on Broken (left menu)
4. all your broken packages should appear
5. select the ones you want to remove
6. click apply (on toolbar)
7. select the type of removal you want
