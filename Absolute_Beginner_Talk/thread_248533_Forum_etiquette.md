### Post by msak007 on 2006-09-02
Just wanted to comment on this. Aside from common courtesy and etiquette, replying with a response that it worked will also help others in the future who may run into a similar problem. Nothing is more aggravating than searching for a problem, finding a thread where someone has posed the exact problem you're having (and thus getting your hopes up), and taking the time to read it only to find that the thread was abandoned without a response from the OP as to whether any of the suggestions or possible solutions worked or not. Simply stating it worked (with a thank you or not) will tell someone with the same problem that yes, the solution(s) provided did work and you should give them a try.
