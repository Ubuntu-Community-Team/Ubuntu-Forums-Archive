### Post by Verbatim9 on 2007-04-04
As far as how well any app works with Wine, check the [WineAppDB](http://appdb.winehq.org/)...if your program is consistently rated platinum, it should work fine, if it's Gold, it may work perfectly, provided that you install the right native Windows DLLs, tweak your Wine settings, and/or install a crack (check the description and comments for your specific program for details). If it's rated silver or below, you should probably expect it to be broken, and to stay at least partly broken no matter how much tweaking and tinkering you do, short of contributing the necessary bugfixes and/or new features to the Wine project yourself (or setting a bounty for their development, or hiring a team to develop them for you).
