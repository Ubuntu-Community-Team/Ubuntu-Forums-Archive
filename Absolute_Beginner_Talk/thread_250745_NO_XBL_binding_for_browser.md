### Post by latecomer on 2006-09-24
I had the same problem after the 1.5.0.7 update. It seems to have to do with extensions, in my case Flashgot. I uninstalled it, reinstalled and that fixed it. Just try disabling your extensions one by one to find the culprit. I had to reboot every time though, because the problem only occurred when starting up ff the first time after booting. Or you could just uninstall all your extensions at once and reinstall, could be quicker if you've got a lot of them.
