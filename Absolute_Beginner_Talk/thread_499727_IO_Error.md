### Post by plong0 on 2008-01-13
I had this problem also.  I looked closely at the bottom of the cd and noticed a couple little scratches.  I found this [link]("http://lifehacker.com/software/cds/macgyver-tip-fix-scratched-cds-with-toothpaste-155741.php") and gave it a go.  Tried copying again and it worked!

Just use a little bit of toothpaste and rub it on the scratches, then rinse it off with a very slightly damp paper towel and you're golden.  Remember to clean from the center towards the edge and not in circles.
