### Post by Martin on 2007-07-06
Answer to 1. Be very careful. If you use the whole drive for Ubuntu you will NOT be able to access your windows files. Back them up first to CD or DVD or to another hard drive.

Answer to 2. You can change resolution more or less easily after the install. There are also themes that can be used, changed customized etc that are part of the basic install.

Answer to 3. Ubuntu functions pretty good as installed. Try that first.
