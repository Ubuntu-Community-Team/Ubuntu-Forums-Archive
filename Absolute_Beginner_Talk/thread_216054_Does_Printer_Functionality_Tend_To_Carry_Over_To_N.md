### Post by avtolle on 2006-07-15
Kiddalee: I don't have experience w/the precise printers you listed, but I have a Brother HL-2040 mono laser which "just works" functionally w/Dapper, and from what I gathered from a similar search, most Brother printers work w/Linux. I will tell you that I didn't try to do anything fancy,and just accepted the default set up when I installed Dapper. Primarily, it is used in a small office situation for only text processing, no graphics, etc. So far, it has been trouble-free. Unfortunately, there are a few features offered under the Windows/OSx drivers which don't appear out of the box, such as duplexing, but that's OK, I don't print on both sides of the page very often anyway. HTH. BTW, there are currently some very good prices on this model, perhaps due to its being replaced?
