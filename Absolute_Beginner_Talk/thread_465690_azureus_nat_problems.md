### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-06-06
If you go through the Azureus help, it will take you to the wiki - [http://www.azureuswiki.com/index.php/NAT_problem]("http://www.azureuswiki.com/index.php/NAT_problem")

You really just need to forward the right ports through your router to your internal IP, typically you forward a range of ports, like say 100 ports as I do.  That's probably a little overkill, but it works well.

You can check your router help guide online, it should tell you how to forward ports.
