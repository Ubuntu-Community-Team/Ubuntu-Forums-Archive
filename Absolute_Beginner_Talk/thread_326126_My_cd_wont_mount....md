### Post by kinematic on 2006-12-27
you don't need md5summer to check the iso(that's a windows program right?).
open a terminal and use this command> md5sum /path/to/iso
or you can use k3b....click on burn cd/dvd image and select the iso image and k3b will calculate the md5sum.
another possibility is that the chipset isn't supported...i've had it happen in the past when i was new to linux and i wanted to try a distro that didn't support my MoBo chipset.
