### Post by crf on 2008-03-02
~ what torrent clients did you try? What torrent client did you use on windows?

Have you tried connecting your computer directly to the modem, if you use a router?

Have you tried configuring your router, if you use one? Many torrent clients are able to forward ports (which helps, often) on your router automatically (using UPNP, which sometimes needs to be explicitily enabled on your router). Otherwise it helps to configure the router yourself. Having your router configured means other people can easily connect to you to upload and download.
