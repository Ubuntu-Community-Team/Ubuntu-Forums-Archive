### Post by z0mbie on 2007-11-03
If you mean the bar where the Maximize, Minimize, Close buttons are located on, then thats not part of Firefox, thats part of Gnome the environment for Ubuntu. That specific bar in Gnome is called Metacity (aka Window Border). You can download Metacity themes to match your Firefox themes at [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) .

### Post by Incense on 2007-11-03
The bar on top sounds like it's part of your system theme. If you change that, the it will update in firefox and all your apps. ### Post by Dragons World on 2007-11-03
OK I figured it out. I went to System/Preferences/Appearance/Theme and changed it there. I also right clicked on the task bar at bottom and chose properties and then background. I then chose solid color. Everything looks pretty good so far.
