### Post by soulpanda on 2006-11-21
Hey, 
I was getting this for about a week everytime I
tried to install and restarted my computer.
I looked around the forums and the consensus was
that my bios was older than dirt and was having
trouble counting over 1024, or that it needed the
first 1024 open...or something like that.

Now, I was able to solve it by simply making a partion
on my drive, which is 320 gigs, that was 10 gigs large,
or 10240 in the GNOME partitioner.
I was then able to just reformat it and install and everything
worked out fine. Since this made me want to go on a 
computer destroying rampage several times, I decided
that it would be best to post my results. I don't 
really understand the problem itself, I know enough to
get by, but I know that this is what worked. Hope this
helps some other poor smuck out there.
