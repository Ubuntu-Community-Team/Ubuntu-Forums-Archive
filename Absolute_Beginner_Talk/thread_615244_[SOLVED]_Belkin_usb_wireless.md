### Post by AndyCooll on 2007-11-16
You probably need the Ralink RT2500 driver (at least that's what mine require). Before you jump completely in however, I'd just check and confirm (using the above post), for I know that Belkin are notorious for changing chipsets within the same brand. I also have two Belkin PCMCIA cards, same series, but one purchased earlier than the other, an they have different chipsets, and hence require different drivers.
