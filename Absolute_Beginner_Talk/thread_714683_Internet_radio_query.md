### Post by CeeDee on 2008-03-04
You have two easy options depending on your situation, if you have broadband through a wireless router then you just need to buy an internet radio from the miriad of suppliers out there.  Be careful you want an internet radio that connects via your WiFi connection not the cheap ones you need to plug into your USB connection on the PC.  A cheaper option than this is to buy a Belkin (or other make) of wireless transmitter (often sold as MP3 transmitter) that plugs into your output jack socket sound card or motherboard on your PC.  This will allow you to tune into the transmitter on any conventional radio so that you can listen in the garden or wherever.
