### Post by Victormd on 2008-02-25
> **Otuvak said:**
> The first being...How do you put different desktop backgrounds on the cube? Where do change the backdrop when rotating the cube?

Unfortunately you can't set different desktop backgrounds to the different faces of the cube. You can however, change the top and bottom faces, and the overall background (skydome).

Go to Preferences>Advanced Desktop Effects Settings>Desktop Cube>Appearance and change the Cube Caps and the Skydome settings.
