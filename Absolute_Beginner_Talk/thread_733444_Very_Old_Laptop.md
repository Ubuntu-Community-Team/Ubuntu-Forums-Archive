### Post by AdamWill on 2008-03-25
rolcol: a stock Mandriva setup will be a bit too heavy for 128MB of RAM. You may want to switch out KDE for Xfce (install task-xfce) or IceWM (should be there already), and look at light applications like Midori (from /contrib/backports) or links-graphic as a browser etc.
