Everyone has different opinions on the hardware (your mileage will vary).  I would suggest you to pick from this list: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport).

As I've said, everyone's opinion is different.  So I'll put my 2 cents in: I don't have much experience with Ubuntu.  But generally speaking, the hardware support has gotten better substantially (even if it's just upgrading one level).  However, I would suggest to get a nvidia graphics card, because nvidia offers much better Linux support (their binary driver is MUCH better than ATI's).  Also, [System76]("http://ubuntuforums.org/www.system76.com") offers computers with Ubuntu pre-installed on them, so you can tweak their list of hardware.
