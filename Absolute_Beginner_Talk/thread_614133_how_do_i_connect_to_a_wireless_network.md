### Post by amaroKer on 2007-11-15
you should see the network icon in the "tray".  click on it.
A menu will open which should show a list of wireless networks.  click on yours.
If it finds the networks, it will ask you for your network key.

That's the book solution to the problem.  I hope ubuntu configured your card drivers correctly.
