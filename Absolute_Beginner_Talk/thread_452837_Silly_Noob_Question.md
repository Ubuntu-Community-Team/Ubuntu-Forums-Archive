### Post by ruy_lopez on 2007-05-23
The answer would be: you can put them anywhere *within* your home folder. 

So that means you could put them on your desktop if you wanted. Or you could create a folder, inside your home folder or on your Desktop, called "my files" or something. 

The important thing is to label the folders and subfolders properly, instead of having one big container.

Personally I prefer to have everything in one folder, with lots of smaller subfolders, that way I don't have too much cluttering up either my home folder or my desktop. But its really a matter of preference.
