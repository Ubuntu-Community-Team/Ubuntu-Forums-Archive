### Post by annda on 2007-01-06
actually, any router will/should do (it would probably be safest to check if your ISP sells any and get one like that). what you want to choose carefully is the network card if you are planning to use wireless connections. 

take a look at this [compatibility list]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported") before you go out shopping.
