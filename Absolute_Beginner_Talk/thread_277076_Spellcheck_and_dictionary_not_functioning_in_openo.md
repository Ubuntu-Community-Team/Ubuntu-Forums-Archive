### Post by i.am.canadian on 2006-10-15
OpenOffice seems to only let you spell check the particular language (or its locational variant) based on the information you entered when you set up your OS.  So, in my case I had told the OS I wanted English as my language and that I am from Canada.  When I installed OOo (OpenOffice.org) it would not spell check using English (USA).  I had to download English(Canadian).  So if you do as the the above post suggests and use the wizard to download the dictionary for the particular language and location you use.
