### Post by NT4.0 on 2006-08-20
SOLVED! After an hour of googling I finally found a solution: I changed the driver name in xorg.conf from "vesa" to "fglrx". And it worked for me! I got excellent refresh rates for any resolution. Now I have 1024x768@85Hz and am happy with it. This thread may therefore be closed or removed. :)

P.S. Why does the Screen Resolution GNOME applet never show the correct refresh rate, anyway?
