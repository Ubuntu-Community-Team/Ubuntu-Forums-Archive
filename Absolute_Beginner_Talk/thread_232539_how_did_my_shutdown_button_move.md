### Post by hopstah on 2006-08-08
sometimes the buttons load in different orders, and then get stuck in places they shouldn't be.  right click on the red button and uncheck 'Lock to Panel' and do the same for the dime / date section.  then right click the red button, and select move, and move it where you want it, then right click it and lock it to the panel there.  then do the same for the date and time, and lock it into place as well.  that should take care of them mysteriously moving again.

### Post by Bragador on 2006-08-08
Yeah it happened to me too. You can actually move it around to where it was but youll have to learn how to do it. 

Make sure when you right click on it that "lock to panel" is not selected.

The right click and select move liek you probably did. Youll be able to move it around. If you can't move it to some places it's because the other place is too small because other icons are lockedin and doesn't permit you to move more icons over them.

One way to fix it:

right click at the top right corner of your screen just under the time. Make sure you DON'T move your mouse over the time though(you must not select it). Then select "add to panel" and select the red button that shuts down your computer. 

After that delete the old red button and make sure the new one is locked down (select the option by right clicking on it).

Hopes this helps.
