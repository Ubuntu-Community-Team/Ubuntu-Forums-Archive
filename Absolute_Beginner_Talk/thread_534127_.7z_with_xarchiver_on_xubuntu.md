### Post by izizzle on 2007-08-24
I downloaded a .7z file and tried to open it with xarchiver on xubuntu. Xarchiver failed to open it. What do I have to do to open the file?

---

### Post by qpieus on 2007-08-24
Look in synaptic for the packages p7zip and/or p7zip-full and install it. I think once p7zip is installed, xarchiver should be able to open your file.
p7zip only installs 7-zip support, while p7zip-full also supports other compression formats like rar, zip arj, cab, etc. (see synaptic descriptions for more details).
