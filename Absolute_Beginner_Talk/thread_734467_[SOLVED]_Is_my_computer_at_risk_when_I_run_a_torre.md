### Post by bschleusner on 2008-03-24
> 1. Is there a relation between what I'm doing and what's happening to my computer?
yes, torrenting is a very demanding thing for a computer to do, especially hard on your HD.

> 2. Is running a torrent manager potentially dangerous or is port management restricting accesses to downloads/uploads?
On Linux, not really. As long as you only open one port for torrenting on your router/firewall/torrent-client

> 3. If there is a risk, how can I protect myself without closing the ports I need?
Again, if you only use one port it is safe.

If you are really security paranoid, scan for open ports on your computer and see where it is vulnerable.
