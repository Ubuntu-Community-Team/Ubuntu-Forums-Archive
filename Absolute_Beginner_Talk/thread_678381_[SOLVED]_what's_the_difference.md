### Post by bumanie on 2008-01-25
Feisty is 9 months old, whereas gutsy is about 3months old. Gutsy has updated features. You really need to try them to see which you like best. I personally stuck with feisty because it was more stable for my computer configuration, however I know there are a lot of people who successfully use gutsy. I figure my problems with gutsy were probably some rare/odd hardware conflict. If you don't have any hardware conflicts or major difficulties, use gutsy.
