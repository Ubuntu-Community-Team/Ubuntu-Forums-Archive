### Post by n8bounds on 2007-02-25
So two different OSs have trouble with it?  **....Is it your network card?**  (Does it work flawlessly in another PC?)  If so, try a different PCI slot.  Is it not detected or just not getting a DHCP lease?  If its the latter, are you sure its not the cable?  If its not the cable, whats on the other end of it?  A switch?  A belkin router from a walmart shelf you got second hand at a pawn shop?
