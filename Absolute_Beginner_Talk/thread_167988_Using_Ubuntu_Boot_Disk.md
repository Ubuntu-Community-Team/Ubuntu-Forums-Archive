### Post by confused57 on 2006-04-29
The iso has to be burned "as an image" , look for that in your CD burning software, with Roxio you can just right click the iso and Nero has an option to burn disk image.  Burn at 4x-8x, not higher, and lower if your software allows it.
If you're still having problems, you might want to do a md5 checksum of the downloaded iso.
