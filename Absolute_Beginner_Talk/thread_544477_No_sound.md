### Post by hiebert on 2007-09-06
I had a similar problem. I have a ASUS A8N-E Motherboard with Realtek integrated Sound and i have another sound card installed. I got sound out of the live cd, but after I installed Ubuntu I got no sound. This was because the wrong sound card was selected.. 

Try to go to System > Preferences > Sound and select another device (Nvidia CK804 (ALSA Mixer) in my case)

Hope it helped
