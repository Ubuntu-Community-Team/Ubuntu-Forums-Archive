### Post by leovA on 2007-10-17
you can also add Option "rotate" "inverted (or left, right)" to xorg.conf

in a terminal get root, and then gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf

then paste "rotate" "inverted (or left, right)" onder the screen or device tab, then press ctrl, alt bckspc to restart X

voila :guitar:
