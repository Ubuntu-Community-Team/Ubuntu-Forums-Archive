### Post by blueridgedog on 2007-12-28
Logic tells me that you have multiple "to" addresses that end up in your server's mailbox under one account.  You want to not download certain emails that are in your pop/imap mailbox.

It appears to me that the filters in evolution are applied after mail is received, not prior.

You can select "message" "create rule" then "filter on recipients", but the actions yo  u can take are all local actions.  

Others may have different ideas, but it appears to me that you best bet is to move them to a folder.
