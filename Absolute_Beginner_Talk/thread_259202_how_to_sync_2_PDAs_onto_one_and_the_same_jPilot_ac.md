### Post by megamania on 2006-09-18
You are looking for trouble.

The two palms are very different, and backing up one's files to the other one is very dangerous and will mess up your systems, because a lot of system files would be copied as well.

If you are interested in having the same copies of databases such as Address, Datebook, Todo and Memo, you may manually copy each file from one palm to the other one - that's much safer and should definitely cause no trouble.
