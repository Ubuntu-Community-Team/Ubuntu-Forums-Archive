### Post by ncappel1 on 2007-05-28
I use a wmp54g wireless card in my desktop. under Edgy it was automatically detected and configured, I didn't have to do any configuration to get it working (and keep it working). Under Dapper I had minor issues where I had to enable to connection each time the computer fired up, and occasional inconsistant dropped connections, where I had to restart the connection through the command line, if that didn't work a reboot did the trick. 

I don't know your level of familiarity with the commands pertaining to wireless, but for me a great resource was this wiki howto that brings the reader through the basics: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WiFiHowTo](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WiFiHowTo)

hope this was helpful, let us know how it turns out.
