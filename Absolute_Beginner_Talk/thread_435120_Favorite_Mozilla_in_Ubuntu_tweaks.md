### Post by jaredssix on 2007-05-06
I just had a Mozilla in Ubuntu hot key remedied. I'd lost the "go back a page" hot key, [backspace], I'd grown to know and love in Windows. The fix, provided by rygar:

> **rygar said:**
> 
In the address bar in Firefox, type "about:config" (without the quotes)
In the Filter field, type "browser.backspace_action"
Modify it's value so that it is set to 0.


What are your favorite Mozilla in Ubuntu tweaks?

### Post by Immolatus on 2007-05-06
My favorite is to turn on http pipe lining 
"about;config" type in http to bring up the line "httppipelining" look on the right of that line and right click on "false" and toggle it to "true".
I use alot of tabs and this allows them to load faster in particular succession.
