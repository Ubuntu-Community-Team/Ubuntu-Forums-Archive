### Post by msak007 on 2007-04-08
While many users do use and recommend Envy, I can't say I have any first-hand experience with it. I do know it's a script, and as such I try to stay away from those and their ilk (apps such as Automatix2). I can however point you to this page:

[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Installation_Guide](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Installation_Guide) (pick your version)

I have used the instructions on that page every single time without fail and they are easy to follow. You only have to go back to it if
a.) There is a new driver update
b.) Ubuntu updates the kernel, so you have to re-compile the module (using the last steps).

Hope that helps.
