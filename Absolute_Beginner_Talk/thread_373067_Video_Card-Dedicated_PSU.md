The biggest enemy that you face is --- HEAT!  Therefore, you should attempt to find a single PSU that will do what you want.

While I am not a fanboy of modular PSU's, in a small case this will assist with cable management.  This is important to reduce heat.  The more open space the better the airflow.

What is the footprint of your PSU (measurements)?  This will help me make some recommendations.
