### Post by skirkpatrick on 2006-03-04
My home has (remember, I have two adult children living here while they go to college and I telecommute):

1 Linux server
1 dual-boot Ubuntu/XP desktop with printer
1 dual-boot Ubuntu/98 desktop
1 XP desktop
1 98 desktop
1 dual-boot Ubuntu/XP laptop
3 networked audo players (Audiotron)

These computers all share files and the printer.  The server stores my music and video collection as well as provides a website development platform.  You'll need to setup Samba to have your Ubuntu sharing with your XP machine.  Search the forums, there are a lot of topics on doing this.

I have used FrostWire (a non-nag version of LimeWire) for P2P sharing.
