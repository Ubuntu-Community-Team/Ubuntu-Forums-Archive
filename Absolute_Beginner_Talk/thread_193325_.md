### Post by Thiesen on 2006-06-11
Then what is a pipe?

Well... a pipe is a pipe... :-)

No seriously, let's say you want to do some gardening and you need to water your flowers. What do you do? You would hook up a water hose to an outlet, right? Well, this is one kind of pipe, in this case it lets you get water from one place to another in a quite convient way.

When you use a pipe in a console session it's meant to take the output of one program (or script) as the input of another program (or script).

Excuse me if this has been answered elsewhere, I didn't bother to look for an better explanation but I think my answer is pretty newbie friendly... :-)
