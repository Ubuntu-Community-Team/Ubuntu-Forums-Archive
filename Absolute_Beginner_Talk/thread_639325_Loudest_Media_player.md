### Post by Sutur on 2007-12-13
> **xzin said:**
> I installed Xmms, where can I get the plugins and install them?

Many of the plugins are in the repositories. There's a whole pack of plugins for xmms named "LADSPA" that might improve your experience too.

Don't bother with the xmms2 plugins, since xmms2 is different from xmms, and doesn't have a decent gui yet.

Some of the other plugins you can get directly from the xmms site, but they'll require more work to get going.

Luck.
