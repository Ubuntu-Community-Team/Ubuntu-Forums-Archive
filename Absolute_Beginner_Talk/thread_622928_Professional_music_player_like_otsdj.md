### Post by reesy on 2007-11-25
On OtsDj you can search for a song and as your typing all the songs with the phrase you've typed so far start appearing. It fades each song in and out. It alters the volume on each song so every songs volume is the same so you don't have to turn your speaker up or down. You can change the tempo and pitch easy. Do some scratching in it. Put songs into categories and save playlists.



Here's a link to a screen shot of OtsDj [Click Me]("http://www.otsav.com/gfx/otsdjshotlarge.png")
