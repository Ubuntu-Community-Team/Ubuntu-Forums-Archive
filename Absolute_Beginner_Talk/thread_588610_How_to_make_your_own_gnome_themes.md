### Post by NilsE on 2007-10-23
I just went through the existing themes in /usr/share/themes and and /usr/share/icons and used them as a base for creating my own.  You can learn a lot from looking at someone else's work.

I started off by just replacing graphics then evolved to modifying the theme files (gtkrc & Matacity) to use the graphics in a different way.

There must be a GUI out there somewhere that reads the files and give a representation of what it will look like but I have not found one.  I didn't look to hard either.
