### Post by P235 on 2007-12-02
Hello, I have tried to search for my problem on the forums, but I have been unable to find any related threads.  When I try to play mp3s with music programs like Rhythmbox and Exaile I can't seem to get any sound.  The mp3 files play properly with mplayer and vlc so I know they are not corrupted.  I have tried fiddling with alsamixer to make sure the volume is up and not muted with no success.  Also, when I use Rhythmbox and Exaile (separately), my cpu usage spikes to 75%-100%.

Solved:

I restarted my computer and all returned to normal.  Though it is true that Linux need not restart for every installation, it does make sense to restart once and a while.  I noticed that the color balance on mplayer was off while playing a few video files and realized that it was time to restart.  Thank you.
