### Post by Bothered on 2007-09-23
It sounds like you have a bad disk. Some things to check:

1. Did you verify the iso you downloaded? See here for instructions on how to do this: [HotToMD5SUM]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM")
2. Did you burn the iso correctly - i.e. did you burn the disk from the image, or burn the image to the disk as a file? See here for iso burning instructions: [BurningIsoHowto]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto")
3. Did you burn at a slow speed? To prevent errors it's recommended that you burn the ubuntu disk at minimum speed (1x).
