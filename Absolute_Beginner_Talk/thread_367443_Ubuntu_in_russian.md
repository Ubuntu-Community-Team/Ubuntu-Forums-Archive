### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-02-22
Yes, it will. When you reboot with the live cd in CD-ROM, press F2 on the very fist screen and choose Russia for the live session. If you then install, you are also asked to choose the default language. Moreover, you can add more language support after installation, and choose different ones at startup.
