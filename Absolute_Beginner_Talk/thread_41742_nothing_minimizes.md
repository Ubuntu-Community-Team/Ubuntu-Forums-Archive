 :smile:[/QUOTE]
 **If you don't have the bar at all:** Right-click on the bar at the top of screen, select "New Panel". If it doesn't appear at the bottom but somewhere else, drag it to bottom. 

**Adding the windw list to the bar:** Right-click on bottom bar, select "Add to panel". From the list select "window list" and click "Add".


If the whole bar was missing, you might want to add "show desktop", "workspace switcher" and "Trash" too.
