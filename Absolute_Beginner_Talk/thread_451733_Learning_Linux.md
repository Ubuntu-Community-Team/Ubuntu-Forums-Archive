### Post by eldepeche on 2007-05-22
It sounds a bit like a troll, but Google is your friend. That's pretty much how I learned everything I know about GNU/Linux. The trick is learning how to phrase your search. 

Also, the manual pages give a pretty useful overview most of the time. For example, if you type ```
man xorg.conf
``` you get a pretty useful summary of the things you would put in that file, and the syntax, and that would give you a better idea of what to search for.
