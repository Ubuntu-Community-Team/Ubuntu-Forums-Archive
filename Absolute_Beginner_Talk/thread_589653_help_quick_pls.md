### Post by linuxlizard on 2007-10-24
Another easy way is to go to system- administration - synaptic package manager and click on gdesklets and install them there. Once installed go to applications- accessories- gdesklets and click on it. A window will pop open and you should click on toolbar/launchers on the left and you will see starterbar appear on the right, click on that and a nice starter bar will appear. If you right click on it you can select move and move it anywhere on your screen that you want. to add a new application to it, simply go up to your applications menus and drag an icon onto the space near the starter bar and it will be added. Very simple to do and use once you try it.
