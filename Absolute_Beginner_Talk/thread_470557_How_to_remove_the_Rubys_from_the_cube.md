### Post by orange2k on 2007-06-11
Rubys are "caps", image behind the cube is "skydome". Look for these names in the Beryl settings manager and change where appropriate. The only thing you must be carefull about are picture sizes.
For skydome something like 2048x1024 (depending on your cards memory) is acceptable (or 1024x1024, 1024x512, 2048x2048 etc.). For caps Im not sure.
