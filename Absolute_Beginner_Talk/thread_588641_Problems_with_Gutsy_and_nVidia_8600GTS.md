### Post by shmengie on 2007-11-05
okay, i was able to get this working. i may have gone about it in a roundabout fashion, but it works. here's what i did:

first, to make sure i hadn't irreparably hosed anything, i deleted the partition and installed fresh. i then did all the updates. i then went to the package manager and in settings, checked 'universe.' i then did a search for 'nvidia' and found the glx-new package. marked it for installation, hit apply, rebooted, and shazaam!

don't know why this would work as opposed to the other way, but it did. maybe it was just 'one of those things.' anyways, hope this helps someone.
