### Post by ashutoshd on 2007-12-27
Pidgin file transfer speeds are truly terrible.
Live Messenger transfers are better. Seems it is the switchboard function, which does not support a direct connection. (like i know what that means...)

Check this link out. 
[http://tiagosh.wordpress.com/2007/07/01/libmsn-progress/](http://tiagosh.wordpress.com/2007/07/01/libmsn-progress/)

An excerpt:
*"One interesting thing is that the switchboard limits the transfer speed to 3k (average), and if you try to send many files at a time, the bandwidth will be shared between them. That’s why kopete file transfer is very slow. Kopete does not support direct connection. aMSN does support and pidgin does not even try to start direct connection negotiation."*
