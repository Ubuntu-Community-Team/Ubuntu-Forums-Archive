### Post by kelnage on 2007-09-18
Right click on the date and ensure "Lock to Panel" is unticked (if not, click on it). Then right click on the date again and choose "Move". Move cursor around screen until happy, click to drop and right click on the date and re-tick "Lock to Panel". Then you can repeat those steps to move everything else where they should be :)
