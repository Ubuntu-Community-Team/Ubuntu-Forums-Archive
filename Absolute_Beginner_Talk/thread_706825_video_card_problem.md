### Post by JoshuaRL on 2008-02-24
Wow, my first time hearing of a Matros card too.

From what I've seen [here,](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=191142) your xorg.conf about your vid card needs to look like
> 
Section "Device"
Identifier "Matrox Graphics, Inc. MGA G100 [Productiva] AGP"
Driver "mga"
BusID "PCI:1:0:0"
EndSection

That means you'll need to look for the "**mga**" driver when trying to reconfigure xorg.

Please report back here if you can't get it configured right with dpkg-reconfigure.
