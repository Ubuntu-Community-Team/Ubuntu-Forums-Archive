### Post by Malta paul on 2007-09-08
Right click on the top control bar>add to panel> System Hardware. Then drag the volume control Icon to the panel (it looks like a volume knob) this will indicate as a 'Speaker' on the control panel.
Then drag to where you want and tick lock to panel.
Have fun:)
