### Post by steeleyuk on 2007-06-04
Do The Police and Lenny Kravitz both have albums called 'Greatest Hits'? If yes, then thats your problem. Rhythmbox will be sorting by Album and presumes they are from the same album.

You'll need to use a tag editing program to change the album field. Or you could re-rip those tracks with a different album.
