### Post by Daveth on 2007-09-12
nothing bleeding edge, nothing dead as a dodo, nvidia over ati, separate soundcards over on-board sound (they always seem to work better in my view), as much ram as you can afford, as much disk space as you can afford, and just about any cpu.
