### Post by Arthur Archnix on 2008-01-21
>System  >Preferences  >Keyboard Shortcuts

Scroll down to where it says "Open a Terminal Window".

It's likely disabled. Just click on where it says disabled, and press what keys you want to use. Eg, press F10. It will warn you if you press a key sequence that is already in use.
