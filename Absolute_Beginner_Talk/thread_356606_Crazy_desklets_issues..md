### Post by GI_Josh on 2007-02-11
Ok, I've actually found a fix to our problems!  The bouncing starterbar is due to beryl.  Just turn beryl off, position the bar how you like, and you can then turn beryl back on.  As for arizone.edu, when firefox is in your starterbar, right click on it and choose edit start.  In the command box, change it to just firefox, and not firefox%u or whatever it is.  That should solve your problems.
