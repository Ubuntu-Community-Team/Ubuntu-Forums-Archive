### Post by northern lights on 2008-04-14
"Wall" and "Cube" do exclude each other. So do "Plane" and "Cube". I dunno, whether a "Plane"/"Wall" combo works or not.

An external monitor will work under Ubuntu as well. Are you cloning the output, or do you have an extended desktop? The latter is not as easy and the earlier very easy if you're happy with the same resolution on both screens. With enough fiddling around, all variants are achievable, though.

The "super" key is what is the "Windows" key under any MS OS and the "Apple" key under OS X.
