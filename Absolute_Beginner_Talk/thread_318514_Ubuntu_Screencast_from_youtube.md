### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-12-14
> **wersdaluv said:**
> How can I make my desktop look like[this]("http://youtube.com/watch?v=GD4SR25OMLc")?Hello!

There are many ways to go about doing this, and they are contingent on various factors such as: your video card (and having the latest graphics accelation drivers), your version of Ubuntu (Dapper or Edgy), and other things too.

To get your machine looking like that, you would need XGL and Compiz for your machine.  You can search the forums for xgl compiz and many results will pop up.  Some research is recommended before you start though.  There are many great guides out there, but they are tailored to specific setups and the like.  

This is a good one to set up XGL and Compiz if you're running Ubuntu Dapper 6.06 :: 
[http://tazforum.thetazzone.com/viewtopic.php?t=2189](http://tazforum.thetazzone.com/viewtopic.php?t=2189)

Here's a good one to set up XGL and Beryl (Beryl is a fork of Compiz...you can do a search on the forums for comparion/contrast, or search Google for the differences) if you're running Ubuntu Edgy 6.10 :: [http://wiki.beryl-project.org/index.php/Install/Ubuntu/Edgy/XGL](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/index.php/Install/Ubuntu/Edgy/XGL)

Happy Compositing! ;)
