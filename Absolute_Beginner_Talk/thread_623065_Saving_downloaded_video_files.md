### Post by bluepowder7 on 2007-11-25
is this similar to having a YouTube video loaded and viewable from within a browser, but you wanna keep it saved even after you close the app?

if that's the case, go to your /tmp folder [Places - Computer - Filesystem - /tmp] and find a big file there.  copy it (Ctrl-C) and paste it to your music or videos directory (Ctrl-V)

what i do is i have two windows open - one that shows the /tmp folder, and another that shows my /media/videos folder (you just open multiple instances from the Places menu).  then, just like in the windows world, Ctrl-C when you're in the /tmp folder, then go to your media folder (wherever it is) and hit Ctrl-V.
