### Post by Kimm on 2006-02-20
if you know for a fact that the contacts are still on the server, issue:

rm -rf .gaim

at the commandline.

[color=red]Warning:[/color]
This will remove all acount information stored on your computer, meaning you will have to reenter all login info for all acounts.
