### Post by atomkarinca on 2007-11-28
Fire painting is also configured through CompizConfig Settings Manager. Hit alt+f2 and type "ccsm". Under Effects category you will see "Paint fire on the screen", check that option. The shortcut for paint fire should be shift+super+button1.
