### Post by steven8 on 2007-03-03
I had this problem, and found that I had actually selected the model just above mine in set-up.  I tried a bunch of times to print, then saw that I'd chosen close, but not quite, my printer.  Selected the right one, and it worked just fine.  Double check you printer selection, and if that is not it, go to the printing menu, and check the output.  Draft, etc.  Make sure it is set on Normal.
