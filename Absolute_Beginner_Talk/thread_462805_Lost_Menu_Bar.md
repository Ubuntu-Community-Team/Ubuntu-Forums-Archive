### Post by vanadium on 2007-06-03
You will need to "recreate" it. Indeed, you probably deleted the panel containing your menu.

Right-click the one remaining panel (your taskbar will be on it) and select "New panel". This creates a new empty panel. Right-click this new panel and select "properties" to position it on top of your screen (if it isn there already). Then right-click and select Add to panel. To recreate the "factory default" menu bar, you will add:
"Main menu", "Notification area" (both under "Utilities), then Clock (under Accessories) and "Quit" (under desktop and windows).
The default panel also includes launchers for Firefox and Evolution next to the menu.
The panels are for you to create and customize. Just play around with it to see how it works. Don hesitate to experiment. You might come out with a configuration that suits you better than the default one.
