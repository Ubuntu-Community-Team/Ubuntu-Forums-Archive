### Post by beechmore on 2008-03-14
I use Firefox with Ubuntu Gutsy.

I noticed recently that web browsing was becoming slower and slower on my pc. After a while I realised that my browser was forever connecting to some advertising/profiling site or other and transferring data  to and from it. This was the cause of the problem. My browser would simply halt, in the middle of opening a web page, and wait for the data transfer to complete before continuing. You can see the data transfer messages appearing in the bottom left hand corner of your Firefox screen.

I installed adblock plus in an attempt to stop this happening and what a difference it makes. It has drastically speeded things up. You can get adblock plus as an add-on to your Firefox browser(Tools/add-ons).

Hope this may be of help.

beechmoore
