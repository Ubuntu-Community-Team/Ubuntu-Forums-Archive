### Post by Cloudsoft on 2007-05-05
I had the same problem.

Look at the URL in your browser.  It probably has two "http" listings.  Erase the first "http://ubuntudownload" or something like that till you get to the second "http".

Then replace the "%3F" type characters with "/" signs.

Reload and you should get a download.

good luck
