### Post by nanotube on 2006-05-10
as to your first question - just right click on the top taskbar, and select "new panel", and then position it wherever you want.
then to recreate what was on it originally, right click on that new panel, and select "add to panel"
and choose "window list" (list of open windows), "show desktop" (the show desktop button), and "trash" (the trash icon). and of course, feel free to customize it and add whatever else.

as to your second question, i am not sure. my touchpad does click-drag by touch just fine. (not the click-drag-drag, but click and one drag is ok).
