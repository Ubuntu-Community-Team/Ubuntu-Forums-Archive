### Post by buuntuu! on 2007-03-06
6.06. (Dapper) is the long-term-suport- version (3years) whereas 6.10 (edgy) has a 18 month lifecycle. obviously edgy has newer software (eg. firefox 2 instead of 1.5)
you could just install dapper and if you feel like cutting-edge upgrading to edgy with  a working internet-connection is just a matter of minutes.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EdgyUpgrades?highlight=%28upgrade%29]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EdgyUpgrades?highlight=%28upgrade%29")
have fun and welcome!
