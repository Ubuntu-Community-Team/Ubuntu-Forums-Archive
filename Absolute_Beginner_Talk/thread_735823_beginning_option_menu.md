### Post by bumanie on 2008-03-26
What type of processor and speed, amount of ram, type of video card etc. Sometimes a live cd won't work on some older computers. Sometimes it has trouble with very new architecture due to drivers not yet being in the kernel. Some users find they can't install via a live cd but have success with the alternate cd (text based). As you have a blank, black screen, it is hard to tell why this may be happening. Even with the above information, it may be hard to tell as sometimes there is just an idiosynchratic conflict that can't easily be explained.
