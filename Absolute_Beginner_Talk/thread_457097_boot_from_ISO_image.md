### Post by taurus on 2007-05-28
I believe you burnt it wrong.  Did you just copy the big file from your harddrive over to a CD?  You suppose to burn that as an image file, not a data file.  If you look at the CD from XP, you would see a bunch of files and directories, NOT a one large file.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto?highlight=%28burn%29%7C%28iso%29](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto?highlight=%28burn%29%7C%28iso%29)
