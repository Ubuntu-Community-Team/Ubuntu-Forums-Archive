Check this page:

### Post by lintoon on 2008-01-22
Also try a manual test print.  Check you rmanual for how to do it.  Usually it's just a matter of holding one of the buttons down for a few seconds while turning the printer on.

If you get nothing when you do a manual test print it proves you have a hardware issue.
