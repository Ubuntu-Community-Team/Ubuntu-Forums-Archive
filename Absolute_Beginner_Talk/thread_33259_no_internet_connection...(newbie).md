### Post by grakhul on 2005-05-10
Post more information about your whole setup.  This will help.
In general you will want to describe, the following:
1.  Are you having a problem booting the Ubuntu OS?  Basically can you use your Ubuntu installation with no problem.
2.  What is the make and model number of your Network card? (The card actually connected to the computer that does not have Internet Access)
3. You mentioned a PNA network.  What kind of Router/Modem does it use?(Make and model number?)
4. Are you hardwired? or is this some type of wireless PNA configuration?
5. Who is your ISP?
