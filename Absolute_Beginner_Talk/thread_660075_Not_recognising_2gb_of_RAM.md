To clarify what others have said, and to add my own bit.

Your sig says you have a presario c300, but that isn't quite enough detail to get a bios update. 

However, it's a new enough laptop, that going to 2 GB should NOT be a problem.

Your post indicates that XP once said it had 2gb of ram, and now it does not. It's not a bios problem. Therefor you have a hardware issue. It's definately not an issur with XP OR gutsy.

It could be something as simple as a stick of ram came loose. Or a failure of either a ram stick or ram slot.


To test:

Reseat the ram.

Test each stick in each slot.

That should be enough to confirm what exactly your problem is. 

If it was upgraded recently enough to be under warranty, send it back to the shop and make it their problem.
