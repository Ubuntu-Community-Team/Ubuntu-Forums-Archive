### Post by matthew on 2006-02-01
When you type your password it is not echoed to the screen. This way no one can see it over your shoulder or even how long it is. It's a security feature. This is by design. It's been this way on Unix style systems since the early 1970's so I don't expect it to change. Now that you know, you'll be fine.
