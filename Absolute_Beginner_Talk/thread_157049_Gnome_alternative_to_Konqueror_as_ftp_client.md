### Post by mostwanted on 2006-04-08
My favourite is nautilus 'cause it integrates my FTP site into the Places menu.

Click the "Connect to server..." or similar in the places menu to make an FTP share. Then you can open that like any other folder (it will be a bookmark in your Places menu). I just open another Nautilus window and drag and drop the files back and forward between them.

Much better than the dreaded gFTP.
