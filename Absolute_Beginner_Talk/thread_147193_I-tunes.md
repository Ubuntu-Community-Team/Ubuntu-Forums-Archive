### Post by celloandy on 2006-03-20
Podcast support has been added to Rhythmbox in Dapper, and it's in the works for Banshee.  At this point, I would say Rhythmbox and Quod Libet are closest to iTunes in terms of the way they're organized (the artist/album browser, etc.), but again, in Banshee, a browser view is currently in the works.  Also of note, is that, again in the Dapper versions, Rhythmbox and Banshee both add support for DAAP, the protocol iTunes uses for music sharing, so you can use them to share your music with other iTunes user, and to listen to music they share.

Andrew
