### Post by bwallum on 2007-10-29
Hi

Take a look at Tools>Options>Language Settings

Check you have your language, then check the writing aids for dictionaries.

That should get you going for OpenOffice.

A natty little dictionary that you can use anywhere, I have one in my top panel, is called Dictionary. Use Applications>Add/Remove and search for Dictionary.
