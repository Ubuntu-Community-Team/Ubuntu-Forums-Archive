### Post by sab0403 on 2007-07-10
Add a new panel like you've done already. Then click on that new panel, click on "Add to panel", and in the "Desktop and Windows" section you'll see a "Window list". Add that, and you're set.

BTW, you can use that interface to add whatever you want to your panels. Browse around and have fun. Good luck!
