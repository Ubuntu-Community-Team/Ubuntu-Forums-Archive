Our preffered solution:
Putting a proxy between the router and our lan. The proxy would have one function: giving people access to internet with a limited amount of download. If he/she exceeds that, his or her connection would be cancelled. The person would need to go to the admin (in this case me) and pay me a bit to get more download limit.
