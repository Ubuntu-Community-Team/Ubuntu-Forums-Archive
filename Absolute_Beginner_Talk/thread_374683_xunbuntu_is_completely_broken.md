### Post by SoloSalsa on 2007-03-02
What is your brand of hard disk?  Usually the manufacturer makes a bootable utility (and usually, DOS-based) for cd or floppy.  They may include such fancy features as firmwares, cache check (like running memtest on the hard disk!), speed test, and advanced SMART tasks that only the manufacturer will bother to utilize.  The only bad thing is that they make these utilities for their own hard drives, so you can't really keep it and troubleshoot anyone (unless they have the right brand of hard drive).
