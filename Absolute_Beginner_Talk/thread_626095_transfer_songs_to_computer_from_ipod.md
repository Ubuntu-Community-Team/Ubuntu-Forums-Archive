### Post by parm289 on 2007-11-28
It's actually different than windows: just plug in your iPod, mount it (or it may be automounted), then go into the IPOD_CONTROL-->Music folder.  You should find a bunch of folders named Fxx, where x is any number.  Simply copy these folders onto your hard drive and import them to any media player.
