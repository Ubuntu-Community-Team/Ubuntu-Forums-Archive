### Post by JayTee on 2006-10-28
I just bought a MAG Innovision LT926Wb widescreen monitor. When I set it to 1440x900 the screen image is shrunk and there is black space on the right and bottom. I've read through all the postings here but haven't found a solution yet. I think it may be a bug in either XGL or Beryl 0.1.1. Someone else reported it on the [www.beryl-project.org](www.beryl-project.org) site as being a known bug in scaling. They mentioned that "organic scaling" seemed to fix it but I don't know where to set that in Beryl or XGL. I don't think it is hardware as Vista RC1 works with it fine.

[Edit] Found where to change scaling to organic. It was in the Scaling section of the Beryl Window Manager. Didn't help my problem though. Neither did turning off scaling. Still get a shrunk screen with black at the right side and bottom. I'm pasting a copy of my xorg.conf file in hopes someone out there has similar hardware and already found a solution for this.
