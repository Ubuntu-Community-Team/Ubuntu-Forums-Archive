### Post by Marsolin on 2006-10-06
I agree that [K9Copy]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/k9copy") is a good app for this, however [K3b]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/k3b") allows you to copy and burn the DVD from within the same program.

If you prefer something with a Gtk front-end then[Thoggen]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/thoggen") or [Shrinkta]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/shrinkta") are worth a look.
