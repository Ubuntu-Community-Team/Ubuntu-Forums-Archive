### Post by candtalan on 2007-07-07
I have a 4 in 1 card reader and also with compact flash I get some trouble. I even took one CF back to the shop and replaced it but the problem did not go away. I found the CF card was not recognised. However, it did actually work sometimes, but I could not predict when. It drove me nuts. I used also a windows dual boot pc and it also gave unpredictable behaviour in windows too with this card reaeder (I only have this one). Sometimes the CF was recognised ok. I have one 45-in-1 card reader (yes, 45 types...amazing) from König.
I attached the USB connected device to my PC running Dapper, and inserted a 4 GB Kingston CF (CF/4GB-U) into it. As expected an icon comes up nicely on the desktop. After a while, I unplugged and plugged it in again for some testing and suddenly it start to behave unpredicatable. Getting messages like 'can not mount'. A closer examination revealed that one of the 40-contact pins inside the reader were bent. These tiny relative long pins are very sensitive and not particular strong -- be careful when inserting CF's.
