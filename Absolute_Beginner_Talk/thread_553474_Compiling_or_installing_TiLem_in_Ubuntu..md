### Post by Maestro23 on 2007-09-17
Take a look at the "Compiling from Source" Section.  Be advised that compiling from source is not recommended to someone who is new to linux.  You might run into many dependency problems during the compiling process of the program.  It will tell you what it needs, and you will have to install those packages from Synaptic.

Compiling from Source: [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_compile_a_program_from_source_code](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_compile_a_program_from_source_code)
