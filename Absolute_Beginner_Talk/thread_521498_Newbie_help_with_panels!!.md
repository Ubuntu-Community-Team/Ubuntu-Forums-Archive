### Post by Dr Small on 2007-08-09
Do you have a bottom panel? if not, right click on the top panel, click "New Panel", drag it to the bottom of the screen (if it isn't there already). Right click on the new panel, and select "Add to Panel..." then go down and find "Window list' which will be under "Desktop & Windows". Click on it, and select Add.

This should add your window list back to your bottom panel.

Dr Small

### Post by dptxp on 2007-08-09
You can right click on any item in menu and left click on add launcher to panel.
You can right click on any panel and click on add panel.
You can move panel to top, left, right or bottom.
You can make make panels transparent by choosing solid colors in properties>background after right click.
You can use add to panel to add from a list that you shall see.
