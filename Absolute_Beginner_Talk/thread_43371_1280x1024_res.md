### Post by GTvulse on 2005-06-21
Read carefully through /var/log/X.0.log. There you'll find if there is any error loading the Nvdia drivers and also what resolution modes are accepted by yoru combination of video card and monitor. Even if your video card supports the screen resolution you want, your monitor may not (in fact your monitor Horizontal Frequency and Vertical Refresh rates suggest it doesn't).

Don't post the contents of /var/log/X.0.log, it is *too big*! If you feel you need to, please post only the relevant sections (say, five lines above and below the lines you are concerned about. That should give enough context.)
