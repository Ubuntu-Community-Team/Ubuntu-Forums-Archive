### Post by houstonbofh on 2007-03-29
Sure.  I did not realize it was not clear how to do it.  My bad. :)  So I go to the [www.ubuntu.com](www.ubuntu.com) site and go to Get it now, and Holy ****!  It's gone!  Time for a stickie!

So now skip the front page.  Go to [http://releases.ubuntu.com/](http://releases.ubuntu.com/) and click on the distribution you want.  Edgy is here. [http://releases.ubuntu.com/edgy/](http://releases.ubuntu.com/edgy/) Scrole down to "Alternate install CD" and pick your platform.  Unless you have a good reason, that is "PC (Intel x86) alternate install CD" which will download an iso you can burn.  Then when you boot it, it will walk you through a text based installer that is MUCH faster, even on fast systems.
