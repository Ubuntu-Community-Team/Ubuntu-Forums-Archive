### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-05-26
I think you maybe misunderstood the dialog. When you look at the dialog (screenshot attached), you have two choices - use the driver suggested and listed on the left by clicking a **Print a test page** or just clicking **Close**. Alternatively, you could install a completely different driver if you know you happen to have a better one by clicking **Install driver**.

That suggested driver is already installed (otherwise the printer type wouldn't be listed), so you need do nothing more than to say that's the printer/driver to use.
