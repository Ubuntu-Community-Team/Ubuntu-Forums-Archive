### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-07-11
You shouldn't be running a low-latency kernel for desktop use.  That will result in a draggy mouse and generally slow response to user input.  Low-latency is for music, video, or graphic editing--a specific workstation use.  You can boot into low-latency for a specific task, but it's not designed for desktop use.  That could be your problem.
