### Post by Princey on 2006-03-20
Just to report erik, the picture opened fine on my windows machine. It opened both in my browser and when I saved it to my hard drive. I noticed you said jpg and the subject said the same. However, in actuality, the picture comes with a *.png (portable network graphics) format. If they can't open it, they can look into getting a windows based free image viewer. There's one that just can't come to mind now. Or, you can use gimp or some other image viewer and convert them first to jpg. You would have to chose save as command though and chose jpg from the options "save as file type" box that comes up. Let me know if you need more help.
