### Post by Kvark on 2006-05-10
The menu in Ubuntu is not called "Start". There are 3 menues that are called "Programs", "Places" and "System". If you lost them then right click on the panel and choose add to panel, then add the item called "menu row".

Get used to that things have different names. It's Firefox instead of Internet Explorer, Evolution instead of Outlook, Open Office instead of Microsoft Office and so on.
