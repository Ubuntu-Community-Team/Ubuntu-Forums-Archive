### Post by bsussman on 2006-10-26
I think you are about to get many many responses.

It may help to organize and subdivide your question

Technical or User?
server or desktop?
Windows or commandline?
Administration or User?
Development or Application User?
Security?
Networking?

'Linux and Ubuntu' is a big knowledge space.

One specific recommendation - if you are sticking to ubuntu and variants, stick to materials related to debian-based systems.  The differences between them and other distributions are often trivial (a different location or file name) but will distract you.
