### Post by MagisterJoe on 2007-05-31
Well, most applications aren't written 'for a resolution' - the scaling for the button size, etc. should be handled by the desktop environment.  This isn't a problem with the application, it's most likely caused by your desktop manager in some way.  Unfortunately, someone more experienced than I will have to figure out what that is.
