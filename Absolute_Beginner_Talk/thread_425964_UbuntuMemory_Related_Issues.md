### Post by Schalken on 2007-04-28
Go to System > Administration > System Monitor > Resources to check your memeory usage. If some of the swap is being used (say, over 10%) then yes you are low on memory. You may be able to shave off a little by using Xfce rather than GNOME (install xubunu-desktop), but no promises.
