### Post by az on 2005-08-18
There are a few other printers that can be made to work with ubuntu by installing other drivers and such. 

By and large, check out linuxprinting.org.

That is the best site for linux printers.  I have become seriously depressed about the state of home computer printers.  They cost a bundle in ink.  The next time I buy a printer it will either have really cheap refillable cartidges available or be a used laser printer which uses postscript.

Photo printing is a joke.  I spent a bundle on the best ink and paper and I look at faded images of my daughter every morning when I open my locker.  I got better results from using walmart photo finishing.
