### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-08-23
It is easy to upgrade.  Generally speaking, you simply change your sources.list file to reflect the new repos (change the word 'dapper' to 'edgy').  However, I would caution against this right now because Edgy has not been officially released and you are most certainly going to run into bugs and/or crashes.  At its current development stage, it's not appropriate to use as your primary desktop OS (unless, of course, you'l like to test it out and don't mind hiccups along the way).  My advice would be to wait until Edgy gets officially released in October.  
If you want more info on this, check out the [Edgy threads]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=144") on UbuntuForums as well as [this post]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=232047").
