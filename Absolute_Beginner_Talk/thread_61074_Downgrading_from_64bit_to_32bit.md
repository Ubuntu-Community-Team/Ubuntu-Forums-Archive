### Post by Kapre on 2005-08-30
Bro-Marquis

If you want to re-install (which I did a lot of times already because of tinkering with Ubuntu), try to download the i386 version/32 bit version and re-install.

Your partition will remain the same as long as you will choose the partition where you 1st installed the 64-bit one. As for your XP, I dont think there will be a problem because it is on a separate partition and youre not touching it.

Grub will install (because you'll have this option again) and will detect your XP again.

Maybe there is still someone there with a better and faster solution.
