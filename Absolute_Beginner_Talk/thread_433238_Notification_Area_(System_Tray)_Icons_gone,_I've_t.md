### Post by kyphi on 2007-05-05
I don't know about Network Manager, I have Networking and Network Tools available under System -> Administration.  Is that what you mean?

To get the Beryl icon or any other to your panel right click on your desktop, scroll to Create Launcher.  After filling in the parameters for your launcher click on No Icon and select the appropriate icon from the list.

That will create an icon on your desktop that you can drag to the panel.
