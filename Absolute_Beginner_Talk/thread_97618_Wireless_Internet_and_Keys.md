### Post by adduds on 2005-12-01
double click on the "2 Computers icon" or if the network isn't working "2 computers w/ a !" default is in top right hand corner. If you believe it is WEP related

1. Double click Icon

2. Go to **Configure**

3. Type in admin password

4. click on Wireless Connection (It should say The interface eth1 is **active**) that means your card is working

5. click on that once

6. go to **properties**

7. If you card wasn't before properties "active" under **Connection** check off enable this device (their should be a check in the bubble)

8. Type in your SSID and enter your WEP key! :)

any other probs just ask
