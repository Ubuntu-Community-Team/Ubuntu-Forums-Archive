Hello,

Just letting anyone know who wants to know that Serpentine won't support mono mp3 or ogg files.

I just spent two hours trying to burn an audio CD from some mp3s and kept getting file conversion errors.  I tried converting to ogg and different mp3 quality and discovered it was the mono files that were causing the problem.
