If you want to view your linux filesystem you can go to your menu and choose Places>>Computer from the menu and then click on filesystem.

I'm thinking that you are asking how to view your windows partitions.  If this is the case, then you will need to mount your windows drives first. Below is a link to a HOW TO in the wiki on how you can mount your windows drives.
