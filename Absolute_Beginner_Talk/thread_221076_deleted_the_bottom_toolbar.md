### Post by Third Thoughts on 2006-07-22
I'm assuming you're using gnome right now. I'm not, so these instructions might be a little off, but I'm fairly sure about them. Just right click on your top toobar and select "Add Panel" This will create a new panel. Drag it down to the bottom and then right click on it. There should be a menu item that says something like "Add applet to panel." You can use that to put whatever stuff you want on the toolbar.
