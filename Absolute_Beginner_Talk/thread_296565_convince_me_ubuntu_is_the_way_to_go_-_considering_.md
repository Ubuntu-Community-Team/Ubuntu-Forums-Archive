### Post by professor_chaos on 2006-11-09
There are a lot of reasons people would want linux over mac.
But it really comes down to what you prefer. No one should try and convince you otherwise. 

It sounds tricky but, you can try dual-booting your G5
[http://www.askdavetaylor.com/how_do_i_dual_boot_ubuntu_linux_mac_os_x.html](http://www.askdavetaylor.com/how_do_i_dual_boot_ubuntu_linux_mac_os_x.html)

It would really be nice if you could test drive linux for a while to give you time to adjust and see if its for you.
