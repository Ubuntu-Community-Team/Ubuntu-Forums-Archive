### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-06-30
> **SnowPunk98 said:**
> When I'm in firefox if I accidently move forward/back on the bottom part of my trackpad it goes forward and back which I don't want. Can anyone tell me how I disable this function?

Ok, I'm not an expert at mouse and xorg, but I'm pretty sure about this. Open up your xorg (sudo nano /etc/X11/xorg.conf) and scroll down to find the section labelled (yours is track, may differ):

```
Section "InputDevice"
        Identifier      "Configured Mouse"
        Driver          "mouse"
        Option          "CorePointer"
        Option          "Device"                "/dev/input/mice"
        Option          "Protocol"              "ImPS/2"
[COLOR="Blue"]##[/COLOR]       [COLOR="Red"]Option          "ZAxisMapping"          "4 5"[/COLOR]
        Option          "Emulate3Buttons"       "true"
EndSection

```

I believe the red line should be what is causing the trouble. [COLOR="Blue"]comment it out as usual with #[/COLOR]. That should do it I believe, if your's is substantially different paste the mouse (or entire xorg) and I'll look at it. If I'm wrong, someone correct me :).
