### Post by Brunellus on 2005-08-27
Tell us how you connect to the internet usually.  Is it A) through a wire or B)over a wireless link?

if A), is that wire

1) a standard telephone cable
2) bigger than a standard telephone cable
3) something other than either of 1 or 2.

if A.1:  what is the make and model of the modem?

if A.2:  Does that cable connect to a Cable Modem or DSL Modem? 

if A.3:  Describe your connection in detail.

if B), general note:  wireless connections have been troublesome in Linux because of poor vendor driver provision.  What is the model of your wireless card?

***
Further general note:  "the internet" isn't something you configure--it's something you set up and access.  You can't really configure "the interent" any more than you can configure the geostationary satellites that give you sat-TV.  You CAN configure the way you access them.
