### Post by DoubleJ19446 on 2007-09-19
I once tried Ubuntu about two years ago and found myself totally lost.  I couldn't get anything to work, and toying around with partitions messed up my computer so bad I was going to the local computer store to get my computer reset to the condition I purchased it in for $100.00 in service charges.  FREE OS = $100.00 in newbie IQ/damage-related charges.

Yesterday was a whole different kind of story.  I purchased the Official Ubuntu Book second edition for about $25.00 after a store coupon.  I used the included software & guide to install my new OS.  I am now typing to you on-line about the ease-of-installation and use that I am experiencing this time around.

After market video card works perfectly, network cards are working great, wirelessly connected to my network printer with no problem.  Everything is going perfectly.  I just wanted to drop in with this message before I dove into another session of playing with my new toy for another few hours straight.  Thank you to everyone in the Ubuntu community for helping to make this enjoyable product.

Thank You,
