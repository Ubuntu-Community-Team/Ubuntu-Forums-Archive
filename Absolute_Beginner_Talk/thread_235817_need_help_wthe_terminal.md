### Post by eXcentra on 2006-08-13
for your ntfs problem, afaik, you can't write to ntfs.
for your mplayer problem, you can either setup your config file (ie. [this](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=96422) and [this](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=77329)) or just press your 'j' key. btw, i love mplayer because the subtitle options are really flexible. :D
As for the terminal thing, I just learned how to use the terminal/its commands by following how-to's on this forum. you'll get used to the simple commands (cd to change directory, cp to copy, rm to delete/remove, etc)
You can just [google](http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&lr=&q=terminal+commands+linux&btnG=Search) for some commands but i think it's more better/fun to learn through experience. ;)
