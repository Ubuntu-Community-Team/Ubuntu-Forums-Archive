### Post by Sef on 2006-04-04
1) when you downloaded the iso, did u do an md5sum check?  (if you used bit     torrent, then it did it automatically.)

2) Did you burn the iso at 4x or slower?  Faster can render the disk unreadable.

3) If those are fine, then to burn, you just burn it as an iso and not a data file.
