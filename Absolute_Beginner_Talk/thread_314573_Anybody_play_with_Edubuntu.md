### Post by Makurosu on 2006-12-07
I have an extra computer that I set up Edubuntu on for my three year old.  I didn't know what to expect, but he just loves it.  I just didn't want to have to constantly be updating malware programs and installing patches.  His favorite thing is TuxPaint, especially the "magic" tools and stamps.  Also, he plays with GCompris a lot.  It has big icons and a big cursor and nice, calming music.  It's very cute to watch him play all the games in GCompris.  His computer keeps him entertained for a long time, and he has found everything himself by going through the menus and clicking everything.  Most kids might not do that, but my kid is a genius, of course. ;)

I suppose Edubuntu was designed for the classroom, but I think it's really fun just for kids to play around on.  From what I've seen, there seem to be educational programs for all ages of children up to high school.  I haven't tried everything yet though.  I just wondered if anybody else has kids that love it as much as mine does.

Right now, he and I are both using our computers.  I run Ubuntu on my machine as well.  I asked him what he thinks of the GCompris music so I can report it here.  He says it's "creepy." lol
