Is there a way I can have windows from all workspaces show up in the gnome taskbar?  Similar to the way KDE does it?

---

### Post by Ek0nomik on 2007-05-26
If you right click where all your windows are being listed in the panel (the left of the very far left window in the panel, you will see a little divider looking like icon), you can change the preferences in that.
