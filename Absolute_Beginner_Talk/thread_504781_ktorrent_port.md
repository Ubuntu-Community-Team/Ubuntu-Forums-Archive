### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-07-19
> **rickycodie said:**
> ktorrent has a upnp plugin that automatically forwards your ports.

Yes, most torrent clients do, but it's not always as effective as forwarding ports.  Some ports get reserved by other computers on the network and take forever to become free again, and the software can be slow to adapt.  Forwarding a range of ports (like 100 or so) starting at your declared port can tend to prevent problems and slow speeds.
