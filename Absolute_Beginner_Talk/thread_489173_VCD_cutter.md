### Post by davidjmayo on 2007-07-02
There are tools available. Look in Applications==>Add/remove

when this is loaded click Multimedia

this will display a list with brief descriptions. Choose what you want by clicking in the white box. you then see a black **x** there
next click on the button Apply changes (or something similar) and wait for it to download

Try the program. If it doesn't do what you want or you don't like it go back into Add/remove and remove it by clicking the black **x** to go back to the white box and again click Apply.

I haven't used any of these so can't recommend one. If you wanted more info before you installed try a search by the name on the net and you should find what formats it supports, screenshots etc.
