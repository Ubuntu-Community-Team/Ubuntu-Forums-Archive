### Post by canadianwriterman on 2005-12-08
Ubuntu, as a Debian-based distro uses DEB files for installation, rather than RPMs. There is a utility called Alien that you can use to convert RPMs to DEBs, but I understand that it may not be 100% reliable. If you check the Synaptic utility in Ubuntu, you may find the programs you'd like to install and then do your installs without having to worry about RMPs or DEBs.
