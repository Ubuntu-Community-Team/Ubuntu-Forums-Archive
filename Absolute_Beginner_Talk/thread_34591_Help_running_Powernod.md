### Post by dave9191 on 2005-05-15
Powernowd is a program that runs in the background adjusting you CPU speed. Its not something that you can put onto your panel/taskbar. 

What you probably mean is the CPU freq monitoring applet that you can put on your task bar that tells your cpu speed. Right click on the panel and go to add applet ( or something like that ).  Im assuming you are using Gnome, and Im not, so i cant give detailed instructions on how to add the applet.
