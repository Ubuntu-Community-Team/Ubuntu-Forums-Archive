### Post by kebes on 2007-01-25
Considerable help can be found on the [Beryl]("http://www.beryl-project.org/") wiki:
[http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Main_Page](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Main_Page)

And the Beryl forums:
[http://forum.beryl-project.org/](http://forum.beryl-project.org/)


In particular they have guides for installing on Ubuntu:
[http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu)


But, yes, all of these guides require a certain number of manual configuraiton changes. (However it is much simpler and easier now than it was a year ago! The process has been streamlined quite a bit.)

At present there is no way to install it "completely painlessly." But that's the price you pay for installing the "latest and greatest" cutting-edge software!

If you do attempt the install, you can find lots of help in the Beryl forums and here in ubuntuforums.
