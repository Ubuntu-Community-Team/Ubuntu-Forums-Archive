After about the fourth try to reinstall , after playing with restricted drivers and trying to get higher than 800 x 600 mode (uninstalling the restricted nvidea drivers usually crashes the xserver permanently , and often the entire ubuntu installation) ... I came to a shocking discovery. 

Ubuntu cheats. 

It reads your configuration , at the very least your video configuration , before wiping everything down and doing a so called "fresh " install. 

I know this because on my last install it crashed the hard drive so hard it was unreadable (could not mount hd0 , was the complaint). Reinstalling at that point ... was a perfect install. Video mode was at 1024 x 768 , every thing worked like a charm. 

Only when it couldn't read the hard drive did it do a perfect install ??? 

Ok then , whats the command for reformatting linux and erasing everything ? For future reference, you understand. 

UBUNTU 7.04

The music box automatically installed the codex's to play mp3's (ok, I had to click on one or two buttons, but there weren't any obscure command lines required ) and I was up and running on that front. 

SAMBA comes pre installed. Clicked on network and it found my xp shared folder on my other box and I was able to download music and play it. I even switched over to xp and made a folder for linux just to do back ups. 

Speed :
   Either I'm crazy or my constant reinstalls of Ubuntu kept inheriting the previous install's mistakes , despite my attempts to make them "clean installs". Either that or 7.04 is a lot faster than 6.10. Fire fox opens way faster and surfs faster at the very least. Maybe it's just a new version of fire fox, the about notes list it as ubuntu-feisty . 

Conclusion
   Looking good so far, but I'm staying away from those restricted Nvidea drivers. Sorry , they caused me so many reinstalls I'm a little gun shy.  Besides , I got my 1000 x 700 + screen resolution , I don't need them  :)
