### Post by JayTee on 2006-12-17
You won't get water working with the i810 driver. While the chipset will support pixel shading for some reasone the developers didn't include it in the current drivers for Edgy. I have the same chipset on one of my systems and it produces the same error trying to load the water effect. This is a known problem and is posted on [http://forum.beryl-project.org](http://forum.beryl-project.org).
