### Post by laidback on 2007-07-23
I've just installed Amarok onto a laptop, it's now reading my ipod. Try this, Settings>Configure Amarok>Media Devices choose plugin Apple ipod from the drop down at the top of the page, now press Autodetect Devices, when finished press OK. Ipod is in the USB socket thoughout.
