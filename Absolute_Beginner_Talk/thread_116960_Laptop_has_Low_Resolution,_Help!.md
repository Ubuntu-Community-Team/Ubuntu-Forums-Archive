### Post by Dr. Nick on 2006-01-27
Well I think I remembered what I changed to make it work correctly. I am posting my xorg.conf for my armada 7400 with a 14in. screen. The option that is most likely causing problems is the monitor section, try adding these refresh rates and see if it helps. Also my color depth is 16 not 24, I had 24 while on breezy , but it doesnt seem to work with the dapper ver of xorg so I dropped it to 16.

Section "Files"
        FontPath        "/usr/share/X11/fonts/misc"
        FontPath        "/usr/share/X11/fonts/cyrillic"
        FontPath        "/usr/share/X11/fonts/100dpi/:unscaled"
        FontPath        "/usr/share/X11/fonts/75dpi/:unscaled"
