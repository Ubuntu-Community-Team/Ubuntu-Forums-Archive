### Post by bobbybobington on 2007-06-04
From what I could dig up this usb wifi has been giving ubuntu (or any linux distro) a lot of pain. I would suggest getting a usb wifi adapter that is based off the [Ralink]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ralink") chipset. The company that makes the chipset is open source friendly so it has good support. Try looking at [this list]("http://ralink.rapla.net/") of wireless devices based off of the Ralink chipset , under the usb column. I wish I could help you more with the usb adapter you already have, but I'm pretty clueless when it comes to  [NdisWrapper]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ndiswrapper"):( .
