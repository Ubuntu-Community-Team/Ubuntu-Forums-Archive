### Post by Joe Moren on 2007-11-26
Hi, I use Evolution to retrieve my Juno & Gmail accounts. My Juno is a paid account & therefore supports POP & SMTP. Check out Gmail as they allow POP & SMTP on your free accounts.  In the past I have also used 'freepops' to retrieve mail from Netzero, Juno & Yahoo, all free accounts. You can set up Evo by >Edit >Preferences >Mail Account. Hope this helps!
