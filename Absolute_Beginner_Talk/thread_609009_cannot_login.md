### Post by matthewcraig on 2007-11-10
Download an installation disk (you'll want to keep one of these around anyway) at UBUNTU.COM, burn the ISO image on the machine you are on right now, then boot to the disk on your new computer.  The installation disk will walk you through the process of formatting the disk drive and installing the operating system.

### Post by reckless2k2 on 2007-11-10
you can just boot into recovery mode and add a user or reset the passwd. you have quite a few options. at startup, you should see at the top of screen about hitting esc. this will bring up the boot menu and you would choose recovery mode. it'll bring you to the terminal with root access. reset passwords or create users and make them admin group and you're fine.
