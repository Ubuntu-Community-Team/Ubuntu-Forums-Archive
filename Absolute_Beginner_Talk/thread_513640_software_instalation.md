### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2007-07-30
You don't need ad-ware scanners or even virus scanners in Ubuntu.  There are virus scanners, but they mostly prevent you from passing viruses on to windows users (we don't need the protection).  However, a good number of us feel that it is windows users' duty to protect their own machines, don't waste your system resources on it.
You can search for programs in add/remove or in Synaptic, and can view a large list of available apps.  If you have any specific questions for certain types of programs, just ask - everybody has their own opinions.
