### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-10-12
In my opinion, Automatix is a more robust app that allows for the download/install of many, many apps vs. EasyUbuntu which only does a few.  If you just need to hit the ground running, EasyUbuntu is quick to use.  On the other hand, if you want more, Automatix is the way to go.

[http://www.getautomatix.com/](http://www.getautomatix.com/)
[http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org/](http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org/)
