### Post by pistcivet on 2007-11-13
I've found it to be **much** easier to partition before installing a Linux distro.

They all tend to use different tools to partition,
and its great to just bybass that step during the install.

I do all my partitioning before-hand with the GParted live CD:
[http://gparted-livecd.tuxfamily.org/](http://gparted-livecd.tuxfamily.org/)

Haven't tried it yet, but I hear good things about Parted Magic too:
[http://partedmagic.com/](http://partedmagic.com/)
