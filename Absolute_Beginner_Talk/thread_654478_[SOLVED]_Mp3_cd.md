### Post by SOULRiDER on 2007-12-31
Go to Places > CD/DVD Creator and drag all MP3s to the creators window. Then hot the Write Disc button and its done.

### Post by The Cog on 2007-12-31
In Ubuntu, click your Home icon to get a file explorer (nautilus). 
Menu Go->CD Creator.
Menu File->New Window
Find the MP3s in the new window and drag them to the CD creator window.
Put a  blank CD in the drive
Click Burn

That should cover it.
