So here's the difference from the way I see it.  I myself use XFCE so not all apply to my WM.

Splash screens =>  when you log in it's that picture that you get before it loads to your desktop.  This can be replaced by other splash screen themes.

Desktop Themes => This could be either wallpapers or the GTK/Metacity themes that get applied to your Window borders and colors of the panels in your window manager

Icon themes => Specific themes that can be applied to your icons so you can change the look and feel of your icons

And the list goes on and on...

Now, the good thing is that it's all customizable and you can change it with ease.  You can choose to use the one's provided from the install - or you can get your own.  Check out these two sites:

[http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org)
[http://art.gnome.org](http://art.gnome.org)

Most of all - have fun with it....
