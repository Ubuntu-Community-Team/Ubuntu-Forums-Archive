### Post by HermanAB on 2007-10-10
Virtualbox is based on Qemu, so they are pretty much the same.  They work properly with Windoze XP, but do not work with Windoze 2003.

VMware works with all versions of Windows.

So, since you wish to run XP, it doesn't matter much what you choose.
