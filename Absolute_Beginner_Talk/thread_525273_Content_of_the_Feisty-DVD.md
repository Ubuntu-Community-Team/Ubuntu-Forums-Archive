### Post by misfitpierce on 2007-08-14
Indeed its up to you... I prefer Kubuntu which is KDE. KDE has it's differences from Gnome and many will choose on many sides of stuff. 1 thing I will agree with is themeing and such is made way simpler in Gnome. That said if starting off I would go the Gnome route as intended since it is the main interface for the distro and then once you get the reigns go ahead and try out KDE.

Xubuntu on the other hand is a lighter version of Ubuntu made to run on newer or older machines but was built around the use of using on an old machine making it very fast on any machine. XFCE is the interface and the app's still hold alot of power but are very light making a light and powerful interface and workspace. Try out Ubuntu then pick another flavor unless your machine is older (ex: PII or PIII) then go Xubuntu XFCE interface. Thats my output :)
