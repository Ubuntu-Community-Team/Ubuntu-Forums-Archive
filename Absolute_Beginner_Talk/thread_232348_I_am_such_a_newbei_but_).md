### Post by JohnJSal on 2006-08-08
Hi there! In my BIOS, I believe CDs are listed before the hard drive as well, yet it always skips the CD and goes straight to Windows too.

What you need to do is press the Boot key for your system while it is starting up, before it begins Windows. In my case, I have a Dell Inspiron 8600 and during the BIOS load screen I have to press F12, which then brings me to a one-time boot screen. From there I can choose to boot from the CD.

Granted, this isn't convenient if it isn't a one-time thing, since you have to do it manually.
