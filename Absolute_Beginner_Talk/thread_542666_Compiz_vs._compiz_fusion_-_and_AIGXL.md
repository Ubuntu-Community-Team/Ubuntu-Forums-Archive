### Post by mckryptyk on 2007-09-04
You have Compiz-Fusion from Amaranths repository. You can tell it is compiz-fusion from its version number. (found in synaptic)

Older versions are just regular compiz.

The "Fusion" means the infusion of Beryl back into the Compiz codebase.
