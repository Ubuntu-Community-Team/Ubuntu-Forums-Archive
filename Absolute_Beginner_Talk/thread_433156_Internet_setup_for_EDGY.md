### Post by Bartender on 2007-05-05
Olds -
I'm about 99% sure that you'll have to power down the router/modem.  Don't just turn off the power switch, you have to unplug it for a minute or so.  Otherwise it's still expecting the other PC's address and you'll have problems.
Also, plug in the PC to the router or modem before turning on the Linux PC, not after.
