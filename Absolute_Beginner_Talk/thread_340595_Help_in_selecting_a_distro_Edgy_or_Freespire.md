### Post by Pobega on 2007-01-17
Ubuntu Edgy is almost fully stable, but I think it's still considered testing. If you want stability download 6.06 (Dapper) and don't upgrade it to Edgy.

Also, I'd stick with Ubuntu because I personally find that the community here is extremely helpful and friendly, I'm not sure about Freespire but all I know is that if you google "Forums" Ubuntu is the third one on the list.

Ubuntu is also a very good distro, although people say it is a dumbed down Linux I personally find it still as hard to use as any other distro; People just seem to get a bad idea about Ubuntu because of it's popularity.
