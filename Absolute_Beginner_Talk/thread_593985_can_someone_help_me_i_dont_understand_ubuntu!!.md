### Post by Haus Neuerburg on 2007-10-27
you can determine where your downloads shall go in your browser. For example in FF2 you choose Edit-Preferences-first tab (should be general or something like that, in German it's called "Allgemein") and there you can tick the download-options and determine where they should be saved. Should be very similar in any other browser.
