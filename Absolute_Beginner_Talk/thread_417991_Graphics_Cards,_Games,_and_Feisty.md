Games today are very graphics intense.  When you ask if this card will be good with modern games, I would have to say to a point.  Most of the latest and greatest will do best with at least 512MB of video memory.

As for the way that you describe to play games in Feisty, I don't believe it will work.  You are probably relegated to using either Wine or Cedegacvs.  They don't like to be called Windows emulators, but a compatibility layer for running Windows programs.
