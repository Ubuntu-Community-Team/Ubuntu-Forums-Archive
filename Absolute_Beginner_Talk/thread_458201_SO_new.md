### Post by Delkster on 2007-05-29
> **JeremyG said:**
> im new and ignorant of the linux operating system i need to find out if my computer is compatable with the desktop effects of cube if so how do i manipulate it

Compatibility depends mostly on your video card, and of course to a certain degree on your processor speed (not really a compatibility matter but you need some power also in that department to comfortably use desktop effects). Anyway, tell us what video card your computer has and we can probably tell or at least give an educated guess whether it will work.

All in all, I wouldn't recommend using the desktop effects for anything serious if you're new to Linux. There are still problems with them. Many of them can be circumvented *if* you know your way around in Linux, but someone not familiar with the system in general might have more difficulties. If you only want to experiment with them, go ahead, but for example I don't currently use on my system because there are still a few too many small but annoying problems.
