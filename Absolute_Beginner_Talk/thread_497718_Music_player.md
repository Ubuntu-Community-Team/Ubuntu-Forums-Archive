### Post by Sparkster185 on 2007-07-10
Go to Applications -> Add/Remove.

From there, find/install gDesklets.  Once this is done, start it up.  Under the gDesklets menu, you should see an option to get more desklets/applets/etc.  From here, they are separated into categories.  I believe the Rythymbox controllers are under Media, or something fairly obvious.
