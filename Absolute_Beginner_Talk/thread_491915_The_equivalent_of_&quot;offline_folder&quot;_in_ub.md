### Post by spd106 on 2007-07-05
I don't think nautilus has this feature, though possibly konquerer (in Kubuntu) might. There are a few tools that could do this among other things, see  [Conduit]("http://www.conduit-project.org/"), [Grsync]("http://www.gnomefiles.org/app.php/Grsync") or perhaps [Unison]("http://www.cis.upenn.edu/~bcpierce/unison/"). They will need setting up properly though.
