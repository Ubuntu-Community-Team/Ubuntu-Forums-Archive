### Post by Madpilot on 2006-03-04
There are lots.

OpenOffice can export any of it's formats as PDF; so can most of the other smaller wordprocessors available (Abiword, Koffice, etc)

Scribus is a full Desktop Publishing (DTP) application, very powerful, that is designed to produce PDFs for printing.

I'm not sure if the GIMP or Inkscape can export as PDF - I've just been importing graphics into Scribus and producing my PDFs there.

There are also lots of more obscure PDF creation methods - command line utilities, LaTeX/TeX applications, etc etc.

### Post by soce_32 on 2006-03-04
