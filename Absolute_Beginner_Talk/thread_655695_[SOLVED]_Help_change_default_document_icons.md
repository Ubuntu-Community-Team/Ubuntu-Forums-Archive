### Post by kyphi on 2008-01-01
Create a file in your home folder (call it Icons, if you like) and place your new icons in that.

Open your menu editor (mine is under Applications), scroll down to Office and then right click on the menu entry for Writer.  From the resulting dropdown menu choose Properties.  Left click on the icon there and from the next menu choose Browse.  Browse to your home folder and your new Icon file and select the icon that you want to use.

Repeat for the other icons.

Doing it this way, you can restore the original icons easily should you ever wish to do so.
