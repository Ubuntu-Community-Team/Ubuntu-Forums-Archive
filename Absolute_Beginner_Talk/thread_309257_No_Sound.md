### Post by jlx on 2006-11-29
Well, that is a hard question. There could be thousand of millions of millions of causes because you can't hear anything. 
First thing you should do is to find the model and manufacturer of your sound car and search in the forums if somebody had the same problem or a similar one. If you can't find anything in the forums then it's time to create a post asking for help. But you have to write your sound card model, your hardware settings and the program you are using to play sounds. I think that could be the beginning of the end of your problem.
Good luck.
