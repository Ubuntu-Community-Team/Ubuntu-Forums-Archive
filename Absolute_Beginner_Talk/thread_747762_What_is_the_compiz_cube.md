### Post by dizee on 2008-04-06
You can enable it by ticking the box for "Enable Desktop Cube" in the Advanced Desktop Settings. To spin it, tick the "Rotate Desktop Cube" box, then press Ctrl+Alt and drag your mouse left or right while holding these keys down.

Assuming you have Compiz-fusion installed of course ;)
