### Post by herrweh on 2006-10-20
I create a folder on my desktop labeled "Wallpaper." then I google "Linux wallpaper" or "JPG wallpaper." When you find a wallpaper you like, right click on it and save it to your folder. Once you have several wallpapers in the folder, close your browser and open the folder. Choose a pic, right click on it and select "actions" -> "set as wallpaper (or background)" -> "centered." That should do it.
Good luck.
