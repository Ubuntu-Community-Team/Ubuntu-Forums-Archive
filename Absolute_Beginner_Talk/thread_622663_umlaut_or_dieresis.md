### Post by argie on 2007-11-25
It's easy. Compose + " (Shift and the ' key, that's double quotes) and then you hit u or a or o or another " and you get ü, ä, ö, ¨. Have fun.

If you're having trouble, it may have something to do with the encoding the files are saved in or the encoding the program runs in. I don't know about NVU but what I'm talking about is an equivalent to the View » Character Encoding menu in Firefox.
