### Post by phidia on 2008-01-30
It will probably help if you take a look at some of the how tos on intel video cards at the multimedia & video forum-one such thread is [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=461263&highlight=intel+845G")

You may be using a very generic driver like the vesa driver.

You can open a terminal, type sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg and use that program to configure your graphics card. You can run through it as many times as you need to. Be prepared to answer questions about your card and monitor. Many entries can be left blank. With your card I think you want to chose the i810 driver.
Hope that helps.
