ACPI is a power-management system which allows the operating system to adjust screen brightness, CPU speed, fan operation, and similar functions to reduce the amount of electrical power your computer uses.  It is also vital to such functions as Suspend and Hibernate.

RSDP I'm a little fuzzy on, but I believe it's tied in to ACPI.
