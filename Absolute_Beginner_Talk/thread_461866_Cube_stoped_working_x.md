### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-06-02
Hi. That happened to me on a fresh Feisty install, and problem is that sometimes Compiz reduces your 4 workspaces to only 1. If this is the same issue, the easiest (and best) way to solve this, I think, is to install **gnome-compiz-manager** (look for it through Synaptic); once that's installed, it will appear in "System-->Preferences-->GL Desktop". Open it, go to the workspaces tab, and select **Number of viewports: 4**.
