### Post by Harpoon on 2007-09-09
I can only speak about my experience, but with Feisty the answer seems to be Yes.  I picked up a cheap Lenovo 3000 N100 and installed Edgy (64bit) and had to fuss a little.  I upgraded to Feisty and had no wireless problems at all.  Had to add "915resolution" to get the 1280x800 (but the default works, just isn't perfect in terms of aspect ratio).  I don't use a webcam, didn't think the fingerprint reader was worth the trouble, and I don't presently have any bluetooth devices, so I am not sure how "out of the box" those are.

I'm not promoting the Lenovo, just telling you what works.  Check out the specs and try to get a machine that matches pretty closely and you will save hours of grief.

Good luck with the purchase - - and in school.
