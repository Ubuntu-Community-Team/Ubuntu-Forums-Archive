### Post by p_quarles on 2007-08-08
Most of the apps are completely compatible with either desktop. There are a few that conflict with each other, but your package manager will warn you before you try to install those.

the only drawback is that it requires your hard drive to keep two separate toolkits. This only matters if relatively small amounts of hard drive space are an issue.
