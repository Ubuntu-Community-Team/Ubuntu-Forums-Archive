### Post by ticopelp on 2007-06-08
If the resolution you've chosen isn't in the xorg.conf, it won't stay put after reboot. Believe me, I went through some very similar pain with my new widescreen monitor a few weeks ago :D

This is a very common issue, if you do a search for screen resolution and / or your model of monitor, you will probably find a solution.
