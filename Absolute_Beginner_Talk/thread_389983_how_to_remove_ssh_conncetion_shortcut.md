### Post by PaganBlasphemy on 2008-02-04
I answered my own question,and figured I'd post it for 'future reference'

Q: How did you manage to pull up a right-click menu?  Mine right click acts just like a left-click.
A: Click on 'Places > Network' and the shortcuts created by 'connect to server' are also located there.  Right click and select unmount from that location.
