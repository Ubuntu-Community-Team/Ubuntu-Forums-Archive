### Post by kripkenstein on 2006-07-27
First, make sure you are following the instructions exactly.

Then, if it still doesn't work, here is a possible idea: what kind of ISP are you using? If it's a normal commercial one, everything should be ok there. But some e.g. academic ISPs limit the ports you can use. They might be blocking the Jabber ports used by Google. If this is the issue, try changing the ports a bit (add 1 to the suggestion Google give in their howto, I have had to use 5223, 5222 or 5224 on different ISPs).
