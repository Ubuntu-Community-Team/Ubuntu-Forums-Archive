### Post by samichx on 2007-08-14
Not to shine on again about OGM, it is however a versatile format with its own ripping program which I believe is in the repos. ogmrip will take multiple audio tracks into the end file as well as some subtitles if configured correctly. This tool has been critical in the quest to save space by digitizing my foreign language DVD collection.

It is better than MKV in terms of quality on my set top NorhTec MythTV setup. Small fast and clear and cost less than a D-link, Apple or Microsoft Media server.
