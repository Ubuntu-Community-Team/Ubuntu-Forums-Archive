### Post by nichipet on 2007-07-13
I'm still rusty on Linux (used it 10 years ago, just returning to it), so I'll only answer the first question. Yes, it is mkdir. In fact, your form - mkdir /myname/home/foldername/ - is perfect in that it is an absolute path, meaning you won't find out you accidentally made foldername in /etc or /myname/home/downloads/ for example. A quicker way when you know you are in the right place is "mkdir foldername". (without quotes)
