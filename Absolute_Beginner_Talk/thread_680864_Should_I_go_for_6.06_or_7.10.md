### Post by emarkd on 2008-01-28
My laptop is three years old as well and has every version of Ubuntu on it since I got it, but two of the three issues you specifically mention still aren't really resolved.

Battery life has never been as good as with Windows.  I think Linux power management still has a ways to go.  My solution was to buy the larger capacity battery, which gets about as much life with Linux as the standard battery does with Windows.

I got standby (suspend to ram) working, although it never did out of the box with any Ubuntu.  I've never gotten hibernate (suspend to disk) to be reliable.

I've never had network troubles.  I think that's just an issue of your network card being well supported or not.
