### Post by kidders on 2007-11-08
Hi there,

Linux has a great many PDF manipulation utilities, but I'm not aware of one specifically designed to do what Tofu does. I'd say the best you'll be able to do is find a utility (or combination of utilities) that can be made to replicate Tofu's functionality reasonably easily ... but not straight out of the box. Another possible approach might be PDF transformation. Perhaps by converting PDFs into other document formats (eg HTML), they could be made easier to read.

Either way, you'll probably need to do a little research & experimentation to get the effect you're looking for.
