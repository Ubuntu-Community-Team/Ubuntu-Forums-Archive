### Post by jdfreshwater on 2007-03-13
Your best bet for Firefox is to export the bookmarks to a file and then import them in the new computer.  This may also what you need to do for Evolution.  I know it may not be the best answer, but it is probably the most simple and easiest to do.

### Post by dstew on 2007-03-13
The email accounts and bookmarks are stored as configuration files in other operating system folders. You have to export your address book and accounts and bookmarks, move the files, and import into your Mozilla and Firefox programs on your new pc.
