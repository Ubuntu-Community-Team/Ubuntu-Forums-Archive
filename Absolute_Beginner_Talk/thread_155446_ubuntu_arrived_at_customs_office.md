### Post by localzuk on 2006-04-05
It is likely due to confusion over the amount of tax being paid - none. Customs do not, normally, understand the concept of free products so they believe that you must have paid for the items and therefore must owe some tax. 

As someone else says, download the letter available at [http://www.ubuntu.com/support/faq](http://www.ubuntu.com/support/faq) and show them this... It *should* sort it out.
