### Post by neurostu on 2008-02-19
its funny you should mention using a shuttle as a MediaCenter PC. I tried that out a few weeks ago but it didn't work out for me. 

I do not know how much you have used shuttles but  would recommend against a shuttle if you plan on opening them up a lot. They're small form factor is FANTASTIC but again they are a real pain to work with.

However, if you want to use a shuttle, I would personally buy the AMD machine as it is a little bit cheaper. 

Where you really want to spend your money is on a good video capture card, you want something that encodes on the card, the better the card the better the picture!  I bought a wintv pvr 150 and it was terrible. (The reason I didn't keep the Mythbuntu machine).

So in the end either of the shuttles will work, but if you're not stuck on the small size get something with a  bigger case.

 Mythbuntu worked great for me, but it took me a good 3 to 4 hours to get everything working.
