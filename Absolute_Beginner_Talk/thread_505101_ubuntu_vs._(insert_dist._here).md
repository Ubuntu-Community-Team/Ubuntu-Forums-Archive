### Post by ARandomKid on 2007-07-19
For a distro, if you can get enough RAM on this and not just 123MB like myself, I would recommend this distro (Ubuntu) to you. Otherwise, I'd recommend xubuntu. They're basically the same, but xubuntu will run with CONSIDERABLY less RAM. The downside is that while Ubuntu will ship you CDs for free (shipit.ubuntu.org), Xubuntu does not due to lack of funding, and you must download and burn them yourself.

I was a total newbie to all of this terminal stuff up until about a month ago or so, and I've advanced enough to be able to survive at least.

The terminal code is really easy to learn, simply asking questions and browsing on here is enough to pick up the basics (updating/installing with apt-get). Unfortunately, I can't help you with the laptop choices...only the distro.

But, at least it's something, right? ;^.^

Good luck!
