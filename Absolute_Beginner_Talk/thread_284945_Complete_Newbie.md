### Post by John.Michael.Kane on 2006-10-26
USB:Works 99% of the deviecs are mounted,and seen correctly

IEEE 1394 should work however I have done no formal tests on these devices.

Video drivers ATI/NVIDIA/INTEL are supported

SoundCard again 99% of the sound card/chips are supported.

Digital cameras and video cameras: For the most part are seen as a new
drive,and mounted.

Security lockdowns If you open ports then you must know what you doing. so you will protect youself. Note: On a standard install of ubuntu Ports are closed.

Firewalls: most here that run firewalls are doing so casue they have servers or ports open they need to monitor.

DVD Burning software yes theres a few programs that will burn dvd's for you.
