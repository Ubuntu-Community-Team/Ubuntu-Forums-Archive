network-manager or the network icon on the panel? networ-manager is not installed by default, so if you did not install it yourself you must be looking at the regular icon.

On the panel go to System > Administration > Networking and see if your card is listed there?
If it is, click on it then on properties and check "enable". This will attempt to configure your interface, be sure to enter your appropriate DND server info and any settings (essid,etc) that you need for your access point.
If not, please post back here with more information on your card - make, model, etc. most cards will eventually work, but some take more effort than others.
