### Post by Bachstelze on 2006-08-27
Intall thoe packages if you want to play them in Totem : 

totem-xine
libxine-main1
libxine-extracodecs
w32codecs 


You could also try VLC or mplayer, VLC doen't need the codecs installed since it has it's own. Alo instal libdvdcss2 if you want to play DVDs.
