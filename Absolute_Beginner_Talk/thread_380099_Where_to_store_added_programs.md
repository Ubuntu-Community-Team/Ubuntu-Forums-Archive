### Post by andreas64 on 2007-03-09
Hi,

no, you don't have to reinstall your programs. If you used Synaptic for installing, they will also automatically  be updated.

All programs are normally stored in /usr; the configuration files in /etc. Only manually installed or additionally programs (outside the distribution like eg. GoogleEarth) are stored in /opt.
