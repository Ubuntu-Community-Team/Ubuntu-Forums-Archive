### Post by kyphi on 2007-11-09
Check this URL [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsSoundCards](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsSoundCards)

I use a Creative Soundblaster Live 5.1 - this card is inexpensive and has worked in all releases of Ubuntu.  The driver is already built into the kernel.

Remember to disable your onboard sound in the BIOS.
