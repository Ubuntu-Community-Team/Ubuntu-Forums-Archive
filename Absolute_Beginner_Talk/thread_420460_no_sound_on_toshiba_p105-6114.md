### Post by digital_sabotage on 2007-04-23
at the terminal you can type > lspci

your sound card should be listed there if it's detected ...yamaha xg?  ...apparently that card should be detected as an i810 (so i read somewhere)

...another thing you can check is at startup in your bios settings make sure your audio is enabled ...this happened to me when i did an install of ubuntu ...it messed with bios settings for some reason ...re-enabled audio and worked fine from there on out ...hope this minimal info i've given helps a bit in troubleshooting ...good luck:-)
