### Post by bryncoles on 2008-04-06
i had a similar issue a while back. i solved it by opening up audio properties (system -> preferences -> volume control, or just double click the speaker icon on your task bar). having opened audio properties, click edit -> preferences. check 'capture' from this list and click ok. then, in volume control, go to the 'recording' tab, and make sure capture isnt muted or the volume tuned down. mine was and as soon as i resolved this issue i was able to record again!
