### Post by DezSP on 2007-09-16
Add both gDesklets and the Beryl Manager to your session (System -> Preferences -> Session). I believe the commands are gdesklets and beryl-manager, respectively (but don't quote me on that).

I am not aware of any partuicular compatibility problems with the two apps, and I certainly didn't have any problem with them running side-by-side. Your case could be different, of course.
