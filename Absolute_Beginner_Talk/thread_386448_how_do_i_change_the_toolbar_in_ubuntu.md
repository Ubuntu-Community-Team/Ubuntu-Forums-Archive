### Post by tom56 on 2007-03-17
As said before just move everything to the lower panel and delete (right-click -> "Delete this panel") the top one. To move an icon/applet/whatever just right-click and uncheck "Lock" then right-click again click "Move" and drag it over to the ther panel. If you find that the default menu "Applications, Places, System" takes up too much space try using the other menu by right-clicking the panel clicking "Add to panel" and choosing "Main Menu".
