### Post by steve.horsley on 2007-06-11
I don't think so. I use spreadsheets (calc) without this problem. It sounds as though the PC is confused about the state of the shift key - have you tried tapping the shift keys to refresh their state?

I don't know if it's relevant, but I have noticed that on Feisty, it occasionally forgets that I am holding down the up-arrow (move forward) key while playing Unreal Tournament, which can sometimes be fatal (in the game). So I think there may be a change in the handling of the keyboard internally somewhere.

### Post by steve.horsley on 2007-06-11
If it's the caps lock key that's causing the problem, you may be able to adjust that. Go System->Preferences->Keyboard and choose the Layout Options tab. The Caps Lock key behaviour has lots of options.
