### Post by KingBahamut on 2005-08-22
Your talking about the Pager , that you use to move from virtual desktop to virual desktop, and rather than be grey, they are colored in such a way as to show you exatly what its on the page and what the page looks like, similar to what you would see in Enlightenment. Its more than likely that in kde , or more specifically Gnome, youd be able to do this with something like gdesklets 

[http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org](http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org) 

apt-get install gdesklets gdesklets-common from the command line to get it.
