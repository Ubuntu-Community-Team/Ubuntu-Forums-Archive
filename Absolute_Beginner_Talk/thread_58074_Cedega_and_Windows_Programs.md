Yes, you can still use it after your subscription expires, but you can't update it. If you would get too much out of date then you'd have to shell out another $15 later for a new three month subscription to update. Kinda like it would be if you bought 'Cedega 1', and then 'Cedega 2' when the first one got too far behind.
