### Post by loell on 2006-06-15
you could create a launcher in xubuntu desktop...
right click any icon on your desktop, and the menu will show..
there is an item "create new" where you can create launcher, folder, etc

also, if you like an icon on your top panel near the applications menu or the bottom panel. you can also right click on any panel and add new item like launchers, etc..
