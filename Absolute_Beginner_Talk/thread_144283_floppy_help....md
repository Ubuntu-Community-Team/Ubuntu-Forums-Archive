### Post by encompass on 2006-03-14
well, in gnome... or ubuntu, just double click on the floppy disk to mount the disk.  from there you may save files and retrieve files from the disk.  WHEN done with the disk... be sure to unmount the disk... this is don't buy right clicking on the disk picture and clicking unmount.  That should help you out, but make sure the data is there when you are done unmounting.  I had issues at first with my old floppy drive.
