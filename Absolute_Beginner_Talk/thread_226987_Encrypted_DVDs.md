### Post by nikku_neko on 2006-08-01
i'm just as much of a beginner as you are, but here's my two cents:

which player are you using?

if it is the totem player, i had trouble with it as well.  in fact, i think totem can't play encrypted dvd's
try downloading another player like Mplayer or gxine
(i prefer gxine)
you can download either one by going to thesynaptic package manager and finding them, or by typing

**sudo apt-get gxine**

or

**sudo apt-get mplayer**

in the terminal

also, just for the sake of covering all grounds,
the [restricted formats]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats#dvd") guide gives a list of packages to install for several formats.  try installing all of them.


good luck,



nick
