### Post by annda on 2006-09-02
don't move any of those files, they need to be accessed by the programs which created them and your home directory is the right place to store them. don't worry about disk space, they are usually small.

as to having a tidy directory, i would advice not to put any of your personal data there but consider it as a system thing. maybe create another dir there, something like my_data, and put your documents, photos etc. there. that way you will be able to keep it clean, and the way you want it to be structured. no unexpected files will be placed there.
