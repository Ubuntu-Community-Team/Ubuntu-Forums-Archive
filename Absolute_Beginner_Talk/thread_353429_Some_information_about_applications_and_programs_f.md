 Most of your HW shoudl be supported. Like the Ethernet, soundcard, MB. So you'll need no drivers there. Graphics cards like Nvida or ATI. Yes, you'll need to get the drivers going for them. Most people have gotten Nvidia to work and better support for them. And then you have to edit xorg I beleive too. If you have a sound card like Sound Blaster. The only thing you need to do is go into your BIOS and disable the on board sound device. This will make all sound go through that card. Or you'll have 1/2 have the sound through the SB card and the other 1/2 through the on board sound. Most people install ALSA to control the sound for a SB card.
