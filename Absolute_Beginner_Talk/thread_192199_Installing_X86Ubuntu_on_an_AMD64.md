### Post by AirRaven on 2006-06-08
Sorry for the *appalingly* late reply- nobody else seems to have picked this up, despite its simplicity.

The simple answer is "Yes- of course". It's no different to installing the standard 32-bit version of Windows XP on an Athlon64- it works fine. You can even use the Athlon64-optimised version of SwiftFox with all the benefits on Ubuntu-x86.

Just go ahead- it works fine.
