### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-07-17
Well - I have two computers each with a MSI motherboard, on 32 bit and one 64 bit. Both have 1 gig of RAM. Both running on Broadcom gigabit ethernet and running a 200 gb SATA disk on each of them too. Samsung DVD-RW works perfectly on both too - So I'd suggest that you get hold of a MSI board with a VIA chipset. Those chipset runs without porbs on both my desktop computer, and my server with AMD Athlon-64. For graphics I'd recommend nVidia over ATI.
