### Post by SteelCore on 2007-12-07
When double clicking on a word in Firefox, under Ubuntu, the coma (if any) right after the word would also get selected which is sometimes very inconvenient.  Is there a way to disable this and make the double-click only select the word without the coma?

---

### Post by parhelia on 2007-12-07
Type about:config in the address bar

In "Filter", type select, or just find "layout.word_select.stop_at_punctuation"

Set layout.word_select.stop_at_punctuation to true by double clicking it.
