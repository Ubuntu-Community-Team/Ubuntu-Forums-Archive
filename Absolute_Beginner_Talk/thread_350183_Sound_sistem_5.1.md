### Post by muguwmp67 on 2007-01-31
1.) Double click on the volume icon next to the date, or enter gnome-volume-control from the command line.

2.) Go to Edit->Preferences

3.) Check off the additional controllers you want to see (surround, center, LFE)

4.) Adjust their volumes to suit

5.) I don't like using it, but there is a "duplicate front" item that can be used to clone the front channels to the rear channels with stereo sources (i.e mp3 music).

Some advice: Wait at least an hour before you assume that no one is going to help you.  We're users too.  No one here is paid staff, sometimes it takes a little while before someone answers.

Bumping your own post after 20 minutes doesn't help.  It actually hurts.  Some users search the forums looking for unanswered posts.  If you've already reply  your own, they won't find these, and won't know that your problem hasn't been answered.
