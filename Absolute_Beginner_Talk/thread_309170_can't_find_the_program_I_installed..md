### Post by CatKiller on 2006-11-30
rtorrent is a command-line torrent client. There are graphical BitTorrent clients in the repositories, like bittornado-gui or bittorent-gui - you just picked one that wasn't. Basically, if it mentions curses, it's command-line, if it mentions QT (KDE) or GTK (GNOME), it's graphical.
