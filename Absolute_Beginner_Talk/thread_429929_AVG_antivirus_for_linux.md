### Post by goumples on 2007-05-01
Just update from the terminal window.  Open a terminal and type:

sudo avgupdate -o

It'll will go to grisoft and get all the new definitions for you and install them automatically.

### Post by teddybairs1 on 2007-05-01
AVG's a great anti-virus, but why would you even bother with it for Linux? There's nothing in the wild right now that could even infect your system, and AVG looks for windows viruses, not Linux ones anyway (there are only forty known viruses for Linux, most of them rendered harmless by modern systems).
