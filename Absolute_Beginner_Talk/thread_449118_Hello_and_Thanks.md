### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2007-05-19
1.  No, partitions are never dependent on each other.  That's the whole purpose of having partitions.

2.  There's really nothing mandatory to learn.  If you ever need anything, I suppose you could learn whatever is needed to accomplish that, but there's nothing to learn for the sake of learning.  You could become familiar with Python if you feel like joining the development community.  You could also help out by reporting bugs on launchpad.net.
