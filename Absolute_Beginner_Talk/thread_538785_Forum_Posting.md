### Post by fjf314 on 2007-08-30
> **measekite said:**
> **Three Questions:**

** Sticky Posts:**

What exactly is a sticky post and how do you post one?

I believe that moderators and admins are the only ones who post stickies. Stickies often get created because they contain information that *many* users ask about. So instead of having new users make threads asking the same couple of questions over and over again, the stickies are created.

> [B]
Boldface Title:[/B]

I notice that some of the Titles (Subjects) are in boldfaced type while others are in standard type.  What does this mean and how can you choose boldfaced it you want it?

By default, bolded thread titles mean that new posts have been added to the thread since the last time you viewed it. If you open a new, bolded thread and then immediately select to go back to the section of the forum you are curently in (such as Absolute Beginner Talk), you will see that the thread title is no longer bold. It's just an easy way for users to keep track of what threads have been updated.

> [B]
Poll Options:[/B]

What exactly is this and how does this work?

Polls allow you to post a set of options at the very beginning of the thread. Users can then select an option and submit their vote for it. For example, you could make a poll for something like "Favorite Linux Distro." The poll options would be various Linux distros and anyone reading the thread would be able to vote for their favorite. Of course, on this forum I have an idea of which one would win in my example. :)
