### Post by Haegin on 2007-08-19
You will have to look into how to change the firmware on the device. It will require some knowledge of the chips inside the device and you will need to know how to write code for those chips.

You could try looking at Rockbox and contacting some people over there if you are really keen to do it. If you just want it to look different then its probably not really worth the effort/

Rockbox website: [http://www.rockbox.org/](http://www.rockbox.org/)
