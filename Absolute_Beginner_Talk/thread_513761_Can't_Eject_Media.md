### Post by jessenw on 2007-07-30
Whenever I plug in my USB portable HDD, or when I pop in my media card, I don't get the option to eject it. This leads to me just taking it out, and getting the unsafe removal of hardware warning.  Is there something I need to do to get the right-click option to eject?

---

### Post by univremonster on 2007-07-31
The "eject" function is the same thing as unmount.  You should be able to unmount just by right clicking the device from wherever it's mounted to, probably your desktop.  In the case that doesn't work you can always go to Places->Computer, right click and unmount.  Hope that helps!
