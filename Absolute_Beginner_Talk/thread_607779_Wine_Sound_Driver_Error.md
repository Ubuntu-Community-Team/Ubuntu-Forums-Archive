### Post by kyphi on 2007-11-09
Just worked it out how to use XMMS to play radio streams.

There is a little box in the top left corner of the player.  Click on that and from the resulting drop down screen choose 'Play' and then 'Play Location'.  Then you need to enter the URL of the station you want to listen to, looks something like this:
[http://blahblah.com:1010/](http://blahblah.com:1010/)

Good Luck.
