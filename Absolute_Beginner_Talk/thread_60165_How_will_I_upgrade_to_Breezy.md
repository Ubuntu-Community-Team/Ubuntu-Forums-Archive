### Post by invalid on 2005-08-26
If you wish to upgrade to Breezy - simply replace all cases of 'hoary' to 'breezy' in your /etc/apt/sources.list and upgrade through apt or synaptic.

Be very warned though - many things are still quite unstable, including Xorg.
Expect problems if you are planning to upgrade now.. the final release is still a good ways off.

Check out the Breezy Development forum for more information.
