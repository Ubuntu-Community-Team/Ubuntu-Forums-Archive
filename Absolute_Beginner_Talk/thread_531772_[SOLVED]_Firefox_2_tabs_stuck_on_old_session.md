### Post by w4ett on 2007-08-22
You have the option of removing firefox and doing a reinstall from the repos...Firefox is not supposed to remove your bookmarks, but to be on the safe side...save them to a different folder on your desktop, then upon reinstall you can drag and drop the folder to your open bookmarks tab.

[http://www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/2.0.0.4/releasenotes/](http://www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/2.0.0.4/releasenotes/)
