### Post by Sef on 2006-01-17
To get on the net:  System ---> Administrator ----> Networking

A) Broadband (cable, dsl, satellite, or other very fast connections.)

     -) Click on Ethernet connection and follow the directions.  

B)  Dial up (56.6 and below: this may be more if accelerators are used.)

     a) Click on Modem Connection

      b) Click on Enable this connection

      c) put in Phone Number, Dial Prefix, User Name and Password in the correct             boxes

C)  Go to the tab labeled Options

    -) Click on Retry if the connection breaks or fails to start

D) Click on OK (twice)
