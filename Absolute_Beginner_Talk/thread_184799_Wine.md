### Post by bluevoodoo1 on 2006-05-30
The wine folder is hidden in your home folder. Hit ctrl+h or View > Show hidden files to see your hidden folders. 

The folder should be called .wine 

Your program, most likely will be installed to 
.wine/drive_c/Program Files/PROGRAM NAME
