### Post by dbd on 2006-12-05
Yeah, in the router config there will be a port forwarding section for it. Gonna be tough to find in Chinese though if your router is made by a company that makes english ones too then there may be a similar layout. Otherwise you'll just have to guess!
If you can find somewhere in the router config where you can input either two or three numbers (one being your local ip) and choose between TCP, UDP (and poss both) then you are in the right area.

As for which ports, you might as well just use the KTorrent defaults, you can see them in KTorrent Settings (Downloads Section).

Good luck!
