### Post by tom56 on 2007-03-17
Both of these are available in Gaim in Edgy.

To log chats:
1. Go to Tools -> Preferences
2. Go to the Logging tab
3. Choose the options you want (probably tick all of them)

To log in silently:
This one seems broken on mine but it might not be on yours
1. In Preferences choose the Away/Idle tab
2. Under "status at startup" uncheck "Use status from last exit" and choose Invisible for the drop down list.
