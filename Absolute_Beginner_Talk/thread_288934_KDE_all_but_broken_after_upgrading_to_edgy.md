### Post by Marsolin on 2006-10-30
I've had similar font issues in the past. Try going into the Fonts section in System Settings. I unchecked anti-aliasing and applied the setting, then checked it and reapplied it. That forced all of the font settings to be redone and any new windows I opened were correct. Hopefully the solution will be as easy for you.
