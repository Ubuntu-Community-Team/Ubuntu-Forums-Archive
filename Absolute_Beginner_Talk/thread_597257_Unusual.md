My friend just set me up with Gutsy recently, and so far I'm extremely impressed! There have been a few issues, but so far I've found solutions for all of them. ### Post by Inxsible on 2007-10-30
Everything depends on hardware as well as the software that uses it.

You probably haven't installed the correct drivers for the speakers in Windows, which is why its giving you problems. Try to find out what sound card you have and then look for the appropriate drivers on the web or even the Microsoft website or better yet, on the website of the manufacturer of your computer.

But  its good to hear that Linux is working for you :)
