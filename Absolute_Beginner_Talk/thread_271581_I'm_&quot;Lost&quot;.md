### Post by aysiu on 2006-10-04
While it is true that viewing a Flash file does copy it locally to your hard drive, I wouldn't use the word *download* in this context to describe what's happening: > I am not able to download Lost off of abc.com because to the end-user, it doesn't *appear* to be a download.

Usually, people will say "I can't play this video" or "it doesn't load," not "I am not able to download this."

Nor, when I visit the Ubuntu Forums and the page doesn't load, do I say, "I'm unable to download this thread off the Ubuntu Forums." The phrasing is misleading, even though the browser fetches the actual thread, links, and images, and stores them in the cache.
