### Post by A4006 on 2008-01-26
The disks you are trying to copy are probably copy-protected in some way or another, which would result in errors when trying to copy them, and also would not read as the original disk.  Use Alcohol 120% or 52% (52% is freeware) to copy a image of your disks in windows, or you could try using it in Ubuntu with Wine (I've never tried it).
