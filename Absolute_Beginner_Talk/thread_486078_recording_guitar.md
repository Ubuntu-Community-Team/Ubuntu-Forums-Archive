### Post by kakalaky on 2007-06-27
if your amp has an effects loop or line out that should work fine.  As far as setting up recording, the simplest way is to open up a terminal and type "alsamixer" to adjust your line in levels and then go to Main Menu -> Sound & Video -> Sound Recorder to record input.
