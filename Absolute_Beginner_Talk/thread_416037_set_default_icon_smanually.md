### Post by aktiwers on 2007-04-20
Look in your /.icons folder.
Its in your home folder. Press CTRL+H to view hitten files (files with a "." infront).

In there should be the Icons for your menu. You can replace them to whatever you like.

EDIT: Just remember to keep the same Filename!
