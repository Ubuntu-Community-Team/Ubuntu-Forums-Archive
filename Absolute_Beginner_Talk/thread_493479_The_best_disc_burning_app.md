### Post by sci-fi guy on 2007-07-05
> **lbelloq said:**
> Are disc-to-disc copy, image-to-disc copy and such supported?

CD/DVD Creator does both. For CD-to-CD, insert the disk to be copied and a blank disc, on the Desktop right-click>>Copy CD and under "Copy Disc to:", select your other the drive with the blank CD.  For image-to CD, right-click the ISO>>Write to disc. You should get a pop-up at some point asking if you want to burn it as a disc with a single file or burn the image. Select Image.
