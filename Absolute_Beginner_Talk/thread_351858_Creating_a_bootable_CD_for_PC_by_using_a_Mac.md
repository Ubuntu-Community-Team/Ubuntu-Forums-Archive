### Post by linear on 2007-02-02
Drag and drop is not the way to go - you need to burn the .iso as an image.

Launch Disk Utility.
Drag the .iso to the left pane.
Use Image > Burn (or is it Burn > Image? not in front of a Mac right now) to burn it.
