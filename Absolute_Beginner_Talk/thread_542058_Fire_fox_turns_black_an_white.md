### Post by P4man on 2007-11-07
It turns black and white because firefox is no longer responding. It is either very busy, or  has crashed.  Since your window manager has not crashed, you can still move/minimize or maximize it. If you try to  close it you will get the question whether you want to force quit..

Firefox crashes frequently under Linux. Too often IMHO. The good thing is that if it does crash, and you restart it, it will restore the session so its not all that bad.
