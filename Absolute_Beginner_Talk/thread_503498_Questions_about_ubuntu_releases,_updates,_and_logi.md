It is your option to upgrade or not.  If you upgrade, you can automatically do it, or reinstall.  The latter is the recomended choice.   Updates are provided for 18 months or 3 years for Long Term Service.   Dapper is LTS which means it tends to be more stable, but not as cutting edge.
