### Post by jimbren on 2007-02-02
I did an online upgrade from Dapper to Edgy and had an almost completely trouble free experience.  Then about a month after the release, I did a clean install of Edgy from a CD and had an almost completely trouble free experience.  

The only hitch I had, and this was with both methods, was that the usplash didn't display on boot.  I fixed that by installing the boot up manager (BUM) package and configuring my usplash from there.

I do have my /home on it's own partiition, which goes a long way in making your upgrade pain-free.  No need to restore all your data after the install, because it is already there!
