### Post by milambyr on 2007-06-30
Go to Applications -> Add/Remove, and do a search for Gstreamer, make sure you have all of the codecs installed.  I think there is Gstreamer extra plugins, ffmpeg video plugin, and plugins for mms, wavpack, quicktime, musepack, there might be one or two more.

I just checked Amazon.com and tried to play a Windows Media clip and it worked fine for me at least.
