### Post by Marsolin on 2007-05-15
It's worth trying [Gnucash]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/gnucash"), but I think it's more for personal finances.  [MyBooks Professional]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/mybooks") and [Quasar Accounting]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/quasar") are a couple targeted at businesses.

[LedgerSMB]("http://linuxappfinder.com/package/ledgersmb") is a web interface option.
