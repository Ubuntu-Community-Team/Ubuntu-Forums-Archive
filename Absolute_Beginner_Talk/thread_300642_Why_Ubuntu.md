### Post by PrinceArithon on 2006-11-16
Well since you asked a slightly philosophical question in this place, I shall give you, your answer.

Why Ubuntu??  Well I'll tell you one thing that most people would here would kill me for.  It has flaws just like Windows.  It's really not safer unless you make it safer yourself, right out of the box doesn't help in safety for anything.  It's harder to use than Windows, there are less things for it out there compared to Windows.  So why??

Well there is the fact that it's free.  Not just in price but in your own freedom.  You can do whatever you want to it and the Ubuntu guys won't get angry with you.  Also there are many cool free things you can get with it.  There are more programs available for it than you can count in one day...well maybe you can.  It's a learning experience as well.  In our age where the computer is in every house, everyone should know how to use it, and should know how to work on it well.  With Ubuntu you get that chance to learn.  You might find you save money in the long run.

Ubuntu has it's ups and downs.  I think it's ups outweigh the downs.  It's been about a month and a half since I installed Ubuntu on my computer.  Now I knew how to work on Linux before, for the fact that I Have had experience with Fedora Core and old versions of Red Hat which are Linux distributions.  The thing is I didn't expect to basically only use Windows for my own home use maybe 2 or 3 times since.  Mostly I only use Windows at work or school.

If you want a great place to start I will give you 2 links.  The reason for the 2 links is because I don't know if you are using Ubuntu Dapper Drake or if you are using Ubuntu Edgy, and this place that I'm sending you has 2 different pages for both, they are similar pages, but there are a few differences.  So here check these out.

Dapper Drake: [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_dapper](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_dapper)

Edgy: [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Edgy](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Edgy)

This place really helped me out a lot.  Also, just out of my opinion if you aren't using Edgy, I say upgrade to it as soon as you can, but first I say get used to what you got if you are using Dapper Drake.  The difference between Drake and Edgy is kinda similar to the differences of Windows ME and Windows XP...or soon to be Windows XP to Windows Vista.

Anyway, you can load in your flash and all that other stuff, it just takes work, and still not all things will work right..unfortunatly that's the world of Linux...but it's getting better and better.  Just because certain things don't work like they do in windows, doesn't mean Windows is better.

I say stick around give it a try and you may learn to love this operating system and the community.
