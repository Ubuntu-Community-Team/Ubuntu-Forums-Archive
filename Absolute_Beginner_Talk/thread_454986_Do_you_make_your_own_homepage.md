### Post by eoghanmurray on 2007-05-26
**Assuming you are using Mozilla Firefox**, follow these procedures:

1. Load Mozilla Firefox   [Main Menu > Internet > Mozilla Firefox]
2. Type the website you wish to be your homepage into the address bar.
3. Press 'Go' or press ENTER.
4. Drag the favicon* to the home button in Firefox.
5. Click 'Yes' in the dialog box (shown below).

*** The favicon is the icon (sometimes a page icon, or a website-specific picture) beside the address bar (to the left of [http://www.websiteaddresshere.com](http://www.websiteaddresshere.com))**
