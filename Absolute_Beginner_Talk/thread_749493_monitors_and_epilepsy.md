### Post by 1scorpio on 2008-04-08
I am posting this to be hopefully helpful to those users who have epilepsy and are having seizures while at the computer. I have found that changing the cycle refresh rate to any thing other than 60hz is just enough to reduce the headaches ie less flicker less seizures. I am not sure that this would work for everyone but it did for me. My refresh rate is now set to 75hz and the only seizure I have had since I changed was the result of a game with many lights and beeps. Many software co.s are now issuing warnings specifically directed towards epileptics (cool huh).
