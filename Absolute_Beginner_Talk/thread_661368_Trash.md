### Post by ispy on 2008-01-07
right click on any available panel in a blank spot and select "add to panel". a window will pop up. scroll down until you see the trash icon and title. select it, press "add" and close. the trash will be in your panel. to empty it at any time, right click it and select "empty trash".

to move it to a more convenient location, just click and drag to anywhere on the panel, or right click and select "move" and move it where you want (which I prefer).

I personally use AWN (Avant Window Navigator), which has an applet available in it.

hope that helps.
