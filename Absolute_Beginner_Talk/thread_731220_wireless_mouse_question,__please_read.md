### Post by annda on 2008-03-21
i have had two no-name wireless mice with USB receivers and they both worked under linux. i suppose they all use standard protocols included in the linux kernel, so there is no need to worry.

just make sure to mention this to the radio shack salespeople so that they know what to tell other customers looking for linux-compatible hardware.

p.s. if you buy something with additional buttons (in addition to scroll wheel with middle-click functionality), you will have to invest some work in configuring them.
