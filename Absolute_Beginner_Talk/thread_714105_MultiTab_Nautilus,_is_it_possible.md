### Post by jken146 on 2008-03-03
Nautilus does not have tabs, although there are two different ways it can display itself.  One is where it opens a new window every time you navigate to a different folder, and the other is where everything appears in one window.  To change to the latter mode, press alt+f2, tyoe **gconf-editor** and press Enter, then find Apps -> Nautilus -> Preferences and tick the box marked "Always use browser".
