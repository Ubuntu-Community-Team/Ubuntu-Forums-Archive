### Post by mivo on 2008-02-20
This should probably be in "Other OS Talk" (may by there by the time you read this).

It sounds like you'd prefer a distro where you decide what is installed. This means more work and requires a little more knowledge, but worthwhile choices here are ArchLinux, Gentoo and Slackware. Personally, I'd recommend [Arch]("http://archlinux.org/") and its excellent [Beginner's Guide]("http://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Beginners_Guide"). You'll start with a fairly vanilla system, though. Ubuntu does have a lot of extra baggage that you may not need, but it is likely to have everything you **do** need.
