### Post by bongobonga on 2007-11-27
Your old kernel will still be on the system after upgrading and will appear on the grub menu  at startup. 
You may have some minor problems if you have any special kernel drivers installed they might not work properly, such as a video driver that has been compiled for a specific kernel. But you should be safe enough though.
