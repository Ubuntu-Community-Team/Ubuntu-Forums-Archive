yes you will be able to upgrade to gutsy from feisty - you should get a "do you want to upgrade" option in your update manager when it becomes available.

### Post by Jimmyfj on 2007-10-07
You can also make the upgrade right away - Just open a terminal and type:

```
gksu update-manager -c -d
``` This will give you the option to upgrade your Fiesty to Gutsy right away.
