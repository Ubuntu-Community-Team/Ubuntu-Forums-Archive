### Post by JayTee on 2006-11-12
In order to help you better we'd need to know a bit more info. What version of Ubuntu are you running? Dapper 6.06 LTS or Edgy 6.10? Is this a desktop or a laptop? The type of video chipset or video card is important info and the type of monitor. You'll need to know the horizontal and vertical sync rates and the refresh rate of the monitor. For starters, go to System | Preferences |Screen Resolution and see if you can't choose a higher refresh rate. If you can't then try and post the other info above here and we'll work with you on it. Your monitor's manual should have the info on sync and refresh rates. If it's a laptop I'd look in the user's manual for that or the manufacturer's websited. I'll post a link or two on the USB wireless in a little bit.
