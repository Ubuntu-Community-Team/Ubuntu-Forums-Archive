### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-09-15
What do you mean by "local printer"? Plugged into the server?

Adding printers is done by a wizard. Use the menu option System->Administration->Printing. Double-click New Printer. If it's a local printer you will be asked if this is the one you want to add. If it's over a network, you will have to supply some network details.
