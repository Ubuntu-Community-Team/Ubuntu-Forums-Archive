### Post by Hellcom on 2008-02-26
I have the M6 and I would recommend it. Its a great media player with a good sound and feature set, and does as fine on ubuntu as does any other MSC media player (it just acts as a usb storage device). For video you can use mencoder instead of the windows software to encode video. I even found a neat script for it:
