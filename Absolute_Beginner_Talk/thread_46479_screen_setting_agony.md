### Post by aysiu on 2005-07-04
Your xorg.conf file says this:
HorizSync	30-96
VertRefresh	75-85

But your monitor's specs are this:
Horizontal scan range 30 - 96 kHz (automatic)
Vertical scan range 50 - 160 Hz (automatic)

Change the vertrefresh in your xorg.conf, and you should be fine.
