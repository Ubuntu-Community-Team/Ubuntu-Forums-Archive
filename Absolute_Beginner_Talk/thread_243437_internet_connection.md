### Post by eternalis on 2006-08-25
Well, my broadband internet connection worked for me without any configuration.

Have you had your router configured before using Linux? If so, it should work without any configuration.

Your username and password is stored in your router. You can access your router from a web browser. The address you have to type to access it varies depending on the brand of your router. What brand is your router? On D-Link you type 10.1.1.1, but I'm not sure about others.

Also, you should be able to see your network device in System-->Administration--->Network Tools.
