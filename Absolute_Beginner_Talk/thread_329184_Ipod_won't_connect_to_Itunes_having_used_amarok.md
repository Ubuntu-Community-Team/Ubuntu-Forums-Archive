### Post by Seamus77 on 2007-01-03
Just an update in case anyone was interested in this problem...

I read the apple forums about itunes, which usually recommends the 5 Rs.
I tried to uninstall itunes from the windows machine to do a reinstall, but even after following the directions, it seems impossible to completely get rid of all traces of itunes. i tired it a few times, but every time I started it after a reinstall, it still had playlists, etc., so something was remaining on the computer, and still would not recognize the ipod.

So, I did a fresh install on a laptop which never had itunes on it before. It recognized my ipod right away. After about an hour it finally completed the determination of the 'gapless playback' (whatever that means), and then I followed some advice from the apple forum. I unticked 'display album art', clicked 'apply', then re-ticked 'display album art', and clicked 'apply' again. After about half an hour during which Itunes was 'optimizing artwork', everything worked. I can now see the album art on the ipod, and everything still works good with amarok.
I guess that other computer just doesn't like my ipod.
