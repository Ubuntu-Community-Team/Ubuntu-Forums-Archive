### Post by southernman on 2007-07-10
Just before giving it to someone, when you have your new lappy do this on your old box from a Live CD:

> dd if=/dev/zero of=/dev/yourdrive

Replace the /dev/yourdrive with the correct information. This will write zeros to the entire drive. It's about as safe as you can expect for free... ;)

Or you can do as our US government does. Their official policy with regards to old hard drives is to permanently format them by use of an incinerator.
