### Post by Brynster on 2007-12-21
Hi
I have kmobiletools work (fairly) well in ubuntu 7.10

I downloaded the latest version from the Kmobiletools website  [http://www.kmobiletools.org/downloads]("http://www.kmobiletools.org/downloads")

I used the kubuntu version (not to bothered about the whole gnome KDE purity issue)

Its a vastly improved version over the one in added removeprograms. It has a lovely wizard to assist in setup. The only advice i can give you is on the phone when it becomes connected is check the "TRUST THIS DEVICE" box as that will stop you pulling your hair out :lolflag:

It is however a beta app and as such has issue's for me as follows
1 Sending sms from computer doesn't work
2 Deleting sent sms doesn't work
3 Its slow to dial a number to call somebody (althoughit does work)
4 Crashes quite regularly whilst setting up
5 Locks up periodically for a few seconds from time to time

That said when it works it works very very well, and your mileage will vary to mine.


Good luck!
