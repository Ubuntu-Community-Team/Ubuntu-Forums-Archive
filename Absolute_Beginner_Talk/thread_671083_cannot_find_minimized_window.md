### Post by SOULRiDER on 2008-01-18
To remove a pannel right click on it and select "Delete Panel". To add a new one, right click on your other panel (you should have two panels, one at the top and one at the bottom) and then select "Add new panel". Remember once you add your panel youre going to have to select all the necessary applets/

### Post by sistoviejo on 2008-01-18
> **SOULRiDER said:**
> ...Remember once you add your panel youre going to have to select all the necessary applets/

Right click on the panel and click "add to panel..." to do this
Specifically if you remove the bottom panel you'll have to add the following applets:
- Show desktop
- Window list
- Workspace switcher
- Trash

If you're doing this with the top bar, these are the applets:
- Menu bar
- Volume control
- Battery charge monitor (if you have a laptop)
- Notification area
- Deskbar
- Quit...
