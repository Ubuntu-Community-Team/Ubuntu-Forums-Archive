### Post by helloimalemur on 2007-11-24
go to [www.youtubex.com]("www.youtubex.com") plug in the url to your youtube video

an ad will pop up, close that, then click "download" next to your video's picture and title.
save as *name*.FLV
go to [www.media-convert.com]("www.media-convert.com") and upload/convert your video to mpg/avi/swf... w.e

if you would like to convert a small video to *.GIF, go to media-convert.com
convert it to .AVI (if it isnt already)
then convert your AVI video to GIF
:D
but the long videos are hella big when in GIF format O.o
