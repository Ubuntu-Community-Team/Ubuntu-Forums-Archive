### Post by loanrangie on 2007-03-20
I just went thru all this about 2 hours ago, i was getting very frustrated that all these talented people can write these great multimedia players yet not one of them will play any standard common audio/video format without stuffing around installing a milion and one codecs and plugins. Easiest thing to do is go into whatever package installer you use, adetp/ synaptic etc and do a search of all the formats you want to play like mp3/ dvd/ wma/ avi/mpeg4 and then install all the codecs and plugins that suit the players you are using. Also go to the guide pages and install the codecs that are listed there, some you can just copy +paste into terminal.

 Good luck !
