### Post by mcduck on 2007-01-20
There's always the option to install Jack audio server and some EQ plugin for it, and the if your audio apps have support for Jack output you can route all audio through Jack& the EQ plugin..

It's not the easiest way, but the one that gives you best results & most options.

Other than that, all Winamp clones (xmms, BMP, Audacious etc. have EQ)
