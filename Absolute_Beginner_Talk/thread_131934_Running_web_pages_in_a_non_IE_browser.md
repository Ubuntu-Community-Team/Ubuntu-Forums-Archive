### Post by evilgold on 2006-02-17
If the reason your page doesnt work is due to it requiring windows media player (IE is not a media player, it just has a wmp plugin), then you can solve the problem by installing gxine (or your favorite media player w/mozilla plugin) and w32codecs.

I had to do that in order to view CNN pipeline and a few other things. I dont know what yahoo music uses, but its probably WMP.
