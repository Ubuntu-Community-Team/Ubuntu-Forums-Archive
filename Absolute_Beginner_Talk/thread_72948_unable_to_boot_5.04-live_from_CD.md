### Post by Ampersand on 2005-10-07
You'll have to change the settings in your bios. When you reboot your computer, you'll see a message similar to 'Press F2 to enter setup' (the key might be different). Press this key, and you'll be in the BIOS setup. The layout of this will vary according to your computer, but in one of the pages there'll be the 'boot order' setting. Make sure that it is set to boot from the CD drive first. Save and exit, and your computer will continue booting into the CD.
