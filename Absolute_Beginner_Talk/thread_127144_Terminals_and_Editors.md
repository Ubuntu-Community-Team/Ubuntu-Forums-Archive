### Post by Jucato on 2006-02-08
to paste in a terminal, you can either right-click and choose paste, or press Shift+Insert.

to copy from the terminal, you have to use the mouse. I don't think there is a keyboard shortcut for that since the terminal does not have capabilities for highlighting and stuff like that.

Terminal apps, there's xTerm , eTerm, Konsole for KDE. They're all found in the repositories. There are also terminal emulators like Yakuake and Tilda.
