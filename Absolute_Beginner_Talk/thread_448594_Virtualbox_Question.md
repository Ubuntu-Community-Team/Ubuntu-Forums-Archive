### Post by kc5hwb on 2007-05-19
How do I get my mouse out of Virtualbox while its running?  When I use CTRL-R it doesn't work, unless I use the RIGHThand CTRL button and it reboots the OS in Virtualbox.  How do I get my mouse to release from VB without rebooting the OS?

---

### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-05-19
This is a common misunderstanding (I misunderstood it originally!). Control-R refers to the RIGHT control key, not pressing Control and R simultaneously. So if you hit the control key right next to your arrows, then your mouse will become freed.
