I had this problem when I downgraded from an earlier Gutsy beta to Feisty, and what fixed it for me was to delete the .fontconfig folder in my home directory (I renamed it, to be exact). After restarting X the fonts looked fine again (ctrl+alt+backspace). I also had the trouble chiefly in Firefox and OpenOffice.
