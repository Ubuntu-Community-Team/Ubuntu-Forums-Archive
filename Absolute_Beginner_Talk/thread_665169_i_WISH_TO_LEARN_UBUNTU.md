### Post by smartboyathome on 2008-01-12
This is more of a support question than a ubuntu idea, but please use proper grammar, as it makes your work hard to read. Also, you should start by reading the official Ubuntu book (try searching google for it).
About the external hard drive: you won't be able to because your BIOS don't support it. Even if you would, it doesn't make Ubuntu portable as it thinks it is being installed to a hard drive, and configures itself for the computer it is installed on (it isn't very flexible after this).
