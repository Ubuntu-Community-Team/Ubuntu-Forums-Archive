### Post by Majorix on 2007-09-04
Wow the processor is pretty fast. Are you absolutely sure you want one that fast? Gaming? Coding? Science? If not, I would go with a slower proc, as it could decrease the cost of the PC dramatically.

I read some place that if you have newer and faster hardware, you might need to recompile the kernel to meet your needs, for example if you have a 4gb ram you have to do this otherwise Linux will consider your RAM is 2gb.

If you believe you can recompile your kernel, go ahead and read some guides on it. I am sure someone here will link you to one.

If you want no trouble at all, go with the 667mhz option.
