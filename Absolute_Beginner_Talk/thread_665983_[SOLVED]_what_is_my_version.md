### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-01-12
Hi. Do you mean if it's 7.10, 7.04 or another one? Press the 'Help' button in the top panel, then press 'New to Ubuntu'; another way to see which version you have is to go to 'System->Administration->System Monitor' and see the first tab (System). Or, from the terminal:
```
cat /etc/issue
```
