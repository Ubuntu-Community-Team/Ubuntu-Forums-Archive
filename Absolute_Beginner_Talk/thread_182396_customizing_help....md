### Post by bluevoodoo1 on 2006-05-26
[QUOTE=LamB]it doesn´t appear in the pic but im also intersted in learning how to change the notification are icon the gaim uses.. 
thanks in advance[/QUOTE]

Find gaim notification icons you'd like to use, perhaps at gnome-look.org, and put them in /usr/share/pixmaps/gaim/icons. Check that location with Nautilus first to make sure it's the correct spot (should be 8 icons there--these are the notification icons). Might also want to make a backup of the images already there so you can revert to the originals in case you ever want them back.

Edit: oh yeah, you can't get transparency with images used as gaim notification icons... thought I'd mention that.
