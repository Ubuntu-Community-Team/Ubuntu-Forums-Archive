### Post by Lanky Juggler on 2007-03-12
right click on your bottom panel and hit "New Panel".  It might appear on like the right of the screen, or something, but just right click on that and go to properties, where you can configure just about everything.  After its set up, right click on it again and go to "Add to Panel" which will give you a nice list of things you might want there (for example, your applications/Places/System menus (Under "Menu Bars" i think), or your Clock.

Anyways, fool around, as some of the stuff you can get there (and isn't there by default) is pretty fun/useful.
