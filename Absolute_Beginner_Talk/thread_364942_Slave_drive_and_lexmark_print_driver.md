### Post by igknighted on 2007-02-18
Lexmark and printing in linux, as a general rule, do not work.  Check [www.linuxprinting.org](www.linuxprinting.org) to be sure, but I wouldn't get your hopes up.  I swapped my Lexmark with my dad for his HP, which has tremendous linux drivers.  You might look into selling yours (ebay?) or trading with a friend and getting a well supported printer.
