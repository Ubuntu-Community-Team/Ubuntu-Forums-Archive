### Post by vkurup on 2007-04-17
I had the same problem. Worked around it by going to Settings Menu, option
'Configure digikam', 'Miscellaneous' section, unchecking 'Deleting items
should move them to trash'.

Now photos get deleted immediately and I don't get the "move" error.
