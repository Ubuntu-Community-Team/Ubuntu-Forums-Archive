### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2007-07-20
Just right click on a blank section of the panel and then click on [COLOR="Blue"]Add to Panel[/COLOR].

A window should open.

Scroll down to the [COLOR="Blue"]Utilities[/COLOR] section and click on [COLOR="Blue"]Notification Area[/COLOR].

Click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Add [/COLOR]button and then [COLOR="Blue"]Close[/COLOR].
