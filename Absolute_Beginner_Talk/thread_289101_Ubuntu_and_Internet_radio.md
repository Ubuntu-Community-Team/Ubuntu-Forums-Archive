### Post by bulldog on 2006-10-30
Well click on the green icon and a GUI pops up.

Choose the desired broadcast,try shoutcast for example and in a few seconds there should be a bunch of entrys.

Choose one and click left upper corner 'tune in' and it should open your xmms player.

That's it.
