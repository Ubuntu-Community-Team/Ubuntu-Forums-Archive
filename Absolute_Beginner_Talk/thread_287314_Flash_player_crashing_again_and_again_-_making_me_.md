### Post by blendmaster on 2006-10-28
Linux Firefox only uses Flash 7 because Flash 9 hasn't been developed for Linux, and you're thus stuck at 7.

You could use Wine to install Firefox for Windows and use Flash Player 9 on it, as the site might not load with Flash Player 7.

Don't worry too much though, Flash Player 9 is in development for Linux, so you shouldn't have to worry for too much longer.
