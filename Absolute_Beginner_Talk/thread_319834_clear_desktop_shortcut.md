### Post by macogw on 2006-12-17
Click on my Ubuntu User # to get to the site where you register for a number.

Hibernate should resume when you press the power button.  If you have trouble with it, stay away from hibernate, because it means it's having trouble with some driver in your computer.

If you have to "sudo" you're not running with full permissions at all times.  Sudo gets you into root mode temporarily.  If you had constant full permission, you'd never have to enter your password.
