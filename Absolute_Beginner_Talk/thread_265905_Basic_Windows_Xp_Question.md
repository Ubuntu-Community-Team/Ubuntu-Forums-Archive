### Post by mozetti on 2006-09-26
You just need a CD-burning program, and burn the iso as a disc image -- don't make a data disc.

Open your CD-burning program, and choose the option to burn from an image. Select the iso file as the source and burn it to CD.

Something is wrong here.  Are you sure you are choosing SAVE and not OPEN.  You do not want to open the ISO from the download source, you want to **SAVE**, it to a location of your hard drive and then burn it as an ISO image to your CD-RW drive.

If you are getting the message that it sounds like you are getting, try right-hand clicking on the file name and do a **SAVE** **AS**.

Good luck.
