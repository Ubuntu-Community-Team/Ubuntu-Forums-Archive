### Post by SOULRiDER on 2008-04-11
Edgy is not supported anymore. Beryl has merged with compiz again and is not called Compiz Fusion.
You should consider upgrading to feisty or gutsy and installing Compiz Fusion. Alternatively you can wait a few days until Hardy comes out, Hardy, as well as Gutsy has Compiz Fusion installed by default.
