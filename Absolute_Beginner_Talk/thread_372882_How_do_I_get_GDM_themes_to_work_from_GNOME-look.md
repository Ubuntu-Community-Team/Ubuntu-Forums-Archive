### Post by bapoumba on 2007-02-28
GDM themes are themes for ... GDM (the login screen you get on startup where you enter your login and password). You use them in > System > Administration > Login Window.

Choose regular metacity themes (if not using beryl/compiz) to change your desktop theme.

### Post by Quillz on 2007-02-28
You want a Metacity theme. You will then download a .tar.gz file, which you just drag 'n drop into the Themes window. The OS will automatically install the theme for you and do all the work.
