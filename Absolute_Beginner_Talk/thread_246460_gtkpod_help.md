### Post by Edward The Bonobo on 2006-08-29
No.  All it means is that it's wiped any information about playcounts and ratings...and I don't know anyone who uses that stuff.

You may (will!) also get some random behaviour from gtkpod when you synch.  Sometimes it will work, sometimes...not.  When it fails, I unplug and re-plug the iPod, delete the tracks I've just added, synch again and start over.  Apparently there's a way to fix the synching behaviour:  [http://www.ubuntux.org/how-to-use-an-ipod-with-ubuntu](http://www.ubuntux.org/how-to-use-an-ipod-with-ubuntu)
