### Post by viniosity on 2005-10-28
To get video on the PSP you have to create a folder called MP_ROOT in the root of your PSP memory stick.  (Breezy will automount your PSP when you plug it in and select USB Connection)

There's a good howto on Engadget:
[http://www.engadget.com/entry/1234000980024404/](http://www.engadget.com/entry/1234000980024404/)
