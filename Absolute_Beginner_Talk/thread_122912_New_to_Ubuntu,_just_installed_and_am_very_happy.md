### Post by Madpilot on 2006-01-29
[QUOTE=Ander]
5.1 Cds in the mail, too. i'm completely new to linux, so it's good to see a beginner's forum here.[/QUOTE]

If you've got Ubuntu installed, you can upgrade to the latest in place without having to wait until your CDs arrive!

Have a look at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BreezyUpgrade](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BreezyUpgrade) for Hoary (5.04) --> Breezy (5.10), and [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HoaryUpgradeNotes](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HoaryUpgradeNotes) for Warty (4.10) to Hoary (5.04).

Note the warning on the BreezyUpgrade page - direct from Warty to Breezy is not recommended!

Given that you're talking about ethernet cards, I'm assuming you're on broadband, which is good - the upgrades will involve downloading a LOT of stuff!

Oh, and one tiny point: it's actually "5.10" and not "5.1", because Ubuntu releases are YearOfRelease.MonthOfRelease for numbering - Breezy came out in October 2005, hence 5.10. (Trivia for the day...) :)
