### Post by syn4k on 2008-04-08
To move the menu bar, right click on it and select "lock to panel" to uncheck lock. Then right click again and select move. I don't know about the other issue.

BTW, if you are referring to the menu panel that the menu bar resides in, you will have to grab the bar and then drag it over. This will allow you to move it anywhere you'd like provided it is an edge of your screen
