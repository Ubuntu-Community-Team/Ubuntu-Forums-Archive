### Post by cotcot on 2008-03-25
Unfortunately the best supported linux printer make (HP) is very expensive in ink. Maybe you could compare it with Brother or Epson. Check the linux support before your purchase. In case of Epson you can find the drivers on the [[COLOR="Red"]Epson Avasys[/COLOR]]("http://avasys.jp/hp/menu000000500/hpg000000442.htm") website. In case of Epson look for a printer that is supported with [COLOR="Blue"]pips[/COLOR]. There is also [COLOR="Blue"]pipslite[/COLOR] but that is missing quite some functionality (printing to different sizez, color selections, ...). Know in case of the Epson that their drivers are only for 32 bit. Have also a look in the Gutenprint supported printers.
