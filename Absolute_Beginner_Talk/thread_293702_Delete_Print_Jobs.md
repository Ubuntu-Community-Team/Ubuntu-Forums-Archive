### Post by Ecthelion on 2006-11-05
There should be a printer icon in your upper right corner

click on it

It should display all the jobs that are going to be sent to the printer.

If you right-click on each job, you can delete it by clicking the right option (I think it's 'cancel' but I have no English OS so I'm not sure)
That's the way you can cancel jobs that aren't sent to your printer.

If those job already are in your printers memory, the only thing you can do to stop printing is shutting down your printer (pushing the power off button ;-) )
