### Post by jbiskupiak on 2007-08-15
When the Login screen appears click the options in the lower left hand corner.  Then choose Select Session and then click on Gnome.  The default is the Last Session, so if your last session was KDE, that is why it keeps coming up after you login.

---

### Post by viking777 on 2007-08-15
When you get to the log in screen, press Alt/T on the keyboard before entering your username and password. You should be presented with a list of session types one of which should be Gnome. Click on that and then enter username/password press enter and see how you go.
