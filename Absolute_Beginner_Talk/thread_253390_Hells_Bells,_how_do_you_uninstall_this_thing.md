### Post by aeiah on 2006-09-08
if you're wanting to keep windows when you delete ubuntu then you should just be able to format your ubuntu harddrive. to be safe though, i suggest just unplugging it and setting your bios to boot from your winxp harddrive. im guessing grub was installed to your ubuntu hdd and the bios booted from that one first. in which case, your standard windows boot loader should be unaffected and should just boot to xp if your bios looks at that hdd first. if not, pop the windows cd in to restore the boot loader.

once you've done that to check nothing disasterous has happened just plug your ubuntu hdd in and format it.
