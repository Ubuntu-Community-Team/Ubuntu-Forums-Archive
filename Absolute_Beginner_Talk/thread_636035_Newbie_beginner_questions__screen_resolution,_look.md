### Post by ryanVickers on 2007-12-09
for your fonts issue, you can change it in "System > Preferences > Appearance > (TAB) Fonts" and pick some new ones.  There, you can also change how they are smoothed, or if at all.  I've heard some things like Firefox has to have it's smoothing changed manually and separate from the rest of the system, but see if it works.  If not, try doing some stuff in "Tools > Options > (TAB) Content > (Fonts & Colours section) (BUTTON) Advanced"
