### Post by shrift on 2007-07-09
Hmmm, it would be easier to add this to your panel than to the applications meny. If you right click on an open area in the panel, select "add to panel" then click at the top "custom application launcher" You can setup a shortcut to any program you want.

---

### Post by shrift on 2007-07-09
If you still want to do this in the meny, Right click the applications menu, click edit menu. Then highlight the sound and video part. On the right click "new item" it's the same dialogue from that point on as adding a custom application launcher.
