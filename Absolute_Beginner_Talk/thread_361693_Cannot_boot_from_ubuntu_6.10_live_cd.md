### Post by link141 on 2007-02-14
Here's a nifty little program I used to burn the iso image to a cd when I was running windows, [iso recorder](http://isorecorder.alexfeinman.com/isorecorder.htm).
They have a link to download it for vista on that page, and once it's installed, all you have to do is (make sure you have a blank cd inserted first) right click the iso file, and click "write as image to cd" (or something like that).  Then boot your computer from the cd, and your golden.

Hope this helps,
