### Post by steve.horsley on 2006-06-10
Reinstalling firefox won't help if your personal settings are scrambled. Your personal settings are stored in a hidden directory in your home directory called either .mozilla-firefox or .firefox (I forget which). Delete this folder, or rename it while firefox is not running. Then start firefox and it'll make a new default settings directory.

### Post by dvarsam on 2006-06-10
It has happened to me in the past!

And I had made a similar posting (like yours) on this!

Some people suggested back then to "clear cache" from Mozilla Firefox.

I think that the best thing to do is the following:

1. From your Browser's Menu, select "Edit\Preferences"

2. Select the Tab named "Privacy"

3. Select the Tab named "Cookies"

4. Click on the button named "Exceptions"

5. Under "Address of Website:" type "www.ubuntuforums.org"

6. Click on the button named "Allow"

7. Click on the button named "Close"

Hopefully you should be fine after that!
