### Post by carlosqueso on 2005-11-25
I don't know about your live CD questions, but I do know an easy way to do a dual boot on your laptop without having to think too much.  First, download Mandriva linux.  Just go ahead and download the public version.  Insall it and resize your windows partition to make room. You can do this with the Ubuntu installer, but it's a lot easier with the DiskDrake utility included in Mandriva.  The,, since Mandriva is a crappy distro, install ubuntu over it, using the linux partitions already created for your convenience.  That's what I did and I still have winderz (as we call it in the hills of Western NC) and ubuntu.  hope this helps.
