### Post by penp on 2007-03-13
For playing DirectX games, VMware wouldn't be the best idea. You'd be better off dual booting or trying to run those games in wine/cedega. Yeah, there can be slowdown in wine but there are patches that help it to work with specific games (I used to play WoW, I quit it 2 weeks ago). However, wine's emulation is short from perfect and I still got about half the framerates I did in windows. I hear it really depends on your setup, though. In short, no, 1gb would likely not be enough.
