### Post by chris.m.ball on 2007-11-04
While I think NoScript is a good idea in theory, far too many major sites rely heavily on Javascript for functionality.  I personally install "Ad Block Plus" and "Flash Block".  Between those two plugins, most of my surfing needs are met.  If you're super concerned with harmful website code, I wouldn't worry too much, so long as you're not using Windows and you keep your system patched.

Others may profess that NoScript is mandatory, but I don't think the plugin makes sense for folks who aren't heavily technical, and willing to spend the time to analyze each and every potentially harmful occurrence.
