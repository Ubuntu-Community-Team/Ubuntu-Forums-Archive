Hi, I know this is probably NOT the answer you want  in the short term, but best advice for sharing a connection (I am assuming a Broadband ADSL one) is to get a good ADSL router modem (usually 4 port) . I have been using a Netgear DG814  for over 3 years now. I flinched at the original cost (£120 ..the moths in the wallet were sincerely unhappy ;) ) but it was the best advice I ever got regarding Broadband and has saved so much hassle.(The equivalent DG834 now retails at around £50-60  as wireless versions are the more popular choice). Start saving...you know it makes sense...in the long term,..
