### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2006-07-04
kookoo, the bar you see in the screenshot is likely a modified Gnome panel.  

To get what you see in the screenshot, right click on the bottom panel, and go to Properties.  Change the size to about half screen, or 50. Then switch to the "Background" tab, and select "Solid color."  Move the handle from opaque to transparent, as far as you'd like...the more to the left, the more transparent the bar is.  

After you've done this, remove everything from the panel except the icons you want to keep.  Add any icons you don't already see, and want.  That's it!
