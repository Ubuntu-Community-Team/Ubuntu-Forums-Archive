### Post by m9ke on 2007-10-05
I got Totem working with *some* DVDs a week or so by reading this thread:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=561169](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=561169)

Also, I found that no matter how I set up my box, some DVDs would play and others would not.  I would advise experimenting with a range of DVDS, from old ones you think have little or no encryption to a couple of new ones that you think might be better protected.

Also note that some plugins you may need are in the Mediabuntu repo located here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)

Good luck,
