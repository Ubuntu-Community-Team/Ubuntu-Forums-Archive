### Post by SendDerek on 2007-01-24
Because Linux has the capability to read NTFS drives, you should be able to simply goto that directly (it's probably mounted under Filesystem->Media or under Places->Computer), copy the pictures that you want, and then paste them into your home folder ubuntu.  

Maybe an easier way would be to just point the desktop wallpaper preferrences directly to that directory.

An even easier way yet would be to goto that directory, browse through the pictures, find the one you want, double click on it, and then Image->Set As Wallpaper.
