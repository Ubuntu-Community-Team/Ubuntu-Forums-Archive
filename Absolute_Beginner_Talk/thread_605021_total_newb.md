### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-11-06
No virus checker is essential, but if you must have one then clamav is the best for linux, and it's in the repositories.  There are no viruses in the wild for linux, and none of the virus checkers run in the background as they have to in windows; you actually have to do a manual scan.

My advice:  get clamav and run it a few times then forget it for a few months and run it again just to keep your mind at rest.  Wonderful, isn't it?

Firewall also included and no action needed on your part.  If you must have something to show get firestarter (gnome) or guarddog (kde), but frankly, I wouldn't bother.
