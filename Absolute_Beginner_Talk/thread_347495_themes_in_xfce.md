### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-03-06
I believe you need to make sure you have all of the right theme engines installed so they can work. For example, goto Synaptic and make sure you have gtk2-engines-xfce and other engines you need for other themes. 

I also had problems with installing themes from xfce-looks and I couldn't find much info or help with my problems. I to this day haven't installed a theme from xfce-looks or gnome-looks.

How I got over this was to just modify my current xfce themes in /usr/share/themes

I wrote some detailed info on that in this link: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=371110](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=371110)

and a "How To" modify your gtkrc for xubuntu xfce-engines writen by me: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=377397](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=377397)

If you have questions, just ask me in the "How To" thread and I'll try to answer it.
