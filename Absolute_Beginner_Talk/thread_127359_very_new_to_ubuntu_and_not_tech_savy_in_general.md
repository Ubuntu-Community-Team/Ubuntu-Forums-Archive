### Post by jasay on 2006-02-08
When it asks for you password just enter your (user) password (the same one you use to log in).  Don't worry if nothing shows on the screen.  It does that so that people can't watch over your shoulder and see how long your password is.

As for kids software, you may have heard about edubuntu (ubuntu for schools basically).  [Here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/EdubuntuSoftwareList") is a list of programs that you can get with some discriptions, rough age groups, etc.  If you see something you like go to System>Administration>Synaptic and search for the program.  Then just right click > Mark for installation and apply(on the top bar). It should auto download and install everything for you.
