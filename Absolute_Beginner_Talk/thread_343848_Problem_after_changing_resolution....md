### Post by tommcd on 2007-01-22
Just a guess, but can you check your monitor's manual (or do an internet search) to see if your horizontal sync and vertical refreash rates are in the same range as:

Section "Monitor"
        Identifier      "Generic Monitor"
        Option          "DPMS"
        HorizSync       28-51
        VertRefresh     43-60
EndSection

in your xorg.conf file.

Also, what video card (or onboard video) do you have?
It should be compatible with this section:

Section "Device"
        Identifier      "Generic Video Card"
        Driver          "vesa"
        BusID           "PCI:1:5:0"
        Option          "UseFBDev"              "true"
EndSection

Perhaps your monitor and video specs can help someone fogure it out, or do a google search.
