### Post by jamyskis on 2007-03-05
#ubuntu-uk is the channel, you need a server to connect to first.

If you're using XChat (which I would recommend) then your best bet would be to click on the IRC menu, select Connect, scroll down to Ubuntu Server, select it and click ok, and then type /join #ubuntu-uk.

The hostname you're looking for is irc.ubuntu.com.
