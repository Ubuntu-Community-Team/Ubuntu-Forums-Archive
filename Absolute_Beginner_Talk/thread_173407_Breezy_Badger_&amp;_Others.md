### Post by WoodyMahan on 2006-05-10
Breezy, Warty, etc are different revisions of the Ubuntu System.  It's like the difference between, Win 95, 98, ME, 2000, XP.  Warty was the first release, then they upgraded and release Hoary, then they upgraded again and release Breezy.  Another update is coming in June and it will be called the Dapper Drake.  Funny names, but all simply older and newer versions of the Ubuntu Desktop Interface.
