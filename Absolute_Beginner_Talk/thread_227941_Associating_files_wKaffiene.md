### Post by SeanHodges on 2006-08-02
The path SHOULD be '/usr/bin/kaffeine' (kaffeine being the binary) You will often find the application 'exe' binaries in /usr/bin/.

I havent checked this, as I don't have my Ubuntu machine in front of me, but if I'm wrong I will find out tonight and post back.

The Media Connectivity extension is good ([https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/446/]("https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/446/")), but really applies to embedded video/audio. For what you want, I think the above will sort you out.
