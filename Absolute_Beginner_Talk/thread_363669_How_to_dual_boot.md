### Post by buuntuu! on 2007-02-17
don't forget to defragment more than once. for some reason xp's defragtool does not move all your data to the beginning of the partition. i recommend "auslogics disc defrag", download it from [http://www.download.com]("http://www.download.com"), its faster than the xp standard-tool. run discdefrag first and then the xp-tool.
then kick off with the ubuntu live-cd flavour of your choice, its quite self-explaining. 
you might want to take a look at [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php") for a quick intro to partitioning before getting started.
have fun :)
