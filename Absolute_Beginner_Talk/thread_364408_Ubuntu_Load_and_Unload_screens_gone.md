### Post by Wayren on 2007-02-18
So after a bad experience with attempting to load KDE in addition to gnome (I'm sure I did something wrong, hehe) and subsequently removing KDE I seem to have lost the Ubuntu load and unload screens. By load and unload I mean the screens that pop up right after you choose the Ubuntu option in GRUB to load and it shows the Ubuntu image with a bar underneath it that rapidly fills up before it pops to the login screen. The unload image (the one that's the same but shows the bar emptying when you shut down, restart, etc) is gone too. All I get is a blinking cursor. Anyone know how to restore them?

I did do a search to see if I could find any previous posts where this question may have been answered, but since I'm not sure of the screens actual names I didn't get very far. :wink:

### Post by solar george on 2007-02-18
The screens are displayed by a program called usplash.

Try reinstalling the "usplash-theme-ubuntu" in synaptic.
