### Post by x1a4 on 2007-03-10
Hi,

The Conexant modem is made **specifically for windows**.  

To get it running, first send a letter to Conexant condemning their practice of creating windows-only hardware.

Second, purchase and install the [Linuxant](http://www.linuxant.com/) driver, or better still, buy a new modem.  A good data/fax/voice modem for Linux is the SmartLink PCI Modem Model 5619PSV.  Costs less than 20 bucks.
