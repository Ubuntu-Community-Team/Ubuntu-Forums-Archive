### Post by Sef on 2007-01-30
If you have saved your word files as .doc instead of .odt or .abw, then a virus could affect them.  However, today you don't have to worry about that too much.  Most malware prefers to quietly install on your machine and steal your passwords, make it part of a botnet, or something else not good.  Of course if you are running Linux, they won't work.  Nevertheless, it could affect it if the malware affects third party apps like Firefox.
