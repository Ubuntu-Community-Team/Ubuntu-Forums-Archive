Go to System/Settings/Removable Devices and click on the tab Printers & Scanners, then markup the point: Automaticly run a program when a printer is connected. 

You need to do this with your printer turned OFF, and not turn the printer on until you have made the above change. This will enable you to install your printer correctly.
