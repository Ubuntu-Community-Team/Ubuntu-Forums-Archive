### Post by Saneless on 2007-10-25
I read that yesterday about using wine..

Here's my findings:

Installed ok, but I had to install it twice to get it to work (did the repair option).

Created a library with ALL my crap without a problem.  15GB of video and 6200 songs. The missing artwork processing took a while, but I had to leave anyway so it was all done by the time I came home.  I never had to do that before so that could be iTunes in general.  We're talking a couple hours absolute maximum, but probably less.

No ipod detected.  I expected that though, since on startup it says that the drivers for that option are missing/corrupted.  Hopefully someone figures that out.

Can't burn cds.  Never used it, don't care (I bought like 1 itunes song, and even that I de-DRM'd)

We're getting closer to having the damn thing work, but for right now at least I'm able to organize my library.. that's a good start.  I'll have to see if gtkpod or amarok or rhythmbox or hell, even Songbird work with syncing.  Songbird seems like a good product but it doesn't have library organization management and I can't even pick what directory to use.
