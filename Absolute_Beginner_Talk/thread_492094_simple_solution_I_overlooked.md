### Post by ieszu on 2007-07-04
First time poster, first time linux user.

I recently tried installing Ubuntu/Kubuntu/Xubuntu (all three to try to get one to work). I kept getting a GUI error, wouldn't let me load in the interface and said it was a V_BIOS error. :(

I realized the solution to the problem, the machine used to by an XP machine, but I decided I wanted Linux for my business computer. I had installed a graphics card, and was using it with XP. I went into BIOS, changed the graphics settings from the PCI card to the integrated graphics card, and all of a sudden it was booting properly. (No errors). I loaded Kubuntu (decided to stick with that variant), then went online and found the proper drivers for the graphics card, went back to BIOS and changed back to PCI from the integrated graphics option and [SIZE="5"]VIOLA! [/SIZE] It works just fine.... now to find a decent Linux RPG so that I can have some fun with the system while I wait for the business to open up.......:p
