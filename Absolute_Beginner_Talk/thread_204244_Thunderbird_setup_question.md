### Post by aysiu on 2006-06-26
It's actually the default behavior in Thunderbird, regardless of platform... at least until the release of Thunderbird 1.5 (thank God!).

Go to Edit > Accounts > Composition and Addressing > Composition > Start my reply above the quote
