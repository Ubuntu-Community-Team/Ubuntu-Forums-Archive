### Post by tkiesel on 2006-09-09
Search the forums for something called NX. It sends all connections over SSH, providing encryption so no one gets to snoop at the contents of your session. Also, it provides data compression at the X level, so it's a pretty snappy performer even on slow connections!

I've used it from the Howto here on the forums with much success!! :)
