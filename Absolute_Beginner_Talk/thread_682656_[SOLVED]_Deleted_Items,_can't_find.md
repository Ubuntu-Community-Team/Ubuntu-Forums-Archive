### Post by stalker145 on 2008-01-30
> **gleble said:**
> I accidentally deleted the Deleted Items icon from the toolbar. Now I can't find the Deleted Items. More important I can't absolutely delete anything and things are building up.

You can fix this in a couple of ways.

1) right-click on the task bar, choose add, and pick the trash applet.
2) open your file manager, view hidden files (in Nautilus it's <ctrl> + h), find .Trash-Username and delete everything out of there.
