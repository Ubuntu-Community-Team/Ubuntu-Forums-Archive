### Post by Ek0nomik on 2007-08-20
Router's are compatible with anything.  You don't actually *use* the router on your operating system.  Your wireless card or adapter or Ethernet cord are the ones who interact with the router and get the information from it.

The real question is, is your wireless card supported by Ubuntu?
