### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2008-02-04
The sound that is played when the login screen first appears can be changed in System->Administration->Login (I think that is what it is called)

The sound that is played after you login can be changed in System->Preferences->Sound(s)  (Not sure if it is singular or plural)
