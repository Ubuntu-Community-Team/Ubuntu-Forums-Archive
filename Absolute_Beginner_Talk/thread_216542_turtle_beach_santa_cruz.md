### Post by Denis on 2006-07-16
The mixer contains lots of volume settings (including for the seperate speakers). 

Open the volume control by double clicking on the speaker icon (on your panel somewhere). 
You can select the device (soundcard) to control in the file menu. 
You can add more sliders with Edit > Preferences. 

You should be able to control the volume of your different speakers (Front, Surround, Center, LFE). 
PCM Front, PCM Surround, PCM Center and PCM LFE sends stereo sound to all your speakers, adjust the volume and unmute these channels. 

An alternative for this volume control is "alsamixer" in the terminal.
