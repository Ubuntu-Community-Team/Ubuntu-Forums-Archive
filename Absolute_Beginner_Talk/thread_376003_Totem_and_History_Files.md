### Post by jlm3030 on 2007-03-06
Sorry about that. Basically to get rid of the history in totem go to your home folder display hidden files and find .recently-used file. To just clear it once delete the file. To disable the history completely right click on the file and go to properties. In the properties menu go to permissions and where it lists the access and change that to read-only.  Hope this helps
