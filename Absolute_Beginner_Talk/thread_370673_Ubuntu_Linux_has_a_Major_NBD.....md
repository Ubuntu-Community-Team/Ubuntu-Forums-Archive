### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-02-26
> **chrish670 said:**
> What is the correct way to do a clean re-install of this OS?  Please don't tell me just to install over the faulty installation... as doing that is what resulted in the current situation.  
I don't know that there's ever a "correct" way, but I make a point of completely erasing the drive between installations. I've had problems somewhat akin to your problems, where pre-existing partitions or data confused an operating system, usually while booting.

I use [Killdisk]("http://www.killdisk.com") to low-level format drives. From there I would suggest downloading the alternate CD (the installation version, not the desktop "live" CD) and installing from that. I don't know if there's a "cleaner" way to do it, but that works for me. :D
