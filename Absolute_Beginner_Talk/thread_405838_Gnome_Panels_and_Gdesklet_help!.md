### Post by arbulus on 2007-04-10
I tried the gDesklets dock and wasn't terrilby impressed.  What I did to achieve a dock-like look was to remove everything from the bottom panel and add only Launchers to it, I arranged the launchers to my liking and then right click on the panel and uncheck "Expand", that will shorten the panel to only surround the launchers you have on it.  I then changed the transparency of the panel to somewhere between 20-30% and then changed the size of the panel to my liking.  Of course you don't have the magnification or bouncing like the gDesklets dock will do, but this actually produced a really nice looking dock.
