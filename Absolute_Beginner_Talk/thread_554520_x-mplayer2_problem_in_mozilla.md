### Post by Bucky Ball on 2008-07-08
Hi there. I know this is an old thread but just in case this helps anyone else ...

I am using Hardy 8.04 on a HP dv6000 series lappy. In Firefox, I went to addons/plugins and at the bottom of the list there is a 'windows media player plugin 10' for Totem. Click on it, enable it, then reload the page you are attempting to play media on and you should see a player (which was missing before because the plugin was).

For me, the player on the page still didn't work so with the songs I was attempting to play selected and ready to stream, I right click the player, select 'open in movie player' and as mentioned, this should open up the streaming media file in Totem Movie Player, all details intact!!!

** Update; just selected another song, hit play on the page and it worked as intended with no movie player involved. I was about to shut down firefox and then bring it back up to see if it made any difference, but I only had to make a new selection for all to work **
