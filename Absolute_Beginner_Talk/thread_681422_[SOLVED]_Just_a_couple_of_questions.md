### Post by erfahren on 2008-01-29
#1) a splash screen is just a intermediary screen or image that shows while something is in the process of loading (a brand-name computer may show its logo while it's booting, software programs have them as well sometimes - GIMP for instance)

#2) open up Synaptic Package Manager (under System > Administration) and search for "wallpaper" - there's an app called "wallpaper-tray" that looks like what you want. (I had you do it that way because there might be other things in there that may interest you.)
