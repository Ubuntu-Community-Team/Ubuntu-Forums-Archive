### Post by igknighted on 2006-12-12
Overall they are very similar.  Here are a few differences:

* Beyl has far more options
* Compiz relies on Gnome, so running it on KDE is difficult
* Beryl used to be a part of compiz, now it is forked off
* [opinion] Beryl community seems to have better support than compiz ([www.beryl-project.org](www.beryl-project.org))
