### Post by freebeer on 2006-11-25
just a thought...

On the less powerful machines, you might want to consider using them as servers for your network.  They can do the routing, internet connection work, plus act as the place to backup the rest of the computers.  Use the ubuntu server distro on these - it's not burdened with a GUI, and therefore requires less resources.  (at least that's my understanding - I haven't actually tried the server version.)
