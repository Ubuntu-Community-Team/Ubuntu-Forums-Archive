### Post by abn91c on 2007-05-29
I used Wubi on my dell dimension 2400 with 512mb ram an it used about 3.5 gig of HDD space. It dualboots fine. Make sure you defrag windows first. When prompted to reboot be patient and allow the computer to do it by itself or installation may fail(dont use reset button or Ctrl/alt/ del). Intsllation of ubuntu will take about 30 min depending on your system speed. If you have a decent graphics card Beryl will work fine. My dell has the video it came with and Beryl and emerald themes work fine. Once intalled all the Ubuntu functions will be same as standalone installation, I have a second PC with Kubuntu and there is no noticeable difference in the two. By the way you can install the KDE files if you prefer KDE over gnome.I did :-) Good luck
