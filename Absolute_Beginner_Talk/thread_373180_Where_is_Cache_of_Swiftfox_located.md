### Post by glotz on 2007-03-01
Swiftfox is just a Linux optimized build of Firefox. So the cache's in your profile. [http://www.mozilla.org/support/firefox/profile#locate](http://www.mozilla.org/support/firefox/profile#locate)

And yes it probably will. And you'll probably want to take the entire profile.
