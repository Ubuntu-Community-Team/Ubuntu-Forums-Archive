### Post by Cruz on 2006-10-02
My first guess would be that the boot order in your BIOS isn't right and you boot first from HDD then from CD. You need to get into there (maybe it's not F2, maybe it's the delete button, or maybe your timing is not quite right) and make sure the PC boots from CD before the HDD.
