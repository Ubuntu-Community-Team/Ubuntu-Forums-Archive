### Post by llamakc on 2006-09-28
w3m, links, and lynx work fine from the commandline for browsing the Internet.

As for mail, many people use Mutt (yeah!) or Pine (boo!), which as you see, is a personal preference.

I use Mutt to connect via imap to my server where I receive email at.
