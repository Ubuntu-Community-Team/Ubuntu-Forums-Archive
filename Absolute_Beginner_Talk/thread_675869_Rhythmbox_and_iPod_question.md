### Post by nhandler on 2008-01-25
> **laidback said:**
> Cheater, Can you give us the process please.

Many Thanks

You make a new playlist or smart playlist using the playlist tab on the left. Add your music to the playlist. Connect your iPod to amarok. Now, go to the playlist tab and right click the playlist you want to add to your ipod. Now click transfer playlist to media device or synchronize playlist with media device. Go to the media device tab and click the transfer button at the top. This should add the playlist to your ipod.
