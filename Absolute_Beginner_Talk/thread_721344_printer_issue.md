### Post by bumanie on 2008-03-11
Unfortunately canon is not a great supporter of open source and in fact have been quoted as stating something like, "Hell would have to freeze over before they began writing drivers for open source."
I know there are some reversed engineered canon drivers around and there are some on a Japanese site (in Japanese of course). If you search long enough, you may be lucky and find something. Even if you find a driver from a similar series, those often work. Sorry I can't remember where to find the Japanese site.
