### Post by sunexplodes on 2006-11-07
In the "Applications" menu at the top left of the screen, go into "Add/Remove Applications". 

It comes up and on the left, in the middle of the screen between the top (list) and bottom (description) panels, there should be a checkbox that says "Hide Unsupported Applications". It's checked by default. If you uncheck it, it should show NVU in your search.
