### Post by darkmatter on 2005-10-03
[QUOTE=mit]wget -c [http://ubuntuguide.org/ubuntu5.04.tar.gz](http://ubuntuguide.org/ubuntu5.04.tar.gz)
tar zxvf ubuntu5.04.tar.gz[/QUOTE]

wget downloads files to your home directory. When you run tar without a path, it will extract to the same directory the archive is located in.

Open your home directory and look for a folder named 'ubuntu5.04' - that will be your guide.
