### Post by timetunnel on 2006-02-05
the ubuntu repositories all have serveral "sub"-repositories called "main", "restricted", "universe" and "multiverse". By default, only the first two are active. Since xboard is not in "main" but in "universe", you have to activate that in Synaptic.

For general info about how to do that, look here:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AddingRepositoriesHowto]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AddingRepositoriesHowto")
