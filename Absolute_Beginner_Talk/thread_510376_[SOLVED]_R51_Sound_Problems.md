### Post by kyphi on 2007-07-26
If you go to System, on the top panel (Applications Places System) and then to Preferences and from there to Sound you can place a tick into the two boxes near the top of the dropdown screen.

At the bottom of that same screen it should list your default sound device.  If more than one device is listed there you can toggle between them to see which one works for you.
