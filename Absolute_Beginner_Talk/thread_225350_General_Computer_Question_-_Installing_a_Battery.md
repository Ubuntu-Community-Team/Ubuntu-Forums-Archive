### Post by jeremy on 2006-07-29
You need to get a UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply), I use one by a company called APC whick gives me about 20 minutes power after the mains supply goes, and have a programme installed (from the repositories) called apcupsd, which senses when the power goes down, and cleanly shuts down the computer before the battery power runs out.
