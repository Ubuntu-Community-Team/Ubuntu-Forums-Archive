### Post by howefield on 2017-05-18
The criteria for this type of posting include...

```
Surveys: Before posting your survey, please post a request in the Resolution Center. An administrator will respond
 to you to let you know if you can post it in the Ubuntu,Linux, and OS chat.

    * Generally, surveys will only be granted to established ubuntuforum.org members who have a significant posting history.
    * Can only used to collect data on open source software, hardware, or licenses.
    * State the reason why you want to post it.
    * Have an end date.
``` 

Several of the criteria are not met in your thread but in any event, looks like you have almost reached your stated goals with the same post at the linuxquestions.org forum, I'm not seeing any persuasive reason for making an exception to the above criteria and having it reposted here.
