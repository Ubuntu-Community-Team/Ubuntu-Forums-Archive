### Post by mikalm on 2010-02-04
When registering a new account (mmmikal) I unchecked the "Let administrators send me email" box. This caused a php error which halted the entire process. It did register my account and I was able to log in, but I did not get sent an activation mail, and found it impossible to get one or to communicate anything from that account.

Upon registering this account I noticed the checkbox and left it checked, and now, I got an activation email.

I suggest doing something about the functionality of that checkbox to not include the activation mail - or leave it out altogether (replacing it with a noticed that you will be mailed, or something).
