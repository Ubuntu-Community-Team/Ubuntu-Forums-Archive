### Post by coffeecat on 2021-05-12
You validated the new email address with Ubuntu One, but you haven't yet validated the new address with your forum account. Simply click on the link in the verification email that the forum software sent you. That will re-activate your forum account.

Please remember: Ubuntu One and ubuntuforums are different domains, administered separately. Ubuntu One is used as a SSO (single sign-on) for the forum and various other Ubuntu sites.
