### Post by coffeecat on 2014-08-02
Your old account has a gmx.net email registered to it, the one used in 2005 to create the forum account. If the gmx email is still active, then set it as preferred in your Ubuntu One SSO account and confirm that you have been able to do that. A forum admin will then disassociate this account from Ubuntu One which will enable you to log into the bigbangbigbigbang forum account. If you are unable to set the gmx email as preferred for whatever reason, post back and we'll do this another way.

Once we have you successfully restored you to the bigbangbigbigbang account, we will disable this account and rename it, if you wish, to a random string.
