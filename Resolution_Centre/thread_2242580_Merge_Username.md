### Post by coffeecat on 2014-09-02
We don't merge accounts.

The reason the system created a new account is that you used a different email in your Ubuntu One account from that in the old Spr0k3t forum account.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004)

Set the email used in the  Spr0k3t account as preferred in your Ubuntu One account and then post back to confirm that you have done this. A forum admin will then disassociate this account from Ubuntu one which will enable you to log into Spr0k3t. Hint - the Spr0k3t email has requestfocus in the domain.

If you can't set the Spr0k3t email as preferred for whatever reason, post back anyway and we'll do this another way.
