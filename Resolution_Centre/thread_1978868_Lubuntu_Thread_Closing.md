### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2012-05-12
The plan is to review the tutorials, close the outdated ones, transcribe the remaining to the wiki.

It is going to be a long process, and we have an entire team to work through the section. You can join #ubuntu-wiki or #ubutnufourms if you wish to discuss or make suggestions or help out.

First step has been to close to new tutorials and write a script to transcribe forums posts to wiki syntax.

The forums will then be used for support questions and discussion. Documentation goes on wiki.

We hope to have the lubuntu documentation transcribed this weekend, the lubuntu team supports this policy 100 %. We were hoping people who posted the information on the forums would help, but we can not force them to do so.

Most teams use wiki or LP pages to track members and mailing lists to communicate.

You are free to work with the team if you wish to help.
