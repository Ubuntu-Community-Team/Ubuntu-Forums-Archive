### Post by coffeecat on 2014-03-10
You have two Ubuntu One accounts, one with which you successfully logged into the Flaggmann account on 1st August 2013. It had a yahoo.com email address set as preferred matching the Flaggmann account email.

On 26th August 2013 you logged into the forum with a different Ubuntu One account with a different yahoo email. As this didn't match any email address in the forum database, the system created a new account - flaggmann2. You need to use the correct Ubuntu One account to log into the Flaggmann account.
