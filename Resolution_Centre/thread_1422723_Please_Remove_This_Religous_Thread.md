### Post by matthew on 2010-03-06
Oh, and the Ubuntu CE subforum was added in 2006, long before we had to limit their proliferation. We can't and haven't added a new subforum for a 3rd party project for a very long time and have eliminated many that were inactive or started their own forums.

The legal term is "grandfathered in." We wouldn't start a new forum for any unofficial derivative today (nor even an official derivative without a direct request from Canonical), but had the other religion based derivatives existed and requested a forum at the time we approved the Christian one, we would have approved and created them.

It isn't favoritism, it is timing.
