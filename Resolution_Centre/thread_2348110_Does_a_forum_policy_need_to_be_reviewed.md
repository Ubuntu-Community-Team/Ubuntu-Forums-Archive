### Post by howefield on 2017-01-02
A thread in the Resolution Centre is unlikely to change Forum policy.

Should you wish to alter a properly convened forum policy the course of action open to you would be to put a cogently reasoned proposal onto the Forum Council agenda, then be prepared to back it up by attending an IRC meeting and debate your case.
