### Post by matthew on 2007-02-12
I will let another Admin give the final word on this. I am directly involved in the controversy so I choose to recuse myself from the final resolution here.

That said, here is my reasoning for jailing the thread and issuing the infraction.

Comparing anyone or anything to the Nazis is never productive. Period. This has been true as long as communication has occurred on the internet. In fact, there's a name for the phenomenon: [Godwin's Law]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Godwin%27s_law").

Your post came across as so final in its judgment and so harsh in its tone that there was no way anyone could have responded to it in a logical manner. In addition, the Nazi stigma is still extremely strong and gives rise to great and powerful emotions. Virtually no one wants to be compared to the Nazis.

By the way, you will notice that I did not give you an infraction for your [next post in the thread]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=2144654&postcount=89") equating Israel's actions with ethnic cleansing. While I find the comparison rather ridiculous, it could be discussed and argued against without the emotion that the Nazi tag was sure to have...this is evidenced by [BLTicklemonster's reply to you]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=2145016&postcount=90").
