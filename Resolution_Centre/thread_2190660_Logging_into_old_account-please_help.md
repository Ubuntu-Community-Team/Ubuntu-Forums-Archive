### Post by coffeecat on 2013-11-28
You need to set your preferred email in Ubuntu One to be the fastmail email you changed to in the faust99 account in October last year. Not the airpost email you used in Ubuntu One when you logged in earlier today. Once you have changed your preferred email in Ubuntu One to match the faust99 account, post back and an admin will disassociate this account from Ubuntu One which will then enable you to log into the faust99 account.
