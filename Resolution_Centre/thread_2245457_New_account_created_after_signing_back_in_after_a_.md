### Post by Iowan on 2014-09-23
The** paperdiesel** account uses a **cox.net** email account. To regain ** paperdiesel**, you need to set your SSO preferred email to that account (and verify it). After that is done, this account can be disassociated with your SSO account, and your next login should re-link you with ** paperdiesel**. If that email account is no longer available/accessible, there are other options.
