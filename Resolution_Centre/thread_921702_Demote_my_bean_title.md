### Post by jdong on 2008-09-16
A bit on the history (read: legend) of the titles:

At one point the staff was in serious debate about what is the best way of organizing the title, after realizing that the typical way online communities rank their members was not entirely in line with the spirit of our community.

I found the debate to be a bit silly so I proposed sarcastically to give ranks based on coffee beans. The idea stuck.

---

With that said, the titles are meant to be humorous and nonsensical and serve only to amuse our community. They shouldn't be taken seriously.
