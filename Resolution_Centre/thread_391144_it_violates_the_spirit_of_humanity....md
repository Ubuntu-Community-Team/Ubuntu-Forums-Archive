### Post by jdong on 2007-03-22
A warning infraction takes in excess of 5 clicks and, due to database response time, around a minute of total time to issue, so it's understandable that for minor edits a staff member may choose to directly do it, and maybe PM  the user about it.

In any case, the FC does not expect that staff will always perfectly follow the warning infraction routine, but should offer an explanation to a user, when requested, why the action was taken.
