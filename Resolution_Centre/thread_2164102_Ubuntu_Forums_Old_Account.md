### Post by lah7 on 2013-07-30
I had a similar problem whereby I somehow had a brand new account created through signing in with my Ubuntu SSO.

I e-mailed the forum admins for support and got told to change the preferred e-mail address in the Ubuntu SSO settings at [http://login.ubuntu.com](http://login.ubuntu.com) to my old e-mail (I initially signed up with)  It appears that was the reason I had a new account created. After making my old e-mail the preferred address and tried signing in again, I was able to access my old account again... in which I clicked **"Settings" **at the top to change to my 'newer' primary e-mail address, and then updated Ubuntu SSO. Now it signs in with the correct e-mail.
