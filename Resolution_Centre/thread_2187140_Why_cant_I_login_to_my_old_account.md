### Post by coffeecat on 2013-11-11
You used an outlook email as preferred in Ubuntu One. This didn't match the gmail in the old account - hence the new account. All explained in the prelogin notice and [linked sticky]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2164051"). 

Set the gmail as preferred in your Ubuntu One account and then post again to confirm that you have done so. After which I or another admin will disassociate this account from Ubuntu One and you'll be able to log into the old account. Please note that there is a period (full-stop) in the local part of your gmail address. Gmail ignores this but you need to get it exactly the same as in the old forum account. The system does a simple string match of email addresses to link Ubuntu One accounts with forum accounts.
