> **Catchpole said:**
> Is there a way to get email notification of a reply to my posts?

catchpole.

Yes. Once you have 10 posts in support areas, you will be able to access your forum account settings from Settings -> General Settings -> Default Thread Subscription Mode. That will autosubscribe you to any threads you post in. Until then, you can manually subscribe to each thread you are interested in from Thread Tools at the top of the thread page. Choose "Subscribe to the Thread", and then one of three email options from "Notification Type". You can subscribe to threads you haven't posted in with the manual option if you wish.
