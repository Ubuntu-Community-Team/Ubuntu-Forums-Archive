### Post by Dustin2128 on 2011-07-24
Well, I've basically had it with ubuntu forums- a private message from a mod about my signature pushed me over the top. 

The main reason I'm quitting is, you guessed it, rather bad moderation. No offense intended to you who donate your time, I'm glad you do it, but moderators here are way overbearing and sometimes downright rude. There are some good ones, uRock is who I'd hold up as an example of what mods here should be like. But here are my major gripes: 

Oversensitivity. Just about anything can get a thread closed here. This is the only forum that I know of that freaks out about recurring discussions and necromancy, for instance, and anything that might possibly be illegal in some backwards nation is not postable. Not to mention the denizens of the forum are oversensitive themselves, almost nobody can take a joke.

Second up, there's people who don't get the point. I see threads get closed for being off-topic. Sorry people, this is the cafe- it's *supposed to be *off topic sometimes. Deal with it. Almost all the mods take everything uberseriously (my sig is a joke. a damned joke. Not an insult or derogatory attack.).

Next up is the CoC. This is the main reason why I don't always blame the mods for being... that way. But I'm sorry, after 3 years, I still feel like I'm "posting in the kiddie pool" so to speak, never allowed to discuss anything deeper than the next shiny piece of technology. I cannot deal with that any more. 

Fourth, I strongly dislike the usual attitude on the forums about proprietary software, and the way ubuntu is going in general. Sorry Shuttleworth, attractive design may be a feature, but I was drawn in to ubuntu because of stability and scripting ability, as only
a modestly technically inclined windows user. If ubuntu was in the buggy, bloated state it's in now, I would never have swapped. The cube was nice, but not a deal breaker.

Finally, arbitrary thread closures, which seem to happen with some mods more than others, *ahem*. Necromancy closures on threads that are less than 4 months old, closures on threads because the mod thinks "/thread", and so on and so forth. 

TLDR: I am leaving the forums because of moderation. I think the mods are good people for donating their time, but some of them should not be mods. Also I don't blame them always, because CoC sucks. 

TLDRTTLDR:Bye.

Anyway, if for some weird reason you want to contact me, PM me and I'll give you my email address.

I might still post in the support forums because I have an ubuntu server.

My only regret is not having quit sooner.

### Post by bapoumba on 2011-07-25
Thread moved to the Resolution Center and reopened.

If you believe threads were closed inappropriately, please report them. We cannot read everything that is posted on the boards and we for sure do not check each other moderation work on the forums. But we do discuss a lot, either in reported threads or in the Staff area and this is how moderation adjustments are settled. We do not expect every Staff to give input, but when 3 or 4 people agree, we consider the decision balanced enough.
Only one voice who does not agree usually is enough for a "do nothing, wait and see" position.

May be it is a good decision to restrain your posting to the support area and choose some other resources for more social and/or engaged discussions where rules fits your needs better.
We gather a large crowd from multicultural backgrounds. Talking here is not talking in the privacy of your home or with a rather homogeneous bunch.

Re: other sites policies and ToS, we have always respected these. I'm not saying we agree with them. We respect them. Their site, their company, their school, their choices, their rules. No more, no less.

All in all, please report threads. There is no other way we can collectively have a look. Thanks.
