### Post by coffeecat on 2018-04-30

At different times, Ubuntu One has required both a name field and a username field, then only a name field, and then both a name field and username field. A couple of years ago the page that you saw started popping up for some people. I believe it appeared for people who didn't have value for a username field, but I can't be sure, because the Ubuntu One people never gave us forewarning that they would be getting up to things that would cause alarm and despondency amongst our members here. 

As for the sentence  "The site 'Ubuntu Forums https' requires that you set a valid username" is concerned, I can assure you that Ubuntuforums is quite happy with the perfectly valid username that you already have here. Where the idea that Ubuntuforums required something came from, I have no idea, but I would guess somewhere between the big blue yonder and the base of the rainbow.
