> **jon54 said:**

Not according to the information that I can see. You need to click on the link in the verification email that the forum software sent you.

If by chance you also changed your email in your Ubuntu One account, that would have caused Ubuntu One to send you a verification email. If you are referring to a verification email sent by Ubuntu One, that is quite separate from the forum. To emphasise: your Ubuntu One account and your forum account are two completely different and separately administered entities. The only link between the two is that you use Ubuntu One to log into the forum. We do not administer Ubuntu One.

I repeat, look for a verification email sent from ubuntuforums, not from Ubuntu One, and click on the link.
