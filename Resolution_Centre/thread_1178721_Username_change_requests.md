### Post by matthew on 2009-06-04
Following a recent Forum Council meeting we have decided to relax the prohibition on changing usernames.
     
    However, 2 points
     
    [list]
    [*]Make sure that any change you request is what you want - we will only change it ONCE.
    [*]Please make sure to give us 3 alternatives, we don't want to have to keep asking for new alternatives if your choice is not available.
    [*]If we find that the volume of change requests increase dramatically - we will revert.
    [/list]
