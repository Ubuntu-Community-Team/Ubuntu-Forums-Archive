After reading all the material, I think the staff member was polite and appropriate. Whether he had done so using an unofficial PM or as he did using an official warning that sends a PM, you were still contacted privately and discreetly with no further repercussions.

The post you made may have been done in innocence, with no ill intent. I believe you when you said so. Even so, it wasn't in the best of taste and hence the warning and polite comments as to why.

I support the actions of the staff in this matter.
