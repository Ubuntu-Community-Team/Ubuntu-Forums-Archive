### Post by coffeecat on 2012-07-23
Your other posts in this thread were certainly unexceptional which made the post that was jailed stand out all the more. Commenting on a regression is not problematic, but the post in question comes across as sarcastic. Did you really intend to mean that Canonical is deliberately inhibiting function in gnome classic in order to "force" people to use Unity? That's an extraordinary assertion, hardly just an "observation of Canonical's practices."

A discussion about the many possible causes of a regression would be useful, but suggesting that there is some hidden agenda behind the regression is simply FUD unless you have good evidence for this.

Let's put this in context. Experience shows that comments such as yours have the real potential to stir up dissension and sour a thread. Hence the the use of the word trolling. Last year, the staff were forced to formulate a three strikes and you're out policy for trolling because the level of trolling - not just about Unity - had begun to sour the whole forum. The policy has been effective. Normally, a strike 1 would be one infraction point. You were given only a warning, which is used to remind members of the forum rules, and this seems fair in the circumstances.
