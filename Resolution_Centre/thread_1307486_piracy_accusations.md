### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-11-01
First I am sorry you are having problems with your games.

In terms of Piracy, we of course have no way of knowing if you own the games or not so perhaps the closure of the threads could have been worded better.

Please understand it is a fine line in terms of what we can support and we do not want to support either piracy or the appearance of piracy.

Second please understand we are primarily a support site for Ubuntu and not wine or windows games or other software.

So asking for support on how to install wine is one thing, but asking for detailed instructions on how to get a game running is another. Regardless of ownership there are some issues:

1. Wine is a 3rd party project.

2. Linux is not a drop in replacement for Windows and you may have problems running some games , or other applications, even in wine.

3. your best source of information on how to get your games running is in fact winehq :

[http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/)

That is the maintained database for how to get your applications or games running.

So, IMO, if you are having problems getting wine to run, please post here.

If you are having specific problems with a specific application or game, please use wineHQ.

You may also try : [http://gwos.org/doku.php/games:start](http://gwos.org/doku.php/games:start)

or google or the game manufacturer / software vendor.

Unfortunately we can offer very limited support for 3rd party windows applications running on wine on these forums.
