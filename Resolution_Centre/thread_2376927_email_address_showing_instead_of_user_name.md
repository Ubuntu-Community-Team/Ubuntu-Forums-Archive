### Post by coffeecat on 2017-11-07
Possibly because your linked Ubuntu One SSO account appears to have your email address as your username. Or perhaps if you've changed your Ubuntu One username since creating your forum account, you may have un-ticked your name in your Ubuntu One Personal Data Request page when you first blogged in. (I see your forum account was created nearly 11 months ago.) There's a link at the top left of the main page before you login, which says "Please read before SSO login" and which leads to this sticky:

[https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004)

More details of how the system allocates your username is there. If you want your username changed, that can be done, but please read this other sticky first:

[https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2234107](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2234107)
