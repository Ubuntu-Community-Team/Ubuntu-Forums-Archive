### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2011-05-31
The Ubuntu Christian Edition is an official Ubuntu release and as such we provide technical support for UCE here.

For example, we would provide support if there were a problem with dansguardian or another application.

The CoC prohibits discussion of religion / politics.

Technical support is not the same as promoting religious philosophy so we do not see a conflict of interest.

We we allow technical support, but would not allow discussion of religion.

The UCE crowd has been very respectful of our policies and contributes to the Ubuntu community without pushing a religious agenda.
