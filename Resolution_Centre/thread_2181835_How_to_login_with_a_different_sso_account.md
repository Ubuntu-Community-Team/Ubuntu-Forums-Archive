### Post by coffeecat on 2013-10-19
It sounds as though in one PC you are logging into your Ubuntu One account which is linked to your forum account, and on the other you are logging into (or already logged into) your wife's Ubuntu One account which is linked to your wife's forum account.

An Ubuntu One account can only be linked to one forum account, and vice versa. If you are logging into two forum accounts at different times then there are two Ubuntu One accounts involved. On the PC that logs you into your wife's account, simply log out of your wife's Ubuntu One account, then log into your Ubuntu One account and thence into your forum account.
