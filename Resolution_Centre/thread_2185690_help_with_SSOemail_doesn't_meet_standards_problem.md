If you see two lines, they will be Full name and Email address. If three, Full name, Username and Email address. Please pm me with all the information on those two or three lines.

Now log out of that Ubuntu One account, log into the one that is linked to this forum account and go through the same procedure and pm me with all the information on the two or three lines for the second Ubuntu One account.
