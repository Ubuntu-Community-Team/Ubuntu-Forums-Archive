### Post by aysiu on 2007-01-07
Freedom doesn't mean "do whatever the hell you want with no consequences."

If people could post *whatever* they wanted *wherever* they wanted, this forum would be overrun with spam and trolls. Moderators exist for a reason--to maintain order and to make sure threads are in the proper place. HowTos being in the HowTo section allow for people to find HowTos easily. Support questions being the support sections allow people trying to help an easy way to give support.

Posting complaints about the forum in the Multimedia & Video section may seem like "freedom" to you, but it's just clutter to everyone else. I've moved it to the Resolution Center. If you're still around, an administrator will get back to you on this issue. If you're not, then that's that, then.
