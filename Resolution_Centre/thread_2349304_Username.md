### Post by coffeecat on 2017-01-13
I did ask for three usernames since, with well over 2 million accounts, a first choice is sometimes/often not available. Fortunately, makouser hadn't been used before and I've been able to change your forum account username to that.

Just to briefly pick up on a couple of other points you raise. Whether or not you had a previous Ubuntu One account, the Ubuntu One account linked to this forum account, and which you have used to log in now, was in existence on April 15 2014. You logged into the forum on that day from your Ubuntu One account, and the system created your forum account automatically. There has been no change in the link between your Ubuntu One account and this forum account since April 2014, so the Ubuntu One account will be the same one as then.

Yes, the system matches on email. You can see the details if you click on the "Please read before SSO login" link at the top of any page. That the system used the local part of your email to generate the forum username would be because you un-ticked your name in the Personal Data request page as described in that link.
