### Post by cariboo on 2016-04-22
The reason you can't change your own user name, is because we have several members who would like to change their user names as often as they change their shirts. It's hard enough to keep track of over 2 million user names, without members randomly changing them when ever they want, and even though the hardware that the forum runs on is more than adequate for the task, the database would need re-indexing on a regular basis which as it stands now takes well over 6 hours to do.
