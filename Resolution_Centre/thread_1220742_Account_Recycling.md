### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-07-23
We have discussed this issue in the past and we do not delete posts nor do we recycle user names.

The reason is because we do not wish to cause data base corruption (and nobody is willing to test it out) and in terms of user recycling the original user may not like it.

If you can give a very, very, very good reason to do so it may be considered, but, based on previous discussions, I would say the chance of granting your request is very very very small.

I advise you choose another name.
