Malicious code and destroying valuable data is simply not funny.

Now that the policy has been explained to you I am going to ask two things:

1. Please stop distorting the intent of the policy. Again it is clear (to me) from your posts you understand the policy.

2. Consider yourself heard and the policy will not be changing anytime soon due to ongoing problems with malicious code, specifically rm -rf , on the forums.
