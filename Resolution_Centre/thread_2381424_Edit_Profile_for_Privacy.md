### Post by coffeecat on 2017-12-31
Hello.

What I've done for the time being is to change the Privacy field from *Display Age and Full Date of Birth* to *Hide Age and Date of Birth*. What this does is to remove your age and date of birth from the ordinary profile page (which, by the way, is only visible to logged in registered members of the forum), but age and DoB are still visible to forum staff via the staff-only control panels. I've done this because, in unusual circumstances, having the DoB on the forum database, albeit only visible to staff, can be useful. But if you want me to remove it entirely, please say so and I will do this.

I'm sure you've seen the profile restriction sticky, but in case not, here's the link: [https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393)
