I believe I should also clarify -

Discussion(s) on Mono is (are) allowed as long as they seem to be productive discussions in some way related to Ubuntu or the Open source community.

The Mono project is controversial and we reserve the right to ask topics/discussions not related to the goals of these forums (Technical support for Ubuntu) to be moved off site.

If you disagree with a decision of the staff to close or move a Mono discussion please bring your questions or concerns here. Please provide a link and a justification as to why you feel the thread should be allowed to continue and we can review the decision.

It may be the topic is indeed better served off site in which case it will remain closed.
