### Post by coffeecat on 2022-02-19
> **Apanta said:**
> I changed my old email to a new one that worked and saved the new settings.

You don't say whether you changed your email in your Ubuntu One SSO account, or in your forum account. However, I can tell from the log associated with your forum account that you have only ever changed your forum account email once back in March 2021, and that you were able to re-activate the forum account from the verification email sent by the forum software.

Which means that what you are talking about now is changing your email in your Ubuntu One SSO account. 

> **Apanta said:**
> Unfortunately I have not received any emails to enable the new settings to access the forum and I still have to log in via SSO. 

That does not make sense to me. What do you mean by "enable the new settings"? You can still log in via SSO so what is the problem? If you are not receiving verification emails after changing your Ubuntu One email, then you will have to contact the Ubuntu One admins. We do not administer Ubuntu One SSO.

> **Apanta said:**
> Is there a way to log in directly via username and password avoiding any other procedure?

wildmanne39 has already answered this. The answer is no. The only way to log into the forum is by means of Ubuntu One SSO.

Having said all that, I'm still not sure what the problem is. You are successfully logging in by means of Ubuntu One SSO, and your forum account is active.

I'll just make one other comment, because it is a common cause of misunderstandings. Your Ubuntu One and forum emails do not have to match. They only have to match on initial linking. Your two accounts are already linked. But if you want to change the email in either or both of the forum and Ubuntu One accounts, you have to do so from your Ubuntu One SSO account for Ubuntu One, and from your forum account for your forum account.
