### Post by coffeecat on 2015-08-07
*Thread moved to **Resolution Centre**.*

You used the wrong hotmail email in your Ubuntu One SSO account. Hence the duplicate forum account. See: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004)

If the hotmail address you used in Browser_ice is still active, set it as preferred in your Ubuntu One account and then post back to confirm that you have done this. One of us will then disassociate this account from Ubuntu One, which will enable you to log into the Browser_ice account.

Hint - you changed the hotmail email in Browser_ice in 2009. The one you need to set in Ubuntu One begins with the letter 'f'. If that hotmail is no longer active, post back and we'll find another way to do this.
