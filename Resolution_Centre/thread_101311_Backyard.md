### Post by aysiu on 2005-12-09
I would propose something like this as a starting point. Of course, the word *inflammatory* is very broad and probably needs to be defined further: > While there will never be a completely unmoderated section of the Ubuntu Forums, the Backyard is a place where some more controversial material can be discussed, and the use of profanity (as long as it does not violate any of the guidelines below) is allowed. The Backyard serves two primary purposes:

1. It allows some users a less moderated place to discuss topics that can get quite heated.
2. It allows users who wish to avoid potentially offensive discussions a way to easily do so.

A thread may be moved from Community Chat or a support forum to the Backyard if it either by its nature seems inflammatory or by its evolution has turned inflammatory.

THIS SECTION IS NOT MODERATED EXCEPT FOR THE FOLLOWING SITUATIONS:
1. "XXX" Rated posts, sexual material or SPAM.
2. Personal attacks towards individuals and groups.
3. No illegal activities in the US, Bittorent sites or pirated software.
4. No posting of drug making or terrorist related material.
5. No racial or racist comments or activities will be allowed.
6. The Ubuntu Code of Conduct is applied here and must be followed.
You are posting here at your own risk. If you choose to participate in the discussions in this forum we ask you keep them in this forum.
