### Post by coffeecat on 2020-12-16
@basicsearcher, I think I can see your confusion.

You need a password to log into your Ubuntu One SSO account, but once logged in you will remains logged into Ubuntu One. If you are already logged into Ubuntu One and you click on the Login with SSO button in the forum home page, you will be taken to the Ubuntu One Personal Data Request page, with your account name already filled in. All you do then is to click on the green "Yes, Log me in" button and you will be logged into Ubuntu forums. No second password needed. That's the whole point of SSO (Single Sign On) - you just need one password for the SSO site. No dedicated forum password needed.

Reading your post I suspect that sometimes you were not logged into Ubuntu One, and Ubuntu One prompted you for your Ubuntu One password in order to log into Ubuntu One. On other occasions you were already logged into Ubuntu One, and you simply needed to confirm your identity on the Ubuntu One Personal Data Request page.

Next time, look at the URL in the URL bar for each page you go through. If the domain is ubuntu.com, then you are on the Ubuntu One site. If ubuntuforums.org, then you are on the forum site.
