### Post by matthew on 2009-12-14
Infraction points are essentially a more formal form of a warning, at least at first. Having one or two points will not affect your account in any way other than giving staff a way to track problems.

At ten points, all posts by the member go into a moderation queue where they must be approved by a staff member before they appear in the forums.

At fifteen points, the member is temporarily banned from using the forums until the points expire.
