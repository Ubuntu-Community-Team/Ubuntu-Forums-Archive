### Post by coffeecat on 2017-05-04
Sorry, no.

When you reach 2000 beans, you will be automatically moved to a usergroup with extra privileges, one of which is the ability to create your own custom title. Until then the forum software assigns you titles allocated to various bean counts. We keep those bean count thresholds secret, just for fun, and change the titles every so often. Even if we did change your user title, it would soon change again the next time you reached one of the thresholds.  

By the way, when you do reach a bean count of 2000 or more, you have to be logged in before the script will promote you. The script runs hourly, so that can take up to an hour after logging in.
