### Post by jdong on 2008-10-29
We do not allow users to delete or close their own threads because it can lead to all sorts of confusion. If you find the need to re-start a thread, comment on the thread that you'd like to close the thread and start over, then report your post (which will alert the staff who will help you get the thread closed)
