### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-09-09
The bottom line is that these are moderated forums and we wish to keep as much as possible to Support.

We do provide the Cafe as an area where people can have off-topic, non support conversations / threads. However that does not mean you are free to discuss any topic you choose and these are moderated forums.

Some topics are deemed inappropriate for these forums and we rely on the judgment of the staff to determine if a topic or thread is allowed.

If a thread is closed you may ask for a review and that the thread be re opened.

Please keep in mind we are volunteers and accusing the staff of actions of being "abserd" is probably not the best approach, it certainly does not motivate me to start searching for the thread in question and to review it.

Please provide a link to the thread in question and the reason you feel it should be re-opened and I will review your request at time allows.

Otherwise, there are many other options available for you to express your opinions including possibly other sites, other forums, or your own personal blog.
