### Post by coffeecat on 2014-10-30
The password would be the Ubuntu One SSO password. We do not administer Ubuntu One.

I would guess that one of your plugins in responsible. I use the self-destructing cookies extension but I have ubuntu.com in the whitelist. If it wasn't, the extension would erase the login.ubuntu.com cookies and I would have to log into Ubuntu One every time I wanted to log into the forum. I'd suggest adding ubuntu.com to your SDC whitelist for starters.

As for being asked to create and confirm a **new** password - I don't know. Is that what you meant?
