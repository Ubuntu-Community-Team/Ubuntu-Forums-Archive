### Post by coffeecat on 2013-11-10
I'm not sure I completely follow you. Are you referring to this forum account?

When you logged in from Ubuntu One with your mailinator email account set as preferred in Ubuntu One, the system found an email match with this forum account and linked the two. That is why you are posting from the forum account with the username ejectHD. The ejectHD forum account was created in October 2009 and the logs show that it was activated from the verification email sent to the mailinator email address. This is standard security practice and would mean that whoever registered this forum account in 2009 had access to your mailinator email account. Are you sure this wasn't you?

We do certainly take forum account security seriously. However, if an email account is compromised, anyone can register a forum account on almost any forum posing as the email account holder. If I am understanding your post correctly, I think the question is: was someone else able to use your mailinator account in 2009 or did you create the ejectHD account and had forgotten about it. If there is any hint of account hijacking anywhere, then we could arrange to get you set up with a fresh forum account from a fresh Ubuntu One account if you wish.
