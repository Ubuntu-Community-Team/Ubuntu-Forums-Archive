### Post by Arup on 2009-11-25
I must say even a old Unix hand like myself learned a lot from this forum and thanks to Ubuntu I can now happily push an alternate to Windows for those who are willing to give it a try. There is lots of good pertinent information in this forum from end users alike and in general, the atmosphere is pretty good. However there are couple of issues here that tend to mar the very purpose of participating in this forum. The glaring factor is the so called fast distro users. They would go to each and every thread and try and peddle their distros like arch or gentoo at the cost of confusion. They do so in a very surreptitious manner or some of them even resort to blatant name calling. They would compare Ubuntu users to Windows users, call Ubuntu a bloated piece of software, put the developers down and even belittle the users. This goes on in quite a few thread. The problem with this is that many new first time Ubuntu users are totally confused and scared away and this just goes to hurt the cause of spreading desktop Linux which till now Ubuntu is doing a mighty fine job. No software is perfect but Ubuntu is trying to offer a distro that works with minimal tweaking. Just imagine when a new user comes for a simple advice and is told that the distro of his choice here which is namely Ubuntu is bloated, slow and he or she would be better off with something like Arch or Gentoo. We all are aware how difficult it is for even a Linux veteran to install these two OS so just imagine a new Ubuntu user being steered in that direction. He or she is bound to stay away from Ubuntu and Linux forever. 

Now the title here suggests Ubuntu forums, I also participate in various other distro forums where the crux is toward the distro, no distractions are allowed there and suggestion of one distro being better than others are just not tolerated unlike here. Out in this forum, calling names and spreading misinformation is common play for various fans of other distros, they don't hesitate to call Ubuntu bloated and would shamelessly peddle their so called faster distro without any hesitation or thought. If someone is to object to this kind of behavior, they would then gang up and attack that person directly and thereby they manage to ruin the entire thread. 

I have been given an infraction for taking my stand on one such thread. Now I feel that I rightfully defended my point of view and also gave a good argument against this kind of behavior. The infraction doesn't hurt me in any way as I defended the software this forum is intended to foster. However in light of all this, I have decided that its not really worth it for me to participate here anymore and therefore would sincerely request the mods to remove my account at their discretion. 

I thank all who helped me out and for all the good advices, I also made some good friends and some real bitter enemies as well ;), in the end it was all worth it as now I can safely tell those who are looking for an alternate to Windows about Ubuntu. Feedback from the forum and the fact that the developers pay serious attention to the pertinent issues discussed here have made this distro what it is and that is number one in desktop Linux and from what I see, it will continue to do so in the future.

Thank you.
