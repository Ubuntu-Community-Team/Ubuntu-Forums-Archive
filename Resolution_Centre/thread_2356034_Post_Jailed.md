### Post by QIII on 2017-03-19
What other forums do or do not allow is not under our control -- and is of no consequence here in any case.  

The recording of streaming media may violate the ToS of some providers and it may be patently illegal in some jurisdictions.  Since our community is world-wide, we err on the side of caution.  We also do not want the Forums to become a resource for those whose intent is to skirt ToS or the law.  Accordingly, the decision to remove your thread from public view was informed by this part of the [Forum Rules]("https://ubuntuforums.org/misc.php?do=showrules"):

> Material that suggests illegal activity or contains illegal content is also forbidden. We do not support circumventing TOS, EULA, etc here. Such threads will be closed and offending users will be penalised with infractions and warnings. 

Notice the last sentence.  At one time, your thread would likely have drawn an immediate infraction, if not an outright ban.  We have decided to tone that down and avoid such draconian measures in simple cases.

Your thread will remain out of public view where it is.
