### Post by coffeecat on 2014-03-29
You used a gmail in your Ubuntu One account which does not match the yahoo email address registered to the wilho forum account. That is why the system created a new forum account. Details here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2164051](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2164051)

Please set the yahoo email as **preferred** in your Ubuntu One account and then post back to confirm that you have been able to do this. A forum admin will then disassociate this account from Ubuntu One which will allow you to log into the wilho account from your Ubuntu One SSO account.
