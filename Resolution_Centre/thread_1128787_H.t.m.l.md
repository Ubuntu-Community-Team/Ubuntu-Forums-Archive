### Post by jdong on 2009-04-18
There's various technical reasons for HTML being disabled:

(1) History suggests HTML sanitization to be a pretty hit-or-miss security-wise, and we'd rather not go through the unnecessary risk.

(2) Malformed HTML affects browser to browser differently and can potentially screw up page rendering.

(3) The checks to make sure (1) and (2) don't happen have a potential of increasing server load or imposing enough restrictions to make the use of HTML unintuitive.
