### Post by coffeecat on 2015-11-23
You did change the email on the forum account, but you missed the verification email sent to the new email address. Until you click on the link in the verification email and reactivate your account, you won't be able to post anywhere except here in the Resolution Centre.

Have a look in your inbox or, failing this, your spam folder. Some email providers put automatically-generated emails such as our account verification email into the spam folder.

If you can't find it, or it has expired, post back here and I or one of the other admins will re-activate your account for you.

We don't administer Ubuntu One, but I can tell you that Ubuntu One uses a similar email verification procedure when you change emails on your account. And so do most other places where you register accounts - something to bear in mind when you find you can't use an online account.
