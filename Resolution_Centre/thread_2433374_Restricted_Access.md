### Post by coffeecat on 2019-12-18
A verification email would have been sent to your new gmail address. The verification email includes a link that has to be clicked in order to re-activate your account. Without re-activation you have restricted access.

Verification emails do sometimes get consigned to the spam folder with some email providers, but you have looked in there. I have no idea why this one would have gone astray, but I've re-activated your account for you. You should be OK now.
