### Post by coffeecat on 2014-06-20
If you are getting the "An Ubuntu One account already exists with this email" message from Ubuntu One, it means what it says. You already have an Ubuntu One account with that email. I suggest you log out of Ubuntu One and then try logging in with that earlier email. If you don't remember your password, click on the "forgot your password" link. If you can log into the other Ubuntu One account, you will then be able to associate it with the "What is in a name?" account and we can then disable this account and you can delete the Ubuntu One account associated with it if you wish.

But, however we do this, you need to be able to prove to us - as a security measure - that you are the registrant of the "What is in a name?" account. Which is why I didn't give you the full email address, only a hint.
