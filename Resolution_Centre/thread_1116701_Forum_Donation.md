### Post by matthew on 2009-04-05
Several years ago, before Canonical so graciously offered to allow the forums to be hosted on their servers, the forum hosting was paid for by one person, ubuntu-geek. Others offered to help, and those who did were given the option to have a special donator title, which they still carry.

We haven't had a need nor a way to receive donations since the forum hosting has moved to Canonical's servers, but thank you so much for your desire to do so.
