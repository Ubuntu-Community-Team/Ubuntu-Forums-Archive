### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-12-26
Since this is not a real resolution center problem, I will respond rather than waiting for an admin.

If your tutorial was against forum policy, we would never have allowed it to begin with.  There is nothing wrong with compiling programs from source or direct from unofficial repositories, it's just not the suggested method for most people.  You gave notice that the guide is for installing a pre-release version and is for advanced users, which is adequate.  If you are still worried, you may just want to add something like "This guide is not the official way to install mplayer/gmplayer in Ubuntu because it is not installing precompiled and packaged software from the official Ubuntu repositories."  You are certainly welcome to make your case about why you prefer your method.
