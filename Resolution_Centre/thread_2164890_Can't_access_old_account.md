### Post by coffeecat on 2013-08-02
The system allows you to change your email on either or both of the Ubuntu One and forum accounts, once the initial association of accounts has taken place, which is why just changing the email isn't enough. I've now disassociated the alanlitster account from Ubuntu One. So long as your Ubuntu One SSO preferred email is the same as the email on the btindie account, the next time you login you will be taken to the btindie account and it and your Ubuntu One account will be associated.
