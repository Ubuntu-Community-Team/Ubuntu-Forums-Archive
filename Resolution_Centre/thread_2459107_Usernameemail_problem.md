### Post by coffeecat on 2021-03-10
Moved to Resolution Centre.

Your forum profile email has nothing to do with login, which is carried out by Ubuntu One SSO. Go to your Ubuntu One account - [https://login.ubuntu.com/](https://login.ubuntu.com/) - and change your email address there under manage email addresses. This won't affect the link between your SSO and forum accounts. Emails only have to match on initial login or forum account creation. The link can only be removed by a forum admin.
