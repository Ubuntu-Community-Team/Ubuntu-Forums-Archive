### Post by QIII on 2017-04-04
Unfortunately, the entire site has the effect of being a very blatant advertisement for itself.  

We understand that sometimes there will be incidental advertising -- say when you link to an article on a webzine.  But so long as the article is of value, that can be tolerated. There are also free blog platforms whose references to themselves (they have to make money to continue to provide the service) are understated and secondary to the blog. 

However, the way Relayo has that thing set up, your blog is effectively incidental to their promotion of their own product. You are, in effect, advertising Relayo more than they are providing a good platform for *_you_*.  For instance, if I click on the top menu and select "Home", I get Relayo's home, not yours.  I'm greeted with how great Relayo is, not how great your blog is.  

We don't want the Forums to become an advertising channel for Relayo.
