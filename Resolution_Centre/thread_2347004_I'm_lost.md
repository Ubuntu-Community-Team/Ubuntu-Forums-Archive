### Post by coffeecat on 2016-12-20
There is nothing stopping you from changing your forum email address, and your account is fully active so you can post in all sub-fora that are open to posting. If you wish to change your email, simply click on the Settings link at top right of any page and then on "Edit Email & Password" on the left. Ignore the password fields. The forum password is no longer used as this function is taken over by Ubuntu One SSO, but we've had to retain the password fields in that page for a certain purpose. If you do change your email address, please remember to re-activate your account from the verification email.

I think you must be thinking of certain restrictions in place for those with fewer than 10 posts in support ares, as explained here: [https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393)

As I say, this does not prevent you from changing your email address or from posting.
