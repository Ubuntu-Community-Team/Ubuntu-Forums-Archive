### Post by Iowan on 2013-12-11
If the original account name was **cuby**, it was registered with a different gmail account. That is why you got a new account name. Once linked to this new account, SSO will access it every time - even if you change the email address on your SSO account. To fix it:
The SSO preferred email address must match the one used to register the original account.
The new account must be disassociated with your SSO account.
After this is done, your next login will re-link you with the old account.

I can send an email to the old registration address - if it would make it easier to find. If that account is no longer accessible, there are other options.
