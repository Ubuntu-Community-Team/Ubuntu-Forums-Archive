### Post by coffeecat on 2013-08-25
If your Ubuntu One account is saying your tiscali address is already associated with it, then that's fine. You can have several emails associated with your Ubuntu One account. However, in order for Ubuntu One to match with your old mune account, you must set your **preferred** email address in Ubuntu One to match the mune account, that is to be the tiscali one. Somewhere in manage email addresses in your Ubuntu One account will be an option to set the preferred email address.

The pre-login notice, and the linked sticky, both state this - the **preferred** email address in Ubuntu One must match.
