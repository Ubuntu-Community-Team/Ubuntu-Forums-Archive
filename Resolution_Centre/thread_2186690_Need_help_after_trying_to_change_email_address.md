### Post by coffeecat on 2013-11-08
It's standard practice with forum software and the like for the user to have to re-activate their account from the verification email. You've been put into a restricted permissions usergroup until you re-activate your account.

Go to your inbox for the email account that you have just changed to in the forum and click on the link in the verification email. That should be all that is needed. Post again if you have any problems.

Changing the email in the forum doesn't affect SSO login, your Ubuntu One account, or the link from your Ubuntu One account to this forum account.
