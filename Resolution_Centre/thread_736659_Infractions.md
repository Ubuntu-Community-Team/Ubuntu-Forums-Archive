### Post by LaRoza on 2008-03-26
Since this isn't really fitting in the Resolution Centre, I will answer.

10 active infraction points means your posts and threads must be approved before showing up

15 means you can't login (banned, while they are active)

Most infractions expire in 10 days, so it takes a lot to get put on moderation (10+) or banned.
