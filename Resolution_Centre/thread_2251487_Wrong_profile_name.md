### Post by coffeecat on 2014-11-04
> **nuklear2 said:**
> Nuklear and nuklear2 are the same person that is me.

Not necessarily. The fact that your Ubuntu One account has the name Nuklear doesn't automatically mean that you get Nuklear as a forum name. There is a pre-exisiting forum account - nuklear - which was created in 2006. That is why the system gave you the name nuklear2, so that there wouldn't be a clash. The forum software does not permit more than one occasion of the same name, and capitals and lower-case are considered the same by the forum software. Nuklear is seen as the same name as nuklear.

We cannot give you the name Nuklear or nuklear unless that 2006 account is yours. My guess is that it is probably not. It was registered from an IP in a different continent than your registration IP. 

If you are unhappy with nuklear2, we can rename your account to one of your choice. If you would like this, please provide three alternatives in order of preference and we can see if any are available.
