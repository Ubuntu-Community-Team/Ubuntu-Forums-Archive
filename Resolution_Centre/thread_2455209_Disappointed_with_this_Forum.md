### Post by QIII on 2020-12-15
If your thread goes unanswered for 12 hours or more, feel free to bump it by just adding a new post with the word "bump".  If no new posts are added to  your thread, it can quickly sink to the bottom of the pile where it will not be noticed.  Don't expect that a 4 day old thread get more notice than one 15 minutes old.  The opposite is true.

Please be aware that the users here are all volunteers, giving their time when they have it.  Nobody is required to be here at any specific time, neither are they required to see a resolution through to the end.

Further, we do not administer a test to the volunteers on the forum to see if they have any particular expertise in any area before they can post answers.  This is a peer support forum, not paid support from Canonical.

Summary:  Bump your threads.  We are volunteers.  Nobody here is an expert in everything.
