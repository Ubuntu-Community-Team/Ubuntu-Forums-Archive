### Post by matthew on 2009-08-21
Occasional frivolity has been present from the beginning of the forums--coffee bean, silly user titles, April Fool's Day weirdness, and occasional theme weeks. It can't all be serious. That's why we have a Cafe for casual talk and a games section.

The frivolous stuff is not our raison d'être, but it really doesn't detract from our purpose and does serve to make the long and often thankless work done by volunteers to make this place what it is a bit more enjoyable.

The conky thread is incredibly easy to find. It doesn't need a sticky. 

Sticking a thread is done to make things that are likely to be easily missed but which the staff desire to draw to people's attention more visible.
