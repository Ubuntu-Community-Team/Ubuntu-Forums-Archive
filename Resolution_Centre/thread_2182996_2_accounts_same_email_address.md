### Post by coffeecat on 2013-10-23
Yes, it's googlemail, not gmail, and it does make a difference. It doesn't seem to matter to Google but the SSO login links to a forum account on a strict string match so it sees [email]username@gmail.com[/email] and [email]username@googlemail.com[/email] as separate addresses. 

I've disassociated this forum account from Ubuntu One. Set your **preferred** email in Ubuntu One to be the googlemail one before you log in next. If you do that and then log in, the system will associate your Ubuntu One account with the Bryan55 account, and you will log into that one. If you log into the forum again before you change the Ubuntu One email, you'll re-associate your Ubuntu one account with this forum account, so here's to hoping you read this before logging in again!
