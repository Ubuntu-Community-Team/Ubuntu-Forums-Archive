### Post by Kernel Sanders on 2008-10-20
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=867285](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=867285)

As I said in the title, is that thread really appropriate for here? It seems almost spammy, and it's getting mildly irritating to ALWAYS see it at the top of the "Other OS Talk" forum due to pointless spammy like BUMPS from the people involved.

It started off as a teenager announcing his own Linux Distro. Since then the thread has gone downhill into nonsense, arguments, and the "project" (and I use the term loosely) is want of a better word, a complete farce. It's undergone several different name changes, website changes, and it's changed the distro it's based on equally as many times. Now they maybe seem to have settled on a name, a website and more importantly _their own forums._

I was hoping that would mean that discussion between those involved would now take place there, but it isn't. That thread is still being used by the people involved to both advertise their "project" here with enough bumps a day to keep the thread at the top of the forum, AND to discuss their project?

Surely at the very least it can be buried in the arch forum (as that's the distro they've settled on, for now..) or some kind of programming forum?

It just seems so spammy? I mean, what would you think if I used these forums to announce the creation of my own distro, and then used these forums as the place to both advertise and discuss development with the people involved so that the thread would always have prominance, despite having my own website and forum for the project? To make things worse, what if the project was a directionless farce, where there was daily name changes along with the distro it was based on?

I'm pretty sure that nonsense wouldn't last long, and that's exactly what's happening.

We've heard time and time again that these forums are simply a support forum for ubuntu. Given that, it's certainly not a place for people to use to base the home of their projects/badly organised new distros from. In fact, i'd argue that allowing that thread to continue sets a bad precident. Otherwise, if people do want to use these forums as a place to both advertise and develop their own distro's or programs, what's to stop them while that thread has been allowed to continue unabated?

One of the number one complaints (that was upheld) about Rav Tux was the irritation caused by a few of his more nonsensical threads (threads that almost everyone had absolutely NO interest in) were constantly having prominence in the forums due to unwarranted bumps. How is this any different?

If people want to make their own distro's or write their own programs then great! I even have no objection to reading a thread about it (although it may fall foul of the spam section of the CoC?). But for them to use this place as a home for it, where their thread is constantly at the top of busy forums despite the fact almost no-one is interested in it, well, it's just irritating, and not what i come here for, and not what the admins have been keen to promote - that this is a Ubuntu support forum, nothing more.

They have their own website, their own forums, and it has absolutely NOTHING to do with ubuntu. So why can't they move discussion there? :confused:

Apologies for the long post! :KS
