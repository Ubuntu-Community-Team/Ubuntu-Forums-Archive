### Post by coffeecat on 2013-08-17
You didn't set your Ubuntu One email to match the email address registered to the zeljkojagust forum account as advised in the pre-login notice. Therefore the system created a new forum account with a username derived from your Ubuntu one name.

I've disassociated the zeljko-jagust account from Ubuntu One. Log out from the forum, set your preferred email in Ubuntu One to the one registered with the zeljkojagust account, and log in again and you will be logged into the zeljkojagust account with that one associated with the Ubuntu One account.

Both the emails are gmail, but you used the wrong one in Ubuntu One for the zeljkojagust account.
