### Post by col48 on 2014-10-17
When I first registered with ubuntuforums, I used my then current main email address.  This (rather long) email address is now obsolete.

I have changed my email address for the forum satisfactorily, but would like to change my SSO login to match.
I want to keep my password, nickname and email address.

Can you make the change for me, please?

---

### Post by Elfy on 2014-10-17
you should be able to change forum e-mail yourself here.[http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editpassword](http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editpassword) ignore the password fields as there is NO forum password.

Anything to do with SSO accounts is not our province - thankfully. To deal with that go to [https://login.ubuntu.com/](https://login.ubuntu.com/)

### Post by col48 on 2014-10-17
Thanks, Elfy.

Forum email was not a problem.
Answer was pointed to through the [https://login.ubuntu.com/](https://login.ubuntu.com/) link you helpfully gave.

Just to document it for the sake of anyone else who wants to do the same....

I made sure I was not logged in to ubuntuforums (just in case it could confuse the issue) and the used my old email address to log in at that link.
I was then able to add my new email address.
This was then sent a verification code which I received within 30 seconds.
Entering this on the screen confirmed I owned the address.
I was then able to change my preferred address to the new one.
Now I have signed on using the new email address.

This is what I wanted.

[Any emails sent to my old email address will have been returned as undeliverable - I guess the server is no longer active]
