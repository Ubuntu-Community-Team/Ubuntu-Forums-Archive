### Post by coffeecat on 2016-03-26
General Help is for technical questions about your Ubuntu system. *Thread moved to **Resolution Centre**.*

The reason for your username being the same as the local part of your email address is explained in [this sticky]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004"), linked from the "Please read before SSO login" link found at the top of every page. Presumably, you unticked your name in the Ubuntu One Personal Data Request page when you first logged in. 

If you provide three alternative usernames in order of preference, one of us will be able to make the change.
