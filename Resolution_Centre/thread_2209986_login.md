**ihatetryingtopickaloginna** registered with an account @gmail.com. The simplest way to regain that account is to set the SSO preferred email address to that registration address. When that is done, this account can be disassociated, and your next login will re-link you with the old account. If the email address is no longer accessible, there are other options.
