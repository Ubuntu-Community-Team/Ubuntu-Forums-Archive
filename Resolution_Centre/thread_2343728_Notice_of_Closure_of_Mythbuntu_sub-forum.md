### Post by coffeecat on 2016-11-18
Threads merged.

As I hope you've noticed, we are not removing support for Mythbuntu from the forum. Far from it. We're merely merging support for Mythbuntu (and MythTV) into the Multimedia Software sub-forum.

This is simply a continuation of the process we've followed over the last few years of trying to make the front page less busy and less confusing to newcomers. Part of this process has been to close over-specialised sub-fora and move their support into more generalised sections. Mythbuntu is not the first; it won't be the last. We really do need a simpler front page than we have even at the moment. Having a separate sub-forum for each Ubuntu flavour is not a good way of organising the forum. Since the focus for MythTV will inevitably and gradually change from Mythbuntu itself to MythTV installed in an Ubuntu flavour by using the PPA, it makes sense to rationalise where we have support now.
