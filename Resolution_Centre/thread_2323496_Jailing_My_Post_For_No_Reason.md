### Post by bapoumba on 2016-05-06
This is just going to be a side comment, not all admins have chimed in yet.

We are help forums, and you love helping users. We are most thankful about that. You also state you were not quite comfortable with VMs themselves. Users seeking help here are waiting for specifics that will help them out. Of course, VB website is full of treasures, but they wont be of much help to someone who may not be able to dig out the relevant info. The web would be filled with tutorials and we would not have much purpose if they could :)

So all in all, helping on topics you are comfortable with and can actually walk someone out of being stuck, writing the solution so that it becomes available to anyone, is a better approach.

In addition, answering a post takes it out of the unanswered posts searches, many users use these searches when they browse the forums to give support.

Specific answers and follow ups are one of our foundations, why we are still here 10 years after.
