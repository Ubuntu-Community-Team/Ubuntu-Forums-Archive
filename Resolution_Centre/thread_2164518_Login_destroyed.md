### Post by coffeecat on 2013-08-01
I've disassociated the philip-alban account from Ubuntu One. What you need to do now, is to reset your Ubuntu One preferred email to match the email in your PhilAlban account, log out and in again, upon which you should be taken to the PhilAlban account and the PhilAlban account will be linked to your Ubuntu One account.

One thing to beware of - there is a period in your PhilAlban email address before the @domain part. That particular email provider has a cavalier attitude not shared by the rest of the email world about email addresses. It considers abcd.efgh@ to be the same as abcdefgh@ or a.b.c.d.e.f.g.h@ and so on. The email matching between Ubuntu One and the forum is done by a simple string match. You need to be sure that your Ubuntu One email has the period in the right place.

If you have any problems, get back to me by pm from whichever account you manage to log on with and we can take it from there.
