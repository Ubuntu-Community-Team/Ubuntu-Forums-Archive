### Post by coffeecat on 2023-04-14
I've moved this thread from Forum Feedback & Help to the Resolution Centre, which is the correct place for queries such as this. Nevertheless, I am glad this has been resolved for you.

For the record, the system did not create a forum account with a username consisting of the first part of your email address. You did that yourself way back in February 2009 when you created this forum account long before the introduction of SSO login. You created an Ubuntu One account a couple of days ago, but when it interrogated the forums database it found a match on email for your account and linked the Ubuntu One account with the pre-existing and pre-named forum account. When the algorithm finds a pre-existing account it always preserves the original forum username. You can read more in the "Please read before SSO login" link on every forum page - [https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004). That's all moot now, but I put it up again as it is often missed.

Let us know if you have any more problems with your account and we will do what we can. Welcome back to the forum after such a long time!
