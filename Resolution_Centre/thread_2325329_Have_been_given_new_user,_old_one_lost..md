### Post by coffeecat on 2016-05-24
The problem is that you have two Ubuntu One accounts, as howefield said, one linked to the Bucky Ball account, the other to the buckyball2 account. When you first logged in with the Ubuntu One account that is now linked to buckyball2 on May 21st, the system created a new forum account, since it could not find a match for the email you used in that Ubuntu One account. If you are landing up in buckyball2 you are using the wrong Ubuntu One account. Your username on both Ubuntu One accounts appears to be the same - the emails will be different.

Check your records for login credentials for your two Ubuntu One accounts. Login with the correct Ubuntu One account and you will log into Bucky Ball.
