> **nknwn said:**
> Then you insisted that I use the email address (before the @ symbol) as the display name. I largely stopped contributing due to my annoyance at having to display my email address name.

We didn't insist on the local part of your email being used as the forum username. Perhaps you didn't read this:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2164051](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2164051)

> When you first login to Ubuntuforums with your Ubuntu One account, a forum account will be created for you with a username based on the Ubuntu One username (where your Ubuntu One account is an older one with a username field), or if this is a newer account with no username field, your Ubuntu One Full Name. If you untick the Full name box so as not to share it, the system will use all or part of the local part of your email address (the part before the @) to generate your forum username.

If you do want help getting into your old account, we can help you but you need to do two things. First read this:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2164369](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2164369)

In your case we need details of both your previous accounts.

Second - please moderate your assumptions. Had you used the correct email in the first place you would have logged into the original account. Had you tempered your annoyance with the second account and asked for assistance we would have been pleased to help.
