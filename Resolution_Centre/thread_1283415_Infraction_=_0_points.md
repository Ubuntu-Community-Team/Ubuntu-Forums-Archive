### Post by bapoumba on 2009-10-05
0-9 points means your account has no restrictions.
10-14 means moderation queue, ie your posts have to be approved before they appear on the forums
15 and up is a ban.

Any combination of any infraction add up at all times.

Only you and Staff can view your own infractions. They stay around, even after being expired, for history. It is better to have only zero infractions vs 5 pages.. All cases are treated individually.
