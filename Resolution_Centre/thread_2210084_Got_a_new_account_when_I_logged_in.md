### Post by Iowan on 2014-03-08
Moved to Resolution Centre.
SloggerKhan registered with an email account  @lavabit.com The easiest way to regain that account is to change your SSO preferred email address to the lavabit address. After the email is changed, this account can be disassociated from your SSO account, and your next login should reconnect you with SloggerKhan.  If the lavabit address is no longer accessible, there are some other options.
