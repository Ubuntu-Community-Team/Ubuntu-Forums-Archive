### Post by LaRoza on 2008-08-28
There are several reasons why people do that:

[list]
[*] Trying to be helpful (ie, finding an alternative that works is often easier than trying to fix something. What would you say to someone trying to install Flash with automatix or whatever?)
[*] Trying to show a "better" app, which may be an opinion, but can often be true (especially if the one being asked about is old or obsolete)
[*] Fanboyism. This ranges from joking to trolling. Trolling is against the CoC and can be infracted.
[/list]

We can't have a blanket rule like you worded (I'm guilty of it, often as a joke for Fx or E***s users, because Opera and Vim are superior), but you can report off topic or flamebaiting posts.
