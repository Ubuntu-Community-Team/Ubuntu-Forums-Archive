### Post by matthew on 2008-07-30
We don't have a specific policy, but resurrecting old posts is something we discourage in general. It isn't prohibited, but when it is distracting or harmful, we have been known to do something.

If you find something that needs staff intervention, please feel free to use the report post function and we will take a look. :)
