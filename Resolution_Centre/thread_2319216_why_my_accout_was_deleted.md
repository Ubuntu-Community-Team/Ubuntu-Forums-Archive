### Post by coffeecat on 2016-04-02
The Skaperen account has not been deleted. The problem is that somehow you have managed to create two Ubuntu One accounts. One is linked to the Skaperen account and you last logged into the Skaperen account 4 days ago. The other is linked to this account. When you logged into the forum via this second Ubuntu One account, the system couldn't find a match for the unique ID of the Ubuntu One account or its email, and created a new forum account. 

I suggest you check your records of your login credentials for Ubuntu One. The two Ubuntu One accounts have the same username but will have different emails. The emails registered to the Phil_Howard and Skaperen accounts are both gmails, but are different.
