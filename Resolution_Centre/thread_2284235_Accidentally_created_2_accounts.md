### Post by coffeecat on 2015-06-28
No problem and all done!

I've disabled the eldavar2 account in line with our [Forum Data Attribution, Retention and Privacy Policy](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2231107). And I've released the gmail from that account so that you will be able to set it in this eldavar account.

Just two points. In the change email & password page of your settings you'll find password fields. Ignore them. The vBulletin passwords are no longer used, but the fields have been retained for a special purpose which you don't need. Also - don't forget to re-activate your account from the verification email after you change the email address. We occasionally get distressed messages from people who miss this and can't work out why they are no longer able to post!
