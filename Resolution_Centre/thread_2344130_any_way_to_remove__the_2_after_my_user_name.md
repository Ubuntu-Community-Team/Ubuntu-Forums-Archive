### Post by QIII on 2016-11-22
No.  The two accounts cannot be merged.  It must be one or the other.  The Forum software will not allow the merging of two accounts.

If you want your old account back, please first read [this]("https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2230004") sticky and see if you can re-associate by selecting the email you used to create that previous account as the preferred SSO email and logging in to the Forums.

Don't worry.  After you re-associate here you can reset your SSO preferred email.

If that does not work, please PM me with the email address used in the old account.  DO NOT post it here.  This is a way for me to ensure you are the owner of the previous account.  We will move on from there to get your old account back.
