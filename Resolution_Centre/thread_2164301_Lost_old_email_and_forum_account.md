### Post by mTtDAbc on 2013-07-31
Solved.

A possible solution for anyone else experiencing problems getting into their original account.

Try to create a new account using the email address from your original account
This should generate an '**Ubuntu One: Warning**' email with a link to reset your password.
Reset password
Login via the SSO link at the top of the ubuntuforums (choose exisiting account), you original alias should now show up. My orioginal account (nico-dk) was recovered this way, unfortunately it doesnøt have the privileges to reply to this thread.
