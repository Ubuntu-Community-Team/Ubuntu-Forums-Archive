Hello,

My name is Neofytos Kolokotronis and am doing a research project as part of a Psychology Degree [investigating the relationship between Personality Types and the Sense of Community in FOSS projects]("https://openss.qualtrics.com/SE/?SID=SV_br0Nzb7UmfYU1EN"). To measure these parameters I implement two indexes in the survey that are freely available; [BFI]("https://www.ocf.berkeley.edu/~johnlab/bfi.htm") and [SCI]("http://www.communityscience.com/pdfs/Sense%20of%20Community%20Index-2%28SCI-2%29.pdf").

Am obviously not active in these forums, but am a long time Ubuntu user (I currently maintain 2 Ubuntu and 1 Lubuntu installations) and FOSS contributor interested in Community Management.

The survey mostly consists of short Multiple Choice questions, takes 5-6 minutes to complete and is configured to be completely anonymous. It is scheduled to run until April 7, 2017, even though the deadline might be extended if the minimum amount of participants is not reached. I will of course be grateful of any time period the admins decide to make it available on the Ubuntu forums if my request is accepted.

I would be happy to provide any further information if needed.
