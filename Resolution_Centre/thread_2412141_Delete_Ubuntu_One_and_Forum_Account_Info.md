### Post by wildmanne39 on 2019-02-10
Firstly we do not administer Ubuntu One, I recommend that in the Ubuntu One account you plan to keep that you set your preferred email address to the email you currently have set in your original forum account, then log out of the forum and back in and you should be in your original forum account if for some reason you are not pm me the email address of the original account and I will take a look that is if my assumption is correct and you have another forum account and please post a link to it, if I am incorrect please clarify your forum account issue so we may better understand how to help you.

> Will deleting my UO account also remove the corresponding information from the forums? If not what should I do to remove my user name and email from the forums so I can restore the user name under my old account?

No it will not:
> We do not delete accounts. However, we are prepared to disable accounts on request, which involves removing personal information from profile fields, with the exception of all email and IP addresses associated with the account. We retain records of email and IP addresses, but this is information which can only be viewed by forum staff (admins and moderators) and is considered to be confidential.

In addition to disabling an account, we are also happy to rename the account so that the username appears as a random string, or any acceptable string provided by the departing member.

None of the information from this new account should stop us from being able to get you back into your original account.

I am very busy with life outside of the forum right now so if you do have an issue getting into your original account after changing your preferred email at Ubuntu One to the one you are currently using for your original forum account or you are not sure what the email is it may take me a little longer to respond then normal but I will get back to you.

Once you are back in to your original account you will not be able to post in this thread so please pm me and let me know then I will disable this account and randomize your username and remove the personal information from your profile according to our policy as quoted above.
