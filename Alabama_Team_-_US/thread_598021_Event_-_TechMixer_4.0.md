Just wanted to let everyone know about the upcoming TechMixer.
It will be Tuesday Nov. 6 at the [Innovation Depot]("http://www.innovationdepot.net/") in Birmingham Alabama.
[Click to get directions from where you are]("http://maps.google.com/maps?hl=en&ie=UTF-8&dq=innovation+depot+loc:+Birmingham,+AL&daddr=1500+1st+Ave+N,+Birmingham,+AL+35203&geocode=12735639352066199681,33.511111,-86.812631&ll=33.511111,-86.812631&iwstate1=dir:to&iwloc=A&f=d")

 This is more a a Social meeting where Birmingham Techies can meet and greet. We will not be setting a booth up for this event. We will just mingle with area techies/IT/Geeks and spread the word.

 We will set up a booth at the Tech Mixer in February.  So we plan to start all planning and preparations with in the next coupe of weeks. We really want to set up a professional looking booth and could use everyone's help.
 For more info on the Tech Mixers go to [Tech Birmingham ]("http://www.techbirmingham.com/default.aspx?id=171")and check it out.
