### Post by crane on 2007-05-23
Well, for those interested the Alabama LoCo Teams Mailing list is open for business!
Please swing by[ https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-al]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-al") to join.
 Not sure how many of you use mailing lists now but they are a great way to stay in contact. There is an option on the sign up page to receive a batch email. This means you will receive one email that lists everything posted that day.
 I also set the list up to tag the subject line. So any email from the list should have a [LoCo-us-al] appended to the front of the subject to let you know it is from the group.

Just thought I let everyone know. 
I will update the Wiki sometime today. If someone else gets to it before me let me know.
