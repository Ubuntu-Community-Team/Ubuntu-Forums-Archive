### Post by Jared Norris on 2011-03-08
Welcome to the Ubuntu-AU Team Forum.

We are the LoCo Team for Ubuntu in Australia. The other resources available from the team are -

Main Website - [http://www.ubuntu.com.au](http://www.ubuntu.com.au)
Team Wiki - [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AustralianTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AustralianTeam)

Mailing List - [https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-au](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-au)

Launchpad - [https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-au](https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-au)

IRC - #ubuntu-au on irc.freenode.net
Don't know about IRC? Use the web client to connect - [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AustralianTeam/IRC](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AustralianTeam/IRC)
or following this guide - [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XChatHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XChatHowto)

Please note not everyone is around 100% of the time in the IRC chatroom, please hang around, if you're in the channel your question will be answered eventually :)
