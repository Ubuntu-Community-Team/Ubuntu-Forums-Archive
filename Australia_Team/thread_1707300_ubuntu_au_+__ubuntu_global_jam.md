### Post by ikt on 2011-03-15
Hi all!

Ubuntu is throwing on a global jam, there are many different types:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuGlobalJam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuGlobalJam)

> #Bugs - finding, triaging and fixing bugs.
#Testing - testing the new release and reporting your feedback.
#Upgrade - upgrading to Maverick from Lucid and reporting your upgrade experience.
#Documentation - writing documentation about how to use Ubuntu and how to join the community.
#Translations - translating Ubuntu and helping to make it available in everyone's local language.
#Packaging - work on Ubuntu packages and improve them.
#Other - other types of contribution such as marketing and advocacy etc. You can also help fix papercuts. 

And we'll be doing a few, we just need more people!

More info here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1693892](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1693892)

Looking for some feedback, if you can suggest a date in the near future you are free to help, or if you can come or if you can even run one, we're interested :)
