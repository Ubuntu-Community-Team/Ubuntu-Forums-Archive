### Post by Jared Norris on 2011-09-26
Well it's official, we're now an official LoCo again!

[https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-au/2011-September/007450.html]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-au/2011-September/007450.html")

Thanks to everyone for their hard work over the last 12 months or more, lets keep this momentum rolling and take it further.

Lets get some more release parties happening and make sure we get as many people along to those that are already planned as possible. Events, as always, are posted on [http://loco.ubuntu.com/teams/ubuntu-au]("http://loco.ubuntu.com/teams/ubuntu-au") so sign up to your local one. If you can't see one you can make, feel free to start your own and email the details to the team mailing list - [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AustralianTeam/MailingLists]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AustralianTeam/MailingLists")
