### Post by Crossroads on 2008-05-17
Well, I'm an absolute [Ubuntu] beginner and have found a lot of useful info  in these forums by wandering about and searching.

... But, I have to say that the Absolute Beginner Talk forum flies by much too fast. The sheer number of new postings causes threads to move down the list very very quickly. So if they are not answered within 30 minutes or so they disappear off the first page.

To me it would seem sensible if the ABT forum were split up into several categories. As each sub-forum would be more focussed it could prove even more useful to people and would be easier to respond to. Each sub-forum would then move more slowly meaning that there would be more chance of people replying to threads.

[COLOR="RoyalBlue"]*Disclaimer: I understand that the forum has been reorganised recently and do not know what went on before.*[/COLOR]
 
My initial suggestions for Absolute Beginner Categories are :
[LIST]
[*]Installation and Upgrades
[*]System Configuration and Settings 
[*]System Administration
[*]System Security
[*]Hardware support
[*]Networking and Communications
[*]Ubuntu Software
[*]Software Applications
[/LIST]

Feel free to suggest other categories.
Does this make sense?
