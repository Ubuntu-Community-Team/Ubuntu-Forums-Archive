If the requester doesn't define  which version their using, often it requires a request from the  assisting member or at the least a search of the requesters previous posts. It may actually be a good idea to separate out the sections by version.  There's already a section for Ubuntu Studio and the Moblin Remix. This might eliminate any need for  sectional changes and help assisting members provide support without  asking for basic information. For example, searching for support requests that fit an assisting  members capabilities.
