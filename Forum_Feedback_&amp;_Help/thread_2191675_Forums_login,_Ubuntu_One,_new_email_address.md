### Post by coffeecat on 2013-12-04
@squakie,

**Ubuntu One:** login.ubuntu.com -> My Account -> Manage Email addresses.

**Forum:** I'm fairly sure the bug preventing you from changing your email has been fixed now. Settings -> Edit Email and Password. Give it a try and let us know if you have any problem. Ignore the password fields. They're only needed for forum staff. The only password you need is for your Ubuntu One account.

As Iowan said, once your Ubuntu One and forum accounts are linked, you can change the emails so that they do not match. From the sticky:

> When you login the first time using Ubuntu One, your forum account is associated with your Ubuntu One account. Once the association has been created between your Ubuntu One account and your forum account, you can set different emails in the two accounts – this will not break the association. The association between the Ubuntu One account and the forum account can only be removed by a forum admin.
