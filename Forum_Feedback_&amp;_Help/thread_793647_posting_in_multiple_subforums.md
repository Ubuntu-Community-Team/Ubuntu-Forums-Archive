### Post by kko1 on 2008-05-14
The policy is: Don't do it.

Find what you think is the best place to post, and post there. If you later think your thread would after all belong to somewhere else, you can report your first post and request the moderators to move the thread. And if a moderator thinks your thread is misplaced, they may also move it on their own.
