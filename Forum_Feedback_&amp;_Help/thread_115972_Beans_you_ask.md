### Post by ubuntu-geek on 2006-01-11
Happy New Year Everyone!

We've been noticing that we could use some freshening up on the forums, and since we've also read some questions asking us for clarification as to post counts - we thought we'd unveil what we've been cooking up!

Go on, have some fun with us!

First, we've added and changed the user ranks and rank icons (gotta love those coffee beans!)

As some of you know, we had implemented a post count change a while back - since we are a technical support forum, we do not count posts in the Community Chat or Site Discussion areas toward your post counts.  In order to more properly reflect this change, we've changed "Posts: " under your name to be "Beans: "   What are Beans, you ask?  Simple!  Beans are the posts you make in the technical support areas of this forum.  To sweeten this up a little, we've added something new as well - if you are one of the top five most active volunteers on this forum in a month, we'll give you a special tag, title, to let you know how much we appreciate your help!  Each month, the five "Beanies of the Month" will change.

Stay tuned for more exciting forum fun coming your way in this new year!
