Google and other search engines get really cool if ya know the 'operators' to use when searching. For instance, let's use **google** to search for all posts here with 'amitabhishek' attached to them.
open a google search field and use the **site:** operator:
```
**site:**ubuntuforums.org  "amitabhishek"
```this will produce the results and following link:
http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&q=site%3Aubuntuforums.org+"amitabhishek"&aq=f&oq=&aqi=

from there, you could sort by 'recent results' etc.
