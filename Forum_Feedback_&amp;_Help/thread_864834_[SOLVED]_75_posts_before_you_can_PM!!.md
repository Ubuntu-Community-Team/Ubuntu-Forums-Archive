### Post by p_quarles on 2008-07-20
> **projecthikari said:**
> Maybe he's speaking of visitor messages, but either way, that still annoys me as well.
Visitor messages were restricted for very good reasons:
* They became a spam target the instant the feature was introduced
* They are publicly viewable and indexed by Google et al.
* The majority of user accounts are not active

Thus, it would have become extremely easy to hide massive amounts of spam.
