Maybe it's just me or a result of the growing community but there seem to be a lot GRUB problems lately as well as others that are both very frustrating and somewhat common. I know there is a lot of good info in the wiki but I'm sure a lot of people either don't know about it or forget.

It seems like it would be useful to have a section of the forum dedicated to some of the most common problems and their fixes. For example, a troubleshooting section with a list of common problems and their solutions. Of course it could just be a link to the wiki but I was thinking of something a bit more user-friendly and part of the forum were users would be likely to find it.

I'm sure there are many ways to do this as well as many places to put it (in the beginner forum is one but I think in its own section would be good). It probably shouldn't be a place to post questions as we already have plenty of those. I guess kind of like a mini-wiki with the top 10 most common problems or something but very clean and simple.

Anyway, just an idea. I'm sure others can come up with some good ideas on how to do this if it seems worth pursuing.
