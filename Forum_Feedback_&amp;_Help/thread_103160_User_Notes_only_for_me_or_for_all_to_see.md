### Post by ex` on 2005-12-23
User notes are usually a way of noting down any particular info for any given user, be it forum-related or else.

For example; if I would start a spam topic in the Backyard and it'd be closed, a mod could make a note about this, which they could look back later on if they'd review me for a staff position.

Tons of reasons really, mostly to note down any info you want to share between you and your fellow crew members about a particular user.

That's how it's used in most cases. Not sure if there's a different purpose for it here, though.
