Kate, I would suggest you stick with it, I've had the pleasure of working with several devs to solve problems I've run into. If I was you, I would follow through with keybuk and work on the problem to see if the two of you can come up with a solution.

The big problem is that during the alpha cycle, it is usually a small number of people that are doing active testing and everyone seems to get on quite well, but once the first beta is released it seems that everybody and their dog seems to pop up feeling that their problem is more important than anyone elses.

An unfortunate part of being a member of any forum, is the negative comments. There isn't much you can do about it, but grow a thick skin, I wish it wasn't so, but that's the way it is.
