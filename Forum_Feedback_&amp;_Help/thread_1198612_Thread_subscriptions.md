### Post by RD1 on 2009-06-27
It's optional.

Go into "User Control Panel" (User CP at top left of this page)

On left hand side, click on "Settings & Options" > "Edit Options"

Under "Messaging & Notification" Select your "Default Thread Subscription Mode"
