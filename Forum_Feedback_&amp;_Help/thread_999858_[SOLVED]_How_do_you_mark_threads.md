### Post by drs305 on 2008-12-02
Solved: Thread Tools link near top right of first post (outside the post). The original poster can mark the thread solved. Note you can also remove a 'Solved' tag from here.

Thank you: Yellow and red (medal?) icon at bottom right of any post (inside the post).

Marking a thread solved helps others find solutions which work and lets 'helpers' concentrate on posts with unresolved issues. Giving thanks is just the ubuntu way.

Welcome to the ubuntu forums.
