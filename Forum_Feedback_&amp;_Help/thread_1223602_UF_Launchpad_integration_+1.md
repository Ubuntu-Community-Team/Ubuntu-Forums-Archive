### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2009-07-26
Well, here's a feature request.
Right now, under User CP, we have "Click here  to update your Launchpad OpenID", where only the "Click here" part is a link. This is a bit silly, and I found it difficult to use since the forums disable text decorations for links. That entire line of text could be a link, which would enhance usability and not damage anything. If I recall correctly, it makes more sense for accessibility as well, since presently the link would just be referred to as "click here."
