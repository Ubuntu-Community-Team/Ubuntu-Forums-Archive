### Post by QIII on 2022-04-01
This is a volunteer peer-to-peer end-user support forum.  It is not a bug reporting venue.  Nobody here, not even the moderation staff and Admins, works for Canonical Ltd, which distributes Ubuntu and administers Ubuntu One, the single sign on for all web venues owned by Canonical.  The Ubuntu Forums are one such venue.  You will have to contact Canonical, Ltd, with your request to allow another sign in method.  I highly doubt they will oblige.

We do not administer Ubuntu One.  You may contact Canonical directly [here]("https://ubuntu.com/contact-us").  You can read up about Ubuntu One SSO [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ISD").

We cannot receive bug reports and affect changes to code.  Therefore, any change to the sign on process for the UF would have entirely no effect on bug reporting.
