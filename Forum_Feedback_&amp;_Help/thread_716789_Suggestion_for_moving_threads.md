### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-03-06
I have seen a lot of threads that have been moved lately for no apparent reason, especially from the Cafe to the Backyard.  One example is the [thread that asks who our favorite singer is.](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=714004)  In that thread, there was really no fighting or controversy.  I didn't see anything really inappropriate.  But it was moved to the Backyard.

My suggestion is that when a thread is moved, the moderator that moves it should have to make a post within the thread explaining why they moved the thread.  It shouldn't be enough for them to just post that it is moved, they should have to explain why.  I have seen a lot of other forums that do that.  It would be helpful to teach people the correct subforums to post under.

Edit:
I now notice that in the original post the words "love and sex" are in bold print.  I guess that is a good reason.  But I stand behind my suggestion that it would help us to learn how to post better.
