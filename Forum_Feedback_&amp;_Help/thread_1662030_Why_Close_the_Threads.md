### Post by lisati on 2011-01-07
One of the problems we face in a technical support forum is that things change. Solutions to problems that work for an older version of a program or OS don't necessarily work for a newer version. This can happen even with a good solution that is well thought out and still contains some useful information.

You might want to read Matthew's explanation of the basic philosophy of moderation among the staff at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=244237](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=244237)
