### Post by jenkinbr on 2009-06-05
Actually, when I reported this yesterday, it was a response to another thread. It was removed to here.  I think what the OP is after is how to start a new thread.

To create a new post, you should browse to the forum you want to post in (example: [Absolute Beginners Talk]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=326")) and click the 'New Thread' Button (it's in a similar location to the 'New Reply' Button in this thread) Give your thread a title, and a body (please make both descriptive, a title should be a breif statment of your issue, while the post should do a reasonable job of describing your problem). When you are done, click submit.
