### Post by LaRoza on 2008-10-17
To contest the infraction, please use the Resolution Centre.

For the question, legal torrents are legal, of course. However, just because something doesn't cost anything doesn't mean it is free. Trial software is usually just as restrictive as the full version, and obtaining it from anywhere other than the official source is not allowed usually.
