### Post by PriceChild on 2007-08-08
Click the multi quote button on all the posts in the thread that you would like to quote. You can even go to other pages of the thread and toggle the multi-quote button on those. - It will remember the ones you chose on previous pages.

When you've chosen all posts you want to quote, then press a normal quote button on a post you want to quote. You'll be taken to a page showing the advanced reply dialogue, and inside will be all the posts you wanted to multi-quote all ready for you :)
