### Post by Peturrr on 2006-05-29
Dapper Drake will be released soon, something I and we as a community are excited about. 

For me and lots of other users this will mean that we can abandon our dapper drake installation and start playing with and thinking about Edgy Eft. The Edgy Eft Development Forum is our home for the next few months.

I would really love to see that the Edgy Eft Development Forum will have subfora. 

Considering the amount of new technologies that we will try to incorporate in Edgy, I am expecting a lot of forum posts about a very wide spectrum of technologies. Hardware support, INITNG, XGL, Beagle, etc, etc. It was very hard to keep up with all the posts in the dapper forum. Subfora would make this a lot easier.

It will help us focus, reduce the amount of time searching for interesting things, bring people together with the same interest, It wil be efficient.

Personally I would like to see as much subfora as the new Dapper Forums will have, plus a forum + subfora for every major new technologie like XGl, beagle, init-ng etc.

I am very interested in what the rest of the community thinks about this. So please share your opinion with us.
