### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-09-23
Someone can correct me if I'm wrong - but AskUbuntu is part of the [Stack Exchange ]("http://stackexchange.com/sites")group of sites (consisting of, among other sites, StackOverflow), whereas UbuntuForums is the official Forums site for Canonical's product: Ubuntu.

### Post by madjr on 2011-09-23
ASKUBUNTU: you make a question (with a mandatory descriptive title) and vote on best answers (no ongoing or offtopic/opinion discussions)

UF: ongoing discussions, opinions, flames, trolling, sometimes no descriptive titles, etc...

is one better than the other? not really, i just use which one i feel during the day. Usually you will get good answers in either place.
