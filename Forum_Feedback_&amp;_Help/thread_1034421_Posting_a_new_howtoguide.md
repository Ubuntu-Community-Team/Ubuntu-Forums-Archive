### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2009-01-08
Well, here on the forums, the correct way to post a guide is in the Tutorials & Tips section where a staff member will review and approve it (See [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=677396](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=677396) for more info).  Since you already posted it on your blog, please include a link to that it an obvious place in your forum post and state that it is your own blog (cover your butt!).  If you do post it to T&T section, please give the staff a few days to approve the tutorial before it becomes visible and available to others.

Such guides don't usually end up on wikis, but it is possible to put them there.  The Ubuntu wiki areas usually don't have full HowTos like that, usually information on specific tasks.
