### Post by aysiu on 2007-08-27
Just click on the number next to the post. That will open the post in a new tab/window. Highlight the URL and that's the link to the single post.

For example, the link to the post I'm writing right now is [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=3265681&postcount=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=3265681&postcount=2)
