### Post by deadflowr on 2019-01-29
Go to login.ubuntu.com
Go to personal details.
Go down and click Manage email addresses.
Fill in the new email address in the  Add new email address box. Click Add Address to apply it.
Set new email as preferred email,then confirm password and then click Save Changes down below.
