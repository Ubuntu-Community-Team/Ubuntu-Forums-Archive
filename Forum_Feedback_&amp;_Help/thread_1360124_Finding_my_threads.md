### Post by Iowan on 2009-12-20
> **howefield said:**
> Near the top of the page, click on Search, then at the bottom of the drop down, "Find All Your Posts"Right above that one is "Find All Your Threads" - if you're looking only for those threads you started.

Another (slightly more cumbersome) way is to click on your username at the top of the page  - where it says **Welcome, <username>,** By [COLOR="Red"]Visitor Messages[/COLOR] is a Statistics button that, when clicked, reveals three sections: Total Posts, Visitor Messages, and General Information. The last entry under Total Posts is a link to "Find all threads started by <username>".  

Did I mention that it's slightly more cumbersome? ;)
