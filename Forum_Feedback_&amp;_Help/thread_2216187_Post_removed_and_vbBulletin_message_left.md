### Post by coffeecat on 2014-04-10
@gifford, I've done a bit of investigating. It seems that the thread starter requested that the thread be removed, probably because they found the answer elsewhere.

Don't worry about your posts being in the jail. It's not a particularly good name for what we use as a general purpose area to remove things from public view. There are various reasons for jailing, and inappropriate content is only one of them. In this case there was no inappropriate content, instead at the request of the OP.

You should still be able to see your own posts, or possibly even the whole thread. If you navigate to Forum Feedback & Help, the jail should appear as a sub-forum but only with your posts showing for you. Do you see them? Or you can try this link:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2215981](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2215981)

That might give you a permission denied error though - it certainly will for anyone else except forum staff.
