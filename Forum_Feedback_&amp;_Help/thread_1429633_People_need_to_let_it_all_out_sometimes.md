### Post by Doctor Mike on 2010-03-14
I realize there are other places to vent, but many of the people here know each other and interact with each other here. Their reasons for frustration often originate here at this forum. If I wish to have a conversation with someone from the forums that is a little out of bounds I could invite them to join me, but if I initiate the post I must be willing to be critical of myself first.

I'm not talking about throwing the rules out, just allowing some people to air their issues without being too serious about it. Dealing directly with a source of frustration is an effective way of resolving issues. Cathartic frustration reduction is temporary and often leads to renewed frustration (stress) at a higher level.

The other major point here is that statements that should not be made in the general forums will always happen. Having a place to resolve issues etc. would help keep the dirty laundry private and possibly reduce the workload for admins.

This method may not be the best way to go, but I think it outlines a need here.

edit: Please note: the reason I've suggest a forum not totally private vs direct messaging is that people will be aware that their statements are observed and moderated. A reasonable code of conduct will still be expected, and some insight for users and observers may be gained. Again, I hope people can offer constructive comments on this subject.
