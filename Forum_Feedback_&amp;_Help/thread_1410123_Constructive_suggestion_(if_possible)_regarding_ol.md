### Post by toupeiro on 2010-02-18
I often see old posts that have sit dormant closed for "necromancing".  Personally, I believe that by extending a forum search for people to look at old threads, you're essentially telling every memember of this board that's OK.  I never agreed with closing a thread someone has a renewed interest in simply because its old.  The thread will obviously contain history about the topic, and you're forcing them to start from scratch on the matter.

That being said, I think it would be very beneficial if the UBB software could somehow automatically close the thread after X amount of months of total inactivity, making it read only.  I think you should also clearly state in the CoC or the Forum FAQ about necromancing old threads because there isn't a shred of information regarding the topic in either place.

Personally, I think in either case, its making more work for your staff and making the forums less flexible for its members, but at least this process would lighten your load up a bit and not waste a forum members time who has searched your site for their information, found it, only to have the door shut on them.

Cheers!
