### Post by QIII on 2020-06-23
Beans represent the number of posts in support areas of the forums.  Posts in chat areas are not counted.

The whimsical icons that come with certain numbers of beans are entirely arbitrary and just for your fun and entertainment.

Neither the beans nor the icons should be interpreted in any way as representing a particular user's expertise or "rank" -- except in the case of Forums Staff and banned users, who do get specialized "badges".
