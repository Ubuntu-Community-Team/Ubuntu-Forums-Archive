This is a large forum, so the "rules" may be a little different.

Double Posting (two consecutive posts) is not usually bad. If you are addressing multiple posts in a thread, or responding to something that was posted while you were writing, double posts will be common in discussions. See: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=333477&page=5](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=333477&page=5) (I just happen to know that was a thread with multiple people at the same time, and "double posts" would likely happen)

You can do either, edit or post again. Usually, I leave edits to clarifying posts and on the same page. Making another post is for when something new came up.

You can do either. I usually make two posts, unless the two other members are addressing the same issue.

Just do what is best for communication.
