### Post by bonzodog on 2006-05-08
This is because using a 'z' is an americanised way of doing things. The proper Oxford English spellings all use the 's'.

### Post by kassetra on 2006-05-08
Depending on the person that wrote the document, or that part of the document, the spellings will be correct - but local to that person's version of English.

American English, British English, and International English are all slightly different, and all are correct, however confusing to some people.
