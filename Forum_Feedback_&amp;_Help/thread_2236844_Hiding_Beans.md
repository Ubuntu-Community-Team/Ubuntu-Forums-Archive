### Post by Morbius1 on 2014-07-29
There is a misconception in this forum that the number of posts is directly proportional to one's knowledge. That unfortunately is not true.

So if you are smart and want to be taken seriously here but are new to the forum your inclination is to hide them so the user guesses about your ability.

As your post count begins to increase your inclination is to unhide them so as to make it appear you know what you are talking about when that may not be true.

As good or bad as this system is it could be worse. The Mint forum uses "Levels" ( which has an ever greater connotation ) where the accumulation of a certain number of posts brings you from Level 1 to Level 2 and so on. Many members abuse this and post in every topic that comes up and in a few short weeks they are at Level 7. But alas the Level is as it is here a reflection of the quantity of posts not their quality.

Case in point: My own bean count ;)
