### Post by coffeecat on 2015-04-24
*Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

Only forum staff can delete or close threads. If you would like one of yours closed, please use the "report post" button in your own post to send a message to the staff area. The report button isn't just for problematic posts or spam.

If you mean to mark a thread solved, you can do this for your own thread by going to the thread tools dropdown at the top of the thread.
