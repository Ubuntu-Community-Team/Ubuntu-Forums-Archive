### Post by matthew on 2007-06-05
On the front page of the forums, near the bottom, just above "What's going on?", there is a link called [Show Forum Leaders]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showgroups.php"). When you click it you will go to a page with a list of all staff. Anyone with [IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/images/uf/statusicon/user_online.gif[/IMG] next to their name is online. Send a PM to any [COLOR=Red]staff member whose name is in red[/COLOR] or [COLOR=DarkRed]an admin, in dark red[/COLOR].. :)
