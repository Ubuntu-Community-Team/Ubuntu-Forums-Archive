### Post by QIII on 2017-07-18
What it means is to stop posting fluff just to make posts, start asking or answering legitimate questions in the support areas of the Forums and in due course you will amass enough posts that you will be eligible to make changes to your profile.  One of those changes will be to add an avatar -- and the size limit will be made known to you.

We take a dim view of those who post just to drive up their "bean counts" in order to alter their profiles.  We call it "bean mining" and it is frequently a precursor to profile spam.  If you keep this up, you are liable to incur an infraction.
