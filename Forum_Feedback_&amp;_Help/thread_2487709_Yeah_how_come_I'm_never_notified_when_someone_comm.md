### Post by coffeecat on 2023-06-14
Your settings show that under "Default Thread Subscription Mode" you have it set to "do not subscribe", which is why you are not getting any notifications. To change this click on the "Settings" link near the top right on any forum page, then in the left pane of the new control panel page that opens click on "General Settings" under "My Settings". In the main pane, a little way down,  you'll find "Default Thread Subscription Mode". In the drop down, choose "Through my Control Panel only" if you don't want emails. Don't forget to click on the save button at the bottom of the page! You won't get in-your-face notifications, but the next time you visit the forum, click on the settings button at top right and in your control panel page you'll find a list of your subscribed threads that have been posted to since you last visited them. 

Caveat: auto-subscribing to threads only happens after you change that setting. All your threads prior to the change remain unsubscribed. You can fix that for individual threads by visiting each thread you want to subscribe to and under thread tools at the top, choose Subscribe to this thread. You can subscribe to others' threads too if they interest you. You don't have to post to them.

General point: in common with most other types of forum software, there is a mass of settings in your control panel so that you can configure your account to your own preference. It is worth spending 10 minutes or so exploring all the options. You may find some useful. For instance, do you know that you can change your message editor interface from the default WYSIWYG to a standard more basic editor, which has advantages if you like to include BBCode tags in your post? Or, under the "Edit Profile" heading, you change the Location of Member Profile Information on Posts from the default on top, to on left? I find it tidier and threads easier to read on the left, but your preference may be different.
