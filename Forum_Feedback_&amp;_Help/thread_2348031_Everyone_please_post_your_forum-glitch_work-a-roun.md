### Post by wildmanne39 on 2016-12-30
If you are trying to post and it does not post immediately hit the reload current page button in your browser then hit the submit button again and the post will go through immediately, you will see a message that says it is a duplicate post but as long as you hit the reload current button in your browser as soon as you see the post is not posting the duplicate will not be created.

If you post and the forum is acting up you have to go into the thread to see your post because it will not show on the forum page.

Also you have to manually check if someone has posted to a thread you are working in because while the forum is glitching you will not receive subscribed emails.

I have also been having an issue where I can not upload images to the forum, in preview it shows the image but it will not post them to the forum, to workaround this I click on the insert inline button and it will insert the image where the cursor is at that moment.

Anyone else know of forum workarounds please post here and not in the thread for reporting the issues.
Thanks

### Post by &amp;KyT$0P# on 2016-12-30
If a reply done through Quick Reply doesn't go through in ~ 10 seconds, use Esc key to stop it.  It will usually then just go through fine without further action.

For threads that have filled up a page - if the next post has gone on to the next page, but clicking to view it leaves you on the next-to-last page, click "Reply to thread".  Most of the time, the post will be visible under "Topic Review".
