### Post by coffeecat on 2016-09-22
@Remson_Felipa, once you have 10 beans - that is, 10 posts in support areas of the forum - you will be able to access your settings page and choose automatic thread subscription. When you do have 10 beans, click on "Settings" at top right of any page, and then on "General Settings" on the left. You can choose your subscription mode from one of the settings there. This will mean that you will be subscribed to any thread you post to.

In the meantime, and if you want to subscribe to a thread you have not posted to, look for the Thread Tools drop-down near to top right of the thread you want to subscribe to. Choose "Subscribe to this thread" and then the notification type when the page changes. You will see a choice of three different email notification frequencies under notification type.

By the way, if you need to add something to a post, use the "Edit post button" rather than making a duplicate post.

[list]
[*]If you change your forum email address, you do not need to change your Ubuntu One account email to match. Email addresses in the two accounts only have to match for initial linking. [*]If you do change your forum email address, please remember to re-activate your account from the verification email, otherwise you will not be able to post.
[*]To change your forum email address, go to settings -> Edit Email & Password, but you can ignore the password field. The only password you need is the Ubuntu One account password and the password field in the forum is redundant for you, but we need to retain the field there for a special purpose.
