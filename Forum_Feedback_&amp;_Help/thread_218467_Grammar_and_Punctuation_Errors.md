### Post by aysiu on 2006-07-18
> **Trent! said:**
> On [http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop]("http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop") there are two errors I thought I'd point out.

Under "Integrated email, calendering and more" it says:

*Whether you need to simply check you email* In the context from which you're quoting, it is grammatically correct. However, the word *calendering* does not mean "to keep track of appointments on a calendar." It means: "To press (paper or cloth) in the rollers of such a machine."

Note the spelling: *calendar* v. *calender*.

> 
And under "Music, video, pictures and their devices" it says:

*Want to move files around with your USB thumb drive?.* Yes, there's a period and a question mark when there should be only a period.

How about reporting these as bugs?
