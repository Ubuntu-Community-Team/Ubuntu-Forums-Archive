### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-06-29
> **Blood//Stain//Child said:**
> How do you do that? Is it an option in about:config?

Yes. Set these options:

network.http.pipelining    **true**
network.http.pipelining.ssl    **true**
network.http.proxy.pipelining    **true** (if you use proxy)
network.http.pipelining.maxrequests    **8**

I used these settings before but I never felt any difference until today.
