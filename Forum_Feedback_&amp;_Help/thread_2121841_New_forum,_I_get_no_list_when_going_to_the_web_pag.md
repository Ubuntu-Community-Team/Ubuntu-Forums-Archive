### Post by coffeecat on 2013-03-03
Do you see the icon that is on the right, on the same line as "The Ubuntu Forum Community"? It looks like this: [img]http://ubuntuforums.org/images/ubuntu-VB4/buttons/collapse_40b_collapsed.png[/img]

Click on it and all the sections and sub-forums will appear. What has happened is that you accidentally clicked on the collapse button - [img]http://ubuntuforums.org/images/ubuntu-VB4/buttons/collapse_40b.png[/img] and "collapsed" the whole of that section. It may seem illogical to have a collapse button for all of the sub-sections and sub-forums on the home page, but in fact there is another one hidden from you for staff use only and we can't remove one without the other. The pre-upgrade forum had the same.
