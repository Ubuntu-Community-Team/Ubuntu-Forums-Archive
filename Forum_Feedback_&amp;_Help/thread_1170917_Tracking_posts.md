### Post by snova on 2009-05-26
Subscribe to the thread. Then you can either check your User CP for notifications, or if you have email enabled check that.

You can set all threads you post in to be subscribed to by default [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editoptions"), under "Default Thread Subscription Mode".
