### Post by nhandler on 2008-10-13
Prior to taking any action, I would strongly urge you to contact that IRC staff member. The staff members are not out there to get you. They are there to help. It very well could have been simply a matter of miscommunication between you and the staff member. By bringing this issue to their attention, they can change their ways to prevent the same thing from happening to another person. If they do not change their ways after you contact them, then, I would escalate the issue.
