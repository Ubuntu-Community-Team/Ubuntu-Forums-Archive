### Post by Joeb454 on 2010-09-22
It looks similar to one of the LoCo forum subdomains - if it was valid, you would be redirected to the relevant subdomain.

What lisati refers to is the fact that, if the subdomain specified is invalid, you will be redirected to the main forum page. Let's say, for example I provide 2 links:

[http://uk.ubuntuforums.org/](http://uk.ubuntuforums.org/)
[http://joeb454.ubuntuforums.org/](http://joeb454.ubuntuforums.org/)

The first redirects you to the UK LoCo team forum, the 2nd works just as the normal domain does.

Hopefully that clears things up for you :)
