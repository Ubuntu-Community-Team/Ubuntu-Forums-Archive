'**Multimedia & Video**' is like having a supermarket aisle labelled '*Fruit & Apples*' - it's redundant.

**Multimedia Production** to me implies anything to do with multimedia *projects* such as recording or mixing an audio recording, editing video or even modelling for 3D animations.

Is the Multimedia & Video forum for desktop users wanting to play mp3s / DVDs and convert formats etc? If so, perhaps there is a more suitable way to label it - such as *Audio & Video*.
