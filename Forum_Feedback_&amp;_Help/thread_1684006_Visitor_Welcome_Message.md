Dear Moderators and Admins,

I read through the forum's visitor welcome message and I was wondering if it would be possible to clean it up?

It currently reads
>   Welcome to the Ubuntu Forums, we encourage you to [REGISTER]("http://ubuntuforums.org/register.php?") on our forums and participate in the community. Ubuntu is a complete Linux-based operating system, freely available with both community and professional support. By [JOINING US]("http://ubuntuforums.org/register.php?") today you can participate in our active and growing community. Registering on our forum gets you access to post new messages, get support, use private messages, download images from our gallery and much much more!After a few minutes of thinking about it/tinkering with it. This is what I came up with as a possible new one.



  > Welcome to the Ubuntu (oo-BOON-too) Forums! We encourage you to **[REGISTER]("http://ubuntuforums.org/register.php?")** and participate in our active and growing community. Ubuntu is a complete Linux-based operating system, freely available, with both community and professional support. Registering also gives you access to get support, give support, use private messages, download images, and much more!I tried to make it shorter and more concise. People tend to zone out with a lot of words. (Hence why most people don't read a EULA. I am guilty of this)


I left the "REGISTER" link in the new one, but is it really necessary? I didn't even realize it was a link until I copied it into LibreOffice and it turned blue. I think the forums are easy enough to navigate, especially with the word 'Register' sitting right about the welcome message.




Thank you for your time!
