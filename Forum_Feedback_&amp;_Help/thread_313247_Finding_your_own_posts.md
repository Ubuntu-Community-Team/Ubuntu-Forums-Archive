### Post by NT4usB on 2006-12-05
Edit your Options:

Default Thread Subscription Mode
When you post a new thread, or reply to a topic, you can choose to
automatically add that thread to your list of subscribed threads, with the option 
to receive email notification of new replies to that thread.
Default Thread Subscription Mode: (set your preference here)

Then bookmark your Subscriptions page.
