### Post by dagroves on 2012-03-09
The Ubuntu Forums are the official forums. Ubuntu.com is just a homepage for Ubuntu and if offers explanations on what Ubuntu is and how it works. It also lists all they ways you can get help. You cannot use your Ubuntu Single Sign On with Ubuntu Forums. You must make a separate account.
