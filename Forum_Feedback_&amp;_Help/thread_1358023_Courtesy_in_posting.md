### Post by spiderbatdad on 2009-12-17
Good etiquette is to wait 24 hours before "bumping" a post. So just reply to your own post with the word "bump. Please."

### Post by Barriehie on 2009-12-17
I like to wait a day, let the world turn around once so everyone has a chance to see/read your post.  Usually in that time I've also come up with more to add if I haven't figured it out already.  I've never actually 'bumped' my threads.  Forum netiquette suggests no more than once a day and 2 is even better!  Good tags can help the right people see your thread!
