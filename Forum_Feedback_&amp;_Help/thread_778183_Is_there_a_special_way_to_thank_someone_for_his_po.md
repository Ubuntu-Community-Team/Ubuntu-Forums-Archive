### Post by Gauvenator on 2008-05-01
> **elvinatom said:**
> Thank you! But what's the difference between "Thanks"  and "Thanked X Times in X Posts"?

Thanks is the amount of thanks you have given, and "Thanked X Times..." is the amount of thanks you have received, and in how many different posts you were thanked (for example, thanked 4 times in 3 posts means you have been thanked more than once in one of your posts).
