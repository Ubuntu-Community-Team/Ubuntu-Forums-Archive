### Post by coffeecat on 2016-10-19
Ubuntu One accounts and forum accounts are separately administered and are entirely different entities. The only link is the one that provides the functionality for logging into the forum from your Ubuntu One account. Changes to your account settings in Ubuntu One have no effect on your forum account and vice versa. You change your forum profile settings where you have always changed your forum profile settings - from the settings link at top right of any page. That takes you to a page where the various types of settings are listed down the left. Change your email in the Edit Email & Password link, except you can ignore the password fields. You do not use the forum password any more, but we have to retain that field for a specific purpose. Profile picture and avatar settings links are also on the left - the two are different.

Perhaps you are misled by the fact that the initial linking of Ubuntu One and the forum account is by an email match. That is for first login and account linking only. Thereafter you can have separate email accounts registered to the two accounts if you prefer, or keep them the same if that is your preference. If you do change your email in the forum account **please** remember to re-activate your forum account from the verification email, otherwise you won't be able to post.

### Post by lisati on 2016-10-20
I can see two different pictures that can be changed using the [settings]("https://ubuntuforums.org/usercp.php") page. Using the [edit avatar]("https://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editavatar") link will change the picture that appears with your posts, and using the [edit profile picture]("https://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editprofilepic") link will change the picture that shows when someone views your forum profile.
