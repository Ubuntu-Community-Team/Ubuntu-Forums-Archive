### Post by Joeb454 on 2010-11-17
Odd - the only tags used in that sig are the [noparse][center] , [color] and [url][/noparse] tags

[center][Test Center tag](http://ubuntuforums.org)[/center]
[[center]Test Center tag (url tags on outside)[/center]](http://ubuntuforums.org)

[s]That seems to be it - put the url tags on the outside and it removes the underline :)[/s]

**Edit:** It seems that you have to set the alignment in order to remove the underlines - that also appears to generate a line break in between links, if you have them on multiple lines as I do.

At least you know how to do it now :p
