### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-02-12
Here's the link, but you can get it from the urlbar in firefox by clicking on the orange feed icon which is there on any forum page and adding the feed subscription.
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/external.php?type=RSS](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/external.php?type=RSS)

It's not so easy in chrome/chromium, which has a dreadful feed reader in comparison, but search the extensions available and you will find several that allow you to do it.  Alternatively use liferea as a specific rss reader.
