### Post by matthew on 2007-12-09
From the [FAQ]("http://ubuntuforums.org/announcement.php?f=48") :
> You may see a few people hanging around the forums with an "emeritus staff" title. These are former staff. Emeritus means retired or honorably discharged from active professional duty, but retaining the title of one's office or position: like a dean emeritus of a graduate school; or a newspaper editor in chief emeritus. Currently the honorees holding this title include az, nocturn, and taurus.You can read the actual details in the [Forums Council log from July 16th, 2007]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=502567"). Essentially, the holders of this title are nominated by the current staff and then the final decision is made by the Forums Council. The process is not open to nominations from the forums at large, no matter how popular a member may be.

Sorry to spoil the fun. If you want to continue discussing it, you may do so, but no discussion that takes place here will have any impact on whether any particular former staff person is or is not nominated and/or chosen.

For this reason, I voted "no" in the poll.
