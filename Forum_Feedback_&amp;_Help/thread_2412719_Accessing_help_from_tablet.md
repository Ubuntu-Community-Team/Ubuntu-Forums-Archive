### Post by coffeecat on 2019-02-16
> **KieranFitzgerald said:**
> However I cannot access the search facility on the forum with the tablet.   When I enter a search and try to answer the random question (tap the box) it bypasses the text entry and reports that the random question was incorrect!  Anyway around this?

The reason you are being asked the random question is that you are not logged in. You need to log into the forum. The question is there to help stop bots. If you are getting the mobile layout, scroll down to the bottom of the page, where you will see links to log in or to go to the full site layout. You should be able to search the forum in either the full or mobile layouts without getting the challenge question so long as you are logged in.
