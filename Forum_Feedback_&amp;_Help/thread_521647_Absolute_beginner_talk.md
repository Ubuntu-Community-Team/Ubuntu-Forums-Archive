### Post by PriceChild on 2007-09-25
Absolute Beginner Talk is intended as the first stop for new users... They have a problem... they're frustrated, they haven't got a clue what's going on and they want as little hassle as possible getting help... so they click on this forum, which is extremely prominently displayed and get their help.

Those who aren't absolute beginners, know what to expect, they take time to craft their question, and place it in a more suitable forum such as "hardware and laptops".

I don't believe that having a "beginners" forum requires an "advanced" forum. All it does is create elitism, and make all the beginners post in the advanced forum expecting better help! I'd suggest "General Help" is the forum you're looking for.
