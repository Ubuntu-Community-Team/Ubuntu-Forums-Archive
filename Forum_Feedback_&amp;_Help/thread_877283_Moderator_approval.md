### Post by matthew on 2008-08-01
I just looked and your how to is in the queue. We only have a couple of staff who review these, mainly because it is easier to keep it organized and the quality of the posts up to a good standard. The downside is that it can sometimes take a couple of days.

Average time for a tip or tutorial to be approved is between 2 hours and 3 days, occasionally it takes longer. We try to be both quick and make sure we actually read through all of them first.
