### Post by prshah on 2009-07-02
> **AoSteve said:**
> I like to help out the members who post and don't get a response.   While there's not many that don't get a reply,  I'd just like to be able to help those people out that haven't got an answer.


You can bookmark / click on this link:

[Threads with less than 2 replies]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/search.php?do=process&showposts=0&replyless=1&replylimit=1&sortby=lastpost&searchdate=14&beforeafter=after&page=2")

Here's a breakup explanation:

Since Ubuntuforums runs on vBulletin, you can submit a search via url. In this case, the url is formed as follows:

[color=red]http://www.ubuntuforums.org/search.php?do=process[/color][color=blue]&showposts=0[/color][color=magenta]&replyless=1&replylimit=1[/color][color=green]&sortby=lastpost&searchdate=14&beforeafter=after[/color]

The red part activates the search feature, and passes it the parameters.

The blue part says to show threads (not posts/beans).

The magenta part says show those which have a less than or equal to (replyless) 1 reply (replylimit)

The green part is sorting (earliest post first in list) and date limit order (after last 14 days).

This is what I use, hope you find it useful too.
