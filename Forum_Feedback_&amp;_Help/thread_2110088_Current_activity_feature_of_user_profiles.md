### Post by santosh83 on 2013-01-29
This question is especially directed to the forum staff, but other users are also welcome to say what they think.

I'm concerned that the 'Current activity' section of an user's profile (which is also duplicated in the Statistics tab) is unnecessary and undermines their privacy needlessly.

I understand that the forum software, and forum administrators need to be able to track users and have editorial rights over them, but is it really necessary to display to the entire community what exactly the user is doing on the forum, minute by minute?

If someone wants to check if user X has been recently active then the 'Last Activity' field is sufficient for that. And private messaging is always there for closer cooperation among users.

In my view nothing useful is served by the 'Current activity' feature, while on the other hand it's a bit creepy.

Again just my opinion but I do feel the forum staff might consider the removal of this field.

Thanks.
