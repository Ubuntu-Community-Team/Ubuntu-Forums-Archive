### Post by cariboo on 2011-01-16
Depending on your browser, it should ask you if you want to save the password for this site.

For Firefox go to Edit->Preferences->Security->Saved Passwords to see if your Ubuntu forums password has been saved.

In Chromium, click the wrench icon on the right, then Preferences->Personal Stuff->Show Saved Passwords
