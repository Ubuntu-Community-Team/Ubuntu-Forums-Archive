### Post by yetiman64 on 2012-04-02

When you click the log out button at the end of your session the session cookies are removed (the login ones) I think that 1 or 2 cookies are left but they will not automatically log you back in the next time you visit. If you always use the logout button you won't have to worry. 

Then using the "Remember Me" button should keep you logged in whilst you visit.
