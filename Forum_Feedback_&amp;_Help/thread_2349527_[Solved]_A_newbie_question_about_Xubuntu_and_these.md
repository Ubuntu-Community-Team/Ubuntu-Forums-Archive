### Post by deadflowr on 2017-01-16
> If I stay at this forum, what is the best way to designate that my questions and comments are specific to Xubuntu? 
Just keep mentioning you're on Xubuntu, along with the suggested prefix option.

But more often than which desktop you run, we'd want to know which release you are on.
As that can sometimes (or most of the time) have a farther reach than what desktop.
Since problems tend to be more commonly desktop-agnostic and release-centric.
(And sometimes (well more often than not) the problem is the particular desktop of a particular release)
