### Post by Joeb454 on 2011-04-03
We disabled the groups a few months ago when we were experiencing severe issues with the database servers, along with some other features & forums.

We have chosen not to re-enable them, partly as a test, and partly based on what we thought the usage of them was. I believe this is only the 2nd or possibly 3rd post asking where they've gone. This indicates that leaving them disabled was a wise choice.

They were difficult to moderate, and a haven for spam, as few people visited them on a regular basis, only adding to the reasons for disabling them.
