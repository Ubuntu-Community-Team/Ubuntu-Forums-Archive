### Post by jenkinbr on 2009-06-02
Try the above to get back on track - you'll want to subscribe to them if you haven't already.

Now, go to this link: [http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editoptions#cb_receivefriendemailrequest](http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editoptions#cb_receivefriendemailrequest)

Just below this anchor you will see the option to change your default subscription type - I have mine set to 'No email Notification'

If you do what I did, you will always subscribe to a thread when you post in it. I don't choose the email options because I don't like the spam I get from 300 different threads (especially when 60 of them are in Cafe Games, but that's a different story...:))

Hope that helps you :)
