### Post by Tamlynmac on 2010-09-01
> tyblogger5

Instead of posting in the Cafe, post in the T&E or Help Sections.

It's quite simple to increase bean count in the [T&E]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=103"), as many of the threads there are opinion based. Assuming, one's only concern is bean count. However, by assisting in the "Help Sections" one can increase bean count and provide support to other members simultaneously.

Questions in the help sections range from extremely complex to basic setup or individual software package assistance. Sometimes, it's a member seeking alternative software options or  requesting assistance with locating information of explanations. It's been my experience that one can participate in the help sections without being a Ubuntu/Linux guru. For example, I periodically scan the help sections for questions that even I'm capable of responding to. :D

Good Luck
