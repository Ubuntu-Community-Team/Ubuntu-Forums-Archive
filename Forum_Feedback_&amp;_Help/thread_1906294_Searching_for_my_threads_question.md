### Post by mörgæs on 2012-01-09
Posts are not deleted. 

There is a 250 thread limit for the search results, and my guess is that this is due to load on the server.

Searching should be done by Google with the 'site' option added, by the way. Much better than the search function in Ubuntuforums.

If you have some particular important threads I suggest that you add to your own list of bookmarks.

### Post by coffeecat on 2012-01-09
You can find your threads or posts older than the last 250 if you experiment with the search parameters on the forum advanced search page.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/search.php](http://ubuntuforums.org/search.php)

Try adjusting such things as "Find posts from" (using "and older"), "search by tag" and "search in forum".
