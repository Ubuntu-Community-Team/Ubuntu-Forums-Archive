### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-08-11
Over time the Ubuntu forums has been quite popular and the resources on the servers are limited.

Our primary focus is for support. Off topic / non-support threads belong in the cafe.

Some topics recur over and over. While you may enjoy such topics the majority of the community (and not just the staff) does not.

So you are free to discuss off topic and/or recurring topics in the appropriate section. This seems to be the best solution, giving a place for such discussions, but not in a way to that affects either our main goal (support) or the majority of the community (who are tired of gnome vs KDE and other such recurring discussions) the majority of users here.

If there is some non-support topic that you feel is inappropriately moved to the cafe or recurring discussions you may discuss it with the staff (be prepared to justify any changes) or find a more appropriate forums for your off topic needs (there are many alternates for you to select from).
