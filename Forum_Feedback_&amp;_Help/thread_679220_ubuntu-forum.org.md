### Post by PriceChild on 2008-01-27
To restate what I have said before, ubuntu-forums.org, ubuntuforums.com and several other variations of the name have been registered by Canonical to keep them in safe hands.

As I understand it, the way vB logs in your session does not transfer across domains.

Please ensure you *always* use ubuntuforums.org Although the sites I listed above are safe, you should still make a habit of never entering your username/password for the forums in any site other than ubuntuforums.org
