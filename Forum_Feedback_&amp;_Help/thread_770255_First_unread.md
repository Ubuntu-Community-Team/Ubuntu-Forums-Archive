As far as I am aware, there are three places you can click on in your CP:

Clicking on the thread's title brings you to the first post and always did.
Clicking on the small button in front of the thread's title (down pointing triangle) takes you to the first unread post, if any. If there are none, the button will not show.
Clicking on a similar small button on the right hand side will bring you to the last post within the thread, no matter if already read or not.
