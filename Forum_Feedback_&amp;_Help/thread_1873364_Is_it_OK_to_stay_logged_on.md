That depends on if you have others who could misuse that or not, or if you trust anyone that could.

Login and out is a security feature to prevent other users around accessing things physically and easily (there are easy ways without login) however it is a simple preventative measure.

If you trust everyone around and/or there is no one else around then it is fine.

If you refer to the system itself, then it makes no difference

if it is a laptop, shut the lid and hibernate or standby.

If Desktop just have a screen saver or turn monitor off to conserve energy
