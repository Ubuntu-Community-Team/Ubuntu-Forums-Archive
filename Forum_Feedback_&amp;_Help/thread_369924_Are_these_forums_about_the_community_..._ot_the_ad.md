### Post by matthew on 2007-02-25
These forums are about community.

The problem with creating a MythTV forum has been explained several times. The decision has NOTHING to do with a desire to hinder community nor with any desire to prevent people from asking questions and answering them.

The problem is that we do not have anyone on staff who is capable of being responsible for that section, for answering questions, for coordinating efforts. These forums are centered around topics that are directly related to Ubuntu. That is what the staff are capable of helping with. The third party sections (where a MythTV forum would be created) have each been started because a developer who is actively engaged with the project requested it. As far as we know there are no MythTV developers active in these forums. There is no way we would be able to do anything to help people and ultimately the forum would probably end up being a source of frustration for users than an actual help.

Now, if you want to find an active MythTV developer who is using Ubuntu and is willing to engage with users in a tech support fashion in a third party forum here that would we welcomed and wonderful.

By the way, the tone of your post comes across as ungrateful and does nothing to convince anyone that you should be helped. I hope you find a community you can be a part of elsewhere.

For the others have requested this in a very polite manner our response is a gentle, "I'm sorry, we just can't do this."
