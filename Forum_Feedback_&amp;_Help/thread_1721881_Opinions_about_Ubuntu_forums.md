### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-04-05
Definitely the best and most helpful forum I have ever used and been a member of, partly due to the huge number of users, both old and new, all of whom have something to offer, even if it is just to remind us that we all had to start in this Linux world with very little knowledge, and there are always people out there who need help.  I know it helped me many times when I started with Ubuntu, so now I try to do my bit by answering other users questions whenever I can.

I agree that the navigation of the forum can be a bit tortuous at times, particularly for new users, but that is not really a criticism of the forum, more of the software used to host it, and I have no idea what the alternatives are, so will leave it at that.

It is also a pity that the forum search facility is not better;  I have just about given up using it, and tend to add the prefix **site:ubuntuforums.org** to a google search instead, or use [www.googlubuntu.com](www.googlubuntu.com)
