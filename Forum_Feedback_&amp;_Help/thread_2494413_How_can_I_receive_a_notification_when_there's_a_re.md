Emails aren't instant most of the time.  Sometimes they never come. Sometimes they come 12 hrs later. Sometimes they arrive in 5 minutes.  It has been an issue at least 10 yrs.

I suspect there is a delay in sending emails to give the forum staff a chance to remove any spam posts in a thread.  The amount of spam posting is huge, so the fact that we don't usually see it is a credit to all the moderators.

If you click on the "Settings", there will be a list of subscribed threads there with new posts. This bypasses the email notification delays.
I tried to find a way to unsubscribe from old threads as a way to help reduce the load on the forum system. Never found a good way to do it. Now I just unsubscribe manually when the thread appears to be closed/answered.  After 6 months, many threads are closed and locked by the moderators. Sometimes it happens when spam posts start showing.
