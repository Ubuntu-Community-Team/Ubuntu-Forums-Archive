### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-12-16
You've set the pic as your profile picture only.  

You will need to go to userCP > Settings and Options (3rd section down on the left of the userCP) > Edit Avatar, and set an avatar from there. 

Your profile picture and your avatar can be different and only the avatar shows in your posts in the general forums.
