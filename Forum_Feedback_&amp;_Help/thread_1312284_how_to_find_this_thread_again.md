### Post by Jayferd on 2009-11-03
abqhandyman: I think you want to go over to your control panel ("User CP" in the top left), go to "edit options" in the left menu under "Settings and Options", and scroll down slightly until you see a small box labeled "Default Thread Subscription Mode".  Set that as you wish; I have "Instant Email Notification", which means that as soon as anyone replies to a thread I've contributed to, the thing emails me with the reply and an excerpt of the first post I haven't read yet.

Have fun exploring!
