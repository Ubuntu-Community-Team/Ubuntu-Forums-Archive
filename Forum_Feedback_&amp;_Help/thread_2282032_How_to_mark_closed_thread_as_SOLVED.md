### Post by coffeecat on 2015-06-11
Rather bizarrely, when I looked in the Thread Tools drop-down, it invited me to mark the thread as unsolved, even though it wasn't marked as solved. So I've used some admin-fu to get the [SOLVED] prefix showing. :)

Yes, I think the auto-closure may have affected what's showing in the drop-down both for yourself and me. Threads are auto-closed after 1 year's inactivity. On those rare cases where it is necessary to re-open an auto-closed thread, the forum staff can do that. And to answer your last question, yes. If you want anything doing to a thread of yours which you can't do, please use the report post button in your own post to send a message to the staff area. The report post isn't just for reporting problematic posts, and we're always happy to help.
