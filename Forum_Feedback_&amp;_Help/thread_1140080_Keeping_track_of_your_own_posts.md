### Post by Eldera on 2009-04-27
> It seems to me that there should be an option to automatically subscribe to threads you post to

Go to User CP>Edit options.

Look under Message & Notification for Default Thread Subscription Mode. (The second large box down] In the drop down menu, choose anything except "do not subscribe" depending on whether you want e-mail, too.

I use "no email notifications" which gives me just automatic subscriptions. Cheers, Eldera
