### Post by lisati on 2010-05-22
For PMs, the forum software has an option you can check that sends a "read receipt" when you send someone a PM. I'm not sure if there's an option to do this for every message.
Some users choose to receive PMs only from contacts and moderators. If this is the case, you'll see an error message when you try to send them a message. 
Failing either of the above, you'll just have to wait until the recipient chooses to respond (if at all).....
