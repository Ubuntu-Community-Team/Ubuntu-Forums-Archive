I recently swapped to X from U when I upgraded to 7.10 to taste it, and I really love it, but there are little things that have taken a while to sort out, that might have been easier to fix if I was only displayed relevant results for X.

The forum has been a massive help while using Ubuntu Feisty, and I'm sure a little modification is a realistic concept to greatly benefit for our diverse minorities!
