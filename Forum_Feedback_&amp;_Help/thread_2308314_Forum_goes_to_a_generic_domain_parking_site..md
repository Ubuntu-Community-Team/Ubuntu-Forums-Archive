### Post by coldraven on 2016-01-02
It's possible that your ISP's DNS server is having trouble or being hacked.
You can change the settings in your router from "Automatic" to "Manual" and use Googles DNS at 8.8.8.8 and 8.8.4.4
I would only use this as a temporary fix unless you are happy for Google to see your browsing habits.
