### Post by handy on 2011-04-04
I have Firefox (3 or 4) set to:

Accept all cookies 

Accept 3rd party cookies 

Keep until: "Ask me every time"

With those settings you get asked a lot about cookies when you go to a site, when the requester appears you tick the "Apply to all cookies from this site" (or something similar).

Doing that builds its own black/white list of cookies for sites & as time goes by you find that you are only being asked about cookies when you visit a new site.

When the requester pops up, I automatically block all cookies, occasionally I have to go back into the cookie preferences & delete one I've blocked as I need it to use the site (a forum, shopping & such). When I use Amazon I need to delete the blocked cookie to use my account, after I have purchased something I go back & delete the Amazon cookie. I do that with other sites also. As I don't trust their privacy policies.
