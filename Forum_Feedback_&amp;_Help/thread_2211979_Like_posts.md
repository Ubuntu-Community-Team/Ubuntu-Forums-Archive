### Post by coffeecat on 2014-03-21
@bhattabhishek, in case you haven't found it yet, you can change your settings so that you autosubscribe to every thread that you post in. Settings -> General Settings -> Default Thread Subscription Mode. There are 3 email options or "Through my Control Panel only" if you don't want to be deluged with emails. If you choose the Control Panel option, every time you click on settings you'll see a list of subscribed threads with posts made to them since the last time you visited the thread.
