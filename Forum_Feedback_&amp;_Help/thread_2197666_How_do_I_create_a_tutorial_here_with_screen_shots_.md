### Post by coffeecat on 2014-01-05
> **squakie said:**
> Could not get the attachments to show larger on the screen - perhaps my not knowing how.

Since a couple of pictures is worth more than two thousand words, click on the "Reply with Quote" button for this post to see how I've done it. You'll need to make sure your message editor is in one of the source modes - i.e. not WYSIWYG - to see the BBCode. 

[img]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=249233&d=1388914039[/img]

[ATTACH=CONFIG]249233[/ATTACH]

You need to preview your partially written post after you've uploaded and attached the image, right-click on the thumbnail -> Open Link in new tab -> copy the URL of the full-size image and then paste that into your post between [noparse][IMG][/IMG] tags. You can then move the stuff between the [IMG] and [ATTACH] tags around within your post as you edit it.[/noparse]

Since that will be a bit of a nightmare to set up if you have a lot of images in a tutorial, you might prefer to use an image hosting site, as I think you are mentioning in your OP, for the images.
