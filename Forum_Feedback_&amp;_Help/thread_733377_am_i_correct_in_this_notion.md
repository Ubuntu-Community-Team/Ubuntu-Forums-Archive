But seriously, a distinction exists in the subforum descriptions:
[QUOTE=Community Cafe]Community Chat area for general discussion.[/QUOTE]
[QUOTE=The Backyard]The Backyard is for debates, politics etc. Please try to keep it clean and professional. Please read the rules posted in the forum.[/QUOTE]

**The distinction doesn't state any reason to limit the Café to "computering" talk only**, there's only a distinction between "general discussion" and "debates".
