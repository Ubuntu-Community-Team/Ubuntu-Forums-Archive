### Post by StuartN on 2009-05-20
> **roel46 said:**
> A thread initiated by me was answered (solved). Therefor I want to change the status of the thread in [SOLVED] or closed. How can I do that?

Groeten, Roel.

You can't close a thread, but you can edit the title of the first posting. Go to the first post, click "Edit" and then click "Go Advanced", which enables editing of the title as well as the body, and insert "Solved:".
