### Post by deadflowr on 2023-10-24
Posts in the support sections increase bean counts.
Posts in the discussion sections do not.

Please refer to the [Forum Code of Conduct]("https://ubuntuforums.org/misc.php?do=showrules") and subsequent [Forum Posting Guidelines]("https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2158945") for guidance as to what is considered proper posting etiquette.
