### Post by coffeecat on 2014-07-25
Did you do an advanced search? If you don't tick the "Exact Name" box, the search will include usernames which include the string which is your username. For example, an advanced search for username dave131, without ticking the Exact Name box, yields 5 results at the moment, your 4 posts, and a post from 2008 from user dave1314. 

If you want a listing of your posts only, simply click on your own username at the top of the page to get your profile page, and then "Find latest posts" or The My Activity tab and click on your own username tab.
