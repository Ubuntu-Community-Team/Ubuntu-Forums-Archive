### Post by roololing on 2011-06-23
When I post on a thread, I want to subscribe to it. This works out well, but I don't want to get an email each and every time someone posts on the thread.

Could there be a new notification option so I only get emailed when the thread changes from "no new posts" to  "new posts?"[-o< That way my email would tell me which threads I should go look at, but my inbox won't be flooded if it turns out to be a popular thread.

### Post by yetiman64 on 2011-06-23
Go to, [http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editoptions](http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editoptions), and set the "Default thread subscription mode" to "No email notifications" (look under the Messaging & Notification     section 2nd down). That way whenever you subscribe to a thread no email is sent about replies to it.

You can individually set email notifications for a thread in your "[[COLOR=Blue]Subscrbed threads[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/subscription.php?do=viewsubscription&folderid=all")" list with the menu at the bottom right of the page (tick the thread first) and choose a type under" Update subscription type"

Edit: the "menu" at the bottom right is a toolbar you need to click the arrow on to get the options you need.
