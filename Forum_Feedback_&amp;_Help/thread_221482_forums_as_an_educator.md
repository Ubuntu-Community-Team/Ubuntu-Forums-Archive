After a week or so to reflect on my recent resignation as a staff member of these forums I'd like to send a rather large thank you to the administrators and some staff members. You have inadvertently solved many issues that I was faced with and released me to pursue new interests.... thank you

also:

1. Thank you for showing me the manipulative nature of humans. I tend to live in a naive universe where people are treated with dignity and respect and opinions of all are considered in good faith. I have learnt many lessons, thank you.

2. Thank you for releasing me when to the truth was revealed by concerned staff members and resignation requested. The relief of no longer being involved in the internal politics of the staff forum has been liberating.

3. I congratulate you for successfully removing the anti-integration members of staff. Rather than face the debate and then give those members a chance to respect that democratic decision, an alternative plan was implemented that reshuffled the staff in a much less time consuming way.... well done :)

5. Finally, I wish the new and existing staff members all the best for the future. I'm sure that you, as others before you, believe in the goodness of this community and will work towards making it a better place to be. Hopefully you won't have to deal with a repeat of last weeks events.

regards,
manicka
