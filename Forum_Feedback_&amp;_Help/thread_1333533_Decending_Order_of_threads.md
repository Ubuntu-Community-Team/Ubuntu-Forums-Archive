### Post by sgosnell on 2009-11-21
At the top of the page, click on User CP.  Then on the left side, find Edit Options.  Then scroll way down to Thread Display Options.  Select the one you want from the dropdown box, probably Linear - Newest First.  Make any other changes you want to other options.  Scroll down to the very bottom, and click on Save Changes.  Done.
