### Post by MisterGaribaldi on 2011-02-13
I know there is an effort to port Debian-based (and probably other) Linux to the handheld range of devices. Moreover, many of them already are Linux-based (Android- and webOS units, for example) but there's really no direct relationship between those sorts of devices *as commonly used **or** hacked* and Ubuntu (or Fedora, or Slackware, etc.)

That being said, I would be interested in seeing what the Ubuntu community would do with this, and as someone who owns an Android-based phone, I would probably participate.

Of course, we already have XDA-Developers and SDX-Developers out there, so I guess we would just have to see.
