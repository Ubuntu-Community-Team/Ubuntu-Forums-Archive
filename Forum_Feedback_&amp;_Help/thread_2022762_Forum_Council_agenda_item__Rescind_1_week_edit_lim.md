### Post by Favux on 2012-07-11
For the Forum Council's 7-18-12 23:00 UTC meeting on the IRC channel #ubuntu-meeting.  See:  [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ForumCouncilAgenda](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ForumCouncilAgenda)

**The decision to limit the ability to edit a post for only one week should be rescinded.**  This will **allow tutorials and HOW TO's to once again be on the forums** and not just limited to the Ubuntu community wiki.  Other methods should be found to encourage the volunteers writing HOW TO's and tutorials to add their content to the Ubuntu community wiki.  Their ability to help other forum users in the way they feel is most useful and comfortable should not have been restricted by this new policy.

[B]Those interested in this issue please attend the meeting.
[/B]
But remember:
[QUOTE=bodhi.zazen]Meetings are most productive if they are used to make decisions. The time for discussion is between meetings - You can use this section of the forums, Forums Council Mailing list, and IRC #ubuntuforums for discussion of the FC agenda at any time between meetings.
[/QUOTE]
From:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1961135](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1961135)

So discussion should be limited to this thread, if it is permitted.  Otherwise you can read the several threads on this topic on this sub-forum (last couple of pages) and the sticky in Tutorials and Tips:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1949027](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1949027)
