### Post by hakermania on 2011-06-09
What do you mean it's not working?
First of all, you need the full image path, for instance, let's say I have this image here:
[http://imageshack.us/photo/my-images/8/screenshot1iiz.png/](http://imageshack.us/photo/my-images/8/screenshot1iiz.png/)

But as you see this isn't the **direct** link to the image, the direct link is:
[http://img8.imageshack.us/img8/5384/screenshot1iiz.png](http://img8.imageshack.us/img8/5384/screenshot1iiz.png)

In order to find what's the direct link of your own image, right click on the Image->View image, and the direct link appears on the URL bar of your browser
