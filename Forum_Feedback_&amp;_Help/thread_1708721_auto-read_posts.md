### Post by Iowan on 2011-03-17
> **adduds said:**
> it just auto puts me back on the page when i say submit
That's why your posts/threads appear read already.
If there are posts in a thread that **you** haven't read, the title is bold. Those "posts all over with 1 read" aren't **your** read - so they appear bold to you. If someone else posts in your thread, the title will appear bold to **you** until you read the thread again. There's another little symbol that indicates if you have posted in a thread.
