### Post by aysiu on 2008-05-22
It's kind of a difficult call to make, as there sometimes can be overlap in topics.

If I feel the general focus is on personal experience (this is what happened to *me* and why *I* am leaving), then I'll put it in Ubuntu Testimonials and *Experiences*.

If, however, the general focus of the thread seems to be more about opinions regarding "desktop readiness" or "Linux for the masses," then I'll put it in Recurring Discussions.

Not all experiences have to be positive experiences. That's why it's called *Ubuntu Testimonials and Experiences* and not just *Ubuntu Testimonials*.
