### Post by ubuntu-geek on 2006-07-20
Do to popular demand the option is now available to change your postbit from displaying the user information on threads from the top to the left.

To make this change goto your **User CP**, click **Edit Profile**, Goto the last option on the page and select if you wish for it to display on top or on the left then click save.

The forum default will be to display on the top.

Enjoy..
