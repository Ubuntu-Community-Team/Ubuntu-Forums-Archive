### Post by Gnuklear on 2005-10-03
After the horrific flamewar that a certain thread on this topic started (I have deliberately refrained from posting a link as to try to avoid opening old wounds), I would like to ask the administrative and moderating team what they believe are necessary features that vBulletin has and Free forum software lacks.

The intent of this question is to make known what exactly the problems with existing Free Software systems are so that they can be brought up to par with their proprietary competitors. Whether ubuntuforums would upgrade to this enhanced setup, should it ever be developed, is of course up to the full discretion of the ubuntuforums team, but it can't hurt to at least know what the people in charge feel is lacking in available alternatives.
