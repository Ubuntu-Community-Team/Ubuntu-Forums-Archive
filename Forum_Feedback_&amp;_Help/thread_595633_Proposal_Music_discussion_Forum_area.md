### Post by multifaceted on 2007-10-28
I know that these forums are primarily in place for helping others and solving problems as a whole. However, just like any community --(as is the basis for the cafe and backyard)-- shouldn't there be an area for music discussion?

I don't think I stand alone when I say that music is the lube in the gears that run my life. Additionally, since I am a lover of all music... the notion of that type of area sparks interest immediately. Think of an area where us all can converse and recommend pieces by our motivation... music!

Okay... Okay... I know... 
Not everyone is as passionate about music as I, some or many of you are. Nevertheless, is not music the universal language of the soul???

Does not a person's music help us understand them before we can even speak???

So, why not have an area of music discussion to relate each others interests with something more tangible other than just an OS?
