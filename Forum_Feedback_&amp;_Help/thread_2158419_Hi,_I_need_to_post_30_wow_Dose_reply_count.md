### Post by Irihapeti on 2013-06-29
*Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

New posts and replies count towards the 25 posts, as long as they are in the support areas. Posts in the discussions areas don't count.

It's also not  good idea to make rather pointless posts to get the numbers up. These can get the attention of staff in a way that you probably don't want.

However, if you have genuine questions you want to ask, or problems you need to solve, you should get to the 25 fairly quickly.
