### Post by coffeecat on 2014-08-10
If you set your default thread subscription mode to through my control panel in your general settings, then clicking on the settings link at top-right of any page will give a list of all subscribed threads that have been posted to since you last viewed them. That also works if you don't set up automatic subscriptions but subscribe to threads individually through thread tools and choose through my control panel.
