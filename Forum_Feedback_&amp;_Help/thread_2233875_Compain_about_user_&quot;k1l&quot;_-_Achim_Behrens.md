### Post by LmOtaku on 2014-08-01
Usually IRC has some misfits, it's true, but in this case it seems they didn't understand what you were looking for, or you pushed for an answer that couldn't be provided.

What you're looking for is a remote screen viewer(VNC). In most cases for any type of server to run or listen on a port requires rooting of the device to get root access to the software.
You asked for a free VNC app for Android, and said google and google play didn't achieve any results. Which a short look I found this: [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.icecoldapps.vncserverultimate&hl=en](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.icecoldapps.vncserverultimate&hl=en)
and VMlite which is a $10 app. As with many apps they don't always work with all hardware or versions of Android, so be sure to read up before making a choice. If you can't find a VNC server that works, it needs to be developed or fixed by those authors for their respected applications.

If you're looking for a free VNC viewer for ubuntu, you have many apps to choose from. RealVNC, TinyVNC, whichever fits your fancy. [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/VNC/Clients](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/VNC/Clients)

(I realize this is an old topic, but I felt the person needed an answer, and people after him looking for similar answers.) I'm kinda bewildered why no one took a minute to provide a good answer, but I'm not surprised since Unity how far Ubuntu has went downhill.
