I can't seem to figure out how to automatically get subscribed to a thread I start myself. Every time I create a thread I have to manually add a subscription to the thread. Is it just me or isn't it obvious to be automatically subscribed to a thread you create yourself?

### Post by frodon on 2007-04-18
Go to "Quick Links" >  "Edit Options" then in the "Default Thread Subscription Mode" area choose "no email notification" if you want only notification within the forum clicking UcerCP when you want to check subscribed thread or the email notification method you prefer.
