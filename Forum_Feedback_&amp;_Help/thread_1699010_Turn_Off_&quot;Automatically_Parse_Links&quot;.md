### Post by dodle on 2011-03-03
Is there a way to turn off the "automatically parse links" option? I can't find it in the User CP.

---

### Post by _outlawed_ on 2011-03-03
When posting, under the Submit Reply and Preview Post buttons there is a space called Additional Options.

Under that in Miscellaneous Options, uncheck "Automatically parse links in text".
