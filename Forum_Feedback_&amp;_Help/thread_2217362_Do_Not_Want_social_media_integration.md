### Post by dewdrop_world on 2014-04-17
Would it be possible to have a user option to disable all social media integration in this forum?

I live behind the Great Firewall of China. This means, every time a site tries to use "connect.facebook.com" or "platform.twitter.com," I have to sit there for an additional 20-60 seconds (sometimes longer) to wait for those connection attempts to get killed before the browser will show me ANYTHING. Here on ubuntuforums.org, *every* attempt to view a thread tries to call connect.facebook.com... every time. I don't need the feature and it costs me time.

For the most part, I just have to accept that this is a fact of life -- only partly about living in China, but also about web developers who just assume that everybody in the world will have no problem getting to those sites. Sometimes I can accept it... sometimes I feel compelled to remind site administrators that this "convenience" is very much not helpful in some parts of the world.

Thanks,
hjh

### Post by QIII on 2014-04-17
Hello!

If you use Firefox, you can add the NoScript addon.  With that, you can pick and choose what you want to show up.  You can forbid facebook.net and facebook.com to keep those elements from attempting to make connections.

Cheers!

### Post by vasa1 on 2014-04-17

If you need help with AdBlock Plus, ask a specific question in the support section and people will be happy to help :)
