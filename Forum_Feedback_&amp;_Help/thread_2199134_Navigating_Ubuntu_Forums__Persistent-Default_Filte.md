### Post by Elfy on 2014-01-12
> Welcome to the forums. I don't know of a way to do it. If you don't shut down your machine, just put it to sleep, and you use the forum a lot, your session will stay logged-in which keeps those settings.

It should keep you logged in for 24 hours regardless.

After that changes you've made to the filter will be lost.

So - I'd deal with the filtering as and when you need it.

This forum was the first I ever had much to do with, Feisty Fawn in 2007 was the first distro I ever had much to do with for any length of time, I used the forum a LOT and never found the default filtering to be much of an issue.

Though at that point there were a couple of good search engines out there rather than use the forum search - only one left now is [googlubuntu]("http://www.googlubuntu.com/").

Have a go with using that - you might find that the forum default isn't an issue then.
