### Post by tgeer43 on 2009-08-30
Ok, I think I'm starting to understand. Apparently you are trying to use some application (Evolution, maybe?) on your computer to compose and send your post. That's not how things work when posting to a forum on the internet.

Normally you would compose your message by typing it directly into the editor provided by the forum when you click 'New Thread' or 'New Reply'. If for some reason you are more comfortable composing offline then just use your favorite text editor to create the post. When you are done pull down the 'Edit' menu in your text editor and click on 'Select All'. Pull down the edit menu again and this time select 'Copy'. After you have started a new message on the forum, go to the body of the message where you would normally type it in by hand and right-click anywhere in the box. Select 'Paste' from the right-click menu that appears and presto!

BTW: When you are on the forums and doing just about anything (posting, searching, reading messages, etc.) you are active and shouldn't be logged out automatically. It's only when you do nothing for a prolonged period (1 hour I think) that you will be logged out.

Hope this helps,

tgeer
