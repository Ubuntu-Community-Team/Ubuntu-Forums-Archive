### Post by yetiman64 on 2011-08-01
Firstly the CoC asks users to keep to one thread per topic, you found out about that by the sound of it.

If you are getting no replies it is perfectly OK to bump your thread once every 24 hours (simply post the word "bump", everyone will know you still need help when seeing it, but only do it once a day to keep within forum guidelines) eventually someone with the knowledge/experience needed will come across it by you doing so. 

Remember there are well over a million threads on this forum. From the forum mainpage, Threads: 1,656,585 (at the time of this post). That's quite a lot for volunteer helpers to wade through, so your patience is appreciated by those who help out.

Also the bolded questions you mention are specific to what you have viewed or are changed when older than a certain time limit (not sure how long that is but it won't be an issue if you bump your thread once a day). For instance if you keep bumping your thread daily what you see as bolded will be different to me depending on what each of us has viewed or responded to recently.

Hope this helps as general info, cheers yetiman64.
