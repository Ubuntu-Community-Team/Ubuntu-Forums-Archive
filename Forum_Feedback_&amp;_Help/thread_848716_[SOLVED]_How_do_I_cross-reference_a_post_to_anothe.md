### Post by him610 on 2008-07-03
Great! I figured it out.
In the original thread, one must right-click on the post number located in the upper right hand corner of the post which will open a dropdown list, then highlight and left-click "Copy link location."
In the second thread, open a Reply, enter your message then right-click the Insert link icon, and paste the copied link location from the first thread. Finish your message, preview and edit your post if necessary, test it to see if it works, and submit it.

Great! Easier than apple pie.
