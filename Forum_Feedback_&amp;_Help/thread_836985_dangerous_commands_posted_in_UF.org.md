### Post by p_quarles on 2008-06-22
Normally, our users are very prompt about reporting such abuses. It would also be hard to make a filter that would recognize the difference that context can make. 

As the global announcement on this topic says, too, the biggest security risk is always the "feature" that gives users a false sense of safety. A filter for certain widely known exploits wouldn't stop anyone from posting a sneakier -- and perhaps heretofore unknown -- malicious command.
