Hi guys.

After I click the Link button and make a link in a post, how do I turn off the linking? When I'm done, any text I type after that is also linked. Even on following lines. I tried the Unlink button but that just undoes the link I just made.

Thank you.

---

### Post by slickymaster on 2017-09-27
The best way to add a link to a part of text is to select it and then click the link button. That way just the selected text will contain the hyperlink.
