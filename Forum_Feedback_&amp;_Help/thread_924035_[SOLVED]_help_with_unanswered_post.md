### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-09-19
Try this....

Reboot and enter your BIOS configuration.  Look for "SATA Mode".  One of the options should be AHCI, and the other may be RAID.  Whatever it's set to, change it to the other.  Now save your changes and exit, and cross your fingers...

If that doesn't work, try reinstalling Ubuntu using the new BIOS setting.
