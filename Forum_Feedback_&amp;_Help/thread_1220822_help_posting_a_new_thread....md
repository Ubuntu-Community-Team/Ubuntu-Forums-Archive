### Post by drs305 on 2009-08-07
> **shihkster1015 said:**
> how do i post a new thread?

First, register. The "Register" link is near the top left of the main page. Get there by clicking on the "Ubuntu Forums" link at the top left of the page.

Review the "stickies" - permanent closed threads that contain information from the moderators, including how to post, etc. They are in the "Forum Feedback" section:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=48]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=48")

Find the most appropriate forum. You can review them by clicking the "Ubuntu Forums" link at the top left of the page.

Click on the forum in which you wish to post. 

On the left side, there is a shaded link for "New Thread".
