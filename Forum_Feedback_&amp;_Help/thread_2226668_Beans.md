### Post by QIII on 2014-05-28
Don't let bean count get you wrapped around the axle.  The only time they are functionally meaningful in any way is the 10 bean requirement to be allowed to modify your profile.  This is an anti-spam measure.  Be aware, however, that making a lot of meaningless posts to add to your bean count will run you afoul of the staff and get your posts jailed (or worse).  If your posts are removed, your bean count will drop.
