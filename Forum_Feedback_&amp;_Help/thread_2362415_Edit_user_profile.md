### Post by coffeecat on 2017-05-28
Not needing Resolution Centre action.

*Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

It's straightforward to change your email. Simply click on the settings link at the top right of any page, and then on Edit Email & Password which you'll find on the left. Ignore the password fields. Your vbulletin password is no longer used, but we have to keep those fields there for a specific reason. If you do change your email, please remember to re-activate your account from the verification email. Until you do, you will not be able to post.

Please note that this only changes your forum account email. If you need to change the email associated with your Ubuntu One SSO account, you need to do this from [https://login.ubuntu.com/](https://login.ubuntu.com/) .

As far as other profile settings are concerned, I see that your post count is not yet 10. Please see this sticky: [https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393)
