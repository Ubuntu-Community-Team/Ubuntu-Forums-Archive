### Post by popch on 2007-12-14
Placing someone on the ignore list does not change anything in the database. The ignore list just filters the entries the forum shows to you. You can experiment with the ignore list without leaving any side effects.

As far as I can tell, you see where people you are ignoring have posted within a thread. The forum says that you are ignoring the member and does not show you the post. Quotes are not suppressed, however. 

To my tastes, that feature is somewhat half-baked. Reading is a bit disrupted by the proxy entry, and you infer anyway what the ignored member said either from quotes or by answers to the invisible posts.
