### Post by coffeecat on 2016-04-27
Once you are logged into the forum, you should remain logged in for 24 hours, even if you close your browser, unless you click on the logout button. If you find that you are being logged out without deliberately logging out yourself, there are two things to look at.

Do you have a browser extension that clears cookies? If you clear ubuntuforums cookies while logged in you will lose your session.

You will be logged out if your external (public-facing) IP address changes. You might wish to check this.
