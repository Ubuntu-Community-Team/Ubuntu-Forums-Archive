### Post by coffeecat on 2020-03-28
Click on Settings at top right of most pages, and then on "Edit profile" on the left of the Settings page. The Ubuntu Distro field, where you can set your release version, is near the bottom. There are several options available from the settings page, including "General Settings". They are quite a labyrinth, but it's worth exploring them.

Good luck!

**Edit:** oops, sorry. I misread your post. uRock has directed you to the edit signature field. Nevertheless, exploring all the profile options either from the settings link, or from quick links is well worth while.
