### Post by col48 on 2022-05-03
@Jeremy31

Thanks, I was able to do the business starting with the link.

I was wrong about one thing, though - it wasn't entirely simple!

1 add new email
2 confirm this via this new email
3 now find this is, unexpectedly, the 3rd email in the list!
4 delete the second (preexisting) one in the list
5 manage email preferences to set the new one to be preferred
6 delete the old one

logout there and then login in here again to check all is well (a whiff of cynicism here), and it is.
