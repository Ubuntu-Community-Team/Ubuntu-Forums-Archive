### Post by cariboo on 2012-07-27
Karma on Launchpad is only for what you do on Launchpad, for instance if you create and participate in bug reports. If you use SSO (single sign on) to log into the forum, you really aren't doing anything on Launchpad. You can also use SSO to create and modify wiki pages on the Ubuntu wikis.
