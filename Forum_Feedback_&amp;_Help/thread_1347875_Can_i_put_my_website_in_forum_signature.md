### Post by matthew on 2009-12-06
That is strictly true. You may have a link to your personal site, but not if it is purely commercial and unrelated to Linux. If it is just a link to your blog, etc., that is fine so long as the site is safe for school/work and so on.

What we really don't want are people who come here just to get publicity for their personal commercial ventures and who put links in their sig to do so, or people who want to post porn, rude stuff and all that.

I hope that helps clear it up a little bit.
