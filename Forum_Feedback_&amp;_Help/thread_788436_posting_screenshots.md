### Post by IHATEDLINK on 2008-05-09
1- Click Manage attachments
2- Click Browse and select your files
3- Click Upload
4- Finish your work and click "Save Changes" or "Preview Changes"

A little dock with the images will appear at the end of the post like in this one.

Remember to thank by me clicking the little star button at the bottom-right of the post if it helped you.  ;)
