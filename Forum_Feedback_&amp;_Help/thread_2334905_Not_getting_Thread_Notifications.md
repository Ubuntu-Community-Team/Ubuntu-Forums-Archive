### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-08-23
Go to **My profile** (Top right of forum page) **General Settings, Default Thread Subscription Mode:** and you can choose to subscribe to the threads in which you post, and in your subscription settings, set the email notification preference.

### Post by coffeecat on 2016-08-25
You can also subscribe to threads individually from the Thread tools dropdown at the top-right of each thread. Under notification type, you can choose notifications in your control panel or from three frequencies of email notification. This is also useful if you want to keep up with threads to which you have not posted.
