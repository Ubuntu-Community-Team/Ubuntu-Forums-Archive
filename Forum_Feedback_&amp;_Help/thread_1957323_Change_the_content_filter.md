### Post by coffeecat on 2012-04-14
Hi - I've had a look at the forum settings and the problem appears to be a limitation of the forum software. Censored strings can be censored *either* as an exact (standalone) word *or* wherever it appears, even as part of a longer word. The 3-letter string you are referring to should only be censored if it appears as a standalone, not as part of a longer word. I guess the preceding dot is the problem and the string is being parsed as if it is an exact word. I can't see any way round this and caps/lower-case won't help. Also - there's no way of whitelisting a string either, as far as I can see.

It's possible that when the upgrade takes place we'll get finer grained control. I'll look at this again then. Thanks for bringing this up.
