### Post by coffeecat on 2016-05-20
Not a Resolution Centre thread.

*Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.* 

From your communication to the Forum Council, it sounds as though you were being logged out between beginning to compose a post and trying to post it. In normal circumstances, if you don't click on the logout link you will remain logged in for 24 hours. Two reasons for being autologged out are browser addons deleting forum cookies, and your external facing IP address changing. If you experience this problem again, and you don't have cookie-munching browser addons installed, check whether your IP changes.

There are several websites where you can check your IP address - this one is as good as any:

[http://www.danasoft.com/](http://www.danasoft.com/)
