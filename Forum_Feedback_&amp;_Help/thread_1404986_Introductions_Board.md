### Post by JackRock on 2010-02-12
The only way there could be a "database issue" is if they have the database locked for editing (in which case the ENTIRE forum is unusable).

However, having designed, implemented and administered several forums in the past for several subjects, I can tell you that whole areas centered around introductions die, and die fast.  You get a ton of people introducing themselves, even though they've been around for years, and a couple of new users.  Then, typically within 4 months, the area slows to a crawl.

BUT, the "I am new here, and I joined because...." posts don't stop.  They just are no longer posted in the correct area - just like now.
