### Post by coffeecat on 2021-04-24
To the first question: mostly no. With the exception of posts from spammers, trolls, and a few other odds and ends, we do not delete posts. (In fact, we don't delete them either, but move them to special areas of the forum where only the poster and forum staff can view a particular post.)

There is an archive section for very old stuff, but that wouldn't affect your post count. As of this writing you have made 27 posts and have a bean count of 25. 25 of your posts were to technical support areas. 2 (including this one) were to non technical support areas, which do not count towards your bean (post) count.

"Bean" is a bit of whimsy that dates back to the founding of the forum - one of many references to coffee. A bean is what you get when you post in a support area.

I don't understand what you mean by "different number being reflecting" but hopefully I have answered your questions. If not, please feel free to clarify.
