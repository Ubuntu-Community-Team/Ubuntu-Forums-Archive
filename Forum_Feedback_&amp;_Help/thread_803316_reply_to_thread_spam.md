### Post by p_quarles on 2008-05-22
> **ballas said:**
> No, because I only get an email and the message in the email is not on the forum itself :(

Maybe a verification code on every posting will stop automated spam-stuff?
The message has already been deleted -- that's why it's not showing up in the thread you got the notice about. There's nothing we can do to stop spam posts from using the "instant e-mail notification" subscription option, though, since we don't know if something is spam until after it's posted.

The problems with CAPTCHAs (the "verification codes"):
1) They pose a for people with any kind of visual problems (and frequently annoy those with good vision)
2) Spammers are beginning to learn how to program bots to crack them
3) Most of the spam we get is not automated

The forum software uses CAPTCHAs for registration, but requiring them for each post would kill the spirit of the board.
