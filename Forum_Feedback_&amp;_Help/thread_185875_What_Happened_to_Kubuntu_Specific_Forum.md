### Post by ubuntu-geek on 2006-06-01
One of the main reasons for using tags from this point forward is the multiple distro's that are being offered off of the main ubuntu tree, Kubuntu, Xubuntu and Edubuntu. Having a seperate category for each one would result in chaos. 

What we are seeing now is phase 1 of the tagging system. Phase 2 will have more filters and search options on the category level.

There are benefits and disadvantages both ways and the tagging seemed to be the best option. I hope that clears some stuff up.
