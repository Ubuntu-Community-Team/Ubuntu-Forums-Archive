Absolutely, all issues should be on launchpad. That's even a criteria for listing an issue on the wiki page!

The motivations for this wiki page are:
- to have a quick view of critical issues, sorted by hardware, before considering installing or upgrading, before searching long reports on launchpad
- launchpad can be a little intimidating especially for new Ubuntu users, and they will not find it easy to search for issues
- the official release notes are reluctant on presenting too many issues, and this wiki page can be a little more comprehensive and honest

The thread also might help forum users to find information in (and discover the usefulness of) launchpad.

See also the EdgyKnowIssues ([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=280231](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=280231)) from last year to get an idea.
