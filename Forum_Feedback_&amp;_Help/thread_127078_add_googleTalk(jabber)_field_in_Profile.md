### Post by mflaig on 2006-12-25
In the profile there is no field to enter a jabber id. 

For the ubuntu forum I think it would be most needed to allow an jabber id entry for a users profile. IMHO Jabber/XMPP is a trustworthy instant messaging system for FOSS people due to it's decentral design and it's documented and open protocol (RFC). It's even quite easy to use and it would be not too tough to even allow reminders on new posts to be sent to the specified jabber id instead of email.

In case you don't already know: google is using Jabber/XMPP for their gtalk, too and their voip addon is an open sourced lib (libjingle) :)

Just think about it ... since the other IM networks often have a clause in there license agreement which says that they allow themselves to do everything with the information you send through their servers. 

MSN, ICQ, AIM, Yahoo, Skype are proprietary protocols with an extremly high market share I think they should be handled like bug #1 ;-)

I hope you'll consider it ...
