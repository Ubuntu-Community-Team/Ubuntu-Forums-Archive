I try to leave my stupid posts, (I find I have more than my share). Sometimes, I'll throw in an edit/addendum to it.

When a post is extraordinary dumb even for me, I will delete it, but leave a reply saying I had deleted it. Like:

That, leaves some semblance of continuity, for others.

When I have had the odd occasion that I didn't need a request for help anymore, or something like that, I have deleted the original post if there were no replies yet, and have edited the title to:  Deleted, staff please remove. Mods and/or admins, have always obliged.

Those ways, I don't make a bad situation worse, by being confusing.
