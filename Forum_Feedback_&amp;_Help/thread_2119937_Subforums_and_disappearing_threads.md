### Post by CharlesA on 2013-02-25
Check what your "Default Thread Age Cut Off" setting is here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editoptions](http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editoptions)

You can also change how far back it goes at the bottom of a subforum under display options.
