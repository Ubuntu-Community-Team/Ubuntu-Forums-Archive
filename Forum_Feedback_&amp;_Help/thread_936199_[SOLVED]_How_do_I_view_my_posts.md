What I think elitenoobboy may be referring to is the different behaviour of some Forum features when one is viewing without being logged-in, as compared to the options that only show up when one is logged-in.  There are quite a few of these.

For example, the 'little green guy' icon that appears on bottom right corner of the envelope to the left of an indexed post title indicating how many posts you have in that thread, only does so if you are logged-in.

Your posting history can also be reached if you are in a thread by clicking on your userID to the left of the post pane.  That gives you a drop-down menu, one of the options of which is "Find more posts by 'userID'".

This works for finding the posting history of any user.

Ubuntu Forums needs a page page, where you can read all about the features that it is not always obvious are before your eyes, doesn't it!

Of course, there is LaRosa's post "Guide to Forum Features" in the stickies at the top of the index page, but that isn't precisely what I was getting at with a page page suggestion.
