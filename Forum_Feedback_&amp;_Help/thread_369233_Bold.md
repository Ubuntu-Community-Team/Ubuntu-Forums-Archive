### Post by PriceChild on 2007-02-24
Bold threads are those with new posts in which you haven't read. Even if you've read half. The icon on the left allows to jump to the first unread post in the thread.

Thread titles in standard text are ones where you have read all posts (or at least had them all displayed on screen)

You may need to refresh for latest changes to take effect.

### Post by Jensor on 2007-02-25
Hey! I finally found out why my read posts were mostly always in bold.  It was because after I read the posts, I mostly always used my back arrow up on the top left of the window to go back to the list of threads.

I discovered by clicking on "Next Thread" near the bottom of the window, or clicking on the path string ("Forum Feedback and Help" on the page you are now looking at) caused the titles in the thread list to change to normal indicating that I had been there.

Therefore, the behavior of the font in the window is dependent upon how one navigates.

Now I understand.  Thanks a lot for your help!
