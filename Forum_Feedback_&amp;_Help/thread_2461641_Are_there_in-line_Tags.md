### Post by &amp;KyT$0P# on 2021-05-04
> **rsteinmetz70112 said:**
> Is there a way in this forum to create inline tags ?

What specific kind of "inline tag" are you seeking to use?

If you mean a way to make mostly-code-like stuff distinctive but still inline, I do
```
[noparse][FONT=Courier New]whatever text[/FONT][/noparse]
```
which in your example would look like:
> They both do the same thing from different directions [FONT=Courier New]writable = yes[/FONT] is the same as [FONT=Courier New]read only = no[/FONT]
