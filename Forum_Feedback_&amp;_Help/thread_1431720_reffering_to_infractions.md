### Post by Giant Speck on 2010-03-17
If you receive an infraction, you should be notified via private message from the moderator or administrator that issued the infraction.  The private message will be titled something to the effect of "You have received a warning/infraction..." and should contain the reason for the warning or infraction, how many points the infraction is worth, and when the infraction expires.

To see a list of the most recent infractions issued to you, simply check your User CP.
