### Post by frodon on 2006-06-28
Go to "Search > Find All Your Threads" or "Search > Find All Your Posts"
In addition you can choose to auto subscibre to a thread when you post in, it's in "Quick Link > Edit Options" then choose "no email notification" and now when you will click on  the "UserCP link" you will see the threads where you have posted before which contain new posts, so it's easy to follow your threads that way.
