### Post by bapoumba on 2013-08-03
You're welcome.
As long as your browser session is logged into Ubuntu One, even if you get logged off the forums because you were idle, it will reconnect when you hit the login with sso link (without re-entering your password).
If you start another session, for ex after a reboot or cleaning cache/cookies (I have not yet got to investigate all the possibilities), you will have to re-enter your login credentials in Ubuntu One. This is how it works for now. There are more urgent points that need to be tweaked, but admins are aware. See here for ex : [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2164503](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2164503)
