### Post by PriceChild on 2007-10-17
Each type also has the powers of those listed below.

Administrators have full control of the board, able to delete users, access or edit your profiles give moderator access, to users and edit the subforums.
Moderators (also called Ubuntu Forum Staff) have access to ip search facilities for investigating trolls, and can hand out infractions to ban users. We have full control over threads across the entire board.
LoCo mods have full control over thread management of their own LoCo forum. They can move a thread anywhere, edit any posts, lock, unlock, sticky, delete, approve etc.
