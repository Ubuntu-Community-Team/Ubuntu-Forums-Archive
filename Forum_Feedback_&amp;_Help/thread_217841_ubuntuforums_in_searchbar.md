### Post by linuksamiko on 2006-08-01
First of all it doesn't matter if you are using dapper or any other distirbution this is a general kde feature (starting with version 3.5 or something like that)

at the top right you have the searchbar (default set to Google)
when you press the littel black arror at the bottom of the google-symbol comes a drop down menu and you can choos "Select Search Engine"

There you have a wide variaty of engines that are "preinstalled" into kde. Just tick the engine that you want to have in your searchbar.

If you want to add a new one you have to go to "NEW" on the right.
