### Post by hellomoto on 2010-06-16
So when I start a thread like this, is there a way can I change a setting in the control panel to email me notifying me of a reply?

---

### Post by cj.surrusco on 2010-06-16
Yes, go to **User CP**, then **Edit Options**, then scroll to where it says "**Default Thread Subscription Mode**" and change it to "**Instant E-mail Notification**".

---

### Post by hellomoto on 2010-06-16
I made the change, but isn't this just for threads that you have subscribed to? How do I set up automatic subscription to threads that I start?

Thankyou for a quick reply

---

### Post by cj.surrusco on 2010-06-16
No, that setting will automatically subscribe you and set up email notification on any thread that you start or post on.
