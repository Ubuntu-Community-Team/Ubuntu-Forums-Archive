### Post by coffeecat on 2019-05-24
Unless you found the setting and changed it within the last half hour, you already had email notification set for automatic thread subscription.

I notice that you changed your email address in your forum account just over a year ago. Are you sure you are looking in the correct inbox?

As a point of information, the location of the global thread subscription setting is Settings link (top right of any page) -> General Settings (on left) -> Default Thread Subscription Mode. Or, to subscribe to individual threads, Thread tools (at top of thread you want to subscribe to) -> Subscribe to this Thread.
