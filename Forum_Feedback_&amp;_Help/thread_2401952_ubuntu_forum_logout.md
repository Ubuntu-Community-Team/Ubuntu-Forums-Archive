### Post by coffeecat on 2018-09-24
Not a technical support question; thread moved to *Forum Feedback & Help*.

To answer your question: no. However, if you don't log out, your session will last 24 hours even if you close your browser. As I usually visit the forum every day, I don't very often have to log in. And, so long as your browser remembers your login credentials at login.ubuntu.com, logging in requires just two mouse clicks, if I remember correctly. 

One thing to remember. If your public-facing IP address changes, you will usually find yourself logged out. That's a feature of the vbulletin software that the forum runs on. If you are in the situation where your ISP changes your IP address regularly, that will affect you, and there's nothing we can do about it.
