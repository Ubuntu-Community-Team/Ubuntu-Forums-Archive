### Post by coffeecat on 2011-06-05
Once you've opened the "Reply to Thread" page, scroll down a bit below the main message field and under "Additional Options" you'll see a Manage Attachments button. You can upload images with that which will appear as clickable thumbnails in your post.

Alternatively, above the message field in the toolbar, look for the [IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/images/editor/attach.gif[/IMG] icon. Click that and you get the same attachments utility.

Good luck!
