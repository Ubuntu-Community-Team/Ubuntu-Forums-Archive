### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2011-05-04
We try not to be too heavy handed and language is dynamic.

"wtf" is a grey acronym, while we all know the full spelling of the f word, wtf is in wide enough use to convey other meanings such as puzzlement.

As has been pointed out in this thread, wtf will certainly offend some, others do not care. Since it does not offend enough people enough of the time we allow the general use of "wtf" and have not (yet) added it to the language filter. Because language is dynamic there may come a time when we feel it would be best to add wtf to the filter, but that time is not today.

Words in the language filter bother enough people enough of the time we have decided to well filter them. The language filter starts with a short list (I'm sure you can imagine the body parts and body functions in the list, :lolflag: ) and we add (and remove) words as necessary.

With that in mind, as with all use of language, if you use "wtf" in an insulting tone or as a personal insult you would receive an infraction , just as with any other insult that does not involve words in the language filter.
