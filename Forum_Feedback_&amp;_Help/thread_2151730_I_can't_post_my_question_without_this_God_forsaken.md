If you are referring to the need for a prefix when submitting a new thread in one of the support areas, see the two screenshots. After clicking on the Post New Thread button, you are presented with the message editor as shown in the first screenshot. Above the title field (which is also mandatory), there is the prefix field. The second screenshot shows some of the choices after you click on the drop-down. Simply choose the prefix that applies to you. If Ubuntu, then "ubuntu". If Kubuntu, then "kubuntu", and so on. The prefix is designed to help you attract the people that have expertise with your Ubuntu variant or desktop  and who can help you.
