### Post by Tomatz on 2008-06-21
I had this problem.

I just disabled ip v6 and it was fine after that ;)

Link below:

[http://ralph.n3rds.net/index.php?/archives/177-How-to-Disable-IPV6-in-Ubuntu.html](http://ralph.n3rds.net/index.php?/archives/177-How-to-Disable-IPV6-in-Ubuntu.html)

P.s 

You may not like vim if not you can use gedit. Just change:
> 
sudo vim /etc/modprobe.d/aliases

to

> sudo gedit /etc/modprobe.d/aliases

:)
