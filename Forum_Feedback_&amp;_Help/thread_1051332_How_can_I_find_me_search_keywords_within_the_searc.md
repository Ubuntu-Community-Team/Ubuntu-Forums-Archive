### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-01-27
There are two methods, one is to use Advanced Search, title only.  There you can specify words or phrases, and even control the number of posts.  Another way to limit is to do your search through google ie: ubuntuforums.org <search terms>  :p

### Post by jenkinbr on 2009-01-27
Correction to the above: use:

**site:**ubuntuforums.org <search terms>

that will force google to list entries for ubuntuforums.org only.
