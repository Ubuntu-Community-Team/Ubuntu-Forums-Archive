### Post by flamacue on 2008-12-28
Why does the ubuntuforums search include all posts with *any* of the terms in the search field instead of *all* of them?  (i.e. Why does it search for "this OR that" when I want to search for "this AND that"?)  This just decreases the signal-to-noise ratio by showing multiple posts that are unhelpful to me.

I would find this feature immensely more useful if it would search for all terms.  Is there even a way to do this?

Thanks for your consideration.

---

### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-12-29
You can use the + sign to use AND searching. It's a pain, but it works.

For example, searching for **dapper install** would search dapper OR install, while **+dapper +install** would search for both terms in the same post. (The search system still shows the searched keywords as OR, but the results do come up correctly.)
