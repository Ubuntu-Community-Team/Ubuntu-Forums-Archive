### Post by doas777 on 2011-06-25
@OP,
There is a reason the Internet DNS system has a authority hierarchy, and it is designed to prevent individual server operators from inserting their DNS servers in queries to others. 
you appear to be attempting to circumvent that protection, and get others to use your dns servers, despite a lack of authority to do so. 

the only responsible response to your question, is to tell you not to do that. there is NO service on the internet you can use to advertise your dns server. this is not about governmental law as you implied in your DVD ripping jab, but about internet self governance. without such rules, the internet would be rent asunder.

if you wish your clients to make lookups via your dns server, you will have to either instruct them to configure it in your support documentation, or create an application to configure it for them.
