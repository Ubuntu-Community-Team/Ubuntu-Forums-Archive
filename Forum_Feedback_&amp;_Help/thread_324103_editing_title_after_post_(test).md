### Post by matthew on 2006-12-23
> **rajeev1204 said:**
> 1)i want to know how to edit a title after i post it.

i tried doing that in a recent post under go advanced options , but it doesnt seem to changeYou are on the right track. Edit->Advanced->change the title. If it is the first post in a thread it will also change the title that appears in the thread listing.

> **rajeev1204 said:**
>  2)what do things like first cup or flaming beta roaster mean . experience based on number of posts? They're just for fun. Click the Forum FAQ link in my signature...there's an entry about this that will give you more info.

> **rajeev1204 said:**
>  3) how do i know whose advice to follow in forums? do u check if a particular solution posted actually works?That's a good question. Generally if a solution has been posted for a while and has received feedback you will know it works. The original poster of a thread can also mark the thread [resolved] by going to Edit->Advanced and checking the box, so that's a good way if you see it done. Otherwise there isn't a good method. Everyone here, staff included, are volunteers and while the help received in these forums is usually quite good it isn't authoritative nor is it sponsored/provided by Ubuntu developers, at least not usually.
