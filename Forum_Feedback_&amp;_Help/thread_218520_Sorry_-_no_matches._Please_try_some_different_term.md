### Post by matthew on 2006-07-18
I just tested this out. I think the reason is that the list of unanswered posts is generated when you click on the link and it takes you to the first page. If no one posts a new thread while you are there everything works out fine, you can go from page one to two to three, all the way to page twenty with no problems. As soon as someone posts a new thread, however, a new unanswered thread will appear and it messes up how the algorithm calculates the pages. I have no idea how to or even if this can be fixed. The same thing happens in the various forums if you try to page through posts, it just doesn't happen as often there because every thread starts out as unanswered, so therefore as soon as a new thread is made anywhere the unanswered posts list will change, making the paging scheme freak out for those trying to page through.

What I will suggest is not ideal, but will work when you encounter this. Click "Unanswered Posts." Look quickly at the first page, then choose whatever page after that you want to see. If/when you get the error, go to the first page again, note the new threads, then pick whatever page you were on. 

Tedious? A little. I wish I had a better idea. We really appreciate people like you who use the function. I'm sure an admin will look into if it is even possible to change/fix this issue. Until then, at least you have an idea of why.
