### Post by scragar on 2008-04-29
Is there no way to make it so people can reply to messages(to add just a touch more info), without then robbing this thread of it's unreplied status that so vastly increases the chances of a reply?

I have seen this several times now, where people have replied to threads to add nothing that get's it solved, and the thread then falls down the lists on the page because replying to unanswered threads in theory offers the best chance of being helpful at all. It also goes the other way, if someone makes a thread asking for help, and I can see a possible solution but it's outside of my experise for the most part am I expected to post a reply, hoping this fixes it, but knowing that if it doesn't the persons odds of a working reply fall? or maybe I should just leave the thread and hope that someone else notices it? neither option sounds particularly nice to me.

I know given the fact that these are vbulletin board this would be very hard, and highly unfeasable, but I just thought the idea should be considered.
