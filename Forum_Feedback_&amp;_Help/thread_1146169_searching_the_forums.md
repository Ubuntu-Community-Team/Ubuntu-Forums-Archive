### Post by drs305 on 2009-05-02
What you may be doing is entering a search term and then hitting a time frame (Last Hour, 2 Hours, etc). It's a bit misleading, but if you do it that way you will get all the posts from the past time frame and it won't look at your search criteria in the selection box.

If you want to search for a specific term/phrase AND a time frame, go to Search, Advanced Search, enter your words and then select a time period mid-way down the left panel. 
Added: Otherwise, from the main Search link, enter your search term and press the "Go" button to the right.

And welcome to the Ubuntu forums. :-)
