### Post by aysiu on 2007-05-06
Click on **Go Advanced** (right below the message text box)

Scroll down to **Additional Options** > **Attach Files**

Then click **Manage Attachments**

A new window should open where you can **Browse** for the image file on your computer and then **Upload** it to the Ubuntu Forums.
