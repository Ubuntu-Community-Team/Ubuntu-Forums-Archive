### Post by forrestcupp on 2010-01-11
The search can go back that far if you set it to.

Click on "User CP" toward the top left of this page.  Then click "Edit Options".  Under the "Thread Display Options" section, set "Default Thread Age Cut Off" to "Show all threads".  Then click the "Save Changes" button at the bottom.

After you do that, your search results will go all the way back to when the forum began.
