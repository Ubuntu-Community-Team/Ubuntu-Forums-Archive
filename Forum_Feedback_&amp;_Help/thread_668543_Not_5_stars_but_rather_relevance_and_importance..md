I am thinking of having something similar implemented that is based on relevance  and importance of a thread. This is more bias towards how to's and solving problems.

 This rating is now based on how much you like a thread but more on how useful it is for problem solving. I also kind of like the idea of having both relevance and importance ratings as one rates how important it is to the title and content and the other importance relates to how useful it is for solving that particular problem pertaining to that subject.

 Now this can be done in other ways but the basic formula is to make a rating system that strays away from popularity and Implements a rating on usefulness,
