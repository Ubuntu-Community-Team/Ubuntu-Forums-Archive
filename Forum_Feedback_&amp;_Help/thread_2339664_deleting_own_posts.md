### Post by QIII on 2016-10-11
No, you may not delete your own posts.  Only the Forums Staff may "delete" posts by moving them out of public view to The Jail.

This is necessary to avoid causing indexing problems and orphaned records in the forum database.  It also keeps users from removing posts in such a manner as to leave threads without continuity and context.  If you edit your own post with a period and that renders the thread incomprehensible, we will often restore the original text.

If you want a post deleted, a duplicate for instance, simply use the report button to ask the Staff to clean up.  We will decide what to do.
