### Post by coffeecat on 2011-12-05
> **tillie said:**
> Because the last few weeks, after installing linux, and talking to people about it I met a lot of people with criticism, including myself, but you can't find that criticism here on the internet.

Linux or Ubuntu? Can't find criticism of Linux or Ubuntu on the internet? Have another look. There's plenty of criticism in blogs and tech news pages, running in a spectrum from useful constructive criticism through misinformed grumbling to outright FUD.

> **tillie said:**
> Probably because there hasn't been a place to vent the criticism.

It depends what you mean by "criticism" and "vent". Venting is the same metaphor as "blowing off steam" which means getting rid of pent-up anger. The forum membership, mostly ordinary Ubuntu users who enjoy helping each other with technical issues, would probably not be interested in listening to people grumbling, but may be interested in well thought-out and constructive evaluations (to use Lisati's word) of Ubuntu from newcomers. And for that you have the Testimonials section, and for general discussions, the Community Cafe.
