### Post by Sidewinder1 on 2009-09-07
As cariboo said, your searches look correct, they are listed "newest first" that is so that you receive the most current results from a search. Individual threads, however, can be harder to follow that way and are usually displayed "oldest first" , which would be chronological order. If the threads need adjustment, at the top of your page go to: User CP---->Edit Options----> then scroll down to Thread Display Options, then, in Thread Display Mode, make sure it says "Linear - Oldest First.
That's it, you should be good to go.
HTH,
Side
