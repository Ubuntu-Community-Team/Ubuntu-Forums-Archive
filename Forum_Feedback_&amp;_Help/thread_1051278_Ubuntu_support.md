Go to  "user CP" then under "Settings and Options" select "Edit Options" and under "Messaging and Notifications" tick the box for "Receive Email Notification of New Private Messages"

If you are wanting email every time you subscribe to a thread you can make the adjustments under "Default Thread Subscription Mode" of the "Settings and Options" menu.
