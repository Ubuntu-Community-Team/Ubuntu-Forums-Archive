### Post by munkyeetr on 2008-04-07
What do you find is the most efficient way to keep updated on the threads you are actively participating in?

When I post on a thread, I find I am constantly checking back to see if there's been another post on the thread. Currently I find the best way is to use:
> 
**Search** -> **Advanced Search**
Then under **Search by User Name** I enter my own name

I have also tried using:
> 
**Search** -> **Find All Your Posts**

but it doesn't really help in the same way; it doesn't alert me to a new post.

Any suggestions? Insight?
