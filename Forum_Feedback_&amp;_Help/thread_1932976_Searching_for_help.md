### Post by Ms. Daisy on 2012-02-28
Are you looking at the date of the thread you're reviewing? Something from 2009 may not apply today.  Also, most threads mention which version they're working with.  If they don't, then you should assume they're talking about the latest version as of the date of the thread.

I think the review you're suggesting falls into your lap. It's important to keep old threads as they are because people run different versions of Ubuntu.  So a thread that discusses 10.04 is still relevant to some people today, but it may not apply to your specific system running (say) 11.10.
