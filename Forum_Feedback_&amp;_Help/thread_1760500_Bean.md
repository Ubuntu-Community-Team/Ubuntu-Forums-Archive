You get a bean for each post you make on the support section of the forums. No beans are given for posts in the "Community cafe" and "cafe games" or Forum Feedback and Help though.

Beans = Post count.

You can read more about them at [[COLOR=Blue]http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=239560[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=239560"). They are not really important, more just a bit of fun etc. 

Some people like myself hide their beans so others don't put any emphasis on our bean count in relation to advice given.
