### Post by Forrest Gumpp on 2008-03-07
Here is a thought I had in relation to dealing with a somewhat similar problem in an Australian setting:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=4465663&postcount=55](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=4465663&postcount=55)

Grudge Switching - tell the world why.  On record.

The thing about grudge switching, if it is realistic and sensible in your particular situation, is that it conveys a simple commercial message in brutal terms, in public.

The threat perceived by the recalcitrant provider is that there may get to be a snowballing effect among other customers, to that particular ISP's detriment.

It is but a suggestion that the above thread is the place to tell such a story, but it could become so.
