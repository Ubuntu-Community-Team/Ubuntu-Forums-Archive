### Post by TheFu on 2012-12-28
> **VbProg said:**
> hi all. I want to ask a new question.How can I do that?

Besides all the responses before this, you might want to read [http://www.gerv.net/hacking/how-to-ask-good-questions/](http://www.gerv.net/hacking/how-to-ask-good-questions/) How To Ask A Good Question ... so your question will provide as much data and facts for people here to be able to answer.

Also, please, please, please choose a useful title, since that is what we use to filter out questions we may know something about. Selecting a good title is hard.  Too specific and nobody answers, too wide and everyone answering doesn't have specific enough knowledge.

In short, look at the FAQ, the man pages, error messages AND the log files (/var/log) before you post a question. Almost always, the log files and error messages will provide a hint to what is wrong.
