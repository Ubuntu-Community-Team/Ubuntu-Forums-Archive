### Post by coffeecat on 2016-04-02
Since there is no particular issue with your account, I have moved this thread from the Resolution Centre to Forum Feedback & Help. The bean counter for your account is not stuck, for the reason Bucky Ball has explained. You have 9 posts altogether, only three of which are in support sub-forums.
