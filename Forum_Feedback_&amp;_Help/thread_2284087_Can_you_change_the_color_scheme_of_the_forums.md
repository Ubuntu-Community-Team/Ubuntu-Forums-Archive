### Post by coffeecat on 2015-06-27
Quick answer to your thread title question: no.

This forum is the official one for Ubuntu, is paid for and maintained by Canonical, and the theming on this site follows the Ubuntu design guidelines here: [http://design.ubuntu.com/](http://design.ubuntu.com/)

However, it is possible to create a different colour scheme for this site for your own use by using the the browser add-on Stylish.

Stylish for Firefox: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-us/firefox/addon/stylish/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-us/firefox/addon/stylish/)

Stylish for Chrome/Chromium: [https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/stylish/fjnbnpbmkenffdnngjfgmeleoegfcffe?hl=en](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/stylish/fjnbnpbmkenffdnngjfgmeleoegfcffe?hl=en)

There are even some pre-prepared themes and skins specifically for Ubuntuforums available here: [https://userstyles.org/styles/browse/ubuntuforums](https://userstyles.org/styles/browse/ubuntuforums)

A word of caution about the last. Any in that list dated prior to 2013 were designed for earlier versions of vBulletin and results will be unpredictable or unusable now. We updated the forum to v4 of vBulletin in early 2013 and anything later than this will be as good as the submitter made it. There are only a few which are 2013 and later and even a couple of those are variations on the Ubuntu orange theme.
