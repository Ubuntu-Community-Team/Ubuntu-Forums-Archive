### Post by aysiu on 2006-07-19
Hundreds of *views* don't mean it's a common problem.

Hundreds of *threads* might mean it.

If people are viewing something, it means they're seeing what you wrote and intending to help. If they see they can't help (i.e., the problem is obscure or outside their level of expertise), then they leave without posting, "Hi, I wanted to help, but I have nothing to say."

For more details on this phenomenon, read [this thread](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=82471).
