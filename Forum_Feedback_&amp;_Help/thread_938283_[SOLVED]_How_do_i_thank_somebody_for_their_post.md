### Post by lisati on 2008-10-04
One way is to click on the little blue/yellow medal at the bottom right of their post.

To find out where you've been thanked, click on your user name in the welcome message at the top-right of the screen, click on "show all statistic" when it appears, and the click on the "find all thanked posts" link on the left of the screen.
