### Post by aysiu on 2006-03-04
The issue isn't how long the *post* takes to compose--the issue is how long the *poll* takes to compose.

Step 1: Create post (no one sees this until you press **Submit Thread** button)

Step 2: Create poll. While you're creating the poll options, everyone sees a thread that refers to a poll that doesn't exist.

I've had people respond to my poll initial post before I've finished making the poll. It's odd.
