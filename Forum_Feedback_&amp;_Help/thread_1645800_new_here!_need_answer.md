### Post by amsterdamharu on 2010-12-15
> do i create a new thread or do i post in that thread?
I guess that depends on how old the thread is, no point in reviving a months old thread.

You can post something like ... and ... and ... did not work for me, I get ... and ... and ... but you can't post completely unrelated stuff in the thread because that's considered hijacking.

To find your posts and see if someone replied to it I go to:
user CP -> [Edit Options]("http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editoptions") -> under "Default Thread Subscription Mode 	" -> choose anything but "do not subscribe"

Now whenever you post something if it's a new thread or just a post reply to someone else you will subscribe to it and you'll find it under [User CP]("http://ubuntuforums.org/usercp.php") -> [View all Subscribed Threads]("http://ubuntuforums.org/subscription.php?do=viewsubscription&folderid=all")
