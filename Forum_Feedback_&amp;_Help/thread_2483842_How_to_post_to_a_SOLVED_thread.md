### Post by coffeecat on 2023-02-10
*Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

How were you trying to add a bit more information? By means of a new post, or by editing an existing post? There's nothing mysterious or different about a SOLVED thread in terms of posting. The solved tag is simply a tag. It doesn't prevent anyone from adding to the thread, and the tag can be removed by the OP.

You've posted new posts before, so I don't need to go over the process for that. In order to edit a pre-existing post simply click on the edit post button, do whatever edit you want, and don't forget to click on the save button when you are done.
