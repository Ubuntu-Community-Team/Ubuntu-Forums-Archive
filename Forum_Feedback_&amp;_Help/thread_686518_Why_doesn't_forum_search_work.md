### Post by jaytek13 on 2008-02-03
When you do a "search" on a new post topic and it bring up the matches underneath, it is searching through the topic titles.

When you do a "quick search" from the drop down box it searches everything for matching keywords.

If you click on search, and then click on advanced, you will see a drop down box underneath the search that allows you to search through post titles only. That will bring up the results you got from the "new post search."
