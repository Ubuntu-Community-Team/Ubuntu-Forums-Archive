### Post by kassetra on 2005-04-13
[QUOTE=muzza]Is it just my computer, or does anybody else's scroll wheel not work when writing a reply / new thread?
[/QUOTE]

For some versions of browsers:
If your cursor is in the text box, you will not be able to scroll - you first have to click outside of the text box in order to scroll.  The reason for this is that by typing in the text box, you've given the text box the "focus" ... so all mouse movements outside of resetting the focus somewhere else respond inside of that box.
