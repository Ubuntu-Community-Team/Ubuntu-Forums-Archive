### Post by Cool Javelin on 2011-12-22
Having an option to delete a thread would be good. It would trim a lot of excess fat.

Yes, alienclone, in a perfect world, people would do more research and perhaps find the answer, but sometimes frustration lends itself to short searching and quick "help me" posts.

After some cooling time, my guess is more then a few of us find we've posted prematurely wishing for a delete thread option.

Actually, I am guilty of this once or twice.  :roll:

### Post by Iowan on 2011-12-22
From Code of Conduct:
> If a post is older than a year or so and hasn't had a new reply in that time, instead of replying to it, create a new thread. In the software world, a lot can change in a very short time, and doing things this way makes it more likely that you will find the best information. You may link to the original discussion in the new thread if you think it may be helpful.
