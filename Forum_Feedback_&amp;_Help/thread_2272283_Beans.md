### Post by QIII on 2015-04-05
Hello!

Beans are a count of the posts users have made in the ***support*** areas of the forum.  Posts made in other areas, such as the Cafe, do not earn beans.

Beans should not be taken too seriously -- except that the Staff uses them early on for new users to decide when to allow new users to change their profiles.  That happens at 10 beans right now.

The "ranks" that go along with the bean count are completely arbitrary and add a bit of whimsy.  

A bit of warning is in order, however:  Don't try to pump up you bean count by making meaningless or hollow posts.  We are a bit grumpy about that and will remove the posts from public view and reduce the bean count.  Making a habit of it might precipitate more serious action.

So have fun.  Help where you can.  Ask questions where you need to.  Beans will come in due time.

Cheers!
