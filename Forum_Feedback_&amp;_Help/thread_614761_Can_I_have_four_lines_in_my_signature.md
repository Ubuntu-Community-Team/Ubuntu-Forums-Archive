### Post by matthew on 2007-11-18
Sorry I missed this the first time around. This is actually a small glitch in the vBulletin software. It counts all the characters in a signature, including markup for bold, colors, etc., when calculating length, and sometimes it mysteriously considers 3 line sigs as though they have 4 lines. We have not found any solution...sorry.
