### Post by coffeecat on 2014-09-03
The prefix has disappeared because your thread has been moved to Forum feedback & Help which is the proper place for questions about using the forum. Specific desktop or flavour prefixes are not relevant for Forum Feedback threads. The General help section of this forum is for:

> All your general support questions for Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Edubuntu, Xubuntu, Lubuntu and UbuntuGnome.

By general support, we mean **technical** support.

The Kubuntu community set up their own forum at kubuntforums.net which, as far as I am aware, is nothing to do with Canonical. They have every right to maintain their own independent forum, and we wish them well. The [kubuntu.org community webpage]("http://www.kubuntu.org/community") also points to us here, although they have got it wrong by referring to "the Kubuntu related areas", but there is nothing we here can do about that. If the Lubuntu people wished to set up their own independent forum, I'm sure they could do so and we would wish them well too, but in the meantime it appears they link to here on their website. As does xubuntu.org, and I'm sure the others do as well, but I haven't checked them.

This is the official forum for Ubuntu, paid for by Canonical, but as the official flavours are part of the family, users of Kubuntu, Xubuntu, Edubuntu, Lubuntu, UbuntuGnome, Ubuntu Studio, and Mythbuntu are very welcome here too.
