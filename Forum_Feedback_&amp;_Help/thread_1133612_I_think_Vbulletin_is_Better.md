I generally frequent two forums, here(Vbulletin) and Neowin.net(IPB) as person has set up and maintained websites in the past I have installed and administered both IPB and Vbulletin. So I am familier with both systems. 

I just want to say that I commend the Ubuntu forums for choosing Vbulletin, from a users perspective, Vbulletin in my opinion has much more to offer. Anyway just some random thoughts ;)
