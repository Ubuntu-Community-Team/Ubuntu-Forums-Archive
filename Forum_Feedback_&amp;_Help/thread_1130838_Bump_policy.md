### Post by bapoumba on 2009-04-20
It is more a recommendation and good practice than a real guideline. We have already discussed it and did not feel it needed a mention in CoC. You can find it in one of the stickies in FFH:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=885580](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=885580)

It would be hard to draw a line. Someone bumping their thread to keep it up on the top page is clearly annoying and this is what we try to avoid. Once every 24h is a good period to bump. Remember that after a bump, a thread has a reply and does not show up in the unanswered posts any longer.
