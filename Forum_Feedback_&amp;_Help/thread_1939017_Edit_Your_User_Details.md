### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-03-10
You may not have permission right away after getting 50 posts . Select the report abuse button to alert a mod or go to to forum feedback and help. when the permissions are activated you will be able to make changes. 

If permissions are activated select your user name on the top right and select user cp.

### Post by lisati on 2012-03-14
The script that updates user permissions runs hourly. You need to wait a while after reaching the required number of posts before you are able to update your details, usually about an hour or so. After three days, you should be fine.
