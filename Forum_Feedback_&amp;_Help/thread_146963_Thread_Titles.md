### Post by 5-HT on 2006-03-19
When you see bold threads, AFAIK they represent new threads since your last login.
Not sure if you can override this and create a bold face title permanently, or that it would be a good idea to do so if you could.

Maybe it's the latent cynic in me, but I think that (along with other things like mass exclamation marks, etc) may lead to a thread title race whereby everyone tries to outdo the other in order to stand out and grab attention at the expense of other equally deserving threads...only leading to annoyance, and people possibly avoiding such threads because of it.

If you want to get rid of the bold, Quick Links> Mark Forums Read should do the trick.

HTH
