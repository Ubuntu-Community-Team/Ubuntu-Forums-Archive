### Post by aesis05401 on 2009-10-12
Slashdot allots moderator points on a rotating basis to established users in good standing.  Each user can also set their viewing thresh-hold, so posts that drop below a particular thresh-hold are not displayed.  Finally, users can choose to boost types of comments and comments made by particular users so that the comment results usually include particular types of comments and comments from users they like. 

They are currently working with a new system that is designed to make the selection of stories and the moderation of comments into an even more distributed system, but there are still some problems.  

Unfortunately, using the slash system requires a dedicated team to down-mod the trolls until your user base is large enough to handle it, and you need to start user comments pretty low and rely on your community members using their points wisely to form the conversation.  So there is still a good investment in keeping things running.
