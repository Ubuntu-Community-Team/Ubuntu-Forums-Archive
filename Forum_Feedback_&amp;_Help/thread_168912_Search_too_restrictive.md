### Post by tht00 on 2006-05-01
I've found that the search is far too restricive here, killing several search terms that are 4 characters or less in length.

A simple search for 'best programs' killed the term 'best' and gave back to me the results for 'programs', which wasn't useful at all.

Granted, there is a fine line here -- too many small terms in a search will kill useful results; however, at the same time, taking out terms can provide equally inconvenient/useless results.  

I could understand having it set this way if there was a way to force it to search for that term as well, but I haven't found any.  In this case and IMHO, it'd be better to err on the side of a less restrictive search.
