### Post by coffeecat on 2014-08-06
> **bulrush2 said:**
> ow can I get the forum to use the Enhanced (Advanced) editor on replies also without having to click the Advanced button?

You click on either the "Go advanced" large orange button under the quick reply message editor, or the black "adv reply" in any of the previous posts. That's how it works. The quick reply message editor is visible as a convenience to those who want to make a - well - quick reply.

This is a fairly common setup for forums running on vbulletin. If the quick reply message editor wasn't there, such as in forums running phpBB, you'd still have to click on something in order to post.
