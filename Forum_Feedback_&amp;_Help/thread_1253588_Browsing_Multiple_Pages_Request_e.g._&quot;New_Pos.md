### Post by DaithiF on 2009-09-01
slightly off-topic to your request, but if you're using Firefox then something I find very useful is the AutoPager extension.  As you scroll towards the bottom of page1 it automatically loads page2 for you, and as you get towards the bottom of page 2, it loads page 3, etc.  So no need to click any of the paging links, and it saves time by loading the next page slightly ahead of time, so that the request has usually completed by the time you get there.
