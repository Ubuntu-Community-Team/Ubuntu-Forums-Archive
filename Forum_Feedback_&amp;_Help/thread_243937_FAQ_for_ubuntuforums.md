### Post by benfindlay on 2006-08-25
I wouldn't worry about duplicating questions. Everyone is usually more than happy to answer any questions you have. All I'd suggest is to try the SEARCH function on the main page first. If you can't find it there, then it probably hasnt been answered yet!

There are plenty of HOW-TOs, cunningly hidden in the HOW-TO section ;) which I personally look at quite a lot; there are some ace ones that talk you through EVERYTHING you need to do to get a certain thing to work (samba and vnc notably for myself, cheers guys!).

As for FAQs, there are sticky posts at the top of the Absolute Beginners area of the boars which cover lots of issues too.

So don't be afraid to ask anything, no-one is seriously gonna have a go at you for asking something some-one else asked a while ago!
