### Post by callan79 on 2009-02-07
> **Eldera said:**
> Specifically: How does one put a quote from another post into one's own post?


Hi Eldera,

Welcome to the forums.

Regarding the quotes, like above, that's easy. Can you see the 'quote' button at the bottom right of this post? Well, just click that and it'll quote my entire post.

Be aware though, it's good practice to remove any useless text - like I have done above. You just delete what you don't need.

Effectively, you just put your quote between a (QUOTE) and (/QUOTE) tags, but replace the ( and ) with the square brackets [ and ].

Regarding links, it's the same idea.

You put (URL=http://www.something.com) CLICK HERE (/URL), again replace ( and ) with [ and ].

Hope this helps :)

Cheers
Callan
