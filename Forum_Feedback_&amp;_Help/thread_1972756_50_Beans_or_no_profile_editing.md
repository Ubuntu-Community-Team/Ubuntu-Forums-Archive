### Post by The Cog on 2012-05-04
It is the forum spammers that have forced that action upon us. My understanding is that they will place links to web pages they are trying to promote in their personal profiles. Then they post lots of unhelpful garbage posts just to make sure their profile (with web links) gets seen on lots of threads by the search engine crawlers.
