### Post by Joeb454 on 2008-06-02
Keep posting in the support forums, and your bean count & title will increase.

You should note that these aren't levels as such, it's just a fun little thing. The only difference is at 3,500 posts, you can change your user title.

The only "levels" that exist are becoming a LoCo Moderator, Global Forum Moderator, or an Admin :)
