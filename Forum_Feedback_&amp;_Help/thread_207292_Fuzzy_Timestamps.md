### Post by Lux Perpetua on 2006-07-01
I personally am not a fan of the fuzzy timestamps on posts in this forum, and I never have been. I don't want to see that a post was made "5 hours ago"; I want to see that it was made at 6:21 AM on July 1, 2006. 

I've gone to the User CP, but I did not see an option to change this. I think it would be ideal if users could set this behavior for themselves via the control panel, so fuzzy users could see their fuzzy timestamps and the rest of us could have our normal timestamps. 

The poll is partially for curiosity's sake, but it could perhaps also be informative (if enough people vote).

P. S. Just to be clear, **fuzzy** timestamps are of the form "2 minutes ago" or "2 hours ago" or "2 days ago" or "2 weeks ago," etc. **Traditional** timestamps just give the date and time, like "June 29, 2006, 11:56 PM."
