### Post by unutbu on 2009-05-01
[list]
[*]Click the "User CP" button in the upper left corner.
Click Edit Options in the middle of the left-hand panel.
Go to "Default Thread Subscription Mode.
Select any option other than "Do not subscribe", and you will be automatically subscribed to any thread in which you post. 
If anyone replies to your thread, you will be notified there is a new post when you click the "User CP" button.

[*]Another way is to click your username. This takes you to your Profile page.
Then click the Statistics button.
Click the "Find all threads started by ..." link.
[/list]
