### Post by matthew on 2009-05-12
I serve on one of the membership approval boards. If you have testimonials from staff, either on your wiki or in person, that are clear and can show a sustained and genuine contribution to the Ubuntu community through your forum work, you will be accepted. It is better if you have forum staff who are ***also*** Ubuntu Members (that is most of us) and are known to the membership board reviewing your aplication to give testimonials on your behalf.

Your idea is a good one. We have the right, in theory, as the Forum Council to give membership to Forum community members, however the details have never been worked out. In the meanwhile, use my first paragraph as a guide.
