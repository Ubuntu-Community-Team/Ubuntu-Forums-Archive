### Post by Forrest Gumpp on 2008-03-10
@sajro

You say that you have made no screen resolution changes.I note, however, that you are using Ubuntu 7.10.  So am I, and one thing that I have experienced on several occasions is that despite having set my screen resolution to 1024 X 768, upon boot-up the display will revert to the default resolution.

When this happens, I can obtain the screen resolution dialog and reset to 1024 X 768, **but no change to resolution takes place** when I click 'Apply'.  Rebooting seems to fix the problem.

Rebooting also seems to fix the problem without first calling up the screen resolution dialog.

Should it be that the resolution you are using is not greatly different from the default, it may be that this behaviour has occurred on your computer, but you have not noticed it straight away.

You may have in fact had a screen resolution change, but one that you did not initiate.

Just a thought.
