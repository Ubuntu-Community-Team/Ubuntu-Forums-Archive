### Post by x33a on 2010-01-01
First of all you should have a fair grasp over the subject to be able to write any tutorials.

Then, ideas for tutorials should come from your own experiences (like fixing some problem), and you feel that it will benefit other people.

Also, you should make sure that the topic hasn't been covered in the past by someone else. use forums search for that.

And, in the end, good writing skills would be a nice addon.

note: These are my personal views, and i am not any authority to decide on any matters (apart from my own :P).
