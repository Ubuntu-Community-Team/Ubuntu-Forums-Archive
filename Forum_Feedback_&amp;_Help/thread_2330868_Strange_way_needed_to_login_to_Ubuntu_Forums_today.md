### Post by mikodo on 2016-07-17
I'll mark this as solved for me. I am able to log into the forums as before.

Just a note about what is happening for me. I had never used the stock Private Viewing Mode *always. I prefer setting my own in about:config. Since v47 FF, I have been struggling to completely stop FF from saving them in about:config. Since the changes in UF these last days when I couldn't log in the normal way, what I have done is keep the about:config settings I still have but, reverted to the stock Private Viewing Mode *always again, to have it not save any history. I don't believe this is ideal as, FF remembers if I have any other instances of FF open. In doing so though, I have no problems logging into UF at any time anymore.

So, if I close *all instances of FF and then go to UF I need to re-enter my credentials. But, if I have any other instances of FF open and I close UF and open another instance of FF and go to the forums, FF *remembers then and auto logs me into the forums. With this one must close all instances of FF before it "forgets history".

Here is how I am enforcing Private Browsing mode in the stock way: 
[https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/private-browsing-use-firefox-without-history#w_can-i-set-firefox-to-always-use-private-browsing](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/private-browsing-use-firefox-without-history#w_can-i-set-firefox-to-always-use-private-browsing)
