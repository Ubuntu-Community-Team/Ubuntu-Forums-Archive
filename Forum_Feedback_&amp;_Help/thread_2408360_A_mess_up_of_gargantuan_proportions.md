### Post by ajgreeny on 2018-12-17
I have done that as well a few times and if it's a long post I've just written, it's very annoying!

The first thing to do is simply hit F5 to reload the page, when you may find that quite a lot, if not all, of your post has already been autosaved.

Look for the red coloured info text at the bottom left of the text entry box, "Restore autosaved text" or similar will show, but only once you have reloaded the page, so just click on that.
