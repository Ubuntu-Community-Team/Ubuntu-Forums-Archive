### Post by matthew on 2007-05-21
All reported posts are listed in a special forum just for staff. As soon as any of us are able to look into the issue, we do. Sometimes it happens really fast, sometimes it takes a while, solely dependent on how busy each of the staff are in real life.

We don't usually PM users who make the reports.
