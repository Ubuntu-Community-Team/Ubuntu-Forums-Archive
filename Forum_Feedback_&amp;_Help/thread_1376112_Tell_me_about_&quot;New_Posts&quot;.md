### Post by garryknight on 2010-01-08
> **Chris Edgell said:**
> So is that what it is?  If it's my thread that information will be elsewhere?

It *is* elsewhere, but that's not why it doesn't appear. When you click New Posts, you get a list of all of the new posts that you haven't read. And you've read the post you wrote. Next time someone posts a reply to it, it will show up in New Posts because the thread contains a post you haven't read yet. Makes sense?

The "New Posts" button will display the first 25 most recent posts throughout the **entire** forum. If your thread doesn't show up - that is because no one has replied to it for longer than the bottom entry (which - given these forums busyness, is not that difficult - and can easily slip off the page in 5-10 minutes.
