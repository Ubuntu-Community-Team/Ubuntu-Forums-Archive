### Post by coffeecat on 2019-10-25
You are confusing your forum account with your Ubuntu One Single Sign-On account. Now that you have changed your forum account email address and re-activated your forum account from the verification email, notifications will go to your new email.

When you log in, you do so from your Ubuntu One account. Presumably, your Ubuntu One account preferred email is the old one. That will not affect forum notifications. If you want to change your preferred email in Ubuntu One, you need to go to [https://login.ubuntu.com/](https://login.ubuntu.com/) . Please be aware that email addresses in your Ubuntu One account and your forum account do not have to be the same. Use whatever works for you. An email match is only needed for when your forum account is first created or when a forum account created prior to July 2013 is linked to an Ubuntu One account. The link between a forum account and the linked Ubuntu One account can only be removed by a forum admin.
