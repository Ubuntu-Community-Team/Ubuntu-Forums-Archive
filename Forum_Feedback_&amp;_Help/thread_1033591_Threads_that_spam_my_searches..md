### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-01-07
When you do a basic search in Ubuntu Forums, it looks for your terms in the entirety of the thread.  To get better results:

There are 2 methods:

1. google:  ubuntuforums.org <your problem>  this gives more precise results

2. use advance search, <your terms> title only selects only threads with your terms in the title.  :P
