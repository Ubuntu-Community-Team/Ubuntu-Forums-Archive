### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-08-22
Actually ....

We do have an Unanswered posts team.

Most of these unanswered posts are thus not ignored in any way and bumping them can be detrimental.

The UA team searches for posts with no answers, and if you bum your thread it will not be found by the search.

Sorry, there is no simpler way to identify unanswered posts :(

With that said, I understand sometimes bumping can seem productive. You will get different answers, I would say wait 2-3 days before bumping your thread and bumping more then once every 24 hours is considered by most rude.

If your thread is unanswered, you have other options. Google and IRC.
