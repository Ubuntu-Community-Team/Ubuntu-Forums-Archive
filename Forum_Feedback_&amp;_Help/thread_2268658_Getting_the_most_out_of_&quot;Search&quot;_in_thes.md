### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-03-10
The forum search software does have some issues.  In the "Advanced Search" page, you need to select "Search Title Only" which is a pulldown list to the right of the "Keyword" box.  This type of search will find the word "purge" in the titles and bring up a bunch of threads.  Sometimes thread titles get changed, so the list will have some that do not contain "purge" but it could be in a history file or in some other metadata.

For casual use, just ask a question to Google:  How do I purge 12.04?  And a bunch of responses (many from these forums) will show up, with a page rank based on popularity.  I find this much quicker than wading through thousands of meaningless posts that get selected through the forum's search algorithm.
