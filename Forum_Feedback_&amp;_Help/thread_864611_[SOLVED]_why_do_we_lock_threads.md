### Post by p_quarles on 2008-07-19
Generally I'd remove/jail a thread if it's completely inappropriate and difficult to just prune. If it's just getting out of hand, going in a bad direction, or (most commonly) asking for legally questionable assistance, we lock it.

A locked thread can also stand as a record of our reasons for doing so. We can lock a thread with a note saying "please don't post stuff like this," but that would be impossible with deletion.
