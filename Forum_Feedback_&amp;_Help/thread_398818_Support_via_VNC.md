### Post by Matt Yun on 2007-04-01
VNC support sounds like a good idea for a paid service provided by Canonical.  They may even already offer it with their Ubuntu support packages.

However, it is no substitute for ubuntuforums.org.  The whole purpose of these community based forums is to share knowledge, not just to fix problems.  

If a user's problem is fixed on a private basis, then the knowledge won't necessarily be shared with the rest of the user community.

It's amazing how a simple search of these forums, either from the site itself or through Google, invariably turns up an answer to practically any hardware or software question that I've had.
