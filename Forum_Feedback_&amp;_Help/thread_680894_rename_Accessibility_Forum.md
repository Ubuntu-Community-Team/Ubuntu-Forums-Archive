### Post by frafu on 2008-01-28
Hello, 

Could you please consider renaming the "Accessibility Forum" into "Assistive Technology Forum". 

Indeed, Accessibility is sometimes interpreted as "how can I access a disk?", or "How can I access the Internet?",... instead of software to enable or help disabled people to use the computer. 

You might be interested to have a look at the following thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=289896](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=289896)

Maybe that the subtitle could also be improved; for example: 
For discussions about software that enables or helps disabled (mobility, visual, hearing, cognitive,... impaired) people to use the computer.

Best regards. 

Francesco 
(moderator of the Accessibility Forum)
