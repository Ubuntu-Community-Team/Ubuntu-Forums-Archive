### Post by QIII on 2012-08-16
Posts in non-support areas do not get beans.  I see you have quite a few in the Cafe.  Those won't count.

Your bean count may sometimes go down if the staff jails posts (yours may not be bad, but to maintain thread consistency sometimes a few others are also jailed.  Don't take that as an indication of punishment in that case) or moves them.

Beans really mean little except in the case of getting to the magical 50 to change your profile.  But spamming for beans generally gets caught and various levels of "corrective action" can be taken.

Don't get wrapped around the axle about bean count.  Concentrate on meaningful questions and positive help.
