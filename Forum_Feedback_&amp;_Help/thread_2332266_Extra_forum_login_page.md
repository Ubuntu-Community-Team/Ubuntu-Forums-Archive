### Post by coffeecat on 2016-07-30
The page that you are seeing is not a forum page, but one that Ubuntu One is serving up, and quite why it started doing so about 4 weeks ago, and what it has to do with the forum is not clear. More discussion here: 

[https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2329269](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2329269)

The username field is an additional Ubuntu One field. Your Ubuntu One and forum accounts are not linked by username so I suggest that if you want to complete the username field, you do so according to the rules Ubuntu One are laying down. It should not affect your forum login.
