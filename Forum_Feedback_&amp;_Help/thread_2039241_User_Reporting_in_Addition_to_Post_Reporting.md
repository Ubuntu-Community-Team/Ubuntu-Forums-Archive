### Post by drs305 on 2012-08-08
As *chamber* posted, just reporting a post or user once is normally all that is needed. When we review a reported post for spam, we check the number of posts. Normally a spammer doesn't last long on the forum and has only made the initial post. 

If we find multiple posts, we generally can 'bulk' inspect them and remove them all with just a few keystrokes.

Thanks for helping to make the Ubuntu Forums as friendly and spam-free as possible.  :-)
