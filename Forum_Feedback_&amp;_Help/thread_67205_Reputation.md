### Post by matthew on 2005-09-19
when you post something that another forum reader finds helpful they can add to your reputation by clicking on the little checkmark and x at the bottom left of your post. This is just a way to give you a complement as for the moment the reputation points are not being used for anything. On some forums they are used to help classify users as more or less helpful and there are even ways to subtract from another users reputation...not here, though.
