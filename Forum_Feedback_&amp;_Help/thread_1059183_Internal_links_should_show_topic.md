The one part I like about the links the way they are commonly posted is that they tell you [for the most part] exactly where they go without having to look at the status bar.

for example, when I read [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=520091](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=520091) , I know that the thread in question is [or should] be on ubuntu forums.  Of course someone could do [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=520091]("http://www.google.com/") and I would be fooled (It points to google this time :)).  To fix this, maybe prepending the phrase 'ubuntuforums:" to the title would help, i.e. [UbuntuForums: The BUMP Thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=520091") would do the trick.

That's just a few random ideas, although I don't see this happening anytime soon.  I could be wrong, though.
