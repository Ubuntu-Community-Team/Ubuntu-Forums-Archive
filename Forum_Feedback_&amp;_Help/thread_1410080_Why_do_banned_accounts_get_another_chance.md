### Post by Joeb454 on 2010-02-18
Personally, I'm happy to give 2nd chances.

That said, it depends why the original account was banned, and how long after the ban somebody comes back.

If, for example, the user was banned 2 months ago, and has come back and somehow been missed by the staff (we have various ways of checking :)), I'd be happy for them to continue, as long as the behaviour is acceptable.

If the account was created 5 minutes after being banned, the new account would also get banned.

**Obligatory disclaimer:** *This is **only** my opinion, and not a rule/guideline taken by all staff*

### Post by matthew on 2010-02-18
If a user is banned, we can not always know if they come back and create a new account. This is especially true if they use the new account completely within the guidelines of the CoC. If they come back and behave, I'm not interested in performing a witch hunt to find them.

That said, most people who are banned who also come back almost always end up posting in the same style that got them banned the first time. We don't always discover they are the same person right away, but when this happens the person generally says something (usually bragging) that tips us off as to their identity. We have often contacted users at that moment via private message to get a confirmation. Even then, we have allowed some to remain after promises to abide by site rules. There have been some cases where this has been a good thing and, most recently, obvious cases where we went overboard in our attempt to be welcoming and forgiving.
