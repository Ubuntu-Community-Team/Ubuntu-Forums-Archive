### Post by QIII on 2017-06-23
Hello!

We have no control or say over how Ubuntu One works, so we cannot help you there.  Canonical, Ltd, uses it as the login for all sites, so to get here you have to use it.  This has been imposed on us, too.

The password on Ubuntu Forums (that is, after you have passed Ubuntu One) is of no consequence.  It is no longer used for any purpose and you won't ever need to use it.  If you had one several years ago, in fact, it has been randomized and salted.

Your email here is separate from your Email credential used for Ubuntu One.  Once you have associated your account here with your Ubuntu One login (sometimes we have to help people do that), you can change your email here all you want.  Your ability to log in to UF is predicated on a UserID determined by Ubuntu One.  If you change your preferred email at Ubuntu One, you are still logging in as that UserID, so UF doesn't care.

With regard to your impatience, please consider that there are exactly 6 of us who access the Admin mailing list. Two in the UK, one in Portugal, one in Western Canada and two in the US. We are volunteers like everyone else.  The "exigencies" of being an Admin on a volunteer forum take a back seat to the most mundane of events of real life.  Your email arrived at Fri Jun 23 16:38:20 UTC 2017.  It is currently Fri Jun 23 19:32:42 UTC 2017  Your email arrived only 3 hours ago.  You will have to be a bit more patient than that.

Cheers!
