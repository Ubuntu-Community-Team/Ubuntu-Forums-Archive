### Post by kassetra on 2006-07-03
Ok, for those of you that don't know:

Under your name you have a saying and an image.  These items are for fun, and are not serious.  They are not a rank of any kind.  They don't tell anyone how long you've been here nor how many posts you've made.  The sayings and the images are a semi-random feature we have to provide a little kick.  There is some structure to it, but only on the implementation side.  We did this on purpose - because it was fun and whimsical.

There isn't a list of what you get, and when, because that's not the point and it's not how it's done here.  :)

Beanies of the Month are slightly different in that they are awarded a special saying and image for helping the most people during the last month.

Under all of those things is something that looks like, Beans: 1234.  This is a post count - but we only count posts made to the technical support areas, and not to areas like the Community Chat.  If you post 20 times to the Community Chat area, none of those posts will be counted.

Try directing people to this sticky if they seem confused or want to know how these things work.  :KS
