### Post by coffeecat on 2012-11-23
Not a Resolution Centre thread. *Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

Only forum staff can move or delete threads or posts. In fact we don't delete threads, but move them to the jail if they need to be moved out of public view where only the staff or the person who made the post can see them.

If you want one of your own threads moved to another forum, click on the [img]http://ubuntuforums.org/images/rebrand/buttons/report.gif[/img] button in your post to send a message to the staff area where someone will deal with it for you. Don't worry that the button says “report abuse” - it can be used for anything that needs staff action with a particular post or thread.
