### Post by m_duck on 2012-07-27
Your account is probably set not to subscribe to threads you post in by default. To change it, go to your control panel, and on the left hand side options, click "Edit Options" (under the "Settings & Options" heading). The setting you probably need to change is "Default Thread Subscription Mode"; change it to "No email notification" if you just want thread subscriptions, or one of the email choices if you also want email notifications.

For now though, you can find your threads/posts by clicking search (along the top bar of links) and "Find All Your Posts" or "Find All Your Threads" respectively.
