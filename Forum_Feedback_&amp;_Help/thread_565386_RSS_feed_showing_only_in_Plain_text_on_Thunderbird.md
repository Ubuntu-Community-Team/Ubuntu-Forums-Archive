### Post by aos101 on 2008-04-28
You say you want to see the HTML version of the feed in Thunderbird.  Do you mean you want it to show the thread on the Ubuntu forums page within Thunderbird (instead of just the text of one post from the thread)?  If that's what you want, subscribing to the RSS 0.91 feed (instead of the RSS 2 feed) seems to work.  Take the RSS feed address and just remove the "2" from after RSS and subscribe to that in Thunderbird.  The RSS 0.91 feed address for the latest posts from all forums would be:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/external.php?type=RSS](http://ubuntuforums.org/external.php?type=RSS)
