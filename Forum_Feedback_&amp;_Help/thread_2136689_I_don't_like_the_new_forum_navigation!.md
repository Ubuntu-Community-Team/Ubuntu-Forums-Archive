### Post by coffeecat on 2013-04-18
Do you not see the Quick Navigation drop-down to the left of the top button? It's helpfully labelled Quick Navigation and the actual dropdown shows the name of the page you are on - "Forum Feedback & Help" on this page. Click on the actual drop-down and you will see a list of all the sub-forums plus some site areas at the top such as settings (the old UserCP) and Private Messages. The only difference from the old pre-upgrade version (as far as I recall) is that it defaults to the top of the list when you click on it whereas before it set the scrollbar to show you the current page.

The top button takes you to the top of the page - useful when you have a long page such as a long thread or forum thread listing. Many websites have top buttons.
