### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-11-23
> **drubin said:**
> I know this was a "hacky de hack work around" :).

ADMIN/MOD:
I wonder if you can book mark that search id?? Or will that also cache the search results and you will never get newer results.

Searches after they are performed are cached, so the results are not updated and will usually expire in less than 30 minutes.

If you are able to bookmark your search before the ID is generated (ie, [http://ubuntuforums.org/search.php?do=getnew](http://ubuntuforums.org/search.php?do=getnew)), then the search will be re-done every time. To do this with the advanced search form however requires looking at the source and grabbing the form postdata and putting it into a URL, a tedious task.
