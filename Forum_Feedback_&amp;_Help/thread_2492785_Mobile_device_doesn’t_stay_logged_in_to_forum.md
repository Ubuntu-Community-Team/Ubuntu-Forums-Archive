### Post by coffeecat on 2023-11-23
Nothing to do with Apple and, yes, it probably is a forum "issue", although a feature rather than a bug. If your public IP address changes you get logged out. Check your IP address with whatismyipaddress.com (or similar site) occasionally to see if changes coincide with being logged out.
