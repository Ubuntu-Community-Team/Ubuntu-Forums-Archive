### Post by coffeecat on 2014-08-14
*Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

That's an interesting observation but I doubt very much that it has any significance. Something that allows you to stay logged in for only a minute rather than a few seconds is not much of an improvement. I suspect that whatever is causing you to be logged out simply took a little longer on the occasion(s) that you unticked the Real Name box in Ubuntu One, and it was was simply coincidence that it took longer.

As is obvious from surveying the forum, the vast majority of members are not experiencing what you are seeing. Many active members are moving from thread to thread, posting in some of them, and are not having to log back in again. There is nothing in your account settings that can account from this, so your problem must be local to your system, computer, or internet connection.

I've moved this to Forum Feedback & Help so that the wider forum membership can offer their thoughts. Only you and the forum staff can post to your thread while it is in the Resolution Centre. In the meantime, a few suggestions:

All the usual stuff, including: clear your browser cache, make sure you are not blocking ubuntuforums cookies, and don't block scripts in ubuntuforums. 

One other thing which I doubt is the problem but which is worth checking. Do you have a static or dynamically-assigned IP? If your IP changes while logged in you sometimes get logged out of the forum. This isn't consistent but it does occur on occasion.
