### Post by bigsmitty64 on 2010-07-05
On the main page of the forums, every time you click on a subforum (aka: absolute beginners) you will automatically see the newest posts. Plus when you see a thread of interest to you in the forum you can subscribe to that thread by clicking "thread tools" to the right and above the first post, then whenever a new post is made in that thread you'll be notified on your user page, or "user cp" which you get to by clicking the link  "user cp" in the upper left of the forum. Hope this helps also. :)  Have fun and welcome to the forums!
