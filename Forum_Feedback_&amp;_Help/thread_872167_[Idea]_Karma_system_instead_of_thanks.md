### Post by litemotiv on 2008-07-27
Instead of only rewarding people for contributing useful posts, wouldn't it be a good idea to start using a system where also negative points can be given for invalid, irrelevant or unfriendly posts (i.e. a karma system)? 

I think this will make it easier for people to spot notorious trolls and skip irrelevant posts, but it also might encourage people more to react constructively in 'precarious' situations.

Your opinions on this are appreciated. ;)

---

### Post by matthew on 2008-07-27
Thanks for the input!

We actually had a karma system in place (a very long time ago...say three years or so) and it turned into an unproductive popularity contest that we have no desire to revisit. We went with nothing for a very long time after that, before implementing the thanks system, which is working as a reasonable compromise.

I have seen karma systems work, but very rarely, and after our experience here, we aren't terribly interested in another go around.

Sorry if I came off as a wet blanket, that is certainly not my goal. We truly appreciate forum members giving ideas and input, so accept my sincere thanks!
