### Post by themeone on 2007-05-19
I hope this doesn't come across as too much of a moan, but I really dislike the new design of the Ubuntu Forums, and use them much less since the design changed some months ago.

What's the point of the brown coloured surround? It just wastes screen space.

Also when I click on a thread, I want to read the posts.  Instead all I am presented with is the login area, then the menus, then Unanswered Posts underneath, Welcome to the Ubuntu Forums underneath that, and at the bottom of the screen the name of the first poster to the thread, but with none of their post visible.  I have to page down. It's really annoying.

Surely we don't need all this information on the screen all the time.  We certainly don't need it filling the whole screen, and surrounded with lots of useless white space.

This really is poorly designed in my opinion.
