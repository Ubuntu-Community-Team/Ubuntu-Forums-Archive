### Post by igknighted on 2009-02-03
Ubuntuforums doesn't rank, and displaying post count is optional.  Good advice can come from anyone, regardless of post count, so we don't want anyone's advice discounted due to a lower "rank".

And stickies are general information (guides, how-tos, warnings and other such things).  They exist so everyone reads them before posting, to avoid answering some basic questions over and over again.
