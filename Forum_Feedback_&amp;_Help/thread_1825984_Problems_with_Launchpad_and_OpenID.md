> And here's another problem: when I sign in through my Launchpad, I am not asked for any password.  This is a real security hole. Anybody having access to my PC could log in to my forum account with no difficulty...

If you are already signed in to Launchpad and then sign in to the forum through your Launchpad account you are (obviously) not asked for your password.

If you are not already signed into Launchpad and you sign into the forum through your Launchpad account, then you are asked for your password. There is no security hole.

If there is a security hole it would be down to you leaving your computer unattended while still logged into an account. Do you leave your computer unattended when still logged into an internet banking account? :wink:
