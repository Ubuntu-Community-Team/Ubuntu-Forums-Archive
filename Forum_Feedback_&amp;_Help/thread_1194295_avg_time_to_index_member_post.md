### Post by HavocXphere on 2009-06-23
> Do the spiders consistently index all ubuntu forum member posts?
Afaik yes. The only thing bots skip is stuff marked as don't crawling using a robots.txt.

Plus some servers are configured to give the google bot access to stuff that is normally not accessible without being registered. Not sure why :confused:...

In my experience, it takes ~2-4 hours for something to get indexed if its on a moderately or very busy forum.

Also, not all sites are equal when it comes to the google bot. Many big sites get indexed more regularly. Not sure whether google selects big corp sites for this or whether the corporate sites apply.

There are also rumours that sites with google ads get indexed/updated more often. I think thats just a rumour though.

The admins of UF will probably be able to give you a more exactly...the google bot identifies itself as such and will show up in the logs.
