### Post by jenkinbr on 2009-06-01
> **The Toxic Mite said:**
> EDIT: I don't think it worked

> **overdrank said:**

That's how it's supposed to work.

If you want to make the nested quotes, you have to do so manually, like this:



> **overdrank said:**
> [QUOTE=The Toxic Mite;7381587]EDIT: I don't think it workedWorking fine :)[/QUOTE]

Also, When you click the multi-quote button, those quotes typically stay on their originating thread. However, when you quote in another thread with other quotes selected, you will have a line like:
```
You have selected 3 posts that are not part of this thread. [Quote these posts as well]("111"), or [deselect these posts]("000"). 
```
under the 'Message' dialog box in the 'Reply to post' page.
