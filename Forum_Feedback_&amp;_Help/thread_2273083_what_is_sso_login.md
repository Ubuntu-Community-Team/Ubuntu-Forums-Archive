### Post by coffeecat on 2015-04-10
Assuming that SSO login to ubuntuforums is what prompted you to ask the question,

*Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

This is a good general interest article about single sign on:

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Single_sign-on](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Single_sign-on)

Ubuntu One is the SSO for a number of Canonical-run sites, including ubuntuforums.

[https://login.ubuntu.com/](https://login.ubuntu.com/)

Non SSO login is what we had here before July 2013. Examples of other Linux forums that do not use SSO would be [http://forums.linuxmint.com/](http://forums.linuxmint.com/) and [http://fedoraforum.org/forum/](http://fedoraforum.org/forum/) but there are many others.
