### Post by kofte on 2009-08-27
Just thought I'd drop by and say a few things that I feel need to be said.

First of all, I am truly impressed with Ubuntu in many ways. I'm not going to lie, I tried another linux distro (actually more like several) the other week and I've had nothing but horrible time with it. The install went smooth, but I was having an awkward internet problem and the fault wasn't from my end either.

Since then, I've spent hours and hours trying to figure it out, with horrible support from the community (regardless of how mean that sounds, the officials never really got anything going, not to mention random accusations were being tossed around and it really showed nothing but complete immaturity) and I've given up. Ubuntu has came out on top and I cannot stress this enough; the support and help that is offered from this community and its users, is truly outstanding. I am absolutely greatful for the individuals who help out others that are in need of help and are very professional about it. 

I've had nothing but a great experience with Ubuntu and I hope it remains thath way. 

I just wanted to say thank you to all who contribute to making Ubuntu what it is today and to not take antyhing for granted as some other "communities" of distros do. This isn't a suckup post, but rather my experience after looking for the "right" distro.

Keep up the good work fellas (and gals). 

Thanks again.
