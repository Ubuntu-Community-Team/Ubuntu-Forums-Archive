### Post by matthew on 2008-01-15
I'm not sure why that happened.

For the sake of your nerves, ph.ubuntuforums.org is a sub-domain of this site that apparently mirrors the main site. What you were viewing were these forums, but by accessing it through the sub-domain address you would not appear logged in because the ubuntuforums.org "logged in" cookie is specific to access via ubuntuforums.org.

You haven't been phished, we haven't been hacked, nothing of that nature is going on (that I can tell, anyway). :)

The question I'm pondering is why you were forwarded to that sub-domain in the first place. Hmm. Now that is odd. If it were something nefarious, you would probably have been linked off-site, not to a sub-domain on the same server.
