### Post by robsoles on 2011-06-07
You shouldn't need that flameproof suit.

I agree that the games I like to haunt shouldn't be returned by a 'default' search within the site, I wish search functionality was trimmed to default to the section you are viewing now if you are not more specific with the 'advanced' options.

Often Google index has a better take on any site, than a site's inbuilt search function, which can be accessed by specifying the site, in Google search box:```
site:ubuntuforums.org [COLOR="Blue"]fairly specific about problem[/COLOR]
```where something between 3 and 10 words to be 'fairly specific about problem' is usually pretty darn fruitful.
