To sum up: **no benefit for the person needing help** because ignored threads can be bumped and people are actually less likely to find the thread if it's divided up even further, and **no benefit to those trying to help**, as they will either go to one or two forums (not twenty separate ones) or just search for new threads. > If it was broken up this would be less of a problem, and it would be easier for new people, like myself, to search for revelant threads.
