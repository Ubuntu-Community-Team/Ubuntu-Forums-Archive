### Post by yetiman64 on 2011-08-19
Also further to what forestpiskie mentions about using the the text editor, when you get asked to log back in, DON'T, immediately hit the back button on your browser and you will get back to your text and can save it into a text editor BEFORE logging back in.

This has saved me a few times on some of my more "long winded" replies :). Once you log back in copying and pasting from a text file on your desktop can save heaps of time even if you need to reformat some of your post. Also the "remember me" button is handy to use for staying logged in.

### Post by Tamlynmac on 2011-08-20
Another potential solution is to type your message then select all your  message and copy, then refresh your browser. It will ask you for your  user name and password, after you log back in, it should take you to a  bank (thread or reply) window. Then just right click inside the  blank window and select paste. This should copy your message back into  the window. If after refreshing your message remains in the window, you  haven't timed out.

Generally, you don't need to worry about it, if cookies are turned on. I  don't know what the time out period is, but with cookies turned on it  seems to increase substantively.
