### Post by QIII on 2018-12-03
Hello!

Logging in is accomplished via Ubuntu One (a requirement imposed on us), which holds the keys for logging in to all Canonical associated web resources.  Changing your email in your forum profile does not have anything to do with that.

We do not administer Ubuntu One, so we can't directly help you.  But you should be able to go to [https://login.ubuntu.com/](https://login.ubuntu.com/) and log in.  There, you should be able to change your preferred email address -- for Ubuntu One.
