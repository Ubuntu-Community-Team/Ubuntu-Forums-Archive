### Post by compmodder26 on 2007-05-11
You can choose Search->Find All Your Posts.  Then navigate to the thread in question.

edit:  If it is a thread you started then choose Search->Find All Your Threads

### Post by Seisen on 2007-05-11
On the thread go to "Thread Tools" and then select "Subscribe." Then you can check the thread out in your UserCP under subscribed threads.

Goto the top of this page, click on UserCP
You will be in your User Control Panel.
On the left side select Miscellaneous thec goto Messaging & Notification - Default Thread Subscription Mode and select Instant email notiication.

You will receive an email for every post which as been published on threads you subscribed which means if you open a thread or you replay to a thread, you will receive an email every time someone will reply
