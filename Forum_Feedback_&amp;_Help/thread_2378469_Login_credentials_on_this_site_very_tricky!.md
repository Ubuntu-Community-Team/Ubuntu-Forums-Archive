### Post by QIII on 2017-11-23
Hello!

Your Ubuntu One preferred email address has nothing at all to do with the email address associated with your Ubuntu Forums account.  Your Ubuntu Forums email account has nothing at all to do with your ability to log in to the Ubuntu Forums and is really not useful to anyone but the Forums Staff.  The email/password combination found in the UF Settings is obsolete and has been disabled.  You do not sign in to the Ubuntu Forums that way any longer.  This was none of our doing.  Canonical imposed Ubuntu One SSO on all Ubuntu-associated sites hosted on its servers.

Are you satisfied that you can log in to the Ubuntu Forums with your current Ubuntu One SSO login?  If so, your Ubuntu One SSO account has been correctly associated with your Ubuntu Forums account.  Ubuntu One SSO has assigned you an OpenID, which is the credential used to log in to the Ubuntu Forums.

Changing your Ubuntu One SSO preferred email address will have no affect on that.  To change your Ubuntu One SSO preferred email address, you will have to go to the link provided by lisati.  We do not administer Ubuntu One SSO, so we have no way to affect any change.

Cheers!
