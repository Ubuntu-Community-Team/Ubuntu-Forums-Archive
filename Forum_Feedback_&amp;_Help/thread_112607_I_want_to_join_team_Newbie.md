### Post by kassetra on 2006-01-04
We have a "Green Team" to help new users.

We do not call new users "Newbies" because we feel that the word is insulting, given it's history - therefore, we would not have a "Newbie Team."

The Green Team is made up of people that help out in the Absolute Beginner area.  If you would like to join that team, you should hang out in that forum area and help out as much as you are able to.

In general, if you want to join any team, participate in what that team does - Team Bahamut has been concentrating on the UDSF as of late; Tweaker Team writes howtos & in general works in the Tips & Tricks areas;  Hardware Team (you can guess); etc.

Participation, even if it is sporadic, is a large criteria for joining a team.  Each team also has it's own set of tasks and duties.  Ask how you can help.

Kass
