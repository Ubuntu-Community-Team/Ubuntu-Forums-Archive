### Post by LaRoza on 2008-10-13
To me, infractions are just a way to allow people to know they need to change on the forum. They are not a punishment or anything.

Most infractions expire over time. When people get more infractions, mods may change them to not expire. For the worst, there will be a progression of warnings, expiring 1 point infractions, non expiring 1 point infractions, and then multi-point infractions.

The ones that don't expire are called "strong hints", as continuing the behavior that go them will result in more non expiring infractions and these will build up if one doesn't change.
