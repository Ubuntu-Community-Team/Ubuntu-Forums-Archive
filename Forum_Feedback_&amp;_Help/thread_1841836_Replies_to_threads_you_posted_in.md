### Post by coffeecat on 2011-09-10
> **haqking said:**
> 
I guess it comes down to subscriptions

I think it does. Subscriptions should give you all you are asking for, so I'm not quite clear why you are hesitant to use subscriptions. 

Under User CP -> Edit Options -> Default Thread Subscription mode, I've set the drop down to "no email notification". Which means I automatically subscribe to all threads I've posted in without getting hundreds of emails. When I first log in I go to User CP and all the threads I'm subscribed to and which have new posts are listed with clickable links. That solves the problem of the OP you are helping not coming back for a couple of weeks or more.

During a logged in session, I go back to User CP occasionally and pick up new posts which have been made since I logged in.

It's also a useful way of detecting the odd spam or necropost.
