### Post by matthew on 2009-08-27
1. E.T.R. is a cool game!

2. In the past our criteria for giving user titles has been directly related to Ubuntu world activities, except in an extremely small number of cases early on when we had the resources to offer to non-Ubuntu upstream people to host a third party forum for them. We haven't started any new third party fora in a long time and have closed down many, solely due to resource scarcity issues. The Community Developer title came from that early era and wasn't used much. I can't recall it being given to anyone new for at least a couple of years. Sorry for the confusion.
