### Post by coffeecat on 2019-05-26
If you click on the "Login with SSO" button on the ubuntuforums.org page, that should take you to the "Personal Data Request" page at login.ubuntu.com if you are already logged into ubuntu.com. If not, you should be taken to the "One account to log in to everything on Ubuntu" page where you can log into Ubuntu One. There is a "Forgot your password?" link on that page, but I don't believe I have ever seen a "reset password page for when we forget/lose password" page. Which is why I suggested a screenshot so that we can be sure we're talking about the same thing.

**Edit:** I see that if you click on the "Forgot your password?" link on the "One account to log in to everything on Ubuntu" ubuntu.com page, you are taken to a page headed "Reset password". Is this what you mean?

I suggest you see whether lastpass is interfering and effectively clicking on too far for you.
