### Post by LaRoza on 2008-09-12
You can do both if you wish.

Thanking is handy for much advice, so you can give thanks to individual posts, whereas a post is more global. 

You wouldn't want to post a "thank you" to each time a person posts (say, if they are walking you through something) as that would be cluttering for example.
