### Post by aysiu on 2007-04-09
Click on **Go Advanced**

Scroll down to **Additional Options**

Select **Manage Attachments**. A new pop-up window will open.

Click **Browse**

Find the file you want to upload

Click **Upload**

Close the pop-up window.

Click **Submit Reply**
