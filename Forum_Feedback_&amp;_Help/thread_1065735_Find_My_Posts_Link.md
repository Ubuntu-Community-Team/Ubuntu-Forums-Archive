There is one on all your posts.

Click your username and then 'find more posts by 'user'

--OR--

On the top navigation bar, click the search dropdown and at the bottom is the 'Find all your posts' entry.  Just above that is the 'Find all your threads' entry.

--OR--

For all threads that I follow, I add a subscription to them.  Click 'Thread Tools' in the thread you want to subscribe to, and near (or at) the bottom, is a 'subscribe to this thread' link.  You will jump to a page where you can set your notification type, and a folder (if you have set these up) for where to store the subscription.  After setting up the subscription, you can go to your user control panel (User CP in the upper left on navbar), and your newly updated threads will be displayed.

Right now, I have roughly 150 threads I am subscribed to, most of them are solved, but occasionally, someone will post there and surprise me.  None of these have e-mail notification, because I am on the forums enough.
