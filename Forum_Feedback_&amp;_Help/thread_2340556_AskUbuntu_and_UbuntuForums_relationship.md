There is no relationship, other than both are about Ubuntu.
They are not part of Canonical, but StackExchange, and is a question & answer site, not a forum. 
We think our forum is more helpful :)
But if you have a question you can search and see if they have an answer.
They also are very specific about only Ubuntu questions. And move or redirect to other Stack Exchange sites.
