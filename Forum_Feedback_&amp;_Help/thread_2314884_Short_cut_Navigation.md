### Post by deadflowr on 2016-02-24
Okay.
First : Moved to Forum Feedback and Help,
as this is a forum question/help thread.

Second, what I do is this.
Write up the post.
Then highlight the word you want to make a link.
Then click on the Link icon in the toolbar, then paste or type the link of the thread or link you want to use.
Click ok and the word will now be the link.
I think it's simple, but I also hope it makes sense.
