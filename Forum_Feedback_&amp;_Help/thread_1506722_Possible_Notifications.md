Is there an option somewhere to enable notifications for Ubuntu Forums other than email? Such as when someone replies to your reply or when someone replies to a thread you posted? ### Post by sisco311 on 2010-06-10
Yep, set the *Default Thread Subscription Mode* to *No email notification*. You can check your subscribed threads under *User CP*.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=4537471&postcount=9](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=4537471&postcount=9)
