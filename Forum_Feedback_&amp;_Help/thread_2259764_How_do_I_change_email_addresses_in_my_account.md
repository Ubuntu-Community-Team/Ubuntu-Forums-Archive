### Post by coffeecat on 2015-01-07
I see that you successfully changed your forum account email on 29th December, and then re-activated the account from the verification email. I can see nothing that would be giving you problems now.

This is the procedure that I have just tested with a test account:

Click on the "Settings" link at the top-right of the page while logged in, then on "Edit Email & Password" on the left. Ignore the edit password fields - they are not needed. Type your new email address in the New Email Address and Confirm New Email Address fields and click on the Save Changes button. Now re-activate the account from the verification email sent to the new email address.

### Post by lisati on 2015-01-07
If you want to change the email address you use to login to the forum, you'll have to change it from within the SSO system rather than directly from the forum. Go to [http://login.ubuntu.com](http://login.ubuntu.com) and click on "manage my email addresses".
