### Post by coffeecat on 2022-04-25
As this question is about using the forum, not technical support, *thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

There is not a specific Ubuntu Studio sub-forum, but as ajgreeny said, [New to Ubuntu]("https://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=326"), where you posted, would be suitable for beginners. The [Multimedia Software ]("https://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=334") sub-forum is for problems with individual application problems. Or you can use General Help, Networking & Wireless, or one of the other official Ubuntu flavour sub-forums depending on the nature of the problem you want help with. All of those sub-forums have taglines describing their intended purpose. Wherever you post, please add the Ubuntu Studio prefix to your thread title when creating your thread. This helps others to help you.
