### Post by PriceChild on 2007-07-20
The subject has come up among the staff and it was discussed in a Forum Council meeting.

The general consensus was that we would all be informed, and be able to discuss whether posts could be stickies, or if they should be unstuck etc.

I'd advise you to use the REPORT button on any sticky you think should be unstuck and we'll have a think about it :)

The problem is, there's just so much useful information people need, we know people don't read many stickies, and its just hard to decide which is most important and what limit to put on it.
