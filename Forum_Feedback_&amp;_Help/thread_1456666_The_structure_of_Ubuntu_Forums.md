### Post by TheHimself on 2010-04-17
I believe the structure of this website is not optimal. It happens frequently that somebody asks a question which has been asked and answered already and this happens because one can't find the answer by searching. I think one should have a hierarchal structure here. I mean this website should be more intelligent in helping users to get to posts they need. 
Something similar to the organization of eBay website.  

Say, I have a question about wireless performance of Lenovo s10 in Karmic. If I search the three words "wireless", "Lenovo s10" and "Karmic" I'll get  a lot of threads and it takes me a lot of time to go through them.  But in a more intelligent setting, the website knows more about each thread. Is it about an upgrade problem, is it about wireless, is it about not being able to connect or wireless card not being recognized, etc.

### Post by chessnerd on 2010-04-17
Honestly, as far as "structure" is concerned I think the Ubuntu Forums has one of the best structures out there. The search is terrible, and it is annoying to search via Google, so I suggest a third option similar to the OP's:

Hierarchical tags.

Basically, when you make a post, you have to give it at least one or two tags, and it will take you through a hierarchy until you think you are close enough to the exact detail of your post. Let's say I want to post about an issue with my graphics card when working with Compiz effects. I would write my post and then need to choose some tags. Out of a list of maybe 15 options I would select something like "Graphics" which would then change the options to 15 or so graphics-specific issues. So maybe next I would select nVidia Legacy Cards for problems with a that type of card. Then that would break into even more options, which might include the TNT2 Pro card I have installed on my desktop. Then I would select another tag from the original set, probably "Graphics" again. I would then choose Compiz and under there I might select specifically the Desktop Cube or just leave it as a general Compiz issue. I would post and people could easily see I was having trouble with Compiz on an nVidia TNT2 Pro card.

I think that would be a nice setup to have that would allow people to give intelligent and organized tags to their posts and it could give better search results by having a similar setup for searching for issues.
