### Post by running_rabbit07 on 2009-09-06
"How much do you feel your thread is more important than others?" Is the question you should be asking yourself.

When people that want to help others start scanning the threads they look through a few pages to find unanswered threads that they may have an answer to. Bumping the thread just makes them more likely to skip your thread because they may think you are impatient.

If you have a special needs type of problem, you may be waiting for days before you get a reply.

Also, if you are asking something that has a very obvious fix on google with a quick search, then it is likely nobody will take the time to answer you.

### Post by lisati on 2009-09-06
One "rule of thumb" is "don't bump your thread more than once in 24 hours."
There's a group of us who, from time to time, search the forums for unanswered posts, and if a thread has been bumped, it doesn't always show up in the search for threads with absolutely no replies.
