### Post by drs305 on 2010-07-16
You can click on Search (Top/Center-Right), Find All Your ..., or even easier, make a shortcut to:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/subscription.php]("http://ubuntuforums.org/subscription.php")

Added: if you aren't being automatically subscribed to your own posts, go to the User CP, Edit Options, and make sure you don't have the Default Thread Subscription Mode >  "Do not subscribe" option enabled.
