### Post by Jadd on 2008-05-08
Please get rid of the annoying prefixes that go: [ubuntu], [xubuntu], etc.

They wouldn't be half as annoying if they were suffixes. But now, if I scan the pages downwards, I have to move my eyes a different distance every time to read the title of the thread, which is much more important than the prefix.

What's even more annoying is that they're in the page's <title>, meaning they show up in Google's results, as the first word in the heading for the search. This makes ubuntuforums.org look like a complicated developer bug tracker system, not a friendly community. Plus it makes it look ugly.

Besides, the prefixes seem to be pretty useless anyway.

In short, I vote: remove the prefixes!
