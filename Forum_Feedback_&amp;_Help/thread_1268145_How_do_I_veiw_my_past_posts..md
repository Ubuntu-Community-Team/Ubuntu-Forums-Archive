### Post by bapoumba on 2009-09-16
If it's a recent post > Search > Find all my posts.
If it's an older one > Search > Advanced search > enter your username > Go bottom left > Find posts from (please choose) > and older (or newer) > sort by ascending order (this will show older first) > Tick beans > Go
