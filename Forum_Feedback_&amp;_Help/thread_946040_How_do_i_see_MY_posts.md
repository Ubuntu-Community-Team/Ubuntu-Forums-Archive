> **latrosicarius said:**
> thank you. finally. it was so frustrating, why cant there be a big button geeze

If you are already in a thread in which you have posted, there is a big button.  Just click on your userID to the left of the post pane and you will get a drop down menu with a choice of "Show more posts by 'userID'".

You need to be logged-in to get all the options in this and many other features buttons on the Forums.

I keep a tab open in which I have one of my own posts on display.  Any time I need to check on any post I have made I just go there and click on my userID to get my posting history.
