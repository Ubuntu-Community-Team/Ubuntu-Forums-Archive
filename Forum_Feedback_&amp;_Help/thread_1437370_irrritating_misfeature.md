### Post by lisati on 2010-03-24
One option that might help avoid the possibility of timeout would be to use the "remember me" check box when logging in. Don't forget to log out again, particularly if you're using someone else's machine or someone else might use your machine.

### Post by coffeecat on 2010-03-24
If you are composing a particularly lengthy and complex post, you might want to use a text editor or word-processor first, saving it to HD. That'll give you more opportunity to go over your post to make sure it's clear. Then you simply copy and paste into the message box.

By the way, using your email address as your username is inadvisable. Unless you want to be flooded by spam. You might want to post a request [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=123") to change your username.
