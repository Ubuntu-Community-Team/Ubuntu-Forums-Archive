### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-05-23
Moderator viewpoint:

This is a nice idea in principle.  However, I suspect it will not work.

People would be reliant on posters to update the status of their "forgotten" threads here once resolved, in addition to in their actual thread.  Much as I would like to believe that this would happen - it won't.  A significant number are never reported as solved, even under the current scheme.

The forum works on the principle of one help request per thread and one thread per help request - this would merely act to confuse things.

A better solution would be to enforce a requirement that all threads that are solved are marked as such.  This would allow the Beginners Team to search all the "unsolved" threads.  Unfortunately, this is also dependent on human cooperation, so is equally difficult to achieve 100% concordance.

For the time being, an occasional "bump" after 24-48 hours to bring your own problem to the front page is acceptable.  Also - please read the link below re: Getting Help - most people simply don't ask the right questions or include the right information to allow help.

Additionally, this is not a specific request for technical help, so doesn't really belong here.  Accordingly, I have moved it to Forum Feedback.
