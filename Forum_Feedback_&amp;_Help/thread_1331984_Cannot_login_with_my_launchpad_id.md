### Post by jpeddicord on 2009-11-20
Hi,

You need to associate your account with your Launchpad ID before using it. The forums currently do not support signing in with a new Launchpad account without having an active forum account.

When you sign in with Launchpad for the first time, just type in details of an active forum account and it will automatically be linked.

If that doesn't work, sign in to the forums first. Then go to [Edit your Details]("http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editprofile") and click the link at the bottom of the page [(or here)]("http://ubuntuforums.org/launchpad_login.php?do=editprofile"). This will take you to Launchpad. Hit Sign In, and your accounts will be linked. You'll then be able to sign on with your Launchpad account.
