### Post by 67GTA on 2007-03-11
They are posts that you are subscribed to. There is a drop down menu under the boxes that will move, delete subscriptions, anything you could imagine doing with them. If you want to unsubscribe to several posts that you are subscribed to, just check the boxes beside them, select delete subsrciption from the drop menu and then click GO.
