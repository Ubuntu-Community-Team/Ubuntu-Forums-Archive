### Post by Macskeeball on 2010-08-06
Yes. That's called subscribing to threads.

[list=1][*]At the top left of the page, click on "User CP" (CP stands for control panel).
[*]In the sidebar on the left, look for the "Settings & Options" section and click on "Edit Options" below it.
[*]In the main part of the page scroll down to the "Messaging & Notification" section.
[*]In the box for "Default subscription mode," choose your desired setting.[/list]

If you ever want to unsubscribe to a thread you posted in, there should be an unsubscribe link in the emails or you can do it by going to the first page of the User CP, which lists the threads you've subscribed to. By default, it only shows the ones with new replies, but there's a popup to make it show them all).

Edit: LowSky's method would require you to subscribe manually to each thread. My method makes it so you get subscribed automatically whenever you post in a thread, with the ability to opt-out (uncheck a box) when making a post.
