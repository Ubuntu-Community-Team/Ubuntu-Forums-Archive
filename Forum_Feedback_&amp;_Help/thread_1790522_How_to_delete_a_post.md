### Post by yetiman64 on 2011-06-25
You have to be a moderator to delete a post here or contact one via the "report abuse" button in the post concerned and request the post be removed. Self deletion of a post is not possible.

Most people here just snip the contents out and leave a note like "<duplicate removed>" to save the mods the bother and show the forum a glitch occurred (it happens reasonably often here).

Seems you have put the duplicate to good use for more info in this case :).
