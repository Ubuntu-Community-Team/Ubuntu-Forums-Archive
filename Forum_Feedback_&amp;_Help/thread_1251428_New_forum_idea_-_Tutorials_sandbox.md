### Post by Bodsda on 2009-08-27
Hey, 

I have a dream...

well, more of an idea actually. I am in the middle of writing a rather lengthy tutorial for these forums and at first I had been using a new post to write it up, however I soon realized that I could not finish the tuto in one sitting so I have a choice. I can either save it locally and continue tomorrow, leave the page open, or start again when I have more time.

I dont think that any of the above options are the best way to go about writing tuto's, for example you would not be able to use the preview feature for a tuto you were writing on OOo.

I propose that a sub-forum be made. In this sub-forum, people should be able to make new, unmoderated threads where only they can edit and reply. When that person is finished, they can 'submit' their thread for review and it should act as if they just made a thread in tuto's and tips forum. No one else should be able to reply to someone else's thread unless the OP specifically enables them to (via a tick box maybe). That way, people could get collaborative help on their guide or they could write it on their own over a period of time. It would also be nice to be able to see what people are thinking of writing.

What do you guys think?

Kind regards,
Bodsda
