### Post by p_quarles on 2008-08-07
Moved to Feedback & Help.

No, it won't automatically be locked. If the thread was a support question that hasn't been active for some time, we might view a bump now as a bit late in the day. 

In any case, you should link to the thread and say what you want to post if you'd like a more definitive answer. The general answer is that "necromancing" isn't officially against the rules, but is often considered the less desirable way of posting new information.
