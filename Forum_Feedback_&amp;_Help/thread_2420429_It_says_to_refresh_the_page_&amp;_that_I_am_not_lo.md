### Post by coffeecat on 2020-03-14
Since you joined the forum, you have logged in or posted from a little under 500 different IP addresses. Your past four posts were each from different IP addresses. Please re-read what slickymaster wrote in post#4. There is nothing we can do if your IP address changes.

If your ISP is changing your public IP address while you are online, then that is something you would have to take up with your ISP. Or do you have a faulty router that keeps disconnecting and connecting, and your ISP is allocating you IP addresses dynamically, so that you get a different address when the router comes back online? If you want to see whether your IP address is changing while you are online, you would need to check periodically with one of the what is my IP sites, such as [https://www.whatismyip.com/](https://www.whatismyip.com/) or [https://www.whatismyip-address.com/](https://www.whatismyip-address.com/) or [https://whatismyipaddress.com/](https://whatismyipaddress.com/) .

By the way, I don't know whether any or all of those sites are reputable or safe, but they are what came up in a google search. They are all certainly giving me the same information about my connection.
