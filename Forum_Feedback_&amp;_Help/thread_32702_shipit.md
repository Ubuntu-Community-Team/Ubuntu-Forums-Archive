### Post by mattheweast on 2005-06-14
Couple of points here.

First is, check out what panickedthumb said above, this explains (to some extent) why many people have not got their cd's yet.
Second is that, again as panickedthumb says, this is not the best place to ask. Have a look on the [Shipit FAQ](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/support/documentation/faq/shipit/).
Third is just to suggest some alternatives: if you want to install Ubuntu and can't wait for the CD's, the following alternatives are available:
[list]
[*]Download the ISO ([http://releases.ubuntu.com/](http://releases.ubuntu.com/)) and burn it to a blank cd (only if you have a decent internet connection and a cd writer)
[*]find a friend who can do the above
[*]contact your local Ubuntu team ([http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/LoCoTeamList](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/LoCoTeamList)) and ask if they have CDs available or can find someone to do 1 for you
[/list] 

hope this helps!
