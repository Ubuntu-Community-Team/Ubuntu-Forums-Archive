### Post by prem1er on 2009-05-01
At the top of the post there is a drop down menu titled 'forum tools' you can 'subscribe' to a thread and have an email sent to you once it has been responded to.  Also, if you click on your user name and then select 'statistics' in your profile you can 'view all threads' and 'view all posts' by yourself.
