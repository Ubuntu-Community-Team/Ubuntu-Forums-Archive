### Post by coffeecat on 2021-05-03
Yeah, what the others said.

However, spammers sometimes mark their spam threads with the solved tag. Exactly what is going through what passes for their minuscule minds to do that I have no idea. All they achieve is to add a waving, pulsating, flashing red flag to their posts, easily recognised by forum staff. As all forum staff in every forum everywhere know only too well: spammers are complete and utter morons.

So... if you see an unanswered thread (one with only one post) with a solved tag, and which consists of a question that doesn't appear to belong here, or is a bit off, or is recognisably copy-pasted from somewhere else, please report it as possible spam or proto-spam. (Proto-spam is a seemingly innocent post that the spammer puts up as a smokescreen, and then comes back later to add their spam link or replace the text with overt advertising.)
