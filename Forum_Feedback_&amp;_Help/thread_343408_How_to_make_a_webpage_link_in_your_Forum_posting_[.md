### Post by aysiu on 2007-01-21
I've moved your thread to a more appropriate place.

In answer to your question, you add the appropriate tags, like so: ```
[url =https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Webcam#head-836881f8aaa64508507ce1f2091b24b8aead02cb]what I want to appear as text that goes to the link[/url]
``` Just take out that extra space after the word *url*
