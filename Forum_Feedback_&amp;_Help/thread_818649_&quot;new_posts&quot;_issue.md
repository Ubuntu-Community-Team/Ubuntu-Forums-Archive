### Post by mkendall on 2008-06-09
Try doing this: 
Click **New Posts**
Look at the top threads listed. Remember the thread titles.
Click on the second thread.
Click on **New Posts** again.
You will most likely find the threads moved down the list, but the thread you cliked on will no longer be listed.

When you post to a thread, you have read that thread, and it will not show up on the **New Posts** list for you until someone else posts to that thread.
