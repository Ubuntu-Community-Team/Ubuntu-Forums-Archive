### Post by matthew on 2010-06-21
We are working on moving our vBulletin installation from 3.x to 4.x. This requires a completely new skin and tons of little adjustments. We are hoping that there will be some good mobile features in the new version without requiring the use of plugins since we have discovered that most plugins do not seem to scale well to a forum our size, at least on the 3.x and older codebases--we had severe database corruption issues more than once caused by plugins that work great on smaller sites but which weren't scalable to one our size.

That said, we are thinking about the issue of mobile browsing and it is among the things we hope and desire to make possible in a more beautiful and appropriate way.

I'm sorry that I don't have an ETA, though.

### Post by MisterGaribaldi on 2011-02-19
Personally, I'd rather see UF implement a dedicated, auto-detecting mobile skin than go with someone elses' app or committing resources to write their own. It's very simple: maintaining a skin is a heck of a lot easier (from what I gather), there are far more people out there who are expert at it, and if for instance Tapatalk were to go bust next year, UF wouldn't be left holding the bag.

That being said, this isn't a "nag the UF admins" post. They've got enough work ahead of them (in addition to what's regularly on their plate) and frankly I have no personal desire to add to it.
