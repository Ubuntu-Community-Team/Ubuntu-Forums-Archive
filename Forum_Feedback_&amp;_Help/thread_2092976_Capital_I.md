### Post by coffeecat on 2012-12-09
Different fonts. After you've posted, the default font in posts is set by the forum theme and is Ubuntu font. In the message field when you are typing your post you see a different font. It's a fixed-width font too unlike Ubuntu font, possibly Monospace. Tbh, I'm not sure whether the fixed-width font is determined by a Firefox setting or a forum default. I'd not really thought about it until you asked. :)
