### Post by floke on 2007-01-17
Well, the original pondering from Decartes was designed to demonstrate the fact of his existence (i think therefore I am), but he was not saying that on those ocassions where we don't think per se (e.g. when unconscious or acting on instinct) we cease to exist. To the extent, then, that the Ubuntu line is an homage or a nod to Decartes, I think we can consider it safe on the existence/non-existence angle.

Who'd have thought that Ubuntu could be so ontologically challenging?
