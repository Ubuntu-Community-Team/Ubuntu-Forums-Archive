The title for this thread shows "bold" on my computer. There are also formatting options showing on my screen as I type. "B" is for **"bold"**, "I" for *"italics"* and "u" for _"underline"_ - if **bold** is not showing in **bold** on your screen it could be something to do with your browser settings.

EDIT: Highlight the word or words you want to emphasise and then choose bold (or italics or whatever)
