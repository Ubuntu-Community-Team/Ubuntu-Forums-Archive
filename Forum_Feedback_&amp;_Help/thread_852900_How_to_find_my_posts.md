### Post by nothingspecial on 2008-07-08
Click on your name, top right of the screen after "welcome"
On the right of the screen near the top it says "mini statistics"
Click show all statistics.
On the left it will give you your post count.
Underneath it will say "find all posts by cpcnw"
Click it.
You can do that for any user that allows it.:-({|=
