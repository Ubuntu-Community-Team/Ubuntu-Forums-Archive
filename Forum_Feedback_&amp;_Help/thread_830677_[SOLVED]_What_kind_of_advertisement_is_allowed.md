Should such threads be reported and user banned?Users who register only to post any sort of advertisement are violating the site terms of service, also known as the [Forums Code of Conduct]("http://ubuntuforums.org/index.php?page=policy"). This is a bannable offense. We tend to err on the side of leniency when advertising is done by long time forum members with a history of real contributions to the community, so long as the advertising isn't rude, over the top, and is confined to only one post.

The details/rules are in the CoC thread I linked to above. Within those guidelines, staff have the leeway to use discretion, often doing so after discussing the matter with other staff and coming to a consensus.

If someone feels that there has been a violation, reporting the post is the best thing to do. That will bring it to the attention of all the staff, and the post/thread will be reviewed as soon as a staff member can do so.
