### Post by coffeecat on 2014-10-28
*Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

I don't understand your question. Closed threads are visible. Why do you think they are not?

Threads are autoclosed after a year's inactivity for two main reasons:

1 - To prevent aimless and repeated necromancy of old threads where people needing help are better served by starting new threads.

2 - Old threads tend to refer to obsolete versions of Ubuntu and the advice in them may not be applicable to the latest versions. Again, it is better for those seeking help to start new threads.

If you need help, simply start your own thread. If you think that there is useful information in an old closed thread, simply link to it in your post.

Do you have a link to a thread that you can't see?
