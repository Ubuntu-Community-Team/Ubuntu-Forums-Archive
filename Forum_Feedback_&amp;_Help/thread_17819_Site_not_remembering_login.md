### Post by aysiu on 2005-06-12
Instead of having Firefox "remember" your password, you can simply not log out of the forums and have Firefox always keep the [www.ubuntuforums.org](www.ubuntuforums.org) cookie. Go to Edit > Preferences > Privacy > Cookies > Exceptions. Add *[www.ubuntuforums.org](www.ubuntuforums.org)* and click "Allow." Then, before leaving the Ubuntu forums, just don't click "log out."
