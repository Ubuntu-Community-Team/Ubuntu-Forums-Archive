### Post by Wobblybob on 2009-10-04
click on the # number of the message you wish to quote at the top far right of the post. Yours is #1, clicking on it gives the address [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8052602&postcount=1](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8052602&postcount=1) in the browser address bar then just add this in your post [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8052602&postcount=1]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8052602&postcount=1") gives you a link to just that post.
