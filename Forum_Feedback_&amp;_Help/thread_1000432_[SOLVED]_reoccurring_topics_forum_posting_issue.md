### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-12-02
While you can reply to existing topics, users cannot make new topics in Recurring Discussions. The goal is to *not* post a thread that you know occurs often, but to just have staff move threads that _do_ happen to appear often into that area.

Chances are the topic you want to post about already exists in that forum as well, give it a search.
