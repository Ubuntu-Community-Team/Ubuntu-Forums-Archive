### Post by PriceChild on 2007-07-24
[SIZE=2]For your avatar (appears left of your name on every post) go here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editavatar](http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editavatar)
Then upload a picture using that tool  of a maximum size of 90 by 90 pixels or 19.5 KB (whichever is smaller).

Same for the Profile picture (appears on the right of member profile page, e.g. on here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=43712](http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=43712) )
Go here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editprofilepic](http://ubuntuforums.org/profile.php?do=editprofilepic) and upload a picture [/SIZE]with maximum size 100 by 100 pixels or 64.0 KB (whichever is smaller).
