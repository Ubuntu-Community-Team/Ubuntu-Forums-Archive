### Post by coffeecat on 2020-03-04
Assuming you have selected the Ubuntu Mobile Style in the drop-down bottom left of any page, simply click on the three dots icon top right of any page, then click on Profile. On your profile page, scroll down a bit and you will see "Find latest posts" and "Find latest started threads".
