First, let me start off by saying that the concept of the "How-To's" Forum is a value-added feature.  I personally find it challenging at times to wade through the postings to find the exact content I'm looking for.  This can be especially difficult for the many members that recently joined the world of Ubuntu who aren't up to speed on the terminology.  My suggestion is to provide a site that would list by master category ie. Multimedia, the included subjects with associated links to the master How-To.  This would, in essence provide a semi-drill down approach to the how-to's.  

Just a friendly suggestion.
