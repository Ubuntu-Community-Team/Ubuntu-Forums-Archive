### Post by Giant Speck on 2009-09-20
If I have it correct, when the moderators close a thread "for review," it doesn't necessarily mean that there is anything wrong with the thread in question.  The moderators close it because they have received enough post reports that they have to go through them to make sure that the complaints are valid and relevant and decide on further action.  Sometimes the reports are invalid and therefore the thread is reopened.  Other times, the reports are valid, and the thread remains closed.  If the thread remains closed, the moderators usually tack on an additional post to the closed thread that indicates why it remains closed.
