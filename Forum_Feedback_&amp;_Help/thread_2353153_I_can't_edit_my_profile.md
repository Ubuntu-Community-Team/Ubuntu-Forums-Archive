### Post by coffeecat on 2017-02-19
Presumably you hadn't logged in for a while. Since 2012 perhaps? You need to wait for up to an hour for the script to adjust permissions on your account. If you haven't logged in since about 2012 you've been caught between the 25-post and 10-post rule. With 22 beans you should be fine once the script has run. Hang in there for a few minutes and you should be fine.

For more details, see:

[https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393)

>  Once an account has reached this level the hourly script will adjust permissions on recently logged in accounts. If you have logged in for the first time after a period of inactivity, please be patient - it may take up to an hour for your permissions to change.
