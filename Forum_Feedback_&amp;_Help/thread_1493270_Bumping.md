### Post by SlidingHorn on 2010-05-25
To "bump" a thread, you just post a new reply, which will bring it back to the top of the list.  It is recommended that you wait about 24 hours or so before doing this.  If you've waited a week, it's more than enough time and you're a lot more patient a person than most ;)  

Give it a bump and let's see what your issue is.
