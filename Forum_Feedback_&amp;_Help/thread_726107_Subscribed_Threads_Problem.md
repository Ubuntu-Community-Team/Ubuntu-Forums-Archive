### Post by kko1 on 2008-03-17
I believe this is as designed, not a problem.

If you use the *"Instant notification"* subscription mode, you will only be notified of the first reply. A direct quote from the notification e-email:
> There may also be other replies, but you will not receive any more notifications until you visit the forum again.

If you would instead prefer reading all the replies through your e-mail inbox, you can use the *"Daily notification"* subscription mode.

I can understand why it works this way - the volume of traffic would probably increase quite a lot if an instant notification were sent for each and every reply. The daily notification is of course a bit slower to arrive, but you do get all the replies this way.

Hope this helps.

kko1
