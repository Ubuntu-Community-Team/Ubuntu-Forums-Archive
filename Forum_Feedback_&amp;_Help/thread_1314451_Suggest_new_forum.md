I'd like to suggest a new forum for ubuntuforums.org, devoted to third-party web sites' support (or lack thereof) of people using Ubuntu.

Time and time again, when I have a problem with a web site, their support blames it on the fact that I'm using Ubuntu. Sometimes this is correct, as with stupid coupon-printer software that's designed for Windows but won't run under Wine. Often, it's just a lame excuse (it doesn't work; I checked a box for an OS they don't "support"; thus it must be the OS's fault). Other times, there's a workaround, and people here are much more likely to know it.

Whaddaya think?
