Writing 'funny' English is a good recipe for not fetching many answers. 

Many people can read regular English really fast. As soon as the language becomes non-standard, the reading speed drops to a fraction. If you give the impression that you intentionally make your posts harder to read or that you can't be bothered to make them readable, people like me will then quickly pass on to posts that ***can*** be read.
