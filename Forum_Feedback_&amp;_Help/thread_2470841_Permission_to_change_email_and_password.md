### Post by coffeecat on 2022-01-13
There is no post number restriction for changing your forum email. Now that your forum and Ubuntu One SSO accounts are linked, you can change the email in either or both of your forum and SSO accounts so that they no longer match, if you wish. The link between SSO account and forum account can only be broken by a forum admin. Email matching is only needed for initial linking.

If you do change your forum email, please remember two things:

[list=1][*]You will need to re-activate your forum account from the verification email sent to the new email address. If you don't verify the new email, you won't be able to post.
[*]You change your email in the "Edit Email and Password" section of your profile settings. Ignore the password field - as CharlesA pointed out, you use your SSO password to login, not the forum password, which is now superfluous.[/list]
