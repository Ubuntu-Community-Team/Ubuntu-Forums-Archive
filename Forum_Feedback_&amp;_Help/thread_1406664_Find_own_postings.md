### Post by m_duck on 2010-02-14
Two quick ways to find your posts using the top bar:

*Quick Links -> Subscribed Threads*
I think by default, when you make a post to a thread, you are subscribed  to it.

*Search -> Find All Your Posts / Threads*
Will display all the posts or threads that you have made.
