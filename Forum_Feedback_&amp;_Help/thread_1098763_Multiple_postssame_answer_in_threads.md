### Post by OrangeCrate on 2009-03-17
As with many, I have enjoyed reading about other distros here, and was disappointed to see those forums disappear. But, I do understand the burden that they, and older threads place on the servers, and most important here, is that we concentrate on Ubuntu and current topics.

A few minutes ago, I lost my temper, and fired a quick post to a member who had posted an answer to the OP's question, seven hours after the previous last post, which offered the same identical answer. I've since edited out my comment (which really just asked him if he had read the post right above his), and have requested that a Mod delete the empty post.

(And no, I'm not talking about two people who are posting at the same time, which is understandable duplication.)

But, not to let it drop, I've decided to post my observations here, and though I'm sure that it is probably not a new topic to staff, it'll make me feel better to share my thoughts with you.

In order to make the forums interesting and fun, one of the concepts introduced was the beans. Looking at a lot of threads over time, I think a lot of the bloat on the forums are members who have been bitten by the "post whore" bug, and a preponderance of many of the answers being the same in threads, proves my point.

A lot of people are chasing the beans, rather than even reading the threads, to see if their answer has already been posted. Cumulative, that has had to be a significant part of the bloat the forums have experienced as they have gotten larger.

This suggestion will probably get me stoned by the masses, but I think a lot of the multiple posts would disappear if the bean thingee was discontinued, and replaced with a simple post count.

I know there's a fine line between providing fun to the members, and providing a technical data base for Ubuntu specifically, and Linux in general. (Particularly since there's no customer service to call, and the community is all we have.)

I for one, would have much preferred the other operating system forums, and a larger resource base, than the beans, and other "fun" forums that clog up the system.

I know that my suggestion will not be implemented, and this post is moot, but hopefully, people who read this, will think twice, and read threads before they post in them. Maybe a ground swell of members knowing about this will reduce some of the bloat going forward...
