### Post by aysiu on 2008-06-06
A bump is any post that has its main purpose drawing more attention to your thread. You shouldn't bump more than once every 24 hours.

If you're not receiving any responses, it's likely that your problem is an uncommon one or one that isn't easily solved, or both.
