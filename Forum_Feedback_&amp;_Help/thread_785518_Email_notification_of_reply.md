### Post by p_quarles on 2008-05-07
> **forestpixie said:**
> Subscribe to it - when you get the confirmation dialog there is an option to be notified
You can also change the notification type after having subscribed. Just go to "subscribed threads" (under the "Quick Links" menu at the top of the forum), check the threads you wish to change, and select the type of notification you'd like. The choices are "instant e-mail notification," "daily e-mail," and "none."
