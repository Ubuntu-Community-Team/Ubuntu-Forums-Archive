### Post by Bucky Ball on 2010-06-15
At top right near thread tools: Quick Links->Subscribed Threads.

Best to make a folder for threads that you create called 'My Threads' or something like it. Much easier to find than trawling through, in my case, over a thousand. ;)

### Post by linuxisfree on 2010-06-15
If you wanna see the threads you've started go to 'Search' --> 'Find All Your Threads'
 
If you wanna see all your posts regardless of whether you started the thread go to 'Search' --> 'Find All Your Posts'
