### Post by bapoumba on 2009-01-21
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=677396](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=677396)
Here are the guidelines I was referring to (I thought it was in the Staff area, but they were also posted in the T&T section).
One important point is the support and update of tutorial, general interest and accurate infos.

Rather than not approving, it was decided to move to the general areas if a tutorial did not meet the requirements. If you have threads that qualify (say it became of much interest) you should report it so that the mods involved in the approval process can have a look and move it back to T&T.
At some point, we had a lot of reposts from blog articles by 1 bean posters (looking for traffic, most probably), so we felt we needed guidelines for the T&T be useful to users.
