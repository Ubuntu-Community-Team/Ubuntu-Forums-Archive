### Post by coffeecat on 2012-02-23
Since you don't know your login password, you can request a new one. Try to login (not from Launchpad) and you will get an error page but with a link for resetting a forgotten password. Click on that and you are taken to a page where you have to enter your email address and answer a simple anti-spammer question. Instructions will then be emailed to you.

Make sure that your email account is not putting emails from ubuntuforums into the spam folder. That maybe what happened to your original registration email.
