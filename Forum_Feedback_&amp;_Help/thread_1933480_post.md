### Post by Iowan on 2012-02-29
**dragonfly41**'s way is easier - but for the sake of completeness, you can also click on your username in the top right corner where it says: **Welcome, lunarbane1**
By [COLOR="Red"]Visitor Messages[/COLOR] is a "Statistics" button that opens an area with:

Total Posts

    Total Posts: 5
    Posts Per Day: 3.62
    Find all posts by lunarbane1
    Find all threads started by lunarbane1

You can click on one of the bottom two selections to get a list of your posts/threads which has a link to take you to the post/thread.
