### Post by drs305 on 2012-01-14
Normally when a spammer is reported we will issue an infraction and move the spam (post or thread) to Spamalot. Then we search for other posts by the spammer and remove them as well (as often they post multiple spam links in a short period of time).

Yesterday I handled 5-6 of your reports. I would rather have too many reports than too few, but as has been mentioned one report per spammer is generally sufficient if the mods follow the general methods above.

Thanks for your help in keeping the Ubuntu Forums a positive experience for us all.  :-)
