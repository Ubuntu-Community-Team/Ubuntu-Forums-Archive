### Post by lisati on 2008-07-18
I sometimes have a little fun with the spammers, particularly the ones who send me variations of the "Nigerian" scam and those that tell me I've won in a lottery that I've never entered. One way is to politely point out that they know enough about me to contact me, why not find out the rest of the contact details through an online phone book..... I also point out that they don't need my bank account number to send me the money by Paypal, which would also save them having to pay a courier for the bank draft (or whatever other "fee" they're asking for). Sometimes they reply (which gets a response with similar information) and sometimes they don't.....

There are also other not-so-nice ways of dealing with them.......
