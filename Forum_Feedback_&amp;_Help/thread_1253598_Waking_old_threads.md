### Post by lisati on 2009-08-31
One of the potential difficulties I see with re-opening old threads is that the information contained in the earlier posts can be out of date or the thread might have been closed because things got out of hand. 

One possibility is to start a new thread with a comment to the effect of "I am experiencing a problem similar to this" or "I experienced a problem similar to this but found a solution" followed by a link to the old thread and any other information that might be relevant.
