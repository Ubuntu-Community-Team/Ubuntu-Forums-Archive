### Post by nick_h on 2008-05-23
Select Quick Links -> Subscribed Threads from the menu.
Click the check box to the right of the threads that you want to unsubscribe from.
At the bottom of the page under "selected threads" change "move to folder" to "delete subscription".
Click the "Go" button.
