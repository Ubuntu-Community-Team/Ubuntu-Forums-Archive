### Post by slickymaster on 2021-07-22
If you don't log out, your session will last 24 hours even if you close your browser. 

There's one thing though, if your public-facing IP address changes, you will usually find yourself logged out. That's a feature of the VBulletin software that the forum runs on. If you are in the situation where your ISP changes your IP address regularly, that will affect you, and there's nothing we can do about it.
