### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-12-07
> **Chris Edgell said:**
> I just tried the button, multi-quote this message...nothing down here.

Click the multi-quote icon on every post you want to quote and then click "New reply" button. All selected quotes will be included in the new reply body.


> **Chris Edgell said:**
> And how do you get that box around the quote, anyway

The box around the quote text is parsed automatically. You can click the "Preview Post" button instead of "Submit Reply" to see the quote with the box.

To quote something, use something like this:
 
```
[noparse]
> At this point I can only follow the simpliest of instructions..
I thank you so much!
[/noparse]
```
It will appear like this:

> At this point I can only follow the simpliest of instructions..
