### Post by John.Michael.Kane on 2008-08-01
This was discussed, and debated.

For reference. 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=361608&highlight=expert](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=361608&highlight=expert)

IMHO, separating the forums in different factions, would give a perceived notion of elitism, and in most cases not solve anything, as new users would simply post their questions where they think they would get a better response, in this case the proposed expert help section, and not the beginner sections.

At the end of the day these forums if I am not mistaken was built around making all members welcome, no matter their level or knowledge of Gnu/Ubuntu/Linux, and to section it off would do a disservice to it's foundation.
