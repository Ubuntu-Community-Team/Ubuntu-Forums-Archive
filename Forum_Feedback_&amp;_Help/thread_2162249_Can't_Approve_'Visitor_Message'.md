### Post by coffeecat on 2013-07-15
@VinDSL, I have a simpler possible explanation than yours. :) I think it's simply a bug/undocumented feature of Vbulletin 4. With a dummy account in the >25 posts usergroup, I can see what you and Elfy see - delete, approve, and unapprove messages under moderation tools. There's a forum setting which allows people in the >25 posts usergroup, but not those with fewer posts, to manage visitor messages in their profile. This is intended to allow people to (soft) delete VMs. I can't be 100% sure that I remember this correctly, but before the upgrade from vBulletin 3, I think that's all you could see - delete VMs. It looks as though approve and unapprove - which are moderation tools - have come along for a pointless ride. Since non-moderators shouldn't be seeing moderation tools and since you can't do anything with them, I shouldn't worry about it.

My guess is that if this is not due to a bug, that it's a quirk of some obscure setting deep within adminCP. I'll have a rummage around in there when I get time.
