### Post by matthew on 2007-12-17
We agree. This has been discussed many times. The problem is how to fix it. The search mechanism we have now is a bit of a hack, and is much better than the original search that comes with the forum software. Even so, we are trying to balance high server loads with limited resources and doing what we can.

I hate to say it, but much of the time searching via Google is more effective by putting the following in the Google search box:

```
searchterm(s) site:ubuntuforums.org
```

This won't give results that are up to the minute, but they will get you within the last day or two, which is usually more than adequate. 

We have this in our minds and will make any and all improvements that we are able to make, the minute we are able to do so.

Thanks for the feedback, and sorry we don't have a better response (yet?).
