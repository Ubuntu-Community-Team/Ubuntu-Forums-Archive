### Post by PaulW2U on 2015-06-29

> Am I missing something? Shouldn't a search take you to the specific comment, and not the whole thread???

Hi john409,

If you use the Advanced Search you'll see that near the bottom of the screen is an option to choose "Threads" or "Beans". If you select "Beans" then the search results are listed by post and not thread.

Hope that helps.
