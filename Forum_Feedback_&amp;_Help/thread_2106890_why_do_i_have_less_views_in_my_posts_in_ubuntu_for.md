### Post by Paddy Landau on 2013-01-20
I've had a quick look at your threads.

The answer is that when people see the title, they don't know what you're asking, so they don't bother looking.

Consider the title of this thread: "viewing". What's that supposed to mean?

Please make your title precise, descriptive, specific, and clear, so that people can see at a glance what you want. For example, this thread could have been called, "Why are people not viewing my threads?"

Another example is your thread, "[tree view in nautilus]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2106884")". Again, what is that supposed to mean? What do you want? How about this title: "How do I hide the System Folder from the tree in the Nautilus side-bar?"

Do you understand the difference? Imagine that you are telephoning your friend to ask for advice. You wouldn't say, "[failed to fetch!]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2103005")" You'd say, "Update Manager failed with error message: failed to fetch. How can I fix this?" Then, in the body, you would say what you were doing, what you expected to happen, and what happened instead. If appropriate, you also say how you tried to solve it. Also say what system you are running (e.g. Ubuntu 12.04 64-bit), and any other relevant information.
