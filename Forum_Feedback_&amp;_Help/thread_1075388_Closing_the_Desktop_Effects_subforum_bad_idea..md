### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2009-02-20
There is a sticky in the Cafe [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1071591") explaining some recent changes.

Desktop Effects was one of the forums that was closed down and merged with General Help.  When that forum area was first created, effects did not come installed by default in Ubuntu and it was felt that they deserved their own category due to their uniqueness and growing popularity.  Beryl merged with Compiz to form Compiz Fusion and has since stabilized pretty well and become a default window manager in Ubuntu and is well integrated.  There were already tons of questions about effects appearing in ABT and GH, so it made sense to get rid of the forum as part of recent housecleaning efforts.  Effects are an important topic, but GH and ABT are perfectly acceptable places to ask these questions.

And people do go through GH and ABT looking for unanswered posts, some join the Unanswered Posts Team here on the forums.

We're sorry that this has affected you, it's impossible to make changes without somebody getting upset, but we are doing the best we can with what we have.  Re-arranging the forums is nothing new, it just happens to have affected you this time around.  I hope this doesn't put you off from providing support to users here.
