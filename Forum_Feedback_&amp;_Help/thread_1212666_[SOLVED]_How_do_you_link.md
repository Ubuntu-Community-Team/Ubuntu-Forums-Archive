### Post by raymondh on 2009-07-13
I've seen posts with a word underlined which links to a webpage/reference.  How do you do that?

---

### Post by pbpersson on 2009-07-14
You highlight the phrase you want to link in your message.  Then you click on the "link" icon in the toolbar above your text and when it opens you insert the web address into the blank field and click on OK
