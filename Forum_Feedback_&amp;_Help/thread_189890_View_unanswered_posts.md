### Post by detour on 2006-06-05
Following the release of Dapper, support posts are coming fast and furious. I worry that some posts might get lost in the shuffle and discourage new users. They may think that no one is able/willing to help them with their problem. I have seen on other forums a link that allows you to view recent posts that have not received any replies. I think it would be really helpful if this forum also provided such a link so that more knowledgable people can easily find posts that have not been answered and address them appropriately. Even if that means just linking a common question to another thread discussing the same problem. We were all beginners at one time, we remember what it is like to sit there, completely stumped, waiting for someone to come along and point us in the right direction. Hopefully something like this will make things a little better for everyone, "street cred" for the regulars and more posts getting answered for the beginners.
