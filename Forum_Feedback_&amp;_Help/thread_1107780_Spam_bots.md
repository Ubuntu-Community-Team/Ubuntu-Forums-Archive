### Post by wirepuller134 on 2009-03-30
Our experience with them, we manage just over 300 commercial forums presently, is they are looking to boost their search engine ranking.  They are using link backs that are picked up by search engines, thus boosting their ranking and giving them occasional traffic. 
 The best solution we have come up with is to put a key word on a page of the website and have the registration page tell the person where to go to get it.  We also don't allow any personal information to be entered into the user profile until after registration is approved. These two changes have been working well for us for over a year, I'm sure they will break it one day but until then!!
