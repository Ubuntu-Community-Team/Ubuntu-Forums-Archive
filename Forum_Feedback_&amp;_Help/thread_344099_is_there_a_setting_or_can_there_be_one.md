### Post by matthew on 2007-01-22
For threads you have already posted, open the thread and then choose Thread Tools->Subscribe to thread.

For the future look in User CP->Edit Options->(under Messaging & Notification) Default Thread Subscription Mode and change the default to automatically subscribe to all threads you post in with your preferred mode of notification.
