### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2009-09-12
Nope, you can't delete a post.  You can only edit the post and remove the contents.  However, the forum software won't let you save an empty post, so you have to leave something in its place...  I usually just do something like > Edit: Accidental double post
The only other option is to click the "Report abuse" button on your second post and ask a mod/admin to remove it for you.  They tend to be pretty busy though, so I would just go with editing the second one like in my example.
