### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2009-02-04
T&T is OK so long as your post meets the criteria defined in the sticky.  Making symlinks is easy once you know how, I wouldn't say it's complete trivial though.  I certainly had problems!

BTW, wiki.ubuntu.com is the team wiki area and also contains a lot of very unofficial documentation and guides.  help.ubuntu.com is the official system docs in html format, and help.ubuntu.com/community are the Community Docs.  The Community Docs are where the best supported guides should be hosted and maintained, and there is a lot of linking and redirecting between the team wiki and the community docs.
