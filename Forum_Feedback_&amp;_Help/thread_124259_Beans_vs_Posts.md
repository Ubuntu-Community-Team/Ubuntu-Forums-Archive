### Post by kewl1uk on 2006-02-01
On a vBulletin board such as this, an Admin can decide whether posts in a certain forum are counted in the post count or not. The post count in posts are shown minus posts in disallowed forums but the post count in profiles shows all posts since that releates to a search for all posts when the find all posts by user is used. It's the way a vBulletin board works. It's pretty common on many boards for chat forums which don't relate to the subject topic of the board to be configured so that posts are not counted. Posts or Beans - just a cosmetic change in the board theme and they both mean the same thing.
