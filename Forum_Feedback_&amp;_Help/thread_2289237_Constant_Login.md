### Post by coffeecat on 2015-08-21
> **seanos said:**
> I also seem to get logged out often and SSO login is very slow. Is there a default time in which you will be logged out? Can it be changed?

You will be auto-logged out after 24 hours of inactivity, or if your external IP changes. If you are being logged out before then, you need to look for local causes. Do any of your browser extensions clear cookies? For example, do you have something like Self-Destructing Cookies installed and ubuntuforums not whitelisted?
