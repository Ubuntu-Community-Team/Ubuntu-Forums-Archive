(although this is out of context for this thread, I believe I should give you a few notes as to how we handle some situations in particular.)

When threads are already in the backyard, and they have been step-by-step getting to a point where removing half of the threads leaves the thread un-readable, we generally close them.

Also, when threads are in the backyard, and one half of a debate is "out of hand" ... instead of removing that one-half of the debate and thus, rendering the debate one-sided, we will simply close the thread.

Threads in the backyard have a general rule of "closed at the sole discretion of staff, no questions asked," and while, yes, we could dump them into the jail, the usual reason they are in the backyard to begin with is that they weren't "appropriate for all ages," and by moving them to the jail, we move them from an age-restricted area to an area that is not restricted... which isn't what we want to do.
