### Post by QIII on 2014-09-04
I have read through a number of your posts.

I suggest that the issue is that your questions are vague.  "I'm having trouble with resolution on my TV when I connect to it with an HDMI cable" is not sufficient.

Perhaps if you posted your hardware specs and your release/flavor you would get more responses.  People don't have crystal balls.  The more detail you include the better.

Don't expect people to go out of their way to drag the information out of you.

Also, when you are in the habit of bumping by referring to "talking to a brick wall" your attitude puts people off.
