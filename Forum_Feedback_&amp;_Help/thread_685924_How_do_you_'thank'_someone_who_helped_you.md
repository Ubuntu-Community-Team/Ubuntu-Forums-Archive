### Post by popch on 2008-02-04
> **pmasiar said:**

Bugs re cheaper to fix early.

In my opinion, it is quite possible that the people who are about to develop the 'thanking' feature know how to do that and are proceeding according to their knowledge.

There are several process models which lead to finished software, and most of them consist of several phases. In earlier phases you collect requirements which also include functionality, usability, data security and others. In later phases, you do not collect o revise requirements again unless it becomes obvious that you missed important ones or something in the projected environment changed so much that the original requirement list no longer applies.

In other words, unless I was invited to make suggestions in a project of others I would not insist in doing so.
