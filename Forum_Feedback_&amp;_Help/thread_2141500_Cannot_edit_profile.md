### Post by Shansie on 2013-05-14

EDIT: I think I've found the issue. It refreshes every hour. so someone who has just recently hit 25+ beans won't be able to edit their profile until permissions are refreshed. So in an hour or two, it should work fine, as long as everything is running smoothly. Here's the quote from the sticky where it answered it:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2034393)
> [COLOR=#000000]Once you have achieved [/COLOR]50[COLOR=#000000] 25 posts in support areas, all profile customisation (including avatar and signature) will be available. A script runs every hour on the active (logged in) accounts to adjust permissions so it may take up to one hour for this to happen after your post count has reached [/COLOR]50[COLOR=#000000]25. Please note that posts in the Community Cafe and its sub-forums, Forum Feedback & Help and its sub-forums, and the Testimonials & Experiences forum do not increase your post count.[/COLOR]
