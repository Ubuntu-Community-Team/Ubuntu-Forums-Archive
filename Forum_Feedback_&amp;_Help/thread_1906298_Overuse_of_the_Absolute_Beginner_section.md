### Post by Tamlynmac on 2012-01-08
If you were to read the sectional guidelines. 

Absolute Beginner:
> The perfect starting place to find out more about computers, Linux and Ubuntu.

General Help:
> All your general support questions for Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Edubuntu, Xubuntu and Lubuntu.

The descriptions are different. Each section has a description (top of page) of it's suggested contents. These can be used as basic tools to help identify what types of threads one might expect to find in that section or where to place a thread.

I think some are confused as to which section a thread best fits and thus they often wind up in either Absolute Beginner or General Help.
