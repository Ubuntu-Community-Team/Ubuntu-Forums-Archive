### Post by geirha on 2008-10-31
Just edit one of the posts, and replace the content with something like "Double post". 

There's also a report post button at the lower left of each post, where you can alert moderaters that it needs removing, but just clearing the content of one of the posts is easiest, and people won't mind it much.
