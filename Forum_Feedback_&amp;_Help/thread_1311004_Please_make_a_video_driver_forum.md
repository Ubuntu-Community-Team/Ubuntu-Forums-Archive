### Post by tormod on 2009-11-02
Currently people are posting problems about video drivers in many different forums: Multimedia, Desktop, etc.

It would be nice to have this in one place. Of course people will have to understand what is an issue with a video driver vs problems with a movie player or a desktop setting.

Having subforums for each driver or card manufacturer would be a plus.

With the current situation I feel there is no good way for bug triagers and developers focused on the Xorg stack to get a feeling for what issues people experience in this area, or to post information. Personally I use the phoronix.com forums. I would not recommend the average Ubuntu forum user to post there though.
