This came up the other day. Only a forum moderator or administrator can close a thread:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1074946](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1074946)

Forum members used to be able to mark threads as solved. The following was posted by ubuntu-geek:

> In a post earlier this month I mentioned the "Thank you" feature and the ability to mark your posts "Solved" have been removed. We hope to add them back or find an alternative method that is less intense on our database. 

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1044714](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1044714)

As an alternative, you can edit your original post and add [SOLVED] to the thread title.
