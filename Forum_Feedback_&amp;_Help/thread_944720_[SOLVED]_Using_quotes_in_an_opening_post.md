### Post by pp. on 2008-10-11
Not quite. You can insert a quote wherever you please. It's just a matter of correct markup. There's even an icon in the Reply_to_Thread editor.

This is an example of a manually entered quote:

[QUOTE=Forrest Gumpp]Spurious quote[/QUOTE]

You could have inserted this even in the first post of this thread.

### Post by Joeb454 on 2008-10-11
