### Post by drs305 on 2009-02-11
'Solved' and 'Thanks' are both currently disabled as the servers are worked on.

You can edit the title to include 'Solved' by hitting Edit, and then Edit again. As you noted, you don't see the title change until you open it.

The other thing you can do is edit the text and put 'Solved' as the first word. You would see this if you hover over a thread and get the balloon. 

Neither is a good substitute but are at least a bit of assistance to helpers.
