### Post by coffeecat on 2015-06-21
*Thread moved to **Forum Feedback & Help**.*

Click on the settings link (top right), then on the &#8220;View all Subscribed Threads&#8221; button. You can select threads with the tickboxes on the right, and then mass delete subscriptions, or change subscription type, with the &#8220;Selected Threads&#8221; button at the bottom on the right.

As far as more than a certain age is concerned, there is no specific search function for subscribed threads, but if you page back through your subscribed threads until you get to the age you are interested in removing, you can mass mark each page with the checkbox at the top.

You can also create subscription folders under "my subscriptions" at the left if you wish to move specific categories of interest into your own defined folders.
