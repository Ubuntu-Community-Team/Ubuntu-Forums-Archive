### Post by geraldm on 2008-01-13
I have thought that the renewed attention to "Thanks" is a nice addition to the forums.
A persons first thought about the forum is to ask, and answer, a one-to-one relationship.
But it raises an awareness that any answer may be searched for and provide a solution for others, later on.  I have seen some older threads come with updated thanks from being sent by other persons with a similar problem.  It encourages responders to state clearly in a context that  
other observers can understand as well.  Then those answers can be developed into more helpful articles in the community documentation area.   All in all, this is a very friendly community to grow with.

Gerald
