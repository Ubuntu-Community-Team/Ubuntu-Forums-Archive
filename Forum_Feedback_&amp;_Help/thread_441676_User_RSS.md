### Post by bapoumba on 2007-05-13
Hello,
not sure it would be easy to set up custom rss feeds for many users.

- In the search option you can select all your threads or posts to view
- You can subscribe to threads you post in automatically (in user CP > Options) and get an instant email once someone posts in, and subscribe to threads you have not posted in from the "thread tool" menu.
In the email, you can read what has been posted.
