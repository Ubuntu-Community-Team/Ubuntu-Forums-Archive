### Post by mörgæs on 2014-05-30
Your posts are long, which scares people away, especially if they are not born English speakers. You will do yourself a favour by cutting the text down to the very minimal. 

Some of the problems are complicated, for example the thread to which you link. There's not always an answer to give, at least not for a bunch of volunteers like us.
