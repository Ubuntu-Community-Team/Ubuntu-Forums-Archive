> **matt_symes said:**

On top, to the right, you can see something like this

>  		**Welcome, *NickName***
		You last visited: nn Hours Ago at HH:MM A/PM  		 			Private Messages: Unread nnn, Total nnn.
 		  		 	

Click on the "Private Messages" part, it is a link. This will take you to your "Control Panel", on the left you can find "List subscriptions" (Or click/bookmark [THIS]("http://ubuntuforums.org/subscription.php?do=viewsubscription") link)

From there on, you can select single threads or the whole page and in the drop-down, select an action (*Delete subscription*) and click "Go"

### Post by hansdown on 2011-11-10
Hi matt_symes.

You can delete an entire page at a time, also.

You need to view all threads, and put a check in the box of each thread you wish to delete.

Next change the setting at the bottom of the page, from "move to folder", to "delete subscription".
