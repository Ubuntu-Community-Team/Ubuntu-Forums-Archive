### Post by dmizer on 2009-03-12
Just because you have been using Ubuntu for a long time does not mean you are a veteran. I've been using Ubuntu for over 4 years now, and there are still many aspects I would consider myself to be an absolute beginner in.

If you feel more comfortable posting in absolute beginner, there's nothing wrong with that, but be aware that you may get more specialized help by posting in specific categories. Also, think about the question you're trying to ask. Is it something that a beginner would want to do or even know about? Configuring cron or iptables, for example, might be something better suited for a specialized forum.

Really, it comes down to just using your own judgement. No one is going to reprimand you for using the absolute beginner forum even though you have a long history with Ubuntu. At most, someone will suggest that your thread be moved to a specialized forum with hopes that it will get more attention there.
