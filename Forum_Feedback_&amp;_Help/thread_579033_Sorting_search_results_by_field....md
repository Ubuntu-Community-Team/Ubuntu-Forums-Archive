I only recently figured out that if you want to sort your search results you have to do so via an advanced search. Although simple enough once you know this, I have to suspect that a large subset of new users are clicking in frustration on those grey column headers on the search page, wondering how to find the thread with the most replies, or how to sort threads according to their forum. Given that new users are less likely to use the advanced search, I would think that providing basic sort functionality on the results page would go a long way in helping new users find the information they need and keep them from flooding the forums with duplicated threads.
