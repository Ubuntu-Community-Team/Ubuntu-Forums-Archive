### Post by coffeecat on 2013-04-07
@zika, do you have your settings set to profile on top or profile on left? If profile on top, then a one line sig takes up a little more room that a one-line post. But if on the left, the sig area is much smaller than the post area, with a large amount of blank space in the post area if the post is short. As Elfy, mentioned, this latter is on our todo list. But as far as sigs being a little larger than posts, I'm not sure a forced smaller font size would be a good idea, even selective forcing. There would be complaints about readability.
