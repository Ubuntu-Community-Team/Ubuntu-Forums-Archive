### Post by coffeecat on 2015-05-08
> **khelben1979 said:**
> All I can see is that you can  post [URL] and no HTML is supported either, so I cannot post a link without writing out the whole link, and it's very irritating, especially if the link is at above 100 characters.

As others have said, the link icon is still present in the toolbars of **both** the quick and advanced post editors. They haven't gone away. And if you want a basic url hyperlink, you don't need them. Simply copy-paste your link into your post and the forum software will automatically add BBCode url tags around them in order to create a hyperlink in your post. No need to to type them out by hand.

I'm not sure what you mean by html support. If you mean that the forum doesn't support active html in your post that affects how your post is rendered on-screen, then of course it doesn't! That would be a major security hole. What it does support is a very comprehensive range of [BBCode tags]("http://ubuntuforums.org/mi - I never use them. sc.php?do=bbcode") that allow you to achieve some sophisticated formatting, if you wish. 

> **khelben1979 said:**
> I seriously don't know what has happened to this forum (!)

Nothing has happened to the forum, but perhaps something has happened to your browser. Make sure you aren't blocking scripts of the forum or yahooapis.com. See if a new profile in firefox helps.
