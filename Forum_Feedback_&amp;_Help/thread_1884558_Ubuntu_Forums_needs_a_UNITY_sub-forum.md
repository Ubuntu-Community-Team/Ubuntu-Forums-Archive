### Post by snowpine on 2011-11-21
A Unity sub-forum should be created and prominently listed among the main support categories. This sub-forum could have helpful Sticky threads on topics like Fallback mode, alternate desktop environments, 10.04 LTS, and other distros still using Gnome 2. I'm imagining a polite and respectful sub-forum for discussing the pros and cons of, and alternatives to, Unity. Members who are sick of the topic can easily avoid this sub-forum altogether.

Currently Unity is Ubuntu's #1 most visible and unique feature that sets it apart from other distros. Unity discussion should be front-and-center, not tucked away in Recurring Discussions. I sense frustration from forum members who want to share their experiences but are then rebuked for "whining" or "pointless complaining." But it's a two-edged sword: if the feedback is "pointless" (in the sense that decision-makers at Canonical are not closely following the Forums) then it is also harmless. Best for the moderators to come to terms with this and set up an appropriate venue for such discussion. 

With respect from a long-time forum member posting his first-ever Forum Feedback,
Snowpine
