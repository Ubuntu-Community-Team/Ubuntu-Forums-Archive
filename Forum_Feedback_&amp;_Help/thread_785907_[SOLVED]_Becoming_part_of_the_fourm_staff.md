### Post by bapoumba on 2008-05-08
> **p_quarles said:**
> The Forum Council (made of the administrators) select staff. You can always suggest someone, but the ultimate decision lies with them.

Just to add a comment here. When new staff are needed (usually with new releases and an increased number of members), we have a round up in the Staff area, and current mods are free to suggest new names. Then we discuss. Beans are not a proof of skills, as someone with a low number of posts may be able to code circles around most of us, but if someone has been around for some time, for 1000-2000 posts, without any issue, been nice and helpful, not easily upset, it is an element we consider.

Being on staff requires more human skills than computer skills, as we have to deal with the grumpy users :)
And being able to work in a team, be trusted, be able to start something and have others finish it (we are scattered all over the timezones) etc.

The final decision is on the Forum Council. They send the invitations based on the discussions we had. Works pretty well :popcorn:
