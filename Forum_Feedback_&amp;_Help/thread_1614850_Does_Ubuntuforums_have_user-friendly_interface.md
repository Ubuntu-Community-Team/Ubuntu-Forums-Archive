### Post by P4man on 2010-11-06
Go to User CP > options >  Messaging & Notification 

Make sure you subsribe to each thread automatically (with or without email notification thats your choice. I have it off, too many subscriptions :) ). Then you can click quicklinks > subscribed threads. Thats actually the page I have bookmarked, so upon entering the forum I get to see all threads I posted in.

### Post by Elfy on 2010-11-06
Top of the page - search - drop down - show my threads or posts

User control panel - set to subscribe and get instant notification e-mail notification.

Going to your usercp [http://ubuntuforums.org/usercp.php](http://ubuntuforums.org/usercp.php) will show you if there are new posts on any of the threads you are subscribed to.
