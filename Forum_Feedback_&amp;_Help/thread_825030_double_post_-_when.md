### Post by forestpixie on 2008-06-11
If you have posted a thread - if after 24 hours it hasn't had an answer then reply to you own thread - it 'bumps' it to the top of the list again.

But - you need to wait for a couple of good reasons - it's good manners to wait a while, obviously everyone is volunteeering their time here - if you keep bumping you could end up with some cross replies.

Also, there is an unanswered thread team who look out for them - if you keep bumping then they won't see it.
