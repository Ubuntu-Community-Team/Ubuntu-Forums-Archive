### Post by ajgreeny on 2021-04-15
I'm not sure if your inactivity for the past 10 years will make any difference to your ability to make these changes but try **Settings** at the very top of the forum page, and then the **My Settings -> My Account -> General Settings**

There you should scroll down to see **Default Thread Age Cut Off:** where you can make what changes you want. I do not know if there is a forum-wide maximum age for posts to be kept in archives but that is where to start.
