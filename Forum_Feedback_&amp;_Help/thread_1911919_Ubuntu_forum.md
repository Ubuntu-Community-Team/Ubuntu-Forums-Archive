### Post by anewguy on 2012-01-19
Your post in question:

- you need to provide the detail for us to try helping.  Just saying the directory isn't created isn't enough.  You're using a command line tool - copy and paste the command and it's outcome as a reply so people can actually see what you are talking about.  I would imagine that at a minimum you need to use sudo - but I suspect you'll need more than that.

- you need to also be aware that this isn't the path that your average everyday users uses to add a user, so it's going to take time to get an answer.  Waiting 24 hours, even a LOT more, is not unusual in these cases.

- while waiting for an answer, try using google, yahoo, or the search engine of your choice to find an answer.  You can always check launchpad to see if anyone has reported a similar problem.  These are the same tools and paths that many of us use trying to answer people's problems.

So:

- don't bump for 24 hours
- do post all the detail of the problem - including screen prints, etc., to help us see your problem
- be patient.  Everyone's problems are handled as best they can be at the time.  45 views is not unusual, nor is waiting over a day or more for a response.

These are just the nature of the beast.  Please provide the information requested and perhaps someone may be able to help you.

### Post by QIII on 2012-01-19
To repeat, we are all volunteers.

None of us, as individuals, knows the answer to every possible question.

Most of us will only answer questions we feel we may be able to help with.  We have to view the question to determine that.  Views mean that someone is interested.  There is no need for someone to say "I looked, but I don't think I can help."

Most of us do not live in your time zone and may be asleep, at work or having dinner and watching a movie with our grandchildren when you post.

I don't mean this to be hostile.  Everyone's problems are very important to them.  We all understand it.  But you have to be rational and understand that if the guy who has the perfect answer lives in Bangalore, you might not get the perfect answer within ten minutes of your post.
