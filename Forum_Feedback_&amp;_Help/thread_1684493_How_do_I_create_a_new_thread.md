Ok, Im not a very good artist to draw things apart from making screenshots.
Go to the Ubuntu Forums icon on the top left, click it, it will open now all the subcategories the forum contains (Beginners, Hardware etc...). Click on one of them, e.g Beginners Talk and then you will see some threads. On the top left of the threads is over the big red title 'Threads in Forum: Absolute Beginner Talk' an area 'New Thread', click on this and you will be able to open a new thread.
