When I get some rare free time, I like to surf the forums looking for unsolved posts to help out with. I find that very often, someone else has already put up as good (or better) answer than I had. ### Post by papibe on 2013-03-01
Hi reedk.

Go to "Advance Search". Then scroll down to the field " Find Threads with:" set it to "At Most 0".

If you are doing this frequently, after a few days you may want to also restrict your results to the last day by setting the filed " Find Threads with:" to "Yesterday and Newer".

If you are particularly knowledgeable on one subject, you may also add a keyword on the "Keyword" field. For instance, nfs, samba, raid, etc.

I hope this helps. Let us know how it goes.
Regards.
