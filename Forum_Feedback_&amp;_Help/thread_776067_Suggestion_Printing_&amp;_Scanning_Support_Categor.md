### Post by j2fraser on 2008-04-30
Yes, they certainly are hardware! My feeling is, though, that printing and scanning support issues get a little bit lost in there. "Hardware & Laptops" is a BIG topic area for support (e.g. different laptop configurations, webcams, modems, mice, keyboards, etc.), and it would seem to me to be easier to have one place for users to put support requests for printers, which is a big support area in and of itself (e.g. CUPS, drivers, etc.). Also, printing is a fundamental function of any personal computer and, therefore, an inherently important OS function. Wading through the postings in "Hardware & Laptops" for support on printing issues is very time consuming and even advanced search can be of limited utility (e.g. I searched the site for "HP" this morning and, mysteriously, got 0 results).

I also think scanning goes hand in hand (and sometimes, in the same device) with printing, in logical terms. Fax maybe?... mmmm, not so sure about that.
