Try clearing the cache or even only reloading the page.

The cause of the problem is that some sites which offer images for viewing analyze the request your browser sends. If the site then decides that you link to the image from within another page, it will refuse to yield the image. The purpose is, of course, not to have anyone have your content when you don't have his traffic. Otherwise it would be too easy to bypass any adverts. It would also be too easy to claim the images linked to as your own.

Other sites substitute the picture in that situation for another one which you might not like. I had this happen to me just the other day.

EDIT: I see that you have just found out.
