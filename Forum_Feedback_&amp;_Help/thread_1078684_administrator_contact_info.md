### Post by tmetro on 2009-02-24
> **mips said:**
> You have to be signed else the "contact us" link is not vissible.

There seems to be some confusion over how to accomplish this among you regulars. Consider what it would be like for someone new to the forums, and either not registered, or having login difficulty, and thus not able to use features that require being signed in.

Pointing to a list of admins isn't ideal. I saw the "Forum Council" list and it raises two questions: 1. Are these people there to make high-level decisions about the forums, or do they deal with the day-to-day administration; and 2. Who do you pick from the list to contact? Any name at random?

Whatever mechanism is chosen, I'd recommend:
[LIST]
[*]Making a link to the contact info readily visible on the forum help pages. Perhaps on every page as part of the footer.
[*]Linking to the contact info from the appropriate places in the FAQ.
[*]Making it obvious who or how to make contact, such as a form or single email address.
[*]Making sure that there is at least one mechansim that works for users that aren't logged in.
[/LIST]

The contact info could be a page or FAQ entry that describes more than one means of contact, such as listing (and linking to) this forum as the preferred mechanism for users who can login.

 -Tom
