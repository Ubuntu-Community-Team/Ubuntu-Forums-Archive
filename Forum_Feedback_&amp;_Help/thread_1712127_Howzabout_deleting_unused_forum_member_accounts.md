### Post by t0p on 2011-03-22
Recently there were problems overstretched servers or something like that.  And that wasn't the first time.  So how can we unstretch the servers a little?

Someone in the Cafe just suggested a "fix" that might help a little: delete unused member accounts.  I'll bet there are lots of accounts where the user has only logged in to activate his account, then is never heard from again.  I know I'm guilty of this on other sites; and I doubt I'm very unusual in this respect.

I don't know how helpful a little action like this would be.  But it's got to help a little bit, huh?

Anyone who agrees, plz add a "me too" post to this thread.  And if you can think of other solutions/helpful actions, plz add them too.

Cheers.

**EDIT:** Forgot to mention: deleting unused accounts may help a tiny little bit in ascertaining how many Ubuntu users are out there.  I know there are Ubuntu users who don't use the Forums.  But it'd help an incy-wincy little bit, surely?
