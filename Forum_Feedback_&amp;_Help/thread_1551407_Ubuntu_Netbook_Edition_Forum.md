### Post by cariboo on 2010-08-13
The UNE is based on the gnome desktop, as a matter of fact if you check the session menu on the log in screen, Gnome Desktop is one of the choices. There are no specific UNE solutions for the underlying OS, the only thing that is really different is the Desktop Envirinment.

The Ubuntu Moblin Remix sub-forum was created by request for a large Customer the sells Ubuntu preloaded on their netbooks.

We have been trying to cut down the number of sub-forums due to problems with the database, so quite a bit of thought went into the process of creating yet another sub-forum. I would suggest that the chances of another sub-forum being created for the UNE are pretty close to slim to none.
