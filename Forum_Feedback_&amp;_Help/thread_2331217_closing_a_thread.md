### Post by bapoumba on 2016-07-19
For info, you can mark a thread Solved from the Thread Tools Menu. Older threads are auto-closed after a year of inactivity, so they may pop up in searches but are locked. So may be not the best of solutions, but at least one that works for us :)

### Post by oldos2er on 2016-07-20
Closing a thread and marking a thread solved are two different things for two different purposes. As bapoumba said we have a script that auto-closes threads that have had no activity for a year, whether or not they are marked solved. Staff may also close a thread if they deem it necessary.

Someone who starts a thread may mark it Solved if they feel their question has been answered to their satisfaction. The main reason we encourage people to do this is to assist others who might be having the same problem/question. Marking a thread Solved does not prevent further discussion in a thread.
