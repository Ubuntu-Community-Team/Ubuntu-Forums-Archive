### Post by QIII on 2016-11-21
Hello!

This has been discussed a number of times and we have always decided against it.  It is up to the original  poster to determine if his/her issue has been solved, not the Staff or other users.

The staff do often ask OPs to mark their threads as solved, but some folks, having gotten their answer, simply leave without regard for the community.
