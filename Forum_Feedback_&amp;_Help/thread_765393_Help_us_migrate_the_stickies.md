### Post by matthew on 2008-04-24
This is a call for volunteers.

All of our old sticky threads are filled with useful information that we want to have readily available and easily visible. However, they are still in their original locations in the subforums that are now [in the Archives]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=325").

We have a team of staff working on updating and posting new stickies in the proper, new locations. However, we all have stuff going on in real life, and there are a lot of stickies.

If anyone wants to help, please find an old sticky in one of those archive areas, make a similar post in the proper new forum section, and then use the "report" button to let the staff know (quote me if you think it will help) and we will try to get it stuck quickly.

I think if we get 3 or 4 people working on this (or more) we can get this done in a few hours or less. Interested? Just jump on in and do it! I'll go let all the staff know what is going on.

If you update a thread, when you report the post, include a link to the original post in the message body as well as the words "sticky update ready" in the report title. 

Thanks!!!
