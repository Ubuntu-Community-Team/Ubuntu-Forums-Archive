### Post by jpeddicord on 2010-02-11
General tip: If you sign in, don't use your back button to return to a previous page that you weren't signed in on, otherwise you will likely be required to sign in again. Just open a new tab and go back to the pages you want to use. This applies to a lot of sites on the web.
