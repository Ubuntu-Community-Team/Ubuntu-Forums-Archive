### Post by QIII on 2016-04-02
If, in a thread that *you started*, *your question* is answered, then you may close it.

If it were me, I might make a link to the bug report, but leave it open.  People looking for a solution look for the *Solved* tag -- and it would be a bit of a pain to find only a bug rather than a solution.
