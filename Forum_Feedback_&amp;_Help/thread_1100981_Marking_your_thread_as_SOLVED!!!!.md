### Post by drs305 on 2009-03-19
Normally it is done via the *Thread Tools* link to the top right of the original post. This feature has been temporarily disabled while the ubuntu folks work on the server.

You can edit the title to include the word "Solved" if desired by hitting "Edit" and then hitting the "Edit" button a second time after the original post opens for editing. 
**ADDED:** The problem is that I don't think the new title is displayed until after you open the post for reading.

You can also open the original post for editing and add "SOLVED" at the beginning of the thread text. It won't show in the title, but if someone hovers the mouse over the thread title they will see the first line of the post and perhaps note that it is solved.

Neither of these are a good replacement for the SOLVED option we had previously but they are better than nothing.
