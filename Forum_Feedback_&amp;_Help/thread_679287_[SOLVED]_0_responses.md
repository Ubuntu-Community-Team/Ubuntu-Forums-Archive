### Post by popch on 2008-01-27
The standard ordering of the threads in a subforum is by last post. You can reverse the order by klicking on the down pointing triangle thing to the right of the column header.

You can also order the threads by the number of replies (and presumably by the number of times it has been viewed). To do that, you just klick on the appropriate column heading, in your case on the word **Replies**.

I don't know if the number of replies is a useful metric for ordering threads. Imagine that the thread with two replies and 1339 views was something which was of interest to the viewers and that those viewers had sufficient discipline to avoid posting that they would like to know a clue as well. The third reply could contain the crucial clue which was of immediate use to all 1339 viewers. Provided they managed to look into the forum within 40 minutes if they wanted to find it on the first page.
