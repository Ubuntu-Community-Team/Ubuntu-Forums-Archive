### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2009-07-18
Moved to FF&H.

There is no defined path to become an admin on the forums, and indeed I wouldn't spend time worry about how to become one.  When new staff (moderators) are needed, they are chosen by the existing staff - there is no application process.  When new admins are needed, they are chosen by the [uwiki]ForumCouncil[/uwiki] (the current forum admins) and approved by the [uwiki]CommunityCouncil[/uwiki].
