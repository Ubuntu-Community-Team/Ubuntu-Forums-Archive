### Post by jenkinbr on 2008-12-16
About logging in under a different alias: I have had absolutely no problems logging in to ubuntuforums.org using a wide variety of subdomain names. Just to test it, I logged out and logged back in at the sites:
[http://eat.fruit.loops.ubuntuforums.org/](http://eat.fruit.loops.ubuntuforums.org/)
[http://this.forum.rocks.ubuntuforums.org/](http://this.forum.rocks.ubuntuforums.org/) and yes, as I have posted elsewere,
[http://bump.the.bump.thread.ubuntuforums.org/](http://bump.the.bump.thread.ubuntuforums.org/)

I have also tried browsing to the LoCo forums using these (and other) subdomains, and have had no problems accessing them or other forums on this site, and following the links above transfers me seemlessly between them.  This is likely due to the fact that the domain value in the ubuntuforums.org cookie is set to ".ubuntuforums.org", which allows just about anything to preceed the 'ubuntuforums.org' part of the address.
