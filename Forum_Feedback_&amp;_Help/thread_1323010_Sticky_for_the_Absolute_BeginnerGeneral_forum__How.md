### Post by The Funkbomb on 2009-11-11
I've noticed something lately, quite a few people don't know how to ask a question.

The first thing I've noticed is a lot of people don't know how to write a title.  Titles like, "I'm a noob" and "I need some help!" aren't really descriptive enough.  Titles in all caps or with elongated letters are just annoying.

That's just me nitpicking I guess.  The next suggestion is more important.

People aren't nearly descriptive enough.  Someone makes a post like, "I can't get my video card to work."  Narrows it down a bit but what video card is it?  What exactly isn't working correctly?  What have you tried already?

I just think the forums would be more efficient if people made thought out questions in the first place instead of trying to pull information.

Maybe as part of this suggested sticky, we could list common terminal commands that will display pertinent information.
