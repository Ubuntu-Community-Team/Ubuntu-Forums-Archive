### Post by QIII on 2022-09-28
Unfortunately, there is nothing we can do about it.  You are not logging in to the Ubuntu Forums, per se, so much as going through Canonical's Ubuntu One SSO process that is required to get to their resources -- like the Ubuntu Forums -- that are hosted on their servers.  You go through that and are directed to the Forums.

This is an issue you would have to take up with Canonical directly.  I am pretty sure that you would get nowhere with them.  They don't pay much attention to us, either.
