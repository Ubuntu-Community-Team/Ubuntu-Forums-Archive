### Post by apochry on 2011-03-26
I supose you are using Gnome GIU.
If so click the **speaker icon in the Gnome panel** and then **Sound Preferences**...
Go to the **Input** tab and **uncheck** the **Mute** check-box.

[IMG]http://img508.imageshack.us/img508/2718/soundpref.png[/IMG]

Alternatively: **System** --> **Preferences** --> **Sound** --> **Input** tab
