### Post by snowpine on 2011-08-31
Sorry I didn't mean to stress you out, it is no big deal. :)

Ubuntu is not allowed to support the Broadcom card out-of-the-box so you need to enable the firmware yourself. 

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/bcm43xx#STA_-_Internet_access](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/bcm43xx#STA_-_Internet_access)

But actually it is even easier than that, because Ubuntu has an application called Hardware Drivers that will do it for you. Just point-and-click. :)
