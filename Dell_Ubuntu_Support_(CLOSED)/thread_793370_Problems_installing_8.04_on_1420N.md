### Post by shae on 2008-05-13
I have a 1420N and was trying to install Ubuntu 8.04LTS, but I could not get the kernel image in the cd to work.  I checked the md5sum of the iso, burned at x4, and tried two different disks burn through that method.  

The Symptoms:
The Live CD goes to the menu, but the only entry that works is "Boot from first hard disk"

I tried using the advanced boot prompt (by pressing escape) and any options yielded a cannot decompress the kernel image error.

Note: I used the ussg.iu.edu mirror.  I am downloading an iso from kernel.org as we speak.

---

### Post by shae on 2008-05-14
The problem was the install media.  I ended up downloading the DVD version and burnt it to higher quality writable media.  8.04LTS works great on my 1420N!
