### Post by ugm6hr on 2009-03-22
[gnomad2]("apt:gnomad2") is supposed to allow Zen transfers according to [http://www.opensourcesociety.org/2008/10/19/music-to-go-the-creative-zen-mp3-player-on-linux/](http://www.opensourcesociety.org/2008/10/19/music-to-go-the-creative-zen-mp3-player-on-linux/)

Sharing of copyrighted music is obviously not sanctioned here, but [http://creativecommons.org/](http://creativecommons.org/) and [http://www.jamendo.com/en/](http://www.jamendo.com/en/) have some free (as in beer) music that can be shared via any torrent application.
