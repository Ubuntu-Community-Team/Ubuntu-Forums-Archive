### Post by Bartender on 2009-03-16
For most of us Linux newbs, you're better off to spend some money on an external hardware modem than trying to get those infernal winmodems to work.

Buy a US Robotics 5637 USB modem.  It'll be detected at /dev/tty/ACM0.  That's a zero at the end.

Or scrounge up an old USR serial external.  You may know someone who has one of these in their closet.

Or find one of those Actiontec serial/USB modems that AOL was selling/giving away by the thousands.
