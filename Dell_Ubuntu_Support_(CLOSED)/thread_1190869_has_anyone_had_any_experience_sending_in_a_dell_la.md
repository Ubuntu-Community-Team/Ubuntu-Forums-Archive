### Post by mtbsoft on 2009-06-22
I bought mine from Dell Outlet in Oz, didn't buy (specifically) any ext. warranty;  just the usual 12 months statutory.  Had a problem with the motherboard fairly soon after - the Dell ID in the bios was incorrect against the sticker - plus the SD slot was too tight.

I simply reloaded XP so they could run their diagnostics and they sent an engineer out to correct the problem.  You may find you have a warranty that entitles you to have on-site.

************

NOTE - make sure to remove your SD card before they get it!  The engineer took mine by accident and it took them FOUR weeks to track it down and return it!
