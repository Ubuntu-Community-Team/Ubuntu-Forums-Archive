### Post by ridgeland on 2009-01-05
I found that Google Earth did not work right for me.  My fix was to turn off Compiz.  From the main menu System -> Preference -> Appearance --- Then visual effects = None.  Then Google Earth works.
Turning off Compiz also solved SuperTux not working.
