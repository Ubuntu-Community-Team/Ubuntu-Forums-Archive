### Post by ugm6hr on 2009-06-18
Simple answers:

Ubuntu will probably work, but we need more detail about your computer.  Minimum requirements for Ubuntu are on the ubuntu.com website.

If this is your computer: [http://hjelp.ovgs.no/barbar/nedlasting/Latit_110l_uk.pdf]("http://hjelp.ovgs.no/barbar/nedlasting/Latit_110l_uk.pdf")
I would not expect major problems.  The latest Ubuntu (9.04) does have issues with Intel graphics cards, but the can generally be managed (Ref: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/359698](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/359698) )

Office 2003 can be made to work with CrossOver / Wine, but I would not rely on it; Word and Excel generally behave themselves, but PowerPoint and Access less so.  If you are intending to move to Ubuntu, I would first migrate to OpenOffice on Windows, and then Windows to Ubuntu.

Essentially: consider why you are moving to Ubuntu, and whether the investment in migration is worth it in the long term (i.e. no future payment for upgrades, open source, currently virus free etc).
