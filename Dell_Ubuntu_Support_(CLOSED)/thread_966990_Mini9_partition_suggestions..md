### Post by emshains on 2008-11-01
I think you should put the /home directory on the internal drive, just because its more stable and was made to work like an hdd. Although, if you are planning on transferring a lot of media from one place to another, I'd suggest to keep it on the SD, as long as you make weekly backups. 

The system stuff should be on the comp, the swap has no place but the SD, because, correct me if I'm wrong, you are planning to change the SD cards as they brake down, and changing an SD card is easier and cheaper than changing the "hdd" on that thing.
