### Post by mikeh on 2009-04-27
A Core2 can fit the same socket, but I don't believe that the chipset in the gx520/620 will support them.
The best you can do is a pentium-D dual core, which is not worth the trouble.
Normally I'd suggest a new motherboard, but the gx520 is in the BTX form-factor, which is good, but never took off.
