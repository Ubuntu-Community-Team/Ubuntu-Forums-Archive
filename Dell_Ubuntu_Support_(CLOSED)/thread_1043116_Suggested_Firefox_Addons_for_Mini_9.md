### Post by sirebral on 2009-01-18
These are some suggested addons for Mini 9 users.

Download Status Bar:  This is a must have for all users I think.  It places the downloads into the status bar.  No annoying pop up.
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/26](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/26)

Hide Menu Bar:  I just found this one and it is an instant Favorite.  This addon hides the menu bar.  If you need to find it just press Alt.  This is a great way to get a few pixels back!!
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4762](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4762)

New Tab Button:  This add on gives you a little button that you can press to open a new tab.  It lets you choose if you want it to the right of the furthest tab, the the left of the tab bar or to the right of the tab bar.  Less right click > new tab for me!!
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5338](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5338)

Suggested Setting:  Before you hide the menu bar you should customize your tool bars and click the Use Small Icons check.  This suggestion really cleans up the browser space for easy viewing.

I hope you enjoy saving all of that space for viewing with such a small screen!
