### Post by Janeleaper on 2008-07-17
Hi

I'm a newbie to Linux too and got my Dell up and running yesterday.  I'm surprised that you haven't had a reply to this.  I can't give you a technical answer, but I can give you some bad news.  Probably your printer is not supported by Linux/Ubuntu.

I actually bought a new printer from Dell thinking that they would only sell me one that works with Linux, but I was wrong.  I've arranged to return the printer and get my money back.  

Basically the cheaper printers don't work with Linux.  If your printer can work with Linux, there will be a driver for it somewhere.  Go to the printer manufacturer's site and look for it.
