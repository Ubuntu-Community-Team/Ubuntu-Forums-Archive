### Post by starcannon on 2009-01-24
A work around I use is to stop the boot process at the grub screen, then using the FN key combo for your laptop choose the screen or screens you wish to view, then allow it to boot as normal. This has allowed me to use projectors, external monitors, plasma screens, etc... etc.. without having to tweak my xorg.conf.
