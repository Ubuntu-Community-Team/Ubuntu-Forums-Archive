### Post by snowpine on 2009-07-18
Dell Mini 9 only has 1024x600 resolution, so some windows get cut off. The solution is to hold down the Alt key while you click anywhere on the window and drag it. This will allow you to move the window so you can see the parts that are cut off at the bottom. Hope that helps!
