### Post by groeswenphil on 2009-04-06
Hi group,

For weeks I've been lusting after a new Dell Netbook. With my birthday just around the corner I decided to place my order with Dell last Friday evening.

If you find yourself in the same position as myself, you might like to consider the following.
The delivery date was only given after I'd finally pressed the "Place Order" button. It was only after I'd received the confirmation e mail that I realised that I'd have to wait over three weeks for delivery.
As soon as I learned this, I tried to cancel. Dell don't man their phone lines over the weekend. I phoned first thing Monday morning but was told that I couldn't cancel my order but that I could send it back and get a refund as soon as it was delivered.

Despite pleading and two telephone calls, they were not prepared to accept my cancellation.

I cannot see how anybody benefits from this decision.
They are apparently building a netbook to my specifications....one that might not appeal to anybody else.
They are then transporting it across country for no point as I have already told them that it will be sent right back and a refund requested. 

In the meantime I have to wonder how to go about financing a birthday purchase for the correct date.

In short, if you buy from Dell, you cannot cancel after placing the order, but you can return the item after delivery and get a full refund.

Whilst on the telephone I found it really hard not to use words like illogical, stupidity and waste of  time.

Anybody else had a similar experience?

Phil
