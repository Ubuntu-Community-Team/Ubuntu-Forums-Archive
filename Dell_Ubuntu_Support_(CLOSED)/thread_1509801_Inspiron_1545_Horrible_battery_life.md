### Post by jarviser on 2010-06-15
Screen and Wifi card are biggest wasters of battery.

For brightness where hardware buttions are not working, try click on battery icon then Preferences. There's a slider on "On Mains Power" and also option to dim even further on "On Battery Power"

If you are not using Wireless, and if you have a hardware switch, turn it off.  I think on the 1545 it's F2 for off and F2 long hold for on. Google it if not.
