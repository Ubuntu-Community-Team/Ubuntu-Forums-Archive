### Post by armandh on 2009-02-24
if the problem persists across several OSes
it is likely hardware

possible power supply issues?
bad bios battery?

bad CD drive? causing bad load
re load with known good CD & drive!

pull out or disable in bios everything but the video/kb/mouse
still have the problem? change....video card kb & mouse
change memory  
change power supply
change processor

still have the problem? change mobo.

the above assumes a large junk box of known good working parts, 
testing old obsolete boxes with purchased parts is too expensive.
