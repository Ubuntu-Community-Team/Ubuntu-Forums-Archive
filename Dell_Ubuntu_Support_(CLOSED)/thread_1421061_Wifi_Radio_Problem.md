  Ubuntu comes with a link to the drivers for most computer hardware.  Under Menu/Administration find Hardware Drivers and open it.  This should give you one or two choices.  Then you need a wired connection to the Internet to download the driver.  Next click on the two monitor icon in the task bar, open Edit Connections and in Wireless "Add"  your Internet Provider information.  Hope this helps.
