### Post by jaqrah on 2009-03-20
Hey all Mini 9 users,

If you are familiar with the following site: **Ubuntu on the Dell Mini 9 blog**, you might know about a potentially interesting app - Desktop Switcher.

[http://www.ubuntumini.com/2009/02/desktop-switcher-released-for-intrepid.html](http://www.ubuntumini.com/2009/02/desktop-switcher-released-for-intrepid.html)

I am one of many people who have had their desktops crash because of this app.  It was initially suggested this might be a Xorg issue...but I am not so sure.  I definitely could not get the following fix to work for me.

[http://www.ubuntumini.com/2009/02/repair-broken-xorg-file.html](http://www.ubuntumini.com/2009/02/repair-broken-xorg-file.html)

Whatever it really is, the app does not play nice with the Netbook Remix.  I tried using the app to switch from a compiz enabled theme running on highest setting to a Netbook Remix theme.  Epic Failure ensued!

Just to keep new Mini 9 users from having to re-install...think twice before using the Desktop Switcher app.


Jack
