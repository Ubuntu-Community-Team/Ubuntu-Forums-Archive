Hi there-

All these troubles can be basically fixed.  Here's a couple places to start:

Read and follow the instructions *carefully* and the wireless will work.  Read everything first before you go poking around.  Really.  I mean it.  I'm guessing you have a Broadcom wireless chip like many of us.  The Intel ones work out of the box apparently.

The touchpad is a little wonky, but it can be made to behave.  Until you get things ironed out, I recommend using any old USB mouse.  You will need a working Internet connection to get both to work, so hopefully you can hook up by means of a wire.

You may need to look around in the Archives section for Dell support for the touchpad-- I was in there this morning myself.  That's where I found most of the information.
