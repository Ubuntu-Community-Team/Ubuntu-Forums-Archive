### Post by hawthornso23 on 2010-11-29
The camera on my E6510 seems OK. But I haven't tried it with those applications. A useful way to test your camera is via VLC media player. Open it and click on 'media'  >  'open capture device' and then 'play'. If your camera is working you should be able to see an image on the screen at this point.
