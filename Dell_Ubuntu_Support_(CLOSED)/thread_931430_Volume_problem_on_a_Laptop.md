### Post by fallenstar on 2008-10-05
I've also been having fun with sound on my Dell Inspiron 1525, I have volume control above my keyboard on a panel, do you have anything like that?

When you go into preferences/sound, on each of the options (ie front, pcm etc), hit the volume up key on the keyboard, or the volume up key on your taskbar (rightclick on taskbar/add to panel if you don't have one), and increase the volume for all that way. 

Yours might be slightly different as I'm running Ubuntu Studio, but the principles are the same :KS
