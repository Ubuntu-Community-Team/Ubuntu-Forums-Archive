### Post by Janeleaper on 2009-12-21
I've updated to 9.10 and I do have some problems.  I can't get the BBC iplayer to work properly.  If I let the pc go into sleep mode it freezes and won't recover from sleep mode, and then there are problems in rebooting.

On the other hand, I found it easier to install a webcam and get Skype up and running on 9.10.

However, there do seem to be a few bugs in 9.10, so I'd consider carefully whether you need to upgrade.  Unless there is something that 9.04 won't do for you that you know 9.10 will do, then I'd put off upgrading until the bugs have been sorted out.
