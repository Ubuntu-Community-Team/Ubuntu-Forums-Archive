### Post by veritas366 on 2008-07-04
One of the big issues has been batteries.  An accident at a manufacturing plant has made obtaining batteries difficult.  In some systems it's the small battery that's the issue and in some it's the big one.  

Processors have had a bit of a big lead time as well.  You should be able to get some indication of the lead time when building the system...and if you are confused...save it all in your cart under your email name and call into Dell.  They can pull in your exact config without much effort and then can tell you where the lag is. 

Not ideal but I can guarantee that there are some people who make a lot more money than I do scrambling to get this fixed before the "back to school" shopping season really kicks off.
