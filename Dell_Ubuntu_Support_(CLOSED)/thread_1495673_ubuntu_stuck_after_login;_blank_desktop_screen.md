### Post by jarviser on 2010-05-28
I expect there's a fancy way to fix it, but as you have only just installed it, personally I would do another clean install with a "format" option on the ext4 "/" partition. Should only take 20 minutes. Take care of any /home data in the usual way. 
My 1520 is very well behaved, but I steer clear of hibernate or sleep, as with the latest Ubuntu it's as quick to shut down and boot and much cleaner.

As you have guessed this is a non-tech response!
