### Post by sidzen on 2010-07-13
You have basically the same system I do, just a different name (much like lubuntu and peppermint) -- Pentium P4 running at 2.8GHz, 512MB PC2700 SDRAM, etc. -- this is why I mentioned the specs in my first reply.

I would not bog the E310 down with KDE or Gnome but, rather, use an LXDE distro.  And if you want to go ubuntu-based, then peppermint or mint-9-lxde are two out of three for desktop;  element 1.3.0 for HTPC will likely slow the system down a bit, but not as much as the full-blown ubuntu or kubuntu.
