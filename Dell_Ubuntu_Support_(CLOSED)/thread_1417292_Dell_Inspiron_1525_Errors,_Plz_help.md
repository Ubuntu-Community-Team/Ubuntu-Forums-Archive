### Post by coffeeaddict22 on 2010-02-27
Hi,
and welcome to Ubuntu!  

There's a comprehensive sound troubleshooter [here in the forums.]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=205449")  In particular, suggest you have a look in alsamixer first; open a terminal( Applications/ Accessories/ Terminal) and type in ```
alsamixer
```There's a series of mixers in there which you can manipulate with the arrow keys; often there's a couple at 0 which once turned on solve your problem.

Post back if you need more help.
