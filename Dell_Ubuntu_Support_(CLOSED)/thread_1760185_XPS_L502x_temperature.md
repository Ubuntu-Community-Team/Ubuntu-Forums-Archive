### Post by amemes on 2011-05-18
I don't notice a difference between the two. In both ubuntu and windows7 the fans seem to kick in quite fast when I do some intensive tasks.
The fans are controlled by the bios/hardware and so should behave exactly the same way unless you installed some fan control software.
You can switch off the nvidia card to reduce heat in linux, maybe that's the difference you are experiencing.
