### Post by shezif on 2010-08-04
When I decided to became a UNR user I've felt that my Dell 1012's had a less buttery life.
In the Windows XP I've got something like eight and half hours to work without charging
while in the UNR i've got something like five hours only.
I'd tried  to find a solution how to save my buttery life and I fund a solution.

Probably as you know the Aircraft-Manager (that make it able to turn WIFI/BT off) doesn't work by the box in 10.04 (Unfriendly installation).
So. I'm turn BT and Wifi off from the Bios!
I didn't knew that it can be able to turn this off from the BIOS, and I decided to share this with you.

So I hope you will be get some help with this issue now.
Have a good day :)
