### Post by gbrainin on 2011-02-08
Well, it depends on what you mean by "clone."  One of my favorite tools for this sort of thing is a utility called partimage, which can make a compressed file image of an entire partition, then restore the partition (to the original or another place).  There are other ways to accomplish the same sort of thing.

Copying to a larger partition is trivial.  To a smaller one might not work.

There are many other tools available for this sort of thing.
