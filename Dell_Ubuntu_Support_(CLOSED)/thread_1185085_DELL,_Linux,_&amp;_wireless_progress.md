### Post by anjilslaire on 2009-06-12
Everyone needs to remember that yes, your internet speed is obviously limited by whatever service you have, ie. 3mb DSL, or 8mb cable, etc, and that standard G speeds will far outrun your outside connection.

But, for transferring your data on your internal LAN, going N can have a lot of advantages. I you have a standard 100mbit wireed network from your router, desktops, etc, you G-enabled wireless devices will be running about 1/2 speed compared to your wired  machines. Moving to a wireless-N solution will allow them to be quicker on the internal LAN.

Just a thought.
