### Post by niteshifter on 2009-10-25
Hi,

No, that 39MB is not a OS recovery partition, it's  Dell Diagnostics software. AFIK Dell doesn't do Windows OS recovery partitions, they give CD's for that. Dell does provide a recovery parition on machines they pre-install Ubuntu on.
