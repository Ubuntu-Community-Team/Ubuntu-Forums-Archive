### Post by Preserved Killick on 2010-01-29
I have read that the microphone will not work on a Dell Mini 10v if one moves from 8.04 to UNR (Ubuntu Netbook Remix).  It's true, it doesn't work **until you uncheck Mute** under sound preferences.

I booted UNR from a thumb drive on my daughter's Mini 10v, installed Skype, unchecked Mute in sound preferences, and it seems to work just fine.

My daughter wants Skype on her Mini and I can't get it working under Ubuntu 8.04, so after she backs up some files she's accumulated since Christmas, I'll be installing UNR on her Mini.

I hope other people find this as easy as I did.

G'day!
