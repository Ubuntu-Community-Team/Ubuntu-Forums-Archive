### Post by Talon2 on 2009-10-23
I've ordered several Ubuntu pre-loaded systems over the last 2 years, not all for myself.  Here is my experience:

- If you want shipping within a week go with System 76 or Zareason.

- If you don't mind 2 - 4 weeks shipping then Dell is an option.

Other comments:  Dell does not do a good job on most of their notebooks of using components that are compatible in the long term.  They seem to like to use Broadcom wifi which is a bad idea.

If I needed to buy a Mini right now I would go for the [Latitude 2100]("http://configure.us.dell.com/dellstore/config.aspx?oc=blcwwfp&c=us&l=en&s=bsd&cs=04&kc=laptop-latitude-2100") with the Intel wifi option.
