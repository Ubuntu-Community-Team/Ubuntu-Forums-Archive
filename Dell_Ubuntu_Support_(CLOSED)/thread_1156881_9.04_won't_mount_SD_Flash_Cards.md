### Post by bswilson on 2009-05-12
OK, I found the hotfix for mounting SD cards, etc.:

```
sudo killall gvfs-gphoto2-volume-monitor 
sudo chmod -x /usr/lib/gvfs/gvfs-gphoto2-volume-monitor
```
