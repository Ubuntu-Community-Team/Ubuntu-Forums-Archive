### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-06-18
First, check the temperatures to make sure the computer doesn't overheat. Here are some simple steps:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1784521](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1784521)
