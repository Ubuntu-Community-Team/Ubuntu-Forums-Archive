### Post by evanrmurphy on 2009-05-05
In addition to the one Dr.Vista mentioned, [IE View]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/35") and [IE Tab]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1419") are two other Firefox add-ons that might do the trick for you.

If none of these options work, then you might try IEs4Linux. Things get more complicated with that however, since IEs4Linux isn't native to the architecture, but rather runs through [Wine]("http://www.winehq.org/"). There's a [whole subcategory of the forums dedicated to Wine]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=313"), by the way, if you decide to go down that path.

Give us an update and we'll try to help you get to the next step.
