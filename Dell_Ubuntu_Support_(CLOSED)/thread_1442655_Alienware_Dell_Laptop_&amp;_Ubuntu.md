### Post by FFred on 2010-04-17
> **MorleyPotter said:**
> Has anyone set up a dual boot Win 7/Ubuntu Karmic on an Alienware Dell Laptop running the i7 Intel processor?

Your poll just says "Dell" and I don't have an i7 Dell anyway. So I didn't answer it.

I have a M11x Alienware laptop which requires some work to get Linux working. Probably not the best choice unless you want/need a small machine and are comfortable with the system. 

Regarding the i7, that's what my desktop runs, and it works fine. It's seen as 8 discrete cores by the system but it has no impact on anything. 

All in all Alienware laptops are more likely to use weird peripherals so they may not be the easiest to set up I think (although it will certainly be possible for most models).
