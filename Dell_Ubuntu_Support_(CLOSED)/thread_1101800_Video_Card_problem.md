### Post by ridgeland on 2009-04-01
I bought a Dell E520N with Ubuntu Linux May 2007.  I got it with an nVidia geforce 7300LE 256MB.  I noticed that one game (Einstein) caused the screen to go black for a few seconds then come back.  Later I had more troubles.  Then even the boot BIOS screen had vertical lines.  It was 11 months old so Dell replaced the card.  The new card was fine for six months then I noticed some times it would flash to black for a couple of seconds.  My solution was to remove the nVidia card while it was still working and run with just the on-board Intel graphics.  No problems since.  I bought nVidia because my 2002 Gateway has nVidia and still works great.  SuperTux and kbounce are about as demanding as I get for graphics though.
