### Post by gbrainin on 2008-11-17
I can't answer most of your questions, since I have not used my 530 with either a modem or wireless.  However:

When I bought mine, I believe there was still an option to purchase with an internal modem.  This may have changed; I bought mine a long time ago.

Yes, there are only USB ports (and an optional firewire) for external peripherals.  However, the USB adapters in the world seem to work pretty well.  We've got an old parallel printer which works perfectly on a USB to Parallel adapter.  Note that we did have trouble with the first one we bought, but the second one (different brand) worked transparently, without any configuration necessary.

I haven't exactly beat up on my 530, but it's worked without a hitch since I bought it in mid-2007.  (It did come with a bum floppy drive, which Dell exchanged by overnight mail without problem, once I discovered the LINUX phone support line.)
