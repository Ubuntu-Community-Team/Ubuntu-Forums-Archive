### Post by daehenoc on 2009-05-04
Hi all,

I just installed Ubuntu 9.04 on a Dell Optiplex 755.  There was no sound and after searching for a bit and fiddling around, I remembered that to enable the audio socket on the ***rear*** of the computer, you have to open your volume control manager, find the 'switches' section and enable the '**Front**' switch.  Obvious, really.

The PCM mixer will be set to zero by default, so bring this volume up, as this controls the audio levels for wav and ogg files etc.

Then you should be good to go with audio :)
