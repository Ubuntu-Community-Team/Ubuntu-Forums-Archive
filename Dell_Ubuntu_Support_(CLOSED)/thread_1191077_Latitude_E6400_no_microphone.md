### Post by kszys on 2009-12-29
I had a similar problem. For some reason Skype managed to set the volume of the mike to 0. It was sufficient to set a reasonable volume level for the mike (I put it on "unamplified") and then disable Skype mixer level adjustments. Since then, the internal mike works fine. External mike is another story...
