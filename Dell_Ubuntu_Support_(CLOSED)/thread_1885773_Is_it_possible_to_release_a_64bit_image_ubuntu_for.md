### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-11-24
The image that you think is for Dell vostro 1450 is, in fact, a regular 32bit image from [http://releases.ubuntu.com/11.10/](http://releases.ubuntu.com/11.10/). It is not a special image for Dell vostro 1450.
As you might imagine, releasing a special image for every Dell, HP, Acer, Asus, Toshiba, Samsung, Sony, etc model is not practical, so, I am afraid, the answer to your question is no.
If you want a [-->64bit image<--]("http://releases.ubuntu.com/11.10/ubuntu-11.10-desktop-amd64.iso"), get it, and tell us if it works.
