### Post by snowpine on 2011-12-26
Welcome to the forums!

I have a Dell Mini 9 (Inspiron 910) and it is a nice little computer. One thing I always do when I buy a new computer is wipe the operating system and reinstall. I just don't like the idea of inheriting someone else's personal data and documents (and possibly malware/viruses) so I like to start fresh. I can tell you for a fact that the operating system is replaceable on the Dell Mini computers.

Installing Windows from scratch on any computer is not necessarily a beginner's task. (That's why most people buy computers with the operating system pre-installed.) Your best bet for information is [http://mydellmini.com/](http://mydellmini.com/) They have some great tutorials and support forums. Of course you will need to verify that your specific Dell Mini (they were manufactured in several models and you don't mention which one you have) meets the hardware requirements for the version of Windows you want to run, purchase a legal retail copy of Windows, find an external USB CD-ROM drive, download and install the necessary drivers from Dell, and seek advice on relevant forums (since we aren't really Windows experts here).

If you decide to stick with Ubuntu we are happy to help! Merry Christmas, and that was a really thoughtful gift to your granddaughter. :)
