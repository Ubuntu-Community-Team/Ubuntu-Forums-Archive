### Post by gbrainin on 2011-02-27
Well, most of us here think it's better than Windows, but that's obviously a matter of opinion.  As for speed, software can't work miracles, but Ubuntu is generally less demanding of your system's hardware than Windows is, leaving more for the programs you're running.

While you're waiting for your CD to arrive, you should read up on switching from Windows to Ubuntu.  There are some things that are different, and that often catch Windows users by surprise--for example, that the system does not come with things like Flash by default, you have to install them manually (and should use the package manager to do so, not downloading it from Adobe's website).

A good resource for this sort of thing is [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index").
