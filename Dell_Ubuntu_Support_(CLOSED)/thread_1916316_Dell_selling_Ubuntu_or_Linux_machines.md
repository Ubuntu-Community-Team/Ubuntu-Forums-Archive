### Post by gbrainin on 2012-02-21
I'm going to disagree slightly with RedRat.  I, too, bought an Inspiron back in the 7.04 days when Dell was first selling Linux preinstalled.  Even then, however, the official support from Dell was hardware-only.  Yes, they had a separate phone number for Linux support (which, I am told, has since been shut down), and yes, sometimes they would go beyond their mandate and get into software issues.
