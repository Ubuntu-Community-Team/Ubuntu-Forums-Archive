### Post by T J Wells on 2011-11-16
Update on Dell 1100:  I found that the Debian installation was locking up, so I reinstalled Linux Mint 11.0.  To get rid of the "ghost screen" I ran Startup-Manager.  I changed the setting on the first screen to 1024 x 768, using 24 bits.  I closed startup-manager and rebooted and the screen now functions properly.
