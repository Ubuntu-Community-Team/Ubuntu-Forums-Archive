init: broadcom-modules main process (4235) terminated with status 1

Your modem and configuration failed to load.

This white screen you talk about does NOT equal a white screen of death.  The BSOD that Win has is usually caused by a driver conflict, but it occurs while the system is running killing every process.

You just need to make sure your stuff loads properly and the white screen goes away.  I've had the problem before.  Mine was resolution based.
