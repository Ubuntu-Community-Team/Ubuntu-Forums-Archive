### Post by tyroeternal on 2009-06-16
Are you talking about two computers using the same keyboard, mouse and monitor?  If that is the case you are looking for a KVM Switch.  This will allow you to connect both desktops to a switch, and that switch to your keyboard, mouse and monitor (or whichever combo suits you).
