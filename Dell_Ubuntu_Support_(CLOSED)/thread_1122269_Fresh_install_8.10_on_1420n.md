### Post by Spooky5 on 2009-04-11
I have a 1525n and just recently did a fresh install using the 8.10 iso from the [Dell linux wiki]("http://linux.dell.com/wiki/index.php/Ubuntu_8.10"), using the directions listed there. Everything works perfectly on my laptop. :) I can't imagine it being much different on the 1420.

Did your laptop not come with an ubuntu cd/dvd? Mine did. Somebody else might be able to tell you how to burn an image of your current configuration but the 7.04 iso is also available on the wiki if you need to download it.

Depending on how big your home folder is I recommend backing up to either a flash drive or an external hard drive. Then copy it over after you've installed the new version.

Good luck!
