### Post by oceanfirehawk on 2008-11-15
Hello all,

I wanted to give my two cents on the Dell XPS M1530 I bought and later returned to dell. This laptop had Ubuntu (Hardy) pre-installed.

Here is why I returned the laptop:

1) Hardware compatibility issues:

Built-in webcam is not stable, touchpad scrolling turns off (maybe after coming back from standby), bluetooth setup causes whole system to freeze (everything works afterward), touchpad is awful in regards easy scrolling, speakers are lame, eject button on top of keyboard doesn't work unless you unmount the CD drive first, and other distros don't seem to work with the laptop.

2) Dell's support:

I called Dell for support. Their answer? They told me it was Ubuntu's software causing the problem. They gave me Canonical's phone number! They must know the compatibility issues are awful if they don't even want to address them.

3) Tired of big corporate:

Dell is simply trying to take a piece of the Linux pie! I'm buying from Zareason or System76 once I get my refund. 


Linux rules! You just gotta get the right hardware...
