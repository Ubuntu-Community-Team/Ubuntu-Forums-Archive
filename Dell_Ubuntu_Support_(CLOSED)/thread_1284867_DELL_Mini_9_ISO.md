### Post by snowpine on 2009-10-07
> **nibbslang said:**
> I have a DELL Mini 9 with a netbook iso which isn't LPIA compatible and therefore it runs slow, what is the best iso for the little DELL?

Also where can I get it and how do I install it as I have no disk drive?

Hi Nibbslang,

You do NOT need to use lpia on the Mini 9; "regular" i386 Ubuntu or Ubuntu Netbook Remix will work just fine.

If you prefer the lpia version for whatever reason, it's available here: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/jaunty/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/jaunty/release/)

You can use an external USB CD-Rom drive, or use Unetbootin to create a bootable thumb drive.

You can also purchase a Dell Mini 9 with a special version of Ubuntu 8.04 lpia that is custom tailored for the Mini; it may be too late for you in this case however. :(

Good luck!
