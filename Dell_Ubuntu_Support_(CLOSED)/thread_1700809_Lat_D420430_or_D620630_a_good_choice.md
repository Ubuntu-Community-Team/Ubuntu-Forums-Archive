### Post by nm_geo on 2011-03-05
I have a Dell D620 and I really like it.  Mine today runs (5) different flavors of Ubuntu let's see Lucid 10.04, Xbuntu 10.04, Maverick 10.10 and (2) Natty 11.04 development versions and I still have XP SP3 for some programs that run in windows only.

Generally my emailing, web-surfing and other internet are done with one of my linux Ubuntu versions.

More info on my D620:

I have the WXGA screen & Intel driver better for graphics. 
I bought mine on Ebay and it had a 320 GB drive (more than needed).
I upgraded the memory to 4.0 GB (more than needed also) 1or 2 GB is way plenty.

You may have a little challenge getting the wireless working or dual booting if you purchase with XP installed.
But hey that is what we do here :D
Check TigerDirect too.. Lots of D620's are coming out of business lease use.
