Hmn, looks like you have been into the sound system changing settings.  Are you using the laptop's speakers, headphones or it's outlet for an amplifier, or a USB sound system?  It makes a difference.  If you right click the  speaker icon in the task bar you will find the Mixer there too.  You can mute the Master or the headphones in that little window.  Also in Kick off there are a couple of places to get to sound controls, through multimedia for instance.   Check them all to make sure your output is where it belongs and it should work with your keyboard as well.  Hope this helps.
