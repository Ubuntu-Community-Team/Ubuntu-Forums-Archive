### Post by mvkfoster on 2009-11-13
Try the Fn-F7 key combination when you have that black screen. I have issues with a C600 showing dual screens on the laptop LCD and can't navigate. toggling the Fn-F7 key combination fixes the issue. Not sure of that is available on the D610 though. The C600 I have has the SXGA+ (1400x1050) LCD which toggles between high and low resolution using the Fn-F7 keys.
