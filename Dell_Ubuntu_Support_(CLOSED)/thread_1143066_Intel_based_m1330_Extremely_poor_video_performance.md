### Post by pormogo on 2009-04-29
So outside of this issue I've been relatively impressed with the move to 9.04. However my video performance has totally gone to ****. I can't even watch hulu videos anymore :( Even the compiz effects seem to no longer run smoothly (changing virtual desktops/alt+tab task switching is clunky and slow). I know a lot of people here are using the nvidia graphics chip in this particular laptop but I was wondering if any other intel users have noticed their performance in the toilet since the latest update.

---

### Post by pormogo on 2009-04-29
BTW I was able to restore a decent amount of video performance by enabling UXA support for my video card. Before this my video performance was absolutely awful. I would recommend all m1330 users on intel GMA chipsets to give this a shot (I have not seen anything negative occur).

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/UxaTesting](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/UxaTesting)
