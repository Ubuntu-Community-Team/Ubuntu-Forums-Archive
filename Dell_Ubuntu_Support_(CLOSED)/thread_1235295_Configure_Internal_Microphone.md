### Post by Katalog on 2009-08-08
One thing you can do is go to "Preferences>Sound" and under Audio Conferencing there should be a dropdown menu that says "Sound CaptureP and gives you a number of devices to select. Just go through the list and try each one. There should be a button on the right that says "Test". Press it after you select each device and once you're able to hear yourself you know you found the right one.
