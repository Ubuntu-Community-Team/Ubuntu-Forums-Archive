### Post by heikodachi on 2011-04-04
I think my problem was that I had switched my wireless off, while booted in Windows (holding Fn and F2 switches wireless off on my laptop). I was then not able to switch it back on while booted in Ubuntu. 

I booted back into Windows and made sure that the wireless was switched on. When I booted back into Ubuntu wireless networks were enabled. After that is was simply a case of typing in my wireless router information.
