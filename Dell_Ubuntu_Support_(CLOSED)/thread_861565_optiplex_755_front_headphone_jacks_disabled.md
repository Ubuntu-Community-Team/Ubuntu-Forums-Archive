### Post by joenieburg on 2008-09-26
So for me I figured it out. When u install Ubuntu out off the box your pcm is set to off.:confused:

So to enable this one.:)
On your desktop double click on your speaker icon.
In the new window check the PCM setting this should not be disabled (with a red cross through it) 
After that I had control over my sound volume.:guitar:
