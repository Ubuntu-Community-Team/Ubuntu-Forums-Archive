### Post by notwen on 2009-01-14
This [iso]("http://linux.dell.com/wiki/index.php/Ubuntu_8.04#Dell_OS_Factory_Recovery_8.04.1_DVD_ISO") should work just fine w/ your Inspiron 530n.  It's Dell's customized Ubuntu install, it includes all extra packages/drivers for all of their N-Series machines. Be sure to also take notice of the [known issues]("http://linux.dell.com/wiki/index.php/Ubuntu_8.04#Known_Issues") associated w/ Hardy and your 530n.  It's not a bad idea to check [Dell's Linux Wiki]("http://linux.dell.com/wiki/index.php/Products/Client") every so often for each custom Ubuntu image they release, this will give you the option of trying to the newer versions of Ubuntu while also insuring that your machine will be as functional as if it were shipped from Dell.  Let us know should you run into any issues.

Hope this helps.  =]
