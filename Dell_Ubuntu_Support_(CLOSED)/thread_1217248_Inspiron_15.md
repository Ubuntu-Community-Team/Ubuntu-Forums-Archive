My understanding is that it is difficult to install Windows **_*after*_** you have installed Ubuntu. It has to do with overwriting the MBR. It is not impossible but seems to require a bit of editing grub and and redoing the MBR. 

It may well be that you 15n with preinstalled Ubuntu may have had some factory tweaks to the PCI bus. You might want to go over to the Dell site and ask around about that.
