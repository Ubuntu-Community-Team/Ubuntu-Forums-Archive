### Post by Lauxmonster on 2009-09-28
Well this is just my story....

I got an old Dell Optiplex GX260 from a business that was getting rid of its old computers. They sold one to me for $30 (No OS installed). It is a Pentium 4 2.4GHz machine and came preloaded with 256MB of RAM. 

I got an extra 1GB of RAM off Newegg.com, and a Nvidia GeForce 6200 card for around $70, which makes it total about $100 for a fully functioning Ubuntu computer.

And yes...it is quite fast.


So, if you know any business who is getting rid of old PCs, you might want to give it a try because they are generally really cheap if you buy from them. Now its not new....but I haven't seen ANY computer under $200 unless you get it refurbished or used.

You could probably get the same off Ebay or a website like Joysystems.com where they sell cheap refurbished desktops.

Does this answer your question?
