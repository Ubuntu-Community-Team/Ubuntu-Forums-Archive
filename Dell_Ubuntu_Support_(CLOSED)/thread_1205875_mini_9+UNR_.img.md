### Post by snowpine on 2009-07-06
Just to make sure you understand... you don't just "transfer the file" onto a thumb drive... you need to use an image writing application/command as described here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromImgFiles](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromImgFiles)

This will delete all data on the drive and create a USB startup device with the installer.

And I recommend you use a USB thumb drive (1gb is plenty) for this purpose, not your 500gb hard drive. :)
