### Post by teward on 2009-11-30
Ubuntu Desktop Version, either 9.04 or 9.10.  I recommend 9.04, as its a more stable release in my opinion, and that 9.04 works fine on the Dell Latitude Business-line Laptops.  And judging by the specs of the model, I'd recommend 32-bit version.

However, if you want to use the most recent version (9.10 Karmic), be my guest.  But I've heard lots of problems with it.
