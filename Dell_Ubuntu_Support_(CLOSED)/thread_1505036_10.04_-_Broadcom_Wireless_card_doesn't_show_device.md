### Post by bobcollard on 2010-06-10
Your device is a BCM 4313.  Ran it through Google and came up with a possible solution Here:   [http://wiki.debian.org/wl](http://wiki.debian.org/wl)
It is listed as the network controller in the lspci -nn listing below.
02:00.0 Network controller [0280]: Broadcom Corporation Device [14e4:4727] (rev 01)
