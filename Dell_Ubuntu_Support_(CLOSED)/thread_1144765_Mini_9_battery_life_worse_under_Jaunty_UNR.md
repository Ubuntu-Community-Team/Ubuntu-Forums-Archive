### Post by anjilslaire on 2009-05-01
I've noticed a significantly lower battery life on my mini 9 since installing the Jaunty Netbook remix .img, replacing my previous i386 8.10 install.
Previously, I my mini was reporting about 3:15 on a charge. Now, its about 2:30, tops. 
The only changes I've made is a clean install of 9.04 Remix and flashing the bios to A05, which was done prior to installing Jaunty, back on 8.10

Has anyone else noticed a lower battery life on Jaunty?

Update:
Alright, I let the mini fully drain the battery until it shut itself down, then charged it overnight, allowing it to recalibrate itself, so to speak. This morning I booted it and let it sit unattended mostly, running the occasional app to prevent it from sleeping. As the system was about to die after a few hours, I ran uptime, and was given ~3:30 before it went down again.

So, despite what th battery meter is displaying, I'm now getting real-world life of well over 3 hours in Jaunty, about 15 more minutes than Intrepid.

I'm pleased :)
