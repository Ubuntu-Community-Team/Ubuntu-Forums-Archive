### Post by Arndtwe on 2009-06-18
You will not be able to upgrade within Ubuntu itself, Dell's custom kernel will not allow that. I tried it through thumb drive and it worked fine. You may want to play with 8.04 for a while before deciding to ditch it completely. It is very functional.

Jaunty will run fine on the A90, I personally would suggest installing the NBR version, since, after all, it is a netbook.
