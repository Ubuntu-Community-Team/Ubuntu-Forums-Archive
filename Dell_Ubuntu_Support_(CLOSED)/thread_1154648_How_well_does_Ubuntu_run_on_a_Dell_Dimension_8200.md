### Post by salvador24 on 2009-09-03
I am running Ubuntu Server edition on a heavily modified Dimension 8100 without any problems, and that's even older than your machine!  I use it as a NAS, torrenting appliance, firefly server, mpd server, as well as sbnzbd client.  Still has stock 1.5ghz Willamette P4 with 768mb RDRAM.  I have added a PCI SATA controller as well as a PCI USB 2.0 card to at least give some more storage options.  Currently the computer has 4 HDD in it with a grand total of 2TB of storage!  I had to remove the front panel to add more fans to cool all the HDDs...  That said, I have NO compatibility issues even with lots of modifications.  Ubuntu runs on this ancient piece of hardware like a champ!  I would have junked this computer long ago if it weren't for Ubuntu, but now it serves in integral role in my network!
