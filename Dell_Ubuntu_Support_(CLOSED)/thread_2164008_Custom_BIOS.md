### Post by lah7 on 2013-07-31
Well, if you was to have your own home-made custom BIOS, you should be extremely careful... since if wrong or corrupted firmware was flashed into your computer's BIOS, you risk bricking the entire system and would need special equipment or a specialist (and some money) to restore the BIOS back to working order. I would strongly suggest you don't fiddle with perfectly working BIOS' in computers.

What are you trying to accomplish using a 'custom BIOS' ? Are you wanting to change its POST screen appearance of the Dell logo for instance?
