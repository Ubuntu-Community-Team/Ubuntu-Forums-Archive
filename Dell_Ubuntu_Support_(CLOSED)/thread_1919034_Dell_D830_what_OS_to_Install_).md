### Post by nazaroo2 on 2012-02-03
I wonder if your main beef is the browser speed, you should probably look at that, not the OS.

Firefox out of the box might not be blocking ads and popups, and may be open to all kinds of attack.
You can add an extension to firefox to block ads and popups, and that alone would tremendously increase browser speed.

But if its an internet speed issue, it probably ain't the OS, its probably your internet service provider,
even if they deny it.
