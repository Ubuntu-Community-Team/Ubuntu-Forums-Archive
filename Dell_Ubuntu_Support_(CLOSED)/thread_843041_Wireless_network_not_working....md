I don't know which wireless you have in there, but most of the Dells seem to have the troublesome Broadcom wireless chips in them.  If that's so, you might check out this link:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766560](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766560)
