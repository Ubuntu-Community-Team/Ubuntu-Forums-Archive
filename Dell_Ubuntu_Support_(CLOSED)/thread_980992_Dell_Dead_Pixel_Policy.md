### Post by zelthian on 2008-11-13
Be advised: the Mini 9, being an Inspiron, falls under the Inspiron/Studio/Vostro category as it pertains to dead pixels. That means, even though the screen resolution is only 1024x600, you have to have 6 dead pixels before Dell will replace it.

Yes, this just happened to me. I have a dead pixel in a very bad spot. Bummer.

The policy is here:

[http://support.dell.com/support/topics/global.aspx/support/dsn/document?c=us&cs=19&l=en&s=dhs&dn=1018431](http://support.dell.com/support/topics/global.aspx/support/dsn/document?c=us&cs=19&l=en&s=dhs&dn=1018431)
