### Post by Toz on 2010-08-26
Xubuntu ([www.xubuntu.org](www.xubuntu.org)) and lubuntu ([www.lubuntu.net](www.lubuntu.net)) are supposed to be the lightest ubuntus. If you're game, have a look at crunchbang ([http://crunchbanglinux.org/](http://crunchbanglinux.org/)). I find it faster and more responsive (using it on an 10-year old laptop). The stable version is based on ubuntu 9.04. The newer versions are based on debian squeeze.

/ToZ
