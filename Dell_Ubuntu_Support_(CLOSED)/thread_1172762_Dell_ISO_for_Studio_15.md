### Post by coffeeaddict22 on 2009-05-31
Not sure about the Dell ISO.  I'm running vanilla 9.04 on a Studio 17 with next to no problems.  The fingerprint reader isn't supported, although theres a fairy active driver program, and it's looking promising; the rest of it works pretty much out of the box.  Apparently since I bought mine the video chipset may have changed- someone was asking about the next Radeon card in Ubuntu the other day on a Studio 15, so you may be limited by that for a week or two, although from what I read the driver has come out (have a look [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1114683&page=2")  The wireless is a Broadcom chip, so the driver is a post-install download, and my bluetooth mouse took a bit of tweaking, but it's all functioning fine now.
Overall, can't complain!  I've still got the Vista install on another partition, but nowadays that's mainly to remind me just how slow Windows runs, and how many antivirus updates I've been missing.
