### Post by efflandt on 2009-11-23
After you update your system you may find those errors going away.  In 9.10 apport was enabled by default, which seemed to be overzealous reporting non-critical warnings as critcal warnings and bugs.  The solution in updates was to disable apport by default, and you can enable it to do bug reports if you have real problems that crash programs or your system.
