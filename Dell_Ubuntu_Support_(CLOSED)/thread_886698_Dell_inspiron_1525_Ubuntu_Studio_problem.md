### Post by Ichido on 2008-08-11
What you describe sounds like a Hardware Issue.
First I would try, with the PC shut down, hold down the *Fn* key as you press the Power-On button. This is a "Start-up" boot test for Hardware.
The *Fn* button is on the Bottom Left side of the keyboard, between the Ctrl Key and the "Weird" Button with a Black Circle containing 4 little Bent Squares ;-)
Have you tried the Dell Forum?
[http://www.dellcommunity.com/supportforums/board?board.id=insp_video](http://www.dellcommunity.com/supportforums/board?board.id=insp_video)

Try it.
