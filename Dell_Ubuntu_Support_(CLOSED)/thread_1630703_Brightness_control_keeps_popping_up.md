### Post by sanderd17 on 2010-11-25
could it be that your power supply stops when your battery is 100% and starts when it's less than 99%? the brightness pops up when you change from net power to battery power. 

If this is the problem, you could try removing your battery (this will make the power supply to keep working and it will even have a good influence on your battery life. you could also try to set the brightness for power and battery the same. I believe ubuntu doesn't give a notification when there is nothing changing, but I'm not sure.

---

### Post by jkohama on 2010-11-25
Thanks that was it. I changed the setting in the general power settings to only display an icon when the battery is citically low and it stopped popping up. Have a great day
