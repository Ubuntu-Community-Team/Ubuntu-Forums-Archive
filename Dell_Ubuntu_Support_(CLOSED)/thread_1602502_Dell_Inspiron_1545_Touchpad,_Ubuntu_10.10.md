### Post by Knowle on 2010-10-21
Is it not installed by default? I have a 1545n I got a little over a year ago. Right now in 10.04 all I have to do is go to System>Preferences>Mouse. Then select the Touchpad tab. Under that you have settings to disable the touchpad while typing, enabled/disable mouse clicks with touchpad, and enable/disable edge scrolling.
