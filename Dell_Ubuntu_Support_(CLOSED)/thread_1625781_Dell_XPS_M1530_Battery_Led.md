### Post by marcog42 on 2010-11-20
Try looking at it in a darkened room.  It's probably flashing blue a few times, and then red once.  If it is flashing blue and then red it means that your battery is dead/dying.  Mine did the same thing and when I checked the battery info it showed it as having only 47% capacity.  Essentially, it was only capable of charging half way.  Of course, this happened about 2 weeks out of warranty.  Got a pretty good deal for a couple of 9-cell batteries on Amazon, though. :D
