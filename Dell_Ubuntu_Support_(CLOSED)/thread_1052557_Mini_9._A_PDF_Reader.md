### Post by sirebral on 2009-01-27
The Mini 9 may have just erased my distaste for reading PDfs.  Here is how.

I can, with Evince, turn a PDF 90 degrees onto it's side.  Then I can place the PDF into presentation mode.  With just a press of an arrow key I move to the next page.  Very very nice.

Some people like PDfs because they save trees.  I hate PDFs because they suck energy, require I sit at a desk, and the light from the monitor is terrible on my eyes.  But now that I can turn my Mini sideways and treat it like a little book, I might actually like PDFs for a change.  I new it would happen some day!

What do you guys think about using your Mini 9 as a PDF reader?
