### Post by theboneripper on 2011-01-22
Hi, 

Even i had a problem connecting to my wifi but somehow managed to fix it. 

The easiest way to connect to wifi is by clicking the "network manager" icon on the top right of your panel (near the volume, battery or message icon). Enable "networking" and "wireless" there(right click) and connect to the network.

If you don't see this icon, then right click on panel> add to panel> add "notification area" to the panel.
 
Also check the help file. Hit F1 and search for wireless troubleshooting. Follow those steps.

Hope this helps.
