### Post by ugm6hr on 2009-07-31
> **jnolan said:**
> Are these related?

Yes.

The problem is exactly as stated.  Your HD is 100% full.

I presume you have a 4GB Mini 9; Dell's updates cause the tiny HD to get full.  Once this occurs, it causes a variety of different problems.

You have a few options:

1. Download the updates to an external storage device and then install them (after emptying your personal files etc).

2. Just start again with Ubuntu 9.04 NBR.

You may have to take precautions to ensure the problem does not recur.

There is a thread about this problem with details here already.. I will try and find it.
