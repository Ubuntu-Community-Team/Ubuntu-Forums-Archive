### Post by wojox on 2010-09-02
The easiest for starting out is Ubuntu Software Center.

Followed by Synaptic Package Manager.

If you know exactly what you want you can open a terminal and use apt-get or aptitude.

Those are about the three easiest in that order. 

There's more as well such as ppa's and installing from outside the repositories.
