### Post by PaulW2U on 2012-05-18
> **khurtsiya said:**
> I mean 3 buttons on the top right above keyboard section.

I know this is a little late but just for the record these buttons, according to page 35 of the N7110 manual only work with Windows. The N5110 and N7110 are obviously similar models and the fact that the manual makes a point of stating what it does surely means that any attempt to get them working with a non-Windows OS is pointless.

They are not "keyboard" keys and presumably the BIOS only enables them whilst a Windows OS is active. Presumably the software that supports them is in one of the hidden [Windows] partitions.
