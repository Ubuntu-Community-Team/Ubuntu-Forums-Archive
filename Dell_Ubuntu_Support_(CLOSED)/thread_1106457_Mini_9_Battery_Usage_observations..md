### Post by sirebral on 2009-03-25
I just wanted to post some of my battery charge observations.  About two weeks ago I fell asleep with my laptop running while I was watching videos.  Afterwards the battery had lost about 20 minutes of charge time, which later turned into 40 minutes.  

After that I have been more careful with my Mini 9, thinking I had overheated the battery and damaged it.  I have noticed though that if I place my Mini 9 into suspend mode and leave it plugged in it is pretty good for the battery, giving me another 20 minutes of charge time back.

Just some observations.  Hopefully I can get the other 20 minutes back.
