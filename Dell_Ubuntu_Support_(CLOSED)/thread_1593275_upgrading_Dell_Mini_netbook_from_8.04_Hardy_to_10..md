### Post by snowpine on 2010-10-11
The only way to "upgrade" is to back up all of your data and do a fresh reinstall of 10.10. The reason for this is that your architecture (lpia) is no longer supported (you should now use the "32-bit (recommended)" architecture).

Instructions for getting started with 10.10 are here:

[http://www.ubuntu.com/netbook/get-ubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/netbook/get-ubuntu/download)
