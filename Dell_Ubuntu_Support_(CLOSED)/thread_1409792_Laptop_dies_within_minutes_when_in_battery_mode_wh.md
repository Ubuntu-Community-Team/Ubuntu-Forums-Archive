### Post by niteshifter on 2010-02-19
Hi,

Ever drop the battery? That can take the life right out of 'em (a damaged internal cell). Packaging damaged when you got the battery? 

Definitive test (removes the OS as a cause):
Boot into the Dell's BIOS. There's a screen in there somewhere that shows battery charge level. Watch that indicator - you may have to move off and back onto that screen for it to update.

If the battery fails here after a few minutes, plug the charger in and let the machine charge while powered off.

Repeat the BIOS check. If it fails again, your battery or the charging circuitry has failed. Buy (or borrow) another battery to isolate the problem further.
