### Post by LowSky on 2009-08-26
1. update the bios if needed, sometime it fixes bugs and/or add/removes features. 

2. BIOS and Drivers are two different things, 
BIOS is the firmware for the motherboard. Drivers are the software that runs things like your trackpad and screen and printers and such.
usually upgrades to BIOS and Drivers help things like battery life and functions like hibernation/suspend. I would recommend doing them if possible.

3. dependd on how it install. the website will give you instructions. USB flash drives cost a little as $10 on sites like Newegg.com



Note is is up to you, if you not comfortable handling new installs than by all means carry on doing what your doing.
