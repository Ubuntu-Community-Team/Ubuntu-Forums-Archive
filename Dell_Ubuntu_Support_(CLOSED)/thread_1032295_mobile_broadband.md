### Post by stevehaig on 2009-01-19
I have bought a top up and go vodafone 3565 usb modem, and downloaded VMC and modeswitcher from the betavine website.  I was struggling to get a connection, but then vodafone told me their APN was pp.internet, not pp.vodafone.co.uk as is commonly reported.  This solved all my problems, and it is working fine, without needing to update to 8.10.
