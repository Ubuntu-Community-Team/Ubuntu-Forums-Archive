### Post by vikingstork on 2013-11-25
I am not familiar with this machine, but my Dimension 3100 lets you enter CMOS setup, just after POST. A notice pops up "press F2 for setup", or something like that, and you can choose the boot sequence. If you choose USB as first one, it should boot into it. And if you choose you HDD as a second one, it will boot into HDD when USB drive not present. I don't know if it helps with newer ones, tho.
