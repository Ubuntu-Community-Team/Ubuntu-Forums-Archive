### Post by Anzan on 2009-01-16
Perhaps not germane to the original post but we have found that Fluxbox is ideal for the 7 and 9 inch Eees.

As with laptops, just put gnome-power-manager and nm-applet to the Startup file. 

Set workspaces for specific tasks. CTL+SHIFT A (maximize) apps in each workspace above the taskbar and tab those you want together.

I've had an Eee up for 68 days like this with not a glitch. (Set sleep in power management in Gnome Control Centre.)
