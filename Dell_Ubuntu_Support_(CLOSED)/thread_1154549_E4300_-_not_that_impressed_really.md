### Post by dunbrokin on 2009-05-09
I recently replaced my D610 running 9.04  with (what I thought would be infinitely superior) e4300....but, in all honesty, I am not that impressed.

The e4300 does not seem like a step up performance wise. Sure the candy works better than with the D610...but that is about it really.

The sound is woefully inadequate, even when everything is turned on full volume, the 3D effects are better...but day to day performance is pretty negligible. I am not sure if this means that Ubuntu does a really superb job in an old machine lie the D610 or that the E4300 is just not an impressive machine oeverall.
