### Post by gbrainin on 2012-02-14
I'm not familiar with your particular model, but the most common sources of problems are the video card and the wireless card.  Based on what you've described, I'd look at the wireless first.  What you need to do is find out exactly what wireless card/chipset you're using (sometimes a given computer model will have more than one over its lifetime) and search (here or elsewhere) for that card and the version of Linux you're using.  Usually, someone will have posted something with a similar problem, and sometimes a solution.  If not, then post to see if someone can help you out.

Alternately, you can try installing an older version.  Sometimes problems creep in along with updates, and a computer that works fine with version 10.10 will start having problems with 11.04, for example.  The flip side is that these problems tend to get fixed over time, so perhaps the next version will work better.
