### Post by irfannp on 2009-11-21
Its working for me perfectly...check whether the keyboard shortcut is set or not.
Goto System->preferences->Keyboard Shortcuts. Make sure that shortcuts  in the different sections like  'Sound' section is set .If not, click on the item, say Volume mute and press the shortcut key.(in inspiron 1525 , there you can see a touch key with symbol of mute just above the functional keys .)
