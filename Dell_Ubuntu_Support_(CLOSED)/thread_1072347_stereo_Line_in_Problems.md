### Post by RD1 on 2009-02-17
Open "Volume Control" (Applications>Sound&Video>Volume Control)

You should see 3 tabs: "Playback" Recording" and "Switches" 

Under "Playback", unmute Line-In so that you can hear the signal to be recorded (you can mute this during actual recording if you wish).

Under "Recording", unmute "Capture" (this is the onlt slider I have on the "Recording" tab).

You should now be able to use the "Capture" slider to adjust the recording level.

You mat also need to check your recording settings in Audacity's preferences. (under Audacity's "Edit" menu)

Good Luck with your recording. 

I used to do a lot of recording myself. Mostly turning cassette tapes into cd format. I used Sound Forge in Windows but now I need to learn Audacity for Linux.
