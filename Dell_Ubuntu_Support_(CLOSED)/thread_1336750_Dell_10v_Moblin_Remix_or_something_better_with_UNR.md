### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-11-25
It's a bit hard to advise not knowing any of your specific needs, but here are a couple of general pointers:

 - 8.04 may be older, but it's rather solid these days, and you can always install a different version. (Recommended.)

 - Moblin Remix in nice, but is still somewhat 'raw' and less polished the the current version of UNR.

 - SSDs are faster and have no moving parts, but you get 10 times less storage.

Other netbooks may or may not work with Ubuntu, I don't know, but you'd probably not get the sweet price you are getting on Dells, and will have to pay the Windows tax.
