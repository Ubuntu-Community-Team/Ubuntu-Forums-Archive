### Post by RedRat on 2009-08-18
Well if your vanilla 9.04 is working and you have no problems why do you want to go to the Dell ISO? 

When i got my Dell 530n with 7.04 pre-installed, I managed to screw the computer up completely by trying to install Ubuntu-studio. To make a long story short, I wiped the hard drive clean and just installed the vanilla 7.04 Ubuntu from Canonical. I went on to upgrade to 7.10 with no problems and then on to 8.04. Minor problems having to do with graphics (nVidia driver and kernel updates) but that had nothing to do with a Dell ISO. A lot of people had that problem. 

Unless you have some very dire reason for mucking about with what you have already on your system, I would advise you to stay put. Being conservative in this case can be a good thing.
