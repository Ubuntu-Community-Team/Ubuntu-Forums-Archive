### Post by frogo on 2010-08-02
Latitude e4310. Good enough to run all i need for work, which really isn't a lot of demanding things.

WRT hardware, connection (stupid mic/headest combo jack and a single usb) is a bit frustrating. One nice feature I thought would be gadget is the backlit keyboard (despite the utterly absurd 'pointing stick' in the very centre of the keyboard). The rest must be good enough for I can't think of another complaint.

WRT running ubuntu, I have less than a handful of problems but they are not insignificant. Anecdotally, it's fortunate I don't care for playing DVDs on this machine. More significantly it's fortunate that I don't need to be on batteries for more than an hour in a row and especially that I don't mind shutting down instead of putting it to sleep (since it wouldn't restart). But to be fair, that doesn't seem to be specific to Dell...
