### Post by MacSob on 2008-11-16
Hey,

I had the same problem on my D600 and solved it by going into the BIOS settings and making wireless always on, not OS configurable.  Once I did that all of my problems went away, on my next reboot the hardware wizard found new drivers and installed them.

I also did this in 8.10, so I'm not sure if it will work in 8.04.

Hope this helped.
