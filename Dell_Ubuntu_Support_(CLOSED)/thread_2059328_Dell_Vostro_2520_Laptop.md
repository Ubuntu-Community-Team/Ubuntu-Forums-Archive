### Post by jeffjardine on 2012-10-02
I just got mine and I'd say my experiences are mixed so far.  

It came out of the box with 11.10 installed.  There was an annoying flickering green line about six pixels wide across the top of the display.  A few attempts at eliminating it failed so I upgraded to 12.04, which solved the problem but broke the wireless of course.  Fortunately this fix worked like a charm:
[http://askubuntu.com/questions/175104/how-do-i-install-bcm43142-wireless-drivers-for-dell-vostro-3460-3560](http://askubuntu.com/questions/175104/how-do-i-install-bcm43142-wireless-drivers-for-dell-vostro-3460-3560)

So far I like it a little better than the Lenovo B570 it replaced due to a failed motherboard.  It's lighter and the battery life is a little longer.
