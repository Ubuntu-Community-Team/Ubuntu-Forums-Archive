### Post by x C0MMAND0 x on 2008-12-15
1. Double click on the sound icon in the top right corner

2. Choose "Preferences" and then check the box "Digital Input Source"

3. Close "Preferences" and go the the "Options" tab.  For "Digital Input Source" choose Digital Mic 1.


That should do it for ya.  The volume for recording isn't very loud; I'm still trying to work that out, but it seems to work okay with Skype when I talk to my girlfriend.

Enjoy!

EDIT:  Also, this is for the internal mic.  I have not yet tested/worked with an external mic.
