### Post by wrench70 on 2009-04-10
Ubuntu 8.10 doesn't work on the mini 12 unless you want to do some tweaking there is a thread on here explaining it. I had jaunty 9.04 running on my mini 12 but you don't have accelerated graphics just 2d desktop and slow frame rates. The problem is the GMA-500 (poulsbo) graphics chip that there are no drivers yet. Hopefully Jaunty will get it done with the final release on the 23rd. I don't mind 8.04 on the Mini since it's lpia and I get 4+ hrs on the 6 cell battery.
