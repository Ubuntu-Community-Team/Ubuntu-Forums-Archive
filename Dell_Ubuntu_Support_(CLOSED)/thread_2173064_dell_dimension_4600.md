### Post by Hedon James on 2013-10-04
i installed Ubuntu 12.04 on my Dimension 4600 and completely wiped the WinXP OS.  I had problems with the nVidia card, but swapped with another machine in the house and learned the card was failing.  ordered a replacement card and everything is working fine.  using a linksys usb 54wrt(?) wireless required a little tinkering, but the installation of a firmware package solved that too.  Recently ordered a Bolse mini-usb wireless stick from Amazon ($15) for another linux box in the house...plug & play with NO issues whatsoever.  Everything is fine...go ahead and do it.  If you want to dual-boot, I'd allocate around 20GB for XP, depending on how many programs you load into XP.
