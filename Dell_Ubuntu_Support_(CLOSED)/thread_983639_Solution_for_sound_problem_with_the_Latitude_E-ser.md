### Post by lpchouinard on 2008-11-15
I only test this in 8.10 i386

When you dock your E-Serie (e4300,e6400...) and use external speakers plug into the headphone jack of the docking, the internal speaker of the laptop isn't disable.

The solution is simple, edit (dont forget sudo) /etc/modprobe.d/alsa-base and add at the end of the file this line:

options snd-hda-intel model=ref

save and reboot
Then, go into the Volume Control applet and click the Preference button, check the IC958 option, close, chose the Switches Tab and check the IEC958 option, close and it should now only send audio signal in the headphone jack of the docking.
