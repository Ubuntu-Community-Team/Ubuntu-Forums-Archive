### Post by grizzler on 2011-03-06
Look at one of the many other current threads about the problems with YouTube. Quick fix: disable YouTube's cookies or switch off hardware acceleration (right click on flash video, click Preferences...*), first tab).
You'll need to visit a site where the Preferences...*) item is actually selectable, which seems to depend on cookie settings.

Edit: switching off cookies appears to make the Preferences...*) entry accessible in YouTube as well.


*) Could be Settings... or something like that. I'm not using an English language version.
