### Post by starcannon on 2008-11-17
So far every USB DVD/CD drive I've tried works. Spend a little extra, some of the external drive manufactures like to load those shiny cases with cheap parts that let you down. Or better still build your own, cases and parts can be shopped for at pricewatch.com, pricegrabber.com, newegg.com etc... etc...

To build it yourself you will need a Dvd Rom or nicer, and a case suitable for the form factor you chose. A screw driver, and about 15 minutes of time to assemble.

GL and have fun.

### Post by notwen on 2008-11-17
You can rip your re-install media to an iso and use [Unetbootin]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/") to boot up a USB drive as a CD/DVD drive.  Just be sure to select Boot from USB during startup.  I used Unetbootin w/ the Ubuntu 8.04 iso to install Hardy onto my Mini 9 w/ XP pre-installed.  Hope this helps. Post back if you run into any issues.  =]
