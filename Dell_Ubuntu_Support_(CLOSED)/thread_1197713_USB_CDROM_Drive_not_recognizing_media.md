### Post by coffeeaddict22 on 2009-06-27
Hi,
Going on my own experience, were you fiddling with something? :-)

Seriously, The most likely problem is your permissions.  Go into System/Adminsitration/Users and Groups/ and unlock it with your root password.  Then open your user name, and under permissions ensure there's a tick in "Use CDROM's" and also in "Access external storage devices aautomatically". 

That should fix it.  Post back if it doesn't.
