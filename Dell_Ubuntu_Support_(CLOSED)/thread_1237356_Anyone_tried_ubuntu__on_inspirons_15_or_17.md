### Post by NormanFLinux on 2009-08-11
Installs without issue on a Dell Inspiron e1505. Wireless works with Ubuntu out of the box. The ATI proprietary drivers do not work in Jaunty 9.04 so the Radeon HD open source driver will allow Compiz to be enabled - that requires making it the default driver in the xorg.conf file.
