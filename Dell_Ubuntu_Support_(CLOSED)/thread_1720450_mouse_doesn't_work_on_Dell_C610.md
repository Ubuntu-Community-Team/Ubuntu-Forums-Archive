### Post by blindganger on 2011-04-03
My P/S2 mouse on my Dell Latitude C610 (which is OK with Windoz XP) doesn't work with Ubuntu or any of its variants (lubuntu, Mint or Puppy) even though it was working with Puppy before I switched. The track pad and switches work fine.

When I do a system profile it tells me that I have a generic P/S2 mouse and NO trackpad. What do I do next?  :confused:

blindganger

---

### Post by blindganger on 2011-05-04
Problem Solved!

After weeks of hoping that someone would come up with an answer I started trial and error problem solving. it seems the problem was caused by Dell bios setup.

When running Windows I had set up the bios to disable the trackpad which I never use. When I switched it back to 'trackpad and PS2 mouse', the mouse started working properly. 

blindganger
