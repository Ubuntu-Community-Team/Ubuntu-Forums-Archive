### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-09-30
If I understood the description of the problem correctly, it is fairly to most netbooks because of the smaller resolutions of their screens. I suspect you are using the version of Ubuntu optimized for 11024x768 or higher resolutions. The easiest workaround would be to drag the windows upwards, which is done by dragging while holding the Alt key. In other words, place the mouse pointer over the window, and move the mouse upward, while holding AL and the left mouse button.
Most of these were tackled in Ubunu Netbook Remix, but that means reinstalling, which probably isn't what you want.
