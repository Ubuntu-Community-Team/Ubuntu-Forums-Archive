### Post by will103 on 2008-07-25
Hi,

I just accidentally pressed the 'home' button above the keyboard rather than the power button (to boot the machine). It launched some Dell restore software. It started to reformat my disk, so that my linux partitions are hosed. The output from TestDisk showed a slice straight through my root partition cutting it in half.

In short this is a warning to all Dell Inspiron 6400 owners to avoid pressing this button while the machine is switched off. Luckily I had just finished a full backup the night before.

With best wishes
