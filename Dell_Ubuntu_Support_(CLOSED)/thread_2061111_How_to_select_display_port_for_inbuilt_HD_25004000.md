### Post by resander on 2012-09-23
I phoned Dell support and asked about this. They said the ports are mutually exclusive. When the graphics card is present the vga port on the motherboard is deactivated. To activate the motherboard port one would have to remove the graphics card. A bit primitive I think and not very user-friendly.
