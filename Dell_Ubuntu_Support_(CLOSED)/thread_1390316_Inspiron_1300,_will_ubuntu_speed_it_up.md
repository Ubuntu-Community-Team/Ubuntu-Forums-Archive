### Post by snowpine on 2010-01-25
Windows XP is generally faster than Ubuntu on older hardware (in my experience). XP is 9 years old and was released at a time when 256mb was a lot of ram. Ubuntu 9.10 was released in 2009 and really shines on modern hardware (Windows 7 is a better comparison).

I would suggest maxing out your ram before trying Ubuntu. You should check out the "lightweight" distros such as Puppy, SliTaz, etc. if speed is your #1 concern.
