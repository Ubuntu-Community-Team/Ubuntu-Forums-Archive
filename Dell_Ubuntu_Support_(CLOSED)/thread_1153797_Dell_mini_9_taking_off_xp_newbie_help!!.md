### Post by ugm6hr on 2009-05-09
There is no need to uninstall XP at all.

Ubuntu will install itself over the top of XP.

A USB IMG [file]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GettingUbuntu#Download%20the%20Live%20Netbook%20Image") is now available for the netbook remix, with details on how to install here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromImgFiles](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromImgFiles)

### Post by ugm6hr on 2009-05-09
Plug the USB stick in, turn on the Mini.

Press the key "2" to enter setup at the "Dell" boot screen (or just keep pressing it after turning it on for the first 20 secs).

This might help: [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mN0TkBWZZaY](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mN0TkBWZZaY)

Just move USB to the top of the list (Use F6 and F7 (Fn H and J respectively on the mini) to move your intended boot device up and down the list).

The USB drive should be a the top of this list.

Should be fairly self-explanatory from there.
