### Post by Retiredman2006 on 2008-12-13
Just go to the toolbar View/Toolbars and turnoff or on which ever one you like.  Doing it this way leaves the toolbar gadget on your system but it's just not seen. If you don't want it at all, go to the software management section and uninstall/uncheck the package and hit apply.  I'm a very new Ubuntu user (4days old) and find that it's a very friendly system to customize.
