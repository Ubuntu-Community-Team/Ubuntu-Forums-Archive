### Post by delogren on 2009-07-07
Yes!  I got spell check to work!

Firefox was underlining most of the words I typed.  I thought spell-check was broken, or not installed.  I was wrong!

When I moved the cursor to an underlined (misspelled) word and right-clicked for the correct spelling, something didn't look right.  But, at the bottom of the correct spellings list was "Languages".  Clicking that showed me that Firefox had defaulted to something that might be German.  Scrolling through the list of dictionaries, I found US English, clicked that, and problem solved!
