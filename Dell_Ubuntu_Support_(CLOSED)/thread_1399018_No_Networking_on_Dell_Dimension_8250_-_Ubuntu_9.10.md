Dell uses proprietary drivers that are not included in "Free" system software.  In your menu find System/hardware drivers and open that.  You will need an Ethernet connection to the Internet to download and install the driver that is recommended.  Then after a reboot you click on the icon in your task bar and open Manage Connections to enter your ISP information.
