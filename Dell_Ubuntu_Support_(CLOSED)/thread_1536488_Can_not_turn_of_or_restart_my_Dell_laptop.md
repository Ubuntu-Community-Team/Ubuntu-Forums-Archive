### Post by camorri on 2010-07-22
Hold the power button to the power off/on position for several seconds. Most hardware ( laptops ) will then turn off. 

There can be many causes for a hang on power down. See if you can see the messages on power down. Sometimes that will give you a clew to the problem. 

Often it will be a hung application not responding.
