### Post by Bucky Ball on 2011-05-01
Firstly, if you install 11.04 get ready for a steep learning curve and possibly a heap of issues (latest is *not* always the greatest). Check the forums for the growing number of 11.04 probs.

Secondly, System>Administration>Network or just right click on the network icon and 'Edit Connections'. Click on the appropriate wired or wireless device, click edit and put your details in the IPv4 tab.

Good luck with it. ;)

ps: If you go ahead with 11.04 I strongly suggest you 'Try Ubuntu' from the LiveCD first and see if it is running ok *before* you do a full hard drive install.
