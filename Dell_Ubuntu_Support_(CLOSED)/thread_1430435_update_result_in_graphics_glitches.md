### Post by coffeeaddict22 on 2010-03-18
Hi,
Welcome to Ubuntu.
You really should be on 9.10.  8.04 is almost so... 1990s.  Well, not quite, but things have improved, especially wireless networking, bluetooth and Intel graphics in particular.
Secondly, 8.04 is stable, but most of the video gurus have moved a long way since then.  Wine is also actively worked on, so using it on a fairly old kernel may be the source of some of your problems.  The Ubuntu Wine version is usually quite a bit behind the version at [www.winehq.org](www.winehq.org) too, so a temporising measure may be to try installing the most recent version of wine.
