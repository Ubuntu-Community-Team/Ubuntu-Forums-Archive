I was just messing around with the elements on the superior panel and I clicked something wrong... I don't know what I did, but now the battery icon, the networks icon, and the keyboard rest icon have disappeared.

---

### Post by lswb on 2009-08-30
Sounds like you accidentally deleted the "Notification Area."

Right-click on the panel, select "Add to Panel" then scroll down to and install "Notification Area"

If the network, battery, etc. icons don't show up right away, log out of the desktop and back in again.
