1. Pressing 'z' gives me a 'y' and vice-versa

3. Pressing '-' gives me a greek beta

etc...

### Post by mat106 on 2008-11-28
Ok, for anyone interested the problem here was that a US/UK keyboard layout had to be added via System->Keyboard.
