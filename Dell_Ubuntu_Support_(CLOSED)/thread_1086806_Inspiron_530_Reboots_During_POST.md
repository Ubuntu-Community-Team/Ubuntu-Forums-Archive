### Post by gbrainin on 2009-03-04
This is probably a hardware question, but I haven't found a solution so I thought I'd give the forum a try.

Normal boot-up behavior for my Inspirion 530 is as follows: press the power button, it turns yellow then blue.  Fan turns on high for a couple of seconds, then turns down.  POST happy beep, then runs through normal boot sequence.

Just yesterday, it started this sequence instead: the fan stays on (I think) a bit longer than normal, the power button turns back to yellow then off, then the computer shuts off completely for a couple of seconds, then turns itself back on (as if doing a reboot).  This happens once or twice, then it goes into the normal boot sequence.

Based on internet suggestions, I tried cleaning out the inside, removing and re-seating the video card and memory, and running the hardware tests.  This did not fix the problem, nor did any of the hardware fail the tests.

The machine is of course well out of warranty.  Any ideas what might be causing this, and how to fix it?

### Post by gbrainin on 2009-03-06
Never mind.  By judicious juggling of hardware, I have determined to a high level of certainty that it is the video card that's causing the problem.  I'll be getting a new one of those.
