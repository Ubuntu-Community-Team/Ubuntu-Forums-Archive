### Post by RedRat on 2009-12-05
I heard sometime back that Dell-Canada was dropping support for Ubuntu. This might be a matter of just combining the two operations. I think it would be wise to remember that to Dell the number of Ubuntu users is quite small compared to Windows. Why offer support to small number in Canada AND a small number in the U.S. when if you combine the service you might get a larger pool of users. Basically just efficiency, i.e., support costs per user. 

I do not believe there is any legal or copyright reasons why there ought to be two separate Ubuntu groups, one in Canada and one in the U.S. Yes, Europe and the European Union does have a different approach so it would make some sense to maintain a European Ubuntu site (Does Dell do this?? I don't know).
