### Post by anjilslaire on 2009-01-17
If you set the right functions  on your top panel, you'll have  mini icons on the top bar that hold all of your windows to restore, etc.

[http://www.ubuntumini.com/2008/10/installing-ubuntu-netbook-remix.html](http://www.ubuntumini.com/2008/10/installing-ubuntu-netbook-remix.html)
> 
The Ubuntu Remix Layout
To get the Ubuntu Remix look, remove the bottom pane, by right clicking the bottom panel and selecting the "Delete this Panel" option. Then add the Remix functions to your top panel. Right click the top panel and select the "Add to the Panel" option. You Should Have (but not limited to): GoHomeApplet,** WindowPickerApplet,** NotificationArea, and VolumeControl.


Bolded emphasis mine.  I believe WindowPicker should  fix you up.

Now if you could figure out the Mic... ;)
