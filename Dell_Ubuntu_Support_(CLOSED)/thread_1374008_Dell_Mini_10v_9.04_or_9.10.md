### Post by snowpine on 2010-01-06
[This site]("http://www.ubuntumini.com/") has detailed install guides for both 9.04 and 9.10 on the Dell Mini 10v.

I have the similar Dell Mini 9, and in my opinion, either 9.04 or 9.10 would be an improvement over 8.04. (Ubuntu 8.04 itself is a great release, but the pre-installed version in this case is a special custom "Dellbuntu" that I did not personally care for.) My advice is to try both and see which you like better, keeping in mind that 9.10 would give you an extra 6 months of support.
