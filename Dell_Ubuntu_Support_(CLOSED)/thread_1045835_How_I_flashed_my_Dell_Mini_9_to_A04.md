> Upgrading the BIOS

As of Jan. 5 2009 it is not recommended to apply any BIOS upgrades unless they are specifically approved for Ubuntu (ie any BIOS upgrades available from the Dell website).

The only BIOS recommended update for the Dell Mini 9 as of Jan 5 2009 is version A01. There is no know mechanism for doing this update from Linux at this time. A DOS-based mechanism will be posted here shortly. For more details see this thread in the Ubuntu forums.
