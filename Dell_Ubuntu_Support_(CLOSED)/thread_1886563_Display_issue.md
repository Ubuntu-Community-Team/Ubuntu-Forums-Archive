### Post by dinusuresh on 2011-11-25
hi smaxton, jus follow these steps to make sure your display is in proper condition

 - power off your system
 - hold down the 'D' key for 5-10s and press the power button.

if your display turns on showing up different colors then your display is in working condition. else, it is definitely a hardware issue and the solution would be either 'mend it' (if possible) or 'replace it'
