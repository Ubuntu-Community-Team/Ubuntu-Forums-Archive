### Post by OrangeCrate on 2009-02-02
If I'm understanding your question correctly, make sure that you've changed the option in Software Sources > Updates > from "Long Term Support" to "Normal releases".

(And, a point of order for future reference. The "8" stands for the year of release, 200"8". The ".something" stands for the month of release within that year. Intrepid was released in October, hence ".10", not January, ".1".)
