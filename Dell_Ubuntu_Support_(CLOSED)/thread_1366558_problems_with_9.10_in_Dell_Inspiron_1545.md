### Post by efflandt on 2009-12-28
I just installed 64-bit Ubuntu on a 1545 and everything seems to work fine, but the only thing I have to compare it to is older systems that I had practice on before I ordered this for someone.  Originally I installed Ubuntu on the hard drive after shrinking 64-bit Win7, but now I removed that and installed it on an external USB backup drive instead, since the person I ordered it for is not too Linux aware.

Its BCM4312 needs the Broadcom STA enabled, but it works better and is more sensitive than BCM4311 on an older work Dell laptop I have.  I had no issues using Rythmbox to play music on CD or SD.
