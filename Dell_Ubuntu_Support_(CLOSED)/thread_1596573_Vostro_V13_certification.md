### Post by brieweb on 2010-10-20
I booted my Vostro V13 with 10.10 using the thumb drive and all works well. I did not do extensive testing, but I am sure it all works. Perhaps the change is due to the change in the Broadcom wireless? When I bought my V13, I changed the broadcom wireless card to an intel one so that it would not have to use a proprietary driver. Otherwise, the Vostro V13 uses hardware that is all supported. The video works great. It uses DDR-3. The only downside is that it has a 30 WHr battery. But, you still get a good 2 to 3 hours out of it. I think new, I got 3.5 hours.
