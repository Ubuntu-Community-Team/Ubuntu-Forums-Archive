### Post by nwadams on 2009-04-07
umm, i've only ever removed my control key on my xps m1330 and it was a bitch to put back on (the left control key) But probably no harder than any other key. The thing about our laptop keys is that they have a catch and a little rod that you slide in some holes so the rod has to go in and then it has to be pressed onto the catch. Some of the smaller or larger keys (f keys, or spacebar) might be slightly harder due to their size but i don't belive it would be too difficult

I would also advise removing from bottom up and replacing in whatever order seems fit.

EDIT: its actually not as bad as I make it out to be. Putting the first key back on will be the hardest, once you figure out how to get one on it will get easier from there.
