### Post by walterk46 on 2008-11-22
Yes, I installed the Dell Ubuntu image on a mini 9.  I just followed the instructions and it worked fine.  I had to install it as an image on a flash memory, don't burn it as a DVD.

I bought a refurb unit with Win XP and immediately flashed it to Ubuntu.  (quicker and cheaper than waiting for a mini 9 with the Ubuntu option).  The only hiccup I've had is that the SD card reader doesn't work with most cards, and Dell won't provide any support because it is not the original operating system.  Yes, I told them that it is a real Dell OS and is an original equipment option, but it just didn't matter.
