### Post by bobcollard on 2010-08-29
Getting a new battery is a good idea.  If, like me, you are using your computer mostly at home then let your battery drop to 50% and remove it while using your laptop as a desktop.  That is the recommended procedure.  Then charge it up to full when you intend to take it out again.  It is a good idea to run your battery down to almost (0) before charging it all the way up so it does not get a "Set" where it only holds less than 100%.
