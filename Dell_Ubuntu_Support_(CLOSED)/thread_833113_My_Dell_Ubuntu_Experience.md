### Post by B0SS on 2008-06-18
Hello Everyone,

8 Months ago after installing Ubuntu on my home server I found it so good that I decided to give it a try on my new laptop (XPS M1330). I first started by installing Ubuntu 7.10 since it was the newest version that time, the installation went fine and really started to like the new linux environment. The only 2 problems were; Compiz (problem regarding the X3100) and No sound over HDMI. I got a little mad but still! I used my laptop speakers as a sound output and used my LCD as video until Hardy came out.. In hardy I was supposed to get Video and Audio ( [http://ph.ubuntuforums.com/showthread.php?t=689390](http://ph.ubuntuforums.com/showthread.php?t=689390) ) but unfortunately I got none, I can see that nvidia users have sound and video but what about mine?

Other problems I had were with Hibernate and Shutdown, Once I almost burned my computer with the hibernate "Feature" it left the computer on in my laptop bag... Lately shutdown stopped working.... These are small things that NEED to be working at all costs.

I recognize all the work being done on this OS and BELIEVE ME when I tell you that I promoted and spread the word.

Yesterday I had enough and it marked my last day using Ubuntu... I will still continue using linux on my server and other laptops.

Please fix these issues, These are the kind of things that drive switchers off.

See you in 9.10!
Cheers,
Bernard Gatt.
