### Post by Darque1137 on 2011-03-19
As it turns out, zornp, a lot of people have had that problem with this computer.  I've got the XPS 15, too, and you can see some of what others have gone through with it here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1618801](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1618801)

It looks like some people are just turning it off to save battery power, although there isn't a consensus as to whether or not it's actually drawing less power.  I've got the GT540M in mine, and I get the same result with the "Additional Drivers" option: usually a tty terminal, but sometimes hanging on different parts of the boot.  I had to back the driver out altogether to get X to start.
