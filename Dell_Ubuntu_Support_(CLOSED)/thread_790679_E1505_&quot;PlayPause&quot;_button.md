### Post by Keyper7 on 2008-05-11
There is such a program, but I forgot its name. =P

Here's an alternative:

Go to System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts

Click on the "Play/Pause" line (a sub-item of "Sound") and press your play/pause button. If it's working, the application should get the key code and associate it to the "Play/Pause" command correctly. If nothing happens, the button is not working or it was not recognized. I would guess the first, since it doesn't make much sense to recognize the other multimedia buttons.
