### Post by jpl888 on 2010-03-02
Having more RAM is never a bad thing, but I can tell you I have a Dell Mini 10V with 1GB or RAM and it's plenty. 

If you were running a a few services on it you might want a bit more but TBH I have servers running with 1 to 1.5 GB or RAM and they work fine. The only time the OOMkiller ever comes out is if a Clamav scan goes wild (either direct scan or via HAVP).
