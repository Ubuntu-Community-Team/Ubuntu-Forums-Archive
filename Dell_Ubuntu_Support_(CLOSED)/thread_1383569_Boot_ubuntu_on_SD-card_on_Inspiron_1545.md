### Post by C.S.Cameron on 2010-01-17
You can give it a try using unetbootin, (for windows) or usb-creator located in system/Administration on the Live CD.

You could also try a full install but this can be a little dangerous on a laptop as it is best to disable the internal harddrive first. Your Win 7 bootloader can get messed up if you get things wrong.
