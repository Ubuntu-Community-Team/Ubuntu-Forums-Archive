### Post by snowpine on 2011-01-29
Relax, everyone here is a volunteer Ubuntu user like yourself, and it is good etiquette to wait 24 hours before bumping your own thread. While you are waiting, you might find the tutorials on these sites helpful:

[http://www.ubuntumini.com/](http://www.ubuntumini.com/)
[http://www.mydellmini.com/](http://www.mydellmini.com/)

Also don't forget the forum Search feature at the top right of the page. ;)

Personally, I have had very good luck with Linux on my Dell Mini 910, a similar (but slightly different) netbook model. The only real obstacle is the Broadcom wireless card, which requires temporary access to a wired ethernet connection to download the proprietary Broadcom driver. I assume the 1018 also has a Broadcom card, but Your Mileage May Vary. Good luck!
