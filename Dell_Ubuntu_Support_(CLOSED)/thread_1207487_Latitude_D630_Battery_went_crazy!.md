### Post by recluce on 2010-07-08
Yep, your battery is dead. Nothing to do but to buy a new one. You should consider to install the new battery only when you plan to use it. The batteries seem to die a lot faster if they stay in the docked computer all the time, especially if running 24/7.

Best keep your battery at 30% to 60% charge in a plastic bag with some silica gel in the fridge (above freezing temperature!) when not in use. This prolongs the shelf life quite a bit. 

Li-Ion batteries don't like being kept at full charge and high temperatures all the time - both conditions exist in a docked computer.
