### Post by bobcollard on 2010-01-20
> **cement_head said:**
> Can one install AMD64 on Dell Dimension 8400?
First question would be do you have a 64 bit bus?  Most recently built computers, especially with dual core CPUs do.  Then there is how much RAM do you have?  If it is under three Gigabytes you won't be able to take advantage of the number crunching abilities of  a system made for 64 bits. It's really a question of need for speed, with a large amount of work, graphics and big databases benefit.  Otherwise you will find a 32 bit system will suffice for everyday computing no matter what you have in your machine.  Hope this helps you understand a little better.
P.S.  AMD 64 and x86_64 Intel are the same thing in software systems.
