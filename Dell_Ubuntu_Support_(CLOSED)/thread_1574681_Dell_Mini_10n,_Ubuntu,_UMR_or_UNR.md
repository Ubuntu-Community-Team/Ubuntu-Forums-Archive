### Post by ac1dslayer on 2010-09-14
I've been running a dell mini 10v which is pretty close to your model for some time now.  I'm currently running 10.04 and it runs like a champ.  As far as "suspend, sleep and hibernate, and the Fn keys do not work with ubuntu installed." Everything works great for me.

As far as the dell netbooks are concerned get a 6 cell battery.  The 3 cell tends to last about 2 hours-ish.  As far as games don't expect to play too many graphic intensive games.. the emulators for older systems work great!

I've had my netbook for almost 2 years now and loving it.
