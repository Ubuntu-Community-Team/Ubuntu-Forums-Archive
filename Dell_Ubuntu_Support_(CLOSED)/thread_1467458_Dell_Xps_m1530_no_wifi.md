### Post by my_linux on 2010-05-02
You probably need Broadcom STA driver, see:

[http://www.broadcom.com/docs/linux_sta/README.txt](http://www.broadcom.com/docs/linux_sta/README.txt)

Assuming you have internet connection try System > Administration > Hardware Drivers in GNOME or the equivalent in other desktops. This should download the Broadcom driver.
