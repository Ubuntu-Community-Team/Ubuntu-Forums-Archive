### Post by jeff betts on 2008-11-20
I've just sent back my 2nd Inspiron 1525 Ubuntu. The first had a screen fault that couldn't have passed Dell QC unnoticed. The second, I got as far as the latest upgrades 2-6-21? and the soundcard was not found or present.

Its one thing to chase around after drivers when installing onto a Windows system but not one bought with Ubuntu pre-installed. Dell were completely unhelpful, claimed that they thought the fault had been rectified and couldn't fix it. I now have my money back.

If your thinking of buying a Dell with Ubuntu pre-installed get it in writing that it will work properly, or better still buy from someone else and make sure it doesn't have the SigmaTel 9205 soundcard in it.
