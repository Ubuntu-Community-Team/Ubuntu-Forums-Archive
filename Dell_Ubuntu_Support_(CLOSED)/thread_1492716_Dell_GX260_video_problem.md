### Post by Dr.FrankenMod on 2010-06-07
I think I have figured it out.
   	 	 	 	 	 	  

There is a "Server Version", "Desktop Version", Net-book Version, and a "Alternate Version".
 
 
 

 I first downloaded the “Alternate Version” from the direct download from Ubuntu, and had nothing but trouble with that version. Every 15 min. or so It would just crash and I had to shutdown my computer. It would be really bad when I was streaming music from Live365 or just cursing the web. I reloaded several times, and it would still be the same.
 

 I went back and downloaded the “Desktop Version”:  (ubuntu-10.04-desktop-i386.iso.torrent)
 via Bittorent  to my XP box.    	Burned it and loaded it up. 	 	 	 	   
 

 I loaded that version and so far I have not had any problems. That was 5 days ago, and the computer has been running without crashing. I have been streaming live365 when I am not home, and no problems yet.
