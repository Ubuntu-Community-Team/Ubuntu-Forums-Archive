### Post by jml on 2009-05-04
There is a good chance that Ubuntu 9.04 will include all of the drivers (open source and restricted,) that you need.  Can't be absolutely sure until you have the laptop in hand.  But 9.04 supports a lot of hardware so the odds are very good that it will work just fine.   In my opinion, Dell's support is not that strong.  They only "support" Ubuntu Linux on the few computers that they sell with it pre-installed.  Their after purchase support consists of a service contract with Cannonical, the company that introduced and supports Ubuntu.  To the best of my knowledge, they do not supply Linux compatible drivers for their other laptops.  Only Windows drivers.

Joe
