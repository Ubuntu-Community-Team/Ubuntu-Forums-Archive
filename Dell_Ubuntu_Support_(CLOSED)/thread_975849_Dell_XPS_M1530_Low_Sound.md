### Post by aw4lly on 2008-11-09
Hey I had the same problem when I installed Hardy for myself the first time on my XPS M1530.

If you double click on the speaker icon next to the clock there is a volume control on the Playback tab labeled "Front". If you turn that right up the rest of the volume will be increased.

If front isn't there go edit>preferences>front and it will come up.

I hope this helps
Aw4lly
