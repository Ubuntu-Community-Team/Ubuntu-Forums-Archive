The recovery disk can only bring you back to the state your system is in at the time you make it.  This could be a good thing if it is stable right now.  If you are having problems with an issue get it ironed out before making the disk.  I'm using a 1545 myself with the Mobile 4 chipset, so I don't know about your ATI graphics.  If it works, don't fix it.  The people who have the most problems are trying to make their system something it is not.
