### Post by armandh on 2009-03-02
by network controller do you mean on board network interface?
if so, first be sure it is turned on in the bios.
second try a plug in nic. 
I worked on a friends [Dell] computer last week that had a blown up on board nic.
The nic is a prime point for lightning damage.
if you try a "known good working" nic try several slots, if at first you don't succeed.
