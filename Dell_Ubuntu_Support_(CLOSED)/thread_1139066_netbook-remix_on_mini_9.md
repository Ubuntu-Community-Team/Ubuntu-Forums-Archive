### Post by Aearenda on 2009-04-26
To right-click and add, you need an empty space to right-click on - but there is no empty space on UNR. I usually work around this by moving the window picker to the right, so that there is a small space between the 'go home' applet and the window picker. It's easier to do this if you switch to a theme with a light-coloured panel temporarily.
