### Post by gbrainin on 2011-02-18
Right at the moment, it appears that Dell is only selling a desktop (XPS 7100) with Ubuntu pre-installed.  Over the years, various models have come and gone in the pre-installed area, so I'd hazard a guess that the models you found used to be sold that way.

That doesn't, of course, mean that one of their current laptops won't work fine with Ubuntu (though you do have the problem of paying for a useless second OS).  It just means that nobody is promising you that it will work.

If you must have a laptop with Ubuntu pre-installed, you can wait for Dell's offerings to change (yet again), you can buy a used laptop, or you can seek another source.  If you choose the latter, I strongly suggest you let Dell know, in a paper letter, that you would have bought something from them but for the fact that they don't offer it anymore.
