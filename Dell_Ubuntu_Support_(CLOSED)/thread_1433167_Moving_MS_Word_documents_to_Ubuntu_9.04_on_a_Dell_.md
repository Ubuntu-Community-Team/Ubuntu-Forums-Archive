### Post by Synthros on 2010-03-18
The .lnk file extension indicates a shortcut.  It sounds like the shortcuts for the documents may have been accidentally burned to the CD rather than the actual documents.  If still possible, you would need to find the actual location of your Word documents and burn those to CD.  Then you'd be able to open them up on the Ubuntu side.
