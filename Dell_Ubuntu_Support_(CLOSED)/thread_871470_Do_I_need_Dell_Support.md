### Post by gbrainin on 2008-07-27
Personally, I did not get the Canonical support, and have not had any issues with my 530n that I couldn't figure out myself with help either from this site or elsewehre on the internet.  The only exception was some faulty hardware, which the Dell linux hardware-only line dealt with promptly and efficiently.  (NOT the regular 800 number; they don't know what to do with you if you're not running Windows.)

It's hard for me to estimate the value of Canonical's support, since I did not buy it.  As for the accident protection, well, that depends on how accident-prone you (or your family) are.
