### Post by jher on 2010-04-06
I've got a Dell E6400 (Bios A20) with a Dell Dock PR03X (I've tried Rev A0 and A3).  Under Ubuntu 7.x, 8.x and 9.04, I did not have this problem.  When I upgraded (not clean install) I've been having artificial loading problems.

What I mean by this is that when I'm docked, processes that normally take half a second are taking minutes now.  The system load sits around 4-7.  I attempted to debug this by disconnecting various items from the docking station and eventually came across my current solution which is to remove the dock completely.  This is obviously inconvenient but it works.

Has anyone else had this problem?
