### Post by ellalan on 2008-09-29
Hi
You have to enable "Cube Reflection and Deformation" as well in ccsm,in addition to what you already have done. To get cube, deformation has to be set as "none"(see the Attachment).
To get transparent cube, Opacity has to be 0 under transparent cube tab(see the Attachment) HTH.
PS: Transparency opacity can be adjusted to your liking and not necessarily 0(You can have different transparency values for during rotation and not rotating.
