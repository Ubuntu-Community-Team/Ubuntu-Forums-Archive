### Post by jackhe22 on 2010-05-15
Ah, my good buddy, I had this exact problem on my old HP Tower and my friend had it on their Acer Laptop. It seems to be something to do with the BIOS not detecting a running System or something. Anyway, he fixed it by Disabling Auto-Shutdown in BIOS or something. Anyway, do have a look around the BIOS settings and see what you can find. Installing the Updates can sometimes also help.
