### Post by manishmotwani on 2009-04-27
I initially had the same problem. This is easy to fix:

Just go into System -> Preferences -> Sound. 

There, under the Device tab, under Default Mixer Tracks, the device you choose will be the one that is controlled by the volume controls on the laptop. I have a Dell Latitude D830 and I chose HDA Intel (Alsa mixer) and everything seems to work fine.
