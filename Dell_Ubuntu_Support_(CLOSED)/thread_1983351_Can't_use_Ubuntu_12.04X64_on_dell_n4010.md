Sounds like grub got trashed. There are a number of pages here on how to reinstall grub and get ubuntu up and running. Sorry, don't have the pages at hand right now. Search with Google or search here. I had a similar problem with my laptop after an update. You need the original installation disk for your version of Ubuntu, i.e., 12.04x64. You will need to boot from that disk and then copy grub from the disks to hard drive.
