### Post by tecky on 2009-10-25
The only issue i've ever had w/ ordering from dell is the Dell branded wifi cards, which are broadcom chipsets relabeled as "Dell 13xx". They sometimes can be a pain in the rear to get to work right away, though usually they have drivers already compiled into release kernels. I would recommend you try to seek out a Intel wifi card (5100 / 5300) if possible for the little extra it might cost you, it will save you headache's :)
