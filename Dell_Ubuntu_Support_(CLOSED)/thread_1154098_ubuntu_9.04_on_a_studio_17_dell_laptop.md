### Post by Murrquan on 2009-05-09
Hellos!

You can try Ubuntu without messing anything up, just by popping in the CD and restarting your laptop! You can even install it from the CD while Windows is running, and if you don't like it you can uninstall it from the Control Panel -- it won't be able to Suspend / Resume and it's limited to a 30 GB partition in this way, but it's a lot easier to undo than if you totally repartition your drive. ^.^;

Just try it out, and if something's not working come back here and we'll see if we can help! Have fun!
