### Post by Craig73 on 2008-07-04
I have the 700m and had the same issue.  It wasn't happening with all video - but I could more consistently cause it to occur by kicking off Apple Trailers in HighDef.

I worked around the issue by 
1) uninstalling Totem
2) uninstalling ffmpeg.
3) using a different player (have VLC and MPlayer installed)

This has improved it greatly, not 100%, so see if that works for you
