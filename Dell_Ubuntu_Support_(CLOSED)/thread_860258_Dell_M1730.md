### Post by sdennie on 2008-07-15
I've never setup SLI before and I would research to make sure it's going to work well.  The only other thing I can think of is that with 4GB of RAM, a regular 32bit install will not see all 4GB of RAM.  However, there are various ways to make Ubuntu see all 4GB: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=855511](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=855511).  Other than that, the wireless should work out of the box and the T9300 works fine on Ubuntu.
