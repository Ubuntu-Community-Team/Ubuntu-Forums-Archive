### Post by defishguy on 2008-08-02
Follow these instructions to get the fingerprint reader to work.  Remember to reboot before trying it.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ThinkFinger]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ThinkFinger")

The webcam worked for me out of the box.  You need to install 'cheese'.  You can do this by opening up the terminal and typing:

> sudo apt-get install cheese

Good luck
