### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-08-02
Problem #1: The cooling system.
You have to get it fixed, yourself or by sending it off to a lab.

Problem #2: Reallocated sectors.
Reallocated sectors mean physical damage or defect. Dropping a computer usually helps producing these. There is nothing you can do other then replace the hdd. Do you have to? Depends on how severe the damage is. I've had an hdd with some reallocated sectors for about 4 years, and it still works.
