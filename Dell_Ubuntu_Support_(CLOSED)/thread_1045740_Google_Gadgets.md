### Post by alexeix on 2009-01-20
I haven't done this on a Dell, but I don't see why it should be any different.

Go to the particular gadgets that you want to appear on the desktop, right click on them and select 'Undock From Sidebar'.

Now, at the top of the sidebar, press the Menu icon.  Select option 'Change Hotkey', press the button you want to activate your Gadgets (e.g. F4) and then click <Okay>.
Minimize the Sidebar.

Drag and drop the gadgets you undocked to the desired location, e.g. the right hand side of the screen.

When you press the Hotkey which you set (F4), your gadgets will now appear on top of whichever window you have open, for example Firefox.

There may be an easier way, but these instructions will do the trick.

Good luck!

### Post by alexeix on 2009-01-20
Note that this will enable you to call the Gadgets easily by pressing the Hotkey, whenever you want to view them, but they won't stay on top if you open a new application.

If you want them permanently on top, you will need to use the sidebar and select the 'always on top' setting.
