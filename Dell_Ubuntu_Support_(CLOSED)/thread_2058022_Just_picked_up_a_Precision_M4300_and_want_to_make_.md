### Post by katanacb on 2012-09-17
Depending on the hardware configuration, I'd say you could run just about anything you want to on there.

I have an "old" M6300, which is similar to the M4300 you have (I think the only difference was in screen size and RAM capacity).  This "old" M6300 runs circles around many newer machines today I have to admit, and Dell originally marketed the Precision M-series as mobile workstations -- read, lots of horsepower on the go.

So, depending on how much RAM you have, then I'd say go for it, and you should be just fine.  I ran 10.04 on the M6300 for a l-o-n-g time, then put 12.04.1 on there recently and it runs great.

HTH
