### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-03-01
Hi,
and welcome to the forum.

You are being asked to unlock the secure password storage utility (System->Preferences->Password and Encryption Keys) that stores passwords for wireless networks and such. It is a feature of the Gnome Desktop, intended to protect those passwords and keys from unauthorized access. It's not the same as the login password, although the password itself can be the same. ;)
As for the internet connection, go ahead and ask.
