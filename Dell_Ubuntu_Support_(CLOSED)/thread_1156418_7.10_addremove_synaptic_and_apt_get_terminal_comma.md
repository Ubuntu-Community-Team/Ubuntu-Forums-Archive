Hi

I had this exact problem - I wanted to upgrade to 8.04 (LTS) and beyond, but 7.10 went End of Life while I wasn't looking! (Alright I took my eye of the ball)...

I didn't want to do the upgrade because all the help/guidance said to check for updates to the existing Distribution 1st and I couldn't because the Repos had gone!

After all that there weren't any.  So I am going to upgrade this weekend to 8.04 through Update Manager. 

I'd do it now, but I need a flash drive to put the Dell Reinstall ISO on (too big for a DVD) in case everything goes bad....
