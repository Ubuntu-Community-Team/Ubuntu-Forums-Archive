### Post by lswartz on 2009-04-21
I can only answer the trash problem:(.

Click on the Ubuntu icon in the upper right of your screen & switch desktop mode to classic. You will now see a little trash can icon in the lower right of your screen. Right click on it & empty it.

Don't forget to switch your desktop back to the Dell Desktop if you want. I am sure there is a way to do this from the Dell Desktop - I just haven't found it yet.
