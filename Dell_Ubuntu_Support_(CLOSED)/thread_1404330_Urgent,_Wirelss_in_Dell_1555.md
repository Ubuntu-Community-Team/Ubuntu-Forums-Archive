In your Menu find System/Hardware Drivers and openthat.  It will show you two drivers the second is Broadcom STA, That is probably the one you need.  You will need a wired connection to the Internet to download it and install it.  After a reboot find the Internet Icon in your task bar and right click it.  Open Edit Connections and put your information in the Wireless after a click on New to get that menu.  OR go to this link and follow the instructions:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1368699](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1368699)
