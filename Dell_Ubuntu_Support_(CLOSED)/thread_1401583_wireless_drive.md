### Post by bobcollard on 2010-02-08
> **steve4608 said:**
> have a dell 600 latitude and the driver B43legacy is not working - can some send me the link to the driver BCM43xx  or another driver that will be recognized by broadcom so I can use my wifi
In your menu go to system/hardware drivers and open that.  The Broadcom drivers are proprietary to Dell and as such cannot be included in the System.  Choose the Broadcom STA driver and to install it you need a connection to the Internet.  There is another way to get it through your installation disk, see this link:
[http://www.superjoesoftware.com/articles/dell-1525-debian-wireless](http://www.superjoesoftware.com/articles/dell-1525-debian-wireless)
Hope this helps.
