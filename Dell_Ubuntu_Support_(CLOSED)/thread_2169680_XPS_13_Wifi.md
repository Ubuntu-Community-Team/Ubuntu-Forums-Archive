### Post by varunendra on 2013-08-24
As far as I know, Dell uses broadcom chips on their laptops, but I can't be sure. Broadcom chips are usually easier to troubleshoot and once fixed (usually a matter of a couple of easy steps), they perform nicely.

The fixes you mentioned are not applicable to all wifi chips/drivers. Better confirm which wifi chip you are going to get and then search the forums for issues, if any, pertaining to them.

You may find these links useful in your search :
Laptop Compatibility Thread : [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1543006](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1543006)
Supported Wifi cards : [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)
