### Post by notmatt on 2009-07-08
This weekend is the the second South Wales Linux User Group Technical Meeting will be held on Sunday 12th of July 2009 It'll start from 1730 with talks etc., starting at 1800 in Swansea Meeting house.  The meeting is an informal series of talks and discussions, with the possibility of extra activities such as installfests, advocacy, keysigning and maybe some gaming. 

**Confirmed Talks:**

Matt Moore - Fun with Text Editors

Neil Jones - Perl and web spidering

Mark Summerfield - Introduction to Unicode

Alan Cox - Everything you never wanted to know about caching and memory,and how to make your apps run faster

The directions can be found here - [http://www.swanseaquakers.org/findus.htm](http://www.swanseaquakers.org/findus.htm).  Obviously all are welcome to come along.  It costs £4 for waged and £2 for unwaged (this is purely to cover room hire costs).
