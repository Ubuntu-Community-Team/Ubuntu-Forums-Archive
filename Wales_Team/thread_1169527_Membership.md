### Post by ianto` on 2009-05-25
As discussed on [Sunday 24 May's meeting]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WelshTeam/Meetings/24052009") we now have new for of membership. The idea was a combination of how the Catalan LoCo managers their members and how the official Ubuntu membership is managed. There are two forms of membership now available. Firstly is launchpad membership which is open to anyone who wishes to join at [https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-cym](https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-cym) which grants privileges such as voting and participation in how the team is run. There will also be an official approval form of membership where the applicant needs to show some contribution to OSS and/or Ubuntu communities. This can be anything ranging from forums assistance, mailing list contributions or even assistance in IRC. What will you gain from this other form of membership? Well it is still in draft form however you will be granted an @ubuntu-cym.org email address and the option of having your blog aggregated to the LoCo's planet [http://98.142.208.108/planet/](http://98.142.208.108/planet/) . To apply for the approval form of membership, please contact the team's current point of contact or ask in our IRC channel for more information [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WelshTeam/IRC](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WelshTeam/IRC) .
