### Post by ianto` on 2009-06-05
For members who request it, we now have the option of being able to give users an @ubuntu-cym.org email address and having their blog aggregated to planet.ubuntu-cym.org. Simply all that you need to do is ask for it in this thread stating your blog's feed URL & your preferred username for email. To be permitted to have these benefits all that is required is that you apply in this thread and have been a members of the launchpad group for one month or more and that you have helped with Ubuntu Cymru, Ubuntu itself or some other OSS project in some way that can be verified. Any from documentation to programming is considered and very rarely will you get turned down so go ahead and apply for it!

Please bear in mind that email is currently unavailable, you will be contact when the mail server has been finalised.
