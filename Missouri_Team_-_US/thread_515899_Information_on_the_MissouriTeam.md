### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-08-02
A fond hello to everyone in Missouri.  Welcome to the Missouri LoCo Team Forum!  We are a young and aspiring LoCo striving to offer the best we can in support, education, installation, and deployment around the state of Missouri.

*Ubuntu-Missouri Loco Team - The Show-Me LoCo!*

**Who is the Missouri LoCo?**
We are a group of Ubuntu Linux users and enthusiasts from all walks of life. We reside in Local Communities in the State of Missouri. Our team goals are to help educate and increase the awareness and use of Ubuntu Linux and Open Source in the State of Missouri. We will strive to educate ourselves in these fields so that we may better assist those who show an interest in our knowledge. Our Community Projects will be geared so that we return to the Community more than we receive, and we will always endeavor the live up the Ubuntu Code of Conduct.

**What is a LoCo Team?**
*'A LoCo Team is a Local Community of (in our case) Ubuntu users. A LoCo can involve a lot of things such as local promoting, support in the local language, general support to local users and much more. Most importantly however, it lets people find other Ubuntu users near them.'* (Ubuntu Wiki [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LoCoFAQ](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LoCoFAQ))

If you live in Missouri, and strive to adhere to the Ubuntu Code of Conduct, WE WANT YOU!
