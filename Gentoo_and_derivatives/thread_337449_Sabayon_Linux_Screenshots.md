### Post by Shay Stephens on 2007-01-16
Beautiful.  I did some searching and also found other related in style backgrounds.  So thank you for the name :-)

I got this in a PM:

> i saw when you had your cube rotating you had a background. how can i do that. i'm sorry to bother you but i don't know where to look for this on the forum. i've tried and no luck. i'm new to this and need some help. thanks

I was able to specify the background using the program beryl-manager, which places a red gem icon in the system tray. Click once on that and it will give you a menu. Select the "Beryl Settings Manager".

Then click the down arrow for "desktop", select "desktop cube", click on the "Skydome" tab, and change the "skydome image" to whatever background image you want visible.
