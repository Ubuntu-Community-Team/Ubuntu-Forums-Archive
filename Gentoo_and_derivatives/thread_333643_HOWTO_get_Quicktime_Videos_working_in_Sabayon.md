### Post by RAV TUX on 2007-01-07
Written for Sabayon 3.25

Open your Konsole:

> su*password*

> emerge mplayerplug-in then since the nspluginwrapper is masked you have to unmask it:

> echo "net-www/nspluginwrapper-0.9.91.2 -* ~x86" >> /etc/portage/package.keywordsThen enjoy.


(Example Image: Curse of the Golden Flower [Man cheng jin dai huang jin jia] Trailer)
