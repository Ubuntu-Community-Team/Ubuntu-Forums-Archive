### Post by trimeta on 2008-10-03
Grab a copy of the minimal CD (not the graphical LiveCD), boot from it, and see if you have working internet (through your ethernet). If so, check [this section](http://www.gentoo.org/doc/en/handbook/handbook-amd64.xml?part=1&chap=8#doc_chap2) of the [Install Handbook](http://www.gentoo.org/doc/en/handbook/handbook-amd64.xml) for how to configure your system identically to the minimal CD. If you don't have working ethernet automatically from the minimal CD, try [this section](http://www.gentoo.org/doc/en/handbook/handbook-amd64.xml?part=1&chap=3) for tips on how to get it.
