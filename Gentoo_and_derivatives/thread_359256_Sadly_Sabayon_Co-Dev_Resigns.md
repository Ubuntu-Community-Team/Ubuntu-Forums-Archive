> Resignation                                                                                                                                                  Written by Christopher                                                                                                                                        Sunday, 11 February 2007                                                                                Hello all,
 I have decided that I need to resign as SabayonLinux's Co-lead position and move on.  I would like to focus more on my studies and graduate school but I hope to be active in the linux community.  It has been a pleasure working with everybody and I encourage you all to support a project that is well worth it in the linux community.
As such, today I officially resign.  If you would like to get in touch with me my email is [EMAIL="cvillareal@gmail.com"]cvillareal@gmail.com[/EMAIL]        and jabber is  [EMAIL="cvillareal@jabber.org"]cvillareal@jabber.org[/EMAIL].    My nick will still be cvill64 where ever I go, so I hope to see you all on occassion.
 Thank you for all for understanding and I wish the best to the SabayonLinux project in the future.
 
NOTE: I hope the transition goes as smooth as possible, but if there are slight delays, please be patient and all will be well :)   Hopefully, there will be no FUD involved with this.
 Christopher Villareal
