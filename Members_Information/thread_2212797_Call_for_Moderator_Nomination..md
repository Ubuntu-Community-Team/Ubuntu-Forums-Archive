Call for Moderator Nomination. 

Following on from the [Moderator Nomination and Voting]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2208857") thread, the Forum Council would like to begin the process of adding to the moderation team.
 
So, it is now over to you (all Ubuntu Forums Members) to make your nominations, this can either be yourself, or someone you feel would be a good addition to the team (please discuss with nominee beforehand). **_Nominations should be made as specified in the wiki, that is direct to the [Forum Council]("mailto:ubuntu-forums-council@lists.ubuntu.com"). _**

Closing date for nominations is 06th of April 2014.

Anyone needing a reminder of the how the process is intended to work can find it [here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ForumCouncil/TeamNomination").

Thanks

The Forum Council.
