### Post by Mustard on 2005-12-16
Its definitely possible to install and keep your /home using manual partitioning.

You are going to have to prepare yourself for extended lengths of time without a functioning operating system.  It can take days of waiting before things get fixed as it goes through development.  Ideally you would install on a seperate hard drive and run one unstable OS and one stable one.  You could even try repartitioning your hard drive and use one partition for dapper and another for breezy.  It would depend on how much hard drive space you have got to work with.
