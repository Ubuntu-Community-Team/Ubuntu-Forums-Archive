### Post by nalmeth on 2006-02-19
What makes dsl fast, is that its only 50 megabytes, but mostly because it uses the fluxbox window manager. 

You can setup your computer to be as fast or as slow as you want. Your specs seem fine enough. I use gnome (the default ubuntu window manager) on a pentium 166 with 96 megs of ram, and it runs fast. If you really want it to be quick, Xfce runs fast, and is a little heavier than fluxbox, but with lots of good features.
