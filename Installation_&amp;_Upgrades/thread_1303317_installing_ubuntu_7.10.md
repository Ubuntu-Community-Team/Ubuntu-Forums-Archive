### Post by mikechant on 2009-10-28
Really there are only three versions you should consider installing at present:
8.04 Is the current long term support version and will be supported until April 2011 (server version - April 2013).
9.04 Is the current version and will be supported for another year. Possibly more stable than 9.10 since it's been in general use for 6 months.
9.10 Will be the new 'current version' and is officially released tomorrow, and will be supported for 18 months.

All other desktop versions are now either unsupported or only have a very short support life (6 months or less). The only reason for installing any other version would be if your hardware did not work with any of the three versions listed above.
