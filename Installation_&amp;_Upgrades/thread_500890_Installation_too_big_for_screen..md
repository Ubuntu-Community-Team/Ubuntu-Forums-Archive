### Post by netimen on 2007-08-01
I had the same problem - installation started at 800x600 with invisible buttons. Also when I tried to enable NVIDIA's video drivers I was asked to reboot, which of course was useless - the drivers would be reset to default. 

So, I rejected rebooting and went to **System->Preferences->Fonts** where I set *Application font* size to *7*, and *Window title font size* to *8*. Finally I enabled *auto-hiding* on the bottom panel, and then I was able to see the upper halves of the buttons.
