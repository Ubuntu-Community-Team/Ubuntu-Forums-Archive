### Post by howefield on 2016-05-20
Booting from a Live DVD/USB will give you a rough idea of how it would perform when installed, but a machine that old probably also has a pretty old graphics card which would likely be the biggest bottleneck to acceptable performance. Your best bet might be to try Lubuntu or perhaps Xubuntu.
