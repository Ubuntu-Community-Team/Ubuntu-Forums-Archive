### Post by MAFoElffen on 2016-01-04
No. 14.04.03 is an LTS, so will not prompt you for a distribution upgrade until this March, after the next LTS is released. That is what is recommended to most, as it is the most stable and a long-term, less-bumps-expected upgrade path. You could always override that, but not the norm.
