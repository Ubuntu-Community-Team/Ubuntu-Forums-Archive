To obtain the options that would allow you to change the OpenOffice interface to Spanish, you need to install the right packages.  You can search Synaptic for "Openoffice spanish" and apply the ones that look right.  

Alternatively, you can look in the menus at the top of the screen and navigate your way to *System > Administration > Language Support*.  Look through the scroll list, check Spanish (which, if I remember clearly, is not even there for some reason (odd because you can get obscure *languages* like Lojban, Esperanto and Old French))) and click apply.
