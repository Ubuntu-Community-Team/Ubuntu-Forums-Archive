### Post by LiquidSam on 2008-01-08
With nothing installed, I would be looking on your build. Make sure all your peripherals are working. Does the machine issue any POST warnings? i.e. beeps more than once or beeps at all. 

After ruling that out, I would take all unnecessary parts out and add them back into the system one by one to troubleshoot. Your best friend at this first would be the beeps. Consult your Mobo guide to see what the POST warnings are.

 If it doesn't beep check all your connections to the Mobo and make sure they are connected right. I've had a couple problems on machines like this because I forgot to check all my connections. I've even forgotten to make sure the Mobo speaker was installed and thought the worst (power supply dead) because I couldn't hear the beeps for a bad memory chip. Haha

Let me know if that helps :-)
