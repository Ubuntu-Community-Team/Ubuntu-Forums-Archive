### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-09-01
I think that the emt64 architecture is only a clumsy way of emulating 64-bit on a 32-bit chip and that it won't really support a 64-bit kernel.  I could be wrong, and at the risk of wasting a blank CD, you can just burn a 64-bit Ubuntu image to find out, but I think that you'll have to use 32-bit given your processor.  Beyond that, as the poster above says, any version of *buntu should be fine.
