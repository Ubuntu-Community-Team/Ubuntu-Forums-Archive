### Post by louieb on 2007-07-22
I have had the hanging problem. I have found that some things just take longer that others. Did you give it 10 - 15 minutes? 
The other thing that I believe causes the installation to hang is a bad CD burn. It just takes a few scrambled bits in the wrong place. Did you run the media check on the CD? 
I've had installs hang even with a CD that passes the media check. Now I just burn another CD. I burn it slow 2x or 4x. If you gave the installer plenty of time, its my guess that you have to burn anther CD.
