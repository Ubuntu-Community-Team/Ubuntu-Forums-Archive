### Post by aheckler on 2009-04-02
Due to differences in the way drive manufacturers and operating systems calculate exactly how much memory is in 1 GB, that difference is normal. For example, I have an 80 GB drive, but Disk Usage Analyzer says I only have 72.4 GB total. There's nothing to worry about.

EDIT: For more info, see [this link]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gigabyte#Consumer_confusion").
