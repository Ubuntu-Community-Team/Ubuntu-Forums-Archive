### Post by ZaphodFJ on 2011-05-08
Problem fixed by following the advice here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1639198](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1639198)

and Solution #2.

In brief - the Ubuntu upgrade process tried to upgrade Grub, which turns out to be a bad idea within Wubi.  The solution is to install Lilo.
