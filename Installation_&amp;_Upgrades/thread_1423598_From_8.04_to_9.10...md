### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-03-06
To upgrade go to System>Administration>Software Sources and click on the Updates tab. When you do that, select Normal Releases from the Release subcategory.

After this is done just open Update Manager and it will find the 9.10 files and just click update. This will update your computer to 9.10. 

I don't really know how long it will take for you but with my laptop (upgrade from 9.04 to 9.10) connected to DSL at 768 KBps and with my single core CPU it took me about 2 hours for the whole process to finish.
