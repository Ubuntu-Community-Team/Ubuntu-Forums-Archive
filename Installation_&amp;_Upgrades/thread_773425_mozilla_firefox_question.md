### Post by hariprs on 2008-04-29
In windows by default your thunderbird local mail folders will be stored under C:\Documents and Settings\<username>\Application Data\Thunderbird\Profiles\<some profile name>\Mail\

Just copy those folders to mail folder in ".thunderbird" in your home dir in ubuntu.
