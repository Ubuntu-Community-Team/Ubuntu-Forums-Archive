### Post by Mr. Shannon on 2011-07-23
You need to boot to the Ubuntu Classic desktop session and install the proprietary drivers for you video card by going to **System->Administration->Additional Drivers**.  Then choose a video card driver and click activate.  After it finishes installing the driver it will ask you to restart the computer.  When you boot back up choose Ubuntu as your desktop session from the bar at the bottom (this is after you click your name but before you confirm).  Now you should be in the Unity desktop environment.
