### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-09-14
Check the MD5SUM of the downloaded images.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

If the CD has got no defects and the MD5SUMs are correct, you possibly need the alternate installer. It surely will do the job for you.

[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors#alternate](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors#alternate)
