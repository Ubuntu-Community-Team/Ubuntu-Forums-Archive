### Post by aysiu on 2006-09-18
It is very possible to get lost in the shuffle--there are many, many posts, and people are all volunteers here (i.e., other users, like yourself).

Entertain the possibility that you could have posted at an awkward time when North America, Europe, and Australia are not heavily online.

Also consider that most people here are not pros who have the answer to every problem. It's entirely possible that no one knows how to solve whatever your problem is.

Rather than create a new thread asking people to help you on another threads, it's acceptable and even encouraged for you to "bump" your old thread after 24 hours so that it'll rise to the top of the "new threads" list and someone who may have missed it before can see it again.
