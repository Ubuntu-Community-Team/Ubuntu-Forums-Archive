### Post by Bob D. on 2005-03-25
Try the SystemRescueCD ([http://www.sysresccd.org/)](http://www.sysresccd.org/)). It is a bootable CD that contains a copy of Partimage,  a Ghost clone for Linux.

If you can make a DOS floppy, Image for DOS ([http://www.terabyteunlimited.com/image.html](http://www.terabyteunlimited.com/image.html)) works quite well also. While a trial version, it is fully functional. At least the version I have now is...I don' t have this most recent update but I'd assume it is the same. The IFD images on the trial version are only valid for 15 or 30 days (can't remember), but it will be good enough for your project.
