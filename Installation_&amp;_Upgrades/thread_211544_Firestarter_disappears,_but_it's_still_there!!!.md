Just an FYI--

Prior to upgrading to 6.0.6 I had Firestarter installed and it showed up in one of the Application menus in my upper task bar and also as a icon in the right side of the upper task bar.  After updating to 6.0.6 those indications disappeared.

I then spent WAY too many hours trying to re-integrate a wi-fi device (a Squeezebox) with a new router, a Linksys WRT54GL.  Turns out that Firestarter was still installed and preventing the Squeezebox from seeing the slimserver and viceversa.

Other than that everything was very easy install and works great.
