Just wanted to say that the upgrade was from Intrepid to Jaunty was seamless for me. I was affected by something in a package update in Intrepid which meant that I lost gnome-panel and got some other glitches.

General feel - excellent.

Big thanks to everyone who works at Ubuntu and upstream at Gnome. Love this OS and this distribution.
