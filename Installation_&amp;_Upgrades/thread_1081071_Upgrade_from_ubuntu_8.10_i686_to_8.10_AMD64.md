Dear grimanda,

Although you cpu may be an AMD64, that doesn't necessary mean you need to use Ubuntu 64bit. There's not so much benefit to be gained performance-wise moving to 64 for now, only many issues and extra things to setup as many apps are not 64bit. Search the boards here and elsewere on the net and you will probably come to the same conclusion.

So unless you have +4gb main memory I advise you to save yourself a lot of trouble and stay with your current i686 install. 

I have an AMD64 myself and also use 32bit (i686) mainstream Ubuntu to my satisfaction.

Best wishes,

Wiebeest
