### Post by thathatman on 2006-10-19
After upgrading to the 2.6.15-27-686 SMP kernel, my USB printer stopped working. I got this message:

```
drivers/usb/class/usblp.c: usblp0: nonzero read/write bulk status received: -71
```

It would load a page, but the page would sit there and nothing would ever print. ](*,) 

I tried just about everything -cups this, foomatic that... nothing worked. After pulling my hair out for a while, I switched the printer from my USB hub and put it directly to my computer. *Problem solved!*

Not a profound solution, but a solution nonetheless.
