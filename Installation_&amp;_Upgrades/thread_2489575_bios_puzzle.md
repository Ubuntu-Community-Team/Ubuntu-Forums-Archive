### Post by Xian on 2023-08-03
For starters the SGX message isn't an issue. 
It's just letting you know that SGX is not in use,

To address:

If the setting exists -- then just enable the "Intel Software Guard Extension" in your BIOS
If not you might look at installing this application: [https://github.com/intel/sgx-software-enable](https://github.com/intel/sgx-software-enable)
