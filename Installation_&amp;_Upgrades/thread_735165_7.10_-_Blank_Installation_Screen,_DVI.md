### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-03-25
Yes, the LiveCD does not work for everybody, most often because of video card issues.  You should try installing with the Alternate cd.  It is a text based installer, but very intuitive and easy to use.  You can download it at [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download) by checking the box at the bottom for Alternate cd.
Once you have installed you will be able to setup the restricted nvidia drivers easily.
