### Post by imronak on 2008-07-31
Yes, there is this issue with skype and/or other applications.

My movie player stops/hangs badly.

What you can do is, after you use gxine, go to System->Prefernces->Sound, then check what device is selected. In my case, after I use skype, it goes to USB headset, which i disconnect after the use.

So check, if gxine is messing with that sound device properties.
