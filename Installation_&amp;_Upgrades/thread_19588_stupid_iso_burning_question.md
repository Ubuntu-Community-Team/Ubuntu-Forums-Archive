I don't think there'll be any compatibility problems. An ISO is (if I understand it correctly) a bit for bit record of what will actually be on the physical disc. SO, it shouldn't matter which platform you're burning it on, the same ISO should yield (ideally) the same bits on the disc.

Make sure to double check the MD5 sum of the image you downloaded, to ensure that nothing got scrambled in transit to you, and also burn the disc at 1/3rd or less of your drive's max burn speed, just to be extra sure. :)

Good Luck!!
