### Post by dstew on 2008-05-01
Try reconfiguring your keyboard. Boot into Recovery Mode, and on the command line enter```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```That gets you a text-based reconfiguration program that asks questions about your keyboard, mouse and display. After you are done reconfiguring, enter **startx** and see if it is better.
