### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-10-27
Unfortunately, in all these years, Canonical STILL has not seen fit to implement any kind of system-level Restore capability.  You have to manually backup the system yourself, do the system upgrade, and then, if something goes terribly wrong such that you need your earlier system back, manually restore it yourself.  That's not terribly hard to do, but to find out about it AFTER your system is hosed is very disappointing.
