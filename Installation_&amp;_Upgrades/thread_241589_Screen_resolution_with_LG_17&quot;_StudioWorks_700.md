### Post by avtolle on 2006-08-22
I don't have nvidia graphics card; however, be aware that the update to 6.06 brought a new kernel, and, if you manually installed vid drivers on 5.10, you will need to manually install the drivers to 6.06. Of course, there might be something more simple here, but this crossed my mind, and thought I'd pass it on.
EDIT: in looking at the three xorg.conf files you posted, I see that the one resulting from the dpkg-reconfigure changed your driver from vesa to nv. This may be helpful to a reader more knowledgeable than I in these matters.
