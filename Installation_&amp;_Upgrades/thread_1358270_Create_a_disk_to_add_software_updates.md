### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-12-18
Yes, apt-on-cd is one way. On the other hand, if you reinstall a lot, use Partimage or Clonezilla to keep an image of the entire installation. That way, you'll never have to reinstall again, just restore the image, and the system is always uptodate.
