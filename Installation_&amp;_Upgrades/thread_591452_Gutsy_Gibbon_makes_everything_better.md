### Post by MindFork on 2007-10-25
I was reasonably pleased with FF, but GG fixed my printing to a windows machine, fixed firefox crashing problems and is a LOT smoother on the desktop with compiz than beryl on FF ever was.

My only gripe is that I had to dig around to find out that I needed to install the compizconfig settings manager in order to get the desktop cube.  That should just be installed by default, because everybody loves the cube! :)  

I had to keep my old xorg.conf for my dual monitor setup, but I expected that.  

Overall, I am VERY pleased with the upgrade, and for anybody who is on the fence, JUST DO IT (after backing up your files, of course)

Enjoy.
