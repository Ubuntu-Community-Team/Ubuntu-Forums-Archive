### Post by reacocard on 2006-07-16
if you're just looking to create more space for data, just create a new partition on your main drive, and turn it into a /home partiton. that will leave ubuntu and its programs on the second drive, but put your data on the main drive. feel free to email/IM me if you want more information on how to do this.
