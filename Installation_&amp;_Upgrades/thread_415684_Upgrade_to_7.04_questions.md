### Post by kpkeerthi on 2007-04-20
1) How much free space i need?
>> Depends on how much your Edgy installation is. See my answer for the next question.

2) How large is upgrade download size?
>> Generally around 500 MB. The upgrade process upgrades all the packages you currently have in Edgy. So it depends on how much extra installation you did on top of the base Edgy install. If you have installed 300 MB of extra debs on top of base Edgy, you are likely to download more during the upgrade process. There is no definite number. 

3) Is there some bugs or something like that?
>> It all depends on your hardware and current edgy setup. Did you ever compile programs from source and have them installed? Are you currently using other 'unofficial' repos? Chances are your upgrade will fail. Again, there is no definite answer.
