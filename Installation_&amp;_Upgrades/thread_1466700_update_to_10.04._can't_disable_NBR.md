### Post by pauljwells on 2010-04-30
It's really easy! :)
At the login screen, once you've clicked on your userid and before you enter your password you get a set of options at the bottom of the screen like language and accessibility. One of the options lets you choose between UNE, Gnome, failsafe Gnome etc, just select Gnome and your good to go. The choice will stick too so you only have to do this the once.

Personally I rather like the UNE, but on my netbook!
