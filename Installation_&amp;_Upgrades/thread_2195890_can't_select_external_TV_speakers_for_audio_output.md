### Post by efflandt on 2013-12-27
HDMI would handle video and audio, but VGA only handles video. So you would need a cord going from the headphone jack of the laptop to whatever audio input the TV has for VGA (PC). Depending upon the TV that might either be a cable with 3.5 mm stereo plugs on both ends or a Y-cable with 3.5 mm stereo plug on one end and red/white RCA jacks on other end. If you already have a separate audio cable I am not sure what you would need to do if sound does not automatically go to the headphone jack when something is plugged into that.
