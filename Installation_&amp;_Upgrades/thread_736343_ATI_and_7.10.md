### Post by SGAZ on 2008-03-28
7.10 does not recognize the HD3850 as a valid ATI restricted driver candidate. 
I am using the same card and I installed the ATI Proprietary Drivers which are currently version 8.3.  
I just did this a couple of days ago and all is well.

Just to be clear the HD3850 will work with the default driver in 7.10 just fine.  You will just be in the Basic Graphics settings until you install the ATI Proprietary Drivers.
