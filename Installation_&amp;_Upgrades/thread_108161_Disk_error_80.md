### Post by Sef on 2005-12-26
Most likely you have one of two problems:

1) Your BIOS is bad

2) Your disk is bad.


Do you have another computer you could install the disk on?  If the install goes well, then I would suspect your BIOS; if it doesn't, then I would suspect your CD has some corrupt drivers on it.
