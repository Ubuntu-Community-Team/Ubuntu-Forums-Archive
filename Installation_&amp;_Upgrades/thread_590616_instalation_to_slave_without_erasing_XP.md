### Post by gtdaqua on 2007-10-25
Just install ubuntu on top of XP - Ubuntu's boot loader will automatically find other XP/Win2000 Partitions and give you an option of loading the OS of your choice at every boot.
go [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=541561&page=2") and read my post. 

I have Kubuntu 32bit installed on my home desktop alongside XP - both in diff partitions of course.

ADD: Ubuntu automatically discovers bootable OS on other HDDs too! I have win 2000 server on a separate HDD and Kubuntu automatically added it to the boot choice list. Something that is very cool indeed!
