### Post by whiskey212 on 2008-04-14
Actually your information is not totally correct. I happen to be a technician with Future Shop and I can say with total certainty that installing Linux on your laptop, or desktop should someone desire, will not void the warranty, assuming you are referring to a Future Shop product service plan. The PSP covers hardware issues ONLY and nothing software related. For example: should your fan malfunction or the webcam stop working those would be covered, but if you get a virus or delete some necessary drivers those are not covered. With this in mind, think of Linux as another piece of software and should you install something that crashed your system it would be on you to fix it or have it fixed, none of which voids the warranty on the hardware. This being said, I would highly recommend keeping Vista at least on a partition if for no other reason than to preserve a higher resale value when you decide to move on to another computer.
