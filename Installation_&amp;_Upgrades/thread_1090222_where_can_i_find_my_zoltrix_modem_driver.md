### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-03-08
If you go into the Networking and Wireless forum, and do some searched on NDISWrapper, you'll see posts about installing that which will allow you to use MS Windows drivers.  You can't use MS Windows drivers natively in Linux, and if your modem didn't come with Linux drivers (and the Zoltrix website) doesn't have them, you're out of luck.
