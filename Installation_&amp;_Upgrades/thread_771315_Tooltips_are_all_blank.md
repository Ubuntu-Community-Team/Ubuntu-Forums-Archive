### Post by akrevolt on 2008-04-28
Had this problem too. The fix is super easy.

sytstem -> preferences -> appearance


then click the customize button on your chosen theme.

then go to colors and change tooltips colours.

(I noticed it was saved as black text on black background, just make them whatever you please and the problem is solved!)
