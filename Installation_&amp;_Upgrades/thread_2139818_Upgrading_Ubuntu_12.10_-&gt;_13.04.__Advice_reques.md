### Post by bro on 2013-04-28
I have had plenty of successful upgrades. 12.10 gave me plenty of errors and was not a good release for me. I upgraded successfully. No grand new features but overall a better release. 

I am not sure if upgrading through a dvd is an option. You could try to enable it as a source in software sources, but I have no clue how that works out.
