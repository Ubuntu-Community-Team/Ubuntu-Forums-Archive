### Post by julianbury on 2010-07-02
Problem solved.

After much frustration and re-arranging and experimentation, the problem turned out to be the 30" monitor.

Because the Ubuntu installation didn't come with a driver for my graphics card, it was not able to present the installation options.

I dug up an old and tiny Packard Bell LCD monitor and was then able to complete the installation, including the latest graphics card driver.

Good night   :-)
