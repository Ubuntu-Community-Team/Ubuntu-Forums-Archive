### Post by aysiu on 2006-11-13
Settings should stay if you upgrade, but you can back them up in several ways, actually.

Basically, they all live in your /home/gironja directory, so if you back up that directory, all your settings will be backed up, too.

My advice, though, would be to back up *everything*. Some people have had issues with an Edgy upgrade and asked, "Is there any way to go back to Dapper?" So an easy restore to Dapper is a good thing to have.

Here's one way to do it:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=81311](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=81311)

Here's another way:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partimage](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partimage)
