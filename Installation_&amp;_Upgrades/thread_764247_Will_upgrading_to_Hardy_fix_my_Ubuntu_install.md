### Post by twin_57103 on 2008-04-23
You might want to do some hard drive diagnostics while you're at it...I probably have hard drive failure on the brain since I've just had it happen (destroyed my Ubuntu installation), but it's worth checking for safety's sake.

There are a couple utilities you can use - [Speed Fan]("http://www.almico.com/speedfan.php") for Windows and smartmontools for Ubuntu (it's in the repos).  Otherwise, the hard drive manufacturer may have a bootable diagnostics program that you could use.

I'm probably being paranoid, but it's worth checking.  After living through 2 hard drive failures, I figure I'm entitled to a little paranoia :)
