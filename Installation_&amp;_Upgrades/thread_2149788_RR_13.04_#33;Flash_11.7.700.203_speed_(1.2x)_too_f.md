### Post by klyshas on 2013-05-31
I also have this problem with chrome and flash 11.7.700.203 . I couln't find any solution. Chromium and Firefox play the videos with normal speed as it uses the 11.2.202.197 flash.

edit:
It seems I found a solution - as described here: [https://code.google.com/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=229918#c31]("https://code.google.com/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=229918#c31")

create/edit: ~/.pulse/daemon.conf and insert/edit the line 
default-sample-rate = 48000

pulseaudio -k  didn't help. BUT after a restart the flash videos don't play too fast anymore.
Hope it helps.
