### Post by matthew on 2006-11-07
I can't speak authoritatively on this, but my understanding is that each release (eg. Breezy, Dapper, Edgy...) gets the most recent and stable edition of the driver possible at some point in the development cycle. When the final release is made that is the version of the video driver that will be in the repos...there won't be an update. To get the latest and greatest you either have to install them yourself or upgrade your installation to the latest Ubuntu version.
