### Post by diesch on 2010-06-22
Ubuntu supports quite a lot of printers out of the box. For some others you need to install PPD files or drivers first, and some just don't work.

Have look at the [OpenPrinting database](http://www.linuxfoundation.org/collaborate/workgroups/openprinting/database/databaseintro) to see if your printers are supported or tell us what printers you have so we may be able to help you.
