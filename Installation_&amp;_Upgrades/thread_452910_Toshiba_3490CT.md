### Post by Craigus on 2007-05-26
The easiest method with the 3490CT, if you have the port replicator is to do a PXE network install.

However, since you do not have the replicator, I think the easiest solution may be simply to remove the hard disk and connect it a another laptop or desktop computer (using an adaptor) and install ubuntu on the hd that way.

What PCMCIA CD drive do you have?
