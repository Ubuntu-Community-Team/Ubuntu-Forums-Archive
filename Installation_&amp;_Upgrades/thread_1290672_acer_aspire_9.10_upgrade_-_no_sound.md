### Post by MiloszK on 2009-11-01
If anyone is still having trouble with missing sound or unresponsive touchpad or anything else driver-related, you need to update your /boot/grub/menu.lst so that it loads the latest kernel.

Full story [_here_]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1308857") and [_here_]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1306012").
