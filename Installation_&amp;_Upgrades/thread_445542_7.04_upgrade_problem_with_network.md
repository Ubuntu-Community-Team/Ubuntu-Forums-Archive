Hi muka,

I also had similar gzip errors.
You can try to change your /etc/apt/source.list to some mirror site closer to you so that you can download the package more reliably.
I have written down my upgrade process at my blog. Perhaps you can have a look at it [http://sonofusion82.blogspot.com/2007/05/journey-to-kubuntu-704-feisty-fawn.html]("http://sonofusion82.blogspot.com/2007/05/journey-to-kubuntu-704-feisty-fawn.html")
