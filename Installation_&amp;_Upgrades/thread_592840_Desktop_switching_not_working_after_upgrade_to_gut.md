### Post by sr4794 on 2007-11-18
Snap. I too have this problem. 

After switching desktops (and losing all sight of everything) Alt-F2 brings up the run program box and entering 'killall gnome-panel' seems to temporarily fix the problem. Although I have to do this every time I log in. I am still searching for a more long term fix... until then I just don't bother switching desktops!
