### Post by dstew on 2008-03-26
> I can't for the life of me understand why something goes from working to not workingThe intermediate distributions are not meant to be improvements on the older distributions with respect to stability, but are more test-beds for new software (hence the name "Gutsy", which implies that it is a little "bold", experimental). Newer distributions use different kernels and drivers, and so often cause lots of hardware-related issues that have to be dealt with. The real improvements are the stable distributions like Dapper Drake and Hardy Heron.
