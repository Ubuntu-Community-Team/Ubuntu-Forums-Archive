### Post by PorkyPie on 2009-09-05
On the Guest account, click on the icon at the top of the screen that looks like a loudspeaker, and make sure that it's set to the full. And if that doesn't work, try the different sound devices by right-clicking on the loudspeaker and clicking "Open Volume Control".
