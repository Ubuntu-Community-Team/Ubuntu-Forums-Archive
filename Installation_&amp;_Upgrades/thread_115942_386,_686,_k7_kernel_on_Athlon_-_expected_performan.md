### Post by frodon on 2006-01-12
You will not see a big difference but do it because it's really easy to change and i didn't meet anybody here who got problems after swiching to a new kernel. 
However in your case swiching to the 686 kernel is a really bad idea because this kernel is made for intel processors.

All the needed informations are here : [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=85917](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=85917)
