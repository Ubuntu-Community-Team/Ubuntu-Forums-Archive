### Post by slackmaster on 2008-03-24
I'm not sure about Kubuntu, but I upgraded from Ubuntu 7.04 to 7.1 a few days ago via Internet.  I've been using 7.1 now for several days and I have not noticed any issues whatsoever.  
I have noticed however, when you try to burn a disc using the HTTP method, the discs often get corrupted even with a high speed internet connection.  Bit torrents seem to be more reliable, and less prone to corruption in my experience.

If you try to upgrade, I think any corruptions or issues will be identified during the upgrade anyway.  I'd be sure to back up any important data before upgrading, just in case.
This is just based on my personal experience and opinion.
