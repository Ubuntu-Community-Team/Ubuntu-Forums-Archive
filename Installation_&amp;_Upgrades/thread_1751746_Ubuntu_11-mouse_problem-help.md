### Post by epictetus on 2011-05-09
For using your cursor while your mouse isn't working so that you can try to fix the problem:

Do you know how to use the number keys to move your cursor around? If you go to Keyboard Settings (do you know how to do that without your mouse?), there is a tab at the top that gives you options for using your number key pad to move the cursor. If you enable that, you can move the cursor with 7, 8, 9, 4, 6, 1, 2, and 3. 5 is click. 0 is click and hold. + is double-click. - sets 5, 0, and + up to click with the right mouse button. * sets 5, 0, and + up to click with the middle mouse button. And / sets 5, 0, and + up to click with the left mouse button. Hopefully that will let you at least navigate your computer without your mouse.

Good luck.
