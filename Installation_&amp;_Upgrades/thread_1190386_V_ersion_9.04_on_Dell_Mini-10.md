### Post by lemming465 on 2009-06-18
You don't have to wait for the optical drive; use something like [the USB procedure]("htthttps://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick") to boot a downloaded 9.04 LiveCD image and test how all your hardware does.  If you like how the live CD works, then you can install.  If you want to use Ext4 as the filesystem, you probably want a fresh install.  Note that to get from 8.04 to 9.04 by upgrades you'd first have to upgrade to 8.10 as a waystation.
