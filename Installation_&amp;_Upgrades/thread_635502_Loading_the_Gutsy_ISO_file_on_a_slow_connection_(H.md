### Post by hornetcoach on 2007-12-08
Hello all - my first post, and it's not a question! - I am on an incredibly slow connection from the Al Asad airbase in Iraq.  Also - I am only allowed 150MB of download per day....so I needed to chip away at the ISO file a little at a time.  I am using Firefox on XP and found the little program FlashGet.  I loaded FlashGet and it seems to be working great.  My connection drops off all the time and FlashGet seems to pickup right where it left off.  I am downloading from a Russian mirror site listed on the Ubuntu site.  Just thought I'd share my slow speed download method!

UPDATE - 12-15-2007:  The FlashGet download worked great and although it took 7 days at 100MB at a time the ISO file saved and burned with no problems and the Ubuntu Gutsy install was flawless.  Very happy with the new OS.  Works great with my Seagate FreeAgent external too.
