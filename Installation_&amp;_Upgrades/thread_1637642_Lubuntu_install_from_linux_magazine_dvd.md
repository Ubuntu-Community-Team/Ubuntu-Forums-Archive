### Post by Habeouscorpus on 2010-12-04
I tried to boot/install ubuntu from one of those magazine disks... Linux Format Magazine, yeah? It didn't quite work. My recommendation would be to burn your own disk using a disk image: (Direct Download) [http://people.ubuntu.com/~gilir/lubuntu-10.04.iso](http://people.ubuntu.com/~gilir/lubuntu-10.04.iso)

From there, you just feed it with a blank disk to your favorite burning program, and it burns a disk for you.

(Usually you have to boot from the disk in the BIOS menu to get it to do its thing, if it matters any. :) )
