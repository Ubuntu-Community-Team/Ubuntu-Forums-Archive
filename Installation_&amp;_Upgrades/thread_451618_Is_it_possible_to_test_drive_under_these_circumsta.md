### Post by kebes on 2007-05-22
If you follow that guide, and do steps 1 and 2  you will be booted into the LiveCD. As long as you don't continue (don't do step 3, which is clicking on the icon that says "Install") then nothing will be installed to your hard drive.

In the LiveCD session, you can try out Ubuntu and see if you like it. Note that the LiveCD runs much slower than a real installation.


So it's safe for you to follow those initial instructions. Those instructions just amount to carefully entering a few commands, one at a time.


I hope that works for you! If you have any further questions, don't hesitate to ask.

### Post by merlinus on 2007-05-22
```

wget http://www.mylittleubuntuguide.com/files/ati
sudo chmod +x ati
./ati

```

is meant to get your ATI video card working.  It will not install Ubuntu unless you click on the Install button once the system is up-and-running.

Good luck!

-merlin
