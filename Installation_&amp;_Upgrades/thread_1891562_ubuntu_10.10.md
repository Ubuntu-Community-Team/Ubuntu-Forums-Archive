### Post by oldtimer7777 on 2011-12-05
I don't know. But a good work-around might be creating a live stick of bootable Ubuntu on Flash Drive with Startup Disc Creator.  Download the iso you want to use, and put it on your Flash Drive with  Startup Disc Creator, then reboot and boot from your USB Live stick of Ubuntu 10.10. Flash Drives are pretty cheap these days, around 6.99 at Staples.
