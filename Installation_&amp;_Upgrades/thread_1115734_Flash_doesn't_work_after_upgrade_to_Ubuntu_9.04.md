### Post by Pasdar on 2009-04-04
If you have the same problem, just uninstall your current flash from Synaptic Package Manager, then download the latest flash version from Adobe website:

[http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/thankyou/?installer=Flash_Player_10_for_Linux_(.deb](http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/thankyou/?installer=Flash_Player_10_for_Linux_(.deb)) (Select .deb for Ubuntu 8.04+)

Download it, its now on your desktop, double click on it and click install. Now flash should work again.
