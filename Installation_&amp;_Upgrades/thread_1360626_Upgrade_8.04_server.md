### Post by Mighty_Joe on 2009-12-21
Is there some functionality or fix in 9.10 that you can't get in 8.04?  If not, I'd stick with 8.04.  It is the current LTS and will be supported until April 2013.  
If you just want to upgrade for the sake of it, I'd recommend waiting until April 2010 when the next LTS is released.
[Here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/upgrading#Network%20Upgrade%20for%20Ubuntu%20Servers%20%28Recommended%29") are the instructions for upgrading a server.  Note that you cannot upgrade from 8.04 to 9.10 directly ([Upgrade Notes]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes")).
