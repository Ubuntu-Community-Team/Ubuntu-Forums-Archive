### Post by aysiu on 2007-02-25
No. Edubuntu isn't a desktop environment, unlike the other *buntus (Ubuntu, Gnome; Kubuntu, KDE; Xubuntu, XFCE). Edubuntu is Gnome with some educationally-related packages and artwork. Ubuntu is Gnome with some other packages and artwork.

They are both Gnome.
