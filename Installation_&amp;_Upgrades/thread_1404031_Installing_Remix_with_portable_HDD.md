### Post by aryan22 on 2010-02-11
You will need:

1> Your portable drive to be atleast 1GB in size 

2> Formatted to fat32 filesystem.
use cmd
```
format /FS:FAT32 z:
```
Replace z: with your drive
or [swissknife](http://www.compuapps.com/download/Swissknife/swissknife.htm)

3> UNetbootin to prepare your portable drive.
[DOWNLOAD HERE](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/unetbootin-windows-latest.exe)
[UNetbootin Main Page](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)

And finally, you need to boot into your usb drive.
