### Post by sandys on 2010-11-21
Managed to find a solution which suits me, changed from netbook edition to normal Ubuntu session and configured my own launcher setup on the top panel and made that panel auto hide itself, this is better than the old Netbook setup I liked as I get to use the full 1024x600 for my applications, no panels at all using up my precious pixels :cool:

Don't know why I didn't think of this sooner.

Hope that helps someone else.

Cheers
