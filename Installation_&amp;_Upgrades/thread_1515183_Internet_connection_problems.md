### Post by varunendra on 2010-06-22
Just an advice rather than an answer:
If you love your current installation, (Win7 or whatever it is) you should keep a backup of the boot & installation partitions on a separate media such that you can restore them if & whenever needed.
I use Norton Ghost or PartImage (can't save mbr by default though) for that purpose. The simplest & guaranteed way is to clone the whole disk if possible as it also includes the MBR in the image.
