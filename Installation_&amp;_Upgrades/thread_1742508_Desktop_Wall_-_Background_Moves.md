### Post by zalberico on 2011-04-28
Trying to stop the background from moving and have it look nice again?  In the ccsm under desktop wall in the tab viewport switching add this to Non Sliding Windows: type=Dock | type=Desktop | state=Sticky

Now just have to get chromium to actually remain the default browser instead of switching every time.  Also Unity is terrible.
