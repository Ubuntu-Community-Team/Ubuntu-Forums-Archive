### Post by kansasnoob on 2008-11-02
I recommend first clicking on the Tools tab in Firefox, then Add-ons > Plug-ins and looking to be certain you have only Flash 10 installed. If you have both Flash 9 and Flash 10 then "tick" Disable for Flash 9 and quit Firefox by going to File > Quit.

When you restart Firefox you should find only Flash 10 enabled. See if that helped, if not I recommend this guide:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=866965&highlight=pulse+audio+intrepid](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=866965&highlight=pulse+audio+intrepid)
