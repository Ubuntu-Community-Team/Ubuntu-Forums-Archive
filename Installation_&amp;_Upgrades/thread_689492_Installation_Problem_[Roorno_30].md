### Post by jken146 on 2008-02-06
You could have faulty CDs.  Check the md5sums of the ISOs you downloaded; also run the CD integrity check from the first screen when you boot from the CD.

If the checksums are OK but the CDs are not, re-burn them at a very slow speed and try again.


You say your Windows installation 'disappeared'.  Could it be that your hard drive failed?
