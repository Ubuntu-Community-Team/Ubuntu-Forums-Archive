### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-10-11
If you use firefox just install the **flashaid** add-on.  Even if you don't use firefox still use this add-on as it will more or less ensure that flash works, not only in firefox, but also in the other browsers you may have and use on your system.

I do not know of any problems associated with running oneric, though I suppose it is possible that flash has not yet caught up with the oneric system quirks, if there are any.

It is especially good for 64 bit systems, as apparently it manages to overcome the problems that many people find with 64 bit flash installation.
