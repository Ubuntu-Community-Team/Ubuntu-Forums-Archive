### Post by memoryproblems on 2008-01-17
hello, If you go to ubuntu.com and fill out the download form and click "start download", it will take you to a new page and try to start the download. Click cancel and on that page, there should be a link near the top next to "Download Location". For me its like [http://mirrors.easynews.com/linux/ubuntu-releases/](http://mirrors.easynews.com/linux/ubuntu-releases/). Copy and paste this into your address bar, and it'll take you to an index of /linux/ubuntu-releases, at the bottom there'll be some folders. Click on 7.10, and within that you can navigate down a little and click to download the Alternate-Install CD for your installation. 

I also prefer to install from the alternate-install cd, I've had problems from the live-cd one as well, it just doesn't seem to be installed as well (theres wait time after the grub boot loader, it won't recognize my wired network), but everything seems to work pretty smooth with the alternate install cd.

I hope this helps you
