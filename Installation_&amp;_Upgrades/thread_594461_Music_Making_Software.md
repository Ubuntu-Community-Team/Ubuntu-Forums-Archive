### Post by markbuntu on 2008-05-01
I just installed the Ubuntu Studio packages with Synaptic into my brand new 8.04 installation  and there is a whole bunch of audio and video production software included. Dj mixers,synthesisers, recording and mixing, metering packages, eq, pretty much everything you need and all for free. If you are into audio and video production you need this. You also need a high speed connection or a lot of time to download it all but it is totally worth it and painless to install.
