### Post by Original Brownster on 2006-08-30
Hi,
Many problems are simply caused by a corrupt download or the burnt dvd/cd not being perfect, I would firstly verify the iso image you downloaded using md5sum and comparing to the md5sum on the download page.
Assuming this is ok I would re-burn the image at a slow speed, alternatively there is a method to verify the burnt cd against the iso image but I could only find a way in linux, see this [article.]("https://secure-support.novell.com/KanisaPlatform/Publishing/556/3926996_f.SAL_Public.html")

Are you installing to a laptop or normal pc? 
Is the spec. reasonably high, free memory, free disk space etc?
