### Post by monton on 2009-11-14
#1 Gateway ML6230 Laptop Celeron M 1.6, 1.5GB RAM, Intel video (don't remember which). Fresh install to 9.10 from 9.04

Faster boot time
Faster wireless/better connection
No sound

1 of 3 computers I moved to 9.10

Downloaded backports etc., alsamixer, etc

Stumbled on a post that said there was a conflict w/smartmodem driver. Disabled that and sound was back. Everything on the system works flawlessly. I love it!

#2 AMD XP3200 A7N8X-E, 512MB RAM. Video issue was low refresh rate and unable to edit xorg.conf to save settings. Changed refresh rate in "Display", saved - fixed that problem. On the sound issue I believe I downloaded backports, alsamixer, and perhaps made changes to files - I'm not sure. That mostly fixed the sound issue. I still get a few "pops" now and then but minor.
