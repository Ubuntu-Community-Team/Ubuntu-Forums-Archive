### Post by meng on 2006-07-02
I am wondering what your specifications are. How much RAM is your "small" RAM? Myself I have a 3-year old Dell notebook with 512 MB RAM and an ATI Mobility Radeon, and Ubuntu is just a tad too slow for my liking (not as slow as your experience though). I ended up opting for Xfce instead of GNOME. So consider a Xubuntu alternate CD install as a streamlined option. You may, however, find this a slightly more difficult transition from Windows.

Also, IMHO, being pissed with Windows is a common motivation for switching to Linux, but is not always sufficient reason to overcome the (hopefully minor) difficulties of transition. All I can say is: be patient and be sure to ask for whatever help you need.
