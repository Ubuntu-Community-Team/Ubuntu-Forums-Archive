### Post by EmporerD on 2011-06-03
Yeah just download a live cd and boot that. During installation it should recognize your other OSes and give you the option to make a new partition for ubuntu. If it doesn't you can just do it with gparted which is on the cd and is pretty easy to use. Once installed grub should give you the option to boot to any of your operating systems when you turn on your computer.
