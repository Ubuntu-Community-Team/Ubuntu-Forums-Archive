### Post by coffeecat on 2011-07-28
You are running an obsolete, unsupported version of Ubuntu - 9.04/Jaunty. It passed end-of-life in October of last year.

Your best option would be to make a fresh installation of a supported version, either the LTS (long-term support) 10.04 or the most recent 11.04.
