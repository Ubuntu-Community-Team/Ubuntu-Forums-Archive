### Post by varunus on 2005-08-09
To do this, use smart boot manager, found here:
[http://btmgr.webframe.org/](http://btmgr.webframe.org/)

It is os independent, boots from a floppy, and then just lists all of your devices (CD drives, hard drives, etc).  You just pick which one to boot from.  Great for older computers.
