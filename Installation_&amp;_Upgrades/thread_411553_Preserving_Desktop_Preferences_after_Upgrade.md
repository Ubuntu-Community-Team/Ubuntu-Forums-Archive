### Post by dfreer on 2007-04-17
Have you already performed the upgrade, and then *lost* the files?
Or are you just worried that you might lose them?

If you upgrade by using apt-get/aptitude/synaptic/update-manager, all your settings should be preserved, you shouldn't lose anything.

If you upgrade by reinstalling your system, simple keep your /home/user folder as all user settings are saved in there, specifically the ~/.icon ~/.gnome folders I think.
