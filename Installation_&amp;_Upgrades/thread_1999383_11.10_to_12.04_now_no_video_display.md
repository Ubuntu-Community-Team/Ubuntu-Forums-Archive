To answer your questions though, the upgrade that caused the problem was the distribution upgrade. 

The fix finally came after about 4 hours of combing through various forums with no luck. 
I decided to try to reproduce rather than just do a clean load of 12.4. 

I did a clean load of 11.10. Downloaded the 12.4 Desktop Live CD and tried the upgrade with it, and got the same result. 

Finally after the 3rd reload of 11.10, I decided to try the Alternate Installer DVD and upgraded using nomodeset option, rebooted and now I have video.
