### Post by Hymyly on 2008-04-27
The HTTP-servers are swamped this soon after the release date. Try downloading via Bittorrent instead. Then you get automatic checksum testing for every downloaded chunk, and the download runs quicker the more people are downloading. I downloaded all four images simultaneously at 200 kiB/s each.
