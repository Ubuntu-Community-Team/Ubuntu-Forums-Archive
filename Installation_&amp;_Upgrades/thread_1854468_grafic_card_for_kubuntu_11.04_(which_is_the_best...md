### Post by sgaap on 2011-10-04
Tbh, overall best choice is nvidia (or intel, not sure about the state of their drivers but they seem to improve steadily), ati's official drivers are crap (11.9 even breaks kde 4 and still bugs majorly when using gnome 3) so you should avoid those, the oss drivers are ok-ish but still lack a lot of functionality, so best choice would be the nvidia binary blob (and the ati oss drivers if you care for keeping your system fully oss)
