### Post by deadgobby on 2007-02-24
Here try this at the boot up of the live CD.
Often when installing Ubuntu i386 on an AMD64 board you will have to hit F6 and enter the following paramaters

irqpoll pci=noacpi noapic nolapic acpi=off

to get The Ubuntu i386 Alternate or LiveCD to Boot correctly. 
More information on AMD
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/?action=fullsearch&context=180&value=AMD&titlesearch=Titles](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/?action=fullsearch&context=180&value=AMD&titlesearch=Titles)
