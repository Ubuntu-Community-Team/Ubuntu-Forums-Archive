I upgraded from 10.04 (through 10.10) to 11.04 and my desktop retained the theme and customizations from the earlier version (I have everything on one taskbar at the top of the screen, and an Awn dock at the bottom).
That computer can't handle Unity, so it defaulted to classic Gnome.

The only settings that I had to set up again were the compiz ones.

The only thing that got a bit broken in the upgrade was Ubuntu Tweak, which seems to have disappeared in the first upgrade from 10.04 to 10.10, and can't be downloaded from synaptic. But the settings I changed with it (GDM wallpaper, and displaying Home, Computer and Network icons on the desktop) Have remained.
