### Post by Caffeine_Junky on 2007-10-28
Well, I could suggest that you  burn a fresh disk of your 7.10 ISO at a slow speed, as slow as is possible with your burning software and see how that go's...

Then if the problem still persists, download a fresh 7.10 ISO from: [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)

Once again, burn it as slow as you can and see if that works to get you installed.

Failing that, well, I don't like to speculate, but (and this is just a thought) you may have a problem with your disk-drive (dirty lens worn out drive), BUT don't worry about that at this point because I think you will find a fresh SLOW burn will get you up and running.

Good Luck
