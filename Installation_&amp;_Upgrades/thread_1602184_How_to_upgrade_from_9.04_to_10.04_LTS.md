### Post by Rubi1200 on 2010-10-21
Hi and welcome to the forums :)

No, you cannot upgrade directly to 10.04.

You have 2 options:

1. upgrade from 9.04 to 9.10 and then from 9.10 to 10.04

2. backup all important data (which you should do also for option 1) and go for a fresh install of 10.04
