### Post by chessnerd on 2010-04-22
On nvidia's website they offer Linux drivers. If you go to their driver site ([http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index5.aspx?lang=en-us](http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index5.aspx?lang=en-us)) and input the info of your card, and your OS, it should give you a driver.

However, look at System>Administration>Hardware Drivers first to see if Ubuntu can get you any proprietary drivers without needing to install them from nvidia.

Hope this helps.
