### Post by computx on 2007-07-17
I had a similar problem with an intel video adaptor. I believe the problem is in the fact that Google Earth wants a video card with 3D Acceleration. If your card doesn't support. 3D accelleration you can try mesa.  Also if you do have 3D accel and have compiz turned on you may have problems with G.E. not behaving nicely. 
Things to try are: 
A. run it in a window as opposed to full screen. 
B.  enable DRI and GL. 
C. Disable compiz.
Good luck!
