### Post by Fafler on 2009-11-16
I have just upgraded my Compal IFL-90 laptop to Ubuntu 9.10. Now, after rebooting, the mouse stopped working. My debian experience tells me to edit /etc/X11/xorg.conf, but it seems to be autogenerated. What to do?

---

### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-11-16
In 9.10 there is no xorg.conf by default, but if you create one it will be read exactly as it was in earlier versions of ubuntu. So if you have a copy of the xorg.conf from a previous version that you know allows your mouse to work properly, use that instead of whatever you have at present.
