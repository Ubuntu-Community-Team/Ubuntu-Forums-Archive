### Post by TopEnder on 2008-10-28
Phil,

You need to do a search for your printer in Synaptic Package Manager and install the packages (drivers)for your printer I think there is about four (4) you have to install.  You could also check for post by me **TopEnder** and others re: Brother printer - DCP-130C, DCP-150C installation and most of the DCP range of printers a very similar to install.  
I beleive the ones you need are:
brother-lpr-drivers-extra
brother-lpr-drivers-common
brother-cups-wrapper-extra
brother-cups-wrapper-common


The Scanner side of your printer is a little different to install but if you follow the post started by BroadDWorld (do a search) - many posts but worth the read also the Brothers Web site will help you with solutions and they a pretty good at help you out with problems.  Back to work now on this Windows machine, hope the above helps you in getting you printing. TopEnder
