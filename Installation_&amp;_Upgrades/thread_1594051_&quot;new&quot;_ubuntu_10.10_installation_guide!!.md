Once you download your UBUNTU 10.10 copy you can either burn it to a CD or copy to your USB.


If you burn the cd just set up your PC to reboot from CD. If you choose to use an USB device you need to download Unetbooting. I have a link available on my site as well.Once you download it, just add the Ubuntu image to Unetbooting and reboot your PC via USB device..
