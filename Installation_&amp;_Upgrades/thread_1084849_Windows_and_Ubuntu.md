### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-03-02
Given the touchiness of Vista to any changes, before you mess with it, would be a good idea to ensure that you have a Vista recovery CD handy, or a genuine Vista DVD.  Why? Because if anything goes wrong with resizing the Vista OS, it will render Vista unbootable -- until you boot from one of these two media and run Startup Repair.

If you don't have such media, go to the NeoSmart forums and look for their download of the Vista recovery CD.  You can download and burn a CD from that.
