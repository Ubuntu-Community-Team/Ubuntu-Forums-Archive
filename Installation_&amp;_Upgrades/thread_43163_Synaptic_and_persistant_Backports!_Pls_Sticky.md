### Post by Omnios on 2005-06-21
Found this out through trial and error.

 When you add your backports to etc/apt/sources.list you may add two copies one commented out and one set active. When you remove backports from synaptic it normaly deleates the entry from the list. With the commented # entries it will now allow you the option to add the backposts from the list though | settings | Repositories | settings | now ceck dissabled software sorces which allows you to click on backports from the sources list. 

 Wish I would have figured this out a long time ago lol.
