### Post by HereInOz on 2006-07-03
Your only possibility is to use some data recovery software to recover what you can, or get the disk to a data recovery company and pay them heaps to get the files back - if they still exist.  

I know that you can recover files from a HDD formatted by Windows, but whether it is possible after a partitioning during an Ubuntu install, I don't really know.  Installing Windows on the HDD at the moment will probably make matters far worse than they are now, by overwriting more of the HDD.

The first question I would ask of your father is "Do you have a backup of your data?"  My guess is that the answer will be "No", so if that is the case, there are two people who have been a little silly :-).

You will need to re-format the HDD to install windows xp again, but before you do that, you need to get hold of some good data recovery software, or investigate the data recovery companies in your area, to see if any files are recoverable.  You have to accept the fact that the files may be lost.
