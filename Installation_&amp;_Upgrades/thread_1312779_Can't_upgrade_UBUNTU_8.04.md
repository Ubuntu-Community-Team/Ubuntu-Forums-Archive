### Post by Onesimus on 2009-11-03
I don't think it is possible to upgrade from 8.04 straight to 9.10, because 8.04 is a LTS (Long Term Support) version and 9.10 isn't.

You could either wait until 10.04 is out, or you would have to upgrade to 8.10, then 9.04 and finally 9.10
