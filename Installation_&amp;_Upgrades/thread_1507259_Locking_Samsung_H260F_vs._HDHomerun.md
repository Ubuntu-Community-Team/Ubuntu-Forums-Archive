### Post by winewood on 2010-08-18
Sorry for not seeing this faster.
 
I would check your network cable or your switch.  Many tuners that are inside the box or directly attached to your TV do not use a network to send data to the MythTV box.  The HDHomerun requires good solid connectivity to provide a good seamless picture to your Myth setup.  I would check 2 areas.  Use another cat5 network cable other than your current setup.  You may have a loose connection.  I try to always use pre-made cables instead of home crimped solutions.  Second, make sure your switch has been rebooted and or you have a more robust switch to connect the HDHomerun and your MythTV box.  Using network you have new bottlenecks that are impacted by high data usage (backing up your movies, or NFS mounts).  Make sure you are using a 100Mb or 1 GB switch/hub instead of a 10Mb (ie. older switch)
