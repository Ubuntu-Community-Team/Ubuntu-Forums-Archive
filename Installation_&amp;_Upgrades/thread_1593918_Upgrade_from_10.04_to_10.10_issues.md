### Post by kentechy on 2010-10-11
I took the plunge and upgraded from 10.04 to 10.10 yesterday.  I was pleasantly surprised that it actually booted up without crashing, which it did on my previous upgrade...

Two things I have noticed:

1.  The boot is noticeably slower.

2.  When coming out of suspend mode, the wireless manager is very slow to reconnect to my network.  In 10.04 the wireless connect icon would be spinning away almost immediately after logging onto my laptop.  It now takes at least a half minute longer.
