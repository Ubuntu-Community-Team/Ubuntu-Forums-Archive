### Post by MadCow108 on 2009-04-26
An upgrade will not reformat your disk nor change the filesystem.
A few system configuration files may be overwritten but none of your personal files.
But a backup of important data never harms.

You can change to ext4 filesystem later without reformating or data loss. A tutorial is somewhere in the tutorial forum.
