### Post by Mith73 on 2006-12-02
Well was having problems with edgy. Lag, wine crashing and over all just poor speed. Tried a few things suggested nothing worked. Then playing around noticed that my swap file was fraged up. Used gparted rebuilt as a linux swap. Now everything is back to normal and running just as fast and stable as before the upgrade. Just dont know if anyone else has come across this. If you are getting lag and and massive slow downs. check this out. It may have just been a fluke with me.
