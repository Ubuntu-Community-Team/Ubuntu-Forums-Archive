### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-10-21
There is a long list of bug fixes, and, as your case suggests, some regressions, between the two kernel versions. You could try troubleshooting the black screen by removing the 'quiet splash' options from the boot line. You can edit the bootlines temporarily in the grub menu by pressing 'e', or permanently, by editing /etc/default/grub.
