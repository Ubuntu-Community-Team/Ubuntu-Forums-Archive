### Post by Thomas_Tanner on 2015-08-04
Today I upgraded a WIN 7 laptop (DELL) to WIN 10 - then using the Ubuntu 15.04 CD resized the WIN 10 partition and created a dual boot WIN 10 / Ubuntu 15 system.  Everything went smoothly and everything works with no errors.  This was an old DELL D630C with a 64 bit processor.

Ubuntu saw the WIN 10 partition as a WIN 7 setup but did not seem to care and it seems to have made no difference.&#8203;
