**IF YOU ARE GOING TO UPGRADE, PLEASE DO WHAT EVERYONE ADVISES: BACKUP**

After backing up your data, You might also consider imaging your current working HDD.  You can do this by using this tool ( [http://www.remastersys.klikit-linux.com/](http://www.remastersys.klikit-linux.com/) ) prior to backup and the if something doesn't go as well as you'd hoped, you can revert to your old install.
