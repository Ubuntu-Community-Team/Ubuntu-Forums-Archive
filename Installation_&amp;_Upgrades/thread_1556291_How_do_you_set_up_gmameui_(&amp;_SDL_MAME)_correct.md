### Post by gohmifune on 2010-08-19
In Options > Directories you set the directories you wanted to use. Where it says MAME executables, place the location of your executable. Mine is located in /usr/games/mame.

ROMs is the location of where you store your ROMs. Say ~/Games/Mame or something to that effect.
