### Post by TBABill on 2010-10-18
I upgraded and then reverted back to 10.04. I was not, however, on 9.10. I did fresh 10.04 installs and ran them, then decided to upgrade. Wireless is horrible on 10.10 right now and one machine will not even connect but works perfectly (no proprietary driver needed) in 10.04.

Not to be rude in any way, but the distros are not 9.1 or 10.1. They are not numbers with decimals. The 9 denotes calendar year 2009 and the .10 denotes it was released in October of that year. Thus, 10.04 was released April 2010.

Good luck. Try out your wireless and video BEFORE you install 10.10. Those seem to be heavily problematic right now, particularly so for ATi from the posts I have seen.
