### Post by bluenova on 2007-07-27
I always do a clean install when a new version comes out. It's just so easy in Linux. I would advise following [this guide]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome") to make a seperate home partition, then when you reinstall all your home folder and settings will be just as they were previously.
