### Post by eradicator on 2007-02-23
Kinda new to Ubuntu 6.10 myself, but, copying/burning CD's is amazingly easy.

Just like the last post said, right-click the icon of the CD on the desktop to copy the CD, but you can copy it to an ISO file on your computer instead of burning the CD right away. Just change the selected copying drive and change it to ISO file.

Then, insert blank CD into drive, and right-click on the ISO file, select 'Write to CD' (I believe it was something like that for wording)

My burns with Ubuntu can be read by more drives than when I burned with Windows..
