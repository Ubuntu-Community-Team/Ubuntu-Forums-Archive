### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-05-21
Before you install, I strongly suggest you try the LiveCD approach.  That will allow you to determine how well Ubuntu detects your hardware and loads the proper drivers.   

There have been a lot of posts about folks just installing 9.04 and then not having graphics.  While minor glitches like WiFi are easily fixed, not having graphics is a more serious problem.
