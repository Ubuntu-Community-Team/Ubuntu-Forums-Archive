### Post by dvlchd3 on 2009-11-04
You should ALWAYS backup your data before doing a dist upgrade/installation!

You may be able to recover your files by booting into the live CD.

I usually recommend (especially with 9.10) doing a fresh install when upgrading to a newer distribution.  There are several items that cannot be upgraded through Synaptics, and several things can become broken very easily.
