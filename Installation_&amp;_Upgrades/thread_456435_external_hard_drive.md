### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-05-27
If you are using a mac, I like the LaCie d2 drives.  The firewire 800 is super fast, so you can mirror your entire drive in a few minutes.  The all-metal enclosure makes them tough as well.  Since they don't have fans, they are not good for real-time music or video recording or editing.  For that application, you will have to read reviews on other's experiences.
