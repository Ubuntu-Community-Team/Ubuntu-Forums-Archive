### Post by Pumalite on 2007-08-09
I don't know about 6.06, but I used 6.10 for a while and then upgraded to 7.04. IMHO it offers more packages, more up-to-date, faster kernel. I had a number of programs in 6.10 that didn't work or I wanted a newer version that I could only get upgrading to 7.04. I'm happy with the change. I think it is a personal matter though. And there is always the chance of the upgrade going wrong. Good luck
