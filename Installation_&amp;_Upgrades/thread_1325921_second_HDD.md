### Post by finwake on 2009-11-14
You need to go into BIOS at bootup.  Somewhere in your BIOS will be your HDD boot priority; make the Hard Drive that you put Ubuntu on to be the first one in your boot priority.  Save and reboot.

Grub (the bootloader) should come right up and let you pick to load Ubuntu or Windows.  Pick Ubuntu and welcome aboard!!!  :)
