### Post by shriramrs31 on 2010-02-11
First try out the live CD OR make a live USB with UNetBootin. Mostly everything must work out of the box. If not, you might have to put a little effort. In this case, have a look at the Ubuntu hardware compatibility list.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/)

This will give you an idea of the drivers for the hardware present in your laptop.

I have a lenovo thinkpad and the only device that I cant make it work like I want it to is the fingerprint sensor. All the other hardware PERFECT !
