### Post by ElGaton on 2011-11-12
I think it may be dangerous to upgrade from KUbuntu 11.10 to Ubuntu 11.10 as I don't know if that particular scenario has been tested.

However, there's a quicker way to install the standard Ubuntu desktop: boot into your existing installation and install the "ubuntu-desktop" package, then log out. At login time you'll be able to choose to use KDE or Unity for your next session.
