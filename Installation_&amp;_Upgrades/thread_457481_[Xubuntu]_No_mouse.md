### Post by kerry_s on 2007-05-28
I'll try and explain the keyboard mouse control.

Press Shift + Ctrl +NumLK , you will here a beep telling you it's on.
( i don't know your keyboard setup, but on mine you have to hold the fn key for the keypad)
keys 7, 8, 9 are your up directions
keys 4,6 are left and right
keys 1, 2, 3 are you down directions
key 5 is your click
key / is the left click selection
key * is the middle key selection
key - is the right click selection
(example: you pick your button( /, *, -) and press 5 to use it)
key 0 is the drag lock
key . is the drag unlock


I hope that helps you for now.
