### Post by basotl on 2009-10-31
I believe your question may be lacking support as you posted it under **Installation & Upgrades** you may have done better posting it under [Wine]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=313") as it's a Wine issue.

That being said I have run CoH in the past under 32bit Ubuntu using Wine. It works but there are a few issues. See the Wine App Database for information on it.

Personally I suggest using Cedega to play CoH with Linux. It is produced by the same company that made the Mac "port". It costs money but it currently provides the best user experience for CoH on Linux. In addition Transgaming provides help and support. I currently use Cedega to play CoH under 64bit Ubuntu 9.10.
