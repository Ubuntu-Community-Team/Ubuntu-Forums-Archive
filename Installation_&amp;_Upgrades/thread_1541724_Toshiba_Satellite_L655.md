### Post by canucked on 2010-12-09
I recently configured a Toshiba Satellite L655-S5115 laptop for a friend and summarized the hardware issues I encountered here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1641931](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1641931)

The ethernet wasn't immediately recognized in Ubuntu 10.04 Lucid 64-bit, so I installed 10.10 Maverick 64-bit.  I had already upgraded the Toshiba BIOS to v1.70, and the problems I had were with wireless, audio, and the battery.

If you're still having problems with running Ubuntu on your L655, perhaps a BIOS update would fix or improve the possible ACPI issue.

Good luck!
