### Post by VietCanada on 2013-04-29
I had the same problem. In my case the solution was to click on the sound icon at the top right and select 'sound settings'. It opened into the 'output' window. My system had two choices, 'digital output; and 'analogue output'. Analogue was hilited. I changed the selection to 'digital output' and my sound was back. IOW the wrong output was selected by default.
