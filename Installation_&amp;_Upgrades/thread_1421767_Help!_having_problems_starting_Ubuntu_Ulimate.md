### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-03-04
This means that it will ask you for your password to do whatever you are trying to do. The password that you need to enter is the password you created while you were installing Ubuntu. When the computer asks you for sudo privileges it is basically asking you "Are you sure you want to do this?". Its just a safety measure put in to Ubuntu to protect from unauthorized access to your computer.
