### Post by tkiesel on 2005-02-14
Hi kmarcus and jbrader,

Are you using pressed CDs that you got in the mail or purchased online, or CDs that you've burned yourselves?

If you've burned them yourselves, one thing to check is that you burned the ISO as an image and not as a file.  If you pop the CD in and look at it under your OS of choice, you should see a handful of directories and a few files.  If you see the ISO file itself as the contents of the CD, then you'll need to burn another CD, but keep that one for achiving purposes, it's not a waste.    :grin:
