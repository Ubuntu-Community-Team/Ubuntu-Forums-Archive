### Post by thegreatsnafu on 2009-09-12
Hi,

Go to this menu:


[LIST=1]
[*]"System" -> "Administration" -> "Hardware Drivers".
[*]You should see a driver available for your Wireless Card.
[*]Select it and click "Activate".
[*]Your Wireless Card should work!:)
[/LIST]
