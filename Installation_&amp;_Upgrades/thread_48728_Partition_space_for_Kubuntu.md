### Post by Xian on 2005-07-13
The bare minimum that the [Install Guide](http://archive.ubuntulinux.org/ubuntu/dists/hoary/main/installer-i386/current/doc/manual/en/) recommends for a standard Ubuntu installation is around 2.5GB, not including your swap partition.

Most people if they have the space will opt for sizes of 5GB and up, to allow for expansion of packages and other files.
