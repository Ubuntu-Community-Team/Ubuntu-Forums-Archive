### Post by blroth on 2011-05-14
Much to my surprise and amazement, the upgrade worked perfectly.  I was quite hesitant after reading of all the mishaps and disasters, but

I am running Win XP (don't know why), 10.04, and learning how to use Natty.  Unity is definitely a major change and will take some time to get used to it.  Over the past few years, I always kept a LTS version as a daily OS and installed the newest on a separate drive. I did back up 10.10 and would have done a clean install if I encountered major problems.  Fortunately all is perfect and everything works!  There is hope!!
