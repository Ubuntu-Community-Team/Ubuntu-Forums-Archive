### Post by Herman on 2007-11-16
It depends on your CPU and RAM.
Ubuntu can be fully installed in probably about fifteen or twenty minutes in a very fast computer, or it can take overnight in an old, slow one.
Average is about half an hour to forty minutes for the entire installation.

Sometimes it does seem to pause for a long time at certain stages of the installation, it is best to be patient and wait for a long time, especially if you know your computer is not the fastest.

Regards, Herman :)
