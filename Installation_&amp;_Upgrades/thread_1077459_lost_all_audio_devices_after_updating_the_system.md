### Post by bananafishbones on 2009-02-22
If you've just lost your audio, then double click on the volume control, select the Switches tab and un-check the Analog/Digital Output Jack.

I've never touched this setting before and after updating my system this past week, it must have gotten updated/set. I found another thread here with this suggestion and it worked like a charm.

I have an older Audigy sound card. See if this works for you. 

It had to come from an update of some sort. I lost my audio 'out of the blue'.

Good luck.
BF
