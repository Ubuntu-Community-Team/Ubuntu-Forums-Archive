### Post by pseudo-random on 2008-03-30
A bit about batteries:
Rechargeable batteries have a sort of memory. When they are partially charged they remember it and set it as the new threshold for next time. With time this threshold decreases and decreases.
 If you continuously leave a laptop plugged in it will reduce your batteries life. The correct way to battery-manage is to charge it fully, then unplug, let it run all the way down and then plug in. OR leave it plugged in but remove the battery.
Doing this will retain your batteries life.
