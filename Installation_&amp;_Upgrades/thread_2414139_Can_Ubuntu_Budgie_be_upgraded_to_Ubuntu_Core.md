### Post by kc1di on 2019-03-06
You can upgrade fro 18.04.2 to 18.10 by following [this]("https://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2018/10/how-to-upgrade-to-ubuntu-18-10"). 
I would do a fresh install myself though. Some upgrade with no problems other have many problems.   How ever I would consider the fact that 18.04.2 is an LTS release and will be support for a long time yet 18.10 is not and will reach end of life in July of this year and will no longer be supported and you will have to upgrade again or reinstall a supported version.  LTS stands for Long term support and it remains stable for a long time. Your Choice though.
P.S. I'm not sure if you intend to keep budgie desktop or want to change to gnome.  If the later then definitely do a fresh install.
