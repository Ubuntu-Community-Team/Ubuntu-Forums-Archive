### Post by earthpigg on 2009-07-08
there may have been an error when writing to the CD.

have you tried burning a second cd to see if that is the case?

-only burn on a CD-R. not a CD-RW or DVD.
-burn at the slowest speed possible. minor errors are fine on a music CD, as you will never notice them. this is not the case when burning an operating system to CD. 2x or 4x would be best.
-it cant hurt to test the CD for errors, just in case. [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/CDIntegrityCheck](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/CDIntegrityCheck)
