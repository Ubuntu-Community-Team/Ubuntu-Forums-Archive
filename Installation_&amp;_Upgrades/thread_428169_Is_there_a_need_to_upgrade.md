### Post by blackened on 2007-04-30
Your mileage will always vary depending on your hardware. If you don't require any bleeding edge software and Edgy runs solidly, then I would suggest sticking with it until it proves necessary to upgrade. 

Personally Feisty has been hit and miss on my hardware. At first it was flaky with hard freezes, then I did another clean install and it was fine for about 3 days so I left it alone, then recently xorg has begun to crap out on startup and to randomly bite the dust for no obvious reason. As I said, it will depend on your hardware. I'm about to go back to Edgy for the next few months though.
