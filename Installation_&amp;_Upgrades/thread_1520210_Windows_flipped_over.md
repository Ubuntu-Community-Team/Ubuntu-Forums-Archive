### Post by lechien73 on 2010-06-29
Glad you got the buttons back where you want them. Personally, I've got used to them on the left now.

The preferred method of changing the button location is by customising the theme, rather than changing them in gconf-editor. The reason for this is that changing them in gconf-editor is a global change that may affect how other themes work in the future.

To edit the theme, check the "May 5th Update" section of the following blog post:

[http://uri.tl/5](http://uri.tl/5)

Or look at this sticky elsewhere in the forums:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1469475](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1469475)
