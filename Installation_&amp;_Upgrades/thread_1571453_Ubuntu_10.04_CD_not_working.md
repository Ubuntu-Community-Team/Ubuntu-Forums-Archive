### Post by phillw on 2010-09-09
Hi,

If you are certain you have your BIOS set to boot from the CD/DVD drive (It will be listed in your BIOS boot order), I'd suggest running a lens cleaning cd through your system. The general one looks like [http://www.amazon.com/3-Piece-DVD-Lens-Cleaning-Kit/dp/B001F0LXVO](http://www.amazon.com/3-Piece-DVD-Lens-Cleaning-Kit/dp/B001F0LXVO) but they are available very cheaply at discount stores. Any dirt / residue on the cd lens would not affect music cd's but would affect an iso cd.

Regards,

Phill.
