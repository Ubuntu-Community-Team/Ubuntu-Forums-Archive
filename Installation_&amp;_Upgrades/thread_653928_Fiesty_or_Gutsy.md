### Post by erfahren on 2007-12-30
oh, don't get into trying to install a newer version of the ATI driver (unless you really want to!). The ATI driver that's available in Feisty is pretty stable. Once connected to the internet, updated and everything you just need to enable it by going to System > Administration > Restricted Drivers Manager

I use Feisty on my laptop with an ATI card. I tried Gutsy but reverted back because of the suspend problems among other things. Feisty just worked better for me even though the included ATI driver is an older version.
