### Post by paleheretic on 2008-04-27
I had this problem and managed to get round it. At the grub startup screen, hit escape and choose the recovery version of the most recent OS version.

The problem is that the most recent version of the 'Human' login theme is damaged. Once logged in under recovery version, change your login theme to something else ... No promises, but it worked for me.
