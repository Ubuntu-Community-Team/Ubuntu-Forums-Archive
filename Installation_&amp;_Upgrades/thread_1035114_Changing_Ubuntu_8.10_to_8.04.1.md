### Post by avtolle on 2009-01-09
Is there a reason you want to do this? I presume you want to go back to 8.04.1, which involves downloading the iso for 8.04.1 and doing a clean installation of the operating system. If this is what you want to do, back up your files found in the home directory (if you do not have a separate /home partition) before you start, so you can add them back once you are finished.
