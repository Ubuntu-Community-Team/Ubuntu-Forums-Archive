### Post by coffeecat on 2010-04-14
> **WendyWeber said:**
> Update Manager.  Should that work properly in a beta version?

With one huge caveat. If it offers to do a partial upgrade, **do not accept the offer**. :) See here for more:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1343434](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1343434)

A prompt for a partial upgrade seems to have occurred less frequently in the Lucid testing cycle than in Karmic - at least in my experience. Because it happened so much in Karmic, and people were less aware of this problem then, update manager was nicknamed "update mangler" during Karmic testing.

Just to emphasise - this won't be an issue once Lucid goes final. It's just something to beware of while it still has testing status.
