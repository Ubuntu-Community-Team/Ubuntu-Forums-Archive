### Post by avtolle on 2009-02-26
The first thought that occurs to me is the RAM on your system. To use the Live CD (in your case, DVD) installer requires a minimum of 384 MB RAM. I'd give the alternate CD a go, a text based installer which requires a minimum of 256 MB RAM. 

The next thought that occurs is to inquire whether you have considered installing Xubuntu rather than Ubuntu, as it comes with a lighter desktop, and runs better on lower RAM systems than Ubuntu.
