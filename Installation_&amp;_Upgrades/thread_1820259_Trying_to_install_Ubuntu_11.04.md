### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-08
When you get the Ubuntu screen with the red dots ... don't press ANYTHING -- just wait.  Eventually, you will get a desktop panel that will have two main entries.  The one on the left will say Try Ubuntu, and the one on the right will install it.  Click on the button on the right.
