You can backup your system's home folder, export your bookmarks, and use password exporter firefox plugin to backup your passwords in Firefox.   Here is a guide that mostly works with 10.04 too:

[https://debianhelp.wordpress.com/2011/07/05/to-do-list-after-installing-ubuntu-10-10-aka-maverick-meerkat/](https://debianhelp.wordpress.com/2011/07/05/to-do-list-after-installing-ubuntu-10-10-aka-maverick-meerkat/)

Just make sure you replace the word "maverick" with "lucid" in any of the commands.
