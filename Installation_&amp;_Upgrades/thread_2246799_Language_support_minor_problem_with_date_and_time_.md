### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2014-10-03
You looked in the wrong place.
Go to language support and click in the second tab, something like regional settings or formats. Mine, which is in Portuguese, shows "Formatos Regionais". That's where you should change the system language affecting dates, currency, etc.
