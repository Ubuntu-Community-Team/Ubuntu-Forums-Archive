### Post by SteveF on 2006-06-05
OK, I got sound again.  I ran alsamixer and saw the wav setting was 0.  I didn't notice this when I went into Kmix because wav is on the input tab and I was looking at output.  I up the wav setting in alsamixer and now I have sound.

Steve
