### Post by Luke771 on 2006-01-23
If that happens efter you have used your computer for quite a while, it may be a  cooling problem.
I had kind of a similar problem some days ago: I left the PC running while downloading stuff, after something like 10 or 12 hours I turned on the screen and everything was gone: panel, icons, everything. All I could see was the background picture and a mouse cursor. Right clicking didn' help.
I rebooted and got some Grub problem.
I rebooted again and looked more carefully at the sequence: BIOS didn't find my hda.
I tought it was some loose flat cable (or DC cable or whatever), so I opened the case, and it was HOT. I mean the two HDDs were heating up too much, I could tell without even bothering to take the temprature; it was really hot.
Well, I let it cool down for a while and it worked again.
Now I run My PC with  a small fan on the floor pointing at the open case... you should see that; it looks like some mad scientist's lab
