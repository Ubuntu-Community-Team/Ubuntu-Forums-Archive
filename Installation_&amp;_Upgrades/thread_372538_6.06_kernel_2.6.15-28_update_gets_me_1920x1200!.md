### Post by wkulecz on 2007-02-28
Up to now I'd been totally unable to get anything better than 1600x1200 with the Nvidia proprietary drivers.  Not seeing horror stories here I updated everything from 2.6.15-26 (154 patches since I'd last updated to when 2.6.15-26 was current) and after reboot I now have 1920x1600 in my display resolution menu!  Chose it and it looks great! 

Its possible this was fixed in 2.6.15-27 as I've not been chasing updates using an "if its not broke don't fix it" strategy.   But I needed kernel source and only 2.6.15-28 was easy to find with Ubuntu patches installed -- I've a project growing in scope and it looks like some custom mods to bttv driver will be the best way for me to proceed so I took a chance on the update.  If no problems surface I'll soon be updating all our boxes to finally get what we paid for from these rather expensive LCDs.

I wish some useful info was in the description/changes tabs under "details" in update manager, I might not have waited so long to update!
