### Post by howefield on 2010-09-06
Here's some material to read through...

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery)

Files which have been overwritten are probably toast, but there may be some hope for those files not over written, you'd be best to stop using the disk whilst you try to recover what you can.

Depending on the value of your data, a professional service might have some success but probably fairly expensive.

In any event, once you are done, devise a backup strategy so you are not bitten twice.
