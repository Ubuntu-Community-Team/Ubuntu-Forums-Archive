### Post by keasden on 2010-05-08
Thanks for that, but I've actually finally got it sorted.

In case anyone else hits this problem: because there's no 'install' option on startup, when you boot from the remastersys live CD/USB (custombackup.iso), you boot up fully (from the CD/USB) and then use the 'Install RELEASE' utility under System/Admin. It took 10 - 15 mins and installed the custombackup.iso version perfectly, solving all my wireless-connection problems. In Windows-Speak it is, in effect, a System Restore. Great facility!
