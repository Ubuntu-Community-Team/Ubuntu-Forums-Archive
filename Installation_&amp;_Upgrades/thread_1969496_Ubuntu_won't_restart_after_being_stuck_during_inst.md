### Post by kc1di on 2012-04-30
In my personal experience upgrades for one version to another have been hit and miss at best I prefer to do clean installs. just MHO.

but that being said download and burn a copy of 12.04 to disc or usb stick run the live session from that  should get you to your important files.  back them up to cd's or other media and do a fresh install. that's one way out of it. There are others and I'm sure some will come along with further help.

you may want to try re installing grub see if that helps. 

instructions are here :[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows")
though this is talking about after a windows install the procedure is basically the same.
