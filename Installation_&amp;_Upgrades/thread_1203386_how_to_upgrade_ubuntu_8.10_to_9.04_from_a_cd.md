### Post by zwdev on 2009-07-05
Hi

Download and burn the Ubuntu "alternate" installation CD 

Once you put it in your cd drive a menu should appear with the option to updrade;

OR you can mount the iso without burning it by typing :
sudo mount -o loop ~/Desktop/ubuntu-9.04-alternate-i386.iso /media/cdrom0
