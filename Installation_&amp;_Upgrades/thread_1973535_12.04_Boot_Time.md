### Post by owise1 on 2012-05-08
I did a multiple upgrade - 10.11 to 12.04 in stages and 12.04 boots in about 70sec on an eeePC 1005HA (Atom 270 with 1G ram -pretty basic netbook).  There is a delay of about 15- 20 secs due to a check on the file system on sda1.  Not sure why as shutdown cleanly.  Have a look as dmesg and see what happens during boot sequence. Open a terminal cntrl alt T and type dmesg and scroll to see what is taking a long time to start / initiate.  Your machine has pretty good specs so should be a racehorse compared to my old plonker!
