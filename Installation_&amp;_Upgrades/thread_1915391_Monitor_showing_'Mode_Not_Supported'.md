### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-01-26
Slow down a bit!  More details please; how did you do the upgrade, and from what version to what?  You can not upgrade from 10.04, if that is what you had, to 11.04 or 11.10 in one jump, so it will be useful to have more info.  You may not need to reinstall.

You may simply need to check if a proprietary driver is available for your graphic card.  Try logging in to **failsafe gnome**, if it's available at the login screen;  I'm still on 10.04 where it is certainly an option, but I'm not sure about 11.04 or 11.10.

If you get to a gnome desktop that way you can try the **additional/restricted drivers** utility from the menu, though I am afraid I am not sure how you get to it from the unity desktop.  Search around and I'm sure you'll find it, and there you may find a driver can be installed for your system.
