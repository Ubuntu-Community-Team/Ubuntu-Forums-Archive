### Post by LDRoamer on 2007-10-23
I have an network connected HP Photosmart 3200. I got it working but I found out that the HP driver suite hplip requires you to have snmp installed. It was not installed on  my machine. I got the idea for this from the troubleshooting section of the hplip web page:

[http://hplip.sourceforge.net/troubleshooting/network.html](http://hplip.sourceforge.net/troubleshooting/network.html)

Once I installed snmp I deleted the printer and reinstalled and it worked fine.  You do need to know the IP address of your printer as you need to enter it in manually (wasn't auto detected). Just thought I would post this in case it helps someone.
