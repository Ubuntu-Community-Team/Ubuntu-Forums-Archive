### Post by sudodus on 2014-04-27
Welcome to the Ubuntu Forums :-)

If 13.10 is working well, I suggest that you keep it (or reinstall it), and wait for 2-3 months until the worst bugs of 14.04 LTS have been squashed. Then test it live before installing. In most cases it will work, but in some cases there are problems with the driver for some hardware, and it is better to stay with an older version. The version 13.10 has end of life in July, and after that you should choose between 12.04.4 LTS and 14.04.1 LTS (download the new point release).
