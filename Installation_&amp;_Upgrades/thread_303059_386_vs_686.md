### Post by lloyd_b on 2006-11-19
In a word, performance.  The kernel compiled for 686 will incorporate instructions not available on the 386, and will have been optimized to take advantages of the other differences between the two types of processors.

Will you actually be able to notice a difference between the two?  Maybe, maybe not.
