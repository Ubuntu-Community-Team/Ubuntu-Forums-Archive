### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-03-28
Sorry, but you can't do that.  Distro updates can only be done from a normal version of ubuntu to the next version, eg, from 10.04 to 10.10, or from one LTS version to the next LTS version, eg, from 8.04 to 10.04.

Are you running 7.10, or jaunty jackalope, 9.04, as I am confused as to what you really have at the moment.

If you really have 7.10, which has not been supported for nearly two years, I suggest you totally forget about the distro upgrade path as it will be pretty well never ending.  I think you should bite the bullet, beg or borrow a 1GB USB key, and use that to install clean with either 10.04, the most recent LTS version, or 10.10, if you really must have the latest.  In another month 11.04 will appear, so you could even wait for that, if you want to.
