I am a newbie to Ubuntu too, but I am replying on the basis of current experience. I've been trying things out on a couple of old Dell laptops. One has just gone into the bin (trashcan) but the other is alive and kicking.
I found that the latest release 7.04 was too much for a laptop with 256 meg memory, it will install but the highest screen resolution it would give was 800 x 600, and it was very buggy.

I am optimistic that this is the future, I have XP on my workhorse desktop computer but I'm not going to be buying Vista!
