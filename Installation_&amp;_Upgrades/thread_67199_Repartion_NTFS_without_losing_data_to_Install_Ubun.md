### Post by aysiu on 2005-09-19
[QUOTE=johngotip]she doesn't want to lose her data[/QUOTE] Vulpus' link is a good one, but pay special attention to the bold and underlined #3: "you have backed up all your data."

Not backing up before repartitioning is like riding a motorcycle without a helmet. 99% of the time (or more) when you ride a motorcycle, you don't get into a serious accident, but if you do get into one, you're dead without a helmet. Likewise, 99% of the time you repartition, nothing will go wrong, but if you chance upon than 1%, all your data could be lost.

Back it up!
