I hope I've understood your question correctly...

I just installed Ubuntu 7.10 for a friend a couple of days ago (he didn't have a blank disk, and I didn't have a newer Live CD with me), and then updated through 8.04 to 8.10, and everything went fine.

Just skip all of the individual updates, and go directly for the distribution upgrades in both cases, and remember to change the "Release upgrade" to "normal" rather than "Long term support only" in Software Sources in 8.04, so that it shows the distribution upgrade to 8.10.

Good luck.
