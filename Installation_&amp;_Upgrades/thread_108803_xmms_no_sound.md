### Post by hca on 2005-12-27
Why do  I get no sound when playing a cd in xmms? On the playlist I can see that xmms finds my music on the cd. I can change between the tracks, and I can see the progress indicator moving. But I get no sound on the soundcard output socket.
It works fine to get sound when I play the cd in Ubuntu's cd-player, then why not in xmms?
All good ideas are highly appreciated

### Post by Perfect Storm on 2005-12-27
You need to change audio to digital.

open xmms and press <ctrl>+<p>

In audio I/O Plugins pick **CD Audio Player 1.2.10** and press **Configure**.

Under play mode pick **Digital audio extraction**
press ok and then press apply.
