Most people will not recommend that you upgrade. You will run into some issues - what issues, will depend on your hardware (for that matter, you may not have any issues, but that would be an exception).

It is unlikely that anyone can give you a cast iron guarantee that all will be hunky dory for you.

Read these pages, then make your own mind up.

[http://www.ubuntulinux.org/getubuntu/upgrading](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/getubuntu/upgrading)
[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/910](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/910)

There are features that you may not get by upgrading, eg ext4 unless you already have it. 

Either way, remember to back up everything that you wouldn't want to lose, before upgrading or reinstalling.
