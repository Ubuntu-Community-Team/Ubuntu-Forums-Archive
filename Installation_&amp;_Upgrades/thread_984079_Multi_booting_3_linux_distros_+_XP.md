### Post by bulldog on 2008-11-16
It depends, as always,on your hardware.
Do you have more than one hdd for instance.

I should install windows definitely as first,because if you don't,it will wipe out your GRUB from the MBR,so you can't start any other OS.

How large your windows partitions would be,is up to you of course.
I would say,make three 10GB partitions for each of your linux OS's,for the / partition.
Make a swap about 2GB and maybe a separate /home for all three distro's,but how large it would be,is up to you and your hardware.
