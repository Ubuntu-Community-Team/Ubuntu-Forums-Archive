### Post by ubuntu27 on 2007-10-14
Gusty is not en LTS.

Now, if you want to upgrade to Gusty G. You will have to upgrade to Edgy, then Feisty, then Gutsy. 
[URL="https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes"]
Upgrade Notes[/URL]

> Skipping versions is not advised and can cause a lot of damage to your installation. If you are using an earlier version, and want to 'skip' a version, the only safe way is to backup your data and do a fresh install, or to progressively upgrade to each successive version. An example of this would be if your are running 6.06 (Dapper) and want to upgrade to 7.04 (Feisty). You would have to first upgrade to 6.10 (Edgy), and then again to 7.04 (Feisty) or simply do a clean/fresh install of 7.04 (Feisty



If you do the above method of upgrade, you will use waste bandwidth and time. It is better to do a backup of your files, and do a fresh install of Gutsy.
