### Post by pjsmetana on 2009-12-08
Many apologies for the late response, but I have just finished installing UNR 9.10 on an HP Mini 110 1030nr. 

Heres what I ran into... 

My initial install was a dual boot with XP. Worked great except internal mic. There was an easy fix posted on this forum, and it worked flawlessly. After a few days trying it out I decided it was just way too superior to winXP to even allow XP to take 1/2 my HDD space. I then reinstalled UNR 9.10, this time taking the whole drive in 1 partition. Sure, I was expecting the mic issue again, but what I did not expect was the wireless to stop working as well. I had to learn how to use ndiswrapper and install a windows driver for the low powered Broadcom wireless card. It was the only way that worked, so if you do this, save yourself some time trying to do what everyone else says to do what 'worked for them' and just go the ndiswrapper route. Its much easier than I expected. 

Right now, I am running UNR 9.10 with flawless execution. It is extremely fast and the interface is fantastic. When using firefox, i reccomend skipping all the addons and just using the F11 key to get your whole screen for the browser. 

I am extremely happy that I kept UNR 9.10 even with the few issues I had. It is just so much faster at everything, as compared to WinXP.
