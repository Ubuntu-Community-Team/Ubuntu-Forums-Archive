### Post by nocturn on 2005-07-07
Laptops are horrible for Linux

Most of them have nice logos on the casing saying things like "Acpi 1.0" or any other standard.  Unfortunately, a big part of them do not support these standards but instead default to an implementation that will only work with XP.

I have been windows-free for 6 years now with little hardware problems.  Yet, when I decided to buy a laptop, I spend a month selecting machines that would work well.  I'm waiting for the delivery of mine now, it should work well.

Please do not judge Linux by these issues, it is very hard to support hardware when nobody sticks to standards or provides you with documentation.
This kind of thing will only get better when Linux reaches critical mass and OEM's have to take notice.
