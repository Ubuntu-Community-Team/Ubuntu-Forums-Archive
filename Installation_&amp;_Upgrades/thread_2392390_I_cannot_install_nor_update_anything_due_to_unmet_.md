### Post by QIII on 2018-05-20
Hello!

17.04 has passed its EOL (End Of Life).  It is no longer supported and its repositories have been moved.  You won't be able to update from them easily.

An EOL upgrade is not terribly likely to end in success without some issues.  I would suggest that you back up you important files and re-install a supported version, an LTS would be best, which would be 16.04 or 18.04.

Cheers!
