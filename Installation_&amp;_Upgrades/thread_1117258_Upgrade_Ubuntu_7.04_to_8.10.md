### Post by lisati on 2009-04-05
The "easy" way would be to use the update manager to step you through it. (Down side: a good internet connection is useful)

Another way is to backup your data and settings, replace your existing version with a fresh install of the latest version (you can order a free CD), and restore your data and settings (Down side: you might have to reinstall some applications and/or drivers)

Other forum users might have suggestions as well.
