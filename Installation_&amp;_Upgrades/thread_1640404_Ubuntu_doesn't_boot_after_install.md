### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-12-08
Grub2 is installed to the boot sector of sda2 rather than the MBR of your HDD.

You need to chroot your install, purge the existing Grub and re-install it to the MBR.

Follow **Why Chroot?** section in this guide.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1581099](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1581099)
courtesy of forum member **drs305**
