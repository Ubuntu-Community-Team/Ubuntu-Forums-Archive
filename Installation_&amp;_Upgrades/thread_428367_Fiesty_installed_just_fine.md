### Post by Lord Landis on 2007-04-30
I just wanted to pop in and talk about my Fiesty installs.  I put it on both of my systems yesterday - a clean install of Kubuntu on my laptop (Dell Latitude P3-700 w/ 384 megs of RAM and an ATI Rage Mobility) and an upgrade from Edgy on my desktop box (custom built P4-2.26 w/ 1 gig of RAM, SoundBlaster Audigy 2, nVidia GeForce 5200 [I think], and 380 gigs of storage on two drives).  The desktop box runs regular Ubuntu with KDE added on as the default window manager.  Both 'installs' worked more or less perfectly.

The only problem I encountered was that my OpenOffice menu items were missing after the upgrade, but I fixed that by removing Ubuntu's packages and installing from the RPMS using the methods posted elsewhere on this forum.

I'm sure that lots of people have had little to no difficulty with their upgrades, but given the number of posts where something is broken or something just didn't work out the way they wanted to, I thought I'd jump in and say that it worked just fine for me.  This may also be because I waited two weeks after the release before upgrading, but I doubt it.

Anyway, now I just have to wait for KDE 4 to come out...
