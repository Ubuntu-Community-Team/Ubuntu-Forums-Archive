As far as I konw, they are both exactly the same. I think, in fact, that GParted is just a GUI for Partman. I've personally installed Ubuntu several times, and the partition manager has been wonderful; no loss of data. Just to be safe, you may want to defrag (if it's a windows hard drive). Anything that is *extremely* important should always be backed up anyway, but the chance of you losing something, especially if you defrag the drive, is extremely low.
