### Post by darkod on 2010-05-29
Personally, it comes down to visual in my opinion. Especially if you have a laptop (with a bigger screen), not a netbook.
On my netbook I install UNR 9.10 when it came out without trying how it looked first. I didn't like the menu look, and after 2 days installed the Desktop 32bit version over it.

Even though it's just a netbook with 10" screen and 2GB ram, it works just fine with the desktop version.

I suggest to try both version in Try Ubuntu mode, and see what you like best. Otherwise, both will work fine I guess.
