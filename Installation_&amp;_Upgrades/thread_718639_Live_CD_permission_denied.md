### Post by Pumalite on 2008-03-08
Install with the Alternate CD. Download from here:
[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)
Mark box below sign 'Start Download'
Follow this guide:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto?highlight=%28burn%29%7C%28](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto?highlight=%28burn%29%7C%28)
Do md5sum on the iso, burn at 4x or less, use CD-R media of good quality, check for CD integrity after burn and before install.
Clean the lens in your burner, just in case.
