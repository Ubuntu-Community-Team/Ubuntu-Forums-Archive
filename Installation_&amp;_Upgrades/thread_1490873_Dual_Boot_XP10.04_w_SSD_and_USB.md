### Post by jumping_snake on 2010-05-22
Interesting Case: I tried to dual boot XP and 10.04. When I installed 10.04 after installing XP to the SSD, I created a bootable USB stick and ran the 10.04 install without a hitch. However, after I removed the USB stick after Ubuntu had finished installing, GRUB wouldn't load as it appears that it may have been installed to the USB stick instead of the SSD drive. XP would load automatically with no mention of GRUB. If I plugged the USB stick back in, GRUB would load.

However, after I removed that Ubuntu partition on the SSD and installed 10.04 from CD, everything is currently working fine. 

I just wanted to share this just in case it happens to others and in case this isn't a known bug. I can post my hardware if needed. Solved...kinda :)
