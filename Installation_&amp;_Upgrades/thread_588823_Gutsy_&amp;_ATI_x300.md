### Post by Fire_Chief on 2007-10-26
I fought with the aticonfig and the built-in screens & graphics panel but to no avail. I finally left those two alone and configured the dual monitor setup from the ATI Catalyst Control Panel (under Applications -> Accessories). It was the only one which so far has allowed the setup to work nearly perfectly (or at least as expected) for a dual monitor spanned desktop setup.
