### Post by 23meg on 2006-05-30
If you wipe all your partitions all your personal data will be lost. If you have a separate /home partition you can keep it and instruct Dapper to use it at install time, so all your previous settings and data will be intact. Alternatively you can make a backup of your home folder somewhere and when the install is done, copy it over your new home folder.
