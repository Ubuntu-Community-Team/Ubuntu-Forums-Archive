### Post by bluloo on 2018-03-19
Not sure where to post as this is more of an FYI. If it belongs elsewhere feel free to move or let me know and I'll delete and repost - 

 I'm running an X1 carbon G3, Ubuntu 17.10, gnome 3.26.2 and kernel v4.15.9. 
After updating to v4.15.11-041511.201803190530 today, click and drag, double click two finger scrolling etc was broken.
Downgraded to 4.15.9 and all was well again.

FYI in case others are considering a similar update/test, (particularly because touchpad functionality under Wayland and 17.10 is already a problem for many)
