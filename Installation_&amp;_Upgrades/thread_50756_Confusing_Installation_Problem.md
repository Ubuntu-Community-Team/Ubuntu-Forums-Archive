### Post by FLeiXiuS on 2005-07-22
Just burn the image as normal.  No need to set any specific parameters.  Ubuntu has the boot structure pre-setup.  

I believe the "Burn Image" option is under one of the menu's at the top.  Browse around through "File" and "Burn".  It's there some where depending on your version of nero.


Also give a MD5sum a shot to make sure your disc is complete without any errors.  Many times users download a corrupted version.  Read the wiki for howto instructions on checking the MD5 of your downloaded ISO.
