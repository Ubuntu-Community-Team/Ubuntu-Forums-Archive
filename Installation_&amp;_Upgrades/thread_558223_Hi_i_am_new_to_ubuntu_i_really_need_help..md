### Post by R.Bucky on 2007-09-23
the BIOS - when the computer first starts up look for something like "To enter BIOS press F11" or one of the "F" keys. No GUI or mouse usage here... Use your arrow keys or follow the instructions for moving around at the bottom of the BIOS screen. It looks scary at first. Move up "Boot from CD" and all of your worries are over. Boot up using the Ubuntu CD.
