### Post by finwake on 2009-11-14
Very easy to do.  Boot the Install CD (or USB) and follow the instructions that come up when you get to the partitioning section.  The installer will know you have 9.04 and will give you the option to resize that partition or to use free space on your drive to create a new partition for 9.10.  

After installing, when you boot up, the GRUB bootloader will let you pick between your 9.04 and 9.10 systems.
