### Post by Sef on 2006-05-10
Yes, you can do that.  

If you're running Gnome, then open the terminal (Applications > Accessories > Terminal)

sudo aptitude install Kubuntu-desktop

At the log in just click on session and pick your desktop.

Note: use aptitude instead of apt-get as it is easier to uninstall if you decide to do so.
