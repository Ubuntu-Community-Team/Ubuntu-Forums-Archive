### Post by Tomosaur on 2006-08-08
1: You can remove kernels you don't want, or you can just stop them from showing up in the Grub menu (the thing you see at startup). Take a look at my script if you want to limit the number of kernels it shows.

2: Yes, your hard drive can be this size.

3: With 1GB of RAM, you'd probably only need 512mb of swap. A while ago, most people would have just said 'you need double your RAM', but that's not really the case any more. If would say you'd need 1GB at MAXIMUM for your swap, but I shouldn't think you'd need more than 512mb.

Hope that helps.
