### Post by sandyd on 2012-08-27
Once you install Ubuntu, Open the Unity Dash (the Ubuntu logo at the top right), and type in "hardware drivers" or "jockey"

Then, open the Hardware Drivers utility and install the Nvidia drivers listed.

^^ as a result, the drivers needed are not indicated as they can be installed directly in Ubuntu
