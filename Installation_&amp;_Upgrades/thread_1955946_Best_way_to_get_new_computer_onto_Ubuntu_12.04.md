### Post by Curtis6767 on 2012-04-10
I've been using the Beta release for about 10 days and have had only minor problems which have been solved through re-boot.

It is a pain to upgrade and took me several hours even though I have DSL because downloading the upgrade is just the first part of a two step operation.

12.04 LTS stable release is just a few days away and most would advise you to wait on that release and some would say wait for a month afterward just to be safe.

Really no sense pushing the river when the final release date is so close, IMHO.
