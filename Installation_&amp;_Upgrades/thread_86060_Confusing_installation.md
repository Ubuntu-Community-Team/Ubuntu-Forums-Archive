### Post by SickTwist on 2005-11-04
After all of the packages (programs) are installed the installer will tell you that it needs to reboot and to remove the installation CD. When it reboots, Ubuntu will configure all of the programs that were just installed (there will be a progress bar displayed during this process). When it has finished you should see a log-in screen that says "Ubuntu" and has a place to enter your username that you picked during the installation. Type your username and press "Enter". Then type your password and press "Enter". Your session will then start.
