### Post by dstew on 2009-07-16
If you have a partition set aside for Ubuntu, then during the installation at step 4, "Prepare Disk Space", check the "Specify partitions manually (advanced)" selection. Then you can designate your third partition for Ubuntu, and leave your other partitions untouched.
