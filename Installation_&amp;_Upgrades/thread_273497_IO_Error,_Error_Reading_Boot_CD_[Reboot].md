### Post by wpshooter on 2006-10-08
How are you burning the CDs ?  Are you burning at 4x or slower ?

How are you checking the MD5 ?  Are you checking the integrity of the CDs by running the verification function found on the initial Ubuntu boot screen menu ?

Have you tried cleaning your CD drive ?

Have you tried a different and/or better grade CD ?

Are you using CD-R or CD-RW ?  I recommend using CR-RW media.

Have you checked to make sure your CD drive has the latest and greatest firmware installed ?

Have you tried switching the CD drive to a different motherboard port/connector ?

Have you tried checking the drive cable connections ?

Have you possibly tried switching to another CD drive cable ?
