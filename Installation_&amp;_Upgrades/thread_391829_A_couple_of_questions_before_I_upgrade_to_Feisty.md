### Post by barney_1 on 2007-03-23
First off, you should wait until Feisty has a Release Candidate available.  Right now it is still a bit unstable.

Secondly, you customizations, and customs installed packages will make the change for the most part.  There will probably be some that have to be re-installed but nothing too major (hopefully).

I guess if you want a stable system you should stay with Edgy (if it's stable right now).  If you need some of the new features or just want to have the newest thing just wait until April and Feisty will be here.
