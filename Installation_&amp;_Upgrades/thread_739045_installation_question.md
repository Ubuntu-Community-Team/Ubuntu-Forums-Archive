### Post by banewman on 2008-03-29
You make a partition then from the options at the right you select a mount point.
The best partitioning format I've found is to have a root partition(/) of 5-10Gig and a swap of 1Gig and the rest as a home partition - that way if you reinstall your data is safe where it is and your settings for firefox etc are retained.
