### Post by unlimitedz on 2009-07-14
Are you dual booting windows and ubuntu?  If windows isn't started correctly (i.e. you power off during the startup process of windows) the NTFS partition puts a "safemode" flag on it and Linux can't mount it.  This may be your issue.

For the graphics issue, you probably have to enable third party drivers.  System-Admin-Hardware devices, and see if there are anything in there.
