### Post by hajk on 2006-10-29
Just completed fresh installs of Edgy on two computers, AMD64 (x86_64) and i386. I have specified in both cases "en_US" (US-English keyboard, etc), but notice that the default locale on my AMD64 system is "en_US.ISO-8859-15", whereas on the i386 system it is "en_US.UTF-8".
Strange, I thought that Ubuntu had opted for UTF-8 as the default character set in all its versions...
