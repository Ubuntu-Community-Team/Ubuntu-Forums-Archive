### Post by arpanaut on 2012-11-11
While in Update Manager choose settings then under updates menu item change to:
[ATTACH]227047[/ATTACH]
close that out then refresh your repositories by hitting check that should bring up the option to upgrade to the new LTS.

Before you do that make sure that your 10.04 is totally updated and back-up your important data, just in case something goes wrong with the upgrade.
Better safe than sorry!
