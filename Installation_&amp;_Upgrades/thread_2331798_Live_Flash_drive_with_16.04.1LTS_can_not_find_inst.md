### Post by ubfan1 on 2016-07-26
Any day now the  14.04 LTS will get the 16.04 upgrade offer.  Be careful -- what you describe doing may wipe your entire disk.  Maybe the fix is finally in to stop offering a "reinstall" in such situations when the real offer is to "wipe disk and reinstall".  The upgrade from a disk may be done by changing the software sources -- sofware updater has a settings button at the bottom left of the window, and you can add the CDROM to the upgrade sources.  You may get an offer to add the CD to the list anyway when it is present and you are running.
