### Post by OldSmoky2 on 2011-02-17
I have a Toshiba NB205 and had been happily running 10.04 for a few weeks on it when 10.10 was released. I hit the upgrade button and had major problems with it. I lost sound and, worse, the computer would freeze whenever I wasn't hitting the touchpad every 10 seconds. It was very slow in general. I just went back and reinstalled 10.04 Netbook Remix, which runs great on it. A lot of people had issues with 10.10 Netbook, and I figure I'll just upgrade when they get the bugs worked out. And 10.04 is supported into 2013 anyway.
