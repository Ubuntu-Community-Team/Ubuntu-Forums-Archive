### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-02-02
It is possible, but it can be a real pain in the butt, and to be honest, using a lot of small, older drives is a setup for disaster because if one fails, you can be in a heap of trouble.
If you're still interested in proceeding, check out [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/RAID1+LVM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/RAID1+LVM) as a good place to start.
If I were you, I would just wait until you get a new hard disk to do a regular install.  If you choose to give this a run, don't expect much out of it and don't put any faith in its reliability.  I would only try it for the fun and experience, not to get a usable system out of it.
That's about all I can offer you.  Good luck!
