### Post by rjvdkooij on 2009-01-10
hello, 

please note that (!!) More than one possible primary device found is an X11 notice, not an error. 
Specifying the devices BusID in xorg.conf seems to offer a partial solution.

Section "Device"
    Identifier     "Device0"
    Driver         "nvidia" 
    VendorName     "NVIDIA Corporation"
    BoardName      "GeForce 8500 GT"   
    BusID          "PCI:2:0:0"         
EndSection                             

Section "Device"
    Identifier     "Device1"
    Driver         "nvidia"
    VendorName     "NVIDIA Corporation"
    BoardName      "GeForce 6150SE nForce 430"
    BusID          "PCI:0:13:0"
EndSection

off course after that you might have to figure out how to activate Twinview/Xinerama and tell your display manager it should double it's efforts
