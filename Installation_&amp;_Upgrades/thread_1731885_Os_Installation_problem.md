### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-17
If booth OS installed are having issues, you ***may*** be facing a HDD failure.

You can find out by booting off the LiveCD, Selecting 'TRY' and then menu to System >> Administration >> Disk Utility.

Once you select your hard drive, please look at the SMART status (this come from the drive itself and is not affected by the OS being run).

Here is an example of a really bad SMART status:

[IMG]http://img225.imageshack.us/img225/7057/diskfail03.png[/IMG]

Please let us know the SMART status of your hard drive.
