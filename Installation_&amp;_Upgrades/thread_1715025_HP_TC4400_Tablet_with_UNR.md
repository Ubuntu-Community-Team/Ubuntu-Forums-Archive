### Post by nymusicman on 2011-07-21
The HP Tablet works almost flawlessly with Ubuntu. Can't seem to get screen "buttons" working, but wacom works fine. You have to look up wacom rotation to get that working but it's really not that hard. Some software I have found invaluable to my TC4400 experience is the following:

Cellwriter - for handwriting/virtual keyboard

Jarnal - is probably one of the best note taking apps (feature wise), although it's still no onenote replacement. Ah... if only basket had tablet support that might one day come close.

easystroke - is a great shortcut tool for stylus

magick-rotation - for auto rotation [https://launchpad.net/magick-rotation](https://launchpad.net/magick-rotation)

You can even get the fingerprint reader to work.
