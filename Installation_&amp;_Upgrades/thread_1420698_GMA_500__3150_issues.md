### Post by snowpine on 2010-03-03
I'd strongly advise against the GMA500 based on comments I've read in these forums (use the Search feature at the top right of your screen) and this wikipedia article: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intel_GMA#GMA_500](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intel_GMA#GMA_500)

I've had good luck with my two Intel GMA 950 netbooks (Asus and Dell); this card is well supported by all Linux distributions (though it is not particularly powerful for full-screen video). If watching video is a high priority, I've heard great things about the new Nvidia Ion platform.
