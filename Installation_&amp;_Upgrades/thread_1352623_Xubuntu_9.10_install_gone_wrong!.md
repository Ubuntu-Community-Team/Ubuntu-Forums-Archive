### Post by Gene Rodgers on 2009-12-11
Do you have a disk which allows booting from disk?  You should put the disk in your drive, reboot your computer and make sure you have your cd/dvdrom set as 1st boot option, Hd the last. The screen will come up with language choice first, then choose install xubuntu to hd. Don't choose any encryption offered. You can encrypt your files after installation. The rest is simple, thats why it is the KISS method.
