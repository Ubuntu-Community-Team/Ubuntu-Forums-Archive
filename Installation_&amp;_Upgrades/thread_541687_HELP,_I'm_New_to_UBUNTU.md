### Post by Game_boy on 2007-09-03
Virtual PC 2007 only officially supports Windows, OS/2 and DOS as guest OSs.

Crashing on install may be due to graphics drivers. Instead of clicking start/install, try looking at the bottom for a menu with an option called VESA. Switch to that VESA and try again.

Also, try and use the latest version of Ubuntu, 7.04. You can download and burn it from many sources.
