### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-06-15
First off, 12.04 is too large to fit on a "CD".  So, trying to burn a CD is fruitless.

Second, I've tried myself to burn a DVD of it, and every time, the DVD gets rejected and the burning program says it's the wrong medium.  It wants a CD, but as I said, CDs are way too small.

I've ended up creating a USB instead -- but that doesn't solve the DVD problem.

And, before you asking, yes I know how to burn DVDs, as I have them of other 13.04-type Linux distros (i.e., Mint 15).
