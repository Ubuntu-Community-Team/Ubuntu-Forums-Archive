### Post by cotcot on 2006-07-09
Upgrade is safe as long as you backup your partition (partimage). If the upgrade fails (look to the forum threads about upgrade and you will see that a backup is mandatory) you can reset your old installation back. It works fine with partimage. I have done this 3 times to test partimage before i rely on it.
