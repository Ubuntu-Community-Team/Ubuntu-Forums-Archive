### Post by Pumalite on 2008-06-11
Download from here:
[http://www.edubuntu.org/Download](http://www.edubuntu.org/Download)
Burn the iso to disk as an image:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto?highlight=%28burn%29%7C%28](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto?highlight=%28burn%29%7C%28)
You can install on top of the old one. During the installation, choose 'Manual' and pick the old partitions. You can ignore /swap
