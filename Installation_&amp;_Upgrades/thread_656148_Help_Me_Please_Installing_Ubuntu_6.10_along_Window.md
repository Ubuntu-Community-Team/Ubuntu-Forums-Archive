I am fairly new too, but I don't understand why people get so hung up on using old distro's!? I get the stability and support argument, but from my experience (especially having ATI) I had a horrid time getting my first distro to work, although to be honest feisty (the latest distro at the time) was rubbish for me and a mission to install, I had to start with dapper and update through to feisty. since edgy and feisty discs had the same thing occurring as you and text based installation wouldn't work either for my graphics card.
