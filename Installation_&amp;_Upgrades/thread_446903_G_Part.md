### Post by hal8000 on 2007-05-17
Before resizing any windows partition, you should always defrag it first so that all the free space is at the end of the partition.
This is possibly why gparted is taking so long, it has to reallocate all the data, as a defrag under windows cam also take hours it may be prudent to wait a few hours longer, turn off your monitor to save energy but leave the computer on and check on it every 30 minutes or so.
