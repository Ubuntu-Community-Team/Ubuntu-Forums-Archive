### Post by dbroenni on 2008-05-01
I have managed to resolve the problem. 
In the end the ATI restricted driver fglrx caused the wine/crossover crash. (Upgrading with EnvyNG did not work with my setup.)
I resolved the problem in two steps (at the cost of 3D support, however):

- Switch from restricted ATI/flgrx driver to open source driver (which did not work in 7.10 with my Ati FireGL 5200 in Lenovo Thinkpad T60p)

- Resolve Wine/Crossover problem with memory allocation as suggested here: [http://wiki.winehq.org/PreloaderPageZeroProblem](http://wiki.winehq.org/PreloaderPageZeroProblem)

For me this is OK as I don't need 3D in most cases (except for googleearth...)
