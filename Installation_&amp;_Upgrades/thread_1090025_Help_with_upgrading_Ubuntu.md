### Post by simtaalo on 2009-03-07
i don't think that version is supported so you *probably* can't upgrade to 8.10 that way.

you will have to do a fresh install of 8.10, if you don't have your /home on a seperate partition then you will have to back-up your data then do the install. when you do the install you can make a seperate partition for /home which means that in the future you can reinstall without having to touch any of your personal data.
