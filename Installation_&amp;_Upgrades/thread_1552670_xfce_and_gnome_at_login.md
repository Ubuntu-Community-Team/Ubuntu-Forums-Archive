### Post by lemming465 on 2010-08-15
Try going to System -> Administration -> Login screen, and change the radio button to "show the screen for choosing who will log in".  Then when you click on a user, you should have a row of selectors at the bottom of the login screen turn on to choose session type (Xfce or Gnome), language, etc.
