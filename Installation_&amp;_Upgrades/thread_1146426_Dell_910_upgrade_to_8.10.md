### Post by snowpine on 2009-05-02
You would have to do a fresh install of 8.10. 8.04 is the most recent release for the special Dell version of Ubuntu; it is a "long term support" release and will be supported through 2011.

There are some minor hardware support issues for non-Dell 8.10 on the Mini 9. A good tutorial: [www.ubuntumini.com](www.ubuntumini.com)
