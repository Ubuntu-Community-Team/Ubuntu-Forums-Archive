### Post by johannes on 2006-06-28
I guess it means that they are developed to work by default in Red Hat and Suse. That does not mean they can't be used in e.g Ubuntu.

Also, i think they don't want you asking their Support on how to make them work in Ubuntu, but you can ask them how to do it in Red Hat and Suse.

If you need help installing them in Ubuntu see [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI)
