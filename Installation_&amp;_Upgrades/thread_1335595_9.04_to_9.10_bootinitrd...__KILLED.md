The solution was quite simple.  The laptop would not shut off, so removed battery. Downloaded iso file on desktop. Next restarted laptop and halted in grub to bootup in 9.04 recovery.  This was successful.  Next I put in the iso DVD and it stated that I had a pkg error and offered a command to rectify it. It did it's thing and I checked system monitor to discover I now had 9.10.  I restarted the laptop and it worked.  I had a problem with no sound, but was resolved by checking this forum.  Life is great.
