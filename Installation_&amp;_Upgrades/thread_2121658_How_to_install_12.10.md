### Post by darkod on 2013-03-02
Try downloading another ISO file, best with torrent since it checks for corruption during download. Burn the DVD at very low speed, even at 1x. The image is not big and it won't take too much time, but the burn is better on low speeds.

Have you had any video issues earlier? Does it support your video card good?

I have noticed few 12.10 threads where it doesn't install grub2 on the MBR, so it looks like it can't boot. If you don't have another bootloader it might install but not boot until you add grub2 to the MBR from live mode.
