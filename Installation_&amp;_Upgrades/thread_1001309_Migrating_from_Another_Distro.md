### Post by omegamike3 on 2008-12-05
While I'm not terribly familiar with Mandriva, I would assume that just like most other distro migrates/upgrades, you would just want to backup your home directory and then move everything back after the Studio install. This should move all of your configuration back. Granted, that's assuming as well that Enlightenment keeps any configuration settings in the home directory as well. I find that rsync works best for a quick onetime backup of your home directory.
