### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-01-18
Strongly advise that unless BOTH of the following are true, you do NOT attempt updating the BIOS:
1) You have a way to save off the current working BIOS to external media
2) You have a way to boot the PC and restore the working BIOS from the external media -- should the BIOS flashing fail

If these are not BOTH true, you are risking turning your PC into an "electric brick" by flashing the BIOS.

Might be better if you told us why you want to do this -- as flashing a BIOS is rarely a good solution.
