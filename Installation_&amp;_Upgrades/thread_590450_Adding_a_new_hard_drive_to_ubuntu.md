### Post by Pumalite on 2007-10-24
Use Gparted Live CD for your erasing/repartitioning. You can format your store drive ext3 since you are not using Windows. As for installation of Ubuntu; a good scheme is:
10 GB for '/'
2x RAM up to 1 GB for /swap
The rest for /home
