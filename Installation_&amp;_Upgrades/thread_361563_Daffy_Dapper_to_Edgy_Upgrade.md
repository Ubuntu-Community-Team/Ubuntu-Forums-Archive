### Post by dannyboy79 on 2007-02-14
well I am sure this is exactly what you are not wanting to hear but my guess is that your system will never be back to normal. even if you do straighten out all the library issues and dependencies who knows that when you click on something that you haven't clicked on in awhile that you won't then have to fix that also. my best advice I can give you is to save all the critical config files that you have customized (ie smb.conf, fstab, interfaces, proftpd.conf if you have one, and any other config files you have modified since installing dapper) as well as all you personal files, then do a new install. that's going to give you the best results.
