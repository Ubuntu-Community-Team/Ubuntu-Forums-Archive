### Post by PaulW2U on 2019-06-26
[Old Ubuntu Releases]("http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/releases/") is the official archive but it only contains ISOs that have been 'released'.

For each release, taking into account the various flavours and architectures, several thousand images are built, some of which are not even bootable and/or include serious bugs. As far as I am aware they are simply deleted from [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/) a few days after a new image is built as there is little to be gained from keeping them. Most of course would never be downloaded and at certain stages of the development process there is little change if any from one day to the next.
