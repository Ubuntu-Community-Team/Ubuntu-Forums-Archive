### Post by Arkitekt on 2010-03-13
easiest way is to press Alt-F2 and enter ```
update-manager -d
```

you will need to have an internet connection though for that to work, does ethernet work?

I don't see the HP 5101 on the list of netbooks on the UNR compatibility page ([https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks)) but you can check and see if it is listed somewhere.
