### Post by dstew on 2009-06-09
Are you sure the DVD is good? Does it boot on other computers? Did you try the "Check Disk Integrity" menu item? I don't know much about burning DVD's, but if you burn a CD too fast, often the disks are no good. You have to burn an image at a slow speed, 4x maximum on a CD. High speed burns are fine for video or music, but not for disk images.
