### Post by oldsoundguy on 2007-12-22
I want to be one to thank the moderators and contributors to these forums and wish a happy holiday season to ALL

I started using Mandrake 6 years ago and moved to Fedora as soon as core 1 came out.  However,  after the treatment I saw of newbies on the Fedora Forum, became disillusioned with the support. (Too many snooty "why did you do that dumb thing" responses.)(sufficient that I blocked responses from several of the supposed moderators!)  PLUS Fedora really SUX when it comes to hardware support for some reason .. totally neglecting the wireless network users for just one example.  AND their video drivers leave a LOT to be desired.

I had two friends recommend that I investigate Ubuntu and for that I will be forever grateful!  I now have 3 running machines that sing and dance (and talk to each other and to a pair of Windows boxes .. AND print from 5 printers in 3 different locations .. wirelessly!)  .. no not gaming machines, but they WORK and ALL of the hardware works.  You can't ask for much more than that from a FREE system with FREE support.  PLUS the fact that I don't have to spend 6 hours a week on each running cleanup and protection programs to be able to stay up and running!
