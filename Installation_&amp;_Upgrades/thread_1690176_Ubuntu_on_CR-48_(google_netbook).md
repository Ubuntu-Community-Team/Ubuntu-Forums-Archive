### Post by Limitlesschannels on 2011-03-18
I actually ran this through the other day.  The download took ages but everything ran silky-smooth and I am shocked by how well ubuntu runs on this thing.

The script works like a charm, just make sure you plug yours in before carrying it out, even with the killer battery life I wouldn't chance it.

With just a few tweaks to get the multitouch working (I used this link [HERE]("http://bigbrovar.aoizora.org/index.php/2011/01/12/enable-multitouch-support-for-clickpad-on-ubuntu-10-10/")) and some updates and remapping of the keys, it's a great machine.  Only thing I haven't quite gotten working yet is the horizontal multitouch scrolling, but no big deal.
