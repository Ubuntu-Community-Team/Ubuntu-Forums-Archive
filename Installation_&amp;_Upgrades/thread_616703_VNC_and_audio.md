VNC - VNC does not have sound.  I don't know if it's possible.  I do think esound (gnome), and arts (kde) sound daemons can transfer sound between systems, but I've not tried these.  There are some other sound daemons also.  I don't think this is well worked out or general.

Creating CDs -- I assume you'd use a CD Burning program like K3B.  There is also a CD/DVD creator that comes with gnome.
