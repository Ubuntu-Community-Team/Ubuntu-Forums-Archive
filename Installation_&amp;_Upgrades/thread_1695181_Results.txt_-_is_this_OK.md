### Post by oldfred on 2011-02-25
I usually only glance at the unknown boot loaders.

The  script has signatures for most boot loaders and tries to match. With new partition schemes or moving partitions, data may be left in the partition boot sector. Then the script is just reading old data, not related to a boot loader or boot issues.

I do not see anything in boot script. Lots of details so others may. But with one system, grub does not show menu, so it may be booting and a video issue or other BIOS parameter issue. Try shift and see it menu appears. Ask if nVidia as that often has black screen issue on first boot.
