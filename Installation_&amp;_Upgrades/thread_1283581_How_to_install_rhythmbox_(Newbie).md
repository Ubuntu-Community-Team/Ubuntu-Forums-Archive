### Post by scragar on 2009-10-05
You deleted rhythmbox?

If you open the package manager by going to system>administration>Synaptic
You can click search, type in **rhythmbox**, in the results right click rhythmbox.
Choose reinstall or install(only one of the two will be available, obviously).
Click apply at the top right.
Check it worked.
