### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-04-05
Understand, that if you choose to do an upgrade, their is no option to roll-back to 11.04.  It will overwrite what you have in place now.

Since 12.04 is STILL in beta, I would advise against doing this until it gets released -- especially since there is no way to "go back" to what you had earlier.
