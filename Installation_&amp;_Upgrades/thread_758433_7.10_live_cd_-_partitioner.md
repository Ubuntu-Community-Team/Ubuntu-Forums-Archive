### Post by mikewhatever on 2008-04-18
Follow this tutorial [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installing](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installing)

You must have a swap partition, but don't have to have a separate /home.
I'd go with the following manual sizes:
/ ~ 10 GB
swap ~ 1 GB
/home ~ whether you make it and the size is really up to you

The guided option resizes one of the existing partitions, and automatically creates / and swap. You don't get to choose the side of the slider.
