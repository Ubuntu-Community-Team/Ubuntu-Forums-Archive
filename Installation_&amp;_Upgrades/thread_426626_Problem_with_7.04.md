### Post by spd106 on 2007-04-29
Evolution is quite well integrated into the gnome desktop so I'm not too surprised you opened a can of worms here.

I suggest that you try to re-install the ubuntu-desktop meta-package. This will show you which packages should be installed. Whether you want them all or not depends on your preferences, but if you proceed it should rectify your problems.
