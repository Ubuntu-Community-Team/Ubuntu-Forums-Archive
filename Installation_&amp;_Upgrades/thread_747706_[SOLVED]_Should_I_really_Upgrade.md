### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-04-06
It's really your call.  If you are happy with it, then stay.  Over time it may become more difficult to get support if something does break since it is hard to help people with non-up-to-date systems.  Feisty has an 18 month support cycle, so it is good until the fall of this year.  Hardy Heron will be an LTS release, so it may be worth your time to do a fresh install of Hardy when it is released, then just stay there for two years or so until another LTS comes along, or even for its 3 year support cycle.
