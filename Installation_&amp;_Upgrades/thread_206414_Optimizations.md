### Post by mibadt on 2006-06-30
3 recommendations:
1. ADjust your  moniter refresh rate. In KUbuntu (which I use) just right click in he middle of the background (without open applications) and select display. Might be similar in Ubuntu.
2. Check your memore usage and unnecessary processes by using (from terminal):
sudo top
sudo free
3. Recompile ypur kernel (at least to reflect processor type and the use of all your memory).
