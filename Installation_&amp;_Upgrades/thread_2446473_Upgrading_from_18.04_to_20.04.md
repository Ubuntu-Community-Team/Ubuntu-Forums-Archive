### Post by Impavidus on 2020-07-01
The upgrade process will be officially released with 20.04.1, but upgrading is already possible. Call it experimental, but it should be quite reliable by now. It's mainly so that people who like stability (and therefore use an LTS release) will only get the upgrade offered once the annoying bugs are gone. Else most of them would upgrade right away and complain about the many bugs (because who reads the list of known issues?).
