### Post by podunk on 2006-10-22
Only if you click the wrong thing, or the power flickers, or something like that. 

Is there data on your drive you can't live without? The vast and I do mean vast majority of folks that shrink their windows partitions do so with no problem at all - but if there is a glitch - whether it's because of something you did, or a hiccup - your data will be gone.

If you have some data you can't live without -- you should back up - irregardless of whether or not you install Linux.

That warning given - it's a very straight forward process. The installer does a very good job. Defragment several times before you start. See this page for a how to install - complete with 8x10 glossy photos. :-)
