### Post by coffeecat on 2011-06-21
Well - to answer your original question. Install compizconfig-settings-manager. In ccsm, General > General Options > Desktop Size tab. Change the horizontal and vertical sizes, say horizontal = 10 and vertical = 2 for your 20. They're called workspaces in Unity.
