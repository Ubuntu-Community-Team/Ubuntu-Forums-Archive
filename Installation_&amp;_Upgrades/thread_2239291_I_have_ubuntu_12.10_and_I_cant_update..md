### Post by QIII on 2014-08-13
Hello.

12.10 is past its EOL (End of Life). The repos have been moved, which is why you are getting the 404s. We no longer support questions about EOL releases.  Please see the sticky at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2229730](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2229730).

The staff recommends that you upgrade to a supported release.  Since the upgrade path to 14.04 would take you through two further unsupported releases, I would recommend that you start a new thread asking how to do a fresh install while preserving your data.

Closed.
