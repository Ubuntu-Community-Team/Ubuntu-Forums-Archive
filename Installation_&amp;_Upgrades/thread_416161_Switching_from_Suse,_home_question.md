### Post by kidders on 2007-04-22
Hi there,

What you're describing *should* work, provided both your username and UID are the same in Suse & Ubuntu. If either is different, you'll have to intervene to get things working (*before* using the account in question). 

You might encounter some problems where the versions of the software being used by both distros are sufficiently different to make your personal settings unreadable ... but I wouldn't worry too much about that tbh. It's just something to be aware of.

In backup terms, I would suggest putting a tarball of your ~ aside somewhere ... just in case something goes haywire.
