### Post by Herman on 2007-07-13
Hello rpjanaka,
Just right-click on your two workspaces and click 'Preferences'.
Then in Workspace Switcher Preferences find where it has 'Number of Workspaces 2', and change that number to as many as you like. I like 12 in my computers.

Regards, Herman :)
