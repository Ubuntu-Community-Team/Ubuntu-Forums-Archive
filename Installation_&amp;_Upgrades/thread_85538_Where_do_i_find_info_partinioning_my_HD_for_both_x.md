### Post by az on 2005-11-02
Breezy will default to offering you to create some free space automagically.  All you have to do is boot the installer and when it asks you about partitioning, make sure it says something to that effect.  It will then ask you haw big you want your ner (linux) partition.  How much of your hd do you want to devote to linux?  10Gigs, 50?  You pick and then it will partition for you.
