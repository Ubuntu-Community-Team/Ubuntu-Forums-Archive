### Post by haqking on 2013-03-17
I recommend that you research what all the updates are and what for, so that you can best assess how they affect you or your system especially if it is a production system.

Updates are not a requirement, they often bring security and operational fixes of course, but remember the updates are the future vulnerabilities and issues that need patching later ;-)

Read the changelogs and updates and research them before applying, or at least have a easily resumable backup or clone in the event something breaks.
