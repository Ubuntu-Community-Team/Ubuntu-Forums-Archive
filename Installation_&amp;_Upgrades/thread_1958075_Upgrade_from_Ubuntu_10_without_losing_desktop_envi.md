### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-04-13
12.04 is a big break from how things worked in 10.04.

My advice is to create another partition and install 12.04 in that. Then alter it to see if it will alter to the way you want it to be. Do this before upgrading 10.04 to 12.04. Once you upgrade you cannot go back and you do not want to risk messing up your working system.

Remember, things change and we do get used to what is new and different. I have been using 12.04 development branch since November and when I switch to 11.10 it feels like I am using an old OS. 11.10 is closer to 12.04 than 10.10 is but we do get used to new things.
