### Post by corncob on 2009-11-02
I upgraded to 9.10 from 9.04 and everything is fine except that now I have no shutdown button.  It was in the upper right corner of the screen in Jaunty with a dropdown menu of shutdown, reboot, suspend, etc but now its not there and I don't see it any other place.  I guess I could
```
sudo shutdown now
```
and hit the power switch but I'm hoping somebody has a better idea.

---

### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-11-02
Right click in an empty part of the panel, Add to Panel -> Shutdown.

---

### Post by efflandt on 2009-11-02
Is your user name in the upper right corner to the right of the date/time?  The button is usually left of your name (unless the date/time covers it or your screen resolution is not right).  But if you click on your user name there is a drop down list of standby, shutdown, etc.
