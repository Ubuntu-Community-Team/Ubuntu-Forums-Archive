### Post by howefield on 2015-11-04
Follow the information [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades") to upgrade to 14.04, the current LTS release which is supported till 2019.

You could stay there or continue upgrading to the current 15.10 which expires next July, so probably not a great idea, the next LTS is released next April (16.04) can be upgraded to directly from 14.04.
