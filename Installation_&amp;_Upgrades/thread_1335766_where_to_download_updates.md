### Post by mac9416 on 2009-11-24
Howdy, sidene!

You should take a look at [Keryx]("http://keryxproject.org"). It allows you to take a "snapshot" of your offline computer and download packages for it from any online computer (Windows or Linux).

Take a look at the tutorial to get started. [http://crashsystems.net/2009/01/keryx-tutorial/](http://crashsystems.net/2009/01/keryx-tutorial/)

If you want to, you can make a DVD repo with the .debs using [APTOnCD]("http://aptoncd.sourceforge.net/"). That way you can take them to any number of friends' computers and install software for them too.

Let me know how that works for you!
