### Post by dFlyer on 2011-06-30
Reboot off the cd, when the little stickman shows press the space bar and select check cd or something to those words to make sure you have a good burn. Also did you check the md5sum of the iso before you burned it.

> **triger said:**

How to md5sum:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

Although you've burned your CD, a useful link. Burning ISO Howto:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)

But simply run the CD integrity check that dFlyer suggested. If the CD passes the integrity check, then you can assume the downloaded ISO was good.

After you tap a key when the stickman and keyboard icons appear, and after you choose your language, the disc integrity check is third in the menu, if I remember correctly.
