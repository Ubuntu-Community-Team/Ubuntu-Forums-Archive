### Post by rsambuca on 2007-09-21
Because the gnome desktop environment is so tightly integrated with all of the ubuntu components, the developers never backport the full gnome desktop.  The easiest way to get it will be to upgrade to Gutsy Gibbon 7.10 in late October, or else you will have to find an external source yourself.
