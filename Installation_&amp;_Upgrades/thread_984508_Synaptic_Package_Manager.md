### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-11-16
Without knowing what your problems are it is impossible to say what will and what will not help you.  However, I suggest you enable the [medibuntu]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu") repositories and get all the codecs etc etc that may be needed for a start.  Then you can start looking elsewhere if you still have difficulties, with possible changes to your default sound system settings and other system-wide changes that may be needed.
