### Post by Thelasko on 2007-10-23
What specifically is wrong with your system.  If it's just video driver issues it can be saved.  If you were upgrading from Feisty to Gusty you may want to reinstall.

To do a completely fresh install simply insert the CD into the drive and reboot the computer.  You may have to change some BIOS settings to get it to boot from CD.  This varys from computer to computer.  Once Ubuntu boots up just click the install icon and follow the instructions.

You may want to save your /home directory so you can restore your files.
