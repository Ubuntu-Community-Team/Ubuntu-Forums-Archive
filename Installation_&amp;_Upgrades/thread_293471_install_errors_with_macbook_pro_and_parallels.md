### Post by tll123 on 2006-11-05
If you are having trouble installing on a MBP 15 inch with parallels.  For instance, you may get to 91% of install and then it hangs on the aec62xx module.  Other strange errors are also possible.  Try the following,  In your  Paralles VM settings turn the acceleration to normal.  After a successful install and reboot,  you can turn it back to high.  Worked great here.
