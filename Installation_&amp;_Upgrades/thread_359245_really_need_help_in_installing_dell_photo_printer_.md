### Post by wickstopher on 2007-02-11
If it's asking you for your printers' model, that's not a bad thing, and you're not doing anything wrong.  It just needs to know what model your printer is!   Just answer the questions you're prompted to answer.

If that doesn't work, try installing Printers (foomatic GUI printer configuration tool) through your Applications > Add/Remove tool.  After installation, it will be available via your Applications > System Tools menu.
