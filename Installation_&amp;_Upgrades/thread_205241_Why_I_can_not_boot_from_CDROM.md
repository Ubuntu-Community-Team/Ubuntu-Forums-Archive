### Post by ajgreeny on 2006-06-28
Sounds as though you may have one of the few comps that will not boot from CD, (have you tried other bootable CDs in the system?)

If that is the case get a copy of smartboot manager and use the floppy disk option for this, if you have a floppy, of course.  Boot from the floppy and you will get the option to boot from a CD.  I've never had to use this myself, but I understand it works well.
