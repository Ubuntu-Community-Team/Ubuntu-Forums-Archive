### Post by billklee on 2010-02-28
One data point: It does not work on a Fujitsu Stylistic ST5010D. Or, rather, it does not work at all well. My details [here](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8491206&postcount=32).

There are some things I do like about 9.10: built-in virtual keyboard at the login screen, bluetooth works well and Ubuntu/Gnome screen rotation and display working.

What I do not like: no acpi support, no stylus and incredibly unstable.

These are **my** results on **this** tablet, and should not be considered a blanket condemnation. In fact, 9.10 seems to work fine on my Dell 1505n. Of course, that's not a tablet, so take that for whatever it means in this case.
