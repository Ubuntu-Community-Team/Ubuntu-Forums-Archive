### Post by LunatikOwl on 2008-03-02
You do type the password. It just not shown on the screen. I know it's confusing at start but not so terrible after getting used to it. You can also get graphical authorization dialog if you type gksudo instead of sudo(at least it works in 7.04 and lated).
I believe you meant downloading in Firefox. You can choose the download directory if you go to Edit->Preferences and under the 'Main' tab change to 'ask me every time I download' or something.
