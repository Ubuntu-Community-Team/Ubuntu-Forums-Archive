### Post by ShodanjoDM on 2008-04-01
Have you check your video setting in BIOS? Make sure that you've disabled the onboard VGA and use the AGP instead (I presume your card is AGP type since it's quite old).

And also make sure you've installed the nvidia-glx (not the nvidia-glx-new or nvidia-glx-legacy) driver package since it's the reccomended one for your card.
