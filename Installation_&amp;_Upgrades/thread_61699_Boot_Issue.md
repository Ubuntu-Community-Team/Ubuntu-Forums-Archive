Ok macgyver2, that was a good point.macgyver2

Here is what I did to correct the problem.

1..  Clicked on the System Icon
2..  Clicked on the Administration Icon
3..  Clicked on Login Screen Setup
4..  Under the General Tab>Greeter>Local....I selected "Graphical Greeter"

I made a few other changes, for my personal settings, however the above will allow you to boot up into the Graphical Mode.   

Hope this helps.
