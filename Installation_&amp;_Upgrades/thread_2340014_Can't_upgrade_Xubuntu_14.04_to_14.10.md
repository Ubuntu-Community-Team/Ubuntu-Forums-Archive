### Post by QIII on 2016-10-14
Hello!

14.10 has passed its End Of Life (EOL) and is no longer supported.  The files that would be required to update to it have been moved.

I am not sure of the capability of your hardware, but upgrading to 16.04 would be the only upgrade path.  However, I generally recommend installing fresh.  This would require that you back up your important data and, if you have a separate /home partition, maintaining that.

Would you prefer to go to 16.04?
