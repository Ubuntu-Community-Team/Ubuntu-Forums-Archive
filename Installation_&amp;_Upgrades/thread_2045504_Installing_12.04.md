### Post by OM55 on 2012-08-21
Here is another possibility to consider.

When you make a full install, the Ubuntu 12.04 will most likely need to reformat some or all of your hard-drive (depending on your installation options).
If your hard-drive is faulty and some areas of it are unusable (while you never noticed because they were not used so far) - the result would be similar to what you experienced.

But I think the first thing to do is if you can give us the exact error message you receive and when it happens. That would help narrowing down the problem instead of guessing...
