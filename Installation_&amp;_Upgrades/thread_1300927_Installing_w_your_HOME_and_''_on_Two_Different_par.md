### Post by TBerk on 2009-10-25
After trying an upgrade in place w/ 9.10 rc over the top of 9.04 I had clobbered my dual booting system, at least on the Ubuntu side.

Finally I reformatted the partition that the OS had resided on and reinstalled from the LiveCD. 

What made it not quite so painless was I had originally installed Ubuntu on this system (back w/ 8.10) in a multi partition arraignment.

My '/' partition is different from my 'HOME' partition. For the most part nuking the OS didn't loose all my data.

(Of course my really important stuff was archived to CD prior to...)

If you are in a position to be doing a new install, I recommend this as a way to go.
