### Post by 2F4U on 2012-10-23
The rough equivalent of a drive in Windows is a partition in Linux. To prevent your data from being deleted during a reinstall you would, for example, create the /home folder as a seperate partition. For partitioning basics look here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowtoPartition](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowtoPartition)
