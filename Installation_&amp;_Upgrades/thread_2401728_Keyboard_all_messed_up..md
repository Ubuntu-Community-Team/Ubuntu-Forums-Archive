### Post by ubfan1 on 2018-09-21
Some keyboards don't have a light for the NUM-Lock, and while password warnings may include the CAPS Lock key , they seldom include the Num lock.  On keyboards without a keypad, the  u i o keys become 4 5 6, the j k l keys become 1 2 3, and the m becomes 0.
