### Post by WakkiTabakki on 2009-05-18
Hi
Just to see if I understand you correctly; you do get sound from your headphones, but unplugging the headphones from the laptop there is no sound.
In that case:
1. Look at the tray-area next to the clock, you should see a speaker-like icon (see attached picture "Speaker.png"). NOTE! This icon may look different from mine, depending on what icon theme you're using...
2. Right-click and choose "Preferences"
3. In the window that pops up, click the tab "Switches" 
4. You should now see the checkboxes controlling where ubuntu sends the sound (see attached picture "Sound-prefs.png")
