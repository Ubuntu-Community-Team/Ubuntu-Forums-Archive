### Post by wpshooter on 2006-10-01
I personally do not recommend installing Ubuntu on the same system with Windows.

But I don't **think** the Ubuntu O/S is able to read NTFS formats except maybe under specific circumstances.  You are probably going to need a common parition (say FAT32) to share information between the two O/Ss.

My recommendation would be to **INSTALL** Ubuntu on the hard drive of an older computer as the lone O/S, at least until you can take a better look at it.

Good luck.
