### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-11-01
To add a  link in the unity launcher, run the application (like the setup screen or chrome or whatever).  While the app is in the launcher, right click on the launcher button for the app and select 'keep in launcher'.

You can use this feature to add and drop applications from the launcher to suit your taste.
