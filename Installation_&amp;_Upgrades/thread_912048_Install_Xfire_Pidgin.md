### Post by Partyboi2 on 2008-09-06
Download the [COLOR=Blue][gaim-xfire_0.6.1~20071109-pidgin-2.2-gutsy1_i386.deb]("http://gfire.sourceforge.net/snapshots/gaim-xfire_0.6.1%7E20071109-pidgin-2.2-gutsy1_i386.deb")[/COLOR] deb to your Desktop or where ever your downloads go.
Then double click on the downloaded gaim-xfire_0.6.1~20071109-pidgin-2.2-gutsy1_i386.deb package. This will open up Gdebi package installer, once it is open you can install the package by selecting "Install Package"  Once it is installed close and restart pidgin (Applications>Internet>Pidgin) Then open up pidgin and go to "Accounts" >> "Manage Accounts" >>"Add" >> "Protocol" >>"Xfire" then fill out your username and password.
