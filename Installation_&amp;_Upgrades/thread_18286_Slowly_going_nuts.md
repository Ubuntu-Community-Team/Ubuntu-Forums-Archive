### Post by zcox on 2005-03-05
Are you trying to install from the LiveCD or the installation CD?  They are separate things.  You also need to burn the .iso to a CD, to my knowledge you can't install from the .iso on your hard drive.

Also, be careful when you install Ubuntu so you don't format your entire hard drive (sounds like you want to keep the last 5 years of your life intact).  If you want to keep your existing stuff you can install Ubuntu so it dual-boots with Windows.  Check out [this tutorial](http://ca.geocities.com/zachandloricox@rogers.com/ubuntu/ubuntu.html) for details.  Or you can just burn what you want to keep to CD and blow away your existing Windows.
