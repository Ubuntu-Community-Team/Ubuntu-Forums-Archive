### Post by floweringmind on 2009-11-01
After installing Ubuntu 9.10 I was left at a black screen on first time boot or just the word GRUB.

To solve this I unplugged all my other drives and then was able to boot into the drive. This means your Linux drive needs to be the first drive. Once I booted, I shut down and plugged in my Windows 7 drive which was the first drive before and now is my 3rd drive. 

Upon booting the grub menu worked fine and windows 7 booted fine.
