### Post by az on 2005-09-14
That is the great thing about Open Source software.  It is an open process.  

To upgrade to Breezy, it will be as simple as booting int your Hoary system, putting the Breezy cd into your drive and pressing okay.  You will upgrade without data loss.  You do not have to boot the installer cd and reinstal.

You can also change your repositories to point to breezy instead of Hoary and upgrade from the net.
