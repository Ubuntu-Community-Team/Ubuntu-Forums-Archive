BTW, that white bar in a red circle is, to those familiar with the [Vienna convention on road signs and signals]("https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vienna_Convention_on_Road_Signs_and_Signals"), also known as a no entry sign. Many icons in Ubuntu seem derived from traffic signs, although it isn't always clear why a particular sign was chosen.
