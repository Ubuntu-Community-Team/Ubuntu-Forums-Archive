### Post by wyth on 2007-04-23
I went ahead and re-installed Edgy, and here's what I ended up with:
* Network Manager that connects to my WPA2 network perfectly
* An fglrx that works with the kernel, meaning the fan isn't constantly zipping, there's no screen noise when anything graphics-intensive (video) or a QT app (Amarok) runs, and it's plenty snappy
* Can even run Democracy Player again

What I don't have:
* Desktop Effects, which were fun, but with the radeon driver, caused no end of screen noise
* The latest OpenOffice with the nice Tango icons
* The latest F-Spot that would upload photos to Picasa correctly

On balance, for certain hardware, I'd say Edgy is edging out Feisty at this point.  Perhaps when some of these issues get worked out, this won't be an issue and some of us can upgrade, or perhaps it's time to get comfortable with one certain release.
