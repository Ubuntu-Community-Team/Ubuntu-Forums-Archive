### Post by PmDematagoda on 2008-01-05
No, you do not have to upgrade Ubuntu 6.06 especially if it is working out for you. Also Ubuntu 6.06 would fit your needs better than Ubuntu 7.10 would since Ubuntu 6.06 is pretty much dedicated to productivity and professional tasks with it being an LTS and also being more matured than Ubuntu 7.10.

So in a nutshell, unless you have a lot of problems with Ubuntu 6.06 then it is absolutely **not** necessary to upgrade it to Ubuntu 7.10 or greater unless support runs out for it, and there is still a lot of time left before that happens:).
