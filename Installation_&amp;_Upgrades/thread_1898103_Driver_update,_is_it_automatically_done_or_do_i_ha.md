### Post by snowpine on 2011-12-21
Virtualbox on a netbook is not a good platform for games, in my experience.

If the games are Windows games then my recommendation is to dual-boot and run them on Windows using hardware that meets the game's hardware requirements (your netbook may not have a powerful enough video card for modern games).

Your best bet is to visit the [Gaming & Leisure]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=93") sub-forum and post with a descriptive game title like "Help me play (name of specific game) on (your computer hardware model number)?"
