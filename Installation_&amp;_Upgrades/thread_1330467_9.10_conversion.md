### Post by dstew on 2009-11-18
One way is to [create a /home partition with your data on it]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome") before you install, and use the /home partition in your new install. I am not sure if it will work with Ubuntu inside Windows, but I can't think of any reason it should not. This will only save your data. Your programs will have to be re-installed.
