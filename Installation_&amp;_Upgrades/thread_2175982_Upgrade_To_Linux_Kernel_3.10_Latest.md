### Post by psfal on 2013-09-22
I recently upgraded my Ubuntu 12.04 from Kernel 3.2.5x to 3.10.1 using info from this page, it worked flawlessly on both of my laptops. I don't know about any other flavors of Ubuntu, use at your own risk.

> [http://www.liberiangeek.net/2013/07/upgrade-to-linux-kernel-3-10-latest-to-resolve-some-issues-in-ubuntu/](http://www.liberiangeek.net/2013/07/upgrade-to-linux-kernel-3-10-latest-to-resolve-some-issues-in-ubuntu/)

I boot from the grub2 in my Debian partition, just make sure you do a "sudo update-grub2" (or whatever the equivalent of that distribution) from whatever partition your grubloader is located in.

Enjoy :-)
