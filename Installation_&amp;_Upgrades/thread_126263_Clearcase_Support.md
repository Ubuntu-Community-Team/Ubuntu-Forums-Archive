### Post by vijirajan on 2006-07-02
Clearcase doesn't support Ubuntu. Your best bet is to use Clearcase web or Clearcase remote client. Clearcase remote client is Eclipse based, so if you use Eclipse IDE I suggest that you try Clearcase remote client. These two products are part of the Clearcase, so they will be available with most of the installations. Try this URL to see if its installed on your network: http://<clearcase host>/ccrc/update/

Hope this helps.

- Rajan
