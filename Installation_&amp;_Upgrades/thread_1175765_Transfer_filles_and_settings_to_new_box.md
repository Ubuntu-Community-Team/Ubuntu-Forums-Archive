### Post by gordintoronto on 2009-06-01
Have a look at remastersys, you can make a "distribution" with all your programs on it.  (On a DVD, assuming you've installed a few things.  Or with Unetbootin, you can probably do it on a 2 GB flash drive.)


After that, your biggest hassle might be e-mail.  Evolution can export from there, and import here.
