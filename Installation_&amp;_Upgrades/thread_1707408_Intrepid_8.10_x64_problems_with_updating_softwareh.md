### Post by ~Plue on 2011-03-15
support for Intrepid was dropped on april last year. 
since then, they no longer provide updates and moved the repositories to [http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/dists](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/dists) (you could try editing the /etc/sources.list file to that address but you wont get any new updates since april 2010)

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases#End%20of%20Life%20%28EOL%29](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases#End%20of%20Life%20%28EOL%29)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Ubuntu_releases#Release_history](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Ubuntu_releases#Release_history)
