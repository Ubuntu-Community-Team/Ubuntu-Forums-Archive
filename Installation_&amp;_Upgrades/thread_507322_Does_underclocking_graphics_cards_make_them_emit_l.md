### Post by dando80 on 2007-07-27
6600 has two performance levels. It alternates between 300Mhz and 500Mhz (on the 6600GT anyway) You can control the clock speed and fan speed using nvclock. Drop the speed and then you can drop the duty cycle on the fan also without any problems. I used to do it before I replaced the stock fan.
