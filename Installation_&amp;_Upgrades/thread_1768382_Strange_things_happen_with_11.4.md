Without knowing what you did, it is difficult to diagnose this. If I were you I would cut my losses and do a clean install of Ubuntu 11.04. It takes only about 20 minutes. Then you need perhaps another hour or so (at the most) to get the updates and reinstall the programs you use.

If you have a separate home partition, then all of your data will be safe. If you do not have a separate home partition, then this would be a good opportunity to create one.
