### Post by hashimoto on 2006-02-06
Now, I don't know what you mean exactly by "extracting the iso to a CD", but normally what you should do with the iso is to burn it to a CD as an _image_.  No extraction is needed inbetween or during the process. This could well be the reason for your problem. 

Try looking for something that refers to burning an image in your burner software and use it to make the CD from the iso. You should be able to just point to the downloaded iso-file and the software takes care of the rest.

Best of luck and welcome to Ubuntu!
