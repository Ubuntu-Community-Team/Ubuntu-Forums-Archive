### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-04-03
If your intent on installing PlayOnLinux is that you plan to use Wine, then BEFORE you do that, do yourself a favor, go to the linked Wine website and do searches for the apps and versions you want to use.  Anything not found, or rated lower than Gold, is essentially going to be a waste of your time installing Wine to use: [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true")
