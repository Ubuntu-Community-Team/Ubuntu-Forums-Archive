### Post by TBABill on 2010-11-14
I don't believe you can because there are different configuration files in each distro, or locations of those files. For instance, I fix sound in Ubuntu differently than in PCLinuxOS so I am sure it is different again in Mandriva. What you can do, though, is copy your files (.doc, MP3, jpg, etc) so as to not lose those.

The down side is you'll have to reconfigure again in Ubuntu and download any non-repo apps again. But enjoy the repos...they are huge!!

Good luck and welcome.
