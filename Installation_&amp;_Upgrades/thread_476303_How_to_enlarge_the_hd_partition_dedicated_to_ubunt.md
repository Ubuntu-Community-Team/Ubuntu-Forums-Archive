### Post by floke on 2007-06-17
Could be a problem since you can't extend a partition right to left, only left to right - I'm guessing you have Win on left and Ubunut on the right. Best thing to do here is shrink Win and use the free space as a /home partition for ubuntu. There's loads of stuff around here for setting up a home partition so do a few searches and you'll be good to go.
