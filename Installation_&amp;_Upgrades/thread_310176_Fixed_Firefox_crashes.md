### Post by Mangetout on 2006-11-30
I installed Edgy on this machine a couple of weeks ago, but had been experiencing problems with Firefox - on a lot of web pages, it would load most of the page, then the Firefox window would just disappear suddenly.

Turned out to be anything with Flash on it.  I tried uninstalling and reinstalling Flash (there are various different versions of plugin listed in Synaptic, but none of them worked for me); tried uninstalling and reinstalling Firefox; even tried backing up all my data and completely reinstalling Edgy - still the same problem.

In desparation, I downloaded and installed the Adobe Flash 9 beta plugin from here:
[http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer9.html](http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer9.html)

And it works fine now.  So... dunno if anyone else is having a similar problem, but I felt it worth mentioning.
