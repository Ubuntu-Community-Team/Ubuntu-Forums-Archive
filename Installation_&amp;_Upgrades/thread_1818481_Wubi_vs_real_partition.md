### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-08-04
Hi and Welcome

Wubi installations are not meant for long term use , it is a trial application . Performance may decrease over time and if the Windows boot-loader fails so does Ubuntu. A traditional dual boot tends to be faster in my experience . Wubi has a 30 Gb max and you can't hibernate due to lack of swap. In the end if your happy and the virtual installation works well that's what matters . If you are install the new releases every 6 months and are not upgrading , you won't have to worry as much about the performance degrading .
