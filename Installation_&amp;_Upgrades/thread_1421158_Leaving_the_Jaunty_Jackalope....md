### Post by Enigmapond on 2010-03-03
IMHO...Don't. I upgraded to Karmic and it didn't go well at all. I was used to Jaunty running so very stable and then encountered a ton of problems when I upgraded. Now, I admit, had I just gone for a fresh install of 9.10 I might have avoided some of the misfortune but it was a nightmare. My theory now is, if it's not broken, don't fix it. I'm happily back to Jaunty running virtually problem free and will await the new LTS to upgrade with a fresh install. I love Jaunty and you're really not missing out on too much by staying with it and awaiting Lucid.

Good Luck whatever you decide...but I think by just reading some of the problems with Karmic, that alone should keep you here...:D
