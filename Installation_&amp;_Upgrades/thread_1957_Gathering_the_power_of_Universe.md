### Post by im_ka on 2004-10-24
you can burn a cd/dvd with universe, but it'll be VERY large. you'll then have to add your cdrom/dvdrom drive to the sources list: you can do that by hand (editing /etc/apt/sources.list with your favourite text editor) or in synaptic (settings/sources).

never done it before, i think you'll just have to copy the whole tree as-it-is. but you'll have to copy the debs every now and then to have the updates.

if i would do it, i would certainly not go below the size of a dvd.
