### Post by barbarossa on 2006-06-05
After upgrading to Dapper the only real problem I encountered was getting Flash to work. I installed flashplayer-nonfree and Firefox would either crash or freeze. I tried all the suggestions about setting "arts" or "none" in the firefoxrc file, but nothing worked.

Finally I removed the libflash-mozplugin package, and lo and behold! Everything worked!

Hope this saves some frustration.
