### Post by 2F4U on 2013-06-21
The additional drivers are not present on the USB, since it is a proprietary driver. This article explains, why it worked on the live but not on the installed system:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/bcm43xx](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/bcm43xx)

The article has also instructions on how to possibly obtain the drivers from the install media.
