### Post by windowsfree on 2009-11-02
Being cautious of the new release after reading the posts here, I decided to load in as a virtual machine.  The CD image would stop loading at 73%, but when I mountained the ISO image on my hardrive of my Host machine, it loaded and installed without any problems.  I have not noticed any slow downs & all hardware works.  I connected to my XP and Jaunty shares without any problems either.  (wireless).
I didn't find the software updater as much of a problem as I read in posts.  It does seem a little slow, but all updates cued up and installed without problems.

My laptop is an older Gateway with 1gb ram and a 60 gig HD.
