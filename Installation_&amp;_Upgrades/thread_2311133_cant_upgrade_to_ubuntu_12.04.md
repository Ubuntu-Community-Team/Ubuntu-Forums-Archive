### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-01-24
Ubuntu 10.04 was the last desktop LTS version with 3 years support. So, 10.04 has been end of life for more than 2 years. From 12.04 onwards LTS releases of desktop Ubuntu were given 5 years support. Ubuntu 12.04 will reach end of life April 2016.

So, think seriously about fresh installing 14.04 at least. Or, 15.10 and then upgrading to 16.04 this April.
