### Post by cotcot on 2007-12-31
Depends on your applications. In case of much apps with video editing, photo, ... you have some benefit but do not expect that things are going twice as fast. I did some benchmarking and saw that rendering dv video to mpeg for instance was some 30 % faster. I also read that the benifit of 64 bit comes if you have 4GB ram or more. I bought 2 GB instead of 1 GB to check the effect.The 64 bit system only uses some 500 MB (!?). I had gentoo on 64 bit as well and there the 1GB ram was fully used. Unfortunately I lost my gentoo install on a serious update problem and cannot test anything there. 
Conclusion : I just turned back from 64 bit to 32 bit.
