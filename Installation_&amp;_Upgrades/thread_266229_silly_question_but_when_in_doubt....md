### Post by cotcot on 2006-09-27
No, but also yes.
When the upgrade is successfull you will keep your settings. When the upgrade fails you will loose then. And it can fail ; see post about this topic. 
I would not upgrade without backup. I recommend to partimage your breezy partition (partition table and mbr included) before upgrading. Maybe it seems annoying or complicated now. It is worth a little time to exercise partimage. Once you know how and you have organised a backup destination (what you should do anyway) it is simple and you will be able to upgrade without concerns.
