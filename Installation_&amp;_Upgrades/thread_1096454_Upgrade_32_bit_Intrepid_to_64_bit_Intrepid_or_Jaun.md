### Post by taurus on 2009-03-14
If you want to use the same partition(s), just pick the Manual option when you get to the partition screen.  Then tell the installer to mount whichever partition you want to / and format it.  And if you have more than one, mount the other one at the window.  But if you have one for /home and you want to keep all your personal files & settings, then mount that partition to /home but _do not_ format it or you can kiss your stuff good bye.
