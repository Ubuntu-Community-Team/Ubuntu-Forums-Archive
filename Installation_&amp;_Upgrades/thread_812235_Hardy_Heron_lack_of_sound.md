### Post by ridgeland on 2008-05-31
Two places I would try to change things.
First is the volume icon, the speaker icon.  Double click on it and see if there are some invalid settings.
The second place to try is System->Preferences->Sound.  See if your web cam is the default speaker.  Don't laugh, I saw it happen on my PC.
