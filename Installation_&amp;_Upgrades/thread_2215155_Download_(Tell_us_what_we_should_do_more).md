### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-04-05
Currently available Ubuntu releases are here:  [http://releases.ubuntu.com/](http://releases.ubuntu.com/).  It's easiest to click one of the links at the very top of that page, which will open a page for that release. There, again at the top of the page, you will see two "Desktop Image" versions, one for 32-bit machines and one for 64-bit machines (plus a version tweaked for Macs). Click on the appropriate link in you browser and the download will begin.

I believe the download links on that page cycle through a range of mirrors, so if something goes wrong, try again.
