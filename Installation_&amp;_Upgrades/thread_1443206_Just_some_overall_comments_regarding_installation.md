Of the years I have toyed with Ubuntu, and linux in general, this had to be the most easiest, trouble free, worry free installation of a distribution I have ever had. If you are new to linux, and you want something that is quick and painless to install on your computer, use Wubi. Dont do anything else. Download through windows, (or you can download through your existing OS), click on the file, and watch it work. It took a total of about an hour and a half to download the iso and install the os. Better than any os I have ever installed in my life. Im running an AMD Athlon 64x2 with 2 hard drives, xp on one Ubuntu on the other.
