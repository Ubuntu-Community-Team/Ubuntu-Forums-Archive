### Post by iaculallad on 2008-05-06
Click on the "System" menu located on your left-uppermost screen, then select/click on "Administration" sub-menu, then select/click on the "Restricted Drivers Manager". If it detects that you have an NVIDIA card, then it allows you to click on "Install".

Be sure that you are using GNOME and not KDE desktop
