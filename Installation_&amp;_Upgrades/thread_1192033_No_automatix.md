### Post by starcraft.man on 2009-06-19
Welcome. I assume by looking for music on myspace you mean flashplayers, in that case an easy fix and you won't need automatix (I think it's discontinued now...).

Top panel, System > Administration > Synaptic Package.

Search for ubuntu-restricted-extras. Check the box, push apply, and it will install flash as well as numerous other plugins your sure to be missing. Have fun.
