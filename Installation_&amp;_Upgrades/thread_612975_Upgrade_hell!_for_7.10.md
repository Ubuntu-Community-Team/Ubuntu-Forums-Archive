### Post by bongoMaster on 2007-11-14
for your thunderbird-problem: look into the folder ~/.mozilla-thunderbird/
there you should have a folder with a name like this: 3f8qzser.default

You can backup this folder,all your Mails and settings and other stuff are in there. If one day you install another linux, just put this folder into the right place and your thunderbird looks like before. Your mails are all stored (in plain text) in the folder  ~/.mozilla-thunderbird/ xxxxxxxx.default/Mail/
