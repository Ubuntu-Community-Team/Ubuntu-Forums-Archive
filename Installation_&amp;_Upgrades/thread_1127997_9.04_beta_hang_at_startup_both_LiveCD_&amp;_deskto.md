### Post by Skadge on 2009-04-17
Ok. It comes from the saa7134 module.

Cf [https://bugs.edge.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/316405](https://bugs.edge.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/316405)

A workaroud is to add

blacklist saa7134

to /etc/modprobe.d/blacklist.conf
