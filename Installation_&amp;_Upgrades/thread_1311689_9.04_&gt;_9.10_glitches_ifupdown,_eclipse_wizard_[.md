### Post by dsaronin on 2009-11-04
Actually there are two workarounds (see below) and it is a known issue with a non-standard Eclipse5.0 hack:

From: Arthur Nisnevich
Subject: Ubuntu 9.10 karmic upgrade causes wizard boxes to be unactionable

I had this problem as well, and it seems to be a known issue with Eclipse 3.5 (the default that comes with RadRails 2.0) and Ubuntu 9.10.

Found this workaround:
[http://www.norio.be/blog/2009/10/problems-eclipse-buttons-ubuntu-910](http://www.norio.be/blog/2009/10/problems-eclipse-buttons-ubuntu-910)
(this worked great for me and solved several issues!)

(Another simple workaround is to press the button, then hit Space or Enter on your keyboard.)
