### Post by RedSquirrel on 2008-01-15
Try running a window manager instead of a desktop environment. They take a little getting used to, but the speed improvement is worth it (IMO). Openbox, Fluxbox, IceWM, etc. 

These can be installed onto your existing Xubuntu installation, and they will appear in the Sessions menu at the login screen. (And you will have access to the usual programs... Synaptic etc.)

For maximum speed, you can do a custom, minimal installation:

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/minimal#barebones](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/minimal#barebones)
