### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-03-12
When 13.04 is released later next month you will be asked if you wish to upgrade, it's up to you whether or not to accept.

It's always a good idea to have a full backup of your system before you upgrade, as although there shouldn't be any issues upgrading can sometimes result in a broken system.

(You should always have a backup anyway, there is just more chance for something to go wrong during an upgrade than during normal usage).
