### Post by elvinatom on 2010-06-17
Try to click into the black screen until there's no display-mode change in a few seconds.  Then hold down <Ctrl> + <Alt> and click <Right>.  Wait 1 second and then <Ctrl> + <Alt> and <Left>.  If you see the desired Diablo Screen but nothing moves, double-click the little icons in to top left corner.
