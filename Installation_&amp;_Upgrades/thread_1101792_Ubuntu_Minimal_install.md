### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2009-03-22
You can't go wrong with [Urukrama&#8217;s Openbox Guide](http://urukrama.wordpress.com/openbox-guide/) for all things Openbox.  If you want a panel you can try [tint2](http://code.google.com/p/tint2/).  I just moved to tint2 from pypanel and it works well and looks good.  You'll probably have to compile it yourself since it doesn't seem to be in the repositories.

If you're going through the trouble to simplify your base installation you may want to look into [Arch Linux](http://www.archlinux.org/).
