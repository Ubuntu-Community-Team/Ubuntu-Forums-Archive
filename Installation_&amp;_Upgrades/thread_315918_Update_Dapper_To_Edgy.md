### Post by K.Mandla on 2006-12-10
If there are updated versions of those programs in the Edgy repositories, they'll (most likely) be updated as well. If not, the old versions will stay on hand.

Anything you compiled or installed from outside the repositories -- like Swiftfox -- will also stay as it is. Things like Automatix and Easyubuntu will likewise stand as they are.

That doesn't mean things will necessarily work together like they have in Dapper. But it doesn't mean they'll necessarily break, either. The only way to know for sure is to try it. ... [-o<
