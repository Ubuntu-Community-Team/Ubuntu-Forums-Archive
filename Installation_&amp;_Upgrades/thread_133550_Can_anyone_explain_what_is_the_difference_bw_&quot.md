### Post by bscbrit on 2006-02-20
5.10 is Breezy, 5.04 is Hoary.  Basically, Breezy has 6 months more updates, fixes and enhancements over its predecessor, Hoary.  Incidentaly, the .10 and .4 indicate the month in which it was issued, and the '5' indicates the year.  So, Hoary was issued in April 2005 and Breezy in October 2005. :D
