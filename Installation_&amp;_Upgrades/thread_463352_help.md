### Post by WiFi Ed on 2007-06-04
Since you don't have the bios password you are moderately screwed.

Go to the Dell support website   and see if you can find a manual for your laptop. There should be instructions in it on how to reset your bios defaults (hopefully a jumper, or maybe remove the bios battery) and then you should be able to get into the bios.

Use caution, in the worst case scenario you could bugger up the bios and be left with a unbootable machine...
