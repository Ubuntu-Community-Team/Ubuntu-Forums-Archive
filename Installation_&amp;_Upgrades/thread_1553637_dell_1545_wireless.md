### Post by MSPdwalt on 2010-08-15
If you have a Dell wireless Card (Broadcomm) you probably need to authorize the drivers System > Administration > Hardware Drivers.

Ubuntu makes you authorize the use of closed source drivers on your system.  The last time I ran it on a system with a Dell card I had to enable the drivers.  You may also want to do this with NvIDIA/ATI graphics drivers.
