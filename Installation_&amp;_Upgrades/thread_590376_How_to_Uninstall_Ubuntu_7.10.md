### Post by jdzitro on 2007-10-24
Change your BIOS settings to boot from the optical drive. 

Easiest/fastest way to get rid of Ubuntu and put Winblows back on is to wipe HD. You may have to re-install the MS-DOS boot loader. I recommend using [Super GRUB Disk]("http://supergrub.forjamari.linex.org/?section=download")'s Winblows boot restore app.
