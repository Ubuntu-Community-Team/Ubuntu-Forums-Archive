### Post by kidders on 2007-01-10
Hi there,

I'm not sure why your box just sits there :-| but I just wanted to mention that partition resizers can't "swap files about" ... they can only reclaim space that is completely empty. Did you remember to defragment your XP partition a couple of times, and make sure your pagefile is sitting somewhere sensible?

**Edit:** Partition resizing should only take a few moments ... maybe you should restart and try one more time.
