### Post by Objekt on 2010-04-20
By "factory configuration," do you mean some version of Windows?  If you want to restore that, you will need either

1) an image of the hard drive as it was before you installed UNR, or
2) "recovery" or "restore" discs that were supplied with the eeePC, or which you made before installing UNR.

If it had Windows on there and you formatted the drive when you installed UNR, you won't be able to get Windows back without one of those two things.

Some manufacturers will sell you replacement recovery discs, if you didn't get any with the eeePC.  Don't know whether that is true for Asus.
