It says it reinstalled grub ok.
With one install, grub menu is not shown by default.

And can you boot recovery mode or second grub entry? May be in sub menu.

What video card/chip?
Have you updated UEFI? If not, updating may reset some settings, so know what you changed & redo those.
Have you updated SSD's firmware?
