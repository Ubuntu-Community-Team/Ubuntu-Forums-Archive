### Post by thumper13 on 2011-01-10
I'm sure I'm not the only one with this issue, but on my HP Pavilion dv6, after rebooting from the first batch of updates, my keyboard and trackpad would stop working. It's also happened on my other laptops as well. 

After hours of googling and searching these forums I couldn't really come up with a solution.. I dug my usb keyboard and mouse out of the closet to log into my laptop, and it came up and said updates to install. I let it install the updates, and amazingly everything works. not only on the HP, but my others as well... I just thought this needed to be stated, it may help other people.:D
