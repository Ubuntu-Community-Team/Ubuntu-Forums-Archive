### Post by astro4travel on 2010-05-02
Problem solved. I found in BIOS that the floppy disk was enabled for some reason. I don't even have a floppy! I disabled that and viola! Disk Utility is back on the scene and my other drives are detected as well. Just thought I would post this in case anyone else encounters this problem.
