### Post by majed on 2006-09-19
Hi,

Just open synaptic, click on update, then search for what you want. After getting a list of whatever had your search term, look at packages and descriptions. Pick the higher level packages and synaptic will get all necessary packages for you.

It is automatic, as long as you know what you are doing, and it is simple too! :)

enjoy!
