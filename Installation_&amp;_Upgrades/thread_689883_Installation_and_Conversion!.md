### Post by louieb on 2008-02-06
Just last week did just that. Used GParted on the [SystemRescueCd]("http://www.sysresccd.org/Main_Page") to resize  the partitions on my laptop. Not a problem. 

But better safe that sorry - the System Rescue CD also has **partimage **which I used to backup my partitions in case there was a problem.
