It is a user forum. If there are no users here who have done what you want to do, then how can anyone advise you how to do it?

Ubuntu Touch is only officially being developed for 3 Nexus devices. Community developers are porting Ubuntu Touch to other devices with varying degrees of success and your device is not in the list. The community developed ports are extremely experimental. The only option for you that I can see is for you to port Ubuntu Touch to this device yourself but I do not see any easy to use instructions on how to do that.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Porting](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Porting)

The people with experience of porting to mobile devices are the CyanogenMod community.

[http://wiki.cyanogenmod.org/w/Main_Page](http://wiki.cyanogenmod.org/w/Main_Page)
