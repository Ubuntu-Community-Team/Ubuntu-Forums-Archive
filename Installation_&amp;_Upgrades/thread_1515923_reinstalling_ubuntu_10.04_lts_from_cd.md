### Post by schnelle on 2010-06-23
When you come to the step in installation where you choose where to install ubuntu, choose advanced option and then all partitions on your system will be listed. Choose your existing ubuntu partition and check "format". This way your existing ubuntu installation will be erased and new one will be installed on clean partition.
