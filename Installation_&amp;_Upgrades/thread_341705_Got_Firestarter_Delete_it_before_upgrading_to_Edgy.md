### Post by SectionThree on 2007-01-19
I just upgraded to Edgy (yeah, I know, it's a bit late, whatever) and had some trouble getting the X server to work. After much heart-rending frustration, and then a good meal, I realized that Firestarter kept causing the error. I removed Firestarter and everything turned out fine.

Then when all was said and done, I re-installed it!
