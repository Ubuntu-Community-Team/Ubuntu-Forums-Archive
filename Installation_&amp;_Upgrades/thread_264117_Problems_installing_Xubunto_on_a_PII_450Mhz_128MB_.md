### Post by Herman on 2006-09-24
I agree, the 'Alternate' installer should work, it has installed Ubuntu hundreds of times in my 'Book PC, with 128mb ram, pictured in my avatar here. (Right now it has been out of service for some time waiting for a new power supply, which that type of computer has problems with). Anyway, the 'Alternate' installer is the best for computers with more modest specs, or for people who want to do all kinds of special customized installs. You can choose between Grub or Lilo bootloaders, and specify exactly where you would like them installed.
