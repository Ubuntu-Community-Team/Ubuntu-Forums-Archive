One of the options in the installer for 11.10 will also attempt to install without disturbing the home folder, even if it is not on a separate partition. I can't remember how the option is worded, but I'v'e used it on a couple of machines with success. It's still a good idea to do a full backup though, in case things go horribly, tragically wrong!
