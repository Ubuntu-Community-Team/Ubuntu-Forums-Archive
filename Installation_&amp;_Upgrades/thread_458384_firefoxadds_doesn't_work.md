### Post by rondackcpu on 2007-05-29
Hi, Fan,

You're trying too hard.  Simple way is to open Firefox and "go" to the addons page of the Firefox website.  There you will find lists of the addons.  Choose the one(s) you want (one at a time) and click on their install now button(s).  A pop-up shows what you have chosen.  Click install.  No monkeying with terminal or apt-get.

Good luck.
