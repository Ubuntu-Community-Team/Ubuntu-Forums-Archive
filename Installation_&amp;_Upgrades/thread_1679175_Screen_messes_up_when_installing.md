### Post by uRock on 2011-01-31
In the **Your Name** line, you can have capital letters.

In the **Computer Name** line, you can have caps but no symbols at the beginning or end ot the name.

In the **Pick a username** line, you can not have caps.

The passwords have to match.

Once those rules are met, the forward button should work.
