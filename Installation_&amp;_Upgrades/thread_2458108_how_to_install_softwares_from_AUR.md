### Post by QIII on 2021-02-16
PPAs and AUR are analogous.

PPA = Personal Package Archive, which includes packages provided by members of the community.  They may or may not be safe or useful for you.
AUR = Arch User Repository, which includes packages provided by members of the community.   They may or may not be safe or useful for you.

Each is specifically designed to work with the particular OS.
