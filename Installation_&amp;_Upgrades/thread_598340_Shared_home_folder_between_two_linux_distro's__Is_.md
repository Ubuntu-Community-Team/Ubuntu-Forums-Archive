### Post by logos34 on 2007-10-31
Not advised, because /home also has your config & settings files--you'll mix them all up and possibly bork your system.  Just manually save any multimedia files or documents to your main /home partition, which you can do as long as it mounts in the test distros.

---

### Post by dabl on 2007-10-31
Logos is correct.

The better strategy, for data-sharing with another OS, is to use a separate partition or drive, outside the main filesystem, for that purpose.  :)
