### Post by oldfred on 2013-03-17
I had issues only in grub with my motherboard, until in BIOS I turned on keyboard  & mouse USB support. It seemed both Windows & Ubuntu had standard drivers but grub relied on the limited drivers in BIOS. I was able to also use a USB to ps/2 convertor  and that worked for grub.
