### Post by Dutch70 on 2011-05-03
> **ubun2warrior said:**
> which is better ubuntu 11.04 or 10.04.02.?

Whichever one you like better. If there was one that was better for everyone, it would be the only one.

10.04 is more stable because it's been out longer for starters. It's also an LTS which means it will be supported for 3yrs from the day it came out. The number is the date it came out. 10.04 is April 2010.

11.04 will be a little buggy for a couple months probably, as it was just released on April 28th. 11.04 is not an LTS, so it will be supported for 18 mths from the date it came out.

So, even though 10.04 Lucid Lynx is a year older. It will be supported until April 2013, 11.04 Natty Narwhal will be supported until October 2012.
While we're on the subject. 10.10 Maverick Meerkat came out October 2010 & will be supported until April 2012.

As was mentioned earlier. LTS versions come out every 4th release, or every 2 yrs. The next will be 12.04 in April 2012.

The next regular (not LTS) will be 11.10 Oneiric Ocelot & will be released October 2011.
