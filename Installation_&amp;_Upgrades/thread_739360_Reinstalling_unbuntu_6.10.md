### Post by dstew on 2008-03-29
No, if you reinstall you will have to reformat your partition. All your files will be lost. To reinstall, save the contents of your /home folder, then reinstall. Copy back the contents of /home, and at least your data and configuration files will be there. However, you will have to re-install any software you might have installed in the old system, and you might have to reconfigure to get your hardware to work.
