### Post by audiomick on 2009-12-13
Years ago your friend was right. In the mean time, the updater has gotten to be pretty reliable.

In system> administration in the update manager, it should offer you the option to update.
If it doesn't, in the options down at the bottom of the pop-up on the third Tab, which is probably called "updates", (mine is in German) there is a dropdown at the bottom that offers, approximately, "never", "only LTS", and "all". That needs to be set to "all", or whatever approximates that.

Bear in mind that, while the updater works pretty well these days, 9.10 appears to be not quite as polished as 9.04 was. If you don't really need the update, it might pay to wait another month or so.
