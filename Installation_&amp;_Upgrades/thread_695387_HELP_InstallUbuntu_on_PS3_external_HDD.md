### Post by tommohawk on 2008-02-13
Don't think it is possible.  I have tried several different ways of trying to do that and had no success.  If, like me, you have the 40gb PS3 I suggest you upgrade the harddrive and then partition it and install the OS using the standard XMB options.

Back up your PS3 data onto an external USB drive first and this should keep all your game data.  Anything you have downloaded from the PS3 store can be re-downloaded up to 5 times for free so you won't loose anything you have paid for.
