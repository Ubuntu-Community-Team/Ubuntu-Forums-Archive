I'm cautiously considering installing Ubuntu 11.04.  I hear mixed things about it.  Some say the Unity format is buggy. ### Post by e79 on 2011-09-07
Here's my opinion and suggestion on this matter, if it can be of any help. Its simple but you might not like it...

1. Burn up a 11.04 LiveCD and boot your Thinkpad with it. Play with the OS and test it as extensively as you can.

2. If step 1 is a success and everything work, backup all your important files, zero-out your drive and do yourself the favor and honor of a fresh install.

Yes this will involve tracking and reinstalling your apps and maybe tweaking things a bit, but having seen too many post about borked installations and bad thing happening from upgrades, I would stay far from that road imho.

Waz my $0.02
