Sorry it took me so long to answer you, but I didn't see your answer for a long time. I you have sorted everything out: Great! But, if not; here are a few advices: Defrag XP several times, erase all temp files, get rid of everything you don't need. Then use Gparted and make one partition with the rest; formatted to ext3. Then install and choose 'Guided>Largest Free Available Space', then let Ubuntu do the rest.
