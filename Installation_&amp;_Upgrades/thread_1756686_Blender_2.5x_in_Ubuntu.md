### Post by zia.newversion on 2011-05-19
Hey no worries! Actually, I am not sure, but I think Ubuntu has now included the shiny new 2.5 version of blender in the repositories. Last night I did "apt-get dist-upgrade" and apt told me that there was an unverified upgrade for package blender available and asked me if I want to upgrade it without verification, I selected 'y' (obviously) and there it was... Now I have blender 2.56 on my system and as they say in Blender community, I am blending happily. :)
