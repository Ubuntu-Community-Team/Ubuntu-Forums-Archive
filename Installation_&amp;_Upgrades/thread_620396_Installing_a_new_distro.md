### Post by rsambuca on 2007-11-26
You can share a /home between distros, but not everything will work perfectly (and some things not at all).  If you really need to share it, then I suggest using different user names.  In my opinion, it is better to have a shared media and data drive, and leave the /homes in their respective distro's partitions.
