### Post by TNFrank on 2013-06-27
For those of us who run FireFox(and who doesn't) there's a couple add-ons that I'd like to pass on to ya'll. The first one is Self-Destructing Cookies 0.4.0.1 that basically will "kill" all the tracking cookies that web sites put into your browser.  Double Click, Google, et al, are totally "Destroyed" from your system. You can also click so that cookies for sites that you visit(like here) are never destroyed so you'll stay logged in. Awesome add-on, worth checking out.
  The 2nd one is No Script 2.6.6.6 which blocks Javascript for automatically opening up apps in your browser. You can click to allow or or forbid so you won't get as many adds and stuff popping up while you surf the net.  If you go to a site that has something you'd like to see that's a javascript app you can also click to allow one time so you can see whatever it is but not give the site permission to continue using javascript after you leave the site.  This is another one that's worth checking out.
  Anyway, just wanted to pass these along to ya'll. Have a great day. ):P
