### Post by magisterludi on 2006-03-18
Probably in your bios the hard disk has a higher boot
priority than the cd drive.
Go into the bios setup on the early boot up phaseby 
pressing the setup key (this one is different from
bios to bios)  F1 , ESC, DEL are some of the usual keys.
In the BIOS you have to put your cd drive before the
hard disk in the order of boot devices.
