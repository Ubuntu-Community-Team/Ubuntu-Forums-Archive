### Post by snowjunkie on 2006-02-06
You need to burn the iso image to a cd using nero or similar cd buring software.  Burning software you get with your computer may not support the burning of cd images.

Then you check to go into your computer BIOS.  You should see what key to press when the computer loads up...  In there you should change your bootup order so that it checks CD before your harddrive.
