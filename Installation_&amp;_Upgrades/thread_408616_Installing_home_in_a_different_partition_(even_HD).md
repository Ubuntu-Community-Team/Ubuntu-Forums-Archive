### Post by aysiu on 2007-04-13
If you put your /home directory on an external hard drive, your external hard drive must always be on every time your computer is on. Otherwise, you'll run into problems.

I think you're better off keeping /home on your internal drive and then symlinking folders full of documents (pictures, music, personal files, videos) to the external drive.
