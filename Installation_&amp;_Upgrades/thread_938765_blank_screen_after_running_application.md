### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-10-05
What do you mean when you say that the screen switches off?  Does the monitor physically shut off, or does the screen just go blank?  If you press control-alt-backspace (this is supposed to refresh your graphical server), what happens?  Also, what is the exact model of your video card, and if it's an nvidia or ATI device, did you install the closed-source drivers for it?

You may also want to try burning an Ubuntu live CD and booting to it just to see if the problems occurs there as well (booting to the live CD won't cause any changes to your Windows or Ubuntu installations on your hard drive).
