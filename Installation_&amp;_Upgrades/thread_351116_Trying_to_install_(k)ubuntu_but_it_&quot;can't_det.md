### Post by StormTrooperVII on 2008-04-22
I know this is an old thread, but I was having this problem with Ubuntu 6.10 (long story short, vserver works easily in 6.10 but not any other version I tried) on a newer ( 3/08 ) dell optiplex with a sata cdrom.  I had to go into the bios and change the sata operation mode from ATA to Legacy.  Works just fine now.  Thought I'd share that, in case someone else was banging their head on a wall over it.
