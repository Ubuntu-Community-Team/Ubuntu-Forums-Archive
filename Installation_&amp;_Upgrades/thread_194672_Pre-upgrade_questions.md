### Post by glotz on 2006-06-11
This is the very issue with Ubuntu/Xubuntu/Kubuntu/.... naming convention

They're all Ubuntu + some graphic desktop. (In Ubuntu it's GNOME, in Xubuntu it's Xfce, Kubuntu KDE...)

So the update will update the basic system ("Ubuntu") however it won't touch your desktop, so if you start the update on Xubuntu, you'll come out of it on Xubuntu. (Or that's what I think... :) Really reassuring, huh?)

GNOME and Xfce will go untouched as will your apps. Dunno about your wifi issues.
