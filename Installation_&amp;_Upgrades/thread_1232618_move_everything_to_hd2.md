### Post by running_rabbit07 on 2009-08-06
You should be able to just save non-system files to the 2nd hard drive. Will your system let you mount that drive? If not you may want to look into setting up a /home on the second drive. Check out this site to learn more about [setting up /home]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome").

Otherwise your next best thing will probably be to back up your files and reinstall to the other drive.

Let us know how it goes.
