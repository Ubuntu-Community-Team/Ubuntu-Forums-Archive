### Post by aysiu on 2005-09-22
Burning at a slower speed often helps (I actually consider 4x, not 10x, to be slow), but you may, in fact, have a corrupt CD image. Maybe your live CD image was fine; the installer CD image may have been corrupted during download. Did you do a checksum on the image?
