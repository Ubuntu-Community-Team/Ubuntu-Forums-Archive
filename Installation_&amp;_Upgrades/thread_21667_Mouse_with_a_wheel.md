### Post by RiotNrrd on 2005-03-24
I have a mouse with a wheel.  Ubuntu recognized it without my having to do anything at all, and it works perfectly.

Linux is designed to work with a three-button mouse, and so it interprets my two-button wheeled mouse as a three-button mouse (clicking the wheel is the same as clicking a middle button).  The wheel also scrolls just as one would expect.

The earlier advice is good.  Turn off the computer, replace the mouse, and boot up.  My guess is that it will work fine.
