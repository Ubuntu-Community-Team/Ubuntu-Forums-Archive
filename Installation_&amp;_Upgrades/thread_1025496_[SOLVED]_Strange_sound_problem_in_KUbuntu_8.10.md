I recently installed KUbuntu 8.10 (Intrepid Ibex) . I seem to be having a strange problem with sound . I am able to hear the sound that gets played on logon/logout.
But im unable to hear any sound in VLC and also while playing YouTube videos.

### Post by AlanR8 on 2008-12-30
Right click on the sound icon Kmix in your system tray. Select the Master channel rather than PCM. OK that.

Then right click the icon again, show mixer window and then Mixer. Make sure the PCM slider is at 100%.

You should now have sound that works in all applications.
