> **robertchahine said:**

Did you verify the iso download by checking the md5sum?:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso)
The iso has to be burned as an "image", not as a data or bootable cd, and burn at 8x or less.

I assume your pc is capable of booting from cd, just not the Ubuntu cd you burned.

Added: The md5sum should be correct, since you used ktorrent, but it still may not hurt to check.  I think there is an option in ktorrent to verify the integrity of the downloaded iso.
