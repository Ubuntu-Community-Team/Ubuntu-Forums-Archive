### Post by slickymaster on 2013-09-20
Of course you can. Ever since Hardy (Ubuntu 8.04) you can reinstall Ubuntu without loosing data in **/home**, even without a separate /home partition.

Please see these two links:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HomeFolder](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HomeFolder)
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuReinstallation](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuReinstallation)

Of course, you should always have a backup of your data.
