### Post by amauk on 2009-02-05
when it asks for the password, there won't be any visual response on the screen when you are typing (security feature, so noone can see over your shoulder, etc.)

- type in your username
(you will see it on the screen as you are typing)
- hit enter
- type in your password
(you won't see it on the screen)
hit enter

you should now see
user@machine:~$

you are logged in at the terminal
