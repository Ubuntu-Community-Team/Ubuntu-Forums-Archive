### Post by Herman on 2006-03-25
Another thing, you can make use of the number keys that are above the letter keys on your keyboard, and the installation will go well.
If you prefer to use the numeric keypad, you must keep checking that your NumLock light is on.
For some reason Ubuntu keeps turning NumLock off, and when you type in any numbers for your passcode, since those are invisible, you will not be aware of the fact you are actually typing in arrow keys instead, which will be invalid, and cause the installer to cycle back to the beginning of the username/password phase for ever (or until you get tired).
I'm not sure if that's what your particular problem could be, but quite a few people do have that problem. 
Try looking at my left-hand sig link for install help. Regards, Herman. :D
