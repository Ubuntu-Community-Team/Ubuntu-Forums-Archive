### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-08-04
You won't be able to install a restricted driver for that card because AMD dropped support for legacy cards/chips and the older restricted drivers that DO support those cards won't work with  Ubuntu 9.04.

Best thing to do would be to boot from a LiveCD and confirm that you get video (and other hardware as well).
