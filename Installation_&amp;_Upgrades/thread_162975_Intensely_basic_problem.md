### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-20
Well, either something's wrong with your laptop or the CD.

My guess is your laptop, but it could be the CD. I know, I know--it worked on the other computer, but maybe the other computer has a better reader.

Something like that happens with the DVDs we get from our local library. If we try to play them on a regular DVD player, the DVD skips and is almost unplayable. If we play those same DVDs on our computers' DVD-ROM drives, they play just fine.

So maybe that different computer just has a better CD-ROM drive--not that yours is necessarily faulty, but the CD could be just this close to being corrupt.

Or... your laptop's drive could just be broken.
