### Post by gettinoriginal on 2008-12-30
Install Simple CompizConfig Setting Manager from Synaptic, then:

System > Pref > SCSM > Edges and you can set the cube to rotate at your choice of lacations.

You can also do this with CCSM by clicking on "Rotate Cube" to rotate with keyboard and mouse settings.
