### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-09-18
Vista will indeed overwrite your MBR.  You may know this already, but it bears repeating: XP (and Vista, for that matter), should usually be installed first, then Linux should get installed on top of them.  With this in mind, you can take a look at the following page to aid in your "super-uber-tri-boot-install": [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows)

Read the entire page before attemping anything and remember to always backup your important data!!

If you need further help, please don't hesitate to ask!
