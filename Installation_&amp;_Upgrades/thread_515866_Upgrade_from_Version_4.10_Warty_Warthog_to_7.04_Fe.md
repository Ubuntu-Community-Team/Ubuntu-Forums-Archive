### Post by bulldog on 2007-08-02
A clean install of Feisty is your best and fastest option.
The best you can do before reinstalling is to make a backup of your important data.
You have to reformat your existing install of Warty,I don't think you can install on the same partitions without formatting.
You can make a separate /home partition so your data should be safe when you have to upgrade again.
