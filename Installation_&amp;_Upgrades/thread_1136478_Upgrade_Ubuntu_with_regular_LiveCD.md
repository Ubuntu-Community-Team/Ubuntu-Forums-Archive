No, although it is technically possible; I think it would take you a lot of time and effort, considering you have the knowledge first.
Choose the easiest way, upgrade via network; or download the alternate CD, I recommend network upgrading as even when most packages come with the alternate CD, it is very likely you'll end downloading a lot of packages for your installed applications, libraries, tools, etc.

Use BitTorrents to get the alternate cd while is hot, tons of us are seeding right now, and I'm pretty sure your download will be very fast.
