### Post by ReaderRat on 2006-12-03
I think you may have a "Bad Burn" on your LiveCD. Did you burn the iso file as an **image** file or as a **data** file? It must be burned as an image....
ISO - Downloading & Burning
          **[http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso](http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso)**
          **[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)**
Also, you tend to get better burns at a slow (8X) speed. Try that and see if you get a bootable LiveCD.
