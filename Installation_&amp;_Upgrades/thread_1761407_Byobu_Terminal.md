### Post by DaithiF on 2011-05-18
regarding what the difference is ... byobu terminal is the regular gnome-terminal but with a byobu session running inside, rather than a plain bash shell.  And in turn Byobu is a derivation of **screen** which is a terminal multiplexer, or in laymans language a way of running multiple terminal sessions within a single window.
