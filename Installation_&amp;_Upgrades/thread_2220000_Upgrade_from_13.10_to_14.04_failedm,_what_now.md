### Post by QIII on 2014-04-30
Hello!

There is no path to "fall back to a previous installation" unfortunately.

"Plenty of problems" are hard to fix without details.

"Laundry-list" threads (which give a list of problems) become confusing and sometimes contradictory.

What I recommend is that you leave this thread and don't bother with it any more.  Start a new thread asking for help with one problem only -- maybe the one most vexing to you.  Ride that thread out until you get resolution on that one problem, then start a new thread on the next problem.

One problem per thread is much easier to deal with for you and those who try to help.

Best wishes.
