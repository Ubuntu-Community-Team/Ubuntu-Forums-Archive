### Post by beaucoder2 on 2014-07-04
Hi,

SOLVED!!!  The MSI970A G43 mobo suffered  chip failures:  sound and ethernet circuits. Huh. Was only 4 months old. 
                   Replaced with an ASUS M5 A97 R 2.0.   
                   Put the FX 4350 4.2 ghz Quad Core/ 16gb 1600 RAM, GT620 dual monitor nvidia card back in.
                   Shazzam!! My chair now needs a seatbelt!!!

A challenge, this one. Ubuntu 14.04 on an AMD MSI 970A G43 mobo. 64 bit. Zotac GeForce 620GT (nvidia) card.
Card and sound worked just fine in another Ubuntu 14.04 box. This same mobo with 14.04 and 8400GS nvidia card worked just fine.

Here there is only slight crackling of sound. Pavu control shows "Analog Output" for output port. Config: GF108 HD Audio Contrller is 'Off'. Doesn't matter whether it and HDMI are off or on. Built-in Audio is "Analog Stereo Output".

Alsa Mixer changes don't help. Sound and channels are not muted. BIOS only allows audio enable or not, thus it remains enabled. Disabled just leads to total silence. 

Reinstalled Pulse, AlsaMixer. Ran many thread fixes: used x-swat, different nvidia drivers, various terminal commands. No change. At the moment the Volume Control has disappeared from the system tray. Yet even when it is there via Sound Settings "Show Icon in Menu Area", Sound Setting show correctly but not sound. 

'speaker-test' in term runs normally showing left, right sounds etc. But nothing is audible. Same with speaker test in Pulse Audio. 

Also tried adding user name to mod group. No help.

sudo apt-get --reinstall install build-essential linux-headers-`uname -r`did not work, either.

Speakers worked fine with other computer setup of Ubuntu 14.04. 

Any ideas? 

Would love to hear,
