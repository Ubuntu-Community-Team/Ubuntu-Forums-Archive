### Post by skyiscrying on 2009-11-11
I was using Windows 7 and Jaunty 9.04  in a dual boot situation some months ago. I upgraded Jaunty to Karmic 9.10 in EARLY October - prior to the final release of 9.10 and had problems with shutting down to computer. Had to manually turn it off.
I could still boot into Windows ok and that system would turn off the computer. Also, I had no problems booting into the upgraded Ubuntu system which I must admit was slower than Jaunty. It was that just the shutting down that was not correct and I didn't like that.

Now I have Karmic running by itself and all is well. I installed from an ISO image downloaded from Ubuntu's MAIN site, and burned to a DVD - as I didn't have a blank CD.
