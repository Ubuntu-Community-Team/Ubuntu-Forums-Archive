### Post by lsald on 2006-06-24
By memory do you mean: **A) **Hard drive space or **B) **Swap Space

If you chose A then you'd be better off to format it ext3 like the rest of your drive (If you went default, and I'm assuming from this post you did)

If you chose B then you format it as Swap space.

Best of luck.
