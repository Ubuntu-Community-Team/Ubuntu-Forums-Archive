### Post by windscape on 2012-10-01
Hi,

I had a similar issue on my PC with an older NVIDIA card. What I did was at the initial screen with a keyboard symbol at the bottom, hit a key. It should stop booting and ask you to choose a language and have other options at the bottom. Hit Esc, then F6. Arrow down to nomodeset and hit Enter or Space to select it. Hit Esc again, then choose the option to Install Ubuntu or something similar.

I'm not in front of my PC, so this is all from memory. I also installed Xubuntu 12.04.1, so it might be a bit different than regular Ubuntu.

Also, if your monitor and video card have VGA connections, using them might workaround the issue.

I hope this helps.
