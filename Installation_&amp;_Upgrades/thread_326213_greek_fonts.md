### Post by simosx on 2007-01-04
> **standart75 said:**
> does anyone know how can I download greek fonts for mozzila?:-? :-?

Ubuntu Linux (since 6.06) comes with Greek fonts by default. The basic font, DejaVu supports both Greek (modern) and Greek Polytonic (ancient).

To verify, open up Applications/Accessories/Character Map and find the Greek section. There it will show you the Greek characters.

Some (greek) websites do not specify the correct encoding for their text and Firefox may misinterpret it. This is a problem of the website and a simple workaround is change the encoding within Firefox. It's under View/Encoding/ You can choose between UTF-8 (the standard) and Iso-8859-7 (old legacy standard).
