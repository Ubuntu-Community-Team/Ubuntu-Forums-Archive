### Post by jameswilhelm on 2005-08-26
You should be able to change this under the menu System -> Preferences -> Screen Resolution.

In case it is too small to make out:
"System" is the third option from the left on the top toolbar.
"Preferences" is the top entry under System.
"Screen Resolution"  is the sixth one from the bottom on the Preferences menu.

I have installed Ubuntu several times now and have never done any changes to screen resolution during installation.

I hope this helps.
