### Post by grege on 2009-04-19
Creative X-Fi support is limited and not very stable. The best option is to install OSS in place of ALSA. Not a job for a beginner, however a quick Google will lead you to instructions. 

[http://www.opensound.com/](http://www.opensound.com/)

ALSA info here

[http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Main_Page](http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Main_Page)

They are working on it, but not in near future.

[http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Vendor-Creative_Labs](http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Vendor-Creative_Labs)


I got so frustrated with Creative I jumped ship and bought an MAudio card.

Sorry, but the news is not good for now.
