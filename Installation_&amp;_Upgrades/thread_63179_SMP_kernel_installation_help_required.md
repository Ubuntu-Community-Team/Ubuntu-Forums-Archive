### Post by sethmahoney on 2005-09-13
If you download a new kernel, you should still have the option of booting into the old kernel.  When you start your computer you should get a message that Grub is loading.  Hit ESC while that message is showing (I think its escape - it tells you, anyway) and you can choose to boot the old kernel.  Then just uninstall the new kernel and you will be in business again!
