### Post by Autodave on 2018-07-28
18.04 is a LTS (long term service) release.  That means that it will be supplied with security and other updates for 5 years.  The next LTS will be 20.04.  In the interim, any other release will only be covered until the next release is available.  18.10 (which will come out in October of this year) will only be covered until 19.04 comes out in April 2019. 19.04 until 19.10, etc.

Unless you need the cutting edge of Linux, stay with the LTS releases.
