### Post by ayourk on 2009-08-09
Here is my solution:

gpg --keyserver wwwkeys.eu.pgp.net --recv-keys 106A92EF1E5F9737
gpg --armor --export 106A92EF1E5F9737 | apt-key add -
apt-get update

Substitute each line where NO_PUBKEY exists with the appropriate key not found.
