### Post by snowpine on 2010-01-22
My recommendation:
0. Thoroughly test a 9.10 Live CD to make sure it works flawlessly on your hardware!
1. Back up all your data (on both Ubuntu and Windows)
2. Burn an Ubuntu 9.10 Live CD
3. Reinstall 9.10 on the partition that currently holds 8.04
4. Restore your data from the backup and re-tweak to your liking

Option #2
1. Wait until April
2. Upgrade directly from 8.04 to 10.04
