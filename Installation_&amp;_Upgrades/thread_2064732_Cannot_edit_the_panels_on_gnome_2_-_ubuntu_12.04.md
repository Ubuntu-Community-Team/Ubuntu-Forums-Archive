### Post by claracc on 2012-09-30
How did you installed gnome 2?, I suppose you mean gnome fallback desktop.

Perhaps your compiz is not working well, try to log out and login again in classic gnome without effects.

To add icons, remove or move to the panel you have to use key ALT +right click in the mouse, this is for sure in gnome fallback desktop.

Here,there is an excellent guide to configure gnome fallback in 12.04: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1966370](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1966370)
