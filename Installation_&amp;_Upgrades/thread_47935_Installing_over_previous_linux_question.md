### Post by darkmatter on 2005-07-10
In a word, yes.

Grab yourself a SystemRescueCD [http://sysresccd.org](http://sysresccd.org) or the Unofficial Ubuntu Add-Ons CD (contains the SystemRescueCD). 

The disk contains several utilities (qtparted, ranish,etc.) that will aid you in accomplishing the desired task of resizing/moving your home directory.

Good luck.
