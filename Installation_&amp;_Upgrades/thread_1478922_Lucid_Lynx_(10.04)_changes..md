### Post by lechien73 on 2010-05-10
The preferred way of changing the button location can be found in [this sticky post]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1469475"). I don't feel it was an arbitrary decision, Mark Shuttleworth explains some of the reasoning in his blog post [here]("http://www.markshuttleworth.com/archives/333").

With regard to the blank screensaver, I wouldn't call that a change. Locking the screen is the default screensaver behaviour for many operating systems, and it can be disabled in System -> Preferences -> Screensaver.
