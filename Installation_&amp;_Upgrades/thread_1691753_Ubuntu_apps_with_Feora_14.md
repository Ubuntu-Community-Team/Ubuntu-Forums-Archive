### Post by coffeecat on 2011-02-20
Most of the apps you can find in the Ubuntu repositories you can find in Fedora repositories, and vice versa, but they are packaged differently. Ubuntu uses .deb packages, Fedora .rpm. Use Fedora repositories for Fedora.
