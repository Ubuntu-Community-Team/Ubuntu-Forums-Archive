Apple has apparently decided that itunes will never be made available for linux. See this for itunes alternatives on Ubuntu: [http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/itunes](http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/itunes)

Exaile is very nice. It would be my choice for a full featured music player.
