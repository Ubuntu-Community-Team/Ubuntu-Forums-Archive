### Post by CastilleV on 2008-07-11
***[Installing and updating your flash player for Ubuntu]***
Step 1. Go to [Adobe.com](http://www.adobe.com/shockwave/download/download.cgi?P1_Prod_Version=ShockwaveFlash)
You should get a screen that looks like this
[IMG]http://i241.photobucket.com/albums/ff281/Clairvoyant_teen18/Screenshot-AdobeFlashPlayerDownload.png[/IMG]

Step 2. Download the .tar.gz for Ubuntu (though it could also be .rpm)

Step 3. When you are done downloading, a screen should pop-up saying "Downloads" and will have two links "Open" or "remove"
Click "open" then press and hold CTRL and click on both files. 

step 4. Right-click and click "extract" you will then be able to find these files in your user-name folder
[IMG]http://i241.photobucket.com/albums/ff281/Clairvoyant_teen18/Screenshot-brian-FileBrowser.png[/IMG]

Double click on the file called flashplayer-install, and click "Run in terminal". Be sure that you have closed all firefox (or other browser) windows before you upgrade/install the latest flash version.

Hope you all found this to be useful ;)

  ~CastilleV
