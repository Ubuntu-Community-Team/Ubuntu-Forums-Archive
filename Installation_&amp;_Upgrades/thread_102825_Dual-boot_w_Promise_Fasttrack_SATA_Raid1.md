### Post by Nukleuz on 2006-02-10
Hey gpagnon, I'm completely new to the scene also and I'm currently in the process of breaking my dependency on Windows.  I am running a similar setup with two SATA HDDs in RAID 0 as my main setup.  I decided to bypass the resizing and just buy a new HDD to install Ubuntu 64.  Linux doesn't need much room, and if I'm correct, as long as you have at least one partition on your computer that is in FAT, you can run SAMBA to share files between your different OS'.  You should be able to get away with a moderate drive for cheap.  I do hope this helps.

Good luck.
