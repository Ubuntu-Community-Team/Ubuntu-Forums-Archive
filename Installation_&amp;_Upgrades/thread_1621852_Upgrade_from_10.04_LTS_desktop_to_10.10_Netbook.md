### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-11-14
Hi.

Simply upgrade your 10.04 desktop to 10.10 desktop and once successful, add the ubuntu-netbook package to 10.10.

```
sudo apt-get install ubuntu-netbook
```

From the login screen, you will be able to select Ubuntu Netbook under Sessions menu once you click your username.

You can make Ubuntu Netbook your default session from System > Administration > Login Screen.
