### Post by Afkpuz on 2008-02-08
No, thats not microsoft word.  It's openoffice with the Microsoft office icon used in the max OSX icon set.  I actually use the OSX icons too, but it's still good old OO.  It also looks like the folder icons are vista-ish.  If you want to change your icon set, check this out

[http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=120x121&PHPSESSID=3a5da6ce32ab93243f89ddaaa93d5b3c]("http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=120x121&PHPSESSID=3a5da6ce32ab93243f89ddaaa93d5b3c")

Most sets you can just download the package and drag the whole thing into the "theme" tab within System>Preferences>Appearances
