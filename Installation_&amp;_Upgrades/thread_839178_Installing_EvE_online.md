### Post by t.rei on 2008-06-30
I used the wine + premium-graphics client approach, since this gave me far less bugs. 
Just turned off the premium content after patching.

The only problem I have right now running eve on my x700 ati on my ubuntu is really bad performance in space and not-completely-satisfying performance in station.

I traced it to some error, wich forces lighting to be calculated in software emulation appearantly, wich would explain the really bad FPS. 

But installation was easy: wine-homepage, added the repos, installed wine, added the arial.ttf font to the .wine/drive_c/windows/fonts folder, installed eve-premium, patched premium, switched to classic and everything worked. 

But yes, I do need those fps. Cause 10 fps in space just isnt doing it for me. :)
