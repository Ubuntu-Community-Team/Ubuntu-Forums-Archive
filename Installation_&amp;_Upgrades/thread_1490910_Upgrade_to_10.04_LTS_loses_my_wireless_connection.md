### Post by drs305 on 2010-05-22
Let's start with the easiest solution. Go to System, Administration, Hardware Drivers. Is your wireless driver listed there? If so, is it activated?

If that's not it, do you still have a network icon on the top panel? If you have the icon, is your wireless connection visible or grayed out?

We will probably need more information for indepth troubleshooting, such as your wireless card, the results of "iwconfig", etc.
