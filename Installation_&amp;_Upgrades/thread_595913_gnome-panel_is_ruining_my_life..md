### Post by geokker on 2007-11-01
Your options are these:

1. Don't use a panel and map gmrun to a keystroke
2. Use xfce4-panel instead - it's virtually the same thing - but uglier (yes, it's possible)
3. Use xfce instead of gnome - it's faster, it's got all the gnome giblets (because you're switching not freshly installing)
3. use the mac dock-like AWN launcher, it goes a hell of a lot better with compiz-fusion

If you stick with gnome, make sure you kill gnome-panel on boot.
