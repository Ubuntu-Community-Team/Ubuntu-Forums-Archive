### Post by kc1di on 2019-06-27
Your machine has no provisions in bios to boot from the pen drive.  You will need to create a DVD disc with lubuntu on it. 
[See here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto") for how to burn Lubuntu .iso to dvd.
