### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-04-12
Ubuntu 10.10 had 18 months support and it reached End Of Life about a year ago. The repositories of software for 10.10 are now closed because they are no longer maintained. All things come to their end.

### Post by howefield on 2013-04-12
You can change your sources.list to point to the old-releases repositories which will allow you to download from the 10.10 packages.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades)

However, as 10.10 is end of life, you are better off getting something which current and supported, eg 12.04 which is supported for about another 4 years.
