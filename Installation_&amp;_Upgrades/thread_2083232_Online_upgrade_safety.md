### Post by Mr_JMM on 2012-11-12
An upgrade the skips versions is very hit and miss. Lots of people have done this successfully but many have failed.

I personally would always recommend a clean install, especially when jumping versions.

If you already have a separate /home partition then this is easier. If not, now is a good time to repartition your harddrive and perform a fresh install.
