### Post by Sef on 2006-12-06
1) Does your Live CD work on other machines?

a) If not, then you likely have either a bad download, a bad burn, or a bad cd.

b) If yes, then you could have a problem with the hardware, e.g. the cd rom is not working right, or another problem.

c) If yes, also your hard drive could be too full.  To check that, download and burn [GParted]("http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php").

d) What are your systems specifications of that computer? (ram, processor, graphics card, etc.)

2) Please post if any one of these resolves your problems.  Thank you.
