### Post by earthpigg on 2009-07-10
when you wrote the img file to the thumb drive, you wiped it :(

it may be possible to recover some of the data, however:

how big was the img file, how large is the thumb drive, and how much space is left?

if half of the space on the thumb drive is free, for example, it _*may*_ be possible to recover up to half of what you had on it before.
