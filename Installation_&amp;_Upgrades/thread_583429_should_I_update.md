### Post by confused57 on 2007-10-20
You might want to read over these instructions, if you decide to upgrade to Gutsy:
[http://www.ubuntulinux.org/getubuntu/upgrading](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/getubuntu/upgrading)

If Feisty is working well for you, I would recommend not upgrading...there's always the chance that the upgrade will fail, requiring you to reinstall Ubuntu(Gutsy).  A successful upgrade "should" retain all your settings and configurations from Feisty to Gutsy.  Try the Gutsy desktop live cd & see if upgrading would be worth it to you.
