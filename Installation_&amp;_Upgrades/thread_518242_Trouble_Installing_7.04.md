### Post by dabl on 2007-08-05
You have to "burn as ISO" -- it is a different menu item in your CD burning software.  It sounds like you have been burning "data".  You only want 1 file -- the ISO file that you downloaded, and then you need to tell your Nero or whatever burning app you use to "burn as ISO".  Sometimes it is the "Type of CD = ISO" -- depends on your app.  :)
