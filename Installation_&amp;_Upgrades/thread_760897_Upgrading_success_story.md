### Post by ekravche on 2008-04-20
I'v been successfully upgrading ubuntu since 6.06 and everything is working great. That's 4 successful upgrades, and I don't mean fresh installs.  Although, the latest kernel in 8.04 broke wireless, so I had to switch to an older kernel. 8.04 is running pretty smoothly. I did have a huge problem when upgrading from 5.10 to 6.06, but that's because I manually modified the /etc/apt/sources.list. The 5.10 to 6.06 failed for me and I had to re-install, and reconfigure everything from scratch.However, modifying sources.list and upgrading did work for me in the upgrade from 5.04 to 5.10, but again that's not the correct way to upgrade your system.
