### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-08-17
In Linux-speak it is the "pipe" key character and I think that its position on the keyboard will depend on the keyboard layout you use.
In my English-UK keyboard it is on the <shift> backslash key just to the left of Z on the bottom row, but on a English-US keyboard it appears to be on the far right of the top row of keys, just right of the square/curly brackets but again is <shift> backslash.

Other layouts I have no idea about but a search will quickly find it for you.
