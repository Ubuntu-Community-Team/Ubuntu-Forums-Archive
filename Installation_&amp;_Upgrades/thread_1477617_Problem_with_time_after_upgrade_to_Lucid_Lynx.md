### Post by ffsct1 on 2010-05-13
Problem solved. After trying System>admin>time&date several times I got a message that said I was not synced to a time server. I did that and rebooted still no love. I gave up and shut down, next time I booted up it worked. Go figure! Thanks for the suggestions.
