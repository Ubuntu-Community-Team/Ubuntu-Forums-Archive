### Post by mikewhatever on 2014-03-08
If by the option you mean the upgrade button, then it's probably going to stay there forever. Anyway, you can call it ondemand by running <update-manager -c>. The upgrade process will work as long as the 13.10 repositories are in place, which is till July 2014. That said, even after that, you could still upgrade one end of life release to another.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades)

PS: @monkeyb People usually come here with problems. When an upgrade is successful, there is nothing to report, and no forum threads get started. In my limited personal experience, upgrading worked very well indeed.
