### Post by msgyrd on 2005-06-15
The red and blue screens at the beginning are normal and won't hurt your mac.  Go ahead and answer the questions and then it will boot into an autodetect mode.  The live cd can't easily hurt your mac so don't worry about any boot screen you see (yes, they look worse than the OSX's boot screens). 
Also, don't be discouraged by the speed of the live cd, on my 1ghz g4 it was painfully slow to load, but a real install makes things as fast as you would expect.
