Basically, no.  You would have to reinstall Ubuntu and choose the option to use the entire disk -- but that would overwrite everything you have at present.

Is your current Ubuntu installed inside Vista as a Wubi installation? Do you see the Vista boot menu on start up where you can choose Vista or Ubuntu, or do you see a Grub menu first on start up?
