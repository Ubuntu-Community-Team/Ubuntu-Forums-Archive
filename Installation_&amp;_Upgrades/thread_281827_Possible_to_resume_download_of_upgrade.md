### Post by daller on 2006-10-21
You can resume the fetch-process of the upgrade at any time!

Just press Ctrl+C when the bandwidth is cut - Setup the Internet again, and then resume! (run "sudo apt-get dist-upgrade" again)

Good luck!

(you might want to wait for about 5-7 days from now! - Then Edgy final will be out! - Saving you a lot of bandwidth!)
