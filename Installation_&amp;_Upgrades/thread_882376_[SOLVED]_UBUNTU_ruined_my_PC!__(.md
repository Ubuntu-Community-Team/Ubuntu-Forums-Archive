### Post by wpshooter on 2008-08-07
Assuming that you don't have anything on the hard drive that you need to keep, get [www.killdisk.com](www.killdisk.com) and WIPE that hard drive completely clean.

Then get the hard drive diagnostic software from the hard drive manufacturer's website and test the integrity of the hard drive.

If the hard drive is O.K. then try reinstalling Ubuntu from an integrity checked ALTERNATE version of Ubuntu.

Good luck.
