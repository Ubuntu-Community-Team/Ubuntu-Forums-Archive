### Post by RobGoss on 2017-02-11
Have you tried using a different kernel? shut down your machine and when you reboot keep pressing the **shift key,** choose the **Advanced option** and you should see a list of kernels choose one from the list

If that does not help you can also use Boot repair : [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair) and reinstall the **Grub loader**

I had this issue one one of my machine using the **Boot repair** solved my issue
