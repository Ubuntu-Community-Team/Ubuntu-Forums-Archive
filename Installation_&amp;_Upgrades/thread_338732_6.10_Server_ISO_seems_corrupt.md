### Post by marx2k on 2007-01-15
The ISO is not at fault-

Power Calibration Errors are due to either the media being burned to or the burner itself, unfortunately.

I was seeing these errors at the 48% point on burning all my data DVD's with a Sony DRU-500A DVD+-RW drive :( 

Replacing the drive (which originally cost me $500) with another drive fixed the problem.

Clean your laser, reflash the firmware or see if there is newer firmware available, use different media or replace the drive/try another drive.

Power Calibration Errors are rarely from the ISO side and usually from the burner side.
