### Post by Onay on 2007-10-18
Is it dangerous to upgrade blindly to Gutsy with my custom setup? Or should I just do a clean install and reconfigure later?

---

### Post by Bluejacket on 2007-10-18
You always should have backup before you update/do clean install. You should have backups made even when you simply use your computer normally. After you've made backups of the important files, you can try upgrade. If it works, good for you. If it doesn't and you don't feel like trying to fix it, do clean install and return data from backups.
