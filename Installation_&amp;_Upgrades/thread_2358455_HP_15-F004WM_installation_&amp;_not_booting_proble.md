### Post by DuckHook on 2017-04-13
I cannot speak for HP support or the old battery recall, and you do not provide sufficient details to determine whether the old problem is connected with your current one, but by your description, your laptop may simply have given up the ghost. It's possible that your MOBO is gone, which would lead to the symptoms you describe. Since even a SDD doesn't work, it doesn't appear to be the HDD. You may still want to try a LiveUSB. If that leads to a working desktop, then it may just be the HDD (if you are lucky), but even if the problem is the SATA subsystem, it makes no sense to repair since laptops have everything integrated onto the MOBO.
