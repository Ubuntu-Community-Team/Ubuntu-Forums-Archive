### Post by Impavidus on 2019-02-06
Yes, when it says it will wipe the drive it will indeed wipe the entire physical hard drive.

Don't use your hard drive. Have a look here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DataRecovery)
You can probably recover quite a lot of files, but not everything, and it may take a lot of work to get the recovered files organised.
