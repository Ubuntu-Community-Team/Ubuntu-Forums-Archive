### Post by az on 2005-04-27
The modem you have (rockwell) is a conexant modem.  You need to buy a driver.  It costs 15 bucks and will only be supported for a year.  If you need to upgrade your kernel after that, you will have to pay again.

Buy another modem.  

Lucent or intel are linux-supported winmodems.  You can get one for less than 15 dollars.  Buy a hardware modem to avoid all trouble.
