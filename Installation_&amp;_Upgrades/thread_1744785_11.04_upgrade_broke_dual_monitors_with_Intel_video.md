### Post by Blasphemist on 2011-05-02
Mine is doing fine now though I had issues at first with resolution. Here are some things to look at.

Run this and look at the results for clear messages of something not up to speed for natty.
```
/usr/lib/nux/unity_support_test -p
```

Does the monitor show connected in either xrandr or the monitors application as shown in this now outdated page?

[http://www.thegeekstuff.com/2009/08/ubuntu-tips-how-to-setup-dual-monitor/](http://www.thegeekstuff.com/2009/08/ubuntu-tips-how-to-setup-dual-monitor/)

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/Resolution](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/Resolution)

This is the lucid manual xrandr page and I don't know of a newer one.
[http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/lucid/man1/xrandr.1.html](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/lucid/man1/xrandr.1.html)
