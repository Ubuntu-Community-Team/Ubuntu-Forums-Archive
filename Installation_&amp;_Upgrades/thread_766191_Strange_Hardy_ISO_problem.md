### Post by gtdaqua on 2008-04-25
Its best to have ISO images burned at slower speeds. I once burned an Ubuntu  7.04 at 48x and ran into problems. Burned again in 24x and it worked. Slower the better. 

When you are experimenting or experiencing problems with ISO images, always try burning again at a slower speed.
