Since no one else has jumped in, I will with what little I have to offer. I have never had any luck upgrading from one version to another. At best, I get a somewhat broken system that takes more time to repair than it would have to just do a clean install.  I have 13 machines that I maintain and last time I tried an upgrade, one worked. The other 12 got reinstalled.

Try booting to your DVD or USB and try the live (Try ?buntu) version. If the USB ports work there, I would reinstall a new version.
