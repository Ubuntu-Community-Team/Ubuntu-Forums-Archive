### Post by lechien73 on 2010-06-08
If you don't currently have an Internet connection on the machine, then you can download the packages individually and copy them to a flash drive.

I would download and install in this order:

[http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/wicd-daemon](http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/wicd-daemon)
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/wicd-gtk](http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/wicd-gtk)
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/net/wicd](http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/net/wicd)

There may be other unsatisfied dependencies on the machine, so you may have to download even more packages and install them manually.
