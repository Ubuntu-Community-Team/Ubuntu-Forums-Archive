### Post by Neo_The_User on 2009-03-08
Well I know for certain you can do it all using the nVIDIA card with the open source drivers by going into System > Preferences > Screen Resolution. At the top left, uncheck the box that says mirror screems, drag the monitors around to your liking. Then click apply when you are satisfied.
