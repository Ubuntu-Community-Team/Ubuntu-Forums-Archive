### Post by az on 2006-09-13
Once Edgy is released, you will get notified by the update-notifier.  You will be given the option to click a button and that will dist-upgrade your system.  It should work fine and without a hitch.

That being said, Edgy is supposed to be bleeding-edge and not as rock-solid as Dapper.  A lot of new technologies are being integrated and they will take several more releases before they become as bug-free as the stuff in Dapper, a long-term release.  YMMV.
