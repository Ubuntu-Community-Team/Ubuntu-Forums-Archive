### Post by Ub1476 on 2008-01-02
1) Boot up Vista and open its partition manager (search for "management" and you find it I guess).

2) Create a partition on 10gb + (Ubuntu doesn't require that much but it's good to have).

3) Bootup the live cd, go through the install process and choose "largest free space available" (that would be the gb you resized from the ntfs/Vista partition).

Good luck!:)
