### Post by aJayRoo on 2007-09-28
In short you cannot 'upgrade' from the i386 to the 64-bit version as they are fundamentally different. However, if you have your '/home' mounted on a seperate partition you can install the 64-bit system over your 32-bit system but keep the '/home' partition untouched in the same manner as you might do when installing/reinstalling the 32-bit version over the top of a previous install. This should allow you to keep most of your files and settings, though I have never done this, I think it should be ok.
