### Post by matthew on 2006-12-18
> **po0f said:**
> rmadhu,

A fresh install after a backup would serve you a lot better than going through the headache of such a massive upgrade.Agreed. To upgrade you need to go one release at a time so this would be a Warty->Hoary->Breezy->Dapper->Edgy upgrade which would get hairy and be incredibly slow.

If your /home is on a separate partition you can do a fresh install (after backing up the data just in case) and keep your setup intact. Otherwise, back up the data, do a fresh install, and then resotre your data to the new installation.
