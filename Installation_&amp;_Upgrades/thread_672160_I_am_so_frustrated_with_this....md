### Post by Porpoise on 2008-01-19
Without wishing to sound superior or anything, I would say that your system sounds quite old from the spec you gave. I suspect that it may just not be up to running the latest OSes of any flavour.
P4 1.3 is pretty slow and 256Mb of RAM isn't much good for anything these days.

The next step after that would be a complete system upgrade.
