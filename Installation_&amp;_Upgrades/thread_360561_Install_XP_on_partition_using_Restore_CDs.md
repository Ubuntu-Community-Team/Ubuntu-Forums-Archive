### Post by rsambuca on 2007-02-13
I think it depends on the type of restore process and from which company.  I recently had to restore my XP from those stupid restore CD's.  I backed up everything and then crossed my fingers (I have XP, Vista RC2, ubuntu, ubuntu 64bit, Sabayon32 and 64 bit installed).  My restoration only touched the XP partition, but I had an option to do a complete reformat/restore, or a quick format/restore.  I tried the quick format type and it seemed to do the trick.  Of course grub was wiped out, but that was easy to reinstall from the ubuntu live CD.  I also had sixty-something XP updates!

Check with your manufacturer first.  Maybe they will have more detailed info.
