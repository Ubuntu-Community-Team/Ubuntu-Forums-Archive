### Post by cilynx on 2006-04-17
Linux actually mounts CDs.  When the filesystem on the CD is active, you can't eject the CD.  You can always eject by unmounting the filesystem.  (To ease this process for normal people, there is an "Eject" option associated with your CD icons which unmounts, then ejects, all transparent to the user.)  I would guess that's why your CD wouldn't eject.
