### Post by culliard on 2007-04-07
Hi All
I'm quite new to ubuntu and I'm trying to get MythTV running. I had all the devices installed, and configured everything in mythtv-setup. I was waiting on getting my aerial improved so that I could watch TV. I had the mythplugins installed that I required. Happy days....

Until the 5th April when I installed about 12 updates, some of which related to mysql and some to xserver. I tried to run mythfrontend and I got a black screen and a few seconds later was greeted with the login screen!! Oh no! I thought OK, it is the mysql database and I tried everything I could think of (including totally uninstalling mysql and mythtv!!) and searched and searched but no joy...

I eventually figured out by looking at the install history that it could be xserver that is the problem and I after searching forum I found a post (not related to mythtv but same problems) that suggested re-installing th Nvidia graphics driver - **This fixes it** - simple as that!

[URL="http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=402962&highlight=xserver+update"]

This email will hopefully save someone some blood, sweat and tears!
I learnt the hard way and I'll be more careful about what updates I install!
