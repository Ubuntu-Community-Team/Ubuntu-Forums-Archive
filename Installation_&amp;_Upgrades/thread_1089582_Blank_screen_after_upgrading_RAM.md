### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-03-08
You sure that combo will boot?  Most motherboards come with documentation that lists the legal memory combinations and the slots involved.  You generally can't just insert two sticks of different sizes and expect them to work.

You could try booting from the LiveCD and running Memtest -- but if you can't get that far, the combination is not valid for your machine.
