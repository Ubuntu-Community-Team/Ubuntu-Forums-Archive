### Post by LaRoza on 2007-09-26
You could transfer them with some form of removable media, CD, DVD, USB Drive.

You could also just remove the hard drive from the old computer, and attach it to the new one, that is what I would do.

Assuming the drive still works, you shouldn't through it away with the computer. (Save the RAM too, just in case you can use it someday)

Setting up a network, and sharing the data will work also.
