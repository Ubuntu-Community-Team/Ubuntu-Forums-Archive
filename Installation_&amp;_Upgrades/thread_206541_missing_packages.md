### Post by thingy on 2006-06-30
Hi ya

You didn't say what KDE app you wanted MP3 support on, so I'll assume you mean Konqueror.

In which case, look at this thread:

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=186489&highlight=kde+mp3](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=186489&highlight=kde+mp3)

Which basically says, to ensure you have the:

libxine-extracodecs

and

libarts1-xine

packages installed.

You may need to add additional repositories to install the above two:

[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Dapper#How_to_add_extra_repositories](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Dapper#How_to_add_extra_repositories)
