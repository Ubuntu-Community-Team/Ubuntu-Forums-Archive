### Post by darkod on 2010-01-19
As far as I know, you can not turn it off. I think that was the difference introduced with 9.10. The menu will always look like that.
Because of that reason I actually installed desktop 32bit over UNR, I didn't like the menu to look always like that.
If you have cd/dvd drive on your laptop the easiest is to download an image of UNR 9.10, burn the image on a cd and use the Try Ubuntu option to see how it will work on your laptop and how you like it.
You can get the image here (among other images):
[http://releases.ubuntu.com/karmic/](http://releases.ubuntu.com/karmic/)
