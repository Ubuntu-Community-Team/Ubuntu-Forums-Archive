### Post by aysiu on 2006-02-02
Well, there are two separate issues here, and I may not be explaining them well enough.

Downloading the disk image is the .ISO you get from the Ubuntu website.

However, once you've downloaded the .ISO, you must burn it *as* a disk image and not as data.

Just as an example, [here's how to burn a disk image in Nero](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=111589). You can do something similar in Roxio and CDBurnerXP, too.
