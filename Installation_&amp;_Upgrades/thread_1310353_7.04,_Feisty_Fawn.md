### Post by unamanic on 2009-11-01
Dapper and Hardy are Long Term Support Versions.  Your only hope is to hit [https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+archivemirrors](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+archivemirrors) and pray you find a mirror that hasn't deleted the most recent updates from Oct 2008.  Although really you need to get to something that has had security updates over the last year.
