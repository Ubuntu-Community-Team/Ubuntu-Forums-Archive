### Post by davidmohammed on 2011-01-10
during the XP setup it will offer you to format the drive.

If this doesnt work/doesnt offer you, it could be that the laptop has a sata drive - normally XP doesnt see this.  You'll need to slipstream the sata driver into the XP install.
