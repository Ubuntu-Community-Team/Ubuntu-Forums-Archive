### Post by Peadogie on 2011-02-02
To use a GUI, in PcmanFM navigate to /usr/share/applications and launch 'Network Connections'.

If you want it in your menu, under 'Tools' in the tool-bar choose 'Open  Current Folder As Root', right click on 'Network Connections', open in Leafpad and edit the line OnlyShowIn=GNOME;Xfce; to OnlyShowIn=GNOME;Xfce;LXDE;.
