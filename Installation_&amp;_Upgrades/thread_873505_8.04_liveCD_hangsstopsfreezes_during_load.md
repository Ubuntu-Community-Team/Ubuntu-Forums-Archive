### Post by GrnFrag on 2008-07-31
i'd suggest verifying your hadware settings.  i had a similar issue when i started and it turned out that my old plextor cd burner was running in UDMA mode.   When i changed the setting to PIO 0 in the BIOS, the installer ran.   Also make sure your HDD is jumpered correctly, without using CS (cable select).
