### Post by JohnMorrow on 2013-05-23
If you have another PC handy, make a bootable USB stick with Knoppix on it. Then you can go back to your damaged install of Ubuntu, boot from the USB stick, and copy your files onto the same USB stick. I did this when my wife's hard drive was failing - works great!

[http://knoppix.net/](http://knoppix.net/)
