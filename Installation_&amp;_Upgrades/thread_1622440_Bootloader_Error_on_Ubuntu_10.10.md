### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-11-15
Hi. Welcome to the forums.

Sometimes manufacturers lock the MBR to prevent viruses. Look under your Laptop's Bios if you've got an option there.

For reinstallation of Grub, see here.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling%20from%20LiveCD](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling%20from%20LiveCD)

If you are not sure how to do that, you can post the output of bootinfoscript as per instructions here.

[http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net](http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net)

So we can know more about your setup and advise accordingly.

Good Luck!
