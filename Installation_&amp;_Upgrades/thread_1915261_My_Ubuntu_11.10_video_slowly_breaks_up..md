### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-27
You may be able to install different video drivers -- but we can't help you without knowing the make and model of your video chipset.

To find that out, open a terminal and enter "lspci" (without the quotes).  Then, look for the line containing "VGA" -- that will list the make and model of your video chipset.  Post that information back here.
