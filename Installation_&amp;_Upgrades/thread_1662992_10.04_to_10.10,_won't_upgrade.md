### Post by scrivener on 2011-02-24
Open Synaptic, search for xserver-xorg-video-nouveau, mark it for removal and Apply. 

Or open a terminal and enter the command:
sudo apt-get remove xserver-xorg-video-nouveau

Then try the update again.  It worked for me.
