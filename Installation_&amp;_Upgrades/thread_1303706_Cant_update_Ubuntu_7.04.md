### Post by p2bc on 2009-10-28
You can, but not directly. You would have upgrade from 7.04 -> 7.10 -> 8.04 -> 8.10 -> 9.04 -> 9.10

You see 7.04 is about 2 and a half years old. It would be easier and quicker to download the latest version (which is coming out tomorrow, Oct 29) than to all those updates.

You could install either 8.04 or the 9.04 since they are both LTS (Long Term Support/Service) meaning they will be supported for a longer period like 7.04 was but has since been discontinued given the 2 years are already up.

By the way new version come out every six months, April (04 month), and October (10 month).
