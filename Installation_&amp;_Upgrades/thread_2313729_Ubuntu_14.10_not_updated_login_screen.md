### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-02-15
*Thread moved to **Installations & Upgrades**.*

Welcome. If you're on 14.04 bit puzzled why you're going here. 16.04 LTS will be released late April and 14.04 LTS upgrades directly to it via the net. 14.04 to 15.10 is problematic. 

You are taking the hard road by attempting to upgrade through an unsupported release to get to one that has five months support left. That is also problematic. :|

Backing up your personal data and clean installing might be easier if there is a specific reason you are wanting to get to 15.10. To find out what you're really on, open a terminal and:

```
lsb_release -a
uname -a
```
