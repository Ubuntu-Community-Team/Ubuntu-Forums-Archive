### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-10-04
> **james_smith said:**
> I...is there anyway of having itunes running on ubuntu, as i need itunes for my iPhone.

Depends on the version you want to use.  The WineHQ site page for Itunes is below:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=1347]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=1347")

A rating of "garbage" means it won't work no matter what you do; a rating of "bronze" is one level above garbage, meaning, that it will barely work, if at all.

And even if you DO get it to work, the Itunes store will NOT work. Period.
