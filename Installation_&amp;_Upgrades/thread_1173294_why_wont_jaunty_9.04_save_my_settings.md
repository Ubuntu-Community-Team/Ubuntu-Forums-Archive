### Post by rpaar63 on 2009-05-30
For the wifi settings issue try going to "Adminstration" "authorizations" and look for "network-manager-settings" "system" "Modify system connections" and make sure your Login ID is listed, when i first installed NBR on my EEEPc i had the problem of it not saving the settings when i logged off, after adding my name to the list it remembered my settings.
