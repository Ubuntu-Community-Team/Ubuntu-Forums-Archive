### Post by HalfNote5 on 2012-05-24
If the video card doesn't work after you've completed the installation using the onboard VGA, then you may need to compile/install/load the driver for your particular video card.

What you'll then have to do is to install its drivers in Ubuntu, and THEN insert the card (with the computer off, of course) and it should work. ; )
