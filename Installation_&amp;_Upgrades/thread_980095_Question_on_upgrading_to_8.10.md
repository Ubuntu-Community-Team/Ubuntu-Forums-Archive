### Post by linnuxnub on 2008-11-12
To answer your question about data loss during the 8.0.4 to 8.10. upgrade.
 
When I did my upgrade all my files remained intact, all that was changed were the new features and a couple settings (eg.:desktop background). So it works like an update from microsoft (SPIT!). I assume your are referring to upgrading using the Internet from your 8.0.4. installation. If you use the iso image i do not believe your files and setting will be kept.

As for your wireless problem; in 8.0.4 i could not get any wireless working on my laptop but once I upgraded to 8.10. everything worked fine for me.

### Post by Pumalite on 2008-11-12
If you have room in your hard drive; make some space for 8.10 and boot it with the rest for a while. If you like it; you can erase 8.04 and expand your 8.10 partition. Your settings don't work that well with 8.10
In any case; if you decide to go ahead with the upgrade: have your 8.04 fully updated first; also remove all third parties from your /etc/apt/sources.list; including the Medibunt6u folder if you have one.
