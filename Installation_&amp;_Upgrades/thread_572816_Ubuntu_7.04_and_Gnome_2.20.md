### Post by rsambuca on 2007-10-10
Gnome is very tightly integrated into many aspects of Ubuntu.  In order to upgrade to 2.20, you would be almost running Gutsy anyways.  That is why the Ubuntu releases are timed closely with the 6 month gnome releases.  Just wait until Gutsy is officially released.
