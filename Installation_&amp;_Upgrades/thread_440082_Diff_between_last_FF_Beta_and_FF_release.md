### Post by Yoooder on 2007-05-11
Canadians?  Despite the question already being answered I've got to put in my fraction of a looney--it's how I was raised in Saskatchewan (dang talkative old people).

Depending on how customized your upgraded machine is it may or may not be worth reformatting and reloading.  When I upgraded a stock Edgy machine to Feisty it produced no noticable differences from installing Feisty by CD.  However, on my MythTV box with a lot of custom packages and reconfigured applications the upgrade didn't go quite as smoothly as I hoped--and I had to fight some fires to get it to work.

So:  If you upgraded a stock Edgy box, you should be fine as is.  If you upgraded a customized machine it might be worth the trouble.
