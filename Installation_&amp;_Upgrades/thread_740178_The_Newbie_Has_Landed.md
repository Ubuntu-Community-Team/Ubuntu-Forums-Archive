### Post by warp99 on 2008-03-30
If your going to use MythTV I would suggest using Mythbuntu:

[http://www.mythbuntu.org/](http://www.mythbuntu.org/)

Very noob friendly with extra GUI installers. As far as your computer the specs look good for both analog and digital recording:

[http://www.mythbuntu.org/requirements](http://www.mythbuntu.org/requirements)

and it looks like your Lifeview Fly TV Card is supported:

[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Tuner_Card#Cards_that_work](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Tuner_Card#Cards_that_work)

but your Compro T750 is a different story:

[http://www.mail-archive.com/linux-dvb@linuxtv.org/msg23136.html](http://www.mail-archive.com/linux-dvb@linuxtv.org/msg23136.html)
