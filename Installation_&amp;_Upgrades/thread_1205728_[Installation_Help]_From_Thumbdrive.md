### Post by Mighty_Joe on 2009-07-06
There's more to making a USB flash drive bootable than just copying the files.  The easiest way to do it is to use [UNetBootin]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/") or the [usb-creator tool]("http://www.pendrivelinux.com/ubuntu-810-install-using-the-built-in-usb-installer/") on the Live CD.  If you are dead-set on rolling your own, [these instructions]("http://www.pendrivelinux.com/usb-ubuntu-804-persistent-install-from-linux/") should give you a starting point.
