### Post by T-MAN3000 on 2007-06-03
You are using a repository that in all likelyhood doesn't exist anymore. Also, it's a repo for breezy, not for dapper. I would advise to remove the lines in your sources.list. You can do this by typing 
```
 sudo gedit /etc/apt/source.list
```
then remove the lines with the repositories that give you errors. If you're not sure about which lines to remove, post the contents of your sources.list and I'll be glad to help.
