swap should be around 2.5x your RAM . so in your case around ~800/900 MB
for more infos: [http://www.linux.com/news/software/applications/8208-all-about-linux-swap-space](http://www.linux.com/news/software/applications/8208-all-about-linux-swap-space)

using a large size of swap (which is to large for your RAM to handle) may slow down your system since your pc will be busy writing and saving data constantly to the hhd.
