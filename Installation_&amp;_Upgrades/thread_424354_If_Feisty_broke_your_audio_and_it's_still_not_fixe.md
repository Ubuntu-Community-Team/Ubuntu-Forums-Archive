### Post by rrwo on 2007-04-26
**This post could be related to an Ubuntu bug filed at**: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+bug/57141](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+bug/57141) [br].[/br] ---------------------------- [br].[/br] [br].[/br]
				I've just realised that my sound works if I start up from a cold boot (that is, my computer is off).  If I start up from a warm boot (reboot without shutting off machine) or resume from hibernation, sound doesn't work.

I went to report this in the bug system, and already found Bug #57141 there.

So if you're like me and still having problems with audio after upgrading to Feisty, check to see if it works from a cold start.  If so, you may want to confirm on the bug system, and help out with more information.
