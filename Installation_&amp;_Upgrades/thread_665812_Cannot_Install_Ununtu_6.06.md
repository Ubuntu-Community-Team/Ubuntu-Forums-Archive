### Post by Pumalite on 2008-01-12
You have 256 MB of RAM+ Integrated graphics eating at it. My suggestion is that you stick to a light Desktop. Burn a new 6.06 CD (download a new iso if necessary, do md5sum, burn at 4x, etc) or install Xubuntu Alternate CD. With your RAM you cannot boot a 7.10 Live CD, unless you make a 500 MB /swap partition ahead of time.
