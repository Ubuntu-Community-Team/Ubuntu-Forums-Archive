### Post by lemming465 on 2009-08-26
The X-windows system is in the middle of a painful multiyear transition to newer and better ways of supporting video chips.  Ubuntu in particular has been suffering a lot of video breakage along the way.  Probably 9.04 is having issues with your video chip.  Try a different version, either older such as 8.10 or bleeding edge such as 9.10 alpha 4.
