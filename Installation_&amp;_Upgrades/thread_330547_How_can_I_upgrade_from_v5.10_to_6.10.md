### Post by AlanRogers on 2007-01-03
See [here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/support/faq?highlight=%28dist%29%7C%28upgrade%29#head-554a5bc453f68051a6b83c83fbfe61b322c6b3fd"), on the Ubuntu Support/FAQ pages, under _**Once I have installed Ubuntu**_.

In short, you need to download 6.06 and upgrade in two stages. Alternatively, you can edit your sources.list file and use apt-get/aptitude but you'll still need to do it twice. Search the forums for dist-upgrade - it's been covered plenty of times.

Remember to back up all important files, that is anything you don't want destroyed _and verify it_, before starting.
