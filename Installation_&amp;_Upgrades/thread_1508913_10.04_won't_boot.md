### Post by quixote on 2010-06-14
the dev/fb0 message is just telling you there's no floppy drive, and is not a problem.  But the Radeon line is telling you it's having trouble with the Radeon graphics driver and that's what's causing the issue.

Do you have ATI Radeon graphics, or is it maybe trying to use the wrong driver?

It would help if you list what your computer is, what the exact graphics card is, and which version of xubuntu (ie Lucid 10.04, Karmic 9.10, etc) you're using.
