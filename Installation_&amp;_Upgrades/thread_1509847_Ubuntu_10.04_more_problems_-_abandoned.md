About 4 to 5 weeks ago I replaced with a fresh install, one of my older Ubuntu (ArtistX – Ubuntu 8.10) with the latest 10.04 My first problems started soon after the initial clean installation and were reported in [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=9307116](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=9307116)

The Xubuntu issue was solved and proceeded to discover all the nice new features of 10.04. Unfortunately my problems continued in a major and minor way and were reported in [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1507586](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1507586) a few days ago. Only under the installed 10.04 (when it was not crashing) I had absolutely no access to keyboard. I tried to change the keyboard model from Preferences/Keyboard but nothing worked.

So after all that and despite the fact that I liked very much some of the new features of 10.04 I decided to replace it and ended up reinstalling ArtistX (Ubuntu 8.10) and all is working fine.

I am really sorry for this because indeed 10.04 was very promising. I will watch for a new update and may risk to try it again.
