### Post by Amorphous_Snake on 2006-10-29
I tried installing both Ubuntu and Kubuntu 6.10 from the LiveCDs and in both I got stuck at partitioning my HDD. A user advised me to get the alternate CD. I did so for Kubuntu. It worked! But after installation, the system stopped during boot. I pressed reset and I pressed ESCAPE while Grub is loading. I chose recovery mode and I found an error. In the error line I found: DMA.

I disabled DMA from the BIOS. And voila! It worked.

I hope this helps.

My specs are:
1.2 GHz Celeron, 384 MB SDRAM, GeForce 4 MX 440 and 40 GB HDD Western Digital.
