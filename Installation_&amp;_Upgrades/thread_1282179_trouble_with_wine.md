### Post by ad_267 on 2009-10-04
All your wine settings are stored in ~/.wine. You have to turn on "show hidden files" to see this directory. If you don't have any wine applications installed or you don't mind losing your wine applications and settings just delete that whole directory, otherwise you'll have to look through the files and figure out what to change.
