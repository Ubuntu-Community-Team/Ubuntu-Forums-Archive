### Post by adwait on 2005-10-14
Synaptic first downloads all the necessarry packages, and then once all of the mare downloaded it starts setting them up. If Synaptic is still downloading, you can safely stop the upgrade. If it has started unpacking and installing stuff.....it may or may not be safe to stop the upgrade.

### Post by Kirky_D on 2005-10-14
once a package has been downloaded you wont have to redownload it. so if you had 100 MB of the download done, you won't have to download it again as the packages are on your hard drive already. 

I am not sure about individual packages, say if you are halfway done downloading a single package but even then it will only be a few megabytes more.
