### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-04-25
Examine your reasons for upgrading carefully.  If your system is working, what capability will Edgy give you over Dapper?  If you have some non-standard hardware (video cards, network cards, wireless, etc) then you can expect some breakage.  This will take some time to get working again.

If you just want to test out the new stuff then try the Live CD of Feisty and see what works and what doesn't.  

I would make a partition (or add a new disk if possible) and do a fresh install of Feisty, they you will have access to all of your Dapper files while running Feisty, and you can boot back into Dapper when Feisty acts up.

The time spent will be about the same, yet you will have two operating systems on your machine.  One that you know works, is stable, and supported for a couple more years, and one that is newer, more features, and under development--which means some things may not work.
