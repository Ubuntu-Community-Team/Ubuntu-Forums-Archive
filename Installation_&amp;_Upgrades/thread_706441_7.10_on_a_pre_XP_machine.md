### Post by Pumalite on 2008-02-24
DBan the drive: [http://dban.sourceforge.net/](http://dban.sourceforge.net/)
Then get Gparted Live CD: [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php)
Burn to disk and boot from it. Delete anything left in your hard drive. Make new large partition of your whole hard drive, format ext3. Then install: Xubuntu Alternate CD::
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/releases/7.10/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/releases/7.10/release/)
