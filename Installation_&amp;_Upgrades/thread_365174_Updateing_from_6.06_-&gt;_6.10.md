### Post by bapoumba on 2007-02-19
Well, you have the choice then ;)
ubuntu 6.06 (dapper) is LTS, meaning long term support (3 years on desktop and 5 years on server). Works for many people.
ubuntu 6.10 (edgy) is the current stable release. A new one is coming out in april (7.04, feisty fawn). The release cycle is about 6 months.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation")
You'll find several useful links there. My only recommendation would be to set up a separate partition for your /home, ie for all your user files and configurations.
