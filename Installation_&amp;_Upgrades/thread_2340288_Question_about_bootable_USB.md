### Post by sdansmith on 2016-10-18
I use UNetbootin ([https://unetbootin.github.io/](https://unetbootin.github.io/)). I've made bootable thumb drives or disks of just about every distro out there and it works every time. Instructions are pretty straightforward too. 

While you can use a distro in their repository, they are almost always a few releases behind, so I recommend downloading first and then using UNetbootin to make the bootable thumb drive from the ISO. 

Hope that helps!
