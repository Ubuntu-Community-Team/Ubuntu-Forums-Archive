### Post by Impavidus on 2022-05-17
22.04 was released in April and is no longer in development. The upgrade from 21.10 to 22.04 is available too; it was released about a week after the live disk image. There's no need for the -d option. The upgrade from 20.04 to 22.04 is still in development, so if you want to upgrade from 20.04 to 22.04 you need the -d option, or it will tell you no upgrade is available.
