### Post by Mark Phelps on 2008-12-07
If you're talking about the MS Windows versions of Dragon  Nat Speak, you need to search the forum for information on Wine and read up about what's involved in installing and running Wine -- it's not simple or easy.

Also, suggest you check the CodeWeavers website (the suppliers of Wine) to check their list of working applications to ensure that it's on their list and has a high rating.  If it's not on their list, or if it has a low rating, you're wasting your time trying to get it to run under Wine.
