### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-02-01
I don't know what is slowing your connection. There could be a problem.

However, you ask what I would do in your place.

I find clean installations much cleaner and less error-prone than upgrades. I would download the latest ISO and burn to CD or USB; back up all my data; do a clean installation; and restore my data.

If your /home is on a separate partition from your root, you can tell the installer not to format your /home, and you probably won't have to restore. But do a backup just in case.
