### Post by snowpine on 2009-10-05
Hi kmuzheri, I strongly recommend the following: 
1. Back up all data to an external drive.
2. Perform a fresh, clean install of Ubuntu 9.04, replacing your existing 7.04 partition (but keeping the Windows partition).
3. Restore needed files from the backup.

I recommend this for two reasons:
1. Ubuntu 7.04 has reached "end of life" and is no longer supported.
2. Even if it was, you would have to upgrade 7.04-7.10-8.04-8.10-9.04, as you can't skip releases.
