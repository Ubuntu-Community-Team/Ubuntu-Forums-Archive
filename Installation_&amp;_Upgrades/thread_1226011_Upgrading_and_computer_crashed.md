### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-07-29
Unfortunately this sounds very like a catastrophic hardware fault of some sort, and quite where to start I will have to leave to someone else.  However when you say your computer won't read the drives, which drives do you mean?  If it is just the CD/DVD drive, it may be simply a case of replacing that.  If the drive went awol while upgrading, that could be why you have an unbootable system at this point, and there should be no reason other than hardware failure for the CD drive not to boot assuming the motherboard has not also done the dirty on you.
