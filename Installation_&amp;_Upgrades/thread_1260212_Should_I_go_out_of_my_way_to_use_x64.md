### Post by ronparent on 2009-09-07
Unless your box has more than 4 Gb of memory, there may be little benefit to taking the extra trouble installing x64. It you want to take the trouble, bring the .iso to your grandma's machine to burn the install disk (converting the iso image to bootable cd). Whatever gives you the most fun.
