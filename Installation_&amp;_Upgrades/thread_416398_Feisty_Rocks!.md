### Post by Ephilei on 2007-04-21
I wasn't impressed at all by reading about Feisty's new features, but I upgrade anyway. I was pleasantly surprised a host of minor improvements! Here are the improvements I noticed so far: 

Deteted my monitor correctly this time. In Edgy I had to manually edit xorg. It also used a higher resolution than Edgy ever could.
Auto mount the slave IDE. No more fstab edits for simple setups. That's a step up from WinXP
Correctly installed driver for my wifi card out of the box.
Network browsing is blazing fast, ~10x faster than Edgy
Add multiverse repositories is now totally intuitive
The entire interface is significantly faster. With 190 RAM, buttons and menus were sluggish, but no more! Especially impressive considering the machine is faster enough to run XP at all.

I can't wait to find more little enhancements!
