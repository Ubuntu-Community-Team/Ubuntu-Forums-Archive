### Post by mingus on 2005-05-31
[QUOTE=breeze]Yes.Even now also I m doing it. Problem is what are the pkgs and from where can I find them.
Thanks for the quick reply as well.[/QUOTE]
 1.  Go to - [http://packages.ubuntu.com/hoary/allpackages](http://packages.ubuntu.com/hoary/allpackages)    (it's a long page!)

2.  Find and click on your package

3.  Scroll down to download and click on your architecture

4.  You will taken to a page with mirrors, when you click on one it will invoke a download of the .deb install file

5.  Save to disk, copy to CD, copy to your system at home, install . . .
