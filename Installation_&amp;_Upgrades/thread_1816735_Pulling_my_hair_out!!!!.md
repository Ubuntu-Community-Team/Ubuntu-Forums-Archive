### Post by papibe on 2011-08-02
I hear you because I have a Sony Bravia. You're very close!

By default the Bravia is configured to do a mild zoom on the signal. To get the whole signal displayed:
[LIST]
[*]Connect your computer to the TV.
[*]Select the necesary input so you can see the signal from your PC.
[*]Go to the 'Screen' menu.
[*]Make sure 'Wide Mode' is set to Full, and
[*]Change 'Display Area' to 'Full Pixel'.
[/LIST]
I hope it helps,
Regards.

EDIT: try this first with the 1280x720 resolution.
