### Post by darkod on 2012-06-12
The first thing I would do is remove /grldr from /dev/sda1. That's some kind of grub loader file and has no business being on the win7 system reserved partition.

Restart after that and see if it helped.

The rest of the results doesn't look bad. But if the above doesn't work, there are still more things to try.

### Post by e79 on 2012-06-12
That was just...too easy....

Seriously, thanks **[COLOR=Red]darkod[/COLOR]**, that indeed fixed my issue ! I didn't know about this ***grldr* **file**...**I simply went in my ***System Reserved*** folder, deleted the file, rebooted and voila !
