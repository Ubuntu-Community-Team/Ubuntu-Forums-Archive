### Post by patricia-e-miguel on 2017-01-04
Well, I was able to get the grub  to work and system to boot. Keyboard was disabled from BIOS.
Just went into the BIOS and from Integrated Peripherals enabled USB keyboard.
Then, once in GRUB screen, selected Ubuntu advanced options, recovery, root mode and from there I ran dpkg-reconfigure lightdm.
