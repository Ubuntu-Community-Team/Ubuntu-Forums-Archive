### Post by The Cog on 2012-11-12
You could try going to the extra options on the installer boot screen and choose **nomodeset** - that helped one of my recent installs. But you may have to go back to 12.04 and use the alternative (text mode) installer there instead. They have abandoned the alternative installer in 12.10. Hopefully they will have a working installer for 13.04.
