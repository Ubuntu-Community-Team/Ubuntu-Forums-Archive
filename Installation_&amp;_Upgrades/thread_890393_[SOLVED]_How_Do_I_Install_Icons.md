### Post by jualin on 2008-08-15
Just extract the icons file to the hidden ".icon" folder located on your Home folder. Go to the home folder and press CTRL+H to show hidden files, and then open the .icon folder and place the icons there (the extracted files from your tar.gz compressed icons file).

### Post by jualin on 2008-08-15
[This thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=3757") expands a little bit more on the subject. Hope this helps!

***edit. Just remember to vary the steps a little bit. For instance the correct place to go after you place the icons on your ".icons" folder is System, Preferences, Appearance, Customize, Icons.***
