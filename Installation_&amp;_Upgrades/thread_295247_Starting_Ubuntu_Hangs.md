Sorry to leave you hanging, DarkCore, had to get some sleep.
To mention some things; Did you download and burn an image file (.iso) for your copy of Ubuntu? Did you burn it at a slooow rate (4X)? This seems to give fewer CD rejects. Did you run an MD5 checksum on your disc (one of the options on the Live CD) to make sure your disc was good?  If so, I would suggest Dapper instead of Edgy, because it is more stable at the moment.
You can also install from the Alternate CD, in text mode, and see if your video card is part of the problem. I couldn't get any info on your card 'cause there was no number (i.e.-8000,9500,etc.) so I am leaving a link for  Radeon cards you may want to peruse:
[**ATI Radeon 8500 & Higher Video cards**](https://support.ati.com/ics/support/default.asp?deptID=894&task=knowledge&folderID=27)
