### Post by aysiu on 2008-01-30
> **lsutiger said:**

Depending on how many programs you have installed, / will usually range in size from 1 GB to 4 GB. To be safe, I'd recommend at least 6 GB. 10 GB should be plenty.

### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-01-30
Your 10GB is plenty since programs don't tend to get very large.  It's data like movies, pictures, and audio that chews up space quickly, so if those are in your /home partition or on an external drive, you should be fine at 10GB.
