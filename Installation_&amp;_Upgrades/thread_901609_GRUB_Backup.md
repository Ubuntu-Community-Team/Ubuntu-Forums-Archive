Easiest is to download/burn [Super Grub Disk]("http://www.supergrubdisk.org/") and after "the killing of Grub" let it reinstall Grub based on the /boot/grub/menu.lst you still have left on your Ubuntu install. So.. it will reinstall Grub and you'll have your bootmenu back.

[Other method's.]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows")
