### Post by wpshooter on 2006-12-06
My question is why do you want to move from Dapper to Edgy.  IMO, mostly of what you are getting with Edgy are comestic enhancements.  And I don't think it is as stable as Dapper.

Having said that, I don't recommend upgrading from one version of an O/S to the next via upgrade method.  If I want to move from Dapper to Edgy, I would do a complete install of Edgy from scratch.  I don't think it was good to move from one version of an O/S to another in M/S windows, and I believe the same applies to Ubuntu/Linux.

Good luck.
