### Post by prshah on 2008-03-10
Grub Error has nothing to do with your primary partition being or not being NTFS. What is the error number (it comes after Grub Error). That will tell us where your problem lies.

If you cant see the splash screen it's no real loss, in fact, while booting, if you switch over to Ctrl+Alt+F8 terminal, you will get to see the whole process in action, and learn a lot while at it.
