In Windows and **in Mandriva**, I could press/click and hold the wheel button and it would allow me to scroll vertically or horizontally on a document.  Is there a way to get this feature working in Ubuntu (Dapper)?

### Post by BitTorrentBuddha on 2006-08-07
I'm not able to help with documents, but in firefox such a feature is enabled by going to Edit->Preferences, and then selecting the 'Advanced' section. Under the 'General' tab, there's an option called 'Use autoscrolling,' select this and you will gain said ability in firefox. I hope you find someone more knowledgable than myself to help with your other problems.:D
