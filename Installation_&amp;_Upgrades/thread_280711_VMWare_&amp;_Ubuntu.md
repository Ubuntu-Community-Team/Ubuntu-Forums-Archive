### Post by SendDerek on 2006-10-19
Maybe I should just leave this one up to the veterans, but I don't think that it requires a lot of HDD space.  My root drive and home drive together only take up 5.3GB worth of space (and I have a lot of "stuff" installed).

8GB sound like a pretty good size considering you'll probably want to leave enough room to save another 3GB worth of "stuff" and you're still giving 2GB of room to your main system (osx86).  

I also think that 6GB would be pretty good too.  It seems to be enough room for the full installation and for some documents, and you're still leaving your main OS with 4GB to spare for "stuff".
