### Post by falcon61102 on 2008-12-03
How are you burning your CDs and are you using CD-R's?

Ubuntu is very picky about CDs and it's best to burn a Ubuntu CD at 1x and only to CD-R's.  Also, when you boot up, select the Check CD option to ensure you have a good CD.
