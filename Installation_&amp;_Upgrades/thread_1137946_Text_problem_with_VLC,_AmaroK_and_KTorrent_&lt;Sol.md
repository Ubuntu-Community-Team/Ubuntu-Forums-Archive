### Post by myk.dinis on 2009-04-26
I had a huge jaunty upgrade issue...

I couldn't read the text in three programs I used all the time...

I love the "it just works" of Ubuntu, but really like AmaroK and KTorrent.

When I upgraded to Jaunty yesterday, I couldn't read any text in these programs (including VLC).

Turns out the subpixel hinting was to blame...

I changed from VRGB to RGB and I can read again!!

Yay!!
