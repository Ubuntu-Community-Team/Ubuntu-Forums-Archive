### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-07-09
> **Zirts said:**
> Hi,

Will it work if I install the driver that ATI gives me on their homepage to play games like doom3.

My gcard is: ATI Radeon 9550 128MB.

In short -- NO.  

There are NO ATI proprietary drivers that will work with your card and any recent versions of Ubuntu.  You will be using the default open-source drivers.  So, basically, there is nothing to install and if you try to force the installation of those drivers, you will trash your disp[ay and have to boot into console mode to remove them.
