### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-10-21
In short -- NO.  You can not "upgrade" from 32-bit to 64-bit, or vice-versa. You have to do a clean install in either case.

### Post by stlsaint on 2009-10-21
agreed to MarkPhelps response...for architecture changes you must do a clean ugrade meaning backup all your files, documents etc and perform a fresh install like you did the first time for your 32bit version.
