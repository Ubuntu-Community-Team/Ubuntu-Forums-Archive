### Post by Herman on 2007-10-09
> I used Parted Magic CD to move my 10 gig Windows partition to the new 100 Gig partition on the new drive. More than likely Windows doesn't like being copied and pasted to another disk and would have anti piracy software devices and other booby traps planted in it to prevent people from being able to use it after an event like that.

You should read your Windows EULA carefully or contact Microsoft and check with them and make sure you are allowed to do that. They should be able to help you fix it if your use is legitimate and legal, or if not they should be able to explain in a way that you understand what you are allowed or not allowed to do.
