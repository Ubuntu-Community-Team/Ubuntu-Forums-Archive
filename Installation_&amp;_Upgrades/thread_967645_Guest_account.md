### Post by cariboo on 2008-11-02
I'm not really sure what your question is, but Intrepid has a builtin guest account. Just click on your name in the upper panel and select Guest. This opens another desktop with limited permissions. While using the Guest account, the user account is locked and can only be accessed by entering a password.
