### Post by marks40x on 2012-01-26
I installed Ubuntu on one computer with no problems. With the second I kept getting this error message. initramfs unable to find a medium containing a live file system. All the searches I did showed it to be a hard drive setting in bios causing the problem. After changing all the settings I could find I noticed my DVD drive was showing up as slave in the POST screen. I missed it when I installed the drive a few years ago. I swapped it to master and Ubuntu installed with no problems. You will have to forgive me if the terms used are not correct as I am not really computer fluent. Anyway I never saw this mentioned to solve the problem and thought I would let you know what worked for me. Mark.
