### Post by everytimeref on 2009-09-18
the only problem I have had with Shiretoko is Online banking. If you use Online banking and there is a problem then you may need to follow the advice given here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-785190.html](http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-785190.html)

i.e. in about:config

change general.useragent.extra.firefox from Shiretoko/3.5.4 to Firefox/3.5.4 ( or possibly 3.5.3, as .4 is a beta)
