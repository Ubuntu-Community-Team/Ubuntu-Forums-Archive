### Post by Mez on 2005-06-15
It's always good to have some sort of swap, espesically for when your computer gets a heavy load.

I wouldnt suggest getting rid of it altogether, but it'd be ok to resize it.

I dont think it would be a good idea to have it any smaller than your ram size though
