### Post by RobGoss on 2016-11-05
Hello and welcome to the forum, Have you tried accessing the sounds settings? at the top of your desktop you should see a little **speaker** click on that and click sound settings
There are a few options there you can alter to get the sound working like making sure your sound is not **muted**, then make sure your sound output is listed and highlighted  this should ensure that your sound can be heard

As for your WiFi issue you'll see two arrows at the top of your desktop, click on the the arrows and see if your wireless network is available to connect to if so just click on your WiFi access point and login it should connect. 

If you still have trouble connecting to your WiFi opening up **Software & update,** then** Additional drivers** and see what proprietary drivers are available for your system

Hope this helps
