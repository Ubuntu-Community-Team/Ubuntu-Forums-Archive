### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-05-04
Messages like this indicate the app is not supported in the new OS version.

SO that begs the question -- why upgrade?

If your current version is working OK, unless 13.04 brings something you sorely need, and considering that it's only going to be supported foir 9 months, there is little sense in removing an app you use, just to upgrade.

Despite what many folks think, NEWER is not always BETTER.
