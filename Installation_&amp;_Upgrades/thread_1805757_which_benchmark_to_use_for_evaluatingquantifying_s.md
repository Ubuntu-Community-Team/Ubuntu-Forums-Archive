### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-07-16
With that hardware I think you would do a lot better with Xubuntu.

The gnome/unity version of ubuntu 11.04 seems to run poorly on older hardware as far as I can make out; it is certainly slow on mine, but Xubuntu with the lighter xfce desktop should be OK.

You might even like to take a look at Lubuntu, with the even lighter lxde desktop.  That goes very well on my old laptop with only 512 MB ram.  Xubuntu and Lubuntu only come with abiword and gnumeric as office software, but like all other (?)ubuntu family distros can easily be augmented with anything in the repositories, such as Libreoffice, etc etc.
