### Post by iaw4 on 2012-09-01
I just wanted to note for others a problem with my standard Shuttle SH67H3.

I have a dual-link cable and 2560x1600 30" monitor.  I have run this with many video cards and computers.  unfortunately, the Shuttle SH67H3 with its built-in 3000 video set does not seem to be capable of driving this high a resolution.  it maxes out at 1920x1200.  (yes, it is 2012 now---dual-link DVI is only about 10 years old.)  the same issue occurs under Windows 7, so I suspect it is a hardware problem.   I tried different cables and verified elsewhere that they work on full 2560 res.

on the web, there are rumors that the intel HD3000 graphics full resolution works only under displaylink, not under dvi.  (and my shuttle does not have displaylink).

my solution will be to buy a cheap video card. annoying.
