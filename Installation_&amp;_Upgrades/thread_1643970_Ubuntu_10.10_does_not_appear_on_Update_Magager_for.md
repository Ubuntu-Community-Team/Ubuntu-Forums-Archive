### Post by Quackers on 2010-12-12
Go to System > Admin > Update Manager and click Settings in the bottom left corner. Software Sources will open up > click on Updates tab. At the bottom of the screen under Release Upgrade does it show "Long Term Support Releases only"? If it does click on the little arrow and select "Normal Releases" then reload.
