### Post by Sam Fallow on 2010-04-30
Done the same upgrade this morning and found the same problem.

I went to change my display by clicking
System\Preferences\Appearance...

and noticed the 'visual effects' had reverted to 'None'.

After changing to 'Extra' it searched for the driver and all is back to how I want it.

I'm sure there are other ways, but this was quick and easy and worked for me.
