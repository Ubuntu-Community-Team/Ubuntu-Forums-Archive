### Post by earthpigg on 2009-10-16
hi,

Ubuntu 7.10 is no longer supported "officially":

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu_releases#Ubuntu_7.10_.28Gutsy_Gibbon.29](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu_releases#Ubuntu_7.10_.28Gutsy_Gibbon.29)
> Ubuntu 7.10's support ended on April 18, 2009.


the easiest way to save all your stuff is to boot the 8.04 CD, and back up your /home folder to a thumb drive or some other external media.

from there, you can do a fresh install of 8.04 or 9.04 or (in a few weeks) 9.10 and copy that /home folder back onto the new install. restart your computer and *poof* your settings and documents will be restored.
