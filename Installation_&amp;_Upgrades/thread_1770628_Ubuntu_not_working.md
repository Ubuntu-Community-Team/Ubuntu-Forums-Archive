### Post by coffeecat on 2011-05-29
Welcome to the forum and sorry to hear that you are having problems with your HP Mini. Was 11.04 working OK before you encountered this grub problem? I have a HP Mini 110 netbook and Ubuntu 11.04 works very well on it, so I am hopeful we can resolve your problem.

I'm not quite sure what you mean by, "If i try reinstalling it through USB, it gets hanged at the splash screen" Do you mean that the live USB session hangs at the splash screen or that you were able to re-install Ubuntu, but that the new installation hangs?

If you are able to run the live USB session, boot up with it and go to this site:

[http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net/](http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net/)

Follow the instructions there to download and run the boot info script, and then post the contents of the RESULTS.txt file. This contains information about your system and partitions and will help to see what might be wrong.
