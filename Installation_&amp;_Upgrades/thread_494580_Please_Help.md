### Post by 67GTA on 2007-07-07
Go to SYSTEM>ADMINISTRATION>LOGIN WINDOW>>enter your password>LOCAL tab. Drag the package on to the window where it is previewing your gdm login pics. This applies to wallpaper, themes, icons, etc. They can be auto installed by opening their respective managers.

Wallpaper: Go to SYSTEM>PREFERENCES>DESKTOP BACKGROUND(drag&drop)
Themes,icons: Go to SYSTEM>PREFERENCES>THEME(drag&drop)
