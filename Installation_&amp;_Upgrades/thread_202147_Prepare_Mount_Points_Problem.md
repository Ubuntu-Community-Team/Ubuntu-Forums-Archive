### Post by NZ-Wanderer on 2006-07-27
Found a temp workaround to this problem...

When at the screen, right click the icon on taskbar and click "UNMaximise" then right click icon again and click "MOVE" this will allow you to move the screen up so that you can see the prompts at the bottom.
