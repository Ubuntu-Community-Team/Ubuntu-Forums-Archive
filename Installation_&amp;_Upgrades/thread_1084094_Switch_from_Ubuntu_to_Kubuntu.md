### Post by davec64 on 2009-03-01
Why not just install KDE from synaptic (assuming you've got enough hard disk space)?

Then when the login screen comes up change your session to KDE in the Options menu.

When you install apps they should all work as they will install their specific dependancies. i.e. if your in Gnome and install a KDE app the required dependancies will install.

The only issue you might have is the appearance of the app.
