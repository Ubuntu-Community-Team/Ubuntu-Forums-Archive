### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-12-10
That's because there isn't one. You have three choices:

Upgrade through 12.10>13.04>13.10
Do a clean install of 13.10
Wait until 14.04 is released (April 2014) and you *can* upgrade directly from one LTS to the next. (12.04>14.04.)

Just out of curiousity, why are you wanting to upgrade? 12.04 is supported for five years. The interims are supported for nine months. The bugs might be just about out of 13.10 by the time it becomes obsolete and is out of support.
