### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-04-15
Performance wise, with 2 or 3 GB of RAM, you will not need swap at all. However, if planning to hibernate, some amount of swap is essential, because RAM will be copied to it. In my case, (1 GB RAM) swap started to get used only after running Beryl and vmware, not until then.
