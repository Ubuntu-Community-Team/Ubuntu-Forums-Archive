### Post by QIII on 2014-09-27
Hello!

Attempting to access that url is returning an internal server error.  What that is would be pretty hard to determine from outside.

You might try a different mirror -- I assume "ph" is Philipines.

Open the Update Manager and click the "Settings" button.

On the "Ubuntu software" tab, change "Download from" to another mirror -- the United States, for instance.

Attempt your updates again.
