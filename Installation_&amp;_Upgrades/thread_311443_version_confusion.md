### Post by Sef on 2006-12-02
> Also, if Edgy is final, what's all this about Dapper being the long-term supported version and nothing about Edgy being long term supported?

Dapper is Long Term Support because it is the stable version, so it is supported for 3 years on the desktop and 5 years on the server.  The packages are older than Edgy, but more stable.

Edgy is an unstable version, so it is supported for only 18 months.  The packages are newer than Dapper, but they may also be more unstable.  

> Is Edgy in final release form? If so, are they going to release it for free on shipit?


Yes, it is in its final form.   No, it will not be sent out via shippit since shippit sends out the long term stable version.
