### Post by bruenig on 2007-04-17
Ubuntu releases a new version every 6 months. Upgrading ubuntu should be as easy as opening your update-manager once it is out and installing it.

Here are the feisty specifications: [https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/feisty](https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/feisty)

And here are the upgrade notes for it, although they seem to indicate that once it is released, it will be point and click stuff as opposed to having to issue a command to allow you to upgrade to what is now a developmental release: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FeistyUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FeistyUpgrades)
