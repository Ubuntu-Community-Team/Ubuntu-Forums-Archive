### Post by tornadoss on 2010-05-25

---

### Post by rijidij on 2010-05-25
When you start your computer there should be a message saying press <key> for setup. Where <key> is usually, but not necessarily, F2 or DEL.
Go into setup and set your CD drive to be the 1st boot device.
Then reboot with your live CD in the drive.
