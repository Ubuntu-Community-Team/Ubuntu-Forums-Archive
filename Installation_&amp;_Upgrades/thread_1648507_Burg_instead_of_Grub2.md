Burg brings some eye-candy to your Grub menu. I haven't tried it myself but we keep on seeing some problems on and off caused by Burg here on the forums. If you can survive those, no harm in trying Burg. If you don't feel confident or it is a critical system/server or whatever, I would recommend not to try it.

Anyways, if some problems arise, they can be fixed without a re-install. Just a matter of re-installing Grub and we can definitely guide you then :-)

And as a last note, many people are using Burg (I know) and it works for them ;-)
