### Post by bcbc on 2010-10-25
You can use a normal swap partition with wubi. You add it exactly the same way as you would on a direct install. It even allows you to hibernate provided you update the relevant files.

However, this may not help with your ram issues. I have read about problems in e.g. ureadahead that can blow it's brains out on 256MB ram. You might want to consider some of the stripped down ubuntu's of which I know v. little.
