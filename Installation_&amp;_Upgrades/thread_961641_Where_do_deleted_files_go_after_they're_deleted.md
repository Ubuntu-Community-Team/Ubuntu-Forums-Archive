### Post by Therion on 2008-10-28
Open your "Home" folder.  In the toolbar at the top open the viewing options and select the option "Show Hidden Folders".  

Look for and check inside of .trash for your missing file or files. 

A "." before a folder or file name makes the file or folder invisible unless, and until, you've enabled viewing hidden files and folders.

Oh, forgot too mention there's the regular trash bin found most easily, I suppose, under your "Places" menu.
