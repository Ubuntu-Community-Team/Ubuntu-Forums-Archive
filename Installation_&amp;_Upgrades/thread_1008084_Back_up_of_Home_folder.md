### Post by Partyboi2 on 2008-12-11
You can move your current /home folder to its own partition which will allow you to reinstall without wiping your /home. See [[COLOR=Blue]here[/COLOR]]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome").
If you want to backup your /home to another medium then you could try using [[COLOR=Blue]hubackup[/COLOR]]("http://www.ubuntugeek.com/hubackup-backup-application-for-ubuntu-home-users.html")
