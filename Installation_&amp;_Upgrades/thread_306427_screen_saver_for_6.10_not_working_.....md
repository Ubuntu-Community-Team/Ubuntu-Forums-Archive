### Post by K.Mandla on 2006-11-25
Is it possible your graphics card needs different drivers? If you get a lot of flickers or slowdowns during 3D acceleration, you might need proprietary drivers. 

What kind of video card are you using? 

As an additional note, the last time I checked, Xubuntu still enabled a lot of screensavers that weren't installed by default ... which means xscreensaver showed errors whenever it kicked in and picked an uninstalled-but-enabled screensaver. (Did that make sense?) I don't know that that is your problem, but if you're using Xubuntu, it's worth mentioning.
