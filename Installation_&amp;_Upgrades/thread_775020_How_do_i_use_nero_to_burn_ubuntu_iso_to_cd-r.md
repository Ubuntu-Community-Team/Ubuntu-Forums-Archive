Make sure to check the md5sum of the downloaded iso:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso)

Nero should have an option "Burn image to disk", burn at a low speed(8x or less)...don't burn as data or a bootable cd.

### Post by Rallg on 2008-04-29
If you have a good download (by checking the MD5) and the problem is with your CD burner, and if you cannot figure out how to make Nero burn an ISO image: Have a look at Alex Feinman's "ISO Recorder." It has few buttons to push, and thus fewer opportunities to go wrong.

One other thing: If you are at a public computer (such as at a college library), then the function you need may simply be disabled.
