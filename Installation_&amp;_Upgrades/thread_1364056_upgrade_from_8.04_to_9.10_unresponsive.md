### Post by raymondh on 2009-12-25
Hello and Merry Christmas, as well.

Previous poster is correct.  You have to upgrade sequentially from hardy - intrepid - jaunty and then karmic.  Also, you have to be updated in each version before upgrading to the next.

Consider a fresh install of Karmic.  Or, since there is no downgrade path, a fresh re-install of hardy and wait till april 2010 for the next LTS version.  That upgrade you can do without the 6-month versions as you will be going from one LTS version to another LTS.

Post back if you need assistance in fresh (possibly MANUAL) installs.  If so, post also a screenshot of gparted to help visualize the HD.
