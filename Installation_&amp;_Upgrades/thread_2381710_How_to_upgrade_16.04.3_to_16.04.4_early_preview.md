### Post by deadflowr on 2018-01-04
You can get the xenial dauily build here:
[http://cdimages.ubuntu.com/xenial/daily-live/current/]("http://cdimages.ubuntu.com/xenial/daily-live/current/")
and you can enable the -proposed repository to get an early preview of what's to come.
Fair warning that proposed is all about early testing so don't be surprised if something breaks with it enabled.
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Testing/EnableProposed]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Testing/EnableProposed")

Note you have over a month before the current planned release of 16.04.4 (Marked currently as February 15th)
So not much may be any different from enabling proposed or downloading the daily build.
