### Post by ElSeeDee on 2007-10-19
I'm not calling it a problem because I resolved it very easily. I was one of those that, other than it taking a long time, had an essentially flawless upgrade. When I re-booted I was presented with the opportunity to install an ATI restricted driver. The pop-up said it was necessary for 3d & 2d effects. Being a big fan of Beryl in 7.04, I installed the driver. Then immediately went and tried to activate compiz. And was promptly informed by pop-up that the compiz manager was not loaded. 

Synaptic. Checked compiz dependencies. Yep, all loaded. Tried again. Failed again. 

Ooh! Wait a minute. How 'bout if I uninstall the ATI driver? And ya know what? When I unloaded the driver that was allegedly required for 3d & 2d effects, compiz ran beautifully.

So, if any others are having compiz issues with the restricted ATI video driver, unload it and try again.
