### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-12-10
Yes of course, that is the problem, you are burning the CD to fast. Burn the CD at a speed of 4X or less so as to reduce the chances of the Live CD being corrupted. That can solve your problem.

### Post by inselaffe on 2007-12-10
That did the trick! A disk burnt at 4x booted. I really didn't consider that that would be a problem on a brand new machine. Thanks.
