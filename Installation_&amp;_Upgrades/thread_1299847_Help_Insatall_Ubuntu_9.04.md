### Post by phillw on 2009-10-24
One of the 1st things to check is your cd.

Ensure you burn at 4X speed (or slower) - many people report 'funnies' at higher speeds.

Next, check your md5 checksum on the disc.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

says how.

also - clean your cd-drive !!
