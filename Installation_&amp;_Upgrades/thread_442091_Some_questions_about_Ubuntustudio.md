### Post by amaroKer on 2007-05-13
1) You may need to install the proprietary nVidia drivers to get your 1200x800.  Mine worked on 1200x800 out of the box, but it still might be worth a shot.

2) Automatix2 can be installed from [http://www.getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation#Easy_Direct_Installation]("http://www.getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation#Easy_Direct_Installation").

3) They just wanted something that looks professional, I guess.  I don't think it looks like Vista.  

4) Ubuntu Studio is an Ubuntu variant that includes a bunch of sound, video, and graphics programs, and drivers for some specialized hardware.
Do those hardware items work in Mandriva and normal Ubuntu?
If you're getting it all to work with Mandriva, there are probably driver issues with all of your non-working hardware.  Unless you want to pick around and make those things work (which I assume you do), I'd suggest staying with whatever is working for you right now.
