### Post by threexk on 2009-11-26
Found this thread (among several others) on the same general problem:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=291183](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=291183)

Greyed-out distribution updates appear to be a not-uncommon problem.

For this specific problem, I opened Synaptic and searched for gij.  Its information said "This is a transitional package.  It can be removed."  So, I completely removed it and "gij" disappeared from Update Manager.

Not sure why it stayed there in the first place, but it's gone.
