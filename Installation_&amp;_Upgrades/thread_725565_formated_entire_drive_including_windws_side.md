### Post by Cresho on 2008-03-16
disk recovery is simple and easy if you know what you are doing.  Do not do anything else to that disk.  just unplug it and keep it away till you get someone very knowledgeable to restore it or you can restore when you gain enough knowledge.  The more you tinker with it, the more you overwrite on the space where your "invisible" data is located.

There are some tools that can undelete a ext2 and ext3 partition.  Your stuff is not lost,  Just not accessible through common use.  I have recovered lost ext2, ext3, fat, fat32, ntfs partitions with no problems.
