### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-03-18
Do you have winzip installed by any chance?

Winzip automatically associates itself with .iso files, making them open with winzip when double-clicked.
If you take a close look you should find that the files you downloaded ***are*** iso files, you just need to open them with a burning application rather than winzip.
