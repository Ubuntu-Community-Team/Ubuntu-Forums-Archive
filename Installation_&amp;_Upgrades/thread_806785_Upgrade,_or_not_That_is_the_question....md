### Post by DJ_Peng on 2008-05-25
You may not be able to upgrade the apps too far unless you manually installed them. Most of the upgrades won't reach the repos unless someone backports them. But your customizations should stick after an upgrade. I've done upgrades from Feisty to both Gutsy and Hardy and didn't lose a single one of my settings (other than some sound settings when Hardy changed the default folder but that was no big deal to fix). You rarely need to wipe and do a fresh install to upgrade. The dist-upgrade handles it all for you, plus that way you do get the newer versions of things that got upgraded in the OS update.
