### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-08-12
Just to be clear. The third image? You do not see that when you click on the Ubuntu icon at the top right of the password panel? By the way, Gnome Session Flashback will not give you the Gnome window manager.

The default option is Ubuntu. When we install Gnome Session Flashback we get two more options, Gnome Flashback (Compiz} and Gnome Flashback (Metacity). We get the option to switch by clicking the Ubuntu icon at the top right of the password panel. And which ever session we choose then becomes the default and its icon replaces the Ubuntu icon and we can switch sessions by clicking that icon.

Both Unity and Gnome Flashback (Compiz) run on the Compiz compositor/window manager. The Gnome Flashback (Metacity) option runs on the Metacity compositor/window manager. What were you expecting to get? What do you mean by "gnome window manager?" Gnome 3 shell?

[http://www.gnome.org/gnome-3/](http://www.gnome.org/gnome-3/)
