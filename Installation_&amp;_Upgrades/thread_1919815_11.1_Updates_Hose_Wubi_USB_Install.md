### Post by darkod on 2012-02-03
Wubi is unpredictable with updates and upgrades. It was never developed to be a long term install, just a quick way of testing if you like it. Even the main developer himself said that he never planned people will intent to keep using wubi long term.

Personally I always recommend installing ubuntu properly in its own partition as soon as you decide you like it.

Apart from that I am not aware of any exact issue right now, but I do remember wubi 9.10 was also giving loads of problems when updated too.
