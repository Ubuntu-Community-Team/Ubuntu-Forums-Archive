### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-04-28
None.

What do you expect? Unity is Ubuntu. But as Ubuntu is also Linux/Gnome and open source as well, we can at present install other user interfaces.

Ubuntu/Unity is the distribution from Canonical.

By the way, things have had to be left out of the distribution to keep the ISO image size as low as possible. That is another reason why other user interfaces would not be part of the default distribution.
