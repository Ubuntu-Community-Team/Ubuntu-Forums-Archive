### Post by bruban on 2007-10-22
Jay,

Two of your options (there are probably many more):

_Option 1_

Its been a while since I used 5.10, however there is an update manager with the product that should allow you upgrade the install.

With 7.04 and 7.10, it is under the menu:  System>Administration>Update Manager


It should be something similar under 5.10. Open this tool and you should see an option at the top labeled Distribution Upgrade.


As long as your PC is connected to the Internet, this tool should allow you to upgrade, however it may take you through a number of interim Ubuntu versions e.g. 6.06, 6.10, 7.04 then 7.10 (sorry I'm not really sure if this is the case). Then again it may take you straight to 7.10.
Hopefully someone else who knows can shed some light on this.



_Option 2_

Is there anything in your 5.10 installation that you want to keep? 

Have you been using it to store files, send/receive email etc?

If not you may be better off installing 7.10 from the LiveCD and not worrying about trying to upgrade 5.10.
