### Post by snowpine on 2011-09-09
10.04 means "April 2010" and so most software in Lucid is over 1 year old.

The browser is the exception, actually. Firefox 3.6.22 was [released just 3 days ago]("https://www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/3.6.22/releasenotes/") and is considered very stable and secure by the Ubuntu developers.

If you want to install a newer Firefox/Chromium then just do a quick forum Search, the topic has been discussed many times. ;)
