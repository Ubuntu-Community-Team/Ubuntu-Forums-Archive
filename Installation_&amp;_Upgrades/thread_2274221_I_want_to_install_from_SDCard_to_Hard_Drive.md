### Post by westie457 on 2015-04-18
Unless you can somehow get the bios to recognize the SD card at boot time it will not work.

However you could try booting the ISO with Grub or Windows boot-loader. Here are links for each.

[http://www.sevenforums.com/tutorials/70559-boot-iso-without-burning-disk.html](http://www.sevenforums.com/tutorials/70559-boot-iso-without-burning-disk.html)
