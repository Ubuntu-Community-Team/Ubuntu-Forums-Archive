### Post by linear on 2006-03-30
Things to look at:

1. Check integrity of iso (MD5 checksum).
2. Did you burn the iso to CD as an image? (Some help here: [BurningIsoHowto]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BurningIsoHowto"))
3. Try burning CD at a slow speed (4x).
