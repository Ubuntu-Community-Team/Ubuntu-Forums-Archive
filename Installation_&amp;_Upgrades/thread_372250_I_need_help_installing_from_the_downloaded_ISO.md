### Post by ComputerGuy56 on 2007-02-28
First make sure you followed the instructions on how to burn it. Then restart your computer without the CD and in the begining it says something like "Press F1 for booting menu" Press F1 and make sure the computer boots from your CDROM. Then press save changes, restart your computer, insert the CD, restart the computer again, and it should start going.
