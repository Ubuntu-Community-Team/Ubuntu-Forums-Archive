### Post by spr18 on 2010-09-13
FYI... Installed 10.04 on an MSI Xslim X600 yesterday.

The System:
Intel Centrino 2 Solo 1.4ghz
4 GB Ram
320 HD

It feels like an out of the box computer.  I have had no hiccups so far.  Graphics work great.  Both USB ports are completely recognized.  All installs seem to work flawlessly with laptop. Windows seven made the computer real sluggish.  Ubuntu runs faster and cleaner.  I have not noticed the lag at all.  Bluetooth was recognized immediately.  I have done nothing to tweak the machine.
