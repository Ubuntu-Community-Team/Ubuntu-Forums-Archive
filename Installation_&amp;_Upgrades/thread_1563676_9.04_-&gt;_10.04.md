### Post by mörgæs on 2010-08-29
No, if you really want to upgrade, you have to go to 9.10 as a middle step.

I would not recommend this, though. Best is to install 10.04 with wired internet access during the installation and then fix the BCM4312 problems after the installation. 

It is a classical problem and normally no big deal. You can google these fora for 'BCM4312', and then you will find countless threads on the topic.
