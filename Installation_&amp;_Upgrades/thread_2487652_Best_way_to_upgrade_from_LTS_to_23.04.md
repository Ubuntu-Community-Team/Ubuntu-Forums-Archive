### Post by deadflowr on 2023-06-11
That would be one way to do it. Make perfect sense.

Another would be to wait until 22.10 reaches end of life.
Unless they've changed things very recently, they will move the upgrade path to the next supported release when that happens.
So a direct upgrade from 22.04 to 23.04 should be possible.
(And when 23.04 hits end of life the upgrade path will move up to 23.10, and so on)
They've been doing it this way for 10 years now. Ever since they set non-lts releases for only 9 months support.
