### Post by linuxwizard on 2007-10-22
I have seen alot of issues with people having problems with the extra repos they have added to their source list. Removing or disable them will take care of the issues.

Using packages from repositories not controlled by Ubuntu is not recommended as it can be a security risk and may break or [COLOR="Blue"]complicate your upgrade[/COLOR].
