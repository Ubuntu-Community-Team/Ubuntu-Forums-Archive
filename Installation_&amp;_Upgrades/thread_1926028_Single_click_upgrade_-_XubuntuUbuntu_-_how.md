### Post by mörgæs on 2012-02-15
An upgrade will preserve the Xubuntu environment as you know it. No Unity packages will be added. 

However, at lot of other things can go wrong during an online upgrade. I recommend staying with 11.04 for now, and when 12.04 is getting stable a few months after release you can do a fresh install of this one.
