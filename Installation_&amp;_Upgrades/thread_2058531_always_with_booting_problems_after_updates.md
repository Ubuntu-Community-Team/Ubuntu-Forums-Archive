### Post by SeijiSensei on 2012-09-15
You didn't tell us what version of Ubuntu you are using.  If it's not 10.04 or 12.04, you did not install a "long-term support" version.  It's possible your version has reached end-of-life which means updates are not being maintained for it any longer. [Check here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases").

Also if you installed any software from "PPAs" they may not be maintained any longer either.  Those are not official repositories and may fall out of date if the maintainer loses interest.
