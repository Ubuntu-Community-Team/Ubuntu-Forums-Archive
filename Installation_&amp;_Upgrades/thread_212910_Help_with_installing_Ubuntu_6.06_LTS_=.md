### Post by diepruis on 2006-07-10
You want option 1 (Resize IDE1 master, partition #1 (hda1) and use freed space). You might want to defragment your HD first though. Also, be warned this could damage your windows partition irreperably, be sure you have a backup of your important stuff and a winXP cd, or better yet an image of your HD in something like Norton Ghost.
