### Post by BaffledMollusc on 2010-10-10
Go to System->Administration->Update Manager, click on "Settings" button, click on the "Updates" tab, then change the "Release upgrade" setting to "Normal releases."

That setting is probably currently set to "Long term releases only", and since 10.10 isn't a long term release, it won't show up.

Oh, and after thant you might have to click on the "Check" button in the Upgrade Manager to make it see if an upgrade is available.
