### Post by darkod on 2010-02-15
LTS means Long Term Supported. Usually it comes out every 2yr but it's not always a rule.
The next version, 10.04 comes out late April, and that's why it's called 10.04 (2010 April). It will be LTS. Next would be 10.10 which won't be LTS version, and so on... Every six months, so far.
