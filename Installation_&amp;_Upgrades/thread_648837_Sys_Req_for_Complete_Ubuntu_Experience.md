### Post by PreviousN on 2007-12-24
I think you don't have enough ram. I may have misread the attached file, but it said you have 256 MB of ram. If you got 512 it'd run great. 

Personally I've got a 2.4Ghz pentium 4 with 512 megabytes of ram and a geforce 440mx and I've got wobbly windows and the cube. I also run stuff with wine (which is not an emulator...so it should, and does, run programs just as fast as they would on windows). 

The thing about linux is it fits a lot of people with a lot of machines. I see people on this forum with geforce 8800s in sli (approx 700$ + worth of video cards) and crazy cpus. I'm sure they get the most, but then theres' people like me, who get pretty good effects on a.....7 year old computer. 

Cheers! Buy more ram and you'll see the cube :-)
