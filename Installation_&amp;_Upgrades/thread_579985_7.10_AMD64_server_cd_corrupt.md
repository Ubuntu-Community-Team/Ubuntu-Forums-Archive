### Post by cboling on 2007-10-18
I discovered the real problem:  Out of disk space.

I accidentally created my /var partition as 8MB instead of 8GB (that lovely default minimum when you leave off the suffix).  Naturally, that wasn't big enough to hold the components.   

So... Problem = No free space on destination.  Error Msg = Corrupt Source.

Kind of the opposite of what I used to see on Windows all the time, where an app would report that you were out of disk space, when in reality you didn't have read permissions (or similar) on the source....
