### Post by snowpine on 2009-09-21
No, because Ubuntu 8.10 uses Openoffice 2.4 by default.

A better way to get OO3 is from the launchpad PPA: [https://launchpad.net/~openoffice-pkgs/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~openoffice-pkgs/+archive/ppa)

You could also download and install it directly from the OO website if you prefer.
