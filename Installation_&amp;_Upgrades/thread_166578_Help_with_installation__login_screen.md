### Post by Herman on 2006-04-27
> Hi I have again tried to instal and was successful but now have a problem with drivers i can download with my windows computer but need to instal them on to linux how can i do this.Congratulations on your successful install. What drivers do you need?
 Do you mean drivers for things like your printer and digital camera? Probably you won't need to add extra drivers. They are probably already there.
Just try plugging in your printer and click 'System', 'Administration','Printing', and click on the 'New Printer' icon. From there you should be able to read the instructions and just follow the prompts, and it's pretty simple to get most printers working,
  As for your digital camera just try plugging it in and turning it on, it will be simple, you'll see.
I'm not so sure about scanners, but they if they aren't in with the printer drivers there might be some in 'Applications','graphics','Xsane Image Scanner'.
Good luck and have fun, regards, Herman :-D
