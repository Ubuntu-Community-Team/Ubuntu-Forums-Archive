### Post by sikander3786 on 2012-05-20
As you've already experience the upgrade from 11.04 to 11.10 working for you, it might be successful this time again.

It isn't mandatory to upgrade to 12.04 at all. 11.10 is a recent version, although not newest obviously, and it will be supported till April 2013. You can keep using it till then. 12.04 on the other hand, will be supported for the next 5 years.

But if you had to do a clean install, why did you go for 11.04 again? Why didn't you choose to do a fresh install of 11.10 or 12.04?
