### Post by darkod on 2010-05-28
You can't upgrade directly from 9.04, only from 9.10 and 8.04.

You would have to do two sequential upgrades, so it leaves a lot of space something to go wrong.

I suggest you start planning for a clean install of 10.04. You don't have to do it right now, 9.04 is supported for another 6 months. Just start planning it, and how to transfer your settings and data with least effort.
