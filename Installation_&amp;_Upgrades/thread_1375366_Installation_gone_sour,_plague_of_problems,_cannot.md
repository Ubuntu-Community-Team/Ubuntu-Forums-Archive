### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-01-07
Just remove the Ubuntu Wubi in the Add/Remove programs list in XP. When you install Ubuntu the installer will walk you through the partition process. All you have to do is move a slider to specify how much you want Ubuntu to take up, hit next, and you are basically done.
