### Post by lao_V on 2005-02-16
Ron,
 
 You can type the following at the bash prompt, once logged in as yourself
 
 $ sudo shutdown -h now
 
 You can then safely switch off your PC

### Post by Wim Sturkenboom on 2005-02-17
