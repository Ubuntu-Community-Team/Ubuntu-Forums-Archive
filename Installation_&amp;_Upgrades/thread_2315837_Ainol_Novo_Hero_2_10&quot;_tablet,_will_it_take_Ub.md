### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-03-03
People have problems putting Linux on laptop & desktop machines even though the developers work hard to make Linux compatible with all kinds of hardware. It is a completely different situation with phones & tablets. You would have to port Ubuntu for devices to that hardware yourself.

[https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/](https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/)
