### Post by snowpine on 2009-10-09
When 10.04 comes out in April, you will be able to upgrade directly from 8.04 to 10.04. (Hypothetically, of course... since 10.04 doesn't exist yet. :))

9.04 is not an LTS release, so you'd have to upgrade to 8.10 first. (And then to 9.10 to get to 10.04.)

(edit) You will find there are many users on the forum who recommend a fresh reinstall irregardless. I do not necessarily fall into this camp, however, there are good arguments for a fresh reinstall--for example, it is the only way to take advantage of the new ext4 filesystem.
