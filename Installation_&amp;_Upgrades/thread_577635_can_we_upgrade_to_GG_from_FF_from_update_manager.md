### Post by mysticrider92 on 2007-10-16
Yes, you can upgrade to Gutsy from Feisty. Once it is out, you will just need to run: > gksu update-manager -c -d in a terminal, and it will tell you that a new distribution upgrade is available and allow you to upgrade.

Some people have had problems with this method breaking their systems, while I have never had a problem with it. Just be careful, and follow all of the steps they show and you should be fine.

Also, for simple questions like this, Google is your friend. ;)

### Post by MaximusTG on 2007-10-16
When Gutsy Gibbon is officially out, it will appear as a distribution upgrade in Update Manager. However, I would advice you to backup your /home and maybe conf files you edited. Placing your /home on a separate partition is also a wise idea. And don't forget to burn a separate Gutsy Gibbon CD just in case the upgrade fails.
