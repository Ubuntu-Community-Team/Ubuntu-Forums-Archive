### Post by DJ_Peng on 2008-12-15
There is a [known]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=6088320") [issue]("http://bugs.winehq.org/show_bug.cgi?id=14658") with fonts not showing up in WINE. It's been fixed in WINE as of version [1.1.18]("http://www.winehq.org/announce/1.1.8") so you may need an update. You may also need to update your video drivers. I have posted a bit of info on my [blog]("http://nancib.wordpress.com/2008/11/02/does-anyone-know-what-borked-wine-this-week/") about the problem and the fix, although it's not working for 100% of my readers. (Mods: Please forgive the self promotion, but it's the best link I've been able to find that deals with the problem.)
