### Post by Pumalite on 2008-02-25
256 MB of RAM or less>Xubuntu Alternate CD: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/releases/7.10/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/releases/7.10/release/)

You cannot boot a Live CD unless you make a 500 MB /swap partition ahead of time, and if installed, Ubuntu is too heavy a Desktop for your system.
