### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-12-30
It is System Settings>Software and Updates>Updates tab and change the setting at "Notify me of a new Ubuntu version" from "For Long Term Support versions" to "For any new version." And then update.

But my advice would to be stay with an LTS version because if you upgrade to 14.10 you will also need to upgrade to 15.04 and then to 15.10 and then to 16.04. Whereas, if you stay on 14.04 and leave the setting at "For Long Term Support versions" you will be notified when 16.04 comes out that you can upgrade directly from 14.04 to 16.04.
