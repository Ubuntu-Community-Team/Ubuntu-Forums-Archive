### Post by Spaceman9 on 2008-05-13
Thankx for the help Pumalite. I was reading the page you gave me a link to when I thought of a different way.

I have just 3 hard drives so I unplugged the cable and power from drive 2 and 3 and tried installing Ubuntu 8.04 again. It worked! I didn't have the Error 15 boot problem.

Then I hooked up the cable and power to drive 2 and booted my computer and Ubuntu saw the drive with no problems. Cept I had to change permissions to access the files of course. Then I hooked up the cable and power to drive 3 and that works too. I found krusader is great for changing permissions.

This probably isn't the way most people would do it but it worked.
