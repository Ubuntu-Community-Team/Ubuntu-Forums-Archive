### Post by prshah on 2010-05-02
> **ITT1 said:**
> 
Happy to login at startup, or if using sudo, but having to put in password everytime I sit down to do something is such a time waster!

Open System-Preferences-Screensaver, and uncheck the option "Lock screen when screensaver is active".
