### Post by Pariah819 on 2011-09-15
It is likely that rebooting during a partial upgrade won't work out well.  Since your upgrade seems frozen though it sounds like you don't have a choice, did you back everything up before you started the upgrade?  I would recommend backing up as much as you can before you power down if you haven't already (this post is already 9 hours old).  It is always a good idea to boot the new OS off of a CD or USB so that you can verify everything works on your hardware.
