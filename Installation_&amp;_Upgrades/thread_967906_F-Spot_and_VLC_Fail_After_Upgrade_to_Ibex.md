### Post by gregconquest on 2008-11-02
I thought mine was fine, but:

- When I try to play a video in VLC, I get the VLC frame and controls, but no video. It is in minimized mode with the audio playing in the background.

On a slightly different problem with a fix, F-Spot was automatically opening when I clicked on my "Places" menu item. I fixed this by:

Places -- Computer -- File System -- right-click "mnt" -- Properties -- Open With -- Open Folder.

After doing the above, all my shortcuts to folders on mnt now open in Nautilus and not F-Spot.
