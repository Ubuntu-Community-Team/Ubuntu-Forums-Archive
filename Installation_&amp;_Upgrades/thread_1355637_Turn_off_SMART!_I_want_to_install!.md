### Post by Grenage on 2009-12-15
Do it in the BIOS.


When the computer loads up, you generally press DEL to get into the BIOS menu (watch the screen closely when you turn it on).  Also, triple-check your disks using manufacturer tools; SMART doesn't lie, and your disk will have developed a 'fault'.  Whether or not that fault will cause you any problems is another matter.
