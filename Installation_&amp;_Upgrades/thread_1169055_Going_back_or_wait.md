### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-05-24
> **ElectroWaycrow said:**
> I upgraded and now my machine works less than it did with 8.10; mainly video issues and loss of sandisk connectivity. I'm on a Toshiba Satellite AMD 64 with ATI Radon graphics x1200.

Should I wait until fixes/patches come out, or just back up my files and reload 8.10? 
The video problem has been documented again and again in these forums -- AMD/ATI dropped support for "older" cards -- which, in some cases, has resulted in lower quality or slower video, and in other cases, no video.

There will be no fixes or patches because the card vendor has stopped writing the drivers needed.  The only solution now is to use the open source driver -- which is installed by default.

Don't know about the sandisk problem; can't help you with that.
