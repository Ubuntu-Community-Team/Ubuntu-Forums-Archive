### Post by Melpomene on 2011-05-24
I fixed this, 
I downloaded the package with wget from [http://packages.ubuntu.com/natty/amd64/ngircd/download](http://packages.ubuntu.com/natty/amd64/ngircd/download) 
I then installed it with dpkg -i ngircd.deb
Everything worked out perfect.
