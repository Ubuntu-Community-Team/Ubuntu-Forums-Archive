### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-02
Ubuntu 9.10 will work fine with your ATI chipsets -- but you will be relegated to using the open source drivers.  

Good news is that these will be installed by default, so there is nothing you have to do to make that happen.

Bad news is that there are no ATI restricted drivers that will work your your chipset and Ubuntu 9.x versions.

So, as long as you're not trying to do 3D accelerated gaming, you should be OK.
