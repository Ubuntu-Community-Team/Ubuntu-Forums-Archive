Heres the deal, I was an ubuntu 6.06 user because of the LTS, but I realized that the siht is free and always will be so why worry about how long theres support. If canonical wants to support this distro indefinitely so be it.  IDK, I gave up on 6.06 because of the shttiy ati driver, I mean come on ...my radeon is capable of 2000 fps+ and i was getting 320.  I play openarena,stepmania,wolfenstein3, etc. I need some fps.  Not to be greedy but the ATI driver in feisty by default is giving me 700-1000 fps with no modification to my xorg.conf, thats sweet for linux.  in fedora, redhat, yellow dog, and debian, i never broke 150 fps which is lame and ubuntu is the first distro to give me stable 3d acceleration.  Thanks UBUNTU TEAM, AND CANONICAL, you guys made a linux lover out of me. I brag about this OS to all my friends and tell them its time to switch to linux because of it.  OH and btw, someone needs to coordinate with the Ndiswrapper team and tell them that in order to properly install drivers for an unsupported wifi card you MUST DO FOUR THINGS  1)be logged in as your user and under sudo to your desktop, 2)make sure the file names.inf have matching cAsES, 3)have and corresponding(like-named) .sys files in the same directory as the .inf files you are trying to install, 4)RESTART !!!!! this will recompile the kernal and allow for ndiswrapper to reload and run the drivers for your card.
