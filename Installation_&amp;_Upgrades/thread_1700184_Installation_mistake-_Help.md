### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-03-04
I think you've lost XP if you used the entire disk.

"Entire disk" means exactly what it says, I'm afraid; a disk is a disk, not a partition as M$ would sometimes have us believe, with their C and D disks etc etc.

You may get something back with [**testdisk**]("http://download.cnet.com/TestDisk-PhotoRec/3000-2248_4-10511775.html"), but I hope you have backups of important data, as much of it could be lost.
