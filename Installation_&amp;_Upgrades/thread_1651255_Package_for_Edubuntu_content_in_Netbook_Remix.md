### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-12-25
The Netbook Edition is not a separate OS, it's a stock Ubuntu with modified interface. That said, I really don't know what installing **edubuntu-desktop** would do to the artwork of the Netbook Edition. You might want to try installing **ubuntu-edu-primary** or **ubuntu-edu-secondary** instead. The two are application bundles without all the Edubuntu related bling.
