### Post by rickdog on 2007-09-17
As I write, I'm actually installing Kubuntu on a friends laptop. It's a Toshiba M35X-S114 and aside from the need for a memory upgrade and later a larger hard drive, it's an okay low budget system that's a couple of years old.

After a 6 or 7 installs on my home computers, I finally convinced one of my friends to let me straighten out her old tired windows laptop.

I didn't push either, I just suggested it after fixing her wireless configuration and telling her that she didn't really need the linksys wireless card she bought.

The reason I went with Kubuntu instead of Ubuntu was the way KDE's network manager is totally user friendly with wireless or wired, without the need for any helper apps, like wlassistant or the other one.

Anyway, let's wish her luck!
