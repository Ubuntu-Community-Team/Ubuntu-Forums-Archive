### Post by louieb on 2007-03-26
You can try the [super grub disk]("http://supergrub.forjamari.linex.org/"). Just be aware that because the hardware changed that its likely that neither Ubuntu or XP will work. And you could screw up booting to VISTA.  A reinstall of XP and Ubuntu is probably the safer path.
Look at the Dual Boot link in my signature and see what Herman has to say about Creating a boot disk. You could try that first and see if Ubuntu and XP will work on your new computer without being reinstalled. There are some other boot manager disks out there might try Google to see what out there.
