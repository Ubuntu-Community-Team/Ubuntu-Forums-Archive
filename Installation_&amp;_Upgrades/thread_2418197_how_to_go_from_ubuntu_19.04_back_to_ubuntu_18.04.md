### Post by Rubi1200 on 2019-05-03
Downgrading has as many potential pitfalls as upgrading. For example, when you upgrade certain packages and their dependencies are upgraded to fit with and make the new version happy. But when you downgrade not everything goes back to the way it was before.

If you can describe what the problems are perhaps we can help resolve them with you.

If not, then the only "safe" way is to do what kc1di suggested which is to backup important data and do a clean reinstall. Anything else is asking for problems again.
