### Post by miriad on 2008-02-18
With it being an external hard drive, there are a few questions.

1.) Does the hard drive adapter support booting from the drive (External drives are really internal drives with some sort of adapter to USB or Firewire, sometimes the adapter is cheaper and wont support booting, and thus not support MBR read/write)

2.) Does your motherboard support external hard drive booting? (USB or Firewire, whichever the hard drive has)
