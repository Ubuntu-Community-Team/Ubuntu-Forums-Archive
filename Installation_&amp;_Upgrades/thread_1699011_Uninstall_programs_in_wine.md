### Post by An Sanct on 2011-03-03
Programs installed through PlayOnLinux must be uninstalled with that program too.

The location of the C: drive is different for POL too, its somewhere in ~\.PlayOnLinux, you can right click in the POL list of programs and select "open the applications directory", to get there.
