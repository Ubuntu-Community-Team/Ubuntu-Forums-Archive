### Post by nugencs on 2008-04-09
That happened to me before, when I was trying to install ubuntu 7.10 on my old Satellite Dynabook laptop. The liveCD worked well but problematic when I tried to install it. After several frustrating searches on the internet, the closest I could come up to a solution was to install Ubuntu Feisty first, then upgraded to Ubuntu Gutsy. Although it was a lengthy installation, it worked like a charm. Apparently, there's something wrong with the Gutsy installer, which seemed to be incompatible with certain computer configurations or hardware, I may be wrong.
