### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-04-20
Well, it is possible, but it's a pain in the a**.

You would have to wipe your ipod, or make a 700 MB partition and hope your ipod music doesn't get hosed.

You bios has to support USB or Firewire booting.  

You have to dd write the iso image to the ipod's empty partition.

What version are you upgrading from?  You would probably have better luck doing a network-based upgrade.

Find a Linux User's Group near you and bum a CD.  

Fill out an application for free Feisty CD's.

Enjoy your existing distro until you get a new drive.
