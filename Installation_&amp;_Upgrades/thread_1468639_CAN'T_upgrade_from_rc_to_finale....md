Like I have said lots of times before: If you installed the from rc cd (or any other milestone or daily cd or upgraded from karmic at any point during development) and have installed all updates that have come after you installed, you already have the final version.
Did you expect a bunch of updates when the final version was released? Who would have tested those new packages, if they had not already been in the repos well *before* the final release?
