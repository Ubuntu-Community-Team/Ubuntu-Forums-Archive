### Post by QIII on 2009-09-09
If you are interested in helping to make Karmic better, it would help the community if you would jot down your experiences and report bugs on Launchpad.

I've seen a couple of your other posts (ie:  an easier method -- read "GUI" -- for making resolution work), which might get some traction if you make the suggestions there rather than (for want of a better word, I'm not trying to be critical here) "complaining" about it here.

(Just as a note:  drivers are a touchy issue.  Most OEMs have only recently come around to the idea of making truly Linux compatible drivers, so some of your graphics issues probably stem from the open source community trying to cobble things together as best they can.)

This is a community effort and we can all get our two cents in.

It might be too late for Karmic, but the next release might be a good target.
