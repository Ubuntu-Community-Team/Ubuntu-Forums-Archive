### Post by mörgæs on 2011-03-08
If you manage to get 9.10 working, you will soon have to upgrade again, as this one is about to run out of support. If you manage, you will still have the old Grub 1 running and not Grub 2, as is default in the new Ubuntus.

It is better to test a live 10.04 and 10.10, back up your files and install the release that works best on your system.
