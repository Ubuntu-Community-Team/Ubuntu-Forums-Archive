### Post by dbw on 2006-12-10
The Ubuntu install CD should give you the option that you are looking for.
-if dual booting - have cd partition a spot out of extra space.
  This might be good if you want to extract your sister's data.  I usually do that with a livecd, though, which should allow you to access her hdd.
-if replacing - cd Ubuntucd just repartition everything and kill it.
  This will destroy all of your data!!!!


-if she runs XP or W2000, you might have to enable ntfs support if you want to see the old data.  There are [many]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=316054&referrerid=48700"),  [many]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=310076&highlight=ntfs"),  [many]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=309339&highlight=ntfs") posts on these forums dedicated to explaining how to do this.
