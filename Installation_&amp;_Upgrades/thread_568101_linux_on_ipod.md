### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-10-05
You need to install an iPod "Restore" program.  There is one for Mac that installs iPodLinux from the Mac side and there is one for Windows that installs iPodLinux from the Windows side.

Don't get your hopes up.  Music-playing is sketchy using iPodLinux.  Oggs run at 90% speed if at all (depending on what iPod you have).  

Just be mindful that if you use your iPod for music 90% of the time, you may be happier with Rockbox.  If you have a spare iPod and are just fooling around, then install iPodLinux and tell us what works and what doesn't.  It's been several months since I played with it on a 2nd Gen Mini Green iPod.
