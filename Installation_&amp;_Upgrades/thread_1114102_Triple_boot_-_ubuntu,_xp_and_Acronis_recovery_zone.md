### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-04-03
The concern I would have is not the Ubuntu side, it's the Acronis Recovery Zone.  I don't know exactly how that works, but it would need to map out some space on your drive to store image information.  That might be inside your OS partition; it might be somewhere else.  Either way, resizing partitions to make room for Ubuntu runs the risk of "breaking" Acronis recovery.

My suggestion would be to go the the Acronis forum and seek their advice on what you're trying to do.  The link is below:
[http://www.wilderssecurity.com/forumdisplay.php?f=64]("http://www.wilderssecurity.com/forumdisplay.php?f=64")

Choose the Acronis True Image product line entry.

Good Luck.
