### Post by northrup on 2010-02-12
How old is the laptop?  If there are signs of life, but no image on the screen, it might be an issue with the display hardware.  I had to troubleshoot a couple of HP monitors not too long ago at my high school.  They had dead backlights; the screen would show an image at first, then flicker for a bit, and then seem to go black but really there's a really faint image.  If your laptop is a bit old, it's likely having a similar problem.
