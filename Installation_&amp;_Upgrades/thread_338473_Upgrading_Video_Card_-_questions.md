### Post by cilynx on 2007-01-14
It all depends on what you want to do.  If you're just doing normal computing, stick with what you've got.  If you absolutely need 3d acceleration, go with the nVidia.  ATI works sometimes.  (I have a couple ATI cards.)  You're not going to get fun 3d stuff (ie, compiz, beryl, etc..) easily with ATI.
