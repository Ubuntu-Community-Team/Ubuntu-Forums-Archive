### Post by Darael on 2009-04-20
The disc label (as in what you see in another OS when you insert the disc) is, as far as I know, the only easily accessible indicator of processor architecture.  By the way, I DON'T mean the label stuck to the disc, I do mean the volume label.  You could try opening a terminal and typing "arch", which is supposed to show you which you're on, but it never worked for me.
