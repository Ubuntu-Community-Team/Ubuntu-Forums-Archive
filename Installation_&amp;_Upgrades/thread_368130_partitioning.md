### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-02-23
Let me verify something here. By free space, do you mean free space on the Windows partition, or do you mean unallocated space outside of Windows partition? If the first option is the case, and there is no unallocated space on the HDD, then Windows partition needs to be resized first as shown on one of the pictures. However, I would not advise installing Ubuntu at all, because there is not enough space. You'd have to free at least 3-4 GB and leave the rest for Windows, and you'll probably find yourself running out of space in no time.
