### Post by jken146 on 2007-12-19
You'll have to be more specific about your problems.  Search around for a sound troubleshooting guide - I'm sure there is one on these forums somewhere.  As for compiz, what exactly can't you do?

The steps to get compiz working are:
1.  Enable the restricted drivers for your graphics card.
2.  Install the package **compizconfig-settings-manager**
3.  Go to System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects and have fun with the plugins.
