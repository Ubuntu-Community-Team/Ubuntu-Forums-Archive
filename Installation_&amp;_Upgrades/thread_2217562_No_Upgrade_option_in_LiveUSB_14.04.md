### Post by jbaerboc on 2014-04-17
You cannot [to my knowledge] upgrade from a Ubuntu Installer [usb or cd]. You'd need to boot into your current Ubuntu and run updates. During the process your updater should say "hey there's a new version, wanna update to it?". If you then say yes it should upgrade it to 14.04.
