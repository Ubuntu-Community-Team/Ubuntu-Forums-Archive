### Post by az on 2006-01-27
Breezy will detect and offer to split an existing partition for you.  If you already have windows on your machine and if you say yes to the option it offers you by default, you will be dual booting when you are done.

You do not have to do any fancy partitioning.  The installer will do that for you.  It is very safe.  You should always back up your data, but it is very safe.
