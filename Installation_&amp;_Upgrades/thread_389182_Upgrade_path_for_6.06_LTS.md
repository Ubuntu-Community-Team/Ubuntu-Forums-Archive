Hi,

from: [http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatIsubuntu/releases]("http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatIsubuntu/releases")

[INDENT]Enterprise releases

In addition to the regular six-monthly releases, the Ubuntu team may make an Enterprise Release (based on an existing time-based release), also known as a "Long Term Support (LTS)" release, that has received additional stabilization, polish and translation work. These Enterprise Releases will be supported for a longer period than the standard 18 month support of the time based releases. Upgrades will be supported from enterprise release to enterprise release.[/INDENT]
