Not sure why the LiveCD asks that, but it should be noted that Ubuntu/Debian runlevels are different than most other distros, see here - [http://www.debianadmin.com/debian-and-ubuntu-linux-run-levels.html](http://www.debianadmin.com/debian-and-ubuntu-linux-run-levels.html)
You want runlevel 2 (in other systems this is runlevel 5 - multiuser GUI)
