### Post by emmet on 2005-10-17
I noticed a strange difference between upgrading from the Breezy CD and upgrading online.

Yesterday I was thinking of just changing my sources to Breezy from Hoary and then asking Synaptic to do the upgrade. In order to have more "control" - and to be more friendly to the source mirrors - I decided to use the CD instead. I downloaded the ISO and burned it and then added the CD as a source in Synaptic. 

When I marked all upgradable packages, I noticed that Apache2 was listed to be removed instead of upgraded. This was not something I really wanted to do.

I therefore went back to the original plan and just changed all the sources to Breezy and reloaded the changes. This time, Apache2 wasn't marked to be removed, but instead to upgrade.

Could the use of the Breezy CD be the cause of the some of the errors that we've been seeing on this forum? I have no idea why there should be such a big difference between the CD route and the on-line upgrade....

Just thought I'd mention it.

Emmet
