### Post by nu2this on 2006-08-11
You can also try the Media Playerconnectivity Fx extension. 
from here:[https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/446/](https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/446/)
 Once installed
1)right click the menubar( the bar with File,Edit...)click customize
2)Drag& drop the logo that looks like a fim clip with a music note on it to the menubar
3)left click the logo clik configure, clik the wizard tab
4)start the wizard it'll check ur media players then give u alist of what plays what
5)for QT it'll have usr/bin/X11/gmplayer click the dropdown menu select
usr/bin/X11/totem click next,then finish, done!
If all has gone well you'll be able to play most QT movies.
P.S. you'll also need totem for this in case u don't have that you can install it thru Automatix
