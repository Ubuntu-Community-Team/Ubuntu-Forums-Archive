### Post by 0n0w1c on 2020-04-17
I found and resolved this issue. During a previous upgrade, there was  package that had errors during installation and the issue was "resolved" by removing the bind mount in /etc/fstab for /boot/efi/grub.
Once I restored this mount and performed an "apt --reinstall install grub2" the history of snapshots via grub were once again available.
