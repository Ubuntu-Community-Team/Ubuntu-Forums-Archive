### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-04-06
Just out of curiosity, take a look at your BIOS settings under USB. See if that USB Bios support is enabled. If it isn't, enable it and try again.

Here is the site I got the idea from:

[http://www-307.ibm.com/pc/support/site.wss/MIGR-58298.html](http://www-307.ibm.com/pc/support/site.wss/MIGR-58298.html)

It has a cool little BIOS simulator, and I found that setting by clicking around in it. It's worth a try, anyway...

I hope it helps...
