### Post by Pumalite on 2007-07-16
Tweak your resolution until you are 'in range' or choose 'standard VGA'. Also check your monitor for vertical, horizontal refresh and/or 50, 60, 70, 85 Hz frequency.. Also try Alternate CD, avoid graphical interface problems and then fix from within. If returned to a command line, try: sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
