### Post by matthew on 2005-11-20
To help you answer your question:
-Does everything work well right now under Hoary?
-Do you need the latest versions of software or is what you have now sufficient?
-Is fiddling with your setup something you consider fun or a chore?

I upgraded 3 different computers. One was cake--very easy. Another was a pain in the hindquarters. The third was somewhere in the middle. Unless your hardware is identical to one of those three setups I can't guarantee which sort of experience you will have. In other words, it's a risk, but one with rewards as well. Whether it is a risk that is worthwhile is something only you can answer.
