### Post by mikewhatever on 2008-11-17
The problem may happen because of bad/old/scratched cd or too fast burning speed. Try using a good quality cds and burn at x4 or so. If the iso's md5sum matches, re-downloading wouldn't give you a better iso. It looks like the errors happen after the download, most probably during the burning.
