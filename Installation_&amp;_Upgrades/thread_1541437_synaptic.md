### Post by roger_1960 on 2010-07-29
Hi

Have you clicked on "reload" in synaptic before searching?  This needed with a new install.

Click on the "origin" button in synaptic.  Does it show the main, multiverse, universe and restricted repos for Karmic?  If not, in system>admin open "software sources" and select these under the "ubuntu software" tab.  Then go back to synaptic and reload.
