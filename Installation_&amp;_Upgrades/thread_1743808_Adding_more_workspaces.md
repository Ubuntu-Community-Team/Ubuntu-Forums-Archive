### Post by u.rusty on 2011-04-29
[FONT=Courier 10 Pitch][SIZE=2]Install CCSM (CompizConfig Settings Manager) using Ubuntu software center. Then open System|Preferences|CompizConfig Setting Manager and open general options. Click the Desktop Size tab at the top. Those are the settings I used to adjust the number of workspaces and their orientation on my system.[/SIZE][/FONT]
