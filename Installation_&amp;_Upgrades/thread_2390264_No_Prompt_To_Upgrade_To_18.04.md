### Post by sudodus on 2018-04-27
There are reasons, why you are not prompted to upgrade now.

- The upgrade process is rather complicated and needs more testing.

- The brand new version of Ubuntu is only tested by a few persons in a few computers. During the next few months bugs will be found and squashed, and the upgrade process is more likely to succeed.

See this link for more details: [Ubuntu Development version / How to participate](https://askubuntu.com/questions/1018033/ubuntu-development-version-how-to-participate)
