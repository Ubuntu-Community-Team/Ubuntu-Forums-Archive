### Post by ader10 on 2006-07-15
Simply hold alt and drag the window on a white space.
To make the resolution better, go to the control panel and scroll over to the display part, open it up and go to administrator mode, find the tab with the drivers and monitor, and change the driver to "radeon" and apply.
