### Post by njenkins on 2008-03-20
Hello all,

While I haven't used Linux in awhile, I figured my install would be complicated at the very least. How wrong was I!! I downloaded and burned the ISO at my college. Then I went home and put the CD in and Ubuntu pretty much took over. The partitioning went super easy and after the install, Ubuntu even detected my Belkin USB Wireless Adapter. I didn't really do anything at all. That was probably the easiest install I've done. (I think the last time I did a install was Breezy)

Just thought I would put this out there to motivate some of you guys. Not everybody has problems with the install, and in my case, I had zero. Just to be on the safer side I switched to Xubuntu (because of my older resources) and everything works great.
