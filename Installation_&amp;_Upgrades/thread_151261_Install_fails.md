### Post by John.Michael.Kane on 2006-03-27
could be a bad iso image or bad burn.

First check the md5sum of the iso image and compare it to the md5sum given in the download directory where you saved your file.

md5sum <your-filename-goes-here.iso>
If those figures match up, then I would suggest burning a new disk at a slightly slower speed.
