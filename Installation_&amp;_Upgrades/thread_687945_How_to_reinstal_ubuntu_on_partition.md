### Post by shutslar on 2008-02-04
Insert the livecd into your cd-rom drive and reboot.  Click the install.  When it ask you where, install it into the exact same partitions your current ubuntu is installed.  The only thing that will be overwritten will be  your current ubuntu system.  none of your ubuntu changes you have made previously will be there (this is a re-install from scratch).

Hope this helps.
