Please make more descriptive questions.

Can you do or can you not do a complete reinstall?
Do you want, or do you not want to reinstall?
Do you want, or do you not want to preserve your information?
Do you want, or do you not want to format the disk?
If you format your disk, you will lose your data. Is this fine with you?

You should always use a password in Linux. It is essential to keep your system secure. The fact that you don't want a password means you have not understood the implications of having a secure system. I suggest you try to cope with it. Otherwise, you should use a different operating system, as in Linux every distribution will ask you to use a password.
