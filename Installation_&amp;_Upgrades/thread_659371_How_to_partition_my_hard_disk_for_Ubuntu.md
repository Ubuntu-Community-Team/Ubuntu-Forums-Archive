### Post by jimbob on 2008-01-05
Use the stand-alone version of Gparted and resize your Windoze partition (assume that is what you have) to give Ubuntu at least 6 Gb or more of unallocated space.

Then install Ubuntu and tell it to use the largest unallocated space available.
