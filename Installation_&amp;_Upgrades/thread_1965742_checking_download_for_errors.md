### Post by peculiar penguin on 2012-04-26
You can compute the checksum of your local file (md5sum filename) and compare it  to the one published on the download site  (ubuntu-12.04-desktop-i386.iso would be  d791352694374f1c478779f7f4447a3f, for example).
