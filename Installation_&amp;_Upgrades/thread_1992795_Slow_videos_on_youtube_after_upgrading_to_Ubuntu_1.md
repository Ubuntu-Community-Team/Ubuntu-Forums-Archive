### Post by Sularco on 2012-06-01
Uninstalling flash would mean that ilouboff could not watch flash on any website. Though I firmly believe that the quicker flash is rendered obsolete the better, many websites still make heavy use of it and even youtube can not yet render all video in HTML5.
There are known issues with the Adobe flash plugin in 12.04 and there is a dicussion ongoing whether it is Adobe's fault or not. There are workarounds available, I propose you check out these links:
