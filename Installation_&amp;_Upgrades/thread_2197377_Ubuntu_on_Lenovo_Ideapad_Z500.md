### Post by bertan2 on 2014-01-03
You'll probably have to go into the UEFI/BIOS on the Lenovo and change the boot method from "UEFI" to "Legacy" or something like that. I've done this with an ASUS and an Acer, but never with a Lenovo, and terminology does vary a bit from manufacturer to manufacturer.
