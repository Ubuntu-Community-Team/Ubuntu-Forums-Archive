### Post by howefield on 2016-03-15
Your version of Ubuntu has passed end of life, which means the software repositories have been moved.

You could change the source.list file to point to the old.releases but your best bet would be to get to a supported release.

15.10 is the current version which goes end of life in July but can be upgraded to 16.04 when it comes out in April and has a  support life of 5 years (LTS release - Long Term Support).
