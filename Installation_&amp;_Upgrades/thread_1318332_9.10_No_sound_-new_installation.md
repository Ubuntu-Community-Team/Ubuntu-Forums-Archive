### Post by shaboodles on 2009-11-07
Im not sure if this will help you with your speaker problem but I had no sound after a fresh install so i re-installed ubuntu 9.10 and I had sound until after I booted to windows to set up a few things. When I booted back to ubuntu I had no sound. I went into the terminal and typed "alsamixer" to bring the alsa volumes up (which maybe could fix your center speaker because there is a volume option for center) and messed around with those but no luck. Then when messing around in the sound preferences I switched the hardware from digital to the analog stereo duplex selection and messed around with the volume in that. I slid the volume down all the way which toggled mute so I slid it back up to 100%, unmuted and I suddenly had music blasting in my face and when I switched back to digital I still had sound. Now I have to do this every time I log in, otherwise I get no sound, it must be something to do with the alsa driver. But hopefully this helps some people, and hopefully a fix is released soon.
