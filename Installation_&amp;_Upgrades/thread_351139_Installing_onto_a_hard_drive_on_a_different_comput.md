### Post by LotsOfPhil on 2007-02-01
You could do it, but there might be some problems when you boot up in the new computer.

What will happen is during install, Xubuntu will detect and configure all the hardware. It will think "I have this video card, this sound card, this mobo, this CPU, etc, etc."
When you switch computers, unless they are identical, it will say "I used to have this video card, now I have that one. Should I uninstall this and install that? etc, etc."

What is your motivation for doing this?
