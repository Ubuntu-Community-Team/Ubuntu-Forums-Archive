### Post by merlinus on 2007-08-30
/home contains settings, preferences and configurations, e.g. firefox bookmarks and thunderbird email and accounts.

You can back this up, but remember to show hidden files.

As for apps, better to reinstall them, as you will have to back up gigabytes of files from different locations.

Also, if you reinstall, consider making a separate partition for /home so you will not have to back it up when upgrading or reinstalling again.
