I'm using ubuntu 10.04. I have a LifeView FlyVIDEO3000 analog tv card  and to use it on ubuntu I've installed XawTV. At first it worked smoothly but when I plugged my web cam and reboot it's started showing my web cam image. I a couldn't changed it to tv again in right-click properties. So to watch tv I have to unplug the cam and reboot. 

How can I use tv without loosing the cam.

---

### Post by hasantahsin on 2012-06-12
Well, thanks for helps but here is whar I did: 
I create a shortcut and on command I wrote
```
xawtv -c /dev/video1
```
And it allowed me to open XawTV with tv card.
