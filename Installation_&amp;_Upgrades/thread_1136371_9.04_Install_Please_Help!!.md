### Post by inobe on 2009-04-25
ignore the lilo issue

i used my computer as a test machine to upgrade from 8.10 to 9.04, i received the same message and and hit ok.


upon a reboot i had no problems at all.

i even went into synaptic and removed lilo and restarted with no issues.

simply said you can totally ignore it and proceed to reboot as normal after the upgrade.

apparently it's some sort of mix up/ bug

if you lost internet and you absolutely need to reboot' you will get a partial upgrade notice, you should be able to continue the upgrade where it was left off.

to be safe backup your personal data
