### Post by presence1960 on 2010-01-29
Did you [MD5SU]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM")M the iso prior to burning it to CD?

Did you burn the iso as an image to CD at no more than 8x speed?

Did you choose "check disk for defects" when you booted your machine from it?

I would go through all of those as I suspect you have either a corrupted iso or a bad burn.
