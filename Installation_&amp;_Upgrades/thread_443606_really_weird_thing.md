### Post by vtel57 on 2007-05-14
Probably a corrupted CD burn for Ubuntu. If you still have your original Ubuntu.iso file on your system somewhere, run an md5sum check on it. If it's good, then re-burn it at the slowest speed on your burner (no faster than 4x). Try the newly burned CD. If the md5sum check shows an error, re-download the .iso from Ubuntu.
