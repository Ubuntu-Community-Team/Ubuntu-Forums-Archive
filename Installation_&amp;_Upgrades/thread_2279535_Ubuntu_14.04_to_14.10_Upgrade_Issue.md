### Post by mörgæs on 2015-05-24
Upgrades are risky, as you probably also experienced when posting you other thread.

If you manage to salvage 14.10 you will soon have to upgrade again, because support is running out in a month or two. I would suggest that you do a clean install of the version you want (15.04 or 14.04.2) and keep it for as long as it's supported. After that a new install.
