### Post by netboy541 on 2007-05-09
download KTorrent 2.1.4 

I was having the same issues... 

[http://ktorrent.org/index.php?page=downloads](http://ktorrent.org/index.php?page=downloads)

download the fiesty package (if that's what you're running)... save it in your home folder or desktop or something.
Right Click the file, pick Kubuntu package menu, and then install.
A shell session will start, and ask you for your sudo password.
Type in your password when it asks.  dpkg will start, install the new version, and all your problems should end, at least with KTorrent. :)
