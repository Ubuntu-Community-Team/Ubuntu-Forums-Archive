### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-03-15
Either try lovinglinux's flashvideoreplacer add-on for firefox [http://www.webgapps.org/addons/flashvideoreplacer](http://www.webgapps.org/addons/flashvideoreplacer) or try the flash 11 beta version avaialble from [http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplatformruntimes/incubator/flashplayer_inc_debug_lin_022711.tar.gz](http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplatformruntimes/incubator/flashplayer_inc_debug_lin_022711.tar.gz)
