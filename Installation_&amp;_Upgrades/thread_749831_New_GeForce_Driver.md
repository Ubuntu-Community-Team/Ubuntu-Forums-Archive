### Post by Pumalite on 2008-04-08
FYI:

At last! New GeForce driver!

After a long dearth of new NVIDIA drivers for cards other than the 9600 series comes the brand new 174.74 driver. The new driver supports existing GeForce 6xxx, 7xxx, 8xxx and 9xxx GPUs and adds support for:

    * GeForce 9800 GX2
    * GeForce 9600 GT
    * GeForce 8300
    * GeForce 8200
    * GeForce 8100/NVIDIA nForce 720a
    * NVIDIA nForce 730a

New features include:

    * Dynamic Contrast Enhancement
    * Dynamic Blue, Green & Skin Tone Enhancements
    * Dual-Stream Decode Acceleration

As always, you can pick them from NVIDIA
