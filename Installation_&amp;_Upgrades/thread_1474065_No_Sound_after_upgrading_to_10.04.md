### Post by mackenzie2995 on 2010-05-07
I ran in to that problem. 

1. Type "alsamixer" in a terminal.

2. Use arrow keys to select and increase all the volume controls to their maximum values.

3. Press the escape button to exit.

The sound will now work, if your problem is the same as mine.
