### Post by giants_fan on 2006-10-28
I just thought I'd share my upgrade results.  I have a IBM T42 laptop and have successfully upgraded to Edgy.  Edgy is great!  It's UI is **noticably** faster than Dapper (drawing windows, moving windows, etc) and all of the new apps work fine for me. 

I did run into two small problems, however...  

1) I did a cdromupgrade and even though I chose "No network" about 3/4 of the way it, the installer felt to need to go out the network and fetch packages.  This took about 5 hours.

2) I had to reinstall the fglrx drivers.

Not too painful of an upgrade.  Not perfect, but well worth it.
