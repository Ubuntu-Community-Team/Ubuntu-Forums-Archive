### Post by snowpine on 2010-08-31
I would recommend you try Ubuntu first. It is the original and, in my opinion, most polished of the 'Buntu family. But the other "flavors" are basically the same operating system with different desktop environments or "skins" so really it is a question of cosmetics and personal taste. If you prefer KDE, go for Kubuntu; Xfce, Xubuntu, etc.

You are correct that Ubuntu support for Atom motherboards is hit or miss. I recommend using the forum Search feature to find success/failure stories for your *specific* motherboard before you begin your build. Good luck! :)
