These lines should go in your Repositories file ([Adding Repositories]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu")). Go into Synaptic and go to Repositories -> add these lines to third party.

Refresh/Update your repositories after you save those additions and close that window. Then you can just search for easy cam 2 through Synaptics.

Find more here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EasyCam](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EasyCam)

Good Luck!
