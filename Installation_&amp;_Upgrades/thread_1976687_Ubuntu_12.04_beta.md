### Post by Peter09 on 2012-05-09
Ther menu bar appears when you move the cursor right to the top of the screen, its called the global menu.
 
A fast alternative for bookmarks/favourites is to tap the super key to bring up the dash, start typing the name of a favourite, when its the only thing left hit enter and it will come up in a new tab in firefox, or start firefox with the website in the first tab.
 
Also - if firefox is running, tap the alt key (for the HUD), type the name of a bookmark and when it appears at the top of the list hit enter.
