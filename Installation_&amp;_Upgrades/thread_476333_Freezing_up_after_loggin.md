### Post by octavianh on 2007-06-17
What kind of sound cards do you have in this computer?  Do you have an on-board sound card and another sound card as well?  I've had this happen on several computers with strange soundcards where esound causes the desktop to crash.

You can also try hitting CTRL-ALT-Backspace and then log in again once X restarts.  If esound has crashed already then you should be able to get in (in theory).
