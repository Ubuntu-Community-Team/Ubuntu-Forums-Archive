### Post by Mark Phelps on 2008-12-15
From what you're saying, you didn't install "updates" (i.e., upgrades to existing packages), you installed new packages.  

If you know which ones you installed, you can open Synaptec and uninstall them -- but that may removed LOTS of other packages, depending on what you installed.

There is no generic "system restore" capability that will rollback changes to a certain date.
