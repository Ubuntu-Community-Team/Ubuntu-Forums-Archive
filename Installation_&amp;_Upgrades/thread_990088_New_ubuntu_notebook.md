### Post by Bucky Ball on 2008-11-23
There are a lot of items to download. How long are you giving it and how fast is the connection? Getting to 75% then back to 1 could just be one finishing and the other next beginning. Have a good look at the screen while this is happening and see if you can pick up any more detail, as specific as you can get. What text are you getting whilst all this is happening? Is it showing an error or just taking an age? Is this behavior going on for an hour and causing the user to reboot or kill the process? Let us know.

Whilst this is happening, you have an arrow at the bottom of that window and next to it is written 'Details'. Click that arrow and it will open a terminal and let you see what is happening 'behind the scenes'. Is the behavior normal, as in it is just going on from one update to the next? Or can you actually see it is trying to load the same update repeatedly?
