### Post by phidia on 2007-08-04
Run md5sum (from a terminal) on the iso you downloaded.
Check the output you get with the official md5sum.
You may have a bad iso from downloading. It's usually a good idea to download by using a torrent file.

 I don't know your geographic location but you can start [here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors") to find torrent downloads and the respective md5sum values.
