### Post by meindian523 on 2008-07-17
Actually,it's not passwrd,it's ```
passwd
``` you are looking for.What you believe to be typing only one character is not that,it is in fact obscuring your password so that someone over your shoulder can't read it.That's right,you won't even have *s to mark your progress.Just go ahead and type your entire password and press Enter.It will accept your new password and allow you to login.
