### Post by Robk29 on 2021-04-02
Hi,

I'm seeking any suggestions on how I could get my Z130s to function correctly on 20.04. I've had them for years on Win 8.1 and 10 and they have really decent sound. I just installed 20.04 on a PC I built (MSI 450M-Pro MAX motherboard + Ryzen 5 2600) and somewhat ironically MSI states 'Enyoy crystal sound with audio boost.' However the opposite is true, and while they do function, there is a rhythmic popping sound when there should be silence. Overall the sound has deteriorated too. I don't blame MSI and suspected that it may have somthing to do with Pulseaudio so I killed it. Worked for about 20 seconds!

Resolved:
I managed to resolve this myself. After trying a number of things what actually worked was swapping the socket it was plugged into from the from the front of my new Cooler Master case to directly into the motherboard via the I/O shield. Why this worked I don't know, but it worked!
