If I remember correctly (it's been a while since I installed Ubuntu), when you go through the installation it will ask you if you want to use just the free space avalable on your hard drive or the whole drive.  Just tell it the whole drive and Ubuntu will completely take over your machine :D

### Post by Herman on 2006-03-04
You can follow this example [here]("http://users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/p2.htm"), but when you get to step 8 (8th illustration) , rather than doing what it says there, choose the next option down, as skirkpatrick already said, and erase the whole drive. There  may be some other things to interest you on that site on other pages as well.
