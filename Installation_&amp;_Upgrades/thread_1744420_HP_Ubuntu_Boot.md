### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-30
Most newer HP systems have all 4 primary partitions already taken, and this confuses the 10.10 install.

To determine if your HP has this same situation, please do the following:

Please boot off the LiveCD/LiveUSB, select 'TRY', and then:

[http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net/](http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net/)
Follow the instruction on the website and post the results here.

Please press the '#' button when posting and place the the script results between the two 'CODE' tags.
