### Post by Megaptera on 2011-01-16
Wubi is for people who want to try Ubuntu but may be worried about the idea of partitioning and messing it up (like I was).
Having used Wubi and deciding that I wanted to use Ubuntu I read up on dual booting, partitioning and backing-up everything before starting.

Now dual booting happily and distro hopping. But wouldn't have got this far if I'd not had Wubi and mintInstall to try first.
