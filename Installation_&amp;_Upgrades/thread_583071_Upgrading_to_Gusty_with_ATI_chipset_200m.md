### Post by acadiansteph on 2007-10-20
My clean install with Ubuntu 7.10 went horribly wrong since there is a bug with the ATI chipset. Black screen!. So yesterday after installing Feisty , I upgraded trough Update Manager to 7.10 . It went off without a problem. All I had to bear in mind was to keep my my modified etc/X11/xorg.conf file has it was in 7.04: I used the following tutorial: for direct rendering: [http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Feisty_Installation_Guide](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Feisty_Installation_Guide)  . I also used the following to get my bcm4318 wireless, keeping in my to keep the settings when upgrading :  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=197102](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=197102) .

So in short, my suggestion for members who had problems with ATI chipset when doing a clean install, is to upgrade from Feisty 7.04 to 7.10

It may work for you. Although bearing in mind some of you may have different ATI and wireless chipsets.

I hope this will help some of you who experienced a black screen.
