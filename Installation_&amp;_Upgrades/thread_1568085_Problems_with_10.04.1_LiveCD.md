### Post by gibbogle on 2010-09-04
For the possible benefit of others having problems like mine:

When I downloaded the .iso yesterday and burned a CD, the bootup from the CD failed (with a message about mounting /dev/loop0).  After looking for solutions without success, I decided to try downloading the iso again.  To my surprise the size of  the .iso that I received was much different (bigger) than the previous  one.  I burned another CD, and this one works.:D  Just to be sure, I did  the md5sum check:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)
and confirmed that the new .iso is correct.  I don't know why the first  one was so wrong.  It seems that there can be a problem with the  download, and this might explain many of the issues that people are  reporting here.
