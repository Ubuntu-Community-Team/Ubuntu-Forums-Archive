### Post by howefield on 2008-10-03
I've taken this from the mac4lin documentation, guess it should work with your download.


"Goto System > Administration > Login Window > Local Tab. Enter the Admin password when asked. Now Add your theme by clicking Add and selecting your theme.

After adding, a preview of the theme will be shown. To apply, click on the radio button next to preview".
