### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-08-05
Ubuntu 11.10 has now reached End of Life. It is no longer supported with security updates. You should think about installing a later version of Ubuntu (12.04 - four more years support) on your PC. It is free to download and install.

[http://www.ubuntu.com/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/download)

To show you how complicated a process you are asking about read this.

[http://askubuntu.com/questions/2724/best-way-to-clone-an-installation-copying-to-identical-hardware](http://askubuntu.com/questions/2724/best-way-to-clone-an-installation-copying-to-identical-hardware)
