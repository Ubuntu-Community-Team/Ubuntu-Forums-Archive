### Post by cariboo on 2009-08-08
Blender is in the repositories, Have a look at Applications-->Add/Remove or System-->Administration-->System-->Synaptic Package Manger. You don't need to go searching for programs on the internet like you do with Windows. Almost everyting you need is in the repositories. Use the two above mentioned applications to ad and remove applications.
