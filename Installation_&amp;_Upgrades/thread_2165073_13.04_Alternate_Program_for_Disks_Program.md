### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-08-03
If you want to simply and securely wipe the disk, what about a simple dd command from a live DVD/USB
```
sudo  dd if=/dev/urandom of=/dev/sdx
```will do that for you, and as I  understand things, in spite of some people saying it is not very secure,  a single overwrite of data on a disk will make it completely  non-recoverable by anyone other than forensic data recovery geeks, who  would need to spend huge amounts of time and money to do so, and may  still not succeed.

See [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2035123](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2035123) for a thread discussing this subject; old but still relevant.
