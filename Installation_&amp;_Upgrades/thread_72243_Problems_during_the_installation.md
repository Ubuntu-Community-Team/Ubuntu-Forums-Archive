Hello ndenicano. Welcome to the Ubuntu Forums.:) 

In regards to your question, you are telling me that Ubuntu has installed properly and is now asking for your user name and password?

During the install proccess, you would have been asked to create a user. The installer asks for youre name, a user name, and prompts you to create a password for your account.

At the log in screen, you simply need to input the username you entered during install, press enter, and then enter your password, then hit enter again.

This will allow you to log into your account.

I hope that this was helpful, and that it provided an answer to your problem.
