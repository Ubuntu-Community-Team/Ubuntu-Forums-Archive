### Post by dandnsmith on 2009-08-01
For various reasons, I'd start with the XP installer CD, get it to do the partitioning, and then do the 2 XP installs into different partitions.
Next you can do the Ubuntu install, modifying the partitions as you want in the initial stages - when you've finished, with a bit of luck, the GRUB loader menu will be set up and ready to go.
