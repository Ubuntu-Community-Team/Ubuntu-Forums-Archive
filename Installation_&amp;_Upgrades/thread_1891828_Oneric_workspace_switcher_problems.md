### Post by hodad on 2011-12-06
Upgraded to Oneiric Ocelot aq month ago.  In 11.04, I could use four workspaces, each with a separate instance of Firefox, so I could keep 4 websites open at the same time, or four different documents, one on each screen.

Now, every workspace has same instance running.  If I open a WP document on one screen, and try to open another doc on another screen, the same instance comes up (same document). If I open a website, and go to another screen to open another website, when I bring up firefox, I get the same website.  In effect, I have only one workspace  in 11.10.  

Anyone know how to fix this?

### Post by buntudawg on 2011-12-06
Hi.

There are two ways to do this.

First:
Move to the new workspace
Hold 'super' key and numbers will appear on the apps in the sidepanel
Hold 'shift' and hit the corresponding number/letter and there is your second instance open in the new workspace.

Second:
More cumbersome but some applications (eg firefox) allow you to 'open in a new window'.
Do this then ctrl+alt+shift+arrow key to move the window to the workspace you want.

Hope this helps.
