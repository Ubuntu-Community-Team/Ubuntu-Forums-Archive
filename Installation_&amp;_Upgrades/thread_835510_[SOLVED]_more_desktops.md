### Post by wpshooter on 2008-06-20
If you are talking about workspaces, then yes, right click on the workspace icon at the far right hand side of the bottom panel (but not the trash icon) and go into I think preferences and change the number of workspaces.

---

### Post by G_wiz on 2008-06-20
Right click on the right hand side of your lower task bar where you see two desktops (which is the default setting), select Preferences, the increase the number of columns to four...or however many you want.  Enjoy.
