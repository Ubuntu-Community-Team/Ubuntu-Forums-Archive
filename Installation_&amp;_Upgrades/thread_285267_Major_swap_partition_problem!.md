### Post by bulldog on 2006-10-26
Yes you can boot the live cd and use the partitioner to delete the swap.

Then create a new swap with the size you want.

Then you can assign the rest of the space to a partition at your liking.

Don't mount your hdd just boot the live cd and use gparted.
