Before the "splash" screen with words Ubuntu and the "loading bar", you should see "Grub Loading Stage 1.5" and "Grub Loading..."   very briefly.  

Are you seeing this?   If not, then something is wrong with your bootloader (GRUB) installation.   It may be instructive to ferret out the problem but you'll probably save time by starting over with a fresh install of 7.10  Furthermore,  I can't think of a reason why you should install 7.04 and then upgrade to 7.10.    (Just my opinion having been through the installation process about 25 times):(
