### Post by sistoviejo on 2009-03-16
In my case it was because of an Ati PCI-slot video card.
When I changed the card to an AGP video card, the problem was solved.
Other reasons for this error could be bad memory, bad hard drive, bad CD-rom drive or bad Ubuntu live CD (if you're booting from one).
Good luck!
