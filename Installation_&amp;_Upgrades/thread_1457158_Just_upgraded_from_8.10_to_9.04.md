Basically no problems upgrading using update manager. 
But it did ask for the installation disc to be inserted into the drive. Good thing I had a 9.04 disc I ordered about 4 months ago.
After that, installation went perfect.
Everything seems to be working fine at this point and faster.

Thought I may have a video card problem, hoping not but........
I have been using this card in Ubuntu 8.10 with slight problems playing back videos with special effects enabled. The video would be choppy flickering with dark lines. So I would not use special effects, that would solve the problem. Also, no problem playing games like Urban Terror.

The card I have been using:
ATI Radeon x1900xtx, Pci-E, 512 GDDR3

After upgrading to Ubuntu 9.04.
Video card still works using the drivers 9.04 automatically installed. The flickering in video playback is no longer happening with special effects enabled. Had a slight video problem with Urban Terror, that was fixed by making a few adjustments within the game settings.

Plan on working my way up to Ubuntu 10.10
