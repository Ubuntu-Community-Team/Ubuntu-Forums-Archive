### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2011-07-20
Ctrl+alt+del doesn't have the same 'stop everything, do THIS' effect as it has on windows. It may be a bug with the program, try checking out launchpad (bug centre) Here:

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/](https://bugs.launchpad.net/)

Or you may have a problem with the cd/usb/wubi install. Burn cd again, (slow speed setting) recopy the usb carefully, etc. Try a reinstall, basically. 10.10 is a release behind, so you may want to get 11.04 instead.

Or you may have a hardware problem. If none of the above yield any joy, report back and tell us what hardware you're using.

A little more detail always helps! :)
