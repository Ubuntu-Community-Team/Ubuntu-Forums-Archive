### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-01-13
Hi and welcome to the forums :-)

Download the image again and check the MD5SUM before burning to CD or USB.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

Burn at the lowest possible speed for CD and try UNetbootin for USB installs:
[http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)

You may have to use nomodeset again when booting since you have an NVIDIA card.
