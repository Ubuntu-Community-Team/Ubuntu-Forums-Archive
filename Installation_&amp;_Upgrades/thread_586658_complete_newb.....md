### Post by scrooge_74 on 2007-10-22
Easy,

with the Live CD, you have to partition the drive (why not use one of the other drives?)

You need to make three partitions: (/) which is your system, swap partition and /home partition.

You have to use the manual partition because if you dont, the system will wipe clean the drive first and wont make a separate partition for your /home folder.

You may want to look at this site from one of the Forum's staff members:
