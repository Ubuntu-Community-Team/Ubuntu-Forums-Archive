### Post by Technoviking on 2011-11-18
There have been some people have tried  "upgrading" from 11.10 to 11.04, mainly to get back the classic Gnome desktop. Please do not do this!!! There is a bug they will let you try to "downgrade" in place, but really all you will end up with is a non-functional Ubuntu System.

For more info please check out the bug report at:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/ubiquity/+bug/891711](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/ubiquity/+bug/891711)
