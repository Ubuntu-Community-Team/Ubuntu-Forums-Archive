### Post by arubislander on 2010-07-22
If you choose Use entire [hard disk/disk/drive] it will erase all your partitions and make two: one for the installation and one for swap.

---

### Post by ccarrow14 on 2010-07-22
If you choose to use the entire hard drive for the Ubuntu install, It will get rid of your current installations of Windows 7 and Ubuntu, and give you two partitions, one for Ubuntu, and one for swap.
