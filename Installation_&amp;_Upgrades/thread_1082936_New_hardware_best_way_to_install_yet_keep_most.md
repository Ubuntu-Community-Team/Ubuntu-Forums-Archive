### Post by cariboo on 2009-02-28
Linux is not Windows, you won't have to reinstall if you replace the motherboard,  cpu and ram. Most of the drivers are already installed. The only thing you might need are the restricted video drivers. Once you have booted to the desktop go to System--Administration-->Hardware Drivers.
