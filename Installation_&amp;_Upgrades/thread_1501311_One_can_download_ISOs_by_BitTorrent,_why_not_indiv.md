It's no secret that only BitTorrent allows you to download something using your connection to the maximum (if it's a popular torrent LOL).

So why not download .deb packages this way? I mean, there's huge software like GIMP, OOo, Krusader with all its libs -- it's a pain to wait till it is all downloaded from a single mirror which gives you 100 KB/s when you have a fancy 16 Mbps connection.
