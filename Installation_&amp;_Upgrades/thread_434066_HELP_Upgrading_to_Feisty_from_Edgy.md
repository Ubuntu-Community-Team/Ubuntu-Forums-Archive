### Post by thetechgeek on 2007-05-05
I had that same problem. Try getting the live cd of feisty. If it dosent work, upgrade your BIOS. The BIOS is what starts up your computer before giving controll of it to the operating system. You can get an upgrade by going to your computer manufacturer's site and searching "model BIOS upgrade" where model is your computer model.
