### Post by Kobalt on 2007-07-18
There are plenty of third party repositories available out there, though I highly recommend you not to add many of those. You should add the ones you can trust only. Read [this]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories") for more info.
You could check out the [Medibuntu]("http://www.medibuntu.org") repos if you don't know them already.
