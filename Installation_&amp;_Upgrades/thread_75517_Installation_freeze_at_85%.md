### Post by SilentCacophony on 2005-10-13
> Can this still cause problems?

Yes, it can. md5sum just checks that the download wasn't corrupted. It can still be burnt badly, or onto a bad CDR. Any time you're burning and install cd, 4x or 8x burn speed is a good choice to minimize errors.

Anyway, mine stopped at 85% for about 15 minutes, then continued. I don't know why it did, though. It then failed at a later point. ;)
