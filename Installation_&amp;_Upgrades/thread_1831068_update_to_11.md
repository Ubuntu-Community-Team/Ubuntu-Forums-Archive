### Post by Dngrsone on 2011-08-22
I think LTS versions don't give you an upgrade option until the next LTS.

With that said, if you go to System/Administration/Software Sources and click on the Updates tab, there is an option at the bottom to change your release upgrade option to Normal Releases.
