### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-06-10
With laptops, it's very important that you try out the LiveCD first -- to prevent the situation of forcing an install that later, does not work.

Any problems you encounter during running Ubuntu from a LiveCD are going to range from trivial to impossible to fix later.  You can then come back here, search on specific problems you encounter, and see what's involved in fixing them.
