### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-02-07
The only way is to go 6.06->6.10->7.04 and even to ->7.10 in that order.
That will be a HUGE HASSLE, and going that far could break a lot of things or just create trouble.  I would suggest backing everything up, downloading the latest version (7.10) and doing a fresh install.
You may want to consider waiting for 8.04 which is the next LTS release - it's due in April - just do a fresh install then.
