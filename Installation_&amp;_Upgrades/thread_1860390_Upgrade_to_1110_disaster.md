Thats because your previous config, you where running Gnome as your desktop, which gnome or Ubuntu Classic was the fallback.  In 11.10, Unity 2D is the fallback and gnome is not even installed as a fallback.  But do not dispair...

To get back to that back, or a semblance of that (It will be gnome3 instaead of gnome2):
Press on the "Software Center" Icon in the left toolbar > In the Software Center, type "gnome shell" in the filter box > Install Gnome Shell.  

After install completes, close the software center. Reboot system.  At login screen, click on the icon at the upper right of the Login box.... > a pulldown menu will appear with your desktop options > select gnome... Continue logging in.
