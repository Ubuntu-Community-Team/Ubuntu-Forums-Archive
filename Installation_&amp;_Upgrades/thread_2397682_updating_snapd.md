### Post by QIII on 2018-08-01
17.10 went EOL and its repos have likely been moved to old-releases.

Upgrading from an EOL release is a ticklish business and often results in catastrophic emotional events.

My recommendation would be to back up your important files and do a fresh installation of 18.04, preserving your /home directory if you wish.
