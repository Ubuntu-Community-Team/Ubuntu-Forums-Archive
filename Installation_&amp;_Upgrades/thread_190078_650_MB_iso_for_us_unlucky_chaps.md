Mode 1 is data (650MB) and Mode 2 is audio (750MB, ~74 minutes).  You can't actually burn 750MB of data because audio CDs get that extra space by throwing out all sorts of error correction.

You can always order CDs for free from shipit, or have a friend burn the CD for you.
