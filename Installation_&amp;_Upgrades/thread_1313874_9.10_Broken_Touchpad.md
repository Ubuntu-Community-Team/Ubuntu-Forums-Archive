### Post by awam66 on 2009-11-04
I had a friend who had a similar problem with his HP laptop. It turned out that the upgrade had not updated grub and it was booting to the wrong kernel. Try checking your menu.lst if you are still on grub legacy for the up to date kernel. If on grub2 do an update-grub.
