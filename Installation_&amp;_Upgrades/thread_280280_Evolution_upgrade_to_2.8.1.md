You cannot simply do this.  Dapper (6.06) came with Gnome 2.6 which has Evolution 2.6.  To go to Evo 2.8, you'l need most of Gnome 2.8 too (at least libraries).

Those are available in the upcoming release, Edgy.
If you really want/need Evo 2.8, you can switch to Edgy, but it will not be an LTS (long term support) release like Dapper was.

Edgy is about new technologies and taking risks...
