### Post by mtrim on 2007-04-23
Here's a workaround to this problem that I read somewhere on the web that might work for you... Open the website with the stream then when mplayer is trying to load it right click on the embedded mplayer and choose Copy URL... then paste the url into a new firefox window except change the mms:// at the beginning to http://

it's annoying and you have to view the video in its own window but it works for me

hope this helps until someone can tell us a better way
