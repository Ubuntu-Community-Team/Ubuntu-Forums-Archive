### Post by DarkStarAeon on 2007-04-22
I had some upgrade issues, but they were easily fixed. I got a bit frustrated at the time it took to download (33 hours) but I have to keep reminding myself that's free software and millions are trying to download it at the same time lol 
Keep in mind, most people post when they need help or they are freaking out, not to sing praises. You'll always hear more negative than positive, even if the real numbers are mostly positive.

Feisty is faster, much faster than Edgy (Edgy wasn't slow, but in comparison..) it's also got an Internet Connection Manager for wired or wifi, which is cool. VMware works much faster. There are a a lot more apps available than ever before. CrossOver Office seems to be working much better/faster now too. Also the graphical upgrades are a nice touch, slick lookin but subtle.

If it ain't broke don't fix it in your case man, but if you've got nothing to lose (data backed up) and you could use more speed and power, then it's worth an upgrade. You can always reinstall Dapper if Feisty doesn't work.

 I am glad I upgraded though, this computer flys now! lol
