### Post by MAFoElffen on 2023-09-19
Have you gone to Settings > Sound > Output > Output Device... and checked the devices within the dropdown box? Then Selected "Configuration" to try different config's?

If that does not work, the run the [UbuntuForums 'system-info' Script]("https://github.com/UbuntuForums/system-info"), but start it adding the "--details" startup option, and post the URL of where it uploads to a pastebin. That mode will turn on the sound device details in the report so we can look at your devices, what device drivers are installed, and what the settings are set to.
