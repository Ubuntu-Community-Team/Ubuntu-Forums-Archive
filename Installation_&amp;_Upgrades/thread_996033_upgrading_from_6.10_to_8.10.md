### Post by louieb on 2008-11-28
> **icanfly0307 said:**
> Is it possible to upgrade from an old release.
LTS (long term support) releases can be upgraded to the next LTS release for 3 years. That means 6.06 can be upgraded to 8.04 until July 2009. 

Normal releases can be upgraded to the next release for 18 months. That mean if your release is 6.10 or older it can not be upgraded  and in order to get the latest (or newer) version you will have to do a fresh install. (except for 6.06 as noted above).
