### Post by joft on 2006-07-27
> **PoisoN2003 said:**
> 
Am i gonna loose the grub menu if i replace the motherboard and cpu but keep the hd?

No. Nothing is written to your harddisk by changing motherboard and cpu. The only thing that you have to make sure is, that you connect your harddisks in the same order to the new motherboard as it was with the old one (harddisks have to have the same the "names" from grub's (and linux's and windows's) point of view.

Then there will be no problems with grub.
