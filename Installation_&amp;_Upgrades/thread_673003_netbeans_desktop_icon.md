### Post by aXr on 2008-02-23
System>Preferences>Main Menu
click on **programming** on the left hand menu
on the right hand side click **New Item**
a new window pops up called **Create Launcher**
Name= netbeans
Command=/usr/local/netbeans-6.0.1/bin/netbeans
click close on the Command Launcher
and make sure that the check box next to the new netbeans item is checked
it should now be in your menu under programming
