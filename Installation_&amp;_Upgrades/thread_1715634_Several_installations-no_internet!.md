### Post by fela on 2011-03-27
If you can't connect to your ADSL modem under Ubuntu, the modem probably has a non-standard configuration and you need to manually configure the network on the Ubuntu box.

Are there any other computers that connect to the modem fine? If so, have a look at their network configurations to see what might need fixing.
