### Post by mlxx on 2009-11-06
Almost the same thing happened to me. I was installing 9.10 on a Dell XPS M1330 laptop with nVidia graphics (GeForce 8400M GS) and I couldn't make it work. The only thing that worked for me was to choose the "safe graphics mode" from the install screen, and now I have Ubuntu 9.10 up and running. 
One last tip: ignore the message that recommends to download and install nVidia controllers, this messed up my graphics and I had to reinstall again...
Good luck!
