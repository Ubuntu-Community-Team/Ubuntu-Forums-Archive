I take it that the drive from your old  computer was installed on the new computer without being formated, is this correct? Personally in 10 years I have yet to take a hard drive from one machine and use it as the master drive on a different computer and have it work properly. Reason is the drivers, etc are different in the registry because of the difference in the mother board and other hardware configurations, which causes errors. My experience is that it's best to wipe the new/drive after you install it in the new computer, then load the O.S. because of the difference in the hardware configurations.
