### Post by jimrz on 2007-11-29
> **jimbogy said:**
> how to do upgrade from Ubuntu 6.1 to 7.1 ?

You would first have to upgrade from 6.10 to 7.04 the upgrade again from 7.04 to 7.10 and likely would have some issues as a result. This would be especially true if you have a fair amount of customization and/or have installed software from other than the official repo's on your 6.10. I believe you would be much better off to back up your data and do a fresh install of 7.10.
