### Post by tread on 2005-05-17
To get those special characters in latex, you need to use codes, not just type them in.

For eg.,
In the word naive, if you want two dots above the i, you need to say na\"\i ve 

Get the Not-so-short-introduction-to-latex [here](http://www.ctan.org/tex-archive/info/lshort/english/lshort.pdf) .. it should help.
