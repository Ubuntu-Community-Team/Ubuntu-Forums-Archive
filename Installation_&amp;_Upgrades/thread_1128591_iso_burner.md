### Post by arpanaut on 2009-04-17
What has always worked in every Ubuntu I have used since forever is:

Insert blank disk in burner, go to where I have the iso saved, right click on iso file, choose "write to disc...", select X4 speed, Done!

I usually use torrent download to save myself integrity checking of files downloaded.
And when I first boot the disc, I choose "check disc integrity" just to make sure.

Works 99% of the time for me...
