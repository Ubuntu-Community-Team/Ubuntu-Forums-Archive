[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/ubiquity/+bug/1039405](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/ubiquity/+bug/1039405)

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/ubiquity/+bug/45646](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/ubiquity/+bug/45646)

I have a feeling that the Ubiquity team is just making excuses.  I hate to put the blame on them, but maybe the bug isn't as obvious to them as it is to me.

Any comments about moving the bug forward?
