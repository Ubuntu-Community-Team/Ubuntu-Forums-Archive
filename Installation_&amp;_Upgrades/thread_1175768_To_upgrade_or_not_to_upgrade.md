### Post by mcduck on 2009-06-01
> **matamoscas said:**
> 2 second background...

My question is..since I am not opposed to updating my existing software, but I DO NOT want to upgrade to 9.04:

No, you won't. Updating to new distribution version will never happen simply by installing normal updates, or without specifically asking if you want to do that or not.

Unless you tell the package manager to start looking for programs in another Ubuntu release's repositories it will only look for packages available in your current version. You can safely install all updates that are available.
