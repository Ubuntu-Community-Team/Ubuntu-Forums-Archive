### Post by duhfactor on 2008-10-29
Problem solved,

I burned a new disk with ubuntu 8.04, and installed it in safe graphics mode after hitting F4 to select other boot options at the initial screen. The diagonal lines disappeared. I may have to modify my display/monitor settings later, but at least it is working good now, and I can get in and use the OS.....I think safe mode uses the VESA driver, (suggested by someone else)and for whatever reason, it's good to go...
