### Post by lisati on 2009-04-23
People often notice multiple kernels on their boot menu after doing an update - this is normal.

Usually using the one that is automatically highlighted on your boot menu will be the one to use. The others are there to give you a choice just in case some aspect of the update doesn't work for you as well as might be hoped.
