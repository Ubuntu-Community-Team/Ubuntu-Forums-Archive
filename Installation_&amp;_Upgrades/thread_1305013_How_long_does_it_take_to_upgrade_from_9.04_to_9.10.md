### Post by retrow on 2009-10-30
It also depends on how many additional programs you've installed. If you have any programs from third party repositories, it can take an awfully long time to upgrade on day 1, since all third party repositories don't have as many mirrors as the standard package repositories.Also, for Jaunty I'd choose a mirror of some educational institution close to where I live, that way the sources for Karmic have path of the university repositories instead of the main mirror in the country. The main mirror gets overworked on the day of release. If you have good connection speed and don't have many 3rd party programs installed, you should be done in little over an hour.
