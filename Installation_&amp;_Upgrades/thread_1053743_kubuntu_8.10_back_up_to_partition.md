### Post by caljohnsmith on 2009-01-29
Unless you specify otherwise with VirtualBox, I believe all of your VirtualBox personal settings, VirtualBox drives, etc. are in the .VirtualBox directory in your home folder. You could copy that folder to a DVD or however you wish to back it up. To access that folder, you could go to Places > Home Folder, and then when the nautilus file browser comes up, check the box under View > Show Hidden Files. Then you should see your .Virtualbox directory in your home folder. Good luck and let me know how it goes or if you run into problems.
