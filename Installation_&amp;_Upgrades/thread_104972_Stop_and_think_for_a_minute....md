### Post by BLTicklemonster on 2005-12-17
Okay, I'm going to provide some humor here to get a point across. Lots of people from all walks and all stations of life come here because they have embarked on a totally new journey in life, and need help. Many of the people here can't remember or don't know at all what it's like to be confronted with something totally, so totally different that they have absolutely no comprehension of what it is they have in front of them. For instance, there are people who think that AOL is the interenet. Skoff all you wan't but until you know any different, how do you know any different?

What I'm trying to say is, when someone comes in saying they don't get it, try to realize that, yeah, you may get it, but to them, this is all a great big mystery, and they need help and understanding, not impatient jabs. Some people see Windows as the best os for reasons that make total sense to them, and for them, this is legitimate. 

I've seen some replies that make that vein in my forhead start bulging, and I feel really sorry for the person posting. Both persons, actually. What if those two were the last two people on the earth, and they had one chance to do something in linux, 10 seconds to do it, or they both die, and the noob is at the keyboard, they 1337 linux user is physically incapacitated but can talk. Bye bye mankind, right?

With this in mind, here are some funnies to peruse, see if any of them ring a bell:

Customer: I'm trying to connect to the Internet with your CD, but it

Tech support: OK, you've got the CD in the CD drive, right?

Customer: Yeah....

Tech support: And what sort of computer are you using?

Customer: Computer? Oh no, I haven't got a computer. It's in the CD
player and all I get is weird noises. Listen.....

Tech support: Aaaarrrrgggghhhh!!!

===============

Tech support: What kind of computer do you have?

Customer: A white one...

Customer: Hi, this is Celine. I can't get my diskette out.

Tech support: That doesn't sound good; I'll make a note.

A woman customer called the Canon help desk with a problem with her
printer.

And last but not least:....

So yeah, it could be worse! Be patient and understanding of other's feelings and views, because hey, not everybody gets it right off the bat.
