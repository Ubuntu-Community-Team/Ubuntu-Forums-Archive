### Post by sochin on 2013-04-23
dino99, thanks for the 'obvious' suggestions.

i found out that the EFI partition has to the at the start
of the disk, and, as you suggested, that the installer has
to know where on the hdd this partition is... as described here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UEFI#Creating_an_EFI_partition](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UEFI#Creating_an_EFI_partition)

with those changes : i'm in!  

Thanks.

One more detail, to get into the BIOS on a Vaio SVS13, you can
press F2 or Esc or Del all day long, there is an Assist button at the
top of the keyboard that is pressed when power is off, that takes
you into the Vaio setup for BIOS etc...
