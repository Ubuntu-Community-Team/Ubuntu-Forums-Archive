### Post by Partyboi2 on 2008-04-15
You don't have to reformat your ubuntu partition. After you have installed the 64bit vista you will need to restore grub. You can do that following [these]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows#head-bf3232f10ddf1b078de064622ccbb25225cdb3c0") instructions.
