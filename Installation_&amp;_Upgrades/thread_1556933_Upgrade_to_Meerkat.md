### Post by prshah on 2010-08-20
> **saurabh_nnegi said:**
> Is there any way that I can update 10.04 to Meerkat from CD rather than over the net.

Pick up the "alternate" CDs of 10.10 from [here]("http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily/current/")

You can upgrade an existing system off the alternate CD. If you allow it, (and I suggest you do), it will download updated files from the Internet during the upgradation; if you choose not to (or disallow downloading of updates) it will upgrade your system off the files on the alternate CD.

Since it's an Alpha, I suggest that you allow updation from  the Internet while upgrading.
