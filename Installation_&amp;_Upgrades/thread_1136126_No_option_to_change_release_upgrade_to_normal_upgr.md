### Post by snowpine on 2009-04-25
8.04 is the most recent release of Dell's special version of Ubuntu. There is no "Dellbuntu" 8.10 or 9.04.

It is completely your choice; if you are happy with the Dell version, it will be supported through April 2011. But, if you want to try something "newer," you can re-install the "regular" (non-Dell) version of Ubuntu 8.10 or 9.04 from scratch. I personally am using 9.04 on my Mini 9 with no major problems.
