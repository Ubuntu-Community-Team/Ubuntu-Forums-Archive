### Post by bapoumba on 2008-04-06
I have a laptop close to your specs (with an intel graphics card). What I did, with the alternate cd, is run a minimal install. You end up with no gui, perform all the update/upgrades from there, and then install xubuntu-desktop.

Depending on availability of network during the install, you may have to adjust the sources.list file.
