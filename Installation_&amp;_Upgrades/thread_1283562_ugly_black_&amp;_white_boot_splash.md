### Post by Frantic_Earthling on 2009-10-30
> **2roombas said:**
> After upgrading my old Dimension 2350 desktop to Karmic, I now have a very boring black & white boot splash. Some very small text on the screen before that too.

When you are on the login screen, click on the small round icon on the left-hand side of the date in the bottom panel (there's a small man standing in it). This will present you with a new windows and several options. Tick "Enhance Contrast in Color". Things will look a little bit brighter on your next boot!
