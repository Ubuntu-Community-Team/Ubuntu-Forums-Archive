### Post by Vargster on 2008-01-31
I don't want to come across harsh, but if you choose to install a new operating system, then pick the "USE ENTIRE DISK" option, what exactly where you expecting to happen?

The "use entire disk" option does just that, it blows away the entire contents of the drive and sets up Ubuntu from scratch - using the entire disk. There is a warning, that you clicked OK to, that said the disk will be reformatted and you will lose the data on it...
