### Post by bluepowder7 on 2008-06-10
i just installed the ubuntu-studio software packages (so, audio / video / graphics packages only, NOT an upgrade to a whole new OS flavour), and it kept everything else that i had.  same wallpaper, same everything.  oh, the ONLY two things that were odd were:

1 - added a new boot entry in GRUB, which i haven't used, i now manually have to arrow-key-down to the ...generic one, and maybe one day i'll edit the GRUB list to remove the "new" one that i haven't used

2 - my compiz-fusion had to get re-enabled (and i had to re-enable the nVidia restricted drivers).  i'm not sure but i MIGHT have needed to restore my settings as well (i did it anyways, before i realized that the nVid thing was the issue).  best to export / save your c-f settings first.
