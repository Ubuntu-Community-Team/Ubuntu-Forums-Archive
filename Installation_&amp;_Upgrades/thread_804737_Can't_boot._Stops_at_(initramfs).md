### Post by wpshooter on 2008-05-23
You said that you moved the computer.

Were you very careful not to cant or twist the computer housing when you moved it.

This could be something as simple as a connector not being properly seated.

Check all of your drive connectors at both the drives and on the motherboard (also check all of your other connectors) and then take out and reseat any addon cards into the same slots that they are currently in.

Good luck.
