### Post by darkod on 2012-08-27
If there is no data in 10.04 that you need, you can boot the 12.04 and delete the 10.04 partition(s). After that you can resize the 12.04 partition. But what you can or can not do exactly will depend on the disk layout, how the existing partitions are ordered.

And resizing partitions always has a certain degree of risk so you better make a full backup of your 12.04 in case something goes wrong.
