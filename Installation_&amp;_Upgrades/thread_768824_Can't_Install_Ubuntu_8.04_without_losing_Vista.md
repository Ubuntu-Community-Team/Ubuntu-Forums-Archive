### Post by Pumalite on 2008-04-26
Don't worry. All is well. Get a gfood CD of Hardy. First allocate space for Ubuntu with the Vista partitioner. Then boot your CD, start the installation, gfo Manual and in the 'free space' make 3 'New' partitions:
10 GB for '/'
1 GB for /swap (/swap=RAM in laptops)
The rest for /home
Then press 'Forward'. The installer will do the rest for you. We can fix menu.lst later if necessary.
