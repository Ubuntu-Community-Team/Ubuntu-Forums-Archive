### Post by deadflowr on 2013-07-04
> [COLOR=#000000]It may be a video card problem but why is the whole of Ubuntu so slow = XP?[/COLOR]

With Ubuntu, it typically is the graphics card.
Unity, the default desktop for Ubuntu, is a heavy desktop, compared to Xfce, or Lxde.
On a system ten years old, you're much better served running either of the choices listed above in post #2.

Unlike Windows, however, you will find that there are a plethora of choices, and you can even install much lighter gui's than xfce or lxde.
