### Post by Quackers on 2011-07-11
Welcome to UF.
What size does your computer's bios report for the size of the hard drive?
Some bioses can't see past the 137GB mark in a disc and need a bios update or a setting changing. The setting can be called "large drive" or LBA, or something else.
While you are in your bios have a look around for such an option.

Does your XP installation use the whole hard drive or is there free space on the disc (outside of a partition - not inside it)?
