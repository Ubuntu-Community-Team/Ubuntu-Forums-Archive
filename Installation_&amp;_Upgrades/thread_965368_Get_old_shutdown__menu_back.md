### Post by Kobus Retief on 2008-10-31
I suspect you'll find yourself disappointed.  There's a big thread on the subject here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=955176]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=955176"), but it seems like it's yet another "let's-change-things-without-consulting-anybody-else" situation. My solution: run my laptop without any shutdown/logout/user_switch applets, and depend on the power manager to suspend when I close the screen, and shut down when I press the power button. Seriously, guys, sometimes change purely for the sake of change is just a little overrated.
