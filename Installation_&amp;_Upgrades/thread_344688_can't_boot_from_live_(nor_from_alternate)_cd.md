### Post by taurus on 2007-01-23
Well, I guess you burned it wrong then.  Basically, you just copied the iso file from your harddrive to a CD.  Otherwise, you would see a bunch of files and directories on the CD.  You need to burn the iso image again.  Again, this link will walk you through the whole process.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)
or
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso)
