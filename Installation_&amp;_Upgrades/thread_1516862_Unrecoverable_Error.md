### Post by Joe of loath on 2010-06-24
> **mblanco2000 said:**
> Ok cool.  I think I am going to like ubuntu alot more than windows.  How would I run memtest from the CD?

When you see the purple splash screen after booting, press up or down on the arrow keys. A menu will appear, after asking for your language, and 'test memory' is about the 3rd down. Select it, hit enter, and leave your PC until it finishes. You'll know if something's wrong, because there will be lots of red :lol:
