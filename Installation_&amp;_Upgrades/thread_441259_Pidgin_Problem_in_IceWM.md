### Post by Palpy on 2007-05-15
Alt+F2 shows the "Run" dialog in GNOME and XFCE. In IceWM, the shortcut is Win+Space -- you get a text box in place of the taskbar. As for the terminal emulator, "pidgin &" should do the trick. What do you mean by "no icon"? In the menu? IceWM has no desktop icons on its own...
