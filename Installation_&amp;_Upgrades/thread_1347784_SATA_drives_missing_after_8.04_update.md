### Post by darkod on 2009-12-06
Did you upgrade to 9.10 or just updates for 8.04?
9.10 seem to "lose" drives that have some traces of raid on them. Unless you are running raid setup, and if that is your problem too, I've seen this to help:
sudo apt-get remove dmraid
