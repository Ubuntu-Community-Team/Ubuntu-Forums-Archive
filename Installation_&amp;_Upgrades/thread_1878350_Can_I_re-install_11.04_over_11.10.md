Hello and Welcome 

If you move home to its own partition your data will be safe . A web search would help with getting that done and there are members here that do that as well  .

Otherwise you will have to create a backup on a removable device . I have begun to use Ubuntu One cloud storage , they supply 5 Gb of storage for free . I keep no music on the hard-drive so this works great with a limited amount of data .
