### Post by varunus on 2005-07-19
Ubuntu uses ".deb" packages with Synaptic.  If you want to download a package manually, you can go to [http://packages.ubuntu.com/hoary/](http://packages.ubuntu.com/hoary/) and get the .deb file.  You may have to take care of dependencies manually too though, which can be irritating...It may be better just to use the 56k connection and keep synaptic on one virtual desktop, and do other stuff on the other desktops.  (Overnight works too.)
