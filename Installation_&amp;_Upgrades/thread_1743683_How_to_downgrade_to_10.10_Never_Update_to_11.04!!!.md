### Post by punkybouy on 2011-04-29
When you get to the login screen at the bottom there is a box where you can select "Ubuntu Classic" and that will take you to a Gnome 2.3 desktop.  Put your user name and password in and the choose "Ubuntu Classic" and you should be good.  The setting is sticky too so you shouldn't have to keep selecting that when you log in.
