### Post by Hugh Mulqueen on 2009-12-07
To get the optical out working on your motherboard do the following:

1. Goto System > Preferences > Sound

2. Click on the Hardware Tab.

3. Select the Internal Audio Button.

4. Then under the heading [Settings for selected device] choose Digital Stereo(IEC958) Output + Analog Stereo Input

5. And thats it!, play an sound file to test.

6. Make sure you have a sound system hooked onto the optical out or else you'll hear nothing.

Now as regards the USB DAC I hear most of them are supported with Linux.

I'd say the device would come up as an External Audio button in Sound Preferences and you would configure it the same way as the motherboard. Dont be phased with the multitude of options you have.
