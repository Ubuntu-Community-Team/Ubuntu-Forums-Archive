### Post by FenrirXIII on 2013-04-24
also if you dont have any driver available, go to:

 [http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/search/01/search/?OSC=LX](http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/search/01/search/?OSC=LX)

and search your model driver

after accepting terms, select the correct .DEB files and download. once downloaded, locate the file and double click to open in Ubuntu Software Center, install and enjoy.

also dont forget to get a scanner driver while you are at it. :D
