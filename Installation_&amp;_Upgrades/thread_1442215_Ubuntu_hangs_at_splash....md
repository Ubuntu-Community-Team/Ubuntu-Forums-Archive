I'll see the OS GRUB menu, select the top (of 4) Ubuntu entries, and wait. I'll make it to the white Ubuntu splash symbol and things hang up. I reset and repeat, and usually after a few tries, I am able to get into Ubuntu. It is a minor inconvenience having to reset over and over again, and Ubuntu works flawlessly once I am running; I'd just like to sort this out so that I can better enjoy my machine. 

As usual, any help will be greatly appreciated.
