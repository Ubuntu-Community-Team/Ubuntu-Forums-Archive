### Post by NonPapa on 2007-05-18
Okay, here is my report of what happened next, and - SPOILER NOTICE - I am a very happy ubuntu bunny again:

1. Using the CDR for an upgrade didn't work. Okay.
2. Installing it from the CDR when starting my PC didn't work, at first, but
3. after removing some partitions (I had about 8!), and defragmenting a Windows account
4. Ubuntu didn't get stuck with my 512MB Ram any more.
5. Installation went without a hitch, and I was even able to
6. Import my documents from the previous Ubuntu (6.10) account !
7. Also, it is nice to see a new feature: 7.04 makes it easier to
[URL="https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats"]
configure Ubuntu to allow restricted formats[/URL] - it's almost too easy. 

So, all in all:
**if you are happy to install fully Ubuntu again, it works nicely.**
