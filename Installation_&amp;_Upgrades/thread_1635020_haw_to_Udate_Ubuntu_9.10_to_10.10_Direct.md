### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-12-01
Welcome to the forums :-)

Direct upgrade is only possible in case of an LTS to another LTS release. You'll need to upgrade 9.10 > 10.04 > 10.10. No direct method.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MaverickUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MaverickUpgrades)

> You can directly upgrade to Ubuntu 10.10 ("Maverick Meerkat") from **[COLOR="Red"]Ubuntu 10.04 LTS ("Lucid Lynx")[/COLOR]** [COLOR="Red"]**only**[/COLOR] 

Before upgrading, I would advise to burn Live CD of both 10.04 and 10.10 and test all of your hardware with that. Grab the one that better supports your PC.

If both do support, obviously you can upgrade to 10.10 ;-)

Good Luck!
