### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-06-28
We cannot go from 12.04 to 16.04. We have to go from 12.04 to 14.04 to 16.04. At some point you will have to move to 14.04. Whether you stay on 14.04 for 1 or 2 years or longer before moving to 16.04 is up to you. Or make a quick move from 14.04 to 16.04 is entirely up to you.

If we want to run LTS versions for the maximum length of their support lives then we cannot avoid the situation where 1 LTS version in 3 is used only for a short period of time.

To me, it seems sensible to create a partition with a duplicate install of 12.04 in it and then test the upgrade to 14.04 before doing it for real with your working 12.04. And then repeat the process with the upgrade to 16.04. I do this with each release of Ubuntu desktop. 

Regards.
