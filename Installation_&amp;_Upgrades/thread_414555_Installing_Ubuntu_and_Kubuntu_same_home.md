When upgrading to Feisty, I will be installing both Ubuntu and Kubuntu, since I want to have a 'pure gnome' and a 'pure KDE' environment. I am planning of partitioning my 250 Gb harddisk like this:

10 Gb root Ubuntu
10 Gb root Kubuntu
2 Gb swap
20 Gb /home
rest files

Can Ubuntu an Kubuntu share the same /home directory? Or is it better to set up a seperate /home directory for each environment?

---

### Post by aysiu on 2007-04-19
If you share a /home, then you won't have a pure Gnome and a pure KDE any more. You'll have a bunch of empty (no icon) menu entries in your applications menus.
