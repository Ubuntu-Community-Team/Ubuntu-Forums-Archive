Edit your BIOS settings to boot from CDROM before Hard Disk.

To enter your BIOS:

Either push F2 during POST boot process. (DELL PC's Only I Believe)
Press 'delete' key during POST.  Push several times just to be certain! (Not the BackSpace key)
