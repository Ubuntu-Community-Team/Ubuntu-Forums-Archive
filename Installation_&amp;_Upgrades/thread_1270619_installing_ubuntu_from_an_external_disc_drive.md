### Post by hambone79 on 2009-09-20
If you continue to have problems with the external drive and you have a USB jump drive laying around, you may want to consider creating a bootable jump drive using [UNetbootin]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/"). I have used this trick several times when dealing with laptops that have a flaky or non-existent CD/DVD-ROM.
