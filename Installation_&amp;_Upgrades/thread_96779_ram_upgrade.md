### Post by bwog on 2005-11-29
By searching the web you can check if your motherboard is 'sensitive' for the kind of RAM it needs. The manufacturers site will also show which RAM has been tested on your machine. 

Often you can use only 2 slots without problems, even if you have 3. Sometimes you need to use pairs of similar RAM.

It is a often a good idea to upgrade to 1GB though.
