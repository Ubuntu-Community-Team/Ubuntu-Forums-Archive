### Post by narendraD on 2010-05-10
Probably Ubuntu Desktop (which is part of Ubuntu) got marked for deletion when you installed Xubu and Kubu. 

With 3 DE there (Gnome, KDE and XFCE), i will be uncertain if an upgrade will be smooth and trouble free. I would suggest a fresh install after you backup all important data and this time choose your flavour well. Multiple DE's are not good for an upgrade.
