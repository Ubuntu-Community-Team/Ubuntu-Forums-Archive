### Post by Joe2Shoe on 2008-10-08
I had Ubuntu installed on the D:/ partition of my drive of a 8.1GB hard drive on my old ThinkPad 770X running Windows ME.
The drive was partitioned C:/ (2.1GB) and D:/ (6.0GB).
After uninstalling Grub and Ubuntu, and rebooting to Windows, the D:/ drive space is now only 1.78GB.

Where is my hard drive space on D:/?
How do I correct this?

### Post by Joe2Shoe on 2008-10-09
SOLVED!
After the 6th time booting into DOS-mode and running FDISK, I finally regained the other 6GB of hard disk space in Windows.
Close, please.
