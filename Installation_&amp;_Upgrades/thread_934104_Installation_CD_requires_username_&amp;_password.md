### Post by Elfy on 2008-09-30
No it's not normal and quite often is indicative a dodgy burn or bad download.

Did you check that the download you burnt your livecd was good to start with - did you check the md5sum for it, if you didn't then do so, info [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM#MD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM#MD5SUM) on Windows

If the download is good, when you burnt it did you use a cdr and not a cdrw and did you burn it ata slow speed - [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)
