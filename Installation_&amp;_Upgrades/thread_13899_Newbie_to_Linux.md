### Post by trivialpackets on 2005-02-03
ndiswrapper.  down load this and do a search for ndiswrapper tutorials.  the ndiswrapper will 'wrap' around and utilize the driver for the windows version of the driver.  I believe if you have your driver cd, unbuntu can look through the cd and find the .inf file that is necessary.  There are good tutorials that walk yout hrough, but it will depend upon what your wireless card is and such.  This forum is a wealth of info. Welcome.
