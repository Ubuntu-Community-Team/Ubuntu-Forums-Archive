### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-02-06
11.10 Wubi unreliable. Most Wubi installs seem to be. Is intended to try Ubuntu for short time, not permanent. If it runs well from the disk ('Try Ubuntu' option) then perhaps think about a side-by-side dual boot (install Ubuntu in its own partitions on the hard drive). Faster, better user experience, and less problematic.

If you really want to go with Wubi install, try a stable release. The current long-term support release, 10.04 LTS, might be the go. Welcome and good luck. ;)
