### Post by Frogs Hair on 2013-07-06
It is recommended that upgrades go in order except for LTS releases . 12.04 could upgrade to 14.04 when it is released. I would use 12.04 until  a way to create a disk is found because if something goes wrong you will need a disk or USB anyway. 13.04 is only supported until January while 12.04 is supported until 2017 . 13.04 requires a DVD or USB due to the size of the ISO and won't fit on a CD.
