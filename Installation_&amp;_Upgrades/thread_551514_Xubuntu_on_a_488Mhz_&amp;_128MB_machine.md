### Post by vampire666eng on 2007-09-15
I found what was the problem.:#-o
Simply a bad cd media.
When I tried the desktop-cd and then the alternative method I used the same cd-rw. (that was causing the install not to finish for the desktop-cd and the error at 83% for the alternative method).

I just burned the alternative cd on another cd-rw and now everything is fine.

Damn! If I knew that before I wouldn't loose all the day trying to figure it what was causing the problem (searching the net, etc...etc...).;)

Some times it's just...CD/dvd media fault!

EDIT: anyway thanks Pumalite, if xubuntu is too much for my secondary PC I will try the distros you mentioned.;)
