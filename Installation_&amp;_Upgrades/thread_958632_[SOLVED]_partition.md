### Post by jespdj on 2008-10-25
Yes, you can install Ubuntu on the other partition. Before you install it, make a backup of all your important files on your Windows XP partition. Be careful and pay attention during the installation of Ubuntu, so that you don't accidentally make Ubuntu take over the whole harddisk or delete your XP partition.
