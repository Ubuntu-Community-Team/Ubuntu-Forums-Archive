### Post by leg on 2013-06-19
I have recently updated my laptop from 12.04 to 13.04 and after a few issues with my wireless I am very happy with the outcome. The upgrade itself went fine but after my wireless network card did not work. I have a BCM4312 LP-PHY and my mistake was using a thread that described getting it working with a slightly different driver. Once someone pointed out my error I had it working very quickly and my laptop actually seems to run better than it did before.

My system appears to boot quicker and I am in a working session earlier that I was with 12.04. Applications appear to load quicker and, as I said, I am just overall pleased with the outcome.
