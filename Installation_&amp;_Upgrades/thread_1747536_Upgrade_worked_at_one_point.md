### Post by Dutch70 on 2011-05-02
Edit: See the link from madjr below. Much simpler, I forgot about that.

Your settings for your apps are in your .config folder. Go there and delete the contents. You have to select "show hidden files" in 10.10, I'm not exactly sure how to do that in 11.04.

home folder > (show hidden files) > .config > compiz > compizconfig & delete the contents of that folder.
