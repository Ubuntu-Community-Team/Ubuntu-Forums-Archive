### Post by meng on 2006-12-15
Yes it could be a problem with the download or burn. If you suspect this, check the md5sum on the download, or burn at a slower rate. On the other hand, it could be that your PC doesn't meet minimum requirements for a LiveCD install (192 MB RAM, but this is a guideline only) or for some other reason just isn't playing nice. In that case, download and burn the Alternate CD instead of the Live CD.
