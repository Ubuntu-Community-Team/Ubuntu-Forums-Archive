### Post by sparklyprgs on 2012-04-30
I saw that problem on a box I was installing Xubuntu on but I can't replicate the fault! however I think it's due to a particular combinations of styles and window manager settings being set BEFORE upgrading to 12.04. This might be because 12.04 has a much smaller list of styles in the appearance settings area, so what you had before doesn't transfer over well.
At any rate all I did was change the styles in the appearance settings *as well as* in window manager settings and somehow, the problem was fixed.
