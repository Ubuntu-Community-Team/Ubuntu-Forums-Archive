### Post by ve4cib on 2009-02-21
Hardy (8.04.x) and Dapper (6.06.x) are the only two LTS releases so far.  (Dapper is still supported until June of this year, and Hardy will be supported until 2011 on the Desktop side and 2013 on the server side.)

As for the next LTS release, no formal announcement has been made of which I am aware.  I would speculate that the next one will probably be version 10.04 (the one after Karmic).  The "trend" (if such it can be called given that there are only 2 LTS releases to date) seems to be to release an LTS version every two years in April*.  This gives a one-year overlap during the LTS support period.

So, to answer your question more precisely the next LTS releases will likely be:

- 10.04 (Leaping Lemur?**)
- 12.04 (Pouncing Puma?**)
- 14.04 (Reticulating Rhesus?**)

*Dapper was held up for a couple of months and came out in June, but it was scheduled for April originally if I remember things properly.

**Release names are complete speculation on my part; I honestly have no idea what those releases will be called.
