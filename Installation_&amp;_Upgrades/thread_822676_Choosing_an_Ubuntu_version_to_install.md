### Post by aysiu on 2008-06-08
LTS doesn't stand for "stabler than other releases." It stands for "long-term support."

LTS releases get longer support, meaning that they'll get security updates for three years on the desktop/laptop (five years on the server) instead of the typical 1.5 years non-LTS releases get.

So Dapper will be supported until June 2009, and Hardy will be supported until April 2011.
