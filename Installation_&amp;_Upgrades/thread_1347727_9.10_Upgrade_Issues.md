### Post by paradigm2 on 2009-12-06
A clean install seems to always be the best approach.  I would use a secondary drive whether it be a normal hard drive or a flash drive and copy over all your needed data to the 2nd drive.  Then do a clean install and intelligently copy the data back to the primary hard drive.
