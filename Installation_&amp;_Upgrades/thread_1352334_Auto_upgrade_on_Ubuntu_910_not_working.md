### Post by EdWh on 2009-12-19

Hi All,   I seem to have solved my problem.  On my desktop I dragged the top application bar to the bottom of the screen. The icon for updates appears in the bar under the application bar.   I also put a extra shutdown notice on the bottom left side of the screen and placed it where the icon for updates appears so the icon was in the way.  This area is also used to notify user of where he is at at the moment so placing anything there blocks the notices, I believe.  I moved the shut down icon up to the application bar and today was notified updates were available.   Just info to any concerned.   Thanks for reading the post.  'Merry Christmas. :popcorn:  EdWh.
