### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2011-10-23
Using the menus "System > Administration > Update Manager" open the update manager. On the bottom of the window that opens, click the "Settings" button. On the "Ubuntu Software" tab, remove the check in the check box for "Installable from CD-ROM/DVD".

Then try to update again.

### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2011-10-23
Another thought...

Ubuntu 10.04 LTS should only be offering to upgrade to 10.10 and not 11.10. Have you edited the sources.list file manually? Have you tried to upgrade to 11.10 or tried to install 11.10 and failed? Do you have a CD/DVD of 11.10 in the CD/DVD drive?
