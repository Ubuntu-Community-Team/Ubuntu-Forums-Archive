### Post by spotdog14 on 2007-10-20
I though i might let you guys know that i have upgraded a few laptops with intel graphics cards that have booted to a black screen with a blinking cursor and though i would post the answer here, since i found it to be super helpful.

I read it in another post on here a few pages back, what you do is go in and edit the xorg.conf and change the driver that is used from i810 to intel, and reboot and everything should be dandy. At least it was for me and my Asus S5n, Dell D420 and an unknown Compaq that i did.

Hopefully this will help some other people. And keep up the good work community and Ubuntu!
