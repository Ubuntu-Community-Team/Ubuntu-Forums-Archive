### Post by 23dornot23d on 2011-10-24
Try this one ..... [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1863430](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1863430)


> 

At the login screen, press CTRL+ALT+F1.
Login to the terminal with your username and password.
enter the following lines, where {{ username }} == your username:
 

    Code:
     cd /home/{{ username }} mv .Xauthority Xauthority.bak 


Now press CTRL+ALT+F7.
You should be able to login normally :smile:
