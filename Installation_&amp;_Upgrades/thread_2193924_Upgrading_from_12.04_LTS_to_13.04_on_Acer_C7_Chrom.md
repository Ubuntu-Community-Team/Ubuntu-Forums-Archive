### Post by ubfan1 on 2013-12-15
You'd probably be better off waiting until 14.04 and upgrading the 12.04 directly.  The usual upgrade you are asking about is done by changing the update settings (look for the button at the bottom of the screen) to something other than just the next "LTS" version.  Then upgrade to the next offered version, probably 12.10 in your case, then upgrade it to 13.04, which at this time, has only a very limited support life left.  Then you will be stuck with upgrading to 13.10, before you can upgrade to 14.04.  Your choice.
