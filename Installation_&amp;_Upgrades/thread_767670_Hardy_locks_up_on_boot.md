### Post by sarsippius on 2008-04-25
Think I found the problem!

I have a Linksys WMP11 v2.7 wireless card installed.

I booted into recovery mode, and it started listing out the hardware installed on my computer.  Once it got to the wireless card, it locked up.

Once I took out the card, the computer booted up perfectly!!!

If I had not tried that, I would have never guessed that card would have been the whole problem.  I think I've tried loading my system at least 10 times!
