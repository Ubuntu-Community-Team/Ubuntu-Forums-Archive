### Post by wpshooter on 2006-10-20
Here's what I would do.

Go to printer setup of Ubuntu administration and then I would delete all existing printers.  I would then reboot Ubuntu and I would then go back into printer setup and redo the printer setup wizard for your Epson printer and make sure you set it as the default printer before you exit out of printer setup section and then see if it does not come up correctly in your OO program.

Good luck.
