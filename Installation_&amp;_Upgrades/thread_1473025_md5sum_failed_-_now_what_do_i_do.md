### Post by lavinog on 2010-05-04
I have noticed that many times the problem is the cd burner.  There is very little tolerance for error on the live cds.  CD burners tend to have write errors as they get older.
My DVD burner was about 4 years old, and I would occasionally get a bad burn (1 out of 4)...I bought a new DVD burner for $20 and have had no bad burns since then.
