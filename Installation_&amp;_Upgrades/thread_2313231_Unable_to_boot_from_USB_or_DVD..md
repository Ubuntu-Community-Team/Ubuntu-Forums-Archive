### Post by yancek on 2016-02-10
> They are loaded properly, 

The only way that can be actually verified if you have booted either/both in another machine and verified they boot.  Did you do that?

I guess the F12 key method isn't working.  Watch the screen on boot and when you see the Gateway logo, watch the bottom of the screen for the key to press to enter the BIOS setup and do that.  Look for a boot  tab and change the priority to the DVD or flash drive and Save/Reboot.
