### Post by GnuSense on 2012-04-29
Do you have nvidia graphics?  I understand the proprietary nvidia driver that ships with 12.04 won't handle Unity 3D (the default desktop). When you get to the lightdm login screen try choosing Ubuntu 2D instead.  Or simply use the command line to install a less demanding desktop like XFCE and wait until Ubuntu sorts out its driver issues.
