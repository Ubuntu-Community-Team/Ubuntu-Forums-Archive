### Post by Elfy on 2010-08-07
Backports are not officially supported afaik - [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuBackports](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuBackports) [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu)

Updates downloaded in the background are downloaded without any intervention on your part - once downloaded the update manager will tell you there are updates available.
