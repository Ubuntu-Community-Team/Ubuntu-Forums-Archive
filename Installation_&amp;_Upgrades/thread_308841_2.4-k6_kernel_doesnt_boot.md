### Post by diepruis on 2006-11-30
According to [this site]("http://batleth.sapienti-sat.org/projects/FAQs/ext3-faq.html") only kernels from 2.4.15 onwards support ext3 filesystems natively (without patches). If your kernel is earlier than this one, and if your root filesystem is ext3fs, then that is your problem and you need to upgrade to a newer 2.4.* kernel.
