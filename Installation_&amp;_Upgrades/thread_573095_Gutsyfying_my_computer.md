### Post by Forlong on 2007-10-11
A fresh install is only possible with a CD.

If your concern is only about the configurations files: they are all stored in your /home directory, so you can delete them there (they are hidden so you have to press [Ctrl]+[H]] to make them visible).

But there's no such thing as resetting your package management automatically to the status of a fresh install.
