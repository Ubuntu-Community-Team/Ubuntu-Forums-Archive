### Post by BackwardsDown on 2007-05-21
When you upgrade your installation to feisty it downloads the package's you need, and extract them to your hard drive.

But on the cd there is far more, on the cd there are the packages, but not only the packages that needed upgrading to feisty. It also contains the file's for the live-cd and many packages for all sorts of hardware.

Simply said, you cant extract an installation cd from an installed system. When you have to upgrade more than 1 pc and your internet is limited. You could just download the install cd and upgrade from that (not 100% sure about it but I think it should be possible).
