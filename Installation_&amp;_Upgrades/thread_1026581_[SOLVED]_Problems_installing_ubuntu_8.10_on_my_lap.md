### Post by thraxy on 2009-01-01
The problem could be the ISO file. You could try downloading it again and trying to burn another CD. Or if you have a USB flash drive and your computer can boot from USB, there's an easier way.

You can download unetbootin from [http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)

Run that and you can choose Ubuntu from the dropdown menu at the top. That will then automatically download and extract the liveUSB files to the USB drive.

After that, all you need to do is boot from USB.
