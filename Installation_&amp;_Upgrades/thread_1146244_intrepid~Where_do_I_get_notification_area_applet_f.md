### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-05-02
Open Synaptic, search for "awn", mark all *awn-applets-c-core* and *awn-applets-python-extras* packages for install and click "apply". There are 3 different "Notification Area" applets: "Notification Area", "PyNot" and "PyNot RGBA". I prefer the first.
