### Post by wannadumpwindows on 2008-07-30
Search synaptic for 'qgrubeditor".

It makes things really easy. It lets you change or modify anything you'd like to about grub.

You can make the screen flash, change colors and even create grub splash images from your own pictures. It's a pretty nice little tool to have.
