### Post by yeats on 2009-05-02
I have one of these on order, so I'm interested in the solution.  As far as I understand it, they are working from Dell-run, Dell-specific repositories.  You can verify this by looking at "Software Sources" under System -> Administration or by looking at the file at /etc/apt/sources.list or files in the /etc/apt/sources.list.d/ directory.  The solution may be to add/enable Ubuntu repositories and disable the Dell ones...
