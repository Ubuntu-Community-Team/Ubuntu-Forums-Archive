### Post by king_arthur on 2007-05-24
Hi Hexo,

no way to do what you are asking AFAIK.

There is no substitute to experience: just do the upgrade and, in case, fix what went wrong.
However, you might be lucky and get it to work straight away.
Personally, if your server/desktop runs critical apps, I suggest asking yourself first if the added features are worth the effort.

Most of the times they are not.
It's just the "upgrade frenzy" pushing people. :)
In my experience, new releases just make clean installs easier although, in the case of Feisty, it really made my system "zippy".

And that's all about it... ;)
