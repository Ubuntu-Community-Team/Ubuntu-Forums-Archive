### Post by StevenX on 2007-03-10
I'm trying to install 6.10 from the LiveCD and have ran the installer succesfully up until step 5 of 6. I have two hard drives, the first with WinXP installed and the second with just loads of music (no OS) but about 20 GB free space. After selecting the second hard drive, I clicked the "resize, and use freed space," option.
The installer then put the "new partition size," bar to 75% (26.3 GB) and I clicked "Forward."

Then a small window popped up which said something like "Installing," or "Preparing to install," but it came up VERY briefly. When it left, I was on the same screen with the "Forward," and "Back," buttons active again. After clicking the "Forward," button again the cursor changes to the busy cursor, and the forward and back buttons become inactive, but nothing happens. The computer is idle and it appears no partitioning is happening.

Any ideas on how I can pass this screen? Thanks in advance!

I got it fixed by dragging the partition size bar down to the lowest available (which was 51%). I assume that the 70% was more than was actually available on the hard drive?

Fairly simple solution, but it's not a very user friendly way to start out giving no error dialogs! :)
