### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-08-27
Did you run the Live CD for a week continuously to explore what works and what doesn't on your laptop.?

If you make a list of things that don't work when running the Live CD, then you will have an idea of what needs fixing when you install it.

If many things are broken with the Live CD, then try Dapper 6.06.1 LTS Live CD.  If you are still having trouble, then try another Linux distribution such as Fedora 7 or openSuse.

Don't forget to burn your disks at 4x speed and check the md5sum.

Edit:  If you have a slow connection, then try Damn Small Linux.  It's 50 MB, and it runs well on older hardware.  Look up a local Linux User's group and bum a CD of Dapper from them.
