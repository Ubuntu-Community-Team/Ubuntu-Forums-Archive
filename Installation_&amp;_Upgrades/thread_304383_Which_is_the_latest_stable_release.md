### Post by 23meg on 2006-11-21
Edgy is the latest stable release as of today.

It's only *supposed to be* less stable than the previous stable release, Dapper (which also happens to be a LTS release), because Edgy was developed in four months instead of the usual six months. The two months taken from its development time went into Dapper, which was developed in eight months instead of six, to make it more stable, since it's a LTS release.
