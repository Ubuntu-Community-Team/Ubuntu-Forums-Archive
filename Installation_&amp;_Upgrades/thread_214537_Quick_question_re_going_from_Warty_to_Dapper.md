### Post by jrib on 2006-07-12
It's safer to jump releases one at a time to get to Dapper.  From what I have heard, skipping releases isn't supported (it may work for you, or it may go terribly wrong).  I would try to go release by release if it is possible for you, if you can't, then be sure to backup your data.

---

### Post by Jagot on 2006-07-12
If you try to go from Warty to Dapper your Ubuntu installation will break.  You need to upgrade through releases or do a clean install.
