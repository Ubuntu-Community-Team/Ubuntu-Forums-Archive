### Post by beegary on 2010-10-12
I have done this for the last two versions. The only thing to watch is that there will be a whole bunch of .* files and folders in your home (hidden). These are for configuration of all the software you use.
I normally copy of of them to a backup folder before I do the new install, as they may cause conflicts with the new version. 
You may want to save/backup your Firefox bookmarks etc, and any other software specific settings first.
