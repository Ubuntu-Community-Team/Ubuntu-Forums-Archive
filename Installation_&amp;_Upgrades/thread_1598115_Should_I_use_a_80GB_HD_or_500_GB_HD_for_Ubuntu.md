I have to say that 80 is plenty for any of the ubuntu distro's (ubuntu kubuntu edubuntu mythbuntu and so on). All of the ubuntu distro's use very little hard disk space off the start and just about every program in the software centre is small. So, yeah your good with the smaller drive. don't attempt that with windoze though, they like to hog all of your space and bog your system down.
