### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-02-16
Launching a Live CD from my computer is possible, but you'll only get a menu with a few programs to install for your Windows, and not Ubuntu installation. You have to boot from the CD, and to do that, enter the BIOS (by pressing Del or F10) at boot. In the BIOS, find the boot section, and make sure your PC boots from CD-Rom, then save and exit.
