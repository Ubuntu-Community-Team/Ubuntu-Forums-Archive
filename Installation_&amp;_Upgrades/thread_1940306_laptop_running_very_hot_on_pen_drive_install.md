### Post by 2F4U on 2012-03-13
What are your hardware specs and what version of Ubuntu are you running? This can be due to several reasons:
1. Graphics driver. For ATI/nVidia it is often the better solution to use the proprietary drivers since they have better power management.
2. Fans are blocked by something.
3. Dust collected inside the laptop case, so that the air flow is not as designed
4. Old distribution not optimally supporting new hardware.
