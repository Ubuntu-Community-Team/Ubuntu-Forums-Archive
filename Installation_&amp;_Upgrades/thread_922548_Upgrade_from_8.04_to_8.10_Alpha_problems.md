### Post by waspbr on 2008-09-17
To upgrade from Ubuntu 8.04, press Alt+F2 and type in "update-manager -d" without the quotes and accept the "New Distribution Update."


do that at ur own peril, I would much rather have a clean install. I personally have a partition set for hardy and another partition set for intrepid so in case something breaks in intrepid I will always have hardy to fall back on.  

remember that the OS is still alpha, on the very least back up ur important data.
