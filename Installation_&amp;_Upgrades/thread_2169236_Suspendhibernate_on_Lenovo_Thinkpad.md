### Post by AussieGuy on 2013-08-21
I'm using a newly installed Ubuntu 12.04 on my Thinkpad laptop. When I close the lid, however, all my open applications are killed, so when I open the lid I have a blank Unity screen, and have to open everything up again.  Also, the laptop doesn't power down as it should; even with the lid closed the laptop keeps on drawing power.  How do I change this strange behaviour to one for which the laptop goes into hibernation when I close the lid?

---

### Post by zacktu on 2013-08-21
On my Lenovo Thinkpad I have set

[INDENT]System Settings/Power

When the lid is closed: Suspend on battery power and Suspend when plugged in.[/INDENT]

This works for me.
