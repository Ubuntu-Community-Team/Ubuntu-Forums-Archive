### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-04-06
It all depends on whether you will use the new features that come with 13.04. Wait a few weeks, download the ISO image, burn it to DVD and try it out. There will be a new LTS release in 14.04. Since 12.04 we get 5 years support on the desktop (just like the server edition) instead of 3 years.

I always go for the newest Ubuntu but if you are happy with what you have and you can do what you want to do, then you do not have to upgrade just because a newer version of Ubuntu is released.
