I have done upgrade, where current kernel 2.6.22-12 was updated to 2.6.22-13. But, as usually, old kernel was not removed, due to safety and stability issues. So, restricted-manager for *-12 kernel was not removed and then new restricted-manager for *-13 was not installed. So, after rebooting I didn't have a new restricted nvidia driver.
