### Post by aysiu on 2007-05-21
It's about as safe as if it's ever been. There were two polls--one on people's success with upgrading to Feisty and another on people's success with upgrading to Dapper last year. Both had about a 68-70% success rate (no problems... or small problems easily fixed).

Your best bet is to [make a backup of your current installation](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/backup) before you do the upgrade.
