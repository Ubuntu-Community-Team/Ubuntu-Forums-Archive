### Post by efflandt on 2010-11-21
The order of icons in upper right depends upon when different things load simultaneously during boot, depending upon resolution, so that is nothing to be concerned about.  They may also shift if you change resolutions in X.

The only problem I had is that for some reason the one with my username would occasionally duplicate, half over itself and over the shutdown icon, requiring shutting down from a terminal.  But I never noticed exactly when it happened (maybe accidental mouse drag?) and it has not happened lately.
