### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-04-18
Ubuntu 10.04 has ATI drivers in its repository.

If you've got an older ATI card (anything that doesn't have the words "Radeon HD" in the name) then ATI does not support those cards anymore with official drivers. However Ubuntu ships with an open-source driver that may give you some degree of 3D acceleration.
