### Post by Partyboi2 on 2008-04-12
trying booting with vga=792 as a boot option. To do this when you boot your system and see grub loading... press ESC then highlight the kernel line and press "e" to edit then highlight the line starting with "Kernel" and press "e" again then at the end of the line add vga=792 then press "enter then "b" to boot.
