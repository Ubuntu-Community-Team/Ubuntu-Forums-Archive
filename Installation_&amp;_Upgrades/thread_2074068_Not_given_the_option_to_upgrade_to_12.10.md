### Post by RyanRCanada on 2012-10-20
Go to your GUI "Software Updater" and select "Settings". Under the "updates" tab, make sure that you have selected the option "For any new version" beside the parameter "Notify me of a new Ubuntu version".

I'm going to take a guess and say that at the moment, you've only got LTS selected. Best of luck!
