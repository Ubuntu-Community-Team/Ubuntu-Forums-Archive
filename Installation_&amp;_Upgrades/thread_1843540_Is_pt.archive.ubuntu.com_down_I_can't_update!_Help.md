### Post by TrueStory on 2011-09-13
I am a completely new user here.

Those packages are supposedly to be located at pt.archive.ubuntu.com, and I can't ping to it, nor open it from a web browser.

---

### Post by TrueStory on 2011-09-13
Okay, problem solved.

I need to go to Systems-> Administration-> click the button "Settings", and click the tab "Ubuntu Software", and change the "Download from" selection.

Then I could finish my update.

Some servers are down, but others are running.
