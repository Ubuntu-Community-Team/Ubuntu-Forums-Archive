### Post by merlinus on 2007-07-27
Standard version.  If you cannot get to the opening startup menu screen, then perhaps your cd has errors.

Make sure to do the checksums from the download site, and burn at no faster than 4x.

Also, make sure you burn the cd as a disk image and not as a data disk.

Even with an ATI video card, you should be able to get to the opening screen.

-merlin
