### Post by arpanaut on 2009-10-31
Not on an upgrade, legacy grub will remain, fresh install will result in Grub2

As Grub2 is the future, grub legacy is being depreciated, best to begin learning the new...

but as of now on upgrade legacy is retained and can be used, You will need to do specific steps to move to Grub2, so all should be well re-> grub upon upgrade.
