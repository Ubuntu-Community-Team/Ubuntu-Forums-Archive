### Post by Shazaam on 2009-08-23
Just a few suggestions...
1. Use Vista if you want to resize the Windows partition. Once done with that reboot to Vista and do a chkdsk and a defrag.
2. Pop the Ubuntu livecd in and reboot to it.
2a. Open the Partition Editor (System>Administration), choose the Ubuntu partition and click "Resize/Move". Adjust to your hearts content.
