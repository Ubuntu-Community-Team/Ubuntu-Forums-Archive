### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-04-25
You probably need to press a key on your keyboard to see the boot menu and be able to choose the USB.

Have a look in the laptop hardware manual to see if the key is mentioned, or try F2, F8, F11 or F12, which are most often used in my experience.

However, it could be almost any key, depending on the BIOS and motherboard so keep trying.
