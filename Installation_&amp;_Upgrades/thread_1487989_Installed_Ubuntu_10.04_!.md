### Post by SlidingHorn on 2010-05-19
If you already have Photoshop, you can try installing it in Wine.  According to their appDB, support isn't great, but it's been done: [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=17]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=17")

When it comes to image manipulation & editing, Gimp is probably your best bet when it comes to running something natively in Ubuntu.  Best of luck & welcome to the community!
