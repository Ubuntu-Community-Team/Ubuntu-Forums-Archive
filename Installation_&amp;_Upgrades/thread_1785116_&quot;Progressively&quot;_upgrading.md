### Post by snowpine on 2011-06-17
If you have a slow network connection, you can get an Ubuntu CD from your local user's group or from a Linux magazine at the newsstand. A fresh reinstall is my recommendation and will actually save you download time in the long run.

If you choose to upgrade instead of a fresh reinstall, then you can follow one of the recommended and supported methods described here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes)
