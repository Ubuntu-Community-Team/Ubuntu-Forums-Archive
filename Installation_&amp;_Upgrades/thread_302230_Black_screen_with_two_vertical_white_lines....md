### Post by Ledah on 2006-11-23
There is an AGP problem with the Asus K8V Mainboard. If he has another partition with windows, he can try to boot windows first and after that boot Ubuntu. 
The problem affects both graphic cards. Ati and nVidia.

Have a look at [this]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=302902&highlight=k8v").
