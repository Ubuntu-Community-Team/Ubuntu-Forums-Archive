### Post by dabl on 2007-10-03
The burn quality and the media quality of homemade Live CDs seems to be a source of issues for some CD ROM drives.  No way to tell from here whether that is your problem, but:

1. Burn the ISO image at 4X speed.

2. If that makes no difference, try a different brand of blank CD media.

HTH!  :)
