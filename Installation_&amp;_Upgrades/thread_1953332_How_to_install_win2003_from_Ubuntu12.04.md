### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-04-06
Please don't post your email address here -- you will get spam!

You will need the original installation disk for Windows 2003. Boot from the disk and install.

**Warning:** Older versions of Windows usually wipe out existing partitions, so you will most likely lose your Ubuntu installation. Back up your data and prepare an installation disk before trying.

After you have installed Windows 2003 (and, presumably, lost your Ubuntu installation), you can then reinstall Ubuntu alongside Windows and then restore your data.
