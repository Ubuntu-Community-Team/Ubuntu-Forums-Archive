### Post by mörgæs on 2011-04-05
There is no technical distinction between alpha, beta and the finished version. They are only snapshots on a continuous development. There is no major change from the last day of alpha to the first day of beta.

This means that if you just update your system as usual using Update Manager, you will get to the final version without any reinstalls.
