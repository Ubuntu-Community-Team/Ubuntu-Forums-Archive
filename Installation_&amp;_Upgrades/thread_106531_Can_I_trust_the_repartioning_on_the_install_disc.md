### Post by newuser111 on 2005-12-21
if you cant trust the ubuntu partitioner you could try and find a copy of partition magic, even an old version, that will let you resize your ntfs partition, then just let ubuntu create its partitions in the free space, because fdisk that comes with windows cant resize partitions without destroying data

another option is to use qparted from a live cd to resize your ntfs partition
