### Post by presence1960 on 2009-08-25
If you did an MD5SUM of the downloaded iso then you probably have a bad burn. Burn the iso as an image to CD-R at no more than 4x speed. if your burning software will not allow you choose 4x as burn speed see [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/9.04/switching/installing-burning.html").

Then boot the live CD and choose "Check disk for defects".
