### Post by Herman on 2006-11-18
Since you have Super Grub Disk already, the world's fastest and easiest way to restore your Ubuntu's Grub to MBR is right at your fingertips. Just boot Super Grub Disk, and go 'English Super Grub Disk'-->'Gnu/Linux'-->'Fix Boot of Gnu/Linux (Grub)'...and you're done! :D

it's that simple.
