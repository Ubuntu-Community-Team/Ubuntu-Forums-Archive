### Post by rsambuca on 2007-11-06
It depends entirely on what applications and tasks you will be doing.  If you are going to do anything that is cpu intensive such as video or audio transcoding (eg backing up your dvd's and cd's), or any other cpu stressing task, then you can notice significant gains in performace, upwards of 30% on the 64bit version.  If you are just surfing and emailing, then no, probably not much difference.
