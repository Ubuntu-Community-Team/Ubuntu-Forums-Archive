### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-05-16
I would recommend you investigate booting with either

i915.modeset=0 or
i915.modeset=1 in your grub

(when rebooting press shift to display grub, followed by e to edit.  Add the above before "quiet splash") and then boot as instructed.
