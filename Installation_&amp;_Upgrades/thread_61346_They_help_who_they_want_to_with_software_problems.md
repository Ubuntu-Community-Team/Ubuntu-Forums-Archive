-describing the nature of the problem clearly
-being gracious in your request with a friendly tone in your writing
-giving as much detail about the problem as possible along with a good description of your hardware setup
-giving your post a clear and applicable title so people know what you are asking about before they open the thread

It's not a guarantee, but those things tend to make helping someone easier.
