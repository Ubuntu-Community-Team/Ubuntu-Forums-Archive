### Post by TheBuzzSaw on 2009-04-24
Generally speaking, you should backup important documents when attempting *any* major upgrade.

With that said, you should not lose your data when you upgrade.

### Post by Peter09 on 2009-04-24
Think about it another way. The lifetime of a hard disk can be as little as two years, while above five is going well. Your hard drive could fail tomorrow - really - its worth backing up.
