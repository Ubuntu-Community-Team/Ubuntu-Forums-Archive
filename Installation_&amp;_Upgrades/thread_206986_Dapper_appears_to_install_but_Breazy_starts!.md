### Post by randell6564 on 2006-07-01
HI!

Dont know about the install issues as I downloaded the iso, but as for the "wireless" being "toast" I ran into a similar issue with my Linksys Wusb 11 V.3.1.

I just had to go to System>Administration>Networking and enter the info for my network eg...network name, WEP key, DHCP etc...

You should see 'Wireless Connection'. Highlite it and then click on 'Properties'.

Good Luck! :)
