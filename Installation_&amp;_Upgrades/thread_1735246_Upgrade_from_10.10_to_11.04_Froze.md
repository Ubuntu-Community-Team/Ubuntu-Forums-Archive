### Post by user1397 on 2011-04-21
When an big upgrade like that fails (probably one of the servers time-out but it could've been a number of things), the only practical solution is to do a fresh reinstall.  Backup all of your important files, and reinstall maverick.  I would wait till the final release of natty to try upgrading, although I would probably wait a week or 2 after that so that any major bugs would be squashed and the download might be smoother.
