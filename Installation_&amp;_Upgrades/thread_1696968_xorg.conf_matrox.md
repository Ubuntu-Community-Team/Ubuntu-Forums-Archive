### Post by munzli on 2011-03-28
are you using a matrox triplehead2go adapter with an nVidia graphicscard in your PC?

instead of a modeline try a simple "modes" in your screen section:
```

Section "Screen"
    Identifier     "Screen0"
    Device         "Device0"
    Monitor        "Monitor0"
    DefaultDepth    24
    Option         "TwinView" "0"
    # Option         "metamodes" "5040x1050"
    SubSection     "Display"
        Depth       24
        Modes      "5040x1050"
    EndSubSection
EndSection

```
