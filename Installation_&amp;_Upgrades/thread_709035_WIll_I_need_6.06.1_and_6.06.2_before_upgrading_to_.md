### Post by mikewhatever on 2008-02-27
The move from 6.06 to 6.06.1 is and update, but the version remains the same. The move from 6.06 to 6.10 or 7.04 is an upgrade, you can check the difference in package versions [HERE]("http://distrowatch.com/table.php?distribution=ubuntu"), middle of the page.  Once Hardy is out, there will probably be exact instructions on how to upgrade from Dapper.
