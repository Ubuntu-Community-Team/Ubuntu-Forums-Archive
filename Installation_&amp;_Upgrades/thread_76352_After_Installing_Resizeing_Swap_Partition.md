### Post by stuporglue on 2005-10-15
Your question wasn't very clear, but if you mean you want to convert your FAT partition into swap, then you'll need to select it in System-->Administration-->Disks and choose the "Partitions" tab of your disk, select the FAT partition, and click "Format". 

If you just want more swap with no partitioning here's a way to do it. Although this is a bit messy, it's not too hard. 

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SwapFaq](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SwapFaq)

Check item number (6) about how to add more swap without re-partitioning.
