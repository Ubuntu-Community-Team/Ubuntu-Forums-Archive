### Post by alanmike on 2010-05-09
Resolved. I wish someone would have taken a minute to help me with this.  

For those of you who have the same problem:

Click on Applications/Office and right click on the Evolution icon.  Select a suitable option from the dropdown menu, such as "add this launcher to panel".
