### Post by lemming465 on 2010-05-14
Not easily.  In general, the only way to downgrade to a previous release is to reinstall.  It's theoretically possible to remove the existing ubuntu packages without reformatting the partitions, but that can be very tricky unless your 3rd party apps were very careful about where they installed themselves.
