### Post by Alfille on 2008-10-15
Boy did I make a mistake.

8.04 was a dream. Tripple head mode (Xinerama with 2 nvidia cards, one running twinview). Three heads is very nice, there is pleanty of space, and a true center.

Upgraded to 8.10. First X configuration failed entirely with a PCI and PCIX card in the same machine. Lots of hand-configuration later, I head at least one head. Then two, but not Xinterama -- that crashes.

I can get three heads, but either 3 separately, or 1 + twinview. No trick gets Xinerama.

Ok, you'd thank would be ok, but gnome-panel can't deal with this configuration. It starts taking 100% CPU. I killed all but one instance (quite a nuisance without a menu available on one screen), but even that has problems.

Ok, try another window manager. Well the KDS4.1 supplied doesn't seem to understand more than one screen. XFCE is ok, but not great.

So.... After a full day of suffering, I wish I could revert.
