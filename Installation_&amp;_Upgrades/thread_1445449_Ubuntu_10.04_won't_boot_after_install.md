### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-02
Since, by your own admission, you're NOT an advanced user, why on earth would you be messing around with a Beta version of an OS??  Those are known to be incomplete, buggy, and unstable.

Furthermore, the Wubi version of 10.04 is still in beta testing mode, too.  So, there's a very good chance it will not work.

If you want to follow the Wubi 10.04 progress, go to the following forum: Development & Programming, Lucid Lynx Testing.

However, I would strongly suggest, that as a new Ubuntu user, you replace the Beta with a stable version ... 9.10.  You will have far less problems that way.
