### Post by yancek on 2015-08-04
Reboot and when you see the grub menu, highlight the Ubuntu menuentry if it is not, then hit the e key on your keyboard and then use the down arrow key to navigate to the line beginning with linux, use the right arrow key to get to the end of the line and enter nomodeset.  Hit the key (F10) indicating reboot at the bottom of the screen.
