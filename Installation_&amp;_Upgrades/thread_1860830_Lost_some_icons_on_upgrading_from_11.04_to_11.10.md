### Post by ubifree on 2011-10-15
I fixed it almost by accident. Go to System Settings > Appearance. At the bottom there is a dropdown for different themes - Ambiance, Radiance, etc. Choose a different one to the one you had and you'll see all your icons reappear!  You can even change back to the theme you just had.

All my icons have reappeared - Home Folder, Rubbish Bin, etc, in the Launcher bar, and all the icons in my System Settings dialog.  The Gear cog that was missing from the top right of the screen has also reappeared - phew!
