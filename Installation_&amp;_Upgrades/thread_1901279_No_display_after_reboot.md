### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-12-28
Sorry for your problems with the display. I wish I had the answer for that for you.

If you boot from a Live CD, you will have access to all of your data, even Windows. You can back up your data onto a flash drive, an external hard drive, or even a CD or DVD.

However, if your home directory was encrypted, it becomes more complicated. Let us know if that applies to you.

(In future, please do daily backups in case this type of thing happens.)
