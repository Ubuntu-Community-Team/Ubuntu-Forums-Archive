I don't think you can go from 6.06 to 7.10 without first upgrading your machine to 6.10 and then 7.04.  

Your old machine doesn't have a CD-ROM.  You don't need a burner to install it.  You just have to use your other machine to burn it and then install it on your old machine as long as it has a CD-ROM.
