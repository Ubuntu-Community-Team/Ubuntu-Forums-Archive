### Post by Partyboi2 on 2007-12-29
Also check that your source.list has the right things uncommented. Sometimes with fresh installs the source.list is  unable to be verified. Or just go to "Software Sources" (System>Admin>Software Sources) and make sure that under the "Ubuntu Software" and "update" tab the correct boxes are ticked.
