Hello and Welcome to Linux!  First off, you are using an old Operating system. The new Ubuntu is 6.06 LTS (Dapper Drake).  But thats not really a big deal at the moment!  

If you erased your Hard Drive then it's just a matter of making sure that you set your Bios to boot from CD first.  Then just stick your Ubuntu CD into your CD Drive and reboot.  After that you will just follow the installer through the installation!

I'm not quite sure what the heck you did when you say that **"i launched a hd install of Ubuntu 5.04.I than switched my bios to boot from C and to my surprise, i have no C at all".**

You do not want to boot from "C" (that is your HD, and you erased it, so of course nothing will happen.)  You want to boot into your installation CD. Thats why your Bios needs to be set to boot from CD.

After you get that worked out and get booted into Ubuntu, then just follow the instructions from there!

Good Luck, and have fun!
