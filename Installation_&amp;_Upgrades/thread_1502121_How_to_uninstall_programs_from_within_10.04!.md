### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-06-05
using software centre you'll notice a "installed software" section on the left hand side.  
Click on it. 
On the right hand side you'll notice all your install applications.
Click on the application you don't want.  
A remove button will high-light.  
Click on the remove button.

Alternatively - use synaptic manager to remove your applications.
