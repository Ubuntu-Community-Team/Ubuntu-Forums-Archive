### Post by ManamiVixen on 2013-03-26
The amd64+mac ISO is for post PowerPC Macs. The G5 is a PowerPC Mac. What you need is the PowerPC ISO. Ubuntu 12.04 was the last Ubuntu Version to support PowerPC on the desktop. You there arel Lubuntu PowerPC ISO's though.

To the Ubuntu 12.04 PPC:
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/precise/release/ubuntu-12.04-desktop-powerpc.iso](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/precise/release/ubuntu-12.04-desktop-powerpc.iso)

To the Lubuntu 12.10 PPC:
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/quantal/release/lubuntu-12.10-desktop-powerpc.iso](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/quantal/release/lubuntu-12.10-desktop-powerpc.iso)

To Lubuntu 13.04 PPC BETA:
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/raring/beta-1/lubuntu-13.04-beta1-desktop-powerpc.iso](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/raring/beta-1/lubuntu-13.04-beta1-desktop-powerpc.iso)
