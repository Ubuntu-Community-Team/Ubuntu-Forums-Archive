### Post by BrockStrongo on 2009-11-25
Just a heads up to anyone using Cairo Dock and Opera Web Browser.

After reverting to opera 10.0 cairo dock is acting properly again. I have the terminal output if anyone wants to have a look.

Ubuntu Karmic 64bit
