The failing computer has a N1996 XFX motherboard. I am using a DVDrw drive and have verified that the connections are snug and seated properly.

### Post by Mark_in_Hollywood on 2009-04-23
Unplug the power cord. If this is a laptop also remove the battery. Press the Power On switch and hold it 1-2 seconds. Reinstall the power. Press the Power On button.

### Post by max_power on 2009-04-23
Well, believe it or not, it was a RAM issue. I removed one of the 1GB stick and poof, no problems installing.
