### Post by logos34 on 2007-06-21

LOL  that is a looong time...You did get the ALTERNATE INSTALL CDs (NOT the live cds), did you not?  Otherwise I think I know what you'll be doing for the next to weeks...

You can only upgrade up one release at a time, so you'll have to essentially do TWO upgrades.  With the CD in the tray, you go:

> [B]
sudo apt-cdrom add[/B]

or 

**> [B]gksu "sh /cdrom/cdromupgrade"**[/B]

[B]
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/?action=fullsearch&context=180&value=upgrade&titlesearch=Titles](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/?action=fullsearch&context=180&value=upgrade&titlesearch=Titles)[/B]

If you could get your hands on a usb stick, go to the library or a cybercafe or somehting and use their fast internet to download and save the .iso to that, then take it home and burn it to disc.  Life is short.
