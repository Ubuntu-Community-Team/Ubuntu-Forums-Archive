### Post by martalli on 2008-04-22
Usually, everything is a little better with version upgrades.  You would generally be well served with a version upgrade as newer drivers will often support hardware not previously supported, newer software may have more features, and so on. 

However, if you have a troublesome piece of hardware like your wireless card, there are two things you could do.  Since you have already gotten to work, you must know some details (chisept and such) about the hardware.  A little googling may bring up other people's posts about the same hardware, and whether it works with hardy, or at least with 2.6.24 kernel (vs the 2.6.22 in gutsy).

However, the easier way to tell is to get a hardy desktop cd and boot from cd.  If your hardware works, then you should be golden.  Poke around and check if everything else is working, too.
