### Post by Bashing-om on 2014-08-19
abel3; Hi !

There is a means to release upgrade from EOL release,
In your case; a rather long and bumpy road:
12.10 -> 13.04 (EOL also) -> 13.10 (EOL too) ->14.04
See:[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades)
Old, but still apt.
Back up your data, and also give serious consideration to the faster-easier option to do a clean fresh install of release 14.04.
