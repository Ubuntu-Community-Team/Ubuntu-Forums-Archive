### Post by Seisen on 2007-09-25
You can't upgrade directly to Gutsy, you could back up your information and do a reinstall and install Gutsy. If your server is just for fun or something than go ahead but if its something you need it  to run in perfect condition I would wait.

### Post by yuri_rage on 2007-09-25
I've been enjoying Gutsy Tribe 5 for a month or two on my primary desktop machine and have had a couple of minor growing pains regarding some applications (mostly media apps like Gimp and streaming video/audio), but connectivity and general functionality have not been a problem.  I don't think you'll have any issues upgrading a server to Gutsy, but you will need to back up all your data and do a fresh install.
