### Post by xelu01 on 2008-01-20
I have just installed vmware server and constantly try to put in the serial number but it says

You do not have permission to enter a serial number. Please try again using the system administrator account.

How do i switch to my administrator account.

---

### Post by elliotjreed on 2008-01-20
Go into a terminal and type:

```
gksudo vmware
```

Enter you password (computer, or root, password) and there you go, enter your serial and it should work!
