### Post by craig10x on 2013-12-17
Did you enable workspaces?  You go to System Settings....then to the Appearance section...then to "behavior" tab....enable workspaces...It use to be on by default...now you have to add it on...
Other then that, there aren't any settings...if you have enabled it and are still having problems, perhaps someone else will come along with an idea...I have 13.10 installed and once i enabled it, it works fine...

### Post by hodad on 2013-12-17
Found it!

I went to adjust my screensaver under "System Settings", and it's under "Appearance" -> "Behavior" as a checkbox.
