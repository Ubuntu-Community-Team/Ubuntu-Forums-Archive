### Post by Dark Hornet on 2008-08-16
You should really read the wiki...but in my experience, the easiest and quickest way I got the game working was to just copy the entire WoW folder from a Windows install to my WINE folder.  From there I just had to make a couple of changes to my "Config.WTF" file, and I am good to go.  In fact, I get MUCH better frames per sec running WoW on my Ubuntu box than I ever did running it under Win XP!

Good luck!
