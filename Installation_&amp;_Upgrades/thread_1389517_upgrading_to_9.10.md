A strong caution...

Though nothing should happen to your files and folders in your Home Folder, please make sure you backup your Home Folder before trying any upgrade. (The simplest way, is to drag the contents to a stick.)

The first time you don't backup, is the time that the upgrade is going to bork, and you're  left holding a pile of ashes. It's always better to be safe than sorry.
