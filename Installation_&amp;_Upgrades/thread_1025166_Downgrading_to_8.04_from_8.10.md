### Post by cariboo on 2008-12-29
Unfortunately you will have do a clean install of 8.04, as there is no way to downgrade. I would suggest using the LiveCD to backup your important files and then do the installation, make sure your Ubuntu partitions are unmounted, then pick manual partitioning and use the partitions you previously used.
