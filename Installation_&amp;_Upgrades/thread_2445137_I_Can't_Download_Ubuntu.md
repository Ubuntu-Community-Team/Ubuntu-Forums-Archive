### Post by QIII on 2020-06-09
If you download "normally", you will be limited to the volume that the mirror can pipe out.  That will depend on their capability and the number of downloads going on concurrently.  This can't be changed from your end.

There is nothing inherently unsafe about the torrent protocol. You may be associating torrents with pirating software -- which isn't at all safe.  But that is not due to the torrent protocol.

If you trust the ultimate source of the file and the P2P network, it's as "safe" as using a mirror.  Mirrors can be compromised and mirror maintainers can have deliberate nefarious intent.
