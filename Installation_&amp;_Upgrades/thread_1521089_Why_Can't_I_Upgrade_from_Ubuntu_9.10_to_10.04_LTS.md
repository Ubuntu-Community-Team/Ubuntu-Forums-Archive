### Post by snowpine on 2010-06-30
A couple of options:

1. Back up your existing data to an external drive. Use your Ubuntu 10.04 Live CD to install the new release in place of (or next to, for a dual boot) your existing Ubuntu 9.10 install. Restore your documents from the backup.

2. Download the Ubuntu 10.04 Alternate CD. You can do an upgrade from the Alternate CD. 

3. Upgrade over the net (which you said you can't do because you have a slow connection)

More details:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/LucidUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/LucidUpgrades)
