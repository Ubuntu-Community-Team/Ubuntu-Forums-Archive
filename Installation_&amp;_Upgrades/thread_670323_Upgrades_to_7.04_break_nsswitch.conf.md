### Post by PmDematagoda on 2008-01-17
As it is, upgrading is not the most reliable method to use when you want to move from an older OS to a newer one since problems like the one you had are rather common and should be expected and be prepared for before undertaking the upgrade(this is pretty much the same for all OSes since it is very difficult to carry out an upgrade without facing a few problems). 

In fact, you would have had better success with a clean reinstall, especially in terms of time consumption and and the amount of problems received in reinstalls as opposed to an upgrade.
