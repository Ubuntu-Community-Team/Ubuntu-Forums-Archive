### Post by sirdilznik on 2007-11-23
64-bit compatibility to 32-bit programs is pretty much seamless these days so there is almost no drawback to the 64-bit install.  On the other hand you will probably not see much in the way of improvement over 32-bit unless you encode a lot of video/audio or do database type stuff.  I do 64-bit myself and I love it.
