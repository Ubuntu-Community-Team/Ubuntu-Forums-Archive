### Post by funkydan2 on 2005-12-04
Two things - firstly, when you look at the CD in a file manager (e.g. Windows Explorer) do you see lots of files & directories or just the 1 file.

Can your computer boot from a CD?  If it can, but it isn't booting from the CD at the moment, you tell your PC to look at the CDRom drive for a boot disk.  You may need to go into the BIOS's menu to do this, or more hopefully be able to choose "boot from CD" in a startup menu.
