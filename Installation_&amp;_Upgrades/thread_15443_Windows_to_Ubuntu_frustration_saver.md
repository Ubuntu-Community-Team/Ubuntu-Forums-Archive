### Post by Bite_me_Bill on 2005-02-14
Going to a new operating system can sometimes be frustrating.  How many of us know every part of our computer(s) in the case that something goes amok and we need to know what we have?  I thought I would share a little something that has saved me more than once on my transformation from the windows world to the linux galaxy.  A quick search and download of a program called **Aida32**.  Once you have the program running create a complete text report and save it somewhere.  You will have every bit of information about your system incase you need to know exactly what you have in your system.

Just with this transformation it has saved myself much grief and frustration.  I would just about consider this a must do before making the journey.  Little program is almost an indispensable little treasure.  


*Don't know how quick this will get lost into the depths of the forums but thought I had to share this!
