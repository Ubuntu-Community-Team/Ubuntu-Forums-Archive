### Post by Anxious Nut on 2009-05-08
you can do this for the mean time if you have keypad (using your keypad as your mouse):System-->Preferences-->Assistive Technologies-->Keyboard Accessibility (button)--> Mouse Keys (Tab)

and check the box,

 now you can use your keypad as your mouse till you get your problem solved, 

7 = upper left
8 = up
9 = upper right
4 = left
5 = [B]if "-" was pressed then right click
    if "/" was pressed then left click[/B]
6 = right
1 = lower left
2 = down
3 = lower right  

used this the previous week for 4-5 days, then bought a new one

ps: your key pad not your keyboard
