### Post by TWhyman on 2009-11-11
As reported here

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gdm-2.20/+bug/475281](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gdm-2.20/+bug/475281)

the gdm.conf installed by gdm-2.20 is broken, You will need to edit it and replace all references to "/usr/X11R6/bin/X" with "/usr/bin/X"
