### Post by wilee-nilee on 2010-05-25
If it really was 9.04 to 10.04 that was a impossible upgrade. So if you can shrink 9.04 and enlarge 10.04 then open the 9.04 partition from the home sidebar drag and drop what you need into 10.04. Then once you have everything moved delete 9.04 and expand 10.04.

Any partition resizing should be done with a live cd, and you will have to turn the swap off to do some of this resizing from the live cd.

If you can drag and drop before you re-size then just delete you will probably be better off. Also don't try to go to fast with the transference of stuff, or choose to move to much at one time.
