### Post by Ehtetur on 2007-11-23
I would suggest you boot up with a Gutsy x64 LiveCD to see what hardware works...
The liveCD doesn't install restricted drivers for ATI or nVidia video card, so your screen won't look too good.
You can configure your system to use a dual monitor setup.

There's also a nice sticky in the x86 64-bit Users forum that should help you to decide on going with x64 or x86..
