### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-09
1) If you mean upgrading from the alternate CD, no, I don't think there's any difference... except maybe that the CD won't have every single update likely released after it was compiled, you can always do a regular update to get the few extra ones.

2)Yes, to my knowledge IF the upgrade succeeds you should have an upgraded OS with all your programs and settings... Disable your 3d drivers for nvidia/ati if you have them installed as well as disabling beryl, these things often cause problems upgrading. May even wanna just outright uninstall them both if you have them.

3)I prefer clean installs, call me a bit picky. I don't think theres any advantage if your upgrade works the first time. I've read many posts of woeful tales of upgrades, though some did work. If you are sure to disable your 3d drivers and beryl/compiz you likely have a greater chance of not having a critical problem when upgrading. Do have the CD though should anything happen...
