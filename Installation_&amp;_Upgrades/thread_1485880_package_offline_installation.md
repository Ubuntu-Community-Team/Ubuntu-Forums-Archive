### Post by mac9416 on 2010-05-19
Hi, king_wwang, welcome to UbuntuForums!

[Keryx]("http://keryxproject.org") is a program that allows you to download packages for your offline computer at a friend's house or wifi hotspot, then take them back home and install them offline. Reading over the Tutorial will give you an idea how it works. [http://keryxproject.org/tutorial/](http://keryxproject.org/tutorial/)
