### Post by NormanFLinux on 2011-05-18
You need to install the Ubuntu classic netbook remix packages from the repositories to get the classic netbook remix and then logout and login to the classic netbook remix interface. I have the same MiniNote as you do.

However, I strongly recommend you download Oz Unity, which has the default classic netbook remix interface. Its based on Ubuntu 10.04 LTS and has nearly all the applications you could ever need. Its by the makers of Ubuntu Ultimate Edition.
