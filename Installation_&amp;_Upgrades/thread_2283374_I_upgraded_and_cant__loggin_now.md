### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-06-23
Do you really want to upgrade to 14.10?

It is about to lose support in a few weeks time, when you would need to upgrade again to 15.04, and then again in a few months to 15.10.
I would strongly recommend that you stay with 14.04 until April next year when the next LTS version will appear, ie 16.04.

The LTS versions are supported for 5 years; the intermediate versions for 9 months only.  Like may other users, I use only the LTS versions on my working machine and try out intermediates in VirtualBox.
