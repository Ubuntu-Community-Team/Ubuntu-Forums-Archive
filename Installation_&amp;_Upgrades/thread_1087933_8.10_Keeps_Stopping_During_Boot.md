### Post by martrn on 2009-03-05
Apparently the fix is to add "acpi=noirq" to your /boot/grub/menu.lst (specify the kernel), which (apparently) works OK.

The bug is at:  [https://bugs.launchpad.net/linux/+bug/272247](https://bugs.launchpad.net/linux/+bug/272247)
