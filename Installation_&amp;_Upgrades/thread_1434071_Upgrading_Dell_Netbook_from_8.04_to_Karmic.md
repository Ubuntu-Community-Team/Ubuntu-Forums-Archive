### Post by wojox on 2010-03-19
In order to get to 9.10 you need to upgrade to 8.10, 9.04, and 9.10. Probably best to download, burn and do a fresh install of 9.10. You could also wait about a month and upgrade directly to 10.04.

You could also Press Alt+F2 and enter:

```
update-manager -d
```
