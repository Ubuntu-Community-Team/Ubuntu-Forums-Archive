### Post by kidders on 2007-07-10
Hi there,

Technically, it doesn't really matter. It *might* turn out that, for some reason, installing one before the other could be marginally quicker, or somehow more straightforward ... but I wouldn't spend too much time thinking about the issue. :-)

In terms of bootloaders, you can use anything you want ... the choice is entirely yours. Another (aside from grub) common choice is lilo, but it's all down to personal preference/requirements.
