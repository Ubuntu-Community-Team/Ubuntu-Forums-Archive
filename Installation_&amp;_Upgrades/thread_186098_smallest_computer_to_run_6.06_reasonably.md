### Post by metoo on 2006-06-01
Well, your system is clearly outdated! :-)

I've read that the new graphical installer needs at least 256 MB of RAM. The text based would do.

My fallback box is a Duron 600 with 396MB RAM and an old Geforce2 64MB doing 1280x1024. The speed is totally OK. Takes just a little more time during boot but then internet browsing, openoffice etc. is just OK.

The problem is your old 4GB HD = slow and your 8MB Video card = what resolution??

Running ktorrent all night on my machine (using KDE) gave a memory footprint of about 78MB (with breezy, should be similar to that with dapper). But the torrent wasn't connecting much. :-)

So my bet is it should work but take your time. I guess you know what kind of system you have at hand.

Btw.: just updating from breezy to dapper:
Downloading 640MB = 45 minutes.
Processing all the packages = 2 hours.
This is on the Duron 600.

Have a good glass of wine at hand, if you're old enough for that. :-)
