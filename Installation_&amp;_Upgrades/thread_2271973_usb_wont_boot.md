### Post by HMN on 2015-04-03
When you power on the computer, you can press a certain key (e.g. esc) to prompt you with boot options. Then you select booting from the usb. On the other hand, you can enter the BIOS after powering on and choose usb as your first boot option.

### Post by HMN on 2015-04-03
Press the special key to bring the boot options. If you dont see your usb listed, hit ctrl/alt/del to reboot while the usb is still attached. Press the special key to bring the boot options again, and you should see the usb listed--probably in listed twice, one with efi support.
