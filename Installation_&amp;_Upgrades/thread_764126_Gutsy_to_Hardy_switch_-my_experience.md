### Post by knavarathna92 on 2008-04-23
So today I decided to install the Hardy RC, since I like to be on the cutting edge and didn't want to DL when the servers were crowded.  I upgraded and when I restarted I had severe lag, caused by the ATI drivers + xgl-server combo that I used to enable advanced desktop effects in gutsy.  

  After numerous tries to fix the problem, I decided to give up and try a fresh install.  Luckily I backed up my data.  Everything went well and there was no lag.  So far I'm loving hardy but now I absolutely despise the upgrade process (though the problems were probably caused by me in gutsy:lolflag:).  

  This was my first ubuntu upgrade as Gutsy was my first system and the whole process from a usable gutsy system to a usable hardy sytem took 2 hours.  A little more than expected :( but I am very happy with the way Hardy turned out.
