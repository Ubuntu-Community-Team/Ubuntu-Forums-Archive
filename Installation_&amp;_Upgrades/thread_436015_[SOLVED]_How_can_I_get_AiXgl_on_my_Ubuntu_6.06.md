### Post by Steven_B on 2007-05-07
AIGLX is just an Xserver that uses hardware 3D acceleration to draw your windows.  XGL is another one that is similar.  Edgy and Feisty have AIGLX installed by default, but ATI cards typically reqire XGL to work right.
The real eye candy comes from [Beryl]("http://www.beryl-project.org") or [Compiz]("http://compiz.org/").
As I understand it, Beryl only works for Ubuntu 6.10 and later, and I think the same goes for Compiz.  Ubuntu 7.04 has Compiz installed by default.  You can enable it with the "Desktop Effects" settings.
Hope this helps!
