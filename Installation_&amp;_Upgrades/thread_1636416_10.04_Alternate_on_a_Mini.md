### Post by kimiboy on 2010-12-10
You can use the alternate ISO and mount it to a drive and do rt?

Like this:

> sudo mkdir -p /media/cdrom
sudo mount -o loop ~/Desktop/ubuntu-10.10-alternate-i386.iso /media/cdrom
