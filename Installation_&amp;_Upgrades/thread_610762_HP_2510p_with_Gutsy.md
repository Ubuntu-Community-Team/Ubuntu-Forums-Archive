### Post by stevetxt on 2007-11-12
Here is a summary of what works and doesn't with my HP 2510p.  I installed a couple weeks ago with the stable 7.10 Gutsy and did pretty much a standard installation though with dual boot.  I like this model a lot.  It's light, it's strong, the keyboard and display are nice, it is energy efficient, quiet, and does not get hot.  Mostly it works great with Gutsy.  It is hard to find much information on it though so I would like it if others would share their experiences and any way they find to get additional things working.   

Here is my summary.  As you see, my only issues are with possible irregularities in suspend and some peripheral items such as the media card reader.  

Suspend works, most of the time, with either f3 or quit menu.  To
resume I have to push the power button and then hit f9 or f10 to
recover the display from sleep.  Hitting other buttons or touchpad
does not seem to resume the display from sleep or whatever it is in.
When i resume from suspend the login screen is actually there but is
so dim as to be almost blank.  If i enter my password anyway i recover
from suspend but it remains dim until i press a brightness function
button.

Every now and then though I can not get it out of suspend at all
except by restarting with alt-fn-sysrg-b, which is awkward.  And on
the other hand, every now and then resume from suspend works the way
it should, without having to press a display brightness key or
anything.

Suspend by closing the lid, an option in power management which I
tried, does not work.

Hibernation works, sort of, but gives all sorts of funny messages as
it goes down.
 
Wireless works right off.
 
f4 function key, which should enable connection to another monitor
or projector i have not tested yet.

f9 and f10 work to decrease and increase brightness. 

f11 works to turn on and off the ambient light sensor, and the sensor
works when it's on.

f8 gives battery information.

Hot buttons, or whatever they are called above the keys:

"i", the info button, does nothing.
 
Wireless on/off button works.
 
Display button untested, but does not seem to do anything.

Touchpad on/off button works.

Sound mute button works

Sound volume scroll buttons work

Multi-card reader is not recognized at all, putting an SD card in
it has no effect.

USB stuff works.

HP Bluetooth PC Card Mouse - I have not been able to get this to
work.

I have experienced an occasional problem while working on battery
power in which the display shuts off or goes to sleep while working on
battery.  May be related to dimming or ambient light sensor setting or
something.  It can be restored by pressing f9 or f10.  It is
possible too that I pressed some key combination by mistake since I
was working in dim light both times this happened.
