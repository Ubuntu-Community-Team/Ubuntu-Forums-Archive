### Post by Partyboi2 on 2008-05-31
If you think its the hard drive you can download diagnostic tools from the hard drive manufacture that can test the drive. Or you could try wiping the drive with a program like [[COLOR=Blue]killdisk[/COLOR]]("http://www.killdisk.com/") or [[COLOR=Blue]dban[/COLOR]]("http://dban.sourceforge.net/"). and try installing ubuntu If you think the cdrom is causing the problem you could look at installing ubuntu via alternate methods eg [[COLOR=Blue]usb[/COLOR]]("http://www.ubuntugeek.com/how-to-install-ubuntu-linux-from-usb-stick.html") or [[COLOR=Blue]unetbootin[/COLOR]]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/")
