### Post by eentonig on 2007-04-19
As long as you don't use an USB connection to your Broadband modem, it should work just fine.

Ubuntu can do PPPoE if you don't use a router.
But by far the most flexible and secure solution I would propose to anybody, is to use a router to connect to you SP however. You get the added security and internet sharing that comes with NAT (Network Address Translation).
