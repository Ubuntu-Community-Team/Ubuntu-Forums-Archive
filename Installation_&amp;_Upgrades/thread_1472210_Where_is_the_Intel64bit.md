Broadly, the "AMD" version is for both AMD and Intel 64-bit processors. I have read that on older machines, it can depend on whether the motherboard and BIOS support 64-bit, even with a 64-bit processor. On my Intel Core 2 Duo laptop, the 64-bit "AMD" edition works beautifully.
