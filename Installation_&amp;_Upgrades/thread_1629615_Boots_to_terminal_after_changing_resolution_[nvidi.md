### Post by Kneen on 2010-11-23
Kubuntu Version: 10.04 Lucid Lynx

I am a new Kubuntu user and Linux in general. I have installed Kubuntu and nvidia drivers.

The problem is, when I go to NVIDIA X Server Settings and change the resolution from 1024x768 to 1152x864, even though it applies the new resolution successfully, but when I copy the xorg.conf generated by nvidia into /etc/X11/xorg.conf and reboot, it doesnt boot into kde, it just shows the terminal 1, and when I go to the terminal Ctrl+Alt+F8 it is stuck at "Checking battery states [OK]".

So, I replaced xorg.conf with xorg.conf.failsafe and chaged driver to 'nvidia':

```

Section "Device"
    Identifier    "Configured Video Device"
    Driver        "nvidia"
EndSection

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier    "Configured Monitor"
EndSection

Section "Screen"
    Identifier    "Default Screen"
    Monitor        "Configured Monitor"
    Device        "Configured Video Device"
EndSection


```and it again boots into KDE, boot the resolution is 1024x768.
