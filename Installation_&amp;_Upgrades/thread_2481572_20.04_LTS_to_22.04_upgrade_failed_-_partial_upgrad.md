### Post by ian-weisser on 2022-12-03
During a release-upgrade is the worst possible time for apt to be interrupted.

Since you actually are on Jammy now (it's right there in your sources), try "sudo apt dist-upgrade"

Review the list of proposed removals carefully before agreeing. Do not agree if it looks like key system components will be removed.

Your system *might* be repairable. It might not. The next few steps will tell.
