### Post by matthew on 2005-08-20
I apologize that I can't answer your primary question, but I think you will find this helpful in your studies. Look in Synaptic for a package called Gnomesword. Install it and read the help files and so on. Then go to the page below and download all the modules you want.

[http://www.crosswire.org/sword/index.jsp](http://www.crosswire.org/sword/index.jsp)

When you are done you will have Bible software rivaling Bibleworks, Quickverse and the like in terms of quality and usability. The only thing you may miss is modern, copyrighted translations like the NIV. You can get Hebrew and Greek as well as tons of commentaries and study helps.

Anyway, this kind of relates because the greek and hebrew fonts work in this program so there must be a way to get them enabled system-wide as well.
