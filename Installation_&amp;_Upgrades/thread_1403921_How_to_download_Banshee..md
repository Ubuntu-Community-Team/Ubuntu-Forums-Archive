### Post by sgosnell on 2010-02-10
You don't right-click on the desktop.  In the panel at the top (or bottom, or either side, depending on how you configure it) there should be a menu that says Applications, Places, and System.  Click on System, then Administration, then Synaptic Package Manager.  On the right pane is a list of all the available packages.  You can scroll down to Banshee, or you can just highlight any package name and start typing banshee, and it will go to it directly.  Click on the icon to the left of the name, select Install, then click on the Apply button at the top of the window.  Done.
