### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-06-06
Unfortunately, "along side" is such a vague term that it tells us nothing specific about how your PC is configured.

If you installed Ubuntu using Wubi, your Windows filesystem is located under /host.

If you installed Ubuntu into an actual separate, Linux-formatted partition, your Window filesystem can be found in Places.
