### Post by Dennis N on 2015-06-18
They do have a description. You just have to look a little deeper under the surface. In Ubuntu Base, for example, click on the wedge on the left for the included packages in category 'Ubuntu Base'. Then you can go further with 'technical description' of a package describing changes. See the image attached!
