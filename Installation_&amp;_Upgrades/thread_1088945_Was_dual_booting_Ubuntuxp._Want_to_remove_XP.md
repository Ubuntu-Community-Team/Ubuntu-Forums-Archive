### Post by 67GTA on 2009-03-06
I would recommend gparted. Extra space is always good. You could expand Ubuntu to cover the space left by XP. You could also create another partition for storage or testing other distros. When I decided to get rid of Windows for good, I decided to create a separate /home partition in the free space. Now when I reinstall, my /home folder is never touched.
