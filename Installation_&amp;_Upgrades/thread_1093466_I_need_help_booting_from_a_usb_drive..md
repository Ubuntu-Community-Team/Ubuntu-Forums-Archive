### Post by ubudog on 2009-03-11
Sure.  Plug in the mp3 player to the computer.  When the computer starts it should say something in the bottom corner of the screen like press F10 for boot list or F12 or escape.  Whichever one it says press it and it should show a list of devices to boot off of.  Select your mp3 player - It should have Zen Mosaic in it - Press enter and you should be booting off of your mp3 player.  Sorry that your sisters haven't seen the light of linux yet.:(
Anyway good luck!
