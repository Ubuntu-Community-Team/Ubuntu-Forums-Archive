### Post by ahaurw01 on 2007-05-17
In the partitioning menu, you can click on 'edit partition' after selecting the partition you want to put ubuntu in and then on the drop down menu where it says something about how to mount it, choose " / " or "root," I forget exactly what it calls it. Probably " /". Good luck!
