I tried to install Ubuntu 7.10 on a computer that has a 3DLabs Oxygen VX1 video card, it got to Loading Linux Kernel 100%, it then froze there

I swapped the video card with a nVidia and all worked well. That video card is probably not supported at all by the kernel.

Others that have a old/rare video card and that are having similar issues my want to try to swap the video card with a more common one.
