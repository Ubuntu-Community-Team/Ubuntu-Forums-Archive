### Post by kebes on 2007-02-06
Well you could check a given board on the Ubuntu Hardware List, and see if there's any info:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport)

That list is far from exhaustive, however. Be aware that the Intel 965 chipset does not work with Ubuntu Edgy (this will be fixed in Feisty, but as of now there are many issues with this chipset). See here for more info, and lists of the motherboards affected:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Core_2_Duo_Support](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Core_2_Duo_Support)


To make a specific recommendation of course depends on how much you want to spend and what current hardware you have. (Are you planning on using existing RAM, hard drive, video card, etc.?) I will say that the Intel Core 2 Duo chips are really good performance per dollar. Just make sure you stay clear of that 965 chipset! The nVidia nForce 4xx and 5xx chipsets appear to work very reliably in Linux.

Hope that helps.
