### Post by TunaCanTommy on 2010-05-18
Hi nictu, welcome to Ubuntu.  You'll get plenty of help here on the forums.  Just read and take notes and don't jump in until you feel confident in what you're doing.  I have 10.04 UNE running on an ASUS EeePC 701. Not dual booting with Windows on it though.  I have a desktop I dual-boot with windows xp and 10.04.

On your netbook, good idea to keep WinXP installed for a while, in case you want to update drivers, BIOS or other things the Ubuntu doesn't yet support.  You'll like using linux, I am - especially the netbook version of 10.04. No problem on mine with web-cam and wireless, don't have blue-tooth or 3G on mine so I can't say.  Just search the forums for info about these two on the EeePC.

When you do decide to load Ubuntu be very careful and follow the instructions in the Ubuntu.com documentation and in these forums - lots of help and direction.  Not likely you'll break anything but be careful and finally make sure you understand GRUB and how it works in a dual-boot circumstance.

Have fun and stay in touch . . .
