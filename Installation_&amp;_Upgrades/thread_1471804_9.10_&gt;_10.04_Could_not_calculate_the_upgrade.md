### Post by Crio on 2010-05-04
I've got the same problem. In my case the reason were x.org packages installed either from development tree or somewhere else - I don't really remember when and why. Probably to get better support for my video card.
Solved it by following instructions in [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CleanUpgrade](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CleanUpgrade), "Reverting Nonstandard Versions" part.
(there is no need to actually remove *all* third party packages; the ones that cause problem are usually replacements of standard packages.)
