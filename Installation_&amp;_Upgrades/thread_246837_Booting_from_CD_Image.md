### Post by confused57 on 2006-08-29
Here's an excellent guide to burning an iso:
[http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso.html](http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/iso.html)

Basically, once you've downloaded the iso, select burn "as an image" in your cd writing program...you can't burn it as a data disk. Burn it at a low writing speed(8x or less).  After you've burned your iso to cd:
Go into your bios during bootup(should indicate on your screen what key to press to enter setup) and select your cd drive to boot before your hard drive.

On my Dell computer, I can press F12 during bootup and select which drive I want to boot to first, during that particular bootup.
