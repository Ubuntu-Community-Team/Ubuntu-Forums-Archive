### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2011-09-17
The one on the left looks like the System Monitor indicator. Did you try to  add the System Monitor indicator to the panel? Next time they appear try to right click one to see if you get the option About. If you get the About option then select About to determine what it is.

I'm thinking that you may have had an indicator in the panel when you had 10.10. When you upgraded to 11.04 there was a problem and the indicator wasn't handled properly.
