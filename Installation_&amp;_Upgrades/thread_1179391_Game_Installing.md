### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-06-06
You should check the CodeWeavers compatibility site first to see what rating your Windows app has.  If it's not listed, that means it hasn't been tested and is most likely NOT to work. If the rating is silver or below, it's not going to be worth installing Wine just to try and get the game working.

CodeWeavers site is listed below:

[http://www.codeweavers.com/compatibility/]("http://www.codeweavers.com/compatibility/")
