### Post by daengbo on 2007-12-13
1) Check the MD5SUM of the .iso image you used to burn the CDs.
2) Check that against the MD5SUM of the CDs themselves.
3) If that is all OK, try burning the CDs at a lower rate, say 2X or 4X.
