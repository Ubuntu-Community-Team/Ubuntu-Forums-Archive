When you are already able to use Ubuntu and want to back up your current IPL in your MBR (GRUB) so you can restore it after re-installing Windows, [*Click Here.*]("http://users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/p15.htm#How_to_back_up_and_restore_your_MBR")
