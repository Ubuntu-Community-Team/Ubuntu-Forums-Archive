I solved it-- I *completely* removed Texlive, Lyx and Kile, and then installed the full package of TL 2009, and then Lyx and Kile.  Now everything works, although I now have support for Korean, Czech and other useful languages (when I try to remove them individually, I am told that texlive-full will be removed, which is not what I want, since now, finally, everything is working).
