### Post by logos34 on 2008-03-17
You mean you got the deb from the ubuntu archives, or you dl'd the source tarball from mplayer.hq?  Synaptic will only recognize .debs, and you have to set settings>repos/ubutnu software>installable from cdrom/dvd.  

Why not just use the default player, Totem?  Maybe codecs is your problem (libdvdcss2, etc).

Have you read the [restricted formats]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs") page?
