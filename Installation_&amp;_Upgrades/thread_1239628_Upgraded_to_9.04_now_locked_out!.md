### Post by mikethehard on 2009-08-13
solved.

Ubuntu had reset to a German keyboard when it upgraded for some reason. (odd given, it was qwerty by default previously, and I only switched once as an experiment in 8.10 for a day months ago)

Now it has fully booted up its back to UK/US with the z and y the "normal" way round.

Odd and resolved by chance, but logged in now nonetheless. Thanks for your link.
