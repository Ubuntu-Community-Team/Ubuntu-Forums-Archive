### Post by lhtown on 2007-02-05
This is a pretty easy problem to fix. Your setup wasn't properly recognized. You just need to reconfigure x.org with the correct vertical and horizontal range for your monitor. You can look in the paperwork that came with your computer for that information or else on the manufacturers website.

When you find it, write it on a sticker on your machine or something so you don't forget it.

### Post by lhtown on 2007-02-06
Hmm... I am not too good at troubleshooting X.org problems.

How about going to System>Preferences>Screen Resolution and making sure that it says 1600x1200 and 60 Hz. I am guessing that the Hz is the problem.

You did reboot?

If it DOES agree with the specs you found for your screen, I would double check the screen specs with the manufacturers data. It is common that similar computers or computers in the same line and even with the same model numbers will be somewhat different although they should have gotten the screen right.
