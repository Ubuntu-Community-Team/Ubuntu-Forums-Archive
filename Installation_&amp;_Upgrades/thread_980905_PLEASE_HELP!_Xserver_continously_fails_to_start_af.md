Hi,

the end of your xorg.0.log looks like the one I had...

I solved the problem by disabling DRI (Direct Rendering Interface). To do that add the line :
Option "NoDRI"
to the "Device" section of you xorg.conf, then reboot or restart X.

(please not I had no "UseFBDev" option in mine ; you may try w/o it ?)


You may not get full speed afa graphics are concerned, but at least your system should be functional.

Hope it helps.
