### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-10-14
Use the **flash-aid** add-on for firefox written by one of the ubuntu forum members, lovinglinux.  It is a "must-have"!

Even if you prefer chrome or chromium, that add-on for firefox will give you a flash installation that works in the three browsers, and is particularly good at sorting out problems with flash on 64 bit systems
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/?src=search](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/?src=search)
