### Post by Hakunka-Matata on 2011-03-04
then go ahead and use the 'install along side other operating system', but after selecting that option, go straight to 'continue', ignore the 'use entire disc' and 'use entire partition' choices.  If you want to adjust partition sizes later, fine, don't attempt to do it here, now though.
