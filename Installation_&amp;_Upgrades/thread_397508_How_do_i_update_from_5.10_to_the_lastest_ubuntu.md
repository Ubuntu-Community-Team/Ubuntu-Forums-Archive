### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2007-03-30
> 
o yer and i dont want to burn a new copy...(i ran out of disc's), so how do i do it just from the ubuntu os?

Usually I'm a fan of upgrading but... Feisty (7.04) is coming in a month, and you are so far behind that you'd need to dist-upgrade three operating systems before you'd be current. Just burn the disk, it'll be easier.

There is another supported upgrade path, if you aren't interested in upgrading every six months: the Long Term Releases let you dist-upgrade every 1,5 years. If you want to get on the LTS train, dist-upgrade to Dapper . Then your next upgrade will be 7.10, but then you are covered for a while...

Instructions here:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes)
