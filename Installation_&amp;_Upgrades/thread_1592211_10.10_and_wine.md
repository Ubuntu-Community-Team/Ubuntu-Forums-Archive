### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-10-10
Go to ```
/home/papahonk/.wine/dosdevices/c:/World of Warcraft/Wow.exe
``` Right Click Wow.exe > Properties > Permissions > Mark as executable.

.wine is hidden and it'll be visible after you press Ctrl + H.
