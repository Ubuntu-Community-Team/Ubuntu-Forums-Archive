### Post by mister_pink on 2008-05-14
That'll almost certainly be a bad burn. Try the integrity check on the menu as it starts up.

If it fails, then you need to check if your download was bad or the burn just went bad. [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

If its the burn process, try again at the lowest speed it will let you.
