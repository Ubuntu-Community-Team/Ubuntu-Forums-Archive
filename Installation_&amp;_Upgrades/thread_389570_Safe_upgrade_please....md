### Post by aysiu on 2007-03-21
I think the answer is... no, no one can tell you unequivocally that the upgrade will have no problems whatsoever.

Your best bet is to [create a separate /home partition](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome) and do a clean reinstall.

You can also [do a full backup](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/backup) of your current installation that can be restored in case something goes wrong with the upgrade.
