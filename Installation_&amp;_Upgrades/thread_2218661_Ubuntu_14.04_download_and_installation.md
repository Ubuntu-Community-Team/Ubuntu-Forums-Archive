### Post by jdeca57 on 2014-04-21
The md5 (or other) checksum after a download may help. Personally I tend to skip that myself, but then again it is one reason why an installation fails and unless you downloaded 4+ times it is still the original that may be in fault. But OK, if the download was good there may be options you can give at boot: see [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions)
