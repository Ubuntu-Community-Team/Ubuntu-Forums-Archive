### Post by Minn3h on 2007-04-03
There's no reason you can't keep your ubuntu install and put Windows on your computer too.  Back up your really critical data, resize the Ubuntu partition, put windows in the free space, pop in an ubuntu CD to put grub back on the mbr, and away you go.
