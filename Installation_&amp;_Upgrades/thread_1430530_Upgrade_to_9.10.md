### Post by herbertportillo on 2010-03-15
If you have the .ISO image of Ubuntu 9.10, the next step is to burn the image onto a CD.

To burn the .iso image onto a CD, open Brasero (Applications -> Sound & Video -> Brasero) and click the "Burn Image" option. Click on "Path" and go to the folder where the .iso is saved. Then click "Open" to add the .iso to the path. For burning speed, choose the slowest possible speed. It might take longer, but it will ensure that the CD burns with no mistakes. Then click "Burn" and wait until the CD finishes. When it finishes, you should have a Ubuntu 9.10 install CD.
