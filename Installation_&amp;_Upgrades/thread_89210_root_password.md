### Post by 23meg on 2005-11-12
You don't need the root password. Append "sudo" to the "rm" command and you'll become root during the execution of the command, by entering your user password.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RootSudo](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RootSudo)
