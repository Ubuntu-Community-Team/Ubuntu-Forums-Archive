### Post by sXeChris on 2009-07-23
I'm sorry about your problem and I can't help you with it but I'll take the liberty of offering some advice; when I first got Ubuntu I also installed 9.04 64-bit. Although it works just fine, it's not the best one to get for a beginner and especially someone who just started using Ubuntu. I recommend getting 8.10LTS, it is the most stable and always get 32-bit unless you have 5g+ RAM/quad CPU because it only makes a difference in performance under those circumstances. 64-bit also is known to have compatibility issues with programs that are only meant for the 32-bit. I experienced these problems myself.

I would say to reinstall Ubuntu with 8.10LTS 32-bit.
