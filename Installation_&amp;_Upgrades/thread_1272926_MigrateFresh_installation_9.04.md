### Post by tgeer43 on 2009-09-22
It's really just a matter of finding the files that you want to retain and saving them to another location then copying them back to your new installation. If your music ends up being stored in a different location in your new installation, it's a simple matter to open up the playlist in any text editor and do a global search and replace with the new location. Deluge will start your partial torrents right back up if you copy them to the correct location in your new installation.
