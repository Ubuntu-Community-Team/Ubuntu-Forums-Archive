### Post by Paperclips4u on 2007-05-25
I'm not familiar with "Wubi," but it sounds like crucial parts of the hard drive that define the partitions and the type, etc, have been formatted over. You may HAVE to reformatt to make any use of your drive now.

You probably want to rescue data though. I don't know of any free programs that work well, but Get Data is a really great program that will recover most of your files for you. I've used it before and it works great. Worth the buy. Try to boot with another hard drive and then use Get Data on your dead one. 

Hope this helps.
