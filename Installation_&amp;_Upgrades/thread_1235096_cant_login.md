### Post by merlinus on 2009-08-10
Checksum your .iso, burn at no more than 4x, and check cd for errors at opening menu.  One of these is amiss, and that is why you are being asked for a password -- this should not happen until installing.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)
