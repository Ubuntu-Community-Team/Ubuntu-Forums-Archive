### Post by krpnm on 2006-07-09
The live CD was a no joy, period.

Downloaded, md5summed the "alternate" .iso file.

Burned it to Cd.

Xubuntu installed.

However upon GDM loading I had no mouse.  Good keyboard and video display.
The mouse was a Micro innovation 3 button scroll wheel optical mouse (PS2 style).

Driver was imps/2.

Solution:  I got another mouse, same style different brand and same driver.  Plugged it in and now it works.

I would not recommend a Micro innovation mouse to anyone for any reason.
