### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-30
Basically, unless you're a seasoned, expert Ubuntu user, you should NOT be messing around with a Beta version of the OS.  These are released for testing purposes (to get feedback), are known to be inherently unstable, prone to bugs, and can often crash your system without warning.

You should go to the proper forum with beta-related questions: Development & Programming, Lucid Lynx Testing.
