### Post by Dave_L on 2014-01-22
13.04 is not LTS. It reaches end of life this month.

The current LTS is 12.04, supported until April 2017.

The next LTS will be 14.04, scheduled for release in April.

Reference: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases)
