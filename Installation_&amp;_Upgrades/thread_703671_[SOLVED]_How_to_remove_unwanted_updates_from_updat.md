### Post by Shazaam on 2008-02-21
Easy. Open Synaptic (System>Administration>Synaptic), find the offending file(s) and highlight them. Go to Package (at the top of Synaptic) and check "Lock Version" for each file. Done.
If you want to re-enable auto updates for the file(s) just uncheck "Lock Version" and then hit Reload.
