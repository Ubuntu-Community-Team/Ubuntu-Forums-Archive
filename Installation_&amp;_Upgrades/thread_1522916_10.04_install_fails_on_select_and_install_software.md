### Post by RJARRRPCGP on 2010-07-02
The majority of the time, it's because of a corrupted burn. Sorry. 

I found the best way of avoiding a corrupted burn, is to use Puppy 5.0.1 and use PBurn, because of its MD5 comparer. It will actually compare the MD5 of the burn to the MD5 of the ISO.
