### Post by rsambuca on 2007-03-10
Both the liveCD and the alternate CD have partitioners.  During the installation process, you simply have the partitioner allocate some hard drive space to ubuntu.  After it is installed, the Grub bootloader program is installed and will give you a choice of which OS you want to boot from when you start your machine.

What is your existing OS, and do you have just one hard drive?
