### Post by yancek on 2019-10-18
Take a look at the Ubuntu site below which gives information on doing various upgrades.  You need to upgrade from one LTS release to another (14.04 to 16.04 then 18.04).  

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes?_ga=2.72078234.889505526.1571345935-1455427143.1529748621](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UpgradeNotes?_ga=2.72078234.889505526.1571345935-1455427143.1529748621)

You might read the pages at the link below on upgrading from EOL systems such as 14.04 which has not been supported since April of this year.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades)

Upgrades generally take longer than a new install and you need to have a good/reliable internet connection.  You may be better off with a fresh install after backing up your personal data.  Good luck.
