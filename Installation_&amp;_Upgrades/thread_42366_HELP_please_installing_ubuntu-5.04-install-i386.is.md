### Post by maryjo on 2005-07-04
Using Roxio, click on the classic icon, then when the typical selection screen appears, instead of clicking on the file you want, click on "file" on the menu bar, then in the drop down menu you will see - midway down - "Record disc from image..."  - Click on it and you will get a pop up window that lets you find and choose the ISO file.  At the next pop up screen you should choose a slower burn speed (I used 8x) and then let her rip.
