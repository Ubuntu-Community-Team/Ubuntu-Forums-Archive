### Post by Zelut on 2005-10-11
Well if you've just got one drive most people just use the entire drive for your / and /swap.  Making a seperate partition for /home is a good idea but from what I hear thats generally if you're using two drives.

If you'd like though I would say it was a good idea to make two more divisions.  One for / and one for /home.  This way you can fresh-install your entire system and not lose any of your information in your /home partition.  The trick here though is deciding which deserves more space.. the OS or your /home :)
