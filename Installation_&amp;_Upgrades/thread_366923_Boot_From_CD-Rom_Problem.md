### Post by Mattjai on 2007-02-22
I got it to work !

For some of you that may come across the same problem I will explain what I did and did wrong.

Downloaded Ubuntu's ISO image to HD
Used MagicISO to extract the files to a folder.
Burned those files to CD and then tried to boot from that disk.

And thats why I was here asking help.

Solution

Did a little more research this morning and.........

Downloaded ISO Recorder and used it to burn the ISO image to CD (without extracting the files) and success.

Must of interpeted the instructions incorrectly regarding the "burn to disk part"

Mattjai
