### Post by deputydave on 2007-10-26
Never mind!

I decided to purchase a new USB adapter card,  figured that it was not worth wasting any more time over a $20 adapter card.  Went by CompUSA and bought a generic card with 5 USB 2.0 connections.  I installed the card and plugged in my printers and they were discovered and installed in CUPS, now everything is working great.
