### Post by drs305 on 2009-04-08
On a clean install when you get to the partitioning section (part 4) you will designate your existing /home but DO NOT format it. There is a tick box I think, but in any case you have a choice of formatting it or not - so just be sure formatting isn't selected. Then it will be preserved.
