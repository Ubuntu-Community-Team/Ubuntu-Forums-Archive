### Post by oldfred on 2017-05-21
Is this an old machine? Most are 64 bit now.
But if older  & less than 2GB of RAM then 32 bit and Lubuntu are the better choices.

Are you dual booting with another operating system or Windows or erasing entire hard drive and installing Lubuntu?
Have you done good back ups of all your data first?

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2/Installing](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2/Installing)
 [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DiskSpace](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DiskSpace) 

If originally Windows, it may have used all 4 primary partitions allowed with MBR(msdos) partitioning.
