### Post by The other One on 2010-07-26
Hi guys,

I came across your post whilst looking for this same solution. I ended up finding it at [here]("http://www.supportforums.net/showthread.php?tid=2831").

1. Go to System>Preferences>Main Menu
2. Select the "Preferences" menu in the right hand panel
3. Click "New Item" on the left hand panel
4. Call it what you want, but the command is "brightside-properties"
5. Save, and you're done. It'll now come up in your System>Preferences menu:p
