I use Grsync to backup my data on Ubuntu. You can find that application in Synaptic Package Manager. By updating with upgrade manager it is impossible to lose your Windows data (if you are dual booting, I don't know about Wubi though.). If the your worst nightmare occurs (something bad happens to ubuntu during the install) just copy the backed up files back onto your hard drive from the directory you saved them to, via your Live CD.
