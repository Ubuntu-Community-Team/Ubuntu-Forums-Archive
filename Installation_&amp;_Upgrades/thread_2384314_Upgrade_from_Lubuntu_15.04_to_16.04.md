### Post by Impavidus on 2018-02-05
15.04 reached end-of-life 2 years ago and its successor is dead too, so, yes, get to either 16.04 or 17.10. You can try this link: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades)

I can't guarantee it will work or even that it's likely to work. Be prepared to do a fresh install. In fact, I recommend you try a fresh install right away.
