The absolute easiest way is to do a google search on "super grub".  Download the .iso and burn the CD.  Boot off of it and just follow the instructions, which are a little weird because it's written in European English.  It'll fix your GRUB loader and put you back up. The other technique involves going into the grub tool and doing a bunch of command line stuff that I always have to look up. Too much like work. :)

Good luck.
