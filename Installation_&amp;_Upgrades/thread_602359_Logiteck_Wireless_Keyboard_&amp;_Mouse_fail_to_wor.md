### Post by jamesjpa on 2007-11-04
On the bluetooth preferences, click the services tab. Then make sure input service is enabled. Click input service. Then click Add and it will then search for your devices. You might need to press the connect button under your keyboard or mouse just before you press search.
I've just had the same problem with my bluetooth mouse and doing the above made it work.
