### Post by eks on 2011-05-01
The hidden files in your /home are generally configuration files. Like .gnome, or .config. If you want to preserve your data but start completely clean with a fresh install, just delete what you don't want.

You can save your data plus .mozilla, .opera or .config/google-chrome if you want to save your profile and bookmark from the browsers for example.
