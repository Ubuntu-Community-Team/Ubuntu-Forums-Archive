### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2012-08-10
I'm not sure why you are having so much trouble upgrading from one version to another.  This is not a WUBI install is it?
I would download the ISO of the version you are trying to upgrade to and do a fresh install.  Be sure and back up all of your data before you do because it will over-write everything on the disk.  Also check the Md5Sum of the download and burn it to a CD at the lowest possible speed.  Try the CD before you install to ensure that everything works on your computer.  Have fun.
