Your situation could be caused by any or all of the following.

1 a corrupt iso. Which would also mean

2 a bad usb Live creation. Number 2 can happen even with a good download.

3 your usb stick could be developing problems.

Ignoring the above for a moment, the transfer speeds from usb are faster than those from a cd and a lot slower than an internal hard drive. 

If the usb was working there would have been a flashing light on the stick as it writes to RAM for test-mode or to disk for an install which would have a corresponding light on the pc/laptop.

If there was no lights refer to the suggestions above.
