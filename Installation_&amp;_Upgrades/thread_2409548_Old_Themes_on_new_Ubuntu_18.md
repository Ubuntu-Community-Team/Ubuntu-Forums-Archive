### Post by CatKiller on 2019-01-03
Emerald doesn't exist any more.

Compiz forked into compiz and beryl. Emerald was the window decorator for beryl. Then compiz and beryl settled their differences and remerged as compiz fusion, and then simply compiz. Compiz was only maintained because Canonical needed it for Unity, so they paid the developers. Unity has now been discontinued.
