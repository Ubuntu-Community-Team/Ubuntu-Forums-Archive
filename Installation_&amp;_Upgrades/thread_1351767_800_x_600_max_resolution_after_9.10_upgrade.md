### Post by lemming465 on 2009-12-12
You probably have to either reinstall 9.04 (there is no easy downgrade) or wait for 10.04 next year.  Lots of folks are having video troubles with 9.10; the kernel driver and Xorg situation is in a state of flux, and won't settle down for several years yet.  Personally, I like to try live CD's before doing upgrades; if the live CD gets the video, sound, ethernet, wireless etc. right the odds are excellent the upgraded system will too.  One can't take video for granted these days, alas.
