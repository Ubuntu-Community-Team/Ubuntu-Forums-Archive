### Post by spcwingo on 2009-07-23
I've had a problem similar to this before.  You can try backing up your bookmarks (~/.mozilla/firefox/lgip3q7y.default/bookmarkbackups/) then deleting your ~/.mozilla folder.  The next time you open Firefox it will regenerate this folder.  Then just move the backed up bookmarks to the proper location and reinstall your addons.  Although this worked in my particular situation, as always, your mileage may vary.  :)
