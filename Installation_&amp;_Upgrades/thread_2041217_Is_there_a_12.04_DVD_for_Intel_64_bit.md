### Post by sanderj on 2012-08-12
> **sofasurfer said:**
> why do they have AMD and Intel?

AMD invented and introduced the x86-64bit extensions, and probably called it AMD64. Linux was made compatible with it. AMD64 became a success. And only then Intel also introduced the 64bit extensions.

Linux still uses the AMD64 name. It is confusing indeed. It can also be seen as a 'reward' towards AMD for inventing and introducing it.
