### Post by unixeducation on 2009-02-15
I agree with spcwingo here; a virtualized operating system is going to be very slow on your system. A full dual boot would indeed be the best option; if you elect to resize your existing XP partition, please be advised that it may take a *long* time to complete.

In all honesty, throwing an extra 256 MB of RAM in your machine would also make a huge difference in performance. Good luck!
