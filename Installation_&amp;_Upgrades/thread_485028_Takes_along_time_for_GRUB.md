### Post by akane on 2007-06-27
It's most likely taking a while to get to the GRUB menu due to the BIOS searching for the floppy.  When you say you disabled it in the BIOS, how did you do so?  You might want to go back into the BIOS and look at the boot order, and place the floppy drive last, or take the floppy out completely.
