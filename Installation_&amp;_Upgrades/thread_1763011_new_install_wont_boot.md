### Post by linuxinstalledfromhdd on 2011-05-20
If your system is old you should do a system check with the live disc to see if everything is working ok.  Check your hard drive first with Disk Check.  See if it passes. Keep checking the rest of your hardware.  If your live disc works, but not your hard drive after installation, that could mean it is time to replace out your hdd.
