### Post by earthpigg on 2009-07-06
ubuntu will mail you an install cd of 9.04, for free, if that is what you are asking:

[https://shipit.ubuntu.com/](https://shipit.ubuntu.com/)

you can also download it and burn an iso yourself.

[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)

download it to your desktop or somewhere convienent, put a blank cd-r in, right click on the .iso file you just downloaded, and hit 'burn'
