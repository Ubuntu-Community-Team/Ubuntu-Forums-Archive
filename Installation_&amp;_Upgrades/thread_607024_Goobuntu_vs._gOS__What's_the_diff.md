### Post by smartalecks on 2007-11-08
goobuntu is used solely by google and it's employees (private, not distributed), and is based on ubuntu
gOS is distributed publicly (commonly sold on PCs by walmart), and is not based on ubuntu.

Goobuntu may or may not be made to integrate with google online services- it isnt known, because it is private.
gOS is made to work with google online services (gmail, docs, etc).
