### Post by cgarre on 2008-05-19
When the installation comes to where to install, at that time do "Select Manual Configuration". 
And then allocate mount point as "/" to the 40 GB space. also a swap space. 

If your partitions show only 1 partition of 40GB, and no other space (I mean if your hard disk size is 40GB) then possibly you lost vista !! 

Reboot and see if there is Vista and be careful the next time.

Check this step by step guide - [http://news.softpedia.com/news/Installing-Ubuntu-8-04-LTS-84314.shtml](http://news.softpedia.com/news/Installing-Ubuntu-8-04-LTS-84314.shtml)
