### Post by aysiu on 2007-04-19
If you have a separate /home partition, you can keep all your settings when you reinstall Feisty Fawn.

If you decide to upgrade, theoretically all your settings would be kept... *but* you'd have to upgrade from Dapper to Edgy and then from Edgy to Feisty. This could take many hours (five or more) even on a broadband connection.

I'd highly advise either sticking with Dapper or [creating a separate /home partition](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome) and then reinstalling Feisty (remember not to format the /home partition!).

As for it being "worth it," it's up to you. Feisty has newer versions of programs and a few new features.

For me, it was worth upgrading even before Feisty was released as beta. My laptop's resume from suspend was broken in Dapper and Edgy but worked perfectly in Feisty.
