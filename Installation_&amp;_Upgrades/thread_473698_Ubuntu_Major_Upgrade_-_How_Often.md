### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-06-14
There is some information on the release types, here [http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatIsubuntu/releases](http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatIsubuntu/releases)

LTS releases are supported for generally 3 years (Desktop) and 5 years (Servers), such as security and bug releases.  Non-LTS releases (Such as 6.10 or 7.04) have a general support time for 12 months, I believe.
