### Post by Dusenberg on 2013-05-08
You might want to try installing the deb package using apt or synaptic rather than compiling from source. That way all the dependencies will be included. See  [http://packages.debian.org/squeeze/twinkle](http://packages.debian.org/squeeze/twinkle) The download list is at bottom of page, and you get an instructions page when you click on the package for your setup.

You could also try sflphone as an alternative to twinkle.
