### Post by Pumalite on 2008-04-22
I answered your other thread, but if you are willing to reinstall, I'll give you my two cents. Use the 1st HDD to install your OS. Use the 2nd one for storage. A good scheme for your 1st HDD is:
10 GB for '/'
1 GB for /swap
The rest for /home
Install Ubuntu, go Manual and choose the already prepared partitions.
