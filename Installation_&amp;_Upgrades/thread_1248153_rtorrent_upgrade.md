I would guess not.  I have, to my knowledge never updated rtorrent.  I usually just run all the updates and not really pay attention to what they are, so maybe I have updated it and not known it.

Even if you did lose your session directory, the worst thing that could happen is that you would lose your stats on your upload/download ratio.

Do you use the option to monitor a directory and automatically load the torrents from that directory?  If you di, then it will just pick up all of those and start downloading or seeding them again.  I don't know if it would do a rehash first.

If you don't use that option, and you simply loaded each torrent from the user interface, you may have to go find all the torrent files for the torrents you're currently participating in, and have them ready in case you lose your session.

But, again, I would think you would not lose your session.
