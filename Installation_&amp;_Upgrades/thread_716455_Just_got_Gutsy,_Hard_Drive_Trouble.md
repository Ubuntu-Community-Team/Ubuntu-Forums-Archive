### Post by Shazaam on 2008-03-06
Sometimes on a multi-drive system, when one drive is flakey it MAY cause trouble with the other drive(s). Unplug the faulty drive (both power and data cable), set the other drive's jumper to MASTER and try the livecd again.
Since you are in there it would be a good time to give the pc a good cleaning and you also might think about re-seating all of the cards and cables.
