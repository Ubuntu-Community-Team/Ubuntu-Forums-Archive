### Post by aysiu on 2006-07-01
Synaptic is the more advanced version of Add/Remove Programs, and both are just graphical frontends for the *dpkg*/*apt-get* command-line utilities.

Add/Remove programs seems to feature mainly graphical applications. Synaptic will list everything available to you.
