### Post by 2F4U on 2011-07-27
Just to be clear, can you login to the desktop or not? The fact that things may not look the way you are used to may be due to graphics drivers. If this is Ubuntu, then Unity places a higher demand on graphics drivers than previous versions. I don't know your graphics card, but if it is ATI or nVidia, you could try to use the proprietary graphics drivers.
If this option is not available to you, then maybe another flavour of Ubuntu, for example Xubuntu, may be better suited.
