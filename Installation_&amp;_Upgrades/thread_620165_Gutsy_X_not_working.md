### Post by ahickey on 2007-11-22
I have read about other people having problems with Blank Screens after installing Gutsy (7.10) and I had the same problem.

I installed Gutsy from the Alternative CD selecting the appropriate resolution for my LCD monitor (1280x1024, 1024,768, 800x600 and 640x460)
Once the install was completed I just got an 'Out of Range Error' from my LCD monitor.

I then pressed [CTRL]-[ALT]-[Num-+] once the hard drive stopped reading to change video resolution and it came up at 1024x768.

I logged in and it promoted me to install the NVIDIA restricted drivers from the install CD.  I did this and then rebooted.

After the reboot, my system came up in 1280x1024 with no problems.

So, if you are getting a blank screen after installing Gutsy, maybe the above info will help.
