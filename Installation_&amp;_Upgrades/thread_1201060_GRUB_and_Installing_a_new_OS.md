### Post by oldos2er on 2009-06-30
Every version of Win* to date has overwritten the MBR with its own bootloader; I see no reason why Win 7 would be any different. There are numerous tutorials on how to restore Grub after this happens, one is [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows)
