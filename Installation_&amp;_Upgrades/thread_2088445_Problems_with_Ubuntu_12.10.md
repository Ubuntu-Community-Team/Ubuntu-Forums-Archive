### Post by arpanaut on 2012-11-26
Have you done all the updates and upgrades offered from the update manager?
In the popup window have you looked at the "show details" results, that may give an indication as to what the issue is.  If there seems to be problems using your system you may want to hit "continue" and go through the bug reporting process, there may be solutions from the bug reports.
Sometimes these popups are in error and can be ignored but if you have issues using your system, please follow through on the bug reporting.

See: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Apport](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Apport)
