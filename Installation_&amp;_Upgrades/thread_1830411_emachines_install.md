### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-22
It's generally a BAD idea to force an install on an untried machine.  You just don't know what is and is not going to work.

Instead, boot from the CD, select the Try Ubuntu mode, wait (maybe a LONG time!), and when you FINALLY get to a working desktop try it out -- to see what works and what does not.

Anything that does not work is goind to range from trivial to impossible to fix.  But, at least you can then come back here, search for solutions and/or post threads with the problems you've found.
