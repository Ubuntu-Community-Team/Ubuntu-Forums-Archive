### Post by taurus on 2005-11-17
Boot ubuntu and at the prompt, just hit enter to install it.  When you get to partition, pick expert and tell it (using the up-down arrow keys) to use the partition that Mandy is currrently on.  Don't forget to tell it to format the partition as well.  For partition where XP is, you don't have to do anything with it.  After that, you just have to sit back and wait for it to finish (probably less than half an hour, depending on the speed of your CD and how fast is your computer).  After the reboot, it will go into phrase two where it will finish installing it...  It should be real easy.  The only thing you have to watch out is to make sure you pick the right partition for ubuntu to install on.
