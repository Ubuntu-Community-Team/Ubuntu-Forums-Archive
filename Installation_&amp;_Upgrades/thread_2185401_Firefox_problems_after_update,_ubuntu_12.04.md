### Post by heir4c on 2013-11-02
When you open FireFox, there is not an icon in the Launcher? If there is one you can right click on it to pin it to the Launcher.
Make also a Backup of the .mozilla folder. Open your home folder and click Ctrl+H and than you see the hidden folders. Now you can backup the .mozilla folder. So if you loose some day your bookmarks and settings you can get them back with move the backup folder to your home folder. Even if you do a new install you can use this folder and your bookmarks and add-ons are directly in action.
