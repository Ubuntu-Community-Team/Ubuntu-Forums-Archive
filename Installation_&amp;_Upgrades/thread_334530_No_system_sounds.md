### Post by Johan! on 2007-01-09
Double click on the volume applet, and make sure that nothing is muted.

If that is OK, and you still don't hear system sounds, go to the sound preferences (system>preferences>sound).
There you can check the settings and test the sound.

### Post by Johan! on 2007-01-09
I also had this problem.
I found out that the system sounds and the normal sounds use different devices on my system.


Open the volume control applet.
Click file>change device.
Do you see 2 devices?
Try them both, and make sure nothing is muted.
