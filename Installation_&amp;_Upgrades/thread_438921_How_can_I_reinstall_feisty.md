### Post by starcraft.man on 2007-05-10
Easiest way to save your data, is to use the partitioner in the live CD and make a /home partition, then copy your existing home folder onto it. Then make sure its mounted. As for XP, as long as you don't delete it and you leave it installed when installing Ubuntu, it will be detected by GRUB and let you boot it. That should be it :)

Or you could always back up your data with DVDs, I don't know how much info it is though.... that could be long >.>
