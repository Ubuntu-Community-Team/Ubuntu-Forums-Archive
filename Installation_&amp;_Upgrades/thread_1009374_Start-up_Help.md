### Post by aysiu on 2008-12-12
Freeze-ups generally occur because of one or more of the following reasons: [list][*]Not enough RAM (for a live CD, I'd recommend at the bare minimum 256 MB of RAM, but more like 512 MB or 1 GB for better performance)[*]The CD image got corrupted during download or during the burn process[*]Hardware failure of some kind[*]A graphics card Ubuntu didn't detect correctly[/list]
