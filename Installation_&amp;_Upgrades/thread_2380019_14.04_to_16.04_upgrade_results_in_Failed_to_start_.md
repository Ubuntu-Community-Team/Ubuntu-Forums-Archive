### Post by shiyali2 on 2017-12-14
Audio problem solved


With the help ofthis pagehttps://help.ubuntu.com/community/SoundTroubleshootingProcedure Irealized that the online upgrade from 14.04 to 16.04 failed toupgrade the kernel. Therefore no audio. After running [COLOR=#0000ff]sudo aptinstall linux-generic[/COLOR], the kernel updated from 3.xx to 4.xx and theaudio was back. Thank you to the community for all the helpfulinformation.
