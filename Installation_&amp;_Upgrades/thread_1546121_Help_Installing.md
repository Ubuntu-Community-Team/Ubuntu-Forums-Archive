### Post by RamsesII on 2010-08-09
Well my dear zarz4 when it likes that in most of the cases it means that the distribution (in your case Kubuntu) doesn't include by default compatible video card driver for your video card. And there's not much to do in that case.

The simplest solution from my standpoint would be to install it if possible then look for the appropriate driver for your video card and install them manually in your console-only-session Linux. If you cannot install that distribution at all, try an older one. Upgrade to Ubuntu 10.04 and install yourself KDE and the required drivers from Synaptic. Hope this would help.
