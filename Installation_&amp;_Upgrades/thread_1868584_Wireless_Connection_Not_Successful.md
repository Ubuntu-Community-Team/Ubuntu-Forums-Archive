### Post by jnorthr on 2011-10-25
1. read this: [https://help.ubuntu.com/11.04/ubuntu-help/power-batterywindows.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/11.04/ubuntu-help/power-batterywindows.html)
2. this is a big problem, you'll need to do more research yourself on this ubuntu forum - look for the topic networking and ask specific questions there
3. the driver in ubuntu for your video chipset may not be the latest or the fastest, meaning that it might not be tuned to work with your specific chipset. many vendors do not release specs about their chips, so we have to build a free open source equivalent driver which is REALLY tough without specs, hence slower performance or not all features may be implemented.
