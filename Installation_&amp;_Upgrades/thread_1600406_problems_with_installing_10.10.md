### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-10-19
Hit enter at the screen with with a man in circle and a keyboard. Keep hitting until you see a menu. Now press F6 and select **nomodeset** and continue booting. Can you see the desktop now?

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions)

If unsuccessful, first of all check the integrity of downloaded image.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)
