### Post by tripokey on 2007-10-24
When bulletproofX tells you that your video card cant be automatically configured:
*DO NOT PRESS ANY BUTTONS ON THE SCREEN
*press ctrl+alt+f1
*type sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg
*select the screen resolutions you want
*type startx
*Install your system =)
