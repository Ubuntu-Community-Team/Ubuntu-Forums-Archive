### Post by louieb on 2007-12-18
I assume you will have both drives installed for a while. I believe you can use GParted to copy the partitions from one drive to the other. 
It should work - that would be the easiest.  Good luck. 
GParted is available on the [SystemRescueCd]("http://www.sysresccd.org/Main_Page")
partimage might be another option also on the System Rescue CD.
Nice intrduction to GParted here [GParted Documentation - general]("http://gparted.sourceforge.net/larry/generalities/gparted.htm")
