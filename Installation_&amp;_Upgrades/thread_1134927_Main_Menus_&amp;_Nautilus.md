### Post by GJLenon on 2009-04-24
Ok, I may sound like a complete newbie, but I thought I'd share.

When I installed 9.04 I was horrified and dismayed to see Nautilus (labeled "File Browser" by default) missing from my Applications > Accessories menu.  I noticed it worked when I went to the Places menu, but for the life of me I could not find it in Applications.

After searching high and low on the forums, I came up with nothing; when I found the answer to my quandary, I thought I'd share.  

Go to:

System > 
Preferences >
Main Menu >
System Tools >

Activate:

File Browser


Again, sorry if this sounds stupid to y'all, but I thought it was important, and wanted to share.
