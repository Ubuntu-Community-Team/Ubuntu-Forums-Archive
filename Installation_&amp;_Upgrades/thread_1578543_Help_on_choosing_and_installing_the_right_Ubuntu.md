### Post by oldos2er on 2010-09-20
I would install 64-bit on your i3; it is appropriate for both Intel and AMD processors.

To setup /home on its own partition, choose manual installation when the partitioner runs during install. See [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome)
