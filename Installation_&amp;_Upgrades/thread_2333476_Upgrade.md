### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-08-12
What do you get after you log in? The desktop wallpaper but without a top panel or the launcher on the left side of the screen?

Right click the wallpaper and select Change Desktop Background. That will open System Settings at the appearance utility. Now open the System Settings main window and select Software & Updates>Additional Drivers tab. You need to be connected to the internet and to allow the utility time to find the drivers. Now, change video drivers and restart. You might want to try the open source video driver.
