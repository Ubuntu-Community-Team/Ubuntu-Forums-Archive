From the log, looks like you were trying to print a PDF file.  There are  problems with printing PDFs in 8.10.  I found that trying to print anything from the file viewer in 8.10 often failed with "too many errors".

Can you print a test page using localhost:631 ?

Since your Epson appears to be connected locally through USB (like mine), what might work is disconnecting the printer, removing the Printer, and then reconnecting the printer.  In my case, that reinstalled my Epson and it worked again.
