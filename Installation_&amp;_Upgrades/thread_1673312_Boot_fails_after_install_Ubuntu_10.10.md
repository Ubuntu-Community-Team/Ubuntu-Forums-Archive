### Post by oldfred on 2011-01-22
I do not see anything in boot script that looks unusual.

The error seems to be from BIOS. Often portables do not have much in the way of settings, but I would look at BIOS. It must see hard drive or you could not have installed & run script, but it sounds like it does not see it to boot.  Some BIOS need a boot flag, even though grub does not, but you have one. Not sure what else BIOS might be looking for.
