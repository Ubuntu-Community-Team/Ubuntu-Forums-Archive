Your best bet to downgrade back to Ubuntu 7.10 would be to reinstall it.

### Post by russ18uk on 2008-04-28
The upgrade system will upgrade files which are older, not newer. Hence Upgrade heh. Like Windows, Ubuntu won't go back in time. Back up your /home and anything else you deem important and go back. Or, create a new partition specifically for Gutsy but you may damage your existing home using two different versions of the same desktop.
