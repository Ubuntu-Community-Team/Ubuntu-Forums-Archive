### Post by excogitation on 2009-08-17
The Vaio P doesn't boot off the internal SD-card.

But using the alternate install CD you could put /boot /home on your HDD and the rest on your SD-card. (provided the kernel supports the card reader)

I wouldn't recommend it either though.
