### Post by merlinus on 2009-07-13
Do a checksum on your .iso, burn at more more than 4x to a cd-r, not cd-rw, and check the disk for defects at the opening menu.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

Also, you might try safe video mode, or use the text-based alternate install cd.
