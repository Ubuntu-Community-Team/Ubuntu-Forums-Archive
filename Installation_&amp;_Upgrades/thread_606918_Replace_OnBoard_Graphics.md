### Post by old_geekster on 2007-11-08
It is extremely difficult to recommend a video card without knowing the other components that are in your rig; such as, PSU (Watts/brand), memory, CPU, etc.  However, since your onboard video is nVidia, I highly recommend that you stay with nVidia.  Their drivers seem to work best with all Linux versions.

I am using a XFX GeForce 7900GTX with Gutsy 64-bit.
