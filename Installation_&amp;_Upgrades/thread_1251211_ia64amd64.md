### Post by tenghoward on 2009-08-27
IA64 and AMD64 are TOTALLY different architectures.

IA64 is only for your computer if your computer's CPU is Intel Itanium series.

If not install AMD64 version on your computer.

And no, Intel Itanium CANNOT handle AMD64.
