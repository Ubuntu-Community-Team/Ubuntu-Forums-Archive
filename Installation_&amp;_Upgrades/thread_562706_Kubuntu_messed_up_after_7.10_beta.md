### Post by DFreeze on 2007-10-27
I'm sure you already went ahead and did something, but I didn't want your post to be left unanswered. You can just install kubuntu and manually install it on the right partition. You'll see a table with your partitions, and when you select the kubuntu one (edit it to become / (slash)) you can also check the checkbox to format it before installation. That will do what you want: format the partition before reinstalling kubuntu. Good luck!
