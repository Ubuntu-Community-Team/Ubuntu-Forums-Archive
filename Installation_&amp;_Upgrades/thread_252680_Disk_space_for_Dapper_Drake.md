### Post by tribaal on 2006-09-07
512Mb of RAM is enough.

Ideally, the swap space should be something around the same size than your RAM, and some people recommend to double that.

Of course, if you have only 2Gb available, you'll want to keep swap space as close to 0 as possible (I'd make somehting around 256Mbs... And save my work often). :rolleyes:
