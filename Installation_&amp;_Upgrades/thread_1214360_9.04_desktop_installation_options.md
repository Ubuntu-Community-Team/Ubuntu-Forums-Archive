1. You will be given the opportunity to choose how much to use. Read the options carefully, to help avoid muckups.
2. See 1.
3. Yes.
4. It's optional. Some people choose to use it, others don't.

It will be a good idea to defrag your Windows partition a few times before you begin, so that as much of the data as possible is at the start of the disk. It's also a good idea to make backups of important data (including recovery partitions) "just in case"
