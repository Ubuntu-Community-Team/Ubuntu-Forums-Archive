### Post by neoanderthal on 2008-09-25
This is just a heads-up - the Firefox update that was released yesterday (3.0.2 for x86 and AMD64) does not work at all with Microsoft's Office Live, and the Live webmail client is downgraded to lower functionality. Ubuntu Firefox 3.0.1 works with both sites correctly, and 3.0.2 from tarball on Mozilla.com also works correctly with both sites.

I suspect this isn't going to be an issue for many users, but I do know at least one user who couldn't access her business-management tools as a result.

I verified her problems with 3.0.2 on x86, and tested 3.0.2 on amd64 as well. I also verified that the tarball available from Mozilla.com works, so something specific to the Ubuntu packages released yesterday is causing the problem.

gaz
