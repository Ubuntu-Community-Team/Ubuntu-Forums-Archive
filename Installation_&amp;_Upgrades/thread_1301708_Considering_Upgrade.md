### Post by bsntech on 2009-10-26
With Ubuntu, if you swap a motherboard, it should automatically pick up on what drivers are needed for the board.

The only difference may be the driver to support your video card.  Audio/network cards are typically built in and won't pose a problem.

But, if you go from an ATI card to an nVidia card, this driver will need to be changed out.
