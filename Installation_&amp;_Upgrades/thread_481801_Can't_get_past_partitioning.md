When installing Ubuntu's desktop, at step 5 of 6, "Prepare Disk space", I am only given the choice of 'Manually edit partition table". I do not want to partition my hard Drive. I am installing Ubuntu as the only OS.  How can I get past this and not partition my drive? Thanks,

### Post by merlinus on 2007-06-22
You still have to partition your hard drive.  

Select the entire disk.  You will then need to make at least two partitions, / (root) and swap.  You can also make one for /home, and store your data there.
