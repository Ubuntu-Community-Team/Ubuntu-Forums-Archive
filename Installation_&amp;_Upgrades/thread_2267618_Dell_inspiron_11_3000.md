### Post by billy30 on 2015-03-12
I just installed Linux Mint on my inspiron 11 3000 last night, successfully. The problem i had was that i kept getting a blank/black screen after selecting to run LiveCD from grub. I posted this problem on the mint forum and got this response from BlackVeils and it worked for me. Perhaps it will work for you.

> [COLOR=#333333][FONT=Lucida Grande]have you disabled 'Secure Boot' in the BIOS/UEFI setup? and fast startup in Windows? read here:[/FONT][/COLOR]
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UEFI](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UEFI)

Just a heads up, there are some bugs with the touchscreen/keyboard. Touch screen works, but the touchpad will turn off after you use it and will not come back until after a restart. Also, there is no virtual keyboard. i have been trying to search for fixes, but i don't think enough people have installed ubuntu on a 2 in 1 system like this. Probably wont find a fix for a while.

Other than that, it works great. Just remember, if you plan on using touch, plan on being stuck with it until restart. The keyboard will still work, just not the touchpad.
