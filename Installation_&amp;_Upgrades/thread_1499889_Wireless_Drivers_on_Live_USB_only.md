### Post by lemming465 on 2010-06-04
**Broadcom wireless** support is notoriously bad, because the chip vendor isn't releasing documentation and isn't providing a GPL driver.  You'll have to live with the ethernet-to-install problem unless you switch to better supported hardware, I fear.
