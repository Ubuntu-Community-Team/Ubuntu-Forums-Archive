### Post by Dennis N on 2019-01-03
Lubuntu 18.10 is available in a 32-bit iso, available here:
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/18.10/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/18.10/release/)
An in-place upgrade wasn't recommended from Lubuntu 18.04 to 18.10. You will likely get better results with a fresh install.
From the Lubuntu 18.10 release notes:
> ...upgrading Lubuntu from 18.04 to 18.10 causes a fair amount of issues. Therefore, we are not officially supporting this upgrade path at this time...
