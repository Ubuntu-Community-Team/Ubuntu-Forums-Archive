### Post by hizaguchi on 2006-08-31
The whole panel is gone?  It probably just died and the session manager saved the session that way.  Right-click on the desktop and go to "run" and then enter "kicker" in the window that appears.  That should restart the panels.

Just the taskbar is gone from the panel?  Right-click the panel, click on "add applet" and pick "window list" in the window that appears.
