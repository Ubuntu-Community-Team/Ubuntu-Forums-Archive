### Post by kvonb on 2007-04-22
Looks like a font issue.

For a quick dirty fix, try creating a new account for testing.

Press <CTRL><ALT><F1> and if the text looks proper there, login with your nornal username and password.

Then type this:

sudo adduser test

Go through it's questions, you can just keep answering yes apart from the password of course :).

Now press <CTRL><ALT><F7> to get back to yuour desktop and press <CTRL><ALT><BACKSPACE> to quit.

Now, you should be at the login screen, try to login with the new username and password.

Tell us what happened.
