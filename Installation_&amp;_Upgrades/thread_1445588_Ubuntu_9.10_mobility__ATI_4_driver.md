### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-02
Best way to find out is to go to the AMD website, select the drivers screen, select Linux (32 bit or 64 bit) and enter your card information.

If the version it comes up with is catalyst 9.3, then no, there are no ATI drivers that will work with your card and current versions of Ubuntu.
