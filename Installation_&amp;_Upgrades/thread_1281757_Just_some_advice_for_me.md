### Post by earthpigg on 2009-10-03
> **Bobmas94 said:**
> Thank you, but if i were to install 8.04 would i be able to upgrade to 9.10 easily?

upgrade options are two:

1: from LTS to LTS. you will be able to go from 8.04 directly to 10.04.
2: 6 month release to 6 month release. 8.04 -> 8.10 -> 9.04 -> 9.10. i'd bet money that somewhere along that upgrade path, something will break.

9.04 -> 9.10 is much less likely to break, but 9.10 is going to come with some new stuff that will only be applied if you do a fresh install.

if you are eager, i'd suggest going ahead and installing 9.04 now and playing around... then, at the end of the month, do a fresh install of 9.10 while keeping your /home intact. 

(back up your /home folder from 9.04, do a completely fresh install of 9.10, restore your home folder... like magic, all your firefox bookmarks, your pidgin settings, your panel setup, etc, will be restored the way they where in 9.04)
