### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-12-17
Welcome to the forums :-)

Even by pressing the F2 key, you are not gaining access to the Bios menu. The menu that is showing up is Grub menu (Ubuntu bootloader). You need to refer to your manufacturer's documentation to find out your Bios Setup key and then from Bios, you need to select your USB drive as the first boot device.

Mostly that key is either F2, F10 or Del. Or keep a good eye at your Bios screen as soon as the computer starts. Setup key is printed on that Bios logo mostly ;-)
