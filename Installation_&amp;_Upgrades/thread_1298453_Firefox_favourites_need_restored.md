### Post by cwbar_1 on 2009-10-22
If you backed up your home folder (including hidden folders), open your backup file, mark show hidden files, and navigate to home/.mozilla/firefox/k2muz2ay.default/bookmarkbackups.  This will show you the dated bookmark backups in the .json format.  If they are there, open firefox and navigate to Bookmarks > Organize bookmarks > Import and backup > Restore > choose file, and locate the backup .json file you identified in your home backup.
