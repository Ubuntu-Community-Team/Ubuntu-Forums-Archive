### Post by NexusZA on 2009-01-07
I was wondering if there was anyway to stop apt from caching packages. I am installing xubuntu onto the 2GB EEE PC and once a package update has been downloaded and installed there really is no point in keeping it.

Thanks in advance for the help :)

---

### Post by oldos2er on 2009-01-07
In Synaptic Package Manager, go to Settings, Preferences, Files, and tick the box 'Delete downloaded packages after installation.'
