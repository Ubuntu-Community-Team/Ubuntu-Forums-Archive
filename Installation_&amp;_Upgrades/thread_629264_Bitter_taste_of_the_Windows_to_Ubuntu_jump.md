I am sorry, you did not get your reply as soon as you expected, but I think there are a couple of things you must consider about the forums and the transition period to Ubuntu. Please, see that this is not meant as an angry reply (honestly, it is not), but there are a couple of things that might have been misunderstood, and I would like to help you understand how the forum works..

I understand that the change can be frustrating at first, but you have to be a bit patient, open to the idea that things are done differently than in Windows and ask clear questions.
More often than not someone will come with a suggestion to the problem you face, but that might not happen immediately, as people that might know how to help you might not be online when you post.
Again, please just be a bit patient and you'll see someone most likely will answer your question. We are all volunteers trying to help each other, but not all of us can be all the time around.

That said, you should always start by searching the forums, to see if someone has already had the same problem before. This way you couldl get your answer fast and double threads are avoided.

About the original question you posted, I just had a look at your thread and there were already 2 replies.

I hope you'll consider giving ubuntu another try, as you would probably like it. I have seen a lot of successful cases where beginners asked questions, got suggestions and managed to solve the problems.
