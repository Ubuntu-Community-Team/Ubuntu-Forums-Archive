### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-05-22
Hi and welcome to the forums :-)

This appears to be a known bug in Wubi:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+bug/365881](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+bug/365881)

The solution is to either remove any attached devices such as card readers and/or click through the error message until it stops and then try installing.

Let us know if this was successful for you.
