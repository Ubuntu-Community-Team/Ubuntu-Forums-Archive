### Post by hermes0710 on 2008-04-25
It seems that your card's driver is not installed. Disconnect the second monitor, reboot, go to System>Administration>Hardware Drivers, tick you card's entry to enable it (internet connection must be present) and after installation reboot. From that point on try to plug your second monitor and see what happens.

Good luck
