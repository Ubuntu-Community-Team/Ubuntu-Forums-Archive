### Post by foob on 2007-01-23
Try an upgrade to see if it might fix the problem:

1   open your Terminal (Click Applications/Accessories/Terminal

2   at the command line type sudo apt-get update

3   press enter

4   if needed type in your password and press enter

5   restart your machine

Hope that helps... :)
