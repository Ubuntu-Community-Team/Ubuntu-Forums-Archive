### Post by Scunizi on 2006-11-01
If you have two monitors plugged into a dual head graphics card, disconnect one of them and try again.  After installation plug in the second monitor and reconfigure xorg.  That worked for me with Dapper.  I can't even boot to a visable screen with Edgy and two monitors.
