I usually suggest people have a look at [the OpenPrinting database]("http://www.openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi"). Trouble is, your printer has three entries for the PIXMA IP1500:

[Pixma IP1500 - Works perfectly.]("http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-PIXMA_IP1500")

[Pixma iP1500 - Works mostly.]("http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-PIXMA_iP1500")

[Pixma ip 1500 - Paperweight.]("http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-Pixma_ip_1500")

Whatever, you might get some useful information from the OpenPrinting site.
