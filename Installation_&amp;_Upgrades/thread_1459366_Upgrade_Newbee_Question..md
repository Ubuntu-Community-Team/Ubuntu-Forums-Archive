### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-21
Couple of comments ...

Upgrading is not really "trying" the update; it's replacing what you have today with a beta version.  There's no simple way to roll-back to what you had before.

You should look into using Clonezilla to image off your current installation to an external drive or USB stick.  That way, if anything goes seriously wrong in the update, you can restore your old version quickly.

Also, would uninstall the restricted drivers before you do the update.
