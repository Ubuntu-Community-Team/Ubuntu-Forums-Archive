### Post by tommcd on 2009-04-10
I don't know about air crack. But your wireless card uses the Broadcom b43 driver. See this 1st post of this thread to get it to work:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=186538](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=186538)
Note that the stuff about ndiswrapper is unnecesary. Just enable the b43 driver and install the b43 firmware to /lib/firmware as the thread describes.
