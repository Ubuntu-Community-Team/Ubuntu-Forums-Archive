### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-09-08
Have you tried all other sound output hardware settings?  Try running alsamixer in terminal and check that the levels of any outputs are not set too low.

Move from slider to slider, and raise and lower levels with the cursor keys;
Mute and unmute with the "M" key.
Tab will move you from "playback" to "capture".
Esc will save and exit.

As regards wine, I assume you have actually installed the programs into your ubuntu OS, or are you trying to run the executables of the programs that already are sitting in your windows partition, if you have one.
