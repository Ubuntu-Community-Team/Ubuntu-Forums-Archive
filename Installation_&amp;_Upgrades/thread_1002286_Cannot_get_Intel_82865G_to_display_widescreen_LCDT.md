I upgraded to a wide-screen display with 8.04 and had a nightmare trying to get Xorg to recognize it properly. It seemed to pretty much ignore all the modes and modelines that I entered.

Try looking into the Screen Resolution applet (System --> Preferences --> Screen Resolution), selecting your monitor (or generic, if it's not listed), clicking on wide screen, and seeing what resolutions are available. I did that for mine and was able to get 1920x1200 for the first time.
