### Post by icanfly0307 on 2009-01-28
Restart your computer. When you come up to the GRUB screen, scroll down to Ubuntu (Recovery Mode). Once you get the blue screen with 4 options, select 'xfix' and press enter. Reboot normally and you should be good to go. Hope this helps! :)
