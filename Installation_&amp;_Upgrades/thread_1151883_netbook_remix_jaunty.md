### Post by Brandon Williams on 2009-05-07
Here's what I do ...

Right click on the go-home applet in the upper left and unlock its position, then move the go-home applet just to the right of the window-picker applet. This will open up a whole bunch of space to the right of the go-home applet's new position.

Now you can right click on the big empty space to add an applet to the panel. You'll have to unlock the positions of things in the upper right corner so that they will move out of the way, after which you can move your new applet over into that corner.

Finally, move the go-hom applet back to the left of the window picker, and then relock the positions of all the applets.
