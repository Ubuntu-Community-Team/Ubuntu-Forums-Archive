### Post by bulldog on 2007-08-18
As far as I know your BIOS should  autodetect your hdd.
The live cd will not touch your hdd in anyway,it's entirely working from the cd.
So there should be an option in your BIOS which you can use to detect your hdd again.
In most cases you can set it to auto and the BIOS will fill in the necessary things.

### Post by Pumalite on 2007-08-18
If your hard drive doesn't show in BIOS is because a cable is loose or the drive just died. Check connections. Also make sure you have checked all the options in BIOS for IDE Configuration. Also check boot order. See if it shows up there.
