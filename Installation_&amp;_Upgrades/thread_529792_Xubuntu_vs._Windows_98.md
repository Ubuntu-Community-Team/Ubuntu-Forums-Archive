### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-08-19
It's going to be mixed. I've put Xubuntu on machines that slow, and I hated it. On the other hand, I know [CoachDANNY]("http://www.coachdanny.blogspot.com/") puts Xubuntu on some of that speed, and gives them away, and people are comfortable with that.

I think it's going to depend on what you want out of it. I wouldn't put anything but Openbox or [Fluxbuntu]("http://www.fluxbuntu.org") on that, but that's because I know you'll get amazing performance out of it. But try Xubuntu first, and if it's acceptable, keep it.
