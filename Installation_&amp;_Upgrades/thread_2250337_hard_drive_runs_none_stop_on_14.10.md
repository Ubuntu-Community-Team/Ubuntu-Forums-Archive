### Post by lonewohlf42 on 2014-10-28
After upgrading from 14.04 to 14.10 my hard drive runs all the time, none stop. Everything seems to be working, just slower and the hard drive light is always on.

---

### Post by lonewohlf42 on 2014-10-28
I checked the processes on system monitor and found mediascanner-2.0 running. I stopped the process and the problem of the hard drive running all the time stopped, so I uninstalled it and now things are working fine.
I don't know why it was installed. I checked my other computers and that service was not running on them. If I find a problem I'll let you know.
