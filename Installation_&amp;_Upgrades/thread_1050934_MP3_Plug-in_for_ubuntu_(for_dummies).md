If you just need a media player to play music, I recommend VLC Media Player. It will play mp3, ogg, etc. out of the box.

### Post by mocoloco on 2009-01-27
I just replied in another post that needed to do something similar.  Check out [this post]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=6626644&postcount=4").
The package you want for mp3 playback is libmp3lame0, but I would suggest you just mark ubuntu-restricted-extras as that includes mp3, flash plugin for your browser, and other multimedia plugins that will let you play quicktime and such.
