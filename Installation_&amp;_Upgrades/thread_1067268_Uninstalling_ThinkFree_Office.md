### Post by arch1k on 2009-02-11
Today I made the mistake of installing the ThinkFree Office on my Ubuntu 8.10. The office is nothing more than a resource hog with ads. Despite all the good reviews it doesn't even have a thesaurus.

Anyway, I couldn't remove the darn thing and neither could I find a tutorial anywhere on google.
I finally figured it out, so here it is:

If you installed ThinkFree Office using a ".sh" file (eq. TFO-3.5.1088.34-linux-en-gui-online.sh)
open the terminal and enter:  ```
sudo /bin/sh "/usr/local/tfo3.0/uninstall"
```
Enter your administrative password and presto!
