### Post by budgie9 on 2006-12-29
Did you do a MD5 checksum on your disk? It sounds as isf the disk is corrupted.
If you have the distro on your computer do a MD5 checksum on the .iso file first, if that checks out, write to the disk at the slowest speed you can. This will often ensure a better write.
