### Post by howefield on 2011-04-30
You can't upgrade from 10.04 straight to 11.04, so update manager should show you 10.10 first.

As for stability of 10.10 guess it depends on your hardware, it was fine for me - it will go end of life before 10.04, meaning that you will have another upgrade decision in about 12 months whereas 10.04 still has a couple of years support on the desktop.

I generally believe (up to a point) newer is better so would always want the latest.. as long as it works ;) but there is the old adage that says if it aint broke... don't fix it. :)

Remember to back up all data you wouldn't want to lose before going ahead.
