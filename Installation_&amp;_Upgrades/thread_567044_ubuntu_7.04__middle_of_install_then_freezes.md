### Post by dabl on 2007-10-04
Most of these "it freezes on xx%" problems end up being either a burn quality or CD media quality problem.  The basic installation routine is 15 or 20 minutes, not hours.

Burn the ISO image at no more than 4X speed on your CD burner.  If that doesn't get you a better CD, then beg/borrow/buy a different brand of blank CD media and try burning on that one at 4X.  :)
