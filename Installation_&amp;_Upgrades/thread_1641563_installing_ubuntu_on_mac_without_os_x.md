### Post by Fire_Chief on 2010-12-09
If you bought it new, it comes with the OS pre-loaded and also includes reinstall disks for OS X.

If it is an Intel Macbook, I believe you can just boot from the Ubuntu LiveCD and go.

Have a look here [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook")
