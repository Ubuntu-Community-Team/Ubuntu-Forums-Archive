### Post by darkod on 2012-03-08
What is your spec? It might be too old, or at least the video card might be.

Lesson for the future, always try to run the live mode of the version you plan to upgrade to, and see if it loads and works fine or not.
Also, the more subsequent upgrades you do, the higher the possibility things to go wrong, even if your computer has the spec.
An upgrade can never be as clean as a clean install. Consider having separate /home partition and then you can make clean installs when ever you want.
