### Post by torgrot on 2007-11-21
On my HP Laptop, the key to get into the BIOS is F2.  By default HP has a very short timeout on the BIOS screen.  In order to get into the BIOS on my laptop, I had to just keep trying, pressing F2 repeatedly during startup.  I eventually got in and changed the timeout along with the other settings I wanted.
