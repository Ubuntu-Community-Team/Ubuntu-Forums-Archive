### Post by mikewhatever on 2008-10-09
There is no way to downgrade as you've described it. You may have installed xubuntu-desktop, but in order to use it, you have to choose it from options at the login screen. You can also choose xubuntu to be default, and after making sure everything works, remove ubuntu by uninstalling ubuntu-desktop.
Hope that makes sense, but if not, do not hesitate to ask for more info.
