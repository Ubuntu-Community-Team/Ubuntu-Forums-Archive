### Post by darkod on 2012-05-19
Since you are running the previous LTS, the new one becomes available after the first .1 is released. Due in July.

Update Manager will offer upgrade to 12.04.1 in July.

If you don't want to wait, you can do it with:
sudo update-manager -d
