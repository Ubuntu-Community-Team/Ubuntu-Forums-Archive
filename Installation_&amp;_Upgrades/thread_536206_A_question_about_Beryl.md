### Post by notwen on 2007-08-28
As stated above make sure you have Beryl selected as the Window Manager and Emerald selected as the Window Decorator, this can be done by right clicking on your red gem icon in your panel's notification area.  

For your cube effect, simply Hold CTRL+ALT and right click your mouse and hold, this should zoom out, enabling you to view your cube, move your mouse to move your cube.  TO learn more about how Beryl works and al of it's abilities, go to Beryl Settings Manager from the right-click menu on your red gem icon.  =]
