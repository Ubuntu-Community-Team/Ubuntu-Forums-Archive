### Post by sturdy on 2012-10-31
Problem solved...the fglrx driver appears to be installed by the upgrade process and conflicts with the proper drivers. I was aware of this problem because it seems to occur with every upgrade (why?). However, there were many other issues and I was unable to get a GUI/CLI to remove the drivers. Finally found my way to Synaptic under Gnome and was able to use the GUI to remove the fglrx drivers. All is well...
