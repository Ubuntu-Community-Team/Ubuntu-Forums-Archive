### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-10-20
If you are an enthusiast, just curious for the newer software, you can always try the Live CD, see if everything works fine. Look if all of your hardware is detected and then make the switch.

I always recommend a clean/fresh install, not the version upgrade. Why? Because it is faster, doesn't break any thing on the system, doesn't make you fall in all those troubles etc etc.

However, on my production machines, I use the LTS versions till the new LTS version comes out. Always your own choice.
