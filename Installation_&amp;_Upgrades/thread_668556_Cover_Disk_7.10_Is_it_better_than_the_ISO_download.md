### Post by philinux on 2008-01-15
Problems that are common are.

1. Burned to fast, 4x has been recommended or at least the slowest your burner can do.

2. ensure md5sum checks out ok.

3. Chose the option on the main screen to check the disk out.

4. Graphics card - use either safe graphics mode or get the alternate text based installer then sort out graphics after. Check your cards compatibility.

5. Some brands of CDR CDRW have caused a few coasters too.

I've personally burned knoppix feisty gutsy and gustsy 64 bit. All to CDRW and they all work fine. Using the cheapest pcworld had.

The magazine disk should be ok too.
