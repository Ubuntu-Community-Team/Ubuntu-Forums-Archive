### Post by mahy on 2007-03-22
Step 1.) sudo aptitude kubuntu-desktop

Step 2.) the next time you'll be logging in, check all those buttons on the login screen. One of them is for changing sessions. Click it and change Gnome to KDE. That's it!

Forget about clean installation.
