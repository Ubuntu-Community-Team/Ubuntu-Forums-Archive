### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-08-12
You never "have to" purchase DVD or CD images of Ubuntu, it's just that some folks make them available and charge a minimal fee to cover the cost of the media and shipping.  Canonical will actually send you them for free if you ask them.

The more common approach is to download the ISO images from the GetUbuntu website and burn your own media.  Link is below: 

[http://www.ubuntu.com/GetUbuntu/download]("http://www.ubuntu.com/GetUbuntu/download")

However, realize that the DVD contains the same OS and app stuff as the CD, it just has additional languages.
