### Post by tkjacobsen on 2006-01-17
You can easily keep windows and all your games. You just need some unpartitioned space on your harddisk. If you don't have that, and don't have an extra harddisk, you can resize your eksisting windows partition with a program like partition magic. When you have made some free space (free like unpartitioned) you can install ubuntu. You'll need at least 10 GB i think.
