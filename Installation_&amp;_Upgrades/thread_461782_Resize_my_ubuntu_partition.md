### Post by Stephen Howard on 2007-06-02
I recommend that you burn a [gparted cd]("http://gparted.sourceforge.net/"), boot from it and then use it to change your partition size.  gparted boots entirely from cd and ram, so it doesn't use your harddrive thereby allowing you to change them.
