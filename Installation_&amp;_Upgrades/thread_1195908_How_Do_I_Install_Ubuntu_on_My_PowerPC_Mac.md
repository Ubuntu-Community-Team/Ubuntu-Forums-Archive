### Post by .Maleficus. on 2009-06-24
Assuming you downloaded the right version (from here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCDownloads](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCDownloads)) you put the CD in the drive and restart the computer.  The CD drive needs to be first priority in the boot menu.  The LiveCD or alternernate installer (depending on what you downloaded) will then start.
