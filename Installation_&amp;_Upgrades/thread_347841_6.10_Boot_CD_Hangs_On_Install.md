I am running this boot/install on a Dell Inspiron 8100, 128M Ram.

Any suggestions on resolving this problem? Thanks.

---

### Post by housam on 2007-01-28
I suggest you to go for the alternate cd , As your RAM is less than the minimum requirements. You need at least 190Mb for the live CD to run correctly and 256 to install ubuntu. and when burning the cd , make it at the lowest speed available i.e 4x or 2x .

### Post by bper on 2007-01-29

Thanks for your help. I downloaded the alternate cd, burned the image at 2x, performed the install and it worked like a charm.
