### Post by skirkpatrick on 2007-10-19
Well, you can certainly speed up the upgrade time by downloading the alternate CD via torrent and burning it.  Then when you insert it into a 7.04 machine, it'll pop-up a window saying it has detected an upgrade on the disc and do you want to upgrade.
