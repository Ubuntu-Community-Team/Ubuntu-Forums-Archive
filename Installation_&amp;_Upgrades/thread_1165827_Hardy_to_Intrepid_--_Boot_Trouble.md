### Post by tommcd on 2009-05-21
If your upgrade from Hardy to Intrepid cased some problems, and you plan to upgrade to Jaunty, then I would just do a *clean install* of Jaunty. 
A clean install will take all of 20 minutes, plus an hour (at the most) to reinstall your apps and settings. You will then have a pristine Jaunty system.
I always do clean installs. It is the best way to avoid the kinds of problems you are experiencing.
