### Post by Mighty_Joe on 2009-09-14
Some people disconnect their computer's hard drive before installing to USB just to be sure.
Personally, I use the manual partition selection rather than the automatic one, that way I can view all the possible drives and make sure I'm installing to my 8GB USB drive rather than my 100Gb hard drive.
