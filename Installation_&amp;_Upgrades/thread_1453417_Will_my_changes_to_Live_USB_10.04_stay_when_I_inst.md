### Post by Phenex1978 on 2010-04-13
Hello,

I did not test it with the 10.04 but for the older versions I'm almost sure the settings are not kept. You will have a standard install. What you can do is to copy your home directory from the USB key to the HDD. This will transfer lots of your customizations, but not the extra packages. You will have to reinstall those packages after the fresh install.
