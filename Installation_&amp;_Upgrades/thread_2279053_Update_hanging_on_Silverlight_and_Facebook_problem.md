### Post by QIII on 2015-05-20
Hello!

When upgrading to a new release, it is important to disable any third party repositories you have added.  It might be advisable to first uninstall any software you have installed from them.

PPAs and third party repos are often aimed at particular releases and dependencies my not be fulfilled in a different release.
