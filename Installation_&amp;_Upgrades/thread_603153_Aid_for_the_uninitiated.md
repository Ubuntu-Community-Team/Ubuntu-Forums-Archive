### Post by drratchet on 2007-11-05
When you install Ubuntu, it will *completely erase and overwrite* everything on your hard disk. You don't need to un-install anything, Ubuntu won't care, it will erase everything and start fresh. This is a good thing.

Remember, Linux won't run your XP programs, and it won't copy any settings or accounts from your old XP install. Ubuntu will look at your hardware for itself to decide what drivers to install. You will get to add accounts yourself after it is installed. It's pretty easy.

Although Ubuntu won't run your old XP programs, it comes with loads of programs that work (in my humble opinion) much better, Good luck, and have fun!
