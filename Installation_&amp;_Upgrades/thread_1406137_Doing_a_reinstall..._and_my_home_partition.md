OK, 

Is there something I need to watch out for to prevent my /home partition from being reformatted during a reinstall of ubuntu?

Thanks

---

### Post by SecretCode on 2010-02-13
At the partitioning stage of installation, choose to specify partitions manually, and just make sure (a) not to set the home partition to be formatted and (b) to mount it as /home.
