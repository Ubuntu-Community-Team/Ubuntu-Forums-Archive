### Post by kbielefe on 2010-10-10
My upgrade to 10.10 64-bit desktop got hung up for a long time on the line about installing a new apparmor profile for cups.  On a hunch, I stopped cups, at which point the upgrade continued just fine.  I don't know if that's standard practice or not, but I thought I'd throw it out there that you might want to stop cups before your upgrade.
