### Post by moephan on 2006-02-07
Perhaps you need to "Window List" to one of the panels to give the running applications a place to show up. Try right clicking on the panel that you want to have the window list, and choose "Add to Panel..." Then in that dialog choose "Window List" 

Hope that works.
