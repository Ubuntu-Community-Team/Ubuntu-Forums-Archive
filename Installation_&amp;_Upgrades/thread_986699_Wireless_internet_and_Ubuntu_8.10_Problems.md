### Post by sargenthp on 2009-02-03
Use your favourite dial up program and setup a dialup connection. (I used gnome-ppp).
Your username is: [email]xxxxxxxxxx@alltel.net[/email] (the x's are your card's phone number)
The password is: alltel
The phone number is: #777

Then it is just a matter of setting up your card with in the dialup program. Mine is the "Huawei EC168" and is setup as the device /dev/ttyUSB0.

Just a note: I am pretty sure that your card needs to be activated first and the narrow minds at Alltel only has software that runs in M$ to do this.

Good luck!
