### Post by rondackcpu on 2007-05-11
Carefully.  There's lots of bad advice out there.  This worked for me:  using Synaptic Package Manager, scroll down to sun-java6-jre, and Mark for installation, then Apply.  Synaptic does the rest.  Avoid any attempts to install by a terminal command line.  They are likely to cause great harm to your system.
