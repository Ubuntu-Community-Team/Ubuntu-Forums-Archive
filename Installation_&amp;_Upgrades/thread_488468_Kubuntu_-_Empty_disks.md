### Post by dabl on 2007-07-01
What is your computer, CPU, and BIOS?

Does this computer automatically boot other bootable CDs?  If YES, then perhaps Canonical has goofed up your CDs. If NO, then there's a problem with your hardware setup.

Before declaring the Canonical CDs "bad", you might pass them to someone else with a known working computer, and see if they'll boot on another machine.
