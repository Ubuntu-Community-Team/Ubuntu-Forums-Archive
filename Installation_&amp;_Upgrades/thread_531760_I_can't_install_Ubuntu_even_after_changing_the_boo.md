Sometimes you can drop all the other boot devices from boor order list in BIOS settings. That may help. And watch carefully what texts you have on screen in the beginning of boot, any do you see anything with "Press F1/esc/space to enter boot menu" or similar? If so, try it. Also you may want to check that you've burnt the CD correctly (if it wasn't shipped to you from ShipIt service or similar). Did you burn .iso file as an image to the CD, and if you open it, do you see lots of files or just one?
