### Post by HyRax on 2009-05-08
Do a right-mouse on the Second Life launcher icon and bring up Properties. There you will see the default "launcher" spring icon (spring/launcher, geddit? oh never mind...).

Move that window aside but don't close it.

Now bring up Nautilus and locate your funky icon. You will see a thumbnail of it as normal.

Drag that picture icon over on top of the spring icon in the properties window and let go. Viola! Your launcher icon will change to the new image.

Close the windows, you're done.

NOTE: The launcher will LOSE the icon if you move the original picture on your system to a different location, because it's loaded directly, not copied.
