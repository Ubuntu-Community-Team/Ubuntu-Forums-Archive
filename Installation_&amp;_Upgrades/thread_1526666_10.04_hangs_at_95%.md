### Post by snowpine on 2010-07-13
Sorry to hear of your trouble installing Ubuntu. A good first step is to check the integrity of the CD. There is a "test CD for errors" option on the first screen after you boot the Live CD.

Another thing that caught my eye is that your hardware specs are lower than the recommended minimum (see below); have you considered using Xubuntu (Ubuntu's lightweight cousin)? Have you tried any other distros outside the Ubuntu family (for example Puppy Linux, designed specifically for older hardware)?

> Ubuntu Desktop Edition

    * 1 GHz x86 processor
    * 1 Gb of system memory (RAM)
    * 15 GB of hard-drive space (although this can be split onto 2 drives, a 5Gb / and a 10Gb /home fairly easily)
    * Graphics card and monitor capable of 1024 by 768
    * Either a Cd/Dvd-drive or a Usb socket (or both)
    * Internet access is helpful
