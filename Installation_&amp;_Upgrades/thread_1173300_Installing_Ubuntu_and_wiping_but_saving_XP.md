### Post by wpshooter on 2009-05-29
My understanding is that ideally the order of installation should be M/S O/S first, then installation of Ubuntu O/S.

So, if it were me and I wanted M/S O/S on the computer (which is **_HIGHLY_** unlikely, in my case), then I would leave the M/S O/S that is currently on the computer as is and just install Ubuntu, after first doing 1 or perhaps 2 scandisk and defrags under the M/S O/S.
