### Post by K.Mandla on 2008-01-03
How much speed you get out of it will depend on how much you can overclock it, and that in turn will depend on the motherboard and the chip itself.

Judging by the comments on [this page]("http://www.cpu-world.com/CPUs/Pentium-III/Intel-Pentium%20III%20500%20-%20RB80526PY500256%20(BX80526F500256).html"), you might get it up to 667Mhz, but I don't know how much of a change that will be.
