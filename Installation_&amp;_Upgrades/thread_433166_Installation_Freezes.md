### Post by kyphi on 2007-05-17
From what I have been reading about the Dell Inspiron 5100 and an installation of Feisty Fawn (Ubuntu 7.04) it does work.  Plenty of others users with the same equipment have had no problems.

I do not know where you got your Ubuntu disc from.  If you downloaded it your burn might have been incomplete.  Some people try to burn discs at too great a speed, regardless of what the burner is supposed to be capable of.  Slow and steady is better than fast and faulty.  You may like to try to get the live version of your disc working on another computer.  You did not say if the live version works on yours and it is just the install that hangs.

Most probably the disc is faulty.  Could you try another disc?
