### Post by cariboo on 2009-06-08
Whatever you do do not stop the ugrade, just let it finish, hopefully you shouldn't have any problems.

The suggested way of doing an upgrade is to disable any ppa's and third party repositories, then make sure you are fully up to date, then do the upgrade.

Once you are finished Ubuntu may not work properly, so do whatever upgrades it suggests.
