### Post by kansasnoob on 2016-05-04
You need to open Software & Updates > Updates tab, and set Notify me of a new version to LTS only. But even then you won't be notified of the upgrade until 16.04.1 is released around July. IMHO the wait is worth it because Xenial is really buggy!
