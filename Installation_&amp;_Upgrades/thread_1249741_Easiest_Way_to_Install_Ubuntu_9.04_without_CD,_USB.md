### Post by darksniper404 on 2009-08-25
I'm making a tutorial for those of you out there who don't have an External HD, USB stick, or Disk and want to install the latest Ubuntu (9.04)

[SIZE=3]Step 1
[SIZE=2]Download Ubuntu 9.04 on the official Ubuntu website [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)

[SIZE=3]Step 2[SIZE=2]
Download the CD Emulator here [http://www.ztekware.com/download/ocdene.exe](http://www.ztekware.com/download/ocdene.exe)

[SIZE=3]Step 3
[SIZE=2]Open the CD emulator after its installed and drag Ubuntu onto your virtual disk and click the "Create CD Image" icon (first one).

[SIZE=3]Step 4[SIZE=2]
Click on My Computer, your virtual disk drive will be there, double click on it and the Ubuntu installer should pop up. 

Just complete the installer and your done.
