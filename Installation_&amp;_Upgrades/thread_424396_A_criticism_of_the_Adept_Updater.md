I had an idle moment this afternoon. So I took the wife's Edgy box. It is stock standard AMD XP 2800+, 1G RAM, basic NVIDIA AGP card. No fancy software. The only deviation is she runs Firefox and Thunderbird.

I followed the "upgrade" instructions to the letter. 

It downloaded for 90 minutes and then started setting up. After another thirty minutes a message flashed up that said things fell over and the "update files could not be found". It then set about, I assume, "restoring" things. 

It didn't. Restore, I mean. It rebooted to a black screen, no cursor, no nothing.

What a crock. But let's thank the gods for Acronis. ;)
