Yes, but it would be better to backup everything important and reinstall. To upgrade you will have to go from 5.10=>6.06=>6.10=>7.04, in other words upgrade three times. I think the chances for it to work are not great. If there is no way to backup and reinstall and you must upgrade, do from 5.10 to 6.06.
