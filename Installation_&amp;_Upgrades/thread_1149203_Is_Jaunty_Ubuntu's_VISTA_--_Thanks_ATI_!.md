### Post by edantes on 2009-05-05
The ATI affair exposes Linux hardware support model in a nasty way.  The truth is that it is very hard to live without the binary ATI drivers once you get used to them.  I can't even run GoogleEarth with the open source equivalent, didn't even try to go 3D this time around.

For those without ATI graphics in their machines here is the short version of this fracas:

ATI declared most of their installed graphic engines to be "legacy".  A new version of their linux drivers is compatible with Jaunty's version of Xorg, but the updates version for their "legacy" hardware is not.
