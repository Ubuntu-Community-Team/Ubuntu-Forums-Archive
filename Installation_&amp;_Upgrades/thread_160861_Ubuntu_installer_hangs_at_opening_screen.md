### Post by link141 on 2006-04-17
I've had some strange scenarios with stuff like that happening on my computer, on both windows and the kubuntu live CD.  Everything froze, and the keyboard lit up.  This would happen four to five times a sessions until my computer totally locked up. I too have an ASUS motherboard.  Except, in my case, the OSs were already up and running, so it took longer for that to happen.  Strange enough, I replaced the mouse (which I think went bad) and the problem totally dissapeared.  It could be that your keyboard is bad, maybe your mouse, or even an issue with ASUS motherboards.  What I would do is borrow a keyboard from a freind, and if that doesn't work, use your warranty for your board (if still good) and have them replace it.  Hope that helps:) .
