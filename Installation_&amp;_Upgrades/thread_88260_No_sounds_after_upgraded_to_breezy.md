washman - Check if your soundcard is detected. Click on System----Preference----Sound.....a new window will open and 3 tabs...1st tab is General---here you can check if the card was detected correctly (see if the checkboxes are marked).....2nd tab is the sound events---check if the wav files will sound...3rd tab is System Bell --- check the box...If still no sound...Click on System----Preference---Multimedia Selector---a new window will open which will show your Audio and Video Daemons ----on the Audio Tab----play with the default sink and default source ---check as to which of them will give you the sound.
