### Post by robert shearer on 2012-10-25
> How do I go back to 12.04? 
You re-install it from scratch, there is no roll-back.

> When I got the system prompt to upgrade to 12.10, I just clicked it and let it run.


Which is why, as the administrator of your own computer, it is wise to check any changes before making them...

There are inummerable threads here about teething problems with 12.04 to 12.10, reading some of those would have been of use in your decision rather than just being a click-bunny. :-(

A quick browse of the forums would find advice on trying the new o/s from a live cd/usb drive first to see if hardware and programs were supported.

You would also discover that a fresh install is generally less problematic than an upgrade especially when moving from a LTS install to a 'tween release. (was there anything wrong with 12.04 ?, was it broken, )

Whatever you do now....BACKUP YOUR DATA before taking the next step.
