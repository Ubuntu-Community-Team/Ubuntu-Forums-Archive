### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-06-04
The login screen looks weird in 11.10 because the developers are converting from Gnome 2 to Gnome 3.

What you are seeing is the Gnome 3 login screen.

The 11.10 release will not be released until October 2011 (hence the name 11.10).  You have loaded an alpha 1 version of the next Ubuntu release; and this is really only for folks who are helping test the new (and still under heavy development) release.

It should not be used for your production system.  **That will only end in tears.**
