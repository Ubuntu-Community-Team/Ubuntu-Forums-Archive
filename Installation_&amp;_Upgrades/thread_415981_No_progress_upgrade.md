### Post by w77jdp on 2007-04-20
The servers are experiencing heavy load. I had the same problems. My way around this was to download the alternate CD, install from that, then change the server I was trying to download from in System->Administration->Software Sources. I switched to a server at a university near me. Got great speeds from that.
My suggestion is try somewhere near you, or somewhere obscure that may not have much of a load. (For me, it worked both ways, my university is relatively obscure, and on the same ISP... :)  ).
