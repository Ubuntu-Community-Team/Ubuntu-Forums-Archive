### Post by marknewlyn on 2008-01-19
Hi

This might help someone. I couldn't find a solution on these forums before.

I upgrade a IBM T43 from 7.04 to 7.10 using the update manager. I had no problems with the update but after updating firefox could be crashed by using Yahoo Mail Beta. I could log into the service without any problems and read messages. It would crash if I clicked on reply or create new message. 

I found lots of links to possible flash issues and I messed around with flash but this didn't solve the problem. Eventually I just ran FF from the commandline and noticed the following:

/usr/lib/firefox/firefox-bin: symbol lookup error: /usr/lib/firefox/components/libmyspell.so: undefined symbol: _ZN8Hunspell5spellEPKc

as the last message before the crash. I have 3 myspell packages installed and just removed them all - I have no idea what the long term repercussions are - but Yahoo Mail works perfectly now :)

Mark
