### Post by YesWeCan on 2011-08-15
Ah, we'd all like a simple explanation for these things. ;)

The first thing I would do is run "Check disc for defects" which really ought to be called "Check this CD for defects".

After that, it is a matter of playing around with options in the F6 menu. You will need to Google for what they mean but the 'nomodeset' is helpful when there is a graphics issue and has enabled difficult systems to boot on many occasions.

If they don't work you might want to provide more details about your hardware.
