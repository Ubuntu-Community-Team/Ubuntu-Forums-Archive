### Post by aheckler on 2008-11-25
Xubuntu does not come with Gnome, it uses the Xfce desktop instead. If you installed it on top of regular Ubuntu, you probably wiped out your Gnome installation.

If you want Gnome back you may have to reinstall regular Ubuntu.
