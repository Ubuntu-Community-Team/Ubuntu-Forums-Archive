### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-08-18
I think synaptic did download and install FF3.5, it just doesn't make a launcher for it.  Right click on the desktop and choose Create Launcher, in the dialog that appears give it the title **Firefox 3.5**, and in the command box type **firefox-3.5**.  Now when you click on that FF3.5 will open instead of FF3.  All your profile will have been copied to a new folder called firefox-3.5, a subfolder within the hidden folder in your home called .mozilla, so you will have all your bookmarks, cookies, etc etc.

Give it a try.
