### Post by MonkeyPaw on 2008-12-14
Well, I have an Eee with just 4GB of HDD space, and I'm satisfied with the results. I use an SD card for my documents, music, etc, so that helps. Really, it just depends on what you want to use your netbook for. I don't want a lot of data on mine, as it's a field machine where bad things can happen. If you want your whole world with you, you'll probably value that 120gb HDD more than the silent zippiness of the SSD.
