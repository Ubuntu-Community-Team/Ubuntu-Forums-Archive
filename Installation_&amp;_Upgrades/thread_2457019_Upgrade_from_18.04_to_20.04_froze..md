### Post by Impavidus on 2021-01-24
Many things can go wrong for a failed upgrade. There's one possible cause that may be easily solvable, which is a graphics driver issue, which could lead to black screens. Can you boot in save graphics using recovery mode? If that works, it may be easy to fix. Otherwise the best approach is a fresh install.
