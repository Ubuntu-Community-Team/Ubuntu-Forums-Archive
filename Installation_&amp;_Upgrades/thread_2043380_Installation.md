### Post by Jt00 on 2012-08-16
This is kind of combining the previous two posts, but you need to edit your BIOS settings to boot from CD or USB first. When your computer boots up, you should briefly see something that says "Press <this key> for BIOS options/ROM based setup/etc." where <this key> is a certain key (i.e. f2, f10, ESC/Escape, etc.) and after "for" comes some kind of setup options.
