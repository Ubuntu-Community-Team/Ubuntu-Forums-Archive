### Post by foresthill on 2011-09-15
From my experience, if you have the blank screen problem on the Live CD, you'll also have them once you get 11.04 installed. 

Linux Mint 11 is based on 11.04, so that's why you are having similar problems with that distro.

If you don't feel like banging your head against a wall for days or weeks (or months in my case) trying to fix the problem, I would just install 10.10 (or 10.04 LTS) for now, and hope these blank screen problems are fixed next month when 11.10 is released. 

My .03 cents.
