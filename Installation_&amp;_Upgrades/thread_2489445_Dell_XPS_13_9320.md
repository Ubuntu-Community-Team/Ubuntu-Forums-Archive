My Experience YMMV:


[LIST=1]
[*]Shrink big partition:  [https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-server/storage/disk-management/shrink-a-basic-volume](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows-server/storage/disk-management/shrink-a-basic-volume)
[*]Turn off Bitlocker: [https://www.dell.com/support/kbdoc/en-us/000125409/how-to-enable-or-disable-bitlocker-with-tpm-in-windows](https://www.dell.com/support/kbdoc/en-us/000125409/how-to-enable-or-disable-bitlocker-with-tpm-in-windows)
[*]Burn ISO to USB
[*]Install Ubuntu
[*]Enroll keys: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Dell?action=AttachFile&do=view&target=mok_enroll_stock_ubuntu_2.pdf](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Dell?action=AttachFile&do=view&target=mok_enroll_stock_ubuntu_2.pdf)
[*]Install conical kernel ppa: ppa:canonical-kernel-team/ppa
[*]Install dell specific firmware and patches and drivers: [https://www.dell.com/support/kbdoc/en-us/000203830/webcam-is-not-detected-on-xps-13-plus-9320-laptops-running-ubuntu-22-04](https://www.dell.com/support/kbdoc/en-us/000203830/webcam-is-not-detected-on-xps-13-plus-9320-laptops-running-ubuntu-22-04)
[*]Ensure step 7 creates a new kernel entry in grub, for me it added a 6.2 kernel version. Reboot, and happy linuxing.
[/LIST]


If you think I skipped a step or am missing a step please let me know in the comments.
