### Post by coffeecat on 2011-04-23
There will be a key to press when you first switch on the machine in order to enter the BIOS. The screen usually says "Press <*something*> for set up". Something can be del, esc, F2, F10 or one of a number of other keys. If it's not prompted on screen, check your machine manual.

Thereafter, hunt through the BIOS settings.
