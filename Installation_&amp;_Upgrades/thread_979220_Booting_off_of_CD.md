### Post by jimsoccernut on 2008-11-11
Press F10 at the blinking cursor and see if that gets you into the BIOS where you can change the boot order.

### Post by tuxxy on 2008-11-11
When you boot your computer press the ESC key to enter your BIOS, look for the option of boot priority and set it up to boot your CD drive first HD second.

### Post by hansdown on 2008-11-11
Hi Garrison_O.

When your computer first starts, you should see, probably in the bottom left corner, two options; setup, and boot. They should tell you which key to hold down. Try the boot option, and change it to boot from your cd or dvd drive. Reboot, and hopefully this will do the trick.

The thankyou button is in the bottom right corner of the post you are reading, beside quote, etc.
