### Post by WiFi Ed on 2007-04-24
Everyone's been friendly to me so far (but, of course, they haven't actually met me yet):) 

I triple-boot Feisty, Vista and XP, and since your already dual-booting with XP you most likely know to start with the XP installation first, then install the Linux OS's.  As one who learned the hard way, my only advice is to decide on partition sizes first and don't re-size your XP partition during the Linux installations.
