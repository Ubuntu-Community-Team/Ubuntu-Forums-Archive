### Post by PmDematagoda on 2008-02-09
> **tlinux said:**
> 2. Is the upgrade process seamless? That is, will it take my present setup and my installed software, and go from there, or will it wipe out Kubuntu 7.04?
While the upgrade process is usually seamless there are cases where it has not been so seamless and problems have been caused by it. Also one cannot be too sure about the outcome of the upgrade since there have been cases where people with flawless upgrade processes found their OS to have problems(in some cases unsolvable problems). So if you are going to do an upgrade, remember to **backup** all your important files and data before doing so.
