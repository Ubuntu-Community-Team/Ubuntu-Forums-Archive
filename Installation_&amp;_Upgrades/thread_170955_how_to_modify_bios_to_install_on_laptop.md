### Post by kingmonkey on 2006-05-05
Try pressing F1 to F12 and try "Delete" and "Insert".

No harm will come of this. Also if there is a picture when you boot up try pressing "Esc" to see the real bootup screens (where it will probably tell you which button to press)

Hope this helps.

### Post by Sef on 2006-05-06
To get into the BIOS, the 3 usual buttons are F2, esc, and delete.  At times there is another one.  

On my Dell 4000, it is the F2 button.  Start the computer and keep pressing the button.
