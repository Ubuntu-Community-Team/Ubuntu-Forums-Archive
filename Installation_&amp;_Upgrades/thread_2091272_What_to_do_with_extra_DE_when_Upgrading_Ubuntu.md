### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-12-04
11.10 and 12.04 use different versions of Gnome under the hood so I would definitely remove the desktops to ensure best results for the upgrade. 11.10 is based on Gnome 3.2 and 12.04 uses 3.4 so even your 3rd party themes will be incompatible if you have downloaded any.
