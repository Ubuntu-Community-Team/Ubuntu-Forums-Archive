From what I understand, you want to boot Ubuntu from a usb flash memory.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/9.04/installation-guide/i386/ch05s01.html#usb-boot](https://help.ubuntu.com/9.04/installation-guide/i386/ch05s01.html#usb-boot)
