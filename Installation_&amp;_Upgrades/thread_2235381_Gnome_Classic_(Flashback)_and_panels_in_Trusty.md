### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-07-20
Select a category (Apps Department and then compare Available Versions.

[https://apps.ubuntu.com/cat/](https://apps.ubuntu.com/cat/)

In 14.04 + Unity we install gnome-session-flashback. Do not install Classic as that is a Gnome 3 shell extension. Are you sure that Applications, Places are in the bottom panel? Are they not in the top panel and running applications in the bottom panel?

I have just checked my Gnome Flashback (Metacity) session in Utopic Unicorn and Applications and Places are in the top panel and the running applications are in the bottom panel on the left. On the right of the bottom panel are the four workspace icons.

There is also a Gnome Flashback (Compiz) session that is the same.
