### Post by enri2 on 2009-03-26
well I think I fixed my problem.
this is what I did:

when you restart your system (when the boot loader appears) do not choose the first option , choose "other operating systems" instead.then a error will come up saying that  there are no other os , just hit enter then a blue screen with the same options as the boot loader will come up; choose  the first option  and press  your  right arrow or enter. Notice: this worked on my computer but it may not work  on yours.
