I am having a hard time understanding what your problem is.  I think you are asking how do you upgrade from 8.04 to 8.10 without an Internet connection?  Is that the question?  If so, then the answer is:

* Download and burn the alternate install CD (The Live CD will not work for upgrading)
* Boot into 8.04
* Insert the 8.10 disk into the drive
* The 8.10 disk will autoload and ask if you wish to upgrade.  Just click "Yes" and it will then begin.
