### Post by pansz on 2008-11-11
If you want to upgrade the ubuntu distribution (i.e. 8.04 to 8.10, 8.10 to 9.04, etc.), you must turn "unsupported updates" on, otherwise you may encounter problems.

One conclusion: you need to turn it off *only* if you purchased commercial support from Canonial, otherwise, it is much better to leave it on because it contains many *valuable* updates.
