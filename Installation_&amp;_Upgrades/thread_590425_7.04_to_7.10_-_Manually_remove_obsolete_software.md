### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-10-25
The command won't actually show you if anything has been removed.  Sticking with Unix philosophy, no response means everything went fine.  The other thing you can do is open Synaptic and check the 'Status' listing for 'Obsolete or local packages'.  You can remove everything from there (assuming you haven't installed any packages that are not in the repos that you do need....they show up there as well).
