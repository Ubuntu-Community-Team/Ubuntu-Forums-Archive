### Post by barinov2000 on 2010-05-11
I have same problem. It also affected my USB/EDGE connection via a Samsung mobile.

Go to the menu of Rhythmbox: Edit: Plugins 
and remove check-mark from MTU plugin.

### Post by kato223 on 2011-05-28
> **barinov2000 said:**
> I have same problem. It also affected my USB/EDGE connection via a Samsung mobile.

and remove check-mark from MTU plugin.

Thank you, worked like a charm, now I can access my iPod without problems, and the mouse does not lock up at all.

Without a mouse you have to press Alt+E, G (for Plugins), Then Tab to the window of the plugins, then down to Portable Players - MTP, then Space Bar to deselect it, then at the moment the mouse should return to normal.
