### Post by Threnners on 2008-07-22
Update:  Fixed

1.  Booted into GNOME session
2.  Went to terminal window, typed: sudo dkpg --configure -a
3.  Sat back and watched the fireworks
4.  Booted normally

Thanks: Forums, Google
