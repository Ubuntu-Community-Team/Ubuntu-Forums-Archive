### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-12-06
Welcome.

oldfred can help with your issue, but just a note: If you are going for a clean install I wouldn't bother with 15.04. It reaches end-of-life in January 2016, will then be unsupported and you will need to upgrade. Go with the very stable 14.04 LTS (til April 2019) or 15.10 (around July next year), both of which are upgradeable to 16.04 LTS, the next long-term support release, from the second half of next year. 

Good luck.
