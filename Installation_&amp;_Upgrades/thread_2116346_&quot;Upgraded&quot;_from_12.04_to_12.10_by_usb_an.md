### Post by snowpine on 2013-02-15
Welcome to the forums, and sorry to hear of your upgrade error. Hopefully you performed the recommended-in-the-first-sentence-of-the-upgrade-instructions backup of your data/documents and can easily restore from the backup?

If you have no backup, and your data really is gone, the first step is to stop using the computer in question, do not boot it, the more you use it, the harder it will be to recover the data.

Can you tell us which update method you used? ([This page]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop/upgrade") may be helpful in jogging your memory.) The first step in a forensics investigation is to reconstruct the steps that you took.
