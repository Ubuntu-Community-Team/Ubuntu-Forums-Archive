### Post by kitty500cat on 2007-07-20
If you have Ubuntu on a compact disc, simply insert the disc and set your BIOS to boot from CD.  Restart your computer.  Once it boots into Ubuntu, click the Install icon.  Then the installer will start, where it will prompt you to choose your location, keyboard layout, etc.  At one point it will display the disk partitioner, where you can choose how much space to allocate to Linux.

Does this help?
