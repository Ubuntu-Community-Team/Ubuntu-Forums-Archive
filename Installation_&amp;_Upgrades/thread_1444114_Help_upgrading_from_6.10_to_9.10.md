### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-03-31
> **Timek33per said:**
> 
I downloaded the 9.10 ISO and burned it, but can't seem to install from it. The disk seems to be non-bootable, as well.
Could mean you burnt it as data instead of an iso image.

Did you verify the md5sum of the iso?
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

Did you follow the instructions for burning it?
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)

That's what everyone will want to know first.

6.10 is no longer supported and several of the versions between that and 9.10 don't have any support so upgrading isn't likely.
