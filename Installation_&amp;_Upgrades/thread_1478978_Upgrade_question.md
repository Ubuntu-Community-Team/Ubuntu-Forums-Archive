### Post by snowpine on 2010-05-10
You can upgrade in two steps, from 9.04 to 9.10, then from 9.10 to 10.04. You cannot switch architecture (32 bit to 64 bit) during an upgrade.

At the end of the day, you would be best off to back up your documents, then install 10.04 64 bit from scratch, in my opinion.
