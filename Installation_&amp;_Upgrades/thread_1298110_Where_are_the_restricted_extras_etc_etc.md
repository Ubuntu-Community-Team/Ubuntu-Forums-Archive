### Post by earthpigg on 2009-10-22
remove the CAPITAL letters and try again :D

nearly everything is case sensitive in linux.

flashplugin-nonfree
mozilla-mplayer
ubuntu-restricted-extras

another thing to look at:
in the applications -> add/remove applications, make sure you click the little option to "show all software" and not just "Canonical supported software".

yet another thing to look at:
run 'sudo apt-get update' or start synaptic and hit 'refresh' or 'reload' or whatever it says.... on a super duper brand new fresh install, this must be done to get your system talking to the ubuntu software repositories.
