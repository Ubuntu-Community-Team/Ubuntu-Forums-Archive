### Post by Pumalite on 2008-07-02
You can make a separate /home:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome)
Or simply backing up your /home and later restoring it. Some settings might not work right out after the upgrade. You might have to fiddle with them, but is workable. More important is to remove all third parties from your sources.list including the Medibuntu folder (if you have it as a repo) and having your OS fully updated before attempting the upgrade.
