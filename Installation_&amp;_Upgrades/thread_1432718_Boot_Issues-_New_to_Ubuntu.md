### Post by dusdus on 2010-03-18
Seems like your harddisk is having some troubles. 
It's unlikely that it has anything to do with your installation.

Try and boot a LiveCD and see if you can access the harddrive (it should be visible under "Places".

If not, you could manually try and mount the harddisk (mount = attach the harddisk to Ubuntu)
