### Post by rocketman768 on 2006-07-26
No, it is not normal for the installer to not find a hard drive. It will format the disk as needed. If it "can't find" a hard drive, then something is definitely wrong. And no, a hard drive should not rattle at all...or at least I can't get any of my 5 hard drives to rattle. What sound does it make when it is powered-up and spinning? You could also try any other live-cd distros to see if they recognize your hd. I would almost bet that the hard drive is broken tho :( sorry. They are extremely cheap tho, so buy a new one if you think yours is bad. Hope that helps a little!

Oh, have you made sure that the jumpers on the back of the hard drive are set to master? What is the model # of the hd btw?
