### Post by darrenadams on 2005-03-17
I'm not to sure, but perhaps you can add yourself to the CC list for that bug report?

Go to the [bug you are referring to](https://bugzilla.ubuntu.com/show_bug.cgi?id=1261) and to the right-hand side of the page you will see a text box that says "Add CC". Enter your email address in that box, then press the "Commit" button, and whenever somebody updates that bug report, you will be notified. If you do not have a Bugzilla account for Ubuntu you will be asked to create one before being allowed to continue.
