### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2011-04-07
Well here is a page that instructs you how to upgrade:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/NattyUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/NattyUpgrades)

Although the first one will not work because 11.04 is still in beta. If you do a clean install from the disc it will wipe your documents and settings in Ubuntu. Although I believe you can burn a CD with an 11.04 ISO file and insert it into the disc drive while you have update manager open, it will update and not replace your documents and settings. (but I would suggest backing your data up on an external hard drive)

As for your friend, 11.04 has no netbook edition. When you install 11.04 it will look exactly like the netbook edition because the desktop and netbook editions merged.
