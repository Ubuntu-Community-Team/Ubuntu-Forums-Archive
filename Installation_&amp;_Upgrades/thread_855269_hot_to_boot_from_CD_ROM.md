### Post by SkonesMickLoud on 2008-07-10
When you boot your computer, look for something that says "Boot Options" or "Boot from..." it differs on every computer.  On my laptop I have to press F12 but on my desktop it's F7.  Your computer may even boot automatically if it senses bootable media in the drive.

It might take you a few restarts to catch what you have to press.
