### Post by not_yet on 2005-06-22
just a note for first timers 

when you read   [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/forum/installation/Partitioning](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/forum/installation/Partitioning)

it says   > Select the size. Enter a smaller size and hit enter

this was a bit confusing to me at first

lets say your ntfs partition is 40gigs

if you enter     50%   in the size field, the result will be a 20 gig ntfs and  20 gigs of free space

then you can go ahead and setup  Ubuntu in the free space :)
