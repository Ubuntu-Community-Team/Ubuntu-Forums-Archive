After searching a while I came across this Howto on installing greylisting, well a 'lite' version called 'qgreylisting' which works off the source IP address. I have had it running a while with no adverse affects and a marked 75%-80% reduction in spam getting through.

[http://www.jonatkins.com/page/software/qgreylist]("http://www.jonatkins.com/page/software/qgreylist")

It is not definitive, but hardly a hassle to install, you may need CPAN to update your Sys::Syslog module for Perl.

I like it but if you screw up your server, don't blame me or Jon Atkins :)
