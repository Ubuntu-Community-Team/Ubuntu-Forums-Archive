### Post by starcannon on 2009-07-23
Heres a walk through.

[LIST=1]
[*]Find the md5 hash for your version of Ubuntu here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes)
[*]In winmd5sum - solid blue software, click on the file browser to the Right of the "File Name" text field, browse to the Ubuntu ISO you downloaded.
[*]Copy paste the MD5 hash found in t step 1 into the "Compare" text box.
[*]Click the "Compare" button on the right; if all goes well you'll get an "MD5 Check Sums are the same", meaning it is now safe to burn your ISO image to a CD.
[/LIST]
