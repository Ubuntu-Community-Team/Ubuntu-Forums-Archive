### Post by xyphur on 2007-12-16
Do you mean clone the drive and all partitions to a new (larger) one? If so, grab the Ghost4Linux ISO here:

[http://downloads.sourceforge.net/g4l/g4l-v0.23.iso?modtime=1187368807&big_mirror=0](http://downloads.sourceforge.net/g4l/g4l-v0.23.iso?modtime=1187368807&big_mirror=0)

...burn it to a CD, hook up both drives (your source as master, target as slave) then boot to the CD and you'll be presented with options to clone your drive to the new one.

Hope that helps. :)
