### Post by hal10000 on 2007-01-08
You usually don't have to reinstall ubuntu (at least not if you have a standard kernel). But you should have installed the discover package, which looks for (new) hardware at boottime.

Maybe you have to reconfigure some things (like graphic card, network, sound etc.).
