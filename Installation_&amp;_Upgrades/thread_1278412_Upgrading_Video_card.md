### Post by rreese6 on 2009-09-29
IMHO and quite a few Video cards tested with Linux, choose Nvidia
I have a few older ones (AGP) like FX5900 and 6800GS ...stay way from the Chinese built 6800GT you see on ebay at a cheap price..most of these do not work well with Linux and some use  "Nvidia" chips that act nothing like the real ones. I assumed they were cheap copies.
if you stick with Nvidia, you can't do too wrong..just Google the card you find to see what problems other have had with them in Linux. 
I am no longer using ATI because AMD, who now owns ATI, had dropped all support for AGP cards that would work in your system.
