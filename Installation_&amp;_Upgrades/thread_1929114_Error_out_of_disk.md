### Post by YannBuntu on 2012-02-21
Hello and welcome among us :)

Please run [Boot-Repair]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair"), click "Advanced options", go to the "GRUB options" tab, and select the "out-of-disk" option.

If it fails, you may want to try installing 11.10 instead of 10.04.
