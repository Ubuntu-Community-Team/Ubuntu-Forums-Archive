### Post by upadhyayanm on 2011-05-03
I encountered the same problem of Screen Flikering after installing Ubuntu 11.04 in my cumputer.
 
Activating the relevant (recommonded) Additional driver [in my case NVIDIA accelerated graphics Driver (current version)] and rebooting fixed the flikering problem in my comuter. In my case I navigated through 
 
System Settings > Hardware > Additional Drivers 
 
and picked and activated the driver and then rebooted.
 
When went back to  
 
System Settings > Hardware > Additional Drivers  
 
I could see selected driver "activated" (green cirecle)  which indicates that "This Driver is activated but not currently in use". I assume this driver will be called for use only when software requiring this are run.
 
Cheers- Narayana
