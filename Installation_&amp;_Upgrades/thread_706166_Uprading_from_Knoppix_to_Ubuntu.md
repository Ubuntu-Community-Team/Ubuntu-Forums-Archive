### Post by steveneddy on 2008-02-24
Use your Flash drive and save your /Home folder.

This will save your settings, although you will manually have to re-install the software.

If there are pics or other things you want to save, save them also.

Install Ubuntu and when you are finished, plug in the Flash drive and copy your old /Home folder to the new /Home folder.

I hope this helps.
