### Post by cop.elektro on 2007-10-30
Hi,

I'm a bit late but I wanted to let you know about my experiences with the same printer.

I had good luck with the BJC-7100 driver.  In Gutsy, I selected the Gutenprint option on the first screen, and the Canon BJC-7100 driver (Gutenprint advanced) on the next.

Then I opened the printer properties page and changed the resolution to 1200x600.  The test page printed as expected.

I'm a bit dubious about printing photos with it, but at least it's adequate for letters and business graphics.

Good luck to you,
