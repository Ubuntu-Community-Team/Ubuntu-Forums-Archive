### Post by lemming465 on 2010-10-17
What happens at a disk partitioning step is very flexible; it depends on your choices.  In your case things are complicated enough that you should pick "manual" partitioning, and then do whatever you want.  A typical choice in your situation would be keep the existing partition layout, but reformat them with fresh filesystems (which will destroy any existing data contents, so have backups of anything you care about.)
