### Post by ridgeland on 2008-03-30
I used Envy with 7.04, very easy.

With 8.04 Beta I just go the menu
System -> Administration -> Hardware Drivers
and click [x] Enabled on the NVIDIA line.  Ubuntu downloads the driver from Nvidia I think.  It installs I reboot and it's done.

I thank Alberto Milone for Envy but 8.04 handles it for me and Ubuntu's approach may prove simpler with upgrades along the way.
