### Post by dje on 2008-04-15
Just open nautilus to your home folder (it contains all documents and personal settings) and press Ctrl + H and then Ctrl + A, right click on a file and select Create Archive, choose a destination and then click OK. Move the archive to the home folder on your new computer, right click on it and click Extract here

hope that helps,
