### Post by fantab on 2014-05-02
> The GPU that is enabled by the BIOS during boot may be dependent on whether the laptop is plugged into AC power or not.

In some machines with '[Hybrid Graphics]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HybridGraphics#Hybrid_Graphics")' it is necessary that you plugin the AC power during installation for the BIOS/UEFI controlled 'default' graphics card to work.
