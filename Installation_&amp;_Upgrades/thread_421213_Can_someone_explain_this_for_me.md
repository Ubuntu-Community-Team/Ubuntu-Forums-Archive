### Post by xp_newbie on 2007-04-24
Have you heard of term **[Hysteresis]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hysterisis")**?

It seems that the complexity of operating systems has reached a level in which they exhibit properties of [Hysteresis]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hysterisis").

DELL decided to revert back to shipping Windows XP (instead of Vista) with its systems, due to customer demand. Apparently, the customers have realized too that Vista, too, exhibits [Hysteresis]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hysterisis") behavior.
