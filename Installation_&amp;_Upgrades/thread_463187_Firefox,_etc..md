Personally, I would not install an updated version of Firefox that is not distributed with the particular version of Ubuntu that you are using.  I would update with the updates that become available thru update manager only.

More than likely you do not need a printer driver.  Have you tried the printer drivers that are available for your particular printer that are already available in the printer section of Ubuntu.

I also, have downloads directed to my DOWNLOAD directory and not my desktop.  Make yourself a download directory under your HOME directory and then go into Firefox and go to preferences and change the parameter so the your future downloads go to the download directory.
