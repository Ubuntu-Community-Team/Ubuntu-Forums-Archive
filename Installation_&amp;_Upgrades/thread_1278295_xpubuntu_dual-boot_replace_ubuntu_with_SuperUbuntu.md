### Post by PRC09 on 2010-03-31
Sounds like a video issue...On the flash drive I think it uses a generic video driver until you actually activate the driver on the system it is booting from.Did you or have you activated the video driver on either install?? That is under System > Administration > Hardware Drivers and see if there is a driver there and activate it.I have had problems in the past with activating the video card drivers on the flash drive install when booting from a different system that didnt use those drivers ,so be warned it could fail on the flash drive install when booting from a different system......
