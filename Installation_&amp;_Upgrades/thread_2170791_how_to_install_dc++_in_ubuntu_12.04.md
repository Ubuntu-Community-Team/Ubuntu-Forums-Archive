### Post by sanderj on 2013-08-27
Start up the Ubuntu Software Center. In the upper right corner, type "DC++" (without quotes) and then ENTER. It will show LinuxDC++. Click on it, and click install. After a minute or so, the LinuxDC++ icon will appear in the left bar. Click on it. Ready.

Disclaimer: tested in Ubuntu 13.04
