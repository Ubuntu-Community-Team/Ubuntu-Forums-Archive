From personal experience, the Live USB can do some crazy things.  It is unusual to reinstall the Live CD and not have it boot.  Give [unetbootin]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/") a try.  It's just another way to make a bootable USB Live CD.
Your boot partition should be fine.
