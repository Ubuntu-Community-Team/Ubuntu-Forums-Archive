You would need about 10gb free for a useful Ubuntu install, if you've only got 2gb spare, perhaps it's time to increase your hard drive capacity.

Also Windows will slow dramatically if you don't have about 30% free space on the C: drive and may not be able to defragment with less than 20% free space.

If it's a desktop PC and you can add another hard drive, this may help: 
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=275728](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=275728)
