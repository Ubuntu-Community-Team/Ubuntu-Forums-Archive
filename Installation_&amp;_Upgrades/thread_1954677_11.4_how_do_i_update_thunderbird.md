### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-04-09
You need to add Thunderbird's repository to your system. The instructions are on the [Thunderbird PPA page]("https://launchpad.net/%7Emozillateam/+archive/thunderbird-stable"), but the simple way is to open a terminal and enter:
```
sudo apt-add-repository ppa:mozillateam/thunderbird-stable
sudo apt-get update
```Then open Update Manager and follow instructions.
