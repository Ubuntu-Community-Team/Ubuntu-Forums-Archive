### Post by starcraft.man on 2009-06-25

[Link]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPC").

I think you must have downloaded a standard x86 or 64 bit image. Neither of those run on PPC chips. Follow the above link to be directed to the ubuntu wiki on PPC version. You should probably read the faq and known issues ahead of time, while you download the PPC version from [here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCDownloads").

I'm also a bit uncertain, I read that G3's have a bit of a configuration problem with the xorg. You might have to do some research on Google to see how others got around it. I can't help you past that, I never owned any Macs.
