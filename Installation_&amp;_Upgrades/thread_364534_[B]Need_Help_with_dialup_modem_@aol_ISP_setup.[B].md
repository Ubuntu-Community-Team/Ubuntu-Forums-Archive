### Post by solar george on 2007-02-18
I believe that AOL use their own proprietary dial up system. You are unlikely to get this to run under ubuntu.

Try fasthost's dial up.
Go to [this]("http://www.fasthosts.co.uk/broadband/dial/") site and at the bottom of the page you will see a login for people who don't have a fasthosts account.

If you have a supported modem you can set up dial up through the networking control panel.

Do you have any modems already - they may be supported by linux.
