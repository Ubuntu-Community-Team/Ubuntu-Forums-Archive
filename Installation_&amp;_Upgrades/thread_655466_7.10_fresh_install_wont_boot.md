### Post by Pumalite on 2008-01-01
Do the following before you install:
DBan your drive: [http://dban.sourceforge.net/](http://dban.sourceforge.net/)
Then get Gparted Live CD: [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/download.php)
Burn to disk and boot from it; delete anything remaining in your drive. Then make new large partition of your whole hard drive. Format ext3 if you want. Then install Ubuntu.
