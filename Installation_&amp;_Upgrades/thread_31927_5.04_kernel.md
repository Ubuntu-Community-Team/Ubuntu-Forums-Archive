### Post by 23meg on 2005-05-05
you can track kernel developments at [http://www.kernel.org](http://www.kernel.org) . the latest stable branch as of today is 2.6.11.8 . the 2.6.11 kernel is available at the repositories, but currently there's a conflict with Gnome that makes it crash at login; check out [this thread](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=25247&highlight=2.6.11+gnome) for a solution to that problem.

to update your kernel, launch Synaptic, reload the package listing, and do a search for "linux image". you'll see the available kernel versions listed on the top pane.
