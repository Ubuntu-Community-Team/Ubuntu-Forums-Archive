Hi, 

After several attempts at getting Ubuntu 7.10 to work on my laptop, (o.k its old IBM R30), with different copies of the iso etc ubuntu just wouldn't install. 

However, I found this website and thought I would post the address, as it helped me install Gutsy.... I'm typing this post from a working ubuntu os :-) 

its [http://www.linux-on-laptops.com/](http://www.linux-on-laptops.com/) 

its got a whole list of laptops and people describing experiences and codes to put it at install. 

For example for my r30 to have gusty on I had to ensure the floppy drive was active in the bios and then at the startup screen hit F6 and type 

"floppy=thinkpad pci=noacpi"

as you can imagine being new to this I would never have guessed this in a billion years!! but it worked so I'm happy :-)  

anyway, hope it helps ease the heartache/headaches of installing this OS for some people.
