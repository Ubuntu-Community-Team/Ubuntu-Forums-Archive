### Post by bscbrit on 2008-11-03
What you will see is the data that was held in your /home folder.  You **have** a new install but the data that you have asked it to keep in your /home has been kept.

If you want it to delete everything, when you get to the partitioning part of the update, tell it to reformat both the / and /home partition.  I can assure you that, after that, it will all be gone.
