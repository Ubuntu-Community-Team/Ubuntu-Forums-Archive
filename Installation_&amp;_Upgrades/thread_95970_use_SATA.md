### Post by bwog on 2005-11-28
Like many users, I have ubuntu on sata and there are no problems at all.

There is a small chance that very new hardware or exotic controllers will give a problem. However, that goes for all hardware, and is not especially related to sata. 

To sum it up, there are no fundamental problems with sata.

You can always search for your mainboard, controller or computer, to see if there are problems reported on the net recently.
