### Post by bored2k on 2005-07-24
What do you mean by "affect" things ?

Basically, the higher the upload speed, the higher download speed you would be allowed. You should cap the UL speed to more or less 80% of your total speed, because failing to do so will degrade your DL speed. In case you get slow speeds on BT, you need to do port forward on your router (what ports ? check your client for them). [http://www.portforward.com/routers.htm](http://www.portforward.com/routers.htm)
