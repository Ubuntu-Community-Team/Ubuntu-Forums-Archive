### Post by kestrel1 on 2009-04-23
For what it is worth, here is my input.
I would suggest a couple of options.
1) Use VirtualBox to install Jaunty (may not be the best option, but least distructive.
2) Install Jaunty to a new partition along side 8.04. Run jaunty until you get it configured the way you want. Either keep 8.04 or remove once you are happy with Jaunty.
