### Post by deadflowr on 2014-12-22
I've got that card in one of my machines.
And truth be told, it's dealer's choice.
Both the open and closed source drivers work perfectly well.

So if you do not want to deal with installing the closed source drivers, then by all means you should be fine without them.
For me, the one true benefit of the closed source driver is the use of the catalyst control center for a little more nuanced tweaking.
