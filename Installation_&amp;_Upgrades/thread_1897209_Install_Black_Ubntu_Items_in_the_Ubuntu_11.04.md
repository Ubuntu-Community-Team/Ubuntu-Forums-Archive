### Post by Dangertux on 2011-12-18
Holy giant centered font batman.

Anyways you can add the repositories from the desired distribution (blackbuntu or Back Track) to your /etc/apt/sources.list

You can attempt to install the packages with synaptic at that point. However, it's important to understand that both other OS'es in particular Back Track rely on a heavily modified kernel and this may in fact break your install.

Hope that helps.
