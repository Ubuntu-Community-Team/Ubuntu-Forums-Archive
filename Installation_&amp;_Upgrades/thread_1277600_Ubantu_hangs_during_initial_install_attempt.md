### Post by QIII on 2009-09-28
earthpigg is spot on.

Your machine appears to have Intel graphics.  Jaunty has regression issues with Intel graphics.

Once you get installed, please take a look at 

[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/904](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/904)

and pay special attention to the section about Intel graphics regressions.
