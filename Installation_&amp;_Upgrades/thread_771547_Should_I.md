### Post by chewearn on 2008-04-28
Here is wiki to help you on dual boot:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WindowsDualBoot](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WindowsDualBoot)

You should first boot into the livecd to test your laptop; make sure you are satisfied with the hardware support, check they are working and so on.

With 8.04, if you run the livecd installer from within Vista, you will be using Wubi.  Here is more reading material:
[http://wubi-installer.org/](http://wubi-installer.org/)

Wubi is the alternative to installing ubuntu on a separate partition.

As for people having problem, you are just getting a skewed perception, because hardy heron 8.04 has just been release and tons of people are installing it.  The number of people arriving at the forum with problems are sure to temporarily increase.
