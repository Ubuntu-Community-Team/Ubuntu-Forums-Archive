### Post by nad on 2005-05-13
Your 64MB of ram may be causing problems. This is a minimal amount and will not run a full featured desktop environment satisfactorily.

Other problems may be support for older hardware or configurations that have been dropped.

You may wish to review your bios settings to see if there is anything obvious that may increase your load to a point where an error message is generated.
