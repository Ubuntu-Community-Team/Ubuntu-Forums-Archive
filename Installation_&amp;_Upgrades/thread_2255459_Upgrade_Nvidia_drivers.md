### Post by deadflowr on 2014-12-05
> [COLOR=#000000]Any idea of what I should do? Does it make video games run faster? Is it safe?[/COLOR]

Can you play your games with the current driver?
If yes, then you probably have no need to upgrade the drivers.

Depending on the gpu card, the newer drivers might have better support and/or a little bit of performance tweaks.
Of course, also depending on the card, the newer drivers might also bork the performance.(rare, but can happen)

Personally, until it turns from an option to a requirement to upgrade the drivers I would ignore it.
