### Post by The Spectre on 2013-10-30
You would have to update to 12.10 before you could update to 13.04 but then you would have to update to 13.10 in January 2014 when 13.04 reaches its End of Life.

You might be better off waiting for Ubuntu 14.04 (Trusty Tahr) which is the next LTS Release 

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases)

### Post by vasa1 on 2013-10-30
