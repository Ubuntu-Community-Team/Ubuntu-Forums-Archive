### Post by aysiu on 2005-10-19
Two bum CDs? It sounds as if there's something wrong with the CDs. It doesn't matter how many times you burn them. What matters is the disk integrity.

The first thing you should do is make sure the images (ISOs) didn't get corrupted during download. Do you know how to do a checksum in Windows? If not, do a Google search for *md5sum windows*.

Once you're confident the image itself is not corrupt, make sure you burn the CDs at a *slow* speed--4x or 8x. That will lessen the likelihood that the burning process itself might corrupt the image.
