### Post by rivalarrival on 2007-09-07
Vichi,

Whenever you make any serious changes to a computer, you should back up all your important data.

However, Ubuntu won't necessarily delete your old files. the default installation tries to install in new partitions created from the unused space on your old partitions. 

If English isn't your primary language, this site might help:
[http://www.ubuntu.com/support/local](http://www.ubuntu.com/support/local)
