### Post by gordintoronto on 2017-08-06
I Googled "alloc magic is broken " and found quite a few results which pointed at hardware errors such as memory or a bad cable.

As an aside, I have had better results installing to a USB drive from an optical disc. Booting from a USB, it sometimes is SDB, and the drive you are installing to is SDC.  But when you boot from the new installation, it is usually SDB, and that doesn't work.
