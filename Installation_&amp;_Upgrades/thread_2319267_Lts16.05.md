### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-04-02
16.04 LTS, and yes, theoretically nothing you have installed yourself will be removed. Should be the same as you left it, just upgraded.

Be aware that you should disable any PPAs you have manually added and switch off third-party hardware drivers prior to the upgrade.
