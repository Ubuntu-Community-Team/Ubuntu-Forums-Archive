### Post by darkod on 2010-06-21
Wubi won't really solve anything because it's designed to be short term try, not long term install. Sooner or later you will have issues with it.

You really need to have a bootable device on your computer. For repair, restore, etc. You can't leave yourself without it. If USB is no option, is it too much to invest in new optical drive?
