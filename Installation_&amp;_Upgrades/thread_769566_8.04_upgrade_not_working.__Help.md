### Post by mathenge on 2008-04-26
There are a number of success stories in this forum. And there are many frustrations - which is why the forum exists in the first place.

Have you tried running the live-cd? Take the live-cd and make sure that the hardware works. You might have to do a little research to make sure that you don't have work-arounds in your existing installation that are no longer needed.

For example, in my case, I had to use Envy to set up my video card. However, I needed to uninstall Envy AND the nvidia drivers BEFORE the upgrade otherwise I would have had a lot of trouble getting the video drivers installed and then making sure they worked with X. The same story with my wireless setup.
