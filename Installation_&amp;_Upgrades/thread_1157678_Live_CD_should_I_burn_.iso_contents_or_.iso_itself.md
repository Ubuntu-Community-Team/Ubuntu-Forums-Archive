### Post by tommcd on 2009-05-13
What operating system are you using?
If you currently have Ubuntu with gnome installed, then the easiest and very reliable way to burn an iso is to simply right-click on the iso and choose "write to disc" (or whatever it says exactly). Choose the slowest possible speed. 
Yes, a DVD will work.
