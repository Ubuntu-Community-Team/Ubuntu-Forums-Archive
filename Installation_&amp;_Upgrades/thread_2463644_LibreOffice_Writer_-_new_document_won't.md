### Post by grahammechanical on 2021-06-16
I have three installs of Ubuntu and in two installs each LibreOfiice component has its own icon on the launcher. If I right click the icon and select New Document that is exactly what I get - a blank open writer document.

I recently had an other install of Ubuntu that had a single combined icon in the launcher. Click the icon and a windows opens. It has a panel on the left side that includes options to load new document windows. Most of the window was taken up with images of recently used documents that I could click and that document would open. I think this is the "stuff" that the OP is talking about.

Strangely, I recently re-installed that version of Ubuntu 20.04 and I ended up with a single Writer icon in the launcher. The answer to the OP's problem is to open Writer, Calc & Impress and with a right click on each icon that appears in the launcher select Add to Favourites. Then the icon will remain in the launcher and a click on the icon will load a new document window.

The combination icon can be removed from the launcher by a right click and selecting Remove from Favourites.
