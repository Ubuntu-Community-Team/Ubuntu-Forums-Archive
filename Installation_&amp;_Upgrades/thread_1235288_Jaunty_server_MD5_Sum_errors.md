### Post by Partyboi2 on 2009-08-08
Hi, if the md5sum do not much before burning to disk try using a torrent to download the iso. 
If the iso md5sum matches before you burn, try burning at x4 or less to avoid data corruption.

### Post by presence1960 on 2009-08-08
partyboi is right, sounds like you need to burn at a slower speed since you said your iso MD5SUM matches exactly. Those fast burn speeds work well with a data burn, but when burning an image they frequently leave corruption.
