### Post by dstew on 2009-12-03
The problem may still be the CD. You need to burn it at a slow rate, no faster than 4x. Also, check the MD5 sum on the downloaded .iso as recommended on the [Ubuntu Burning ISO How-To]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto"). If you get to the Live CD menu, you can also choose the Check CD for defects item to check the CD.
