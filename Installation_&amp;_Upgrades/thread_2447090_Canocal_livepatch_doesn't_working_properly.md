### Post by deadflowr on 2020-07-12
Where is it telling you it's not working?

Personally I wouldn't be too worried about it.
I'd worry if it persisted beyond a couple of hours.

Livepatch runs a check every hour, 
on occasion I've seen a rare fail to connect to the server.
But it's usually solved the next hourly checkup.

Also, If you keep rebooting and keep the system up to date,
then most likely most live patching will have already been mitigated by having already up to date kernels.
