### Post by dksau on 2008-06-13
I had the same problem right after my first install of hardy heron on this computer (its a tiger direct bare bones kit).  I'm using an Acer X163w monitor.  The problem cured its self after I attempted to adjust the the settings on the monitor its self.  My maximum available screen resolution setting is currently 1440X900 but this happened after a second install I had to do after making the mistake of hooking up a different tower for the purpose of updating with power to all other devices.  Luckily I had backed up most of my documents and pictures with CDs.  When restoring these files they came up with locks on the logos and I got warnings the the documents were open in read only mode.  you can cure this by right clicking on the document and then clicking on properties.  you will then see a permissions tab, after clicking this find the appropriate access menu and make the desired selection for permissions for that document.
