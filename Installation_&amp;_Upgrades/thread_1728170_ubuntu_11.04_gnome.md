### Post by gdonwallace on 2011-04-13
I have been running the beta since it came out, just to see how unity would work.  I did switch over to the "Ubuntu Classic" look, which loads up the original Gnome Desktop, and everything was fine.  The only weird thing I found was when an app launches in full screen mode, it isn't actually in full screen.  I was able to see my background because the bottom of the app was above the bottom panel.  Min / max the app and no issues.  

Something small, but other than that, it has been the only thing that I have noticed that was off.

On a  side note, when 11.10 comes out, you won't have the option to switch to "Ubuntu Classic", unity will be the only option.  Unless you install another desktop.
