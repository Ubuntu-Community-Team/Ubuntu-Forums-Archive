### Post by oldfred on 2013-09-27
I have a much older Toshiba that just works. 

But have seen some models where wireless may need configuration or keyboard for brightness. Is this AMD or Intel based system? Intels seem to have fewer issues, but still depends on which combination of chips used in your build. LiveDVD or flash drive is a good test. But often live version uses lowest common denominator and actual install may not and need some extra updates.
