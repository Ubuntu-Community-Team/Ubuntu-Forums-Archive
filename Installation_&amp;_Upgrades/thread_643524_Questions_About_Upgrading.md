### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-12-17
> What are the main differences? Are there any main ones?

Well there are many differences:-

1) More updated applications and packages.

2) A bullet-proof X-Server which is more reliable than the normal once.

3) Compiz-Fusion is integrated into the system.

There may be some more, but I can't think of them.

> Is 7.10 faster/slower?

That is pretty much a matter of personal opinion, I found it to be faster than Ubuntu 7.04.

> I have compiz-fusion installed. Will upgrading "break" it? (I've heard that it has.)

It might since Compiz-Fusion is built-in, I suggest you upgrade after removing Compiz-Fusion to avoid any problems. **But the upgrade might break other things as well, so watch out for some problems after the upgrade and backup all your important data before the upgrade.**
