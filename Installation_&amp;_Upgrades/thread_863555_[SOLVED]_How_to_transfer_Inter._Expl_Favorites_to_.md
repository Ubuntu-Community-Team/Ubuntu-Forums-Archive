**> To export your IE Favorites to a file**> 

 
[LIST=1]
[*] Open Internet Explorer.
[*] Go to "File > Import and Export...", then click Next.
[*] Select "Export Favorites", click Next, click Next again (unless you only want to export a subfolder of your Favorites, in which case, select that subfolder now).
[*] Click "Export to a File or Address", click "Browse" to choose a location such as the desktop (or accept the default location) and save the file, which is named "bookmark.htm" by default.
[*] Click Next, then Finish to complete the export.
[/LIST]
 After you've finished exporting your IE Favorites to a file, use the Mozilla Suite or Firefox Bookmarks Manager and [import the file]("http://kb.mozillazine.org/Import_bookmarks#Import_from_file").
