I just installed SP3 on one of the boxes here with no issues. Add in that it is one of the AMD based system's that everyone is jabbering about (read Asus A8N32-SLI Deluxe) having issues with SP3, I am pretty pleased so far with it. From some of the post I read SP3 has particular issues on that model with a single hard drive installed. Plugging a USB drive *prior* to boot seems to take care of it. I do have a second internal hard drive installed outside of the RAID array on which might account for my good fortunes on that unit. Make sure you shut down all other apps and unecessary  processes before trying to load SP3.
