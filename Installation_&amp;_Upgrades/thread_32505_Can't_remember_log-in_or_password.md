### Post by Professor X on 2005-05-08
You need to boot into your BIOS and change your boot order.  Select your CDROM drive as your first boot device, followed by your hard drive.  Once you've done this you can place the install CD in the HD, reboot, and at the prompt type 'rescue' (assuming you don't want to reinstall).  This will give you root access without knowing your password.
