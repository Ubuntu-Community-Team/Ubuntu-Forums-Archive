### Post by jimbob on 2007-12-15
Since you say "in my computer" I assume you mean it is built in to the mobo.
In that case it is probably a Win modem and the odds of your getting it to work are slim.

Save yourself a lot of grief and get an external USB modem which is a full-hardware modem (Hayes-compatible modem).  Google for one or look at Ebay.  Zoom is one manufacturer that you can try.
