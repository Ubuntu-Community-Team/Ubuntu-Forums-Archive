### Post by macintosh on 2008-12-24
Have you installed Ubuntu-Desktop correctly reinstall it by going to Terminal then typing in "sudo gksu /usr/sbin/synaptic" without the quotes then type your password and a new window will launch. Then at the menu click "Edit" and then "Search". Type "Ubuntu-Desktop" then click on it then in the Menu bar click "Package" then "Mark for Reinstallation" then click "Edit" in the menu bar and click "Apply Marked Changes" then when finished click "close". Click "System" the the top left hand of the computer screen and click "Quit" then click "Restart".
