### Post by JK3mp on 2009-04-29
Yes many ATI graphics cards were blacklisted (not supported with new ATI Catalyst drivers) Thus you should run lspci and find out the model of your video card and compare against ati's blacklist if it is an ati. I've heard of a few pple with nvdia cards having problems too though.
