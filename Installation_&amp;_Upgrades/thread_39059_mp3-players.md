### Post by DrMoxie on 2005-06-03
iRiver H320

**Pros**
* Playback of MP3, WMA, ASF and OGG
* 20 GB storage
* Plugs in as external drive, no software needed
* SRS Sounds system
* Can double as removable storage

**Cons**
* Kludgey Firmware
* Currently End-Of-Life
* A tad heavy

I figure that I'll get several years out of this unit, though I am disappointed in iRiver for going the route of needing proprietary software to transfer music to the player with their H10 series. Having your MP3 player just show up as an external drive increases the versatility of the unit by allowing the user to decided the what, where, and how files get transfered.
