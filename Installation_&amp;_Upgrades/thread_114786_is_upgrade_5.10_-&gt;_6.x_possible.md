### Post by Corvillus on 2006-01-09
By default your 5.10 installation will stay 5.10, but any time you wish, you may change your sources.list to point to the Dapper (6.04) sources and then do a dist-upgrade, although I'd suggest not doing this to your primary install until Dapper is actually released.
