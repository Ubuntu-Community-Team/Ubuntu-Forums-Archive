### Post by mikewhatever on 2007-07-24
7.04 has newer version of most of the packages, compared to 6.06, including the kernel and the desktop environment. That does not mean one is better then the other, nor does it mean that 6.06 is out of date. 6.06 gets regular security updates, + it will be supported till 07/2009 on desktops and 07/2001 on servers.
Choose what suits you best.
