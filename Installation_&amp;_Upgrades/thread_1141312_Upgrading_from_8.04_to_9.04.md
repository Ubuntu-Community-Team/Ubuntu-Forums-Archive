### Post by Slovak on 2009-04-28
Read [this]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/IntrepidUpgrades"). Then read [this]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/upgrading"). You can also try this first, press alt+F2 and type in &#8220;update-manager -d&#8221; (without the quotes) into the command box. Be sure you have ALL updates applied to 8.04 before trying to upgrade, then do it again to upgrade from 8.10 to 9.04.
