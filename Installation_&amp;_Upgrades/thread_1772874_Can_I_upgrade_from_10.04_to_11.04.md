### Post by ivanovnegro on 2011-06-01
I would really choose the second option, if at all, because you are on the very stable side of an LTS release.

If you want to upgrade, yes, you have to upgrade first to 10.10 and then to 11.04. It could take some time, really faster with a fresh install.
