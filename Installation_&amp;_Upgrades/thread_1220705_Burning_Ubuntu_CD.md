### Post by starcannon on 2009-07-23
Extract the ISO from the RAR, then burn the ISO using your favorite ISO image burning software. Where did you get this rar from? My advice is download from an official source, such as: [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)

I recommend 8.04 it is a Long Term Support(LTS) release. Again, use your favorite disk burning utility to burn an ISO to CD.
