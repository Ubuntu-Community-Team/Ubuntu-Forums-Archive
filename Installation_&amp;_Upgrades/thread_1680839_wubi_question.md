### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-02-03
It may be better to back up your files from your current Wubi installation And do a fresh Wubi installation.

Wubi for 11.04 will only be available from the ISO when it first comes out. Some people have upgraded Wubi installations , but it does not end nicely for many .
  
A clean installation is better than trying to repair a broken installation , at least for me.
