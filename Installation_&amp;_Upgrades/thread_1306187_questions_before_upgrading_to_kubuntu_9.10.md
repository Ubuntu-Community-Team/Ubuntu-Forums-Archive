### Post by NormanFLinux on 2009-10-30
1. If you upgraded to KDE 4.3.2 in Jaunty, the settings will be kept during the upgrade.

2. Everything should work after you boot into Karmic.

3. Some third party sources disabled during the upgrade will still be usable. Others won't be found and will have to be removed until Karmic repositories appear.
