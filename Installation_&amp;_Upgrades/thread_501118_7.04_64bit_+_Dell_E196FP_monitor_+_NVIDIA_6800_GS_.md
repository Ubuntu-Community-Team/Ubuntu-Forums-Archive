### Post by Pumalite on 2007-07-14
I would first check iso: do Md5sum. Burn at 4x or or less. Check CD for corruption. Beyond that you might be confronting graphical interface problem. Try Alternate CD (text mode ) If at this point you can get to a command prompt; you could try: sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg . Most of the questions would be easy for you to answer according to your hardware. When you get to 'Driver', choose 'vesa'
