### Post by sloggerkhan on 2009-10-29
if you know your raid works now, you could always back up your mdadm conf file and restore back to 9.04 if the upgrade doesn't go right. You could even upgrade with your array offline if you're just using it for storage.

I might be upgrading an 8.10 server install with an mdadm array to karmic... won't be for a few days at the earliest, though.
