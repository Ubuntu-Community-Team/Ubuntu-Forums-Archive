### Post by Skidpad on 2007-02-20
Have you defragged your hard drive?  If so, were all of the files moved "to the left"?  If not, your page and hibernation files may be stopping the installation; I had this problem ([http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=327923](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=327923)) when installing.

When you say 'check apps", are you referring to the md5 checksum?  If not, you may want to compare the values to make sure your cd is identical to the source file.
