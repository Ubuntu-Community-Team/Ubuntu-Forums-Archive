### Post by Arkenzor on 2008-01-03
Solved it by removing the "web" entries since there was no web subfolder in archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/dists/gutsy. I don't know what they meant though, nor why they were there. I'm pretty sure I didn't add them myself, and I didn't use any program to modifiy my sources.list except for the standard gui and the wicd installer.

Well at least it works now...
