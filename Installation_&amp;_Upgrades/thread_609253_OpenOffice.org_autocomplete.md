### Post by linuxbz on 2007-11-10
You didn't say what part of OpenOffice.org. If you want to disable AutoInput in Calc,

Tools -> Cell Contents -> AutoInput

In Writer (in fact most parts), you can go to

Tools -> AutoCorrect

and uncheck whatever you want, probably under Options.
