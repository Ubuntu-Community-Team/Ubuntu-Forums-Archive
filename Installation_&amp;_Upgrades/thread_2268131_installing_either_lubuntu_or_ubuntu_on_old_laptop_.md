### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2015-03-06
Yes to your first question.  You can install from a flash drive if your computer will boot from a flash drive.  (It may appear as another hard drive in the BIOS).
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick)
With regard to your second question.  It depends upon your hardware.  How much RAM do you have?  It would seem that you most certainly could install Lubuntu.
