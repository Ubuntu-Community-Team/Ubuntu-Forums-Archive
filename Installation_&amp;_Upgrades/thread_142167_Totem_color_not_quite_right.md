### Post by shreepads on 2008-07-12
While this may be a little late for Jeremy, updating this reply as this is the first post that comes up in a Google search..

As suggested by Giovanni Figliozzi (and appended by me :-) at [https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/totem/+question/7373:](https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/totem/+question/7373:)

In Totem -> Preferences -> Display:
- Set Hue to 0
- Saturation bumped up 10%
- Contrast bumped down 10%
- Brightness bumped down 5%
