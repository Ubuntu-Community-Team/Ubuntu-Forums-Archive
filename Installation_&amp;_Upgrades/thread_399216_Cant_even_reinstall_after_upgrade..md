### Post by tmray on 2007-04-02
I was dual booting with vista and kubuntu edgy on two separate physical drives and everything was great. I installed my new nvidia card and everything was fine. Then I tried the 7.04 beta upgrade and the wizard didn't start. I eventually rebooted and it wouldn't let me open anything. On reboot it would just say something like "grub error 15". Then if I tried to run the kubuntu edgy cd it wouldn't even let me run the live CD it said stuff like -

"init:/etc/event.d/tty5:13: unknown stanza"
and
"kernel panic - not syncing"

I reinstalled vista on the one hard drive and reformatted the other. Then when I tried to install the Kubuntu 7.04 cd it still said "unknown stanza" then scrolled line after line of numbers for 20 min. finally I just tuned it off and tried to install the Kubuntu edgy CD. And once again it wouldn't even boot up to the live cd.

Is it cause I'm trying to do from my nvidia card. I was originally using the onboard intel card.

What can I do to fix this?

---

### Post by tmray on 2007-04-02
well, to answer my own question it was the video card. I went back into BIOS and set the card to read the onboard intel graphics card and I am now loading Kubuntu 7.04 onto my computer. Now here's to hoping I can get the nvidia card working on it next.
