### Post by Grenage on 2011-02-10
Tip 1: Make your subjects meaningful; not "help".

When you reinstall Windows, it will undoubtedly overwrite your bootloader and leave only Windows as a bootable OS.  Your Ubuntu OS will still be there (unless you overwrote the whole disc), but you'll need to reinstall Grub.

Have an Ubuntu CD handy.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling%20GRUB%202](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling%20GRUB%202)
