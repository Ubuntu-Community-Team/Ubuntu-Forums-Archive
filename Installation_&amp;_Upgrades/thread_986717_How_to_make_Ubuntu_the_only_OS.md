### Post by kspncr on 2008-11-18
If you boot the Ubuntu live CD you can use gparted delete your Vista partition and grow your Ubuntu partition to fill the space. You may have to reinstall GRUB after that, though.

Here's a tutorial on reinstalling GRUB: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows)
