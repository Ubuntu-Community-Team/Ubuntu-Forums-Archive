### Post by drs305 on 2010-10-02
Wubi is just a large file on Windows. Did you allocated 80GB to wubi? I only use it for answering questions and didn't even know you could make it that large. It can only take as much space as you allocated it during the install.

I would imagine that by using the Add/Remove app in Control Panel to remove wubi you will get the space back in Windows.
