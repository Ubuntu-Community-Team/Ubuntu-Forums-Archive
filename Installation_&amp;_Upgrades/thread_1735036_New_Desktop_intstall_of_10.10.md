### Post by Dutch70 on 2011-04-20
Hi and welcome to UF

You don't want to use the "alongside" option with 10.10. It will probably mess up windows.
10.04 is the LTS (Long Term Support) version. It's supported for 3 years. 10.10 is only supported for 18 months & it's not much different than 10.04. 

If you just must have 10.10, use the live cd/usb to partition your hdd before you install, the choose your partitions manually.
