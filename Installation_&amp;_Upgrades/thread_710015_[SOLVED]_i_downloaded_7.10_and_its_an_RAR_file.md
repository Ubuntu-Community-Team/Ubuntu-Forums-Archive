### Post by YldGuy on 2008-02-28
most probably your WinRAR recognizes ISO file and is showing it as of RAR type. Just check the extension of the file you have downloaded. It should say .iso. .iso is itself compressed. DO NOT UNZIP it using winRAR. Let it remain as it is and burn to a disc and you are good to go.
