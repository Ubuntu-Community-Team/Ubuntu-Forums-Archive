### Post by Quackers on 2010-12-13
I know it's a bit of a pain, but it will tell us something if you can turn off the netbook and take out your thumb drive, then power it up again while holding down the shift key. Hopefully the grub menu will appear.
If it does please press the "e" key . This will bring a new screen up. On this new screen using the down arrow and the right arrow move the cursor to the end of the line that says "quiet splash" then backspace to erase "quiet splash" and then type in "nomodeset" but without the quotes.
Then press ctrl + X to reboot - then cross your fingers :-)
