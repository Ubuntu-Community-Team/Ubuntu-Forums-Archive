### Post by louieb on 2006-09-30
By DR DOS I guess you mean Digital Research DOS. If I might ask how old is your PC. THe last PC I had with DOS installed was 486.  But anyway it sounds like your pc will not boot on its own to the CD drive. Did you check the boot sequence in BIOS. When you boot with the floppy you will get the option of booting the CD.
