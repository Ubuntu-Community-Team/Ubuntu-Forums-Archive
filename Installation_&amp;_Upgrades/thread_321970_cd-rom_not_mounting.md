### Post by wpshooter on 2006-12-19
IMO, you should try Ubuntu/gnome 6.06.1 from either the DESKTOP (live CD) or from the ALTERNATE CD.

Are you burning your CDs at 4X or less ?

Are you checking the integrity of your Ubuntu CD by running the verification function found on the initial Ubuntu boot screen menu ?

Are you running the memtest, also found on the initial Ubuntu boot screen menu ?

Trying cleaning your CD drive.

Make sure your CD drive has the latest and greatest firmware installed.

Are you using a good quality brand named CD, and as far as I am concerned I would use CD-RW media ?
