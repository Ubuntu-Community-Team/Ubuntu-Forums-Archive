### Post by fantab on 2013-07-05
Since you want remove both Widnows and Ubuntu before doing a clean and fresh Ubuntu install then **Reformating** the Hard Drive or its partitions is the simplest way to remove any and all OS. 
Use this opportunity to re-create your partitions according to your needs and Re-install ubuntu.

Reformatting Hard Drive or its Partitions will wipe out ALL the data, so have good BACK UP of your data before your reformat.
