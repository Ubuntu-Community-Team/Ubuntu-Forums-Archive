### Post by ubfan1 on 2023-10-05
Maybe you are defaulting to the wrong desktop.  Click on the "Show Applications" button at lower left, and at the top, see the desktops, the leftmost one should have your Desktop Icons. Click on it. On the other hand, Icons may show on both these days.(does on my 22.04).  Depends upon which DE you are running.
