### Post by Therion on 2009-03-11
Go to System/Administration/Software Sources.
Click on the "Updates" tab.
Down at the bottom you'll see a menu for "Only Notify About Available..." with a menu for choices.  
Choose "Normal Releases" from the drop-down menu.
Close, Reload.  

You'll be prompted about a new version being available for download.  
Follow the prompts to install.  Be patient; it will take a while.
