### Post by mörgæs on 2012-08-31
You are welcome :-)

During the installation one gets the option of erasing the entire drive and creating a Buntu-only system. If you for some reason want to reinstall, this will solve the problem.

After the system is installed you can also change the partitioning with Gparted. From a live boot open Gparted, which will give you a graphical overview of the hard drive. Here you can delete unused partitions and after that increase the partition(s) you want to keep.

I have never had trouble with Gparted, but still it's a good idea with a backup, if you have important data stored.
