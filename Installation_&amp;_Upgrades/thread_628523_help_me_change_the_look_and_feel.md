### Post by kmac on 2007-12-01
try [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org)

-on the left click gtk 2.0 to filter by themes for ubuntu. 
-download the theme you like. 
-unpackage the theme
-drag the theme into the "theme" toolbox
(System >> Preferences >> Theme for Feisty and
System >> Preferences >> Appearance for Gutsy)

There are also a bunch of little gizmos and wallpapers, etc on gnome-look, i recommend browsing through there and learning how to do a few little thigns to completely customize your machine.
