### Post by jonathangillings on 2008-05-23
Exact same thin happened to me. The alsa mixer has defaulted to a ridiculously high level.

Heres your fix dude.



go to the command line and start up alsamixer  (sudo alsamixer)
go to the PCM volume change it from 6xx to 100



Thanks to Suvir Kumar on launchpad for that.
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/222192](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/222192)
