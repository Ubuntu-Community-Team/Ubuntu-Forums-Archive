### Post by gjtoth on 2007-04-19
I jumped onboard Feisty with Herd 2 (I think :) ) and had upgraded from Edgy.  It was running just fine and I only had one minor problem -- not being able to get desktop effects to work... something that I could live without, certainly.  However, after a little thought and a good backup, I decided to just go ahead and do a fresh install.

I am extremely glad I did!  EVERYTHING runs perfectly -- including desktop effects!  Now, I'm busy reinstalling my favorite apps.

If you're angonizing as to whether or not to do a fresh install, maybe this will tip the scale for you.

Granted -- it's NOT necessary.  But, IMHO, it sure is nicer.
