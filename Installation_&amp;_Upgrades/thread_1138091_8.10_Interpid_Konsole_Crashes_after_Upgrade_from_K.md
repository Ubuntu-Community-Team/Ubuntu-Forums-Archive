### Post by Triptol on 2009-04-26
Seems you are trying to start konsole as root, although you logged in as a different user.

Seems the KDE session (including D-Bus) can't be found. Try again as your normal user shawn.

Yea, soon as I switched back to user shawn the same thing with the VT420PC translator appeared.  Which the error is for some keybindings that don't appear to exist anymore in the 4.2 version. I checked the konsolerc and yaukuakerc files and they have nothing about keybindings in them,  everytime I try to downgrade the konsole back to the old on it wants to downgrade the entire KDE desktop so thats not quite a option either.

I have finally found the problem :) In your ~/.kde/share/apps/konsole/ directory is the config files to your profiles, open them up and delete the keyboard section out of them.
