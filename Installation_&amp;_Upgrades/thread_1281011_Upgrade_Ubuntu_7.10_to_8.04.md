### Post by ram2500 on 2009-10-02
Maybe your drive for some reason won't recognize the CD-RW, so the next idea is a bootable USB key. However, your BIOS must be able to boot from a USB device.

---

### Post by ram2500 on 2009-10-02
Also, I forgot to say, you would probably need at least 1GB of space. And, to get your machine to boot to USB, you'll need to go to your BIOS setup and choose USB to boot as 1st device, that is, if it will boot USB at all.
