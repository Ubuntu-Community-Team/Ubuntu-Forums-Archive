### Post by erginemr on 2008-02-29
Default usplash (i.e. orange startup screen) resolution in Gutsy is 1280x1024, whereas your desktop screen can be something else. Monitors can have different settings with different resolutions, so you can play with your on-screen menu of your monitor during the bootup screen to correct the aspect ratio and location of the image on screen.

If you are not satisfied with this, or with your startup resolution, then you may refer to:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=700673](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=700673)
