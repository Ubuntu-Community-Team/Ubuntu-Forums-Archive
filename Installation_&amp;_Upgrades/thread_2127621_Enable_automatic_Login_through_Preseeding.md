### Post by gordintoronto on 2013-03-20
If you click on the dash and type login, do you see "login window" as something you can run? If so, select the Security tab, and "Enable Automatic Login" is a checkbox item.

Or, it might be under "User Accounts", where you click on "Unlock", provide your password, then turn "automatic logon" on.
