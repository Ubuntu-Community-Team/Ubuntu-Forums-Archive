### Post by snowpine on 2008-12-01
Simply open your Synaptic Package Manger and do a search for "ubuntustudio". You should find everything you need. :)

---

### Post by cariboo on 2008-12-01
Or even better go to System-->Administration-->Synaptic-->Edit-->Mark Packages by Task, and select Ubuntu Studio from the menu.
