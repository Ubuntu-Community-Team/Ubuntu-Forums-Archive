### Post by lrcaballero on 2012-07-15
ekie95 Welcome to Ubuntu!

I've had experience this issue my-self several of times and I can conclude that it was not my video card, are you able to explore the OS prior to installing it to your HDD?  If so, how is the behavior of  the OS while exploring with it?

My case was that I was going directly to installing the OS rather than exploring with Ubuntu 12.04 and for a weird reason it continued to Froze at this point! So after several attempts I rebooted and first explored with 12.04 first then I proceeded to installing it into my HDD after playing with 12.04 for minutes and this did the trick for me!

I would also try burning the ISO onto another DVD even though you have tested it your current DVD but this time at a lower speed.
