The PowerPC archive has been moved from archive.ubuntu.com to ports.ubuntu.com. ([http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/804](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/804))
Also hoary reached its end of life awhile back so having those repositories in your sources.list will be the cause of some of your problem, also not a good idea to mix different repositories from different versions of Ubuntu as this can cause breakages.
What I would suggest doing is backing up you current sources.list and try using a new one.
```
sudo cp /etc/apt/sources.list /etc/apt/sources.list.old
```then open your sources.list
```
gksu gedit /apt/sources.list
```delete the contents and replace with
