### Post by PorcupineTR33 on 2011-10-18
After extensive research I found out that my Linksys E4200 Router doesn't support Linux :( But I was not going to just give up without a fight ;) Turns out that the Linksys E4200 has been recently added to the DD-WRT list of supported devices and so I flashed my router with their custom firmware and it all now works fantastic! Not to mention I just totally freed my router and unlocked a whole bunch of new features that I couldn't before on Cisco Firmware :popcorn:
