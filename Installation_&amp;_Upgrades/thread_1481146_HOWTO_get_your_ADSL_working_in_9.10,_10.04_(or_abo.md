### Post by dE_logics on 2010-05-12
First of all add yourself to the www-data group - 
usermod -a -G www-data <your login>

Then to the right side of the top panel, there should be a network icon in the notification area, right click on that and edit connections...
Then goto DSL>add; enter your username and password and check "available to all users" if you're not in a multiuser environment, this is safe.

Then IMO that's about it if your ISP doesn't have any kinky ways get connected.
