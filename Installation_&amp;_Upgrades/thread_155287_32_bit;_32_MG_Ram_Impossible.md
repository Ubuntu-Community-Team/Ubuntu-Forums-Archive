### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-04-05
Unless you upgrade the memory, it's probably hopeless, even with Fluxbox or IceWM or something.

Upgrade the RAM to 128 or higher, and then you'll have yourself an Ubuntu'ed computer. (or 64 for a command-line Server installation. 80 megs might get you one of the above desktop environments)

However, in terms of running a GUI Linux, 32 megs isn't hopeless. Try downloading Damn Small Linux. It's a 50 meg live CD which can reportedly run in 32 megs.

It's not a patch on Ubuntu, but it can still breathe new life into your computer.
