### Post by codeslicer on 2008-05-22
What do you mean they do not work? Perhaps you just need to reconfigure them at System->Administration->Printing?

Reversing back to 7.10 might be possible, but not recommended as you are going back to previous versions of various software which may not work with the new configuration.

Anyway, [here's the link]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/DowngradeHowto") but you might want to change the versions there to hardy and gutsy...

**In my opinion, I wouldn't downgrade as that will only cause more problems, instead search for a way to make your printer work, you'll likely get that accomplished faster.**
