### Post by P4man on 2010-10-20
Open this in a browser:
[http://127.0.0.1:631/printers](http://127.0.0.1:631/printers)

Add a printer. At some point it will ask for a driver or .PPD file, and you can download that here:
[http://www.openprinting.org/printer/NRG/NRG-P7325N](http://www.openprinting.org/printer/NRG/NRG-P7325N)

(you can also fetch the .DEB package there, and just double click it to install)
