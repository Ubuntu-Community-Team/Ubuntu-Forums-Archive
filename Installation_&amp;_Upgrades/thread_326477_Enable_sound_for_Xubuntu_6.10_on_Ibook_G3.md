### Post by user2037 on 2006-12-27
After a fresh installation of Xubuntu 6.10 on a white, Ibook G3 there was no sound. And no audio devices appeared to be set up. Finally a Google search turned up the solution on the Yellow Dog forums: add snd-powermac to /etc/modules.

If it already appears there you may need to check that its not muted using alsamixer.
