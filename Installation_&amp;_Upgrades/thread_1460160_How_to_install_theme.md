### Post by 1999panos on 2010-04-22
G' Day nonabhai,

to install a theme in Ubuntu, you follow the steps.

1) Right Click on your desktop and choose "Bhange Desktop Background",
2) Then click the "Theme" tab,
3) Then click "Install...",
4) Then choose the ".tar.bz" file with the themes, no need to extrat,
5) After that, click "Customize...",
6) In "Controls" tab, choose from the list your theme name,
7) Next, click "Window Border" and choose again the theme name,
8) Finally, click "Close" and "Close" and all right.
