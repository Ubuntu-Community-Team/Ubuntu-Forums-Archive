### Post by akudewan on 2009-07-31
> **lse123 said:**
> alternate CD burned from iso, must be inserted when already logged in Linux 8.10 ? or prior startup the PC ?

Insert it when you are already logged in to Ubuntu.

You don't need an internet connection if you are using the alternate CD. If your WLAN is cut off, it will be ok. But make sure the laptop doesn't run out of battery. If you have to shut down the laptop in the middle of the upgrade, the system would break.

There may be some upgrades remaining after the alternate CD has done its work. These you can upgrade the usual way, using your net connection.
