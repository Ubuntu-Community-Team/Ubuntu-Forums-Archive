### Post by m_duck on 2009-03-14
The AMD64 version is just the 64-bit so will work on Intel hardware as well. It's something to do AMD's naming system just becoming more common for whatever reason. I think technically Intel's equivalent is EM64T or something, but it tends to be AMD64 which is used for 64-bit generally, or x86_64. I believe there's an article on wikipedia about it.
