### Post by al_sharpe on 2006-09-21
Hello dopski:

To change hostname goto
System - Administration - Networking

2nd tab reads General - click it

and change Hostname and click OK a pop up will show up 
and click Change name

you will have to logout and back in for it to change

Enjoy
Al
