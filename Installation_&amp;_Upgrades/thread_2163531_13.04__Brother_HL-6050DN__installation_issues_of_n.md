The Brother driver is available here:
[http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/download_prn.html#HL-6050DN](http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/download_prn.html#HL-6050DN)

[http://www.openprinting.org/printer/Brother/Brother-HL-6050](http://www.openprinting.org/printer/Brother/Brother-HL-6050)
