### Post by taurus on 2005-11-19
Let me ask you a real simple question.  The first time you used Windows, did you know where everything was right away or it took a little time to learn the ins and outs of it!!!  Please don't expect to move to Linux and assume everything is the same as in Windows.  Thanks goodness it is not or I will migrate to another OS!  So, spend some time playing around and once you get used to where everything is, I bet you don't think Windows is more superior than Linux.  And if you reall want to do whatever you want with your system, then create a password for root and log in as root.  Then you can modify or change everything on your system.  And soon enough, you will break your system and have to install the whole thing over again...

sudo passwd root
(your password first)
(new root's password)
(retype it again...)
