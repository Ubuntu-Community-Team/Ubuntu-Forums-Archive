### Post by warfacegod on 2010-06-14
Keep in mind that Wubi wasn't really intended for anything more than giving Ubuntu a try. If you like it, your best bet is to defrag XP and then create a separate partition for Ubuntu figure 10 GB and another for your data file marked as /home.

If any of this appeals to you, please don't go off half (or fully) cocked and just start resizing and creating partitions on the spot. Do some research, consider your options, and ask us for help and advice.
