### Post by PurplePenguin on 2006-12-24
Here's the [download part]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download") of the Ubuntu site.  You can pick either version 6.06 or 6.10.  When you select your version and geographic area (for quickest download), it will offer a couple different downloads... the last link leads to the alternate install disc ("Other installation options").  It is for computers with low ram (seems like this might be your problem).  Just pick one of the isos with the word alternate in the filename, along with the proper platform (i386, AMD64, PPC, etc). :)
