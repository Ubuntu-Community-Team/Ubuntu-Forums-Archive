### Post by Mighty_Joe on 2009-11-17
My advice would be to identify or report the bugs you had in [launchpad]("https://launchpad.net/") and wait for those to be fixed or you're just doing another blind upgrade and asking for trouble.
If you want to experiment, try installing to a flash drive or spare hard drive.  
Personally, I don't do upgrades.  I prefer to install the new version to a new drive so I have the old version available should something go wrong.
