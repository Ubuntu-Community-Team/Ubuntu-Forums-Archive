### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-05-13
Select a 'LivedCD' of 10.10 that fits your system (32 bit or 64 bit) from here [http://releases.ubuntu.com/10.10/]("http://releases.ubuntu.com/10.10/")

Don'y worry that is says CD or DVD, it is really an ISO file, and that can be made into a CD, a DVD or a LiveUSB.

If you are downloadng this on your other Ubuntu system, you can use the 'Startup Disk Creator' to turn the ISO into a LiveUSB.

If you are doing this from a Windows system, you can use this to make an Ubuntu LiveUSB from the ISO: [http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/")
