Just for reference, this is a supported upgrade.  The idea is that you can skip from LTS to LTS directly.  It is a little unreasonable to support an OS for 3 years (or 5 for server) and then expect the user to install every intermediate level.  So basically you have the option of upgrading every 6 months or skipping LTS-LTS every 2 years or so.

But I agree at this stage waiting would be more sensible :)
