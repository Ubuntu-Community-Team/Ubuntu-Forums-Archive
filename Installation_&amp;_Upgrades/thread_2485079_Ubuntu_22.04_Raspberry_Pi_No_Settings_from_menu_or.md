### Post by MAFoElffen on 2023-03-19
Let's see if I remember the right hot-keys... There is a gliche, where the setting is open, but on a ghost display. If you press <Cntrl><Shift><Right-Arrow> it will toggle to that session. To fix that, in settings, when it comes up, go to Display, and mirror (join) the displays is says you have.
