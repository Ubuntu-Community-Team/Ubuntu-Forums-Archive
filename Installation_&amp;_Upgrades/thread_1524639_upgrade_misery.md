### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-07-05
the upgrade from feisty to 9.10 is not supported.  I would suggest you download 10.04 and burn the ISO file to a CD.  Boot from the CD, but when asked - choose "Try ubuntu without installing".

When it gets to a desktop, browse for your home folder copy any files you want to another hard-drive/USB stick.

Then go ahead and double click "install to disk" icon on the desktop.

After installation, copy back your documents and files from the hard-drive/usb stick.
