### Post by ChameleonDave on 2008-05-03
Just go into Synaptic and install the package called *kde*.  Then use KDE from then on.  In the KDE menus you will see a few GNOME apps still hanging around.  If you don't find them useful, remove them in Synaptic.

Unless you have a tiny hard-drive, there is no great hurry to clean every trace of GNOME from your system.
