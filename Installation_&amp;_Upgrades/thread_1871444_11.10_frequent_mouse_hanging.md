### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-10-29
I have installed 11.10 on 3 systems (desktop, netbook and laptop).

I saw this behavior only on the HP laptop.  In my case it happened when the update manager ran (until I got caught up on the updates, then that stopped it hanging there).

I hung one more time when running 'software center', but just the once.

To un-stick the mouse it, I used CNTL-ALT-DEL and logged out and back in.

So far it has not occurred again, but the HP laptop has only been on 11.10 for one day.

If you have not completed all your updates for 11.10, please do so and let us know if that helps at all.
