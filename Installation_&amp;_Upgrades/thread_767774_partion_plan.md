### Post by Pumalite on 2008-04-25
You don't need 8 GB of /swap. With 4 GB of RAM you'll be fine with 1 GB.
First:
15 GB for '/'
1 GB for /swap
The rest for /home.
Install Ubuntu, go 'Manual' and use the prepared partitions.

Good luck.
