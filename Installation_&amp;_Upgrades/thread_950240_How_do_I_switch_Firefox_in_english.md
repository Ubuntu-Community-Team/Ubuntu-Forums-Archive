### Post by spawn. on 2008-10-16
Open Firefox, Go to 2nd Menu (should read Edit)
Scroll to the bottom and click the last option in menu (this should read Preferences)
Go seven options to the right and click the gear icon (this should read Advanced)
Navigate to the third section on the bottom (should read Languages)
and click on the box to the right (should read Choose)
.then there is a scroll-box you can use to select a different language.

-worst case, if your preferred language isn't there, then download it via synaptic.
