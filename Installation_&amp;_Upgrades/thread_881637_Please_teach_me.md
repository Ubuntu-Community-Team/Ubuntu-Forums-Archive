### Post by wpshooter on 2008-08-06
Yogs:

If you have the Live CD version of Ubuntu just put it into your CD drive (after making sure that CD-ROM drive is your first bootable device) and just run the "live session" (as opposed to actually installing it on your hard drive) and see if when you get up to the Ubuntu desktop you can click on Firefox icon in the panel and connect properly to the internet.  If you can you are petty much on your way to using Ubuntu.  Then you just need to decide if you actually want to install it on your hard drive.  Please note that the performance, etc. of running Ubuntu from the CD is NOT necessarily a good indicator of how it will run/perform when actually installed on your hard drive.

Good luck.
