### Post by Pumalite on 2008-03-29
Better have a good backup of everything, including: /boot/grub/menu.lst; /etc/fstab; and /etc/X11/xorg.conf. And don't try to upgrade until 8.04 is a Final Release on April 24th. Besides that; make sure you eliminate all third parties from /etc/apt/sources.list.
