### Post by cogier on 2010-03-24
In your situation (no internet access) I would wait until 10.04 is released and avoid the beta versions. You can then check at ubuntu.com to see if there are any issues regarding upgrading v fresh install. For example if you upgraded from 9.04 to 9.10 then you will not have the ext4 file system that a clean install would have provided.

Wait to avoid disappointment.

### Post by mkvnmtr on 2010-03-24
I have been through quite a few upgrades. I would recomend that you back up your home directory, download and burn 10.04 and then if you wish you can give an in place upgrade a try. You will have the disk to fall back on if the upgrade does not work. Sometimes I have had an upgrade work great and sometimes not. 
I would wait until after it is realesed. You do not want the problems that go with fixing a beta.
