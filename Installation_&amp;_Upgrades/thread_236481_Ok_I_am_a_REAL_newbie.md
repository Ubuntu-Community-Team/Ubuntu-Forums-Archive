### Post by confused57 on 2006-08-14
You may need to go into your Bios setup menu during bootup and change the CD drive to boot first...it may flash up on your screen during startup, which key to press to enter bios.  Usually, it's Del, F2, F12, etc...you may need to try different keys(only one at a time).  There may be a temporary boot option menu that can be accessed during startup, with my Dell it's F12.
