### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-04-19
The two Unities are called the same, but they are, in fact, very different. The 10.10 Unity was based on Mutter, which is Gnome3's new window manager, whereas the 11.04 Unity is based on Compiz. That said, both require 3d, and if your graphics card can't handle it, neither will work.
