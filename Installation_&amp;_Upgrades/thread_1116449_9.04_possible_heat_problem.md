### Post by redfox7691 on 2009-05-02
If you download and install from

[https://launchpad.net/~tormodvolden/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~tormodvolden/+archive/ppa)

latest xserver-xorg-video-ati and xserver-xorg-video-radeon you can use two option in your /etc/X11/xorg.conf

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Configured Video Device"
	Option		"ClockGating"		"true"
	Option		"DynamicPM"		"true"
EndSection

It's work very well for my X1600
