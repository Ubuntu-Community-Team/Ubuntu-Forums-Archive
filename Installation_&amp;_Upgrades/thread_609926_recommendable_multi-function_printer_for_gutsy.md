### Post by tubasoldier on 2007-11-11
Both HP and Brother make multifuntion printers that work well with linux. They both also have a web page about linux printing. Brother's drivers are not open source, however, they do provide packages. Also do you need color printing? If not I would reccomend a laserjet.

Here is a brother MFC-7820N from tigerdirect.
[http://www.tigerdirect.com/applications/SearchTools/item-details.asp?EdpNo=1284431&CatId=2713](http://www.tigerdirect.com/applications/SearchTools/item-details.asp?EdpNo=1284431&CatId=2713)
