### Post by presence1960 on 2009-06-13
Did you MD5SUM the iso after downloading? Go here to learn how to MD5SUM [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM) . check the MD5SUM results against the hashes here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes)
Your MD5SUM must match the hash exactly , if not your iso is corrupted!!
If your MD5SUM is perfect then :

I would try burning another CD : use a CD-R, burn it at the slowest possible speed (i.e. 4x). 

If you still have a problem try the alternate text based installer. You can get it here : [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors#alternate](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors#alternate)
