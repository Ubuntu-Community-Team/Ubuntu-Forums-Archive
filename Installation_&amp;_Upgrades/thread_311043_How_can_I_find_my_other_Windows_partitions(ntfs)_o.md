### Post by LLRNR on 2006-12-02
Hi and welcome !

Usually you don't get your Ubuntu partition from within Windows... it might be a little tricky.

Still you'll be able to access your Windows partition from within Ubuntu. If it's NTFS as you say, then you'll (*normally*) have read-only access to it. Anyway, you have to [mount it first](http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/mountwindows).

For any other issues, please see [this first](http://psychocats.net/ubuntu).
