### Post by Partyboi2 on 2009-04-26
hi, you can remove the jaunty install you don't want by going into gparted (System>Admin>Partition Editor) and deleting it, just make sure you know the correct one to delete. 
To upgrade to jaunty make sure you have the [[COLOR=Blue]alternate cd[/COLOR]]("http://noncdn.releases.ubuntu.com/jaunty/") to upgrade as the live cd can not be used to upgrade.
