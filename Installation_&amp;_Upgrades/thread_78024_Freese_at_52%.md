### Post by aysiu on 2005-10-17
When it freezes during install, it's one of the following problems:

1. ISO corrupted during download (did you check the MD5 Sum?)
2. ISO corrupted during burn (did you burn at too fast a speed?)
3. Bad hardware (CD-ROM not working)
