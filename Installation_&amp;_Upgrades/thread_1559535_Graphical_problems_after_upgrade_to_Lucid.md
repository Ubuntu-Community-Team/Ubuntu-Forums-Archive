### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-08-23
The setting of "None" in the appearance dialog should stick after a boot up, but to make sure, go to System ->Preferences ->Startup Applications and remove Compiz from the list, or at least remove the tick from the item.

It may be that your gf needs to install a better graphic card driver, so tell her to go to System ->Administration ->Hardware Drivers and see if there is anything available there for her to use, then try using compiz again.
