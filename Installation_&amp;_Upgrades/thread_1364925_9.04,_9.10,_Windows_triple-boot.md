### Post by Objekt on 2009-12-27
I was feeling adventurous, so I went ahead and tried it.

I was quite pleased that the Ubuntu 9.10 installer offered me the choice of whether or not to modify the bootloader on the first hard drive, which is where 2 of my existing 3 OS's were installed.  I went ahead and upgraded to GRUB 2 for everything.

I can still boot Kubuntu 9.04 if I want.  Happily, Ubuntu 9.10 is working just fine so far.  I haven't yet had the sound mysteriously stop functioning.  Some things work better than in Kubuntu 9.04.  Mouse input lag in Urban Terror is gone!

I'll probably move to Ubuntu 9.10 as my main OS in the next few days, assuming I don't have a repeat of the mysterious breakages that happened in Kubuntu 9.10, even with a clean install.
