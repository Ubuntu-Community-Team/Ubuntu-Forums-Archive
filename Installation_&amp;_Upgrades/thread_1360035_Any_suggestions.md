### Post by Cheesemill on 2009-12-20
> **red33m said:**
> also how do I change between gnome and KDE on the startup?

You can't switch between your dual boot installs on the login screen, you need to either install the ubuntu-desktop package into your Kubuntu or install the kubuntu-desktop package on to your Ubuntu install and then you'll get the option when you login.
