### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-01-10
Your specs are just fine for Ubuntu. I had to ask, because sometime, people try running live cd on P2 with 64MB RAM, and nothing happens, just like in your case.
With that out of the way, I'd recommend checking the downloaded iso file for errors. If that's ok, reburning at the slowest speed available might help.

### Post by SlowYaRoll on 2010-01-11
I actually have the same computer.  Despite the notorious power connector issues, it's a pretty good machine.  I must admit, when I installed 9.10 on the laptop, after selecting the Install option, it did take a while before it proceeded to the next step.
