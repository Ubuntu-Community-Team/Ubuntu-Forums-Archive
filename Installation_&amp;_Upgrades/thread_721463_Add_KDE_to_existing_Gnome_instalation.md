### Post by Vadi on 2008-03-11
When logging in, click on "session", and change it to KDE. You'll login into a full kde desktop.

### Post by aysiu on 2008-03-11
*kde-core* gives you barebones KDE.

*kubuntu-desktop* gives you Kubuntu's set of default KDE applications.

*kde* gives you just about every KDE application under the sun.
