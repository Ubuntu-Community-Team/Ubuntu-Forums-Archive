### Post by Paddy Landau on 2020-07-27
I'm sorry, I can't answer your question, but I've spotted what could be critical errors in your code: You aren't quoting your variables!

Add single quotes around the variable here:
```
echo "partmon-crypto passphrase password '$PASS'" |debconf-set-selections
```
But if the user enters a passphrase containing one or more single quotes, you will still have problems. You have to take great care with variables when piping like this.

And, did you really mean [FONT=lucida console]partmon-crypto[/FONT] instead of [FONT=lucida console]partman-crypto[/FONT]?

Add double-quotes here:
```
debconf-set partman-crypto/passphrase "$PASS"
```
I hope that you manage to figure out your problem.
