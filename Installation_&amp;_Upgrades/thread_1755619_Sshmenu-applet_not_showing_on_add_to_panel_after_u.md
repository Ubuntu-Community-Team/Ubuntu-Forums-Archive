### Post by gmclean on 2011-05-18
Two possibilities spring to mind:

1. Do you have the 'sshmenu-gnome' package installed?  The 'sshmenu' package provides the standalone tool but not the applet.

2. After installing the 'sshmenu-gnome' package, you may need to log out and back in before the SSHMenu option appears on the list of available applets.
