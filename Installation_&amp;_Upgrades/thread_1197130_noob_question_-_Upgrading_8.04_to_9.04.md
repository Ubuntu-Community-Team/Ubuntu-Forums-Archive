### Post by Sub101 on 2009-06-25
First go to System ==> Software Sources, and change the Release Upgrade in the Updates tab to Normal.

Bear in mind you will need to update to 8.10, then to 9.04. Depending on your system it may be quicker to just reinstall from a 9.04 disc.
