### Post by fela on 2008-03-25
don't bother setting up anything off a liveCD. When you reboot all your changes will be lost (as it uses the RAM like the harddisk, and the RAM gets erased on shutdown).

I have managed to get email setup on a LiveCD, but i'm just telling you not to bother, and to do all of that stuff once you get it installed to your hard disk.
