### Post by ZALMAN on 2008-04-13
That refers to you existing XP (ntfs) partition, your free space will in the next few steps become your ext3 and swap partitions. Leave yourself enough space to install additional programs a good safe size 10G - ext3 and swap 1 to 2  Gig. You'll enjoy Ubuntu.
Have a good day.
