The OS itself takes up 1.8 gigs when installed.   You will also get a swap partition which will be something like twice your ram.  You should also count on leaving a gig of free space so that the filesystem does not fill up and slow your computer down.  So, a minimum of 4 gigs is okay.  It all depends on what you are going to do with it.  If you are going to download DVDs over bittorrent, then your drive will fill up.  If you are just going to browse the web and read email, your drive won't fill up that much.
