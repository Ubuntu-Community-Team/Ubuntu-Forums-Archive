### Post by searchfgold6789 on 2010-11-14
If you installed Ubuntu from within Windows, I think all you have to do is uninstall it just like any other normal program.

If you did all of the partitioning and a full install, you will have to remove the Ubuntu extended partition, and then rewrite the Windows MBR using the Vista CD. (otherwise known as "Fix Boot problems")
I would do the partition deletion from within Vista, rather than from Ubuntu or from he Live CD.
