### Post by solitaire on 2008-05-12
They should be in "/Documents and Settings/<username>/" on the partition that has Win XP. You may have to mount the XP partition (though it should see this and mount it automatically i think.)

In Ubunti go to "Places" then "Computer" you should see 3-4 tiles there: 

Floppy, 
CD/DVD Rom, 
Filesystem 
and your 
XP partition.

in the XP bit browse to "/Documents and Settings/<username>/" then copy/paste the "My Documents" to your Home folder..
