### Post by The-Don on 2009-06-29
Usually, with major updates (kernel / OS) the drivers on your system would therefore be configured for the earlier kernel.

To fix, I can think of two options (I'm a noob btw):

[LIST=1]
[*]Reinstall all the drivers, compiling them with your new kernel;
[*]Backup your files (go to grub menu > netroot > login > backup). Then install Ubuntu 9.04 skipping 8.10.
[/LIST]
