Under 20.04, it appears that once I Lock the screen, my open accounts in Pidgin get cut.  I get this message:  **"The account has disconnected and you are no longer in this chat. You will automatically rejoin the chat when the account reconnects."** So no new posts to my IRC channels are recorded.

Additional data (Dec 05 2020):  This problem is not limited to Pidgin.  About 15 minutes after Screen Lock, **any** Internet connection is severed.  For example, something as simple as *ping* is severed.

Is this expected behavior in 20.04?

Is there anyway I can restore the previous interaction between Lock and my IRC client?
