### Post by jgcobra on 2011-10-13
I think it is due to the servers being overloaded. You can try downloading from one of the mirrors, which seems to be working for me. 

Try this:

1) Go to Update Manager.
2) Click on 'Settings' at the bottom left.
3) Go to the 'Ubuntu Software' tab on the settings dialog that appears.
4) Click on the 'Download from' dropdown box and select 'Other...'
5) On the dialog that appears, click 'Select Best Server'.
6) It will scan all the servers for the fastest. When it has finished, accept it and try the upgrade again.

Reply to this thread if it works for you.
