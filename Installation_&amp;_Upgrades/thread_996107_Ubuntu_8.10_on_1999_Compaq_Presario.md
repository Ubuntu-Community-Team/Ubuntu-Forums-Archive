### Post by Coreigh on 2008-11-28
I think the cutoff message refers to the ACPI or power management functions.

You can add "acpi=off" to the boot parameter (F6 I think) when booting from the CD

do some googling for apci=off and noacpi and ubuntu and you should find many ubuntuforum threads that discuss options.
