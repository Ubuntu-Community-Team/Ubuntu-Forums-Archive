Just wanted to pass along what might be an issue I had with upgrading to 9.10.  I had 9.04 Jaunty installed and had it set up pretty well and then did the upgrade to 9.10 Karmac Koala but I had not installed Wine in my 9.04 Jaunty version.  I had not had Wine installed on my 9.04.  After I upgraded to 9.10 Karmac Koala I had issues and could not run Steam, DOD or Counter Strike in WINE.  I went back and started over, reinstalled 9.04 Jaunty and then Installed Wine for 9.04 Jaunty then, set up everything the way I liked it.  Everything was working great.  I could run Steam, Day of Defeat, Counter Strike then I upgraded to 9.10 Karmac Koala and everything works great.
