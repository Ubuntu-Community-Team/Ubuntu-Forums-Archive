### Post by Slim Odds on 2008-05-03
I'm on a mission to terminate the "low speed burn" myth.

If you can't burn a higher speeds, there is something wrong with your computer. If that is the case, burning at low speeds is probably going to fail also.

Almost every CD/DVD burning program has a "verify after burn" option, use it.
