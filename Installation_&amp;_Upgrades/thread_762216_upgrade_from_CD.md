### Post by Partyboi2 on 2009-02-21
If you currently have 7.04 installed (which has reached its eol) you will need to upgrade to 7.10 first
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GutsyUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GutsyUpgrades)
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades)
Then you can follow the link that overdrank posted and upgrade to hardy. Once you have upgraded to hardy you can follow those some steps as you did for the hardy upgrade to get to Intrepid. Or you can remain with hardy which is a long term release version.
