### Post by QIII on 2012-06-20
It's a scary warning the first time around.

Easy to set your own mind at ease, though.  Check the size of the disk you are installing to.  It will be the size of the virtual disk you just created, which will be smaller than your physical disk.  Compare the sizes.  

If you are still not convinced, post the sizes and we'll take a look.
