### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-02-01
There is nothing shown because, basically, there is nothing to install anymore.  ATI dropped support for your card back in May of 2009.  There are no ATI drivers that work with your card and Ubuntu 9.10.

You will need to install the open source drivers instead.  Instructions are in the link below:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver")
