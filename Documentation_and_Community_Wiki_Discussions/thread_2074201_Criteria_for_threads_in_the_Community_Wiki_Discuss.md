### Post by Elfy on 2012-10-21
This section is purely for threads _discussing_ community wikis. 

Title should link to the wiki url.

Support for the wiki subject should take place in normal support forums.

Support posts will be moved from here to support sub-forums.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community](https://help.ubuntu.com/community)

Please note that all new threads are moderated. That is, they are not visible until approved by a member of the moderation team.
