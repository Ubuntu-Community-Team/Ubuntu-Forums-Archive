**Why Virtualization?**

- Increase your hardware utilization
- Save of electricity and reduce Carbon Dioxide
- Reduced physical hardware and fewer maintenacne personnel is required
- Simple backup and restore operations

A virtualization featured CPU (such as Intel VT or AMD-V) is required for the full virtualization by Kernel-based Virtual Machine (KVM).  Please check the CPU vendors website to confirm.

Proxmox VE has a control panel for easy deployment and installation.  Proxmox VE is Open Sourced.  I prepared a tutorial for how to install it at ease.

[The tutorial is here.]("http://samiux.blogspot.com/2010/11/howto-virtualization-platform-by.html")
