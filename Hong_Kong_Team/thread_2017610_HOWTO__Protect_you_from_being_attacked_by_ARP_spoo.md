Hi all,

One of the threats in computer security is ARP spoofing.  ARP spoofing is a kind of Man-in-the-Middle (MiTM) attack.

This attack can be performed via wireless and wired network.  It poisoned the gateway or/and router in order to get your valuable data even it is using SSL (HTTPS).

How this attack works and how to prevent from being attacked?  You can visit this [link]("http://samiux.blogspot.hk/2012/06/howto-protect-you-from-being-arp.html") for details.
