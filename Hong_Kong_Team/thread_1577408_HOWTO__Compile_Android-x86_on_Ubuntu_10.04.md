### Post by samiux on 2010-09-18
Mr C.W. HUANG (of Taiwan) is working on Android-x86 project which ports Android to x86 hardwares.  Up to now, the Android-x86 is working fine on ASUS EeePC as Mr HUANG is staff of ASUS.  The Android 2.2 (Froyo) for Android-x86 is under heavy development.

If you are interested in this project, you may eager to compile from the source yourself in order to suit your hardwares.  I have a Gigabyte TouchNote T1028X which is equipped with eGalax touchscreen and Intel Atom CPU.  This piece of hardware is ideal for me to test this project.

The tutorial is [here]("http://samiux.blogspot.com/2010/09/howto-compiling-android-x86-on-ubuntu.html").
