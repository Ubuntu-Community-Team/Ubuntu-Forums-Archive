### Post by samiux on 2010-08-01
Listening your favourite songs that hosting at your Ubuntu server or desktop (or even on FreeNAS / NAS) on the road is your dream.

If you have a Nexus One or Android 1.5+ smartphone (or even iPhone), your dream comes true with Firefly (mt-daapd) and Ubuntu desktop/server.

It also can share your songs over your network with Rhythmbox or Banshee (or even iTune) on any desktop.

Please follows the instructions [here]("http://samiux.blogspot.com/2010/07/howto-streaming-music-from-ubuntu-1004.html") to make this comes true.

Enjoy!
