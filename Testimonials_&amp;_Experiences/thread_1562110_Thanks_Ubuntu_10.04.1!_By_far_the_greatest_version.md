### Post by Spydr4590 on 2010-08-27
I have to say Ubuntu has come along way! I love it! My fiancée recently got a virus on windows 7. A very nasty one that would not even let me go into save mode, restore, or run any anti virus.

Instead of installing windows I decided to install Ubuntu 10.04.1. It automatically installed the drivers for everything including the wireless usb card I gave her. I am by far impressed. I´m also impressed that you can now automatically install the latest nvidia driver automatically also. Compiz fusion was a breeze also. Including wine. The only thing I told her not to do was run sudo wine. ;)

I haven´t used Ubuntu much since version 8. I wasn´t impressed with version 9 at all.  Thanks for making a great os guys and keep up the great work. You saved me lots of future headaches ;)
