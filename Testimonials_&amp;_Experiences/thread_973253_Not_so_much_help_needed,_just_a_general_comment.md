### Post by Dave54 on 2008-11-06
It looks like you're having hardware compatability issues. You think, "If Windows supports my printer/computer/monitor/GPU, why doesn't Ubuntu?" Well, here's the answer: Windows DOES NOT support your hardware. It's your hardware that supports Windows! Don't believe me? Go over to your computer, and take a good look at it. On the front, top, side or even back, you'll find a little sticker that says "Designed for Microsoft Windows XP" or Vista, or something to that effect.

So, when you buy hardware with a little sticker that says "Designed for Ubuntu," you'll be able to plug in your printer and have it be configured automatically.
