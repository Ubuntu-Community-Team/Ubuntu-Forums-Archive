### Post by Cavsfan on 2010-07-08
I just ran Bleachbit (in terminal "sudo bleachbit"), not root. I had every thing checked except Deep Scan - Backup files, 
Firefox - Passwords and places, Flash - cookies (I have Better Privacy as an add-on to Mozilla for that and control what
is deleted). And System - Memory.
That was the settings I had and it cleaned 1.47GB and 1187 files. It overwrote the free space and everything.

So, now my system is just taking up 14.3GB! I just keep my language too, which frees up more room. I dual boot windows 7
and set aside 100GB for Ubuntu and I'm loving it!

One thing I absolutely love about Ubuntu is the fact that I just have to move my mouse over whatever window I choose
and can use the middle mouse wheel to scroll up and down! I can move my mouse from one window to another and it works!
Windows 7 cannot even do that! In windows 7, you have to left click first and then you can use use the wheel.

Windows sux! Ubuntu rulz! :guitar:
