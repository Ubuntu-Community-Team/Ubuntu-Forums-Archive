### Post by Achetar on 2008-08-09
Hey, this testimonial isn't about me, but about some friends of mine.

Anyway, their Mom got a new laptop running windows vista. They tried it out...and absolutely hated it. They had been total M$FT PWNS ALL before that. A few weeks ago I showed the Slax running from a USB drive. They had asked me to prove that Windows was really less secure than Linux. I got all of their passwords in under 45 seconds (and then ran a cracker on the Slax passwords, took it 12 minutes to guess the same passwords). 6 passwords total.

I am going to take a couple Ubuntu CDs next time I see them (after Intrepid comes out anyway, Kubuntu w/KDE 4.1 and Ubuntu).

They have both tried Ubuntu and Arch on my laptop and were amazed how much faster it ran than Windows XP, even with Compiz transparencies on (no wobbly windows though).

One of them has even gotten somewhat comfortable with the CLI.

Wish me luck and thanks for all of your hard work on Ubuntu!!!
