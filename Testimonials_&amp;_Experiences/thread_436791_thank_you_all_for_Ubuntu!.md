### Post by rose34 on 2007-05-08
Hi everyone,

Don't know if anyone will read this, seeing as there are numerous threads with similar titles, but I feel I should thank everyone who has had an input into the development of this marvelous operating system!

I am a student at university on course that has nothing to do with computing, and due to my limited student budget I brought a cheap system that was on sale for £250. This is a fairly basic system for a new computer, and came with XP installed (brought in October). I use this for word processing, internet, email and playing music.

After a couple of months, I noticed XP getting slower and slower, so decided to do something I've wanted to for a while and try linux. I downloaded Ubuntu, and was amazed! Everything worked fine, apart from my wireless connection which I had fixed in a day thanks to this forum. 

Ubuntu is now the only OS on my hard drive, and I have not had any problems since. So once again, thanks to you all for developing this system which someone who knows very little about computers can get running with hardly any trouble!

Joe
