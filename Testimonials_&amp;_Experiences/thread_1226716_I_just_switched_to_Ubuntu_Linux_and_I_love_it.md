### Post by EM man on 2009-07-29
It has been 100% trouble-free for the last couple days.  I was planning on making the switch after buying the laptop that I bought last weekend; my old computer has Windows XP and I bought it five years ago.  

Everything is streamlined, logical, and convenient so far.  I can do everything that I did in Windows, except for a small set of things that I was able to do with Yahoo Messenger.  The bootup time is very low, and applications actually open within a reasonable amount of time, rather than a significant fraction of a minute.  Garbage does not accumulate.  I am not pestered left and right with insipid messages from the OS, nor am I annoyed by popups.

The Windows-only applications that I am going to miss out on constitute a small price for all of these benefits.  People who continue to love Windows despite its absolutely wretched characteristics are like battered wives who continue to live with and love their husbands.
