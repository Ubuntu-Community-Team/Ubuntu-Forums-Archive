### Post by viking777 on 2007-08-28
I couldn't agree more. I am doing what they say you shouldn't do and using it as my main day to day operating system (although I have plenty of alternatives should it all go wrong). I have never seen a system that can accept maybe 50+ updates/day and not end up being trashed. Any issues that I do have with it are usually kernel related (like shutdown-which doesn't) but they apply to every version of Linux out there, Gutsy is way ahead of anything else I have tried (and I have tried plenty). The only thing it doesn't do well (on my system that is) is 3D effects (and PCLinuxOS works well in that respect). However I regard these as a complete and utter waste of time and effort on behalf of both users and developers, so I really don't care if they never work. So overall I am supremely impressed with Kubuntu Gutsy Gibbon.
