### Post by tsanoff on 2006-09-13
Hey I am writing this post just because I am finaly free of the windoze sickness. I downloaded and installed ubuntu about a month ago. I have played with diffrent distros of linux over the years but there was always something missing, and BAM i found ubuntu. I couldn't be happier!!! The best OS i have ever used. Now when I touch a windoze machine i feel so bad for it, knowing it is bound to crash. Its funny i was setting up a server for a friend, he wanted windoze 2000 adv serv, it took me more then 4 hrs to reformat drives, install system and find drivers, wasted a whole day. I came back home and deleted all my windoze partitions and now i am microsoft free. Ubuntu took me 30 mins to install and download all needed software. UBUNTU is awsome i love this freakin distro. I just wanted to thank all the developers, Mark Shuttleworth, and anyone else who has contributed to the development of ubuntu, and debian. Keep up the great work, the world needs you!!!
