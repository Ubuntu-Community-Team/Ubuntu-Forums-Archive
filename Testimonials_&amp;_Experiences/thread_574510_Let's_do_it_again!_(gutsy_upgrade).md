### Post by LT1Caprice57L on 2007-10-12
OK, so I'm an impatient bloke, seeing that countdown thing on ubuntu.com did me in by "07 days to go" so I folded and upgraded to the beta.

Flawless upgrade, aside from one of my GTK themes going on the mad, and the bloody thing reverting the background back to brown during login.  Fixed the latter issue quite quickly and easily with a search on here.

I noticed right off the bat that it's faster, font rendering is better, everything makes more sense and GNOME 2.18's schizophrenic-size taskbar buttons (this bothered the snot out of me) are all but gone.  LOVE the new Appearance panel, and all similar regroupings, etc.

I was shocked to find that desktop effects works 90% on my shitty old ATI Radeon IGP345M chipset w/ shared memory...only things that don't work are blur effect and water effect, but that's ATI's fault, not Ubuntu's or Compiz's.

I haven't even found a real issue.  "Beta" my butt! :biggrin:

So, yep, that's another BIG thumbs up guys!
