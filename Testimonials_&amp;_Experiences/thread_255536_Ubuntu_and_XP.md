### Post by dmpo5450 on 2006-09-11
I've been using Ubuntu for about a week now.  Been a Microsoft user for years.  From MS DOS through XP.  Like XP and never had any problems with it.    Tried Linux a few years ago (I think it was 6. something) but never could get it to work because of driver problems.  Did try again until now because I was put off by some of the attitudes shown by Linux users in some of the general computer forums I read.  A co worker told me about Ubuntu and afer a little research tried the live CD.  After one question was answered in these forums about partioning it took less than 20 minutes to have a dual boot Ubuntu/XP up and surfing the internet.  I do a lot of DVD creations and transfers.  Linux just doesn't have the software to do what I need to do.  Other than that I pretty much just use Ubuntu.  As a newbe I've had a lot of questions but by researching the wealth of "How Tos" and searching these forums, so far I've found answers.  Still trying to figure out how to get my ATI TV tuner to work but figure I'll find that answer too.  Just want to thank all the good people who answer the questions from us newbes.  Would recommend Ubuntu to anyone wanting to try Linux.
