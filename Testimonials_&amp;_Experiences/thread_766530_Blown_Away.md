### Post by ace007 on 2008-04-25
The Wubi installer is great for getting beginners on their way. however you will receive better performance while in Ubuntu if you do a traditional install, and not use Wubi.  Wubi is usually recommended as an initial way of installing, but once the user is convinced they would like to keep Ubuntu, they are recommended to do a traditional install.  

Also, from Wubi FAQ

> 
What is the performance?

The performance is identical to a standard installation, except for hard-disk access which is slightly slower than an installation to a dedicated partition. If your hard disk is very fragmented the performance will degenerate.
