### Post by talrasha on 2006-09-24
Thank you my Lord for giving/spreading humanity among your nations :p

I'm now 2 weeks old Ubuntu/Linux happy user. Thanks for the help of a friend, Jed, for helping me holding on to continue configuring my printer and modem drivers and now I'm online and ready to explore the world of humanities and share my spirit to everyone.

At first I thought that it's hard to install a printer in Ubuntu coz I didn't saw my printer listed in the supported printers of Ubuntu but my friend advised me to try using the Add printer functionality of Ubuntu so I did and it works! No sweat, no frustrations, and no downloads, it just works!

Second, installing modem driver. I'm afraid bec when I run the auto-detect modem functionality of Ubuntu my modem was not detected. I was alarmed coz not all pc users has a luxury of having a DSL/Broadband connection... like me, still depending on the bandwidth given by my modem and my favorite ISP. But thanks to the people of Ubuntu's Wiki DialupModemHowto guide and in no time I'm accessing this forum using my modem sitting above the throne of Ubuntu!

God bless all and have a nice journey bringing "Humanity to Others" a.k.a simply UBUNTU :cool:
