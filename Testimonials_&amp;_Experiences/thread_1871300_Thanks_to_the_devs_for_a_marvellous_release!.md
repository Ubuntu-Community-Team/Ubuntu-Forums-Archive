### Post by BigSilly on 2011-10-28
Just wanted to post up to thank and congratulate the Ubuntu team and everyone involved for such a fantastic release. 11.10 has so far been excellent for me, with no problems or hitches to spoil things. The big story is Unity imho, which is now really starting to get into its stride. It's been nothing less than a joy for me, and works exactly as it is claimed to. I may have been lucky considering some of the negativity around here, but it has slipped easily into my own desktop usage without any hiccups or difficulties. I'd been using Gnome 3 before Unity on a different distro, but Unity has sufficiently wowed me.

So it's nice to be back using Ubuntu, and even nicer to see that it is developing so sweetly. I had such problems with Natty that I had to switch distros, but 11.10 has comfortably won me back over and Ubuntu T shirts will be bought this weekend!

So, in the face of constant and now very boring complaining, I doff my cap to the team for what is a pretty amazing free operating system that should start to get even more interest all over the world. I hope it does anyway. It deserves to.
