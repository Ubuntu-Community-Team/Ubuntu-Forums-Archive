### Post by z.s.tar.gz on 2008-11-09
I guarantee you can read this whole post in less than 1 minute. (or should be able to)

**Ubuntu** - Works great, all hardware supported, relatively fast, decent theme.

**OpenSuse** - Looks good but is too slow and not enough hardware support.

**Fedora** - Pretty good theme and fast, but forget about restricted anything.

**Debian** - Debian as a distro is dead. Don't even kid yourself.

**Mandriva** - It was alright I guess. I didn't test mandriva very extensively.

**Mint** - Haven't tested yet. Looks good though. Downloading now.

**PcLinuxOS** - Looks good, is fast, a few issues with hardware though.

**Mepis** - The best hardware support, fast, theme needs update though.

Don't like what I said? Post argument w/ source then!


I'm getting around to writing a full review of each w/ a chart.
