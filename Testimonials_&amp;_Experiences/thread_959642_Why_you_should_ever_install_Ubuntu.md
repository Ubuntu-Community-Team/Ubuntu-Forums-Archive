### Post by utnubuuser on 2008-10-26
:guitar:I discovered Ubuntu a year and a half ago.  (Never even considered Linux before that).
At first I installed it only on my desktop PC, then, with trepidation, on my IBM Thinkpad.
At 7.04, almost everything worked wonderfully.
At 7.10 there were a bunch of regressions, so I went back to 7.04
At 8.04, everything is groovy, and 8.10rc is as if it were factory installed.

I've installed and messed with and re-installed this OS at least 50 times since I've found out about it, and I absolutely love it.

Please don't change a thing.   -Just keep making it better, (and maybe slow down a little, this rapid-fire release cycle is dizzying).

PS -I like the Human theme too. 
It really is Linux for human beings.
