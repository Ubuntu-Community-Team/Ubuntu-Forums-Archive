### Post by Dumpen on 2009-11-27
Hello all

Well I have been on Ubuntu for a few weeks now, and at first I was very impressed since the last version I used was 6.06 (As I recall).

It all worked and I just happy about it, I found new great programs and the slick design of Ubuntu is great.. But after that I wanted to try something I did not look forward to: Gaming in Ubuntu

Since im a casual gamer I did not require alot, only that Counter-Strike:Source worked and I had a Ventrilo client (I found [http://mangler.org](http://mangler.org) to be a great alternative to Ventrilo)

I tried using wine for probably a good 8 to 10 hours overall, but it wouldn just do. So now im saying goodbye to Ubuntu and hello to Windows.

Im probably going to miss Ubuntus design, easy installing/removing of programs and lots more, but for now I am (or Ubuntu is?) not ready.

I really hope popular games will support Ubuntu and Linux in general since I do see it as a Valve/Steam (And the game community) problem rather than Ubuntu.

Greetings Casper
