### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-08-04
Windows XP generates thumbnails every time, whereas Ubuntu generates thumbnails and saves them to disk ONCE; then the thumbnails are read in subsequent times. More efficient, and once the thumbnails are made it's quicker.

If your netbook uses one of those dreadful SSDs, then the initial thumbnailing step will be quite slow simply because the SSDs used in netbooks have very bad latency and low write speeds.

This does not indicate that you should use Windows XP on your netbook, or on ANY computer for that matter.
