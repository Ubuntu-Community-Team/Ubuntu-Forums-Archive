### Post by Huw on 2006-07-12
When I first decided I was going to try out Linux, everyone said "use Ubuntu!  *Use Ubuntu!*"  Naturally, I avoided it like the plague.  For the last six months I've tried out loads of distros, each having their own good points and bad points.

I settled on Fedora Core 5 for the longest time, and to this day I think it's a superb distro, but after expressing my distaste for yum and a few other things, someone in my LUG talked me into trying Ubuntu.  I've been using it for a few days and although I tried my best not to like it, I just can't help it.  It takes all the slickness and polish of FC5 and marries it with Debian repositories, a *decent* package manager, and what seems to be a *great* community.  I just can't help it...I think I'm here to stay.

So...hullo! :)
