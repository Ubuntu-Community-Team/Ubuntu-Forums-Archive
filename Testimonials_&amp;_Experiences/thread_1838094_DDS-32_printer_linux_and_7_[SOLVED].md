### Post by armandh on 2011-09-03
some printers are just not going to have new drivers.
I have acquired 2 real hard working NETWORK printers,
[a DDS-32 and an IBM infoprint 40]
besides dragging them into the basement 
the hard work on my part was finding the drivers.

the last factory supplied DDS-32 driver was a win 2K beta
this worked in xp but nothing since

for Ubuntu a generic postscript, Hatachi DDP70, or Talley ML450 driver will work for the DDS-32  
setting the IP address in the printer in the range the router DHCP likes 
for the LPR port with a postscript Q name is also needed

my son has a windows 7 gaming box
after search for more drivers button is selected
the IBM infoprint 32 PS driver worked for the DDS-32
the IBM infoprint is a close cousin with PS emulation.

may your searches for hardware drivers be short and successful
