### Post by sayakb on 2008-01-30
I couldn't resist myself from posting about my experiences with Ubuntu. To start with, I chose Gutsy 64-bit. To express it in one word, it is: MARVELOUS.
Simply amazing operating system. The customizability, I have made my own themes and icons from misc collections. Compiz-fusion rocks!! No viruses hurray!! I literally DELETE the so called Deadly Windows viruses in Ubuntu with just a keypress.. I use gnome, but KDE4 is excellent, fresh.. Who needs windoze?? I feel free with ubuntu, free to do anything I like. It supports my hardware, extremely smooth graphics it offers me.. simply amazing
Now the scene in my college is that people are approaching me from all branches, almost spellbound, with their mouth open, and dying to install Ubuntu..
Cant wait for Hardy to be released (or is it out already??)

On a final note: I LOVE LINUX
