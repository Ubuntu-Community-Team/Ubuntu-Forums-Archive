### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-05-03
I've been using Ubuntu 10.04 for the last few months and I'm really appreciating it.

It was solid even during the alpha stages, and it's still impressively solid. Also pretty quick.

And unlike previous versions, I haven't yet found any regressions. You know - Karmic had those mobile broadband problems, Jaunty had slow Intel graphics... well Lucid doesn't have any regression problems that I can see.

I still can't get Pitivi to work (maybe I need to add it to my RGBA blacklist), and the Ubuntu One Music Store has eaten half of the album that I downloaded while it was in beta; but otherwise I'm quite happy.
