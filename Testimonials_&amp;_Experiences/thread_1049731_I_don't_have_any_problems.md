### Post by relay_man on 2009-01-24
I new here, and though this seems to be the forum for complaints and difficulties, I have none.
I believe however that everything needs balance, so here goes.

My daughter and I assembled our first new computer about two weeks ago.  
After studying some help info, we figured out how to burn the OS install disc from the download and installed ubuntu on the new machine.  We had no problems.
We are learning about Ubuntu (and Linux in general) a little at a time, and so far everything is going great.  What freedom!
With a little reading and research, we were even able to manage an internet connection using the dreaded static IP.

We are both looking forward to not only learning, but contributing in some way to the community soon.
Many thanks to those patient individuals here who seem never to tire, can answer the same question fifteen times a week without losing their temper, and have a true desire to help us new folks, regardless of the frustration level.:D
