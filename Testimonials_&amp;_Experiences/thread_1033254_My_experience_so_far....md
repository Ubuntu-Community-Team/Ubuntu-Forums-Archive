### Post by Grumpey on 2009-01-07
I got annoyed with Vista and decided to switch to Ubuntu. 

Machine: Gateway p-6831fx
Ubuntu 8.10 intrepid
E-mail Thunderbird 2.0.0.19
Browser Firefox 3.1b2

Likes:
Installation was a breeze, I was able to get a majority of the software installed and configured in less than a day with very few issues.
Having multiple work spaces without installing extra software.
Softlinks Nice to be able to link to pictures and music vice having to point apps to a separate directory. 
Package manager makes it fairly simple to install and remove software with some exceptions.
No issues with hung applications that require a reboot to correct. 
Hurray for shell scripts, I do some work in a Unix environment and it's amazing what can be automated with a little research and a few lines.

Dislikes:
My volume control seems to be more on/off than up/down. I haven't had a great deal of time to look into this.

Minor annoyances:
After login, start-up seems a bit slow. Most likely just some tweaking I need to do.
Sun Java Plugin being associated with Firefox 3.05 package. Easy enough to work around.
Trying to decide which media player/jukebox to use. There are so many good choices .... 
Repositories don't always seem to have the latest versions of software. I believe this is intended and is easy to work around. ((This is me being lazy and wanting to do one click install/updates))
Not being able to sync I-pod touch(Apples problem not Ubuntu's)

Overall it's been a good transition a majority of the issues I had were easily worked out between google and these forums.
