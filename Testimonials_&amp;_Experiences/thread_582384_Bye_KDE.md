### Post by firephoto on 2007-10-19
Compiz is nothing to do with KDE and takes away one of the major benefits or running KDE and that would be kwin so don't try to blame compiz problems on KDE.

64 bit anything doesn't offer advantages unless you're doing processor intensive number crunching or media conversion amongst other non common uses so it's no surprise it doesn't run a standard desktop faster.

The kmenu looks and works fine here so a dependency of compiz probably changed the menu on you. There's been lots of bugs filed about similar things over ubuntu's lifespan and the problem isn't with KDE.
