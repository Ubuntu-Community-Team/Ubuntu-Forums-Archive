### Post by ArtificialSynapse on 2007-04-03
Since probably 4th grade, I've always had an avid fascination with computers, always busy doing something or another. I'd always looked fondly at Linux, and usually had it running on at least one of my computers, but never really made the switch because I'd grown too comfortable with a couple day to day programs on my Windows machine, and was just caught in that nitch that windows created for me. In the past few months however, I finally made the switch, and I've got zero Microsoft on all my different computers. I generally don't like to marvel for too long at any one thing that I've come to enjoy, however I can say with 100% honesty that Ubuntu and Linux have brought me more genuine enjoyment towards computer use since when I first got my old Mac IIsi, it feels like that sentimentality has come back and is here to stay!(I'm sure I could run a copy of linux on that anyways!) I feel like I've been born again into the intrinsic, and fascinating world of computing. 

Thank you Ubuntu community!!! :)  :)

Also, if anyone is interested in chatting, please feel free to message me on IRC, AIM, or E-mail, I'm still new here and need to make some friends! Don't be shy!
