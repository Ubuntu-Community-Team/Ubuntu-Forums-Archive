### Post by bsalt on 2007-12-09
Hey guys, I have a dream. I have always loved to travel, and after visiting Costa Rica - which is not a very poverty-stricken nation - I noticed something. All of their computer stuff comes from other countries. A 512mb Flash drive would cost $40.

I also like teaching people about stuff in general.


Here is my dream.


I think it would be completely awesome if seminars could be hosted in countries like Costa Rica and less fortunate nations. If more people than just Americans, Europeans, and some others knew how to program a computer, use it, run a web page, develop an application, and otherwise use a PC for their benefit, I believe the wealth of those nations would increase. I also do not believe that manufacturers like Intel, AMD, resellers like Dell and HP would oppose such an idea. My dream is to see poverty disappear, and I believe computers can be a great way to see this through.

The reason I am writing this is to share the idea with the world, but also to ask if any of you guys know of such an organization willing to spearhead this cause. I know the governments of most of these poorer nations would be for it.



***“Give a man a fish; you have fed him for today. Teach a man to fish; and you have fed him for a lifetime”***
