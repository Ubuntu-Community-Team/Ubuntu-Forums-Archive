### Post by bruno9779 on 2009-09-11
I am very displeased with Ubuntu Jaunty amd64.

Let's start from the beginning: 
After running Ubuntu on my laptop for 3 years I have decided to build my own box (in my signature) and ordered all the components online. 

After assembling everything carefully I prepared a carafe of coffee and popped ubuntu 64 bit in the cd slot.

Everything worked out of the box. EVERYTHING! All hardware detected and running smoothly!! 
I had to throw that carafe of coffee away and I feel like I have been robbed of a fun night googling for compatibility issues on a 800*600 EGA screen.

Luckily Windows came to my rescue. When I tried installing a copy of XP on a partition (i only need it to update the firmware of my tech toys) It refused to format the empty space I left for it, It refused to write to the MBR 
And although it didn't even get to start the installation my system fan started going at full throttle. It doesn't go like that even when I play Savage 2, Nexuiz or Spore in Wine.

I wasn't able to get to the end of it, and at 5 am I have gone to bed with that familiar WTF feeling.

Windows has saved the day once again...

:lolflag:
