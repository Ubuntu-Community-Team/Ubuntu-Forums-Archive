### Post by jnorthr on 2007-09-08
God bless me, i never thought it would be this easy. My Daughter gave me her old 1GB black NanoPod. Ok, so will my old laptop with brand spanking new ubuntu 7.0.4 be able to cope ? RhythemBox did not want to know. VLC did not have the feature set for it. So i looked here:[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPod?action=show&redirect=IPodHowto#head-3b2c75d25879e7a1bab76d98b54b23143f183fc3](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPod?action=show&redirect=IPodHowto#head-3b2c75d25879e7a1bab76d98b54b23143f183fc3) for some help. And this beauty called gtkPod came to my rescue. Since i keep synaptic open on one of my four virtual desktops, just did a search for gtkpod, did a quick install and presto, Miss Nanopod was connected and working as sweet as could be. Now, i'm gonna have a try adding video to my pod next, dunno if this little black stick can do it but, hey, what the heck, let's give it a go.:KS
