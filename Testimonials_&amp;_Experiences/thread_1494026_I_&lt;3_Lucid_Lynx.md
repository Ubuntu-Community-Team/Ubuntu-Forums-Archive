### Post by Imxset21 on 2010-05-26
Well, first time poster, long time reader, longer (?) time user of Ubuntu.

I tried using Ubuntu seriously on my machine starting with 7.04 (Heron?) but I could never get into it because of lack of driver support, Wine was still full of bugs, installing things was a headache, etc.

However, with Lucid Lynx, I can't stop using Linux! Now that full-blown Wine is supported and with Steam and Netbeans onboard, I'm actually seriously considering starting to do full-time development on Linux rather than going back to my MewMewSoft machine.

And thanks to Ubuntu, this old machine has found new life! Now, I just have to figure out whether it's worth it to upgrade its RAM or get a new one >w>

Thank you to everyone on this forum for always being there with the answers to my dumb-mule questions (e.g. "What's a terminal?" :rolleyes: ).

And thank you to the people who work tirelessly day and night (cough-cough) to keep Ubuntu working and stable :D
