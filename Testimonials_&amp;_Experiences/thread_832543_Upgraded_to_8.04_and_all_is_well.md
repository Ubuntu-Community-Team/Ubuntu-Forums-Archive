I upgraded to 8.04 from 7.10 and the only problem I noticed was firestarter was hosed.  A uninstall and reinstall did not help, Firestarter is like mission impossible it only works once at the moment.

I started with 6.06 LTS right after xmas 07 and have not looked back to Ms since.   

I do not use wireless lan, call me old fashioned but no matter how secure WLAN is it still is not secure to me.  

I upgraded 3 PC's to 8.04 LTS and no problems, in fact on my amd 6000+ pc Open office & totem work all the time.  in 7.10 on some reboots they would cough up an error and quit.

So far so good, thanks Ubuntu Folks for a great OS.
