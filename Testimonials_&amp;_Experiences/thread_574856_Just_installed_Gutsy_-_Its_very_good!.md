### Post by Ultra Magnus on 2007-10-13
I've just installed Gutsy! - All I can say is that its nice when an upgrade really is better than than what you already have!

I have an ati card so I was bracing myself for a few hours of fun trying to get any kind of image on my screen - but it actually worked without any tinkering (well my screen resolution was a bit low (1126x768 - I think) but just enabling the Restricted driver did the trick and that was it! - Even getting the visual effects to work was pretty painless, install xserver-xgl and it was sorted! - (1 thing though, perhapes instead of just saying that visual effects won't work on your computer it could instruct you to download this but warn you that xgl is slow etc)

Feisty was my first proper linux distro - ie the first time I felt I could use Linux exclusively instead of windows but even then I needed to do allot of tinkering to fix little things so I felt a bit loathe to say "Ubuntu is ready for my gran" etc...

... but this time everything works fine and I'm actually a bit disapointed there wasn't more tinkering to do. Barring the old multimedia codec problem, everything "Just Works" TM - This is the best distro I've tried so far!

One disapointment - TimeVault doesn't seem to be included like it was speculated but I suppose that'll be included when its ready

And Another curious thing - the solution to every problem with touchpads seems to be to add SHMconfig "on" to /etc/X11/xorg.conf  - why isn't this already there? - (The first thing I always do is disable the damn tapping)

Overall, Gutsy is brilliant!
