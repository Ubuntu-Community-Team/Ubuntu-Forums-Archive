Two months ago I assembled a computer and it was one of  the most rewarding experience for me.  At the time I didn't have the  money to buy Windows 7 so I went with Ubuntu. In  those two months I have been googling info about Ubuntu and I always  get directed here.  I can find answers here for most of my questions.   So I thank the people that contribute to the forum.  If I have any  questions I'll most likely post it in the forums.

### Post by coldraven on 2013-02-08
Well done and I hope that you get much pleasure from your new rig.

If you like the terminal try using guake (Ubuntu) or yakuake (Kubuntu).
They are both drop down terminals, you just press F12 and you are in business.
You can configure the size, transparency etc. to your taste.
After installing add it to "Startup Applications".

Did i mention that it also looks cool when your friends are looking?  :)
