Well, I was given a copy of Ubuntu 6.06 about a week and a half ago.  Installed it at the Linux SIG of my computer club.
I must say that after trying other flavors of Linux (like Simply Mepis, etc) I have fallen in love with the ease of use of this version of Ubuntu.
One of the things I like is the ease of use of the Sinaptic program for updating, downloading and installing programs.
I downloaded and installed the KDE Desktop program, as I like it better, and nothing could have been any easier!
