### Post by Chicane-UK on 2007-10-26
After booting up my recently installed Ubuntu 7.10 laptop this morning, I just had to come on here and express my congratulations to all those involved in this project for what they have achieved.

I've been using Linux now for about 10 years and despite loving the work that went in, and wholeheartedly agreeing with the ethics behind Linux as a whole, could never really get to grips with it on the desktop for all the common reasons - problems with hardware support, lack of good apps, and the amount effort required to get it doing what I wanted it to do. 

Ubuntu 7.04 was impressive but 7.10 has just blown me away. The visual effects are all working, reliably and smoothly on my Toshiba Tecra M2 (including reflections, wobbly windows, etc etc) and the system is so quick and smooth. And it detected everything out of the box - sound, wifi, video.. i'm just shocked frankly at how effortless the whole thing has been.

Its an astonishing piece of work and I just wanted to congratulate the devs and wish them every success for the future of Ubuntu.. its looking pretty rosy!
