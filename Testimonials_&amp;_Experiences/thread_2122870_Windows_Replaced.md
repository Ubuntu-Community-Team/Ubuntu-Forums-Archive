### Post by DMGrier on 2013-03-06
I have been using Ubuntu since 2008 with Ubuntu 8.04. At the time I was running Windows Vista and I was not very happy about moving to Apple for OS X was not a option for me, I was talking to a guy at work and he told me about Ubuntu and I figured I would give it a try. I installed it on a two year old Dell B130 I had and the only problem was the wifi would not work, at this time though I still needed Windows in my life. From Ubuntu 10.04 on I have been windows free thanks to the advancement in the open source community, Ubuntu, ubuntu software center and Ubuntu One services I have been Windows free and no reason to look back.
