### Post by L815 on 2009-11-05
Just want to stop by and drop that I am very pleased with Karmic. It is much MUCH faster for me compared to previous releases. Everything is running splendidly smooth. I especially like the new icons :D

Secondly, I'm glad there are PPA's :). After having been over at arch, I was spoiled by AUR. I got a bit tired of having to configure everything, and bumped back over to Ubuntu and settled with PPA's. So, awesome.


Only comment I have to add: why is firefox improvement slim to none?    
The browser itself isn't so slow, it's the page rendering. It's really sluggish. Thankfully, chrome and midori are there to help me out instead. (Opera, you too but your still a bit sluggish on linux :P)


So thanks developers for the great work!
