### Post by iameatingjam on 2009-06-18
Somehow my installation screwed up and destroyed the windows partition on my HD the other day when I was just hoping to try it out for fun (luckily not the drive with important files) so I ended up just installing ubuntu on the whole thing and was afraid it wouldn't do everything I needed it to. 

I was wrong, in fact I am absolutely blown away, it runs way faster than windows and osx. Everything I need is bundled or installed in minutes, and free, I can't get over it. I can do everything I could before without the pain of searching the internet for free software, video conversion and DVD authoring was a pain in the *** and expensive to do on windows and mac, but software installed in a couple minutes on ubuntu just works, and fast too.

 Now I've never really liked windows, I've grown up with apple, but switched to MS the last year just because I could not keep paying for jobs new quickly outdated hardware, as much I wished I could. So the fact that I actually can say ubuntu is now my choice over mac says quite a lot. Just using my computer knowing I don't have to worry about viruses and will always stay updated for free is amazing. The fact that I don't feel like I have somebody telling me what I can and cannot do with my computer is also nice.

The only experience I had with linux before this was ppc kubuntu a few years back, which I didn't like very much. 

I haven't even been using it for a week, so great job everyone involved :D
