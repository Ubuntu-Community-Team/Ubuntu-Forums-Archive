### Post by Dethv on 2008-12-16
I have tried Ubuntu on different occasions over the years after becoming fed up with Windows. I would usually mess around with it for a few days and then end up going back to Windows because I didn't feel that I knew what I was doing. Finally that has all ended. With the help of this forum, I have been having a blast configuring this OS to just the way I want it. I am very impressed with my theme after spending hours trying to figure out what the hell Compiz or Emerald were and how they worked. So far my biggest accomplishment was configuring Conky and getting it to check my emails with the help of a guide on this forum. It's not much, but its a small step into doing other cool things I never knew were possible.

With that said, thank you to everyone who provides amazing support on these forums. Every question I have ever had has been found with search and answered in great detail. Also, I'd like to thank the Ubuntu team for this wonderful OS. I never knew I could have so much fun playing with a config file. :)
