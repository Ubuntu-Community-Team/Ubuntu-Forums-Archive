### Post by Lowcountry on 2008-04-28
I don't post here much, but I've been using Linux for about 6 months now.  I've tried quite a few different distros since, and I've always had screen resolution problems.  I have a 32" Samsung tv, which is also my monitor.  I've never been able to get 1360x768.
Today I loaded up the livecd of 8.04 to see if I could get the res. I wanted.  Well, guess what?
The default res. went to 1360x768!!!
Its so great and I just wanted to say THANKS!
It seems a lot of other people are having problems, but its been smooth sailing for me!
