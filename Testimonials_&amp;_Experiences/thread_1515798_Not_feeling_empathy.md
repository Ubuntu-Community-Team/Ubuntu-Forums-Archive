### Post by Thelasko on 2010-06-22
Well, I've finally upgraded to 10.04 from 8.04 and things went pretty smooth.  I can't browse my Samba shares, but other than that everything works great.

Unfortunately, I'm not digging this new default chat client, Empathy.  Yeah, it can do video and voice chat, but it's missing a few key features.

[LIST]
[*]No support for hyperlinks
[*]No mail notifications (I thunderbird)
[*]No support for plugins
[/LIST]

I'm going to try to stick with empathy since it's integrated with the desktop.  However, I may just end up switching back to Pidgin.
