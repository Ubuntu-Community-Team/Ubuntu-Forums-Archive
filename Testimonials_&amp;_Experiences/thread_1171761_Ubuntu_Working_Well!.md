### Post by Penfold73 on 2009-05-27
Just wanted to say after 6 months of Ubuntu it's working very well. As a computer science grad I had reluctantly stuck to windows for years until I came across Vista and got so frustrated I took the seismic shift to Linux. I am very surprised at how far it's come since I used it at university and how well the GUI has developed (I initially tried Fedora but after a few frustrations with drivers switced to Ubuntu). Since then it's been a revelation and a pleasure. I've been encouraging a lot of friends to try it out and have been really impressed with the stability and sheer volume of free apps and good will within the community. Long live Linux, and long live Ubuntu.
