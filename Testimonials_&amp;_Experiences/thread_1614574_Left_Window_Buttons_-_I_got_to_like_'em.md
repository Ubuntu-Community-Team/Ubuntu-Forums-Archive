### Post by luckylucky on 2010-11-05
Honestly at first I too was opposed to the window buttons being at the left side (close, minimize, maximize), and I've even hacked it back to the right on my main machines.  

Recently I bought a new netbook, installed 10.10 (desktop, better than other IMHO) and never bothered to hack the buttons right.

I got used to it.

Now it feels normal.

Started using another main machine (after my brief infatuation with the netbook) and found the buttons on the right annoying.  I hacked it back to the left!!!

As with anything, we as humans resist change.  But given a bit of time the change quickly becomes "normal".

I actually like them being on the left now, and consider it an "ubuntu thing", just like mac has it's things that feel unusual to windowers trying it (I still don't like the one button... it scares me, grrr).

I look forward to what Mark Shuttleworth has up his sleeves for that freed up real estate.
