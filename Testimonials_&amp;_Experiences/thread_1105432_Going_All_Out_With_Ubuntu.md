### Post by phaed on 2009-03-24
I've been Ubuntu-only for 4 years now.  It is nice.  No worries.  My one suggestion, though, would be that you wait until 3-4 weeks after a final release to upgrade.  Final releases are never final.  They're just Beta 3,4 whatever.  All that being said, I *have* upgraded to Jaunty and it's remarkably stable for an alpha (just one sound issue).  If this holds up, it should be a rock solid Final Release.
