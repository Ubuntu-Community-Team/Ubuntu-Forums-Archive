### Post by SonicSteve on 2008-03-07
> **Niteowler said:**
> Tried making the cd rom a slave and putting them on the same ide channel and there was a boot disk error.  I've always heard not to put hard drives and cd roms on the same channel because they will conflict.  My motherboard owners manual said not to also. It was worth a shot though.

I do this all the time, they only conflict if you don't change the jumpers on the drives themselves. Make the sure the HDD is set to master, set the CD to slave (using the jumpers on the drive, not just by placement on the cable. I've never found that the "cable select" options on the drives work right. Set them master, and slave manually with the jumpers on the drives works best about 95% of the time. I can count on one hand the number of times that cable select has worked best.

PS that 95% estimate is conservative, it's likely closer to 98 or 99%
