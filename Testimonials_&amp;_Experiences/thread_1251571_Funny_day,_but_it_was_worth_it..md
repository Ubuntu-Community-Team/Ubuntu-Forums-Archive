### Post by bterminalx on 2009-08-27
Okay, here's the short version.....

I was going to school today (Everyone, and I mean everyone knows me. I'm not popular, just a small school and i've been there since I was a kid) and just yesterday, I got my UBUNTU DESKTOP & SERVER DISKS in the mail ( i was so excited, heh ) 

To my suprise, It came with stickers. So I put them on my school stuff. I was walking around with my drawing note-book with an ubuntu sticker on the cover. In the hidden pocket in the back is the ubuntu disks, and my edubuntu disk for highschool. Now, the princible is a real good friend. Anyway, my friends noticed these and asked about them. I made a video at lunch of my ubuntu + tv-output + media center + compiz and everyone loved it. So my friends wanted me to make them ubuntu disks and show them how to use it. My princible noticed my edubuntu and wanted me to show her my modded version (it for personal, it's google earth and squid-cache for firewall for schools (now)). My school is considering (and my librarian) to getting edubuntu and wanting me to setup the squid cache firewall. Since everyone at my school IS NOT computer savvy, I can safely block sites. Anyway, that's my day. :-)
