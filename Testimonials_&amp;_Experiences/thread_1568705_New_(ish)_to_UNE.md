### Post by demag on 2010-09-05
Hello,

I have been fairly well documented elsewhere as not being a great lover of Ubuntu.:oops:

After finally leaving the clutches of Mr Gates a couple of years ago and moving to Linux I eventually settled on PCLinuxOS as my favourite system. I used v2009.2 for quite some time before support for Kde3 was dropped and Kde4 was used. I then noticed the speed of my system slowing down with constant problems. Earlier this year 2010 final was introduced and I decided to try it. Unfortunately my system wouldn't boot and no amount of tweaking would improve it!

I was being forced to move on even though I didn't want to. I tried various distros over the months but couldn't settle with them. Then I tried the UNE 10.04 disc free with Linux Format magazine and I have to admit it was a revelation!:D I installed it on my desktop (Amd 965 and 4gig ram) Well it flies! From pressing the "on" button I can be surfing the net in 40 seconds! How does that compare to my old Xp setup of around 3-4 minute boot! And from clicking the shutdown button with the mouse, the computer powers off in 15 seconds! Now that is fast!

Thankyou Ubuntu!):P

Dave.
