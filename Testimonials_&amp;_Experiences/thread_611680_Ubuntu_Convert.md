### Post by Aerin on 2007-11-13
I've just started using Ubuntu (since just last week) and I'm simply loving it! Earlier I had tried 6.10 and 7.04 but none of them supported my network card so I had to let them go. Only the latest linux kernel has a driver for my NIC, so Gutsy supported it out-of-the-box and after that my printer, sound-card, graphics card started working automatically, and I even have Compiz-fusion running flawlessly. 

Now my specs aren't what you'd call "high-end". 667mhz Intel Pentium III processor, with 256 mb ram, 40gb harddisk, nVidia FX5200. And xp ran really really slow. It took ages to boot and shutdown, and to get anything done, and I could never running more than 3-4 applications brought my system to a standstill.

But ubuntu... wow. :o I've never experienced a slowdown yet! I'm running compiz-fusion with full effects, firefox has suddenly new life, and i use multiple workspaces with over 10 applications open! Something that I couldn't imagine in windows. I'm using emerald too and have customized my desktop to just the way I want it. I've also followed a couple a howto's, solved font issues, tweaked the gtk gui, compiled and installed stuff - just by searching through the forum posts, most of the times someone has already encountered my problem and solved it. Though I'm stuck at installing DevCpp and CodeBlocks, and I can't seem to find the solution to it, but I think I'll start another thread on that. ;)

Now Ubuntu Gutsy Gibbon is the only OS on my hard disk.
