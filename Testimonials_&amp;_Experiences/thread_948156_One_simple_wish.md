### Post by Irihapeti on 2008-10-15
Audacity doesn't work with PulseAudio, which is the default sound server in Hardy. You can either temporarily disable PulseAudio or remove it altogether. I've seen instructions for doing the former on Audacity's website, and there's a post on removal elsewhere on these forums.
