### Post by curvedinfinity on 2009-04-24
Just some quick feedback... I have used Ubuntu Server for a year or so now, and this week I had to deploy a new server, so I decided to give 9.04 a whirl. I had to configure Tomcat w/ ssl, MySQL, and IMAP/SMTP. Here I've spent only three days on the project, and the application is fully deployed. The dovecot-postfix package is AMAZING! -- I installed it and was wondering what the next step would be to get mail working. For the hell of it, I tried connecting to IMAP on the server and low and behold it worked with absolutely **zero** configuration -- IMAP+SMTP through TLS. I was simply astonished. Great work guys -- you saved me days of configuration time!
