### Post by etomic13 on 2008-08-02
I've been what you might call a slider, I have extensively used Windows XP, Windows Vista, OSX, and multiple Linux distributions. But I havent really found anything that  did it for me until I loaded Ubuntu Hardy Heron. I had been trying out Ubuntu since Dapper Drake but something about it seemed unfinished and it never worked correctly with my Compaq Presario F500. I had been using Vista for awhile on my laptop and I was starting to use Blender, I decided to try out Hardy after being fed up with the ridiculous render times and constant crashing in Vista. To my surprise everything loaded in Hardy right out of the box (considering in Gutsy I couldnt get the display drivers working correctly). There was just something in Hardy Heron that really made it feel like a polished and stabile operating system, not to mention the amazing amount of customization, I have fallen in love with the “[Gelatin]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Gelatin?content=74451")” theme and using full desktop effects it truly blows all other operating systems out of the water. Most importantly my Blender times are almost cut in half! I was going to get a Macbook Pro for college 3D design courses but I will be doing all my work on a much cheaper XPS M1330 preinstalled with Ubuntu.
