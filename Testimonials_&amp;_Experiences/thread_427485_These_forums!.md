### Post by Flavian on 2007-04-29
Hello to all the people out there ;)
I do not know if this is the right place to post stuff like that, if not, please move the topic ;)
I also did NOT read the Cafe policy (shame on me) because it is so freaking HUGE!

I just wanted to say a big THANK YOU to all the people out there that give support, quick answers on complex problems and issues with bugs and errors on my system. I really do appreciate the forums a lot. I love it because people here are so friendly, they are not just like "you stupid noob, hahaha, try to figure it out on your own" or like other people in some german forums I post in that write a page of useless crap basically just telling you "THIS is the reason why I am not going to really help you, but since you are a newbie I will explain to you the basics of computers" and I am like: "NO, I already know that. Thank you for treating me like an idiot, and thank you for not answering my question!" :D

So a big THANK YOU to all the nice, quick-to-answer, and quick-to-help people out there.
Since I have switched to Ubuntu I have NOT regreted it AT ALL. I've fallen in love with it.
I've learned so much about Linux since posting here. And I really wanna thank you guys for making that possible. It is really good to know if something just doesn't work there is a place to go where people KNOW what they are talking about and can kindly help your problem.

I really like the friendly atmosphere here a lot and hope it will never change :)

That's about it ;)
Thanks for reading.

Florian
