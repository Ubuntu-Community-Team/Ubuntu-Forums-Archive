I just swapped motherboards in one of my Gutsy computers from:

Pentium 3 750Mhz Intel chipset motherboard (440BX)

to 

AMD Athlon XP 2200+ VIA chipset motherboard (KT600)

With the exception of moving from a PCI network card to onboard, all other hardware remained the same.  I just rebooted and it worked!  The only thing I had to do was unplug and replug the network cable to get it to get network access.

Simply amazing.
