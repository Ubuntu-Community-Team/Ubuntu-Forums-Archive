### Post by drs305 on 2011-10-19
Once Oneiric was released, the Ubuntu+1 forum rolls over to the next development release. While in the short term the forums are often dominated by the new release the Ubuntu Forums does not create a forum for each specific release. 

In your case, most of your problems appear to be related to Unity. While there are several forums which deal heavily with this topic, Desktop Environments or General would probably get you the best response. Include the symptoms you reported in the original post. 

We've all had bad experiences installing OS's, and you do have our sympathy. Hopefully you will stick around and have our support as well.
