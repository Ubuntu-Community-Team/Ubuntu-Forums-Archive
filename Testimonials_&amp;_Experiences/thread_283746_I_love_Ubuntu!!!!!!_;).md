### Post by bsimpson on 2006-10-24
Well, what to say now...

I might just be another one of those disenchanted, former Windows-users...

As if there weren't enough of us out there... lol :)))

It's kind of creepy how everything in the computer-scene went down that slippery slope towards absolute control, when back in the late eighties/early nineties, billyboy and his minions took over the computer-scene (at least, that's what they thought,...)!

Sad beyond words....

They had you as end-users test their crap and made you pay for testing their crap!

They came up with all kinds of ways to have you pay double or triple for the stuff, you already owned (DRM?!?!?!?) and then they had the nerve to try to bash everything open-source in a very ill-conceived ad-campaign....

But that's the past!

The future is LINUX in all its flavors, in all its modularity and therefore customization-abilities! 

And, yes, I chose Ubuntu! I ran across it by chance, because I needed an OS that was not Windows (go figure!!!!!!!!!!!!!) and yet was able to handle my oh-so-bad wireless-usb-stick....

Well, after trying a few other flavors, I got stuck with Ubuntu!

That was in June this year, 2006!!!!

And I have not the slightest reason to look back at all!

Ubuntu with all its versatility already built in, with a constant and very well maintained support and update/upgrade-system is satisfying my needs so much better than the Redmond-Behemoth!

I'm running it on an old PIII-laptop and it's working linke a charm! It always did and even with the RC-update to 6.10, it still does!

GREAT!

I'm loving it!

AND: Working as a PC-tech for over 16 years now, I would recommend it for the typical 'Joe Ordinary'-user anytime over the MS-BS!!!!!

Okay, that's all very opinionated and biased but trust me for once: UBUNTU in any version is way more than just worth trying it! It's the superior operating system unless you plan on only playing  games with your PC!!!!!!

THANK YOU!!!!!
