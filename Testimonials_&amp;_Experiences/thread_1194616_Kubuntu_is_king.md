### Post by froid on 2009-06-22
A big thank you to the Kubuntu development/design team for a truly outstanding OS. After living in a Microsoft environment for years (MS-DOS, Win95, Win98, Win-XP) and with some Mac experience (I still enjoy Mac OS X), I tried several Linux distributions - some on Live CD and one or two installed - and then discovered Kubuntu. Such beautiful it-just-works functionality and some added eye candy (I have a weakness for it). I found it so easy to add Firefox via the package manager (still my favourite browser), and found Dolphin a pure pleasure to use.

As you can tell, I am still a raw newbie - no doubt with heaps to learn - but so far I am hugely impressed.:D

Thanks again Kubuntu team.

I must be your biggest fan.

Froid.
