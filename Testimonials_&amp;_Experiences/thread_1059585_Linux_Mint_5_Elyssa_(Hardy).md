### Post by Mrandersonjr on 2009-02-03
Hello everybody,
The Linux experience in general,is way better than anything you can get on Windows! I've been using Linux Mint 5 for about 3-4 months,and linux in general for a couple of years (Fedora 9,Mandriva,Puppy,etc.) I have been telling all of my friends about the greatness of linux! I have had many issues,but have managed to fix most of them (only problem is my lexmark Z645,the drivers won't ndiswrap,and I have tried multiple tarballs from various sites.)BUT,I almost have no need for printing,I really like Linux,even my mac friends are jealous when they see that I don't need to pay for the operating system,and it is fully customizable and open source.Please,continue to spread the word about linux,because,if more people had it,they wouldn't need to go through a WGA error in xp if they changed their RAM,and the slew of viruses on windows.The faliure rate of vista is also tremendous,seeing as I have two other computers in the house with vista,I am often stuck fixing them,and of course my family won't allow linux on them,but who is going to stop me?:D Thank you for being a good community and being close together,Cheers!:popcorn:
