### Post by Dark_X on 2007-11-17
About 4 months ago I was searching youtube for videos about Vista.  I hate people who bash other operating systems so I was going to get in an argument with all the XP fanboys.  While searching I came across this video:
[http://youtube.com/watch?v=xC5uEe5OzNQ](http://youtube.com/watch?v=xC5uEe5OzNQ)
I think I drooled a little when I watched it, it was so amazing.=P~  I did some research and found that my wireless card would work in Ubuntu.  The first couple of times I used it I couldn't get Compiz to work and I destroyed my system every time, trying to get it working.  Eventually I gave up on Compiz for a while.  I really wanted to mess around with Linux a lot more and I found out that Ubuntu has open beta testing.  Eventually I learned how to solve my own problems because of this community and I even got Compiz to work with my ATI card.  I have damaged my system so many times I could probably start a support froum.:roll:  Today I have convinced one of my friends to try Ubuntu after showing him what it could do.  Ubuntu has changed my life.
Amazing what can happen in 4 months...:lolflag:
