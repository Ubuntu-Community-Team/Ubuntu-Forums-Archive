### Post by ahood on 2008-11-29
Oh man! **AWESOME**!

I upgraded (complete reinstall) to Ubuntu Intrepid and I am absolutely thrilled with the seamless music playing between Rhythmbox and my Mythbuntu (7.10) running mt-daapd (i.e., firefly). Never before (i.e., pre Intrepid) would Rhythmbox see and play the _streamed_ music on the Myth box. 

Wow, what a difference Intrepid makes. The first time I started Rhythmbox (Intrepid), Firefly immediately appeared under the "Shared" library. I clicked on Firefly and 568 artists and 2,957 songs quickly appeared on my slow/moderate performing PC (AMD Athlon XP 2200+).

With previous versions of Ubuntu, I had Rhythmbox "fetch" the music collection from a smb shared folder on the Mythbox. This worked but Rhythmbox was slow at listing the artists and songs.

Now, I do absolutely nothing. It just works!
