### Post by fugazi32 on 2009-02-09
Wow! That's all I can really say....just under a month ago I installed Xubuntu 8.10. Before that i'd been using SuSE 10.0/Windows XP. I'd been using SuSE since December 2005, and *cringe* had been using the root account for day to day activities for over a year, since I knew little about such things back then. I noticed both my SuSE/XP partitions were getting slow and buggy, and SuSE's RPM files & missing dependencies made me want to switch to an Ubuntu-based distro more than ever, as i'd installed Ubuntu before on a mate's PC.
I decided upon Xubuntu because i've always found KDE/Gnome to be a waste of memory and i've always used light-weight window managers/desktops. Xfce is a breeze, runs fast and the candy is tops too!
I got rid of both SuSE and XP (I'D REALLY HAD ENOUGH OF ANYTHING TO DO WITH MICRO$OFT!!!)
My computer runs faster than ever now, i'll certainly be recommending Xubuntu to everyone I get the chance to tell!!!
