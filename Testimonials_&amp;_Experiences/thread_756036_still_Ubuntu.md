### Post by AnimateDeath on 2008-04-15
I made the switch from vista to ubuntu 7.10 last fall and posted about it here  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=565602](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=565602)

I just moved on to 8.04 and I am still loving it. I have still yet to find a way to run Photoshop CS3 and Lightroom in ubuntu so I have to have a stupid windows dual boot for those 2 programs. I tried other progs in place of them, but I just like them too much to give them up (not to mention how much I paid for them).   8.04 recognized all of my hardware including sound and wifi, plus my external HD took nothing more to use it than to plug it in to the usb2.0 port. 

My computer is a Toshiba Satellite A135-S4467, and no problems at all. I just wish I had more time to learn more about linux than I do, but slowly and surely as the saying goes....
