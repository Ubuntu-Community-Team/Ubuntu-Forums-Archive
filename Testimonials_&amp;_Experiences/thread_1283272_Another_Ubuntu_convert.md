### Post by mrky on 2009-10-05
Hi everyone!

I use Ubuntu for five months now, and couldn't be happier with it. During this time I also recomended other people to give it a try. So far i installed it on 3 of my friends computers. I have to say that none of them are PC power users. They are ordinary people that use couple of apps for work and need a computer for watching movies, listen music and other hobbies: gaming, 3d modelling etc...

Switching to Ubuntu wasn't so complicated for me thanks to You people on forums and Your help, since i consider my self an advanced user. The real test for any OS are people i mentioned above, average people with average computer skills. 

Anyway none of these people decided to switch back to windows for which i'm very glad. Of course they needed help at first couple of days tops, but all in all conversion went very smooth for them.  I just wanted to write this down in case if someone is thinking to give Ubuntu a try but isn't sure if he/she can make it a smooth transition.

So to all of you out there who are thinking to go to Ubuntu. Give it a try. You won't be sorry. It's easy, user friendly and fun and besides community on forums is incredible and very helpfull.
