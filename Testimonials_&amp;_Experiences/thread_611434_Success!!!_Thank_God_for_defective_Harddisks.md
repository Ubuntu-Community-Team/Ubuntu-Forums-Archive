### Post by Takmadeus on 2007-11-12
Well this thread is to tell a testimony about something tht happened today. My good friend had his WinXP C: partition toasted (dunno how) and he asked me to help him reinstall it... I couldn't disagree and tried installing it, but when formatting it said the HD was defective and had to be replaced. I told him he had 2 options, to buy another HD or to lemme try and install linux in his PC, so as he is kind of short of money he doubtedly told me to try and install linux. So as he needed a fast distro with support for multimedia codecs I installed Dreamlinux 2.2 and it installed fast and effortless (except for the partitioning that I had to do manually).

After 20 minutes it installed and it ran FAST.... I've never seen such a fast system not even by optimizing ubuntu... it was quite pleasant, and even if 3d did not work properly, everything else was so good that he decided to keep it. I mean, that thing is Packed with a lot of applications and Xfce runs so smooth that I am surprised. Thank God for that defective HD because only linux would be able to recover it ;)

This is the first time I can convince someone to make the switch having them completely satisfied.... way to go!

PS: you klnow what's funny? he asking me where was the antivirus :p
