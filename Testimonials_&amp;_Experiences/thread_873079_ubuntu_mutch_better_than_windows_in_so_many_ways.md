### Post by ad_267 on 2008-07-30
You have to have the compizconfig-settings-manager installed. Then under General Settings - Opacity Settings you can click on New and add PopupMenu and set the transparency. You can't have a fully transparent menu with text though as the transparency is applied to everything.
