### Post by luckylucky on 2010-12-11
So I finally bought an iPod touch 4G and though part of me is really loving the experience it has also served to remind me why I LOVE Ubuntu and despise both Mac & PC.  Before I get started I am aware of Android, and would far prefer it to iOS but, at this time, there is now cheap way to get an android device locally that is similar to an iPod. 

I am frustrated by how locked up the iPod is. I am not "allowed" to use it as I wish. The killer is that you are not even allowed to use flash. For me, and surely many many many other people that is the equivalent to having one arm tied behind your back. I searched online for why apple won't allow flash and my conclusion is that by cutting off users from awesome flash app websites they'll make millions of dollars more profits via the app store. 

Found some vaporware sites claiming to be able to hack the iPod 4g into android... But unfortunTely it is as I sAid vaporware. 

My suggestion - buy an android phone instead of iPhone.  Skip the iPod and wait for an android clone to come.  My dream solution (not going to happen) would be to install iUbuntu... Or even android on the iPod.
