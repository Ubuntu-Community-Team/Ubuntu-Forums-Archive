### Post by troykm on 2007-11-25
i have been going back and forth between ubuntu and vista for a while now, trying to decide which i want to keep. 

last week my cable internet wifi setup started going funny, slowing down, then every time i connected my laptop, the modem would reboot! i tried connecting the ethernet cable and got the same thing.
i got Mr internet provider technician to come out and see what was wrong. after testing with a new modem, line testing, bla bla bla, we tried connecting my old laptop that has XP on it, no problem. we connected my flatmates desktop pc (also xp) and no issue. as soon as we re-connected my vista laptop, bang went the modem.
i formatted and re installed vista, same prob! i thought there was a hardware issue with wireless card, so i did a full install of ubutnu (to rule out vista being the issue), surprise, not one single problem!  
i have no idea what was happening with vista, the tech guy said he has never seen a wireless card shutdown a modem b4. (he did say "vista is nothing more than excrement")
so my decision was made for me, Ubuntu won. and to tell you the truth, im glad! 

long live ubuntu/linux!:KS
