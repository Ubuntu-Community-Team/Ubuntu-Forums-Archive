### Post by Uchiha_Kori on 2011-11-14
Hello everybody. I would like share my experience out my move from Windows 7 to Ubuntu 10.04. In all, I could not be more pleased.

I was dual booting both at first using Wubi.exe. After playing around with Ubuntu, I decided to 100% abandon Windows and completely install Ubuntu. Best idea ever! Never before has my computer ran so smoothly! I was also greatly surprised at how many apps there are for Ubuntu (and Linux in general) and how they are all free. I also like Open Office better than the over priced Microsoft Office 2010. I hope that one day, the world will realize (if not already) that it does not have to revolve around Microsoft or Windows. 

Thanks to all who contribute to Linux and Ubuntu,
-Kori
