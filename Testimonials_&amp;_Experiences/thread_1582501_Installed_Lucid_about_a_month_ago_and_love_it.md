### Post by loooongcat:3 on 2010-09-26
I have been using Ubuntu off and on as an "experimental" operating system since 7.10, late 2007. With issues like ACTA looming around the corner I want to rely on a free open-source operating system with great free software so as of now I am a full time Ubuntu user.  

All I have to say is that I love the smoothness of this operating system. It performs simple tasks with ease. I typically use my computer to listen to music and surf the web. Unfortunately a fraction of my gaming library is incompatible with WINE, but the WINE development team is doing an awesome job. 

I have never thought that obtaining all of this stellar (and free!) software would be imaginable and with "alternative programs" like Open Office I no longer have to worry about buying from Microsoft. 

I am a full time student and if I get a notebook computer for school I will definitely be running Ubuntu on it.
