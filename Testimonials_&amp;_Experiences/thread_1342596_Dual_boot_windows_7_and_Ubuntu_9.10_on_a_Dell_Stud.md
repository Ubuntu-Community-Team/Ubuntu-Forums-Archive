### Post by ndefontenay on 2009-11-30
Hi everyone,

I want to share my appreciation of things now that I got Ubuntu dual booting on a good piece of hardware.

Installation:
It was easy and fast. Being installed on a laptop with a one partition huge Hard Disk, I was hoping I would have the chance to shrink the partition and use whatever space it could gain.

Instead I had to go to windows to shrink it up. When space was freed, I went boot back from Linux installation Disk and used option "Use next available space:.

Speed:
This is SO awesome. I had to load pictures from a wedding, there was like a 150 of them. It took 10 seconds to read the memory disk and it was instantly on the disk. I couldn't believe it. 

Startup is as fast as windows 7. I was hoping for a 10 seconds benchmark but 26 secs is not bad either. Grub is really long to start for some reasons though.

Sound:
No sound at all!!! I've posted a thread which remains unanswered but basically I can play any music or movie or whatever there's just no sound. It does detect a sound card but nothing comes out : ( I'm still [seeking help]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1337872") for this one.)

Effects:
With a speedy laptop, all the effect goodies are a breeze. It makes me much more productive and with Ubuntu One I got all my files on my new laptop within minutes.

I love Ubuntu. (I'll love it even more once this sound problem is fixed)
