### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2011-06-30

All those [COLOR="Purple"]k[/COLOR]applications can be a little [COLOR="Purple"]k[/COLOR]omplicated and [COLOR="Purple"]k[/COLOR]onfusing to a newcomer, but alot of them (Qt applications) completely rock compared to their gtk (Gnome, Xfce, etc) counterparts.
