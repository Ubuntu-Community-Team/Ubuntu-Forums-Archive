### Post by beew on 2011-10-29
Don't need to stick to LTS. Just wait a few months and do a fresh install instead of upgrade and you should be fine. LTS only get longer support, it is not "better" in any cosmic way. It is only more "stable" in the sense that things don't change, it doesn't imply more reliable performance than non LTS (I find 11.04 and 10.10 way ahead of 10.04) Thanks to repo freeze if something is broken it will stay broken longer (you can use ppas but that defeats the argument for LTS as being more stable, and beyond a certain point even ppas are not going to be updated because of too many old dependencies). New releases also tend to have better hardware supports.
