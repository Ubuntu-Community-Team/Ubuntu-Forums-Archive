### Post by jamiepgs on 2008-03-24
I installed Ubuntu today on my Mac Mini, and I have to say... flawless! ALL the hardware worked perfectly 'out-of-the-box' even the internal speakers which I've heard people have had problems with. I was certainly impressed by it. My previous Ubuntu experiences were not the best. I tried it on my PC and it didn't work very well. I recommend anyone with a Mac Mini (current generation) to install Ubuntu, well worth it! The built-in wireless works, the ethernet works, the sound (in and out) works and I can access all my work and music from my Mac HDD, truly brilliant!
