### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-09-25
The Unity launcher on the left side makes perfect sense on any wide screen (1366x768, 1024x576, etc), touch or not. It auto-hides when a window is maximised, thus getting out of the way. Place it at the bottom, and it will waste the more scarce vertical screen space. Auto-hiding, if turned on, will also have to be done differently, probably time based.
