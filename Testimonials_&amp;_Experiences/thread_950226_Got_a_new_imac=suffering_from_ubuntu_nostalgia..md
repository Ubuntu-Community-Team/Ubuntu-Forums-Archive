### Post by twistedbydesign on 2008-10-16
I installed ubuntu back when Edgy came out. I loved it. I put it on my 5 year old emachines computer. It was an amazing learning experience and I fell in love with the os.
 Due to the fact that I'm a musician and I needed a new computer that would fulfill all of my needs (not just musical..but overall), I decided to save up my cash and get a new imac. I couldn't find linux friendly hardware/software that i really liked. So i took the Apple plunge. I've had the imac for about 4 months.
   If I had gone straight from windows to mac, i would love it. Leopard is a great os as far as stability, usability etc.
  But lately I've been getting Ubuntu Nostalgia. I don't really want to put ubuntu on my mac because it's a newer imac and it seems like there are still alot of issues. But I miss Ubuntu so much. I'm not really a fanboy of any particular OS...Even Windows has it's merits.
The mac forums seem like they are just one-shot jabs at whoever posts a question or insults apple's integrity. I miss being on these forums. People are much nicer on here. lol

Anyway...I guess I just wanted to thank the ubuntu community and the developers for introducing me to ubuntu. I didn't fully appreciate it until I stopped using it. Maybe sometime soon I'll work up the courage to dual boot on this hog.

Still like OSX. Still like Windows to an extent. LOVE Ubuntu.

I realize this post doesn't say a whole lot. I'm just bored wanted to get it off my chest.

Peace
