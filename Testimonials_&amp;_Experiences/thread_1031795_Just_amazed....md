### Post by cariboo on 2009-01-06
I have to say that I am constantly amazed at how much hardware just plain works. Over the past couple of months I have purchased, a D-Link usb wireless adapter that just plain worked, a BFG Nvidia 8400 video card, I plugged it in started the computer, it just plain worked. A noname usb headset, I had to play with some settings in System-->Preferences-->Sound, it took maybe 10 seconds, it works quite well. I just got one of [these]("http://www.intel.com/Products/Desktop/Motherboards/D945GCLF/D945GCLF-overview.htm"), installed Intepid on it this afternoon everything works I even get 1600X1200 resolution by default, the above mentioned D-Link wireless device was detected right from the start when connected to this motherboard. 

The only hardware I haven't been able to make work, is a Canon Canoscan 4200F, but for the amount it gets used, I can put up with using windows once in a while. :)
