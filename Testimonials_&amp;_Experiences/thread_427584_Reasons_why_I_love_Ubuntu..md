I promise once I get the hang of this I'll help newbies like myself. Ubuntu is a masterpiece. Couldn't be happier.

### Post by AmyRose on 2007-05-01
I'm a 3-year Linux user. And I must say that Ubuntu's the friendliest distro out there. (I use KDE though, which is part of why I like this--the second release had KDE support! That's fast!)

I've used Ubuntu since Warty. I didn't like the Dapper release (it was terribly slow on both my computers), and I went with Fedora for a bit, but now Feisty is awesome. I was on the #ubuntu-release-party channel and saw the excitement over Feisty. It reminded me of the release of new video games or other proprietary software, but instead of that, it was free software. I got the moment here: [http://i149.photobucket.com/albums/s59/amyrose1024/itsoutnow.png](http://i149.photobucket.com/albums/s59/amyrose1024/itsoutnow.png) :)

I don't think I'm going to leave now. :) I admit I used Fedora because it was taught in Linux class, but now that I've moved out of computer-related majors (I'm now majoring in children's education), I'm going to stick with Ubuntu because it doesn't matter what the structure of /etc is because I only have to worry about what I use on my own computers. (If that made any sense at all)

So yeah... And I have no reason to go back to Windows. :) (I haven't had a reason since I ditched it in 2004... :D )
