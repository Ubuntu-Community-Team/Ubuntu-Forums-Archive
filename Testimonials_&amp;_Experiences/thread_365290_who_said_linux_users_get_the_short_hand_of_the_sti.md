### Post by Leipe_Po on 2007-02-19

i also explained synaptic to him, he could not believe that there was so much free software going around, hes going trough a install frenzy right now,

he followed the restricted formats steps himself, although not hard to understand, it adds something to his understanding of the system, witch i also told is to remember the folder structure, cause in a terminal that would come in handy, and we talked about loads of other stuff...
