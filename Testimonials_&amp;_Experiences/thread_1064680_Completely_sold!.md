### Post by ambidextrousone on 2009-02-09
Hello peeps! im sure some of the usual Testimonal lurkers mights have bumped into me before, this is my final report of my experience with ubuntu, anything further would simply be a confirmation of the new things ive learned.

Thats the beauty of my operating system, it asks for my patience, and repays me by running smooth, and doing as its asked, this is no windows rant, but ive never experienced such a unique feel of command, for the first time since i started using computers, im in control.

So, i suppose you need to be smart, fast learning, and some kinda groundbreaking hacker, after all, you have to use a terminal and enter all sort of 'other-worldly' commands from time to time, i hate this opinion.. firstly, ive only used it myself because its been essential to my needs as a user, rome wasnt built in a day, and most certainly, neither was this operating system i speak to you from now, does this make me clever? Lol, please, i was useless with windows, im probably useless with linux, i cant set the timer of the video downstairs. yeah.

So, how does such a poor computer user use such a specialist operating system like linux, easy, i put a live cd in my drive, let it do its business, i came right here, to these forums, to ask lots of stupid questions, and what was more surprising than how LITTLE i needed to configure ubuntu after install, was how willing this community was to sort every little outstanding issue i had, i almost pity them, must have been painfully difficult to explain some of it in laymans terms, but there was a willingness to help, and the sort of persistence that most well payed I.T professionals should learn to adopt, theres a passion for helping people (bring back the thanks tool BTW!)

I hope im ok to name a few of the people whos helped me get where i am with ubuntu, ugm6hr, taurus, Sorivenul, toupeiro.

theres been plenty more people who helped out, and probably be a whole lot more who will too.

can i also thank wolfen69 and jrock612 - just for being controversial ;)
