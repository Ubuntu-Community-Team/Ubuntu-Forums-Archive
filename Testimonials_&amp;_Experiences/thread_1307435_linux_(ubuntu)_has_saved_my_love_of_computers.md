To add:
Linux has saved my computer. 
I'm using Xubuntu on a 7yr old computer with 512 MB of RAM. 
M$ operating systems won't boot up on my machine. The XP that came with it, became so slow to boot after all the upgrades, that I was sick of waiting for it to start. Xubuntu boots from button press to log in screen in 44 seconds. That's good, I think, for the age of my equipment. My Wife's 1 yr old laptop with Vista boots in minutes, not seconds. I will have to measure again now and see if there has been an improvement. 
Why buy a new computer every few years when Linux works so well?
