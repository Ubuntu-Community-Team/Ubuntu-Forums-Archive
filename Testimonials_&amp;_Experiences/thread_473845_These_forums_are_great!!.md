### Post by ts51 on 2007-06-14
> **samuraiCat said:**
> Cool! Would you mind posting the links that you used to do it? I'll be doing it myself tonight or tomorrow. Thanks!


Well, as long as you have a flash drive (or a cd or something like that) then you should be able to get your broadcom card working pretty easily. Just download Wifi-Radar to your drive and download the driver attachment in this thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=472153&highlight=ts51&page=4](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=472153&highlight=ts51&page=4). It is located on page 4 post #35. Put those 2 downloads on the flash drive. Install Ubuntu. Copy the downloads off your drive. Double click the drive, install it. Reboot. Double click the wifi-radar. Install it. Reboot. As long as you have a bcm43xx card, this should work. If you get lost, just read the post the attachment is in. Mazza558 is to credit.
