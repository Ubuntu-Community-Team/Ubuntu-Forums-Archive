### Post by Melindrea on 2008-11-03
Alright, so today I installed Ibex, since my laptop has been in need of a reinstall for quite some time now. I really love the themes, and the background picture is gorgeous. It quickly recognized my graphics card and wireless, and even though I can't swear that my wireless was working (since it's not working at home right now), it certainly behaved as it should.

However... I can't have it on my laptop. I need Skype on one of my computers, and I can't get Skype to work on Ibex. My microphone refuses to work, and the webcamera doesn't work either. The microphone issue is in Ubuntu, though I'm fairly certain that the webcamera is due to skype.

So, right now I'm reinstalling Hardy on the laptop. In a few days, however, I'm going to be installing Ibex on my stationary, since I can't use Skype on that one due to problems with the sound card (yet another Audigy 2 issue =), so I at least can enjoy some of the niftier things on that one.

oh! And my final slight complaint: I can't get the weather to work on Ibex!

Ah well. I like Ubuntu. =)
