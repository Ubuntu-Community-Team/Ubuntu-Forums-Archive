### Post by barmaxNY on 2012-02-28
I've been toying with Linux ever since Redhat was the buzzword back in the mid 90s. Never committed to it because of some reason or another. But every couple of years I try to get back in it and finally I can say Linux is permanently on my home server. It looks great and does everything I need.

I also happen to be the family and friend techy, rebuilding PCs every weekend... I have a big family on my wife's side. I already told everyone the next time I rebuild their PC it's going to be with Linux, specifically a flavor of Ubuntu. Well I just rebuilt one with 10.04 LTM because it was a P4 Dell and it just flies. One word, theyloveit.

I would love to go with the latest Lubuntu on older machines but I find 10.04 still much faster. Will hate to see 10.04 EOL.

Well, that's pretty much it, another convert and converting others!
