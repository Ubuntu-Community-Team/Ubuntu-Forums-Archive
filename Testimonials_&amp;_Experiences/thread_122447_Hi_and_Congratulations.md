### Post by kewl1uk on 2006-01-27
Congratulations Ubuntu, the first Linux distribution to apparently faultlessly install on my eMachines 370 after both Mandriva Discovery/LX and SuSE 10.0 failed. (Mandriva had problems accessing the floppy and SuSE 10 froze at the hardware detection stage of the install). I installed Ubuntu with some trepidation having experience of nightmare scenarios in the installation of media players and codecs. But installing those was easy following the instructions in the Wiki. Needless to say that I'm sold on Ubuntu and will be sticking with it of course.

The only problem is with the Epson C42 *Plus* printer which has a different driver to the usual C42UX and C42CX. But I'm installing a HP PSC 1410 this coming weekend so all should be running smoothly. It's about time I got myself a new printer!

btw Ubuntu came to me on the DVD with Linux magazine.
