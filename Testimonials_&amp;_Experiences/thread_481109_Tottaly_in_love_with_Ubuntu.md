### Post by galvao on 2007-06-22
I work for more than 10 years with web development, so I always worked with Linux, but in the "shell level". I was a long time Windows user at home, which always bothered me, because of this professional/personal paradox.

Recently I've acquired a new hard drive and decided to give Ubuntu a chance, and to put some effort on it. To my surprise the effort part was minimal and Ubuntu just - excuse my french - kicks Windows ***.

I mean, really, it's ridiculous to compare the two. Ubuntu (of course, it's Gnome's credit as well) is faster, more stable, less annoying... 

It's impossible to put into words, you have to see it to believe.

Oh yes, I have dual boot with my XP still on the machine - after all we still live in a very "windows-oriented" world - but I assure you: I can't use Windows anymore. It becomes really disgusting after you know Ubuntu.

Well, anyway, this post is not intended to trash Windows - although the comparison is unavoidable - but it's all about cheering Ubuntu and the people who develops it.

Congratulations, guys! Now I'm a believer!!!

Ubuntu 4 ever!
