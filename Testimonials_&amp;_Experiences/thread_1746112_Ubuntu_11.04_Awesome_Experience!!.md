### Post by heavydose on 2011-05-01
Painless experience here. I'm on a XPS M1710 laptop. Been using Ubuntu since 9.10 and other than having to fine tune my volume settings it's been great. Easy to configure and maintain updates. Everything works including the lights and the external buttons. Trackpad works great with the scroll side.

I do wish I could move the Launcher to the bottom of the screen but it's a minor complaint. Very usable search catches everything else you don't have a shortcut for. I mostly just use this laptop for steaming to a tv (vga) while playing the sound on the speakers, which are very loud on this model.
