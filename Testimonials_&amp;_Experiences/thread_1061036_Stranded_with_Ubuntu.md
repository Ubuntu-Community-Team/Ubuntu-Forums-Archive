### Post by groeswenphil on 2009-02-05
Picture the scene. I get home from work on a Friday evening and find that my broadband connection is down. Worse news....it can't be fixed until the following Thursday.....and then, it starts snowing and I'm stranded in the house with no net connection except from a very slow connection through my mobile phone.
And then I start fiddling........and discover what people mean when they say that Ubuntu is  so easy to customise.

My windows now look gorgeous. Every icon has its own little image clearly showing what can be found inside. Sad, I know, and I really should get out more, but I will say this. Try this yourself:-

Download some Icon themes. I particularly like Ubuntu-Brave.
Next, go to any open file window.
Right click on any  folder icon.....and pick Properties.
Next  click on the icon in the properties window.....then move through your files or icons to find a suitable image for that folder.

I expect lots of people knew about this, but it was new to me.
