### Post by Pirate Zoro on 2011-08-21
Like the title says, I've been using Xubuntu and XFCE exclusively for about a month (not counting an ill-fated attempt to try and get e17 to play nice ) and it's just so great, I may never go back to GNOME. Everything is just so quick and crisp. I finally have a system set up exactly how I want it. exactly how I need it to be that looks amazing, works even better, without the slowdown of Unity or GNOME on my little netbook (Asus Eee PC 1001PX). Originally, I had planned on using XFCE for 3 days to try it out. By the second day, I was enjoying it so much I went ahead, backed up my files and installed Xubuntu. Those 3 days turned into a week, then 2, then 3, and now a month. I love it. It's just so elegant, crisp, and most of all lightweight and fast. XFCE has found a permanent user in this guy right here.

### Post by davesalyers on 2011-08-21
I'm also a recent Xubuntu fanboy. I have some older hardware and so I am not looking to buy brand new hardware just to play with the unnecessary eyecandy in Unity and GNOME3. Much of the reason many folk switch to Linux is the first place is to slow down the cycle of having to upgrade hardware (something which seems to be forgotten by developers). I upgraded my computer from Ubuntu 10.04 to Xubuntu 11.04 and the difference is night and day even between those two on this hardware. I did the same for my mom's - she went from Windows Vista to Xubuntu 11.04 and thanked me for changing her OS as it is a lot more responsive for her.
