### Post by zzottt on 2006-09-07
I wanted to say that I am a total Ubuntu convert.
I have tried a few differnt distro's over the years and never liked them all that much. I am just sick of Windows and wanted something new. Somehow I found Ubuntu and so far since installing I have only booted into Windows twice. 

It kinda feels like the first time I found Firefox and tabbed browsing... I tried it, went to back to Internet Explorer and found that it lacked sooo many things I wanted and just stopped using it. I have even converted my wife, who also enjoys the ease of use Ubuntu offers... her only complaint is she wants iTunes and flash to work better, oh well you cant win em all.


So I have been running Ubuntu for 2 months now and I love it.
I am thinking of backing up my harddrives and removing the dual boot and putting my Windows install through a VM so I can still use it for work reasons, the SSLVPN software doesnt work right in linux :cry: 




Thank you Ubuntu Team
