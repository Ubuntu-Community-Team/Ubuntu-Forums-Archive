### Post by tom56 on 2007-04-25
They look no different to me. Probably a change in the default settings. Whenever I install I have to change hinting to full in System->Preferences->Fonts otherwise lots of apps (especially Firefox) look rubbish. However monitors display differently so expect the settings are for something newer than mine (7 years old).
