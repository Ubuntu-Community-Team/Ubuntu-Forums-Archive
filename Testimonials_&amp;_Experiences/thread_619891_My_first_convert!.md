### Post by Snargledorf on 2007-11-21
I am currently attending a two year school for computer networking, and one of the courses I took this semester was communication.  So one of the requirements of the class is giving a persuasive speech.  I decided to try and persuade my class to switch to Ubuntu (trust me everyone in this class can switch).  So I feel I did a pretty good job on this speech the presentation was flawless and I felt really good about it.  So I sit back down and the guy in front of me turns around and begins to ask me questions about running it from the disk and such.  So by the end of the conversation I am fairly sure he had decided he was going to switch!  So the next time class gets together I guess I will have to see how things went.  This whole experience was very exciting for me, I felt like a mini Steve Jobs or something ;-).  Well I thought I would share this little story with you guys. Thanks for reading
