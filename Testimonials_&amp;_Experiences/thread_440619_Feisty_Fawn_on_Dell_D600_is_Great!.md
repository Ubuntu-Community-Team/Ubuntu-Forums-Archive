### Post by MichiganBill on 2007-05-11
I've been a lurker for a while and I just wanted to put in my 2 cents worth...

I am a newbie to Ubuntu, I did have RH 9 a few years ago but didn't do much with it. Ubuntu is GREAT!

It installed in about 30 minutes on a Dell D600 from *Bay just perfectly, no problems at all. The WiFi worked out of the box, all the function buttons work (screen brightness and so on). The WiFi light itself doesn't work, but that's the case with all Linux distros, so it seems.

I've installed Crossover Office and a few Windows apps. Also a few apps using Automatix2. All in all I'm a happy camper.

I can play music with Amarok and play with photos with Rawstudio and Photoshop (under Crossover). The reason I got the laptop was to open and manipulate PDFs-- and the Adobe Reader and pdftk are all I need.

Just a note of encouragement for anyone else thinking of taking the plunge. Come on in, the water's fine!
