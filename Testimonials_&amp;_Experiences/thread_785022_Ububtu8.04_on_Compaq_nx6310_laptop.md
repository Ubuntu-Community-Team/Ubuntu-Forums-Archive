### Post by Fedz on 2008-05-07
A few days ago I acquired a Compaq nx6310 laptop, intel centrino dual-core: 1.83 Ghz, 80 GiB HD, blutooth and wifi (Intel PRO/Wireless 3945ABG) with Mobile 945GM/GMS/GME, 943/940GML Express Integrated Graphics Card.

It had window$ XP pro sp2 with M$ Office and several games ... etc. preloaded. Vista ready the sticker says on the laptop!

Upon first bootup Window$ loaded several massive programmes - all sat in the sys tray. It took an age to load :roll:

After many hours and a sleepless night I managed to take them off loading on startup and decided to update window$.

That took 2 hours as window$ update was going so slow, it stalled to install them on 3 occasions and the processors were constantly going bonkers :roll:

After nights trying with window$ I decided to give in an install Ubuntu/8.04 ...

What a dream the install was, bootup was less than 30 seconds and off I was :)

The processor is getting a rest, Ubuntu is 101% better on the PC :)

Visual effects work a dream and the wifi is spot-on perfect :)

Ubuntu/8.04 has made this laptop a dream to operate and use and I'm positive the processor ...etc is thankful :)

I'm astounded how Microsoft get away with providing such a dismal OS on computers/laptops when all it does is drive them mad :roll:

Again, thanks to Canonical ©, it's developers and the community for providing Ubuntu - it's far, far better than Window$.

I honestly can't praise you enough :guitar:
