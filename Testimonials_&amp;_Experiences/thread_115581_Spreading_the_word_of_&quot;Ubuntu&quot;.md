### Post by f0rw4rd sl4sh on 2006-01-10
Greetings my global peers,

I wanted to start off by thanking Mr. Shuttlesworth, Canonical, and everyone who made Ubuntu possible.  A little about myself, I am a  A+/Net+/MCP instructor in California. Ever since the realease of "Hoary" I have been enlightening my students, and other people of the solidity of Linux (Ubuntu in paticular) I explain everything from the least likely chance of virii, spyware infections, to system speed, also java, flash plugins, and codecs availability. My friends who have never heard of Linux are very willing to give it a try after asking me a few additional questions. I will tell them nothing is bullet proof, but it doesnt have a bullseye on its forehead like some proprietary OSes we are all familiar with.  Up to this day I have installed and configured about 10 computers with Ubuntu (Doesnt seem like much, but its a start). Not one person has called me about a problem with it. The last above statement has been enough for me to keep spreading the word of "Ubuntu". I want to remind people they have a choice, and that the alternative can suit their everyday computing needs just as well as any other proprietary OS.
