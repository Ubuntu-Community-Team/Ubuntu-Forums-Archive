### Post by reg4c on 2008-12-14
Hey, 

I started using Ubuntu with Kubuntu 6.06
And it has been a good and fun two years but the thing is there are a few things that really bothered me that made me go back to Winblowz. There are three main things that really bothered me with Ubuntu:

1. The fact that there was some unwritten rule that stated that every update must break a few things: usually some things that need to be compiled manually and some of the drivers.

2. The fact that there were always a few things on my computer that could never work: it was sound or wireless, or dial-up. Basically lack of driver support although that is improving.

3. And finally the fact that Wine is still not fully developed hence there are some compatibility issues with some of the programs that I use, mainly Warcraft III :D

Anyways, I hope this is not the end. I am installing Wubi as we speak I just cannot use Ubuntu as my primary OS. I will be however installing the 9.04 as soon as it is released hoping to find some of my "prayers" fulfilled.

I just thought that someone should know.
Cheers
