### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-06-05
It's good that your new system works just as you want it to.

The 8400GS with 256mb will perform fine for your desktop and Compiz, and some 3D games. And knowing that my 8600 works in Ubuntu, I have no doubts that an 8400 will work as well.

But it's a little bit behind the eight ball. The integrated graphics in the Mac Mini will outperform an 8400, I believe; that's some comparison for you. If you find yourself wanting a bit more 3D power without breaking the bank, try getting a 9600. They also work well with Ubuntu and they are the same cards often found in mid-range gaming desktops. You might also be able to get one cheap as Nvidia is changing its naming scheme and some stores might be desperate to get rid of their old-named stock.
