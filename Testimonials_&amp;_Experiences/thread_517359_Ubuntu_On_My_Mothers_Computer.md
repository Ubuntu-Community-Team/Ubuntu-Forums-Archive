### Post by zero244 on 2007-08-04
My mom is 85 and has limited computer experience. I built her a new system. Installed XP then installed Feisty in a dual boot setup.
XP is there only for a back door or for a piece of hardware that might not work on Ubuntu.
So she has beryl and feisty running very well.
I installed Ubuntu manly because it should be all she needs and I don't have to worry about viruses etc.
Its all customized up and running great on a wireless network card.
It took me a bit but I finally got the Edimax wireless card to work with her WEP enabled modem.
With Ubuntu she cant do too much damage to it because of the sudo feature and she should not pick up any of the garbage she was getting running XP.
So with some assistance I think Ubuntu is definitely ready for Grandma.
