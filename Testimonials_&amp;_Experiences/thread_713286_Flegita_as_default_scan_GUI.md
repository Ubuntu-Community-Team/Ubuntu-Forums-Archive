### Post by nealaustin on 2009-03-23
I couldn't get "Insane" to work well with my Cannon Pixma MP160. It actually would crash every time I wanted to change anything. What frustrated me the most was that the scanner utility that came with the printer/scanner worked well in windows. It was the only reason for me to boot into Win at all. I finally found Flegita and it actually works(well sort of). All of the graphics modes work well but scanning as a PDF is still off. It will make a postage stamp sized corner of the document into a 20 MB sized PDF. What I do is just take a Jpeg and load it into Open Office and the save it as a PDF. Flegita really does look promising and I can't wait for the 1.0 version to come out.
