### Post by Dragonbite on 2008-07-24
I was in the Library today and saw something that made me happy. I was helping my daughter at the computerized catalog set aside specifically for kids when I noticed a familiar, circular logo in the lower corner!

Sure enough, I clicked on it and the Ubuntu main menu popped up!  

The other computers running Windows were in some half-way-done process of being upgraded, or going through updates or something.

Made me smile to see the familiar Ubuntu in my library!

I doubt they will move all of them to Linux, though maybe just the card catalogs. The computers they have available for internet access I doubt they will change over.

She also mentioned her uncle works for IBM and that their are some friendly jabs between them about it.
