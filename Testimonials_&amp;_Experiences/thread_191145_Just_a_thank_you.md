### Post by iainm2 on 2006-06-07
I have been a user of Ubuntu since Warty and love it. It just installed with no hassles on the Dell Latitude CPt. Will be upgrading to Dapper soon. I have a Dell Inspiron 3000 (148Mb RAM and a slowwwww 233MHz processor) which has been running another small linux distro quite well. When I heard about Xubuntu, I followed the development closely and have tested the pre-releases as they came out. Xubuntu Dapper has been worth the wait. The Inspiron runs very smoothly on it, all of the niggles with the previous distro have gone..... for example - Netgear WG511T wireless network card found and easily configured. I love the fact that Abiword and Gnumeric are compatible with MS Office files but are also able to open and edit OpenOffice files. 
You developers may not realise how much of a difference you make to the rest of us lesser able mortals but I would like you to know that I am very grateful for all of the hard work you have put in to this project. 
Many thanks

Iain
