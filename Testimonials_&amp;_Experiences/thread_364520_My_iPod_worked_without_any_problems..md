### Post by Baelfael on 2007-02-18
I'm using Ubuntu Edgy, and I have a 30GB video ipod. I used the Add/Remove program to get a program called "gtkpod" (something like that at least), and I plugged in my iPod and let me tell you, it worked like a charm! It was more convenient than iTunes itself. I could easily manage my music, add files, and simply sync my iPod and I'm good to roll.

I dunno where Ubuntu falls into this, but hell, great job for making my life easier without windows! :)
