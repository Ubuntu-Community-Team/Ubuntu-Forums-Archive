### Post by cblnchat on 2011-02-08
Within the last few weeks ive been using Ubuntu again!!  And im loving it!  Everything works right away with no problems at all!  About 2.5 yrs ago.  i cant really remember but i was using 8.04.  I was having such bad problems with ubuntu!  Mainly a wireless usb that i just couldnt get to work.  After a few months of searching and learning up on ubuntu and linux i finally found out about ndiswrapper.  The worst thing ever, it wouldnt work at all.  Many many sleepless nights went to trying to figure it out but nothing worked.  And then i gave up after many months.  Then finally i was able to connect to the internet via a cable to my aunts router.  And i finally updated ubuntu, it took many hrs....then ubuntu stopped working altogether!  I was so pissed i stopped using ubuntu completely for the last few years.  Now ive come back cause i missed the look and feel and idea of ubuntu, and how changeable everything is!   So now im back and im loving it!  Everything is working right away.  I even put in that old wireless usb and it worked!!  Tho that was kinda pointless cause i have a netbook now. Lol.

Long story so thanks for reading!
