### Post by MasonM on 2006-05-13
I've been pretty impressed with the hardware detection on Ubuntu up until now. Today I decided to try something new. I have a Canon digital camera which I haven't used in a while.

The last time I tried to use it in Linux (Slackware) I had to go through a lot of hassles to get it working properly. Driver etc...

So today I got the camera out, snapped a few pics, and connected the camera to the laptop fully expecting it to not be recognized. Imagine my surprise when a window popped up asking me if I wanted to download the images from the camera! :eek: 

Still skeptical I told it to download the images and, sure enough, it actual did!
