### Post by solwic on 2008-12-08
Three scenarios:

1.  You spend the whole of your life blindfolded.  This is Microsoft Windows.

2.  You cut eye holes in your blindfold.  This is Ubuntu.

3.  You rip the blindfold off, gain super vision, and see everything there is to see.  This, my friends, is Kubuntu.  

I've been a loyal Ubuntu fan, but a mixture of boredom and curiosity led me to install Kubuntu in VirtualBox yesterday, which led me to giving it my whole hard drive today, wiping out Ubuntu completely.

Yeah I know they're the same, except for a handful of programs and the GUI, and that's what I mean.  I seriously recommend any of you who have yet to try Kubuntu to download Kubuntu 8.10 and give it a whirl.

I mean...use whatever you like and whatever is best for you, but I still think you should try it out.  :)

Wow.  That's all I can say right now.  Wow.  :cool:
