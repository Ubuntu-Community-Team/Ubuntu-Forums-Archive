### Post by Friatz on 2010-05-19
I just got _BOTH _of my parents on linux, permanently, I think. Mom has been using it for a few years now for general internet usage, and Dad, as of now, is finally beginning to show some interest.  Tonight, when I showed him how to copy his music over to his sansa fuze, he said the words "far out."   This is quite a break through considering the fact that he had constantly dissed the linux machine because he couldn't use microsoft programs.

I have them both on 9.10 over a wireless network, and I haven't had any major issues or complaints out of them since getting them settled into ubuntu.   *knock on wood*

Also, the fact that there is no more yelling and screaming about viruses and crashing computers is also nice ;)

I want to send a very deserving and much appreciated "thank you" to the Ubuntu software group on behalf of me and my parents.  You've finally allowed us to actually "USE" the computer rather than deal with viruses and bluescreens :)
