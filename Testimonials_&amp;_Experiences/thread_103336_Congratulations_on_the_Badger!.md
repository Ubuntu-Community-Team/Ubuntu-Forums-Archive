### Post by Lin-X on 2005-12-13
Earlier this fall, I tried Ubuntu for the first time, after several years with Xandros (an excellent beginner's distro), Mepis and some small distros --- Damn Small and Puppy, etc.
    I was a little disappointed because I found the Hedgehog very, very slow on the internet --- almost unusable, in fact, and I spend a great deal of time online. After problems with some other things (not being able to play my music, not being able to see all my hard drives and partitions), I got rid of Ubuntu and tried Suse.
     About two weeks ago, as I was struggling with Suse, I received my disks for the Badger and could not resist installing it. What a difference! The Badger is faster on the internet than my Windows and I have had no problem solving sound and partition problems. You people have really done a great job with this distro. I think I've found a home!
