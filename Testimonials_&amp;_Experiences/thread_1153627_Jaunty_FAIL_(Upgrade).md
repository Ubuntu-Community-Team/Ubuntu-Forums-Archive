### Post by nojjy on 2009-05-08
I have a 3 1/2 year old dual core amd64 with a nvidia geforce 7800.  I just upgraded to Jaunty from Intrepid.  It sucks.  Why?  There are several reasons:
- Root terminal will not load
- sound crackles
- nvidia drivers were not upgraded, it defaulted to the nv drivers
- video plays back at an uneven speed on ALL VIDEO PLAYERS
- boot up time really isn't that much better
- haven't really noticed anything better that backports couldn't have improved
- tried downgrading the nvidia drives after installing them, from 180 to 177 through the Hardware Drivers interface.  After working for about 15 seconds, the result?  Nothing.  It won't change it.
I guess I'm too busy of a person anymore to be on the cutting edge, because I simply don't have time to report, track and follow the potential fixes to all of these problems.  I guess I've learned my lesson, stick to the LTS releases.
