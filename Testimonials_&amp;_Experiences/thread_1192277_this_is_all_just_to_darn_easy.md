### Post by stinger30au on 2009-06-19
wel, i have upgraded my kids pc today

it was running a pentium 3 933Mhz cpu with 512 meg of ram and a cd rom drive and xp with kids programs on it

i replaced the mobo with an athlon 2700 with 1 gig of ram and a nvidia mx440 64 meg agv video card.
i kept the 60 gig ide hdd and dropped in a dvd burner

nothing flash at all

installed ubuntu 9.04 and once installed ran the desktop install for edubuntu-desktop


sudo apt-get install edubuntu-desktop

have also added codecs to play the kids dvds with and a few extra games for the kids to play with

all done, in under and hour

and with it plugged in to the net it does all its updates in the background automagcially

damn!

my cant all o/s'es be this damn easy to install/config/use

all the dvds play, all web sites work and have audio/video, the nvidia card works, it prints to my canon i865 across the lan, kids can save homework on the server too

UBUNTU ROCKS!\\:D/
