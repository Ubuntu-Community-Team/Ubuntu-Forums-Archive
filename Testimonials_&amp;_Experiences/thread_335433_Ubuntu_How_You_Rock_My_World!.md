### Post by motlive on 2007-01-10
Ive had a dabble over the years with some linux desktops, Gentoo and Knoppix, but never had any real passion to see how far i could take it as I played games and I couldnt play them. 

Anyway the company I work for was changing the OS on their dedicated servers from FreeBSD to Ubuntu, never hearing of Ubuntu i had a little investigation. 

seeing the screenshots of the desktop i was caught.. got the live CD that night and played around with it for a few days, then went and got an external drive and used that on my laptop to install Ubuntu on.

I then asked my work if i could use Ubuntu instead of Windows, but no go :( 

and now i am going to build a Media Centre with Ubuntu as the main client :)

Ubuntu is the turning point to a world of goodness, and i will be making a donation for the effort of the people involved.
