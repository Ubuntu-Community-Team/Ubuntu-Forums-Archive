### Post by Motorhead Kaze on 2010-11-16
No questions today, just wanted to voice my satisfaction with Lucid. Installed it today and it is the first release I have installed that I have liked right away since Hardy...which I have just continued to use all this time because each release after 8.04 just seemed to fly in the face of the "if it ain't broke don't fix it" rule. Except for the fact that I had to move my window min/max buttons BACK to the right where I think they should be, I would say this is the most smooth Ubuntu I have used since the mighty Hardy was released. Setup was a breeze, and would be the best into to Ubuntu for new people to date.

Side note, installing Japanese and setting up dual-heads is seriously easy now. Very nice system we got here. Love it.
