I can't stress the last sentence here enough.  Always, always, always back up before upgrading.  It doesn't matter if you installing ubuntu, or a new version of the same OS, there is always the chance something won't go well for some unseen condition.  This is one of the most fantastic things about a live-CD.  you can do this in-line with your installation, as a step before actually installing.
