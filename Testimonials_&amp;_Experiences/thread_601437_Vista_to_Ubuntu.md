### Post by starboyhull on 2007-11-03
Hi guys,

Just wanted to add my expereince (although very limited) to this forum.

I have been running a Dell latitude D820 for about a year now with XP, then Vista and finally Ubuntu. Having had soooo many problems with Vista - network failing for 5 minutes at a time, "my computer" not working, cdrom drive stopped working, etc etc etc the list was betting bigger and bigger, which made support our clients impossbile, if my pc isnt working, unlikely i'm going to be able to help anyone else.......

So after being on another forum and someone mentioned moving from XP to Ubuntu I thought "lets give it a try" after a few hours of playing about and getting very frustrated with the wpa security issues I finally downloaded a few extra apps for ubuntu and hey presto, all up and running, and very surprised how quick it is.......

I now have VPN access to our network, access to my exchange mail our internal instant messenger and everything else I use, with one exception, not managed to get the printer to work yet :confused:

Apart from that everything seems to be running very smoothly, with vmware server now running and a copy of XP in vmware (to remind me of how XP looks) what a great expereince this is becoming, well done to everyone who contributes to this community.
