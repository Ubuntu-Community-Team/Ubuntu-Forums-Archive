### Post by ventrical on 2012-10-19
After spending a lot of time  beta testing  ubuntu products  I can say from experience that Ubuntu-Unity is the fastest ever.  There is a definite increase of response times in the dash/search  fields making Unity much faster than it's counter-part Precise. Also , the Oct. 14th Daily live quantal-desktop-i386.iso replicated a strange phenomenon that happened during the the 10.10 (maveric meerkat) cycle, in that the USB live iso runs faster than the hard drive!! 

  The Bird of Paradise trumps the land dwelling crumudgeon anteater by more than just a nose. Although Precise is solid in it's own right it lacks just a little bit of the *zest* that Quantal Quetzel has been able to employ.

 I have to give a pat on the back to the developers for their hard work on this.

  Also , older machines have not been left out of the fray. Although  I do have some sticking points with the way the new Unity works on obsoleted video graphics adapters there is more  than enough tools  and desktops in the free-store to make quantal a great transition piece for future releases. Just a great job.... and thank-you to the Canonical Team(s) for your dedication to exellence, snappiness and verve.

Regards,
ventrical
