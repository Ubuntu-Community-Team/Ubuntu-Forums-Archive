### Post by binukavumkal on 2008-12-31
I started dual boot on my Desktop with Hardy Heron/Windows XP on June 2008. 

Since then I very rarely used Windows XP. Hardly 3 or 4 times to check my Internet connectivity.


1.For a common user, Ubuntu is stable enough to manage all the  daily requirements like Internet browsing, emailing, Office applications

2.The Ubuntu Community is one of the best community I have ever  seen in my web life.:popcorn: This community helped me in many critical conditions

3.Free Open Source Software lacks professional support. In case of Ubuntu, Ubuntu Forums tries to fill this gap

4.If you are experienced MS Windows user, you may feel a lot of difficulty initially. Again don't t keep the problems/issues to yourself. Google it or post it in Ubuntu forums. You will definitely get an answer.

I would like to thank all my friends who are part of this community, Ubuntu Software development/testing community.

I am ready to help to this community in whatever way I can. I don't know where to start, how to help. Please suggest.

A very happy, prosperous, peaceful new year ahead to all of you.

Binu
