### Post by rolnics on 2010-02-15
But I'm loving in it!

Let me explain, the reason its been force upon me. My main system's psu has gone to a greater place and until I get a replacement I'm having to use a Dell gx150(PIII). I'm so glad I burnt the RC I had a few weeks ago, otherwise I don't know what I would have done?!

Anyway, apart from the dodgy graphics because of compiz, which is now switched off, everything runs like a dream! Boot times seem really fast, not sure of times, but it seems faster than my P4. I'll time them both when its up and running again. Installation was a breeze, and I was impressed how fast it seemed. I'm only really using the net with this machine, although I did transfer some pictures and all went well. So far so good, I'm impressed by Karmic.

Having said this though, I'm probably going to stick with the Heron(8.04) on my main system until 10.04 comes out. But for now 9.10 Karmic, is here to stay, I might even stick with this setup, its doing what I want for now. And save the pennies to upgrade my main system a tad!

### Post by stalkingwolf on 2010-02-15
another thing to watch on older systems is the processor heat sink and fan.

I was having a problem with random shut downs.  I was about convinced that it was the power supply.   Finally I had all my ducks in a row and started
the job of replacing the P/S.    In the process i decided to pull the fan and clean it.  The first thing I saw was that the heat sink was about 2/3
plugged with airborne gunk. and so hot you couldnt hold it.

That was 3 weeks ago.  I cleaned everything and put it back together with out touching the P/S.    havent had a problem since.
