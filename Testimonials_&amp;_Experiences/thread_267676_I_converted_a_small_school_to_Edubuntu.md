### Post by Zweih on 2006-09-28
Well, I'm in the process of, anyway. I've installed Edubuntu on 3 old Dell OptiPlexes so far, and the teacher is thrilled with all of the educational apps available. Currently, he's teaching the class typing with Ktouch (3 at a time [it's only a class of 10 btw :P]). It's great, and it gives me a great feeling that I'm helping these kids become more computer-literate through the power of Edubuntu. I give my thanks to the Edubuntu team for creating a wonderful distro.
