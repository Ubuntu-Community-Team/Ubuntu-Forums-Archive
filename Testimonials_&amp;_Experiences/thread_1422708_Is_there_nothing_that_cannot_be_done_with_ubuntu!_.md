### Post by Fenris_rising on 2010-03-05
Tomorrow I am helping a friend fix his XP based laptop. Sadly not by installing Linux but my Ubuntu has played it's part in preparation.

There is no driver disk for the laptop so with the 'name, rank and number' I googled and found the drivers. The download was an .nrg so I figured I'd have to whip the file from my ubuntu onto my wifes vista monster to use nero to burn the iso.

As a habit since my conversion to ubuntu I google 'burn nrg with ubuntu'

WOW! There was an answer so I installed nrg2iso and I now have the iso burnt all done on ubuntu.Simples!!

Yet another point for ubuntu/linux in my book. It just makes computing so much fun =)

Of course the title of this post is just inviting a plethora of 'yes there is plenty ubuntu/linux can't do' So lighten up. My point is such a small thing just makes life that bit easier.

regards

Fenris (continually impressed with linux/ubuntu)
