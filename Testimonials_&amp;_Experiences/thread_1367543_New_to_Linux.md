No questions yet, I have been able to find all my answers in the forums and on the web.

After being a windows user for many many many years, I decided to try linux on my new laptop.........  tried a few different flavors but came back to Ubuntu 9.10 after finally geting a

So far im very Happy with Ubuntu
special thanks to those in the forums who are always available for help
and the top notch team who write this software !!
