### Post by Tanjer1588 on 2007-11-30
Just last sunday Nov 25 i became a Ubuntu 7.10 Linux user and here is how my trip has been going so far.

I installed Ubuntu via live cd, it was very easy to do and i didn't seem to have a lot of trouble with it, it was very user friendly and clean. The only thing that turned out for the worst was the partition sizes. So after i got it installed i jumped into it right away eager to explore my brand new OS (the only OS other then windows i have ever used) When i first got in, i was amazed at how clean and organized every thing was, there was no searhing need to find the most basic programs.

My first task to accomplish with Ubuntu was to get my music and photos from windows over to Ubuntu, I spent some time looking for a way to do this and most of what i found told me to just back up the info before i installed (witch i didn't do). After exploring a bit more i was surprised to find that i could go right into the windows partition and pull them out of there O.O i was stunned.

In my search for my music i learned about partitions and noticed that my storage was only 20 gigs XD, so then came a new project, figure out what happened. After spending hours searching and reading, i was ready to go... After a number of attempts to shrink my windows partition without success i decided to just kill windows... So... with a click of a mouse i killed windows. I then had to move my Linux partition, delete the newly formated partition and expand my home :D. This took some time but was a success.

after that it was smooth sailing, find new visual effects, like painting fire on my screen and the cubed desktop was a blast. I am havening so much fun with Linux.

I would say that Linux dose require some one willing to learn and fix things on there OS lucky for you guys im one of them. The fact that things don't work right off the bat (only some times) makes it fun to learn how to fix it and edit it. But i could see why this would be hard for a common user, some one who is used to a GUI where one click gets them on the internet or makes there computer do something really cool. 

All in all i would say that Linux is amazing and im glad that i made the move, im looking foreword to making my computer the best that it can be

Can i call you dad?
