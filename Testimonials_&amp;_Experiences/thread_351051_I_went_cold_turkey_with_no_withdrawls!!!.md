### Post by cbrehm on 2007-02-01
Well I went cold turkey!!! I wiped my hard drive and installed Ubuntu 6.10 today.

It is working great. I am very impressed.

A few years ago I tried replacing windows but Red Hat 6 would not work with my computer at the 
time. But I tried several live CDs and they all booted up and recognized most of my peripherals
immediatly. The interface is clean and easy to navigate and most importantly I can access all
of my data and files I have with no problems at all.

I've been reading a lot of the forums to find replacements for all the programs I use in windows.
The user support is increadable and was one of the one things that made me comfotable with
making the change.

I still have to get my HP 845c printer driver and my Nvidia Gforce 4 so I can get my dual monitors
working again. Ubuntu won't boot with the Geforce card in the machine. But that is minor stuff
that I can work on and learn about my new OS.

Thank you Ubuntu team and thanks to all the great people on these forums. I'm so excited I'm
telling everyone I know about Ubuntu and getting some of my friends with old computers to use
it instead of windows 98 or ME.
