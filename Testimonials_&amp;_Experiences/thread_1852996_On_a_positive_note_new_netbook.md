It's ridiculously easy to tether an Android phone to Ubuntu, and I love it.

Plug in the USB cable, go into the Settings on the Android phone and enable USB tethering. Network Manager on the Ubuntu machine will then connect to the phone and you can start surfing in seconds.

Love it! I just feel sorry for the people whose phone contracts don't allow them to tether.
