### Post by Eternlloveoflife on 2005-12-01
Dear fellow ubuntu users, 

I've been using microsuck since 3.1 days, and given I'm only 20 years old, that's quite a bit of time.    You see I was practically raised on a computer, but never had one of my own until a little recently.  I came to the conclusion quite quickly that I wanted to get rid of this POS they call windows XP home edition. So I began to check out various linux distributions.

First, there was SuSE, which, wasn't quite too bad, but the fact that I had to burn some ungodly ammount of CDs, and PAY for it, wasn't exactly appealing. So there went that idea...

Second, came Knoppix.  Now, no disk install mind you, just messing aroudn with it, and I liked it, I liked it a lot, aside from not supporting WEP, it was perfect, but I had no idea how to install it to disk, so I didn't use it much...

Third, Fedora Core 4.  Oh my god.  Fscking Fedora Core 4.  I can't stand this distro.  The endless RPM hell, the fact that my wireless wasn't supported nor the fact that I couldn't get my sound to work at all, made it that much worse.

Fourth, and lastly, Ubuntu.  Now, last time I checked, Ubuntu isn't sold in stores, and henceforth wasn't known to me until quite recently.  A freind of mine from a radio station that I listen to, (di.fm (sorry folks, don't mean to advertise, hehe)), a freind named kvidell.  He's all, hey, check this out.  So I goto the site, spend about 3 hours on the wiki page, several more on the forums, and have been glued in the #ubuntuforums/#ubuntu rooms ever since.  

One of the things that caught my eye, was the fact that it could be shipped for FREE.  That's insane, I can't believe they'd do that free of charge!  That right there told me a great deal about this community.  Then I learned that Mark Shuttleworth funds it directly, Mr OuterSpace himself.  I read on his bio for another hour or so, and was greatly impressed.  So far I'm loving everything about this community

Ubuntu, is beautiful, aside from some minor burning problems I had, (which were due in part because of my unability to figure out how to use FC4), the installation was flawless.  My sound works perfectly, screen resolution, WEP, gaim, video, burning, you name it, I can do it.  

Thank you so much, as I continue to learn more and more about the debian core, the linux kernel, and Ubuntu in general, I hope to contribute to the developement of this great and powerful operating system.

Thank you so much!

Paul Waldorf
