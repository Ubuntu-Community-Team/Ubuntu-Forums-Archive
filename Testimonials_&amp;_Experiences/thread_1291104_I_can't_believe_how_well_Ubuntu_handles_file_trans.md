### Post by Algus on 2009-10-14
When I originally transferred all of my music from my PC to my laptop (around 5 gigs so not as much as some but a good amount) it took hours, crashed my modem, and bogged down both systems so badly that they just had to sit.

Just now finished transferring from my Vista PC to my Ubuntu laptop (running the transfer through Ubuntu) Took barely more than an hour, router is running and I was still able to use my computer, with net browsing only having minimal slowdown.  

I was amazed by how wonderful it worked and just felt I had to share.  I'm still looking for a good manager or something for handling my files but for anyone wondering about the ease of transferring data between machines (over a wireless network in my case) Ubuntu does it efficiently and quickly!
