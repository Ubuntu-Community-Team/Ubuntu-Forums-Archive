### Post by echolink on 2007-11-07
Hello all, my name is Ben Otten, I recently moved from PCLinuxOS 07 to Ubuntu 7.04 Feisty, yesterday. Now I have used a few different Linux Distro's in my time, and all I have to say is WOW!!! This is my first experience on the Gnome Desktop and I have to say all in all it has alot of nice features compared to KDE, now dont get me wrong I like KDE, but Gnome is simply amazing on Ubuntu. So what do I like about it well first of all it actually detect all my hardware out of the box(other than a minor router/proxy issue), and most importantly I didnt have to have and slice to get my HP PSC 1210 All in One to work, it simply worked :) What about my video card well I have a Nvidia PCI BFG 6200 256mb car (mobo dosnt have agp :( oh well its linux lol) well I had to origionally boot up with the onboard video because X would not detect the resolution correctly the onboard actually handled ubuntu with desktop effects quite well to my surprise so I ran that all day yesterday being lazy :). So today I dl Envy to handle my nvidia driver install, ran the installer, saved my X file rebooted, went to bios reset primary video adapter to PCI, booted and bam!!! Ubuntu on my BFG 6200 with no probs. So other than that I was able to get everything up and going, set up my fav apps, plugins, dl some themes, and icons, customized a bit, and I was set. Here we are Day 2 on Ubuntu from PCLinuxOS which is still a equally great distro as Ubuntu is and I have made the decision to run Ubuntu as my primary OS. So to Ubuntu, and the Community I give you all a thumbs up and am proud to say I am a permanet part of the Ubuntu Community from now on....:guitar:
