### Post by mingle on 2008-07-19
Hi,

I have one of those cheap and cheerful (and pretty damn good) HP PSC-1310 printer/scanner/copier machines and today I finally plucked up the courage to connect it to my Ubuntu (8.04) box. 

I connected the USB cable and after about 30 seconds, with no fuss or annoying requesters, it was installed and ready to go! I tested it using XSane image scanner and it worked perfectly - I dare say faster and slicker than on my XP box!

There was no need to install the hugely bloated HP printer/scanner drivers like for XP...

Printing too was a speedy and trouble-free delight!

To say I'm impressed is an understatement. I'm not sure if HP printers are well supported under Linux - I did no research, I just connected the printer via the USB cable and that was it.

Ubuntu isn't perfect and I can't use it to completely replace my trusty old XP box, but it's getting there! :-)

Just thought I'd let others know!

Cheers,

Mike.
