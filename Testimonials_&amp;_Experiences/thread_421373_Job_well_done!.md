### Post by exploder on 2007-04-24
Until now I had not been too much of a Ubuntu fan. Releases before Feisty never seemed to work very well with my hardware. I downloaded the Feisty Live CD and thought I would play around with it just for curiosity's sake. I was completely blown away by how fast and stable it was! Applications such as Firefox seemed optimized for Ubuntu, fonts looked great, the icons and overall appearance  looked finished and professional and Evolution worked without crashing! Also, Gnome and the included applications were up to date.
    I liked what I saw but before I blew away the distribution I had spent weeks configuring I checked out how Feisty handled multimedia. I found that Automatix2 was available for Feisty and offered and easy route to get my codecs and some nice extras I had always wanted to try but could never get to work in other distributions. Once my homework was done I was ready to install.
    The installation was quick and trouble free! Once installed I ran Automatix2, installed the Gnome Slab menu, set up e-mail and changed the wallpaper. Everything works, all my multimedia plays, Google Earth, DVD playback, streaming audio, movie trailers, EVERYTHING! 
     The entire process took about an hour! I still can not believe how easy the whole process was! The Ubuntu team has done an excellent job with Feisty. It should not take hours or weeks to get multimedia working. Ubuntu has taken the lead in my opinion for being multimedia friendly. I sincerely wish to thank the Ubuntu Team for their hard work, job well done!
