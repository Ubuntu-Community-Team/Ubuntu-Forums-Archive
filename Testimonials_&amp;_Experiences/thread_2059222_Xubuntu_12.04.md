### Post by OrangeCrate on 2012-09-18
I have nothing against Unity, and the software center, but, I've been around Linux for a long time now, and old habits are hard to break. I started looking around for an alternative to Ubuntu, and though I had tried Xubuntu in the past, I wasn't impressed. That is, until I tried 11.04, and immediately kept it.

I'm typing this from my old T43, loaded with Xubuntu 12.04, and I've been so happy with the last couple of Xubuntu releases, that I've now even converted the office computer, that has only had Ubuntu LTS versions since Dapper, to Xubuntu 12.04 as well. 

I add several programs, such as Libreoffice, to suit my needs. Overall, for the forseeable future, Xubuntu is the one for me. Very nice indeed! Simple, quick and familiar, are words of praise, that immediately come to mind.
