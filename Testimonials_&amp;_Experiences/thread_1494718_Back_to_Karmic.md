### Post by plurworldinc on 2010-05-27
For the past few weeks I have been racking my brain on my wifi issues with Lucid. Now I have been telling myself that isn't not the operating systems fail and there has to be a fix out there somewhere that would allow my laptop to stay connected more the 15min to and hour depending on what I was doing and if I am surfing the web or not. 

   So for me it's back to Karmic for my laptop and I am now looking to upgrade my desktop system with Lucid instead because I don't have to worry about wifi on my desktop. Beside the wifi issue, which for a laptop is a very huge and normally deal breaking issue, it's a great OS. I love the look and feel of where Ubuntu is going and I am proud to be a user and promote Linux and open source to all of my friends and family.

   I am hoping that in a few months or even shorter there will be a fix for my wifi on my current laptop, but if there isn't I am still hoping to purchase a new laptop later this year and hopefully I will be able to install Lucid with out a problem.
