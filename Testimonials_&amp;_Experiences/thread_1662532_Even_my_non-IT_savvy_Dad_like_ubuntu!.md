### Post by vcrpex on 2011-01-08
just wanna share, I just installed ubuntu 10.10 64bit on half of my dad's desktop computer with the other half still on vista yesterday. the Vista is so slow on the HP dx2300 MT. he is just using that for email, surfing and citrix to access his workplace's computer. Yeah, the machine may not be state of the art but it still shouldnt be so slow. Once i boot it up with my usb installer, my dad was surprised how fast it is just running on the flash usb. now that I have installed ubuntu as well as the ICA client and mt4 trading on it, he have a decent machine to use without buying a new desktop. Thank you Ubuntu!! honestly i hope this can reduce the amount of times i have to go down to fix my dad's pc as well.
