### Post by christopher.wortman on 2011-10-25
Ubuntu 11.04 Unity was a disaster. As was 11.10's (no surprise) but I expected better, but meh.

Kubuntu is like day to Unity's night.

I have been using Kubuntu since 6.10 as my main OS and always did even during the hard times of 4.0~4.3. There was always a fix or my sheer laziness to keep me using it.

Holy Yeshuah Messiah KDE is just fabulous. Why Canonical developers hold onto the hope that Unity and GTK will somehow magically be OSX instead of looking like a cheap knockoff clone is beyond anyone here...

I keep reading all these never ending articles about how much Unity sucks. It is something to love to hate I suppose instead of feeding the monster and switching to KDE people still cling to it... I am not saying give up Linux, heck don't give up ubuntu, just give up the trash heap that is Unity. It is only 2 releases old, but from the times I have played with it, I can not figure out why any sane person would spend more than 20 minutes trying to figure it out, realizing they killed any sane menu system and killed task icons and going "this is a piece of crap, let's try something else". No instead they sit and complain about things, trying insanely the same thing over and over expecting different results. 

Why is this in the experience section? Because it is becoming a bad experience coming here, with the intent on helping people only to read thread after thread of issues that simply don't exist, and have never existed KDE side. Come to the light side, the dark side may have cookies, but we have cake and pie, and you can eat it too. On the dark side they may have cookies but you are tied down and forced to stare at them without more than a crumb for a taste to educate the palate.

Move on people! It obviously doesn't work and by the looks of it, they have no intent on adding options we want. Instead they are actively removing ones we didn't know we wanted until they were gone. Make a statement to the developers and switch, then maybe they will realize "hey people don't want this crap" This is Linux and you don't have to take developer-user abuse. Go somewhere else and try something different, the beauty of choice.
