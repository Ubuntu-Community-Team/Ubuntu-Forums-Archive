### Post by Ice Dragon on 2008-04-27
Hi all, im a new linux user, I decided that with the release of ubuntu 8.04 I would wipe out windows and dive right in, as ive been reading things for awhile about linux and ubuntu specifically.

At first when I installed from the CD it would keep crashing and kernel panicing, which was very disheartening, I thought several times, well if it doesnt "just work" then im going back to windows. But I decided to troubleshoot a bit, I took my computer apart peice by peice and kep trying to install.

Finally I figured out it was my Gigafast wireless card that was causing hte kernel panic. I unplugged it, installed and everything worked right away. I installed compiz fusion advanced effects, installed advant window navigator, and the cube, now my system looks and runs beautifully. 

Im running on wired internet right now until i can buy a new wireless card or figure out whats wrong with the usb gigafast one, but overall.. Ubuntu is a thing of beauty.

Thanks, keep up the good work, now i have to keep learning and figuring this thing out!
