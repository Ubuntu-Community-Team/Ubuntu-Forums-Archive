### Post by JamezQ on 2010-03-27
I remember the day vista came out, I thought it was going to be so cool. Begged my dad and he got me a computer with it installed.

I hated it, I was underwhelmed and it got me looking into other options. Vista would take minutes just to login, and it got slower every time. I had a way better experience with XP. 

However I knew I didn't want a mac, I never have liked them, and probably never will. But I did hear about this "Linux thing". Back then I didn't even know what linux was. So I did what I always do, I typed it into google.

The first distro I tried was DSL, I used it from withen vista and I thought I was really cool. I diden't know a OS could even run off a CD.

Then I looked into Ubuntu, I read about it and heard that you can even keep ubuntu and windows on the same computer! I burnt a disk and tried it post haste. 

Everything worked when I logged in after installing 9.04 for the first time, internet was instantly up. I played around with it and really enjoyed it. I already used firefox at the time so that was a plus. 

Then after my fun I logged back into windows....

I had overwritten my windows partition, all my data. All my parents data, was gone. I eventually got vista back up and running but I never recovered my data. I said I wouldn't do something like that again. Good think that diden't last.

At first I blamed ubuntu, so I tried other distros. I diden't like any of them. But then i tried one that changed whole view on linux. Puppy linux.

I was in love, It was so cool and easy. It diden't harm anything and I felt like such the little hacker using it. I stuck with puppy for a while, using on and off. Eventually I looked into ubuntu again. I read more about how to dual boot and finally did it successfully. I was happy.

However I still used windows, because I played guild wars. I tried many linux games, I didn't like most of them. Except tremulous. But that diden't seal the deal for me yet. What did was ubuntu 9.10. It was faster, cooler looking and ran tremulous so fast my ping went from 198 to 50. I started playing better and better in trem, people started to know me, and I finally choose to play tremulous over guild wars for my main game. However, It probably doesn't hurt the guild I was in failed, and all my friends left that game.

So it's because of Puppy that I use linux today, even though I primarily use Ubuntu, puppy is still my favorite linux distro.

So thats me. I still play tremulous a lot, and run a rather popular clan there. I am also trying ubuntu 10.04 on my laptop. I really like how it is evolving.

I have tried windows 7, I like it, a lot. More then xp, more then vista. Even more then ubuntu (expect maybe 10.04).

However even though I know I can get Windows for free, I use ubuntu on principle because even though it is better then vitsa. It is in no way worth 150$ more then ubuntu. Honestly now that I know about ubuntu the most I would pay for windows is 30$. And i would still keep ubuntu on my computer.
