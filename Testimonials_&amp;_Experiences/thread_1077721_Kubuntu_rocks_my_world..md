### Post by BlakeM on 2009-02-22
OK, first a huge thank-you to Mark Shuttleworth for supporting the creation of Ubuntu and its cousins (Kubuntu, Xubuntu, etc.) and for making them free for (relatively) poor students such as myself. I can't thank you enough.

If possible, I'd also like to make an ever bigger thank-you to the developers who donate their time and write open source, free software. It's people like you who inspire others to contribute their time and knowledge to the community. In fact, I intend to use my skill with writing to create many HOWTOs and other articles about Ubuntu and the software you write.

The first article I write will be about KDE and how incredibly awesome it is. I'm amazed at the quality, ingenuity and (I'll be honest) sheer sex appeal of this desktop environment. KDE for the win!

Blake

(a cynical, skeptical med student)
