### Post by el_heffe on 2010-11-02
Go back in time just about two years, I was forward deployed to Iraq and finally got some extra cash.  Took me about five months but I finally had my media center PC completed.  Low profile Desktop with two winTV cards in it that were FULLY supported by Mythbuntu.  Turned it on, installed the OS, ran the setup and shipped it back to my wife in Hawai'i.

Boy was she PISSED!  ANOTHER COMPUTER?!?

Then I got home, hooked it up to the series of tubes known as the internet, got everything working, and hooked it up to our cable.  Then I started playing with it.  Recorded some Sex in the City, lots of different shows for the little man, and more.  She watched and learned, and still didn't say anything.  I recorded Sex and the City and she didn't say anything- I knew this was going to be tough.

A week later I get back from work and she turns to me, smiles, and says- you know- I can watch SATC all the time now!!  Translation: she is no longer pissed.

Move forward about a year, and I am moved to Arizona, where they do not have analogue cable, they have digital cable, and it requires the use of their box to change channels.  I get a IR Blaster, and it doesn't work.  I try this, I try that, no luck.  So I toss & turn, hem & haw, and decide to install Winblows Media Center.  It works. :(

Fast forward another six months, and I am now in Afghanistan talking to the Mrs on the phone.  Turns out that she finds Windows too complicated and wants Linux back.  
Translation: she prefers linux!!

So now, when I get my sorry behind back to the states, its back to Mythbuntu(YAY!!!!) and back to the drawing board on getting that DAMNED IR blaster to work(YAY PROJECT!!).  

tl;dr- wife likes linux more because it is easier and looks better than windows.  Go figure.
