### Post by bd@cb8be8510 on 2010-06-21
Some years ago I installed 6.10. It took some time to get familiar with Linux (and I had to compile my wireless-driver :( and had to buy a new scanner :(). The guys on the forum were very helpful, I had a warm feeling to be part of a community where people were willing to help each other, were ready to share knowledge and so I could live with some minor issues.

After using 7.04, 7.10, 8.04 and I stuck to 8.10 as I could not solve my wireless problems after installing 9.04. I was happy with 8.10 after all.
Because 8.10 was no longer supported this year I decided to try 10.4. My first impressions were waw, waaw, waaawwww!! Everything installed smoothly and worked out of the box! Elegant themes ... eye-candy everywhere. After a while a got a rather uneasy feeling ... how is it possible to you can install such an OS for free? I know, it is not that free as you're supposed to cooperate, but for the time being I haven't
the time available to do that. I felt uneasy about that. The only thing I can do so far is recommend Ubuntu to relatives and friends. Try to report problems as good as I can ...
And last but not least say thank you to you all for the good work done!
Thank you all! ):P):P
