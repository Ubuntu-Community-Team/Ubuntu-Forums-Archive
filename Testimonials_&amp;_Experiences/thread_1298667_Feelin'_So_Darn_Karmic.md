### Post by 1roxtar on 2009-10-23
I have to say that I am totally geeking out with Karmic Koala!!!  I have downloaded and tested these three Karmic Release Candidates....Ubuntu, Kubuntu, and Ubuntu Netbook Remix.

Ubuntu:  I LOVE IT!!!  Ubuntu 9.04 was my introduction to Linux and the Ubuntu distro.  Linux was something I heard about but never knew just what it was, until this year.  Since then, I have tried other distros out of curiosity....Fedora 10, Linux Mint, OpenSuse, Damn Small Linux...but embraced UBUNTU.

Thanks to the Ubuntu Community, I quickly learned about repositories, Synaptic Package Manager, The Terminal and so much more.  I am a complete Linux convert and I talk about Ubuntu all the time.  I take my laptop to work and netbook to college.  My laptop is full-on Ubuntu and my netbook is dual-booted with Windows XP, though I rarely ever use it. 

In my opinion, Jaunty (9.04) was pretty darn close to perfect for my needs.  I have so much fun personalizing it.  I have never felt like this about any Windows OS.  I'm a PC Tech too and hated installing a Windows OS and then having to search for all the drivers, antivirus/spyware, and firewall programs.  Ubuntu supports and detected all the hardware on ALL my machines, old and new.  

I am so exited about the upcoming final release of Ubuntu 9.10 Karmic Koala.  In my opinion, it's as good and better as any OS can be.  It's one of the "Big Boys" now....powerful, stable, attractive, full-featured and #1 in my book.  Oh, and did I forget 100% FREE!!!  Thank you Canonical, Mark Shuttleworth and the Ubuntu Community.  You are my Heroes!!!

:guitar:

*Had to add this:  (Ubuntu 9.10 RC) No more choppy video on my Acer Aspire One netbook.  THANK YOU!!!!!!!!!!!!!
