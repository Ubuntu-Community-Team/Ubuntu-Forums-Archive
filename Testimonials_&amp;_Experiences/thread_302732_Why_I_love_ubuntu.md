### Post by sonic7 on 2006-11-19
I just got another 20 GB hard drive for my computer. I say the ubuntu CD lying around on my desk and thought, "What the heck, I'll do it" and inserted the Live CD. Having used a Knoppix Live CD unsuccessfully, I was a bit curious to what would happen. Ubuntu started up, and I was faced with the desktop. I started up Firefox, no internet, so I went into System>Preferences and went into the network panel. With two clicks, ubuntu connected up to my other PC running Windows XP! That was seriously simple, so now I love it!
