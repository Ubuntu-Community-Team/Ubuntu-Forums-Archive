### Post by SeanBlader on 2009-11-05
Maybe I got lucky, maybe I just have the right hardware, but absolutely EVERYTHING got better with Karmic on my system. I really have nothing but raves for it. I have the 4gb Dell Adamo, and with Jaunty it was about 80%, sound was rough, and I couldn't use external displays, but with Karmic, I just found out my DisplayPort works like a charm, I can even plug into analog projectors at the office with the VGA dongle, the sound is 90%, volume is much improved, headphone jack works perfectly, and there's one last hangup on the system, it's the on-board microphone that doesn't work. I'm pretty sure it's the wrong setting in alsa-base.conf but given that everything else works pretty good, I'm not keen on trial and error testing to find the right one. I feel bad for everyone who's had problems, and I picked up the Adamo specifically to use as a super portable Ubuntu system and have been very satisfied with how well everything works on it.

There is one thing I haven't tried, and that's using the built-in 3G GSM card, haven't wanted to pay for a data plan when I have wi-fi everywhere I use it.
