### Post by coffeecat on 2013-03-23
The Testimonials and Experiences sub-forum has been a useful resource for those wishing to post about their experiences of Ubuntu, both positive and negative, but it has caused problems over the years. To address these problems, a [discussion about the management of this section of the forum](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1848659) took place followed by a [vote by the forum membership](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1884598). Since then threads have been closed after one week, or earlier if they have strayed from [the sub-forum posting guidelines](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1921679)

This has helped to reduce the number of trollfests but has not been satisfactory. A large number of closed threads looks bad and staff are always criticised or accused of censorship if threads are closed early. The staff are placed in a no-win situation with the current arrangement and are, frankly, tired of it.

Because of this, the Forum Council has been looking for a solution which better meets the needs of the forum membership. We will be opening a new “Ubuntu, Linux and OS Chat” sub-forum for non-support discussions in a few days time. The Cafe will then become a place for any non-tech non-support chat, with the exception of religion and politics. People who wish to post testimonials will be free to do so in the new Ubuntu, Linux and OS Chat forum, but we hope the chat will be more wide-ranging than that. The old Testimonials and Experiences sub-forum will be closed to new threads at the same time as the new Ubuntu, Linux and OS Chat sub-forum is opened, and then closed to all posts a week thereafter.
