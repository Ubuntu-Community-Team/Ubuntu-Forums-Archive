### Post by computerex on 2011-12-05
Hello. I started using Ubuntu at the release of dapper. I then took a break from using it much for some time, until today. I would just like to say that the improvement from Dapper/Gutsy to 10.04 is simply mind blowing. I had absolutely *no* problems in the installation. It was complete in minutes and was throughly uneventful. The first time I booted Lynx I was very surprised. There was barely any boot time, and it loaded to the desktop with lightening fast speed. This being a laptop, I was expecting to spend at least an hour configuring video drivers and wireless. I had my ethernet ready. But there was no need... The wireless has been working perfectly out of the box! I don't even have to install my video drivers! Everything has literally worked without me having to install anything! 

I am so impressed I had to make this post. I can honestly say that now I have seen the day on which linux is easier to install and use then Windows. Thanks to everyone involved in the production of Ubuntu for producing such a phenomenal OS. You guys rock.
