### Post by CaptainCabinet on 2008-02-23
Hi everyone. Just thought I'd introduce myself. I'm hoping to spend a good amount of time on this forum so I thought "why not say hello". :)
First off I'd like to say I used to use Windows XP like everyone else. I used to love it too because it could do everything I wanted to do on it (play games and surf the web mainly).
At the time I had no idea linux even existed. Micro$oft must have been keeping it well hidden from me. But just a few months I discovered that linux did indeed exist and it was 100% free (cost wise) which was perfect for me. I was very interested and excited to try this new alternative world out. So I burned a copy of Ubuntu 7.10 to a CD and tried. It was love at first sight. As soon as I installed it I realised how restrictive Windows had been. I was now free to do whatever I wanted with my desktop and fully customise it which just isn't possible with Micro$oft Windows.
I was so intrigued and excited that I proceeded to try out about a dozen other linux distros (wasting several CDs) but none of them worked as error free and as smoothly as Ubuntu. So I switched back. 
I then made another discovery. Ubuntu Studio. I tried it of course and loved it even more than the regular Ubuntu. It looks beautiful and works perfectly too. I now plan to stick with Ubuntu Studio for good. It wipes the floor with Micro$oft Windows in so many ways.
I'm glad to be a part of this very lively community and I want to say well done to everyone who made Ubuntu what it is today. It truly is an inspiration when so many people put so much effort into something purely because they enjoy doing it. 
Thanks for reading! :) (my story has finally ended)

P.s. I made sure to wipe my hard drive of all traces of Windows XP before I installed Ubuntu Studio. A big risk I was willing to take.
