### Post by AdrianStrays on 2008-05-21
Okay, so this is kind of stupid, and probably not deserving of a post, (sorry) but I was totally caught of guard by it.  I was writing and email and mentioned the attachments I was going to attach, but then clicked send without attaching, I immediately caught the mistake, but before it sent Evolution informed me that I had not attached anything (based on my use of the word attachments) and asked if I wanted to attach things before sending.

Thats freaking awesome.  Its little innovations like that make me appreciate Open-Source software.  You use Microsoft products for years, and callously hold all other products against them, but often times you miss some of the niceties that make them awesome in the first place.
