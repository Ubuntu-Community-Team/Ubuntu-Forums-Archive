### Post by Dirkzen on 2008-09-27
I'll admit, i've been mostly a windows guy most of my life, and I never really knew anything about any other OS's out there, besides the strangely regarded 'Mac' thing i'd heard my friends talk about.
I forget exactly where I heard about Linux first.. though probably some obscure tech forum somewhere :)
((software/app pirate here ^.~ ))
I saw screenshots of it, and it was just soo.. beautiful, and different, and I looked into it a bit more, and got a little confused with all the different things and ways you had to do stuff.  Though what kept me away the most was the horrible, evil.. 'command line'..
But, quite a few years down the road, I peeked back into what was going on around here, and heard about 'live cd's'
Huh..
Booted up, checked it out, and.. well.. I don't wanna go back  ^^ <3<3
My little rustbucket of a pc here runs it smooth as silk, and i've got it skinned and themed to perfection. :D
Still learning all the little tweaks and tricks to it here and there, and ironing out all the bugs, 
((..and hoping I can get WoW to run ..))
But other than that, I don't honestly think i'd ever go back to windows now.
