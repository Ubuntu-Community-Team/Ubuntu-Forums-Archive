### Post by sanjeevpunj on 2008-05-09
Finally, when I am able to do the things that I usually do with Windows,I find that Ubuntu is stable, fast and slick.It is cool and pretty logical.Windows on the other hand seems whimsical and user-unfriendly now.Some games which I play on Windows are also going to be released on the Linux platform soon, so I feel it is worth going through some trouble learning Linux,and doing things the way we always wanted, instead of the way Windows wants us to do things! It is more interactive to use Ubuntu and I have been making friends through these forums! Never mind the paikstaking reinstalls, I think Ubuntu will survive! I find youngsters a bit reluctant to try Ubuntu though, they seem to have got brainwashed by Windows, but well I havent seen all the kids in the block, so my view may be a limited one.I am making free Ubuntu CDs to gift to others! Last year I had tried out PCLinuxOS, and it was good too, but I find Ubuntu a much more improved platform, more user friendly and well organised.the forums are awesome.
