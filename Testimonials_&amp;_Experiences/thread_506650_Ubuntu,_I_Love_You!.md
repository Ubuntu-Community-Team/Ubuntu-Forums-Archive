### Post by leaber on 2007-07-21
Hello guys, 

I would like to share my personal experience with ubuntu.

I started in the world of linux when a friend of mine borrowed me a knoppix CD because my XP install was broken!, and that little CD saved my entire hard drive!!
Since then, I started looking for reviews of some distros. First I installed Mandriva but didn't like it very much.
Then I found ubuntu, I started with 5.10 and since then I almost dropped XP (I dual boot cause Visual Studio 2005, for freelance projects ;) ).

Righ now I use my PC mainly for programming (PHP, Java w/JSP, and some rails) and eclipse runs really smooth, better than XP, also the desktop effects are really nice :D.
What I like most of ubuntu (and linux in general) is that i can customize almost everything, from the GUI to the kernel, I can choose from various desktop environments, its FREE, and one of the most important things is the community, always ready to give you a hand :wink:. Here (South America)   is really expensive to buy program licenses (windoze, photoshop, office, visual studio).

I really love ubuntu, right now is my distro of choice.
My father is struggling with XP in an old box, I will try to "turn" him to ubuntu :-P.
Also saved some of my friend's data with DamnSmallLinux when their windoz exploded...they just cant believe it.

Thanks guys
