### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-08-25
I am impressed!

My old 1280x1024 monitor died three days ago, and with some trepidation I have purchased a new Hanns-g HL198 widescreen monitor with a resolution of 1440x900.  I was anticipating having to mess around a little with configurations to get the new resolution, but I needn't have worried.  I unplugged the old, plugged in the new, switched on and there it was with no action on my part.

I seem to recall in Windows XP I had to mess around with the graphics driver for this same machine and same graphic card when I changed from a 1024x768 to 1280x1024 monitor, but Ubuntu did everything for me.

Brilliant!!  Well done to the devs, and note this is not even the most recent Ubuntu; I'm still on 10.04.
