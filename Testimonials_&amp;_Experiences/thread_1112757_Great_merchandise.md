I just recieved my Ubuntu cap and hoody in the mail today. The cap is really great - the logo on the front is embossed stitching and the little adjusting clip on the back has an embossed Ubuntu logo too! The colour of the cap is said to be "beige" but it's more "camel".

The hoody isn't the thickest one I've worn, but it's reasonably thick and has arrived just in time for the start of winter down here. There are little Ubuntu logos in the lining at the neck, too, which are really cool.

And the parcel arrived pretty quickly too - I think it only took a week.

I really like the cap and hoody and I might just get myself a T-shirt in a few months time for summer!
