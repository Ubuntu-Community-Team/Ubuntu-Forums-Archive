### Post by mousestalker on 2009-03-31
I installed Ubuntu 8.10 over a week ago. Since that time, my wife and I have spotted a large number of little black ants in our kitchen. Our cats have been licking them up, so they really haven't threatened anything, but they were not there before I installed Ubuntu.

Further, my sinuses clogged up last Sunday. I have seen the doctor and he prescribed some antibiotics. I'm still stuffed up and feel achy, so it's like a virus. I didn't have the virus before Ubuntu. So it's clearly the fault of Ubuntu.

I read somewhere on these forums that Clam helps with viruses. I took my wife to Red Lobster on Saturday night. I couldn't even taste the clams. I did add some tabasco sauce to them and then I could taste them. My sinuses also cleared up (temporarily).

Executive summary: I installed Ubuntu. I now have bugs and a virus. Clam only helped after an add-on.
