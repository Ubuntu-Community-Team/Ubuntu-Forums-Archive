### Post by dark joev on 2009-12-25
I made the switch out of my laptop being filled with viruses and being very slow and instead of taking the time to get rid of all the viruses I decided to get rid of Windows and make the switch to something besides MAC. So I decided to give Linux a try hoping that it wasn't going to be like Red Hat 2.6 which was the last time I ever used Linux. It was for a CS server. I looked into Debian and found Ubuntu and started to read up on it and decided to make the switch and wished that I did it sooner. I love all of the features, the price oh and the great support that you get from the community. It also made my laptop run faster than a new Vista machine which made my friend mad.:D
