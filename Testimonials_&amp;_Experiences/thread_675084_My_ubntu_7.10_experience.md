### Post by rosegarden78 on 2008-01-22
There is a Decompensation Bell Curve for breaking Windows/Mac addiction.  Expect your dopamine levels to fluctuate as you acclimate to the new computing environment.  Comparing Ubuntu 7.10 to Mac OS11 is like comparing Apples & Oranges because Mac had a head-start.

Give your new Ubuntu installation a full 90-day test drive.  I had to reinstall Feisty and Edgy a few times but it was my fault for messing up xorg.conf and I didn't know it at the time.  I also tried running Beryl because I thought eye-candy was the shizzit at that time.

Now I've come to appreciate the flexibility of XFCE with Xubuntu.  I can run my KDE kicker and GNOME Nautilus file manager at the same time.  Plus making custom keyboard shortcuts is easier for me.  As far as the fix is concerned I always got Flash to work by downloading plugins from Synaptic.  Firefox seems to work well for flash for me.  Now it should be as easy as checking Ubuntu Restricted Extras and Xubuntu Restricted Extras from the repos.
