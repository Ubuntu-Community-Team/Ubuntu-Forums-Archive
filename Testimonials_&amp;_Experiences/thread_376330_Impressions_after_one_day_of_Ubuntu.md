### Post by Escanor Tanthul on 2007-03-04
Hiya,

Just thought I'd post some of my impressions after a little over one day of using Ubuntu.

1. Video Drivers, after a little bit of playing around I was able to get my video drivers updated and installed via ENVY. This worked like a charm and I have great display quality now. 

2. Limewire, this was a little tricky to install at first. I had to install ALIEN to convert the limewire.rpm into a .deb, after that I was golden. However when I ran Limewire I noticed I needed Java Runtime Environment. This installed fine but limewire still wouldn't open, it kept telling me I didn't have the right Java version installed. After a little googling and forum searching I got it figured out and can run Limewire just like on Windows.

3. FireFox 2.0, This installed without a hitch and with FasterFox Extension I think my web browsing is much faster than IE7, and I imagine much safer.

4. I was kinda dismayed when I opened Rhytembox and tried to play some mp3's. So I installed Amarok and then the mp3 capability for Amarok and I was good to go. 

Overall, I'm quite impressed and enthused about using Linux everday. The only reason I have a Windows Parition is for school related stuff, when that's over I may nix windows for good.

--Escanor
