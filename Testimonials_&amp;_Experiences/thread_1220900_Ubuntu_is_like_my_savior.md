### Post by kikiasumi on 2009-07-23
between me and my sister we had two desktops and a laptop which where slow, even on xp.

The laptop was very slow, seemingly because it was like 4-5 years old, her desktop was slow because of all the photos and music she had, and mine was slow because I got so many freaking viruses just from trying to download the files for east asian language support. and two of the three eventually crashed.

I installed ubuntu on all three and they work just fine now, thoug at the expense of my sister occasionally asking me to help her figure something out on it. :P

The reason I call ubuntu my saviour, is because on both crashed hard drives, I had my artwork saved, and I hadn't made a backup, because I lacked cds

I was facing the prospect of having to reformat over my precious art files, stories, and photos, until my friend slipped me his ubuntu installation disk and I was curious enough to try the live boot, in which I was able to copy all of my stuff onto a thumb drive.

I have to thank ubuntu for saving my last years worth of work, with their live boot :)
as well as all the money I would have had to spend to get a crappy window xp cd x .x
