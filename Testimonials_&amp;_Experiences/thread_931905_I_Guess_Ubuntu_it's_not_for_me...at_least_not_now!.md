### Post by Gokudan on 2008-09-27
Hi, this is not ranting i just need to take this out of my chest,

    I'm 30, i work as a SAP Basis consultant now, but also worked as servicedesk/helpdesk IT guy for almost 10 years; so mainly what i do is serve as DB/SAP R/3 System/Server Admin., so i daily need to supervise 3 or more servers via RDP, connect trough sap gui to SAP R/3 to check and monitor the system, sometimes even connecting to servers that are not in the same city or country, run propietary apps on servers via RDP, some of them run on command prompt windows (mainly oracle stuff), and other stuff related. My main and only PC is a Dell XPS m1710, it came with Vista Business, Office 2007,etc.

     Two months ago i decided to give linux a go, specially since i wanted from a long time ago to learn unix also, i chose Ubuntu this time, since i failed to set up Gentoo x64, omg that distro is hard for noobs.... -_-

     I was happy to give microcrap the finger sign...you know....middle finger, lol XD, and i decided to stop this money spending habbit that they submit us to, also it is more easy (and more safe for you pocket in case of theft) to travel with only 1 CD with everyting thant to carry all your legit software with you in case of an OS reinstall, so, i downloaded Ubuntu 8.04LTS and everything went "fine", or so i thought in the first place.

     Installation and updates worked like a charm, an in general it works fine, but since the 2nd day i used it i was getting some strange apps hung ups now and then, but as i begin to use more the PC they got more and more frequent and i even started to have os hung ups, they just happen randomly, sometimes i use amarok flawlessly for some hours and everything is cool, but then i try to open amsn or pidgin, or gimp and it just won't open, or maybe i wanna log off i click on the icon and there you go again, it won't do anything, even if i do Ctrl+Alt+Supr..., and this happens with every piece of app i use, and not a 3rd party software, i mean apps that come with ubuntu, i also get this error with movie player or avidemux (gtk+);  sometimes i use terminal server client to connect via RDPv5 and it won't just connect, and even worse it hungs up, but this does not happen with every windows server i try to connect, just with a few ones.

     Also one very annoying bug is when i connect to a Win2k or 2k3 server and open a command prompt, i start to work with some apps that run scripts or commands that scroll down and voilá it does not scroll down entirely, so i need to use the mouse to do it. This happens every time i need to work with those apps.

    Sometimes i open amarok and i hear everything perfectly and all of a sudden sound dies and guess what...i need to reboot.

I know using linux it's free, i know it means freedom from microcrap but it's a pain in the @ss to be in the middle of a support session and to have to shut down manually the pc, this happens to me several times a day when terminal server client or amarok hungs or any other thing hungs up....it's a waste of precious time (and sometimes time's critical when u have to sut down a server in another city, because one of the UPS's battery failed and u only have 10 minutes to down services and everyting)

   When i work i need to enjoy what i am doing...and restarting or shutting down manually my pc when i need to edit an image or screenshot or listen to music to lower my stress it's not helping my daily stress quota...but what i think is amazing is that i do not have a single problem with World of Warcraft, it's a shame i cannont play while i am working haha.

 When i have the chance to get a 2nd pc i'll give ubuntu or any other linux distro a try, it's a shame but for now it has to be this way.


I thank everyone that answered my questions over the forum, i really appreciate all the help you gave me.

It ain't a goodbye it's just a See ya later.

BR's
Gokudan.

P.S. I'm sorry if i am killing english with my grammar lol, it's my 3rd language.
