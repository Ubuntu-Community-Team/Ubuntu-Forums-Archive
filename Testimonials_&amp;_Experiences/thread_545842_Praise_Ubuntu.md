### Post by Chillout2k7 on 2007-09-08
After installing Ubuntu and playing around with it for a day or two, I felt I just had to post some meaningless praise on the Ubuntu-forums.

I recently received my Vista upgrade for my Dell laptop, and installed it ready to be blown away. But after a few weeks, I really can't stand it (even though I've been a DOS/Windows user all my life), hence my decision to install and dual-boot the much-talked-about Ubuntu... and the experience has been incredible! The installation was a breeze, and it just feels good to work with. Stable, good looking, responsive and customizable. I really believe, which has probably been said a million times before, that the Ubuntu team has made a great distro for Windows people wanting to convert. I'm glad I made this decision, and I am set on using it for everything I can, and only use Windows for gaming, 3ds Max etc.

Anyway, sorry for the ranting. Just wanted to say a big thank you to Ubuntu and all the other authors of the apps I've installed. It's an incredible thing, to be able to get an OS like this, with a ton of programs that match and surpass the commercial ones, for free. And a big thanks to all the Linux people that support the newbies like me in forums like this.

Thanks again, and keep up the good work!
