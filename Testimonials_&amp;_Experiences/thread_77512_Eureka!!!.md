### Post by kperkins on 2005-10-16
You guys have got it this time.  
I've been reading all the threads about problems with Breezy installs, and with much trepidation, upgraded through Synaptic on my Gateway laptop.
There were no problems.  Sound works, video works (I saved my W32 codecs), my battery monitor actually works (it never worked exactly right in Hoary), my special keys all work, netapplet works properly (in Hoary it wouldn't shut down porperly when I logged out), and hibernate/suspend works when I close the lid.
Plus I have a newer Firefox, Gimp, and OO.org, and usplash (with my own graphic, by the way, thanks to the wiki.
Thanks to the development team for making this a great release.
Well actually there is one problem--firefox and thunderbird are, both, still, very slow loading (over 20 seconds for both), but they do work ok.
(For everyone with problems, I hope you get them fixed-it is doable.)
