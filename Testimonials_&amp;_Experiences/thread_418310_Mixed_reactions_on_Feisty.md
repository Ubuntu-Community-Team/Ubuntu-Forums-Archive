### Post by aimran on 2007-04-22
Hi guys! 

I am bored at the library so I decided to do a little bit of ranting :)

About 4 weeks ago I installed openSuSE onto my Compaq V5000 laptop. I was unhappy with it (openSuSE) since it was slow and resource hungry. Not to mention that it's so user unfriendly (during and after installation). 

I was so unhappy I wanted to go back to windows. But then I realised that I forgot to backup my windows CD! I WAS SO F*CKED! Undaunted I decided that there was no turning back. I am now stuck to linux.

Luckily I got hold of an x64 Edgy asap and installed it. Installation was a breeze! Not to mention that everything in ubuntu was so sensical and user friendly! I got beryl to work and was very very impressed... except that getting my broadcom card to work was a pain in the back orifice. I never got it to work :(.

Then came the much awaited release of Feisty! I was even more sexed up over this release and stayed up refreshing the download page for Feisty. 

This has got to be the best release of ubuntu so far. I managed to get my broadcom wireless working. Just one snag though. I can't get desktop effects to work (ATI card) and neither can I get beryl to work with Feisty. Oh well not that I need beryl.

So that was my bittersweet rant on Feisty. I will try to convert as many friends as I can! Cheers to the community! I <3 you!
