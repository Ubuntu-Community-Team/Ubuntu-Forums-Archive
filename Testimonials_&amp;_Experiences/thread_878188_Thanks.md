### Post by Bobiskey on 2008-08-02
Registered and making my first post a Thank You to the Ubuntu Linux community at large for Ubuntu. the reason is as follows.

I've ran Ubuntu since 6.something on my laptop dual booting with windows XP. My daughter uses the laptop to and makes use of both XP and Ubuntu installations to errr have fun and play I guess. 

This week she turned 12 and though she has access to all the PC's in the house. I got her a laptop. On this I installed Windows Xp and all the software she requires and also dual installed the latest Ubuntu and left it with just the CD software to see if she remebers how to use the package managers. She did, she spends much more time in Ubuntu then she does in Windows. Favouring Ubuntu for her general use, youtube kitten videos, "X country" has got talent videos and that kinda thing only useing Windows to add music/video to her MP4 player. 

I suspect my next post will be from me trying to get the MP4 player working and calling on someone here to tell me "yes you are stupid its as easy as...." :) but for now, Hi and Thanks for all your great work on a very good product. 

Bob :)
