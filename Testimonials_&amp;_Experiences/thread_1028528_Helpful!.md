### Post by OllieGab on 2009-01-02
I have now used Linux for a couple of months or so and so far I have absolutely no regrets switching from Windows. The Ubuntu community has been most helpful with suggestions and recommendations when I've been stuck.
For several years I thought of trying out Linux but was always under the impression that it was a very 'geeky' OS. Not really for me, who likes to fiddle around a **little** bit but who feels more comfortable in a GUI environment.
I found that I had some problems with getting some of my applications from my "Windows environment" to work well in Linux but with help from the community, magasines and web sites I have usually found the right application "for the right job".
I have learned a great deal in a short time and even though - I am sure - I have asked some really stupid questions now and again I have always "been met" with understanding and patience.
I will more than likely keep Windows on part of my hard-drive - if not for the fact that not all people in the household want to venture outside Microsoft's world!

Cheers from Ollie
