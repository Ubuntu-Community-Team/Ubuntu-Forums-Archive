### Post by guitarchris on 2007-05-19
First thanks to all the guys at Canonical for making this wonderful OS, Feisty Fawn(Studio) blows my mind, rocks my world and sets me free.

Everything works! (Haven't tried the scanner yet so fingers crossed)  Camera, broadband router, Synaptic, PDF reader - none of which worked in Mepis 6.5. A top notch OS, fast, flexible, accessible, processor friendly and idiot-friendly (I am a total noob with only the slenderest of grasps of what's actually going on in that gently humming box on my desk).

Add to that a totally integrated set of music-making tools (my raisond'etre) which integrated seamlessly with my Terratec Phase 22 Soundcard and which I haven't managed to break yet and I am in heaven.

Then we have the community. What can I say that is strong enough. This is the final ingredient in a  successful Open Source project and I think the most important. I followed a string yesterday where a guy was totally frustrated trying to get Ubuntu up and running and wanted help to "get rid of it". From being clearly furious and really quite unpleasant he was brought gently round by the community until he calmed down and  finally had a fully functioning Feisty! I love that! So refreshing compared to the sort of flame war his first post would have started on other fora (it's a Latin word so Latin plural - call me a pedant...).

So thanks again to all of you and expect to hear from me if I can help....
