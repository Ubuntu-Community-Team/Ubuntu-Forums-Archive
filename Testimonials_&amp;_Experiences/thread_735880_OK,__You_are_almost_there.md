Hi there,
I installed new Ubuntu 8 using wubi today. I know thats the way you guys dont like but its a good option for a graphic designer like me afraid of loosing control of things and never be able to restore them,
I was always amused with ubuntu and linux in geenral and always wished for one that could work for me. I know about Gimp and wine, but no where near what i get with Photoshop and Flash CS3 in windows.
Apart from that, I was amased with how easy things are in ubuntu. want VLC ok...package manager>search>select and u have it.Update automatically any program running on your computer. kick *** compiz effects already installed.
I always said to myself, a normal user like my dad would not survive with linux, but this is so damn close to get there. I can almost recommend it to him now knowing he wont have big troubles with it. I mean before to install a program you had to type in some 10 lines of code.

After all, fantastic work. Please please keep up the good work. Im very confident that one day you will get the reward for your hard work.
