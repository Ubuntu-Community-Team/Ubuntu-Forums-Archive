Windows has market share because everything works with Windows.  All the hardware vendors write drivers for Windows, all the software people port their stuff for Windows.  It's a self-sustaining cycle:  the more stuff built for Windows, the more it will dominate, the more stuff will be built for it.  

Ubuntu struggles with simple stuff like hibernate and sleep; until things like that are fixed, Windows (or Mac) will dominate (although Mac won't dominate until their prices come down.  If you could get an iMac for the price of a Dell Studio 15, Windows would vanish.)
