### Post by darkghost on 2006-10-17
Or "Why I still can't uninstall WinXp from my PCs"

I know it's an old story but I'd like to express my ideas. Please don't take this as a flame. In no way I want to sound as a flamer, these are just my ideas (or my two eurocents...as they say =). Also, english is my third language, so please forgive my poor grammar.

I've been curious about linux since 5 years ago. I began installing it on my pcs when on the box of Mandrake was proudly announced that the box contained KDE2.0...
Since then I've been trying to leave MS for Linux , but always without success. I'll try to explain my problems, maybe someone will have a use for this.

1- We live in a world where communication is very, very important. My main assett in my job (more about it later) are contacts, phone calls, etc.
So I'm always with my pocketPhone with me (right now NokiaE61, two months ago an heavy i-mate JasJar. Unfortunatly I cant in no way sinchronize with linux. Contacts, agenda, todolists, etc. And this is very important to me. Imagine if I loose the phone, or they steal it to me etc...
I still have to try a Treo (waiting for the 750 to come in the shops in europe), or maybe a Sony (the new P910 or M600).
I guess the problem are companies not releasing soft to synchronize on linux, they don't do it 'cause linux is not very popular, especially in companies etc. But this is one point for that I need a win partition

2- Work. I'm a trader, I buy & sell stocks, bonds etc to gain my life. Unfortunately I'm a young and very little trader, still can't afford a Reuters connection etc.
I couldn't find an alternative to metastock running on linux, no development or alphas of similar soft. Plus all the data providers are equipped for win (not even mac, just win!), no way to download (professional) data on linux. 
The only bonus is that the bank uses a java platform for the orders, so I can use it from linux box.
I guess that very few common users need such software. But maybe why not to try develop something similar (to metastock) for linux ? Many common people owns some stocks or bonds. If someone would like to try I can't programm but I know a hell of a lot of finance, tech analysis etc. I can help 
(ok you can trick and use prorealtime.com (for free!!), an online java platform very well realised, but then data are on their servers, and when you are offline you cant work...)

3- Kubuntu is great, it recognizes 99% of my hardware. Works like a charm, is super stable etc. I love KDE much better than win, and I really dont feel I need a Mac to have a nice working desktop. But still I cant speak with people on msn or icq 'cause I cant find a soft that accepts my hearphone and mic (a simple sennheiser hearphone and mic) ... Is this a problem with msn/icq etc. ? I know of Skype, but not everybody uses it.

So little is still missing , and I guess some others are still waiting for some apps, or services to appear under linux to switch defintely. Can't we users help in any way (besides paying for linux, I do it often, but I still  miss apps & services...)

Andrew
(Italy)
