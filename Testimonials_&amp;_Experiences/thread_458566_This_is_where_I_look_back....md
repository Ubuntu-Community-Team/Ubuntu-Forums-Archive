### Post by DJ Wings on 2007-05-29
I've been using Linux for over a year and a half now, and so far, I haven't missed Windows. But now, I'm starting to question the decision of not looking back.
Here are the pros and cons of switching back to Windows for me:
Pros:
-easier to install games
-most Linux apps have ports
-...err...
Cons:
-DRMs. I will not be missing them.
-Even Vista's 3D effects pale in comparison to Beryl.
-Most Windows apps I can get equivalents of for free.
-Much faster...
I can understand why people would have some trouble diving into the world of Linux. But for me, it's at the point where I'm more fluent with Linux than Windows. People say I'm a freak because I'm a penguinisto (check it: male users shouldn't be calling themselves penguinistas) at 15, to which I say that if they were raised on Linux, they'd be too.
