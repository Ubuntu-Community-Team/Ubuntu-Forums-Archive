### Post by JaradT on 2010-10-27
I like Ubuntu. I had to install it when Vista decided not to let me use my USB mice anymore.
So I went through the installation, and it was actually quite quick.
It intstantly recognised my mouse and keyboard, and my wireless adapter (try putting a wireless adapter into Windows, and you'll need a disc for it.)
And it removed two useless partitions, so now I have more hard drive space :)

My biggest grudge is the fact gnome-screensaver still pops up, even though I don't want it anymore. I installed xscreensaver through a tutorial, and I like it. But I don't want gnome-screensaver popping up on startup.

Other than that, Ubuntu is great.
