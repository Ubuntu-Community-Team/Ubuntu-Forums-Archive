### Post by TheConverted on 2007-03-14
I haven't "used" linux in about 12 years - so I have fogotten most of what I used to know.  I installed a few distro's over the last couple of weeks, freespire, redhat and now Ubuntu.

Colour me impressed!  I installed this as a dual boot on my new Dell XPS 1210 and almost everything worked immediately.  I had to install the drivers for the Nvidia card, and fool around with my wireless and ndiswrapper - but everything is now working perfectly.  It even detected my usb port replicator without a problem!

I was blown away how far linux gui's and installers have come.  I run an IT deparment for a larger company, and I could actually see this taking the place of windows on the desktop.  It would probably reduce most of my support headaches too.

A big kudos to the community for their incredible support forms and the developers who have put out a first class OS.
