### Post by phasee on 2007-08-22
...I am trying to do work for university but I just keep getting drawn in by the customisation level of Linux. I need to constantly stop and change and add things to my linux environment :P

So, now you know, I dont really hate Ubuntu. Been using it for a few months now. At first on and off but now it is almost exclusively. Only switch back to windows (reluctantly) if I need a program which is not available here for uni work.

I've only had a few problems with linux and I for them, I have maybe posted 2 posts on here asking for help, otherwise I google it and the answer is waiting for me.

I now have a beautiful ubuntu environment, running 64-bit Feisty with compiz-fusion and AWN.

And ET:QW is coming out next month and soon after a linux version will be released so that is great news.

Now, if only adobe would support linux I would very very very rarely need windows for uni.

A huge thanks to all the community members who make software, help others and share info and scripts.
