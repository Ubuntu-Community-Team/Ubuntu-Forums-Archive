### Post by linuxbeatswin on 2008-04-03
I finally convinced a good friend of mine to let me install Ubuntu Gutsy on his old computer. It's an old Compaq- don't know all the particulars about it. So, I go over and install it, show him around it for a bit, got some new, bright wallpaper to keep him happy, and went on my way. A week later, I get a call from him, "Windows crashed again on me, I can't get it restarted, and I have to use Ubuntu to get my project done before tomorrow- what do I do!?!?!" 

I went over, walked him through an installation of Wine, and he was golden.

Being that he was worried about getting a firewall, I installed lokkit for him, showed him the old Alt+F12 deal, and he was good to go.

Although I had to get his windoze machine working again, he told me today that he hasn't touched it since installing Wine- he's strictly using Ubuntu. 

Although he's not completely sold on Linux because of printing issues (I have 'em, too), I think that we may have a convert very soon.

Linux- freeing minds one at a time......
