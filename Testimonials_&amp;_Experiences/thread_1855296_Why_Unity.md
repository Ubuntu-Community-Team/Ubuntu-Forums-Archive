### Post by airper on 2011-10-12
I wasn't at all a fan of Unity when it came out, immediately reverted to the Classic Gnome option. But the more I read about it and found out, the more curious I became.

Decided a month's diet was required (give it a proper try) So booted up into Unity on the 1st Sept determined to stay until the 30th.

Actually I'm still there and so are all the other family machines now. Like all things different it took a bit of getting use too, a bit of figuring out how it all works, but I have to say I'm converted and look forward to the upgrades in 11.10.

I would like to be able to re-size the icons on the launcher (I've gotten use to it's location) I'm not a big fan of the application windows, I tend to like list views rather than icon views, but the pros, very easy to adapt the launcher, good dynamic menus, stolidity of the windowing, out way the things I don't like.

I'm converted, albeit it took six months thinking about it and a somewhat painful diet for a month.
