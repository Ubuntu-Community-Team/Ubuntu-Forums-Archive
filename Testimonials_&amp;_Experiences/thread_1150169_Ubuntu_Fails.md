### Post by Kareeser on 2009-05-05
trizicus... we realize that the Suspend and Hibernate issue is a tricky one. For now, the bugs are being squashed one by one, and the more exposure there is to a problem, the quicker it gets fixed.

For example, the Dell Inspiron series (and other related notebooks) suffered suspend and hibernate regressions in Jaunty. Prior to that, suspend/hibernate was broken in Hardy, but later fixed in intrepid. Soon after the beta, the problem was fixed once and for all in Jaunty, but only after many individual bug reports and suggestions from members.

We're sorry to see you go, but in the meantime, we'll keep working on it, so when and if you come back later on down the line, you won't experience the same problem again :)

I will ask, however, that you keep track of your bug report, as commenters might request some more information about your system, in an attempt to fix it.
