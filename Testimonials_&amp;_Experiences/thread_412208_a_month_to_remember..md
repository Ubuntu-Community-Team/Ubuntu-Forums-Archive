### Post by wheels. on 2007-04-17
A month ago (give or take a week) I saw this thing called Ubuntu.
I was skimming through digg.com when I noticed it. I decided to check out this thing.
It was truly love at first sight. Ubuntu looked beautiful. CLEAN. Stunning in fact.
I decided to chekc out the forum. I didn't know what to download whether it be 6.10 or whether to go for 7.04!
I decided (being a new user) to do what every new user should do. Stay away from the beta!!
Anyway I successfully installed Edgy Eft. Dual boot on my Toshiba Sattelite laptop. But my USB mouse wouldn't work!! I found out that it could be my ATI driver messing with it?? I wondered to my self. How could a video card driver screw up my mouse. That was a learning experience to say the least.

Even then I was not discouraged. I ended up solving the problem myself. It felt fantastic. Using the computer was fresh and exciting. 

Then I decided hey why not try out vista. I really liked the look of this aero effect.
I was youtubing aero when I came across this gem called beryl. Vista was washed away from my memory before you can say Wow. Beryl was the new goal. I did not heed the rookie warning for beryl though. I was getting my hands on that gem no matter what. IN short I did get beryl working with my ATI card, and XGL 
flawlessly. Beryl was gourgeous. absolutely stunning. I could play with beryl endlessly.  The laptop was great.

I started to gain confidence in my linux experience (experiment) and when my XP desktop failed to recover from the xp cds after a format I decided it was time to try out feisty.

My desktop being a work computer probably should have been running edgy instead of a beta, but I was too anxious, actually excited to wait. I installed feisty without a hitch. Everythign supported right off the get go. Ease of use in feisty was tremendous. My new goal was to get feisty looking beautiful.
No beryl on this computer though my graphics card= crappy.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/g/images/253173/1_Screenshot.png](http://ubuntuforums.org/g/images/253173/1_Screenshot.png)

MY desktop partially customized. Eventually to include an embedded terminal in the desktop at about the bottom right of my screen shot was where it was located. It looked like text on the background. it was gourgeous. its called Tilda.
Also it came to include a search bar in the bottom right

anyway The conditions of me having feisty on the desktop was having to get Microsoft Office 2003 to work for my fathers business. It took eons of work it felt like anyway. 
To make a long story short. Mission accomplished using Wine.
If anyone had the problem ( word is not installed for current user blah blah, email me I can explain what I did [email]calcium20@gmail.com[/email])

MY family however needed a desktop with windows. dual booting not an option.
I finally got my system disks to work(by booting them through ubuntu)
So I am forced to install an OS I don't want and don't enjoy on the eve of the long anticipated release of 7.04 :( 

All is not lost however.

Once I purchase my new computer (september)
I will return.
These few months will be hard, painful and most of all depressing, but I will make it through.
I can see the light at the end of the tunnel, that brown earthen glow coming from the ubuntu desktop of my dreams.
All in all I absolutely  100 % recommend Ubuntu Fesity 7.04 ( the final version) to anyone wanting a change, a change for you, a change for your buisness, a change for freedom, and a change in way of life.

That was the greatest part about my journey. The feeling of freedom, the feeling of community, and the feeling of fun.


I will be back.

Until next time,

Snods./Wheels./calcium20


p.s. I am writing this from an XP computer running IE6 ( I miss my built in spellchecker:( )
