### Post by indigoshift on 2006-10-14
The hard drive in my wife's old Compaq laptop finally gave out, so I set up an account for her on my ThinkPad, which runs Ubuntu.  She absolutely loves it, as can be proved by the following conversation:

Her:  (giggles)
Me:  What?
Her:  This computer can do the Time Warp!
Me:  ???
Her:  (ctrl+alt+left)  It's just a jump to the left!
Her:  (ctrl+alt+right)   And then a step to the right!

I swear I'm not making this up.  :p 

She even set up the printer by herself, with no problems at all (I posted about that in the "Hardware and Laptops" forum).

Now, you gotta understand:  I do all her tech support for her, even the simple stuff.  This is when she's using XP, though.  She mentioned to me that everything seems simpler with Ubuntu than it does with XP, which is the opposite of what I expected her to say.

And now she's put Open Office on her XP desktop.  She absolutely loves it.

Once we get her a new laptop, she says she wants Ubuntu on it, instead of XP.  I'm in heaven. :cool:
