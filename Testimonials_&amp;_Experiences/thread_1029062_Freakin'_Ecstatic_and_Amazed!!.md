[FONT="Impact"][COLOR="Red"]**[SIZE="4"]Freakin' Ecstatic and Amazed!!**[/COLOR] That's the only way I can describe what mood I'm in right now.[/SIZE]

***System Specs.***

[COLOR="Blue"][I]AMD 1700+ XP Overclocked@2.0ghz
2GB GSkill RAM
Nvidia GeForce2 MX 400 64mb
(1) 40GB WD HD(ubuntu installed)
(2) 250GB Maxtor HD(RAID 0)--working and fully accessible
(2) DVD-RW Drives
Logitech MX Duo Wireless KB & Mouse --most all extra button working
Logitech Quickcam IM Webcam & Skype --working
Epson Perfection Scanner --working
Canon i850 Printer --working
Samsung Blackjack II WM 6.1 --syncing[/COLOR]
[COLOR="Red"]Zoltrix TV-Max TV Card --haven't got the nerve to try, still reading.[/COLOR][/I]


I installed ubuntu 8.10 on my desktop box, which by the way is getting up there in age I built it back in 2002.  Yeah, I had some hick-ups along the road, but most of those where my fault. Example of one was we I first started having issues with the install I should have immediately disabled my onboard raid controller, but I didn't and had issues until I disabled it. Now I have every hardware and peripheral I own installed and working, I even just managed to get my Samsung Blackjack II which is WM6 syncing with Evolution. I even got some nice eyecandy thanks to Compiz and Emerald, 3D-box and Avent Window Manager is freakin' cool, even my wife like that.

Linux and most importantly ubuntu has impressed the hell out of me. Later this year I will be building a new desktop, and I can now get a lot more hardware because I'm NOT buying any Microsoft OS, or software that depends on Microsoft.

**[SIZE="3"]None of this could have been possible without this forum, and google of course. *[COLOR="Red"]So thank you to everyone who has posted in this forum, you have no idea how much help it really is.[/SIZE][/COLOR]***[/FONT]
