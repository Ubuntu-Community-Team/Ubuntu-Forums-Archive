### Post by laughingquarter on 2007-05-10
I got tired of the complicated love triangle between me, ubuntu 6.10, and XP. After dual booting since December and a slight partitioning mishap last week (caused by me goofing around with gparted), I am now in a committed relationship with Ubuntu!

Things have been good between she and I, though I can't say it's been perfect. It took me a while to figure her out (though, isn't it that way with all operating systems?), and it took a fair bit of research to to get my webcam and cpu-scaling working, but it was totally worth it!

I love Ubuntu, and if it were legal for me to marry her, I would. We could even move to South Africa and our children would have such lovely accents...
