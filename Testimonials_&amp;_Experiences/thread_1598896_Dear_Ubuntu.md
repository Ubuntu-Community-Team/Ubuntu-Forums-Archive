### Post by timfinley on 2010-10-17
This is my love letter to my current obsession. An obsession that has lasted five wonderful years.

Dear Ubuntu,

    How can I love you more? You have a fantastic community full of smart, sometimes dumb, sometimes witty, [without a doubt, I am all of those] but always fun people to talk to. The ideas are always flowing to become quicker, smarter and more user friendly.

I love the way Ubuntu means so much in one simple word. It means community. It means brilliance, it means usability, it means [with Linux] security, endless modification. 

I can not deny that I appreciate the passion I have. For Linux, for Ubuntu. Thank you Ubuntu, for helping find a reason to go to college. For reinvigorating my hope for computers. Before all I saw was a bleak sense of worth [Windows and Macs (my previous OSs)]. I have learned a great deal from this community. And I do not doubt that I will learn a great deal more.

Thank you Ubuntu.
Thank you Ubuntu community.

Cheers,

~Tim

P.S. I know I missed many points. Please feel free to tells me why you love Ubuntu...
