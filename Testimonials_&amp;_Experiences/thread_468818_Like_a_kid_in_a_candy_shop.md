### Post by L1vingd3ad on 2007-06-09
Being in 22 year old uni student, I've been exposed to windows my whole life. I can even still remember the time where I was 8 or nine trying to draw santa claus using corel draw. My whole life I believe that windows is the one and apart from mac is the only OS out there. Of boy how I was wrong.

Around 2004 I started hearing about linux and how they rock. So being a curious cat that I am, I went and downloaded Suse linux. Didn't like it one bit. Lots of things in my computer didn't work and I ditched the green iguana. I tried mandrake linux based on a friend's recommendation, again hated it. Fedora core....... same thing happened. So I gave up. I thought linux is just not for me and the thought of being free from Bill gates' evil clutch is buried deep inside me.

Then I heard of Ubuntu just last week. And the voice just never stops. I guess the will of freedom just wasn't ready to die yet. A friend of mine offered me one of his free shipped cd and I took it. And my world changed that instance. I couldn't believe how easy it was to install ubuntu. And plus I didn't have to keep installing other programs such as adobe reader or irc client, it was all there ready to be used. The desktop looks great after I fiddle around with the panels and everything just works. It is light (uses around 200MB of RAM only compared to 500+MB in my windows), installing new softwares was simple and there are helps whenever I got into trouble. Sure I messed some things but it was fixed easily, just need to look in the right place for the solution. In short, ubuntu brings back the simplicity and fun back into computer.

What I want is all here with ubuntu....... well mostly. There are some things that holds me back to switch entirely. It's been 4 days since I installed ubuntu alongside my windows and I only switched to windows twice since I have to do some assignment which uses MYOB. Other than that, I've been playing around with ubuntu like I've never seen a computer before.

Kudos for ubuntu developers and I can't wait till Gusty is out.
