### Post by Bao2 on 2011-10-20

Another example: Every app was two clicks away in old gnome. Now first time you search for one, just count the clicks you need!!!

The Global menu is a stupidity because when you have several apps in the desktop you go always with the mouse to the upper left to be able to access the menus of every window. And if you disable Global menu you find a beautiful upper bar that has only the clock and logout and wifi. You can't add anything to that bar and make it usable as it was in old gnome (I had lots of launchers there, actually with only the top bar I had much more launchers that with the left launcher that comes with gnome now with these giants icons (even in size 32 they are huge).
