I know this is probably a really stupid post, and I have no idea why the heck I am posting it, and I'll probably get some flames for it, but I find it really ironic how 99% of threads on this page are pretty much duplicates of one of the following:

1. I Love Ubuntu- This variety talks about how Ubuntu is the greatest ever. Comments generally revolve around how everyone is happy for the person, or the comments deviate from the topic.

2. I Hate/Left Ubuntu- these threads are generally either Ubuntu bashing or "Why isnt Ubuntu more like Windows?". Comments generally turn into flame wars or attempts at convincing contentions.

In both cases very few people lay down substantially backed cases. They just spurt out emotive opinions, good or bad. Why is it that no one can tell an informative story anymore?
