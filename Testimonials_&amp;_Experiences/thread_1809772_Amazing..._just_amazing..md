### Post by kasuals on 2011-07-22
I have avoided any X11 gui for quite awhile, since 1999 anyway. I just don't have the downtime in my life to make things work aside from work.

Ubuntu 11.04 has changed my opinion on Linux being my desktop OS. I have since installed Ubuntu on every desktop in my house (4), and have had nothing but an amazing experience since. It's 10x faster (or more) than any Windows desktop I've ever run, and with the package managers you have provided it makes installing anything a breeze.

The default apps are great, but the software centre has really shined through on this.

Great job, this could be Windows killer. Even people who have never used Linux are using these desktops with little to no transition issues. (Does take getting used to window management icons being on the left instead of the right).

From a guy who has been using computers since he was literally 4 y/o loading joystick controls on a Commodore, great job... just great job.

I appreciate the open-source aspect as well, I know many Microsoft programmers whose code is trash and the open-source project leaves your code to scrutiny... which in my opinion causes only the best and brightest to come out or those who aren't to be corrected.

I can't say enough, but THANK YOU.

btw, gparted is probably the best thing ever.

### Post by kasuals on 2011-07-22

Again, thank you. You have a real product here.
