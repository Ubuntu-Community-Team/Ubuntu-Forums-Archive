### Post by aklo on 2010-02-01
I'm about 2mths into my first ubuntu 9.10 and i've got to say i'm impressed.

I have no idea why do so many people complaint of problems with ubuntu but that is probably hardware problem; your hardware is not supported or something. And my epson stylus T10 printer works really good in ubuntu.

I've installed every single update till now and i have ZERO errors due to updates. This is in response to people who said an update screwed up their os.

So far i can only remember 1 forced restart but that is no big deal and i'm up and running 20 secs later.

I am by no means a linux/ubuntu professional. I still have no idea why the heck i have to use "tar xvf" to extract files (yes you can just right click).  All these doesn't matter since i could just use google search "How to extract tar.gz files"....yes i'm really bad with memorizing linux command line.

I absolute like ubuntu and the interface, the unique desktop feel of something different from windows. Although i'm still on dual boot due to game and microsoft visual studio 2005 for school project, i try my best to use ubuntu for other stuff and  hobbies like: 

Creating vector art using inkscape, watching my pirated mythbusters series, learning php ,surfing the web and listening to music.

My greatest wish for ubuntu: to have more game companies making games for linux so i can further stay away from windows. Also openoffice although is a really good software at this stage, there are still some problems with opening ppt, which is enough to keep me away from it when i'm doing a report for my school project. Nevertheless, i still rate ubuntu 9.10 an 8/10. 2 more points for improvement.

Sorry for bad english.
