### Post by Darrell Lawrence on 2009-04-08
We have just taken delivery of our 2nd Dell XPS M1530 laptop preinstalled with Ubuntu 8.04 LTS. We have started an online business and just received our articles of incorporation for filling with the state.

We sell on ebay and will also have our own website up and running within 2 weeks.

Since we are just in the beginning stages 2 laptops are all we need. We have purchased web hosting rather than purchasing our own server at this point. I've had to put studies for Ubuntu Certified Professional on hold until I get our website development finished, and complete are agreements with our suppliers.

But, It's quite amazing what you can do with two Dell XPS M1530 laptops preinstalled with Ubuntu 8.04 LTS. We will be sticking with the LTS releases for stability rather than upgrading every 6 months. We like the fact that this release has support until April of 2011.

Thanks Dell and Ubuntu developers for a great combination of computer and operating system. Linux does everything we need.

Kennedy-Watters Associates Incorporated
