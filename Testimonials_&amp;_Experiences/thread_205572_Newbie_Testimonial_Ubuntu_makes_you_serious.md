WARINING! Following testimonial may contain overambitious praisin' & corny language.

Hi! I'm Peter from Sweden :cool:  I recently decided (after trying out the LiveCD) to switch over from using XP. It's like being born again. Sure, i don't know the Linux enviroment yet since i've been using Win32 systems exclusivly since '97, but hey!... when i first started to use Win98, i could hardly make out the difference between a executable file & a folder :oops:  But thanx to google and all my (hopefully) future friends here on the ubuntu forums, i've been able to look up solutions to most of my problems :mrgreen: 

The most remarkable thing with Ubuntu for me is how it changed my computer usage. Normally i'm a "computer slacker", who don't care to know more than he needs to get around. But now i find myself using a OS enviroment that challenges me to get involved, to strive for more knowledge & slowly float away from the dull, restricted world of Microsoft, no matter how cosy & familiar it may be. I found something better for both me  & my computer. The solution is spelled: **U-B-U-N-T-U !**
