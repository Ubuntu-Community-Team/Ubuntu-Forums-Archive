### Post by MooPi on 2010-12-30
A client came to me the other day because their crippled demo/trial/paid for, software used to duplicate music demos in his studio couldn't do the job. I created some scripts using cdrecord to simultaneously burn three CD images at once. This impressed my client enough to ask me to install Ubuntu and my scripts onto a dedicated duplication tower. I couldn't be any prouder of Linux and myself than I am right now. It's not because it was such a difficult task but I convinced a die-hard point and click Windows user to convert to Linux. He had never heard of Linux until I mentioned it and he looked at me so puzzled when I told him the software was completely free. That look said this guy is either nuts or crazy to think I believe that free software kicked his paid for application. I've been smiling all day thinking of that expression. FTW baby !!!!!! ;-)
