### Post by Chewy22 on 2006-11-17
I'm a total newbie to linux and ubuntu, been on Window's as long as I can remember and I've mastered Windows so I can get it to do my everyday tasks with ease.  One day, after reading the various PC magazines around the place, I thought, hmm... Linux seems like a hard platform to crack, so many commands to remember, but I said to myself, if I can do Windows, I can do linux! So off I went to order Ubuntu as a few places said it was a pretty good place for exWindows users to start.  I was so excited the day I got it.  I installed it without a hitch and started fooling around with it.  "Yeah it looks great," I said to myself, "...But how the hell do i get it to do the things I want it to do.."  So off I went to read documentation and HOWTO guides.  Yeah they got me somewhere, but it still didn't measure up (well I didn't measure up to Ubuntu).  So I started hitting this forum.  You know, what I love about this place is that when you ask a question, people KNOW what you're talking about straight up!  They usually will provide an answer right away and when you try it, it works first time round!  I remember when I was schooling using Windows, and when I went to the tech guys, it'd take DAYS for them to fix a simple little problem and usually end up deleting, reconfiguring and installing things that I really didn't need!  With Ubuntu, you tell it to do something, it'd go off and do it!  Ubuntu, will you be my friend? :D
