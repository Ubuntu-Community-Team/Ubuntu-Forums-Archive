### Post by Joseph IronEagle on 2007-04-25
The day has arrived, and though I will miss the Total War games I so enjoyed, and the Myst games  I have made my decision, the stability, reliability, and ease of use in so many things with Ubuntu has ended the seemingly endless frustration of Windows, with the satisfaction of it just works. ( oh there were some frustrations the first few weeks as with learning anything new, and a couple of remaining issues with news videos not yet resolved)

       At first I was of mixed feelings as to whether Ubuntu would serve or not till I realized Ubuntu had run longer without trouble than I was ever able to achieve with windows, (my neighbors had started calling me crash master after the first month with XP I had better stability with 98 go figure).
         
        Unless I make a drastic mistake Ubuntu just works, and has been far easier to fix when I do make a mistake than windows ever was for me. A job well done in my opinion, seems a shame when I read where others have given up so easy, and returned to windows.
  
        The problems I've had with Ubuntu were with what I didn't know, or understand yet,  with windows it didn't seem to matter what I knew.  Downloading Ubuntu has been the best decision  I have made in a long time.

       Wadv (Thank you)  for providing a choice to windows that works.

        Joseph
