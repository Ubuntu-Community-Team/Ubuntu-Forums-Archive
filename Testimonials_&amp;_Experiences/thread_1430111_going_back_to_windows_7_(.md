### Post by DeusExM1 on 2010-03-16
I think its a bad idea to go to windows  7 just for gaming. Recently i had a huge conflict with this myself, as i could not understand what to do. I love ubuntu, and i hate Microsoft, but i LOVE gaming. So i kept wondering wtf to do?

I decided to get a PS3. I havent purchased it yet, but i will soon. Now before you call me a consolefag think about the pros and cons of PC vs console gaming. I myself hated consoles and console users, but recently realized that the reason why most people are moving to consoles is because it makes sense to.

PC gaming pros:

-10$ cheaper new games
-much better interface (keyboard + mouse) for FPS games.
-May be able to view the most up to date graphics (DX11).
-May be able to get cool gadgets if your wallet is big enough (eyefinity / 3d Vision).
- You can customize your own skins + mod the game.
- RTS genre pretty much only viable on the PC front. 

Console gaming pros:

-Console cost is 400$ tops. When you need to upgrade (new console), thats pretty much how much you will need to spend. So an upgrade will cost you a few hundered, as opposed to a thousand or more (PC gaming).

-Consoles offer SOCIAL gaming (unlike pcs which offer isolated gaming). Can you play with your gf/wife/friends/siblings on your PC? On a console you can. Also games like guitar hero... etc are very fun drunk and with good company. Why sit alone like a hermit in a corner and play PC games?

-NO HACKS ! You go PC gaming, you will be facing cheaters. How much fun are those games? 

- Gaming controls are ALL Standard for EVERYONE. So everyone gets the same controller handicap. Considering that with PC, everyone will be using a mouse and a keyboard, having the latter does NOT give you an advantage.

-More and more console games are AAA titles. PC is getting fewer and will continue to do so, because a lot of PC gamers dont pay for their games and just pirate it. Game companies arent particularly fond of this.

- The price that would buy you a pc, you can get a 42 - 46 inch HDTV AND a console. That is multi purpose use right there, and can use that for blue ray movies etc... More entertainment for the buck.

-Games in the racing/fighter genre play much better on the console.

-You get to keep Ubuntu and finally chuck away MS for ever on your PC.

Considering all of these facts, i have decided to get a new G10 Viera HDTV 42 inches + PS3 slim. If you want to get an xbox you can, but why would you support a company you are trying to run away from in the first place? PS3 is the way to go IMO. All for around 1000$ + taxes and shipping. Ill get used to the different controls in exchange for games without hackers and the other advantages i listed.
