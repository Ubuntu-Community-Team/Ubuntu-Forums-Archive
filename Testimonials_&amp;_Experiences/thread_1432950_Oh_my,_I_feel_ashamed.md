### Post by dusdus on 2010-03-18
In 2003 I've been using linux a lot: starting with suse, redhat, mandrake...ending with gentoo building my desktop from scratch, compiling every piece of software.

Then I got really lazy and a bit annoyed because of some hardware trouble. Ow, and I played world of warcraft which I couldn't get to run in wine back then. I wanted something which simply worked out-of-the-box. So I installed Windows XP (iirc). And yes everything worked out of the box, including WoW.

Three months ago I realized, as I quit WoW a long time ago, there isn't any reason for me to use windows, so I tried Ubuntu.

Now, I've just reinstalled Kubuntu (building up from a minimal install), while keeping my /home intact because it's on a seperate partition, just to try and do something fun, just for the heck of it. 

And it freaks me out! Everything simply works, it's fast, even with compiz enabled, smooth, good looking...
Damn I feel ashamed I've turned my back onto linux back then.

Sorry, my sincere apologies...

And now...back to some more fun! :D

greetz
dusdus
