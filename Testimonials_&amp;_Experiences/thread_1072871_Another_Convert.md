### Post by rayle on 2009-02-17
Well, I have to say, after returning to Linux from such a break, it is very refreshing and welcoming.

On a whim, I installed Ubuntu 8.10 onto my Acer Aspire One. I followed the appropriate guides and now my system runs smoothly. And only after such a big gap do you realise how much you miss it.

8.10 is wonderful. It's fast, customisable and seems a lot more stable to some of the earlier releases of Ubuntu i've used. I have WiNE installed as a just-in-case measure, but I can find all I need and more in Synaptic, for FREE. I'm still trying to wrap my head around that :D

So thankyou Ubuntu, for welcoming me back into the FOSS community.
