### Post by Darrell Lawrence on 2008-09-03
After I receive my Dell XPS laptop with Ubuntu preloaded, I'm giving my current laptop with Ubuntu 8.04, my printer, and my scanner to my sister in Ohio (I live in Missouri).

My sister is really excited about it. She is in her 50's and has never owned or operated a computer. Her daughter is working on her degree in IT and will set everything up for my sister.

The laptop I'm sending her only has Ubuntu 8.04 on it. So she will be starting out as a "Linux only" user. I think that gives her an advantage - no resistance in changing from Windows to Linux, no relearning to do, etc.
