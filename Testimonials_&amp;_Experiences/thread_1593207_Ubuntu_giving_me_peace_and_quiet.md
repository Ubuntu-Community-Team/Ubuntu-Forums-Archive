### Post by ade234uk on 2010-10-11
About 10 months ago I installed Ubuntu on my dads friend machine. He is 70 years old and computing still confuses him sometimes.

After I installed Windows it only took 2-3 weeks and I was getting calls to help with silly little problems. In the end I made the decision and just installed Ubuntu on his machine. 

I took the machine back around, booted up Ubuntu in front of him and to my surprise he actually found his way around very quickly.
 He even managed to shut down Ubuntu without any prompting and his words where "That's very fast, its looks nice and clean"

Well its been 10 months on and I have only had one issue. The only issue he had was that he was trying open Firefox with 20 tabs where he had obviously saved his previous session. Apart from that I always ask if he is having any problems, and it always, "Machine is running fast, its great, thank you" That's all we need to hear in the home family IT Support unit.

Another happy Ubuntu user.
