### Post by kpkeerthi on 2007-01-10
This was on a 5 year old Pentium 4 PC (1.4 Ghz, 650 MB RAM) my wife had been using. I've been  running Ubuntu on my laptop for about 7 months. My experience with Ubuntu has been so far pleasant. I convinced her what it would mean running linux (no piracy, no antivirus, no spyware and a much stable system & free OS) and we took the plunge. Edgy installed super fine and detected every bit of hardware including the modem that I never was able to find a driver in Windows 2000 that we were running before. Then I walked her way around ubuntu ( this was tougher than I expected :) ) and helped her find the things she might need and use everyday. Then we customized the look and feel to her likings and now she's enjoying the breath of fresh air. We are happy that we were able to put an old desktop to a good use now. Thanks to Ubuntu and the wonderful community.
