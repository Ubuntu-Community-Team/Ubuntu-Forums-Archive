### Post by nickdbliss on 2008-09-09
Last post that i made was of using alpha 3 and my experience with it.([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=890534](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=890534)) But i must say that with this new alpha release i am very much impressed!! I am testing it for couple of days now.Some of the issues that i faced with alpha 1,2,3 and 4 are not present in the new release. No bugs of usplash are present now. Moreover mouse and keyboard work fine at login. I still had to install ndiswrapper but WAP works flawlessly.Also surprisingly i got fps of 1800 with default graphics driver (Integrated ATI ).

Issues which are yet to be worked upon are of shut down button (I actually love to do it from terminal. #sudo halt.) and frequent crashes of audio_playback etc. One problem that i faced in previous versions and which is still not resolved is of not able to use keyboard short cut keys when i have clicked to open applications or places or some other menu item.

Anyways i hope they fix them soon before final release. i have a lot of expectations from this release. Cheers.
