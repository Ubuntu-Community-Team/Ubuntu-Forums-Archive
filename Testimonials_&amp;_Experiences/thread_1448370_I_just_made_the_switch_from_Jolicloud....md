### Post by kjzz12 on 2010-04-06
And I've got to say, everything works better in Ubuntu Netbook Remix 9.10! Video plays faster, I can actually run compiz smoothly, my battery life is much longer, pages load quicker, Skype is actually usable, and I can sync my iPod Touch! Jolicloud is based on 9.04, and the benefits from the upgrade are very noticeable. The only difficulty I found was installing my wireless drivers, but I solved that with the aid of ubuntumini.com. Also, I was surprised at the number of updates my fresh install required when I opened the update manager, but I guess that's to be expected with a distro that is soon to be updated to the next version.

I also understand that we are getting closer to the 10.04 update. What's the protocol for that? I understand that it's in beta testing, and that's why I'm not going to upgrade my only machine (a lowly Dell 10v) until it is a stable, official release. So from day one, will there be a fully function NBR 10.04? Or should I hold off, and if so, until when?

Anywho, I'm having a fantastic experience, and enjoying what feels like a brand-new, and finally fully functional netbook. Thanks!!
