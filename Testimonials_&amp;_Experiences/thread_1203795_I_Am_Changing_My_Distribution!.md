### Post by Pauly Psychotic on 2009-07-03
Just wanted to say thank you Ubuntu for being my beginner distribution.  You will always be my first Linux Distro.  However with the release of Jaunty my desktop (main system) is lagging pretty heavily, I prefer to use Gnome as I am used to it so I did not want to switch to Xubuntu.  I am also tired of reformatting my HD every six months to get the newest software and even then it's still not completely new.  

I have switched to [www.PCLINUXOS.com](www.PCLINUXOS.com).  Easiest rolling release distribution I've found to setup.  So far it's running pretty quick with Gnome and I have all the same programs on it. 

I will see how it goes for a few months if all goes well then I am to say farewell to Ubuntu.  As it makes it much easier to keep up to date with my programs etc.  Without having to reformat.

Again you will always be my first distro my first real escape from Windows.  I have learned and learned now it's time for another change.  I will keep an eye out possibly on future releases as they come out.  But for now I'm PCLINUXOS.
