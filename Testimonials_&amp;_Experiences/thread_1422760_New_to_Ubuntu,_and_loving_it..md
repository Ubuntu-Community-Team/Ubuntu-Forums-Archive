### Post by sixthwheel on 2010-03-05
Wow, this is so cool. I've been on Ubuntu for a few weeks now, and I don't miss Windows at all.
I thought I would come crawling back like a whipped child, but no way!!!

The last straw for me , was back a few months ago.
I caught some virus, or malware, or who knows what, but it completely destroyed my XP.
I could not even boot up....It would just keep restarting endlessly.

I put the desktop in the spare room to collect dust.

I still had my laptop. I loaded it up with all kinds of antivirus, anti phishing and what not.
I stayed only on sites, that I knew I could trust.
I would not dare download anything, ie/ videos, music etc.

Then I asked myself, how can I live like this?..I need to do something different.
I really could not afford to buy a Mac, and I was really scared of Linux.
Then I started to do a little research on the web.

I came across this thing called Linux Slax, where you can download it  to a CD, and run it live.
I gave it a shot, and it looked pretty cool, so I did some more researching, and came across Ubuntu.

I said, what do I have to lose? Windows was already leading me down the path of insanity, how can Linux be any worse?

So I downloaded the ISO for Ubuntu and burned it to a disk.
Then I walked in the spare room, dusted off the Sony Vaio .
Lucky I could still get into the BIOS, and set it to boot from CD.

Popped in the disk, and wow..there was the Ubuntu install screen.
It asked if I wanted to wipe Windows off the face of the Earth. Absolutely!

I haven't looked back since. 
The best part is, that learning is so easy...I come across problems all the time, but I just Google it, and solve it in no time.

So, good bye Windows, and hello Linux..I have a feeling it's going to be a long and happy relationship.:P
