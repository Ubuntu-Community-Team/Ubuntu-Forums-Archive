Hi all,
I myself am not an Ubuntu user, I mostly use Kanotix on my desktops, OSX does my laptops.  BUT, I have a friend whose Windows box was cluttered up with over 150,000 viruses & spyware, who just started with Ubuntu.  She has dedicated herself to learning this operating system.  While she is very willing to learn her prior knowledge of Linux was zero.  I gave her a copy of the cd...I had ordered in the past.....6.06 and walked her through the installation over the phone. She is encouraging them to make the jump from Windows.  While I do not personally use Ubuntu I do promote it to others, and will act as a very limited IT guy for all she converts.  Thank you for creating and distributing an OS that people can actually use quickly without any prior knowledge.
