### Post by Troy Martin on 2010-07-04
Ah, Wubi. That wonderful installer that lets one dual-boot Ubuntu and Windows using the Windows bootloader. I picked it up today and downloaded the latest Ubuntu ISO, as I have not had Ubuntu installed for a while and used to run it in that horridly slow VirtualBox -- not that VBox is bad, it's just slow.

So about an hour ago, I fired up the installer and put Ubuntu on this machine.

I love the new colours in 10.04 and I must say, I am very impressed. I installed tweet and gimp, and a few other necessities, and already I am enjoying the Linux distribution more than I ever have before. It truly is one of the best operating systems I have ever used in my lifetime as a computer geek.

So, to the Ubuntu development team, I give you my thanks for creating such a wonderful system distribution that I can use for my day-to-day programming needs alongside Windows.

Cheers, and here's to the future of Ubuntu.
--Troy
