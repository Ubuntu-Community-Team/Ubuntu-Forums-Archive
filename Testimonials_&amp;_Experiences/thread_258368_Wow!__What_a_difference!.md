### Post by powdercarrot on 2006-09-15
I just want to say that over the past few months, Ubuntu has taken huge strides on the desktop and in the media.  I was so frustrated with the initial Dapper release, that I went back to XP.  I just installed 6.06 LTS and it was amazingly pleasant.  I would especially like to commend those who fixed the problem with synaptic touchpads.  My first install of Dapper never worked well with the touchpad on my HP dv1000 despite many attempts to modify the settings in the GUI and terminal.  With this release, all I had to do was go into the mouse prefs menu, and all is well. There are many reasons Ubuntu is getting great press!

Now then only thing keeping me from fully migrating to Ubuntu is iTunes.  As much as I hate to admit it, Apple has a fantastic app.  It is intuitive, stable, fast, and integrates many functions well.  

Anyway, keep up the good work and here's to all those out there working hard to further the cause of open source!
