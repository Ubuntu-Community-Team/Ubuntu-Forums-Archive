### Post by thach562 on 2008-01-30
Hello all, I don't really know how to say this so I'll just blurt things out as they come to mind. 
I'm a broke college student basically independent and like most of you, trying to pave my way through the crazy world. Lets just say that at the end of the month my moral and emotional well being depends on the positive or negative amount located on my bank statement. 

I was using Windows XP before, which I got free from my college until I switched to VISTA which I obtained from a friend. The short end of the long story is basically it drove me batty. 

I learned about Ubuntu from a friend in class and decided to check it out. After some dual booting expeditions I just got too frustrated with Windows and jumped ship unto the Ubuntu life raft. It's been a coupe of weeks and I just received my Ubuntu CD's and I just have to say I am very appreciative of the quality of the packaging and overall dedication to the free software philosophy Ubuntu and all the other linux distros have shown. 

Every time I want to do something (watch streaming, install an app, configure video-drivers) I actually have to go and poke around and not just mindlessly click buttons. This has provided a great sense of accomplishment once I actually figured how to do said task. 

Well enough of the rant, I just wanted to post something somewhere to show that I applicate the work and dedication to this GREAT and FREE operating system. 

<3 Ubuntu and the Ubuntu community.
