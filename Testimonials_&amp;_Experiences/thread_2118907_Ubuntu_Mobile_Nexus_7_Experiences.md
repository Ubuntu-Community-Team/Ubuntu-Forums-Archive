### Post by jonashendrickx on 2013-02-22
**Performance**
I was trying out Ubuntu for Tablets. Let's say I am satisfied with the performance. There is slight lag but it doesn't bother me much. Although it can be better. I think it will be worse on my Galaxy Nexus. You will notice some stuttering in some occasions or lag. Wel don't worry. It doesn't hang and you barely will notice it.

**I lost my way**
Navigation was fairly difficult. I got lost all the time. I couldn't find where to change date and time. And compared to Android it's fairly difficult to use. Older people may and will find their way to Android. Ubuntu is not recommended unless you are a easy learner with computers and electronics.

In Windows 8 I learned it all very quickly and it was easy to get to know the new environment. But with Ubuntu Mobile it seems all strange to me. Sometimes I accidentally close my application because I accidentally swiped it away.

**Camera**
The camera didn't work.

**Facebook**
Facebook went fairly well but crashed when switching to another task. When I wanted to continue my Facebook session I couldn't visit another page anymore.

**The beautiful**
The positive things are screen and display quality which seem to be far better than with Android. I really loved the colors and themes. There were much better colors with Ubuntu than I ever experienced with Android. I was like oh my god. Also no sign of the irritating Nvidia Prism feature. It may be there but you will notice it less than in Android.

**My suggestions and bugs:**
- You can use your magic corners and your magic swipes. But make sure there is something visible on the screen for the less capable people. I don't want to walk around with a manual 24/7.
- The screen doesn't turn off after a certain amount of time. You need to turn it off manually.
- Camera not working

Keep up the good work. I hope to see a Ubuntu Software Center in the final version.
