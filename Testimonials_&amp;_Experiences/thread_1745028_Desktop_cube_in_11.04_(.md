### Post by JheregJAB on 2011-04-30
I'd just like to report that I have desktop cube working fine (though after quite a bit of fiddling) on Ubuntu 11.04 with unity. 

here are the steps I took, some of them may or may not apply to you, I wouldn't know as I haven't tested this more than the once I did it to get it to work for me.

open ccsm
enable wobbly windows
enable desktop cube. It'll complain, tell it to enable anyway.
enable animations, again it'll complain do it anyway.
disable animations.
enable window decorations
enable move window
enable resize window
re-enable (mine turned itself off) wobbly windows
re-enable desktop cube and rotate cube
re enable unity
log out and log back on.

about that last step, in order to be able to log out and back on I had to log into a terminal (ctrl-alt-F6) and execute "sudo service gdm restart"

I don't know if it will or not, but I hope this works for you. Good luck!

Jacob
