### Post by aaargh486 on 2007-11-29
I started a while ago with Suse. I'm pretty sure it's much better right now, but the 9.1 version didn't exactly do it for me.

Luckily I bumped upon the Feisty Fawn years later. It didn't work flawlessly, but quite well. It converted me completely to Linux. I solved the problems the hard way (compiling drivers etc...) and I did very good. It became the dominant OS in a couple of days.

And then came the Gibbon!:guitar:

It worked flawlessly with all hardware I had and a lot faster than XP. 
And luckily, my motherboard broke down. For Gutsy it was a case of just switching the hard-drive into the other PC, but Winblows of course does not allow that. 

So I can now state with extreme pride I'm completely, 100% and fully Windows-free. :popcorn:

I want to give a huge THANK YOU to the Ubuntu community, for making me a fantastic, free operating-system that works like a fly.
