### Post by dasqnz on 2007-09-02
Hey everyone,

Thought it was about time that I posted to say how much I love Ubuntu.

I recently bought a new laptop that came with crappy Vista.  Average loading time for the laptop while running Vista was around 2mins.  Loading time for Ubuntu is 15 seconds or so.
It is just a great piece of software that works flawlessly.
I have been using Ubuntu for just over a month now and have managed to convince the majority of my friends and family to switch over to it.  Heck even my grandmother has switched over and thinks it's real easy to use.

Today I threw out all of my old Microsoft software, I was going to give them to my neighbour, but instead I gave him a copy of Ubuntu.

Many thanks to all that helped make Ubuntu. :)
