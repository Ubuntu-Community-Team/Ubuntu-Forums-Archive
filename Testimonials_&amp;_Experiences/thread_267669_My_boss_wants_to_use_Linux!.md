### Post by geekchic9 on 2006-09-28
Hello, all. For those of you who may remember me (like KingBahamut and Kassetra), I'm glad to be back.

My testimonial:

My new boss is very much into open source software, but he still uses Windows. During my job interview with him today, I asked, "Would you consider switching to Linux?" He was shocked that I used Linux, much less installed it. I told him that I've used Linux before (off and on for a few years now) and just recently installed Ubuntu 6.06 on my spare hard drive--and it was incredibly simple, as you all know. He hired me for an administrative assistant position, and told me that he would include "Linux usage" and "training other people to use Linux" to my job description. 

It's off-topic, but I am glad to be back and using Linux. I have to use Windows software for school, but I will continue to use Linux as much as I can at home, and soon, at work. My first day at work is Monday, October 16. I will keep you all informed on my process of converting people to Linux at work.
