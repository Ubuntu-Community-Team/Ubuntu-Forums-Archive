### Post by peitschie on 2008-05-20
Ubuntu is upgradable... it just (in my experience anyways) ALWAYS has some major kinks to work out.  HAving said that I've managed to upgrade my laptop Feisty -> Gutsy -> Hardy successfully... Just had a lot of clean up to do.  

Some generic tips for upgrading:[LIST]
[*]Get rid of any custom drivers or applications
[*]Uninstall all programs that didn't come from the offical ubuntu repositories
[*]Uninstall any self-compiled software (hopefully installed using checkinstall ;))
[*]Clean up all cache files, un-needed settings etc.
[*]Roll-back customised config files where possible (this will be taken care of during the upgrade though)
[/LIST]

For me this has made things a little less painful.  It still is not quite as easy as a full install... but sometimes it can be easier than starting utterly from scratch.
