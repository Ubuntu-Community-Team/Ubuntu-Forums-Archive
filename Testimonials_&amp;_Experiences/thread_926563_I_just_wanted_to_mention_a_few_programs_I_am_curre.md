### Post by pluckypigeon on 2008-09-22
I am just going to mention some of the programs I am currently having fun with. 

It's up to you what you post but [COLOR="Red"]**I think it would be more interesting to the reader if you don't post other programs, but instead post about the programs that I mention.** [/COLOR]

I think that will save another one of those favourite app posts.

**Virtualbox** 

I recently installed this and I was surprised how easy it was to set up. I am running Win98 and DSL from it. 

I'm only doing it for a bit of fun and nothing serious and it seems really cool the way it has the distro in a window on your desktop.

**Audacity**

I play a lot of guitar so I love this program. I connect my guitar in to my amp and then in to my pc and it sounds great. You don't actually need any form of amp to record though. 

**Putty**

I haven't found any real use from this yet but I think it's cool that I can connect to my pc remotely from any other pc. I just use it for a bit of fun.

**Prism**

I could never find a use for Prism before but now I have found loads. You have to give it a chance though. Websites run really fast on it. If you can find a website that doesn't need a navigation bar then I really recommend this. 

I hope that I have helped broaden your minds on mentioning this. Let me know what you think!
