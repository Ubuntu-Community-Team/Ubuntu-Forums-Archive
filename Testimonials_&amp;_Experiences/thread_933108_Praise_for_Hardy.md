### Post by metafractal on 2008-09-29
I've been meaning to write this email for a long time now. I'd just like to say that I'm very, very impressed with how well Ubuntu 8.04 runs on my HP Pavilion laptop. I'd been able to get Gutsy running quite smoothly but this had taken a lot of work and certain things still didn't work (particularly annoying was that I had to disable certain features of my processor to get everything to run). When I first installed Hardy I was disappointed because it didn't seem to recognize my graphics card even though it's quite a common one (GeForce Go). Fixing this required messy manual editing of xorg.conf, but since then almost everything has run like a dream. My digital camera and microphone work out of the box, as do the media keys on my laptop and remote control. The greatest claim to fame however is, without a doubt, that the Suspend feature now works! Flawlessly! I am able to press the "off" button on the remote control to my laptop and Ubuntu faithfully goes into suspend, ready to wake up into exactly the same state.

Well done Ubuntu team!
