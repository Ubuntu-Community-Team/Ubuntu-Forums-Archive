### Post by Ian dewhurst on 2009-06-14
I have been an ubuntu user for many months now and I would like to share my experience with superOS (another spin on ubuntu) and wow just wow.
Some would call it bloated but for me its ubuntu that works OOTB I can't fault it!
It has so far played ever DVD, MP3, flash files etc etc with no messing about what so ever its amazing!;
I also love the other bits and pieces it enables by default:
ubuntu tweak, file manager (root mode), wine, opera, vlc, realplayer 11, java6, startup manager, and I think firewall config GUI (could be wrong on the last one and perhaps missed a few things)
But for this is exactly how I wanted ubuntu to be just no messing from the word go. I have hardy installed on another partition and here is where superOS/jaunty comes into its own. 
I have a broadcom bcm4311 device that I didn't even know about! In XP for wireless internet I had to used a sketchy WPN111 device (some may remember my earliest posts) so I didn't even know it had this, hardy never recocnized one but somehow jaunty does and its sooooo much better than the WPN device I now have a download of 250kibs per second rather than 110 (we don't get fast broadband where I am in England)

Well to sum up thanks for an amazing OS you have made my year with this I can't stop smiling the way just everything well works:D
