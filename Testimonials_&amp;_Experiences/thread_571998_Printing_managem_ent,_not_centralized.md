To whom it may concern:

I have finally managed to make my magicolor 2430 DL to print at Ubuntu Feisty, arch = 686.

Would like to comment that under System/Administration/Printing I setup the printer configuration to my preferences.

However, when I go to print from the various softwares, none of them recognize that setup.

That means that I have to check and change the various softwares to the preferences that I already had established under System/Administration/Printing.

It's OK to allow for flexibility and latitude. However, I feel that the sotware should reflect the Properties that one establishes at System/Administaion/Printing, at least initially.

Otherwise, what's the point of setting them in System/Administration/Printing (which ought to be Central) if softwares, which actually do the printing, don't bother to check the parameters and use those as default?

Thanks for reading and considering this.
