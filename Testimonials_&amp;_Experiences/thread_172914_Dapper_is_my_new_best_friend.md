### Post by Kibbo on 2006-05-09
Wow, 

I am impressed.  My first install with Dapper was more of an experiment than anything else, and I beat my head against the wall a bit due to ndiswrapper woes. (A part of every linux install I've ever tried.)

I decided to reinstall, partly to start fresh, partly to get my partition tables set up the way I wanted. (I wanted my /home on my second, larger HD).

Putting my root on a Reiserfs partition and putting my swap on the second drive made everything much, much, snappier.  The GUI on the partitioner was great.

What's more, I tried ifconfig for a lark.  What did I see there but my wireless card! A quick input of my encryption key and I was online and jubilantly posting here.

Next up:  Fun with XGL!
