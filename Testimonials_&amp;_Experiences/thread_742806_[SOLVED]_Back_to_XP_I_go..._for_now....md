### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-04-02
EasyTag has a slightly unusual interface, but once you use it a couple of times it becomes virtually second-nature. I don't have any trouble with it.

Your portable hard disk is probably formatted as NTFS, which explains the permissions problem. You should get the "ntfs-config" program to properly enable writing onto NTFS drives.

The ffmpeg that comes with Ubuntu is devoid of support for certain patented formats, for reasonable reasons. The good news is, the version on the Medibuntu repo ([www.medibuntu.com](www.medibuntu.com)) has all the support you need.

I originally had to build a custom version of ffmpeg (there was a bug in the Medibuntu ffmpeg) in order to encode video for my Sony Walkman. So I know what you're talking about. But compiling custom versions of things is a very rare occurance; and thank goodness we actually have that option on Linux!
