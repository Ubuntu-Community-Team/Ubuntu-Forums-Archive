The good:
[LIST]
[*]I used adept's dist upgrader to upgrade from 8.10. For once in my life this worked well (and broke no graphics drivers).

[*]Kubuntu comes with KDE 4.2, which is pretty; I'd been using it from the PPA anyway.
[/LIST]

The bad:
[LIST]
[*]The update-notifier applet crashes every so often on startup.

[*]Adept's list of proposed updates now has REALLY LARGE FONTS. BECAUSE BIGGER MAKES IT SIMPLER, right?

[*]Sound. Somehow, in 2009, sound is still an issue. It was quite solid for me on 8.04 and 8.10...but now, not so much. Issues: 1) sometimes when Amarok has been playing for a while, all of the sound coming from the sound system will suddenly become slow motion (like those bad youtube videos where they show you the action replay with sound); 2) Amarok and Flash on Firefox do _not_ play well together. Specifically, if I try to start a Flash video while Amarok is playing, I can't get sound out of Firefox again until I kill Amarok, kill Firefox, and then restart Firefox. Wut.

[*]Occasional KDE bugs; sometimes plasma just dies and I have to restart X. Oh, but wait:

[*]X restart is disabled. Seriously Canonical, WTF. Yes, I found dontzap --disable, but you cannot ship both a) buggy desktop environments and b) no way to restart X. The day you ship a desktop environment which is perfect is the day I'm happy for you to turn off restarting X. Until then, leave it alone, PLEASE?!
[/LIST]
