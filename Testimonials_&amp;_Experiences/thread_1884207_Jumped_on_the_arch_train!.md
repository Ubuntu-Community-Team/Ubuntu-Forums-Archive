### Post by Atamisk on 2011-11-20
Hello all. 

After 2 years using Ubuntu, i finally came to terms with the fact that i was wanting to 'get around' or 'fight' all of the little automations Ubuntu uses to make beginner's lives easier. So, i decided to try Arch. All i can say is that, ironically, i spent all day making my desktop look EXACTLY the same as it did in Ubuntu. :P

However, i *am* happy with the level of control Arch affords, and i think it will stay my destktop distro for a while. Still, nothing beats the 'just works'-ness of Ubuntu, so i think it's here to stay on my laptop. Anywho, thanks to the Ubuntu community for eventually growing me to the point that i could get arch *installed*, much less configured with the shinies, such as compiz and 3d gaming. 

Thanks again, and i'm loooking happily forward to 12.04 on my laptop!

--Aaron
