### Post by Chayak on 2008-01-01
Well a buddy of mine was working on a revamp of their network so we did some testing on Vista and while it may look pretty with some new features it is a memory hog.  On identical hardware Vista was idling at 1gb of memory usage just sitting at the desktop.  Ubuntu about 150mb.  What applications would someone normally use in an office?  We opened a word processor, spreadsheet, and email, and a web browser because we all know they do it.  We were using Office 2007 on the Vista side and Open Office on Ubuntu.  The difference was still huge.  Then we used Open Office on Vista and it was still wanting over a gig of memory.  XP was also included in the tests and it's funny but it performed better than Vista on all counts.

What happened?  Well my friend put forth his recommendations with all the data to switch to Linux, but management overrode him and they're still on XP.
