### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-06-28
> Matt05
So for the next 5 years or so official system updates won't randomly break my computer.

I've been running Ubuntu on my home built P4 for 3 years and never had an update break my system. My family has another 5 systems (various makes and models) that have never been broken by an update either. Neither has an a upgrade broke our system or any of the other five. We are 100% Ubuntu with no dual boot or Windows programs (wine).

We also don't upgrade immediately upon release and usually wait for at least the second update prior to installing a new upgrade. For example I didn't install 9.04 until just about a month ago and only after assuring everything worked with the live CD.

The only issue we ever had was with Brasero in 9.04.
