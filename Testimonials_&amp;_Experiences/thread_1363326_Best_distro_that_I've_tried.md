### Post by albannach1 on 2009-12-24
This is all my opinion obviously but...

Ubuntu is the best Linux distribution I have ever tried. I've used it off and on for around 2 years now. I found my old laptop the other day and decided to try some other distros just to see if there was anything better out there. I tried Gentoo (they should have mentioned that installing takes 10+ hours!), Arch, Debian, and even the Ubuntu derivatives kubuntu and xubuntu (which is actually xfce running on top of gnome??? and its supposed to faster how???).

Ubuntu 9.10 is my favorite distro out of all of them, its easy enough to install, the hardware drivers detection tool thing is amazing (saved hours of work, all other distros couldn't detect my broadcom wireless card), and it just works.

I know a lot of people have had problems with 9.10 but I just wanted to report that it's working beautifully for me. I wiped out Mac OS X on my Mac Mini and am now using just Ubuntu (its faster and leaner than OS X).
