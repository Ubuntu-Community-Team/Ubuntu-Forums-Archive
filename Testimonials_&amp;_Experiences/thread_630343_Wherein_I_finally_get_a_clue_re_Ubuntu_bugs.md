### Post by samuraiCat on 2007-12-03
Okay, I think I must have bumped my head on the way to Linux, because I just got it. Finally.

I hated Windows, couldn't afford the hardware for a Mac, and I heard great things about Linux, so I tried Feisty. It rocked. It still does. But I was still stuck in my closed-source world of computing.

Then I tried Gutsy, and it broke two systems. I was mad. So mad that I went around complaining, "Whose fault is this? I want heads on sticks!"

I just now got it: The head? Yeah, that's supposed to be me. 

I'm supposed to get in, have problems, and post them to Launchpad. Then try to fix them. 

That said, I am going to put Gutsy and Hardy Alpha on an old IDE drive and multiboot with Feisty as my main OS. Until Gutsy and/or Hardy plays nice with my computers.
