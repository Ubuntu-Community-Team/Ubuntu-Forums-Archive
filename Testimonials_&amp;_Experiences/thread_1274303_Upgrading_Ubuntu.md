### Post by canam101 on 2009-09-24
I'm probably preaching to the choir, but I am impressed with how easy it was to upgrade ubuntu 8.04 to 8.10 to 9.04.

Actually, I was impressed that it was possible to do it at all without re-installing everything, including all my tweaks, etc.

I was so used to windows that I didn't even realize until yesterday that somebody had figured out how to do such a normal, user-friendly thing.

The two upgrades to get to 9.04 went almost flawlessly. The only extra thing I had to do was specify a new repository for Tor and re-install it.

Good for Ubuntu!

### Post by vrkalak on 2009-09-24
I just upgraded myself, from Ubuntu 9.04 to Ubuntu Karmic 9.10 (development/testing)

First I backed-up everything. Then, I downloaded and saved the LiveCD/ISO files for Ubuntu 9.10 on my laptop.  (as a back up plan, in case, the upgrade went bad.)

Then, I updated/upgraded the kernel and distro via terminal and Update Manager.

It was very easy and with only a couple very minor problems with the 9.10 desktop thus far.  Easily fixed.  

Overall, I am impressed!!
