### Post by tom66 on 2010-04-11
I'm running off the LiveCD of Lucid Beta 2 right now, and I love it! Great work guys and gals who worked on Lucid (and are presumably still working on it for the April 29th release). I'll probably be installing Beta 2 as it seems stable enough for me.

[list]
[*] RadeonHD working out of the box, full 2D/3D/Xv (plus Compiz) support for my Mobility Radeon HD 3450 card.
[*] Virtual terminal switching is much more seamless, and a higher resolution for the terminals, which is great if you're doing lots of stuff on them.
[*] Brightness control bugs are fixed.
[*] It's very fast even from the CD. Bootup is seamless - there is no switchover from boot to desktop!
[*] It's only using 557 MB of memory (no swap), compared to 9.10's 1 GB - 1.5 GB (memory leak in Xorg).
[/list]

Some things I don't like:

[list]
[*] Close/Minimise/Maximise buttons on the left, because I'm right handed and they've always been there. I'll figure out how to put them on the right.
[*] Default Yahoo! search but if it helps to make some money for Canonical I don't mind (I just changed it to Google.)
[*] The dark theme is too dark for my eyes but the light theme is just right.
[/list]

Great work! Another happy user. :)
