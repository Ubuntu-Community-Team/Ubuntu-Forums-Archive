### Post by keres on 2008-09-21
I've been using Ubuntu-only for about a week now. very impressed and i have learned a lot. my dad is also intrigued about the different file system and computer workings in the terminal. And the fact we can load just about any windows application through wine. he keeps asking me why Microsoft hasn't gone out of business!

the rest of my family likes the 3d special effects option, and they love open office because it is much easier to use then MS word, and it can export to a .doc to use at schools and stuff. They also enjoy the new look it gave the computer and the freedom of browsing the internet without worrying about getting viruses.

I, like the new file system. Manually installing all the Nvidia drivers on my friend's pc (he really loves it too) and it really got me warmed up to Linux and what i should do when this happens and so on. I also like the application loader and the wireless setup area at the top bar, displaying signal strength and you can easily switch back and forth between connections with a breeze. I also like how Ubuntu has basically automatic drivers for computers. No need to install sound card drivers or motherboard drivers - it is all automatic (with the exception of the vid card), and i also like how customizable the system really is, and the ease of performing common tasks.

I will never go back to windows!
