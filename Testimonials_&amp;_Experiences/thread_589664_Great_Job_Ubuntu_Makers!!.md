### Post by usererror on 2007-10-24
This may not be the right place to post this, but I did a fresh install of 7.10 on my Desktop, my laptop, and my media center PC and everything has been great so far!  I've had some minor issues with my wide screen LCD (but Microsoft setup has the same problems, so it's not limited to Ubuntu)

Things that make me the happiest are:

-Network Manager just *works* in this release, for LEAP and WEP, and everything I have tried so far to connect to.
-3D effects built in, just work and look great!
-Adding on Rhythmbox support for mp3 or codecs for video files just works and so much easier.  mp3s were playing in less than 2 minutes!!!

I think this Distro is so close to truly becoming Linux for Humans!!

\\:D/ Kudos to all developers and helpers!!
