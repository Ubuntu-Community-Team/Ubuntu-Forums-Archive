### Post by Markg55 on 2011-05-29
Just a  post to give my 2-cents worth (before taxes). I bit the big one and started delving in to Linux via Ubuntu 11.04 and Xubuntu 10.04. I have installed them both (Dual Boot) on a IBM TP R50e that I found in a dumpster. Outside of a shot battery, crashed hard drive and some minor cosmetic scratches this ThinkPad is fully functioning. The more I tinker with Ubuntu the the more I am digging it. Yes I am still a fan of Win 7 and XP , probably will be for some time to come. I am totally impressed with all the apps out there that are available for Linux. The help that can be found for Noobe's on forums such as this one is amazing. Most times one can find the answer quite quickly without posting a question. Maybe someday I will find a Linux app that can totally replace Adobe Lightroom. All would be well as I am into photography as well. I am also looking for a Quick Time Virtual  Tour app that would work in Linux as well. 

To anyone out there who might be considering a journey into Linux by all means go ahead. You will be pleasantly surprised as it is not as scary as it once was.
