### Post by 0N3 on 2010-09-27
My favourite feature is the partioning options i setup mine as follows:

/ (20-30GB) root partition

/swap 4GB swap partition

/home as the rest of the disk home partition

Doing this helps you later on with reinstallation of Ubuntu as you only format the / partition

remebers all your settings bookmarks keeps all your pics vids and movies cause you dont format the home partition its awesome unlike windows you format everythnig :)
