### Post by vikramaditya on 2008-08-02
Earlier tonight I was on the forum using Opera.  I opened a new tab to check into something and Opera prompted me that the (reputable) webpage I'd gone to wanted to run some kinda activeX control.  I said "sure, go ahead" and the expected result never materialised.  So, I closed that tab, logged out of the forum, hit "Delete Private Data", & closed Opera.  However, the Opera icon was still showing in the top panel's notification area.  Intending to close it, I right-clicked the icon and no context menu appeared.  I soon determined that Hardy had seized up tighter than a crab's bung & only a hard reset fixed things.  This was the first lockup I've ever experienced on any of the 4 ubuntu distros I've used.  Hopefully, it was just because the OS didn't know what to make of the alien activeX control.  but, what do I know? :)
