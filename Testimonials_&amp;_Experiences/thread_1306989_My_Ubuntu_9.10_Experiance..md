### Post by claymater on 2009-10-30
I have to say, I have never had any linux distro work as well as this one has. I installed it, and it found my drivers (and did it for me), It works out of the box with dual monitors, I have only had it crash once! (Empathy's fault). I have a ATI x1300 radeon video card, and I have had NO problems! It is fast, videos run smooth (not flickering), and compiz was enabled from first boot up. 

I have almost no complaints about it! BUT, The ones I do I will list! 

*System*
-Software Center is a little slow, and lags a bit when it starts to install... But I know this will get fixed. 
 
-I installed another driver (my old one) and compiz stopped working... Now this isn't to bad, I just uninstalled that driver, and reinstalled the old one. (The one from the fresh install) 

-The Notifications are kinda annoying, I wish I could use them more like Growl (OSX), but this will get fixed... and I have yet to find ways to edit this.. but I really haven't looked that hard. 

*Third party* (linux in general, not just ubuntu)
-I have yet to find a good video editor. 

-Adobe air and flash have some weird problems sometimes (like not working or showing controls) 





Now the good! 

- Runs fast
- boots up fast
- works well
- stable
- doesn't fart out on me
- kernel seems stable
- skype works (with pulse)
- video runs amazing
- everything worked right out of the box! 
p.s. Chrome is getting very pro! ;)  

Now I came into this without even expecting it to boot up (like in 9.04) and after I had it all installed, I found myself VERY impressed with the outcome of what ubuntu has become! It has a way to go, but I would feel comfortable installing this on my friends computer. 



So what has your experience on 9.10 been?
