### Post by Jacobonbuntu on 2011-07-04
The human memory is not a reliable thing....
 

 I used Windows for many years. I can get it for (almost) free (legally), as well as a lot of well known programs like Adobe CS5, Office, Filemaker, Finale and whatever you can think of. Although I would miss this forum, to me the competition between Ubuntu/Linux and Windows is not such a fascinating phenomena that I would base my choice of OS on it. I would always use what fits me best.  
 This afternoon I had to set foot on the Windows 7 part of my wife's computer, after a few months of only Linux/ Xu-, Lu-, Ku- and U-buntu. Apart from the fact that I spent an hour waiting for the updates to finish (with no possibility to interfere), I didn't like what I saw at all..... Cheap looking windows, annoying desktop items, ugly start menu, where are my lenses? The lack of a good software management...., the terminal,  I even missed the global menus! Why is everything so hard to control??  This is not what I remembered :-k.
 

 In other words, I am afraid you won't get rid of me anymore.....
