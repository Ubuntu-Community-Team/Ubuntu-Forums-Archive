### Post by jongers on 2009-05-08
It's time to take on Microsoft and hopefully defeat their monopolizing marketing strategies with FREE easy to use and functional software thats seamlessly integrated into ubuntu.

I had been a Windows user for more than 15 years, without the need or the want to move away.   That was until Windows Vista - or at least untill i realised it was incompatible.

About 2 years ago I started looking around for alternatives, I tried the "Commercial" solutions - CENTOS, SUSE but the software repos wern't able to fulfill my needs.  I found an ubuntu disk by accident whilst trying to recover a hard drive and havn't looked back since.
  It was a revolution to my computing needs, it was free and an almost limitless amount of support and software opened up new avenues to my productivity.
After 12months, windows is no longer a requirement.  I think I have a couple of virtual machines with XP, and apart from many servers, that run the machines i maintain for others I don't think ill ever need one again.

The day ubuntu has an equal market share as Microsoft will be awesome.  Im very thankfull for Microsofts delightfull cock-up of a resource hungry operating system with absoloutly NO native compatability with the world.
