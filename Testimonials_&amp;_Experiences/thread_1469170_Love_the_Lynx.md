### Post by Rodney9 on 2010-05-02
I love Ubuntu 10.4 Lucid Lynx.
It is so fast.
It boots in 10-12 seconds.
It shuts down in 3-5 seconds.

It was so easy to get codecs, fonts etc set-up with - [10 things to do after installing Ubuntu 10.4]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/04/10-things-to-do-after-installing-ubuntu.html")

I've set up conky with weather details so easy with help and thanks to kaivalagi [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=869328](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=869328)

I have set up my clock to tell me the time each half hour.

I also have conky showing the song title from the internet radio that moc is playing.

I used a background from Appearance Preferences, Get more backgrounds online.

Everything is so easy, installing new software and it shows up automatically in the menu.

I swear my network is 10% faster.

My only disappointment is Ubuntu One Music Store it is missing Australian and popular classics music ie the Stones etc. I presume and hope it will grow in time.

I love Lucid Lynx and we have 3 years support and this forum. The Lynx blows away every other OS.

Rodney
