### Post by NightwishFan on 2010-04-01
I have been a member of the Ubuntu community for a very long time and I am proud to be so. I regret to say that I no longer will be using Ubuntu. I have finally found an OS that fits my needs, I have switched over to Windows 7. I have to say that I have never had a more enjoyable time using a computer. It has all the software that I want, and it is fast, stable and elegant. The glassy themes go with all the pictures of my family I put up, and everyone enjoys being on the computer. I no longer have issues with administration and playing the media I want. Windows just works.

I still plan to be a member of these forums and post here. I am glad me and Ubuntu had such a long run and pretty much without any bumps. I hope that my opinions and advice are still needed here, though in the future I will advise everyone to try Windows 7. Does anyone else have any great 7 stories to tell?
