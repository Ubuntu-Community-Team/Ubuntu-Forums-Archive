### Post by MalfunctioningMartian on 2007-09-19
I liked FOSS ever since I was handed The OpenCD by a friend. A while back I decided to take the plunge and switch to Linux. This was shortly after I found 8 spybots on my system.

After googling all the popular Linux distro's I decided on Ubuntu. I then asked in the za-mailing list if anyone could post me a cd. Within a day I had 3 responses. To date this is still my favourite thing about Ubuntu, the community.

The installation went off without a hitch and I was very impressed about the way it automatically detected and joined up with a LAN. :popcorn:

After the installation I had a glitch with my graphics card (Which I fixed by enabling restricted drivers) and a few GPG errors :confused: with synaptic (after installing WINE) but on the IRC channel these were quickly resolved. :D

After reading a bit online I managed to get transparent panels and now I'm really impressed with how nice the desktop looks.

In short, I think Ubuntu is a great Linux distro which is a nice combination of simplicity and customizability.
