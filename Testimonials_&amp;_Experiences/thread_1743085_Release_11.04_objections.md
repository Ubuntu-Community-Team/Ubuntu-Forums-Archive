### Post by beew on 2011-04-29
1, 4 and 5 are "features". That means they are the way it is by design rather than oversight. 

1 is this horrible idea of the global menu, apparently Mark Shuttleworth thinks it is really cool (he probably has a Mac) and decides that you should be forced to use it.  You can remove it and restore the menu to the application windows by uninstalling indicator-appmenu, but that may have some side effects on maximized windows, though so far I have not encounter anything serious that makes me want to reinstall the global menu.

Don't know about 2 and 4.
