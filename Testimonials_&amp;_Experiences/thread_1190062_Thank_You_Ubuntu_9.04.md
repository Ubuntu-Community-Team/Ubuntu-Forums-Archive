### Post by yerdon on 2009-06-17
Thank you Ubuntu 9.04.

I've been with you since version 6, and love most of my experience.  The 3-D desktop is awesome.  But I have always had stability issues with sound and video.  It seems no matter what I tried, my audio or video would always crash every couple of days, forcing me to reboot.

Now I've been using 9.04 since it came out and I have never once had to reboot.  I use Pandora, YouTube, along with Amarok and VLC and NEVER have to reboot.  Every video runs, every song plays. Every time.  Thank you.

Ubuntu, you are ready for prime-time.
