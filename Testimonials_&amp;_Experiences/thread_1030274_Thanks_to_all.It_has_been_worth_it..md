Dear happy Ubuntu folk
In the early summer of 2008 i decided to give Ubuntu ago.
Not being particularly p.c minded i picked up a copy of Linux format one of the special packs with a hardy disk.
I had to learn a lot but kind of got the hang and finally reformatted my hard drive and have been completely windows free for months now and will not be going back.
I did a fresh upgrade to ibex which has had its problems and i am sure it would have been OK if i just updated as Hardy in the end was running oh so smooth.
I would like to say all on here have been fantastic and a big thank you to compiz guru Forlong.
The support From the members and Forum team has helped so much.
Not just when a few and only a few things went wrong but also to read posts to help myself improve in computer skills and understanding how to use Ubuntu correctly.
My 4 girls age 5 to 13 love using Ubuntu as well and also have learnt many new skills needed. 
If i can with your help make things work then any one can.
THANKS SO MUCH.
If any one is struggling persevere don't give up its part of the fun. 
Keep up the great work.
