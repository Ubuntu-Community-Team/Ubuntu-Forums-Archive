anyway sorry such a long story its just that i feel like ive broken free of the chains of microsoft, even though I didnt realize it was so bad until recently.

I just wanted to thank the developers of ubuntu for making a great product, (I know they probably won't see this, but oh well)  hopefully I will learn enough to become helpful in bringing more people to try linux and decide for themselves.
