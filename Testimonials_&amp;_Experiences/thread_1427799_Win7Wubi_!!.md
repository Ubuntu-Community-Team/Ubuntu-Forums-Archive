### Post by derekeverett on 2010-03-12
So I broke down and went and bought myself a copy of Windows 7 Ultimate. And I gotta say folks... I'm loving it! I never thought I would say that about an MS OS again but there you have it. It's using half the system resources that Vista was and I am absolutely over the moon about the new taskbar. Some say it's not worth the $300 and change but I am in front of the puter 12+ hours everyday and I appreciate the improvements.

That being said, I decided to try the Wubi installer this time around for Ubuntu. And again I find myself impressed. Apparently a 30gb partition is as big as one can go using Wubi but as I have all my files backed up onto an external drive that's not a big deal.

I don't understand why or how but in the last couple years of dual booting with more traditional methods I have never had Ubuntu work so well. Don't ask me how but I am finally getting smooth playback out of VLC and Youtube, something I have struggled with in Ubuntu in the past. 

Bottom line, I have never been so happy with my computer configuration and wanted to say something. Two years ago I was a die hard Mac OSX guy, my how that's changed now!
