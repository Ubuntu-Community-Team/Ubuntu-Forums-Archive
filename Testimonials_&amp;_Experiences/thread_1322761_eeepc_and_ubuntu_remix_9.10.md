### Post by Oliv' on 2009-11-11
Hello,

I bought a eeepc 1008ha last week. Downloaded the iso for ubuntu 9.10 remix, wiped out the useless Windows and installed Ubuntu.
Conclusion : not a single problem ! Perfect. Well done Ubuntu guys !!!

Some comments :
- I really like the remix interface. Very simple and well thought.

- Ubuntu One is very interesting. As &#304;'m currently traveling I can have some files on my computer and have them synchronized autmatically when I f&#305;nd an &#305;nternet connect&#305;on. For now I'm more than happy with the concept !
 
- I hate the new 'ubuntu software center'. What's the point of it? very slow, hard to do things in parallel, couldn't find a way to select more than one soft to install or remove at the same time and not intuitive at all. I installed the good old 'add/remove' which is just perfect.

- why so many games ?

- wonder where your disk space went ? have a look at the great 'Graphical disk map'.

- I tried to play with some different desktop : kde and xfce. After some time decided to uninstall KDE... what a mess ! if it's easy to install, to remove it is just a pain in the... Something need to be done about it !! Then I was stuck with the xubuntu log in picture. I tr&#305;ed the usplash tricks to get ride of it and have back my ubuntu-gnome login page. I still don't understand but couldn't get ride of the xubuntu picture. I started to be quite angry about it and just got ride of xubuntu (thanks to [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/puregnome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/puregnome), furthermore I had still a lot of package from my kde experience that were cleaned up). 

- does someone know where &#304; can find explanations about the battery statistics ? very n&#305;ce to look at, but I don't understand what it means ;-)

Take care,
Oliv'
