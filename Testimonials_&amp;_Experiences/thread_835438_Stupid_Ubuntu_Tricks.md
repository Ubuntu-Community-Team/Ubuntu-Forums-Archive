### Post by Geekkit on 2008-06-20
The name of the post may sound derogatory but I was thinking of David Letterman's "stupid pet tricks" so this is really about funny little things that can be done that you wouldn't expect to work. I'll start ...

Bought a Parallel-to-USB adapter. Forgot to remove the printer settings in Gutsy. When I plugged the printer (HP Laserjet 6P) back in using the USB cable instead, Gutsy did this:

1.) Informed me that the (Parallel) printer was not available anymore
2.) Informed me that a new printer device was available and that I should identify what it was (via the wizard)
3.) Asked me if the old settings from the parallel printer should be copied over to the new USB printer since it (Gutsy) seemed to think they were similar *wink* *wink*
4.) Installed the printer driver and correctly printed a test page

Voila!
