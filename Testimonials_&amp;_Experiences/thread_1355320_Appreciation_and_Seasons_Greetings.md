### Post by Janeleaper on 2009-12-14
I've been using Ubuntu for about 16 months now, and I just want to offer my appreciation of Ubuntu, and this forum.

I'm a noob, so much a noob in fact that I didn't know what noob meant.  I switched to Ubuntu because I'd had a lot of problems with Windows XP and with getting technical advice from Dell.  Sixteen months on our household has 3 computers running Ubuntu, that is 2 desktops and a Dell mini 10 v.  I've just installed webcams and Skype on all 3.

I've had loads of help from these forums, and you have all been very patient with me when I ask stupid questions, or things I should have worked out for myself.  I don't think I've spent any more time on the forum than I would have spent trying to get sense out of a Dell technician if I was still using Windows, but I've had much more helpful advice.

I'm very happy with Ubuntu.  I prefer the interface to Windows.  Ubuntu is quicker than Windows and it is great not having to use anti-virus software.  Open Office could do with improvement, but it is good enough. It does everything I need a computer to do.

Seasons Greetings to you all and thank-you.
