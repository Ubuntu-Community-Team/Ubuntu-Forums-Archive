### Post by sledd on 2009-09-29
Just installed 9.04-AMD64 on the laptop, and it is running good! A lot better than Vista(What a Dog). I'm not an expert, and had trouble with Wifi so I switched to WICD. Running the usual commands in terminal to verify Driver and config. Had to start Wlan0, and configure WICD. I tried different settings and eventually used the Password Hex setting for WEP. Everything else worked ok,sound, X, Advanced Graphics, Compiz-Fusion. I had to configure MP3, and DVD settings to watch movies and I am happy! I am slowly learning Security for Ubuntu and Apt-get and Love to play!

Everything came together by searching the net, and using Google to find all of the answers I needed. I have some experience w/Linux, Slackware for 4 years and , Ubuntu for 3 yrs, off and on w/spare computers. This forum is a great resource if people would take the time to search thought it carefully. The How-To's are great, and so are the users!

Thanks for everything, I am happy and proud to be running Ubuntu on my Laptop, and plan to do so from now on.
