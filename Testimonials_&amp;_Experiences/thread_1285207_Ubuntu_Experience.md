### Post by alan2796 on 2009-10-07
Started using Ubuntu about a year ago had a few problems on the way but now everything works fine and I  wont be going back to MS Windows.

admittedly have had a few problems on the way but with some perseverance and a look around you alway find a answer somewhere. mainly these forums. The only issue i found was that there is so much information around I found myself going over stuff that was now obsolete because the things  work out of the box, or a easier solution was available.
such as getting my iPOD to work or my 3G Mobile Broadband Modem and had a few issues with installing on a HP Laptops  The Wireless was not working but plugged it into the router and install all the updates and activate the restricted drivers and then everything worked fine.

Very Happy and would recommend it to others.
