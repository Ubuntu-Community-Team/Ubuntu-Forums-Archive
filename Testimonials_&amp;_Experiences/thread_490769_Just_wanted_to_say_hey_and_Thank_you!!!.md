### Post by dolphoneman on 2007-07-02
I have been using Fedora for quite a few years as a server platform. I have been trying time and again to get Fedora to run on my laptop using my wireless card. My laptop is about three years old and uses an Atheos wireless card. Well Fedora 7 came out, I decided to give it another try. No dice. 

I have heard about Ubuntu but never really got into it. My wife's friend's Husband had given her a CD of Ubuntu about a month ago. After failing with Fedora 7, I figured "what the heck?" and gave Ubuntu a try. After a little tweaking with the settings, bang:popcorn:, I was on the internet. Now the one thing holding me back from going full blown without Windows on my laptop is a thing of the past.

Thank you to all the folks that have made this possible!!!:p
