### Post by haqking on 2012-05-06
Hi Guys,

I just wanted to let you all know that though I am remaining a member of the Ubuntu Forum Community and retaining my UF membership (unless removed of course) I wont be as active as a poster any longer compared to how I have been previously.

I have found that lately I am posting less and less and it is to do with many things, from the current majority of material in the forums along with the influx of people not really suited to Linux *coughs and tries to remain polite* IMHO

I have less and less, well to be honest zero interest actually in Canonical/*buntu at all or any of its projects.  I last used Ubuntu as a installed day to day OS with 10.10 and since then only the current versions in VM's and they hold no interest to me anymore.

I would say I will withdraw my UFM but to be honest it doesnt matter to me either way so I may aswell retain it as i I'll continue to provide support on the forums here and there but probably mainly for generic *nix type stuff and staying away from the posters who cant be bothered to read existing posts or stickies or who need spoon feeding for everything, which seems to account for the majority on here lately.

I have met some great people here and i enjoy the social IRC channel, but i have removed my Ubuntu affiliated cloak from it and will only really be there for social chit chat and generic *nix discussion where i find interest or see suitable.

I was going to start helping out a little more with community projects but they just dont hold any interest to me anymore and I have other personal projects i should be spending my time on, though i take my hats off to the guys and gals out there making the Ubuntu community projects work and dedicating there time to them such as the wiki team.  I even recently did a community interview but have now withdrew it, and it was really when answering those questions that i discovered I had no interest in Canonical/*buntu anymore.

I have also removed myself from the various mailing lists and launchpad teams.

I wont say good bye cos im not really going anywhere and will be logged into the IRC social channel daily as usual and check on here from time to time but certainly less support active.

Peace to all you guys..
