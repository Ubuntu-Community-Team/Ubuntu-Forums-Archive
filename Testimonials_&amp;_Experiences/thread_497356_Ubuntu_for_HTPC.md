The only problem I have atm is with my dtv tuner card but I;m sure that is just a matter of time. I'm not confident enough though to completely ditch XP on my other PC with an x1950. Continue to develop this OS and continue to refine the How to's in the beginner forum. It's really appreciated and a real help
