### Post by High Roller on 2007-06-01
I never thought I'd see the day.  I have people at work asking me for Ubuntu to put on their computers at home and they love it.

They're still keeping their Windows install to play games but find themselves using Ubuntu to do work and surf the web as there's far less risk of getting nailed by a virus.

I installed Ubuntu on their machines and they're happy.  It wasn't easy to install on either of their machines with drivers etc.  But I feel they may have given up if they were to attempt an install on their own.  Given that, I'm glad to see that Ubuntu is being delivered on the new Dell systems.

Thanks Ubuntu community!
