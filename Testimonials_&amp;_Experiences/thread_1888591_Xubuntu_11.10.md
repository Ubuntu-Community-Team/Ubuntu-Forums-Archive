### Post by tehcavil on 2011-11-29
I just upgraded my laptop from Mint 11 -> Xubuntu 11.10. I tried xubuntu last release (or was it a few releases ago?) and wasn't very impressed, but 11.10 is pretty good.

Had a few minor hiccups (had to do some terminal hacking to get chrome installed... missing dependencies? the .deb for it doesn't work on the latest xubuntu without some minor tweaks). Also had to replace the xfce volume daemon with a gnome-settings-daemon to get the laptop volume buttons to work correctly (thanks for helping me, Lewis of ubuntu forums!).

Other than that, though, Xubuntu has been an awesome system to work with. It looks a bit like gnome 2 and Mac OSX crossed, but much more lightweight (dunno how else to describe it). Xubuntu makes Xfce look really good. It's polished, fast, and doesn't get in the way like Unity seems to. I like it better than the new mint 12, and when I have the time will be switching my desktop to it as well.

keep up the good work canonical+xfce+xubuntu team.:guitar:
