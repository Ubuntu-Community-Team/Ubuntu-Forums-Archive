### Post by dannyboy79 on 2013-01-15
I have been very pleased running Ubuntu since Breezy Badger was released however many years ago. I have struggled through various driver issues with WIFI, Graphics Cards and other hardware compatibilty issues BUT have always stuck with it. I have filled bug reports to help out and try to contribute where I can.
I can honestly say I love Ubuntu for what it has become. I was able to contribute as a Technical Reviewer for Mark Sobell's book entitled, "A Practival Guide To Ubuntu Linux Third Edition". I have learned so much over the years and love trying to help others who are converting over from the "other" OS's out there. 
I am thrilled to see more manufactures like HP, Dell, System76 and Lenovo provide hardware with Ubuntu pre-installed on them. This will be an exciting year for Ubuntu with the Mobile platform taking shape as well.
I am currently running Xubuntu 12.04 on my main workstation and am looking forward to upgrading my File/MythTv/Backup/Media server from a headless Ubuntu 10.04 to 12.04 soon as well.

THANK YOU TO UBUNTU and to the entire community for always being there to help when one needs it whether it be on the forums here, on IRC channels, various Ubuntu Blogs or where ever else I receive help from.

Sincerely,
Ubu the Tech Guru
