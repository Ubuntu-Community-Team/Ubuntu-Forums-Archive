### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2009-08-30
Today I reinstalled Ubuntu with the ext4 file system. I have to say that I am really impressed. Ext4 has made my computer read, write and copy files significantly faster. Programs load faster and I like it. My boot up time is around 4 seconds quicker. Also when copying files from an external drive to my internal hard drive I experienced a speed improvement from 15 MB/s to around 22 MB/s. The problems with the data loss that I have heard of have not occured but I will be upgradeing my Linux kernel from 2.6.28-15 to 2.6.30.5 just to make sure I am safe. This is definatly an improvement that everyone should at least try.
