### Post by Darrell Lawrence on 2008-09-25
Welcome to Linux and the Ubuntu distribution. My situation is such that I no longer need Windows so I run Ubuntu only. I'm pleased to hear that your experience with Ubuntu has been quite good.

HP printers really have great support for Linux, and they keep their drivers up to date to cover new printer models. I just bought an HP Deskjet all-in-one that works perfectly under Ubuntu. All I had to do was go on the net and download and install the latest drivers. Here is where you get them:

[http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html)

Over 1400 HP printers supported. Just a thought. You might consider selling your Kodak printer and buying an inexpensive HP. Then you could go strictly Ubuntu if you  wanted to.

Again, welcome and enjoy.

Darrell
