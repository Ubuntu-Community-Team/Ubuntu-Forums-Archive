### Post by Zubatron on 2008-08-12
Even though none of you know who I am, I just wanted to announce how happy I am to be back in the Linux world. Here is what happened, a small gist:

I had Linux running on here perfectly but my fiancee hated it. Mainly because pogo.com wouldn't work and she hated Open Office and hated using Wine for Microsoft Office. She has to write a lot of papers for college. So she nagged and nagged and nagged until I gave in to her and tried to dual boot windows and ubuntu. After many usuccessful tries I gave up and just completely installed Windows.

That was 2 months ago. Before that I had Linux maybe 2-3 months and I was loving it. Being a Windows person all my life I had never felt more free with an OS than I did with Ubuntu. I learned so much with Ubuntu in those 2-3 months than I ever did with Windows.

So tonight after being completely fed up with Windows general stupidity. (It was XP btw) I put in my 6.06 disc and resized my windows paritition, but my 8.04 disc in and installed Ubuntu and voila! I'm back! :D I feel so free again and happy with my computer!
