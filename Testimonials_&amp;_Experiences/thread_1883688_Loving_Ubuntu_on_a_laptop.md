### Post by Mazate on 2011-11-19
I just installed Ubuntu on my laptop as a dual boot.  Ironically, it seems to work better with my laptop than windows.  The unity desktop seems to be much better in a laptop environment than a desktop environment, IMHO.  The one major difference is that when I close my laptop, it goes to sleep as it should and comes back on when I open it.  However, when windows is loaded I close the laptop and windows keeps right on running until the battery dies.  It doesn't suspend, sleep, or otherwise shut down.

Unfortunately, my laptop doesn't like the Gnome 3 desktop because when I installed it, the menus across the top were all garbled.  Not sure why that is since Unity is built on Gnome 3.  In any case, I wanted to share a positive experience since it has become popular here to announce to the world when you're leaving Ubuntu.
