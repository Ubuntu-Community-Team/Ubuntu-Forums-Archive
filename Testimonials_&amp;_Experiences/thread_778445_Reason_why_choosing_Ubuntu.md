### Post by tommyhakinen on 2008-05-02
I started using Ubuntu two months ago. After reading a review on a magazine, I get excited and curious of trying it. My first impression was WOW!!! I personally love Ubuntu. People might have different opinions. Here are main reasons why I love it.
1. It's free, safe, easy to use, stable, easy updates and detects my hardware well.
2. It has a very reliable forum to exchange knowledge and always ready to help whenever I got a problem
3. It's customized-able as whatever I want it to look like
