### Post by Rinzwind on 2008-04-12
works like a charm. Screen, sound, hibernate/ suspend, wireless, bluetooth, builtin webcam, mutimedia keys all work out of the box.

Compiz works perfectly. Gutsy sometimes had trouble with that.
But I now have an nVidia card where I only had a notebook with an ATI card before this one.

Dvd playback works with the standard package update.
BluRay mounting works after installing udf 2.5 (just needed to do this:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=718744](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=718744) )

BluRay ripping with aacskeys and DumpHD.
This took some thinking but I had it working within 30 minutes O+ 
with aacskey active aswell O+

I like the new add to panel listing. Nicely arranged :)
And there are many smaller changes that make me go 'hmm cool!' :) 
Excellent OS and a THANK YOU YOU ROCK to anyone involved :)

Oh and it's not perfect but I only have small annoyances. To name 2.

Auto-login does not seem to work perfectly. My gnome session stalls after every restart with the screen asking for my password for nm-applet. Any input is impossible; neither can I activate any icon or the cancel button. Dunno how to taclte this but it's not a showstopper. (only cntrl-alt-tab works and relogging in after that works too).I think nm-applet gets to ask for a password before gdm is ready or something like that.

Fast user switching: my own name is not clickabe. Why? When I login info/from failsafe mode it would be obvious to login back into your own account.

I still have some tinkering to do but Hardy Heron is my OS-choice allready.And that's a first: up to now I had to wait until a couple of weeks after official release before it would an OS that I could use.
