### Post by khightower on 2006-04-25
I know this is not as exciting as getting XGL to work, or even setting up XEN to run every version of Ubuntu, but today I really decided to support Ubuntu and Open Source Software. I wanted to do more than what I have been doing, which was telling all my friends and customers to switch to Linux, especially Ubuntu.

Thanks to paypal I was able to donate to my favorite Open Source Projects:

Ubuntu (Canonical Limited) -$10.00USD
GNOME Foundation (GIMP etc)- $25.00 Associate level donation
OpenOffice.org -$6.36 USD = 5.00 EUR

Now I can recommend people donate as well as enjoy free software.


I have only been a full time Linux user for a month, I am studying for my LPIC (Linux Professional Institute Certification) and Ubuntu exams. While learning I see the benefits of the whole open source idea.

I plan to switch my IT services to focus more on Linux and Open Source.

I like the fact that Ubuntu, Open Office, The GIMP, and GNOME are free, so I would like to help keep it that way.

I just want to say thanks to all the people in the forums and the whole Ubuntu Team.
