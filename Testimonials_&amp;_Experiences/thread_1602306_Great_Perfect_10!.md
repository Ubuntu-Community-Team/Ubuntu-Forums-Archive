### Post by marbertone on 2010-10-21
Hi!
I'm a Ubuntu-addicted-lover. I passed from Karmic to Maverick with a fresh install and I noticed a lot of good things despite of only one "bad" thing:

The *Good* ones:

* driver compatibility is AMAZING! I got webcam and internet-key configured without adding anything
* speed: definitely faster than Karmic on logout (8 seconds), almost equal on start (30 seconds)
* graphical look: very fresh!

The *bad* ones:
* my touchpad still goes upside-down sometimes
* shame on gcalctool! The new one does NOT read scientific notation like 1.4385728758E-002 . Very bad note for a physicist like me -- I had to install SpeedCrunch (what a name...! :) )

Anyway... very good OS, since I left Vista (Jan 10) my informatic life improved a lot!

Have a nice day!
M.A.
