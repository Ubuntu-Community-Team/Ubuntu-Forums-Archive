### Post by solwic on 2009-03-04
Been using Ubuntu for about nine months now (steadily, that is), and after years of Windows was very pleased with the performance.  Ubuntu is certainly faster than Windows XP.

Tinkering today, I found XFCE (well, the xubuntu-desktop package in the repos).  I installed it beside Gnome, logged out and switched the DE, and only have one thing to say:

Holy ^&%$#^%! this thing is fast!

Plus, it integrates so well with Gnome.  All of my Gnome themes, that I had installed under regular Ubuntu, are available in XFCE.  All my settings (browser, e-mail, even OOo3) carried over.  

Did I mention it was fast?

Barring major issues, I don't see how XFCE didn't just replace Gnome as my DE of choice.  

And c'mon...the little mouse logo is just awesome.  :)

EDIT:  And did I mention it was fast?  :biggrin:
