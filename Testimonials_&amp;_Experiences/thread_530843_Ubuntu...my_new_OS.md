### Post by DownTown22 on 2007-08-20
I stumbled into Ubuntu a while back when I was looking for an alternative to windows, ordered some CD's, installed it, and hated it.

Now that a year and some has passed, I decided to give it another go, and all I can say is wow!  My previous experience had a lot to do with me being stuck in the MS-mind sent, and as such, I did very little reading on how to get things working, etc.  Now that I've done lots of reading I decided to give it another go and things went much smoother this time.

Talk about things "just working"....and fast!  Things never ran this fast under XP - and my XP installs have always been free of spyware, viruses, etc with almost zero problems.  Things just never seemed fast enough - I mean, with all this new hardware these days, my computer should have been lightening fast.  Not until I recently installed Ubuntu on my fiance's laptop, which had lower specs than my desktop, did I realize just wow amazing Ubuntu actually is!

When something is this good, it's really hard to describe it's "awesomeness"!

Also, this forum has been amazing....most of my questions are answered without me ever asking a question!

Thanks a million Ubuntu!
-from an Ubuntu lifer
