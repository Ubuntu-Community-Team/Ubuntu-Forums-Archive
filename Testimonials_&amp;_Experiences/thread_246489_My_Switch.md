I realized that it was time for a change. So I got rid of windows all together and installed Ubuntu. It took about three hours to download and burn and set up. I changed the theme to a nice bussiness blue the first hour I had it. I love Ubuntu it just always seems to work. Everything would be perfect if I could a get a hang of downlading and installing, but im doing okay with the pre-installed apps. So far im pretty happy with my linux box and i have my windows box next to it to fill in the gaps.
