### Post by grunge09 on 2011-01-03
I have this friend and her son, I gave them an old computer.  

Amd x2 3200+
19" LCD
512 MB RAM
160 GB HD
CDRW
1.44 floppy
Geforce FX 5200 AGP Video Card

I wiped it clean and installed XP on it and they I was having to go over to their house once a week and fix something, or listen how it was slow.  About 2 weeks in the son got one of those fake anti virus software pop ups and clicked on it and it really messed up the system.  I removed it (it would pop up that you were under mega attack on all fronts and demand you spend 40 bucks for their software to fix it).

3 weeks later He got a real nasty virus, wouldn't let anything run, blue screens.

So I was pissed off at this point.  I grabbed a 10.04 Ubuntu i386 CD and went over and again wiped the system and installed Ubuntu 10.04.  Set up their e-mails, flash, favorites they wanted on web browser firefox.

They could not be happier.  They rave how fast the system is now, no more viruses, No more waiting for the IE to load a page, by the time they finish clicking on FF browser its up and instantly opens the page.  

They love the FREE Open Office, the games, NO VIRUSES.  

I have not had to go over to fix their computer since.  Thanks Ubuntu.
