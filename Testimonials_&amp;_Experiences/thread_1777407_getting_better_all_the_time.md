To be real, you can take *any* DE and tweak it, and make it your own. It's just a matter of learning how. That's the beauty of linux. There are people out there that have taken G3 and made it *very* G2 like. So people should realize that g2 (of sorts) is still available as an option.

I think people are kind of blowing these changes out of proportion. The changes that are taking place right now are laying the foundation for DE's for years to come. And then there will be *more* changes.......

But I love G3, it fits my personality. It's cool if other people don't though. I still contend that THIS time period is a *very* exciting time if you are a linux lover. There are *so* many choices it makes my head spin. But isn't that what most of us wanted to begin with? 

I can't believe how many distros there are now too. Every time you blink there's another distro. But I have to admit I love trying out a lot of stuff. I'm not married to any linux, but rather just use what works best at the moment. If you can't adapt, oh well then perhaps linux isn't for you. And that's OK. No *one* OS can please *everyone*.
