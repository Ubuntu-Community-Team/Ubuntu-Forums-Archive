### Post by kg4cna on 2010-03-12
A radio station I work part-time for got it's first Ubuntu machine today!

This computer was the problem child of the entire station...always getting crippled by virus and malware/spyware invasions. Despite cleaning it almost every week...it just wasn't working out. It sits in the front office and handles email, web and copy writing duties (mainly).

Talked with the GM and Engineer and told them I recommended an Ubuntu install. They said to go for it.  So I did and it's done.  Runs much better and everything works great. Even the Lexmark T640 laser printer across the network :) Everyone's happy now!

I hope to be able to convert at least one more office machine over, possibly two. Ubuntu rocks and now this radio station does too ;)


Thanks for letting me share!
