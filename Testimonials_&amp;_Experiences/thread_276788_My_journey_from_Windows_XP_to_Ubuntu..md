### Post by Woobles on 2006-10-13
Hello fellow Ubuntu users.

First time poster here, so I'll introduce myself to you all. My name is *** (yes a *** Ubuntu user ;) ), I'm 20 years old, and I'm a student at University studying BSc Information Technology. I was referred to this forum by *** (on these forums.. and he's my friend :lol: ).

Okay, so on with my er, post. Okay, I started out using SuSE Linux 10.1, I liked it at first, well in fact, I loved it. But then it started going wrong, everything got corrupted, and wouldn't let me update. So after several attempts of reinstalling it - I gave up. So I went back to Windows XP Media Centre Edition.

About 2 weeks of using XP, I got fed up - I hate it. So, while visiting Scott, he showed me Ubuntu, and showed me how to install it as he was reinstalling it himself.

So, last night, I got my mate to download it for me as my Internet Connection as University is ridiculously slow (and I mean SLOW), and I've had Ubuntu installed for less than 30 minutes - and I LOVE it!

It actually WORKS. No errors when updating unlike SuSE. I really do like it. I'm just getting used to GNOME as I used KDE in SuSE. Overall, I love it.

So, that's my introduction, and I'm glad to be part of the Ubuntu community :D
