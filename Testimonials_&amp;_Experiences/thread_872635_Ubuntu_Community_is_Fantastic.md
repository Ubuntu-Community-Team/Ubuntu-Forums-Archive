### Post by Lateforgym on 2008-07-28
While I have had some difficulty with Linux, coming from a simple "one click" world the support here, when you articulate the problem in the proper form, is one of the best I have used over the years. 

Generally with my hobbies, I have belonged to groups who are local to my time zone which means no top-level support for late night emergencies and sometimes you have to wait a day for a response. 

Its very handy to have people in other time zones answer questions in minutes. Particularly when its at 3AM. :) 

Thank you for responses to my questions.
