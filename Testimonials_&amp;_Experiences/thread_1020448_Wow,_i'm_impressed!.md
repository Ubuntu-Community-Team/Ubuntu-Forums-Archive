### Post by farmerlee on 2008-12-24
Recently got me an asus EEE 1000H. I wiped the pre-installed windows xp and installed intrepid. Like everyone else i had a few issues but thanks to the great support i have it working now and everything works great.
The real wow factor came when i got myself the new nokia n96 smartphone yesterday. One of the reasons i got it was so i could use it to access the web from my eee while travelling. Seeing as linux isn't really supported i was expecting a few problems however i simply plugged it in, clicked on the pop up, selected my network and away i went surfing the web. This is absolutely fantastic and i just want to give a big thanks to the all the people who put in the hard work making such a fantastic distro. You guys (and girls) are awesome.
