### Post by runim on 2006-08-03
I registered on these forums just to come and say this: I LOVE ubuntu =)  

I must have tried at least 20 different distros and I like this one the best.  I have been using windows since 3.11, and I was curious and wanted to learn linux.  I bought a laptop to mess around with and spent a long time working through distros.  I eventually came back to Ubuntu.  It just has a different, polished feel to it that windows doesn't really.  I love how everything is free and the community here rocks!!

At first, I was disheartened by the lack of wireless support but all it takes is a little research and it "just works" =)  As much as I love Ubuntu though, I am a hardcore gamer and my desktop will follow the windows suites, simply because games are more compatible there.  I just downloaded Cedega on this machine but I haven't tried it yet, so we'll see.  I'm sure the native linux stuff works well too.

Anyhow, this went on longer than I wanted it to... for those who TLDR, basically the devs, community, and ubuntu ROCK!!
