### Post by the badger on 2008-09-19
I've been running ubuntu (from Dapper to Hardy) on my (first and only) desktop PC for the past year and a half. There have been glitches and problems to fix, but all said it's been pretty great. Today I bought a second hand laptop, and of course it came with winXP installed. now, there are certain programs i need which i can't run in ubuntu (natively) and so i thought this  would be fine with me: have winXP on the laptop, keep hacking away at ubuntu on the desktop. but wow, after only a few hours i really miss ubuntu. i've used windows plenty, at college and at jobs and old housemates' systems, and i always thought it was kind of okay and my main reason for using linux was the lesser system requirements and like of the whole philosophy of FLOSS. i actually really dislike windows, it makes working pretty uncomfortable, and i'll be damned if i don't set up a dual-boot sometime soon on this new laptop.
so, i just wanted to say 'thanks' to all the programmers and developers and supporters who are making this project happen. it's pretty rad!
