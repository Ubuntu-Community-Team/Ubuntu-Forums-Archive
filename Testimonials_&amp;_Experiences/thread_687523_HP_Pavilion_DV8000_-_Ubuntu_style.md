### Post by desertboy on 2008-02-04
Just one more reason I love Ubuntu and boot it on anything I can.

My mother has a HP dv8000 laptop, it's a pretty smart piece of kit I have to say as well, just a crap video chipset so gamin's a no no. Not that she cares but after owning it a year, she gave me the laptop proclaiming it to be broken. I found windows had many registry errors on boot up, spyware and probably viruses. HP had kindly partitioned the HD in 2 at source and my mother had like 4 gig of data so I installed Gutsy on the 2nd partition.

I have to say I was very impressed with install, I had to plug the laptop into the router to get internet so it could download the restricted drivers, ticked the box. Within 5 mintues everything works great, except compiz which is very flaky (But system stable as a rock without it). Set it up with her wireless (Relatively  painless but windows is easier) and gave it her back, that was a month ago.

I went to see her the other day and fixed her original XP partition (IE reformatted and reinstalled, including grub.) she told me I shouldn't have bother she preferred that bunting thingy better.

If you can sell it my mum reckon you can sell it anyone.
