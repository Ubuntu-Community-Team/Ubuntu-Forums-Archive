### Post by Ivor Grumble on 2011-06-24
Here's my story :)

I have been using pc's and the internet for 15+ years, they are my favourite toys. Never used Linux and the word always scared me a little. Always used Windows because everything was just 'done' for you. Anyhow, I thought ME and XP were fine systems, never had much problems with either. Those computers got old and I was given a computer by a friend, it had Vista on it. Thus my relationship with Bill Gates soured to the point where I began wandering the streets shouting obscenities at stationary objects. (I didn't really do that but you get the point)

A few days ago I made up my mind to banish Windows from my life and checked out some open source ops. I had heard of Ubuntu over the years so I took the plunge. I thought if it mucks up my computer I don't even care anymore I can't stand Vista that much, and I didn't want to pay for another op system from those greedy sods. I did burn the iso and did all that but it didn't happen for me so I used your online installer thing, and boom! I am totally gushed with Ubuntu. All the aps I used on Windows can be either found or replicated with Ubuntu and it's so fast.

I just wanted to say thank you so very much to all the developers and people who help do this Ubuntu thing. :D
