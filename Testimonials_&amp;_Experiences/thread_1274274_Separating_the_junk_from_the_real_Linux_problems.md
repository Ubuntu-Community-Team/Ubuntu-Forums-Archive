### Post by fela on 2009-09-24
For all the people that are having problems with Linux, please post them here. If you come across this thread and notice a problem with a solution, please post it. This way we can separate the junk that people spill across the internet from the actual issues that Linux has that need to be sorted out.

I'll start with my main fixable problem with Linux: not enough cooperation between developers.

I'm talking about within the confines of a certain distro here. A huge reason for alot of the problems people have with Linux is compatibility issues and this is often because there are so many protocols/standards that have been developed by different people separately. If they came together and all worked on the same thing, it would get alot better alot faster, plus you wouldn't have all the confusion of, for example, having dozens of different audio drivers (such as OSS4, OSS3, Alsa, Pulseaudio, Esound, Jack, and the rest).

PS. post your problems no matter how trivial they are.
