### Post by Flyingjester on 2007-10-05
I LOVE this OS, i accidentally crashed my xp laptop about a week ago, and since then needed an operating system desperately. I am a student, and currently not employed so i'm strapped for cash. I remembered Linux being out there and have never given it a shot, hell didn't even know it came with a GUI. Well, on first shot tried out debian, liked it but was looking for something more, found fedora, really liked it. Then i saw more and more people in the community talking about ubuntu, decided to try it out. I don't think i will ever have anything else on this laptop, or on any computer i build ever again. Thank you to all the developers, programmers, and community members for making this an absolutely incredible operating system. :)
