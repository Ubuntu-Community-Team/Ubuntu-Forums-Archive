### Post by gosh on 2005-12-06
Hi Everyone,

I just want to say I love this distro.

I had an 7 year old Dell Inspiron 7000 which runned on Windows 95. I upgraded it to Windows ME and that worked reasonably fine for a period. Because it was to slow to run XP I left it with ME.
But it kept having problems with my wireless LAN. Sometimes it worked, most of the time it didn't. Murphy's Law in action;) .

It eventually ended up in the cupboard, collecting dust.
Then I ran into a Ubuntu-cd at our local library and thought I would give it a try.
And.....it works brilliantly!!!
Even my kids (12 years and 8 years) are more interested in my notebook than they are in there own XP desktop.:cool: 

I fought my way through ndiswrapper, wpa_supplicant and other unknown territory, but eventually made it work. I then decided that I also should have Ubuntu at my (perfectly working) XP desktop. I now only use XP when I absolutely have to (for instance to run Partition Magic to convert my ntfs USB Harddisk to FAT32).

And then, about those old habits...
I actually graduaded as a MSc in Computer Science back in 1989. We did have Unix at our univ. but I started to work with a MS-Dos based software company.
After some years in business consulting, I've lost my interest in computers, expect for them to work problemless (is this English????).
Now I again I love to hack with my computers to make them perform as best as possible, of course using Ubuntu.

As I am a big supporter of openess, freedom and fairness, I love what you people at Ubuntu are doing.
I recently read Linus Torvalds' book (Just for fun) and again realised that this is the way forward.

So, compliments, hugs and kisses for you all.
I hope to contribute to this through this forum when I can.

Gosh
