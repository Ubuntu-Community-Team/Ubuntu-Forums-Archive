### Post by Thanh-BKK on 2008-10-02
Hello :)

A couple of days ago my work colleague surprised me by handing me a few CD's.... GENUINE Windows and MS Office!!

And of course, good software, free on top of it, must be put to good use.

So i fired up the computer and installed it. Did a few tweaks, installed all available updates and other software, and now have a GREAT Windows installation.

See this: Power "ON", full boot. Open Internet Explorer, let it load it's home page, close it. Open Firefox, let THAT load it's home page, and then close it. Open a picture editing program, then close it. Access a second computer on the LAN, open it's shared folder and look at the files, then close it. Then "shut down" and wait till the machine powers off.

How long would that take? 

ONE MINUTE THIRTY-THREE SECONDS. No joke!!

Oh, forgot to mention... the "computer" is a virtual machine in VMware server, under Ubuntu 8.04 Hardy. And the OS is - Windows 95! The Office version is "97". But it works heck beautifully! Starting up that baby and opening my beloved iPhoto Plus to do my picture tinkering (it has functions that Gimp does not have or that are too difficult to use for me) goes faster than putting the pics on a thumb drive to work them on my office machine. I gave Win95 3 GB fully allocated, and 256 MB of RAM. The thing FLIES.

I wish Microsoft would still make it's Windows like that...... 

Oh, and no worries - it's just a program running under my powerful Ubuntu which i will NEVER replace by any MS system again :) 

Best regards....

Thanh
