### Post by Ob1 on 2006-07-12
I installed Ubuntu on my Laptop 2 months ago, and i have been nothing but extremely happy with it, i deleted my Win partition shortly after the Ubuntu installation.

The features that i like the most:

* The general computer performance, it is so much faster compared to my windows XP experience. 

* The Terminal is great!, in Windows almost every action has to be performed with the mouse.

* Valuable resources, like the knowledge-bases(Wiki and the official one),Linux programs, functions and just general Linux computer usage is commonly well documented.

* Free software, I love the entire non-profit idea.

* The security, Now I feel complete freedom from risk and danger, the fact that no Linux user has been infected is a huge relief, and Linux security apps, are usually quite powerful and they are not even needed in most cases. I always had security problems with Windows.

* Knowledgeable users, pretty much every Linux user i encounter on web-pages are normally well experienced and knowledgeable.

* Support, The quality of free technical support on the Internet came as a huge shock to me.
