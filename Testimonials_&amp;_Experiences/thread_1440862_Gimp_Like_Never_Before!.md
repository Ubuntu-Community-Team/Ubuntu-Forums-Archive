### Post by thekanuk on 2010-03-28
I have only recently installed Ubuntu on my bots, everything is going wonderfully! Once I finally settled down and got back to work, "Gimp projects"... ...I realised, I had been working on a complex design for hours with no freeze ups. :shock:  I had forgotten to save my work every few layers like I used to in Windows. Thinking it may have been a fluke, I continued on with more large projects; no problems... I'll continue to save as I progress, but this is a real treat to work without the added stress of crashes! I am used to constant freeze ups using my old OS on the same machines. 

Ubuntu has breathed new life into my design projects. The fear of regular crashes seems to be gone, FINALLY! :D
