### Post by jso2897 on 2010-10-09
Two things: You can always find challenging things to do. I have found that getting mainstream games to play  on Linux platforms can be difficult and fun - and there are plenty of other things that push the envelope as well.

Also dispel the myth of mutual exclusion. Many people have multiple computers, and hard drives are big these days, and multi-booting is a snap.
