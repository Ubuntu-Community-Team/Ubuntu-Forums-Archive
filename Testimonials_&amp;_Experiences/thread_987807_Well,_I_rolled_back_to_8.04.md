### Post by goeagles520 on 2008-11-19
So after battling with 8.10 for a few weeks, I finally rolled back to 8.04. Among other things, the major problems I experienced on my Dell m1330 included:

1) Compiz being very choppy, lot's of tearing in minimization effects, and general lagginess in the effects. I have the Nvidia card, and while its no powerhouse, animations are once again silky smooth now that I'm in Hardy.

2) I had persistent sound problems in Intrepid. Particularly, if I controlled my system volume via PCM, I would get popping and crackling when I muted the system. When I attempted to workaround this by killing pulseaudio (and uninstalling it) it rendered my system inoperable...

3) I experienced more than a few system crashes with Intrepid (as compared to nearly none with Hardy).

Maybe I'll try again a few months down the road as bugs get patched and things improve. So, I ask...how many of you have faced/are currently facing problems with Intrepid...particularly things that worked well in Hardy? As I understand, things breaking in new versions of ubuntu that used to work in older versions is common. It's too bad though, because I was excited about intrepid and now I'm quite disappointed...long live Hardy...
