### Post by bradford72 on 2007-01-24
Okay, here's my Ubuntu testimonial.  I recently installed 6.06 Dapper Drake, and threw out windows for good!  Although I wasn't able to "just go" like magic (I had to chown my usb hdd, and change a file to get on the internet), most everything else worked like magic.  Most every problem I have had I've been able to resolve by coming here to the forums, and when I made a mistake, I was gently corrected (yes I said CORRECTED...nobody told me what a dummy I was and laughed, they showed me how to do it correctly....) I was also very happy to see that I could open my old MS Word files using Writer, and even happier to find that if I save a file with a .doc extension in writer that I can open it with Word (windows is gone from my house, but unfortunately not from my life!)  All in all, I would say that ubuntu is incredible, and I'm sorry I didn't switch to linux about 10 years ago!
