Enlightenment E17 is a Window Manager much like Fluxbox, Icewm, Window Maker.  Enlightenment E17 is entirerly its own working environment.  You can use Gnome GTK and KDE QT applications in E17.  

I would suggest installing E17 and giving it a try.  It is very easy thanks to the Elbuntu team.  Just follow my guide.  

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=319336]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=319336")
