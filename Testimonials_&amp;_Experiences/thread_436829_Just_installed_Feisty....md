### Post by LaRoza on 2007-05-08
...and it went great.

I have it dual booted with 6.06. I do not have an Internet connection at home, so out-of-the-box is good for me. I ordered the 6 DVD set with the repostitories and Live DVD.

With 6.06, I couldn't use my printer (HP Deskjet 380) with out downloading anything off the Internet, and I couldn't transfer it to my flash drive and bring it home. I also couldn't network with Vista, rather, I couldn't read the Vista file system.

With 7.04, my printer works perfectly, I can see the Vista folders, and Feisty runs just as well on less than 512 MB of RAM as Dapper.

I have a screen resolution issue, but I just got the solution by searching the forum, it should be fixed by tommorow (when I come back to school).

I hope Ubuntu doesn't get too easy to use.
