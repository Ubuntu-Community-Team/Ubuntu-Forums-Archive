### Post by hockeyhead019 on 2009-04-20
ok so I've been using Ubuntu for almost a year now and I figured I'd share my story because idk people seem to bash the OS quite a bit but it has helped so many of us but not a lot of us post haha...

so anyways around this time last year i had a teacher mentioned linux as the best os so i was like WTF is linux haha

so i googled my face off and finally ran into what i thought was the most user friendly version... Ubuntu... then i read and read and read and finally installed...

complete failure haha crashed my system and had to reinstall Windows and just try it again haha... second time was a charm in my case haha and i had it installed inside windows... but now the kernal had a problem because i have a Dell and no idea why but my hardware just didn't wanna let the OS work haha so then with the help of the forums i got the whole thing up and running.  

that was a mistake as i quickly ran out of the amount of room i had left for the os at which point i held my breath and with the help of the forums made a separate partition and installed the system.

now i have what i think is the best OS i've ever experienced with the best support i've ever even heard of... they've helped me through hardware changes and countless upgrades and other installs of apps

so thank you Ubuntu Forums and Ubuntu Dev's haha :D
