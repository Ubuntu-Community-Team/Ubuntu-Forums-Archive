### Post by JPMaximilian on 2006-11-27
I did a clean install of 6.10 today, and I must say that I like it.  I'd heard some bad things about 6.10, lots of bugs, but I haven't experienced any.  Here's what I think:

Good:
My system is noticeably snappier
Amarok 1.4 rocks
mp3 support for Amarok is easy to install
Firefox 2.0
Latest ALSA Drivers
"Just Works" WIRED networking

Bad:
Flash working is hit or miss, I can't play videos
I can't/don't know how to get wireless working
Can't customize my mouse without editing xorg.conf
I can't edit song tags in RythmnBox??

All in all I'm pleased, I like ubuntu so much more than windows (and its free!), I just miss videos (no synced videos in Dapper either)
Not having wireless hurts, I don't know how to use wpa_supplicant.

My thanks to all the devs and supporters.

### Post by PrinceArithon on 2006-11-28
For our newbie here is a place you will want to go to get your computer set up and running great with Edgy, and trust me, it will get rid of all your problems except for your wireless.

[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Edgy](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Edgy)

Now for your wireless.  In your system tray right click on your icon for your wireless and all that, and click properties.  Then change it from lo to eth1.  That is if you haven't done that yet.  If that doesn't work then I Don't know what to say.

Anyway welcome here to the world of Ubuntu.
