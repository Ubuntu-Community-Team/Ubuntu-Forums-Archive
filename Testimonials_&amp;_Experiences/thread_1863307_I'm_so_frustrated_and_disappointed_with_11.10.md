### Post by gruadp on 2011-10-17
I have to vent my frustration here:

After using Ubuntu for more than 6 years, Ubuntu 11.10 is just a nightmare to me. I wasted my hole day. First I tried to upgrade from 11.04 and then I reinstalled from the scratch and 

1) unity just does not fit my needs at all. Finding rarely used applications takes lot of times. Even accessing "open" applications takes much more time than the known task-list.  And its just slow and laggy on my not-so-old-machine.  Unity looks and feels like my android-mobile, but I run it on my working-desktop with two monitors where I want to work and not watch fancy effects while it takes me triple the time just to switch betweens applications like it took before. Thats a fact. And people like me using a lot of applications simultanously just get angry by this tiny slow autohide-launcher which is nothing compared to a task-list running over two screen.   I think people should have the choice if they want unity or not and its really makes my upset that I cannot choose anymore cause the classic desktop is removed. 

2) software-center was not able to install me openssh-server cause it didnt find it. I need to use apt-get install openssh-server.  A linux-distro not offering openssh-server ist just sad.

3) ubuntu offers me to install restricted drivers but completely fails doing so. It fails to install with some cryptic error-messages (googling them got me countless other people having the same problem). After a second try my package-repository got damaged and it took me all of my many years of ubuntu-experience to get it fixed. a simple "apt-get install -f" was not enough.  Finally I got my fglrx-drivers so broken that I had to reinstall.

4) didnt find anything to tune unity (like smaller icons on the launcher, stop autohide). I installed compiz-manager and tuned some settings there but no effect. And no explanation what the settings mean.

5) Ubuntu One crashed on first usage

This is all just a nightmare. I'm so disappointed. Ubuntu was so good and so cool back then (I use ubuntu since 5.04 back then in 2005) and it was so simple to work with. 

And now its just a bunch of not-working graphic-stuff that eats all my system-resources.  Its going the completely wrong way here.  I love this ubuntu-one if it would work seamless, but it doesnt.

I'm frustrated and I think Ubuntu 11.10 is the worst distro I've seen in years. Time to look for a different distro.

I really use ubuntu since 2005 and I helped to share it a lot. I ordered many CDs back then and distributed it in communities. And now I feel somehow betrayed cause it reminds me more to a game-cube than an efficient OS.

This means that even if they developed for a century, users would still find bugs on the release date.
