### Post by Vince4Amy on 2007-08-02
This isn't the first time I've used Kubuntu. I've had it on my laptop for ages, though that was just for the general stuff. 

I decided to put it onto my main Computer that used to have Windows XP. That I do everything on & play games on. Again like on the Laptop Kubuntu ran flawlessly. So I decided to Install WINE, Installed some of my games & I was amazed. They ran without any noticeable difference to running them on XP. By this time I was really impressed, these games I was playing aren't old either, so playing a recent game was amazing. 

I put all the users from my Windows partition onto the Kubuntu partition. They being not very tech minded noticed the changes, but they found it extremely easy to navigate through everything. Funny enough they didn't ask any questions, they found all the apps they were used to more or less straight away. Overall they are impressed by the way things run now & my 6 year old brother really likes the effects Beryl gives. 

The only thing that has been noticed. We need to use the Windows Version of Firefox under WINE For shockwave. I did contact Adobe through the ideas link on Contact us. (Any info of a Shockwave version coming soon?).

Anyway my thanks to the Ubuntu team on making a great OS.
