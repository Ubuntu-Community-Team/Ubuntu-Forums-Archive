### Post by murderslastcrow on 2009-12-16
I love that I can buy a brand new Canon All-in-One printer, have it automatically recognized, connect it as a wireless network printer, and never have to put in the installation CD with all the crapware on it.

It seems like most driver CDs try to put extra junk on your PC. So having such wide hardware support on a UNIX-based OS is just wonderful. I love this stuff, it's amazing how much work has gone into making everything work out of the box, and to be simple and clean.

I just love it- thanks Debian devs, thanks Canonical, thank you Linus, thank you Richard, thank you CUPS driver writers. XD You've all made my computing experience so easy. I can only wonder how much time this has saved me over the past two years.
