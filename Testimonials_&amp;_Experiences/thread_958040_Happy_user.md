### Post by cooperkevins on 2008-10-25
I had a old computer Emachines 5150 and the Power supply went out and also took out the motherboard and few other goodies.
Being on a tight budget i saved some money up and asked around.
I didn't want a used computer that i couldn't tell if would work or not.
So I asked my IT guy at work and he buys from ascendtech.us so i checked it out.
I walked through configuring a bare bones Dual core AMD 6400
and a cd burner and a cooling fan and case which they assemble for you.
I already had  DVD burner from a upgrade.They shipped within 2 weeks for 225.00

Being a huge Ubuntu fan i told them not to load any OS on it.
I loaded Ubuntu 8.04 on it and I'm a happy dude.The only thing id have
done different would be to add at least a Nvidia 8500gt for 
the better graphics and compiz.
 I want to thank everyone in the community here for opening up
and sharing their time and knowledge and putting together such a great OS.
