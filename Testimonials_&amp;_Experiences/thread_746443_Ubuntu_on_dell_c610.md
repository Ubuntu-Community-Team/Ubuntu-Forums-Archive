### Post by el_guazu on 2008-04-05
I have a dual boot on the laptop, XP & ubuntu, both works great.

In XP i had to install some drivers, to get the wireless to work, and other stuff...

But in Ubuntu, Gutsy, it detected the wireless automatically, Until now all works perfectly, i was afraid of all the "bad" testimonials 'bout this laptop, but it really give me no problems...

Just the bug where the splash screen does not appear, but modifying the /etc/usplash.conf to have xres=1024 and yres 768, update-initramfs -u -k 'uname -r' was solved...

I like to add that the OS is very good, and that i can eliminate some viruses, from windows systems, in ubuntu is fantastic.
