### Post by 1roxtar on 2009-12-03
I am a computer tech and over the last few months, I have accumulated 6 desktops, two laptops and one netbook.  Only two of these machines runs Windows XP (for now).  The rest run Ubuntu Jaunty and Karmic.  On the last desktop I acquired, I installed XP, but as I was scouring through the internet to find all of the needed drivers, I said to hell with it.  I popped in my Karmic LiveCD and installed Ubuntu instead.  

I love Ubuntu so freaking much, that my buddies are starting to call me "Ubuntu" and I take it as a compliment.  I pop in the cd, let it run it's course and voila! all my drivers are detected and installed.  No rummaging through the Interwebs to find them. I start doing the Happy Dance.  From there on, it's just the little tweaks to make it all my own.  I am so sick of MS and it's stinkin' Windows.  So far I have had nothing but good experiences with my installs.  Any little hiccups have been easily worked through.

With Ubuntu, I'm amazed cause it's FREE and it WORKS!!!  With Windows, I get sailor cussin' MAD cause I pay so much and deal with so many headaches on a daily basis.  The few times I've dealt with serious problems with Ubuntu have been because of my own curiosity  to tamper with things.  Nevertheless, because of the community, I have been back on my feet quickly.

Bottom line, I am done with MS.  Let them have their shining star (Seven).  It's not worth the money and the needless hassles.  I've tried other distros too, but I'm sticking with UBUNTU!!!!
