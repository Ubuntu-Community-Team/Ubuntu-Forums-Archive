### Post by Jacobonbuntu on 2011-06-09
I guess we are making circles in the reviews of Unity, and I am joining the carousel one more time :). Just to remark that it has always been the developers and the desktop-designers who are ahead of the public, and most of the successful break-troughs (in general) are not achieved by democratic decisions, nor are they recognized at first sight.  
 While I was skeptical in the beginning, I find Unity beautiful and extremely comfortable to work with now (after I made some modifications to the launcher), and I am using it intensively for complicated jobs. A good stress test! Just use your imagination to see the opportunities of this desktop environment for future growth!
 I am about to build a new system. The choice of the hardware will be fully determined by Linux compatibility, as I have complete faith in the future of this OS.
