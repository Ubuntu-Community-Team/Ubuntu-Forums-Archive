### Post by orb9220 on 2013-01-29
Yep left Ubuntu back in 2008 as no serious photo & video editing software.Except for Gimp which I hated.

Been using Windows 7 and Lightroom 3 for my photography work last 4 years. 

This winter slow on booked shoots so thought to give Linux a checkout.

And was surprised how it has matured in many areas. But still lacking in other's.

But to my surprise ground was moved forward with [darktable]("http://www.darktable.org/") & [Rawtherapee]("http://rawtherapee.com/blog/features") for Serious Image editing. And now soon to be released [Lightworks]("http://www.lwks.com/").

So plan is to stick around and see what Linux evolves too.
