### Post by iheartubuntu on 2009-01-07
Glad you are here! While I consider myself a newbie still at linux, ive been using Ubuntu for almost two years now and love it. These forums are really a valuable tool and allow you to be in control of your computer, not the other way around. And certainly not let Windows or Mac be in control of you either.

Ive installed Ubuntu on 20+ computers now. Ive hit a couple of glitches at the very beginning, probably because I wasnt burning a good copy of the Live-CD. I had ordered a free disc from Canonical and presto... Not a single problem since (with installing that is). Never really had a system crash on me, but the more you tinker and screw around, especially with the command line, the more you'll be prone to system screw ups. Trying installing Ubuntu on an older computer and tinker on that one, not your main one :)

Good luck!
