### Post by alindgr1 on 2010-02-05
So, I have been using Ubuntu for about a year now, and I love it.  My kids had a desktop computer in my sons room that has WinXP on it, and I was dual-booting it with Crunchbang (old computer).  This Christmas, we got our kids each an Acer Netbook that came with WinXP pre-installed.  I left it on there for them, figuring that they would prefer that.

Well, two nights ago, my son hands me his computer and says, "Dad, please put Ubuntu on this for me, I like it better".

Inside, I was jumping for joy, and I did as I was asked, still dual booting with windows.

Last night, my daughter saw that my son was running Ubuntu, and thought it was unfair that he got to use it, but not her.

Once again, I was jumping for joy.

So, I put Ubuntu on hers also.

I don't think either one of them has turned Windows back on since I put UNR on their computers.  I will give them a few days, then ask them if they want me to take windows off.  keep your fingers crossed.
