### Post by franklee on 2006-04-08
Ive used Linux and BSD for twelve years now. Mostly BSD variants actually and Slackware - which is a rockin distrobution, close to the manual installations of yesteryear. I have to say I was totally against using any new Bleeding edge distro, especially one so popular if only because I have seen many come and go from the top ten at distrowatch, fallen by the wayside unsupported and neglected into dependancy hell.

After several muck abouts with Ubuntu, Kubuntu AND xubuntu....I have completely reconfigured my servers and home systems to run Ubuntu....because its so well supported, so well put together, so easy to configure and a delight to muck around with.

Mark Shuttleworth and the developers who have made this distro what it is, I salute you.
