### Post by deputyjones on 2006-09-14
I have been toying with Linux as a second OS for 6 years or so. I have used everything from Slackware to Mandriva to SuSe without ever finding one that really is true XP contender until finding Ubuntu. 

The Live CD picked up my wireless card in my laptop right away, and everything has been great ever since. All my hardware worked right away and all the software I needed was easily available.

Thanks to the developers and the Ubuntu community for a great distro.

O yeah, why I say almost is that I am using a Lexmark Z65 printer with no love on the drivers yet.
