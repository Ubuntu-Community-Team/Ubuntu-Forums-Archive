### Post by Waltomatic on 2009-01-28
About four months ago, after finding out that Micro$oft had completely stopped supporting Windows XP 64-bit (worst investment ever), I decided to cut my ties with them completely. I canceled my Xbox Live account, I sold my Zune, I used four letter words in describing Bill Gates and Steve Ballmer, and I resolved to find an alternate OS.

Having had frustrating experiences with Apple products (I've owned two iPods, and they both stopped functioning no less than 30 days after the warranty period), I figured that OS X was not the choice for me.

I decided to explore the world of Linux, and minutes later I happened upon a user friendly distro named Ubuntu. Needless to say, it was a painless and _cathartic_ transition. My desktop thanks me for my decision every time I turn it on.

I am proud to say that, "I am a recovering Windows user."
