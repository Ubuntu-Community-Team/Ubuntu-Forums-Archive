### Post by UBUminJ on 2011-05-07
I am using Computers since 1979 (UNIX, Apple, Windows, SuSE) and Ubuntu since 2008.
I like the new desktop UNITY as the new standard - it looks much fresher than GNOME. I am sure it will attract more new users than GNOME would.

Those who don't like it, I am sure you are able enough to switch back to GNOME.
Perhaps, someone of you could write a detailed explanation "How to" for those users who are not yet able and publish it in the forum.

No visible change makes people think of a stand still.

I think Mark Shuttleworth and the makers of Ubuntu have a good point for considering touch screens as the future. As soon as there are 12-15" tablet PCs available, I will also switch from desktop/netbook to tablet. 

But I want to use this chance to say thank you to everyone who contributes to Linux and Ubuntu (developer, forum contributers etc). You give people a choice.

Michael
