### Post by steveneddy on 2008-11-30
I have an 18 month old laptop from System76 and I get suspend/hibernate, full USB speeds, Firewire and wireless.

All worked out of the box. 

Ubuntu 8.04.

Intel Core 2 Duo, 2 Gig RAM, Nvidia
