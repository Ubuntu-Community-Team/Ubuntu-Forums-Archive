### Post by Lavaeagle on 2009-07-28
1st Month of Jaunty Jackelope:
After years of being choke d and controlled by windows inability to adapt to what the user's really want, I made the decision to switch to Ubuntu 9.04 aka Jaunty Jackalope.  

After doing some back up of all my files I finally got ready to switch to jaunty.  You have no idea how nervous I was.  I mean i was seriously set in my ways like an politician.  I had figured everything out about windows 7 and had to learn an entirely new system, especially since I had several parties dependent on my Web development skills and my video editing skills.

I swear when I finally finished installing Jaunty a huge sigh of relief came over as i found it easier and easier to replace the expensive programs with open source that was comparable and mainly "Free" and unincorporated.

I started playing around with things and changing the looks and feel of the desktop.
Now I find literally by the hour a new feature that I love.
-Update manager
-Add/Remove Programs (can actually add programs unlike windows)
-Applications Organizational system
-HDD's show on desktop
-System (Much better listings)
-Open Source (You have no idea how much my teacher praised this and I hadn't really known why)
