Greetings,
First of all, thank you Canonical for a absolutely great operating system. Also, thank you developers for equally great applications. So much for my Testimonial!

Now for the experience. People if your are looking for help it makes more sense to me to use Google/Advanced search. This is because the answers are scattered over a multitude of support websites. Certainly if I find a answer on Launchpad it may also link back to this forum with a solution regarding implementation, but it may also link to a dozen others. It has never made sense to me to look for an answer on a small part of the Linux community. When I do this I am just asking members to do my Google search for me. Thats why you may not get an answer to you problem post. The answer is elsewhere and you need to do your own work. This being said I try to answer the limited posts on which I have knowledge. And I also want to thank all of you members for your support over the last two years that have taken me from the nice crop of rich black hair to the glowing chrome dome that I have today. Ubuntu, I couldn't have done it without you!
