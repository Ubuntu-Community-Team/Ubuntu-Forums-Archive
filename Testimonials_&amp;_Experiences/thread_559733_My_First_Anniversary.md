### Post by rsambuca on 2007-09-25
Well, it has now been a year since I first plugged in a Dapper Drake liveCD to test it out.  I first heard about Ubuntu from watching the old *Call for Help TV* shows with Leo Laporte.

What a year!  I have gone from testing the liveCd, to dual-booting by installing Dapper onto an old 6GB hard drive I dug out of an old machine, to adding a larger partition onto my main drive, and then finally to getting a new sda drive just for linux.

Since then, I have repartitioned it and now have 5 different linux distros on one drive, to go along with XP and Vista on the other drive.  

I wish to convey my deepest thanks to the Ubuntu community, without whose generous donation of their time and expertise I would not be where I am today.
