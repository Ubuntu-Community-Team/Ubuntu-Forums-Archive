### Post by qwazert on 2007-12-27
This may not be the best section of the forums to post this in, I hope the mods can grant a little variance or just move it to where they think it best fits...
I have been using Linux for about 2 years now, and although I have been tempted and led astray by other flashier Distro's...I always find myself coming BACK to Ubuntu for several reasons:

i) All of my hardware WORKS...
ii) All of the software WORKS...
iii) The forums here are THE BEST, bar none!

I had a rather unpleasant experience in the PCLOS forums a while back...awfully unfriendly crowd there....not to mention that distro didn't support most of my stuff.
Without a doubt, the overwhelming proof of Ubuntu's superiority came to me today, after spending THREE HOURS (yes...3 hours!) in WINDOWS...trying to configure my new wireless router and network! Seriously....Bill Gates needs to be slapped!
When I finally got it working (somewhat), I booted into Ubuntu to get it working in this environment and ALL the hardware was recognized immediately! It was then, that I discovered the reasons for my woes in WINDOWS...the USB adapter was intermittent!
So rather than WASTING an afternoon (in WINDOWS) trying to configure a piece of junk that didn't work from the get-go, I should have simply tried in Ubuntu FIRST...whereon I would have seen the problem right away!

Ubuntu rocks! Really....
