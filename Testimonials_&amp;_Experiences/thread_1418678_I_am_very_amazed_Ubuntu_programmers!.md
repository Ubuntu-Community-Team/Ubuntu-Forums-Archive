### Post by klasjosh30 on 2010-02-28
Okay so after trying MANY different distros of Linux and many different versions of each distro each had a problem with my laptop. Most common ones all (or mostly all) distros and versions I tried had the following problems:

-Sound not working
-Wireless internet not working/not even getting recognized 
-Flash player not working

and a lot more smaller errors. 

With ubuntu 9.10 the sound works, wireless works, flash player works, and hopefully no more smaller errors. (now lets hope it doesn't come back to get me for posting this)

Anyways I am just happy everything is working great! 

Also my linux has 244 available updates in the Update Manager. I am scared to update just incase it causes something to stop working. :|

GOOD JOB UBUNTU!
