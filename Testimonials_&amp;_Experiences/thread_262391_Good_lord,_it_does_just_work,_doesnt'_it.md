### Post by ClarkePeters on 2006-09-21
Well, what can I say? Ubuntu is just the best best best.
I was so long waiting to go to Linux. I'm an old programmer (and an old man :D ), but even for me I really got tired of having to be a Linux techie just to get the computer to do what I wanted it to (that was several years back though). Plus, I'm married, have six kids and a job! Who has the time to fool around with computers? I need my computer to serve me in my work, not the other way around.  

Obviously, I'm a happy camper. 

When my friends ask me what Operating System I use if I"m not XP, I smile and say, The Ubuntu OS (of course it's Linux, but who cares? It's Ubuntu and the community that makes it all work).   

I'm so lucky, I came across Ubuntu by accident, in one of my frustrating Windows episodes I just did a little web research and found Ubuntu. Used it because I don't have access to other distro CDs where I'm from, so I downloaded the single CD iso image. 

I love the idea of starting small from a single CD (which, btw, is all most working people need) and building up to more if desired. Keeps all the crud off my machine.

Thank you Ubuntu and the Ubuntu community!
