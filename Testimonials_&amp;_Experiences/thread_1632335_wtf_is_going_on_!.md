### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-12-01
I hate it when people complain "Ubuntu took away Control-Alt-Delete!" or "Ubuntu took away my GDM themes!". Ubuntu did no such thing. The upstream developers, in these two cases Xorg and GDM respectively, removed or shifted those features.

The cube really doesn't look good on integrated graphics, whereas the desktop wall looks smoother and zippier even on slow graphics. It's only a couple of packages to install to get it back the way it was.

I can't comment on Quake Live and AppArmour.

So, basically, good luck finding a supported distro that contains the old themable GDM. Because all recent distros are using the upstream GDM.
