### Post by mendozaro on 2008-05-06
My "other" OS is a crap... nothing works out of the box. After installing it i get 640x480 resolution, no wired network, no wireless network, no NOTHING. I have to download tons of drivers, install antivirus programs, antispiware... it took me about 5 hours to get it to a decent working state. 

After that i had to install documents processor (Word), Adobe PDF reader, Nero - burning cd/dvd's, Firefox (explorer just sucks)... and this list really goes long.

.......

Hardy: EVERYTHING listed above works out of the box! Not one hick up. Now some of you might go and say: you are lucky boy... you have a PC that is supported by Ubuntu... hardware that is good for linux... CRAPPP!!! I installed Hardy on 2 computers at my home, 3 in my office and one at my mothers. All are different configurations with different video cards, different processors, different motherboards, different types of internet connections, different sound cards and so on. The only things i have really noticed from gutsy to hardy are IMPROVEMENTS.

My conclusion: Ubuntu is a real contender for MS and is getting better and better every time. In time, more and more software for Linux/Ubuntu will get on the market (commercial or not), games will start to be ported to linux also and the circle will be broken eventualy.

P.S. This post should be a response to all that just come here and start ranting about the problems they have with ubuntu. Get real people. Ubuntu is still not ready for all but, i can say it is ready for most (80%). The question is: Are you ready for Ubuntu? Cause you cant expect it to be windows!
