### Post by tdawgf on 2010-05-07
Ok so it has been a hectic couple of months with me lately. HDD failure removed my 500 gig drive for windows gaming (/cry I miss you Batman, Farcry 2, and Oblivion), but I also had spent the last month stepping away from Ubuntu 8.04 and trying OpenSuse 11.2 and Fedora 12. Maybe Ubuntu is the Windows of Linux cause I keep coming back to it. So when I came back to Ubuntu, I came back to see 10.04 was finally out. I must say, WOW, even more stable than 8.04 was for me and everything simply works. I love that feeling. It was wonderful to have gnome at boot again. I prefer it anymore over KDE although back when I first tried Linux I would have chosen KDE3 any day.

I would also like to say, The new default theme is simply gorgeous. Although unique in it's own way, the human themes were very bland for me. I am sure that some will miss it though. I want to give major props to the developers for their efforts. I have been having so much fun I keep forgetting to replace my Windows drive.
