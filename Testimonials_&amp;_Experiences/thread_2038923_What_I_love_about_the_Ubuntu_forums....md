### Post by Kalanac on 2012-08-07
I just thought I'd take a moment to say how awesome I think the Ubuntu forums and community are.  I've used them often to fix problems I've encountered and the other users have been super useful.  

I've also seen posts by other users about relatively simple problems or mistakes and not once have I seen those users yelled down, belittled or called a noob.  It's truly heart warming, in all the internet, forums are the most likely place to encounter negative social behaviour and I've yet to see it on these forums.  Whether this is because the mods do a fantastic job of troll hunting, I've just not seen the dark side or (as I suspect) the community is just too nice, it's worth noting and being praised.

Kudos everyone :)
