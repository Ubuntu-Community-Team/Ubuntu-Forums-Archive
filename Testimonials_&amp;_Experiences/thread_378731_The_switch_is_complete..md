### Post by AngryGnome on 2007-03-07
I have finally crossed (fully) into Ubuntu (and linux in general) and I couldn't be happier.  I have always been a Windows user... started out on Windows 95 when it first came out and have been with them ever since.  However, I have always been an open source junky and, when I finally realized that the only piece of software on my computer that was made by Microsoft and/or not open source was the operating system, I thought "Hey, why not give linux a shot."

I've tried out a couple of other distros, but what I finally settled on (about a year ago now) was a dual boot of Win XP and Ubuntu.  Slowly, switching back and forth between them became too much of a hassle.  So now I have fully crossed over and my system is completely dedicated to Ubuntu.  I highly doubt if I will ever go back to Windows again (especially with all the small print garbage associated with Vista).

To all those people out there considering Ubuntu... I highly recommend giving it a solid try.  It's very different than what most long term Windows users will be accustomed to... but it's well worth the learning experience.

-One Very Happy Ex-Windows User
