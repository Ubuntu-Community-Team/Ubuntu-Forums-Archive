### Post by JensenDK on 2005-10-02
Hi everyone,

Today it is one month ago I downloaded and replaced windows with Ubuntu. After a couple initial sound/hardware problems that I managed to solve by reading around on these forums I got it working. Not knowing much regarding linux this was a bit hard on me and I was close to giving up and going back to windows a couple of times. 

I did not and since then I have not looked back for one second. 

When I had some spare time when running windows windows I could always use 5-6 programs to scan for spyware, run a virus check or defragment my harddrive. Now I find myself staring at my desktop. 

It is the oddest sensation being able to use a computer without having to worry, without having to maintain this and that.

I guess what I wanted to say was: Thank you ubuntu team (and all of the developers that make all of the programs etc. that is a part of ubuntu). 

All my bases are belong to you!

Regards
