### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-12-22
**[COLOR="Blue"]Xubuntu 9.10[/COLOR]** runs awesomely on my 5-year-old Dell with its meager 512 of RAM. There are lots of even lighter and faster Linux mixtures, but none have the out-of-the-box readiness of the Ubuntu family (Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Xubuntu, and soon Lubuntu) and their many siblings.

You might not ever even *want* to switch to Ubuntu from Xubuntu! I've found it less troublesome and very simple to customize and use. Xubuntu doesn't ship with PulseAudio, which has been very troublesome to folks using the other 'buntus. And it doesn't ship with "Mono" if that is an issue for anyone. 

From what I've read in these forums and elsewhere, it looks like **[COLOR="Blue"]Xubuntu has been on a diet![/COLOR]** Karmic is very much leaner and faster than previous versions of Xubuntu.

Enjoy!

-Robin
