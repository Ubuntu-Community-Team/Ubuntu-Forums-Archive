### Post by mobo on 2012-09-18
I have tried Ubuntu in the past and thought quite well of it but could never get away from Windows totally so I constantly gravitated back to it. However, after picking up an Asus K53E notebook several months ago, I installed 12.04 on it and the rest was history..Install went better than expected, everything works, even HDMI which is necessary and printing support. 

If I may be so bold, I must say that even though this system ran very well on the Intel i3 chip, it runs even better on Ubuntu. Fatser all the way around. Never a bottleneck. I have not used Windows now for several weeks and I am now in the planning stages for migrating a server to Ubuntu.

Thank you Development team and congrats on a job WELL done...\\:D/
