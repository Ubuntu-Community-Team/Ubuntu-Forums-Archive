### Post by zeeR on 2007-02-14
I just wanted to let you know how funny all this Windows vs. Ubuntu got...

On Windows, I couldn't play Counter Strike, because after less than 5 minutes, the game would simply quit, and I got the error "WSAEINTR"..

So, since I dual boot, I thought I could try to install Steam/Counter Strike on Ubuntu with Wine...Well, I did, and it works flawlessly!!

It runs completely smooth, without any slowdown, with less lag than on Windows, AND without the stupid error!!

This is one of the games I play the most, it's made for Windows, and I can only play it on Linux! Now I don't have to dual boot to do it!

This made my day...

Ubuntu rocks :guitar:
