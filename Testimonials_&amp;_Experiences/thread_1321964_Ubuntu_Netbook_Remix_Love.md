### Post by ElSlunko on 2009-11-10
My cousin (yes I'm writing on his behalf) is having a great time with UNR. He often complained about how slow his netbook was so I installed UNR for him and after setting it up I haven't heard one complaint from him (yet). He's been using it for a month now and highly prefers it over the factory XP installation. Oo to not having any word processor, the look and feel, and ultimately the performance are all praises of his thus far.

For reference, he's a Senior in High School with very little computer knowledge. In fact he hates computers for the most part (according to him). I would gather he's not too bright either, but you didn't hear that from me. That is all.
