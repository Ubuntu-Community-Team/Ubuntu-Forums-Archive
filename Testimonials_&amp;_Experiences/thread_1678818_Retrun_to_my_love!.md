### Post by NikS on 2011-01-31
Hi all..

For the background, I used Ubuntu almost 3 years ago! I guess, Hardy was the last version I used..
For whatever the reasons, I had to leave...!
No, the reasons were never related to capabilities of Ubuntu, though..
Its just that, I was to stay at hostel, had to leave my desktop at home, everyone else at home is not into computers much and had a general thinking as Ubuntu is a Linux (which of course is correct! :) ) and Linux is not for general users! Which left me with no choice but to leave it with the Ms Windows OS when I left.. There, my employer provides systems with Windows installed, they even limit the Internet access for beginners...! :mad:
Anyway.. So the 3 years passed..

1 week ago, I installed Maverick and its A-W-E-Some! Just 1 word!
Anything I need is merely a few clicks away! While I would love to work on a CLI, this is a great advantage for everyone at home.. My sister has already agreed that it is not as difficult as she thought and so has my wife! Most importantly, I did not have to convince them, they saw me setting it up, and falling in love with it, AGAIN! (This is the reason my wife actually hates Ubuntu for! :p ha ha..) Out of curiosity they checked it out and were WoWed!

What I liked amongst many other things is Ubuntu Software Center.. The stop solution for all applications needs! U can search, browse, read about, install and even remove applications from 1 single interface, and that too offline! Superb! Then the simplicity in setting up Bluetooth, connectivity etc etc etc...! Bluetooth in Ubuntu gives me almost 1.5 times higher upload and download speeds than the Bluesoleil proprietary drivers in Windows! Loving it!
What I am feeling like is to 'Yell to the world about Ubuntu'!

Here I post this, to express my gratitude towards the developers, community and everyone else liked to Ubuntu.. Thank for your efforts! You have made it really really awesome!
