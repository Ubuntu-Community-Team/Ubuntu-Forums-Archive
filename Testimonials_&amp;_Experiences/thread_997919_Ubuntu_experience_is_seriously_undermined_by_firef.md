### Post by robeff on 2008-12-09
Aha! I finally found some settings that fix this issue for me. Here's my original post on the subject; [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=6333374#post6333374](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=6333374#post6333374)

From this link;

[http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?t=118088](http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?t=118088)

Intrepid ships with;

Version: 177.80-0ubuntu2

of nvidia-glx-177 (the binary driver)

So, I added the following to /etc/X11/xorg.conf (after making a backup)

Option "PixmapCacheSize" "1000000"
Option "AllowSHMPixmaps" "0" 

Under the nvidia video driver section.

Then, when you restart X, you execute;

nvidia-settings -a InitialPixmapPlacement=2 -a GlyphCache=1

After I made those changes, the sites that were freaking out for me were behaving fine. Two big offenders were;

[http://mozillalinks.org/wp/2007/08/firefox-3-gets-smooth-tab-scrolling/](http://mozillalinks.org/wp/2007/08/firefox-3-gets-smooth-tab-scrolling/)
