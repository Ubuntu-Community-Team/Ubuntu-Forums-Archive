### Post by hoppipolla on 2009-08-10
What a fantastic improvement over the previous 4.x KDEs!

I have nearly always gone for KDE over Gnome because of it's progressive nature and the potential of libraries like Qt, but it's so nice seeing things finally come to some fruition!

Every element of it is beautiful :)

It does still need a little more polish in places and the occasional bug still pops up (and it would be nice if we could match some Windows features such as inner-window transparencies), but for the most part when you combine it with Compiz it looks staggering!

I really hope work on KDE rockets forward (I will be encouraging new coders to work on it!) and hopefully this should really start fuelling the future of Linux and OSs in general!

Hoppi :)
