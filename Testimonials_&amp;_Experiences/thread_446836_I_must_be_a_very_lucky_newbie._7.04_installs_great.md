After seeing so many bad Feisty Fawn experiences, I feel compeled to share my experience.
In short, I've installed it in my main system, (Dual Opteron, Asrock Dual-Sata2, 2gb RAM, Nvidia 7900), my laptop (HP pavillion dv1140), and an old PC I'm donating to my hospital (Athlon 900, 384Mb RAM, NVidia GF4 MX) with no problems whatsoever.

So I guess I must be very, very lucky...
