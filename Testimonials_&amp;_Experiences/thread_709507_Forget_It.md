There is a learning curve associated with any true multi-tasking multi-user commercial grade operating system.  This is because they have more capability to do more things than simple one-user operating systems.  Some people are not willing to make the necessary effort and others do not have the time to learn.  In that case, any advanced operating system may not be for them.

Please do not be embarrassed if you didn't have a good experience with Ubuntu.  It may not be the operating system for you, or your interests and capabilities.  Feel free to come back from time to time and re-try multi-user operating systems.  As your knowledge and skills increase you may find it a fit at some future time.
