### Post by Rival11 on 2011-10-22
11.10 is definitely not too bad and it suits users like me perfectly (I'm mostly a web user only who only uses one or two local FTP apps and of course...RDP) but anyone who uses Ubuntu honestly cannot say that 11.10 is perfect or that there is no reason to complain.

They really stripped things down here for the average user and while of course since this is Ubuntu we're talking about here and you can get pretty much get anything back...the average user is not going to want to do this.

I agree with them sliming things down but I honestly just think it was too much at one time (again not for me but many other users).

Was I just instantly pleased upon installing??  Absolutely not, Unity was very buggy until the first patches were released (my trash bin still freezes upon trying to empty it on the first time).

With all that said.......I like the new direction but I have a feeling they're going to bring back a lot of stuff that was missing because of the complaints.....something I don't want but do see happening.
