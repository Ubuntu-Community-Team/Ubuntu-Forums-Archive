### Post by hazelnut on 2012-10-25
I am sorry that I upgraded to 12.10.  I was being adventurous, and thought surely Canonical have gotten their act together and the upgrade should go smoothly.  It's called an "upgrade" afterall! 

Well, no, it's not an upgrade. It's an experiment.  If you upgrade, you are a **beta tester**! ...and that's all.  There is nothing new here for you.  Stay at 12.04 lts.  It works.

For some reason the user switcher is moved into the gearbox menu and they removed "Startup Applications" from that same menu.  Why aren't these things listed in the change log?

The biggest issue for me so far is the broken window effects.  This marks the 3rd release in a row that broke the window shade roll-up effect for me. What do they have against the window shade roll-up effect?  WTF?  That is one of the few effects that make my windows friends say, "wow, that's cool!"

The Amazon search is irritating, but I knew about that going into it.  The main issue I have with that, is it seems to slow down the lens searching.  What if I don't buy from Amazon? No Newegg search?  But seriously, the search isn't even relevant. 

Immediately on upgrade, my compiz effects were turned off.  I had to reactivate window wobble, and like I said the window shade rollup doesn't work.  And the default animations they showed on the youtube video, are not active either.  

Yep, I should have stayed on 12.04, and I will be recommending that strategy to everyone.
