### Post by irishwoody on 2010-01-20
Left Window$ about two months ago and haven't really looked back.  Tried Linux about three years ago on a laptop - thought at the time that it was not ready - tried 9.10 too months ago and stayed.
 
1. Love the CLI and all the power it gives you.
2. Big thanks and thumbs up to all who spend their time helping others fix their support issues - very good community feel. 
3. Still using VirtualBox and Vmware for some apps that I have yet to find a suitable alternative to (AutoCAD for Linux Please ?).  Vmware with XP runs as fast as I had it native !
4. Have had some learning issues with Open office (but then had a similar issue with M$ when they introduced the ribbon and changed all the menu and toolbar layouts.
5. Big learning curve but most of the info. is out there - if you look for it
6. Open source - such a breath of fresh air !!

Couple of minor issues I still noticed or need to be fixed.
1.  When I boot in dual monitor - login screen appears on right hand monitor - not the primary one.
2. Firestarter firewall - seems to be bound to one NIC at a time and needs to be manually switched when moving from wireless to wired networks.
3. evince document viewer printing pdfs - on network printer - Sometimes doesn't print but reports the job as successful.
4. Using Gparted to create a new partition sets the owner to root - couldnt place folders into newly formatted partition until I changed ownership of new partition.
5. Occasionally having an issue with Opera and mouse focus - have needed to reboot a couple of times.
6. Sound under Karmic - needed a few tweaks to get going correctly.
7. Cant upgrade to latest Nvidia drivers - problem with the upgrade process.
8. Hardware driver for modem was causing an issue with sound if I recall. - disabled it.
