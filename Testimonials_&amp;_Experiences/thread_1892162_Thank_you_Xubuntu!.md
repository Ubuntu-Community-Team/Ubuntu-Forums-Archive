### Post by Todd42 on 2011-12-07
I posted last night on the forum, but I had to post again.  I have been distro hopping for a while now.  And, I am so happy with Xubuntu that I am quite certain that no other OS will touch this laptop.  So, thank you very Xubuntu team.  I wish you knew how grateful I am for all of your work.
