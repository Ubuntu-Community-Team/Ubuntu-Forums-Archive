### Post by brydonhunter on 2011-06-06
While Natty was in Apha and Beta, I tried it out and no matter how much I tried, I could not get used to Unity. I vowed to give it a chance anyways in the final release.

Natty is released to many a problem, I resist the urge to upgrade until this weekend. The upgrade itself went very well, no issues until...

When I rebooted, the computer went into memtest and would not even show me the grub menu. I tried all the standard repairs without success until I purged grub completely and re-installed it. The system booted without anymore problems.

Still do not like Unity, no problem. Installed Gnome 3, I like it better. A couple of small systems tweaks and so far everything works perfectly. I even installed Evolution 3, they have fixed some irritating issues and it seems to run faster.

So after a couple of frustrating  hours (grub) all is well and I'm happy. Now I need to look at changing themes, etc.

Take care
