### Post by Emerzen on 2007-04-24
First, thanks to all those involved in the open source community and the Ubuntu team in particular.  I've been pleased w/ Ubuntu since I put my toes in the water back with Hoary, but this is the first release that has allowed me to experience the full potential of GNU/Linux/Ubuntu.  I got my home wireless network going in a snap and am doing everything and much more than I did w/ XP.  

I would add that being able to leave MS also was due to Google's online apps.  One of the key features of XP, Office and my Windows Mobile Device, depended on that setup.  Now that Google has delivered their online suite, I'm not dependent on it as I'm able to use Google Calendar via any browser so I don't need the mobile device.  (I have Sunbird installed which can subscribe to your remote calendar if anyone else is wondering).  The more apps that go online, the less dependent people will be on MS, or any OS for that matter.

Well, thanks to all involved and I'll keep spreading the word and the good spirit.

Almost forgot this unexpected  benefit: on my Dell XPS M1210 Feisty nearly doubles my batterly life compated to XP!
