### Post by alecjw on 2006-08-27
Well, my brother asked for a computer. A simple one which plays games, has Microshaft Office and nothing else. So I brought a computer back from school, a Pentium II with 96MB RAM. I tried all of these OSes, none of which worked:
Windows 2K
Linux XP
Xebian
Debian
GNU Halo
PC Linux
Santa Fe Linux
DSL (Worked, but he found it too hard to use)

Then I tried a minimal installation of Ubuntu. It works a charm and you wouldn't believe how fast it is.
It also found drivers for the ancient video card and made my webcam work, which didnt work in XP.
And my computer-illiterate brother could use it!


Thanks Ubuntu Team!
