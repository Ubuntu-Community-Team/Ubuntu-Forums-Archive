### Post by wvscout76 on 2009-05-18
After extensive reading and research on Ubuntu I figured Id finally take the dive and give it a try,and excuse the cliche and simplistic response, but "its freaking awesome!" I gave Linspire a shot a few years ago, and while not bad, I wasnt overly impressed. I have constantly tinkered with the possibility of switching to a Linux distro to get away from the Microsoft Monopoly and the Apple "holier than thou" attitudes. And I am definately impressed.
    The hardware support, ease of install, simplification of the OS for the everyday user (I have tinkered with command line interfaces, but mainly just DOS variants). Everything the average home user needs is found. The "it just works" mentality is evident in the whole thing. Now while I am certain that I will find chinks in the armor and slight nuances that I dont agree with over time, but who wont. First impressions are everything, and this was a good one.
    If any Linux distro can break through the blanket of Microsoft advertising and potentially "strong arm" tactics to force their OS on 99% of systems, this may be the one.


Anyways, to anyone and everyone working on this, great job! You've got a believer.
