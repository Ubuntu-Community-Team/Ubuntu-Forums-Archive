### Post by Ibuntu_52 on 2005-10-01
This is the story of how I started using ubuntu and why I stuck with it.

  A couple of years back my dad gave me his old computer.It was a an old pentium 200mhz dell with windows 95.I decided to modernize it ie upgrade it.I had never done anything like this but I figured it shouldn't be too difficult.

  First I bought a new mainboard and proccessor combo an amd 2600+ with an elite group board for $170.Then I relized that my old memory would not work in my new mainboard.So next time I got paid I went out and bought some ddr ram.Then i got a new harddrive the 2gig drive it had was just to small so I got a 100gig maxtor drive.

     I put everything together and started it up.The bios came up so I knew it worked.Next I needed an operating system.I went to the store to buy windows but I saw something else.there was windows for $300 and another OS suse for $80.I went with the suse.

 Suse (9.1 I believe) worked great for a while.After a while I found things I didn't like about it.I began experimenting.I started trying many different distro's.I tried knoppix,slackware,fedora,mandrake,yoper I tried gentoo and was really impressed but it just seemed like to much work.
 
    I tried debian.That was the one.I installed debian sarge, I loved it.But I wanted the untable version.I tried doing a apt-get dist-upgrade with the unstable repo's but it broke my system.Anyways somehow I heard of ubuntu I heard the name before and thought it was just another noob distro.

      Ubuntu was and is based on debian unstable.I installed it and here I am!
