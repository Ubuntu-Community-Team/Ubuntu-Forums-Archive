### Post by Kickerk on 2006-08-04
There is a new page on the [http://www.ubuntu.com/mystory](http://www.ubuntu.com/mystory) looking for business and organisational case studies of Ubuntu use.  

These case studies will be used globally over the coming months and are vital to the spread of Ubuntu.  

If you have a story that matches the criteria below, as well as posting it here in the forums, please send it to [email]mystory@ubuntu.com[/email]

*****starts*****

[INDENT]If you are using Ubuntu in a business, school, government department, charitable organisation or research center we would like to hear from you.

We are particularly keen to know the following:

[LIST]
[*]How many computers you run on Ubuntu?
[*]Which applications you use?
[*]Why you chose Ubuntu?
[*]Whether you have shared Ubuntu with other organisations?
[*]What you like about Ubuntu?
[*]Which country and language(s) you use Ubuntu in?
[/LIST]

Of course the more detail the better, but even if its just a paragraph that would be great. Photos are very welcome AND please state clearly if you are happy for us to share your story!

Over the next couple of months we would like to start sharing these stories and let others hear how Ubuntu works in your lives, schools and businesses.

So don't wait, email us at [email]mystory@ubuntu.com[/email][/email]. [/INDENT]

*********Ends*********
