### Post by Stancel on 2010-05-06
Although I signed up for this forum in 2007, I only really started using Ubuntu last year. I started with Jaunty Jackalope 9.04 after ordering the free Live CD. The reason I didn't already have Ubuntu is I had a very old computer with Windows 2000 that was so old it wasn't able to even install the version they had back in 2007. But I got a new computer and I was able to install it perfectly. I loved Jaunty, it was a refreshing change from Windows, and I noticed how much faster it was. 

I upgraded to Karmic and then recently I upgraded to Lucid. Karmic was fine. But Lucid made me want to cry. 

The problems I experienced were:

* Sound was muted but it didn't say it was muted. Despite my best efforts to find info about fixing it, it was hopeless.

* Internet connection issues. It kept kicking me off the Internet even though all the other computers in my Wi-Fi network were working fine. I kept restarting the computer about a dozen times. it would often start working again only to kick me off in 15 minutes or less.

^ if it were not for the above two issues, I would have stuck with Lucid. But if I can't connect to the Internet and have sound, I might as well not have a computer at all. 

At this point I was excited to dust off my 9.04 Live CD. Figuring out how to make it bootable was very difficult, since it's designed only to install on default on a Windows computer.

I feel so happy right now with Jaunty. I think that Lucid looks flashy, but I don't think Ubuntu should put style over functionality. I think Jaunty looks better, to be honest. Immediately after installing Jaunty (and deleting Lucid!) my sound and Internet connection was back to normal. 

Others will have different experiences, no doubt. But those were mine.  Perhaps it is my computer that is too slow or not advanced enough to handle it. It handled Jaunty and Karmic perfectly well. Most 3D games run really slow but they ran slower on Windows. 

I am very interested in what Maverick Meerkat will be like. I will be waiting around for that. I am not sure I want to upgrade to Karmic, though. I feel fine with Jaunty and I don't think Karmic changed much.
