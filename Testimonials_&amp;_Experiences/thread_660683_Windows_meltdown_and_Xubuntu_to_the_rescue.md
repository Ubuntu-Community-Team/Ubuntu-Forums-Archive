### Post by Chayak on 2008-01-07
Well this morning my younger cousin was trying to play on the computer and it was so bogged down that it wouldn't hardly work.  So I installed ClamWin and beheld one of the most infected windows machines I'd seen in quite a while.  I didn't bother trying to remove them and after a quick talk with the parents I slicked the machine and loaded Xubuntu.  All they really do is surf the web and go to hotmail anyway so it shouldn't be difficult for them.

They've used it all day and I've had no questions and the only direction I gave them earlier is showing them how to kill firefox if it locks and not the usual turn off the computer and turn it back on when it locks up.  I couldn't have reloaded XP as any disks I have are in my case back in TX so I downloaded and burned Xubuntu as the computer only shows 186mb of ram but it's at least usable now unlike before.  My parents seem quite happy with it and they are not technically inclined.

I have installed hamachi and connected it to my hamachi network and set up ssh to only accept connections from that network.  I know eventually they'll need help with something and I can at least have an easy way to remote in and help without having them try and figure out how to find their IP address and such.
