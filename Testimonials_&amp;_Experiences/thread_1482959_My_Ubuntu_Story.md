### Post by beavis5551 on 2010-05-14
Hi everybody,
 
ubuntu came into my way in late 08 when i grew completly frustrated with Windows XP / Vista and wanted to look into alternative OS's. I started out with openSuse but I just not did get along with KDE and the way some things worked. Then, somehow and rather unexpected, I found Ubuntu... think it was Version 8.10. I first tested it a couple days in a virtual machine but then quickly decided to ban Vista and openSuse from my notebook and give Ubuntu a try in 'full scale'.
 
I was quite impressed with the way it worked and almost instantly fell in love with GDE. I can't pinpoint what exactly it is that makes Ubuntu so awesome for me but I just love the way it works. But the thing that really blew my mind was the AWESOME community support and the real great documentation over at the wikis and forums. I virtually never ran into a problem that wasn't documented / taken care of somewhere. this is just awesome!
 
Since my start with Ubuntu I already 'converted' some of my friends and co-workers who also grew frustrated with the whole windows thing over to Ubuntu. Its amazing how fast even a 'not so avid'-user can learn and understand how to use this wonderful OS.
 
Anyhow. My thanks goes out to all that have contributed to Ubuntu, the Wikis and the forums. I can't stress it enough: I love Ubuntu!
 
just my european two cents :-)
 
beavis5551
