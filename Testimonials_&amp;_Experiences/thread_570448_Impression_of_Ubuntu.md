### Post by LaRoza on 2007-10-08
It may seem strange for me to make this post after using Ubuntu for so long, but something new came up.

I don't have internet access at home, so any internet I use is at school on their computers. I have never used Ubuntu with an internet connection. I am using it now, flawless wireless detection, much better than Vista's (OEM), and also much faster.

I am a wired network, I am at school using an cable. I can't believe how easy Ubuntu is if you have an internet connection! I liked it before, when I had the repository DVD's, but the internet makes it very easy. 

I envy those of you with home internet connections.
