### Post by chris86wm on 2005-10-12
I first concidered installing linux after a **CRAZY** problem that i was having with windows. Long story short bittorrent wouldnt work on my newly built computer even after fresh installs (yes, i am a very competent bittorrent and windows user, so something was really screwy). So i began searching for alternatives. I had already tried out Auditor for some wardriving stuff, but i never concidered using a linux distro as a major OS. So i ran through the live cds.....Suse, knoppix, kanotix,etc and finally tried ubuntu. Well for starters it was a little rocky. Live cd wouldnt work, but i saw ubuntu jump to #1 on distrowatch so i said what the heck and did a full install. I ran into some walls, but then i was pointed to [www.ubuntuguide.org]("http://ubuntuguide.org/") and my god a was amazed. After that i flew the the problems and started using my desktop the way i wanted to. After some other hickups i finally got the feal of ubuntu and now i hate using anything else. It just works, and that is what i love about it.

I would like to say thank you SO MUCH to all the developers of this wonderful operating system. After using Ubuntu i doubt i will ever switch to anything else. Also thanks to the people of this forum. You guys are the most polite and intelegent people i have ever met on the net. Without you guys answering my noob questions, i would be no where.
I guess what i am trying to do here is just say a big [SIZE="5"]THANK YOU![/SIZE]
