### Post by jaqie on 2008-09-11
It has a lot more to do with the hardware capabilities of the video card in the computer for DVD playing then the CPU speed.  I had a P3M 1Ghz laptop which had a trident cyberblade XP Ai1 video card which did not have hardware mpeg2 acceleration or stretch functions in xp or linux. It dropped frames like mad DVD playing.  On the other hand I also had a P3 500Mhz laptop with an ati rage128 video card built in, which does have the proper mpeg2 and hardware stretching capabilities. That system played DVDs perfectly, without any dropped frames or anything and with a lower CPU usage then the 1Ghz laptop.
