### Post by tact on 2007-07-20
I was running a dual boot system with edgy and winXP on my corporate laptop.  After about 2 months, when feisty came out, I decided on a nice clean install and took that opportunity to deleted the XP partition totally.

Lately I discovered how well XP runs in a VM.  I installed a pristine clean instance of WinXP with Office in a VM just to see how it works, and leave it there as a "just in case".  I love how I can keep a backup copy of the XP-VM file, in its pristine state just after installation,  aside - and have a "working copy VM" on my laptop.   

If ever the working copy gets tired - I dont have to do an XP reinstall, I can just copy over the backup VM file and I have an INSTANT clean instance of XP with Office ready to run.  hehehe.

I use it (XP in VM) rarely...  and will NEVER have to reinstall from Windows CD again.   I love that!
