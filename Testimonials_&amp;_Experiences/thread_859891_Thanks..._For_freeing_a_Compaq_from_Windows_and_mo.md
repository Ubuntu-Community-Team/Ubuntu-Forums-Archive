### Post by ryaxnb on 2008-07-15
So I've been interested in underdogs always, and hated MS since I was around 9, so I've tried Red Hat 7, Mandrake 8.1, Mandrake 10.1, Mandriva 2006, Suse 9.3, and etc. But it was Ubuntu 8.04 that got me hooked on Linux. I'd say for an advanced user, it's definetely way more fun and easier to work with then debugging Windows XP. I use Mac OS X still (I prefer it to Ubuntu) but I can't afford a Mac for every machine, and I like free software, there's so much fun stuff at a click of a button. :) I use Ubuntu Linux and MacPorts to solve that problem. I love Ubuntu, it's freed my Compaq from Windows, and my Dell from a broken XP install (I don't have the Windows disc). It rocks! Keep it going guys, your support and community here is great, and I hope Ubuntu just gets better and better! I love GNOME and JWM and LXDE and all that junk too. 8-)
