### Post by Lavaeagle on 2009-08-19
I am on my 2nd month of use at the moment and I have to say, I have to say I am starting to see Ubuntu as more of a chisel that wants me to create something out of it and then prosper from it.

I constantly have to work with XHTML and CSS and it was never this simplistic on Windows, even though I am learning GIMP stuff just seems to fall together!!

Though it hasn't all been fun and games, I have had a few problems, only to then be solved by the great community of Ubuntu literally within minutes.  I just wish I could give something back.(13 pounds = 22 dollars! tshirt)

My update manager messed up because I had some how checked the CDROM box, got a few screenshots and a guys was walking me through it (Oh I see what you did there...well that'll fix it)

With windows there was never this much stuff, to learn from it, I just looked through windows and instantly you're a pRo.  I still have ALOT to learn which leaves me extremely enthusiastic to continue to learn more things about this OS.  Especially now I hear that commanding the computer from the Terminal is actually faster, I now have a new goal!

Thank you ubunut peeps.
