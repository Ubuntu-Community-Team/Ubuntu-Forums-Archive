### Post by derrick81787 on 2010-03-11
Why can't you get on the internet at school?  My school required VPN software, but that was all.  They only mentioned programs for Windows and OS X, but there are several for Linux.

Take a look at network-manager-vpnc or network-manager-openvpn.  They integrate right into the network-manager applet in Ubuntu (the applet on the panel that connects you to the internet).
