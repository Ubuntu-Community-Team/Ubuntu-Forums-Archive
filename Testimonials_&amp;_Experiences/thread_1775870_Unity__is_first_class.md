### Post by alan.klates on 2011-06-05
Good afternoon all. First let me say that I have used Ubuntu from 8.04 and every time there was a new version I took the bull by the horns and upgraded with no problems.when Natty arrived I went on to the forums and found that the overall impression was that Natty not the distro to use. People migrating away from Ubuntu to other distro's or back to previous ones or back to windows. |I thought it quite sad that Ubuntu had gone this route., but then I thought Progress is progress, I would give it a try anyway. This I promptly did. I had a terrible time with crashes, hardware not working . I must admit that I did the unthinkable and reverted to 10.10. I carried on reading the forums  and checked OMG Ubuntu every day. I was still of the opinion that I should try again so after one month of forum reading I again took the bull by the horns but instead of upgrading I did a clean install and guess what  fantastic every thing worked out of the box. I use I use a Gateway Laptop ML6226 with 2meg of memory on board graphics and 80 gig hard drive.about 4 years old. Unity is running in 3D not 2D, what a great distro. My advise to those disapointed by their first experience is don't be put off, don't upgrade, backup and do a clean install. I think and hope you will be pleasantly surprised how good unity is and how it can only get better. Well done Canonical.   
Sorry for the long post.
