### Post by sgwebb on 2010-01-12
Well.. I am starting out 2010 with a new OS. I have 2 computers one I use for mostly work, and my own personal one.. my work computer I have to use Windows for work specific programs, but they are not supported under Windows 7, so I have an extra Windows 7 CD & License. 

I was going to upgrade my personal computer to Windows 7, but started doing some research and found all kinds of problems with it and my dell Optiplex GX620.. mainly taking 10 mins or more to boot.. and decided to give Linux a try.. I am not new to Linux, and I have tried Ubuntu a long time ago version 5 or 6. I have to say my first choice was going to Fedora 12, since I use Linux AS 4 and 5 at work on my servers there, and sudo is quite odd to me.. but I could not get my TV Card to work with it. So I decided to give Ubuntu 9.10 a try.. I have to say it is great.. my TV Card mostly works, at least TVtime picks it up in 9.10, and I have a workaround for the sound. I found the software to do everything I need to do on my personal computer, and I love it.. I am never going back to Windows as my main PC, there is still a lot I have to learn, but each day I am tweaking a little more and trying new programs, and I do not miss Windows at all.. 

I really owe a great deal of thanks to everyone that has made such a great open source OS.

THANK YOU!
