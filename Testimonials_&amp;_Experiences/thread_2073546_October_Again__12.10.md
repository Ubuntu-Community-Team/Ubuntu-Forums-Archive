### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-10-19
I installed the Beta version of 12.10  because of my schedule. I fully intended to report bugs and even with the hundreds updates in  the last couple of weeks the error reporting Apport application opened very few times.

Installed desktops include the XFCE 4.10 session with Nautilus , Unity and the Gnome Shell. All these are running very well. Browsers tested include Opera, Opera Next, Firefox, Firefox Nightly, and Chrome All of them play video just fine.

I like the single Unity option and this version is very fast on my hardware and after installing the movie lens watching trailers via Movie player has worked very well so far. 

Of the three proprietary drivers offered I  found the Nvidia experimental works best on my hardware. I have had no crashes freezes or strange behavior of any kind . Libre Office has worked fine to the extent that I have used it

Gnome 3.6 themes are scarce and many of the 3.4 themes simply don't display or work properly to the point affecting the file rollers ability to work.

Hardware: AMD 64 X II 2.8 ghz CPU - Nvidia 8 serires card - 4 GB of ram.
