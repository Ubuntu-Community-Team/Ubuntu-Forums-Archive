### Post by Teknyk on 2007-12-09
...Windows that is!
My laptop is straight Ubuntu now and I am never going to back to the dark side.
I had a few minor issues getting my Broadcom wifi and my ATI Video working but it wasn't really that hard. With a fresh install of GG it was even easier. Then I found Compiz, freakin sweet deal!

I should have been fixing my partition settings before playing with all that though. I had everything on one large partition (except the swap) so I bit the bullet.
I had just finished resizing my partition and moving /home onto its own partition and was pretty bummed when it loaded what looked like default desktop settings and a new home directory.

My files, my pics, my desktop settings, my backgrounds, my browser settings/addons, all my bookmarks, my emails, address book were gone!

A quick copy of the /home off the backup, into my new "default home" and I got it all back! :guitar:
