Your second post was vaguely titled "Problem After Upgrading to Ubuntu 10.04." It also referred to the application as "Zine" Neither post gave enough information. 

I'm going to guess that the media you want to play is encrypted and that if you try to play unencrypted media Xine will work fine. If that is the case, you need a driver for decrypting videos which Canonical cannot automatically provide due to licensing issues (it's not Open Source, not GPL) The driver is part of the "Ubuntu Restricted Extras," which you will find in the Software Center.

When I had a similar problem, I found the answer by searching the forums via Google.

As for who is responsible for maintaining the free software you have on your free operating system, that would be you, among others. As a member of this community, you have a responsibility to describe your problems with enough accuracy and detail so that others *can* help you.
