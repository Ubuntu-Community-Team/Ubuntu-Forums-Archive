So orthogonal that it frequently things work by behind-the-scenes kludges. Of course, the Eclipse platform people do not think they are kludges and are proud of it.

Similarly, you people who write all these sound software may not think you are forcing people to kludge around but you ARE kludging around. I can't get my sounds to work. A LOT of people cannot get their sounds to work. So stop mucking around with trying to make things "flexible". Just make it work.
