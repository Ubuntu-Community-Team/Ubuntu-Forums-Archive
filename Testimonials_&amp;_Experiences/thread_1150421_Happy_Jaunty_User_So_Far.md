### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-05-06
I must agree 9.04 is a nice upgrade. It increased my system speed and as with others it just worked (for the most part) OOTB. I did need to update my HP printer driver. Which was a minor task.

Seems that 9.04 is a pleasant improvement. The installation procedure is simplistic and on my system everything went smoothly. I've come to expect this to some extent. Rarely do I upgarde this soon. However, I had a hardware failure and decided after replacing the component to try 9.04. It appears to have been a wise move. In the future, I will still have reservations with regards to upgrading immediately upon release. I generally wait for at least 2 significant updates prior t o even considering upgrading. I also tend to wait for an LTS release prior to upgrading.

Must admit this version did improve my system functionality as certain applications have definitely seen an increase in speed. I haven't witnessed a significant change in my startup time, but I don't consider that critical (others may).

To the OP:
I'm glad you returned and found the newest version meets with your expectations (for the most part). Good Luck and I look forward to your future posts.
