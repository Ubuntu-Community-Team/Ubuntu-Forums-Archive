### Post by TheForumTroll on 2009-06-01
Hi =)

I just wanted to share my own little success story. I have been using Linux on and off for quite some years now but it's first with the new Ubuntu I have migrated completely (I only have Windows Vista for games that doesn't work in Wine). What made me try Linux again was that my girlfriend kept saying that she hated Windows. For some time I couldn't do much as some strange BIOS bug kept me from installing Ubuntu on her Fujitsu laptop but then I gave her a PC and she is really happy about Ubuntu now.

I asked her what it is she likes so much. The first thing she said was how easy everything was to get used to "even for a non-geek like me". She also love Add/Remove and even use Synaptic a bit. No need to search the internet and not knowing what you get with your download. She also talks a lot about how cool it is that she can tweak everything and make it look exactly as she wants.

I also asked what she didn't like or if there was anything that she wanted to be better. The only two things she could remember was Flash and that the different MSN clients wasn't very good.

Her favourite applications are Gimp, Firefox, Thunderbird, Open Office and Evolution.

We are both running Ubuntu 9.04 64bit btw.
