### Post by einherier on 2008-01-28
Well, about 48 hours into deleting Vista from my hard drive and becoming Ubuntu-exclusive it's clear I am in WAY over my head.  I did expect Ubuntu to be more user friendly.  I think I played around in putty one time before for about a half a week, but it really didn't prepare me for quitting Windows cold turkey.

So here I sit.  Realizing I am either going to have to do one of two things.  Switch back to Windows to carefully regroup and counterattack, or Forrest Gump my way through this whole mess.  I am already starting to feel bad, cause I really have been beating the hell out of the forums with questions, and although I have done a really good job of checking to make sure I'm not reposting questions that someone else has already asked, but I feel guilty about not being able to offer a damn thing back to this community.

I seriously thought about digging out my old trusty standard issue Dell Vista reinstallation DVD, which, of course has bailed me out of a bind on an occasion or two before.

Then I realized I would rather stick my face in a homeless guy's ***.

So no matter how much it's going to hurt, I'm parking it right the hell here, and right the hell here is where I aim to stay.  I got a 12-pack and a nice Cohiba cigar, and I am about to give myself a hardcore Ubuntu Indoc (099-level) class.  I got no classes tomorrow, so I'm pulling an all-nighter.  It's about to get downright medieval in this motherfather.

So... ::raises beer bottle:: here's to all you guys out there who pack up your bags at the first sign of code.  I doubt I know more than you-- I just got a little bit of guts.  Cheers.:guitar:
