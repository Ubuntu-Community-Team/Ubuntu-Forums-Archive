The desktop wallpaper is something I got from either art.gnome.com or gnomelook.org.

The top and bottom bars are set to transparent and tinted a slight shade of green.

I found the icon set at gnomelook.org. It is called Human Ultra green and there are three or four different colored font themes there.

I choose the background in Nautilus that you see in my /home folder there from the default set and colored that one in Gimp to be shaded a little green.

You just have to play around with it yourself and try different things. You can always change it back or save certain theme profiles to change whenever you like.
