### Post by real_ate on 2007-04-29
well i didn't rightly know where to put this, but i felt the need to express myself. 
a long time ago (maybe about 7 months) i posted up a thread "the end of real_ate" which was taken up in the completly wrong way by someone thinking that i was giving an ultimatum to keep another person on the linux team. but the truth was that i just didn't have time to set up all the nonsence that i needed to become a fully fledged linux user then, and i was looking for help. 
over the last few quiet months i have been working like a mad fiend with college and life troubles and haven't had any time to do anyting but right now i'm living in a success story. i agree totally with the open source movement and believe that it is one of the only things that can give us all hope for a community that really does care about what is best for everyone and not just for themselves. i found it crippling that i was (in a way) being forced to use alternatives (i'm not sure if i'm allowed to use the M' word so dammingly, don't want to be sued :) ) in my every day working life. but now, the success is that i'm writing this post on a computer that was posing to be an enemy to my struggle to depend soly on open source! i have finnally gotten wireless working on my laptop and i just wanted to shout it to the world!! 
it took me so long to find out the information to explain why i wasn't getting it right. now that i'm on this side looking back it seems trivial but back then it was such a hard endevor! so i am now moving forward with a new resolve to help out in development in ubuntu and really get involved! i want to make my journey easier for the ones to follow!

over and out 
real_ate
