### Post by paker on 2007-08-03
After not being able to make an internet security suite work in Windows, I switched over to ubuntu. It's been about 7-8 months since then.

The best thing about ubuntu is that there is just ONE PLACE TO ASK ALL QUESTIONS, and QUESTIONS ARE ANSWERED. Before, I had to find the right forum to ask right questions. But here, I can post all my questions in Absolute Beginner. And the more experienced members don't frown upon us for asking same quesions repeatedly. I don't know about other sections of this forum, but Absolute Beginner is absolutely beginner-friendly. Paid consultants wouldn't be any more friendly.

I thank all experienced users visiting Absolute Beginner and posting answers. May your God bless your kind heart.
