### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-03-05
So, I needed an OS for our new media center (Well, new to us. It's an old HP mini-tower thing, with a P4 HT processor). Regular Ubuntu was too hard to see on the TV screen.

I thought - Hey, the netbook interface is designed to be easily visible and efficient with space on small screens close up, maybe it'll work on a big screen that's far away?

And you know what? It looks like it was designed to be on the TV. It's perfect! The fonts are the right sizes, the buttons and icons are easily visible, the screen space is used properly.

Kudos to the Unity team, for creating such a flexible interface :D
