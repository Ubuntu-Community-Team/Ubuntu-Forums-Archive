### Post by ayampanggang on 2007-10-12
Hello all :)

This is my first time using ubuntu and i must say it's great! Everything just worked. My wireless, my ethernet, even my ATI card comes preinstalled with the driver! (i can straightaway play dota with wine)

Tried quite a few distro before and most notably gentoo. Gentoo almost come as far as my ubuntu now. The reason is gentoo works jst like that. When i use other distro often i get quirks here and there, which is not the case with ubuntu and gentoo.

I must say ubuntu is already top-notch when it comes to user-friendlyness! :)

One that i like is ubuntu's gnome already come with various widgets that i need, so its preinstalled for me.  (E.g. the laptop battery power) This is not the case with gentoo, which comes with the stripped to the bone window system, and we must search what we want one by one, when at the same time wondering if the package we wanted might not even exist at all.

I use gentoo dualboot with XP but now with ubuntu, i've let go my windows haha

Now i know why ubuntu sat for a long time at the top distro section at distrowatch.
