### Post by 3Miro on 2010-09-01
My guess is that the video card on the laptop is struggling with the visual effects. Windows XP doesn't have any effects on, if your card supports effects, then they will be on by default. If your card is older (as it probably is the case of an Pentium M) then this will needlessly slow down the machine. Disable the effects.

On older hardware, you might also consider xubuntu, it lacks a feature or two, but it is much much faster.
