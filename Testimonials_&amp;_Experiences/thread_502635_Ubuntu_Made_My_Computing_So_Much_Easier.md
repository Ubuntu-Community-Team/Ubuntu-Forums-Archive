### Post by sparks0548 on 2007-07-16
I don't usually hail much about a product.  After upgrading and getting FF up and running on my HP DV2120, I am thoroughly impressed.  With the product and with the community.  Fast answers are always easy to find and everyone appears to be happy to help those experiencing problems.

In my civilian life, yes I am military, I work as a Litigation Engineer and Data Forensics Specialist.  There have been numerous times where I have had to extract data from tapes backed up with Tar, gtar, and numerous other utilities.  Ubuntu saved me when I needed to deliver extracted tars from tape back to tar on a ext3 formatted hard drives.  Hardware support and package delivery is far superior in my opinion.

I feel as Linux moves farther and farther into the marketplace we'll see more home systems and business systems migrating to the open source technology.  With unparalled development and support I can see Ubuntu leading the way.  I can testify it has its place in my data center.

If you're new to Linux, saddle up and hold on for the ride......It will be one rich with new experiences and community friends.  I've enjoyed it thus far and I look forward to getting back home to setup a home security system with Ubuntu.  

ROCK ON!!!!  :guitar:
