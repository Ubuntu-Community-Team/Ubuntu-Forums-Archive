### Post by FredB on 2006-07-06
Linux and me ? An old story.

The first time I've heard about linux was about 9 years ago. I found on a CD a slackware which was bundled with kernel 1.2.9 (or so).

It tooks me 2 years, testing some distros - like the now so "mythical" Red Hat 5, and some Mandrake to use linux on a more regular basis.

But I take the first big jump in 2003, with the mandrake 9.1. After 4 months, my computer died, but the new one I bought won't work with that linux distro. It tooks around 6 months to get a distro that works with my hardware.

In october 2004, I installed Fedora Core 3, and used it until february 2005, when I bought an Apple MacMini. I spent a year (13 months to be more precise), and I go back to a PC.

March 2005 : Breezy for 2-3 weeks, then Fedora Core 5 and OpenSuSE 10.1, until Dapper RC was released.

I installed Dapper RC the day it was released, and since, Dapper is working flawlessly on my computer.

I can't wait for Edgy to be released. Thanks to ubuntu coders !
