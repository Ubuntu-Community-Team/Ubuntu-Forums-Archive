### Post by lancest on 2011-05-08
Prefer Xubuntu 64- especially when running on battery.
MSI U230 Netbook with 4 GB ram. Also MSI U90 w/ 2 GB

[LIST=1]
[*]Stable, fast  (does not hang, stutter or freeze with no Compiz)
[*]Flash seems more stable. HD movies play smoothly
[*]HDMI works excellent with OSS driver -including sound
[*]Gmusicbrowser is clean looking. Random play is a nice feature.
[*]Battery life acceptable. 
[*]Theme and color scheme are sleek.
[*]Nice notify indicator when mounting disks.
[*]Smooth Ubuntu fonts
[*]Xubuntu terminal- I've always preferred it.
[/LIST]

Has an authentic Linux feel.
Really it's quite possible to enjoy Linux without Compiz! 

BTW I also have Ub 11.04 on desktops, it kicks there.
