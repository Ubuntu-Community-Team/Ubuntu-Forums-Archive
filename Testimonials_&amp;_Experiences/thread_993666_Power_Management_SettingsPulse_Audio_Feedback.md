### Post by bhankins on 2008-11-26
Currently using Ibex and I wanted to leave my input on two issues:

1) The Power Management settings in the screensaver menu are quite nice, but I think the slider needs to be adjusted to allow for longer delays than just an hour.  

Supporting information: Lately I have been watching a lot of stuff on hulu.com and I am sure more of these types of video streaming sites will pop up in the near future.  Occasionally I happen to fall asleep while watching a two hour movie which would leave my display on until I wake up at some point.  I know how to set it without the interface, but I can see it being a problem for new users with a similar issue.  I suggest the slider have multi-hour increments instead of skipping from one hour to never.

2) Pulse Audio Applet is quite a handy tool for me.  I switch back and forth between the speakers on my tv when watching hulu as mentioned above and some USB headphones for other stuff.  The applet I mentioned has been an easy way for me to switch between the two, with a few minor issues that is.  It doesn't really detect or display or really even explain the sinks to use, I pulled that info out of some other pulse audio information program.  To switch between the two I have been pasting the sink line from the information program over and over into the applet since it doesn't store more than one "other" sink at a time.  I am a fairly experienced user and I am sure I am doing it wrong, so I imagine this could be really frustrating to a newcomer who would probably never get this working as it stands.

These are my experiences, and I think the progress has been great on all these fronts.  I would be happy to see a few tweaks is all.
