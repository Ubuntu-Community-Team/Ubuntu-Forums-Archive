### Post by jhoo on 2013-01-23
Per the title, thank you to the folks that brought us Lubuntu!

I left the Win environment a year ago by jumping ship to Ubuntu (11.10).  Was never happy with Unity and dumped it as quickly as possible but pressed on for the rest of the year with Ubuntu.  By the end though it was making my old P4 system choke and I was itching for some app upgrades so I started looking around for somewhere new to roost in the Linux world.

Tried just about every LiveCD I could download, from which I installed Kubuntu, AntiX, eLive and Bodhi and gave them a serious run.  For various reasons I decided it best to stay in the *Buntu family and Lubuntu turned out to be the best overall compromise of all the systems I tried.

So far Lubuntu is pretty darn good.  Amazing that they're still able to squeeze enough juice out of my 10 year old desktop to give me a workable system. Responsive enough to not be an irritant and looks quite decent.  Of course I developed an E17 addiction along the way so I've got that installed so I can login under E when the craving comes.

So kudos to Lubuntu and LXDE!  Keep on truckin'!  ;)
