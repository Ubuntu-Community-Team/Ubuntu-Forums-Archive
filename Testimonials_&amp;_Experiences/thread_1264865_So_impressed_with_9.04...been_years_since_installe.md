### Post by gordong11 on 2009-09-12
I just wanted to say how impressed I am with 9.04. I last used linux/Ubuntu back in 2006, and I think it was version 5.

As a noob and know very little about linux and how to use the terminal processes and commands, and back in '06, I just couldn't get my video card and sound drivers to work properly. So I gave up and went back to windows.

Now I downloaded 9.04, and it is so sweet, everything worked out of the box. plus:

1. Was simple to use latest Nvidia Drivers
2. Upgraded to Alsa 1.0.21 sound drivers through the terminal with no problems(didn't need to, audio worked perfect out of the box)
3. So many more compatible apps. there is nothing I can't do with Ubuntu that I was dong with windows (except MagicJack phone, but can use skype or Ekiga). DVD rips, gaming, office apps a plenty.
4. GUI is so easy to use and looks great, panels with gdesklets add to the experience.
5. Ubuntu runs my PC so much faster than windows. Apps load super-fast no waiting, my lound Raptor hard drive isn't constantly running...9.04 is just better than windows.

I'm so happy I switched, no reason for windows anymore...btw I like Ubuntu better than MAC Snow Leopard too. Ubuntu is a great combo of simplicity, functionality and intuitive.
