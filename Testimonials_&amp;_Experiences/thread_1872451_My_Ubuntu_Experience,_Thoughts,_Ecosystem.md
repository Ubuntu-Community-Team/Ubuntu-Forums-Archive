### Post by dnzz on 2011-10-30
2011 is my 6th year with Ubuntu. I remember my days at collage trying to use Ubuntu 5.04 with my old P4 2ghz 128mb Ram laptop. I dig Ubuntu Forums many times to find solutions. I taught myself how to use Ubuntu. As years passed Ubuntu evolved, became more stable, more user friendly, less buggy.

I believe that Ubuntu became usable for most of the people after 10.04. After that moment Ubuntu is trying to increase user experience (Gnome3,Unity) and profitability (Ubuntu Software Center). 3rd Parties like Wine are also increasing Ubuntu's usability (PlayOnLinux). After this stage, I hope to see MS Office for Linux and Adobe Tools (Photoshop, Illustrator etc) for Linux.

Some people think that Ubuntu is going in the same way with iOS. I am one of them but I am not against it as long as they don't limit hardware :). In my own opinion if Ubuntu had limited its supported hardware like Mac's, we would have far more better OS then iOS with this community.



I have 3 physical + 1 cloud (Amazon EC2) total 4 PC's. 3 of them using Ubuntu. 



1- My Laptop (Ubuntu 11.10 - HP dm4-1300 with MomentusXT Hybrid HDD)

Features: Standard work PC, VirtualBox (Windows 7 - just in-case) 

Ubuntu works extremely fast on this machine. I am using it all day at work. I have to admit that switchable graphics are mess (IMHO Ubuntu's fault, I disabled the discreet one), and nobody but me can use touchpad (Totally HP's fault, it is better in Windows but still bad). 


2- My old PC as Home Entertaiment Center and Home Server (Ubuntu 11.04 - CoreDuo 2.6Ghz 2gb Ram)

Features: Squid WebCache Server, Privoxy, HAVP, Torrentflux, SSH, aMule, VPN, FTP, Movie Center, MP3, Router, Firewall etc.

This is my home server. It is connected to my TV. I watch movies, listen MP3, make downloads and also I can access all my data when I am not at home.  Works 7/24 with no problems.


3- My Cloud PC (Ubuntu 11.04 - Amazon AWS EC2 Micro Instance)

Features: Apache, FTP, Wordpress, VPN

This one hosts my blog. Also I use it as VPN server to secure my internet when I am at public places.


4- Game PC (Windows 7 - Core 2 Duo 4Ghz, 6GB Ram, Geforce 9800GT, Raid0 HDD)

If I want to play games or left my laptop at office, I use this computer.



I just wanted to share. If you have questions I answer them gladly.
