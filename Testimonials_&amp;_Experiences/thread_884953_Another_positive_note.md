### Post by michaelkahl on 2008-08-09
Well, I posted a while back about my switch over to Linux.  I had been running Ubuntu at work and Fedora at home.  Today I finally made the switch to Ubuntu 64bit at home.  I am very pleased, This was a very painless process and my pc seems to run smoother.  I was skeptical because of my prior experience with 64bit fedora and Ubuntu 7.04, but one of my co-workers made the switch from fedora 8 to Ubuntu 8 64bit and he was very pleased.  This inspired me to give it a shot.
I may consider switching my work pc to 64bit when 8.1 comes out later on.  Fedora wasn't terrible, I just like Ubuntu better.  Now I just need to figure out how to get argyllcms working in Ubuntu.
