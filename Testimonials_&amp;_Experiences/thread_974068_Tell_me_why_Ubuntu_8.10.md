### Post by Idefix82 on 2008-11-07
During the transition period I almost always found that the alternative programs available under Ubuntu are far superior to their windows counterparts. Sometimes I really felt like shedding a tear for the user friendliness of the software. So if you decide to switch, don't start installing windows programs under wine immediately. Have look at what alternatives there are first.

Some reasons to switch:
1. It's free
2. It's stable
3. There is much more free software and better software available
4. Better support (you will find nothing like these forums and if there is a genuine bug you report it and it gets fixed)
5. You are not sponsoring an evil company
6. You don't need to worry about viruses, programs that don't respect your privacy,...

The list goes on. But you will not actually appreciate any of what I am saying unless you try it out yourself.
