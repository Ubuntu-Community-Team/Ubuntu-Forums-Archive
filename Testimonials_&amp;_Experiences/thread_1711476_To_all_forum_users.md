### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-03-21
Dear friends and fellow forum users,

Tomorrow, March 22, is my first anniversary since joining these forums and using Ubuntu as the main operating system on my computer. 

Although I previously used Fedora, OpenSUSE, Linux Mint, as well as other distros, usually in a dual-boot with Ubuntu, I finally decided a year ago to stick with Ubuntu alone.

One reason for this is that Ubuntu suits my needs at the present time.

The more important reason, though, is this community.

I want to thank you all for making this a wonderful, enriching, and educational experience.

I also want to extend my thanks to the forum staff, whose tireless efforts to maintain a semblance of order are much appreciated.
