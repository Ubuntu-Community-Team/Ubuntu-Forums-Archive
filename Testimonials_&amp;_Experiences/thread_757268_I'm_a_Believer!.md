### Post by shmoe010 on 2008-04-16
I have been interested in computers for a while, but I never really learned any programming, and I was scared at the prospect of using an operating system that requires command-line execution. But after I had finally lost my patience with Windows, and went to Mac, and got sick of their bull, I took the dive, wiped my disk and installed Ubuntu 7.10.

I am so happy now! It was a little daunting at first, but my roommate is a computer engineer and of course has pretty much every linux distro on one pc or another so he's helping me out. The coolest thing is that all of the drivers, language packs, codecs, and everything else that take forever to collect and install in Winblows I can just get through Synaptic or Add/Remove programs in like 30 seconds. Plus, I was blown away by the fact that Audio/Video/Photo editing software that costs hundreds of dollars in Winblows is not only free here, but I don't even have to look hard to find it!

Probably the best thing about Ubuntu is the forums. I've been using it a little over a week, and EVERY little problem I've had, I've found a concise solution to on the boards.

Just wanted to tell all the developers and contributors thanks, you've made my life a lot easier. :)
