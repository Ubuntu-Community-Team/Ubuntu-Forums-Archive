### Post by bks on 2007-03-17
**Note: I do not intend this to be a bashing thread.**

In the past few weeks, since installing Ubuntu 6.06, my attitude towards the "Other" operating system has changed considerably.  Before Dapper, I had thought that it (the "Other") was a good OS.  Stable, fast, relatively secure (I hadn't had any trouble), powerfull and generally cool, but after installing Dapper the fog has been cleared.  I love Ubuntu and I enjoy using it, even for mundane everday tasks.  The unfortuante part of this tale is that I am forced to use the "Other" OS at work and it just feels archaic, slow, clunky and ugly.  I am overjoyed to have such an excellent alternative, THANK YOU TO ALL WHO PARTICIPATE(D) IN DEVELOPMENT AND SUPPORT!! UBUNTU ROCKS!!
