### Post by jeffus_il on 2009-08-17
Sorry UUID, I thought you were a pain in the rear end...
I can't remember your long and complex numbers. I had gotten used to good old /dev/sda2 etc.
Until one day I copied and expanded a partition from one disk to another. I expected to have to change an entry in /etc/fstab, since the device had changed.  To my amazement the new partition was automagically mounted! Well done UUID!
