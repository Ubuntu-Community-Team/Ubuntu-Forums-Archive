### Post by ranch hand on 2009-03-03
My wife was given an old HP (ex)XP box.  It did not work.

Could not load Ubuntu LiveCD because of 128Mb ram.  Got Dam Small Linux and found the HDD, all 5G was fried.

Ordered 2 256Kb DIMM ram sticks, got them and an old 10G HDD I had and went to town and installed them.

Loaded Intrepid.  It does push the CPU pretty hard but it runs great on the old sucker.  I am very happy.

I think that I can get some more old boxs and play with them some.  This really is too much like fun.

I had a couple of older P3 boxes that I upgraded with some ram and an HDD. Then gave them to a divorced neighbor for her 3 children.
