Very well said!

To the OP:

Where I work we got into a situation where we were supporting 6 to 8 versions of our software. It got to the point that we spent all our time supporting the older versions, and couldn't spend time on new development. We worked to reduce the versions we supported to 3 or 4. We also use a 6 month release cycle.

There are several Linux distros to choose from. You can pick one that is a rolling update, one like Ubuntu with a 6 month cycle, or one that has a release cycle that is much longer. 

It is your preference. You choose what is right for you.
