### Post by Farley69 on 2008-02-05
I have been on MS operating systems forever DOS, win3.1, 95, 98 ME, 2000, XP, and Vista.  I saw a video on Ubuntu Gutsy Beryl on utube, thought it looked kinda cool.  I had just bought an new machine with Vista, so I decided to wipe my  old XP machine P4 2.53ghz, 1GM RAM, Geforce vid, installed Gutsy + compiz and use as a media repository,  a small FTP server, and torrent client.  The install was a piece of cake, I struggled with FTP (VSFTPD) for about a day before I got it going, I have a few other projects in mind for the future.  But i gotta say I am very pleased so far.  It was intimidating at first but I stuck with it and refered to the many help forums available online.  

Being a Gamer I will need to keep my Vista image alive, but I can pretty much do everything else on Ubuntu.  So far a great experience, and I am looking forward to the next release.
