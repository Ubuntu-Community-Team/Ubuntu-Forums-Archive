### Post by caravel on 2010-02-11
> **nerdy_kid said:**
> And that im using the nvidia 190.53 drivers.

I think that's maybe part of your problem.  You're using non free drivers so your system can no longer be considered stable.

S3 is ACPI sleep or "suspend to RAM mode".  Quite a few systems have trouble resuming from this state (even certain motherboards running under certain version of windows).  There are some known issues with the nvidia drivers and suspend to RAM.

If you don't play 3D games and can do without compiz-fusion, I would suggest getting rid of those proprietary drivers and see if that resolves your problem.
