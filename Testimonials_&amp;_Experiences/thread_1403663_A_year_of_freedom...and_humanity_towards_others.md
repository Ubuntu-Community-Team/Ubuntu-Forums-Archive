### Post by rahilm on 2010-02-10
This is probably the best part of the forums. As this is the Ubuntu Testimonial and Experiences part, I am here to share my experience. A year's story with Ubuntu:

Precisely, this is the day i got introduced to ubuntu one year back. Back then, I was completely unaware of terms like linux, open source free software etc. I didn't know firefox nd chrome are OSS and i didn't care.

I got the Ubuntu 8.04 live Cd through a magazine. I spent an entire day googling what ubuntu is and what are the ways to install them. I did a Wubii install to try it out. Once inside ubuntu, it was a completely alien terrirory. I spent only 4-5 hrs a week on ubuntu back then.

A started getting used to Ubuntu and started spending more time playing with it. Since I had limited broadband, installing software was the most difficult part and i didn't like it a bit..that kept me off from using ubuntu. Switched to 8.10 in April.

In May, my PC got infected with Win32:Virut (For those who don't know, you are lucky). I didn't even know how to re-install windows, so stuck with wubii ubuntu 8.10 for a month and a half. And it is exactly because of that forced stay that I have become so comfortable with it.

What was the most amazing part of ubuntu that held me back to  ubuntu once windows was restored? Speed? Stabilty?. It was you. The ubuntu forums is the biggest reason i am still with ubuntu. Also the shear pleasure of trying something no one (I know) has tried before. I even ignored the pathetic software installation procedure.

With 9.04, I quit Wubii and did a normal partitioned install which is still present on my system unhampered. I must add the 9.04 was the turning point of Ubuntu for me. It was here that I first encountered a driver issue. In my opinion, 8.10 is still the best version i have used. Its good I had started liking it before jaunty, else i might not have been here.

I had switched to almost full time on ubuntu by then. For the first time, i installed Kubuntu-Desktop. It was a disaster. and i even can't remove it because it will take all the kde apps i had previously installed (Kmplot etc). I have heard that Kubuntu is the worst maintained kde distro.
I started playing with the looks, got introduced to compiz-fusion in 9.04. Loved it!! I should have clicked the expression on my friends' faces and uploaded it here just for fun. Came to know about AWN..but now the ubuntu world was changing.

September end saw the karmic koala release. I got it as fast as i could (via a friend because i had already ordered 2 cds from shipit). Put it in and bam, super bad resolution, no network. all gone..was very disappointed. I had heard so much about it. How karmic was very different from the previous releases and that it was the fastest booting distro yet (i can vouch for that).4 days with ubuntu forums and all problems are solved..(and they were all solved here in the testimonial section)..

Now, I am a full time ubuntu user, switched completely to ubuntu. I am also spreading ubuntu in my friend-circle but uptil now, no one seems interested. I am on karmic all the time, tweaked it beyond recognition. i have over 250 posts in this forum. And the future looks bright with all the lucid hype..

I just want to thank all the people for all there support. I love being here.

Links that have helped me through my journey:
