### Post by PHATSPEED7x on 2008-07-25
I took my laptop down to the local coffee shop, and did about a hours worth of surfing. This guys sitting next to me noticed my desktop, and looked lost. He asked me "What operating system is that?" I laughed and told him "Ubuntu". Still looking like a deer looks at a on coming train, I said "Linux". "Oh, what made you want to run that?" I just told him I liked to be different, plus I was getting tired of windows. He had a little newer Compaq laptop with vista installed. I asked him how he liked vista, and he said "It's ok, just wish it was faster". I asked him what his specs were, and he had a 1.7Ghz processer, 1GB RAM, and 80GB harddrive. I asked him if he would like to try ubuntu on his computer. He said why now, and I gave him my copy if the Ubuntu Live CD. He tried it, and loved it. He said "how much for the CD?" I told him don't worry about it, and that this was free software. He paid for my coffee.
