### Post by Hylas de Niall on 2011-10-12
:D Wow, i haven't logged in since i tried Hardy!

Well since then i've been distro-hopping like a mad thing, trying Puppy, Bodhi, various releases of Mint, JoliOS, Mageia, Fedora 15, Lubuntu (11.04), Kubuntu (11.04), Xubuntu (11.04), whilst revisiting Ubu for the Netbook Remix, Natty, and now Oneiric (Beta 2), looking for the perfect OS for my old netbook.

I think i've found it with Oneiric. Unity seems to be much much improved for this release (watching it grow wings and fly by updating the beta was very educational), and although it's still not everyone's cup of tea it suits my netbook needs admirably.
I for one really like the new style of DE's, and i'm very taken with Gnome Shell too.

I'm sorely tempted to upgrade my Win7 desktop to Ubu, and if i finally figure out how to get the sound working in Ardour (and the like), i can finally dump Windows completely!  ...but that's for another day and another thread!

Anyway, bottom line: I really like this iteration of Ubu, it suits my needs very well indeed. The team are doing a great job with it!
