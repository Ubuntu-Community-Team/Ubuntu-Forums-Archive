### Post by Exershio on 2008-02-19
I have to start off by saying I am really impressed with what I've seen. This is perhaps my 4th time trying Linux (I've always had major problems that I wasn't up to solving myself), however, I have finally fully converted from Windows XP and am leaving Microsoft and their greedy hands behind.

I did have a problem setting up my graphic card (ATI Radeon 9550), but I just used the ones from the restricted drivers repository and all worked right out of the box this time. Games aren't perfect, but I don't do any heavy gaming anymore anyway.

I'll have to admit, this is the first time in 5 years I have run a OS without ANY warez. I used to be really big in the warez community and all that, but now I realize I don't even have to be. XChat is free, and much nicer than mIRC! GIMP is my alternative to Photoshop. I haven't had any time to really "use" it yet, but I'll get to it. I've heard good things about GIMP. I've got OpenOffice.org to compare to Microsoft Office 2007 (which is way too crazy looking for me. I prefer old school office 2003, and OOo looks like that.) But most importantly, I'm now using Ubuntu 7.10 which is free, compared to the bloated Windows XP!

I thank all of you who have helped me tremendously over the past year during my adventures with linux. Now all that's left is for me to try OpenSuSE (it looks really interesting)! You guys will find me here a lot more often nowadays as Ubuntu is now my primary OS.
