### Post by Braytok on 2007-12-02
I'm a long time computer fanatic, been playing with them for around 20 years. My first computer that was entirely mine that I didn't have to share with my older brother who was also as addicted as myself, was a Commodore 128D. Thats was the machine I cut my teeth on in BASIC and CP/M and I was hart broken when the company went belly up. 

Grudgingly, I moved to a Tandy 1000 from Radio Shack and continued to supplement my addiction to computers as best I could on a system I really didn't like. I more or less fell out of love with technology when I discovered girls at around age 15.  It took me about 3 years to learn that unlike computers...girls are not "garbage in garbage out" If you feed them information they don't like you can't downgrade to version 1.0 from version 2.0, you cant roll back the driver and if you've created child nodes with them...well your stuck with that program. 

So I reacquainted myself with my first love and discovered she'd  grown up. I used Microsloth products for the next 12 years and even landed a job as a net admin for a nationwide company with 43 plants across the US. I dabbled in various Linux incarnations including Mandrake, Lindos, and Fedora but none really seamed to "do it" for me. 

Then I discovered Ubuntu Feisty... and the rest is history! Currently I'm running Gutsy and I will NEVER, repeat NEVER, go back to Windblows. I'll be switching  my WoW guild web server this weekend and that will be the last of my systems to change over. 

Long story short... UBUNTU ROCKS! I give it 10/10 on the BestOS Meter.
