### Post by tablloyd on 2008-07-13
I can see that my mental processes will require much more post-Windows reconfiguring than my desktop did! I just installed 8.04 as a dual-boot on my hard drive, and upon boot-up, I suffered the common "no mouse" situation. Being well versed and confident from years of fighting Windows compatibility issues, I immediately began searching for the solution, be it editing of boot files, config files, gathering  and installation of obscure drivers, whatever! I was ready and dove in.

Hmmm, most of the references to this problem dated back to the 7.04 days. I saw a few workarounds and such, but then I noticed a comment that someone had actually just unplugged and re-inserted the USB cable for the mouse and voila!. "Hey," I thought, "Could this be one of the areas where Ubuntu is going to make my life a new and much easier experience?" So, with a hearty "Nothing ventured, nothing gained!" I yanked out the cable and reinserted it and BINGO!—mouse-o-rama!!

I'm sooo glad I decided, finally, to begin my journey into freedom.
