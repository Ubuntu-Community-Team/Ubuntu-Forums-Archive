### Post by em3raldxiii on 2008-02-17
But therein lies the problem for us computer fix-it guys.  Once they install Ubuntu, they never need us to come over to fix anything anymore!  Sure, they might want us to come and undo the odd accident or show them how to do something specific, but in short order you will never hear from that customer ever again because they are happy.

Which of course leads to word of mouth, "Hey, just call that Wolfen69 guy and he'll get you fixed up." so in the short term you will have substantially more business ... and then poof.  You will reach a saturation point and .... no more software-related repair work ... ;)

But that's okay 'cuz hardware is more fun anyway :D
