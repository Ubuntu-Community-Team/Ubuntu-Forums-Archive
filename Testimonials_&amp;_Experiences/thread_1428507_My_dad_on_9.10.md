### Post by sheem on 2010-03-12
Quick storey, moved my dad from xp to Karmic on his old IBM desktop. Took 30 mins to install and setup.(won't do compiz) No install issues at all, everything worked "out of the box" as they say. Gave him a basic overview on his main programs, firefox and Thunderbird. Gave him the lowdown on open office, made sure his Epson printer worked and let him loose. Its been about a month now and zero issues. He has had no problems converting from XP. It's business as usual for the old guy.
OH yeah, he is 81 years old!!
