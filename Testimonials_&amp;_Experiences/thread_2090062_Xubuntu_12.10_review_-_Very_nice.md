### Post by LewisTM on 2012-12-03
Nice review!
A few pointers for you:

- You can configure the action buttons plugin (which shows your name at the top right) to show Restart and a number of other actions. Just right-click it.

- The days of Thunar not supporting remote bookmarks will be over soon. You can already toy with the [development version]("http://www.webupd8.org/2012/11/install-thunar-with-tabs-support-in.html") which works well in Xubuntu 12.04-12.10

- One thing you missed: The messaging indicator is no longer available in Xubuntu 12.10. I was able to replicate its function with Thunderbird's Firetray addon.

- Xfce 4.10 now offers automatic window tiling, a welcome and much anticipated feature. Go to Settings Manager -> Window Manager Tweaks -> Accessibility -> Automatically tile windows... Unlike Compiz, it actually tiles the windows in real time as you move them around.

Cheers!

### Post by Tamlynmac on 2012-12-04
> mastablasta
the point of review was that function should be there by default and it is missing.

I  would agree in principle, but if it's easily customizable the default  is not that critical. Historically, many of the customizations I prefer  have never been default.

> but basic functions every user needs should be there by default.

Since  Xubuntu is so easily customizable, the default only really applies to  those who prefer OOTB. As a minimalists, I immediately turn off the  bottom panel and don't use desktop icons. It's just easier (for me) to  maintain - a simple, clean desktop with open space for multitasking.  Rarely, do I have less than 4 or 5 apps running and with the top task  bar it's easy to switch back & forth.

But that's just my  preference, while others may prefer/enjoy the bottom panel. The ease of  customization is (for me) critical and IMHO Xubuntu meets that  requirement. In my mind the default may apply to some, but not  necessarily to all. I believe the key is to make it easy to customize  and then the defaults become irreverent. 

However, I do recognize the value of OOTB defaults for new users.

Just my $0.02
