### Post by Selbstmord on 2006-03-24
I've been using Ubuntu for about a month now and am very pleased, although I had/have some trouble getting Fluxbox to work they way I want it to, I find Ubuntu very pleasing. So pleasing that I ordered for 5 cds to be shipped out for me, and I got them today, it took around 2 weeks, which I think is pretty fast, considering I live in Sweden... Anyways, these CDs give me and people who've seen them a very professional feel that puts one at ease in thinking Ubuntu is not another one of those linux distros that'll die out eventually, and I love how there's plenty of support/how-to's available on the net, should you encounter any trouble. I ran Fedora Core since the first release before trying Ubuntu, and as I've said, I'm very pleased. The only thing I can really whine about is the incompatibility with some music-related applications, such as Hydrogen and Rosegarden, but other than that, it's working terrific, and I've already given away 3 of the 5 cds that were sent to me.

Cheers for Ubuntu :)
