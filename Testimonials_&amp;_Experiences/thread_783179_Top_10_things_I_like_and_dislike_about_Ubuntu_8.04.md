### Post by hawksbill on 2008-05-05
Top 10 things I like and dislike about Ubuntu 8.04

1.Fast and stable
2.Gnome and how it is used has come a long way (very clean and organized)
3.Customizable
4.Finally proprietary drivers have been included
5.Detected and installed appropriate drivers for every piece of hardware installed out of the box!
6.Compiz is a nice touch
7.more polished than past releases
8.omg my wireless network worked out of the box! (not to mention much more stable than windows no dropped connections...etc)
9.great base of applications included, many of which have improved dramatically preventing me from adding third party applications).
10.its plain sexy!

10 things I would like to see changed or improved.

1.Samba is still a pain to set up.
2.Discrepancies between nvidia control panel and the x-server control panel for display settings.
3.An easier way to manage my storage media (like formatting, mounting and unmounting drives)
4.The sound system although drastically improved still seems buggy
5.Where did my gorilla theme go? :)
6.Why not include wine right off the bat?
7.Well I can't think of anything else for 7-10

I have to say this is the best release to date. I abandoned windows for my personal work station a long time ago.  I cannot see why Ubuntu isn't ready for mainstream users other than compatibility. And as far as that goes there is a replacement for just about every windows application in the Ubuntu repositories.     I really hope that the Ubuntu community stays on its current path. Ubuntu shows us just how well things can be done when everyone works together. Well done and cheers on an excellent Operating system.
