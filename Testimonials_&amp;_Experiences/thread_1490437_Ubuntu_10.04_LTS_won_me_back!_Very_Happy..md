### Post by tuxtorch on 2010-05-22
So after 9.10 Karmic, I switched to PCLinuxOS for about a month or two, I liked the rolling release until an update blew my system out of the water and I couldn't even boot into my system! So I downloaded 10.04 again from my wife's laptop, reinstalled and obviously the first mistake last time installing this version I didn't look closely at the Nvidia Driver Recommendation. I thought it said to use 173 last time but it didn't. That was my mistake since I just got a newer computer and I new Nvidia card. Doh!

Anyway I'm back with Ubuntu, and will stay here, whatever changes gets made that I don't like I have the ability to change it myself.

Thank you Ubuntu for being so stable, and friendly. If it wasn't for my bone headed mistake with the graphics card the first time I most likely would not have gone to PCLinuxOS.
