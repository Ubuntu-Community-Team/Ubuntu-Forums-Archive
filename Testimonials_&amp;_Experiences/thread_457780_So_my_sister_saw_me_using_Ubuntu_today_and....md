### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-05-30
More precisely, it's because you're running the 2.6.20-15 or 2.5.20-16 Linux kernel. Earlier versions handled multitasking better than Mac or Windows, but these new kernels just blow the others away in terms of responsiveness. Amazing, powerful stuff - thanks to open-source!
