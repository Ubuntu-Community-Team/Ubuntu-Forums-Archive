### Post by BlackJavaBean on 2008-03-14
I've used Ubuntu since Dapper LTS. What else can I say, but it is one of my favorite distros. I remember the good ol' days of searching through these forums for how to get my stinkin' BCM4318 wireless card to work or how to keep beryl from crashing. But these forums are what kept me with Ubuntu, this has got to be one of the best communities out there!
I switched to Fedora8 since my job uses its sister RHEL, but I still pack my 7.04 liveCD!
Ubuntu is like my first car, yeah - I use Fedora or openSUSE but there are time when I miss my first (well, close to first) Linux.
