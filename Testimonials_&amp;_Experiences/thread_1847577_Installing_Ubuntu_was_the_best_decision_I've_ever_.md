### Post by karen729 on 2011-09-22
Ex-Windows user here, too. I just plain got fed up with anything and everything remotely related to Windows. I spent more time trying to make it work securely than actually using it.
Since switching to Ubuntu, I have time to actually do things that I want to do with my computer. Now I'm working on switching my two daughters over to Ubuntu.

I like to tell new Ubuntu users what I tell myself: Try not to become overly discouraged and/or frustrated while learning and first starting out. "Rome wasn't built in a day." Remembering the hours, days, weeks, and years spent learning Windows helps to put things in perspective. I think of learning as 'broadening our horizons'. The more we know, the better off we are.

Welcome to the Ubuntu community and good luck!
