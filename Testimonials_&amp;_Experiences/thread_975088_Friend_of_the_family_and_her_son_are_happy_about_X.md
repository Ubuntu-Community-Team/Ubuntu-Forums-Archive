### Post by wandalalakers on 2008-11-08
A girlfriend has a HP desktop that came with Windows ME.  I installed Windows 2000 on this PC a while ago.  Some tax software she has needed Windows XP.  I told her XP would slow her pc down.  She went ahead and installed XP anyway.  The next time I went over her house, her son was saying the computer is slow.  I started to say, "I told you XP would slow your PC down".  She brought the computer over to my house and I said to my self, "I am not reinstalling Windows 2000 on this thing".  Since her pc didn't come with Windows 2000, I would have to reinstall everything from scratch and I didn't use ghost when I installed 2000 because her cd burner was broken and that probably would have taken a million disks.  I tried to install linux mint 3.0 xfce but her PC didn't seem to like booting from the disc.  I ended up putting Xubuntu 7.04 on her desktop.  She and her son are happy.  Her son says the Internet is so fast.  I think I have a new linux convert.  At least I made an old PC faster.  I even emailed her the Xubuntu pdf user's manual.  I also burned a Ubuntu 8.04 cd for a user at work.  I booted up a 8.04 live cd and I instantly won him over.
