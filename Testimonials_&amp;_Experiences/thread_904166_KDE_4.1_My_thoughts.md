### Post by Crafty Kisses on 2008-08-29
So just recently I decided to try Kubuntu using KDE 4.  I have to say a lot better than KDE 3, the desktop effects work a lot more smoother and  I really like the whole "Widget" feature, even though you can do that in GNOME and Ubuntu easily, I like how it's kinda built it by default on the desktop.

I did have a couple of problems for some odd reason anytime I replaced Compiz for the window manager I couldn't have more than 2 desktops and X restarted a few times for reasons unknown, I checked my error logs nothing seemed to be out of the ordinary. I also noticed in Konqueror all the login boxes are pretty weird, they are off color and what not, but that was easily fixed by using FireFox.

All in all it's OK I guess, I can't really use my effects to full potential, but nonetheless it's a good upgrade from KDE 3, and if you like the KDE interface I'd say at least try it and see how you like it. My thoughts on it is, it's a work in progress. I'm impressed with some things though, and disappointed with others.

Remember though I installed the kde-core package using Ubuntu, so that could be one of the issues too, I'm not natively using Kubuntu. Like I said though try it and see if you have better luck than me with the internet and desktop effects.

I also wanted to say I had a old wireless card that wasn't getting detected on KDE 3, but now  it does in KDE 4 for some strange reason, just thought I'd let you guys know about that as well. What suprised me was it worked out of the box, so that was really awesome and slick.

The last thing is, the new look is really awesome I'm likng the default wallpaper and the slick panels, I'm really liking those. As of right now I'm still kind of skeptical about using it, but KDE 4 is not done, in my book it's too early to judge, so I guess only time can tell.

Overall I give KDE 4 as of now: **7/10**
