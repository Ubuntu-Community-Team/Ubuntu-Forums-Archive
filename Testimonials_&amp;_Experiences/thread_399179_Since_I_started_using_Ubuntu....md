### Post by quagee on 2007-04-02
I have used Ubuntu for a little less than a year and I am quite happy with it.  At first I was just curious about all the hype surrounding 6.06.  Hearing it from diggnation and TWIT.  They had nothing but good to say about it, of course!!  So one day I decided to give it a try.  I had an old laptop sitting around with XP on it and did what some would call the unthinkable and erased the hdd and replaced it with ubuntu.  To make a long story short it worked great.  Most everything worked even the wireless after bootup.  I then started playing around with the OS and found it to be a wonderful alternative to XP so much so that I started a podcast around August of 2006 to increase the word about ubuntu and hopefully spread the adoption of it.  Though, it would be likely that had I not started a podcast people would still migrate to it by the droves because it is that good!  Needless to say that I am sold out to ubuntu.  If any of you are listeners thank you for subscribing and helping to make it a success. It is based on the Pligg CMS and gives the user the ability to vote on the content that is most useful or relevent to them at the time.
