### Post by eheyl on 2007-10-24
I've been dual booting Mepis and winxp for a few months now. I primarily kept windows for my wifes ipod charge capabilities. She was devout windows but I had her try mepis and she loved it. Still, there were some things that I didn't like (for instance kde) and was considering trying ubuntu. I waited till 7.10 was out and tried a live cd. I also figured out how to get her ipod to charge. So, as of monday night I wiped the drive and am now solely using ubuntu. No bad viruses, no more "WGA" crap. no more windows constantly killing my drive space with updates. The only thing I've get to get working right is the back speakers I can have true 5.1 surround sound (thats in another thread). Its quick, stable, beautiful to look at and FAST.

Long live Ubuntu!!:guitar:
