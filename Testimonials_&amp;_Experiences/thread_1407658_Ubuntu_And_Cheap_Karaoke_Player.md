### Post by wandalalakers on 2010-02-15
If you have an old laptop lying around, try installing Pykaraoke on it and download some MP3+G songs.  This makes a very nice and fast Karaoke player.
You can also purchase a PA system online to complete your karaoke outfit.
You can get PA systems for around $200.  After that, purchase some wired or wireless mikes.
