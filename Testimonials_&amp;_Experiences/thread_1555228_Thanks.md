### Post by cloyd on 2010-08-17
I don't know where else to post this, but I wanted to just says "thanks" to the folks who have jumped in and helped me from time to time. If there is a better place to post it, let me know,  or just move it there. I really want to thank the community. I also want to say thanks for the times when no one jumped in, or jumped in when I had more or less solved it myself. I think some of you had seen my posts, and thought I could or should figure it out for myself. I didn't learn as much about windows in years as I've learned in Ubuntu is the last 9 to 10 months.

 The community is great, and the support is the best. I have yet to be told, "just restart the computer," or "re-install the software or driver". I have seen some posts where someone was told to reinstall ubuntu, but that is not quite the task it is in Windows. (This morning, I was talking to my son who said "I'm going to have to reinstall Vista."  My comment was, "Have fun. I can reinstall Ubuntu is 20 minutes or less.").

Thanks again. I appreciate all the people who have taken time to help me.
