So Far I have not had any problems on my newer machine ( three years old) I have a couple of slightly older more stock machines that it wont seem to run on, but I still have 8.04 for those two.

Kinda confused why it doesn't keep the hard drives mounted on the desktop? so for right now I have to remount them ( just go to places and single click on them) every time I restart the machine.

Over all on the one machine 8.10 runs on. it seems to run ok.
