### Post by AlphaLexman on 2009-11-16
My favorite reason to use xfce is the window manager.  I have dual screens, and xubuntu will give me the EASY option to have seperate wallpaper, and even seperate screensavers on each monitor. If gnome or KDE can do this, its not working for me. BTW I have all three installed on my machine. I truthfully don't use xfce for it's lightweight features, because my machine has lots of muscle. However there are little things I like about each window manager. For example the gnome terminal is the only emulated terminal I can use my wheel mouse to scroll man pages.
