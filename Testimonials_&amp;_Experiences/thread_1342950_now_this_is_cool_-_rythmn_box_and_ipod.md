### Post by gatorbrit on 2009-12-01
I just plugged in my old 30GB ipod with a ton of songs on it and I can play it through my computer using rhythm box.  I thought RB just allowed you to sync music and play stuff that was actually on your PC, not stuff on the ipod.  Of course, clever linux just treats my ipod as an external HD with music on it.  This is so cool - I don't have to load the songs on to the PC.

Ubuntu continues to delight me!:KS
