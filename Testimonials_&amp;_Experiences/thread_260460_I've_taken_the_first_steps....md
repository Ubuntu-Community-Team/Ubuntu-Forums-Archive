### Post by GregB on 2006-09-25
Well, it's one week and counting.  I'm learning alot...well, not alot, but I AM learning.  I've come to the conclusion that Ubuntu is AWESOME.  Right now, I'm updating my Boy Scout Troops website by editing and uploading the new files...all in Ubuntu!  That's something that one week ago I didn't think would be possible.

I know, all of these things are simple things that some of you do every day, but all of this has helped me to come to a conclusion:

Ubuntu is indeed a competent OS.  While it may be intimidating at first,  anyone CAN learn to use it, just as they learned to use Windows.  Even though things in Windows are second nature to me now, I've realized (as many need to) that Ubuntu, or Linux in general, seems difficult because it's not second nature; it's something new just as Windows was oh so many years ago.  Thinking back...those first few weeks of Windows weren't easy either!

I'm going to keep working at this, and I've set a deadline to get rid of Windows completely off of this box within six months...something I'm looking forward to!
