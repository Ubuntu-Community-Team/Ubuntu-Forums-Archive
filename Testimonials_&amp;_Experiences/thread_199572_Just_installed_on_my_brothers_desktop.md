### Post by drizek on 2006-06-19
Hes been using dapper for a while now, since about february, cause he wanted xgl/compiz. Obviously, his system was pretty broken by the time dapper was released and he wanted me to install windows for him again. I convinced him to stick with kubuntu and I did a fresh install of dapper. 

His hardware is:

Compaq desktop
AMD 3200
512mb ram
17" lcd
ATI 9600xt
integrated audio

On first boot, the lcd was set at the correct resolution, the computer was connected to the internet, the speakers were working, I had full 3d acceleration(on a new ATI card!) and the mouse/keyboard worked just fine. I honestly cannot find a single fault. In just 20 minutes his computer was totally set up with everything working the way it was supposed to. 

The only thing thats giving me a bit of trouble is the printer, but ive used it many times in linux before and it didnt cause problems. Right now the printer itself is complaining of paper jams(or something) so it isnt cooperating too well. Ill figure it out later...

Anyway, very happy with ubuntu. Im SO glad i didnt spend 2-3 hours trying to setup XP only to have it break in another month or two.
