### Post by Riffer on 2008-02-17
[SIZE="2"]Well I did it.  Got rid of windows and repartitioned my HD.  And it was dammed easy.  

I just used the partitioner in the install, chose manual   partitioning and away I went.  All the tools were there, straight forward and easy to use, with guided help that pretty much made sense.  I'm once again impressed.

For this install I chose LinuxMint for reasons that are not all that clear to me LOL .  It went in well, but the Envy that came preinstalled was an older version that didn't get the most up to date nVidia driver, easy fix though.  AWN only paritally worked (couldn't add any apps through the launcher), no big deal just added stuff to the panel.  And evolution once I installed the proper plugin restored all the data from my old install, quite slick.

I must say this was one of the easiest complete re-install that I've ever done.  Well Done Ubuntu and the guys at LinuxMint. [/SIZE]
