### Post by cwbar_1 on 2008-01-27
First let me say that I am not totally new to Ubuntu.  My first experience came w/6.06.  I moved up in versions until I got to 7.10. (HP and 7.10 don't get along)  I knew better than to even try 7.10, having read all the posts concerning the issues.  With that said, I tried several other distros, each with their own particular issues with my HP laptop.  I guess with the experience gained with trying to get others to work, I decided to heed the advice of this forum and go back to "Fiesty."    I suppose this installation will be my last, unless 8.04 proves to be "great!"  With the way I have my lappy configured now, I see no reason to ever want to look at others again.  After almost 2 years of skipping around, my system is perfect!!!!!!!!
   Many thanks to ALL the great posters and to the Ubuntu Team!:)
