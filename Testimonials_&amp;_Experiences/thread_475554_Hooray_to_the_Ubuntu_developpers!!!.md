### Post by acadiansteph on 2007-06-16
Well I'm happy and extremely satisfied with Ubuntu 7.04.  I've been using it for about  3 month or so.  
When I started with Ubuntu, I thought to myself: Where the heck is my C: drive:confused: (former Windows user for 13 years)? Then quickly learned everything is a file system, including CD-Rom. I have to thank the Ubuntu devs and maintainers in giving me a good, reliable, fun, esthetically pleasing free operating system. I am by no means an expert in the Terminal, but I'm learning slowly and gaining valuable info in the wikis and how-tos. No viruses, adware, spyware: Ahh, OS heaven. And with no M$ and BS on this machine, Bill G. company is no longer acting like Big brother with me anymore. Free at last!!!:D. I've got control over my OS, hey! Well, in closing everything is positive. Got wireless, 3d acceleration, DVD playback working, burned a couple of CD's. Hope most Ubuntu users have similar positive experiences.


P.S I just wish I had known about Linux in the early 90's before going with M$
