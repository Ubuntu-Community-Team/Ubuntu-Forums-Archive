### Post by mellym on 2010-03-13
I've been lurking the past several weeks, while learning Ubuntu, knowing that I could sign up and ask for help if I needed it. I've read several people that were unsuccessful, and who went on tangents, some of them bashing the free help they've received. Some of them bashing a (free ) OS, some of them just lashing out at the closest target.

While I do think bash is pretty awesome, bashing is not.

While my install was flawless, which shocked the hell out of me...it's never easy in Windows, rebooting after every update is enough to make me scream. I did have problems, but won't list them...no point as they were solved simply by reading the forums. 

I am not a geekm or don't think I am, more curious than anything else...but I did it, and am very pleased with the result. Up t a few weeks ago, I never knew a choice existed, other than the outrageously expensive Mac. A friend I game with told me aout it, and I was curious, so he mailed me a copy Karmic, I've never ad more fun with a computer. 

I thought, that with all of the unhappy posts, a happy one would be a welcome relief. 

If someone with virtually no skills beyond using a computer for everyday things can learn all she needs to know, plus more, simply by experimenting and reading these forums...anyone can. For those who are willing to quit because it's easier than solving problems, then come on here and whine about something you got for free, not working like you think it should, remember that Linux isn't Windows. 

Now the only thing I need to figure out is what to do with my Windows disk!
