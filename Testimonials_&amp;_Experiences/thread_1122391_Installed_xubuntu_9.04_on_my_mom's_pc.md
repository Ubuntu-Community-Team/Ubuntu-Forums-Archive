### Post by walter_j on 2009-04-11
I installed xubuntu 9.04 beta on my mom's pc. She was so worried about viruses. Xubunto installed w/o problem, and to my surprise - imported all her data and repartitioned the HD without destroying XP. I didn't know linux could repartition like this! I had to import outlook express into thunderbird, but it went well. I showed her that firefox and thunderbird were her email and browser, and how to shut down: thats it. She's been checking her email and browsing the web now and hasn't had any problems. She was set to give away the pc, since she didn't want to worry about viruses: the antivirus software was set to expire and was giving her all kinds of warnings. She's 81 years old and worries too much. She uses it to keep in contact with family scattered all over the province...

Even though xubuntu is a beta version, it seems rock solid to me. I use ubuntu 8.10 and am looking forward to 9.04. The xubuntu team's doing great work.

Walter
