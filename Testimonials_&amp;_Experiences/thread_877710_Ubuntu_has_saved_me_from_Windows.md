### Post by collinp on 2008-08-02
I used to think that the errors that Windows bucked up daily was a fact of life in the computing world. That was before i found a Ubuntu cd laying around in a store, 1 1/2 years ago. I booted it up, made a second partition for it, installed it, and after that i was hooked. It was bumpy at first because i knew next to nothing about Ubuntu at the time, but it got better after a few days. I had deleted my windows partition 2 months after finding the ubuntu cd. Now, look at me. I am learning how to script and code in Bash, Python, and HTML, I know how to do almost anything with ubuntu and where to get things at. Overall i love Ubuntu, and i plan on sticking with it for a very, very long time.
