Chinese is showing up mis-encoded at random, files look significantly different once she's sent them to colleagues using Microsoft Office, and running the presentation fullscreen on her own computer causes mis-displays like table lines not showing up. So… she curses a lot. I'm not convinced OpenOffice would be any better, though -- at least LibreOffice has the advantage of being a little faster…
