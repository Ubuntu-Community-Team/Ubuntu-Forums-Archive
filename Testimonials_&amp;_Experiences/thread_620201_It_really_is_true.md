### Post by Bandicoot on 2007-11-22
When I read all the buzz about Ubuntu I was sceptical and thought that the promises couldn't be true. I've used windows since 3.1, and had a little sidestep to Linux some years ago when LILO was still hot ( and that was not a very memorable experience).

Still .. not being satisfied with Vista (the ouch starts now in my experience) I decided to give Ubuntu 7.10 a try .. what an eyeopener it was !! The install only took about 25 minutes, 30 minutes including the downloading of the latest updates, I could not believe I had a working sytem in half an hour ...no downloading of endless security updates patches no scavaging the net for hours to find the right drivers or programs I needed.

Sure to make everything the way I wanted it to be it took me hours and hours of reading this and other forums (it's hard to get rid of the windows way of thinking) but it never felt like a burden and put a smile on my face every time I achieved something new. I still have a long way to go but I allready have the feeling that I control the OS and not the other way around (like I allways had with Windows).

You haven't seen any posts from me, because all the questions I had were allready answered here in the forums, which brings me to another point : the will to help other people is simply amazing in the Ubuntu community .. I feel I have finally found my way home !

I could go on and on about what I find so great about Ubuntu, but I think I'll just and with : Ubuntu made from a sceptic into a believer !
