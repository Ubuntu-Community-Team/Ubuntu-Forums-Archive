### Post by yourwhiteshadow on 2011-02-11
A little over a year ago, a friend gave me a super OLD laptop with windows xp on it, a celeron, and only 512 mb ram. i couldn't install XP cuz the CD drive was broken, and to be honest, i really didn't want to.

i gave ubuntu netbook a try, and LOVED it. 

then...i bought a MBP. i like it and all, but it was super expensive, and its not super fast for the price i paid. anyways...

recently, i thought i'd make some good use of an old computer laying around. P4 3.0 ghz, 512 mb ram, installed ubuntu server with SSH. its awesome and headless right now, so i just sit from my other desk and play around with it. i've recently installed server software for team fortress 2 and counter strike source, but no one seems to use my server, even i don't use my server. however, i'd like to do something cool with it, mostly something productive (for me, or some other organization), even if its like F@H (but I already have a folding machine). so, any ideas?

edit: so i decided to install 10.10 on an old laptop we had laying around, its an amd turion 64 single core with a 2.0 ghz single core processor (ML-37). runs very well. super fast and its practically revived this OLD, otherwise useless computer. now my mom can youtube, email, etc on this. awesome. i'm having ubuntugasms. i'm now also considering installing this side by side with windows 7. the only thing is all my game are on win 7 and i do after effects, PS, premier pro, and all those. otherwise i'd switch in a heartbeat.
