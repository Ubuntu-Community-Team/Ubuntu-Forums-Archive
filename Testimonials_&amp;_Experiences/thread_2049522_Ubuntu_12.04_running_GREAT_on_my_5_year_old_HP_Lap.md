### Post by exploder on 2012-09-02
I had hardware issues with quite a few earlier Ubuntu releases but 12.04 has worked well on every machine I have loaded it on. I had a little trouble with the graphics driver on my HP DV6 laptop but I did not really have to jump through any hoops to get it working.

I like how Unity is looking and I have seen the refinements being made in 12.10. I plan on staying with the LTS but I figure by the time the next LTS rolls around Unity is going to be a lot nicer. 

I have been a Linux user for many years and I think Unity adds some excitement to the mix of Linux distributions. I appreciate good work in many distributions, PCLinuxOS because it is such a good rolling release, Linux Mint because of the Cinnamon desktop environment and Ubuntu because they took their own direction and are developing Unity.

I like quality and choice and I see some really great choices these days. Gnome 3 is the best thing that ever came along because so many distributions are developing their own shells and making their own choices on how things are being built. I think that we are seeing some of the most exciting things happening in the Linux world right now and it is great!
