### Post by marl30 on 2010-10-05
I got introduced to Ubuntu and Linux by Karmic. Got spellbound by Lucid, which caused me to dump Windows 7 for Ubuntu. Now Maverick has certainly left me thinking that I'll most likely be using Ubuntu for years to come.

Maverick is just awesome! Everything just works! Not one issue here! If you think Lucid was great looking, wait until you see Maverick. :D

It's a bit faster than Lucid as well. I attribute a great deal of the speed to the new Kernel. I had this new Kernel on Lucid and was getting almost the same speed; however, Maverick is still a bit snappier. 

The Software Center is so gorgeous! I think the only thing missing in it is a rating system, and the ability to read and add comments like in Linux Mint. Besides that, everything else in the software center is just perfect. 

So far, my experience with Ubuntu has been getting better with every release. I hope I'll start seeing just positives from others who share their experience in this section after they try Maverick.
