### Post by spyke01 on 2006-06-05
Hey everybody, i began using Ubuntu at the beginning of breezy, and had been waiting patiently for dapper, and i have to say that it was well worth it. 

My upgrade process wasn't the greatest, i tried a dist upgrade that killed my xserver and menu.lst file so i had to fix the menu.lst download the server install cd and wipe the 2 partitions i had been using for breezy. After that, things went pretty smoothly, i had been hoping for the new graphical interface, but text is ok for me. Got everything going so far, and am working on nvidia drivers.

Dapper is great, i like the look and feel, and its overall more organized and stable(haven't pushed it yet). I like the extra programs like clustering and the look of the controls. Screensavers are great as is the new icons and splash screen. I tried out the gdm themes(i believe theyre new) and they were pretty nice, ending up customizing everything and using a different one i got from gnome-look.org

I just want to say thanks to all the developers and those who helped to finish Dapper, can wait for edgy. I hope that its features will be designed to rival micro$oft's vista. I would completely stop using XP if i didnt need Photoshop(sorry Gimpers gotta keep it).

I also want to say thanks for finally using ship-it for all the distros not just Ubuntu, its a shame to see the larger quanities go, i always get a lot to pass them out to friends and coworkers, sometimes even strangers(of course i keep some for myself as well).

Anyways thats my rant, Thanks for providing for tommorow today.
