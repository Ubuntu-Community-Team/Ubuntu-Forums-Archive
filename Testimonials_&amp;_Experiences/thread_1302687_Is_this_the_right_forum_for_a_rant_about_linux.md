### Post by running_rabbit07 on 2009-10-29
I guess the biggest problem with ranters, is that they try to pass blame, instead of saying, "Hey, Why does this work this way?" And, to start multiple rant threads like the OP is planning, is not being productive, unless his intention is to start flame wars.
