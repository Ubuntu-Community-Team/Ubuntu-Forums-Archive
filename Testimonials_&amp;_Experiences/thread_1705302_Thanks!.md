### Post by ogresav on 2011-03-12
I love ubuntu, got me into the linux world years ago, and I haven't left since. I couldn't imagine studying computer science without linux, and now I can imagine a world without Micro$oft. See there? I used a dollar sign instead of an S in Micro$oft. Whoops I did it again. That means I use linux and that I think they are an evil greedy corporation.

But seriously, I have been using this free, open-source operating system for a long time now, and I just realized it is time to say "thank you" to all those who helped develop and maintain it. That "thank you" should be extended to all the good people who work on debian, and the linux kernel as well. Someday I really do aspire to contribute to open-source projects such as those, myself, but for now I will study C code, x86, and computer architecture. All in the linux environment, and mostly in Ubuntu (i wouldn't be a real linux enthusiast if I didn't switch flavors every once in a while. It's not cheating I swear. If anything it helps me remember how much I love you.)

THANKS for an amazing operating system!
