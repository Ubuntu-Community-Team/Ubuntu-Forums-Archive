### Post by MattDTownsend on 2007-06-27
If you're still having trouble with your camera, try switching USB modes on the camera itself.  Make sure it's on PTP (should be recognized and an import dialogue should come up as soon as you plug it in) or DISK (will automatically mount it as an external disk, like a thumb drive).  Your camera almost certainly has disk mode, and if all other methods fail, you'll be able to import your photos by drag-and-drop.
