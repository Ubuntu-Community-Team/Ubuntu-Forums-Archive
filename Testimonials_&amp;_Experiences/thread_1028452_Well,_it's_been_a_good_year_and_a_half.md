### Post by blakjesus on 2009-01-02
I have been using Ubuntu for about a year and a half. I have to admit, it was an amazing experience after switching from Windows. You guys have done an excellent job at making GNU/Linux ready for the everyday person, and i had a very enjoyable experience using it.

But, sadly, I have to move on. I find myself in need of very important proprietary software that i just can't manage to find good alternatives to. I will be moving on to OS X and look forward to using the amazing FOSS apps that i have found to be quite useful in Ubuntu.

This is not the end though. I will continue to monitor the progress that Ubuntu and GNU/Linux in general will make. It's amazing how thee open source community came together to make such an excellent OS.
