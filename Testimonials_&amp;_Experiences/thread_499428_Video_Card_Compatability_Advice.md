### Post by mkuzava on 2007-07-12
After trying to install Ubuntu on my Dell laptop with a Radeon Mobile video card and discovering it was basically incompatible, I just wanted to let people looking to switch to Ubuntu know that I decided to build a new and very cheap (whole thing ran me about $600) computer.  I recommend using the Asus GeForce 7600GS EN600GS Silent.  It was quite cheap (less than 100 dollars), extremely quiet (I recommend that your case have a decent number of fans since this card uses a large heatsink instead of a fan) and is surprisingly fast for the money.  Just trying to offer some helpful advice to any new users who encountered the same problems I did.

Matthew

P.S. Loving every minute of Ubuntu, I've now stripped my Dell bare of all programs save Word and Mozilla and strictly use it for taking notes in class :)
