### Post by Jellicletrb on 2007-03-24
I had been wondering for quite a while about everything I'd heard about the Mac, and wanted to try one, but just didn't want to up the dollars for one.  But, I finally decided that a Mac Mini was within my budget, and ordered one direct from Apple.

It showed up in only 3 days, packed in the absolutely cutest box ever (it looked sort of like a big pet rock)...and I used it for 2 months.  

I didn't like it.

The box is fantastic, it almost get lost on the desk.  But apparently I'll never be Mac OS X people.  I can't really point out anything bad about the OS, I just wasn't feeling it  :(   

So, I came here, did some reading and research (I LOVE the search feature, search is better than fried bologna and whipped cream), and tonight I installed Edgy on the little Mini.  It's the mostest cutest little Edgy box ever, and everything works, every the wireless and bluetooth.  

I'm so happy :KS
