### Post by wadelewis4 on 2008-05-12
Just wanted to say that no OS is perfect. (but Ubuntu comes the closest)

Lots of posts lately about how people are leaving Ubuntu and how disappointed they are with Hardy. The funny thing is a lot of people don't specify problems they are having &/or don't wait for people to respond to their posts. Ubuntu users aren't clairvoyant - help will come when someone reads your post who can identify with the particular issue and direct you a helpful way. It will come - just be patient. 

Anyway, just wanted to say THANK YOU to the Ubuntu Development Team - for all the work you've done and continue to do, I certainly appreciate it! 

And another big THANK YOU to all of the Ubuntu Community for both posting newly experienced issues and taking the time to help others. 

I'm having a blast using Ubuntu - Never looking back.
