### Post by ventrical on 2010-09-05
Hi all,

  I installed Ubuntu 10.4 Lucid on a Compaq DeskPro-550MHz/384MBRAM and it shows a much faster response time than Windows XP (SP2). The start up is very fast as well as the shut down. The only  noticable  downside is that the video compiz and effects cannot be  enabled, but I plan to try another video card.

The Lubuntu works a little bit better than Knoppix on even some of the older P3 with 256MBs and I reall like the Osmo Personal Organizer which I think is a good feature in and of itself. so one can use a derelict PC as a committed workstation for Osmo and music CDs etc..

Still, I have yet to see a Linux program that can trump Fedora 13.

great work
