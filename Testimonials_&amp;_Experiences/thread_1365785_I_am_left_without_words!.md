### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2009-12-27
My daughter had an old HP psc 2210 all-in-one printer-fax-copier-scanner that was not being used. It has a flatbed scanner. 

I have an old flatbed scanner that has not worked since Microsoft moved from XP to Vista. With its proprietary interface it will not work with Linux either.

I checked the HP website, and found Linux drivers posted in February 2009 for the HP 2210. I thought, what the heck, I'll give it a go.  So, I cleaned all the gray dust off it, getting that gray dust all over my cloths in the process. I found a power cable and a USB cable in my junk box.

I hooked the thing up expecting to experience the necessity to download the drivers, and troubleshoot other problems. 

I am totally left without words! The darn thing justs worked without doing anything to the computer using Ubuntu 9.10. 

Did I say, **I am totally left without words!**
