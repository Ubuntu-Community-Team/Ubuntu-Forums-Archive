### Post by PocketDog on 2010-05-09
I've been reading about problem after problem after people have upgraded to 10.04. After having a trouble-free life with Hardy, I upgraded to Karmic after much soul-searching (I loved Hardy) and had no problems at all. I wanted to be able to fix something!
My ATI card worked perfectly, my RTL8187B wireless card connects first time, every time.
My touchpad supports 2-fingered scrolling, my webcam works, hell, I can even use my Sony Ericsson W890i as a modem *just by plugging it in*. Call that fun?

So I did a dist-upgrade to Lucid. I was prepared. I was ready. I watched in feverish anticipation as 751MB of hell downloaded itself into my life. And what happened?

My Huwei O2 broadband modem started to work properly, that's what; I didn't have to eject it and plug it back in. Compiz didn't even get broken. Grub2 was in order. My webcam works. Lucid even fixed my wireless switch, so it turns wireless off when it's off.

I wanted something to be broken so I could fix it!

Other people get all the luck :'(
