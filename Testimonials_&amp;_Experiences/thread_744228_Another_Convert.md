### Post by Greglu on 2008-04-03
Wow, congratulations and kudos to all involved.  What an awesome experience, Ubuntu just works ... eventually. I have converted all my home machines, 6 in total - I will be doing my office machines in the near future  - over 20 computers.  What an impressive piece of work.  It took me a week and 6 or 7 reinstalls to learn and get things working the way I needed with 7.10. It then took only 40 minutes a computer to be up and running. It takes practically a day or more to install a Vista machine and get it configured the way one needs it. The amount of reclaimed hard drive space is great and the compiz special effects are just awesome to name but two of many, many awesome things about Ubuntu.

It would be great if there were a really simple way to create iso images of each computers working config that could be burned to disc - so a complete re-build would be easier, I am sure there must be???

One last thing, the forums provide stellar support.

Cheers and thanks
Greg
