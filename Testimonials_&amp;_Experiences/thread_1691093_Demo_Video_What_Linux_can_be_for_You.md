### Post by Halzen on 2011-02-19
I put together a short demonstration video showing off what my desktop looks and feels like, and what it is capable of in an average day's use. Various features like Compiz and the GLX Cairo Dock are displayed, along with WINE and several other apps/utilities.

The point of this is that I want to try and knock down the stereotypes that Linux is just some bulky software pile that takes a powernerd to handle. With a little bit of configuration and patience, Linux can become the most beautiful and functional OS you will ever see or use. It certainly has become that for me. :)

The video is available in full-screen HD, so don't be shy: [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UpSGKETros](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8UpSGKETros)
