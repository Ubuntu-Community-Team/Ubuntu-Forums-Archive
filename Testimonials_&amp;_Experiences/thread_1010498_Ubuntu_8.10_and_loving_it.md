### Post by vgrisham on 2008-12-14
An added note for the OP from Middle Tennessee (gotta nice ring)...

There is another solution to KDE vs. Gnome--have both at the same time.

All you have to do is pop a terminal and code:
> sudo apt-get install kubuntu-desktop

It'll install and from then on you can choose which desktop environment to log into (click Choose Session from the login screen's lower left hand corner). Then you can be gnome or kde depending upon your mood. 

Now that's something you can't do with either a Mac or Windows.
