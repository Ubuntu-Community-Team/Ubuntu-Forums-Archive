Well Ibex keeps locking up on me so on a spare drive I have installed Jaunty with the ext4 file system.

Boot time is down by a massive 25 seconds which is impressive. Everything works out of the box and so far it all seems rock solid and stable.

The as and when I tried different media the system downloaded the appropriate drivers. Only the realplayer audio codec is being persistent in it's absence.

DVD's play but need a nudge on the slider to get moving. I have seen others are having the same issue there.

One question from me though re my GPU card. It's a radeon X300 (ATI) Am I correct in understanding what I have read that my card is amongst a family of cards for which there will be no propriety drivers?

I did try installing the ATI drivers but got no further than a corrupt boot screen :(

If this is the case what reasonably priced replacement do you guys suggest? The slot is a PCIE X16 and I am considering changing to an NVIDIA type card as the 'composite' behaviour is supposed too be better.
