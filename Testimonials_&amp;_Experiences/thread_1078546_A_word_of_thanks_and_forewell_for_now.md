### Post by RVA Dennis on 2009-02-23
Well, after two fresh reinatallations and the attendant time/effort spent to attempt to configure a workable Ubuntu platform, I have decided the time has come to (sigh) return to XP and abandon this project for now.

There are three main reasons for this but the primary one is the issue of fragility. Installing updates and components such as WINE and dosBox have twice crashed Ubuntu leaving me in a maze of trying to resolve what should be a very basic, simple fix. The second is the issue of running an external monitor on my laptop which works perfectly in XP but has been a timeconsuming, frustrating project in 8.10 that I haven't come close to resolving. Third and perhaps the most puzzling is the resolution of the screen and rendering of fonts and webpages, which despite protracted tinkering, look "fuzzy" or "smeary" and are extremely hard on these old eyes of mine.


On the positive side, there are many things I enjoyed about 8.10 but since this is a "business" proposition for me I shall have to wait until such a time as I add a second computer I can dedicate to Ubuntu whilst doing my day to day work in XP since this is a bread and butter issue.

I want to extend my thanks and gratitude to all those who responded to my previous requests for assistance. Your suggestions were invaluable.

I'll be popping in occassionally to check on progress with Jaunty and gain some further foresight into the possibility of installing and running at a future date.

Dennis
