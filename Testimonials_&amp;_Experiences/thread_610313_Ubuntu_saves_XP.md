### Post by santiagoward2000 on 2007-11-11
HI!
I know there are probably lots of similar stories, but I wanted to share mine (my brother's actually).
About a week ago, his computer stopped working. When he turned it on, all he got was a black screen with a white dash on the corner.
So I told him to try with Ubuntu. He downloaded it from his girlfriend's computer. First, all he wanted to do was to backup his files before formatting the disk. It's and HP Compaq, so he tried to use the Recovery partition, but it wouldn't start, saying there was a file missing.
So he got a Windows CD from a friend and installed it. It worked well, but now he had no drivers for the LAN card, so he couldn't connect to internet.
Now, once again, he used Ubuntu's LiveCD (which had no problems with the card) to download the Windows drivers from HP. Now, he says everything's working great!
Too bad I couldn't convince him to keep Ubuntu :(
