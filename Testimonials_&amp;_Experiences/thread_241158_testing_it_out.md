### Post by reacocard on 2006-08-22
Well, there isn't anything like that. but let me introduce you to a nifty little tool called the package manager. it's in System->Administration->Synaptic Package Manager. just search for what you're looking for, mark it for install, click Apply and that's it! the software will be automatically downloaded and installed. flash, java, all the multimedia formats, and lots more are available from this one spot.
