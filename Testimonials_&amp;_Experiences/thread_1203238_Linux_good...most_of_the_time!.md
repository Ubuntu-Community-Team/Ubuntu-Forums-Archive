### Post by OllieGab on 2009-07-03
I'm still in my first year of using Linux. I have tried a few distros out now I have settled on Ubuntu. I have not problems with Ubuntu - or Linux in general - and I will probably never go back to Windows, "full time".
However, I have found it a bit frustrating that things are  not always working as smoothly as I'd like them to. For instance, using my ipod I use gpodder for getting my podcasts and gtkpod to put my music on. Most of the time it works, sometimes not. It is a bit of hit and miss. As far as my ipod is concerned I have actually given up and now use Windows for that particular activity.
I can see the reasons for things being this way but hopefully, eventually **everything** will work as well with Linux (probably better!)as with W.
