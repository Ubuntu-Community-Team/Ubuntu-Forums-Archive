### Post by Urabewe on 2009-06-24
So it was a few years ago that I thought I would delve into the world of Ubuntu. It was Ubuntu 6.06 that I first tried, I had no CD burner at that time because I was poor. So I hate to wait about 2 months or so to get my "official" cd in the mail. Well it hated my computer at that time. My NIC never did work correctly, and I'm sure that this one was my fault but, I totaly hosed my Windows partition.  Well I got fed up and reformatted the whole thing and reinstalled Windows and set aside my Ubuntu disc.

Well now it's... now...  and I came back to the light a few months ago and haven't looked back. Ubuntu is wicked awesome! A new tower and some versions later and everything works! So far I have installed Ubunut 8.10 on about 3 different computers and I just recently upgraded to Jaunty and I think the first thing out of everyone's mouth has been "You mean you just plugged it in and it worked!!!".  One of my friends loaded Ubuntu and plugged in his phone to use as a modem and he plugged it in and ran through about 3 quick setup pages and boom he was connected. Said it took him over 3 hours in Windows.

So basically Ubuntu has come a far way and I'm sure it will only become better. Thank you to everyone that works on it and with it and those out there that give support, give links to support or just about anything that helps a brother out. One of the best communities I've seen. Thanks
