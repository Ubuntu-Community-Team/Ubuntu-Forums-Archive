### Post by J V on 2010-01-08
Hmm, I agree that most new users are scared when they have to paste into the terminal, but are relieved when they find out how easy it is...

I understand the absolute necessity of the CLI, not only does it make seperation of the view and model much easier, it *is* actually faster...

Faster doesn't mean easier... There isn't anyone on the planet that doesn't still need to run man every now and again, while a gui may overall be slower, seeking through 2000+ lines of a man page can easily balance that out.

And then there are the truly great GUIs: firefox, synaptic, the simple front-ends, these little things can make work much *easier* than the CLI (with a fast enough computer)

So even though the CLI definitely has much value, you've gotta admit, there are plenty of GUIs that make it so much easier it saves time and effort...
