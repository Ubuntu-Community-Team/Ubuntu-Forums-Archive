### Post by Tamlynmac on 2012-07-23
Fun is a perception. What some may find as PC fun, others may see as annoying. I think mastablasta got it right - "but we'll see what happens".

I've been Ubuntu only since 7.04 and with each new release, there's excitement that some how Ubuntu/Linux will compete with Windows. Unfortunately, it's been my experience that the majority of Windows users have no interest in migrating anywhere.

Certain PC activities tend to target Windows and the monetary rewards can be enticing. One thing I've learned is that regardless of quality or belief, humans tend to remain in the environment that they are familiar and comfortable with. Doubt that is going to change anytime soon. "But we'll see what happens".
