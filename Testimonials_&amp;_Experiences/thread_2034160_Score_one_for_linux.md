### Post by Balthazar54 on 2012-07-28
My wife took a bunch of pictures of the two tots she takes care of on her new LG Optimus Slider phone. She wanted to give copies to the parents, but did not want to have to send them all individually from here phone.

Found a cable, plugged it in to my laptop running win7, which said no driver found. Went to lg's site, downloaded driver, and got the same result.

Rebooted into Linux, shotwell popped up. It took one click to import all the pictures, plugged in a USB stick and dragged them to the stick. Was not quite sure how to do a safe disconnect. Right clicked on the stick in files and was able to do it there.

Last time I played around with SCO Xenix, this would have taken me a looong time going through the docs to figure out if it had even been possible.

Linux has come a long way!

Now if I can only figure out why my Kindle 3 (or keyboard) shows up when I plug it in and my Fire does not...
