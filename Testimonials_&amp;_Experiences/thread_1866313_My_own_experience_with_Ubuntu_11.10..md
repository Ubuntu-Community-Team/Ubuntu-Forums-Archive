### Post by khelben1979 on 2011-10-21
2 days ago I installed Ubuntu for the first time on my computer at home. And since I've been on this forum for a while now I have felt that the knowledge for Ubuntu among the people which are on this forum have increased and so have many questions gotten tougher with my own knowledge limiting me to help out. :)

My own experience with Ubuntu 11.10 is positive. To be honest, I'm impressed. Even the unity interface works good on my old aging computer, although it slows it down a little bit, it works OK.

Everything worked just out of the box, no serious installation issues what so ever and I didn't haveto install anything to have a fully working system. It's very positive to see all the positive changes which have happened as to get things working from the start, and this has not been how I have experienced Ubuntu in the past.

In any case I stay to my old and outdate Debian system over here, but it has been interesting to test out and I also feel that I'm gonna test out more as time moves on.

Ubuntu 11.10 = :KS
