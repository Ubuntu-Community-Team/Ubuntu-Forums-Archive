### Post by murderslastcrow on 2010-08-03
Plugging in a printer and immediately seeing that "New Printer Added" notification. Mmm, so good.

But seriously, Ubuntu (and Linux in general) is just so nice. I find I have nothing to complain about and tons to revel in. Even though I've been using it for over two years, it still surprises me just how easy it is, just how much it takes care of me instead of the opposite. Productivity x10, seriously.

And I love how there's not even one single person I can thank. It's all of us, together. But the developers especially play a key role, and I'd like to thank everyone who contributes to the code here publicly- you're doing amazing work, and it's freed so many of us from the issues that lie elsewhere.

I find it fascinating just how many options you have in using Linux. It fits everyone. From graphic designers to scientists to Grandmas to dual-booters, to LiveCDs for online banking, whatever- it's an OS by the people for the people. And that's definitely something I can live with.
