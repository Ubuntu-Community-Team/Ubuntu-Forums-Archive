### Post by michaelkahl on 2008-09-12
Today I took my first serious look at using xfce and I must say....I am impressed.  I simply installed it from synaptic package manager and logged into the session.  
I love the simplicity and customization out of the box.  While I don't ahve Compiz turned on in this session I don't feel like I'm missing out.  With Gnome I had to have Compiz effects with Emerald Theme to really make it feel like a gui I enjoyed using.
I do like the ability to set different desktop background for each screen(on dual monitor display).  This adds another layer of easy customization that I really enjoy.
Vanilla gnome Ubuntu is great but I really like xfce and plan on making it my default gui enviroment.
All-in-all I just wanted to share my positive experience with xfce.  It really is a beautiful interface.
