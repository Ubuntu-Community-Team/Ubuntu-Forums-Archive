### Post by Psychodox on 2010-03-09
2 Months in, after setting up my systems to dual boot XP/Ubuntu (haven't touched XP since) The only thing I miss is Maya/Max control of individual vertices/lines in 3D modeling. I have spent a month in Blender and other tools to feel comfortable with Linux tools now.

I don't remember when I first started using Linux. I remember LILO setups and how fun it was too learn how to control my computer rather than M$ controlling my computer for me. 

I still have my windows partitions now only because I plan on replacing them sometime soon with some other distro's to try out.

Last week I pushed Ubuntu on my brother and some neighbors. Watching them 'play' with the games and programs reminded me of when I first started 'playing' with computers, and it was fun watching them explore something that wasn't windows :D
