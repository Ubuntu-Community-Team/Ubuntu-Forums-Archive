### Post by mess110 on 2009-03-30
Recently I became a uni student and I walked proud in an IT Eng course with Ubuntu on my laptop and a LiveCD in my pocket. My whole class was amazed by the shiney eye candy it had but I wasn't that experienced in Linux to make everything work. For instance I barely found a LEGO RCX Controller that worked on Linux. But with some boots in Windows I managed to pass the course with a great grade.

Ubuntu isn't perfect but it has way more advantages than Windows. (not gonna relate to mac cuz I don't have enough exp with it)

Security is incredible. Gotta love nonCrossPlatform Trojans and Worms.

Fastest OS from my exp. Even with all that eye candy. You just have to know how to balance everything. (how do you do that? dip your feet in the water and start testing stuff)

I recently got my sound jack working properly (no more usb reroute for sound) - with research everything works

It is a nice day and I had a few minutes.

Just wanted to say thanks
