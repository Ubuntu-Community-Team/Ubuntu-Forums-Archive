### Post by egraham23 on 2005-11-02
Breezy reconizes my Logitech Elite keyboard and, most importantly, has deftly configured all but five of the "multimedia" keys. It handles the volume, mute and music player controls better than Logitech's crapware ever did in Windows. It's so elegant: Rhythmbox doesn't even have to be the active application for my controls to work. All I can say is...wow. Once again, Ubuntu proves to me that it gives more bang for the buck than Windows XP ever did--I'm no GNU/Linux expert, but I have so much more control over my machine, so much more confidence in my OS than I ever hoped for. Breezy is a class act as far as operating systems go: big ups to everyone who makes it happen. Aside from my Windows license I've never paid for software (I use freeware in Windows, no piracy here) but I fully intend to give something back to Ubuntu and the free software foundation as soon as finances permit. Thank you Ubuntu!!!
