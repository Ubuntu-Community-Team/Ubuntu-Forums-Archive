### Post by braingram on 2009-07-28
I would like to faithfully and hole heartedly thank Ubuntu and the Ubuntu community for all it has done to advance open source software. This thanks extends beyond Ubuntu to those responsible for all upstream products (Debian, GNU, Linux, etc...) because without these pieces of software wonder I would not have been able to complete what I have. Early today I defended my dissertation (in the field of neuroscience) and without the support of open source software I would have been unable to complete the work necessary to earn me a PhD. For these reasons and many many more I would like to personally thank every single person who has contributed to open source software in any form.

I love the open source community and hope that I can return this debt in some way. Thank you and I am truly in awe of this community.
