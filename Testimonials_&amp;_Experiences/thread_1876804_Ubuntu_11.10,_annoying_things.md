### Post by jc-sama on 2011-11-06
Hi, i've been using ubuntu since its 9.x releases and i have to admit that 11.10 is not a good one. I use an Acer Aspire 5602, these are a few things that are annoying about it:


[LIST]
[*]**The wait to open a program**, if you didn't set a launcher in the left side bar, you have to wait for ubuntu make a list of all you programs and then search for it, and that takes too much time for me, also i can't search by category 'cause the buttons don't work.
[*]**Two tries to open**, sometimes when i want to open the text editor i have to try it twice, 'cause something happens, i don't know what, and it doesn't open at the first one after appears the button on the left side bar.
[*]**Buggy two monitors mode**, if i plug a second monitor to my laptop, the top bar duplicates itself, so i have my top bar info on both monitors, but i just can access the one on the second monitor, 'cause the one on the laptop monitor hides when i release the mouse left click, Also, the windows doesn't remember on which monitor has to appear, so i always end dragging windows from one monitor to the other.
[*]**Buggy top bar**, sometimes when i open a program, its menu doesn't appear on the top bar and i have to click on some area of the desktop and then click again on the program's window.
[*]**Buggy left side bar**, sometimes when i open a program with a large menu and its window is not maximized, the left side bar appears and when i go to see the menu "File", the options appears below the left side bar.
[*]**Buggy nautilus refresh**, when i plug my iphone to the computer and i paste/move files from my computer to the iphone, i have to close or manually refresh the window to see the changes that i have made.
[*]**Sometimes the wireless adapter doesn't activate**, when i turn on my laptop and i open my session, the system see that i don't have my wireless adapter activated, but it doesn't allow to activate it.
[*]**Terminal always on top**, when i open a terminal window to do some stuffs and then i switch to other program, the terminal window always stays on top, the buttons or stuffs under the terminal window reacts if i do something, but i can't see them 'cause the terminal window doesn't allow it.
[/LIST]
Well, these are a few things that i don't like about ubuntu 11.10 and i think the developers team has to fix them, i know that i have a few things more, but i'll post them when i remember them, i attached a few photos, see ya =)
