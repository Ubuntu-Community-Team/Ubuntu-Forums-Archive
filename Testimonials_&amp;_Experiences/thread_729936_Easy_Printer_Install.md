### Post by vikigal on 2008-03-20
The more I use my Ubuntu the more I love it. I had to replace my printer in sort of a hurry, not a lot of shopping time. I ran into an H.P. Deskjet D1415 for under $30. I bought it knowing I could use it on the other computer if it didn´t work for me.

It turned out to be plug-and-play! Never expected that at all. I didn´t even have to re-boot!

I continue to be impressed. (and so is my other half. I caught him trying my live CD in his xp pc the other day. He almost admitted to being impressed.)

Thanks again to you hard working folks bringing this to us.

Wish I could do something to help. Besides run my mouth to anyone who´ll listen, I mean.
