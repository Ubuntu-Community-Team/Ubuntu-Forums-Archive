### Post by dmizer on 2009-04-07
I've posted in your thread here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=7033416#post7033416](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=7033416#post7033416)

Some pointers for receiving help more quickly.

[LIST]
[*]Most importantly, **create a very informative thread title**. In case you haven't noticed, most thread titles start with "please help, noob here". That doesn't attract knowledgeable people to your problem, because knowledgeable people generally scan thread titles for questions they can answer. In your case, something like: "Help connecting with Hauwei UMG181 in 8.10"
[*]Don't create multiple threads. You had no less than 5 threads on the same topic. This actually DECREASES your chances of getting good help because not everyone will notice you have multiple threads. This means that different people will provide the same help multiple times.
[*]Post detailed descriptions of what you have done to try to solve the problem on your own. People are more likely to help you if they see that you've already made an effort to help yourself.
[*]Determine what you need to know to help you move to the next step in your troubleshooting, and phrase your question accordingly.
[/LIST]

For example:
Help connecting with Hauwei UMG181 in 8.10

Hello,

I can't seem to get connected with my T-Mobile Hauwei UMG181 in 8.10.

I've tried to follow A (provide link), but I get stuck trying to do B.

I've also tried to search google like this:
list search parameters

What can I do next to figure out how to make my Hauwei UMG181 show up in Network-manager?
