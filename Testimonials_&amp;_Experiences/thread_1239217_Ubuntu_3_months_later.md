### Post by mrky on 2009-08-13
Hi everyone.

I installed Ubuntu 3 months ago on my work and home computer. I didn't want to post anything here at first, but decided to try it for awhile and then "report" back to you.

Anyway, all I have to say is goodbye Windows!!!

At home i have a 10 Yr old PC, and everything works perfect, eyecandies like Compiz fusion included. At work? No problem also, I just installed Office2003 because of workflow.

Stability is unquestionable, after three months I cant detect that my computer is slowing down, which wasn't the case with Windows. We even started moving our PCs in company to Ubuntu desktop. I also installed Ubuntu to several of my friends PC's which also said goodby to Bill.

The only thing that suffered during my transition was my hobby. I like 3d modelling, unfortunatelly Linux is a little bit behind with 3d modelling software. I was using 3d max, Poser, etc. But after all i started with Blender.

Annyway, if you are considering to swtch from Win to Linux, do it as soon as possible, it makes life easier.

See you all!!!
