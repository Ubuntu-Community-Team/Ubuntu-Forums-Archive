### Post by small_bigguy on 2007-08-03
I was reading about linux on another website and i saw that some guy took a 30 day linux challenge with ubuntu. So i though what better than to do it myself, as i hate windows so much and im not sure about mac OS, also i had heard lots of good reviews about ubuntu.

After the 30 days i refused to go back to windows. Ubuntu's possibilities are endless. It hardly ever crashes and all the programs are just as good.

Best thing = ITS FREE!!!!:KS

If you are reading this and you haven't ever used linux before, take the 30 day challenge.:)

### Post by Vince4Amy on 2007-08-03
I got into Linux when I lost my drivers CD for my Speedtouch.I went to a family members house & I downloaded the Speedtouch Windows drivers from their website & it said I'd need my original ISP Disc in order to use the service. So that was a waste. Anyway I went back & I thought how about Linux whilst I wait for my new disc to arrive (15 days it took). 

I downloaded Ubuntu as it was highest up on the list on Google. I had a good browse on the site & I found the Kubuntu link & I thought the screenshots looked more appealing to me. So I downloaded Kubuntu. I also found a guide for the Speedtouch 330 which was amazing & worked first time.

So then I had something to use on the net Excellent. My Windows driver disc finally came. Well lets say it never got used in the end.
