### Post by ten on 2005-11-10
Hi

I started off with RH8 due to my work, I was a S/390 mainframe operator and I just got word that some people where working on some patches for running Linux on the same HW we used at work, this captured me and I started looking for new on the net, A few moths later I came over the release of the first draft for a IBM Redbook (Think I still got a printed copy of that) Linux/390.
Now I started using linux as console replacement in around 2000 (Guess what os went out the door) Time flies and then I got RHCT. Missed RHCE by 2 points and I never got the time to retake it. 

The the big thing happend, I was waiting for the latest FC when they got delayed a week, now Debian 3.1 was just released and I desided to have a go at it and I loved it, a week later I installed FC4 for about 4 hours, boom out it went. Now I was happy, very happy. And then out of nothing I got the word about ubuntu (READ: I dont remember) I tried it on my test puter and I never ever looked back. 

As a coworker said after he got the DVD from me: I'm impressed, everything just works AND it is easier than windows
