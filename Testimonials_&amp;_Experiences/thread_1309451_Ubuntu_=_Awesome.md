### Post by ChrnoMaster on 2009-11-01
I am a newbie to Ubuntu (though not completely, I've been using it since *7.04*, but deleting it from my computer from time to time only to re-install it at a later date =P) and I've got to say, it's **awesome**.
I installed *9.04* on my Macbook 4.1(dual boot w/ OS X) and everything worked, except for a bug in the sound system when using headphones. When *9.10 *came out, I installed it immediately and **everything worked out of the box** (proprietary drivers aside)! It even recognized the video card instantly, which didn't happen in Jaunty.
This OS is great, not to mention its community. It boots up a lot faster than OS X and it's becoming my OS of choice. Thank you very much Ubuntu Dev Team!!:p
