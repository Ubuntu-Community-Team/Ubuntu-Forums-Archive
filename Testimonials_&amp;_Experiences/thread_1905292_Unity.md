### Post by inkrypted on 2012-01-06
I thought I would try to get past some of my predujices and pre concieved notions and try Unity. I am really glad to be back in KDE. First if you are going to move the close,minimize,and maximize buttons perhaps you should move the Dash menu because it interferes with closing operations of full screen windows quite often. They must think their users are somewhat thick putting a Firefox icon at the top of Dash and another Giant one in Dash home. I remember being able to batch rename in Gnome 2 but for some reason rename is there just greyed out when you do a select all. Was this a far to usefull feature to be included? Oh and you better make sure that your programs gives you a menu icon when it's installed or you will be editing alot of hidden files to make one. Also you have to do quite a bit if you want to use other programs than the ones that are installed. Unity may be good for some people but I really think it needs work. It seems like it was designed by engineers without the input of users or graphic artist. This is however just my opinion.
