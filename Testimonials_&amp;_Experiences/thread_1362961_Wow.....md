### Post by AlanR8 on 2009-12-23
I have to say I'm impressed!

Have been using *buntu for three years now and generally things have gone well. The wife's been using XP on a Dell Dimension and there were no file sharing issues between XP and *buntu.

Until today.....

I bought her an Imac for Xmas and I have to say I'm SO impressed! The design, the performance, the look...just pornography!

Oh, and did I not mention how it behaves on the network at home? No problem. Can I save and share files, YES!

Her Dell failed by power supply so I went to PC World today and bought an enclosure for the hard disk.

Sorted it out, plugged it into this Sony with Linux and immediately got access to everything. Plugged it in to the Mac and there it all was.

She's a happy bunny......and now NO WINDOWS in this house!!!!!
