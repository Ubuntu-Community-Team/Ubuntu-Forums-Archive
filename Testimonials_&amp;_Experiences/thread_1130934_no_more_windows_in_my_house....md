### Post by MN Noob on 2009-04-20
Last Friday was the last of Windows in my home. I now run OSX, 8.10 server, 8.10 desktop, and Xandros. The desktop was the last to be converted. My wife was using Windows XP Home. I wanted to make sure I got everything backed up, as she had multiple gigs of pics and video from college years.

So Friday, my wife logged onto her 'new' machine and was quite impressed at the bootup time, internet speeds, and overall look and feel. That was a big relief, but I was not too scared she wouldn't like it as she has slowly been moving away from Windows and has been using my Mac more than I do, and even my netbook from time to time. So this was my hope to win my Mac back, and I think it worked. :)

Anyways, this has been a long journey that started about 1.5 years ago when I purchased an old p3 for 20$ and installed 7.10 desktop to mess around. Then installed 8.10 server to run as a home server. Then Xandros on the netbook. And finally getting rid of windows on my wifes and installing 8.10 desktop. I have gone from a complete n00b to just a n00b.

Now to move onto family members. First up, my Dad whom I have been working on for a couple months. I think I convinced him to try it out via live cd. I almost got my brother onto it, but he needs windows for pharmacy classes. :( Then onto grandparents for skype...
