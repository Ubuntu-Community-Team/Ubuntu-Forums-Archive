### Post by Rytron on 2008-03-13
Hi,

I have only been using Ubuntu since mid February 2008. I have a dual boot pc with Xp on as the other OS. 

After changing the appearance of Ubuntu and the system sounds, it now looks and sounds amazing. XP looks garbage now compared to Ubuntu Linux.

I only keep Xp for some old games I have such as Colin McRae Rally 04 and Silent Hunter III and certain applications I need for college such as MicroSoft Visual Studio and Access Database. I have used WINE but I need to familiarise myself better with it.



Initially when I started to use Ubuntu I ran into many frustrations and problems trying to install/configure stuff. However with this wonderful Ubuntu Forums site at hand in helping to offering friendly and prompt help I soon go through many problems. The add/remove and synaptic features really simplify installing new programs/games. 



I am looking forward to the April release of Hardy Heron. I'd recommend Ubuntu to anyone. I am delighted with it.



My rating for Ubuntu 9/10 (This could soon change to 10/10)
