### Post by coffeecat on 2009-12-05
I've had a look at your (brief) posting history, and I can tell you partly why you are not getting help. Your last three posts consisted of bumping two old, dead threads with no information on your specific problem. One thread was a single unanswered post from 4½ years previously! That is called necromancy and is frowned on.

But it's not the necromancy that is causing you to be ignored. It's the lack of information. Start your own help thread in a support forum and concentrate on one issue. Give a concise but complete account of your problem and provide details of all your relevant hardware. This is the Testimonials forum - you won't get technical support here.

Also, if you don't get a reply immediately, don't lose heart but read this:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=82471](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=82471)
