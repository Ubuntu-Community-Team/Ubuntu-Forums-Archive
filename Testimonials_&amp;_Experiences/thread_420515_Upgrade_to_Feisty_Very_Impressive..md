### Post by number3pencil on 2007-04-23
I recently bought a notebook for college and installed 6.10 on it.  It was kind of a chore getting drivers for my nvidia card, wirelss card, etc.  When Feisty came out I decided to do a clean install.  After installing Kubuntu, I was INCREDIBLY impressed.  All of my laptop's hotkeys were recognized and my wireless card was functional automatically.    It also automatically installed a program that deals with the notebooks battery and laptop services something not included in 6.10. Installing the Nvidia drivers were also easy.  I am running into a few minor problems, but overall I am very impressed with the out of the box functionallity and the upgrades for notebooks.
