### Post by jdmss1 on 2008-03-13
I made the complete switch to Ubuntu a few months ago and i just want to say i am very pleased.  I was running Windows XP before and had to either re-install or restore every few months.  Unfortunately, i just couldn't afford the time spent hassling with that.  During a discussion with a buddy of mine, Ubuntu was mentioned, i checked it out, ordered the CD's.  I installed it on a spare HDD of mine, got a few laying around in the classic "I like to build my own computers" fashion.  I was impressed....i think what impresses me the most is:
1. Quick load time....literally seconds
2. The Community and support
3. Customization options
4. User control and personalization
5. It's FREE!!!!!!
6. The amount of available programs
7. Has NOTHING to do with Microsluts!!!!
8. Bill Gates isn't getting even richer off of it
9. Ubuntu is what computing is (and always should have been) about
10.  If there is something about Ubuntu you don't like...DL the source code and change it (and noone will take you to court over it)
11.  Did i mention it's FREE?!?!?!?

Anyway, thanks for reading and everybody for all their help and information. :guitar:
