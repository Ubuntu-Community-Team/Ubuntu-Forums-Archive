### Post by wolfen69 on 2009-02-15
as some of you may know, i fix computers for a living (also sell pc's on the side). earlier today, a customer of mine who is running linux exclusively on 2 of his computers, called me up and said he bought a brand new laptop for his daughter. (Dell Vostro 1500, nice) without ever even turning it on yet, he said his daughter wants ubuntu on it! she says no way to windows! sure, i can do that for you. my pleasure. (plus, i get paid :P )
i'm typing from the laptop right now as i install. but of course, i tried the 8.10 live cd to make sure everything is OK, and it works great.

these people are what you would call average users. so, anybody that says ubuntu is not ready for "joe six pack", is sadly mistaken. the only person in the family who still uses windows is his youngest son. oh well, can't win em all.

it is so much easier (and more fun) for me to set up an ubuntu machine than dick around with windows. even though windows was preinstalled, doing a fresh install of ubuntu is probably quicker than going through the first time setup, configuring, and deletion of unwanted trialware. plus you still need to install antivirus/spyware, codecs, apps, etc. PITA! gotta go finish setting it up and get it back to him. chalk another one up for ubuntu.

just thought i'd share.
