### Post by spiral777 on 2006-08-08
I work for Dell, one of the largest purveyors of Microsoft products in the world, and this week I finally struck back. :D 

I have an Ubuntu wallpaper on my system, have Firefox and Ubuntu posters in my workspace, and have a nice stack of Ubuntu CDs made and ready to hand out to anyone who asks. 

So far I've helped three coworkers play with the live cd at home, and had 1 install it as a dual boot system, and 1 of the others wound up wiping his entire harddrive and installing it as the sole OS on his machine.

I'm still learning all the tricks, and I'll admit I don't know everything yet, but the community here is great, and I've always had an answer to any questions within the hour.

I love Ubuntu!
