### Post by karellen on 2007-09-17
after a month or so of using only Windows (Vista for this time) I came back to Ubuntu. I guess I never left at all, as I watched those forums carefully and, deeply in me, I longed for Linux. probably it's addictive, but I felt somehow incomplete and hypocritical. I missed the old trusted "sudo apt-get...." and the feeling of control and power Linux gives to me. and let's not forget the freedom :)
so last night I popped-in the Feisty live cd and I installed it. I had a fat32 partition and I even didn't have the patience to move the data from there (some music) to another partition. I just formated it and then proceeded. I knew that all my hardware will be properly detected, after the install completed I just did "sudo pppoeconf" and I was connected with the outside world. now I'm in the "sweet tweaking phase", if you know what I mean ;)
so for now I'm enjoying my new fresh Feisty and I'm looking forward to Gutsy. cheers
