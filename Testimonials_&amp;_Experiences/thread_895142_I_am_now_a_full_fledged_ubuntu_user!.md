### Post by TenPlus1 on 2008-08-21
Set your Swappiness level to 20 or under and that will speed up your system with 512mb...

sudo gedit /etc/sysctl.conf

add this line to very end:

   vm.swappiness=10

and instead of rebooting, lets make it live by typing this:

   sudo sysctl vm.swappiness=10

Done...  as for programs, DeVeDe is a great Video DVD creator, Avidemux transcodes movies, SoundConverter does music and [www.getdeb.net](www.getdeb.net) has all the latest gear :)
