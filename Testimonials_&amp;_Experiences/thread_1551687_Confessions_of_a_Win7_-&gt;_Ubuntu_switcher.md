### Post by julianb on 2010-08-16
in the article posted (post#1) "IT Guy" mentions many updates per day; "Joe Wilcox" says that's just false. 

Here's the deal though - the windows OS only does a couple updates per month, but the software attached to it may do several updates per day. My work machine (windows) bugs me about updates separately for Windows, Java, Adobe Reader, Logitech camera software, and sometimes other stuff. The virus scanner runs and updates itself automatically. In Ubuntu I update everything using apt-get, and even if I don't i'll be safer than windows.
