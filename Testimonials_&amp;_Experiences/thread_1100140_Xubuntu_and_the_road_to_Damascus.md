### Post by TideMan on 2009-03-18
This machine was on its way to the rubbish dump..............
It has an AMD Sempron 2800+ CPU, 40Gb HD, but only 256Mb RAM
It was running WinXP like a dog, with the HD continuously whirring and clacking even when we weren't using it. And everything took ages to run.

But, just before we discarded it, I thought I'd try Xubuntu.
So, I installed Xubuntu (inadvertently overwriting Win XP in the process).  The transformation has been amazing.  
Now it's like a new machine.
Basic applications like Firefox that were painful to run on Win XP, now run as well on this machine as the other more powerful machines in our LAN.

And thanks to Nautilus and to this forum for advice, it's networked into the LAN.

I just have to figure out a good use for it............

### Post by fluxlizard on 2009-03-19
The rubbish heap?!? Holy smokes I can't believe what people throw away!

Just up the ram (double it or get it to 1 gb)- won't cost much of anything and you can run ubuntu not xubuntu on there very comfortably indeed. I've got intrepid running pretty comfortably on a machine that is a p3 1000mhz (really 960 or something like that). I got it from the "free take me" pile of discards at our local computer shop. *The only thing* that was wrong with it was that windows xp needed to be cleaned up (easy enough by uninstalling a bunch of trash and then using about 4 free applications every couple of weeks- though it bugs me because linux never needs cleaning up) and it needed more ram and I decided to put a more powerful video card in there that I had sitting in a box when I upgraded another machine a few years ago. With 1 gb ram (cost me $10 used), 128mb geforce video card, this machine is fine for day to day things dual booting with xp and intrepid- a little weak in the games department, but 2d and older 3d games work fine. Video and everything else works fine.

I built an amd 1800+ for my folks several years ago and it still runs great with intrepid now. I upgraded the ram a couple years back to 756 mb but that's it. Still running smooth and fast.

 I can't imagine someone tossing a machine with your specs! Send the next one to me! I'll put it on our lan so my kids can have more friends over for urban terror and warcraft 3 battles!
