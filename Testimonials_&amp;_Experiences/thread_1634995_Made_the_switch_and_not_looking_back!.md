### Post by u-noob-tu on 2010-12-01
So last week my step dad started talking about ubuntu as a way to recover the admin password on my computer (he had forgotten it). so he brought home a live cd and we booted it up and made a partition and all that stuff. well, i was so impressed with ubuntu that i asked him if i could get rid of windows completely. he said sure cuz it is technically my computer, and we have other windows computers laying around. words cannot articulate how refreshing ubuntu is. theres just something about it that is so alluring. theres something to be said for the freedom to make your computer work and look the way you want it to. 

up until last week i knew very little about ubuntu, and even less about linux. now my goal is to learn programming so i can hopefully get a good job as an IT guy. 

so thats my little testimonial. 

Ryan

PS: i am also very impressed with the ubuntu community. my first post was a plea for guidance and i got a plethora of replies and even a PM.
