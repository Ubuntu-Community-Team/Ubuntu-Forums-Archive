### Post by muguwmp67 on 2007-04-22
So I decided to upgrade my ubuntu laptop yesterday.  I'd booted up, saw the dist upgrade notice and said 'what the heck' and just went with it.  Noticing that the power was low, I laid the laptop on my desk and plugged it in to charge while doing the upgrade.

I went off to make some coffee and stared messing around on my desktop PC. After a while, I noticed that the screen had blanked on the laptop, so I tapped the shift key to refresh the screen.

Nothing happened.

I looked down at the other end of the power cable and it wasn't plugged into the outlet, and the PC had run out of juice mid-upgrade.

Nothing good happened at boot, so next up I'll be installing fiesty on the laptop.  I'll make sure the  power light is on this time.
