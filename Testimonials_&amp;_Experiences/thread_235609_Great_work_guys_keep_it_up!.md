### Post by lagagnon on 2006-08-13
Just to let you all know how impressed I am with Dapper Drake. My main distro is VectorLinux, (because it is designed for use on older computers and I volunteer with a charity that refurbishes and donates older computers). VectorLinux is very fast and slim but a bit lacking in the repository and detection of scanners, webcams, etc.

Since Breezy Badger there seems to have been a huge improvement in the speed of boot-up and shutdown and also of the install process.  Dapper Drake has so far never failed to pick up any new hardware I have inserted. Very impressive. It makes WinXP look like a gaudy relic from the stone ages.
