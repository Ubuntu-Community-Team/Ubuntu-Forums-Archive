### Post by castaneda on 2008-07-02
I was going to set up a dual boot with XP and Ubuntu, but somehow I  managed to erase my windows partition. It was not a big deal since I have good backup routines. I'm an avid Wow-player, and when I couldn't find my CD-key for XP i was a bit more worried. I finally gave up looking for it and set out to try installing the game through Wine. 

There was no shortage of horror tales in my Google searches, but I decided to try one of the many guides anyway. Computers are not a mystery to me, but all my previous experiences with Linux have ended in failure. Always due to some piece of hardware I never could get to work. 

I am really happy to say that apart from messing a bit with sound drivers, everything went really smooth. The best part is that my machine seems at least twice as fast. All the little waits and pauses I was used to in XP are nonexistent now. There is virtually no load times when i start up Wow. Exiting and booting the system is also much faster.
