### Post by 1roxtar on 2010-07-21
This week, I've had the pleasure of peeling off two more Windows stickers (1-XP and 1-Win7).  

The first this week was my best friend's laptop.  I had already installed Lucid on his desktop and he loves it.  He held on to his XP laptop so he can become more familiar with Ubuntu.  After having Ubuntu for two weeks, the slow XP boot time (10 mins) on his laptop made him scream,"ENOUGH"!!!  Needless to say, I was more than happy to oblige him.

The second occurs today.  I have been servicing this family's desktop for the better part of two years.  Every three months or so, I make my routine house call because viruses had hijacked their home computer.  Today, I made such a call, but this time I took along a flash drive loaded with Ubuntu 10.04 LTS.  During my last call, I had shared briefly about Ubuntu but didn't have a LiveCD/Flash Drive with me.

Surprisingly to me, the teens in the house had already heard about Ubuntu but hadn't seen it in action.  I booted into Lucid and took them through a tour and let them get comfortable with it. The parents' selling point was the Security and No Windows Viruses.  Their needs are pretty basic and since these are good friends of mine, they have the assurance that I will help them along through the transition.

I am an avid promoter of Ubuntu in my area, but I have learned to TEACH and DEMONSTRATE what Ubuntu is and what it can do, rather than simply "preach" Ubuntu or come off as an "Anti-Windows" militant.
