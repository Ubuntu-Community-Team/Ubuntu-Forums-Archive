### Post by stanjam on 2008-05-07
One of my students frequently goes to his town's recycling center.  He recently found an old laptop, an IBM Latitude c600/c500.  It has 382 MB memory, and a processor (somewhere in the 800 Mhz range).  I bought it off him for $20.  He had CentOS running on it, experimenting.  It ran well, but for my purposes, I put Ubuntu on it and am running XFCE to get better performance.  A disabled friend that we live with is now using it and learning to use computers, send email etc!  It needs a wireless card and a new battery to get full use of it, but so far it is a great little machine!

I ran a project last year at our college, taking donated computers from a local business, putting Ubuntu on them, and getting them out to students in need.  No other operating system does this job so well.  Certainly Windows could not have.
