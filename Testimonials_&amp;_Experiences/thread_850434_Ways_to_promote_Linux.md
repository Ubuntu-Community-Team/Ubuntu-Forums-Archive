### Post by mexpolk on 2008-07-05
1. **Install your favorite flavor of Linux on your laptop:** When people see you working width thousand programs, nice effects, people start paying attention. An then you come up and say: it's Linux.

2. **Word of mouth:** Many people don't even know what Linux is; they think Windows is the only answer. When talking with people about computers, let them know you're a proud Linux user!

3. **Email Signatures:** If you only send five emails per day, that is almost two thousand emails per year! What is it going to hurt to add something about Linux to your signature?

*and my personal favorite:*

4. Whenever you are, if there are laptops on sale with Windows, open a Word or PowerPoint (better) and put some BIG red letters saying:

DON'T USE WINDOWS. USE LINUX IT IS BETTER AND FREE!

or

DON'T USE WINDOWS. IS INSECURE AND FULL OF VIRUSES. USE LINUX INSTEAD!!!

It is funny to see people faces asking: ¿What is Linux? 

*And that my friends is a good start! Let's promote Linux!!!*
