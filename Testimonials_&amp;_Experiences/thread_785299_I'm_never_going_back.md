### Post by pbeesley on 2008-05-07
Well goodbye windows!!! 

My experience with Linux started around a month ago when I needed a 'live cd' to get some data off a dead laptop. The only thing I could find was PCLINXOS... omg so much better than xp :) since then (after I got the laptop working again) I found that I wasn't using windows any more and instead was booting off that 'live cd'. Two weeks later I hadn't once booted into windows and I wasn't missing it. :KS

After some research I installed Yellow Dog Linux on my PS3 and the next day wiped my hard drive on my PC and installed Hardy Heron instead. :shock::shock::shock: It was so much easier than windows (installed in full screen 1280 x 1024) Wireless worked 'out of the box', graphics worked otb, printer worked otb) everything just.....well works

So far the only thing I haven't been able to do is burn lightscribe cd's.............that's it. As soon as I get that sorted my laptop with Vista will be wiped and Linux will be on all devices in my house. And its free. I spent like £100+ for Vista and Hardy blows it away. 3D desktops just to name ONE feature. And this is all coming from a free piece of software.

To Sum Up:
1 Month ago I was ripping on Linux and laughing in its face. Now....well Windows sucks....my eyes have been opened.

Thanks You All
