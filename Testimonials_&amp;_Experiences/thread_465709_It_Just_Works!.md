### Post by MichiganBill on 2007-06-06
I've been using Ubuntu 7.04 for about 6 weeks now. It just works! That's the bottom line for me.

My main computer is a Mac mini with OS X, which I just love. However I needed a laptop for church work and I couldn't justify buying a MacBook. So I purchased a Dell D600 on *Bay, put in a new hard drive, and installed Feisty Fawn on it. (I also sold the old hard drive, which paid for the shipping!)

Installation took half an hour. The WiFi worked, screen worked, function buttons worked, everything worked! I am a happy camper! My wireless HP printer works like a charm.

The main reason I needed a laptop was because I go to meetings where we review dozens of PDF files, and sometimes we need to print to pdf, extract pages, join files together, and so on. Between Acroread and pdftk, I'm in business!

Something must be working right, because my son (who has a WinXP laptop) borrows mine when he wants to watch DVDs!

So while I'm unlikely to give up my mini, I must say this about Ubuntu--  "It just works!" :p

(And thanks to everyone on the forum. You guys (and gals) helped a lot.)
