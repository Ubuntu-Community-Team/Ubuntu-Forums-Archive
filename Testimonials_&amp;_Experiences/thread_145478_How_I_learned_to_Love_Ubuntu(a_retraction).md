### Post by troyDoogle7 on 2006-03-16
Dear All
I left a message a while ago saying that I was fed up with linux and ubuntu  Well I was, but no longer.  I would like to retract my previous comments.  I love ubuntu.  I really do. 

I think that the reason I cut and ran before was because out of frustration and lack of knowledge.   I was used to the microsoft mindset and the methods.  I am an expert at xp (Microsoft certified) and I think it made me feel uncomfortable to be running a new environment.  

Over the last weekend I installed both breezy and dapper onto my hardisks and for some strange reason perhaps the bits of reading that I have done, it all came together and  I just got into the mindset and everything worked...REALLY well.  (dapper gave me a cpu freeze after I accidentally shut it down, but old breezy is working like a charm. )  

For all those who are struggling to get to grips with ubuntu, watch the google videos and just skip through some of the beginner guides, but just keep knocking through and you will enjoy the zen like experience of a non windows pc.

 I have xampp installed and working.  Open office is working.  My old machine has been resurrected. Networking is running great.  I just can't wait for native xgl.  

I think this migration has given me a really great understanding how my end users feel when they are using windows.  I definately wll give them a bit more patience and understanding, like so many of you have shown on these boards.  

Please be patient with me and I look forward to being an expert ubuntite in the near future.  

For the Ubuntu hoarde!
