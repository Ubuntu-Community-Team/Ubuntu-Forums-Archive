### Post by floke on 2007-10-28
As we all know there have been many issues surrounding 7.10. In the past 24 hours I have posted a strong rant against the whole release schedule affair that has attracted the wrath of the forum mods. For balance I want to start a "Be Nice to Gutsy" thread.

Do not post any bad things about Gutsy here, only the good stuff.

Lets give some love to the Gibbon!

For me, the main plus points are:

* The autodetect printer configuration setup is awesome.

* Compiz-Fusion runs on my ATI Radeon Xpress200M for the first time (well actually it worked on Mandriva, but I'm not going to install that now am I?)

* Boot up and shutdown times (though not always working properly) are blazing - comparable to Arch on my rig.

* I actually do like the new default wallpaper.

* While my wife's laptop has shutdown issues, she has not once complained about wanting to go back to Windows - and this may well be the biggest plus of all!

How about you?
