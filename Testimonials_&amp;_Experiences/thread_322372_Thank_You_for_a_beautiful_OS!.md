### Post by memyselfnie on 2006-12-20
I have been using Ubuntu for some 4-5 months now, after years of struggling with windows.  Altho my original 3.1 is still functioning on a 20 mg seagate, I have had it with every version of windows produced since.  One install after another corrupted by everything under the sun,  Pouring money and effort into what I knew to be a losing battle, when I knew of Linux for years, was clearly a bush-league fubar-ed messed up waste.  But thanks to all those who have created and shared freely, I have moved on into the light.  It seems beyond belief that an OS could still function after months of net'ing with no firewall, anti-viri-spy-ware, apparently totally defenseless and yet, not a scratch.  I gave it the most rigorous test I could, (my click happy "other half")  and not one single error yet.  Truly a miracle.  
 =D>
Ubuntu is clearly the best piece of software I've ever seen.  I suspect that is because of the 
ideals of the people behind it.  Free, open source, collaborative brilliance.  
Keep on!
