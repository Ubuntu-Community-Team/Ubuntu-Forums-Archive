### Post by zenithdave on 2009-03-12
I have been using Ubuntu 8.04 on a eee laptop for 2 weeks now at work :D Im using it for running open office to create wiring schedules in a spread sheet.
As this data is being paid for i was concerned i may have a crash, no need, stable as you like, its on 14 hours a day  but do regular backups of the data on a usb stick anyway.

Im also running FOSS pcb design (freepcb) in WINE, also very stable. :)

The people i am contracting for have been coming around and having a look and asking questions initially pulled in by the tiny laptop and then i explain the OS to them, even had a few lessons on how to say Ubuntu :popcorn: (assuming i have it right) and what the word means :D 

I cleaned up a XP users hdd and installed  open-office and she easily switched to open office word processor without a problem despite not being to computer literate.

Now im home on the same laptop i have chilled soma fm streaming (via wireless) in VLC, firefox up to its usual standard :D

I could go on and on . You could call this a Ubuntu moment :D

Dedicated work for everyone involved, thanks :D
