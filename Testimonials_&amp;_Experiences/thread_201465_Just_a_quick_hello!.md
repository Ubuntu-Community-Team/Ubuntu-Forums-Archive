### Post by MikePnKY on 2006-06-21
I have been reading over these forums for several weeks, but only just registered today, so I thought that I'd post a quick hello and a "thank you" to, well, just about everyone here.

Sometime early last year, a friend of mine gave me a Ubuntu 4.10 CD set that he'd found somewhere, and it was in a desk drawer here for over a year.

Well, about 3 weeks ago, fancy new HP Pavilion a1223w Media Center PC with windows factory installed, just decided that it wasn't going to get past the splash screen in windows.  On the windows desktop was a folder with 3 weeks worth of digital photographs that I hadn't bothered to put on my external drive yet, and it was causing me stress and discomfort not being able to get to them, and knowing that if I re-installed the factory CD's, the photos would be gone.  So, I took a long shot.  I remembered from the one time that I'd played with the live 4.10 CD that it had let me see what was on the desktop in windows, so, I put it in the drive and gave it a whirl.  Lo and behold, I was able to retrieve the photos and Ubuntu also recognized my external drive, so I was able to save my pictures.

Since then I've downloaded Dapper and have been researching the forums to get it set up (Automatix is the most awesomest thing =D> )  I was even able to figure out how to use Grub and keep windows (well, at least for now..looking kind of grim for windows right now) And, I just wanted to say "THANK YOU" to everyone thats posted such good stuff, and to say "Howdy".  

I don't know how active I'll be as far as posting, but I'll probably be reading up here ALL the time  :-\"
