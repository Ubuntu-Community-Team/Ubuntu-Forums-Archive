### Post by matera on 2006-12-10
Several days ago I got a new (used) computer, with WindowsXP pro installed. As usual, the 40GB hard drive was not partitioned - and no install disk came with the computer, so I was feeling very edgy about backups. Putting in a second hard drive improved the backup options, but not enough, the partition sizes are not what I need. I also wanted to see if I could get more unallocated space, for other reasons...

So, today I put in my Ubuntu CD, which my previous computer refused to allow to load completely, and enjoyed a look at it. MMMM! It's the best Linux I've ever tasted :-D 

Better yet, I found gparted on the menu. It wasn't long before I had the primary hard drive nicely divided, and Winderz back up and running.

The next step will be to install Ubuntu on the second drive. w00t! Then I can really turn myself loose.

I am extremely grateful to everyone who made today's little adventure possible. Ubuntu has given me a fantastic amount of freedom. It will also help me to improve security for other people who are stuck in Windwoes. \\:D/
