### Post by Handssolow on 2013-03-12
Consider that your mouse might be faulty, I didn't initially.
For several months I was getting a variety of problems navigating with the mouse, a Trust USB wired optical mouse. With Google Earth I was unable to move the map with a left click, instead it would zoom in. Highlighting words before cut and paste was a challenge, often I'd get the whole line rather than a word. Mouse movement were either over-reactive but selecting  from a menu would be slow and mostly needing a second left click , so here it was under-reactive.

Nothing I did in mouse settings made a difference. I wasn't sure if the upgrade to Ubuntu 12.04 LTS was the cause. Reactions to the mouse were so delayed at times, I started wondering if an OS re-install was required. It wasn't that the mouse didn't work, it just wasn't responding normally. Today as a last resort I replaced the mouse with an old PS/2 ball mouse I had, it worked normally. In the afternoon I bought a replacement USB Trust optical mouse and it too works normally.

I find ballmice pick up fluff and wireless mice need batteries, time will tell if it was a mistake me buying a replacement Trust optical mouse.
