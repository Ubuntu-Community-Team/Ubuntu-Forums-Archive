### Post by woofti on 2011-12-18
Sorry if all this has been said before. I moved to Linux in 2008 and Hardy was my first experience. It was a very good one. Lucid has been the pinnacle of my computing life. I get a lot of pleasure out of Lucid.

I think they have COMPLETELY screwed Ubuntu up since Natty with the big, blocky icons down the side, and the incomprehensible desktop. Total step backwards if you ask me.

If I had come to Linux with Natty I would never have moved away from Windoze.

I think it's terrible, what they've done to Ubuntu. If I wanted a desktop looking like an iPad I'd buy an iPad. If I wanted my computer looking like a Mac, I'd buy a Mac.

I will stay with Lucid as long as it is supported. I have got an experimental machine upgrading to Oneiric now. I am going to try my best to tweak it so I can use it. But I am DISAPPOINTED and thinking of moving to Mint after Lucid.
