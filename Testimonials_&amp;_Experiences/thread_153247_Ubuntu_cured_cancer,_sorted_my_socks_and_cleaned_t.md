### Post by Rikostan on 2006-03-31
Well okay not really, but it does pretty much everything else I need it to do.

I have been a windows geek since 3.1, but I have tried different flavors of linux for years. I usually install, get things set up correctly, check out a few apps or games and then go back to my comfortable windows machine.

This time is different. I am dual-booting XP and Ubuntu, but I am spending far more time using Ubuntu over XP.
I still have some tweaking to do, but I think I might just blow the windows partition away in the next couple of weeks and go with just Ubuntu. 

Kudos to the devs for making such a user friendly distro and to the community for posting so many How Tos. 
I have not had to ask one single question because everything I needed to know has already been asked and answered in a very clear and straight forward manner.
