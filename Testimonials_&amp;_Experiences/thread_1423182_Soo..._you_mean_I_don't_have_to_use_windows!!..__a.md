### Post by Rasa1111 on 2010-03-06
Hiyah Ubuntu Tribe-ers.:KSlol

I just wanted to comment on, and share a little of what has been going on "here" (in this little corner of Earth) with people that i know, and people that they know.... lol

  I have been aware of Linux for a long time,  but never really "had the time" to do the research and learn about it.  I also didnt (and still do not) know anyone in my personal , everyday life that uses anything but *COUwindowsGH*,   besides the president of our Astro Society, and he uses the real old skool stuff for plotting the heavens. :lol:

  I learned of Ubuntu a few years back, and not long after learning about it, I decided it was something I wanted to learn and use.  
 I then ordered a disc from Canonical , and it was Gutsy Gibbon,  which I used for a couple weeks maybe, ( on dial up)~[ god that was a pain for a straight up newb]. lol

 Shortly after I started to feel a little more comfy with it, something happened to my system , that was beyond my comprehension ... and I could not find a remedy... so.. back to XP i went. :(   ( good thing I decided to partition that time and not fully wipe and install!) 

  Well, a couple years have passed since then,  and my fuse with windows was getting shorter and shorter,  and I have a better machine now, so I figured I would give Ubuntu another go....   I really wanted to rid of windows forever, .....  but I wasnt sure if I could "hack it"..lol,  and thought I would probably have to partition my HD again to keep windows on half of it.

 As you know...  the Universe is good!~  
and within about a week or two of looking/playing around in Karmic, ..
 I realized that........ hey...  I can do this!! :lol:
 So I stopped using Ubuntu with Wubi, and completey 'closed' and wiped windows away..... and started to open some doors!! 

 This machine, you would have sworn (when it was infected with windows) was ready for the trash heap..  No matter what one would do,  It would "get better" for a week, then go right back into nothingness.  

 When I was running Ubuntu with Wubi, the machine was acting 100x better than when I would run windows.. (and it was running on straight RAM!) lol
  
After I wiped the virus, excuse me.. windows from the system,  and started using Ubuntu as the only OS on the machine....

 My machine now works better than everyone else I know, with windows, vista, 7, xp, you name it.  ANd those computers (most of them) are better than my own. 

 **Onto the topic of the thread.. (sorry) lol...**

 Once I started showing these people my new system, and they saw how well it works, and  that it is actually possible for a "normal person"  to operate and enjoy....

 These people are coming to me to get windows off of their machines, like it is my job or something. :lol:

  and the people they know, are now inquiring...  and saying things like.. 
"you mean, we dont *have* to use windows?"
   and
" there are other options? *really*?"
  and
"well, if there is another great system out there for free, why do people pay soo much for windows"
  and
and..
you get the picture.   lol

In the past 2 weeks, I have installed Ubuntu on 4 peoples computers ( only 2 of them guttsy enough to delete windows straight away) but the others will come around I am sure. lol

  Every one of them is extremely pleased with Ubuntu . ( 9.10)
and everyday, or every other day, I get someone else asking me to do the same for them or for a friend of theirs. 

   Are people really this un-aware that they dont need to be slaves to windows? 
or has MS just done a really good job at doing what those with the most money (power) do... corrupting people and systems? 

 I really couldnt believe that some people truly think windows is the only option they have to use their computers!?!?

Anyway........
 There are literally more people asking me about, and wanting me to "fix" their computers and add a new OS and/or remove windows from them, than I can handle at the moment. lol

  It seems there is a huge majority of people using/"stuck with" windows, 
who would really prefer something different, but they are not even aware they have a choice!  :o


"absolute power corrupts absolutely". 


 Thanks everyone here, for all the help you selflessly provide others. 
It really does say a whole ton about the OS itself and those behind it. 

*deepest bows*
