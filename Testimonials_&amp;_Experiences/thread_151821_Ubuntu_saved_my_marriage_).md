### Post by blakegrover on 2006-03-28
I would just like to mention how ubuntu has brought me closer to my wife and helped out our marriage.  A couple years ago I put Gentoo on my home computer.  With Gentoo I had a lot of compiling and learning to do.  I also had to change a lot of things so it would run ok.  My wife thought linux was horrible, how much time I spent on it and how little progress I had made every night.  Well 3 months ago I put ubuntu on my laptop, my wife was not very happy and wondered why I would do such a thing if I already had Windows XP.  I talked to her about it and she noticed how easy it was to setup and how fast I was doing stuff in it and how fast I could put games in it.  3 months ago I also ask to get the free ubuntu cds shipped to my house and they got there yeserday.  My wife mentioned it to me and asked what was in the box and I said there were 10 cds ubuntu sent me to pass around to my friends and workers because it is so good.  About an hour later I got a text message from my wife saying I give up I want to learn how to use Ubuntu.  Last night we spent time together installing ubuntu and doing stuff in linux.  She loved all the games I could get working on my gentoo box a couple of years ago like frozen-bubble, etc.  After installing she saw how easy it was to install more games and other pograms and now she is thinking about getting rid of Windows XP all together.  Maybe ubuntu didn't really save my marriage but it sure has help my wife learn to love linux and try something new.  I would like to thank all those involved in testing drapper and getting ubuntu to where it is now.

Blake
