I was walking through Detroit Metro Airport with my Ubuntu Laptop in hand getting ready to sit down and make use of the airport WIFI while I waited for my parents to catch up to me.  On the walls of the terminal were video screens showing advertisements of all sorts.  Then I passed one with the Ubuntu Logo on it.  I stopped and looked closer.  An Ubuntu AD?  NO WAY!!!  Then I realized that this screen had simply failed to start correctly and was merely showing the Ubuntu Login screen with the Ubuntu Logo displayed prominently, below it, Linux For Human Beings.  Still I thought.  Way cool.
