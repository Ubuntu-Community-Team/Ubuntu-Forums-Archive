### Post by voxman69 on 2007-05-06
Hi all!

I've been using computers on a daily basis for some 15 years now (since windows 3.0) and gone through all the win versions. With Win2k I finally found myself USING my computer rather than fixing it for the first time, and both XP and Vista has improved from there. So why am I here now?

Well...there's always been issues with windows...For me, the main things have been the constant restarts due to Windows getting slower and slower by the hour, the "improved" security features that really just makes me annoyed (activation routines that won't work and then you have to _PHONE_ MS to get your copy activated), the accelerating hardware requirements, the constant battle with viruses...well the list goes on, but as you can see i'm not having any issues with how Windows actually runs and gets the job done. It still has some advantages in ease-of-use over any Linux distro.

All the above and then some has made me look to the Linux world every now and then. I've tried SuSe, Red Hat, Mandrake (which came very close to win me over with version 10) and a few others. The problem though has always been that i've found myself spending half my time in the terminal window putting long lines of commands i don't even understand together...

And so came Ubuntu (Feisty)!!! I've marked april 19th as an annual anniversary in my calendar!!  :)  Had a few minor problems with my wireless Belkin card, the Nvidia drivers and a little stability issues with Beryl, but all those were solved quickly thanks to the amazingly helpful people in this forum!! Thank you all!!!

Since the release of Feisty i've booted into Windows one (1) time, and that's only because i've yet to get my Nokia N70 to work in Feisty...workin' on that though! :) Found equally good or better appz for my daily work, it's just the gaming part that gets me...but i rather use my Xbox anyway for that...:) And finally...With Beryl the eye-candy outshines Vistas every day of the week! 

So...back to work!

//Voxman
