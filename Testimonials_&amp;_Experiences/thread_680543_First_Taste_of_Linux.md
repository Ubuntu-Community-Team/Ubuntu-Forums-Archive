### Post by dse.37 on 2008-01-28
This is my first taste of linux in my 9 years of PC history. The only thing I've been asking myself since installing Gutsy 7.10 is "Why didn't I do this sooner?"

As an out of the box experience I've only had one major problem, and even then that wasn't down to Ubuntu... After dual booting with Windoze XP (I can't avoid this seeing as this is a family PC) I had no internet. I asked around on forums and searched google for two days solid with sheer determination and a heart full of hope trying to figure out what was wrong with my configuration with no avail. Then there was a godsend. A link pointing me to this forum mentioning something about enabling the wake on lan feature in Windoze. I had finally broke free from the machine known as Microsaft.

It's a shame so many people give up as easily as they do when there's so many people here and all over the net ready and willing to help. It's not even as hard to get your head around as I thought it would be... And not only that, how is anybody meant to appreciate the good times without the bad?

Anyway, I've been using Gutsy for a few months now and I can honestly say that I love Ubuntu and everybody that's made this possible. From the help here, to the developers that give so much time and hard work all for the love of the operating system. I'll never look back again.

Thank you!
