### Post by Steelmourne on 2009-05-12
Trying out Ubuntu through Wubi was a perfect choice. Vista on my old 1.6GHz laptop with 1GB of memory ran sluggishly with around 80% of memory in use. Ubuntu only uses 45% with a couple of apps opens. Its very refreshing that there arn't any security apps always needing updates and the whole look and feel of Ubuntu make Vista feel redundant. 

My next pc will be barebones with Ubuntu as the OS. I'm still surprised people are still sticking with WIndows. There is a learning curve but at least its something you want to do as opposed to stagnant Windows. Video drivers for a geforce 7400 installed smoothly and wirless with an Intel pro 3945 worked straight up after a post-install reboot. All up very impressed. Looking forward to try the KDE environment soon
