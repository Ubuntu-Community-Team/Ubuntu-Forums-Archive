### Post by techno-mole on 2007-02-11
Hello.
i thought i would post this, because it may give out a bit of hope to people who have had trouble, or are having trouble.
ive only been using linux (ubuntu) for just over a week, i had only heard about linux a year ago, but i had no idea what it was about, or even what it was exactly, so i decided to give it a try out of curiosity, and because i really dont want to be held hostage by microsoft, i was running xp, and i have 3 pc's in my house, so you can imagine how much it would cost to get vista for all of them, and not only that i would have to upgrade some components to even run vista, so after doing some reading i thought right thats it.
i tried a knoppix live cd first, and thought it looked quite good, so i then tried ubuntu, first i had it on a separate drive so i didnt get confused, well i have to say im glad i tried it.
i now use ubuntu as my primary os, i have to keep windows until some of the games i play are ported to linux, anyway to cut a long story short, i have the latest nvidia drivers running (took some doing) but they run very well, i am using beryl with all the whistles and bells on, and it runs smooth as silk, i have managed to get my external hard drives running using ntfs-3g.
basically i am very happy as i have a stable system, and it is far more usable and user friendly that windows ever was.
so if your having problems, then stick with it, because it is well worth it in the end, and it makes it all the better knowing that you have a choice and its easily as good and better than windows, even there new effort vista, i know some people who are using it and are having no end of trouble with it, i think linux systems can seriously rival microsoft any day, its just a shame that companies dont do more in the way of support for drivers and such like.
i am one very happy linux user. :lolflag:
