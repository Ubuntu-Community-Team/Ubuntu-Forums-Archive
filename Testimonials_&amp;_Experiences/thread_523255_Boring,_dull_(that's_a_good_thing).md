### Post by MichiganBill on 2007-08-11
Recently I had a friend over whom I hadn't seen for a while. He had told me that he was thinking about putting some unnamed Linux distro on his laptop. Unbeknownst to him, I had put Ubuntu on my laptop.

When he came over, he had put Ubuntu on his laptop as well! So he booted up his, and I booted up mine. He had no problems installing Ubuntu, same as me. Everything worked, same as me. He added some apps, no problem, same as me. (Well no problems worth noting, anyway.):KS

No heroics. No stories of overcoming difficulties by dint of clever computer derring-do. No excitement at all! How boring!

But boring is OK. We had lots of other things to talk about. But it goes to show...  Ubuntu just works.

Thanks Ubuntu Community!:KS:KS:KS
