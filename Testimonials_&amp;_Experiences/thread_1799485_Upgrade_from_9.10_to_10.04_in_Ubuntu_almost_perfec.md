### Post by deserthowler on 2011-07-07
About 2 years ago I gave my apartment manager, also a professional writer, a Dell PIII with 512 MB memory and a 20 GB hard drive.   At the time the box had Win 2K installed.  We spent some time installing his printer, a large professional quality HP laserjet about the size of a refrigerator.  I also added an old ATI 64 bit graphics card.

Shortly after that he got a virus and asked me to fix it.  I installed 9.10 Beta and put OOWriter and Firefox icons on the desktop. That is my Windows fix as I find Windows too complex to deal with ... no good command line.  

On July 4 I upgraded 9.10 to 10.04 in my apartment as I wanted to run ethernet cable instead of the WiFi (he runs off my WiFi router).  My tests showed the upgrade was fine.  However, today, July 7, he got it hooked up and no video.

I did a blind shutdown and looked at the machine more carefully.  He'd connected to the motherboard video and not to the ATI card.  I changed connections and rebooted and it works fine ... I haven't heard from him for about 4 hours anyway so I'm assuming so.  Next upgrade will be to 12.10.  Hope that works.  By then he might even have a P4.

BTW, sure was a lot easier to set up the printer in 9.10.
