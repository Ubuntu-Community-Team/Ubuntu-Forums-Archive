### Post by Hur Dur on 2011-01-03
I thought the topic implied that Ubuntu was light, so I was going to burst into this topic and flame you.

Anyway; It's great that you made the switch over to GNU/Linux, hopefully you learn a lot from your switch, and enjoy your new OS.

A piece of advice: Do not get rid of Windows before you have spent a month with GNU/Linux as your main OS. It's always good to have something to fall back on if GNU/Linux is messed up, and you don't know how to fix it. This is unlikely, of course, but just in case.
