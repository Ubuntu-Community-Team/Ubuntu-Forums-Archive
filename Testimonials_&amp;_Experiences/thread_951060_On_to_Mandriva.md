### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-10-17
i just started a thread a couple days ago called "back to gutsy". well, that was rather short lived. gutsy had a couple of freeze ups and i freaked out. then i decided to try mandriva gnome. i had tried mandriva kde, and thought it was awesome. but i had never given the gnome version a chance. all i can say is wow! it runs extremely fast and smooth. for some reason even the 2.0 firefox runs better than gutsy's. i think i'm going to stay with mandriva for a while and see what happens. 

to be honest, i may have had a bad hard drive that i was using with ubuntu, but i'm not sure. so i installed mandy on a different one. i'm not going to be leaving the ubuntu community, as i think it's awesome. plus, i will be installing ibex on a spare drive just to keep up on ubuntu's development. but for now, mandriva has won me over. peace.
