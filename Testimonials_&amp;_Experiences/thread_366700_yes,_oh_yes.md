### Post by jon61484 on 2007-02-21
I am running a home built setup. I've got no actual money invested in this machine, and it's pieced together from spare computers that weren't "fully functional" because of either hardware issues or software issues. I picked the best of what I could, traded in some stuff to get some better stuff, and I have what I have now. my only issue is for some reason when using the CD player it will sometimes freeze the computer. (ie: no movement) but otherwise, I've installed this system on two computers and had great success.

Overall, this dual 500mhz P3 with 256mb of ram with Ubuntu 6.10 does MORE of everything I do on the Windows XP powered P4 3Ghz /w 1GB 800mhz DDR2 ram. Download rates are faster, overall web browsing is MUCH faster, I can run waaaay more applications doing alot of work without it being boggy or slowing down much in comparison.

Life is good. And since this is linux... I can alter or edit anything I want. And that I like.
