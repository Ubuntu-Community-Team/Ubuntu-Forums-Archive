### Post by lennyjames on 2006-09-04
That statement stands true for a new convert! I have been using Ubuntu for almost 4 months now and I must say that it is absolutely brilliant! To start, I am not tech guru nor am I am terminal wizard, but I do know how to install OSs and build PCs from scratch. Ubuntu has been very good to my work and to my sanity. No longer am I bound by licences, crashes, fragged drives, viruses, trojans, dialers, registry cleaners, temp file cleaners, dogged networks...I think I should stop now. ;) 

Sure, Ubuntu has been fustrating for my printer, vid cam, and...that's all! Even though my Canon IP4000 has been intermittent, and that my work depends on it, I am a throughly satisfied Ubuntu user. This was certainly not the way I began trying a Linux platform - I was terrified in the beginning - literally. There were so many questions and apart from knowing whether my XP programs could be replaced, I didn't know where to start.

Since Ubuntu, I've tried other distros and they are all good, but I gravitate back to good 'ol Ubuntu. One heck of an amazing product. The one thing that keeps me amazed is the completeness of the OS. Sure, some things are not there in the begining (ATI and printer drivers for one). However, when Ubuntu was finally installed (in less than 30 minutes!), the first words out of my mouth were: "That's it? It's done and everything is there?" I couldn't believe that everything I needed was there and working. I then appreciated that this was a superb OS! Even after a few days had passed, I was still in awe!

There are just so many to thank out there for such a great OS and fantastic support for the new comers. Some of you guys are here on these very forums! So, if you read this short testimonial - THANK YOU!
