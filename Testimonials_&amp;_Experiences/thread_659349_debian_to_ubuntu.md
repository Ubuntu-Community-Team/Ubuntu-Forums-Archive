### Post by markharding557 on 2008-01-05
here's my account of my journey in to linux.
around feb 2005 i was searching through a freeware site called webgrid,on here was a link to an o/s called debian ever curious i had a look and after some reading i gave it a go and installed debian sarge,what a learning experience that was.
it was like being trown in the deep end,i knew nothing,green as grass,and yet after much googling and reading i knew how to install sarge from a commandline.
learned many other things as well on this.
anyway later that year august i think i started looking at other distros mainly ones based on debian,discovered ubuntu dapper drake,i installed it along side debian and it was wow! polished debian!it was like they had taken debian(which i still like)and took it to the next level and beyond,iv,e used it as my main o/s ever since.
can't get rid of some debian habits though i still tend to use apt-get and i customise my gnome desktop to be debian-like,icons on the desktop,root user etc.
basicaly i see debian as a diamond and ubuntu as skilled polishers who give it class
