### Post by patchin_house on 2008-07-06
*Saluton!*... It has been too long since I last used Ubuntu on a regular basis. I am finally up and running with Xubuntu 8.04 (Gnome taxed my video card a bit much, but XFCE fits just right), and so far the only hangup is getting Video CD (MPEG&ndash;1) discs to play... (I *did* enable DVD playback with VLC. Go figure.)

This is shaping up to be the year I turn 100% penguin, after years of using a Mac Mini for everything under the sun (or so it seems). (Especially after the news leaking out of Apple says that their next OS X won't be for PPC Macs like mine.) I expect to be doing a lot of posts to the Forums, especially on multimedia and digital video issues. I hope the Kino and Open Movie Editor regulars are ready and standing by...

I've enabled Japanese input support, but it would be nice to know which graphic applications can play nice with SCIM, so I can really have some fun. 

Other things seem to working just fine: Firefox and Thunderbird work as dependably as I would want them to, and it's nice to be able to hear [Radio Caroline]("http://www.radiocaroline.co.uk/") using VLC in the background. My webmastering chores get done with a Bluefish/gFTP/GIMP combo, although I really should explore Quanta, too. I should spend a day or two on Xara and Scribus. (And far less time playing Same Gnome...)

Philip David
2008.07.06
