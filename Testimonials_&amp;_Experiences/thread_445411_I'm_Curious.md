After ten (or more) years from my last (disastrous) try of Linux (red hat back then) and having two boxes (work-home) in windoze XP I gave Ubuntu another try.  I was so impressed that I now have two dual-boot boxes and I am using Linux more and more, as it is more stable and faster with my modest setups (AMD64 3200-3500 only, and nvidia cards 5200 and 6600) than the microshit OS.  I keep the windoze for games and some software that I cannot replace with Linux soft (although wine and vmware are doing a great job), but I can see that in some time, I'll become a full time Linux user.
