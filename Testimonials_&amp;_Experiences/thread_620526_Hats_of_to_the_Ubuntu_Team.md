### Post by rnwright on 2007-11-22
On a google search I came across a program called Wubi .... I installed the program went thru the set up - thru the dos like setup screens thinking that Linux must be a throw back OS, I was not expecting too much from this free operating system, to say the least. Well when the system rebooted and came to the dual boot screen and I opted to boot to Ubuntu ............... and to my surprise I came upon this robust OS that after a very enjoyable day of personal tweaking I must say is actually better than Vista ...with the cube desktop, fast web browser all the tools I need ... I have this feeling that my Vista desktop will be feeling a bit lonely ....  not that there aren't issues to resolve yet,  like getting Linux to see my older wintv card, and my webcam but those are small issues with the vast amount of knowledge posted on this and other websites.  Out of the box it recognised my network and my cable modem all drives and cd burners, usb ports etc .......amazing ...just amazing .Ubuntu has reinvented the wheel and made it better, with more traction and sleek refined looks. Ubuntu gets an A+
