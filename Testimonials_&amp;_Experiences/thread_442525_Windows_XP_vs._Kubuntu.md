### Post by Tanker Bob on 2007-05-13
When I built my new PC a few weeks ago, I simply plugged my old hard drives into the new box and booted.  Kubuntu Edgy booted effortlessly--as if nothing changed except it was much faster.  :)

Windows XP Pro, on the other hand, took about 4 hours and 10 resets to become fully functional.  Plus, it didn't seem to take advantage of my new hardware like Kubuntu does.  What a disappointment after all that effort!  Good thing that it isn't my primary system.  :)

For all the gory details, I blogged about the experience on [MobileTechReview](http://www.mobiletechreview.com/ubbthreads/showflat.php?Cat=&Board=tankerbobblog&Number=27604&page=0&view=collapsed&sb=5&o=&fpart=1).  I hope that folks enjoy reading about the experience more than I enjoyed living through it.  If I hadn't already decided to pitch Windows in favor of Kubuntu, this would have done the trick!

Thanks, Team Kubuntu!
