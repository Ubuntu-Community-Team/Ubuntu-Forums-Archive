### Post by wanderingarticfox on 2011-10-14
I just thought I'd say that yesterday when the Ubuntu notification popped up in regards to 11.10 being available I decided to go ahead with the Upgrade. I am still somewhat of a novice so I normally wait a week to 10 days to Upgrade and use the terminal. I decided to try the GUI on I guess was Day 1. Now it took quite a while but that is because of my connection speed.

I got nervous when it appeared that Gnome would not longer be supported by Canacal [sp?] but went ahead anyway. I also figured it was time to loose the training wheels and get on board with the Unity Desktop.

I was pleasantly surprised that my AMD/ATI Catalyst Driver and 'Centre' remained intact and that with only a few things needed to be done I was off to the races again.

I first started with Ubuntu 8.04 and have learned quite a bit but have more to learn in the coming years. I am reading through 'rutebook' and I am getting started on reading a guide/manual for Unity today. Have not found the screen saver controls yet but I am sure that is in plan sight and as I often do I am trying to go to fast looking for  it.

I just thought I would share, I also use Kubuntu on this new system so I will be updating it too. If you do not understand why you can ask or PM. I imagine most people figure all is good in Linux):P
