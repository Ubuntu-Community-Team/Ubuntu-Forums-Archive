### Post by craig10x on 2012-10-21
I'll bet you never gave unity a chance...work with it for a week then see if you still dislike it...many ubuntu users (including myself) reacted just like you did initially but came back to try it and worked with it for awhile and then found out that indeed it is very nice to use.. :)

I look at it like it's a shortcut taskbar/dock...i simply placed all my favorites and frequently used applications on it (for easy quick 1 click access) and for much less frequently used apps (like the terminal) i just pop open the search (dash) and bring it up in a second or two...

I also shrunk the icons in the unity bar down to around 38 pixels and also have it autohide so it is out the way except when i need it...
That all makes for a very pleasant operating experience...

Once you look at it in that "mind frame" your opinion may well change...There is a natural tendency for people to hate change (anything that is different then their normal routine)...
