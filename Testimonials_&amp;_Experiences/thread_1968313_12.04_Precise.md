### Post by 73ckn797 on 2012-04-28
I performed 2 fresh installs of 12.04. Unity seems improved over the beta and 11.10. I still like Gnome-shell and likely will use it mostly. Gnome Classic does not work with the alt-tab function. Unity and Gnome Shell have the alt-tab function and it works fine.

I stuck with 10.04 LTS until 2 days ago. I have 2 more computers that I will update to Precise.

Precise seems to run with a noticeable increase in speed. I am using the 32 bit version on one 32 bit and another 64 bit computer. Was using 64 bit and feel there is a slight performance advantage with 32 bit. I have no more than 4 GiB ram on the 4 computers so I will use the 32 bit. 

For work purposes I must use 32 bit since I have to use Adobe Reader and on the 64 bit OS I cannot download editable documents that I must have access to. The acroreader in the repositories will not work with 32 or 64 bit for my purposes. Using the Adobe Reader downloaded from the Adobe site, which is a 32 bit version, fills the bill for my needs.

I compliment the devs for the great job accomplished in 12.04 LTS.
