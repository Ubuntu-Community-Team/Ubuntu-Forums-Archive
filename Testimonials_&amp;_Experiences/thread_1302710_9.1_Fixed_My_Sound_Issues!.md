### Post by Maheriano on 2009-10-27
First I bought a piece of garbage HP laptop with Vista on it and I noticed the volume on the laptop didn't go very high. I had to actually wear headphones to hear anything unless it was dead quiet in the room, you couldn't even reposition yourself in your seat because it would be too loud to hear anything.

Then I got a Dell laptop with Ubuntu 9.04 on it and had the same issues, the sound wouldn't go loud enough to hear, I still had to use headphones.

I thought this was just a laptop hardware thing so I thought nothing of it until I installed 9.1 on my Dell and now it's super loud, I can watch movies and listen to music no problem. I can hear it even if I'm in the next room. I don't know how it did it but somehow it has fixed a sound issue I thought couldn't be fixed.

Thanks guys.
