### Post by wouterommeslag on 2006-04-16
Me and six friends from my local computer club migrated to Ubuntu. The reasons

1) Curiousity: we want to know what linux is about, how it works, what the difference is with XP, etc

2) Safety: we are really fed up with adware/spyware etc. We had to run ad-aware, spybot etc just to keep or pc in shape.

3) Speed:we heared that linux is much faster to work with (proven right!)

4) Money: I heared that the Vista releases will cost more than 150€ upgrade. If you want the aero version, even more euros have to be spend ....

How did it work out?

I had some trouble with my graphic card (ati mobility), but I found (on this forum!) a nice "howto" to upgrade my drivers, I followed it and now I have a nice 43 fps with caroussel screensaver!

A friend had some sound trouble, but there has been taken care of too.

One user switched back to win. The other six will use ubuntu for the rest of their computer days! I just hope that the upgrade procedure to dapper will be as easy as upgrading win ...
