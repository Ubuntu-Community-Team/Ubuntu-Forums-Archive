### Post by jonede on 2009-02-06
This is my very first experience with Linux (about 5 hours of use now).

I got crazy one night and loaded on this laptop, wiping out XP.  Brave, since I don't have recovery disks for this box.

I was expecting problems getting the internal Wifi card to go and getting the Citrix web client working.  I didn't have any problems - everything just works.  The display issues I read about with other Sony boxes didn't happen either.

There is the learning curve with any new OS, but I'm impressed.  This is one fast OS, and feature rich for taking up ~2 GB on the drive.  Those guys in Redmond, WA could learn something.

I'd like the wireless networking to start up and connect when the box powers on, but I'm sure that's just a setting somewhere.  Another setting I have to find is the workspace switching when scrolling sideways with the track pad.

All in all, I'm very happy with my little experiment.
