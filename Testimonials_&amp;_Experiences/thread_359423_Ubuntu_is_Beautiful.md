### Post by rko618 on 2007-02-12
Ubuntu is beautiful.  My desktop looks orders of magnitudes better than my XP desktop ever did (even with all the deviantart customizations).  My fonts and icons look absolutely incredible.     Colors are vibrant.  With beryl the wobbly effect when you drag a window seems so natural to me now that when I drag windows in another OS I think something is wrong because "it didn't feel right".  I could go on and on about Beryl/compiz but instead I will just say that while its true Beryl does not do a whole lot for usability, it definitely makes my desktop more aesthetically pleasing.  Too pleasing in fact.  I often find myself aimlessly playing with Beryl, completely distracted from what I had planned to do.

Every time I go to turn my computer on I get a warm feeling in my body, a feeling of excitement to use Ubuntu and glance upon its beauty.  

I thank everyone who has made this possible.


p.s. Who knew orange would be an excellent color for a desktop theme
