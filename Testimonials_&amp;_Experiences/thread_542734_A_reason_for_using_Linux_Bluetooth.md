### Post by deiseach on 2007-09-04
I've tried Linux a few times over the years, more out of curiosity than anything else. I never fail to be impressed by the fact that it's free, but it's never seemed to have anything 'killer' that makes me use it ahead of Windows.

Until now.

Moving items between my phone and my PC is difficult on Windows and downright impossible with my wife's phone. But I was playing around with Ubuntu today when I thought I might give it a go. Imagine my delight as the PC picked up (via the USB dongle) my handset with no fuss and I was soon shuffling photos and MIDI files around with abandon, just the way I should be able to. So, one up for Linux there.
