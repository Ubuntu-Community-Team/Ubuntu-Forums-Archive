### Post by Methuselah on 2009-11-25
I have had my share of stability problems in the Live environment but there are a couple fairly impressive things about Karmic:

1. My widescreen monitor is taken full advantage of from the outset without the need to edit Xorg.conf

2. It automatically recognizes my MB's Fakeraid and offers to install to a mirror. 
I almost fell out of my chair when I saw that!
[EDIT: Though I now realise I had the RAID feature disabled in the BIOS which means this might not be a good thing]

Karmic may be lacking polish and reliability in some areas there seem to have been some positive strides esp. wrt hardware support.
