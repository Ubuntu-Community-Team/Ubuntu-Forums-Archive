### Post by spudder on 2010-04-03
Hello Folks, I have found an easy way to install Ubuntu 9.10 on a Toshiba L505D- S5986. I used Ubuntu 9.10-Alternate-i386.iso burned to a live CD. When I started to install, I pushed F6, then checked ACPI=NO, and EDD=yes, I then installed as you normally would, everything worked perfect. Next I up dated everything I could, then upgraded to Beta2. I have found everything is working perfectly. Ubuntu Rocks, I love it.
