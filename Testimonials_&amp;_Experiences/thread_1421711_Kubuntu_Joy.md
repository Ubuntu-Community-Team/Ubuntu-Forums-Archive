### Post by CJ56 on 2010-03-04
Well after several years of devotion to Gnome I thought I'd try KDE

So I went & got OpenSuse 11.2 Everyone seems to think OpenSuse & KDE are the perfect fit. Without wishing to tread on anyone's toes, I found it clumsy & unreliable & a pain all round

So I tried Kubuntu with KDE 4.3.2

Absolute joy. It's responsive, beautiful to look at, seems to have exactly the same features as OpenSuse BUT has loveable Ubuntu underpinning it & can tap into all those .deb applications which make life so pleasant

If you're experiencing Win7 envy, forget it: Kubuntu is right up there. I will always have a soft spot for Gnome & will probably use it again some day, but while Gnome beats WinXP, it's just not as thought-through, sleek, smart, modern, as KDE

Just had to get it off my chest... ;)
