### Post by Izek on 2008-12-11
This was back when I had my Gateway MX Laptop with Intel 945GM Graphics and stuff, which was quite some time ago. Around 7.10 or so.

Ubuntu at the time was really peaking my interest, and I decided to install it. At first run, I had most of the menus and stuff memorized. The problems came after that when I had to use terminal for certain things, among remembering what commands do what. Soon after, I tried Xubuntu, which wasn't all that easy to get used to when going from Ubuntu to it.

Compiz worked much better on my Intel 945GM than it did on my ATI Radeon HD 3200 that I have in my desktop computer. I regret selling my laptop now... boy I was a fool. If I hadn't, I'd still be using Ubuntu, it's a great system with lots of potential.
