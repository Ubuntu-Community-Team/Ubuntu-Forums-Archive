Hi,

Here is my setup, I am sure that without linux I would not be able to make such use of my very old laptop.

I use it as a music server. Here's my setup. 

I use a samba server to share my media on from my newest desktop. I mount the samba share on this very old laptop. Where I run mpd, which can be controled from any other computer (windows, mac, linux, via internet...).

Today, I just found out that one can also run: 
vlc -i ncurses

Which is great, and easy. So far, that's the easiest way I found to listen to internet radios. Just a need a way to run Pandora (browsers like firefox are just beyond that computer's capability).

Anyway's, to arrive at this set-up it was quite easy. Shouldn't take more than 2-3 hours, even with the reading.
