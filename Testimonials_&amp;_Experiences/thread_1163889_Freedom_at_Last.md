### Post by Makatozi on 2009-05-19
I swicthed from Windoze to Ubuntu 9.04 and the feeling is so good. It is like facing a new life or perhaps coming out of jail. The feeling of freedom is wondeful.
 
I used MS-DOS and Windoze for many years due to the software for the kids education and games they paly. We could only afford the one PC but noe they both have their own and I switched back to Linux.
 
The last time I used Linux was in 1995 with a Slackware version. The difference is amazing and Linux has really grown up.
 
Wonderfull OS with great free software. Keep in mind what you put in is what you get out, persist and you WILL see Linux is the way to go.
 
 The installation is smooth, it runs stable and the the bundled software is really good. Small issues can be resolved the the few Windoze apps that I do need to run works great in WINE.
 
Really great stuff. I feel like a teenager again. Really exited about the OS.
 
If I do battle with something I know there are hundreds of knowledgable people on the forum that will gladly help.
 
Cheers ans all the best.
 
C ya on the forum,
