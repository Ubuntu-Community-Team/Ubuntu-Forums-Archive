### Post by whitegourd on 2008-07-16
One thing to consider are the capacitors on the motherboard.  If there are any of them have a warped surface rather than a flat one, that could be the cause of the random reboots.

I know the old Gateway Select SB systems had some problems with random reboots and the their tech support found out that the issue was due to bad capacitors.
