### Post by the8thstar on 2009-03-02
Hello there,

It's been two years since I joined the bandwagon. Overall I am extremely satisfied with the OS.

Thanks to my relative seniority, I feel that I'm entitled to give my recommendations to new users:

[LIST=1]
[*]If you can’t live without Windows, keep it and dual boot
[*]Always perform a clean upgrade to end up with a leaner, faster system
[*]Save yourself hours of painful reinstallation by using a partition for the system (/) and a separate one for your Home folder (/Home)
[*]Read ubuntuforums.org: most of the answers to your questions are already there
[*]Feel free to experiment with the command line but always backup your important files first
[/LIST]

That's all folks!

- ONLY use trusted repositories like medibuntu.org and getdeb.net if you must use 3rd party repos.

- NEVER create a root account and NEVER run Firefox as sudo.
