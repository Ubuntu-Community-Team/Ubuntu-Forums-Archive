### Post by Padie on 2008-04-30
I have been an avid Windows supporter for as long as I've known computers, and I would have never given up Windows for anything, until now.

My friend and I would have arguments over Apple and Microsoft the entire time - him having a glossy Mac and me having a pimped out Windows rig. We would argue about everything from viruses to the dock! I would always stand up for Windows, giving excuses and trying to find reasons, and so would he for Apple.

Now, I tried Linux prior to my switch. I absolutely hated it! I didn't know how to work it and thus didn't know how to configure hardware and get Compiz Fusion going, and so I didn't even try it again.

One day however, I decided that I wanted to go on a mission and learn about Ubuntu and so I went ahead and installed it. And my word did I get a change. It was the first time I installed it using and internet connection as well, and so everything configures itself! I have actually fallen in love with this great piece of software. But me being a gamer has to go a little Dual Bootie ;)

But honestly, this is my primary operating system, and in my opinion, Apple and Microsoft can get lost! Nothing compares! Now I can actually state cold hard facts when I argue with my friend!


Long live Ubuntu!
