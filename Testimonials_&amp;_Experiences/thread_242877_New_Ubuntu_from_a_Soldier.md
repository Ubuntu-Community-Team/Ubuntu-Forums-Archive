### Post by jchapman on 2006-08-25
> **franklee said:**
> Hell, Ubuntu is a pretty well rounded *nix distro. Its got everything anyone needs to run *nix and its very VERY friendly.

I'll say! I actually installed 6.06 on an 8-year-old laptop running an AMD K6 3D processor! I tried a bunch of distros on this antique and, believe it or not, Ubuntu's latest was the best mix of performance and features! 

I tried putting Windows 98 on it (for giggles, since it actually sports a "Made for Windows 98" sticker) and *that* OS didn't even know the LCD monitor could do 800x600; neither could it recognize the audio hardware [-( . Ubuntu, on the other hand, ran the system perfectly and - here's the kicker - it even correctly accessed my Ethernet-to-USB adapter which got this thing on broadband! :KS I downloaded program updates and added the usual Java and media codec support and found it all working as expected.

I'm going to return the laptop to the friend who gave it to me as a "broken" Win98 computer and show him what a *real* OS can do (albeit a bit slow on the antique)... Ubuntu totally ROCKS!!! \\:D/
