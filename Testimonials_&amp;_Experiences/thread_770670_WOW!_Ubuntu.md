### Post by ringer on 2008-04-27
I love it.  I was having trouble with my XP about a week ago, I couldnt do anything with my xp, I had lost all my school notes, music, and all the apps I loved.  So I went to a friend who gave me an burned copy of windows xp professional, and while I was at his house, I noticed he wasn't running windows on his computer.  he explained to me that it was something called Linux.  He explained to me all the benefits compared to windows.  

I went home that day to totally reformat my computer and dual boot with xp and ubuntu.  The first thing I loved about Ubuntu was the fact that I didnt need to put any product key or serial code or anything that was illegal! It's all free, amazing.  Everything I do now impresses me more, from how I can read files from XP, the speed of boot-up, the speed once it's booted, no spyware or virus threats, among a lot of other things...oh and we can't forget all the amazing graphic options with Compiz, and the personalization.

All in all it is the best OS I've ever used.  I was told not to upgrade to Vista, and that it would be just another headache.  Well to be honest, now that I've seen Ubuntu, I don't think I will ever go back to Windows.  

The only thing I now have a problem with is when I first boot into Ubuntu, my wireless settings are different than what they should be.  My WAP password is something completely different than what I had when I shut-down.  But all it takes is for me to open up my wireless properties and change the password and I'm all set.

Anyways, just wanted to give a shout-out to all the developers, you are awesome and keep it up.

-Ringer
