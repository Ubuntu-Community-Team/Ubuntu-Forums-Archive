### Post by Clemens on 2005-12-06
After 12 years and many grey hairs managing windows web, mail and list servers and using win95/98/xppro to manage remote web and mail servers I am in love. Head over heels in love with Ubuntu. I can't stop thinking about her. 24 hours a day.

Falling in love was easy when you see your productivity go up by at least 100% so that you have so much more time to play, when you find support in forums like this and so many other places... Ubuntu is fantastic.  This forum is fantastic and  the commitment made by the Ubuntu Team almost brings me into a state of religious fervor.

Eventhough I set Ubuntu up (on a slave drive) to dual boot with XP (on the master) I must admit that I have NOT booted into XP for weeks. Why should I; all my XP files are there when needed.

And I am still amazed how fast Internet seems to be when using Kubuntu. Mostly I seem to get a 10kbps bonus on downloads compared to XP.

Reading back over my post above I fear that Bill will come after me for alimoney or child support or something... Moderators, please spoof my IP address. ;) 

I hope that in the not to distant future I will be able to 'give something back' by participating in these forums to help. But I first better start learning something about this Linux thing behind Ubuntu.

In advance, a big thank you to this Ubuntu community where everyone is ready to help to make sure that my Ubuntu experience will be the best.

Thank you, everyone. :KS

Clemens Vermeulen
Cairns, tropical north Queensland, Australia
