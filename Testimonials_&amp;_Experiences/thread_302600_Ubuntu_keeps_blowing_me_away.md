### Post by oskie on 2006-11-18
I have been using Linux for a little over two years (mostly SuSE and Fedora) and recently switched to Ubuntu with the Dapper release last June. Dapper really impressed me but Edgy knocked me off my chair. I now have Beryl up and running on my PC (HP Pavillion a320n) and I can't get over the performance increase I have witnessed.  My daughter uses a Macbook and even she is thoroughly impressed with the pretty eye candy and snappy response of my new Edgy box. My Presario 2000 notebook is also running Edgy but w/o Beryl yet.

I originally took the Linux plunge because I was fed up with viruses, spyware and bluescreens of death ](*,) Now, thanks to Edgy, my 3 year old PC has been rejuvenated beyond measure. Thank you Ubuntu community! Keep up the good work. :D
