My other machine, a desktop I'm still dual booting with xp and Gutsy.  I love some windows games and haven't dedicated enough learning time for wine. 

Ubuntu is AWESOME,  I love the way  you can change anything you want in it!  Totally on the fly and not have to reboot.  Every time you turn around you were having to reboot windows.
