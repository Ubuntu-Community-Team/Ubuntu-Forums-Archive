### Post by sean lyon on 2011-09-27
Came to Ubuntu by accident...literally
After struggling with the almost redundant Win98se running an old dell PIII beige box, upgraded by getting one of them fancy laptops, got a pre installed  windows 7 as couldn't stand xp......but one day as i stood up from the table the dog ran to the kitchen thinking he was going to get fed, caught the power cable and launched it across the room. The HD was toast, no install disk for win7 from the vendor and not a warranty issue, no boot sector and with an ominous rattle.
Bought a new HD and was told to get Ubuntu on a CD as some of the tools were useful to get the info off the damaged HD.
After about an hour of messing about with Ubuntu it was on the HD forever and havn't looked back. In fact if i sit at my partners win7 or the worse Vista,i usually loose patience waiting for it to boot-she needs it as an OU tutor the software needs to be on a MS machine( might seek help here!)

Its easy to use stable safe and just better for me- kids on it now too as they cant mess it up.

Isues? - getting the wireless HP printer to load, and sometimes updating or installing packages....but thats just 'cause ive been on MS products since 95 and so the process wasn't familiar.
Then there was "Update manager" tempting me with 11.04 which i rushed into and got in a mess with- the support on here saved a fortune in support call charges and there is usually someone thats solved it so cut and paste a few lines into a terminal and its done!

Brilliant.
