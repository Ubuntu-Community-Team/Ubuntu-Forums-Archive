### Post by sneeks on 2008-07-10
a friend of mine asked me the other month about putting pc's in his children's rooms (4 of them) and how much it would cost.
so trawlled the web for some referbs and gave him the price.....
what!!! said he,that cant be right,are they stolen?
no i replied and explained that they would be referbs and would have linux as the os.
he then told me that the local pc shop had quoted him £2000.
so i get on with the job,but now 5 base units,3 with ubuntu and 2 with xubuntu.
3 days latter i turn up to install them all,he says..i hope there wont be to much cable around the house,to which i replied,no they are all wireless.
the man collapsed in a heap on the kitchen floor !!!!
after 2 hours all were up and running and all children were very silent,and his wife.
so total cost of hardware......£400,this includes some old crt monitors,keyboards etc.
payment for me...old base unit(athlon 64 4000,160hd,512 ram)plus beer for 2 months.....nice.
