### Post by mkvnmtr on 2009-03-07
I think some of you might enjoy hearing about my sound problem with Ubuntu. I recently installed on a 25 dollar computer I bought. Everything worked fine but the sound. I only got a little beep sometimes out of the speaker in the cpu. I have been busy so tonight I decided to see what the problem is. I have read lots of threads about how hard it is to get sound working so I figured I would surely mess it up a time or two.
 I checked to see if it was muted and it was. Fixing that and fooling with the controls didn't help. I was kind of afraid to do too much I had this install just the way I like it. I found the sound card in the terminal and which module it should use . It was using them but no sound. Streamtuner showed music playing but no sound. Golly I hate to screw something up. Then I pluged the speakers into another computer . No sound. Looks like my 25 dollar computer came with broken speakers. Gosh that is going to make this an expensive one. $25 for the computer, $25 for the memory and now $5 for the speaker.
