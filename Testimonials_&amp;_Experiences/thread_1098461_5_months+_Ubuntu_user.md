### Post by null.byte on 2009-03-17
Hello. My name's Stefan, I am 15 years old. I'm from Romania.

I am using Ubuntu for more than 5 months, and I can say that I simply adore it! I switched to Linux when Ubuntu was 8.04. I fell in love with it, so I kept only it. I don't like dual-booting.

At first, I was a bit frustrated that some applications weren't working, not even with Wine. But soon, I found Inkscape being better than CorelDraw and GIMP better than Photoshop.

On Windows, I used Fruity Loops to create beats... but version 8 doesn't work in Wine, so I had to replace this one two. I saw that LMMS is the perfect clone. It is missing some features that I hope the devs will add.

The GUI is great, I love GNOME with Compiz. Also, I like the community a lot. Very friendly people around here, and on IRC too!

It is very fast, secure and efficient. I am hosting my own website and the speed is amazing.

Overall, this is the perfect OS for me, and I'll never switch back to Linux. Even my mom finds Ubuntu easier than Windows.

My contributions to the Open Source community? Well, not many until now, I only translated [Why Linux is Better]("http://whylinuxisbetter.net") from English to Romanian, and Manu said that when he'll have time, he'll upload the translation.

Having these things said, nice to meet you all :)
