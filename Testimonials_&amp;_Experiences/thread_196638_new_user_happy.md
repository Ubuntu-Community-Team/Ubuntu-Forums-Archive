### Post by ooZAFLE on 2006-06-14
Hello fellow ubuntu users!
I just thought i'd share my story.
My friend is a complete n00b on all fronts to computers, he barely could get around in windoze. He kept on having issues with spyware and what not even after i did a complete make over to his winbox. So i asked him if he wanted to be  my experiment and try out linux. He said sure so i formated his dell laptop and isntalled dapper on it. I went ahead and installed all the multimedia codecs, flash etc for him since he probably wouldn't be able to figure it out off the bat. But I did show him what i was doing and told him how to do it. I gave him a tour of gnome and showed him what did what.
Three months later he is still using it, he has only had a few complaints and most of them i have ironed out and he is still a happy ubuntu user. He has figured out synaptic and how to do what he needs to do on it. He is no power user yet but this just goes to show that maybe linux is becoming closer to an average persons DE then some have thought.
