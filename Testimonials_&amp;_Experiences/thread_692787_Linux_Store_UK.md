### Post by garethh on 2008-02-10
I don't know if this is the right place to post this but i couldn't see a better one.

I decided to buy a set of Ubuntu disks  and figured that the Linux Store UK was the closest based on this site's recommendation.

I placed the order and waited...nothing turned up. I complained - they said wait (they were very slow in responding to my emails as well). I complained again - they said wait. I continued to complain so they dispatched the order again (according to them). Again nothing turned up so I complained - they said wait (that's if they chose to reply at all!).

I asked for my money back - they said wait So I waited some more and then repeated my request for my money back - no reply. I continued to request my money back and still got no reply.

To date I have neither received my order nor have I received my money back.

My recommendation based on my personal experiences - DO NOT TRUST LINUX STORE UK.

Has anyone else had a similar experience?
