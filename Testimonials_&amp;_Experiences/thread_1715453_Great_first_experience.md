### Post by CP001 on 2011-03-27
Just a quick but sincere thank you to everyone involved in the Ubuntu project.

I am not an IT pro. nor a very sophisticated user although my first exposure to PCs was DOS 2 (I think) and Lotus 1-2-3, circa 1983/4. Three days ago I installed 10.10. It was flawless except, no sound. Spent a few hours playing with Alsa and more hours in the forums. No luck. Suddenly thought about BIOS and that provided the solution. My Maverick_Meerkat is now working superbly. On reflection, much less time was spent on this installation than recent conversions (not upgrades!) to Windows 7.

I still have work to do however. I need to find a fully fledged, multi-user, double-entry accounting software and, an AVCHD editing/authoring software, which work with Ubuntu. When successful, I can dump Windows, finally.

So, well done Ubuntu and thank you.
