### Post by degenerate1991 on 2008-01-27
I have a not quite spectacular year and a half old acer aspire 3620. intel GMA somthing or another, 512 ram, 1.6 GHZ celeronM, 40 GiB HDD, atheros wireless :),  XP runs like crap on this machine and soon became unusably FUBAR. Of course no restore disk and I don't feel like pirating another XP VLK. So I tried my usual favorite distro MEPIS. MEPIS 7 was a bit buggy and my favorite wireless utility doesn't yet exist in the 7.0 repos. So I download gutsy from the website and installed it. I was suprised to find that everything just worked. Everything work)ed. Even the function keys that I had forgotten about. my HP printer requirred absolutely no configuration. No install cds needed. My machine is now  quite responsive and I have, amazingly managed to add 30 min to my year and a half old b battery despite the fact that I cant make speed step work. Now I laugh at all of the people I know who paid out of their nose for a MAC. I now have the functionality and reliability of osX on a mediocre laptop. And, I'm not stuck in the apple proprietary deathtrap. I will never have to pay for an os upgrade. Even the pretty effects now work. UBUNTU FOR THE WIN
