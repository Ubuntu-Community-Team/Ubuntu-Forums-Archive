### Post by u-noob-tu on 2011-01-10
what began as a solution to recover a password from my vista laptop became an absolute dream come true. im the kind of guy who loves to hack, tweak, and modify everything when it comes to gadgets. i like it even more when the makers of those gadgets make it easy for you to do those things. linux is the absolute epitome of freedom of choice. in the 2 1/2 months ive been using ubuntu, i dont think there is a part of my computer i havent messed with (with the exception of root. aint gonna touch that). there are so many things things i love about ubuntu that i probably wont be able to list them all here (and to keep this from being a rant).

First: "Have it your way" 
I hate how MS and crapple have their idea of how a computer should operate and thats what it will do and youd better like it. with linux, you can start from nothing if you want to and get what you want, how you want it. 

Second: "Everything you can do, I can do better"
I have found that everything i ever needed in windows is available for linux for FREE. Plus, with wine i can run windows .exe's without even a copy of windows.

Third, and lastly: "The community that shares together, grows together"
the linux community is absolutely incredible (id even go so far as to say it beats out mac and windows communities). when someone has an issue, somebody else is there to help. 

i have had an absolute blast learning linux and enjoyed myself so much that i made two screencast tutorials on youtube. to wrap up, it seems that the saying "the grass is greener on the other side" is very true.

Long live the power to choose! :P
