### Post by stchman on 2009-02-16
Hello all I am celebrating two years using Ubuntu.  I now use Ubuntu 99% of the time.  I find that there is no need to boot into Windows.  I dual boot on two machines.

I have Ubuntu installed on (5) different PCs including two laptops.  64 bit is also no problem either on my two Core 2 and Athlon 64 machine.

I have an Acer Aspire One with Intrepid installed and it runs great.

I was over at a friend's house and he was running a virus scan, WOW i cannot remember the last time I needed to do that or a defrag.

I also laugh at friends that spend time on torrent and warez sites downloading illegal software when they could be using Ubuntu and have the repos at their disposal.

This whole deal about audio, on all of my 5 machines the audio works just fine.  I gather that I am just REALLY lucky.  Also COMPIZ works just fine on all 5 machines.  I had to disable COMPIZ as it interferes with netbook-remix on my AAO.

All in all I really love Ubuntu and the usablilty of it.  Is it perfect?  No, but what OS is?  I can confidently say that I have had FAR fewer problems with my Ubuntu installs than any WIndows install I have ever had.  I do play some games, and I am surprised that WINE does a great job of playing them.  Occasionally I will boot back into Windows to play some games that WINE cannot.

I recommend Ubuntu to everyone I meet that asks me PC questions.
