### Post by MinoltaLuvR on 2007-05-02
been chugging along nice and stable for quite some time on 6.06 x32. 
upgraded to an nvidia 7800GS 256meg card and had added another gig of ram to bring me up to 2gigs total.

thought why not... downloaded 7.04, x32, burned it, tested it.. while browsing the forum in firefox via the live cd, decided to go ahead and install the x64 version instead.
reboot to hdd, download and burned the iso.

booted up, used ENVY for the nvidia drivers, which worked very well for that.
out of curiosity, i double clicked on an avi, it prompted for codec installs..
next thing i know i'm watching a movie with no effort!

pushing my luck a bit, fired up add/remove apps... installed PAN, Xchat, and of all things.. Beryl.
well.. i'll be darned if it all dont work nicely! i mean little to no effort at all!

and all just in time to show off to my brother in law, who just bought a laptop with vista on it.. wait till he gets a load of beryl lol.

great job on this release! loving it so far.

cheers!
