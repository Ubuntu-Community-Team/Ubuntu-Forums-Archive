### Post by Mattlock on 2008-09-24
I've just recently switched to full Ubuntu, and not looking back!
It all started at University with a bit of programming, then a flatmate of mine converted after making a major partitioning mistake , and I soon followed! 
Took literally less than a minute to sort out my wireless and it was all go. After minimal setting up and a minor headache getting ATI drivers/my display to work, everything is now working perfectly. It seems I can do pretty much do anything with ease - c, java, gaming, tedious office work and even sorting out my music/videos.
The forums, support, and amount of information out there is amazing! Keep up the good work, it makes me smile every time I boot up (knowing that I'm finally free), and I figure I'll be preaching to everybody to join us all!

Cheers!
Matt.
