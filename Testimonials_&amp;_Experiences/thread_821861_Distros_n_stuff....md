### Post by freebeer on 2008-06-07
Slavishly upgrade?  Depends on the person, I guess.  While I'm using 8.04 on most of my machines, I still have a 7.10 running.  No reason to upgrade it, so I didn't.  With regards to 8.04, I tried out the beta on a spare machine, just to have a look.  When 8.04 was officially released, I then put it on that test machine and took it for a spin.  Since it worked well, I rolled it out to other machines without issue.  While on two machines 8.04 wasn't necessary, it solved a wireless issue I had with a 3rd machine.  That was definitely worth upgrading to in this case.


But I'm an old fart... I haven't been lulled into buying (or buying into) the "latest and greatest" for decades now.  One does learn from experience, I guess. :D
