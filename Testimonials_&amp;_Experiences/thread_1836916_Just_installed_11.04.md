### Post by saltywalsh on 2011-08-31
I needed to wipe my computer because it was cluttered with so much junk from the past couple of years so I decided before I throw Windows 7 Pro back on my machine I would give Ubuntu another shot.

I started using Ubuntu back in college 4 or so years ago and had a decent experience and was using an older model Dell Inspirion at that time for testing purposes.  

I can say that I still use a Windows box in the living room for live streaming but I must say that this recent installation of Ubuntu went without a hitch and found all drivers and the installation of other programs was extremely improved over the early versions of the OS.  This is attributed to such a great community that has stuck with and improved the distro.

Anyone looking to transition over will find that the interface and installation is extremely easy and stress free and if a problem does occur that the community is there to assist them with any issues.
