### Post by Craig Logan on 2010-08-29
Hello community,

well, I'm not exactly a new ubuntu user. I have been using ubuntu ever since the release of feisty, even though I permanently adopted ubuntu as my primary OS when gutsy became available. I never felt any need to join these forums, even though I have been browsing through them occasionally.

Finally, though, a friend of mine and long-time member of the ubuntu forums convinced me to register, so here I am.

As stated above, I have been an ubuntu user for several years now and I don't have much to complain about. There was nothing that I didn't manage to fix and the main reason I use ubuntu is that even as a programmer, I don't want to spend time getting my operating system to work.

In fact, setting up a development environment in ubuntu is much more easily done than under windows. For the most part, its just a few shell commands.

Earlier this week I finally upgraded to 10.04 (I've been happy with 9.04 ever since it was released) and while it works perfectly for the most part, there are some things that worry me (for example, you manually have to add back the "edit path" button in nautilus and then this thing with the window icons being at the wrong side...).

Thats all I can think of right now.
