### Post by k64 on 2010-01-27
I have run Karmic, Jaunty, Lucid, and Mint 7 on my desktop. All of them recognize my hardware especially well.

My hardware:

AMD Phenom X4 9750 CPU @ 2.40 GHz
Kingston KVR667D2N5 2 GB RAM
500GB Western Digital OEM HDD
ATI Radeon HD 2400 Pro graphics
ATI TV Wonder 650 tuner
ECS A770M-A motherboard
Antec EarthWatts 380W PSU/Antec case bundle
LG Super Multi DVD/CD drive
HP vs 15 monitor, resolution: 1024x768
Micron wireless mouse (that came with a now broken keyboard)
Compaq 5185 keyboard
Creative SoundBlaster Audigy SE sound card
Extra PCI bus fan (to enable overclocking) [brand:Thermaltake]
Cooler Master AM2+ CPU fan
External 500 GB SimpleDrive

Out of all this hardware, the only thing that didn't work is the TV tuner. Nice job, Ubuntu! You recognize more hardware than Windows 7 RC ever did!
