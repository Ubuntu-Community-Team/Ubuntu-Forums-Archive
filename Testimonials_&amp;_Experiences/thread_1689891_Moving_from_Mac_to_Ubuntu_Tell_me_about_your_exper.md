### Post by arunb on 2011-02-19
> What do you love about Ubuntu?

Its Free, open source and the best of all secure and does not need any anti-virus software. With windows systems this was a major headache as the AV software slowed down the entire system. 

> What apps do you miss most?

Microsoft Frontpage, actually I use it through VirtualBox so I don't really miss it.

Unfortunately I haven't found an open source application that is comparable to Microsoft Frontpage.

> Was the move easy or hard for you, and why?

It was hard initially, my first attempts (Ubuntu 7.10, 8.04) ended in failure as I found the OS very different from Windows but it became easy after I started playing around a bit. So when 9.10 was released I was able to install and use it very easily, this was also partly because most of the hardware was compatible with Linux.

I now use 10.04 on my laptop with absolutely no problem. 

I think most problems (at least in my case) were driver related.
