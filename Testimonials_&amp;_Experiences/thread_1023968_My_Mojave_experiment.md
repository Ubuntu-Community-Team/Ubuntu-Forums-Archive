### Post by tomeriker on 2008-12-28
Ok so I got this Idea from the Microsoft Mojave experiment. You know the commercial where MS "fools" people into thinking vista is the "next" windows and of course they all say they love it.

I have a vista / ubuntu dual boot laptop. It's a real nice Alienware M17 so it turns heads as soon as I pull it out of the carry case. I then ask if they have seen MS's new OS. When they say no, I first show them Vista. Then I reboot into Ubuntu and start to explain how there is no need for antivirus software and how they don't need to buy MS office or photoshop, or anything else for that matter because the new "windows" already has it installed for free.
Then I blow their mind with compiz and the cube and burning windows and water effects.

Last night I took my laptop to the local convenient store/ coffee shop and was showing one of the clerks and people just kept stopping what they were doing and started asking how they could get this "new windows", how much did it cost. I bet 15 people were asking me to load it on their pc.

I got the same response from every one; whats linux/ ubuntu?
Is that what the next MS is called?
No really, how much does this cost?

It was really sad, because only a couple even believed me that it was free, saying, " nothing is free in this world, there has to be a catch".

Over the past year I have converted 5 people to Ubuntu. All of which I was doing XP tech support for. I was weekly being called to repair windows. I haven't been called back to any of them since I setup their Ubuntu. I only see them in passing and ask how it's going. All respond positively.

I FEEL LIKE THE MAYTAG REPAIRMAN.

Well I've written too much they way it is. Thanks to everyone who has anything to do with linux/bsd and Ubuntu for making a great distro.
