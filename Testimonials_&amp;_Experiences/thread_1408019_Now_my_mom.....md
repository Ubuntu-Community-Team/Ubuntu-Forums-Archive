### Post by rmccutchan on 2010-02-15
First, my three year old grand daughter is using Ubuntu, and now I just cleared off Windows for my mom and installed Ubuntu 9.10.

She is 60 and very non-knowledgeable about computers and called me about to cry because her computer didn't work right.  Now, she has emailed me several times to tell me thanks for changing things for her.  She even tried to pay me!  Sheesh, mom!

The only hiccup we had was her Lexmark printer did not like linux.  So she bought a Canon all-in-one, and it didn't like it either.  We could have probably got it to work, but I needed something absolutely bullet proof for her.

We bought a new HP printer, plugged it in, and it worked perfectly.  Did not have to install or change anything.

Now she is even happier!

Again, a HUGE thanks to the many people who make this Ubuntu Linux thing work like it does.  I have been a convert since last August, and could not be happier!!! :P
