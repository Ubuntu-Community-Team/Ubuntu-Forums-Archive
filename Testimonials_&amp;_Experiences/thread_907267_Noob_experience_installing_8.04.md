### Post by wgarider on 2008-09-01
I've been running Ubuntu 8.04.1 now for just at a week. I thought I'd relate my experience since I consider it very successful.

I purchased an off-the-shelf Dell 1525 at Best Buy last Tuesday. It came with Vista but I knew I wanted to install Ubuntu before I even purchased it. One thing to note is that I had been pricing the Dell's online for a couple  of days and online pricing ranged anywhere from $35-$300 more than I payed at BB for the same model.

A bit about my knowledge level....I've spent the last 15 years primarily as a Windows SysAdmin with a bit of RedHat and Solaris experience thrown in. With that in mond, the tool I found most useful getting started with Ubuntu was this forum!

After I got the laptop home, I downloaded the Dell ISO, burned a DVD and installed the base OS with that. Subsequently, I did a lot of reading here - other than doing the updates through the Synaptic manager, the only thing that required some time to get working was wireless. I followed the instructions for using the ndiswrapper and after completing that, I was up and running. One other thing I did was use the Dell Wiki to check and fix any of the known issues with the 8.04.1 install.

I've also used the great instructions here to install VMWare Player and a few other things but by and large, everythig I've run into was covered here. I appreciate the terrific resource this forum is.
