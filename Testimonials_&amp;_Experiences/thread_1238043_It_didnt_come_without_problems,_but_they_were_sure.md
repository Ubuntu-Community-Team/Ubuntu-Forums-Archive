### Post by madman118 on 2009-08-12
So I dual-booted Ubuntu to my Windows XP system, and I actually like computers again! Although Ubuntu did have an issue working with my graphics card drivers, I didn't have to spend 30 miiutes to an hour fighting my computer, instead a few lines of cade and the system was running smoothly. It(Ubuntu) runs faster than windows, has only crashed on me once(see above, not really a crash per-say) and i am glad to report that there have been 0 malware/virus intrusions in a month and a half. I also just recently installed Ubuntu netboox remix on my new Acer Aspire, and it seems to be taking to the Atom processor alright.
