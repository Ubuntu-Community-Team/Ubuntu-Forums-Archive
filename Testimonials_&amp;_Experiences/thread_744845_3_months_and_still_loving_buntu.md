### Post by RJ Hythloday on 2008-04-04
I've used kubuntu dapper, gutsy, fluxbuntu, xubuntu, mint kde.
 
At the moment I'm using xubuntu on some older hw, and have a new project (still old hw, but faster) that will get kubuntu Hardy w/ KDE4, tried the live cd, love it, just need flash to work.
 
Took a lot of effort to get flash working right on the first system, removed sound card, usb speakers didn't work, finally down to cheap speakers w/ onboard sound, it's going to be the kids pc so no big complaint.
 
Best part is open office, excel was taking 30-50 seconds to open every file, I have a phone number, and checking register I use all the time so waiting on those was murder, open office opens the same files in 2-3 seconds even on 1.5 ghz!
