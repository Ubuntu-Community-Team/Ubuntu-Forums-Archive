### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-01-03
If you performed an update recently, that sometimes messes up CUPS the printing system.  Try deleting the printer completely and reinstalling.  Then print a webpage from firefox or chrome.  If that works, then try printing from thunderbird.

Updates that break things are called "regressions" and it happens, but many times it can be fixed.

Good luck and post your experiences.
