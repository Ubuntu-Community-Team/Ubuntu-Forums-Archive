### Post by harishg on 2006-04-17
I have been using Suse for a long time.It was working alright.But I always wanted to shift to a debian based distro.I was reading through some distro watch site and found that ubuntu was on the top of the list and so browsed through the website and got the CDs shipped to me.

The installation was smooth, everything worked perfectly from my wireless card to a winmodem.Also with access to debian repository it was easy for me to get all the other necessary softwares installed.Even the DVDs worked fine out of the box.

I have distributed the remaining CDs to 5 of my friends and hope to have my professor convinced to change our windows based workstations to linux.

-Harish
