### Post by Maci_01 on 2006-06-22
I have recently installed Ubuntu Dapper about three weeks ago. 

After a few hurdles (nvidia driver, software, users, keyboard media controls, setting up sound, networking, printers, compiz, mpegs, mp3s, flash, java etc) I am confident to say I am going to use Ubuntu full time. These problems may have been annoying but getting past them was well worth the effort.

My desktop of about six years (with video card, motherboard & case upgrades) runs a lot smoother and looks a lot better than how it did on Windows. I can play music, go on Gaim, visit websites and even play games on Ubuntu better than XP.

I have found a new appreciation for open source software and value the freedom it provides. Dapper has given my old box a reason to live.

Thank you everyone at the Ubuntu Forums for all your help and thanks to the wonderful people who made this release successful ;) Can't wait for Edgy Eft.
