### Post by Caligula on 2006-05-17
I only use linux/ubuntu for a few months, and it's by far the greatest OS(for me..)!

Until two months ago I was still using windows, but I always wanted to try linux, you know, the usual Firfox story..:-D 

So when my computer needed another format, I had enough, and installed Ubuntu breezy on it.

Everything worked out of the box(except wireless, still not working), no extra configuration was needed, just plain good.

A few weeks later I decided to try some other distros, I tried debian, gentoo, arch, and "ehad"(mandriva). 
Out of all those I still think ubuntu is by far the best, it's not too easy and not too hard, it's exactly in the middle... unlike mandriva it's not teaching me to be afraid of the command line, and it's also not expecting me to know everything that goes on in the computer, like gentoo or arch.

There is no real difference between ubuntu and debian, for me installing ubuntu is simply installing debian the easy way, without all the configuration... not to mention that ubuntu actually works in the 21st century...

So, ubuntu(actually kubuntu..) is exactly for me, my first and best distro.:D
