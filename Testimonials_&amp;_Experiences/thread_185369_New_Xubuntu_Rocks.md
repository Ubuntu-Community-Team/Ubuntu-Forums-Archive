### Post by aysiu on 2006-05-31
With Dapper, Xubuntu is almost at the point where I might recommend it to regular folk who have old computers they're willing to ditch (and that can be saved!).

**New Stuff in Dapper**:
1. Much better-looking
2. Great splash screen (the running mouse)
3. No password needed for logout
4. Automounting of external media
5. Thunar instead of Rox-Filer
6. Defaults to Thunderbird for email

**What I'm hoping for Edgy**:
1. Some kind of trash instead of just deleting files
2. A GUI for adjusting the time
3. A really editable menu instead of having to create custom menu to get it how you like it

Basically... a workable desktop environment but still slim enough that it doesn't eat resources the way KDE and Gnome do.
