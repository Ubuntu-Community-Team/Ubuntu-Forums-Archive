### Post by LowMen on 2008-07-26
I have been a Windows user since 3.1 and just accepted that Microsoft ran the market on O/S' and lived with it.  Over the years I would try Linux for a few days say "bah" and delete it from my system.  But a few years ago I started playing with open source software and thought "sure it's not as pretty as the software I buy! hey it gets me from point (a) to point (b) and was free!

So the other day after spending an hour with a customer trying to work out a bug in Vista I banged my head on the desk and accepted she was going to need to reload vista (I hate taking that route). Went home and loaded Ubuntu that a co worker mentioned the other day *****WoW*****

Installed under 20 minutes, most of the apps I wanted were in a drop down list and the community (you guys) are fantastic :popcorn:

Every time I need an answer I run a search and b-i-n-g-o it's been asked and answered (sometimes even with pictures).  So I just wanted to say 

Thank you for all your answers and stories and although I am not windows free and prob never will be (have to support it at work) at least when I come home I can escape and enjoy this very well put together O/S.

Lowmen.....
