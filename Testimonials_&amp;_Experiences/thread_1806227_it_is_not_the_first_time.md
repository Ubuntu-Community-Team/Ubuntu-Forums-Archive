### Post by armandh on 2011-07-17
the I phone/pad/etc is a new interface not unlike the original windows was in its day. windows 8 will be catch-up and unity is in the field testing mode. but I am not ready to leave 10.10 [no surprise as, where I must use win, it is xp]

however, last thursday I had the fun of exposing elderly computer users to ubuntu [and puppy] at a local computer club meeting. open to 55 and up they are mostly window centric save one guy with a tux net-book along side his 7 laptop. mostly it was opening there eyes to the opportunity to re-purpose old hardware showing puppy on a 633 celeron and suggesting that above 900 mhz [1ghz recommended] with at least 512 mem and 64 meg video, ubuntu is #1 

given the amount of hardware that runs xp and the few years before the os is no longer supported, there will likely be a lot of hardware available. 

but with the age range of this group [and I fall in the middle] we may need a refresher course then. [if possible, given most of us already avoid buying green bananas]
