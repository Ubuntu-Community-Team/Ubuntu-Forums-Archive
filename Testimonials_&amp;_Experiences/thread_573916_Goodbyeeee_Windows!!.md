### Post by nostrodamned on 2007-10-12
Finally done it!

With a growing hatred of anything with the Microsoft symbol over the years I had tried just about every Distro out there! 
From Linspire to Mandrake I was looking for a distro for a semi computer literate user that could ease my transition into Linux.

I either found them too cluncky or way to technical! what the hell is the ndis wrapper anyway!
After rave reviews of ubunto, I thought ok time to see what the fuss was about!
After cleaning the dust of an old Athlon 800 mhz/256mb desktop that i had cunningly disguised as an ashtray holder and installing some new hamsters to power the thing i tentatively picked up my newly burned live CD - and looked for a CD rom....DAMN nufin in it-think it was cannibalized over the years! Ok ebay shoppin! 

So finally with the powerhouse finally adorned with a new 24x CD rom we were ready to go.

I turned this bad boy on and after the fan had thankfully evacuated the 6 kilos of dust that had accumulated(to the screams of joy from my wife when the bedroom got a new powdered look!) the ubuntu logo came up on screen!! Wahey we were rockin!

After a loooong wait and looking at a blinking cursor a very attractive screen appeared.
MMM.  this looks similar to windows - what happens if i click here....
Though off the Live CD ubuntu ran a bit like a dog - but hey who was i to comment? This cant be right it hasn't asked about hardware? Is everything working? Sound - check, Network - check, Screen quality - check...This cant be right...Everything works??

Well blow me bilge pump! Ubuntu is running and detected EVERYTHING.. ok now to the installation - I click the setup icon and away we go.... 
....
....
Should have got a faster CD rom
...
...
almost there
,,,
,,,
Wahey its asking to reboot,

I take the Live CD out and wait for the reboot - 17 seconds on the nose!!!
That cant be right - no operating system startes up in 17 seconds! I usually go for a quick massage at my local health club while waiting for a reboot.  Ok lemme try this again - Rebooting!
15 seconds later im back at a login screen!!!!
My masseuse is gonna fall out with me!

Well ubuntu is alive and kicking - installed a few bits and bobs and it runs like a dream. This super intuative interface is fantastic and my laptop now quivers in fear as it sees the Live CD getting ready to kill its XP installation!

What more can i say guys - i have been using ubuntu for over two weeks and somehow my dusty old pc has become my main computer - congratulation to the programmers and contributers to this great Distro - finally a replacement for Windows....
