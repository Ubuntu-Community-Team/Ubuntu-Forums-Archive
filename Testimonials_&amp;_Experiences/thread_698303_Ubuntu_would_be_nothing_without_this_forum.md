### Post by fatdadkev on 2008-02-16
Although I spent a few years doing basic tasks on unix and our company supports a lot of Linux users (which I have always kept a distance from) the switch from Windows to Linux is always considered a step to the dark side - or as we refer to it "command line junkies".

However - Ubuntu and the features it offers are too good to resist, I started demonstrating Linux Live CD's on our training courses, they are a major tool in testing "dead" or corrupt machines. We then started using them to help us recover data from corrupt disks on site and I just had to find out more ...

Without the stars of the forums I for one would have had no idea what I was doing, every question I have had has been resolved in this forum and its led me to move forward to learn more.
My team of engineers are now hooked on Ubuntu purely because they too found all their questions were answered, they saw the flexibility it offered and the support that is out there from real people.

it might sound like a suck up but without the people who take the time and trouble to answer your questions knowing they are appreciated then this will not exist.

That's my soapbox piece said - all credit to the guys who make this the success it is - Keep it up and get the beans in.
