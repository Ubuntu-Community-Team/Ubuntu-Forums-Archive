### Post by bishop379 on 2011-07-10
All the years (and money) I wasted on Windows based computing. I have only been using Ubuntu for a month, and it keeps getting better every time I log on. After today, every pc I operate is full Ubuntu, with the exception of one dual booter Win 7/Backtrack 5 for hobby purposes.
   I haven't been this excited about computing since I opened my first box. I have spent every second of me time, learning and trying everything I can think of with this distro. The awesome thing is, unlike my old OS, I haven't been bored once. I can't believe I have missed out on this kind of freedom for so long. 
     I want to let the contributors know how cool it is for what they do, I hope I can learn enough so that eventually, I can be one of the people that contribute to keeping this freedom around for alot longer.:guitar:
