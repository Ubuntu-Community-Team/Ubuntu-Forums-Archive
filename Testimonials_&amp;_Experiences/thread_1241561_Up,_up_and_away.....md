### Post by longtom on 2009-08-16
Feel like a superhero....

We have some volunteers here from the UK, helping us with the animals and all the tasks on hand on the ranch.

One of them came to me just now, having accidentally deleted 1.7 GB of photos from his sd card.  His whole trip to South Africa - gone. So here he was, down to the ground asking for help.
Now - I have tried recovery before, in Windows.  What a mission it was.  Either you will be charged an arm and a leg or you believe you are busy with a free program only to find out, that it is only free to scan but not to recover....you all know the tricks of some companies.

So I told him to give me the card - nothing to loose really, and come back in an hour.  And of I went....Google.

After some blanks I found [this](http://blog.mypapit.net/2007/10/how-to-recover-photo-files-from-sd-card-mmc-with-photorec.html) page.  Followed the instructions and...recovered all the photos.  It was as easy as pie.  I mean, no hoops, no wrong promises, no fancy gui, no huge advert - just good value.

Needless to say, for this guy I am the hero of the day.  So I told him about Linux and Ubuntu....he didn't look really interested.  But who knows - a seed is planted.
Another catastrophy in that vein and he might just remember.

Anyway, felt I'll let you know....
