### Post by l.tambiah on 2006-07-30
Ubuntu is a GNU/Linux operating system which demonstrates a great selection of open source and free software. I haven't really had any major issues with Ubuntu in terms of hardware detection, but I have always chose my hardware wisely for GNU/Linux compatibility.

At current I am running Dapper Drake 6.10 on my desktop machine and IBM Thinkpad R50e Laptop. No dual boots either, Ubuntu is my 100% Operating System of Choice.

As part of my contribution I have launched a blog focused around Ubuntu and the GNOME desktop called [OSSGeeks]("http://www.ossgeeks.co.uk") (Open Source Software Geeks), which aims to contain useful posts in these disciplines.
