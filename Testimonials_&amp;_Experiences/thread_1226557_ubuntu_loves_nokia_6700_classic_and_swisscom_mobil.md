### Post by squiddie on 2009-07-29
I just got that new Nokia 6700 and wondered if I can get Ubuntu online with it. So :)

- I started up Ubuntu with the 9.04 Live CD,
- connected my Nokia via USB,
- chose "PC-Suite" on the phone, Nokia's Software which of course isn't installed on the Live CD ^^

- got a popup, something like "new broadband",
- chose "Switzerland" and "Swisscom",
- didn't change any values, it's all just fine :)
- gave the connection a name and
- was asked to give a keyring password.

Now I was able to activate that connection via the toolbar. Eh voilà -> I'm online with my laptop without war driving :P

There was nothing to figure out or to install. This is awesome. Gotta love Ubuntu.
