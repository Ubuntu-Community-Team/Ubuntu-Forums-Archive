### Post by biffta on 2008-08-07
So last night I got hold of a Logictech Quickcam (QC 4000 i think) as I needed to make a video call. I really feared I was going to be in for a night of hackery with Ubuntu 8.04 and probably poor end results, but this was how it went down.

Plugged in the cam.
Searched the forums for some cam software.
Installed camerama - Cam was working straight away.
Downloaded and installed the latest version of Skype.
Made a call.
Started video.

And that was it. I was so impressed. Quality was great and everything.
