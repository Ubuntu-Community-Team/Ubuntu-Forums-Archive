### Post by XysT Reaper on 2008-07-09
Last summer I made the mistake of switching from XP to Vista. I have a P4 3 GHZ, 1 Gig of Ram, 4 year old computer with a new, decent video card. Anyway, after buying the new card Vista ran a bit better, but it was always SLOW. Everything took forever. It always came up with stupid little prompts asking this and that. So, somehow yesterday at work I thought about Linux and googled it and came up with Ubuntu. I decided I would give it a try, so I went home, downloaded and launched my new OS. 

It took me a while to get all my video drivers set up, but I was extremely happy with it for the most part. I'm still a bit of a gamer, so I might have to switch back and forth between the two, but for basic computer use... Ubuntu gets 2 thumbs up for me. - Thank you for all the hard work everyone has put into it. 

So, one day into Ubuntu, and I have seen the light.
