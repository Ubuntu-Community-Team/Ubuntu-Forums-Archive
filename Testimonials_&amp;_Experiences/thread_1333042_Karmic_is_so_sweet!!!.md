### Post by gordong11 on 2009-11-21
I was a Jaunty beleiver and tried the Karmic Beta, and swore I'd never upgrade.......but.....

I was so wrong, Everything on Karmic works fab, and this is by far the best Ubuntu release to date. I love:

1. Software Center
2. Ubuntu One
3. Grub 2
4. Ext 4 (yes I notice a difference)
5. Overall user experience is just better
6. A breeze to configure and apps run better.


I just wanted to thank to developers and Canoical for their hard work. Such a great OS. I haven't had one freeze either. 

May I suggest adding the Nvidia 190.42 driver to the hardware drivers list right out the box on install, now that it has been certified by Nvidia? It's a major improvement over the 185 drivers. For example, you cannot play Eternal Lands (Free MMORPG) with the NVidia 185 Driver, you need to downgrade to the 180 (Im not sure if u can on Karmic) or 173 drivers or upgrade to the 190 drivers. I tried the 173 and 190...The 190 is the much better performer.
