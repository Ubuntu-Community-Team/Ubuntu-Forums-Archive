### Post by asddf on 2010-04-13
Hello.

I first used Linux about 6 years ago, while it was very fast and very stable, it was far too difficult to use.

I decided to give Ubuntu a try about one month ago, it's been fantastic, very fast, very stable and so easy to use.

Ubuntu has taken Linux from the most unfriendly installs to ever exist, to the easiest the software center is brilliant.

After using Ubuntu for a month, I decided to install it on my parents computer, that was literally a permanent snails speed, due to mass viruses and Microsofts poor Kernel that doesn't seem to understand that when you ask a program to stop, it's meant to........stop.

Even my no computer experienced parents are loving Ubuntu.


Thank you Ubuntu Developers!
