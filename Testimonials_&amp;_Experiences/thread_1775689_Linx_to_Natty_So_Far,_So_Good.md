### Post by Whizzospace on 2011-06-05
Compared to my troubleshooting experiment called the 10.04 LTS upgrade, the move to Natty has been nearly flawless. Surprisingly, ALL of the hardware functioned correctly as soon as I installed the Nvidia and Linksys Broadcomm proprietary drivers. And not a single multimedia playback flaw so far.

The best part for me is the easier network and drive sharing tools (through Samba). I have multiple OSes running between desktops, media servers and handhelds, and the shared drives are all playing well together thus far.

Like many people, I'm just not sure on the new GUI. Am still logging back and forth between Unity and the classic view with Docky, trying to decide what works better.

---------------------
Intel 875PBZ w/P4 3.0Ghz
4GB RAM
Nvidia 6200 AGP8x 256MB
