### Post by gholen on 2007-03-14
The day I installed linux, was i foggy day in Uddevalla, sweden, in may 2001. I was fedup with Windows 98 on my laptop (IBM ThinkPad 360). I was desperate for a change, or at least, not having to reinstall every week. So, I talked to a friend of mine, who told me he could instll Linux for m, a free operating system, and I wold have no problems with viruses, worms, and popups. I said you, and the day after, I got my laptop back, with Slackware installed.
The experience from slackware whas all good, and so I started thinking. Why were I using Windows.
The journey inside the world of Linux/BSD begun.
After about a year, I tried out Redhat 7.3, and then Debian, i think it was 2.2, and then, ubuntu.
Today, all of my computers are running Linux, in different sets.
My server are at this moment running slackware 11, and my laptop and WS are running ubuntu.

My very old Mac is unning NetBSD at the moment. And in my hacked La fonera, I'm running OpenWRT.

How did your journey begin?
