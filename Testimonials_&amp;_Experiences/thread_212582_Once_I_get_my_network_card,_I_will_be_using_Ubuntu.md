### Post by ade234uk on 2006-07-10
I was using Ubuntu full time for 8 months, then about three weeks ago I got bored and reinstalled Windows XP becuase I wanted to play Sim City 4. Its always one game or application that drives us back.

Over this three weeks thou I have missed Ubuntu. I have missed the stability and speed. Yesterday I reinstalled Dapper so I now have a machine that dual boots with Windows. 

I am waiting for a new network card to arrive becuase the nforce one does not work with Dapper, once installed I will then migrate back to Ubuntu and use this as my primary machine for internet, email and webdesign.

Windows will just be used for games, I think I can stomach that now.

I have gone past the point of caring what OS is better. Each one is good at its one thing. Windows is good for games because there is so much choice.

Ubuntu is great for Desktop use becuase there are no viruses or spyware and you can change the look & feel within a minute.
