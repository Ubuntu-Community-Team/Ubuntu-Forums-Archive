### Post by dazzlindonna on 2009-02-04
I live with what has to be the grumpiest, grouchiest, most negative person on the face of this planet.

For months, the words "linux" and "ubuntu" have been met with sneers, jeers, derision, and negativity from this person.  I've done my best to explain why it's so wonderful, but as expected, nothing but negative comments resulted.

**Until today.**

I run Evolution on a separate workspace while I'm working on another.  When Grumpy came in to ask if I had any plans for Friday, I said, "Let me check my calendar", and I promptly switched to the workspace with the Evolution calendar on it (with a nice little cube flip effect, of course).

> Grumpy asked, astonished, "Is that Linux???"

I replied, "Yes".

Grumpy said, "Wow, I bet you can be really productive with that neat box thing giving you more space to work on".

I was floored.  Nothing positive EVER comes out of Grumpy's mouth.  And here was Grumpy - IMPRESSED by Linux's ability to have multiple desktops.

 > > > **It was a wonderful moment.**  :D
