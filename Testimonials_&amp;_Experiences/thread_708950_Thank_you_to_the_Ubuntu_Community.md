### Post by laceiba on 2008-02-26
I just wanted to say thanks to the Ubuntu community for putting together such a cool product. Ultimately, Ubuntu relies on the work of many folks that have gone before, but I just wanted to share a little story and express my gratitude.

I came across a bunch of old floppy disks from my college days, some of which were over 10 years old. Some were formatted on Macs and some on PCs. Ubuntu read them all without once complaining and allowed me to copy my old data off of old decaying magnetic media on to my hard drive. It was quite a nice experience.

So, thanks to all the free software cats out there that enabled such a smooth experience.
