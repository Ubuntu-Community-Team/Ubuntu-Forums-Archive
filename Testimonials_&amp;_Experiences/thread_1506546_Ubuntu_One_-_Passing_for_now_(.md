### Post by luckylucky on 2010-06-10
I think the Ubuntu One service is a fantastic concept.  I for one would certainly use it.  However I have disabled it on my systems for now.  It seems to be far too buggy, not responsive, or simply not working.  I believe that they're still working hard to get everything running smoothly.  I figure that I'll wait a couple of months and then try again.  For now it's back to sneaker-net for me.

Please don't misunderstand this post as a trashing of U1, just posting my experience for the benefit of others who are experiencing similar issues to share my thoughts on the matter - - - wait a while before using it.

This being in a LTS release means that this service is here to stay, and surely they will get it running smooth sooner or later.  Same thing with the OS, 10.04 is still buggy, many things still crash for me, though I'm sure that with a bit of time 10.04 will become quite stable.  8.04 was very nice for me lately, but now it is time to go through the experience of 10.04 along with the growing pains.  I'm confident that soon things should be running smooth, for both 10.04 and U1.
