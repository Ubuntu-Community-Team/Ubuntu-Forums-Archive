### Post by Geemy on 2009-02-03
Hello Ubuntu Team,

I'd like to let you guys know I new Linux and Ubuntu. I recently had to build a new computer to replace my old outdated computer, and after ordering the hardware literally part by part, I came to the crossroads of 'which operating system to use'. I couldn't use my old XP installation disk because it was an OEM copy. I wasn't comfortable with shelling out the $179 for an OEM copy of Vista or the full $279 for the retail version. I debated on installing the beta for windows 7 but figured it would probably be a software and hardware support headache the way Vista was. So I decided to take the plunge and try Ubuntu. I had heard a lot about Linux on TechTV, read about it on Digg, watched some cool videos of it on YouTube, and have always been impressed with it. Now I am a user, and everyone I've shown my PC to is envious, including my wife.

Everything so far has been rather intuitive and easy to use. I have already used your guides for installing World of Warcraft, changing my theme, setting up Revolution for my GMail, and installing the extra Compiz Fusion effects that my wife just loves.

Thank you guys, for all your hard work and time you have put into this project. Thank you for making it free and open to the public. Thank you for your excellent support that should be the example for all customer support related fields. Anyone I have had contact with, has been friendly and knowledgeable.

Proud to be an Ubuntu user,
~Geemy
