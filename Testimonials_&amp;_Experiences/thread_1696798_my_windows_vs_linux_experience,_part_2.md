### Post by 3Miro on 2011-02-28
Most people underestimate wine when it comes to gaming. From all windows programs, games run best under wine. I play AND mod windows games under Linux and wine. Older games usually run very well (provided you have Nvidia video card, ATI and Intel are not very wine-friendly). More and more of the newer games run "out-of-the-box" as well. You do usually get a performance hit, but this is too small of a trouble compared to dual-booting.
