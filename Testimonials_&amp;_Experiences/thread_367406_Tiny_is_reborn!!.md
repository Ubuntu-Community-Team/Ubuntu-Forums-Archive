### Post by whitefort on 2007-02-22
Tiny?

Yes, Tiny is an antique PC of mine, dating back to Ye Olden Tymes when Windows 95 was The Latest Thing.  (I bought him from a company called 'Tiny', hence the name.)

Alas, along came XP, and poor Tiny didn't have the power to run it.  So I got a new machine, and though I kept trying to find a use for him, or a good home, the poor, unloved little thing ended up in... the attic.

This week I took him out, dusted him down, and - without much hope of success - installed Xbuntu.  Tiny has such low specs that I had to download the special CD for low-memory PCs.

It installed perfectly.  This surprised me.  But what really, REALLY, REALLY surprised me is hoe fast it runs.  Tiny is reborn, and he FLIES!  He's a far more impressive machine now than when he was running his original Win 95!

I've just scrubbed off the 'WIndows'sticker and replaced it with 'Linux inside', and I'm having a little 'Xbuntu' badge made for him too.  Software like this is something to be proud of!

I'm astonised that Xbuntu can offer such speed and so many cool programs (that work!) on such a 'tiny' PC.

Another reason for me to love Ubuntu!
