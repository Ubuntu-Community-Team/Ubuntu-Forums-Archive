### Post by TBABill on 2010-09-02
I have posted several accounts to other users facing the same issues I had with 10.04 when it released and following release. I have a Dell 1564 laptop with an Intel Core i3 CPU using the integrated graphics processor. These chips with IGP's onboard suffered from system freezes very frequently, no 3D and very poor performance on Ubuntu. With kernel 2.6.33 on another distro my system worked great, but still had no 3D. 

Fast forward to today and I tried 10.04.1. Word on the street was full support for the CPU/GPU in kernel 2.6.34 and beyond. Well, I decided to go for it and try 10.04 again anyway. What do you know?! It works great, compiz is running full steam, effects all over the screen and performance is high. No slowdowns, no freezes, all updates applied and a system working flawlessly. Even CPU scaling is working great using just the Gnome applet on the panel. 

I'm very impressed by all the improvements since release. I'm back fulltime on my laptop with Ubuntu and also on one of my other machines that previously ran fairly slowly on Ubuntu. 

Whatever was fixed, thank you to the devs for all the improvements. It's obvious you have to check often and I appreciate all the work that has gone into bug fixes along the way.
