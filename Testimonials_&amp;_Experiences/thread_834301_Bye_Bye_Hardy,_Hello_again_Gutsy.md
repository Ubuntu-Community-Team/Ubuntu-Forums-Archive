### Post by DrMega on 2008-06-19
Well, I gave it a fair go.

Hardy doesn't work for me just now. Maybe in future it wil come good.

BACKGROUND:
I first switched to Ubuntu (Dapper) after my media PC running Windows XP MCE crashed once too many times. It would crash every few minutes. I put Dapper on it, and it never crashed, not even once, despite running for hours on end playing MP3s with full screen visualisations, games, sometimes mp3s and games at the same time (where the game sound was uninteresting).

Skipted, Edgy and Feisty, and went to Gutsy. Still great. Many months of solid use until it died one night with what appeared to be a hard disk failure (it later turned out that the cable had came off - I didn't assemble the machine).

FAST FORWARD TO THE RELEASE OF HARDY:

Having aquired a new hard disk, and reconnected the old one (whose cable had become unplugged), I decided to install Hardy.

Worked at first. Get on the Internet (FF3 Beta), and random freeze-ups, Firefox crashed all the time. I figured it was just because FF was in Beta, so switched to Epiphany for a while. Same deal. FF got upgraded to RC1, ran that, several freezes, app bombing out, a couple of black screens of death (no text, just black screen).

Also, since Hardy, I don't get smooth video playback anymore (ATI, I know I know), it was fine in Gutsy and Dapper, and Flash movies, just give it up. I get about 5 frames per second.

I waited a while, installed all the recommended updates, trawled the net, posted a couple of questions here, no luck.

As a last straw I decided to download Gutsy and reinstall it. I couldn't even do that. It appeared to download OK (no errors) but when I burned it to a CD, it wouldn't boot from it.

In the end I had to reconnect up my old computer in order to download Gutsy and burn the CD.

Now then, all that said, I'm not going to slate Hardy. It doesn't work for me, but others have had much success with it. I'm sure that in time the issues that have driven me to sack it will get resolved, but as far as I'm concerned, Gutsy worked fine so it is going back on.
