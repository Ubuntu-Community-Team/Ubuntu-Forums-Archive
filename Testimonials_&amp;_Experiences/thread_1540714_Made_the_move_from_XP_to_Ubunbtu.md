### Post by Ballsmasher on 2010-07-28
And so far its been an absolute pleasure..booted up XP and was simply trying to install an AV to remove a rootkit that had found its way through my browser (not iexplore!) and had consistent blue screens, losing my temper i decided i was going to take the plunge and switch. Needless to say my experience in the intitial installation was as smooth as butter, everything worked out of the box for me (although i know some have issues) which was a pleasant surprise.

Everything i required was available, music social tools and of course complete access to my PC. The terminal was my biggest fear, but having a friend on hand who runs a debian insall to answer all my silly and novice questions has been fantastic, some may complain or find it irritating to use the terminal. Personally i ADORE it, i'm learning to use something new and knowing that i have access to the inner workings of my OS is exciting, yeah it takes a little more time to get something working but the joy when i finally get it running is worth it to me.

I'm a huge gamer so i knew i'd make some sacrifices in that regard, although thanks to wine i can play every game i had done previously (albeit with some work and i still haven't worked out some CPU hogging issues with wine) so that completely cements my newfound love for linux.

Not all newbies have a horrible experience! and i had no prior linux knowledge before this install.
