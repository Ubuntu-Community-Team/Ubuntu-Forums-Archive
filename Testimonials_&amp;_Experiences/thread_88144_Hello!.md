Hello to all and WOW! 

I'm new here so i wantet to say hi to all.

I'm am new to Linux running now Ubuntu for almost 2 months and I must say it is a great experience, since my earlier tryouts to switch form windows to linux weren't so smooth. I've tried Suse and RedHat, but geting them to run on a laptop was quite a task in 2001. Now, I wanted to give it a new chance an asked a friend who pointed to Ubuntu. I downloaded Hoary Live CD, booted up with the cd and *Perfect*. 

Ok, almost, but now, after reading in this forum, seeking for help and getting solutions, I know i will stick to Ubuntu. This tweakability is heaven! :D I finaly feel free from the windows-nanny, now Ubuntu treats me as a grown up system-user, held to be able to configure the machine. Obviously not without (lots of nice) help from this comunity. But I'm here to learn.

And that's what I think is what I like most from linux in general: the comunity thinking. Everyone helps everyone and there is no problem to big or to dificult  to get it solved. 

So in the spirit of the tree musketeers, thank you for liberating me, I hope I can serve you as well.
