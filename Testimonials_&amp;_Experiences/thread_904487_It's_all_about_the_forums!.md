### Post by simonuk on 2008-08-29
I've been using computers since the ZX Spectrum and I have dipped my toes in a number of Distro's over the years. Each time I encountered some problems and couldn't find the answers so had to give up because I didn't understand how to progress any further.

It's been a couple of years since my last try and I have a new MSI wind so I thought why not give it another go. 

As per all other attempts I had hurdles to cross but this time I could find the answers (Wi-fi didn't work, The T-Mobile web n walk USB didn't work which meant no work internet for me). Finding the answers to my problems meant this time it's stayed on my computer and I can finally take the plunge and learn how to start fixing things for myself.

Finding the answers to problems is now much easier than it has ever been before and the people giving the answers do not seem as eliteist as they once were. 

For this I thank you all!
