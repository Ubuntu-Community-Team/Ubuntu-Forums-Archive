### Post by JEStone on 2008-03-09
I'm new to the whole thing Ubuntu. And would like to express my thank you to the people who have put this OS together.

Everyone at work told me that i would struggle with finding software and everything that I do would not be compatible. I found the opposite. Everything works. I have email, internet, photo manager, etc... and I share all my documents with my wifes computer and the Mac I have at work.

I love the whole setup. the multiple desktops has made working on multiple project an absolute breeze.

The "Add/Remove" software button is fantastic. I just do a quick search for what I want and then click install.

I can go on and on, but I will stop now and give the ubuntu two thumbs up. Im truly enjoying the new computer setup.

Thank you
