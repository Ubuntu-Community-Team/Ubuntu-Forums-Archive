### Post by AnimateDeath on 2007-10-08
Well, I bit the bullet and did a dual boot with winXP. I hated to do it, but I really needed to use my adobe stuff again.  I searched for the smallest XP install i could find and came across one called "XP stripped to the bare bone". I checked it out and then dug out my xp disk and made one for myself. The complete installation took up a little less than 2gig on the harddrive and is able to run all of my Adobe stuff with no problems.  I hated doing it, but until Adobe puts out these products for Linux, i have no choice.
