### Post by wieman01 on 2007-06-27
I had to upgrade from Dapper last weekend because I borked my system by destroying the root file system (don't ask, stupid mistake). As I had previously deleted all backup files (I know, I should know better) I had no choice but to reinstall the system. I took the opportunity and upgrade to Kubuntu 7.04 and after a few hassles (Bluetooth support for headsets, etc.) I am finally there. 

I must say Kubuntu Feisty is simply beautiful. All apps integrate nicely and the default theme is stunning. Adept has improved considerably, applications that are installed on the default system are more than plenty. Support for restricted formats (e.g. MP3) has also become a lot easier to cope with. 

So on the whole, I do not regret having taken this step. After a few sleepless nights I am happy to report that this is the best Linux distribution I have ever seen. Keep it up, guys (and girls)!
