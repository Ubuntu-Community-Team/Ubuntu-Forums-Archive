### Post by michaelleung on 2009-01-23
I've been an on-and-off Linux user for a few years now (I started when I was 11, now I'm 14). I first used RH Linux 9, then I moved on to Fedora, then Ubuntu. I am so happy about the disc I received in the mail since my Internet was down for a week (OH NOES!). I received those Ubuntu stickers, and I've stuck them to my laptop. Now when I go to school, all those Mac users look at this thing that says "Ubuntu", and they're like, "What's that?" 

I will never go back to Windows. EVER. Thanks for such a great product and speedy mail delivery!
