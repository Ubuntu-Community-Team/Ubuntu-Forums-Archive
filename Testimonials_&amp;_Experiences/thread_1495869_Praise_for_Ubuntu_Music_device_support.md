### Post by holocene on 2010-05-28
I own a Creative Labs Vision:M mp3/media player with gigabytes of music. 

Windows support (at least under XP) was limited, and I recall just seeing very limited, and largely unusable results.

I plug this thing into Lucid, and Rhythmbox box displays all my music on the device! 

And I mean in usable form, with artist, title, etc all displayed.

Fantastic! 

I also had the same result just plugging in my ultra cheap Brother laser printer. It just works!

Very excited and happy in Houston,
Steve.
