### Post by Sunflower1970 on 2007-04-15
I just need to tell someone. Tried telling my hubby, he doesn't get why I'm so happy :D

Last night, I decided to futz around with my testing side of the old Pentium II Compaq. It's been running Feisty for a couple of weeks. I had added in Enlightenment and also Fluxbox to play around with. But, last night, while using the Gnome DE, I decided to try out the desktop effects and then laugh at how slow this computer would be with them (either that or I was expecting a huge crash)..but nothing of the sort....**The cube and wobbly windows works on it without using very much of the CPU!!** I was shocked, stunned! This computer had Windows 98 on it in December, and bearly would run that! (BSOD every few minutes on it) Now it runs the latest Ubuntu with cool 3D effects very smoothly and pretty fast without breaking out into a real sweat!! 

This morning I turned it on just to make sure I wasn't dreaming. And, yup. it still is running it perfectly. Feisty does load slowly on it, but once I'm in it, it's a touch faster than Edgy was on it (and this is also using a 5400RPM harddrive on one side of it) 

I'm in the process of upgrading the second side (the hubby's side of the computer) to Feisty this morning since everything is going so well with the testing side.

Every day I'm amazed at how well this computer works, and Ubuntu amazes me too. I use this old one more often than my laptop or the Dell. I never thought I'd be using it as often, but it's the one we use almost every day.

I can't thank the Ubuntu team enough for this amazing OS. Thank you thank you thank you!!
