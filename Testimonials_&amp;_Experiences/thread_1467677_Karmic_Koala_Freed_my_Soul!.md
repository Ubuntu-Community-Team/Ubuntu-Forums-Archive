### Post by philodice on 2010-04-30
Ubuntu Week Three!
First week, learning, installation, and taking the plunge.  
I had a lot of fun installing Karmic for the first time, it was an adventure, and I felt like I was unwrapping presents from the world Ubuntu community.
When I ran into trouble, there was this awesome army of Ubuntu Ninjas to help me solve my problems.

I gasped with amazement at the software repositories, puzzled in bafflement over the terminal, and read the linux pocket guide.  Twice.
Reading number three, things start to sink in.  Oh, a level of understanding germinates.  Ah ha, that's what that meant, that's what ~ and / mean.  
I had a nightmare that I was trying to run commands in the terminal without my volumes of printed help.  :)

My experience so far should let new users know that switching is an adventure of learning, but not one that we undertake alone.  Our reasons for leaving the unfree world may be many and varied, but let our reason for staying with Ubuntu be:  Community!
