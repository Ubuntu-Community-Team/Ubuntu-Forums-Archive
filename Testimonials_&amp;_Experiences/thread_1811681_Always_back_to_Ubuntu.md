### Post by 1roxtar on 2011-07-26
I have been a devout Ubuntu user since 9.04, when I found out about Linux.  I don't get it when people gripe so much about Ubuntu.  I have had nothing but great experiences with every release.  I have distro hopped simply to find out what else is out there, but I remain an Ubuntu guy.  

Unless you have some obscure hardware, Ubuntu just works every time and out of the box.  I have 8 computers in my house running Ubuntu, from older Dell Optiplexes and HP Pavilions to newer laptops ranging from Acer, Toshiba, Dell and my favorite baby, my Alienware desktop all running Ubuntu 32/64 bit flawlessly.  

Installations are a breeze (my favorite is the upgrade via LiveCD since 11.04), my machines are running fast and virus-free, and Unity is a thing of beauty.  Even 11.10 Alpha 2 is running like a beast on one of my newer machines.  I may get a taste of other distros from time to time (on a virtual machine, no more cd's), but I feast on Ubuntu every day.  ;)
