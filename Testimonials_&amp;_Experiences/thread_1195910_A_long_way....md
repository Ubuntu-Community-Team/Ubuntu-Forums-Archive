### Post by solwic on 2009-06-24
I spent last evening setting up Ubuntu on my new laptop, and it occured to me that it was easier to install it on this lappy than it was on my desktop.  That made me remember the days when having wireless internet on a laptop via Ubuntu would have been a laughable idea.  The OS has come a long way in a short period of time. Everything installed out of the box...video, wireless...all of it.  Now I'm pure Ubuntu on this machine, and couldn't be happier.  

I know the devs don't read the forum (lazy buggers), but I think Ubuntu's success goes beyond those elitists. Without this community, for instance, Ubuntu would be a joke.  A very bad joke, at that.  So props to you guys.  Without you, I'd be running Windows Xp right now....
