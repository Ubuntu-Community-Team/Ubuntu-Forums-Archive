Last year, when I actually got a few computers with a little more "oomph" (1.4 GHz-PM, 2.0 GHz-P4 and 2.4 GHz-P4) I decided to focus less on "playing" (-er, I mean "testing" and "developing") and more on setting it up to be hands-off. Enjoy, ask questions and learn. There is a lot to Linux.
