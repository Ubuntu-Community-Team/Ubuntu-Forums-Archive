### Post by phoenix116 on 2008-11-22
Yeah, a lot of people in my school have installed ubuntu on their notebooks, and I have seen NO system that has been working out of the box.

The first problem with ubuntu( and with a lot of distros) is in my opinion that the support for new hardware is dreadful. If you buy a new notebook or PC with actual hardware you are bound to have problems getting ubuntu to run on it( that was my experience in the last two years ).

Some problems you will most likely never be able to solve because "the vendor of the hardware doesn't make a proper driver". ( e.g. Intel wifi...)

I hope gnash or the other flash projects will come to a stable, working version, for then we will be independent from adobe.
