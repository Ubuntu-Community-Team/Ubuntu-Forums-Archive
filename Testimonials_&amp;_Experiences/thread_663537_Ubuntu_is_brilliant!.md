### Post by RavUn on 2008-01-10
I originally moved to Linux because I liked the idea of a free OS and free software.  However, I did not realize how Ubuntu becomes *my* OS... I can do anything I want with it and I love it.  Not only that, but this community is outstanding.  The main reason I decided to go with Ubuntu was because of all the support I saw in this forum when I was browsing around.

Thanks to everyone for making the switch from Windows to Ubuntu a fun and (relatively) painless experience.  I hope I never need my Windows box again, but I'm sure I'll check in every now and then... gotta update my anti virus and spyware :P
