### Post by Erdmoebel on 2007-01-19
Hi! I´m german but currently living on the spanish island of Lanzarote. I knew Linux for quite a while but never really used it (Tried it a while ago but just didn´t do the job for me). One day I finally got tired of Windows, it´s look, it´s beahviour, basically anything and was looking for an alternative. First I wanted to buy a Mac but that´s kinda difficult in my neck of he woods and decided I´d try Linux again. I installed Suse since that distro seems to be the most popular among germans (It has it´s reputation in Germany, maybe because it is around for like.. forever) but it didn´t really work for me. Then I tried Ubuntu and it worked like a charm!

Okay so my dad was worried about Vista and asked me to install a linux flavour on his computer but the switch wasn´t so easy. I had to fiddle with the system quite a bit to get his DVB card running and after a while I thougt he´d just ditch it but yesterday I actually heard him recommending it to his friends!

Oh and another thing: It seems like Linux is gaining some popularity here. I read an interview with the local minister for the environment where he expressed his deep interest in it and the local computer stores sell machine with Linux preinstalled
