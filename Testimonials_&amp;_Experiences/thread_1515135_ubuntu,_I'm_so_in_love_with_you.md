### Post by billiebub on 2010-06-21
I went through the process of registering to say that I'm so in love with ubuntu.  This is probably not the place to get all emotional and all but I felt like I needed to say this a long time ago when I first started using Red hat Linux 7.0 and I back then I got so scared of Linux that I felt I should stick to Windows.  Well, now this has all changed.  I have both Windows 7 and Ubuntu 10 installed and the more I used Ubuntu, the less I use Windows.  I just love Linux, especially coming from a computing world.  I have always wanted to play with Linux and I have believe me using various Live CDs out there but I felt like there was something missing.  I always hated to ask myself "Can I replace Windows with Linux?" probably not and days went by but I always came back to my lovely penguin.  I burnt Damn Small Linux on a mini CD but was still not 100% happy until I tried Ubuntu by installing as a Windows program using Wubi.  Now I have Linux installed on all my 7 computers at home including my Wife's laptop and to tell you the truth, she's impressed even though she calls Linux "Kleenex".  Sorry for the long wait, but I had to get this out of my chest.  Cheers.  :)
