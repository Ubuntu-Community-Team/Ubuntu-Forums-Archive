### Post by Nytram on 2013-03-21
just wanted to express how much i like lubuntu. i installed it on my netbook the other day, and have been really impressed with it.

i originally had arch on my netbook running gnome shell, and it was pretty good, not fast but i found it acceptable.

then my cmos battery died, and arch wouldn't work without it, so i started using win xp which i had on dual boot.

again xp was ok, not fast but acceptable.

i got a new cmos battery, but thought i should just buy a new modern netbook (this current one is 3 years old and fairly low spec as most netbooks are.)

but instead decided to try some lightweight linuxes, first tried puppy but i didn't like the gui much.

then lubuntu, and, have to say i was impressed, and very satisfied with it.

it zips along, twice as fast as xp or gnome shell did, i figure i can lay off buying a new netbook for a while now.
