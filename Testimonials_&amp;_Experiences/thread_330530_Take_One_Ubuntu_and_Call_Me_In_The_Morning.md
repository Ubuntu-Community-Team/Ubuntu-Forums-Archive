### Post by Severun on 2007-01-03
I have tried many Linux flavors over the years and Ubuntu is the tastiest and never gets soggy in milk!!

Seriously though  I recently had the pleasure of trying Ubuntu for the first time.  I lost my hard drive on my Windoze laptop.  I still needed to work so I thought I would give the Ubuntu Live CD a try.  It worked on my laptop like a charm.  I was able to boot up and work without a hard drive until my new drive arrived.  

Now here's the really great thing that has never happened before on a Linux distro I have tried.  Ubuntu auto recognized my network card, sound, display, infrared port, USB and other doo-dads better than Windoze.  I installed Windoze on the same machine (dual boot) and I had to install several other drivers before my laptop would even see half of the hardware it had in it.

Couple of weeks later, my daughters' new desktop machine was shipped with a faulty hard disk.  Ubuntu Live CD to the rescue again.  My daughter was able to use her machine to surf the net and do her schoolwork using Ubuntu Live CD, until her new hard disk arrives.  She liked it so much that now after I have dual boot installed her new hard disk, she uses Ubuntu and only boots Windoze when she absolutely has to to run some Windoze only application.  In this case too, with all new hardware, Ubuntu was a much easier install than Windoze and didn't require the install of a bunch of additional driver to work, sound, network, video, all were auto recognized by the Ubuntu install.

I recommend Ubuntu to everyone now.  If any Linux distro ever had a chance of whooping the tar out of the kooks in Redmond, it's Ubuntu. 

Thank you team for a wonderful Linux distro.

-Severun
