### Post by kvk on 2009-07-14
Just a quick note in case anyone has been considering a new purchase:

I recently bought a new Gateway NV5214u (AMD processor). I had written Acer/Gateway several nasty letters regarding their anti-Linux advertising, but was forced by circumstance when I was in Seattle to buy a laptop,and I have a fondness for Gateway laptops (maybe it's a cow thing...).

Anyway, I bought one, exorcised Vista, and loaded 64-bit Jaunty from a disc I had burned. Everything worked very nicely right out of the box, including wireless, package manager, and so on. I added some log-in themes and a few other toys with no problem. The only idiosyncrasy it displays is a tendency to hang on Net traffic; it spins its wheels for a while with no data transfer, and I haven't yet figured out why.

If anyone is considering buying one of these, new or used, it appears to work quite well OOTB with both 64 and 32-bit Jaunty.
