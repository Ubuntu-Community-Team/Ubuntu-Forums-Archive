### Post by R.Bucky on 2009-02-01
If you are receiving errors from the image, look no further than yourself. Make sure the md5 sum is correct, ensure your burn at a low speed to ensure accuracy, and that your side of the deal is fine. Try obtaining the distro from a torrent, rather than http to make sure you have all of it. I have installed countless (U)buntu distros. Whenever it fails, I have a bad disk or it wrote improperly. The distro is not the problem.

Also, check that you partition the hard-disk properly if you manually edit partitions (e.g. swap, mount point of "/").
