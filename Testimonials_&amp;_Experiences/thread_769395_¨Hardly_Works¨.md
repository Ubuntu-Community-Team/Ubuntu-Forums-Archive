### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-04-26
No matter what upgrade or update you do, it runs the risk of breaking down. The latest and greatest tend to be buggy, and will take a little time to stabilize. I finally couldn't wait any longer and updated to Ubuntu. It was about 95% smooth sailing for an upgrade from 7.10. I had to rework my compiz a little bit, but other than that, it works. PulseAudio actually works better with my sound card than ALSA did.

My laptop is a Dell Inspiron 1520, and no it did not come with Ubuntu pre-installed.

If Gutsy works better on your system, stick with it till Hardy's bugs are squished. There are still a good number of users that are holding on to older distros simply because they work. Bleeding edge means getting your hands messy. There will always be problems with new stuff. Always.

Leo
