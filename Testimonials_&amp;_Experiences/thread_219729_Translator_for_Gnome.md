### Post by -Phi- on 2006-07-21
This is the wrong forum, but here's a possible solution:

I couldn't find a GUI for it, but if you install the packages dict, dictd, dictzip, recode, dict-freedict-eng-fra, and dict-freedict-fra-eng you can use a french-english/english-french dictionary in the Terminal.

```
$ dict hello
From English-French Freedict dictionary [fd-eng-fra]:

  hello [helou]
     allô

$ dict bonjour
From French-English Freedict dictionary [fd-fra-eng]:

  bonjour [bo&#771;&#658;ur]
     good morning
     good day
```
- Phi
