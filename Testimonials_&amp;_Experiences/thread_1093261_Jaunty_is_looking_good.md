### Post by Keyper7 on 2009-03-11
(the oblig. disclaimer: your mileage may vary)

Testing the Jaunty Alpha 5 Live CD I could check that:

- A bug causing a delay in detecting a change of power state in my laptop was fixed.

- No more worries with the hard drive power management problem. Out of the box, it's now disabled on AC and reasonable on batteries.

- Java 6u12 reached the repositories. Together with the (still alpha but already usable) 64-bit Flash player, this pretty much means no more 32-bit browser and/or nspluginwrapper for me.

- All my keyboard Fn keys work now. Some of them had bugs in Intrepid.

- Webcam working out of the box, even on Skype.

- Skype tray icon not garbled anymore.

- I like the new GDM theme. :)

Have some mixed feelings about the new notification system, but let's see how that goes.

### Post by jbysmith on 2009-03-11
Ditto this sentiment.  Not much terribly new feature wise aside from notifications and EXT4, mostly just updated software, but the thing is *fast*, and seems a bit more reliable too, which is saying a lot for an alpha build. 

Running just fine on two of my systems.  One is a Core2Duo 3GHz with an nVidia 7800GS, the other is an Athlon XP with an ATI X850.  Haven't tried Firewire yet myself, need to dig out my camcorder and see how it behaves.
