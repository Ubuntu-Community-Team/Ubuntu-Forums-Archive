### Post by Rumpty on 2008-05-12
Just installed 8.04, and I think I'm going off Ubuntu. Some aspects are changing just for changes sake, I reckon.

It all started with the UUID in fstab a release or two back, and the latest Grub made a foul-up of recognising my two or three other Linux installations on the computer. Had to manually edit it to get one of them to boot. And there's the hda/sda confusion as well.

Then there was no sign of my FAT32 partitions in the Media folder. Surely that can happen automatically in the installation? Got them to appear by some manipulations as Root, but then I couldn't access them as a normal user. Folders locked. That meant I couldn't import my firefox bookmarks from a FAT32 partition.

It's all getting too hard.
