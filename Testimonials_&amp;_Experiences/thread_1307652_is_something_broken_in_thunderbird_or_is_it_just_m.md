### Post by AE6QE on 2009-10-31
A couple of things to check:

 - Are you on the SBC network?  Some companies won't let you do SMTP from outside their network.

 - Check the port numbers.  A few companies require non-normal port numbers.  Others require you to use SSL, which I believe also uses another port.

I haven't tried it on Ubuntu or Windows, but Thunderbird is working for me on OS X 10.6.1) (better than Mail.app that's broken for Gmail).
