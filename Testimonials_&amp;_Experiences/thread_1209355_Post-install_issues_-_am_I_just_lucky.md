### Post by IHeequ5i on 2009-07-10
I admit that I was a bit nervous about Ubuntu after reading all the issues with Nvidia graphics cards here. But I need 3D hardware acceleration for my games, so I figured I'd have to give it a try.

I went to System -> Administration -> Hardware Drivers and took the option that was recommended (ver 180).  Activated it, rebooted - no problems.

On a similar note (pun intended), my system sounds worked out-of-the-box which was a pleasant surprise. I was able to play mp3s with Amorak after downloading codecs from the restricted repository. This, by the way, was something I was never able to get working with FC11. 

So am I just lucky?
