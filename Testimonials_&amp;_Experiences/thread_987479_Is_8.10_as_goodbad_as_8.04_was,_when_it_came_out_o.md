The first problem I encountered, was a fault with the upgrading. It wasn't thought out as well as it should have been. If some packages were not installed correctly during the process, it should not bring down the whole system. I just chose another configuration, then it choked, everything was going, but the upgrador app. Then I rebooted. That was the last time I'd do something as stupid turning off my computer, while it was running apt.

The next problem was pulseaudio. Following one tutorial after another, I got down to the point where I screwed my X. Though the recovery mode's "Fix X" solved the problem. 

And getting my wireless drivers to work was a pain.

And overall, 7.10 ran so much faster on my old box, than this does. In 7.10, I could listen to music, run WoW, run compiz, all at the same time, and everything was playable. Now audacious starts to take a pause every 5 seconds if my system encounters CPU usage above ~40-60%, like a sixty year old, nicotine addicted, office worker, that has to sprint for 10 $!


So, would it be ok, to upgrade now ? I know its out quite a time, but I'm still scared. So, I want you're opinion about how well does it run on old hardware, and is it worth it.
