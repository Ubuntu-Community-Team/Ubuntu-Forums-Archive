### Post by kzipz on 2009-02-10
I've been going through various distros (Sabayon, Fedora, Linuxmint 4 & 6, Slackware and Ubuntu)to run on my laptop. After trying many I've found that Ubuntu-Studio 8.10 x64 loads and configures almost everything correctly.

For some reason I had the problem with video after installing the codex plugin for 'movie player' After reading the ideas on this thread [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=671994&highlight=green+totem")
I found that I could remove the error by selecting the 'X11 Video Output' option in the advanced settings of VLC media player. A quick and easy solution:P

For some reason I get better processor performance in my Mint installation and still havent sorted out the difference since LinuxMint 6 is also based on the Intrepid kernel.

Overall my experience has been super. All it has taken is some patience and learning to open the doors to a much happier computing experience. Hats off to our community!!!
