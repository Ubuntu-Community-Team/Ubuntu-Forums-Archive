### Post by Bigbird999 on 2007-11-21
Kubuntu killed my ancient Laptop.and I was ready to throw in the towel, but I tried  Ubuntu and it works fine.  It is all the eye candy in KUBUNTU that causes problems. Add that too the frustrations of getting wireless and video cards working and it is easy to run away from Linux.  Also you might find Mint to your liking, I tried it with equally good results. 
 What are your laptop specs?  As you can seein my sig, mine is pretty low on the performance chart but it performs as well with Ubuntu as Win2K.  Not better, just as well.

Having said that, the first couple of weeks are pretty tough but it does get easier and you have to invest some time in it.  Set up dual boot with Ubuntu and play a little bit each day and learn to use Ubuntu.  After a while you may decide you like Linux better, maybe not.  For me the jury is still out, If I depended on my laptop to make a living or for school, I think I would have packed it in by now.  But it is just a hobby for an old retired guy.
 Good Luck

BB
