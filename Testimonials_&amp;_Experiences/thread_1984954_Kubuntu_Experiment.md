### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-05-22
I have often been tempted to install Kubuntu because of the nice screen shots. After two years of curiosity I decided to try installing the Kubuntu desktop. The installation went well and I logged out of Gnome into the new desktop . I found it to be a nice looking desktop and installed some wallpapers and themes.

I left the native applications in place to use as is. After a couple of days of on and off use I decided that KDE was not for me at this point. I had a good experience overall though. Since Kubuntu has a new sponsor it will be interesting to see what the future brings with full time development.  I removed the Kubuntu desktop with this tutorial and removed the residual configuration from synaptic.

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/pureubuntu](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/pureubuntu)  

I am back to the Gnome Shell, Unity and E17 for the moment , but at least my curiosity has been satisfied . Xubuntu and Lubuntu may be next.  :P
