I went thru the past few Ubuntu releases and went back to XP for whatever reason (Excel VBA mainly).

It's all here now for me.  Scanner/TV/Two LCD displays work.  I have a Sony camera, but use a external stick reader, which is a better solution than the software for XP.

I just use XP on my company laptop now, where I spend time programming for scientific stuff.  I only have to spend about .5 hr/day on it at home, unconnected.  Don't want to spend time/money on the crossover office yet.

Lame Gnome desktop to show off here.  I might try KDE again, but Gnome works very well & is layed out well.

Anyway, thanks Ubuntu.  You guys are the only reason I am running Linux for my 'After work computer'
