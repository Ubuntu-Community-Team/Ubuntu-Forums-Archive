### Post by plurworldinc on 2011-01-03
From a simple laptop to an entire household running just Ubuntu in one way or another I have come a very long way with Ubuntu. When I first started using Ubunut I had no idea what I was doing. When I found this forum and a number of videos online it was a major help in getting over the learning curve that came when I installed Ubuntu for the very first time. I have gone from noob to fanboy telling all of my friends about the wonders of Linux and Ubuntu and converting a number of them that have seem my systems and wanted what I had, a stable system.

   Right now I am running Ultimate Edition (Ubuntu) on my desktops and laptop because it's the flavor of Ubuntu that works for me. Besides just running Ubuntu on my computer I am also running Ubuntu on my netbook, tablet, and home entertainment system which I have found to be my true masterpiece. The Home Entertainment System is basically a thin desktop running Ubuntu 10.04 and Boxee as it's interface.Since all of my computers in my household are networked together it because the perfect place to come together as a family to watch films, listen to music and chat with old friends using Skype. When I am not in Boxee it just looks like a giant Mac to be easier on the eyes from a distance. 

   Another great feature I have found when helping my friends convert over to Ubuntu was how easy it was to re-design the desktop to look and feel like the system of there choose. It might just be me, but I love the look of my Mac, with the same interface it came with and a little Ubuntu logo in the upper left corner that shows off the true power behind it. For my Windows friends that are so use to using Windows I just give them a system that looks and feel like Windows to get over the hump. After awhile whenever I go back to work on there systems I have noticed how much people love customizing every little detail to them. You just can't do that in Windows or Mac.

   I have found Ubuntu to be the perfect system for anyone that wants an OS they can really make there own. If you are a person that is not really tech savy you will be after using Ubuntu for awhile since it just makes you want to learn more. I would have never dreamed of networking and entire house before to get the most out of my computers before I started using Ubuntu. I know I can do it with Windows as well, but why would I want to.

   Why settle for Windows, when you can have the entire house !=D>
