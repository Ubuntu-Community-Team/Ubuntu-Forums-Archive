### Post by loserboy on 2006-08-17
I never would have expected this to happen:
I've been using Ubuntu for around 2 months now with minimal problems and I love it, so i installed it on my computer at work and its more stable and faster than ever. 
anyway so i mentioned this to my dad and told him it was *free*, his eyes lit up, he told me to build a computer for my mom. She loves it, with just a lil desktop modding by me, she can do more on ubuntu than she ever could on win (granted mostly she just surfs the internet), also she hasn't had a single problem. Now my dad doesnt even like getting on his slow popup filled compaq since he's seen my moms, so he just told me to build 2 more,  for him and my sister (I love newegg). Now my gf wants linux because shes tired of all the crap...
i guess if there had to be a point to this story it would be: i'm not trying to start an argument but don't tell me linux isnt ready for the desktop, my mom is almost 60 and my dad is 60 and they both use it fine with wireless and printer.... there thats my ramble for the day
