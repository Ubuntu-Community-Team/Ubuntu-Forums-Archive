I've been playing with various Linux distros for about 8 years.  There have always been little glitches, annoyances, and various problems that have forced me to say on Windows. I must give a big THANK YOU to everyone who has worked on Ubuntu. I look forward to future releases with great anticipation and in joining this great community that is Ubuntu.
