### Post by johnalexrob on 2011-09-13
This forum is a great reason for why people should switch to Linux.
 
One reason why people don't make the switch is that they believe it to not have an official support system. While that is true, it is MUCH better than the "support" offered by MS. I had to call them at least 3 times before getting to a person (the problem I had was not in their automated system) and had to call HQ just to find out how. Once I did, they took all my info (with the exception of my social security number, strangely) just so they could tell me they were transferring me to my computer's manufacturer for help with their OS. What do these people get paid to do, anyway? I know they read from a screen, but at least they could try to earn their paychecks. 
 
When I have a problem with Linux, I just go to the distro's forum and usually get a response back within a day or less. And yet WE have no support system...
