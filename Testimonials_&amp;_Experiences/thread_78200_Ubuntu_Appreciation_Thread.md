### Post by yesplease on 2005-10-18
I also would like to thank the developers of Ubuntu and the people who made all those open source programs.

I would also like to state what I like so much about Ubuntu. 

Much work was dedicated to make install easy. Almost everything worked immediately after install. This means that I do not have learn a lot before I can use Ubuntu, but I can use it and learn more when I want to. I also like the choice of packages that is provided.  Being new, I could not migrate my mail from windows to evolution and just installed thunderbird (which I used under Windows). Help is available on forums, in the wiki and in the OS itself. I dont know much about the average time it takes to solve problems, but I am confident that bugs are not ignored, and that there is a continuous effort to improve the programs. Upgrading to Breezy was very easy too.
