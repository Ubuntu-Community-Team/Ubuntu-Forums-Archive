### Post by armandh on 2008-04-24
A die hard M$ fan got his first HH 8.04 RC disk from me yesterday. 

He is retiring from a company that sells and services large computer system printers. He has been a good source of free printers to give away. [Like HP Laser-Jet5 pull outs when they would put all new printers in a hospital and other end of lease situations.] 

some times the good ones go in to their bone yard to keep others running. some times they are the last of an unsupported type and go, without intervention, to the dumpster. 

they were junking two IBM infoprint 40s and I agreed to take one sans the finishing line. [it is a clone of my DDS-32] 

When he was delivering the printer I showed him 8.04 on my wife's lap top. His earlier trips in to the linux jungle, like mine, were less than satisfactory.  BUT..... 

He had never tried Ubuntu and was impressed with what he saw. A real desktop. really just working fine. Since he will no longer be getting steeply discounted M$ licenses his interest is higher than usual. I'll let you know how it turns out.
