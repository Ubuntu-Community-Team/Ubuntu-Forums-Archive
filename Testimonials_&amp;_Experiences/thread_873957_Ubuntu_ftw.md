### Post by jdenicholls on 2008-07-29
Ok I'll make this quick. I converted to Ubuntu because I knackered my Windows XP SP2 installation through repeated experimentation with how things work. After a week of using Mac OSX at work experience I became eager to try out some other OS and chose Ubuntu after getting a recommendation from a friend.

I love the interface, the GUI and the whole Ubuntu environment, definitely a big upgrade from Windows XP ;). Although I'm having a few issues getting some of my games working, the community is being really helpful. Overall I have no regrets about switching to Ubuntu, so far its been everything I've hoped for. Thanks to the Ubuntu developers and for those who read this
