### Post by vwbeamer on 2007-07-08
Day one- down Loaded 7.04. Wasted 2 hours figuring out that you do NOT open an ISO and figuring out how to burn a CD on my XP desktop. Loaded the live CD on my dell laptop. Was impressed because everything worked.

Day two- decide to do a dual boot on the laptop. Screw it up and loose everything on the HD.
Reformate HD and reload Windows and Ubuntu. I give Ubuntu 10 gig of space on a 80 gig HD, now regret as there are lots of Linux programs I want.  I can see that i will have to format and start over soon. Discover that Ubuntu is a lot faster then XP at downloading.

Day three- waste it trying to get beryl to work. learn about the command line and terminal.

Day four- Pimp out the panels at the top of the desktop and  learn how to get addons for firefox. Download ten different themes.

Day five- find old desktop beside road. Dell Optiplex with Pentium II and 256mb ram, 4 gig HDD. Will not boot because Windows 98 is corupted. load Ubuntu on it. Slow, but usable. Download DSL and Xubuntu. Deside I like Xubuntu....almost more then Ubuntu. Load it.
PC works great. Free hardware and free software.

Day 6- Pimp out my Xubuntu install with games, and modify the top panel. Now have weather reports from five cities. waste 5 hours reading the forums

Day 7 Downloading Mint...
