### Post by holadebob on 2010-06-09
This is the biggest reason Ubuntu is my system....My hard drive started reported sector errors day before yesterday, so I went out and bought a new hard drive. I then backed up my personal data folder, burned a new AptonCd, yanked out the old drive, stuck in the new, installed my Ubuntu 10.04, configured IP connection, ran Aptoncd, installed all programs from the cd, copied my personal folder and was back on line about two hours after I opened up the computer case.

It is working exactly like I had it before the problem, no tweaks needed.

I can't remember the number of times I spent many more than 8 hours recovering from some random system destroying loss with different versions of Windows, and then having to tweak the installs for days after.

My Ubuntu install is totally and completely utility, glitch free and essentially transparent. 

I am so grateful to you, the Ubuntu staff, and want to say a very heartfelt thank you!
