### Post by tealflame on 2012-06-25
After 2 months playing with Ubuntu 12.04 and Xubuntu 12.04, I came to the conclusion that there are too many glitches/bugs.  For example, My digital camera would be detected and ask to import pictures one day, but a week later it wouldn't even detect my camera.  

I ended up formatting and re-installing Windows 7.  In my opinion, Linux Ubuntu or Linux in general, is just not quite there yet.  It reminds me of the old Windows 95 or 98 days where you have glitches and crashes now and then.  Linux is ok if you only want to go on the internet and do some email or any internet stuff.  Sure it only uses about half the ram than Windows 7 but thats only good for outdated pc's.  My pc is 4 years old and runs much better with Windows 7 than any Linux Distro I tried and I did try at least a dozen of them.

Maybe next time, until then I will be using Windows 7.
