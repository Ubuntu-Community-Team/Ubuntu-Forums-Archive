### Post by BigSilly on 2009-01-18
I just wanted to post up my thanks to the Ubuntu developers and the community at large, for what has thus far proved to be the best version of Ubuntu yet. I'm currently using it on my desktop, the missus has it on her Dell laptop, and even the youngest has it on her Eee PC (via EeeBuntu). 

I've been using Ubuntu solely since Feisty 7.04, and the experience has always been overwhelmingly positive. Hardy Heron was a real revelation, and I was so happy with it I wasn't going to even try the new release. I'm so glad I did. It's rock solid, speedier than ever before, and simply massive fun to use. I'm absolutely and utterly smitten with it, I don't mind saying that, and I *hate* to sound like a fanboy!

It's even sweeter for me, as my desktop is becoming pretty outdated now, but Ubuntu runs like the wind on it. I'm hopefully getting a new one soon from a Linux retailer hopefully.

But I digress. My thanks again to the devs for a wonderful experience. Intrepid is absolutely and unequivocally the best Ubuntu yet for me.
