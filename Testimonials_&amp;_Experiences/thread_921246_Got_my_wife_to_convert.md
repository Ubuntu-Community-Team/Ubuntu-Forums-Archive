### Post by powerpleb on 2008-09-16
My girlfriend uses Ubuntu too. She is not computer savvy at all, and I find that to be a good thing when introducing people to Linux. I just quickly showed her the programs she'll most likely use and how to open them (icons on the desktop). After about a month I noticed that neither of us had booted into XP at all so I deleted the partition. Couldn't be easier.

Some of my slightly more computer literate friends who I have shown Ubuntu to, look at with complete confusion and distrust. Then they say it is not worth the effort.
