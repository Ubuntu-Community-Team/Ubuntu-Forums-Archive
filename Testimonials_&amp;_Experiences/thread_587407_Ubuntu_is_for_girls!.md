### Post by MrBordello on 2007-10-22
in a positive way!
I had a old Compaq Armada 3500 (360 mhz) laptop around, never really used it, so it was like my playground for different OS', but after I installed Xubuntu on it, I was like "WHOAA!!" that old machine was suddenly _usable_!
My girlfriend actually needed a PC and - in the spirit of ubuntu - I gave it to her.

And NEVER heard anything about it again.

But better: My girlfriend somehow found out that its not Windows running on her laptop and asked me about it. I was like "Oh no, she wants Win98!" but no ways! She wanted to know more about Linux and Ubuntu 'cause people asked her for.

One lucky weekend a female friend of my girlfriend suddenly discovered that her old PC (WinXP) was just too slow and asked her if I could fix that ... But why not install Ubuntu? (reaction of my girlfriend :guitar: )

So I started the Installation, that was one hell slow PC! So I told her what to do and left (expecting the worst).
But no call the next morning - or week.
The next weekend we met again and she told me that it was so easy to install ubuntu! She was a bit surprised that she could open all the files others gave to her (she's a student) without installing additional software.
She _really_ asked me if Ubuntu was made for girls - because of the warm colors and the easy-to-use interface. I told her that it's made for human beings - I think that was the right answer. ;)
