### Post by ctarwater on 2008-08-21
I've finally done it.
After years of being an XP "poweruser" (but never a gamer) I tried dual booting with XP / Hardy.

After my initial tweaking (wireless card, Samba shares, and testing different torrent apps) I haven't used XP for months.

So last night I completely removed it and am now running a purely Ubuntu-box.  Hell, between that, my Nokia n800, XBMC on the xbox, and the wife's Asus, we're completely MS free. 

Not because we hate it, just because we've been trying new things and liking what we see.

That being said, I won't say it was all "working out of the box" but for the most part any problems were easily fixed and I've grown to like the differences between windows interface and the ubuntu interface.

I'll probably try dual booting with another Linux distro just to play around...

Anyhow, just wanted to share a non-fanatical conversion story.
Thanks for all the help so far!
