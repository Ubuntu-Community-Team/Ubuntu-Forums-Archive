### Post by veroslav on 2011-05-10
Hi,

I would like to share my first experience using Ubuntu 11.04 and the controversial Unity desktop environment. I've been wanting to try Unity for a while and was really excited and curious, but also terrified after reading all the negative reviews it received.

I've tried to keep an open mind and be as objective as possible, even though this review is based on only a few hours of usage yesterday.

Positive:
- I like the new launcher, the look of it, the size, placement, and the way it can hide and appear again. I dont find it intrusive at all.
- I love the new way to open and search for applications and files (dash), in my opinion it is much quicker than the traditional way of doing it
- Ubuntu itself feels very stable
- My wireless card was found directly, this was not the case in 10.10

Negative:
- My video card appears to be using the proprietary drivers, but in the Additional Drivers window, it says that the driver is activated but not in use (bug as I understand)
- Few launcher customization options
- Time & Date configuration window wouldn't appear, I had to log in to the classic desktop, open it from there, log out, log into Unity again, then it would open
- The global menu feels unnecessary and clumsy, I am having a hard time finding my way up there, and also, it is quite buggy and often, there suddenly appear traces of the open applications up there, that are never cleared, and the global menu is never repainted and the menu options are invisible. You have to log out and log in again in order to for the global menu to be repainted correctly.
- Selecting visual effects in Compiz often causes graphical glithches on the desktop, and global menu in particular.
- Often, when clicking on a various buttons, nothing happens. I had to move windows around in order for the buttons to become clickable
- The not clickable button issue above applies to the launcher items as well, sometimes nothing happens when I click on these
- In my opinion, a window should be minimized when clicking on its item in the launcher, but I understand that this is a design decision
- Very hard to find applications/configuration items if one does not know the exact name for these
- I would like it if it was possible to always show a maximized dash when it is opened

There are a few more things I dont like, but those are the minor ones, mostly graphical glitches, and again these are probably design decisions I do not agree with. I am going to put much more time into Unity as I think that there is a lot of potential yet to be fullfilled. But at the moment, I think that the release 11.04 is much buggier than either 10.04 or 10.10. Hopefully, a lot of the bugs and issues I have at the moment will be fixed in the upcoming updates.

Kind Regards,
Veroslav
