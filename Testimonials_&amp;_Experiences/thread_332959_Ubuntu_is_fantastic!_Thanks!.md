### Post by Nailz71 on 2007-01-06
I have been promising myself I would drop in here say thank you and that I have found Ubuntu to be utterly fantastic! I have used Kubuntu exclusively since Dapper and Edgy is even better!

 I have zero hardware issues with Ubuntu  :) everything I have plugged into the PC has been recognized straight away! Including my mobile phones, bluetooth usb, my camera and even my ancient umax scanner. all without a driver disk or the drawn out routine any user of MS will know.

 Ubuntu has software available to do everything I need,  there is software available to do things I never knew I wanted to do and I probably haven't seen half of it so far! And its all free!

 My desktop eye candy is far better than anything I have had from MS and Object Desktop. KDE Beryl and the contributions from the community are amazing!

 Thanks to the awesome community around Ubuntu on IRC the web and these forums I have never found any issues that couldn't be overcome even for a Linux noob such as myself!

My PC runs like a dream :D thank you.
