### Post by knowledgeispwr on 2007-12-06
Hello all!
I am new to the Ubuntu family, but not to Unix-like OSes.  I'm a college student, but began experimenting with FreeBSD in middle school and have since spent time with Mandrake, SuSE and Debian (Etch and Lenny).  

I had heard good things about Ubuntu, so I finally gave it a try the other day.  I loved how everything worked out of the box.  Still, GNOME was a little hesitant/laggy at times, even with compiz turned off (I have a 1.5GHz Pentium M w/ only 256 Mb of RAM).  I thought I would try Kubuntu, because although I liked KDE, I hadn't tried it in a long time because of trouble with it under Debian.

To my pleasant surprise, Kubuntu works great :).  It's very smooth and runs better than GNOME on this older laptop!  Anyway, I am a happy camper and look forward to continuing my GNU/Linux adventures with Kubuntu
