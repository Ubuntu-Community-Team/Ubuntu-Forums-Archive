I think the two might be related. Cairo-Dock probably needs compositing in order to work to its aesthetic best, and without Compiz you don't have compositing.

Compiz not working might not be Ubuntu's fault, actually. ATI has stopped supporting its old cards on Linux and no longer makes a driver for them; this happened in late 2008, and of course the old driver doesn't work with any distribution released after that driver.

The Linux community is hard at work trying to write a good open-source driver for those cards, but it looks like yours is currently not supported for 3D and compositing.

If you could get a more recent graphics card you should be alright.
