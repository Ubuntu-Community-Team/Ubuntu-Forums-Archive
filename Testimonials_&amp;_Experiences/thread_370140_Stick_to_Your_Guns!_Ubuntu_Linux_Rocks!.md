### Post by austinwilde on 2007-02-25
I'm just wanting to encourage my fellow newcomers to linux to stay with it. I have been moving into linux from Windows for about three years now, and at this point am almost completel free of Windows! First I ran Red Hat 9, then Fedora Core 4. Then I tried Knoppix 3.6, and that worked better. Then a few months ago a friend at Sun Microsystems turned me on to ubuntu, and I am most happy! My IBM Thinkpad R50e, designed to run Windows XP Pro, works so much better using ubuntu...I pried off the XP sticker and replaced it with the ubuntu one.....UBUNTU ROCKS!

Just remember: the ONLY thing that will ever hold you back using open source software is your knowledge. With Linux there is always a way!
