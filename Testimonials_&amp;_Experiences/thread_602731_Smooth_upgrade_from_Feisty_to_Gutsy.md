### Post by Naan Yaar on 2007-11-04
Just wanted to report my most excellent (Bill and Ted flashback :)) upgrade from Feisty to Gutsy. My machine was running Feisty (upgraded from Edgy). The upgrade went through beautifully and free of any hiccups or need to intervene manually. I have an LVM set-up and Automatix2, both of which I thought may trip up the upgrade; however, my slight trepidation was completely unfounded. I have never had an in-place upgrade go through so smoothly with any OS. In fact, my normal practice, based my experiences with RedHat, starting with Picasso in '96, was to install to a separate partition and then copy user data over. Ubuntu has completely blown me away!

Many thanks to all the hard-working developers who make this happen.
