### Post by timetopat on 2012-06-10
Of all the linux distros friends have told me to try in the past ubuntu/xubuntu has to be my favorite by a mile.  Once everything is setup it is stable, easy to use, and fast.  The boot is way faster than my windows 7 install, and it is way more stable than osx(once owned a mac).  

I love synaptic and the software center, and wine allows me to have WoW and other non linux products at my fingers.  

I have used windows for all of my life but with my dislike of the new metro windows 8(used the rc a lot), i wanted something better.  

If you have been burnt by other distros in the past and want to use a stable , fast,easy to use , and fun os than ubuntu or xubuntu is great.
