### Post by Hammer3344 on 2012-08-30
I have been working with computers since I was 5 years old (now 24) and have always been entranced with Windows and all that it had to offer. Over the past 7 years I have been working as an IT tech and have slowly lost my faith in windows as a whole. I recently decided that I had finally had enough of the shinanigans of windows and decided that it was time to break into the Linux world.
 
I have tried various flavors of Linux including Slackware, Mint, OpenSuse, Fedora, CentOS, and Ubuntu, but eventually decided that Ubuntu provided the user friendly expierience allowing both new users of linux a stable base to begin working off of, expirenced users to thrive in and support for everyone in between. I have yet to have an issue that doesnt already have an answer, and have yet to find any reason to stop using Ubuntu as a whole. 
 
After touting how awesome the distro was, I converted my entire IT department to the way of the Penguin and have began to get my wife into the OS aswell.
 
I appreciate everything that the hard working Devs (of all packages, software, and the distro itself) have put forth and one day hope to be able to assist in some way (big or small) in order to give back.
 
Outstanding peice of work guys!
