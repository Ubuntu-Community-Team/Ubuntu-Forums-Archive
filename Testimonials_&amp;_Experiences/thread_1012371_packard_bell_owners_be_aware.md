### Post by Snappo on 2008-12-15
I love Ubuntu I use it on all of my computers and even try to work with it on my laptop. My journey started about seven months ago with my packard bell laptop, I installed 8.04 which went alright, my first problem was the login resolution then after I randomly typed my user and password I got in. I was met with a 800x600 resolution no battery monitor and no wireless. Seven months later the only issue solved so far (sort of) is the screen resolution but after the kernel upgrade my laptop has decided it no longer wants to allow my USB wifi hub to start. So now I'm left with no choice but install Windows XP. This isn't what I want. I use Ubuntu on all of my computers and this switch is going to be worse than my initial switch to Linux. 

Anyway the moral of the story is if you plan to use your packard bell laptop with any Linux distro your in for one hell of a mess.
