I often sell computers as a side to my pc repair business, and am selling 3 right now. I always reinstall windows on them so I can have a fast turnaround with selling them. For all those out there that think windows "just works" all the time, are misled. Sometimes you just have to use what works on that particular machine. Ubuntu FTW!
