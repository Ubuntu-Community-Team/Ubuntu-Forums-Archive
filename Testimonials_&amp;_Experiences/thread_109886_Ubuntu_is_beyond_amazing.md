### Post by beast2k on 2005-12-29
I can't believe how well ubuntu works, the speed I get from my PC now is amazing, after years screwing with slackware ubuntu is a breath of fresh air. I spent ages looking for the "ultimate distro" and I always dismissed ubuntu because it has only one install CD, I coulden't have been more wrong this distro in effect takes all the challenge out of linux this is how well it works. One small thing though shoulden't automatix be part of the default install or at least be an option at install time ? I have converted 2 people because of automatix (or whatever its called) they would not try ubuntu because of the setup, after showing them automatix they are now trying to convert windows and linux users alike to ubuntu, just a thought, many will not try ubuntu because of the setup of MP3's, codecs, plugins and other stuff (due to ubuntus open source status:confused: ?) that dont come installed and working at first boot up. Keep up the good work though I am officially converted to ubuntu I even deleated Windows XP which I couldent do with any other distro. WOW :p
