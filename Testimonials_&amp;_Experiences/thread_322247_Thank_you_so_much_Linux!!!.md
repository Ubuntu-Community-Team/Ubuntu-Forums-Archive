Hey folks
I changed my operating system from XP to Ubuntu and then Kubuntu on my Toshiba Portege M100 and I want to say: Thanks! 
I installed edgy and everything worked at once (except my v96 modem..)! Even my crapy DVD multi drive "matshita uj810" is able to burn DVDs again with K3B - with Nero6 for which I paid 50€ it was not possible anymore (do not ask why - I do not know - and frankly: I do not care anymore).

Everyone who says Linux will cost you more nerves than Windows did not have my experiences...

So I got a stable, wonderfully usable and cost-free operating system - this is my christmas miracle this year...
