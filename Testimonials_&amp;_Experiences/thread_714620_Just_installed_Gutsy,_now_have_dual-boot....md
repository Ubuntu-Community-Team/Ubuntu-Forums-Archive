### Post by ThreeTrees on 2008-03-04
... and it actually works! :)  My Sony VAIO can now dual-boot Ubuntu 7.10 and Windows XP Pro.

I'm a Linux beginner, although I did have some light UNIX admin-type experience.  I decided to make multiple Ubuntu partitions, and also not to install GRUB in the MBR, which probably caused a few more headaches than I would've had with a more vanilla install.

I originally wanted to be a *BSD user (to remain loyal to my HCX-UX and SunOS roots), but Ubuntu's live CD was a lot more polished than the FreeBSD or NetBSD live CDs I found.  Either they couldn't even boot, or were missing some piece I thought essential.

Also, Ubuntu seems to have a lot of support, which includes forums like this!

Having said that, not everything was perfect (for example, my VAIO SZ can switch between integrated and dedicated graphics, and Ubuntu boots on one but not the other :confused:), and I hope to get some advice from you folks in coming days.
