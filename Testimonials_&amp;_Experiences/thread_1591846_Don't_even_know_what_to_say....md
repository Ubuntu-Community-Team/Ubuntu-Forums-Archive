### Post by dmillerw on 2010-10-09
...I'm simply amazed.

I started using Ubuntu about two years ago, few months after the release of 8.04, the beginning was rocky, but I quickly learned, and here I am today, Windows free, and lovin' it.

Kinda funny how I started with Ubuntu anyway, started using Mac OS X at school, and simply grew tired of Windows, started itching for something else, and that's when I stumbled upon Linux, ran through a few different distros before Ubuntu, but learned something from that, and am happy I did find Ubuntu, as it seems to be the best (in my opinion anyway)

Granted, the journey hasn't been the easiest, I've had my share of hardware problems, but generally I found a workaround, and I liked researching fixes, because I learned from that.

Switched my sister to Ubuntu about a month ago, she couldn't be happier either, which is also proof that you don't have to be a complete geek to use Linux, she knows her way around a computer, but is no means an expert, and yet she uses it fine.

Was honestly amazed how much faster her laptop ran after the upgrade, it's an old ThinkPad, and it runs at least twice as fast now. Hoping to get my dad switched over eventually, but we'll see on that one.

Thank you, oh so very very much, Ubuntu community. My number one resource whenever I do have a problem, it's never failed me.

I'm glad I found Ubuntu, and I'm never looking back!
