### Post by 45acp on 2009-03-14
I had to set up an old HP scanner in Windows XP the other day for my wife because she needed to scan and email some documents. I haven't used this scanner since XP sp1 came out. I now have XP SP3 on a dual boot machine with Ubuntu. Anyway I hooked up the scanner, loaded the software. Could not get it to work. I could not get the scanning program to even open. I went to HP's website and found half a dozen different drivers and patches. I spent a hour and a half downloading, installing and rebooting before I could get it working. After the wife finished what she needed to do I though I would try the scanner with Ubuntu. Opened up XSane and the scanner worked perfectly. No downloading drivers. No rebooting. Really wish I could get my wife to use Ubuntu so I can delete XP.
