### Post by swisscow on 2008-06-21
I recently dropped off the linux map to use a macbook. They are great, if, like my girlfriend, you are happy to do things the Apple way. However I started to miss the playing around and the choice with linux, so the macbook went off to ebay and I hope whoever bought it is very happy.

So back to Ubuntu. I travel a lot for work so needed a laptop. After a lot of research I bought an XPS M1330 (refurbished model). Very pleased with it and I am pleased to say everything seems to work great! Have just got the fingerprint reader working (gimicky but cool) with Hardy. Even got my ipod to work, though my scanner goes through a virtual machine and windows  :-(

So the point of this ramble is:

[LIST=1]
[*]Research the hardware before you buy
[*]Read around the forums to find the solutions (I haven't had to struggle at all......(so far))
[*]Thank the people who make Ubuntu and linux in general work for people like me.
[/LIST]
