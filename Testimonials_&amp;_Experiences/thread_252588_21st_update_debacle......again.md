### Post by hellfried on 2006-09-07
this morning i woke up thinking about the august 21st update debacle and how it resembled a particularly devastating windows virus attack. well maybe the malicious intent was not there but if you look at the fallout, a lot of people experienced total system blackouts and had to spend hours getting everything up and running  . as a result a lot of time was wasted and the level of frustration and heartache was unmeasurable.

the silver lining to this massive rain cloud was of course the speed as which the solutions popped up in this forum. it was heart warming to see the community effort that went into teasing out the problem and the top notch soltuions that were bandied about. the helpfulness of the ubuntu forums have time and again been the saving grace for a lot of ubuntu users but really this time it just outshone itself. 

on a personal note i was not affected by the faulty update because i had the good fortune of reading these forums first before clicking the update icon. however i followed the thread closely trying to learn as much as i could. and i did. thank you guys and girls!
