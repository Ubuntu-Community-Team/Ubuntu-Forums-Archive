### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-02-24
Well , today I recognize my first year as a Ubuntu user and it has been a good one. I started with a Wubi installation of 9.10 and joined the forum the same day . 9.10  worked well until I got the bright idea of trying to update to an early version of 10.04 which borked my installation.

I decided to try a traditional dual boot and that was great , but I went a little PPA crazy and 9.10 became buggy because I did not realize that PPAS don't always play nice together.

10.04 was great except for missing title bars , but that was fixed by an update and the problem returned just prior to the 10.10 Release.

10.10 has been the best release for me so far and I hope this is a trend .
I have come a long way since posting to find out how to restore my trash can .

The people here have been very helpful and I have tried to give back as I learn . Rock On !:guitar:
