### Post by richterlevania3 on 2008-04-14
Hi guys,

Finally I managed to install the video drivers for my nVidia 6800 Ultra and play World of Warcraft on Wine. I activated the Desktop effects and my friends are envy of me now. Men, I feel the power flowing in my veins like I never did before. I´ve been an Linux´s enthusiast for a long time, but only with Ubuntu 7.04 I felt the time had arrived to me fully convert to Linux. 
No more viruses, no more anti-virus. No more malwares and similars. Stability and freedom to install any program I need with no restrictions, zero $... So many benefits that I can´t tell all them.
First I couldn´t initialize Ubuntu, then I discovered that typing -noapic in the safe graphics mode of the Live-CD works for me. Then I had many problems to install the video driver, but with the help of the Ubuntu Forums I got it. Wine I did with no problems.

I couldn´t feel happier. Aleluia Ubuntu!
