### Post by keithpeter on 2012-04-28
+ 10^6

I've been informally testing 12.04 and hanging out on the Ubuntu+1 subforum, checking launchpad for bugs and reading stuff off unity-design mailing list.

The impact that Canonical are making is way above their annual spend! HUD got global attention, even on the BBC. Just hope this translates into contracts...

**@GameX2**: google 'cathedral and bazaar' for an explanation of the open source/free software process that makes it possible for a relatively small organisation to produce and maintain an entire operating system. Richard Stallman would argue that Linux is a kernel and it needs a 'user land' and a 'toolchain' to make a complete operating system. Neal Stephenson wrote an amusing essay with the title 'In the beginning was the command line': worth googling.
