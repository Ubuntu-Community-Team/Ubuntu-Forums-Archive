### Post by bug67 on 2012-05-15
Of relief!  :P

I had been happily using Ubuntu until they switched the buttons to the left side.  Meh, whatever.  I changed the theme.  But it also started me distro hopping.  I landed on Linux Mint 8 and stayed through version 10.  I skipped version 11 due to some weird incompatibility issues.  I then tried Mint 12 which had dropped the Gnome 2 DE.  What a mess!  And Ubuntu's own Unity wasn't to my liking either.  I briefly tried Xubuntu 11.04 but soon, reluctantly went back to Windows.  :(

Today, I bit the bullet and did a full Xubuntu 12.04 install on a spare laptop.  The install, with all the updates was complete in 20 minutes.  I had all my tweaks, icons, cursors, everything done an hour later.  It was like I never left!  

This is the most complete and functional desktop I have experienced in, well, since Linux Mint 10.  The only thing it's missing is event sounds.  Apparently, xfce doesn't support them.  S'OK though.  Everything else is FLAWLESS!  :cool:
