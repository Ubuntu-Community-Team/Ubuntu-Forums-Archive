### Post by armandh on 2010-02-27
one of the great things about Ubuntu linux is the lack of need for cpu robbing antivirals. 
an OS that has, for me, the right balance between services and speed. 
So the other day when I found I could get the DSL moved to a higher speed at a lower cost, 
I thought this is another no brainer, and took the bait.

about mid way through the explanation of features and benefits there came some pitch for software to keep my computer in top form.

the magic moment for a bit of proselytizing on my part.
I got to explain why I did not need it. 
can you imagine a person selling broadband who never heard of linux.

I left with my faster DSL, no extras, 
and a promise from the sales person to google ubuntu at home.
