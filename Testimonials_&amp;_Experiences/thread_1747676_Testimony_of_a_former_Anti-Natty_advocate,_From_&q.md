 Hi wildmanne, :)

Thank you! <3
(In Roswell huh? ~ Lucky!) lol :)

Sure, 
So you have installed Clarity already..
All you should have to do, is **open Dash** {click ubuntu logo or press super/windows key}
*and type in* **Appearance**.
When you see the Appearance icon , click on it.  

WHen the window opens, Click the "Customize" button at the bottom. 
Then in that window, Click the "Icons" Tab, and choose 'Clarity" from the list.

(or have you already tried that?)


To get the "menu" on the desktop, 
You have to have AWN running, 
With AWN running,** right click the "AWN dock"** and choose** "dock preferences".**
 Now in the** "Applets**" "tab" in AWN settings window, 
Scroll down until you find an applet called **"Yet another main menu"** and add that to AWN. 

 Now there will be a little ubuntu logo/button on your AWN "dock", wherever you placed it.
Just click on it to open the menu. 
 Then you can put this anywhere on the desk you want, pretty much. 

 If you'd want me explain it better (with screenshots if you want) to set it up, just ask. 

Hope this helps. :)
