### Post by sledge73 on 2009-11-12
I have only been using linux for a couple of years now & in my opinion Karmic is the best thing to happen. Before I get flamed let me explain.

I was running ubuntu on an old HP WX4000 workstation since I started using linux. I always left a separate partition open to test other distros, none of the other hundred or so distros that I tried played very well with that computer. So I stuck with what works (and it worked flawlessly & still does). At the beginning of the year I went out & bought a Emachines EL1210-09 with windoze vista preinstalled & man it sucked!!!! So I thought I would try linux on it. I had nothing but problems, the desktop would freeze every 15-20 minutes, the integrated Nvidia everything just wouldn't work, when I checked to see if the hardware was detected the only answer I got from the command line was unknown. Unknown??? Mind you this was the same for 8.04, 8.10, 9.04 & every other distro on the market. So I was stuck with the worst implementation of windoze in the world. Then came Karmic, everything worked perfectly out of the box!!! I have not had any troubles since!!! Needless to say Vista is long gone & Win7 aint even a thought! 

I am one of 7 linux users in my community of 25,000 people & we all share the same experience. So I just want to say thank you ubuntu for this great distro & keep up the good work no matter how much people bitch & moan!!!!

On another note I ordered a Live CD from shipit in late October & I just received it to day which surprised me cause I'm in the U.S.A.

Any way thanks again!!!

### Post by [matt] on 2009-11-12
Ditto, I have spent the past year trying to make the shift into Ubuntu. For a newbie to Linux, the change has not been justifiable up until Karmic. Windows is just too easy to use compared to previous editions. Plugging in some random, basic USB device could require ridiculous amounts of research - sometimes with no solution. I wish I could count how many times I have read a post about some moderator or seasoned user on UbuntuForums getting their panties in a wad because some newbie was looking for information and needed a little HUMAN assistance. It was always easier for me to just use Windows, especially since I had no specific reason other than curiosity to use Ubuntu in the first place. I eventually trashed Ubuntu 9.04 completely and went crawling back to Vista. I gave it another shot with Karmic Koala and have only looked back to update my Blackberry Storm(that's another battle in itself; thank you OS 5.0. But I digress). Ubuntu 9.10 has used all of my USB devices with little tweaking so far. I do need to mention that this version is still far from perfect. However, Karmic is refined enough to justify that leap from Windows. With such a drastic advance in usability, I would have expected this to be Ubuntu 10, not just another 9.xx. Thanks Ubuntu!

P.S. Speaking of BlackBerry, does anyone know when (or if) Barry is expected to support the Storm?
