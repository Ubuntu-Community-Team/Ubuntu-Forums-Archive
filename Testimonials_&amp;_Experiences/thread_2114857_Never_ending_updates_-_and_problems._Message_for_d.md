you can stay on LTS version (current one is 12.04, old one is 10.04) which are supported for 5 years from 12.04 onwards. meaning you do not have to upgrade a system for 5 years after install. otherwise the next LTS will be in 14.04 i believe.
have a look here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases)
