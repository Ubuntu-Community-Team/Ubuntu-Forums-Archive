### Post by Comhra on 2008-07-24
It's a year since I discovered Ubuntu.  I'm still using Gutsy and it's disgustingly stable.  Zero problems since before Xmas.  

I've learnt not to tweak it so much.

No crashes at all since early on in November, very few problems apart from Adobe Flash related ones.

Not a virus in sight, ocassionally, I scan the home folder, no rootkits, intrusions, spyware  or malware of any description.  This system is rock solid, almost to the point of boredom.  

Thinking about doing a clean install of HH but Gutsy is so reliable that I'm loathe to risk it.
