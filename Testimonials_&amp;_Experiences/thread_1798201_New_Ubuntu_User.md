### Post by Muttly on 2011-07-06
Hi all, just thought I'd make my first post a happy one!
I have just moved from Windoze (last 12 months) after spending the last 11 years farting around fixing Bills rubbish (like most users). I have been looking for a replacement to my MCE2005 and stumbled upon Myth TV, and saw the strong support for Ubuntu decided to build my new Media Centre on this OS.
I went with Ubuntu 11.04 64bit on a dual core Atom330 with Ion GPU and 4 GB Ram. Installation was a breeze and after sorting out the wireless dongle installed Myth TV.
[I]
May I add here that the Belkin F5D7050UK USB dongle works out the box without any blacklist and cost about a fiver on Amazon!

[/I]Myth TV didnt work for me, no TV card used so kept getting backend server errors so I tried XBMC. Wow this program is amazing and looks stunning, I just had to point the repo at the Mavrick version to get the install to work as there isnt a Natty repo.

I am so made up I installed Ubuntu, just whish I switched earlier!
