### Post by LaDeDa on 2007-10-08
I received the Ubuntu 7.04 CD about a month ago.  Actually, it only took a week to arrive after my request.
I had a mid-nineties, Win98, 256mhz RAM, 400mhz Celeron machine I thought would be a great platform for Ubuntu.
When I initially loaded the O/S, it was a dual-boot affair with Win98.  I had a screen resolution problem (only resolution I got was 640x480) and I knew the monitor and vid card would do more because I was running 1024x768 with Win98.  Otherwise, the O/S worked perfectly.
I decided to reinstall Ubuntu hoping it might pick up the vid card and load drivers to solve my screen resolution problem.
I reinstalled but, I chose the wrong partitioning selection and the hard drive was totally formatted leaving me with Ubuntu only.  It wasn't a problem for me because I had prepared the machine for a charity donation prior to initially installing Ubuntu dual-booting with Win98.
I was impressed with Ubuntu although it wasn't as fast as I'd hoped.
I decided to keep the old dog and do an upgrade.  I ordered a new mobo with onboard graphics and audio, Celeron D 3.46ghz, a gig of RAM, and a WD 80gb hard drive.  This particular online store is known for its fast delivery and my order was no exception.  I received my order the next day !!  
It took about an hour and a half to install the new parts in my old case, two hours burn in, and I was ready to install Ubuntu.
The install went perfectly.  Ubuntu picked up everything except the sound chip, but the drivers it installed worked fine.  The machine is fast and I couldn't be happier.  For less than $200 US dollars, I have a great second system for my purposes.
MANY THANKS to all who have worked, and contributed, to this project.  I am, without question, a TOTAL new guy to Ubuntu, but this O/S has windows beat hands down.  Just for snickers, I started out with a 8088 DOS machine.  We've come a long way since my first 9600bps modem.  Ubuntu, and other Linux based O/S's are the future. :):):)   Keep up the good work !!!
