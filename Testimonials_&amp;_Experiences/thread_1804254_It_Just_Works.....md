### Post by CJ56 on 2011-07-14
Been using Kubuntu 11.04 and Ubuntu 11.04 + Unity back to back (two hard drives & f12) just to compare them...

And KDE 4.6.2 is very, very good, no two ways about it; Kubuntu is every bit as polished nowadays as OpenSUSE - but...

I keep coming back to Ubuntu & Unity. I know it's still a work in progress but I have to say that I can get stuff done so quickly & easily with the Unity DE that it's become the one that I stick with. In fact I like it so much that not only is it on my desktop PC, I've put in on my netbook where (so far) it goes beautifully

Hats off & major thanks to the Canonical team: it was a brave move to drop the classic interface, but once you get used to Unity, it just works....
