### Post by dpaint4 on 2006-08-15
Something that just occured to me -- something I'd never thought of in this particular way before, makes me so angry.

Every single reason I ever want to use Ubuntu has to do with it being an awsome operating system that behaves just as I want it to, is responsive, beautiful, and very flexible.

Every single reason I ever want to use Windows has nothing to do with Windows, but always with some stupid piece of hardware with closed drivers, or some tacky chunk of software that I'm forced to use by a client or boss.

Windows is so unresponsive, unattractive by default, and innefficient. I used to take it for granted that it was like that. It was just my computer, I thought. It was 'just my computer' that took five minutes to boot or shut down, and usually ended in a crash of some violent scary unrepeatable type.

Ubuntu is the only operating system on my laptop, and it handles the task so well it makes me want to cry. Even things I'd never expect to work, like the cheap little built-in card reader from some unknown sweatshop. Or the stupid light to let me know if I'm online or not. Stuff like that! Why would they even bother. Well they did.

Beyond that, I love the default SVG icons, and the gorgeous and bravely different default color pallette and Metacity. I love the easy integration of desktop FTP, and the simple integrated disc burning. I love my free software like Gimp and Inkscape. I love Rythmbox (okay well maybe not love...) but Rythmbox is kind of okay!

Ubuntu is the perfect operating system, or at least more perfect than anything else currently available for my own purposes, and the only thing slowing me down from ditching the alternatives immediately is all the crappy hardware and software that I have to use for other people.

At least I have the freedom to use what I want to use on my laptop, and hands down I choose Ubuntu 6.06LTS! I mean, I thought 5.10 was impressive, but this is just immaculate. I can't wait for Edgy if this is the rate of improvement!
