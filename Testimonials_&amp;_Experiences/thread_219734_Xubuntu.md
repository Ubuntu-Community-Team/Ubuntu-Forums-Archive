### Post by timefortea on 2006-07-20
I installed Xubuntu on my Thinkpad T21 / 512Mb RAM because I wanted something snappy for older hardware and it had to have a choesive desktop environment - and I got it! I had an initial wireless problem (resolved) and unfortunately haven't got suspend/resume working yet, but other than that its everything I hoped for. In fact, I was more than surprised that after installing a few extra codecs, xfmedia seems to play more files than my Windows PC (and it has "XXX Mega Super Codec Pack 30000" on it...) Given, there seems to be a line of garbage at the top of the window a lot of the time but at least its consistent!

Congratulations to the devs for putting this together, its a top-notch product and it brings the excellent XFCE4 into the spotlight a little more, too.
