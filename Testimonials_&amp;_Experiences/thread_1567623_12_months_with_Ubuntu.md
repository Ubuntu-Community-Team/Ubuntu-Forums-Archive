### Post by rmccutchan on 2010-09-04
After 12 months with Ubuntu, I can't figure out why the rest of the world hasn't switched.  I know, the gamers need Windows for their games, but for the the rest of us...........

Long story short, I had to re-format 2 windows computers and my Ubuntu computer this week (I won't go into details as to why....my screw up.  I stepped out of my realm of expertise, and yes, I had everything backed up).  The Ubuntu computer was slick and simple, even though I set it up as a dual boot with windows (so I can do some work from home).  I am STILL getting the windows computers set up, while the Ubuntu computer has been working great within an hour of installing.

I realized that I needed to print a paper for class tonight at the last minute, so I plugged my Epson printer into my Ubuntu computer, and Ubuntu said "Hey!  I know you!"  and seconds later I was printing.  It saw my printer right away and started printing.  And I mean literally 3 seconds after I plugged it in.  It was a different story with the windows computer.  Windows said "Find the drivers your own damn self!"  STILL installing drivers.........

Sorry for the ramblings, but I just wanted to chime with my praises for the Ubuntu team and all their efforts.  PLEASE keep up the great work!  I keep poking our IT guys in the hopes that they will take a closer look at Ubuntu!
