### Post by wootah on 2008-12-25
Incredible.

I purchased a Toshiba Satellite M300 [03C00Q] (3gb ram, ATI Mobile HD 3400 w/HDMI, eSATA, wifi, 14.whatever", your standard laptop) and decided to opt-out of the Vista EULA and asking for a refund for the license as per the license agreement.

Anyways, I used the 8.10 live cd in store to test out the laptop and found out that everything I tested worked! 

Stuff I tested and verified that works:

[LIST]
[*]Networking (wired and wireless)
[*]Sound (including using the physical control)
[*]ALL media buttons ('feather-touch' and Fn-F#)
[*]Touchpad w/both direction scroll and single/double tap
[*]APCI (suspend, hibernate, lid close along with full restoration of all services)
[*]Composition (after proprietary driver installation)
[*]LCD brightness control
[*]MMC access (can R/W to SD cards)
[*]Physical wireless enable/disable switch
[/LIST]
I would say this is an *excellent* laptop to use Ubuntu with. I had zero configuration problems. My only issue is the crappy battery life--which I have extended by use of powertop, disabling some unnecessary services (Hmm, bluetooth included? But I don't have it :)), removing extra TTYs, disabling Firewire (don't use it ever) and a few other things which have yielded to close to 2 hours of battery life... still mediocre in my opinion. I guess that could be blamed on ATI's power management in their drivers (</stab_in_the_dark>).

I recommend Ubuntu and this laptop to anyone! Ubuntu has come a LONG way and I appreciate all the effort that has gone into the development of this distribution! I think more people should post their positive experiences into the Testimonials section :)
