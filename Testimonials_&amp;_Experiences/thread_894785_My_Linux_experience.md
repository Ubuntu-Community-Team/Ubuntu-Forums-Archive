### Post by barzam on 2008-08-19
Hello everyone,
I frequently browse this forum looking for tips and fun how-to's and decided to share my Linux experience with you all. I have been playing lots of computer games over the years so that's been my biggest reason to stay with Windows. Anyway, I got tired of the new crappy games and when a friend of mine encouraged me to try out Ubuntu I went for it. 

I took a leap and removed Windows completely before installing Ubuntu 8.04. It worked perfectly on my Dell inspiron laptop. Even better than my old Vista/Xp installation. I even managed to fix the clicking noice my HD was making with the program Laptop mode tools. The system is working fast, smooth and it looks very nice. 

What I liked the most with Ubuntu was the huge catalogue of free, excellent programs of every kind. I was actually totally not aware of this before trying out Ubuntu. I'm still pretty amazed of the crazy amount of programs available. With some help from my Linux-savvy friend I have managed to solve most of the problems I have encountered. 

After about half a year using Linux I am currently using Mint on my Dell and Xubuntu on my newly bought eeepc 900. It works excellent on both the systems. I have also learned a lot of stuff about computers and really got a huge interest in the field. I'm very satisfied with everything and I can't see myself going back to Windows.

When I play games nowadays I play them through Wine or Dosbox. Windows feels very distant at the moment. 

Thanks a lot for making this possible.
