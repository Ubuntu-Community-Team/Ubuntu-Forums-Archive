### Post by Nekiruhs on 2007-12-29
You don't improve LTS releases. They get no application updates except security ones. Thats the LTS Definition. The next upcoming release will be an LTS, Hardy Heron. The releases in between LTS's focus more on introducing new features than fixing bugs. The LTS release is all about polishing the features introduced in the releases prior. This is the way the system works. The LTS release is designed for corporations who want as minimal a risk of failure as possible and users who don't need the updated apps and only want security and stability. The normal releases are for people who want the latest apps and features. You can't have the latest X, Y and Z programs while maintaining the level of stability expected from an LTS release. Hardy will have the most stable versions of the latest apps in an effort to update the apps but keep them stable.
