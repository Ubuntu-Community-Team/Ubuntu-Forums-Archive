### Post by mdgrech on 2009-04-10
Today my sister had me fix her windows computer.  It was a mess, filled with viruses and trojans.  Just to fix things was such a chore, update computer, update anti-virus, update spyware, run all three, remove programs that might be creating security threats, defrag the hard drive, and even after all that the thing is still a mess.  

With Ubuntu I don't have to spend any of my time doing things like running an anti-virus, or updating my firewall.  Its light-weight, beautiful, and secure.  Keep up the good work team:)

I struggled with that, too.  I'd go back to Windows, tired of Ubuntu's little quirks (which were largely of my own making) and the drama on the forum (some of which, also, was largely of my own making), just to come right back to Ubuntu because I was afraid to use my computer on the Internet.
