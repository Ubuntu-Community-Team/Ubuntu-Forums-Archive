### Post by Mr_Vercetti on 2006-06-18
Hello fellow Ubuntu users! I am here today to tell you a story of love, lust, and romance. Well not really. It's how I came to discovering the power of Ubuntu.

Once apon a time, in my computer, Windows had, yet again, failed me. After breaking yet another keyboard, and some yelling, I decided enough was enough. Seeing as you can't get OS X to run on a PC, I decided to try my luck with Linux. I found a version that seemed to suit my needs, PCLinuxOS, but after a few days I noticed that it wasn't working out between us. That's when I heard about Ubuntu. I downloaded Ubuntu and it was okay. I went to try out the other versions and found Xubuntu to be my perfect OS. I've had Xubuntu for about two weeks now and I must say it has gone far beyond my expectations for a Linux operating system. Thank you everyone who had anything to do with the creation of Ubuntu for a simply perfect OS.=D> =D> =D> =D>
