### Post by ashwinrao on 2010-01-07
I'm using XP as my OS and believe me that was pirated version installed by my PC assembler. In India, I'm sure most of the XP versions are pirated. I was thinking to have my own legal OS without the headache of viruses and purchasing additional software's for my daily work. Then, I discovered Ubuntu and tried it as dual boot. I have came across many hurdles as any newbie in Linux world. Even I lost 30 gb of my stored files in the process. I had installed Ubuntu thrice since I made many mistakes by running commands using terminal.

Now, I learnt how to use Ubuntu OS and using it for daily works at home. I am enjoying freedom of Linux world and learning new things daily. I'm very thankful for this forum and Ubuntu community for the help and guides. 

I'm also wish to give a message to Ubuntu freshers. DO NOT RUN ANY COMMANDS IN TERMINAL IF YOU DO NOT KNOW WHAT EXACTLY YOU ARE DOING! 

I still wish to keep XP in my PC since it is useful only for playing games occasionally by children's who visits my home! 

[B]I take this opportunity to thank every member of this forum and Ubuntu family who helped me to discover the real OS and real computing.

In return of this favour, I will refer more members to Ubuntu family.[/B]:D
