My friend has been running Vista all year on his 'vista ready' notebook.  It has just given him hell... Finally one night, having a few beers, he loses his patience, and asks me how hard linux is (he knows I am an Ubuntu lover).  

I tell him "a hell of a lot easier than that.  Installing really isn't hard, troubleshooting can get fun, but there is ALWAYS an answer to whatever problem you come to."

He has now installed it, and raves about it.  The only thing he has to complain about is he can't find anyone that actually KNOWS how to get an iPod Touch to sync his music, and tell how to with detailed instructions.

But he is working on it.

There is still hope for this world.
