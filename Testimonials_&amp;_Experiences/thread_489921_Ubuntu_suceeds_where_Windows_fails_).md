### Post by prizrak on 2007-07-01
This is nothing but a little anecdote I would like to share. Me and my girlfriend were hosting a little get together. One of the guests brought an HDD that he was trying to copy the files from for another guest. He has an external USB2.0/SATA enclosure that we mounted the drive into. This used to be a Windows XP C:\ drive so when mounted under Windows would not show any files. So I was asked to give it a whack, I plugged it into my Ubuntu laptop. It took about 5-6 seconds to recognize and automount and as soon as it did I had full access to the drive and was able to copy all the files she wanted :)
