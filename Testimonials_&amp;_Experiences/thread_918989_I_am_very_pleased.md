### Post by justin-white on 2008-09-13
I have been an advanced Microsoft user for years. My computer just wasn't fitting my needs when I was using Microsoft (Windows XP). I needed a stable and efficient operating system. I had always thought Linux was very command line like and hard to use. One of my friends suggested this thing called "Ubuntu." I said to him "What in the heck is that!?" I had never heard of it before. So, I started out using Wubi, so I didn't have to lose my Windows stuff. After about a month, I found myself never using the XP. Once I realized this, I thought for a bit; and made a backup of everything. I formatted my hard drive and went full-out Ubuntu! I can honestly say, from a hardcore Microsoft user's point of view, Ubuntu is great! I have yet to turn back to Microsoft. I hope it stays that way.

To all the developers/contributers:

I really appreciate your time and effort, and I hope to get involved soon! :)
