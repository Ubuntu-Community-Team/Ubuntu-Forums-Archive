### Post by armandh on 2008-07-14
my brother-in-law [who sells motorcycles] has a free computer from me. it was running 7.10 but he wanted to wipe and reload.
he was phished and wanted to be sure he had not down loaded anything. I explained about having to enter his computer password, and since this is bypassed at log in he would have to really think about it. he did not remember ever entering such, but wanted to be sure. so a new ver 8.04 went on his "free" computer. [800 Mhz P-III it has a small HDD 4.3 Gig.] the install was uneventful save one equipment problem. only 640x480 or less would fly no matter what I tried. then I remembered the 17" monitor had broken? pnp. plugged in a good monitor and many more resolutions became available with the reboot. updates took forever. flash and codex installs are second nature now.
he is good to go.
