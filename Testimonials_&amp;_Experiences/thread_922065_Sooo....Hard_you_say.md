### Post by Bios Element on 2008-09-16
*laughs* As a gamer, The only thing I figured I was going to miss from Windows was Steam. Well steam "sorta" works just fine! Using Wine to run it is somewhat clunky so i took a look at the half dozen or so Games that somewhat interested me. Nexuiz and Urban Terror simply have me hooked. I spend hours playing and if a friend wants to join, Be he Windows or Linux, I just hook 'em up with a Download Link and away we go.

People are always saying how hard Linux is. About how evil the Terminal is. And yet It's my Primary and currently only OS. I have a copy of XP and Vista laying in a pile and wouldn't consider installing them. Now i admit, The reason i installed Ubuntu was that Windows decided to drive me nuts and out of disgust i formatted. But I don't regret it in the least.

The command line is the single most useful feature I've ever seen. Sure flashy GUI's can be nice for overly complex things, But for installs, Rebooting the gui and watching for bugs, Nothing beats it. And programming on Linux is even easier then it was on windows. Particularly since most of the programs you 'can' use are Open Source, You have access to huge amounts of library's and resources to design with.

So come on, Lets put to rest the excuse that you cannot game on Linux. You CAN game on Linux as well as do just about whatever else you want to do.
