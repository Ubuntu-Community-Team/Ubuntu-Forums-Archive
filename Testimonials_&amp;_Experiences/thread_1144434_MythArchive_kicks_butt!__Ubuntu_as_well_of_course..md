### Post by Committed on 2009-04-30
Ok, it's been a couple months now with Ubuntu.  Got through the quick learning process and am hooked for life.  All computers running Intrepid Ibex now, only my main desktop running dual boot to windoze because I have to have quickbooks for my business.

Now about Mythtv and Mytharchive.  First, it took a good deal of time and configuring, searching forums and googling to finally get Mytharchive working, but now that it is, it rocks.  I used to record shows on Vista with Media Center and record to DVD often.  There was no deleting commercials,  could only fit 1 hour program on a DVD unless you reduced quality and then it had to re-encode, which took forever.  With MythArhive, I can use a cut list to eliminate all commercials, put up to 6 hours of video on one dvd, have animated scene selections and it's faster than Media Center.  Way more configurable, more recording options etc etc.  Love it!
