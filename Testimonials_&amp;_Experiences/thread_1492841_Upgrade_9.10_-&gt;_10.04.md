### Post by MarcusW on 2010-05-25
Thought I'd way in with a little feedback about the upgrade system. The 9.10 install was fairly clean (not mine, a friends), no really invasive changes to the system. The upgrade took a couple of hours, and after the reboot these are the noticable changes after starting the usual programs:

MUCH better performance, both general software and OpenGL. (alot better NTFS performance as well?)

The buttons were on the left.

VDPAU was removed, fixed with a quick "sudo aptitude install libvdpau1".

After all advice about how a clean install is the only way, I am really impressed. All settings left as they should be, the only real difference is very improved performance. Good job! :)
