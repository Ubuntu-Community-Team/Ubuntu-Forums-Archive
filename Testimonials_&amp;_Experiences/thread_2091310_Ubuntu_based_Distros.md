> BertN45

Don't be surprised if it runs slow with 512mb of ram. I believe 12.04 uses about 380mb - OOTB. If your system ram is needed for video or audio, you may find that it slows significantly.

You might try [Lubuntu]("http://lubuntu.net/") or even [Xubuntu]("http://xubuntu.org/") should you find it slowing or locking up. They require less resources, while still remaining under the Canonical umbrella.
