### Post by TrevP on 2012-05-17
Yes, I agree. I tried to like Unity through 11.04 and 11.10 but about 6 months ago, I decided to try Xubuntu and I find it suits the way I work much better than the Unity model. I have recently upgraded to Xubuntu 12.04 and it is an excellent distribution. The only issue I had when I upgraded was that the proprietary Nvidia driver crashed with my older Nvidia card (a recognised problem and NOT a problem with Xubuntu). I'm now using the Nouveau driver with the Gallium 3D acceleration and everything is working perfectly.

Thanks to all at the Xubuntu team.
