### Post by tbeld on 2006-07-02
I d/led the ubuntu 6.06 live cd to give it a whirl because my Windows box was acting up. 

I am completely amazed by ubuntu 6.06! In the past¸I have used RedHat¸ Mandrake¸ and a couple others. Every time I would fight to get things working and spend a ton of time spinning the wheels of my mind.

Ubuntu live cd picked up the wireless settings I had¸ recognized every last bit of hardward¸ and came with many of the apps I need. After I saw how everything was working off the cd¸ I installed over Windows. Everything¸ I repeat¸ everything worked immediately. No problems with wireless wlan0 and all that. (I think I spent a couple weeks on that with a laptop and Mandrake last year). No problems with my HP 5600 series printer. No problems with networking. Ubuntu is simply blowing my mind.

I did have two minor issues. 1. I had a hard time getting my wife´s Windows XP box to print through my Ubuntu. It turned out to be a driver issue on the XP side and not an Ubuntu issue. (For some reason the driver wasn´t available through the add network printer selections but after playing around a bit¸ I got it going). 2. Audacity was hard to install for me. I had to manually satisfy all dependencies because sudo apt-get install audacity would return unknown package and it wasn´t showing up in the package manager. Once I manually satisfied all dependencies it installed just fine. Anyone else noticed this?

I´m rollin now... Samba is working great and I got emacs and auctex and my old brewing app Qbrew loaded and I´m loving how easy it is to work in Ubuntu.

With the past distros I´ve tried it would take several days to a couple weeks to get where I am now after a little more than 24 hours. I did try and d/l another distro´s live cd but it didn´t work off the cd with all my hardware. Is any other distro even close to this good??
