### Post by octopuskevin on 2008-04-29
I know I'm probably going to get blasted for this for being lame etc, but I really think it's important to say a big thank you to everyone that works and contributes to the ubuntu project.

Sure ubuntu has its issues. bugs this, problems that, and often these forums are flooded with discussions of its pitfalls and solutions to errors. Half the time I think we forget that our favorite OS is free, both in speech and beer, and forget that those developing and working on it are volunteers.

I was introduced to ubuntu as my first linux disto when dapper came out, and while my time with linux has certainly not been very long compared to others, I have watched it grow and develop with ubuntu becoming almost the flagship distro. While I would always encounter problems along the way, I found linux to be almost liberating, and that the mundane activities I did under windows all of a sudden transformed into something of joy when I used linux.

So for that, I just want to say a big THANK YOU to everyone and anyone that has worked on the ubuntu project, or on any linux project in general. I have been amazed by the developments, the tools, and the growing ease of use that I have seen evolve over time, and feeling assured to speak on behalf of everyone, we are forever thankful for what you guys do. 

Keep up the fantastic work!

- Kevin
