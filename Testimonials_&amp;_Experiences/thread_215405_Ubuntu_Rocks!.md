### Post by randell6564 on 2006-07-13
Hello!

Well... I've tested out quite a few Linux OS's and I just have to say that ubuntu, by far is the best!

From a newbie stand point anyway.  I've finally found a system that has given me the confidence to throw out my Microsoft cd and go Linux full-time!

There is just too much that I could write about Linux as a community of developers, but WAY TO GO PEOPLE!

I use wireless on my box and I'm sure that a lot of folks can relate to all the headaches that wireless and Linux can bring.  Especially when the hardware manufacturer does not support linux. (linksys wusb11 wireless-B)

You can imagine how impressed I was when I installed ubuntu, configured System>Administration>Networking and in a Split, I was up and running!

I couldnt believe that it was that easy!  

Anyway, thats just a drop-in-the-bucket.  To put it short,  Ubuntu Rocks!

Microsoft,  'KISS MY #$%!!'
