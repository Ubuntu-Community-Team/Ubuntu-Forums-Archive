### Post by Catalyst2Death on 2008-05-12
Hello,

I would like to say that I really enjoy our community.  While there is a fair amount of discussion which criticizes Hardy, I can understand.  On the whole the community is WONDERFUL.  I have not run into a more patient community.  There are some communities which would be nice, except that there are moderators which love the power.  There are other communities in which mods are too lax.  This makes the community more anarchist.  The Ubuntu community walks the tight rope of careful moderation and freedom beautifully.  The members of the community are always willing to be helpful.  Indeed, there is a certain amount of selective pressure involved in the older members of the community.  In either case, even new members are polite, and indeed helpful in their own right (they often force different perspectives on ``old'' problems).  In short, thanks Ubuntu.
