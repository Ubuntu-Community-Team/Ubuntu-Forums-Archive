### Post by Bigtime_Scrub on 2012-06-09
It has been quite some time since I've been here or used Ubuntu. I left back around the time the close button was moved to the left. I thought I'd check out my first love...Ubuntu.... I have to say I dislike Unity. I really tried to like it, I really did. It just doesn't feel right for me. Tried Gnome 3 and found it to be vile... 

Anyway I gave Xubuntu a spin on my extra laptop and I have to admit it feels right and is done right. For the most part it has SANE defaults and with a little sprucing up it looks sleek and professional. I took off the ridiculous auto hide panel and moved the panel from top to bottom, changed wallpaper and added Faenza icons and a new theme and WOW. 

It looks good. It runs goods. That's all that matters. It is the way Ubuntu used to be. (Well maybe not the looking good part ;-)) I don't know if I'm in love with Ubuntu again or if it's just xfce in general I dunno. I like it alot. For what it's worth, and I'm nobody really, I highly recommend it.
