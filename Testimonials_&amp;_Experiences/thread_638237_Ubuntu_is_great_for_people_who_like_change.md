### Post by Afkpuz on 2007-12-11
I dont know about the rest of you, but I tend to like change.  If something has been the same for a while, I wanna change it.  I'm constantly in search of new and better things because of my love for change.  Ubuntu met that need in my software environment.  I love the 6 month release cycle because it's just about that time when I need something new.  There's not really a point to this post.  I just love how ubuntu compliments my personality.  Plus, it's fun to show off.  I'm a student in college, so I usually just take my laptop to class and rotate my cube a few times.  Everyone around me is instantly amazed.  Lol, it's a great way to meet new people!
