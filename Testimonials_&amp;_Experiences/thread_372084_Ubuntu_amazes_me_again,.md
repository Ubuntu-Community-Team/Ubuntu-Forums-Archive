### Post by SonicSteve on 2007-02-27
Hi everyone,
This time I installed Feisty (latest herd version) on a spare Via chipset/amd computer. I really just wanted to test it out. I pulled the hard drive out and put things away. 
Today an old dell was given to me. It is a P4 with intel 850 chipset. The hard drive was dead so I put the feisty drive in. I didn't even realize that it was that drive but low an behold Ubuntu starts booting.
I then realize what I did and expect a crash. The only thing similar between the two systems above is the video card so I really didn't expect much. However Feisty is running and running well. It didn't skip a beat!!

I am totally amazed. I had a similar experience with Dapper not long ago. However the difference in the systems was not so drastic as this time. Feisty is looking good, and way to go Ubuntu Team.
