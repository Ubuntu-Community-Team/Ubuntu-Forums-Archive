### Post by AMegapode on 2008-02-19
Hello to the Ubuntu community,

Here are the reasons that Ubuntu is better for me. I have a Fujitsu C1410 laptop that I originally put PCLinuxOS 2007 on. While PCLinuxOS is a good distro and very popular,  there were a number of annoying things that never did work right.Here is a list of the things that I never could get to work right in PCLinuxOS.

- Wireless networking never would connect to our enterprise WPA2 network. Had the wireless drivers and WPA supplicant, but never could make it connect.

- Laptop would go into hibernation, but then would never come back out ot it. Would always have to end up restarting.

- Suspend never did work at all.

- Keyboard multimedia keys (vol up/down, brightness up/down) did not work.

Amazingly, after I installed Ubuntu 7.10, I tested all these functions and every last one of them worked wonderfully. I am completely sold on Ubuntu now. And, of course, one of the best things about Ubuntu, is the large and active user community, which PCLInuxOS lacks.

Kudos to all the Ubuntu developers and all the community who work so hard for little or nothing to make Ubuntu what it is.

Sincerely,

AMegapode
Ubuntu 7.10
