### Post by Commander Fluffy on 2010-02-15
I am very new to Linux, and I'm enjoying my Ubuntu experience so far. After installing it, most things seemed to be auto-detected, and it downloaded the correct drivers when I performed an update. Just a shame I'm having issues with the Realtek wireless card, I'm not used to using the Terminal, and when it says sockets are blocked my head starts to hurt.

Aside from that, it's really well layed out and quite shiny, and codecs and drivers on hand. 

In conclusion, a positive experience!
