### Post by ahsya on 2007-10-23
Hi all, I'm a totally new to Linux, and after making such a deep and long comparisons, I decided to try Ubuntu. Fortunately, the Gutsy was just released! Some luck, huh?

First, the installation.
I was kinda confused while dealing with partition. After search-here-there-everywhere, everything seemed to be pretty easy. Dare to Read is the key here :D, and I'm bad on this, actually, hehe...

After doing some setups (localization, etc)... I was just realized that the installation process is soooo simple! And quite efficient, I must say...

Okey, succeed with the installation.

Now into the desktop.. nothing special here... Activate the Compiz-Fusion... NOW THAT'S SOMETHING! :D It really kicked (Vista's) butt!

Tried to install app... nos problemos... playing games, burn some cds, playing mp3s, videos... they are all good!

Well.. the bottom line is, Gutsy really IS for me. And I think I'll spread the virus, too :D

For those who are previously a huge fan of Windows, well, it will takes time to learn Linux, I guarantee you. But hey, if you remember the first time you learned Windows, that's exactly what you have to do with Linux now. Learn, learn, learn... read, read, read... try, try, try.. trust me.. I'm a totally newbie, too...

Good job, Ubuntu team! Ok.. now, install some games... :P
