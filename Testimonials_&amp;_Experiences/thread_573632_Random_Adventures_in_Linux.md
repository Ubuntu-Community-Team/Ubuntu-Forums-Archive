Greetings!

I am a long time Dos and Windows user who has recently been convinced to give Ubuntu a try.  You can follow my trial in a [weekly post on my blog]("http://randomnumbers.us").  My next post will be late Saturday/early Sunday.  

So far my experience in setup and initial use of Ubuntu has been rather positive.  My biggest worry did not materialize, as Ubuntu found and loaded drivers for all of my hardware.

I am posting from work, and don't have time to go into too much detail here, but if you visit my blog you can get some idea of where I am coming from and how I intend to approach my trial of Ubuntu.  I hope that my review and feedback as a regular Windows user might help the Ubuntu community continue improving the product and, should I like it enough, help to "sell" Ubuntu to other users.

I get e-mails of all comments sent to my blog, so if you want to be sure I read what you say, copy your feedback to my postings there.

Wish me luck!
