### Post by Mercdya on 2011-11-02
[INDENT]Hi I Have just recently started using Ubuntu 11.10. I have until now been a Windows user since computers were introduced to me as a child. I just want to say WOW.[/INDENT]

[INDENT]Lol there is more to it than that. I have found that the tasks I really need to be able to perform are easily done on Ubuntu. This is amazing mostly in part because this operating system is completely FREE!!! As is the software used to do the same tasks I do on Windows 7.[/INDENT]

[INDENT]I am so glad I gave it a try. Of course when I first installed it my Windows brainwashed mind Screamed NOOOOOOO!!! I was quick to find all sorts of flaws and slow to give any kind of credit. After truly giving it a try however I am now hard put to find a flaw in it at all even with default settings. It is absolutely free and does all the same things I do on Windows. Yes I did have to do some research to make a few things work that did not after a fresh install but it brought back and renewed the usefulness of a Google search which was becoming more of a habit rather than anything fruitful. I am having so much fun learning how to adjust to something brand new. Yet it has been fairly easy as there is so much support on the subject.[/INDENT]\

[INDENT]In conclusion Ubuntu you :guitar: (Rock) Thank you for renewing my love for computers and my happiness in the pursuit of computer knowledge. I give Ubuntu :KS:KS:KS:KS:KS[/INDENT]
