And trust me.  I've read just about every single post in the archived thread about Vaio owners and their motion eye cams..I need to get it working within the next 3 weeks or I better shape up my Windows partition [what can I say, I love me some Sims2] to go online.  But that's not a testimonial.

All in all, however, there's been lots of learning through the mistakes of others, lots of google, and I'm certainly not giving Ubuntu up.  Because it's just too much fun.  And the whole experience has inspired me to learn a lot about programming so I can build my own damn distro [LFS, anyone?], and everything will work to my own quirky specifications [like foobar2000!].

[And I say "own damn distro" in a positive manner :)]
