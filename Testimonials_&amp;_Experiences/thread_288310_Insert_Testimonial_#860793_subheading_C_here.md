### Post by DJ Wings on 2006-10-29
When I first switched to Linux in 2005, I used Ubuntu 5.10, which my online contacts recommended. I was immediately impressed with how easy it was to use and how much of my hardware worked out of the box with it. I've used various incarnations of it ever since, occasionally switching from Kubuntu to Xubuntu, or back again. At the moment, my laptop uses Xubuntu 6.06, and my gaming machine (affectionately hostnamed "gnu-metal") uses Xubuntu Edgy, because it's fast, easy to use and manage, and flexible. Bring on the Fawn! 8)
