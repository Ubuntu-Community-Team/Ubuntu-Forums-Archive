I am in comparason a young computer user, at only 18 years of age. Here's the thing, I have tried many linux distributions over the past 4 years. Fedora (core 1,3,4) redhat 9, DSL, etc.

But I have NEVER had something work quite as well as a Debian based distribution, and I instantly fell in love with it. I have used both Ubuntu and Kubuntu and have found my style, Kubuntu is on my hard drive for good.

Sure there are bugs, but Windows has bugs, every os has bugs. And as I have seen before quoted by someone else... "Do not even think to install linux if you are not prepared for a learning curve of some sort"

Over all? 9/10 for Kubuntu... It is just me all over and I thank the developers. I hope to join you guys in time
