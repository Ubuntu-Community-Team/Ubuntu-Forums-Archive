### Post by granda on 2011-01-18
On sunday I was chatting with long time friend in Thailand and I shared that I had now switched from Microsoft to Linux.  An IT worker and graphic designer my friend had never seen it or used it but had heard of it.  When I explained that I had tired of compatibility issues with cameras and microsoft's lack of support, and also the community aspect of linux I sent him a screenshot of my my desktop, Wow thats impressive he said.  I suggested he try a live CD of Ubuntu and try it out. 

Got an email this morning, in just 2 days he had gone from a never heard of it, pirated Windows user to enthusiastic linux user with no compatibility problems.  

Just thought I would share this as I know he will in that part of the world.
