### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-07-17
good to hear. mine also works great. here's a little info for people. ANY UPS will technically work with ubuntu. that is, it will keep the computer, or any electrical appliance working until the battery runs out of power. the only question is, will ubuntu recognize the UPS and shutdown the computer *before* the battery runs out. if not, the power will cease and you will lose any unsaved work.
