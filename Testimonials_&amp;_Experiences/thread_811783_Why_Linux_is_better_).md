### Post by kaldor on 2008-05-29
My family has used Windows for years. My father seems to think that the open source community is a huge scam, and refuses to download anything free in case it may be infected with a virus. 

I downloaded Ubuntu, installed it, and used it myself when I needed to. My father would keep rebooting the PC to access windows for the most idiotic reasons: "There's no internet in Linux" or "There is no firewall, running linux will crash our computer"

Our Windows became infected with a trojan that shut down our firewalls, and we had to pay 80 dollars for a Symantec person to fix it. My father asked the guy if I was true in saying that Linux can't get infected as easy as Windows. His reply: "I may lose my job for saying it, but Linux is indeed much safer to use than Windows."

My story on how we started using Ubuntu :)
