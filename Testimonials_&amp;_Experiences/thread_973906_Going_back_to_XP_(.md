### Post by sporkubus on 2008-11-07
What can I say? I gave it my best shot. Ubuntu 8.04 ran stable on my laptop, but there were a few extremely irritating problems. I could never get my touchpad to work correctly; none of the music players I found worked as well as iTunes, even despite its bloat; Office 2007 doesn't run under Linux and OpenOffice just doesn't do it for me; and Flash videos haven't worked well in ages.

Then 8.10 came along, and now, I give up. All the same problems as 8.04, plus random lockups and none of my programs seem to work correctly anymore. As much as I hate Vista, I can't see myself continuing to run Ubuntu with these problems, and I don't have the time or energy to devote to solving them right now. I have posted numerous times on this forum for help, but rarely gotten a satisfactory response.

So goodbye, Ubuntu! Hopefully, we'll meet again someday, when you are a more mature and stable OS.
