### Post by powel212 on 2009-04-17
True story.

I had been using my iPod with iTunes exclusively because I know how proprietary Mac stuff can be so I didn't want to plug it into my Ubuntu box. However, recently after updating iTunes to the current version, iTunes erased my ipod for no apparent reason and then would no longer recognize it as being attached to my system. I rebooted and tried everything I could to try and recover the ipod and eventually gave up. The iPod wouldn't work and all seemed lost.

At this point I stuck the ipod into my Ubuntu box to see if I could salvage the damned thing. Ubuntu found the ipod mounted it synced with Banshee and now the iPod is fine and is happily paired with my Ubuntu system. 

I just wanted to share this story and hope someone else finds it as ridiculously funny as I do.

One more reason to never use anything but Linux.

Powel
