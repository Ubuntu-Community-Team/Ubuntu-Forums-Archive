### Post by armandh on 2007-11-26
for a long time I thought 7.04 was the best BUT now I am convinced that 7.10 is better although not as easy. I am trying the 7.10 live disk on the wife's ever slower low end HP laptop with vista. I discovered that if I enabled restricted driver sources that the wifi needed it came right up and all I had to do was remember the password. the BT wireless mouse was a no-go, but another brand worked as soon as it was turned on. now to find a web cam or drivers for the one I have and then the install. 7.04 just worked. 7.10 works on more stuff, a bit less automatically.
