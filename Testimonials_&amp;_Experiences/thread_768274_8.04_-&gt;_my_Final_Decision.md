where do you mount your partitions? 
if you wish to see them on your desktop mount them on /media
if you wish to keep them on the old location too, just make a bind to /media

fstab line:
```

/old/place    /media/partition   none bind 0 0

```

make sure the bind line is after the partition is mounted
