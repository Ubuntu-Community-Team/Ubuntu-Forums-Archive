### Post by vikramaditya on 2008-06-27
Well, I did it - installed Hardy alongside XP on my 85-year-old Dad's Dell Dimension 8400 desktop box.  We did a guided install from the Live CD, carving out a 20gb chunk from the vast, untapped wilderness of his freshly defragged 160gb HD.  I was somewhat apprehensive about the ATI X800 PCIe graphics card, but everything proceeded flawlessly . . . that is, until the Gnome desktop finished loading and delivered this eerie popup . . .
> Your comb-over is exceedingly offensive.  You must, therefore, be destroyed.
How on Earth did Hardy detect my secret shame???  How could it possibly have obtained a view above and behind the follicularly-challenged region of my heroically-proportioned skull???  Little time was alotted me to ponder this vexing mystery, as the CD-RW drive quickly spooled up to an insane tempo and shot the Live CD like a Frisbee from Hell toward my unprotected neck . . .

Seriously, Hardy now rocks on Dad's machine.  After grabbing the updates from Synaptic and tweaking/installing a few things, I left him honing his reflexes on the addictive "Moon Lander" game.
