### Post by saphil on 2012-05-07
I am using XFCE on Ubuntu Studio.  Aside from changing the desktop picture I am running almost stock. 
What I found using Unity on 11.04 and 11.10 was that I used only a couple of programs over and over. Those I put into the launchbar by clicking on their launchbar icon while the app was running and choose to "keep in launchbar" 
That way most of the high-use apps were easy to find.
I have not heard of a way to set up the "old-fashioned" menu app into unity, but on the other hand, I may not put Unity on my Ubuntu Studio.
