### Post by PhilippHD on 2006-07-10
Hey everybody !
It's now about a week ago, that I downloaded the Live-CD from ubuntu.com and took a little testdrive. From the very first moment I simply loved it. Only two days later I sat down, made backups of my whole data, inserted the Live-CD again and started the installation. To avoid myself from relapsing to Win XP I erased all the partitions instead of organizing a dual-boot system. 
About half an hour later I was finished: My first step into the new OS-world.
I have to admit, this is my third attempt with linux. I tried Knoppix and Slackware before, but ... well ... it just didn't work out.
Later that night I even got xgl/compiz running. You can hardly imagine how proud I was, although I coppied everything from this forum. 
That was four days ago. I still don't have any thoughts about returning to the windows-world. 
Of course, there are some things that still don't work the way they used to on WinXP (like the extra mouse-buttons, sound sometimes, vmware). Over the next 2 months I'll have a lot of free time and I'm pretty optimistic about these minor problems. Maybe I'll even find a way of playing CS on Linux (maybe cedega could help).

If I can be convinced, millions more can be, too !!!
Spread the news: Ubuntu kicks *** !
