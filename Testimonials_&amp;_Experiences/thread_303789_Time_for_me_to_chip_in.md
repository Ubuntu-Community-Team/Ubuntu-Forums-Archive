### Post by stuh84 on 2006-11-20
I've done a lot of complaining on these forums before (mainly due to getting wireless working in Ubuntu among other things), but finally I can say I am loaded on my other PC, with Ubuntu, and happy with how it is.

I've been running 6.06 on it for ages. It was nice, I liked having a pretty brown screen to load up into, and XGL + Compiz made it nice and fun too. Only problem was the PC moved out of my Dad's room and into my own when I got a desk for it. Now it needed to be wireless. Windows 2K had no problems with wireless, but Ubuntu hohoho what a joke it was. Every time I tried something NDISWrapper it just fell apart, so I gave up, did the 30 day trial with Linuxant and thought to meself I'll pay the £10 licence fee at some point.

However I decided my Linux and Ubuntu knowledge had increased tenfold by the time the trial was up, and tried hard to get it working with NDISWrapper, and got right to the last stage, but just could not get the damn thing to get an IP address, so that was that.

Fast forward to yesterday and I was getting interested in Beryl and AIGLX, and thought lets try installing it. Got to later stages and it needed Edgy. This wouldn't be a problem normally, but we are on a 2GB a month bandwidth limit, and to knock out a 550mb upgrade in an hour would be senseless. So, I get me dad to download 6.10 at work.

I got rid of 6.06 because I saw so many people having upgrade problems, and expected to have more with this.

Here's where the story takes a turn for the insanely good. I install Edgy (after manually configuring the partitions, wouldn't do the use largest free space option for some reason), and booted it all up. So far so good, no major problems. I proceeded to start installing Beryl and thought hmm the Network stuff looks slightly different, lets test it out. Put in the SSID, WEP key and everything.......wait a second.....THATS AN IP ADDRESS! Firefox.....IT WORKS! IT BLOODY WORKS! No NDISWrapper, no Linuxant, no half baked solutions, just put in the information, and my ACX111 based PCI wireless just WORKS!

I carried on with the Beryl and AIGLX install, and it all worked slightly flawlessly (was having a flickering title bar problem, restarted the PC, never saw it happen again), and now, I have a Desktop PC, booting into Ubuntu, and it's exactly how I would want it to be. Well okay, once I have no bandwidth limit Synaptic is gonna get worn out.......but other than that its great.

Well done Ubuntu, well done Canonical, well done all the coders, and well done to all involved in Ubuntu. Finally, a Distro that just works with everything I've got.

Now if I could just get the wireless on my laptop consistently working in 6.06...........
