### Post by LibertyShadow on 2008-11-11
I only know eleven Ubuntu users at my college, and I am responsible for getting two of them into it, and one of them *back* into it.

I put on my Ubuntu sweatshirt today ... and in just three hours I met two more users!  The first one was at breakfast. Yesterday, I passed this person in the hall of one of the academic buildings with my friend when we were talking Linux, and he asked if we heard of open-source hardware and he told us about Via's Openbook. Today I saw him with an Inspiron e1505 running Ubuntu.  I was late to work so I briefly introduced myself, and I let him know that he's not alone.   The second was at work at the help desk.  Someone came in with a delivery of printer paper and saw my sweatshirt and told me that Ubuntu brought one of his old P3 computers back to life.  Unfortunately, I didn't have a long time to talk to him either because my shift ended and I had places to go.  

Anyway, I thought that was kind of cool, and that I should share it with the community.  I know it doesn't sound like much but I go to a *liberal arts school*.  There are so many macs!
