### Post by patchin_house on 2008-10-17
Just in time for 8.10 (and waiting patiently for the launch), I decided to see if my HP Pavilion simply has a problem with anything Gnome. So it is now running Kubuntu (yes, I went KDE), and so far that seems to make the difference. 

I admit that Adept was a bit of a change (having been used to Synaptic), and it'll be interesting to see how things go when the switches are made to Ibex and KDE 4. But I am happy to see that I can watch full-motion video using VLC, and both audio and video playback are working smoothly. 

This has led me to contemplate getting my Sony VAIO laptop to run the K as well. While it doesn't have to deal with the resticted video card driver issue my nVidia-running HP desktop faces, I am curious to see how better a performer it would be after the switch.

I'll post again when I know.
