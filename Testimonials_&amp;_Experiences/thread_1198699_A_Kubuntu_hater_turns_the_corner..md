### Post by swoll1980 on 2009-06-27
I've been using Kubuntu 9.04 for 7 days now, and I have to say I really like it. I've absolutely hated Kubuntu since I started using Linux over two years ago, but I have always given it the benefit of the doubt, and tried every new release, but after 30 minutes, or so, I would be like "wtf were they thinking?" and switch back. 

It always seemed ridiculously buggy, even in the KDE 3.5 days. I would have windows vanish into no where, programs would start to launch, show up in the task bar, but never open, random crashes, of different programs, strange graphical glitches, and weird stuff like that.I always liked the concept of KDE 4x, but it was unfinished, and had some general flakyness.

 Everything seems to have come together pretty well now. I'm not having any problems with any of the stuff I mentioned earlier. Everything seems really smooth, and acts like it belongs together. I like the KDE apps they look nice, and have a nice flow about them. I hate the kickoff menu, but it is easily replaced by Lancelot, which is a lot like the Mint Menu. I like how kwin has the composting, and effects built in. The effects used to be really choppy, but they have got it running very nicley now.

All, and all I think it's great, and believe those guys at KDE, and Kubuntu deserve some kudos. I don't know if I'll be using it forever, but for the time being Kubuntu has won the 'buntu battle in my house. At least until Karmic is released.
