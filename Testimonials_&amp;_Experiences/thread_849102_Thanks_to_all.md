### Post by GolanTrevize on 2008-07-04
Hi - last time I wrote, I was pretty pissed on the way Feisty was developing. 

This tiime I want to do exactly the opposite. My harddisc died a few days ago and I grabbed the opportunity to install Hardy, because in case it would not suite, it could go back.

I did not. I'm still running Hardy. OK - there are some minor nuisances; my WLAN-LED does not flicker anymore. The connection to my coolpix2000 still does not work. It sometimes gets hard to connect to my WLAN - especially not seeing the LED flicker. After suspend I have to switch manually to my X-console. 

But so far these are either already known issues (my camera) or minor nuisances.

Taking a look at all the vast rest of the system, I am really enthusiastic on the way my laptop is now.

Thank you, to all the people investing their time into hardy development. This really is a great job. 
I got my system running within two hours (also because of my backup) completely - try this with windows :) 

The visual effects are really nice - and they work out of the box.
Every external hardware, that worked with gutsy still seems to work.

All in all - really great work!!! - or regarding the late posts of my ranting thread: **For me** this is a really great work :)

Thanks  a lot

Golan
