### Post by maxxym on 2007-10-18
Well... day 5 with Linux. All I have to say is... Why would ANYONE want to use Vista?? WHY? There is NO WAY in hell that I am going back to windows. 

I was running Kubuntu on my laptop and home desktop, mostly because I thought it had more eye candy than Gnome..I liked it, but there was something missing.. I dont' know what, but something wasn't right and in the back of my mind, I was trying to convence myself to stick with it instead of switching back to Windows. 

Well... finally after waiting long time last night, I got my copy of Gutsy. Wiht nothing to loose since my OS install was still kind of fresh, I got the CD burned and did the install.

Right from the moment the logon screen came up, I got the "OOHHHHHH" feeling. It just looks really nice...so I got this thing to boot up... adjusted my fonts and got the Desktop Effects to work (which are ABSOLUTELY AWESOME)...

Gutsy found everything...no hardware problems with little hickup with ATI card.. fair enough...I clicked on a little info icon and it told me to click next to download the drivers.. so I did...3 minutes later... boom, drivers installed.

I have to say, I was very set on KDE, but now, I am absolute Gnome addict. This OS is awesome. Everything works, it boots up SUPER fast for me....fonts are sharp and crisp....it's easy to use... with enough eye candy to please me, but not too much as KDE to make everything confusing. 

I couldn't understand before when people were saying that Ubuntu is much better than Microsoft OS, but now I do. Tell me why did I waste my time with XP? Why?

I love Ubuntu.. thank you for all those developers who made this thing happen.
