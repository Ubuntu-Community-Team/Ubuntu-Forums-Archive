I wanted to install Ubuntu on my Acer Travelmate 723TX but had no space on the 20GB HDD. So I decided to use my 10 GB HDD but since both the CDRom and Floppy drive on the lappie are broken, I had a dilemma. This is how I did it.
I copied the CD to the XP (20GB) drive. Installed Grub and pointed it to the vmlinuz and initrd files in /install directory. Once I had the installation started, I removed the 20 gb hdd and inserted the 10 GB hdd. After a few tries, it worked. Took about an hour to install. Reason for not booting with the 10 GB: blank disk ie no OS installed. Oh yeah: I ran the install from a Samsung DVD Combo in an external USB case.
