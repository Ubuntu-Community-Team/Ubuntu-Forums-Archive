I was using it for a very simple Task, which is to fetch my e-mail through IMAP and move E-Mails to the Junk-Folder that have X-Spam-Flag: Yes

But some things are kind of confusing: (I did not put effort to research solutions, because user-friendliness means that users can work with the program easily. )
[LIST]
[*]I wasn't able to find the place where I change the subscribed folders
[*]When Items are moved to Trash or Junk it is not shown whether they are unread or not. Very annoying especially with Junk-Mails
[*]When creating Filters there is no way to to move an E-Mail with X-Spam-Flag to the Spam-Folder. This has to be done through Mail-Preferences -> Junk -> Custom Headers
[*]When marking E-Mails in an IMAP Folder as Junk, in Evolution they are removed from that Folder and shown in the Junk Folder, while in reality (Webmail) they are still in INBOX. I wasn't able to quickly find a solution for that.
[*]When I want to add a Field to the E-Mail list, like Date Received, it takes me 6 steps.
[*]You can't use Shift + Delete to permanent delete E-Mails directly
[*]Under Trash you are unable to delete certain E-Mails permantly by using Delete, you have to empty all the Trash
[*]The Junk Options in the General E-Mail Preferences and the individual Account Preferences are not very obvious
[*]When an E-Mail is shorter than the message-preview pane than grey space is shown below the white e-mail field
[*]Whenever I change the IMAP account options the options are saved but evolution crashed Immediately. Couldn't find anything quickly when googled it.
