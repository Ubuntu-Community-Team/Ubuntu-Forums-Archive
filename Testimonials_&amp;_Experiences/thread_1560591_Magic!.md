### Post by bwang on 2010-08-25
I've been using Linux for around 3 years now, and I recently purchased an MSI Wind U135 netbook and decided to install Ubuntu on it.
Being a relatively recent netbook, I thought I would have to compile a zillion drivers from source and fiddle with it for a while to get all my hardware working, this being my experience with Linux and laptops in the past.
Instead, I was surprised and happy to find ALL of my hardware up and running - Webcam, Sound, WiFi, Fn Hotkeys, suspend, hibernate, etc. Never before have I had such a smooth experience with Linux on a laptop!
I must say, despite having its downsides (mainly bulk), Ubuntu is an amazing distro in terms of user-friendliness and hardware support.
