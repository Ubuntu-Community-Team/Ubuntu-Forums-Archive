### Post by Nightstrike2009 on 2011-05-18
Hiya everybody,

I am sad to say in light of Ubuntu 11.04 and the "Unity" issue, I too am going to leave Ubuntu for good.

Back when I started to learn linux (Ubuntu 8.04)I was attracted to its "Community Spirit" and the Benevolence of Linus Torvalds and Richard Stallman (and the wider linux community) and yearned to kick Windows in touch and join you all in the Linux World.  

I wish to thank Ubuntu for making it easier for me to learn Linux via experience and I was an "Ubuntu fanboy" until now. 

At the time I joined these forums many "pro users" were leaving Ubuntu for other distros and I didn't understand why and vowed to help others as I progressed with what I picked up on the way, I now understand their flustrations with Ubuntu perfectly and join them in leaving this once great distro.

The general ubuntu bad Implementation of almost all its new ideas (then binning them halfway through) like "wingadgets" and User interfaces in the past (Ubuntu Netbook Remix) followed recent turn of events and Canonicals ignorance of the community that warned them of the "Unity" error, which they still did anyhow. 

I would have liked to have seen a distro that was reliable and stable and not so full of "experimental prototypes" (that so often are never full implemented and are at best "half cocked" before they are binned to be replaced by yet another stupid idea, In my own opinion).

Nowadays I don't want to be associated with a distro that so blatantly ignores its users wishes and seemingly is seeking to become more like Windows (or Apple Mac to be more precise) instead of less like it sickens me. 

So I bid you all farewell, I hope Ubuntu's owners see sense before its user-base all migrates to pastures new (theres always Debian or Linux Mint, or the others if that does happen) 

The (Ubuntu) ride was a blast but has every great journey comes to an end its time for me to change direction, to a distro that suits me better (and actually listens to its users). I will never use Ubuntu main distro ever again even if "Unity" is canned, I like so many others are not amused, not amused at all!:(

Thanks to all who helped me on the forums and I hope I also helped some of you in return to the best of my abilities, farewell to you all. :-(

**PS:** Its so ironic something called "Unity" has created so much "Dis-unity" in the Ubuntu Community. I have tried "Unity" in ubuntu 11.04 beta 1 and in my own opinion "Un-usable garbage" would be putting it politely.

**PPS:** This "Unity" error has probably sent less able newcomers straight back to WindowsXP, if Ubuntu wants to become "another Microsoft" it should not be surprised if Linux newcomers go straight back to the "devil they know"
