### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-01-24
10.04 LTS is supported until April 2013. I suggest reinstalling it. 

I have used Xfce desktop for ages now and Unity doesn't suit me anyhow but it doesn't affect me. You might want to give it a try. Available in Synaptics, just search for and install Xfce4 (NOT Xubuntu desktop!), log out, choose 'Xfce Session' (from memory) from the 'Sessions' pop-up, log in again. You're there. 

You can tweak it to act pretty much like the Gnome you are used to. Xfce (and Xubuntu consequently) are not going anywhere and Xubuntu would no longer be Xubuntu if it started using Unity or anything else as the standard DE.
