### Post by grunge09 on 2007-05-24
I have been using Bill Gate's OS's since before Windows 3.1.  have been building computers for about 16 years.  So I know my way around a PC pretty well.   I have been looking at Ubunti since 6.06 LTS.  I had that installed, then messed with the 64 bit 6.10 Edgy.  that was a slight speed boost, but some things did not have 64 bit support.  So whwn Feisty came out, I got the ISO, made a CD, and booted it, everything appeared to work, so I installed it (32 bit version) and had no problems.  I was able to see my XP PC and after sharing the XP Drive on the network was easily able to copy all my data over to Feisty.   I have not touched the XP pc for a week nowk, and soley using Feisty.  

I got flash installed (after figuring that NO SCRIPT in Firefox was preventing Flash from working).  I installed Automatix, for the free codecs.  Can't do encrypted DVD's thanks to Bill Gates that Patent freak that he is.   But that is what the DVD player on the DV is for huh.  Got wine installed, ATI FGLRX Driver installed,   Got Pan Newseader up and running, Got e-mail transferred over to Thunderbird 2.0.  Got Hotmail working in T-Bird.   Copied by bookmarks all over, 

I like that if you hold the mouse over a mp3 file it auto plays it.   I had beryl running on 6.10, but that wore itself out fast,  I left desktop effects off for now.  I installed UT2004 and the updates for it.   So far I do not see anything that I need XP for.  

I am NO LONGER a slave to Bill, I am FREE, thanks to Canocal Ltd for Ubuntu.  You guys deserve mad props.
