### Post by svenbertil on 2009-05-30
I realize this is not really something to start a new thread over but I have to say this somewhere.

I had heard that using 3g/gsm usb modems would be easier in Jaunty but its better than that. It could not be better.

I plugged in my modem, a windows pops up saying something to the effect of: "oh hi, I noticed you've just plugged in a gsm modem. Whats your ISP?" My ISP was in the list so I clicked it. I then click my ISP name in network manager, it asks my pincode and I'm online.

Great work!
