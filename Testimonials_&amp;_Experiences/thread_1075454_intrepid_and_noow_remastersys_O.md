Now one of the things that slows or stops me swapping and changing is the fact thast my OS works and does everything I want/need it to. I am so bloody impressed!!!!!! the iso is just short of 1GB I believe this is dependent on what comprises your OS how you instruct remastersys to do the job. The Linux community rocks big time!!!!!!!!!!! Also the create a USB startup disk in intrepid works perfectly with the latest version of remastersys.
