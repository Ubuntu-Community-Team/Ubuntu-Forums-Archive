### Post by youngde on 2007-02-03
So after using Ubuntu for about three months on my "ancient" Sony Vaio, I couldn't be more satisfied. I installed Edgy, with some trepidation, on my pcg-fx140 and it mostly Just Worked; the only issue was my netgear wireless card, but even that worked after I compiled and installed the latest ndiswrapper release candidate.

My little Vaio is a 2001 model; 700 MHz with just 256mb ram. I've used Linux a lot over the years (mostly RedHat), but never on this notebook. Indeed, I fully expected lots of trouble given the horror stories I'd heard about Linux on notebooks. All my fears were unfounded. Simply amazing. Ubuntu is the finest Linux distribution I've ever used.

My hat's off to the Ubuntu developer and testing community for a job well done.

david.
