### Post by GoldNugget on 2009-11-11
I'm sorry to hear of all these problems with Karmic. I installed it on 5 computers: my Intel desktop which was an upgrade, clean kubuntu and xubuntu on an older amd with an invidia card and two clean installs on eeepcs, 701 and 900 (regular Ubuntu, not UNR)

All four of the clean installs were perfect. The upgrade had a minor glitch, couldn't enable direct rendering so no Compiz. After fiddling with it for awhile, I found the older Intel driver in the repositories and replaced the default driver. Everything works perfectly now. 

I am loving Karmic. Its fast, stable and loaded with new goodies and upgrades. There are updates and bug fixes coming in nearly every day so wait a month or two if you are worried about stability. 

Thanks Ubuntu and Deb developers.
