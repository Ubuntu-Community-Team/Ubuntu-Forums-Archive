### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-06-14
One year ago today I joined this forum. Since I joined this forum I have discovered so many new things about Ubuntu. During this time I have also helped other people with their Ubuntu problems. I have to say that the process of learning about Ubuntu and also helping others with it has made me a better Linux user.

Before I started using Ubuntu one year ago I was what you would call a "Windows fanboy". I wouldn't be surprise if I had said "Heil Microsoft" back in those days. As you can tell, this is now the complete opposite of what I am today. 

My experience with Ubuntu started with me going to the local public library and trying it out on their computers. I then became familiar with Ubuntu. Soon after, my computer's Windows install got corrupted, and I decided to try something new, fast, and free. Once I had it installed I started loving every minute I used it.

Keep it up Canonical and the whole Ubuntu community, you are what makes Ubuntu special! I am proud to call myself part of this community.
