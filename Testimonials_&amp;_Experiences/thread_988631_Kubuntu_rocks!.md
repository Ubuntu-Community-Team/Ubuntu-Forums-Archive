### Post by bd@cb8be8510 on 2008-11-20
I installed Kubuntu 8.10 on another partition of my 8 years old computer. Kubuntu installed fine. KDE has a very professional look. It looks as if Kubuntu is running faster on my oldie than Ubuntu. Even the speed of the network is higher :confused::confused: On Ubuntu I only obtain an average speed of 130 Kb/s (wireless-lan with USB-adapter), with Kubuntu a constant speed of 168 KB/s is displayed. 

Adept has a very nice interface. Anyhow I'll start working with Kubuntu for a while (after 2 years working with Ubuntu). That's freedom ... made possible by Ubuntu ... Linux.

I have one complaint. At booting up there are 2 Ubuntu's 8.10 listed up,
one Ubuntu 8.10 (Auto Magic) and one Ubuntu 8.10 on /dev/sda1/ ... I expected to see Kubuntu 8.10 and Ubuntu 8.10:lolflag:

Either Kubuntu, either Ubuntu, there both wonderful!
