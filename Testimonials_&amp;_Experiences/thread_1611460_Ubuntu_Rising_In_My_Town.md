### Post by 1roxtar on 2010-11-01
This week I fixed another borked Windows pc, but as I was gonna reinstall the OS, I decided to try putting Ubuntu on it instead.  I called my customer up and explained that I wanted him to try something new.  If he didn't like it, I'd reinstall Windows XP again.  I explained XP's end of life coming fast and the freedom and security of Linux.  He agreed to give it a try.

I invited him over to my house and fired up his computer for him to check out.  I started with the Ubuntu website, then moved on to each category....Applications, Places, System and then highlighted the Software Center (Think App Store...I told him...only with free software....Cooool was his reaction).  After going over it.....he was quickly impressed, especially over the start up and shut down speeds.

As we were done with the demonstration, he was like "Hell yeah!  I'm KEEPING that!"  My next step is my favorite part of all....peeling off the Windows XP sticker and replacing it with a shiny "Powered by Ubuntu" sticker.  In the past year that I have installed Ubuntu on computers, I have not had a single person ask me to reinstall Windows back.  I live in a small town of 9000 and I follow up with my Ubuntu converts and everything has been going great....no complaints.
