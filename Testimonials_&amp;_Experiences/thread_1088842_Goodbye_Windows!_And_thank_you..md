### Post by daniel014 on 2009-03-06
I would just like to thank everyone who contributes to build the best operating system ever!

I am 14 and everything I had known about computers had always been about Windows. It was the only system we had at home and school so I never knew about the joys of Linux.

About 5 months ago I started playing around with Linux. First, I tried Slax on my USB drive. I couldn't find any simple way of doing things like installing programs, so I gave up on the idea.

A few weeks later, I saw a TV program called Click ([http://www.bbc.co.uk/click/]("http://www.bbc.co.uk/click/")) where they were trying out cheaper alternatives of software. Ubuntu was mentioned. I tested a Wubi on my dad's old laptop, and WOW!

So now here I am with my brand new MSI Wind U100 - my first computer. It runs Ubuntu beautifully and I can happily do the school work, Facebook, MSN, music and everything else without the need to boot back into Win. Ubuntu has become my operating of choice and increased my productivity.

I'd just like to say thanks to everyone here, making Ubuntu amazing. Windows won't rule the world for much longer. :)
