### Post by Lunx on 2009-10-04
Thinking just now that when I first discovered Linux a whole ten months ago, there were quite a few issues people had with fonts and how they rendered etc. etc (particularly Intrepid and earlier releases). I never had a problem once I figured a few settings, but must admit certain fonts were particularly crappy. I upgraded to Jaunty and noticed straight away they looked better, now on Karmic and must admit stuff looks even better!!!

Actually, it's not just fonts, everything seems to look smarter now (more crisp looking is only way I can describe it), including video playback. And sound???, 1000% more betterer  now.

Thanks Linux, thanks Ubuntu, and thanks community.
