### Post by leathco on 2008-01-29
I recently migrated from 7.04, and I love the small improvements so far.  For one, my screen resolution on my laptop was correctly set upon install this time.  I have a widescreen, and last time it set the resolution at 800x600 instead of the native 1200x800.  Not a big issue, just one other thing I had to fix.  It autodetected great in the new 7.10 though.

Also, I no longer have to work to get my wireless up and running!  It automatically guided me through setting up the restricted driver for it without me going to Dell for my HP wireless driver and manually configuring a wrapper.  It simply just worked after a few guided clicks.

I'm sure I'll discover other upgrades later, but for now I just wanted to thank the makers for a great distro that continually improves with every release.  I worked with 7.04 for a while, but I love the steps forward 7.10 made and kind of wonder how much easier the next release will be.  Maybe I finally won't have to put in the /nosplash /noapic /nolapic commands when setting up the install.

Thanks again!
