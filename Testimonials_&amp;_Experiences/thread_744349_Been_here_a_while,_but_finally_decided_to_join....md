### Post by DK-420 on 2008-04-03
Hello all!

I am not-so new to Ubuntu Linux, but still an inexperienced Linux user in general. I have been coming to these forums for a while now, but finally decided to make a new move and joined. 

The Ubuntu Forums has been a great place to come to and get good tips and info in general for all my questions and trials I have gone through trying to learn this wonderful OS.

I have been using Windows for most of my IT life. I am a MSCE and also a Certified Internet Webmaster.... both certifications and $1.50 will get me a cup of coffee.

Anyhow, I started using Linux with the release of Mandrake 7.0; loved it too. However I started using Windows more on a regular basis and got my certs as I have mentioned above. Then a few years later, I decided to give Linux another try with Ubuntu 6.06. Loved the responsiveness of the OS and how much it has grown since I had used it last. I've used PCLOS2007, both official and Gnome versions, Mandriva 2008, Fedora, and many others, but I have found Ubuntu to be the best for my needs and wants. Right now I am using 7.10 on my laptop exclusively, but looking forward to the Hardy Heron release. I've also got my kids into it on their laptops because of the great security and responsiveness on their older machine. Now I am trying to get my wife to take the plunge and run it on our desktop and get away from the Microsoft monopoly.

Anyhow, sorry to have made such a long post, but basically wanted to say hello and thanks for a great OS and great community!

Take care,
DK-420
