### Post by yankeeboy254 on 2010-05-31
I was born September 20th. A great day. So, last year, I was really getting into computers. Customizing Windows and whatnot. Custom themes, editing the registry etc. So, I decided to get a laptop for my birthday. I really wanted one. So, my parents paid for half of my HP HDX16T. Core 2 Duo T6600 @ 2.2GHz, 4GB of DDR2 RAM @ 800MHz and an NVIDIA 9600MGT. One problem. It came with Windows Vista. But, I got a free upgrade to Windows 7! I was loved Windows 7 when I got it, and loved the themes and speed. Then, I read on an online forum about Linux, Ubuntu in particular. After a lot of testing and worrying, I finally got it installed. The rest is history. 

I've been trying to convert my friends to Ubuntu, but it has been a tough sell. Some think Ubuntu is some kind of virus, or just don't need it. I'll keep trying though.
Ubuntu Forever.
