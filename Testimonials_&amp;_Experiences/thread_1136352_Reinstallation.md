### Post by linuxuser21 on 2009-04-24
I was using 9.04 beta and Ubuntu had crashed many times (still does).  Upon updating my system, Ubuntu suddenly crashed and I was unable to login.  I was very unhappy & a lot of cussing and yelling could be heard from my room at around 3:00am.   I knew this meant that a fresh install was the only way (I knew of) to fix it.  Very angry, I gave up for the day and decided to deal with it the next day.  After school, I turned on my machine and reinstalled everything.  When the installation was successful, I rebooted my system.  Expecting to redo all the work that was lost, I was surprised to see my previous wallpaper.  Everything was right where it was.  What is this?  Ubuntu, you are making this too easy.  THANK YOU!
