### Post by gerowen on 2010-11-29
I just noticed something.  I've had this laptop for a couple of years now and it's gone through several versions of Ubuntu.  I seem to remember in older versions that 1 GB or more of my 4 GB of RAM would constantly be in use, even when I was just idling.  Since upgrading to 10.10, with nothing but Firefox and the standard background stuff going, it's only using ~550 MB of RAM.  This is pretty sweet.  I would have assumed that by adding stuff like the built in gwibber applet and stuff that the amount of memory usage would have increased.  Good job Ubuntu team!
