### Post by john_c on 2008-01-21
I have been using Ubuntu since the first distribution was announced.  At the time I found it good, certainly good enough to run as my only computer software for personal and small business use.  In a very few cases I had to look for solutions that were not obvious but overall I have had few complaints.

I have been using Dapper for some time now and dual booted it with Gutsy recently, just out of curiosity.  In a few days of using Gutsy, I abandoned Dapper.  I am amazed at all of the small fixes and improvements that have been made.  A lot of minor annoyances are gone and all of my hardware "just works".

The developers and the community are doing an amazing job and I am posting this to thank them.

John.
