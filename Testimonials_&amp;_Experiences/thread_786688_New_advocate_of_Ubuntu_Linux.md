### Post by CBrendan on 2008-05-08
Hi Everyone

I have just began a exciting journey in the world of Linux and I'm proud to say I have chosen Ubuntu as my distro of choice.  I have, for long time now, wanted to take the leap of faith into the Linux arena and I finally have.  

Being involved in the I.T. industry for almost three years I found myself wanting more of a challenge, something to get excited about and, most importantly, be part of something significant and Ubuntu together with it's community have provided that opportunity.

I started out by trying a distro called Damn Small Linux (DSL) in VMWare and learned the basics of the terminal, compiling and installing software from source and other interesting aspects associated with Linux.

I have researched which distro would be best for me and made the decision to use and "try to" master the Ubuntu distro.  Other options at the time where Mandriva 2008 and Gentoo.

After everything I am proud to be a Ubuntu user and in time be associated with the Ubuntu community.  For now I'm a Hardy boy and I have read reviews of the new LTS version not living up to expectations but when all is said and done it doesn't matter if you're a Dapper dude or a Hardy boy, Ubuntu still surpasses Windows and many other distro's out there.

Your new Ubuntu advocate
Brendan C
