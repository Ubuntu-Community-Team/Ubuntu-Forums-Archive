### Post by nmaster on 2010-08-22
I was a bit worried, since I had read that the webcam didn't work well.  However, everything worked fine for me (apparently a recent update fixed the issue).  

I got two-finger scrolling working ([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1488835](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1488835)) but I still need to do some tinkering to get the pinch zoom in/out.  This isn't critical to me, but it would be nice.  I haven't seen any obvious ways of doing it so I might need to spend some time looking into the documentation.

In short, I love it :)  Everything worked great out of the box. I don't mean to advertise for any corporation but if you're interested, I got it from Office Depot ([http://www.officedepot.com/a/products/492468/Lenovo-ThinkPad-Edge-14-LED-Backlit/#reviewTab](http://www.officedepot.com/a/products/492468/Lenovo-ThinkPad-Edge-14-LED-Backlit/#reviewTab)).
