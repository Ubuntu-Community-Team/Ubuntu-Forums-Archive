One day after getting about 20 BSoDs and 100 "Com surrogate has stopped working" errors, I decided to put in a yellow CD with 4 young people on the CD cover smiling at me. I went along, got it installed and rebooted. I was disappointed when my fingerprint reader didn't work, until I came here. Thanks to  a thread, I got everything set up. I didn't sleep well for about a week.. I worked harder and harder and finally, got my box set-up. I came back to my college after vacations. Thanks for makin me feel free to do anything with my computer.. ANYTHING. The power of independence :)
