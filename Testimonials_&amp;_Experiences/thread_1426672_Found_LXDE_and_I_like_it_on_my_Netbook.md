### Post by uRock on 2010-03-10
I debated trying Xubuntu, but people have been saying that it isn't much lighter because it uses a lot of the gnome libraries and such. I finally searched for LXDE in Synaptic on a VBox and like it enough to install it on the Netbook. It idles at 100MB less RAM than Ubuntu did. It doesn't have the GUI tweaks that gnome has, but I am guessing that is why it runs lighter.
