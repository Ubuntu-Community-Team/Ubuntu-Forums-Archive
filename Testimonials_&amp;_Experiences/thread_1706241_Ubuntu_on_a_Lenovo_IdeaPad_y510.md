### Post by KaiSkylerBliss on 2011-03-13
I am starting to really like Ubuntu 10.10. I have been fiddling around with it for a couple days an my laptop and I am starting to get used to it and I think I am going to stick with it for a long time. I like how I can customize it in anyway possible and how most of the drivers just work and also it's ease of use. It's fast and speedy and boots up in less then 15 seconds, from POST to being able to use it. I am the advanced computer user, I don't usually do any command line work unless it is for OpeniBoot for my iPad. There is only one issue I have found, the sound drivers for the surround sound on this laptop doesn't work but I an live with that. I've been digging the ext4 filesystem and also how the ownership and properties of files work, in my opinion, Ubuntu is really secure. I got this laptop from one of my family members, and Windows was chock full of viruses, so I decided to wipe the disk and install Ubuntu and it works like a champ! Thank you so much 
Canonical for this wonderful and open source operating system!  :D :D
