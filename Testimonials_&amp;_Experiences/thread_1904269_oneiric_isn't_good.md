If the graphics card is 80° hot it is no wonder that the fans are running constantly. What graphics drivers are you using? In my experience, with the open source drivers installed, the GPU tends to get hotter than with the proprietary drivers from the manufacturer. But I haven't checked whether proprietary drivers are available for your graphics card.
A lighter distribution, for example Xubuntu and Lubuntu, with reduced graphical effects could also make the GPU run cooler.
