### Post by armandh on 2011-01-13
Since ubuntu mostly provides its own hardware drivers, 

the latest and greatest may not work yet,
the older and obscure may never work, yet there are finds.

the local micro center sends out their fliers
and there is an off lease refurbished section for the budget minded.

anything $99.99 gets my interest [reduced from &129.99]

HP DX5150
AMD Athlon 64 3200 
XP Professional 
512MB DDR RAM 
40GB SATA Hard Drive 
CD-ROM Drive 
ATi Radeon Xpress 200 [svga & DVI out]
10/100/1000 Network 

so I reduced the XP-SP3-32 partition and added 10.04 Ubuntu 64
everything worked OOTB.
then rummaging in the left over parts 
I upgraded to a DVD and 1 Gb of mem. found a 250 GB sata Hdd too

a few software  bits [medibuntu, VLC, compiz manager]
and it is a twirling cube media player.

so I popped the buttons from the front bezel, scraped off the XP, vista ready stickers 
and sprayed it black to match the rest of the case.

OOPS forgot the flash, 
oh well if I can't sort it out in 64 bit I can always triple boot 32 bit ubuntu

Overall a great cheap ubuntu ready box
