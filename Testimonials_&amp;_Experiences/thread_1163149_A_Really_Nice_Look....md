### Post by CJ56 on 2009-05-18
Well, after two or more years of Ubuntu, I'm now onto 9.04 Jaunty 64-bit...

Like all its predecessors, it's a very nice step forward in so many ways - speed, ease of use, dependability and so on. And in particular, looks: I'm using New Wave (Dark Menus) plus Human icons and this is the first time I've just gone straight for one of the standard options, no hunting around on Gnome-Look or whatever. 

Although it may not seem much, I think the attention paid to New Wave (plus those new integrated notifications) makes a lot of difference - Ubuntu now looks straight out of the box modern, professional, a little bit classy. It adds to the maturity of the whole OS and might even sell it that bit better to anyone new to GNU/Linux. 

So a big hand to Debian, the good folks at Canonical, and all the developers who helped produce such a great (and now, good-looking) system. 

Thanks for everything...
