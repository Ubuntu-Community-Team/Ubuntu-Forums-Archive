### Post by vikigal on 2009-01-18
I have had this computer since 2000. It is a 5000 series w/Intel Celeron Processor, 800mhz, 128kb ram w/L2 pipeline burst cache direct agp graphics and 20 GB HD. It had ME on it and I had tweaked it till it was great for a number of years. When support was pulled I still babied it along until I finally replaced it last winter. My baby went in the closet. I replaced it with a special-ordered ubuntu machine. I wanted as far from windows as I could get.

I am writing this on my baby. I just loaded xubuntu with absolutely no problems what-so-ever. I downloaded the iso, burned it to disc, popped it in and let er rip, so to speak.

WOW! My son's gf has one just like it that as been parked as it has lost drivers, The shop can't repair it any more. It is acting just as mine did, so I am now going to ask a question or two in the forum and try hers.

Thank you to everyone here as I did a lot of reading before actually trying and thank you to every body in the world who has brought things to where even I can keep a computer or two out of the landfills.
