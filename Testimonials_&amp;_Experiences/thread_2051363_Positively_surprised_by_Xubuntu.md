### Post by germanix on 2012-09-01
I had been using Ubuntu as my sole OS (Gnome 2) since version 8.04. I loved gnome 2. Unity/gnome 3 is ok but not really to my liking. So a few days ago I decided to try something different and installed Xubuntu running xfce.
Well, what a nice surprise! 
Runs great on my 8 years old Sony Viao (single core 1.6, 2 GB Ram. Start-up is 45 seconds (Unity 60 seconds), everything worked out of the box, it looks good and i can further fine tune it to my hearts desire.
I am now a new fan of  Xubuntu.
A big thank you to all of the developers. Great job all! :lolflag:
