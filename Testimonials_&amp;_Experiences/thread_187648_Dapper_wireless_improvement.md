### Post by Titus A Duxass on 2006-06-03
Just like to say that Dapper now recognizes my EDIMAX EW-7126 (RTL 8180 Chipset) card and it is configureable through System => Administration => Networking.

The only glitch is that I have to shut down the pooter, remove the card, reboot, shutdown, replace card, reboot before the card activates.  I think this is a problem with my card and router combination.

I have to repeat this procedure if the pooter is shutdown and the power is disconnected, if the power stays connected everything is okay.

Needless to say this is a positive step forward.

I am now install kubuntu on an old Fujitsu-Siemens Trend with a DWL-510 card.
