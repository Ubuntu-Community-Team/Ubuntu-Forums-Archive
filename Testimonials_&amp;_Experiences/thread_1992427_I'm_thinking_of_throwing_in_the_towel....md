### Post by Dngrsone on 2012-05-31
What kind of Periodic Maintenance product do you sell?

Every machine should be given the once-over at least every year-- blow out the dust, make sure all the fans are working, etc.

There are things you can do on the OS side as well-- periodic backups, clearing out old logs, making sure all the updates are in place... it isn't quite as necessary as PMs on mechanical stuff, nor as obvious, but having such a service might help your bottom-line.

If the customers hear from you every once-in-a-while, or see you coming through to make sure everything tip-top, they might say, "oh, since you are here, we need training on this..." or "Hey, we could use a little help with this system."

Just a thought.
