### Post by paul.r.stoner on 2007-09-27
I have been in love with Ubuntu since I first found it a few years ago.  Last year I made the full switch from Windows to Ubuntu and found that everything I could do with Windows, I can do with Ubuntu but better!  I have gotten friends and even family to switch over to Ubuntu, again with great success.  

But here is the real reason for this post....

My mother works for a Catholic school system here in the state of New York.  Because these schools are private, it costs money to maintain licenses for Windows (even though they are discounted).  

Well, I went to them and showed them Edubuntu.  Then I showed them how Wine worked for very specific educational software that there was no Linux alternative for.  A little tweaking and adjusting, and BAM.

Over 600 computers in the school system have been converted over to Edubuntu with Wine with their central server running Ubuntu Server!

It has been in place for 3 months now, and all the children have gone back to school, and so far the feedback has been amazing!  They have had no intrusions, no problems with children accessing things they should not, and it has been a great learning experience for everyone!

Thanks to the entire crew and community all around the world who create and support Ubuntu - you have shown us all the way to freedom, and no bull computing...

Forever Ubuntu,

Paul
