### Post by kitebari on 2009-07-17
Hi - I just wanted to say 'Hello' as I'm new to Ubuntu but boy, am I impressed! Having come from Windows it's been like moving from the Stone Age into the Space Age in a day! Everything works so fast and well. 

I started with Linux on an Asus 701 and got sold on open source Operating Systems  from that moment on, despite having used Open Office etc., for years on my Windows machine. The Asus was by far the best computer I had ever bought for ease of use to date. From the package being delivered, to being online chatting to my mate in Spain with video on Skype, (and everything else working), in 8 minutes, unheard of with Windoze! 

A second,(and second hand), Asus 900 (with Ubuntu 9.04) came last week and then it was time to take the plunge and sort out the big home laptop. Pass on the dual boot, I gave into the Ubuntu experience & what a revelation; I am now productive rather than fixing the latest gripe, anti virus issue, crash etc. How will I fill my days now? Seriously, what a fabulous OS, never again will I trouble that fellow from the other lot.

So a total convert, with the new found zeal of one, why doesn't everyone use this?
