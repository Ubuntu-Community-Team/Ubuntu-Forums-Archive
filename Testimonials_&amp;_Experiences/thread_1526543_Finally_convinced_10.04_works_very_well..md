### Post by arunb on 2010-07-08
Hi,

I started on Linux 2 years ago, I started with Knoppix the experience was very bad, although it worked on my PC, then I discovered Ubuntu 7.10, it worked very well on my PC, but there were some issues like wireless lan, ethernet etc., also since I was new to linux I found the going difficult.

But I nevertheless stuck on and installed 8.10 and found the experience much better. And after 9.10 I found that Ubuntu had 
definitely improved a lot, I also found that many manufacturers had started providing support for Linux.

I have now installed 10.04 on my laptop and PC, I now intend to use this in my office instead of Windows XP. Some applications that are windows specific I plan on running them in Virtual Box

Anyway I just want to say thanks to the community.

ar
