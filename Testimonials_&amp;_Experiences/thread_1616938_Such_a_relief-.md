### Post by ventrical on 2010-11-08
It is such a relief to NOT have some little aggravating Windows Ant-Virus warning tray pop-up telling me that my computer 'may' be at risk and that my virus database is not up to date.

It is such a relief to NOT have my Windows firewall program constantly prompting to update to the latest version.

It is such a relief to be able to reply to my e-mails on-line without having the e-mailer or server double back on the field lines, forcing me to cancel and start over.

It is such a relief that I do NOT have to use taskmanager to kill Internet Explorer because of constant (not responding) tags.

It is such a relief that I can leave my PC unattended for hours and not have to worry about some remote attacker.

It is such a relief that I am actually  running my PC at speed in sync with my internet connection and don't have to pull my hair out while waiting for a web-page to  load into view.

It is such a relief that I do not have to edit  the *registry* for endless bugs and malware
corrections.


It is such a relief to NOT have Internet Explorer 8 reminder notices everytime I boot my PCs.

 and it is such a relief to finally reclaim ownership of my PC.

  Ubuntu ..., thanks.
