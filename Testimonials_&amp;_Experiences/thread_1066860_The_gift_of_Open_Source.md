### Post by thugpoet22 on 2009-02-11
Linux is an amazing thing. The first time i witnessed Linux was on Tech TV and i was watching this guy boot up Knoppix on a computer. I just could not believe that an OS could run directly from a CD in such a way and not require installation. It really blew me away. At that time i didn't even see it as a great tool to trouble shoot my win box, i just saw it as a fail-safe way to try something different. 

From there i got involved in Mandrake and then Suse and then Fedora but i finally arrived at Ubuntu and im pretty sure this is the linux distro for me. I think i might give Slack Debian or gentoo a try later on when i gain more experience but for now Ubuntu has the best support system and still a bit of a learning curve to keep it interesting. 

I just got over the hump of getting my video card to read the proper drivers. So now my system runs so much better and i can now set my system at the highest settings. Linux is really a breath of fresh air. Truthfully it has the potentiality to blow windows out of the water.
