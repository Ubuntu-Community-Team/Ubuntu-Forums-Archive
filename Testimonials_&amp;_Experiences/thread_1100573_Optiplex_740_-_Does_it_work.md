### Post by Roasted on 2009-03-20
To my knowledge, the 330 and 755 are very similar, and I had no issues with the 330 when I uploaded an image.

I heard rumor that the 740 was troublesome with the type of BIOS they used. I just caught wind that there is a FOG kernel that picks up support for the 2.1.6 version of BIOS without any issues.

What makes me even more nervous is the fact that Dell has 2.1.9 on their web site for available download - which makes me think the 740's we'll be ordering in 2 months may come with 2.1.9 out of box. I sure hope I don't run into these issues.

As much as I like Dell's customer service, sometimes their hardware is so headache infatuated.
