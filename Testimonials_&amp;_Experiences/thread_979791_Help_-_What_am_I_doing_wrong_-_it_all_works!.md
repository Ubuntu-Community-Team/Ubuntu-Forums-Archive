### Post by Boss_scout on 2008-11-12
I am very new to Ubuntu (and Linux) as I downloaded Ubuntu 8.04 a couple of months ago and installed with a dual boot with vista on my laptop. (I am using a Sony vaio VGN-FZ38M, Intel Core 2 T8100, 4GB, 200GB HDD, NVIDIA GeForce 8400M GT GPU.)

Then after a couple of weeks along came 8.10 so what the heck I had only had 8.04 for a couple of weeks so I did an upgrade. From there on  I gradually worked through my syatem:

I have sent many and received even more emails using evolution without any problems.

I have surfed the web to many and varied sites using firefox and apart from the occasional request to install an addin I have had no problems

I have written several documents using Open Office 3, emailed, printed, saved and retrieved them all.

The laptop never fails to connect to my wireless router and hence the internet.

I have played my music which is stored on a portable harddrive which mounts automatically everytime I connect it.

I have watched a movie on DVD and played an audio Cd again with no problems.

I have written a DVD of some amateur video footage

I have transfered photos from my digital camera and from its SD card successfully to my computer

I have printed many documents and scanned several photos using my Canon MP610 Pixma printer

I can now (after the recent updates) sync my treo 680 smart phone everytime with evolution. (I did find this a bit frustrating at the start but once the updates were loaded I had no problems it saw and sync my treo straight away)

I have followed a couple of posts and managed to set up compiz so that I have the cube and several other effects working.

I have just downloaded Cario dock which appears to work but i have not tried to set things up yet.

So looking around these forums I must be going wrong somewhere as everything is working great and I am rapidly become a massive fan of Ubuntu. It is faster to boot, it runs smoother, I haven't had any crashes or freeze ups. My emails load considerably faster than they did in outlook and surfing is certainly quicker in firefox. If it continues like this I will be able to get rid of vista completely.

So please help...I have no problem to report at the moment...Ubuntu is great!....am I alone or is it there are millions of users out there who like me just use the system without any major hangups. ( just watch it will all fall around my ears tonight)
