### Post by vizualbod on 2008-05-06
Hi all,
I just wanted to introduce myself, I started to use Ubuntu today (moved form Vista) and it all just works :guitar:

THANK YOU FOR POSTING ON THIS FORUM.
I didn't need to bug or hire anyone as I found all answers for my questions through Google, most of them were replies from professionals using this forum.

My God, ALL this in a couple of hours. Absolutely painless, constant flow. Brilliant software. Terminal is so hot fast way to run all this. I'd never say this could work for me.
I am absolutely melting. Thank you all for your contributions to this great community.
