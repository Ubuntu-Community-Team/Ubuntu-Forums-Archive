### Post by HockeyPlayerJason on 2010-07-05
I am using Kubuntu 10.04 LTS on an eMachines em250 Netbook and I am absolutely thrilled by the experience and eye candy I get over the severely crippled Windows 7 Starter. 

Ive already configured many things including DVD Playback Support, Java, Flash, even emulators for classic games just by reading around the threads on these forums.

Even got Skype to work on Kubuntu, and the built in Mic, I got to work as well. 

Im aware that theres a Netbook edition of Kubuntu that I could install on this Netbook of mine, but I wanted a different experience and so far things have worked great!

So generally, you could call me new to Kubuntu, but its easy to do things yourself if you are new just by reading line for line, word for word, instructions and typing in codes for what you want to do with the OS. It is not that hard.
