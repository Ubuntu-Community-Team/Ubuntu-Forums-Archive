Just a note...using 64-bit "live"CD to access this site. I am dual booting Win-XP/Ubuntu 10.4 LTS (32-bit) on my hard drive and just installed 2GB of ram and maxed out my motherboard. AMD/Athlon-64 processor, 100Gb hard drive, (now) 2Gb of RAM. All hardware works, (sound, video, cable modem)...64 bit is a bit faster "live" than 34-bit "live" Cd was but limited by speed of DVD R/RW.

Amazed how Ubuntu works so well on this 2005 model (W3410) [COLOR=SeaGreen]e[/COLOR]*Machines* "cheap-box" computer....
hear all these horror stories yet it went well so far with my PC....

Getting an 8GB "usb-Flash" drive to back up "my documents" to,  then I'll feel safer if I get "hosed"...if I do Windows-WP is gone and Ubuntu 10.4 LTS will get a clean install...

I really do like Ubuntu 10.4. At leaset as good as Windows 98/XP:KS from an interface point of view.
