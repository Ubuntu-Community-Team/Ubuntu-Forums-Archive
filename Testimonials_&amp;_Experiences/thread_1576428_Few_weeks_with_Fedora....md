### Post by MasterMind Karthik on 2010-09-17
Well guys, 

The title may drive you crazy... But the thing is that in this few weeks that I had with Fedora 13, Goddard, I had a really hard time... 

To begin with, what I enjoyed and miss in Ubuntu...
1. The menu to menu transitions
2. Pinto app, which is better than Gwibber
3. The more variety of wallpapers, themes and icon sets

Now what I missed of Ubuntu, while using Fedora 13...
1. The easy to use application manager
2. Few apps are less stable and crashes alot on Fedora than on Ubuntu
3. No Open Office pre-installed
4. Lack of support... The Fedora Forums is less helpful... You get an answer in a couple of days... That too often a wrong one... Whereas, the Ubuntu Forums are cool, and I get the right and proper answer to my query in a couple of hours... Less waiting time...

So... When Ubuntu 10.10 releases, I am gonna replace my Goddard with it!!!
