### Post by Spot1234 on 2007-07-08
I go round to my parents about once a week they have a 800mz PC that had a copy of windows XP That it could barely run when It was first installed, they seemed satisfied with it at first. but as time went on the computer got slower and slower (true the amateurish attempts to fix it didn't help) untill it was almost unusable, I was spending more and more of my time on the thing. it  was looking a bit long in the tooth for a windows PC and my parents and I were  frustrated and thinking of replacing it  so as a last resort I decided to install ubuntu half as an experiment.
 I wont pretend it was all plain sailing, I had problems Burning the CD rom and getting the wireless card to work and the  but I found all the solutions browsing the forum, and a lot of things i expected to have problems with, graphics sound usb devices worked first time. I think if you used Linix all your life and switched to windows you would have more problems. 
Now my parents PC is loading programs that do the same job as microsofts,  faster then my PC that has twice the spec. It looks like they wont have to worry about viruses and malware  anymore  its still early days so  but so far I'm impressed.
