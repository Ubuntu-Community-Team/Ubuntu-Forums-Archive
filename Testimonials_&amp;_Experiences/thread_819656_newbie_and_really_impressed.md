welcome! and don't fear the terminal:) the reason you see a lot of terminal based answers on the forums is simply because copying and pasting the command line is a lot simpler and faster than talking someone through the menus and options. It also has the additional benefit of giving better feedback by way of error messages. This allows us to troubleshoot more effectively. After all the whole goal in the forums is to get you up and running/resolve your problems as quickly and painlessly as possible.

With all that said there is nothing wrong with using the gui tools to solve your problems. After you get used to the command line you might actually find that you prefer it, but there are times when the gui tools are easier to use so take a little time to become familiar with them as well as the command line.

Cheers!
