### Post by kurt18947 on 2012-03-05
One piece of advice -- keep a small Windows partition if possible.  You'll find hardware related tasks like updating PC BIOSs, GPSs, device firmware and the like which are simply not possible with Linux.  I hadn't used Windows for months until I wanted to update firmware on my printer.  The process required a USB connection, an internet connection -- and Windows.
