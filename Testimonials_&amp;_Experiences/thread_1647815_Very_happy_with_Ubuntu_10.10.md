### Post by johntaylor1887 on 2010-12-17
I've been a linux user for quite some time now, and an Ubuntu user for the past couple releases. I originally tried the beta of 10.10 but could not get it to install on my machine. So I decided to stay with 10.04 for a while and see what 11.04 would bring. But curiosity got the best of me and decided to give 10.10 another try. What do you know, the live CD booted up just fine (the beta would not) and presented me with the option to install. 

Since I had backed up everything ahead of time, I clicked install. Everything went as smooth as silk, and 8 minutes later I was at the desktop. So far it has been the smoothest, fastest, most stable OS I've ever used. I'm simply blown away by it. I can't thank the developers enough for this beautiful piece of software. 10.04 was excellent for me, but 10.10 has been perfect so far. Ubuntu makes my life easier and there's less stress now. Thanks again devs, and keep up the great work!
