### Post by KaiJordanDay on 2008-06-24
I decided after a recormendation from a mate to finally take a chance and install Linux. I decided to do it on my oldest pc so if anything went wrong it did not at all matter. My partitioning went terribly wrong and I decided to just wipe xp and use Linux.

I did not regret a thing.

Ubuntu Hardy is awesome I must say. I have not coustomized or visulized mine because I think doing so it is just another OS that focuses on visulization rater than performance. I have left it completely as it is, with a few updates and installation of shockwave, flash and ndiswrapper. My pc is tiny, 256 mb ram and 40 gig HDD, and yet, it still goes extremely fast. Sometimes faster than my brand new laptop. 

Just my thoughts :]
