### Post by egkeat on 2011-10-15
Okay, more than I expected.

I'm simply in love with the design of Oneiric Ocelot. I still can't pronounce but it's a beautiful release. After using Ubuntu from 8.10, I've been an on again, off again user, attempting to pick at and learn little by little with my limited experience. Yes, I've opened up a pc and changed hard drives, inserted memory, installed and uninstalled countless applications, on top of more than enough troubleshooting in between. Still, I feel like a noob so it's good thing Ubuntu keeps getting easier to use.

This couldn't have come at a better time. Cloud apps, smartphones, tablets, and of course the trusty ol' desktops are at a point of all being useful to people in whatever way they prefer. For me, 11.10 is just that. Something for everyone, something for me. 

As I've said, I know little about all the distros and development. My choice is Ubuntu running Gnome 3 shell. Thanks to all for working hard and getting it right.
