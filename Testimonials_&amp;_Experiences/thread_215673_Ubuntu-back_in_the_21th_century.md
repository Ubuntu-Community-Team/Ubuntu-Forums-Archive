### Post by Helix. on 2006-07-14
Hey,

I've had windows for 9 years now, from 95, 2000 and XP. One day, looking through a magazine, I found out about Ubuntu. So, I went to the website, looking for somthing beisdes windows, at least somthing a little faster...

But when i downloaded the dapper cd-rom, i was amazed.

So I booted from the live CD and installed (took 20min to install, vs Windows's 1hr plus backup installs)

First thing was, it was so damn fast! It took me no time to boot up and login. Even in a new windows xp install, it took me 3min to get booted and logged on (now, it takes 6 min). Second, the new desktop...such an improvment from breezy. Third, I went to system->admin->networking. Right there was all 3 of my network hardware-my modem, my lan port, and my wifi card. I was on wifi in less than 10min (router had problems). Then, thinking it would take me forever to find codecs for mp3 files, I found the ubuntu forums.

A HUGE thanks to everybody who contributed to Ubuntu and this website. Without y'all, I'd still be stuck in the Dark Ages with Winblows 8)
