### Post by majikstreet on 2005-09-28
I have decided to post my ubuntu story.

I've been using computers for most of my life. I got my first computer about five years ago and it was an old computer with windows on it. It worked fine for what I was using it for (nothing).

Then I got a dell with windows xp home on it. That was fine for a while. 
I then decided to install debian on it to "test" it. Debian wiped out everything, and since I couldn't use debian, I had to re-install windows. 

I got a spare computer to test out stuff on.  I installed debian, didn't work. Then red hat, didn't work. Then Fedora- didn't work. My favourite was Slackware- I learned about ndiswrapper about then.

I installed ubuntu server on that spare computer and I WAS IN LOVE! Ndiswrapper was wonderful to install thanks to the CD and apt-get/dpkg.

I thought, well hey, I can try ubuntu on the main computer and see if it works.
I installed and had some trouble with X, but mainly because of my computer.

I got it working and I was really in love.

I really recommend Ubuntu to anyone.

I even upgraded to breezy badger on my main computer; no problems so far.

Windows just gives me too many viriuses, plus when you upgrade you can't even do anything else...

With ubuntu you can do other stuff while upgrading..

majikstreet
"Ubuntu let's me breath easier"
