### Post by evolmachine on 2007-12-22
I installed Ubuntu because I was tired of being hounded by Microsoft and my "unvalidated" copy of Windoze.  I had heard about Linux and its various "forms" (distros i guess) and read up a bit about them on wikipedia... and guessed that Ubuntu was probably my best bet.

I knew I was in for a change, and braved it all the same.  I have to say I was pleasantly surprised.  

Everything worked just like it said it should, and I have no real problems with any hardware.  I have had to make some small tweaks here and there to get some programs in working fashion, but thats nothing compared to what it takes to get an XP install up and running.

Also, I have to say that with all the visual effects turned to max on a working video card, Ubuntu is visually VERY cool.  It blows me away that all of this, including all these programs, is offered for free.  

Thank you.  You all friggin rawk.
