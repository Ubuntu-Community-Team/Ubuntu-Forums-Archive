### Post by 1roxtar on 2010-09-29
Last night, I finally got to convert the last computer in my house with Windows XP to Ubuntu (Maverick).  It was my 14 year old daughter's computer and she had gotten fed up with the viruses and her system always slowing down.  She has a wireless card in it and her connections were always around 11%-27%, so her internet ran really slow.  I used that card with another computer that had Ubuntu on it and I always got at least 70%-85% on it.  She came into my room, with my other daughter who LOVES Ubuntu, and asked if I could change her computer.  I did, with excitement, and sure enough her wireless connection is holding at 75%.  I am rid of MS Windows and the air smells so much cleaner.  As it was said in Poltergeist, "This house is clean."
