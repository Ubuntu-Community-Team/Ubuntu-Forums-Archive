### Post by deat on 2007-02-03
Lets go to the beginning. It was around 2001/2002 and I'm 11/12 years old and my father was fiddling with a computer in our office. So I go in and ask him what he's doing. He tells me that he's installing a new operating system called Red Hat. He goes on explaining what an operating system is, and how Windows isn't the only one. :P

I'm very young so all of this is *whoosh* way over my head. Let's skip ahead...*skip* to 2005, where I hear about this thing called Ubuntu from a friend at school. He just received a shipment of install CDs so he gave me a few and i race home to try it out.

Things didn't go so well and as i kept trying to install the newer and newer version of Ubuntu, I would always manage to mess up my whole computer.

Then finally one last stab at trying it out happened last week. I installed it, and completely rid myself of Windows and everything Microsoft. So far I have encountered a few small problems, but other than that I am positive that Windows in any form will never be installed on this computer again.

Hello Ubuntu community. :D
I've been lurking the forums constantly for the past 4 or 5 days now. I'm trying to soak as much knowledge as I can. :)

Zach
