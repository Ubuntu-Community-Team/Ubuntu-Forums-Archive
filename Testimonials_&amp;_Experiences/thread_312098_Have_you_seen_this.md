### Post by veli on 2006-12-03
Hi guys, I'm kinda a minimalist, I want only what I need. So I install ubuntu server, and after I added only the packages I need. I ended with a quite slim system, consuming about 70 MB RAM when idle. But few hours ago, I restarted my PC (PIII, 660 MHz, 256 RAM, Dapper, kernel 2.6.18) and look at the attached screenshot.
