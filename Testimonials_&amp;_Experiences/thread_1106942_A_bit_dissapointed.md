### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-03-27
CPUs have been getting faster, at a more rapid rate than hard disks. So what I'm finding is that I can overclock my CPU to 3.6GHz and it won't feel faster, because the speed of my day-to-day operations is being limited by the speed of my hard disks.

Encode some H.264 video with --threads=4 and you'll watch it fly.

Depending on what quad-core processor you've got, you might have a lower speed-per-core than before; this can also affect how fast things "feel".
