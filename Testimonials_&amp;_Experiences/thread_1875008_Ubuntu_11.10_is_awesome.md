At my first glance of Ubuntu 11.04 i thought...this is it...i am leaving this ship now...And i did...well at least temporarily...

But as i used Kubuntu 11.04 and finally 11.10 more and more....more bugs arrised...and there was this feeling...this is not for me...

My wife also complained about Kubuntu beeing buggy and slow.

Then i gave it a shot...i downloaded Ubuntu 11.10 and tried it...

Well i have this feeling....Windows 7, 8......maybe you have a growing champion in Ubuntu 11.10.

I know that there are many that doesnt like the Unity interface and i respect that. But i was there myself...and now...well its different...

I have big hope for Ubuntu 12.04 LTS...

Just dont give up!
