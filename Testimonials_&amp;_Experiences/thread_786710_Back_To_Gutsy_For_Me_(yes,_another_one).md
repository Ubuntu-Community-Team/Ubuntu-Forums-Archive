### Post by mcgarry83 on 2008-05-08
Ok, I know there have been a few other people who have written similar threads about going back to Gutsy, and listing the reasons. I too am forced to do the same. I thought I would share with you all my reasons in hopes of getting some feed back.
 
**1. Firefox Beta** - I have to many problems with firefox freezing and then going back to what it was doing. I know that it is a bug and I may have fixed it by following others posts, but I feel like it was not a good idea to make beta software as the default browser.

**2. Graphics** - This is the most major problem I have. My graphics card (ATI) worked PERFECT in gutsy. Compiz looked amazing, UrbanTerror ran smooth. Everything was fine. After the upgrade to Hardy, I was unable to turn desktop effects on with a fresh install. I thought maybe I needed to install the ATI drivers. I first tried the open source drivers, which kind of worked but slowed down my system A LOT. I decided to try EnvyNG, BIG MISTAKE! It would not auto load the driver for some reason, so I did it manually. On the reboot, my desktop looked huge, all the icons and everything. Seems the screen res was very low, but only gave me the one option to set it. I never got this fixed, and was unable to undo it.

**3. External DVD burner** - I have already posted a thread about this. My LG external DVD burner that worked perfect in Gutsy, will not burn DVD's in Hardy. It is recognized, it mounts, it burns regular CD's, but will not burn DVD's at all. It tries for about 2 minutes, then throws up an error. Others have had this problem. Burning DVD's is a must for me.

**4. Urban Terror** - This is probably related to #2, but I can no longer play my favorite Linux game, Urban Terror. It is so slow it looks like your playing in the dark with a strobe light. I set all the settings to the lowest they will go with no luck. I love this game and cant stand not being able to play it.

**5. Keys** - Every single time I plug my wireless usb adapter in or start the system with it already in. A box pops up for the key ring and I have to enter in my password or else I cant get on the internet. This is not as big a problem as the others but its very annoying. I dont have to do that in Gutsy. I think there is a work around but it sounds very complicated and I shouldnt have to do that. 

Well, that about sums up the major problems I have had with Hardy. There are a few minor issues that dont seem to be a big deal but for now, I have to go back to Gutsy. I reinstalled it last night and everything is back to normal. I guess Im just going to wait for 8.10 and see if these issues are resolved. Please reply if you know what might be causing some of this.
