### Post by pauljwells on 2006-09-05
Five years ago I knew nothing about computers

I started with a mac ibook and learned to love osx

I bought a PC and learned to tolerate XP

I then installed Ubuntu!!

Now I am running CFD programs on my PC, I can log in over ssh from my MOBILE PHONE (using PuTTY) and submit or cancel jobs. I can view my desktop over VNC, I can writewebpages on NVu and program C++ in Kdevelop

All this stuff I have learnt because it is free, and because there are so many friends out there who want to help me to do so.

So thank you Steve Jobs, for getting me started, thank you Mark Shuttleworth, for starting the popularising Ubuntu project, and thank you to all my fellow linuxistas, who help me and let me help them to learn and achieve more than I imagined was possible!!!
