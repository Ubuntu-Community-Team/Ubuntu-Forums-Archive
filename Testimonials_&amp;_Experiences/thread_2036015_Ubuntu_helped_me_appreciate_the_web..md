### Post by epikvision on 2012-07-31
16 years old, I was once a skeptic.  I wanted to be a recluse from technology and how it belittled close interaction and networked into proliferation.  At first, it seemed so inane, that people will actually want to communicate so instantaneously and not take the time to grow it as we traditionally would upfront.  

Then along came Ubuntu.  From the launchpad to the forums, Ubuntu helped me on something that not  even school, Mac, and Windows could provide: an appreciation for the web.  Networking suddenly became so appealing!  And so did joining the conversation - commenting, blogging -, something I was afraid to do long before.  After all, the open source community breathes on it!  Not to mention the many inspirational beings out there keeping their communities alive with their sweat and toil!  

Now I use Ubuntu with glowing pride, that I too have a voice to share.
