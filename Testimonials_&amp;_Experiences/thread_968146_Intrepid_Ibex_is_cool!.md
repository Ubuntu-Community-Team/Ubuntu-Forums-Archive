### Post by terabyte1 on 2008-11-02
Well on 30th October 2008, I downloaded both Kubuntu 8.10 and Ubuntu 8.10 Intrepid Ibex, and after copying the respective Iso's to CD's I installed Kubuntu on my main machine, and Ubuntu on my Secondary machine - both are performing well!


The Kubuntu version, is in KDE (Kool Desktop Edition) 4.1.2 but does not contain the 3.5 version of KDE, nor is it easily changed to that version. Either way, the KDE 4.1 is great and worth using - Thank You Ubuntu Team for bringing us this magnificent Edition and doing all that fantastic work in the last few months to bring us Intrepid Ibex (8.10) By the way an Ibex is a form of Mountain Goat with huge horns - at least, that's what I can see on my green Limited Edition T-Shirt :guitar:!


Why the guitar? Well, I play both an Accoustic Guitar and a Fender Stratocaster Electric one :)
