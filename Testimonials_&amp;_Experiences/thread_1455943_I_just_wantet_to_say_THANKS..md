### Post by daniel-strae on 2010-04-16
I switched last month to ubuntu in my office, and i'll never go back.
I have a notebook, works perfectly on Ubuntu, except for the AMD "unsupported hardware" watermark in the bottom-right of the screen, but it works perfectly (even the graphic card).

I  have a home-desktop, which until now I could not use with Ubuntu because of  an incompatibility problem with the video card (yes, amt ati again), which forced me to use  800x600 resolution .

Now, i just installed the 10.4 beta 2, ati restricted drivers (the same that i tryed with ubuntu 9.10), and finally it works!

As the title, i just want to say thanks to all whom helped me, here and on others forums, to enjoy at best ubuntu.

Thanks guys, really, the 29th April i'll finally be 100% free from windows :mrgreen:


p.s: after the last virus (and format), 2 weeks ago i switched even the notebook of my mother to ubuntu: she really doesnt know anythings about computer, but she havent got any problem yet. who said that linux was difficult to use?
