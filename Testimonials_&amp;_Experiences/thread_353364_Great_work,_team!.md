### Post by Tigera on 2007-02-04
Dear Ubuntu team,

I wanted to give you all a big "thank you" for your hard work in making Ubuntu what it is today.  I have Edgy installed on my laptop and I could not be more impressed.  Over the years, I've tried a number of distributions, including Red Hat, Mandriva, SUSE, and even Slackware, but Linux is about choice, and I finally chose Ubuntu - for all four of my machines.
This morning I wanted to update two packages from source, umbrello 1.5.6 and kplato 0.6.1, and I had been battling with trying to create a Debian package.  I had found instructions online for this under Debian, all of which involved a dozen or more steps of editing files, but I finally found out that the Ubuntu guide had a simple three-step process which worked flawlessly (the link for which is [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_compile_.deb_files_from_source]("http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_compile_.deb_files_from_source")).  Never in my experience with Linux have I ever found anything so easy to do.  So, thanks to everyone, and keep up the good work! :guitar:
