### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-03-08
> **'[Shadow] said:**
> Is there anything you guys would recommend doing to become more proficient...

[LIST]
[*]Learn terminal commands, because it makes life easier. You usually don't need it, but still it can speed your tasks. Your greatest friend is the 'man' (manual) command -- for example, type 'man man' into the terminal for more information about 'man'.
[*]Whenever you need to do something, and you don't know how, come to the forum and search. Also use Google; other people will have asked your question before. If you still don't get your answer, post a new thread.
[*]Contribute to the forums when someone asks a question that you've already figured out.
[/LIST]
You'll learn just by doing.
