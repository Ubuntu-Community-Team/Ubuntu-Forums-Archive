Hi Folks. Finally did a fresh install of 11.10 and after doing a few installs with perfect results I thought I best add this little bug I ran into.

I did a fresh download and check the download for errors, all ok. 

Installed the DVD and booted the computer and all seem fine for the 1st few mins then the black screen. Never did get the ubuntu screen either. So With a black screen and no cursor or mouse and DVD stopped at this point. I press the Tab Key once and hit enter and the DVD started to spin again and 15 seconds later or a bit longer I got the ubuntu screen. Needless to say the rest of the installation went perfect but there is a small bug in the installation program. GL :)
