### Post by pricetech on 2009-10-06
I've been tinkering with different flavors of Linux over the years and have been using Ubuntu for the last few.  I'm currently using 8.04 LTS and will probably stay with it until the next LTS.

While I won't call my experience perfect, the vast majority of what I have wanted to do has "just plain worked" which is exactly what I want an OS to do.

When something doesn't work right off the bat I search the forums to see if anyone has already invented the wheel in question.  If searching doesn't help, I post a question.  Overall the attitudes exhibited in the forums are vary positive and most people are helpful.  I in turn have tried to be helpful to others when I think I can help or at least input something useful.

You can see by my "beans" I am not new to the forum.  I just wanted to throw my two cents worth out there for others to see.

Thanks for your time.
