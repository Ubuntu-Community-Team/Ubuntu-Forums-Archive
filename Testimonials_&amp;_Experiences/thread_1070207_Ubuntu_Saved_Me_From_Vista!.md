### Post by Nicodemus144 on 2009-02-14
So this is Nicodemus's girlfriend. We just spent the afternoon loading and configuring Ubuntu to my Dell laptop. This thing is fricken sweet. I have been dealing with Vista nightmares for 2 years now and was looking at spending a lot of money on a Mac. 

No more though; this thing is far easier and more intuitive than I ever imagined. I may not set my mom up with it, but for me this is amazing. The sheer speed and difference in processor and RAM usage alone is worth it. I love the customization and all the animations. I really can't wait to show it to other friends; first see if they notice a change, then show them all the amazing things my cube of fire can do. :-)

I want to thank all you guys for helping Nicodemus with issues that sprung up and educating him about how to load it. It has really made a difference for me. Thank you again!
