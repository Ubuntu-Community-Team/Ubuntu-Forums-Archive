### Post by demosthene1 on 2009-08-27
Over time I've made a complete switch to Ubuntu Linux. I was never a fan of Microsoft. In the days of DOS - before Windows was invented -  I used a Radio Shack 'color computer' running a unix type os with a primitive desktop! Without Ubuntu, for me, computing would be boring. Thanks to all the developers that have created such an amazing operating system.
