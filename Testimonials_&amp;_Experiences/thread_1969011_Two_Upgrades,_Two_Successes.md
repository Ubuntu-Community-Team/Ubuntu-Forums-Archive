### Post by Frantic_Earthling on 2012-04-29
Usually I like to do fresh installs when a new version is available.

This time, feeling a bit lazy, I decided to upgrade.

First time I attempted doing this.

I first upgraded my Asus M70Vn laptop computer from 11.10 to 12.04. The upgrade went without a hitch. Everything works. I am happy to report that I have no problems.

Then I upgraded my HP Pavilion m9680 from 10.04 to 11.10 and immediately afterwards, from 11.10 to 12.04. (Since then I have discovered that I could have gone directly from 10.04 to 12.04 :???:) But who cares? I have no problems and my HP Pavilion works like a charm with 12.04:)

I guess somebody did a very good job at Canonical! :cool:
