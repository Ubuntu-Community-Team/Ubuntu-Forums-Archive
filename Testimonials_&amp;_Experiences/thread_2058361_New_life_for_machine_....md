### Post by exploder on 2012-09-16
Really great post! I can imagine the huge difference between Windows XP and Ubuntu 12.04. Ubuntu 12.04 really turned out to be a great release. My Step-Dad had used Ubuntu a few years ago but had gone back to running Windows. My Step-Dad had to use my computer that at the time had a beta version of 12.04 on it to find directions to take me to an eye specialist in Indianapolis.

My Step-Dad was really impressed with Unity, so much so that he went out of his way to get it installed as a duel boot with Windows 7. My Step-Dad has called me several times to get help and because he just could not believe how many good games are coming to Linux. My Step-Dad said that he is seriously considering just switching to the LTS full time.

People have really bashed Unity in the past but new users love that it does not resemble Windows and it sure seems to be winning people over. I hope that other distributions adopt Unity in the future. I believe in giving credit where credit is due and I think Canonical deserves a lot of credit for creating Unity and standing by to their decision. I give Canonical credit for including articles that praise and bash Unity in their weekly newsletter, these people have a lot of heart for keeping things real.

Seeing Unity win over someone that is such a hard core Windows fan like my Step-Dad tells me that Ubuntu is going in the right direction.
