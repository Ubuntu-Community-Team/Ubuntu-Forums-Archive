### Post by th2 on 2006-01-11
That's right!  I have a Compaq Presario R3000 with the following specs:

-2.8 Ghz Celeron
-768 MB of RAM (640 for system available)
-ATI Mobility Radeon 9000 IGP (128 MB set aside for Video Memory... just to make me feel good)
-SoundMAX for audio
-Realtek NIC
-Unkown modem (don't need it though)
-DVD/CD-RW
-Keyboard
-Synaptics touchpad!
-USB 2.0!
-30 GB Hard drive

All the crucial hardware was picked up by 5.10 Breezy Badger, which shocked me.  I was even more shocked upon finding out that the special audio buttons on the side **worked in GNOME**.  :D 

I absolutely like this OS.

Sure, I don't have 3D acceleration yet (buth with the open source R300 drivers coming, that might soon change).

I do alternative news and other stuff, and thanks to Ubuntu, I decided to permanently go to Linux.  Thanx! :cool:
