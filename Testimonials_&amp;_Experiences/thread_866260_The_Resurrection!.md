### Post by vikramaditya on 2008-07-21
I found a clone box out on the curb last week and lugged it back to my lair.  Upon inspection, everything was present except (naturally) the hdd.  There's an Asus mobo, AMD Thoroughbred 1.8ghz cpu, 2 128mb sticks generic ram, generic cdrom & floppy drives, ATI Radeon R300 AGP vid card, Creative SBL Audigy sound card, USR 56k modem, & el-crapo 250w psu.  I had an ancient 10gb Hitachi hdd laying around, so in it went.  Bootdisk in, power up OK, FORMAT C:/S/U, set bios to boot from cd, insert xubuntu live cd, wham-bam-thankya-ma'am! :) Now, all I gotta do is figure out why I should even keep the thing.  I'm afraid of donating it to anybody, cuz they'll probably put xp on it. :mad:
