### Post by armandh on 2009-02-11
his collection of computers are truly the cobblers children's shoes.
good thing I took a cd drive and lan card
did not need to partition as I used the whole drive.

the dell box loaded 8.10 from my cd drive to a small [20gb] hdd  with out problem. the on-board lan was not well but my lan card worked fine with the only good lan cable he had in the whole house. connect to the cable modem and finally the 2xx updates were done + flash + medibuntu.

we tested a movie dvd on the usb dvd drive I took along. 
he is now on-line with 1 computer running. an all ubuntu home.
[given the other computers are not working, yet]

he is happy when I called but he tried it on another box [#2] and suspects a bad cd in it as well. he gave me a 1ghz P-III trade for the lan card. I now have it sporting 8.10 too.
It had no cd, a 16 Gb scuzzy[sp] drive and on-board video. I had to delete the drive from the boot sequence, load ubuntu, and restore the drive to the boot sequence. 

I told him If he continues without success with #2 to bring it over and we will kick it in to shape together.
