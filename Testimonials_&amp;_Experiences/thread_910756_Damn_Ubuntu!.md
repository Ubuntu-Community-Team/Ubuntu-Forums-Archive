### Post by Saint Angeles on 2008-09-05
> **myth01 said:**
> I'm still struggling to get to get to grips with the whole repository/package thing rather than the usual "Install" but otherwise I am loving the sense of freedom and creativity that comes with Linux.

Bill who???

just remember these two commands:

```
sudo apt-get update
```
this will update your package lists letting your computer know which packages have updates available...

```
sudo apt-get dist-upgrade
```

and this will download and install every available update in your repos.

voila! and when you need a new program, instead of searching online for an exe and hoping its not actually a virus, you just open up synaptic package manager (system->administration) and search for the programs you want to install... check them off then click apply!

also, we DO have something comparable to a windows .exe file. its a .deb file. [www.getdeb.net](www.getdeb.net) is a great site for finding deb files that are soemtimes newerr than the packages available in the repos.

good luck.
