### Post by tubman88 on 2011-12-04
First of let me say I'm more of a newbie to Ubuntu than anything else but a long time Mac user. First encountered Ubuntu on a Dell Laptop that I had received in a trade. I was Netbook remix 9.04 if I remember right. I was quite impressed with it but I sold the netbook before I had a good chance to see what it was all about. Recently I acquired a couple of ex school PC's and kept one for myself. Tried 11.10 on it but it seemed slow and sluggish so I went with 11.04 non unity version as the integrated intel graphics don't support it. Very easy install  with no driver problems except finding the driver for my Canon printer which took all of ten minutes.
Now that I've been running it for a couple of months I can say" good job". Most everything works smoothly with the occasional niggle, mine being wake from sleep and my lower panel loses sections out of it every now and then.
 Some things I'd like to see in Ubuntu. First a save to PDF feature ala Apple would be great. I use this all the time in the Apple world and kinda miss it over here. Second would be and all encompassing utility program in the mold of Onyx or Cocktail.Would make things a lot easier for newbies. Overall though Ubuntu is a good piece of work !!
