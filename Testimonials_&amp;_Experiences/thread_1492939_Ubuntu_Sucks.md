1)  I have never had any issues with slow USB transfer speed.  One thing that is often the problem is powering a USB device from the USB bus rather than with an external power supply. But yes, it is a problem that is often discussed.  I don't have an answer because I have never encountered it.

2)  The Handbrake issue is known and either has or is being addressed.  Remember that Canonical did not write Handbrake.  Some MOTU working on his own time late at night after coming home from class or after a long day of work did his very best to make things work as best he could.
