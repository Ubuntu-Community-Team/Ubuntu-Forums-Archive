### Post by Mr A Mouse on 2008-05-18
Well, so far I've installed Hardy on my server, and now I'm dual-boot on my laptop (hp dv6000, AMD Athlon X2, 2 GB RAM). Setting up the wireless (Atheros) was a bit of an aggravation--I tried the madwifi route, but it didn't work, so i went with ndiswrapper which worked like a charm. The nVidia card required activating the driver and nothing more.

Next step: getting Vista working on virtualbox. Wish me luck, but it's been fairly easy so far!
