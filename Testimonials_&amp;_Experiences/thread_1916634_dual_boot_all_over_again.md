which totally sucked. a lot of Ubuntu was missing. 
it had a fast boot time but reduced functionality. 
no 3d or ability to add medibuntu.

mini-9 got a reload of XP and in the end 10.04

the Ubuntu was for everything else

upgrade some of the inner workings to 2Gb ram and 32Gb SSd
and it is now dual booting 7 home and Ubuntu 10.04
7 for Quick-books and office 07, Ubuntu 10.04 for everything else

as soon as I am happy with desktop Unity, likely the LTS, I will try it here too
