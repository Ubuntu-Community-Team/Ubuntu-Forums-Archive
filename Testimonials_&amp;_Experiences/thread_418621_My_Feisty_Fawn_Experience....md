I've tried Suse, Redhat, Mepis, Knoppix, and then, finally, Ubuntu - twice!!!
Why twice? Was I unhappy with my first time? No. I wasn't.

I been a Windows user for years ... just like most Windows users.
I've always know there are alternatives, but after becoming a web developer, I found it cumbersome to install LAMP on Win. So, I came to Ubuntu ... and just like most Windows users ... I doubt I'd ever go back to Windows.

If you like a straight outta-the-box experience and familiarity - stick with Win.
