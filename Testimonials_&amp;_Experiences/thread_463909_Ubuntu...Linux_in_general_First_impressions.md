### Post by Alucard_Duskmoon on 2007-06-04
This is my first time using a Linux OS *not including DSL* and I have to say, i'm pretty impressed. It's obviously more difficult then the Windows i'm used to, I'll have to learn with time. Anyways, I used the live cd at first...that was WAAAAAAAAAAAY too slow so I decided to try an alternative method and make a virtual hd and install it from there. I have a 10 gig v drive for Linux now and it works perfectly with little slowing. Some of the things that I thought were awesome about Linux are that most Linux oses' are free and so are most of the programs compatible with them, I was even more glad when I learned that Firefox and Thunderbird worked well, and even better, firefox came pre-installed. I will experiment with it more before deciding to give up my reliable windows xp.
