### Post by ventrical on 2010-09-19
Again , Ubuntu ceases to amaze me. About a year ago I purchased a USB to IDE&SATA converter and there was no way I could get that thing to work correctly with Windows (even with all the proprietary drivers). Now .. I am into security and malware removal of Windows systems and Ubuntu and other lLinux drivers turned that little USB converter into a Super Malware Removal ROBO IT !!

  I have Ubuntu  loaded persistively (meaning it will boot on any PC, read/write/save etc) and I was finally able to get AVG Anti -Virus  installed on it and with the USB converter interface I can scan ANY HDD for Malware.

  This saves TONS of work as all I have to do is bring  the Thumb Drive and/or the USB converter during a service call.

  And not only this .. but all those files  (from 20 years of work) which I thought were impossible to recover can be rolled up from the IDE to USB converter and onto  newly formatted HD , DVD or pen-drive.
