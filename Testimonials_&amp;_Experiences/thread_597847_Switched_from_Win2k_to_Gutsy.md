### Post by will1911a1 on 2007-10-30
Hello!  This is my first post on these fourms and I thought it would be appropriate that it would also be my first impression of Ubuntu.

A little background about me:  I've been a long time computer user and hobbyist (started with a C64).  I worked in the IT field for quite a few years and had a number of certifications, but deicded I'd be happier doing something else.

The vast majority of my experience has been with Windows and MacOS/OSX.

So anyway, I decided to give Ubuntu a shot (mostly because I was bored with Windows 2000).  I backed up the files I thought were important and took the plunge by wiping my drive and doing an install of 7.10.

I have to say that I'm really impressed.  Just about everything worked right away and with very little configuartion on my part.  I had to download some codecs to get some multimedia working but that was a breeze.

The only thing I'm dissapointed with is that the drivers available for my ATI card won't let me play WoW and even that I can deal with since I have a kid on the way and probably won't have time for WoW anyhow.

Anyway, I see a lot of negative install stories posted here and thought I share a positive one. :)
