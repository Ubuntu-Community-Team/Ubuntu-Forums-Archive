### Post by falonyn on 2006-12-29
This is not a question or a request for any help. I just wanted to make a post stating that this is the first time that I have installed any version of Linux on a computer of mine and I am very impressed. 

I am not totally new to Ubuntu as I have installed it on my girlfriend's computer after she got several viruses back to back on Windows. This is, however, the first time I have installed Ubuntu on my own system (my brand new laptop) and within a couple hours I have everything working besides the media card reader, and I am very impressed. 

I would just like to state that Linux is the way to go unless there are some very specific things one needs Windows for, and even then I would suggest learning how to setup a dual-boot system so one only has to use Windows when necessary. 

I would also like to thank this wonderful online community for all of the help you have provided. Ubuntu is the way to go.
