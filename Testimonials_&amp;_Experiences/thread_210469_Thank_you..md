You have voiced my opinions exactly. Thing that makes me choose Ubuntu over any other distro is the amazing speed I get on my creaky old computer. I had Xandros 3 Delux installed as a duel boot with Win98SE (yeah, still Win98; I refuse to give MS another dollar of my money). I thought Xandros was a pretty cool distro, easy to use, and handsome, in the way that KDE desktop is always a looker. The choice of Linux distro is really a matter of your particular hardware; Linux is Linux is Linux. Ubuntu does everything I need and does it dependably and consistently and F-a-a-a-s-s-t!
