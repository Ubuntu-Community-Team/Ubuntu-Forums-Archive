### Post by patagriff on 2012-08-20
A month ago, I downloaded and installed Ubuntu-12.04-desktop-386.iso and installed it on my** acer Aspire 5515 laptop**. From the very moment I booted the installation, I had wireless internet access. 

That day, all updates were completed, and I can honestly say I've had **no performance issues whatsoever**. As a matter of fact, the touchpad, which I was unable to get working in Windows no matter what I tried functioned properly from the start with Ubuntu 12.04.

No freeze-ups. No crashes. Answers to question are easy to find on-line. Very happy, and have **abandoned Windows, for good **it would seem.:guitar:
