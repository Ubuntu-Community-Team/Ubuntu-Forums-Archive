### Post by Erios on 2008-08-24
Whoops. Sorry for the miscategorization-- this site has a lot of categories and information on it. If I had realized that there was a testimonials category, I would have posted there originally.

Yes, there is a reason why I went with Gutsy Gibbon. Here in Iraq my Internet access is very time limited, and I am not under any circumstances allowed to hook my own computer up to the network that we use to browse the Internet. Only carefully configured government computers are.

So I wanted to have one shipped, but was unsure how long it would take to get here if I got the free DVD from the Ubuntu site, or if they would even ship to Iraq. 

I decided instead to order it from Amazon, only to discover that they don't ship electronics like that to Iraq. I knew that they shipped books, however, so I simply ordered "A Practical Guide to Ubuntu Linux", which comes with a Live DVD. Silly that I had to go to that much trouble, isn't it?

It's been tough slogging without the connection, as Linux is obviously very Internet dependent for its packages. But I'm playing around with it as I can; not sure yet how to install codecs and things from the Live DVD, and actually I'm not sure that they can be. Either way, learning what my situation will let me and will continue upon my arrival home.
