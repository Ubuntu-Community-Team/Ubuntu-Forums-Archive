### Post by Wiley Hunter on 2009-05-24
Wow!!!  I've been an Ubuntu user for a while (converted back in version 6.10) and have always been happy.  Installs were sometimes challenging, what with various USB WiFi's and such.

I don't know what exactly happened between 8.04 and 9.04 but something spectacular went down.  I just installed on a Toshiba (dunno which model but it was a new one) for a friend this weekend and it recognized his WiFi and Verizon dongle as well as everything else straight away.  Definately an awesome experience.  I have since upgraded 3 of 4 computers at my house to 9.04 and will continue to spread the "gospel" of Ubuntu's wonderful works.  Keep it up folks, slowly but surely Linux WILL rule the world!  We are winning them over, 1 user at a time.

Thanks again to ALL involved with this distribution.
