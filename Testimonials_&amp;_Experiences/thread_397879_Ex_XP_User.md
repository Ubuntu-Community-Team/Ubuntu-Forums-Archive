### Post by Ender Black on 2007-04-01
Yep, I am in the same boat as you mate.  I did have some difficulties at first with my graphics card and wireless but through surfing these awesome forums and actually reading the documentation, pretty much all was solved.  

In fact yesterday I went back to XP for a bit so I could download more music onto my *gasp* Zune media player.  Just to get to the Zune program I had to wait an hour for a Microsoft update, Anti-Virus update, Zune software and firmware update.  Prior to this experience, I was thinking that I would go back to XP permanently because I love my Zune and I don't want to buy a new portable media player so soon after my Zune purchase.  Well...I guess it is time to iRiver Clix or an Archos 404.

But the biggest thing in my return to XP experience yesterday was how small my screen felt when I knew I was limited to one desktop.  Claustrophobic would have been the best way to describe it.

Cheers and thanks to the UBUNTU devs and helpers!
