### Post by midnightray on 2008-01-31
Well ubuntu works near perfect on my Dell Inspiron 6400. I bought this computer over 1 year ago without realising that soon dell would be shipping these as linux. It has been my dream for ages to have a working Linux machine to play around with.

1. Live CD. everything worked, wasn't expecting the laptops speakers to work and made me jump !

2. Installed perfectly (3rd time round, the others i messed up by trying ndiswrapper for my wifi card. Restricted drivers worked on the 3rd install fine).

3. Stable. Struggling with Net Beans on Windows Vista. It kept crashing and loosing my work. NetBeans on ubuntu starts about 5 times faster and my work remains uncorrupted!.

However.......

My friend inpressed with Ubuntu decided to install it on his Asus X50RL. Oh dear, slow slow slow. It works ok, boots slow, desktop takes ages to load the icons and so on. We have tried to fix it, he is on his 4th install, he has given up and is sticking with XP. :(
