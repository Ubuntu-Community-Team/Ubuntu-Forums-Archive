### Post by stchman on 2011-02-07
Hello all.  I have been on Ubuntu Linux now for 4 years and am loving it.

I have 10.04 running on 4 different machines VERY well.  I have Ubuntu running on the following machines:

Core2Quad home build (64 bit)
Athlon 64 home build (64 bit)
Toshiba Core2Duo laptop (64 bit)
Acer Aspire One netbook (32 bit)

I do have Windows Vista and XP as dual boot.  I use Windows maybe 1% of the time.

Nearly everything I need to do I can do in Ubuntu.  No worries about viruses, spyware, trojans, etc.

I have converted a few friends over and they like it too.

Keep spreading the word people.
