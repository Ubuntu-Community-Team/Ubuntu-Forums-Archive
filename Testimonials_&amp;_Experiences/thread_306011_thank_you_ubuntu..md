Ive been messing around with linux for over 3 years now. Mostly shells, and remote machines from work.
 About 2 months ago i've totally switched to linux from microsoft @ home.. ive went through alot of distros.. slackware, bsd, fedora, gentoo.. list goes on and on. But i just cant turn to anything els other than ubuntu. Everything just "clicks" - and any software that i need to run, 98% of the time does not give me any fuss about working.

 keep it up!
