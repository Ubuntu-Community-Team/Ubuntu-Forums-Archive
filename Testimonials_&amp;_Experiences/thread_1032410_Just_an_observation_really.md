### Post by MickAld on 2009-01-06
Hello again folks, I have posted a couple of threads to do with upgrading my system and it led me to new adventures. Now I am not saying I have solved everything (with considerable help from this forum) but it is getting there in stages. As a result of this forum I have learned that mobo is short for motherboard but not only that I have managed to discover what my own machine is using AND I have been to a website (via technologies) and downloaded a manual which shows me all the layout and connectors. I have taken the machine apart and identified cables that were once a mystery to me. I have also tidied them all up with tie wraps so that they do not get in the way of other cables. 

I have discovered that part of my problem with using my microphone was down to the front panel connectors not going anywhere. A cable was connected at the panel but it went nowhere on the board. Of course that doesn't resolve the problem I am still having with getting the mic input on my new audio card to work. I used vol control to identify that it was switched off and easily corrected that, but still it isn't up to the mark. I will fiddle for a while longer. (maybe I should change my alias to Nero). 

I have learned about how to connect my usb cables and where they attach to the motherboard. It really is a great deal of fun and is giving me a new lease of life with understanding hardware and associated problems. 

My conclusion is that perseverance pays off, that one should not be too proud to ask for help and advice.
