### Post by phlembob on 2009-03-13
OK, I read a lot of people with slow moving video or bad sound and etc.  I have been using Hardy for a long time now, but I did have video problems with Intrepid.  After losing video 3 or 4 times with Intrepid I went with Hardy only.  Hardy has never had a speed problem of any type.  I have used Hardy on 3 different motherboards with 2 different processors.  Intrepid was used with 2 different motherboards and 2 processors also.  Hardy and Intrepid were/are both faster than Windows XP. Yes, most common software is made for Windows, but I don't play games on the computer so Hardy offers near everything I need.  I have learned to read closely driver support for add-on hardware items so not to choose un-supported equipment.  It makes me wish I were a programmer.  I would write my own for things not supported.
;)
