### Post by wishy77 on 2006-05-19
I am now running 100% Ubuntu. I have ordered Dapper Drake and will now sit impatiently waiting for it to arrive.
I am in the "senior citizen" (60+) category and am still cautiously trying to wrap my head around many of the programs and commands, but the old Athlon 2100 has remained rock solid from the first install of Breezy.
Many thanks to ALL the developers, special thanks to those behind Automatix...it made all the difference to a new linux user.
I have tried several Linux distros over the last 12 months, some with little success, but Ubuntu, and the great Community help files/posts, gave me the confidence to finally stop using Win XP altogether.
