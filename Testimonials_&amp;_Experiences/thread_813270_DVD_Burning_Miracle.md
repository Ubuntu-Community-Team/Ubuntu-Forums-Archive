### Post by Dwood15 on 2008-05-30
Well, I have a DVD+RW Drive, and my family keeps buying the DVD-R dvds because they use macs (dvd-rw drives) 

I was trying to burn some content to a DVD-R, miserably failing in Windows, so I said "why not try it in Linux?" after various attempts, failing with Brasero, I decided that there has to be something good in the Application > Add/Remove Section.

I find Gnome Baker, install it, and to my surprise, it worked! Gnome Baker worked whereas Brasero and various other DvD apps in Windows didn't work with DVD-R!

1. Thanks GnomeBaker! 
2. Why would it work with GnomeBaker and no other application I used?
3. Why wont Ubuntu come with GnomeBaker because to me it seems to have more quality put in it than in Brasero..
