### Post by Robgould on 2005-10-13
From the looks, this is just more of the same, but here it is anyway.  Ubuntu is great.  I have tried Feodra 3 & 4, slackware, debian, mandriva, and opensuse.  I generally kept migrating back to fedora which is quite good, but takes some work.  One of the main reasons is yum.  Finally I tured ubuntu...almost did not, so glad I did.  This is the ONLY distribution that I have tried where everything just worked on my laptop.  Everything.  Atheros sound card, Alps touchpad, ATI graphics card....all worked.  Getting that working in Fedora is several hours work, and the touchpad is not possible that I have found....there are may threads about it in their forum.  I have been looking for my Linux "home", and it looks like this is it.  That is my two cents.
