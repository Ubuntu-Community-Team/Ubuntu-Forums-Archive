### Post by kraziyk on 2012-02-13
So about a week ago I got tired of the slow speed with my computer. I, like most people, hate slow computers. Worse than dial up connection.

Anyways I decided to run a dual boot of Ubuntu 11.10, and I fell in love with the start up speed alone. I can boot, login, and start using what ever program or do whatever in roughly five to ten seconds.

Bam! +1 for Ubuntu, 0 for Windows.

Next my wireless internet felt faster. I use Chrome, and it just felt like it loaded faster. Maybe it's just me, or maybe not. Either way.

Word docs worked better, and faster. Video editing is iffy, just because I cant use a main stream program such as Adobe Premier Pro, or Sony Vegas. But not a huge thing. I can do a work around, not including Wine. Same goes with photo editing. No Photoshop.

The whole no photoshop thing, was a huge thing for me. The one big down side of Ubuntu. I know of the Wine program. I haven't really tried to use it, though I have it installed. So hopefully I can get Photoshop working.

Finally the lack of games, primarily MMO's. Bummer, but not a big deal.

After all this I got to say I won't be going back to windows. So much so, that I booted Ubuntu onto my pc, and got rid of Windows. Now this is kind of a big step. But I have a windows pc still, that is typically my back up pc. I also have access to Macs. Which are better than windows pcs so.

I love Ubuntu, the community is better than the windows community. The lack of  official tech support sucks, but the community is more helpful than any tech support I have ever worked with.

So all in all I give ubuntu a 4.5 out of 5 stars.
