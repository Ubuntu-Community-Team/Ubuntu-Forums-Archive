### Post by indytim on 2006-07-22
I just plugged my Cannon SE30 PowerShot into my USB for the first time with Dapper.  I expected next to nothing as I did no prep work.  To my amazement, the camera was detected up front and and I was quieried on where I would like the download to be placed.  I won't bore you with the gory details of what I had to do in the "other" operating system to get the download feature for the camera working.  Needless to say, "that" effort was significantly more that what was required in the Dapper world to get things working properly.

Another nice illustration of how computing was meant to be.  Good work Ubuntu team!!!

IndyTim
