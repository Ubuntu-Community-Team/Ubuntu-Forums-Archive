### Post by shashanksingh on 2011-05-15
The thing that I miss the most in my ubuntu is the Office experience, i.e, a neat and aesthetic document processor.

I sometimes switch to Windows 7 just to get the "neat" feeling while working with documents. LibreOffice (OpenOffice before) is just not soothing to the eyes. And its not just while working with .docx files that it is understood to have compatibility issues. Even making a new document (irrespective of the format) is a much better experience in MS word.


I love open source sofware and am proud to use it. I have no other problems and I am very much at home with everything else, just this one part is something I really hope improves in Ubuntu to make it a great desktop experience.
