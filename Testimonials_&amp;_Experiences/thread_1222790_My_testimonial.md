### Post by SLEEPER_V on 2009-07-25
I will start with saying that I have my main rig running nothing but ubuntu 9.04.  I love it when compared to windows.  The freedom to do and change is astounding coming from a windows only environment.  It is a distinctly different feel than win xp, vista, or 7.  The bad things are that there are issues.  Mainly with drivers, random freezes and wine.  Oddly, firefox has been the bane of my existence.  It crashes seemingly every ten minutes.

In conclusion, I will stay with ubuntu because of the overall experience.  It is one of freedom, but with that freedom comes cost in regards to chasing down every little issue on my own.  Its kinda fun though.

Rick
