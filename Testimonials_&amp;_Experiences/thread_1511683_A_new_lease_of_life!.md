### Post by CurtisJK on 2010-06-17
I've recently installed the netbook version of Ubuntu 10.04 on my HP Mini 2133 last night after being sick of how slow it was under Windows XP.

I know it isnt meant to be a powerful machine but it was just too slow! It had been sitting on my desk for about 5 months unused as i just couldn't be dealing with how slow it had become. So yesterday I thought I'd give linux a go.

I'm sorta new to linux (but i do have a lot of knowledge on computers) but the first version that came to mind was Ubuntu.

When I came to researching it, I was impressed from the start at the fact that there was a version optimized for netbooks!

The install process was easy via USB and everything worked after i rebooted. The only thing that didnt was my broadcom wireless chip, but after plugging in my ethernet, the driver popped up from the internet within minutes.

The best bit - it's not sluggish! Sure it suffers from the occasional lag, but what machine doesnt? And besides, its only a small laptop!

I can finally have IM, Chrome and Music playing simultaneously without any slowdown!

All i can say is thanks for an amazing Operating System - I've already got the hang of it and I've not come off my netbook yet!!

Curtis
