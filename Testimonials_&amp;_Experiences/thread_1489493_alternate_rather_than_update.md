### Post by armandh on 2010-05-21
one by one I am changing my linux computers to 10.04
but since my update experience has been like a 
"Hey watch this" moment, I am alternating the OSs

right now the atom/ion box that feeds the family room flat
screen is getting an new 10 gig of unpartitioned space
from the largest storage partition. 

that is where 10.04 will go, and I will configure it as I like it.

some day in the future the other 10 gig OS partition 
will be wiped and some future Ubuntu installed.

given my experience with upgrades why give up a working OS
until the next has settled in, and does what I want?
