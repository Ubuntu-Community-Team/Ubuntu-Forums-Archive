### Post by Mason Whitaker on 2008-12-28
Thank you Canonical, and all other the people who contributed to making such a great flavour of Linux. Ubuntu 8.10 completely knocked me off my feet as soon as I booted up the Live CD to install it to my laptop. It recognized my drivers without needing me to tinker around with it. 8.10 also recognized my wireless card, whilest Ubuntu 8.04 did not, which personally showed me that some programmer took the time to make it work on my driver. 

For Christmas, I was given the Ubuntu Ogio bag and the Ubuntu Wireless mouse. I hope to carry these around at my school, and hopefully I'll get some questions on what Ubuntu is. I'll do my part to spread the Ubuntu message.
