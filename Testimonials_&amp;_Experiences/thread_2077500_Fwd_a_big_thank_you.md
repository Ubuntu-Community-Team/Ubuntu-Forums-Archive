The following is from the Vancouver Free Geek mailing list, but I think that it's as much a thank-you to Ubuntu as to Free Geek, so I'm posting it here for you:

> 
Hi Free Geeks,

This is an official "thank you" note for the volunteers who have helped this incorrigible non-geek convert from windows to Ubuntu!

You see, I used to be a rather nasty girl, the type who'd sneer at those overly-friendly sales people in Future Shop (as they tried to sell me more and more external hard drives because my stupid windows operating system was slowing down. Again.)

But now? Now I have a spring in my step. I say "hello" to passers-by on my way to work. Why is this, you ask? Well! 

Last night I easily held 4 people's attention in a noisy pub as I asked 'if anyone here has a computer that runs too slowly?'. I waited for them to lean in close and listen..then I bragged about how fast my $50 desktop runs. (I bought it second-hand at your store!) Page after page comes up-boom,boom, boom! instantly. I'm watching streaming videos in high definition-boom! instantly...it's called Ubuntu. And it's free. No antivirus needed. Updates? Yup, that takes about 3-4 minutes to do. I tell these people that, and all 4 mouths drop open.

I have spent some time getting to know this operating system, but that's time I would have spent learning the ins and outs of the next windows vista, windows 7, 8, 9, 10, windows bore me senseless with your constant updates, trojans, spyware this and that, and which bloody antivirus software is the most reliable this year that will catch every conceivable whatever worm, I mean bejeesus murphy!

Never again will I hang my non-geek, farty-arty head and cry, for now I have the fastest OS on the planet, and it's all thanks to the volunteers at Free Geek.

Thank you so much. I will be promoting your organization by word-of-mouth any time I get the chance, for this un-geek is now a believer :-)

Cheers,
