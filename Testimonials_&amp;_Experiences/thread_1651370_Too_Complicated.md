### Post by inobe on 2010-12-24
too complicated was actually sarcasm, it was the opposite, it was too easy, almost idiot proof "no offense intended" :D

things like opening a browser and seeing a dialog containing extra functionality options is a surprise to me.

i didn't have to hunt down drivers/ modules, didn't have to compile anything from source, everything was done for me.

i didn't have to search for an nvidia driver, the opensource driver produced a pretty decent 3d desktop with affects.

all too simple from installing the distro to now, in fact it wasn't much of an experience, i didn't have to learn much to achieve this fine working system, navigating around is quite explanatory, everything makes sense, everything works.

since then i was looking to expand/ upgrade the kde desktop, i thought that would offer a challenge, i was wrong on that too, opening packagekit  and adding the ppa repo then refreshing the sources was all it took, the upgrade packages appeared in packagekit, very little effort and kde is upgraded to 4.5.4

folks i don't know how else to put it, it's just too easy.

of course with any operating system, individual results may vary :lol:
