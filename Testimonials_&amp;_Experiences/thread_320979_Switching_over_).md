### Post by bobslaede on 2006-12-18
I started at my current job, about 4 months ago. This is not a computer business at all, more like a "wal-mart" for, lats say, carpenters.

So far we have seven compaines around the country, we're a pretty big player here (Denmark). With 50+ pc's, and more to come. We have been using illegal versions of windows, and ms office for ever, which is not great. I pointed this out to my boss, and our "head-of-computers" guy, and we all agreed it wasn't good.

At one point, a couple of weeks ago, i told my boss about OpenOffice.org, and how we should try it out. My main point being, that it was free - he likes free stuff :).

I installed oo.o on his workstation, and he couldnt tell the difference.

Then i proceeded to tell him about linux, and how that was free aswell, no legal ties, and whatnot.

He seemed quite interested in it, and i showed him the basics of it. I was using it on my workstation at work, for my webpage, and internet related programming and testing.

Getting to the point, now we have a couple of Ubuntu machines running as a test, in one of our departments, and its going great. I managed to get all the mail from outlook, over to Evolution with no hassle, and the people using it now are impressed.

Hopefully within the next six months, we will be switching all our pc's to Ubuntu.
