### Post by L815 on 2008-07-29
I'm excited for this new version because the newer kernel supports my laptop hardware better and other reasons also.

Since I dual boot, I decided to put the alpha on here and see it grow.
I like the simplicity of gnome, but the speed of xfce. So I heard about lxde and decided to give it a try on this new fresh install.

The fonts on default with lxde are amazing!!!! So smooth crisp, readable. The terminal font is very smooth and not jagged like my other experiences.
I'm not sure if this is an intrepid thing or a lxde thing, but I didn't know fonts could look this good.

The fonts are on all defaults, nothing added or changed. This is probably a weird testimonial to say the least, but I'm just so exited! My eyes don't hurt!
