### Post by thordk on 2009-10-14
I absolutely love Ubuntu! So glad that I finally made the switch from Microsoft. I was running Vista Home Premium. 
There are several reasons why I made the switch. For a long time I have observed the business practices of Microsoft and don't like what I see, and they only get worse as time passes. Customizing Vista UI is a pain. Then BSOD began showing its ugly face. At one time I had the pleasure of having to call Microsoft, because out of the blue, my key had stopped working and I was asked to re-activate my Vista. Then, the network problems I had, drove me nuts and over the edge -- constant pauses and other strange things occurring with the network.
So, I installed Ubuntu. All my problems are gone. The network is rock solid with my 3Com 905. Big smile on my face. No stability problems either, which to me, indicates that my hardware was not at fault, when I had the BSOD problems in Vista. Anyway, I could go on and on.
I just wanted to thank all the people who have made Linux and Ubuntu possible. 
I could easily recommend Ubuntu to my friends and family. It's easy to use, security is good, and it's really nice looking.

My system:
Ubuntu Jaunty 64
Gigabyte GA-MA78GM-UD2H
AMD Phenom 9600
4 gB memory
ATI onboard HD3200

I had problems with the onboard graphics after installing the ATI driver. The problem was horizontal lines flashing across the screen constantly. I solved the problem by updating to the latest BIOS.
