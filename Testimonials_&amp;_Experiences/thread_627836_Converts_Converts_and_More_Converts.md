### Post by datadrop on 2007-11-30
I have been using Ubuntu as my sole operating system of choice for the last 2 years.  Recently my parents had a problem with their windows 2000 computer.  After a virus attack and other issues I decided to show them  Ubuntu Gutsy Gibbon on my laptop.  Actually I loaned it to them for about a week so they could use the internet.  After the 3rd day my father called me and asked if we could put Ubuntu on their machine.  I really didn't think this question would arise, but oh well we gave it a shot.  Everything installed without a hitch including sound, network drivers and video drivers.  I installed Mozilla Thunderbird for email and setup separate profiles for my mom and dad.  Their habits of checking email, weather and news made it excellent for what they did.  After a week of general questions and pointing them where to go in Ubuntu vs Windows they had it down pat.  My father is learning really quick.  Being they have never used anything but Microsoft products, it was quite interesing to hear my dad say "We never have to reboot anymore."  Everything just works when we go to use it.  I told them it's much more stable than Windows.  

My second convert of the week was my wife.  I do consulting work everyday with Microsoft products.  She is my worst client ever.  One night she asks me to look at her laptop.  She just couldn't get it booted up.  After messing around with it for a while she was getting upset that it wasnt fixed right away.  She needed it to do some work from at home.  I gave her my laptop and setup what she needed, while i fixed hers.  About an hour and a half later she poked her head in my office and asked why she couldn't run Ubuntu on her laptop.  I told her it would take a day or so till I could get it all running for her if she wanted that.  Long story short, she loved Ubuntu.  When I got my laptop back she had turned on desktop effects changed the background to something fancy and was chatting away on pidgin.    Her laptop is an Inspiron B130.  When loading Ubuntu the only hitch was the wireless card.  Ndiswrapper took care of that.  Lets just say she is So happy with Ubuntu that she never wants to use Windows again.  

My 3rd convert of the week was my toughest.  I wrote [an article on my blog about this if you want to check it out]("http://www.data-drop.com/2007/11/30/helping-the-neighbor-surf-porn/").  

All in all 3 Happy Ubuntu Gutsy Users this month.  I'm pretty impressed it came that easy.
