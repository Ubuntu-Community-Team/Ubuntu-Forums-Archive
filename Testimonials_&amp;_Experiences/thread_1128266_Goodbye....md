### Post by demoflare on 2009-04-17
...vista! Finally! Made the right move. Posting this for personal preference, and to encourage others to make that important switch :)

Recently been using a dual boot with the "vista" that came pre-installed on my laptop. And i noticed something not quite right... almost as if vista had a mind of its own. Corrupting partitions? Well, I'm not sure what, but i fired Microsoft from my computer! A fresh install of ubuntu on "one partition" has changed my laptop forever.

Here's the differences I've noticed;
I'm never getting horrible meaningless error messages!
I have an amazing customisable desktop, which doesn't leak memory!
Never being nagged for pointless functions!
Tidy file structures & easy to locate programs!
Never, ever, had a "scare" from malware, worms, viruses!
Disk writing/reading speed is FAST AS HELL!
And i don't have to put up with "Are, you sure you want to do this", "Click yes if you are sure, that your sure, you want to do this"!

As for Microsoft, i think its easy to say... I'm never letting you into my home again.
Thank you Linus Torvalds, Canonical, and the Linux community. You have all opened my mind to the endless possibilities of open source.

The best thing of all, has to be... EVERYTHING :D
I'm so happy right now lol. And looking back at the Windows Experience... i can say, it was not a good one ;p

Thats my personal opinion, and i'm sure alot of people think the same.

~Ricky :popcorn:
