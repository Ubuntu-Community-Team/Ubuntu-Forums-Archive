### Post by tarek on 2007-06-17
Just wanna share my feedback with everyone on Feisty. 
I used Dapper then Edgy and now Feisty. I couldn't install a fresh copy of Feisty cuz I kept getting a frame buffer thing and takes forever to load and then doesn't work. So I used Update Manager to upgrade to Feisty from Edgy and now I find Ubuntu is a lot slower. Firefox takes a long time to load and uses 100% of the CPU if I open a link in a new window or watch a flash video. Eclipse is also slow and not just at startup but even while programming. I used the new commands to download codecs to run movies and videos but they did not work, I had to use Edgy's. OpenOffice spell checker didn't work until I found a tutorial to fix the problem. Overall, it's slow.

Don't get wrong, after years of using Windows, I love Ubuntu but so far I feel like Ubuntu is getting slower with each new version. It's like Windows, new version is worse than the previous one.

Thanks for Ubuntu's team, great job and hope the next version will be better.

tarek
