### Post by azimuth on 2008-05-01
> **gwachtel said:**
> I'm a satisfied Fiesty user. I was able to get over a few glitches such as wireless card driver etc. . Is there a good reason for me to move to Hardy?

If you are happy with Feisty, keep it. If you would like to explore Hardy, then dual boot. You'll never know what you're missing if you don't at least sample it. Even the nay-sayers around this forum shouldn't object to you informing yourself. Not everybody is having the problems reported by the vocal few and some of us, after a sample, are no more likely to go back to an earlier Ubuntu than we'd go back to Windows. If Hardy doesn't work for you, the extra partitions are easy to integrate back into your current system. The way things look on my laptop, it's the Gutsy partitions that will get the boot.
