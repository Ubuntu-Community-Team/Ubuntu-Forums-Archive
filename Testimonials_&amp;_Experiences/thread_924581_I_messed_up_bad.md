### Post by Therion on 2008-09-19
Install a secondary hard drive.  Nothing expensive, just pick up a cheap little "junker" drive somewhere and install it.  Thrift Stores can be gold mines for parts on the (way!) cheap.

Use this drive for nothing but experimenting with new distros if you're going to be distro-hopping.  Keep your primary drive disconnected while you're playing, and with a distro loaded out to it that you are generally happy with, and jump around all you want using the secondary drive.  If it bursts into flames, who cares!  Your primary is there, loaded and good to go at a moments notice.
