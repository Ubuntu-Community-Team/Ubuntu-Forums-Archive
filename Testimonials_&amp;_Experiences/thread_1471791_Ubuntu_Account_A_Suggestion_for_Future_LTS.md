Having bookmarks waiting for you in opera is a great thing. Why not have programs/plugins wait for you with a fresh install? Another benefit with Opera links is the ability to be cross platform. The links I save on my smartphone is syncronized with my desktop. It really is convenient!

### Post by denham2010 on 2010-05-04
The functionality is already in synaptic.

Open Synaptic, in the file menu you can save and load "Markings" (think it's the file menu, not on my Xubuntu box at the moment).

Save the markings (make sure you select all files not just what has changed) and put the file on a usb stick, or in your home directory (providing you aren't wiping that with your fresh install).

Do your fresh install then load the "Markings" in synaptic and click apply, bingo, all your previous apps are re-installed.

Cheers.
