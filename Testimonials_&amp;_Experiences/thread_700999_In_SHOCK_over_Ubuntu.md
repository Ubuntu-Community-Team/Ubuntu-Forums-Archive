### Post by Black Market Baby on 2008-02-18
I downloaded and installed Ubuntu while i was making dinner a few hours ago. I am absolutely in shock over how easy it was. Internet, sound, everything works.

This is so vastly different from my previous experience installing SUSE linux a few years ago. That time it took me a whole weekend just to get my printer setup, and i never did get any sound.. then the whole thing reverted to the german language and i couldnt figure out how to get english back. 

But this time... Ubuntu is actually easier than installing XP! I was on my fave websites, listening to mp3s and chatting on msn faster than i wouldnt have been if i had done a reinstalled of XP. 

I was sure i wouldnt be able to have internet because ive forgotten my username and password and settings for for my DSL connection.. but it all just worked without asking me for anything. Are you sure this is linux??

All I can say is, this is amazing. I'm still in total shock. 

And yes probably i'll have a few issues down the road and maybe even have to *gasp* edit a few config files, But so what? All the basics are up and running perfectly which is totally and completely different from what i expected from linux. 

:KS:KS:KS:KS:KS
