### Post by stinger30au on 2008-07-22
It has taken me about a year and a half now, but i have finally gone 100% ubuntu. The final piece of the puzzle for me was last night buying "turbo print 2" 

[http://www.zedonet.com/en_p_turboprint.phtml](http://www.zedonet.com/en_p_turboprint.phtml)

this allows my Canon i865 to not only print from 300dpi to 2400 dpi but allows me to clean my print heads and give me ink levels too.something the standard ubuntu drivers unfortunately do not do.

yee-haw. 


no more do i need to either purchase or run any antivirus software that turns my pc in to a  steam driven unit cos its so slow. thunderbird takes care of spam so does the filter from my isp. i dont have to worry about mal ware any more. ever since i installed ubuntu on this pc it has *NEVER* crashed or frozen or skipped a beat in any way shape of form.


loving it.

Thank you Ubuntu team and thanks so Mark Shuttleworth the man behind it all.

Oh yeah and of course, thanks microsoft for all the memories :-({|=, you will never be in this house ever again, good bye, so long, and good riddance.
