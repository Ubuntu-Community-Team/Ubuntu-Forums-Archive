### Post by Romanus81 on 2007-08-24
and I am addicted to eye candy.
I used to be one of those people who would look at linux and laugh. I was happy with windows, but I was SO tired with the Windows desktop. It was boring, bland, I tried every piece of free software I could find, but nothing worked. I hacked my way to try and find the best desktop I could get.
Then I heard about ubuntu. Linux? Me? No, never. LiveCD? Are you insane? Why should I give m address to some website so they can send me free software? It's probably like those stupid free AOL CD's we used to get when I was a kid. And then partitioning? Forget that. 
Then I was looking around Download.com, (for eyecandy) and found out that they had gotten a new download called Wubi. No partitioning, can be uninstalled through ADD/REMOVE programs, no risk. I tried it. Installed it. It took two hours, but I did it. I finally got it downloaded, set up on my pc, and I was exploring. 
So I clicked on the "Desktop Effects" option in the menu bar, enabled them to see what it did.
My windows wobbled. I was in love.
The rest is history.
Now, my school computers use windows and macs, and I try moving my windows, and it looks as if my monitor is movie bricks. It's so UGLY! It hurts to watch. I can't believe how much I rely on compiz now. 
And with windows, everything was broken, I mean everything, every single piece of hardware I tried to install, didn't work. I had to go online and go through Microsoft's help page and look for help on forums and it was just annoying. It hasn't changed with ubuntu, (I think it might be possessed hardware or an evil motherboard) but with ubuntu, I almost have fun, I don't know why, maybe it's the desktop effects, but it's like a puzzle I have to solve. 
Thank you, ubuntu forums community, for everything.
