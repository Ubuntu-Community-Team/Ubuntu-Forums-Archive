### Post by Aetherius on 2006-12-05
Oi,oi dear ubuntuers!

Once upon a time I bought myself an nVidia gForce 6200, and was blessed with a tv-out. 'Twas not long until this tv-out was roundly pumping a nice 1024x768 to my old 21" Bush. Then I wrote a simple little script to automatically play video files full screen on my tv.


But this was not enough.

I endeavoured to learn more of the myth of MythTv, and after some minor footering I had a nice version of it running on my system. Then after some X-Server indentity issues, MythTV finally decided to start up at boot on my TV.

YAY! but the best was yet to come.

I bought a Sony Ericsson k800i, and after some self-education I got it syncing with evolution and remote controlling my mouse. Nice. But then on the sony website, i stumbled across official documentation telling me how to write my own remote control profiles for the phone!

Now a matter of days later I have a mythTv setup, which is not only totally controlled by my phone via bluetooth (this includes all aspects of mythtv, the mplayer controls, dvd player controls, music player controls,etc) I also have set up a SNES emulator within MythTV and have managed to get my phone to work as the SNES controller!

(I also have electricsheep, which will run when a certain key is pushed on the phone, allowing me to put on impressive visuals instantly and .....remotely :D)

So YAY for ubuntu, YAY for mythtv, and YAY for that evil money grubbing barrel of fiends that are SONY.
