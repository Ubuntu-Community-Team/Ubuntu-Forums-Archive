### Post by candtalan on 2011-02-11
PC PRO magazine recently ran a multi page item on Ubuntu, which has good reports, although I have not been able to get one to actually look at.

But, more - the staff later had an 'Install and use Ubuntu' day, and this blog entry with detailed comments from staff is excellent, well worth a full read.

Anyone associated with our excellent Ubuntu development will hopefully find this snapshot to be a priceless gem.

The initial advice they got was correct - it *is* crazy to expect an instant migration from Windows to Ubuntu in a production environment - in a day, and get the day's work done! But they, and Ubuntu, survived to live on, with honour.

Qudos to them for the experiment. 
Qudos to Ubuntu for being what it is.
Qudos for the power in our elbow!
