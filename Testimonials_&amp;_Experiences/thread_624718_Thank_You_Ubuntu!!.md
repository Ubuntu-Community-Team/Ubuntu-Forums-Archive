### Post by drjulesd on 2007-11-27
Just a quick one to say thanks for Ubuntu!!  Never have i been more happy with an OS, everything about it, well...just works!  I'm not a gamer, nor am i a technical linux boff; i'm just a casual user such as browsing net, music, pics, vids, work and the odd game of tali.  My wife is the same as me.  Been using Ubuntu for about 18 months now, at one point i ded revert back to Windows due to printer probs, but had it sorted within a week, so Ubuntu was re-installed.  We both CANNOT do with using windows now, in fact we despise it with a passion!  We both feel safer using computer online now, love the fact that it's so much faster than windows, everything's FREE! It's so user friendly and there's always help for you if you need it.  I use a laptop with 256 ram, 1.3, 30g hd and it runs sweet as a nut, and since i've just put Ubuntu 6.10 on...wow, impressed.  

So, why can't everybody know that there are alternative operating systems out there which blow Microsoft out the water?  Even if Ubuntu isn't their choice, it may be SUSE or Mac, whatever their choice, let it be heard! TV advertising, Radio ads, internet ads on all major sites like Yahoo homepage, MSN, Youtube...anything! Grab the nation by the face and thrust alternatives in their eyes!  

God Bless Ubuntu
