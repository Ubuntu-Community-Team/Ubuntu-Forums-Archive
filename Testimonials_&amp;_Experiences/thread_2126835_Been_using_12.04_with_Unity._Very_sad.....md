### Post by Sesshomurai on 2013-03-18
Sadly disappointed. Despite its attempt to be 'easier'. maybe it is for computer novices but that's not the majority of linux users.
Therefore we all get screwed. Let's break it down.

1) I have to use the keyboard more. The gnome shell in all its glory and wisdom is faster for keyboard jockeys, but swapping between keyboard and mouse constantly is annoying. before, I could get by with just the mouse **OR** the keyboard.

2) The unity toolbar is really lame. I realize it takes up less space than the window bar and separate launchers,etc, but at a price. Confusion. Those tiny little glyphs to indicate number of windows?!? Really?!?! Who thought of that. I now have to tab/wait/tab or make multiple clicks to get to a subwindow. How is this better than **ONE CLICK**?

3) Gone are my built in draggable widgets on the screen top bar. yes, I can kludge them back (weather, stocks, etc.) but why have we regressed here? 

4) Menus only on the top of the screen like old fashioned macintosh? **Really**?! How is this better? Now I am having trouble knowing what window goes with the menu I am looking at....the whole point of having them all self-contained is that there is no confusion. Plus, for a window that is tiny at the bottom, I have to mouse all the way up to the top and back down to the window. Lots **MORE MOUSE MOVEMENT** than before.** How is this better?** This is especially annoying in Terminal when you have multiple ones open and don't know which one the menu is for...so you end up double checking every time. This, in practice, takes twice as long now.

5) Again with shell. Now I have to "search" for everything when I used to "Browse" for it in simple menu or docked toolbar? This might be better when I'm looking for something I don't use often, but for everything else, its a pain.

6) Despite the attempt to make unity bar informative, it is not. I hardly ever know how many apps or windows I have running.

A handful of people got bored with the old linux and decided to change it.....at the detriment of the masses....

My opinion.
