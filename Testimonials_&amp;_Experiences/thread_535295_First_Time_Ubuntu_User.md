### Post by scawa on 2007-08-26
I want to thank you for the usability of these forums.

I am migrating from Gentoo Linux to Ubuntu to test it out.  Having used Gentoo Linux for 4 years, I am not an unexperienced Linux installer or user.  I like the Gentoo Community and have learned A LOT about the internals of Linux there.

I am upgrading some hardware (new video card, Matrox TripleHead2Go, additional SATA Hard Drive) and setting up a dual boot with Windoz XP (some of the programs I use for my job can only be found on Windoz or I would get rid of it all together) and Ubuntu Linux.

I know the advantage of searching out all potential problems and asking the right questions **_before_** you begin an install. 

Each time I have begun to post a question in a new topic, your forum comes up with a list of "[COLOR="Red"]Here are the similar threads we found:[/COLOR]".   So far, I have found the answer to my questions in that list.

This is an excellent feature for a forum and prevents multiple conflicting solutions to the same problem.  I haven't installed Ubuntu yet because all the hardware had not arrived (should be here Tuesday or Wednesday), but I am looking forward to having an excellent system up and running quickly (Unlike Gentoo which takes several days and sometimes weeks to get up and running like I want even though I have a thorough log book of all the installs I have done there.)

Take care.  I will keep you posted on my progress in this thread.
