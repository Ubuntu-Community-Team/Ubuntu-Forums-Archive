### Post by gwachtel on 2008-07-27
Last year I installed Feisty 7.04 on my Thinkpad x20 pentium 3 laptop.
After mastering some tweaks, such as wireless configuration,
I was able to get a very good system up and running. But since
there are no software upgrades for feisty any more, I decided to upgrade
to Gutsy. It was a disaster (IPv6 and such) so I returned to feisty. 

Next, after reading some good reviews of Hardy, I thought its time
to upgrade to the LTS release. I installed 8.04.1, but I was disappointed by some malfunctioning features. First, music from rhythmbox sounded terrible.
Next, my laptop keys weren't enabled. And finally, speedstep didn't scale down my p3 speed, which is very important for battery life.

So I'm back to using Feisty, at least until I'll be able to fix these problems.
