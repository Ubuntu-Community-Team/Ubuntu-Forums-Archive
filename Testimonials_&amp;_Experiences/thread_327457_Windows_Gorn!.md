### Post by david_2001 on 2006-12-29
Can I just say that I finally made the move to using GNU/Linux and only GNU/Linux on my PC!  After 5 years dual booting between Windows (ME then XP) and Linux (first Mandrake then SuSE, Slackware, Debian Sid, Ubuntu Dapper and now Edgy), I find that I can do everything I need just with Linux and that Ubuntu is a neat distribution to do it with.

Specification:
Asrock 775Dual-VSTA
Intel Pentium D 820
1Gb DDR 400 RAM
XFX GeForce 7100GS
Samsung 250Gb SATA and Maxtor 200Gb SATA
KWorld DVBT-100
MSI TV@nywhere Plus
Epson Stylus C80
Epson Perfection 3170 Photo (yes, really - works perfectly with libsane-extras properly installed)

Sadly, the Windows applications haven't completely gone, as I still need to use Office 97 from time to time, but Crossover Office does the trick on that front.

Tada!
