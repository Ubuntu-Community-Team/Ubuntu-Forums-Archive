### Post by Trilogin on 2011-07-12
A while back I was attempting to get a second OS on my windows 7 computer and I wound up using OpenSUSE. I did not think it was very good because I had a lot of trouble customizing things and accessing things. I wound up getting rid of it.

Now I have Linux 11.04 installed on my second hard drive and I have to say, the Unity desktop is an extreme improvement. It's truly gorgeous and in my opinion it tops Windows 7, easily. I think Ubuntu should start a marketing campaign in the US and try to beat out Microsoft, this OS is simple to use and with all the competition being focused on the mobile market right now, I feel that now would be a good time to rock the foundation that Microsoft is standing on. People are leaving Windows for Mac and other platforms, Linux could really get a foot in the door.
