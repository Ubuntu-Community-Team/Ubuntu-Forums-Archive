### Post by wPwLUi3N on 2008-04-04
Hi Guys!!!

You must be thinking i am going nuts digging old articles, yeah maybe it was a stupid idea but here is why i did it?

Just give it a honest thought and do tell me if i am wrong and i will stop immediately.

I am with pure ubuntu for 1.5 years but the feeling of taking out of community and not giving back was bugging me.

I am a freelance hardcore web designer (part time) and Business consultant (Full Time) and thanks to god i have a big client base to serve, i have very good brand image of delivering beyond expectation. But the point is i am not much into software coding and OS coding.

So how to contribute feeling was killing me? In past i have bought beers for all my favorite developers, bought ubuntu gears, bought and distributed ubuntu CDs in past and even personally installed many. And I am proud to say i have more than **100 converts** to my kitty who have dumped windows for good adopted ubuntu or linux in general for a complete new life style by adopting underlying community philosophy.

**I did all i can to spread the word.**

Now i still wanted to do more. Since i am not into codes i cannot contribute into support section neither can i troubleshoot others problem. But then something strike my mind. I love to read ubuntu testimonies and success stories.

Then I saw a whole list of unanswered post in that section and i felt really bad. Why didn't any body replied to them. Why those endeavors of people to convert were not appreciated. If we have such a good community much like a family, why there is such that some people effort got ignored or unappreciated.

Then just a stupid idea stuck my brain >> **Why dont i answer all those post and make sure there is no unanswered post in this section.** After all it will not harm anyone. Will it?

This is the whole story which is the reason behind my digging old post.

Looking forward for your replies.

Also I will stop for now, till i get green light from you guys. Maybe i am doing some harm and not recognizing it.

But consider this as a small project for contribution  from a non-technical mind.

If i have done something wrong, i apologize in advance, but i did have genuine good will at heart. So please accept my apologizes. 

Thanks for Reading.
