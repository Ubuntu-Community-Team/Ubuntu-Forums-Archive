Hello folks

I'm a 22 year old student from Greenland who's currently in Denmark. I tried Ubuntu for about 4 months ago and I was sold. After about 2 weeks I finally decided to reformat my harddisk in favor for Ubuntu (the best decision ever). 
Since I'm using a laptop, Asus A6T to be precise, I've had some minor issues but they were quickly resolved due to the suberb support in the forums.
Since then I've been promoting Ubuntu to my friends and others in my dorm and I've managed to convince 3 people to install it. I've fixed their issues when they had them and I think they too have had a great experience with Ubuntu. 
My girlfriend back in Greenland work in a after-school centre and they asked me if I could install Ubuntu on their computers too. I offered to install it on a test-machine and do the rest of their computers if they liked it. The best thing is it's a payed job ;) So I'm looking forward to come home and work on their computers.

Many thanks to the developers for making such a great distro and many thanks for you guys for offering such great support.

Apu
