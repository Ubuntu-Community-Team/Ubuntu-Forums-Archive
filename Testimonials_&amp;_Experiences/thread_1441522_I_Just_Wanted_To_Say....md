### Post by amnite on 2010-03-28
Well im newish to Ubuntu. Being as me and my roommate share a computer I use wubi. My first few weeks with Ubuntu(Karmic) was a pain in the ***... It takes a little effort to switch and adjust to the way Ubuntu works. But there comes a point on the learning curve where you no longer need your ice-pick and rappelling gear to get menial tasks completed. And when that happens you fall in love. I did anyway... The freedom of everything being able to be messed with and tweaked at my whims is THE main reason i love Ubuntu. Sure im not going to lie and I had to do alot of research and messing to get it to run just right but i will tell you if you don't like a cage you'll find your shackles fall off once you make the switch. So all you peeps who threaten to give up Ubuntu your not hurting anyones feelings, only your self. Stick to it and have persistence and patience as your ally and you'll get there. I LOVE Ubuntu and TY to whoever made this wonderful computer nerd playground...
