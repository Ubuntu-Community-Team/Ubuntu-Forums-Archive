### Post by Lisimelis on 2009-01-03
Hallo to everyone! My name is Yiannis and i am from Thessaloniki Greece. I have been using linux for about a year now and even though sometimes i got stuck and frustrated i always found answers in the forums. The past month i decided to use Ubuntu exclusively and life has just been great. I ordered my cd from canonical and it came in no time. I wanted to be a full blown registered in everything user and I installed in my laptop Satellite Toshiba. Everything went smooth. What impressed me the most was that once i started to really get involved with the system it became addictive. DVD playback worked in minutes with the vlc player and i was really impressed with the screenlets and themes i could try out! Now i am looking to install one of those gorgeous mac like docs! So Thank you Linux community for making me feel part of something that seems to be the future and i promise to expand the use of Linux and Ubuntu in particular as much as i can!
