### Post by regonzal on 2009-05-15
I have stuck with Jaunty since the betas and am currently using Jaunty as my default desktop. Unfortunately, I do not know where I stand with Ubuntu given that I feel that notify-osd has taken a lot of the customization that I liked to do out of my experience as an user. 

I feel that the "box" does not go so well with my preferred icon theme and just does not look right to me. I wish there could be some way to customize it, but I don't think this is something that is even being planned for the end-user. 

I have used Ubuntu since 7.04 and just feel a little lost at the moment. Ubuntu has always been my Linux distro of choice, but I am kind of weary as to these recent changes.
