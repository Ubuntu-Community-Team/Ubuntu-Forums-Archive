### Post by ambie on 2010-04-29
My first post, im new to this community, please take my words lightly, they are an opinion, and im sure ill get more than one of you disagree, please mind the noob speak too, im no pro.

Things i like, the bootup is mind blowing. this old laptop which i rely on has never started so quickly, i miss the fancy startup screen which doesnt seem to have time to even display these days, which is great!
this was also the case with the liveCD, very fast considering the poor speed of my reader, however, the CD is automatically ejected before a shutdown/restart is completed, no real issue, but i can imagine the mile long list of I/O error would scare most people from putting the disc back in.

I miss the rounded tabs when you click on applications/places/system, no big deal, but going square seems to go against my expectations of lynx being the most visually polished, maybe im just being picky?

the speed of general browsing (for me), does seem greatly improved, however, im finding more resources being used, and the speed of firefox actually starting, is slower (following my use of 8.10>9.10)

wheres thunderbird? or infact any other type of proper email client?, maybe most people are using online clients, but perhaps i like my thunderbird, but ofc, the software centre gave me what i want.

i love 10.04, its buggy for some, but not for me. thanks to the developers for bringing another great release, and this community, for all ive learned by lurking around and learning, with your help, how to resolve every single issue ive faced with ubuntu, and infact most other linux issues ive faced :-)
