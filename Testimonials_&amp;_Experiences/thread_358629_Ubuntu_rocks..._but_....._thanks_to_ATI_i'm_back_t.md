### Post by rj686 on 2007-02-11
I love my ubuntu setup i really do. I've gone windows less for about two weeks. Completely no dual-booting or anything. I was actually quite proud of myself. But today i gave in, I have an ATI card and due to it's lackluster drivers i was getting terrible FPS in my games. Due to me being a gamer first I had to go back to the darkside in order to play WoW/Farcry/Civ4. I know wow runs in wine, and it runs well....except for BurningCrusade ( new expansion pack). My FPS crawls in BC and its somewhere between 3-5 fps which is not playable. 

I realized what a pain in the *** windows was as soon as i got back. No longer could i config things how i wanted, and get this, on my Asus W3J, and OEM copy of windows detected next to NOTHING of my hardware. Not my touchpad, not my x1600, even my Wireless remaind "unknown" to xp. Luckily i had my handy dandy drivers cd. What would happen if i had to re-install and i didn't have the drivers for my ethernet card? I'd be screwed. Anwyay I'd just like to praise the ubuntu team for making a wonderful OS. Someday when AMD/ATI gets their rear in gear, or cedega actually becomes worth the money, (right now wine offers better performance) i will be a permanent convert, i got used to gaim/amarok/ even gedit for c++ programs :). But for now i need to dual boot.


-Cheers
RJ
