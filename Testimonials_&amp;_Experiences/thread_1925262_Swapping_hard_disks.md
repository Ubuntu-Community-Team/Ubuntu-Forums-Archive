### Post by AlanR8 on 2012-02-14
This is just a mention of how adaptable Ubuntu is!

My old "server" is getting old, a P4 about 1.6 GHz I think, Several years ago I built a desktop machine for my son with a core 2 duo at about 2.6 GHz and lots of RAM. In its day it was fast!

So, I took the system disk and data disk out of the old server and bolted them into the desktop. Despite the completely different hardware, the system just booted up as usual with everything recognised! 

Awesome.
