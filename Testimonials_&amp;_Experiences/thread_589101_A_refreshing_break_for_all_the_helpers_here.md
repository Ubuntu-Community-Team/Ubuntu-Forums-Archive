### Post by ed-koala on 2007-10-23
No problems here, I am new to Ubuntu, and am very happy with it.  I realize a lot of folks have problems with hardware/software, but I found my experience with Linux overall to be a good one.

Sure, my printer won't work, but it isn't Ubuntu's fault, the maker just doesn't support Linux well on some models.  I keep Win XP around in case I need to print a few pages, which isn't often.

I've gotten a lot of help here and elsewhere on the web learning and dealing with minor issues ... I appreciate the help!

To all those who need help, and can't find an answer ... don't give up hope or give up on Linux.  The folks here are great, and work hard for free just for you.

The only way to keep M$ honest (if that's even possible!)  is to make Linux, and Ubuntu in particular, every bit as popular, or more so, than M$'s stuff.

Long live Ubuntu!
