### Post by Serpher on 2010-09-22
My grandfather constantly complained his computer running Windows XP was slow, especially on boot up, was fed up of getting it wiped clean all the time because of viruses, and just general difficulties with simple tasks like zipping up files. I did a wubi install Ubuntu 10.04, changed the theme and showed him around including how to install stuff, and he can do everything with ease, and he loves it. He loves how his computer turns on in a fraction of the time it used to take. Thank you Ubuntu.
