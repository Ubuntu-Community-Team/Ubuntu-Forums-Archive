### Post by grunge09 on 2008-01-11
Well after years of her using Windows and a few viruses along the way.  I backed everything up, and put in the ALT Gutsy Install CD and installed 7.10.  

Her PC is:

AMD 3200+ skt 939
512 MB RAM
120 GB HD
Geforce 5200 AGP
SB Live
CDRW and DVD Rom Drive

7.10 installed fine, installed k3b, Thunderbird, RKHunter, Firestarter (she wanted this one), Virtual Box (so she can do her taxes, and ebay turbo lister in an XP virtual machine).   Flash, Totem Xine, Nvidia Video Drivers (still working on getting Compiz working) & Conky.  7.10 automatically found her Minnolta Laser Printer and installed the drivers for it.  Also her camera card reader was auto installed.

So far she likes it, Made her some top bar links for her favorites, a few desktop links for her most used items.
