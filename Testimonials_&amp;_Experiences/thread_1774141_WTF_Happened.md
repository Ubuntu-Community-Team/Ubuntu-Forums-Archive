### Post by Stormcurse on 2011-06-02
I have not had any reason to upgrade to Ubuntu 11 before now, but I just got an aspire one and figured it needed ubuntu, as windows runs slower than molasses on the thing.

Seriously, wtf happened.  
Ubuntu used to be easily customisable, with a simple gui that made complete sense, and was rather intuitive.

With this new ubuntu and the unity panel and all, I can't figure out wtf is going on.  I LIKED ubuntu.  Why would they do this to my favorite OS?!!

And more importantly, how do I change it?  I can't figure out how to access the personalization aspects that existed previously.  If I could do that, perhaps I could make it so that, in time, I would like it... but geez...

When I first saw it, I thought "Huh. This could be interesting", and then I found myself stymied at every attempt to change stuff, and began thinking it was/is worse than the windows gui BECAUSE IT NOW IS.  It is not at all intuitive, and very difficult to modify.  Why they did this to me, I'll never know.
