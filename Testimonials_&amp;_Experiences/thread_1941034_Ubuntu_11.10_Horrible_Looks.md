### Post by electrojustin on 2012-03-14
What you are describing as horrible is the new desktop environment called Unity. There are other desktop environments out there, such as KDE, XFCE (a personal favorite), Gnome 3, and even old Gnome 2 (what Ubuntu used to look like). I believe you can revert back to this at the login screen after installing gnome-session-fallback; the option for this would be called Gnome Classic under the little gear icon. As for the other desktop environments, all you have to do is do a quick search in synaptic package manager (or aptitude or whatever you prefer) and select them at the login screen at the same place after installing and logging out.
