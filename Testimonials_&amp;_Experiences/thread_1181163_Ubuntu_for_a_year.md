### Post by Hastings101 on 2009-06-07
Last spring I discovered Ubuntu and ended up installing it on a second partition just because I was surprised there was something other than Mac OS and Windows, and I'm really glad I did.  Over time I learned a lot about how to use the system and have found that it functions even better than Windows ever did.  About two or three months ago I even removed my partition containing Windows and made Ubuntu my permanent operating system.  I'm really glad that there are people out there creating and maintaining things like Ubuntu and I'd definitely like to thank them for giving such a great alternative to the two mainstream operating systems.  Although I did have a rough time at first, these forums definitely helped a lot.  I'm glad that not only is this operating system a nice alternative to Windows & Mac OS, but also has a very strong and helpful community.  Thank you Ubuntu for allowing me to get away from a slow, buggy, and very impersonal computing world into a quick and friendly one :).
