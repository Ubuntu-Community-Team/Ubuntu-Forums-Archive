### Post by jethro10 on 2008-05-04
I dived into linux - ubuntu, over 2 years ago and bought a new, cheap PC to force myself to use it properly, But always found it crashed as much if not more than windows.
This Heron one (beta) was way worse, so I decided to investigate the hardware.
After a bit, I replaced the processor which ended up being faulty it seems.

I've been rock solid now for about a month. A totally different experience.
Who would have thought a processor could keep a PC going well enough but not quite. 
I was getting application crashes perhaps every day, getting kicked out of X once a week, and hard lockups about 1 per week.

Now I've had ZERO probelms.
and I'm happy :-)
Just shows though how easy it is to blame the wrong thing.

Jeff
