### Post by squirrel_chaser on 2008-06-14
Just thought I would post some very positive experiences with 8.04 Desktop and Server.

This past week I needed a to set up a tftp server to collect router and firewall configs.  I could not get a tftp server in CentOS to work.  Tried 8.04 Server and had atftp working in about 10 minutes.

I then setup a MoinMoin wikki on 8.04 Server to collect department systems documentation.  The setup was easy and worked great.

Both of the above projects were for work.

Also, I use vpnc a great deal to get into servers at work when I am at home.  Vpnc in 8.04 Desktop worked on the first try and seems solid. 

There is more to life than sitting in front of a computer monitor.  Hardy is producing time savings and workable solutions for me.
