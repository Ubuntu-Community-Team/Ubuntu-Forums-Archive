Good luck with that. The problems on your Fujitsu laptop sound like ACPI issues. I can assure you that the make and model of laptop does make a difference. Ubuntu runs better (and quieter) on my Sony Vaio than the pre-installed Vista, and I have had only minor (fixable) issues with an Acer Aspire.
