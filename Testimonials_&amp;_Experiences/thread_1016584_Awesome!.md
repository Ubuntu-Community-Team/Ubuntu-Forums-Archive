### Post by lazman on 2008-12-20
I just installed Ubuntu and I am truly impressed! 

I have been using windows 2000 for, well about nine years now. I decided to give linux another look and just installed this. It's amazing how user friendly it is! I was up and running in no time! I didn't have to enter commands on the command line, there is a nice add/remove for software which is better than windows I think, because you can just type in what you want and click a button and it's downloaded and installed. oh ya; if you need something else to make it work I was amazed to see it downloaded that as well!

I have been writing software on windows for a while now. I'm curious as to how that is going to work out on Ubuntu.

 I just had to share my amazement at the ease this got up and running. Duel boot even! 
Awesome job! Truly Awesome!
