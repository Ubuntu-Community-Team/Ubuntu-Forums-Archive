### Post by andy b on 2013-01-12
Hi All:

I'm new here. I just installed my 5 year old Brother printer on my Ubuntu computer after many trail and errors.

PC: A 13 year old Dell Dimension XPS B866r, Pentium III, 866MHz. 384 MB ram.

Dual boot, 2 separate hard drives, one with Win98SE and the other with Ubuntu 10.04 LTS.

After getting all kinds of messages with the included drivers (Broken, can not install) I was thinking my ancient Dell was just too old. Then I happened across these files and tried them with great success!

[http://packages.ubunut.com/quantal/text/brother-lpr-drivers-bh7](http://packages.ubunut.com/quantal/text/brother-lpr-drivers-bh7)

Installed the A2ps and then the bh7 Cups wrapper and lo and behold my old Brother MFC-240c printed out a test page!
