### Post by jimv on 2008-06-04
I've had Ubuntu on my laptop for a year, and I never tried printing until the other day...lets just say that I had some bad experiences printing with Linux a few years ago, and I was reluctant to try it again.

Anyway, I used my wife's new HP printer.  I didn't think it was going to work because it needed 100mb driver package to run on her Windows machine.  But lo and behold, I plugged it into my laptop, I saw a bit of HD activity, and BAM, the printer popped up in the available printers, and I was able to print my doc on the first try.

I have to say, I was impressed.
