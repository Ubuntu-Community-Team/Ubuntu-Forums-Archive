### Post by armandh on 2009-11-12
I have come to enjoy Ubuntu and 99% of the time it just works for me, but today was not a good revelation.

A new computer store has opened up in the area and I know this because I got a ad in the snail mail. included in the usual PR & map was an offer for a FREE 4gb usb stick or 4gb SDcard.

well who can pass up free? So I go, and I find a well organized store with lots of nice new stuff open just 4 days. Lots of help and a guy with enough time to walk me through the "U build it" parts shelves. just hard to walk by 160 Gig hard drives for $47. opening days sale at $42.

near the end of the walk through the "U build it" area there is a shelf of OEM XP-Sp3 and 7 OSs, and it dawned on me not a bit of Linux anywhere in the whole MicroCenter store.

BUMMER !!!!!


PS yep with 500 Gb drives on sale at $50 and 1 Tb on sale at $80 the larger is the cheaper/mb
