### Post by freebeer on 2008-04-08
Nice.  For #20, I'd say "Upgrades are installed automatically ** if you want them to and with your permission**."

While not strictly a "reason", you might want to mention the Live CD and how it lets your try it without committing to it, or changing your current installation.  Then perhaps mention about dual booting.  I'd probaly put that at the end in the "conclusion" section.  Basically, you've given them reasons to try Ubuntu, now you want to "close" them, and the Live CD and dual boot help overcome the last objections of risk ("what if I don't like it", etc.) and inertia.
