### Post by absintheminded on 2008-11-05
It's been a rocky month since I first set up a dual boot XP/Ubuntu configuration on my machine. 

Tonight has been the final straw. I've had crashes, freezes, uninteligible text commands, GRUB problems, a myriad of downloads, graphic card driver problems, CPU problems, it's just been one thing after the other. No less than three times have I had to reinstall Ubuntu completely.

Frankly this OS is too much worry for one man who just wants to check his email and surf and game occasionally and to be pretty stable out of the box. I was delighted to find an alternative to XP, but I can't say I fell in love with Ubuntu. It's not ready yet. It's a shame because nobody likes Microsoft, but I stick with it because there's no plausible alternative - at least until I can get my hands on a powerful Mac... 

This board has been incredibly useful though. It's a great resource and probably the only thing that has kept me from going insane. 

So thanks for the help everyone, and goodbye!
