### Post by wv25m on 2008-01-22
After first using Ubuntu 6.10 about a year ago for some time and then switching over to Fedora 7, I am back using Ubuntu 7.10.  I have a HP DV9030 laptop (dual booting with vista) and the differences between either of the two previous distros and 7.10 is nothing short of amazing.  I have always had wireless/built-in webcam/printer(canon MP830)/touch key issues.  With 7.10, EVERYTHING works.  Straight out of the box.  Period.  The only minor thing that I haven't been able to figure out is why I don't have sound on Kopete webcam calls.  I have Kconsole installed and have the sound settings initialized properly... I dunno.  No big deal.  I love Ubuntu and with the way it has been working, I have absolutely no need for vista which I currently have on my other HDD.  This is by far the best OS out there.  After stating to use Linux a year ago I have been 100% converted.  This newest release absolutely seals the deal.  I love Ubuntu.
