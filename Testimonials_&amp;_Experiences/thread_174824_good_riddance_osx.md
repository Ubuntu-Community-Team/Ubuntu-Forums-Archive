### Post by b7j0c on 2006-05-12
i am sure it is rare to see posts ripping on osx, which is a geek media darling, but after weeks of degraded performance on my powerbook g4, hourly instances of the "beach balls of death", i capitulated and started up the dapper flight 7 live cd. it was nice to have my computer back. after a couple of days i decided to simply install from the live cd environment. i have my computer back. setup was easy, and in some ways easier. osx seemed to have issues talking to our local printer, ubuntu doesn't. system load is much lower than when running osx, everything is a little snappier. there is a huge plus in that my home desktop and my work desktop now are identical...even though one is a PPC laptop, one is a P4 desktop. i would think more businesses would see the value in a consistent interface regardless of the hardware.
