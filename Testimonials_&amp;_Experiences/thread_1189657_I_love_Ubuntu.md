### Post by mdgrech on 2009-06-16
I converted to Ubuntu around 7.04 time and have been loving it ever since.  I have a php development environment that mimics my web servers, along side an awesome Ruby on Rails development environment.  

I also have gedit totally pimped out, and have learned my way around the Gimp quite.  Every night I back up my hard drive to web server using rsync.

Prior to Ubuntu I had no experience with Linux, new nothing about terminal, and had never created a web page.  Since using Ubuntu my knowledge of computers has sky rocketed.  

Ubuntu is installed on our home computer, both my parents use it (they are 60+) and find it easier to use.  My parents don't understand the difference between Ubuntu and Windows, they just know its faster, easier to use.  

For my parents the real kicker is using super plus the scroll wheel on the mouse to zoom in.  Try it right now if you haven't its great.

So story told I love Ubuntu, it changed my life.  It has turned me into a total tech nerd who no longer has to do bs tasks like updating antivirus, or running antivirus checks, or playing w/ firewalls.  God I'm so glad those days are behing me!!!!  Go ubuntu!!!
