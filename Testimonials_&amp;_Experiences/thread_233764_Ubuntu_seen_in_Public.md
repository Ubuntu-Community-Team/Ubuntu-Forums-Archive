### Post by KFASheldon on 2006-08-10
Well as I have not seen a mention on the boards I thought I might as well have a shout.

Over the last few weeks Ubuntu has had a good ride in the UK shops, with no less than 5-7 UK mags covermounting it on their  included CD's.  Better still its not just your Linux techo mags either, PCW has it and a good artical in the magazine about Linux, and then there is a great KUbuntu exclusive magazine. That is sure to boost the numbers somewhat.

Top that anyone browsing the recent computer books table in Waterstones should see 'Begining Ubuntu' and 'Ubuntu Hacks' both great dedicated books. Make a change from Red Had and SUSE books that are normaly infesting the book shops.

And looking through the Linux bookshelf in Waterstones I find Ubuntu (Breezy) is mentioned quietly in most older books.

Anyone wanting a good couple of books to help tune, setup, adjust and mess with Ubuntu should go and get 'Ubunto Hacks' from O'Reily and a compliment is 'Linux Anoyances for Geeks' -O'Reily aswell- both great little books for new or old Linux folk. Just got them and they have good stuff in them - much easier to work with than searching the forums and google.

Begining Ubuntu is good, especialy if your very new to Linux.

Any one else spotted some good Ubuntu promotion going on ?
