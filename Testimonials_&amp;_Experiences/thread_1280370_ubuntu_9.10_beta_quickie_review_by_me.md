### Post by wildman4god on 2009-10-02
I haven't tried the installing software from the new software center yet but I believe the idea is you browse and click install and it starts downloading and then you keep browsing and when you click install on another app it ques up with what's downloading/installing currently, this is supposed to speed things up so stuff downloads while you browse. Also the new software center is much faster, add/remove to forever to search but this new one searches much faster, no annoying lag, very nice :) Also I too like the new colors and icons for the human theme, reminds me of chocolate, they tray icons look better as well.
