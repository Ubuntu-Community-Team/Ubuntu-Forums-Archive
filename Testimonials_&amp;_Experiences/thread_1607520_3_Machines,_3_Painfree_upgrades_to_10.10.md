### Post by StarLab on 2010-10-27
Ok, I have to say this was the easiest OS upgrade EVER! Hit a button, sit back, reboot. (Times 3)

Upgrade times varied between machines. From 2hrs on my old P4 2.0Ghz, to 1.25hrs on my P4 2.8Ghz, then finally 35 minutes on my dual core 2.7Ghz. 

I didn't lose so much as a password during the process. Network came right back and all was good. Picked up and carried on as usual. Kinda ticks me off I wasted time on all those backups. lol

This release marks my first anniversary of using Ubuntu. Been a blast so far! 

Kudos to the team!
