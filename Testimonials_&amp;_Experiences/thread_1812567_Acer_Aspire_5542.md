### Post by teeleef on 2011-07-26
I took a gamble with Acer and purchased their Aspire 5542 Laptop for my daughter.

Switched it on held down F2 to boot from the CD.  Hit the install button for Natty, re-partitioned the drive etc and hoped for the best.

A total and utter success!!!!!

Wifi, sound, webcam and microphone all without a single glitch.

Many thanks to the Ubuntu developers for their tremendous work and the Open Source ethos, and of course to Acer for a great Linux PC for £300.  It really does put the others OS'es somewhat in the shade.

Only disappointment was the Windows tax :-(
