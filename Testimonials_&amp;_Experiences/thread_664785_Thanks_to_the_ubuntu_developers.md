### Post by stevescripts on 2008-01-11
Just a word of thanks to this community.

For the last few weeks, I have been searching for an up-to-date Linux
distro, that would play nicely with my new Dell at my office.

Due to a current lack of availability of high-speed access at our shop location,
I have d/loaded (from my box at home) and tested many of the Linux live-cd iso images.

Although several have played reasonably well with this hardware and network,
Ubuntu 7.10 did the overall best job (to include getting this widescreen monitor
running at full resolution!).

I had only one problem post-install.  gcc and friends (ld, make ...) did not work.

Just a few minutes after searching through the Ubuntu forms, found the fix!
(although the d/load on dial-up took the best part of 2 hours :( ).

I have run and used several linux distros over the last several years,
and a couple of the BDS's.  This was the most painless install I have
ever run across.

So, again  THANKS!

Steve
