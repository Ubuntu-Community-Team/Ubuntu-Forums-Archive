### Post by kevinws on 2008-05-25
Hey. I just wanted to say that that my Ubuntu experience has been very good and it is because of the great help from the Ubuntu and Linux community. 

I picked up an EEE 701 a few months back. I did not like the way the default distro. To be honest I did not give it a chance. So I switched to eeeXbuntu after a day or so. That was a much better experience for me. I had compiz working it was really and impressive little machine. So impressive that a sold my 15.4 laptop. 

When 8.04 I came out I was not paying attention to what I was doing and upgraded by mistake. It bonked my system I was not that happy. I was thinking about switching back to XP.  Anyway I jumped on this forum and eeeuser.com and after a few minutes of looking I found that someone had put together a script that would fix the situation. Most of the post suggested a clean install of 8.04. Anyway I downloaded 8.04 and did a clean install. I followed all the suggestions that I found.  In about an hour I had a fresh install of 8.04. I immediately ran the script and after another ten minutes everything was working. I must say it felt like a new machine. 

There were still a few minor issues but all I had to do was jump on a forum and search. I never took me more than a couple of minutes to find out what I needed.  Even though I never posted a question on the forums I just want to thank all of y'all who post faithfully and keep Ubuntu going. 

As of today both my eee and my 4 year old desktop are running 8.04 with out any major issues. And I know help is just a few keystrokes away. 

Thanks
K
