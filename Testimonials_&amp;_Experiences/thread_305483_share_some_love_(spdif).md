### Post by Craig Caldwell on 2006-11-23
I just wanted to express my gratitude to the team that made the spdif for NVidia CK840 -IEC958 work. I've been using ubuntu with my computers since breezy and this is the first time I've been able to use my digital out.
All I had to do was:

System > Preferences > Sound and then change all sound playback boxes to NVidia CK840 -IEC958.

It was that easy.

I have a fresh install of edgy as of yesterday.

Woo Hoo!
