### Post by Comhra on 2008-09-21
You know, I backed Ubuntu up at the weekend and tried out PC-BSD 7 and it's nice but slow compared to Ubuntu. It wouldn't mount my: external, my sd-card or even my pendrive.
 
I was sure that I could work these problems out but finding answers proved surprisingly difficult.  

Log-out didn't work and switching the computer off was a bit of a lottery and required a hard shut down on one occasion.

These problems just don't happen in Ubuntu, for me, things work out of the box and a google around will solve most problems.

Thanks to the back-up, I was able to get Ubuntu back in record time.

If I get an old desktop somewhere, I'm sure I'll try BSD again, but my first impressions were that compared to Ubuntu and other Distros I've tried, BSD has some catching up to do.
