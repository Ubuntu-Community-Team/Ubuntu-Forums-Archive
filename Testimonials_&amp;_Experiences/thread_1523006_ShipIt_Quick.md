### Post by k3lt01 on 2010-07-02
A few weeks ago I ordered some free Ubuntu CDs and honestly didn't expect to get the 10 I asked for, I didn't even expect to get any. I also didn't expect to get them a little over 3 weeks from the day the request was made when the request page says 6-10 weeks IF you ask for free CDs which I did.

I'd like to take this opportunity to publicly thank Canonical, the parent company of ShipIt and Ubuntu, for such a brilliant service. I shall give these CDs away with bulk orders of the PCs I am refurbishing. This service helps to make it easier for people like myself to show that Ubuntu (and its derivatives) are a quality professional standard product that is worthy of consideration.

Thanks again.
