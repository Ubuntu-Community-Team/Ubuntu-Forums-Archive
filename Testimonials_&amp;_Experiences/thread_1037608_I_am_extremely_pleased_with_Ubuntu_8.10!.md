### Post by ogwilson on 2009-01-11
In the past, I've used versions of Ubuntu in vein as I had to a **** ton to get things up to speed (incompatibilities with my hardware, poor performance as a result of those incompatibilities, and a world of other problems.)

I just installed Ubuntu 8.10 and I got up to speed without a single hitch. No hunting down obscure fixes for my hardware issues, no having to change any files or settings, not a single thing. I also like the new changes to the overall OS.

More than that, it seems to perform alot smoother now (again, not sure if this is because of the hardware issues being eradicated).

I just want to extend a bucket of kudos and thanks to everyone who worked on this great release and made it easier for others like myself to get started with the Ubuntu OS.
