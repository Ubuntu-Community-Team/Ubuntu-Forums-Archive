### Post by trinitydan on 2010-12-28
I have installed Ubuntu 10.10 on five computers now with very few hiccups.  Nothing out of the ordinary.  There are only a few things I miss and they are admittedly really petty.  Like the login window manager, tweak doesn't really do it for me.  And didn't the screensaver used to give you an option for what it says in the text mode?  They should change it so the default isn't the kernel name.  To a novice, a bunch of random numbers followed by the word "generic" floating around in 3D is not nearly as cool. ;)  All in all, Ubuntu 10.10 is great!  I am very happy with the look and feel of it and for it's purpose as a intuitive, user friendly, and full featured OS, I think it works great.
