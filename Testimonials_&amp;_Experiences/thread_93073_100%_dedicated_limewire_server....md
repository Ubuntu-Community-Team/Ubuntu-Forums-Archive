### Post by fourchannel on 2005-11-21
Ubuntu is the greatest. I took an old dell, 450mhz 128m ram, 10G and 40G harddrives. (40 was my old one), and installed breezy and ever since it's been running LimeWire and Apache.

I personally love LimeWire and wanted a setup where I could start a download and *know* that I could count on it to still be on task 3 days later. Having limewire on wouldn't be much of a problem but I share this server with my dad.

It's also a good feeling to know that even though you don't have anything to look for, your system is always up so that someone else can download off of it, at any time. The whole sharing idea.

and it also runs Apache out of the LimeWire share directory, so that any files that have completed downloadling are then avaliable via the web. I like to stream a lot of mp3's from it while I'm at work.

but without Ubuntu there would have been a huge struggle to setup and install all this for me.


plus it's really freaking stable. Like, scary stable.
