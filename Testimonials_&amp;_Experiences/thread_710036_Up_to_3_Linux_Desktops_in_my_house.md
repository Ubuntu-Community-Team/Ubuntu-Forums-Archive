### Post by pbpersson on 2008-02-28
I have been using home computers since 1983.

I have been interested in Linux since around 2000.  Linux has always been way too complicated for me and I have always gone back to Windows.

Last year I tried Ubuntu and I was hooked.  Everything just worked!

Since that time I have played with Ubuntu and Kubuntu.....had a few weird issues....but for the most part this has been a breeze.

Recently on one of my desktops I had a problem with Kubuntu so I tried:

SUSE
Fedora
FreeSpire
Knoppix
PCLinuxOS

however I ended up back on Kubuntu and figured out how to fix my problem.

Now here in my bedroom I have a 32-bit version of Kubuntu 7.10 running on one machine and a 64-bit version of Kubuntu on the other machine.  Kubuntu is awesome.  :KS


Phil
