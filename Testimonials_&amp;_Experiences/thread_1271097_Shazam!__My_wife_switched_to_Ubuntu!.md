### Post by dean_z on 2009-09-20
I have to say, I have been a linux hobbiest for several years.  I am not the type of person that knows linux backwards and forwards but am a person that saw the potential in the OS.  Since I only know how to follow instructions of other users... I know what I have experienced over the years.  

My wife has never cared much for linux distros in the past.  Because of some issues that were having with her dell laptop, I expressed to her that we should throw in the live CD of Ubuntu and just try it out.  It was the first time in 10 years that she looked at me and said... can we load this on my laptop?  I think the neighbors heard my shout of glee!  My wife loves the Ubuntu and is totally impressed on how user friendly it is. 

So long Bill gates!  We are moving all of our compuers to Ubuntu!  She has a Latitude C640 and despite some mouse issues that luckily were posted... i was able to disconnect the touchpad so she could use a regular mouse with her system....  In fact all computers in my house except for my work computers that require windows for VPN because of the way my work has them setup are being switched today.  Eventually, there will be solutions so I can run the software i need natively from Ubuntu, but for now... they remain slaves to the Gates foundation... but I know that will end eventually.. hahahaha

A strong thanks to everyone in the Ubuntu community for all of your hard work!  I can't wait to learn more and more about Ubuntu now that we will be running it on a regular basis!  It's great to have a such a strong community behind this distribution.  Again... thanks!

Dean
