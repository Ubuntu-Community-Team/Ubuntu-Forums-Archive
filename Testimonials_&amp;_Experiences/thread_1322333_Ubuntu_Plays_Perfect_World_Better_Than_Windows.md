### Post by GryGhst on 2009-11-10
OMG its true Ubuntu through W.I.N.E does play PWI better than windows vista on my old and marginal notebook.
Under vista PWI struggled to run on my old notebook with all the settings turned down low or off, and would on occasion crash even though i performed all the usual tricks to optimize performance, it was playable however and i became an addict.
Windows also had the frustrating habit of breaking in to my game and alerting me to things it considered more important than running my game crashing or temporarily freezing the client in the middle of something crucial like a duel, ie "windows has updates ready for you NOW, would you like to stop everything your doing and do this instead...never mind your now doing it anyway love bill"
When i installed Ubuntu i kept my windows os as i thought it would be the only way i could continue to play my beloved game.
However i have discovered that right out of the box Ubuntu and W.I.N.E will not only run the client without fuss but also loading times are more than twice as fast and the client is more stable ie it has not crashed or frozen once plus i can turn on a few of the visual goodies previously denied me under windows, i can now play like the big boys and run PWI while doing other things like IM or web browsing. "some up to date hardware is required for this kind of thing under windows".
I dont know why this is but im grateful for it , im thinking Linux may handle memory in a different way i dunno.
I would add that the game appears to look crisper under windows but like i said this is all straight out of the box and i havnt begun to fiddle with settings yet as it has not been needed.
We have 6 users and all have stopped using their windows accounts in favour of Ubuntu 4 of these are children who are by far the most critical and demanding of things that either work well or dont, this i believe speaks for its self.
:D
