### Post by murderslastcrow on 2009-11-20
The new improvements in the kernel improving desktop speed, the new boot sequence, the Software Center, the icon themes, Ubuntu One. I'm like, on overload.

I mean, Linux was already like a theme park full of awesome choices to be made and fun things to do without a care in the world. And now it's even more so. SOMEHOW.

I swear, every new release so many good things happen I can hardly put it into words how the Linux community continues to find ways to further satisfy me. No wonder I love computers so much now. XD

Seriously guys, great job with Karmic. I love the improvements you've made with everything. Speed, sound, bluetooth, and the new programs are always a plus. But I dunno'... each release surprises me in a pleasant way. And this release is showing a level of completeness and attention to detail that seems to emanate futuristic and personal technology.

Linux is by far the coolest operating system. Ever. Just, seriously, never have I used a computer that just overloaded me with mental pleasure like this- maybe it's just because I'm a geek, but I'm not even doing geeky things with it.

Open Source will always serve us better than companies think they can serve us. I hope Canonical continues to be the companies that gives us the innovation we desire. To everyone in the Linux community and the Ubuntu developers specifically, thank you. This release is continuing a strong tradition of excellence, despite the few minor hardware hiccups that come with every brand new release.

I can't imagine Lynx being much better, but apparently it will be. XD
