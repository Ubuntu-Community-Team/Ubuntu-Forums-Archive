### Post by connorh123 on 2009-04-16
Hey guys, I gotta get tell you guys my story about Ubuntu.
Well, let's start from the beginning, and hopefully you guys won't get bored reading a leave.
I finally got a computer in my room, and was assuming Windows as software. To my surprise, it was software I was new too, "Ubuntu". I had no idea what this was, but I assumed it was somewhat similar to Windows. However, I was way off. Ubuntu, to me, is a challenge. It challenges me in everything I do. Whether it's solving a problem, adding onto my computer, or even changing the desktop background. I feel Ubuntu makes me work to solve my problems, which is what I like. I now know why I loved Windows, it was so simple to use, and I grew up with it. I thank my dad for introducing me to this wonderful software, instead of that Windows ****. Thanks for reading guys. 
Oh by the way, I felt I needed to tell you this because I went to a career center today, and a guy was talking about Ubuntu. So I was asking him questions about it, and it motivated me to do this. So thanks to whoever that guy was ;)
