### Post by Ububtubobl on 2010-04-23
My experience with Ubuntu after a few weeks has been a positive one. I had an unused P3 at work onto which I installed 9.10. Some issues with video card and Samsung monitor which went unresolved so I switched back to the VGA monitor and everything went fine. After a few days I installed Ubuntu at home without a hiccup. My wife's windows Literati game worked fine so she was happy and has had no problems as long as I put the icons to her programs on the desktop so she can find them. We have already been using Firefox, Thunderbird and Open Office for several years.

I can see no reason to continue to use Windows. Everything I need to do appears do-able with Ubuntu. I have six computers so open source makes economic sense to me. The learning process is , so far, no more difficult than Windows and learning something new is fun.
