### Post by Al Fairclough on 2007-06-22
Well, I have been using Ubuntu 7.04 for a few days now and the joy seems boundless. At every turn I discover something that Ubuntu does better than Windows.

One of the first things I discovered was how simple it was to change a theme. A couple of 'clicks' and it's done. I prefer a darker theme, so I went with this one -

[[IMG]http://i5.photobucket.com/albums/y178/Percival222/th_Screenshot.jpg[/IMG]](http://i5.photobucket.com/albums/y178/Percival222/Screenshot.jpg)

But that is far from all. I just love all the apps that are available. Someone did some serious work in choosing the default packages. The default 'Movie Player' works great, but I did discover that it doesn't play a BIN file, so in a couple of clicks, VLC was playing the BIN with no problem. As for sound, I am just loving Rhythmbox! I don't know what Ubuntu did for the sound, but I have never heard clearer and cleaner sound from my onboard chip. It sounds as good as a separate sound card. I can now listen to internet radio and it sounds a good as a CD.

And on it goes. A few clicks and a couple of lines in the terminal and viola, MB and CPU temps as well as fan speed on my upper bar. All I can say is "Thank you!" to all of the people who have contributed to this wonderful OS. It has put the 'fun' back into computing. :D
