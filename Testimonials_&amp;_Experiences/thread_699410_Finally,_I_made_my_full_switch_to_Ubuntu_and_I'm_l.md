### Post by Kivech on 2008-02-17
I have to admit that in preceding years I've tried several distros and always ended up reverting to windows due to lack of support of something in linux (be it software or hardware).

I can't express how content I am that now finally with Ubuntu 7.10 I can run my system problem free and it looks better than Vista, it runs better than Vista and it's faster and more stable and I can customize it to my hearts content in just about any fashion. Not to mention, the community is absolutely awesome.

I'm in love guys, this time I made the final switch and I just now finished customizing all I wanted to. And to be honest, I feel lost, lol. It's the first time in my life since windows 3.1 that I have a problem free stable system.

Thanks to all who helped and of course the Ubuntu team. You guys got yourself another believer.

Kivech
