### Post by Toz on 2010-09-17
The original thread is here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1576188](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1576188).

ATI dropped support of the X1300 card from their proprietary fglrx driver. If you have a problem with that, call ATI. The opensource radeon driver still supports the X1300. Have a look at: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1491346](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1491346) for instructions on how to setup your system to use this driver. If, while following these instructions you run into some problems, post back with those problems and we will offer our assistance.
