### Post by dskyracer on 2010-04-19
I just upgraded from Jaunty to Karmic after playing it safe and backing up all my files onto an external hard drive. I worried that some of the issues I'd read about with flickering screens and all would affect me but to my delight and satisfaction I have had minimum problems. I found most everything is working perfectly except for K3B which wouldn't burn DVDs for me though it did before the upgrade. I switched over to Brassero and found that it's doing the job I wanted. I may investigate the K3B problem later. I saw when I googled my K3B problem that many others have had the same issue and it was addressed somewhat in the forums. 

I know now that my choice in using Ubuntu over windows has been a wise one. I've found it to be stable, secure, and liberating. The community is also beautiful in the way it tries to promote help to others in a reasonable kind manner. Thanks to you all for the work you do to free people from computer hell. I admire you all.
