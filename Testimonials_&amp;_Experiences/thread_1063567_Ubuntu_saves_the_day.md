Just a quick note to show my appreciation to Ubuntu.  Was running Mint until I brought home my new HP Deskjet F4280.  Plugged it in, turned it on...nothing.  Searched the net for a few, couldn't find a straight answer, so what did that leave?

The Ubuntu LiveCD, of course.  Booted it up, turned the printer on...ten seconds later it was identified and printing.  A bit later Ubuntu was reinstalled and the printer works beautifully.  

I really don't know why I bother switching from Ubuntu....
