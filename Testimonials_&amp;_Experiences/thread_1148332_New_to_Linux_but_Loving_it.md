### Post by n5pjy on 2009-05-04
Hello all, I am new to Ubuntu/Linux. Never worked with it until about a week ago. And I am very impressed. My first goal was to get my Wireless card going, and it took a few hours, but voila! I now can download the updates for the 8.04 version. My next step was to download the updates. After about 5 hours (due to slow internet connection) that was complete. Now the soundcard is working, the AVP draivers are loaded, and I have begun loading various Amateur Radio programs to work with. So far as of this Monday morning, nearly 2 weeks of being a Linux user, I am operating HF (FLDIGI) and VHF (AIRMAIL) communications, and can get around the OS fairly easily. I am extremely Happy with this OS. I will give updates as they arise.
 
Thanks everyone for such a great OS
