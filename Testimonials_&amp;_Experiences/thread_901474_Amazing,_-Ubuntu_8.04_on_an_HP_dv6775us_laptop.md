### Post by ubuwatson on 2008-08-26
I have to admit, the latest release of Ubuntu is impressive to say the very least.  I installed Ubuntu a few nights back to replace Vista and out-of-the-box Ubuntu detected all my hardware:  bluetooth, wireless nic, webcam - you name it, it detected it. Man I sure don't miss Vista.  I do plan on running XP in virtual box for a few application that I may need from time to time, but Ubuntu is fantastic - it so fast, I enabled the visual effects and I am just so impressed with how fluid everything is.  I love it, the best OS I have used in a long time and I am an IT guy with years in the field using various platforms.
