### Post by WarrenKarl on 2011-05-01
Hi,

I just upgraded to 11.04 and everything went so nicely that I wanted
to say "thank you" and "well done" to the Ubuntu team.

1.  The upgrade from 10.10 was amazingly simple.  Everything worked.
I cannot possibly imagine anything better.

2. After reading all the discussion of Unity, I was prepared for a steep learning curve.  In 5 minutes, I was ready to work.  The interface is
intuitive enough that anyone who knows how to use a mouse can use it.

3. I love the use of workspaces.  It is better than the implementation in OS X and infinitely better than anything in Windows.

4. The integration of LibreOffice with Ubuntu One is much better than the integration of Office 10 with Skydrive.

In short, I am thrilled.  I cannot say "thank you" load enough!

Good work, team!
