### Post by weird_c00kie on 2007-06-13
I am of course talking about the new Ubuntu Feisty.

I started using Dapper (and Linux in general) for the first time towards the end of last year and really liked it, but I had to abandon it once the university semester started because I couldn't get it to print stuff properly (and I had a LOT of stuff to print)
Once again, the semester has finished and I'm back to Ubuntu for another try!

I'M SUPER HAPPY!

I did a clean install of Feisty 7.04 and so far everything is working beautifully.
The Beryl effects which took me hours and hours to get on Dapper before mysteriously losing them again took no more than 5 minutes in Feisty. Once I had Automatix 2 installed everything else followed as well.
EVEN MY PRINTER WORKS! For the first time I managed to print a test page and have it cover the entire page! (before it used to always shrink things to 1/4 of the page and only use magenta and black ink :/ )

I hope this keeps up. My faith in Ubuntu is wholly reinstated as of now :D


Keep up the AWESOME work and help me keep grinning like an idiot every time I show Ubuntu off to someone :D


*** EDIT ***
I forgot to mention... The "Burn" effect is the coolest thing that happened to desktops  since the 3D Desktop Cube with adjustable transparency :D
