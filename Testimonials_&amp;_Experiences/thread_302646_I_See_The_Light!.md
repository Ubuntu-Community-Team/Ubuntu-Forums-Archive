### Post by at0mic on 2006-11-18
using and experiencing linux is like waking up from a bad dream. a dream that was indoctrinated into my head by MS that it was the only reasonable solution out there for easy computing. WHen you wake up and see the real world for what it is and the choices and options that are out there its a powerful thing [reminds me of the matrix]. On my first contact with the ubutnu / lee-nucks community i was welcomed with open arms and was informed i would be assimilated. I was shocked how easy it was to install the OS, install anything i needed from the phenomenal syaptic manager in 2 seconds, as well as the insanely fast and helpful community assistance on any road blocks that came up. My desktop wallaper is now the "penguin army" from kde-look.org. Assimlation is complete. I now completely believe in the ideas, principles and community behind ubuntu. I was once one of those windows power users. now after witnessing the power , flex ,potential and freeness of linux i will now become a linux poweruser and help others realize the same. I am now constantly talking about how great and easy ubuntu and its community is and have gotten my uncle to install it on his laptop and he loves it too. THe first of many to be assimilated in my family and group of friends. I Thank you all for making it a highly enjoyable experience. I am learning the OS at an extremely rapid pace and i know I will continue to use linux for as long as i shall live and give back that which has been given to me.
