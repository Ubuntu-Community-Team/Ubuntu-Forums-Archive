Hi Ronald.

Since you are a new Linux user, may I hear what you think about Unity? Have you already compared it to other desktops like KDE, Gnome2, Gnome-Shell, XFCE, LXDE or others?

I'm not really happy about Unity, even if it was stable on my hardware, it still has some issues by design in my opinion.
