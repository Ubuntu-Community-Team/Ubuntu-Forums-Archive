Well, I thought I'd finally register and join this forum.  I've spent enough time 
reading it over the last 10 months, I figured it was time to start contributing.

As you've guessed, I've used Linux in some way for about the last 10 
months, before that I tried Red Hat back in 2000 for about a day.  
Anyways I got started in Linux mainly because of my major in school. I in 
the CIT program at my school and going into the IT career field I figured 
it would be good to familiarize myself with Linux.

only Windows I have is for my HTPC, but I don't think mythTV will be

Anyway, I just wanted to introduce myself and look forward to joining the
