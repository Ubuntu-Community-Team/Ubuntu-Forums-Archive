### Post by -Mentality- on 2010-10-19
I have never considered myself a big fan of Linus and have been one of the many bipassing Windows License for many years now. After a number of weeks now dual booting Ubuntu, starting originally with VMWare and being very impressed. I am now seriously considering a complete install of Ubuntu and dumping Windows all together.

My previous experience of Linux had been with Yellow Dog on the PS3 and I found it quite difficult to get my head around.

As an operating system Ubuntu is easy when it needs to be, but has the power of a developer platform, so it meets both audiences on every level. For new users it is so easy to get your head around and master. My favorite feature is the Ubuntu Software Center. I never knew there was such a wealth of free software out there that was in direct competition with Windows Applications that at times works better and is free with no stings attached unlike the malware and ad-ware that comes with Windows 3rd party programs. Meaning I have often been left with the only option to illegally download software that is overly priced.

Thank you Ubuntu, I do not have to resort to these methods anymore.
