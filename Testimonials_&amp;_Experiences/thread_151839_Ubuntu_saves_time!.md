### Post by teet on 2006-03-28
I tried ubuntu before I ever tried Debian (I had been a redhat/fedora guy before that), but I got curious one day and decided to give Debian a try.  After all, Ubuntu WAS based off of it.  I spent the whole night doing a base install, then apt-get installing gnome, firefox, openoffice, the 2.6 kernel, etc.  When I was all finished, I found that I pretty much had made an Ubuntu desktop the hard way!  Ubuntu really does save time!  It gives you a workable gnome desktop without a lot of hassle...throw in Automatix and you can setup a COMPLETE system in an hour or two (depending on your internet connection).
