### Post by AlanInVancouverBC on 2008-07-21
Today was the first day when I choose to boot into Ubuntu, instead of XP for my morning browsing session. (I dual boot)

It has taken almost a year, 4 re-installs, lots of failing, learning, failing, re-learning along the way.

While my usage is around 4-5 hours a day, most of it is internet, email, and the odd game here and there. 

With Ubuntu, Firefox, Foxmarks, Thunderbird, and Wine, I don't really miss the XP Experience.

Will I visit XP? Yes. Will I miss XP? Like the good old days!

I bought a good second-hand tower from Free-Geeks in Vancouver for my kids (ages 9 and 10). They are starting with Ubuntu...

I'm volunteering with Free-Geeks, so I can get my wife a good used tower for herself (instead of sharing this). She also will have Ubuntu installed. There! a family of Ubuntunics!
