### Post by izalac on 2006-11-16
I finally managed to get XGL+Beryl to work on my Dapper...

... I'm speechless.

...

...

... No, I'm really bloody speechless. To describe it as "impressive" would be a huge understatement.

Videos of Beryl in action on Youtube and Google Videos don't really give it a tenth of its deserved justice.

I'm getting real close to the point of making my dual boot computer to run solely Ubuntu. I won't be getting Vista for sure.

MacOS, Vista... Don't offer even near this much eye candy and customizability.

It's funny though. I got attracted to Linux because of whole ideology. Then I learned to love its power, architecture and available software. And now... XGL+Beryl managed to leave me completely shocked, in the most positive way imaginable. With cleaner, safer and more powerful system I used to have, now I got more eye candy than I ever thought possible, and it STILL runs fast on my old 1.8 GHz machine...

Could this get any better? :D
