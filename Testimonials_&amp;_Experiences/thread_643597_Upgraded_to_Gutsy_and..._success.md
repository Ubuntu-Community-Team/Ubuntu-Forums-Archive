### Post by JohnnyBoy022 on 2007-12-17
A few weeks ago I downloaded the 6.06 Dapper live cd and tried it on my Dell Inspiron E1505 laptop. I became very frustrated after the system failed to mount my internal hard drive. I also spent a few days trying to configure the wireless card (Intel PRO 3945) without any success. I decided to try the 7.10 Gutsy live cd to see if I would get better results.

To my surprise, me wireless led started flashing as soon as I started the system, and everything worked fine. I was able to mount the hard drive. Whether something went wrong with the Dapper cd or I just needed better hardware support, Gutsy worked perfectly.

I want to thank everyone in this community for the awesome support. Soon I will be attempting to install Ubuntu on an external hard drive. Keep up the good work!
