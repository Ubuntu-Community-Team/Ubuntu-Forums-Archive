### Post by randyklein99 on 2009-05-24
I've only been playing with Ubuntu for a couple of months now, but I'm just loving it.  I'm able to do things with it that I just couldn't figure out how to in XP.  

I've already set up a file server to hold all my family photos and music and can share it via samba with my wife and even ssh into it.  She's thrilled that she doesn't have to log into my side (in the XP world) to do anything with the pictures.

Of course, there was some growing pains and mistakes made along the way, but I was expecting that. It really helped that I did/do all my Linux Learning (LL) on an extra laptop, so the threat of data loss is zero.

My next feat is setting up a cron job to rsync all that critical data to an external drive. Then I'll feel complete.

Thanks for all the help I have received here. It saved many hours of reading man pages...
