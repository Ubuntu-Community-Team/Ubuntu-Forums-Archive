### Post by spitfirenut on 2007-08-26
A friend came to me with a dilemma.  Her son had trashed his laptop's windows install, his ipod had died and all of his music was still on the trashed laptop with no backup.  I decided to see if I could access it with the live cd and voila!  Accessed and copied to another box to burn to DVD for him.

I've been dabbling with linux since Red Hat 7.2 and Feisty is the best I've seen yet.  It just works.  I haven't booted windows on my primary PC in over a month.  Wifi was my only issue going from Dapper to Feisty and it was solved with Wicd.

Love it!
Don
