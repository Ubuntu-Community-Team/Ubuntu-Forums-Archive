### Post by HegonBadde on 2005-09-10
Thank You!

I have been looking for a linux distribution for 5 years.

I started off with Caldera, ManDrake, Red Hat, some odd balls that disappeared and I forgot their name. Then I found Suse 5 (maybe 6) Loved it. Bought 4 versions of it. Suse 8 killed me on Suse though. I switched to Red hat and had a paid Membership, but I wasn't happy with it. Then Fedora Core came out, and membership became too much expense. Then I moved on to Gentoo Linux, then Linux from scratch. 

At this point I really wanted to Remove all versions of Windows from my PCs and Servers, but I kept finding something that would prevent me. That has been going on for the last year or so. I finally knocked out the last thing I needed to completely switch to Linux on all of my machines.

A distribution I really find agreeable.  It's Debian based, so when I go under the Hood it is correct. Almost Everything is where it is supposed to be. In less than an hour after I finished burning the install CD, I was watching a commercial DVD and Updating the World of Warcraft client that I was playing online later that evening.

I had already cleared my entire weekend to Installing and Configuring linux. I had sworn to my wife that if I wasn't happy with it by monday morning, I would reinstall windows on my main box, and regulate gnu linux to all the rest.

I don't know what to do. I walking around in stupor, that in less than an hour I had completely installed and configured a brand new distribution, and the only buttons I pressed was "enter", and "down arrow".  I didn't need to know a darn thing about my hardware.

Now I can get on with my life. I can actually start Using the install instead of Fixing the install. My wife is impressed and she absolutely Hates anything but Windows XP.
I think it might actually be possible to talk her into installing it.

Thank You.
Roland J Rankin Jr.
