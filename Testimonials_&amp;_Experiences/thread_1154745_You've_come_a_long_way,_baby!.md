### Post by JiggyMan on 2009-05-10
I gave up on Windows years ago but used "other flavors" of Linux until a few months ago. I decided to try Ubuntu 8.04 and am now using 9.04. All I can say is...SWEET! I'm really loving this OS.

I only have one issue to resolve with my video capture card but I'm not ready to ask for help yet. I'm still going over newly discovered documentation (thanks to this forum!) and trying things on my own. Okay, I admit it. I trolled the forum for awhile before joining, gathering some very useful information on setting up Ubuntu.
 
And now you get a two-for-one sale: The only things my mom uses her computer for is email, solitaire and a little web surfing. She's almost 70 (but don't tell her I told you!). She was an XP user until a couple weeks ago. I installed Ubuntu on her computer, got her email set up, then had to leave for a few minutes. I went back to show her how to use it but I was too late. She had already read her email and was playing solitaire. She's finding her own way around. Now that's what I call "user friendly".

BTW, this is a great forum. Loads of useful information here. I hope I'll be able to contribute in some small way in future. Keep up the great work!
