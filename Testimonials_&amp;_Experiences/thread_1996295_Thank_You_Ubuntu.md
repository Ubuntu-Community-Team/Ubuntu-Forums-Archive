### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-06-04
I'm glad Ubuntu has been of use to you.

I know how frustrating it can be to be unemployed, and I'm sure it's even more frustrating when it's due to illness and there's little you can do about it. The last thing you need is the frustration of viruses, annoying notification bubbles and a computer that always seems to be having problems. I'm happy to hear that Ubuntu gives you no such frustrations, and I think this is one of the real advantages of Linux desktop systems. It's certainly the biggest reason why I'd never use Windows as a main operating system.

When I hear comments like this, I feel somewhat proud because I've tested development versions and reported bugs that have subsequently been fixed. Everyone who has reported bugs and had them fixed should feel very proud. They've contributed to your enjoyment of Ubuntu.

Good luck for the future, I hope everything goes well with finding new employment.
