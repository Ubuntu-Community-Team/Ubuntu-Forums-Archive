Story:

I bought XP Pro, Office 2003 and One Note retail for a used laptop with XP home.  I wanted XP pro for the wireless capabilities I had heard about (didn't really need them I guess)

Didn't use the laptop/apps enough to justify cost, so I wiped it and sold it with the original installed disks of XP Home.

I'm just tired of it.  I don't update XP anymore because of WPA. 
I've been reading forums like

Anyway, been learning and using ubuntu for about 4 months and can get by just great with Firefox and Openoffice on a dual boot machine.  I find I really don't need Office anymore.  Never needed One Note :) 

Dave

Again, another thing I thought illegal in the United States...monopolies.  Already they have the market of hardware...virtually every thing is designed for Windows OSes. So maybe I sound paranoid, but I honestly believe Microsoft's reach has gotten out of control and it just astounds me that information about their illegal practices are out there on the internet for anyone to see but still they buy the crap.
