### Post by Cyraxzz on 2006-09-21
Been around here for quite a while now, and never stated anything about my OS experience.

I owned Win95, Win 98, Win ME and Win XP before i entered the world of UNIX-like systems. I never liked any of the Win systems, from 95 to 2000 were just simply unstable and would fail to run some of my programs, and not to mention it's high insecurity, i'm not bashing Microsoft's products these are just facts. I started learning about Linux(books, online docs) in 2001, Gentoo was my first Linux distro and it was my best OS so far at that time. I switched to Ubuntu during January 06' and it has been nothing but pure excellence. There has been some reconfiguring but it was no effort for me.

the values of ubuntu which impress me the most are the following:

*General performance

*Stability

*Extreme security

Thank you to anyone involved in Ubuntu.
