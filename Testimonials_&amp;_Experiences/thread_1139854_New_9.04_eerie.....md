### Post by nateperson on 2009-04-27
I'm a relatively new linux user (1 1/2 year) after many (10+) years of microsoft.  And I've never seen a system run noticeably faster like mine did after I upgraded to 9.04 from 8.10, and I'm not just talking boot time.  Programs opening and running faster and what not... I mean, I didn't notice a speed change when I upgraded to 8.10 which I thought was pretty surprising, in that it didn't slow down.... but to have it speed up after an upgrade? And both times have not been fresh installs, just me clicking upgrade in the upgrade manager...  And I didn't reformat anything into ext4...  

Dunno....  maybe its all the medication I'm on... but its just been a bit eerie...
