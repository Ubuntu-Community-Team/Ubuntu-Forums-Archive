### Post by god0fgod on 2008-09-03
The first time I tried ubuntu was with my PS3. lol I truly do not recommend it. It did not work well at all.

As this computer is not mine I had to get permission to install ubuntu and that took time. While I waited I installed ubutnu on someone else's broken PC and it worked perfectly. I was very impressed with the mostly the efficiency of ubuntu. It lived up to everything I expected.

When I went to install on this PC I had problems with the live CD. It simply wouldn't work at all. I eventually after taking a while to find it installed Wubi. The first install worked but was ridden with bugs. No wonder why. It installed a stupid 64 bit version. Very stupid. i downloaded a 32 bit ISO and tried again. Now it works perfect. I had bugs with the audio and flash player but I fixed that.

I am very happy. It took me a while but I'm glad now.
