### Post by ergo-proxy on 2011-05-17
A week ago the last microsoft bastion, my pc was migrated to Ubuntu 11.04 and I'm very happy with it!

I love tweaking everything I want and Ubuntu give so many possibilities (sometimes in a very unexpected way ;)

I'm also very pleased to be a part of Ubuntu community!
