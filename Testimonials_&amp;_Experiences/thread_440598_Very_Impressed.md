### Post by kame on 2007-05-11
I really support open source, and I make fun of people who thought that Windows was the best thing since sliced bread, but I never really installed Ubuntu until just recently. And now that I'm using it...all I can say is wow! I was always afraid of recurring technical problems, but within the past 24 hours I've gotten the bulk of them ironed out with help from a friend, and this website (special thanks to the enabling multimedia thread in the beginners discussion). I am incredibly inept when it comes to programming and trouble shooting mostly anything computer related, and Ubuntu was made to make set up easy. Even the problems I'm having (which mostly seem to be Wubi related) are small enough to tackle. I was also excited to see my basic needs, like Open Office, Gaim, Rhythmbox, Firefox, even Gimp were already installed. That is so incredibly awesome, good job Ubuntu developers, and thank you for your help forum!
