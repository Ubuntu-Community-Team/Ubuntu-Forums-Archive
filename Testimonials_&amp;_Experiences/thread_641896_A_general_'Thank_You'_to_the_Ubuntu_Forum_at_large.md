### Post by Radius Vector on 2007-12-16
Greetings all, 

About a month ago I had begun the process of building a configuring a server for some light duties (backup storage, media streaming, video game servers, that sort of thing), and I decided that I was going to do it in a Linux environment.  While I'm an old Windows/DOS vet, the Linux (indeed, any *nix) environment was new to me; a little daunting, even.

After researching the various distros and testing one out (openSUSE, for those curious), I eventually settled upon Ubuntu, mostly due to the fact whenever I had a problem, my google searches would invariably lead me to this forum, and more often than not, it'd be the answer I was looking for, neatly wrapped in an easy-to-read howto, no less!

I'd like to extend my thanks to everyone on this forum; it's your hard work that truly helped me better understand and enjoy Linux. I thank you!
