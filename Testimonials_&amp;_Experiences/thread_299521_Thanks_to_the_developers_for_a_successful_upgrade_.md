### Post by jrjazzman on 2006-11-14
I had a successful upgrade last night from Dapper to Edgy.  The upgrade process couldn't have been smoother (I followed the instructions, i.e. update-manager -c).  The only glitches I've noticed so far is usplash didn't show up during boot and no tomboy icons were added.  I was able to fix both of these things pretty quickly.  Now I'm trying to figure out where alacarte went to.

Overall, this was the best OS upgrade experience I've ever had.  I give a  big thanks to the developers, sponsors, bug reporters, and anyone else who played a role in Edgy.
