### Post by BandD on 2011-04-29
I feel like I have to write this.  I have a eeepc 701 and put Ubuntu on it as soon as I feasibly could.  I fell in love with 9.10 Netbook remix on this little device and thought it was perfect for such a device.  I kind of use the 701 as my testing device since all I really do is browse the web with it. When I fist installed 10.10 netbook edition on this bad boy, it made it virtually unusable.  Unity was in an alpha state at best.  I honestly only used it a handful of times since October.  I recently installed the beta2 on it, and man, the devs have been hard at work!  The more I use it, the more I like it.  

Unity is really coming along at an astounding rate.  I think there is still some fine tuning to do, especially for a screen as small as the 701.  But I'm VERY impressed!  If it development keeps up at this rate, Unity is going to be some HOT piece of software by the next LTS.

My kudos to any devs out there.  You guys are kickin' some serious A!
