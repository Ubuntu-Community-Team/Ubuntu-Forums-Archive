### Post by coolglobal on 2012-06-17
I very much like the new LTS with Unity.  It seems that I will need to upgrade my computer (current is a dual core) to get the most out of it. Could certainly be snappier. My netbook is sluggish, so I went with Lubuntu. This is very good indeed.

One key thing that holds Unity back in my mind is the workspace switcher. I would love to see and standard powerful switcher just like gnome2.
