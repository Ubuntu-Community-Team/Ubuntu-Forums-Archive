### Post by llmunro on 2011-10-18
I realize that everyone's upgrading and posting about 11.10, but I'm still using 11.04 and continue to love it.

I'm in Guatemala currently and got a USB broadband modem for my internet needs.  I assumed that it would only work with my Windows partition which made me sad, as I really do prefer Ubuntu.  I was having persistent nightmares about trying to get it to work with WINE and read lengthy, complicated blog posts in which various people explained how they had gotten similar modems to work.

On a whim, I tried the USB modem with Ubuntu.  I plugged it in and IT JUST WORKED.  I am using it right now!  How happy am I?!

Ubuntu for the WIN! :D
