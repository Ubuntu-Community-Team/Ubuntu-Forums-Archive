I presume you used to use Windows before?

Imagine if you went back to Windows so quickly because you couldn't find the Start button on Ubuntu. Obviously you didn't do that - because you knew that Ubuntu was different and that there would be a period of adjustment, after which you would be a lot happier than when you used Windows.

The Unity desktop is a definite improvement over the old Gnome 2 desktop, but of course if you're accustomed to Gnome 2 you'll need some time to re-learn the new desktop.

You're right about Ubuntu Software Center being slow (I believe it's being worked on for 12.10) but if you want Synaptic back, it's still in the repositories.

You can't use Lucid forever. It'll reach End of Life around the middle of next year. You will have to adjust to something new - and it might as well be sooner than later.
