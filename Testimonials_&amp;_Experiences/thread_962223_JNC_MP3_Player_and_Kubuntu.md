### Post by CBrendan on 2008-10-29
Hi Everyone

So I just bought a JNC MP3 media player this week and I must say it works like a charm on my Kubuntu 8.04.  Kubuntu detected the player without any problems and it shows up with the cool "iPod" like icon on my desktop.

Once again Ubuntu does not seize to amaze me.  I am aware that this is quite obvious to experienced Ubuntu users as the player primarily works like a flash drive but I just thought I'd put it out there for everyone to know that Ubuntu works well with this type of device without any issues.
