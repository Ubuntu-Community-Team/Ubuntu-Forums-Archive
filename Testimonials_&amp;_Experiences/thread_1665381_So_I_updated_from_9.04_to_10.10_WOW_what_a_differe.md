### Post by grunge09 on 2011-01-12
So I updated my moms PC since it was a little slow, and WOW is all I can say.  Even my sister was impressed.  

Ubuntu 9.04 i386
AMD x2 3800+ CPU
1 GB RAM
320 GB SATA II HD
CDRW
1.44 Floppy Drive
Nvidia Geforce 210 

New PC is:

Ubuntu 10.10 64 bit
AMD 965 3.4GHz
2 GB Kingston Hyper X DDR3 Ram
500 GB SATA II WD Drive
Samsung Sata DVD RW
3.5" Multi Card Reader
Evga Nvidia 210

Wow what a difference.  Websites load literally in a snap of a finger.  Even the ones that were slow before.  I can view videos on youtube at 1080p with no stutter.  Before the max I could go was 720p and it would stutter.

I can open multiple JPEGS and it does not crash Gimp.  

E-Mail loads faster, well everything loads faster.

And I have HDMI Audio out now to my 55" LCD.  Could not get that working before in 9.04 Ubuntu.

Cost about 370 bucks to make the new machine case and all.
