### Post by RaiD_5 on 2007-12-22
I have been monkeying with linux distros for about a year now and really couldn't settle on one. One day I needed a nice SAMBA server for my local network so I decided to see what Ubuntu was up to. I downloaded Gusty and OH MY GOD! It is amazing! The new GUI effects are beautiful and lag free. I LOVE the add/remove utility, it makes finding and installing things with the correct libraries so much less painful.

That was 2 months ago and now I have Ubuntu installed on ALL 7 of my desktops and 1 laptop. They all work perfectly and are completely customized. And NO MORE Winblows partitions. 

Now I have been converting people I know to this Linux revolution and so far I have converted 5 people, all of them %100 satisfied. I love spreading the word of a beautiful operating system with a even better community. You guys beat the pants off Microsoft's help center. If I ever had a problem, this forum saves the day.

Games and Linux; The Ultimate combo. I have gotten %95 of all game I love to play to work on Linux with some minor tweaking.


You guys really do great work :) . 

 I am now official a pure Linux user because of you :)
