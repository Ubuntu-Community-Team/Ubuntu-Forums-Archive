### Post by whitefort on 2011-10-16
Back in the dark ages, I discovered an operating system called 'Dapper Drake'.  I loved it, and it helped me escape the tyranny of Microsoft.

I've been using Ubuntu ever since, but I'm afraid I'm another Unity-hater.  I tried to like it and couldn't.  Then I tried to get used to it and couldn't.  I hate the way my favourite operating system has become lowest-common-denominator and dumbed down.

So, with major regrets, I'm going to move to another distro (dunno what yet, though - I'm running Mint as a stopgap, and thinking of looking towards Arch, which I once tried and liked.)

However, I really, really REALLY couldn't leave without a final word of thanks to so many of the amazingly kind and helpful people in this forum.  _***When people draw up lists of Ubuntu's features, they usually forget one of the most important ones:  The help that's available here in this forum.***_

So, probably my last post in this forum, but my last word is to repeat a heartfelt THANK YOU!  There are some amazing people here, and I'll miss you!
