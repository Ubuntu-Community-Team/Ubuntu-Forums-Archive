### Post by rasmus91 on 2009-02-06
Yes the title says it all i know. I would like though to share my experience of Ubuntu Linux with all of you.

For over a year now, I've been Dual booting Linux (ubuntu) and Windows (both XP and Vista) I've had Vista on this laptop ever since i got it. I hate vista, I mean I really hate it (but it still made me quit).

I've been having some good experiences with Linux, I've been reading a little bit about programming, and enjoyed every bit i learned. and thanks for that. 
For all the time I've been using this OS I've always known, that sooner or later i didn't wanna do any Dual booting any more. And I don't.

As I said Earlier This laptop came with Vista, and so I've been Dual booting. 4 days ago this computer got totally messed up, (not only software but also hardware) so i delivered it to get the hardware repaired. and so it did. In the mean time i made my decision for what I would like to be the (only) OS on my computer.

And now, Based on my decision, and my skill with computers, and the help you've given me. I'd like to say thanks and Goodbye and **** you! To who?

Thanks to: This forum for offering help, you sure did a better job than Microsoft ever did. Thanks for the passion and compassion for my stupidity.

Goodbye and **** you: Windows, **** for all the "blue screen of death" and all the stupid problems with a solution that didn't help me the least bit.

To this Forum I'd like to say: as you've properly already guessed i chose Linux. First of all i hate Vista, But it wasn't easy letting go, as i have all my studying programs in Vista. But now, i feel free!

I'd like to say i look forward to learn more about Linux, and that if it wasn't for you guys i wouldn't have made this decision. (I'm of course counting on your help later on as well :P )


Well, i guess thats it for now. once again thanks for all the help, you guys are the best!
