I really love this thing that I've installed on my box...

In fact I have got loads of Linux Variants with me...
I had this Ubuntu CD with me for a long time... and for some reason I didnt realise to experiment with it except with the LiveCD... Anyways I forgot about it... till two days ago...

I have had Fedora Core 4, Mandrake Linux, etc. with me ... I gave test runs with Slax DSL... But none compare to Ubuntu... because of the following reasons...

Ubuntu comes in just one CD... This is one very +ve point...
Not much time required to install unlike... 4 FC , or 4 Mandrake 10.1 CDs...

Ubuntu has this Synaptic Pkg Manager(apt-get), which is still the best experience for me... Like, in fedora core yum yumex sucked... there were lots of problems while updating and doing stuff, and often I got missing dependencies...
With synaptic and all that I have installed, I have had no problems yet and my System is running so smoothly...No extra hot-potch... I mean... I program in Java and pgSQL, etc...Whenever I need something I just download that through Synaptic...

Basically, I feel that its nice Ubuntu is a minimalist and simplistic system, and doesnt give troubles when you need to expand...

Plus I really love the warm affectionate concept of Linux, ie., Linux for Human Beings... The soft system sounds.. all combine for a nice pleasant aestheic Linux...

I just want to say thanks to the Ubuntu Team...
Great Work Guys... Keep it up... :D
