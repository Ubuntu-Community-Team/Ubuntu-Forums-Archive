Hi Guys,
Im at University and have worked a LITTLE with Linux (Fedora) in the terminal, writing a few shells etc...

Anyway, my **cheap** laptop is starting to get on a bit now (2.5 years old) so I thought to give it a new lease of life- put Ubuntu on it.

I did have a problem booting (which I've posted about) which got solved when it updated.
I also had a problem getting on the internet. My wireless and Ethernet etc... installed fine- but there was no VPN support (which is the only way I can get on the Internet at Uni). So I went to McDonalds and downloaded everything I needed- and now its all great (fingers crossed).

Im still getting used to it, but I am really liking it! 
My mates have taken the Mick out of me (because they could never get Linux working on their machines) and they are all Windows fanbois.

Yeah! LOVE it!
