### Post by Off Topic on 2010-07-14
It is to me.  I have not been disappointed by Ubuntu.

Back in April I switched both my home PCs,  a Dell and a Sony,  over to Ubuntu and later put together a Ubuntu sever out of an old Acer Aspire to take over for my HP Mediasmart Server.  I even put Ubuntu on my moms Acer netbook.  I tried putting Ubuntu on my moms Dell 1720 laptop with Vista and that was disappointing,  couldnt get the wireless working.  Both PCs are faster and more stable then they where with XP.  The server has faster transfers and downloads then the Mediasmart Server it replaced.

The Acer netbook with its lower specs with Ubuntu outperforms the Dell laptop with Vista in every aspect,  speed,  networking/wireless,  audio/video,  and extras that dont bog the system down.  Thunderbird worked flawlessly with Ubuntu and is flakey with Vista.

I cant wait to get the netbook back and this Dell can go back to collecting dust.

At work Ive been offering Ubuntu as an option to people who are tired of getting viruses and malware on their Windows machines.
