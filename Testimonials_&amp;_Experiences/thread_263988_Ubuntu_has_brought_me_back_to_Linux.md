### Post by dkline on 2006-09-24
I'm a windows guy.  It's what I work with and what I know.  I've tried Linux in the past and it was an uphill battle.  Hours/days spent just getting it connected to the net, getting the monitor to work... I was looking for a change and thought I would give Ubuntu a shot.  I'm really happy so far.  In fact it's going much better than expected.  I'm running Windows on my laptop, and I have VM workstation 5.5 installed.  In three days working here and there I have installed a desktop, a LAMP server, with Webmin and other basic apps to help a windows guy get around.  The remakable thing is everthing is working...  My Ubuntu VM's are sharing the wireless connection on my windows laptop and seamlessly getting to the net. The display/gnome works on first install no tweaking. FTP,Apache, port forwarding all good.  Thank you Thank you Thank you Ubuntu community. This is fun again. I hope to be able to start to contribute to the community in the future as my ubuntu skills grows.
