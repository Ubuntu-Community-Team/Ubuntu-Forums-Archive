### Post by hoppipolla on 2010-05-14
Overall... excellent! I love it :)  I love the theme, I love the social features, the sound it gets out of my little laptop sound hardware is so crisp, I'm sure it sounds better than my old Ubuntu version and Zenwalk managed to get out of it O.O

However, I have had some issues ._.

It totally hung with my graphics hardware in this like, 8 year old laptop, I had to do a couple of workarounds in the terminal and stuff.

The sound is all stuttery on start-up, I don't know why.

I can't log into MSN at the moment but then neither could eBuddy yesterday, I think they changed something.

Oh, no Wine by default, that's a bit bad o.O  I thought we were gonna get some fancy new Wine integration?

Lastly erm.. oh, yes software installation is still a bit slow.


Other than that, honestly, very, very good. Incredibly smooth, clean to use, and it runs so well on such an old laptop, Windows Vista or 7 would have probably both failed on here, at least with 3D window rendering. The Software Center is great, so is Empathy and the built-in social broadcasting :)

Top stuff, well done Ubuntu devs!

Hoppi :)
