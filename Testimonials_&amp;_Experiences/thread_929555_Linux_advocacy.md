### Post by xeth_delta on 2008-09-25
I believe there are a large amount of advantages derived from switching to FOSS, for example not being locked in a strict software ecosystem (MS) with a certain set of software, as well as respect for genuine international standards (ODF, as a previous poster mentioned) and not pseudo-standards proposed and pushed by dubious means (MS OpenXML).
In other words, it sets you free of another's company agenda.

Other advantages you could mention have already been mentioned but I will post a few sites I hope you find useful and that would not only help you explain why it Linux might be preferrable in your environment, but also why it is different from Windows, and that being different is not bad:
