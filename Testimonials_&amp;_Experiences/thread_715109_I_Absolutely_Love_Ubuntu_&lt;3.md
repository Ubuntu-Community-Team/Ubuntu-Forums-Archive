### Post by crazygopedder on 2008-03-04
Let me start off by saying hello to everybody here :)  I'm new to the forums, not to Linux though.  I run a Thinkpad T43 with a 1.86ghz Centrino 5400rpm 60gb HD, 2gb RAM, and an ATI X300.  I've tried many other distributions in the past, but had problems with certain drivers and programs.  Well, Yesterday I installed Ubuntu 7.10 onto the ~10gb free I had after cleaning up my XP Pro partition.  EVERYTHING WORKED OUT OF THE BOX.  Wi-fi, ATI X300, Compiz(!!!), Firefox, Pidgin, OpenOffice, my 802.11g HP Deskjet 6800(!!!), etc. etc.  I couldn't be happier with this distribution.  I only had to go into the terminal once to sudo apt-get xgl-somethingorother after system update broke desktop effects lol.  All I had to do was Google search the problem and I found the solution in a minute.  A big thanks and props to the community working on Ubuntu, you guys are awesome!  Easiest distro I've ever installed.
