### Post by Lightmaster on 2008-09-04
Good evening to everyone.I tought I'd share my experiences with the Open Source evironment.It started in 2005 when I found some old Fedora 1 CD's.I remember installing 6 of them in total.

I remember I quit using it because I had a fail attempt at installing rppoe.Then I discovered Ubuntu in it's 6.10 version.I was amased.Everything went well, and it had a good feel overall.I was still a kinder then, and went back to XP for my games.

I went trough 6.10,7.04,7.10,8.04 and some other distro's like Fedora core 8, PcLinuxOS, Mandriva 2007/2008.Most of the times, Linux stayed on my hard drive for about 1 week then it would be formated and I would install XP.Same, for games.

Now i'm 17, and I feel like a grew a little.I manage my own online business and I am very concerned about security.Thus, I installed 8.04 again.But this time, not only to keep myself safe from viruses and malware, but to keep myself focus on what I'm doing.

Sure, Linux has it's fun games, and as they say"All work and no play, makes Lightmastes a dull boy" so I will be trying some Open Source games.Furthermore since my "business" is open source, why souldn't I be like it?

This is how Linux is helping me on my very first business :) .
