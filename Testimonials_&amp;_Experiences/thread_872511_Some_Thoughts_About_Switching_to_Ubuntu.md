### Post by brook adams on 2008-07-28
I started using Ubuntu in November and made a complete switch from xp in February. I have spent a lotta time on the forums (mostly reading) and I see a good deal of complaint about this that and the other doesn't work.

I can certainly relate to the feeling of helplessness as one struggles to get some functionality out of this new system. I have felt the urge to bail out once or twice and finally, I still have a few apps on my kids' XP machine that I turn to in a pinch but here's the thing... It's because I know those programs better than their Linux equivalents and as time passes, the need to go to the windows machine decreases.

I recenly managed to solve some driver issues for myself and I'm getting better with the command line. In short, I'm 100% happy with Linux and I'm never going back. I love this operating system!
