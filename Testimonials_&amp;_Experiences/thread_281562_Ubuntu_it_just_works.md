### Post by Ronin1970 on 2006-10-21
Just a little note to let you all know how much I like Ubuntu over Windows.
The other day I upgraded my CPU and motherboard. My system is a dual boot.
When I started back up I went into Ubuntu first and was happy to find that everything just worked:p .

Next I booted to Windows, or at least I tried to. All I got was a blue screen telling me my hard drive was faulty or to remove the new hard disk controller I installed.](*,). Ok so I used Windows install disk to repair windows and then it booted, but now I had to install updates and new drivers and such.](*,) . After installing booting, installing booting, you get the picture, about six hours later Windows was back up and running.

The funny thing is that I spent all that time just because I use Windows to play a couple of games and for Quicken. Can't wait unit I just wipe it totally away.
