### Post by Sinkingships7 on 2008-04-27
My new Ubuntu installation (8.04) is 100% stable. Been running since the release candidate. I have never had ONE hang-up, system failure, lock-up, or crash in any way. My only gripe is with Firefox. All is well for me.
