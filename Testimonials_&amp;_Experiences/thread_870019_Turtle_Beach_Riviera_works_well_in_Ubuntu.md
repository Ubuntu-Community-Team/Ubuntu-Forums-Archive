### Post by motang on 2008-07-25
I got an Creative XFi for my new computer that I build (this was few months ago) but Creative didn't have any support for Linux, so I promptly returned the card. After months of using the on-board sound card (it was ok not that great) I decided to buy a new sound card. After a bit of research I decided to buy the Turtle Beach Riviera. Let me tell you it has been my best choice for a sound card. Installed the card into my computer and booted into Ubuntu and everything was setup already without any input from me. I had beautiful sound and my music, movies, and anime never sounded better. I high recommend this sound card.
