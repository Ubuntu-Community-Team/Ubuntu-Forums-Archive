### Post by sirjoebob on 2007-04-05
I am very pleased with the new feisty fawn edition of Ubuntu. In BETA stages, it trumps any and every operating system I have ever used (especially windows vista). The ease of install is amazing when compared with even the most user friendly windows installers and the amount of programs at your disposal from the add/remove programs is rivaled by none! this operating system is solid and secure and very eye pleasing.

the Ubuntu startup noise is a great sound by which to start the experience that is this OS. And dont even get me started on  BERYL! Beryl and the Compiz windows management blows vista clean out of the water. the amount of customization is astounding and something completely foreign to a long-time windows user such as myself... i mean, changing toolbars without registry hacks? my life is so much easier!
