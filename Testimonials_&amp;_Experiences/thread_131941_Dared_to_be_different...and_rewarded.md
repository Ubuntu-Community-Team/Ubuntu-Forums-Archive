### Post by Mishal on 2006-02-17
Needless to say ALL my friends, relatives, teachers, students, acquaintances and whoever else use Windows. Now, I always had this desire from childhood to go against the wave.

So naturally, I couldn't just languish in 'Windows' world and be a nobody for long, could I?:mrgreen: A friend of mine had ordered 20 CDs from shipit.ubuntu and offered it to all of us for free. I took 1 from him but kept it unused for more than 2 months, procrastinating. But then my university classes finished and I decided to have a go. 

So for the last 3 weeks, day and night, I have been on Linux. Unfortunately the CD was a Hoary and I frequently had dependency problems and God I hate those. Ultimately I gave up and downloaded an ISO of Breezy.

Now I am running fine with just 1 or 2 problems yet to solve. Otherwise, I have come a long way to learn about the world of Linux and I am enjoying my stay here. The only decision to make is *when* to delete Windows and not *if* to delete it.:) 

I would like to thank you people on this forum who have been of great help. One thing is for sure...it is IMPOSSIBLE to enter this strange new world without the help of fellow travellers who entered it before that person did.
