What exactly do you consider backdoor tricks?

Certain hardware/software configurations may require patches/fixes to assure functionality. Stability, IMHO has nothing to do with OOTB functionality. But rather  "term" functionality - is it repeatable over time?

Even certain bugs are consider stable, as mastablasta suggests. They are  known and repeatable. Thus, they can be identified and addressed with  fixes. The concept that any OS is completely stable OOTB on every known  configuration, is questionable at best.
