### Post by JonNicholson on 2009-04-24
Hi, all -- In case you find that your numeric keypad on your keyboard isn't working after updating to Jaunty (9.04), I found the solution to this problem.  Go to System>Preferences>Keyboard.  The "Keyboard Preferences" window will be visible.  Select the "Mouse Keys" tab.  De-select the "Pointer can be controlled using the keypad" checkbox.  Problem solved.
Best regards,
Jon
