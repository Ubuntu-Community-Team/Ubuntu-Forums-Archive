### Post by samden on 2009-05-17
Just thought I'd say I'm very impressed with the hardware recognition of Ubuntu 9.04. Installed it on my emachines D620 laptop, and everything just works:
- Wireless worked out of the box.
- Suspend and hibernate are flawless.
- The webcam works beautifully.
- Sound & graphics are great (haven't had to use the microphone yet though, so can't guarantee that one).

The development team have done an awesome job with this release, I hardly feel like I'm using Linux, it works so well!

Well done! And thankyou. ):P
