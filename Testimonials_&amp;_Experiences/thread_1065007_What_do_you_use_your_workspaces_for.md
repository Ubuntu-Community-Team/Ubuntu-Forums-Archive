### Post by The Clown on 2009-02-09
Hi all,

Just a quick question to everyone, because I'm interested:

For those of you that use the multiple workspaces feature, what do you use it for?

Currently, I have the Compiz cube set up with four workspaces, and I have a specific use for each of them.

Workspace 1 is my general usage workspace...I use it to surf the web, play around with stuff, tinker with my configuration, try out new commands in the CLI, etc.

Workspace 2 is my e-mail workspace. I keep Evolution open and un-minimized on it, so when Mail Notification says I have a new piece of mail, I can just switch over to the next workspace to view it.

Workspace 3 is my school workspace. When I'm in class, I use OpenOffice here to take notes. For psychological reasons, I don't use this workspace for anything else...it helps me stay focused if I do it that way.

Workspace 4 varies in use. I usually keep a terminal with PowerTop running in it to monitor my power usage and save battery life when my laptop is unplugged, and it also has the UsefulWallpapers CLI-Commands wallpaper on it so if I forget a command I can just switch over and look at the chart. Also, if I'm testing a distro or using the Windows 7 beta in a virtual machine, this is the workspace I do it on.

What about you? What do you use your workspaces for? :popcorn:
