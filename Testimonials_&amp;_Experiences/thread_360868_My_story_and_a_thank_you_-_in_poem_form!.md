### Post by FurryNemesis on 2007-02-13
I've touched upon why I switched a few months back, when I was still totally new to the Ubuntu. It's been a year now since I switched, so I knocked up a testimonial poem in between my essay work as a sort of thank you thing. Go on, read it. It even rhymes...

Ode to Ubuntu...

O Toshiba! With a gig of RAM
I'll sing your praises if I can
Four years I've worked your slinky keys
and rejigged your directories

A GeForce Four, a Pentium too
You were the height of aptitude
A sticker shouting out to me
That you were built for Win XP 

At first your processes ran smooth
But things began to come unglued
The nightly wash of updates stank
and soon your speedy output sank

O Clog! O Chug! O WGA!
“This is where you will go today”
I reinstalled, I hoped and prayed
but SP2 then came my way

BSODs, leaks, illegal ops
My bundled OS was for the chop 
But, noob I was, just what to do?
Was my machine not Windows too?

I looked, I scrounged, I scoured all
Searching for a clean install
I searched away, and at last clicked
A link, a gate, the world of *nix

Jaw dropped, brows raised, I wandered through
FREE OS! It screamed – could it be true?
My efforts doubled, my interest peaked
It looked all light and shiny – sleek!

Backed up my data, burnt CDs 
Bemoaned my lack of DVDs
Badly burned Fedora Core
Good thing too, t'was version 4...

Went back out upon the web
Found Ubuntu 5 instead
Thought “This works great, so where's the problem?”
Ah... Damn my silly Speedtouch modem.

Forum searches soon fixed that
And took me to the world of apt
After that it was synaptic
Tons of stuff – I was ecstatic

After that was candy time
Not forgetting Totem-xine
Tremulous was lots of fun
(Pity I can't aim a gun)

Compiz, Beryl, Emerald were found
I got the desktop bouncing round
I found the madness it inflicted
Desktop cube?! I was addicted...

Forums, Users, IRC
Everyone was helping me
No more pleading tech support calls
but links and source and sticky tarballs

Friendly are the 'butu lot
Lots of people here we've got
Rarely do we get a spammer
What with mods and kickban hammers

So here we are, it's 2k7 
I've found myself in linux heaven
My thanks to all, you've all been great
And let's roll on to 2k8
