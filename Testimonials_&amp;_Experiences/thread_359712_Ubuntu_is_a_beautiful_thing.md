### Post by lamalex on 2007-02-12
About 6 months ago I was talking to a friend online, we got to talking and somehow ended up on the topic of Linux. I had never tried it and was one of those people who thought GNU/Linux was just for server use. He and his brother are Ubuntu users and they recommended I try it. I downloaded the iso and was amazed! Everything worked and it wasn't windows. Even my HP media remote works. I've been an anarcho-syndicalist for most of my life, so getting away from Windows was wonderful, and then I found the Ubuntu community. I guess this post was really just to say thanks to everyone in the Ubuntu development team and everyone who trolls the forums helping other users and trying to learn themselves. So, thanks! Let's spread the word! If more people just tried GNU/Linux and were willing to learn something new we could take a big part of the market away from microsoft/apple. I've been spreading it around my college and town and we're starting a LUG on campus to help each other out and hopefully draw more people to Linux/ubuntu.
