### Post by brandonterrill on 2010-05-20
Ubuntu is hands down the best disto to suit my needs. The new 10.04 works like a dream on my Acer Aspire 5535, I haven't experienced any problems since I did a clean install from the RC1 which I did an upgrade from the beta (when I tried to restart or shut down it would just log me out). I'm so glad I gave Lucid Lynx a second chance, I haven't been this happy with it since Dapper Drake on my old HP desktop I got in 2000, which I ended up having to install XP on because I had to start using a wireless module :(

I dual boot Windows 7 and Ubuntu 10.04 on a 500GB hard drive, and honestly I only go back to Windows to sync my Zune or my iPod Touch. (In case you couldn't tell I like leaving my options open and take advantage of the best of both worlds, -1 to fanboy-ism :P). I always try other distros but I always come back to Ubuntu due to my own impatience and ease of use. 

Congratulations Canonical, you've made a full-time user out of me once again.
