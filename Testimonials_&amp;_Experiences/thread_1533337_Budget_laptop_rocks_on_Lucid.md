### Post by sailorboy on 2010-07-17
Last laptop I got was over 10 years ago (a Presario) - for $1500 I got a 450mhz AMD cpu with a 5 gig HD, 128m RAM and ran W98. It went to the closet 5 yrs ago and so I finally decided to upgrade. In ten years, the change of power vs price is amazing.
 A new CQ61-420 packing a 2.1 ghz duo core Athlon, 7200rpm 250g HD, DVD burner, ATI 4200 video, 3g RAM and 15.6" WS HD dsplay is UNDER $500 WITH tax. 6lbs, <2" thick.
 Comes with Windows 7 Home 64bit, but nuking the recovery partition allowed a dual boot.
 Compiz, cairo dock, widgets, WinFF, VLC - it all just ROCKS, rarely need to go over to the dark (Windows) side, it's so lame in comparison.
 No 'print screen' or speaker audio?- Google the forum, answered, fixed. Done.
 I like the flexibility of desktops but seems tough now to match on price.
 You guys are spoiling us. Thank you, Thank you so very much!
