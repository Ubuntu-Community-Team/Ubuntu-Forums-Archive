### Post by 1roxtar on 2010-04-06
One year ago, I was born into the wonderful world of Linux and UBUNTU, in particular.  My journey began with an 8.10 LiveCD that I kicked around until 9.04 Jaunty Jackalope came out two weeks later.  I learned to do a Wubi install, then a dual-boot and then "boarded up my Windows" completely on my personal laptops and home desktop.  I love everything UBUNTU.  In a whole year I've been very Karmic and now feeling totally Lucid.  Tonight, I'll raise my bottle in a toast and say thank you Mark Shuttleworth, Canonical, Ubuntu and most of all to my Community family for all your help!!!  UBUNTU ROCKS!!!!!!!!!
