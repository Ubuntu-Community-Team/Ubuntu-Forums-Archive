### Post by qamelian on 2009-04-14
> **bobmatino17 said:**
> no you have to modify your /etc/apt/sources.list 

you just replace everywhere you see "intrepid" with "jaunty"

then you run the command

sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get dist-upgrade
You don't have to modify anything. If you are waiting until the official release, the update manager will tell you than a new release is available and will present you with a button that allows you to upgrade to Jaunty rather than just updating your current installation.

You can also use alt-F2 and run:
```
update-manager -cd
```
If you want to upgrade now to the beta in its current state.

The update-manager will look after selecting the new repositories for you without you needing to edit sources.lst at all.
