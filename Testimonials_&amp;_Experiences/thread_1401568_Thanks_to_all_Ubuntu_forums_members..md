### Post by rocket16 on 2010-02-08
Hello all. I am glad to announce that recently in our survey, we discovered that out of a total of 1200 users of Computers in our locality, we have transformed (promoted!) 592 users to Ubuntu users. Thus, nearly 50% of users now use Ubuntu! In our earlier survey which I reported here a few months ago, 30% were Ubuntu users, but now, this has increased to 49.33%! 

Our Linux club (better to call Ubuntu club) is working nicely to promote the shift to Ubuntu, but we could never have succeeded without the help from you all, my precious friends here, who helped me many times to solve several issues! 

We hereby give you all a big hand, and thank you all earnestly, along with the developers of Ubuntu, for your hard work and invaluable contributions to the development and progress of Ubuntu. We hope, in the next few months, the number of Ubuntu users shall be more than 80%. Additionally, in our locality, we have sent a small group of volunteers, who helped the Cable Operators to replace their pirated versions of Windows XP with Ubuntu. A local user said "Earlier, we were nearly thieves, who stole Windows in a way, by using Windows pirated-cds. But now, we are free, and are no longer considering ourselves guilty, for we have the support, power and help of Ubuntu with us". Thanks to all of you, and on behalf of our Club, I thank you all. Please give your suggestions regarding the promotiom of Ubuntu use in our locality. (The only one drawback for Ubuntu was unavailibity of fonts, according to common users. We solved it using mscorefonts). Thanks to all again.
