### Post by bks on 2008-03-11
I have to be honest with everyone, I own a computer with Vista and I tried to dual boot it with Fedora. The short version is, everything got messed up. The Fedora installer ruined the Vista boot loader and prevented me from loading Vista. Now this could be viewed as a good thing, except that it's my wife's computer that she uses for college. Needless to say I had to recover the data, without corruption. I tired all kinds of thing to repair the boot loader, but still got only Grub errors 16, 17 and 18. When at last I was discouraged and ready to reinstall Windows, I booted Ubuntu Live CD and within 30 minutes I was back into Vista with no errors! When Gutsy installed Grub, it fixed the boot loader and allowed me to get back into Windows! So now I have the best of both worlds, my wife is relieved that her school work is intact and I get to use Ubuntu instead of Vista! Thank you thank you thank you Ubuntu!:)
