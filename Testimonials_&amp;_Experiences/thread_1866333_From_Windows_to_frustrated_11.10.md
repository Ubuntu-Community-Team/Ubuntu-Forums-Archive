### Post by Juliolp on 2011-10-21
Hi! I have an old Pentium 4 with 1 mg Ram and i'm so happy! :D

I had Windows XP and suddenly i thought about testing Linux. A bro recommend me Ubuntu. I was so happy! The machine was faster; i got most of the apps i need, and i got Wine, of course, for a couple more.

I enjoy 11.04 with Gnome last 3 months. And now 11.10. I said: let's go for it. And the nightmare started:

1) The upgrade fails 2 o 3 times. I fix them here in the Ubuntu community. Too many errors in the new 11.10. Gnome was break -in installed GShell-, errors in links, etc. Weird. Unity was buggy too. I backup my files and do a fresh new installation from CD.

2) Unity was a bit slow -Unity 2D, of course- than Gnome in 11.04. I got to write the apps i need or the documents or the music or whatever if they're not in the most popular in the search index. But with Gnome i got a menu in the upper left corner and listed by categories. Click, click, and voilá, what i need. And most important: ¡with a simple look i can see all the apps in a category! Simple. Don't like Unity in 11.04.

3) New installation. Yes, Unity is here. Ok, it's still the same. Slow, i got to write the apps, i can't customize Unity -icons, align, etc., tried Compiz but...-. In Gnome Shell i can't use the Volume Control to manage Banshee. What? I liked to manage music with the Music Manager in the top right Corner, was so cool! And, of course, some more errors that i got to fix. Like most of repository has changed their name and are off. What? Oh, my God, there's a lot of work here to have my desktop SIMILAR as my 11.04 was!! :(

4) Next time i turn on my PC, Gnome was doing some weird things. I can't customize with themes, the date and time in the middle top can't find my location in Spain -so i must add the time manually-. Some items from the 11.04 Gnome System menu are not there, in a simple Menu called System, they'are in Others. ¿Others some System Apps? Well, is more friendly call the things with an easy identificative name...

5) I installed the Tweak for Gnome, do the classic "things to do after installing Ubuntu 11.10", surf some good ubuntu blogs to tweak Gnome, fix some mistakes... and suddenly i remembered: i was funny with 11.04. I know how to work with it, what i don't have to do -the first classics newbie mistakes with Ubuntu-... So, again, fresh 11.04 installation. Again, all go as smooth as the first time i installed 11.04. Add repositories, customize Gnome and add my cool Equinox theme, the Faenza icons... -that i installed in Gnome Shell but i can't see in my Desktop-... and now i got my Ubuntu working as smooth and i write this post with my experience: a 8/10 in 11.04, a 5/10 in 11.10.

6) So... I really think if the launch of 11.10 was too fast. Maybe 11.10 need some more tweak from the development community. Gnome Shell is, definitely, worst than 11.04 Gnome Desktop. Very far from usability, clean and design... 

So, i'll wait to 12.04. Maybe i buy a new PC in a few months. But with 11.04 still. I know we have a great community -always helped me, a newbie in Ubuntu- but i don't think Unity was a great way to work with Ubuntu. That's my point. 

A hug to everybody.
