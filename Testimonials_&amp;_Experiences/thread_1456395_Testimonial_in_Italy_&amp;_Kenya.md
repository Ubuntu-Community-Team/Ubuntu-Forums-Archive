### Post by willywongi on 2010-04-17
Hi everyone, I'm a ubuntu user and fan. I also play in a band with some friends here in Italy, and some time ago we had the chance to play for a small event in support for the building of a school in Muyeye, Kenya. Given that I steadily bought the ubuntu tshirt from the ubuntu store to show our endorsement, and I wore it the night of the gig. Then a friend of mine took some pictures, we made a little poster, and sent it to the children in Muyeye. This is a very little act, but I'm very proud of it!

The project involved a lot of volunteers and no profit associations, including some that help relieving people with every sort of mental illness. Some of them went to Muyeye with the volunteers. Here are more info on that (italian):
[http://www.leparoleritrovate.com/?p=175](http://www.leparoleritrovate.com/?p=175)

The band name is "Le prugne della California" (the italian form of the California Raisins), we play some rock songs from the 80s.

Thank you for this little spot!
