### Post by Robert.Thompson on 2010-09-15
It has been well over a month since I trashed windows and went 100% Ubuntu and I have not regretted it yet.

I am not a linux geek and was not a windows geek - I'm just an average person, who can read and can use Google to search for any solution I might require.

Truthfully, I have not missed XP one bit! Everything that I ever did in windows, I now do in Ubuntu. I have not paid one red cent for any application and, so far, everything works perfectly. (I did pay for support for one application but it was a negligible amount.)

All of my hardware works. I can connect to the net wirelessly, wired or dialup.

I want for nothing!

My only criticism is that I found that some applications are not simply 'click and install' as they were in windows. Linux forum people have told me that I am wrong for thinking this way but I just think that they are wrong for thinking their way.

People tell me that I should try windows 7 as it is so much better but everything they told me about that is 'new', I can already do in Ubuntu! (but remember, I am no geek.)

Anyway, I'm happy and see no reason to switch back. I also like that people think that I am some sort of geeky guru because I use linux - if they only knew the truth...

If we were to compare the steps and the time it takes to install an app between Windows and Ubuntu:
