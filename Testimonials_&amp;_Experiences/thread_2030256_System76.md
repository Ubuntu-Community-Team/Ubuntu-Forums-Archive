### Post by Shaun_Yute on 2012-07-20
This post is long overdue and I apologize. When I first started toying with Ubuntu a few years ago I instantly wanted a laptop with this amazing OS. I know you can download and install it but I wanted to purchase a laptop with Ubuntu that had no hardware issues or conflicts of any kind. But for some reason I couldn't find anything so I just continued toying with Ubuntu on old laptops I had or virtually on my desktop. Luckily, several months ago, my brother-in-law sent me a link to System76. I was instantly sold and purchased the 15.6" Pangolin Performance all tricked out. I have had the laptop now for a couple of months and have had no issues with it what so ever. Boot time is amazing and so is the i7 processor, especially when you have an OS like Ubuntu that is not processor intensive unlike other operating system's out there. This is by far the best laptop I have ever owned with the best OS I have ever used. So thank you Ubuntu and System76.
