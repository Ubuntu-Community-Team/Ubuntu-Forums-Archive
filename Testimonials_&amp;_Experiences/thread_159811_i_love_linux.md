### Post by Derek Djons on 2006-04-13
Congrats with your switch or dual boot. Well, user friendlyness is still part subjective. People who don't like a GUI often tend to call it user unfriendly. But it doesn't necessarely means it technically is.

Along the way you will find out that GNU/Linux is very customizable. So is Ubuntu, Gnome and KDE. If you are a basic user you won't find this exciting. Features such as the packetmanager will thrill you more. But if you are more of a power-user or hobbyist chances are you will be customizing Ubuntu to your desires. That's something which is only limited possibile with Microsoft Windows and Mac OS X.
