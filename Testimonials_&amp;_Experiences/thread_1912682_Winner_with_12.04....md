### Post by Baldrick_NZ on 2012-01-21
As a former detractor and sceptic of Unity, I now find myself humbled after installing 12.04 Alpha 1 onto my 8g flash drive.

I was a bit of a hater of the unmovable/un-deletable dock, but now with some recent configurablity added, it's not so bad. i can live with it.

What really impresses me is the speed apps start and work, even while apps are working in the background. I could never achieve this using 10.04 on an 8g stick!

Can't wait until April for the final release. If it's anything like this Alpha, It'll be a winner for sure!!

My only comment would be to preload Chrome web browser. Not necessarily as default, but have it on offer. ;-)
