### Post by jchapman on 2007-10-31
The "unveiling" of my pastor's wife's previous-Vista-now-Ubuntu laptop was last night. I spent about an hour going over the basics with her; she was pleased beyond my wildest expectations!

She couldn't believe how fast her laptop was now. She couldn't get over how she could open 5 Firefox tabs and not have the computer slow to a crawl. Movie trailers at Apple.com played as if she was watching a DVD and DVD's played smooth as silk, without a single jitter. Her husband literally sat with his mouth open, watching her fly across web sites and figure out where things were and how they worked on her own.

Later, when I got home, I chatted with her across Pidgin/Gaim. She was using exclamation after exclamation telling me "I get it! I actually get this!" regarding how friendly her system is now and "WOW!" regarding how fast it finally is. She thanked me profusely throughout our conversation. What they thought was a waste of money on a slow computer is now the stellar performer in their home - even faster than their new desktop (running XP).

I don't think it'll be too long before her husband wants me to do the same for his laptop and desktop. Oh, and his wife got a really big chuckle when I told her her password was "novista." \\:D/
