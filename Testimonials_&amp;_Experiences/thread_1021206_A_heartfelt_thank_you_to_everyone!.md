### Post by webventures on 2008-12-25
Ubuntu has been a life saver! Who I begin with? Linus Torvalds. the linux pioneer? The people who appreciated what he has done and expanded up on it?

My Windows crashed for the last time! Especially with the XP corrupt system/config missing corrupt error message!

I ordered the free Ubuntu disc 5 months ago. I gave it a quick look and then put it on the back burner, so to speak.

At this point in time, with my XP crashing yet again, Ubutnu came to the rescue!  I have to thank the entire community of developers and supporters. I am now running Ubuntu on the CD only (until I buy a new hard drive)....because my current drive's SMART thingy is dead. With the Ubuntu CD I can at least get on the internet, check email and listen to some beautiful music on this Christmas eve! You have helped make my Christmas! (yes, on Ubuntu Pidgin I was able to contact friends tonight on this Christmas Eve! Thank you everybody! :-)

I will buy some Ubuntu stuff from the store...and if the developers are interested, I can provide them with free traffic at my traffic exchange...in website, banner and text format!

Everyone here rocks! 

Bye for now!
