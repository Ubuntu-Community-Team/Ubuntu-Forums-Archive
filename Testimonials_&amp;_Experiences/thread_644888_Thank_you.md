### Post by mjcritchie on 2007-12-19
I installed Ubuntu a few months ago. I was a long time windows user previously but was tempted to give Linux a try.
After accidentally deleting my old OS (thus ruining the dual-boot plan I had envisaged) I was stuck with Ubuntu!
There was a fairly steep learning curve but I've started to get the hang of things now and I'm so glad I made the switch!

My machine is a very basic laptop mainly used for emails, internet and word processing. I'm very impressed with the amount of customisation Ubuntu offers in terms of desktop layout and style; I can set things up how I like.
There have been a few teething problems, but every single one has been fixable (with great support from these forums - thanks!) and now, touch wood, everything seems to be sorted and stable. My laptop runs smoother and feels more streamlined; without great threat from viruses or spyware clogging up my system.

It's amazing that you can get all these things (the OS and the many many programmes) for free and, for the most part, it's better and more innovative than the software you have to pay for.

So a big thank you to everyone behind Ubuntu and indeed everyone who supports Linux. Things are looking good now, I can't wait to find out what happens in the future.

MIKE
