### Post by Exonimus on 2007-09-30
Hey everyone, I decided to make the switch to ubuntu about two weeks ago. I had an old live cd somewhere that I only used once to boot up and mess around with gnome a little. (was about two years ago) Now, a few weeks ago, a friend of mine told me he'd be ordering a new ubuntu cd, and asked me if I wanted one as well. I said sure, few weeks later, I got it. I didn't expect to be  doing much with it, but you never know.. now, I've got this pc upstairs that's been really irritating, it rebooted randomly, wlan didn't work properly, it was extremely slow, etc. Now, awhile back, I decided to just go and give ubuntu a try. I installed it, wlan worked right from the first login without any problems. I've also gotten a few vnc servers to work so more people can work on several desktops remotely.. There was, of course, stuff I had to do and had no clue how to. However, one quick look on google and I'd always find a handy tutorial. I even got MS office to work(I need powerpoint to create presentations. I'd love to do it in openoffice, but my school only supports .ppt presentations)

anyway, just wanted to say thanks to the community. I'm only a newbie, but I'm learning quick.. never thought linux could be this easy. I've had one problem that I didn't solve yet, but I think I might even solve that. Pretty sure I'm not going back to windows. So thanks for making this work! :)
