### Post by cjax1 on 2008-03-17
Hoary Hedgehog was the first Linux distro I tried out. I went back to Windows for a while but came back to Ubuntu. Tried Breezy Badger. Also tinkered with several other distros. I couldn't give up that easy I guess. I'm glad I didn't. I really started using Linux when Dapper Drake 6.06 was released. I'm still using 6.06 although I have used 7.04 and 7.10. As soon as Hardy Heron is released I'm upgrading. I can't wait.

Even though I still get frustrated once in a while I just cannot see myself going back to Windows. Every once in a while when I use XP I'm reminded just how much more frustrating that is.

I've turned two of my friends onto Ubuntu so far. One loves it but the other isn't so sure yet. She's not sure if she can live without her Windows and yet she's so tired of the usual problems with it and she sees how well Ubuntu works for me. Every time she comes over and sees my Ubuntu she always says wow about something under her breath. She's so impressed and yet so unsure of leaving Windows totally even though she can do everything she does in Ubuntu that she does in Windows now. I tell her Linux isn't for everyone but neither is Windows or Mac. It's her choice. At least she knows she has a choice.

I really do love Ubuntu. I hope the Ubuntu team keeps up the great work. I think I'll be around Ubuntu for a while.
