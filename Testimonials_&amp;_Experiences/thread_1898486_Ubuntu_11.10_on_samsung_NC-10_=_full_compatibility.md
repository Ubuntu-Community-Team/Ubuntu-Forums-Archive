### Post by dukeinlondon on 2011-12-21
I've bought one of these little machines on ebay for £100 for the sole purpose of trying a few linux distros after 11.10 seemed to play very well with my bigger samsung q320. 

I was not disappointed although one aspect didn't work so well : brightness control. This was however fixed by the addition of a additional ppa holding a few samsung specific drivers.

Battery life is excellent and performance are reasonable considering the hardware. suspend/resume works flawlessly. In short a completely usable little machine for very little money. 

Mine has got only 1gb of RAM and it seems to be plenty (right now using 400mb + 160mb of swap)
