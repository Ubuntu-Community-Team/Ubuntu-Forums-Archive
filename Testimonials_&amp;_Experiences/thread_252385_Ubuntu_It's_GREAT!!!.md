### Post by joep on 2006-09-06
Hi I installed Ubuntu 6.60 on a spare 80 gig hdd 3/4 weeks ago. I have tried linux in various forms over the years but could never get the hang of it. Thanks to Automatix I was able to install all the software I needed to get going. My kids now use my Ubuntu box without complaint most of their sites run and they are happy a big + .  I now never boot XP I can do most things myself by reading, searching and following instructions on the forums. I can load pics, wallpapers, music and ringtones to my mobile. I can stream/rip music. I can do every thing I want. I have 4 computers at home the other 3 will not be upgraded to Vista. When the time comes they will be switched over to Ubuntu. I would like to thank everyone who contributes to these forums with their time and advice which makes it possible for the likes of me to have a viable alternative to Windows.

### Post by joep on 2006-09-06
Hi I'm telling anyone who will listen switch to Ubuntu. My kids 3 boys 8, 9 and 15. 8 and 9 year old totally accept it. 15 year old doesn't mind it but to busy playing football. Younger ones at first didn't know what Linux was they have I C T at school but really they only learn about Windows. They don't like the start up or colour of Ubuntu but they have had computers in the house since they were born, and were working on getting the look they want. Because they can't use Shockwave it stops them using certain sites they like. But they are realy happy with the fact it works with Cartoonnetwork etc. If my kids warn't happy I would be reduced to only running it on a spare hdd.  It is now a family thing they like to use. They are under no pressure to use it and are free to use the XP box's. But the fact is they like it and the games that I downloaded. My wife also uses Ubuntu and plays the games.
