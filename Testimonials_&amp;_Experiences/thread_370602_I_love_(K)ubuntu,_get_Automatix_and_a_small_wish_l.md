### Post by maestrobwh1 on 2007-02-25
It is the most amazing thing... free and it looks cool.  It boots so much faster than that other OS.  I will never buy another OS and will only tolerate one that is preinstalled on a new computer.

I try to "sell" this version of Linux to anyone who will listen.  Everyone is clamoring for Vista.  Please. I actually put Kubuntu Edgy on my new Acer as my primary OS, read a few posts to get some of the hardware going and everything there works better than the XP is also on it.  I also feel confident that when I am on a public network, that my OS isn't going to be "invaded."

If you go this route, as soon as you install, get Automatix ([www.getautomatix.com](www.getautomatix.com)) before you install anything else to get Java, Flashplayer, codecs for media files and if you have an iPod, you'll be glad to have followed my advice!  I tried to install it on my desktop after I did some other software installing and it wouldn't work.

There are two things I wish for though:

1. Suspend and hibernate to work on desktop machines (this might be a KDE issue)
2. For Adept/Synaptic update notifier to permanently remember what packages I don't want updated.
