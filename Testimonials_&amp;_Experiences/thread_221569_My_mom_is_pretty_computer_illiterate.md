### Post by msg on 2006-07-23
I have a bad habit of staying up several hours past my bedtime... I'm just a normal 15 year old, no big deal.

My mom, using her sly ninja tactics, waits until I get up to go to the bathroom, and then tries to turn off my computer.

When I used to only have Windows I would usually find that:

1) My mom was in a daze trying to figure out how to turn off my computer. Her mind was probably thinking, "Start??? no... that can't be it, I'm not trying to start something...".

or

2) My mom had unplugged the computer, but to her dismay (and confusion) it was still on, only making beeping noises (I have an UPS).

Now, whenever I'm using Ubuntu, I come back from the bathroom to find that my computer is shutting down properly, and my mom is back in her room. Triumph or defeat? You decide.
