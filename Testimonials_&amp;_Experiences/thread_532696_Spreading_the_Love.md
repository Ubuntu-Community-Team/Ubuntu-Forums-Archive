### Post by Gausian on 2007-08-23
The other day at work, we were having some trouble with our exchange server, and i said something like "we should just switch over to linux".

Everyone got really interested and wanted to know about linux.  

So now one of my co-workers who is 64, cant wait to get home and try out Xbuntu on some of his old machines.

Another keeps coming to my office with questions on how to dual boot with XP.

I guess people are really open to new OS's, and interested in freeing themselves from high costs.
