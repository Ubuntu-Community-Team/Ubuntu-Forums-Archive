### Post by Talon2 on 2010-05-14
I've been using Ubuntu since not long after the first release.  I've seen a lot of improvements.

There are 2 changes in 10.04 that I have tried to love but I've decided to throw in the towel and make changes:

- The new dark theme (the default).  I like dark themes but this theme just does not work for me.  The lack of contrast in areas where I need contrast to see what I'm doing is just not there.

- The window control buttons on the left.  I have tried to love this.  I've given it every chance to work but it just isn't working for me.
