### Post by The Judderman on 2009-11-04
Dear all,

I had such a great experience yesterday that confirmed why I use Ubuntu, that I thought that I would share it.

I decided to completely wipe my laptop to resize windoze partition and install Karmic.

I started off by reinstalling windoze, which took me over 6 hours, and I still haven't got all the security updates or all the software installed that I wanted.

Then I did a clean install of Karmic. (I've had issues with upgrades in the past and so always do a clean install to new distro). This took me a grand total of 2.5 hrs, with everything I need installed, and the latest security updates! 

You might say why did I keep windoze! Good question. I think for my own security, if ever I mess something up on ubuntu, my wife can still access that computer if I'm at work.

But this is great! I love Karmic! I love Ubuntu! I love being free......

Thanks to the Ubuntu Team and everybody at the forums who has helped me over the years! I've now been successfully running Ubuntu for 2 1/2 years and it has just kept on getting better!

The Judderman
