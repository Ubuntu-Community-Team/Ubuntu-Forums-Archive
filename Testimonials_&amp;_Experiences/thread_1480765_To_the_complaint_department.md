### Post by ashakeandfrys on 2010-05-11
On and off, I've been using Ubuntu since 7.04. Since 9.04, I've switched completely from Windows to Linux, aside from the fact that my Kodak ESP3 doesn't work with Linux.

NOW.. the point of this posting - I don't understand what all the complaints about 10.04 are about. First and foremost, USB transfer speeds have been fixed, and overall the system is just much faster than any other release I've used. Grub2 now works with my RAID 0, and my pulse audio issues are gone. Somehow, the devs have even made the memory footprint smaller as well. I could go on and on..

IMO, this is the best release yet!
