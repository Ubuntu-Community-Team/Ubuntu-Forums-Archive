### Post by Tamlynmac on 2012-05-04
> BlastXng

Sorry you experienced an online upgrade issue. This issue has been  around for quite some time. Should one perform a quick search of this  section, they would learn that the online upgrade process has been  questioned many times in the past. For example, this [thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1780775&page=2").

I attempted upgrading in the past and the experience was unpleasant to  say the least. At that point, I was informed (on these forums) by a long  term member "wolfen 69" and others - not to use the process. Since that  time I've only performed manual, clean installs and have experienced  zero issues. Regardless if the issue is related to PPA's, connections,  etc. IMHO a warning should be associated with the process to notify  users that a failed upgrade is possible.

I doubt that anyone notifying you now - that it worked for them -  improves your point of view. The success rate matters little to those  that experience failure. My advice is to always perform a clean install.  After downloading the iso, I place it on a stick or CD assuring it's  integrity and use that for the actual new version installation.

Once again, I'm sorry you experienced the problem and can only hope that in the future you'll see the value of clean installs.

Good Luck

Just my $0.02
