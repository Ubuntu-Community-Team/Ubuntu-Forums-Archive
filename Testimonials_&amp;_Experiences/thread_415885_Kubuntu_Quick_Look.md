### Post by zgornel on 2007-04-20
The last 2 days have been very exciting for the ubuntu community and with good reason. I decided to try Kubuntu 7.04 for the simple fact that I did not find my present ubuntu 6.10 efficient enough for my demands. No need to get into a Gnome vs. KDE discussion, it is not the issue here. 
I was somehow expecting the Kubuntu experience to be a very pleasant one and indeed it was: all hardware recognized and working properly, fonts looking excellent, 3D performance OK, overall performance more than satisfying. I would like to say more but you have to try it to convince yourselves. Simply a great experience.
