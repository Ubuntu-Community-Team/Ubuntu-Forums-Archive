### Post by cg0191 on 2010-05-19
First time **ever** in all these years I was able to throw a Linux onto a machine (netbook) and everything 'just works' much like my Mac's do.

Blazing fast startup from my 16Gb SSD, dramatically lower disk/ram usage compared to the Vista that infested the machine prior.

Wow, just wow, I'm very impressed to have a better alternative to Micro$oft & a lower cost one to Apple.

I really like the what Ubuntu has done with Gnome. To say that it is heavily inspired by Apple design but goes one better is a compliment. I wish Apple would put Applications/Places/System in **their** top menu bar! I really like it!

Kudos to all those involved with Ubuntu, job well done!
