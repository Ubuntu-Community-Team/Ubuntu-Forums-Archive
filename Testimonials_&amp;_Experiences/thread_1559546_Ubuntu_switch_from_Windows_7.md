### Post by furiousjason on 2010-08-23
About 3 weeks ago my Windows 7 machine crash for the 2nd time in a year. The first time I used Ubuntu boot disc to recover my data. Had to do the same thing the 2nd time but this time decided to dual boot Windows 7 and Ubuntu 10.04 LTS. I personally have really liked the switch. I ran into a few problems but with the power of google and these message boards I was able to fix the problems without much trouble. The only reason I keep windows 7 would be for some gaming and my fiancé has not warmed up to Ubuntu just yet. Short and sweet, thanks to anyone who has helped me or in the future will help me. Keep up the good work!
