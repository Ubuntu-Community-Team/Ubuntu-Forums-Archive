### Post by hidarikani on 2008-01-19
I'm using Xubuntu Gutsy

Pros:
   Compiz Fusion
   Advanced text editors
   XFCE -- minimalistic GUI
   Powerful terminal
   Add/Remove and Synaptic
   No viruses
   Easy LAMP

Cons:
   System locks after inserting blank CD
   Mplayer crashes when trying to play .avi, so I use VLC
   HD video lags
   Writing 10 MB of data to flash drive takes 30 minutes
   Configuring my 1680x1050 monitor took me two days (5 minutes on xp)
   No driver for Canon i350 printer and never will be, so for printing I switch to xp

To sum up, I love Xubuntu most of the time, 
but sometimes it makes my life miserable.
