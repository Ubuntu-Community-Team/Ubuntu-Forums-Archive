### Post by asv on 2007-10-27
Overall, I've been very impressed with Ubuntu's quality and package decisions over the years, but if there is on thing about Gutsy that annoys me, its trackerd. 

Trackerd has no business being installed by default. Its buggy, resource intensive, and ineffective. Even beagled 3 years ago is better. 

I understand the need to deploy a dynamic search application like Beagle and Spotlight, but don't deploy something by default thats ineffective and resource intensive. 

Trackerd should be pulled, and be made as an optional install. Its not consistent with quality users have come to expect from the Ubuntu default package set.
