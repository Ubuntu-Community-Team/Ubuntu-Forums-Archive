### Post by Sohrab Kakakhel on 2011-10-16
I love Ubuntu but I have to say Ubuntu 11.10 has been a broken release for me. I have no issues with Unity or anything of the sort. I've just had some really annoying technical issues which I'd like to point out. Maybe if I get some answers I can try 11.10 once again. Background information, Im using a Z series Vaio with 2.4 Ghz, Core 2 duo, 4 GB DDR3. I installed the 64 bit version.

THE BIG STUFF:

1) Wifi was a complete mess on 11.10 for me. I have an intel 5100 chip. The internet was fine on ubuntu 11.04 but I noticed a sharp decline in performance. I have a 5 MB DSL connection and I noticed when I tried uploading a video to facebook. My upload speeds were 2-3 kb. I searched, disable IVP6 and all that jazz but it just didnt work.

2) Annoying issue with the sound preferences. The sound would automatically go really low when I plugged in the headphones. And id have to go into the preferences again and again to disable and adjust the volume. This would happen everytime I plugged in the headphones. 

3) I couldnt transfer files via USB to my phone which is an android handset. It would just give errors in the middle of the transfer. My dmesg USB showed a bunch of errors with g-photo-volume. I couldn't be bothered to try to fix that it was too damn hard. 

4) Gnome 3 sucks. Unity is a shell for Gnome 3. Big problems because of gnome shell. For example, if you set the brightness from the screen option it doesnt save. So you have to do it every restart. Where's the screen saver? Wheres the power options for spinning the disks down? Why do I  have to install gnome-tweak tool to access simple things like changing the theme? or fonts? I mean seriously. 

5) Why does the music lens just work with banshee? Banshee is so resource hungry. It takes ages to start up connecting to useless radio channels and stores. Who would watch video with banshee?

6) Sending files in empathy, you have to go to the main contacts list and chose the contact and then click send file. There is no way to send from inside the window. 

SMALL ISSUES:
1) Everytime you open a config file with gedit, another untitled document opens up for no reason.

2) Google chrome does not install.

3) A small annoying thing I noticed in the installer was when you click the details button to see the progress of the install theres no output in the mini terminal. It was very useful for me because many a time the internet would stall during the install. Theres no output in the update manager either. 

4) Super + D no longer minimizes all windows. I used to use this so much. The gnome shortcut is CTRL + ALT + D but that minimizes unity aswell and its a pain to bring it back. 

I don't know how just 4 hours of use gave me so many bugs. How could they release this? Its back to 11.04 for me, which works flawlessly. Im not flaming the UI, I actually had techincla problems.
