### Post by ryanVickers on 2007-07-23
I've heard a few different sayings about what Ubuntu is all about, and "they" say that it's easy enough for your grandma to use!  Well, it's now a proven fact that this is true!  In just **2 days** I had converted my grandma from xp to ubuntu 7.04 and taught her everything about the basics, like file browsing, email, surfing the web, music, etc.  So for those of you out there finding it just impossible, I'd make sure you don't give up so easily - this proves it can't be that hard!  The computer in question was a Dell 4550* (I think) with 768 ram (512 added after) and a new drive (80Gb Seagate 7200rpm IDE barracuda).  I didn't bother with video drivers because the video card was really old.

*2Ghz Pentium 4
*256Mb ram
*40Gb HD
*nVidia GeForce 2 mx (~I think~)
*Dell 16" CRT Monitor (17" LCD Resolution capable, running at 15" LCD resolution(1024x7688)(please ignore smiley))
