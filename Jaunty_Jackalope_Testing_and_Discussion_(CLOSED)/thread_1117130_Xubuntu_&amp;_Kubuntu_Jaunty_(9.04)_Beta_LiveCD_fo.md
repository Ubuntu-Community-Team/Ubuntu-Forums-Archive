### Post by tiresia on 2009-04-05
I found out right now, that Xubuntu and Kubuntu Jaunty are released as LiveCD also for PowerPC! So, you can try them without installing, just to see how they work.

You can download here Xubuntu
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/ports/releases/jaunty/beta/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/ports/releases/jaunty/beta/)

and here Kubuntu
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/kubuntu/ports/releases/jaunty/beta/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/kubuntu/ports/releases/jaunty/beta/)

(There you will find also .torrent files)

They are not yet released, it is the Beta version. For Xubuntu you need 128 MB Ram, for Kubuntu you need 256 MB Ram.

At the yaboot prompt, press as always the TAB-key to see the different options. You can choose the kernel for 64-bit computers (G5) or 32-bit computers (G3 and G4). To see what happens during booting, choose a 'nosplash' option and be a bit patient (for me it takes 4-5 min) 


__________

I tried the Xubuntu Jaunty Live CD on my PowerMac MDD Dual 1,25 GHz with 2 GB Ram and it works perfectly! 
You will find AbiWord (instead of Open Office), Firefox and Thunderbird (actually I prefer Epiphany-Browser and Claws-Mail, because I find them really light). 
No problems at all with xorg. My dual processor is not recognized - but it happens always to me with debian and ubuntu. Anyway I can of course install the correct kernel after installing.
