### Post by weaver4 on 2009-04-04
I do a fresh install of 9.04 beta.  Everything looks good.  Then I get all the updates and reboot.  The only thing that shows up on the screen is the background; no panels across the top or bottom.  I can right-click the background and add a new folder and then this folder will show up, but nothing else.  I can insert a DVD and when it mounts it shows up.

Again; everything was working before the upgrade.

What should I try?

---

### Post by weaver4 on 2009-04-04
In case someone else has this problem.

My motherboard has both a vga output and a LCD panel connect.  Turning off the internal LCD panel before installing the updates and rebooting solved the problem.

I think when it rebooted it showed the wrong display.
