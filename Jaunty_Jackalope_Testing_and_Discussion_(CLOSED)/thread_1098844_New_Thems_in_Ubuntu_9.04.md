### Post by motang on 2009-03-17
Looks like Ubuntu 9.04 will have new additions [out of the box for themes]("http://news.softpedia.com/news/Ubuntu-9-04-039-s-New-Themes-106961.shtml") and some of them will be taken out.  The ones that will be added are *New Wave, Dust, and Dust Sand*. The ones that were taken out were *Crux, Glider, Glossy and Mist* themes.  

Now I like the new additions, but for my notebook (which is primarily used for school), I switch off between Glossy and Glider, but those can be added later.

These additions are really cool, and I am really looking forward to Ubuntu 9.04 even more now.  With the new notification system, new log on screen, all we need is a sweet looking wallpaper and with the new compiz effects like fading wallpaper changer and fading logon we are set for one **awesome** release!
