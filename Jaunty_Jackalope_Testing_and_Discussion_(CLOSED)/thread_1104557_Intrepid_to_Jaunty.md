### Post by wjp.reg on 2009-03-23
Press Alt-F2 and enter ```
update-manager -d
```.  You may wish to backup your home partition first, just in case;).

Ran it this AM overtop my 8.10 install with no hiccups or dissapointments.  All systems go!

P.S. I did have to reset the gnome default font size in the System > Preferences > Appearances

Cheers!
