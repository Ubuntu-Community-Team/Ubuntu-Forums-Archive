### Post by Kow on 2009-02-12
> **jfernyhough said:**
> I don't see how it should affect your PC, possibly apart from the power cycle on the hard drive (2.5" have a much higher limit that 3.5" IIRC).

Drives do not power down on reboot. Rebooting does not stress the components of a PC any differently than just letting the computer run. It will do init on the hardware but that does not involve anything mechanical unless you have a floppy drive and have boot-up floppy seek enabled.
