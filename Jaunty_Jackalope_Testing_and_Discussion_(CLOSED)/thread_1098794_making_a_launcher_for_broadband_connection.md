### Post by volanin on 2009-03-17
Temporary and dirty solution:

1. Right-click on any empty space in the panel.
2. Select ADD TO PANEL.
3. Choose CUSTOM LAUNCHER from the list.
4. Enter "gksudo pon dsl-provider" in the command field.

But people here are right.
This should not be happening, connection is automatic.
