### Post by collinp on 2009-04-13
Alpha, Beta, etc are just the development states for the distro, they are not official releases, in terms of how update-manager and apt-get handle them. Only the packages are updated, and if you update after you install, you will be in beta.

> **fcuk112 said:**
> will there be any difference in the way ext4 is implemented?

I very seriously doubt that there will, seeing how major it could/would be to the people that use it if it was changed.
