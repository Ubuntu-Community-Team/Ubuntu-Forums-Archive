### Post by mickbuntu on 2009-04-15
Kareeser, It can be moved see my thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1120677](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1120677) What I feared has become a reality. That is because of the changes upstream by Gnome. You need to basically now  hit "Alt" while moving your panel or use Panel properties.  That is Right click on your panel --> Properties --> Top, Bottom, Left, Right.
