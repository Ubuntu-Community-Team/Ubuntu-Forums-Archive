### Post by OrangeCrate on 2009-02-09
I don't think I'll report this as a bug, due to the number of people that would actually be affected by this. Take this post as just general information...

This older computer is a 1.4 GHz Celeron with an i810e chipset. In one partition, Hardy runs fine, but when I boot into the development partition with Jaunty running, there's a high pitch whine coming from the box.

Also, on a related issue, though the Gutsy Live CD (and other earlier ones) worked fine, the last two final Live CDs (Hardy and Intrepid), try to make you believe that the onboard video will handle normal effects, and it defaults to that setting. However, it won't, and when you play the Live CDs, there's a little square that pops-up wherever you click your cursor. You can go into appearance settings and change to "no" effects, but the video rendering, and colors are slightly off, not only on the Live CD, but after you install too. I would expect that a Jaunty Live CD would be the same.

This computer isn't doing too well with Jaunty. I'm backing out of testing until I find another box to play with.
