### Post by tommyhot on 2009-04-07
Ok, I found a solution.

Change UI settings via kcm-gtk-kde4 (systemsettings -> appearance ->gtk style) according to your needs. Uninstal openoffice.org-kde and install openoffice.org-gtk. That's all you have to do :)
