### Post by shafin on 2009-03-27
Yes.
You **upgrade** to a new version, like from 8.10 to 9.04, but you **update** an existing version. So updates will take you from 9.04 alpha 6 to 9.04 beta. And actually beta milestone release is an accumulation of all the updates you had since alpha 6. The same goes for the final version.
