### Post by cdekter on 2009-01-12
Firefox works pretty well on Jaunty (I'm running the Kubuntu Jaunty Alpha). A few tweaks here and there and it works just as well as on Ubuntu. 

KDE4 is really coming along very nicely. I have to confess, I'm one of those people who likes pretty icons and some eye candy, something with a bit of polish. I also like to be able to configure things (gnome-power-manager, I'm looking at you!), and a real file manager (Nautilus is a joke). 

It also now has an Add/Remove facility similar to Ubuntu, and the new Adept is a great step forward (still lacking in features in some areas, but coming long nicely as well).

I also like the fact that you can have 90% of the eye candy without ever turning on the compositing feature in KWin.
