You're saying that GNOME Do won't appear when you summon it when the desktop isn't in focus (so the window is in front)?

If so, there are a few threads on this now, go to Compiz Settings Manager > General Settings > Focus & Raise Behavior > Focus Prevention Windows to "off". 

This should do the trick, but please search or "research" in future.
