### Post by todak on 2009-02-13
In Terminal go to **Settings> Edit Current Profile...**. Click on the **Appearance** tab. At the bottom is a setting for **Font**. Try different fonts and sizes and try clicking **Smooth fonts** to see if there is any difference. I use DejaVu sans mono size 9 and have smoothing enabled and have perfectly clear text. But then again., I am using an LCD with 1440x900 resolution.
