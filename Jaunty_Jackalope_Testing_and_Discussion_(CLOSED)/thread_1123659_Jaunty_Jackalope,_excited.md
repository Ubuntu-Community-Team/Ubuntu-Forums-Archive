### Post by blueturtl on 2009-04-12
Looks like it's going to be awesome.

I've already tested the beta and I have to say I like the included theme 'New Wave'. It's easier on the eyes and still legible. Also the new notification balloons/tips are neat and professional looking.

Otherwise it looks to be more of what we're all used to by now; newer applications, yet better hardware support and small touches all around with the exception of one thing:

Boot speed is phenomenal, like promised. They've really worked hard on that.

On the downside, it still has small bugs, regressions and stability issues here and there, but that's what you get for living on the edge. If you're lucky enough not to be touched by any of them, Jaunty is probably the best Linux based OS for the desktop currently.

My brother came to me during the Easter holiday and asked me to put Ubuntu on his computer. Apparently he was done playing with Vista. He left happy with Jaunty Jackalope now on his hard-disk... The time and effort it took me to get everything set up for him was -- like expected -- very little in the end.

So yes, I am very excited with the way things are going for Ubuntu although I now find myself running Debian instead. :-\"
