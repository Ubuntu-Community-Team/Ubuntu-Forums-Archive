### Post by obreiro on 2009-03-28
Obviusly your Gnome configurations overshadow all new configurations. New configs are for new users.

Into folder [FONT="Courier New"]~/.gconf/desktop/gnome/[/FONT] there are xml's about gnome interface, and in [FONT="Courier New"]~/.gconf/apps[/FONT] there are configs of gnome of other programas.

If you remove app's folders and gnome's folders you will remove the inferace's preferences.

Then, logout and login and Gnome will create new configs from default interface.

---

Other way to change this, Is creating new user and moving your files and your " ~/.* " configurations folders. But you must change permissions of all files.

I NOT RECOMMEND THIS WAY.
