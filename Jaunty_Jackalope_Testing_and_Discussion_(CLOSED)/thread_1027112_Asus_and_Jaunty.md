### Post by Gina on 2009-01-01
If you are going to install Ubuntu on a machine that you have never used Ubuntu on, first try a version that is known to be reasonably likely to work.  Jaunty is the very latest "bleeding edge" developmnet version and is NOT intended for general use yet.  It is even at an early stage in the development and although based on mainly working modules, some parts are definitely experimental and virtually certain to fail on some machines.  That is the purpose of this group - to test out the developers ideas on a much greater range of hardware than they have themselves.  We are not newbies and have years of experience of testing software.  We don't discourage those new to testing but to be of help to the project they do need to know how to report bugs and how to get out of difficulties.
