### Post by ddrichardson on 2009-04-09
UNR is not just a desktop launcher. Its a couple of things - UME (the new launcher, which is better suited to small displays), go-home (which takes you back to UME with one click), maximus (which makes everything, well maximised), window-picker (which shows small icons of open applications to save real estate) and the themes.

The most recent snapshot even has a live disk mode so you can test it [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UNR](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UNR)
