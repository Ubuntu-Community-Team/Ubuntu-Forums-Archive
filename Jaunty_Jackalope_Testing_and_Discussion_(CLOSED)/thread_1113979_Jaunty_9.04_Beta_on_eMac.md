### Post by MikeJEvans on 2009-04-02
I thought I might post my experience of installing the Beta on my 1Ghz eMac (ATI)

Overall, it worked much better than under 8.10.  The LiveCD booted without intervention into a 1024x768 desktop, no more "modprobe ide-scsi" and "modprobe snd-powermac" needed to get sound working, it all just happened.

I changed my screen to the native 1280x960, no problems.

The airport extreme was detected and it offered to download the restricted driver for it, and it all seems to be working.  Last night I left it downloading all the updates, and I've not had a chance to really play with it, but it's looking good so far.

Medibuntu, however, still hasn't got the dependencies for libdvdcss2 sorted out :(  It's a good job there are only three packages it requires and that you can download them from the older release repositories.
