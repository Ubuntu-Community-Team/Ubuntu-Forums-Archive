### Post by tormod on 2009-03-25
> **douham said:**
> When suspending, I experience brief display of any particular screensaver before the PC suspends. Other than this supend/resume is fine.

This is normal. The screensaver is always started before suspending to make sure the screen is locked when the computer wakes up. Depending on the driver and settings, things might go so fast that you do not notice it.
