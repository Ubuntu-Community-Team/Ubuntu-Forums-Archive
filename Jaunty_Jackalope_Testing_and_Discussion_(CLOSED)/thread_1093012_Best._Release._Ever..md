### Post by gnomeuser on 2009-03-11
Dislikes:
I am still missing proper PackageKit integration. 
I kinda miss the new volume applet which while not perfect was very nice. 
I wish we had preempt enabled in the kernel as it lowers latency a lot an generally makes the desktop a much nicer place to be especially for audio (or if insanity hit one might experiment with using the -rt kernel for the desktop and see how that fares).
I hate that I can't get ride of all of things like gimp and openoffice without losing my translations. There definitely needs to be some sanity bestoved on that. 
Finally I miss Monsoon, I suspect I will have to learn dpkg magic and put it in the repos myself.

Likes:
Moonlight in the repos.
The single best maintained Mono stack around (maybe topped by openSUSE).
Banshee + mirage are in the repos in very recent versions (and hopefully the PPA will take over updates after release).
It boots like the wind, I haven't timed it but I think I boot to gdm in roughly 10sec on this machine.
It doesn't open pdfs inside Epiphany like Fedora did for me (Mozplugger I believe this is called), boy was that annoying like all manners of hell.
Tap as click and side scrolling remains the default for my touchpad.

Overall jaunty is very nice, nothing hugely new and revolutionary but incremental improvements all around.
