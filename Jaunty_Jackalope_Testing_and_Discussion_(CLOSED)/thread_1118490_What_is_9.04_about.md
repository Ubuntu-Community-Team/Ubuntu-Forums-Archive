### Post by ubuntu27 on 2009-04-07
Ubuntu releases a new version every 6 months because it is GNOME-centric.  GNOME releases a new version every six months.

Ubuntu 9.04 or Jaunty will have the [GNOME 2.26]("http://library.gnome.org/misc/release-notes/2.26/")


You can read the GNOME's Release Note to see what is new on [GNOME 2.26]("http://library.gnome.org/misc/release-notes/2.26/")
