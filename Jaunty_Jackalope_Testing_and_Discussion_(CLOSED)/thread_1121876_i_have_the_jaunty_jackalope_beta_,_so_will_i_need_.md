### Post by drs305 on 2009-04-10
No, regular updates of beta will upgrade you to the final when it's released. If you don't do updates very frequently, you might want to do one a day or two before the final is released. You will get almost the identical product but the servers will be much less busy than they will be when the product is released.

### Post by Michael.Godawski on 2009-04-10
hi djmh,
if you have been keeping up-to-date via the update manager, you automatically have the latest version. To do it via the command line, just enter

```
sudo apt-get update
sudo apt-get upgrade
```
