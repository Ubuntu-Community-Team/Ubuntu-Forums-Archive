### Post by dualscreenman on 2009-04-20
It is possible that your graphics card doesn't have the shader functions needed for the cylinder and sphere effects.

Does adding the "Blue Marble" plasma widget work? If it tells you that OpenGL shaders aren't supported, then that is what your problem is.
