Splashy is a bootsplash, grub is a bootloader.

Your basic misunderstanding of these two concepts would indicate a potential for learning.

It is very informative as to why splashy is not viable as a usplash replacement and why plymouth presents us with an interesting forward/backwards compatible solution for the future.
