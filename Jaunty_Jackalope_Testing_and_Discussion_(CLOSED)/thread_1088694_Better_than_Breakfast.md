### Post by other guy on 2009-03-06
So far Jaunty is very much to my liking. Why is it better than breakfast?


Well it is the first meal when you wake up. You expect it to be fast, taste good and sustain you until lunch. So far Jaunty fills all of that. Minus the silly lunch anecdote.
Ubuntu for many is the first step past Windows. They wake up and see free as in beer is a good thing. Not all partake, some do. Some go further into Linux and evolve into great contributors. Some simply keep using it. Then again, some gripe about every tiny issue there can be.
I just want to say. I been really enjoying this testing release of Jaunty. So far I only had a few things not satisfy me. No major breakage or issues. Even if it did, I would not be unhappy about it. It is not done cooking yet.
Overall it works pretty darn good and **makes me very happy using it**.
I think it has the nimble feeling of 7.10, yet the robust features of 8.10. Very nice delicious combo.

I just want to tip my hat to everyone who is pitching in getting this out the door for late April. I think most who do move over to it then. Are going to be very pleased.
