### Post by entangled on 2009-04-16
On Apple Mac Mini with Intel 945 problems, so have to use vesa driver in xorg.conf.
Otherwise this release is good and has the best device detection yet. My All-in-One Epson was plugged in and worked immediately, printing and scanning. File sharing with Windows Vista works. Wireless network card works. USB sticks and hard drives work. Firewire DVD burner works. Running VirtualBox 2.2 and all devices work. Can copy the entire filesystem over to a Vaio laptop and it runs (after adjusting disk UUIDs in grub menu.lst and in fstab).
Overall a very good product and much more impressive (to me) than the Vista on my wife's laptop or the windows systems I build at work.
