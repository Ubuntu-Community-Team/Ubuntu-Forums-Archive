### Post by Baggers on 2009-04-09
From a tech support standpoint I can understand the change. 
Over time we are hopefully going to have slews of converts joining the ubuntu community from the windows world...and when they come they bring their 'experience' warts 'n' all.
One thing I have seen time and time again is when I get to computers where users have, in a fit of clickage, flung their panel to another side of the desktop, at this point they either panic or just ignore it.

The alt+drag will remedy this, albeit with a slight change to us who like the existing system, but it's not really breaking any fundamental rules, we already have alt+drag for moving windows from the center anyway.

On another note how often do the majority of people move their panels? If alt+drag is to confusing, right-click->properties is still available to them.

Would love to hear your views on this.
