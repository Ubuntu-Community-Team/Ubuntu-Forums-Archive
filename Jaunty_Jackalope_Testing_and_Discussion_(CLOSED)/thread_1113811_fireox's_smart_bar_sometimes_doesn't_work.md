### Post by Korcia on 2009-04-23
Well, I just found out a way for fixing the problem without closing and restarting firefox. I click on the favicon on the left of the smart bar, then you will get a small notification window saying something like: "This web site does not supply identity information". You click again the favicon, without doing anything else, and the smart bar is working again. At least it works for me.
