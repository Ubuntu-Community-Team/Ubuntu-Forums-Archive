Well I was pretty excited after the rather disappointing Hardy versions...so decided to try the Jaunty beta on my OLPC clone...basically everything OLPC except an internal 30 GB  storage. 
Okay, so the thing is neither work for it. The netbook remix is optimized for 10 inch screens while the OLPC's is 7 inch. So there were problems with the larger fonts and cut window borders and buttons.
So then I tried the MID version...which didn't work at all(the hardy version did work)...neither the keyboard nor the touchpad worked. None of the shortcuts (CtrlALTBackspace etc.) worked...so I had to remove the battery to shut the thing down. 
As usual...I'll probably end up installing the desktop version instead , which works better than both. I have a feeling, not as much is being contributed to the Mobile Ubuntu project as the Dektop (or Server) projects.
