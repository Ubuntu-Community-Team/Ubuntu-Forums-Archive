### Post by ugm6hr on 2009-03-07
The downloads themselves should take about 800MB, so you will probably be OK.

But if you've got a separate /home, why no just download to a LiveUSB and reinstall fresh?

You can use this: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=261366](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=261366) to reinstall all your apps.

Download size will be similar, but you can then keep the Live iso for future use.
