### Post by Miguel on 2009-04-09
One comment: The reason why Jaunty mentions there is no proprietary driver for your T42 is because... there isn't. Jaunty uses Xorg 7.5, and no official fglrx driver supports it. As a tradeoff, Ubuntu uses an ATi-supplied preview of Catalyst 9.4, which will be released later this month. This will be the first ATi driver to support Xorg 7.5 and xserver 1.6 but... it drops support for R500 (X1000 series) and older video cards. 

Your T42 has, I think, an ATi 9600 Mobility Radeon (r300), so forget about a proprietary driver in Jaunty.
