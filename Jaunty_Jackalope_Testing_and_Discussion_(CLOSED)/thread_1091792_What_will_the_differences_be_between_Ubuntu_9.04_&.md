### Post by RAOF on 2009-03-10
> **GARoss said:**
> ...
What will the main differences be between Ubuntu 9.04 & Kubuntu 9.04 *other than one's Gnome & the other KDE?* For example, will Ubuntu 9.04 have plasmas or similar to what's in Kubuntu jaunty now?
Thanks

Essentially: none.  That is the foundation of the difference between Kubuntu & Ubuntu - Ubuntu ships a GNOME desktop (by default), Kubuntu ships a KDE desktop (by default).  All the changes are in what's installed by default.  Ubuntu ships with the *ubuntu-desktop* package installed, Kubuntu ships with the *kubuntu-desktop* package installed, but it's a common repository.

Since plasmoids are essentially the base of the new KDE desktop shell you won't find them in ubuntu-desktop.
