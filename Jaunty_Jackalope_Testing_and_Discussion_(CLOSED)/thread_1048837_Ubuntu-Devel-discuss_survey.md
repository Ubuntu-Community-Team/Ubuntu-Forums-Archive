### Post by autocrosser on 2009-01-23
There is a survey available about signal-to-noise on Ubuntu devel-discuss list. I think that we really need a conduit to the developers & in the last few months there has been quite a bit of non-important, non-constructive & quite frankly ranting posts to the list.

This has prompted several developers to remove themselves from the list & has generally reduced the list effectiveness. I have proposed something along the lines of the Code of Conduct being a prerequisite to be able to post there.

I encourage anyone with a constructive point of view to take the survey & help make the list more relevant to the community at large.

In the view that we in the testing group would need the list more than anyone in the forums, I strongly suggest we as a group help shape it's direction.

The survey is available at: [http://pileofstuff.org/ubuntu-survey/](http://pileofstuff.org/ubuntu-survey/)  and will be available until Friday 6 Feb.

PLEASE only post once to the survey to keep it valid.
