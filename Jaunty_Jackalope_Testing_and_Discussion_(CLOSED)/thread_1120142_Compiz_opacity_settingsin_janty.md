### Post by uBeer on 2009-04-08
Well, if you've installed CompizConfig-Settings-Manager, then you could do this:

System > Preferences > CompizConfig Settings Manager

Then you look in the second section which is called "Accessibility" where there is a button with "Opacity, Brightness and Saturation". That's probably the one you were looking for.

Have fun tweaking!
