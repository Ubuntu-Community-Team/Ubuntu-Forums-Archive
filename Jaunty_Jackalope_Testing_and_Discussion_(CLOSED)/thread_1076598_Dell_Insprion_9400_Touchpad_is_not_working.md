### Post by gabriele.bianchi on 2009-02-22
I have an acer 5930g, with a synaptics touchpad, and, after a fresh install of ubuntu 9.04 alpha 4, the touchpad did not work at all. Playing around, I installed gsynaptics and also one of the required dependencies, that is xserver-xorg-input-synaptics (which was not yet installed). Gsynaptics did not work, but after the next reboot, the touchpad was working correctly. Hope this helps.
