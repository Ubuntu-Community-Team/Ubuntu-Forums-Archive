### Post by Abandon on 2009-03-28
Same here. There is a solution however. Use the mscorefonts or liberation. Then go to Firefox preferences, hit the content tab. Go to "Fonts & Colors and hit the Advanced button.

Change minimum font size to 10 and remove the dash on the "allow pages to choose etc. etc.

Makes a big difference!
