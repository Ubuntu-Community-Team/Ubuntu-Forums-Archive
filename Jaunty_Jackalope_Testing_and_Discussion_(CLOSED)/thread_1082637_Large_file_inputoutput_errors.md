I don't tend to agree that DVD is unreliable for backup. It depends on the dye brand+model you use and how you store them. Single use discs (non RW) are also not prone to accidental deletion and filesystem corruption like harddrives and flash units. Often people tend to buy the cheapest discs they can find and also keeping them exposed to sunlight.

I have started getting input/output errors with DVD+R's as well just about when alpha5 started but it seems It's a bad batch (100 pieces) in my case. I cannot be 100% sure until I've tried Windows. I've upgraded my firmware of the drive.
