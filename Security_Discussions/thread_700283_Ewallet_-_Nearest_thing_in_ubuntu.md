### Post by notwen on 2008-02-18
You may want to look into [kwalletmanager]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/utils/kwalletmanager").  It is a KDE app so installing it will also install a couple of other KDE libraries/packages.  It looks to match the description of app you're after, but I cannot verify whether it can open your previous eWallet data files.  You should be able to find it in Synaptic.  Hope this helps and best of luck to you. =]
