### Post by davarino on 2008-03-10
I assume that you have checked your Edit > Preferences > Inbox preferences and have the General box ticked in the Junk preferences for the mail account in question. Additionally, I assume that you have checked your Edit > Plugins menu and have the box ticked for your spam filter.

I have had problems with Spamassassin in the past and run Bogofilter instead.
