### Post by chewearn on 2008-02-12
You should just update.  At this early stage in your installation, if the update breaks something (which should rarely happen), then it's a simple matter to reinstall immediately.

But if you don't update, you will have 188 updates sitting there, and more new ones coming in frequently.  Meanwhile, the security holes are not patched and bugs are not fixed.
