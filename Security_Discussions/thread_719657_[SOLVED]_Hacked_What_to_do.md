### Post by tubezninja on 2008-03-09
You haven't been hacked.  The hammersound.net site server, on the other hand, has been.

You don't need to worry, though you'll be probably be unable to get that sound font until hammersound's folks fix their site.  Though once they do that, I'd give anything you download from there a good, thorough virus scan.

By the way, a [SoundFont]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SoundFont") is a pre-recorded instrument pattern used in MIDI audio compositions.

EDIT:  Unfortunately, getting hacked seems to be a [common occurrence]("http://web.archive.org/web/20070323133407/http://www.hammersound.net/") for hammersound.
