### Post by mthei on 2008-01-06
Just go to the download page and select Fedora or "Other/Static RPM" (as I don't know if it's Fedora or a deriviative on OLPC) from the drop down menu. To get flash to work, you have to go into Tools > Preferences > Advanced > Content >Plug-In Options and then add a new path for flash directing it to the Firefox plugin (or that's the easy way). HOWEVER, please be warned that the newsest version of Flash _does not work_ on Opera 9.25, on any Linux distro. Opera blames Flash, Flash blames Opera.
