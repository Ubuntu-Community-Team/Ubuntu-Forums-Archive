### Post by Frumious Boojum on 2008-03-19
Red Hat Enterprise Linux is basically selling the support, not the software.    Like sr20ve said, you can download and install it legally.

However, if you would like support and not pay, then go for an OS based on Red Hat, such as Fedora.

Yellow Dog, likewise, is based on Red Hat, but again, they sell the technical support when you purchase it.   Yellow Dog, though, doesn't enforce buying and hide the freeness.
