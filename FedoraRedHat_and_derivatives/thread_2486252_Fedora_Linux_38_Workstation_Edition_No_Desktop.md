### Post by BBQdave on 2023-04-24
> **Kurt_Alan said:**
> Any way to get a traditional desktop with Fedora?

Fedora Workstation default DE is Gnome. Other DE's are available with [Fedora Spins]("https://spins.fedoraproject.org/").

You can even load a Fedora base and use something like [Window Maker]("https://www.windowmaker.org/") to create your own desktop :)
