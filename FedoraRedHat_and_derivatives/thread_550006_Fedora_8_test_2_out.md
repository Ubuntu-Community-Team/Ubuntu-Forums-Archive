### Post by igknighted on 2007-09-13
[http://fedoraproject.org/wiki/F8Test2/ReleaseNotes](http://fedoraproject.org/wiki/F8Test2/ReleaseNotes)

Also, there are a ton of new liveCD choices: standard gnome (i686,amd64, ppc), KDE (i686, amd64), Developer Live (i686) and Fedora Electronic Lab for electrical engineers (i686).

Highlights include Pulse Audio which is a very advanced sound server which looks really good to me (lets you individually adjust the volume of each stream for example), OpenJDK, CodecBuddy for painless audio codec installation, and a new Gnome theme.

NOTE: I am not sure all of these got into test2, but they are listed as done or almost done on the features page, so the likelihood is high that they are included.
