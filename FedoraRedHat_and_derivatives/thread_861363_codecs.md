### Post by mthei on 2008-07-16
For multimedia codecs, USE EITHER:
rpm.livna.org
OR
freshrpms.net

DO NOT mix these two repositories, as they contain different versions of the same packages and are completely incompatible together.

However, I've had some issues with Livna as of late. Mainly with their XMMS plugins.
