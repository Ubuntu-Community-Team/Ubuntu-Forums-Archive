### Post by Elfy on 2012-11-15
Further to [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2012592](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2012592)

bringing this up in FC meeting 

I am still positive about 

1. If Staff are going to be absent for an extended time it is only good form that the they advise the Forum at their earliest opportunity, then the Council will know what is happening and there is no need to consider replacement.

2. If a Staff member is going to be absent for greater than say 6 months they should consider stepping down and of course be able to rejoin the staff upon their return if they so desire .

3. Staff absent for greater than 6 months with out any notification then it can be deemed as abandoning the role and replacement can be sought.

In addition I believe we should adhere to 

[http://www.ubuntu.com/project/about-ubuntu/leadership-conduct](http://www.ubuntu.com/project/about-ubuntu/leadership-conduct)

Very very specifically the Stepping Down section.
