### Post by Tommy on 2009-01-28
The CARLUG Install Fest will be Saturday January 31, 2009, 9am to 5pm at the [Faulkner County Library]("http://fcl.org/"), 1900 Tyler St., Conway, Arkansas. Electricity, network connections, linux installation CDs, and food will be provided. Special topics TBA.

For the latest information, see our web site at [http://www.carlug.org/](http://www.carlug.org/)

### Post by Tommy on 2009-01-28
An install fest is a gathering / special meeting / party -- 

[LIST]
[*]Where we help folks get FOSS (Free Open Source Software) installed and running.
[*]Where we share and discuss the latest free software.
[*]Where we discuss topics of interest to enthusiasts.
[*]Where we trade, and buy and sell used hardware.
[*]Where we eat donuts and pizza and have a great time...
[/LIST]
Door prizes through the day (must be present to win) -- 

[LIST]
[*]Complete GNU/linux system, including CPU & monitor with Ubuntu installed
[*]O'Reilly computer books
[*]Accessories
[*]Other items
[/LIST]
What to bring --  

[LIST]
[*]Bring your computer if you need help installing or debugging
[*]Bring $5 to register for door prizes
[*]Bring a voluntary donation to defray cost of food
[*]Bring your old and new hardware for trading or selling
[*]Bring copies of your favorite distribution
[/LIST]
GNU/Linux Distributions -- 

[LIST]
[*]Ubuntu / Kubuntu / Xubuntu / Edubuntu
[/LIST]
(Mirror of Ubuntu Repositories will be available for *i386* and *X86_64*) 

[LIST]
[*]Knoppix
[*]Gentoo
[*]Debian
[*]Fedora
[*]...bring your favorite!
[/LIST]
