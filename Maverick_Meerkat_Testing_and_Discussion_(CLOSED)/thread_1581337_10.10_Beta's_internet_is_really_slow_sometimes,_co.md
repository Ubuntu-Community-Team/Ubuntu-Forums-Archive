### Post by andrewthomas on 2010-09-27
enter the following into terminal```
gksu gedit /etc/default/grub
```and change
```
GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT="quiet splash"
```to 
```
GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT="quiet splash ipv6.disable=1"
```then 
```
sudo update-grub
```and reboot
