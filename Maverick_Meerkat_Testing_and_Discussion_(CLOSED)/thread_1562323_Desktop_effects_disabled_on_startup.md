### Post by Untitled_No4 on 2010-08-27
This used to be a known problem with ATI cards, perhaps Intel cards have the same problem?
The workaround for ATI cards was to go to the Advanced tab in Desktop Effects in System Settings and mark "disable functionality checks". This would probably work for you as well.
