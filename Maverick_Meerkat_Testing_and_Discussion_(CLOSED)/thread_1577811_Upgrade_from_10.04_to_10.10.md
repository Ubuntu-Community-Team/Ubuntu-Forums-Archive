### Post by cpmman on 2010-09-19
Being mildly cautious but happy to test - especially when it is testing what I propose to do anyway, I copied my live 10.04 over to an external disc and satisfied myself that it was operationally good for me.  I then did the ```
update-manager -d
``` method of upgrading to 10.10.  A quick reboot and ```
sudo apt-get update
``` pulling in a few changes followed by another reboot to allow the brilliant ureadahead to do its job and I was good to go.  Apart from having to restore the sound applet it seems so far to have performed just as it should do and the only significant item still to test is Virtualbox.

I realise this does not either pose or answer any problems but I believe that such a positive experience may give confidence to anyone else who may choose the upgrade rather than the clean install method.
