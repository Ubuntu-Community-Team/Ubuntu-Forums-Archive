I had the same issue; surprisingly, switching the destination to DVD+RW media (vice CD-RW media) fixed the problem.  This problem has happened before (and it's not unique to 'buntu, either); for some rather weird reason, sometimes, the automated process in creating ISO images bungs things up to where CD-sized images only work properly on DVD-sized blank media.

(For a reminder, harken back to both 'buntu 9.10 and openSuSE 11.2; in both cases, live CDs had to be burned to DVD media due to imaging errors during the testing program.)
