### Post by teachop on 2010-09-26
A few days ago Rhythmbox would sync with my Droid X, which was Android 2.1, as a music player.  I thought that was pretty cool.  The phone is updated now "OTA" to 2.2 Froyo, and the music sync is gone.

I don't know if this constitutes an issue for Maverick discussion or not.  I pretty much keep my Maverick testers updated daily, so I cannot guarantee that the Android revision caused this, but that seems most likely.

There is a menu in the phone for the USB connection.  The choices are first "PC Mode", which does nothing.  The second is "Windows Media Sync" - that makes Rhythmbox crash and toss up the bug dialog.  The third/fourth are "USB Mass Storage"/"Charge Only" and they work in their way, but are invisible to Rhythmbox (no music player).
