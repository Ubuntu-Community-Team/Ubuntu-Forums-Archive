### Post by kansasnoob on 2010-09-24
> So, do you think I should leave grub2 in sdb2, even though it looks for core.img and doesn't find it?

Yes, that won't hurt a thing.

It should be OK to install grub2 to the mbr of any drive and also either it's own OS's "/" or "/boot" (if one exists).

The trouble begins when people install grub2 to other OS's partitions.
