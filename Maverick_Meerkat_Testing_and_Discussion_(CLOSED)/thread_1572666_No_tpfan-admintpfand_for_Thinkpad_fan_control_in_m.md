### Post by atomic777 on 2010-09-12
So, to anyone that comes across this, one workaround is to just install the thinkpad fan utilities ppa from here: [https://launchpad.net/~tp-fan/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~tp-fan/+archive/ppa) 

The PPA only has packages for jaunty, but they seem to work just fine on 10.10 so far.
