### Post by 23meg on 2010-09-05
Obviously it does, since it's pre-release. But broad statements in the lines of "It needs work", "It's not ready", "It's better/worse than $OTHER_RELEASE", especially addressed to testers, never help towards making it better, and only stir further uncalled for negativity.

The only way you can help make the final release better is through being as specific as possible as to what isn't working (usually possible through [debugging]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DebuggingProcedures")), and [filing bug reports]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ReportingBugs"). If you need any help with those, don't hesitate to start appropriately titled threads and ask for help.
