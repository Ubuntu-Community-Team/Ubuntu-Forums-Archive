### Post by ronparent on 2010-09-09
Just for your information, I found that the 10.10 desktop was installable on a raid 0 (a first!). And, at least for the desktop version, it does install grub 2.

What is the behavior you are seeing with Mandriva? Is Mandriva in the menu? If it's in the menu, what are the boot instructions (found in /boot/grub/grub.cfg). If it is not in the menu, then for some reason it is not being picked up in the grub-update.

For your guidance the following reference outlines most of what you need to know about using grub 2 : [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2) 

I have no reason to believe that Mandriva wouldn't work with grub. If the grub update isn't finding it, it may be possible to manually add it to the configuration files. I don't think there is any reason to revert to grub(0.9x) or that it would give you better results.
