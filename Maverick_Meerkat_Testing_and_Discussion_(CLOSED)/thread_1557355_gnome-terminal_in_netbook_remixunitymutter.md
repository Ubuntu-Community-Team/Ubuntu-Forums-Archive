### Post by cariboo on 2010-08-20
Gnome-terminal is under Applications-Accessories, just click the ubuntu icon in the upper left corner, then click the applications icon in the panel, on my system, it's just above the trash can. Once you have gnome-terminal running, right click the icon and select keep in launcher, it should stick there from then on.
