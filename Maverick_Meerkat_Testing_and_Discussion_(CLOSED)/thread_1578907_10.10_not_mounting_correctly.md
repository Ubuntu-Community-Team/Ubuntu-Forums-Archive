### Post by seeker5528 on 2010-09-22
> **tymanthius said:**
> I assumed that was b/c I chose to encrypt my home dir.

I have not played around with encryption, but that would make sense to me.

I would guess then that automatically mounting any partitions inside your home directory from fstab will not produce the results you wish.

The easier solution is probably to create a mount point in '/media/' or '/mnt/' then make your '/home/tymanthius/Pictures', /home/tymanthius/Docs', and '/home/tymanthius/Shared' directories symlinks to the desired partitions.

Typically this means creating directories with the desired name on the target partition and chown it with your UID and GID, then you can symlink to them and move all your files into them.

Otherwise you have to either manually mount the partitions after you log in and unmount before you log out or figure out how to set things up so they will mounted after you log in and unmounted before you log out using mechanisms that will be done after login and before logout.

Later, Seeker
