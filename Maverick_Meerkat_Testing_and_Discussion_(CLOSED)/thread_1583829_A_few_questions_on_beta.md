### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-09-28
I realise it's very personal, but I like the themes and wallpapers of 10.04 and also now 10.10, where the default wallpaper is much like that of Lucid.  Buttons are still on the left, but that's easily dealt with, so not a worry for me.

I installed the daily build last night (27 Sept) on an old Acer Travelmate 2200 laptop, which has the ATI 9000M graphics card, using the open source ati/radeon driver.  Lucid would not run on that as it overheated terribly, but maverick appears to have overcome that, and the graphics performance is much, much better than it was on Lucid.

I think the ATI 5570 has a proprietary driver, but I'm not sure about that, so why not simply try the live CD of beta or daily build and see what you think.

What I see, I like a lot, but as I said, it is personal choice in many cases.
