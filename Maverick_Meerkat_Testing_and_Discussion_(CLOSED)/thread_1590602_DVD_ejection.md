There is a long standing series of issues related to this where fixing/updates  to one thing has created other issues.
Probably started as the eject button not working on some installs/hardware and gone from there.
Best thing to do w/ disks containing a filesystem is to use the r. click eject (or unmount if that's still available or been manually restored to nautilus

orig. 'bug'
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/baltix/+bug/397734](https://bugs.launchpad.net/baltix/+bug/397734)

Edit: 'failed to eject' from a burn is unrelated - nothing was 'mounted' to begin with
