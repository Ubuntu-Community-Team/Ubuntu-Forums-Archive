### Post by wolf187 on 2010-10-06
Hello

I recently had a problem with the SiS 771/671 Graphics card drivers so then I asked for help but nothing had worked so then I had decided to communicate directly with the developers of ubuntu and the SiS chipset. Mark Shuttleworth had told me that I should communicate with SiS and they had told me that they will get an engineer to sort this problem out and implement a 3D driver for Linux for SiS 671 graphics card. I will post the emails that I had sent and the reply to these emails that I had sent, hopefully these drivers will be implemented soon.
