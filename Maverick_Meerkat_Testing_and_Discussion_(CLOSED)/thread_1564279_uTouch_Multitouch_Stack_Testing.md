### Post by 23meg on 2010-08-30
Most of you are probably up to date on the multitouch-related happenings in Maverick, namely the introduction of Canonical's [uTouch stack]("http://www.markshuttleworth.com/archives/455"). With days to go until the beta release, things are advancing fast on that front, and some extra testing is needed to ensure that everything is working fine, and non-multitouch pointing remains functional with the new components.

As usual, Ara Pulido has [detailed testing instructions]("http://ubuntutesting.wordpress.com/2010/08/30/testing-your-multitouch-device/") that I don't need to repeat here; check them out, register on the tracker (which is derived from the ISO testing tracker many of you are familiar with), subscribe to the mailing list and report your findings as new test cases arrive.

Non-multitouch devices (mouse, tablet, trackpad, etc.) can be prone to regressions as new versions of the components land, so people who don't have multitouch devices can also perform the test cases for those devices to make sure that there are no regressions.
