I installed wubi from alpha3. Dropped me at a grub prompt. To continue the installation (and also subsequent boots) I had to load up the wubildr.cfg from the grub prompt:
```
configfile (hd0,msdos2)/ubuntu/winboot/wubildr.cfg
```

(it's on /dev/sda2 => hd0,msdos2)
