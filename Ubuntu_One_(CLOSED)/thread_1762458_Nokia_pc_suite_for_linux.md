### Post by Fedz on 2011-05-19
Howdy!
Yes! Network Manager does support Nokia XpressMusic :)

I have a Nokia 5800 XpressMusic & it works perfectly fine & no problems, I can't imagine it been different with a 5310.

Plug in fone to usb > goto network connections (icon in system tray by clock or via system settings/control panel) > mobile broadband > Mobile Broadband (tab) > Add.
