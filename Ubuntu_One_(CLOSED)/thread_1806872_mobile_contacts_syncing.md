### Post by duanedesign on 2011-07-18
Their is work being done to update the website to reflect the changes to Mobile Contacts. In the meantime here is an exerpt from the [Ubuntu One Blog Post]("http://voices.canonical.com/ubuntuone/?p=943"):

> We’re working on completely revamping contacts sync for mobile to give you an overall better experience. As of June 1, 2011 the current Ubuntu One contacts sync for mobile will no longer be active. We’re stopping support for our current service so we can focus our energy on launching a much better service sooner than we could otherwise. For those who use music streaming, please continue to enjoy music streaming and you will have the ability to sync contacts later this year. For those who have the Ubuntu One mobile package and only use it for contacts sync, please [contact us]("https://one.ubuntu.com/support/contact/") and we will provide a refund for all your mobile package charges as well as cancel your mobile package subscription.
