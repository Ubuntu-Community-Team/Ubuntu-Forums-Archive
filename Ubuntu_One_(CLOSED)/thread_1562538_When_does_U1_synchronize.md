### Post by duanedesign on 2010-08-30
It syncs when you add/remove/change files in your Ubuntu One folder or a User Designated Folder. Their is a limit bandwidth feature (Me Menu--> Ubuntu One, 'Devices' Tab. You could reduce the upload/download rate. Also on the 'Services' Tab you can turn the file sync service on and off.
