### Post by duanedesign on 2010-09-21
Your Ubuntu One folder needs to stay where it is. You can however sync any directory in your $HOME folder. This is called User Designated Folders(UDF). You do this by right-clicking on a folder and selecting 'Sync with Ubuntu One'.
