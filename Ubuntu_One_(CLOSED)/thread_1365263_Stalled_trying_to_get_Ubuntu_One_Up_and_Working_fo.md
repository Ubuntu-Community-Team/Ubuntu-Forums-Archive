Hello,

I continued playing with Ubuntu One. I read in another post that if I didn't have an icon (which I didn't), to right click on a panel area; click on the add to panel from the drop down list and type in Notification Area in the search area and then click Add at the bottom.


That gave me an icon, and with that I was able to start playing successfully with Ubuntu One. (It's looking good!).
