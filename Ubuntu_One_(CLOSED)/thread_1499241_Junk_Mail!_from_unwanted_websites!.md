### Post by lisati on 2010-06-01
Avoid replying to junk email if you can. You'll either risk annoying some innocent bystander (email addresses are easily faked and forged in outgoing emails) or you'll just confirm to the spammers that they managed to reach a real email address. The same can be said if you are considering bouncing emails - you can't rely on the "From" or "Reply To" email address to work out where to send the bounce message.

I report any junk mail that makes it past my email server's spam checks to [Spamcop]("http://spamcop.net"). I've put a few more tips here: [http://lisati.homelinux.com/email/email-tips.html](http://lisati.homelinux.com/email/email-tips.html)
