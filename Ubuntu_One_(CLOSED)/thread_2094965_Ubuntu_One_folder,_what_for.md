### Post by uzumakifahim on 2012-12-16
In short, cloud storage keeps your files online. In short cloud storage like Ubuntu One give you several features. 
1. All files and folders you keep in the ubuntu one folder will not be destroyed, even if you harddisk has been crashed or destroyed.
2. You can access those files in the ubuntu one folder anywhere in the world, through the one.ubuntu.com dashboard.
3. You can share your files and folders easily, who have ubuntu one account.

You just need to paste your desired (which you want to keep online) in the Ubuntu One folder. It'll automatically upload the files to Ubuntu One server.

### Post by sdowney717 on 2012-12-16
In this world anything can happen.
You could accidently delete your synced files, then ubuntu one will delete them from the cloud. You may empty your trash then they are gone. Ubuntu one server could explode, mad admin deletes files, then its synced back to your drive and deleted. A malicious code could do the same. Ubuntu one could go out of business, be shut down.

Your best bet is to have multiple backups, not synced for files you never want to loose, preferably stored elsewhere.

I sometimes email myself an attachment on web mail.
I have a lot of pictures I dont want to loose and some programming code.
