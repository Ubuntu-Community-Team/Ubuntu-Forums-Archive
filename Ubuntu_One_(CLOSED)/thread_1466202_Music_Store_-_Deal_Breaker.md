### Post by tricomp on 2010-04-30
Once the files are downloaded to your computer you can delete them from your UbuntuOne storage. I have transferred files after they downloaded to another computer -a Mac -and they play fine, so you obviously don't need to be connected to the file copies in storage for them to work. 
Having them in storage does make it easy to access them from any other computer, but once they download and are in Rhythmbox it's just as easy to transfer them from the local computer instead of the cloud. My plan is simply to delete everything once I fill up my (free) 2G (and have done a grsync backup) and start over again. The UbuntuOne storage just provides you with cloud backup, it isn't necessary to listen to the files once they are downloaded.
The Ubuntu One FAQ is here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/FAQ](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/FAQ)
