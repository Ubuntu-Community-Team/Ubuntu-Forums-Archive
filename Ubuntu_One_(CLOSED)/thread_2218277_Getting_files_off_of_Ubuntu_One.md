### Post by thnewguy on 2014-04-24
Download the u1ftp package. Run "python u1ftp.zip". Then you can use any FTP client to connect to your local machine. The u1ftp osftware creates a tunnel to your files on the One servers, seamlessly linking your FTP client to your remote account.
