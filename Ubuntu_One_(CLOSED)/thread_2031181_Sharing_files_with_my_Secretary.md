### Post by uzumakifahim on 2012-07-23
Hi [Ifaistos,]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=45182")
actually you can't setup multiple account on same computer. If I'm right then you are trying to say that you have a adminstrator user account on a PC and your secretary is using another user account on the same PC. In this condition your secretary is trying to setup Ubuntu One on his account (Secretary user account), but it is showing wrong password. 

The fact is that, you can sync multiple computer or device with Ubuntu One or Dropbox, but you can't setup multiple Ubuntu One or Dropbox account on a single computer. Even if you tried with different user account on a single computer the result will be same.

I think this will help you to understand your problem.

Thanks.
