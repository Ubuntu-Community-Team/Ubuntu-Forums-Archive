### Post by kostkon on 2011-03-25
Concerned? Don't be.
> **tshirtdr1 said:**
> Am I able to re-download the software in Ubuntu through the software center onto a new setup if I reformat?
Your purchases are connected to your u1 account. Thus, your purchases are never lost. There is a option in the software centre that allows you to reinstall your purchased software.
> **tshirtdr1 said:**
> What about installing onto multiple systems at once in my home? I don't like buying the same software twice.
Yes, just login with the same u1 account you used to purchase the software.
