### Post by geoffmcc on 2011-11-29
I am having problems with my tomboy note sync to the Ubuntu One service and it is caused by the fact that I had my notes organized into separate notebooks. 

I am sure this issue will be resolved, I am currently going threw email support with Ubuntu One and have received a confirmation that the problem is because Ubuntu One does not support or plan to support the Templates and Notebook features of Tomboy.

I cant wrap my head around why an organization feature would not be implemented into Ubuntu One's syncing of Tomboy notes. He went on to say "although it wont be supported they do need to look at how these things are handled by the syncing process." so if that's the case the best users can expect is not having Notebooks break Tomboys syncing with Ubuntu One and allow us to organize the notes after they are synced with the computer.

If you are a Tomboy note user and an Ubuntu One user please contact Ubuntu One and inform them that you think it is a mistake to not support the ability to keep notes into separate notebooks (folders) for organization. 

P.S. - I hold no ill feelings toward Ubuntu One, it is a great service I just feel there has got to be others out there like me that want this, and we need to let them know.
