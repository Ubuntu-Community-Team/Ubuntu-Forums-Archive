### Post by duanedesign on 2012-01-26
Their are two ways to share a folder with other people. You can 'Share' the folder or 'Publish'. If you publish a file you get a public URL that you can share with your friends and family.

If you 'Share' a folder you enter the email adderess of the other Ubuntu One user who you would like to share the folder with. You can choose to share with or without giving the other person editing capabilities. This means they can only view the file if you choose. With editing capabilities this is a great way to collaborate on projects.

Folders shared with you will appear in the 'Shared With You Folder'
