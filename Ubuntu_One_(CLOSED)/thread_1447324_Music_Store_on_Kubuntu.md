### Post by undoIT on 2010-06-02
It is a shame that Kubuntu is treated like a second-class citizen. I have been using Ubuntu as my primary OS since 7.04 Feisty Fawn. After Hardy Heron, I haven't been all that happy with the changes going on with Gnome in Ubuntu and I switched to Kubuntu for my primary laptop. And, some things seem to be getting worse in Gnome. I just removed Ubuntu 10.04 from my second laptop and put on Kubuntu. Now both laptops are KDE-based.

I am much happier with KDE as the desktop environment for a variety of reasons. I also recommend Kubuntu to friends and family.

Is Canonical going to be shifting gears to only focusing on Gnome even more and letting KDE fall by the wayside?
