### Post by ndefontenay on 2010-03-14
Ok. Just had a look and on this link:

[https://one.ubuntu.com/account/machines/](https://one.ubuntu.com/account/machines/)

You should be able to see the machines connected to your dummy account. Remove your machine, create your new account, connect your current machine to this new account. Easy :)

You can also simply keep using the same account and remove all machines and files to start fresh.
