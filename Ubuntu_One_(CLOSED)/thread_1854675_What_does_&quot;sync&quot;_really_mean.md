### Post by mkarnicki on 2011-10-26
No, there is no way, unless you unsubscribe from a cloud folder that has just finished uploading to the cloud.

Sync means keeping your files synchronized in multiple places - between all of your computers. If you change a file on your home PC, it changes on your friends PC or at your work station. Ubuntu One is not a remote locker. Access to files from the web is just an added value. We try our best to keep your files *in sync* on your machines.
