### Post by growingneeds on 2012-04-18
To delete the contents of your synced documents really quickly, sign in to Ubuntu One via the web interface at one.ubuntu.com. Delete the files that are taking up space in your account and sign out. The deletion should trickle downwards to the client, restoring the 5 GBs of space.
