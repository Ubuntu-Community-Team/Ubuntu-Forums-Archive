### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-01-31
Ubuntu-One is, in its simplest terms, just an online, free, 2GB cloud area for you to sync files and folders so that you can keep separate computers synced with whatever you want
[https://one.ubuntu.com/](https://one.ubuntu.com/)
If you don't want to use it, remove it from your System->Preferences->Startup Applications listing (as I have).
