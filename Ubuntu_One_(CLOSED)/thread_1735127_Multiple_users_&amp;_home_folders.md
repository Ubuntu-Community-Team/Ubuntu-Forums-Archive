### Post by aaaaalex on 2011-04-27
What you want to do is give each user his or her own Ubuntu One Account and then share your photo folder to them as read only. The photos will then pop up in their ~/Ubuntu One/Shared With Me/YOURSHARE

You can share a Folder with other Ubuntu One Users conveniently with a right-click and then selecting the Email from the Ubuntu One (Evolution) Address Book. Or you can simply set it up over the Ubuntu One Web Interface (there is also an option to tick/untick to make sure its read only).
