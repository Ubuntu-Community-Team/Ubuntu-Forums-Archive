### Post by duanedesign on 2010-11-30
The easiest way to add all your own music is just to mark the folder it's in as being synced with Ubuntu One. See [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/FAQ/HowDoIMoveMyExistingMusic](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/FAQ/HowDoIMoveMyExistingMusic) for details: basically, just right-click on your Music folder (or wherever your music is stored) and say Ubuntu One > Synchronize this folder. This will copy your music into your personal cloud, and then it's all available for streaming.
