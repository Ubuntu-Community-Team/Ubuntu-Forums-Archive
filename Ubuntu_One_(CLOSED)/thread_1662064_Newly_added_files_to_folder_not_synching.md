The newly added file did eventually synch up in U1, but it took about 1 hour and it was just a small test text file I created.

Out of curiosity, I downloaded Dropbox and installed it and it works like a charm. The 2 drawbacks are that their plan is a bit pricey at 50 GB at $99/yr. I like how U1 just allows you to keep the folders where they are and synch folder by folder. I think I'll give U1 a chance and hopefully in future releases and updates, the bugs get worked out.
