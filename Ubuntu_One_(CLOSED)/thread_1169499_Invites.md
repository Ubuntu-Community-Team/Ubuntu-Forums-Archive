### Post by joshuablount on 2009-06-18
> **mxboy15u said:**
> Not much right now...but I think Canonical has more cloud based plans that will build on Ubuntu One.

That's a pretty good description. 

Right now Ubuntu One is basically a file storage, syncing and sharing service that looks very similar to box.net or dropbox, but we're planning quite a bit more interesting stuff in the relatively near future (including screen sharing, providing syncing APIs to all the apps on the Ubuntu Desktop that can take advantage of your Ubuntu One account, syncing preferences and settings files in an interesting way, etc)

As far as invites, the best thing to do right now is jump on the queue at [https://ubuntuone.com](https://ubuntuone.com), we're trying to send out invites as quickly as makes sense, while scaling our service so that all of our existing users will still be working without trouble. 

Thanks for your patience!
