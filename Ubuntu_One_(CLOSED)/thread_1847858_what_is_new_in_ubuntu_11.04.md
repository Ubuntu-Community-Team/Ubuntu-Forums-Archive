### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2011-09-21
Why the Ubuntu One threads?

Previously to Ubuntu 11.04, Ubuntu's Desktop Environment (the bit that you see, often known as DE) has been GNOME2.

At 11.04, Canonical rolled out Unity, a new DE. It features a dock-like launcher and a program/document finder combined. It is, however, highly controversial. Many people think that the community should have been consulted, and they want their old GNOME back.
