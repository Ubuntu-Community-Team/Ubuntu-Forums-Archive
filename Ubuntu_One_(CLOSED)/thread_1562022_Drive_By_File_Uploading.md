### Post by Ellipsis on 2010-08-27
Drive by file uploading. 

The idea here is to give another person (or large group) the opportunity to drop files into your Ubuntu One account WITHOUT having their own U1 account. The idea here is that a separate password could be handed out to a group of people and they could drop files into your storage through a website interface.  

This would be best implemented on the website because 

1) No software to download/install 
2) Opportunity to market Ubuntu/One from there.

Use case-

A professor asks her students to hand in their assignments digitally. The students upload their work to U1. The professor instantly has a copy on her computer. 

This idea borrowed from: [Stuff my Drop Box ]("http://lifehacker.com/5622534/dropbox-uploader-allows-anyone-to-upload-files-to-your-dropbox-account")
