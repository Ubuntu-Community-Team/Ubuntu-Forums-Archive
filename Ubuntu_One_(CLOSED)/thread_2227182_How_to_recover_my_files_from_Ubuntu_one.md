### Post by deadflowr on 2014-05-31
You need to go to Ubuntu one home page on-line.
When you login, you will see an option to download your files in a .zip.
Click on it, it might take a moment, but eventually it will start the download process to a simple zip folder, presumably in your Downloads folder.
Once it finishes, you can then unzip it and move the files where you want.
