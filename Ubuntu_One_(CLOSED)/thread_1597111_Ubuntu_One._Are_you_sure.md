You should be able to login to Ubuntu One website. From there navigate to the files tab on your dashboard. Each directory or file on there should have a drop down link on the right called 'more'. If you click on that one of the options revealed is download. At present it appears that this is only available on files and not complete directories, which is a bit of a pain.

You will need to re-register your PC in order to be able to synchronize from it again. It's probably a good idea to remove the old machine account token from your Ubuntu one account first. To do that, got to the account tab on your online dashboard, select the link to 'view machines connected to your account' and then select the account entry and click 'Remove Selected Computers'.

To Re-register select System - Preferences - Ubuntu One to launch the client, and when prompted re-register the PC in your we browser.

Hope this helps

Nobby.
