### Post by the.scarecrow on 2010-01-30
I think the primary intention is for sharing files between your and others computers and as such its got great potential and is very useful.

That said, there is no reason why you can't use it just for storage but 2GB isn't much so you may want to buy additional space.

I would make sure you use a very strong password on Launchpad to protect your files and even then I would not put really sensitive information like bank account details on it.

It also provides an easy way to keep backups of some files "off site" like for example family photos that can never be reproduced. A good hedge against fire and flood damage.

Hope these ideas help you, welcome to Ubuntu.
