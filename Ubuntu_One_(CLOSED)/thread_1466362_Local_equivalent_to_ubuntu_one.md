### Post by spuzzdawg on 2010-04-30
I think that the simplest way to sync your calendars like that is to get yourself a google calendar account and sync both computers to it. Then whatever you add to one calendar will appear on the other through the Internet. The advantage of this method is that you can access the calendar from outside the home network if you want to.

There are probably other ways to do it with rsync or by running an LDAP server or somesuch but I can't really help you with that. You could have the google calendar technique up and running in 10 mins though.
