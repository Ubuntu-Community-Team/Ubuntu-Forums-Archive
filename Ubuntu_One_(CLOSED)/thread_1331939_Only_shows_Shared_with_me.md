You should see an "Ubuntu One" folder under "Places", do you not see that? If you go to ~/Ubuntu One/ do you not see any files? I'm guessing you may have uploaded files via the web. If so, you'll want to quit and restart the Ubuntu One client for files from the web to show up. If that doesn't work, please right-click on the Ubuntu One client and select "Report a Problem" and provide as much information as you can about your problem, including the steps you took and any errors you may be receiving.

### Post by punkybouy on 2009-11-28
The "Shared with Me" folder is where you would go to see files and folders that are shared with you by other users (collaboration is one of the uses) Otherwise you can create a folder on your local PC and then right click it and one of the options is "Share on Ubuntu One" and when that is selected you can share that folder with someone else by entering their email address.
