### Post by nixi on 2011-01-04
Hi all


Has anyone here managed to cancel the Mobile subscription without having the account's files erased? The support page on how to cancel the Ubuntu One Mobile (Monthly) Subscription seems to conflate the subscription with the Ubuntu One account. 

For example, it tells me "what will happen" to my Ubuntu One account if I cancel my Ubuntu One Mobile subscription: 

> "We will delete your files from the Ubuntu One online storage space". 

"Files which you have shared with other people will no longer be available to them after the next time they synchronize." 

Moreover, after I clicked on the Cancel button a message appeared saying that something went wrong, and now I seem to be stuck for yet another month's subscription. 

Ubuntu One Mobile is buggy, and that's why I want to cancel my subscription, yet I find it difficult to interpret the support page more charitably than extortion to demotivate paying subscribers from leaving. The Subscription is offered as an additional function to the account, and cancelling an addition should not imply deletion of the account's files. 

:/

P.S. 
Attached jpg files are screen prints of the (non-informative) error message, and the support page on how to cancel the subscription (aka extortion).


EDIT
I recieved an email from Ubuntu One confirming that the information on their support page on how to cancel the Mobile subscription is wrong. The files on my account are OK, my subscription is cancelled, and I will get a refund :)
