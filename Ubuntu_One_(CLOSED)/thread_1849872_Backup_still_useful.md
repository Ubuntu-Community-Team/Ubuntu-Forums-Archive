### Post by Th3Alchemist on 2011-09-25
Once a folder is synced, it takes time for the upload process to be completed, depending on your data size and internet connection... Sign in the Ubuntu One homepage and check if your files are there... Double check it before you lose any important data. There also should be a checkmark on the icons of your folders which shows if files are entirely on the Ubuntu One server.
