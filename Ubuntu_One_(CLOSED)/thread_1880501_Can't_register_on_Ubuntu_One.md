### Post by An Sanct on 2011-11-26
Ubuntu One and many other things are powered by the SSO - Single Sign On authentication service, this groups everything together.

Sign up online at [https://login.ubuntu.com/](https://login.ubuntu.com/)

And use Ubuntu One with that login data.

PS. I just signed in and noticed, that the forums (yes, this place here) is also covered by the SSO login...
