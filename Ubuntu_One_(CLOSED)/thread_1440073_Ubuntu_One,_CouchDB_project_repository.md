I have started a wiki page with the intention of bringing together people who are developing projects using Ubuntu One, CouchDB, and desktopcouch. If you are making an app that uses CouchDB or Ubuntu One, or adding this functionality to an existing application please add your project to the wiki. You can also email or msg me on the forums with your info and I will add it to the page.
