Whoever implemented these captchas: **** YOU!

Originally, recaptcha was created by a computer sciences guy as a two in one tool: text that computers couldn't decode via traditional OCR meant that it's a good way of distinguishing humans and computers, and and the same time books that couldn't be digitized via same OCR now can digitized with some human help. The idea wasn't too bad, but now Google runs this. Their gain is another way of collecting data about who visits what site as the captchas are loaded from their servers. I guess google analytics and google ads wasn't enough.
