### Post by duanedesign on 2010-06-23
I am guessing you are using Lucid.

Before starting to work on your document open the Me Menu > Ubuntu One  to go to the Preferences Panel. Click the 'Devices' tab and next to your computer name is 'Disconnect'. Clicking this Ubuntu One should not sync until you finish your work and click the 'Connect' button. Which will have replaced the 'Disconnect' button on the 'Devices' tab.

That is my best guess as to how to achieve what you want Hope it is helpful.
