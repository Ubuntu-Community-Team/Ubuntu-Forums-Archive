### Post by Tank Jr on 2011-11-29
I have music purchased from other sources in my 'Music' folder. For the UbuntuOne store I created a folder called 'Music_U1' in my home and inside that folder I added a symlink to the '~/.ubuntuone' folder.
This works, as all the players I use (Rhythmbox, Amarok, Exaile...) can see the music.
