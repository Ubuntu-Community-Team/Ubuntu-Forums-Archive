### Post by ndefontenay on 2010-03-14
Ok one thing that could be happening is that the same 3 files already exist on your other account?

Since this computer is also connected to your dummy account, it could be that even though the files are not on your computer, they are already on the dummy account.

You get a conflict problem because it sees 2 versions. One from the dummy account, one that you just added to the ubuntu one folder.

If you can confirm this, then there's 2 way to fix it:

1) Manage to connect to your dummy account and remove your computer from it
2) You won't like this but: Make a clean install of your laptop. This will literally be like you have a new computer altogether and you can then attach it to your new account.

Nico
