### Post by john_roshi on 2010-04-15
Dear Ubuntu One,

I was really hoping our relationship would work out,
but alas, you've let me down too many times.

I put up with your idiosyncracies hoping I would
get used to it.  But I have certain expectations 
that you just aren't living up to.

When I drop a file in your folder and a green check mark
appears, I expect to go to another registered computer,
power up and find that file in your folder.  

Maybe it's partly my fault; perhaps I don't have your
configuration set properly, or I failed to reconnect
after suspending, or something. But I'm a busy guy,
I can't stand a high-maintenance relationship for
something that should be nearly transparent.  

So I'm leaving you and going back to my flash drive.
I'm taking my data out of the cloud and putting it
back in my pocket.  

regretfully yours,
--john
