### Post by duanedesign on 2011-11-24
You are safe to remove the entry if no devices are using it to connect to Ubuntu One. Click the remove button to remove it from your list of devices. You can also manage your device  list at  [https://one.ubuntu.com/account/machines](https://one.ubuntu.com/account/machines) .

It also does not hurt anything to leave the entry.
