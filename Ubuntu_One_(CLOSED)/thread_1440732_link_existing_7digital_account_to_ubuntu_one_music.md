### Post by thebear78 on 2010-03-28
Sorry... there's no way to connect your Ubuntu One account with your existing 7digital.com account. Though they sell the same songs and 7digital is our partner, 7digital.com is a completely different store than the Ubuntu One Music Store. This is covered in the music store FAQ: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/MusicStore](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/MusicStore)
