### Post by duanedesign on 2010-08-13
looks like this is dependent on the file format. I tested this with three different formats.
.ogv
.avi
.mpeg
The .ogv and .avi opened in the browser and the .mpeg did not, it went straight to the download prompt.

So what I did was the following:
[LIST=1]
[*]*Copy* the URL of the published video.
[*]Go to *Applications --> Sound and Video --> Movie Player*
[*]In the Movie Player open *File --> Open Location*
[*]*Paste* in your URL(if it is in the clipboard it should automagically appear there)
[*]Click *Open*
[/LIST]

The Movie opened and played without me downloading it. I hope this helps.
