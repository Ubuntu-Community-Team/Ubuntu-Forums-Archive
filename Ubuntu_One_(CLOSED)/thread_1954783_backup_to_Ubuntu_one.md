### Post by d4m1r on 2012-04-08
Unless you paid for an upgraded account, you cannot upload more than 5GB so my guess is its taking this long for 2.8GB. Based on your slower connection however (5-7mbps) that is basically normal. If 5-7mbps is your download speed, then you upload speed will be even slower.

If 5-7mbps is your actual upload speed, even then it can only upload at a rate of ~800kb/s which will mean it will take several hours to upload 2.8GB. As well, I doubt you'll be able to even max out that 800kb/s because Ubuntu One is a shared service so you'll only be able to upload as fast as their servers allow.
