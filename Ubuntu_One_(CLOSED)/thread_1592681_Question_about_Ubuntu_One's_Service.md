### Post by duanedesign on 2010-10-11
If you are worried about losing files and can not make a backup what you could do is remove your computer from your Ubuntu One account. Then once your upgrade is complete you can add your computer again. The following steps will make sure your computer is completely removed and that the add computer process will be triggered the next time you open Ubuntu One.

   1. Quit the Ubuntu One Preferences
   2. Open Applications->Accessories->Passwords and Encryption Keys or System > Preferences > Password and Encryption Keys on Maverick
   3. Click on the arrow next to "Passwords" to expand the folder
   4. Right-click on the Ubuntu One token and select "Delete"
   5. Go to [https://one.ubuntu.com/account/machines/](https://one.ubuntu.com/account/machines/)
   6. Click on the checkbox next to your computer
   7. Click the "Remove selected computers" button


When you have completed your upgrade and are ready to add your computer again do the following:

   1. Open Me Menu->Ubuntu One
   9. a web page (or dialog box on Maverick) should open, prompting you to add your computer to your Ubuntu One account
  10. Add your computer
