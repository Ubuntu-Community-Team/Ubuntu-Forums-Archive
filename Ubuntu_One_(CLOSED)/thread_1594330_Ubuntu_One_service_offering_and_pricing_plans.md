**Ubuntu One Plans** - updated 25. november 2011

**Ubuntu One Free**
This plan offers 5GB of space to sync your files. You also get access to the Ubuntu One Music Store with this plan. This is the base plan and you can add on additional features  with the 20GB packs and Music Streaming.

**Ubuntu One Music Streaming**
With our Music Streaming service, you can wirelessly stream the music you&#8217;ve saved in your Ubuntu One personal cloud to your mobile device. You also get 20GB of additional storage so you can continue to grow your music collection. You can use the 20GB to sync any type of data. Early next year we will have a Web based Music Streaming player so you can listed to your music collection on any computer with a browser.

Ubuntu One Music Streaming is available for $3.99 (USD) per month or $39.99 (USD) per year. Users interested in this add-on can try the service free for 30 days. Ubuntu One Music Streaming will be the perfect companion to your morning exercise, daily commute, and weekend at the beach &#8211; we&#8217;re really excited to bring you this service!

**Ubuntu One 20-Packs**
A 20-Pack is 20 GB of additional storage for your data. Users will be able to add multiple 20-Packs at $2.99 (USD) per month or $29.99 (USD) per year each.

All add-ons are available for purchase in multiple currencies &#8211; USD, EUR and, recently added, GBP.

We know that you will enjoy these new add-ons as well as the performance enhancements we&#8217;ve made to Ubuntu One in recent months. If you have questions, our recently updated [support area]("https://one.ubuntu.com/support/") is a great place to start. There you&#8217;ll find a link to the [current status of Ubuntu One services]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/Status"), a link to our frequently updated list of [frequently asked questions]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/FAQ"), and a way to [send us a direct message]("https://one.ubuntu.com/support/contact/"). As always, you can also ping the team on IRC (#ubuntuone in freenode). We welcome your questions, comments and suggestions.
