### Post by PJSingh5000 on 2013-08-01
satymM,

I think you have a couple of choices.

First, othe Devices tab, you could remove your computer/device, so it doesn't synchronize  (but that could make installing the Ubuntu One app pointless).

Alternatively, you can chose which folders to synchronize on the Folders tab.  Uncheck the "Sync Locally" checkbox next to folders that you do not want to download files into).  Then continue to use the web interface ([https://one.ubuntu.com/files/](https://one.ubuntu.com/files/)) to upload files, without them being downloaded back to your computer.
