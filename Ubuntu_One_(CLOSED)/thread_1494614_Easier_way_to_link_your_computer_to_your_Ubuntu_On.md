### Post by chewit on 2010-05-27
Some of you maybe struggling to link your computer to your Ubuntu One account. In the Ubuntu One FAQ it is suggested to run this command:

u1sdtool -q; killall ubuntuone-login; u1sdtool -c

However, this will not always work.

A method which seems to work for me no problem, is to open rhythmbox and clikc on the Music Store. This will link your account and computer together no problem.
