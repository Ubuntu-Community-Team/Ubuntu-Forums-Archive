### Post by gwoodruff on 2010-06-12
Howdy all, I just wanted to give everyone a little heads up if your using Ubuntu One on a DSS service. I installed and set it to sync my firefox bookmarks. I do not think it ever actually synced my bookmarks between my pcs, but it did so much constant uploading of data that it made me violate my ISP's fair usage policy. If your provider also has a limited fap you may want to monitor your upload usage while running Ubuntu One sync. My ISP's upload limit is 2.3gb per a rolling 30 day period. This is limited but the price I pay for living in the country.
