### Post by jehiva on 2010-11-22
GoDaddy storage is $29.99 a year for 100gb of storage, Ubuntu One is $29.99 a year for 20gb of storage (so five times as expensive).

While auto sync is nice, the same thing can be accomplished by setting a cron job with rsync to any FTP server (no special client or software needed).

If Ubuntu One offered an equal pricing structure I would definitely be a paying customer.
