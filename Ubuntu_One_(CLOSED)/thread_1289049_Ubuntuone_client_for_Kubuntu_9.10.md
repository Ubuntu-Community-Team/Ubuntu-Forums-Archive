### Post by joshuahoover on 2009-10-15
> **kwokyinc said:**
> Anyone know whether there will be a Ubuntuone client for Kubuntu in the coming 9.10 or in future release?

We've discussed doing it and would like to. Even better if we can get folks in the Kubuntu community to contribute something. We'd love to help others build more clients for more distros and operating systems. If you're interested and able, please contact me directly joshua.hoover at canonical dot com.

In the mean time, we're focusing on improving the core services we're launching with. Kubuntu users are advised to install the GNOME client package. I've done this and it worked well. I realize this isn't ideal for KDE users, but it does work.
