### Post by Curtiss on 2010-04-25
I've been getting my music at amazon and there was an album I wanted real bad. But I'm using Lucid Lynx 10.04 RC 64 bit and amazon does not have a down loader thing for 64 bit only works with 32 bit. So I decided to give the music store a try. The music store had what I wanted and for 2.22 US dollars cheaper than amazon and at the same bit rate 256 gotta love that LOL.

So I already had a Ubuntu one account the free 2 gigs. Making the purchase was pretty painless but when the download process started it was rather slow and out of 18 tracks 4 of them did not download the music store was showing the tracks in Que. So I let it run overnight and next morning same thing. I then rebooted my PC and back to the music store and was able to down load the tracks and all was good.

I think the music store is coming together nicely and will most likely use it again just hope things get better in time. I also like how the music is backed up to the Ubuntu one.

Curtiss
