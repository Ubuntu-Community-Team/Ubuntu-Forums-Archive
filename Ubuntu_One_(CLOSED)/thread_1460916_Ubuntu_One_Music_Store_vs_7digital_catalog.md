### Post by thebear78 on 2010-04-24
> **artofsnow said:**
> Hello,

I found some artists/albums available on 7digital that are not available on Ubuntu One Music Store. Is that done on purpose? 


AoS: 7digital.com (7digital's retail brand) has more country-specific regional stores than we currently do with the Ubuntu One Music Store. There's a fair amount of manual maintenance for each store and in order for us to run lots and lots of country-specific regional stores, we would now need quite a large staff. We will build some automated tools to help us manage multiple stores easier and expand the number of country-specific regional stores for the Ubuntu One Music Store. 

In the meantime, we have grouped some countries together into larger regions (most of the EU and World-wide). This helps us give more people access to the store but the trade-off is it reduces the selection of music available from the major labels. Most major artists license their music on a country-by-country basis. The more countries we add to a region (EU or World),  the less of a chance of having the same artist terms apply to all of those countries and fewer major artists are available. There is still lots of fantastic music available from great artists in both the EU and World stores and we are working with 7digital to figure out ways to highlight them for customers.

I hope I haven't added to the confusion but that's how it works :)
For a complete list of our regional stores, go to [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/MusicStore](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/MusicStore) 

Thanks!
