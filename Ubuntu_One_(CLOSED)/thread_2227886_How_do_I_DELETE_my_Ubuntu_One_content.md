### Post by qamelian on 2014-06-04
Point your web browser at [http://one.ubuntu.com](http://one.ubuntu.com) and login. Go to the Files tab. To the right of each of you files/folders you should see a link that says "More". Click on this link and you see the option to delete appear under the name of the file or folder.
