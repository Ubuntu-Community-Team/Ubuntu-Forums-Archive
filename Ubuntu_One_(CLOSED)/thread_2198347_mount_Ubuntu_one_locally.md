### Post by Irihapeti on 2014-01-08
You can access Ubuntu One via FTP ([https://one.ubuntu.com/help/faq/how-can-access-my-files-via-ftp-on-ubuntu/](https://one.ubuntu.com/help/faq/how-can-access-my-files-via-ftp-on-ubuntu/)), which mounts your space as a local drive. But, in my experience, you can't actually work with the files, e.g. open and edit them, in the U1 area. You'd still need to copy them to your computer to do that.
