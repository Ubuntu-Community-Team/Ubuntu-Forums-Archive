### Post by jonest1 on 2009-12-25
The desire to know how long we should expect for synchronization to another ubuntu one client is drawn out of a practical question.  

If I am sharing a directory with another Ubuntu One customer, I may be in communication with the other party and place some files in the shared Ubuntu One directory per the course of that discussion.  It is useful in that type of scenario to know about how long it should take for the files to be uploaded and made available to a 3rd party.

It may be useful to have a service which would synchronize on demand for these types of instances.
