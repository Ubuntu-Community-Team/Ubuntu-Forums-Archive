### Post by duanedesign on 2011-11-11
Ubuntu 11.10 does not have an Ubuntu One Music Store Plugin for Rhythmbox. Because Rhythmbox and Banshee use different toolkits/webkit stacks we would of had to ship it twice built against two toolkits. This was unacceptable with our limited resources.
