### Post by duanedesign on 2010-06-07
Hmm. I just purchased a song. It downloaded to my computer in seconds. It seems to be working fine.

If you are having a problem with the music store a couple of things.

Is the song showing up in your cloud storage. Do you see the music at [http://one.ubuntu.com/files](http://one.ubuntu.com/files) under Purchased Music.

If the song shows up there but not on your computer the issue is local. For that I would file a question on Launchpad Answers, or go to irc channel #ubuntuone on freenode and ask for rye. He is generally on weekdays between 13:00 - 21:00.


If the song is 'stuck' and not showing up in your online storage than I would recommend going to the #ubuntuone channel on freenode between 13:00 -- 21:00 on weekdays for help. Ask for alecu. Or you can file a Launchpad Answer.

You might be asked to file a bug. This helps the devs see your logs so they can better understand your issue.
