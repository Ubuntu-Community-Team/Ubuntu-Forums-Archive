### Post by duanedesign on 2012-02-10
Currently contact sync is being completely revamped. Mobile contact sync is not available and contact sync between desktops is not available to some users. Their [were issues]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-desktop/2011-November/003474.html") that arose with the technologies that we were using. Because of this we are developing U1DB. This will be the new platform that we will be building our data sync services on. We have made good progress on developing U1DB and recently [released a technical preview ]("http://voices.canonical.com/ubuntuone/?p=1342")for developers to play with. We are hoping the new contact sync will be available for Ubuntu 12.10.
