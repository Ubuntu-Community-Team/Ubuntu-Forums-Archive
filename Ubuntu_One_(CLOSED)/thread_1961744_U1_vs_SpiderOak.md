### Post by adamsu on 2012-06-02
I use all three, mostly because I am cheap and want to back up a lot of files without paying for extra storage. 

What I like about each:
[LIST]
[*]Spideroak is slow but secure. The backup tool's interface is a bit unweildy, but once you get it going it runs well on its own.
[*]Dropbox is easiest to share with. It is great being able to just right-click and get a URL to share with others. I know U1 does this as well but my experience sharing those links with some people hasn't been as good as with Dropbox.
[*]Ubuntu One integrates so well with Ubuntu. I love having it on the different machines I run, it is fast, and 5 GB free is very generous.
[/LIST]

Try them all, use them all. As long as you are backing up different things you should be OK.:)
