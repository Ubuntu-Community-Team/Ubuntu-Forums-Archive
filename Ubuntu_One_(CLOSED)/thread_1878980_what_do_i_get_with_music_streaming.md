### Post by duanedesign on 2011-11-11
The Music Streaming Plan gives you 20GB of space to sync your files and music. It also lets you stream music to your mobile, and soon the streaming web player. You can stream any mp3 or ogg file. This includes music you rip from CD, music bought at the U1 Music Store, mp3's bought at other music stores, etc..
