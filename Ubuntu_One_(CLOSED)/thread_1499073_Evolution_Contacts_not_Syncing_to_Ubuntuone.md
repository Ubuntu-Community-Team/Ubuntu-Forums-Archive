### Post by scarey9 on 2011-05-08
I am successfully running U1 sync with everything functioning including contacts sync  on Natty 64bit. This is what i did. Make sure you have couchDB installed and have the evolution-couchDB plugin

1st, quit evolution
$ killall -9 e-addressbook-factory
$ /usr/lib/desktopcouch/desktopcouch-stop
$ rm ~/.config/desktop-couch/desktop-couchdb.ini
$ /usr/lib/evolution/e-addressbook-factory

Then did a restart, presto contacts appear. Hope this helps. Reference this link. 

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/evolution-couchdb/+bug/727370](https://bugs.launchpad.net/evolution-couchdb/+bug/727370)
