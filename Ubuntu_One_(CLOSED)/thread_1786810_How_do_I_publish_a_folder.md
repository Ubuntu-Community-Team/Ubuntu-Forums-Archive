### Post by mkarnicki on 2011-06-20
Hi there,

Currently you can't publish a folder (like on Dropbox). We only support shares, which sadly has the disadvantages you mentioned. We are, however, looking into better ways to share folders.

Temporarily, if you want to share graphics, you could look into this nifty script by Roman:

[http://blog.rtg.in.ua/2010/12/ubuntu-one-gallery.html](http://blog.rtg.in.ua/2010/12/ubuntu-one-gallery.html)
On launchpad: lp:~rye/+junk/ubuntuone-galleria

As far as Android is concerned, we're working on that as well. Stay tuned! :)
