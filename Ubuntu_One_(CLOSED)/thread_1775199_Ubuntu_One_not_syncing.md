### Post by coffeecat on 2011-06-05
The Ubuntu One server seems to be working again this morning. The Ubuntu One control panel syncs and I can log into my account at the Ubuntu One website.

Just a tip for others who are in the same situation. I purchased a couple of albums yesterday before I realised there was a problem. I received an email confirming that payment had been taken but, of course, couldn't download my mp3s. This morning, when I logged into my Ubuntu One account, the two new albums were still not yet in my Purchased Music folder. So I opened Banshee > Ubuntu One Music Store > My Downloads tab and immediately the albums started to be transferred from 7digital to my cloud storage and within a minute or so they were being synced with my local machine. I have them all now.
