### Post by mikeputnam on 2007-10-09
Hey everyone,

In the last few months, the North East Wisconsin Linux Users Group (NEWLUG - [http://newlug.org](http://newlug.org)) has become pretty active with 10-20 people meeting monthly in De Pere, WI to discuss Linux and other techie things.  If anyone happens to be in the De Pere area on the 2nd Thursday of each month, stop on by!

For those much too far from De Pere, see [http://wisconsinlinux.org](http://wisconsinlinux.org) for a LUG near you!

Mike Putnam
