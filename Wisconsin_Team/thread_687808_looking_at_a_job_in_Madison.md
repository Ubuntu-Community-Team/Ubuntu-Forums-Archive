### Post by lifewithryan on 2008-02-18
As Ek0nomic said, there are alot of windows friendly folks but I'm seeing some changing tides.

For instance:  my current employer provides mainly open-source and java solutions and runs entirely on open source, (with the exception of some of our admin staff that want to run windows).

I am currently contracted out to a place that runs their entire web presence on open-source technology as well.  Outside of that, American Family here in Madison is dropping all of there UNIX servers with WebSphere and converting over to JBoss on SuSe Linux.

More and more folks are open to this idea.  

For developers though, its moslty Java when it comes to open-source.  There are some PHP jobs...(my fav).  I see a TON of ASP, .Net crap as well so I'd say that there is a lean towards M$, but I also see a shifting tide.  We just need to get out there and get the word out.
