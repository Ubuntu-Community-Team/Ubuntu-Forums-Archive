Hi Aeonis.

A really great place to do research is in the _*community*_ wiki:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community")

There are lots of really informative articles, if you know what you're looking for. Also, one of my favorite resources for Ubuntu security is this forum post:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=765421]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=765421")

Besides that, here's a few other links you might enjoy:

[The Big Ol' Ubuntu Security Resource]("http://www.itsecurity.com/features/ubuntu-secure-install-resource/") - Perhaps a little outdated, but still great.
[LinuxSecurity.com]("http://www.linuxsecurity.com/")
[Linux Cheat Sheets]("http://www.linuxscrew.com/2009/12/21/best-of-linux-cheat-sheets/")
[Lintuts]("http://www.lintuts.com/") - A Linux/Ubuntu tutorial site. I actually follow them on Twitter.

I hope these were informative.
