### Post by wisd0m on 2009-02-27
for accounting software, you might try GnuCash [http://www.gnucash.org/](http://www.gnucash.org/) or [http://www.osalt.com/quicken](http://www.osalt.com/quicken) or [http://www.osalt.com/quickbooks](http://www.osalt.com/quickbooks)

for printing, look here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsPrinters/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsPrinters/) This is a good starting point to find printers that work with Ubuntu. I see on Dell's site they list some that "work" in Linux.
