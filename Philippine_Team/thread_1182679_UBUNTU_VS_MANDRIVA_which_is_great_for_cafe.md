### Post by badrra on 2009-06-11
I guess depende sa objective. It may even be a choice between Windows and linux, then Mandirva and ubuntu. 

Like kung puro surfing then linux is best. Word processing, printing, graphics and all that, pwede din linux dun. Gaming is another issue. Kung pang gaming ittayo mo then windows. 

Depende din sa budget. Kung medyo mataas, at ang objective mo is mostly games, then Windows. Otherwise, Linux. Some games can run on linux, others will not like O2Jam for example. 

I tried Mandriva once, it did work fine and it looks nice, but looks can be changed sa linux regardless kung anong distro. You can even use compiz on both. Familiarity is another thing to consider. Applications are basically the same OO for instance, Gimp, etc. 

i suggest you first look into the business side of things, list down your objective, constraints, and then base your decision on that.
