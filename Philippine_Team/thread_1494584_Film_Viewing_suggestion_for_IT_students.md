### Post by creek23 on 2010-05-27
Patent Absurdity ([http://patentabsurdity.com/](http://patentabsurdity.com/)) is a the documentary on software patents. The film is free and open. Sa mga IT student dyan, you can do a film viewing activity on any of your subjects related to Professional Ethics or even on your school's IT Week (or IT Day).

Grab a popcorn and enjoy a 20 minutes long documentary. :popcorn:
