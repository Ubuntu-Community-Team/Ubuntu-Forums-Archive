### Post by scotty64 on 2010-04-29

I find the whole Broadband (and home LAN) over powerline idea completely uncool. Why ? This stuff is causing loads and loads and loads of noise in the already highly polluted electromagnetic spectrum. Think about overhead powerlines carrying a digital RF spectrum of several MHz (literally broadband) - These powerlines will act as huge aerials, broadcasting the signal like any radio transmitter does.
Being a licensed radio amateur (ham) myself I support the BBC, hamradio organizations and others who have an interest in a working HF band to oppose these developments.
And - last but not least I have experienced how a LAN over powerline home solution caused interference on a switching power supply, completely frying it and almost causing a fire...
