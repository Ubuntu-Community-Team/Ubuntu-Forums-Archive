### Post by jsgotangco on 2008-12-13
The general practice of swap is 50-100% of your current memory. Since it is generally part of your hard drive, setting it to more than that won't really give any tangible advantage.

### Post by pendletone on 2008-12-13
the general consensus is (or was): it should be twice the size of your ram. however, i think this doesn't apply anymore. palaki na kasi nang palaki ang size ng ram ngayon eh. so if you have 4 gigs of ram, it would be overkill to make your swap size to 8 gigs!
