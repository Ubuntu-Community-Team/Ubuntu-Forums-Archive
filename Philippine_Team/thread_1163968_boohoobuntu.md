If you're going to Hanoi in Vietnam, make sure you visit Halong Bay. Don't cross roads by yourself - make sure you go along with the locals. Sample the local beer and coffee shops.

In Bangkok, the Tuktuks will usually bring you to a jewelry shop because they want free gas coupons. You don't have to buy anything though from the jewelry shop, but there are some nice finds there, albeit a bit expensive. Better use the sky train for all travels (its much faster and you'll avoid the traffic). Pat Pong, we'll leave that for them to know what perversity is out there. Chatuchak - definitely a must go if you like buying stuff. Don't miss the malls too like the MBK, but i find the malls in the Philippines to be more superior in a lot of ways. Geeks should check out the Phantip plaza.
