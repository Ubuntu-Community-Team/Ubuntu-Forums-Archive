### Post by dynastywarrior on 2008-08-14
This is just to inform everybody here in the Phil. Loco Team that the Netgear wireless PCMCIA card, model WG511, version 1 works well with Ubuntu Hardy desktop out of the box.

This wireless card uses the Prism54PCI driver which was quite notorious to previous versions of Ubuntu.

Caution though when connecting it with routers having the AOSS feature....make sure its disabled in the router's configuration...
