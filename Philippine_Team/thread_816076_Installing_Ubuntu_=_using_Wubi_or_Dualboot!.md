### Post by jeffimperial on 2008-06-02
Grabe ka naman bro sa color! baka next time pwede less noisy. :) Para naman easier on the eyes..

> hassle..may pinagkaiba pa sila kung idudualboot ko siya!?
Ang alam ko, isa lang in terms of performance. Disk I/O [as in read & write to your hard disk] is a little slower in Wubi install. Aside from that, it's a full-fledge Ubuntu install.

More info here -->[http://backports.ubuntuforums.com/showthread.php?t=798212](http://backports.ubuntuforums.com/showthread.php?t=798212)

Kung halimbawa naman gusto mo nang mag conventional install with dual-boot, hindi mahirap. I've installed Ubuntu in more than three machines pa lang naman. I haven't encountered any unsupported hardware yet. Everything worked out of the box. 

Sa boot menu, Ubuntu uses Grub. Google will help you find out more about it. Grub should be able to give you a boot menu when you install Ubuntu *after* windows.
