### Post by wersdaluv on 2010-07-04
[GNOME.Asia]("http://gnome.asia/") [Conference 2010]("http://2010.gnome.asia/") will be in Taipei, Taiwan on Aug 14-15. It will be held with COSCUP (Conference for Open Source Coders, Users and Promoters), Taiwan's largest FOSS conference. Registration starts soon here [http://coscup.org/2010/en/register](http://coscup.org/2010/en/register) . 

I was fortunate to have my talk accepted and be arranged into the program. Sana, may iba pa sa Ubuntu-PH na pumunta rin :)
