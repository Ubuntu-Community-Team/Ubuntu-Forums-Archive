### Post by jaceleon on 2011-03-16
This week, eto ako at busy kasi may nangyari dito.

Paano kasi, there's this friend who suddenly have a problem with his HP desktop na parang for public use na toilet ang paggamit ng madla (as in madlang people na hindi kaano-ano...and btw, siya ang may description niyan sa PC niya). Ayun, sabi niya sawa na siya sa Windows, puro virus, disk check every boot up at ang tagal bumukas. So I suggested using another OS (paki-imagine anong OS ang prinomote ko :D ). And ang sabi niya, do it and please get rid of Windows for good.

After installing Ubuntu (with the restricted plugins) plus Firefox 4 and Chromium 12 from PPA, I decided to give the poor guy something to boast his PC on sa mga friends. And then I installed Macbuntu on it. And he was so "freaking" astonished. Mukhang mamahalin na raw desktop niya. Hindi na rin siya need mag-maintain ng antivirus, or defragment. Ang bilis daw ng Firefox 4 at Chromium 12. The PC never gets that haggard in his simultaneous and multiple browser usage ng PC.

He test run on it immediately. E di ayun, nasiyahan siya, lalo na nung naayos niya yung kanyang mp4 player na hindi madetect sa Windows (nasira partition so I helped him on using GParted).

Kinabukasan I got loads of his neighbors asking me to do that "same" setup on their computers and laptops. He really did boast it over, literally. And yung mga nakakita were so amazed sa speed ng loading ng webpages, the "put any device and it will work" feature and the Mac Theme, which makes his "public toilet" PC turn into a very expensive-looking Macbook Tower.

Also, marami-rami ring nagpaayos ng mga player na may virus while I'm at it. TY nga dun sa isa, binigay nga yung extrang player niya sa akin as a token ng gratitude :D.

So What do you think is the best selling point for these guys sa Ubuntu? Is it the plug and play feature, the speed of the computer, the latest software, or the sosyal-looking Mac Theme na masyadong inaasert na kailangan meron din daw sila? I am asking because this could be a very good reference later when someone asks me kung bakit better ang Ubuntu than Windows. Of course, I could just tell them all of those, pero in this case, what is the best selling point?
