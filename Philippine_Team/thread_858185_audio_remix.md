### Post by pendletone on 2008-07-13
it depends on *how* you'll be remixing your songs. kung simpleng audio editing lang, like crossfading to the next song, you're best bet is audacity.

but if you're looking for more control over your audio (albeit more complex), like recording/editing/mixing multi-track audio, look no further than [ardour]("http://ardour.org/").
