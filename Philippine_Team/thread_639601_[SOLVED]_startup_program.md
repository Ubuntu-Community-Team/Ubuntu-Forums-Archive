### Post by loell on 2007-12-13
in **System Menu** --> **Preferences** --> **sessions**

you can add your startup program from there, click add  and enter the  the program with the parameter you want in the **command entry**, in this case a  video player playing on fullscreen mode

```
totem --play --fullscreen  sample.avi
```

where sample.avi is the video filename that you'd like to play.
