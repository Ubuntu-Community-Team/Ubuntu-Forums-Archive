### Post by Jucato on 2007-12-01
The question that launched a thousand flamewars. :D

I am a KDE user, only because it is personally what I prefer, what I find to be more usable, what I think is better for me. I would not and cannot tell everyone that they should use KDE or that KDE will be better for them, because I don't believe that it is.

Bottom line, ang mas magandang gamitin ay yung sa tingin mo ay makakatulong sa iyo, sa pangangailangan mo, sa kagustuhan mo, sa situation mo. There is no absolute best, only a best for the situation. Ika nga ni Alanis Morisette, "it's like ten thousand spoons,when all you need is a knife."

In Linux, we have outgrown the one-desktop-to-rule-them-all technological limitation. It's time our minds catch up too. Use what is good for you. Try them both out. Try out others. Then you'll know. :)
