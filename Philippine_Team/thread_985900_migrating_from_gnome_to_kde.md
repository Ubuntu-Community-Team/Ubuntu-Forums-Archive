### Post by pendletone on 2008-11-24
@jepong: yung kubuntu ISO/CD mo ba e 32-bit at yung pc mo naman e 64-bit? dine-detect kasi automatically ng wubi ang architecture ng pc mo and downloads, if necessary, the 64-bit ISO for you. pero kung gusto mo talagang mag-32bit, kelangan mong i-place yung 32-bit ISO in the same folder as wubi.exe, or start wubi with the "--32bit" argument. kung pareho naman ang architecture ng pc mo at ng kubuntu, baka naman corrupt ang ISO/CD kaya siya nagda-download.
