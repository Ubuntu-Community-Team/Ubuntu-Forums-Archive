### Post by reneorense on 2010-05-24
Kung CMS po, I go for Wordpress...

Madaling gamitin, user-friendly, and very accessible for any user... Madaling gumawa ng theme at ma-customize ang php and sql codes.. Shempre, lalo na ang CSS... :)

Second would be Drupal... Hindi sha kasing accessible ng wordpress, but you can create accessible themes for clients...

As with joomla and others, they may have quite a lot of good features, pero po kasi for me, it will all boils down to user-experience, usability and accessibility... :) And Joomla won't comply.. :)

Hope this helps...
