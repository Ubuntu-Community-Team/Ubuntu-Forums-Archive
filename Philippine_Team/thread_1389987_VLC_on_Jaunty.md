### Post by andrew.46 on 2010-01-27
Hi homer,

> **I_have_seen_the_light said:**
> when I try to open a DVD movie, the VLC screen would come up  for about .5 of a second and then disappear.

This is usually a sign that the default video out device selected by vlc does not work with your current system configuration. A simple test for this is to select another video out from:

Tools ---> Preferences ---> Video ---> Output
