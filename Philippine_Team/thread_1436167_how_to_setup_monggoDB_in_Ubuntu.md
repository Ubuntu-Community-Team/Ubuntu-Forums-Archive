### Post by jsgotangco on 2010-03-22
```

$ mkdir -p /data/db
$ curl -O http://downloads.mongodb.org/linux/mongodb-linux-i686-latest.tgz
$ tar xzf mongodb-linux-i686-latest.tgz
$ ./mongodb-xxxxxxx/bin/mongod &
$ ./mongodb-xxxxxxx/bin/mongo

```
