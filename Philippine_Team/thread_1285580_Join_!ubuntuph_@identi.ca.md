### Post by Laibcoms on 2009-10-07
Inviting everyone to join the ![ubuntuph](http://identi.ca/group/ubuntuph) group at identi.ca.

Identi.ca is a FLOSS Micro-blogging service based on the Status.Net (formerly Laconi.ca) platform.  If you are a Twitter and/or Plurk user (even Facebook), Identi.ca's features (and all Status.Net based services) are the best of both worlds (ie Twitter+Plurk/Facebook) and unique features it's proprietary counterparts does not offer.

We have an ubuntuph group there where we share news, updates, ranging from ubuntuph group, linux community, and general topics.

If you don't micro-blog, you can still join and just listen to the conversation.  Who knows, one day there might be something that will prompt you to share your views and opinions ;)

To join, just click: ![ubuntuph](http://identi.ca/group/ubuntuph)  See you there!
