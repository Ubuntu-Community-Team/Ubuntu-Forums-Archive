### Post by echo2knight on 2008-07-02
Yes... compiz will work. My notebook has the same video chipset such as yours. Hardy has a working driver for such a chipset.

However, the optimal settings are still an issue. There's a bug in the driver. If the laptop you plan on buying is widescreen, the setting is not set to the optimal. You must do some thinkering. In my box, compiz is functioning but with issues. Like, running GL programs in fullscreen, then all of a sudden it returned to windowed mode; I still cant figure how to make the gears appear in the cube, as I have properly made the correct settings in compiz; having artifacts on the screen playing videos using the GL extension.

Since this is open-source, I know that one of these days, these issues will be resolved with finality.
