### Post by wersdaluv on 2011-02-24
GNOME 3.0, the next generation desktop, will be released on April 6, 2011 (learn more here [http://gnome3.org/](http://gnome3.org/) ). Let's celebrate its released along with [the rest of the world]("http://live.gnome.org/ThreePointZero/LaunchParty/")  at 8Layer's office on the day of release at 7pm onwards. 

For those who can't join us in the said venue, feel free to organize a release party in your area! Register your event [here]("http://live.gnome.org/ThreePointZero/LaunchParty/#Phillipines"). There are also parties in Cotabato and Zamboanga and you can find the details here [http://live.gnome.org/ThreePointZero/LaunchParty/#Phillipines](http://live.gnome.org/ThreePointZero/LaunchParty/#Phillipines) .
