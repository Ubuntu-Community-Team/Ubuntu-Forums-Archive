They were talking about a statement attributed to Jose Rizal, our national hero. The statement, roughly translated to English, "a person who cannot look back cannot move forward".

I will, however, contend that "looking back" does not necessarily mean "walking backwards" as "ang **hindi marunong tumingin** sa pinanggalingan(1)" in no way says, "ang **hindi nakatingin** sa pinanggalingan(2)."

In English:
(1) a person who **does not know how to look back**
(2) a person who **is not looking back**

So, looking back and appreciating where we come from is all well and good. But in order to move forward, we have to look forward most of the time.
