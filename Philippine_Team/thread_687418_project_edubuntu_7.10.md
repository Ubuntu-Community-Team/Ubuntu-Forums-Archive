If you really want to see a list of supported printers, use the Ubuntu Live CD.  Go through the motion of installing a printer.  Somewhere along that process, you'll be asked to choose among the list of supported printer.  You can look for your printer there.  Or...you can visit [www.linuxprinting.org](www.linuxprinting.org).

AS for installing the printer, as far as my experience, it's really plug-n-play.  Plug the printer to the USB and a message will tell you that your printer is ready to print (if it is indeed supported).
