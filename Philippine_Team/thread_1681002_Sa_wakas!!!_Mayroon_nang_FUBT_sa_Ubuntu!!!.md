### Post by jaceleon on 2011-02-03
After figuring out how does this free/unlimited browsing technique the people of symbianize works, I pickled my head and naisip ko na there must be a way to put this on Ubuntu.

Then I installed it on Ubuntu. Yung mga dependencies ng tor naman e inalam ko (since sasabihin ng synaptic na may broken packages) then downloaded those using Keryx. Next, I installed din yung mga dependencies and rcconf (to stop the auto-start of the tor and polipo services).

Lastly, I connected my Globe Tattoo to Ubuntu Maverick and made Vidalia run. And there it was! It WORKS!!! Pero unlike sa Windows counterpart nito, I have to manually set all my browsers, apt, synaptic, and IRC software to use tor and polipo. And there I am, updating Ubuntu using apt-get under tor and voila! It also does!!!

NOTE: hindi ko po to sinabi to brag and to be banned. I am just saying that it is possible. And to help some Ubuntu users out there;).
