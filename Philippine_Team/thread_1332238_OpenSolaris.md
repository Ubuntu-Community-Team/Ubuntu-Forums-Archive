### Post by puppywhacker on 2009-11-20
(Open) Solaris. (This also applies to NetBSD) has as main difference a different kernel, which means that the hardware driver is different. It is more posix compliant the linux is, but other than that it's just another unix. I like it's JavaDesktop and most applications for linux will also run on (Open) Solaris. For work I work a lot with Solaris and it's not bad. Although privately I prefer Ubunutu over anything.
