:confused: bakit ano nangyari?!.. how did you install the XP?.. this is how i install an OS on VBOx.. 
open VBox> click [COLOR="Blue"]New[/COLOR]> choose the OS platform from the dropdown menu> you need to type in your OS name> create Virtual HDD> after creating Virtual HDD on the main screen you can now see the name of your OS, on the right hand side click on [COLOR="Red"]CD/DVD[/COLOR]> click on the box that will mount your CD/DVD drive to your virtual machine> if you have an .iso image of OS, just click on the box at the bottom and guide it to where the OS image is located> click OK> you should be back on the main Virtula Box screen> then click on the [COLOR="Lime"]Start button arrow[/COLOR] right on top> it will open up another window that will be your screen for your new OS> just proceed with the installation process on that same screen!.
