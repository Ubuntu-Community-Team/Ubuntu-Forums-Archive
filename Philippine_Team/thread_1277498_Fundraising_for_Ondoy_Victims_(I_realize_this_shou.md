### Post by reneorense on 2009-10-09
Hello, once again everyone.

It's been quite a long time since I last posted something here.

Yes. Such event did affect everyone seriously, and we now are in our way to recover. We were alright, but friends of mine, students of ours and even relatives had been affected badly down in Cainta. We are just ever so thankful to God that none of them were in a severe state.

Days after Ondoy, a friend of mine from Pasig, they had to dig their house back from an awful-smelling mud of a hip-high in order for them to recover at least some of their items. Still, we thankful that all of them are fine and in one fine piece.

Nakakalungkot ring isipina malaki rin ang kinalaman nati sa nangyaring pagbaha, pero alam nating naging abre-lata rin ang nangyari. Sobrang galing ng naging tulungan ng lahat ng tao sa pangyayari. Nakakatuwa talagang isipin na sa kabila ng lahat ng mga nangyari sa ating lahat sa nagdaang dalawang bagyo at malalakas na pag-ulan ay naging mas bukas na ang isipan nating lahat na hindi dapat ipagsawalang bahala ang panaghoy ng kalikasan, at higit sa lahat walang problemang masyadong mahirap kung lahat ay may nagagawang paraan para tumulong.

At dito po sa atin sa LoCo ng Ubuntu Forum, ang ganda po ng nagiging dating at impluwensya nito para humikayat po ng tulong mula sa mga mabubuting puso.

"Mabuhay po ang mga Ubuntureros!"

God Bless us all...
