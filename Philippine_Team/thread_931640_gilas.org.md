### Post by jsgotangco on 2008-09-28
What Gilas does is basically connect the public high schools to the Internet, for the teachers and students to use for their studies and research. It was not meant to be used in conjunction with curriculum, but they have been steadily building a library of local references for the academe to use. The AF have a different program on the grade school level called Text-to-Teach where they deliver materials on math, science, and english on very far flung areas not covered by the wired service, so they deliver this via 3G.
