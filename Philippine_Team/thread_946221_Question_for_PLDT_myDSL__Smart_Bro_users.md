### Post by dodimar on 2008-10-14
> **wersdaluv said:**
> Did you manage to use it with more than one computer? If so, how?

Yep, bought a linksys wrt54gc wireless router with 4 port switch.

Connected all computers first, then I copied the MAC address of the PC that I used to activate my Smartbro account. Then I set the router's internet connection to DHCP, then plugged in the smartbro in the router's WAN port, then yun, may internet na lahat computer sa house.

Tama si nhatz, style lang nila yun. They want you to buy the more expensive plan or get another subscription. Even sa PLDT (cause they're just the same company actually). They can't limit the subscriber from one connection per computer. Kahit basahin mo pa yung terms and conditions nila, walang restrictions for those. Ayaw lang nilang bigyan ng technical support yung mga naka-router, so you would opt to get their higher plans or get additional subscription.

If you want to share your internet subscription, bur a router. di siya mahirap i-setup. Mura lang ng mga wireless router ngayun.

With regards sa lock-in, may lock-in period din po ang Globe Visibility. Unless your planning to have mobile broadband, maganda ang Visibility, pero kung home use, pwede na DSL or smartbro.
