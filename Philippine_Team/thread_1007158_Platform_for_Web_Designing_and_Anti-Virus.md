### Post by Samhain13 on 2008-12-10
Err... your OS is your platform. Those web design applications mentioned are just your tools. Do not mistake "web design" for just being able to operate one or two applications. Hehehe! There are good designers out there who use the text editors that are available in their platform (i.e., Notepad and Gedit).

Anyway, for cross-browser testing, you can install IEs4Linux. It offers you versions 5 to 7 (AFAIK). The other popular browsers like Firefox and Opera are available for your Ubuntu box also. Konqueror, which is the default browser in KDE, can be used as a proxy for Safari.
