### Post by gbutalid on 2007-12-05
Outkast08 natry ko na yong live CD pero April pa ang version at hindi na yata yon iuupdate. Kaya I tried the emulator. Medyo kulang ang activities sa emulator kasi may problema sa dependencies pero in due time maaayos din to.

I got it from this link.
[http://www.tuttlesvc.org/2007/11/running-sugar-on-ubuntu-gutsy.html]("http://www.tuttlesvc.org/2007/11/running-sugar-on-ubuntu-gutsy.html")

Simple lang, In essence, add the software repositories through Synaptic, then install sugar and sugar activities. The howto from the link is pretty easy. 
[B]
sugar-emulator[/B] sa terminal to start, **Alt+q** to quit. Don't forget Alt+q to quit. Di ko alam magquit kaya nagbrowse pa ako using the web browser in XO para malaman paano mag-quit.

Sabi dito sa link
[http://janimo.blogspot.com/2007/11/try-sugar-xo-laptops-interface.html]("http://janimo.blogspot.com/2007/11/try-sugar-xo-laptops-interface.html")
madali din mag-add ng Activities/Apps. I'll try this later.

If you start it, you'll immediately open the Journal, point mo lang mouse mo sa baba para makita mo ang other apps. Press the stop button sa upper-right hand corner ng Activity to quit it. Kung di ka pa ganun ka-expose sa Sugar Interface - much better kung manood ka muna sa Youtube, nakakapanibago kasi. Pero kung exposed ka na then you're ready to go.
