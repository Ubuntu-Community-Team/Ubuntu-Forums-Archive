### Post by daxumaming on 2009-06-26
If yours is the newer ZTE model (ZTE MF627), you'll need to install  usb-modeswitch - 0.9.7-1~ppa1  and zte-mf627-switch - 0.1-1  from Liam Green-Hughes' PPA[1] to get it working. This freaking device wasted me 2 hours of my time trying to figure out what I need to configure. 

There's another method[3] though (the topmost note regarding Jaunty), but it didn't work for me, but you could also try that out for yourself.
