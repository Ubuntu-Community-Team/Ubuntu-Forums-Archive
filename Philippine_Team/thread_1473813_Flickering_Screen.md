There is another thread on this topic...Please do your research first. Besides, it's hard to read and understand your post, at least for me...

Try this:
**  Screen Keeps Flickering **

 If you have an Intel Corporation Mobile 915GM/GMS/910GML card, your  screen may flicker every 5-10 seconds. To prevent this: 
 
[LIST]
[*] System -> Administration -> Advanced -> Service  Manager
[*] Uncheck "Detect RANDR (monitor) changes"
[/LIST]
Source: [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Lucid](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Lucid)
