### Post by gabz8472 on 2009-09-02
I just happened to try plugging in a smartbro usb prepaid kit into my MSI Wind U100 netbook and lo and behold, Ubuntu 9.04 (Jaunty Jackalope nbprompted me to set up a mobile internet connection. I selected Smart from the pop-up dialog and it worked! No tweaks or cammand line stuff!

Tried the same thing on an IBM Thinkpad R40, also running Jaunty - same results!

The pop-up dialog occured only the first time I plugged in, in subsequent attempts, it did not appear again.

Now I can remove Windoze from my MSI and just keep ubuntu.
My smartbro is the Huawei E156C unit. Download speed reaches up to 100+ kbps.
