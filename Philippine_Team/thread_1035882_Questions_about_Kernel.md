A.3 Why upgrade?

    There are several reasons to upgrade your kernel. Most of the reasons are that you perhaps need a new device driver for your new or old hardware (the previous device driver could have had a bug somewhere) or you need to upgrade due to a bug in the kernel (security one or a non-security one). Usually new kernels run faster than the old ones and are more stable and reliable. ([http://www.faqs.org/docs/lnag/lnag_kernel_upgrade.html](http://www.faqs.org/docs/lnag/lnag_kernel_upgrade.html))
