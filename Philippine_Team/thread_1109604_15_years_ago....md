### Post by jsgotangco on 2009-03-28
15 years ago, March 29, 1994, a 64kbps direct connection to the Internet from the US was established in the Philippines by a consortium named PHNET, signaling the availability of the Internet in the Philippines. Before that, the Internet was made available by way of UUCP. A year after that, commercial providers came to be.
