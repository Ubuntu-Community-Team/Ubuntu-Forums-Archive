### Post by daxumaming on 2009-07-11
> **jsgotangco said:**
> yeah, we used to have a pretty good site at ubuntu-ph.org but ever since Dax requested to reinstate it from C, things have gone south.

Right, a memo was sent out previously[1], but we failed to act on it - due in part to my sabbatical from the community so I can focus on learning PHP and Java, thus my absence from the list and irc. When Mekong (the name of our host on ServerPronto) was retired, our site went down with it. Right after I got back, I was, ***gulp***, more than a year too late. I asked around and seemed that no one bothered to send a request... and so I did. You can check out the progress on Ubuntu RT[2] using **ubuntu** as both the username and password.

> **jsgotangco said:**
> I'm not sure what's keeping C from activating it, or it could be in our end where there is no site ready yet.

No update from Canonical, not even a list of requirements was sent back to us. I can only suspect either a lack of resources, or they just can't fit our request into their already busy schedule.

> **dodimar said:**
> I think sir dax already working with it... don't know.. hehe 

I am.

I've already asked kiminaiseah[3] for an account. He was kind enough to provide VPS hosting. However, his hardware's undergoing upgrades as discussed in the irc channel[4] but hopefully be back up by next week. 

I've also asked[5] EU-Admins to point our domain to our new host, but since our site's still down, they wouldn't be able to verify our LoCo site and thus, wouldn't be able to point our domain - at least not until it's up.

I also got our old dump file working with Drupal 6.x. So our 2005-2007 data is still there together with our old user accounts. 

Planet would've to be started from scratch though. I'll be asking interested parties to submit their RSS feed and hackergotchi once we got it working.

And last, but not least, I've uploaded our drupal theme[6] to our LoCo account. There's still a few rough edges, so any help from the community is greatly appreciated.
