### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2011-10-18
Hi people! I just want to share with you, last October 15 we held a [release party here in Santa Rosa City (Laguna)]("http://loco.ubuntu.com/events/loco-philippine-team/1349/detail/"). It was great! Eight people showed up at Starbucks in Paseo de Santa Rosa, perfect place for a small group meeting!

We had short discussion about Ubuntu, its benefits and a little introduction to the community. (You can download the slideshow [here]("http://ubuntuone.com/5T1MsElfLkYCKYoLBRHenQ")). We also did a raffle where four lucky winners got their first Toyota (pillows and umbrella) before they even get to see Ubuntu in action. :-) Each participant had a free CD (or maybe two?)

Overall, city folks thought the release party was a success and we look forward to meeting more often.

If you like to see the pictures, I posted them on [Google Plus]("https://plus.google.com/photos/104168843479135841309/albums/5663877292408463473").

By the way, instead of creating a new thread, why not start organizing the next party here?
