### Post by jsgotangco on 2009-02-18
Hi Guys,

Not directly attributed to Ubuntu, but we've discussed it nonetheless. Me and a group of friends decided to do a podcast dedicated to local technology news and events. We basically call it the "Pinoy Tech Podcast" but we're open for better names. We've set up a blog at [http://pinoytechpodcast.blogspot.com/](http://pinoytechpodcast.blogspot.com/) where you can download the MP3 or M4A copy of the first episode, or you can also stream it online on the main page. We're going to put RSS and iTunes support by the next episode, scheduled to be recorded Feb. 27 and released the next day.

Our plan is to have this biweekly, or every 2 weeks. If we get traction, we'll change the scheds as needed.

Also, your feedback would be very valuable in improving the show. Please feel free to give suggestions for topics in the future.

Again, the site is at [http://pinoytechpodcast.blogspot.com/](http://pinoytechpodcast.blogspot.com/)

I apologize in advance for the quality of the audio. My mic got busted in the last minute so I had to use the built in mic of my computer, and of course, since its our first episode, its quite raw, but I think this is a good thing.

Thanks!
