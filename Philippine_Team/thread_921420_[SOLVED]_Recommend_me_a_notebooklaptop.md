### Post by killer_d76 on 2008-09-16
with regard to HDD or SDD.. based on my own opinion, i would still go for HDD.. browsing through all of those netbook forum, the problem with SSD is its lifespan, ilang reformat lang "toinks!", sorry ka day, downside with HDD is the speed, battery consumption and durabiltiy, kasi unlike SSD if you accidentally drop HDD "poof!" goodbye files!, pero madaling hanapan ng replacement na hdd!.

now withe regard to brand name.. eeepc 1000 or MSI Wind ang medyo naghahabulan.. but if you go for the price and spec, my opinion.. i would go for MSI Wind, pero kung slickeness ng design or look ng netbook,and support ;), EEE pc is for you!
