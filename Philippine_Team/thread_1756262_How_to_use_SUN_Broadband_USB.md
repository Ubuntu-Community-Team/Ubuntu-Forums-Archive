### Post by tech-hero on 2011-05-25
What modem are you using? I've tried two different modems from SUN, one worked directly, the other one still need some tweaking. You have first to verify if your modem is detected as a USB modem device. Go to Network manager, under the mobile connections tab, you should be able to see an entry from your mobile modem. Just edit the AP and set it to "fbband". To verify if your modem is working, right after you plug in your modem, click the network-manager applet from the panel. You should be able to see "Sun Broadband (Digitel)" from Mobile connections group.
