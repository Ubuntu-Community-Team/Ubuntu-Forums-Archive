### Post by loell on 2008-07-30
from [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=606311&highlight=WL-8313]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=606311&highlight=WL-8313")

you need your windows wireless driver, or download [ftp://ftp1.planet.com.tw/Wireless_Lan/WL-8313/DR-WL8313v230.zip]("ftp://ftp1.planet.com.tw/Wireless_Lan/WL-8313/DR-WL8313v230.zip") extract it, it should have **mrv8k51.inf**.


install [ndisgtk]("apt:ndisgtk") , and in **Systems** menu -->**Administration**-->**Windows Wireless Drivers ** and browse  mrv8k51.inf to wherever you extract it,


then reboot..
