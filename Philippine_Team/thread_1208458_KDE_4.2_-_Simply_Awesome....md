### Post by dannybuntu on 2009-07-10
> **pinoyskull said:**
> hala... flamewar na

Hindi naman, I think we Pinoy users of Ubuntu are all sensible, mature, and professional people. Gnome is actually a good choice, I've been using it for a year na alongside with IceWm. 

Ako kasi, I treat OSs, DEs, Apps and WMs as toolsets used for something bigger. 

For me, saying that "hey guys, look at this new version of XXX, I think it's simply awesome, what do you think about it? What are you using now" **is just like saying** "hey guys look at this new *[insert toolset **eg. hammer**]* I think it works great - it could help me do this particular set of activities which would be of service to all of us. Do you have a favorite hammer brand? Tell us about it."

So no harm done. I think the DE flamewars is just a beneficially fabricated means to promote interest, discussion and consequently adaption - **it makes Windows people curious.** - to the point na si WindowsXP user asks - "Hey Linux people, what are you fighting about? Huh? Desktop Environments? What's that? In Windows we just use themes."

Discussions will ensue, yada, yada, then Windows User gets curious and then decides to install Ubuntu. Then he decides to install another DE, then opts for performance thereby switching to a Window Manager - Finally he realizes **Zen** and does away with the whole GUI. After a year or so - he buys a faster PC and installs the DE with the freshest bling and the cycle goes on again.
