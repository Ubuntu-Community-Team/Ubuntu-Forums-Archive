### Post by pinoyskull on 2010-12-13
If you're always online, I suggest installing Dropbox, it's a cool app that will backup your important docs automatically, I've been using this for a year now.

Create an account and install the app, it can be downloaded from their [website]("https://www.dropbox.com/referrals/NTExMTkzNDg5?src=global0")

To automatically backup all your files from your Documents folder to Dropbox, do this (assuming you your Dropbox folder is ~/Dropbox)
> mv ~/Documents ~/Dropbox/Documents
ln -s ~/Dropbox/Documents ~/Documents
