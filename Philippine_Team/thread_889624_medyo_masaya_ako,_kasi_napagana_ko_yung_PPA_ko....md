### Post by pendletone on 2008-08-17
Oo naman wersdaluv. Actually, I don't know of a better alternative to Audacity. Yan din kasi preferred audio editor ko eh. I use it for resampling and trimming down audio samples before exporting it to other audio applications.

I have to clarify that Audacity isn't really a DAW, but rather a digital audio editor. So although you can do multi-track recording with it, I still think it's more suited for editing audio files.

If you're looking for a 'Pro-Tools' kind of DAW to do your multi-track recording & mixing, you may want to check out [ardour]("http://ardour.org/"). IMO it's the best DAW for linux! :)
