### Post by pendletone on 2008-07-07
Pwede mong i-upgrade ang graphics mo bro. Merong 1 AGP & 3 PCI slots ang motherboard mo. Pwede mong i-override ang built-in video card mo by connecting an external one.

Although newer motherboards offer PCI-E slots which is far faster than PCI, pwede mo pa rin gamitin yung PCI slot mo and still expect a *slightly* faster performance compared sa S3 Graphics ProSavage8 -- which is your integrated video card.
