### Post by pinoyskull on 2010-08-24
**System Administrator Level III**

**QUALIFICATIONS:**

1.) Education:
A graduate of a college level course is required. A degree in computer  science or engineering or equivalent is preferred. Actual systems and  network administration experience may be substituted on a year-for-year  basis for the required education. Professional certifications plus  extensive experience may also be substituted for the required education.

2.) Experience:
Four years experience in designing, evaluating, configuring, and  implementing server and network systems is needed for the position.
• The four years experience must also include extensive knowledge in the following areas :[INDENT]&#9702;  Experienced in implementing solutions using Open Source Technology  particularly the flexibility and stability of Linux Operating System  (Debian, Ubuntu, CentOS and Red Hat)
&#9702; Open BSD's Pf for Firewall redundancy with CARP and pfsync
&#9702; Setup and implementation of HA Proxy, Apache and other modules likes Reverse Proxy[/INDENT]• Working knowledge and concept in the following subjects:[INDENT]&#9702; Repository such as SVN and Git
&#9702; Asterisk
&#9702; Cloud computing[/INDENT]• Programming and scripting experience is an advantage but not required

3.) Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
Strong foundation in networking concepts and data communication is  required. Extensive knowledge of the current operating systems used by  the company is required. The position also requires thorough  understanding of computer hardware components being used. The successful  candidate should have strong troubleshooting skills in both the  hardware and software networking issues. Good communication and  interpersonal skills is also needed to be able to provide satisfactory  support to end-users.
