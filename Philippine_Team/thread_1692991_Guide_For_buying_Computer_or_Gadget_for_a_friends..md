### Post by jao_madn on 2011-02-22
As a person working related to IT or in simple term Computer related works. Im always or constantly ask how to or what to buy a gadget specially a personal computer or laptop specifically by my friends or someone i dont know but reffered by one of my friends.

I just want to share my experience or the answer i always told to someone asking me.

I personally create this 3 Guides so that if anyone bother asking me again im ready always and not consuming my time explaining over and over again specially napo sa mga nag aask na medyo hindi ba kabisado like MGA FRIENDS KO NA BABAE or wala pa masyado alam..

1. I told them " First You must know how to use the gadget or the computer you want to buy or kng papanu nyo gagamitin ang gustong bilhin na computer" Based on my experience kasi marami nagtatanung na mga friends ko or mostly ang nagtatanung naman talaga is yung medyo wala pa alam..Thats the reason kng bakit sila nagtatanung..To explain it: tinatanung ko sila kng panu nila gagamitin its either pag surfing, internet, casual games not hard core, email, Instant messaging, voice chat, then OR hard core gaming, hard core video editing, in short hardcore na paggamit ng computer or mga applications. Pagnasagot na nila then i move to second question?

2. " Whats the price na kaya nila or Magkano budget nila or Kng merun ba sila marami pera" actually lahat ng question is inter-related to each other..kailangan mu muna masagot ang una bago mu masagot ang next. To explain: kng surfing, chat, email, then masmababa ang budget OR kng hardcore gaming, application like photoshop or video editing then it will cost them more money.

3. " The design, looks, brand and style na gusto nila " Pagnasagot na nila ang una and pangalawa then pwedi na sila maghanap ng kng anu gusto nila ng brand, style and looks, na fit sa first and second question.

Thats my three guide pag may nagtatanung..at hinahayaan ko nalang sila mag decide kasi mostly or yung tatlong tanung ko sa kanila is inter-related naman sila kng baga nagloop back lang..Kaya hindi na sila paulit ulit na mag ask..hehehehe

Thanks for reading..
