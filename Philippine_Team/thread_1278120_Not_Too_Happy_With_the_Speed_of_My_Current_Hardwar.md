### Post by indigest on 2009-09-29
First of all, run "top" in a terminal to see how much of your CPU you are using.  It's possible that something unexpected is hogging your CPU.

If you think you might want to upgrade your hardware, check out your current CPU/GPU ratings at [http://www.cpubenchmark.net/](http://www.cpubenchmark.net/)  This will help you evaluate how much you stand to gain from upgrading.
