### Post by killer_d76 on 2008-05-29
i'd just want to share my happy experience with Ubuntu! and perhaps could also be a reason for me or to some as well why they need to install ubuntu on their computer :popcorn:

My wife was having trouble printing her documents on MS Word using our Epson printer (she was using Windows XP)and it endlessly sends an error report to who knows where.. when this happens, normally you do "ctrl+alt+delet" to open up task manager to end or stop the job on windows, but she found out that she was not able to end the task there at all!.. i look on the "processes" tab, and i saw three or more "E_FARNBZIP.exe"!!!.. i use the "search" program to look for it's location and took note of it.. i reboot to ubuntu and open up C: and go to the exact location of that file and delete E_FARNBZIP.exe and then boot back to Windows, we tried printing her documents and without a hic-up it works again!.. thanks Ubuntu! ;)
