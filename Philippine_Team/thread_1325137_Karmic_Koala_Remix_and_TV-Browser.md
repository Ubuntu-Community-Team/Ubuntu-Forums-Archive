### Post by chitx2000 on 2009-11-13
Recently I've been into installing Karmic Koala Netbook Remix on HP Mini 1137NR and everything looks good from skype call to playing videos and music then I was tempted to install [TV-Browser]("http://www.tvbrowser.org/en/home-mainmenu-1.html") . . . an open source application for digital tv guide. TV-Browser gets the daily TV program from the internet and shows it clearly aranged - like a printed TV guide.

But right after the package is installed karmic hanged up. Decided to reboot the netbook and just right after the splash screen it hanged up again and won't be able to reach the login screen. I tried running recovery but no luck at all. I didn't bother to do further troubleshooting and check some logs. Decided to rebuild the netbook from scratch. Now am back on my feet and won't attempt to re install TV-Browser again. 

At the moment I still don't know the real issue of the hang up. . . Just be careful when installing the TV-Browser package on Karmic Koala Netbook Remix.
