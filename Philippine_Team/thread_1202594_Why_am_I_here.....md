Its features are useful for testing your frontends; you will frequently need use the "disable" features like: disable images, disable page colours, disable Javascript. Also, the "validate local HTML" and "validate local CSS" features because you will make a lot of mistakes (as everyone does), and it pays that someone/thing points them out to you so that you can correct them.

Undisclosed amount doesn't necessarily mean a million, but if he only got himself a million pesos, then that's not much. He's already getting a lot of revenue from SEO for starters. So probably more than that.

I'll give you a good story here, probably bigger than Sulit.com.ph in terms of valuation and relevance in open source. 

Around the early part of 2001, a small company named Gluecode was born and created a software stack called Gluecode Joe.
