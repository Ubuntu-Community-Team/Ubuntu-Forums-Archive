### Post by echo2knight on 2008-06-26

Filipinos are generally bilingual. Tagalog/Filipino is our common local language, while English is the medium of instruction, whether it be for business or for education.

Moreover, the Philippines have so many local dialects depending of the region. Meaning, there is a local dialect for one province, as there is another dialect for the other province.
