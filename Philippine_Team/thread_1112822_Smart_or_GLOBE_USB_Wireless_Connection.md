### Post by aljoriz on 2010-02-28
Share lang ako how to use / set up the usb dongles

   a.  Plug the smartbro 
   b.  click on the smartbro cd icon from the desktop, do a right click and press eject
   c.  go to Menu>Preferences>Network Connection>Mobile Broadband>click ADD
   d.  You should your dongle (zte of huawei) press next and next UNTIL you are asked to select 
       your provider CHOOSE SMART then press add
   e. editing smart window would appear change APN to smartbro
   f.  Go to PPP settings configure method unlock all except for PPP then press APPLY then close
   g. Go to your connection manager click and you should see SMARTBRO

to disconnect just click on the connection manager and DISCONNECT. 

The trick also applies to globe tatto just do the following:
     when asked for the internet provider select globe telecom and replace the APN to http.globe.com.ph
