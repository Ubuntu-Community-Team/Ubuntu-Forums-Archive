### Post by Samhain13 on 2010-01-21
1. Create a Launchpad account:
[https://launchpad.net](https://launchpad.net)

2. Pick a "project", like "Ubuntu":
[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu)

3. There should be a "Translations" link at the top. Clicking that will take you to a page where you can pick the language that you wish to translate to.

Recommended: join the Pinoy community in Launchpad, here
[https://launchpad.net/~loco-philippine-team](https://launchpad.net/~loco-philippine-team)
