### Post by loell on 2010-01-29
As part of our ongoing effort to promote the use of  Ubuntu, we will be requesting CDs through shipit under the  privilege of our LoCo Team. 

Should you need CDs, we will be more than happy to request it for you. 

Just provide us with your contact details ([COLOR="Blue"]name, mailing address, zip code, and contact no.[/COLOR] ) by default, it's 1 CD per request but if you'd like to request two or more CDs, please state your purpose for those CDs, then PM it to one of the eligible loco team members that could process the request.  

The Current Loco Team Members that can process CD requests are as follows:

1.[COLOR="Blue"][Zakame]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=48313")[/COLOR] (Zach B. Elep)

2. [COLOR="blue"][daxumaming]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=31288")[/COLOR] (Dax Solomon Umaming)
