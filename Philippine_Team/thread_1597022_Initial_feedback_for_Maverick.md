### Post by oobermensch on 2010-10-26
Overall, I love it. I find it a little more responsive when compared to 10.04. A couple of quirks though.

[LIST=1]
[*]The brightness adjust applet does not work. When I unplug my laptop from the charger, it doesn't automatically dim the screen. It was working when I was still using 10.04. Also, when I adjust the brightness using the hardware keys, it does change, but I don't get the notification/status at the upper right-hand corner of the screen (like when you adjust the volume using your computer's hardware keys). I'm guessing it's a bug.
[*]When I wake my laptop from sleep, automatic reconnection to my wireless network takes a couple more seconds. In Lucid Lynx, it automatically starts to reconnect after 2 or 3 seconds when logging back in. With Maverick, it takes like 5-7 seconds before it begins to reconnect. It's a little annoying. :mad:
[*]Lastly, my bluetooth indicator applet disappears when logging back in from sleep. Yeah, seems like they have some stuff to sort out in the **"waking from sleep"** department. Haha.
[/LIST]

But overall, I'm still happy with it. Haven't booted into Windows for almost a month now. Actually, now I think Windows is so archaic! :D Loving each day I spend using Ubuntu.
