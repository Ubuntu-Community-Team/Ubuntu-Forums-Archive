Yes, but you have to configure from the client side.

In Gnome
System > Preferences > Remote Desktop

*Allow other users to view your Desktop
*Allow other users to control your Desktop


Uncheck:

*You must confirm each access to this machine

Check

*Configure network automatically to accept each connection

**WARNING: I enable ko po ito - but with notifications, at agad agad mayroon pong client na nagtry magconnect. Tinignan ko IP address ko at ng mga client ko at walang nag match sa IP address. Deny ko. IP address is 56.x.x.x@pldt so alam kong taga pinas din. Ingat ka sa tool na ito.
