Greetings fellow Ubuntu lovers!

There will be a **Free Culture Day** event at the **Shangri-La Plaza Mall area** on **October 1, 2008, Wednesday, from 12 n.n. - 7 p.m**. Ubuntu Philippines have been invited to have  a boot to demo and present Ubuntu, and maybe even give a short talk about Ubuntu.

***EDIT: The date has been moved from October 4 to October 1***

Other activities for the day include:

[LIST]
[*]Live Mural drawing, Trading Post & Software Demos
[*]Introduction of Ubuntu Linux (c/o Ubuntu-PH.org spokesperson)
[*]Speech by Teodoro Casino, Bayan Muna Representative - RA #5769 aka "Free and Open Source Bill", September 2006, House Bill #1716, August 2007
[*]A short story by Fr. Michael Condes, O. Carm., "LIBRE"
