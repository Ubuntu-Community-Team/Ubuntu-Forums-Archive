### Post by eilu on 2007-12-26
all packages in a lot of CD's/DVD's is not a very good idea. For one thing, packages on the online repos get updated- pero yung nasa CD/DVD ganun pa rin. Plus napaka-labor & resource intensive... someone has to dl all those packages and burn them to CD's/DVD's one by one.

ang ginagawa ko noong naka-dial up pa ako is I'll download the deb's I want and their dependencies from [www.getdeb.net](www.getdeb.net) (or other online debian repos) from an internet cafe then burn to CD, then install sa bahay.

or use APTonCD... get the packages from someone else who already has them.

hindi na talaga maiwasan na kailangan ng internet. heh...
