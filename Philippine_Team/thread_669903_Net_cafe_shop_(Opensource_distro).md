### Post by ragadanga63 on 2008-01-17
I'd still go for Ubuntu.  My cafe is now running partially with it (had to keep one xp pc since my printer is not Linux ready).  The advantage of Ubuntu is the abundance of software in its repositories and .deb packages.  With other distros you may still have to compile several applications like gyachi (kailangan dahil YM-crazy mga kababayan natin) and Xara LX.

If you offer gaming, I suggest you install wine-doors, a program which automates the installation of several non-native apps like Office 2003(why would anyone install this?), DOTA, WOW, Call of Duty 2, etc. etc.

So far okay naman ang Ubuntu.  I don't offer gaming (too many kids spending too much time playing instead of reading) so you may have a totally different situation.

### Post by ragadanga63 on 2008-01-26
Ayos yang plano mo Ichi.  Same lang pala biz natin.  Ito lang tips ko sa iyo:

1.  Location is everything.  

2.  Yahoo Messenger is the  "killer app".  Nahirapan akong ibenta ang Gyachi dahil karamihan sa mga clients don't care to learn new UI's.  At since marami sa mga customers ko ay mga kapamilya ng OFW's, you'll need voice and video chat.  They'll need all the assistance they can get with Gyachi.  Somehow, may ilan din akong suki na di na bumalik since I converted to Ubuntu.  Kaya kailangan mong ingatan ang mga clients mo especially those YM addicts.

3.   You mentioned Xerox.  Gawin mong 2.  Ang isa ay yong powder, preferably laser copier/printer at A3 capable, with reducer and enlarger.  It's expensive pero madaling mabawi ang capital mo dito( I started with one now I have 4).  Ang pangalawa ay yong refurbished liquid copier (pwede ding powder).  Karamihan kasi sa mga customers mo maghanap ng murang photocopying.  With refurbs using refill toners, you can lower your price to as low as .50 centavos per copy.  Dudumugin ka ng mga estudyante nyan lalo na kung clear ang copy mo kahit mura lang.  Hanap ka ng supplier na may magandang aftersales service.  Refurbished copiers break down more often than new ones.

More next time.  Nagugutum na ako...hehehe.

Good luck sa negosyo mo.
