### Post by antoniomiguel on 2011-11-07
..and it could also be a case of interference.

Check if there are other wifi signal in your premise which uses the same spot freq (being channel 1, 6, and 11 the most used).

I used to use ch 1. But when my neighbor fired up their new AP they unknowingly overlapped mine, so i had to change to ch 11. Reception was better after this.
