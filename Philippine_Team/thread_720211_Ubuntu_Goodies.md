### Post by ragadanga63 on 2008-03-11
You can make your own Ubuntu Tshirt by using iron-on stickers. Once you have the design, flip it horizontally so that it will become a mirror image and print it using an inkjet printer into the sticker.   Ironing it on the shirt  is a bit tricky though (you might burn your tshirt) and having it done on a printshop is pricey (250.00).  An iron on sticker costs 60.00 and is quite hard to find (bought mine from Compuspeed, Inc. sa Quezon Ave.).
