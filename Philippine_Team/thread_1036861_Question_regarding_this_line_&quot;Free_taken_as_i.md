### Post by pendletone on 2009-01-11
hi shengton!

the line "free as in free speech, not as in free beer" relates to the definition of the term *free software* as explained by the Free Software Foundation.

to quote FSF's definition:

> "Free software" is a matter of liberty, not price. It is a matter of the users' freedom to run, copy, distribute, study, change and improve the software.

So to understand that concept, one should think of the word "free" as in free speech (freedom/liberty), and not as in free beer, or free ipod :), etc... (zero price)
