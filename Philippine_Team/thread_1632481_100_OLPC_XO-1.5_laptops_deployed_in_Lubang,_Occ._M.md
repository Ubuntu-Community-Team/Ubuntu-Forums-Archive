### Post by jsgotangco on 2010-11-28
I know this is not directly related to Ubuntu at all, but I just want to share to everyone the first pilot of deploying 100 OLPC XO-1.5 laptops to Grade 4 students in Lubang, Occidental Mindoro by Education Kindling (eKindling) and with the help of the local government of Lubang and the donors. This is just the pilot program but we expect to continue deploying education materials and laptops to kids in Lubang in the following months.

Each laptop is a full Linux system installed with the Sugar Learning Environment and Gnome.

You can check out the pictures at [http://picasaweb.google.com/jgotangco/2010EKindlingOLPCLubangDeployment?feat=directlink](http://picasaweb.google.com/jgotangco/2010EKindlingOLPCLubangDeployment?feat=directlink)
