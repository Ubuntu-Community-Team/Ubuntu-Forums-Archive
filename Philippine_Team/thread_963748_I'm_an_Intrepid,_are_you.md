### Post by eric_son on 2008-11-06
I just finished upgrading from Hardy to Intrepid.
Oh man, it took more than 11 hours!  Grabe!

First, I tried to upgrade using the updater service...after updating the repos, it told me that it will be downloading 1.2gb of data and would take around 10+ hours.  Good grief.  Instead, I resorted to downloading the alternative CD via Torrent.  That took only 1 hour and 30mins on my connection (bayantel).  

I mounted the alternative CD and started the upgrade process.  It then offered to download updated packages..around 300MB worth, and would only take 1.5 hours daw on my connection. I clicked on yes.  That's when I was shocked to see the download running at only 8kbps~15kbps.  Grabe!  Just to make sure my connection was not at fault, I downloaded a 100MB file elsewhere... okay naman.  Mabagal lang talaga yung connection to the repositories.

I didn't want to abort so I let it continue na lang and left for work, hoping that when I get back from office, I'd be greeted with the LOGIN window of Intrepid.

When I got back from office, I checked the progress.  Guess what... the download was just finishing up.  

Anyway, despite that problem, the upgrade went smoothly.  Very happy.

Kaya lang, I think I would have saved more time by nuking my system and re-installing from scratch. :)   (That's actually what I did when moving from Gutsy to Hardy.)
