### Post by ahurt on 2008-11-24
For the love of god, don't get the HP Mininote to use for Ubuntu. My employer purchased those for a group of us at work and they suck so hard! VIA CM-7 CPU is abysmal and good luck getting the microphone to work for things like Skype. After much research and tweaking, the machines still wouldn't reliably sleep/resume 100% of the time.

   They were so bad that we offered to trickle them down to some of the Windows users in our organization in exchange for something else. They were simply unusable as a primary linux laptop.

   Fortunately, our director let us spec our own machines the second time around and we settled on the ASUS N10J-A2. Even though they are shipped with Windows only, they are a stellar netbook to run Ubuntu on. They are like the big brother of the fantastic, albeit watered down, Eee PC series.

    Hope this helps.
