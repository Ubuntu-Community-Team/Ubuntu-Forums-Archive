### Post by jsgotangco on 2010-04-08
Powerbooks run on the PowerPC platform (G3,G4, G5, etc.), therefore, you need drivers that were compiled specifically to that platform. In OS X, this support is called the Universal Binary (for Intel and PPC). The current OS X Snow Leopard is Intel only now.
