### Post by jsgotangco on 2008-04-25
Greetings,

In the ubuntu-ph mailing list, we have started preliminary planning for the Hardy Heron Release Party in Manila. The tentative schedule is May 2, 2008 starting at 6:00PM onwards at a still unidentified area in the Ortigas Centre.

The purpose of this get together is celebrate the release of Ubuntu 8.04 LTS at the same time share the spirit of Ubuntu with a burnfest as some of us will be bringing our laptops and Hardy ISOs. It's also a good chance for people to network so please do signify your intention to join by replying to this thread.

Just to make sure, don't expect too much as:

[LIST=1]
[*]We don't have pressed CDs for Hardy (yet). Instead, we can have a burn fest (I already have some ISOs)
[*]There's no sponsor here whatsoever, its all community so we will have to shoulder our own expenses (drinks, food, etc)
[/LIST]

There is also a plan to do it in Cebu as well. For those outside these areas, We encourage you to plan for a small but fun party as the main purpose is to congregate and get to know each other more.

Share the spirit!
