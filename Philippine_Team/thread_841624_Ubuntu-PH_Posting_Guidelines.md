### Post by Samhain13 on 2008-06-26
**I. Always Abide By The Forum Code of Conduct**

[http://ubuntuforums.org/index.php?page=policy](http://ubuntuforums.org/index.php?page=policy)

**II. Creating A New Thread**

a. *Use Tagalog/Filipino/Taglish* in both thread title and in your initial post if your topic is intended for discussion among _Filipino members only_.

b. *Use Bicolano/Cebuano/Ilocano/etc.* in both thread title and in your initial post if your topic is intended for discussion among Filipinos who speak the same language as used in the thread's title (i.e. for regional discussions, meet-ups, etc.).

c. *Use English* in both the thread title and in your initial post if your topic is intended for discussion among _all Ubuntu Forums members_, including non-Filipinos.

**III. Responding In A Thread**

_*Use only English* when responding to a discussion that has an English title_ (refer to item II). This is for avoiding confusion among non-Filipino members who find their way into the discussion through the forum's search feature, only to find that they cannot understand the language used in the discussion.
