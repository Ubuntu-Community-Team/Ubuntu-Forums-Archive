### Post by aljoriz on 2010-01-17
download the ISO of UNR 9.10 tapos download ka nag UNetbootin Remix.  You don't have to install UNR 9.04 but you have to download the iso and I suggest using 9.10 as most devices like Wi-fi for 'new' laptops are supported.

simple lang gamitin yan.  
Use UNetbootin, plug ur USB flash driver (1g is the minimum but the recommended is 2gig) sa DISKIMG point mo saan yung ISO file ng UNR9.10 tapos execute mo.  Reboot 

Go to your flash drive na nilagyan mo ng ISO ng UNR and look for WUBI.exe.. at mag iinstall yan run and your good to go

In case di mo gusto ang ubuntu netbook remix (UNR) uninstall it sa ADD/REMOVE just like any other windows applications
