### Post by jos-vh67 on 2016-07-17
It used to be in the "System settings" - "About" menu, but it disappeared somewhere in one the the OTO's.
When you have "Telegram" installed, you can check it under Telegram settings - Accounts.
Otherwise you have to phone a friend and let him/her send your number back to you and save it in the messages or notes ;-)
