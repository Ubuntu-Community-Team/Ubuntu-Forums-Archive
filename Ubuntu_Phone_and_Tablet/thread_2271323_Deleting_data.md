### Post by howefield on 2015-03-29
Then I don't believe you can, from the user manual...

> Deleting (Uninstalling) Scopes and Apps
You can&#8217;t delete the main Scopes and apps that were on your phone when you took it out of the box. All the others &#8211; the ones you have downloaded and installed yourself &#8211; can be deleted (or uninstalled) by visiting the Ubuntu Store and searching for your app or Scope. In the preview screen for the Scope or app you want to delete, you will see options to either update or uninstall it. Tap &#8216;Uninstall&#8217; and let Ubuntu do the rest. The quickest way to access the preview screen for an app is to give a long press on the icon in the Apps Scope.
