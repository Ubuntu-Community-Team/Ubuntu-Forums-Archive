### Post by dumazdamaz on 2015-08-03
another way is to send them over wifi using the wifitransfer app which essentially creates a ftp-server that you can connect to with the other phone. Advantages: over wifi is faster. You can mount the devices drive and have other editing rights (rename, delete). Disadvantages: devices have to be on same LAN.
