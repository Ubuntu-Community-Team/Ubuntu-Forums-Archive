### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-10-10
Canonical engineers confine their work to a) the Ubuntu phone OS; b) Cooperating with OEM partners. This is what Canonical engineers are presently working on.

[https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/devices]("https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/devices/")

There is nothing stopping anyone from experimenting with their own device. Read the disclaimer.

[https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/installing-ubuntu-for-devices/](https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/installing-ubuntu-for-devices/)

[https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/porting-new-device/](https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/porting-new-device/)

There used to be a list of community developed ports to a variety of devices but I do not know how successful those developers were or how accurate this page is.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices)

Regards.
