### Post by Sven6210 on 2016-11-20
The problem is caused by ebay. A work around is to search for any product link on ebay with a search engine and to enter ebay with such a product link. Then ebay allows you to surf the website.

To me it seems the official starting page from ebay is not allowing Ubuntu Touch to use the classical website fronted. It tries to open the mobile version and then identifies the browser as incompatible even though the browser is working perfectly fine.
