### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-07-30
Hopefully, that BQ & Meizu at least recovered their production costs and may be made a bit of profit. If that happened then they may be tempted to bring out new models of Ubuntu mobile devices. And other OEMs may be tempted also to get in on the act.

[http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2016/07/meizu-mx6-official-announcement-specs](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2016/07/meizu-mx6-official-announcement-specs)

A few other things are certain. (1) This is a user forum and we have no more idea of what is going on than you do. (2) Very few Canonical employees are in the "know" and those that do know what is going on are restricted by confidentiality agreements. (3) OEMs, like BQ & Meizu, reserve for themselves the right to make product announcements at a time and manner of their choosing. Canonical respects that right.

Meanwhile, Canonical is getting on with the job of developing the Ubuntu for Devices OS and updating existing Ubuntu phones & tablets. It is doing this as well as progressing towards Convergence and developing other areas of interest.
