### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-04-08
> If I wanted that ability, would I need to buy a newer cellphone?

What one do you have now? If the hardware has HDMI out then convergence will be available. Otherwise, the user will have to wait until an Over The Air update brings in the code to make a wireless connection to the monitor. As previously mentioned.

Keep an eye on the Ubuntu Online Summit 3rd to 5th May 2016. There are tracks about convergence. The details are yet to be put into the schedule but there will clearly be information about progress in the area of convergence as well as other areas.

[http://summit.ubuntu.com/uos-1605/](http://summit.ubuntu.com/uos-1605/)

[https://launchpad.net/aethercast](https://launchpad.net/aethercast)

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/DisplayCasting](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/DisplayCasting)

> WARNING: This currently only works on the upcoming Meizu MX Pro 5  aka turbo. There is work in-progress to get this work on other devices  as well.
