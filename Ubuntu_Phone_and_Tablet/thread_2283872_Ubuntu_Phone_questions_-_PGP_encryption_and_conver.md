### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-06-25
From what I have heard, It would be inaccurate to describe either the present BQ Ubuntu phones or the new Meizu Ubuntu phone as "converged" Ubuntu devices. I think that the hardware has to be capable of doing what we expect a Ubuntu converged device to be able to do. I have heard that later in the year BQ will be bringing out a Ubuntu converged device.

For a year or more people were coming on this forum and asking when will a Ubuntu phone be released and what will the specifications be? We, on this forum do not know. If Canonical engineers knew the answers they would not be allowed to give out the information. What products and when they are released is entirely the responsibility of the OEM. That has often been the reply given by Canonical employees. I say this just so that everybody knows the situation.
