### Post by mily2 on 2017-05-02
Hello,

We must not lose the hope. Until June we have official support (fixes and security patches), and then there will always be the great Community. All effort and work can not be lost.

If you don't want AOSP, CM, Resurrection Remix... it's complicated.

Sailfish it's a great OS (heir to the genius Maemo; I have not seen better multitasking that on a Nokia N900, with permission of UT Windowed mode). Make a port for E4.5 I think is difficult; all port that I know are for Qualcomm SOCs, although Oysters SF (Official Sailfish phone) is expected to come out with MTK MT6753, and there is a lot of source code released for MT6582.
