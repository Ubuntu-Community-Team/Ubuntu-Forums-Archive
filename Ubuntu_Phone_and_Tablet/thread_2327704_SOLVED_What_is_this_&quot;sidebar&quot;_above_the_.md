### Post by peter d on 2016-06-13
That's the Ubuntu Touch sideqstage. You open it with a three finger tap. You can then drag an open app into the side stage. You can hide the side stage by swiping it from itsleft hand edge to the right. If there's an open app in it it can be opened by dragging from the right edge of the screen. I it's empty it will simply disappear and you can open it again by the three finger tap or by opening the last app open in it. A three finger drag will take an open app out of the side stage and restore it to full screen.
