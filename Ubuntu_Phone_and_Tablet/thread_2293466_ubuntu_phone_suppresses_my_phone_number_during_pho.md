### Post by coffeecat on 2015-09-05
I have two SIMS from two different UK network providers in my BQ Aquaris E5 Ubuntu edition. If I phone my landline, or another mobile which uses a third network, with either SIM, the number appropriate to the SIM I am dialling out from is displayed in the receiving phone. I can't find any way of suppressing my caller ID display in the Ubuntu phone settings.

Are you sure this is not a global setting in your phone provider's service, which you are temporarily over-riding with the *31# GMS code? Have you tried the SIM in a different mobile phone which uses a different operating system?
