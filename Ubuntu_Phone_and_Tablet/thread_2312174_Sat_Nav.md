Hi All,

I'm not sure if everyone knows but there is a sat van app for the Ubuntu phone.

Its called uNav 

I tested it out tonight and it works a treat.

It took a couple of minutes to get a gps signal the first time but it came straight up the second time.

You need to have here maps installed and your location settings set to the bottom option, I restarted my phone and off I went.
