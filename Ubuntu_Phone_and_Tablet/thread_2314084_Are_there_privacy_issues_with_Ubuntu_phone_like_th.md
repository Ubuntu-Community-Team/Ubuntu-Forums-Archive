### Post by 1clue on 2016-02-19
Location services (E911) cannot be turned off on any cell phone.

The very technology which allows you to talk in a moving car, that enables your phone to pick the next radio tower, also enables others to locate you.  Most notably, the police.

If you don't want to be tracked, then don't own a cell phone.

That said, even not owning a cell phone probably won't protect you in any city with traffic lights and cameras.  Or people who have a webcam pointed outside at the street, or networked security cameras.

If someone you know has Facebook, then you're on Facebook whether you have an account or not.  Same thing goes with anyone who lives or works or passes time near you.

The age of privacy is gone.  The end.
