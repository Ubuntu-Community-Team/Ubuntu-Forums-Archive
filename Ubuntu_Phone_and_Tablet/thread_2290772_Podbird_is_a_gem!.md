### Post by robcar on 2015-08-15
I tried the Podbird app and I have to say it is a gem! Consistent interface and nice initial wizard.
I noticed that it doesn't 'suffer' from the life-cycle feature, which forces the user to keep the app in foreground in order to keep listening to it, as for example in CuteSpotify.
How come? I mean, that is exactly what I want: select a podcast, turn the display off and listen to it; but how is this behaviour different in other apps?
