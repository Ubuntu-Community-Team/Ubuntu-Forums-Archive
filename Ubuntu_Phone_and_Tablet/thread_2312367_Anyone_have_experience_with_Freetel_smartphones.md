### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2016-02-05
No experience with such phones - really new to me - but I can tell you Ubuntu won't go with them, the same way it doesn't go with the vast majority of phones currently on the market.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices)

A mobile OS for (ARM based) phones or tablet isn't the same thing as its desktop counterpart (x86, x86_64). You don't install the same way (read above) and unless you're a developer working closely with the manufacturer you simply won't make it.
