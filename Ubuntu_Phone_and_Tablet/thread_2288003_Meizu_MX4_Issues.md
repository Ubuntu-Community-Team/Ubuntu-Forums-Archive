### Post by Harald_Walker on 2015-07-23
> **poselianovns said:**

And there are many more. The good thing is that updates roll out regularly. Things are getting better. 

I also think that the toolbar on the lock screen is strange. It is something you can turn off. 

1. Is already reported: [[MX4] Battery statistics are incorrect]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/canonical-devices-system-image/+bug/1471913") 
3. Might be: [Impossible to log in on **Instagram** using account setting]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/bugs/1475010") or [Instagram Photos scope and Photos scope don't recognize Instagram online account]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/bugs/1415595") and it might be fixed.
7. Reported in: [Soundcloud don't let me log in.]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity-scope-soundcloud/+bug/1475776")

I suggest you register that the bug also effects you and subscribe to the bug and do the same for other bugs that you also have come across.

More known MX4 issues: 
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/bugs/+bugs?field.searchtext=mx4&search=Search+Bug+Reports](https://bugs.launchpad.net/bugs/+bugs?field.searchtext=mx4&search=Search+Bug+Reports)
