### Post by wlbi on 2016-11-15
> **Leon8200 said:**
> Hi all, I am looking to replace my androids os with ubuntu or linux, is this possable?
Probably not... 
[B]Why?
[/B]Smartphones usually using ARM CPU and not Intel or AMD. The ARM CPU can not do hardware detection, so the installed operating system must be exactly for this specific device and will not work on any other device. 
It's not that you just have one installation medium for all devices, you need one medium for each device. 
[https://ubports.com/](https://ubports.com/) is a good website of ports for different devices.
