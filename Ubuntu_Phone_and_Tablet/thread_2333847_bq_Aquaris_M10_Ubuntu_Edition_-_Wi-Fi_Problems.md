### Post by paulgreen35 on 2016-08-13
I have a bq Aquaris M10 Ubuntu Edition tablet, but it seems to be having problems staying connected to the internet.

When the tablet is connected to my broadband router/hub, I can initially browse the internet and download software from the Ubuntu Store without any problems. However, after any given time during browsing, the next webpage I want to navigate to hangs and I get a 'connection error' message. This happens no matter what website or what part of the Ubuntu store I'm visiting.

The only way I can get the tablet to load webpages again is to turn the Wi-Fi off on my tablet and then back on again. I've made sure it's not a fault with my broadband router/hub as I have a laptop connected to it and have no problems at all. 

It's making me wonder if it's a problem with the Ubuntu Touch operating system, or if it is a hardware fault in bq's products. I used to own a bq Aquaris E4.5 Ubuntu edition phone, and I had exactly the same problem.

With that being said, has anyone else been having problems with this issue, and those who have, could you give me any advice?
