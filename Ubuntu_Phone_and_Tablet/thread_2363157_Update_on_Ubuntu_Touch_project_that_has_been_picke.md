A quote from their site.
> Every official Canonical Ubuntu Touch device (except the devices that needed to be flashed by users, like the Nexus 4 and 7 2013 WiFi) are now working with UBports Ubuntu Touch images. More information at:
[https://blog.ubports.com/2017/06/01/community-update.html](https://blog.ubports.com/2017/06/01/community-update.html)

Great news UBports has a sponsor for the project and taking donations also, crossing fingers.
