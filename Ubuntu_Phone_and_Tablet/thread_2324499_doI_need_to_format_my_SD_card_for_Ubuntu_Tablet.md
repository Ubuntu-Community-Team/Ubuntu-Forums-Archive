### Post by Old Jimma on 2016-05-17
Solution:

Leave your micro sd card in Fat32 format.

Turn your tablet off.

Insert the micro sd card in the slot on the top.

Turn your tablet on.

If you haven't already, install file manager.

Open file manager.

Tap on the right-most icon in the upper right hand corner.

Enter your password.

You now have access to your Micro SD card!!
