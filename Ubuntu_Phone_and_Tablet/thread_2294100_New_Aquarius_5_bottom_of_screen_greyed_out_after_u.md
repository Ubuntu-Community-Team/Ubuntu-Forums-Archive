### Post by jopub on 2015-09-11
I suppose, you write about an update of the OS.
My Aquaris 5 arrived three weeks ago. The ubuntu release 14.x was installed and I upgraded to the recent 15.04. My screen did not show any lines.
I'm a newbie to this forum and to ubuntu phone, but without better advice by one of the experienced people here, I would do the following:
1. I would rotate the phone to rotate the screen. If the lines rotate with the screen, something could be gone wrong with the graphics driver.
I would suppose a software problem.
2. If the lines do not rotate with the screen, I would suppose a hardware problem. But I were not absolutely sure, because I'm not familiar with
linux graphics control.
In any case, I would reset the OS to delivery  state. If the screen problem remains, I would send back the phone.
