### Post by KingBongo on 2015-12-28
Hi. I am having some problems with my Nexus 4, very annoying ones. The problems are two-fold:

1. Somehow it seems like the wrong screen size is detected, I can no longer do long sweeps to display all active apps in order to switch between them or close them. Often though it is possible to lay the phone on its side and then do the long sweep. Most of the time it is also hard (or impossible) to display the dock by swiping from the left.

2. When texting messages there seem to be some "dead areas" on the screen making it hard or impossible to type some letters, for example "p" and the swedish "ö". The funny thing is that the letters to the left and right of them are working fine. Again, the problem can be circumvented by laying the phone on its side.

Right now I am using the **ubuntu-touch/rc-proposed/bq-aquaris.en **channel, but I have tried the **ubuntu-touch/stable/ubuntu** and **ubuntu-touch/devel/ubuntu **channels as well with the same results. I am kind of confused since everything used to work previously regardless of channel used.

Any ideas? Is the phone giving up on me or is Ubuntu Touch to blame?
