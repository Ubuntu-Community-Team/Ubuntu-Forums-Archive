### Post by handaxe on 2016-03-14
I am unsure that this is a MX4 issue alone, as various descriptions of location weirdness exist for the Bq devices as well.

Ubuntu Touch's Location Service uses Nokia HERE service for wifi location and uses just the attached cell mast (not triangulation of visible masts like most other OSs) and uses this position for a short while until it is regarded as stale. The last means less accuracy at the onset. Thereafter one is in the hands of Nokia's wifi geo-database and YMMV. For instance, my home location apparently is unknown to Nokia wifi wise as it puts me elsewhere in a default location I resides some 6 months back.

In short, depending on variables, UT location service can well be a mess at present.
