### Post by bernard-godard on 2016-07-24
Successfully tested:
- MUX: AVI, VIDEO: XVID, AUDIO: MPEG1-LAYER3 
- MUX: AVI, VIDEO: DIVX4, AUDIO: MPEG1-LAYER3 
- MUX: AVI, VIDEO: DIVX5, AUDIO: MPEG1-LAYER3 
- MUX: MP4, VIDEO: H264, AUDIO: MP4-AAC
