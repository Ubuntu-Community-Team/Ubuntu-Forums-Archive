If I'm not mistaken, Apple's iTunes uses some sort of proprietary method to display the track/album artwork... I remember years ago, I was using another (non-Apple) device with iTunes and I had the same problem.

Since that time, I "rip" all of my music in the FLAC (free lossless audio codec) and as a "backup" method, I keep a copy of the track/album artwork in the same folder as the music...

It doesn't answer your question I'm afraid, but it might explain what the problem is.
