### Post by naomibrown on 2016-01-22
I learnt how to do this today! You can just change the background picture that you see on the lock screen by making your own! The original picture is 1080 x 1920 so that's the size I used for mine. I just made a white background in GIMP with the text I wanted at the bottom. To choose this new image you need to open Settings > Background and upload the new image. 

This is from nik90's answer at:[http://askubuntu.com/questions/601342/how-do-you-configure-the-lock-screen-in-ubuntu-phone]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/601342/how-do-you-configure-the-lock-screen-in-ubuntu-phone").
