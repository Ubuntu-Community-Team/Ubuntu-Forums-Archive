### Post by RobTheBold on 2017-01-16
I've never gotten the button push timing just right to unlock the screen after it goes dark, but there is another (kinda lame) workaround (at least for stable 15 and 16):

0. (optional) Get a timing device that you can count seconds on like a watch with second hand. Vulcans can skip this step.
1. Note the screen timeout setting: 30 sec, 60 sec, or 2 minutes.
2. Push and release the power button and wait for (timeout-10) seconds. So if you use default 60 sec timeout, wait 50 seconds. Don't touch anything else, just watch the phone.
3. When 50 seconds (or 20 sec. or 1 min 50 seconds depending on your timeout) is up, the screen appears dim for 10 seconds as if it had just been sitting there inactive and was warning you it was going to go black.
4. Do whatever you need to unlock (pw, code, etc.)

*Mildly* less inconvenient than a full reboot.

I have *never* gotten the browser to work.
