### Post by donquichot2016 on 2016-06-13
Connect an USB Otg cable. Connect a mouse, click and swipe the screen. Quickly connect a keyboard and type your passcode (and maybe press Enter). This should unlock the screen.

Have you turned on Developer mode, or enabled the SSH server, before the screen broke?
