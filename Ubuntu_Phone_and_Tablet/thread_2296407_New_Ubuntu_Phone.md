### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-09-25
I recently saw an online newspaper report claiming that Apple's latest offering had sold out. Now, if that can happen to a corporation like Apple it can certainly happen to the much smaller suppliers of Ubuntu phones. It is all a question of the size of production runs. Make too many and you make less profit. Make too few and you make less profit. If Miezu have sold out their production run of its Ubuntu phone (and it is definitely not being offered for sale at present on the web site) then Meizu might be tempted to invest in another production run or a new model, even a tablet.

New models, release dates & sales figures are confidential information belonging to the manufacturers that Canonical is in partneship with. If anyone involved in Ubuntu knew anything they would not be allowed to say.
