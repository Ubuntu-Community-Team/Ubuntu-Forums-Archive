### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-05-02
This is a user forum. That is all it is.

Ubuntu is open source so there is nothing stopping anyone from porting Ubuntu to other suitable devices. There is even a porting guide for doing it. But from what I understand about the plans of Canonical regarding mobiles devices, Canonical has no intention itself of porting Ubuntu to the great variety of mobile devices already on the market.

The intention of Canonical from the beginning was to get Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) interested in producing and selling Ubuntu mobile devices and then working with OEMs to provide the OS for them to install on their hardware.

It is it that method that has brought the BQ Ubuntu phone to market and hopefully, the same method will bring other Ubuntu mobile devices to market as well.

Regards.
