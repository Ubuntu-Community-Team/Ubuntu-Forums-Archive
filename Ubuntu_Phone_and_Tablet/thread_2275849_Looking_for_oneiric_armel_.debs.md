### Post by bcschmerker on 2015-04-28
**Sri, can't help you** - the [Ubuntu® Old Releases server](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/) has only i386 and AMD64 ISO's for [11.10 Oneiric Ocelot™](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/releases/11.10/).  The [Main](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/dists/oneiric/) (14 October 2011), [Security](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/dists/oneiric-security/) (25 April 2013), [Updates](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/dists/oneiric-updates/) (25 April 2013), [Backports](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/dists/oneiric-backports/) (21 February 2013), and [Proposed](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/ubuntu-dists/oneiric-proposed/) (23 March 2013) Repositories are frozen as of the dates indicated.
