### Post by _sluimers_ on 2015-05-08
There are some serious issues I have with the Ubuntu Touch phone that doesn't make it just a phone with few apps, but a non-working phone (I don't even use it as a phone yet, I just like to try out the apps it has so far.).
Here's a couple of them:

1) **There's no paste button for wi-fi passwords**. My wi-fi password is 128 characters long and I like too keep it that way.
2) **It often crashes** at the store scope. Like I can't press the back button on store and so **I am forced to restart the phone.**.
3) **I** constantly **get asked for the Ubuntu One password** in the store scope, when I do, **it's not accepting my login credentials**. Even when I already have logged into Ubuntu One via settings -> accounts.
    When I have done the latter, I get a Login Error first, that asks me to "Go to account", instead of directly taking me to the login screen.
4) Updates stay frozen at 0% (again).
