### Post by hatfield2 on 2015-08-20
I've had a MX4 for a couple of weeks now and the battery discharge seems rather erratic.  Generally a full charge will last me a couple of days, my usage is fairly light.  


However, I had it charging overnight on Sunday so it was fully charged when I left the house at 07:30, all was normal (just a slight drop) until I got into the office at 9:00 when (looking at the battery status graph later) it started a rapid discharge dropping to below 50% by lunchtime and reaching 8% by 18:00 even though it had little use that day, 5 or 6 text messages to and from the wife but otherwise just sitting on the desk.  


I charged it up normally that evening and then left it overnight, with only a 10% drop by morning with it still being above 20% by Wednesday evening (even after some 4G web surfing on Tuesday) when it was fully charged up again.  


This time, overnight it declined to 60% but around 9:00 this morning (again according to the battery status graph), the rate of decline slowed and it had only just dropped below 40% by 18:30.  Again, little use apart from texts and one call during the day with no use overnight.


Has anyone else noticed a similar "randomness" in the battery discharge?  Is this a quirk of the MX4 or is it a quirk of Ubuntu Touch, also affecting the Aquarius phones?
