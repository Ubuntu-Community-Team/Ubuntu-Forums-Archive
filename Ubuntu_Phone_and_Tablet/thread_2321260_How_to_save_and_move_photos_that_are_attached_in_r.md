### Post by nothingspecial on 2016-04-23
Hi

If you hold your finger down on the image (long tap), that is when you will see what you describe, the only options are select or delete. But if you tap the photo quickly it should open full screen, if you tap it again you get the option to download (an arrow pointing down) or the option to share (3 circles in a triangle).

So either download them and connect your phone to your computer with the usb cable or there is a flickr uploader app, email them to your self, wifi transfer app etc etc lots of options there.
