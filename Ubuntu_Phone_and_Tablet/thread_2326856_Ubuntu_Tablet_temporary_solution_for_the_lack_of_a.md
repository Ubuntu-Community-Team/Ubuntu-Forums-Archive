### Post by jyoti3 on 2016-06-05
While the OTA 12 does not bring us the on-screen keyboard we are waiting for, there is an indirect way of having a temporary alternative, although not very good: we can go, in Firefox, to "add-ons / extensions" and search for a &#8220;virtual keyboard&#8221; or a &#8220;screen keyboard&#8221; and install it.
 
 
 See some keyboards here and tell us witch is, in your opinion, the best to the Ubuntu Tablet:
 
 
 [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search/?q=virtual+keyboard&appver=46.0&platform=linux](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search/?q=virtual+keyboard&appver=46.0&platform=linux)
