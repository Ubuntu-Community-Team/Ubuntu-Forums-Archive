### Post by ziggutas on 2015-10-18
Hi David_Farkas,

I have a similar problem with my bq aquaris e4.5 ubuntu edition: every now and then the phone freezes upon receipt of a text message notification and I have been unable to turn it off, so just have to wait for battery to drain.I had read somewhere that holding power button and volume button at the same time might turn it off...but no luck. Until this week when it happened again and I was just about to give up trying anything when I did this: hold power button for 10+ seconds, then whilst still holding the power button, press vol down briefly (vol down being the lower end of the long volume button). The phone immediately switched off and rebooted...deep joy!
