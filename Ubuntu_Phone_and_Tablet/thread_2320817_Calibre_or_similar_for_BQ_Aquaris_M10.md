### Post by bernard-godard on 2016-04-19
DocViewer is good for PDF but does not support most other ebook formats such as epub. It would be good if they could add epub support but epub is not even supported by evince or most other doc viewer on the desktop (that's why people need calibre). Beru works for epub but is less friendly than DocViewer.
