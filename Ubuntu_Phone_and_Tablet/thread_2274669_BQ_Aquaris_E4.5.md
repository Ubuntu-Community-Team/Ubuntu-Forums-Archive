### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-04-21
Is this a BQ Ubuntu Phone or the BQ Android version? The two phone devices might be Aquaris E4.5 but the screens are different because Ubuntu phone does not require an on-screen button.

The Ubuntu phone does have an Email app or it should be getting one. I think that it is called Dekko. 

[https://uappexplorer.com/app/dekko.dekkoproject](https://uappexplorer.com/app/dekko.dekkoproject)

The apps on Ubuntu phone, including the phone core apps, are all community developed. Canonical has opened the door wide to community developers and provides assistance such as advice on design and supported hack days.

[http://mhall119.com/2013/07/ubuntu-weather-app-hackfest/](http://mhall119.com/2013/07/ubuntu-weather-app-hackfest/)

So, when it comes to apps on Ubuntu devices we should not think in terms of "Canonical should do this" or "Canonical should do that."

Regards.
