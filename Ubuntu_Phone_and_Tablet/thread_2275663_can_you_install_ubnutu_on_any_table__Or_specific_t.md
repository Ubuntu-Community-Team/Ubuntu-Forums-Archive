### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-04-27
At the moment Ubuntu for Devices as  it is called is mainly about Ubuntu phone. Work is ongoing to produce a Ubuntu tablet. And there is a minimum hardware specification.

[http://www.ubuntu.com/phone/partners](http://www.ubuntu.com/phone/partners)

Canonical used 3 Nexus devices as reference devices but apart from the BQ device those were the only devices officially supported. Community developers have been working on ports to other devices with differing degrees of progress.

[https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/installing-ubuntu-for-devices/](https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/installing-ubuntu-for-devices/)

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices)

Regards.
