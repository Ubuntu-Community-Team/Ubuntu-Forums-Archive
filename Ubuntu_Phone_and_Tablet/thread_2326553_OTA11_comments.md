### Post by lapor2 on 2016-06-03
You can turn on/off for the desktop mode or tablet mode in the top right corner, where you can turn your phone on and off. There is a slider for desktop mode. Change that to green and you will have "convergence".
Rotation-lock does what is says. It locks the screen position, so the picture on screen does not rotate if you rotate the phone. Check it out. It can be quite handy if you watching a movie or reading something and at the same time surfing or bungee jumping ;)

Cheers
