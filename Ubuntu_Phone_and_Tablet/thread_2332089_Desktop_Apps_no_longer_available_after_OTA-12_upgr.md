### Post by lapor2 on 2016-07-28
Ok, now slide from the bottom and add xapps scope as your favorite by "staring" it. It should add a scope to your previous scopes (the last one on the list). Now just swipe, until you get to the last scope which should be xapps scope :)
You can also rearrange them, so it can be your first scope or whatever.
