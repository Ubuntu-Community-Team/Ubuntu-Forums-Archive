### Post by steve2602 on 2016-12-16
"Also, in the meantime, I'm considering installing Ubuntu Phone on my Android (I have a Samsung Galaxy Note5). The thought of doing this makes me a little nervous. Does anyone have any success stories to share or advice for me?"

I think your best option is to buy a Meizu Pro 5 Flyme version - they are still able to be purchased, and install OTA 14. I did exactly that (well, initially I installed OTA 13, and upgraded). It is now my main phone, and works exceptionally well. There's a few minor issues, but nothing to stop the phone being used for everyday use (calls, contacts, emails, calendar, clock/alarms, torch, turn-by-turn navigation, sms, camera, browser, terminal, bluetooth). I especially enjoy fast 4G data connections, best phone yet for that.

Oh, and with wifi and bluetooth turned off, battery longevity is exceptional (e.g. after 20 hours, still at 80% -- which, by way of extrapolation, means 100 hours on standby).
