### Post by mssever on 2008-09-19
[LIST=1]
[*]Right click an empty area of your panel and choose Add to Panel.
[*]Add the Keyboard Indicator
[*]Right click on the keyboard indicator applet and choose Keyboard Preferences
[*]On the Layouts tab, install whatever layout you want. There are, of course, Spanish layouts. I use the layout USA International (AltGr dead keys) which is primarily an English layout but makes it easy to also type Spanish and other western and central European languages.
[/LIST]
