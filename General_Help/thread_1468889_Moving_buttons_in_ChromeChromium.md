### Post by scouser73 on 2010-05-01
In Google Chrome, go to **Options > Personal Stuff > Appearance** and make sure that **Use system title bar and borders** is selected.

If it's not and you don't wish to make that selection then there is no way that you can move the buttons as far as I know.
