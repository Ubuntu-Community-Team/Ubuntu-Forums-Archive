### Post by kamstrup on 2005-05-07
Doesn't the CF show up in the "Computer" folder (when you have a card in it)? You can just move/copy the images out of it.

There has been problems with a specific type of SanDisk CF-readers, namely SDDR-05b and SanDisk SDDR-0. If your reader does not have an eject button, then it's probably one of those... (you might also be able to figure it out by looking in System->Admin->Device Manager)
