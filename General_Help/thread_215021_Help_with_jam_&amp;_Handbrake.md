### Post by pnutzh4x0r on 2006-07-27
Did you get an executable called HBTest.  That is the command-line version of HandBrake...

For instance, to scan your DVD, you can do something like:

[INDENT][/INDENT]$ HBTest -i /media/cdrom/video_ts -t 0

For a short handy script, check out my short little howto for installing HandBrake on Ubuntu at:

[http://www.nd.edu/~pbui/blog/tips_and_tricks_20060724-103000.html](http://www.nd.edu/~pbui/blog/tips_and_tricks_20060724-103000.html)
