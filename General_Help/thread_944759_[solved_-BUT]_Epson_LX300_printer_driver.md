ubuntu 7.10 & 8.04

I spent sometime today trying to get my Epson LX300 working and noticed a couple of posts earlier today of folk who had had trouble. As one of the most common dot matrix printers it seemed odd that the developers hadn't included drivers. One suggestion was using IbmproII.

Like its LQ sisters (or brothers!) it is ESC/P technology but unlike LQ570 the LX ones don't seem to be listed apart from the LX1050. When you look for it go to Generic and then scroll back up (for some reason it automatically scrolls down). It is called ESC/P DOT MATRIX
[System~>Administration~>Printing~>New printer~>LPD/LPR Printer~>Generic]

I use a sheet feeder on mine and that is not catered for. Works fine for those with tractor paper BUT for a sheet feeder or single manual feed the positioning leaves you with a large top margin. So once again one might as well dual boot until this one is solved!

Hope this helps someone.
