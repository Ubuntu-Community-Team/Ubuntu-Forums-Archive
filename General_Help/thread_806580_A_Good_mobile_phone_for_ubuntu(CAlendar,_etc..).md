### Post by SneakyWho_am_i on 2008-05-25
I'm not sure about Sunbird, but I have a Nokia 6234 and I use it like a memory card. I transfer pictures and stuff back and forth over Bluetooth. Also the computer can send and receive text messages.

Cons:
- all the video has sound in a format for which no decoder is available in the repositories (any of the official or semi-official repositories!)

What works well:
- gammu, I think

What I haven't tested:
- using the phone as a modem
- usb or serial cables
- making or receiving calls, flashing the firmware
- calendar synchronizations

So my phone is probably not what you're looking for, but hey, may as well add my two cents, yeah?
