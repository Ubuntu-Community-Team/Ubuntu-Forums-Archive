### Post by drs305 on 2008-12-07
I have a bookmark for the following link. It opens a google search bar which will search the ubuntu forums exclusively. Just enter the key words and set the time frame:
[Google the forums]("http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&as_q=+&as_epq=&as_oq=&as_eq=&num=30&lr=&as_filetype=&ft=i&as_sitesearch=ubuntuforums.org&as_qdr=w&as_rights=&as_occt=any&cr=&as_nlo=&as_nhi=&safe=images")
 
It sets it for the past week, which would normally be too short for most users, but you can click on the time selector to expand out the search dates.
