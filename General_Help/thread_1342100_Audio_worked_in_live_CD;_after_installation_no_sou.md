### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-30
Make sure things aren't muted.  In karmic, the gnome sound in muted by default.  I don't remember if it was the same in jaunty.  

Check the sound preferences to see if the speakers are even recognized by Ubuntu, and see if anything is muted.

You can also run "alsamixer" from a terminal, which may give you some more devices than those listed by gnome.
