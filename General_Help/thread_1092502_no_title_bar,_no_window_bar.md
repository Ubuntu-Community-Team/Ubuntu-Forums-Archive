Is this on all programs or just on Firefox? If it's on all programs you can open up CCSM (CompizConfig Settings Manager) and make sure the "Window Decorations" is selected, if the option is selected, next time you open Firefox pres "F11" twice and resize the window, and that should get you up and running.
