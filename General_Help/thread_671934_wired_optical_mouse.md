### Post by amo-ej1 on 2008-01-19
Well that only means that there's still power on your usb port. Which basicly means that your motherboard is still under power (or at least certain parts) which is required for waking up on a power key press, or waking up from standby, or waking up on lan (or keypress).

I don't there there's much you can do about it. Certainly not from a linux standpoint, perhaps take a look in the bios and disable all 'wake from' alike things ?
