### Post by Cherry Support on 2010-12-03
[FONT=Arial]Not all adapters work properly. Some adapters may work with some keyboard models but not others. Some adapters may work with some computer models and not others.[/FONT]
 
[FONT=Arial]Cherry has found during testing that &#8220;cable&#8221; type adapters have a better chance of working than the &#8220;stub&#8221; type adapter.[/FONT]
 
[FONT=Arial]Because of these inconsistencies, Cherry does not recommend the use of PS/2 to USB adapters. We strongly recommend that you purchase a USB keyboard.[/FONT]
 
[FONT=Arial]For Point of Sale keyboards, with integrated MSR, we have found that increasing the delay time of the keyboard may help with the dropping of characters. Follow the instructions below in order to increase the delay time.[/FONT]
 
[FONT=Arial]1. Open NotePad or some other text editor[/FONT]
[FONT=Arial]Hold down both Ctrl, Alt, and Shift keys (all 6 keys at the same time) until you hear 2 beeps.[/FONT]
 
[FONT=Arial]2. Press: Z then 1 (above the Q) then 7 (above the Y)[/FONT]
 
[FONT=Arial]3. Press: Spacebar and you should hear 2 beeps.[/FONT]
 
[FONT=Arial]4. Test the card reader.[/FONT]
