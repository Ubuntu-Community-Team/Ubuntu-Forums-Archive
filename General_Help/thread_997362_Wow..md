### Post by jimmy the saint on 2008-11-29
If the livecd won't recognize the filesystem on your disk it likely because you mistakenly damaged it when you attempted to delete the vista partition.  If you haven't tried to run a live cd and then explore your drive to recover the files, do so.  Just because it doesn't boot doesn't mean that you cannot access the files on it, just like you would an external harddrive.  Also, in the future, back up all of your important data BEFORE you do anything that could destroy it it (like partitioning).

Good Luck!
