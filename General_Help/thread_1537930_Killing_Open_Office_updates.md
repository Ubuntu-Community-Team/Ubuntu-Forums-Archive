### Post by snowpine on 2010-07-24
I would recommend going in to the Synaptic Package Manager and uninstalling any packages you don't need. 

If that is not possible for whatever reason, Synaptic also gives you the option to go into the Package menu and choose Lock Version to prevent future updates on a package or packages.
