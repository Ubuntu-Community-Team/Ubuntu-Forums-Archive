While you can't actually "disable" it, you can "pause" it, and just not re-enable it.  Simply open your web history ([http://www.google.com/history](http://www.google.com/history)), and go to the bottom left where it says "Pause".

Of course, Google tracks your searches no matter what, but by disabling your web history, they just don't show it to you.
