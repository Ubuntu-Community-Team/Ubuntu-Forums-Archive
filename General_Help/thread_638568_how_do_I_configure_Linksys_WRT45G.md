### Post by elspiko on 2007-12-12
You should be able to access the web interface by going to 192.168.1.1 in your browser.

### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-12-12
The WRT54G is configured through your browser. Hook up your machine with a cable and access it at the address elspiko gave you. Enter the correct password and you'll be given a configuration page where you can change it however you like.
