Do you have an Intel motherboard?

Some Intel boards have a problem that they call an "Inductance singing issue".

It is a thin, tinny ringing buzz noise that pulses on and off from inside the computer.

My board (Intel DQ45CB) had that problem, and the latest bios update fixed the problem (they even mention it in the "fixed items" list for the bios).

I'm not sure that I know what you mean by "processor sound", but it was worth a shot to tell you about the singing problem.
