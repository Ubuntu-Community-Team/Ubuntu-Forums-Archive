### Post by magicmanfk on 2008-07-01
try hitting alt+f2, typing in "gconf-editor" (no quotes), then going to desktop, gnome, background, and making sure "draw background" is checked. 

Also try going to (in gconf-editor) apps-->nautilus-->preferences and making sure "show desktop" is checked.
