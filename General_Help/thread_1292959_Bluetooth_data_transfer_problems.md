### Post by fragos on 2009-10-16
Right click the Bluetooth icon in the upper right and select Preferences. If your Bluetooth chip-set or USB dongle was detected you should see 2 tabs. The 1st of those shows the name used by your system and the known devices it has paired with. I'd probably select "Always visible" as well. Select your  phone from the known devices and click the star icon which will make your phone a trusted device. At this point you can send data to the phone. For the phone to be able to send data to your PC you must also install "Bluetooth File Sharing". Add/Remove can be used for this purpose. You will now see another blue icon that looks like a triangle in front of two circles. No configuration files need be edited as was required in the past.
