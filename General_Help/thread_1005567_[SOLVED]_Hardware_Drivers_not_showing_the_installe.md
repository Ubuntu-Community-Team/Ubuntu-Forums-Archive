### Post by Scunizi on 2008-12-08
The reason you don't see them is because you installed the drivers direct from Nvidia's site.. the system doesn't recognize them in the Hardware Drivers section because of this.  To turn them off so to speak, you have to uninstall them.. sudo sh NVIDIA<bla, bla> --uninstall

Also I see that you're using the 180.08 version.. if they are doing good leave them, but there is a 180.11 version out now.. I just installed them this morning.. they fixed my 8200 card pretty good.
