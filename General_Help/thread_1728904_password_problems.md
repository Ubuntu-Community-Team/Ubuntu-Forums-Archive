### Post by mcduck on 2011-04-14
When in the Passwords and Encryption Keys, you need to right-click on the keyring itself, and select "Change Password".

The password isn't contained in the keyring, that would cause a bit of a problem as the keyring manager would have to unlock the keyring to check the password, but it can't do that before it has already checked the password... Kind of like if your front door only had a lock on the inside, doesn't really work too well. ;)
