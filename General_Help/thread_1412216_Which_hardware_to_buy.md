### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-02-21
Video cards: Nvidia-based video cards are much superior to ATI, if you're using Linux.

Printers: You can't go past HP for Linux support in printing or scanning. Unfortunately their ink cartridges are not especially cheap, and you can't get generic brands - but assuming you're not going to be printing out a lot, then you definitely want HP.

Otherwise there's a website called openprinting.org that will tell you what printers work with CUPS (the Linux and Unix printing system). It won't tell you today, though, because it's down at the moment.
