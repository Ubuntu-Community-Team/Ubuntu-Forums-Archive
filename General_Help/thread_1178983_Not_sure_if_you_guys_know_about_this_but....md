### Post by JECHO on 2009-06-05
I have been looking both on the forums and in google for a way to have adobe reader working on 64-bit Ubuntu... I could not find any answers anywhere so I checked the Medibuntu repository and what do you know, they have packages of adobe reader for 64-bit Ubuntu! Even plug-ins for Firefox and multiple language packs! Here are the repos:


Ubuntu 8.10 Intrepid Ibex: 
```
## Medibuntu
deb http://packages.medibuntu.org/ intrepid free non-free
```

Ubuntu 9.04 JAunty Jackelope:
```
## Medibuntu
deb http://packages.medibuntu.org/ jaunty free non-free
```


You're welcome. :)
