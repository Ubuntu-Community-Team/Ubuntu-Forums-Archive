### Post by wwwil on 2010-05-06
Is the strip of background like a "hole" in the windows or does it just stay on the right of the desktop?

If it is just static on the edge of the desktop it could be that you've added an extra panel, just right click on it and click "delete panel"

Otherwise try going to System > Preferences > Appearance, switch themes and wait for it to change, then switchback to your previous theme and see if it looks any better.

Hope this helps.
