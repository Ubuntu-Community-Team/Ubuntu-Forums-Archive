### Post by Icebear on 2009-10-19
I think you will need to add a Hebrew keyboard layout

goto System>Preferences>Keyboard

then hit the layout tab

then hit the +add button

to choose what key to change the layouts in the layout tab

hit the layout Options button

then hit Key(s) to change layout

and chose what you want
