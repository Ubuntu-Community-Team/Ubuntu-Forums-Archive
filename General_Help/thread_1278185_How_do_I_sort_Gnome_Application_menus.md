### Post by sharkbite1414 on 2009-09-29
try this:
go to:
System > Preferences > Main Menu 
then click on the menu you want to move, then click move up or move down.
if what you want to move is in a menu (say Office, for example) click on that menu on the right hand bar and move the application form there.
