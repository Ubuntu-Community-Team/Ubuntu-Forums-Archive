### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-04-01
All you have to do is go to System->Administration->Software Sources and in the 'Ubuntu Software' tab, uncheck the CD box.  Then it will get packages from the repositories on the net instead of your CD.  Make sure all of the other boxes in that tab are checked, though.
