### Post by cariboo on 2008-06-03
.daa is a poweriso disk image format. you can download a linux version of poweriso and use it to convert the images to a standard iso disk image. You can get poweriso here:

[http://www.poweriso.com/download.htm](http://www.poweriso.com/download.htm)

By the way it's a free version.
