### Post by agentnixon on 2008-11-01
If you have problems with the volume being way too low, even if the volume is set to 100%, try this: under Gnome, try double clicking on the volume icon (where simply clicking it lets you adjust the slider). Then a window should pop up where you can adjust the sound settings as you please. 
Simple, yet took me ages to figure out. :\
