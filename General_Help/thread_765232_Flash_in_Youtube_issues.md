### Post by retrow on 2008-04-24
Several folks who were in development group for Hardy encountered these problems with alpha and beta releases too. Except for flash videos, FF3 runs really well. You could download the stable FF2 from Mozilla and extract it in your home folder and use that one when you want to watch Flash videos. Alternatively, you can get rid of FF3 and go with FF2 or Opera 9.5.
