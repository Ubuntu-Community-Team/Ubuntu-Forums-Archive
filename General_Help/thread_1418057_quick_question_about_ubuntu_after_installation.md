### Post by themusicalduck on 2010-02-28
Have you installed any video drivers? What graphics card do you have? You could find out by typing ```
lspci | grep VGA
``` in a terminal (Applications > Accessories > Terminal) Copy the line and then paste it into the terminal with ctrl+shift+v.
