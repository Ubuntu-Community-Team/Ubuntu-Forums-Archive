### Post by colobix on 2011-12-01
Have you check the temp folder?
Go to Home and ctrl-H to see the hidden folders. Go to .libreoffice and dig to find temp. If you are lucky it's there.

You'll probably find it there since that's where all your saved versions of the document is.
You will probably not find the changes you did the last time you wrote on that story.

### Post by scarleo on 2011-12-01
For writing stories I can definitely recommend [Celtx]("http://celtx.com/"). It provides a less cluttered interface and a lot of nice tools to i.e. help you store characters and other stuff. Also much easier to rearrange chapters and so on. It's way more convenient to just write the story unless you really need fancy formatting stuff on your text. You can also have online backups to prevent loosing anything.
