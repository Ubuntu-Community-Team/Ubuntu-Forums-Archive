### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-05-16
I would look at [http://sugarcrm.com](http://sugarcrm.com) with a time-tracking plug-in.  Depending on your business model, your customers and repeat business may be more important than just tracking support time.  If all you care about is support, then I would look at help-desk or trouble-ticket software.  The list you provided is geared for personal time tracking--such as a consultant who works with multiple clients and needs to bill time for each project.  These solutions don't scale well for an enterprise.

Sugarcrm has an active forum, I would spend some time there and learn the power of using CRM.  [http://forums.sugarcrm.com/](http://forums.sugarcrm.com/)

I've used Farkhad's tool [supertimesheet]("http://forums.sugarcrm.com/f19/supertimesheet-26732/").  It may be overkill, but it is scalable as your business grows.
