### Post by flyfishin on 2005-02-01
gThumb uses the libgphoto2 library.  You can see what camers libgphoto2 supports here:

[http://www.gphoto.org/proj/libgphoto2/support.php](http://www.gphoto.org/proj/libgphoto2/support.php)

You can find out more about gThumb here:

[http://gthumb.sourceforge.net/](http://gthumb.sourceforge.net/)

Compare the version of libgphoto2 on your system with the gphoto website.
