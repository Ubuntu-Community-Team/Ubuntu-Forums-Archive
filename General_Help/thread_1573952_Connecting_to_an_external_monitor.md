What do you mean you can't change it after? Is it recognized, but you can't change the resolution?

Go to Systen, Preferences, Monitors. You should be able to independently change each monitor's resolution, refresh rate and rotation. Uncheck "Same image in all monitors".

You may want to also check "Show monitors in panel" so you can just reference it that way in the future.
