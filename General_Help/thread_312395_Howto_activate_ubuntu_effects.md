### Post by LLRNR on 2006-12-04
Xsss4hell,

Nice dream ;) Welcome to Ubuntu !

On with your question now:

Q1. There are many ways to do a backup... Here are some:

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partimage](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/partimage)

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=81311](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=81311)

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=35087&page=21](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=35087&page=21) post # 210

Q2. You'd have to install Beryl/Compiz... first you'd have to install your graphic card'd driver.

For Edgy, take a look [here](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/BerylOnEdgy)

For Dapper, look [here](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/EyeCandyDapper).

Q3. I think this is related to the driver for your graphic card, which you'd have to install first. Take a look at the links in Q2 I gave you, they take you through the drivers' install first.

Q4. What's a MS Bluetooth Desktop ?!

Q5. Yes, you can access your NTFS partition(s) in read only mode. Err... that is, if you don't want to try some beta software for write access... Of course, either way, you'd have to [mount](http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/mountwindows) your partition(s) first...

For any other things, I'd suggest you take a look at the [Edgy Guide](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy) or at the [Dapper Guide](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Dapper) (if you'd like to install Beryl/Compiz later, then in these guides you'll find a way to install your graphic card's driver first).

LLRNR
