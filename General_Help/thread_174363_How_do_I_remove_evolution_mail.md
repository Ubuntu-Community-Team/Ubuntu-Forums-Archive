### Post by GreenfrogCT on 2006-05-11
[QUOTE=morbid_bean]I need to know how to remove elolution mail from my ubuntu...I dont use it!!!:neutral:[/QUOTE]

Go to  System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager

Click "Search"  Enter:  Evolution

For each of the Evolution components (evolution, evolution-data-server, evolution-exchange, evolution-plugins, evolution-webcal) that are marked as installed (solid green icon) right-click and select "Mark For Removal".   Then click "Apply".  

That should do it.
