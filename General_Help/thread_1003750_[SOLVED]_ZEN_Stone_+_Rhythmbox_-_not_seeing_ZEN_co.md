### Post by sleepingdragon on 2008-12-06
Here's a small puzzle relating to my ZEN Stone 1Gb and Rhythmbox on Intrepid.

I can connect my ZEN perfectly well, it shows up in Nautilus and I'm even prompted to open my ZEN in Rhythmbox when inserted. Nautilus has full read/write with smooth transfers. So far, so good. 

When Rhythmbox opens, it lists the ZEN as a media device, and I can transfer files to it, but for the life of me I cannot see the contents in Rhythmbox. It shows as "Importing 0/0"...

Any ideas on this would be greatly appreciated.

EDIT: Solved it. Wiped off the ZEN and reinstalled the music. Seems as though Rhythmbox can only see files if they're inside a folder called MUSIC on the ZEN... go figure.
