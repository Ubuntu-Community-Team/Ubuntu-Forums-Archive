### Post by zombifier25 on 2012-05-04
I no longer use Lucid Lynx, so...
right click on the bottom taskbar, choose Add New Applet, and search for Windows Switcher List (if that what it's called) and add it to the taskbar.
The windows switcher will appear like a list.
