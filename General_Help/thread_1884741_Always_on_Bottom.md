### Post by rectec794613 on 2011-11-22
One extra note:
If you have KDE you can right click a window's title bar and select "keep below others" from the Advanced menu. Then you can select "No border", maximize the terminal's transparency, and remove the desktop icons. That's as close as you can get in KDE without extra modifications.
