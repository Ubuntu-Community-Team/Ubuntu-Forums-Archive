### Post by Akli on 2006-03-19
I had a problem getting a high Id with amule, when [testing]("http://www.amule.org/testport.php") my tcp port (4662) I found out that it was closed.

I was using RP-pppoe to get an adsl connection, it's got an integrated firewall that allows you to choose between 3 config : stand alone, masquerading and none.

I tried the three configs with no results, my port was still closed.

One day, I decided to install firestarter (a firewall) just out of curiosity, it did not work, I uninstalled and tried amule again, and surprise! my tcp port was open and I could get a highId.

Well, I hope my experience might help some of you but if there is anyone who can explain the phenomenon, feel free to do so.
