### Post by limit223 on 2005-12-31
I use Kontact like this:
Go on Contacts tab(left panel) and unselect all groups from Address Book panel(resource)...Click Add button down in the panel and choose Directory option- Name it : eFamily (leave format vCard) click Ok.
 Now add one by one your family email members ( you can do it in many ways) - the simple way is to go to their email(already sent to you) right click on their address and choose Add to Address Book - if you have checked just only one directory as above described(eFamily) you will see a message of confirmation.
 Sending emails to all in eFamily directory as simple as you probably know already...is...just click send icon - Select on to To box- Address Book: eFamily - Select all -....and you have them all as recipient.
