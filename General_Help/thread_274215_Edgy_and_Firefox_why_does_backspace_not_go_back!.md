### Post by meng on 2006-10-09
Ensure under preferences that you haven't got "allow text to be selected with keyboard" checked. (I don't THINK that could cause your problem, but it does cause strange behavior sometimes.)

Otherwise I don't know the answer to your specific question, but alternatives to backspace include: ALT-left arrow, and installing mouse gestures (Hold right button and motion left).
