### Post by Zill on 2012-04-06
> **keevill said:**
> Further to my original post I can confirm that I have no installed 12.04 ( Beta ? ) on two buggy 11.10 installs and the problems I had, have gone....
Personally, I wouldn't even consider installing beta software on systems required for business use.  Similarly, I would only install LTS versions of an OS, such as Ubuntu 10.04.  The "interim" releases are generally not as stable as the LTS releases and so these are, IMHO, unsuitable for business use.

If you also consider the "human factors" of staff retraining, then I suggest you should install a standard LTS release across all your PCs, to give a similar interface to all users.

Please note that even LTS releases take time to settle down before they can be considered "reliable" and so I would wait at least a couple of months before installing 12.04, the next LTS.
