### Post by Impavidus on 2012-10-31
Then it may be the touchpad that is autonomously clicking. Maybe you are touching it while typing. You should be able to deactivate the touchpad while typing (or permanently). In xubuntu it is straightforward to do so in the mouse and touchpad configuration dialogue, accessible in the settings. I don't know about lubuntu, but it is probably similar.
