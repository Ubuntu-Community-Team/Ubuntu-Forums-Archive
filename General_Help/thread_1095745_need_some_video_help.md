### Post by emarkay on 2009-03-15
Look at the NVIDIA cards too - pick a couple of models numbers and search them here before buying to see if there are any issues.

Also, note that the ATI(They are part of AMD now) cards have poorer support from the OEM and it's a bit more complicated using the "open source" drivers for them than the NVIDIA ones supplied by NVIDIA; NVIDIA is a more "Linux friendly" company IMHO.
