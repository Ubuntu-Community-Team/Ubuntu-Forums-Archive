### Post by gdiloren on 2011-03-21
Download the tar.bz file in your Downloads folder from the official Mozilla site [http://www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/RC/](http://www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/RC/)
then extract in in a new folder called ff4beta on the Desktop that you will just create right clicking on your Desktop with your mouse.
Right-click on the Desktop with your mouse and click "Create Laucher",choose "application",  type the name you want (e.g. FIREFOX RC 2), browse your ff4beta folder on your Desktop in "Command" and double-click firefox file, click ok and that's it, you have a new icon launcher on your desktop for FF4 RC2 without upgrading tediously to Natty and staying with Maverick.:D
Note: This will not remove FF 3.6.15. Just play it safe, keep the old version just in case
