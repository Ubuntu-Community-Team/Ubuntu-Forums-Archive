### Post by Anduu on 2008-01-06
Compiz is easy to turn off....

Go to System>Preferences>Appearance>Visual Effects tab and select none.

Do yourself a favor....create a second account and use it to tinker with Compiz.That way you will learn it and maybe even start using it regularly ;)
