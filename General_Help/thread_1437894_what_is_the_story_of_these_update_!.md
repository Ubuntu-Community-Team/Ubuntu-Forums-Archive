### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-03-25
Use the "Changes" tab to find out what has changed in the new version.

Samba and associated packages (like Kerberos) are in such wide use that there are often new versions, new security fixes and updates. And also, it's a package that Canonical's biggest customers depend on, and that Canonical is contracted to maintain.

If you don't use Samba, then you don't need to install the updates - but if it's a security update it would be a really good idea to. It shouldn't take too much of your internet speed away from you.
