### Post by sdennie on 2009-03-18
It's not uncommon for the video card and various other things (including the kernel) to eat some RAM.  Installing 6G of RAM will get you ~6G of ram but, not 6G to the byte.  Also, this table may help you: [http://xkcd.com/394/](http://xkcd.com/394/)
