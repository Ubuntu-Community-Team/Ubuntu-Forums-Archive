### Post by jbicha on 2017-10-29
You can also search for apps from the **Activities Overview** like you could with Ubuntu 17.04.

Click on one of the results to open it in Ubuntu Software where you can install the app.

The Activities Overview is what GNOME calls the big full-screen area  that you can open by clicking the word Activities or you can open the  Show Applications view by clicking the 9-dot icon at the bottom of the Ubuntu Dock.
