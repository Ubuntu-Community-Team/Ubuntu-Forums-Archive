### Post by DrKenobi on 2010-05-10
Maybe what u have is a bug in Gwibber. Check it here: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/gwibber/+bugs?field.searchtext=&search=Search+Bug+Reports&field.scope=project&field.scope.target=gwibber]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/gwibber/+bugs?field.searchtext=&search=Search+Bug+Reports&field.scope=project&field.scope.target=gwibber")

You can also try to download the pre-release updates of Gwibber. You activate this in 'System --> Administration --> Software Sources --> Updates --> Pre-release updates (lucid-proposed)'. Then you open 'System --> Administration --> Update Manager' and update Gwibber.
