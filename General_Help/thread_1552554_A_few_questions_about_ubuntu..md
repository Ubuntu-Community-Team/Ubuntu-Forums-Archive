### Post by AlphaLexman on 2010-08-13
'Noise' from your computer is created by vibrations in the equipment that are transferred to the air, which oscillates to your eardrum.

It is the movement of machinery, i.e., hard drives, cooling fans, that 'create' noise.

Simply plugging in and turning on the equipment will generate the noise, therefore an operating system cannot reduce the mechanical vibrations inherent to the equipment.
