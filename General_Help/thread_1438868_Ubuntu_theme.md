### Post by darolu on 2010-03-25
Yes, you can simply tar and compress with gunzip (gzip) so your file is a .tar.gz and you can upload it, the person who wishes to install has to extract and paste to their /usr/share/themes/ or ~/.themes/ directory (or drag the tar.gz to their themes window).
