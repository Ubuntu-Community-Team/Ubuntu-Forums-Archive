### Post by Toz on 2011-06-21
According to the FAQ ([http://wiki.xfce.org/faq](http://wiki.xfce.org/faq) - section: "Is it possible to have windows remember their position"), the answer is no. 

However, you can use tools like wmctrl ([http://tomas.styblo.name/wmctrl/](http://tomas.styblo.name/wmctrl/)) and devilspie ([https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Devilspie](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Devilspie)) to accomplish this.
