### Post by dmneoblade on 2006-08-31
I solved this high-tech problem with a nice low-tech solution.

Step 1: Get 4 pairs of broken headphones (cords must be ok. Ask neighbors with kids. ~.^)
Step 2: Get 1 Female headphone plug (radioshack, parts drawer, wherever.)
Step 3: get as much cable length as you can from the various cords, and strip about a half inch of wire off each.
Step 4: Match colors and twist together.
Step 5: cover with electrical tape.
Step 6: Plug speakers into female plug, and the male plugs into the soundcards of the computers.
