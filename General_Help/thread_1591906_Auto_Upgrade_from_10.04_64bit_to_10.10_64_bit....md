### Post by QLee on 2010-10-10
[QUOTE=vickoxy]... but just have no time to make clean install...
...[/QUOTE]
Then, clearly, in my opinion, you have no time to do the upgrade. I can't recommend doing any changes to a mission critical system, which is working, when you have no time to fix it if it breaks. At the very least, you need to have time to restore the backup if the upgrade is problematic. This stuff shouldn't be done when there is no time for error, it has the potential to create a great deal of stress, which itself will make it harder for you to fix any problems. Add to that, waiting for answers that are relevant to whatever problems arise and you have what I consider a recipe for disaster. Wait until you have space to "breathe" before upgrading.
