### Post by coffee412 on 2010-05-04
In the setup (options) area of skype unclick the "let skype manage my mic sound" option. Then reset your mic sound levels in pulseaudio. You should install the pulseaudio Volume control (look in software center for it) to help with the controls. 

Im using skype beta with logitech pro 8000 cam no problems.
