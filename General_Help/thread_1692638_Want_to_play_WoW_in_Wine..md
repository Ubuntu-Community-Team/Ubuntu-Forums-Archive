### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-02-21
Here is the [WineDB]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=20549") article on running WoW.

As for your gpu, obviously, the better graphics card you have the better your graphics will be (up to the limitations imposed by the program itself). Integrated graphics are usually not so great - for a better "experience" you'll want to use a separate video card.

It not like you'll damage anything by trying to run it; I'd suggest starting with everything turned off or to the lowest settings possible though and see if it is acceptable to you (everyone will have different levels of what is acceptable for them)
