### Post by bogan on 2012-06-02
Hi!, **z3r08**8,

 > how [to] add your own custom background with Ubuntu Right-Click on the desktop and select 'Change Desktop Background'.

Under the group of default wallpapers there are two buttons: '+' & '-', Click on the '+' and browse to where your custom wallpaper is select it and click on 'Open. That's it, no problem.

You can also do the same from 'System Settings>Appearance'.
