### Post by cilynx on 2009-10-23
Virtual desktops have been around much longer than Compiz.  If you get rid of Compiz entirely and go back to good old Metacity (Gnome's default window manager), you'll still have your virtual desktops and will be able to navigate between them much more swiftly.

---

### Post by FrostyC on 2009-10-23
Well I found out that Gnome Panel has a built-in "Workspace Switcher". I just added it to the panel, turned off Compiz (SCROLLING DOES NOT WORK UNLESS COMPIZ IS COMPLETELY DISABLED) & now I can simply mouseover it & switch workspaces by scrolling. It's much faster than Compiz's Viewport switcher & I find it to be a completely acceptable alternative, (even though I can't do it by scrolling on the desktop background anymore) so now all is well!:)
