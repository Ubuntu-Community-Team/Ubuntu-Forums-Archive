### Post by Pentagonees on 2007-11-03
I recently set up a computer at the office to be used by multiple colleagues. Popped in the Ubuntu CD, and all went great. Then I configured my own account (nice theme, background, main menu items, icons, etc.).

Is there a way to, somehow, export my theme-, background-, main menu items-, icon-, etc. settings to the other users on the computer, so I don't have to configure them manually?

---

### Post by taurus on 2007-11-03
You could just copy your $HOME (/home/**username**) from your machine to the others and all your personal settings will be carried over.
