### Post by handy on 2006-10-27
It is there in my install, which was clean, except I have a separate **/home** partition?

So try this:

Right click on the ***Menu's*** to access ***Edit Menus*:- ** **System / Administration** (in the **Menu Layout** Dialogue), then tick **Show** *box*, **Disks**, it should be the 2nd item in the Item's list.
