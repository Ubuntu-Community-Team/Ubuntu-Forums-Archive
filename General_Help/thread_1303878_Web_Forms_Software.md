### Post by mbeach on 2009-10-28
If I remember correctly, typolight had a pretty neat form generator.

Online demo at:
[http://demo.typolight.org/typolight/](http://demo.typolight.org/typolight/)
then browse to the left and pick forms. You may be able to download, and view their code to see how they did it. I remember that code being fairly easy to follow.

Also I think concrete5 has a nifty little form generator. Again, might be useful to take a gander at it. They have a 15 day free hosted demo (no download required) at:
[http://www.concrete5.org/](http://www.concrete5.org/)
You don't need a real email address to create your demo to get in and play with the form generator. After your demo is created, click the "Edit Page" then scroll to the bottom of one of the sections and you'll see something like "Add to Main", then pick "form" from the resulting list. You'll get the idea.
