### Post by VCoolio on 2010-01-08
in the compiz wallpaper plugin you can set your wallpapers; hit 'new' and add 4. The order in which the images are listed there is the order they are on the desktops.

in the rotate cube plugin set flip time to what you want; just play with the settings.

for conky: in the config file, change own_window_type to anything but 'desktop', try normal or override. Also in compiz settings, general options, uncheck 'hide skip taskbar windows'.
