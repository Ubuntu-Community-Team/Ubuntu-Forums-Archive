Not necessarily.  You could use a 15 pin VGA to RGB RCA adapter.  One end plugs into the laptop and the other end plugs into the Red, Green and Blue RCA jacks in the back of the TV.  

I haven't tried it but it should work just fine.  I've heard of people using those cables for projectors.  It's worth a shot anyways.
