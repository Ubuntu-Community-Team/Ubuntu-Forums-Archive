### Post by sgosnell on 2009-05-16
Get a card reader.  They can be purchased for ~$10.  Put the SD card in the card reader, connect the reader to a USB port, and it will show up as a drive.  Drag and drop the music files to the card, then put the card back into the phone.  Done.  

You do need to make sure the reader supports microSD, or else buy an adapter, available for a couple of dollars.
