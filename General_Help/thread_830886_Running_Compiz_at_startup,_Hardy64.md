### Post by caver1 on 2008-06-16
I am running Hardy 64 bit. Running Compiz fine. 
The only problem is when I reboot (power outage) The visual effects settings are always reset at none. So I have to go back to reset the at custom. How do I get my machine to keep the custom settings at startup?
caver1

---

### Post by bvm on 2008-06-16
add 'compiz --replace' as a startup session in system->preferences->sessions
