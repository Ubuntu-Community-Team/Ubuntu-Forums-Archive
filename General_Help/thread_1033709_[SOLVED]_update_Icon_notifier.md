### Post by gilloz on 2009-01-07
Can someone tell me the meaning of the notifier update icons that appear when an update is ready to be downloaded?  Sometimes it is an arrow pointing downwards and other times it appears to be like a sun-burst.  What is the meaning of these two icons.  I know it's a dumb question, but I really am curious.  I use Ubuntu 8.04.  Thanks.

---

### Post by avtolle on 2009-01-07
The "down arrow" icon indicates that there is at least one security-related update to be installed; the "sun burst" icon indicates suggested or recommended updates. At least, this is how it appears to me.
