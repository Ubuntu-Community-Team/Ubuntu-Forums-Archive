### Post by Tamlynmac on 2010-11-23
You might check your keyboard shortcuts "system/preferences/Keyboard Shortcuts"

For example, I believe the default for "take screen shot" is the "print button".

You may also wish to check your layout "system/preferences/keyboard/layouts tab" for by country "USA or ?", keyboard model (mine is Generic 105-key (intl) PC) , You can even test your setting there.

Good Luck & Hope this helps
