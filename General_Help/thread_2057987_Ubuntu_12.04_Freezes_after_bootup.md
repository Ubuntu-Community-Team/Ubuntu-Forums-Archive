Try this, go to user group and change the password option to must sign in.  (you can change it back later)  I really suggest you leave it at 'login' though, it may save you down the line if something happens.    Reboot.

When it stops at login page, click the little round cog in the top right corner of the login page.  A dropdown will open, click on 2d then enter.  See if it works in 2d.
