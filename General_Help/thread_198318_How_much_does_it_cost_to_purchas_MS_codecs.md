### Post by x64Jimbo on 2006-06-17
This might just be semantics, but you can't "buy" codecs. You license them. I'm not sure anyone's ever licensed those codecs on such an individual level, so the pricing structure could be pretty unexplored. It's an interesting thought, but considering the DMCA and its provisions that make reverse-engineering anything illegal, you might not be able to license them, no matter how much it costs, since the codecs that work in linux were hacked to do so.
