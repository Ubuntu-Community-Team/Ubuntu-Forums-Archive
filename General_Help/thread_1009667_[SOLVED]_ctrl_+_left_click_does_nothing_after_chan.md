### Post by rudygrabmore on 2008-12-12
i was playing around with compiz hotkeys and i got one of those "this setting conflicts with another setting messages" i clicked to disable the other setting.

after this i couldn't open a link in a new tab in firefox by holding ctrl and left clicking it, and i couldn't select multiple items in any program by holding ctrl and left clicking them (except for icons on my desktop)

i went back into the settings manager and started clicking all the little broom icons on the settings i had been editing until i got my ctrl  left click function back.

the setting that did it was "initiate window resize" in the window resize plugin.


many people are having weird problems with keyboard and mouse after changing compiz settings, so if you experience something like this, just start resetting the last things you changed.
