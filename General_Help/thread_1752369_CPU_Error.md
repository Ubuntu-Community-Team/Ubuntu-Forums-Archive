### Post by WorMzy on 2011-05-07
From what I can tell, this looks to be a false positive error generated by memtest86. Try memtest86+, and see if the error persists, then it's likely that you have a faulty processor. Contact Intel, or whoever you bought your PC from, and see if you can get it replaced.
