### Post by Olav on 2008-10-29
> **Sightblinder said:**
> 1. i got the blue screen of death thanks to windows. can i restore the computer with an ubuntu install disc?
Do you mean you want to restore Windows, using an Ubuntu disk? Or do you want to wipe Windows off of your computer completely and install Ubuntu instead?

If the latter, I think Xubuntu will do just fine. It has the lightest GUI of the three main variants (Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Xubuntu).

In general your options would increase, and your experience would be better, if you could upgrade your RAM (to say 1 GB).

You should never expect mind blowing performance from a 1 GHz processor with any modern operating system and applications, but you already knew that. However it should not be terribly slow either. Adequate for web browsing, word processing, playing music and a lot of other common tasks.
