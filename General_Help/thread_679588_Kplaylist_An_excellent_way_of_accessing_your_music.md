### Post by gvartser on 2008-01-27
It is not often but once in a while you find that special nice thing that just makes life a little bit easier.

When i stumbeled upon Kplaylist a couple of years ago, i immediately know that this was such a thing.

So what is it:

Its a php driven (*AMP)streaming service where you index and store your entire music collection, and then accessible from anywhere, (Well you need an internet connection).

> 
kPlaylist is a free PHP system that makes your music collection available via the Internet.

kPlaylist is a music database that you manage via the web. With kPlaylist you can stream your music (ogg, mp3, wav, wma, etc.), you can upload, make playlists, share, search, download and a lot more.


I use it everyday, control the music in my house, from work, and even from my car, using my 3g phone.. ;)

Check it out there:

[http://www.kplaylist.net](http://www.kplaylist.net)
