### Post by TheFu on 2020-10-06
Change the resolution?  Look in the display settings. Larger numbers make for smaller fonts, smaller images, smaller text.

I'd use **lxrandr**, but there are lots of options. Google found this: 
 [https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/look-resolution.html.en](https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/look-resolution.html.en)

I'm assuming you are using a desktop, not "server".

### Post by HermanAB on 2020-10-06
The best HiDPI support for bad eyes is on Apple Macintosh.

I tell folks to buy a Mac when they are over 60 and set the Accessibility Preferences up for bad eyes, before their eyes get so bad that they cannot find the controls for it.
