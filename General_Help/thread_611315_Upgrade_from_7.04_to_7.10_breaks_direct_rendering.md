### Post by WebChat006 on 2007-11-17
I fixed the problem by adding 

```
Option "DRI" "true"
```

to the "Device" section and adding

 ```
Load "glx"
```

to the Module section in "/etc/X11/xorg.conf".

Credit to [http://www.nec.fct.unesp.br/marcos_umino/mandriva-radeon-beryl-dual-desktop.html]("http://www.nec.fct.unesp.br/marcos_umino/mandriva-radeon-beryl-dual-desktop.html") .
