### Post by chevy1976 on 2012-10-28
I had the same problem with my HP Mini 311.  I was walked through troubleshooting by HP (the India branch) and they actually fixed the problem.

Its cause was a build up of static in the unit.

The solution is to remove the battery, unplug the power cord (and any other peripherals), and then hold down the power button for 40 seconds.

This dissipates the static.  Next, plug in the battery and the power, and turn on the unit.

It should turn on normally.
