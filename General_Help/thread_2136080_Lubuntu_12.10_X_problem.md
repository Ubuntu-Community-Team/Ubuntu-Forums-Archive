Have you tried removing or renaming /etc/X11/xorg.conf and rebooting.

I had a problem with nvidia-settings and xorg.conf. I was getting an error something along the lines of 'Fatal error: No screens detected' and I wouldn't get past the Splash screen. I renamed xorg.conf to xorg.conf.old rather than completely deleting it (for safety's sake) and rebooted. A new xorg.conf was generated on boot and everything was fine again.
