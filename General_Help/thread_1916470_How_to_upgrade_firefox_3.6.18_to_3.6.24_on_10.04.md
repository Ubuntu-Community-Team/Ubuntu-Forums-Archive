### Post by Karlchen on 2012-01-28
Hello, Johan_D.

Firefox 3.6 is a deadend by now. So allow Synaptic to upgrade to Firefox 9.0.1. I am using Firefox 9.0.1 on Lucid Lynx as well, and it looks (almost) exactly the same as Firefox 3.6 used to.
I.e. on top, there is the complete menu bar.
Below the menu, there is the addressbar. Left of the addressbar there are the icons "Back", "Forward", "Reload", "Home".
Below the addressbar, there is the bookmarks iconbar.
And below this, there are the browser tabs.
You will have to look very closely to find out that this is Firefox 9.0.1 and not 3.6.25, or go to Help => About :wink:

In short words:
Not liking the default interface design which was introduced by Firefox 4.0 is no reason at all to cling to Firefox 3.6.x.

Karl
