### Post by SickTwist on 2005-10-21
If you're asking about sharing files over a [local area network]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Local_area_network") (LAN), like within your house/apartment/dorm/what-have-you, then [Samba]("http://www.samba.org/") is what you should use for serving files to Windows users.

If you want to make your files available over the Internet, that is a different question entirely and the answer will vary depending on what you're trying to accomplish.
