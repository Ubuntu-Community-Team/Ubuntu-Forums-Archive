### Post by misGnomer on 2005-02-01
You could try modifying the Knoppix boot commands suggested in [this earlier message](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=57526&postcount=3).

Let us know if they work, and also specify the affected hardware which could help fix the problem or aid troubleshooting efforts in the future.
