### Post by Zorael on 2008-06-10
There is no way to do USB transfers between linux and an iPhone or an iPod Touch as of yet, so the only way is to jailbreak it and install the OpenSSH server. Then you can transfer your songs via a wireless network to your device. If you don't have a wlan, no luck yet.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone)
