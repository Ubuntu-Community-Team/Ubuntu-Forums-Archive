### Post by clonne4crw on 2009-09-28
I guess you could do a number of things. One is print the webpage as an image, as in a png or something, open that in an image editor like GIMP, and do the editing there, and crop it so it works with your label printer.

I think that would work pretty well for what you're trying to do here, but I would take the shortcut and take a screenshot of what you want to use, and directly paste that into an image editor, crop it, etc.
