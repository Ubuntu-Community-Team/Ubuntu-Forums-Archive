### Post by purecharger on 2011-11-16
I am using Gnome Classic with Ubuntu 11.10 and have an annoying problem:

I can't move the taskbar portion of my gnome panel to the bottom of my 2nd monitor. I have a laptop with a 2nd monitor, and using Alt+left click I had no problem moving the top panel (with time, applications and places menu, etc..) to my 2nd monitor, but I cannot get the taskbar panel to move to the bottom of the 2nd monitor. I use Alt+left click and the hand icon moves to the bottom of the screen but the panel doesn't get dragged. I also used Alt+right click + 'Move' on the taskbar panel, same thing.

(I am calling the "taskbar panel" the place where your apps show up when they're running, and where you can minimize/maximize them to/from)

Any ideas?

### Post by purecharger on 2011-11-18
I was able to solve this by adding new panels until every edge of both monitors had one (the last one to appear was the one I wanted, d'oh). I then added the task switcher to this panel, deleted the old one from the top bar, and deleted all the other panels. Hopefully this works for you!
