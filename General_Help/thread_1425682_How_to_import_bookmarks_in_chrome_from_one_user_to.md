Hello to you.
No problems.
Just synch your bookmarks to chrome with your old account, then [ctrl+shift+B], tools->export bookmarks... Export them to file.
Then change profile, again [ctrl+shift+B], tools-->import bookmarks--> choose file.
Enjoy.
