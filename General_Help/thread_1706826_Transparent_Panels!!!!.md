### Post by Tamlynmac on 2011-03-14
Not quite sure what your asking for. If your trying to make the panel transparent...

Right click on the panel and select "Properties" - from the drop down menu. When the properties window opens select the "Background Tab". Select "Solid color" (if you wish to make it completely transparent what color you select is unimportant) and move the slider all the way to "Transparent" (left).

If your trying to place an image in the panel, select Background image at the bottom of the "Properties" window. Than find the file to display in the panel from the select folder located in the box just below the words "Background image".

Hope this helps.
