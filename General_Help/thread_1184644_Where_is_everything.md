### Post by benj1 on 2009-06-11
go to system-->preference-->appearance-->background then add to add a wallpaper.
system-->preference-->sound to change sound (don't know how to add sounds cousult the help button and google)

your question is quite broad but generally system thing like images are in /usr/share.
but if you want to add things they tend to go in your home folder 
eg ~/.wallpaper or ~/.icon
