### Post by nandemonai on 2009-04-23
Rhythmbox + Easytag

30G of music and never had an issue.

Of course I make sure any music I'm adding to the library has tags and is named correctly before throwing it into the mix.

Why do you have so many untagged files anyway?

Oh and as for Rhythmbox, renaming / moving files around isn't an issue. Just make sure 'watch folders' is on and it'll update.
