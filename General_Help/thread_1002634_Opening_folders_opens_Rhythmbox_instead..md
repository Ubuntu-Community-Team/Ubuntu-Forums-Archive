### Post by Servechilled on 2008-12-05
This is a weird problem I'm having. Whenever I try to open a folder from firefox or any other application, it opens Rhythmbox instead of Dolphin. For example, I've finished downloading something in firefox and right click on the download, click Open Containing Folder, Rhythmbox opens. Same thing happens if I open a folder from a firefox addon such as DownThemAll. Also happens if I open a folder from Transmission BitTorrent client.

Any ideas?

I'm running Kubuntu 8.10, kernel 2.6.27-generic on an AMD Athlon64 X2 5200, 4GB of DDR2-800 RAM.

### Post by john183 on 2008-12-05
Right click on one of your folders and open the properties and check the "Open with" tab. make sure that dolphin is selected as the program to open the folders with.
