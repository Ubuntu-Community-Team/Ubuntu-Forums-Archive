### Post by slumbergod on 2009-11-26
Usually, when I can't connect to my MSN account it turns out to be a temporary issue with the MSN servers. The default MSN setup for pidgin works very well but if in doubt it is always worth trying out the msn-pecan alternative. Some people prefer it; for others it is the only way of connecting to MSN:
