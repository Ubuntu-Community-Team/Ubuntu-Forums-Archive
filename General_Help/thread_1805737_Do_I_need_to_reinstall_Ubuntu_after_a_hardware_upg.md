### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2011-07-16
> **mendhak said:**
> Everyone, thanks for confirming this, I feel much easier now and sit here slightly more impressed with Ubuntu/Linux.

Before Microsoft started trying to prevent piracy of their OS, you didn't have to reinstall Windows either. You just had to install the drivers for the new hardware. There is technically no reason to reinstall Windows; however, they check the hardware in an attempt to make sure you are not installing the same OS license on multiple computers. That has the side effect that changing several hardware components at the same time looks to them like you are installing the license on another computer.
