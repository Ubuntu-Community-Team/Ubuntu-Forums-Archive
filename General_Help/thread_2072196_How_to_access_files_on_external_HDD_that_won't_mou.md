### Post by Grenage on 2012-10-17
Short of moving the platters to another drive (don't bother trying), you're very unlikely to get anywhere.

A 'whirrr click whirr click' means that the arm is probably shagged, or completely misaligned; you could try a sharp jarring, or one of the many other fluke techniques (boiling/freezing/anointed Yak urine/...), but on the off-chance it does start working, it won't be for long.
