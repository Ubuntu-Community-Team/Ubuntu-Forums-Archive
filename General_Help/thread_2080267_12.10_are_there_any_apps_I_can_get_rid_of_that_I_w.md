if you are a newbie,  it would not be advisable to uninstall anything that came pre-installed,  especially when you do not want to save space.  there is no need to clear up app drawer as it would have not much effect on your daily use,    i mean you can enter few letters of any apo to find it out from the dash.   you can keep the most frequently used ones in the side bar launcher by drag n drop or right clicking them when the app is open and showing in the side launcher bar.


still if you want to remove the listed apps, it will have consequences.,  if you remove archive manager, you will not be able to open any archive in nautilus, similarly removing other apps would also affect functionality of other connected apps and may cause some stability issues. if you don't know how to fix dependency issues, it would be better to keep them.
