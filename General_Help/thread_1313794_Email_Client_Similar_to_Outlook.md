### Post by donato roque on 2009-11-04
Sending images inline:
  If you always want your images inline, my advice is to set preferences to send email in html.  Go to Edit->Preferences->Composer Preferences->General tab.  You want to tick Format messages in html. 

  In the compose window, just click Insert (just like in Outlook).  Direct evolution to the image file you want to insert.  

  You can even resize the image if you want. Oh you can also drag and drop the image to the compose window.  

Receiving images automatically:
  If you want evolution to automatically download images and show them right away by default.  Go to Edit->Preferences->Mail Preferences->Html Messages.  You may tick Always Load images from the Internet.  Although I would like to caution you that virus has been known to be delivered through email images.  Prudence is ticking Loading images only from contacts (in your evolution address book).

  You might elect to look at each email and decide if you want to load the image.  Then skip the above step and just hit Control+I.
