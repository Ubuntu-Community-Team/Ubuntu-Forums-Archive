Is there a way of showing the bottom screen tabs side by side rather than a single tab saying (3)Pcmanfm for example - when I am working between several folders it would be easier to know which ones were there - I have been through right click panel settings etc but must have missed the setting I need.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-12-29
Go to **Panel Settings** from a right click, and then **Panel Applets**.

Highlight the **Task Bar (Window List)** and click **Preferences**

Deselect the **"Combine multiple application windows into a single button"** item.
