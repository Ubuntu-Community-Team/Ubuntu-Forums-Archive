### Post by Sef on 2008-10-04
> Avira-(Antivirus)/ OnlineArmor-(firewall)Spybot Search & Destroy/ SuperAntiSpywar

1) Unless you use an email client, no anti-virus is needed.

2) A firewall is included with Ubuntu.  It is called IPTables, which is command line.  If you want a gui for it, then download ufw from Applications > Add/Remove.

3) No spyware dectectors are needed as there is none for GNU/Linux.


> Also Thunderbird, dowloaded but will not install. 	

Go to Applications > Add/Remove and downlaod it from there.
