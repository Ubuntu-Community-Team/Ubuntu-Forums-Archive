### Post by jpkotta on 2006-10-24
If the two computers are identical or very similar, it might boot.  But it probably won't.  Just install the old HD in the new computer, boot from new computer's HD, and mount the old HD and use it.  You can of course copy your data over too, but why bother when you can just use both HDs?
