### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-04-22
Assuming your PC can boot from a CD drive, you would do better going to Kaspersky Labs site, downloading and burning their FREE Rescue CD.

When you launch that, be sure to do a definition update right away.

Kaspersky is one of the BEST Windows AV tools out there.

Also, if you suspect a root kit, while at their site, look for the TDSSKiller download.  Once you do virus clean-up on Windows, you should run that to find and remove rootkits.
