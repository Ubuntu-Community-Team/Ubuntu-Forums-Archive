### Post by davidmohammed on 2011-01-22
btrfs is marked by the developers as experimental - its not feature complete but has been marked as "usable" since around the time of Lucid.

Why do you want to use btrfs for the desktop?

Unless you want to stuff like snapshots - I can't think of any particular advantages over ext4.

Suggest you head over to phoronix where they have lots of articles and benchmarks for btrfs vs ext4.
