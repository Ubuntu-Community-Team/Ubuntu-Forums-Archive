### Post by Hei Ku on 2008-11-08
KMyMoney, as packaged by Ubuntu, has a screen resolution error. Controls are widget are outside the screen, even in large resolution.
This is a problem that happens only in Ubuntu, and perhaps Debian, but not in the original source.
My advice is to install from source or from a PPA provided by a user of KMyMoney, and NOT from the package provided in the repositories.

Here is the link to the PPA:
[https://edge.launchpad.net/~claydoh/+archive](https://edge.launchpad.net/~claydoh/+archive)

Here is the link to the bug submitted in Launchpad:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/kmymoney2/+bug/295715](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/kmymoney2/+bug/295715)

I have enough with errors from my own application. I do not know what you do to the packages, but you are not passing that upstream to confirm the modifications are ok. Please talk to us, we don't bite.
