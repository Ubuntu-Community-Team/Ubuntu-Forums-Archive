### Post by zambiandreams on 2009-04-29
I was having the same problem, but figured it out after reading piratebill's response.  Your iPod is now listed as a 'Collection.' Mine was right above 'Local Collection.' If it doesn't show up immediately, you may have to add the 'Device' applet in that middle pane and then click the green plus. To transfer you then select the playlist, album, artist or whatever and choose 'Copy to iPod.'  

I'm still really mixed on the new Amarok.  A lot of the features seem to go well (I had to spend a few hours getting my iPod to work when I first tried Amarok).  I may just be in transition shock. Hopefully they'll straighten those weird bugs out, like not having shuffle and repeat in the toolbar.
