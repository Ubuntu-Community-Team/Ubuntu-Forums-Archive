### Post by utnu on 2009-04-27
when i click Shut Down, a pop-up window opens and reads "The computer will shut down in 59 seconds."

how can i get rid of that pop-up and delay ?

---

### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-27
Right-click the User Switcher applet, go to Preferences, and uncheck "Show confirmation window".
