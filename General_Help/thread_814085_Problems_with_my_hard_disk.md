### Post by cybrsaylr on 2008-06-02
If you need more room you may want to get an External Drive. Ext HDD are cheap now and just plug into usb 2.0 ports. This way you can move files you want to save to the ext HDD which will give you more room on your 80GB HDD. Plus this is a way to backup important files you want to keep. Drives usually run better when are not crammed full.
