### Post by michy99 on 2009-07-03

---

### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-07-03
You are right.  You can run, and many do, a 32 bit OS in 64 bit hardware, but not the other way round, and the codecs need to be right for your OS.

I presume you are meaning the libdvdcss2 for DVD playback, and if so enable the medibuntu repos which will allow you to use apt-get or synaptic to install it  Look here:-
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)
