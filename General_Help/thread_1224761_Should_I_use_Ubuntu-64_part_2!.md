### Post by t4thfavor on 2009-07-27
Absolutely, I have few issues, if any with all of my 64 bit installs. I have about 40 different machines under my control with 64 bit (both Windows, and Linux/Ubuntu), and I rarely see an issue. Its been about 3 months since I have had an x64 issue, and it was with an old multifunction printer from Toshiba.

Its worth noting that you really only get a bit of performance gain, unless you have more than 3GB ram.

After that its a ton more.
