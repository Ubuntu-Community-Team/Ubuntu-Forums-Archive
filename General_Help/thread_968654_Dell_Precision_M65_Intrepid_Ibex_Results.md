### Post by Seanuntu on 2008-11-02
Creating this thread to record some of the bugs I am noticing on my Dell Precision M65 laptop w/ Intrepid Ibex.  If anyone else is experiencing similar symptoms, or knows of any workarounds, please let me know.  Some of these are minor and are definitely not show-stoppers.

1. Sometimes the keyboard stops working.  Sometimes logging out and then logging back in fixes the problem.  Sometimes it may take a reboot or even a couple of reboots to fix.

2. Mouse clicks stop responding.  The mouse cursor moves, and it causes "tooltips" to activate on hover, but clicking (left or right), doesn't respond.  Logging out and logging back in usually fixes it.

3. System --> Preferences --> Sound --> Sounds (Tab) --> Pushing the little triangle play icon doesn't play the sound.

4.  Clicking the "Calendars" button in evolution causes evolution to crash, everytime, guaranteed.  I can see the calendar load for a split second and then evolution crashes.

5.  I can't get Vmware Server to install.  I had it working fine within Hardy.  VirtualBox on the other hand, installed fine.  I am currently using it to run a Windows virtual machine for when I need Outlook 2007 and Evernote.  I use evolution for my exchange/corporate email via evolution most of the time for basic email access.

I will add more bugs as I find them.  I will update with any workaround/fixes that I find.

Thanks,
Sean
