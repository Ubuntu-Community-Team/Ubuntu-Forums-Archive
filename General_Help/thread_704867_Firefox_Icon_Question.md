### Post by nabl on 2008-02-22
I recently used [Tango Generator]("http://mejogid.ohallwebservices.com/site/") to make a custom icon theme (it's beautiful). However, I have come into a small problem with it... The Firefox icon has changed all over the system, but when I open the application, the icon on the taskbar and the top left corner of the window are still the old ones. I have replaced all the icons in both /usr/share/pixmaps and /usr/share/firefox/icons with the appropriately sized and named alternatives, but that didn't fix it.

What should I do next?

**[b]Update: Just in case you're seeing this after a search and are having the same problem, I did find the solution eventually. ;) Firefox has the icon that is used by the panel/window manager stored in /usr/share/firefox/chrome/icons/default. Just replace that with the desired icon, and you'll be good to go.**[/B]
