### Post by wojox on 2009-10-05
Go to System > Admin > Login Window > Accessibility Sounds

---

### Post by Iowan on 2009-10-05
Check under System>Preferences>Sound>Sounds The chorus seems to be the default setting for Login. Double-click Default to change.
