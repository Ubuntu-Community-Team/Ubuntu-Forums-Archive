### Post by diskotek on 2008-03-01
> Epiphany does not allow you to configure through the menus because it's Gnome, and Gnome is made (supposedly) very "user-friendly" by removing all options you may not understand if you are dump-as-a-cow. However, you can enter about:config in the navigation bar, type proxy in the filter box and change the proxy.http.host and proxy.http.port fields (as well as proxy.ftp, proxy.ssl etc).

[http://en.linuxreviews.org/HOWTO_use_the_Internet_anonymously_using_Tor_and_Privoxy](http://en.linuxreviews.org/HOWTO_use_the_Internet_anonymously_using_Tor_and_Privoxy)
