Whenever i log on, the text editor and the auto starting apps dialogue from the settings manager open by themselves. How do i stop this from happening?

---

### Post by Altay_H on 2008-10-19
Go to:
System > Preferences > Sessions > Startup Programs

If they're listed there, uncheck them.


EDIT: The location above is for ubuntu. I'm not entirely sure where Sessions is in xubuntu.
