### Post by heavylifting on 2010-06-30
Ok this is my first time posting on the forums.

However, I have just installed Ubuntu latest version and installed UT2004 on it. After what has now been days of searching for a resolution for my jerky mouse action that I experience when I play it. I finally found the answer on my own while searching through the mouse settings. I am on a laptop, and I noticed that under System>Preferences>Mouse on the Touchpad tab the checkbox was checked for Disable touchpad while typing. And since you use the arrow keys while playing that is counted as typing so you couldn't look around with your weapon and use the touchpad at the same time. Wanted to post this because I searched for a resolution for hours.
