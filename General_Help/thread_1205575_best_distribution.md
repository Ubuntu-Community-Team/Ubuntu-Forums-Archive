### Post by t0p on 2009-07-06
> **asliyanage said:**
> what is the best linux  distribution for home users?

There is no "correct" answer to this question.  "Best" in this context is an objective concept: one user might like Ubuntu, another might prefer Fedora, and they're both "right".  Only *you* can decide what's best for *you*.

The thing to do is, download a bunch of popular distros and see how you get on with them.  Off the top of my head I'd say try Ubuntu, Fedora, Suse... there are plenty of others, check [distrowatch]("http://distrowatch.com/") for ideas.  Spend a few weeks checking them out.  Then decide which is best for you.

Personally, I'd say Ubuntu is best.  But that's just me.  YMMV.
