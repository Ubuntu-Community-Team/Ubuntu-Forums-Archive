### Post by rimshot4 on 2010-01-25
I've triple-checked that Evolution is not in Preferences-Startup, and that "remember applications on boot" is NOT checked, and that I don't shut down with Evolution open. BUt it still pops up full-screen on. every. boot. 

This only happens on one of my two Karmic machines. I suppose I could reinstall, but I've just got it tweaked to the way I like it. But it's driving me nuts. 

Suggestions?

---

### Post by rimshot4 on 2010-02-01
I found the solution in another thread. You have to go to preferences--startup applications--click the "other options" tab--check the "Automatically remember applications..." box, make sure you have no applications running, then reboot. After rebooting uncheck "Automatically remember" tab. Worked like a charm.
