### Post by no2498 on 2011-10-28
look in your system/preferences startup programs
make sure skype and cheese is not set to auto startup
if ether 1 is on turn them off and restart the computer
come back here 
if you need to click the fn+f4 keys do it now to turn the cam on
open a terminal type  dmesg click enter
after it stops type lsusb click enter
close the terminal
install guvcview
now try the cam in guvcview
