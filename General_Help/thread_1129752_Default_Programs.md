### Post by Jimmynemo2 on 2009-04-19
To save you a link as well. Pick any file- right click, properties, then choose "open with" tab, and put the dot onto whatever you want the default program to be for opening that type of file.

Also, if you want to remove items from your open with context menu, this is where you add/remove items from your right click context menu.

Hope this helps...
