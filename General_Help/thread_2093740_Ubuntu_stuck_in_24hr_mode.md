### Post by Butthead on 2012-12-11
What, are you trying to display a 12 hour (am/pm?) clock here? :confused:

If so, click on the main menu, go over to applications sub menu, choose settings menu under that, then choose system settings under that, then click on the "locale" icon.  Under that is a "date and time" tab where you can set how the clock displays the time.
