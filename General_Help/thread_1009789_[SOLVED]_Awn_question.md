### Post by blackened on 2008-12-13
Honestly I've not used AWN since 2006 or so, so I couldn't tell you the exact command, but I can tell you the easy way to find out.

Right click on the main menu (Applications Places System) and select "Edit Menus". Find the menu entry for AWN and press the "Properties" button on the right. In the subsequent dialog, look at the line labelled "Command" and use that as the command in the Sessions dialog.
