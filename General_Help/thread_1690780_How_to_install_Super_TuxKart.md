**How DO I Install Super Tuxkart?**
**Answer:**
Go to the Ubuntu Software Center. Search "Supertuxkart" Select it and click "Install"
**How Do I Play Supertuxkart?**
To go forward, press and hold the up arrow button.
To go left, hold the left arrow button.
To go right, hold the right arrow button.
To stop, hold the down arrow button.
To go really fast and bleed off some nitro, press "l"
To make a sharp turn, press "k"
