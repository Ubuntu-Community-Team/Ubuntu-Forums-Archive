### Post by Copperline on 2011-04-03
OK - solved this one and really quite embarrassingly easily as well!

I'll put this in case anyone else has this issue:-

When I booted into KDE for the first time, what must have been displayed on the bottom of the screen was an 'empty panel'. This had the clock on it and I assumed it functioned as a taskbar.

However...removing this panel (right-click on panel > Remove This Panel) and then adding the Default Panel (right-click on Desktop > Add Panel > Default Panel) to the desktop gave me exactly what I was looking for...with all the programs I'd loaded and lost there waiting for me...plus widgets and the application launcher button!

Feel slightly silly now but at least I've learned something and I'm very grateful for your help! I think I was thinking far too deeply into it - and missed the obvious!
