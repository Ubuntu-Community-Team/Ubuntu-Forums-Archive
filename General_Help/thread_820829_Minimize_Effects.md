### Post by Camuso21 on 2008-06-06
Ok for the minimization effects you need to go to apply the animations plugin in Compiz, then go into the animation options where its pretty obvious what to do. (Note: by choosing to apply the animation plugin it will require you to unapply the minimize effect plugin, this is correct, the animation plugin allows for much more customization.)

For the annoying habit of the cube rotating when you scroll your mouse, that is probably due to you having the viewport switcher plugin enabled. By disabling this plugin that shouldn't happen anymore. Hope this helps, good luck.
