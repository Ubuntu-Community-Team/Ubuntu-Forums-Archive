### Post by linux_tech on 2008-12-20
Most drives are compatible.  If you format, be sure to choose a filesystem that is compatible.  A few sites to check linux compatibily are listed below.  You can search them for a particular drive.

[http://www.linuxquestions.org/hcl/index.php/cat/197](http://www.linuxquestions.org/hcl/index.php/cat/197)
[http://www.linux.org/hardware/](http://www.linux.org/hardware/)
