### Post by Menschenfresser on 2008-09-04
What do you mean the default? Do you want some other application to open when you insert a USB with pictures?

If yes, go to system > preferences > "prefered applications" and "removable drives and media". (yes, it's a bit unlogical that there are settings in two places)

However I am not sure if picasa will appear in the list. If it doesn't I guess there will be some Gconf tweaking necessary
