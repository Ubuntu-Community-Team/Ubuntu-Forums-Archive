### Post by rahul.raj on 2010-11-14
[Maverick Meerkat - 10.10, GIMP - 2.6.11-1]
Had also come looking for a "Solved" item and felt a bit disappointed - however, the advice on housekeeping turned out to be extremely pertinent, guess it does deserve the 'Solved' status in a roundabout way :)

Here's what I did, in case it helps the next ubuntu fan:
1. Disabled getdebs (lucid) repository (getdebs-maverick is still active)
2. Uninstalled libpoppler related items (This uninstalls a lot of items including okular and a lot of python packages - I saved the markings using Synaptics 'Save Markings' option)
3. Removed gimp residual files
4. Update and Dist-Upgrade

And voila, gimp installs just fine. Reinstalled all files.
