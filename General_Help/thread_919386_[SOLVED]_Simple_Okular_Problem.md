### Post by raul_ on 2008-09-23
That wasn't a very smart solution, because in Linux the configuration files remain there even if you remove the program (typically the hidden folders in your home directory)

If you want the TOOLbar back, just click on the Menu->Configuration->Show toolbar

If you want the MENU back, I'm not sure, but you could try Ctrl+M
