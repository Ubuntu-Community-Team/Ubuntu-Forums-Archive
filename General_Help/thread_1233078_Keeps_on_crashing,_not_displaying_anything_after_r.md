### Post by P4man on 2009-08-06
perhaps you can be a bit more explicit? When you say it won't start anymore, what happens?

You won't hear me saying ubuntu is flawless, but when you're having such severe problems, I would suspect a hardware problem. Try running the memory test presented by grub bootloader overnight and/or run a diagnostic program on your harddisk ("ultimate bootcd" is your friend here, google for it).

No one will prevent you from trying out other distro's, but if your harddisk is dying or you have bad RAM, then that won't help much.
