### Post by sayakb on 2008-08-16
First of all, if you keep more than 1 row, they won't appear on the same cube simultaneously. 
Secondly, the top and bottom sides of the cube are always empty. You may add an image to them though. If you want that, here's what you can do:
[B]
Compiz 0.7.5 or higher:
[/B]Press Alt+F2 and type in **ccsm**. In the settings manager window, enable and click on **Cube reflection and deformation** plugin and expand appearance. You may change the top and bottom images.

[B]Older versions of Compiz:
[/B]Press Alt+F2 and type in **ccsm**. Enable and click on **Cube Caps** plugin and change the image from there.
