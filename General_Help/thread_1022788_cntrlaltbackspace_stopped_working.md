### Post by itix on 2008-12-29
The Ctrl + Alt + Backspace restarts the x-server (the graphical elements of your computer). It is usefull if your x-session has frozen. Even if your computer hangs, it is still useful. You can find terminals at Ctrl + F1-6 and the x-server at Ctrl + F7 and F8. The x-server that the computer starts with is located at F7.

Is this firestarter firewall something that you have installed yourself?
