> 1. Evolution is with me by default in the machine as it comes with OS installed. Can you guys guide me in choosing between Thunderbird and Evolution. I also happen to read in this forum that Evolution is giving problem than thunderbird.

I've tried both, and I much prefer Thunderbird... However, it's a personal thing :-)

If you don't really need calendar/scheduling I'd recommend Thunderbird straightaway - Lightning is (may be?) available as a plugin to provide basic calendar functionality.

> 2. If Thunderbird is best (hope i can get it installed thru synaptic manager), can i configure thunderbird mail client to my existing gmail account. If it is yes, please help me how to get this done.

Sure can - click on the "Settings" link in GMail, then take a look at the "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab - links are available there to the configuration instructions for various email clients...

...interestingly enough, Evolution's not mentioned - what does that tell you? ;-)
