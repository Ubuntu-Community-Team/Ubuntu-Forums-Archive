### Post by guiverc on 2021-01-20
If you have a spare mouse, I'd suggest adding it to your box and seeing if it's different with the second mouse.

I've been having issues with mice in recent months, having had 3 give problems before they got worse and two stopped working completelyl. My mice are early *HP* or *DELL* LED mice, so I've assumed it's they've reached the end of their reliable life (I don't know how old they are; this box is from 2009, but the mouse/keyboard/screens I'm using were used on the box before that too).  I've also wondered if my old boxes PSU's are getting old & insufficient power is being provided at times (causing mice to misbehave).

My conclusions are I'm having hardware issues anyway with mine (could also be it's just me).

 To validate the mice operation, I boot into a *live* system, which I do quite often as I assist with QA-testing of Ubuntu & *flavors*, and at least in my case, the strange behavior doesn't just occur on my installed system, but whatever system I boot and use.  My systems are also dual-boot (this box has *hirsute* I'm using now, but also has *bionic*) so they act strangely on multiple installed systems.
