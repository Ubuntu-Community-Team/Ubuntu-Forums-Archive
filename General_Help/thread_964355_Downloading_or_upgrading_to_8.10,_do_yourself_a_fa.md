.... and don't use the "standard" repositories - they will be getting hammered at the moment.

If you use these instead of the overloaded sources that most people will now be using, you will get your download/upgrade done quicker and also reduce some of the load on the other sources.

Not only will you personally benefit, but others still using the standard sources will also benefit!
