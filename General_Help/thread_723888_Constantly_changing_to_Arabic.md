### Post by phissure on 2008-03-14
When it changes, there should be a red-letter icon in the system tray.  Open the options from clicking on the icon.  There should be a tab/tree that includes a "Keyboard Shortcuts" list.
Clear it and make sure you restart all the SCIM (The program that's annoying you) processes.  Or you can restart your computer afterward.
I've had that same problem and found it VERY annoying.  My friends did NOT like me IMing them in arabic, with me helplessly typing away. :)
Good luck.
