### Post by llama320 on 2008-05-20
What I did to fix this was to go to System > Preferences > Sound and set all "Sound Playback" options to PulseAudio. Then I reinstalled flash-nonfree. 

I also set my amarok audio settings to PulseAudio (under Settings > Configure Amarok > Engine). I just checked and it doesn't look like Rhythmbox has any of those settings. If you still can't get music playing along with flash, you might want to try a music player that has those options..I know amarok has them but if you don't want KDE dependencies Exaile is a comparable player that (I think) has them.

Good Luck
