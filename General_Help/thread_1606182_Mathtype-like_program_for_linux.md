### Post by 3Miro on 2010-10-26
If you type a lot of math formulas, then you should be using LaTeX. It is the standard for all Math journals and papers, most people also make their presentations on it. Initially it is much harder, however, once you get used to it, you cannot use anything else. LaTeX is especially powerful for large documents, I could have never written my 100 page dissertation in anything other than LaTeX.

If all that you want is something small that you can use every now and then, go for OpenOffice.org Math.
