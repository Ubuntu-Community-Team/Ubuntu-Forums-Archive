### Post by 2F4U on 2012-08-25
What are the details of the problem? I got such a problem report  everytime I booted my MacBook and the report said that it detected a GPU  hang. As in your case, I couldn't find any real problem and everything  was working as expected. After doing quite some reading on the web, my  conclusion is that such problems happen quite often, but are not  necessarily an indication of a real problem.
Other than what the Wiki says, Apport was enabled by default on my system:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Apport#How_to_enable_apport](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Apport#How_to_enable_apport)

and I reversed the steps in the Wiki to disable it. It may also be save  to completely remove it from the system, but I think this is not  necessary. Disabling Apport should be sufficient.
