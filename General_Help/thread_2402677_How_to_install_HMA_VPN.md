### Post by pearlyjam on 2018-10-09
I would strongly recommend against HMA mostly because of this [https://invisibler.com/lulzsec-and-hidemyass/](https://invisibler.com/lulzsec-and-hidemyass/). Even if you don't do anything illegal, sharing users' information with the authorities is a very bad sign. Who knows, maybe they keep and will sell your data to 3rd parties. Get something like NordVPN, PIA and such which are considered more trustworthy. They even have Linux clients.
