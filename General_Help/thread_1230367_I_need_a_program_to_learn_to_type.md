### Post by jocampo on 2009-08-03
Here we go:

[http://linuxreviews.org/beginner/learn-touch/]("http://linuxreviews.org/beginner/learn-touch/")

it's called Ktouch. But believe or not, my brother and I learned how to type fast in highschool with a teacher who forced us to cover the letters (keyboard) with small pieces of tape. Yeah, sounds stupid but after couple of weeks, you won't see the keyboard while typing. Not useful if that's your only PC, but get a spare keyboard and cover the letters and switch back and forth with a normal one during the process.

Good luck! :-)
