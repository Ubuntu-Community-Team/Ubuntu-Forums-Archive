### Post by PaulFr on 2007-06-16
Not really, since Linux file systems do not have the same fragmentation issues that Windows/DOS have (see [http://www.linux.com/howtos/Partition/appendix.shtml#fragmentation](http://www.linux.com/howtos/Partition/appendix.shtml#fragmentation) for details). However, if you plan to play video from this disk, you should consider a separate partition for your video files (see [http://www.mythtv.org/docs/mythtv-HOWTO-24.html#ss24.2](http://www.mythtv.org/docs/mythtv-HOWTO-24.html#ss24.2) for some pointers). Hope this helps.
