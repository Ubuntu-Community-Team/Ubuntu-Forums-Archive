### Post by HydroMX on 2011-09-06
I had the same problem.  Then I started searching the internet and came across your problem.  Then I read, you may have uninstalled something and that may have done it.  Well I just put a fresh install of Natty 11.04.  When I was done I removed some things I didn't need.  Somehow attached to something I removed, I guess I agreed to remove Ubuntu-Desktop (1.220).  I went back into Ubuntu Software center and typed in Ubuntu-Desktop and re-installed it.  That is what fixed the problem.  You will also end up re-installing the items you may have removed before, but if you decide to uninstall, look out for Ubuntu-Desktop and don't make the mistake again.  I hope this helps you or anyone else with the same problem.
