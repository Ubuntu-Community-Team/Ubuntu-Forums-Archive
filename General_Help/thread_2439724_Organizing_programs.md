### Post by TheFu on 2020-03-31
Any ideas?   Perhaps thinking a little different could be more efficient?
Use keyboard chords to launch programs directly. No dock needed.  super-c ... launches a calculator.  super-1 launches firefox, super-2 launches thunderbird.  super-3 launches keepassxc.  .... you get the idea.  super-t launches my complete set of xterms and logins to the main other systems used.  The terminals are laid out in exactly the way i prefer.

Of course, that won't work for everyone.  Just something to consider.
