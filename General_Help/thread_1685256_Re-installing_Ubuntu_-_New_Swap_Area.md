### Post by perspectoff on 2011-02-10
Yes, you can use the old one. Re-format it or Leave it alone (i.e. don't re-format it) -- it makes no difference. 

Every Linux OS on your computer can use the same swap area, in fact.

There is generally no need to make your swap area greater than 2 Gb, btw.
