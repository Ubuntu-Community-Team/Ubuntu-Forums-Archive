### Post by peppe_a82 on 2008-01-29
I think that an easy way to do this is: first to make an account that supports POP3 or IMAP (like GMail) and then use a mail client (like Thunderbird) to download all the messages to your pc. Once all the e-mails are downloaded, backup all your e-mails to a dvd.
I don't remember very well but Hotmail doesn't support POP3 or IMAP mail retrieval.
