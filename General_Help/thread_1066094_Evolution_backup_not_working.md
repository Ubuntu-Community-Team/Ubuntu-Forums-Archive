### Post by howefield on 2009-02-10
Can you open the backup file and browse it ? 

If you can, you will be able to print out your important email at least.

For example, double click the backup file and it should load into file roller, double click the evolution folder, then mail > imap > account folder > folders > inbox and from there you may be able to double click each mail to load in your default text editor.

You may have a pop folder instead of imap but you get the idea ?

Sorry can't give you pointers on how to fix the issue, evolution backup/restore has never failed me but hopefully you will still have access to your mail.
