### Post by varunus on 2004-12-30
Try using non-spatial nautilus.  This is a lot more like konqueror.  Open up any nautilus window, choose edit-->preferences, go to the behavior tab, and choose "always open in browser windows".  Nautilus will now act just like konqueror.
