### Post by karan.batra on 2008-03-02
The problem might be a permission one. Just type "sudo nautilus ~" in the terminal. Then when the home folder opens, press Ctrl+H to see hidden files. Delete the folders named ".adobe" and ".macromedia". Thats it. Now sound in youtube will work fine.
