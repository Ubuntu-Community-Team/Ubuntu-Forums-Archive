### Post by clickelliott on 2008-02-12
(xmms menu) Options, Preferences, Audio I/O Plug-ins:

DISABLE the WMA plug-in (if you have it),
ENABLE the mplayer plug-in (if you don't have it, get it.)

This will enable XMMS to play WMA files with no problem.
