### Post by hal8000 on 2009-11-08
Yes there is and its a setting in BIOS so not OS related.

Look for a section in your BIOS called "wake on Power" or similar.

In my BIOS I have the options wake on mouse, wake on keyboard, wake on
power button.

Wake on keyboard allows the system to power up from a softkey on my BTC9000 keyboard (no software needed as its a BIOS routine).

If you need further help, either post motherboard and BIOS make/model.
