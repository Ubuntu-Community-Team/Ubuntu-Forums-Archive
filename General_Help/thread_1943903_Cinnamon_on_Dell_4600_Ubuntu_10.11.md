I installed cinnamon 1.4 from the PPA in Ubuntu 11.10 on my Lenovo Thinkpad T60 supporting Unity 3D.  Cinnamon loads and seems to work OK. :D My conclusion is "the ability to support Unity 3D (a suitable video card) is necessary to run cinnamon 1.4 from the PPA."

It could also be the difference in processors, a P4 on my Dell 4600 and a dual core on my T60, but this seems unlikely.
