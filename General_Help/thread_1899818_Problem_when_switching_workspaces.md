### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-12-24
What you are seeing is a feature. If you grab the edge of a window to resize it, the orange pane will appear and reduce in size as you move the mouse. Its purpose is to show the actually new size that you are selecting.

If this happens by accident, as it sometimes does to me, try clicking the window's top panel. The orange panel should fade away.

Regards.

### Post by stinkeye on 2011-12-24
It's actually the grid plugin and can be disabled in
compizconfig-settings-manager > window management > grid.
It will resize a window to half the screen when the titlebar is grabbed and moved to the left or right.
