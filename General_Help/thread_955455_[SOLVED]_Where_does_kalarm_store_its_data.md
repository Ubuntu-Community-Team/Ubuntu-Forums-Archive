### Post by editrix on 2008-10-25
I don't use kalarm as a standalone app, but rather as part of my calendar in Korganizer.

That being said, my calendar is located in /home/username/.kde/share/apps/korganizer

The file itself is std.ics

And when I click on it, it opens as my regular calendar, with all my appointments and alarms in place.

Hope this helps. If not, as least I bumped the thread.
