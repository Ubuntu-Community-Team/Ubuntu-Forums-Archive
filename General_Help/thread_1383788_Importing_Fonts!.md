### Post by RINKO on 2010-01-17
If your having trouble importing fonts its simple


home folder: show hidden files

find the .font file... if there isnt one, create one.
drag and drop what you want to import from the archive mounter if they are zip files, or just drag and drop if they are not compressed.

should work system wide.
good luck
