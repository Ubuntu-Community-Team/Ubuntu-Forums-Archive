### Post by audiomick on 2009-12-06
I am using a CLP 300, which is closely related to the 315, I think.

It first belonged to a friend who sold it to me because she wasn't happy with the quality for her job applications. It is fine for me, as my stuff is nearly all black and white.

I am using the drivers that Ubuntu supplies (foomatic/foo2qpdl), after trying to install the Samsung Linux drivers on my friends machine and failing dismally.

The quality isn't as good as what I get when printing from my Vista laptop, but is good enough for me. I don't know if there is a better option for Linux now. I got the printer 18 months ago or so, and haven't looked lately.

When I was trying to install it for my friend, I found quite a lot of good info in the net by searching the model name of the printer.
