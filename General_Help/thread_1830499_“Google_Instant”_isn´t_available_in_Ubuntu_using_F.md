### Post by AlphaLexman on 2011-08-21
You must have a cookie on your machine, from the google instant help page...
> **Can I opt out?**

 If you don't want to see results as you type, you can opt out by accessing your [search settings]("http://www.google.com/preferences") under the [IMG]http://www.google.com/help/hc/images/accounts_icon_gear_padded.gif[/IMG]  gear icon on any search results page. When you opt out, we'll save that  preference on a cookie, so you'll stayed opted out until the cookie is  cleared. Note that the preference will only apply to that particular  computer and Internet browser, and it is not tied to your Google  Account. Try deleting your cookies and re-starting firefox.
