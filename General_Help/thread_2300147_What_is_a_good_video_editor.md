### Post by NikTh on 2015-10-24
For simple tasks I would suggest **Openshot** (it is available through Software Center) 
For more advanced tasks **Kdenlive** in a very good video editor.

Also, some users prefer **[Blender]("https://www.blender.org/")** (the 3D animation maker) and using it as a video editor. Plenty of tutorials are exist. 

Last, I have installed and tested **[LightWorks]("https://www.lwks.com/")** and it's also quite good, for simple tasks (in Free edition).
