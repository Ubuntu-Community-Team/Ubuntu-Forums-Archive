### Post by Locomorto on 2007-10-12
You will probably want to avoid usb adaptors as my understanding is that these do generally work as well. I did a quick bit of research and the PCMCIA (the port on the side of most laptops) Netgear WG511T seems like a safe choice. The WG511 is cheaper, but the v2 sometimes have an unsupported Marvell chipset. If you can find a v1 (if you look on the netgear site it should be show how to tell them apart), then that one should work great as well. Whatever you do, make sure you check on the internet for any problems people had with that model and ensure that you are getting a revision that has a supported chipset because they often change it.
