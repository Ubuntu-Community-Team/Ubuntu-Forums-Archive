### Post by mgmiller on 2006-12-05
I don't know about the driver, but I have quieted loud video cards by substituting heatpipe passive coolers for fans for the last few years on both nvidia and ati cards that I use for UT2004 without any overheat problems as long as the case is well ventilated.  I even run mild overclocks on the cards without trouble.  Look around on the internet, there are a lot of different brands of these types of coolers and you can read user reviews of them to judge suitability for your card.
