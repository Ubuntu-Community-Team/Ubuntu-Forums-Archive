### Post by Naggobot on 2011-02-28
Waiting ...

(You know the old joke WWW = Wait Wait Wait)

I do not know what the Firefox is waiting but it can be annoying. 

I have too laptops and the older one tends to "freeze" temporarily. Actually other applications seem to be affected by this also but I just use them a lot less. 

Graying out is Gnomes way of telling that the window is busy and not responding right now. 

There is a possibility to adjust the delay before the window grays out but I found that it is actually more annoying having a window that just does not respond and does not tell it at all. 

I have tried looking in to the System monitor to find the cause of this freeze and I have not found any processes that would be busy. Evidently something deeper in the system is not responding as it should. For me the behavior started with 10.04. My Firefox is 3.6.13. Typically my Firefox freezes some while (seconds, minutes) after first starting the computer+fox. I generally use only short periods so it is difficult to tell if it repeats.
