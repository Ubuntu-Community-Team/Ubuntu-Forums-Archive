### Post by sloggerkhan on 2008-08-03
Sounds fine to me. Interesting graphics card choice.
My only comment is that an ATI card might give you better video playback.
(My basis for this is that my ATI x700 mobility 128mb can do noticeably better video scaling than my nVidia 8600gt 256mb card. Using an opengl overlay, the scaling is flawless with the x700 but uses a lot of CPU. With xv overlay, it's still pretty good and slightly better than the nVidia 8600gt's video overlay. The key thing to note is that until DRI 2, compiz will have to be disabled to use the opengl overlay for video with an ATI card.)
