### Post by cmost on 2007-05-14
Thankfully, for the moment, spyware and adware are practically non-existent on Linux / *NIX platforms.  There are a dearth of viruses for *NIX systems as well but they do exist.  As Linux's popularity increases, however, (which can be anticipated as big OEM's like Dell begin to offer pre-installed Linux systems) the crapware is bound to start migrating over from Windows.  Fortunately, *NIX systems are designed for security from the ground up and it's unlikely that any real damage beyond a user's home folder will be wrought by any future Linux spyware / adware.
