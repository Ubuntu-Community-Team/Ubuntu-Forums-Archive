### Post by Sky Pixie on 2009-07-07
Has the video card been recently tested in another computer?  Perhaps it's defective.

If I remember correctly, a quality 300 W power supply with 15+ A on the 12V rail is needed to run the card.  The computer won't boot without enough power.  Power supplies degrade overtime, therefore a higher rated power supply is necessary if the power supply is greater than, say, 3 years.

The maximum power and current output is printed on a sticker attached to the power supply.
