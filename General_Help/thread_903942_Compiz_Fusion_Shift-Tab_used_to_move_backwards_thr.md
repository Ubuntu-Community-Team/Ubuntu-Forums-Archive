### Post by Giggity on 2008-08-28
I installed Compiz Fusion and Emerald as per a desktop customization tutorial, and everything looks really cool, but I lost the ability to move backwards through form fields by hitting "Shift-Tab."  Now that command switches between applications the way Alt-Tab used to, only now it's nifty and animated.

I cannot find the setting anywhere that causes this, and so I do not know how to fix it.  Any ideas?  Thanks.

---

### Post by mssever on 2008-08-28
Install compizconfig-settings-manager. Then you can look through the settings and configure things like you want.

---

### Post by Giggity on 2008-08-30
Thanks.  I had already installed that program, but I hadn't thought of double-clicking on the different actions to change the key bindings.

Under "Window Management" in CompizConfig Settings Manager, I double-clicked "Ring Switcher," and changed the key binding for "Next Window."  Now things are back to the way I like them.

Consider my issue resolved :)
