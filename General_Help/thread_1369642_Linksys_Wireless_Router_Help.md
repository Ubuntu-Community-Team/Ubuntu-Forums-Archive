### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-01-01
Wired or wireless?

Either way, if you have lost all connectivity, both for XP and ubuntu, it should be easy enough to reset the router with the web-browser in either XP or ubuntu by going to [http://192.168.0.1](http://192.168.0.1) or something similar, and logging in under the router name and password.
If you originally changed them, you will need to know what they are, otherwise they will be noted in the router's manual (on my Netgear thay were "admin" and "password").  If you can't remember there may be a "restore factory settings" button, or something so you can start again.

If it is just wireless in ubuntu that has gone, ity is most likely the adapter that is the problem and not the router, so you will need to know what adapter you have and then search out linux drivers for it.
