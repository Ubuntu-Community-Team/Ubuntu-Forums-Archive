### Post by Nepherte on 2009-04-23
If you unplug your external hd, you can still be able to use your OS on the internal hd. It all depends on what hd grub is installed. If grub is installed on your internal disk, it should work rightaway. If grub is installed on your external disk, you will have to install it on the internal one as well.
