### Post by RRS on 2006-07-04
Don't open or extract the download, simply burn the iso image to a cd. Recommend 4x, not faster then 8x.

Also before installing it's a good idea to backup any data you don't want to risk and fully defrag your windows install.

[Here's]("http://psychocats.net/ubuntu/windowstoubuntu.html") a good guide to the full process.
