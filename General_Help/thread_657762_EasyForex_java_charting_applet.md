### Post by seamouse on 2008-01-04
I'm still after a solution to the initial post, but I've got a workaround for people in a similar position to myself.

EasyForex licenses their charting software from a company , NetDania ([http://www.netdania.com/](http://www.netdania.com/)) if you go to their website directly and goto 'Products > ChartStation > Try Demo Now'  you get basically the same software (possibly a newer version, or at least you get more options available), the direct link is [http://www.netdania.com/ChartStationDemo.asp](http://www.netdania.com/ChartStationDemo.asp).

As an added bonus you don't have to login to EasyForex if you just want to see how a trend is progressing.

Please post if anyone of you have found better Forex charting programs that Linux/Ubuntu support.
