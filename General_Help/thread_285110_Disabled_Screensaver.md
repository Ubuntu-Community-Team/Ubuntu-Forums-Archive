### Post by autocrosser on 2006-10-30
I was having problems with Gnome-screensaver & got tired of it (power-manager problems)--so I went to Xscreensaver & shut down Gnome-Screensaver & Gnome-Power-Manager--I don't have problems now---23 Meg has a guide for Dapper & it works very well in Edgy--I also went to sessions & disabled Gnome-Power-Manager--If you want to keep GS--I'd try to turn GPM to Sleep>never & see what happens.

Personally--I think Gnome never should have gone from Xscreensaver--GS in my opinion is still beta software.
