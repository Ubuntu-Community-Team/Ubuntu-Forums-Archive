### Post by anotherpunk on 2007-05-30
I have two rather small pet peeves with Firefox in Feisty Fawn at the moment. The first is that the url bar doesn't automatically highlight everything with one click like in Windows and that when I open a page in a new tab, it automatically switches straight to the newly opened page.

Is there anything that can be done? Thanks.:KS

---

### Post by merlinus on 2007-05-30
Edit/Preferences/Tabs --

Make sure "When I open a link in a new tab, switch to it immediately" is unchecked.

If you double-click the url in the address bar will be hilighted.

-merlin

There is an about:config entry called browser.urlbar.clickSelectsAll. Change this to true.
