### Post by t4thfavor on 2010-02-07
I have a securespot, which is subscription based. It works great, except for the subscription. Also I have used ipcop +Dansguardian which is typically used for larger installs like schools, and whatnot.

If there are very specific things you want to block, the WRT54GL can do it with custom firmware as it has a keyword blocking feature.

If your looking for cheap and easy, the securespot is 49.95 a year, and about 50USD new, but blocks everything you could ever want.

It's also easy to defeat as all you have to do is unplug it...
