### Post by Hairy_Palms on 2006-09-03
i figured out what was wrong, after much searching, the problem was that /usr/lib/libGL.so.1 was linked to the wrong file, it needed to link to libGL.so.1.8774 but instead it was linked to libGL.so.1.2
