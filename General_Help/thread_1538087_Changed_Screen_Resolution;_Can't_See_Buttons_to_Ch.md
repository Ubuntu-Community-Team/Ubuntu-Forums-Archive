### Post by Teasdale on 2010-07-24
I googled this and saw posts about typing a command into the terminal about the xorg or whatever, which didn't help me at all because my screen was so big that the top toolbar had disappeared and I couldn't access my terminal.

I accidentally found an easy way to get my screen back without accessing the terminal, or re-installing Ubuntu, or even rebooting.

The bottom toolbar apparently doesn't disappear, no matter how big the screen is. If you right-click on it, you have the option to add elements to the panel. I added "Main Menu" to the panel. The main menu gave me everything that's normally on the top toolbar. from there, I was able to access system > preferences > screen resolution and re-set my screen settings.
