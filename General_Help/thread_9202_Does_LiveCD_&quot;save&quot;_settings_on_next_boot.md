### Post by TravisNewman on 2004-12-26
A couple of live cds support saving settings to a hard drive or thumb drive, or even a floppy. That would be handy in a future release of the Ubuntu LiveCD, but unfortunately, as it is,  you'd have to manually edit some files on the cd image and then burn it to get this to work.
