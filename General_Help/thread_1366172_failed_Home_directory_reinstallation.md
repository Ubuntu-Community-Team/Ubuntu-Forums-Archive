### Post by yanvolking on 2009-12-28
Hello Community,

I had to completely reinstall Ubuntu Netbook Remix 9.10 on my acer aspire one.  I had made a tar backup of my /home directory.  When I reinstalled the home directory, most of the applications such as Wine, Virtual Box, Mailx, Compiz and many more just did not appear, although all the relevant files were loaded in the reinstalled home directory.

Can anyone tell me why this is so and if there's anything that can be done to get these applications back without having to reinstall them all over again?

Thanks

---

### Post by Cheesemill on 2009-12-28
Your going to have to re-install the apps. Your home folder only contains the application settings, not the actual applications. Once re-installed the apps will pick up all of their settings from your home folder.

---

### Post by modellerau on 2009-12-28
You have backed up all your data and settings by making a tar of /home, but you need to reinstall the programs you usually use as these are usually found in /etc and some other places.
