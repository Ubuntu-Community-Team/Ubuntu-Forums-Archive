### Post by Light- on 2010-01-17
What kind of network do you have? Wired? Wireless?

If the Network Manager icon in the notification area does not show any networks when you single click on it, it is likely that the drivers for your network card arent loaded.

Have a look in System->Administration->Hardware Drivers to see if there are any proprietary drivers for your network card.
