### Post by Jebtrix on 2010-10-23
Goto System > Preferences > Monitors

Differences come into play when your using proprietary drivers from ATI or Nvidia. These can be installed through System > Administration > Additional Drivers. These usually have their own control panel for multi-monitor setups.
