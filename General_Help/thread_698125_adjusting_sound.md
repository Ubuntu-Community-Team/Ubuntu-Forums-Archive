### Post by happyhamster on 2008-02-16
Try as follows: double-click the volume slider icon on your top panel. Choose Edit -->Preferences.
Select all available sound channels (Master, Headphone, etc). Choose close. (Make sure all instances of Volume Control are closed.)

Double-click the volume icon again. The sliders/options for all sound channels should be visible. Now it's probably just a matter of experimenting to find the right settings. First thing you should look for is if there's something "jack-sensing" related in the Switches or Options tab. And if ticking/unticking the External Amplifier helps or not.

Note that some sliders won't do anything useful for your setup, so you can disregard those (mute them).

Good luck.
