### Post by mrMango on 2008-06-05
System -> Preferences -> Sound

"Sounds" tab. Check "Enable software sound mixing (ESD)". Logout and back in and it should work for you.

---

### Post by Oreo Priest on 2008-06-17
Oddly enough, I already had that enabled, and after disabling it, now it works. Thanks, (but in a weird way)!
