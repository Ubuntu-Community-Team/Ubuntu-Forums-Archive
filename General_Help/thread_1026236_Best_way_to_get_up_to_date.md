### Post by sdibias on 2008-12-30
Ubuntu will keep you up to date automatically, to set-up click  System>>Administration>>Software Sources then click the Updates tab, from there you can tell Ubuntu how to handle updates. This is similar but much better than Windows automatic update. 

To update manually you can click System>>Administration>>Update Manager

Welcome back and Enjoy!
