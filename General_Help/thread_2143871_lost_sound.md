### Post by 25tom on 2013-05-10
Is the problem just a lack of sound? And have you tried going into the System Settings, then to Sound, and checking the levels there? Also, have you tried opening an application that you want to play sound, attempting to play sound using it, and then, while is is still playing, checking the Applications tab of the Sound window in System Settings, and making sure the level is not set to zero or something?

Hope this helps :)
