### Post by underquark on 2009-11-23
A lot of frames come with their own software which will only run under Windows but many also have the option of reading from a memory card or USB stick and transferring files to their internal memory.  I bought as a gift for someone that does that and they simply top it up with images from an SD card.  Philips do some that claim to run under Windows, Mac OsX, Linux etc. and it may be that their device's memory is read/writable externally just like a USB stick rather than having any fancy software.  Most frames seem to have all the fancy fade/transition effects built-in.
