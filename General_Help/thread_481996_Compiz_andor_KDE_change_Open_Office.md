### Post by rax_m on 2007-06-23
Hi 

I don't think it's normal.. I had the issue when I did a clean upgrade to Feisty.. 

The way I resolved it was to edit the OOo preferences (Tools->Options)

Then under OpenOffice.org->View option 
Check and uncheck the "show icons in menu" then apply the changes. 

Voila .. icons are back :) 
Not sure the root cause of the problem tho.
