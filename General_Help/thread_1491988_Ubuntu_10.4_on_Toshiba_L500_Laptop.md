hey! I'm afraid I can not share rodneys enthusiasm concerning the Toshiba L500 models... I'm using Ubuntu 10.04 and have some troubles:

 - no blue-tooth recognized
 - 3d acceleration is very buggy (but according to my card, a HD4650, its no big surprise)
 - usb-speakers (should be OS-independent) do not always work (sometimes music works but no sound w. flash-player and vice versa; always needs new manual setting to work).
 - did not figure out yet how to activate the touch-pad ("toshset" is not working)
 - Wifi- and Touch-pad On/Off button don't work

but working from beginning on are:

 - Wifi
 - Micro/Camera/Internal Speakers
 - Mailbrowser etc.
 - VOIP applications
 - Amarok (w. tweak for mp3)
 - Toshiba Controls for LCD-brightness and "mute"-button

I hope at least to get the problem w. the blue-tooth device fixed so I can use my BT-mouse.
thats all so far. If anybody knows a solution to my problems - don't hesitate to let me know :)
