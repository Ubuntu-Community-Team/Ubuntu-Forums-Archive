### Post by omegamike3 on 2008-06-17
Emerald takes advantage of compositing, such as Compiz. Which helps add a few more effects to the themes used. In the end, it's just a matter of preference and the amount of eye-candy you want. If there's a Metacity theme that you really like, then just stick with it, such is the power of Linux: Choice. :)
