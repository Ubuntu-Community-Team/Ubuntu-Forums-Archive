### Post by mempman on 2008-07-07
If this happens often, i would suggest you monitor this behaviour and then post your error message.

you can get your error message by launching "compiz --replace" from a gnome-terminal window. Do this at the beginning of you session, and when compiz crashes collect the error message from your gnome-terminal.

Also, you can find excellent trouble shooting support at #compiz chanel on IRC.
