### Post by keithk50 on 2007-02-26
Since the installation of Ubuntu onto my laptop 6 days ago I have had no sound at all.   Being brand new to Linux I have spent many hours going through the forums trying to find a solution.   I am grateful for the help & support received so far.
Whilst experimenting with the controls in 'alsamixer' I muted the  'External Amplifier' (named just External within the mixer) and heh presto sound arrived.   I also ensured that the PC Speaker and Master volumes were set to loud within the mixer and all others were unmuted.
Now this may not be the answer for everyone but if it helps just one 'no sound' person and saves a lot of frustrating hours looking for solutions then great.   It has certainly made my day.:guitar:    Thanks again to those who replied to my plea for help some days ago.
