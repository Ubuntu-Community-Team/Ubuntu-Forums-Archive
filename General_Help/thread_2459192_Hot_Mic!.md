### Post by TheFu on 2021-03-13
In the PulseAudio settings, you can disable any microphones.  I just unplug it. Then I 100% know it isn't listening, but in a laptop, that isn't possible.  **pavucontrol** is an application that can disable/enable PulseAudio stuff.

There is also a mute toggle in almost all sound apps.
