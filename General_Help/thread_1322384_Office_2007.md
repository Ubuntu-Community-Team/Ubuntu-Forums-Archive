### Post by QIII on 2009-11-10
Since PlayOnLinux is a Python front-end with Wine ([http://appdb.winehq.org/index.php](http://appdb.winehq.org/index.php)) as the back end,  you should check Wine's database for compatibility.

Some of the components are not rated highly, and they have only been tested by a small number of users.

Access 2007, for instance, is rated as "Garbage".
