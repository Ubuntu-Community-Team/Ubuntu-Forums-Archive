### Post by WakkiTabakki on 2009-07-08
> **5nak3 said:**
> Also in case you don't like cairo dock or AWN or if your graphics card does not play too well with them.

You can also use Gnome-Do [http://do.davebsd.com/index.shtml](http://do.davebsd.com/index.shtml)

It may be a little bit more difficult to set up than the other two but I prefer it.

Docky that comes with the Gnome-Do (ver 0.82 I think) is the best overall, in my opinion. It works as a combination of Mac:s Quicksilver and Dock (gnome-do is the equivalence of quicksilver, and the dock is actually just a theme for gnome-do).

It's a breeze to install:
1) Add the gnome-do ppa to your software sources to get the latest version. Go to [https://launchpad.net/~do-core/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~do-core/+archive/ppa) to get the details
2) Install gnome-do through synaptics
3) Start Gnome-do (Applications->Accessories->Gnome-Do)
4) Start the preferences by right-clicking the gnome-do icon in the tray

Good luck
