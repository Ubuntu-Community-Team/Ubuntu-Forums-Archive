Right click while on the application tool bar (top left normally).  Edit Menus...  Pops up the box with all the menu items...  Drag and drop them where you want them.

Also - to note (which is a pain)...  When you drag one to the new place you want it - notice it will still be in the old one.  Just uncheck it where it was and it will not display.  Also note (on some of the older releases of Ubuntu) - when you delete it from the original spot - it may delete from where you put it as well.  Thus unchecking in the old spot is a good route.
