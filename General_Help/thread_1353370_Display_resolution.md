I figured out how to do this... like right after I posted this thread, and forgot about it. I was just looking for the same answer again, because I just installed Mint on the same box and forgot how I fixed the problem before. :P

But, I just solved this problem in Mint 8 by going to Control Center > Hardware Drivers, and updating/installing the video card drivers there. I had to restart and the display is now a perfect fit. I'm pretty sure that in Ubuntu I did something similar, and installed video card drivers. It wasn't anything complicated in either case.
