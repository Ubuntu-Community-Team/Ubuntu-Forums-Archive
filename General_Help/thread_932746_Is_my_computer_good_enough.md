### Post by Excalibre on 2008-09-29
A big thing to check is your video card. In windows, go to Start - Settings - Control Panel - System - Hardware tab - Device Manager and look under Display adapters. Some of them are very well supported on Linux and others are not. It's hard to tell from the Live CD whether you'll get Compiz working, but doing some research into your video adapter will probably help you figure that out.

But yeah, consider adding more RAM -- it's a fairly cheap thing that will likely extend the useful life of your computer by a substantial amount. $50 worth of additional RAM might make the thing run good enough to be useful for another year.
