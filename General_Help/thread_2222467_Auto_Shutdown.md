### Post by Gyokuro on 2014-05-06
Hi

Could be an overheating problem and correct the power down of your system - it's a feature built in your CPU which powers down your system in case it's gets to hot and may damage your hardware. Try to clean the fan area of dust and put your laptop at solid ground - I have damaged one as I was tired and fall asleep, CPU fan got no air and CPU overheated.
