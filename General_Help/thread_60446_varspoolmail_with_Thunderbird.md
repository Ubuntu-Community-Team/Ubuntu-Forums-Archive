[QUOTE=swamytk]1. How can I integrate linux system mail (/var/spool/mail) to thunderbird? I mean how to read the /var/spool/mail from thunderbird?[/QUOTE]Sure.  In thunderbird, it's called a "Movemail" account.  Set-up is just like any other account except pick Movemail instead of email.

[QUOTE=swamytk]2. Is there any way to redirect the mails from /var/spool/mail to any pop mail like gmail?[/QUOTE]Do you mean automatically forward your local mail to a gmail account?  I'm sure this is possible.  A google search on "mail forwarding" will probably help.
