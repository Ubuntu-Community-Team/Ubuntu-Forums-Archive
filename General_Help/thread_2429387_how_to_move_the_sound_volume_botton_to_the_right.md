### Post by Dennis N on 2019-10-17
In Gnome Shell, the volume icon is in "system status area". The design is for the power off icon (always displayed) to be on the right end of the status area. There seems to be a specified order for icons - probably not user modifiable.

Read about its design, and specifically see: "Top Bar Icons":
[https://wiki.gnome.org/Design/OS/SystemStatus](https://wiki.gnome.org/Design/OS/SystemStatus)
