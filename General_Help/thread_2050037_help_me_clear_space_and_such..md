### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-08-30
One likely cause for Ubuntu suddenly dropping back to CLI mode is that an update changed the kernel version and the restricted drivers no longer work with that version.  If you had AMD/ATI or Nvidia restricted drivers install, and your Dad did an update, that could cause this.

Given the other problems, unless you have critical data in Ubuntu that you can't afford to lose, or unless you can't save that data offline somewhere, your best bet would be to blow away the installation and reinstall from scratch.
