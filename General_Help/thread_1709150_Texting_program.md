### Post by wormyblackburny on 2011-03-17
If you have a G-mail account, go into the labs section under settings and add the text service. Works awesome and if you are offline, it drops the message that was texted to you into an email. I used to use it all the time before I got a droid and unlimited text. Even better, I used it to shut my kids up from wanting phones when they were 10 just so they could be "cool" and text their friends. "You want a phone, get a job. You want to text your friends, use this. Now quit bugging me, cuz a phone ain't happening!" :)
