### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-07-27
> **villalcalde84 said:**
> Is there a way to fix this?

Yes -- two ways:
1) Do not create files using "docx"
2) Tell the folks creating the "docx" files to save them in Word 2003 format (i.e., "doc"). (We had to do this when other folks, who had newer PCs with Office 2007 on them, were saving documents as "docx" by default).

Or (3) -- use MS Office.
