### Post by davidgurvich on 2009-04-28
More likely to be liquid spilled on the keyboard.  If the spill were water just unplug for a few hours and everything should be fine.  You might also clean the keyboard with 50/50 ispropyl alcohol/distilled water. 

Cleaning the contacts on the keys may be more trouble than it is worth.  You should consider getting a new keyboard.
