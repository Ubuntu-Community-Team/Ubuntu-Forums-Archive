### Post by blueridgedog on 2009-08-15
One nearly random idea is that the disks are not finalized/closed.  Do you see your files if you open Brasero and look at the disk?

Another idea is the laser on the disk in your PC is not aligned similarly to the laser in the drive that burned the disk.  Can you try the disk in another system?
