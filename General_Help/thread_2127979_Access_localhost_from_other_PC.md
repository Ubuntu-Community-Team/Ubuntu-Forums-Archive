### Post by papibe on 2013-03-21
Hi Yuva Raj.

If you are not on a LAN (no router, not switch), you have s options:
[LIST]
[*]Use a [ethernet crossover cable]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethernet_crossover_cable"), or
[*]If they both have wireless cards, you could create an [ad hoc network connection]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/136859/how-to-make-ad-hoc-network-connection").
[/LIST]
Hope it helps. Let us know how it goes.
Regards.
