### Post by fermulator on 2009-05-07
Does anyone know where gnome panel stores the launcher files which reside in a "[drawer]("http://www.novell.com/coolsolutions/feature/14521.html")"?

[IMG]http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/bb/Gnome-panel-drawer.png[/IMG]

**_ANSWER_**:  In ~/.gnome2/panel2.d/default/launchers

The launchers here are named not by their "Name", but by the "Command" they run.

I'm not sure how gnome determines where the launchers belong on the panel (because it appears launchers on the panel and in drawers appear in the same directory ... but at least they all go to the same place.)
