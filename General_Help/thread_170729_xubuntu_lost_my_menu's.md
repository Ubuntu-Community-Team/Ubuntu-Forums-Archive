### Post by Sutekh on 2006-05-05
Right-click the panel, and choose **Add to Panel**

Firefox/Terminal
 - choose a **Launcher** and use ```
/usr/bin/mozilla-firefox
``` and ```
/usr/bin/xfce4-terminal
``` in the **Command** section of the dialog

Shutdown
 - choose the **Action Button**
