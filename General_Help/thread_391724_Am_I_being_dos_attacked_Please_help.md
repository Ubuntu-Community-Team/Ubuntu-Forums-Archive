### Post by kidders on 2007-03-23
Hi there,

What kind of traffic are we talking about here? From the seemingly arbitrary port numbers, I'm guessing you use filesharing (maybe bittorrent) apps. If that's the case, these errors are probably just the result of bad packets, rather than a deliberate attempt to cause damage.

In any case, assuming your router *is* correctly identifying a teardrop attack, you shouldn't notice any adverse affects. It's a rather archaic IP manipulation.
