### Post by ffuts on 2007-10-08
Well... I'm not 100% sure because i'm new as well.  I'm trying to do the same thing with my D600 and its dock to a 46" sharp via DVI-->HDMI.  The Xorg.conf MAN page says that all identifiers have to be unique.  You're using the same name for everything.  change your video card to something else.

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"LCD TV"
...

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"LCD TV"
...

Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"Default Screen"
	Device		"LCD TV"
	Monitor		"LCD TV"
...

_**CHANGE TO**_
Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Nvidia_Card"
...

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"LCD TV"
...

Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"Default Screen"
	Device		"Nvidia_Card"
	Monitor		"LCD TV"
...


Good luck, please wish me the same.
