### Post by Fatfool on 2008-11-10
you could attempt to use another CUPS driver. doesn't have to be the exact same model.

for example I know the Canon MP220 works with the Canon MP180's CUPS driver.

try a model which has a similar model number. perhaps a predecessor to your printer's model. no guarantee it'll work though.
