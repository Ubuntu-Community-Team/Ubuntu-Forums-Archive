### Post by Matticus on 2007-11-02
After you have tried what neilengineer said, as that seems like the most simple and also most likely cause/resolve.
You can try a few more things, one issue ive seen a lot of times, which usually results in a freeze, a freeze with screen blanking or a reboot, but usually in windows, is simple things like a harddrive cable not being in properly or being damaged, this is especially true of the first gen of sata drives.

When the crash happens, can you turn on and off caps lock/numlock/scroll lock on your keyboard (ie does the LED for them go on and off when you press), is the harddrive working light (red LED on front of case usually) stuck on solid. If they are, youve probably got a cable error, or sadly a harddrive error, though the former is much more common.
