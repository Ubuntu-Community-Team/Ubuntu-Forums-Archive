### Post by Stigmata13 on 2010-05-29
I would suggest booting into your BIOS/CMOS and looking for the option to "load setup defaults"
It is possible that the sound is just turned off in the bios, it's worth a shot.
Common keys to enter bios are pressing "del" or "f2" while your system is booting and showing your computer model's logo.
