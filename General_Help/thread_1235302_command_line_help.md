### Post by Katalog on 2009-08-08
I can't recommend this enough, but there is a free book that is very good called The "Ubuntu Pocket Guide and Reference" that gives advice and instructions on everything, including common commands used in the terminal. You can download it here:

[http://www.ubuntupocketguide.com/download_main.html](http://www.ubuntupocketguide.com/download_main.html)

They should actually include a copy of that on the desktop with every new Ubuntu install. If you find it useful enough, you might even consider supporting the author and purchasing a hard copy from Amazon (I believe there is a link to it on the download page).

EDIT: Disclaimer - no, I am not the aothor and neither do I know him. I've just found this thing useful more than once in the past and it should be a must read for all noobies.
