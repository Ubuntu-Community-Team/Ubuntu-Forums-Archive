### Post by pastalavista on 2011-03-19
To get rid of the icons on the desktop, open the run command box (Alt+F2) and enter 'gconf-editor' or Applications>>System Tools>>Configuration Editor and navigate to apps/nautilus/desktop and uncheck "show_volumes".
To download youtube videos, I use the Firefox extention "Video Download Helper"
