### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-06-05
"Recovery mode" is not a case of starting up and the whole system recovers from a disaster all by itself.

It is simply a command line system, (with root permissions activated, if that is what you want), which usually allows you to repair problems that have occurred.

As Quackers says, tell us what went wrong, why you have booted to recovery mode, and what you wish to do now you are there, and everyone will do their best to assist.
