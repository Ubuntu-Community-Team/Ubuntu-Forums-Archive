### Post by phaedrus on 2008-07-23
I realise this is a very old question now... but for people searching for this in the future: It appears that snmpwalk is part of the package snmp, rather than tinysnmp-agent. I had tinysnmp-agent installed, but not snmp, and it didn't work. I've now installed snmp, and removed tinysnmp-agent, and snmpwalk now works.
