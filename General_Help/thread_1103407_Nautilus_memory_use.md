### Post by scottuss on 2009-03-22
Nautilus is known as a memory hog but that seems rather high. Remember Nautilus runs all the time because it is responsible for more than just showing folders.

If the computer is getting slow because of it you can restart X or even do a full reboot. Or you could just kill nautilus and wait for it to restart itself....
