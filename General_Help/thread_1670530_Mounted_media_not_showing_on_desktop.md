### Post by jakev383 on 2011-01-24
Seems I figured it out. This is my laptop and I put it in a docking station at home. I have a 2nd monitor connected when it's dicked that has a different screen resolution than the laptop screen, and this is where the problem lies. Seems on the laptop screen my desktop actually extends ABOVE the tool bar at the top - I can move my mouse up past it and it will complete disappear. It also appears that my mounted media icons are in this area above the toolbar, where I cannot see them. If the laptop is undocked or removed from the dock, the desktop is correctly sized and I can see the icons again.
Strange behaviour. Not a show stopper though.
