### Post by cbowman57 on 2011-12-30
> **davethewave83 said:**
> ok, do I copy paste that into the grub.cfg?

When you get to the grub menu select the entry you want & type **e** to edit, then just add noacpi or acpi=off to the end of that line, press ctrl+x or f10 to boot with that setting.

If you want that to be your default you add it to /etc/default/grub, location should be obvious.  Then sudo update-grub.
