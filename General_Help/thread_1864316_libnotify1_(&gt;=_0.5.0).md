### Post by fomelkaos on 2011-10-20
I had to go here to install it: [http://packages.ubuntu.com/natty/libnotify1](http://packages.ubuntu.com/natty/libnotify1)
At the bottom it says 'Download libnotify1' click on your chip architecture.  Then pick your favorite mirror for the .deb package.  Open and install with synaptic and you should be good to go when you try to install handbrake.
