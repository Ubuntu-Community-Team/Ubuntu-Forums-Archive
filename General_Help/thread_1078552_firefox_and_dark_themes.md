### Post by fragos on 2009-02-23
Gnome applications follow the theme but those not written specifically for Gnome, Firefox, may not. White text may end up in white background. I tweaked my themes to give the best compromise between these aps. For example using a pastel color rather than white for text still shows up in a dark background but is also readable when the background switches to white. Web sites are all over the map inheriting some colors from the theme while specifying others. On my sites I specifically specify every color wwhich gives a consistent result. I ended up changing the color of "Input boxes:" to white while keeping the rest dark. Now the many web sites that assume they are inheriting a white background will always get one. Poor web site coding on their part.
