### Post by pRedat0r on 2006-08-15
Well, I determined that the issue I am having getting XGL/Compiz running is related to the Big Desktop mode in the ati driver.  I commented out the following lines in my xorg.conf:

```
	#Option	    "DesktopSetup" "horizontal,reverse"
	#Option	    "OverlayOnCRTC2" "1"
	#Option	    "ForceMonitors" "tdms1,crt1,notv"
```

When I comment them, effectively going to a 2 monitor clone mode setup, then XGL starts fine.  The instant I re-enable those commands, then X yells at me that there are no screens found.  I have also tried DesktopSetup "horizontal" as well, without the reverse command, and that makes no difference.

Anyone else with an ati card found a workaround?
