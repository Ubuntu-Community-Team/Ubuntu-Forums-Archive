### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-03-05
I have the Nvidia GT220. My monitor is the Samsung Syncmaster T200HD. The documentation says that the optimum resolution of the screen is 1680x1050 @ 60Hz. This is the setting that the Nvidia X-Server utility is using.

It is my understanding that the monitor may be damaged if it is not run at the optimum resolution. I suggest that you forget about the resolution of the games. Perhaps they were developed when 800x600 was the standard screen resolution.

 It is important that the screen is run at its native resolution. Which it what the Nvidia driver is doing.
