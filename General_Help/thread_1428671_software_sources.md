### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-03-13
Your screenshot is of the least important part of Software Sources.

On the first tab ("Ubuntu Software"), you should have main, universe, multiverse and restricted ticked, at the very least.

Under the "Updates" tab, you should have "karmic-security" and "karmic-updates" ticked. In addition you might want to have "karmic-backports" ticked too, and if you want to help test new updates you can have "karmic-proposed" ticked.

Under Authentication, you should have the Ubuntu Archive Automatic Signing Key at the very least.

And under Third-Party Software you can have whatever you like.
