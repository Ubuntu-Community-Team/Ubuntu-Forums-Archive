### Post by SyvanX on 2006-12-31
That firefox problem you're having is a feature of firefox.  When you shut down, all your apps are closing, firefox is just trying to remember what tabs were open in case your computer happened to be in an unstable operating system and froze up from time to time.  This would let you go right back to your previous browsing session.

If you go into Firefox Preferences, you can turn off the Option to resume your previous browsing session.
