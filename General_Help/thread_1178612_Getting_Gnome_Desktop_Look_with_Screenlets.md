### Post by sylentdogg on 2009-06-04
1. The menu is called gnomenu.  It is available at [http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/GnoMenu+-+consolidated+menu+for+gnome?content=93057]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/GnoMenu+-+consolidated+menu+for+gnome?content=93057")  You can also find Themes for the menu there.

2.gnome-look.org also has transparent panel themes. Search for panels in the "other" category. Right click on the panel properties under background, and add as a background image.

3. & 4. Once you have screenlets installed, you can put them anywhere in the desktop and right click on them to adjust size and properties.
