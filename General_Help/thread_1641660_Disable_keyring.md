### Post by lmarmisa on 2010-12-09
Open System -> Preferences -> Passwords and Encryption Keys . Under the Passwords tab, select the item **Passwords: default** and pressing and holding the right mouse button  Change Password. Fill your old password and leave blank the new password fields. Finally select Use Unsafe Storage.
