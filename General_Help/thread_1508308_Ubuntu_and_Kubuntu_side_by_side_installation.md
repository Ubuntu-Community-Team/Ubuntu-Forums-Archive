### Post by randyklein99 on 2010-06-12
The difference between Kubuntu and Ubuntu is not in the kernel, but the desktop.  KDE vs GNOME.  You won't see any difference at the grub menu.  But you will see an option to login into a gnome or KDE environment at the login screen.
