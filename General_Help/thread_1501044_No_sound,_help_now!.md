### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-06-03
What I do is to install pulseaudio, then go to "System Settings >> Computer Administration >> Multimedia" and put pulseaudio on the top of all lists. It works for me, but I'm using a KDE minimal installation, not Kubuntu, if that makes any difference.
