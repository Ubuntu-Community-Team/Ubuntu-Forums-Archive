### Post by wannadumpwindows on 2008-05-11
There's a good chance you can add, it's just a matter of how you have to do it. If you have one ram slot, or two. If you have two slots, you can just add a stick to the empty one. But, if you only have one slot, you're going to need a single 2Gb stick. Google your name and model number of your computer, and you should be able to find some info, or post the model here, and I can give you a hand.
