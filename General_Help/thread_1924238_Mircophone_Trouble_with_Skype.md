### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-02-13
Skype is poorly supported on Linux, and I guess it will remain that way now that Microsoft has bought it.

But it can usually be made to work.

Open Sound Preferences (search for the *Sound* app, or click on the speaker in your panel and select *Sound Preferences*, or go to *System Settings* and find *Sound* there).

Go through the tabs and ensure that the right hardware is chosen, and that the input is not muted.
