### Post by Mindswap1 on 2010-12-19
I  managed to fix it. Its very simple too! All you have to do is go to go to your home folder ( where your documents,pictures, downloads,and videos is located.) and then simply make a new folder, and name it exactly what the old folder was named. This automatically formats the folder to have the video icon or w.e folder u are restoring, and the same preferences, and it will appear in Places again in the global menu after a quick restart.
