### Post by yancek on 2018-09-04
Did you verify the Debian iso after the download?  Did you try booting the usb on another computer?  Check various settings in the BIOS.  Some computers (particularly laptops) will not boot from all usb ports and there may be an option under HDD in the BIOS showing the usb drive, often by manufacturer name.
