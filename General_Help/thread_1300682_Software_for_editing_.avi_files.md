_Simple video creation and editing _	

Windows: Windows Movie Maker

Linux:
    * Broadcast 2000
    * Avidemux
    * Kdenlive!
    * Kino 

_Professional video production enviroment _	

Windows: Adobe Premiere, Media Studio Pro

Linux
    * CinePaint
    * Cinelerra
    * MainActor
    * Broadcast 2000
    * Lives
    * Heroine Virtual 

_Cutting video _	

Windows: Virtual Dub

Linux:
    * Avidemux
    * Kino 

_Converting video _	

Windows: Virtual Dub, Mencoder (from Mplayer)
	
Linux:
    * FFMpeg (from MPlayer)
    * ffmpeg2theora
    * Mencoder (from MPlayer)
