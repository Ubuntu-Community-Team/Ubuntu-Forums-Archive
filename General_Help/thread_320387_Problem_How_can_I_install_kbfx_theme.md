Hi Michele,

Right-click on bottom taskbar.
Click Panel Menu -> Add Applet to Panel -> KBFX Sphinx (name might vary, not sure).
Wait while it loads, can take a while.
To change the theme, click Change the Settings at the top of the kbfx kicker under your username.
Icons on the taskbar, e.g. the KDE kicker (K Menu), can be removed by right-clicking the taskbar, selecting Panel Menu and then Remove from Panel --> Applet > K Menu.
