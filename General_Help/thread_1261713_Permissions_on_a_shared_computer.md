### Post by BslBryan on 2009-09-09
You can't easily limit what kinds of programs a user can or cannot install, but you can limit how much access they have to your system.

Follow System --> Administration --> Users and Groups --> Unlock, and select a user and modify what they are allowed to do! :-)
