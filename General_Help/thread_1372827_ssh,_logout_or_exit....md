### Post by cenzorrll on 2010-01-05
as far as i know, logout is a command for your console to logout of the computer.  so you would logout of ubuntu (if that's what os you're using) and you would exit your ssh session, or su session etc. i guess it's sort of like closing a program for the console (exit), versus logging off your computer (logout)

i'm sure someone else could explain this better, but that's how i see it.
