### Post by 2CV67 on 2013-04-06
OK - now it's working!

> OCRFeeder suffers from a lack of information on how to use it. A quick start guide provided with the installation or included in the Help menu would easily solve this weakness.

> Knowing what to do after the image is on the screen is the application's make-or-break factor.

For example, you must select the Unpaper function to prepare the scanned image for editing.

[http://www.linuxinsider.com/story/76510.html](http://www.linuxinsider.com/story/76510.html)

You also have to wonder what to do with "Clip" & "Bounds", before finally discovering that you have to drag to define a zone to include your text...

Obvious once you have seen it, but far from obvious with no instructions!

Glad it works anyway!
