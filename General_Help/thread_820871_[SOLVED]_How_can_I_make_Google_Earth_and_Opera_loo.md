### Post by jimv on 2008-06-06
Change your googleearth launcher to this:

```
googleearth -style plastique
```

Not perfect, but better than the default.

As for Opera, this guy posted how to make it look better awhile back:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-78468.html](http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-78468.html)
