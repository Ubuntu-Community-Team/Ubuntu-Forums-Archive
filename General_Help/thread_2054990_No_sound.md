I went to Hampshire LUG today, and a nice young man called Chris sorted out this problem for me. There is incompatibility between the IBM Lenovo, Windows and Linux.  When Windows sound is muted, Linux sound is also muted.  Chris fixed it by typing clever things in the terminal.

I do urge folks to use their local Linux User Groups, so helpful. :KS
