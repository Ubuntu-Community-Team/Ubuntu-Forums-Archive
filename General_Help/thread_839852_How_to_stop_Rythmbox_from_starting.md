### Post by txtinman on 2008-06-24
When I insert an audio cd Rythmbox starts up. Thats fine if I want to play the cd, but sometimes I want to do other stuff like run Grip. How can I make it where nothing happens when I put in a cd? I'm using Hardy BTW.

---

### Post by cariboo on 2008-06-24
Open Places-->Home Folder, then click Edit-->Preferences, Click the Media tab and make your selection. You will have to log out and back in again to make the changes take affect.
