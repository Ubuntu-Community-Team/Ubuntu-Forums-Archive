### Post by Merrattic on 2012-08-26
Your laptop doesn't have a/any video input provision, so you would need to get your hands on a Composite to USB convertor (e.g. easycap or similar) and a composite cable and audio cable. Once connected up you can use mplayer or vlc to view XBOX output on your laptop.

Do a search on youtube for easycap, you will see how it is done
