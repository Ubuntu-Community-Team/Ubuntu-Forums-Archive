It will not CONVERT tablature to sheet music, but have a look at tuxguitar. You have to input the tabs yourself, but you can then print the tablature, regular sheet music, or both. Once installed, go to Track-->Properties. From there you can choose the type of instrument. Toward the bottom of that list, there are a couple of listings for drums.
