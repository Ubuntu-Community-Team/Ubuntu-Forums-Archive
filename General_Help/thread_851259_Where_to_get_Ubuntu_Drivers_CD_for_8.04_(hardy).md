### Post by steveneddy on 2008-07-06
System --> Administration --> Network

Unlock, click on Wireless Connection

Click properties

Make sure the "Enable Roaming Mode" check box is checked.

Close the window.

Restart.

Unplug any ethernet cable during the restart.

After you get to a desktop, left click the Network Manager and see if your wireless SSID is visible.

Then post back.
