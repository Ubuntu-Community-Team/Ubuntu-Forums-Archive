### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-07-15
Cookies are in .mozilla/firefox/*.default/cookies.sqlite. You can also backup the whole of .mozilla and then reuse it on new installations. To see .mozilla folder in the file browser, open your home folder and hit ctrl+h.

---

### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-07-15
I would also recommend making regular backups of your profile folder, since they can become corrupted. This would save you a lot of trouble. For that use [FEBE](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2109/) extension.
