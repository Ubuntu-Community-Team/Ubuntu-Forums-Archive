### Post by RedDwarf on 2007-04-13
> **vsandz said:**
> well unfortunately for me all of my friends are on skype and it seems a bit selfish of me to ask them all to switch over just because i'm linuxing now.
If you use Ekiga you are asking your friends to switch to ANY program or hardware VoIP phone with support for SIP or H323. And that is the 99,99% of videoconference programs and phones. They are who are asking you to use Skype and only Skype...

Any way, right now Ekiga only supports H.261 for video (bad quality). There are plans to add H.263 support, but this will no be before version 2.2.0.
If you want the best quality you shoukd search for a SIP or H.323 program with support for H.264. iChat, on Mac, supports it... on Linux and Windows I don't know, but probably OpenWengo 2.1 supports it (and for sure a lot of other Windows programs).
H.264 is the CODEC used in next-gen DVDs (Blue-ray and HD-DVD), so I am sure that everybody will have enough quality.
