### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-11-04
Have you tried Applications > Sound and Video > Brasero Disc Burner > Burn Image button? 

It is the longer method of doing the same job if the context menu entries don't work (basically by a few more clicks). Won't fix the context menus, but should let you burn a disc from an iso.
