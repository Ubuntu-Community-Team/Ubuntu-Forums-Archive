### Post by P4man on 2009-10-15
You will see the other computers once you (or rather they) have available shares. They don't show up if they don't share anything. And no, its no problem to share with windows users. Just right click a folder, choose sharing options, and share it (and give guest access). It may prompt you to download a few things, but should explain itself.
