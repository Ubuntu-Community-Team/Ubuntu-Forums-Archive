### Post by samzklub on 2008-05-03
So this has been bothering me for some time. Every time I loaded Compiz, the titlebars on my windows would disappear. I tried just about every thing I found on the forums to no avail. I then started poking around in the Advanced Desktop Effects Settings menu (compizconfig-settings-manager in synaptic) under System->Preferences.

The problem ended up being an oversight on my part. Click on "Effects" in the menu on the left and then click on "Window Decoration." It turned out this plugin wasn't enabled. All I had to do was click "Enable Window Decoration" on the left under "Use This Plugin" and my titlebars came back to play! 

I figured something like this would have been enabled by default but I guess not. Hopefully this helps someone out.
