### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-05-06
I do not understand your complaint. What file brower applet are you talking about? In 11.04 I have two file browsers. There is Nautilus hiding under the folder icon and then there is the icon of the magnifying glass over a document. Click on that and you will see your recently accessed files with an option to view all files and folders. Click on a folder and the standard file manager utility opens that folder. With this new feature you do not have too open folder after folder to get access to files. It works for me.
