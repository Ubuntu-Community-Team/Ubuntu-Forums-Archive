### Post by geekyhawkes on 2009-01-08
I have sort of found a work around.  IN case anyone else is having this issue, one way around seems to be is you set the screenlets as you want them, hibernate the PC.  Next time you shut the pc down and start up the screenlets revert to the settings you had at the hibernation.  Not ideal, but defiantly better than them not remembering anything!
