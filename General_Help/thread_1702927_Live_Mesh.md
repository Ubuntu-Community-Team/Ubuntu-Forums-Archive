### Post by cbolourchi on 2011-03-08
As of November 1st, 2010 Amazon Web Services S3 has a free tier that would probably suit your needs. 5GB storage, 15GB transfer in, 15GB transfer out (a month) all for free. And if you use more than that, it might as well be free. I'm storing ~100GB and transferring in/out daily and it costs $12 for one month. If you have a solid internet connection, S3 is the cat's pajamas.

Dropbox is nice, but S3 is nicer...

[http://aws.amazon.com/free/](http://aws.amazon.com/free/)
