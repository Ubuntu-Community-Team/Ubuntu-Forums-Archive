### Post by StuartN on 2009-10-03
> **lupo1939 said:**
> I am considering installation of a web cam.

Try following the links at the top of [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Webcam](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Webcam) which include specific models.

If a camera is UVC compatible it will say so on the package, even if it doesn't say "Linux". Logitech E 3500 and S 7500 are UVC compatible, but there are many both UVC and non-UVC that work.

Compatible cameras will "just work" when you plug them in without you actively installing or configuring any drivers.
