### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-07-25
Not to discourage you ... but ... there have been a LOT more posts about overheating with 10.04 than I have seen with earlier versions, and I keep reading the future kernel upgrades will fix this ... but that doesn't seem to be happening.

Also, if it's NOT overheating in Vista (which, from personal experience, is a real reasource HOG), then it's not a BIOS problem.

Hope you realize that the more you use your laptop and have it crash due to overheating, the more you're probably damaging your CPU and motherboard.  Keep doing this and eventually, it won't boot any more.

It's your machine, but if it were mine, and I was not able to get a fix real soon, I would quit using Ubuntu 10.04 and just check back here periodically to see if there is a fix yet.

What good is there from running Linux if your machine burns up in the process?
