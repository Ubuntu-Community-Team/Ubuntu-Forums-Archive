### Post by philinux on 2008-11-12
If you're using an lcd monitor then choose lcd smoothing and then under detail try slight or medium hinting. Or try all 4 options. I prefer slight.

### Post by notbitmonk on 2008-11-23
Have you checked how different fonts look in the different OSes.  It may be that Intrepid doesn't have a font installed and is doing a substitution with no so good results or results that are not what you expect.  For example, DejaVu Sans and FreeSans doesn't have a very nice appearance on IE7 running on XP at certain sizes however they appear very nice on FF3 in Intrepid, Word 2003 in XP and OpenOffice 2.4 in Intrepid.
