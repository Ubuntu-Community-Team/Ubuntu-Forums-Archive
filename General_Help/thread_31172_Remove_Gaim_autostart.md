Okay, Gaim is autostarting un my Ubuntu but it isn't in gnome sessions? Where is it? How do I remove it?

---

### Post by Leif on 2005-05-02
I'm guessing you logged off with gaim running at some point and chose to save the session. Close gaim, and everything else you don't want autostarting, and log off, choosing the save session option. When you login again, it shouldn't start.
