### Post by qamelian on 2007-03-08
> **ltk5 said:**
> any ideas? I wanted to try kwrite, but I can't find the 'export to pdf option'.

In KDE, PDF creation is handled by a PDF printer driver, not by an Export to PDF function.

Depending on how each application creates PDFs, you should be able to adjust various settings that will affect the size of the file. You may just have to poke around until your familiar with the appropriate settings.
