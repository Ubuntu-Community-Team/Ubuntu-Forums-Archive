### Post by soccerboy on 2008-07-02
nothing wrong with you or with Ubuntu.  Ubuntu is one of the easiest to configure and one with most working out of the box in my experience.  The problem is most of the programs are written for Windows and then ported to work with linux.  Ports are not nearly as good as code written for the os.  There has been a change in the backend audio mixer that promises better support for multi-stream control but has broken many things.  THe audacity problem exists for me too.  For now I just export and then playback in nautilus.
