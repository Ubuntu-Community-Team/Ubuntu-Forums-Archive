### Post by TheFu on 2022-01-08
You can create a PDF file, then use PDF editing, really just putting text over the empty boxes to complete the forms. It will take longer than having a friend fill out the forms. If you have issues with hand coordination, clicking on the text tool then placing a mouse in the location you want with the font you want sized as you want could be difficult.

**Xournal** is normally what I use after scanning with **gscan2pdf**.  The only trick is that when everything is filled as you like, then choose "Export to PDF" in Xournal, followed by printing the PDF.
