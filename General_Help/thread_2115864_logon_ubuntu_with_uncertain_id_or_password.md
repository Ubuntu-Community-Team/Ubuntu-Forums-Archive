### Post by Impavidus on 2013-02-14
If it's a used computer anything can be wrong with it. You don't know what happened to the computer before, which packages have been installed, what has been installed manually and what has been (improperly) uninstalled. Furthermore, you haven't put anything useful on it yet. Therefore I'd recommend you just reinstall. Then you will be sure of the current state of the computer, making it less likely you will encounter problems.

Besides, it's generally unwise to leave your e-mail address in a forum.
