### Post by mockdeep on 2009-01-04
Ok, check out this thread:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=990648&highlight=volume+control]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=990648&highlight=volume+control")

It helped.  All I did was go into System, Preferences, Sound and change it to PCM in the bottom panel.  It worked when I hit close.  Then I went back in and changed it back to Master and it still worked.  Guess it's just a matter of reminding the system what setting it is supposed to be using.
