### Post by adam.smith on 2010-02-07
have you tried using the "store copy of file" option in the Zotero new item menu (the green plus). This might take a while for larger bunches of pdfs - you may also which to disable indexing while importing files (set the max number of words in the search tab of the preferences to 0) and then re-index later.
