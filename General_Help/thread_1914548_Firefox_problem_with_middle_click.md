You know how it usually lets us scroll up and down through pages when we middle click?

### Post by guestolo on 2012-01-24
In Firefox, click on EDIT>>Preferences>>Advanced tab
Choose "Use Autoscrolling" under 'Browsing'

It may require you to restart Firefox
