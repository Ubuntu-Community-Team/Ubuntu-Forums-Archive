I have a 32Gb iPod Touch 4G with iOS 4.1 installed and jailbroken. Originally it was previously synced with a Windows iTunes library and I tried to re-sync it with Banshee. Despite appearing to work fine on Banshee's side, the new tracks did not actually show up on the iPod's music player and, worse still, it resulted in every piece of album art being randomly assigned to a different album (ie every album started to have the wrong artwork).

I then tried restoring my iPod (on Windows) and trying to synchronize the library from Banshee to the empty iPod - still the songs did not show up. I have also tried repeating both of these attempts using Rhythmbox instead and it had exactly the same problem. I read that the iPod Touch 4G is compatible with Ubuntu, so I don't know why this is happening?

You could add songs with iTunes and the add more with Rhythmbox, but you cant delete the iTunes MP3's with Rhythmbox or visa versa.
