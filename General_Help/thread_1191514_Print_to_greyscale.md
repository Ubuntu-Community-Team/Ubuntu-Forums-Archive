### Post by redilyn on 2009-06-19
You can check the following:

With my deskjet printer you need to go System -> Administration -> Printing.

Right click on your printer and choose properties.

Look for Printer options.

Change Resolution, quality ink type to XXXDPI, Gray scale, Black catridge
