sorry my fault, I had to search the forum, 
searching the forum I solved my problem.

for those who have the same problem as me , 

1- right click on the Network monition Icon (u can add it to your panel )
2- click on properties
3- rename the "Name" to "eth0"
4- go to system-adminture- then click on networking , and now Activate your Eth0.
