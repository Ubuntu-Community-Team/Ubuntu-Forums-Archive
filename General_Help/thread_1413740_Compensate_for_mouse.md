### Post by llamabr on 2010-02-22
Couldn't you just slow down the mouse pointer speed?

Or, depending on which apps she's using, she could learn a couple of keyboard shortcuts.

In fact, Konqueror might be just the thing here.  In Konq, start on a page, and press ctrl, and it'll flash up a whole bunch of numbers and letters.  you pick the letter that's closest to the link you want to pick, instead of having to reach for the mouse.  excellent.

There are keyboard shortcuts for nearly everything else that she wants to do, too.  And with gnome-do, it's even easier.
