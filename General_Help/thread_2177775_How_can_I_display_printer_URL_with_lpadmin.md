### Post by slickymaster on 2013-09-30
Open up your cups via [http://localhost:631](http://localhost:631), navigate to the printers tab and examined the print queue of the desired printer.

See this for more info: [http://www.cups.org/documentation.php/network.html](http://www.cups.org/documentation.php/network.html)
