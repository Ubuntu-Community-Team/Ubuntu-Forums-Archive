### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-05-09
Installing themes is as simple as dragging the archive (usually tar.gz or tar.bz) to the Preferences > Appearance window.  To delete a theme, select it in the Theme tab of the Appearance window and click the Delete button.  To delete a background image, select it on the Background tab and click the Remove button.

### Post by collinp on 2009-05-10
To install new themes:
System -> Preferences -> Appearance. Click "Install" and navigate to the theme or drag-drop the theme into the window.

Themes can be found at: 
[http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/)
[http://art.gnome.org/themes](http://art.gnome.org/themes)
[http://customize.org/gtk](http://customize.org/gtk)

Great blog post on installing themes: [http://davestechsupport.com/blog/2009/03/02/how-to-install-new-themes-in-ubuntu/](http://davestechsupport.com/blog/2009/03/02/how-to-install-new-themes-in-ubuntu/)
