### Post by rrnbtter on 2014-06-03
Greetings,
Hopefully you installed with a journaled partition. Turn  off the computer and then reboot holding down the shift key. You should  get a boot menu. Select the previously installed kernel in Recovery  mode. From the recovery menu you can check your drives and also check  for software that may have failed to install. Just for your information  it pays to keep a copy of Puppy linux or something like it on a cd or  flashdrive. So that you can access the internet. I recommend Carolina  Puppy Linux. It has saved me more than a few times. Good luck!
