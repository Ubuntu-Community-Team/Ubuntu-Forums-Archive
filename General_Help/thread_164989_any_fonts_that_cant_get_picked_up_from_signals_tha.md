### Post by robglinka on 2006-04-24
Sort of, the intent is that he is trying to build a [Faraday Cage]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Faraday_cage"). There are many ways to build them, but the most popular way is to build a box with the walls covered in brass mesh screen. 

This will prevent almost all RF travelling through it. 

So if you really **really** ***really*** want to prevent people from spying on you. Staple brass mesh screen over all the walls and windows of the room with your computer in it, making sure they cover every inch of the walls/windows/doors/floor/ceiling. But don't expect your cell phone to work in there.

### Post by nanotube on 2006-04-24
[QUOTE=cosmoshell]hmm where can i get some of that mesh? cant find it on ebay.[/QUOTE]
just about any conductive material will serve just fine as a faraday shield. it doesn't have to be a "special mesh". so, e.g., aluminum window screening will make a fine faraday cage. 

you might want to start by reading the faraday cage article on wikipedia, and click on further to expand your understanding of the effect, before you start making your cage - or else you might end up making something completely ineffectual, if you don't understand the basic concepts. :)
