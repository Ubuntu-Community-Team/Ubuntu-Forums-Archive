### Post by autocrosser on 2007-04-19
I would say "if you DO NOT allow the packages to be installed" you can stop the upgrade--Be carefull, a partly installed upgrade is VERY unstable & most likely un-bootable. I also WOULD NOT reboot until you have carried out the full upgrade---just in case.
 
to kill the update you could pull up system monitor & kill update manager or in a terminal do: sudo killall update-manager
 
Or just wait it out & enjoy Feisty when update manager is done ;)
