### Post by Brandon Williams on 2009-05-05
I run the TACO addon (Targeted Advertising Cookie Opt-out). It handles Google and a bunch of others.

Also, you can check [here](http://www.networkadvertising.org/managing/opt_out.asp) and [here](http://www.privacychoice.org/) to see cookies that you've opted out of and attempt to opt out of others.
