### Post by -kg- on 2009-01-02
OK, number 1) - If you are trying Ubuntu by running from the LiveCD you might be able to connect to an open network, but you won't be able to enter a WEP or WPA key to connect to a secured network.  You must have Ubuntu installed to your computer in order to enter a security encryption key.

Look at your top taskbar to the right.  You should see an icon that looks like several side-by-side strength bars.  Left click on that icon and it will show you available networks as you describe.
