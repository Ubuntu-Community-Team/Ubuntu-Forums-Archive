### Post by spcwingo on 2008-12-11
Try uninstalling and deleting your personal settings folder.  To do that just uninstall pidgin, then open your home folder click view at the top and select show hidden files.  After that, delete the folder named .purple.  Finally reinstall Pidgin.  Let me know if that gets the job done.

Edit:
First just try renaming the folder named .purple to .purple.bak then restart pidgin.  If that doesn't work then just delete the new .purple and rename the .purple.bak back to .purple.  That way there is the changes are easily reversable.
