### Post by pseudonym on 2007-02-02
> **monomaniacpat said:**

It may just be the form of words you're using (or my eyesight :) ), but I don't see any evidence of what you describe in that screen shot.

Have you got font anti-aliasing turned on in your desktop settings? If not, turn it on. Also, some fonts that are supposed to be similar (eg. Sans and Bitstream Sans) can actually look quite different (in terms of ugliness) depending on what size you've set them at.

All I can suggest is to try different fonts and sizes until you find one which looks the best on that display.
