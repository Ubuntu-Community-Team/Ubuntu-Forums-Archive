### Post by TheFu on 2016-09-20
The easiest way I know is to use an email client - connect it to both email systems, then drag-n-drop the messages from WLM into whatever you are using on Linux.  If you switch to an IMAPS Linux email server, then every email client you have will have the same "view" of folders and emails. Very convenient.
