### Post by Alex Yeh on 2009-11-02
Hello, everyone.

Last year, there was a thread about a problem in Ubuntu ignoring "noauto" in fstab, which caused crashes and bootup problems when the system tried to mount a disk that wasn't there. This problem still exists in 9.10, so if you added something to your crontab recently with "noauto", take it back off and just mount it manually as needed, and those weird crashes will stop.
