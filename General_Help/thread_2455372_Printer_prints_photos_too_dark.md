### Post by guiverc on 2020-12-18
FYI: Lubuntu 16.04 LTS being a flavor of Ubuntu had only 3 years of supported life ([https://lubuntu.me/xenial-5-released/](https://lubuntu.me/xenial-5-released/) [http://fridge.ubuntu.com/2019/03/01/ubuntu-16-04-6-lts-released/](http://fridge.ubuntu.com/2019/03/01/ubuntu-16-04-6-lts-released/)) which ended 2019-April.

 Ubuntu 16.04 LTS Server (no desktop) or Ubuntu 16.04 LTS Desktop (Unity 7), and Ubuntu Kylin 16.04 LTS have 5 years of supported life and are still supported.

 Refer release notes, or use `ubuntu-support-status` or your own system to confirm this is the case. I suggest you move to a supported release of Lubuntu for security reasons, unless you're off-line or are aware of risks.
