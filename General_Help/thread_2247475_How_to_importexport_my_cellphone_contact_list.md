### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-10-08
Where do you want to export the contact list to?  Most cellphone providers can read the SIM card or memory card and transfer numbers to a new phone.

Your phone can store 1,000 contacts on a microSD card (up to 8 GB in size).  Simply take the card out, put it in a SD card adapter and then use a USB SD card reader or built-in reader in a printer or laptop.  Copy the files over to Linux and then open them up.  Most contact files are in a standard [vCard]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VCard") format that can be imported into other contact programs.

Sometimes contacts are stored in a database (like mysql) and only a single database file is available.  In that case, you need to export your contacts to a file using an export tool on the phone.  Samsung probably has a contact manager for Windows.
