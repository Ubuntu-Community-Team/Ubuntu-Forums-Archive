### Post by ru-ff on 2009-05-07
In case you haven't found it yet:
It's because of window-picker-applet (The applet is expaned on the panel).

First, you have to change Gnome theme from "Human-Netbook" to see what you do with the panel.
Right click in the middle of the panel, uncheck "Lock To Panel" option, then "Move" it to the right to get some empty space on the panel.
Now you can do "Add to Panel" and so on...
