### Post by Nano Geek on 2006-04-24
All you need to do is to go into Synaptic and search for and install the package automatix. After that go **Applications>System Tools>Automatix**, after you enter your password, put a check mark by **Open Office** and Click **OK**. Automatix will upgrade Open Office for you. After it's done just run Open Office like normal.
