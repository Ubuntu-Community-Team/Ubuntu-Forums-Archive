### Post by carstanje on 2007-11-16
Usb disk mounts works easily in gutsy, just configure your wm5/wm6 device to connect for mass-storage instead of activesync. This will mount your storagecard just like any other usb storage device to ubuntu.

You can find this setting in the phone at:
*Settings > Connections > USB > USB Connection setting*

Syncing with synce USB is still a nogo. Has anyone have good experience with bluetooth on synchronising?
