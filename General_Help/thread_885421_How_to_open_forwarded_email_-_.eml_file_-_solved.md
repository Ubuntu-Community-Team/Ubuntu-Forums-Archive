### Post by djringjr on 2008-08-10
I found an easy way to open an "eml" attachment.

Save the .eml file, then I used Seamonkey to open it by using "open with" and it opened right up.

There was a area with a blank inside - the frame of a picture.

I right clicked and choose "show picture" and the picture showed.

This was discussed since 2005 and this is the easiest way to do it.
