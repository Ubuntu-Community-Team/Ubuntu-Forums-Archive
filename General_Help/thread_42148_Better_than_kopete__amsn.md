### Post by lorenzo on 2005-06-16
Gaim, without any doubt!

It's one of the most active projects on sourceforge.net. It improves gradually but constantly. Best look of all under gnome and very well integrated (minimize to tray, show little alert boxes to announce you have received messages).

the only problem is if you use yahoo behind a strict firewall. MSN a little better behind firewall (you have a "connect through http" option).

There's also a branch of it called gaim-vv that support voice and webcam (haven't tried it lately, don't know if it's good).

Ciao,
Lorenzo
