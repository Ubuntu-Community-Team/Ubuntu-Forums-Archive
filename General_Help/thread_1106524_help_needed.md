### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-03-25
Ouch.  There are a lot of important files in etc, and trying to restore them short of a reinstallation of Ubuntu is really impractical.  (This is why you should be extremely careful when using superuser privileges!)

While reinstallation is not generally considered a solution, I think in this case that your best course of action is to backup your data to some sort of external storage (e.g. an external hard drive, DVDs, etc.) and reinstall Ubuntu.
