### Post by jimmygoon on 2006-07-25
I dunna if you know, but its almost guarenteedly not ubuntu's fault. If the computer is not powering on at all... then there is some hardware problem.

Take the battery out, take the power cord out, and hold down the power button for 30 seconds... Thats supposed to help clear out any built up static charges. Also try plugging it in with no battery and then booting. It sounds like something is toast!
