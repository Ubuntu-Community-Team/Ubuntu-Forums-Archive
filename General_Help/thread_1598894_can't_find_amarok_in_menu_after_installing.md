### Post by claracc on 2010-10-17
You can make a launcher and put it in apps --> sound and video:

You go to system --> preferences-->main menu, in the left window, select sound an video, new element, in the pop up window you fill the fields, in command you can look for where is amarok, usually in /usr/bin, click ok and now you have amarok in the menu
