I just went from a Chocolate to a Boulder as well. I've been toying with this exact same issue for the last week. As far as I can tell, the developers at BitPim haven't created a version of the program that will work with the Boulder yet. The same USB cord you used for your Chocolate will work, but there's no way to manipulate the information on the phone (as of yet). The correct USB driver (for Windows, at least) is located on the Verizon website. 
Your best bet: Use a MicroSD card to move files between your computer and phone. I pulled the old card out of my Chocolate and it recognized my pics, videos, and music no prob. 

Hope this helps...
