Several years ago I was thinking about a scanner, but never got around to it.

Then I decided to buy a portable computer and at the time Fry's threw in a printer which I did not particularly want as my printer was ok. The "free" printer was a cheap Canon MP-160 with scanner. Three years ago I had real trouble finding a print driver and scanning was barely functional.

I do not do a lot of printing so this printer & scanner combination has been fine for me and I like only having one hardware device to plug in and take up desk space.

Hewlett Packard is almost always good. You should also have a look at the Epson V300. Scan quality is good (although I don't know about transparencies); it uses uses LED's for quick warmup; and the scanner can be purchased directly from Epson for $79.99. Deb software is available although some users have encountered problems. Also, build quality is a bit marginal. Do a forum search on Epson 300 for more information.
