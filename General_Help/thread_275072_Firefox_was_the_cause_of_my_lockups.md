### Post by pjbgravely on 2006-10-10
After chasing a hardware problem that caused program and kernel crashes, I finally was able to get another motherboard and I thought I was set. 

Then after X locked up. This was different than before, because the crash always occurred while using Firefox and no other programs including X crashed. This was a full lockup, with keyboard and mouse stopping, requiring a power cycle to restore. I decided to install Firefox 2.0 from a script in the forums and to my surprise the lockups stopped. I am now on 5 days 10 hours uptime with no problems. I have not filed a bug report because I don't have any specific information. I hope this helps anyone who has X lockups.
