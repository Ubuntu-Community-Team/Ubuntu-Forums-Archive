### Post by stefangr1 on 2008-03-28
I estimate 15 minutes till 1 hour @max depending how fast your disk is and how much data is on the part of the partition that has to be freed. However, I would recommend to start with repartition only on a moment that you have enough time, so that you can leave it alone for a few hours.

And (I probably don't have to say this to you, but better safe then sorry) be careful to back up all important data, since resizing is risky and you lose everything when the pc crashes during the process.
