### Post by kanikilu on 2010-03-04
Is this a local (e.g. USB) printer, or a network/shared printer?

A couple things you might want to check:

1) This shouldn't be required to print, I don't think, but are you in the **lpadmin** group? Check Users & Groups or just type *groups* in a terminal.

2) Go to the printer configuration, System > Administration > Printing, right-click on the printer and go to Properties and then go to Access Control and see if anything looks out of whack there...
