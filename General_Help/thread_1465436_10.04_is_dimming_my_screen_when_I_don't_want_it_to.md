### Post by scouser73 on 2010-04-29
Install the Caffeine PPA which will keep your system from going into powersaving mode. The most time it can be up for is 168 hours & 59 minutes - [https://launchpad.net/~caffeine-developers/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~caffeine-developers/+archive/ppa)
