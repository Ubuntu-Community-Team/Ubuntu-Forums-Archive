### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2012-01-24
tyrian,

I use GRAMPS. Some of the features and screenshots are at [http://www.gramps-project.org/wiki/index.php?title=Features](http://www.gramps-project.org/wiki/index.php?title=Features).

I have been using GRAMPS for about 4 years. 

I imported a gedcom file I had from a Widows based genealogy database into it with very few problems (I don't remember if it was Ancestry or Family Tree Maker). The problems I had were because I use the Windows based genealogy database in ways that were not standard. I had added custom flags to identify thing; such as, untrustworthy data so I wouldn't post it on-line. The problems were no different than the problems encountered with switching between other genealogy programs. 

Geneweb and Lifelines were not mature enough for my needs 4 years ago when I made my choice; so, I don't have an opinion about how well they work today. I'm not going to switch programs unless I have a very good reason. There is a lot of data; about 15 generations, I would have to check for errors introduced by switching program.
