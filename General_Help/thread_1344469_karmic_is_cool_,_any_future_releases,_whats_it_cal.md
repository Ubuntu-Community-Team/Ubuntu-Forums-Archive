### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-12-03
Lucid Lynx pre-Alpha testing has begun.  Right now, Ubuntu is just syncing up with Debian, meaning they are pulling in TONS of unstable software every day.  If you want to take a look, you can get the ISO from:
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily-live/current/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily-live/current/)
And either just test it as a live CD or install it to a virtualbox.  

If you really want to stick with that, I highly recommend syncing up your daily CD image with zsync (scroll to the bottom of this page):
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RsyncCdImage](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RsyncCdImage)
