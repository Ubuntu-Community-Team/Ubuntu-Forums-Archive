### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-03-19
Welcome to the forums.

Has the laptop been dropped?  If so, then the heat sink could be loose.  If the machine is over 5 years old, it may need new heat-sink paste.  The cooling vanes may be clogged with dust.  Perhaps blow some compressed air to clean them out.

Otherwise, put some temperature indicators on your panel so you can see what the cpu temperatures are.
