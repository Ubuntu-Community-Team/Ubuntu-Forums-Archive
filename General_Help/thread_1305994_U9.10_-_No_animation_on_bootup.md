### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-30
The ubuntu logo pulsates during boot.

The brightness is probably set too high on your monitor and it can't reproduce subtle brightness changes accurately.

This is a very common problem.

Go through the steps in [this calibration page]("http://www.normankoren.com/makingfineprints1A.html") and everything you do on your PC will be ... better.
