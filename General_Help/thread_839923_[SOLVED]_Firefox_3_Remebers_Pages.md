### Post by solwic on 2008-06-24
When I first installed Ubuntu Hardy Heron, Firefox 3 came with a really cool feature:  it would remember what pages (tabs) I had open when I shut down Firefox, and would reload them when I booted up the program next time.

For some reason, my Firefox isn't doing that anymore, and I can't find the setting to tell it to do so.

Thanks!

### Post by undertakingyou on 2008-06-24
while in firefox go to edit->Preferences and on the main tab where you can choose your homepage, you can do the dropdown and select firfox to start with your last session.
