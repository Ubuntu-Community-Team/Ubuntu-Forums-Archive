60 degrees C is way too hot for most computer parts. A general rule of thumb is... if you can't keep your finger on it, it's too hot.

Running that hot will shorten the life of your computer's components.

Check to make sure your cooling vents and heat sink fins are not clogged with dust, pull the cover off your computer and make sure all the fans are spinning and add more fans if you have to.

And, no you don't need kiddie-tech liquid cooling baloney... just decent airflow and decent heatsinks.
