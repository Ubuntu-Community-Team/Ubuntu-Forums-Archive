### Post by jcsteele on 2007-12-01
Then PLEASE make sure you defragment your hard drive in windows BEFORE installing ubuntu.

PLEASE make sure to make BACKUPS of ANY AND ALL data you may have on your system.

Now, during the installation it should ask you how you would like your partitioning to be completed...just tell it to resize the existing partition, which will "shrink" your existing partition to make room for ubuntu (I personally would use 50% but thats just my opinion)...afterwards just continue following the instructions to install ubuntu.  Your going to be limited to a minimum size, yes....

If you do not feel comfortable doing this, I would not attempt it....any mistake can (and will) result in dataloss.

//jcs
