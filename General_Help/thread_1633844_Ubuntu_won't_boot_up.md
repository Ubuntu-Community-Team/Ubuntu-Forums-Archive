### Post by drs305 on 2010-11-30
If you are having video problems with the Ubuntu LiveCD, as it boots and you see the two icons appear at the bottom middle of the screen press F6.

Press ESC from the Language screen, then F6 again. You will see some kernel options which you can select. One of them might help. I believe the 'nomodeset' option may be helpful. 

After pressing F6 the second time, you can also press ESC and add your own options to the boot line if you know what you need in the way of special instructions.
