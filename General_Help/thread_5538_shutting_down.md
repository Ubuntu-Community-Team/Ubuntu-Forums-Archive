### Post by Tobster on 2006-11-10
Me too but I dont think it a problem with the OS because I have had issues with the old Windows days.  I fixed it but the problem started a few day later.  

But this should do it if there too much power in system (which could one of two reasons)

Follows these steps to see if it will help:

1) Shut down your PC

2)  Fully dis-connect it including  your Keyboard Monitor Printer and the power plug  

The PC should now be sitting on its own without nothing attached it important that the power plug is fully removed.

3) Now press the ON/OFF button on the PC but keep it pressed in and hold it for 1 Min 

This will discharge any build up of power that is inside your PC.  

4) Reconnect your PC and boot up and shut down to see if the problem is fixed.
