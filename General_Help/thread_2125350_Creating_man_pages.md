### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2013-03-13
Manual pages are written in a markup language (usually some form of **roff*, I believe), then compressed and installed in the system manual path under the correct section.

[http://www.linuxhowtos.org/System/creatingman.htm](http://www.linuxhowtos.org/System/creatingman.htm) is quite helpful, although it took some finding.

Good luck!
