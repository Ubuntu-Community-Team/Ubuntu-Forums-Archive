### Post by ruffneck on 2006-04-11
You'll have to mount the shared volume (folder) using samba first. Then you will be able to play the MP3 files directly.

Look Here: 

[http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#mountunmountnetworkfoldersall](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#mountunmountnetworkfoldersall)
