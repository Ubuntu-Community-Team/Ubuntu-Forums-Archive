### Post by echo314 on 2008-07-13
Just checking...you didn't update while *running* the LiveCD, did you? :)

Its strange after an update your wallpaper would have changed, unless you did not make the change while using the HD. When using the LiveCD, any settings you use are temporary, and even if you installed ubuntu, you are *still* using the LiveCD UNTIL you reboot WITHOUT the cd in the comp. This is the first time you are booting off the harddrive, and thus saving settings to the comp that will be more permanent. 

Hope that made sense.
