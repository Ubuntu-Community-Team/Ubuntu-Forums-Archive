### Post by timgood on 2011-07-01
There is no way to 'roll back', but you can backup all your essential files and folders (I recommend DejaDup for this) and re-install 10.10 or whatever. For what it is worth, I had the same issues. Interestingly, the popularity of Ubuntu on Distrowatch has plummeted while Mint and Fedora are on the rise. That say it all, really.

Hope this helps.
