### Post by Ascenti0n on 2009-09-29
[solved - SOLUTION BELOW]

For those who may also be interested, the issue and solution, rested within Compiz.

within the "Window Management" section, I:

1. turned off "snapping Windows"
2. within "place windows", I changed the "placement mode" option to centre.

Now I'm a happy bunny.
