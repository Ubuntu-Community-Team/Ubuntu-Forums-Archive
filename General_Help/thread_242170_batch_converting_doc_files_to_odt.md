### Post by matthew on 2006-08-23
LOL!

It's already a feature in OpenOffice!! Who knew?

Open Writer (or Calc, etc.) and look in File->Wizards->Document Converter.

I just tested it on a small folder filled with 6 old doc files and it converted them all perfectly! Simple, easy, and it worked. Very cool.
