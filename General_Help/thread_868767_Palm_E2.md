### Post by DJ_Peng on 2008-07-24
There should be a folder under your \home called something like \MyPDA. To confirm the location open the GNOME-Pilot Settings (System > Preferences > PalmOS Devices), on the first tab (PDA) select your PDA and click **Edit...** At the bottom of the window that will come up shows the local folder where files for your PDA are stored.
