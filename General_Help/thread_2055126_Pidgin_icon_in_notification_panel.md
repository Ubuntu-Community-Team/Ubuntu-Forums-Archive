### Post by claracc on 2012-09-09
I understand you are using gnome classic (without effects) as desktop.

In this case, to resize panel, you alt+right click in any empty place in the panel, it appears the menu, select properties, and in the general tab you can resize the panel.

Pidgin will appear in the complete indicator mini-app which you can enable with alt+right click in panel and selecting ad to panel.
