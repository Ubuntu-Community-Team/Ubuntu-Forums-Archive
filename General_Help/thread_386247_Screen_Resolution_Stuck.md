### Post by tcrroadie on 2007-03-16
Try changing your available resolution in Section "Screen"

First backup your xorg.conf file.

```
 sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.conf_old
```

Change this 

```
Section "Screen"
Identifier "Default Screen"
Device "NVIDIA Corporation NV17 [GeForce4 MX 420]"
Monitor "EV730"
DefaultDepth 24
SubSection "Display"
depth 24
virtual 640 480
modes "640x480@60"
EndSubSection
EndSection
```

To this.

```
Section "Screen"
Identifier "Default Screen"
Device "NVIDIA Corporation NV17 [GeForce4 MX 420]"
Monitor "EV730"
DefaultDepth 24
SubSection     "Display"
        Depth       24
        Modes      "1280x1024_70"
    EndSubSection
EndSection
```

The _70 after your screen resolution mode, forces xserver to use a vertical frame rate of 70Hz.

This should do the trick for you.
