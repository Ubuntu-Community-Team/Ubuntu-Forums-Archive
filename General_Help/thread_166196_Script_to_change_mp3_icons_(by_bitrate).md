### Post by jazzmuzik on 2006-04-26
You might try moving up to flac files. Bitrate doesn't matter then.

It occurs to me that the bitrate thing with mp3ext wasn't totally accurate. I mean, if a file was encoded as mbr and the first audio frame was compressed with a 56kbps rate, that gives a false impression of the quality of the file. Most of the file may be 320 bps, but the icon would show as 56 bps.
