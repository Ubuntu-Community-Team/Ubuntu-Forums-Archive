### Post by dewclaw82 on 2010-08-04
I tried the System > Preferences > Power and > Screensaver and tried the only two things that looked like they might help.  I added a screensaver - hadn't done so yet - and in the Power section I unchecked the item for "spin down disk whenever possible."  This is a desktop machine so I hadn't paid too much attention to the power settings before.  

I know it's scientifically inaccurate to try changing two things at once but I'm already having an annoying day and I don't want any sass from my computer.
