### Post by brentcarter on 2013-12-26
There are many image viewers to choose as the default. Both [[COLOR=#000000]Windows image viewer[/COLOR]]("http://www.rasteredge.com/how-to/vb-net-imaging/create-windows-viewer/") and [[COLOR=#000000]Web image viewer[/COLOR]]("http://www.rasteredge.com/how-to/vb-net-imaging/create-web-viewer/") are available. With an image viewer installed, users can  efficiently perform basic image management such as loading & opening  an image, zooming in and out an image, rotating and flipping an image,  printing & saving an image to your local file.
