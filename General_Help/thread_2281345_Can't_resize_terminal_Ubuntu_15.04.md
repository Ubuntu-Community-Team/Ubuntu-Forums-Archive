### Post by promet on 2015-06-07
I'm having this problem too. Try right-clicking in the top window bar (not the menu bar, but the actual window bar itself) and choosing "resize". This allows me to draw the window back out. It will still act weird on moving the window, but at least you can regain *some *control. Also, try pulling the window to very top of your desktop to allow the "auto-sticky-fullscreen" which may help as well. Please let me know if you come across a legit fix for this though, if you would...

Best!
