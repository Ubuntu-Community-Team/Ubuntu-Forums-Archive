### Post by plucky on 2009-06-17
Have you changed your keyboard layout?

Go to **System > Preferences > Keyboard** and see what default layout is set.
Try deleting the layout and setting it again to see if that helps.

Was getting this with Hardy Heron and that was what I did to fix it.

Good Luck
