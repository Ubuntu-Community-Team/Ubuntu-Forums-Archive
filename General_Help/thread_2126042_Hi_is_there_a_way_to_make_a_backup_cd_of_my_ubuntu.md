### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-03-16
Just so you know, Clonezilla allows you to make an image of your install.  It creates a compressed file that, if small enough, you can  write to a disk.  However, it doesn't "install" when you do the restore, instead, it overwrites the partition on your drive from the saved image.  You will need to burn a CD of Clonezilla and boot from that, in order to restore using it.  There is more information at the Clonezilla website.
