### Post by Jonor on 2014-09-01
If it is a fresh install, updating software may fix it :

Enter in a terminal :

```
sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get upgrade
```

and provide your password.

Otherwise the [Audacity Forum]("http://forum.audacityteam.org/") is the best way of getting free help.
You will have to register and then be patient as newcomer's questions are 
subject to delays as an anti-spam measure; well worth the wait though.
