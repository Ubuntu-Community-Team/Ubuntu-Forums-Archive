### Post by c0met on 2008-01-24
I have Ubuntu 7.10 and Wine works fine. Another way to remove Wine is to open the Synaptic Package Manager (System >> Administration) and search for Wine. You then just need to right-click on the name and select "Remove" or "Remove All". I'd suggest going for the "Remove" option.
