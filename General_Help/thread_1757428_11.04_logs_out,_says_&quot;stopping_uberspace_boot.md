### Post by xrat on 2011-05-20
Hi ghostcatzero,
The message you see ("stopping ...") is just some message from boot time which is still on the text console. You happen to see it because your X crashes, shortly switches to the text console and then restarts.

See [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10840729](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10840729) for a similar thread. The logfiles you need to look at are .xsession-errors and .xsession-errors.old (both in your home directory) and the various Xorg.* files in /var/log.

As I said in the other thread, it helps a lot if you find some pattern, things you did, times passed, etc. that seem to trigger the crash.

HTH.
