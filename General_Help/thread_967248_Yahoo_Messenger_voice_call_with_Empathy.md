### Post by shishirjaiswal on 2008-11-02
Although Empathy audio capabilities are awesome, it is only working now with GTalk / Jabber protocols (at least for me), and I’m sure with SIP protocols it’ll also work well.

If you need to voice chat on yahoo, you can try Gyche. ([http://gyachi.sourceforge.net/](http://gyachi.sourceforge.net/))

Empathy is still in development and we can hope A/V capabilities are extended to other protocols too soon.
