### Post by Shazaam on 2008-10-14
If it is a .deb it should be listed in Synaptic (System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager). Open Synaptic, click the "Status" box on the left. Find the program, highlight it then a left-click will open a drop down list. Choose "Mark for complete removal" then hit apply.
