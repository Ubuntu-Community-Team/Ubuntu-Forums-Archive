Hi Bilboe,
The Live CD does contain tools (such as Partition Editor) that can be used to completely trash your computer if you don't know what you're doing. :) However, it is designed not to alter your computer in any way, unless you specifically instruct it to. 

Obviously I can't make any guarantees--I'm just some guy on the internet after all--but running the Ubuntu Live CD is a very low-risk activity. Is that risk 0.0000000%? Probably not.

ps Do you know how to use md5 checksum to verify the integrity of your Live CD? This will guarantee there weren't any errors in downloading or burning that will transform your Ubuntu Live CD into a hard-drive-shredding instrument of destruction! :)
