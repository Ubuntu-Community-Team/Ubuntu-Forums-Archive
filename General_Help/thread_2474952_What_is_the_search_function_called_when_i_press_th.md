The official terms used are all explained here:
[https://wiki.gnome.org/Projects/GnomeShell/Design](https://wiki.gnome.org/Projects/GnomeShell/Design)

In particular, the Search Box you describe is just called "Search". If you want to differentiate it from other searches, you can call it the "Overview Search" since it is part of the "Activities Overview".

By the way, the search here will not search for files on your entire computer. Only your home folder is searched.
