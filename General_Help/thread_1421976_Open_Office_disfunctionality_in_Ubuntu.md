### Post by Hagar Delest on 2010-03-06
Firs try to [reset your OOo user profile](http://user.services.openoffice.org/en/forum/viewtopic.php?p=58403#p58403) but don't transfer your personal data during the welcome process (if you had 2.x before), configuration files from the former version might corrupt the new profile.

If no change, try the Sun version: [[Ubuntu] Installing OOo on Debian and Co.](http://user.services.openoffice.org/en/forum/viewtopic.php?f=16&t=68)
