### Post by detroit/zero on 2008-11-14
If you're using VLC, go to Settings> Preferences, and expand the Video section. Go to the Output Modules and switch to the Advanced Options in the bottom right corner. You'll be given a drop-down menu of selections - Default, OpenGL, etc... - switch this to **X11 Video Output**.

That should give you what you're after. Be warned: This setting gives me troubles when I watch a video with a separate subtitle file. When I watch something with subtitles, I have to switch back to default mode so the text is readable.
