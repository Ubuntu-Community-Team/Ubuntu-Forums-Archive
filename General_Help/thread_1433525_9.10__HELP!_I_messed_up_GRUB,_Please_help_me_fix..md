### Post by mridkash on 2010-03-19
This happened to me once, I added a new drive to system and after that noting booted. I found that the numbering of hard drives in the BIOS is important.
The number or order of your old hardrive where ubuntu is installed should be the same as before. Check that.

The problem appears when the device names of harddrives change. /dev/sda can become /dev/sdb on changing drives in system.
