1.  Laptops suspend and hibernate features are highly dependent on the brand, and in most cases aren't fully working.  This is because laptop companies don't open up their specs for linux ppl, so the linux ppl have to reverse engineer everything.  I would recommend searching google for suspend/hibernate and ubuntu, and if your laptop is relatively common, im sure you can find a solution to somewhat solve your problem.

2.  I don't know what you mean by "ants," maybe your laptop screen is burnt up for playing video games for extended periods of time.  Could be a hardware issue?
