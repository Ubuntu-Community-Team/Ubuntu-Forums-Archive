### Post by ltmon on 2005-12-07
The two I have used are:

MrProject: Similar in look and feel to MS Project, but not as powerful.  By the looks of it there hasn't been much development in a while.  I think it's now part of Gnome Office: [http://www.gnome.org/gnome-office/mrproject.shtml](http://www.gnome.org/gnome-office/mrproject.shtml)

TaskJuggler: Super powerful and (for me) a joy to use.  It's very good on resource utilisation (i.e. how much is a contractor charging, when are people on leave, how much time per day/week/month are they dedicating to a project).  Of course there's a down side: it's a text-based entry format.  This is actually quite brilliant once you get used to it, but it definately has a learning curve.  [http://www.taskjuggler.org/](http://www.taskjuggler.org/)
