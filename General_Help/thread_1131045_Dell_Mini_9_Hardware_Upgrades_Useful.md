### Post by OmaSteak on 2009-04-20
Hello,
My Dell Mini 9 running Ubuntu came with 512 Mb of ram and a 4 GB SSD.  I see that I can upgrade both the ram (2 Gb) and SSD (16 Gb) for less than $100.  My question is a a new Ubuntu user, would either/both of these upgrades do that much to improve the Dell Mini's performance?  I use the Mini for checking email, light web browsing and occasionally taking notes in a meeting using OpenOffice Writer.  I've read in the Dell Mini 9 forum that these upgrades are almost basic requirement for conversion to XP.  Thanks for your helpful advice.

PS - I had posted previously about having trouble configuring my wireless network connection.  I discovered it was an interference problem due to my use of an AngelTrax cable-to-wireless TV system.  I shutoff the transmitter side of the TV system and the Ubuntu wireless network connection manager worked perfectly.
