### Post by wallyxwest on 2009-04-23
You could use the service code on your laptop to find the drivers using a different computer. Go to the dell website and enter the service code after getting to the drivers and support sections of their website.

Download the drivers and burn them to a cd or put them on a USB stick and use that. 

I had the same problem when reinstalling windows and ended up putting all the drivers on a USB stick and setting them up from there. Then I reinstalled Xubuntu from an alternate install CD to dual-boot. Everything is working okay so far.

Good luck!

EDIT: also forgot to mention, if you don't mind dealing with support hotlines, you could call Dell and get them to send you CD's for your system. I am sure they have something they could send you with drivers and everything already set up.
