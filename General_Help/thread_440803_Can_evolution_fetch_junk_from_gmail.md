### Post by heimo on 2007-05-12
To me, it looks like it's impossible. There's no way to disable gmails spam filter (which would cause spam to go to the inbox) and gmail doesn't support imap so that you could subscribe to spam folder.

Even forwarding gmail posts won't give you the posts marked as spam.
