The menu you are talking about is called a "Dock". It simulates the desktop environment that is used in Mac OSX.  You can install Cairo-Dock from here - [http://www.cairo-dock.org/ww_page.php?p=From%20the%20repository&lang=en](http://www.cairo-dock.org/ww_page.php?p=From%20the%20repository&lang=en)

Cairo-Dock has a lot of effects and animations for the icons. Also the themes are super!
