### Post by HermanAB on 2022-01-22
Well, my standard advice with an unreliable desktop system is to First get a good UPS.  That will usually make 99.9% of problems go away. (This is the reason why laptops work so well - the UPS is built in).  If you still have a problem, look for a newer version of the ethernet and wifi drivers.  It is also possible to revert the device drivers to an older version, but I haven’t needed to do that in decades.
