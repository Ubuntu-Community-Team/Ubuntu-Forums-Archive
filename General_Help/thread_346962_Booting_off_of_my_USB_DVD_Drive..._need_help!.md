### Post by citizenofnowhere on 2007-01-26
Does your BIOS support this?

Get into your bios, and look around for a setting that says something along the lines of "boot order". You should have a hard disk or two in there, a cd-rom drive, and (if booting off usb is supported) removable media.

You need to give first choice to removable media then try reboot.

If that doesn't help or if the removable media option isn't there, you'll need a BIOS upgrade. generally, you'll find bios upgrades on your pc manufecturer site (if you got one from compaq or dell etc.) I 've done this dance before with a Compaq machine and the upgrade was barely a few months older and didn't really help. 

If you built the pc yourself, or if your pc manufacturer refers you elsewhere, you'll need to figure out what kind of motherboard you have, brand and model (there's a million howtos out there on how to figure that out) and then look for it. 

But be careful - if something goes wrong when you flash your BIOS it won't be pretty :)

Good luck!!
