### Post by hikaricore on 2008-12-13
As the previous poster mentioned, downgrading is generally not an option.

If you don't wish to reinstall, sound can usally be fixed quite easily there are dozens of guides around the forum for fixing sound.
The version of firefox in Intrepid has been called buggy and unstable by many.  Have you tried downloading one of the latest Linux builds from the firefox website?  You may have better luck with a fresh build.
As for the webcam you could always post a thread about it or search for threads with similar issues, I've no doubt it could be resolved instead of a reinstall.
