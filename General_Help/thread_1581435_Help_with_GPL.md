### Post by perspectoff on 2010-09-25
Look at the LGPL.

Medical devices (which includes any software used for diagnosis or treatment) also need FDA approval in the US and CE approval in the European Union. Be diligent about both.

You could also use the Apache license or BSD license.

Here's a thumbnail summary:

[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Lucid#Licenses](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Lucid#Licenses)
