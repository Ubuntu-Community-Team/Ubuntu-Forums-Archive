No, you cant upgrade from Dapper to Feisty. You need to go about first upgrading Dapper to Edgy and then Edgy to Feisty .

Just do this from your terminal,

```
sudo update-manager -d -c
```, It will show for Edgy and than after upgrading, do again same thing , it will show for Feisty.

Enjoy :D
