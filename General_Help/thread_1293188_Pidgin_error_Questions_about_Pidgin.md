### Post by mocoloco on 2009-10-16
I haven't seen issues like that, and don't take this as a cop out but since Ubuntu is moving to Empathy from Karmic forward I've switched to using Empathy on my main system.  It's about the same as pidgin right now but they're doing more work toward integrating webcam, the Karmic version will do video chat for google talk, and they're actively working on AIM and MSN video chat.
Ironically Pidgin has *finally* started working on frameworks for video just as Ubuntu is switching away from it as default, but it's too little too late, and it's not as active as empathy's so I think it was a good switch.
