It looks like most error reports today are linked to grldr. The bad news is that none of us so far has been involved in grub4dos so we can do little once the most common cases are excluded. 

The good news is that I have just discovered that the project has migrated to [https://gna.org/projects/grub4dos/](https://gna.org/projects/grub4dos/) (I was monitoring sourceforge which did not show much activity recently) and new builds are available there. We will test them soon. 

You can already play with the new grldr by downloading the latest build (at the moment [http://download.gna.org/grub4dos/grub4dos-0.4.3-2007-05-09.zip](http://download.gna.org/grub4dos/grub4dos-0.4.3-2007-05-09.zip) ) and replace the grub and grldr files in C:\. Please let us know if it works or not.

I will also try to get in touch with grub4dos devs and see if they can help us out fixing/debugging grldr issues.
