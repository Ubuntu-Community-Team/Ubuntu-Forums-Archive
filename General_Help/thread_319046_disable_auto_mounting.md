### Post by paridoth on 2006-12-15
i am trying to create and format multiple partitions with gparted on my usb flash drive, however every time there is change to the drive (partition getting created) the drive automatically remounts itself and interrupts any other processes. how can i temporarily disable the auto mounter so i can use gparted, and then re enable it after i am done?

---

### Post by aysiu on 2006-12-15
I ran into that same problem--it's annoying.

Go to System > Preferences > Removable Drives and Media and uncheck the box about automatically mounting drives. Leave the preferences window open until you're done with GParted. At that point, you can check the box again and close the window.
