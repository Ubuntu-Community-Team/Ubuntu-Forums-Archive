### Post by dayvy on 2007-10-23
So, you have your panels in one work space and not the others. Are you using visual effects (i.e. compiz). Try disabling them and then switching between your work spaces to see if your panels reappear. That way you'll know where to begin looking. In the mean time you should be able to switch between workspaces using Ctrl + Alt + left or right arrow keys.

> **formula13x said:**

SOLVED!

Right click on the Workspace Switcher in the bottom right hand corner. Select Preferences, and enter the number of columns and rows. Mine was set to 4 columns and 1 row. When I changed it to 4 columns AND 4 rows, I was able to navigate all four desktops with no problems.

Wee Hoo!
