### Post by inameiname on 2010-05-18
Compiz comes installed by default. However, to tweak it, best to:

sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager


I recommend downloading Ubuntu-Tweak, and then adding the repositories for those programs that you want as they are not available in the official ones. (You can also install compizconfig through Ubuntu Tweak as well.)
