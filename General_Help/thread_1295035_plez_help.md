### Post by Yogotiss on 2009-10-19
You have to be more specific... What are the specs on the machine? When you say "net" meaning wireless or ether? If ether... right click the network icon and configure it to auto. Or if you know the DNS,NET MASK, GATEWAY and have a static IP to use, just set the ether to use a static address. If wireless... you may have to click "SYSTEM-ADMINISTRATOR-HARDWARE DRIVERS" to check to see if drivers are available for you wireless card. If so just click the activate button.
