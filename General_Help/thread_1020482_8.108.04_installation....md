Removing your 7.10 installation is simple enough, all you need to do is backup any files you may wish to hang on to and then delete the partition in Gparted (It's best to do this from a live disk).  With the free space created you can increase the size of your 8.10 partition, or create a new partition.

Screenshots are again simple - go to System » Preferences » Keyboard Shortcuts.
Look for the entries under Desktop which say 'Take a screenshot' and 'Take a screenshot of a window', make sure they are enabled (on my system they are set to PrtScr and Alt+PrtScr respectively. You can use other shortcuts if you prefer, but these seem to be the most sensible).

The screenshot dialog gives you the option to copy data to the clipboard, which can be pasted into the graphic app of your choice, or you can save the image as a PNG file.
