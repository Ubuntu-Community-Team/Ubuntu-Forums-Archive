### Post by cjhabs on 2010-03-30
I am not sure how to disable the middle mouse button, but you can reconfigure it as follows:
xmodmap -e "pointer = 1 4 3 2"

You can find how many functions your mouse buttons can have with:
xmodmap -pp

If you use, for example, value 9, then you must define 9 buttons in your xmodmap command e.g.
xmodmap -e "pointer = 1 9 3 4 5 6 7 8 2"

This can be placed in your startup applications.
