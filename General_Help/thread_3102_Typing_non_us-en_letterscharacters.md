### Post by adbak on 2004-11-03
It's very easy.  Right click on your panel and click Add to Panel.  Scroll down to Keyboard Indicator.  Then right click and add whatever languages you want.

Whenever you want to type in Spanish, just click the "us" button and it'll change to "es".
