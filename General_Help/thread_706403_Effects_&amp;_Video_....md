### Post by thecorleonefamily on 2008-02-24
Ok I am also having the same chipset and the same problem. X11 video output looks horrible. Right? So here is a solution. I discovered it yesterday by chance!

You can temporarily disable Compiz and change the output to xv to watch the video. This works best. 

However, try this (what I found and I use!)

Enable Desktop Zoom in ccsm. 

Then start the video with output as X11 and Compiz enabled. Zoom in on the video screen using SUPER (Windows key) + Scroll Mouse Button . :) Voila! :guitar: The video is enlarged and yet the quality is good! 

Try it if it works!
