### Post by rwe061 on 2015-06-15
Running lubuntu, Desktop machine, dual monitors, 14.04 was running fine.

 I upgraded to 14.10 then after a test upgraded to 15.04 and now I have problems.

This issue has similarities to some other posts but no solution found there.

0. Upon transition from 14.10 to 15.04 my left and right monitors were reversed, I just physically moved them
1. On 15.04 boot I get an all white blank screen, however when I left click the mouse I see the login screen
2. When I login, the screen goes blank (black/video off) again, then the display monitors are powered off I think due to 'no video signal', the ready LED goes off as well as the display, however the computer is still on
3. I can only recover by forcing the computer to shutdown and reboot

1. Workaround:  Under 14.04 I had created an alternate login.  On boot I get the blank screen on power up, click to see the login dialog, however then I use my alternate login, and success.
2. I logout of my alternate login ID, then login the user id that I normally use
3. This does get me logged in so that I can use the computer under my normal login ID.

I have disabled the user startup programs I had been running. 

At first I thought this might be a video driver issue, however the video works fine with my work around.

Using the alternate login must be fixing something or avoiding something that my regular login ID does not.
