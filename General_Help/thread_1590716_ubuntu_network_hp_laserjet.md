### Post by searchfgold6789 on 2010-10-08
Well you probably want the company-supplied drivers for anything but simple printing, not the ones you get with Ubuntu. (Ubuntu picks up software for your printer from the HPLIP website, though...)
Try [downloading the latest HPLIP]("http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/install/install/index.html")
