### Post by orev on 2006-06-17
Maybe you could post a little here on your hardware setup?  That would help someone give you a hand.

Also, the contents of your xorg.conf file could be useful.  To get that file, open a Terminal (Applications>Accessories>Terminal), the type:

```
sudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf
``` 

(You will probably be prompted for your password)

This will open the file in a text editor.  Unless you know what you are doing do not edit the file, just copy and paste the contents in your reply.
