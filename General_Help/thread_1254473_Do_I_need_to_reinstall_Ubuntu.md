### Post by howefield on 2009-08-31
If you are happy the machine is running well, why fix what isn't broken...

If you do decide to reinstall, take an image of it with clonezilla once you have the system sitting pristine and configured just the way you want. 

That way you can screw around as much as you like, knowing you have the backup to revert to within 5 minutes or so.

Clonezilla looks more intimidating than it really is, and well worth getting to know.
