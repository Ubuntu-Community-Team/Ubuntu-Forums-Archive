### Post by coskierken on 2009-05-24
I have been working with bluetooth since 8.04 and it has not worked for with my Plantronics 510 until know.  They seemed to have solved it as I tried it yesterday and it now works.  Now, they have automated the pairing by adding automatic pin.  I just left it as the d" default.  When you first activate "Setup new device" have you headset in pairing mode.  It should find it and take of everything else.  If it sees it, you will see it in the list.  If you want to use it in skype, go to "Preferences/Sound" and make see if you have "pulse audio" selected as the default device.  Then, start Skype and go to Options/Sound Devices and see what all is selectable.  If headset is there (could actually list your headset) select it for to all options of sound in, sound out, and ringing.  Make a test call and see what happens and let us know.  Have a great day!):P
