### Post by audiomick on 2009-12-09
As far as I know, this is theoretically possible to an extent.
If your friend wants to try it, she should of course **take the card out or the camera immediately, and stop using it.**

As I understand it, when such a card is formatted, only the "index" is removed. The data are still there until such time as they are physically overwritten. Since your  friend has stored to the card since the format, she will have some losses, but some of the old pictures should be able to be recovered.

Unfortunately, I can't suggest a way to do this, I just know it is theoretically possible.
