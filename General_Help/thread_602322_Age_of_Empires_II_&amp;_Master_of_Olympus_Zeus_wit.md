### Post by kostkon on 2007-11-04
From my experience *Age of Empires II* runs pretty well with *Wine*. Anyway, in general to check if you can run a game with *Wine* and how to do it, search the [*Wine Application Database (AppDB)*]("http://appdb.winehq.org/") at the *Wine* official site.

Thus, go to the *AppDB* and see what you have to do in order to make *AOEII* to run. Do the same for *Master of Olympus: Zeus*. Actually, I don't know if the last one plays in Wine so you have to check it yourself.
