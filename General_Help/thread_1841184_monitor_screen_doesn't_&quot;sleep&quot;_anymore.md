### Post by RotorRick on 2011-09-10
Ok, I have new information:

The other day my computer locked up, requiring a hard restart. That, I believe, was the start of this "monitor won't hibernate" issue. 

Early this morning it occurred to me that that "crash/restart", I had not done a clean normal shutdown/restart. So I did that, and suddenly my problem was solved!

:guitar:

The lesson: after a crash/lockup/reboot/problem, do a clean shutdown/reboot so it works as the creators intended!!
