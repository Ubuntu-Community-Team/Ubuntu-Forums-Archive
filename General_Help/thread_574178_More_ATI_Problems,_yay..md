### Post by Ceemee on 2007-10-12
The easiest way to solve your problem would be to buy a graphics card that acts like they care about Linux.

Nvidia.

ATI is notorius on these forums for releasing a driver that supports limited cards, and only limited features on those cards.

Nvidia is much better about supporting their cards in Linux, as well as most of their features.

While neither is perfect, Nvidia has had a much better reputation than ATI.

Even when I was a Windoze user, I started buying Nvidia just because of the better drivers and support even in windoze.
