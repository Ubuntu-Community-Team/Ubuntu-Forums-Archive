### Post by Tamlynmac on 2010-07-11
There is a KDE app that works pretty good with it's own templates called "KreetingKard". It's in the repositories (Synaptic Package Manager).

I haven't used it for quite some time, but it worked then (about 4 years ago). I believe the site to view is located [here]("http://kde.linux-life.net/3/apps/kreetingkard/").

Or you can try the Open Office greeting card templates (seen [here]("http://templates.services.openoffice.org/en/node/2831")).

Good Luck and I hope this helps.
