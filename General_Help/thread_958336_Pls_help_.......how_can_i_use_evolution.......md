### Post by Bucky Ball on 2008-10-25
Evolution is great! I used to use Thunderbird and since I swapped to Linux for my main box, haven't been there. I am uni student and it has great calendar and everything I need to keep my busy schedule together. Used to have a diary but now i use the laptop and Evolution. As far as being more secure, Linux just is, no one piece of software is any less or more, if you know what I mean. :)

Click the icons at the bottom to access your schedule and all other stuff, add the info as you need, and use the 'backup info' to make sure it is all kept securely. This will backup everything you do in Evolution - addresses, schedules, birthdays .... whatever! Boot up tomorrow, check your Evolution and see where you're supposed to be and what you're supposed to be doing. Couldn't live without it nowadays. (well, I could, but ... ) :)

I think that is why it is called Evolution.
