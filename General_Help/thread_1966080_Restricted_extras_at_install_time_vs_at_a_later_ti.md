There was a problem a while back where the installation actually stopped and failed if the restricted packages repository was down or so busy that an immediate connection could not be made. I am not positive but I believe the developers fixed this problem.

For myself I still choose to install my drivers after the initial system install. I chose to do so because I have a rather slow internet connection and (for whatever the reason may be) those drivers always seem to take substantially longer to download on initial install rather than later. Maybe it's just that they get downloaded near to the end of the install and it just seems to take much longer because of anticipation. :)
