### Post by srunni on 2007-06-01
Hi,

Is there any way to remove the on screen display for Kaffeine in fullscreen mode, where it displays the play time in the top left hand corner?

Thanks in advance for the help!

---

### Post by Jacob Wintersmith on 2007-12-09
From the Settings menu, choose Configure XINE Engine Parameters; under the OSD tab, uncheck the "Show OSD Messages" option. This will disable the OSD time display for both fullscreen and windowed modes.
