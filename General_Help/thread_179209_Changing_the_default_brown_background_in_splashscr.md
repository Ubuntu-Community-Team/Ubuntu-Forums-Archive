### Post by xyz on 2006-05-19
Click on System>Preferences>Desktop Background
You can change backgrounds there or modify 'colors' under "Desktop Colors".
Also you can ADD wallpapers: just type "free wallpapers" in Google. Lots of sites!
Using the Desktop Colors, I made my background go from orange to red as my eyes move left-to-right of my screen. Hope this is what you're looking for.
