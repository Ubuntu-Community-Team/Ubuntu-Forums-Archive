### Post by bsalt on 2008-11-22
Ok, I have an update to the previous post. 

I found the following things to recommend to other novice Ubuntu users:

1) Don't enable **Pre-Released Updates** and **Unsupported Updates**, unless you know what you are doing and what updates you are installing.

2) Don't download and install the Linux ATI drivers from their website, unless you know what you are doing. The other drivers that come with updates have been tested and configured to work with Ubuntu. The drivers from ATI's site are more generic.
