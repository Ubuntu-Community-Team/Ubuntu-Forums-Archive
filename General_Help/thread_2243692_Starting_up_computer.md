### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-09-10
Hard drive? I am thinking graphic adapter. If the hard drive was not spinning you would get an "insert disk with operating system" message. Of course, it could also be the monitor. Or the motherboard.

Do you not get any indication that the motherboard is working? Can you hear the CPU fan? It could also be a break in the cable connecting the monitor to the motherboard.

You do not give enough information to allow confirmation that your hard disk is gone. Run a Ubuntu live session. If that works, then it could be the hard disk. Set up a process of elimination. Think about it logically.
