### Post by MikeCyber on 2012-10-22
Change those two setting to see what's going on at boot:

GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX=""

# Uncomment to disable graphical terminal (grub-pc only)
GRUB_TERMINAL=console

As described here:
[http://askubuntu.com/questions/33416/how-do-i-disable-the-boot-splash-screen](http://askubuntu.com/questions/33416/how-do-i-disable-the-boot-splash-screen)
