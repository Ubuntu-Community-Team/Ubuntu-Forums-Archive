### Post by conscious on 2009-01-25
First, there's an applet for this. Right-click the panel and select Add to Panel... -> Disk Mounter -> Add.

If you want an icon only for your ipod, it's easy to create a permanent one. Open /media in Nautilus and drag you ipod's folder onto the panel.
