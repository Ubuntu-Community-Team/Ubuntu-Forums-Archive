### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-24
Someone said *build-essential* is on the CD, but I haven't verified that yet.

If it is on the CD, go to System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager > Settings > Repositories and uncheck all the boxes except the CD-ROM one. Then pop the CD in and click Reload. Search for *build-essential* and see if it pops up.

If it's not on the CD, [this post may help](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=913191&postcount=12).
