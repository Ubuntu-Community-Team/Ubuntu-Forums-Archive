### Post by Krytarik on 2011-04-14
> **MikeR. said:**
> do you know how to add an image to the dropdown menu of the applications tab/button?
What exactly do you mean? Do you want to *change* the icon before the word "Applications"? Those refers to some image files in the directory of the chosen icon theme. They are called "start-here.svg" or "start-here.png" respectively, and available in different image sizes. If you rename them and add your custom ones instead, with the same image sizes, they take effect on the panel icon after relogin (or at least restart of gnome-panel, with "killall gnome-panel").
