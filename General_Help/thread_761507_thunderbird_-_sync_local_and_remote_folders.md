### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-04-21
Unfortunately I don't think POP email accounts are capable of syncronisation, I don't think you can upload back to the server. If however your email provider supports the IMAP protocol this syncronisation is done automatically. Gmail for example offers both, I use POP for my seldom used email accounts and imap for my main personal one.
