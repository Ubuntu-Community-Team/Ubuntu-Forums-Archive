### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2011-03-21
I moved your post as you posted in a non-support thread.

Booting problems can be difficult and there is no single cause, could be almost anything, including hardware problems.

I suggest you boot an old kernel, review the logs, check your hardware.

If you think there is a problem with the boot (upstart) scripts I suggest you post on Launchpad, they will want more information.
