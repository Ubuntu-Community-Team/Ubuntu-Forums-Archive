### Post by ivarn on 2010-12-06
There is something you need to know, and other ppl need to know this as well.
Ok so the thing is. their basic installation is like an old MS Office, but that's a good thing because MS office is just unoverviewable and messy (imo).
So they have extra extensions on their website for everything thats not included.
