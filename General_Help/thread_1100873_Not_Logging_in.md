### Post by fieroboom on 2009-03-19
I experienced this once a *long* time ago, and don't remember what I did to fix it, but the [google results](http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&q=ubuntu+orange+screen+after+login&btnG=Search) look very promising.

I also found [this page that outlines your problem.](http://www.linuxforums.org/forum/ubuntu-help/105368-newb-help-everything-too-big.html)

Basically what is happening is your X server is attempting to display a resolution that your laptop screen doesn't support. LCDs can be quite finicky sometimes. Old CRTs don't really care, but I've run into several roadblocks several times on my flat panels.
If you need any help getting started, and/or adjusting your xorg.conf, let me know.
