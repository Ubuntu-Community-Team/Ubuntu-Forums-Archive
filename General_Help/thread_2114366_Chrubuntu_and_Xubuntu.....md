### Post by joemartin on 2013-02-09
Just a quick tip for Chrubuntu users....

Yes, Ubuntu on Chromebook is fast and performs extremely well.  I converted a 12.04 install on Chromebook last week and have been using both Xubuntu and Lubuntu.  

On a Chromebook, Lubuntu consistently performed buggy with several programs crashing (for whatever reason).  Xubuntu has been perfect.  Using more resources than Lubuntu but equally fast and no crashing whatsoever.  The difference in speed between Xubuntu and Ubuntu, Mint and Bodhi on my x86 Samsung Chromebook was noticeable right away.

So, if you are using Chrubuntu and looking for a little more speed a visit to Psychocats and a conversion to Xubuntu may be the answer for you too.
