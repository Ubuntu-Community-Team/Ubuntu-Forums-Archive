### Post by 2F4U on 2013-01-05
Are you mixing the terms HUD and dash here? My understanding is that the HUD is kind of an advanced application menu and is not intended to be used to search files, except those used with one particular application. The dash, on the other hand, is a system menu that is also able to perform various kind of searches.
