### Post by Bruce M. on 2008-01-09
> **ubuscout said:**
> Hi people... an advice plz.

I'm looking for configure an account for my child, something in "on/off & game style", what's the best approach to do this... ? 

Sorry for this newby question but here I am :-)

Try this:

 1. Right click on the Panel at the top, select [New Panel] ... when it shows up, right click on it and choose Properties.  Put it at the Top

 2. Right-Click on this New Panel and Add to Panel: **Quit** ... move it to where you want and lock to the panel.

 3. >Applications>Games> *Right Click* on one of the games.

 4. Select [Entire Menu] and "Add this as a Menu to Panel" >> IT will appear on the "Original Panel"

 5. Right Click on it and select **move**, and move you cursor to the NEW Panel you created.  When the Game Menu is on the New Panel Lock it in Place.

You now have the complete Game Menu on the NEW panel with a "Quit" Icon too.

 6. Right Click on the Original Panel with the [Applications Place System] menus and Select: Delete this panel.

You should now have something that looks like this attached

**As an added note:**  Remove the "Trash" icon from the bottom panel as well, because it can be used as a file manager - dangerous to leave it there.
