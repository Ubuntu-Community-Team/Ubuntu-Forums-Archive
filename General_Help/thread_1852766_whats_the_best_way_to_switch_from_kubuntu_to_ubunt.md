### Post by 3Miro on 2011-09-30
If you system will not boot from the liveCD, then you wither have big issues with either the liveCD or your system.

The main difference between Ubuntu and Kubuntu is the desktop environment. You can either install both DE at the same time or you can switch from one to the other with:

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/puregnome](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/puregnome)

Checkout all of their tutorial, they have things for all environments.
