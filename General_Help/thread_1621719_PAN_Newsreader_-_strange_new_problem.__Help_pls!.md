### Post by PhilB on 2010-12-12
Had the same problem crop up in last couple of days.
First thing to do is back up your hidden newsgroup file Pan2.
Completely uninstall Pan and then do a fresh install. You will need to set up your news account again. 
What I have done next is go to one of my groups and download a days headers. I then copy the files from my backed up group folder to the new one and then I can go through the process of resubscribing to my groups again, knowing I have a full archive.
Note that often the larger binary group files seem to get corrupted on a regular basis so I try not to let them get too big.
Hope this helps.
