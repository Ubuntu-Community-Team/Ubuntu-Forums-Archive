### Post by rbmorse on 2008-11-04
On my system (using HPLIP) the available printers seem to be presented in alphabetical order of the name you give the printer when you install it.  

I just added a new printer 1_HP_ColorLaserJet_2605DN and it appears before the printer named HP_ColorLaserJet_2650DN in the list of available printers, as I would expect.
