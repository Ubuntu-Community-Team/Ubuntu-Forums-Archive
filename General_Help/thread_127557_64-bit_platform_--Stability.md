### Post by rusacarr on 2006-02-10
I'm running just fine on **AMD64 3200+** with **512mb RAM**.  No stability issues at all.  

I do have an issue with my video card but I'm buying a new one. (SiS Mirage2 - if you have that card, :twisted: [COLOR="Red"]BURN IT[/COLOR] :twisted: !  No real support at all.  The SiS driver clean up some of the defects, but still trash the card.  Supported nVidia cards are cheap now.).

Actually, I know it's just 'feel', but my machine 'feels' faster running K. 64 than running (gasp) XP Home under my dual-boot; and I have XP stripped down pretty well.

I would suggest you double check that your hardware is supported, latest drivers and such, might also make sure your BIOS and firmwares are up-to-date.  

Just to be on the safe side, check some of the 'usual suspects' like CPU Temp, cards/cables/memory seated firmly, stable power supply, etc.
