### Post by Marinodimare on 2008-07-20
It appears as though my kde taskbar does not function properly: I see no windows appear in it when they are definitely opened, nor when they are minimalized. Any idea how this may happen?

### Post by benerivo on 2008-07-20
You can add and remove elements of the taskbar, by right clicking on it. It may be that the 'Task Manager' has been deleted, and you need to add it back in.

---

### Post by Marinodimare on 2008-07-20
Thanks, it turned out that the taskbar-style was set to "elegant". Obviously it can't handle "elegant", because everything was right when I changed the setting to "transparency".
