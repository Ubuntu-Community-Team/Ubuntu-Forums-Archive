### Post by GSF1200S on 2007-09-23
I would definitly say go with an XFCE based distro. For sheer speed, lightweight, stability and a good selection of software (based off of slackware), I would second the above choice of Zenwalk Linux. 256MB of ram should be good for Zenwalk.

Going to DSL or fluxbox based distros will DEFINITLY make everything faster, but then you lose alot of the easier configuration. Now, some people SWEAR by fluxbox, IceWM (I like IceWM myself), etc... but it will be a little more difficult in areas. I would give Zenwalk a shot, and then go to lighter DE's if necessary. Fluxbox and IceWM, as well as ratpoison, e17, openbox, etc will be the fastest options if speed is your primary goal.
