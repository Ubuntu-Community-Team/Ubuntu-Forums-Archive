### Post by NCLI on 2010-08-14
They're frameworks developed for the same purpose, but don't support the same plug-ins. The purpose of both is to allow you to have interactive widgets on your desktop.

Desklets are developed by Gnome, while Screenlets are developed by another developer. I think neither of them is in heavy development., as Screenlets' last stable release was in June 2008, and Desklets' was in November of the same year.
