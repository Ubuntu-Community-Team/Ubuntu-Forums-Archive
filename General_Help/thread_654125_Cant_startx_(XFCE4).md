### Post by x1a4 on 2007-12-30
Hi,

Looks like the MCS Manager loses connection to the display.  First try to reconfigure  the MCS Manager.  

```
dpkg-reconfigure xfce4-mcs-manager
```

Then reinstall your video driver.  I know that there are some issues with the 915 Intel driver wihich *might* be the culprit.  

Further, if your video card supports it, don't load **dri** in /etc/X11/xorg.conf and load **glx** instead.  Also, check that you're NOT loading both **glx** and **dri** at the same time.  You can have one or the other but not both. 

Hope this helps.
