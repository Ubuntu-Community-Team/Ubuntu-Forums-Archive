### Post by Zorgoth on 2010-08-10
IM is part of the notification area, which you do not want to remove unless you are making another one somewhere, but probably there is something in the preferences of your IM client that will say not to show it in the system tray.

As for the rest, right click the applets and remove them, as simple as that; sometimes you have to find the right place to right click.
