### Post by jocko on 2007-01-15
If the graphics card works on another pc, I'd guess the problem is in the motherboard, or that the motherboard don't support the graphics card. Find out which AGP standards (1x, 2x, 4x, 8x) your motherboard can handle, and which ones the graphics card can use.
