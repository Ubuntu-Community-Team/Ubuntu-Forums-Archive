### Post by ManiacDan on 2009-06-25
Definitely make her (let her) try it out first, either from a LiveCD or on your machine.  And make sure she doesn't use any windows-only apps.  If she uses iTunes, she may hate you for switching her.  If she's a student, she may not be able to take any of the online tests without Internet Explorer.  Similarly, if she works at all from this computer she may need IE.  My wife was recently a student (requiring IE for online tests) and is now an accountant (requiring an ActiveX certificate to log into the work website) so we still have to dual boot.
