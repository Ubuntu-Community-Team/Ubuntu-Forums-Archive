### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-07-01
Look in /var/log for log files, but hardware problems don't often get accurately logged because they happen so quickly.  So don't be fooled by what you see in the log files.  Sounds like possibly a bad battery or loose connection from shipping.  Many times new laptops need a break-in period to work out the bugs.  You might be looking at a warranty return at this point.
