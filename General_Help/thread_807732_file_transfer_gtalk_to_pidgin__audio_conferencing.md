### Post by tom_ on 2008-08-01
Have had file transfer problems in pidgin for several months .... works one way but not the other, with a friend.  Tonight we seem to have cracked it.  In the advanced tab in the account we changed in both places to "No proxy" whereas previously it had been set at "Use GNOME settings."  We cannot prove it but my friend fiddles with proxies a bit and we suspect thats where the problem lies.
