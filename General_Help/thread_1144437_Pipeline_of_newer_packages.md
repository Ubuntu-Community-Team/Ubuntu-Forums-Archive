### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-04-30
Current stable version of software are added to repositories of new releases, then they're locked in for that release.  So it's possible that you'll see ALSA 1.0.19 in 9.10, but otherwise you'll have to look around for a PPA that offers it for your current version of Ubuntu.

[Here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuDevelopment/NewPackages")'s some information about suggesting software for inclusion in future versions of Ubuntu.
