### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-03-31
The Hardy packages are available for version 2.4 and can be downloaded from [http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?keywords=openoffice&searchon=names&suite=hardy&section=all](http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?keywords=openoffice&searchon=names&suite=hardy&section=all) .  Or you could temporarily enable the Hardy repositories and get them that way via synaptic--I am not sure how to do this off the top of my head, but it's easy and there are instructions if you search the forums.  Or you could wait another few weeks for Hardy to be released, unless there is a compelling reason that you want OO 2.4.

And of course, building from source is always an option if you're really adventurous...and have a lot of time to wait while OO gets compiled, which supposedly many hours.
