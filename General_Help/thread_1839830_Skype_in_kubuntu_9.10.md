### Post by zero2xiii on 2011-09-06
First,

Any *Ubuntu variant pre 10.04 are NO LONGER SUPPORTED. I recommend upgrading to 10.04 which is a long term support vversion, and the most stable. see this for some details regarding what is and will be supported: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LTS](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LTS)

As far as the installer issue goes, you should download a *.deb file from:
[http://www.skype.com/intl/en/get-skype/on-your-computer/linux/](http://www.skype.com/intl/en/get-skype/on-your-computer/linux/)

Select on the left hand side under "Download now" the option you require (as you will note, skype does not support any ubuntu before 10.04 either). You may try the debian 5+ package for back support, but I wont recommend it.

The safest will be to upgrade, get Kubuntu, or Xubuntu, but ATLEAST 10.04.

Good luck
