### Post by faqpete on 2005-08-06
HI!
When you take a look at the evolution window, you have a panel on the left side (calendar, todo, ...). Be sure that calendar is activated! Now you should see something like "on this computer" "on the web" "contacts" "weather". Just do a right-click on weather and choose "new calendar". Then choose "weather" from the calendar type, select your country (if it is already listed) and save it. Now you should see the forecast for the country you choosed.
