### Post by zeronothing on 2009-01-14
if your laptop is like mine I had a similar problem. For some reason on my laptop even if I turned up all of the sliders in the "sound" options the volume level never really got up to where I remembered when I had windows on the laptop. Then I discovered, when I would hit the volume up button on on my keypad the volume on the laptop got louder (much louder), even though all of the volume bars in the sound settings where all up. So try increasing the volume up on your laptops keypad if you can. see if that helps
