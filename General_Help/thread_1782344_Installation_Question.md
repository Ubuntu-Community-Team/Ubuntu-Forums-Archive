### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-06-14
oceanfirehawk,
you should be very careful with .deb files from outside sources! In fact, for the security and stability of your system it is not recommended to install software from outside the official repositories.

Unless there is a very specific application you need, most of what you could want will be available through one of the package managers.
