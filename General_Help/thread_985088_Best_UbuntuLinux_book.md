I use this book so often that it's all wrinkled and dog-eared... it's an excellent book.

**_"Linux - The Complete Reference, Sixth Edition"_** by Richard Peterson.

ISBN 978-0-07-149247-8

Amazon.com [[COLOR="Red"]**_LINK_**[/COLOR]]("http://www.amazon.com/Linux-Complete-Reference-Sixth/dp/007149247X/ref=sr_1_4?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1226936266&sr=8-4")

The book covers Kernel 2.6 which is the basis for all current distros of Linux.

It's a thick book... about 2 inches thick (830 pages).

I HIGHLY recommend it.
