### Post by whoop on 2009-06-09
How are you reading the microsd card when it's failing? It could be possible that the "slot" you are inserting the sd card in is dirty or corroded. Try cleaning the copper connectors of the sdcard. See (be careful) if you can shove something (soft) in the slot you put the sd card in (to clean it). You can try some rubbing alcohol (don't use too much).
