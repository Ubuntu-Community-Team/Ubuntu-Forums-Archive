### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-02-24
Just so you know, a big PLUS of installing the drivers the way that 3rdalbumn indicated is that, when you do future updates that affect the Linux kernel, your drivers will be automatically updated as well.

If you install from an AMD download, you will be faced with having to update the drivers yourself every time you do a kernel update.
