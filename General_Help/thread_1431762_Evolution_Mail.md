Set "Loading images" as required.

### Post by howefield on 2010-03-21
You're both welcome, just watch the spam, loading images from the internet can verify a live email address. A hidden one pixel image can be all it takes to verify your email address.

I prefer not to load images, but if I have an email with images that I want to see, for example, from one of the online retailers I subscribe to, then the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + I will load them. Quick, easy and selective.
