### Post by skyjacker on 2010-08-06
Connect to a hard wired connection (ie. ethernet),and go to System, Administration, and choose "Hardwire Drivers". This will enable a search for new drivers. It will "hopefully" find an driver for wireless internet. I use Broadcom STA in my system, but you may need a different one. Choose one and it will install. After that, restart, and you should be able to connect.
By the way, I am using Ubuntu Netbook Remix on a small netbook... Works great!
