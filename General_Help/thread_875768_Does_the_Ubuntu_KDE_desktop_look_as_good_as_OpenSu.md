### Post by randcoop on 2008-07-31
There are MANY differences between OpenSuse and Kubuntu.  Kubuntu does come with KDE 4.1, so you can get the basic look of the desktop that you want, but many other changes will exist.

And NO, you cannot count on everything working in Kubuntu simply because it worked in Ubuntu.  Kubuntu uses a different front end for many of the underlying programs, but it would be wrong to think the differences are only cosmetic.  Sometimes, they make things work differently.

As important, KDE programs used in Kubuntu may be different than KDE programs used in OpenSuse.

I think your best bet would be to run Kubuntu from the Live CD.  While that's not a perfect indicator of how things will work after installation, it will take you a lot closer to being able to decide on whether the differences are insurmountable.
