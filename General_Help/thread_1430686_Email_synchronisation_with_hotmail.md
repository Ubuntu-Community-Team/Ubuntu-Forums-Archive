### Post by mkp123 on 2010-05-12
[COLOR=black]This is an email synchronization problem. In windows operating system, thunderbird synchronizes with the help of hotmail. But the mail program in ubuntu does not synchronize the email of the user. This is also a major issue. Synchronization means time keeping. It stores the time and date of the events that occurs or the emails that are received or being send. It also does back up services. So synchronization problem is more important for a standard email program in the very popular ubuntu operating system.[/COLOR][]("http://www.caas.com/Pages/default.aspx")
