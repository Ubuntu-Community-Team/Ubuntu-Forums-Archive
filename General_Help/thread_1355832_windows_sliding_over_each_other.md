### Post by howefield on 2009-12-16
I'm guessing this is the Dodge animation that you are looking for.

Load up Compiz settings manager and click on the Animations icon, then the Focus Animation tab.

Highlight the single entry in the Animation Selection window and press the edit button. From the Focus Effect drop down field, choose Dodge.

Close your way back out.

Is this what you are after ?
