### Post by kurt18947 on 2012-09-19
It wasn't new but I bought an X61 Thinkpad off Ebay.  No hard drive but for $130 I was willing to take a chance.   12" 4 X 3 screen, weighs < 3 lbs.  Put in a spare hard drive, loaded up Ubuntu and everything worked though I didn't mess with the fingerprint reader thingy.  More powerful and better ergonomics than most small laptops.  I think most Intel based machines  - including Intel video & wireless -  are pretty smooth rides.  Problems to be aware of with notebooks are "really new" wifi chips and hybrid graphics.  A few minutes spent in the networking and wireless forum here will give a feel for which chipsets people seem to have the most trouble with.
