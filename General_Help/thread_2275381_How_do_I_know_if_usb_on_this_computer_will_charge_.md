### Post by Keith_Helms on 2015-04-26
There are no drivers involved in charging a device from a USB port.  If you plug something in to charge that the computer recognizes, it may automatically mount the device.  All USB ports should deliver 5 volts, but they vary in the amount of amps.  Older USB 2 ports may deliver 500mA while newer USB 3 ports may deliver 900mA.  A dedicated charging device might give you up to 3 amps.   More amps often translates into shorter charging time, so using the provided charger for the phone likely would mean a faster charge.

Anyway, this article is a good explanation of charging with USB:
