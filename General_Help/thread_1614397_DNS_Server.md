### Post by cek on 2010-11-05
I cannot think of a really good reason to have "your own" DNS server.  The DNS server as provided by your ISP is usually "good enough", however they are free to do things that you don't want them to do -- they control the DNS and can blacklist/whitelist sites, or reroute invalid requests to other sites, etc.

Personally, I use the OpenDNS servers for all my DNS needs.
