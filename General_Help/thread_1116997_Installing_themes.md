### Post by leonardo_neo on 2009-04-05
It will be better if you could elaborate what themes you have installed/want to install. 

Anyway, in general if you want to change the appearance and colors of your desktop then you can do that system---> preferences---> appearance, and then selecting whatever theme you like. You can also change the icons and pointer theme by further selecting the 'customize' button of your theme.

To download different themes for your icons and pointers, you can refer to this website......

[http://www.gnome-look.org/]("http://www.gnome-look.org/")

If you are interested to install 'dock' themes, then I recommend you to install cairo dock.....Here is the link from where you can download and know how to install cairo dock.....

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CairoDock]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CairoDock")
