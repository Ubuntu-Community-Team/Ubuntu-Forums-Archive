### Post by WiseElben on 2007-04-08
nVidia's 6600 GT is one of the best for it's price, even though they're already on the 8-series now. If you hunt around on NewEgg.com, you might find some 7600 GT's for cheaper than $100. From what I've read, the 7600 GT is successfully living up to 6600 GT's name. There is no need to get an 8-series of a high 7-series unless you really really want maximum settings on all of your games you play on Windows.

By the way, I own a 6600 GT (bought it two years ago) and I can still play games like Medieval II: Total War on High settings and without lag. Beryl also works fine.
