### Post by shinepuppy on 2010-12-18
Hi all. For some reason, Pidgin has *always* made a crackling sound before playing the actual event sound on my linux systems. It finally bugged me enough to go tinker with the sound settings. I changed the sound method from "automatic" to "ALSA" and the crackling goes away :)

Tools -> Preferences -> Sounds -> Method: ALSA

I'm running Maverick 10.10. Hope this helps someone else.
