### Post by laurenbanjo on 2010-11-14
Hold down shift while turning on the computer to bring up the GRUB menu and choose Windows.

### Post by drs305 on 2010-11-14
Take a look at the G2-Tasks link in my signature line for ways to set the menu timeout and change the default OS (if desired).
