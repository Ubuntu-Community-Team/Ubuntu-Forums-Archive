### Post by DoktorSeven on 2006-08-14
Because they see the old Esound page that hasn't had an update since 2000, not realizing that Esound has been [donated to Gnome](http://www.tux.org/~ricdude/dev.html) since then.  There are recent updates to Esound in [Gnome's CVS](http://cvs.gnome.org/viewcvs/esound/) so no, it's not defunct.
