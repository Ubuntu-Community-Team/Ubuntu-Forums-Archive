### Post by bsussman on 2006-10-22
* warty
    * hoary
    * hoary-backports
    * breezy 
    * breezy-backports
    * dapper 
    * edgy 

It seems the ubuntu gods decided they needed their own set of cute names since using release numbers seemed to be just too unfriendly.  Debian does the same thing - their names are even cuter in their own special way.

For instance, edgy is ubuntu 6.10, dapper is 6.06 and they get progressively older as you go up your list.

backports is where upgrades that are backwards engineered to run on an earlier release than that they first appeared in are kept.  This is valuable for folks who do not want to upgrade the os need or want an improvement made later.
