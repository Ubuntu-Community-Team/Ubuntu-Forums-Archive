### Post by mcduck on 2011-10-18
You'll have to use a theme that supports both GTK2 and GTK3, as all the new Gnome apps are using GTK3 now.

### Post by gsmanners on 2011-10-18
> **crazy bird said:**
> Does Xubuntu comes with GTK3 standard?

Xubuntu comes with greybird. Here's a tip for if you need to switch themes: copy /usr/share/themes/greybird/gtk-3.0 into ~/.config until you find a nice GTK2/3 theme you want.
