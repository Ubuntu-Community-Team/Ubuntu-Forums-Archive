### Post by glotz on 2009-02-08
Try this, open the document and click the first page. Then goto format > styles & formatting. Then click on the page style icon and double click on first page (style). Then if you don't have a footer yet, click on the 2nd page and goto insert > footer > default and then insert > field > page numbering.

### Post by giantoz on 2009-02-08
thanks! your help led me to the solution.  but instead of instert>field>page numbering i used insert>field>other-document>page>and set the offset to -1.  that made my 2nd page the 1st page.
