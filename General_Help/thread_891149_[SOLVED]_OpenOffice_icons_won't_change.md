### Post by le singe on 2008-08-16
okay, I got the answer.  Heh, first time I've helped myself with one of my posts.  Maybe someone else has the same question I had, and so maybe I'm helping someone else as well.

So go into synaptic manager and search:
openoffice.org-style

For me, six styles showed up, and only Human was installed.  So even though all the other icon options are visible under the openoffice options settings, they are not actually installed.  A little counterintuitive, I know.  Anyways, just mark the others for installation (crystal, high contrast, industrial, etc) and when you next use openoffice writer, you should be able to effectively switch between the different icons sets.
