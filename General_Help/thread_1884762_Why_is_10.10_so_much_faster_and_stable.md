No real issues aside from no security updates that I know of.  One advantage of newer releases is better hardware support.  For instance I have two USB WiFi adapters that work out of the box with 11.04 and later.  Using them on earlier versions require tweaking.  On the other hand newer releases may drop support for older hardware e.g. older ATI video cards so unless you can replace the unsupported components, continuing to use unsupported versions may be the best choice in certain cases.
