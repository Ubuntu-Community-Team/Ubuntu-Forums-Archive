### Post by Iandefor on 2005-12-03
the GIMP lets you make screen/window captures. In the GIMP, go to file-->acquire-->screen shot. If you want a screen shot with a menu open, set the delay to like, 10 seconds, open the menu and wait (Note that this only works with the Whole screen option).
