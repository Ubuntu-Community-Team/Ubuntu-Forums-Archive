### Post by cascade9 on 2010-07-31
I was going to ask why you are using that on a linux system, then I remembered this- 

> GreedyTorrent can help you survive on trackers that enforce a minimum 1:1 trading ratio, and can keep you from getting banned for not uploading. So [how does it work?]("http://greedytorrent.com/docs/howworks.php") In simple words, it modifies the conversation between your bittorrent client and the tracker, suppressing the actual upload amount. Instead, GreedyTorrent puts you in control of your own upload ratio -- you can define how much upload you want to submit to the tracker.

Sodding around with the dialog between the client and the server could well be making things dodgy.
