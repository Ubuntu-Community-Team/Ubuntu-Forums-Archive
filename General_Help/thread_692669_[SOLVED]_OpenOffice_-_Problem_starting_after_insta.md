I recently installed a number of extensions to OpenOffice, all of them from the extension repository at  [http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/](http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/) (which I would assume to all be safe).

After installing about 5 of them, I closed OpenOffice and tried to restart it, and when I reopened it, it got stuck on the splash screen. The blue bar that indicates the status goes all the way to the end, but stops there.

My question isn't how to fix it, but how to remove the extensions manually, as I would assume that would fix the problem (and also allow me to work out what extension caused the problem), but I can't work out where they are located!

Try to rename your OOo user profile (~/.openoffice.org2).
