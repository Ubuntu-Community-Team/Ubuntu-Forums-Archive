### Post by Dougie187 on 2008-06-16
What download location do you want to use?

Either way, just go here
[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors)

And select a download location. Then you can sift through their ftp for the version you want. Some are better laid out then others, for instance.

This is argonne national lab in chicago
[http://mirror.anl.gov/pub/ubuntu-iso/CDs/](http://mirror.anl.gov/pub/ubuntu-iso/CDs/)

For detailed instructions, when you click download ubuntu on ubuntu.com, scroll to the bottom and click "Click here for a complete list of mirrors" or something like that. Then you get to the first link i gave you.

Hope this helps.
