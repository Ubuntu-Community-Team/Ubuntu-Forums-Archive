### Post by Bigbluecat on 2007-02-04
I completed the survey. If you read the proposal it does recommend that the BBC take a platform agnostic approach. However it will start with Microsoft and the recommendation is that other platforms could be supported within 24 months.

The BBC produces some very good content so I argued that making this exclusively available to Microsoft customers for 24 months would be of significant commercial advantage for Microsoft and that is not something that the license payers money should be used to enable.

Either the service is started as a platform agnostic service or it is enabled as platform agnostic within 6 months of start up. This seemed to me to be a fair time frame.
