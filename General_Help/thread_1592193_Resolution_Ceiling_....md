### Post by Anansi Boy on 2010-10-11
I finally figured out the problem. I have dual monitors, one Samsung Full HD (DVI) and an old Dell SD (AVI).  When installing Ubuntu it seems to think the Dell is the main monitor and only allows resolutions available to that model.

I had to uninstall Ubuntu, unhook the Dell monitor and then re-inistall Ubuntu.  This then allowed me to set the resolution to full HD.
