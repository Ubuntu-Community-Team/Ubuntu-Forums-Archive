### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-08-03
There are some effects in compiz that will do damage to Unity. They will work fine in Ubuntu classic mode but enable them in Unity and problems start. For example, wobbly windows affects the top panel by changing it to a rainbow of colours effect where you cannot see the app indicators.

The answer is to disable those effects in Unity. You might still be able to bring up the power menu by clicking the mouse in the area where it should be. From there you can get System Settings open.

Regards.
