### Post by keyboardashtray on 2007-11-12
Yes, I found that absolutely insane. 

I don't know if this is the program you were referring to, but what I used was AllTray. That can let you reduce Evolution to the system tray. And other programs, too, which can have uses.

Once you download it, it will be in your accessories menu. You then select it, and click on the window to dock. From then on out, until you explicitly click Quit in the program, using the close button on the window will just reduce it to the system tray. There is special functionality for Evolution that is nice, too - hovering over it shows the unread msgs, etc.

To use it on startup, create a session command with it, e.g. alltray evolution

Then Evolution will start on startup, in the system tray already.

Be warned, this may have been unique for me or Balsa (the email client I used to use), that it wan't working with the enhanced desktop graphic effects. It worked when I disabled the effects (I'm not too into eye candy anyway). But it may work fine for you. Definitely give it a shot.
