### Post by lawrence-joy on 2024-07-30
Here is a bad experience. I purchased an Epson ET-15000 all-in-one ink jet printer. They only supply printer drivers for Microsoft Windows. The printer driver they show for Linux (Ubuntu) only gave me 8 1/2" X 11" printout. It would not give me an 11" X 17" printout. Epson told me I would have to find a driver. The reason I went with the Epson printer was because of the ink tank concept. If you only print A4 size paper and don't mind the cartridges then I would stick with the Cannon products. I had a Cannon ink jet printer that would handle wide paper, but because of non usage the ink in the mechanism dried up and it was no longer usable.
