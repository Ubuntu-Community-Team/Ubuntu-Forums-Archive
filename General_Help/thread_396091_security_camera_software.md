### Post by crispy_420 on 2007-03-29
Right now I'm speaking as a windows user as linux software may not work or may be to troublesome to set up.

Pretty much any tv card will have some sort of pvr software, although pretty low quality. But it will serve your purpose. I'm assuming standard tv resolution video will serve you (640x480). Then you could connect your video camera as usual. The software you should be able to download directly from the manufacturer of the card.

Does your camera connect via a standard RCA cable? If so look for that feature in a capture card, most will have that.
