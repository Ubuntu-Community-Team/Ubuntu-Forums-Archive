### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-09-25
You need a graphics card that has multiple connections if you want to do this and use them as one computer.

If you don't have a graphics card that has two outputs another option is to have two separate computers and use a program like synergy to share the keyboard and mouse between both, you can even have windows on one and linux on the other.
