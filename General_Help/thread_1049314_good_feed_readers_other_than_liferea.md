### Post by sirjoebob on 2009-01-24
I use Google reader and LOVE it.

Advantages:
Simple, easy-to-use interface.
Easily add feeds while browsing in firefox.
Cross-platform.
All feeds are stored online and are accessible from any computer with internet access.
Integration with Google contacts allows sharing of stories, etc. 

[Reader.google.com]("reader.google.com")

I like it but miss the free standing application style. For that, I run Google reader through [Prism]("http://labs.mozilla.com/2007/10/prism/").
