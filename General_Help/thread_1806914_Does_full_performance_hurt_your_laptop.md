### Post by TBABill on 2011-07-18
I don't believe it is harmful to run a CPU at its native maximum speed. Prior to speed stepping CPU's always ran at one speed. The harm would probably come in the form of heat, but if you have no heat issue with the machine I don't see why it would be harmful. You'll use more energy, but if that's not an issue then it's just preference at that point.

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-07-18
More heat, especially excessive heat, is going to wear out the components sooner that would happen if the laptop ran cooler.  An overheated CPU will heat up the rest of the case, including the hard drive, lowering its useful life as well.

As to how much life reduction this will cause, no-one really knows.
