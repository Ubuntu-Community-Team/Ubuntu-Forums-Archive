### Post by snova on 2009-03-27
If you installed 'wesnoth', it will also install various other packages that are part of it, such as 'wesnoth-data', and 'wesnoth-music'. Removing it, however, won't automatically remove those too.

Search for 'wesnoth' in Synaptic and make sure all the results (specifically, everything beginning with 'wesnoth') are uninstalled.
