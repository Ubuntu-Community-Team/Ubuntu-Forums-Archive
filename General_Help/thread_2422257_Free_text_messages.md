### Post by cellsite60 on 2019-07-04
You can use pushbullet for that, it's available in **Firefox** [add-on]("http://addons.mozilla.org/sv-SE/firefox/addon/pushbullet/")[']("http://addons.mozilla.org/sv-SE/firefox/addon/pushbullet/")s and in **Chrome** [webstore]("https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/pushbullet/chlffgpmiacpedhhbkiomidkjlcfhogd?utm_source=chrome-ntp-icon").
You also need to install pushbullet on your phone.
