### Post by jamesisin on 2010-01-05
Don't remove Evolution.  It's fundamental to Gnome.

You could probably remove everything under Education, Games, Graphics, Office, and Programming.  Then most of the stuff under Accessories, Internet, and Sound and Video.  Other and System Tools will require a bit more caution.

Post specific questions about items of which you are unsure.
