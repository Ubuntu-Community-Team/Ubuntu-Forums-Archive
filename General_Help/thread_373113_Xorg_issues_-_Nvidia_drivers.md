### Post by hikaricore on 2007-02-28
Automatix sucks imho for installing video drivers.

Try envy: [http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html](http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html)

Works beautifully and even has a ubuntu package for easy installation.

Just install the package, hit ctrl+alt+F1, login and run:

```
sudo envy
```

After 1 input prompt, envy does the rest.

P.S. Choose no when it asks to reconfigure your xserver, since your xorg.conf should be fine.

Good luck,

--Aaron
