### Post by LukeV on 2007-12-01
Hey Guys I think I found a solution.

You need to turn on Power Manager in the sessions.

Go to System>Preferences>Sessions

And make sure Power Manager is ticked. I unticked it becuase I didn't think it was necessary but I guess not.
