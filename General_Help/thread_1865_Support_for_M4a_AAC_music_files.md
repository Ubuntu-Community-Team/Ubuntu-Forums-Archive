### Post by Faramirtook on 2004-10-23
When I switched, i just re-encoded with MP3. Do you still have the Mac? If so, you proabaly used iTunes, and therefore, you can. 

Set the bitrate higher, highlight them all, and right-click and choose "convert to MP3".


Create a smart playlist with AAC for the only requirement, and let it sit overnight. Re-name all the artists to the same thing, using the playlist wiht the AAC files, and then browse for the artist and delete the entry, then empty the trash beacuse you don't want to waste precious HD space!


if you're looking for a way to do it in Linux, I think you need to convert to WAV and then to OGG or something, or get a plug-in, and I have no idea how to do that.
