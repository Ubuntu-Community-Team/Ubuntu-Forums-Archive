### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-07-31
Your hard disk is failing - there are too many bad spots to compensate for. Back up all your data immediately and buy a new hard disk. Put the old one in the bin.

And then thank the folks at Red Hat for making the utility that gives you a warning.

If you keep using the disk, you WILL lose data. Don't delay. Unfortunately Windows isn't smart enough to tell you about the condition of your hard disk.
