probably way late to respond to this thread but its something thats bugged me for a long time being a windows -> linux switcher...

In firefox at least you can enable this behavior just by going to Edit > Preferences > Advanced > and then Select Autoscrolling in the browsing section.


Thats a start at least. havent tested it out in anything like open office or anything but I would assume it would be an option in their preferences as well...
