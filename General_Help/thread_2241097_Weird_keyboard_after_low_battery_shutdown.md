### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-08-24
Try putting a new button battery in your laptop.  Does your computer lose the time-of-day as well?  If the BIOS can't keep its settings (like numlock is on or off) then usually the button battery is dead and needs replacing.  How old is the laptop?

In the meantime, boot into BIOS and reset the Numlock setting.  That way you should not have to boot into a Live Session to fix it.  This should work as long as you leave the computer plugged in.  Does your main battery work OK?
