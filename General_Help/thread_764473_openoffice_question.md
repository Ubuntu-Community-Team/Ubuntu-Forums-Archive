### Post by munkyeetr on 2008-04-24
When you are in OO Writer, do the following:

1) **File** -> **Wizards** ->** Install new dictionaries...**
2) Choose the language you want
3) Press the **Start DictOOo **button
4) Click **Next**
5) Click** Retrieve the List**
6) Choose the dictionary you want, and press **Next**
7) Press **Next** again
8 When it is done press **Cancel**

You should now have a dictionary installed and your spell checker should work.
