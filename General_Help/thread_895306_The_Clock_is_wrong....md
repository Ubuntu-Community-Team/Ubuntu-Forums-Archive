### Post by chrisking8613 on 2012-02-18
Hey buddy,

     Your question was asked almost 4 years ago, but better late than never I suppose. Anyways, you can only adjust the date if your running it as root (this is difficult so don't bother). Instead, just make sure that you have the correct time zone selected in the "Date and Time" settings. Then you should see the time is correct in the "Date and Time" tab. However, your panel widget (bottom corner of the screen) will still be incorrect, to fix this simply right click and choose "Remove this digital clock" then go to "add widgets" in the panel and drag a new digital clock to your panel. This should work for you as it did for me : ).
