### Post by Cotopaxi on 2008-07-18
With Linex, I think you refer to the Spanish "Extremadura Linux".

Linex is based on Ubuntu, so all software available for Ubuntu via Synaptic should be available for Linex or Guadalinex. In Synaptic click on "search" and type: "game". I just got 1056 files listed. I will give you now two links: one for Linex and the second one for "Guadalinex", which is the Linux of the Andalucía region. Linex and Guadalinex are both based on Ubuntu

In: [www.linex.org](www.linex.org) - go to "Soporte Técnico" > Foro GnuLinEx", there you have a Spanish Forum.

In: [www.guadalinex.org](www.guadalinex.org) go to "¡Participa! > Foros", also a Spanish Forum.

Good luck!
