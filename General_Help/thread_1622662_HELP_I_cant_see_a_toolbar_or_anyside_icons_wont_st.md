> **T3ddyjunior said:**
> well 10.4 Ubuntu works on my PC I just don't think 10.10 is compatible with IBM think-pads.

why, because you installed the netbook version?

the new netbook ui is 3d accelerated, if your vid card can't do that, then yes you'll have problems.
they include the standard desktop just for cases like that & for people who need to install drivers first.

in most cases using the normal desktop works better for most people, it's easily tweaked & can do very well on netbooks & laptops just as well as the netbook specific ui.

i use a nettop computer, i've used pretty much all the versions & the standard desktop still offers the most choice.

on this install i've replaced gnome-panel with awn & use maximus to maximize all windows. i don't hide my panel, but there's always that option if i ever want the space.
