### Post by fragos on 2006-12-18
I've had no end of trouble trying to sync my Palm and Ubuntu.  What I ended up doing was geting a Palm program Card Reader from [www.mobile-stream.com](www.mobile-stream.com).  Run it while your Palm is the cradle or USB cable and the SD card on the Palm is recognized as a storage device on Linux.  Drag and drop onto /PALM/Launcher and those apps can be run from the SD.  Copy them on the Palm to RAM and they'll run like any other Palm ap.  One thing Linux teaches us is that there's alway more than one to do things.
