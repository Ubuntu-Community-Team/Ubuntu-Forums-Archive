### Post by llamabr on 2010-02-10
Yes.  The first thing you want to do is quit messing with that disk.  If the data are very valuable, you can take it to a professional, who can probably reclaim much of it for you.  this will be several hundred dollars.  If it's not worth that, the 'quit messing with it' still applies, but follow the above advice.  As a non expert, though, keep in mind that the more you mess with it, the more likely you're over writing relevant nodes, destroying old data.
