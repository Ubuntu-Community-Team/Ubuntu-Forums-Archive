### Post by jimrz on 2008-04-05
From XP you could d/l Thunderbird, export your stuff from outlook and import to Thunderbird. You could then save your Thunderbird profile etc and later use it in Thunderbird running Ubuntu. I believe that, though I have not done so, once you have your stuff in Thunderbird you can then transition it to Evolution. For your calendar, tasks, etc, you can save them as v-cards and later import to Ubuntu.
