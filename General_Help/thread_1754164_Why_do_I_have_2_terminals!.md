### Post by AlphaLexman on 2011-05-09
I can guess from your posts in 'Installations & upgrades' where you want to remove xubuntu and kubuntu...

That each window manager has installed it's own terminal.

[LIST]
[*]gnome = gnome-terminal
[*]kde = konsole
[*]xfce = xfce4-terminal
[/LIST]
You are seeing more than one option in gnome, or xfce, or kde, depending on which window manager you are currently in!

Remove the other window managers as indicated in my other posts, and you will be back to one terminal.
