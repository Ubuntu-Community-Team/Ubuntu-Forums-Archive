### Post by NotRoot:-) on 2007-12-12
Qui,

If Ubuntu 7.04 worked on your system, you can reinstall it from the CD.  Then just keep 7.04 patched and do not click on the update button.

An alternative would be to create another partition on your hard drive to load 7.04 into.  And select that O/S from the Grub Boot Manager during startup.
