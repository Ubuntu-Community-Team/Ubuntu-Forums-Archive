### Post by jenkinbr on 2009-02-13
Just out of curiosity, why 5.04?  It's almost 5 years old, and long out of support.

As to your question, I think it is possible, but probably only as read-only.  I think NTFS drivers of that time were buggy when it came to write support.

I reccomend you install Ubuntu 8.04 LTS or 8.10, availible at [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/) .  The latest drivers support NTFS in both Read and write.
