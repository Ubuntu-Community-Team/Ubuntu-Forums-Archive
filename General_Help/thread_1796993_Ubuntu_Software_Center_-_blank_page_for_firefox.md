### Post by Alwimo on 2011-07-05

> is this version of ubuntu still supported ?
Until April 2013. Every two years/four Ubuntu releases, there's a 'Long Term Support' (LTS) release, which gets longer support (three years) and has a focus on stability. Since 10.04 came out just over a year ago, you have just under two years left of updates.

Every other release of Ubuntu between the LTS releases gets a year and a half of support.
