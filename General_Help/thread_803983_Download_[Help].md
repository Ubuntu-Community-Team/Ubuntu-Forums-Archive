### Post by wrgb2 on 2008-05-22
In Firefox, go to Edit>Preferences.  Click on the Main icon at the top.  Under Downloads, next to "Save files to", check the directory name - this is where Firefox is downloading your .iso to.  
If all goes well in the install, Ubuntu will auto-discover your wired and maybe your wireless network.  Just bring up your browswer and try to go somewhere.  If not, just ask here - there's lots of help to be had.

### Post by wrgb2 on 2008-05-23
So have you found the .iso anywhere else?  It may be that Windows sees an .iso as a system file, and your desktop settings are not set to show hidden and system files?  Try browsing to the desktop in the file manager and make sure that View>Hidden and system files is enabled.
