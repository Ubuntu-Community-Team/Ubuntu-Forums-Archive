### Post by oldsoundguy on 2010-07-30
Think you fail to understand .. the connections from the screen to the base of the laptop are on a cable .. that cable can and does wear out after repeated use.

I have a friend in New Jersey that repairs laptops that have been tossed and turns them into machines that he gives away to schools and such (No, I can NOT convince him to put Linux on them, much as I try .. mostly because the schools there run Windows.)
Biggest issue with most of the laptops is the back lighting will go out or that cable needs to be replaced. Third issue is garbage that has gummed up the keyboard.

IF it flickers while being MOVED .. first .. stop moving it, and second .. yes, there could easily be a connection problem.

Always look at the mechanical issues before you blame the software as PARTS WEAR OUT.
