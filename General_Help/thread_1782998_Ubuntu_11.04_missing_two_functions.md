### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-06-15
The windows selector button or Workplace Switcher is on the Launcher, the panel on the left side of the screen. The switcher icon is a grey rectangle divided into four sections. Click on it and the four rectangles will appear enlarged. Double click on a rectangle and you switch work places. Before you say, that means extra clicks. What is the piont of that? Consider this:

You place Firefox in one workplace and Libreoffice in another. They both have an icon in the Launcher. A click on the Firefox icon will switch to the workplace with Firefix in it and once there a click on the Libreoffice icon will switch back to the Libreoffice workspace. Same number of clicks.

Also, try having several Libreoffice Witer documents open in the same workspace and then double click on the Libreoffice icon and see what happens. I find that switching workspaces is an effective way to reveal the desktop.

Regards.
