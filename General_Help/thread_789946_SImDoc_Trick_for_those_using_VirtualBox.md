### Post by shareme on 2008-05-11
For those of us on a Virtualization program without access to 3d we can only use SimDoc instead of things like AWN.

To get SimDOc to lay like AWN ie stay on top and have windows of apps at full size do the following:

1. move cursorto middle of simdoc and hold down middle mouse button and drag SimDOc to top gnome panel.

2. Now minimize the app you have open than go to one corner and resize to full screen.

DO step 2 for every app you will use.
