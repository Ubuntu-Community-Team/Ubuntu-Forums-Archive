### Post by sgtmcc on 2009-11-08
> **crowdedelevator said:**
> a commercial game, say, fallout, or c.o.d. or something like i dunno, metal gear solid, what would i do?
 how do i get games that dont SUCK like some of the linux games do?

There are a couple of other options, its just wine is the free (as in beer) one. Try to update your version of wine, because if you have the standard 1.0.1 it is a stable yet old release.( [http://www.winehq.org/download/deb](http://www.winehq.org/download/deb)) I have 1.1.32 on mine currently. You also need to make sure your 3D effects are enabled with Nvidia or ATI drivers, or mesa if you have an unsupported card.

The next alternative is cedega (formerly winex) [http://www.transgaming.com](http://www.transgaming.com) It does cost some money but not a lot, maybe $5 US a month. You get access to their gaming version of wine and their support pages where people will help you get a particular game going.

Another way is using crossover [www.codeweavers.com](www.codeweavers.com) Similar to cedega but not geared towards gaming as much as basic Windoze apps.
