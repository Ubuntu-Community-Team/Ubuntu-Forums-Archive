### Post by Dennis N on 2021-09-23
open the screenshot with gthumb. The 'i' icon will show the metadata. See (crop from) screenshot I just took of gthumb with opened screenshot of this page. Upper right shows exact date and time.

Edit: Even simpler, In Ubuntu at least, the default file name of the screenshot uses the time and date it was taken. That may be acceptable.
```
Screenshot from [COLOR="#FF0000"]2021-09-23 06-53-57[/COLOR].png
```

---

### Post by ajgreeny on 2021-09-23
Yes, that is the naming convention of Xubuntu as well so unless you changed the saved screenshot name, that should be what it's called.
