### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2013-03-22
I suspect driver problems. Blender uses 3D acceleration for its interface as well as its rendering (if I recall), so nothing would be drawn if you didn't have any 3D. Pretty much everything has 3D these days though, so I suspect you need a driver. This would also explain what compiz is up to.

Post your hardware when you get back, and we'll see what we can do. Good luck!
