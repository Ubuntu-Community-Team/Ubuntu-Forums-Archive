### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-01-26
Printing PDF files sometimes seems to be a problem for evince in particular.

If that is what you're using, try another PDF viewer such as qpdfview or okular.  The latter is a KDE app and will pull in lots of qt and kdelibs but it is very good in my opinion; it will sometimes display a PDF file that evince baulks at for some reason.
