### Post by fragos on 2008-07-30
If your likk modem is an analog device, left click the network icon in the upper applet bar and select "manual configuration." The "network settings" window will pop up. Click the Unlock button and enter your password. Select "point to point connection" and click Properties button. All the parameters you need will be in the "ppp0 Properties" window.
