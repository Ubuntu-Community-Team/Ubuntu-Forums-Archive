### Post by erpo on 2008-01-13
Support for built-in card readers is poor. On my laptop, my 4-in-1 card reader only works with SD cards in Linux even though it works for SD and xD cards (among others) in Windows.

As a workaround, you can get a cheap 12-in-1 USB card reader. Since the cards show up as USB mass storage devices to Linux, they are handled properly.
