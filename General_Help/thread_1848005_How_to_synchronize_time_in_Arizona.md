How do you synchronize the time to Arizona?  I tried the GUI and it lets me select mountain time or pacific time, but was not able to figure out how to select Arizona time. Arizona does not have daylight savings time.

---

### Post by patryk77 on 2011-09-21
In regular Ubuntu (Gnome), click on the clock, expand Locations and click Edit.

Then Add a location, type Arizona and choose the closest city to your place from the list. If needed, you can set it as the Home Location from the list below the map.
