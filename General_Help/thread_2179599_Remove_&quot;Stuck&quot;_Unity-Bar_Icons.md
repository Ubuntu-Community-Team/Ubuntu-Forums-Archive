### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-10-09
The show desktop icon is not installed by default. We need to tick something to get it there. And we can unlock hard drive/partition icons from the Launcher, at least in 13.10 we can. I am not even going to try putting the work space switcher icon in the trash. Personally I reduce the default size of the Launcher to its minimum and set Autohide to On and that gives me the full wide open space of the monitor screen, except for the global menu at the top. With Compiz Configuration Settings Manager we can reduce the Launcher size even further and set the global menu to transparent. I have not tried this with 13.10 but it does work in 13.04. And then we can install something like Unity Tweak. That may achieve other things. I cannot say.

Stop and think of the convergence concept. All design decisions being taken in Ubuntu are targeted to the ideal of a beautiful, usable user interface that will work and scale on three, form factors, phone, tablet, desktop, with TV in the back of the mind at the moment. There is logic in the seeming design madness.

Regards.
