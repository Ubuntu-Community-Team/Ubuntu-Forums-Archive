### Post by dominiquec on 2011-08-17
Quick answer: no.

Longer answer: I wouldn't wouldn't worry about it.  It's hard enough mounting the host Windows partition on Wubi.  A virus-writer wouldn't bother with that avenue of attack, not when there are easier targets.

See [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=684877](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=684877)
