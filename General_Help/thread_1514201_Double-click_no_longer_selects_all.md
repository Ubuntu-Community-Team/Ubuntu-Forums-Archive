### Post by x-shaney-x on 2010-06-20
Just to add:
I have checked on a second user account and double-click select all is working as normal in every app.


<EDIT>
Should have known it was somet stupidly simple (and caused by me).
After comparing things in both accounts I found the solution:
System > Preferences > Mouse > General > Double-Click Timeout

The slider was a few notches along on the other account and all the way to the left on my main account.
Once I set it to match it is now all working.
