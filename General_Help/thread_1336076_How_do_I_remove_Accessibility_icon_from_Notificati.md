### Post by marmux on 2009-12-13
Try this:

1.Click on "System"
2. Click on "Preferences"
3. Click on Keyboard
4. Go to the Accessibility tab
5. Untick 'Accessibility features can be toggled with keyboard shortcuts'

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/netbook-remix-launcher/+bug/437747](https://bugs.launchpad.net/netbook-remix-launcher/+bug/437747)
