### Post by jamessnell on 2009-04-24
iTunes -> I use [Mozilla Songbird]("http://getsongbird.com/"). My installs of it are getting to be fairly out of date so the fact that they're a bit unstable probably doesn't matter any more.

Google Earth -> I rarely use it, but it seemed stable to me, could you be having a video driver issue? What card are you using? Will the system let you enable 3D Desktop Effects? (is OpenGL Direct Rendering supported?)

Paper Port -> ? Maybe you could try something like Google Documents, as it provides the sharing & revision control features that paper port looks okay for. Open Office is a fairly mature office suite and it'll export documents to PDF.
