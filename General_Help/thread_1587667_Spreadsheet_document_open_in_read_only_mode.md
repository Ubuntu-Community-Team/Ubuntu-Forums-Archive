### Post by DeMus on 2010-10-04
> **sofasurfer said:**
> I created a spreadsheet which I had edited repeatedly. Today I ran out of disk space and needed to resize some partitions. After that proceedure I opened the spreadsheet and when I click on a cell to see the formula I get the message "document opened in read only mode". Other documents open fine. What happened?

I don't know what happened but you can try this. Close the spreadsheet and open nautilus, move to the correct folder, right-click on the spreadsheet and choose properties. Then open TAB Permissions and change the setting for you as user to "Read and Write".
This should set things straight again.
