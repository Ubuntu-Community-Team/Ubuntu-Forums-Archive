How do you skip lines (without pressing enter because it gets too repetitive)? My teacher told me to make it double spaced so that she can correct my work. So...help please.

Thanks in advance!

---

### Post by jcsteele on 2007-12-01
On the menu bar, click on "Format" then "Paragraph..."

There you will see an option for "Line Spacing" and set it to "Double" and you are all finished....
