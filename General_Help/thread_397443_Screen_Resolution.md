### Post by CocoAUS on 2007-03-30
There's a [much better solution]("http://othello.alma.edu/~07tmhopk/ubuntuhowto.html#resolution") for you.  Rather than messing around with all that unnecessary stuff, simply add your desired resolution to xorg.conf, and replace the included HorizSync and VertRefresh values with the correct values for your monitor.  Then restart X.  The process is explained more in the link, and it only takes a minute.
