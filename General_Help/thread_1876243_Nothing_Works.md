### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-11-06
The mouse and DVD reader should work out-of-the-box; there's no reason why it wouldn't. (DVD decryption requires an extra easy-to-install plugin)

I know it sounds like a cop-out, but perhaps you just need to reinstall Mythbuntu again. I had an experience recently where the first install just didn't work, and the mouse worked intermittantly and the system crashed often while booting up. Just doing a reinstall, even though the first install was only an hour old, actually worked. Go figure.

But yes, usually Ubuntu is very easy. Your problems are surprising.

The other thing is, you might be experiencing hardware failure - such as a problem with an internal USB port on your motherboard, or bad RAM. I believe bad RAM caused one of my DVD burners to not actually be usable once; maybe test your RAM as well and see if it turns up any errors.
