### Post by black veils on 2012-09-27
that method will open your email in the web browser, but it wont start to compose new mail with the chosen attachment. if that doesnt matter to you, then here is how:

1. settings manager >> preferred applications

2. under the header *mail reader*, select the button/menu available

3. choose *other*, then paste in ```
firefox your-yahoo-email-url
``` and press *ok*


 if you want the email to start composing and add the attachment automatically, you need to setup your yahoo web account to work within an email application on your xubuntu instead.

you dont have to commit to the email application, but it needs to be setup.

### Post by Linux_junkie on 2012-09-27
From what you have told us.   

You want to send a document (MS Excel) via your email address.   Now as far as I know you cannot do that using the method that others have told you i.e. setting up firefox to connect to your email account.  You need to use an email client (program) soething like Thunderbird.  Do you have Thunderbird installed? Or have you any other email client installed?

To set up your email client in Xubuntu:
1. Click Settings in the menu.
2. Click on Settings Manager.
3. Click on Preferred Applications.
4. Where is says Mail Reader click on the arrow pointing down
5. Select the email client (program) from the list.
6. Close down window.

This will set up your email client so it will be used by Xubuntu whenever you instruct another program you want to send an email.

You will also need to set up your email account for that program.

Once you have done that you can then send your document via email from within OpenOffice.

Hope this has helped.
