### Post by dino99 on 2012-10-29
Some times ago i have had a similar issue, and finally understood that was an usb issue; in fact my ps2 keyboard plugged via an adaptor. Changing it for a direct usb plug solve the issue.
Maybe first pay attention at usb bios settings; and of course clean your system as much as you can (clean/autoclean/autoremove/deborphan/bleachbit)
