### Post by Kris_M on 2016-01-02
Mod move if necessary! :)
I'l put this here instead of cafe as it relates to dynamic problem solving. - and a solution.

Been on 14.04.3 for a couple weeks - new (relatively) to Linux).
I suddenly realized that TBird was no longer giving me notifications.  For some reason, my thoughts immediately went to "oh darn, after all this, I better go back to Win7" This was even though I had solved all my problems over the past 2 weeks on 14.04.3 via this forum and google.

So I googled and didn't really find anything.
For some reason I checked System Settings/Sound and discovered my vol was very low and mute was checked - oops! How???
So fixed that and got my -pop back.
But realized I had never received a visual notification on Ubuntu. Read on!
I posted a question to this forum and then went and googled some more.
I ran into a report of a bug on launchpad.
It spoke to exactly the same thing and mentioned TBird version 38 as the problem (I had been running 38 since I started 2 weeks ago).
I grabbed 31.8 (according to TBird the latest stable)(why oh why does TBird update everybody to 38???)(LOL) and installed it and tested. Voila - a graphic visual notification in the lower right of the screen when an email comes in.

I subscribed to that launchpad - so they would know another person had that problem, and so I would know if there was any progress on that bug - whether fault of TBird or Ubuntu.
I learned how to set up a desktop link to start it (more googling!)(2 terminal commands).  I have no problem using 31.8 as it works just fine.

Moral of story - google lots and/even if you ask on these forums!
