### Post by WlaadDyaab on 2011-03-12
You find Kdenlive (another video editing program) by:

Click on Applications - Ubuntu Software Centre - Type or copy/paste in the search bar "Kdenlive" - click on Install

To find this program after you downloaded it:

Click on Applications - Sound & Video - Kdenlive (it should be there)

If you don't know how to use Kdenlive I'll be very glad to help. If you do know how to use it, then I hope you solve your problem :)
