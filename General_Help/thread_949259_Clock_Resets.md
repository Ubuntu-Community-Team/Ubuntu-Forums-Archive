### Post by jerome1232 on 2008-10-16
If the hardware clock is reseting every boot up it's a good indication your cmos battery is dyeing.

First thing I would do is change it out, If you look around on your motherboard there should be a silver battery a little bit bigger than a quarter, that's the cmos battery.
