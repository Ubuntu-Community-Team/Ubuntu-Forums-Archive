### Post by Jonor on 2013-05-20
Something that might help is to find a music file on your system such as an mp3, right click on it to get to properties and then click on the Open With tab and move it out of either the Default Application or Recommended Applications categories, easily done with a bit more mouse work. It may fix your problem as a knock on effect.
