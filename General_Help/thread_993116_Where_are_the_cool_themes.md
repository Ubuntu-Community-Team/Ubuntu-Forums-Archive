### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-11-25
How about installing some window managers? Just go to: **Main Menu > System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager**, and search for *window manager*,
like **KDE** or **Xfce**.
After having installed one, it's just a matter of logging out, and changing the session, under options.
Hope this helps.
