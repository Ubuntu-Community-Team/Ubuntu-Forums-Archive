### Post by lisati on 2010-04-06
One way to check the iso file before you burn it onto a disk is with [md5sums]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM").

Once you have [burned it to disk]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto") and booted from it, there is usually an option "check disk for defects" (or similar)

By the way, it's probably 9.10 that you downloaded, not 9.01. The "9" is for 2009, and the "10" is for October.
