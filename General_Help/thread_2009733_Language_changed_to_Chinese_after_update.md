### Post by yoramdavid on 2012-06-25
I solved my problem.
Luckily I met someone who could read some chinese and I opened the keyboard layout / language and asked him to tell me which entry corresponded to the Portuguese language, changed it, logged off and in again and it worked. There was no more Chinese on the system. Just the key board was still all messed up. It was set to Portuguese (Native) and the keys did not correspond. I added the Portuguese layout and it worked. Now all is good.
There are still many entries in the language options fro Chinese.
