### Post by westie457 on 2011-12-28
Hi it should be possible however upgrading this way there is more chance of things going wrong. Usually for such a big leap from old to new it is better to download the iso, burn the image to a bootable medium and clean install.

That is the sensible option however if you still want to do this the hard way open 'Update Manager' and click on 'Settings...'. In the pop up window you should be in the 'Updates' tab. At the bottom you should find **release upgrade** or something similar. Your options are there (never, normal releases and LTS)
