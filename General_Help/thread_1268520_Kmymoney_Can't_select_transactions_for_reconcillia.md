### Post by gwads71 on 2009-09-19
I ran into a problem with reconciliation too -- not sure if it's the same as the one you have.  I finally got it to work after I realized that the "opening date" on the account was much later (it defaults to the date you create the account in kmymoney).  After changing the date on the account (if you're at the ledger screen, select Accounts --> Edit Account...), it seemed to solve the problem.

### Post by Hei Ku on 2009-09-23
For reconciliation, click on the status column to clear your transactions. Once you are done with the reconciliation click on Finish. And yes, the reconciliation is done on the same ledger window.
