### Post by sauen on 2012-01-30
You can sell a PC with Linux, even if you modify it. You can't sell anything based on Linux, that is, if it's the operating system you are charging for. Android runs on Linux, that is why anyone can install and use it, even the Chinese :), and even though it has been modified. Any software based on Linux cannot be sold, but anything with Linux can be sold. Do they sell Android phones?
