### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-03-29
No, I'm afraid startup-manager is only of use at the moment for minor changes to 9.10, and can not do anything for theming or decoration of startups.

You can change the grey/brown screen that appears just before and after  the login dialog screen by  renaming the file /usr/share/images/xsplash/bg_2560x1600.jpg
as a backup and replacing it with another image given that same name.
