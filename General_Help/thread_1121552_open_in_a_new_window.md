### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-04-10
In nautilus you can set this option (spatial) by unchecking the "Always open in browser windows" in the *Preferences - Behaviour* tab.  You must be unusual, as most people changed from that to the current browser behaviour in the past when spatial was the default.
