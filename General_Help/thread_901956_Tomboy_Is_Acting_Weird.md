### Post by aloshbennett on 2008-08-27
When you double click "All Notes", you are not creating a note. You are creating a note template. Thats why its not listed under notes.

There's not way to delete a template from UI. You can though delete the corresponding .note file from ~/.tomboy folder. Restart tomboy and you are good to go :)
