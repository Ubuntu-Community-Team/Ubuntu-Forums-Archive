### Post by IWantFroyo on 2012-04-27
Open your Update Manager. You should have a little button asking whether or not you want to upgrade to 12.04. Don't click on it unless it says 12.04 on it.

My personal recommendation is that you back up your Eclipse projects on the web or flash drive, and do a fresh install of Precise. Upgrades can go wrong if you tinkered with your system.
