### Post by chggr on 2007-02-23
You don't need to reinstall the application in order to create an icon for it on your panel. Just do the following:

1) Right click on the panel
2) "Add to panel..."
3) "Custom Application Laucher"
4) Fill in the application name, a comment, possibly a command, etc and press OK
5) The icon should be there!
