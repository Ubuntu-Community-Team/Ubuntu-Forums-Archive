### Post by mlalkaka on 2006-10-26
The instructions above are incomplete.
1. Go to File > Print... . 
2. Select your printer in the "Printer Name" pull-down menu.
3. Click on the "Properties" button.
4. In the "Gap from edge of paper to Margin" frame, increase the values in the fields "Top", "Bottom", "Left", and "Right" to something more reasonable.  Mine are set to 0.3, 0.3, 0.3, 0.3.

Hope that helps
