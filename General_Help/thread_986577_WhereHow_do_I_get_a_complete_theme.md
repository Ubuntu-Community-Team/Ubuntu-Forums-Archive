### Post by metalgtr on 2008-11-18
Yes. I like [http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/) to download themes, icons, etc. For instance, if you want to download a new iconset, download a tarball and drag&drop it into the themes window (by either System > Preferences > Appearance > Themes or just right-click on the desktop > change desktop background > Themes). This can also be done from a terminal window, but this way is easier.
