### Post by WatchingThePain on 2009-03-13
Gnome : Right click the ubuntu icon top left of desktop choose 'edit menus'.
Click 'internet' then type 'pidgin' for name and command and then 'ok'.pidgin should appear in gnome menu under 'applications', 'internet'. Right click it and choose 'add to panel'
This will restore it to the panel.
