### Post by Darkade on 2008-07-07
I don't think they are rally needed. I think linux is a great OS because it's stable, fast, and great administrating resources. Effects are a "cool"  add-on, some of them can increase productivity, others are just eye candy. However I think desktop effects prove that linux won't "eat" your system. even with desktop effects working, levels of resources consumption are LOW.
My personal experience: I have effects enabled in my desktop and disabled in my laptop, and people that have tried linux out of "me showing it to them" have tried linux out of performance not the looks
