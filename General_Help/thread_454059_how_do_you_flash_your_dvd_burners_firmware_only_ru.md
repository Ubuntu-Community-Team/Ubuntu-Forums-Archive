### Post by orb9220 on 2007-05-25
This site has what you need.
[http://tdb.rpc1.org/](http://tdb.rpc1.org/)

They have dos flasher and firmware for many drives including yours.

What you have to do tho is create a bootable blank disk then copy the dos and firmware file to it.
Then boot the floopy at the a: you type the command example: f**lasher.exe d112-f1.bin** with the **flasher.exe** being the program and **d112-f1.bin** being the firmware file and hit enter then you would be prompted to upgrade your firmware.
