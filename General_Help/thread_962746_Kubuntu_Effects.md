### Post by Amburlin on 2008-11-10
in Kubuntu Ibex to enable and edit the screen effects go to system settings, desktop and in the general tab that comes up, click to enable effects. Next to the general tab there is a tab that says all effects. For any of the effects in this list, just click to enable the ones you want. Some of them you'll want to edit the keystrokes to initialise the effect (with the little wrench on the side). You then need to apply changes. Some changes may not take effect until you log out and back in, though they *should* all take effect immediately
