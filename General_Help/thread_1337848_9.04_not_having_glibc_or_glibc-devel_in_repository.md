### Post by ibuclaw on 2009-11-25
You need not google-fu, young padawan. ;)
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?suite=jaunty&arch=any&mode=exactfilename&searchon=contents&keywords=stubs-32.h](http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?suite=jaunty&arch=any&mode=exactfilename&searchon=contents&keywords=stubs-32.h)

As it says there, stubs-32.h is "not for amd64". I suggest you update your sources.

Change any occurrence of "stub-32.h" with "stub-64.h"

edit:
Or even better, just replace "stubs-32.h" with:
```
#include <gnu/stubs.h>
```
and that will make the source just that little bit more 32bit/64bit cross compatible.
