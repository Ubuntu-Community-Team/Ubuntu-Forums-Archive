### Post by mcduck on 2011-07-22
You don't need any extra software to mount .iso images, just right-click one and select "Open With Archive Mounter".

What comes to Windows apps being compatible with Linux, none of them is. They might be compatible with Wine, though. ;) Anyway, the site you are looking for would be this: [http://appdb.winehq.org/]("http://appdb.winehq.org/")
