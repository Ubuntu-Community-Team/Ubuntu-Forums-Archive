### Post by strabes on 2007-10-14
ATI refuses to provide acceptable linux drivers for their cards so AIGLX does not work. They are supposedly working on drivers that are going to be released this month, but actions speak louder than words. 

You can use XGL instead, although it's slower, less stable, and just all around pretty bad. To try it out, install the xserver-xgl package.
