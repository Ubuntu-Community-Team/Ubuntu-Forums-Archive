### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-11-11
Nice remote.  Microsoft makes some decent hardware.   

It is not plug and play, there are several steps that you need to perform to get it to work.  Take notes, and work carefully:  [http://kodi.wiki/view/HOW-TO:Set_up_an_MCE_remote_control_in_Linux](http://kodi.wiki/view/HOW-TO:Set_up_an_MCE_remote_control_in_Linux)

[http://askubuntu.com/questions/607068/how-to-get-mce-remote-working-with-kodi-on-ubuntu-14-10](http://askubuntu.com/questions/607068/how-to-get-mce-remote-working-with-kodi-on-ubuntu-14-10)

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1938291](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1938291)

[http://pdxpastimes.blogspot.com/2014/11/howto-get-mce-usb-remote-working-in.html](http://pdxpastimes.blogspot.com/2014/11/howto-get-mce-usb-remote-working-in.html)

If you get a new one, chances are it will be USB3 and there may be some issues.  Perhaps look for an older/used one that is known to work with the LIRC framework.
