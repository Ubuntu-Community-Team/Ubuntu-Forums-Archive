### Post by electrogeist on 2009-01-11
Well I think that should work, but I think that may be a very low end card.  #'s can be confusing.  AFAICT the lower 8000's and 9000's can sometimes be worse than higher 7000's or even 6000's.  The pros are it is a pretty cheap new card with immediate gratification from walmart.  IMHO used is the way to go, I haven't bought a brand new graphics card for years.  People are always upgrading.  Check your local craigslist, and for sale forums at places like anandtech or hardforum.
