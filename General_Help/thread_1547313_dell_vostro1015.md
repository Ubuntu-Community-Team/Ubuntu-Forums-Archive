### Post by DrScum on 2010-08-06
you have to change the boot options in the setup and make the dvd drive the first drive where the system looks for an OS. 

I am not entirely shure how to enter the setup on a vostro but I think you have to hit F2 during initial booting.

In the setup look for something like boot options and make the dvd drive the first and the harddrive the second priority.
