### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-09
After a bit of research,  I found a few articles discussing using Nokia phones as Bluetooth modems.  I couldn't find anything specifically for the XpressMusic, but from what I can tell all Nokias are a bit tricky in this area.

Here's a [guide]("http://blog.macuyiko.com/2009/01/nokia-n95-3g-bluetooth-and-ubuntu.html") to get a Nokia N95 working on Intrepid that will probably work with your phone as well.

Here's a [step-by-step guide]("http://ubuntu.sg/ubuntu-guides/network/47-nokia-n95-as-3g-modem") for the N95 for Hardy.  It will probably work for Intrepid as well.

If that doesn't work, here's a [slightly different method]("http://badank.wordpress.com/2008/07/08/use-nokia-7600-modem-on-ubuntu-804-hardy-heron-via-bluetooth/") for the Nokia 7600, also for Hardy.

All three methods are somewhat similar, and basically seem to be doing the same thing different ways.  But one may work for you where another might not.
