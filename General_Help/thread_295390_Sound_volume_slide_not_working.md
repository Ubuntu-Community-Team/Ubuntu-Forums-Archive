### Post by aum11 on 2006-11-08
Sound is working fine except that the volume slide on the panel is inoperative.  Thus the volume has to be changed via each individual application( e.g amarok, totem).The volume slider on the top panel does not effect sound at all.  This has only happened since switching to Edgy a few days ago.

When I double click the volume icon I can see that the PC speaker control goes up and down when the panel control goes up and down....but no effect on volume.

Alsamixer looks fine.

This has got to be a simple one surely but I cannot find it.  Quite an inconvenience. 

Any help please.

---

### Post by Cable on 2006-11-08
Right click on the panel item and choose preferences.  Make sure that your sound card and **Master **are selected.

---

### Post by aum11 on 2006-11-08
Thanks Cable... took me a while but realised that I needed to double click on master under alsa...previously the panel volume was connected to PC speaker.

I mention this because others may have the same problem after an Edgy install.

Great to have it working properly again.
