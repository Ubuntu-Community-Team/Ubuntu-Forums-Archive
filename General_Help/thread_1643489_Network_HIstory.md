### Post by dcstar on 2010-12-12
The short answer is that computers have to do many things and they share their resources between these various demands.

Just using a network connection is not the sole thing a computer does, and when other things require attention then that will have to wait its turn.

If you want better network performance then purchase and install a server rated LAN card and see the difference. BTW a decent LAN card will probably cost twice as much as the whole motherboard you are currently using.

If you want to really learn about this then spend a year or so of your life in a university level course learning about network protocols, hardware design and operation and how interrupt driven devices work in PC hardware. That will start your understanding.
