### Post by Cecil on 2005-02-02
[QUOTE=Suzan]Definitly a hardware issue!

It's the missing cable from your cd-drive to your soundcard. Open your computer and put it in.[/QUOTE]Actually, I just solved it. You see, I don't have an actual soundcard, the sound hardware is integrated into the motherboard. In XMMS, I found the Preferences Option, then after looking around, I chose to use the CD Audio Player 1.2.10 plugin, and inside there was an option for digital playback of CDs, which I enabled. I guess I should've mentioned my sound hardware was integrated, nor is there any place to plug-in any cables between it and the CD drive. Thanks, anyway.
