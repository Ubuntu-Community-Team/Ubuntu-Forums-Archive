### Post by Defrector on 2009-03-21
1. use apt-get or synaptic to reinstall any plasma stuff you have installed.

2. in terminal: 

```
kquitapp plasma && plasma
```

3. Wait for plasma to restart. You should see fireworks in your terminal while this happens.

4. Close terminal

5. ???

6. PROFIT!

If that doesn't work, the widgets you are attempting to use might not be up-to-date as plasma is pretty unstable development-wise right now.
