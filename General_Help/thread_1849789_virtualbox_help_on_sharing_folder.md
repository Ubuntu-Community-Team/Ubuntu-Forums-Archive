as long as you have the extensions installed and the vbox additions the just choose shared folders to share between a host and guest.

as for guest to host, then i dont think you can choose it as a setting but you can share a folder in your guest and then connect to it as a share from your host like any other share on a network.

You can also drop the files in your guest into the shared folders folder on the host.

either way you have access to the same files in the guest or the host
