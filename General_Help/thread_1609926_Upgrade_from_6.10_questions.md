### Post by trikster_x on 2010-10-31
I doubt the newer versions of the config files would be fully compatible with the older versions, so you would probably have to reconfigure everything anyway.  And honestly, it would probably be better to do that, since it will allow you to familiarize yourself with the new interfaces and options some of the programs are bound to have gained in the last four years.  The easiest way to back everything up would be to copy whatever you need from your home folder to an external drive, or seperate partition, then paste in what you need as it comes up.
