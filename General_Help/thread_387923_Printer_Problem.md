### Post by brazov on 2007-05-02
I have the same problem with Kubuntu and the printer HP Deskjet D2360 (installed as "D2300 series"). Test print: perfect. However it does not print anything from whatsoever application. If I look into the queue history, the files appear as successfully printed. Help!!!

***Problem solved*** today, May 3, 2007. I re-installed, selecting in the matching thread the appearence of the printer not belonging to the "USB" root. 

Remark: when you are going to print from an application, check in the "printer properties" that the paper is A4. Otherwise you will not obtain any error message, but the printer simply doesn't print.
