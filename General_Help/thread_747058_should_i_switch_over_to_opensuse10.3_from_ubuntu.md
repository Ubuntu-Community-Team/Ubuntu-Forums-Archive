### Post by LaRoza on 2008-04-06
Ubuntu should work fine on that. Perhaps the Hardy beta might be worth trying out.

Also, a RAM upgrade couldn't hurt. Adding another 512 MB will be cheap, and will give it more of a kick.

If you are running Compiz, I suggest turning it off as well.

OpenSuSE is not lighter than Ubuntu, and would run slower on that.
