### Post by WorMzy on 2011-01-13
The password thing sounds like the screensaver is kicking in and locking the screen, you can stop that by changing the setting under System -> Preferences -> Screensaver. I can't be any more specific than that as I don't use gnome-screensaver. The lock screen option should be apparent though, just uncheck the box next to it to disable it. You may want to increase the time it takes for the screensaver to kick in too.
