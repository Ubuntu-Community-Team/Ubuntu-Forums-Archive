### Post by approaching on 2007-12-19
deleting forever means just that, and moving something to the trash bin is also just that. you are not getting back that one you deleted forever, unless someone out there has a nifty trick that i haven't heard about (more than entirely possible). but unless you are absolutely sure you want to never see that again, move it to the trash bin.

and if this is a problem, just get rid of the delete forever functionality, i believe that you had to enable it in the first place, so you should know where it is. you can still empty the trash bin from other places, and isn't that bad of an option unless you are a freak about keeping everything off of your hard drive.

hope it wasn't important!

(as a side note about lost files, make sure you do a save as when you open something from firefox and edit it. often times firefox will save that file to /tmp by default, which when you turn your computer off for the night, doesn't come back when you turn your computer on in the morning to print your essay...)
