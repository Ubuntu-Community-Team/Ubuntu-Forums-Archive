### Post by whoop on 2009-04-22
Updating your system will make it as fresh as technically possible... Possibly even fresher than a yet to be released iso file.

So an RC ISO will most likely not be the same as a final ISO, but when both are installed on different machines and both machines are updated, then both machines will have the same software (versions) on it.
