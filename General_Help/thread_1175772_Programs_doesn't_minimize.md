### Post by Therion on 2009-06-01
Right-click on the bottom panel.
Click on "Add to Panel".
Add the applet for "Windows List - Show as buttons" or words to that effect.  
Then you can slide the little indicator around on your panel to suit your preferences and lock it into place.
