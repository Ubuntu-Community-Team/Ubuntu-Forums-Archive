### Post by cmnorton on 2009-01-31
You have already probably the best answer. It's the box. 

You can try another USB slot, or try your mouse through a hub.

It could be the slot has a slight fault and does not hold the USB plug snugly enough and moving the mouse loosens the connection.

Have you tried a wireless mouse?

Have you tried a PS/2 mouse?
