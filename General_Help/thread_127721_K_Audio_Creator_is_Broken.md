### Post by emanaton on 2007-02-25
I know this is an old thread, but I came here in search of the this answer, so will post a report of my success here.

I had previously opened the System Notifications sound assignment area and disabled all sounds using the Quick Controls section. However, I still received the broken glass noise. To fix the problem, I had to specifically enable and then disable sound on the entry for "A critical message is being shown" in the KDE System Notification group.
