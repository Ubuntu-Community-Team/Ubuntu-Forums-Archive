### Post by higashi on 2009-02-24
okay i got it. go to  Tools > options...   on the left, select "language settings" and expand it.
Next, click 'languages' . Under where it says 'Default languages for documents" , you will see it says "Western" and then to the right, there's a dropdown menu. click that dropdown menu and select the language you want.
In this case, im guessing you want english, so make sure that the english you have selected has a little "abc" icon next to it in the dropdown menu. Those are the supported languages.

Hope my instructions helped.
