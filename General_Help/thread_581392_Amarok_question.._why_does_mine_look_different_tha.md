### Post by nrwilk on 2007-10-19
The lyrics view is on a separate tab in the left pane of the window.  Look at the top of the pane and click on the tab labeled "lyrics."

You have misinterpreted the cover manager as a cover browser.  It is a tool to manage cover artwork, not to browse through your music collection.  To get to it, go to the tools menu and click on "Cover Manager."

Can you please post a screenshot to show us how yours looks?  I've haven't encountered any Amarok that doesn't have these features.

### Post by r-mo on 2007-10-19
For the cover manager go to Tools->Cover Manager

For Last.fm go to Settings->Configure Amarok  Last.fm is one of the tabs on the left, put in your user name and password and you'll get related stuff on the context tab now.

Lyrics and Artist (Wikipedia) should be there on the context tab, only when you're playing a track though.
