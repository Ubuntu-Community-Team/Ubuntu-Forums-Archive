### Post by Nano_ext3 on 2009-03-03
I had a little trouble playing videos with miro right away.  The video would start to play, but no audio, then the video would cut back to the playlist.  For some reason miro was not reading the source folder right.  

To fix this, add the /home/USERNAME/.miro path to your "folders" in OPTIONS in the miro program it self.  UNCHECK show as folder, or it will not work.

This solved it for me, and I hope it does for you :)

Cheers!
