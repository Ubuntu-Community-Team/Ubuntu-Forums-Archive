### Post by Littleweseth on 2009-09-30
Firstly: why not gmail? Seriously, you may change ISP's (at which point you lose your email account with that ISP), but gmail is forever.*

To answer your question, though, why don't you try a mail notifier widget? This will tell you when you have mail; then you use a Real Email Client (thunderbird?) to actually read it.

Check this for email notifier widgets:
[http://www.linuxlinks.com/Software/Internet/Mail/Notification/index.shtml](http://www.linuxlinks.com/Software/Internet/Mail/Notification/index.shtml)

* 'forever' = 'longer than your ISP is going to be around.'
