### Post by Brandon Williams on 2009-05-04
I think you mean "the panel", right? Not "the system tray". I wouldn't expect most applications to show up in the system tray. In fact, they really shouldn't.

To get the apps to show up in the panel, right click on the panel, select "Add to Panel" from the pop-up menu, and add the "Window List" applet.
