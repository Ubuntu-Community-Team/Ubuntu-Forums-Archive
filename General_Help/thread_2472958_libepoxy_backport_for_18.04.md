### Post by QIII on 2022-03-18
Better than a PPA would be finding a backport in the repos.  PPAs are "Personal Package Archives" and you are at the mercy of the random maintainer.

It looks like 1.5.4-1 is available from 20.04.  1.5.9-2 is available in 22.04, but 22.04 is still in development.  It is never guaranteed that a backport will work, however.  Nor is there any guarantee that a particular package will be available for backport.
