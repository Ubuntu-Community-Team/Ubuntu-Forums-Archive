### Post by crashsystems on 2009-03-21
You can look at the contents of /var/log/syslog, as important stuff often ends up in there. You could even attach it as a file to a reply. Can you give us more details on how it is shutting off? Does it shut down, or does the power just suddenly cut off? If it is suddenly cutting off, is the fan blowing, perhaps rather hard? If this was the case, I would assume that the processor was getting too hot.
