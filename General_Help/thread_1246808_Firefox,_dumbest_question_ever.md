### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-08-22
In Firefox preferences go to the content tab then in the Fonts & Colours section click the Advanced button.  A dialog will appear that allows you to set font sizes and stop websites choosing their own font sizes. Just deselect that tick-box.  That should do the trick.
