### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-02-06
Without seeing what you have described, this is a bit of a guess. I figure that your Compiz settings might have been adjusted.

Install CompizConfig Settings Manager ([ccsm]("apt:ccsm")) and see if you can fix the settings there; I think you may need the Desktop Wall settings.

Change one thing at a time to test, so that if something goes wrong, you know what to reset.
