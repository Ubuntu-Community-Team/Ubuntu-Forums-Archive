### Post by ellgor on 2008-05-21
Hi,

Try this, make sure you have Alsamixer(and the gui) open a terminal and type alsamixer, a pretty basic box with columns representing your different hardware should be displayed, use the arrow keys to move along the columns until you highlight Mic, the small box at the bootom of the column should be green (indicating its on) if its not press M to enable it, also if the volume is low use the up and down keys to adjust, that should be it, hope this helps.

Regards, Ellgor.
