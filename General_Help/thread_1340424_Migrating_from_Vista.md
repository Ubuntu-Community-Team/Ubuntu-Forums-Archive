### Post by fluffman86 on 2009-11-28
For the USB drive, follow these instructions:

1.  Plug in the drive.

Ok that's it!  Assuming the drive was unmounted properly in Windows, all you have to do plug it in, and wait for Ubuntu ask you what to do, or else it will show up in Places or on the Desktop.

If for some reason it wasn't disconnected properly last time, it should still show up in Places, but when you click on it you'll get an error message with directions for forcing the mount.

Don't know about evolution, though.  

Edit: Maybe you could ask google how to export your files in the proper format for gmail, import them into gmail, then export them in the proper format to go from gmail to evolution?
