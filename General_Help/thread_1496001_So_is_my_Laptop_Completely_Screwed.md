### Post by Drake2k on 2010-05-28

I agree with Jerenept.  When you boot to a live cd, go to 'computer' and see if you can find the physical hard disk.  Drill down to fine /home/yourname.  Everything you downloaded, worked on in open office, whatever, should be there.  Try to back it up somewhere like on a network resource or a usb stick.

Once you've done that...re-install.

If you're super new to linux you may also consider a variant of Ubuntu called Linux Mint.  Extremely user friendly and takes all the guess work out of pretty much everything.  It's still ubuntu, just with pizazz.
