### Post by Impavidus on 2016-08-23
The first issue is a known bug for Lubuntu and Xubuntu with Intel graphics. You can hit ctrl+alt+F6 followed by ctrl+alt+F7 to get the mouse cursor back. I read that a fix is being tested, so you (and I) should get a fix automatically in not too much time.
