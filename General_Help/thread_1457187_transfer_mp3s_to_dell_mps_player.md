### Post by cogier on 2010-04-18
Your mp3 files are probably stored in the Music folder. Go to **Places>Music** and see if they are there.

If so plug your mp3 player in to the computer with the USB lead. Ubuntu should see it and open a folder window.

Copy your files from Music over to the mp3 player.

Hope that helps.
