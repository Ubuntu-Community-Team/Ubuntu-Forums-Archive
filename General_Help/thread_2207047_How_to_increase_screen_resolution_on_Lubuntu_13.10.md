### Post by Rob Sayer on 2014-02-26
I don't know exactly what you mean my "more useable".  I've used all 4 ubuntu desktop flavors and Mint with the Mate desktop.  LXDE doesn't have all the bells and whistles and eye candy of some other desktops like Unity (which sounds like what was installed, and I wouldn't put on that machine or my netbook).  It's also lacking a lot of the GUI config tools in other desktops.  

That's why it runs so well on old/slow hardware.  You didn't say how much RAM you have but if it's 1Gb lubuntu is an excellent choice.

If your screen is getting cramped use more virtual desktops.  i use 4 in LXDE on my netbook with a 1024X600p screen.  Linux is even better on a netbook than a laptop for just that reason.
