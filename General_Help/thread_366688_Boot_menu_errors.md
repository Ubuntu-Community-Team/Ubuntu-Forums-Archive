### Post by Maestro23 on 2007-02-21
Did you burn the CD at a slow speed (4x)?

In your BIOS, change your boot-order so that your CD-ROM drive boots first.

---

### Post by undertakingyou on 2007-02-21
Your USB keyboard should make no difference.  Mine is a bluetooth wireless with a usb receiver and it works fine. Maestro23 is right though, you should have the BIOS set to look at the CD-ROM as the first boot device.
