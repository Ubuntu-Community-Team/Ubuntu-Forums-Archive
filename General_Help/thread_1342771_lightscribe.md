### Post by timgood on 2009-12-01
The Lightscribe binaries are installed at /opt/lightscribeApplications/SimpleLabeler

Inside that folder is SimpleLabeler - you can launch it from there, but I would create a new launcher for it on the panel so you can launch it with one click. 

Alternatively, open a terminal and type /opt/lighstscribeApplications/SimpleLabeler/SimpleLabeler

Hope this helps.
