### Post by stinkeye on 2013-07-14
They're just 2 different cursor animations and I think it may depend on how the applications implement them.
Eg in chrome when loading a page I get  the pointer with wheel(left_ptr_watch), while in opera I just get the wheel(watch).
The pointer with wheel is supposed indicate something is loading but you can still interact with the UI.

If it's annoying, you could in your cursor theme link the **left_ptr_watch** image to the **watch**  image.
