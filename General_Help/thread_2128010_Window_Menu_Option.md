### Post by Mr. Shannon on 2013-03-21
I think what you are talking about is called **Shade**.  You can right click on the title bar and it is under this menu.  There are also other ways to do this.  In System Settings->Window Behavior->Window Behavior and then under either Titlebar Actions or Window Actions you can configure other ways to activate Shade.  You can also set a keyboard shortcut by going to System Settings->Shortcuts and Gestures->Global Keyboard Shortcuts, and then choosing KWin, searching for "shade", and finally settings a keyboard shortcut.
