### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-08-13
> **Enigmatus said:**
> Rather prompt replies!

T'is a bit of a shame that some of my hardware may be rather troublesome though!

Don't worry about it.  A lot of people have a bit of trouble getting their wi-fi to work at first, but it's usually an easy fix.

As jwaiwit said, the only hardware compatibility problems you might encounter are wi-fi, webcams, and (apparently, though I've never encountered it myself) card readers.  I'd like to add video cards to that list.  However, most such incompatibility problems are easily fixed.  I've seen very little hardware that is completely incompatible with Linux -- at least if you're willing to put a tiny bit of effort into getting it to work.

If it helps any, I just did a clean install of my system with a dual-boot of Vista and Ubuntu, and I had considerably more problems getting everything working in Vista than I did in Ubuntu.
