### Post by davidbilla on 2008-11-29
You have to choose the right driver for your motherboard. If your motherboard is Intel 8x series or 9x series, you can try installing 'xserver-xorg-video-intel' from synaptic. If you have nvidia or ati then you have to install the appropriate drivers after enabling all restricted and third party repositories. For this, a quick web search will provide you the solution.
