### Post by aysiu on 2008-05-01
> **crimsongrim said:**
> um another question how do i uninstall adobe flash player and install Swfdec player plugin?
Go to System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager and search for *flashplugin-nonfree* and right-click it and mark it for removal. Then search for *swfdec-mozilla* and right-click it and mark it for installation. Then click *Apply*
