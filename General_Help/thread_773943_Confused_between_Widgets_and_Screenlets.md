### Post by Ub1476 on 2008-04-29
You can download screenlets from [here]("http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=6700").

Screenlets can also be installed from the repos, but the package (Screenlets 0.1) found on that site is newer. 

When you right-click on a screenlets you can select it as a wdiget. When you use the widget layer, that screenlets will then be viewed.
