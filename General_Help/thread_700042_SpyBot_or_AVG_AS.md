He won't need anti-virus/anti-malware and they already answered with ClamAv...
The best thing he can do is use Firefox with NoScript, AdBlock and Firekeeper, but you might also want to have a talk with your friend about basic Internet security.
In my experience, an infection only occurs after the person was trying to access cracked software and other illegal warez...
Not saying that's the case at all, but just remember that if you're not hanging out on the shady side of the web and not running with Full Admin privileges in Windows, you can actually stay pretty safe.
