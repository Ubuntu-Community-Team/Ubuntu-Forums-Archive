### Post by CoolJohnB on 2010-07-25
Thanks for the link very interesting!  Also I have found a solution that's not mentioned in the other thread and that is to change the My Documents Path:  Go to Tools>Options click on Paths and change the My Documents directory.  As I have 2 HD's in my laptop and keep all my Data on the 2nd one I changed the directory to Documents on my 2nd HD and Load/Save is instant!  As it should be!
