### Post by GreatDanton on 2012-10-31
Full use is also possible on Ubuntu 12.04. I assume you forgot to select: **Stored in reserved extra space** in startup disk creator. This will allow you to store information on your usb (is that what you mean with full use?). So delete the disk image on usb and make a fresh live usb (and don't forget to select: Stored in reserved extra space).

Hope this helps.
