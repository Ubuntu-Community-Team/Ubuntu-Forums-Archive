### Post by x64Jimbo on 2007-02-28
The widget couldn't have messed up your bios. Those metrics are read-only. I recommend unplugging the keyboard, starting the computer, letting it POST, turning it off, then plugging it back in and starting up.

ok got it fixed. took the cmos battery out and let the pc sit for five minutes without the power cord plugged in. thanks.
