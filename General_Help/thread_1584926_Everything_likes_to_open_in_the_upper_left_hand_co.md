### Post by ownaginatious on 2010-09-29
For some reason, all of my windows keep opening in the upper left hand corner of the screen (I'm going to assume this is coordinate (0,0)).

Is there a way to have them open in the middle of the screen instead? It's getting kind of annoying...

If it matters, I'm using Xfce4 and compiz-fusion.

Thanks!

---

### Post by ownaginatious on 2010-09-29
Ah, I managed to fix it. Turns out the solution was going to the ccsm > enabling "place windows" and changing the setting from 'cascade windows' to 'center windows'.

Hopefully someone will find this useful in the future :p.
