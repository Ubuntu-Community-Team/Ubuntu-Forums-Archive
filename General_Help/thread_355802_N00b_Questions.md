### Post by aldenhg on 2007-02-07
Welcome to the world of OSS.
To answer your first question, no, you don't really have to worry about keeping all your drivers up to date. As long as you use Synaptic or Add/Remove or apt-get or any other package manager everything will be kept fresh for you. You can always force and update by running sudo apt-get update; sudo apt-get dist-upgrade if you're really worried about it.
To get your mouse working I would reccomend checking out [this guide]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=65471"). For future reference, a quick google search for hardware issues is usually a bit better at finding answers than the Forum's own search feature. My google search to get to that page was "logitech mouse ubuntu."
I can't help you with the printer, but I would reccomend looking through the ubuntu wiki if you want to know everything you would ever want to. It can be found at wiki.ubuntu.com.
Good luck!
