### Post by dandnsmith on 2009-10-29
What size does your BIOS detect it as?
If it were 8GB, then it would either be a BIOS settings problem, or a jumper on the drive (which seems to be ruled out by the detection by another machine).
Has it ever been used as 40GB with your machine?

I'd be wary about trying it out with Karmic - there are plenty of problems reported, and it would be better to use a full release version, rather than a beta.
