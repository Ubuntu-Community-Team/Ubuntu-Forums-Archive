### Post by matthewcraig on 2007-12-02
If this was caused by software, then the file modification date on those specific files would be modified.  What is that date?  Can you remember doing anything at that date and time?  You should also consider data corruption by your electronic media.  If the date was not modified, since your copy of the files, then your media may be the cause.  I know you hate to hear this after this emotional experience, but keep backups of important data, in the future.
