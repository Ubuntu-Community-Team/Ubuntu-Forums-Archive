### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-11-08
Hi there.
I don't know about 3 icons, but 3 in1 is possible. Just right-click on the panel and click **Add to Panel** and scroll down to [B]Main Menu
                                      The main GNOME menu[/B]
and click **Add**. Then right-click on the old Main menu and choose remove.
