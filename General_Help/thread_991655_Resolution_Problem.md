### Post by jyaan on 2008-11-24
I would say there is something incorrect in your /etc/X11/xorg.conf. This means that you have either the wrong video driver set, or your monitor is not defined correctly (or both). I would double check to make sure. You should also try and look up the HorizSync and VertRefresh values for you monitor (in your manual), since this is one of the most common things to be detected incorrectly. What kind of video card and monitor do you have?
