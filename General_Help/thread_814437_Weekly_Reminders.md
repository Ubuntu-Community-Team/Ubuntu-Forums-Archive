### Post by Prospero2006 on 2008-05-31
I don't know if this is exactly what you are looking for, but the cron scheduler runs jobs and programs at predetermined times.

For example: I'm a teacher. I have to stand duty at 3:30 on Wednesdays. I need to be there, but it's easy to forget. So at 3:31 on Wednesday I have cron play an audio file, 'Ping!' to jog my memory.

apt-get install kcron

for easy management.
