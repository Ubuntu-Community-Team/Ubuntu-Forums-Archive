### Post by marshmallow1304 on 2010-08-17
Some older machines don't support USB keyboards in the BIOS.  If you're using USB keyboards, you could try swapping in a PS/2 keyboard.

### Post by snowpine on 2010-08-17
You can certainly reinstall 9.04 if you choose (and the ps2 keyboard suggestion above is a good one). 

However, keep in mind that 9.04 reaches its "end of life" in October and will not be supported past that date. 

Have you taken 10.04 (the current release) for a test drive yet, to see if it will meet your needs? It is a long-term-support release with 3 years of support, perfect for a 20-machine install.
