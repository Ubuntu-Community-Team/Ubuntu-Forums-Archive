### Post by Fire_Chief on 2008-04-01
Depending on the type of timeline you want to create, you might be able to make one in Planner. It's more of a project management Gantt style chart instead of a timeline layout.

Planner should already be installed on your Ubuntu system under the Office section of the Applications menu (labelled as Project Management).
