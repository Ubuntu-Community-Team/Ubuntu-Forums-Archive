### Post by Cheesemill on 2009-05-19
There's 2 ways you could go about this:

The easiest way is to take a look at your BIOS, most modern machines have an option to wake the computer at a specified time.

If you have no luck with this and you have another machine on your network that is always on you could look into WOL (Wake On LAN), this involves sending a magic packet to your computer which triggers it to switch on.
