### Post by raktarok on 2009-11-26
In command, you must enter gnome-system monitor, and you can choose any name you like. Then you can click on the entry and press your desired key combination. Ctrl + alt + del handles log out by default, so you may have to disable that first.

---

### Post by ninjapirate89 on 2009-11-26
Go to system -> preferences -> keyboard shortcuts and change log-out to something different. Then click Add at the bottom. Give it a name (doesn't matter what) then put gnome-system-monitor in for command. Then scroll to the bottom under custom commands and change the key combination to what you want.
