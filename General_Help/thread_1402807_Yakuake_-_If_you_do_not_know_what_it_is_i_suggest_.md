### Post by Joe Ker1086 on 2010-02-09
Just wanted to share with everyone. Yakuake is a KDE based Drop-down terminal emulator that drops down from the top of the screen and retracts with the press of a button of your choosing. Highly configurable and runs flawlessly. I Highly recommend anyone that uses terminal frequently takes a look. Yakuake can also run in GNOME so don't be discouraged you non-KDE fans. 

```
sudo apt-get install yakuake
```

I like to share things that make my life much eaiser.:D
