### Post by josephku on 2011-05-01
I had this same issue.  Man it was a tough one.  Go into "Appearance" and select the Ambiance Theme.  If just using it does not work, click the "Customize..." button and pick the Ambiance selection under the "Controls" tab.  If that does not work, try going through other control selections.  Hope this helps.
