Welcome to the forums.

> I think the internal SD card reader (If such a thing exists) would have been deleted during the installation process
??  Only you will know if such a thing exists, and why should it be "deleted"?

I have found internal / built-in card readers to be a problem. Some of them have (and "Sony" = troubles) DRM built into the hardware, and so will not work with linux.

In my experience, an external (USB - connected) card reader works well if:

You plug the card into the thing.
*Then* you plug in the USB cable of the external thing to your PC.

Wait a moment, and the device is recognised, and options are offered.

Hope this helps
