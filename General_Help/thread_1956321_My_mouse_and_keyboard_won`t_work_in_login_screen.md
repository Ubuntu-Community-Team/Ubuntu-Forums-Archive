### Post by PhantomTurtle on 2012-04-10
Are you using a wireless keyboard/mouse, because I have had the same problem with my wireless keyboard in the Ubuntu 12.04 beta. You just have to unplug the dongle that comes with your wireless keyboard/mouse and plug it back in. If you have wired one then this is worth a try as well. It works after I unplug and plug back in. Just to clarify you unplug and plug back in at the login screen, do not restart or anything like that.
