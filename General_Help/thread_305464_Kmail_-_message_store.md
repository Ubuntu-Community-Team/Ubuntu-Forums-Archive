### Post by Arisna on 2006-11-23
Your mail should be in ~/.kde/share/apps/kmail/mail/ . The absolute path to this would be /home/**Your Username**/.kde/share/apps/kmail/mail , but your shell will understand the tilde to represent /home/Your Username .
