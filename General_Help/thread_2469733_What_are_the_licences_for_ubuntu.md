### Post by TheFu on 2021-12-08
The licenses are included with the software.  Each project may have a different license, so you'll need to review them all. Most will be GPL. Some will be BSD, MIT, and all of perl uses the Perl Artistic license.

Your legal team will need to decide.

Nobody here works for Canonical. We are users with experience.  IF you need an official answer, contact Canonical directly.
