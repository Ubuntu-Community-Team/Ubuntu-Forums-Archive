### Post by mykey on 2005-10-25
you don't need to install it - download the package here [http://download.mozilla.org/?product=thunderbird-1.5b2&os=linux&lang=en-US]("http://http://download.mozilla.org/?product=thunderbird-1.5b2&os=linux&lang=en-US")
unpack it to e.g. ~/bin/thunderbird - back up your ~/.thunderbird folder - go to ~/bin/thunderbird and fire up ./thunderbird - thats all
