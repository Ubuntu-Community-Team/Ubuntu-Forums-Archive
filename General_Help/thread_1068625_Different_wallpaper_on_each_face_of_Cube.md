### Post by ellalan on 2009-02-13
Hi
In ccsm,enable "Wallpaper" plugin and insert the pictures(see the attachment).
In synsptics you can enable "gconf-editor" then you can find cofiguration editor in Applications->system tools,
When you are in the configuration editor go to "apps"-> "nautilus"->preferences
In preferences uncheck "show desktop".(see attachment)
Thats it, I have covered everything and hope it works for you.
