### Post by JC Cheloven on 2009-08-21
It is often seen how people coming to free software expects to find more guarantees here than in proprietary software. It is both unlogical and unfair. 

As a matter of fact, in many aspects, more warranties are found in free software. But this doesn't entitle anyone to ask for absolute warranties. Something can go wrong, at any time, in any place.  Just as in proprietary software. But you will not find better help there...
