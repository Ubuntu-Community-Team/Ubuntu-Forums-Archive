### Post by sgosnell on 2010-06-02
I'm not sure what you're asking for.  What type view do you want?  On the Month view, it's a continuous stream by default.  You just use the scroll bar on the right to scroll up and down continuously.  You can click on the left and right arrows at the top to get a month at a time if you want, or if you want to scroll through your entire calendar, use the scrollbar.  That's what scrollbars are for.  I'm not sure you can do this in any other views, though.  AFAIK the week views are set for a week, and I've never tried changing that.
