### Post by jimbob on 2007-09-19
You have several diferent aspect ratios in your mode lines.  What is the native resolution of your monitor?

Save a copy of your existing xorg.conf file and then remove all but the 1440x900 resolution and see what happens. Actually you only need the depth 24 section so your screen section can look like this:

Section "Screen"
       Identifier      "Default Screen"
       Device          "Intel Corporation 82946GZ/GL Integrated Graphics Controller"
       Monitor         "VA503 SERIES"
       DefaultDepth    24
       SubSection "Display"
               Depth         24
               Modes        "1440x900" 
       EndSubSection
EndSection

Reboot and give it a try.  I would suggest Envy but it is only for Nvidia and ATI as I understand.
