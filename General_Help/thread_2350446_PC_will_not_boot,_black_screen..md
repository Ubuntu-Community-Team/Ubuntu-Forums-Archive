### Post by Bashing-om on 2017-01-24
dtree46; NO !

Real bad things might result if power pulled while the system is attempting to boot !

If you can not - after reading the manual - boot to the bios settings menu .. then that suggest hardware issues .
Now you break out a multi-meter and learn where the test points are for the power supply and what the levels should be .. and go from there to the mainboard .
Everything starts in bios .. no bios == no operating system.
