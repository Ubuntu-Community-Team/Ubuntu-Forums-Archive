### Post by cariboo on 2009-04-30
You can resize a windows partition by using the manual option. Before you resize an ntfs partition, make sure you defrag it at least twice. Once you are ready to resize the partition, grab the right side of the bar displayed at the top of the application and drag it towards the left to resize it.
