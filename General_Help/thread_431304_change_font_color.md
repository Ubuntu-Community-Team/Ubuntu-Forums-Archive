### Post by m.musashi on 2007-05-02
Which version are you using? Feisty now has a color option in the theme preferences. Go to system -> preferences -> theme and choose customize. Not all themes support that. You can also go to gnome-look.org and download themes.

One of the easiest solutions (other than the Feisty theme options) is to use [gnome color chooser](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/gnome-color-chooser?content=47349) but I don't think it will change panels. You can right click the panel and choose preferences and play around a bit but some themes use graphics for the background and it ends up not looking good. In that case you have to mess around with the themes themselves and edit your gtkrc but I don't really know much about doing that.
