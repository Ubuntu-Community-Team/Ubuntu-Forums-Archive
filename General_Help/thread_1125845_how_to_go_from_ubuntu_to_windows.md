### Post by silentrebel on 2009-04-15
Open your *.odp* with OpenOffice and navigate to File > Save As....  Now under a drop-down menu listing file-types, choose something along the lines of *Microsoft Powerpoint (2000/XP) (.ppt)*.  Go to the directory where you just saved the file and you should have a *.ppt* version of your presentation.
Try opening it in Powerpoint before presenting it in case the formatting is off (it sometimes happens).
