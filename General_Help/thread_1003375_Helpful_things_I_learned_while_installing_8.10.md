### Post by clay7 on 2008-12-06
...hopefully someone will benefit from this!

1. I had to burn the .iso I downloaded at 4x speed. The CD I had burned at 32x caused lots of hangs and ultimately wouldn't install.

2. The second thing is that I had read somewhere that I needed to create a swap partition. After several attempts, I discovered that 8.10 creates one automatically for you. No need to manually make one (unless you want to).
