### Post by ju2wheels on 2008-12-28
I forgot to mention it earlier but in general there are 3 specific pieces, not just the graphics card, but that was the first that stood out to me.

1. Motherboard  (I have an Asus board that refuses to work with Ubuntu, so theres no harm in checking board compatibility as well).
2. Sound Card (Determine of the chip type it uses is compatible, it would suck to have no audio)
3. Graphics Card (I second ITAndrew's suggestion for a go at nvidia as they are providing better support with linux drivers and ease of compatibility at the moment)
