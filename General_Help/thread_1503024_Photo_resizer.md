### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-06-06
OK, I think the easiest way is probably with gthumb, available in the repos, and a good way to look at your photos as well.  In some ways it's a bit like Irfanview from windows, which was always my photo viewer of choice.  In gthumb, select the photos you want to resize and go to Tools -Scale images, and there you can set the size, type, and naming of new images along with the choice of overwrite and remove original, etc etc.
