### Post by One Quick Question on 2005-12-24
Well, here's this page, but only a few of the "secret" configurations are secret anymore:  [http://wiki.kdenews.org/tiki-index.php?page=Secret%20Config%20Settings](http://wiki.kdenews.org/tiki-index.php?page=Secret%20Config%20Settings)
Oh crap, I guess I should read your entire post first.  Well then...

More specifically;
Konqueror isn't as customizable a browser in feeling as Firefox.  So there's nothing fun like Greasemonkey or anything.  As far as I know.   The tabbing isn't as sophisticated, anyway.  You could run through *~/.kde/share/apps/konqueror* files though, I'm sure you'd find interesting ways to change stuff there.  I've changed the rc file to get at intricate toolbar modifications before.  Nothing too special though.

With KGet, you get a button that downloads all links.  Oh wait, you just said that.  Sorry, that's all I've got for that.  Feel like writing your own?

Well then, it looks like you've done your work.   Konqueror the browser isn't quite the glove that Firefox is.  Not yet anyway; hopefully one day it could support FF's extensions.  That would rock.
