### Post by ireneshusband on 2008-08-10
How do I stop the "Bullets and Numbering" toolbar from popping up every time I try to edit a bulleted line in OpenOffice? I'm trying to modify my CVs and this feature is very distracting.

---

### Post by Pom2122 on 2008-08-10
Just go to View, Toolbars, and uncheck the context-sensitive toolbars whenever they appear — Tables, Bullets and Numbering, Frame, Picture, etc.
