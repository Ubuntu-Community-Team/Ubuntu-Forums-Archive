### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-03-26
Certain types of software are more known for utilizing multithreading than others.  Especially things like video editing, 3D modeling and graphics, and also compression.

But multiple cores are also good for multitasking more than one program at a time, and in that case, it's helpful even if the individual apps aren't optimized for multithreading.
