### Post by tekkenlord on 2007-08-22
Well, the linux kernel is the most important thing in your system. It makes your system boots, contains many drivers, displays your desktop etc etc.... So, in general the more updated the kernel is the better. Every newer kernel version may contain some patches to the previous version or include some new features. So, you must select 2.6.17-12.
