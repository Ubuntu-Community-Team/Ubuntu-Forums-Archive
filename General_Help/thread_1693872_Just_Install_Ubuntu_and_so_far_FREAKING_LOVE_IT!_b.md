### Post by Cpierce on 2011-02-23
I think what you may be wanting is prism. This will allow you to turn any webpage into an "app" with it's own icon that can be put on the dock. Go to software center and type in "Facebook" in the search and look for facebook prism. There is also one for Gmail. 

Once prism is installed, just open Firefox, go to the webpage (youtube, etc.....), then click on "tools" and then "convert website into application". It will make the shortcut automatically instead of manually creating a shortcut. 

If you don't like this, just go back to software center and remove it.
