### Post by krivine on 2009-09-29
I got it to work on Jaunty by:

[LIST=1]
[*]Setting port forwarding for these ports: 5060,5004,10000,8000-8019,3478-3497 
[*]Setting the outound proxy to sipgate.co.uk (Edit->Preferences->Protocols->SIP settings)
[/LIST]

I've more or less given up on it in favour of Twinkle though. I don't need video, it's easier to configure, and the call quality is better.
