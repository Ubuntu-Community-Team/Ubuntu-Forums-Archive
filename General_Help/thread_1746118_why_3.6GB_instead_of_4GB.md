### Post by Finalfantasykid on 2011-05-01
I had this happen on my previous laptop.  Thing is my motherboard was physically unable to access more than 4 GB of memory, and once you factor in the other little components on the motheboard, mainly the video card(but also may other things I would imagine), then I only got about 3.2GB.

Do you have a dedicated graphics card, like nvidia or ati/amd?

EDIT: You might want to check the bios to see what it reports for how much memory your computer has.

### Post by idoitprone on 2011-05-01
try to post your internal hardware too next time. It save some googling time for the people trying to answer your question. Sometimes there may be minor differences between models of computers

[https://bbs.archlinux.org/viewtopic.php?id=51692](https://bbs.archlinux.org/viewtopic.php?id=51692)

Your computer has integrated graphics which means it reserve some space for it. That is why you are not seeing the full 4 gb.
