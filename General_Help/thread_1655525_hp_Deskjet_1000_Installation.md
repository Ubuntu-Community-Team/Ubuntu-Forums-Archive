### Post by Biosd on 2010-12-29
Ok, I actually solved this on my own, and I wanted to share. In order to get a HP DeskJet 1000 working on Lucid 10.04 LTS:

1. Go to "System > Administration > Printing"

2. Go to "Server > New"

3. The printer should show up, highlight it,  and click "Forward"

4. Let it go through the motions, then highlight HP and click "Forward"

5. Highlight HP 1120HPIJS 3.10.2

6. Finish up and test print.
