i don't think you understood my answer. remove the hard drive from your computer. and install it in another computer along with the hard drive already in that computer. as a second or slave har drive. hard drive have jumper switches on them. if your computer has only one hard drive it will be set as master or cable select. if the computer has more than one hard drive they are set as master and slave or cable select (cable select means master and slave are determined by how the ide cable  (flat ribbon cable) is installed.
