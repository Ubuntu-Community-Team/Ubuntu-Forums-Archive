### Post by stalkingwolf on 2009-01-23
Try changing your cable to a different USB port.  This has helped me
at times.  Also make sure it is securely plugged in.  I occasionally
have problems if the port is loose or worn.

Failing that check your printer is set as the default.  The only other
time I have had problems with an HP printer was if they were dead or dying.
