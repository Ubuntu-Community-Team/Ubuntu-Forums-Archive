### Post by JBAlaska on 2010-12-26
Open a terminal and do: ```
sudo apt-get install gnome-color-chooser
```
Then open it at **System, Preferences, Gnome Color Chooser**, click on the **specific** tab, then check and change your **tooltips colors**, hit apply (It takes several seconds to activate and your done.
