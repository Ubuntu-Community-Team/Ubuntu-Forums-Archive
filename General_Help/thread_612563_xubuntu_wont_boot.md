### Post by ray bot on 2007-11-14
You might be able to get a more detailed error message by removing the "quiet" and "splash" boot flags.  If you're booting from the livecd, you can do this by pressing F6 at the boot menu, and deleting the words "quiet" and "splash" from the line of text that appears near the bottom of the screen.
