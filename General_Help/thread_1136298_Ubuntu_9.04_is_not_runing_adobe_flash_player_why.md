### Post by bryncoles on 2009-08-04
this (or something similar) happened to me. during the upgrade, firefox switches to using a different flash plugin, for some reason. 

to fix it the way i did: go to a page which requires flash. a littke lego-brick icon appears in the bottom-right of the screen. clicking this will give you a 'select plug-in to use' gui. use this to select the adobe flash plug in and you should be back in business. 

unless you've a different problem to the one i was beset with!
