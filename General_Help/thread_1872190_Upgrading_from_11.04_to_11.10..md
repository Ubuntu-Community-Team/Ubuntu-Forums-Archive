### Post by zvacet on 2011-10-30
In synaptic>updates at the bottom you can choose if you want upgrade from LTS>LTS or normal (maybe it is any,I´m not sure because I don´t use English version).Select normal and refresh synaptic.After that in update manager you should be able to see message about new version is available and you can start upgrade.
