### Post by Slaughterer on 2007-02-16
There doesn't seem to be a solution other than changing your output to X11.
It's explained [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1871612#post1871612").
The quality of the video is quite degraded with X11, especialy in full screen mode. Another thing you could try is jiggling the window till you get video and then leave it. You should be able to see the video as long as something doesn't interfere like fading windows etc.
