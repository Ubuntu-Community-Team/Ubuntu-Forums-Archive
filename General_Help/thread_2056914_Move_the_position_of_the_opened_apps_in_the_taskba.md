### Post by SantaFe on 2012-09-13
In Xfce's Taskbar, Right Click on the taskbar near one of the tabs.  A pop up menu with Window Buttons at the top will pop up.  Click on properties and in Sorting Order, choose None, allow Drag-and-drop.  You should now be able if you have more than one application running be able to drag the tab you want along the taskbar & reorder them in Xfce now.:D
