### Post by az on 2006-01-26
You want to upgrade now?  Dapper is unstable and no one can guarantee that you will not crash your system at some point.

However, when it releases, you will be able to upgrade to dapper as easily as a few clicks, or by popping in the cd once your system has booted into breezy.

If you want to dabble in Dapper now, you can install it onto a seperate partition.  If you have at least three (five?) gigs of free disk space, go right ahead.  Just boot the Dapper install and it will detect and shrink your current partition just like it would do if you wanted to dual-boot with windows.
