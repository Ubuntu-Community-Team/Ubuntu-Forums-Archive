### Post by jarviser on 2010-05-20

> Okay, I just got the live CD of Ubuntu and I was wondering if I could use/configure internet without installing Ubuntu?
 
Thanks.
 You can do almost everything in the live CD that you can do with a full installation, but it is rather slow. Should be able to connect ethernet, wifi or mobile broadband as in the full install with minimal configuration, in fact ethernet router connection needs no config at all, and wireless will only need SSID and encryption key for wifi or APN for mobile (depending on country).  

Just try it, no harm done.  Any "configuration" you may do is lost as soon as you shut down of course.
