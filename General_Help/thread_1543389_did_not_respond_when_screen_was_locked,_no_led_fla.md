system did not respond when i have come and screen was locked. black screen, no hdd led flash, i pressed restart button, after restart it works, i searched errors and found: seems it was gzipping logs. i have lost several log files.
so, in such case, at morning of sunday, or other day as configured, when system renames and gzips log files, if locked screen do not respond to key presses and mouse movings, and no hdd led flash and ctrl+alt+f1 do not work, maybe you should wait a while.
