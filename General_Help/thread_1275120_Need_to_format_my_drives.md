### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-09-25
The drives need to be unmounted before you can work on them.  You can try to right click on the drive and unmount it.  The best way to work on drives is to cownload Gparted from their web site and use that disk to do your work, that way the drives will all be unmounted since you started the computer with the Gparted disk and it does not mount any drives.
