### Post by kokkus on 2008-09-21
There are 100s of programs and websited to do this. I would use [www.keepvid.com](www.keepvid.com). Insert the video site url, and click download.
This works for veoh.com, google video, myspace video and more as well.
THe video will be downloaded in flv or mp4 format. You can use oggvovert
 to covert the format to i.e avi-
Have fun:)
Oggconvert can be downloaded from the add/remove tool in the applications menu.
