Did you d-l an ISO file and did you burn it to the disk as an ISO file?  You cannot just copy the ISO file to a disk: it has to be burned to the disk as an ISO image.  If you did both of thos things, then you probably got a bad d-l.  Try it again and always burn the image to your disk at less than your maximum speed to reduce chances of errors.
