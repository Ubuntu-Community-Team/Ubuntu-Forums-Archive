### Post by mooreted on 2014-09-04
In Chrome you can use the key combination CTRL+SHIFT+N or you can click the settings icon in the top right of your browser and click "New Incognito Window". You can also create a shortcut and add the "-incognito" switch to launcher. The command looks like this:

google-chrome -incognito
