### Post by tommcd on 2009-05-05
You can put themes in ~/.themes and they will just be available for your user name. I think you can put custom icons in ~/.icons.
You can put themes in /usr/share/themes and they will be available for all users on the system. Icons can go in /usr/share/icons.
