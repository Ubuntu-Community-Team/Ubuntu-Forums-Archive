### Post by Backharlow on 2010-11-20
If you want super simple, and if she isn't on Ubuntu as well, I recommend Dropbox. This is a commercial, free data mirroring service. It works very well in Ubuntu/win/mac. You make an account at dropbox.com, install the app on Ubuntu, then add your files to a dropbox folder, which then becomes available to anyone from a simple web URL, or you can add her computer to your dropbox account and that folder will be mirrored on her computer. It's really easy.

If you are both on Ubuntu, you can do the same thing with UbuntuOne - also free.

both are free within a specified amount of data - a couple gigs. Once you have mirroring set up with one of these, it goes the other direction to, your daughter can drop things in her dropbox/ubuntuone folder, and it will show up in yours.
