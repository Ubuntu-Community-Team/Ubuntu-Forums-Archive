### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-04-30
You mean "minimize to tray"?

I'm pretty sure that sort of behaviour is something that Ubuntu wants to get rid of; I personally find it quite annoying.

Those programs probably use notification area icons. Ubuntu 11.04 only uses indicators by default, but the old-style notification area icons can be brought back on a one-by-one basis. If you look at Google, you'll find a script to "whitelist unity" notification area icons.

The whitelist probably won't last forever, so you should tell the developers of those programs to switch over to using indicators soon.
