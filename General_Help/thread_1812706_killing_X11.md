### Post by coffeecat on 2011-07-26
Yes. :) Alt+PrtScr+k will kill the xserver and all running GUI processes and take you back to the login screen. On some keyboards/systems you need to use the AltGr key instead of Alt.

But why the sysadmin asked you to look away is beyond me. Anyone using Linux needs to know about the magic key combinations:

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic_key](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magic_key)
