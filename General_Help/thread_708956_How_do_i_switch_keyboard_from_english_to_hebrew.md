### Post by mikewhatever on 2008-02-27
You can choose key combinations in System>Preferences>Keyboard, Layout Options tab. I think the default combination is Alt+Alt.
There is also an indicator to add to one of the panels. Right click where you want it on the panel, then Add to Panel, and scroll to the last section called Utilities. Select Keyboard Indicator then click Add. Once added, every time you click on it will toggle the keyboards. Finally, it does not matter which languages you want to toggle, both ways should still work.
