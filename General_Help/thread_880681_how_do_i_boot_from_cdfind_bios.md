### Post by wpshooter on 2008-08-05
In all likelihood, it is either the DEL key, or F1 or F2.

Make sure that when you press the power button to turn the computer on that you just start constantly tapping (don't just press it once or twice and quit, because if you do you can miss the timing window in which the key has the opporutunity to get you into the BIOS) one of those keys until you either get into the BIOS or it boots to the O/S.  If the DEL key does not work just do a restart once you get into the O/S and then try the next key on your list.
