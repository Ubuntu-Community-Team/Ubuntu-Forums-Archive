### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-03-30
You may be able to do it somewhere in the *System > Preferences > Preferred Applications* but I am not sure if you can remove the usual email clients and substitute firefox and your gmail/hotmail/yahoo website instead.  Give it a try using *Custom* in the dropdown box and then command ```
firefox web.address
```

### Post by sirjoebob on 2009-03-30
depends on what your email client is. if you use gmail for example, go to edit>preferences, applications, mailto: and change to gmail. that works fine for me using gmail.
