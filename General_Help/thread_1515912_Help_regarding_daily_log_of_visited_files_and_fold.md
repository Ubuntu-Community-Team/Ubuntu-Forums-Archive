### Post by Deadite81 on 2010-06-23
I don't know your specific motivations (security or just wanting to know), but a cool way to interact with your previously used files is the Gnome Activity Journal.  It presents all your previously used files by date in various ways and is handy to see what's been going on under that particular user name.  If you're looking for security reports and such, this wouldn't be what you're looking for I don't think, but thought I'd mention it anyway.

[http://www.webupd8.org/2010/06/gnome-activity-journal-034-released.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2010/06/gnome-activity-journal-034-released.html)
[https://launchpad.net/~zeitgeist/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~zeitgeist/+archive/ppa)
