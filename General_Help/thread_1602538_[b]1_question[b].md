### Post by 3Miro on 2010-10-21
I had good performance on STALKER with GeForce 8600GT 512MB. Counter Strike is an older game I believe, so anything around that or above should fly well. RAM is not the only thing, you have to look at other parameters PCIEx16 1.0 vs 2.0, number of stream processors, clock speed and so on. For good wine gaming any of the GTX series should be fine (depending on the budget, you can look at GF9800 or GF8800).

(for wine, Nvidia is currently the best choice)
