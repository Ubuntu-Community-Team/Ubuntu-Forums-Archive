### Post by XCan on 2009-10-01
Do you mean CPU usage or disk usage? I can't think of any good reason for the screensaver to use the disk, however, there are tons of reason why the screensaver is using the CPU. There are some pretty rediculess screensavers bundled with Ubuntu that will easily max out one thread when run. I think you should set the screensaver to 'Blank Screen' .
