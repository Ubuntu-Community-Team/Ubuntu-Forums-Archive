### Post by Sorivenul on 2009-01-21
Turbo Pascal and Free Pascal are not the same, though they are very similar. If you are looking for a graphical program to use with the Free Pascal Compiler, you are looking for Lazarus. Otherwise, the FPC is a console program, like GCC or TCC.  Lazarus should be in thre repositories.

Good luck!
