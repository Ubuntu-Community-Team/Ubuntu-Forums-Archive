### Post by Armann on 2013-02-24
So that's your startup page ?

If so then you can go to Edit, then Preferences at the bottom of the menu.
Write what page you want in "Home Page:" or just click Restore to default.
Close Firefox and open it again.

If you cant find the Menu bar you might need to press Alt on your keyboard.
