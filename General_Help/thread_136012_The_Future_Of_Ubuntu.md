### Post by Madpilot on 2006-02-25
The next version, 6.04 (Dapper Drake) will be out in mid-April.

You can, as narnian99 mentioned, try the test releases of it right now.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DapperFlight4](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DapperFlight4) has information, screenshots & links for the test version.

If you're new to Linux, stick with the current version until Dapper is fully released - it is in testing and could break things!
