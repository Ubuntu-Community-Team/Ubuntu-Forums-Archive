### Post by perce on 2007-07-13
I have a partial answer: to have Nautilus started instead of Rithmbox, you can go to
system > preferences > removable drives and media > multimedia
and uncheck the box below "portable music player". Unfortunately this will apply to your genuine ogg player too. I'd like to understand why gnome is confusing my flash drive for a music player, and how to cleanly fix it.
