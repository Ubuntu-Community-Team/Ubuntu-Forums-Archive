### Post by b0ng0 on 2007-10-20
Try mounting them manually, either through the terminal or the disk mounting applet.

Right click on the toolbar, click "Add to Panel" then scroll down to System & Hardware and add "Disk Mounter".

This will display all your partitions and allow you to mount them. Hope that helps.
