### Post by doas777 on 2011-03-14

### Post by Spyderkid on 2011-03-14
To see hidden folders press <Ctrl + H> and to hide folders put a . at the beginning of the name

---

### Post by Spyderkid on 2011-03-14
and to see hidden folders in terminal type 
```

ls -l -all

```

for non-hidden folders just type 'ls -l' (without the ' ' )
