### Post by 23meg on 2006-11-07
Launch the settings app (beryl-settings) and disable the *Scale* plugin if you never intend to use that feature, or go to its *Screen Edges and Corners * tab and uncheck the corners and set up another keyboard or mouse shortcut for it in the other tabs.
