### Post by Ampul on 2015-01-14
Hi, 

Got myself the HP Envy 4502 today. Did install HP Device Manager. I am able to scan... But you cannot choose 'scan' by the menu of the Printer itself. You need to open the Device Manager. Wait some seconds to get the wireless connection to your 4502. Then you have the menu 'Actions' where you can choose 'scan'. First I have an error message about permissions... But after closing that, XSane opens and I am able to use it to make scans.
