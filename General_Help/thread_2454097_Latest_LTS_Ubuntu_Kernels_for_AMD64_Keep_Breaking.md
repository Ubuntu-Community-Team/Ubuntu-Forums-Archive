### Post by Impavidus on 2020-11-23
Because your system is remarkably sensitive to some recent changes in the kernel.

New kernels are tested before they are released and are only released when they appear to work. But they aren't tested on every computer imaginable. Without knowing what goes wrong and some details on your hardware this is impossible to answer.
