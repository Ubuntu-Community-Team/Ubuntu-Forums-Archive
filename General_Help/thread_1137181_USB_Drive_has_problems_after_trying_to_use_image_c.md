### Post by fwaokda on 2009-04-25
I was able to get the drive to format by going here:
[http://www.sandisk.com/driverdownload/download.asp?driverId=163](http://www.sandisk.com/driverdownload/download.asp?driverId=163)

And reinstalling the U3 software... you can then go here:
[http://www.sandisk.com/DriverDownload/download.asp](http://www.sandisk.com/DriverDownload/download.asp)

Of course this will only work if you have a Sandisk Cruzer Micro, but perhaps your company's site has similar software.
