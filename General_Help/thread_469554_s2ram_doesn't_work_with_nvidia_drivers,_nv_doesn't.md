### Post by francisco_athens on 2007-06-11
I tried the  uswsusp package recently on my HP/Compaq TC1100 as well.  I tried the grub command various combinations with "noapic" "acpi_sleep=s3_bios" and "acpi_sleep=s3_mode".  I also blacklisted the "intel_agp" driver and enable nvagp in the xorg.conf.  All to no avail as yet.

### Post by ishtob on 2007-06-11
i have a tc1100 also, i also had the same problem with resuming from suspend. the screen turns on but stays blank.

and this is the tutorial i followed to get suspend to work properly:
[Nvidia Laptop Binary Driver Suspend]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/NvidiaLaptopBinaryDriverSuspend")

and now suspend works properly (i am using "nvidia" under the driver section of xorg.conf) suspend also works when the screen is rotated!

:) good luck!
