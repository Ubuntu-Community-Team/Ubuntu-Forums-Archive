### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-12-20
> **yatpay said:**
> Desktop Wall is what I use now, but unless I'm missing something, there is no way to view the entire wall at once and then select a quadrant (or whatever) to zoom back in on.

Expo is what you want, it's a Compiz plugin. When you enable it, the default keystroke is Super-E. I personally remove the keystroke and change it to the bottom-left corner.
