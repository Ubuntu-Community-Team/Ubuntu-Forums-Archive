### Post by h4ck3r on 2006-01-06
Simple, click on system (on the panel), then click on Adminastration, then click on login screen setup. You will be prompted for your password, type it in. Next click on themed greeter, then click on install new theme which is on the bottom right hand corner of the window next select the file you have downloaded. Click ok and the select it.
