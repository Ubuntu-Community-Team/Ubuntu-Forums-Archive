### Post by frodon on 2006-04-03

---

### Post by xenmax on 2006-04-03
In gnome-terminal, go to Edit -> Current Profile. In the "Colors' tab, there is selection option for built-in-schemes. You can use "white on black" scheme like you wanted. If you want other colors, you can also specify Text Color and Background Color individually.
