### Post by cscj01 on 2011-11-16
I am running LO 3.4.3 on Ubuntu 11.10 (Oneiric). When LO was first released as stable, I moved from OO to LO. All my Base Forms displayed correctly. After my upgrade to Oneiric, my Base forms no longer display correctly. First, the forms do not fill the entire window as they have done before. Secondly, because of the first problem, some of the data contained in the forms and/or sub-forms extend beyond the overall form dimensions. I have tried all the view options, but none of them correct this issue. I am including a screen shot of this problem.

---

### Post by cscj01 on 2011-11-16
I just received a reply to this question at OpenOffice.org.  It is a known bug.  The work-a-round is to open the form in Edit mode and choose View>Web Layout from the Menu.  Save the form and exit, and (at least in my case) all forms will open properly.  So, I'll mark this thread as solved.
