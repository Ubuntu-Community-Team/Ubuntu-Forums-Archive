So I got rid of my XFCE bar in my ultra-light Ubuntu I've been customising on a partition, and now I can't get it back? I typed in xfce4-panel and it came back for a little while and I was able to edit it, but as soon as I closed terminal it disappeared.

Help?

---

### Post by taurus on 2009-01-18
```
xfce4-panel &
```
Then, go into the Applications -> Settings and save the current session.  Log out and back in again and see if you get the panel back.
