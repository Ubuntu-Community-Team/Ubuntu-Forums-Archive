### Post by lunarcloud on 2008-10-28
I run the unstable editions, it is obnoxious when a gagillion programs give you reports when you restart your computer for no apparent reason other than they were closed when you shut the system off. 

Apport is very sensetive at times. If a normal user has a problem, they will go to the forums or report a bug report on their own. 

Most users never install the debug packages which give the useful information in a bug report anyways, so there'd be a LOT of junk and duplicate bug reports.

Too many junk reports and they never get triaged (marked for importance and looked over) and they never get fixed, it becomes a mess.
