### Post by Hexydes on 2007-06-03
Ok, so this is a pretty big oversight on the Banshee team's part, but I don't think you can directly put podcasts onto your iPod, at least not without completely syncing the device (which works for some, but probably not for anyone in this thread, I know I don't do it this way).

What I ended up having to do was:

1. Download the podcast via Banshee.

2. Find out the physical location on my computer where the file was stored ("/Home/Music/Podcasts/" for me)

3. Drag the podcast into Banshee, into the "Library" section.

4. Drag the podcast from the "Library" section to the "iPod" section.

The podcast should now be on your iPod. I haven't verified that this worked yet, because the iPod is still plugged in. Pretty crappy solution; hopefully the Banshee dev. team will get on this, because other than this glitch, it is a fantastic program, much better than Amarok, GTK-Pod, etc.
