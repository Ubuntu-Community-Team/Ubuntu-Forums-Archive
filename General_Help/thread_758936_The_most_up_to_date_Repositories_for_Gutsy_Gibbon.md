### Post by odin1965 on 2008-04-18
To keep up to date, you don't need anything in Third Party Software. For access to all software, make sure everything is checked on the Ubuntu Software tab. On Updates tab, you don't really need backports or proposed, but checking these will give you earlier access to some drivers and such. Just make sure security and updates are checked.

### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-04-18
There are hundreds of 3rd party repositories out there, but the Backports and Proposed updates are what you are looking for.  With these enabled, you will have newer versions of a lot of things.  Just keep in mind that the closer you live to the edge, the riskier it is.
