### Post by lacerda_sch on 2007-06-17
If you have Beryl, chances are it's no longer Metacity that handles your windows. Open your Beryl Settings Manager. You can edit commands (Commands tab) in the General Options. Edit Command 0. Then you can assign a shortcut to it using the Shortcuts button.

Hope this helps. :KS
