### Post by EmanresuusernamE on 2008-08-14
> **loganbell said:**
> in my bios settings there is a CD ROM group, Floppy group, Hard drive group and network boot group.
Just reread your post, some motherboards require that you put in the jump drive first.  Other motherboards consider them removable drives.  And some motherboards consider them hard drives flat out.

Insert your drive into your computer, then look at the BIOS again, if there's not another group there, check your Floppy and Hard drive groups.
