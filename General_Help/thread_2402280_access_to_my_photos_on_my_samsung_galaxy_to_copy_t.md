### Post by linux4me on 2018-09-28
With Ubuntu 18.04, all I have to do with my Galaxy S7 is plug it in using the USB cable. I get a pop-up on the phone asking if I want to allow the connection. I just tap "Allow" and in the file browser in Ubuntu I get the Galaxy as a connected device. Click on the device in the left column of the file browser, and I have the SD card listed as a folder. The only time it didn't work for me was when I was using a non-data (charging) cable, as @him610 mentioned.
