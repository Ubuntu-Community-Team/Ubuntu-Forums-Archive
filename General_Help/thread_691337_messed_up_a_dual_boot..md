### Post by jan quark on 2008-02-08
Unfortunately I must tell you that your data is very likely lost 

linux uses the ext3 partition file systems, it overwrites the fat32 or ntfs system of windows
and it is not possible to recover your data

it is too late now but in future always do a back-up before thinking of partitioning
