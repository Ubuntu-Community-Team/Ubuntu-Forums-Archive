### Post by QLee on 2011-05-13
[QUOTE=jt_04]that sounds like a really good idea. how would I go about doing it?
...[/QUOTE]

Two possible solutions occur to me initially and there are probably more possibilities.

1. Move the applets from the "bottom" panel to the "top" panel and then delete the old bottom panel.

2. Delete the "bottom" panel and then add to the "top" panel the applets you want to use that were previously on the "bottom" panel.

Either of those could be thought of as a merge.
