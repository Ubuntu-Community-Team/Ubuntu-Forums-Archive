### Post by mand0 on 2007-01-25
Have you tried running this in terminal?

```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```

It will take you through a series of questions about how you want your computer to be configured.  One of the questions will be your resolution.



Type in terminal:

```
glxinfo
```

Look at the very first line it spits out... does it say that direct rendering is off or on?
