WINE can run [Pagemaker]("http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?appId=329") and [GTA:SA]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=2599") (assuming you have a decent video card).
There are hundreds of Linux programs that can view and edit all types of audio and video media.
And a LAMP (Linux/Apache/Mysql/PHP) stack and easily be installed from the package manager.

Please post any further inquries into WINE in our WINE forum, and read the stickied posts and follow their instructions if you want assistance there: [http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=313](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=313)
