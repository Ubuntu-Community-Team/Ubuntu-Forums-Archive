When I delete files from my usb flash drive on my Karmic laptop (press del key), the properties of the drive remain unchanged (available space/used space).  It looks like the files/folders have been deleted, but when I go to my windows machines, the files and folders are now in a folder marked "Trash".

### Post by Darkness Des on 2010-07-08
You need to empty your own trash can before unplugging the flash drive. Alternatively, you could use the shortcut to permanently delete something. By default, this is Shift+Delete.
