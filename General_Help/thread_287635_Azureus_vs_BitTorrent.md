### Post by paul6 on 2006-10-29
For old machines, I highly recommend running uTorrent (windows client) under WINE. I tried Azureus, Rufus, Bittorrent, kTorrent, rTorrent, and all the native Linux clients I can think of. None of them match the features or small resource consumption of uTorrent.
