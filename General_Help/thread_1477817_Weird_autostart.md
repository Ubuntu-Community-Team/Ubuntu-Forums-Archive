### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-05-09
Yes, 
go to "System Settings", then click on the "Advanced" tab, then click on "Session Manager".

Near the bottom there is a "On Login" section. Tick the box that says: "Start with an Empty Session", then click apply.

Hope this is what you were looking for.
