### Post by meborc on 2010-05-18
when you click on that right upper logout button, then you see a screen with restart, shutdown etc... you also should see a little tick called "Save session"

so if this tick is "ticked" then all the windows you had open will open again the next time you log in

try closing all windows, then log out with the tick "ticked" and log in to check if you got rid of the software center
