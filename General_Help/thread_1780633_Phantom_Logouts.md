### Post by coffeecat on 2011-06-12
This doesn't sound likely as a cause for your problem, but I'll post it anyway. The key combination Alt-Print-k will kill the xserver and any processes running in it - very quickly. After a few moments of a black screen you are taken back to the login screen. With some keyboards/systems, you have to use the AltGr key instead of Alt.

It's quite a finger twister to do either of these, so difficult to do accidentally, but just a thought.

The other thought is: have you re-enabled the ctrl-alt-backspace combination to kill the xserver? If you happened to be pressing the ctrl and alt keys and accidentally hit the backspace rather than enter, that would be a cause. That's why that key combo was disabled by default a couple of gnome versions ago. Apparently, it was being accidentally hit too often
