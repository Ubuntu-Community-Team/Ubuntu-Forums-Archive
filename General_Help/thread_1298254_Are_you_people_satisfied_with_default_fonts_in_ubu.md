### Post by frankwakeman on 2009-10-22
I really think the font rendering improved dramatically as of 9.04, and so far I've found all other distros' font-rendering unnacceptable.  I use 8pt Bitstream Vera Sans for most things, and the 'Slight' Sub-Pixel setting (both my computers have LCD screens).

Even the default Sans looks pretty fine at 8pt, 9 is okay too.  9pt Serif in menus and on the panels is a different but pleasing look too.

For word-processing in OpenOffice I use 160% zoom which makes the roman type fonts pleasant and smooth too.
