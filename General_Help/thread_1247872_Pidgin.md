### Post by finferflu on 2009-08-23
Don't get discouraged, it's extremely easy, just go to Accounts->Manage Accounts and select your protocol (MSN, Google Talk, AIM, ICQ, etc) and fill in the form with your username and password.

More on this: [http://developer.pidgin.im/wiki/Using%20Pidgin#HowdoIuseAIMMSNYahooGoogleTalkJabberXMPPICQoranyotherprotocol](http://developer.pidgin.im/wiki/Using%20Pidgin#HowdoIuseAIMMSNYahooGoogleTalkJabberXMPPICQoranyotherprotocol)
