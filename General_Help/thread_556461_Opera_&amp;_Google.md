### Post by trippinnik on 2007-09-21
I went digging around the Opera site: you could look here [http://www.opera.com/support/search/supsearch.dml?keyword=browser+detection&platformID=](http://www.opera.com/support/search/supsearch.dml?keyword=browser+detection&platformID=)
and you could try this 
Go to Tools > Quick preferences > Edit site preferences
Under Network > Browser identification, choose "Mask as Internet Explorer"
Reload the page

You could also try reporting a bug to opera, and validating the HTML with Ctrl + V
