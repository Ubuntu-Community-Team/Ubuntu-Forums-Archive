### Post by marco-parillo on 2015-01-04
Since you mention Dolphin and Oxygen, I assume you are using KDE, if not all of Kubuntu? I would look for two places in your System Settings:
 - Workspace Appearance > Desktop Theme > Icons
 - Application Appearance > Icons

If you are getting fancier than that, you may want to re-post in [Desktop Environments]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=329").
