### Post by gordintoronto on 2011-01-26
Does your ISP provide a static IP, and let you host a web site, under its terms of service?

To change the domain IP address, you need to deal with the registrar. I assume when you bought the domain, the previous owner changed the owner information to you, at the registrar for the domain. To check this, go to:
[http://whois.domaintools.com/](http://whois.domaintools.com/)

There is a wee bit of HTML at [www.radio.mysite.com](www.radio.mysite.com) to do the redirect.
