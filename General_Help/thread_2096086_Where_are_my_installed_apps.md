### Post by robert shearer on 2012-12-19
Bob, tap the Super key (the one with the windows logo) and the dash will open onscreen.
Type your application name, eg deja dup and its icon will appear.
Click on it or hit enter and it will open.
When it opens an icon will appear on the launcher (LHS to you ;) ) and if you right click that icon and choose keep-in-launcher it will stick.

Cheers, Bob.

You can do this for any app, and you don't even need to know the name, try typing what it does for instance.
eg type back-up and several back-up apps will appear including deja dup.
Type burning and find cd/dvd burners etc...
