### Post by mcduck on 2011-04-21
You can't remove the launcher panel from unity Desktop. If you want to use Cairo Dock you should choose the "Classic Gnome" desktop from the Sessions-menu in the login screen. This will give you the traditional Gnome setup with more configurable Gnome panels instead of Unity.

(By the way, Unity's preferences are handled through CompizConfig Settings Manager, not by right-clicking like Gnome-panel)
