### Post by TeXtonyx on 2008-10-14
I'm sure someone else will answer your question. I'm going to mention something
you might not have thought about. There may be several drivers that work on
Vista that are not the same drivers as XP. Sometimes the mainboard will have
drivers for Vista and XP on the manufacturer website. Anyway, I think it is a good
idea to get your drivers lined up. Back in '95 Windows didn't have a driver for my
video card and I got a black screen. After the drivers, the rest is easy.
