### Post by nothingspecial on 2010-07-09
In your menu go system > administration > login window and uncheck the automatic login box

Right click your panel and choose add to panel, then choose indicator applet or notification area (the network icon is part of one of those)

If that doesn`t work, press Alt F2 and type

```
nm-applet
```
