### Post by CharlesA on 2013-07-09
If you need to have the machine plugged in to the mains in order for it to start, it is more than likely the battery. If you had a multimeter, you could try testing it, but I'm not really sure what the pinout of the battery connection is.

I would contact the manufacturer and see if they cover dead batteries or if they can check the charging circuit on the laptop to make sure it is ok.
