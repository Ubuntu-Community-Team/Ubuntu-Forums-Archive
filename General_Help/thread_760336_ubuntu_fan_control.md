### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-04-20
Unless you have fan control turned off in your BIOS, your fans should go faster or slower depending on the amount of heat inside your chassis and CPU. Having manual control is much more dangerous.

I don't know of any way to manually control fan speeds; it's probably not something that is implemented (more important things to write, like device drivers).
