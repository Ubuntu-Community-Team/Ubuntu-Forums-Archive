### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-11-02
Are you resting your head on your right hand while doing this? ;)

Best guesses:

Your right speaker has a bad connection:

Pull all of the jacks and plug them back in fifty times. Even "gold" plated connectors can get dirt on them.


Your sound mixer's "Balance" control is skewed to the left channel:

Click System -> Preferences -> Sound. Click the "Output" tab. Make sure the "Balance" slider is in the middle. Click the "Hardware" tab. Click the "Profile" dropdown menu and select the type of output that most closely represents what your sound card and speaker configuration is capable of. Click close.
