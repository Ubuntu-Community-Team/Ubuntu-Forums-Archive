### Post by Scott O'Nanski on 2008-02-14
For of those of you who have managed to get your Compiz Desktop Effects running, and have the Compiz Effects Manager running, but are experiencing less than spectacular performance - I would suggest the following tweak;

It worked great for me. I'm running Ubuntu Gutsy at a screen resolution of 1280x1024 with advance desktop effects and it's very slick!

My video card is a AGPx8, 256MB Nvidia Geforce 7600 GS; not exactly top of the line. The performance I'm getting rivals that of my AGPx8 512MB Sapphire Radeon x1600pro.

I also have the proprietary Nvidia drivers installed  by Envy loaded.

Goto>>

System> Preferences> Advance Desktop Effects Settings;

This will open up the "CompizConfig Setting Manager"

Once the "CompizConfig Setting Manager" is open;

Goto>>

"General Options"

While in "General Options", select the "Display Settings" tab. While under the "Display Settings Tab";

Unselect "Detect Refresh Rate"

Then slide the "Refresh" slider to "200"

Then select "Sync to VBlank"


That's all!

Give it a shot and tell me how it works.
