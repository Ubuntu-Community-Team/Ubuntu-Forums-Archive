### Post by nuts2 on 2014-08-01
It is silly to assume your computer is compromised just because your email got breached. A more likely scenario is you used the same user name and password on a site that is misusing your credentials. Did you sign up for any web forums or anything using the same credentials that you log into your email with right before the suspicious activity? Read up on daisy chain authentication to get a better idea where I am going with this and never use the same credentials on different sites because if one is compromised they are then all compromised.
