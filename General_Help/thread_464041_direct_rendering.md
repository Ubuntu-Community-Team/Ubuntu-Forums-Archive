### Post by defishguy on 2007-06-04
There is a substantial difference between 16 and 24 bit.  Every bit that you add literally doubles the possibilities..... ie. 17 bits is double the information that can be managed with just 16 bits so you can just imagine the increased complexity of 24.

Having said that, unless you've got keen eyes for photo editing, or a die hard gamer it's probably best to leave it at 16 bit.  You do have to have 24 bit depth if you want to use aiglx for the eyecandy.
