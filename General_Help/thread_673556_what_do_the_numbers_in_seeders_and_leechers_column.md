### Post by Lord Illidan on 2008-01-20
The number of seeders / leechers that you are currently connected to is shown before the brackets, the numbers shown in the brackets are the total number of seeders / leechers connecting to the tracker(s). You generally dont connect to the whole swarm.

Seeders are uploaders - you are connected to 12 out of 18 possible

### Post by TristanS on 2008-01-20
Hi

> For a torrent, KTorrent is showing me Seeders: 12 ( 18 ) and Leechers 19 ( 42 ) .

Seeders are the people who have downloaded the torrent and are currently allow others to download parts of the torrent from them, and leechers are the people current downloading the torrent from seeders.

The numbers are just showing how many seeders / leechers out of the total are currently seeding / downloading the torrent :)
