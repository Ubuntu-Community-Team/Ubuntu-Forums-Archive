### Post by HappyFeet on 2009-09-19
Just right click your top "status bar" and click **new panel**. Then right click the new panel>properties and move the position to the bottom. After that, you will want to right click the new panel and select **add to panel**. Then select **window list** ,**workspace switcher** and **trash**.
