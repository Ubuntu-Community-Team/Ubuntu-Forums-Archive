### Post by VCoolio on 2009-11-08
It's the new notifications behavior in Karmic. Yay! There are reasons for it but I don't like it. There are others who didn't like it and packaged notify-osd the old way. Add [this repo]("https://launchpad.net/~gilir/+archive/updates") and update notify-osd and you're good. 

deb [http://ppa.launchpad.net/gilir/updates/ubuntu](http://ppa.launchpad.net/gilir/updates/ubuntu) karmic main
deb-src [http://ppa.launchpad.net/gilir/updates/ubuntu](http://ppa.launchpad.net/gilir/updates/ubuntu) karmic main
