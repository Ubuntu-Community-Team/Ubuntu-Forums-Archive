### Post by kshane on 2008-11-05
When visiting on line video sites (i.e., Project Free TV, etc.) Firefox maximizes on it's own.  I have searched all over Mozilla's site looking for a way to stop this, but no luck.  Anyone have an idea what to do to stop Firefox from maximizing itself?

Thanks!

Kevin

---

### Post by ddrichardson on 2008-11-06
Try Edit->Preferences->Content, next to JavaScript theres a button reading "Advanced", click it then un-tick "Move or resize existing windows".
