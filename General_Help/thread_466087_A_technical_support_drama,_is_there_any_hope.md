### Post by steeleyuk on 2007-06-06
Formatting can do two things: a) delete the partition table (in which case data may be recoverable) or b) delete the entire partition (in which case the data is overwritten and generally not recoverable).

However, that may not matter if the SFR's have installed the game in which case the game would have overwritten (at least some of) the 0s and 1s on the disk (if (b) this probably won't apply anyway)

To be fair, it would be difficult to get the data back and her last chance may be sending the disk off to a recovery company but that is an expensive road to go down. someone else may have more advice on programs which may help.

good luck anyway...
