Help? It had been working five minutes ago. Up until I had plugged my 1/8th inch digital out sound to a 1/4th inch into a Ibanez amp. Could I have shorted out my sound card??
Its quite possible.  Amps put out some power, more than stereos, otherwise electric guitars would have to run on batteries.  
Try using alsamixer and alsamixer -c0 to make sure everthing is unmuted.  If you can't get it working then try booting into the live cd... any live cd that you recall sound working by default.  Be sure to also check all your connections, external volume dials and so forth if you have them...
