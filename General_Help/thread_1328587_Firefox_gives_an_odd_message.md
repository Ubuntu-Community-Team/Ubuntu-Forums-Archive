### Post by hwttdz on 2009-11-16
Not a big deal, either firefox terminated incorrectly, or the system went down incorrectly bringing firefox down too.  This is just it telling you something funny happened, it doesn't know the something funny was probably "sudo reboot" or "top|grep firefox|awk '{print $1}'|xargs kill -9".

Anyways, not something to worry about, just shut down firefox cleanly next time (i.e. x mark in the corner).
