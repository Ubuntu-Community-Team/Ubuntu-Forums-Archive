### Post by DracoNyon on 2009-11-10
My specs for my computer are a Pentium III 866mhz and 256mb or ram. I use xubuntu and it is soooo much faster than when I was on Ubuntu. As long as you don't intend to have like 3 heavy cpu applications going at once you shouldn't have a problem. Also don't have any applications going when you use the add/remove programs app. It makes it a little slow to respond. You could use what the above poster suggested but as far as I am concerned xubuntu will take far less time to customize to your liking than with the other distros.
