### Post by BrokeMahPC on 2010-05-01
I have camera troubles - seems to be a conflict - when you plug the camera into the pc it detects the memory card and the separate internal camera memory at the same time down the same cable and does not know what to do.

I get a "cannot lock the device" error or fspot crashes and you can't access the camera from nautilus.
It's a gnome desktop error.

I have solved the problem by buying a cheap usb card reader from my local supermarket. Take the memory card out of the camera, plug it into the card reader, plug it into the pc - it loads like a usb stick, icon on desk top - no problems, no need to use Fspot.
