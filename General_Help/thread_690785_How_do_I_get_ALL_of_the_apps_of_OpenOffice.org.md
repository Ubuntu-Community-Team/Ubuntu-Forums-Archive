### Post by machoo02 on 2008-02-07
HI,

You already have all of the components of OpenOffice already installed.  The formula editor (OO.o Math) simply does not show up under the Applications menu.  If you want to use the formula editor, fire up OO.o Writer then select File->New->Formula.  Boom....Equation Editor!  If you want to insert a formula into a document you are currently writing: Insert->Object->Formula.  The formula editor comes up at the bottom of the window, and you can edit the formulat that you would like in your document.

Hope this helps...
