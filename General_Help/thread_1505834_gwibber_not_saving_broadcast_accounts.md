### Post by Vrroom on 2010-09-17
Ok you all need to do this:

1.Update Gwibber to latest version (Lucid or Maverick)
2.Go to home folder and click on edit tab and check 'show hidden files'
3.Go to .config/gwibber
4.Backup and delete: gwibber.sqlite
5.Close nautilus and recreate your accounts

### Post by 0N3 on 2010-09-21

  close the Gwibber's account window, open it again and redo the facebook connexion process
