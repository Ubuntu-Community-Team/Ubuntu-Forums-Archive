### Post by UncleMonty on 2011-06-23
I'm working on a lengthy document that has a contents page. In microsoft word there is a feature which enables you to click on something in the contents page and it takes you to the relevant page in the document. Is there anyway of linking within a document in open office?

---

### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2011-06-24
Insert bookmarks (insert menu) to the pages and then create hyperlinks to these bookmarks at the contents' page.
Ctrl+click will lead you to your bookmarks.

---

### Post by amanchesterman on 2011-06-24
Why don't you get OpenOffice to create the contents page for you? (Insert > Indexes and Tables > Table of Contents) Then you can simply click on an entry in the contents page and it will take you straight to that section of the document.

If you want to create each entry in the Contents page manually, then you can use the Insert > Cross-reference option, and choose Headings ... or Bookmarks of course, as the previous poster has suggested.
