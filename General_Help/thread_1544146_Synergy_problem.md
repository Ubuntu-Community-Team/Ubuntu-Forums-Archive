### Post by Aearenda on 2010-08-02
I have this too - but I found that pressing and releasing all the shift keys in turn when a window expecting keystrokes is active on the 'remote' system (ie where the keyboard is not local) fixes it. I press left shift, right shift, left ctrl, right ctrl, left alt and right alt twice each.
