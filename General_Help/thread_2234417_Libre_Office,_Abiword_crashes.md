### Post by marco-parillo on 2014-07-14
I have dropped back down to Kubuntu 14.04 (having troubles with 14.10 in a VMware VM), and I have no problems with Libre Office.
But if you want to try something else, try [Calligra]("https://www.calligra.org/").
Muon Discover should find Calligra Words for you, in the Office category.
Their community page is: [https://community.kde.org/Calligra](https://community.kde.org/Calligra)
A deal-breaker for some is that it can open .doc and .docx files, but only save to ODF format.
