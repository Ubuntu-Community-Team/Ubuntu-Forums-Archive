### Post by sekinto on 2008-04-20
As for the internet. You are limited by your internet connection, you can't download any faster than that, but some browsers render faster than others. I've noticed that Firefox renders slightly faster than Firefox, Konqueror is a good one to, hopefully Epiphany will finally switch to WebKit... that should boost its performance a lot. Text based browsers are the fastest, I prefer Elinks, but you don't have any images in those. You should probably enable http pipelining in your browser to if it isn't enabled by default.
