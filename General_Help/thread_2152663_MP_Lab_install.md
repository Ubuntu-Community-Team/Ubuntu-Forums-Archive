Never mind I figured it out. 

  	 	 	 	   For those of you who would like to have it on their system; They have an IDE out that is up to date 2013. It is the seventh one down on the downlads list and is v8.91. You will have to use wine. First download it by clicking on the link and click save. When it is done downloading double click on it in your downloads to bring it up in archive manager. Then create a folder on your desktop and name it IDE. Go back to the archive loader and make sure that nothing in it is highlighted in blue. Click extract and go to desktop to extract to the IDE folder. What I did next is right click on Setup and clicked open with wine program loader and just fallowed the prompt.
