### Post by nathan84 on 2015-08-20
I'm a frugal person, anyways, I managed to solve the problem myself. For future reference if anybody has this problem there is another folder to delete it from.
1. Open your home folder
2. Hold down control and press H (CTRL+H)
3. Navigate to .themes and delete the corresponding files in there.
