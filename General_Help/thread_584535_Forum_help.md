### Post by Herman on 2007-10-21
See up in the second line from the top where it says,
"Home  User CP  Forum Help  Gallery   RSS Feeds   New Posts", on so on?

Well what I do is click on 'User CP', and a drop-down menu appears. 
One of the choices in the drop-down menu is 'Subscribed Threads'
Click on 'Subscribed Threads', and  you should be given a list of all the threads you have participated in.

What I do is keep checking that list quite frequently to see if anyone has replied to any of the threads I've ever been involved in. Sometimes old ones pop up that are ages old, that someone has found using the search function, probably, and wants to add a comment to or ask a question. I read them and decide whether they require a new reply or not.  Sometimes it's an interesting comment and sometimes it's someone who needs help.

That's how I do it. 
Regards, Herman :)
