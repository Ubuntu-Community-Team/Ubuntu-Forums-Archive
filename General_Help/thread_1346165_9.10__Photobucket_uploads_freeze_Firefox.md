### Post by tbzep on 2009-12-04
After upgrading to 9.10 and 3.5.5, Firefox freezes until all photos are uploaded.  I can't access other tabs, pull downs or anything. The Photobucket site greys out, and Conky freezes until the upload completes. I thought it might be Java, so I removed IcedTea and installed the latest Sun Java 64bit jre6u17. With Sun Java, the Photobucket page doesn't go grey any more, but Firefox still freezes.  Conky doesn't freeze now, and shows the upload progress.  Once the uploads complete, I can go back to using Firefox like nothing happened.  

I had no trouble with Photobucket before the upgrade.  I've also gone to a couple of other websites that use Java and the apps worked fine.
