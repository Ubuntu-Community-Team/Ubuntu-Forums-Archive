### Post by Fingers &amp; Thumbs on 2008-06-20
I don't have a fix for the looping issue, but you can disable the login sound. >System >Preferences >Sound, select the "Sounds" tab and then you can either deselect "Enable system sounds" or just set Log in to "No Sound.

   It may not solve the root of the problem, but should help your sanity until you do.
