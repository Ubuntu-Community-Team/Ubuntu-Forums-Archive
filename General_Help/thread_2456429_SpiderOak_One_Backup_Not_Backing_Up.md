### Post by CelticWarrior on 2021-01-11
Instead of reinstalling - a very bad advice from Windows users - try deleting the hidden folder that contains your profile/account/settings. If the problem is with anything inside that folder it doesn't matter how many times you reinstall, the problem will be the same.
