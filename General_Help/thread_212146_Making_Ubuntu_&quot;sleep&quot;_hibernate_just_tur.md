### Post by Sagotis on 2006-07-09
> **Boomy said:**
> Is there any way to make Ubuntu sleep? When I select hibernate it just shuts down the machine.

Go to system menu and then to preferences and then to power management. On the first tab are slide bars to set the time to whatever you want to make both your system and monitor go into suspend mode.
