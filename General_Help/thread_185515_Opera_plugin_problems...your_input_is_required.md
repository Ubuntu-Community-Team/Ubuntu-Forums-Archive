### Post by The J on 2006-05-31
From my understanding, you need one of the Opera 9.0 builds in order to get the MPlayer plugin working and even then you'll have to compile the latest version of it.

I just found [this link]("http://my.opera.com/community/forums/topic.dml?id=131761&t=1149124994&page=1#comment1512034") to a post in the Opera forums that has 2 pre-compiled versions of the MPlayer plugin that work with Opera 9.0, but do not have the onscreen player controls (this is the case for everyone who's tried to compile, not just these versions).  You'll have to try each version by copying the files right into the Opera plugin directory at /usr/lib/opera/plugins.

Mozplugger 1.7.3 works fine for me (but I still don't get player controls).
