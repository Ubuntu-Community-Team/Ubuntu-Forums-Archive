### Post by kgr132 on 2010-05-18
After every reboot, I had to go into Alsamixer, cursor over to 'Speaker' and change the setting from zero back to 100 in order to get any sound.

I found that right-clicking the Audio Applet and selecting Preferences, then the Output tab and changing the 'Connector' setting from 'Analog Output' to 'Analog Speakers' solved this problem and my volume now stays turned up after a reboot.
