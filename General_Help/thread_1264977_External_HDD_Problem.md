### Post by sgosnell on 2009-09-12
Try disconnecting & reconnecting the drive.  The drive might be named something else.  My WD Passport comes up as My_Passport, that's determined by a file on the HDD.  The drive should show up on your desktop, but that can be changed in settings.  The most common problem is a mounting error, which is usually solved by disconnecting/reconnecting the drive.
