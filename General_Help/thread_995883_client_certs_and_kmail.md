### Post by ellgor on 2008-11-28
Hi,

Click on "Settings" (in the Kmail window)and click on "configure Kmail" in the new window that opens click on "security" (in the left hand column)"reading", click on it if not already selected, is the first tab along the top and right at the bottom of this window is the import certificates etc, further adjustments can be made by clicking on the "S/MIME" tab should you have to, hope this helps.

Regards, Ellgor.
