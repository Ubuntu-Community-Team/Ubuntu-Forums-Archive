Hi, I've tried your solution but it didn't work as well as I expected. I get complete screen but with very bad resolution.

I tried to fix it and at last it worked.
I used the sysinfo application to know which was the graphic card  that my presario used [in my case trident cyberblade] and afterwards i entered in the safe mode session and select a monitor with 1024x768 and the trident cyberblade graphic card. I boot the computer and  in Preferences>Screen I could select the 1024x768 resolution.
