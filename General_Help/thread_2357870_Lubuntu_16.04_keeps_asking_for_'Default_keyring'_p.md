Unlock Keyring

An application wants access to the keyring 'Default keyring', but it is locked

The thing is it doesn't prompt me for a password on my administrative account, only on the regular accounts. If I, however, log in to the administrative account, and then switch users and log in to a regular account, the regular account won't be prompted for the keyring password.

In case if any of you are wondering, I did not change the login password on either accounts. I'm wondering if it has to do with the new USB Wi-Fi adapter that I'm using.

My specs and OS:

Sony VAIO VGC RB45GX
Intel Pentium 4
2 GB of RAM
Lubuntu 16.04.2 LTS (fresh reinstall)

The new USB Wi-Fi adapter also came with one of those tiny CDs, which I didn't use, since the internet works fine on the administrative account.
