### Post by strabes on 2007-05-26
KDE by default remembers & restores your last active session. To disable this behaviour, open kcontrol and go to KDE Components, Session Manager. Change "On Login" to "Start with an empty session"

To add programs to startup just drag them from the kmenu into ~/.kde/Autostart
