### Post by benj1 on 2009-10-12
smail is just an example program, that you probably wont have installed, its just to illustrate the command

eg
```
man -f man
```
will give you man pages related to the 'man' command

```
man -f ls
```
will give you man pages related to the 'ls' command etc
