### Post by Legace on 2009-05-16
> **AlanR8 said:**
> In which folder does *ubuntu hide its system/mime type icons? Just installed the updated Cairo Dock and I need to "point" to icons....

System default icons are in: /usr/share/icons
and your custom icons are in: ~/.icons

EDIT: So you're on KDE?
Then it could be in: ~/.kde/share/icons
