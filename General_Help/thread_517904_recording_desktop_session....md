### Post by clintbrot on 2007-08-05
These are 2 programs I've used:

[**Wink**]("http://www.debugmode.com/wink/download.php")
Wink saves files to swf.
or
[**istanbul**]("http://live.gnome.org/Istanbul")
istanbul saves files to ogg. which can be uploaded to google video, blip.tv, and gofish or you can easily convert it to any format using ffmpeg
