### Post by DocHoliday52090 on 2012-05-10
I figured it out. Turns out my monitor, a CHIMEI CMV 221D is notorious for failing to send out the EDID (a signal that tells your video card what resolutions is supported by the montitor) through the DVI cable. 

Someone captured the EDID signal in a file. I put it in /etc/x11 and in my xconf turned

> Section "Device"
    Identifier     "Device0"
    Driver         "nvidia"
    VendorName     "NVIDIA Corporation"
    EndSection


to 

> Section "Device"
    Identifier     "Device0"
    Driver         "nvidia"
    VendorName     "NVIDIA Corporation"
    Option "CustomEDID" "DFP-0:/etc/X11/cmv221d.edid"
    EndSection

I have also attached the edid in case anyone else needs it. Extract it out of the .zip obviously. Note that initially my xconf didnt even have those entries. I had to reconfigure it through 'sudo nvidia-xconfig'

Cheers!
