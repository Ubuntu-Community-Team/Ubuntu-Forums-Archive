### Post by DuckHook on 2020-09-29
Welcome to the forums, pxc3110

I could find only one resource in launchpad that appears to have been started in 2012 to translate Ubuntu into Inuktitut, but has had no activity. I suspect that the effort has stalled. You can try contacting the members on the launchpad team to see what their status is.

[https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-l10n-iu](https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-l10n-iu)

To my knowledge, Ubuntu has no Inuktitut translation.

You may also be interested in the following research paper:

[https://www.researchgate.net/publication/291696693_Socio-Economic_Impacts_of_Translating_Ubuntu_to_Inuktitut](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/291696693_Socio-Economic_Impacts_of_Translating_Ubuntu_to_Inuktitut)
