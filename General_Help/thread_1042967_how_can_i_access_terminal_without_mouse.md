### Post by ajcham on 2009-01-18
Either:
Alt+F2 and enter the command **gnome-terminal** into the run dialog. This will give you a terminal emulator on the desktop.

Or Ctrl+Alt+F1, to go to an actual terminal (also F2 through F6).

---

### Post by mcduck on 2009-01-18
sure. If you want the gnome-terminal, press Alt-F2 and then type "gnome-terminal" in the dialog & hit enter.

Or just press Ctrl+Alt+F1 (to F6) to access the TTY's. Ctrl+Alt+F7 will bring you back to desktop again.
