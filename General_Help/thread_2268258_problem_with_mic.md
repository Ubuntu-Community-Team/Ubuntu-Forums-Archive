### Post by nerdtron on 2015-03-07
Plug in your headphones, open alsamixer. See below for different sound device, you should see "Mic". Select it using the left and right arrow key. If you see "MM" above it, it means it is muted. Press M to unmute it and press the up/down arrow key to increase the volume of that device.
You can experiment on other settings. Or you can post a screen shot here. There are a lot of device on alsamixer so be sure to scroll to left or right to the screen.
