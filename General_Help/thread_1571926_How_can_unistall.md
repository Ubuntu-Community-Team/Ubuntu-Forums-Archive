### Post by searchfgold6789 on 2010-09-10
For version 9.10 and lower of ubuntu, go into Synaptic Package Manager as explained above. Type the name of the software into the search box and click on the green check box next to it, then click "Mark for removal", then click the apply button.
For version 10.04 (the latest version of ubuntu), Click on applications, click on Ubuntu Software Center, type in the name of your software in the search box, click the name of the software, and click remove. It will do all the removing automatically in both of the methods listed above. You may have to restart your computer.
If anything goes awry, feel free to post back and we'll help you out.
