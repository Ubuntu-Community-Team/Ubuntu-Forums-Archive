### Post by Universal344 on 2008-10-30
I found drivers from AMD/ATI's site.

QUOTE (from ati.amd.com)

"Which ATI graphics cards can use this driver? 	

The ATI Proprietary Linux driver currently supports Radeon 8500 and later AGP or PCI Express graphics products, as well as ATI FireGL 8700 and later products. We do not currently plan to include support for any products earlier than this. Drivers for earlier products should already be available from the DRI Project or Utah-GLX project."

SO the drivers should work on your machine.  I would wait to see if anyone can tell you if this the right thing to do, just in case I'm wrong.  Also, I am afraid I won't be able to help with installation problems for my knowledge is not so vast.  

Here is the link to the driver download:

[http://ati.amd.com/support/driver.html]("http://ati.amd.com/support/driver.html")

You will have to select your system architecture and Graphics card model and series.  Again I would wait for confirmation.  Hope this helped.
