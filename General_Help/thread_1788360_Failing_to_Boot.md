### Post by FormatSeize on 2011-06-22
> **Janeleaper said:**
> I'm a bit concerned that this is the first symptom of a worse problem. Does anyone have any ideas?
It could have been a random Thing That Happened, and nothing more than a odd curiosity. 
 
I will say that I have had it happen to me before on the first machine that I built. Everything was working fine one day, and the next day it stopped booting. So I went to take it to the shop that I get parts from to see if they could help me diagnose the problem, but the shop wasn't open. I left it in the car overnight, and when I plugged it in and turned it on the next day, everything was working fine. 
 
Soon enough, I found that having the DVD drive installed caused the computer not to boot. I've since built another machine, but last night when I took the DVD drive from the old one and installed it in the new one, the computer would not boot. I unhooked the cable that linked it to the motherboard, and it booted fine. 
 
Hopefully everything with your machine is fine, but best case scenario if something is wrong it may just be an easily replaceable component.
