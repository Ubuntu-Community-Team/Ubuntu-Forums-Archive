### Post by Rajiv_Nair on 2007-06-02
the image is called a skydome image. Goto "beryl settings manager" -> Desktop->Desktop cube->Skydome and select an image. There is one restriction though. The images u choose should be square images and its length should be a power of 2 like 1024 x 1024 or 2048 x 2048 etc :)

### Post by NikoC on 2007-06-02
I think what you are looking for can be found via:
Beryl settings manager > Desktop > Desktop cube > Skydome

Enable this feature and select an image. You might want to google for those since they have to have certain dimensions.
You might also want to play around with the zoom function of the cube to make it smaller when rotating and thus making more of the background visible.

Good luck!
