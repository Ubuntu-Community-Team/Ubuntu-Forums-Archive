### Post by Fourcultures on 2009-05-28
It's not obvious from our post what driver you're using, but if it doesn't work properly, one of these suggestions may help:

1) Canon Australia has Linux drivers at [http://www.canon.com.au/products/printers/colour_bj_printers/ip3500_support.aspx](http://www.canon.com.au/products/printers/colour_bj_printers/ip3500_support.aspx)

2) The PIXMA iP3500 seems to be equivalent to the PIXMA MP520. I chose this from the driver database in Ubuntu 9.04 and it works perfectly, except that there's no indicator of ink availability.
