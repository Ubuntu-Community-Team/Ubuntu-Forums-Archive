### Post by steve509 on 2010-08-09
Solved the problem...In CompizConfig do NOT use the Rotate left and Rotate Right for the mouse in Bindings, instead go to Viewpoint Switcher, then click on the Desktop-based Viewpoint Switching tab and enable the Move left and Move right using Button 4 and button 5
