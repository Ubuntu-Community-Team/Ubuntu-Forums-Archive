### Post by Kevbert on 2008-04-25
Turn on bluetooth on PC and make visible to other devices.
On Phone. Select Menu > Settings > Connectivity > Bluetooth and from the following options:
Bluetooth — Select On to activate the Bluetooth function.
My phone's visibility — make it visible to other Bluetooth devices
Paired devices — to search for any Bluetooth device in range. Select New to list any Bluetooth device in range. Select PC and Pair. Enter a Bluetooth passcode (up to 16 characters) eg 0000 to pair the phone to your PC. You only need use this passcode when you connect to the PC for the first time. A pop-up box will appear on the PC asking for the passcode (00000). Your phone will then connect to the PC, and then you can start data transfer.
Hope this is what you are after.  If not let me know.
