### Post by Rickytoo on 2007-05-11
I have been having a similar problem. After hitting save, it appears to freeze, but after about 2-3mins the saving progress bar appears and it slowly goes on to save successfully. Program control returns.

I had been experimenting with the .svg filter. On one slide there were hundreds of lines imported and converted to .odg objects that you could edit. I think this proved to be too much. Deleting the slide or using a bitmap version overcame the problem and saving functioned as normal.

I hope this helps.
