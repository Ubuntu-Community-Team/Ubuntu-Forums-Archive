### Post by majidasgari on 2010-11-27
Actually, not related to this post. I couldn't delete my reply after sending :(

Here is a workaround to use Ctrl+Alt+V in klipper at KDE 4.5: (Kilpper Ctrl+Alt+V is not working in KDE too with default preferences)

Unlock widgets
right click on system tray and choose System Tray Settings
Click on Entries and click on the Keyboard Shortcuts button in front of Klipper item.
Press Ctrl+Alt+V then click on Reassign button.
Click on OK
