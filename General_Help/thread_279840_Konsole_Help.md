Anyone know how to change background and font color of the Konsole in Ubuntu?

---

### Post by roland on 2007-02-17
You can use the Settings menu (like in most other KDE apps). There you can adjust the colors and fonts. The fonts are to be found in Settings - Fonts - Custom... and the colors are in the Settings - Schema menu. Try to experiment with it, soon you will find what's best for you. Then choose Settings - Save as Default, and it will be like this next time you start Konsole.
