### Post by mechro on 2010-04-05
Go to **System > Preferences > Sound > Sounds**... **Visual alerts** is probably **Disabled**... Left-click **Disabled** (you might have to click twice or hold the mouse button - it's buggy) and change it to **Flash Window** or **Flash Screen**.

I don't know what effect you'll get but it might be worth a try.
