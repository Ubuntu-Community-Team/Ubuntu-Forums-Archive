### Post by omi291 on 2008-01-28
When i use keyboard shortcuts to switch between desktops, everything is fine. However, when i actually click on a specific desktop at the bottom right corner of the screen, both toolbars disappear, the computer freezes and i have to restart or else i am stuck like that. does anybody know the problem?

---

### Post by accatagon on 2008-01-28
Are you using compiz? If you are using the cube or the wall, make sure the number of desktops is 1.  The "horizontal virtual size" determines the actual number of desktops on the cube (yeah, it's weird). I had a problem with compiz freezing up when moving between desktops and that fixed it. . .
