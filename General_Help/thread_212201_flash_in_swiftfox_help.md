### Post by LordTureis on 2006-07-10
open **about:config**

find **plugin.default_plugin_disabled** and switch it to **false**

you may need to restart firefox/swiftfox

hope that works! :D

**edit:** note that this disables the 'install plugin' message for **any** plugin, not just flash
