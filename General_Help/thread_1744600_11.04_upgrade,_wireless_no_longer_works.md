### Post by spiceisland on 2011-05-01
When I upgraded my Dell Inspiron 6400 (same-ish to the 1501, I believe), wireless also stopped working (sorry, wired carried on working fine).

Some hunting around showed that this is down to the Broadcom wireless drivers being hosed in 11.04.

The trick I used was to follow the instructions here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1700897](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1700897)

and downgrade to the Broadcom drivers used for 10.10. In the posting, above, skip to page 2 and read #14 onwards. The postings before #14 put forward an alternative fix which I, personally, did not fancy.

Hopefully the 11.04 ones will be fixed soon.
