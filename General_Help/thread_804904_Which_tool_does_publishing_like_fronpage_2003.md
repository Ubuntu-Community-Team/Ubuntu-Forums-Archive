### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-05-23
Varies on what your style is:

If you are looking for a HTML Editor, [Bluefish]("http://bluefish.openoffice.nl/") is your toy.
Else, if you want a complete Web Authoring Kit, [Kompozer]("http://www.kompozer.net/") will probably be more in your favour.

They are both in the repositories.
```
sudo apt-get install bluefish kompozer
```
Try them both out, you may prefer one over the other.
You may find that using both in sync will boost your creativity.
