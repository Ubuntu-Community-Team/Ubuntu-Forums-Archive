### Post by DeMus on 2009-01-19
> **abod10 said:**
> What is the defference between an e-mail message body and attachment ?

The message body is the text you type in the e-mail, the questions you ask, the things you report to somebody else.
An attachment is a file you attach to your e-mail. This can be all kinds of files: text documents, pictures, movies, slide shows, spreadsheets, etc.
