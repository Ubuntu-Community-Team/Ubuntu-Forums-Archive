### Post by Sipefree on 2005-05-28
Well, I fixed it.


I think it was because one time, the LiveCD crashed on me, and it never reset the hardware, or whatever.

When I booted back into the LiveCD again just now, it crashed again, and this time the monitor wouldn't start, so I pressed the Firmware Reset button (beside the Hard Restart button) and all was well.

Maybe this should be mentioned somewhere  :neutral:
