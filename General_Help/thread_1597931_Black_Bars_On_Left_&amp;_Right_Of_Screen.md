### Post by SeijiSensei on 2010-10-15
You probably just need to pick a 16:9 resolution like 1280x720 rather than a 4:3 resolution like 1024x768.  Open the NVIDIA X Server Settings application, pick "X Server Display Configuration", and choose an appropriate resolution.
