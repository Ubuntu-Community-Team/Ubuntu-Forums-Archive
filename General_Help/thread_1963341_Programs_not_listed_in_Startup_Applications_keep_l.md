### Post by steveray100 on 2012-04-22
Everytime I logout or restart or shutdown and reboot,  Firefox, Thunderbird, and Rythmbox keep loading automatically, yet I do not have them listed in Startup Applications nor do I have the "Automatically remember running Application when logging out" option checked. Note that I am usually have these three programs loaded when the computer is on.
Don't really know what to do to fix this.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SOLVED IT.
First, closed out of all running applications
Second, I went to Preferences>Startup Applications>Options
Third,  clicked on "Remember currently running applications"
Fourth, Re-booted, problem solved
