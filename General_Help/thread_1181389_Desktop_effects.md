I just installed desktop effects using the add/remove button, but I don't know how to enable the plug in.  Can anyone tell me basically how to get the effects working?

---

### Post by Tipped OuT on 2009-06-07
I'm guessing what you installed was CompizConfig Settings Manager (CCSM)? If so, go to System< Preferences< CompizConfig Settings Manager. From here you can choose your desktop effects, and configure them. 

Also, please make sure to have your proprietary drivers installed from System< Administration< Hardware Drivers.
