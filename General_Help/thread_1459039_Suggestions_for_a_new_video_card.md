### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-04-20
A GT210 or GTX220 would be perfectly fine for what you want.

What might be even cheaper is an 8400GS, and realistically you don't need the extra power of a newer card.

With graphics cards, you'll never find any that are actually branded "Nvidia"; manufacturers take the Nvidia chipsets and designs and put them into a graphics card. You will probably find the Nvidia logo on the back of the box. As long as it uses an Nvidia GPU and not an ATI one, you'll be fine.

Also note that some more powerful cards, but these require a PCI-E power plug or two from your power supply. Always check before buying that the card doesn't require a PCI-E power plug, or if it does that your power supply actually has one.
