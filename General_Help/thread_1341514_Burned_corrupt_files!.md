### Post by zircon214 on 2009-11-30
Different optical media means using a different type or brand of disks. (Personal anecdote: Verbatim DVD+R gives me no problems.)

Are you checking the MD5 hash of the ISO you downloaded as suggested in lisati's link above? [More info here.]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM") You should do this to make sure the file you downloaded isn't itself corrupt before wasting any more disks.

At what speed are you burning these disks? Try burning at half the rated speed of your burner/media.

Forget about the Breezy version; it's old and unsupported.
