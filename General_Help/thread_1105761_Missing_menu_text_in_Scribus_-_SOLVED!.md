### Post by rosalia on 2009-03-25
I thought others might benefit from a fix my husband and I discovered recently. When we installed Scribus onto Ubuntu 8.10, all the menus came up without text. They just had little lines (like dashes or underscores) instead of the text, alongside the few little icons that usually appear in the menus. 

We solved this by going to System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Visual Effects and clicking "None." I think it worked because doing this switched off the anti-aliasing that the NVIDIA card otherwise does.
