### Post by DJ_Peng on 2009-01-19
Spell checking in Epiphany is a known issue ([Bug #149873]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/epiphany-browser/+bug/149873")), and changing settings in about:config is also a known issue ([Bug #201396]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/epiphany-browser/+bug/201396")). I'm hoping we can get this fixed soon because it's the most glaring issue in moving from Firefox 3 to Epiphany.
