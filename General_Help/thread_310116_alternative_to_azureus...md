I don't think this is what you are looking for, but I can suggest what I am using right now- Transmission ( [http://transmission.m0k.org/](http://transmission.m0k.org/) ). It is more popular on Macs, but they have Linux version too. It is really simplistic and no replacement for Azureus if you need it's features, but it does it's job well with very little resources. Can't really feel it when it runs and I can't remember it crashing.
