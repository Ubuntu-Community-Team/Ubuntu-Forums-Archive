### Post by angryhomer17 on 2008-01-06
It looks like the component output is the same as the one on a 7600 GT (my card). I don't think it is necessarily for hdtv's only. You might try hooking a composite (red, white, yellow) cable to the component out. Red to red, blue to white and green to yellow. Or see if you can find an adapter of some sorts. 

Even if you do get a video output the best you can expect is a 720x480 resolution, which isn't all that great. 

And if none of that works, just try putting the cables wherever they fit. Something might eventually work.
