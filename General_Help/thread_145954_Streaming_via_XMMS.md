### Post by tkiesel on 2006-03-17
You should be able to add the URL for the stream and play it to your heart's content.

CLick and hold the "Add" button at the bottom left of the XMMS playlist.  That's click and hold, because if you just click and release, it'll offer to load a file.

As you hold the button down, you should see three options near your pointer: File, Dir(ectory), and URL.  Choose URL and you're on your way to streaming bliss.
