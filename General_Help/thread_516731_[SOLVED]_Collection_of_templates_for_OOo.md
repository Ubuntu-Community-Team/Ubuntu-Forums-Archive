### Post by sci-fi guy on 2007-08-28
I found a decent pack of templates: [OxygenOffice's.]("http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=170021&package_id=208656") The link is to the page where you can download OygenOffice's extensions (which seem to just be templates). Download and extract one of the template packs, and place the templates in your .openoffice.org2/user/template folder. But one might want to delete extra files first.
