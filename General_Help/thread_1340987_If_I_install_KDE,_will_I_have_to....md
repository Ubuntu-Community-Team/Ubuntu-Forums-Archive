### Post by RATM_Owns on 2009-11-29
Yes, they will show up in the GNOME menu.

But there is [this helpful package]("http://www.gtk-apps.org/content/show.php/Gnome+Menu+Extended+%28Debian+Package%29?content=73515") that creates a KDE submenu in your GNOME menu and places all KDE apps in there. If you don't want the KDE submenu, right click the GNOME menu, select "Edit Menu" and uncheck "KDE."
