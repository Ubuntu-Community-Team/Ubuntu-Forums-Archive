### Post by Patb on 2008-10-16
Zalibidas, the short answer is yes, if your drive has adequate space. I would suggest you format the new partition as fat32 for now but that may only reflect my lack of experience with Windows and NTFS. Others may think differently.

For a guide to partition setups, I would suggest Herman's: [http://www.users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/p17.htm](http://www.users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/p17.htm)
