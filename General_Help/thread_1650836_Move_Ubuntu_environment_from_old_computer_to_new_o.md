### Post by jingaling on 2010-12-22
Download the clonezilla iso from [here]("http://clonezilla.org/download/sourceforge/stable/iso-zip-files.php") then take an image of your old computer.

Restore the image to your new computer and boot it up. The first time you boot it may complain about the drivers. Just install the correct ones for your new machine and roberts your mothers brother!
