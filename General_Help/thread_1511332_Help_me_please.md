### Post by tendonstrength on 2010-06-16
Just keep in mind that you will have two network devices listed in the ifconfig output if you have both an ethernet card and a wireless card, so make sure you get the one you want. On my laptop eth0 is the ethernet and eth1 is wireless, but yours may be different.
