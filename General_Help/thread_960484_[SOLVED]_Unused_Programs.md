### Post by bryonak on 2008-10-27
The slowdown from installed programs is next to non-noticable. It's the running ones (services, autostarted,...) that do the slowing down ;)

You can uninstall everything you don't need, just make sure it isn't a dependency of something you want to use (if it has dependencies, you will get prompted when you try to uninstall it).
If you want to reinstall it later, it will wait for you in the Add/Remove menu.

But if you want to speed up your system, go to Main Menu -> System -> Administration -> Services, hit the unlock button and turn off everything you don't need, this will give you a much greater efficiency boost (though not that much, since it is streamlined already).
You can also search these forums and google for speedup howto's.
