### Post by mrdc76 on 2020-12-05
The camera should appear in Files when the cable is attached and be mountable. Otherwise you will have to remove the SD-card from the camera and insert it into the computer's card reader.

---

### Post by Autodave on 2020-12-05
As mrdc76 said, you should have a USB cable that came with the camera.  Connecting that should show an icon on your screen for the camera.  If it doesn't automatically mount the camera, then click on the icon and choose "Open".  That will get you to the contents of the camera.  Create a folder on your desktop or use your "Pictures" folder to move the pics from the camera to your PC.

If you are using an SD card in the camera, then it is much quicker to get an SD card reader that plugs into a USB port on your computer and transfer the card from your camera to your card reader and then transfer the pics.
