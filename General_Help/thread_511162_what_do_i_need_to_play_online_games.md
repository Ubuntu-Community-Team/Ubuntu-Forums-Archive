### Post by zanglang on 2007-07-27
You'd need Wine (package name = 'wine'), and a properly configured graphics card driver. Head down to the Gaming subforum and search around if anyone has posted guides for your games (there were some for RO I remember)
[http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=93](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=93)

Once you have Wine installed you can search the WineHQ's AppDB to see if your game is supported as well:
[http://appdb.winehq.com/](http://appdb.winehq.com/)
