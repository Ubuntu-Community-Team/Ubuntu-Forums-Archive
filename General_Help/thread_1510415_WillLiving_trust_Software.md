### Post by dabl on 2010-06-15
It's just a document.  You can write it with any word processing software, or even a text editor.  Copy a template off the Internet:

[http://www.lectlaw.com/forms/f115.htm](http://www.lectlaw.com/forms/f115.htm)

[http://www.doyourownwill.com/lwindex.asp](http://www.doyourownwill.com/lwindex.asp)

[http://www.uslivingwillregistry.com/forms.shtm](http://www.uslivingwillregistry.com/forms.shtm)

[http://www.livingwillid.com/](http://www.livingwillid.com/)

[http://estate.findlaw.com/estate-planning/living-wills/le23_9_1.html](http://estate.findlaw.com/estate-planning/living-wills/le23_9_1.html)
