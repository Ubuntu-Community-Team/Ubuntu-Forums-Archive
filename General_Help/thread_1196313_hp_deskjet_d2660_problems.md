### Post by 47_MasoN_47 on 2009-08-25
Hey after some more Googling I found a solution that worked for me.  I got the HPLIP package from HP, the one included in the Ubuntu repos didn't work for me.  I uninstalled the printer and disconnected it, then ran the HPLIP .run file that I got from HP.  After it finished installing it's GUI wizard thing came up and I typed a couple of things in and bam! the printer is working :D

[http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/downloads.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/downloads.html)
