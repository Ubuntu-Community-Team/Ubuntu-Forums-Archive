### Post by s_h_a_d_o_w_s on 2006-05-22
Are they MP3s ? CAn your system play them clearly? When you put the music on your Ipod, don't unplug it. Instead, Click on one of the music files (that is on your Ipod, while your Ipod is in USB mode) and you should hear the music begin to play. With your arrow keys, scroll past each file, you shouldn't have to wait to listen to it. This should refresh your iPod and will most likely fill in the holes in your music, because the music file is there, but it is incomplete. So, do that, and your iPod should recognize the holes and fill 'em. Hope that helps

(it was like that on my PSP, so do that, maybe listen to the music by putting your cursor on the icon, and it should work)
