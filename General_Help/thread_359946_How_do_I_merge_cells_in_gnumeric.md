### Post by IYY on 2007-02-12
Select the cells, and click on the merge icon. It looks like a white square with a black outline and four sections partitions by grey lines. It's located in the toolbar where the font selection is. The icon order is... font, size, bold, italics, underline, left, middle, right, center, MERGE, split...
