### Post by hudson7 on 2008-12-22
Hi Oliver,

If you open up a terminal, you can type:

man [command]

to get the manual page for that command. For example:

man ls

You can also use the info system. Type in "info" and move the cursor over "coreutils" and press "enter". Then page down and look for "Opening the software toolbox".

With info, it is kind of strange moving around. When I want to get back to the top, I always just hit Ctrl-c and then restart it ;-)
