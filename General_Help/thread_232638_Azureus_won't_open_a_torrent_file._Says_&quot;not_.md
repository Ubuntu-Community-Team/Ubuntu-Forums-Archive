### Post by bluto20 on 2007-05-18
I think i found a temporary solution with being able to get the torrents to work instead of saying not a file. What I did was download the torrent to whatever folder you prefer, then go into Azurueus and open the torrent from File>Open>Torrent File.. Click on Add Files tab, then search for your torrent file. Click the Ok's out until u be back to your Azureus. It should load that way. Hope this helps.
