### Post by Rudjgaard on 2013-12-11
Hello, I have a sound related problem.
 
Although output sound works fine I can't seem to get the mic working.
I am running 13.10 on a Desktop PC with amd cpu and a SB x-fi titanium hd soundcard
The card and the drivers are loaded fine since it's recognized without any problems
I have the audiodev team ppa in my update list so alsa is at newest version

This is the detected hw:
```
lspci -nn | grep '\[04[80][13]\]'
00:14.2 Audio device [0403]: Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. [AMD/ATI] SBx00 Azalia (Intel HDA) [1002:4383] (rev 40)
02:00.1 Audio device [0403]: Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. [AMD/ATI] Barts HDMI Audio [Radeon HD 6800 Series] [1002:aa88]
03:00.0 Audio device [0403]: Creative Labs EMU20k2 [X-Fi Titanium Series] [1102:000b] (rev 03)

```
Input device is not muted in pavucontrol and dual booting into win the mic works so it's not hw related
Any help is greatly appreciated, thanks

### Post by Rudjgaard on 2013-12-15
After almost a week I managed to solve it. 
1. Purged oss4 drivers and switched to dkms
2. Terminal "alsamixer" -> F4 Capture -> Switch mic source to working one

Cheers
