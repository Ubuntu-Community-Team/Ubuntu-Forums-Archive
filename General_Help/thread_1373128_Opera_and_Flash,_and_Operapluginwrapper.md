### Post by HappyFeet on 2010-01-05
Get rid of the flash you have. Go [here]("http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/") and download the .tar.gz flash file. Unpack it, and put **libflashplayer.so** file into /usr/lib/opera/plugins

Restart opera, and enjoy.
