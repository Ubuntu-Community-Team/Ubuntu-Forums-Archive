Impress is only in OpenOffice.org for compatibility with MS Office.  The vast majority of the time, such presentations are pointless, and could easily be substituted by a landscape PDF document in slideshow mode.  This is cross-platform, standard, streamlined, and creatable in any good word processor (or even a text editor using LaTeX).

If you really want some [Impress templates, then look for some]("http://www.google.com.au/search?q=OpenOffice.org+Impress+templates").
