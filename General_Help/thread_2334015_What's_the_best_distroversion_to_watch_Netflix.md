### Post by montag dp on 2016-08-15
I guess a lightweight one like Lubuntu or Xubuntu, not necessarily because you want to watch Netflix, but because 1 GB ram will be marginal for other desktop environments. The way that I usually watch Netflix is with Chrome, but Google no longer releases 32-bit Chrome. I've heard that Vivaldi will play Netflix, but I'm not sure if it has a 32-bit version or not. Otherwise, there may be some more convoluted ways to do it with Wine.
