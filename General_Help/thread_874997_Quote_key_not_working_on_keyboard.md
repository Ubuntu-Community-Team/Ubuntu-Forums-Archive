### Post by mrboojive on 2008-07-30
There might be something wrong with your keyboard layout settings. Try going to System > Preferences > Keyboard and look under the Layouts tab.

---

### Post by Titan8990 on 2008-07-30
Yep, looks like your keyboard layout is US-INTL when it should be just US.

---

### Post by Bucky24 on 2008-07-30
Awesome, I changed it to USA default and it works perfectly now. Thanks guys!

-Bucky24

System > Preferences > Keyboard->Layouts(tab)->

     Variants: USA
