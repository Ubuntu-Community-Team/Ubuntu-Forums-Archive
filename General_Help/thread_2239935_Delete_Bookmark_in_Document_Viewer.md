### Post by Buntu Bunny on 2014-08-17
Firstly, welcome to the Ubuntu Forums binchunsu! 

At the top of Document Viewer's side pane (View > Side Pane) is a drop down menu (thumbnails, annotations, bookmarks). Select bookmarks and highlight the one you want to delete. "Remove" is at the bottom of the pane.
