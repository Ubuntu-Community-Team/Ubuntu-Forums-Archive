### Post by ykcirt on 2009-04-26
I need to stop Gnome/Ubuntu from autoplaying music files when I happen to hover my mouse above a file slightly too long. It never ceases to startle me.

How do I do that again? I looked at the Sound Preferences, but there is nothing there. I think I did it before for Ubuntu 8. Can't find any good google searches on it though.

---

### Post by todak on 2009-04-26
Open your home folder. In the menu Click on **Edit> Preferences**. Click on the **Preview** tab. Go to the **Sound Files** section and click on the spinner next to **Preview sound files:**. There is a selection for **Never**. Click on that and you are set.
