### Post by Impavidus on 2016-05-14
I doubt that the various lists of 8, 10, 12 or 16 things to do after installing Ubuntu that you can find in blog posts apply to anyone except the author of that blog post. Right after installation you have a working system. You don't have to do anything, but you may want to. These lists may give you a few useful suggestions, but I suggest you don't follow them blindly.

As for bleachbit: that's about as useful in Ubuntu Studio as in all other flavours. Many use it. I don't.
