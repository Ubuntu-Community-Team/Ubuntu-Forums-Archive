### Post by sgosnell on 2010-03-15
The panel is the area at the top, bottom, or both, where the menus are, and where running program icons are shown.  By default, it would be the bottom, but it's easy to change things around.  Mine are on the sides, because I have far more screen real estate on the sides than vertically on my netbook.  The windows-switcher is also easy to remove, so perhaps yours is gone.  Truecrypt should show up as a window like any other program, but I don't know exactly what you did or how your machine is set up.
