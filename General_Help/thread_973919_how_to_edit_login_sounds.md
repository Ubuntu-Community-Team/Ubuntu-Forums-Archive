### Post by _sAm_ on 2008-11-07
System > Prefrences > Sounds 
Go to tab called sounds, here you can change "Sound Theme"(mine is change to Ubuntu studio witch I installed via Synaptic), or you can change the login sounds under "Alerts and Sound Effects".
