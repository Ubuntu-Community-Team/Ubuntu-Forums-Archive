### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-02-08
Are you experiencing problems connecting to the Internet or browsing the web? They are very different things. Your title suggest you have a Firefox problem. So follow these instructions:

>    1.  Type **about:config** in Firefox address bar, press Enter.
   2. Find ***network.dns.disableIPv6*** in the list.
   3. Right-click -> Toggle.
   4. Restart Firefox and try again.

Also see [Firefox optimization and troubleshooting thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1193567).

If you have network problems and the little icon in the corner of the main Ububtu panel shows you are not connected at all, then please specify if you are using wireless or cable. Also would be interesting to specify your computer brand and model in case you are using a notebook. If you know the modem model would help too.

BTW, do you get a graphical desktop after the "DOS looking thing" or just the last one?
