### Post by ElToro on 2011-12-12
I regularly use several languages in my work and use dictionaries for all these languages in both LibreOffice and Evolution. Lately, all dictionaries except the English language ones do a disappearing act on a regular basis. The language choices under Tools - Spelling just shows various English dictionaries and none of the others. :confused:

When this happens, I go to the Synaptic Package Manager and reinstall the packages. That makes them reappear in the Spelling menu. However, it is just a matter of time before they disappear again...

The problem began shortly after updating to Ubuntu 11.10 from 11.04 (64-bit).
