### Post by matteojg on 2009-01-03
In order to get the cube looking like you see in most demos you need to set the following in the compizconfig manager:

1)General>Desk Top Size>Horizontal Virtual Size>Set to 4
2) Enable "Desktop Cube"
3) Click on the "Cube" icon>Behavior>Uncheck "inside cube" if it's selected
4) Enable "Rotate Cube"
5) Click on the "Rotate Cube" icon>General>Use the slide at the bottom to select a zoom level (the higher the level the farther away the cube will appear.

If you want a backround for the cube then Click on the "Cube" icon>Appearance>Expand the "Skydone" menu>check the "Skydome" box

Hope it works for you.
