### Post by dbott67 on 2006-10-27
2 things to check...

1. Beryl has it's own theme manager.  Check there & re-apply (or change) the theme.  Then try setting it back.

2. Using the Gnome Themes Manager, change the theme back to something different, save it, and then change it back to your preferred settings.
