### Post by Tamlynmac on 2008-10-29
Left click and drag you mouse down the left side of the spreadsheet where the numbers are and where you'd like to insert the rows. Highlight the number of rows you'd like to include then right click on the highlighted area and say insert rows. It will insert the same number of rows you highlighted. I found this to be the simplest way,
