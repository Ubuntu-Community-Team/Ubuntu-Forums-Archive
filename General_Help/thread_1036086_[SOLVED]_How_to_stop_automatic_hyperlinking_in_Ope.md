### Post by The Question on 2009-01-10
Whenever I type a url ([www.blah.com](www.blah.com)) into a cell, OpenOffice Calc automatically makes a hyperlink for it.  How do I stop this?  What's more, once it does that, I cannot edit the text (the cursor goes either to the beginning or the end of the hyperlink), nor can I delete the hyperlink without deleting the text.

---

### Post by ithanium on 2009-01-10
go to Tools> AutoCorrect> Options tab and un-check the URL recognition setting
