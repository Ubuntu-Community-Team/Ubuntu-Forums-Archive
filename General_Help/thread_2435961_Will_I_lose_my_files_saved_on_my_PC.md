### Post by Artim on 2020-01-29
Your files are in no danger as a general rule, but it's always best practice to preserve them on a separate media drive whenever you upgrade.  

**+! for what Mastablasta has written**.  Stay on 18.04 and don't upgrade until at least April or May to the next _L_ong _T_erm _S_upport version is out.  I actually go all the way until the current LTS reaches end-of-life before I upgrade to the next LTS.
