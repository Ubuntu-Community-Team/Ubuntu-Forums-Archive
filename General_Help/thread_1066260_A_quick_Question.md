### Post by avtolle on 2009-02-10
I presume you are asking that question relative to your planned Ubuntu installation. The answer is that you should try a Live CD session first, before installing, to see if you wireless card is recognized in that session before installing. If it is, hopefully once installation is completed, you will be good to go. If it isn't, there are steps that can be taken post-install to get your wireless up. One thing: do you know which wireless card you have? If so, post it, and someone can take a look and give you some further information and advice.
