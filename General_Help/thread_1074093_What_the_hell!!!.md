### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-02-18
I'm afraid I can't help you with this particular problem, but if this is an example of your previous posts, it's no wonder you haven't had many responses.

First, the title "What the hell!!!" isn't descriptive of your problem.  People scanning the forum for issues with which they can help will likely skip this one without bothering to look at the body of the post.  Something more relevant to your post like "Video problems with ATI card" would get a lot more attention from the people who can help the most.

Second, that title, as well as the general tone of the beginning of your post, is also fairly confrontational.  Everyone here is a fellow user and volunteer.  People volunteering their own time aren't likely to respond well to anger, aggression, or other negative emotions.  None of us here are getting paid any more than you are, and no one here is under contractual obligation to help you.  However, this is a very helpful community, if you treat it as such as ask for help rather than demanding it.

Third, you can help others to help you by making it easier to read your posts.  Notice how I'm separating my text into paragraphs and using something approaching proper grammar and punctuation?  That's because it's easier for you to read.  

Cramming everything into one sentence and one block of text in one continuous run-on sentence without stopping or taking a break makes it hard for people to read or understand and if someone has to struggle to understand your post they're not likely to spend the time trying to decipher it to respond, see what I mean?

I'm sorry I can't be of any help on this particular issue.  While I have a computer with an ATI card running Ubuntu, I've never experienced the issues you describe.  I truly hope that some of the other knowledgeable people on this board will be able to help.  However, this particular post won't win you any fans.  You may want to consider starting another thread with a more descriptive title, a little less anger, and a little more readability.

Good luck.
