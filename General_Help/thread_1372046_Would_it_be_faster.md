### Post by gsmanners on 2010-01-04
7200rpm drive has a max rate of about 70 MB/s

A typical flash drive can get up to about 30 MB/s

SD cards are rated between 2 and 6 MB/s

I'd say it's a trade-off between convenience and speed.
