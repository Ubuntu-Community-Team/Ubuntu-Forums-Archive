### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-05-01
Pidgin, aMSN and emesene can all use the MSN Messenger protocol.  Pidgin supports multiple messaging protocols, such as Yahoo, IRC, and ICQ as well as MSN.  aMSN has full webcam support.  Some people prefer the look of emesene to aMSN and say it's easier to set up.
