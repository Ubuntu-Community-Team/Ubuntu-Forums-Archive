### Post by coffeecat on 2011-04-26
The answer for the Natty version of Banshee.

Open Banshee and right-click on "Amazon MP3 store" in left-pane. Click on "preferences" > Source Specific tab. Under country, change from "Automatic (Geo IP detection)" - which clearly doesn't work - to "United Kingdom (amazon.co.uk)"

What you don't want to do, which I tried first, is to log in to your UK Amazon account in the amazon.com site. It let me login, it recognised me, but told me I had no purchase/order history. I thought that since I have a .co.uk email login address, I would have been redirected to the UK amazon site. But no.
