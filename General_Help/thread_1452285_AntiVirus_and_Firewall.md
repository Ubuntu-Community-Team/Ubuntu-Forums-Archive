### Post by darolu on 2010-04-11
An anti-virus is not really needed unless you share a lot of files (specially if they are downloaded from not secure sites) with windows users and you want to protect them. Out of this scenario I think an antivirus program in Linux is as useful as a cube of ice in a volcano.

Ubuntu comes with -AppArmor-, you can find information about it in this link: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AppArmor](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AppArmor)

Other useful information: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=823741](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=823741)
