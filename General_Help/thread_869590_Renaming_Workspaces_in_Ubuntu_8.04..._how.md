### Post by Yorkshireman on 2008-07-25
I have play with some settings of that panel area and now the two workspaces have named themselves "desk 1" and "desk 1". How can I rename my workspaces in Ubuntu 8.04 (using GNOME v2.22.2)?

---

### Post by sdennie on 2008-07-25
If you have a workspace switcher on the panel, you should be able to right click on it and select preferences.  From there, if you double click on a workspace name, you can change it.

---

### Post by Mister.Vash on 2008-07-25
Go to System -> Preferences -> Appearance
Select the visual effects tab and change it to none

Go down and right click on the workspace switcher and choose Preferences

I've include a screen of what you should see at that point.  You should be able to change the workspace names from there
