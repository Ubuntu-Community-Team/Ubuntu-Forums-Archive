### Post by deadflowr on 2014-10-05

?

Kwin is the window manager for kde.
Unity uses compiz.
Compiz cube can get you to see the top and bottom, but not a full rotate that direction.

Kwin, probably can, but then you'd be running kde(Kubuntu).
(Though, in some cases you can enable kwin to be used in other desktop's I guess; Unity most likely not since unity is directly connected to the compiz window manager..)

But, yes you can enable wobbly windows in kde/kwin.
(Though I find it to be less than stellar compared to wobbly in compiz/unity)
