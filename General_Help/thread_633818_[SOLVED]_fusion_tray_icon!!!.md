### Post by doctorgreg on 2007-12-07
I type fusion-icon in the terminal and it shows up in the tray.  Then as soon as i close the terminal the window borders go away and then come back and the icon is gone.  What is happening?

---

### Post by ray bot on 2007-12-07
Closing a terminal will usually kill any apps running in that terminal.  Try pressing alt+f2 and entering your command in the run dialog instead.
