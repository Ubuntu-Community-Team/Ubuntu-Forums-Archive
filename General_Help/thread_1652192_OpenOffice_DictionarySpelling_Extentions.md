### Post by germanix on 2010-12-24
Go to synaptic package manager. In the quick search window enter "spell checker". From the resulting list you can choose to add various "spell checkers" including Thesaurus and hyphenation packages. There are spell checkers like.
aspell
myspell
hunspell
that you can add to your leisure
hope this helps
