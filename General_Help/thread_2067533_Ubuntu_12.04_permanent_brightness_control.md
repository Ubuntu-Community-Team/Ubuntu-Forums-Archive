### Post by essex lad on 2012-10-07
Anyone who has a problem saving a suitable brightness level could download "XBACKLIGHT"

Go to Startup Applications and add "xbacklight -set 50" 
(without the Quotes) 

the Number seems to be a percentage of reduction so entering 50 would give a reduction of 50%

It works for me

Good Luck
