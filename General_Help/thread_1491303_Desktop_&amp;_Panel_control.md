### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-05-23
To be able to move them right click on them and clear the lock to panel box then right click and select Move and drag them to where you want them.
For the notification applets you have to click just to the left of the icons to get that menu.

You may have to unlock others that are in the way to move some around.
