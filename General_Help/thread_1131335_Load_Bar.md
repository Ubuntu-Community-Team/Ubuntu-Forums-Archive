### Post by Ticketoride on 2009-04-20
I guess you were referring to the Desktop Panel along the top after a fresh Install.

Right-Click in an Area of free Space on the Panel, hit ** <Properties> ** --> ** <Background> ** Tab, click on the "Color" Field and adjust.

Works the same Way for any other Panels you create on the Bottom and Sides too.

You can also change Desktop Themes: **System ---> Preferences ---> Appearance**
