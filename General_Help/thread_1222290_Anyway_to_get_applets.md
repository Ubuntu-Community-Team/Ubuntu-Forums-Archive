### Post by jmszr on 2009-07-24
Codix121,

Right click on your panel > Click on Add to Panel > Drag and Drop onto your panel or click on the applet and then on Add. To get more applets open up Synaptic and search for ???? applet (example: resolution applet) I would suggest installing  "gnome-control-center" (if it's not default) as it has several gnome configuration applets.
