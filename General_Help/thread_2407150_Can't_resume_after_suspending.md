### Post by ricardo-pedri on 2018-11-30
It goes as this:

1. Suspend current session by closing the lid or pressing the Suspend button
2. I open the lid and move the mouse / press the keyboard
3. It takes about 20s for the login screen to show up
4. After entering the password, I get a black screen 

Ps: the only workaround so far is to press Ctrl+Alt+F1, then #killall -9 Xorg
After killing Xorg, I am able to login again.
