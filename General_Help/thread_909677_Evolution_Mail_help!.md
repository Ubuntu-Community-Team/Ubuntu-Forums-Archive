### Post by charonred on 2008-09-03
Personally I find Evolution a bit 'clunky' and not as configurable as MS Outlook, but it is fairly secure. 

It only asks for the password the first time you open it in a Ubuntu session; if you shut down your OS, in your next session it will ask you again.

For a straight forward email client 'Thunderbird' may suit you better, or even 'Spicebird' (a derivative beta with some Outlook like features).
