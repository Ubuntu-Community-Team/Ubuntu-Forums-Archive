### Post by shanee753 on 2009-10-15
I like playing games in fullscreen, but ever since I moved to Ubuntu games often close the moment they go into fullscreen.
Also as games don't always have the option of fullscreen I was wondering if anyone knows if there I a way I can do "fake" fullscreen, when its just resized to fit the whole screen.

At the moment I'm zooming into a window until it fills the screen then zooming out again after but this seems like a terrible solution. And I wanted to know if any of you knowed a better way.

You could also call this **Force-rescale.**

Anyway, any help would be appreciated.

---

### Post by YSH on 2009-10-15
Go to System > Preferences > Shortcuts and change 'Toggle fullscreen mode', this will make the window fullscreen, but not using it's native method. For example, in Firefox using this to go fullscreen won't hide the toolbars as Firefox' normal fullscreen would. This might work for you as well.
