### Post by Alex1200GS on 2007-09-30
I do have the 750 and I now believe I should have bought the 680 in the first place. 

750 runs Windows and making it sync with linux can be a _real_ nightmare.
680 runs PalmOS and works out-of-the-box with Ubuntu.

Apart from the Treo syncing issue, Thunderbird and Evolution are two different programs. Thunderbird handles only email whereas Evo also handles contacts, calendars, tasks etc. It all depends on your own needs then: if all you need is email, then both applications are good for you. If you also need contacts and appointments, Evo is the way to go.
