### Post by Hobbsee on 2005-10-02
1. You can either use 
> sudo apt-get remove programnamehere 
for the ones installed via apt get, or you can use synaptic.  Remember that you can hit tab to get the full program name.  I think the  .debs can be uninstalled that way as well, but there may be other ways you have to remove them.

As for nautilus, I recall it has at least 2 different modes, one of which you will probably prefer to the other.  Cant tell you how to switch them, as i'm using kde - sorry about that!
