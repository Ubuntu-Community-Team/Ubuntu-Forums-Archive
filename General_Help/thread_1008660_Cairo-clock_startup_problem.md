### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-12-12
Hi there.
Right-click on the clock, and choose Properties.
At the bottom you'll see Animation Smoothness.
Drag it all the way to rough.
Close, quit, and open again. Hopefully your problem will have been solved.
Remember, if your problem is solved, to mark it so, under [COLOR="Red"]Thread Tools[/COLOR], at the top of this page.
Good luck.
