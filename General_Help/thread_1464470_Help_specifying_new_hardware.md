### Post by cascade9 on 2010-04-28
I wouldhave thought "hardware and laptops" myself, but hardware questions seem to end up all over the place here, I dont know why....

Anyway- what sort of budget? I'd get as much RAM as you can afford. Probably better to have a lot of RAM and a not-so-amazing CPU than a great CPU and not as much RAM. 

CPU hint? Unless you have enough money to buy a 'real' server (ie a Intel xeon or 2x xeon system, ECC RAM, etc or the same with AMD opterons) then I'd probably get one of the new 6-core Phenom II CPUs. Lots of cores, good perfromance and cheaper than all the i7s.
