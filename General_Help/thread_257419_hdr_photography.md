### Post by Rexbron! on 2006-09-14
In order to do hdr photography (AFAIK) you will need atleast 2 photographs that are exactly the same, but taken with different exposures. I have done this in photoshop, but not in GIMP.

### Post by Senshi on 2006-09-14
Yes you need at least two pictures of the "exact" same thing with only the exposures different.

Download the script from that site, and drop it into your script folder.

/usr/share/gimp/2.0/scripts/
