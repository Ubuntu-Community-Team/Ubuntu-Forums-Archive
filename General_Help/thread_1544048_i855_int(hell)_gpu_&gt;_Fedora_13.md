### Post by nomnex on 2010-08-02
I own an old CF-W2 Panasonic let's note with a i855 int(h)el(l) gpu.

10.04 LTS is a now go. "i915.modeset=1" or any other options did not help: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Bugs/Lucidi8xxFreezes](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Bugs/Lucidi8xxFreezes).

This might not be news for some, but I have installed Fedora 13 for a test drive. Kernel  2.6.33.3. That worked right out of the box, and installed fine on my machine.

Among the good things on the fedora side: there is no mono, unmask 002 (022 is old Debian tradition), excellent documentation, and the distro is very polished. Unfortunately the package choice is reduced compare to Debian/Ubuntu.

So anyway, I finally have a working distribution for my day job. If you have trouble using the latest 10.04 on a i855 machine, and can't wait for 10.10, Fedora 13 might worth a try.
