### Post by ean5533 on 2010-12-14
Depends on which one you want. Alpha 1 was a milestone released on December 2nd. The "current" release includes all code changes since the last checkin (which happens on Thursdays). So, the "current" is going to have about a week's worth of code patches added onto it. That doesn't necessarily mean it's better or worse. It just means that there is another week's worth of patches in it. Your call. Neither one of them will be stable.

Of course, if you're looking for stable, you should be looking at 10.10 anyway.
