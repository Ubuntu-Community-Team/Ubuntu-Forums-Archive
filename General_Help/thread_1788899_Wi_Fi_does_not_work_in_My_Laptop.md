### Post by howefield on 2011-06-23
Are you able to make a wired connection to the internet ?

If so, try looking for additional drivers in System > Administration > Additional Drivers. You might need to update your package lists first if you haven't already, open a terminal (Applications > Accessories > Terminal) and type

```
sudo apt-get update
```

and press enter.
