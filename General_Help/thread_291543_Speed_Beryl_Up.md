### Post by d3v1ant_0n3 on 2006-11-02
On my system,I have it running smoothly with these tweaks:
 Turning off BlurFx and Water (it doesn't work for me anyway, but still seems to slow things down). 
Turning Trail Focus 'Maximum Number of windows to keep track of' down to 4. General Options>Choices, unchecking 'Detect Refresh Rate' and 'Vsync to Blank'.
General Options>Numeric Values, turning 'Refresh Rate' up to 200.
