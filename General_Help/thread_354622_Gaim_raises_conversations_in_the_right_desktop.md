### Post by tocleora on 2007-02-06
What I do is just leave the window open on the previous chat, or I've added my own name to my friends list (so I can see when I *am* truly invisible or not) and then I'll double click my own name to bring the chat window up.  Then all chats will go into that same window.  Just leave it open on that specific desktop and it will stay there.
