### Post by The Avatar of Time on 2008-02-25
Hello, I have what I think is a very simple question about using gimp. I am making a collage of images. I open an image and paste it to the file I am making my collage in. Okay but once I move it and click of it or paste in another image I cant move the old one anymore. Is there a way to move images that have been anchored (is that the term?)? That's it, thanks for any help.

---

### Post by fragos on 2008-02-25
The trick is to make a new transparent layer and paste into it.  Make another layer and past the next image into it.  You'll be able to move any in these layers.  The background image and all the layers are displayed as if a single image.  You just need to select the layer you want to move in.  before saving you will need to merge the layers.  I had no end of trouble until I understood layers.
