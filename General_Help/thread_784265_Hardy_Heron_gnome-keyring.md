### Post by chrisccoulson on 2008-05-06
The old keyring manager has been replaced by Seahorse. You can view your collected passwords and encryption keys from Applications -> Accessories -> Passwords and Encryption Keys.

You can also change your keyring passwords and other settings related to PGP keys from System -> Preferences -> Encryption and Keyrings
