If you don't like upgrading,you should stick with the "Long Term Support"(LTS) versions.10.04 is an LTS version.

To upgrade from 9.04 you have to upgrade to 9.10 -> 10.04 -> 10.10.

> Can I download a copy of 10.10 and try to install it or do I need to do a fresh install.

There is no difference between the two.You would have to do a fresh install.

Go [Here](http://releases.ubuntu.com/) and download the version you require (by torrent if possible) and check the MD5SUM before you burn to CD as slow as possible.Boot the CD and run the "CD check" in the menu just to make sure the CD is good.

Back up your data and install.

I would recommend the Lucid version.


Good Luck
