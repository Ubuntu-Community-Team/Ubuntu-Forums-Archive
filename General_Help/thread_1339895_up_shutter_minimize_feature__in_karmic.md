### Post by jamesisin on 2009-11-28
It's off by default.  You probably turned it on for your previous system early and forgot you did it.  (I did.)

It's easy to turn that effect on:

System --> Preferences --> Windows

Double-click titlebar to perform this action: [change to Roll up]
