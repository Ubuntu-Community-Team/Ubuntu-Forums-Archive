### Post by gr8 on 2015-05-06
Under the Compiz's "Ubuntu Unity Plugin" you have to uncheck "Show live preview of windows in the switcher". That pretty much solves the problem.

Unchecking also creates a problem in that your mouse cursor position will occasionally be off. If you hover around the launcher and go back and forth selecting apps on the launcher the problem will usually go away.

There's bugs in for these items. Hopefully they'll be solved soon.
