### Post by TheFu on 2017-09-24
I had a similar issue - not with an HP laptop.  Basically all the RAM and swap was being used so the system crashed.  I traced back the issue to 10 firefox addons/plugins.  By removing all the extra ones I'd installed over the years, the memory used dropped 50%.  The largest abusers were the video plugins - flash and cicso's mp4 thing.

Firefox is a hog.
