### Post by Coreigh on 2008-11-12
Yes. Open Nautilus, right-click on the folder in question, choose Properties. On the "Basic" tab you will see the icon picture assigned to that folder, click on the icon picture, this will give you a browse dialog to locate a different picture or icon to assign to the folder.
