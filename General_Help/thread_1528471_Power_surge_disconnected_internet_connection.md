### Post by cj.surrusco on 2010-07-10
First of all, welcome to the forums.

You would want to check your modem and router first, before looking at the computer end. Check that activity lights are active for things such as 'online' on the modem to ensure that it is connected to the ISP. Also, check the router to see if it shows connectivity to the modem.
