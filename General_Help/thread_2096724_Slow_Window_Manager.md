### Post by Cheesehead on 2012-12-20
Well, Thunar opens immediately on my old, slow equipment.
"It is slow" could have a large variety of possible causes.

Is anything else really slow?
Do you have a slow processor?
Are you running a compositor like Compiz?
Does your hard drive show any signs of imminent failure? Is it by chance formatted NTFS?
Do you have a lot of messages in /var/log/dmesg indicating a kernel or hardware problem?
Are you running anythine else at the time Thunar is especially slow?

Thunar is a File Manager, not a Window Manager. Did you actually mean your Window Manager?
