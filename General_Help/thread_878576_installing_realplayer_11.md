### Post by mb_webguy on 2008-08-03
While MPlayer can play Real media files, it's hit-or-miss when it comes to rmvb files.  The few occasions I've needed to play rmvb files, MPlayer would either play the audio but no video, or else play the audio horribly out of sync with the video.

RealPlayer is a terrible media player, and I try to avoid it if possible (and use VLC as my primary multimedia player), but it plays Real video files -- specifically rmvb files -- more reliably than other media players.
