Yes, you need one of three things.

1) A Cross-Over Cable
2) A Switch (or Hub) and two Patch Cables
3) A Patch Cable and at least one Auto Sensing NIC


I suggest using SSH.

### Post by SentientFluid on 2009-04-30
Just an FYI.... A lot of NICs, switches, and hubs automatically apply an internal crossover when needed.  Gigabit ethernet devices are supposed to have this buit-in and a lot of 10/100 devices did as well.  Rather than going out and buying a crossover cable, I'd suggest testing with a regular ethernet cable first and possibly save yoruself a couple of bucks. :)
