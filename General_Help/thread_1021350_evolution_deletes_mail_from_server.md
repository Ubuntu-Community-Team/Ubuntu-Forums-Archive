### Post by namdung on 2008-12-25
> **dkobel said:**
> evolution mail will delete my emails on my server. how do i make it so my email is not deleted from my server, and evolution simply checks my email?

In Evolution, open *Edit-->Preferences*

Select mail account and click *Edit*.

In the *Receiving Options* tab, chk *Leave messages on Server*.
