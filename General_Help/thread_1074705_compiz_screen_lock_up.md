### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-02-19
I found a fix.  I tried many combinations of keyboard keys, shift, alt, etc. with the mouse buttons, and stubbled on one that let me get to the system menu. Then I disabled the fire plug in.  I would suggest that when trying out features in compiz that you write down what combination of key and mouse buttons activate and deactivate the features in case there is trouble or a lock out.
