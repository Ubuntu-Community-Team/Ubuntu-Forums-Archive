### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2007-02-05
6.06 LTS: long term support release, will be supported for 5years on servers, made as solid as possible.

6.10: newer release, newer software versions, 18 months support, could be less solid on some setups but is absolutely stable on most setups.

I advise you to try 6.10 first.
