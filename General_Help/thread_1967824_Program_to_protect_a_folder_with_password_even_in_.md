### Post by papibe on 2012-04-28
Hi xp15.

You need some sort of encryption.

If you don't access your data very often I see a couple of options:
[LIST]
[*]pack your data using tar and encrypt it using bcrypt, or
[*]pack your data with a password-protected app like rar or 7zip.
[/LIST]
On the other hand, if you need constant access to your data, you may need to use an encrypted folder. Take a look at this [tutorial]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FolderEncryption").

Hope that helps, and tell us how it goes.
