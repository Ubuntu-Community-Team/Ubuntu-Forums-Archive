This is to everyone who is looking for cybercafe software solution for Linux Ubuntu. Well I was searching for one myself for a long time and made use a project called Outkafe, which really sucks, let me tell you, dont even look down that road unless you got programming experience and you got good understanding of the Linux operating system.
