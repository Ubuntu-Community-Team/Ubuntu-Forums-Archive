### Post by jombeewoof on 2008-07-22
For compiz to work you need both a window manager and a window decorator.

Compiz is going to be the manager.
I prefer Emerald as the decorator. You can use kde decorator, or gtk decorator. 
Older versions had aquamarine and I think heliodor as options. Not too sure. 
I'm a big fan of emerald. 


If you have fusion-icon installed, right click and choose the manager and decorator of your choice that is installed. 
If you don't have fusion-icon, get it. (sudo apt-get install fusion-icon)

Other trippy background is called a skydome. Look around in the rotate cube options for that. 
Also the "fireworks" is under animations. It's called "burn".
