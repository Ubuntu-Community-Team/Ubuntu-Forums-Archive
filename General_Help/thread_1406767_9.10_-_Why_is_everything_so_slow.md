### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-02-14
> **Crossbow said:**
> By "slower" I mean everything. Applications are slower to open, the browser is slower (any browser), and sometimes when I'm typing it locks up for a few seconds and then races to catch up with me.

You need to [optimize Firefox databases](http://lovinglinux.megabyet.net/?page_id=220#Database--Optimization-1).

If you can't find a solution to overall slowness, then I would recommend creating a new user and login into Ubuntu with that new account. If it works well, then you probably has some configuration settings that are not playing well after the upgrade.

For example, I upgraded KDE 4.4 two days ago and started to experience window decorator crashes and other problems. When I deleted kde configuration files and rebooted, the problems were gone.
