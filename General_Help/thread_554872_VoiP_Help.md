### Post by raptor2552 on 2007-09-19
Hello

I like Vonage, but you will need a high speed cable connection. It's easy to wire to your homes interface and can be used by all telephone outlets, and comes with instructions. The only equipment you need to decide on is the VOIP adapter, I chose the Dlink adapter.

You of course have access to the wired phone network and 911 services (check this out before ordering as 911 works differently). Vonage comes with all sorts of services, voice mail and the like. What pretty cool is you can select the call quality through your account setup, I have a 10Mb connection so have it set to high quality.

Check out what Vonage has to offer at [www.vonage.com](www.vonage.com)
