### Post by Sirman on 2011-01-27
Re: Ubuntu 10.10, when I Open My_Data_Disk to see the CD contents, I get the SAME contents of the FIRST CD/DVD I opened; that is, the contents do NOT change when I insert other CDs, even after clicking Reload, etc.  Help me out with this.  Thanks, Sirman

---

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-01-27
What are you doing between the first and second disks? Are you manually opening the drive, removing the disk, inserting the second, and then closing it?

IF so, it sound like your system is not detecting that the disks have changed.

Try "ejecting" the disk using the desktop icon.  That should clear out any disk-related cache of info, forcing it to reread the disk when you insert the second one.
