### Post by zanglang on 2007-07-17
You mentioned Evolution does nothing when you click submit - Looking at the HTML source in the other thread, I'm guessing that Evolution might be intentionally blocking the ability to submit forms for security purposes.

This may sound counter-productive, but you could try copying out the chunk of HTML from the email, create an empty .html file, paste it in, open that up, and then post it from there. Your 'email' would most likely be sent by [email]server@domain.com[/email], so you probably don't need an email program to do it.
