### Post by David006 on 2011-03-10
I have just installed Ubuntu 10.04 LTS on a (new) HP 4000 Pro, which also has the Intel Gigabit Ethernet 82567V chipset.

It could not recognize the Ethernet chipset (no network, unclaimed), until I downloaded (on another PC) the latest Intel Linux driver (v1.10.3a 02/28/2011). 

see:
[http://downloadcenter.intel.com/Detail_Desc.aspx?agr=Y&DwnldID=15817&ProdId=3003&lang=eng](http://downloadcenter.intel.com/Detail_Desc.aspx?agr=Y&DwnldID=15817&ProdId=3003&lang=eng)


check ReadMe or ReleaseNotes for your exact issue (with WiFi ?).
