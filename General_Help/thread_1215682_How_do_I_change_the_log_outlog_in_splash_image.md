### Post by VCoolio on 2009-07-17
That's the gdm-theme you're talking about I think. Go to system > preferences > login window and choose what you want; you can find gdm themes on gnome-look also, and you can easily modify them by extracting and putting an image of your own in them. They go to /usr/share/gdm/themes.
