### Post by ajgreeny on 2019-01-29
Look for the file in your thunderbird config folder, mine is **~/.thunderbird/*cauxhqch*.default/calendar-data** but yours will have a different alphanumeric name, of course.
With TB totally shut down, rename it as a backup, then restart TB and I think you'll find that the calendar data has gone and you can start again importing whatever you need.
