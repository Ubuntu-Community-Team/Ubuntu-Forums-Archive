### Post by Plasma_NZ on 2008-04-27
Ok i just wanna clear something up here about refresh rates on LCD's etc..


LCD's dont use refresh rates as-in 50hz - 60hz....
LCD's use response times e.g. 2ms - 4ms - 6ms etc...


CRT's are the only screens that use refresh rates if u follow... 
(horrible old technology of the cathode ray tube coupled with the electron-gun)


[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lcd_Response_Time#LCD_monitors](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lcd_Response_Time#LCD_monitors)
