### Post by donato roque on 2009-08-11
Sound Converter is a very useful application.  As long as you have the mp3 codec it can convert to and from mp3's.  First make sure that you download ubuntu-restricted-extras from the ubuntu repositories.  Also use its preferences tab to configure the application.  

Ubuntu-restricted-extras also enable you to play flac and aac.  It's a bit of a big download though, ok?

Once you downloaded the mp3 codec you can listen to mp3's in your system without converting them.  I convert them myself if i'm copying them to my mp3 player. 

Good luck.
