### Post by alvinrr on 2019-12-16
I cannot switch (Alt + Tab) to the other Google Chrome window when I'm into the other Google Chrome.

Why is there only one Google Chrome when I press Alt Tab and not two if I have two Google Chrome open? That goes as well to my Libre Office programs.

---

### Post by vanadium on 2019-12-16
Try Alt+` (key above tab) to switch between windows of the same application. Indicate the version of Ubuntu you are using. It is possible to change to a more traditional switcher that switches between individual open windows.
