For some reason I found that Windows installs don't like you installing in a new partition which had space that was used from an old Windows partition.

### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2009-04-01
The Dell Inspiron 1420 keyboard is sensitive even without coffee. The keyboard may not have been damaged, as the electronics are protected by rubber boots. The keycaps can be removed although you may not thinks so. It scared me the first time I did it because they didn't come off very easily. There are two rectangular arms under the keycap. Make sure they are clean, and the hinges on the back of the keycap are clean. Then snap the keycap pack on. The arms should just snap back into the hinges on the back of the keycap.

I don't have any help for your other problems.
