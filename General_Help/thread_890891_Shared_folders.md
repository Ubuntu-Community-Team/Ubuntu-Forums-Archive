> **timboellis said:**
> How do I share the folders on Ubuntu , with the point of sharing Ubuntu to Ubuntu and Ubuntu to Windows

Right click on the folder you want to share and select **Sharing options**.The system will then install samba.You will get this error [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=772706](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=772706) but you just need to log out and back in to gain the correct permissions.You will now be able to share the folder.

Good Luck
