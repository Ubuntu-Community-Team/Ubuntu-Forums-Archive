### Post by Monotoko on 2009-04-24
Odd, have you tried clicking the thing in the taskbar thats trying to connect, then chose your own connection?

Also, have you tried deleting your connection (Right click the wireless icon, click Edit Connections, go to the wireless tab and delete the home connection) and then disable your wireless (Right click, uncheck the wireless box)

Re-enable your wireless, and try to connect again, inputting the right settings etc
