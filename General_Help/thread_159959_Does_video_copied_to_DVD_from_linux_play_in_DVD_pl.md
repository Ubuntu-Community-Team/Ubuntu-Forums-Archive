### Post by Monster_user on 2006-04-14
Check out the Ubuntu Support pages.
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UserDocumentation](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UserDocumentation)

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DVDAuthoring](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DVDAuthoring)
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats)

For DVDs, open Synaptic, and do a Search for '**dvdauthor**' and '**toolame**'. Mark them both for installation. You may also want to search for "Q DVD Author" (**qdvdauthor**), and mark it as well. Then click apply.

Q DVD Author is for KDE/Kubuntu, but will work on Ubuntu. 

"DVD Styler" is a decent alternative,...
[http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=92301](http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=92301)
