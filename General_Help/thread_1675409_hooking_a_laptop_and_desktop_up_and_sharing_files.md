### Post by DanneStrat on 2011-01-25
The easiest way I know would be to set up dropbox or ubuntu one.

They are very similar in the way they work.

Since ubuntu one is included with ubuntu you

can start by having a look at it:

[https://one.ubuntu.com/](https://one.ubuntu.com/)

How to configure:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/Tutorials/Setup](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/Tutorials/Setup)

How to share files:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/Tutorials/FileSharing](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOne/Tutorials/FileSharing)

In short you create an account. Then you connect your

computers to sync files between. When you drop

files in your ubuntu one folder it will automatically sync

to your other computers ubuntu one folders.
