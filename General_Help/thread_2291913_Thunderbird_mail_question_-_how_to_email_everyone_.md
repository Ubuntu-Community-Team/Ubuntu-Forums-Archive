### Post by amanchesterman on 2015-08-24
If you have the address book open and left click on the first entry it will highlight it. Then hold down the SHIFT key and left click on the last entry and they will all be highlighted. Then right click on the last entry and you will see the option 'WRITE'. Choose that and it will open an email composition screen with all the highlighted addresses entered for you.

However I believe it's not good etiquette to send a mass email like that because it discloses every recipient's address to all the others, which increases the possibility of spam messages. It's better (I believe) to create a mailing list as guided above and then to send your message as a blind copy to all the list members: that way they don't know who else you have contacted.

You may wish to search on the internet for advice about approved etiquette in this matter.
