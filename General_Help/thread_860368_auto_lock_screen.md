### Post by NilsE on 2008-07-15
You could take a screenshot of the screensaver lock screen and put it in your pictures folder and use picture folder option as your screensaver.  When you move the mouse the real one will come up with the cursor in the password field.

You could also make copies of it with the image in different spots on the screen and it will appear to move just like a real screensaver.
