No problem there, they work out-of-the-box, and are seen as 2 processors.
> 
6.)Ive also wondered if it is extremely difficult to make your own icon theme? just i like lots of bits of themes but haven't found one i like for everything, is there any guide anywhere that fully explains it, same for GDM?? and lastly is there anyway of changing the orange that you get from jus general stuff on ubuntu, such as when u hover over a menu or down on the multiple desktop wheres its a kind of grey yello with orangey brown for active desktop
Icon themes are not hard, but they take a lot of work. Best way would be to pick one existing theme and start replacing the image files with your own.

Same goes for GDM, but lot less work depending on what you want to do.

For the orange color, just get a theme you like from [http://www.gnome-look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org"). Of course there are some other themes already installed by default, and the also you can change colors of Clearlooks & Glossy themes easily using the Theme manager.
