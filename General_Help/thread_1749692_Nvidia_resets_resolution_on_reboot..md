### Post by bem9q on 2012-05-17
I had the same problem and tried various solutions to no avail.
11.10 oneiric, Nvidia 440 running the latest drivers - 295.

no matter what I did to save the nvidia settings or xorg.conf file, it just wouldn't take on the reboot.

finally, i found this setting

cd ~/.config
gedit monitors.xml

i deleted all the xml groups except "default" - probably they'd been put there during previous drivers or setups.  Maybe in my case, i think i tried the VGA cable and the DVI cable at some point (i now use hdmi).  However, the "default" was set to, surprise surprise, the resolution it kept booting to.  

i deleted the other groups, and set the resolution to the desired 10920x1080 in "default", and everything worked fine - at last!
