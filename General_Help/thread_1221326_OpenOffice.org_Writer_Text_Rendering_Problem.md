### Post by pkloves on 2009-07-28
Yes, it's Compiz.  Which is really disappointing.  I would assume that it's also related to the video driver?

Just click System | Preferences | Appearance, then the Visual Effects tab.  I set mine to "Normal", and the problems went away.
