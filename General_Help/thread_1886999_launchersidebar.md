### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-11-26
If bandwidth is that limited, using Xubuntu or Lubuntu may not be available to you.

I suggest you simply auto-hide the launcher as a work-around. It's not exactly what you asked for, but it will be close.

You will need to install CompizConfig Settings Manager (ccsm) if it's not already installed. Start ccsm, press Desktop (on the left) > Ubuntu Unity Plugin > Hide Launcher > Autohide. It will hide the launcher except when you move your mouse to the very left of the screen or when you open your Dash.
