### Post by fooman on 2009-04-10
is this kubuntu kde4 ? 

i am sure there are better ways, but...when i mucked up my kde4 and lost the panel (taskbar),  i got it back by just deleting the "hidden" .kde folder in my home directory.  the problem with that is it will reset all of your kde settings to the default settings.  if you do not have a problem with that...open up your home folder and in the toolbar click on "view".  then in the drop-down menu click "show hidden files".  then scroll down,  find the .kde folder,  right-click on it and choose "move to trash". 

log-out and back in again and see if the panel is back.

hope that helps.
