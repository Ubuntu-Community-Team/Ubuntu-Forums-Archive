### Post by john183 on 2008-11-17
Buying the ISO isn't really an option. Most people will not "sell" ISO's as sharing them at all is illegal. Asking for money for them just compounds the illegality. The only way to "legally" get an ISO is it buy the game yourself and create the ISO from that. Be careful though because in many places that is illegal also. (If you are in the US you can thank the DMCA for that.)
