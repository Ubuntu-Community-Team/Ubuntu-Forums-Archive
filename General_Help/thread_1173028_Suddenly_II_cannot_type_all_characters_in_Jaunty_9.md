### Post by paparozoumis on 2009-05-29
OK.. Got it right... 

I will explain what was wrong with my system, though I don't know how it happened as I don't recall tinkering with these adjustments at all .. 

Anyway.. there were a couple of things happening on the laptop

First, for some unknown reason on Preferences/Keyboard/Mouse Keys the "Pointer can be controlled usinf the keypad" was checked. This was giving the move to the mouse pointer on the screen while pressing specifinc keys. I unchecked it. 

Second, probably by mistake, the combination Ctrl-Alt-Num was activated and this made the keystrokes to results in numbers. So, "U" gave me 4, "O" gave me 5 etc.,  I reversed the combination. 

The thing was that all those adjustments were stored and coming back after every restart, instead of resetting. 

Anyway.. now it works and I hope that maybe this mistake can be of help for someone who faces tha same probs...
