### Post by JDArnott on 2010-09-25
Greetings

That was exactly it, in:
Edit -> Preferences, on the Advanced tab, under the Settings button, I had the "Automatic proxy configuration url" radio button selected from when I was connecting through my work wireless...

Changed it to "No Proxy" and I've not had the popup message again.

Thankyou!
