### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2007-02-10
Oh my, a turbo button! I envy you...I had one in my first computer, that button was so cool, although it didn't actually speed things up, it slowed the machine down... The machine was a 8088 clone running at a whopping 8MHz. The turbo button was used to lower the clock frequency to 4.77 MHZ (which was the speed of the original IBM PC processor) so games wouldn't be blindingly fast.

Incidentally that was the last time in my computer use history when I had a State of the Art machine. After that I've always been at least a generation or two behind...
