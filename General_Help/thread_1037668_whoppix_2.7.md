First of all to burn an iso I would suggest either Brasero or K3b - select 'Burn Image' or 'Burn CD Image' from the opening screen.

Second, Whoppix is very outdated (3½ years) - [BackTrack]("http://www.remote-exploit.org/backtrack.html") would be the better option.

Third, using these tools (Whoppix or BackTrack) is relatively advanced stuff.  I don't mean to be condescending, but if you didn't know how to burn a disc image, are you sure you're savvy enough to use them?

Finally, if you do go ahead and use one of the two, be sure that you own or are permitted to access any networks you may be testing.  Cracking is something that can get you in a great deal of trouble, and is something that wouldn't be condoned here or by the guys behind BackTrack and Whoppix.
