### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-02-10
You didn't say how far it gets before it hangs, so it's hard to provide specific advice.

Anyway ... next time you boot, hold down the SHIFT key and see if it boots far enough to get you into a GRUB menu.

IF it does, select the top-most Recovery Mode option and proceed from there.

If it doesn't even get to a GRUB menu, that, and the slowing down, imply hard drive problems.  So, if you can, then try booting from an Ubuntu LiveCD -- to allow you to look at your hard drive.
