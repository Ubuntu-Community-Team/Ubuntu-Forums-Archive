### Post by dragonfly41 on 2019-10-01
I am sure that you will find [these tools]("http://www.laurenceanthony.net/software.html") to be of value.
Consider that each book to be analysed is a corpus.
AntConc is useful in looking through corpus concordance.
Word frequency list will highlight spelling errors.

And research [Scrivener site]("https://www.literatureandlatte.com/forum/viewtopic.php?t=50875") for tips. I installed Scrivener Linux beta some time back but my use has lapsed.
