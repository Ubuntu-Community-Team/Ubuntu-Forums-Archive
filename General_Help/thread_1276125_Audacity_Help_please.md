### Post by StuartN on 2009-09-26
> **squrl said:**
> My question is what am I missing that I can't get a waveform in the recording or when the file is reloaded for editing.

If you can hear the recording and if you see a horizontal blue line where the waveform is supposed to be, then perhaps the scaling is wrong. Click on the waveform to zoom in (expand horizontally) and right-click to zoom out in time. Click or right-click on the volume scale to zoom in (expand vertically) or out of the wave height.

If you have one recording open and select View->Fit Vertically then it will fill the window.
