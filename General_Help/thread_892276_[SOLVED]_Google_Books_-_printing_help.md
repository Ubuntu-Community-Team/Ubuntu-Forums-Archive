### Post by Vivaldi Gloria on 2008-08-17
1. Clear firefox cache.

2. Open the book & pages you want.

3. Open ff cache and find the pages. Copy pages to your desktop. Open them and print them.

ff cache is here:

```
~/.mozilla/firefox/*.default/Cache
```
