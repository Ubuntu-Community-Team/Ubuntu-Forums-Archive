### Post by macabrem on 2009-10-03
I've been asking myself this same question lately.

A couple of things that I do to help me learn linux faster is:

1) Challenge myself - I try to research the command line way of doing typical GUI tasks. I try to learn something new in the CLI everyday.

2) I read these forums often and subscribe to threads that may teach me something even if it isn't relevant to me right now.  If somebody posts a thread about a problem that I theoretically could have in the future, I learn how to solve it now by subscribing to that thread.
