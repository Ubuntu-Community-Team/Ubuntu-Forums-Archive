### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2006-09-10
Add new printer, you can do that with the print dialog of the admin menu.
Then choose a network printer and fill in the URI, I think this is ip-address/printername.
So if your pc is e.g. 192.168.205.100 and your printer is configured as Printer, your URI should be 192.168.205.100/Printer.
Then choose the correct driver and stuff.
