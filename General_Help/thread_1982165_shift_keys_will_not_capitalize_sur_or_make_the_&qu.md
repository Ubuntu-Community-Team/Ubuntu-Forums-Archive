### Post by Kenjitamura on 2012-12-10
Sorry to necrobump, but this post actually helped me out.  I was writing and I noticed shift + d would not output a D.  Then I tested shift with E/C and neither of those worked either.  Well, I decided to test right shift which resulted in E/C working but not D.  When I saw your post I noticed your header stated your & didn't work so I tested my # and $ and neither of those worked either.  I read the part about the insert key being stuck and checked my insert key, wasn't pressed down.  I then, finally, looked all over the keyboard and my F4 key was stuck.  Pulled it back out and now it all works again.
