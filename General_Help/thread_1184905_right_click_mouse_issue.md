[FONT="Courier New"]I had the same problem.  I read a solution somewhere here, but do not remember where.  It is so easy, however, you don't have to refer elsewhere.  When you hit the right mouse button, don't just tap it and go grabbing for the selection on the context menu.  Just hold the right mouse button down an extra quarter or half second, and the problem will disappear.

I guess some of us are just too fast with the mouse.  Since I started doing that, it's become a habit that I don't even notice, and my problems disappeared.[/FONT]

### Post by chefev on 2009-08-19
I had this right-click/no desktop icons problem for a while too.  For me the solution was maddeningly simple.  
Somehow it seems that Nautilus got removed from the Startup Applications list.  Re-add it.  
Done.  
Now the right-click menu works and the desktop icons show up again.
