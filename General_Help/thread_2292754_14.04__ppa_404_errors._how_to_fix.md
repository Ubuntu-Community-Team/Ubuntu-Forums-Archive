### Post by QIII on 2015-08-30
Those are not errors, they are informational warnings.  They indicate that the requested resource does not exist on the server.  Perhaps they have been moved or discontinued.

You should be able to update your machine, but any software you installed from those PPAs will not be updated.

You could remove them from your sources if the warnings bother you.

In any case, it might be better to contact the PPA maintainer(s) to ask what is going on.  PPAs are not supported by Canonical.
