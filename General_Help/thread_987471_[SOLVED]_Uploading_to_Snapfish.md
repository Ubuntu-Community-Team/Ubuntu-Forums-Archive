### Post by trksh22 on 2008-12-31
OK, so I haven't given up on backing up our photo's to Snapfish. Today I came across [this blog]("http://tompurl.wordpress.com/2008/12/15/bulk-uploading-images-to-snapfish-on-linux/") that showed me how to get all of my photo's to Snapfish.

You basically get Picasa 3 [here]("http://picasa.google.com/linux/"). 

Select the photo's you want to upload. 
Click on "**shop**". 
Scroll down to "Snapfish". 
Enter your log in info and viola! You are done! Repeat as needed :)

Thanks to the owner of the blog for publishing this. Might be common sense to some, but might be able to help someone else out...
