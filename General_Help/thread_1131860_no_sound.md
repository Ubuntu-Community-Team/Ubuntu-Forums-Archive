### Post by bumanie on 2009-04-21
If there was a kernel upgrade that coincided with the sound issue, try booting with an older kernel that did work. Sometimes kernel upgrades will kill something that previously worked. If the old kernel works, put a bug report in to launchpad about the new kernel update fragging your sound and the issue will be looked at by a developer.
