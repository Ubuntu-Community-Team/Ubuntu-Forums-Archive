### Post by Matt__ on 2011-09-01
That sounds like the compiz expo feature, usually accessed by super + s (the windows key +s that is)

you can find its settings in system/settings/compizconfig settings manager (assuming you have the manager installed).

//edit: hmm ok, just checked on unity (I'm usually on a heavily modified classic desktop) and super +s brings up all your workspaces and not the open windows on your current workspace.
