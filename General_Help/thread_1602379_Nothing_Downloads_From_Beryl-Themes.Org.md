OK, save it to ~/Pictures then.. ;)

> also as I look at CCSM how do I tell it to set that image as the cube cap?

Load up System > Preferences > CompizConfig Settings Manager.

Click on the Desktop Cube plugin and then choose the Appearance tab.

You may delete what is already in there (Cube Cap section) and add the path to your newly downloaded picture. Activate the plugin and close your way out.
