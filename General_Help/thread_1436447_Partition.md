Generally you need two partitions for Ubuntu, one for root and one for swap.

Otherwise you should be fine so long as you understand the terminology of partitions in Linux.

[HowTo: Partitioning Basics - Ubuntu Forums]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=282018&highlight=partitioning")
