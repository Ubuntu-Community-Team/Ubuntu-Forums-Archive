### Post by Joris Donders on 2011-07-11
For completing multimedia: go to the software-center and download/install the retricted extra's for ubuntu. 

An additional browser is also something you might like; download/install the chromium browser (it's fast and a little more stable than FF ) also in the official softwarecenter. 

The rest is a bit up to you to explore and select-deselect settings you might want; e.g. the setting for the screensaver is something you've got to pay attention to. 

A fast light-weight mailclient = thunderbird (also in softwarecenter) from mozilla. 

It depends a little bit what you seek to do with your computer, and I suggest that you just ask step-by-step on this forum what you really want and how to do it. 

Welcome and enjoy Ubuntu ! ;)

Run the update program first ! This is very essential for a good working desktop.
