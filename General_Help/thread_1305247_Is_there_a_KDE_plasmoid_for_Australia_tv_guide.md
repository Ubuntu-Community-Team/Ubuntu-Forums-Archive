The **[plasma-widget-tvprogramme](apt:plasma-widget-tvprogramme)** can handle xmltv files. I bet there is a good source of xmltv programming in Australia.

Check [http://www.xpmediacentre.com.au/community/xpmce-2005-epg/968-xmltv-grabbers-australia.html](http://www.xpmediacentre.com.au/community/xpmce-2005-epg/968-xmltv-grabbers-australia.html)
