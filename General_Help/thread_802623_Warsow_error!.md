### Post by RobOrr on 2008-05-29
I'm a bit of a linux newbie, but I do know that I got errors similar to that in windows when my old graphics card was simply not good enough (due to the size of on-card memory). Only advice I can offer is to check the game requirements against your card? if the requirements are higher than your cards specification, I guess a better card is in order? Also, make sure Linux  detects your graphics card and is using the correct drivers for it. ATI cards (such as your Radeon) need the proprietary drivers to function properly. Go to [http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Gutsy_Installation_Guide](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Gutsy_Installation_Guide) for a full guide about getting drivers to work (worked for my ATI Radeon X700)
