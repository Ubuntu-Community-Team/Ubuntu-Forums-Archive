### Post by astoltz on 2007-12-19
You've tried the red-eye removal filter in the Gimp?  It's under Filters -> Enhance -> Red Eye Renoval.  The trick is that you have to select the "red" part of the image that you want to remove.  There are lots of tutorials out there - [here's one]("http://www.gimpguru.org/Tutorials/RedEye2/") that I found useful.
