### Post by tom4everitt on 2009-11-14
Heres a guide for pipes in the context of the terminal:

[http://www.linux.com/archive/articles/45679](http://www.linux.com/archive/articles/45679)

The idea of a pipe is really as simple as one application (app1), uses the output of another application (app2) as input. That is, the output of app2 is piped to app1. 
 
I'm not really sure what "piped/unpipped" application would be referring to. Maybe its simply an application that is piped to another?
