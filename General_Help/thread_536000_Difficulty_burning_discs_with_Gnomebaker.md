If not (or even if you do) try using the drive on another channel - just to ensure the controller is serviceable.

As a quick, although not always successful test, you can open a terminal and try to copy a file from the disc to your home directory, if the drive or disc are problematic you'll get a lot of clicks and the drive will keep tryingto spin up then slow down. You can do this with the following commands:```
cd /media/cdrom
ls
cp filename ~/.
```

Obviously replace cdrom with the actual folder you use and filename with a file on the disk.
