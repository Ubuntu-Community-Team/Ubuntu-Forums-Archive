### Post by ibuclaw on 2010-04-26
I do believe wget doesn't have an option to print in a human readable format.

However, you could append '2>&1 | cat' on the end, ie:
```
wget http://releases.ubuntu.com/releases/10.04/ubuntu-10.04-rc-desktop-i386.iso 2>&1 | cat
```
