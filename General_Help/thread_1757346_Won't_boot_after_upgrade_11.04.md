### Post by Sef on 2011-05-13
> Yesterday I upgraded from 10.04 to 11.04

Did you upgrade from 10.04 to 10.10 to 11.04 or from 10.04 directly to 11.04?

If you did it the latter way instead of the former, then a clean install is most likely what will be needed. You can only upgrade to the next version, unless upgrading directly from one LTS to another LTS.
