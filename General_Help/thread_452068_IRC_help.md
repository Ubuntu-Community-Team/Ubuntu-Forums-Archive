### Post by atoponce on 2007-05-23
There are tutorials all over the place.  However, really all you need to know, is how to open your client and join a channel.  If you can get to that point, then you're already 1/2 way there. :)

I would recommend XChat for your first client, then just connect to the Freenode servers, and join a channel with the /join command.  For example:

```
/join #ubuntu-us
```To change your nickname to something else, use the /nick command.  If you wanted to make your nick 'cessna_89702', then:

```
/nick cessna_89702
```If you're serious about really digging into IRC, then join me in the #ubuntu-us channel, and I can help you quite extensively.  [This link at Freenode]("http://freenode.net/using_the_network.shtml") has a couple great resources for getting up to speed on all things IRC.
