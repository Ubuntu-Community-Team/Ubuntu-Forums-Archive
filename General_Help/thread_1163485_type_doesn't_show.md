### Post by geirha on 2009-05-19
I had a keyboard recently, which was a bit flakey. Sometimes the keys I typed wouldn't appear, and instead lots of weird stuff happened. Switching tabs in firefox, new terminals opening, stuff like that. It appeared that one of the modifier keys got kept pressed from a previous use of the key. Even though all keys were physically in an "upright" position. Tapping the shift, alt and control keys, by themselves in turn, got the key (whichever key it was, never bothered to actually identify it) to register the "unpressed" signal, and the keyboard would function normally again for a long while.

Perhaps something similar to that happened to you?
