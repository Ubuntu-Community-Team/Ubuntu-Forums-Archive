### Post by jumpoutofatree on 2013-02-08
I am using "Bengali (India)" keyboard. How can you create [SIZE="4"]&#2453;&#2509;&#2468;[/SIZE]?
When followed by &#2468; tô, &#2453; kô takes on the shape of &#2468;&#2509;&#2468; ttô with the addition of a curl to the right: &#2453;&#2509;&#2468; ktô.
&#2453; is located on "K" key and &#2468; is located on "L" key. but they do not fuse when typed consecutivly.
