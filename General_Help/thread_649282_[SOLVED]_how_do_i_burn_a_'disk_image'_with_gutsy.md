### Post by AndyCooll on 2007-12-24
Though it doesn't say it specifically, right-clicking on the iso and choosing "Write To Disk" and then clicking "Write" will burn it as a disk image. No need to install Gnomebaker or K3B merely for this. Of course if you want additional features then you'll need to consider one of these apps.
