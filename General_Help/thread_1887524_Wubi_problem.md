### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-11-27
Hello and Welcome 

When installing via Wubi you have a 30 Gb maximum and if not selected it defaults to around 15 Gb .

It is possible to move a Wubi installation to it's own partition or resize the virtual disk , but it is a bit of work .

See the bottom of the Wubi Guide about resizing , which will offer a link to this forum .  [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide)

Move Wubi to a partition . [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1519354&highlight=wubi+partition](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1519354&highlight=wubi+partition)

Wubi Mega thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1639198](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1639198)
