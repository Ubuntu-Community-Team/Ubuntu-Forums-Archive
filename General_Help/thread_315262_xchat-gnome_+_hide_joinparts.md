### Post by lilpiggie on 2007-04-29
shirilover answered the same question in [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=417131&highlight=xchat+join+part")

/set irc_conf_mode 1

this will hide join/part messages for all channels. For individual channels, right-click on the channel tab and uncheck show join/part messages.

Both solutions worked for me in XChat 2.8.0
