### Post by PDA1 on 2022-11-12
My iPod 160gb Classic successfully destroyed itself the other day- logic board short. In a moment of weakness I had backed up all of the songs and videos from the iPod a while ago.  The songs on it were ripped back in 2008 so I probably used some Ubuntu 9.04 means of some sort.  All of the  backed up songs were put on an old Android phone with VLC player installed.  Man, what a pleasant surprise that was!  No more GtkPod (which is excellent) or having to convert all videos to a format iPod's like.

You'd think there'd be some option which reads, "you're about to burn a CD.  Do you want the songs to play in the order on the screen or alphabetically...or some other sequence?"

So, the simple solution was to add numbers before each song beginning with 01

01- Ducky Mo mo
02- The Three Stooges theme song
03- Speeches by Julian Assange

....etc.....

It works perfectly
