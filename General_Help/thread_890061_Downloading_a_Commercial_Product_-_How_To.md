### Post by Vadi on 2008-08-18
To enable commercial software, you go to System - Administration - Software Sources, and in the third-party software tab, enable the 'partner' repository.

To browse it, you go to Applications - Add/Remove, select 'Third party application' for Show, and they should be there.

I think. Not too much stuff is there atm unfortunately, need more companies to offer their products from there :(
