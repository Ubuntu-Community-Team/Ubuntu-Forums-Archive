### Post by amitabhishek on 2008-07-27
Before I even proceed let me tell you that non of the work is mine. For all the icon-set, wallpapers, emerald themes etc. the credit should go to the original developers/contributors.

I am in love with green color and Ubuntu/Mint are not just green enough. I was sick of seeing Vista-like, Mac-like themes everywhere. IMO green color soothes the eye and provides that pleasant look.

I have included two wallpapers (one if them is Mint's default), one big icon set and finally an emerald theme that goes well with the entire thing. Download them and have fun. Any advice/improvement/criticism is welcome. I will call this version 0.001:). Kindly let me know if any of you face any problem anywhere.
