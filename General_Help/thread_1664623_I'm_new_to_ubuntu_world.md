### Post by Hippytaff on 2011-01-11
Hi and welcome
 
If you boot from usb it will boot straight into ubuntu, if you boot without the usb in it will boot into windows. If you plan on booting from usb you might want to look into making it persistent which will mean any changes you make and anything you do will be saved rather than starting from a 'clean' statre eah time you boot. Details [here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LiveUsbPendrivePersistent")
 
oh and 8Gb is enough :-)
