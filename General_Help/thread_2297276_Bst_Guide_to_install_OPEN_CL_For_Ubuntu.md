### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-10-02
Do you wish to know how to install proprietary video drivers in Ubuntu?

When we install Ubuntu we tick a box labelled "Install third party software." That installs a proprietary video driver appropriate for our video adapter/device. After installation we go to System Settings>Software & Updates>Additional Drivers tab. If we are connected to the internet and we allow the utility time to do its stuff we get offered a selection of 4 proprietary video drivers to experiment with.

To get versions of proprietary video drivers newer (beta) than those offered by Additional Drivers we have to use other methods.
