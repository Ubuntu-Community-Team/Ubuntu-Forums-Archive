### Post by schdefan on 2006-02-09
You have to mount your second harddrive or partition. Look in the starterguide how to mount ntfs drives. [http://ubuntuguide.org/#mountunmountntfs]("http://ubuntuguide.org/#mountunmountntfs")
Then install gnome-find with synaptic 

Now you have a GUI-based search tool.
