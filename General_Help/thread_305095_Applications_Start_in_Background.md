### Post by mcduck on 2006-11-29
> **Cynical said:**
> mcduck, do you know how to distance yourself from the cube like in [this video](http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-2155353011707841108&q=Beryl&hl=en)?

Yes. The option is in 'Rotate Cube'-plugin, just set the distance you want under Numeric Values/Zoom.. If you mark 'Zoom only on initiate' the cube is only zoomed out when you are rotating the cube with your mouse (ctrl-alt-left mouse button).

Moving windows out from the cube is done with 'A 3D World'-plugin that is not yet in the stable version of Beryl.
