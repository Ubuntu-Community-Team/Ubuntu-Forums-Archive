### Post by VCoolio on 2009-05-02
There are several threads on this, try the search forum options or look in the art and design section. 

You can copy the extracted folder to ~/.icons, no permissions needed for that. Only if you want the icons to be used by root apps too, like synaptic, you need to use 'sudo cp /path/to/folder /usr/share/icons' 
After that, go to system > preferences > appearance > theme tab > customize > icons and select your newly copied icon theme.

Also it may not be a complete icon pack, just icons for several apps or occasions.
