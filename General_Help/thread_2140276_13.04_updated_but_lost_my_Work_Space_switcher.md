### Post by timgood on 2013-04-29
You can enable the work space switcher by going to System Settings/Appearance/Behaviour and ticking the box 'Enable Workspaces'. Hope this helps.

Hi Peter.
Open "System Settings" go to "Appearance" section select "Behaviour" window and check the "Enable Workspaces" box.
