### Post by drs305 on 2009-02-05
You can't really expand the size of a wubi installation (windows file) once you have installed it but you can move parts of the installation onto virtual disks. Here is a link to the Wubi guide:
[#How do I resize the virtual disks?]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide#How%20do%20I%20resize%20the%20virtual%20disks?")

It's in Section 8, Question 9.
