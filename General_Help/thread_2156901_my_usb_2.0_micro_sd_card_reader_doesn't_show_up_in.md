### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-06-23
Sorry to tell you this, but memory card readers in general often don't work in Linux distros because they need special drivers that are not available for Linux.

What I did, which works, was get a cheap USB-plug card reader.  With that, the Linux distros see it as a USB device and it works.
