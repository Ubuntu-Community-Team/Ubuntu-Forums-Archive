### Post by 1script on 2011-05-17
Most every forum has an RSS feed (usually quite a few RSS feeds each, actually). 
Here is one for Ubuntu Forums : [http://ubuntuforums.org/external.php?type=RSS2](http://ubuntuforums.org/external.php?type=RSS2)

Simply setup your RSS reader (I use Firefox Bookmarks Toolbar for that but I'm sure there are dedicated programs) to check on all those RSS feeds of forums you're interested in.

Alternatively, online tools like Google Reader (or My Yahoo or plenty others) will mix that up for you and sort the RSS feed items in a chronological order if you're so inclined.
