### Post by wannadumpwindows on 2008-04-27
Is there a shortcut on your desktop for the drive? If there is, the drive is mounted, and that's why GParted won't do anything for you. If you right click the shortcut and click "unmount", and then go back into GParted, it should work for you without any troubles. It won't work on mounted drives, as far as I can recall.
