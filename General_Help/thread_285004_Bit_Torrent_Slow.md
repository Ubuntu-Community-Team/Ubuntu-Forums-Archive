### Post by javaJake on 2006-10-26
BitTorrent is extremely slow here as well. I'm getting a mere 25 KB/s when I am getting 50 KB/s through the South America mirror.

**Edit:** OK, so BitTorrent is running at 50 KB/s and the mirror's at 30 KB/s. My mistake. Still, that's really slow compared to what it was for Dapper. :-k

**Another Edit:** I just realized that my upload is hitting speeds of 70 KB/s, which is the max for our DSL. That's probably why I have such a slow connection... (upload speeds do affect download speeds!)

**Last Edit:** Yea, that was it. Be sure to decrease your upload speed to at least three-quarters of how high it can go, or let your software automatically detect what speed limitations it should use. (I let Opera manage speeds, and things have rocketed! :D)
