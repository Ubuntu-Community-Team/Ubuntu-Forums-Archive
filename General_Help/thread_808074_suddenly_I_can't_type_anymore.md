### Post by geraldm on 2008-05-26
The window into which you are typing must have the focus to receive what you type.
When a new window pops up, it may steal the focus, and then your chat program can no longer receive what you type.

A left click of the mouse in the chat application window should regain the focus.
You may also try ALT+TAB (one or more times until the chat window gets the focus.)

Gerald
