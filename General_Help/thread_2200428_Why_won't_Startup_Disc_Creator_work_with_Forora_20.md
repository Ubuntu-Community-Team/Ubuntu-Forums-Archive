### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-01-19
Korora 20, being derived from Fedora will not work in the ubuntu startup-disk-creator, as that application will work only with ubuntu or ubuntu-derived OSs, including for example, Linux Mint, Bodhi, and several others.

For your Korora try unetbootin, available in the repos, which will normally work with any .iso file.
