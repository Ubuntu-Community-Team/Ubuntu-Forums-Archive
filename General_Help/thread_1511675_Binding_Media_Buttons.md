### Post by Jake007g on 2010-06-17
Hey all, I have a "Logitech Wave" wireless keyboard, and it has assigned buttons at the top. The calculator button, volume up+down, next track, previous track, stop, start, pause and mute buttons all work out the box, but there is a button which is designed to open up my music player, which doesn't do anything at the moment. 

How would I go about binding it to open up RhythmBox? I'm probably overlooking something simple here.

Thanks in advance

-Jake

---

### Post by sisco311 on 2010-06-17
System -> Preferences -> Keyboard Shortcuts -> Add. In the command filed type **rhythmbox**.
