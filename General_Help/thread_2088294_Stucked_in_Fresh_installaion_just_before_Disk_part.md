### Post by coffeecat on 2012-11-26
Two things:

You prefixed your thread "Lubuntu" when your screenshot shows you are trying to install Ubuntu. I've changed the prefix for you.

Just to be clear - the window with the multiple question marks. You say that appears when you click on continue. Did you try to click on either of download updates or install 3rd party software. I can see that neither are ticked - I just want to be sure that you are not trying to tick either.

I've never seen that little question mark window and because it is so uninformative, I doubt that was intentional and I wonder if there is something wrong with your install medium. Did you check the md5sum of the downloaded ISO? Also - it is worth checking the integrity of the live USB. As soon as it starts to bootup, tap the space key and a purple screen with two small icons at the bottom will appear. Press enter, choose your language and you will see a boot menu. Choose the item from the menu for checking the integrity of the install medium - I can't remember the exact wording.
