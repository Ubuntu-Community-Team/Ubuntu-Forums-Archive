### Post by keplerspeed on 2009-11-11
Some applications can't scale down small enough for a screen that size. For example, I see a quarter of the inkscape window on my 7" eeePC.

So 2 options. Either try to remove some of the icons from the toolbars, or see if you can access a larger screen.

The issue is that the toolbars have lots of stuff on them, and that makes the toolbar taller that the screen. A screenshot may be helpful too.
