### Post by grahammechanical on 2021-07-07
Please provide more information about the hardware. What connectors/ports does the monitor have. What connectors/ports does the laptop have. What make and model is the laptop?

I am guessing that you are using a DisplayLink docking station to connect the monitor to the laptop. You do not give us this information. Advice based on guesses will not be good advice.

What are the DisplayLink instructions for connecting monitors to the docking station and then to the computer? Do the instructions give any information about using the Ubuntu settings utility to activate the docking station?

By the way, for those interested there is a DisplayLink driver for Ubuntu 20.04

[https://www.displaylink.com/downloads](https://www.displaylink.com/downloads)

P.S.  How to configure DisplayLink Displays on Ubuntu

[https://support.displaylink.com/knowledgebase/articles/675136-how-to-configure-displaylink-displays-on-ubuntu](https://support.displaylink.com/knowledgebase/articles/675136-how-to-configure-displaylink-displays-on-ubuntu)
