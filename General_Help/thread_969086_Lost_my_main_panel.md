### Post by pendletone on 2008-11-04
_To move the panel down:_

1. Right-click the panel
2. Select 'Panel Settings' (you should see another panel just beside the panel you're trying to move)
3. Drag that panel towards the bottom.

_To add widgets to the panel:_

1. Right-click the panel
2. Select 'Add Widgets...' (you should see the 'Add Widgets' dialog box)
3. Double-click or drag the widget to the panel.

_To move widgets on the panel:_

1. Right-click the panel
2. Select 'Panel Settings' (you should see a crosshair icon whenever you hover your mouse over the widgets)
3. Drag the widget to the desired location

Note: Add the 'Task Manager' widget to the panel otherwise you won't see your open programs.

i did something similar too, or rather... it did to me(being more precise).
