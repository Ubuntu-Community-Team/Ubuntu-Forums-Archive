### Post by buzzingrobot on 2012-06-03
Font rendering is often in the eye of the beholder.  That said, Ubuntu and Windows do take different approaches.  You aren't the only person who characterizes Ubuntu as fuzzy. 

If your monitor or laptop has a sharpness setting, adjust it. The last time I needed to run Windows, the font display seemed awful to me.  Way too sharp.  I adjusted my monitor's sharpness setting to the point individual pixels in characters weren't visible.
