### Post by PriceChild on 2006-09-18
Its not really synaptic that freezes it... but the devs.

Dapper has been released as a stable distro. This means that ONLY security and bugfixes can be added to the main repositories.

For new features, you may enable extra repositories: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu)

These have no guaruntee of being completely stable. 

Finally if you really want to, you may compile from source from blender's website.
