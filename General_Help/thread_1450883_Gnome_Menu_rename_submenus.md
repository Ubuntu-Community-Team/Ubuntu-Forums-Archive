### Post by drewsus on 2010-04-09
open a terminal and enter:
```
alacarte
```

In the left column (Menus) highlight "Applications"
In the right column (Items) highligt "Sound & Video"
Press the 'Properties' button on the right. 
Edit the name. 

Is that the droid you were looking for?

Drewsus
