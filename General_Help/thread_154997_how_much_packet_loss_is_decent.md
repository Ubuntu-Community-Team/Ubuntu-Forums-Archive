### Post by daWabbit on 2006-04-04
I could live with 5% packet loss here, as that would be a great improvement over what our semi-rural service usually gives. That said; 5% is too much. If nothing else, it indicates the tech you spoke with needs to pull his head out of his cdrom.

Our TOS has no mention of guaranteed levels of packet loss. They say the line will live up to "accepted industry norms" and blithely omit any definition of those norms. I spoke with the Texas Public Utilities Commission and was told that the industry norm is less than 2% lost packets. So, as of a couple days ago, I'm trying to get the telco to live up to that.

You might want to check out what your state or local utility regulators have to say on the matter.
