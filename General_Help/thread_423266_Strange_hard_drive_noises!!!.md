### Post by leg on 2007-04-25
Noise from a hard drive isn't always a problem. It could just have been a seek occuring. I hear my hard drive quite often when a lot of rw is going on. However a clicking noise may be more of a problem as it may be the heads crashing into the platters which could mean the drive is slowly dying. This sound can also occur if the drive is not receiving quite enough power. I hear it (clicking) with an external usb drive I have sometimes and have no problems with it yet. Back up important data so that if the worst does happen you are prepared.
