### Post by kanikilu on 2009-04-03
Sounds like you want a font, more than making this from scratch...

If so, go to [http://www.urbanfonts.com/](http://www.urbanfonts.com/) and type "blood" into the search box at the top. Choose one you like, download the zip, open it with the archive manager, and move the .TTF file into either /usr/share/fonts/ or ~/.fonts/.
