### Post by no2498 on 2011-08-20
with the cam plugged in and turned on
open a terminal type dmesg click enter
after that stops type lsusb click enter
lsusb should give you a number and name for the cam
close the terminal
look in your package manager for xawtv and guvcview install them
try the cam in them 
most webcams just work out of the box
