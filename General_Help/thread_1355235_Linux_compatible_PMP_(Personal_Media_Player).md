### Post by garryknight on 2009-12-15
Going back to the devices question, you might also want to check out the Samsung PMPs such as the YP-Q2. It comes in several sizes but can't use SD cards. It can play wma, flac and ogg as well as mp3. The Q2 also has an FM radio built in, from which you can record directly to mp3, and it has a voice recorder. It can also play videos, either converted in Windows using Samsung's own program, or in Linux using ffmpeg. You can also view pictures, singly or in slide-show mode, and read text files.

The main reason I'm suggesting this one is that it's extremely cheap and the sound quality is very good, even through the supplied earphones.
