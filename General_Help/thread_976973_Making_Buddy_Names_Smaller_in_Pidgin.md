### Post by Seanuntu on 2008-11-09
The Fonts in Pidgin are controlled by Ubuntu (sort-of).

Go to "Preferences --> Appearance" in the main Ubuntu menu, click on the "Fonts" tab, and then adjust the "Application Font".
