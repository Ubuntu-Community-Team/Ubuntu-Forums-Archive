### Post by BslBryan on 2009-05-10
Hello, mormor83. :-)

Firstly, don't freak out.  There is a solution to most anything. :-)

You can use the recovery mode from the boot menu to just get a root-prompt.  You can log in from there and use
```
startx
```

Once you're actually in your desktop, go to the equivalent of System --> Administration --> Login Window --> Security.

Now, you can configure autologin, which will log you in automatically (as you may have guessed from the name.) :-)

I hope I've been of some help to you.

Best to you, mormor83, and good luck.
