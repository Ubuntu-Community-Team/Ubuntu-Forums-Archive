### Post by pointone on 2008-02-07
The logo is compiled into a binary format, contained within /usr/lib/usplash/usplash-theme-ubuntu.so. It is not a simple graphics file you can switch out at will. If you're unwilling to follow the [documentation]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/USplashCustomizationHowto"), perhaps customizing the splash screen isn't quite for you. Frankly, I find it a fairly comprehensive guide.

You could try [Splashy]("http://splashy.alioth.debian.org/wiki/") instead. You may find it simpler.
