### Post by Lars Noodén on 2012-06-08
Not much has happened with the mail clients for a lot of years.  

For graphical clients, your choice was mainly Thunderbird.  You might try KMail, Sylpheed, or Opera.

Except for the formatting you could go for Alpine or Mutt.  They are both very powerful and flexible.  However, since they are text only they have the (dis)advantage of using only plain text.
