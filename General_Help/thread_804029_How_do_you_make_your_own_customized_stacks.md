### Post by damis648 on 2008-05-22
Ok, first add the stacks applet 4 times, ie so you have four stacks.
Then, right click each one, go to appearance, and change the GUI to the "curved GUI" for each one and also change the name of eahc stack to "Videos" etc. Now in some obscure directory (NOT YOUR DESKTOP) make links to the websites and apps you want in each of your stacks (don't forget to add icons ;)). When you have finished that, just drag each of the links into the stacks and viola there you go!
NOTE!!!: DO NOT DELETE THOSE .desktop FILES! IF YOU DELETE THOSE THEY WILL BE DELETED FROM YOUR STACKS

Have fun!

TIP: When making the shortcuts / links / .desktop files try just dragging the icons from the panel menu ;)
