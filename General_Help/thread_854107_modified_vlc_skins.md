### Post by Saint Angeles on 2008-07-09
has anybody else here opened up a vlc skin and modified it in a cool way?

the skin files are .vlt and are in ~/.vlc/skins2). i modified a theme called "Whiteout" by inverting the colors and moving it back to blue. i renamed it "Blackout"

i'm gonna attach the file. i had to change the extension to .zip to upload it. after you download it, rename it to blackout.vlt and put it in ~/.vlc/skins2

make sure in your vlc preferences that you goto Interfaces->Main Interfaces and under "Interface Module", select "Skinnable Interface" from the dropdown menu. save and restart vlc.
