On kde4's kwin compositing, there's an option to make inactive windows fade. I think, it helps for usability. How do I do that on GNOME with Compiz?

### Post by Idefix82 on 2008-09-18
If you go to System->Preferences->Advanced Desktop Effects Settings and enable the plugin Trailfocus under the Effect section, I believe that's what you are looking for. You can then also customise it.

### Post by anotherdisciple on 2008-09-18
GNOME currently doesn't have that option AFAIK.

The post above me is correct, however the "Advanced Desktop Effects" belongs to Compiz-Fusion... that may not be an option if you don't have a good enough graphics card.

If you do have a good graphics card and you can't find that option in your menus... that is because you need to install Compizconfig-Settings-Manager.

Open a terminal (Applications > Accessories > Terminal) and type:

```
sudo aptitude install compizconfig-settings-manager
```
