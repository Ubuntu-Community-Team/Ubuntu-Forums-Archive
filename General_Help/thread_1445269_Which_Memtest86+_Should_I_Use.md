### Post by bpalone on 2010-04-02
I don't know the difference between the two.

You usually only run memtest when you are having trouble that could be caused by bad memory.

Yes, it runs until you stop it.  Many times you want it to run at least over night, sometimes longer.  The theory being that, the more time it runs the better your chance of catching those problematic intermittent problems.


Hope that helps.
