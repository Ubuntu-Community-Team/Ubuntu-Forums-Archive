### Post by fabounet on 2008-05-17
in the 1.5.6 you can have many docks in a single instance of the program !
just choose a launcher or an applet's dock, giving a dockname that doesn't exist already => it will be created
then you can add anything in this new dock and even place it anywhere you want.
you can have has many docks as you want. :-)
