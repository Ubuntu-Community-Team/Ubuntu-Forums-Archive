### Post by Gemnoc on 2012-02-25
Hi David,

Mozilla announced not long ago that they would drop support on Firefox 3.6.x next April, so there was no choice for Canonical but to upgrade Firefox to the latest version, although I didn't think they'd do it so quickly. FYI starting with v4.0, Mozilla release major versions of Firefox a lot faster.

Have you had a look over at the French Ubuntu-fr.org forum? I'm sure I've seen posts about your problem. [http://forum.ubuntu-fr.org/](http://forum.ubuntu-fr.org/)

Also, when such things happen, I always go to the "Système --> Administration --> Prise en charge des langues" menu. Most of the time, the system will detect that language packs are missing, and propose to install them.
