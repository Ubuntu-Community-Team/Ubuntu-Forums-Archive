### Post by 3Miro on 2010-09-17
Flash is a proprietary software and as such the Linux community cannot improve or fix it in any way. Basically we are left at the mercy of Adobe, which time and again have proven that they are not interested in providing us with decent version of Flash.

Basically, heavy CPU usage during flash videos is normal. If it is causing other problems, you can check to see if the laptop is overheating as a result of that.
