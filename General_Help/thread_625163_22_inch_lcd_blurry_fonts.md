### Post by timcredible on 2007-11-28
make sure the resolution is correct, lcd screens only look good at their native resolution.  also make sure the refresh rate is correct.  other than that, for a 22" monitor, it's  necessary to use dvi connection, not a  normal analog connection, or everything will be  blurry.  dvi cables run about $8 on ebay - and since they're digital, there's no need for expensive cables, either the bits get there or they don't, unlike analog cables, where more expensive equals better.
