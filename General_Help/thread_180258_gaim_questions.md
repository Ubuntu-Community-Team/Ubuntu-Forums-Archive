### Post by jones_jj on 2006-05-21
impakt, there are a few ways you can tackle this problem/issue.  One is to use guifications, which I use and love.  Another way is to go to **Preferences** -> **Plugins** -> **System Tray Icon**  This will flash the System Tray Icon when you have a new message, just like in ICQ, and it will flash in the tool bar area the IM window.  Also under **Conversations**, still under **Preferences** there is an option **Raise IM window on events**, this may be another way to let you know when someone is messaging you also.
