### Post by linuxinstalledfromhdd on 2011-05-29
Try installing Flash Aid Plugin for Firefox and after you get it installed, in Firefox Click Tools >> Addons >> and then selecting Flash Aid to execute the plugin.  You can download it over here:

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-us/firefox/addon/flash-aid/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-us/firefox/addon/flash-aid/)
