### Post by Idefix82 on 2008-10-14
Have you installed the compiz-config manager? If you have then go to System->Preferences->Advanced Desktop Effect Settings. There you can play around a bit. For the most fancy stuff, enable the cube plugin and the rotating cube, under "General Options->Desktop Size" set the horizontal size to 4 and then press Ctrl-Alt-left mouse button.

Then click on the cube and on the rotating cube plugin icons and play around a bit with the settings.
