### Post by nonovo on 2008-05-08
Hi, I have the same card and problem, too.
I have added following lines into xorg.conf:
Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"Configured Monitor"
# NN Added 1 line
	Option		"RightOf"	"LVDS"
EndSection

Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"Default Screen"
	Monitor		"Configured Monitor"
	Device		"Configured Video Device"
# NN Added 4 lines
	SubSection "Display"
		Virtual		2560 1024
		Modes		"1280x1024" "1024x768" "832x624" "800x600"
	EndSubSection
EndSection

Its far from perfect, you will loose DRI, also my screen looks a bit odd some parts of gnome are not working. When I find out more, will post the rest.

Hope this helps, BTW, my VGA connected screen is the primary screen positioned right from NoteBook.
Cheers,
