### Post by Genius314 on 2008-09-28
What you want to use is [Xwinwrap]("http://swik.net/xwinwrap").

I *think* this is the command you want to use:
> xwinwrap -ni -argb -fs -s -st -sp -a -nf -- /usr/lib/misc/xscreensaver/glmatrix -root -window-id WID

Hope that helps. :)
