### Post by gearhead_318 on 2011-02-12
I seem to have fixed the prblem, at least for the moment.
I went to system>preferences>mouse>touchpad unchecked "Disable touchpad while typing" a short while later I was looking for something else under "System" it highlighted but the drop didn't go down, the computer looked like it was going to freeze so I did ctrl+alt+delete and hit suspend. After waking it back up again the touchpad works as it should.
