### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-11-11
Since the hooks for KVM are part of the kernel, there is no easy way to remove that message.  Your computer has hardware support for virtualization which you can turn on and off.  If off, you will get the message.  Try turning on and the message will go away.  Your computer will use a little more power.
