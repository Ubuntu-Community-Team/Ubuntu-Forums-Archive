### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-05-30
The slowdown is probably due to the Intel graphics acceleration problems on Ubuntu 9.04.  There are "howtos" around that give instructions for possible fixes, but be aware that these will replace vital parts of your system, and they actually made my graphics performance much worse. It was a bit involved to reverse the changes as well.

I'd recommend getting even a basic Nvidia graphics card, you'll be much happier. You'll be able to burn down windows while having rain falling on your desktop AND playing Nexuiz at the same time :-)
