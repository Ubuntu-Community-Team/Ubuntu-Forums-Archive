### Post by tankerkevo on 2009-04-10
I have the same keyboard and have been trying to get it working on Intrepid since it's release. It is either an issue with Bluez or the latest kernels. I have tried several distributions to try to get it to work, on both 32 and 64 bit architectures (not on a PS3).

It simply does not work. Someone said it has something to do with the "[" and "]" symbols in the way the keyboard reports itself. Also note that it worked on Gutsy but regressed w/ Intrepid; so you you could try loading Gutsy up. Gutsy wouldn't work for me though because it didn't properly recognize my box's HDMI port.

Good luck.
