### Post by coffeecat on 2010-11-07
Are you emptying the trash? If you don't empty the trash, your 'deleted' files are still there in a hidden folder. Even when you unmount and remount the flash drive, because the hidden trash folder for files deleted from the flash drive is also on the flash drive.
