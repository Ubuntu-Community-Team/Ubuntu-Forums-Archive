### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-05-20
Flash uses too much CPU, but if it gets worse over time, then most likely you have dirt on your CPU fan. This will cause the CPU to heat too much due to intensive activity and slow down performance over time.

See the [[COLOR="Sienna"]**Flash Optimization**[/COLOR]](http://firefox-tutorials.blogspot.com/2010/05/flash-optimization.html) section of [Firefox optimization and troubleshooting thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1193567).
