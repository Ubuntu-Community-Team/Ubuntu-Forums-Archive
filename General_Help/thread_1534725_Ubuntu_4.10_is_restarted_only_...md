### Post by oustiti on 2010-07-20
Have you looked in the logs at what happened before the restart?
Next time it restart, go in to log viewer immediately when you reboot and see if you can find something relating to the reboot.

Is your computer heating up / fans spinning hard at all when it goes to reboot? The only rebooting problems I have seen consistently is with laptops with poor cooling/clogged fans that reboot to prevent thermal damage.
