### Post by RikoW on 2005-12-12

[http://freshmeat.net/redir/ganttproject/59435/url_homepage/www.ganttproject.org]("http://freshmeat.net/redir/ganttproject/59435/url_homepage/www.ganttproject.org")

[http://freshmeat.net/redir/kdissert/42508/url_homepage/show.php]("http://freshmeat.net/redir/kdissert/42508/url_homepage/show.php")
