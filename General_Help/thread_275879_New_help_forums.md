### Post by lonce on 2006-10-12
We are currently building an Ubuntu / MythTV based media centers.  We have forums at our support site for Ubuntu and MythTV.  We encourage everyone to ask us questions.  In finding the answers to your questions it helps us learn more about Ubuntu and MythTV that we might not know.  It also helps anyone that might have a question.  We answer all questions personally and are starting to get an extensive FAQ about MythTv and Ubuntu.  Feel free to stop by and ask away or take part in the conversations.  

You dont need to be a member to post in the forums or ask a question in the FAQ section.  You can even add links without being a member.  Basically right now the site is open to all.  Membership is free of course, all it does is put you on the mailing list for when the Hannibal MythTvease media center releases.
