### Post by oldfred on 2017-06-09
A lot of things in Linux work, but are different in how they work. 
You may not have something like "coolerboost" as that sound like something unique. So unless a developer has an MSI system, it may not get added to drivers.

Best to install and see how it works.
Some models of MSI seem to require boot parameters, just to work. And issues are often common across brands.
