### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2013-01-18
I wouldn't transcode anything since you're going from one lossy format to another lossy format. What's the point because you end up with worse quality and less disk space. Moving forward you could rip discs and FLAC or OGG to start with. If you still want to convert everything search for batch ffmpeg commands (there are several threads on this topic).
