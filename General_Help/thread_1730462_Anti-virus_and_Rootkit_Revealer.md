### Post by WorMzy on 2011-04-16
I can't vouch for any of these, but there's chkrootkit, rkhunter and unhide.

You may also want to look at tripwire and aide.


As for anti-virus applications, you don't need one as there's no known Linux viruses in the wild at the present time, but there's clamav, avg, avast, avira, arcabit, bitdefender, dr. web, and probably a few others.
