### Post by pbjoiner on 2008-11-11
Howdy!

I found a solution to a problem that I hope might help others. I have "Check spelling while I type" enabled. While entering a new message every word was being marked as wrong. Now I know I'm not quite that bad at spelling.

I went into the preferences Edit > Preferences > Composer Preferences > Spell Checking. There I discovered that I had selected "English (United States)" but not "English." 

I selected the just plain old "English" option. This appears to solve the problem. I was even able to deselect it afterwards and the spell checker continues to work normally.

My guess is that a setting got hosed when I upgraded to 8.10.
