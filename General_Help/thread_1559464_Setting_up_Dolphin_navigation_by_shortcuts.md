### Post by bergfly on 2010-08-31
Konqueror will do this for you with a bit of setup. Fire it up, and then along the File menu along the top, pull down WINDOW. Under window you can split left/right or top/bottom. One tab will take you to the first split view, the second tab to the address bar, and the third to the second split view. Hope that is what you were looking for.
