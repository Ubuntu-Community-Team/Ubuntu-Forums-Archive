Sometimes it will occur right after the computer boots, though, when I have not even used the mouse at all.

This problem has occurred across multiple fresh installations of Ubuntu 11.10, as well as in Lubuntu 11.10, but it has not occurred in other installed Oss (for example Windows, or other flavors of Linux, or my old install of Ubuntu 9.10).
