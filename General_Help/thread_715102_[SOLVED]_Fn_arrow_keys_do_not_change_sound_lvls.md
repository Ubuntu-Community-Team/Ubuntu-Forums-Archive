I am pretty happy with everything I am seeing with 7.10 so far, however I am having some sound control issues.

I can control my sound from the alsamixer (volume up, down, and mute) but I do not have control via the Fn + arrow keys.  I get a display saying the sound is changing (a little speaker icon with up, down, mute symbols), but the actual volume does not change.  Is this something I have to bind? Or a driver issue perhaps?  Any help would be great, I don't want to boot up alsamixer every time I want to change the sound levels.  

I am running 7.10 on an Acer Aspire 2480 series, if you need more info please let me know.. I don't know what to post to make things easier. 

Cheers

### Post by pointone on 2008-03-04
Go to System > Preferences > Sound and at the bottom under "Default Mixer Tracks", make sure "Master" is selected.
