### Post by QIII on 2012-01-20
To do anything that depends heavily on physical hardware:  dual boot.

For general things like office apps:  virtualize.  (But VirtualBox is really getting better at using the capabilities of the physical hardware.)

For doing something on a computer that you are not sitting at or that is running headless:  remote.

For taking your chances on a mixed bag:  Wine.
