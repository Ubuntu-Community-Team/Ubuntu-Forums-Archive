### Post by psko on 2010-05-19
Try this: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/daily-live/current/]("http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/daily-live/current/")

>  Minimum hardware requirements:

    * Processor: Intel® Atom™ processor
    * RAM: 512 MB
    * Storage: 4 GB Flash disk (SSD) or hard disk

Ubuntu Netbook remix is for Intel Atom processors, you of course have a PPC processor. I am not saying it can't be done, but it would require some work.

I got Ubuntu Netbook Edition 10.04 working on a PPC Mac PowerBook G4 12-inch!

It is still a little rough around the edges (I get the black screen after the yaboot bootloader most of the time). I think that I have an xorg.conf problem or a yaboot problem. Other than that, if you can get it to display, it works great. A lot snappier than Gnome in 10.04, that is for sure.
