### Post by David Corrales on 2006-10-31
The warning means that to the dpkg-reconfigure tool, the xorg.conf file looked modified so it was backed up.

Why don't you try changing the line *Driver "ati"* inside the *Device* section to *Driver "radeon"*. That will use the open source radeon driver instead of the ati one. Remember to reboot after this change.
