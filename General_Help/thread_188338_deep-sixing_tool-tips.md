Can anyone tell me how to turn off the tool-tips that appear whenever the mouse pointer hovers over something. They are extremely annoying.

### Post by carlos1 on 2006-06-03
If you are using KDE and are talking about the panel, just right click on the panel, click on "configure panel", select "Appearance" and under "General" deselect ""Show tooltips".

### Post by flyingbrass on 2006-06-04
Apparently they can't all be disabled in gnome.

[https://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu/+source/gnome-panel/+bug/31102](https://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu/+source/gnome-panel/+bug/31102)

To get rid of the ones on the menu bar type gconf-editor, navigate to apps/panel/global and uncheck tooltips_enabled.
