### Post by mac9416 on 2010-03-07
Hi,

The same thing has happened to me after running out of disk space. I had some free space on a Windows partition, so I fired up a live CD and partitioned off a little more space for Ubuntu. After that, I was able to log in as normal

If you don't have free space on another partition, you can boot a live CD and delete files from there.

Good luck!
