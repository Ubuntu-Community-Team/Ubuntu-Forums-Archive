### Post by leamanc on 2010-08-08
[COLOR="YellowGreen"][refit]("http://refit.sourceforge.net/")[/COLOR] is the tool you want and need.

I have used it to set up a triple-boot Mac before, with help from [COLOR="YellowGreen"][this hint]("http://www.macosxhints.com/article.php?story=20090807033320433")[/COLOR].  Read that hint, the refit documentation, and some other Google-assisted advice **very closely** before starting your triple-boot project, take some notes, map out a plan, and you shouldn't have any problems.

After spending an evening getting it working, the newness wore off, and I realized that I have enough Macs to devote one to OS X, one to Ubuntu, and run Windows in VirtualBox on both of them. :D
