### Post by sekinto on 2008-04-08
The two popular multi-service/protocol messengers for Linux are Kopete and Pidgin.

In Kopete:
Settings>Configure>Accounts
New
MSN Messenger
Fill out your MSN ID and Password information (ID is something like [email]myname@hotmail.com[/email]).
Can set other custom options
Press next
Press Finish

In Pidgin:
Accounts>Manage
Click add
Choose MSN as protocol
Enter your information and edit any custom options that you want to
Click save
