### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-03-29
Have you tried the RCA out to the TV's RCA in? I found that the MX420 wouldn't reliably put the picture on the TV using the s-video out.
I haven't used my MX420 in years but my MX440 uses my TV as its monitor by the SVGA port (my TV has a SVGA port to connect to), I have no standard monitor connected to it.

Once you connect it by RCA cable open System > Preferences > NVIDIA X Server Settings and it should automatically detect your TV (you may have to reboot with the TV on and connected) and be able to show your screen on it.

The MX420 only has 64MB of onboard memory so when you put the display out to the TV the monitor may go black (I don't remember if it did or not). You'll have to switch it back to the monitor using the TV to see what you're doing if it does.

Don't forget to either turn off the screensaver or set it to 2 hours, otherwise you'll be watching something and the screen will go to black or to the screensaver. I always set mine to just go black....lol.
