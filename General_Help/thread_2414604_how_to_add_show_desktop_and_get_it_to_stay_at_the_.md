### Post by TheFu on 2019-03-12
Perhaps search for how to *minimize all windows* instead with the DE you are using? With GUI questions, it is very important to include the release and DE being used to get any useful responses.  Assuming Gnome: [https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/760/minimize-all/](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/760/minimize-all/)
and [https://wiki.gnome.org/Design/OS/KeyboardShortcuts](https://wiki.gnome.org/Design/OS/KeyboardShortcuts)

Or you can use the workspaces.  I use a 3x3 grid of workspaces on my development box, but a 1x4 on my laptop, so there is always one that is empty.  Maybe your DE doesn't support that? IDK.  [https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/shell-workspaces.html.en](https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/shell-workspaces.html.en)
