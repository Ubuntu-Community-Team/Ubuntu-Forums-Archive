As you have not stated where you are at I will give you what I did to achieve my aim.

Firstly I went to System->Preferences->Startup Applications and there ensured that both the wireless network connection and the sound icon were ticked.

Second I then right clicked on the panel and selected add to pannel and from there found the notification area in the list.  I highlighted this and clicked on add and everything was then displayed exactly where I wanted it.
