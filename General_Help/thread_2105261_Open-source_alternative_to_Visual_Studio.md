### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-01-15
Visual Studio and Joomla are completely different things. Visual Studio is an IDE (integrated development environment) while Joomla is a CMS (content management system).

There are plenty of good IDE's available for Linux, to give any recommendations though we need to know what language(s) you want to develop in.
