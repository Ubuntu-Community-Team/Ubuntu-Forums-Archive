### Post by jerome1232 on 2011-04-30
3+ ways to get to it

hit super+a, type sy, it will come up in the search.

Click the power on/off button in upper right hand screen, click System Settings, click Synaptic Package Manager.

Right click the application Launcher (looks like a magnifying glass with a + sign) click system, click show more results, click synaptic package manager.
