### Post by Bucky Ball on 2011-05-01
There is no other way than a re-install unfortunately. If you have a /home partition this shouldn't be a problem. When you get to the partitioning section choose manual and just untick the boxes to format /home and /swap (in other words don't format or change those partitions) and install the OS into the existing / (which will be re-formatted). The OS will automagically use the existing /home and /swap in this case and only format /. 

Good luck (ps: for rock solid stability and longer support I would personally go for 10.04 LTS). ;)
