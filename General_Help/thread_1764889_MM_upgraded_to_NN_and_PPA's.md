### Post by kostkon on 2011-05-24
Open your software centre, select *Edit &#8594; Software Sources*, then select the *Other Software* tab, and edit your ppas (check [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu#Editing%20Repositories") for instructions). You need to  change the *distribution* text-field from *maverick* to *natty* (and click on their checkbox to activate them).

Hope that helps.
