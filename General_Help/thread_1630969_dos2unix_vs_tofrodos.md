### Post by nealmcb on 2012-10-14
dos2unix handles mac newlines also, along with many other character conversions which tofrodos doesn't.  But the latter can make backups, etc.

Check out their manpages at 

 [http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/precise/man1/fromdos.1.html](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/precise/man1/fromdos.1.html)
