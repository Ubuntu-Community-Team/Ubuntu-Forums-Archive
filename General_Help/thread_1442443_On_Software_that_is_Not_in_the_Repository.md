### Post by coffeecat on 2010-03-30
Don't just think of companies. There are plenty of open source and free (as in beer as well as freedom) third-party repositories out there. Examples are:

Bisigi Project Themes - [http://www.bisigi-project.org/?lang=en](http://www.bisigi-project.org/?lang=en)

Medibuntu - [http://www.medibuntu.org/](http://www.medibuntu.org/)

And then there are independent app developers from whom you can download .deb installers suitable for Ubuntu.

Example:

Handbrake - [http://handbrake.fr/](http://handbrake.fr/)

I don't know of a listing for all of these. You just have to find them by trial and error - or by reading threads on this forum.
