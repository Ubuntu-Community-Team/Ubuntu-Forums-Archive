### Post by diablo75 on 2008-05-25
Just reinstall XP as you normally would, but when you get to the screen that asks you about disk partitions, delete all of the partitions and then create a new one spanning the whole drive to use for a fresh XP install.  Doing this will effectively wipe the drive clear and then install XP by itself with no remnants of the previous OS's.
