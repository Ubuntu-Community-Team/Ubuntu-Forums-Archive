### Post by MARP1961 on 2011-01-12
Unfortunately I think you will really need to connect your desktop pc directly to this router. You can purchase 10 metre lengths of ethernet cable quite inexpensively on ebay. A temporary wired connection should get you up and running and I don't know of any other way to install the software to get your wi-fi adapter working, perhaps someone else does? In any case a wired connection to your router is always useful as wi-fi does seem to cause more frequent problems and you will always need a reliable method to get on the internet.
