### Post by GreenN00b on 2008-08-16
Here are some starting points on this as far as I know:
- most Linux file systems will get fragmented in time, especially if they are almost full
- most Linux file systems try to place files in contiguous zones to avoid fragmentation, but this is not working when you have little space left on the device
