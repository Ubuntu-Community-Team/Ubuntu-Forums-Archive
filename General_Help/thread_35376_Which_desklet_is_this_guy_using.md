### Post by darkaudit on 2005-05-18
I regret to inform you that most of these desklets look like the Psi series. These desklets have been obsoleted by the 0.3x series of gdesklets, and most will no longer function. They have been removed from the gdesklets site due to this (and they haven't been updated since 2003).

You can still find them in gdesklets-data, but desklets like the net monitor, CPU, memory, and hdd desklets will not work.
