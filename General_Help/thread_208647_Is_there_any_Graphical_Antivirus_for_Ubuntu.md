### Post by cleentrax on 2006-07-04
If you use proprietary formats, like .doc and .xls, then you can be a virus "carrier," infecting others, even if you never see the viruses in Linux. That's another reason why it might be a good idea for Linux users to use AV software, or at least run a scan periodically.
