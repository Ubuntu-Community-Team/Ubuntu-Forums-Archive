### Post by fela on 2009-09-27
Short answer: you can't.

Long answer: you can't but there are lots of great linux-native alternatives to iTunes, you only need iTunes if you have one of the very latest iPhones/iPods that only support iTunes. Check out rhythmbox (already installed on ubuntu), or amarok, or banshee. There's plenty of others aswell.

Also, if you want to keep your machine free of any MS OS, you shouldn't use wine.
