### Post by chuckyp on 2007-09-30
Envy can seriously mess up your computer.  I would contact the script maker for support its use isn't supported here.  If they have directions on removal I would recomend just doing that.  Then you could just go to System > Administration > Restricted Drivers Manager and install the nvidia drivers.

I actually believe the Envy installation process is more complicated than just doing it the right way the first time.
