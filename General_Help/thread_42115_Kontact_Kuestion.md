### Post by shakin on 2005-06-16
1. Click Settings -> Configure Summary View
2. Check NewsTicker item, then click Ok.
3. Again, click Settings -> Configure Summary View
4. Click new News Sources icon in the left panel.
5a. Select a feed from one of the built-in sources
or
5b. Click New Feed button at the bottom and configure the name and URL to the rss or xml file. Click Ok.
6. You may need to restart Kontact before you see the feed, or wait for the refresh time to load the news.
