How did you see your CPU was at 100% when your processes don't cause these problems?
When you used System monitor you have to make sure you are looking at all the processes, not just your own.
In menu View, choose all processes, then click twice on the columnheader CPU to get the ones whiche use the CPU most are on top.
You can also open a terminal and type top or htop (when installed) to see what is going on in your machine.
