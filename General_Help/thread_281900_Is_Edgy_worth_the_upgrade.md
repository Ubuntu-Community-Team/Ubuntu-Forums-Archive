### Post by der_joachim on 2006-10-22
If you are a glutton for punishment, install Edgy now. I am and I did and I had a lot of fun. :)
If you are not, just wait one more week. 

I upgraded yesterday. Here are my first impressions. YMMV!
- KDM has trouble starting. Sometimes I get a blank screen and I have to Alt-F7 to see a login prompt. Other times it works, albeit more slowly than I am used to. 
- The NVIDIA driver was eaten by the upgrade. I tried using Envy, but I got a MD5 error. I did a manual install of the Nvidia driver and that worked perfectly.
- The default kernel is 386 for some reason. Probably the generic kernel is not stable yet. But now I am speculating.
- Upstart seems to work. I cannot really see, because I still use the default usplash and I have not tried configuring it yet. I am not nagged by KDE anymore for bluetooth sticks, something it did before Edgy upgraded itself to upstart. Furthermore, I see a slight decrease in boot time. 
- Firefox 2 as a default browser works fine. It feels snappier than 1.5. I hope that whatever they did to it, works equally well in Thunderbird. TB1.5 is painfully slow in Kubuntu. 

All in all, I did have some trouble to get things started, but most of it has been solved quite easily. 

Is it worth the upgrade? Maybe. On the plus side, there's shiny new applications and a nice new init thingy. :) 
On the downside. Well it was no perfect upgrade and there is still some trouble getting into my beloved desktop. That will probably be over soon though.
