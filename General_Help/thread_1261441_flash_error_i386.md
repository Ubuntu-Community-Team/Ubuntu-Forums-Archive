### Post by P4man on 2009-09-09
to install flash in ubuntu, go to system, adminstration, synaptic package manager. Search for "adobe flashplugin", right click to mark for installation, apply.

That said, you may experience choppy playback with that videocard. That card is no longer supported by AMD (=ATI), so you can not use their drivers on the latest versions of ubuntu's. You are limited to the opensource drivers (installed by default) which may not perform as well as you'd like.
