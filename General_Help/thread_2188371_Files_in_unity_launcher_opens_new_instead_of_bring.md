### Post by mc4man on 2013-12-09
You may want to mention what release of Ubuntu you're using. (12.04, 13.04, ect.

Anyway can't say I see this behavior except if the nautilus window in question is open to a folder on another partition or external drive. Then what you've described does happen.
The above is a current bug, in that case to bring that nautilus window forward directly one has to click on the  'device' icon in the unity launcher instead of the 'files' icon.
(unless one has 'unlocked' that device icon from the launcher, in that case you've seen what to do as there is no 'device' icon to click on.
