### Post by kkkkdddd on 2008-05-21
1. Set grub delay to 1 second.
With this setting grub only blinks or even do not show up at all.
2. Change default system.

To boot something different than the default you have to rapidly press ESC key just when grub normally is being displayed.
Pressing ESC will stop the 1 sec countdown and you will be able to navigate grub menu normally.  

I have been using this settings on my parent`s computer for about a year.
They use this computer everyday and haven`t yet noticed anything.
