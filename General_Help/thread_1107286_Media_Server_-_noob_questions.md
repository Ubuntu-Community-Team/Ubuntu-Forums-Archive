### Post by evilaim on 2009-03-26
Well, luckily for you I've just gone through this, unluckily for you, if you want a media center that's read by the Xbox 360, it will take a fair bit of time and such.  If I were you, I'd go onto a chat network while you're trying to do this and discuss it with people who know of this, as I find live help much better with this topic then a BB.

You could try irc.freenode.net #ubuntu or even on efnet #ubuntu.  There are a lot of channels.

I have heard that to achieve this you need to run a VirtualBox or VMWare system, and use certain hacks in windows to do this.  If you're new to Linux, it might take a bit of frustration, but I'm sure it's quite possible.

[http://www.liamm.com/tech/how-toxbox-360-media-server-in-linux](http://www.liamm.com/tech/how-toxbox-360-media-server-in-linux)

Try that walk through and if you still have questions feel free to post here again.

-Evil
