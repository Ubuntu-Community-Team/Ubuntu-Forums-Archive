### Post by dal on 2009-07-30
When I took it a little over a year ago it was ridiculously easy. I stressed a little about it since throwing down a couple of hundred (AU) dollars for taking a test was a pretty solid investment for me at the time (uni student, not much spare cash lying around) but it appears it was for nothing - did a couple of sample question quizzes on the net, aced them, walked in and easily passed the test itself (in about half the time alloted). The only questions I can remember getting wrong were laptop-specific (one asked about whether NiMH or LiIon batteries were prevalent in the laptop market - a retarded question if you ask me, a) Google can most likely give you the answer inside of a minute, and b) why would you need to know it anyhow? - but most of the questions were fairly simple general knowledge computing/windows questions).
