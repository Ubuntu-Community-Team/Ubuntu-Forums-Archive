### Post by Theimon on 2007-04-07
AMD advises people _not to use_ CnQ when overclocking their cpu's to make sure the system will remain as stable as possible when overclocking. CnQ might interfere when trying to step down which can result in a unstable, sketchy system.
And why overclock it when you want it to step down?
