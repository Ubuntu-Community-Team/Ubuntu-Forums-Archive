### Post by Audimage on 2007-02-20
I have been converting to using Ubuntu for the past 6 months or so. I have Ubuntu along with vmware installed on both of my machines. I've installed vmware several times and have had no problems installing or using it. Recently though, vmware has stopped working on my laptop. It was working flawlessly, but now when I try to launch it (via the icon) I get a tab on my window list saying "Starting VMware Server Console"  and that is it. The tab goes away after 5 seconds or so but that is it. Have any of you experienced this problem, or know a way of fixing it other than a complete reinstall of it? I'd rather learn how to fix it than just take the easy way out and reinstall vmware. Thanks to all people active on the forums and in the community. You've helped me and others with Ubuntu immensely.

FIXED: I needed to reconfigure for the new kernel. For those of you that also have this problem run: sudo <FILE PATH>vmware-config.pl (where file path is the path to your vmware-config.pl file)
