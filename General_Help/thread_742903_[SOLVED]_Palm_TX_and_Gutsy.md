### Post by PhilCash on 2008-04-04
I"m not 100% sure how though...

Sitting down to have another attempt at getting it working, I connected my TX and ran the System/Preferences/PalmOS Devices applet and the blasted thing worked!!! (Yippeee!)

To confirm, I  sync'd my contacts from the TX to Evolution - it was so much faster than under WinXP that I initialy thought it had failed straight away!
Then sync'd tasks, calander, MAL, Apps, and all seem to work; I havn't tried installing any Palm applications yet though.
The only thing I did that MAY have changed or refreshed anything was to look through all my settings on the Palm TX for hotsync and communications ensuring it was set to Cradle/Cable under communications and under HotSync ensured the modem snyc pref was Direct to Modem, the LanSync Pref was Local Hotsync, and the Conduit set up had all conduits selected.

It seems I was so hung up looking for settings in my shiny new OS (Ubuntu) that I over looked the fact that the Palm TX also has a complex OS with settings of its own. Then again, it could just be a fluke...

Thanks to all those posters in all those threads - I'm sure my checking the TX's settings was triggered by someone's comments from one of the many threads I've read.

Cheers,
Phil
