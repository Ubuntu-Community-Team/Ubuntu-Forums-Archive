### Post by vchapman on 2010-03-05
How do I change the OS version in my profile. I have gone from 8.04 to 9.04 (so far).

---

### Post by cascade9 on 2010-03-05
Click on your username (or get to your profile any other way you want). 

Then go to 'About Me' and click on the pencil icon next to "Ubuntu Distro". Change to whatever version you want in the drop-dwon menu, then hit save. 

Done ;)
