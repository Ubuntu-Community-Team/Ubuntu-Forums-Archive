### Post by Zill on 2011-05-15
Ubuntist:  I don't know if this is *exactly* what you are looking for but you could take a look at "Inserting External Data in Table (WebQuery)" in the OOo Help.

Webqueries *should* extract data from a website and insert it into a spreadsheet.  I have never used the OOo version but I did use them with MS Excel in the old days before I saw the light. ;-)

[This Tip]("http://www.openofficetips.com/blog/archives/2004/11/webquery_scrapi.html") (although old!) should give you an idea of how they work.
