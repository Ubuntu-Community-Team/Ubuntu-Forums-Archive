### Post by Jesterday on 2009-02-21
Some boxes are just too big. so if you need to see the bottom of the dialog box to click a button, just hold the Alt button on your keyboard as your click and drag the dialog box upwards.

On my eeePC and Aspire One I have to do this all the time due to the lower resolution of the machines.
