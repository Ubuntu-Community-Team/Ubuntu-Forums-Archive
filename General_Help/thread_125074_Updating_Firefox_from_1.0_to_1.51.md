### Post by ardchoille on 2006-02-02
From what I understand, Firefox 1.5 is not and will never be in the repos for Breezy Badger. I have, however, downloaded the tarball for firefox 1.5 ([http://www.mozilla.com/firefox/](http://www.mozilla.com/firefox/)) and it runs fine from my home directory. Be sure to backup ~/.mozilla bfore running firefox 1.5 just in case you want to go back to firefox 1.0.7, you'll have your configs backed up.
