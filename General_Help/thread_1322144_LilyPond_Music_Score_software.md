### Post by coffeecat on 2009-11-10
Lilypond doesn't have a gui. The Wikipedia page tells more:

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lilypond](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lilypond)

There are three GUI apps in the Ubuntu repos (that I know of) that use lilypond as a backend to produce printed scores. Denemo and Noteedit are music notation editors, and Rosegarden is a sequencer.
