### Post by squid68 on 2008-06-07
The Beginning Ubuntu Linux by Keir Thomas is a real must. He wrote much of the documentation for Ubuntu Linux and although the book is based on version 5 it still works to learn Ubuntu.  I have that one, the Ubuntu Hacks and Moving to Ubuntu.  The Moving book is excellent to learn the MS replacement programs when switching from XP to Ubuntu.

[http://www.amazon.com/Moving-Ubuntu-Linux-Marcel-Gagn%C3%A9/dp/032142722X](http://www.amazon.com/Moving-Ubuntu-Linux-Marcel-Gagn%C3%A9/dp/032142722X)

My local Barnes and Noble had like 2 books at the beginning of last year and now they stock at least 12 on the shelves.  The books have been invaluable to me and with them and the help of this wonderful forum community I have been completely MS free for one year now.  Good reading.
