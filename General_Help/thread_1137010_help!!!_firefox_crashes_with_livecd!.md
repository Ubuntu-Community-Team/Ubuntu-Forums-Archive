### Post by pbpersson on 2009-04-25
When you first boot the CD, I would check for errors on the CD and also do a memtest

If things are crashing on the Live CD, I would certainly not install it on the hard drive.

Usually it is the other way around, things work fine on the CD and the problems start when you install.
