### Post by SeijiSensei on 2015-05-18
This may not solve all your issues, but if you use multiple desktops you can limit the applications that appear in the panel to the ones on each desktop alone.

Right-click in an empty area of the panel and choose Task Manager settings.  You can limit the tasks shown to those on the current screen, those on the current desktop, or those from the current "activity."
