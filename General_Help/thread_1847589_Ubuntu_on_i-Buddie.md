### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-10-05
If the notebook booted OK to the Pendrive, then either your BIOS located that device BEFORE the hard drive (and worked by default), or you changed the BIOS to boot from the Pendrive.

Try going back into the BIOS to change it boot from the hard drive -- without the Pendrive connected.
