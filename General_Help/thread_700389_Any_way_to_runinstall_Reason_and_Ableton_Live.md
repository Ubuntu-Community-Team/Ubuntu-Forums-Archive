### Post by danwood76 on 2008-02-18
Its not possible to run these apps under linux.
Wine would be bad for descent audio creation and you would have horrible audio latencies.

You should look at alternatives if you are making the switch, otherwise if you are dependant on using these apps you will have to go back to win, or run it on a dual boot.

I found two alternatives:
Rosegarden
Ardour

Have a look at their sites and see if you like them.
I would doubt you could import any of your old songs into them though.

Oh and audacity is good for audio recording etc.
