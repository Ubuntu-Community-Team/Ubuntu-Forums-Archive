### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-19
When I'm playing fullscreen CS or UT, I push the next song on my ipod and it plays the next song. ;)

It has to do with global keybindings and how certain games override those global bindings by grabbing, then clobbering, every keypress event.

UT2004 doesn't grab/clobber keystrokes. Other games do.

You can probably write an uber-grabber and dispatch events directly to your media player before your game ever sees them, or I'll do it for a year's supply of pizza.
