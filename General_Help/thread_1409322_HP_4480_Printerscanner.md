### Post by joeknoodle on 2010-02-17
Not sure if mine is the same, it says C4480.

I love it, and it works great under my 9.04/Gnome system. Make sure you get the HP drivers from synaptic if you don't have them.

The only real issue I've encountered is the rotation of images can be off. I feel confident a bit more work on my part would correct this, but as it is I just don't use the image function that way. I tend to print my images straight from an SD card, and they look and act great.

I am not in any way related to HP other than having the printer. Mine is opinion based on my personal experience, your mileage may vary.
