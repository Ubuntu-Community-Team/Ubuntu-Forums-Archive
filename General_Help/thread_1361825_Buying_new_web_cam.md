### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-12-22
Webcams are a compatibility nightmare on anything other than Windows.

The situation is changing with UVC-compatible webcams. Most of them should work out-of-the-box on any OS. You should still check for reports of compatibility online, but looking for 'UVC' on the box is a good start.

Some cams might even say that they support Linux - mine did.
