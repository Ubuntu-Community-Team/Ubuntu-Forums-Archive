### Post by brake16 on 2010-02-25
Never mind.  I used Pandora in a Windows environment.  Not only did this recover my pictures, but it also recovered the exif data.  All for free.  It doesn't recover many of the other data formats that PhotoRec does, but it does recover many common formats, and getting the exif data makes it a better solution, in my book.

I found Pandora at CNET's download.com site.  Hope this helps someone else.
