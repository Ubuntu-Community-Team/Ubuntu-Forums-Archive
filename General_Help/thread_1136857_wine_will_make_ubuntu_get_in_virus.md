### Post by abyrne on 2009-04-25
It depends...

    If the virus was meant to run on wine and infect Ubuntu than yes, but it couldn't cause too much, if at all, damage due to Ubuntu's privilege control. If the virus was meant to be for Windows, it probably wouldn't do anything at all accept screw up your Wine directory, which can be fixed with a simple reinstallation of wine. Nothing really to worry about...
