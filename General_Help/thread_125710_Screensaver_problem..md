### Post by InsomniacUK on 2006-02-04
There are a number of screensavers that come with Ubuntu that take a screenshot of the desktop and overlay effects on it, such as ripples.

These don't seem to work on my box, instead of taking a screenshot of the desktop, the screensaver just shows something that seems like a TV test card. Is anyone else having this problem?

### Post by gila_monster on 2006-02-04
[QUOTE=InsomniacUK]There are a number of screensavers that come with Ubuntu that take a screenshot of the desktop and overlay effects on it, such as ripples.

These don't seem to work on my box, instead of taking a screenshot of the desktop, the screensaver just shows something that seems like a TV test card. Is anyone else having this problem?[/QUOTE]
Go to your screensaver preferences screen under the System:Preferences menu. Click on the Advanced tab. Make sure that "Grab Desktop Images" is selected under Image Manipulation on the upper left of that window.

Let me know if it works.
