### Post by Rui Pais on 2007-02-20
i'll try to answer.

#1 Is not planned. I read somewhere that that don't even pretend to implement that kind of thing. Most people just use other general systrays. Search google for some or synaptic for the ones that have packages for ubuntu. I read good things about one called alltray, never tried.

#2. Use a general file manager, thunar, rox, pcmanfm are light, or nautilus, konqueror, etc. Sometimes to have thinks look good it helps, if you also have gnome installed, launch gnome-settings-daemon on e17 start session. Don't know about kde/qt looks.

#3 You cannot set e17 cursors with FF or OO. You need to set the general cursor for all e in order to have coherent theme. Click on desktop (left button) and choose Configuration Panel -> Appearance->Mouse Cursor. Choose 'Use X cursor'. (Just a workaround... i know...)
