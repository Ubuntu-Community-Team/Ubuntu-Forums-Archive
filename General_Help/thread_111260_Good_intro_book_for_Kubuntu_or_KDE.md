### Post by Jengu on 2006-01-02
DON'T BUY THAT BOOK

It's from 1999. That book covers KDE v1.0! We're up to 3.5 now!

Post on the forums if you have problems. If you're really dedicated to helping, go to [www.kde.org](www.kde.org) and sign up for the KDE-Usability mailing list. Generally, if you need a *book* to figure out what you want to do, it's not designed well enough.
