### Post by Vaphell on 2014-04-27
you mean these [ home dir ][ dir1 ][ dir2 ] next to [<][>]?
If yes, then these are not tabs but breadcrumbs that allow for easy backtracking.
To get proper tabs you'd have to open subdir with a middleclick, but then it would be perfectly closable with **x** in the tab
