### Post by cilynx on 2007-01-14
F2, del, and/or ESC

All common keys to get into BIOS.  Lots of times, keys / combos work without anything flashing up on the screen telling you that they will.

---

### Post by meng on 2007-01-14
Agree - if F9 and F10 don't work, then they aren't the BIOS-entry keys. Any other F-key, esc or del are common options.
