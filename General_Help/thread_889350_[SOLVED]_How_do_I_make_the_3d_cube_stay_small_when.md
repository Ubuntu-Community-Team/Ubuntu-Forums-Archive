### Post by sayakb on 2008-08-14
I'm not sure that can be done. Although, if you want other workspaces to be visible while not rotating, you may make the cube somewhat transparent while not rotating. To do so, open ccsm (Press Alt+F2 and type in **ccsm**), goto **Desktop Cube->Transparent Cube** taband change *Opacity when not rotating.*
