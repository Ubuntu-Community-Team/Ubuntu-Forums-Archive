### Post by aysiu on 2005-11-26
[QUOTE=SuperDiscoMachine V.5.7-3]try as I might I can't get used to the whole "using the command line", which I can do, it just seems harder.[/QUOTE] A few reactions I have to this:

1. It seems harder because you're not used to it. After a while, you'll realize it's powerful and easier in many ways. The power to be able to copy and paste commands instead of figuring out graphical menus is one way it's helpful.

2. Not all Linux distributions make heavy use of the terminal--even for installation and configuration. Take a look at Mepis, Blag, and Linspire.

3. You can [dual boot](http://users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/). You don't have to take the full plunge.
