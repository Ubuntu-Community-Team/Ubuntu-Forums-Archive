### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-07-05
EasyUbuntu, along with a great deal of other "helper script" programs aren't supported by Ubuntu or the Community.  It is most likely easier and less problematic to use one of the guides such as [http://help.ubuntu.com](http://help.ubuntu.com) or [http://www.ubuntuguide.org](http://www.ubuntuguide.org)  to find the steps to take to get the programs you need.  While they're easy in a lot of respects, they have been known from time to time to cause breakages that can be difficult to track down.
