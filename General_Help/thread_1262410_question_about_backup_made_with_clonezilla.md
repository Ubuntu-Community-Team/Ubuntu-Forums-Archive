### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-09-12
I really don't think making an installable iso, burning to dvd and the reinstalling is the best way to back up your system. However, if that's what you want, try remastersys. A better way of making a backup is simply imaging the system partition with Partimage or Clonezilla.
