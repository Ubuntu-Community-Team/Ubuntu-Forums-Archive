How does one remove these icons? If I right click there is no option to delete the icon, there is one for "unmount" but I dont want to unmount the drives, just not have the icons on my desktop. TIA

### Post by Bumbulum on 2006-01-07
1) right click desktop --> configure desktop
2) click "behavior" on side menu
3) click "device icons" tab
4) uncheck "mounted samba" and anything else you don't want to show up
