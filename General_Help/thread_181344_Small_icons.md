### Post by dmizer on 2006-05-24
no problem ... took me a while to figure it out too.  open nautilus (your directory browser) and click on "edit" and "preferences" <-- bottom of the list.

change your "Icon View Defaults" zoom level to your desired size.  you can watch your icons change size as you change your setting.  then hit close, and you're all done.
