### Post by Mike-97470 on 2007-02-03
To fix this (and by the way it seems to have just started occurring with the 6.10 update, at least for me):

Go to the Evolution "Help" menu--- /Contents/Contacts:The Address Book/Organizing your Contacts/Grouping with Categories............or just:

1. open a contact
2. click "Categories"
  --see the Master Category List displayed
3. highlight (select) "Anniversary"
4. click "Delete"
5. That's All!  -Now it works.

If you really want an Anniversary category create a new category mis-spelled so that it comes after "Any Category" alphabetically.  zzzzzzzzzzzAnniversary should work.
