### Post by unamanic on 2009-11-06
Linux and Unix do things a little different than Windows, rather than store the files by application, it stores them by type.  The executable files are generally kept in /usr/bin while the graphics, documentation, etc. are in /usr/share  while libraries are in /usr/lib  There are also some system wide configurations in /etc
