### Post by ian-weisser on 2016-11-07
Ubuntu seems a poor choice for this kind of kiosk.
Ubuntu is designed to be easy to install, easy to learn, full-featured, and secure.
You seem to want something that is neither full-featured nor secure.

I think that it's generally easier to build up from a minimal system than to remove or workaround many components from a robust system.
That requires skills you may or may not have yet. It may be easy for you, it may not.
