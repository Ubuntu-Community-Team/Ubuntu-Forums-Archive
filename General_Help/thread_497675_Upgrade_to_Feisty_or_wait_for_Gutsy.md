### Post by Talon2 on 2007-07-10
If your current installation of Dapper is working fine then I'd recommend waiting till Gusty is released.

I do maintain an extra partition on two of my systems to do test version installs so as to report any problems in hopes issues will be fixed before final release.  So if you are up for testing you might consider a dual boot and test Gutsy on the 2nd os partition.

Fiesty has not worked as well on several of my systems as did Dapper and Edgy.  Power management in Fiesty seems to be probablematic.  I have a Thinkpad T23 that worked perfectly under Dapper but is broken all over the place with Fiesty... video driver, power management, etc.
