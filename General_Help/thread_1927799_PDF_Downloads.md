> **2burke said:**

The file is not actually in the download dialog; that is just a log of what has been downloaded.  If you did not save it from document-viewer or choose to save in the box that sometimes appears with the options "Open" or "Save", the file just downloaded to /tmp and then disappeared when you closed the browser page or rebooted.
