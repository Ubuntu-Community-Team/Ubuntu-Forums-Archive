### Post by flipper T on 2011-07-13
the skipping could be caused at any of 4 stages:

1 errors / scratches on the original cd

2 errors in ripping

3 errors in burning

4 scratches on destination cd


if you rip a cd to your hard drive & it plays ok from there, it will play ok from a flash drive.

if it skips on your hard drive, try ripping more slowly.

ps play cd without ripping...does it skip ?

### Post by Slim Odds on 2011-07-13
Use Exact Audio Copy: [http://www.exactaudiocopy.de/](http://www.exactaudiocopy.de/)

It runs fine in WINE. You can set it to be as thorough as you like.

There is also a device called SkipDr® that works really well at fixing scratched discs (there are other tricks that you can use as well): [http://www.digitalinnovations.com/disc-cleaning-repair/fix-scratched-discs/](http://www.digitalinnovations.com/disc-cleaning-repair/fix-scratched-discs/)
