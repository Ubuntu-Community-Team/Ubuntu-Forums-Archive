### Post by kamstrup on 2004-11-02
When I mount something in /media, I automatically get icons for it on the desktop. That is all very nice.

The problem is that I really like the "no icons on desktop"-thing Ubuntu has going on. Is there a way I can make my mounts only show up under "Computer", and /not/ on the desktop?

---

### Post by sassur on 2004-11-02
Open Configuration Editor (Applications->System tools->Configuration Editor) and go to /apps/nautilus/desktop and uncheck volumes_visible

---

### Post by kamstrup on 2004-11-03
Thanks. Works fine :)
