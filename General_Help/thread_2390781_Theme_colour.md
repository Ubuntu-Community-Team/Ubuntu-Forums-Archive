### Post by dino99 on 2018-05-02
Each theme use its own builtin colours; so 'appearance' can only be changed by switching themes via gnome-tweak tool.
the good news : select 'extra' themes (at your own risk as usually said)  [https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+ppas?name_filter=theme](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+ppas?name_filter=theme)

### Post by cruzer001 on 2018-05-02
