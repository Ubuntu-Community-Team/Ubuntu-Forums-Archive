### Post by Ubnuut on 2007-11-10
sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.conf_backup

If X fails, you can just copy the backup back agian by doing
sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf_backup /etc/X11/xorg.conf

and then do:  start x

You could also try 
sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg  
to try to detect your monitor
