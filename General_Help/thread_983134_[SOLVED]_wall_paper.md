### Post by lukjad on 2008-11-15
Just download it to your desktop, or to a folder you like to put wallpapers, then right click the desktop, and choose Change Desktop Background. 

Then click Add, and drill to the file and select it. Then click Open and it should appear on you desktop.

Hope that helped!
