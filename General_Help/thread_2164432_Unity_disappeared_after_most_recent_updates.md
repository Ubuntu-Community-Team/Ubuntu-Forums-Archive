### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-07-31
Are you still using the development release? Perhaps you should switch to 12.04.2 LTS if breakage in the development release is unacceptable to you. Are you using a proprietary video driver? Find a driver that works and do not update it. In Software Sources make sure that Proprietary drivers for devices (restricted) does not have a tick mark. Updates to proprietary video drivers often break things in my opinion.
