### Post by lisati on 2008-10-04
Like a television set to "stand by" instead of being turned off at the wall, they'll probably be using a small amount of power - "mounting" and "un-mounting" these days generally has more to do with providing a connection between the OS and the hardware than the hardware's physical presence.

The only way I know of for sure that prevents a device from using power is to completely disconnect it from everything, including its power supply.
