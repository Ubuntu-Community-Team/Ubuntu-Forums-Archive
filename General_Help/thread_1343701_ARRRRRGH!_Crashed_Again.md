### Post by u.b.u.n.t.u on 2009-12-02
I am a bit puzzled why you would want to modify your BIOS? Once setup it hardly needs to be given a second thought.

Installing a printer driver ought not result in the error message "Operating system not found".

You mention a partition error occuring too.

Perhaps starting with some basics. If you are happy to reinstall Ubunu 9.10 then that may be a good place to start.

Review the BIOS that it is correct and then best leave it alone.

Once you have Ubuntu 9.10 up and running, test drive it. Make sure it is fine.

Then revisit the printer challenge. Now I would think that the printer will either work or not work and such will be dependent upon the existence of a relevant driver.
