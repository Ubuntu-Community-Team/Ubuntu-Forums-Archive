### Post by dragonfly41 on 2023-05-03
There is a Ubuntu package Bluetooth Manager - a GUI which I use to "pair" devices and "connect".
There is a launcher icon in top toolbar. Colour blue.This is all you need.
The status of device connection is shown on right hand column of GUI.

I can connect to several devices including Ubuntu <--> Samsung tablet.

Another option instead of Bluetooth you can share using Dropbox account.

For high security I share using Tresorit.com account.  Keep (free, basic) Tresorit account in your Ubuntu and then post secure links to your iPhone (with passwords per asset). You can also revoke such access. You can monitor who accessed what asset at what date/time.  Tresorit does not need OpenVPN.
