There's a few possibilities:
- You can't connect to the peer, or it couldn't connect to you. Make sure you get a High ID (setting up port forwarding and so on) if possible.
- You are connected to a source, but you're on its queue. Basically, you have to wait it out until it's your turn for the peer to start uploading to you.
