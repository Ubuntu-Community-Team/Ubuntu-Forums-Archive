### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-05-17
12.04 has recently lost its support, so to make life easier and safer for you, you should install a supported version; I suggest 16.04.

The repositories for 12.04 will either already be moved out of the normal server site address, or will soon be moved, making downloading of packages very much more difficult.  Any packages from the new address will also no longer be upgraded so security will quickly be compromised.
