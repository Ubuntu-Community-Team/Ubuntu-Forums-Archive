### Post by Xiong Chiamiov on 2009-03-01
You don't need a new cd to update to the next version.  Just change hardy to jaunty in /etc/apt/sources.lst and do a
```

sudo apt-get dist-upgrade

```

It sounds like you really want a rolling-release system, like [Arch](http://archlinux.org).  Be forewarned, though, it takes more effort than Ubuntu.

You should also note that:
a) Devs don't frequent the forums, so your advice on the system will never be heard by the people who can change it.
b) Nobody's really going to be that concerned if you leave, get angry, or whatever.  You're not paying for it, in any way, and most Linux developers are concerned with making something that works better for them.  Giving it to others is a side thing.
