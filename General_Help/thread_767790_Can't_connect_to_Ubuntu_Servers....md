### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-04-25
Try going to System->Administration->Software Sources and click the drop down box for Download from:
Click Other and Select Best Server.
Let it run, close the Software Sources, let it do the update, and then you should be good to go.  The servers are just getting hammered since Hardy just got released.  Only time will resolve the issue.
