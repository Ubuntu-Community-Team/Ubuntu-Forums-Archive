### Post by mc4man on 2013-01-08
It would depend on your theme & or compositor, neither of which you've mentioned.

In unity-3d with light-themes the size of the border, (default 1px) has no real bearing on grabbing the edge. I use ambiance borderless (0 px ) & grabbing an edge is quite simple, it's done automatically as cursor approaches.

If you are using metacity with a light-theme then the 1px could be difficult as you  have to 'grab' the actual edge. 
As mentioned try another theme or increase the border size, 2 px may prove better without becoming too ugly.
