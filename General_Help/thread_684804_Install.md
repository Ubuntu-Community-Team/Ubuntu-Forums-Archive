### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-02-01
You can download the LiveCD for Ubuntu here - [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download)
Once in the live session, you can ever recover data from your windows partition if needed by mounting it + an extra drive like an external hard drive or a USB flash drive and moving over the important files.
If that interests you, let me know and I'll walk you through it, otherwise just installing is pretty straightforward.
When you install it will ask you about partitions - you really only need two.  1) SWAP should be 1.5x to 2x your RAM capacity and 2) the rest you can use for the root partition ("/").
DL the LiveCD and let me know if you have any other questions or problems.
