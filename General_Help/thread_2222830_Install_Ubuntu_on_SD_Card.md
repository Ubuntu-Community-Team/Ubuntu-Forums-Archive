### Post by sandyd on 2014-05-08
Note that SD Cards have limited read/write cycles. This produces an issue if you ever start swapping or have a runaway log file, as the card will quickly run out of writes.
The good thing is that even when the write cycles are gone, the read cycles are unlimited and the contents of the card can still be read.
