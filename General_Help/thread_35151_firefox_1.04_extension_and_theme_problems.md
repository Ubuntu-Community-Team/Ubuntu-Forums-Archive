### Post by bored2k on 2005-05-17
> but I can't downgrade for I will no longer be able to gain access to the extensions and themes False.

Downgrade to Ubuntu's Firefox, then in the location bar type 
about:config
Then search for 
general.useragent.vendorSub

And change 1.0 to 1.0.4. That is it.
