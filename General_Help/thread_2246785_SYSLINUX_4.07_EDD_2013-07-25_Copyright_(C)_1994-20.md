### Post by yancek on 2014-10-03
That message should show on any bootable Linux medium using syslinux as a bootloader.  If you don't see anything after that message, then something went wrong when you put your Linux distribution on the USB.  What software did you use to put it there?  Did you do an mdt checksum of the iso to verify the download after you downloaded it?
