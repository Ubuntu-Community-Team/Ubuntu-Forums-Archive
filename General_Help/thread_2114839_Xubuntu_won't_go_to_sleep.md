### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-02-11
That depends on what you mean by the machine going to sleep, and I do not know that machine to know what should show, as far as lights are concerned.

When my machines go to "sleep" (suspend) rather than hibernate, the power lights flash, and only if I hibernate will the light disappear completely.  Perhaps when you say "sleep" you really want it to hibernate, and  you should be able to set that in the power-management dialogue box.
