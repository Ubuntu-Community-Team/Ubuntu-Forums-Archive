### Post by elpescador on 2005-10-01
As far as I know, Evolutions supports read-only iCal files. There's a bounty of 2000 Dollars out there for the first person to make evolution publish Calendar files... but just reading the calendar file is easy: Click on "New Calendar" and then select "Remote Calendar" or something - and you get it, nice and nifty, even with a special color for only that calendar.
