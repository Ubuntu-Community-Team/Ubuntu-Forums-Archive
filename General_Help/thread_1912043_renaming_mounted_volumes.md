### Post by hotshot247 on 2012-01-19
in the nautilus file manager where you see your hard drives like for example "1TB filesystem", "500GB filesystem", etc, is their a way to change those names to whatever i want just so i know which hard drive is which? i have like 5 hard drives on the same computer and a couple of them are named 500 GB filesystem and i just want to rename it to something like "backups" or something like that. is that possible? thanks for the help

### Post by papibe on 2012-01-19
Hi hotshot247.

Open 'Disk Utility', then locate your external drive. Press 'Unmount Volume'. A new option will appear called 'Edit Filesystem Label'. Press it and change the label to what you want. Press 'Mount Volume' to mount the drive again.

Hope it helps.
Regards.
