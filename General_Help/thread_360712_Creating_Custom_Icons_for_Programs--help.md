### Post by barney_1 on 2007-02-13
The images that you find on the internet are someone's intellectual property.  You usage rights to that image are different depending on the person who owns the IP.  So, technically you need permission to use each picture, in writing or through a terms of use statement, from each IP owner.

That being said, if this is for personal use, no money is changing hands, and you don't plan to redistribute any of the photos the IP owners probably wouldn't care.  But that doesn't make legal either.
