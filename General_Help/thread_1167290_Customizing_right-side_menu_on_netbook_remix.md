### Post by cody7002002 on 2009-05-22
Is it possible to edit the the directories that show up on the right side menu on ubuntu netbook remix? The default ones are "Home, Desktop, Network, Documents, Music, Pictures, Videos"

---

### Post by Brandon Williams on 2009-05-23
That is a representation of the Places sidebar that you see in nautilus. If you open nautilus, you can add or remove items from the Places sidebar, which will change the listing of items that shows up in that column in the netbook-launcher.
