### Post by Dadsgé on 2008-12-16
hmm, looks like you're new to linux?

first, i didn't see the video, because my flashplayer isn't installed yet :P
second, the awnsers
Q1: to change the background, just rightclick on the desktop and 'change desktop background', the rest is rather clear i think

Q2:
1 the water effect is included in compiz, so if you already installed it, you just need to go to 'system' > 'preferences' > 'advanced desktop....' (usually the first one)
then just scroll down till you see 'water effect' and enable it
i think the hotkey was 'shift + F8', but you'll have to check it

2 you probably just installed a window theme
you'll have to search for an icon theme, at gnome.looks you'll find what you need ;)

3 i assume u mean there is a launcher in the top panel? just rightclick on the panel, 'add to panel' and then choose 'application launcher', next, and then you just have to browse trough the menu.

i hope this will help :)
