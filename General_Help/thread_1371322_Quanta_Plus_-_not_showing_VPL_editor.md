### Post by itsols on 2010-01-05
After digging in a bit, I discovered that the VPL issue was indeed a bug with Quanta Plus. I even came across some cases that explained that it takes 'almost forever' to see the design or VPL view of a page.

Strangely thought I had this problem with the 'more serious' pages that I've done with php. As for the smaller pages, I don't think there's an issue.

The bottom line... I can't find a near-match to my old dreamweaver 8, leave a side equivalent. Everytime this issue comes up, groups of people argue about the need for a design view. But the truth is that if you need to work easily and fast and need to create a few layouts/templates, the design view is a must. Let's not debate over this. But as for coding, who cares even if we had to do it on text editor.

Having said that, it was a breeze setting up DW8 on my Ubuntu 8.04 laptop. Just two easy steps.

1. On package manager: locate WINE, select to install, Click apply.

2. Put the DW8 CD and it sets up the program just like you would on Windows.

Now I have my favourite OS with my favourite design/PHP development tool!

Happy coding and bravo to my old DW8! Also many thanks to the folks of Ubuntu and WINE!
