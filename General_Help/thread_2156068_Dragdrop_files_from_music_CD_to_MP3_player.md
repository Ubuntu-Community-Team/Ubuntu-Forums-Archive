### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-06-20
Music CDs contain a totally different type of data than your MP3 player can recognise. You need to "rip" the music from the CD into MP3 format and then drag the MP3s to the player.

In Rhythmbox, go into the Preferences and the Music tab. Select the option to import music in "MP3 format".

Insert your CD and when it appears at the left of the Rhythmbox window, right click it and choose Import. When that is done, close Rhythmbox and go into the Music folder in your home folder. Drag the new folder full of MP3s, to your Sansa.
