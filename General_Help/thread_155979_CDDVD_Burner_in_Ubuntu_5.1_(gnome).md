### Post by damu on 2006-04-06
Gnome baker works really well for me, and it is just 2 clicks from you.
Just go to Applications/Add Apllications
You will find Gnome baker in the Sound & Vidoe Section, in "more programs"

Once you found it , just tick it, and click apply...let Ubuntu do the rest!

That's it, Gnome baker is available now in your Sound & Video section.

No need, to go on Sourceforge, command line or anything like that...so easy!

Enjoy
