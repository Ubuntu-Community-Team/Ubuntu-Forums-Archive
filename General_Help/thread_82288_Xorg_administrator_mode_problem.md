The problem is already known here - when you try to get to the Administrator Mode in KDE or GNOME you encounter problem with the password - sometimes it says that the password is invalid, sometimes "communication with su faild". I have been installing both 5.04 and 5.10 with the same result. BUT then I realized that I'm always choosing "expert" mode during installation. The next time I just pressed ENTER without typing expert, and after installation everything was OK with administrator mode. 

So here is the problem. It still doesn't explain anything, but at least it shows where to look for.
