### Post by lilguy43uk on 2009-09-11
Just a general question really. When I use Norton Ghost on my Jaunty installation I get the option to image one of three partitions to DVD.

Is that all I need to do or do to make a backup of the drive or do the other two partitions need to be included?

---

### Post by akakingess on 2009-09-11
Depends on what the other 2 partitions are. For instance, if you dual-boot Windows with Jaunty then no, because the other 2 partitions are going to be for your Swap for Jaunty and then the 1 for Windows, really, you should make sure what the other 2 partitions are and what you want to back up/image.

Edit: If you want to back up the whole drive irregardless, then you should do everything EXCEPT for the swap partition (which should be the smallest of the three).
