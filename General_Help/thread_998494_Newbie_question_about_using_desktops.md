### Post by PocketDog on 2008-12-01
Are you just using shift, ctrl and arrow keys? Use shift, ctrl and hold the left mouse button (you can let go of the two keys once you've pressed the mouse button) to spin the cube up and down. You can't have desktops on the top and bottom, only static images.
