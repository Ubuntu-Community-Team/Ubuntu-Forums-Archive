### Post by tredegar on 2011-03-21
I gave up with KDE4 (I liked KDE3), but you could take a look at the **/usr/share/sounds/** directory. Find the sound you hate (might take some time), and either delete it, or replace it with a sound you like better, but with the same name as the original.
