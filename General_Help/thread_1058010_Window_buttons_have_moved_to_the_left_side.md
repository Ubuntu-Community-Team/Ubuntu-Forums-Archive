### Post by clawlor on 2009-03-26
It is a hidden metacity option. Here's how to change it back:

1. open gconf-editor
2. Go to apps->metacity->general
3. change the value of button_layout to menu:minimize,maximize,close

### Post by davidshudy on 2009-10-14
Ubuntu 9.04 location on my computers is under the 
".gconf/apps/metacity/general/%gconf.xml" of the user id I am logged in under. 
For those whom are less familiar with Linux and are looking from the root section or the beginning of your file system it is located under the 
"File system/Home/(username)/.gconf/apps/metacity/general/%gconf.xml" 
From your Applications menu go to Accessories and open the Text Editor and then go to the above location from there and make the adjustment noted by previous poster and log out and log back in again. Hope that helps someone else out there.
