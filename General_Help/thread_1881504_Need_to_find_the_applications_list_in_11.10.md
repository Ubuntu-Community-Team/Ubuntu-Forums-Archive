### Post by Larkspur on 2011-11-16
Press the Super key and "a" at the same time, or click the button at the top of the launcher bar (the one with the Ubuntu logo on),  then click "More Apps" in dash that should come up.

### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-11-16
Once you open the Dash you should see 4 icons along the bottom of the Dash. From left to right they represent Search Home, Search Applications, Search files and folders, Search Music Collection.

You should also type Help in the Dash search bar and read the ubuntu Desktop Guide.
