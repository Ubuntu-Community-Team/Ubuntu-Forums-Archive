### Post by HanDez on 2011-06-15
Perhaps you moved them to another folder without realizing it. Use the search function on your drive by typing the name of a torrent file in , this should find them if they are on your drive anywhere.

As for the best torrent client: Hands down and absolute best is TIXATI.
It is fast and very configurable, especially fast in Ubuntu. Extremely easy and no fuss to use.
My favorite functions are the ability to specify which files you want to download within any given torrent itself at the exclusion of others you dont want, this is very easy to do with TIXATI when the pop-up window appears to start your download.

 Also, "local IP address binding", this is especially important if you want to use a VPN connection with your client, indispensable actually. Just type the local IP of the VPN in the last box on the bottom in the network connections settings and TIXATI will not connect to any other even if connection to internet is reset, which may often happen. 

TIXATI is not in the Ubuntu software center, but you can easily get the .deb package installer by going to their website, use this one "tixati_1.61-1_i686.deb",  just download and install.

If you need more detailed advice on how to do this consult the Tixati "Help & Support" or "Forum" on the Tixati webpage.
