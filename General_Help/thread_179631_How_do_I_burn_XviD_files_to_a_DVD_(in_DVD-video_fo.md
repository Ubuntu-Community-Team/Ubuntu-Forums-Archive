### Post by unnes on 2006-05-21
Ive used the Tovid GUI to do this. I'm not sure whether it's in one of the multiverse repos, but you can find info/source here:

[http://tovid.wikia.com/wiki/Main_Page](http://tovid.wikia.com/wiki/Main_Page)

I've tried several all-in-one tools, and nothing's as easy as Tovid.
