### Post by benj1 on 2009-07-02
[gnome look]("http://gnome-look.org/") is probably the main site for stuff like that.

just down load a theme, go to system-->preferences-->appearance, and click install.

what do you mean by changing the app bar, do you mean like gnome do? in that case check to repositories.
otherwise right click on it and select properties. you can change the colour and transparency etc.

have a look through system-->preferences it has a few options for modifying things
