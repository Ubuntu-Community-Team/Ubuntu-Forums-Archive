### Post by drbono on 2011-03-05
At startup, my display is shifted to the left several inches off of my screen.  I have an Element brand 26" LCD TV with VGA being used as my monitor.  I have an Nvidia Geforce 8400GS video card.  I am using the "recommended" version of Nvidia driver.  The only way I can get the display back is after startup to open up Nvidia X server settings, change the resolution to something different, hit apply.  The screen will then recenter itself with new resolution.  I then have to go back and select the resolution I desire at startup 1024 x 768, hit apply, and I am good to go.

Any thoughts?

### Post by drbono on 2011-04-19
In conclusion, the LCD TV is apparently the culprit.  However, I did find a solution.  Install "Startup-manager" from Synaptic package manager, this gave me the option of configuring my resolution during boot screens.  I set to 1024 x 768, which is what I am using normally and the screen is properly centered upon startup!
