### Post by Crooksey on 2008-02-29
GTK are themes for applications.

Metacity, Beryl and Compiz are window border themes, depending on what you are using, by the looks of it, you need compiz themes.

You need GTK themes, to change the look of applications.

To change the icon size, open the file manager, change the zoom level for icons in the prefrences section.
