### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-03-23
When you first connect the Incredible to your PC via USB cable, you should get a menu on the droid asking you what to do.  Select the Disk Drive option and press the button at the bottom of the screen.

Until you do that, your PC won't see the droid.
