### Post by LowSky on 2010-03-04
> **annie71307 said:**
> Then why there is virtual box?

Then use Virtual box, but with that you are actually running on OS within the other. You lose functionality like Video card support and a few other things, and you need lots of hard drive space and RAM for the other machine.

So unless you have 4GB of RAM or more, and a hard drive that can easily spare between 20-50GB of space (depending on the OS in the VM), forget about it.
