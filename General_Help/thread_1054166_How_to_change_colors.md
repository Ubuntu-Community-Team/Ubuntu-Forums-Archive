### Post by mcduck on 2009-01-29
I suppose you have learned about being able to pick your own wallpapers and themes during those 10 years with SUSe. Because I *know* that's possible in SuSe as well. ;) You can get themes from [http://gnome-look.org/]("http://gnome-look.org/"). Personally I like the brown. Ubuntu doesn't use the root account, administration is down using "sudo". Reading this will help you: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RootSudo]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RootSudo"). Please don't as how to enable root, forum rules don't allow helping people to do that. (The idea behind this is that anybody who doesn't know how to do it probably shouldn't be using root anyway..)

### Post by steveneddy on 2009-02-01
Ubuntu 10.4?

That means that you have a version that is two years older than anything that has been released yet. No wonder you hate it.

I suppose that you actually meant Ubuntu 8.04 Hardy Heron.

To change the system colors:

Right click on the desktop

From Menu select "Change Desktop Background"

Up top click the "Theme" tab

On the bottom click the "Customize" button

This is where you change the look of the radio buttons and check boxes, system colors, window borders, icons and pointers.

All of this is customizable on every Gnome desktop, even in Suse.

More themes, icons, pointers and window borders can be downloaded from:

[www.gnome-look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org")

[art.gnome.org]("http://art.gnome.org")

Good luck.

You can go to images.google.com and type in wallpaper and you screen resolution to get wallpapers that should fit your display.

EXAMPLE:

[URL="http://images.google.com/images?hl=en&q=wallpaper+1280x800&btnG=Search+Images&gbv=2"]wallpaper 1280x800
[/URL]
Click the link for 1280x800 wallpapers.

To get a look that I wanted I added icon packs, altered colors, found a Firefox theme, firefox icon theme, window borders, panel background images, changed the Nautilus wallpaper and added a small library  of wallpapers from my collection.

Just spend a Sunday afternoon playing around and you will get something that you want eventually.
