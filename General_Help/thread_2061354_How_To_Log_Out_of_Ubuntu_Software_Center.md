### Post by tshack on 2012-11-26
Hi amtur_poet,

Currently, to log out of Software Center you need to delete its key from "Passwords and Keys."

To do this:

[1] Hit the Windows/"Super Key" to open the dash
[2] Type "passwords"
[3] Open the "Passwords and Keys" application
[4] Expand the "**Passwords**:login" entry
[5] Locate the "Ubuntu Software Center" entry
[6] Right click and select Delete
