I have a bunch of old podcast MP3s from my Windows and iTunes days and I'm looking to import them into Rhythmbox. The thing is it won't drag and drop into my library, in fact it won't even play in Rhythmbox even when I double click the MP3 file itself.

### Post by NoBugs! on 2011-07-05
Copy the files to home/Music or wherever your rhythmbox media directory is, it should show up in Rhythmbox.

---

### Post by Alwimo on 2011-07-06
And to make them play in Rhythmbox when you click on the icon, right-click on it and choose Rhythmbox. You can set it as the default by clicking on 'Properties' after right-clicking on it and going to one of the tabs there.
