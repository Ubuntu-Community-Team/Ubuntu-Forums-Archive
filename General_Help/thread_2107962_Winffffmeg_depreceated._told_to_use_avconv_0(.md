### Post by SeijiSensei on 2013-01-23
The "deprecated" message is misleading and should be ignored.  Most of these issues reflect political conflicts between the original FFmpeg developer and the people who forked it to create libav.  [This article]("http://blog.pkh.me/p/13-the-ffmpeg-libav-situation.html"), linked to above by rpm13, gives the details.  You might also want to look at  [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2096866").
