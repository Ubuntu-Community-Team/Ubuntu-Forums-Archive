### Post by LinuxFan999 on 2011-11-26
I would not recommend buying a Dell/HP/Lenovo/ect. machine, then installing Ubuntu on it right away for these reasons.
 
1. Some OEMs will void your warranty if you install Linux on your computer.
 
2. You will have to pay for Windows (and deal with it too, untill you download and install Ubuntu, unless you already have a CD beforehand), and it is very difficult to get a refund for Windows.
 
Only buy an OEM system if you don't have the skills to build your own computer, or if you don't know about System76 or Zareason.
 
Advantages of building your own computer:
 
1. you can choose exactly what components you want.
 
2. The cost is sometimes less than Buying from an OEM.
 
3. You don't have to pay for Windows if you don't want it.
 
Disadvantages of building your own computer:
 
1. No tech support.
 
Advantages of buying from System76 or Zareason
 
1. More likely to get compatible hardware.
 
2. Tech support
 
Disadvantages of buying from System76 or Zareason
 
1. Less choice of components.
 
2. Can't pick up your computer in a store.
