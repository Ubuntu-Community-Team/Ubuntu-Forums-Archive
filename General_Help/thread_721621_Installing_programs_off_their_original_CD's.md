### Post by eriqjaffe on 2008-03-11
You have three choices (in order of speed & guaranteed ability for your game to work):

1) Dual booting.  Boot into Windows for the games.
2) Virtual Machine (VMWare, etc).  Run Windows in there for your games.
3) Wine.  Works for some games, not for others.  Might be worth a try, at least.
