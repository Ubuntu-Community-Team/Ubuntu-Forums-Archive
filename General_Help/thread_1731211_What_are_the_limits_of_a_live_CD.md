### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-04-16
You'll be able to read your hdd and copy to a different media, yes. If you have a separate /home partition that contains your music you can actually install over the root partition(/) and point your /home partition to the existing one and not have to backup anything.

You can also copy your music to a temporary hdd, then copy it back if you don't have a separate /home partition.

If this doesn't answer your question - could you be more specific please, where is your music, what kind of backups, what backup program, etc.
