### Post by Slushdoot on 2008-03-31
Though not a book, I found that listening to Linux podcasts was a great help.  My favorite instructive one is Linux Reality by Chess Griffin.  He begins at the most basic level talking about Linux in general.  If you're new to Linux I reccomend it highly.

To download a podcast like that just go to the program's web site and look for the .mp3 or .ogg link.
