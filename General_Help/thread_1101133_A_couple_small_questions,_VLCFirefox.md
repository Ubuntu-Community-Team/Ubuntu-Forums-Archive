### Post by mb_webguy on 2009-03-20
I don't know about the MySpace thing, since I can log in just fine.

As for the address bar issue, type "about:config" in the address bar.  Don't worry about the message that comes up.  There isn't a "warranty".  It's just reminding you you're about to change some advanced settings.  Type "urlbar" in the search box.  Double-click the entries for "clickSelectsAll" and "doubleClickSelectsAll" to change their values to "true" and "false", respectively.
