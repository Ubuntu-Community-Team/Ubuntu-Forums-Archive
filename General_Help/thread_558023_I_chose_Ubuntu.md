### Post by AndyCooll on 2007-09-23
Well, apart from anything else Red Hat and Suse have been around for quite awhile longer and have a well-established track record on the server side. Their main focus as a company is the business end of the market, and they have produced for a number of years editions of their distro that are specifically aimed at businesses. Ubuntu's server edition is a relative newcomer to this area having only been around for the last year or so.
