### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-06-04
Gaim should already be installed on your computer which will allow you to chat through Google Talk (I'm presuming you are on Feisty). Pidgin is just the new name for Gaim.

The google page with instructions on how to do this is down atm, but just google gaim+gtalk and you should be ok.

I've never used Google Desktop, so I am not sure of all its features, but you can get a few different widget applications to show things on your desktop. screenlets, adesklets and gdesklets will all do this.

Evolution has mail, contacts, calendars and notes, and this integrates with the desktop clock in Gnome, so this may suit your needs. If you expand what you want a little more I may be able to help more.
