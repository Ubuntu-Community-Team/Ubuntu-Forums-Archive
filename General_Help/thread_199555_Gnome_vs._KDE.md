### Post by aysiu on 2006-06-19
If you have 128 MB of RAM, both Gnome and KDE will run fine on it... as long as you have only one program open. Two programs will slow it down considerably. Three programs will slow it down a lot and may, in fact, freeze up on you.

256 MB should be more than adequate for Gnome and KDE. 512 MB gives you decent working speed so that you can do whatever you want and not notice any slowness in either.
