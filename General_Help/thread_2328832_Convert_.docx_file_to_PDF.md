### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-06-24
You could try copy & paste of the contents of the document into a new Libreoffice document. And then with the appropriate Microsoft fonts installed you will be able to save the document as a Libreoffice document. When Libreoffice converts to PDF we can choose to embed the fonts into the document to preserve the formatting on whatever OS the document is read.
