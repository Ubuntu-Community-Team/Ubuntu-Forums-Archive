### Post by egalvan on 2009-09-04
This  is called "file association"

One place that you can see this is:

open "Nautilus" (file browser)

go to the "Edit" button 

open the "Preferences" button

this opens a window called "File Management Preferences"

open the "Media" tab

the tab contents should be fairly self-explanatory.
