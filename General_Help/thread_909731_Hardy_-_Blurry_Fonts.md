 Right click on your desktop, select change desktop background,  click on Fonts tab, select subpixel smoothing (LCD's), click on details, select slight. Make sure subpixel order is RGB. Font size of 96 is okay. Close. But you can adjust it, try 95, etc. This works the best for me on any distro of gnome. At 1280 x 1024 resolution, nvidia, Dell flat panel LCD.
