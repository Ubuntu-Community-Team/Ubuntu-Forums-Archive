### Post by sigmarhophi on 2008-02-29
A quick and graphical way of porting over to gnome programing using C++ is using QT4.  I would recomend QT Develop and QT designer.  One is the GUI designer and the other is the compiler and they work together quite nicely. And of course there is always a command line version of QT.

For PHP and other web programing, Anjuta and Glade will do you just fine.
