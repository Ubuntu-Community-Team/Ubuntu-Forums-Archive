### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-10-31
It should be fully supported, see [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/models/deskjet/deskjet_1120.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/models/deskjet/deskjet_1120.html) for all the details.  I assume you have hplip installed in your system, along with hplip-gui for the toolbox, but if for some reason you don't have them I suggest you install both.
