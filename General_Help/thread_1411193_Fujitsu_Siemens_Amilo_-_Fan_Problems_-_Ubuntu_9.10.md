Hey, I have also had this problem. I searched quite extensively and could not find a resolution to the fault, and as a result I have had return to using windows OS. :(

The problem with the Amilo is the fans are software driven, and support for these drivers is not currently available to my knowledge. The laptop is over heating after first over heating and will auto-reboot or crash once the core temperture is in excess of 90°C. Once rebooting the fans will keep running continously while the core temperture is greater then 54°C (I think).

I know that this isn't any help but at least it gives you an idea what is causing the problem. I'd like to return to using Ubuntu OS so hopefully a member of Ubuntu community can help us both.
