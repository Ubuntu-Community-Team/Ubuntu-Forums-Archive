### Post by unckybob on 2011-07-06
Since I upgraded my ThinkPad T40p to 11.04, I am having trouble mounting USB devices.

This is particularly with my SDHC card reader, my SanDisk Sansa Clip+ MP3 player, and all my external hard drives. I've tried attaching them after booting up and before booting up. Sometimes they mount, sometimes they don't. Mostly they don't.

Sometimes if I leave a hard drive hooked up for a long time, it mounts and then I see an icon for it on my desktop. Then, sometimes when I have them mounted, they become unmounted and the icon disappears from the desktop. Sometimes this happens in the middle of transferring files.

For the most of the time I can't get them to mount. If I need to transfer any files, I transfer them to my Windows partition under Windows then mount the Windows partition under Ubuntu. I know this isn't a hardware problem because I can transfer files under Windows.

Please help!

### Post by unckybob on 2011-12-24
After some research I learned that flexing of the motherboard sometimes loosens the solder contacts to the chipset causing intermittent problems of this kind. Will try to reflow the board.
