### Post by jimmy the saint on 2008-04-06
First, turn off the monitor. 
Second, HOLD the MENU button down and turn the monitor on.  Do not let go of the MENU button until the monitor is powered up completely.  This will unlock the OSD menu.
[ftp://ftp.software.ibm.com/systems/support/system_x_pdf/t120_user_guide.pdf]("ftp://ftp.software.ibm.com/systems/support/system_x_pdf/t120_user_guide.pdf")
