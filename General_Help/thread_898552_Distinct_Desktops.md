### Post by jkyahoo101 on 2008-08-23
To get 4 different wallpapers hit alt & F2 and type gconf-editor. Then click apps, nautilus, and then preferences. Then uncheck the show_desktop box. If you have Compiz 7.6 you will be able to use the plugin wallpapers. If you don't have 7.6 I think you will need to get it. The only bad part about this is that you can't have icons or right click on your desktop. But if you use docks that isn't really a problem.

As for the different themes it is not possible.
