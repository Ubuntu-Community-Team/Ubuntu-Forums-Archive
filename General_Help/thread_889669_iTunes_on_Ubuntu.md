You could look for another 3 weeks and not find it--Apple does not support Linux users, not even using Wine. :(

There are, however, many excellent music players such as Rhythmbox, Amarok, Banshee, etc. etc. etc.

The only catch is that none of them can play music you've purchased through the iTunes store. Those tunes are Digital Rights Managed, which means you can only play them using iTunes on a Mac or Windows (unless of course you remove the DRM :)).
