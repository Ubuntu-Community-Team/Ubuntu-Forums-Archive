### Post by Koant on 2008-11-26
Hi all,

Since I got my new laptop and installed Hardy Heron (64bit), I experienced a rather strange and extremely annoying behaviour: the system right-clicks by itself, seemingly at random.
For example, I would be typing a long text, and suddenly I end up somewhere in the middle of the editor window, as if I had right-clicked and decided to go wherever I had last left the cursor.
This is annoying to the extreme, I have to constantly make sure that I'm still writing at the right place or put the cursor somewhere that won't be affected by a right click beforehand, eg a gray area in the menu bar.

I have no idea as to what causes this. It's not like I accidently touch the notepad; it happens way too often and it doesn't happen on windows as far as I can tell. 

If you have any suggestions, please share, this thing is driving me crazy!

Make sure unticked "Pointer ....."

### Post by Koant on 2008-11-28
Well guess what? I've unticked '*Enable mouse clicks with touchpad*' from System/Mouse/Touchpad and I haven't got the problem since! So either the touchpad was sending random signals on a channel that is now shutdown, or I've just discovered that I've been developing Parkinson's (by clicking at random) and Alzheimer's (I don't remember touching the touchpad) since I got my new laptop.

Thanks again for your help!
