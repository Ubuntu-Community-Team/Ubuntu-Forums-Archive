### Post by plucky on 2009-04-11
Open **System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager** and search for **MFC-420cn** and two packages should appear.Install both.

The packages are ```
brother-cups-wrapper-extra
brother-lpr-drivers-extra
```


Good Luck

p.s. If your printer has a scanner,you have to go to the [Brother website](http://solutions.brother.com/linux/en_us/index.html) and download the .deb driver package and install.


Good Luck
