### Post by snowpine on 2009-08-22
Hi Maxshn, anyone who has a Live CD and physical access to your computer can compromise your security... There are a couple of things you can do to slow someone down, like encrypt the hard drive and create a BIOS password so someone can't boot from the CD drive. But like we say on these forums, "physical access is root access." Don't leave your computer lying around where strangers can use it. :)
