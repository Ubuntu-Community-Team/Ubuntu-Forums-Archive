### Post by oldfred on 2010-05-08
I do not think you can do what you want. Neither windows nor Ubuntu will update your existing installs and save your current data. 

An Ubuntu install will update some info from your windows but not from the Ubuntu. You can manually update 8.10 to 9.04 to 9.10 to 10.04 but it is a lot of work and clean install is better.

It is my understanding that win7 will upgrade Vista but deletes XP and reinstalls.

You will have to back up all your data and start as if it is a new hard drive which if the data is important a new hard drive may be the best choice.
