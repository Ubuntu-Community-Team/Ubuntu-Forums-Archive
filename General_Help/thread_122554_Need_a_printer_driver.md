### Post by extraspecialbitter on 2006-01-28
Thanks for the pointer!  

In checking out the [http://hpinkjet.sourceforge.net/]("http://hpinkjet.sourceforge.net/") site, I discovered that the driver for the HP DeskJet 5550 was considered a "generic" driver that would work for dozens of other printers, including my OfficeJet Pro K550.  I installed the HPIJS backend via Synaptic, then configured the printer to use the generic driver and everything worked!
