### Post by Toz on 2014-05-12
This is a known bug. See: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xfce4-power-manager/+bug/1303736]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xfce4-power-manager/+bug/1303736"). There is a workaround in that bug report that involves setting an xrandr command to a keyboard shortcut to get the screen to display again. This plus a couple of other issues has resulted in me switching back to xscreensaver until light-locker becomes more stable.
