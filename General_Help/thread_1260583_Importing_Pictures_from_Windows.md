### Post by Windows Nerd on 2009-09-07
There are several options:

1) Plug an external HD in and manually transfer all the photos you need to the HD. Then switch to Ubuntu. Plug HD back in. Transfer the photos back.

2) If you have multiple computers networked to each other, set up sharing (like the guy who posted above said) and transfer your photos to the second computer. Install Ubuntu. Set up networking and sharing again and transfer them back.

3) I believe at install time Ubuntu gives you an option to transfer settings and files from your previous installation, though I am not sure this is there, or how reliable this is.

I recommend option 1 or 2. Hope you like Ubuntu!
