### Post by LonelyAspie on 2011-03-12
My Mom's computer doesn't have a password set to login to the OS, since there isn't one set. I get a keyring asking for a password, since I didn't use one to enter the OS. I have to click close twice, to make it disappear.

I don't have this problem on my laptop, because I enter a password to enter the OS. How do I disable the keyring from showing up every time.

---

### Post by turtle153 on 2011-03-12
Under **System > Preferences > Startup ****Applications**, disable **Secret Storage Service** which is the Keyring component.
