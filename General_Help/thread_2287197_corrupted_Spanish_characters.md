### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-07-17
Hi and welcome.

Please add Spanish in your System Settings > Language Support. Reboot (or log out and log in again) and try again.
Note: This won't change your system language. That is always defined by the language in the top of the aforementioned list.
