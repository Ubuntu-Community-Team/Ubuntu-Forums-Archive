### Post by CatKiller on 2011-07-31
The widget layer can contain anything you like; screenlets, desklets, normal windows. You just configure that section of CCSM to put them on the widget layer.

Screenlets are probably a good place to start. The application and some of the screenlets are in the repositories, and there are many more, and themes for existing ones, on Gnome-look and other places. Have a look at some of the desktop customisation threads for inspiration.
