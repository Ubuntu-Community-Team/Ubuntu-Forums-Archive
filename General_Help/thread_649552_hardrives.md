### Post by indytim on 2007-12-25
One other thing to consider before you put too much time/effort into your project.  Depending upon the age of the hard drives and the manufacturer, you may be approaching (or surpassed :) ) the life-expectancy of the hard drives.  If the data you are planning on storing is of some importance, you may be better off "discarding" the old hard drives and replacing with a new one if that is an economic possibility.
