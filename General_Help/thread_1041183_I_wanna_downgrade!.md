### Post by indytim on 2009-01-16

### Post by ajcham on 2009-01-20
> **terrox said:**
> and can you reinstall without wiping your /home/ folder?
Yes, if home is on its own partition. Just select your existing partitions when you reinstall, and be sure not to format the /home partition.  If it is not, you can repartition your drive first and move /home to another partition (search the forums for instructions).

However, might it not be worth trying to actually solve the problem with your emulators rather than reinstalling?  I wouldn't be able to help much, but if you create a new thread I'm sure someone will be more knowledgeable.  FWIW, I can run Project64 in Wine reasonably well - and my computer is not particularly powerful. Also, I noticed no change in the program's performance when I upgraded to Intrepid.
