### Post by kubug on 2008-08-19
Hi, I am sorry but my post won't help you much, other than tell you that I know how you feel.
8 months ago I wanted to switch over to an ATI 3850 from an nVidia 8600GT, but found myself switching back pretty quickly because of ATIs WINE performance (and other issues). nVidia's binary drivers have a lot more support for wine currently. 
Hopefully soon this will change with ATI opening up their documents, but until then all I can recommend to you is switch to an nVidia card. That sounds a little crazy and may not be for you, but it made my life a lot easier. 
[http://www.phoronix.com]("http://www.phoronix.com") has a lot of information about this kinda stuff. They currently have some heavy topics regarding ATI there. 
Again, sorry for this post not being more informative. :neutral:
