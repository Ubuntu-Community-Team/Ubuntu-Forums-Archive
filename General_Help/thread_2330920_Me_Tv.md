### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-07-16
Why not use the built in autoscan function? MeTV doesn't need you to 'bring your own' channels.conf. If your TV device is working ok it will pick them up for you.

View> Channels> Add> Autoscan. First choice, top of the 'Add' page.
