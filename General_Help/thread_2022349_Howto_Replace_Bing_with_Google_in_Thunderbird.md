### Post by paulmilliken on 2012-07-10
Thunderbird 13.0.1 uses Bing as its default search engine for internet searching.  To replace Bing with Google (a much better search engine):

[LIST=1]
[*]Open Thunderbird and select "Add-ons" from the "tools" menu
[*]In the "search all add-ons" box at the top right, type "google"
[*]Find the add-on called "Google Search for Thunderbird" and click the "install" button to the right of that entry
[/LIST]
