### Post by rmcellig on 2008-11-02
What is the quickest way to quit all applications? Right now I go the panel at the bottom of my screen and close them manually one by one. There must be a quicker way than this?

---

### Post by sj1410 on 2008-11-02
if you want it fast the best way is Alt+F4 or Ctrl+W, you dont need to go to the panel. You can also force quit unresponsive and freezed application. Right click the panel and add "force quit". Also Ctrl+Alt+Backspace will kill all you running application, kills the X-server (quite efficiently, and with no confirmation) and brings you back to the login screen.

enjoy :)

### Post by OneRingShort on 2008-11-02
I usually just use Alt-F4.  Once the first is closed, the next window should automatically become active, then hit it again, and repeat.
