### Post by jocko on 2007-10-06
It's probably safer to download the beta than a daily build, although that will mean you'll have to get more updates from the repos.
Whichever you download, you will get all updates that have been released since the cd image was built, so the end result will be the same.
If I understand it correctly the daily builds are generated automatically, and may not always fit on a cd or may contain broken packages or bugs that makes the cd useless, while the beta cd was tested before it got the "beta" label.
