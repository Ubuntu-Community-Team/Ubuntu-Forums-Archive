### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-05-21
I think the problem is the encoder. If you are recording the videos with recordMyDesktop, then convert them from ogv to avi using mencoder or other transcoder before uploading to YouTube. This is the way I'm doing, since recordMyDesktop videos are totally messed up in Lucid Lynx.
