### Post by isecore on 2007-07-06
If you're going to wipe the box clean from Windows then Feistys importer won't do much good since it can't (as far as I know) import anything from something that's been wiped clean. However, I'm not sure about this since the only time I used it was when I put Ubuntu on my girlfriends computer, and even then we'd backed her stuff up on our server beforehand and therefore skipped the import option.

The easiest way would be to just save whatever stuff she wants to keep onto a thumbdrive or something and then import it back. I recommend the Export-option in Firefox for this thing, or you can go hunting in [windows-drive]:\Documents and settings\[username]\application settings\mozilla\firefox\etc etc etc and find the bookmarks.html-file there. That works as well but isn't quite as slick.
