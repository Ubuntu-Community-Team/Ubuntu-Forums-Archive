### Post by snowpine on 2011-12-05
Ubuntu bugs are documented here:

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu)

If you are worried about a particular bug, you can check whether it's been fixed yet or not. If you are worried about compatibility with your specific hardware, you can search to see if any bugs have been reported for your motherboard/video card/wireless/etc.

If you want the most stable possible Ubuntu then I recommend sticking with the Long Term Support (LTS) releases, currently 10.04.
