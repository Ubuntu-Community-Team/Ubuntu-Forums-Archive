### Post by janpol on 2011-01-13
I know this thread is a little old, but this is related to the following bug:

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/plasma-netbook/+bug/613836]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/plasma-netbook/+bug/613836")

Apparently this issue is related to the "Knowledge Base" Widget, so, removing this Widget solves the problem (or you can also get a valid opendesktop account).

In order to Remove the "Knowledge Base" widget you must go to Page One and close the Knowledge Base Widget by clicking on the cross at the upper right corner of the widget (it appears when you hover the mouse over it).
