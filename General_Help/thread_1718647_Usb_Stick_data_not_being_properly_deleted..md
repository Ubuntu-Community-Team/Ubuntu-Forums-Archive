### Post by PCNetSpec on 2011-03-31
Highlight the files you want to delete, and hit Shift+Delete keys... Gone.

Or enable "Delete" in nautilus:
**Edit>Preferences>Behavior** (tab) and put a tick in:
**Include a Delete command to bypass the Wastebasket.**

Right click options now include **Delete**.
