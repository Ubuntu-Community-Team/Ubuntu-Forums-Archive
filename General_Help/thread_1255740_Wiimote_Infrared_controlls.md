### Post by lexen on 2009-09-01
I got this to work with a wii homebrew program, but you need an infrared pen.  I didn't have one, so what I did was I had the wiimote look at the screen, then I used the sensor bar and moved it around my tv screen to draw.  When I wanted to not draw for a section, I covered up the light.  I would imagine it would be similar with the computer, but I don't know what program you would use, but it is possible without a projector.
