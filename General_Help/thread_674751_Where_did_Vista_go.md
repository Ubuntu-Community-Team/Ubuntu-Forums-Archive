### Post by LaRoza on 2008-01-22
Restore from your backup CD's...

5.10 is an old pre-Vista version of Ubuntu. It doesn't know about the NTFS that Vista uses, and Vista is destroyed. 

You can likely get your data off the partition with another tool, but you can't repair Vista easily (or at all).

Ubuntu 7.10 handles Vista fine in my experience.
