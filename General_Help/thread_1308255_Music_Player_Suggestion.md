### Post by laluz on 2009-10-31
Last time I looked up, the Amarok 1.4.10 was the only usable alternative to iTunes if to be used with iPod. I have configured it to work with MySQL as a backend and it handles about 30000 files acceptably fast.

The Gmusicbrowser for example only lists the iPod support in ToDo, and others could not do iPod ratings sync or were lacking other important features.

The problem with Amarok though is that its developers have all turned to develop the version 2 and have refused to do any support for the version 1. I do not know if Ubuntu 9.10 comes with the version 1 or two. Version 2 was not at a usable state for me at about half a year ago. 

I will be happy to learn if somebody have found an app that does more or less what Amarok 1.4 does and also supports iPod.
