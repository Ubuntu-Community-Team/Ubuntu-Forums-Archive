### Post by beno1990 on 2009-03-06
I'd say go for an Intel chipset.

All of Intel's drivers are open-source and actively developed by the Linux community.

Most of NVidia's drivers (if not all) are proprietary and have a history of causing problems on Linux because the NVidia drivers aren't always made as well as they should be.
