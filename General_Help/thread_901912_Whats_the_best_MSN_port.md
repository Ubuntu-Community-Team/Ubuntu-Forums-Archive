> **Prominence said:**
> Just wondering, because Pidgin doesn't seem to support webcams.

**For Audio and Video:**
Dynamic ports were chosen for the audio-video (AV) stream.... using dynamically allocated UDP ports in the range of 5004 to 65535.

**Application Sharing and Whiteboard:**
Whiteboard (WB) data uses port 1503...

**File Transfer:**
Both incoming and outgoing TCP connections use the range of ports from 6891 to 6900...

**Remote Assistance:**
TCP port 3389 is used for the TCP connection for the Remote Assistance protocol.
