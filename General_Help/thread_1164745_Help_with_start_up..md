### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-05-20
Try going into you bios settings with f2 at startup.  It looks like your computer is looking for a floppy drive. There should be a setting to disable this setting or set it to none.

There may also be a setting to skip the "post" which is what you are getting and load a startup graphic instead which will bypass the post and just give you an option to enter the bios (F2 or the del key).
