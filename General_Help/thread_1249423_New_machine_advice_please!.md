### Post by P4man on 2009-08-26
While fast enough to run ubuntu, I would recommend against the Atom, especially in a desktop case. The only saving grace of the Atom is its very low power consumption and extremely small size which makes it attractive for note/netbooks. Its performance is absolutely terrible though. Even considering the price.  Even at 1.6+ GHz an Atom is often slower than a 1 GHz Pentium 3 (!) and it will be beaten silly by any 7 year old Athlon 64 (or Sempron derivatives).

If you want cheap, go for an Athlon 64 or sempron or Core2 based celeron. Or pick something up on ebay.
