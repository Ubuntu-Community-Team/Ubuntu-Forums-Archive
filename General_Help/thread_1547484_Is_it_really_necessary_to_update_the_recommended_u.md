### Post by inameiname on 2010-08-06
Also, unlike Windows updates, which tend to accumulate to GBs upon GBs on top of what you are usually using on your computer, Ubuntu updates barely take up any more room than what they replace, so it's not as messy as Windows updates. Thus, not as big of an issue as far as worrying about them. Plus, if you are talking about updates from the official channels, as mentioned above, it's a good idea to maintain the most recent security and bug fixes and whatnot. 

Perhaps you could just update them every now and then, when you have a lot of time available to be online, such as leaving it on overnight every now and then, as suggested.
