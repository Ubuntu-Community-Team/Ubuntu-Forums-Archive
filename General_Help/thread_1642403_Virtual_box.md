Here is the official site:

[http://www.virtualbox.org/](http://www.virtualbox.org/)

As indicated, this has nothing to do with backing up your system but it is a fantastic piece of software for running guest OS images on your Ubuntu load. I run Windows as a guest on my desktop at home so that I can keep my iPhone up-to-date and sync podcasts and stuff, and it is almost flawless. Aside from the fact that I have Windows on my desktop. ;)
