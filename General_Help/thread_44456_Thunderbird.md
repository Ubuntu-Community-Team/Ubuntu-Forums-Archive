How can make thunderbird my default email client?

---

### Post by Xian on 2005-06-26
From the main panel in Gnome:

System > Preferences > Preferred Applications

Click on the 'Mail Reader' tab.
Tick the 'custom' box
Type in 'thunderbird %s' in the space provided.
