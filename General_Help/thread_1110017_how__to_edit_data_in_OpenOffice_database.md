You probably have to look for a table with the same name as your imported spreadsheet. Then open the table and you see columns and rows like in your spreadsheet. Only now the columnheaders are names of fields and the rows are the different records of the table.

Like the previous poster also indicated, look for the forums and get as much information there as possible. There are also good books about openoffice and databases in general.
