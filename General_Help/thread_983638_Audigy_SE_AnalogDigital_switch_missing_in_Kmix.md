### Post by mc4man on 2008-11-15
right click on the speaker icon in your bottom panel and click on the 'mixer' button. In the pop up go -> Settings -> configure channels and you can add what you need . The  Analog/Digital Jack is a ways down. 
After enabling back in the 'mixer' the 'mute' button enables/ disables it.
