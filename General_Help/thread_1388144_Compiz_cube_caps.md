### Post by JiuJitsu500 on 2010-01-22
Yeah, this totally does the trick..... sweet......

All you do is open GIMP, make a new image as big as your desktop is (in pixels), go to advanced options and the background image.... click transparent.... then save it as a png file.

In deformations or cube appearance, all you do is turn opacity all the way down in the color chooser for top and bottom images, and put this image there. Simple! and definately awesome! Now my aquarium might spill!
