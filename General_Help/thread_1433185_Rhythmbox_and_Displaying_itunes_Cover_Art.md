### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-03-18
None, I'm afraid, but I do know that the ability of rhythmbox to display cover art is largely broken in the version in karmic.  It seems to work in a few, but not on most of my music, though it worked almost perfectly in jaunty.

Same music files, same rhythmbox configuration in /home, but almost nothing shows cover art any more.  Banshee is much better at that, and to my mind, misses only the ability to crossfade tracks.
