### Post by Tibuda on 2009-03-16
I don't know what is intellisense, but google can always help. Auto-completion, right?

The default Ubuntu text editor (gedit) have a "snippets" package, that displays some completion when you press a key.

You can try also [Screem]("http://www.screem.org/"), [Aptana]("http://www.aptana.com/"), [Komodo]("http://www.activestate.com/komodo_edit/") or [GPhpEdit]("http://www.gphpedit.org/").
