### Post by drdos2006 on 2010-12-18
In order to use KDE addressbook with KMail, kde uses : 
.kde/share/apps/kabc/std.vcf
in your hidden home folder.


Export the list of KAddressbook contacts to a vcf file and copy to std.vcf in your .kde folder.
