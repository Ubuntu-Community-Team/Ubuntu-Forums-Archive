Okey so I have M$ office package installed on my Laptop with Ubuntu 11.10 and gnome 3.2 installed.
The thing that annoys me is that all of the applications have the same wineglass icon.
is there any way to change the icons of the individual apps?

---

### Post by davidvandoren on 2012-03-31
go to google image and type MS office icons and save the once you like somewhere.
 Open terminal and type ```
alacarte
``` in gnome 2

select the entry you wish to change and click on properties to the right.

Click on the icon button on the left and navigate to the icon you have saved from google image.
