### Post by marshmallow1304 on 2011-03-15
[https://www.facebook.com/sitetour/chat.php](https://www.facebook.com/sitetour/chat.php)
Click Pidgin, follow the instructions.  If your version of Pidgin doesn't have the box labeled "Require SSL/TLS", set "Connection security" to "Use encryption if available" instead.
