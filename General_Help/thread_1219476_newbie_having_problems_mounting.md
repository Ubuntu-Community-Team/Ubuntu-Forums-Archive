### Post by pedja_portugalac on 2009-07-21
Go to Places > Compter and click on your Windows drive (something GB Media), when prompt put your password to mount it. 
For the flash and other media codecs go to Applications > Add/Remove and 
put All available applications in the tab after Show: (in the middle above). Then in the search tab write "restristed" and you'll see ubuntu restricted extras, chose that one, apply and install.
