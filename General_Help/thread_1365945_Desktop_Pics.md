### Post by fabounet on 2009-12-30
> What I would like to know is what program is used to put the "scotched taped" pictures on the desktop

it IS cairo-dock that does that.
more precsely, the Slider applet.
Activate it, choose a folder contaning pictures, apply, then drag the applet out of the dock onto the desktop.
Then you can choose a decoration for your new desklet, the one yo see on the screenshot is named "Scotch" (= tape).
you can also rotate your desklet in 3D with the mouse.

Enjoy ! :)
