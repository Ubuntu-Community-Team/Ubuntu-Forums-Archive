### Post by deadflowr on 2014-07-08
Depends upon which gnome desktop.
There are three for 12.04 that I know of.
Gnome-Shell, the new one, which in most cases is almost as heavy as unity.
Gnome-fallback with effects, which uses compiz -- so it, too, can be a tad heavy.
Gnome-fallback without effects, which uses metacity and seems to be much lighter than the other two.

If the machine is really old, maybe look into alternatives such as xubuntu or lubuntu.
