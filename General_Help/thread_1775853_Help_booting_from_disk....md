### Post by hulkman on 2011-06-05
You may have to press F2 to enter your bios and change the boot order of your devices making sure that your CD rom appears first.

You might also try Booting from a USB drive.  In this case you will also need to press F2 to enter your bios and set your USB drive as the primary boot device.

[http://www.dotdeb.com](http://www.dotdeb.com) is a great source for .deb packages.
