### Post by ysangkok on 2011-05-19
It's actually a good thing if your driver doesn't have it's own control center. They made custom control centers because they didn't bother to implement Xrandr properly. If you have an Intel driver you can use all the tools that use Xrandr for configuration. The standard gnome tool is called gnome-display-properties.
