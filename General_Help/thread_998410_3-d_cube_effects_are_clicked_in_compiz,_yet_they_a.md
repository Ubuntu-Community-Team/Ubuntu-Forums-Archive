### Post by drs305 on 2008-11-30
There are lots of reasons your setup may not be working but here is an easy one to check.

*Edit: binbash in the next post has the answer from within compiz below, which I could not remember. Here is the other way to accomplish the same thing:*

Do you have more than one workspace enabled? I use the 'workspace switcher' on the bottom panel (Rt Click, Add to Panel, Workspace Switcher). Then Rt Click on the Workspace Switcher icon (usually lower right in ubuntu), Preferences, Workspace columns. If it is set to only 1 nothing will happen as you only have one workspace and nothing to switch or rotate to.
