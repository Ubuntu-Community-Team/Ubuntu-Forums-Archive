### Post by greywolf73 on 2005-09-30
Go to command line and type the following:
*giftd -d*

Od this as a normal user. 

Apollon and for a matter of fact any gui you used will require you to do that, but no where is that told to you. 

Hope this work for you, it work for me. :)
