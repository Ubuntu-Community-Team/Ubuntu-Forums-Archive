### Post by jcd29 on 2010-10-18
Yes, upgrade to 10.04 LTS first and then to 10.10

There could always be some problems when upgrading, so I suggest a backup of your files.

---

### Post by Schrute Farms on 2010-10-18
Also, when you get 10.04 running, you may not see the upgrade in the update manager.  If you don't then you will have to:  Open the Software Sources application from the System -> Administration menu.  

Select the sub menu Updates from the Software Sources application.  

Change the Release Upgrade drop down to "Normal Releases" and close the application.
