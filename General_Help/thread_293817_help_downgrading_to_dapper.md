### Post by bsussman on 2006-11-05
Downgrade is not a well trodden path - you may be better served by backing up your /home and reinstalling.

If you have a separate /home partition - so much easier.  But be cautious - you may be happier making a new user account and moving into it from your old account once 6.06 is re-installed.

Go slowly.  As I said, this is not a path that is often taken.

I found firefox to be very cranky when I went back to 6.06
