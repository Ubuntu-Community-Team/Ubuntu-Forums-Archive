Use thinkfinger. There was also a manual step or two that I had to take when I set mine up. If you Google thinkfinger you'll probably find it, since I no longer remember the details.

You should be aware, though, that enabling the fingerprint reader will make your system less secure. It might make sense from a convenience standpoint, but if you're paranoid about security, don't do it.
