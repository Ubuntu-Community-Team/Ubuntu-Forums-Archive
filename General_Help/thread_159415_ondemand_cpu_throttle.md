### Post by Robgould on 2006-04-12
jst make sure it is running as a service...you don't have to configure it.  If you want to watch it work, add the CPU frequency applet to your command bar on your desktop.  Just right click on the bar, select add to panel, and follow from there.  You will find it.  When it is there, you can click on it and see some options.  It will show you where your CPU is runing.  If you powernowd is running you will see it scale automatically in response do your systems load.
