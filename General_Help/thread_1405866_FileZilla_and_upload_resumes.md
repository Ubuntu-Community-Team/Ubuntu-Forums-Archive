### Post by Rytron on 2010-02-13
If I am uploading a video or other large file via ftp in FileZilla and the connection drops; do I need to restart the upload process or can I carry on where the uploading stopped (just like the way wget acts; only in this case we are uploading instead of downloading).

---

### Post by Grenage on 2010-02-13
You can resume.  When you try and overwrite the file it will give you a list of options, select *resume* there.
