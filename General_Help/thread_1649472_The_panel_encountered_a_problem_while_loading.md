### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-12-20
I usually delete when something like that happens and then add it back by right clicking in an empty part of the panel ->Add to Panel ->Notification Area.  That way, if there is any corruption in the applet's configuration in ~/.gconf/apps/panel, it may be put right when added back.
