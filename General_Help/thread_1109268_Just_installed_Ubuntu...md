### Post by Tipped OuT on 2009-03-28
To re-intsall Vista, you must put your Vista installation disk into your DVD Drive. Then set your bios to boot from the DVD drive. Restart your computer and if your bios settings are correct and your disk is valid (not scratched, has needed boot files) then you should get white text on the screen asking you to press any key to boot from the DVD. Everything after that is self explanatory. Good luck. :)

EDIT: If for whatever reason you can't boot using a disk. You may also try using a flash drive. Just copy all of the files from the disk to your flash drive, set your bios to boot from your flash drive. And the rest is the same process. Good luck :)
