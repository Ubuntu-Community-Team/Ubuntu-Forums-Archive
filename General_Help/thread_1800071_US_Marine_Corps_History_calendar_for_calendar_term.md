### Post by alindgr1 on 2011-07-08
I have made a calendar for the calendar terminal app that lists significant dates in US Marine Corps history.  Being a retired Marine, it was a labor of love, albeit short.  The entries are rather long, and I plan on shortening them later.  Please feel free to use copy, forward, share as you wish.

To use, copy into your .calendar folder and call using this command:

calendar -f ~/.calendar/calendar.usmc

(mine is copied into my home folder, yours may be in your /usr/share folder)
