### Post by Skidpad on 2007-07-14
If you've already installed Tomboy, right-click on your one of your panels, and select "add to panel".  Select "Tomboy Notes" from the accessories section, and click ok.

You should now have the little yellow Tomboy icon on whatever panel you installed it on.
