### Post by MAMAS on 2010-10-16
Hy - I also have a Samsung Jet S8000 - actually it's really simple:
1) have Ubunut running
2) turn on your cellphone
3) connect the usb cable to your cellphone
4) Samsung cell will now open a window where it asks you about what kind of device you want to use it for
5) select USB media ( on the German cellphone it is the third choice from the top) 
6) Ubuntu will then display your cellphone as a usb device and you can select all folders an perform all operations necessary.

Hope that helps.
