### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-03-31
For older hardware, Ubuntu really is not suitable. I would recommend instead using a lighter distro. Suggestions include [Xubuntu]("http://xubuntu.org/"), [Lubuntu]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu") (very light), and [Bodhi]("http://bodhilinux.com/") (derived from, but not part of, the Ubuntu family, and even lighter than Lubuntu).
