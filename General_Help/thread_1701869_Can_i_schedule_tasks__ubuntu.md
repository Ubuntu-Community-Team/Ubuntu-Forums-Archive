### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-03-07
The tool to schedul tasks is called [cron](http://linux.die.net/man/5/crontab).  That is for recurrent tasks.  One-off tasks can be scheduled with [at](http://linux.die.net/man/1/at)

Gnome-schedule and kcron are two GUIs for them, if you need that.
