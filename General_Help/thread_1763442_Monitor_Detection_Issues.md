### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-05-20
Unity and enhanced effects in Ubuntu classic need drivers that are not on the Live CD. They are available through the Additional Drivers utility. A lot of us have found that even with the correct drivers installed the Utility says this driver is activated but not in use. We think that it is an error.
