### Post by EvanEjk on 2015-03-22
Sometimes the mouse pointer is flickering so much it's near invisible. What's more annoying is that every time I close my laptop and open it back up there is a ghost pointer stuck on my screen. I also get the ghost pointer when I "Suspend" my computer and wake it back up. It just leaves a pointer somewhere on the screen that does nothing. A work around I found for the ghost pointers is restarting lightdm.

"sudo service lightdm restart"

Any permanent fixes?

### Post by EvanEjk on 2015-03-30
This problem went away. I think an update fixed it.

Or maybe it was a setting I changed.

Upper right hand corner gear power button > System Settings... > Displays > Click on "Unknown Display" > Click on the on/off switch and turn it off > Apply > Keep This Configuration

^ Seems to help a lot.
