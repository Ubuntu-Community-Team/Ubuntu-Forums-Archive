### Post by TheViLliN on 2010-03-03
Solved:

So apparently I forgot I was attempting to get the native VNC/Remote Desktop working the other day.

After enabling "Allow other users to view your desktop" in Remote Desktop Preferences was when the issue started.

Turning it off brought the system load under control.
