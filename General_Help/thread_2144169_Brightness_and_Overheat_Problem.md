### Post by TNFrank on 2013-06-06
I kind of have the same issue with the Brightness setting in System Settings. Right now it's at max but I can adjust the brightness with the fn plus my f9(for less) and f10(for more) and it'll work and stay set where I put it. 
 I normally run plugged in and set to max anyway so I can see the screen and not have to worry about battery life.
I did install Jupiter 0.1.9 and I can adjust my power needs from it. 
 As for overheating, I run high 40's to mid 50's but will go to 80*C when I do my virus scan but nothing near the critical temp of 110*C for my system.  I'd check to see that your air inlets and outlets are clean and your fan is free of dust.  I found a nice little "dust bunny" between a couple of my fan blades when I was upgrading my memory(had to pull the keyboard, ect to get into that area and the fan was right there so I gave it a look over) and removed it so it won't be causing any problems in the future. 
 Anyway, hope you have some luck getting things working right.
