### Post by Grenage on 2011-04-08
When I think 'media centre', I think along the lines of XBMC; that said, there are only a couple of points I can see issues with.

Unless something has very recently changed, BluRay on Linux is a no go.  Yes, some things might work on it, but you're otherwise on a pain train to hell.  itunes protected content, you'll be using Wine or Windows unless you can convert it.

Bottom line here, if you're not just using this to play movies and music, and you want bluray support - use Windows.  We use XBMC/Linux for our media centre, a BluRay player fol blurays, and consoles for TV games.  I say 'we', I just use the media centre.
