### Post by BigBaka on 2010-12-31
Hi,

When you say Kana input, you mean that you don't type phonetically, but rather use the Japanese kana keyboard direct? 

I usually use ibus to type romaji using dvorak keyboard, no problems. Depending on which US keyboard you use, ibus will change correspondingly for typing Japanese. Ibus also has an icon you can click to change to direct kana input. I just tried it and it seemed to work no problem for me. Got the to dots (for making ga from ka) by pressing SHIFT-2.
