Hate Ubuntu&#8217;s Unity? Use Ubuntu Classic
April 28th, 2011 by Jade Robbins

I recently upgraded to Ubuntu 11.04 Natty Narwhal, only to immediately decide that I hated the new Unity windowing system. DON&#8217;T PANIC.

You can still use your wonderful Gnome-ness in Ubuntu with little hassle. When logging in, click on your user, and down in at the bottom there is a little selection box that says &#8220;Ubuntu&#8221;. Click on that box and select &#8220;Ubuntu Classic&#8221; and finish logging in.

There you go, Ubuntu without the terrible Unity Desktop!
