### Post by QIII on 2017-06-02
[This bug]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/lxsession/+bug/1315856") may or may not apply.  

I don't know what posts you may have looked at before, but I believe you can get sounds to work in Lubuntu by going to "Preferences" -> "Customize look and feel" -> and selecting "Other"

"Play sounds" should be checked.

If this does not work, please let us know and we can look for something else via the terminal.
