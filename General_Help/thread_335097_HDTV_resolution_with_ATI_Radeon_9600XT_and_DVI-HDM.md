### Post by mapleshade on 2007-01-12
You'll have to edit the xorg.conf to get your resolution.  Search for modeline and you should find a how-to.  This is the calculator I used to determine my modeline: [http://xtiming.sourceforge.net/cgi-bin/xtiming.pl](http://xtiming.sourceforge.net/cgi-bin/xtiming.pl).  I have a 9600XT with DVI out and 1280x720 resolution.
