### Post by xmastree on 2005-12-24
Tha main thing, IMO, is to have something they'll **notice** rather than something to show them.

If you say "Hey guys, look at this" and proceed to demonstrate stuff, it will be like ramming it down their throats.

Better to wait for them to say "Wow, what's that?" when they notice it.

Then you can start to show them how cool it is. :cool:
