### Post by satish_j on 2012-08-14
so,here is the resolution for someone with similar issue.
Launch TVTime,it creates another(active) icon in Launcher.Now,right click the original(inactive)icon->select 'Unlock from Launcher'.
So,there is now only one icon in launcher,which is active.Restart TVtime and there will no duplicate icons.
