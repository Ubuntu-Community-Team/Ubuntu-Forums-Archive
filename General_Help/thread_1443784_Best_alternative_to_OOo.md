### Post by Dragonbite on 2010-03-31
Tried [_KOffice_]("http://www.koffice.org/")?

Also, there is [_IBM's Lotus Symphony_]("http://symphony.lotus.com/software/lotus/symphony/home.nsf/products") which is based on an older version of OpenOffice re-worked.

I haven't tried it, but there is also [_SoftMaker_]("http://www.softmaker.com/english/") Office 2010 for Linux.

Just to make sure you know, there are actually 2 versions of OpenOffice; the "pure", Sun-based [_OpenOffice _]("http://www.openoffice.org")and the Novell-enhanced [_Go-OO_]("http://go-oo.org/") version for better compatibility with MS Office. I think Ubuntu includes the Go-OO version.
