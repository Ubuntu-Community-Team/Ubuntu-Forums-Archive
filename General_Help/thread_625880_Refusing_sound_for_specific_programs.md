### Post by cknight on 2007-11-28
Not sure about any blacklist for specific programs, but there are a few extensions which exist for Firefox to block sound.  Check out the following extension:

Stop Autoplay:[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1765]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1765")

Alternatively, I believe the Opera browser offers the option to turn off sound completely.

Good luck!
