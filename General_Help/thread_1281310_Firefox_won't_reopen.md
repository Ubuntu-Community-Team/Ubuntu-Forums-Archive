### Post by beastrace91 on 2009-10-03
I've had this problem with firefox now and again, basically the process is hanging around after it should have exited. To resolve this with out rebooting manually kill the application via your system monitor (Administration->System Monitor on Gnome, or look for [KDE System Guard](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KDE_System_Guard) if you are on KDE)

On the list of running processes just look for firefox and end it.
