My cube is running everything good to go.  I've seen screenshots of other cubes on web and they have a picture at top and bottom of cube. Mine only shows a solid yellow on the top and bottom. How do I put a picture on the top and bottom?

### Post by ChildOfMana on 2009-02-13
in CompizConfig Settings Manager go to Desktop > Desktop Cube and click the Appearance tab.

Expand the option for Cube Caps, click New and browse to and add whatever image you like.
