### Post by Old *ix Geek on 2010-08-23
Not all windoze programs work with wine...yet. The wine crew does an amazing job of getting apps working, so it's possible they'll get to yours at some point.

A great place to start is wine's web site. Take a look at the [**_Wine Application Database_**](http://appdb.winehq.org) to see if your problem apps are listed. You can get a lot of info there! Things like other people's experiences with your app, your specific version--how/whether it installs, how/whether it runs, if all of its functionality works correctly, and so on.

There are also [**_forums_**](http://forum.winehq.org) where you can ask for help, and [**_bugzilla_**](http://bugs.winehq.org) where you can report problems.
