### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2010-07-31
_About hard drives:_ 
Search about how to customize your fstab file.

_About videoget:_ 
If you use firefox, there are available extensions that let you grab any flash video.
If you use Opera, the video is in the folder .opera in your home partition (cache or operacache).
If you user Chrome/Chromium, the video is in the folder /home/yourusername/.cache/Chrom(e/ium).
After you have the video, rename it to video.flv and use avidemux or ffmpeg or MobileMediaConverter or WinFF or whatever you like to convert to whatever format you wish.
