just for the benefit of anyone searching.  I tried it and it works.  You can just delete the ones you dont like out of /usr/lib/xscreensaver.  You'll have to do it in the terminal with a sudo tho as they are are system files.  This is great, now I have only the most impressive screensavers!  I recommend everyone does this!
