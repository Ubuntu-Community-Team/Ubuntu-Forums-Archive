### Post by Dennis N on 2014-05-21
The scrollbars are defined by the theme in use. The default theme in Lubuntu (Lubuntu Default) does not have arrows on the scrollbars.

Choose a better theme:

The Clearlooks theme does have arrows on the scrollbars and also wider scrollbars. You may find it more to your liking:

**Menu > Preferences > Customized Look and Feel > Wiget Tab > Select Clearlooks**  and press  **Apply**.
