### Post by pointone on 2008-02-23
kubuntu-desktop is just a [metapackage]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MetaPackages"). Removing one package belonging to the metapackage only means you no longer have the "full" metapackage, but all other associated packages should remain behind.
