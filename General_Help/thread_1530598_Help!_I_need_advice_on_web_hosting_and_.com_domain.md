### Post by baligirl on 2010-07-13
You can purchase/register a domain name from one company, and redirect it to your pages that are hosted with a different company.  I've used GoDaddy [http://www.godaddy.com/](http://www.godaddy.com/) for domain registration, and I use Lunarpages web hosting.  Currently I buy additional domain names through Lunarpages [http://www.lunarpages.com/](http://www.lunarpages.com/) as it's just easier, and I've been with them for maybe seven years and am very happy with them. ### Post by snowpine on 2010-07-13
Any reputable company will allow you to transfer your domain name in the future. Make sure, however, you don't allow it to expire! If your domain name expires, you need $$$ to get it back. :( I had a bad experience with register.com on this issue, can't recommend them. I've been using hostmonster.com for a little while now, so far so good (and the price is right).
