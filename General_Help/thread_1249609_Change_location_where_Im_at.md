### Post by riahc3 on 2009-08-25
Hey

I was updating my system today and the updates went VERY VERY slow. I checked it out and it seems my system thinks im in USA and it is downloading the updates from USA servers. How can I change to where Im at and therfore it downloads the updates from the nearest servers?

---

### Post by kellemes on 2009-08-25
System -> Administration -> Software Sources -> Download from: <select your server>
