====================================================================================

([https://help.ubuntu.com/community/NvidiaTVOut](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/NvidiaTVOut)) offers an alternative way to do it, with two independent displays.

I replace Section "Device" of my orginal xorg.conf with

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Device0"
	Driver		"nvidia"
	Screen 0
	Option  "NoLogo"        "true"
        Option  "RenderAccel"   "true"
	BusID		"PCI:1:0:0"
EndSection

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Device1"
	Driver		"nvidia"
	Screen 1
	BusID		"PCI:1:0:0"
EndSection

I replaced Section "Monitor" with the following:

Section "Monitor"
        Identifier      "Monitor" #CRT
        HorizSync       28-51
        VertRefresh     43-60
        Option          "DPMS"
EndSection

Section "Monitor"
        Identifier "Television" #TV
        HorizSync 30-50
        VertRefresh 60
EndSection

The HorizSync and VertRefresh for the CRT are copied from the original, the rest is from the guide.

The single Section "Screen" is replaced with

Section "Screen"
        Identifier      "Screen0"
        Device          "Device0"
        Monitor         "Monitor"
        DefaultDepth    24
        Option  "ConnectedMonitor" "CRT"
        SubSection "Display"
                Depth           24
                Modes           "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"
        EndSubSection
EndSection

Section "Screen"
        Identifier      "Screen1"
        Device          "Device1"
        Monitor         "Television"
        DefaultDepth    24
        Option  "TVOutFormat" "SVIDEO"
        Option  "TVStandard" "PAL-G"
        Option  "ConnectedMonitor" "TV"
        SubSection "Display"
                Depth 24
                Modes "1024x768"
        EndSubSection
EndSection

Finally:

Section "ServerLayout"
	Identifier	"Basic Layout"
	Screen 0	"Screen0"
	Screen 1	"Screen1" leftof "Screen0"
	InputDevice	"Generic Keyboard"
	InputDevice	"Configured Mouse"
	InputDevice     "stylus"	"SendCoreEvents"
	InputDevice     "cursor"	"SendCoreEvents"
	InputDevice     "eraser"	"SendCoreEvents"
EndSection

Result: The TV displays a blue screen (= no signal), VGA works as before, I get a message about a restricted driver in use.
