### Post by mcduck on 2007-05-29
Just get a theme you want from [http://www.gnome-look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org").

GTK2-themes are for your applications, Metacity themes change window borders and icons are, well, icons.

Extract GTK2- and Metacity-themes to ~/.themes and icon themes to ~/.icons. (usually you can just drop the downloaded package to theme manager window to install themes, but as it doesn't always work it's easier to extract them by hand all the time..)
