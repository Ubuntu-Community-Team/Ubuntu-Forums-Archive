### Post by Qrk on 2006-04-22
Try making xmms use the alsa sound plugin instead of the OSS one. 

Right click on Xmms->Options->Prefereces->Output plugin, and change this from OSS to ALSA. 

Its worth a shot, but I have never had your problem exactly.

### Post by AndyCooll on 2006-04-22
The fact that iyou have no volume on either player suggests that it is more likely to be your system sound settings rather than the applications themselves.

System-->Preferences-->Sound. Is your sound card correct?

Also daft as it may seem is your pc's "master" volume control set at a reasonable level? That can be checked either by the icon on the taskbar (if it's there) or Applications-->Sound & Video-->Volume Control
