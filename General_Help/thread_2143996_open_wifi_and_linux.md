### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-05-10
> [COLOR=#000000]placed, [/COLOR][COLOR=#000000]a firewall program to protect against intrusion while on that open wi-fi [/COLOR]

Not for that specific area of risk. But for common risks associated with connecting to the Internet which will include risks from connecting to someone else's wireless router.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UFW](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UFW)

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BasicSecurity](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BasicSecurity)

> [COLOR=#333333][FONT=Ubuntu]You need to realize that Ubuntu's firewall is not enabled by default. You have to enable it.[/FONT][/COLOR]
