> ... And ati radeon 9600 vid card.

There are no ATI Linux drivers for that card anymore.  ATI dropped Linux support for that card over a year ago.  If you're seeing a graphical desktop, you already have the default drivers installed -- and that's all there are now.
>  I had none of these problems, in xp pro...
Different OS -- different video drivers. Nothing whatsoever to do with the video drivers in Linux distros.
