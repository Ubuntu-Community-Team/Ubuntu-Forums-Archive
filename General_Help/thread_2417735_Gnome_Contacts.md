### Post by DuckHook on 2019-04-26
Gnome Contacts appears to default to either phone number or e-mail fields when neither name field is populated. When the e-mail field was left blank and subsequently auto-filled with a random address for Google's reference info, the results are especially bad. I work around the issue by filling the first name field with the company name. Once either name is supplied, the other field is not needed. The minor drawback is that the company name now shows up twice in Google contacts, but aside from offending my sense of tidiness, it doesn't have any other adverse effects.
