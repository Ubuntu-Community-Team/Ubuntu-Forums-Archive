### Post by indytim on 2008-10-19
I'm not sure what you're referencing with a staff rostering.  If it's a listing of members, this can be accomplished with OO or any text file and a standard editor.  If you are headed for something more exotic, then one possible option might be to build in LAMP and get a script for a membership system. In this environment, you would generate and maintain the membership locally and upload the output contents to your public site. You can find an ample supply of such scripts at say, hotscripts.com.

If you're supporting an on-line (i.e. public web site), then I would recommend that you maintain your listing within your public site (assuming the hosting service supports say, php and mysql.

Just a few recommendations.  Hopefully one of them may prove beneficial.

Good Luck,
