``` I get something unusable: I can't even change font there. Is this how it's supposed to be now? Can I launch from CLI anything more usefull?
### Post by jimmydean886-2 on 2011-11-21
try using a utility called the "Gnome Tweak Tool"
```
sudo apt-get install gnome-tweak-tool 
gnome-tweak-tool
```It manages most of the appearance settings, fonts, GNOME Shell extensions, and even a few power options. If you decide to go back to GNOME, you can even choose whether nautilus handles the desktop or not.

Yes, that's the new GNOME 3 Control Center. Great for new users, but EVERYTHING has changed from the old version, and most other users hate it.

Out of curiosity, what DE do you use right now? I really love to explore every possible option.
