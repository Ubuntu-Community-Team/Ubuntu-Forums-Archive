### Post by nikhilk on 2007-05-17
Typically Ubuntu supports every release with security/stability updates for a period of 18 months. 5.10 was released on October 13th 2005 and hence has reached the end of its life on April 13th 2007.
Hence we won't be getting security and stability updates for 5.10 anymore. You can simply comment out every line in your "/etc/apt/sources.list".
