### Post by jimv on 2009-01-21
UPDATE:

So my curiosity got the best of me.  I backed up my Ubuntu install with PartImage and tried installing the newer version of GVFS and libc6 from the Jaunty repo.

When I restarted the X server, my desktop was black and the mouse just kept spinning.  I rebooted, and the same.  So I guess I broked it.

BTW, PartImage rules.  10 minutes after my broken install I was back up and running.
