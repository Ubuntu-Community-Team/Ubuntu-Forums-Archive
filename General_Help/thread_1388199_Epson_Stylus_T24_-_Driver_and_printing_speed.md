### Post by leandromartinez98 on 2010-01-26
Well, I didn't solve my problem. But I installed the printer with
the official driver in a Windows machine and it is as slow as with
the driver for linux.

The conclusion is: The 26 ppm printing speed reported by Epson for
(at least) the T24 printer is simply a lie. It wrong by a factor
of 50-100, two orders of magnitude. If they got this speed for some
test they did, hope to have to print only their test page forever.

Therefore: DON'T BUY THE EPSON T24 PRINTER IF YOU NEED TO PRINT
           A NORMAL DOCUMENT IN LESS THAN HALF AN HOUR!
