### Post by B'man on 2008-02-04
The optical drive should definitely be fine. The wifi adaptor I don't know, but compatibility for them has gotten a lot better recently, so I'd say a tentative yes.

I think the adaptor shown uses Broadcom firmware, which is more problematic than most. The proprietary Broadcom drivers are installed automatically from Gutsy onwards, though they have a low range. To improve it you'd have to install ndiswrapper, which is a little long-winded.

The camera will probably work, but you may have to find the drivers yourself. The biometric scanner... who knows?
