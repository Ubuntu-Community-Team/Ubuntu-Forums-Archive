### Post by drs305 on 2009-01-12
Here is the ubuntu release schedule. The two most recent are Hardy and Intrepid. Hardy is a long term release and if you look at the schedule you will see it is supported until April 2011. An LTS release receives security updates and bug fixes but will not have the 'leading-edge' advances available in the other distributions. Intrepid's desktop version will be supported until April 2010.
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases")
