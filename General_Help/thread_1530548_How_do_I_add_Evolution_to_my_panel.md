### Post by munkedal on 2010-07-13
I started up Amarok and an Amarok icon appeared beside my email icon.  After I was finished with Amarok, I removed it from my panel.  Unfortunately, my email icon and my volume icon have both disappeared.

 When I use add to panel, evolution is not there.  Where is it?

I solved it.  All I had to do was to right click on the top panel, choose add to panel and then choose  Indicator Applet Session.
