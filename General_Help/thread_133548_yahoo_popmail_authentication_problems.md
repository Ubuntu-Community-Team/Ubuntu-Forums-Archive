### Post by bscbrit on 2006-02-20
It depends how old your Yahoo account is.  Accounts that are at least several years old can all use POP access, but more recent accounts must upgrade in order to have POP access, otherwise you are limited to web access I believe.

[http://help.yahoo.com/help/us/mail/pop/](http://help.yahoo.com/help/us/mail/pop/)  &  [http://help.yahoo.com/help/us/mail/pop/pop-10.html](http://help.yahoo.com/help/us/mail/pop/pop-10.html)

says that you have to upgrade to Mail Plus to have POP access.  The second link has all the details regarding log in, password and authentication.

Does Yahoo require "Secure" authentication?

If so, your Evolution setting has the facility to turn this on and then check for what the server supports.

You can then select the method from the drop-down list.

If you also need SMTP authentication for outgoing mail, it can do this as well.

You must also ensure you username is 100% correct as to what Yahoo specify - do not assume you have it right, carefully read their POP instructions.
