Is there a way to determine the total disk space used by all emails in thunderbird?

---

### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2011-08-13
Thunderbird mail is stored in your home folder.
In my case it's
```
/home/teo/.thunderbird/1b3m5se3.default/Mail/pop.gmail.com
```
The different files in there (Inbox, Sent, Trash, etc) should give you an approximate size of your mails.
