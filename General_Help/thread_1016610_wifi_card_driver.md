### Post by drs305 on 2008-12-20
You can find your other posts by clicking on your name in the left pane of  any post and selecting "Find More Posts by ...". It may have already scrolled over to Page 2 or more. If the site is busy, it may not stay on the first page for very long. You can look at the time of the last post to get an idea of where your post is on the list. You can also click on Search (at the top of the main page in each forum), Find All Your Threads.
