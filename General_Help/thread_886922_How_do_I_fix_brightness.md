### Post by OutOfReach on 2008-08-11
You have a laptop, correct?
Well the easiest way that I can think of is to right click your top-panel and select 'Add to Panel', a dialog comes up, now select the 'Brightness Applet' it should up at the top, and click 'Add'. Now you will notice a new Icon will appear, click on it, and adjust the slider. 
Hope that helps.
