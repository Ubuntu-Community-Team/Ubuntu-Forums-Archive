### Post by anspectrum on 2012-05-08
Oh !.....that should not have happened. But since it did. Here's what you can do.

The cleanest way (and depending on the amount of data you have got) is to boot through Ubuntu live CD. Mount the hard drives (usually /dev/sda bla bla) on temporary directory. Access the contents. And save them either on a pen disk (USB) or through a network on another PC. After data is saved make a clean install.:P

Or wait for more expert opinion to recover broken Ubuntu installation.

Good Luck
