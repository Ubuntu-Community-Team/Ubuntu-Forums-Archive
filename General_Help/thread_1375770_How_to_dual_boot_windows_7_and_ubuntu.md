### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-01-08
When you read the info at that link, make sure you notice the part about having a recovery CD ready.  They mean a CD you can use to REPAIR your Vista installation, not the CD that OEMs provide that will wipe and reinstall the OS from scratch.

That's because there have been lots of posts about folks Vista/Win7 machines being rendered unbootable (into MS Windows) after they used the Ubuntu installer to shrink their Vista/win7 OS partitions.

With Win7, you're in luck because it provides a built-in feature for creating and burning a Win7 Repair CD.  Do that before you attempt any dual-boot installation.
