### Post by Egi_Power on 2009-01-27
> I can take screenshots, but don't know how to put them into here, tho'!!

When you write your post, under the text box (where you type in your message) you will find [SIZE="3"]*Additional Options*[/SIZE].
One of those is to attach files. Click on the *Manage Attachments* button.
Then a new window will pop up.
You can upload 5 files (screen shots for example)
Click on the *Browse...* button in the new window, and select the file you want to upload.
When you are done, just click on the *Upload* button, and then the *Close this window* in the top right corner.
That's all.

Best regards,

Egi_Power
