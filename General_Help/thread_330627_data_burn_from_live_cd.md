### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-01-03
The latest version of puppy is great, and it does have a CD burner, though I,ve never used that.  I recently looked at puppy just out of curiosity, and could almost live with it as a quick, simple live CD.  You can remove the CD even whilst running so with one drive you can still theoretically burn another blank one.  You can even save files to a special kind of archive file on a fat32 or ext3 hard drive (you get to chose where when you shutdown).  Give it a whirl; I think you might find it extremely useful.
