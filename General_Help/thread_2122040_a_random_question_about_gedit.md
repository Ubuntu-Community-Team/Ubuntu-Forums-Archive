### Post by maggotbrain on 2013-03-04
I'm not sure about re-creating that specific theme you mentioned. But, yes, you can easily create your own custom color schemes for gedit. These are usually just XML files which are easily modifiable. 

Generally, I keep my gedit color schemes in /usr/share/gtksourceview-3.0/styles/. Your search path preferences may vary. Take a look here: [https://live.gnome.org/GtkSourceView/StyleSchemes]("https://live.gnome.org/GtkSourceView/StyleSchemes") for some themes and instructions for other paths to install the styles.

After restarting gedit, the new style should show up under Edit -> Preferences -> Fonts & Colors
