### Post by chris24300 on 2011-09-21
VICTORY!!!

For all those with my problem, here's the solution

1. completely remove everything compiz

2. remove the graphics driver; enabled unity in the first place(then restart)

3. activate the graphics driver (then restart)

4. sudo apt-get ubuntu-desktop (then I had to restart for changes to show)

5. Enjoy everything working as it should... now ):P F off unity.
