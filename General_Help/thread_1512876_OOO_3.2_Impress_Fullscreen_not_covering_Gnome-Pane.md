### Post by XSAlliN on 2010-06-18

---

### Post by mjuhasz on 2010-06-18
This is a known bug which is targeted for the first point release (10.04.1). See: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/openoffice.org/+bug/525807](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/openoffice.org/+bug/525807)
You can disable compiz or set the panel to autohide as suggested in the previous post.
