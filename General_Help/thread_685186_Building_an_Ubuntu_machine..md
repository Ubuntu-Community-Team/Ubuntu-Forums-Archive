### Post by SpiderGorilla on 2008-02-02
You know, I've never been clear... is 24-pin the same as 20+4 pin? I could swear they're not. If they're not, your power supply isn't going to sync to your mobo. Apparently, per Almighty Google, it can be done... but it does leave exposed pins on your mobo.

Also, the RAM you're looking at is 2 sticks of 512, which effectively throttles you at 1GB of RAM as it eats up all your available slots. If you only need a GIG of RAM (laughable these days), then you're just dandy. Otherwise, you might want to look at 1GB RAM sticks.

You're winding up with what I believe is a 64-bit processor. I'd seriously recommend getting the 64-bit Gutsy release. It's really good, and well-done. There is some hardship in installing some stuff (flash plugin being the most notable), but other than that it works great for me.

### Post by IanW on 2008-02-02
By "glue" I assume you mean heatsink compound?

Normally, I'd say yes, but you've listed the retail CPU/Heatsink combo which comes with some pre-applied to the heatsink.
Just follow the instructions in the box & you'll be fine.

One thing, though. The case will come with several spacers in a little baggie. USE THEM!
I've heard of people who've screwed the mobo directly to the bottom of the case and then wondered why nothing worked.
