### Post by freeworld4 on 2016-11-06
Figure it out this guide works. [https://www.linux.com/learn/how-stream-content-linux-system-chromecast](https://www.linux.com/learn/how-stream-content-linux-system-chromecast)
The chromecast plugin for es files explorer doesn't support all video formats so I recommend just using it to connect to ubuntu or other linux distro to samba. Then when you want to play a file select vlc(free for android) and then pause it go to google home app(free for android) then cast the phone's screen from there. After that go back to vlc and play it. It should play back on your tv. Good luck guys! :)
