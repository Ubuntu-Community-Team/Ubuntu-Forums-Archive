### Post by az on 2009-07-28
It's possible.

There are a few problems that you may run into.  

1.  If the hard drive order is changed, the bootloader may need to be reconfigured.  Search "reinstall grub" to fix.
2.  Your graphics card may need reconfiguring - follow the prompts when you boot.
