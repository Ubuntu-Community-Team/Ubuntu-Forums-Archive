### Post by Naegling23 on 2007-04-25
envy is a script to install proprietary video drivers(both ati and nvidia).  It basically does what automatix does, but only for the video drivers.  Im a big fan of it because when Im greeted by a command prompt after booting my system after a kernal upgrade, all I have to do is enter envy, let it run, and restart x to get everything back.
