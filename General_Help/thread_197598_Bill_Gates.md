### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2006-06-16
A real shame, but it's understandable.  It's hard to imagine Microsoft without Gates.  He's meant so much.  It's much like Wal-Mart without Sam Walton, but then again, I didn't grow up with Wal-Mart having Sam Walton as its head. He, unfortunately, passed away before I became an admirer of him and Wal-Mart. 

Thank God I can say I am from Bill Gates' age (when he was around, as opposed to say, someone else).  A great guy, and it's strange he's already 50.  I hope, before he passes away, he's well documented, because he is indeed a historical figure...someone so critical to the computer world.
