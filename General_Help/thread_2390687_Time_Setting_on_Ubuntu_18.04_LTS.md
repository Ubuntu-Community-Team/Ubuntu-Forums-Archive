I just installed Ubuntu 18.04 LTS yesterday and today I'm noticing the clock on top.  The only thing it shows is the day and time, Tue 9:52. ### Post by Dennis N on 2018-05-01
Yes, that setting is hard to find. It's set in Tweaks.

Tweaks > Top Bar > Clock > Switch on 'Date'
