### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-07-05
I'm sorry, but I'm not sure what you are asking for?????

If you are working behind an ADSL router your Internet should work fine.

As far as I know ISP's don't filter users, depending upon their OS, as this would be illegal in most countries.

If your router is plugged in and has logged into your ISP, your 100 base T LAN connection on your PC should connect fine.

However, if you have a USB router, you could have problems, as they are often locked into Windows, like many onboard 'softmodems'.
