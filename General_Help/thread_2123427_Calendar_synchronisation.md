### Post by gifford on 2013-03-08
If you use Google calendar on your Android smartphone, Thunderbird (with lightning) will sync with Google calendar and with the Google calendar on your Samsung device. Setting up Google calendar in Thunderbird Lightning is fairly straight forward. Use the provider for google calendar extension located here:[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/addon/provider-for-google-calendar/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/addon/provider-for-google-calendar/)
