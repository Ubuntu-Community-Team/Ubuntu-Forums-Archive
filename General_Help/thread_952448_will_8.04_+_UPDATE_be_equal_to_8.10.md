### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-10-19
> **denyboy said:**
> just out of curiosity. How many MB will dist-upgrade download.


It depends entirely on how many other programs you have installed. It basically updates every repository-supplied package on your computer. If you have Alien Arena, Nexuiz, Torcs, OpenArena etc installed, then you're in for a long wait.

If it's a fresh install of 8.04 that you are upgrading to 8.10, then I think it will download just under 700 megabytes. Every package on the system is new.

### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-10-19
> **denyboy said:**
> Should i wait.

If you have a limited download limit - either wait, or go for the 8.10 Beta.

8.10 Beta will update to the full 8.10 with just a few packages.

8.04.1 will first update itself (with a few hundred Mb) straight away, and then dist-upgrade with a further 700-odd Mb to 8.10 when available.  And you also end up with a less-than-perfect 8.10 installation (according to some people, a fresh install is better than a dist-upgrade).  These download sizes will increase further if you want to install additional packages on top of the default Ubuntu.

So, in summary... I'd wait.
