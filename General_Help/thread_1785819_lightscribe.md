### Post by lisati on 2011-06-18
Download the "deb" versions of the files you need from [http://www.lightscribe.com/downloadSection/linux/index.aspx](http://www.lightscribe.com/downloadSection/linux/index.aspx) to your normal downloads folder. You can then open the files from your desktop: Ubuntu should automatically recognise that they are programs to install, and take care of which folders to install them to.

Once you get past the "do you accept the agreement" page on the website, there are instructions on what you need to do.
