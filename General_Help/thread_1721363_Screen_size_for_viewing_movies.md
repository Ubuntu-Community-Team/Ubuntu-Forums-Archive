### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-04-04
How are you viewing them; with what application?

Most video players, if not all, can display in full screen.

Just have a look in the various menus of the application and you will find out how to do it, I'm sure.  In **totem movie player, mplayer, gnome-mplayer**, and **vlc** you simply press "F" to toggle between full and window screen size.

It doesn't come much easier than that!
