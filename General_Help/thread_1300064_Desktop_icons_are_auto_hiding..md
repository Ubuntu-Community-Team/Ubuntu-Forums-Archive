### Post by 101011010010 on 2009-10-24
Hello there,
try opening gconf-editor, by pressing Alt+F2, type in gconf-editor and press run.
Then navigate to Apps> nautilus> preferences> show_desktop and add a tick.
This has helped others in the past.
