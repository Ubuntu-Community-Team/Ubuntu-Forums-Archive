### Post by Spr0k3t on 2007-03-28
From the sound of it, you are trying to move items into or from the notification area.  Unless the applet supports a notification method, you can only move it around the free area.  Notice the little drag bar beside a group of applets, try unlocking it and moving the drag bar around.  You should see what I'm talking about almost instantly.
