### Post by FormatSeize on 2011-06-21
I just downloaded it successfully from [here](http://www.gtkpod.org/wiki/Download).
 
1. Click the link on that page that says "Official release".
2. On the next page, click the link that says "gtkpod". (You probably already have the libraries since you are using a version of it already). 
3. You will now be on Sourceforge, and you want to click the link that says "gtkpod-2.0.2".
4. Click the link that says "gtkpod-2.0.2.tar.gz".
5. You will see another screen with some sort of form on it. Ignore that form, it has nothing to do with the download. It actually says in the fine print that it has nothing to do with the download, but I have no clue why  it's there. (Who stops to fill out a form while downloading something?)
5a. Turn off your popup blocking if you are using any. It will prevent the download from happening if you have popup blocking enabled.
6. While you are staring at this form wondering what the heck is going on, near the top of the screen there is a countdown saying that your download will begin in 5, 4, 3,... (in seconds). Once that finishes, you will get a popup asking if you want to save or open the file. Click save, and save it where you are going to extract and build the tar.
