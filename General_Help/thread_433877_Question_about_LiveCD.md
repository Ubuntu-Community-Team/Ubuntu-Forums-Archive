### Post by Thingymebob on 2007-05-05
Unlikely. The monitoring is probably not on the local machine and will be watching requests over the network, so it doesn't matter which OS their coming from as the request will still be formed the same. I would guess you won't even be able to connect to the schools network with the live CD without some help from the Network Admins who are unlikely to let you do this.
