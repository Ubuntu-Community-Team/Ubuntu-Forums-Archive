### Post by cknight on 2009-04-28
Is it possible in metacity to change the binding for window resize (currently Alt-middleclick) to Alt-rightclick (which is far easier to accomplish on my laptop)?

---

### Post by cknight on 2009-06-17
Well, this hasn't been the world's most popular thread, but I finally figured it out in case anyone else wants to know.  Get yourself into gconf-editor and navigate to /apps/metacity/general.  Tick the resize_with_right_button option.  As simple as that!
