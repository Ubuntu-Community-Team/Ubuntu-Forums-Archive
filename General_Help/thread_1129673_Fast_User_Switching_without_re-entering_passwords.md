### Post by platykurtic on 2009-04-18
My girlfriend and I share a desktop machine, and use different accounts to avoid logging each other out of web accounts everywhere. The fast user switching applet works well for this purpose, and previously it let us switch back and forth easily enough, but since upgrading it's started requiring us to enter our password every time we switch. This is annoying and my girlfriend has started just logging me out of my accounts again to my mild dismay. How can I make it so we only have to enter passwords upon first login? Thanks for your help

---

### Post by balaknair on 2009-04-18
Try Right-clicking on the user switcher applet in the panel> Preferences> Options> Lock the screen after switching users(uncheck the option).

### Post by platykurtic on 2009-04-18
