### Post by Ahava591 on 2010-10-01
Several people and myself have been unable to check for updates to the default repositories when requesting from the U.S. server(s) this morning.

I have been able to mitigate the problem by changing the location from U.S. server(s) to the "Main," server.
-This has allowed me to complete checks for updates to the default repositories and has allowed any pending updates to my system to download and install.

If you are using the U.S. server(s) this could be a problem. Because there may in fact be a failure on the U.S. side, other servers around the world may also be experiencing difficulty.

I understand that you are away from the laptop in question; but when you are able to get to it, if you would like to give this a try it may help.

Before you do so, however, I think it would be better to give us any more information that you can.
