### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-16
IF you're talking about using some application to manage your phone contacts using a GUI, then most likely, this will NOT work.

When you ARE able to connect a cell phone to Ubuntu, all you usually get is the ability to read/write the directories/files on the phone.

When I connect my Android phone via USB, a menu pops up on the phone asking what to do.  If I do nothing, it connects the phone in CHARGE mode only -- meaning, Ubuntu will not see it.

Need to research your phone to see what is supposed to happen when you connect it to a PC via USB.
