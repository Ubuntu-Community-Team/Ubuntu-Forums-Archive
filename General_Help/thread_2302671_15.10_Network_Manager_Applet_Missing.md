### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-11-12
First make sure that network-manager-gnome is installed and then see that **Network** is listed in your startup applications list and uses the command nm-applet in the configuration.

You also need to ensure that indicator-plugin applet is in your panel, assuming the system works the same as in 14.04.
