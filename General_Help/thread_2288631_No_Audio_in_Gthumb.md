### Post by Marcaitus on 2015-07-28
Recently I made the switch from Xubuntu to the more minimal Xubuntu Core. Typically when I installed gThumb in Xubuntu I would be able to play audio and video files (in my case WebMs) without configuration but in Xubuntu Core I've installed gThumb and while the video of the WebM will play, there is no audio and other audio files like MP3 now won't play at all.

I've started gThumb through the terminal to see if there were any helpful error messages about missing packages but there are none.

The WebM files will play fine in other programs, audio and video, it's only gThumb which will no longer play them correctly.

Any suggestions?

**Edit: **Solved, needed to install the 'phonon-backend-gstreamer' package.
