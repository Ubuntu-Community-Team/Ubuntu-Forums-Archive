### Post by steveneddy on 2008-06-25
If so, open up synaptic:

System --> Administration --> Synaptic Package Manager

and click in the upper left big box and start typing compiz.

Scroll until you see

compizconfig-settings-manager

right click and install

up top click the [COLOR="Blue"]**Apply**[/COLOR] button (green check mark)

After installation, close synaptic and go to

System --> Preferences --> CompizConfig Settings Manager

All of the extra settings are in there.

All I can tell you is just start playing with settings.

Checking Desktop Cube and Rotate Cube will give you the cube effect.

Ctrl+Alt+left mouse button, move mouse

Your whole world just changed.
