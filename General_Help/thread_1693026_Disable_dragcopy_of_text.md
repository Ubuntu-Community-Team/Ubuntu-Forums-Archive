### Post by MForey on 2011-02-22
Upon upgrading to Ubuntu 10.10, I was surprised to discover that a hidden setting had been replaced, changing the behavior of text selection.  Instead of allowing me to change my selection within a highlighted area with the mouse cursor, the system now attempts to perform a drag and drop operation with the currently selected text, copying the text to a new location.

Quite often, this feature has proven itself to be a nuisance during word processing operations, and I have as yet been unable to discover the location of this setting.  If anyone happens to know where to find this, as well as how to revert it back to a more "classic" behavior, (i.e. clicking and dragging within a highlighted area changes the start and end points of the selection) your assistance would be welcome.
