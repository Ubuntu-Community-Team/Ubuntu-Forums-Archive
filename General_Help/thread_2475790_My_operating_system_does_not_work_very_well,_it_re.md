### Post by grahammechanical on 2022-06-08
> It's a laptop. It's Ubuntu 10

Ubuntu released two versions in 2010. Ubuntu 10.04 reached the end of its support life in May 9th 2013. Ubuntu 20.10 reach the end of its support life in April 10th 2012. Are you sure you have Ubuntu 10 and not Ubuntu 20.10 or Ubuntu 21.10? Is your laptop more than 12 years old? Hardware wears out. Batteries no longer hold their charge.

When the machine shuts down are you using Chrome and the Quillbot extension? If not, then that may not be the cause of the problem. This shutdown and restart, is it a complete shutdown and reboot or is it just the desktop environment that seems to shutdown and then reappear?

Regards
