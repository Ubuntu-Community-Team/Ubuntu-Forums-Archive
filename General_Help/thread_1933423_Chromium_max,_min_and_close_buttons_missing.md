### Post by malspa on 2012-02-29
I'm seeing slightly different behaviors on this depending on which DE or WM I try it in. But try going to into the Preferences/Settings, and under Personal Stuff in the Appearance area, see if it makes a difference if you're using "Use system title bar and borders" or "Hide system title bar and use compact borders." In KDE in Mepis, the first setting removes my min, max, and close buttons in full-screen mode, but the second setting shows them.
