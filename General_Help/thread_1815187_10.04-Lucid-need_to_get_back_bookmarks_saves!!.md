### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-07-31
You can copy all your browsers settings including bookmarks, by copying these folders:

```
~/.mozilla/firefox

~/.config/google-chrome
```

Firefox also has import/export/sync features. See [http://www.webgapps.org/tutorials/firefox/general/profiles-and-backups](http://www.webgapps.org/tutorials/firefox/general/profiles-and-backups)
