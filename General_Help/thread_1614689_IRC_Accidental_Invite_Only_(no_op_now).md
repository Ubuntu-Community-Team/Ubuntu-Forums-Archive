### Post by GrantStoner on 2010-11-05
I was messing with some of the /mode options and set my channel to "invite only". I forgot that I have to tell ChanServ to make me an OP when I log on. I can't send myself an invite because I can't join the channel - because I need an invite to join. How do I fix this? Is the only way to wait for the channel become unregistered after 14 days of no one going to it - and then registering it again?

---

### Post by Thingymebob on 2010-11-06
if you're the channel owner you should be able to 
```

/msg ChanServ OP #channel nickname
/msg ChanServ INVITE #channel nickname

```
