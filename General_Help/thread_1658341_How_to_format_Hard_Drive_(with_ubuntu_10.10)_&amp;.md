### Post by SirDeBoben on 2011-01-02
That would be pretty hard. I'd say write-down a list of what you have installed and back-up anything like pictures and movies to a partition. Make sure you have a bootable disk with the version of ubuntu that you want on it, then format the drive and reinstall ubuntu. After that reinstall your software that you wrote-down and put the files from you back-up back on. That should do what you want. :D (try formating to ext4, it's better :P )
