Hi,

Typically even though two or more users might be suffering similar symptoms it doesn't mean that they have similar issues and the solutions may turn out completely different.

So for the sake of not confusing anybody trying to use a thread in the future it is typically best to start your own thread with your specific issue so that your problem can be addressed.  Searching for relevant threads is the first and best thing to do because you may discover that a solution to your problem already exists and that you don't even need to make a post.  If not then you are more than welcome to link to posts of things you have tried in your new thread to make it easier for people trying to assist to narrow down the issue,

I hope this makes it a bit more clear, and seeing as you already have another thread on this issue open ([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1846245](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1846245)), I am closing this thread.
