### Post by Yashiro on 2009-01-20
Step 1
> Disable **all** plugins (flash etc.) and extensions (noscript etc).
See if the crashes continue.

Step 2
> Close Firefox.
Open your home folder and show all hidden files.
COPY */home/yourname/.mozilla/firefox/somestuff.default*
To your desktop for safe keeping.
Now delete all files within */home/yourname/.mozilla/firefox/somestuff.default*
Start Firefox.
