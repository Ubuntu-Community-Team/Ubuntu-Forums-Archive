### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-02-04
To use CUPS, open a Browser and enter "localhost:631:" (without the quotes).  That will start up CUPS.

Then, go through the menus to see if it detects your Epson printer.  IF it does, use it to install the driver from within CUPS.

I've used several different Epson printers with Linux over the years and this way has always worked for me.
