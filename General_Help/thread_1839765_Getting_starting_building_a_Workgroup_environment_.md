### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-09-06
Before you rush them into using Linux full-time, you should check with them to see how much they would otherwise rely on document templates for legal forms.  I know some folks working in Law offices, and they have MASSIVE libraries of legal form templates they built using MS Word.  

If they need such templates, and there is no way to build and support those using Libre Office, you're going to make their jobs very difficult.
