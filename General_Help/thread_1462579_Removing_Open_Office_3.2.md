### Post by slooow on 2010-04-25
Use synaptic. If you cannot find it in the menus, do a 'run command' or Alt-F2 and type 'gksudo synaptic', enter your password at the prompt.
Then in synaptic, do a find 'Ctrl-F' for "openoffice". In the results list do a sort (click on the left hand column). 
Now all the packages with a green square are installed. Right click on the corresponding line and select 'Mark for removal'.
That should be it.
