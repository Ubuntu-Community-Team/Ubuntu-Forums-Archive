### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-05-28
you could look at mc (midnight commander) for console based file manager, there is also a text editor included in the package called mcedit which i found very easy when starting out with linux. now i just use nano. you should also have a look at screen as a way of multitasking on a single terminal. there is also wv for those times when you need to open a word document and mutt or pine for a cli mail client.
