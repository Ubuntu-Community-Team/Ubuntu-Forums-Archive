### Post by Paqman on 2012-01-31
Yep, there's a few different ways to do this. First of all, you could just copy and paste the entire disc, or make an image of it. The problem with this is that it'll take up a lot of space. Using something like vobcopy is a slightly more sophisticated way to do this.

Alternatively you can re-encode the content using something like Arista or Handbrake. This will take longer to copy, but will result in smaller files that can be customised to the device you want to view them on.
