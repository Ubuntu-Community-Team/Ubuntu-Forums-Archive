### Post by sloarch07 on 2007-10-27
Yes, digiKam will download photos to folders based on date.  I have a Canon PowerShot and I use this feature exclusively.

Upon connecting your camera to your computer via usb digiKam will open and connect to your camera.  If this doesn't happen automatically you can open digiKam and under the Camera menu select your camera.
digiKam will then open a browser window that shows thumbnails of the photos on your camera.

In this browser window click on the settings icon,  a small wrench icon on the right side of the window.  In the settings menu click on the "auto-creation of Albums" tab.  Check the box for "Date-based sub-albums".

Now when you download your photos using the download button you will still be prompted for where you want to save your photos, but instead of all the photos being saved to that folder, sub folders are created for each day that you took pictures and your photos are automatically sorted for you!
