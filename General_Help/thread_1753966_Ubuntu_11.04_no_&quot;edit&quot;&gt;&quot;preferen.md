### Post by greghill on 2011-12-03
Open a folder of your choice (i.e. - "Home") Then place your mouse over the very top of your screen ( on the panel at the top of the screen) until you see the "File  Edit  View, etc" Select Edit>Preferences>Behaviour>Single click to open folders.
Note: The menu in the top bar is sensitive to the window that is open and active, so make sure the "folder" is the active window. For example, if you have Firefox open at the same time, and that browser window is active, then the menu will apply to Firefox, and not the folder window.Took me a while to figure this out, as there is no setting in the settings module for this, and right clicking on the folder doesn't act the same as previously (Version 10 anyway).
