### Post by C.S.Cameron on 2010-06-14
While running Ubuntu if you plug in your flash drive an icon should appear on the desktop.
If you left click this the folder should open showing what is on the flash drive.
If you right click the icon you will get several choices such as unmounting and safely removing the flash drive.

For Windows games check out wine.
