### Post by aeronutt on 2011-05-01
One of 3 things is happening:
- your hardware may not support Unity 3D. If not, 11.04 defaults to ubuntu classic.
- you may have logged in using ubuntu classic rather than ubuntu (menu at the bottom of the screen AFTER you select your user name while logging in)
- 11.04 installed incorrectly.
