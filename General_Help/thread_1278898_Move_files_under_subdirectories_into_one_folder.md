### Post by ownaginatious on 2009-09-30
Hey guys,

Right now I have MP3s scattered all over one of my hard drives in sub-directories. I would like to iterate through all of the subdirectories and move anything matching *.mp3 to a single specified directory. The goal is to get all my MP3s into one folder.

Is there a simple way to do this through the terminal?

Thanks,

Dillon

---

### Post by credobyte on 2009-09-30
```
find /home/user/music -iname '*.mp3' -exec mv '{}' /home/user/backup/ \;

```
