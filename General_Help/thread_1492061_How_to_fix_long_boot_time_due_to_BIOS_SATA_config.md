### Post by neoanderthal on 2010-05-23
I upgraded my Toshiba NB 205 netbook from 9.10 UNR to 10.04 UNR last night, and noted that the boot process takes a very long time - approximately 10 minutes.
Changing SATA mode in the BIOS from AHCI to 'Compatibility' solves this. Using compatibility mode, boot time is around 30 seconds.
