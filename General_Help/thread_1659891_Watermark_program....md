### Post by jcolyn on 2011-01-07
In Gimp you can create a custom made image to copy/paste into the primary image with whatever copyright info you want.

Just click file-new resize the image to your liking then choose fill with-transparency from the advanced tab. Next use your text tool to create the info. Save as a .png.

You can then copy/paste into your image and position with the move tool.. You can use this new file for as many images as you want..
