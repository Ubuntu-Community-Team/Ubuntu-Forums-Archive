### Post by SteveF on 2006-08-11
It costs money, but I have had excellent success using VueScan.  It works with both my flatbed (Canon LiDE) and film scanner (Minolta Dimage Scan Elite).

When I use Windows, I have found VueScan to produce better results than the software that came with the scanners.

Under Linux I have found VueScan to run a little faster than the Windows version.
