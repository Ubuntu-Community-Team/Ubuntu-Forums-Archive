### Post by jpkotta on 2008-12-08
Most of your "desktop managers" are really window managers.  The only ones that are desktop environments are KDE, Gnome, XFCE, and LXDE.  Also, most window managers are "window decorators".  Window managers draw the title bars, borders, resize handles, etc. that go around a window.  Maybe you were thinking of themes?
