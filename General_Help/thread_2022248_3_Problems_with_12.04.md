### Post by bogan on 2012-07-10
Hi!, **jimmac49,**

     "System program problem detected" &   "Sorry, Ubuntu 12.04 has experienced an internal error", plus  "XXXX program has closed unexpectedly" are all commonly occurring error & warning messages since 12.04.

If there is a 'Details' button it will scan and tell what app failed.

Report them and carry on as though they had not happened, seems to be the usual advice, but the reports do not seem to raise a fluster or cause anything to be done about them.

Apparently you can reduce/stop them by disabling 'Apport' but I have not bothered and the reports are now far less frequent.

The 'official' explanation is apparently that the error detection sensitivity is set high for the experimental stages of release; but, if so, why can they not do an update to re-set it.

Chao!, **bogan**.
