### Post by Bucky Ball on 2011-05-21
The easiest is to plug a cable in and get online, get your updates, check System>Administration>Additional drivers. Anything in there for you card? I had a Compaq up in minutes and you shouldn't need to do much. 

When you update you may be offered the approriate software for your card. Follow you nose there. ;)
