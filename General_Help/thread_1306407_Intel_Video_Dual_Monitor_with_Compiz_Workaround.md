I'm running a Lenovo R400 in Lenovo's full size dock with a DVI and Analog out with an integrated Intel video card. I recently updated my machine to 9.10 and could not get both of my monitors to work (as they do in Windows 7) and read several articles about a bug with Karmic, Intel video, and compiz. Enough chatter, on to the work around:

Disable compiz
Get your monitors set up how you like
Turn compiz back on
Profit
